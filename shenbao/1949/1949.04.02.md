### The Peace Talks Have Begun

The government's peace negotiation delegation, a group of six led by "Peace General" Zhang Zhizhong, flew from Nanjing to Beiping yesterday morning amidst the bright spring sunshine to begin negotiations with the CCP delegation. The progress of these peace talks is not only about reconciliation and mending fences between the Kuomintang and the Communist Party but also concerns the entire nation's destiny and the people's livelihood. Especially for the people throughout the country who have suffered from war, it is like a ray of light in the darkness. Whether the people nationwide can escape from the abyss of suffering and realize a happy and joyful life will depend on the outcome of these peace talks. Therefore, all the people in the country, whether in the north or south, male or female, old or young, watched the government's peace delegation fly north yesterday with urgent anticipation, and earnestly wished the delegation success on their mission, with "only success allowed, no failure permitted." Standing on the people's side, as these peace talks are just beginning, we would like to offer a few points for the reference of the Kuomintang and Communist Party peace talk representatives.

First, the peace talk representatives of both the Kuomintang and the Communist Party must stand on the side of the people. Although we are not excessively superstitious about theories such as "sovereignty rests with the people" or the "people's century," we believe that the power of the people is great, and the trend of public opinion cannot be defied. Any government that is detached from the people, no matter how strong its military force may be, will eventually collapse. Any political party, no matter how great its momentum, if it does not have the people as its foundation and blindly believes in military force, will inevitably fail in the end if it carries the war through to the end. Such historical lessons are countless and cannot be enumerated. This must be known to the peace talk representatives of both the Kuomintang and the Communist Party. It is remembered that when the Kuomintang launched the Northern Expedition in the fifteenth and sixteenth years of the Republic of China, they called their army the National Revolutionary Army. Today, the CCP also calls its army the "People's Liberation Army." Although there is a difference between "national" and "people" in the wording, their meaning is the same. When translated into English, both are "People." Therefore, today, when the Kuomintang and the Communist Party are conducting peace talks, they must stand on the side of the people. Otherwise, the peace talks will lose their meaning.

Second, only by conducting peace talks under the principle of "the people's interests above all else" can the peace talks have a bright future. The joys and sorrows, separations and reunions, between the Kuomintang and the Communist Party have a history of thirty years. The new and old grievances between them are innumerable. If today's peace talks still do not forget revenge and insist that blood debts must be repaid with blood, then we can assert that the peace talks have no future at all. Conversely, if the representatives of both sides can abandon their petty prejudices and focus on the bigger picture, taking the interests of the people and the happiness of the country as a prerequisite, then there is absolutely no hatred in the world that cannot be resolved. In particular, in the political struggles between political parties, success should not be taken as glory, and failure need not be considered a great shame. The outdated notion of "become king if successful, become a bandit if defeated" must not be allowed to remain in today's era of party politics. We would like to remind the peace talk representatives of both sides that the Kuomintang and the Communist Party often tout themselves as "serving the country and the people." The people have already heard enough. Who is genuine and who is fake? These peace talks are an excellent touchstone. Today, the people throughout the country are suffering from the torment of war and can no longer survive. The nation and the people have reached the beginning of survival and extinction. The severity of the situation is greater than when the Kuomintang and the Communist Party cooperated in the early days of the War of Resistance Against Japan. If the two sides still cannot embrace and cry together, resolve their grievances and make peace, unite as one, and build an independent, prosperous, healthy, and free new China, what are they talking about "serving the country and the people"! Whichever side it is, it will be abandoned by the people.

Third, we must further point out, what is the people's position? What are the people's interests? Frankly speaking, whether it is the common people in the government-controlled area or the people in the Communist-controlled area, their positions are consistent, and their interests are the same. In other words, their position is peace, and their benefit is peace. Peace is supreme, peace is first, peace is above all else. We hope that the government representatives will tell the CCP representatives in detail about the situation of the people in Jiangnan living in dire straits and their urgent desire for peace, so that they can realize that making peace at this time can best win the hearts of the people. However, if they disregard the life and death of the people and act willfully, they will be abandoned by the people. On the other hand, the government representatives should also take the opportunity during the peace talks to investigate the lives and feelings of the people in the CCP-controlled area, so that although the people throughout the country are geographically divided into government-controlled areas and CCP-controlled areas, they are still a whole in terms of emotion and cannot be separated. ### Illegal Issuance of Bank Drafts by Commercial Banks: The Financial Supervisory Bureau Dispatches Investigators

[Our Newspaper's Report] Commercial bank drafts in our city are again flooding the market. To prevent abuses, the Financial Supervisory Bureau has dispatched investigators in batches. Bank drafts were originally issued in response to customer requests, and the face value is usually not fixed. The amount, date, and signature on the draft should be written in ink at the time of issuance. Since the autumn of last year, the Ministry of Finance has stipulated that all banks must deposit an amount equal to the outstanding balance of bank drafts issued each day into the Central Bank at the end of the business day, and must not over-issue them. Recently, a few banks have violated these regulations, arbitrarily issuing drafts, either not at the request of customers, or pre-printing fixed-amount drafts with seals, or failing to deposit sufficient funds into the Central Bank to cover the outstanding balance of drafts issued that day. All of these are illegal. It is reported that the authorities are investigating and prosecuting these cases and have reiterated previous orders, instructing the Shanghai Banking, Money Exchange, and Trust Trade Association to quickly relay the instructions to all banks and trust companies for compliance. Any further violations will be severely punished upon verification. As for the fixed-amount bank drafts recently issued by the Central Bank to adjust the circulation of banknotes according to actual needs, they are regarded as legal tender. No other bank or money exchange is allowed to imitate their issuance.

### Ping Pong Knowledge

â Written by Wang Xin Jing, Edited by Chen Linsheng (4)

Choosing a Ping Pong Paddle: The ping pong paddle is the most important tool for playing ping pong, and the only tool. Therefore, the selection of a ping pong paddle should be given careful consideration.

The ping pong paddles we used as children had small, round holes about the size of yellow beans in the middle, ranging from as many as twenty or thirty to as few as a dozen. The arrangement of these holes was very inconsistent. These paddles were mostly made of cedar wood, which is lightweight but lacks elasticity. Nowadays, because the holes in the paddle are both meaningless and unsightly, they are being phased out.

In the past, most ping pong paddles were made of plywood because plywood is made by gluing together several thin layers of wood with the grain running in different directions, so it is less affected by the weather. Better quality ping pong paddles are made of paulownia or Philippine mahogany. These woods are softer and more elastic. Some even use cork for the handle, making the paddle very comfortable to hold. Ping pong paddles for hard balls have a rubber surface with studs on one side. The styles of paddles vary greatly. The most popular paddle in the past was oval-shaped with a flat handle; nowadays, round handles resembling hot water bottles are more common. The size, shape, and materials used for the paddle are all up to personal preference and are not restricted by ping pong rules.

Which type is more convenient depends on personal preference, but in my opinion, the paddle should be lightweight and elastic, and being excessively large is not beneficial.

Linsheng's note: Since the introduction of hard ball competitions in this city from the 35th year of the Republic, most players have switched to using rubber paddles. Wooden paddles have also been largely phased out. However, due to economic reasons, wooden paddles are still used by many primary and secondary school students.

(2) The History of Ping Pong in China

The success of any endeavor depends on continuously identifying shortcomings, gradually improving, and ultimately achieving a more perfect state.

Ping pong is no exception.

Ping pong was invented based on the principles of tennis. At the time, there were no fixed standards for the size, weight, color, or hardness of the ball. These were gradually improved until a standard was established. However, due to individual preferences, ping pong balls were broadly divided into soft balls and hard balls. Ten years ago, soft balls were popular in both China and Japan. Because international ping pong competitions now use hard balls, the Chinese ping pong community has been making great efforts to promote hard balls in the past five or six years. Unfortunately, there are too few outstanding talents. If we want to occupy a place on the world ping pong stage in the future, we must pay special attention to hard ball techniques.

In the past, China relied entirely on foreign countries for its ping pong balls. At that time, ping pong was not yet well-developed, and the sales of balls were limited, resulting in little loss. After China and Japan signed an agreement for international competitions in the 12th year of the Republic, ping pong became increasingly popular, and the sales of ping pong balls gradually increased. At the same time, the outflow of money also increased day by day. We are undoubtedly very pleased with the development of ping pong, but we must strive to reverse the outflow of money.

In the 15th year of the Republic, the China Ping Pong Company, with the aim of recovering rights and promoting ping pong, diligently researched and developed the Lianhuan brand of standard ping pong balls. Of course, to promote ping pong, we must be able to manufacture our own ping pong equipment; otherwise, promoting ping pong is tantamount to promoting sales for foreigners. The more ping pong develops, the more money will flow out. ### Black Bears of North America

â Fang Zhou

We Chinese often say "as strong as an ox," the French like to say "as strong as a donkey," while the British and Americans prefer to say "as strong as a bear." In fact, the strength of a bear is indeed ten times greater than that of oxen and donkeys. It is said that if a bear strikes with its paw, the heads of oxen and donkeys can be smashed to pieces.

The forests of Alaska, Allison La, and Florida in North America are active areas for bears. Their fur is mostly black, with a few being cinnamon, coffee, or light brown. Regardless of the color, North American residents call them "black bears."

Black bears can weigh up to four hundred pounds and stand three feet tall. They can stand upright like humans, at which time their height is six feet. In short distances, they can travel thirty miles per hour. Speaking of their strength, they can be said to be unmatched among animals except for elephants. They can pull three hundred pounds of wood and uproot twenty-foot-tall oak trees. Once, a black bear in Florida grabbed a one hundred and fifty-pound pig with its forefoot and ran nearly a mile before abandoning its prey when farmers chased after it in groups.

Black bears eat everything: fruits, roots, insects, fish, and any animal they can catch. They especially like to eat honey and ants. Telephone lines are often damaged by them because they mistake the "buzzing" sound of the telephone lines for bees, so they often climb up telephone poles and tear down the wires. Speaking of the black bear's ability to climb trees, it is a skill they have trained since childhood. A cub less than three months old likes to climb low trees, and only jumps to the ground when the branch droops under its weight. Black bears are also quite clever at catching ants. They dig out ant nests, extend their forefoot, and let angry ants crawl all over it before licking it clean.

Every year from November to April, is the "hibernation" period for black bears. During this time, they hibernate in rock caves or large tree holes, without eating or moving, and their heart rate and breathing become particularly slow. Female bears give birth during this period, producing two or three cubs per litter, each weighing less than one pound, blind and hairless, and only able to leave the cave to play after a month of hibernation. "Hibernation" is a resting period for black bears, but it is also a period of misfortune for them, because hunters often use this time to catch them, and they have no choice but to submit.

Many black bears are kept in American national parks, and they are very tame. It is said that they love to have their picture taken. When tourists raise their cameras, they will pose in the most beautiful and majestic poses, allowing tourists to capture them in the lens.
