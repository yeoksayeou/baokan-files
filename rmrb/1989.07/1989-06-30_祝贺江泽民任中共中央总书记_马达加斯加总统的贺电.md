Title: Congratulations on Jiang Zemin's Appointment as General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee: Messages from the President of Madagascar, the Central Committee of the Movement for the Liberation of São Tomé and Príncipe, the General Secretary of the Yemeni Socialist Party, and the General Secretary of the Communist Party of India (Marxist)
Author:
Date: 1989-06-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 30th.

**Congratulations on Jiang Zemin's Appointment as General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee: Message from the President of Madagascar**

Madagascar President and Secretary-General of the Revolutionary Avant-Garde Didier Ratsiraka sent a telegram to Jiang Zemin, warmly congratulating him on his election as the General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee. The message reads:

On the occasion of your election as the General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, I am pleased to extend, on behalf of the people of Madagascar and its revolutionary government, the Revolutionary Avant-Garde of Madagascar, and in my personal capacity, my warmest congratulations. While wishing you success in fulfilling your noble mission, I am confident that, for the common interests of the peoples and parties of our two countries, the friendly cooperative relations already existing between Madagascar and China will be further developed.

**Congratulations on Jiang Zemin's Appointment as General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee: Message from the Central Committee of the Movement for the Liberation of São Tomé and Príncipe**

The Central Committee of the Movement for the Liberation of São Tomé and Príncipe sent a message to Jiang Zemin on June 28th, warmly congratulating him on his election as the General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee. The message reads:

On the occasion of your election as the highest leader of the Communist Party of China, the Central Committee of the Movement for the Liberation of São Tomé and Príncipe, the people of São Tomé and Príncipe, and Comrade Manuel Pinto da Costa, Chairman, are honored to extend to you their warmest and most sincere congratulations.

We fervently hope that you, Comrade Jiang Zemin, the members of the Communist Party of China, and the Chinese people will achieve great successes in implementing the resolutions of the Thirteenth National Congress of your Party.

We reaffirm our willingness to continue to take joint action to strengthen and consolidate our fraternal friendship, which binds us to the cause of peace, justice, and social progress for humanity.

**Congratulations on Jiang Zemin's Appointment as General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee: Message from the General Secretary of the Yemeni Socialist Party**

On June 28th, Ali Salem al-Beidh, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Yemeni Socialist Party, sent a telegram to Jiang Zemin, warmly congratulating him on his election as the General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee. The message reads:

On the occasion of your election as the General Secretary of the Central Committee, I extend to you my heartfelt warm congratulations and best wishes, wishing you good health and success in your important mission. I wish that the friendly cooperative relations between our two parties and countries will continue to develop.

**Congratulations on Jiang Zemin's Appointment as General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee: Message from the General Secretary of the Communist Party of India (Marxist)**

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of India (Marxist) sent a message to Jiang Zemin on June 27th, congratulating him on his election as the General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee. The message reads:

I, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of India (Marxist), warmly congratulate you on your assumption of the high office of General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

The Central Committee of my party wishes you outstanding success in the heavy responsibilities you have undertaken. We wish your party victory in implementing the policies determined by the recently concluded Fourth Plenum of the Thirteenth Central Committee for solving the complex problems facing China today. We are confident that the fraternal relations between our two parties will continue to develop and strengthen.

### 祝贺江泽民任中共中央总书记  马达加斯加总统的贺电

1989-06-30
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    祝贺江泽民任中共中央总书记
    马达加斯加总统的贺电
    新华社北京6月30日电　马达加斯加总统、革命先锋总书记迪迪埃·拉齐拉卡打电报给江泽民，热烈祝贺他当选为中共中央总书记。电文如下：
    在您当选为中国共产党中央委员会总书记之际，我愉快地以马达加斯加人民及其革命政权和马达加斯加革命先锋并以我个人的名义向您致以热烈的祝贺。在祝您圆满完成您的崇高使命的同时，我相信，为了我们两国人民和两党的共同利益，马中两国间业已存在的友好合作关系将得到进一步的发展。
    圣普解放运动中央委员会的贺信
    新华社北京6月30日电　圣多美和普林西比解放运动中央委员会28日致信给江泽民，热烈地祝贺他当选为中共中央总书记。贺信如下：
    值此您当选为中国共产党最高领导人之际，圣多美和普林西比解放运动中央委员会、圣多美和普林西比人民和曼努埃尔·平托·达科斯塔主席同志荣幸地向您致以最热烈和最诚挚的祝贺。
    我们热情地希望您——江泽民同志、中国共产党党员和中国人民在执行贵党第十三次全国代表大会决议的工作中取得伟大的成就。
    我们重申，我们愿继续采取共同行动，巩固和加强我们之间的兄弟般的友谊，这种友谊把我们与人类和平、正义和社会进步的事业联系在一起。
    也门社会党总书记的贺电
    新华社北京6月30日电　也门社会党中央委员会总书记阿里·萨利姆·比德28日打电报给江泽民，热烈祝贺他当选为中共中央总书记。电文如下：
    在您当选为中央委员会总书记之际，我谨向您表示衷心的热烈祝贺和最良好的祝愿，祝您健康并在担负重大使命中取得成功，祝我们两党和两国之间的友好合作关系不断发展。
    印共（马）总书记的贺信
    新华社北京6月30日电　中国共产党中央委员会今天收到印度共产党（马）总书记南布迪里巴德27日致江泽民同志的贺信，祝贺他当选中国共产党中央委员会总书记。贺信全文如下：
    我代表印度共产党（马）中央委员会热烈祝贺你就任中国共产党中央委员会总书记这一崇高职务。
    我党中央委员预祝你将出色地完成你所承担的重任。我们预祝贵党将胜利地贯彻最近召开的中共十三届四中全会为解决当前中国复杂问题而确定的方针。我们相信，我们两党之间的兄弟关系将继续发展和巩固。
