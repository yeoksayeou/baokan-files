Title: Foreign Affairs News Brief
Author:
Date: 1989-07-27
Edition: 2

	State Councilor and Minister of Public Security Wang Fang met with a delegation from the Romanian Ministry of the Interior led by Major General Marisescu Ian Mihai on July 27 at the Great Hall of the People.

	During the meeting, Wang Fang stated that the Chinese public security organs will continue to maintain and strengthen technical exchanges and cooperation with foreign police forces in areas such as combating international criminal activities.

	Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Ni Zhi Fu met with a delegation from the Indian National Trade Union Congress led by Vice Chairman Suri Yanala Narayana Rao on July 27. He introduced to the guests the important role played by the Chinese working class in suppressing the recent turmoil and counter-revolutionary rebellion, as well as the situation of the ongoing 11th session of the 3rd enlarged meeting of the presidium of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

	The trade agreement between the governments of China and Ghana and the barter trade protocol for 1989-1991 were signed in Beijing on July 27. Minister of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Zheng Tuobin and Ghanaian Minister and Chairman of the National Economic Cooperation Council Don Arthur signed the two documents. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 外事简讯

1989-07-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲国务委员兼公安部长王芳27日在人民大会堂会见了由后勤装备局长马里泽斯库·扬·米海依率领的罗马尼亚内务部代表团。
    会见时，王芳表示中国公安部门将继续在打击国际刑事犯罪等方面保持并加强同外国警方的技术交流与合作。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长、中华全国总工会主席倪志福27日会见了以副主席苏里杨纳拉亚纳·拉奥为团长的印度工会中心代表团，向客人介绍了中国工人阶级在这次制止动乱、平息反革命暴乱中所发挥的重要作用，以及正在召开的全总十一届三次主席团扩大会议的情况。
    ▲中国政府和加纳政府贸易协定和1989—1991年度易货贸易议定书27日在北京签署。对外经济贸易部部长郑拓彬和加纳政府部长、国家经济合作委员会主席顿·阿瑟在两个文件上签字。　（据新华社）
