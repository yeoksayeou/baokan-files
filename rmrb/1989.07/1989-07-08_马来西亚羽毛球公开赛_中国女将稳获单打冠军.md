Title: Chinese Women Secure Singles Title at Malaysian Open Badminton Championship
Author:
Date: 1989-07-08
Edition: 4

Chinese players have secured the women's singles title at the Malaysian Open Badminton Championship held in Kuala Lumpur. Chinese male player Zhang Qingwu was eliminated in the quarterfinals yesterday.

Two Chinese women, Huang Ying and Zheng Baojun, were eliminated by their own teammates. The four women in the semifinals are all Chinese: Han Aiping, Tang Jiuhong, Luo Yun, and Huang Hua. In the men's singles semifinals, there are two Chinese players, Xiong Guobao and Zhao Jianhua, as well as two from Indonesia, Lu Tianning and Yoko Supriyanto.

Chinese women occupy three of the four spots in the women's doubles semifinals, with the remaining spot held by South Korea's Hwang Hye-young and Jeong So-young. The three Chinese pairs are Lin Ying and Guan Weizhen, Nong Qunhua and Pan Li, and Yao Fen and Lai Caijin. The pair Luo Yun and Shi Fangjing were eliminated by South Korean players. In the mixed doubles semifinals, there is only one Chinese pair: Wang Pengren and Shi Fangjing.

### 马来西亚羽毛球公开赛  中国女将稳获单打冠军

1989-07-08
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    马来西亚羽毛球公开赛
    中国女将稳获单打冠军
    新华社北京7月8日电　据外电报道，中国选手已稳获在吉隆坡举行的马来西亚羽毛球公开赛女单冠军。中国男选手张青武在昨天的1／4决赛中落选。
    两名女将黄莹和郑宝君被自己队友淘汰。进入女单前四名的全是中国选手，她们是韩爱平、唐九红、罗云和黄华。男单前四名选手中有中国的熊国宝和赵剑华，还有印度尼西亚的罗天宁和约科·苏普里扬托。
    中国女选手占居女双前四名的3个位置，另一个位置由南朝鲜的黄惠英／郑素英保持。三对中国选手是林瑛／关渭贞、农群华／潘莉、姚芬／赖彩琴。罗云和史方静一对被南朝鲜选手所淘汰。在混双前四名中只有一对中国选手。即王朋仁和史方静。
