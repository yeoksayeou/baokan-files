Title: Ode to Heroes—To the Martyrs of the People's Liberation Army
Author: Liu Qingchuan
Date: 1989-07-02
Edition: 8

Yesterday, your body was consumed in the twilight of the riot,
Today, your spirit stands tall in the sunlight, narrating,
Narrating the brutality of a handful of villains,
Narrating the perilous moment of the Chinese nation!
Ah! Loyal and steadfast iron soldiers,
For the sake of maintaining order in the capital, you braved fire and water,
As the heroic sons and daughters of the people,
You have merged your youth into the annals of the republic!
Your eyes, full of justice, will never close,
Stirring the indignant souls,
Facing this blood and fire, and fire and blood,
How can the kind-hearted people not ponder deeply….
Gazing at the vast and beautiful rivers and mountains,
The blood-stained splendor is even more radiant,
You are the true defenders of democracy and freedom,
You have written a new page in history with your lives!
Now, the evil has been purged from Tiananmen Square,
The rioters have gotten what they deserve,
The motherland smiles with victory,
Your monument stands tall in the hearts of the people!

### 英灵赞——致解放军烈士
刘庆川
1989-07-02
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

    英灵赞
    ——致解放军烈士
    刘庆川
    昨天，你的身躯被焚烧在暴乱的暮色，
    今天，你的英灵屹立在阳光下诉说，
    诉说一小撮歹徒凶狠残暴，
    诉说中华民族危难的时刻！
    啊！赤胆忠心的钢铁战士，
    为维护首都秩序赴汤蹈火，
    作为人民英雄的子弟兵，
    你把青春溶进了共和国的史册！
    你充满正义的眼波永不瞑合，
    牵动着义愤填膺的魂魄，
    面对这血与火和火与血，
    善良的人们怎能不认真思索……
    凝视着万里锦绣河山，
    血染的风采更加绚丽，
    你是真正捍卫民主自由的勇士，
    你用生命为历史写下了崭新的一页！
    如今，清除了天安门广场上的邪恶，
    暴徒们罪有应得，
    祖国母亲绽开胜利的笑容，
    你的丰碑耸立在人民的心窝！
