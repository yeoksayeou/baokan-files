Title: Severe Drought Strikes Xinjiang
Author: Lǐ Xiàn Guó
Date: 1989-07-13
Edition: 2

	Since this spring, the drought in the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region has been worsening. The rainfall in the affected agricultural and pastoral areas has been reduced by 25% to 46% compared to the average of previous years. The water volume of major rivers has also decreased by 40% to 55%. Among them, the rainfall in Hami, Altay, and the eastern five counties of the Changji Hui Autonomous Prefecture from January to June was only 2 to 3 millimeters, making it one of the years with the least rainfall in nearly 40 years.

	According to incomplete statistics from relevant departments, the total area of farmland affected by drought in the entire autonomous region is 5.8 million mu, of which more than 600,000 mu could not be sown due to severe drought.

### 新疆发生严重干旱
李现国
1989-07-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    新疆发生严重干旱
    新华社乌鲁木齐7月13日电　（记者李现国）今年春夏以来，新疆维吾尔自治区旱情不断加重，受旱农牧区降水量普遍比历年平均值减少25％至46％，各大河流水量也普遍减少40％至55％。其中哈密地区、阿勒泰地区和昌吉回族自治州东部5县1至6月份降水量仅有2至3毫米，是近40年来降水量最少的年份之一。
    据有关部门的不完全统计，全自治区受旱农田达580万亩，其中因严重干旱无法播种的有60多万亩。
