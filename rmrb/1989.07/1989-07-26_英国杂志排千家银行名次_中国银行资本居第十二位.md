Title: British Magazine Ranks 1,000 Banks, China Bank Capital at 12th Place
Author: Ji Hongqing
Date: 1989-07-26
Edition: 2

    A recent issue of the British magazine *The Banker* published its rankings of the world's top 1,000 banks. According to the rankings, the Bank of China is in 12th place in terms of capital and 22nd place in terms of total assets.

    By the end of 1988, the Bank of China's total assets had reached 559.7 billion yuan, with 75% of these being foreign exchange assets. Its capital stood at 10 billion yuan. In March of this year, the Bank of China increased its capital by 5 billion yuan. If calculated based on 15 billion yuan in capital, the Bank of China's ranking in terms of capital among the world's largest banks would be even higher.

### 英国杂志排千家银行名次  中国银行资本居第十二位
计泓赓
1989-07-26
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    英国杂志排千家银行名次
    中国银行资本居第十二位
    本报北京7月25日讯　记者计泓赓从有关方面获悉：最近出版的英国《银行家》杂志第7期，公布了世界1000家大银行的名次。按资本排列，中国银行居世界第12位；按资产总额排列，中国银行居世界第22位。
    1988年末，中国银行的资产总额已经达到5597亿元人民币，其中75％是外汇资产。资本为100亿元人民币。今年3月，中国银行又增资50亿元人民币。如按150亿元人民币资本计，中国银行在世界大银行资本排列的名次，还要提前。
