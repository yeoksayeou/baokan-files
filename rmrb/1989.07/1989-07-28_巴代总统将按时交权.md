Title: Panama's Acting President to Hand Over Power on Time
Author:
Date: 1989-07-28
Edition: 3

Panama's Presidential Palace issued a statement on the 26th announcing that Acting President Palma will hand over the reins of government to the new head of state upon the expiration of his term on September 1 of this year.

(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 巴代总统将按时交权

1989-07-28
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    巴代总统将按时交权
    巴拿马总统府26日发表新闻公报宣布，代总统帕尔马将于今年9月1日任期届满后把政权交给新的政府首脑。
          （据新华社）
