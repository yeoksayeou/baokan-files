Title: The First Private University in Changchun Established
Author: Chen Meifeng
Date: 1989-07-27
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, July 27th.

The first private university in Jilin Province—Songhua River University—was established on July 16th in Changchun. This private university is run by social forces, following the principles of self-management, self-governance, and self-sufficiency, without relying on state investment. It offers majors that are in high demand by society.

The university will begin recruiting students from society this year, initially offering majors in foreign languages, foreign trade, finance, law, and enterprise management, with a two-year program. There will be no job assignments upon graduation.

### 一所私立大学在长春成立
陈美凤
1989-07-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    一所私立大学在长春成立
    据新华社长春七月二十七日电　（记者陈美凤）吉林省第一所私立大学——松花江大学于七月十六日在长春成立。这所私立大学是依靠社会力量办起来的，实行自办、自治、自理的方针，不要国家投资，开设社会急需专业。
    这所大学将于今年开始面向社会招生，先期拟开设外语、外贸、财经、法律、企业管理等专业，学制两年。不包分配。
