Title: The U.S. Prepares to Mediate Between Argentina and the UK
Author:
Date: 1989-07-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Buenos Aires, July 21st.

The U.S. Ambassador to Argentina, Terence Todman, stated on the 20th that the United States is prepared to mediate any conflicts between Argentina and the United Kingdom.

Todman made these remarks after visiting the Argentine General Confederation of Labor. He said that there is a possibility of resuming diplomatic relations between Argentina and the UK, which were severed due to the 1982 Malvinas War.

He emphasized that the United States is ready at any time to do everything possible to help the two countries achieve peace and understanding. If both sides request it, the United States can mediate any conflicts between them.

### 美准备调解阿英关系

1989-07-21
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美准备调解阿英关系
    新华社北京7月21日电  布宜诺斯艾利斯消息：美国驻阿根廷大使特伦斯·托德曼20日表示，美国准备调解阿根廷和英国之间存在的“任何冲突”。
    托德曼是在访问阿根廷总工会之后说这番话的。他说，阿、英之间“有可能”恢复因1982年马尔维纳斯群岛战争而中断的外交关系。
    他强调，美国随时准备尽一切可能帮助阿英两国实现和平与谅解。如果双方提出要求，美国可以调解它们之间的“任何冲突”。
