Title: Sudan Establishes New Government, Bashir Appointed Prime Minister
Author: Lin Jiaoming
Date: 1989-07-10
Page: 3

Sudan today established a new government led by the National Salvation Revolutionary Command Council.

The Prime Minister is the Council's Chairman, Omar Hassan al-Bashir.

The new government consists of 21 members, including 16 civilian officials, 3 members of the National Salvation Revolutionary Command Council, and 2 other senior officials. Key positions such as the Prime Minister, Deputy Prime Minister, and Ministers of Defense and Interior are held by military personnel.

The National Salvation Revolutionary Command Council decided yesterday to form the new government, and the cabinet took the oath of office today.

### 苏丹成立新政府  巴谢尔任总理
林皎明
1989-07-10
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏丹成立新政府
    巴谢尔任总理
    本报开罗7月10日电　记者林皎明报道：苏丹今天成立由救国革命指挥委员会领导的新政府。
    政府总理由指挥委员会主席奥马尔·哈桑·巴谢尔担任。
    新政府由21名成员组成，其中包括16名文职官员、3名救国革命指挥委员会成员和其他2名老资格的官员。总理、副总理、国防和内政部长等重要职务由军人担任。
    救国革命指挥委员会昨天决定成立新政府，内阁今天宣誓就职。
