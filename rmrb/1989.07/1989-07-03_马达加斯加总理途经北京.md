Title: Madagascar's Prime Minister Passes Through Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-07-03
Edition: 2

Madagascar's Prime Minister Passes Through Beijing

New China News Agency Beijing, July 3 — After concluding his visit to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Prime Minister Victor Ramanantsoa of the Democratic Republic of Madagascar arrived in Beijing by airplane early this morning en route to Hong Kong. Those who went to the capital airport to welcome and see him off included Minister of Communications Qian Yongchang and others. Both sides engaged in friendly conversations.

### 马达加斯加总理途经北京

1989-07-03
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    马达加斯加总理途经北京
    新华社北京7月3日电  马达加斯加民主共和国总理维克托·拉马哈特拉在结束对朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的访问之后，于今天凌晨乘飞机途经北京去香港。到首都机场迎送的有交通部部长钱永昌等。双方进行了友好的交谈。
