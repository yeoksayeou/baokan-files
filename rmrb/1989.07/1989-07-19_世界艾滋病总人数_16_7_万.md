Title: Global AIDS Cases Total 167,000
Author:
Date: 1989-07-19
Edition: 7

As of July 1, the total number of AIDS cases worldwide reached 167,373, indicating that more than 10,000 new cases were reported in June.

Experts from the World Health Organization estimate that the actual number of AIDS patients globally is twice the reported figure. By 1993, the number of AIDS patients worldwide is expected to increase by at least 1 million. Currently, 149 countries and regions have reported AIDS cases; only 28 countries, mostly in Asia, have reported no cases to the World Health Organization.

The United States has the highest number of AIDS cases, with 95,561 reported. Uganda ranks second with 6,772 cases, followed by Brazil and France with 6,421 and 6,409 cases, respectively.

(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 世界艾滋病总人数16.7万

1989-07-19
第7版(国际专页)
专栏：

    世界艾滋病总人数16.7万
    至7月1日为止，全世界的艾滋病总人数为167373人，这表明在6月份新出现的病人超过1万人。
    世界卫生组织专家估计，全世界艾滋病患者的实际人数是已报告人数的2倍。到1993年，全世界至少还会增加100万艾滋病人。目前已有149个国家和地区发现了艾滋病；仅有28个国家，主要是亚洲一些国家向世界卫生组织报告没有发现艾滋病。
    美国的艾滋病患者人数最多，已有95561人；第二位是乌干达，为6772人；其次为巴西和法国，患病人数分别为6421人和6409人。
      （据新华社）
