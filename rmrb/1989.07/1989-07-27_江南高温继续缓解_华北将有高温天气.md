Title: High Temperatures in Jiangnan Continue to Ease, High Temperatures Expected in North China
Author:
Date: 1989-07-27
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 27th.

The Central Meteorological Observatory reported that on July 26th, the high temperatures in Jiangnan, northern Guangdong, and the southwestern regions of China continued to ease. The highest temperatures dropped to between 30 and 33 degrees Celsius, and it is expected that there will be no high temperatures in the next two days.

It is forecasted that from July 28th to 30th, the weather in North China will be mainly clear, and the temperatures will significantly rise. The highest temperatures are expected to reach between 33 and 38 degrees Celsius.

### 江南高温继续缓解  华北将有高温天气

1989-07-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    江南高温继续缓解
    华北将有高温天气
    新华社北京7月27日电　中央气象台提供的消息，7月26日，我国江南、华南北部、西南地区的高温天气继续缓解，最高气温已降至30至33摄氏度，预计近两天内不会出现高温。
    预计，7月28日至30日，华北地区天气以晴为主，气温将显著回升，最高气温将达33至38摄氏度。
