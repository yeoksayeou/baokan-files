Title: Beijing Holds Reception to Celebrate the 28th Anniversary of the Signing of the Sino-North Korean Treaty
Author:
Date: 1989-07-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 7th.

Beijing held a reception to celebrate the 28th anniversary of the signing of the Sino-North Korean Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation, and Mutual Assistance.

Nearly one hundred Chinese and North Korean guests attended the reception. At the reception, the president of the China-North Korea Friendship Association, Cui Yuelei, and the Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, Zhu Changjun, both spoke. They pointed out that the Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation, and Mutual Assistance, signed by the late Premier Zhou Enlai and Chairman Kim Il-sung, holds significant importance in the history of Sino-North Korean friendship. Over the past 28 years, both China and North Korea have upheld the spirit of the treaty, trusting and supporting each other. They have made important contributions to strengthening and developing the great friendship and cooperation forged in blood.

### 北京举行招待会  庆祝中朝条约签订二十八周年

1989-07-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    北京举行招待会
    庆祝中朝条约签订二十八周年
    据新华社北京七月七日电　对外友协和中朝友协今天联合举行招待会，庆祝中朝友好合作互助条约签订二十八周年。
    将近一百名中朝人士出席了招待会。中朝友协会长崔月犁和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使朱昌骏在招待会上讲话时都指出，由已故总理周恩来和金日成主席签署的中朝友好合作互助条约，在中朝友谊史上具有重要意义。二十八年来，中朝双方信守条约精神，互相信任、互相支持，为加强和发展用鲜血凝成的伟大友谊与合作，做出了重要的贡献。
