Title: Over 2,000 Muslim Compatriots Celebrate Eid al-Adha in Beijing
Author: Gélái
Date: 1989-07-12
Edition: 2

Today afternoon, over 2,000 representatives from the Hui, Uyghur, Kazakh, Dongxiang, Kyrgyz, Sala, Tajik, Uzbek, Baoan, and Tatar ethnic groups held a grand celebration to observe Eid al-Adha. Vice President Wang Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Xi Zhongxun, Liao Hansheng, Saidihajing Aisijiang, and Fei Xiaotong attended the celebration and extended their greetings to the Muslim compatriots, wishing them a happy festival and celebrating the great unity of all ethnic groups.

Deputy Director of the State Ethnic Affairs Commission Wu Jinghua, on behalf of the commission, presented three whole roasted sheep to the Muslim compatriots in Beijing, wishing everyone a happy festival. He said, "The gathering of 10 ethnic groups of Muslim cadres and masses in the capital to celebrate the festival together is a first since the founding of the nation and is very significant."

Officers and soldiers from a certain unit executing martial law in Beijing also participated in the celebration.

### 两千多名穆斯林同胞在京欢度“古尔邦节”
格来
1989-07-12
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    两千多名穆斯林同胞在京欢度“古尔邦节”
    据新华社北京7月12日电  （记者格来）今天下午，回、维吾尔、哈萨克、东乡、柯尔克孜、撒拉、塔吉克、乌孜别克、保安、塔塔尔等10个民族的2000多名穆斯林同胞代表，举行盛大联欢会，欢度“古尔邦节”。国家副主席王震，全国人大常委会副委员长习仲勋、廖汉生、赛福鼎·艾则孜、费孝通等出席了联欢会，祝贺穆斯林同胞节日快乐，祝贺各民族的大团结。
    国家民委副主任伍精华代表民委向在京的穆斯林同胞赠送三头烤全羊，祝贺大家节日愉快。他说，生活在首都的10个民族的穆斯林干部、群众欢聚一堂，共度佳节，这在建国以来还是第一次，很有意义。
    在京执行戒严任务的某部干部、战士参加了联欢会。
