Title: Lebanese Factions Issue Statement to End 15-Month Hostilities
Author:
Date: 1989-07-25
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tehran, July 24th.

Two Shiite Muslim organizations in Lebanon—the Hezbollah and the Amal Movement—issued a joint statement today, pledging to end their 15-month-long hostilities and restore normal relations.

The statement called for "joint operations" to defeat Israeli forces and the Maronite Christian forces supported by Iraq.

Hezbollah leader Fadlallah highly praised the mediation efforts of Iran's new leader, Khamenei. The Iran-friendly Hezbollah and the Syrian-backed Amal Movement signed a reconciliation agreement here last week.

Since April 1988, the two factions have been fighting over the 1.2 million Shiite Muslims in Lebanon, resulting in 560 deaths and 1,550 injuries.

### 黎巴嫩两派发表声明  结束15个月敌对状态

1989-07-25
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    黎巴嫩两派发表声明
    结束15个月敌对状态
    新华社德黑兰7月24日电  黎巴嫩两个什叶派穆斯林组织——真主党和阿迈勒运动，今天发表联合声明，决心结束他们之间历时15个月的敌对状态，恢复正常关系。
    声明呼吁采取“联合作战”行动，打败以色列军队和由伊拉克支持的马龙派基督教力量。
    真主党领袖法德拉高度赞扬了伊朗新领袖哈梅内伊的调停努力。亲伊朗的真主党和由叙利亚支持的阿迈勒运动上周在此间签署了一项和解协议。
    自1988年4月以来，两派为了争取黎巴嫩120万什叶派穆斯林，战火连绵不断，致使560人死亡、1550人受伤。
