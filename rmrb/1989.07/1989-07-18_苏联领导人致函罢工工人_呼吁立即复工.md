Title: Soviet Leader Writes to Striking Workers Urging Immediate Return to Work
Author:
Date: 1989-07-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 17th.

Soviet leader Gorbachev and Prime Minister Ryzhkov jointly wrote a letter to the striking coal miners in Kuzbass, Siberia, on July 16, urging them to immediately return to work.

On July 10, coal miners in the Mezhdurechensk mine in the Kuzbass region went on strike. By July 14, the strike had spread to nine cities in the region. Currently, the number of strikers has reached 110,000.

Ryzhkov announced that a special committee, consisting of Politburo member and Central Committee Secretary Slyunikov, First Deputy Prime Minister Voronin, and the Chairman of the Trade Union Council, Shalaev, has been dispatched to the mining area to address a series of related issues.

### 苏联领导人致函罢工工人  呼吁立即复工

1989-07-18
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏联领导人致函罢工工人
    呼吁立即复工
    新华社莫斯科7月17日电  苏联最高领导人戈尔巴乔夫和部长会议主席雷日科夫16日联名致函西伯利亚库兹巴斯正在罢工的煤矿工人，呼吁他们立即复工。
    本月10日，库兹巴斯矿区的梅日杜列钦斯克煤矿工人举行罢工。14日，罢工蔓延到这一矿区的9个城市。目前罢工人数为11万人。
    雷日科夫宣布，一个由苏共中央政治局委员、中央书记斯柳尼科夫、部长会议第一副主席沃罗宁和工会中央理事会主席沙拉耶夫组成的专门委员会已前往矿区解决一系列有关问题。
