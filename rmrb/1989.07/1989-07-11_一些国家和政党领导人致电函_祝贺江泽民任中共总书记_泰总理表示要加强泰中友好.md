Title: Some Foreign Leaders Send Congratulatory Messages to Jiang Zemin on His Election as CPC General Secretary
Author: Liu Aicheng
Date: 1989-07-11
Edition: 3

The Communist Party of China Central Committee today received congratulatory telegrams and letters from Nepalese Prime Minister Marich Man Singh Shrestha and Polish and Italian friends, congratulating Jiang Zemin on his election as the General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee.

In his congratulatory telegram, Prime Minister Shrestha said, "On the occasion of your election as the General Secretary of the Communist Party of China Central Committee, I extend my heartfelt congratulations to you. I wish you success and believe that the friendly relations that already exist between Nepal and China will continue to develop in the years to come."

In addition, the Communist Party of China Central Committee also received congratulatory letters from Adam Worapetka, President of the Supreme Court of the Polish People's Republic, and Vittorio Colombo, Chairman of the Italy-China Economic and Cultural Exchange Association, on June 26 and June 30 respectively, congratulating Jiang Zemin on his election as the General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee.

The People's Daily, Beijing, July 11 - In recent days, Jiang Zemin has also received congratulatory telegrams and letters from leaders of foreign political parties, congratulating him on his election as the General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee.

Those who sent congratulatory telegrams and letters include: Rajeshwar Rao, General Secretary of the National Committee of the Communist Party of India, Ali Yata, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Progressive and Socialist Party of Morocco, K.P. Silva, General Secretary of the Communist Party of Sri Lanka, Alberto Moreno, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Peru, and Rolando Breña, Chairman of the National Executive Committee of the Revolutionary Left Union of Peru.

The People's Daily, Bangkok, July 11 - Correspondent Liu Aicheng reports: Thai Prime Minister Chatichai expressed his desire to further strengthen Thai-Chinese friendship and economic relations.

Chatichai made these remarks today during a meeting with a Chinese friendly delegation led by Wang Feng, a member of the Central Advisory Commission of the CPC, who was visiting Thailand at the invitation of the Thai Charoen Pokphand Company.

Chatichai said, "Currently, the relations between Thailand and China are in a highly friendly period. In the future, the two countries will further strengthen economic and trade cooperation and promote technological exchanges. Thailand's investment in China will continue, and this will not change."

Chatichai also proposed that in the future, economic cooperation in the Asia-Pacific region, including China, should be further developed.

The Chinese friendly delegation was invited to visit Thailand by the Thai Charoen Pokphand Company.

### 一些国家和政党领导人致电函  祝贺江泽民任中共总书记  泰总理表示要加强泰中友好
刘爱成
1989-07-11
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    一些国家和政党领导人致电函
    祝贺江泽民任中共总书记
    泰总理表示要加强泰中友好
    新华社北京7月11日电　中国共产党中央委员会今天收到尼泊尔首相马里奇·曼·辛格·什雷斯塔和波兰、意大利友人致江泽民同志的贺电和贺信，祝贺他当选中共中央总书记。
    什雷斯塔首相的贺电说：“值此你当选中国共产党中央委员会总书记之际，我谨向阁下表示衷心的祝贺。我祝你成功，相信业已存在于尼中之间的令人愉快的友好关系将在未来的岁月里继续得到发展。”
    此外，中国共产党中央委员会还收到了波兰人民共和国最高法院首席院长亚当·沃帕特卡、意大利—中国经济文化交流协会主席维托里诺·科隆博参议员分别于6月26日和6月30日致江泽民同志的贺信，祝贺他当选为中国共产党中央委员会总书记。
    新华社北京7月11日电　近日来，江泽民同志又收到了一些外国政党领导人给他发来的热烈祝贺他当选为中共中央总书记的贺电贺信。
    发来贺电贺信的有：印度共产党全国委员会总书记拉哲斯瓦尔·拉奥、摩洛哥进步与社会主义党中央总书记阿里
  ·亚塔、斯里兰卡共产党总书记K·P·席尔瓦、秘鲁共产党中央委员会总书记阿尔维托·莫雷诺、秘鲁革命左派联盟全国执委会主席罗兰多·布雷尼亚。
    本报曼谷7月11日电　记者刘爱成报道：泰国总理差猜表示要进一步加强泰中友好及经贸关系。
    差猜总理是今天在此间会见以中共中央顾问委员会委员汪锋率领的中国友好代表团访泰时说这番话的。
    差猜总理说，“目前，泰中两国关系正处在一个高度友好时期。今后两国将进一步加强经济贸易合作，促进科技交流。泰国对中国的投资将还会继续，这一点不会变化。”
    差猜总理还提出今后应进一步发展包括中国在内的亚太地区的经济合作。
    中国友好代表团是应泰国正大公司的邀请来泰访问的。
