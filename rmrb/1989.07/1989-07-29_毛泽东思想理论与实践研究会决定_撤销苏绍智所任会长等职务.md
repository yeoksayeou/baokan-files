Title: The Mao Zedong Thought Theory and Practice Research Association Decides to Remove Su Shaozhi from His Positions
Author:
Date: 1989-07-29
Edition: 2

The Mao Zedong Thought Theory and Practice Research Association recently held a meeting of its Standing Committee in Beijing. The meeting decided to remove Su Shaozhi from his leadership positions within the association.

The attendees initially exposed and criticized Su Shaozhi's persistent adherence to bourgeois liberalization. The Standing Committee decided: to remove Su Shaozhi from his positions as President of the Mao Zedong Thought Theory and Practice Research Association and Editor-in-Chief of the association's journal; to convene a full meeting of the Board of Directors to further expose and criticize Su Shaozhi's issues.

### 毛泽东思想理论与实践研究会决定  撤销苏绍智所任会长等职务

1989-07-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    毛泽东思想理论与实践研究会决定
    撤销苏绍智所任会长等职务
    本报北京7月29日讯　中国毛泽东思想理论与实践研究会常务理事会最近在北京召开会议，决定撤销苏绍智在该会担任的领导职务。
    与会同志初步揭露和批判了苏绍智顽固坚持资产阶级自由化的言行。常务理事会决定：撤销苏绍智中国毛泽东思想理论与实践研究会会长和会刊编委会主任的职务；将召开理事会全体会议，进一步揭露和批判苏绍智的问题。
