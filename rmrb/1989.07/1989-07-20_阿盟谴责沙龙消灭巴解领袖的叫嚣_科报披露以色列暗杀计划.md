Title: Arab League Condemns Sharon's Threats to Eliminate PLO Leaders
Author:
Date: 1989-07-20
Edition: 3

The Arab League Secretary-General, Chedli Klibi, today stated that Israeli Minister of Trade and Industry Ariel Sharon's call to eliminate PLO leaders is an "unacceptable challenge."

At a press conference in Tunis, Klibi said that Sharon's call to physically eliminate Yasser Arafat and other PLO leaders shows that Israeli leaders are willing to use state terrorism to maintain their policies of aggression and occupation.

Klibi concluded by urging the international community, especially influential nations, to respond clearly and strongly to this issue.

According to a report by Kuwait's *Al-Siyassa* newspaper today, Israel has devised a plan to assassinate Palestinian Liberation Organization (PLO) leader Yasser Arafat and other Palestinian leaders within the next few months.

Under the supervision of Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhak Shamir, this assassination plan targets Arafat as the primary objective.

The newspaper revealed that 100 Israeli commandos, trained in underground camps in the Negev Desert, will be transported by aircraft equipped with radar jamming systems to Tunis to carry out the assassination. Meanwhile, Israeli forces will be deployed within Tunisian territory to monitor PLO activities and track Arafat's movements. The Israeli commandos will simultaneously attack all possible locations where Arafat might be staying.

The newspaper quoted eyewitnesses as saying that over the past four months, these 100 commandos have undergone specialized training from a 30-member urban assault expert team.

Over the past three months, Palestinian security agencies have obtained detailed information about Israel's conspiracy. PLO leaders have held several meetings to discuss measures to counter this plot.

### 阿盟谴责沙龙消灭巴解领袖的叫嚣  科报披露以色列暗杀计划

1989-07-20
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    阿盟谴责沙龙消灭巴解领袖的叫嚣
    科报披露以色列暗杀计划
    新华社突尼斯7月18日电  阿盟秘书长卡利比今天指出，以色列工商贸易部长沙龙消灭巴解领导人的叫嚣是“不能容忍的挑战”。
    在突尼斯举行的一次新闻发布会上，卡利比说，沙龙号召从肉体上消灭阿拉法特和其他巴解组织领导人，表明以色列领导人为了维护他们的侵略和占领政策，不惜采用国家恐怖主义。
    卡利比最后呼吁国际社会，尤其是有影响的大国，对此做出明确和强烈的反应。
    新华社科威特7月19日电 据科威特《政治报》今天披露，以色列制订了一项在今后几个月内暗杀巴勒斯坦国总统阿拉法特和其他巴领导人的阴谋计划。
    按照这项在以色列总理沙米尔监督下制订的暗杀计划，阿拉法特被列为头号暗杀目标。
    这家报纸披露说，100名在内盖夫沙漠地下营地接受训练的以色列突击队员将被装有雷达干扰系统的飞机运往突尼斯执行暗杀任务；与此同时，以色列部队将部署在突尼斯境内以侦察巴解组织的活动情况，从而掌握阿拉法特的行踪。以色列突击队在采取行动时，将对阿拉法特可能居住的所有地方同时发动袭击。
    这家报纸援引目击者的话报道说，在过去4个月里，这100名突击队员已接受了30名城市袭击专家小组的专门训练。
    在过去的3个月内，巴勒斯坦保安机构已获得了以色列这一阴谋计划的详细情况，巴解领导人几次召开会议，讨论对付这一阴谋的措施。
