Title: Hebei Honors a Group of Party Members
Author: Luo Qi
Date: 1989-07-19
Edition: 4

The Communist Party of Hebei Province today honored 49 advanced grassroots party organizations, 103 outstanding Communist Party members, and 92 outstanding party cadres, presenting them with banners and certificates of honor.

The party organizations, outstanding party members, and outstanding party cadres honored this time were selected from among the province's 130,000 grassroots party organizations and 3 million party members.

### 河北表彰一批党员
罗绮
1989-07-19
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    河北表彰一批党员
    新华社石家庄七月十九日电　（记者罗绮）中共河北省委今天表彰了四十九个先进基层党组织、一百零三名优秀共产党员和九十二名优秀党务工作者，并给他们颁发了奖旗和荣誉证书。
    这次表彰的党组织、优秀党员和优秀党务工作者，是从全省十三万个基层党组织和三百万名党员中选拔出来的。
