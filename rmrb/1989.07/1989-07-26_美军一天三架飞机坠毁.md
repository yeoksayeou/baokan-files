Title: U.S. Military Loses Three Aircraft in One Day
Author:
Date: 1989-07-26
Edition: 3

The U.S. military experienced three aircraft accidents on July 24, resulting in 3 deaths and at least 12 injuries. During a refueling exercise at Kelly Air Force Base in Texas, a B-52 bomber caught fire and exploded at 10:35 PM, killing one person. At 5:30 PM, an FA-18 Hornet naval attack aircraft crashed while on a training mission after taking off from North Island Naval Air Station in California, killing two and injuring another who was hospitalized. At 9:00 PM, a naval F-4A fighter jet crashed into the water near San Clemente Island, California.

### 美军一天三架飞机坠毁

1989-07-26
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美军一天三架飞机坠毁
    美军7月24日发生三起飞机事故，造成3人死亡、至少12人受伤。在一次得克萨斯州凯利空军基地的加油演习中，一架B—52型轰炸机于下午10点35分起火爆炸，1人死亡。下午5点30分，一架从加利福尼亚州北岛海军航空站起飞的FA—6B海军攻击机在执行训练任务时坠毁，两人死亡，另一人被送进医院。下午9点，一架海军F—4A战斗机坠入加利福尼亚州圣克利门蒂岛附近的水域。　（据新华社）
