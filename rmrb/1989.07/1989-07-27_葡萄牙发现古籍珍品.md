Title: Portugal Discovers Rare Ancient Books
Author:
Date: 1989-07-27
Edition: 3

Portugal's Ministry of Culture recently discovered several ancient books printed in Lisbon in the 15th century. Experts have described these books as "rare treasures."

According to the *Diário de Notícias*, these ancient books are written in Latin and Hebrew and were printed using movable type. The books, titled *The Hebrew Bible*, *The Book of Eternal Calendar*, and *The Path of Life*, reflect the religious beliefs and daily life of Portugal in the 15th century. *The Book of Eternal Calendar* is similar to the Chinese almanacs published in the past, such as the *Wan Nian Li* (Ten Thousand Year Calendar).

### 葡萄牙发现古籍珍品

1989-07-27
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    葡萄牙发现古籍珍品
    新华社里斯本电　葡萄牙文化部不久前发现了几部15世纪在里斯本出版的古籍。这些古书被专家称为“稀世珍品”。
    据《新闻日报》报道，这些古籍是以拉丁文和希伯来文书就，活字印刷出版。这些书名为《希伯来人圣经》、《永恒历书》和《生命之路》的古书，反映了葡萄牙15世纪时的宗教信仰和日常生活。《永恒历书》颇类似我国过去出版的农历《万年历书》。
