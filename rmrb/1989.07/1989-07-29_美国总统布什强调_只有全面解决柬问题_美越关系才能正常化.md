Title: Bush Emphasizes That Only a Comprehensive Solution to the Cambodian Issue Can Normalize U.S.-Vietnam Relations
Author:
Date: 1989-07-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, July 28th.

U.S. President Bush today stated that only after the Cambodian issue is comprehensively resolved will the U.S. government restore normal relations with Vietnam.

Bush made these remarks at the annual gathering of families of American servicemen missing in action during the Vietnam War. He said, "Once the Cambodian issue is resolved, we will restore relations with Vietnam."

By a comprehensive resolution, Bush means that non-communist Cambodians must genuinely share power, the future government must be led by Prince Sihanouk, and Vietnam must fully withdraw from Cambodia under international supervision.

Bush also linked the normalization of U.S.-Vietnam relations to Vietnam's cooperation in locating American servicemen missing in action.

### 美国总统布什强调  只有全面解决柬问题  美越关系才能正常化

1989-07-29
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美国总统布什强调
    只有全面解决柬问题
    美越关系才能正常化
    新华社华盛顿7月28日电  美国总统布什今天说，只有柬埔寨问题得到全面解决后，美国政府才会恢复同越南的正常关系。
    布什在一年一度的美军在越战中失踪人员家属团聚会上说，“一旦柬埔寨问题得到解决，我们就同越南恢复关系。”
    布什所说的全面解决包括非共产党柬埔寨人真正分享权力、同未来政权由西哈努克亲王领导以及越南从柬埔寨全面撤军经过国际检查。
    布什还把美越关系正常化，同越南在寻找美军失踪人员上的合作态度相联系。
