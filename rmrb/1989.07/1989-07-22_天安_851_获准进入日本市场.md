Title: "Tian'an 851" Approved to Enter the Japanese Market
Author: Gǔyù
Date: 1989-07-22
Page: 2

Column: From All Sides

    "Tian'an 851" Approved to Enter the Japanese Market

    Our reporter learned that on July 13th, the Beijing Tian'an High-Efficiency Technology Research Institute's Beijing Tian'an 851 Super Nutrition Liquid passed the inspection and testing by Japan's Ministry of Health and Welfare for food hygiene and drug regulations, and was officially approved by the Ministry of Health and Welfare to enter the Japanese market.

    Beijing Tian'an 851 Super Nutrition Liquid is a nutritional beverage produced using advanced biotechnology. Scientific experiments over the past two years have proven that this beverage has a strong effect in enhancing human immune function.

### “天安851”获准进入日本市场
古玉
1989-07-22
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    “天安851”获准进入日本市场
    本报讯　7月13日，北京天安高效益技术研究所生产的北京天安851超级营养液，在日本通过了厚生省的食品卫生法和药品法的检查化验，并被厚生省正式批准进入日本市场。
    北京天安851超级营养液，是运用先进的生物工程技术生产的一种营养型饮料。近两年来的科学试验证明，该饮料具有较强的增强人体免疫功能的作用。（古玉）
