Title: Global Tourism Industry Annual Sales Reach $2 Trillion
Author:
Date: 1989-07-07
Page: 7

The global tourism industry generates annual sales of $2 trillion. The tourism industry encompasses rail, automobile transportation, hotels, restaurants, theaters, and museums.

If the tourism industry were considered a country, it would rank as the fifth-largest economic power, following the United States, the Soviet Union, Japan, and the Federal Republic of Germany.

### 世界旅游业年销售额2万亿美元

1989-07-07
第7版(国际专页)
专栏：

    世界旅游业年销售额2万亿美元
    世界旅游业的销售额为每年2万亿美元，旅游业包括铁路、汽车运输、饭店、餐厅、剧场和博物馆等。
    如果把旅游业比作一个国家，那么，它是继美国、苏联、日本和联邦德国之后的第五大经济力量。
