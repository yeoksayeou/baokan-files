Title: Japan Develops New Technology for Nuclear Waste Treatment
Author:
Date: 1989-07-22
Edition: 3

Japan's Nuclear Fuel Development Corporation recently developed a technology that encapsulates nuclear waste liquid in a glass structure, and is proceeding with the construction of experimental facilities.

According to the *Nihon Keizai Shimbun*, this glass encapsulation technology involves melting the waste liquid together with glass and then encapsulating the waste liquid within the glass structure. This process utilizes the property of glass, which, when subjected to 100 kilowatts of electrical power, heats up and completely melts on its own.

### 日研究核废料处理新技术

1989-07-22
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    日研究核废料处理新技术
    据新华社东京电　日本核动力堆核燃料事业团最近研究出一种将核废液封闭在玻璃结构里的玻璃固化技术，并着手建设实验设施。
    据《日刊工业新闻》报道，这项玻璃固化技术是先将废液同玻璃一起熔化，然后把废液封闭在玻璃结构里。这是利用了给熔化的玻璃通上100千瓦的电力，玻璃本身就发热并完全熔化的性质。
