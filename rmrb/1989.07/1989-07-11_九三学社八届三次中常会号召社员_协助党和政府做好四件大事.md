Title: The Third Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Jiusan Society Calls on Members to Assist the Party and Government in Four Major Tasks
Author:
Date: 1989-07-11
Edition: 2

The third (enlarged) meeting of the Eighth Central Committee of the Jiusan Society, which concluded today, called on all levels of the Jiusan Society's organizations and all members to seriously study the spirit of the Fourth Plenum of the 13th Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, to align their thoughts with the resolutions of the Fourth Plenum and the speech by Comrade Deng Xiaoping, to adhere to the Four Cardinal Principles, to persist in reform and opening up, to strengthen self-building, to unite as one, to boost morale, and to work hard.

The meeting emphasized that at present, all levels of the Jiusan Society's organizations must pay special attention to assisting the Communist Party and the government in carrying out the four major tasks proposed by the Fourth Plenum of the 13th Central Committee. In further stabilizing the situation, promoting rectification and reform and opening up, opposing bourgeois liberalization, and strengthening democratic legal construction, they should play an active role.

The meeting adopted the "Resolution of the Central Committee of the Jiusan Society on Studying and Implementing the Spirit of the Fourth Plenum of the 13th Central Committee of the Communist Party of China." The meeting was presided over by Sun Chengpei, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Jiusan Society. Vice Chairmen Xu Caidi, Hao Yipin, Wang Wenyuan, Yang Ye, Chen Mingshao, and Chen Xuejun, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Wang Ganchang, and Vice Chairmen Qiao Yijin, Chen Enfeng, Qi Gong, Xie Lihui, and Li Yi attended the meeting.

### 九三学社八届三次中常会号召社员  协助党和政府做好四件大事

1989-07-11
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    九三学社八届三次中常会号召社员
    协助党和政府做好四件大事
    据新华社北京7月11日电　今天闭幕的九三学社第八届中央常务委员会第三次（扩大）会议要求，九三学社各级组织和全体社员认真学习中共十三届四中全会精神，把思想统一到四中全会决议和邓小平同志讲话上来，坚持四项基本原则，坚持改革开放，加强自身建设，团结一致，振奋精神，努力工作。
    会议强调，当前，九三学社的各级组织特别要注意协助中共和政府做好十三届四中全会提出的4件大事，在进一步稳定局势，推进治理整顿和改革开放，反对资产阶级自由化和加强民主法制建设中，发挥积极作用。
    会议通过了《九三学社中央关于学习贯彻中共十三届四中全会精神的决议》。九三学社中央常务副主席孙承佩主持了会议，副主席徐采栋、郝诒纯、王文元、杨槱、陈明绍、陈学俊，参议委员会主任王淦昌、副主任笪移今、陈恩凤、启功、谢立惠、李毅等参加了会议。
