Title: Mudanjiang and a Soviet City Launch "Eight-Day Tour"
Author: Wang Zengwei  Tian Zhonghai
Date: 1989-07-01
Edition: 4

Mudanjiang and a Soviet City Launch "Eight-Day Tour"

	The People's Daily reports: The city of Mudanjiang in Heilongjiang Province and the Soviet city of Ussuriysk have launched an "Eight-Day Tour" activity. The first Soviet tour group arrived in Mudanjiang on June 15.

	Mudanjiang will send its first tour group to the Soviet Union on July 15 to visit Vladivostok, Ussuriysk, and other places.

### 牡丹江与苏联一市开办“八日游”
王增伟　田忠海
1989-07-01
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    牡丹江与苏联一市开办“八日游”
    本报讯　黑龙江牡丹江市与苏联乌苏里斯克市开办“八日游”活动。苏方第一个旅游团6月15日到达牡丹江市。
    牡丹江市将于7月15日派出第一个旅游团赴苏，参观海参崴、乌苏里斯克等地。
    （王增伟　田忠海）
