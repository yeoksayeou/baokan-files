Title: Tumor Diagnostic Device Debuts in Shenyang
Author: Yang Liansheng, Jiang Min
Date: 1989-07-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, July 9th

A "SAP Tumor Computer Qualitative Diagnostic Device" that integrates traditional Chinese medicine with modern electronic technology recently passed an appraisal in Shenyang.

According to experts, this diagnostic device is a domestic innovation. The device can detect information about tumors growing inside the human body or cells undergoing malignant transformation, thereby determining the nature of the tumor. It can also detect tumors in their early stages.

### 肿瘤诊断仪在沈阳问世
杨连生  姜敏
1989-07-09
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    肿瘤诊断仪在沈阳问世
    新华社沈阳7月9日电（通讯员杨连生、记者姜敏）一种集祖国医药学理论与现代电子技术于一体的“SAP肿瘤电脑定性诊断仪”最近在沈阳通过鉴定。
    据专家介绍，这种诊断仪属国内首创。这种仪器可将人体内生长肿瘤或发生癌变的信息进行检测，以判断肿瘤性质，还可早期发现肿瘤。
