Title: Sudanese Prime Minister Says Future of South Determined by All Parties
Author:
Date: 1989-07-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 13th.

Sudanese Prime Minister Ahmad al-Bashir recently stated that if the residents of southern Sudan decide to secede from the country, he will not oppose it.

Bashir made these remarks during an interview with a reporter from the United Arab Emirates' "Gulf News." The Middle East News Agency reported on the 13th that Bashir said that the various factions in southern Sudan should consult and decide their own future. The government will accept whatever decision they make, even if they decide to secede from the country. If the southern factions decide to establish a federation with the government, the government will also not oppose it.

### 苏丹总理表示  南部前途由各派决定

1989-07-14
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏丹总理表示
    南部前途由各派决定
    新华社开罗7月13日电  苏丹新总理艾哈迈德·巴希尔最近表示，如果苏丹南部的居民决定脱离这个国家的话，他将不予反对。
    巴希尔是在接受阿拉伯联合酋长国《海湾日报》记者采访时说这番话的。中东社13日援引巴希尔的话报道说，苏丹南部各派应当协商决定自己的前途。无论他们做出什么样的决定，哪怕是他们决定同国家脱离，政府都将接受。如果南方各派决定同政府建立联邦的话，政府也不反对。
