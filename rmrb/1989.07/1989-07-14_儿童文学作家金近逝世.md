Title: Renowned Children's Literature Writer Jin Jin Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-07-14
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 13th.

Renowned children's literature writer Jin Jin passed away on July 9th in Beijing. His beautifully crafted fairy tales, such as "Little Carp Leaps Over the Dragon Gate" and "The Fox Hunts the Hunter," will forever bring joy to readers.

Jin Jin, who was 73 years old, began his career in children's literature in the 1930s. Before his passing, he served as a member of the Chinese Writers Association and an advisor to the magazine "Children's Literature."

### 儿童文学作家金近逝世

1989-07-14
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    儿童文学作家金近逝世
    新华社北京七月十三日电　深受小朋友喜爱和尊敬的著名儿童文学作家金近于七月九日在北京病逝，然而他创作的《小鲤鱼跳龙门》、《狐狸打猎人》等许多美丽的童话故事却永远给人们以欢乐。
    终年七十三岁的金近早在三十年代就开始从事儿童文学创作，他生前曾任中国作协理事、《儿童文学》杂志顾问等职务。
