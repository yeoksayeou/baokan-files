Title: Oil Production and Construction Momentum is Strong
Author: Zhang Heping
Date: 1989-07-14
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 14th.

The momentum of oil production and construction is strong, with production in the first half of the year exceeding that of the same period last year. According to China National Petroleum Corporation, despite various disruptions and challenges, oil production and construction have continued to develop in the first half of this year. By the end of June, the country had produced a cumulative total of 67.538 million tons of crude oil, completing 48.59% of the annual national plan and increasing by 41.8 million tons compared to the same period last year. Additionally, 7.2 billion cubic meters of natural gas were produced, completing 51.4% of the annual plan.

Among the 18 onshore oil and gas fields nationwide, 14 fields, including Daqing, Liahe, Xinjiang, Huabei, and Jilin, have completed or are close to completing their assigned crude oil production plans. Notably, Daqing Oilfield has maintained stable oil production, consistently exceeding the monthly plan. In the first half of the year, it overproduced by 1.1 million tons.

The volume of oil and gas construction has significantly increased compared to the same period last year. According to statistics, the scale of crude oil production capacity construction nationwide that has started is 16.66 million tons, accounting for 93.5% of the annual plan. Additionally, over 2,700 new wells have been constructed and put into production, an increase of over 500 wells compared to the same period last year.

### 石油生产建设势头好  上半年产量超过去年同期
张何平
1989-07-14
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    石油生产建设势头好
    上半年产量超过去年同期
    本报北京7月14日讯  记者张何平报道：从中国石油天然气总公司获悉，今年上半年石油生产建设在排除干扰、克服困难中继续取得新的发展。至6月底，全国累计生产原油6753.8万吨，完成全年国家计划的48.59％，比去年同期增加41.8吨；生产天然气72亿立方米，完成全年计划的51.4％。
    全国陆上18个油气田中，大庆、辽河、新疆、华北、吉林等14个油气田完成和接近完成安排的原油生产计划。其中，大庆油田原油生产一直保持稳定，月月超额完成计划，上半年超产11万吨。
    油气建设工作量比去年同期有较大幅度增长。据统计，全国原油产能建设已开工规模为1666万吨，占全年计划93.5％；建设投产新井2700多口，比去年同期增加500多口。
