Title: Minister Qi Yuanjing to Visit Argentina
Author:
Date: 1989-06-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 29th.

The spokesperson for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs announced today that at the invitation of the Argentine government, Special Envoy of the Chinese government and Minister of the Metallurgical Industry, Qi Yuanjing, will attend the inauguration ceremony of the elected President of Argentina, Carlos Menem, on July 8th.

### 戚元靖部长将出访阿根廷

1989-06-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    戚元靖部长将出访阿根廷
    新华社北京6月29日电　外交部发言人今天宣布，应阿根廷政府的邀请，中国政府特使、冶金工业部部长戚元靖将出席7月8日举行的阿根廷当选总统梅内姆的就职典礼。
