Title: Commemorating August 1st, Reminiscing Military Life
Author: Guo Jia Ming Wang
Date: 1989-07-20
Edition: 1

Today, the old generation of proletarian revolutionaries in our military and several hundred old Red Army soldiers and old Eighth Route Army soldiers gathered in Beijing to reminisce about the "glorious years of military life."

To commemorate the 62nd anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Beijing Television and the Chinese People's Liberation Army Veterans' Association jointly organized a song concert beloved by old soldiers. The event deeply commemorated the 62-year glorious journey of the people's children soldiers under the leadership of the Communist Party of China, enthusiastically sang the praises of the great achievements of the old generation of revolutionaries in the military for the founding of the Republic, and the immortal merits they established for building and defending the motherland.

Attending today's cultural tea party were Yang Dezhi and Geng Biao, members of the Central Advisory Commission, and leaders from the Central Military Commission and the three general departments of the PLA, including He Htian and Zhao Nanqi.

### 纪念八一节追怀军旅情  数百老战士在京出席文艺茶会
郭嘉　明旺
1989-07-20
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    纪念八一节追怀军旅情
    数百老战士在京出席文艺茶会
    本报北京7月20日讯　今天，我军老一辈无产阶级革命家和几百名老红军、老八路畅聚北京，共同追怀“峥嵘岁月军旅情”。
    为纪念中国人民解放军建军62周年，由北京电视台和中国人民解放军老干部俱乐部联合举办老战士喜爱的歌曲演唱会，深切缅怀人民子弟兵在中国共产党的领导下走过的62年光辉历程，热情讴歌了我军老一辈革命家为创建共和国立下的丰功伟绩，为建设和保卫祖国，创立的不朽功勋。
    出席今天文艺茶会的有中顾委常委杨得志、耿飚及中央军委、解放军三总部负责人迟浩田、赵南起等。
          （郭嘉　明旺）
