Title: France and Italy Test Anti-Submarine Missile
Author:
Date: 1989-07-19
Edition: 3

France's Matra company announced on the 18th in Paris that the company, along with Italy's Otomelara company, successfully conducted tests on two "MILAS" anti-submarine missiles.

According to Matra, the "MILAS" missile is 6 meters long, weighs 800 kilograms, and has a design range of over 50 kilometers.

### 法意试验反潜导弹

1989-07-19
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    法意试验反潜导弹
    法国的马特拉公司18日在巴黎宣布，该公司与意大利的奥托梅拉拉公司成功地进行了两枚“米拉斯”反潜导弹试验。
    据马特拉公司说，“米拉斯”导弹长6米，重800公斤，设计射程在50公里以上。
            （据新华社）
