Title: Vice Premier Yao Yilin Meets Malaysian Minister of Trade and Industry
Author:
Date: 1989-07-25
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 25th.

Vice Premier Yao Yilin met with Malaysian Minister of Trade and Industry Rafidah Aziz today. He expressed satisfaction with the rapid development of Sino-Malaysian trade, stating that there is still considerable room for growth in bilateral trade cooperation. He noted that the two countries are not only close neighbors but also enjoy very friendly relations.

Yao Yilin congratulated Malaysia on its rapid economic development, saying that China is always pleased to see the progress of its neighboring friends.

Rafidah Aziz mentioned that the Malaysian business community generally believes that there is great potential in developing trade with China.

During the meeting, both sides agreed that direct trade between the two countries, reducing intermediaries, would be beneficial for both parties.

Yao Yilin assured the guest that China will not change its policy of reform and opening up. He emphasized that although the two countries have different political and social systems, this does not affect their ability to be good friends and good neighbors.

### 姚依林会见马来西亚贸工部长

1989-07-25
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    姚依林会见马来西亚贸工部长
    新华社北京7月25日电  国务院副总理姚依林今天在这里会见马来西亚贸易和工业部部长拉菲达时，对迅速发展的中马贸易表示满意。他说，两国的贸易合作仍有相当大的发展前途，因为两国既是近邻，又有着非常友好的关系。
    姚依林对马来西亚经济的迅速发展表示高兴。他说，中国对邻国朋友的发展总是感到高兴的。
    拉菲达说，马来西亚商业界普遍认为，发展马中贸易有很大的潜力。
    会见中，宾主双方都表示，两国发展直接贸易，减少中间转手对双方都是有利的。
    姚依林对客人说，中国不会改变改革开放政策。中马两国政治、社会制度不同，但这不影响两国成为好朋友、好邻居。
