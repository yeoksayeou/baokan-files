Title: China's TV Exports to the U.S. Surge
Author:
Date: 1989-07-29
Page: 2

People's Daily, Beijing, July 29th.

China's TV Exports to the U.S. Surge

In the first two months of this year, the number of televisions exported from China to the United States has surged, ranking fifth among all countries and regions exporting televisions to the U.S.

According to statistics released by the U.S. Department of Commerce, from January to February this year, the U.S. imported 98,500 televisions from China. Among these, 49,900 were color televisions and 48,600 were black-and-white televisions, almost equal in number. Mexico had the largest export volume of televisions to the U.S., followed by South Korea, with China ranking fifth. During the same period, Japan exported 71,300 televisions to the U.S., including 60,500 color televisions and 10,800 black-and-white televisions.

### 我电视机销美量激增  今年头两月总销量近十万台名列第五

1989-07-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    我电视机销美量激增
    今年头两月总销量近十万台名列第五
    本报讯  今年头两个月，我国销往美国的电视机数量激增，在各对美出口电视机的国家和地区中居第五位。
    据美国商务部发表的进口统计资料表明，今年1至2月美国从我国进口电视机达98500台，其中彩色电视机为49900台，黑白电视机为48600台，几乎各占一半。对美电视机出口量最大的是墨西哥，其次是南朝鲜，我国位居第五。在同期内，日本电视机对美国出口量为71300台，其中彩色电视机60500台，黑白电视机10800台。
