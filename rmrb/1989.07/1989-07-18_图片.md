Title: Lomé Port
Author: Qin Dianjie
Date: 1989-07-18
Edition: 7

Lomé Port is Togo's largest port, featuring docks for cargo, fishing, and oil. It has 9 berths, with over 1,000 ships entering and leaving the port annually, handling more than 200 million tons of cargo.
