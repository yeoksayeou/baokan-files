Title: Zimbabwe and Kenya to Mediate Mozambique's Civil War
Author:
Date: 1989-07-30
Edition: 3

Zimbabwean President Robert Mugabe said on July 28 in a press conference held in Harare that he and Kenyan President Daniel arap Moi will soon arrange for negotiations between the Mozambican government and the anti-government forces to end the 14-year-long civil war in that country.

### 津肯两国将调解莫内战

1989-07-30
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    津肯两国将调解莫内战
    津巴布韦总统穆加贝28日在哈拉雷举行的记者招待会上说，他和肯尼亚总统莫伊不久将安排莫桑比克政府和反政府武装进行谈判，以结束该国长达14年的内战。
