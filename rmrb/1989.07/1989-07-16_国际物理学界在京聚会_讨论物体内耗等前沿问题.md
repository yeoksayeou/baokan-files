Title: International Physicists Gather in Beijing to Discuss Frontiers in Internal Friction and Ultrasonic Attenuation
Author: Bāo Ānjùn
Date: 1989-07-16
Edition: 2

More than 130 physicists from China and abroad gathered today at the elegant Beijing Fragrant Hills Hotel to discuss scientific frontiers such as internal friction in solids, aiming to discover new special functional materials.

The "Ninth International Conference on Internal Friction and Ultrasonic Attenuation in Solids," organized by the Chinese Academy of Sciences, is a regular academic conference held every four years by the international physics community. Experts and scholars from the Soviet Union, Poland, Britain, Austria, West Germany, Japan, Hungary, Egypt, and China will exchange the latest findings in this scientific field. The conference received 223 scientific papers, of which 73 were submitted by China.

The conference will also award medals to two scientists who have made outstanding contributions in this field. One is Professor Ge Tingrui, a member of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and the other is Professor Nowick from Columbia University in the United States.

### 国际物理学界在京聚会  讨论物体内耗等前沿问题
包安隽
1989-07-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    国际物理学界在京聚会
    讨论物体内耗等前沿问题
    新华社北京7月16日电　（实习生包安隽）130多位中外物理学家今天会聚在环境优雅的北京香山饭店，讨论物体内耗等科学前沿问题，以期发现新的特殊功能材料。
    由中国科学院主办的“第九届国际固体内耗与超声衰减学术会议”是国际物理学界每四年举行一次的常规学术会议。来自苏联、波兰、英国、奥地利、联邦德国、日本、匈牙利、埃及和中国的专家学者将交流有关这一科学领域的最新成果。会议提交科学论文223篇，其中我国提出了73篇。
    这次会议还将颁发优秀奖章给这个领域做出突出贡献的两位科学家。一位是中国科学院学部委员葛庭燧教授，另一位是美国哥伦比亚大学诺威克教授。
