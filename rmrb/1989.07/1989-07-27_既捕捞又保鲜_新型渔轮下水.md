Title: Both Fishing and Fresh-Keeping: New Type of Fishing Vessel Launched
Author: Wang Xiansheng
Date: 1989-07-27
Page: 2

Column: News from All Directions

The first small steel fishing vessel in China that combines fishing and fresh-keeping has been built on the island of Zhāngzǐ Island and passed a municipal-level appraisal yesterday.

This vessel was constructed by the Changhai County Zhāngzǐ Township Shipyard in Dalian, with a power of 136 kilowatts. Experts believe that this model has high practical value and fills a technological gap in China.

### 既捕捞又保鲜  新型渔轮下水
王贤生
1989-07-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    既捕捞又保鲜  新型渔轮下水
    本报大连7月27日电  我国第一艘集捕捞和保鲜为一体的小型钢质渔轮，在獐子岛建成并于昨天通过市级鉴定。
    这艘渔轮是由大连长海县獐子乡修造船厂建造的，功率为136千瓦。专家们认为，该型号船具有很高的实用价值，填补了我国此项技术空白。　（王贤生）
