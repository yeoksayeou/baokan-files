Title: Various Circles in Xinjiang Celebrate Eid al-Adha
Author: Wāng Yǒufù
Date: 1989-07-13
Edition: 2

Various circles in the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region held a grand gala evening yesterday to celebrate the traditional Muslim festival of Eid al-Adha. Nearly a thousand Chinese and foreign guests attended the gala.

Wang Enmao, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and Si Ma Yi Ai Mai Te, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Minister of the State Commission for Nationalities, attended the gala.

The delegation from the Kazakh Soviet Socialist Republic, led by Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Bayklov, and the delegation from the Soviet Civil Aviation First Flight, led by Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Asanbayev, who are visiting Xinjiang, as well as experts from Japan, Switzerland, the Soviet Union, the United States, and other countries working in the postal, textile, civil aviation, and petroleum sectors in Xinjiang, were also invited to attend the gala.

### 新疆各界庆祝“古尔邦节”
汪有富
1989-07-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    新疆各界庆祝“古尔邦节”
    据新华社乌鲁木齐7月13日电　（记者汪有富）新疆维吾尔自治区党委、自治区人民政府和自治区民委昨晚举行盛大联欢晚会，庆祝穆斯林的传统节日——古尔邦节。近千名中外人士出席了联欢会。
    全国政协副主席王恩茂，全国政协副主席、国家民委主任司马义·艾买提出席了联欢会。正在新疆访问的以苏联哈萨克加盟共和国部长会议副主席巴依克洛夫为团长的苏联哈萨克加盟共和国代表团和以苏联哈萨克加盟共和国部长会议副主席阿桑巴耶夫为团长的苏联民航首航代表团全体成员，以及在新疆邮电、纺织、民航、石油等部门帮助工作的日本、瑞士、苏联、美国等国家的专家代表，也应邀参加了联欢会。
