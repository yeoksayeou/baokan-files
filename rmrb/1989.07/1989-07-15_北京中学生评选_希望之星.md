Title: Beijing Students Select "Stars of Hope"
Author: Mèng Xiǎoyún
Date: 1989-07-15
Page: 2

Transmitted by: Four Corners

    The second "Stars of Hope" selection activity for Beijing middle school students has been concluded, and the award ceremony was held today in Beijing.

    This selection activity conveyed several messages: First, middle school students are beginning to pay attention to the comprehensive improvement of various aspects of their personal development, placing great importance on the question of how to be a good person. Second, students' awareness of facing society and participating in society has been strengthened, and they hope to be able to do some beneficial work for society during their student years. Third, practical skills and literary arts are highly favored by middle school students.

### 北京中学生评选“希望之星”
孟晓云
1989-07-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    北京中学生评选“希望之星”
    本报北京7月15日讯  记者孟晓云报道：北京市中学生第二届“希望之星”评选活动已揭晓，于今天在此间举行颁奖仪式。
    本届评选活动传递了以下几个信息：一、中学生开始注意各方面素质的全面提高，把如何做人的问题放到了重要位置；二、中学生面向社会走向社会的意识增强了，并希望在学生时代就能为社会做些有益的工作；三、实用技术与文学艺术备受中学生宠爱。
