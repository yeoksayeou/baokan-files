Title: The Majestic View of the Longyangxia Dam
Author: Wang Jingren
Date: 1989-07-10
Page: 1

The Longyangxia Hydropower Station reservoir currently holds over 10 billion cubic meters of water, with a water surface area of 155 square kilometers, making it the largest artificial lake in China. The station's concrete arch-gravity dam withstood the second-largest flood on the upper reaches of the Yellow River

### 龙羊峡大坝雄姿（图片）
王景仁
1989-07-10
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    龙羊峡大坝雄姿
    龙羊峡水电站水库目前蓄水已达一百多亿立方米，库区水面达一百五十五平方公里，是我国目前最大人工湖。电站的混凝土重力拱坝，六月下旬经受住了黄河上游出现的建国以来第二次大洪峰的考验。图为大坝雄姿。本报记者　王景仁摄
