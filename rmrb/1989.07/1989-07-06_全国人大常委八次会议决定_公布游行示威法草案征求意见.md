Title: The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Decides to Publish the Draft Law on Assemblies, Processions, and Demonstrations for Public Comment
Author:
Date: 1989-07-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 6th.

The Eighth Session of the Standing Committee of the Seventh National People's Congress decided today to publish the draft Law of the People's Republic of China on Assemblies, Processions, and Demonstrations, submitted by the State Council for deliberation, to solicit opinions from all sectors of society, conduct research and revisions, and then submit it to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for deliberation.

The General Office of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress issued a notice today (no separate document).

The notice requires that the Standing Committees of the People's Congresses of provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the Central Government solicit and collect opinions from relevant departments, people's organizations, grassroots units, and people from all walks of life in their respective regions, and summarize and report them to the Legislative Affairs Commission of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress by August 10, 1989.

The notice states that the Law Committee of the National People's Congress, the Internal and Judicial Affairs Committee, and the Legislative Affairs Commission of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress will respectively hold seminars to solicit opinions from relevant central departments, people's organizations, democratic parties, and legal experts.

The notice hopes that people from all walks of life will send their opinions to the Standing Committees of the People's Congresses of provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the Central Government for summary and reporting, or directly to the Legislative Affairs Commission of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.

### 全国人大常委八次会议决定  公布游行示威法草案征求意见

1989-07-06
第4版(国际新闻·国内新闻)
专栏：

    全国人大常委八次会议决定
    公布游行示威法草案征求意见
    新华社北京7月6日电　七届全国人大常委会第八次会议今天决定，公布国务院提请审议的中华人民共和国集会游行示威法草案，广泛征求各界意见，研究修改，再提请全国人大常委会会议审议。
    全国人大常委会办公厅今天为此发出通知（不另行文）。
    通知要求各省、自治区、直辖市人大常委会征求、收集本地区有关部门、人民团体、基层单位和各界人士、人民群众的意见，于1989年8月10日以前，将意见汇总报送全国人大常委会法制工作委员会。
    通知说，由全国人大法律委员会、内务司法委员会和全国人大常委会法制工作委员会分别召开座谈会，征求中央有关部门、人民团体、民主党派和法律专家的意见。
    通知希望各界人士、人民群众将意见寄送省、自治区、直辖市人大常委会汇总上报，也可以寄送全国人大常委会法制工作委员会。
