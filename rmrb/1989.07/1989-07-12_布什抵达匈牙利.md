Title: Bush Arrives in Hungary
Author:
Date: 1989-07-12
Edition: 3

    Bush Arrives in Hungary

    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, July 11 - U.S. President George H.W. Bush and his wife arrived in Budapest at 7 p.m. tonight, beginning their official visit to Hungary.

    Chairman of the Hungarian People's Republic's Presidential Council, Károly Stróbl, and his wife welcomed them at the airport.

    Bush had previously visited Hungary in September 1983 as Vice President. Through this visit, he became the first serving U.S. President to visit Hungary in American history.

### 布什抵达匈牙利

1989-07-12
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    布什抵达匈牙利
    新华社布达佩斯7月11日电　美国总统布什偕夫人一行今晚7时抵达布达佩斯，开始对匈牙利进行正式访问。
    匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席斯特劳乌布及夫人到机场迎接。
    布什曾于1983年9月以副总统身份访问过匈牙利。通过这次访问，他成为美国历史上第一位访问匈牙利的美国在职总统。
