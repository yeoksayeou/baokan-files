Title: Chinese Foreign Minister to Lead Delegation to Paris for International Conference on Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1989-07-13
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 13th.

Foreign Minister Qian Qichen will lead a Chinese government delegation to attend an international conference on the Cambodian issue to be held in Paris. The spokesperson said today, "We support the convening of this conference and will participate in the discussions and formulate comprehensive, fair, and reasonable solutions to the Cambodian issue." She said, "China hopes the conference will make positive progress."

### 钱外长将率团赴巴黎出席柬问题国际会议

1989-07-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    钱外长将率团赴巴黎出席柬问题国际会议
    新华社北京7月13日电　外交部发言人今天说，外交部长钱其琛将率领中国政府代表团出席在巴黎召开的关于柬埔寨问题的国际会议。这位发言人说：“我们赞成召开这次会议，并将在会上参加讨论和制定全面、公正、合理地解决柬埔寨问题的办法。”她说，中国希望会议取得积极进展。
