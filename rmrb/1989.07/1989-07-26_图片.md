Title: A Visit to the Dock on West Lake in Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1989-07-26
Edition: 8

A yellow banner with the words "阮墩环碧" and a string of red lanterns made from oiled paper hang at a dock on West Lake in Hangzhou. These decorations are both eye-catching and rich in folk cultural elements, blending harmoniously with the simple environment of the island. However, the "大虫" (dragon) in a crawling posture on the base ruins the overall ambiance. Why add this unnecessary element!
