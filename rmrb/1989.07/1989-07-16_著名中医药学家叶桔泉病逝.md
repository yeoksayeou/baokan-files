Title: Renowned Traditional Chinese Medicine Scholar Ye Juquan Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-07-16
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, July 15th.

A memorial service for Ye Juquan, a renowned scholar of traditional Chinese medicine, was held today in Nanjing. Ye Juquan, a member of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Vice Chairman of the Consultative and Supervisory Committee of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League, Academician of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and an outstanding traditional Chinese medicine scholar, passed away on July 7, 1989, at the age of 93, due to illness.

More than 400 people from various sectors in Jiangsu Province and friends of Ye Juquan attended the memorial service with heavy hearts.

Wreaths were sent by Wan Li, Li Xiannian, Peng Chong, Zhou Guocheng, Lei Jieqiong, Yan Mingfu, Lu Jiashi, Ding Guangxun, the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the United Front Work Department of the Central Committee, the Central Committee of the China Democratic League, the Chinese Academy of Sciences, the Ministry of Health, the State Administration of Traditional Chinese Medicine, the Jiangsu Provincial Committee, the Provincial Government, prominent figures in the medical and pharmaceutical fields, higher medical and pharmaceutical institutions, and the Huzhou Municipal Committee, Ye Juquan's hometown in Zhejiang Province.

Ye Juquan, known as a master of traditional Chinese medicine in China, was born in August 1896 in Wu County, Zhejiang Province, into a peasant family. In his youth, he studied under famous doctors to learn traditional Chinese medicine and sought innovation.

After the founding of New China, he devoted himself to the research and clinical work of traditional Chinese medicine with great enthusiasm. Since 1955, he has successively served as an Academician of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Director of the Jiangsu Institute of Traditional Chinese Medicine, Vice President of Nanjing College of Traditional Chinese Medicine, Member of the Blood Flukes Disease Committee of the Medical Science Committee of the Ministry of Health, and Vice President of Nanjing College of Pharmacy. While engaged in the research of traditional Chinese medicine, he personally treated various difficult and miscellaneous diseases for the people, earning great respect and love.

In his more than 70-year career in medicine, Ye Juquan published 27 medical works totaling 35 volumes and wrote hundreds of scientific papers and popular science articles, amounting to about 10 million words. Even at the age of over 90, he continued to write and selflessly dedicated his entire knowledge and lifelong efforts to the cause of traditional Chinese medicine in China.

Ye Juquan served as Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League, a member of the Third and Fourth National Committees of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and a Standing Committee member of the Fifth, Sixth, and Seventh National Committees of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

### 著名中医药学家叶桔泉病逝

1989-07-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    著名中医药学家叶桔泉病逝
    新华社南京7月15日电　全国政协常委、农工民主党中央咨询监察委员会副主席、中科院学部委员、杰出的中医药学家叶桔泉同志追悼会今天在南京举行。
    叶桔泉同志因病医治无效，于1989年7月7日上午11时10分在南京逝世，享年93岁。
    江苏省各界人士及叶桔泉生前友好400多人怀着沉痛的心情参加了追悼活动。
    万里、李先念、彭冲、周谷城、雷洁琼、阎明复、卢嘉锡、丁光训送了花圈。全国政协、中央统战部、农工民主党中央委员会、中国科学院、卫生部、国家医药管理局、国家中医药管理局，江苏省委、省政府、医药界知名人士、高等医药院校和叶桔泉家乡浙江湖州市委等，也送了花圈。
    被誉为我国中医药界一代宗师的叶桔泉，1896年8月出生于浙江省吴兴县一个农民家庭，青年时代即随名医研修中医，力求创新。
    新中国建立后，他以极大的热情积极投入医药学的研究和临床工作。从1955年起，他先后担任中国科学院学部委员、江苏省中医研究所所长、南京中医学院副院长、卫生部医学科学委员会血吸虫病专题委员会委员、南京药学院副院长等职务。他在从事中医药研究的同时，亲自为群众诊治各种疑难杂病，深受人们尊敬和爱戴。
    叶桔泉在70多年的医药生涯中，先后出版医药著作27种，共35册，发表科学论文和科普文章数百篇，约1000万字。他在90多岁高龄还坚持著书立说，无私地把自己的全部知识和毕生精力奉献给祖国医药学事业。
    叶桔泉同志曾任农工民主党中央副主席，第三、四届全国政协委员，第五、六、七届全国政协常务委员。
