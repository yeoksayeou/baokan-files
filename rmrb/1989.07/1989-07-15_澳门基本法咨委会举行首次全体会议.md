Title: The First Plenary Meeting of the Basic Law Consultative Committee of Macao
Author:
Date: 1989-07-15
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Macao, July 15th

The first plenary meeting of the Basic Law Consultative Committee of the Macao Special Administrative Region was held on the evening of July 14th at the Macao Exhibition Center.

The chairman of the Basic Law Consultative Committee, Cui Deqi, presided over the meeting and reported on the committee's recent work. He said, “To ensure the implementation of ‘one country, two systems,’ to guarantee that Macao remains prosperous and stable during the transitional period and after the handover of power, and to ensure that the future Macao Special Administrative Region can truly maintain the current social and economic systems and way of life, we must not relax our efforts in drafting and consulting the Basic Law.”

The meeting discussed and adopted the "Procedures for the Plenary Meetings of the Basic Law Consultative Committee of the Macao Special Administrative Region" and decided to establish a special consultative subcommittee on the structure of the Basic Law.

### 澳门基本法咨委会举行首次全体会议

1989-07-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    澳门基本法咨委会举行首次全体会议
    新华社澳门7月15日电  澳门特别行政区基本法咨询委员会第一次全体会议14日晚上在澳门展览中心举行。
    基本法咨委会主任委员崔德祺主持会议并报告了咨委会近期的工作。他说，“为了维护‘一国两制’的贯彻实施，为了确保澳门在过渡时期和政权移交之后都能继续繁荣安定，确保未来的澳门特别行政区能够真正地保持现行的社会经济制度不变、生活方式不变，当前不应放松基本法的起草与咨询工作。”
    会议讨论通过了《澳门特别行政区基本法咨询委员会全体会议细则》，并决定成立基本法结构专责咨询小组。
