Title: Increase in Crude Steel Production in the West
Author:
Date: 1989-07-24
Page: 3

According to a report from the *Asian Wall Street Journal*, the total crude steel production in the non-socialist world for June this year reached 40.1 million tons, an increase of 2.8% compared to the same period last year.

### 西方粗钢产量上升

1989-07-24
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    西方粗钢产量上升
    据《亚洲华尔街日报》报道，今年6月份非社会主义世界的粗钢总产量为4010万吨，比去年同期增长2.8％。
