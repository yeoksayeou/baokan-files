Title: Funeral for Comrade Wan Zhongyi Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-07-29
Edition: 2

The funeral for Comrade Wan Zhongyi, an outstanding member of the Communist Party of China and former advisor to the Ministry of Agriculture, was held in Beijing on July 25.

Wan Zhongyi passed away in Beijing on June 8 due to illness, at the age of 82.

Wan Zhongyi joined the revolutionary cause in 1928 and became a member of the Communist Party of China the same year. He retired at the end of 1982, having served as an advisor to the Ministry of Agriculture. Before his retirement, Wan Zhongyi held various positions, including Secretary of the Communist Party of China's Tongshan County Committee, Secretary of the Third District Committee of the Su-Huai Area Party Committee, Political Commissar of the Huaihe Brigade of the Eighth Route Army, and Secretary of the County Committees of Lianyuan, Shuyang, and Tongyang. During the War of Liberation, he served as Secretary of the Huaihai Area Party Committee, Director of the United Front Department of the East China Work Committee, and Deputy Secretary of the North Jiangsu Area Party Committee. After the national liberation, he served as Secretary of the Jiangsu Provincial Agricultural Commission, Director of the Agricultural Propaganda Bureau of the Ministry of Agriculture, and Advisor to the Ministry of Agriculture.

Comrade Wan Zhongyi had an unwavering communist belief. He firmly supported the line, principles, and policies of the Party since the Third Plenary Session of the Eleventh Central Committee and resolutely opposed bourgeois liberalization.

Throughout his more than 60 years of revolutionary struggle, Wan Zhongyi remained absolutely loyal to the Party and dedicated himself wholeheartedly to serving the people, devoting his life to the Party's revolutionary cause.

Comrades Tian Jiyun, Chen Pixian, and other comrades, as well as the Central Organization Department, the Ministry of Agriculture, and the Jiangsu Provincial Committee, sent wreaths.

### 万众一遗体告别仪式在京举行

1989-07-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    万众一遗体告别仪式在京举行
    新华社北京电　中国共产党优秀党员、原农业部顾问万众一同志遗体告别仪式，7月25日在北京举行。
    万众一因病医治无效，于6月8日在京逝世，终年82岁。
    万众一同志1928年参加革命工作，同年加入中国共产党，1982年底经组织批准离休，离休前任农业部顾问。万众一在抗日战争时期曾任中共铜山县委书记、苏皖区党委第三地委书记、八路军淮河大队政治委员，涟水、沭阳、潼阳县委书记；解放战争时期曾任淮海地委书记、华中工作委员会民运部长、苏北区党委副书记；全国解放后曾任江苏省委农委书记、农业部农业宣传总局局长、农业部顾问。
    万众一同志有坚定的共产主义信念，坚决拥护党的十一届三中全会以来的路线、方针、政策，坚决反对资产阶级自由化。
    他在60多年的革命斗争中，对党无限忠诚，全心全意为人民服务，将毕生精力献给了党的革命事业。
    田纪云、陈丕显等同志，及中央组织部、农业部、江苏省委等单位送了花圈。
