Title: U.S. and Soviet Foreign Ministers Hold Secret Talks
Author:
Date: 1989-07-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, July 29th.

U.S. and Soviet Foreign Ministers Hold Secret Talks

The Soviet Foreign Minister Eduard Shevardnadze and U.S. Secretary of State James Baker held a three-hour secret meeting in Paris today.

After the meeting, Baker said that he and Shevardnadze discussed the issues of Cambodia, Afghanistan, and Central America.

Baker also mentioned that the possibility of a high-level meeting between U.S. President George H.W. Bush and Soviet leader Mikhail Gorbachev was brought up during the talks, but no specific date was set.

### 美苏外长举行秘密会谈

1989-07-30
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美苏外长举行秘密会谈
    新华社巴黎7月29日电  苏联外长谢瓦尔德纳泽和美国国务卿贝克今天在巴黎举行了3个多小时的秘密会议。
    贝克在会谈结束后说，他同谢瓦尔德纳泽的秘密会谈讨论了柬埔寨问题、阿富汗问题及中美洲问题。
    贝克说，会谈中也提及了美国总统布什同苏联领导人戈尔巴乔夫举行高级会晤的可能性，但没有确定日期。
