Title: National Tai Chi Fist, Sword, and Push Hands Competition Begins
Author: Zhou Dongdi
Date: 1989-07-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, July 10th.

Like flowing clouds and water, like vines entwining. Today's morning opening ceremony of the National Tai Chi Fist, Sword, and Push Hands Competition in Kunming showcased the athletes' outstanding performances, fully demonstrating the unique characteristics of these events.

More than 100 athletes from 26 provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions across the country participated in this event, including most of the country's top practitioners of Tai Chi Fist, Sword, and Push Hands. The competition will last for four days.

The Tai Chi Fist competition includes four styles: Chen, Yang, Sun, and Wu. The Tai Chi Push Hands competition is for men only and is divided into the following weight classes: 48, 52, 56, 60, 65, 70, 75, 80, and 85 kilograms and above.

### 全国太极拳、剑、推手赛开始
周东棣
1989-07-10
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    全国太极拳、剑、推手赛开始
    新华社昆明7月10日电　（记者周东棣）似行云流水，如青藤缠绕。今天上午在昆明揭幕的全国太极拳、剑、推手比赛中，运动员们的精彩表演充分展示了这些项目的特点。
    来自全国26个省、市、自治区的100多位运动员参加了此次赛事，其中包括了全国大部分从事太极拳、剑、推手的精英。比赛共进行4天。
    太极拳比赛分陈式、杨式、孙式、吴式4种竞赛套路；太极推手只进行男子比赛，共分48、52、56、60、65、70、75、80、85以上等级别。
