Title: The United States Eases Restrictions on Computer Exports
Author:
Date: 1989-07-20
Edition: 3

The U.S. Department of Commerce announced on July 18 that it would ease restrictions on the export of personal computers.

According to this decision, starting from July 18, the Department of Commerce will lift the export restrictions on personal computers compatible with the "IBM PC" series produced by International Business Machines Corporation and similar products to Western countries.

### 美放宽计算机出口限制

1989-07-20
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美放宽计算机出口限制
    美国商务部18日决定放松个人计算机的出口限制。
    根据这一决定，从18日起商务部将取消与国际商用机器公司生产的“爱特”个人计算机兼容的个人计算机及类似产品对西方国家的出口限制。
