Title: The Preliminary Rounds of the Fencing Event at the National Youth Games Begin
Author: Wáng Jiànmín
Date: 1989-07-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, July 12th.

The preliminary rounds of the fencing event at the Second National Youth Games began today at the Liaoning Gymnasium.

A total of 243 athletes from 17 teams representing Guangdong, Sichuan, Shanghai, Tianjin, Beijing, Shandong, Jilin, Jiangsu, Shaanxi, Heilongjiang, Henan, Guizhou, Hubei, Yunnan, Xinjiang, Anhui, and Liaoning will compete for the top 16 individual spots and the top 6 team spots in men's foil, épée, and sabre, and women's foil.

Only those who secure these positions will advance to the final rounds.

### 青运会击剑预赛开始
王健民
1989-07-12
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    青运会击剑预赛开始
    新华社沈阳7月12日电　（记者王健民）第二届全国青少年运动会击剑预赛，今天在辽宁体育馆开始。
    来自广东、四川、上海、天津、北京、山东、吉林、江苏、陕西、黑龙江、河南、贵州、湖北、云南、新疆、安徽、辽宁的17支代表队243名运动员，将争夺男子花剑、佩剑、重剑和女子花剑个人前16名、团体前6名。
    获得上述名次的选手才有资格进入决赛阶段比赛。
