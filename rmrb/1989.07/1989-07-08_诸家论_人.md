Title: Various Perspectives on "Human"
Author: Lǐ Cháng'ān
Date: 1989-07-08
Page: 8

Chemists say: Humans are products of carbon atoms.

Biologists say: Humans are aggregates of cells.

Astronomers say: Humans are children of the galaxy.

Anthropologists say: Humans are the result of a slow accumulation of surface characteristics—upright posture, keen vision, diligent hands, and a developed brain.

Archaeologists say: Humans are accumulators of culture, builders of cities, creators of pottery, sowers of crops, and inventors of writing.

Psychologists say: Humans possess extraordinarily complex brains with the ability to think and abstract, capabilities that overshadow the instincts and sensibilities inherited from other animal ancestors.

Literary figures say: Humans are the only animals that know or need to feel shame.

Theologians say: Humans are obedient participants in the grand farce of sin and redemption.

Sociologists say: Humans are successive shapers of the societies to which they belong.

(From June 27th, China Science and Technology Daily, by Li Chang'an)

### 诸家论“人”
李长安
1989-07-08
第8版(每周文摘)
专栏：

    诸家论“人”
    化学家说：人是炭原子的产物。
    生物学家说：人是细胞的聚集体。
    天文学家说：人是星河的孩子。
    人类学家说：人是体表特征的缓慢积累。可立的双足，敏锐的目光，勤劳的双手和发达的大脑。
    考古学家说：人是文化的积累者，城市的建设者，陶器的创造者，农作物的播种者，书写的发明者。
    心理学家说：人是复杂非凡的大脑拥有者，具有思维和抽象能力，这种才能压倒他从其他动物祖先那里继承来的天性和感性。
    文学家说：人是唯一知道羞耻或者需要羞耻的动物。
    神学家说：人是犯罪和赎恶这出大闹剧中恭顺的参与者。
    社会学家说：人是他所归属的社会的依次更替的塑造者。
                  （6月27日《中国科技报》  李长安文）
