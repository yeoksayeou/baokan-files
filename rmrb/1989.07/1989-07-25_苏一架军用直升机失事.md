Title: A Soviet Military Helicopter Crashes
Author:
Date: 1989-07-25
Edition: 3

According to a report from TASS on the 24th, a Soviet military helicopter crashed on a road in the suburbs of Vladivostok while performing a low-altitude flight. At that moment, a small car was passing by the crash site. As a result, all five crew members and the four people in the car were killed.

### 苏一架军用直升机失事

1989-07-25
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏一架军用直升机失事
    据塔斯社24日报道，苏联一架军用直升机在符拉迪沃斯托克郊区的公路上空作低空飞行时坠落在公路上。当时恰好有一辆小轿车驶过坠落地点。结果5名机组人员和小轿车内的4人无一生还。
