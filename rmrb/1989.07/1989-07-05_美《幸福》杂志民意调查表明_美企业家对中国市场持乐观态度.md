Title: Survey by U.S. Magazine "Fortune" Shows American Business Leaders Optimistic About the Chinese Market
Author:
Date: 1989-07-05
Page: 3

According to the latest issue of the American magazine "Fortune," despite recent political events in China, the CEOs of U.S. companies still view China as a market with significant potential for American goods and services. They anticipate that over the next five years, China will become increasingly open and market-driven.

These views were gathered from a survey conducted by an independent polling organization for "Fortune" magazine. The survey included 183 CEOs from the magazine's list of 500 leading companies, conducted between May 30 and June 7.

Allen Jacobson, president of the Minnesota Mining and Manufacturing Company, stated, "Recent events have not changed our long-term view of China's potential. This is certainly a setback... but we do not change our plans. We remain optimistic."

William Esrey, president of the United Telecommunications Company (a long-distance and local telephone company), shares the same view. He said, "This event has not changed my fundamental outlook."

"Fortune" magazine believes that the optimism of American corporate leaders stems from the fact that during the past decade of economic reforms, China's GDP has grown at an annual rate of over 10%, with some provinces seeing a doubling of living standards. Therefore, many of the managers surveyed believe that China's reform momentum is too strong to be halted.

### 美《幸福》杂志民意调查表明  美企业家对中国市场持乐观态度

1989-07-05
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美《幸福》杂志民意调查表明
    美企业家对中国市场持乐观态度
    本报讯　据最新出版的一期美国《幸福》杂志报道，尽管中国发生了最近的政治事件，但美国公司的总经理们仍然认为，中国是美国商品和服务的一个有潜在价值的大市场。他们预期，未来5年内，中国将变成更加开放和以市场为动力的市场。
    以上看法来自美国《幸福》杂志对一些大公司的总经理进行调查所得结果。这项调查是由一家独立的民意研究机构进行的。
    属于《幸福》杂志500家公司的183位总经理是在5月30日至6月7日期间接受调查的。
    明尼苏达矿产和制造业公司总裁阿伦·雅可布逊说：“最近发生的事件并未改变我们对中国的长期潜力的看法。这一事件当然是个挫折……但我们并不改变计划。我们仍旧持乐观态度。”
    联合通讯公司（一家长途、地方电话公司）的总裁威廉·埃斯雷也持相同的看法。他说，这一事件并未改变我的基本看法。
    《幸福》杂志认为，美国大公司领导人所以态度乐观，是因为在过去10年的经济改革期间，中国的国民经济总值逐年以10％以上的速度增长，有些省份的人民生活水平提高了一倍。因此，很多接受调查的经理人员认为，中国的改革势头已强大得无法中止。
