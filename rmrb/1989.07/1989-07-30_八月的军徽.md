Title: The Military Emblem of August
Author: Fang Zedi
Date: 1989-07-30
Edition: 8

    The military emblem of August
    Fang Zedi
    It comes from August 1st
    From the gunfire and bullets
    From the people's anticipation of dawn
    The darkness raised bayonets cannot pierce the glory
    And the tombstones are your arduous footsteps
    Finally, Tiananmen Square
    Flies up countless doves of peace
    People cherish the quiet moonlight
    Cherish the nests built on the poplar trees
    Cherish the ears of grain in autumn
    Cherish every part forged in the furnace
    Some praises and respects
    From military boots and sewing kits become today's textbook
    Texts
    The old landlord has aged
    The old landlord's story of checking the bedding never grows old
    The granddaughter of the old landlord's granddaughter
    Treasures a photo of carrying a gun
    Men and women all love to listen to and sing "The Moon of the Fifteenth"
    As long as you shine
    People go to work, people go to the fields, people read books
    The scenery of cities and villages all love August 1st
    At this time
    The people want to say thank you
    The people want to shout son
    Your stars are the stars on the square's flag
    You will not forget
    Standing before you are 1.1 billion people
    Standing is a country
