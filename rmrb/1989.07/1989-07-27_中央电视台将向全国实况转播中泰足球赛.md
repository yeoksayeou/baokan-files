Title: CCTV to Broadcast Live China vs. Thailand Football Match
Author: Guólì
Date: 1989-07-27
Edition: 4

CCTV will broadcast live the match between the Chinese and Thai football teams in the World Cup qualifiers for the Asian zone, which will be held in Shenyang on the evening of July 29 at 7:45 PM. The finals of the Asian Boxing Championship will be broadcast on July 30 at 1 PM.

### 中央电视台将向全国实况转播中泰足球赛
国力
1989-07-27
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    中央电视台将向全国实况转播中泰足球赛
    本报讯　中央电视台将于7月29日晚7时45分向全国实况转播在沈阳举行的世界杯足球预选赛亚洲区中国队与泰国队之战，30日下午1时播放亚洲拳击锦标赛决赛录相。（国力）
