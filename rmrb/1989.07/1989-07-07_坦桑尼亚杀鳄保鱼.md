Title: Tanzania Kills Crocodiles to Protect Fish
Author:
Date: 1989-07-07
Edition: 7

To protect fishery resources, villagers in southwestern Tanzania's Rukwa Lake have killed more than 5,000 crocodiles. It is said that a single crocodile can consume 10 kilograms of fish meat per day. This action has been recognized by the International Union for Conservation of Nature and Natural Resources. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 坦桑尼亚杀鳄保鱼

1989-07-07
第7版(国际专页)
专栏：

    坦桑尼亚杀鳄保鱼
    为保护渔业资源，坦桑尼亚村民已在西南部的鲁夸湖捕杀5000多条鳄鱼。据称，一条鳄鱼一天吞食10公斤鱼肉。这一行动得到国际动物和稀有物种组织的认可。（据新华社）
