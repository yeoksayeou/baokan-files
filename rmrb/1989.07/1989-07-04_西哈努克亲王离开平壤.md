Title: Prince Norodom Sihanouk Leaves Pyongyang
Author: Lí Nengqīng
Date: 1989-07-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 4th.

Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Chairman of the Democratic Kampuchea, left Pyongyang by plane this morning for Beijing.

Politburo member of the Workers' Party of Korea and Vice President Li Chong-yom, and Politburo alternate member and Deputy Premier of the Cabinet Choe Ch'un-ki, were among those who saw him off at the airport.

Chinese Ambassador to the DPRK Wen Ye-zhan and diplomatic envoys from various countries in Pyongyang also went to the airport to see Prince Sihanouk off.

### 西哈努克亲王离开平壤
黎能清
1989-07-04
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王离开平壤
    新华社平壤7月4日电　（记者黎能清）民主柬埔寨主席诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王今天上午乘飞机离开平壤前往北京。
    朝鲜劳动党中央政治局委员、国家副主席李钟玉、政治局候补委员、政务院副总理郑浚基等到机场送行。
    中国驻朝鲜大使温业湛、各国驻平壤的外交使节也到机场为西哈努克送行。
