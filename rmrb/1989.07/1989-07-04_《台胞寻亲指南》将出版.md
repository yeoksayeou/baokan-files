Title: Guide to Finding Relatives for Taiwanese Compatriots to be Published
Author:
Date: 1989-07-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 4th

A book titled "Guide to Finding Relatives for Taiwanese Compatriots," which compiles advertisements for finding relatives from mainland Taiwan compatriots, will be edited and published in installments by the Fuzhou editorial department of Huayi Publishing House.

### 《台胞寻亲指南》将出版
