Title: Japan Invests Heavily in Developing New Power Lines
Author:
Date: 1989-07-13
Edition: 3

Japan's Ministry of International Trade and Industry and several large power line companies have recently decided to invest 5 billion yen over the next five years to develop next-generation power lines.

According to the *Nihon Keizai Shimbun*, these next-generation power lines will utilize fiber optics and sensor technology to enable the power lines themselves to have the capability of early detection of power line faults and to prevent faults before they occur.

### 日本投巨资研制新电线

1989-07-13
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    日本投巨资研制新电线
    新华社东京7月12日电  日本通产省和一些大型电线公司最近决定，利用5年时间，投资50亿日元研制下一代电线。
    据《日本工业新闻》报道，所谓下一代电线是利用光纤和传感器技术，使电线本身能够具有早期发现电线故障和防故障于未然的机能。
