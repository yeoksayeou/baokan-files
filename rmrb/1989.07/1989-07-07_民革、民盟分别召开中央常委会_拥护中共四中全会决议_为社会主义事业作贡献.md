Title: Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang and China Democratic League Hold Central Standing Committee Meetings
Author:
Date: 1989-07-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 6th.

The third expanded meeting of the seventh Central Standing Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang (RCCK) was held in Beijing from July 3rd to 6th. RCCK Chairman Zhu Xuefan called on RCCK members to focus on studying the documents of the 13th Central Committee of the Communist Party of China (CPC) Fourth Plenary Session, unify their thoughts, boost their spirits, and do more work.

The meeting conveyed and studied the important speech by Deng Xiaoping and the documents of the 13th Central Committee of the CPC Fourth Plenary Session. The attendees unanimously agreed that this plenary session was held at a very timely and successful moment, with significant real and far-reaching historical implications. They expressed their heartfelt support for Deng Xiaoping's important speech and resolutely supported all the decisions made by the CPC Fourth Plenary Session.

The meeting adopted the "Resolution of the RCCK Central Standing Committee on Studying and Implementing the Spirit of the 13th Central Committee of the CPC Fourth Plenary Session," requiring all RCCK organizations to prioritize studying the spirit of the 13th Central Committee of the CPC Fourth Plenary Session, especially Deng Xiaoping's important speech. Simultaneously, they should actively assist the government in completely suppressing the counter-revolutionary rebellion, promoting rectification and reform and opening up; carry out reporting work to promote clean governance; continue to conduct overseas liaison work, promote exchanges and visits across the Taiwan Strait, and contribute to the rejuvenation of China and the unification of the motherland.

RCCK Vice Chairpersons Zheng Dongguan, Hou Jinyu, Peng Qingyuan, Jia Yibin, Xu Qichao, Li Ganliu, He Luli, and Li Peiyao attended the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 7th.

The fourth expanded meeting of the sixth Central Standing Committee of the China Democratic League (CDL) concluded in Beijing today. Attendees carefully studied the documents of the 13th Central Committee of the CPC Fourth Plenary Session and Deng Xiaoping's important speech. They expressed their resolute support for all the resolutions passed by the 13th Central Committee of the CPC Fourth Plenary Session and the new leadership of the CPC Central Committee headed by Jiang Zemin. They are determined to continue to unite closely around the CPC Central Committee, making new contributions to the socialist modernization construction and reform and opening up of the motherland.

The meeting proposed that CDL organizations should pay special attention to assisting the CPC and the people's government in carrying out the four major tasks pointed out in the 13th Central Committee of the CPC Fourth Plenary Session report, viewing the stabilization of the situation as the highest national interest, maintaining stability and unity, adhering to the four cardinal principles, continuing to play a role in supervision, and conducting consultation services and regional development planning research facing society.

The meeting adopted the "Decision of the CDL Central Committee on Studying and Implementing the Spirit of the 13th Central Committee of the CPC Fourth Plenary Session and Strengthening Ideological Construction."

This meeting was convened on July 4th. CDL Chairman Fei Xiaotong presided over the meeting. Vice Chairpersons Gao Tian, Ye Duyi, Tao Dazong, Guan Mengjue, Luo Hanxian, Ma Dashui, Feng Zhiyun, Ding Shisun, Xie Songkai, and CDL Central Consultative Committee Chairman Su Buqing, Vice Chairpersons Feng Sutrao, Li Wenyi, Chen Minzhi, Lin Hengyuan, Wen Jiadu, Pan Daqian, and others attended the meeting.

### 民革、民盟分别召开中央常委会  拥护中共四中全会决议  为社会主义事业作贡献

1989-07-07
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    民革、民盟分别召开中央常委会
    拥护中共四中全会决议  为社会主义事业作贡献
    据新华社北京7月6日电　中国国民党革命委员会第七届中央常务委员会第三次扩大会议于7月3日至6日在北京举行。民革中央主席朱学范在闭幕会上号召民革党员当前要集中力量学好中共十三届四中全会文件，统一思想，振奋精神，多做工作。
    会议传达和学习讨论了邓小平同志的重要讲话和中国共产党十三届四中全会文件。与会同志一致认为，这次全会开得非常及时，非常成功，具有重大的现实意义和深远的历史影响。大家表示衷心拥护邓小平同志的重要讲话，坚决拥护中共四中全会作出的各项决定。
    会议通过了《民革中央常务委员会关于学习和贯彻中国共产党十三届四中全会精神的决议》，要求民革各级组织要以学习中共十三届四中全会精神，特别是邓小平同志重要讲话作为当前的首要任务。同时，要积极协助政府彻底平息反革命暴乱，推进治理整顿和改革开放；开展举报工作，促进廉政建设；继续开展海外联谊工作，推动海峡两岸的交流和往来，为振兴中华、统一祖国尽力。
    民革中央副主席郑洞国、侯镜如、彭清源、贾亦斌、徐起超、李赣骝、何鲁丽、李沛瑶，出席了会议。
    据新华社北京7月7日电　中国民主同盟第六届中央常务委员会第四次（扩大）会议今天在北京闭幕。出席会议的同志认真学习了中国共产党十三届四中全会文件和邓小平同志的重要讲话。表示坚决拥护中共十三届四中全会通过的各项决议，拥护以江泽民同志为核心的新的中共中央领导机构，决心一如既往地紧密团结在中共中央周围，为祖国的社会主义现代化建设和改革开放作出新的贡献。
    会议提出，民盟各级组织当前要特别注意协助中共和人民政府做好中共十三届四中全会公报中所指出的四件大事，将稳定大局视为国家的最高利益，维护安定团结，坚持四项基本原则，继续发挥参政和监督作用，开展面向社会的咨询服务和区域发展规划研究工作。
    会议通过了《民盟中央关于学习贯彻中共十三届四中全会精神、加强思想建设的决定》。
    这次会议于7月4日召开，民盟中央主席费孝通主持了会议，副主席高天、叶笃义、陶大镛、关梦觉、罗涵先、马大猷、冯之浚、丁石孙、谢颂凯，民盟中央参议委员会主任苏步青、副主任冯素陶、李文宜、陈敏之、林亨元、闻家驷、潘大逵等出席会议。
