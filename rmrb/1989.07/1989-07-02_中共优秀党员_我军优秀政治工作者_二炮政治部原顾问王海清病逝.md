Title: Outstanding Party Member and Excellent Political Worker of the PLA, Former Advisor to the Political Department of the Second Artillery Corps, Wang Haiching Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-07-02
Edition: 2

	Outstanding Party Member and Excellent Political Worker of the PLA,
	Former Advisor to the Political Department of the Second Artillery Corps, Wang Haiching Passes Away

	New China News Agency, Beijing, July 2nd - The outstanding Party member and excellent political worker of the PLA, former advisor to the Political Department of the Second Artillery Corps, Wang Haiching, passed away due to illness in Beijing on May 3, 1989, at the age of 73.

	Wang Haiching was from Liu'an County, Anhui Province. He joined the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army in 1932 and the Communist Party of China in 1933. He served in various positions, including Director of the Red Army's Civil Affairs Bureau, Political Director of a Regiment, Political Director of a Military Sub-District, County Party Secretary, Political Director of a Division, Political Commissar of the Air Force Science Research Department, and Deputy Political Director of the Public Security Forces, concurrently serving as Prosecutor of the Military Prosecution Office of the Public Security Forces. He was promoted to the rank of Major General in 1964. During the Second Revolutionary Civil War, he participated in the anti-"encirclement" campaigns and the famous Long March. During the War of Resistance Against Japan, he participated in the renowned Hundred Regiments Offensive. During the War of Liberation, he led his troops in the frontal assault on Changchun. He led from the front, fought bravely, and commanded decisively. After the liberation of the country, he diligently explored and summarized experiences in ideological and political work, striving to ensure the role of ideological and political work. During the ten years of turmoil, he resolutely fought against the counter-revolutionary cliques of Lin Biao and the "Gang of Four." He supported the Party's line, principles, and policies since the Third Plenum of the Eleventh Central Committee, maintaining unity in thought and politics with the Central Committee of the Party. He strictly adhered to discipline, considered the overall situation, united with comrades, cared for subordinates, maintained a democratic style, was upright, lived frugally, and demonstrated the noble qualities of an old Red Army soldier and a Communist Party member.

### 中共优秀党员　我军优秀政治工作者  二炮政治部原顾问王海清病逝

1989-07-02
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    中共优秀党员　我军优秀政治工作者
    二炮政治部原顾问王海清病逝
    新华社北京7月2日电  中国共产党优秀党员、我军优秀的政治工作者、第二炮兵政治部原顾问王海清因病于1989年5月3日在北京逝世，终年73岁。
    王海清同志是安徽省六安县人。他1932年参加中国工农红军，1933年加入中国共产党，历任红军民运科长、团政治处主任、军分区政治部主任、县委书记、师政治部主任、空军科学研究部政委、原公安部队政治部副主任兼公安部队军事检察院检察长等职。1964年晋升为少将军衔。在第二次国内革命战争中，他参加了反“围剿”斗争和举世闻名的二万五千里长征。抗日战争中，他参加了著名的百团大战。解放战争中，他率部参加了围攻长春的正面战斗。他身先士卒，英勇作战，果断指挥。全国解放后，他认真探索和总结思想政治工作经验，努力发挥思想政治工作的保证作用。在十年动乱中，他同林彪、“四人帮”反革命集团进行了坚决的斗争。他拥护党的十一届三中全会以来的路线、方针、政策，在思想上、政治上同党中央保持一致。他严守纪律，顾全大局，团结同志，关心下级，作风民主，为人正派，生活俭朴，表现了一个红军老战士、共产党员的高尚品德。
