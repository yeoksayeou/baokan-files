Title: The Population of the European Community Exceeds 300 Million
Author:
Date: 1989-07-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Brussels, July 13th.

The European Community's Statistical Office announced on July 13th that the total population of the 12 member states has now surpassed 325 million.

In 1988, the birth rate in the Community was approximately 12 per thousand, slightly higher than the 11.8 per thousand in 1987. The death rate remained the same as in 1987, at 9.9 per thousand.

### 欧共体人口逾3亿

1989-07-14
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    欧共体人口逾3亿
    据新华社布鲁塞尔7月13日电　欧洲共同体统计局13日宣布，共同体12国的总人口现已超过3.25亿。
    1988年，共同体的人口出生率约为12‰，略高于1987年的11.8‰；而死亡率则与1987年持平，为9.9‰。
