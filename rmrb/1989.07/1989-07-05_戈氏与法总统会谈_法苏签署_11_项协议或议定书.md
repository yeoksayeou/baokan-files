Title: Gorbachev Meets with French President; France and the Soviet Union Sign 11 Agreements or Protocols
Author: Lǚ Wǎngdà
Date: 1989-07-05
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, July 5th.

Gorbachev met with French President Mitterrand yesterday afternoon after arriving in Paris at noon. Gorbachev first introduced the profound changes taking place in the Soviet Union and the progress of the policy of "glasnost." Mitterrand congratulated the Soviet Union on its reform achievements.

The two leaders discussed East-West European relations and the Lebanese issue. Both leaders pointed out the possibility of overcoming Europe's division.

This morning, Gorbachev laid a wreath at the Tomb of the Unknown Soldier beneath the Arc de Triomphe and then met with Paris Mayor Jacques Chirac at the Paris City Hall.

At noon, Chairman Gorbachev delivered a speech at the University of Paris to Paris intellectuals, praising the 1789 French Revolution and the 1917 October Revolution in the Soviet Union, which he said had driven historical and human social progress.

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, July 4th.

France and the Soviet Union signed 11 agreements or protocols today in Paris on aviation and aerospace, image and text cooperation, and science and technology.

French Minister of Economy Pierre Bérégovoy and First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union Lev Voronin presided over today's signing ceremony.

### 戈氏与法总统会谈  法苏签署11项协议或议定书
吕网大
1989-07-05
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    戈氏与法总统会谈
    法苏签署11项协议或议定书
    本报巴黎7月5日电　记者吕网大报道：苏联领导人戈尔巴乔夫昨天中午抵达巴黎后，下午即同法国总统密特朗举行了会谈。戈尔巴乔夫首先介绍了苏联国内发生的深刻变化及推行“公开性”的情况，密特朗祝贺苏联改革取得的成就。
    两国领导人会谈的内容包括东西欧关系和黎巴嫩问题。两位领导人同时指出了克服欧洲分裂的可能性。
    今天上午，戈尔巴乔夫向位于凯旋门下的无名战士墓敬献了花圈，随后到巴黎市政府同希拉克市长会晤。
    中午，戈尔巴乔夫主席在巴黎大学向巴黎知识界人士演讲，赞扬1789年的法国大革命和苏联1917年十月革命，推动了历史和人类社会的进步。
    据新华社巴黎7月4日电  法国和苏联今天在巴黎签署了航空航天、图像文字合作以及科技等11项协议或协议议定书。
    法国经济部长皮埃尔·贝雷戈瓦和苏联部长会议第一副主席列夫·沃罗宁主持了今天的签字仪式。
