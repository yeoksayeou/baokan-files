Title: Youths Learn Electronic Keyboard in Desert Military School
Author: Xiangfeng
Date: 1989-07-14
Edition: 5

A children's electronic keyboard class was inaugurated at the primary school of a missile test base in the depths of the Gobi Desert. On the first day of the class, 53 young students enthusiastically participated. Look, the children are enjoying themselves so much.
