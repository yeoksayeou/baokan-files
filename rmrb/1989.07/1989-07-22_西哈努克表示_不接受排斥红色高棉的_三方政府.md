Title: Sihanouk Declares Rejection of "Three-Party Government" Excluding the Khmer Rouge
Author:
Date: 1989-07-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, July 21st.

Prince Norodom Sihanouk, chairman of the Coalition Government of Democratic Kampuchea (CGDK), stated today in Paris that he will not accept a "three-party government" that excludes the Khmer Rouge. He reiterated that Vietnam must withdraw all its troops from Cambodia.

Sihanouk made these remarks at a press conference in Paris. He pointed out that Hanoi's announcement of withdrawing from Cambodia by September 30 does not solve the Cambodian problem, "because there are still 130,000 Vietnamese troops hidden within the military and militia of the Phnom Penh regime." He emphasized that excluding the Khmer Rouge from a united government in Cambodia would allow Vietnam's schemes to succeed.

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, July 21st.

The U.S. Senate passed an amendment on Thursday authorizing the government to provide secret military aid to two factions within the Cambodian resistance forces. The State Department welcomed this move today.

State Department spokesman Richard Boucher said, "We are pleased that the Senate, like the House, has expressed support for the government's strategy on Cambodia." Earlier, the House had passed a similar bill.

Boucher said that an international conference on the Cambodian issue will be held in Paris next week, making the Senate's action "very timely." Its purpose is to support Prince Sihanouk's efforts to achieve a solution to the Cambodian problem that all parties can accept.

According to reports, the Bush administration plans to provide 12,000 rifles and other light weapons to the forces of Sihanouk and Son Sann.

### 西哈努克表示  不接受排斥红色高棉的“三方政府”

1989-07-22
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    西哈努克表示
    不接受排斥红色高棉的“三方政府”
    据新华社巴黎7月21日电  民柬主席西哈努克亲王今天在巴黎表示，他决不接受一个排斥“红色高棉”的“三方政府”。他重申，越南必须从柬埔寨全部撤军。
    西哈努克是在此间举行的记者招待会上发表上述讲话的。他指出，河内宣布9月30日从柬撤军并不能解决柬埔寨问题，“因为仍然有13万越南军人隐藏在金边政权的军队和民兵之中”。他指出，在柬埔寨建立的联合政府中排斥“红色高棉”将会使越南的阴谋得逞。
    新华社华盛顿7月21日电美国参议院星期四通过一项修正案，授权政府向柬埔寨抵抗力量中的两派提供秘密军事援助。国务院今天对此表示欢迎。
    国务院发言人鲍彻说，“我们为参议院能够和众院一样表达对政府有关柬埔寨战略的支持感到高兴。”在此之前，众院通过了类似的议案。
    鲍彻说，关于柬埔寨问题的国际会议将于下周在巴黎举行，因此参院采取的这一行动“非常及时”，其目的是支持西哈努克亲王为争取一个各方可以接受的解决柬埔寨问题的方案所作的努力。
    据报道，布什政府计划向西哈努克和宋双两派的部队提供1.2万支步枪和其他轻武器。
