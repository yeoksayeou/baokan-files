Title: To the Military Flag
Author: Gù Liándì
Date: 1989-07-28
Edition: 8

	To the Military Flag
	Gù Liándì

	On your threads,
	Thunder has rolled,
	And so,
	You bear the mission
	To herald spring.

	On your threads,
	Spring breezes have swept,
	And so,
	You ignite the flame
	That illuminates the motherland!

	On your threads,
	Warm rain has bathed,
	And so,
	You nourish the loyalty
	Of the soldiers.
