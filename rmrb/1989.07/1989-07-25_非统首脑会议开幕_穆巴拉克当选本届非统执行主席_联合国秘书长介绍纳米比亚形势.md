Title: The 25th Summit of the OAU Heads of State Opens; Mubarak Elected Chairman; UN Secretary-General Briefs on Namibia

Author:

Date: 1989-07-25

Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Addis Ababa, July 24th.

The 25th Summit of the Heads of State and Government of the Organization of African Unity (OAU) opened this afternoon in Addis Ababa, the capital of Ethiopia.

Ethiopian President Mengistu Haile Mariam presided over the summit.

Egyptian President Hosni Mubarak was elected as the chairman of the OAU for this session.

During the opening ceremony, African leaders and relevant representatives attending the summit praised the achievements of the OAU since its establishment in 1963, particularly in completing the task of decolonization and addressing African economic issues.

Mengistu commended the progress made in Namibia's independence process and called on African countries to make concerted efforts to ensure free and fair elections in Namibia.

He also called for an international conference on external debt, urging each country to adopt appropriate structural adjustment plans based on its national conditions to curb the economic decline in most African countries.

UN Secretary-General Javier Pérez de Cuéllar briefed the attending heads of state and government on the situation in Namibia, praising the international community, especially African countries, for their efforts in easing international tensions and resolving conflicts through dialogue.

He also assured that the UN would continue to work to improve the international trade conditions for African countries and help alleviate their debt burdens.

Ugandan President Yoweri Museveni stated that while efforts are being made to convene an international conference on external debt, African countries should reduce waste, corruption, unnecessary imports, and strengthen talent training to fundamentally improve the severe reliance on exporting primary products to revive the economy.

The outgoing chairman of the OAU, Toure, the chairman of the Palestine Liberation Organization Executive Committee, Yasser Arafat, and the chairman of the South West Africa People's Organization, Sam Nujoma, also spoke at the opening ceremony.

The summit is expected to focus on discussing Namibia's independence, the situation in Southern Africa, especially South Africa, the economic crisis and external debt issues, and the election of a new secretary-general for the OAU.

### 非统首脑会议开幕  穆巴拉克当选本届非统执行主席  联合国秘书长介绍纳米比亚形势

1989-07-25
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    非统首脑会议开幕
    穆巴拉克当选本届非统执行主席
    联合国秘书长介绍纳米比亚形势
    新华社亚的斯亚贝巴7月24日电　非洲统一组织第25届国家和政府首脑会议今天下午在埃塞俄比亚首都亚的斯亚贝巴开幕。
    埃塞俄比亚总统门格斯图主持了本届首脑会议。
    埃及总统穆巴拉克当选为本届非统组织执行主席。
    在会议的开幕式上，非洲国家领导人以及出席会议的有关代表在发言中高度赞扬非统组织自1963年成立以来所取得的成就，特别是在完成非殖民化的任务和解决非洲经济问题等方面所取得的成就。
    门格斯图赞扬纳米比亚独立进程目前取得的进展。他呼吁非洲国家作出一致的努力，以确保在纳米比亚举行自由和公正的选举。
    他还呼吁召开有关外债的国际会议，根据每一个国家的国情采取适当的结构调整计划，以便遏制大多数非洲国家出现的经济下降趋势。
    联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔向与会的政府和国家元首介绍了纳米比亚的形势，并赞扬国际社会特别是非洲国家在缓和国际形势和通过对话解决冲突方面所作的努力。
    他还保证，联合国将继续为改善非洲国家的国际贸易条件而努力，帮助减轻非洲国家的债务负担。
    乌干达总统穆塞韦尼说，在为召开有关外债问题的国际会议作出努力的同时，非洲国家应该减少浪费、腐败，减少不必要的进口和加强人才培训，以便从根本上改善严重依赖出口初级产品复兴经济的状况。
    上届非统组织主席特拉奥雷、巴勒斯坦解放组织执委会主席阿拉法特、西南非洲人民组织主席努乔马在开幕式上也发了言。
    预计，本届首脑会将集中讨论纳米比亚独立问题，南部非洲形势特别是南非形势，经济危机和外债问题，选举非统组织新的秘书长。
