Title: Beijing Holds Art Performance to Celebrate Army Day
Author:
Date: 1989-07-27
Edition: 4

To celebrate the 62nd anniversary of the founding of the army, the Ministry of Culture organized an art performance today featuring the Central Orchestra, the Central Song and Dance Ensemble, and 10 other units to entertain the troops stationed in Beijing and the martial law troops.

Comrades Qiao Shi, Li Tianying, Wu Xueqian, Xi Zhongxun, Lei Jieqiong, Wang Bingqian, Qu Wu, and Hong Xuezhi, among other leaders, attended the performance.

Before the performance began, Vice Minister of Culture Ying Ruocheng delivered a speech. He said that in the recent period, Beijing had experienced a counterrevolutionary riot. The troops stationed in Beijing and the martial law troops had demonstrated a revolutionary spirit of fearlessness in the face of hardship and sacrifice, fully proving that they are genuine soldiers of the people who can withstand any test.

### 北京举行文艺晚会庆八一  乔石李铁映吴学谦等出席观看

1989-07-27
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    北京举行文艺晚会庆八一
    乔石李铁映吴学谦等出席观看
    据新华社北京7月27日电　为庆祝建军62周年，文化部今天组织了由中央乐团、中央歌舞团等10家单位参加的文艺晚会，慰问三军驻京部队和戒严部队官兵。
    乔石、李铁映、吴学谦、习仲勋、雷洁琼、王丙乾、屈武、洪学智等领导同志观看了演出。
    文化部副部长英若诚在晚会开幕前发表讲话说，前段时间北京发生了反革命暴乱，三军驻京部队和戒严部队官兵发扬不怕艰难困苦、不怕流血牺牲的革命精神，充分表明他们是经得住考验的真正的人民子弟兵。
