Title: Jiangsu Implements Registration Management for Trade Fairs
Author: Gong Yongquan
Date: 1989-07-16
Edition: 2

Jiangsu's commercial and industrial administration has implemented a registration and certification management system for trade fairs held within the province, providing participants with a sense of security.

Over the past year, a total of 473 trade fairs in Jiangsu have completed the registration procedures.

### 江苏对展销会实行登记管理
龚永泉
1989-07-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    江苏对展销会实行登记管理
    本报南京7月15日电  记者龚永泉报道：江苏工商部门对省内举办的商品展销会实行登记发证管理，使展销会参与者有了安全感。
    一年多来，江苏共有473个展销会办理登记手续。
