Date: 1989-07-12
Edition: 3

U.S. Military Violates Cuban Sovereignty

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, July 11th - According to a report by the Latin American Press Agency on the 11th, over the past 27 years, the U.S. military stationed at the Guantanamo military base has violated Cuban sovereignty on average every 37 hours.

Statistics show that since 1962, U.S. aircraft have flown over Cuban airspace without permission from the Cuban government more than 6,700 times. The U.S. military has invaded Cuban territory and territorial waters 610 and 1,320 times, respectively.

Guantanamo is a U.S. naval base located in Cuba. The U.S. has occupied this base, which covers an area of 100 square kilometers, under a treaty signed in 1903 that has no time limit.

### 美军侵犯古巴主权

1989-07-12
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美军侵犯古巴主权
    新华社哈瓦那7月11日电　据古巴拉美通讯社11日报道，27年来，驻扎在关塔那摩军事基地的美国军队平均每隔37小时就侵犯一次古巴主权。
    据统计，从1962年起，美国飞机未经古巴政府许可飞越古巴领空达6700多次，美军入侵古巴领土和领海分别为610次和1320次。
    关塔那摩是美国设在古巴的一个海军基地。美国是根据1903年签订的一项没有时限的美古条约占有这个面积为100平方公里的基地的。
