Title: Hunan Stabilizes Enterprise Contract Responsibility System
Author: Zhang Yong
Date: 1989-07-14
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, July 14th.

Hunan Stabilizes Enterprise Contract Responsibility System

Hunan Vice Governor Yu Haichao recently stated at a provincial industrial and commercial work conference that economic system reform must continue to focus on activating enterprises, particularly in revitalizing large and medium-sized enterprises.

Last year, 1,742 budgeted enterprises in Hunan completed their contract tasks, accounting for 93.2% of the total number of contracted enterprises. So far, the contract agreements have been basically fulfilled. According to statistics from 10 cities including Changsha, Xiangtan, and Zhuzhou, 315 enterprises have implemented full-employee risk guarantee contracts in the first half of this year.

### 湖南稳定企业承包责任制
张勇
1989-07-14
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    湖南稳定企业承包责任制
    新华社长沙7月14日电　（记者张勇）湖南省副省长俞海潮在最近召开的全省工交工作会议上提出，经济体制改革仍然要坚持以搞活企业为中心，尤其是要把大中型企业搞活。
    湖南省去年预算内企业完成合同任务的有1742户；占承包总户数的93.2％，到目前为止，承包合同已基本兑现。据对长沙、湘潭、株洲等10个地市的统计，今年上半年有315户企业推行了全员风险抵押承包。
