Title: Afghan Pilot Joins Guerrilla Forces
Author:
Date: 1989-07-07
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency,

An Afghan pilot named Jan Muhammad, who was part of the Kabul regime, flew a Soviet-made fighter jet and landed in the northwestern province of Peshawar, Pakistan, at around 3:30 PM local time on the 6th. He announced his allegiance to the Afghan guerrilla forces based in Pakistan.

### 阿一飞行员投奔游击队

1989-07-07
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    阿一飞行员投奔游击队
    阿富汗喀布尔政权一名叫詹·穆罕默德的驾驶员驾驶一架苏制战斗机于当地时间6日下午3时半降落在巴基斯坦西北边境省首府白沙瓦，并宣布效忠于基地设在巴基斯坦的阿富汗游击队。
          （据新华社）
