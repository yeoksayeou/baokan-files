Title: Huainan Railway Suspended Due to Rain for 19 Hours
Author: Zhang Chuanxuan, Wang Degong
Date: 1989-07-11
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Hefei, July 10th.

Due to a severe rainstorm and flooding, a crucial railway line in Anhui Province, the Huainan line from Hefei to Yuxikou, experienced significant disruptions. From July 6th to 8th, 33 sections of the railway were submerged by floodwaters, with 16 sections completely impassable. As a result, train services were suspended for a cumulative total of 19 hours.

### 淮南铁路因雨断运达19小时
张传宣  王德工
1989-07-11
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    淮南铁路因雨断运达19小时
    新华社合肥7月10日电  （记者张传宣、通讯员王德工）由于遭受暴雨洪水袭击，安徽省境内重要铁路线淮南线合肥至裕溪口路段，7月6日至8日有33处被洪水淹没，其中16处断道。列车被迫中断运行时间累计达19小时。
