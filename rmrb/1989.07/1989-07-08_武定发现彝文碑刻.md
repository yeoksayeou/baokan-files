Title: Discovery of Yi Script Inscriptions in Wuding
Author: Qian Jiang
Date: 1989-07-08
Edition: 4

A Yi script survey team from Chuxiong Prefecture in Yunnan Province has discovered over a hundred ancient Yi script inscriptions of significant value in Jiti Village, Wuding County, since May this year. These inscriptions cover topics such as history, geography, and genealogy, reflecting the lives, customs, production, and religious beliefs of the ancient Yi people. They hold great significance for the study of ancient Yi culture.

### 武定发现彝文碑刻
钱江
1989-07-08
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    武定发现彝文碑刻
    本报昆明7月7日电　记者钱江报道：云南省楚雄州的彝文普查小组自今年5月以来，在武定县己梯村发现有重要价值的彝文古碑刻百余座，这些碑刻内容涉及历史、地理、系谱等，反映了彝族先民们的生活礼俗、生产和宗教信仰，对研究彝族古代文化很有意义。
