Title: Severe Flood Damage in Hunan
Author: Wu Xinghua Liao Keqin
Date: 1989-07-09
Edition: 2

From June 27 to July 4, Hunan Province experienced an average rainfall of 111 millimeters. This rainfall was characterized by its intensity, duration, and wide coverage. Areas in Changsha, Hengyang, Shaoyang, Loudi, Zhuzhou, and Xiangtan were particularly affected, resulting in severe mountain flood disasters. Preliminary statistics indicate that this disaster submerged 2,857,800 mu of farmland; collapsed 5,616 houses; caused 19 deaths and 68 injuries; destroyed 6,030 ponds and dams; and damaged 6,958 sections of canals totaling 158 kilometers.

### 湖南洪灾损失严重
吴兴华　廖克勤
1989-07-09
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    湖南洪灾损失严重
    本报长沙7月9日电　6月27日至7月4日，湖南全省平均降雨111毫米。这次降雨强度大、时间长、范围广，长沙、衡阳、邵阳、娄底、株洲、湘潭等地、市的部分地方造成较严重的山洪灾害。据初步统计，这次灾害共淹没农作物285.78万亩；倒塌房屋5616间；死亡19人，伤68人；冲毁塘坝6030处；毁坏渠道6958处、158公里。
    （吴兴华　廖克勤）
