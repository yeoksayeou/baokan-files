Title: Chinese Foreign Minister Arrives in Paris to Attend International Conference on Cambodia
Author: 
Date: 1989-07-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, July 30th.

Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen arrived in Paris this afternoon from Zimbabwe. Deputy Foreign Minister Liu Shucai and Chinese Ambassador to France Zhou Jue, who had arrived earlier, welcomed him at the airport.

Foreign Minister Qian Qichen is leading the Chinese government delegation to attend the international conference on the Cambodian issue in Paris.

In a written statement to the press at the airport, he said, "The Chinese government has consistently advocated for an early, just, and reasonable political solution to the Cambodian issue and has made persistent efforts to this end."

Foreign Minister Qian believes that "the task of this international conference is to achieve a comprehensive, just, and reasonable political solution to the Cambodian issue." He added, "The success of the conference depends on reaching a comprehensive agreement on the complete withdrawal of Vietnamese troops from Cambodia under effective international supervision, as well as maintaining peace in Cambodia after the withdrawal, preventing civil war, and promoting national reconciliation."

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, July 30th.

Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen, who is leading the Chinese delegation to the Paris international conference on the Cambodian issue, met with Soviet Foreign Minister Eduard Shevardnadze this afternoon at the Chinese Embassy in France.

Both sides expressed their welcome for the convening of the Paris international conference on the Cambodian issue.

Foreign Minister Qian agreed with the Soviet Foreign Minister's statement that the situation in Afghanistan should not be repeated in Cambodia.

The two foreign ministers also discussed bilateral relations. Foreign Minister Qian said that although the high-level meeting between China and the Soviet Union was disrupted, the significant achievements it made were not affected. Both foreign ministers expressed satisfaction with the development of relations between the two countries following the high-level meeting between their leaders.

### 钱外长抵巴黎出席柬问题国际会议  会晤苏联外长谢瓦尔德纳泽
张启华  吕网大
1989-07-30
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    钱外长抵巴黎出席柬问题国际会议
    会晤苏联外长谢瓦尔德纳泽
    本报巴黎7月30日电　记者张启华、吕网大报道：我国政府代表团团长、外交部长钱其琛今天中午从津巴布韦飞抵巴黎。先期到达巴黎的外交部副部长刘述卿、驻法大使周觉两位副团长到机场迎接。
    钱其琛外长是率领中国政府代表团出席关于柬埔寨问题的国际会议而来巴黎的。
    他在机场对新闻界发表书面谈话说，“中国政府一贯主张早日公正合理地政治解决柬埔寨问题，并且为此进行了长期不懈的努力”。
    钱外长认为，“这次国际会议的任务是全面公正合理地政治解决柬埔寨问题”。“会议的成功取决于就越南在切实有效的国际监督下从柬埔寨真正全部撤军，以及在越南撤军后在柬埔寨保持和平、防止内战、促进民族和解达成一项全面协议”。
    本报巴黎7月30日电　记者张启华、吕网大报道：出席巴黎柬埔寨问题国际会议的我国代表团团长、外交部长钱其琛今天下午在中国驻法大使馆会见了苏联外长谢瓦尔德纳泽。
    双方都表示欢迎巴黎柬埔寨问题国际会议的召开。
    钱外长表示，同意苏联外长说的要防止阿富汗的情况在柬埔寨重演。
    两国外长还谈到了双边关系问题。钱外长说，尽管中苏高级会晤受到干扰，但它取得的重大成果并未因此受到影响。两国外长都表示，对中苏领导人高级会晤之后两国关系的发展表示满意。
