Title: Ten Architectural Projects Selected as Outstanding Works of the 1980s
Author: Lǐ Āndìng
Date: 1989-07-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 6th

The selection of "Outstanding Architectural Works of the 1980s in China" was announced today in Beijing. Ten architectural projects were awarded. The winning works are: China International Exhibition Center (Beijing), Memorial Hall of the Victims in Nanjing Massacre by Japanese Invaders (Nanjing), Fujian Wuyishan Resort, Shenzhen Gymnasium, Dunhuang Airport Terminal, Xinjiang Welcome Hotel, Shanghai East China Power Management Building, Shanghai Longhua Hotel, Qufu Que Li Guesthouse, and Beijing Stepped Garden Residential Complex.

This selection was organized by the China Academy of Art, the Contemporary Chinese Architecture Culture Salon, and the Chinese Environmental Art Society to commemorate the July 1st World Architecture Day.

### 十个建筑项目入选八十年代优秀作品
李安定
1989-07-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    十个建筑项目入选八十年代优秀作品
    据新华社北京7月6日电（记者李安定）“中国80年代建筑艺术优秀作品”评选活动今天在北京揭晓，10个建筑项目获奖。这些获奖作品是：中国国际展览中心（北京）、侵华日军南京大屠杀遇难同胞纪念馆（南京）、福建武夷山庄、深圳体育馆、敦煌机场航站楼、新疆迎宾馆、上海华东电业管理大楼、上海龙柏饭店、曲阜阙里宾舍、北京台阶式花园住宅。
    这次评选是我国建筑业为纪念7月1日世界建筑节而由中国艺术研究院、中国当代建筑文化沙龙、中国环境艺术学会联合主办的。
