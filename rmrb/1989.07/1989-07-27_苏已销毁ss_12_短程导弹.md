Title: The Soviet Union Has Destroyed SS-12 Short-Range Missiles
Author:
Date: 1989-07-27
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency,

The Soviet Union announced on July 26 that, in accordance with the Soviet-American Intermediate-Range Nuclear Forces (INF) Treaty, it has completed the destruction of its SS-12 short-range missiles.

### 苏已销毁SS—12短程导弹
