Title: UK and US Plan to Develop New Missile
Author:
Date: 1989-07-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, London, July 2nd.

According to the latest issue of the magazine "New Scientist," the United Kingdom and the United States are planning to develop a new air-to-ground tactical missile, designated TASM.

This missile is designed to carry a nuclear warhead and can be launched from a fighter aircraft, with a range of 600 kilometers. The TASM will not be subject to the constraints of the U.S.-Soviet Intermediate-Range Nuclear Forces (INF) Treaty, as the treaty only covers missiles launched from land.

### 英美计划研制新导弹

1989-07-03
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    英美计划研制新导弹
    新华社伦敦7月2日电　据最新一期《新科学家》杂志报道，英国和美国计划研制新的空对地战术导弹T A S M。
    这种导弹携带核弹头，可从战斗机上发射，射程600公里。T A S M将不受美苏关于中、短程核武器条约的约束，因为条约只包括从陆地发射的导弹。
