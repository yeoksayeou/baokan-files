Title: Japan's Food Self-Sufficiency Rate Only 30%
Author:
Date: 1989-07-20
Edition: 3

Japan's food self-sufficiency rate is only 30 percent. According to statistics recently released by Japan's Ministry of Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries, Japan's food self-sufficiency rate for the 1987 fiscal year was only 30 percent, down one percentage point from the 31 percent recorded between 1984 and 1986.

### 日本粮食自给率仅三成

1989-07-20
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    日本粮食自给率仅三成
    据日本农林省不久前公布的统计资料，1987年度日本的粮食自给率仅为30％，比1984年度至1986年度的31％又减少一个百分点。
            （据新华社）
