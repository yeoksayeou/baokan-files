Title: Retired Teacher Breeds High-Yield Potato Variety
Author: Zheng Weiguo
Date: 1989-07-28
Page: 5

Retired teacher Liu Zhishan from Changtu County, Liaoning Province, has successfully bred a high-yield potato variety. He established the Dawa Township Farmers' Potato Research Association, transforming the township into a renowned seed potato base known throughout the province and beyond. The photograph was taken by Zheng Weiguo.
