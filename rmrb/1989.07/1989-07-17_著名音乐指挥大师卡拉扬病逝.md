Title: Renowned Conductor Herbert von Karajan Passes Away
Author: Jiang Jian Guo
Date: 1989-07-17
Edition: 3

The renowned conductor Herbert von Karajan passed away yesterday afternoon at 1:30 PM in his home near Salzburg, Austria, due to heart failure. He was 81 years old.

Karajan was born on April 5, 1908, in Salzburg to a family of doctors. At the age of 30, in 1938, he conducted performances of Beethoven's "Fidelio" and Wagner's "Tristan and Isolde" at the Berlin State Opera. In 1955, he became the lifelong chief conductor of the famous Berlin Philharmonic Orchestra. He also served as the artistic director of the Vienna State Opera, the chief conductor of the London Symphony Orchestra, and the artistic director of the Salzburg Music Festival.

### 著名音乐指挥大师卡拉扬病逝
江建国
1989-07-17
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    著名音乐指挥大师卡拉扬病逝
    本报波恩7月17日电　记者江建国报道：当代最著名的音乐指挥大师赫伯特·冯·卡拉扬，昨天下午13时30分因心脏病在奥地利萨尔茨堡附近的家中去世，享年81岁。
    卡拉扬1908年4月5日生于萨尔茨堡一个医生之家。1938年当他只有30岁时，在柏林国家歌剧院先后指挥演奏贝多芬的《菲德里奥》和瓦格纳的《特里斯坦和伊索尔德》。1955年他就任著名的柏林交响乐团的终身首席指挥。他还同时担任过维也纳国家歌剧院的艺术指导、伦敦交响乐团的首席指挥和萨尔茨堡音乐节艺术指导。
