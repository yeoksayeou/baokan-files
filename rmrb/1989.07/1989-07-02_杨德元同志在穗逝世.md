Title: Comrade Yang Deyuan Passes Away in Guangzhou
Author: Zhong Yuming
Date: 1989-07-02
Edition: 2

Comrade Yang Deyuan, a member of the Seventh National People's Congress, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial Advisory Committee of the Communist Party of China, former Deputy Governor of the Provincial Government, and Chairman of the Board of Guangdong Development Bank, passed away in Guangzhou on June 15 due to a heart attack. He was 67 years old.

The memorial service for Comrade Yang Deyuan was held in Guangzhou on June 24. The service was presided over by Wang Ning, Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial Advisory Committee of the Communist Party of China. Guo Rongchang, Deputy Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, delivered the eulogy. Comrade Yang Deyuan joined the Communist Party of China in 1937. From 1981 onwards, he held positions including Deputy Governor and Secretary-General of Guangdong Province, and Chairman of the Board of Guangdong Development Bank.

### 杨德元同志在穗逝世
钟玉明
1989-07-02
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    杨德元同志在穗逝世
    新华社广州电　（记者钟玉明）七届全国人大代表、中共广东省顾问委员会副主任、原省政府常务副省长、广东发展银行董事长杨德元同志，因患心肌梗塞，于6月15日在穗逝世，终年67岁。
    杨德元同志追悼会6月24日在广州市举行。追悼会由中共广东省顾问委员会主任王宁主持，中共广东省委副书记郭荣昌致悼词。杨德元同志1937年加入中国共产党。1981年后，历任广东省常务副省长兼秘书长、广东发展银行董事长等职务。
