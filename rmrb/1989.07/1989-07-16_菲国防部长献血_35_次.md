Title: Philippine Defense Minister Donates Blood for the 35th Time
Author:
Date: 1989-07-16
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Manila, July 14th

Philippine Defense Secretary General Fidel V. Ramos donated blood at the Philippine Red Cross headquarters on July 14th. This was his 35th time donating blood.

The 61-year-old General Ramos is also the vice-chairman of the Blood Donors Club. Today, he led more than 200 volunteers to the blood donation station to support the Philippine Red Cross's blood donation week activities.

At the award ceremony for outstanding blood donors, the Red Cross leaders highly praised Ramos' humanitarian spirit.

The Philippine Red Cross holds a blood donation week every year. This year's blood donation week will end on July 17th.

### 菲国防部长献血35次

1989-07-16
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    菲国防部长献血35次
    新华社马尼拉7月14日电　菲律宾国防部部长拉莫斯将军14日在菲律宾红十字会总会献血站献了血。这是他第35次献血。
    今年61岁的拉莫斯将军也是血库俱乐部副主席。他今天带领200多名志愿者前往献血站献血，以实际行动支持红十字会举办的献血周活动。
    在向贡献突出的献血者颁奖仪式上，红十字会领导人高度赞扬了拉莫斯的人道主义精神。
    菲律宾红十字会每年举办一次献血周活动。今年的献血周活动将于7月17日结束。
