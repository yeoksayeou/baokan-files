Title: Argentina's New President Plays Soccer with Maradona and Other Stars
Author: Lǐ Zhìmíng
Date: 1989-07-28
Edition: 4

Argentina's new President Menem, who has been in office for just two weeks, donned the number 5 jersey and played a match with Maradona and other famous soccer stars on the evening of July 21.

The Argentine national team has recently performed poorly in the Copa America, drawing criticism from the public. The president's participation in the game has boosted the morale of the team, which is now regrouping.

### 阿根廷新总统和马拉多纳等球星比赛
李志明
1989-07-28
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    阿根廷新总统和马拉多纳等球星比赛
    本报布宜诺斯艾利斯电  记者李志明报道：就职刚刚两个星期的阿根廷新总统梅内姆7月21日晚身穿5号球衣，与马拉多纳等著名球星联袂踢了一场比赛。
    阿根廷国家队最近在美洲杯比赛中战绩不佳，受到国人批评。总统和他们一起踢球，使正在重整旗鼓的他们受到了鼓舞。
