Title: Zhu Liang Leaves Beijing for a Visit to Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1989-07-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 10th.

At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, Zhu Liang, a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the head of the International Liaison Department of the CPC Central Committee, departed Beijing this afternoon by airplane for a visit to Mongolia.

### 朱良离京前往蒙古访问

1989-07-10
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    朱良离京前往蒙古访问
    新华社北京7月10日电　应蒙古人民革命党中央委员会的邀请，中共中央委员、中共中央对外联络部部长朱良今天下午乘飞机离京前往蒙古访问。
