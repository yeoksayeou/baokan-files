Title: Chinese Athletes Break World Youth Shooting Records
Author: Wáng Wénjùn
Date: 1989-07-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, July 13th.

At the shooting preliminaries of the National Youth Games held in Chengdu, one athlete broke and another tied the men's slow-fire pistol world youth record.

On the 12th, 17-year-old Zhang Ming from Jilin Province scored 573 rings, surpassing the 569 rings set by Hungarian shooter Pepanito in 1986, breaking the world youth record for men's slow-fire pistol. Shaanxi Province's 20-year-old Sun Baotang tied this world youth record with a score of 569 rings. Jilin Province's 20-year-old Xu Dan also broke the national youth record for this event, which was previously 560 rings.

### 我选手超一项世界青少年射击纪录
王文俊
1989-07-13
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    我选手超一项世界青少年射击纪录
    新华社成都7月13日电　（记者王文俊）成都举行的全国青运会射击预赛，有一人超过、一人平男子手枪慢射世界青少年纪录。
    吉林省17岁的臧明12日以573环的成绩，超过了匈牙利选手佩帕尼托在1986年创造的男子手枪慢射569环的世界青少年纪录。陕西省20岁的孙宝堂以569环平了这项世界青少年纪录。吉林省20岁的徐丹也破了我国这个项目560环的全国青少年纪录。
