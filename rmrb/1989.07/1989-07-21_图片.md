Title: Singapore's Urban Development and Environmental Beautification
Author:
Date: 1989-07-21
Page: 7

While Singapore is engaged in urban construction, it places great emphasis on environmental beautification, mobilizing every household to plant flowers and grass. As a result, Singapore's air is fresh, and it is renowned worldwide as a "garden city."

Xinhua News Agency
