Title: The U.S. Formulates a New Strategy Against the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1989-07-25
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 24th.

According to a report by the American *Los Angeles Times* on the 23rd, the U.S. government is formulating a new strategy aimed at paralyzing the Soviet Union's war machine within hours of the outbreak of a conflict.

This new strategy will include hypersonic stealth reconnaissance aircraft, remote sensing devices hidden within the Soviet Union, and reconnaissance satellites equipped with sensors sensitive enough to distinguish objects as small as 8 centimeters on the ground. Additionally, the U.S. will develop a new weapon powerful enough to penetrate the deepest underground bunkers in the Soviet Union, capable of eliminating all Soviet leaders hiding there within hours of the outbreak of war.

### 美制订针对苏联新战略

1989-07-25
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美制订针对苏联新战略
    新华社北京7月24日电　据美国《洛杉矶时报》23日报道，美国政府正在制订一项旨在使苏联的战争机器在冲突爆发后数小时便处于瘫痪状态的新战略。
    这项新战略将包括：四倍于音速的隐形侦察机、隐藏在苏联境内的遥感装置和性触敏感到可以分辨地面上8厘米大小物体的侦察卫星。此外，美还将发展一种威力强大到完全能穿透苏联最深的地下堡垒、在战争爆发后数小时便把隐藏在那里的苏联领导人全部消灭的新武器。
