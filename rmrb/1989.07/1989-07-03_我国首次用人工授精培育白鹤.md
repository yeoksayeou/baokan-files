Title: China Successfully Breeds White Cranes Using Artificial Insemination for the First Time
Author: Ou Liang
Date: 1989-07-03
Edition: 2

Two adorable baby white cranes hatched in early and mid-June at the Beijing Zoo. This marks China's first successful breeding of white crane chicks using artificial insemination. This achievement not only fills a gap in domestic animal research but also places China at the forefront globally.

### 我国首次用人工授精培育白鹤
瓯亮
1989-07-03
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    我国首次用人工授精培育白鹤
    本报讯　今年6月上旬和中旬，两只可爱的小白鹤在北京动物园破壳降生。这是我国第1次用人工授精办法培育成功的白鹤幼体。这不但填补了国内动物科研的一项空白，在世界上也居领先地位。
                                        （瓯亮）
