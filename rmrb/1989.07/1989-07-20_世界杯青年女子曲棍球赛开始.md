Title: The First World Cup Youth Women's Field Hockey Tournament Begins
Author: Zhang Yuanting
Date: 1989-07-20
Edition: 4

The first World Cup Youth Women's Field Hockey Tournament began today in Ottawa. The Chinese team won their first match against the Canadian team with a score of 1:0.

A total of 12 teams are participating in this tournament: West Germany, New Zealand, the United States, the Soviet Union, South Korea, Argentina, Britain, the Netherlands, Chile, Zimbabwe, Canada, and China. The competition starts with a round-robin format. In the coming days, the Chinese team will face the United States, the Soviet Union, West Germany, and New Zealand in Group A.

Field hockey is a relatively new sport in China. Last year, the Chinese women's youth team finished second in the Asian qualifying tournament held in New Delhi, India, securing a spot in the final round of this World Cup along with the South Korean team.

Yesterday afternoon, Governor General Jean Sauvé attended the opening ceremony of this tournament, which was held on Parliament Hill.

### 世界杯青年女子曲棍球赛开始
张元庭
1989-07-20
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    世界杯青年女子曲棍球赛开始
    新华社渥太华7月19日电  （记者张元庭）中国队今天在这里开始的第一届世界杯青年女子曲棍球赛首场比赛中，以1∶0战胜加拿大队。
    参加这次杯赛的共有12支球队，它们是联邦德国、新西兰、美国、苏联、南朝鲜、阿根廷、英国、荷兰、智利、津巴布韦、加拿大和中国队。比赛先进行分组循环赛。在今后几天里，中国队还将同A组中的美国、苏联、联邦德国、新西兰等队交锋。
    曲棍球在中国是一个新兴的运动项目。去年中国女子青年队在印度新德里举行的亚洲区预选赛中，获得第二名，与南朝鲜队同时出线，取得进入这次杯赛决赛阶段比赛资格。
    昨天中午，加拿大总督让·索维出席了在议会山举行的本届杯赛的开幕式。
