Title: Foreign Affairs Brief
Author:
Date: 1989-07-13
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency,

Vice Premier Yao Yilin met with a delegation led by Rayes, Chairman of the Rayes Group from Kuwait, on July 12 at the Great Hall of the People. The Rayes Group is a multinational conglomerate comprising over a dozen companies from the Gulf countries. Rayes' visit aims to understand China's current political and economic situation, inspect the progress of the joint venture project with China International Trust and Investment Corporation (CITIC) Pacific Limited— the Riverside Garden Apartment Complex, and explore further cooperation possibilities with relevant Chinese departments.

During the meeting, Rayes expressed his appreciation for the friendship and cooperation with China, hoping to expedite the completion of the ongoing joint venture project to showcase the fruits of Sino-Kuwaiti cooperation. Yao Yilin stated that countries with different social systems can indeed achieve successful cooperation. China and the Kuwaiti government have already engaged in some fruitful collaborations, and cooperation with the private sector in Kuwait has also begun. He believes that cooperation between the two governments and their peoples will continue to develop.

State Councilor and Minister of Education Li Tieying met with Shukshunov, Deputy Minister of the Soviet State Committee for Education, at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse on July 13. Both sides exchanged views on strengthening educational cooperation between China and the Soviet Union. Li Tieying told the guest that sending students abroad for further studies is an important part of China's reform and opening-up policy, and this policy will be maintained for the long term. Shukshunov's delegation came to attend the third meeting of the China-Soviet Union Education Cooperation Working Group.

State Councilor and Minister of Science and Technology Song Jian led a Chinese government science and technology delegation to Moscow on July 13 to attend the opening ceremony of the China Science and Technology Day and to conduct visits. The China Science and Technology Day will be held from July 14 to 23 at the Soviet Exhibition of National Economic Achievements.

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Rong Yiren met with a delegation from the Iran Chamber of Commerce, Industries, and Mines led by Chairman Hamershi in Beijing on July 13.

### 外事简讯

1989-07-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲国务院副总理姚依林12日在人民大会堂会见科威特雷耶斯集团董事长雷耶斯一行。
    雷耶斯集团是由海湾各国10余家公司组成的跨国集团。雷耶斯此次来访旨在了解中国目前的政治、经济形势，视察与中信保利科技有限公司合资的项目——滨河花园公寓工程的进展情况，并与中国有关部门探讨进一步合作的可能性。
    会见时，雷耶斯表示珍视同中国的友谊与合作，希望尽快完成正在进行的合资项目，让世人看到中科人民合作的成果。姚依林说，不同社会制度的国家完全可以进行成功的合作。中国与科威特政府已进行一些卓有成效的合作，与科私营企业界的合作也有了开端。他相信两国政府间及民间的合作均将发展。
    ▲国务委员兼国家教委主任李铁映13日下午在钓鱼台国宾馆会见了苏联国家教委副主任舒克舒诺夫，宾主就加强中苏两国在教育方面的合作问题交换了意见。李铁映对客人说，派遣留学人员出国进修学习是中国改革开放政策的重要组成部分，这项方针政策将长期坚持下去。舒克舒诺夫一行是前来参加中苏教育合作工作小组第三次会议的。
    ▲国务委员兼国家科委主任宋健13日率中国政府科技代表团离京赴莫斯科出席中国科技日开幕式，并参观访问。中国科技日7月14日至23日在苏联国民经济成就展览馆举行。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长、全国工商联合会主席荣毅仁13日下午在北京会见了由主席哈默希为团长的伊朗商、工、矿业会代表团。
    （据新华社）
