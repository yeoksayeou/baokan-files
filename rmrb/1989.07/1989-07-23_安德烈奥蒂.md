Title: Giulio Andreotti
Author: Jin Biao
Date: 1989-07-23
Page: 3

Giulio Andreotti, the new Prime Minister of the Italian government, was born on January 14, 1919, in Rome. He graduated from the Faculty of Law in 1941 and is one of the founders of the Christian Democratic Party.

In 1946, he was elected as a member of the Constituent Assembly of Italy and has since been continuously re-elected as a member of the House of Representatives for 10 terms. He has served as the minister of seven departments, including the Interior, Finance, Treasury, Defense, Industry, Budget, and Foreign Affairs, in 20 Italian governments.

From February 1972 to June 1973 and from July 1976 to March 1979, he served as the Prime Minister of the Italian government for five terms. During his tenure as the Italian Foreign Minister, he visited China on multiple occasions.

### 安德烈奥蒂
晋标
1989-07-23
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：人物介绍

    安德烈奥蒂
    意大利政府新任总理朱利奥·安德烈奥蒂，1919年1月14日生于罗马，1941年毕业于法律系。天民党创始人之一。
    1946年当选意大利制宪议会议员，此后一直连续当选10届众议员至今。在意大利20届政府中历任内政、财政、国库、国防、工业、预算、外交等7个部的部长。
    1972年2月至1973年6月、1976年7月至1979年3月曾先后担任5届意大利政府总理。
    安德烈奥蒂任意大利政府外交部长期间曾多次访华。　·晋标·
