Title: Utilizing Tidal Flats for Aquaculture
Author: Tao Junfeng
Date: 1989-07-24
Edition: 5

To better fill the "basket of vegetables" and improve the living standards of the people, various districts and counties in Jinan City have fully utilized the Yellow River tidal flats and low-lying areas to develop freshwater aquaculture over 30,000 mu (approximately 2,000 hectares), producing 2.5 million kilograms of fresh fish annually. This is a tilapia fingerling breeding base in Changqing County, which can produce 600,000 tilapia fingerlings each year.

### 利用滩涂发展养鱼（图片）
陶峻峰
1989-07-24
第5版(国内专页（经济）)
专栏：新闻摄影比赛

    利用滩涂发展养鱼
    为了更好地充实菜篮子，提高人民的生活水平，济南市各区、县充分利用黄河滩涂及涝洼地，发展淡水养鱼3万亩，年产250万公斤鲜鱼。这是长清县的一个罗非鱼育苗基地，每年可育60万尾罗非鱼苗。
　陶峻峰摄
