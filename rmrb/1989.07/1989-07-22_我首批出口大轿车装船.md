Title: China's First Batch of Exported Large Sedans Shipped
Author: Zhou Ciping
Date: 1989-07-22
Edition: 2

    China's first batch of exported large sedans is being loaded onto ships today in Guangzhou and will soon be sent to Manila. This is the first time China has exported a large number of large sedans overseas. If the vehicles operate normally in Manila, the Philippines will continue to import from China.

### 我首批出口大轿车装船
周慈朴
1989-07-22
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    我首批出口大轿车装船
    据新华社北京7月22日电  （记者周慈朴）中国向菲律宾出口的145辆大轿车今天开始在广州装船，不久将运往马尼拉。这是中国首次大量向国外出口大轿车。如果车辆在马尼拉运行正常，菲律宾还将继续从中国进口。
