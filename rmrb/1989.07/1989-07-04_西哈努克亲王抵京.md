Title: Prince Norodom Sihanouk Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-07-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 4th.

Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Chairman of the Democratic Kampuchea, and his wife arrived in Beijing by airplane from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea this noon. Deputy Foreign Minister Liu Shucai and the Democratic Kampuchea Ambassador to China, Jiang Yulang, were among those who greeted them at the airport.

### 西哈努克亲王抵京

1989-07-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王抵京
    新华社北京7月4日电　民主柬埔寨主席诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和夫人今天中午乘飞机由朝鲜民主主义人民共和国抵达北京。外交部副部长刘述卿以及民主柬埔寨驻华大使江裕朗等到机场迎接。
