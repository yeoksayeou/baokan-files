Title: General Secretary Jiang Zemin Meets with Romanian and Ethiopian Guests
Author:
Date: 1989-07-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 10th.

General Secretary Jiang Zemin met with Romanian and Ethiopian guests yesterday.

People's Daily, Beijing, July 10th—CPC Central Committee General Secretary Jiang Zemin met with Nicolae Constantin, a member of the Romanian Communist Party (RCP) Central Committee and Chairman of the Central Control Commission, this afternoon in Zhongnanhai. The two sides engaged in an in-depth conversation in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

Jiang Zemin warmly welcomed Comrade Nicolae Constantin's visit to China. Nicolae Constantin conveyed Comrade Ceausescu's good wishes to General Secretary Jiang Zemin. He said that Ceausescu expressed that Romania and the Romanian people are and will remain loyal friends of the Chinese Party and the Chinese people. Jiang Zemin thanked Comrade Ceausescu for his congratulations on his election as General Secretary and for the RCP's understanding and support of the Chinese Party during its temporary difficulties. Jiang Zemin asked Nicolae Constantin to convey his and the Party leadership's cordial greetings and best wishes to Comrade Ceausescu, wishing him good health and greater achievements for the RCP and the Romanian people in their socialist construction.

Jiang Zemin pointed out that the CPC attaches great importance to Sino-Romanian friendship and will, as always, strengthen the friendly and cooperative relations between the two parties, the two countries, and their peoples.

Jiang Zemin briefly introduced China's current situation to the guest. He emphasized that the Party will continue to adhere to the basic line of the Party, uphold the Four Cardinal Principles, and persist with reform and opening up.

Nicolae Constantin said that the RCP has always believed that the Chinese people led by the CPC will be able to solve their own problems. The RCP highly evaluates the resolutions of the 14th Plenary Session of the 13th CPC Central Committee. He also reported on the recent meeting of the RCP Central Committee.

Nicolae Constantin and his delegation arrived in Beijing yesterday after visiting the Democratic People's Republic of Korea for a short visit to China.

People's Daily, Beijing, July 10th—CPC Central Committee General Secretary Jiang Zemin met with the Ethiopian Workers' Party delegation led by Politburo member and Central Committee Secretary Smolys Marengia this afternoon in Zhongnanhai.

Jiang Zemin warmly welcomed Comrade Smolys and his delegation's visit to China. He said that the two parties, the two countries, and the peoples of China and Ethiopia have deep traditional friendships and good cooperative relations. Mengistu's two visits to China laid a solid foundation for the development of Sino-Ethiopian friendship and the friendly cooperative relations between the two countries and the two parties. Recently, when I was elected as General Secretary, Comrade Mengistu also sent a warm congratulatory message. Please convey my thanks and greetings to him.

### 江泽民总书记昨天分别会见罗马尼亚和埃塞俄比亚客人

1989-07-10
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    江泽民总书记昨天分别会见罗马尼亚和埃塞俄比亚客人
    本报北京7月10日讯　中共中央总书记江泽民今天下午在中南海会见了罗共中央执委、中央检察委员会主席尼古拉·康斯坦丁。双方在亲切、友好的气氛中进行了深入的交谈。
    江泽民热烈欢迎康斯坦丁同志来华访问。康斯坦丁转达了齐奥塞斯库同志对江泽民总书记的良好祝愿。他说，齐奥塞斯库表示，罗共和罗马尼亚人民现在和今后都是中国党和中国人民的忠实朋友。江泽民感谢齐奥塞斯库同志对他当选总书记的祝贺和罗马尼亚共产党在中国遇到暂时困难的时刻对中国党的理解和支持。江泽民请康斯坦丁同志转达我党领导人和他本人对齐奥塞斯库同志的亲切问候和最良好的祝愿，祝齐奥塞斯库身体健康，祝罗马尼亚共产党和罗马尼亚人民在社会主义建设事业中取得更大的成就。
    江泽民指出，中国共产党重视中罗友谊，将一如既往地加强中罗两党、两国和两国人民的友好、合作关系。
    江泽民向客人扼要介绍了中国目前的形势。他强调指出，我党将继续贯彻执行党的基本路线，坚持四项基本原则，坚持改革开放。
    尼古拉·康斯坦丁表示，罗共始终认为，中国共产党领导的中国人民会解决好自己的事情。罗共高度评价中共十三届四中全会的各项决议。他还通报了不久前召开的罗共中央全会的情况。
    尼古拉·康斯坦丁一行是在访问朝鲜民主主义人民共和国之后于昨天来到北京，对我国进行短暂访问的。
    本报北京7月10日讯　中共中央总书记江泽民今天下午在中南海会见了由政治局委员、中央书记施莫利斯·马仁吉亚率领的埃塞俄比亚工人党代表团。
    江泽民热烈欢迎施莫利斯同志率团来华访问。他说，中埃两党、两国和两国人民有着深厚的传统友谊和良好的合作关系。门格斯图同志两次访华为中埃友谊和两国、两党的友好合作关系的发展打下了坚实的基础。最近，在我当选为总书记的时候，门格斯图总书记又给我发来了充满热情的贺电。请转达我对他的感谢和问候。
    江泽民说，我非常高兴地看到，我们两党的友好合作关系已进入稳定的发展时期。我们相信，通过相互往来，经常互通情况和交换意见，我们两党、两国的友好合作关系一定会不断得到发展。
    施莫利斯表示，埃塞俄比亚工人党重视同中国党的友好关系。这次来访有机会了解更多的情况。我们高兴地看到，中国的局势得到了控制。
