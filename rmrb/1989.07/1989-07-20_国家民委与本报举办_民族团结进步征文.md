Title: State Ethnic Affairs Commission and the People's Daily Jointly Hold "Ethnic Unity and Progress" Essay Contest
Author:
Date: 1989-07-20
Page: 1

To celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and to fully publicize the development and changes in ethnic regions, the State Ethnic Affairs Commission has decided to jointly hold a "Ethnic Unity and Progress" essay contest with the People's Daily. The essay contest announcement from the People's Daily is published on page five today. The State Ethnic Affairs Commission will also hold a similar themed essay contest with the Central People's Broadcasting Station, which will begin broadcasting in September in the "National Family" program.

### 国家民委与本报举办  民族团结进步征文

1989-07-20
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    国家民委与本报举办
    民族团结进步征文
    本报讯　为了庆祝建国40周年，充分宣传民族地区的发展和变化，国家民委决定与人民日报举行“民族团结进步征文”活动。本报征文启事见今日五版。国家民委还与中央人民广播电台举行同一主题的征文，9月份开始在“民族大家庭”节目中播出。
