Title: Moroccan Long-Distance Runner Bouta Wins 5000m Championship
Author:
Date: 1989-07-28
Edition: 4

Moroccan long-distance running star Bouta won the men's 5000-meter championship today at the third International Athletics Meet held in La Coruna, Spain, with a time of 13 minutes and 12.10 seconds.

### 摩洛哥长跑名将布泰获5000米冠军

1989-07-28
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    摩洛哥长跑名将布泰获5000米冠军
    新华社马德里7月25日电　摩洛哥长跑名将布泰今天在西班牙拉科鲁尼亚举行的第三届国际田径赛上，以13分12秒10的成绩获得男子5000米冠军。
