Title: Asian Zone of World Cup Football to Abolish Home and Away Format
Author:
Date: 1989-07-07
Edition: 4

The Asian zone of the 1990 World Cup football qualifiers will no longer feature home and away matches. Instead, the second round of matches will be concentrated in a neutral city.

The International Federation of Association Football (FIFA) announced this decision in Zurich today. The aim is to avoid the inconvenience caused by transportation difficulties.

The specific location for the concentrated matches has not yet been determined. FIFA will make the final decision at a meeting in Pattaya, Thailand.

The first round of the Asian zone qualifiers is nearing its end. Qatar, Saudi Arabia, South Korea, and the United Arab Emirates have already secured their spots. The remaining two qualifying spots will be decided between Japan or the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and China or Iran.

### 世界杯足球赛亚洲区将取消主客场制

1989-07-07
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    世界杯足球赛亚洲区将取消主客场制
    新华社日内瓦7月6日电　国际足联发言人今天在苏黎世宣布，亚洲地区的1990年世界杯足球预赛第二轮比赛将集中在一个中立城市进行，而不搞主客制的来回赛。
    集中比赛的目的是为了避免交通困难给比赛带来不便。
    新的比赛地点尚未确定。国际足联将在泰国的帕塔亚举行会议做出最后决定。
    亚洲区预选赛第一轮分组赛已接近尾声，卡塔尔、沙特阿拉伯、南朝鲜和阿联酋4个队已经出线。另两个出线队将在日本队或朝鲜民主主义人民共和国队、中国队或伊朗队之间产生。
