Title: The Ministry of Civil Affairs Issues Notice: Strengthen Military Support and Preferential Treatment for Dependents During the August 1st Period
Author: Zhou Dinghua
Date: 1989-07-07
Edition: 4

The Ministry of Civil Affairs issued a notice on July 6, requiring all levels of civil affairs departments to carry out military support and preferential treatment for dependents in a solid and practical manner during this year's August 1st Army Day.

The notice requires that, in conjunction with the study and implementation of the spirit of the Fourth Plenary Session of the Thirteenth Central Committee, various forms should be used to vigorously publicize the great achievements of the People's Liberation Army in stopping the turmoil and suppressing the counter-revolutionary rebellion, as well as their moving deeds of supporting the government and loving the people. Local governments and grassroots organizations at all levels should, in accordance with the principles of enthusiasm, frugality, and practicality, organize visits and condolence activities. They should do good deeds for the army, families of revolutionary martyrs and military personnel, revolutionary disabled veterans, and retired military cadres. At the same time, they should earnestly implement various preferential treatment and resettlement policies.

The notice points out that during the August 1st period, various places can select and commend a batch of advanced units and individuals in military support and preferential treatment for dependents. In particular, those who, during the period of stopping the turmoil and suppressing the counter-revolutionary rebellion, bravely and wisely rescued the People's Liberation Army (armed police) soldiers, protected military supplies and equipment, and supported the martial law troops in carrying out their missions, should be commended as advanced units and individuals in patriotism and military support.

### 民政部发出通知  “八一”期间搞好拥军优属
周定华
1989-07-07
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    民政部发出通知
    “八一”期间搞好拥军优属
    本报讯　民政部7月6日发出通知，要求各级民政部门在今年“八一”建军节期间扎扎实实地开展拥军优属活动。
    通知要求，结合学习贯彻党的十三届四中全会精神，利用各种形式，大力宣传人民解放军在制止动乱和平息反革命暴乱中的伟大功绩和拥政爱民的动人事迹；地方各级人民政府和基层组织应本着热烈、节俭、务实的原则，组织走访慰问活动；为部队、烈军属、革命伤残军人、军队离退休干部办好事。同时，要认真落实各项优抚安置政策。
    通知指出，“八一”期间，各地可评选表彰一批拥军优属先进单位和个人。尤其是要表彰那些在制止动乱、平息反革命暴乱期间，不畏艰难、机智勇敢地救护人民解放军（武警）指战员，保护军用物资和装备，支持戒严部队执行任务事迹突出的爱国拥军先进单位和个人。
        （周定华）
