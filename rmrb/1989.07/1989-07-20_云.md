Title: Clouds
Author: Bian Zhongzhou
Date: 1989-07-20
Edition: 8

	Clouds
	Bian Zhongzhou

	The azure sky
	Is like the deep blue sea
	You are the sail
	In the background of the sea
	You are sailing towards me

	Summer is a green leaf
	Hanging down a shade
	Bringing a hint of affection
	You are drifting towards me

	Spring is a butterfly
	Your colors
	Remind me of her youthful figure

	Last night, I had a beautiful dream
	Blushing into a red dawn...

### 云
卞中洲
1989-07-20
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

    云
    卞中洲
    湛蓝的天宇
    像碧绿的大海
    你　是海的背景下
    衬托的帆影
    你向我驶来
    夏天是一片绿叶
    垂下一片荫凉
    送来一丝情意
    你向我飘来
    春天是一只蝴蝶
    你的色彩
    叫人想起她的青春的倩影
    昨夜　我做了一个美好的梦
    羞成你一个鲜红的黎明……
