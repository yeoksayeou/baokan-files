Title: Afghan Guerrillas Retake Gatrag Fort
Author:
Date: 1989-07-25
Edition: 3

Afghan Guerrillas Retake Gatrag Fort

NEW DELHI, July 24 (Xinhua) - According to a report by the Afghan News Agency on July 24, the Afghan guerrillas recaptured the military stronghold of Gatrag from government forces on the night of July 23 and seized 5 tanks.

The Afghan guerrillas had captured the strategic stronghold of Gatrag, south of Jalalabad, on March 6, but it fell on July 7. The guerrillas recaptured it on July 14, but had to withdraw from Gatrag on July 20 due to heavy artillery shelling by government forces.

Since March 6, the guerrillas have been besieging Jalalabad.

### 阿游击队夺回加特拉吉要塞

1989-07-25
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    阿游击队夺回加特拉吉要塞
    新华社伊斯兰堡7月24日电　据阿富汗新闻社24日报道，阿游击队23日晚再次从政府军手中夺回军事要点加特拉吉，并缴获5辆坦克。
    阿富汗游击队曾于3月6日攻克了战略要城贾拉拉巴德南部的加特拉吉据点，但该据点于7月7日失守。游击队又于7月14日夺回了该据点，后在政府军的猛烈炮击下，游击队不得不于20日离弃加特拉吉。
    自3月6日起，游击队一直在围攻贾拉拉巴德。
