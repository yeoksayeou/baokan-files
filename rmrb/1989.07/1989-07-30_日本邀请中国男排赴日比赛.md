Title: Japan Invites Chinese Men's Volleyball Team for Competition in Japan
Author: Jiǎn Zhàn Guó
Date: 1989-07-30
Edition: 4

    Japan Invites Chinese Men's Volleyball Team for Competition in Japan

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo — The Japan Volleyball Association has decided to invite the Chinese, Indian, and Spanish teams to participate in the Nagoya International Volleyball Tournament. The Nagoya International Men's Volleyball Tournament will be held from August 11th to 13th. In addition to the aforementioned three teams, the host team, Japan, will also participate. All four teams are national teams. On August 11th, the Chinese team will play their first match against India.

### 日本邀请中国男排赴日比赛
姜占国
1989-07-30
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    日本邀请中国男排赴日比赛
    新华社东京电　（记者姜占国）日本排球协会已决定邀请中国队、印度队和西班牙队参加名古屋国际排球赛。名古屋国际男排赛将于8月11日至13日举行。除上述3个队外，还有东道主日本队参加。这4个队全是国家队。8月11日中国队首战对印度队。
