Title: Brazilian President Meets Chinese Special Envoy
Author: Lǐ Zhìmíng
Date: 1989-07-08
Page: 3

People's Daily, Buenos Aires, July 7th. Reporter Li Zhiming reports: Brazilian President Sarney, who came to attend the inauguration ceremony of the new Argentine president, met with Chinese Special Envoy Minister Qi Yuanjing this afternoon at the Brazilian Embassy in Argentina.

The meeting was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere. Qi Yuanjing conveyed greetings and good wishes from Chairman Deng Xiaoping and Chairman Yang Shangkun to President Sarney. President Sarney also asked Qi Yuanjing to convey his greetings and good wishes to Chairman Deng, Chairman Yang, and other Chinese leaders. During the conversation, President Sarney happily recalled his friendly visit to China last July. He expressed his confidence in continuing to consolidate and develop the friendly cooperative relations that have already been firmly established between the two countries, unaffected by any external influences.

### 巴西总统会见我特使
李志明
1989-07-08
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    巴西总统会见我特使
    本报布宜诺斯艾利斯7月7日电　记者李志明报道：前来参加阿根廷新总统就职典礼的巴西总统萨尔内今天下午在巴西驻阿使馆会见了中国政府特使戚元靖部长。
    会见在亲切友好的气氛中进行。戚元靖向萨尔内总统转达了邓小平主席、杨尚昆主席的问候和祝愿。萨尔内总统也请戚元靖部长转达他对邓主席、杨主席和其他中国领导人的问候和祝愿。交谈中，巴西总统愉快地回忆了他去年7月对中国的友好访问。他表示将继续巩固和发展两国业已牢固建立起来的友好合作关系而不受任何影响，对此充满信心。
