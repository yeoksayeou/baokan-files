Title: Renowned Cuban Poet Nicolás Guillén Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-07-18
Edition: 3

Renowned Cuban poet Nicolás Guillén passed away due to illness on July 16 in Havana, at the age of 87.

### 古巴著名诗人纪廉逝世

1989-07-18
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    古巴著名诗人纪廉逝世
    古巴国家诗人尼古拉斯·纪廉因病于7月16日在哈瓦那逝世，终年87岁。
