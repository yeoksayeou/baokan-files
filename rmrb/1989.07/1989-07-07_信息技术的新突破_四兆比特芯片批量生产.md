Title: Breakthrough in Information Technology: Mass Production of 4-Megabit Chips
Author: Jiang Jian Guo
Date: 1989-07-07
Edition: 3

    A significant step in the development of the world's information industry—mass production of 4-megabit chips—became a reality yesterday in West Germany. IBM's subsidiary in Sindelfingen, West Germany, officially started production on this line yesterday, marking an important victory for the United States and Europe in their joint efforts to catch up with Japan.

### 信息技术的新突破  四兆比特芯片批量生产
江建国
1989-07-07
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    信息技术的新突破
    四兆比特芯片批量生产
    本报波恩7月4日电　记者江建国报道：世界信息工业发展的重要一步——4兆比特芯片的批量生产昨天首次在西德成为现实。美国国际商用机械公司（IBM）设在西德辛德尔芬根的子公司昨天正式启用这条生产线，从而表明美欧在联合赶超日本方面赢了重要的一分。
