Title: Spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Comments on the ASEAN Foreign Ministers' Meeting Statement
Author:
Date: 1989-07-07
Edition: 2

The spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs said today that the statement issued by the ASEAN foreign ministers' meeting proposed a comprehensive political solution to the Cambodian issue. China appreciates and supports this.

The spokesperson said that China has always advocated for a comprehensive, fair, and reasonable solution to the Cambodian issue to achieve peace in Cambodia and avoid civil war. With the withdrawal of Vietnamese troops, a temporary joint government led by Prince Sihanouk should be established in Cambodia, consisting of the four parties.

### 外交部发言人评东盟外长会议公报

1989-07-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外交部发言人评东盟外长会议公报
    新华社北京7月6日电  外交部发言人今天说，东盟国家外长会议发表的公报中提出要全面地政治解决柬埔寨问题。中国对此表示赞赏支持。
    发言人说，中国一向主张，为了在柬埔寨实现和平、避免内战，应全面、公正、合理地解决柬埔寨问题，随着越南撤军，在柬埔寨成立以西哈努克亲王为首的四方临时联合政府。
