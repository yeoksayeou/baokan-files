	The Art of Color Coordination
	Colors can be divided into cool and warm categories. Cool colors include blue, green, cyan, and purple, which give a sense of coolness and coldness. Warm colors include red, orange, and yellow, which evoke a sense of warmth. Black and white are neutral colors.

	Colors evoke a wide range of associations: Red represents passion, vitality, fire, the sun, and blood. Orange signifies warmth, joy, autumn, and lamplight. Yellow symbolizes brightness, hope, and golden light. Blue conveys tranquility, eternity, rationality, depth, the ocean, the sky, and water. Purple embodies elegance, nobility, mystery, lilac, and roses. Black represents solemnity, strength, fear, steadfastness, and night. White signifies purity, hygiene, sanctity, light, snow, and clouds.

	In winter, bedroom colors should primarily be warm. Bed sheets can be red, orange, or warm coffee tones. Pillowcases should harmonize with the bed sheets, using light red, yellow-green, or yellow-orange. Comforters and blankets should be yellow-green, purple-red, coffee, or brown. Lampshades should be cream, light red, or orange-yellow. Window curtains should not be too dark in color.

	In summer, indoor colors should lean towards cool tones like light blue and green. Bed linens should be white, green, light yellow, or light green. Curtains should be in a light, green hue to create a serene, comfortable, and cool atmosphere. Additionally, placing some green plants can make the environment more lively.

	(From the July 2nd edition of the Xinjiang Daily, by Huang Zhi Chang)

### 色彩搭配的艺术
黄炽昌
1989-07-15
第8版(每周文摘)
专栏：

    色彩搭配的艺术
    色彩可分冷色和暖色两种。冷色指蓝、绿、青、紫等使人感觉清凉、寒冷的颜色；暖色包括红、橙、黄等使人产生温暖的颜色。黑和白两色为中性色。
    色彩会给人带来广泛的联想：红——热情、活力、火、太阳、血。橙——温和、欢喜、秋色、灯火。黄——光明、希望、黄金光。蓝——平静、永恒、理智、深远，海洋、天空、水。紫——优雅、高贵、神秘、丁香、玫瑰。黑——严肃、刚健、恐怖、稳重、夜。白——洁净、卫生、神圣、光明、雪、云。
    冬天的卧室色彩应以暖色为主，床单可用红橙和暖咖啡色；枕头套或枕巾应与床单相协调，用淡红、黄绿、黄橙色；被套、毯子应选用黄绿、紫红、咖啡、褐色；灯罩宜用奶黄、淡红、橙黄色；窗帘色彩不宜过深。
    夏天室内色彩宜采用淡蓝青绿等冷色调。床上用品以白、绿、淡黄、淡绿为好。窗帘采用淡雅的绿色彩，给人以清幽雅静、舒适凉快的感觉。同时放置一些绿色植物，可使气氛更活泼。
                          （7月2日《新疆日报》黄炽昌文）
