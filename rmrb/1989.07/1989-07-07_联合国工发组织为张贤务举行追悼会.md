Title: The United Nations Industrial Development Organization Holds a Memorial Service for Zhang Xianwu
Author:
Date: 1989-07-07
Edition: 3

The United Nations Industrial Development Organization (UNIDO) held a memorial service for Zhang Xianwu at its headquarters on July 5. The Director-General, Sumi Aoki, and China's Permanent Representative to UNIDO, Ye Yunge, delivered eulogies.

	Part of the delegation heads from various countries attending the UNIDO Council meeting, ambassadors from several missions to the United Nations and international organizations, representatives of Chinese international institutions, China's Ambassador to Austria, Yang Chengxu, as well as Zhang Xianwu's wife and children, and over 100 others attended the memorial service.

	Zhang Xianwu passed away in Vienna on the afternoon of June 27 at the age of 57.

### 联合国工发组织为张贤务举行追悼会

1989-07-07
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    联合国工发组织为张贤务举行追悼会
    新华社维也纳7月5日电　联合国工业发展组织总部今天为中国在该组织总部任高级协调员的张贤务举行追悼会，总干事夏松和中国常驻工发组织代表叶元格先后致悼词。
    正在维也纳参加工发组织理事会会议的部分国家代表团团长、一些驻联合国和国际组织使团的大使、中国驻国际机构代表、中国驻奥地利大使杨成绪以及张贤务的夫人和孩子等100多人参加了追悼会。
    张贤务是6月27日下午1时在维也纳病逝的，终年57岁。
