Title: Bush to Attend Summit of the Americas
Author:
Date: 1989-07-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, July 21st.

White House spokesman Fitzwater announced today that President Bush has accepted the invitation from Costa Rican President Arias to attend the Summit of the Americas to be held this fall in San Jose.

The two-day summit will take place from October 27 to 28. The leaders will also participate in Costa Rica's celebrations marking the 100th anniversary of the establishment of its democratic system.

White House spokesman Fitzwater said the summit will primarily discuss ways to promote democracy in the Western Hemisphere. Issues such as drug trafficking, debt, development, and deforestation will also be on the agenda.

This will be Bush's fifth overseas trip since taking office in January. He has already visited Canada and several countries in Asia, Western Europe, and Eastern Europe.

### 布什将参加美洲国家首脑会议

1989-07-22
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    布什将参加美洲国家首脑会议
    新华社华盛顿7月21日电　白宫发言人菲茨沃特今天宣布，布什总统已接受哥斯达黎加总统阿里亚斯的邀请，参加今秋在圣何塞举行的美洲国家首脑会议。
    这一为期2天的首脑会议将于10月27日至28日举行，首脑们还将参加哥斯达黎加庆祝建立民主制度100周年活动。
    白宫发言人菲茨沃特说，这次首脑会议将主要讨论在西半球促进民主的办法。毒品走私、债务、发展和森林破坏等问题也将在会上提出来讨论。
    这将是布什今年1月就任总统以来第5次出国访问。他已先后对加拿大和亚洲、西欧及东欧一些国家进行了访问。
