Title: Rafsanjani Elected as President of Iran
Author:
Date: 1989-07-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tehran, July 30th.

Rafsanjani Elected as President of Iran

Former parliament speaker Ali Akbar Hashemi Rafsanjani has won the national election and been elected as the fourth president of Iran.

The final election results show that the 55-year-old Rafsanjani won 15.5 million out of the 16.4 million votes nationwide, securing a decisive victory.

His sole opponent, Abbas Sheibani, sent a congratulatory message to him yesterday. The message said: "I hope, under the leadership of Ayatollah Khamenei, you will achieve success during your presidency. This will make the late Imam (Khomeini)'s soul feel at ease."

### 拉夫桑贾尼当选伊朗总统

1989-07-30
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    拉夫桑贾尼当选伊朗总统
    新华社德黑兰7月30日电　据伊朗伊斯兰共和国通讯社今天报道，前议会议长阿里·阿克巴尔·哈什米·拉夫桑贾尼已在全国大选中获胜，当选为伊朗第四任总统。
    公布的最后选举结果表明，现年55岁的拉夫桑贾尼赢得了全国1640万张选票中的1550万张，以压倒的优势获胜。
    他的唯一竞选对手阿巴斯·谢巴尼已于昨天向他发出贺电。贺电说：“我希望，在阿亚图拉·哈梅内伊的领导下，您将在担任总统期间取得成功。这将使已故伊玛姆（霍梅尼）的亡灵感到欣慰。”
