Title: Gao Di Meets Yamaguchi Toshio
Author:
Date: 1989-07-07
Edition: 2

People's Daily Editor-in-Chief Gao Di met with former Japanese Minister of Labor and Liberal Democratic Party Member of the House of Representatives Yamaguchi Toshio this afternoon and had a friendly conversation with him.

### 高狄会见山口敏夫

1989-07-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    高狄会见山口敏夫
    本报北京7月7日讯　人民日报社社长高狄今天下午会见了日本自民党众议员、前劳动大臣山口敏夫，同他进行了友好的谈话。
