Title: The General Political Department Presents Books to Beijing District and County Military Sub-Districts
Author: Wáng Bótíng  Wáng Zǐyàn
Date: 1989-07-27
Edition: 4

The General Political Department of the People's Liberation Army held a ceremony in Beijing yesterday to present newly published "National Defense Knowledge Textbooks" to the district and county military sub-districts and reserve units in Beijing. Confucius, the director of the General Political Department's Work Department, on behalf of the General Political Department's leadership, highly praised the outstanding achievements of the district and county military sub-districts and reserve units in Beijing in stopping the turmoil and suppressing the rebellion. He also expressed gratitude for their support to the troops enforcing martial law.

### 总政向北京区县人武部赠书
王伯廷　王子彦
1989-07-27
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    总政向北京区县人武部赠书
    本报讯　解放军总政治部昨天在京举行仪式，向北京区县人武部和预备役部队赠送了新出版的《国防知识教材》。总政群工部部长祝庭勋代表总政领导高度评价北京市区县人武部和预备役部队在制止动乱、平息暴乱中的突出功绩，感谢他们对戒严部队的支持。
                      （王伯廷　王子彦）
