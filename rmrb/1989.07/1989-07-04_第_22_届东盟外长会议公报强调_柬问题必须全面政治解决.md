Title: The 22nd ASEAN Foreign Ministers' Meeting Communiqué Emphasizes that the Cambodian Issue Must Be Resolved Politically
Author:
Date: 1989-07-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Bandar Seri Begawan, July 4th.

The two-day 22nd ASEAN Foreign Ministers' Meeting concluded today in Bandar Seri Begawan, the capital of Brunei. The meeting issued a joint communiqué emphasizing that the decade-long Cambodian issue must be resolved comprehensively through political means.

The communiqué called for the establishment of an international organization to "effectively supervise and verify" the withdrawal of Vietnamese troops from Cambodia. It also proposed the deployment of an effective UN-led international peacekeeping force to maintain peace and order during Cambodia's free elections.

The foreign ministers urged Vietnam, the Phnom Penh regime backed by Vietnam, and the Cambodian resistance forces to adopt a more conciliatory attitude in seeking a political solution.

The ministers reaffirmed their support for Prince Sihanouk to head the four-party provisional government of Cambodia before the elections.

The ministers welcomed the international conference on the Cambodian issue scheduled for August in Paris. They hoped the conference would promote a comprehensive resolution to the Cambodian issue. They expressed concern that if the Cambodian issue is not resolved fairly and comprehensively, Cambodia might face civil war similar to Afghanistan after the withdrawal of Vietnamese troops.

Regarding the Afghan issue, the ministers welcomed the Soviet Union's withdrawal of troops from Afghanistan but expressed concern over the ongoing war in Afghanistan.

The communiqué also expressed concern over the unequal treatment of developing countries in economic relations with developed countries and the adverse effects of certain economic policies of developed countries on developing countries.

The ministers expressed deep concern over the expanding global arms race and its impact on world peace.

### 第22届东盟外长会议公报强调  柬问题必须全面政治解决

1989-07-04
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    第22届东盟外长会议公报强调
    柬问题必须全面政治解决
    新华社北京7月4日电  文莱消息：为期两天的第22届东盟外长会议今天在文莱首都斯里巴加湾港结束。会议通过的一项联合公报强调，长达10年之久的柬埔寨问题必须得到全面的政治解决。
    公报号召成立一个国际机构以便“有效地监督和核查”越南从柬埔寨撤军；同时在联合国主持下向柬派出一支有效能的国际维持和平部队以便在柬举行自由大选期间维持和平与秩序。
    外长们在公报中要求越南、越南所扶植的金边政权和柬抵抗力量三方联合政府在寻求政治解决中采取“更加和解”的态度。
    外长们重申支持西哈努克亲王在柬大选之前担任柬四方临时政府的首脑。
    外长们对订于8月份在巴黎举行的柬埔寨问题国际会议表示欢迎。他们希望这次会议能够促使柬问题的全面解决。他们表示担心，如果柬问题得不到全面公正的解决，在越南撤军后，柬埔寨如同阿富汗一样会出现内战。
    关于阿富汗问题，外长们欢迎苏联从阿撤军的行动，但对阿富汗的战争仍在继续表示关切。
    在公报中，外长们对发展中国家与发达国家在经济关系上的不平等待遇和某些发达国家的经济政策对发展中国家所带来的不利影响表示关切。
    他们对日益扩展的世界军备竞赛对世界和平所产生的影响“深表不安”。
