Title: Tianjin Shipping Company Established
Author:
Date: 1989-07-07
Page: 2

From the People's Daily, Tianjin, July 5th.

A combined maritime and port enterprise, Tianjin Shipping Company Limited, was established today. This company will leverage Tianjin's port and shipping advantages to undertake sea transportation tasks for Tianjin's domestic and foreign trade, thereby enhancing its distribution and radiation capabilities.

### 天津轮船公司问世

1989-07-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    天津轮船公司问世
    本报天津7月5日电　一家港航结合、运贸结合的联合海运企业实体——天津轮船联合有限公司今天诞生。它将利用天津港口和航运优势，承担天津对内、外贸的海上运输任务，以增强集散功能和辐射力。
