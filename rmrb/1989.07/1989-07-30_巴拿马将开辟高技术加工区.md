Title: Panama to Establish a High-Tech Processing Zone
Author:
Date: 1989-07-30
Edition: 3

Panama is planning to establish a high-tech product processing zone near Omar Torrijos International Airport.

Ricardo Munios, the person in charge of formulating this plan, said that the high-tech product processing zone, which will utilize Panama's unique geographical location, will cover an area of 100 hectares. It will have 400 buildings equipped with various facilities convenient for industrialized countries' companies, such as telecommunications and information services, office buildings, assembly plants, and scientific laboratories. Additionally, residential areas for managers, hospitals, international schools, and recreational facilities will be built near the processing zone.

### 巴拿马将开辟高技术加工区

1989-07-30
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    巴拿马将开辟高技术加工区
    新华社巴拿马城7月29日电　据报道，巴拿马正筹划在奥马尔托里霍斯国际机场附近开辟一个高技术产品加工区。
    制定这项计划的负责人里卡多·穆尼奥斯说，利用巴拿马特定的地理位置开辟的高技术产品加工区将占地100公顷，拥有400座楼房，配置便于工业化国家公司工作的各种设施，如电讯和信息服务、办公楼、装配厂、科学试验室。此外，还在加工区附近兴建经理人员住宅、医院、国际学校和娱乐场所。
