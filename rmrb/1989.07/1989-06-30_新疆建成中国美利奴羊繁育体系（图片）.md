Title: Xinjiang Completes Breeding System for Chinese Merino Sheep
Author: Lán Xuéyì
Date: 1989-06-30
Edition: 2

The breeding system for Chinese Merino sheep (Xinjiang Military Farming Type) has been completed in Xinjiang and was recently approved by experts from the Ministry of Agriculture. Chinese Merino sheep are a new breed of fine-wool sheep with advanced global standards. The image shows a flock of Merino fine-wool sheep bred at the Ziniquan Seed Sheep Farm.

	New China News Agency, reporter Lán Xuéyì.

### 新疆建成中国美利奴羊繁育体系（图片）
蓝学毅
1989-06-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    新疆建成中国美利奴羊繁育体系
    中国美利奴羊（新疆军垦型）繁育体系在新疆建成，最近通过农业部专家鉴定。中国美利奴羊是具有世界先进水平的细毛羊新品种。图为紫泥泉种羊场繁育的美利奴细毛羊群。　新华社记者　蓝学毅摄
