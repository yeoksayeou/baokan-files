Title: Standing in Front of the Bookshelf
Author: Wang Jiaoyang
Date: 1989-07-23
Page: 8

I stand in front of the bookshelf—
Engaging in dialogue with the wise elder of knowledge
Stroking those books
Is like stroking
Mother's hand
I quiet my heart
And read page by page
A hundred years later
I hope
To be
A page in the book
And become an exciting passage…

### 站在书架前
汪娇阳
1989-07-23
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

    站在书架前
    汪娇阳
    我站在书架前——
    和知识老人对话
    抚摸那些书
    就像抚摸
    母亲的手
    我静下心
    一页一页地读
    一百年后
    我希望
    自己也是
    书中的某一页
    成为精彩的段落……
