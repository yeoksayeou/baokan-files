Title: Atlanta, USA, Bids for the 1996 Summer Olympics
Author:
Date: 1989-07-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, July 27th.

Atlanta, USA, is preparing to bid for the 1996 Summer Olympics and plans to submit its application to the International Olympic Committee in Puerto Rico next month.

Organizers in Atlanta stated today that out of the 18 sports venues required for the Olympics, 15 of Atlanta's existing facilities meet the standards. If Atlanta secures the hosting rights, the city will construct an Olympic Stadium with a capacity of 85,000 seats.

### 美亚特兰大争取举办1996年奥运会

1989-07-30
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    美亚特兰大争取举办1996年奥运会
    新华社华盛顿7月27日电  美国亚特兰大市准备争取举办1996年夏季奥运会，并拟于下月在波多黎各向国际奥委会递交申请。
    亚特兰大的组织者今天说，在奥运会所需18个体育场地中，亚特兰大现有的15个场地设施符合标准；如果能获举办权的话，亚特兰大将建造一所拥有85000座位的奥林匹克体育场。
