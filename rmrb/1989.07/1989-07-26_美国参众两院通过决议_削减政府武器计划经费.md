Title: U.S. Senate and House of Representatives Pass Resolution to Cut Government Weapon Program Funding
Author: Zhang Qixun
Date: 1989-07-26
Edition: 3

The U.S. Senate and House of Representatives today each passed a resolution to cut funding for the government's B-2 stealth bomber and Strategic Defense Initiative (SDI, or "Star Wars" program).

The Senate passed the resolution with an overwhelming majority of 98 to 1, prohibiting the Pentagon from purchasing B-2 stealth bombers in the next fiscal year. The budget for B-2 aircraft was reduced from $4.7 billion to $4.4 billion. The resolution also requires the total procurement plan for B-2 bombers to be reduced from 132 to 60 aircraft.

On the same day, the House of Representatives passed a resolution with 248 to 175 votes, reducing the funding for research on the anti-missile defense system from $4.9 billion to $3.1 billion for the next fiscal year.

Local newspapers have described the votes in both the Senate and the House of Representatives as a major setback for the government's weapon programs.

### 美国参众两院通过决议  削减政府武器计划经费
张启昕
1989-07-26
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美国参众两院通过决议
    削减政府武器计划经费
    本报华盛顿7月25日电  记者张启昕报道：美国国会参、众两院今天分别通过决议削减政府提出的有关B—2隐形轰炸机和战略防御计划（星球大战计划）的经费。
    参议院以98对1票的绝对多数通过决议，禁止五角大楼在下一年度采购B—2隐形轰炸机。把用于B—2飞机的预算经费从47亿减到44亿美元。还要求把B—2飞机的采购总计划从132架减少到60架。
    同一天，众议院以248对175票通过决议，把下一年度用于研究反导弹防御系统的经费从49亿减少到31亿美元。
    此间报纸称，参、众两院的表决是对政府武器计划的重大挫折。
