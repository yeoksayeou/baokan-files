Title: Launch of the Factory Manager Report
Author: Jun Hui
Date: 1989-07-16
Edition: 4

    The Factory Manager Report, a new type of economic newspaper aimed at China's enterprise management level, has recently been approved by the State Administration of Press and Publication and has been launched.
