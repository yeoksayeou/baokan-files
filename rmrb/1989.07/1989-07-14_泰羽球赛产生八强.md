Title: Eight Strongest Players Emerge in Thailand Badminton Tournament
Author: Zhang Qiu Lai
Date: 1989-07-14
Edition: 4

The 1989 Thailand Badminton Open produced the top 8 players in the men's and women's singles, doubles, and mixed doubles categories today. The Chinese women's singles players who advanced to the top 8 are Han Ai Ping, Tang Jiu Hong, Luo Yun, Huang Hua, and Zheng Bao Jun.

The Chinese men's singles players who advanced to the top 8 are Xiong Guo Bao and Zhao Jian Hua. The Chinese women's doubles pairs that advanced to the top 8 are Guan Wei Zhen/Lin Ying, Yao Fen/Lai Cai Qin, and Luo Yun/Shi Fang Jing. The Chinese men's doubles pairs that advanced to the top 8 are Li Yong Bo/Tian Bing Yi, Wang Peng Ren/Jiang Guo Liang, and Zhou Jin Can/Zhang Qiang. The only Chinese pair that advanced to the top 8 in the mixed doubles is Wang Peng Ren and Shi Fang Jing.

The 1989 Thailand Badminton Open began on July 11, with over 200 players from more than 20 countries and regions participating in the competition.

### 泰羽球赛产生八强
张秋来
1989-07-14
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    泰羽球赛产生八强
    新华社曼谷7月13日电　（记者张秋来）1989年泰国羽毛球公开赛今天产生男女单打、双打和混合双打5个项目的前8名。进入女子单打前8名的中国选手是韩爱平、唐九红、罗云、黄华和郑宝君。
    进入男子单打前8名的中国选手是熊国宝和赵剑华。中国的关渭贞／林瑛、姚芬／赖彩勤以及罗云／史方静进入女子双打前8名。进入男子双打前8名的中国选手有李永波／田秉毅、王朋仁／蒋国良、周金灿／张强。进入混合双打前8名的中国选手只有王朋仁和史方静。
    1989年泰国羽毛球公开赛是11日开始的，共有来自20多个国家和地区的200多名选手参加比赛。
