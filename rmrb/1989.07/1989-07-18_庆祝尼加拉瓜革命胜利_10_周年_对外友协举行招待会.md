Title: Celebrating the 10th Anniversary of the Nicaraguan Revolution
Author:
Date: 1989-07-18
Edition: 2

The China People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries and the China-Latin America Friendship Association held a reception tonight to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the victory of the Nicaraguan Revolution.

Lin Ping, vice-chairman of the China-Latin America Friendship Association, said in his speech at the reception that over the past 10 years, the Nicaraguan people have made unremitting efforts to carry out social reforms, develop national economy, safeguard national independence and sovereignty, and oppose foreign interference. The just cause pursued by the Nicaraguan people has won the sympathy and support of the Chinese and world peoples and will surely achieve final victory.

He said that since the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Nicaragua, through the joint efforts of both sides, the relations between the two countries have developed smoothly. The exchanges between the two peoples have also increased.

He expressed the belief that the friendly cooperative relations between China and Nicaragua and the friendship between the two peoples will be further developed in the years to come.

Nicaraguan Ambassador to China Alfredo Aránis Donning, in his speech, reviewed the course of the Nicaraguan Revolution and the achievements made after the victory of the revolution. He stressed that Nicaragua is a peace-loving country. He also highly praised the Nicaragua-China friendship.

Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhu Qizhen and others attended the reception.

### 庆祝尼加拉瓜革命胜利10周年  对外友协举行招待会

1989-07-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    庆祝尼加拉瓜革命胜利10周年
    对外友协举行招待会
    新华社北京7月18日电  中国人民对外友好协会、中国拉丁美洲友好协会今天晚上在这里举行招待会，庆祝尼加拉瓜革命胜利10周年。
    中拉友协副会长林平在招待会上讲话时说，10年来，尼加拉瓜人民为进行社会改革，发展民族经济，维护国家独立、主权、反对外来干涉进行了不懈的努力。尼加拉瓜人民所进行的正义事业赢得了中国和世界各国人民的同情和支持，必将取得最终的胜利。
    他说，中尼建交以来，通过双方共同努力，两国关系得到了顺利发展。两国人民之间的交流也不断增多。
    他表示相信，中尼两国的友好合作关系和两国人民的友谊在今后的岁月里必将得到进一步发展。
    尼加拉瓜驻华大使阿尔费雷多·阿拉尼斯·多宁在讲话时回顾了尼加拉瓜革命的历程和革命胜利后所取得的成就。他强调指出，尼加拉瓜是热爱和平的。他还高度评价了尼中友谊。
    外交部副部长朱启祯等出席了招待会。
