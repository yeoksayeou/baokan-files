Title: The Second International Wushu Coach Training Class Concludes
Author: Yu Renren
Date: 1989-07-02
Edition: 4

The International Wushu Federation Preparatory Committee entrusted the Chinese Wushu Association to organize the second international wushu coach training class, which has now concluded in Macao. A total of 84 coaches from 13 countries and regions participated in the training. This training class was hosted by the Macao Wushu Association and began in early June. Renowned Chinese coaches Wu Bin, Men Huifeng, and Bai Wenxiang, among others, taught the seven wushu routines that will be featured in the 11th Asian Games. Teams from Japan, Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Indonesia, Italy, Algeria, Brazil, Australia, Israel, and the Hong Kong and Macao regions participated, with South Korea also sending 12 participants to join the training.

### 第二届国际武术教练员训练班结束
于鲁人
1989-07-02
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    第二届国际武术教练员训练班结束
    本报讯　国际武联筹委会委托中国武协举办的国际武术教练员训练班已在澳门结束，13个国家和地区的84名教练员参加学习。这届训练班由澳门武协承办，从6月初开始。中国著名教练吴彬、门惠丰、白文祥等人执教，教授第十一届亚运会比赛的七个武术套路。日本、新加坡、马来西亚、泰国、印尼、意大利、阿尔及利亚、巴西、澳大利亚、以色列等国和港澳地区组队参加，南朝鲜也派12人参加学习。
　  （于鲁人）
