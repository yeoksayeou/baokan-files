Title: The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Approves the National Budget for Last Year
Author:
Date: 1989-07-06
Edition: 4

The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on Approving the 1988 National Budget

New China News Agency, Beijing, July 6th - Decision of the Standing Committee of the Seventh National People's Congress on Approving the 1988 National Budget

(Adopted on July 6th, 1989)

In accordance with the decision of the Second Session of the Seventh National People's Congress to "authorize the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress to review and approve the 1988 national budget," the Eighth Session of the Standing Committee of the Seventh National People's Congress listened to the report by Wang Boping, State Councilor and Minister of Finance, on behalf of the State Council, titled "Report on the 1988 National Budget." After deliberation and based on the review report by the Financial and Economic Committee, it was decided to: approve the 1988 national budget proposed by the State Council, and approve the report by Wang Boping, State Councilor and Minister of Finance, titled "Report on the 1988 National Budget."

### 人大常委会批准去年的国家决算

1989-07-06
第4版(国际新闻·国内新闻)
专栏：

    人大常委会批准去年的国家决算
    新华社北京7月6日电　全国人民代表大会常务委员会关于批准1988年国家决算的决议
    （1989年7月6日通过）
    根据第七届全国人民代表大会第二次会议关于“授权全国人民代表大会常务委员会审查和批准1988年国家决算”的决议，第七届全国人民代表大会常务委员会第八次会议，听取了国务委员兼财政部部长王丙乾代表国务院所作的《关于1988年国家决算的报告》，经过审议并根据财政经济委员会的审查报告，决定：批准国务院提出的1988年国家决算，批准国务委员兼财政部部长王丙乾所作的《关于1988年国家决算的报告》。
