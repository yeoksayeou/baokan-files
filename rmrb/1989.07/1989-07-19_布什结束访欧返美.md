Title: Bush Ends Visit to Europe and Returns to the U.S.
Author:
Date: 1989-07-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, July 18th.

President Bush, after visiting Poland, Hungary, and the Netherlands and participating in the summit of the seven Western countries in Paris, returned to the United States by plane on the morning of the 18th.

### 布什结束访欧返美

1989-07-19
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    布什结束访欧返美
    布什总统在访问波兰、匈牙利和荷兰三国并在巴黎参加西方七国首脑会议之后，18日上午乘机回到美国。
            （本报专电）
