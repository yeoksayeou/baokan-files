Title: Tsaldaris Elected as Greek Parliament Speaker
Author:
Date: 1989-07-05
Edition: 3

	Tsaldaris, a member of the New Democracy Party of Greece, was elected as the new Speaker of the Greek Parliament on July 4.

	Tsaldaris was elected Speaker after defeating the candidate from the Panhellenic Socialist Movement in two rounds of voting.

### 察尔达里斯任希腊议长

1989-07-05
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    察尔达里斯任希腊议长
    希腊新民主党议员察尔达里斯7月4日被选为新的希腊国民议会议长。
    察尔达里斯是在两次投票中击败全希社运候选人后当选为议长的。
