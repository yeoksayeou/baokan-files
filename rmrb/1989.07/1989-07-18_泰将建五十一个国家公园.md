Title: Thailand to Establish 51 New National Parks
Author:
Date: 1989-07-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, July 16th.

Thailand's Ministry of Agriculture plans to establish 51 new national parks in forest areas over the next few years, bringing the total number of national parks in the country to 110.

Currently, Thailand's national parks cover a total area of 3.36 million hectares. After the establishment of the 51 new parks, the total area of national parks will increase to 5.12 million hectares.

The Thai government has already identified the locations for 20 of the new parks and is in the process of selecting sites for the remaining 31.

One of the purposes of increasing the number of national parks in Thailand is to promote tourism. The country is currently developing tourism facilities in existing national parks.

### 泰将建五十一个国家公园

1989-07-18
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    泰将建五十一个国家公园
    新华社曼谷7月16日电　泰国农业部计划在近几年内在森林地区再建51个国家公园，从而使泰国的国家公园总数达到110个。
    泰国目前国家公园的总面积为336万公顷，51个新公园建成后，国家公园总面积将为512万公顷。
    泰国政府已确定了20个新公园的地点，并正在选定另外31个园址。
    泰国增加建立国家公园的目的之一是发展旅游业。这个国家正在现有国家公园兴建旅游设施。
