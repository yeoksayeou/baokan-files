Title: "101" Hair Regeneration Essence Wins International Gold Award
Author: He Huangbiao
Date: 1989-07-11
Edition: 2

The "101" series of hair regeneration essences, invented by Zhao Zhangguang, recently won the highest honor at the 1989 Paris International Invention Expo—the Chamber of Commerce Gold Cup Award—and also received a gold medal.

It is understood that the "101" series products are now exported to 43 countries and regions. This year, the export earnings of "101" have reached over 2 million U.S. dollars.

### “101”毛发再生精又获国际金奖
何黄彪
1989-07-11
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    “101”毛发再生精又获国际金奖
    本报讯　赵章光发明的“101”系列毛发再生精，最近在1989年法国巴黎第8届国际发明展览会上荣获本届展览会最高荣誉奖——工商会金杯奖，同时还获得一块金牌。
    据了解，“101”系列产品已远销43个国家和地区。今年以来，“101”的出口创汇额已达200多万美元。　（何黄彪）
