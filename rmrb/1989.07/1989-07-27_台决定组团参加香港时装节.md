Title: Taiwan Decides to Send Delegation to Hong Kong Fashion Week
Author:
Date: 1989-07-27
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, July 27th.

Taiwan's "Textile and Garment Industry Promotion Center" and the "Foreign Trade Association" have decided to select 25 manufacturers to participate in the "1990 Hong Kong Fashion Week."

It is reported that Taiwan's apparel industry is currently in a transitional period, with few buyers placing orders for high-end fashion in Taiwan.

It is understood that the Hong Kong Fashion Week was established in 1966 and began allowing overseas manufacturers to participate in 1989.

### 台决定组团参加香港时装节

1989-07-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    台决定组团参加香港时装节
    据新华社香港7月27日电　台北消息：台湾“纺拓会”和“外贸协会”已决定征求25家厂商共赴香港，参加“1990香港时装节”。
    据称，台湾成衣业目前正值转型期，到台湾下单采购高级时装的买主不多。
    据悉，香港时装节创始于1966年，1989年开始开放外地厂商参展。
