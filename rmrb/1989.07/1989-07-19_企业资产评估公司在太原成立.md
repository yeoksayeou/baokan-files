Title: Enterprise Asset Evaluation Company Established in Taiyuan
Author: Zhang Suilin
Date: 1989-07-19
Edition: 2

The first enterprise asset value evaluation company in the nation was established in Taiyuan, Shanxi Province, on July 13. This company was organized with the leadership of the Taiyuan branch of the Industrial and Commercial Bank of China.

The company was established to serve the rational flow of enterprise assets. At the same time, it will also handle the evaluation of disaster losses, conduct asset value investigations upon request, and provide consulting services.

### 企业资产评估公司在太原成立
张遂林
1989-07-19
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    企业资产评估公司在太原成立
    新华社太原7月19日电　（通讯员张遂林）全国第一家企业资产价值评估公司7月13日在山西太原成立。这家公司是由中国工商银行太原市支行牵头组建的。
    这家公司是为了服务企业资产的合理流动而成立的。同时，它还承担灾害损失的价值评估，受委托办理资产价值调查，进行咨询等。
