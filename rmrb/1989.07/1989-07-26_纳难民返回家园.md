Title: Namibian Refugees Return Home
Author:
Date: 1989-07-26
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 25th.

The spokesperson for the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees announced today that since June 1988, nearly 27,000 Namibian refugees have returned home. Among them, 24,344 returned from Angola, 1,790 from Zambia, and 580 from other countries.

According to statistics, there are a total of 41,000 Namibian refugees living abroad.

### 纳难民返回家园

1989-07-26
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    纳难民返回家园
    新华社日内瓦7月25日电　联合国难民事务高级专员办事处发言人今天宣布，自1988年6月以来，已有将近27000名纳米比亚难民返回家园。其中从安哥拉返回的有24344人，赞比亚1790人，其他国家580人。
    据统计，流落在异国的纳米比亚难民共有41000名。
