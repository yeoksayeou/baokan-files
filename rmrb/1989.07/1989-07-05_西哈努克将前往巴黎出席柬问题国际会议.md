Title: Prince Sihanouk to Attend International Conference on Cambodia in Paris
Author: Lǐ Huáilín
Date: 1989-07-05
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 5th.

Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Chairman of the Democratic Kampuchea, stated that Vietnam and the Soviet Union will create obstacles at the upcoming international conference to resolve the Cambodian issue, but the three factions of the Cambodian resistance will not yield.

At a press conference held this morning for both domestic and foreign journalists, Sihanouk said he will attend the international conference on resolving the Cambodian issue to be held in Paris in August.

He said representatives from the five permanent members of the UN Security Council, the six ASEAN countries, Vietnam, Laos, India, Japan, Australia, and the four Cambodian factions will participate in this international conference.

UN Secretary-General Javier Pérez de Cuéllar will also attend the conference.

Prince Sihanouk said that on July 25, the leaders of the four Cambodian factions will hold a round-table conference in Paris to discuss ways to resolve the Cambodian issue.

He said the round-table conference is important, and Prince Norodom Sihanouk will attend the conference as his representative.

He said that during the round-table conference, he will be present as an observer.

### 西哈努克将前往巴黎出席柬问题国际会议
李怀林
1989-07-05
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    西哈努克将前往巴黎出席柬问题国际会议
    新华社北京7月5日电（记者李怀林）民主柬埔寨主席诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王说，越南和苏联会在即将召开的解决柬埔寨问题的国际会议上制造困难，但是柬埔寨抵抗力量三方不会让步。
    在今天上午举行的中外记者招待会上，西哈努克说，他将参加今年8月在巴黎召开的解决柬埔寨问题的国际会议。
    他说，参加这次国际会议的代表将来自联合国安理会的5个常任理事国，东盟6国，越南、老挝、印度、日本、澳大利亚等国和柬埔寨4方。
    联合国秘书长德奎利亚尔也将参加这次会议。
    西哈努克亲王说，7月25日柬埔寨4方领导人将在巴黎举行圆桌会议，讨论解决柬埔寨问题的途径。
    他说，圆桌会议是重要的，诺罗敦·拉那烈王子将作为他的代表参加会议。
    他说，圆桌会议期间，他的身份将是一名观察家。
