Title: Soviet Union Has 20 Million Overseas Citizens
Author:
Date: 1989-07-24
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

Currently, there are approximately 20 million Soviet citizens scattered across various countries around the world. Among them, 7.5 million are in the United States, 3.5 million are in Canada, and about 1 million are in various countries in Latin America.

### 苏在国外侨民二千万人

1989-07-24
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏在国外侨民二千万人
    目前分散在世界各国的苏侨共约有2000万，其中在美国750万，在加拿大350万，在拉丁美洲各国约100万。
            （据新华社）
