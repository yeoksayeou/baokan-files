Title: A Cry of Joy: Green Mountains and Waters
Author: Hu Ke
Date: 1989-07-09
Edition: 8

The camera lens captures a moment of pure joy. The photographer's heart resonates with the rhythm of nature. The mountains and rivers, under the gentle caress of the sun, exude a vibrant green hue. This is not just a photograph; it is a testament to the harmony between man and nature.

In this era of rapid development, we often overlook the beauty that surrounds us. The hustle and bustle of city life, the relentless pursuit of material wealth—all these can make us forget the simple pleasures of life. But when we take a moment to pause and look around, we find that nature is still there, waiting to embrace us with its warmth and beauty.

The green mountains and rivers are not just a scenic backdrop; they are the lifeblood of our existence. They provide us with the air we breathe, the water we drink, and the food we eat. They are the foundation of our well-being and the source of our inspiration.

As we stand at the threshold of a new century, let us remember to cherish and protect our natural environment. Let us strive to create a world where man and nature coexist in harmony. Let us make a commitment to preserve the green mountains and rivers for future generations.

In the words of the ancient poet, "The mountains and rivers are eternal, but man is fleeting." Let us ensure that our fleeting presence on this earth leaves a positive impact on the mountains and rivers that have sustained us for generations. Let us make a difference, one photograph at a time.
