Title: Kodak Experts Return to Xiamen
Author: Cong Wei
Date: 1989-06-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Xiamen, June 29th.

Three experts from the Eastman Kodak Company, who left Xiamen for Hong Kong on June 12, returned to Xiamen yesterday morning.

The color photographic materials production line in Xiamen is the second-largest project of Sino-American cooperation to date and is also the first time in over 100 years that Kodak Company has transferred its complete set of technologies overseas.

### “柯达”专家重返厦门
丛伟
1989-06-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    “柯达”专家重返厦门
    本报厦门6月29日电　6月12日从厦门撤离到香港的伊思曼·柯达公司3名专家，昨天上午重返厦门。
    厦门彩色感光材料生产线是至今中美合作的第二大项目，也是柯达公司100多年来首次对外转让的成套技术。
                          （丛伟）
