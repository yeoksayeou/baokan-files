Title: Chinese Kite Exhibition in Santiago
Author: Liu Zhongyang
Date: 1989-07-03
Page: 7

Recently, "Changjiang" Primary School in Santiago, the capital of Chile, held a Chinese kite exhibition. The teachers and students showed great interest in the Chinese kites on display.
