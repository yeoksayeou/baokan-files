Title: Funeral of Comrade Lü Zhe Held
Author:
Date: 1989-07-24
Edition: 2

The funeral of Comrade Lü Zhe, a member of the 7th National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, honorary member of the China Buddhist Association, and advisor to the State Council's Ancient Books Compilation and Publication Planning Group, was held this morning at the Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery in Beijing.

Comrade Lü Zhe passed away in Beijing on July 8, 1989, at the age of 93.

Lü Zhe, originally named Lü Wei, with the courtesy name Qiuyi, was from Dan Yang, Jiangsu Province. He dedicated his life to the study of Buddhist philosophy and was highly respected in the field of Buddhist studies. His works include "An Outline of the Sources of Chinese Buddhism," "An Outline of the Sources of Indian Buddhism," "Methods of Buddhist Studies," and dozens of other books. He also had a deep understanding of aesthetics, publishing works such as "Introduction to Aesthetics" and "Modern Aesthetics and the Principles of Beauty." Before his passing, he served as a standing member of the China Buddhist Association, deputy chief editor of the China Buddhist Encyclopedia Compilation Committee, and a member of the Philosophy and Social Sciences Department of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. He was also a member of the 3rd, 4th, 5th, and 6th National Committees of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

Today, more than 200 people from Beijing, including Zhao Pujie, came to the Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery to pay their respects to Comrade Lü Zhe. Deng Yingchao, Li Yimang, Habibullah, Peng Chong, Yan Jizhi, Sun Qimeng, Chen Junsheng, Wang Renzhong, Yan Mingfu, Yang Jingren, Babala Gelianjie, Zhou Peiyuan, Hu Sheng, Cheng Siyuan, Lu Jiashi, Qian Zhengying, Hou Jingru, Ding Guangxun, and Chu Tuonan, as well as the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, the State Administration for Religious Affairs, the China Buddhist Association, the State Council's Ancient Books Compilation and Publication Planning Group, the Chinese Academy of Social Sciences, Tsinghua University, and the Jiangsu Provincial Government, sent wreaths.

### 吕澂同志遗体告别仪式举行

1989-07-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    吕澂同志遗体告别仪式举行
    新华社北京7月24日电  第七届全国政协委员、中国佛教协会名誉理事、国务院古籍整理出版规划小组顾问吕澂同志遗体告别仪式今天上午在八宝山革命公墓礼堂举行。
    吕澂同志是1989年7月8日在北京逝世的，享年93岁。
    吕澂原名吕渭，字秋逸，江苏丹阳人。他早年攻学佛典，一生致力于佛教哲学研究工作，在佛学界享有盛誉。他的著作有《中国佛学源流略讲》、《印度佛学源流略讲》、《佛教研究法》等数十种。他对美学研究也有较深造诣，出版有《美学概论》、《近代美学和美的原理》等。他生前曾任中国佛教协会常务理事，中国佛教百科全书编纂委员会副主编，中国科学院哲学社会科学部学部委员等职，并是第三、四、五、六届全国政协委员。
    赵朴初等首都人士200余人，今天上午前往八宝山革命公墓向吕澂同志遗体告别。邓颖超、李一氓、习仲勋、彭冲、严济慈、孙起孟、陈俊生、王任重、阎明复、杨静仁、帕巴拉·格列朗杰、周培源、胡绳、程思远、卢嘉锡、钱正英、侯镜如、丁光训、楚图南等同志，全国政协、中共中央统战部、国务院宗教局、中国佛协、国务院古籍整理出版规划小组、中国社会科学院、清华大学、江苏省人民政府等单位，送了花圈。
