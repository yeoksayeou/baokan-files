Title: China's National Football Team Defeats Thailand to Secure Group Qualification for the World Cup
Author:
Date: 1989-07-29
Edition: 4

The Chinese national football team defeated Thailand 2-0 on the evening of July 29 in Shenyang, securing a spot in the qualifying round for the 14th FIFA World Cup in Asia's Group 5.

In this match, the Chinese team achieved the necessary points and goal difference to qualify for the next round in less than a minute. This was accomplished by a long-range shot from number 5 Jia Xiuquan. For the Chinese team, the remaining 89 minutes were about maintaining this result. Three minutes later, number 17 Mai Chao scored with a header, further securing the team's qualification and maintaining a 2-0 lead until the final whistle.

This is the second time the Chinese team has qualified for the group stage since the 1980 end of the 12th World Cup qualifiers.

In mid to late October, the Chinese team will compete in a single round-robin tournament in Singapore with the other five qualified teams from Saudi Arabia, the United Arab Emirates, North Korea, South Korea, and Qatar. They will vie for two spots to advance to the final round in Rome.

### 世界杯足球赛亚洲区预选赛  中国队力克泰国队获小组出线权

1989-07-29
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    世界杯足球赛亚洲区预选赛
    中国队力克泰国队获小组出线权
    本报讯　中国足球队7月29日晚在沈阳以2∶0战胜泰国队，取得第14届世界杯外围赛亚洲区第五小组出线权。
    在这场比赛中，中国队在开场不到一分钟，就达到了小组出线所需要的积分和净胜球数，这是由5号贾秀全的一脚远射完成的。对中国队来说，剩下的89分钟只是如何保持这一成果的问题。3分钟，17号麦超一记头球攻门扩大了战果，为中国队小组出线保了险，并把2∶0的比分一直维持到终场。
    这是中国队继1980年底第12届世界杯预赛后，第二次小组出线。
    今年10月中下旬，中国队将同其它五个小组出线的沙特阿拉伯、阿联酋、朝鲜、南朝鲜、卡塔尔队一起，在新加坡进行单循环赛，争夺进军罗马参加世界杯决赛的两个席位。
