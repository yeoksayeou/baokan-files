Title: Three African Presidents Meet to Discuss Peace and Stability
Author:
Date: 1989-07-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Brazzaville, July 3rd.

Three African Presidents Meet to Discuss Peace and Stability

According to Xinhua News Agency, Brazzaville, July 3rd - The presidents of Congo, Gabon, and Angola met today in Pointe-Noire, the economic capital of Congo, to continue discussions on peace and national reconciliation in Angola.

This meeting of the three heads of state follows the suspension of national reconciliation talks between the Angolan government delegation and the National Union for the Total Independence of Angola (UNITA) in Kinshasa on June 29th due to disagreements.

### 非洲三总统会晤商讨安和平问题

1989-07-04
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    非洲三总统会晤商讨安和平问题
    据新华社布拉柴维尔7月3日电　刚果、加蓬和安哥拉三国总统今天在刚果经济首都黑角会晤，以便就安哥拉和平与民族和解问题继续进行磋商。
    这次三方首脑会晤是在安哥拉政府代表团和争取安哥拉彻底独立全国联盟（安盟）代表团6月29日在金沙萨举行的民族和解谈判因产生分歧而中断后进行的。
