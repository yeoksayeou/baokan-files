Title: The Soviet Union Withdraws Another Batch of Troops from Poland
Author:
Date: 1989-07-11
Edition: 3

The Soviet Union withdrew a battalion of troops from the southwestern province of Wroclaw in Poland on July 10. According to the Polish News Agency, this unit was an independent chemical defense battalion of the Soviet Northern Group of Forces.

### 苏又从波撤走一批部队

1989-07-11
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏又从波撤走一批部队
    苏联10日从波兰西南部的弗罗茨瓦夫省又撤走一个营的部队。据波通社报道，这支部队是苏军北方集群的一个防化独立营。　（据新华社）
