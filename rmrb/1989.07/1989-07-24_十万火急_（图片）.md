Title: Urgent Matter
Author: An Jian Guo
Date: 1989-07-24
Page: 5

Original text from 《工人日报》:

    “十万火急”　安建国（原载《工人日报》）

    7月19日，中共中央政治局常委、国务院总理李鹏在北京主持召开全国经济工作座谈会，听取各地、各部门关于当前经济形势的汇报，研究部署今后经济工作的重点。李鹏在会上指出，当前经济形势严峻，各地、各部门要认真贯彻中央关于经济工作的方针政策，努力克服困难，确保国民经济持续、稳定、协调发展。

    李鹏强调，要坚定不移地推进改革开放，增强经济发展的活力。他指出，改革开放是我国经济发展的强大动力，是实现现代化的必由之路。各地、各部门要坚持改革开放的方向，进一步深化改革，扩大开放，推动经济结构调整，提高经济效益。

    李鹏还指出，要加强宏观调控，保持经济的稳定发展。他强调，宏观调控是经济发展的重要手段，要通过科学的宏观调控，保持经济的稳定增长，防止经济波动。各地、各部门要认真贯彻中央关于宏观调控的决策部署，确保经济的稳定发展。

    李鹏最后指出，要加强党的领导，发挥党员的先锋模范作用。他强调，党的领导是经济工作的根本保证，要充分发挥党员的先锋模范作用，带领广大群众共同努力，克服困难，推动经济发展。

Translation:

    **Urgent Matter** by An Jian Guo (originally published in the *Workers' Daily*)

    On July 19th, Li Peng, a member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the Communist Party of China Central Committee and Premier of the State Council, presided over a national economic work symposium in Beijing. He listened to reports from various regions and departments about the current economic situation and discussed the key tasks for future economic work. Li Peng pointed out at the meeting that the current economic situation is severe. All regions and departments must seriously implement the central government's policies on economic work, strive to overcome difficulties, and ensure the sustained, stable, and coordinated development of the national economy.

    Li Peng emphasized that the reform and opening up must be steadfastly promoted to enhance the vitality of economic development. He pointed out that reform and opening up are the powerful driving forces for China's economic development and the inevitable path to modernization. All regions and departments must adhere to the direction of reform and opening up, further deepen the reform, expand opening up, promote economic structural adjustment, and improve economic efficiency.

    Li Peng also pointed out that macro-control must be strengthened to maintain stable economic development. He stressed that macro-control is an important means of economic development. Through scientific macro-control, economic stability and growth must be maintained, and economic fluctuations must be prevented. All regions and departments must seriously implement the central government's decisions and deployments on macro-control to ensure stable economic development.

    Finally, Li Peng pointed out that the leadership of the Party must be strengthened, and the vanguard and exemplary role of Party members must be brought into play. He emphasized that the leadership of the Party is the fundamental guarantee for economic work. The vanguard and exemplary role of Party members must be fully brought into play to lead the broad masses of the people in jointly overcoming difficulties and promoting economic development.
