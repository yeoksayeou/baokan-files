Title: Murderer of Feng Yimiao Captured
Author: Cai Xiu Cun
Date: 1989-07-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, June 30th

The murderer of Feng Yimiao, a representative of the National People's Congress and the general manager and party secretary of the Dongjiekou Department Store Co., Ltd. in Fuzhou, has been captured in Yong'an City.

The murderer, Yuan Zihu, is from Taixing County, Jiangsu Province. On June 13, he traveled to Yong'an City and checked into the Zhonghua Hotel. At 3 a.m. on June 15, he broke into Room 216 of the Yong'an Guest House, entering through the window. While attempting to open a locked box, he woke Feng Yimiao. Yuan then used a spring knife and strangulation to brutally kill Feng Yimiao.

### 杀害冯依淼的凶手被捕  作案者是江苏一流窜犯
蔡珪村
1989-07-02
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    杀害冯依淼的凶手被捕
    作案者是江苏一流窜犯
    新华社福州6月30日电（记者蔡珪村）记者从福建省公安厅获悉，杀害全国人大代表、福州市东街口百货大楼股份有限公司总经理、党委书记冯依淼的凶手袁自虎今天在永安市被抓获。
    凶手袁自虎，江苏省泰兴县人。6月13日，他流窜到永安市，住进中华旅社。6月15日凌晨3时，窜入永安市招待所，爬窗进入216房间，在盗开密码箱时，惊醒了被害人冯依淼，就用弹簧刀捅、手卡等残忍手段把冯依淼杀死。
