Title: Winnie Mandela Visits Nelson Mandela on His 71st Birthday
Author:
Date: 1989-07-24
Edition: 7

Winnie Mandela, the wife of Nelson Mandela, the leader of the African National Congress in South Africa, visited him in prison on July 18 to celebrate his 71st birthday together. The picture shows Winnie Mandela's lawyer displaying a large birthday card sent by the miners' union to Nelson Mandela in Cape Town to the reporters.
