Title: China Women's Volleyball Team Wins the Chinin Cup International Women's Volleyball Tournament
Author:
Date: 1989-07-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 26th.

China's women's volleyball team today won the final round of the Chinin Cup International Women's Volleyball Tournament by defeating the Soviet Union team 3-2. With this victory, the team secured the championship with a perfect record of five wins in five matches.

### 中国女排夺得苏联国际女排赛冠军

1989-07-27
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    中国女排夺得苏联国际女排赛冠军
    新华社莫斯科7月26日电　中国女排今天在奇尼林杯国际女子排球赛最后一轮比赛中，以3∶2力克苏联队，从而以5战全胜的成绩夺得这一重要国际比赛的冠军。
