Title: Guangxi to Eradicate Spiritual Poisons Starting with Government-Run Institutions
Author: Liu Huading, Du Xin
Date: 1989-07-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, July 23rd

Guangxi has launched a comprehensive campaign to eradicate spiritual poisons and rectify the social cultural market starting yesterday. Leaders of the Guangxi Autonomous Region Party Committee and the regional government stated at a teleconference held on the evening of July 21st: This work should be listed as an important part of the campaign against corruption. The investigation should start with government-run institutions. This investigation will not be lenient, no matter which department or individual is involved, it will be thoroughly investigated.

According to statistics from the public security authorities, the number of cases involving the production, reproduction, trafficking, and dissemination of obscene materials in the first half of this year is 122.6% of the total number of such cases last year. A total of 624 criminals were apprehended, and 483 boxes of obscene videotapes and 108 types of obscene books and magazines were seized.

The investigation revealed that some government-run cultural institutions have forgotten their principles in the face of spiritual poisons for the sake of profit. The New Asia Video Room, managed by the Nanning Municipal Cultural Department, openly sells tickets to screen more than 10 types of obscene videotapes. Among the batch of smuggled imported videotapes approved by the Wuzhou Municipal Propaganda Department for screening, some contain erotic content.

### 广西扫除精神毒品  先从官办单位查起
刘华丁  杜新
1989-07-23
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    广西扫除精神毒品
    先从官办单位查起
    新华社南宁7月23日电　（中央人民广播电台记者刘华丁、本社记者杜新）广西昨天起全面展开清查精神毒品、整顿社会文化市场工作。广西自治区党委和区政府负责人在7月21日晚召开的电话会议上说：要把这项工作列为惩治腐败的重要内容。要从官办单位查起。这次清查，不管涉及到哪个部门、什么人，都要一查到底，决不手软。
    据公安机关统计，今年上半年全市查处制作、复制、贩卖、传播淫秽物品案件是去年这类案件总数的122.6％，抓获犯罪分子624人，缴获淫秽录像带483盒、淫秽书刊画册达108种。
    查处的情况表明：一些官办的文化单位在精神毒品面前见利忘义。由南宁市文化部门主管的新亚录像室等单位，公开售票播放10多种淫秽录像带。梧州市宣传部门批准播放的一批走私进口录像带，其中部分有色情内容。
