Title: Fighting Resumes in Beirut
Author:
Date: 1989-07-28
Edition: 3

On the night of the 27th, the Lebanese capital Beirut once again saw intense fighting. The sound of exploding shells and whistling rockets echoed through the night without ceasing, resulting in over a hundred casualties.

### 贝鲁特炮战再起

1989-07-28
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    贝鲁特炮战再起
    27日夜里，黎巴嫩首都贝鲁特再次发生激战，炮弹的爆炸声和火箭的呼啸声彻夜未停，死伤百余人。
