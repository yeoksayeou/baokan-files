Title: De Cuellar Condemns Israel's Expulsion of Palestinians
Author: Liu Kai Chen
Date: 1989-06-30
Page: 3

United Nations Correspondent Liu Kai Chen reports from the United Nations, June 29th:

United Nations Secretary-General Javier Pérez de Cuéllar today expressed "great shock" over the Israeli occupation authorities' expulsion of eight Palestinians from the West Bank and Gaza Strip to southern Lebanon.

A statement read by a UN spokesperson here today pointed out, "The actions of the Israeli authorities clearly violate the Fourth Geneva Convention and infringe upon Lebanese sovereignty."

De Cuéllar, in the statement, urged Israel, as the occupying power, to adhere to the obligations stipulated in the convention and to revoke the expulsion order.

De Cuéllar fervently hopes that the expelled Palestinians can immediately be allowed to return to their homes and reunite with their families. De Cuéllar concluded by stating that Israel's actions will only further exacerbate the already unstable situation, and thus he is "deeply concerned."

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, June 29th:

The U.S. State Department today confirmed that the Bush administration has quietly increased its contacts with the Palestine Liberation Organization.

According to reports, recently, U.S. Ambassador to Tunisia, Robert Pelletreau, was tasked with meeting twice with the second-in-command of the PLO, Abu Iyad, to discuss issues related to U.S.-PLO dialogue and to explore the prospects of elections for the 1.4 million Palestinians in the Israeli-occupied territories.

### 德奎利亚尔谴责以驱逐巴勒斯坦人  美国同巴解组织增加接触
刘开宸
1989-06-30
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    德奎利亚尔谴责以驱逐巴勒斯坦人
    美国同巴解组织增加接触
    本报联合国6月29日电　记者刘开宸报道：联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔今天对以色列占领当局把8名巴勒斯坦人从约旦河西岸和加沙地带驱逐到黎巴嫩南部一事表示“十分震惊”。
    联合国发言人今天在这里宣读的声明指出，“以色列当局的这一行为明显地违反了《第四日内瓦公约》并侵犯了黎巴嫩主权”。
    德奎利亚尔在声明中呼吁做为占领国的以色列遵守此公约规定的义务并撤回驱逐令。
    德奎利亚尔热烈希望被驱逐出境的巴勒斯坦人能立即获准返回家园与家人团聚。德奎利亚尔最后指出，以色列的行为只会使已经不稳定的局势进一步恶化，因而他“深为关切”。
    新华社华盛顿6月29日电　美国国务院今天证实，布什政府已经悄悄地增加了与巴勒斯坦解放组织的接触。
    据报道，最近美国驻突尼斯大使罗伯特·佩莱特鲁受命与巴解组织第二号人物阿布·伊亚德进行了两次会晤，讨论了与美巴对话有关的问题，并探讨了以色列占领区140万巴勒斯坦人的选举前景。
