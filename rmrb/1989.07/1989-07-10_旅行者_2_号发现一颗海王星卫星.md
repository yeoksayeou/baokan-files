Title: Voyager 2 Discovers a New Moon of Neptune
Author:
Date: 1989-07-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 9th July

NASA officials said that the "Voyager 2" spacecraft has discovered Neptune's third moon.

Scientists at NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory said that the newly discovered Neptune moon is temporarily named "1989N1". Its diameter is estimated to be between 125 to 400 miles, smaller than Neptune's first moon discovered in 1846 and the second moon discovered in 1949.

### 旅行者2号发现一颗海王星卫星

1989-07-10
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    旅行者2号发现一颗海王星卫星
    据新华社华盛顿7月9日电　美国宇航局官员说，“旅行者2号”太空探测器发现了海王星的第3颗卫星。
    宇航局喷气推进实验室的科学家说，这颗新发现的海王星卫星暂时命名为“1989N1”。它的直径估计有125—400英里，比1846年发现的海卫一和1949年发现的海卫二都小。
