Title: Vice Premier Yao Yilin Meets with Guests from the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1989-07-13
Edition: 2

Vice Premier Yao Yilin met with a delegation from the German Democratic Republic in the Great Hall of the People this afternoon. The delegation was led by Walter Halbritt, Director of the State Price Bureau.

Both sides introduced their respective price systems and the challenges they face.

### 姚依林会见民主德国客人

1989-07-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    姚依林会见民主德国客人
    新华社北京7月12日电　国务院副总理姚依林今天下午在人民大会堂会见了由国家物价局局长瓦尔特·哈尔布里特率领的民主德国物价代表团。
    宾主双方就各自的物价体系及所面临的问题向对方作了介绍。
