Title: Japanese Banks Dominate Top Ten in Global Assets
Author:
Date: 1989-07-26
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, New York, July 25th.

According to a report by *The American Banker* magazine, the top ten banks in the world by asset size are all Japanese banks. This is the first time in history that this has occurred.

The Industrial Bank of Japan ranks first, with assets totaling $387 billion (as of the end of the Japanese fiscal year 1988, which ended in March of this year; the asset figures for other Japanese banks are the same. The asset figures for foreign banks are as of the end of 1988). The rankings of other Japanese banks are as follows: Sumitomo Bank ($376.1 billion), Fuji Bank ($364 billion), Sanwa Bank ($348.4 billion), Mitsubishi Bank ($343.6 billion), Dai-Ichi Kangyo Bank ($257.6 billion), Norinchukin Bank ($241.9 billion), Tokai Bank ($225.1 billion), Dai-Ichi Kangyo Bank ($219.7 billion), and Mitsubishi Trust and Banking Corporation ($210.5 billion).

The largest bank in the United States, Citibank, has assets totaling $203.8 billion, dropping from the 10th position last year to the 12th position this year. France's Crédit Agricole fell from the 8th position to the 11th, with assets totaling $208 billion.

The main reasons for the continuous expansion of Japanese banks are the appreciation of the yen and the increase in Japan's savings rate.

### 日本银行资产丰囊括世界前十名

1989-07-26
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    日本银行资产丰囊括世界前十名
    新华社纽约7月25日电  据《美国银行家》杂志报道，按资产额排名世界十大银行全部是日本的银行。这是有史以来第一次。
    日本第一劝业银行名列榜首，资产额为3870亿美元（到今年3月底的日本1988财政年度止，其他日本银行相同；别国银行的资产额为1988年末的数字）。其他日本银行的名次为：住友银行（3761亿美元，单位下同），富士银行（3640），三和银行（3484），三菱银行（3436），日本兴业银行（2576），农林中央金库（2419），东海银行（2251），三井银行（2197）和三菱信托银行（2105）。
    美国最大的银行——花旗银行的资产额为2038亿美元，从去年的第10位降到第12位。法国的农业信贷银行从第8位降到第11位，资产额为2080亿美元。
    日本银行规模不断扩大的主要原因是日元升值和日本的储蓄率上升。
