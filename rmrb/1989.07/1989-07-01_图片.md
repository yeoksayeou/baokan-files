In Longyang County, Hubei Province, near the long-distance bus station, several individual food vendors are only concerned with making money and pay no attention to environmental hygiene. The ground is littered with garbage and dirty water from washing, flies swarm, and the stench is overpowering. Dumplings, steamed buns, and other foods are prepared in this filthy environment, carrying bacteria into the mouths of travelers from all directions.
