Title: Kadar's Condition Serious
Author:
Date: 1989-07-04
Edition: 3

	Kadar's Condition Serious

	Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, July 3rd: According to a report by the Hungarian News Agency on the evening of the 3rd, János Kádár, former chairman of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, was hospitalized in the morning of the 3rd due to pneumonia and severe breathing difficulties. His condition is serious.

	Kádár is 77 years old this year. Since last winter, his health has been poor. In May of this year, he was relieved of his duties as party chairman and central committee member for "health reasons."

### 卡达尔病情严重

1989-07-04
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    卡达尔病情严重
    新华社布达佩斯7月3日电　据匈通社3日晚报道，匈牙利社会主义工人党前主席卡达尔·亚诺什因患肺炎和呼吸严重困难于3日上午住进医院治疗。他的病情严重。
    卡达尔今年77岁。自去年冬天以来，他的身体一直不好。今年5月，他因“健康原因”被党中央解除党主席和中央委员的职务。
