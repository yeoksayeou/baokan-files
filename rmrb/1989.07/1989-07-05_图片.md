Title: The Harbin Songhua River Highway Bridge
Author: Wang Dong
Date: 1989-07-05
Edition: 5

The Harbin Songhua River Highway Bridge is a crucial link connecting the northern part of Heilongjiang Province to the Soviet Union and the southern part of the country. Since its completion and opening to traffic in 1986, this bridge has played a vital role in facilitating the exchange of materials in Heilongjiang Province. The bridge spans a total length of 1656 meters and is designed in a scissor-like shape.
