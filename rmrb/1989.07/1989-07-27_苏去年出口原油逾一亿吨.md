Title: The Soviet Union Exported Over 100 Million Tons of Crude Oil Last Year

Author:

Date: 1989-07-27

Edition: 3

The Soviet Union exported 144 million tons of crude oil in 1988, an increase of 7.6 million tons compared to 1987. The export of petroleum products amounted to 61 million tons, an increase of 1.8 million tons compared to 1987. The total export value of these two items was 19.7 billion rubles.

The yearbook also revealed that in 1988, the Soviet Union exported 39 million tons of raw coal, an increase of approximately 4 million tons compared to 1987; 2.3 million tons of coke, an increase of 100,000 tons compared to 1987; and 38.9 billion kilowatt-hours of electricity, an increase of 4 billion kilowatt-hours compared to 1987. Oil is the Soviet Union's main export commodity.

### 苏去年出口原油逾一亿吨

1989-07-27
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏去年出口原油逾一亿吨
    新华社莫斯科7月26日电　据刚刚出版的1988年苏联外贸统计年鉴报道，去年苏联出口原油1.44亿吨，比1987年增加760万吨，石油加工产品6100万吨，比1987年增加180万吨，此两项出口价值达197亿卢布。
    该年鉴透露，1988年苏联出口原煤3900万吨，比1987年增加约400万吨；焦炭230万吨，比1987年增加10万吨；电力389亿度，比1987年增加40亿度。石油是苏联主要出口商品。
