Title: U.S. Plans to Build Freedom Space Station
Author:
Date: 1989-07-03
Edition: 3

The U.S. National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) recently announced that it has selected 27 scientific experiments as onboard test projects for the planned "Freedom" space station, including cosmic dust detection and tropical rainforest surveys.

NASA said that equipment for 14 of these experiments will be installed as components on the space station, while the remaining 13 will rely on ground-based facilities for more complex research.

NASA hopes to begin construction of the "Freedom" space station in the mid-1990s, ultimately making it a laboratory where eight crew members can live and work.

The selection of experimental projects primarily considers power supply issues. The initial power source for the space station will be solar panels, and NASA hopes to add more efficient solar energy collectors.

Some of the selected experimental projects will be conducted inside the space station's living quarters, including tests of superconducting electromagnets at extremely low temperatures, cosmic ray measurements, and cosmic dust collection. The cosmic dust collection device can capture cosmic particles and record their direction and speed.

### 美计划建造自由号空间站  已选定27项科学实验项目

1989-07-03
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美计划建造自由号空间站
    已选定27项科学实验项目
    新华社华盛顿6月30日电  美国宇航局近日宣布，已经选定27项科学实验作为计划中的“自由”号空间站的机上试验项目，其中包括宇宙尘埃检测和热带雨林调查等。
    宇航局说，其中14项实验的设备将作为部件安装在空间站上，其它13项将依赖地面进行较复杂的研究。
    美国宇航局希望在90年代中期开始建造“自由”号空间站，最终使它成为一个可供8名机组人员居住和工作的实验舱。
    选定实验项目首先要考虑的是动力问题。空间站的最初动力来源于太阳能电池，宇航局期望增加较有效的太阳能收集器。
    已经选定的一些试验项目将在空间站的生活舱内进行，其中有超冷超导电磁测试宇宙射线，以及宇宙尘埃采集。宇宙尘埃采集装置可以捕获宇宙粒子并记录它的方向和速度。
