Title: American Scientists Develop Surgical Incision Sealant
Author:
Date: 1989-07-13
Edition: 3

	American scientists have recently developed a surgical incision sealant.
	According to the *Sunday Times*, the sealant is made from two natural coagulants: one is fibrinogen extracted from the patient's own blood, and the other is bovine thrombin extracted from cow blood.
	During use, the fibrinogen and bovine thrombin are mixed in the operating room and applied to the incision, forming a rubbery white clot. The sealant is eventually absorbed by the healing tissue.
	The incision sealant has successfully undergone the first round of human clinical trials in the United States.

### 美制出手术切口闭合胶

1989-07-13
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美制出手术切口闭合胶
    新华社伦敦7月11日电　据《星期日泰晤士报》报道，美国科学家最近研制出一种手术切口闭合胶。
    切口闭合胶用两种天然的凝血剂制成：一种是从病人自己的血液中提取的纤维蛋白原，另一种是从牛血液中提取的牛凝血酶。
    使用时，把纤维蛋白原和牛凝血酶在手术室中现配，涂在切口上形成橡胶状白色凝块。切口闭合胶最终被愈合组织吸收。
    切口闭合胶已经在美国成功地进行了第一批人体临床试验。
