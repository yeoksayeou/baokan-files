Title: Former Sudanese Prime Minister Arrested
Author:
Date: 1989-07-07
Page: 3

A spokesman for the Sudanese military government said on the 6th that Sadiq al-Mahdi, the prime minister of the Sudanese government that was overthrown on June 30, was arrested on the 6th and taken to the Revolutionary Command Council for National Salvation. Former Minister of Cabinet Affairs, Salim, has surrendered to the council.

### 苏丹政府前总理被逮捕

1989-07-07
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏丹政府前总理被逮捕
    苏丹军政府发言人6日说，6月30日被推翻的苏丹政府总理迈赫迪6日在被捕后被押送到救国革命指挥委员会。前内阁事务部长萨拉姆已向指挥委员会自首。
