Title: Pérez de Cuéllar Ends Visit to Italy
Author:
Date: 1989-07-09
Page: 3

The United Nations Secretary-General, Javier Pérez de Cuéllar, concluded his two-day official visit to Italy on July 8. The "Disarmament Archives Association" of Italy awarded Pérez de Cuéllar the "International Peace Dove Award."

### 德奎利亚尔结束访意

1989-07-09
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    德奎利亚尔结束访意
    联合国秘书长德奎利亚尔八日结束了他对意大利的两天正式访问。意大利“裁军档案协会”给德奎利亚尔颁发了“国际和平金鸽奖”。
