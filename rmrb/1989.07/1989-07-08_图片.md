Title: One of China's Largest Department Stores, Beijing's Longfu Mall, Strives to Recover from Recent Turmoil
Author: Wáng Xiáoguāng
Date: 1989-07-08
Page: 5

Beijing's Longfu Mall, one of China's largest department stores, is actively organizing supplies and expanding sales to recover from the losses incurred during the recent turmoil. The store is committed to ensuring a stable market supply. The image shows the mall's department store warmly welcoming customers.
