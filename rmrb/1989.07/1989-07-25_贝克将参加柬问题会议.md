Title: Baker to Attend Cambodian Issue Conference
Author:
Date: 1989-07-25
Edition: 3

The U.S. State Department announced on the 24th that Secretary of State Baker will lead a delegation to attend the international conference on the Cambodian issue to be held in Paris on the 30th.

### 贝克将参加柬问题会议

1989-07-25
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    贝克将参加柬问题会议
    美国国务院24日宣布，国务卿贝克将率代表团出席于30日在巴黎召开的柬埔寨问题国际会议。
