Title: President of the Supreme People's Court, Task Jianxin, Meets with Charles L. Ray and His Delegation
Author:
Date: 1989-07-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 10th.

President of the Supreme People's Court and Chairman of the Chinese Organizing Committee for the 14th World Congress on Law, Task Jianxin, met and hosted a banquet for Charles L. Ray, President of the World Center for the Rule of Law for World Peace, and his delegation this evening at the Great Hall of the People.

Ray arrived in China on July 7th at the invitation of Task Jianxin. Both sides had a friendly discussion on the issue of holding the 14th World Congress on Law in Beijing.

### 任建新会见赖恩一行

1989-07-10
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    任建新会见赖恩一行
    新华社北京7月10日电　最高人民法院院长、第14届世界法律大会中国组织委员会主席任建新今天晚上在人民大会堂会见并宴请“通过法律维护世界和平中心”世界主席查理斯·赖恩一行。
    赖恩是应任建新的邀请于7月7日来中国进行访问的。双方就在北京召开第14届世界法律大会的问题进行了友好的会谈。
