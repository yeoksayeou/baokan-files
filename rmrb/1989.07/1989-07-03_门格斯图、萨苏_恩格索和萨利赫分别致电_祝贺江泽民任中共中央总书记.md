Title: Mengistu, Sassou-Nguesso, and Saleh Congratulate Jiang Zemin on Becoming CPC Central Committee General Secretary
Author:
Date: 1989-07-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 3rd.

Mengistu, Sassou-Nguesso, and Saleh Congratulate Jiang Zemin on Becoming CPC Central Committee General Secretary

On June 30, Mengistu Haile Mariam, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Ethiopia and President of the People's Democratic Republic of Ethiopia (also Commander-in-Chief of the Revolutionary Army), sent a telegram to Jiang Zemin to congratulate him on his election as General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

The telegram said:

"On the occasion of your election as General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, I, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Ethiopia, the working people of Ethiopia, and in my personal capacity, extend my heartfelt congratulations to you."

"Your election to this important post clearly demonstrates the firm trust of the Communist Party of China and the Chinese people in you. Therefore, we believe that the efforts of the friendly Chinese people to achieve advanced socio-economic development, maintain and defend world peace, and realize the ideals of socialism will be further strengthened."

"We are confident that the friendly relations and comprehensive cooperation between our two parties, governments, and peoples, based on common goals, will be further consolidated in the years to come."

"Finally, I extend to you my best wishes for success in your historic duties. I wish you good health and a happy and prosperous life for the friendly Chinese people."

"With comradely greetings."

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 3rd.

On June 29, Denis Sassou-Nguesso, Chairman of the Congolese Labour Party and President of the Republic, sent a telegram to Jiang Zemin to congratulate him on his election as General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. The telegram said: "On the occasion of your election as General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, I extend to you my warmest and most sincere congratulations. On this happy occasion, I reaffirm my commitment to strengthening the good relations between our two countries. Comrade General Secretary, I wish you every success in your new duties and accept my best wishes for your health, happiness for your family, and all the best for the friendly Chinese people.

With the highest combat greetings!"

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 3rd.

On July 1, Ali Abdullah Saleh, President of the Republic of Yemen, Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces, and General Secretary of the General People's Congress, sent a telegram to Jiang Zemin to congratulate him on his election as General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

The telegram said: "On the occasion of your election as General Secretary of the friendly Communist Party of China, I am honored to extend my congratulations to you and the members of the Central Committee. On this occasion, I wish for further development in the organizational ties and cooperation between the General People's Congress and the Communist Party of China, for the benefit of the two friendly countries and peoples. I wish the friendly Party and country continuous progress."

### 门格斯图、萨苏—恩格索和萨利赫分别致电  祝贺江泽民任中共中央总书记

1989-07-03
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    门格斯图、萨苏—恩格索和萨利赫分别致电
    祝贺江泽民任中共中央总书记
    新华社北京7月3日电　埃塞俄比亚工人党中央委员会总书记、埃塞俄比亚人民民主共和国总统（兼革命军总司令）门格斯图6月30日打电报给江泽民，祝贺他当选为中国共产党中央委员会总书记。
    电报说：
    “在您当选为中国共产党中央委员会总书记之际，我谨代表埃塞俄比亚工人党中央委员会、埃塞俄比亚劳动人民，并以我个人的名义，向您致以衷心的祝贺。”
    “您当选担任这一重要职务清楚地证明了中国共产党和中国人民对您的坚定信任。因而我们相信，友好的中国人民努力为实现先进的社会—经济发展、维护和保卫世界和平、实现社会主义理想的斗争将得到进一步加强。”
    “我们深信，建立在共同目标基础上的我们两党、两国政府和两国人民之间的友好关系与全面合作在今后岁月中将会进一步得到巩固。”
    “最后，我谨向您致以最良好的祝愿，祝您在履行您的历史性职责中获得成功。”“祝您身体健康、并祝友好的中国人民生活安定与幸福。”
    “致以同志式的问候。”
    新华社北京7月3日电　刚果劳动党主席、共和国总统萨苏—恩格索上将6月29日致电江泽民，祝贺他当选为中国共产党中央委员会总书记。电报说：“在您当选为中国共产党中央委员会总书记之际，我谨向您表示最热烈和最诚挚的祝贺。借此愉快的时刻，向您重申，我将为加强我们两国之间的良好关系而不断努力。总书记同志，希望您在履行新的职务中取得圆满成功，并请接受我最良好的祝愿，祝您身体健康、家庭幸福，祝友好的中国人民万事如意。
    顺致最崇高的战斗的敬意！”
    新华社北京7月3日电　阿拉伯也门共和国总统、武装部队总司令、总人民会议总书记阿里·阿卜杜拉·萨利赫上校7月1日致电江泽民，对他当选为中国共产党中央委员会总书记表示祝贺。
    电报说：“在您当选为友好的中国共产党总书记之际，我谨荣幸地向您和中央委员会委员们表示祝贺。借此机会，祝总人民会议和中国共产党之间在组织上的联系和合作进一步发展，以利于两个友好的国家和人民。祝友好的贵党和贵国不断取得进步。”
