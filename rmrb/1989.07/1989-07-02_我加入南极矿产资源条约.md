Title: China Joins the Antarctic Mineral Resources Agreement
Author:
Date: 1989-07-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Wellington, June 28th.

China Joins the Antarctic Mineral Resources Agreement

China's Ambassador to New Zealand, Ni Zhengjian, signed the Antarctic Mineral Resources Agreement on behalf of the Chinese government today.

The signing ceremony was held at the New Zealand Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Trade. China is the 16th country to sign the agreement.

### 我加入南极矿产资源条约

1989-07-02
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    我加入南极矿产资源条约
    新华社惠灵顿6月28日电　中国驻新西兰大使倪政建今天代表本国政府，在南极矿产资源条约上签了字。
    签字仪式是在新西兰对外关系和贸易部举行的。中国是第16个在该条约上签字的国家。
