Title: The "Dictionary of Military History" is Soon to be Published
Author:
Date: 1989-07-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 20th.

To commemorate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and the 62nd anniversary of the founding of the People's Liberation Army, the "Dictionary of Military History of the Chinese People's Liberation Army" published by the Modern Press will soon be available to readers.

Nie Rongzhen, Peng Zhen, Bo Yibo, and others have written words of praise for this book.

This is a comprehensive and systematic reference work that reflects the glorious history of our military.

### 《军史大辞典》即将出版

1989-07-21
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    《军史大辞典》即将出版
    新华社北京7月20日电　为纪念中华人民共和国成立40周年和中国人民解放军建军62周年，由现代出版社出版的《中国人民解放军军史大辞典》即将与读者见面。
    聂荣臻、彭真、薄一波等为这本书题了词。
    这是一部系统、完整地反映我军光荣历史的大型工具书。
