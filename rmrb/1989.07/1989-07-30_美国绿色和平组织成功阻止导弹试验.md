Title: Greenpeace USA Successfully Halts Missile Test
Author:
Date: 1989-07-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, July 28th.

According to reports, members of Greenpeace USA successfully halted a test launch of the Trident II missile by a U.S. submarine today.

Four Greenpeace vessels entered a restricted danger zone about 50 kilometers off the Pacific coast and conducted a several-hour protest against the U.S. submarine "Tennessee" and its supporting vessels, ultimately forcing the cancellation of the launch test.

This is the first time in over 30 years that public protests have successfully halted a missile launch test at Cape Canaveral.

### 美国绿色和平组织成功阻止导弹试验

1989-07-30
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美国绿色和平组织成功阻止导弹试验
    新华社华盛顿7月28日电　据报道，美国绿色和平组织成员今天成功地阻止了美国潜艇进行三叉戟Ⅱ式导弹发射试验。
    4艘绿色和平组织的船只驶进离太平洋海岸50公里左右的危险禁区，对美国“田纳西”号潜艇及其支持舰只进行了数小时的抗议活动，最终迫使其取消了这次发射试验。
    这是30多年来群众的抗议活动首次在卡纳维拉尔角阻止了一次导弹发射试验。
