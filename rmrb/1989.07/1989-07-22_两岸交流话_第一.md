Title: Two Sides of the Strait Discuss "Firsts"
Author:
Date: 1989-07-22
Page: 8

The first Taiwanese journalists to visit the mainland for reporting: On September 14, 1987, reporters Li Yongde and Xu Lu from the Taiwan-based "Zili Evening News" conducted a 14-day reporting trip to the mainland.

The first Taiwanese resident to return to the mainland for a visit: On November 2, 1987, the Taiwan Red Cross began accepting registrations, and Xiong Guangyuan was the first to complete the registration procedures for returning to the mainland for a visit.

The first batch of Taiwanese scientists to attend an international conference on the mainland: Members of the "Taipei Science Association," Su Zhongqing, Zhou Changhong, and Ye Yongtian, attended the 21st General Assembly of the International Council for Science in Beijing on September 11, 1988, under the name of private individuals.

The first mainland resident to visit Taiwan for a sick relative: On November 9, 1988, Qinghua University teacher Qian Yi, who was visiting the Netherlands, obtained a travel permit from Taiwan to visit his ailing father, the renowned scholar Qian Mu. He arrived in Taiwan on November 14.

The first stranded Taiwanese soldier to return to Taiwan for a visit: On February 19, Xie Yuanba, originally from Miaoli County, Taiwan, became the first to fly to Taipei to visit his 90-year-old mother.

The first Taipei youth gymnastics team to participate in an Asian Youth Gymnastics Championship on the mainland.

The first "official" delegation from Taiwan to attend an international conference on the mainland: On May 1, 1989, Taiwan's "Minister of Finance," Guo Wanrong, led the Chinese Taipei delegation to Beijing to attend the 22nd Annual Meeting of the Board of Governors of the Asian Development Bank.

### 两岸交流话“第一”

1989-07-22
第8版(每周文摘)
专栏：

    两岸交流话“第一”
    第一次到大陆采访的台湾记者：1987年9月14日，台湾《自立晚报》记者李永得、徐璐，到大陆进行了14天的采访。
    第一个回大陆探亲的台胞：1987年11月2日台湾红十字会开始受理登记，熊光远第一个办好回大陆探亲登记手续。
    第一批到大陆参加国际性会议的台湾科学家：“台北科学会”成员苏仲卿，周昌弘、叶永田三人于1988年9月11日以民间名义出席在北京召开的国际科学总会第21届年会。
    大陆赴台探病第一人：1988年11月9日，正在荷兰访问的清华大学教师钱易，获准台湾发出的大陆同胞赴台探病旅行证后，于11月14日抵台探望病中老父国学大师钱穆。
    第一个返台探亲的滞留大陆台籍老兵：2月19日原籍台湾省苗栗县的谢源拔第一个飞抵台北，探望九旬老母。
    台北青年体操队第一个到大陆参加亚洲青年体操赛。
    第一个到大陆参加国际性会议的台湾“官方”代表团：今年5月1日，台湾“财政部长”郭婉容率中国台北代表团抵京，出席亚洲开发银行第22届理事会。
    （1989年第12期《半月谈》）
