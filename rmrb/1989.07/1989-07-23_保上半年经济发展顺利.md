Title: Bulgaria's Economic Development Proceeds Smoothly in the First Half of the Year
Author:
Date: 1989-07-23
Edition: 3

The first half of this year has seen a favorable situation for industrial and agricultural production in Bulgaria. Major quality and quantity indicators have met the requirements of the national plan. A series of major industrial products have completed more than 50% of the annual plan. Major economic indicators such as total industrial sales, industrial net output, total profit, and social labor productivity have all seen significant increases compared to the same period last year.

### 保上半年经济发展顺利

1989-07-23
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    保上半年经济发展顺利
    今年上半年保加利亚工农业生产形势良好。主要质量与数量指标都达到了国家计划的要求，一系列主要工业产品都完成了全年计划的50％以上，销售工业产值、工业净产值、总利润和社会劳动生产率等主要经济指标比去年同期有较大幅度增长。
          （据新华社）
