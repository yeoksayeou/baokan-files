Title: Bulgaria and the United States Establish a Joint Venture Company
Author:
Date: 1989-07-16
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, July 15th.

Bulgaria and the United States formally established a joint venture company here on July 14th, specializing in the production of electronic instruments for electric vehicles and motorcycles.

According to reports from the Bulgarian press, this joint venture company is a collaboration between Bulgaria's "Transport Electrical Company," "Balkan Electric Vehicle Company," and an American company specializing in the production of electronic transportation tools. It will serve as a production and technology center for the members of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance in this field, with its products primarily targeting socialist countries, the Balkan region, and Asia.

The American side will provide the company with production technology and foreign exchange investment.

### 保加利亚和美国建立合资公司

1989-07-16
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    保加利亚和美国建立合资公司
    新华社索非亚7月15日电  保加利亚同美国14日在这里正式建立一家专门生产电瓶车和摩托车用的电子仪器联合公司。
    据保报报道，这家联合公司是保加利亚“运输电器公司”、“巴尔干电瓶车公司”同美国一家专门生产电器运输工具的公司合办的。它将成为经互会成员国在这一方面的生产工艺中心，其产品销售对象主要是社会主义国家、巴尔干及亚洲地区。
    美方将向这家公司提供生产工艺与外汇投资。
