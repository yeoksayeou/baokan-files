Title: Thailand Open Badminton Tournament Begins
Author: Zhang Qiu Lai
Date: 1989-07-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, July 11th.

More than 200 athletes from over 20 countries and regions are participating in the 1989 Thailand Open Badminton Tournament, which began today in Bangkok.

The tournament features five events: men's and women's singles, men's and women's doubles, and mixed doubles. The competition follows an elimination format. A total of 58 preliminary matches were held today. Among the Chinese participants, Zhang Ling was eliminated in the women's singles event.

### 泰国羽毛球公开赛开始
张秋来
1989-07-12
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    泰国羽毛球公开赛开始
    新华社曼谷7月11日电　（记者张秋来）有20多个国家和地区的200多名选手参加的1989年泰国羽毛球公开赛今天在这里开幕。
    这次公开赛设男、女单打，男、女双打和混合双打5个项目，采取淘汰制。今天共进行了58场预赛，参加预赛的中国选手臧玲在女子单打中被淘汰。
