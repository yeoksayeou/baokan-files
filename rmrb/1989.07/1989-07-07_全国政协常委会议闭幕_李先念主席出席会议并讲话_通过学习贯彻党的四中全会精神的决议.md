Title: National CPPCC Standing Committee Closes
Author:
Date: 1989-07-07
Edition: 1

The seventh session of the Standing Committee of the Seventh National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) concluded this afternoon at the Great Hall of the People.

The four-day meeting thoroughly studied and discussed the documents of the Fourth Plenary Session of the 13th Central Committee of the Communist Party of China (CPC) and listened to and discussed Chen Xitong's report on the situation regarding the suppression of the counter-revolutionary riot.

The committee members expressed their firm support for the spirit of the Fourth Plenary Session of the 13th CPC Central Committee and Deng Xiaoping's speech, as well as the new leadership of the CPC Central Committee headed by Jiang Zemin. They agreed with the organizational handling of Zhao Ziyang. The committee members also provided valuable opinions and made many good suggestions on how to do a good job for the Party and the government in the coming period.

National CPPCC Chairman Li Xiannian attended the meeting and delivered a speech. He said that the facts once again prove that the solid foundation of the various democratic parties, people's organizations, and people of all ethnic groups in China, who have weathered storms and shared honors and disgrace with the Communist Party of China for nearly 40 years, has withstood the test. At this crucial moment concerning the future and destiny of our country and nation, the CPPCC, as a united front organization led by the Communist Party, has withstood the test (the full text of the speech will be published separately).

The meeting adopted the "Resolution on Studying and Implementing the Spirit of the Fourth Plenary Session of the 13th Central Committee of the Communist Party of China." National CPPCC Acting Secretary-General Song Demin explained the situation of the committee members' discussion on the resolution at the meeting.

Committee members Mei Xing, Qian Sanqiang, and Wu Lengxi delivered speeches at the meeting. Vice Chairman Pabala Gelengjie, Wang Feng, Zhuang Mingli, Zhao Zili, and Liao Yuanxiong submitted written speeches to the conference.

Today's meeting was presided over by Vice Chairman Wang Renhe. Vice Chairpersons Yan Mingfu, Fang Yi, Kang Keqing, Pabala Gelengjie, Wang Guangying, Deng Zhaoxiang, Zhao Pu, Qu Wu, Ma Wenrui, Liu Jingji, Qian Xuesen, Hu Sheng, Sun Xiaocun, Cheng Siyuan, Lu Jiasi, Qian Zhengying, Su Buqing, and Hou Jingru attended the meeting.

### 全国政协常委会议闭幕  李先念主席出席会议并讲话  通过学习贯彻党的四中全会精神的决议

1989-07-07
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    全国政协常委会议闭幕
    李先念主席出席会议并讲话
    通过学习贯彻党的四中全会精神的决议
    据新华社北京7月7日电　全国政协七届常委会第七次会议今天下午在人民大会堂闭幕。
    这次为期四天的常委会认真学习和讨论了中共十三届四中全会文件，听取和讨论了陈希同同志关于制止和平息反革命暴乱情况的报告。
    常委们在讨论中表示坚决拥护中共十三届四中全会精神和邓小平同志的讲话，拥护以江泽民同志为总书记的中共中央新的领导集体，赞同对赵紫阳同志所作的组织处理。常委们还就今后一个时期如何做好党和政府的工作，发表了宝贵的意见，提出了许多很好的建议。
    全国政协主席李先念出席了会议并讲了话。他说，事实又一次证明，近40年来，我国各民主党派、各人民团体和各族各界人士同中国共产党风雨同舟、荣辱与共的牢固基础，是经得起考验的。在关系到我们国家和民族前途命运的关键时刻，人民政协作为共产党领导的广泛统一战线组织，是经得起考验的（讲话全文另发）。
    会议通过了《关于学习和贯彻中国共产党十三届四中全会精神的决议》。全国政协代秘书长宋德敏在会上就常委们讨论决议的情况作了说明。
    梅行、钱三强、吴冷西常委在会上作了大会发言。帕巴拉·格列朗杰副主席、王枫、庄明理、赵子立、廖延雄常委向大会递交了书面发言。
    今天的会议由副主席王任重主持。副主席阎明复、方毅、康克清、帕巴拉·格列朗杰、王光英、邓兆祥、赵朴初、屈武、马文瑞、刘靖基、钱学森、胡绳、孙晓村、程思远、卢嘉锡、钱正英、苏步青、侯镜如，出席了会议。
