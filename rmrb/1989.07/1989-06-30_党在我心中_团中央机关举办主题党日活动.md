Title: The Party in My Heart: The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Holds a Themed Party Day Activity
Author:
Date: 1989-06-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 30th.

To celebrate the 68th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of China, the party organizations of various departments of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League have carried out themed Party Day activities centered around the theme "The Party in My Heart."

The party organizations of various departments of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League have taken the spirit of the Fourth Plenary Session of the Thirteenth Central Committee as their guide. They have led the broad masses of party members to study the Party Constitution, reaffirm their commitment to the Party, review the Party's history, reflect on the recent serious political struggle, closely link their thoughts with reality, compare themselves with the standards for party members, review their performance over the past two months, summarize their experiences and lessons, and have conducted a lively and practical education on party nature and discipline.

The entire Party Day activity was both serious and lively, and it was warmly welcomed by the broad masses of party members. The comrades of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League also participated in the activities of their respective branches.

### “党在我心中”  团中央机关举办主题党日活动

1989-06-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    “党在我心中”
    团中央机关举办主题党日活动
    据新华社北京6月30日电  共青团中央提供的消息：为庆祝中国共产党建党六十八周年，团中央机关各部门党组织普遍开展以“党在我心中”为主题的党日活动。
    团中央机关各部门党组织以党的十三届四中全会精神为指针，引导广大党员通过学习党章、重温入党誓词，回顾党的历史，回顾近一段时间的严肃政治斗争，紧密联系思想实际，对照党员条件，回顾两个月来自己的表现，总结经验教训，进行了一次生动、实际的党性、党纪教育。
    整个党日活动既严肃认真，又生动活泼，受到了广大党员们的欢迎。团中央书记处的同志也参加各自所在支部的活动。
