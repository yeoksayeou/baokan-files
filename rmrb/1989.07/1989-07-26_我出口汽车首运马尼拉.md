Title: China's First Export of Cars Arrives in Manila

Author:

Date: 1989-07-26

Edition: 3

The first batch of 20 brand-new Chinese-made public buses arrived at the port of Manila, Philippines, today.

This is the first shipment of 145 public buses ordered by the Philippines to alleviate the traffic congestion in Greater Manila. The second batch of more than 20 buses will arrive in the Philippines in early August, and the remaining buses will be delivered by the end of this year.

In April, the Greater Manila Transit Company negotiated with relevant Chinese parties to import Chinese public buses and formally signed the contract in May.

### 我出口汽车首运马尼拉

1989-07-26
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    我出口汽车首运马尼拉
    新华社马尼拉7月25日电  第一批20辆中国制造的崭新公共汽车今天运抵菲律宾的马尼拉港。
    这是菲律宾为缓和大马尼拉市的交通紧张状况而向中国订购的145辆公共汽车的首批。第二批20多辆汽车将在今年8月份初运抵菲律宾，其余的将在今年内交货。
    今年4月，大马尼拉市运输公司同中国有关方面就进口中国公共汽车进行了谈判，并于5月正式签订合同。
