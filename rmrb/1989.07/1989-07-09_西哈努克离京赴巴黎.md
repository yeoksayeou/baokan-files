Title: Prince Norodom Sihanouk Leaves Beijing for Paris
Author:
Date: 1989-07-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 8th.

Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Chairman of the Democratic Kampuchea, and his wife departed Beijing by plane this evening for Paris.

Deputy Foreign Minister Liu Shuxiang, Democratic Kampuchea's Ambassador to China Jiang Yulang, and French Ambassador to China Marc, among others, were at the airport to see them off.

### 西哈努克离京赴巴黎

1989-07-09
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    西哈努克离京赴巴黎
    新华社北京7月8日电　民主柬埔寨主席诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和夫人今天晚上乘飞机离开北京前往巴黎。
    外交部副部长刘述卿，民主柬埔寨驻华大使江裕朗以及法国驻华大使马乐等到机场送行。
