Title: Poland and the United States Sign Two Agreements
Author:
Date: 1989-07-18
Edition: 3

Poland and the United States government signed two agreements in Warsaw on the 17th. One of the agreements stipulates that the U.S. will provide Poland with 11,500 tons of sorghum as feed and 4,500 tons of butter free of charge. The other agreement is to establish a joint committee for humanitarian assistance between the two countries.

	(Xinhua News Agency)

### 波兰美国签署两项协议

1989-07-18
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    波兰美国签署两项协议
    波兰与美国政府17日在华沙签订两项协议，其中一项规定，美将向波无偿提供11500吨高粱作为饲料和4500吨黄油。另一项是，两国将建一人道主义援助事务联合委员会。
          （据新华社）
