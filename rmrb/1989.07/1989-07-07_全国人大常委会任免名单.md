Title: List of Appointments and Dismissals by the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress
Author:
Date: 1989-07-07
Edition: 4

The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress announced the following list of appointments and dismissals on July 6, 1989, at the eighth meeting of the Seventh National People's Congress Standing Committee.

1. Appoint Yang Bo as the Deputy Chairman of the Financial and Economic Committee of the National People's Congress.

2. Appoint Zhang Fengge (female), Zhang Qiong, Zhao Dengju, and Chen Zhendong as members of the Procuratorial Committee of the Supreme People's Procuratorate.

3. Dismiss Xie Baogui from the position of member and procurator of the Procuratorial Committee of the Supreme People's Procuratorate.

4. Dismiss Ma Jikao, Wang Qi, Zhang Ming, Fu Xingmin, Yin Baichuan, Wang Ranji, Cui Jin (female), Li Youming, Di Shuangsheng, Zhang Mi'en, and Chen Jie from the position of procurator of the Supreme People's Procuratorate.

### 全国人大常委会任免名单

1989-07-07
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    全国人大常委会任免名单
    新华社北京7月6日电  全国人民代表大会常务委员会任免名单
    1989年7月6日第七届全国人民代表大会常务委员会第八次会议通过。
    一、任命杨波为全国人民代表大会财政经济委员会副主任委员。
    二、任命张凤阁（女）、张穹、赵登举、陈振东为最高人民检察院检察委员会委员。
    三、免去谢宝贵的最高人民检察院检察委员会委员、检察员职务。
    四、免去马计考、王琦、张明、付兴民、殷百川、王然冀、崔进（女）、李有明、邸双生、张弥恩、陈捷的最高人民检察院检察员职务。
