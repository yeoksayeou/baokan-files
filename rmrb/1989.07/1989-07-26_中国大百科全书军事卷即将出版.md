Title: The Military Volume of the Chinese Encyclopedia is About to be Published
Author:
Date: 1989-07-26
Edition: 4

The military volume of the Chinese Encyclopedia, in two volumes, has been completed under the leadership and care of the Central Military Commission and will be released domestically and internationally before the "August 1" Army Day. The history of China not having its own military encyclopedia will thus come to an end.

### 中国大百科全书军事卷即将出版

1989-07-26
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    中国大百科全书军事卷即将出版
    据新华社北京7月26日电　在中央军委的领导和关怀下，《中国大百科全书·军事》两卷本已由中国大百科全书出版社编纂完成，并将在“八一”建军节前向国内外发行。我国没有自己的军事百科全书的历史从此结束。
