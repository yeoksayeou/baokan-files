China's Foreign Ministry Spokesperson Responds to Questions from Reporters Denying Any Relationship with the Illegal Regime in Phnom Penh

New China News Agency, Beijing, July 27 - China's Foreign Ministry spokesperson today denied that China has any relationship with the illegal regime in Phnom Penh.

A reporter asked the Foreign Ministry spokesperson, "Hun Sen recently said in Paris that there are currently Chinese engineering and technical personnel in Phnom Penh. What is your comment on this?"

The spokesperson replied, "China has never had any relationship with the illegal regime in Phnom Penh that is backed by Vietnam. I am not aware of the matter you mentioned."

### 我国外交部发言人答记者问  否认同金边非法政权有任何关系

1989-07-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    我国外交部发言人答记者问
    否认同金边非法政权有任何关系
    新华社北京7月27日电　中国外交部发言人今天在这里否认中国同金边非法政权有任何关系。
    有记者问外交部发言人：“洪森近日在巴黎说，目前有中国的工程技术人员在金边。你对此有何评论？”
    发言人回答说：“中国同越南扶植的金边非法政权从未发生过任何关系。我不清楚你所提到的这件事。”
