Title: Population Press Established in Beijing
Author: Jixuan
Date: 1989-07-13
Page: 4

The Population Press of China was recently established with the approval of the News and Publishing Administration. Comrade Li Xiannian inscribed the name of the press.

### 人口出版社在京成立
季萱
1989-07-13
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    人口出版社在京成立
    本报讯　经新闻出版署批准，中国人口出版社最近宣布成立。李先念同志为中国人口出版社题写了社名。
          （季萱）
