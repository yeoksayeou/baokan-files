Title: Shijiazhuang City Has the Highest Population Density Among Provincial Capitals
Author: Yu Shao Liang
Date: 1989-07-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shijiazhuang, July 7th.

    Shijiazhuang City, the capital of Hebei Province, currently has the highest population density among all provincial capitals (and municipalities) on the Chinese mainland. The average population density within the city limits is 4,100 people per square kilometer.

### 石家庄市人口密度居各省会城市之首
于绍良
1989-07-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    石家庄市人口密度居各省会城市之首
    据新华社石家庄7月7日电　（记者于绍良）河北省省会石家庄市的人口密度，目前居我国大陆各省会（首府）城市的首位。市区内平均每平方公里有4100人。
