Title: The First World Youth Baseball Tournament Begins
Author: Jiǎn Zhàn Guó
Date: 1989-07-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, July 24th.

The first World Youth Baseball Tournament opened today here. In the opening match, the South Korean team defeated the Japanese team with a score of 9 to 6.

The World Youth Baseball Tournament is recognized as a major international event by the International Baseball Federation. This tournament stipulates that the participating players must be between the ages of 13 and 15.

### 首届世界少棒赛开赛
姜占国
1989-07-25
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    首届世界少棒赛开赛
    新华社东京7月24日电　（记者姜占国）第一届世界少年棒球赛今天在此间开幕，南朝鲜队在首场比赛中以9∶6胜日本队。
    世界少年棒球赛是国际棒球联盟承认的世界大赛。这届比赛规定参赛队员的年龄为13岁至15岁。
