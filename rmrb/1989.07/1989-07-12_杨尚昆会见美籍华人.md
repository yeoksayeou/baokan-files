Title: Yang Shangkun Meets with American Chinese
Author:
Date: 1989-07-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 12th.

State Chairman Yang Shangkun this morning met with the delegation led by Hu Shun, President of the Los Angeles branch of the American Chinese Federation, and Zhou Guohua, a member of the board, in the Great Hall of the People.

Chairman Yang briefed the guests on the situation regarding the suppression of the counter-revolutionary riot in Beijing and praised Mr. Hu Shun for his long-term efforts in promoting friendship between the United States and China.

Hu Shun expressed his commitment to continue working for the development of Sino-American friendship.

The guests were invited to Beijing by the China International Trust and Investment Corporation to attend an international tourism conference organized by the Beijing Municipal Tourism Bureau.

Liao Hui, Director of the State Council's Office for Overseas Chinese Affairs, was present at the meeting.

### 杨尚昆会见美籍华人

1989-07-12
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    杨尚昆会见美籍华人
    新华社北京7月12日电　国家主席杨尚昆今天上午在人民大会堂会见了全美华协洛杉矶分会会长胡顺和理事周国华一行。
    杨主席向客人们介绍了北京平息反革命暴乱的情况，并赞扬胡顺先生长期为发展美中两国友谊所做的大量工作。
    胡顺表示，今后要继续为发展美中友谊努力工作。
    客人们是应中信公司邀请来京参加北京市旅游局举办的国际旅游会议的。
    国务院侨办主任廖晖会见时在座。
