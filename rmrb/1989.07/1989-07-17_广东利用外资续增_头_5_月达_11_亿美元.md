Title: Guangdong Continues to Increase Utilization of Foreign Capital
Author: Liu Xiao
Date: 1989-07-17
Page: 2

China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Guangdong Branch, Director Tang Xiangxin revealed at a press conference held in Beijing on July 17 that in the first five months of this year, the province approved 2,946 new foreign capital utilization contracts, with an actual utilization of $1.1 billion in foreign capital, more than double the amount for the same period last year.

The three special economic zones of Shenzhen, Zhuhai, and Shantou utilized $137 million, $84.78 million, and $15.72 million in foreign capital respectively in the first five months, representing increases of 0.6 times, 4.7 times, and 3.5 times over the same period last year.

From January to June, the province's total industrial output value reached 5 billion yuan, an increase of 25.2% over the same period last year. In the context of a general decline in economic performance of industrial enterprises nationwide, Guangdong's industrial enterprises achieved a growth of 20.98% in taxes and profits, and an increase of 26.23% in taxes and profits remitted to the state. The province's export value reached $3.27 billion, completing 63.3% of the annual plan. Among these, the export value of "three types of enterprises" (foreign-funded, Sino-foreign joint ventures, and Sino-foreign cooperative enterprises) was $670 million, an increase of 66% over the same period last year.

### 广东利用外资续增  头5月达11亿美元
刘霄
1989-07-17
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    广东利用外资续增
    头5月达11亿美元
    本报讯　记者刘霄报道：中国贸促会广东省分会负责人唐向欣7月17日在北京举行的新闻发布会上透露，今年头5个月，全省新批准利用外资合同2946宗，实际利用外资11亿美元，比去年同期增长一倍多。
    三个经济特区深圳、珠海、汕头头5个月实际利用外资1.37亿美元、8478万美元和1572万美元，分别比去年同期增长了0.6倍、4.7倍和3.5倍。
    1至6月份，全省完成工业总产值500亿元，比去年同期增长25.2％。在全国工业企业经济效益普遍下降的情况下，广东省工业企业实现税利增长20.98％，上交税利增长26.23％。全省出口额达到32.7亿美元，完成年计划的63.3％，其中“三资”企业出口额6.7亿美元，比去年同期增长66％。
