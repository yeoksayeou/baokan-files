Title: Xinjiang and the Soviet Union Sign Cooperation Planning Agreement
Author: Fan Yingli
Date: 1989-07-15
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, July 15th.

A delegation led by K.K. Baikenov, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Kazakh Soviet Socialist Republic of the Soviet Union, recently signed a cooperation planning agreement for 1989-1995 in the economic, technological, and trade sectors with the People's Government of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region in Urumqi. The plan includes 78 projects, among which 25 are large-scale projects and 53 are medium and small-scale projects. The total value of these projects will exceed 10 billion yuan.

The two sides agreed to carry out various forms of cooperation and exchanges in the fields of power, chemical industry, non-ferrous metallurgy, steel, geology, communications, textiles, machinery and electronics, building materials, light industry, consumer goods production, and labor services over the next six years. The signed minutes of the talks also include: actively creating conditions to open the Alashankou, Bakht, and Jeminay ports; and establishing direct postal communications between Urumqi and Almaty.

### 新疆与苏联签订合作规划协议
樊英利
1989-07-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    新疆与苏联签订合作规划协议
    据新华社乌鲁木齐7月15日电  （记者樊英利）由苏联哈萨克苏维埃社会主义共和国部长会议副主席卡·卡·巴依克诺夫率领的代表团，日前在乌鲁木齐同新疆维吾尔自治区人民政府签订了1989—1995年经济、技术、贸易合作规划的协议。规划包括78个项目，其中有25个大型项目和53个中小型项目。这些项目的总金额将超过100亿元人民币。
    双方商定在今后6年中，在电力、化工、有色冶金、钢铁、地质、通讯、纺织、机械电子、建材、轻工、民用品生产和劳务合作等领域开展各种方式的合作与交流。双方签署的会谈纪要还包括：积极创造条件开放阿拉山口、巴克图和吉木乃口岸；开通乌鲁木齐—阿拉木图直接邮政通讯。
