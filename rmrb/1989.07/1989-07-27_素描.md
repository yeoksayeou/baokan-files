Title: Sketch
Author: Lü Shaoping
Date: 1989-07-27
Edition: 8

    Sketch
    Lü Shaoping

    A Rose

    Just now, a rose
    Leapt gently from the branch
    A tablecloth
    Is a pool of tender water
    Carefully cradling a light dream
    Thus, the dream also blooms

    The sketchbook still covers half the face
    From the noisy afternoon sun
    Secretly carves out a quiet corner
    To hold the cloth
    To hold the scene
    To hold the rough lines of the charcoal boat
    To fully play the pipa

    Rose
    Layer upon layer, it blooms
    Petal by petal, with heartfelt dedication
    Framed by pure snow
    Seemingly drunk, yet only slightly
    Standing in a windless storm
    Whispering its fragrant name

    素描
    陆少平

    许刚刚从枝上

    从阳光喧闹的下午
    偷辟一隅静谧
    容布
    容景
    容粗线条的炭笔之舟
    尽奏琵琶
    玫瑰
    叠叠开放
    瓣瓣苦心孤诣
    被雪洁映衬
    似醉而微醉
    置身无浪的风波
    低吟芳名
