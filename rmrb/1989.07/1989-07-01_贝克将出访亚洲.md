Title: Baker to Visit Asia
Author:
Date: 1989-07-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, June 30th.

U.S. Secretary of State Baker will begin a five-day visit to Asia next week. A high-ranking official from the State Department said that Baker's trip to Asia will signify a clear and specific commitment from the United States to this region.

In Tokyo, Baker will attend an international conference with 25 participating countries to discuss providing $10 billion in aid to the Philippines over the next five years.

There, Baker will also hold talks with Japan's new Prime Minister Uno and Foreign Minister Mitsuzuka, expressing the U.S.'s intention to advance U.S.-Japan relations to a "global partnership."

In Brunei, Baker will attend a meeting of the Association of Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN) and discuss the Cambodian issue with ASEAN officials.

### 贝克将出访亚洲

1989-07-01
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    贝克将出访亚洲
    新华社华盛顿6月30日电  美国国务卿贝克将于下星期一开始对亚洲作为期5天的访问。国务院一位高级官员说，贝克的亚洲之行将意味着美国对这个地区明确而又具体的承诺。
    在东京，贝克将出席一个由25个国家参加的国际会议，讨论在今后5年里它们向菲律宾提供100亿美元的援助。
    在那里，贝克还将同日本新首相宇野和外相三塚会谈，向日本新的领导人表明，美国打算将美日关系推向“全球性的伙伴关系”。
    在文莱，贝克将出席东南亚国家联盟的一个会议，同东盟的官员们讨论柬埔寨问题。
