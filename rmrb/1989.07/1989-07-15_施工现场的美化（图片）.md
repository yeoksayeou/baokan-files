Title: Beautifying Construction Sites
Author:
Date: 1989-07-15
Edition: 7

When Paris was repairing the stone sculptures on the Place de la Concorde, large billboards were erected on the street side. The craftsmen worked behind these billboards to avoid affecting the street view.

### 施工现场的美化（图片）

1989-07-15
第7版(国际副刊)
专栏：

    施工现场的美化
    巴黎在修复协和广场的石雕像时，先在临街面竖起巨幅画板，工匠在画板后劳作，以免影响街面观瞻。
