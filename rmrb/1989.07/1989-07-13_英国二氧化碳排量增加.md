Author: Wang Guorui
Date: 1989-07-13
Edition: 3

Britain's Carbon Dioxide Emissions Increase

Xinhua News Agency, London, July 11th — (Reporter Wang Guorui) Britain's primary greenhouse gas, carbon dioxide, has seen its emissions steadily increase in recent years, far surpassing the global average growth rate.

The latest data from the United Kingdom's Oak Ridge National Laboratory indicates that in 1987, Britain's carbon dioxide emissions rose from 152 million tons to 156 million tons, an increase of 2.6%, while the global average growth rate was 1.6%.

In contrast, France's carbon dioxide emissions in 1987 decreased by 3.2%, and West Germany's by 2.2%.

Carbon dioxide emissions can be directly calculated based on the consumption of mineral fuels. Figures from the British Energy Department show that with the increase in mineral fuel consumption, Britain's carbon dioxide emissions have continued to rise since 1987. The British Energy Conservation Association predicts that over the next 15 years, Britain's carbon dioxide emissions will increase by 20%.

Carbon dioxide contributes to the greenhouse effect, leading to global warming, which has drawn the attention of the world.

### 英国二氧化碳排量增加
王国瑞
1989-07-13
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    英国二氧化碳排量增加
    新华社伦敦7月11日电　（记者王国瑞）英国主要温室气体二氧化碳的排放量近年来不断增加，大大超过世界平均增长量。
    英国奥克里季国家实验室发表的最新数据说，1987年，英国二氧化碳的排放量从1.52亿吨增加到1.56亿吨，增加2.6％，而世界平均增长量为1.6％。
    法国1987年的二氧化碳排放量下降了3.2％，联邦德国下降了2.2％。
    二氧化碳的排放量可以直接根据矿物燃料的消耗量来计算。英国能源部发表的数字表明，随着矿物燃料消耗量的增加，英国二氧化碳排放量从1987年以来仍在不断增加。英国能源保护协会说，在今后15年内，英国二氧化碳排放量将增加20％。
    二氧化碳造成温室效应，导致全球温度升高，已经引起全世界的重视。
