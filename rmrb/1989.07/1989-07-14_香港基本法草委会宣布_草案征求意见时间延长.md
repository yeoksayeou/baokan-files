Title: The Hong Kong Basic Law Drafting Committee Announces Extension of Consultation Period for the Draft
Author:
Date: 1989-07-14
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, July 14th.

The Hong Kong Basic Law Consultative Committee announced today in Hong Kong that the Hong Kong Basic Law Drafting Committee, with the approval of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, has extended the consultation period for the draft of the Basic Law to the end of October this year.

The Drafting Committee will continue to entrust the Hong Kong Basic Law Consultative Committee to conduct consultations on the draft of the Basic Law in Hong Kong and request the Consultative Committee to submit a consultation report to the Drafting Committee by the end of November. The ninth plenary session of the Drafting Committee will be held as originally scheduled from January 9 to 13, 1990. The draft of the Basic Law, as approved by the ninth plenary session, will be submitted to the third plenary session of the Seventh National People's Congress for deliberation and adoption as originally planned.

In addition, the 27th meeting of the Executive Committee of the Basic Law Consultative Committee will be held on July 20. The meeting will discuss how to further advance the consultation work on the draft of the Basic Law.

### 香港基本法草委会宣布  草案征求意见时间延长

1989-07-14
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    香港基本法草委会宣布
    草案征求意见时间延长
    新华社香港7月14日电  香港基本法咨询委员会今天在此间宣布：香港基本法起草委员会经报请全国人大常委会委员长会议同意，将基本法（草案）征求意见的时间延长至今年10月底。
    基本法起草委员会继续委托香港基本法咨委会在香港开展基本法（草案）的咨询工作，并请咨委会在11月底前向起草委员会提交咨询报告。起草委员会第九次全体会议的会期不变，仍在1990年1月9日至13日举行。经第九次全体会议通过的基本法（草案），仍按原计划报请明年举行的七届人大第三次全体会议审议、通过。
    此外，基本法咨委会执行委员会第27次会议将于本月20日举行。会议将讨论如何进一步开展对基本法（草案）的咨询工作。
