Title: The Old Shek Tea House is Bustling with Guests Again
Author: Xú Jiànzhōng
Date: 1989-07-10
Page: 2

In the midst of the summer, the Old Shek Tea House in Beijing's Qianmen, which had once seen a decline in visitors, has once again become bustling with guests as social order improves.

Top photo: Guests from China and abroad, along with compatriots from Hong Kong and Macau, fill the house.

Bottom photo: The Ricks, a couple from the United States, enjoy tea while appreciating the cultural performances. Photographed by reporter Xú Jiànzhōng of this newspaper.
