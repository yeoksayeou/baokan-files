Title: United Nations Development Programme Provides Assistance Project to China
Author:
Date: 1989-07-05
Edition: 2

The signing ceremony for a project document of assistance provided by the United Nations Development Programme to China was held this evening at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse in Beijing. Those who signed the project document included Maurice Strong, the representative of the United Nations Development Programme in Beijing, Wu Lanmu'er, Assistant Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade, and Zhang Zhijian, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Personnel.

Zhao Dong'an, Minister of the Ministry of Personnel, attended the signing ceremony this evening and had a friendly conversation with Maurice Strong. Minister Zhao Dong'an, on behalf of the Ministry of Personnel, expressed sincere gratitude to Maurice Strong and, through him, to the relevant organizations of the United Nations that care for and support China, as well as to the individuals who have made positive contributions to the project plan.

### 联合国开发计划署向我提供援助项目

1989-07-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    联合国开发计划署向我提供援助项目
    据新华社北京七月四日电　联合国开发计划署向中国提供援助的一个项目的文件签字仪式今晚在北京钓鱼台国宾馆举行。在项目文件上签字的有联合国开发计划署驻北京代表茅瑞先生、对外经济贸易部部长助理乌兰木伦和人事部副部长张志坚。
    人事部部长赵东宛出席了今晚签字仪式，并与茅瑞先生进行了亲切友好的交谈。赵东宛部长代表人事部向茅瑞先生并通过他向联合国关心、支持中国的有关组织，向为项目计划做出积极贡献的人士表示诚挚的谢意。
