Title: China's Men's and Women's Volleyball Teams Set Out
Author:
Date: 1989-07-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 15th.

China's national men's volleyball team departed Beijing today to head to Poland for the international men's volleyball tournament beginning on the 19th.

On the 17th, China's women's volleyball team will also depart for Kiev, Soviet Union, to participate in the annual "Chinilin" International Women's Volleyball Invitational Tournament.

### 中国男女排出征

1989-07-15
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    中国男女排出征
    据新华社北京七月十五日电  中国国家男子排球队今天离开北京，前往波兰参加十九日开始的国际男排赛。
    十七日，中国女排也将前往苏联的基辅，参加每年一度的“奇尼林”国际女排邀请赛。
