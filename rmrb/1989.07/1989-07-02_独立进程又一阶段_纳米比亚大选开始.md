Title: Another Stage in the Independence Process: Namibia's Elections Begin
Author: Lǐ Jǐngwèi
Date: 1989-07-02
Edition: 3

Namibia's elections officially began in Windhoek on July 1. This marks another stage in Namibia's independence process.

Relevant administrative authorities stated that the voter registration office began operations on July 2 under the supervision of the United Nations. A spokesperson said that political parties and groups hoping to secure candidate positions in the elections should also register.

July 1 was designated by the United Nations as Namibia's official election day.

### 独立进程又一阶段  纳米比亚大选开始
李景卫
1989-07-02
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    独立进程又一阶段  纳米比亚大选开始
    本报哈拉雷7月2日电　记者李景卫报道：纳米比亚大选7月1日在温得和克正式开始。这是纳米比亚独立进程的又一阶段。
    有关行政当局说，选民登记办公室7月2日在联合国的监督下开始工作。一位发言人说，那些希望在大选中得到候选人席位的政党团体也应该登记。
    7月1日是联合国指定的纳米比亚正式选举日。
