Title: Outstanding Leader in Health Work: Farewell Ceremony for Cui Yiting Held
Author:
Date: 1989-07-13
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 12th.

The farewell ceremony for Cui Yiting, an outstanding leader in health work in China and the military, a renowned expert in war wound surgery, and a medical educator, former Vice Minister and Advisor of the Ministry of Health, was held today at the Babaoshan Revolutionary Cemetery in Beijing.

Cui Yiting was born in 1906 in Jinxi, Liaoning. In 1938, he joined the New Fourth Army and was one of the earliest senior physicians to establish the New Fourth Army's health work. In 1940, Cui Yiting joined the Communist Party of China and in 1943, he became the Director of the New Fourth Army Health Department. During the War of Resistance Against Japanese Aggression, he bravely and tenaciously treated a large number of wounded and sick soldiers. He also founded the Huazhong Health School, training a large number of health personnel, and was highly praised by Zhou Enlai, Ye Ting, Chen Yi, Su Yu, and others.

During the War of Liberation, Cui Yiting led and participated in the rescue of a large number of wounded soldiers in major battles such as Suzhong, Lusong, Laiwu, Menglianggu, Jinan, Huaihai, crossing the Yangtze River, and the liberation of Shanghai. He established a complete system of military medical and health work suitable for large-scale troop operations. In 1949, he served as the Director of the Shanghai Municipal Health Bureau, taking on the task of reforming the old Shanghai health work. In 1950, Cui Yiting served as the Director of the East China Military and Political Committee Health Department, doing a great deal of work to restore and develop the health work in the East China region. During the Korean War, he actively organized a large number of senior medical personnel to form volunteer medical teams to go to Korea to participate in the war. In October 1954, Cui Yiting served as the Vice Minister of the Central Health Department. For a long time, he devoted himself to the construction and development of the health cause in New China, making significant contributions.

During the "Cultural Revolution," Cui Yiting was falsely accused and cruelly persecuted. After the Gang of Four was smashed, he was completely rehabilitated and served as an advisor to the Ministry of Health. He firmly supported the line, principles, and policies of the Party's Third Plenary Session of the Eleventh Central Committee, adhered to the Four Cardinal Principles, cared about health reform, and devoted his last efforts to China's health cause.

Cui Yiting was a member of the Fourth, Fifth, and Sixth National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and was known as an outstanding member of the Communist Party of China and a loyal communist warrior who had been tested by many trials.

Those who sent floral wreaths to Cui Yiting's body included Jiang Zemin, Deng Xiaoping, Yang Shangkun, Chen Yun, Wan Li, Li Xiannian, Peng Zhen, Deng Yingchao, Yao Yilin, Wang Zhen, Li Tieying, Song Renqiong, Wu Xiuquan, Li Yimang, Yu Quli, Zhang Aiping, Lu Dingyi, Chen Xilian, Hu Qiaomu, Duan Junyi, Huang Hua, Huang Zhen, Xi Zhongxun, Peng Chong, Fang Yi, Gu Mu, Kang Keqing, and others.

Jiang Hua, Song Shiwu, Zhang Jinfu, Chen Pishan, Ji Pengfei, Cheng Zihua, Ye Fei, Hong Xuezhi, Zhang Zhen, and others attended the farewell ceremony.

### 优秀的卫生工作领导者  崔义田遗体告别仪式举行

1989-07-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    优秀的卫生工作领导者
    崔义田遗体告别仪式举行
    新华社北京7月12日电　我国我军优秀的卫生工作领导者，著名的战伤外科专家及医学教育家，卫生部原副部长、顾问崔义田同志遗体告别仪式，今天在北京八宝山革命公墓举行。
    崔义田同志1906年出生于辽宁锦西，1938年加入新四军，是最早创建新四军卫生工作的高级医师之一。1940年，崔义田加入中国共产党，1943年就任新四军卫生部部长。在抗日战争期间，他出生入死，英勇顽强地救治了大批伤病员，并创办华中卫生学校，培养出大批卫生技术干部，受到周恩来、叶挺、陈毅、粟裕等人的高度赞扬。
    解放战争时期，崔义田领导并参加了苏中、鲁南、莱芜、孟良崮、济南、淮海、渡江、解放上海等历次重大战役的大量伤员救治工作，建立了一整套适宜大兵团作战的军队医疗卫生工作体制。1949年，他任上海市卫生局局长，担负起改造旧上海卫生工作的重任。1950年，崔义田任华东军政委员会卫生部部长，为恢复与发展华东地区卫生事业做了大量工作。在抗美援朝战争中，他积极组织大批高级医务人员组成志愿医疗队赴朝参战。1954年10月，崔义田任中央卫生部副部长。长期以来，他呕心沥血，为新中国卫生事业的建设、发展做出了重大贡献。
    “文化大革命”中，崔义田受到无端诬陷和残酷迫害。粉碎“四人帮”后，他得以彻底平反，任卫生部顾问。他坚决拥护党的十一届三中全会以来的路线、方针、政策，坚持四项基本原则，关心卫生改革，为我国卫生事业尽了最后余力。
    崔义田是全国政协第四、五、六届委员，并被誉为中国共产党优秀党员，久经考验的共产主义忠诚战士。
    向崔义田同志遗体敬献花圈的有：江泽民、邓小平、杨尚昆、陈云、万里、李先念、彭真、邓颖超、姚依林、王震、李铁映、宋任穷、伍修权、李一氓、余秋里、张爱萍、陆定一、陈锡联、胡乔木、段君毅、黄华、黄镇、习仲勋、彭冲、方毅、谷牧、康克清等。
    江华、宋时轮、张劲夫、陈丕显、姬鹏飞、程子华、叶飞、洪学智、张震等参加了遗体告别仪式。
