Title: Cars Developing Towards Fuel Efficiency
Author: Wang Guorui
Date: 1989-07-27
Edition: 3

According to a report by the *Times*, by the year 2007, cars may only consume one gallon of gasoline to travel 160 kilometers, while the current fuel consumption is approximately 3 gallons.

This simple comparison of figures shows that by the end of this century and the beginning of the next, the automobile industry will undergo significant changes in considering fuel efficiency.

The development trend of the automobile industry, of course, can save a large amount of fuel economically, but its main driving force comes from environmental protection.

Currently, Western countries are taking measures to impose taxes on fuel-guzzling old cars to promote their rapid elimination.

### 汽车向节油型发展
王国瑞
1989-07-27
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    汽车向节油型发展
    新华社伦敦7月24日电　（记者王国瑞）据《泰晤士报》报道，到2007年，汽车跑160公里可能只消耗汽油一加仑，而目前的耗油量约为3加仑。
    从这一简单的数字对比可以看出，本世纪末下世纪初，汽车工业将在考虑节油问题上发生重大的变化。
    汽车工业的这种发展动向，固然可以在经济上节省大量的燃料，但是其主要推动力是来自环境保护。
    目前，西方国家采取的措施是，对耗油量大的老式汽车要上税，以促其尽快淘汰。
