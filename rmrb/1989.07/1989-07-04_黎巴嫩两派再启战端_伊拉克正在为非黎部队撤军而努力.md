Title: Lebanese Factions Resume Hostilities; Iraq Works on Withdrawal of Non-Lebanese Forces
Author:
Date: 1989-07-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, July 3rd. Lebanese security officials reported that the "Amal" movement's militia and the "Hezbollah" militia resumed fighting in the western part of Beirut this morning. Both sides fired rockets and artillery, causing damage to several residential buildings.

This latest round of fighting occurred after a night of ceasefire, which was called for by the Syrian command in the western area. The Syrian military command is currently in contact with the opposing Muslim organizations to halt the fighting.

The "Amal" militia and the "Hezbollah" militia began a new round of intense fighting on July 2nd. In the fighting on that day, three people were killed and 17 were injured.

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, July 3rd. Iraqi President Saddam Hussein emphasized today during a meeting with foreign ministers from the three-member Arab committee mediating the Lebanese crisis that Iraq is working to withdraw non-Lebanese forces from the country and ensure Lebanon's independence and sovereignty as an Arab nation.

The foreign ministers of Saudi Arabia, Morocco, and Algeria arrived in Baghdad on July 2nd to brief the Iraqi leadership on the committee's recent efforts to mediate the Lebanese crisis. The committee is also seeking to end the 14-year civil war by stopping Iraq and Syria from supporting the Christian government forces and Muslim militias in Lebanon.

Currently, Syria has 35,000 troops stationed in Lebanon, Israel occupies parts of southern Lebanon, and Iran has 2,000 Revolutionary Guards present.

### 黎巴嫩两派再启战端  伊拉克正在为非黎部队撤军而努力

1989-07-04
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    黎巴嫩两派再启战端
    伊拉克正在为非黎部队撤军而努力
    新华社贝鲁特7月3日电　黎巴嫩治安人士说，黎巴嫩穆斯林“阿迈勒”运动民兵同“真主党”民兵今晨在贝鲁特西区再次交战。
    双方发射火箭和大炮，使一些民宅受到毁坏。
    此次战斗是在叙利亚支持的“阿迈勒”运动民兵与伊朗支持的“真主党”民兵在叙利亚驻西区部队指挥部的呼吁下实行一夜停火后再次打响的。
    安全人士说，叙军指挥部正在同两派敌对的穆斯林组织进行接触，以使战斗停止。
    “阿迈勒”运动民兵与“真主党”民兵于本月2日开始了新一轮的激战，在2日的战斗中有3人被打死，17人受伤。
    新华社巴格达7月3日电　伊拉克总统萨达姆·侯赛因今天在这里会见阿拉伯调解黎巴嫩危机三方委员会成员国的外长时强调，伊拉克正在为非黎巴嫩军队撤出这个国家和确保黎巴嫩作为一个独立、主权的阿拉伯国家而努力。
    沙特阿拉伯外交大臣费萨尔亲王、摩洛哥外交大臣菲拉利和阿尔及利亚外长贝赛伊2日抵达巴格达，向伊拉克领导人通报了该委员会最近调解黎巴嫩危机的情况。
    这个委员会还寻求伊拉克和叙利亚停止支持黎巴嫩交战的基督教政府军和穆斯林民兵，以结束这个国家历时14年的内战。
    黎巴嫩目前驻有叙利亚军队3.5万人，以色列军队占领着黎巴嫩南部的一些领土，伊朗也驻有2000名革命卫队。
