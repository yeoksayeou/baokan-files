Title: China Science and Technology Exhibition Opens in the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1989-07-15
Edition: 2

	The China Science and Technology Exhibition, which showcases the achievements of China's 40 years of socialist construction, particularly the scientific and technological advancements made during the past 10 years of reform and opening up, opened on July 14 in Moscow.

	Song Jian, the leader of the Chinese government delegation and the Minister of the State Science and Technology Commission, emphasized in his opening remarks that under the guidance of the reform and opening-up policy, China's doors have been opened wide to the world. Lavrov, the Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers and Chairman of the State Science and Technology Commission, responded by saying that the achievements of the past 10 years of reform have made people respect China even more, and he hoped to further tap the potential for Sino-Soviet economic and technological cooperation.

	Subsequently, Lavrov and the President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Marchuk, accompanied by Song Jian, visited the exhibition.

	The China Science and Technology Exhibition is held in the spacious and well-equipped Soviet National Economic Achievements Exhibition Hall, covering an area of 7,000 square meters. It displays 400 pictures and models, and 1,600 real exhibits, of which more than 400 can be purchased and left behind. Many Soviet units have already expressed their desire to purchase them. This exhibition is the main activity of the China Science and Technology Day held in Moscow from July 14 to 23. During the Science and Technology Day, famous Chinese scholars will also hold academic seminars and screen nearly 100 films and video tapes.

### 中国科技展览会在苏开幕

1989-07-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    中国科技展览会在苏开幕
    新华社莫斯科7月14日电  介绍中国40年社会主义建设成就，特别是改革开放10年来取得的部分科技成就的中国科技展览会14日在莫斯科开幕。
    中国政府代表团团长、国务委员兼国家科委主任宋健在致开幕词时强调指出，在改革开放方针指引下，中国的大门已向全世界敞开。苏联部长会议副主席兼国家科委主席拉维奥罗夫致答词时说，10年改革的成就使人对中国更加尊重，希望进一步发掘中苏经贸和科技合作的潜力。
    随后，拉维奥罗夫和苏联科学院院长马尔丘克等在宋健陪同下参观了展览会。
    中国科技展览会设在场地开阔、设施齐全的苏联国民经济成就展览馆，展览面积7000平方米，展出图片和模型400件，实物展品1600件，其中400余件可以留购，已有不少苏联单位表示希望购买。这一展览是7月14至23日在莫斯科举办的中国科技日的主要活动。在科技日期间，中国著名学者还将举行学术报告会，放映近百部影片和录像片。
