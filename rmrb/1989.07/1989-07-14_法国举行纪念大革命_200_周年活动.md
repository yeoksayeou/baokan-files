Title: France Holds Activities to Mark the 200th Anniversary of the French Revolution
Author:
Date: 1989-07-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, July 14th.

France's 200th anniversary celebrations of the French Revolution reached their peak today. Tens of thousands of citizens and foreign tourists gathered along the streets from the Arc de Triomphe to the Place de la Concorde to watch the traditional military parade. Tonight, a cultural evening with the theme of singing "La Marseillaise" will be held at the Place de la Concorde, with an estimated audience of 1.5 million.

Yesterday, French President François Mitterrand and 33 heads of state and government attended the official ceremony held in Paris at the Place du Trocadéro.

### 法国举行纪念大革命200周年活动

1989-07-14
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    法国举行纪念大革命200周年活动
    本报巴黎7月14日电　法国大革命200周年庆典活动今天达到高潮。几十万市民和外国旅游者上午聚集在凯旋门至协和广场的街道两旁观看传统的阅兵式。今晚在协和广场将举行以歌唱《马赛曲》为主题的文艺晚会，估计观众达150万。
    昨天法国总统密特朗和33个国家总统和总理出席了官方在巴黎特罗卡德罗广场举行的庆祝仪式。
