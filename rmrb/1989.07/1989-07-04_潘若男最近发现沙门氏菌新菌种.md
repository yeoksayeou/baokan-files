Title: Pan Ruonan Discovers New Strains of Salmonella
Author: Lu Jing
Date: 1989-07-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, July 4th.

Pan Ruonan, deputy chief physician at the Nanchang Medical Research Institute, has recently discovered new strains of Salmonella. Among these, four strains are the first to be discovered internationally, and eight strains are the first to be discovered domestically. This achievement has been verified by the Chinese Medical Bacteria Preservation and Management Center.

Salmonella is an intestinal pathogen that can cause a wide range of diseases, including typhoid fever, paratyphoid fever, and food poisoning. It is a mandatory inspection item for epidemic monitoring and import-export food.

### 潘若男最近发现沙门氏菌新菌种
卢晶
1989-07-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    潘若男最近发现沙门氏菌新菌种
    据新华社南昌七月四日电（记者卢晶）南昌市医科研究所副主任医师潘若男最近发现了沙门氏菌新菌种，其中四株为国际首次发现，八株为国内首次发现。这一成果已通过中国医学细菌保藏管理中心的鉴定。
    沙门氏菌是一种肠道病原菌，能广泛引起伤寒、副伤寒、食物中毒等病症，是疫情监测及进出口食品的必检项目。
