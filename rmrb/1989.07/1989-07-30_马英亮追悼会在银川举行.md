Title: Funeral for Ma Yingliang Held in Yinchuan
Author:
Date: 1989-07-30
Edition: 2

The funeral for Ma Yingliang, an outstanding member of the Communist Party of China, a Hui leader, and a member of the Standing Committee of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region Party Committee and vice chairman of the autonomous region, was held today in Yinchuan.

Ma Yingliang passed away on July 23 in Yinchuan due to a sudden illness at the age of 59.

Yang Jingren, Qian Zhengying, and other officials, as well as the State Council Office, the Organization Department, the United Front Work Department, the National People's Congress Ethnic Affairs Committee, the State Ethnic Affairs Commission, the Ministry of Water Resources, the Ministry of Forestry, and the Ningxia Party, government, and military leadership sent wreaths.

The funeral, attended by over 1,000 people, was presided over by Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region Party Committee Secretary Shen Daren. Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region Chairman Bai Licheng delivered the eulogy.

The eulogy stated that Ma Yingliang was one of the first outstanding minority cadres cultivated by our Party in Ningxia. For a long time, he had been deeply concerned about changing the poverty-stricken and backward situation in the southern mountainous areas of Ningxia. In accordance with the State Council's guidelines for the development of the "Three Western" regions and in combination with the actual situation in Ningxia, he summarized and formulated a set of methods and measures, accelerating the pace of poverty alleviation and prosperity for the various ethnic groups in the southern mountainous areas.

The eulogy also said that throughout his life, Ma Yingliang was loyal to the Party's cause, with a high sense of revolutionary responsibility, and selflessly worked for the Party and the people, dedicating his entire life to the cause.

Ma Yingliang was born in Yinchuan, Ningxia. He joined the Communist Party of China in 1950 and began revolutionary work the same year. He held various positions, including Director of the United Front Work Department of the Yinchuan Municipal Party Committee, Deputy Mayor of Yinchuan, Director of the Autonomous Region Water Conservancy Bureau, Member and Standing Committee Member of the Fifth and Sixth Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region Party Committees, and Vice Chairman of the Autonomous Region.

### 马英亮追悼会在银川举行

1989-07-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    马英亮追悼会在银川举行
    新华社银川7月29日电  中国共产党的优秀党员、回族领导干部、宁夏回族自治区党委常委、自治区副主席马英亮同志的追悼会今天在银川举行。
    马英亮同志因患急病，于7月23日在银川逝世，终年59岁。
    杨静仁、钱正英等和国务院办公厅、中组部、统战部、全国人大民委、国家民委、水利部、林业部、宁夏党政军领导机关等送了花圈。
    中共宁夏回族自治区党委书记沈达人主持了有1000多人参加的追悼会，宁夏回族自治区主席白立忱致悼词。
    悼词说，马英亮同志是我们党在宁夏培养的第一批优秀的少数民族干部。长期以来，他一直很关心改变宁夏南部山区的贫困落后面貌，按照国务院关于“三西”地区的建设方针，结合宁夏实际，总结制定了一整套办法和措施，加快了南部山区各族人民脱贫致富的步伐。
    悼词说，马英亮同志一生忠诚党的事业，以高度的革命事业心和责任感，忘我地为党和人民工作，贡献了毕生的精力。
    马英亮同志生于宁夏银川市，1950年加入中国共产党，同年参加革命工作。历任中共银川地委统战部部长，银川市副市长，自治区水利局局长，中共宁夏回族自治区第五届、第六届委员会委员、常委，自治区副主席等职。
