Title: Book News
Author: Huang Chengju  Tie Fan
Date: 1989-07-13
Edition: 6

	Book News
	△ The book "Modernization and Cultural Change" by Yao Shuping has been published by Shaanxi Science and Technology Press. The book introduces modernization theory and various viewpoints. It discusses modernization from the perspectives of its categories, essential content, China's historical process of modernization, the challenges faced, possible paths, and its relationship with traditional Chinese culture. It proposes that modernization is a comprehensive transformation process involving economics, politics, society, and culture. The book emphasizes the discussion of cultural change in group behavior reconstruction, considering it an important aspect of modernization. (Tie Fan)

	△ The book "Outline of Propaganda Science Research" by Lin Zhida has been published by Sichuan Academy of Social Sciences Press. The book provides a thorough argument for the necessity, feasibility, and irreplaceability of establishing propaganda science. The author aims to dispel the notion that "news equals propaganda" and the method of conflating propaganda with news, communication, education, and other fields. The book conducts a series of comparative studies on propaganda, communication, news, education, military command, administrative leadership, and ideological and political work, proposing the unique connotations and extensions of propaganda that distinguish it from news, communication, education, and ideological and political work.

	The book selects and explores the direction of propaganda science research in China. (Huang Chengju)

### 书讯
黄成炬  铁帆
1989-07-13
第6版(理论研究)
专栏：

    书讯
    △姚蜀平所著的《现代化与文化的变迁》已由陕西科学技术出版社出版。该书介绍了现代化理论及各类观点，它从现代化的范畴、实质内容、中国进行现代化的历史、面临的困难、可能的途径以及和中国传统文化的关系等方面进行了探讨。提出现代化是一个经济、政治、社会和文化全面变迁的过程，并着重讨论了群体行为重建的文化变迁问题，认为它是现代化的一个重要方面。　（铁帆）
    △林之达所著的《宣传科学研究纲要》已由四川省社会科学院出版社出版。该书对建立宣传科学的必要性、可行性和不可替代性进行了充分论证。作者力图破除“新闻等于宣传”的观念、和把宣传与新闻、传播、教育等混和一体的考察方法，对宣传与传播、新闻、教育、军事指挥、行政领导和思想政治工作进行了一系列比较研究，提出了宣传不同于新闻、传播、教育、思想政治工作的独特内涵和外延。
    该书对宣传科学研究的方案进行了筛选，探索了我国宣传科学研究的方向。　（黄成炬）
