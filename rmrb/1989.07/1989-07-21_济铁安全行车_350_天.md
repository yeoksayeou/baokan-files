Title: Jizhou Railway Achieves 350 Days of Safe Operation
Author: Kong Jian, Yang Changqing
Date: 1989-07-21
Edition: 2

Jizhou Railway Achieves 350 Days of Safe Operation

People's Daily reports: As of July 20, 18:00, the Jinan Railway Bureau has achieved 350 consecutive days without major or serious train accidents. The number of safe operation days ranks first among the 12 railway bureaus in the country.

Since late April this year, the 140,000 employees of the Jinan Railway Bureau have, in the face of severe disruptions to railway transportation, adhered to the principle of "safety first, prevention foremost." Leaders at all levels have insisted on going to the front lines of production, focusing on the weak links in safety production.

### 济铁安全行车350天
孔鉴　杨长青
1989-07-21
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    济铁安全行车350天
    本报讯　截至7月20日18时，济南铁路局实现了连续350天无行车重大、大事故。安全生产天数在全国12个铁路局中名列第一。
    今年4月下旬以来，济南铁路局的14万干部职工，在铁路运输秩序受到严重干扰的困难情况下，坚持贯彻“安全第一、预防为主”的方针，各级第一管理者坚持深入生产第一线，狠抓安全生产的薄弱环节。（孔鉴　杨长青）
