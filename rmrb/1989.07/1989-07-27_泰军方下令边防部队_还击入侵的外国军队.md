Title: Thai Military Orders Border Troops to Counterattack Invading Foreign Forces
Author: Liu Aicheng
Date: 1989-07-27
Edition: 3

Thai military spokesman Naradol said this morning at a press conference that Acting Supreme Commander and Army Commander Chavalit has ordered border troops to counterattack foreign forces invading Thai territory.

The spokesman said that since the beginning of this month, Vietnamese troops invading Cambodia have fired 1,385 shells into Thai territory, destroying many houses, injuring at least four Thai villagers, and forcing many border residents to relocate. Yesterday, Vietnamese troops invading Cambodia fired more than 100 shells into three Thai villages across from the Malai Mountains while attacking Khmer Rouge forces.

Naradol also revealed that the Soviet Union sent 5,000 tons of weapons and oil to Cambodia last month. These weapons are evidently part of a planned aid to the Phnom Penh regime's forces to suppress the resistance.

### 泰军方下令边防部队  还击入侵的外国军队
刘爱成
1989-07-27
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    泰军方下令边防部队
    还击入侵的外国军队
    本报曼谷7月27日电  记者刘爱成报道：泰国武装部队发言人纳罗敦今天上午在记者招待会上说，泰国武装部队代理最高司令兼陆军司令差瓦立已下令边防部队还击侵犯泰国领土的外国军队。
    这位发言人说，自本月初以来，侵柬越军向泰国领土发射了1385发炮弹，击毁许多民房，至少有4名泰国村民受伤，大批边民被迫转移。昨天，侵柬越军在泰国亚兰对面的马莱山进攻红色高棉部队阵地时，有100多发炮弹落入泰国三个村庄。
    纳罗敦中将还透露说，苏联上个月向柬埔寨运送了5000吨武器和原油，这些武器显然是有计划地援助金边政权部队打击抵抗力量的。
