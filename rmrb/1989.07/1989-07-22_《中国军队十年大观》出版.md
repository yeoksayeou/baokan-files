Title: Publication of "A Decade of the Chinese Army"
Author:
Date: 1989-07-22
Edition: 4

A large-scale documentary literature titled "A Decade of the Chinese Army" (co-authored by Wang Jinjun and Zhang Liyuan), which reflects a series of major events of the People's Liberation Army from 1978 to 1988, has been published and distributed by the PLA Press.

### 《中国军队十年大观》出版

1989-07-22
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    《中国军队十年大观》出版
    本报讯　反映人民解放军一九七八至一九八八年间一系列重大事件的大型纪实文学《中国军队十年大观》（王晋军、张莉娟合著），已由解放军出版社出版发行。
