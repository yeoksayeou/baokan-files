Title: U.S. and Soviet Foreign Ministers to Meet in Paris
Author:
Date: 1989-07-27
Page: 3

State Department spokeswoman Margaret Tutwiler announced on the 26th that Secretary of State Baker plans to meet with Soviet Foreign Minister Eduard Shevardnadze in Paris. Tutwiler said that the foreign ministers of the United States and the Soviet Union will exchange views on bilateral relations and regional issues in Paris on the 29th, with a focus on discussing some urgent issues, including the Middle East problem.

### 美苏外长将在巴黎会晤

1989-07-27
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美苏外长将在巴黎会晤
    美国务院女发言人塔特怀勒26日在新闻发布会上宣布，国务卿贝克计划在巴黎会见苏联外长谢瓦尔德纳泽。塔特怀勒说，美苏两国外长29日将在巴黎就双边关系和地区问题交换意见，并集中讨论包括中东问题在内的一些紧迫问题。
