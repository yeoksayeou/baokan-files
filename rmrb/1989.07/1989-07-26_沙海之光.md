Title: Light in the Desert Sands
Author: Yan Yan
Date: 1989-07-26
Page: 8

	Light in the Desert Sands
	Photography by Yu Junhai, Poetry by Yan Yan

	Just at this clear evening
	The gentle breeze flows freely
	On the body of the earth
	Many beautiful curves rise…

	Unable to resist this
	Intoxicating softness
	I entrust my dreams
	Gently, I wipe clean several times
	The pupils that have long been prepared
	But in this moment
	I will advance my life into the future

### 沙海之光
严妍
1989-07-26
第8版(副刊)
专栏：诗画配

    沙海之光
    于俊海摄影　严妍配诗
    恰恰在这清丽的傍晚
    细风尽情地流淌
    在大地的胴体上
    隆起许多美妙的曲线……
    忍不住　这令人
    陶醉的柔和　寄与我
    梦幻的嘱托
    轻轻地　拭洗了几遍
    瞳孔中　早已未雨绸缪
    只是在这一瞬间
    我将生命　预支未来（附图片）
