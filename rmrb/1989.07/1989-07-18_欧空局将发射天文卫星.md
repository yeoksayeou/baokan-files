Title: European Space Agency to Launch Astronomical Satellite
Author:
Date: 1989-07-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, London, July 17th.

According to a report by the *Nature* magazine, the European Space Agency will launch an astronomical satellite on July 27th from French Guiana using an Ariane-4 rocket. This satellite is named Hipparcos, after the name of a Greek astronomer.

The satellite is planned to have a lifespan of two and a half years. Its mission is to determine the positions of approximately 120,000 stars with an accuracy of 2 to 4 milliarcseconds. Additionally, it will measure the astronomical parameters of 400,000 stars.

### 欧空局将发射天文卫星

1989-07-18
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    欧空局将发射天文卫星
    新华社伦敦7月17日电　据《自然》杂志报道，欧洲空间局将于7月27日在法属圭亚那用阿丽亚娜—4号火箭发射一颗天文卫星。这颗天文卫星名叫喜帕恰斯，以希腊天文学家名字命名的。
    这颗卫星的计划寿命为2年半，任务是测定约12万颗恒星的位置，精度为2—4毫弧度秒。此外，还要测量40万颗恒星的天文参数。
