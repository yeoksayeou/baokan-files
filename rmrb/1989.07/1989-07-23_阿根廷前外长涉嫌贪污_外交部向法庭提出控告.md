Title: Argentine Former Foreign Minister Accused of Embezzlement: Ministry of Foreign Affairs Files a Lawsuit
Author: Lǐ Zhìmíng
Date: 1989-07-23
Edition: 3

According to reports from local newspapers, Argentine former Foreign Minister Caputo is suspected of embezzling public funds and has been sued by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

The deputy secretary of administrative management of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs formally filed a complaint with a criminal court. The complaint states that from 1984 to 1988, Caputo made multiple official visits and spent a total of $538,200 on entertainment expenses. Out of this amount, $189,577.16 in receipts are suspicious. These receipts, although from different countries (the United States, Sweden, Cyprus), all bear the same paid stamp.

The court has accepted the case.

### 阿根廷前外长涉嫌贪污  外交部向法庭提出控告
李志明
1989-07-23
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    阿根廷前外长涉嫌贪污
    外交部向法庭提出控告
    本报布宜诺斯艾利斯7月22日电　记者李志明报道：据此间报纸报道，阿根廷前外交部长卡普托涉嫌贪污公款，被外交部提出控告。
    外交部行政管理副国务秘书向一刑事法庭正式提出检举。检举书称，卡普托从1984年到1988年多次出访期间，共开支交际费538200美元，其中189577.16美元的发票有疑点。这部分发票虽然分属不同国家（美国，瑞典，塞浦路斯）却盖着同一个已付印章。
    法院已经受理此案。
