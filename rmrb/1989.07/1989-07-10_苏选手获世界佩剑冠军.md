Title: Soviet Athlete Wins World Sabre Championship
Author:
Date: 1989-07-10
Edition: 4

Soviet Athlete Wins World Sabre Championship

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 9th: According to foreign news reports, Soviet athlete G. Kirienko won the men's sabre individual championship at the World Fencing Championships held in Denver, USA, last night.

The 24-year-old Kirienko defeated Polish athlete A. Koniusz 10-7 in the final. Koniusz won the silver medal, and the bronze medal was taken by F. Becker of the Federal Republic of Germany.

### 苏选手获世界佩剑冠军

1989-07-10
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    苏选手获世界佩剑冠军
    新华社北京7月9日电　据外电报道，苏联选手格·基里恩科昨晚在美国丹佛举行的世界击剑锦标赛上夺得了男子佩剑个人冠军。
    24岁的基里恩科在决赛中以10∶7战胜波兰选手亚·科纽什。科纽什获得亚军，取得第3名的是联邦德国的费·贝克尔。
