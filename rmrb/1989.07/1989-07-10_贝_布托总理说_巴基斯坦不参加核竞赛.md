Title: Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto Says Pakistan Will Not Engage in Nuclear Arms Race
Author:
Date: 1989-07-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, London, July 9th.

Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto of Pakistan, who is currently visiting the UK, stated in an interview with a British television station today that her country will not participate in a nuclear arms race and is willing to work with India to prevent nuclear proliferation in the subcontinent.

Benazir Bhutto said, “We hope to make this region a place free from the threat of nuclear proliferation. Therefore, we are willing to work with our neighboring country, India, to avoid any potential pressure in the future.”

She also pointed out that if any country in this region detonates a nuclear device, “it will exert tremendous pressure on other countries, forcing them to follow suit.”

Benazir Bhutto had previously called on India to make efforts to prevent a nuclear arms race in this region.

### 贝·布托总理说  巴基斯坦不参加核竞赛

1989-07-10
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    贝·布托总理说
    巴基斯坦不参加核竞赛
    新华社伦敦7月9日电  正在英国访问的巴基斯坦总理贝娜齐尔·布托今天在接受英国电视台采访时表示，她的国家不参加核武器竞赛，并愿意和印度一道努力防止在次大陆地区的核扩散。
    贝·布托说：“我们希望使我们这一地区成为不受核扩散威胁的地区。因此，我们愿意和我们的邻国印度一道做出努力，避免今后可能出现的任何压力。”
    她同时也指出，如果这一地区的某个国家爆炸了核装置，“它就会对其他国家产生巨大压力，迫使它们仿效”。
    贝·布托前天也要求印度作出努力，以避免在这一地区出现核武器竞赛。
