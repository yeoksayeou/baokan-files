Title: Pakistani Senate Delegation Concludes Visit to China
Author: Yu Shichang
Date: 1989-07-04
Edition: 2

The Pakistani Senate delegation, led by Chairman Wasim Sajjad, successfully concluded its friendly visit to China. Today at noon, they departed from Kunming by airplane, heading to Hong Kong before returning to their home country.

Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) Yang Jingren, Chairman of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the CPPCC Liu Shusheng, and others saw them off at the airport. Both parties bid farewell warmly at the airport. Chairman Wasim Sajjad said, "We have been warmly welcomed wherever we went, feeling the warmth and friendship of the Chinese people, which is a symbol of the friendship between Pakistan and China. We have personally witnessed the tremendous progress China has made in the past few years, and we would like to congratulate the Chinese leadership and people on this."

The Pakistani Senate delegation visited China at the invitation of Chairman Li Xiannian of the National Committee of the CPPCC.

### 巴基斯坦参议院代表团结束访华
鱼世昌
1989-07-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    巴基斯坦参议院代表团结束访华
    据新华社昆明7月4日电　（记者鱼世昌）由瓦西姆·萨贾德主席率领的巴基斯坦参议院访华代表团，圆满结束了对中国的友好访问，今天中午乘飞机离开昆明，取道香港回国。
    全国政协副主席杨静仁、云南省政协主席刘树生等到机场送行。宾主在机场热情话别。瓦西姆·萨贾德主席说，我们所到之处都受到热烈欢迎，感受到中国人民的热情和友爱，这是巴中友谊的象征。我们亲眼看到中国在过去几年中的巨大进展，对此，我们要向中国领导人和人民祝贺。
    巴基斯坦参议院访华代表团是应全国政协主席李先念邀请前来中国访问的。
