Title: China's Stamp Collecting Magazine Wins Award in the US
Author: Lǐ Méi
Date: 1989-07-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 10th.

The English-language magazine "China Philately," published in China, recently won a gold medal at the 13th Cardinal Spellman International Philatelic Literature Exhibition held in the United States.

"China Philately" was first published in 1982 and is now distributed to more than 60 countries and regions across five continents. Its primary goal is to introduce Chinese stamps and philately activities and to promote the export of Chinese stamps. Since its inception, this English magazine has won awards four times at international philatelic literature exhibitions in Italy, Bolivia, Canada, and the United States.

### 《中国集邮》在美获奖
李玫
1989-07-10
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    《中国集邮》在美获奖
    新华社北京7月10日电　（记者李玫）我国出版的英文《中国集邮》杂志，前不久在美国举行的第13届卡迪纳尔·斯别尔曼国际集邮书刊博览会上荣获镀金奖。
    英文《中国集邮》杂志1982年创刊，现在已发行到五大洲的60多个国家和地区。它以介绍我国邮票和集邮活动，促进我国邮票出口为主要宗旨。从创刊至今，这本英文杂志已先后4次在意大利、玻利维亚、加拿大、美国的国际集邮书刊博览会上获奖。
