Title: The Yangtze River's Flood Peak Passes Through Jiujiang, Heavy Rain in Northeast and Other Regions
Author: Dǒng Hóngbiāo, Liú Xiǎoníng
Date: 1989-07-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, July 19th. The largest flood peak of the Yangtze River this year safely passed through Jiujiang, Jiangxi Province this morning. This flood peak arrived at Jiujiang at 14:00 yesterday, reaching a water level of 20.27 meters, which is 1.27 meters above the warning level. The water level has now started to gradually decrease.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 19th. According to the Central Meteorological Observatory, between yesterday 6:00 and this morning 6:00, heavy rainfall of 10 to 30 millimeters occurred in most parts of Northeast China, western Shandong, Guanzhong, Longdong, and western Sichuan.

It is forecasted that from today to tomorrow, most parts of Northeast China, eastern Inner Mongolia, southern Hebei, northern Shandong, northern Henan, Guanzhong, southern Shaanxi, Longdong, and western Sichuan will experience rainfall of 5 to 20 millimeters. In particular, Guanzhong, northern Henan, northern Shandong, and parts of Northeast China may receive rainfall of 20 to 50 millimeters.

### 长江洪峰过九江  东北等地降暴雨
董鸿彪  刘小宁
1989-07-19
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    长江洪峰过九江
    东北等地降暴雨
    新华社南昌7月19日电  （通讯员董鸿彪、记者刘小宁）长江今年入汛以来最大洪峰今晨已安全通过江西九江市。这次洪峰是昨天14时到达九江的，到达时水位高达20.27米，超出警戒线1.27米。目前水位已开始缓慢下降。
    据新华社北京7月19日电  据中央气象台消息，昨日6时至今晨6时，东北大部、鲁西北、关中、陇东和川西等地出现了10至30毫米的降雨。
    预计今天到明天，东北大部、内蒙古东部以及华北南部、鲁北、豫北、关中、陕南、陇东和川西等地将有5至20毫米的降雨，其中关中、豫北、鲁北和东北部分地区降雨量可达20至50毫米。
