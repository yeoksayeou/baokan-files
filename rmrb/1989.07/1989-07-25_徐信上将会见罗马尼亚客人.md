Title: General Xu Xin Meets Romanian Guests
Author:
Date: 1989-07-25
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 25th.

General Xu Xin, Vice Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, met today in the afternoon with a group of Romanian military officers led by Major General Nicolae Kishkiev, who are on vacation in China. Both hosts and guests praised the traditional friendship between the two countries and their armies.

Xu Xin analyzed the current international situation and briefed the guests on the suppression of the counter-revolutionary riots in Beijing.

### 徐信上将会见罗马尼亚客人

1989-07-25
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    徐信上将会见罗马尼亚客人
    据新华社北京7月25日电中国人民解放军副总参谋长徐信上将今天下午在此间会见尼什基奥普少将率领的罗马尼亚军官休假组，宾主盛赞了中罗两国、两军的传统友谊。
    徐信分析了当前国际形势，并向客人介绍了平息北京反革命暴乱的情况。
