Title: Poland Solemnly Celebrates National Day
Author:
Date: 1989-07-23
Edition: 3

Poland solemnly celebrated its National Day on July 22. In the capital, Warsaw, a solemn ceremony of laying wreaths at the Tomb of the Unknown Soldier and the changing of the guard at the tomb was held to commemorate the 45th anniversary of the issuance of the July Declaration by the Polish National Liberation Committee. Other cities also held corresponding celebrations. Polish President Jaruzelski and others attended the memorial activities in the capital.

### 波兰隆重庆祝国庆节

1989-07-23
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    波兰隆重庆祝国庆节
    22日是波兰国庆节，首都华沙举行了隆重的向无名战士墓献花圈和墓地守卫战士换岗仪式，以纪念波兰民族解放委员会7月宣言发表45周年。其他城市也分别举行了相应的庆祝活动。波兰总统雅鲁泽尔斯基等出席了首都的纪念活动。
