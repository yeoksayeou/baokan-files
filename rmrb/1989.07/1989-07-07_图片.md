Title: Thailand Police Burn 2600 Kilograms of Drugs in Bangkok
Author:
Date: 1989-07-07
Page: 7

Recently, Thai police in Bangkok set fire to and destroyed 2600 kilograms of drugs to demonstrate Thailand's efforts as one of the major drug-producing countries in the world to combat drug trafficking activities.

Xinhua News Agency
