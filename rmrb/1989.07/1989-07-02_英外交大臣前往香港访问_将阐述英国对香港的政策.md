Title: British Foreign Secretary to Visit Hong Kong
Author:
Date: 1989-07-02
Edition: 3

British Foreign Secretary Geoffrey Howe today departed for Hong Kong for a three-day visit to elaborate on Britain's policy towards Hong Kong.

According to reports, during his visit, Howe will reaffirm Britain's commitment to Hong Kong's future.

The British House of Commons Foreign Affairs Committee yesterday released a report stating, "The best future for the people of Hong Kong is in Hong Kong. The best way to ensure this future is to maintain Hong Kong's economic success and to strengthen and stabilize its democratic institutions."

Regarding the Sino-British Joint Declaration, the report states, "Abrogating this declaration and terminating all negotiations with China in the foreseeable future is not an option."

The report suggests using the Joint Declaration as "the best and most reliable foundation for Hong Kong's future."

### 英外交大臣前往香港访问  将阐述英国对香港的政策

1989-07-02
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    英外交大臣前往香港访问
    将阐述英国对香港的政策
    新华社伦敦7月1日电  英国外交大臣杰弗里·豪今天动身前往香港，进行为期3天的访问，以阐述英国对香港的政策。
    据报道，访问期间，豪将重申英国对香港未来的义务。
    英国下院外交事务委员会昨天在一份报告中说，“香港人的最好前途在香港，保障这一前途的最好办法是保持香港的经济成功及加强和稳定民主制度。”
    关于中英香港联合声明，报告说，“放弃这一声明，在可预见的未来中断同中国的所有谈判，不是一个办法。”
    报告建议将联合声明作为“香港未来的最好和最可靠的基础”。
