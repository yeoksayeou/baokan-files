Title: Papua New Guinea Joins the Southeast Asia Treaty
Author:
Date: 1989-07-09
Edition: 3

The Association of Southeast Asian Nations announced on July 8 in Bandar Seri Begawan, the capital of Brunei, that Papua New Guinea has formally joined the Treaty of Amity and Cooperation in Southeast Asia, becoming the first non-ASEAN member to join the treaty.

### 巴新加入东南亚条约

1989-07-09
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    巴新加入东南亚条约
    东南亚国家联盟八日在文莱首都斯里巴加湾港宣布，巴布亚新几内亚已正式加入东南亚友好合作条约，成为第一个加入该条约的非东盟成员国。
