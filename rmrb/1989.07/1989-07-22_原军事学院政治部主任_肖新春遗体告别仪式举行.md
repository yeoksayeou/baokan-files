Title: Funeral Service for Former Political Department Director of Military Academy, Xiao Xinchun, Held
Author:
Date: 1989-07-22
Edition: 2

The funeral service for former Political Department Director of the Military Academy, Xiao Xinchun, was held in Nanjing on July 6.

Xiao Xinchun passed away on June 27 in Nanjing due to illness at the age of 80. Xu Xiangqian, Wang Zhen, Wang Ping, Wang Shoudao, Xiao Ke, Yu Qiuyi, Liao Hansheng, and Wang Enmao, along with the Central Military Commission, the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the Ministry of National Defense, the three main departments of the PLA, the Military Commission's Discipline Inspection Commission, the Nanjing Military Region, the National Defense University, Jiangsu Province, and the original hometown of Xiao Xinchun in Jiangxi Province's Jian and Yongxin County, sent wreaths.

Xiao Xinchun joined the labor union and peasant association in 1927, the Communist Party of China in 1929, and the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army in 1932. He served in various positions, including Political Department Director of a Red Army division, Political Commissar of a regiment, Political Department Director of the 8th Route Army's direct unit, Political Department Director and Political Commissar of a brigade, Political Commissar of a division, Secretary of a regional committee and Political Commissar of a sub-region, Deputy Director and Director of the Personnel Department of the Military Academy's Political Department, among others. He was a member of the 5th National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and was awarded the rank of Major General in 1955.

Xiao Xinchun was a seasoned communist warrior, an outstanding member of the Communist Party of China, and an excellent commander and political leader of the army. He had a firm communist ideology and belief, was loyal to the revolutionary cause, and took his work extremely seriously. He had rich political work experience and excellent political leadership abilities, a strong sense of party discipline and organizational principles. He was morally upright, modest, fair, consistent in words and deeds, united with comrades, approachable, set an example, sought truth from facts, was open and upright, honest and upright, and strict with his family and children, always maintaining the political integrity and fine traditions of hard work and self-reliance of our party and army.

### 原军事学院政治部主任  肖新春遗体告别仪式举行

1989-07-22
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    原军事学院政治部主任
    肖新春遗体告别仪式举行
    新华社南京7月22日电  向原军事学院政治部主任肖新春同志遗体告别的仪式7月6日在南京举行。
    肖新春同志因病于6月27日在南京逝世，终年80岁。徐向前、王震、王平、王首道、肖克、余秋里、廖汉生、王恩茂等，中央军委、政协全国委员会、国防部、解放军三总部、军委纪委、南京军区、国防大学、江苏省以及肖新春同志的原籍江西省吉安地区和永新县等党政军领导机关送了花圈。
    肖新春同志1927年参加工会和农民协会，1929年加入中国共产党，1932年参加中国工农红军。
    他历任红军师政治部主任、团政治委员、八路军师直属队政治部主任、旅政治部主任、旅政治委员，师政治委员、地委书记兼分区政治委员，军事学院政治部副主任兼干部部部长等职。他是第五届全国政协委员，1955年被授予少将军衔。
    肖新春同志是久经考验的共产主义战士，中国共产党的优秀党员，我军优秀指挥员和政治工作领导者。他有坚定的共产主义思想和信念，对革命事业忠心耿耿，对工作极端负责任。他有丰富的政治工作经验和优秀的政治工作领导才能，有坚强的党性和组织纪律观念。他品德高尚，谦虚谨慎，公道正派，言行一致，团结同志，平易近人，以身作则，实事求是，光明磊落，廉洁奉公，对家属子女要求严格，始终保持了我党我军艰苦奋斗的政治本色和优良传统。（附图片）
