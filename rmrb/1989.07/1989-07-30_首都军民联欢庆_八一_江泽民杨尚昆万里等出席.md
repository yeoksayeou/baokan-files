Capital Military and Civilians Celebrate "August 1st"
Jiang Zemin, Yang Shangkun, Wan Li Attend

BEIJING, July 30 (Xinhua) — A military and civilian celebration was held in the capital today to mark the 62nd anniversary of the founding of the People's Liberation Army.

The celebration, jointly organized by the Ministry of Civil Affairs, the General Political Department, and the Beijing Municipal Government, took place at the Great Hall of the People. At 3 p.m., CPC Central Committee General Secretary Jiang Zemin, State President Yang Shangkun, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Wan Li, and other leaders including Tian Jiyun, Qin Jiwei, Liu Lan Tao, Jiang Hua, Yang Dezhi, Yu Qiuyu, Habib, Peng Chong, Zhu Lirun, Ni Zhifu, Chen Muxia, Zhou Jiaxuan, Li Guixian, Chen Xitong, Ren Jianxin, Yan Mingfu, Hong Xuezhi, Liu Huaqing, Chi Haotian, Yang Baibing, Zhao Nanqi, and Cui Naifu, along with responsible officials from the PLA General Staff, the Beijing Garrison, the martial law troops, the People's Armed Police, the Ministry of Civil Affairs, and the Beijing Municipal Government, gathered at the Great Hall of the People. They joined over 3,000 military and civilian residents of the capital to celebrate the August 1st Army Day.

The celebration began with the military band playing "The People's Army Marches Toward the Sun." During the event, artists from the General Political Department's Art Troupe, the Navy Art Troupe, the Beijing Military Region's Friendship Art Troupe, the China Acrobatic Troupe, the Beijing Quyi Troupe, and the Beijing Song and Dance Troupe performed a series of exciting artistic programs. They sang and danced, celebrating the truth that "Without the Communist Party, there would be no New China," the patriotic spirit of soldiers who "willingly dedicate their youth to the motherland," and the deep bond between the military and the people as they "advance shoulder to shoulder."

Jiang Zemin, Yang Shangkun, Wan Li, and other party and state leaders watched the performances at the celebration.

### 首都军民联欢庆“八一”  江泽民杨尚昆万里等出席
贾昭全
1989-07-30
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    首都军民联欢庆“八一”
    江泽民杨尚昆万里等出席
    本报北京7月30日讯　记者贾昭全报道：今天下午，首都举行军民联欢会，庆祝中国人民解放军建军62周年。
    由民政部、总政治部和北京市政府联合主办的军民联欢会在人民大会堂举行。下午3点钟，中共中央总书记江泽民、国家主席杨尚昆、全国人大常委会委员长万里和田纪云、秦基伟、刘澜涛、江华、杨得志、余秋里、习仲勋、彭冲、朱学范、倪志福、陈慕华、邹家华、李贵鲜、陈希同、任建新、阎明复、洪学智、刘华清、迟浩田、杨白冰、赵南起、崔乃夫同志以及解放军三总部、驻京部队各大单位、首都戒严部队、武警部队、民政部、北京市的负责人来到大会堂，同3000多首都军民欢聚一起，共庆“八一”建军节。
    联欢会在“人民军队永远向太阳”的军乐声中开始。会上，总政文工团、海政文工团、北京军区战友文工团、中国杂技团、北京曲艺团、北京歌舞团等文艺团体的文艺工作者表演了精彩的文艺节目。他们载歌载舞，歌唱了“没有共产党就没有新中国”的真理，歌唱了人民战士“愿把青春献祖国”的爱国主义精神，歌唱了“军民并肩向前进”的鱼水情谊。 （附图片）
