Title: Tianjin Youths Win National Junior Football Championship
Author: Wang Jianmin
Date: 1989-07-08
Edition: 4

Tianjin Youths Win National Junior Football Championship

According to Xinhua News Agency, Stenyang, July 7th: The 1989 National Excellent Junior Football Team Tournament concluded on July 7th in Stenyang. The Tianjin team won the championship with a record of 5 victories in 5 matches. The Dalian team secured the runner-up position, and the Beijing team finished in third place.

### 天津小将夺全国少年足球冠军
王健民
1989-07-08
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    天津小将夺全国少年足球冠军
    据新华社沈阳7月7日电（记者王健民）1989年全国优秀少年足球队调赛7日在沈阳结束。天津队以5战全胜的战绩夺得冠军，大连队获亚军，北京队名列第三。
