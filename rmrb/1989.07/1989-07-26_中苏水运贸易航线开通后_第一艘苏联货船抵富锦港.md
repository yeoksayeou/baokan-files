Title: First Soviet Cargo Ship Arrives at Fujin Port After Opening of Sino-Soviet Waterway
Author:
Date: 1989-07-26
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, July 26th.

The first Soviet cargo ship, loaded with 1,300 tons of fertilizer, has recently arrived at Fujin Port via the Songhua River after the opening of the Sino-Soviet waterway.

The cargo ship departed from Khabarovsk in the Soviet Union. Meanwhile, China's first cargo ship to sail to the Soviet Union has also recently arrived in Khabarovsk.

### 中苏水运贸易航线开通后  第一艘苏联货船抵富锦港

1989-07-26
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    中苏水运贸易航线开通后
    第一艘苏联货船抵富锦港
    新华社哈尔滨7月26日电　中苏水运贸易航线开通后，苏联第一艘满载1300吨化肥的货船日前驶入松花江，抵达富锦港。
    这艘货船来自苏联哈巴罗夫斯克。我国首航苏联的货轮也于日前抵达苏联哈巴罗夫斯克。
