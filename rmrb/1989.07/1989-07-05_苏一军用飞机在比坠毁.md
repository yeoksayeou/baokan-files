Title: A Soviet Military Aircraft Crashes in Belgium
Author:
Date: 1989-07-05
Edition: 3

A Soviet MiG-23 fighter jet crashed near the Belgian border in the vicinity of Kortrijk on the morning of July 4. The aircraft destroyed a farmhouse, resulting in the death of an 18-year-old youth who was at home.

### 苏一军用飞机在比坠毁

1989-07-05
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏一军用飞机在比坠毁
    4日上午，一架苏联米格23战斗机在比利时边境的库尔特雷附近坠毁。飞机摧毁一座农舍，正在家中的一位18岁青年因此丧生。
          （本报专电）
