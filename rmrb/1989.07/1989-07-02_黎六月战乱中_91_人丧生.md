Title: 91 Killed in Lebanon's June Turmoil
Author:
Date: 1989-07-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, July 1st.

According to a Lebanese police official, 91 people were killed and 183 injured in various conflicts in Lebanon during June.

The official stated that 72 people died in the artillery battle between Syrian-backed Muslim militias and the Christian government forces led by Oren. Since the outbreak of intense fighting between the two sides in mid-March this year, 380 people have been killed.

Among the casualties in June, 10 people were killed in several Israeli air raids.

### 黎六月战乱中91人丧生

1989-07-02
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    黎六月战乱中91人丧生
    新华社贝鲁特7月1日电  据黎巴嫩警方人士透露，在黎巴嫩6月份的各种冲突中，有91人被打死，183人受伤。
    这位人士说，有72人是在叙利亚支持的穆斯林民兵与奥恩领导的基督教政府军的炮战中丧生的。自今年3月中旬双方发生激战以来，已有380人被打死。
    在6月份的伤亡人数当中，有10人是在以色列的数次空袭中被打死的。
