Title: UK Announces Increased Aid to Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1989-07-11
Edition: 3

The British government announced on the 11th that it will increase aid to Pakistan by more than 55 million pounds over the next few years. Of this amount, 25 million pounds will be used for teacher training, improving living conditions and sanitation for the poor, and improving supply conditions in rural areas.

### 英国宣布增加对巴援助

1989-07-11
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    英国宣布增加对巴援助
    英国政府11日宣布，在今后几年内增加对巴基斯坦价值超过5500万英镑的援助。其中，2500万英镑用于教师培训，改善贫民居住、卫生条件和农村地区的供应状况。
            （本报专电）
