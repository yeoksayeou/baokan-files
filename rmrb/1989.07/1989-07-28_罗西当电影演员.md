Title: Rossi as a Movie Actor
Author: Jin Biao
Date: 1989-07-28
Edition: 4

People still vividly remember how the Italian football star Paolo Rossi contributed significantly to Italy's victory in the 1982 World Cup held in Spain. In April 1990, just before the World Cup held in Italy, Rossi will appear as a member of the Italian national football team in a movie, meeting the audience.

### 罗西当电影演员
晋标
1989-07-28
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    罗西当电影演员
    人们记忆犹新，意大利足球名将保罗·罗西，在西班牙举行的一九八二年世界杯足球锦标赛中为意大利队夺得冠军立下显赫功劳。一九九○年四月，即在意大利举行世界杯足球赛前夕，罗西将作为电影中的意大利国家足球队队员与观众见面。晋标
