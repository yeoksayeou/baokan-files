Title: Qian Qichen Visits Six Southern African Countries
Author:
Date: 1989-07-24
Edition: 2

At the invitation of the foreign ministers of Botswana, Zimbabwe, Angola, Zambia, Mozambique, and the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Lesotho, Foreign Minister Qian Qichen departed this evening to begin a friendly visit to the aforementioned six countries starting on July 26.

### 钱其琛出访南部非洲六国

1989-07-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    钱其琛出访南部非洲六国
    新华社北京7月24日电　应博茨瓦纳、津巴布韦、安哥拉、赞比亚、莫桑比克外长和莱索托外交大臣的邀请，外交部长钱其琛今晚离开这里，将于7月26日起对上述六国进行友好访问。
