Title: Hai Cheng Hospital Rewards Whistleblowers
Author: Wáng Zhōngqí
Date: 1989-07-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, July 22nd.

Hai Cheng City People's Hospital in Liaoning Province recently implemented a new policy: staff members who engage in bribery, such as accepting gifts or money, are encouraged to be reported. Those who report such behavior and are verified will be rewarded handsomely. Since the announcement of this policy, medical staff have collectively refused to accept 1,140 yuan in gifts and have done over 30 good deeds for patients.

### 海城医院奖励举报者
王忠奇
1989-07-22
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    海城医院奖励举报者
    新华社沈阳7月22日电　（通讯员王忠奇）辽宁省海城市人民医院最近作出新规定：院内职工如有收取酬金、物品等索贿受贿行为，欢迎举报。经查实后，举报者得重奖。新规定公布后，医务人员已累计拒收礼金1140余元，为患者做好事30余件。
