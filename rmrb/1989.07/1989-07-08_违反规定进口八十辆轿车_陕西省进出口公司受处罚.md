Title: Shaanxi Provincial Import-Export Company Fined for Violating Regulations to Import 80 Cars
Author: Xià Lín  Zhū Hóng Bō
Date: 1989-07-08
Edition: 4

The Shaanxi Provincial Import-Export Company was recently fined by the Tianjin Customs for violating customs regulations by importing 80 small cars. The company's appeal for reconsideration was also rejected.

In late February of this year, a batch of imported Fiat small cars arrived at the Tianjin New Port. As the consignee, the Shaanxi Provincial Import-Export Company, without approval, arbitrarily changed the original order form from 80 Zotye small trucks to Fiat small cars, violating national regulations. Based on this, the customs confiscated the 80 small cars and imposed a fine.

### 违反规定进口八十辆轿车  陕西省进出口公司受处罚
夏林  朱洪波
1989-07-08
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    违反规定进口八十辆轿车  陕西省进出口公司受处罚
    新华社天津7月8日电（记者夏林、通讯员朱洪波）陕西省进出口公司违反海关监管规定进口80辆小轿车，最近被天津海关依法处罚。这家公司提出的复议也被驳回。
    今年2月中旬，一批进口的菲亚特牌小轿车运抵天津新港。作为收货单位的陕西省进出口公司，未经批准，擅自将原订货卡片上的80辆茹克牌小货车改为菲亚特牌小轿车，违反国家规定。据此，海关依法将80辆小轿车没收，并予以罚款。
