Title: Joint Venture Successfully Develops 5600 kW Synchronous Motor
Author: Ní Miàozhāng
Date: 1989-07-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, July 30th.

A joint venture between Shanghai Electric Machinery Factory and Siemens of the Federal Republic of Germany has successfully produced its first 5600 kW synchronous motor. Recently, German experts verified that the quality of this motor meets the advanced international standards of the 1980s.

The 5600 kW synchronous motor is a key component for large-capacity air separation equipment used in the metallurgical and chemical industries.

### 中外企业合作研制成功  五千六百千瓦同步电机
倪妙章
1989-07-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    中外企业合作研制成功
    五千六百千瓦同步电机
    新华社上海7月30日电  （通讯员倪妙章）上海电机厂与联邦德国西门子公司合作生产出来的第一台5600千瓦同步电机，最近经德方专家验证，其质量达到国际80年代先进水平。
    5600千瓦同步电机，是冶金、化工等行业大容量空气分离设备的配套电机。
