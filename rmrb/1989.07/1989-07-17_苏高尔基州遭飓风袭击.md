Title: Gorky Region Hit by Hurricane
Author:
Date: 1989-07-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 17th.

A hurricane struck the Gorky Region of the Soviet Union on the evening of the 16th, resulting in three deaths and severe damage to state and private property.

According to a report by TASS on the 17th, the hurricane was accompanied by heavy rain and hail, with wind speeds reaching 24 meters per second. The hurricane caused trees to fall, roofs to be blown off, crops to be destroyed, and power outages.

### 苏高尔基州遭飓风袭击

1989-07-17
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏高尔基州遭飓风袭击
    新华社莫斯科7月17日电  16日晚，一场飓风袭击了苏联高尔基州，致使三人死亡，国家和居民财产遭受严重损失。
    据塔斯社17日报道，这场飓风伴有暴雨和冰雹，风力达到每秒24米。飓风所到之处，树倒屋掀，庄稼毁坏，电力中断。
