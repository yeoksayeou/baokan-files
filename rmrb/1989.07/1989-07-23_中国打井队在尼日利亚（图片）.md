Title: Chinese Drilling Team in Nigeria
Author: Duán Jíyǒng
Date: 1989-07-23
Page: 3

The Chinese drilling team successfully drilled multiple wells in Sokoto State, Nigeria, alleviating the local residents' water shortage and receiving praise from all quarters. The team consists of 22 workers and 4 drilling rigs. Along with the hired local Nigerian workers, they have been operating in a 250-kilometer expanse of wilderness, enduring hardships and working day and night. In just nine months, they completed the contracted task of drilling 650 boreholes.

The Chinese drilling team's rig operator (left) and the Nigerian engineering supervisor discuss a new construction plan (top image).

Nigerian drillers take turns to taste the cool, sweet well water (bottom image).

### 中国打井队在尼日利亚（图片）
段吉勇
1989-07-23
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    中国打井队在尼日利亚
    中国打井队在尼日利亚索科托州成功地打出多眼水井，解除了当地群众缺水之苦，受到各方好评。这支打井队共二十二名施工人员、四台钻机，他们和雇用的尼籍工人一起，在纵横二百五十公里的荒原上流动作业，风餐露宿，日夜奋战，九个月里，就完成六百五十个钻孔的承包考核指标。
    中国打井队钻机机长（左）和尼方工程监理一起研究新的施工方案（上图）。
    尼日利亚钻工们轮流品尝清凉甘甜的井水（下图）。　新华社记者　段吉勇摄
