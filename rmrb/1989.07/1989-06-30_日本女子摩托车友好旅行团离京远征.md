Title: Japanese Women's Motorcycle Friendship Travel Team Sets Out from Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-06-30
Edition: 4

	Japanese Women's Motorcycle Friendship Travel Team Sets Out from Beijing

	According to a report from our newspaper, at the invitation of the China International Sports and Tourism Company, the Japanese Women's Motorcycle Friendship Travel Team departed from Beijing's Temple of Heaven's Hall of Prayer for Good Harvests on the morning of June 28, beginning an 85-day motorcycle journey across the Chinese mainland. The route will cover Beijing, Hebei, Shandong, Henan, Shaanxi, Gansu, Xinjiang, and Qinghai, returning via the original path to Zhengzhou, then passing through Hubei, Anhui, and Zhejiang, and finally departing from Shanghai. The total distance is approximately 16,000 kilometers. The team consists of 7 members, with an average age of 24. The oldest member is 27, and the youngest is 22.

### 日本女子摩托车友好旅行团离京远征

1989-06-30
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    日本女子摩托车友好旅行团离京远征
    本报讯　应中国国际体育旅游公司邀请，日本女子摩托车友好旅行团6月28日清晨从北京天坛祈年殿出发，开始为期85天的跨越中国大陆的摩托车旅行，行驶路线为：北京、河北、山东、河南、陕西、甘肃、新疆、青海，再由原路至郑州，经湖北、安徽、浙江，最后从上海出境，全程约16000公里。该队7名队员平均年龄24岁，最大年龄27岁，最小年龄22岁。
