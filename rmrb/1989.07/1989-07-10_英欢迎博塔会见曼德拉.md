Title: UK Welcomes Botha's Meeting with Mandela
Author:
Date: 1989-07-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, London, July 9th.

The British government spokesman today welcomed South African President Botha's meeting with Nelson Mandela, the black leader of the African National Congress who is currently imprisoned.

The British spokesman said, "We consider this to be a very positive step."

It is speculated that Mandela may be released, and the conditions for his release may involve him influencing countries that have imposed economic sanctions on South Africa.

### 英欢迎博塔会见曼德拉

1989-07-10
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    英欢迎博塔会见曼德拉
    据新华社伦敦7月9日电  英国政府发言人今天对南非总统博塔会见被监禁中的南非人国民大会的黑人领袖纳尔逊·曼德拉表示欢迎。
    英国发言人说：“我们认为这是一个十分积极的步骤。”
    据猜测，曼德拉可能将被释放，释放的条件可能是要求曼德拉对向南非实行经济制裁的国家施加影响。
