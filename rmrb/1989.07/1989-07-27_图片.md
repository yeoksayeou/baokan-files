Title: French Youths Protest Against Nuclear Weapons
Author: Xú Bù
Date: 1989-07-27
Edition: 7

On July 8th, two French youths carried a model of an atomic bomb on their shoulders while protesting on the streets of Paris. Their placard read: "Money should be used to aid the Third World, not to produce bombs."
