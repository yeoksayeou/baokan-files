Title: Nigeria to Host the 1991 World Youth Football Championship
Author:
Date: 1989-07-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 26th.

Nigeria will host the 1991 World Youth Football Championship.

According to foreign news reports, Nigeria has been chosen to host the 1991 World Youth Football Championship. In the vote by the International Football Federation, 11 votes were in favor of Nigeria, while 6 votes were against, defeating competitors Portugal and Australia. Portugal and Australia are expected to host the 1993 and 1995 World Youth Football Championships, respectively.

### 尼日利亚将主办世界青年足球锦标赛

1989-07-28
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    尼日利亚将主办世界青年足球锦标赛
    新华社北京7月26日电　据外电报道，尼日利亚将主办1991年世界青年足球锦标赛。
    在国际足球联合会的表决中，支持尼日利亚的有11票、反对的有6票，击败了竞争对手葡萄牙和澳大利亚，获得了1991年世界青年足球锦标赛主办权。而葡萄牙和澳大利亚有望分别主办1993年和1995年世界青年足球锦标赛。
