Title: Ode to the Military Flag
Author: Gù Liándì
Date: 1989-07-20
Edition: 8

	You are like a soldier,
	With a face of pride,
	Shining with the fiery
	Glory of warriors.

	You are like a soldier,
	Longing for blooming flowers,
	Unhesitatingly using blood
	To loyally sprinkle.

	You are like a soldier,
	Raising mountains,
	Displaying indestructible strength
	And an immortal life.
