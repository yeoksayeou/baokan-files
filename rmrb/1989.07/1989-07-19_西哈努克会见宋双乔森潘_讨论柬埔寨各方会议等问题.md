Title: Sihanouk Meets with Son Sann and Khieu Samphan
Author: Zheng Jinfa
Date: 1989-07-19
Edition: 3

Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Chairman of the Democratic Kampuchea, met with Son Sann, Chairman of the Khmer People's National Liberation Front, and Khieu Samphan, Vice-Chairman of the Democratic Kampuchea, in Paris on July 18 and 19. They discussed the upcoming meetings of various Cambodian factions and the preparations for the international conference on the Cambodian issue.

Sihanouk pointed out that he will attend the meeting of various Cambodian factions to be held from July 25 to 26 in La Celle-Saint-Cloud, France.

Sihanouk also revealed that the head of the delegation from his faction to the international conference on the Cambodian issue will be his son, Prince Norodom Sihanouk.

The international conference on the Cambodian issue is scheduled to be held in Paris on July 30.

### 西哈努克会见宋双乔森潘  讨论柬埔寨各方会议等问题
郑金发
1989-07-19
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    西哈努克会见宋双乔森潘
    讨论柬埔寨各方会议等问题
    新华社巴黎7月19日电  民柬主席西哈努克亲王18日和19日在巴黎分别会见了高棉人民民族解放阵线主席宋双和民柬副主席乔森潘，同他们就即将召开的柬埔寨各方会议以及柬埔寨问题国际会议的准备工作进行了讨论。
    西哈努克指出，他将出席将于25日至26日在法国的拉塞勒—圣克卢举行的柬埔寨各方会议。
    西哈努克还透露，参加柬埔寨国际会议的西哈努克派代表团团长将由他的儿子拉那烈王子担任。
    关于柬埔寨问题的国际会议将于本月30日在巴黎举行。
