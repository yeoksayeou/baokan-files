Title: High Concentration of Brine Discovered Underground in Northern Bo Hai Bay
Author: Zhang Hongwen
Date: 1989-07-20
Edition: 2

The Chinese Academy of Sciences' Institute of Oceanology has recently discovered high-concentration underground brine resources in the Nanpu Salt Field area on the northern shore of Bo Hai Bay. This discovery opens up a new avenue for salt production in China.

This brine is a type of Quaternary coastal underground brine, a new kind of liquid evaporation deposit, with a salt content three to six times higher than that of seawater.

Prior to this, the institute also discovered a 20-kilometer-long underground brine resource along the coast of Lai Zhou Bay in Bo Hai, with abundant reserves.

### 渤海湾北岸地下发现高浓度卤水
张宏文
1989-07-20
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    渤海湾北岸地下发现高浓度卤水
    新华社天津七月二十日电  （记者张宏文）中科院海洋研究所最近在渤海湾北岸的南堡盐场地区发现高浓度地下卤水资源，为我国原盐生产开辟了新的途径。
    这种卤水为第四纪滨海相地下卤水，是一种新型液体蒸发矿藏，含盐量比海水高三至六倍。
    在此之前，这个研究所还在渤海莱州湾沿岸发现长达二十公里的地下卤水资源，储量十分丰富。
