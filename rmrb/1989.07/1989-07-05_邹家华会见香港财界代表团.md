Title: Zhou Jiahuai Meets with Hong Kong Financial Sector Delegation
Author:
Date: 1989-07-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 5th.

State Councilor Zhou Jiahuai met with a delegation from the Hong Kong financial sector this afternoon in the Great Hall of the People.

Zhou Jiahuai welcomed the delegation's visit. He said that some Hong Kong compatriots and foreign friends do not fully understand the recent events in Beijing and may have some misunderstandings. As the saying goes, "Seeing is believing." He welcomed Hong Kong compatriots and foreign friends to come to China to see for themselves. He emphasized that China's policy of opening up to the outside world will not change, either now or in the future.

The delegation's leader, Liu Lianhong, Administrative Director of the Hong Kong Amex Group Limited, said that mainland China has vast territory and enormous potential, and will continue to implement the policy of reform and opening up. We are full of confidence in China's future prospects.

Present at the meeting were Beijing Vice Mayor Zhang Baifa and others.

### 邹家华会见香港财界代表团

1989-07-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    邹家华会见香港财界代表团
    新华社北京7月5日电  国务委员邹家华今天下午在人民大会堂会见了香港财界代表团。
    邹家华对代表团的到来表示欢迎。他说，有些香港同胞和外国朋友对北京最近发生的事情不太了解，甚至有点误会。俗话说，“百闻不如一见”，欢迎香港同胞和外国朋友到中国来看一看。他表示，中国政府实行对外开放政策，不仅现在不变，而且将来也不会变。
    代表团团长、香港爱美高集团有限公司行政董事刘銮鸿说，中国内地幅员辽阔，潜力巨大，并且将继续执行改革开放政策，我们对中国未来前景充满信心。
    会见时在座的有北京市副市长张百发等。
