Title: The Third World Games Open
Author: Jiang Jian Guo
Date: 1989-07-21
Edition: 4

The third World Games opened yesterday in the city of Karlsruhe, West Germany. Thousands of athletes from 60 countries gathered in the "European Hall." International Olympic Committee President Juan Antonio Samaranch attended the opening ceremony, expressing the IOC's support for the goals of the World Games.

The World Games include 21 non-Olympic sports, such as tug-of-war, rugby, bowls, trampolining, and orienteering. The first two games were held in California, USA, in 1981, and London, UK, in 1985.

Karlsruhe will host a variety of cultural events during the games, including music, theater, and dance performances. Some sports events will also invite spectators to participate.

### 第三届世界运动会开幕
江建国
1989-07-21
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    第三届世界运动会开幕
    本报波恩7月21日电　记者江建国报道：第三届世界运动会昨天在西德的卡尔斯鲁厄市开幕，来自世界60个国家的几千名运动员聚集在“欧洲大厅”内。国际奥委会主席萨马兰奇出席了开幕式，以此表示国际奥委会支持世界运动会的宗旨。
    世界运动会包括21个非奥林匹克运动项目，如拔河、橄榄球、地滚球、跳床、定向跑等等。1981年和1985年先后在美国的加利福尼亚和英国伦敦举行过两届。
    卡尔斯鲁厄将在运动会期间举办丰富多彩的文化节目，如音乐、戏剧、舞蹈演出等。一些体育项目还邀请观众一道参加。
