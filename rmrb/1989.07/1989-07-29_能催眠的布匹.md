Title: Fabrics That Induce Sleep
Author: Yuè Zhái
Date: 1989-07-29
Page: 7

A Japanese company recently developed a type of fabric specifically designed for bedding materials for insomnia sufferers. Once insomnia patients use bedsheets, comforter covers, and pillowcases made from this fabric, they can sleep peacefully at night without the torment of insomnia. The reason this fabric has a sleep-inducing effect is that the polyester threads used to weave it have been soaked in the essential oils of 50 different plants known for their calming and soothing properties. The scent released by this fabric can "guide" insomnia sufferers into a tranquil sleep.

### 能催眠的布匹
月翟
1989-07-29
第7版(国际副刊)
专栏：

    能催眠的布匹
    日本的一家公司不久前研制出了一种专供失眠症患者制作床上用品的布匹。失眠人一经使用上用这种布匹制作的床单、被罩和枕套以后，夜里便能安然入睡而不再蒙受失眠之苦了。这种布匹之所以具有催眠的效能，是因为织成这种布匹所使用的聚酯线曾在具有镇静和安神作用的50种植物之芳香焦油中浸泡过。而由它散发出的气味则能够“引导”失眠人进入宁静的梦乡。
