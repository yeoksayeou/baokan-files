Title: Pakistan Rejoins the Commonwealth
Author: Chen Te'an
Date: 1989-07-10
Edition: 3

    Pakistan Rejoins the Commonwealth
    Our reporter Chen Te'an reports from London: The 48 member countries of the Commonwealth have unanimously agreed to allow Pakistan to rejoin the organization. In October, Pakistan will attend the Commonwealth Heads of Government Meeting in Malaysia as an official member.

    Pakistan announced its withdrawal from the Commonwealth in 1972.

### 巴基斯坦重返英联邦
陈特安
1989-07-10
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：本报最后消息

    巴基斯坦重返英联邦
    本报伦敦七月十日电　记者陈特安报道：四十八个英联邦成员国已一致同意巴基斯坦重新加入英联邦。今年十月，巴基斯坦将作为英联邦的正式成员国出席在马来西亚举行的英联邦政府首脑会议。
    巴基斯坦是于一九七二年宣布退出英联邦的。
