Title: Former Cuban Minister of Transport Sentenced
Author:
Date: 1989-07-25
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency,

The Cuban newspaper *Granma* reported on the 24th that former Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Transport of Cuba, Diocles Torralba, was sentenced to 20 years in prison. At the same time, all of his ill-gotten wealth has been confiscated.

### 古巴前运输部长被判刑

1989-07-25
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    古巴前运输部长被判刑
    古巴《格拉玛报》24日报道，前古巴部长会议执委会副主席兼运输部长迪奥克莱斯·托拉尔瓦被判处20年有期徒刑。同时，他的不义之财全部被没收。
          （据新华社）
