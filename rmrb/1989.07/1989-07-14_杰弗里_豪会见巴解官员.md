Title: Geoffrey Howe Meets with PLO Officials
Author:
Date: 1989-07-14
Edition: 3

British Foreign Secretary Geoffrey Howe met with advisor to PLO Executive Committee Chairman Arafat, Shalif, on July 13 in London. This was the first time a British cabinet minister had met with a senior official of the Palestine Liberation Organization.

### 杰弗里·豪会见巴解官员

1989-07-14
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    杰弗里·豪会见巴解官员
    英国外交大臣杰弗里·豪13日在伦敦会晤巴解执委会主席阿拉法特的顾问沙利夫。这是英国政府一位内阁大臣首次会见巴勒斯坦解放组织的高级官员。
