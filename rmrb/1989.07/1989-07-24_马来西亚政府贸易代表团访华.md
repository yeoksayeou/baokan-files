Title: Malaysian Government Trade Delegation Visits China
Author:
Date: 1989-07-24
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 24th.

A Malaysian government trade delegation led by Trade and Industry Minister Rafidah Aziz arrived in Beijing today to exchange views with relevant Chinese authorities on the development of bilateral economic and trade cooperation.

Chinese Minister of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Zheng Tuobin said at a welcome banquet this evening that the economic and trade cooperation between China and Malaysia has been steadily developing, with last year's trade volume reaching 870 million US dollars.

Minister Rafidah expressed satisfaction with the increasing trade volume between Malaysia and China over the past few years. She hopes to strengthen the ties and cooperation between enterprises of the two countries.

### 马来西亚政府贸易代表团访华

1989-07-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    马来西亚政府贸易代表团访华
    据新华社北京7月24日电　以贸易和工业部部长拉菲达为团长的马来西亚政府贸易代表团今日到达北京，就发展双边经贸合作问题同中国有关方面交换意见。
    中国经贸部部长郑拓彬今晚在欢迎宴会上说，中马经贸合作关系一直稳定发展，去年贸易额达到8.7亿美元。
    拉菲达部长对几年来逐年增加的马中贸易额表示满意。她希望能加强马中企业间的联系和合作。
