Title: Lake...
Author: Zhou Peili
Date: 1989-07-09
Page: 8

The most beautiful scenery
Should be in the lake of your heart
And also in the lake of my heart
But—
Is it because of the sun's unpredictable appearance and disappearance?
Is it because of the moon's phases of waxing and waning?
Or is it due to the winds, rains, and the misty fog?
The surface of our hearts' lakes
Is either a mix of bright and gloomy
Or clear yet indistinct
Your waves cannot flow into my realm
My surges cannot surge into your domain
May the flow of time
Create a clear, sparkling stream
Flowing through your heart's lake
All the lakes converge together
Merging into a crystal-clear sea

### 湖……
周培礼
1989-07-09
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

    湖……
    周培礼
    最美的风景
    本应在你心的湖
    也应在我心的湖
    可是——
    是日之隐露无常
    是月之阴晴圆缺
    还是风风雨雨蒙蒙雾的缘故
    你我心的湖面
    不是朗丽阴晦交错
    便是明媚而又模糊
    你的波，流不进我的境界
    我的浪，涌不进你的领域
    愿时间之流
    流出一条清亮的小河
    流通你的心湖
    所有的湖汇流一起
    融成一汪晶莹透明的海
