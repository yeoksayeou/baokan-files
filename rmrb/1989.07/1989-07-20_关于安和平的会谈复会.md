Title: Resumption of Talks on Angola Peace
Author:
Date: 1989-07-20
Edition: 3

The committee responsible for supervising the implementation of the ceasefire agreement between the Angolan government and the anti-government armed group "National Union for the Total Independence of Angola" (UNITA), consisting of representatives from the Angolan government, UNITA, and Zaire, reconvened in Kinshasa on July 19 under the chairmanship of Zairean President Mobutu Sese Seko.
