Title: Global AIDS Patients Exceed 160,000
Author:
Date: 1989-07-07
Edition: 3

According to foreign news reports, by July 1, the total number of AIDS cases reported to the World Health Organization had reached 167,373. This indicates that more than 10,000 new cases emerged in June.

Experts from the World Health Organization estimate that the actual number of AIDS patients worldwide is twice the reported number. By 1993, the number of AIDS patients is expected to rise to 1 million.

### 世界艾滋病患者逾16万

1989-07-07
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    世界艾滋病患者逾16万
    新华社北京7月7日电  据外电报道，到7月1日为止，向世界卫生组织报告的艾滋病发病总人数为167373人，这表明在6月份新出现的病人超过了1万人。
    世界卫生组织专家估计，全世界艾滋病患者的实际人数是已报告人数的2倍。到1993年，艾滋病人将增加到100万。
