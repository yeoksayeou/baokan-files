Title: Jiang Zemin Congratulates Nersh on Election as Hungarian Party Chairman
Author:
Date: 1989-07-04
Edition: 1

Jiang Zemin sends a telegram to Nersh Rezső to congratulate him on his election as the chairman of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party. The telegram says: "On the occasion of your election as the chairman of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, I, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and in my personal capacity, extend my warm congratulations. I wish you success in your new and important work, and I wish the cause of Hungarian socialism continuous progress."

### 江泽民电贺涅尔什当选匈党主席

1989-07-04
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    江泽民电贺涅尔什当选匈党主席
    本报北京7月4日讯　中共中央总书记江泽民近日打电报给涅尔什·雷热同志，祝贺他当选为匈牙利社会主义工人党主席。贺电说：“在您当选为匈牙利社会主义工人党主席之际，我谨代表中国共产党中央委员会，并以我个人名义，表示热烈的祝贺。祝您在新的、责任重大的工作中取得成就，愿匈牙利社会主义事业不断向前发展。”
