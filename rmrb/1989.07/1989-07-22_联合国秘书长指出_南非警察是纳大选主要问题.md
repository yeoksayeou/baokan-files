Title: UN Secretary-General Says South African Police Are the Main Issue in Namibian Elections
Author: Lǐ Jǐngwèi
Date: 1989-07-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Harare, July 21st.

United Nations Secretary-General Javier Pérez de Cuéllar stated today in Windhoek that the presence of South African police in Namibia will be the main issue in Namibia's independence elections. He will strive to completely eliminate the shadow of the riot police.

De Cuéllar said that the existence of South African riot police is like a ghost threatening the people of northern Namibia.

De Cuéllar, after concluding his three-day visit to Namibia, expressed his dissatisfaction with the South African authorities' refusal to withdraw riot police from the northern border of Namibia.

### 联合国秘书长指出  南非警察是纳大选主要问题
李景卫
1989-07-22
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    联合国秘书长指出
    南非警察是纳大选主要问题
    本报哈拉雷7月21日电　记者李景卫报道：联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔今天在温得和克说，南非警察在纳米比亚的存在会成为纳米比亚独立选举的主要问题，他将力争彻底清除防暴警察的阴影。
    德奎利亚尔说，南非防暴警察的存在，就像幽灵一样威胁着纳米比亚北部人民。
    德奎利亚尔在结束对纳米比亚3天的访问时说，他对南非当局拒不从纳米比亚北部边境撤走防暴警察感到不满。
