Title: Hangzhou Builds Su Dongpo Memorial Hall
Author: Cao Yinkang
Date: 1989-07-16
Edition: 2

Hangzhou Builds Su Dongpo Memorial Hall

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, July 15th - (Reporter Cao Yinkang) The Su Dongpo Memorial Hall, located at the southern end of Su Di in Hangzhou, opens to the public today.

The Su Dongpo Memorial Hall covers an area of 1,300 square meters, with a building area of 400 square meters. It is reconstructed from an ancient-style small building. In front of the building, under two large trees, stands a stone statue of Su Dongpo. The entire memorial hall is elegant and classical, hidden among the green trees by the West Lake.

Su Dongpo once served as a local official in Hangzhou for two terms, totaling five years.

### 杭州建苏东坡纪念馆
曹银康
1989-07-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    杭州建苏东坡纪念馆
    新华社杭州7月15日电　（记者曹银康）位于杭州苏堤南端的苏东坡纪念馆，今日起对游人开放。
    苏东坡纪念馆占地1300平方米，建筑面积400平方米，由一幢仿古小楼改建而成。楼前小园中两棵大树下竖立着一尊苏东坡石雕像。整个纪念馆古朴典雅，掩映在西湖畔绿树丛中。
    苏东坡曾在杭州两任地方官，历时达5年之久。
