Title: Iranian Presidential Election Concludes, Rafsanjani Secures Majority
Author:
Date: 1989-07-29
Edition: 3

Iranian Presidential Election Concludes
Rafsanjani Secures Majority of Votes

Xinhua News Agency, Tehran, July 29th - In the recently concluded Iranian presidential election, the current Speaker of the Parliament, Rafsanjani, has secured the majority of votes, virtually guaranteeing his victory in the presidential race.

According to the Islamic Republic News Agency of Iran, the public voting for the Iranian presidential election ended at midnight on the 28th. Preliminary results of the vote count indicate that Rafsanjani received 91.4% of the votes in 84 polling stations.

Rafsanjani's sole opponent, Shebany, received a very small number of votes.

### 伊朗总统选举结束  拉夫桑贾尼已获多数票

1989-07-29
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    伊朗总统选举结束
    拉夫桑贾尼已获多数票
    新华社德黑兰7月29日电  伊朗现任议长拉夫桑贾尼在已经结束的总统选举中，获得绝大部分选票，已稳操当选总统的胜券。
    据伊朗伊斯兰共和国通讯社报道，伊朗总统选举的公民投票在28日午夜结束。选票初步统计的结果表明，拉夫桑贾尼在84个投票站获得了91.4％的选票。
    拉夫桑贾尼的唯一竞选对手谢巴尼只获得了数量很少的选票。
