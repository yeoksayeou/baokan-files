Title: United Nations Development Programme Approves Aid Plan for China
Author:
Date: 1989-07-02
Edition: 3

The United Nations Development Programme has approved a $57 million five-year population plan to help China achieve its population and development goals. This aid plan, proposed by the United Nations Population Fund for the 1990-1994 period, was approved at the 36th session of the United Nations Development Programme Board, which opened on June 5. The meeting concluded this morning.

### 联合国开发计划署批准援华计划

1989-07-02
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　联合国开发计划署批准援华计划
    新华社联合国7月1日电　联合国开发计划署已批准一项5700万美元的5年人口计划，以帮助中国实现人口和发展目标。这项由联合国人口基金会提出的1990至1994年度的援助计划，在于6月5日开幕的第36届联合国开发计划署理事会上获得批准。会议已于今晨闭幕。
