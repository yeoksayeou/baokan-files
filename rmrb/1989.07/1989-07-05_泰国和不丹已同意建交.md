Title: Thailand and Bhutan Agree to Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1989-07-05
Edition: 3

Thailand and Bhutan have agreed to establish diplomatic relations.

The agreement to establish diplomatic relations between the two countries was reached during a recent visit by Bhutan's Foreign Minister, Dawa Tshering, to Thailand.

### 泰国和不丹已同意建交

1989-07-05
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    泰国和不丹已同意建交
    泰国和不丹已经同意建立外交关系。
    两国的建交协议是不丹外交大臣达瓦·策林在最近访问泰国时达成的。
