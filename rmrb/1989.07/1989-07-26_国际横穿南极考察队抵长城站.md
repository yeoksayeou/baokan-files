Title: International Trans-Antarctic Expedition Arrives at Changcheng Station
Author:
Date: 1989-07-26
Edition: 2

The Chinese Antarctic Changcheng Station reported that the International Trans-Antarctic Expedition arrived at the station on July 24. The expedition will subsequently proceed to the Antarctic Peninsula and plans to begin crossing the Antarctic continent on July 29.

To ensure the safe landing of the International Trans-Antarctic Expedition, personnel from China's Changcheng Station, the Soviet Union's Bellingshausen Station, and Chile's Marscher Base have recently worked together to clear and widen the runway at Marscher Antarctic Airport. On that day, at 4 PM local time, a large aircraft carrying six members of the Trans-Antarctic Expedition, more than 20 journalists from various countries, and 41 sled dogs, safely landed at the airport.

As the forward base for the International Trans-Antarctic Expedition, the Chinese Changcheng Station was decorated festively. Flags of China, the United States, the Soviet Union, Britain, Japan, and France fluttered in the wind above the station. The Chinese wintering team at Changcheng Station stood in formation to welcome the arrival of the Trans-Antarctic Expedition members. Subsequently, the expedition members and the sled dogs will be transported in six flights aboard small aircraft to the Antarctic Peninsula.

### 国际横穿南极考察队抵长城站

1989-07-26
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    国际横穿南极考察队抵长城站
    据新华社北京7月26日电  中国南极长城站来电说，国际横穿南极大陆考察队7月24日到达中国南极长城站。日后将转往南极半岛，计划在7月29日开始横穿南极大陆。
    为了保证国际横穿南极大陆考察队安全降落，近日来中国长城站、苏联别林斯高晋站和智利马尔什基地都派出人员打扫和拓宽了马尔什南极机场的飞机跑道。这天的当地时间下午4点，一架大型飞机载着6名国际横穿大陆考察队员、20多名不同国家的新闻记者，41条极地狗，安全降落在机场。
    中国南极长城站做为国际横穿南极大陆考察队的前哨基地，布置得像过节一般。中、美、苏、英、日、法六国国旗在长城站上空迎风招展。我国在长城站的越冬队员列队迎接国际横穿南极大陆队员的光临。日后，横穿南极大陆队员和极地狗群等，将分6个航次，乘水牛型小飞机飞往南极半岛。
