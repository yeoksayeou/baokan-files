Title: Special Committee for Mediating the Chad-Libya Dispute Holds Meeting
Author:
Date: 1989-07-21
Edition: 3

The Organization of African Unity's special committee for mediating the Chad-Libya dispute held its first round of secret talks tonight at the Hotel de l'Amitié in Bamako. The meeting was convened at the initiative of the OAU's executive chairman, Malian President Moussa Traoré.

The committee's chairman, Gabonese President Omar Bongo, Chadian President Hissène Habré, Libyan leader Muammar Gaddafi, and Algerian President Chadli Bendjedid attended the meeting. Nigerian President Ibrahim Babangida will participate in the closing session of the meeting.

### 调解乍利争端特委会举行会议

1989-07-21
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    调解乍利争端特委会举行会议
    据新华社巴马科7月20日电　非洲统一组织调解乍得—利比亚争端特别委员会今晚在巴马科的友谊饭店举行了首轮秘密会议。这次会议是在非统执行主席、马里总统穆萨·特拉奥雷的倡议下召开的。
    特别委员会主席、加蓬总统邦戈，乍得总统哈布雷，利比亚领导人卡扎菲以及阿尔及利亚总统沙德利参加了这次会议。尼日利亚总统巴班吉达将参加这次会议的闭幕会议。
