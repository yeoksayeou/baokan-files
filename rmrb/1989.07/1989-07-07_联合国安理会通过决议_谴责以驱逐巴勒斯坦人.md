Title: UN Security Council Passes Resolution Condemning Israel's Expulsion of Palestinians
Author: Liú Kāichéng
Date: 1989-07-07
Edition: 3

The United Nations Security Council today convened an emergency meeting to discuss the situation in the occupied Palestinian territories and passed a resolution expressing "deep regret" over Israel's expulsion of eight Palestinian civilians at the end of June.

The resolution, drafted by Algeria and six other countries, was worded mildly while adhering to principles. During the vote, representatives from China and 14 other countries voted in favor, while the U.S. representative abstained.

The emergency meeting of the Security Council was held at the request of the Arab states group. The meeting, originally scheduled for the beginning of this week, was postponed three times due to U.S. obstruction.

The resolution demands that Israel ensure the immediate and safe return of the expelled Palestinians and refrain from expelling any other Palestinian civilians.

### 联合国安理会通过决议  谴责以驱逐巴勒斯坦人
刘开宸
1989-07-07
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    联合国安理会通过决议
    谴责以驱逐巴勒斯坦人
    本报联合国7月6日电  记者刘开宸报道：联合国安理会今天召开紧急会议，讨论被占领的巴勒斯坦领土局势，并通过决议，对以色列于6月底又驱逐8名巴勒斯坦平民出境，“深表遗憾”。
    决议由阿尔及利亚等7国起草，在坚持原则的前提下，措词温和。表决时，中国等14国代表赞成，美国代表弃权。
    安理会紧急会议是应阿拉伯国家集团的要求召开的。会议原定于本周初召开，由于美国的阻挠，曾三次推迟。
    决议要求以色列保证已被驱逐出境的巴勒斯坦人立即安全返回，并不得驱逐其他巴勒斯坦平民。
