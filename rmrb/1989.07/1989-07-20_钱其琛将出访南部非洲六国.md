Title: Qian Qichen to Visit Six Southern African Countries
Author:
Date: 1989-07-20
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 20th.

Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen will visit Botswana, Zimbabwe, Angola, Zambia, Mozambique, and Lesotho from July 26 to August 12 for friendly visits.

The foreign ministry spokesperson announced this news at a press conference today, stating that Qian Qichen will be visiting at the invitation of the foreign ministers of the aforementioned six Southern African countries.

The spokesperson also announced that Assistant Foreign Minister Yang Fuchang will visit Senegal, Ivory Coast, Gambia, Sierra Leone, and Ghana from July 22 to August 8 at the invitation of these five West African countries.

### 钱其琛将出访南部非洲六国

1989-07-20
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    钱其琛将出访南部非洲六国
    新华社北京7月20日电　中国外交部长钱其琛将于7月26日至8月12日对博茨瓦纳、津巴布韦、安哥拉、赞比亚、莫桑比克和莱索托进行友好访问。
    外交部发言人今天在新闻发布会上宣布这一消息时说，钱其琛是应上述南部非洲6国的外交部长的邀请而去访问的。
  这位发言人还宣布，外交部部长助理杨福昌将于7月22日至8月8日应邀访问塞内加尔、科特迪瓦、冈比亚、塞拉利昂、加纳等西非5国。
