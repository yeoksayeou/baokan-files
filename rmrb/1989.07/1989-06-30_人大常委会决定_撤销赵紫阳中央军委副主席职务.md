Title: The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Decides to Remove Zhao Ziyang from the Position of Vice Chairman of the Central Military Commission
Author:
Date: 1989-06-30
Edition: 1

The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress
Decides to Remove Zhao Ziyang from the Position of Vice Chairman
of the Central Military Commission

New China News Agency, June 30, 1989

Decision of the Standing Committee of the Seventh National People's Congress on the Removal of Zhao Ziyang from the Position of Vice Chairman of the Central Military Commission of the People's Republic of China

(Adopted on June 30, 1989)

The Eighth Session of the Standing Committee of the Seventh National People's Congress, upon the request of Deng Xiaoping, Chairman of the Central Military Commission of the People's Republic of China, decided to remove Zhao Ziyang from the position of Vice Chairman of the Central Military Commission of the People's Republic of China.

### 人大常委会决定  撤销赵紫阳中央军委副主席职务

1989-06-30
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    人大常委会决定
    撤销赵紫阳中央军委副主席职务
    新华社北京六月三十日电　全国人民代表大会常务委员会关于撤销赵紫阳的中华人民共和国中央军事委员会副主席职务的决定
    （一九八九年六月三十日通过）
    第七届全国人民代表大会常务委员会第八次会议根据中华人民共和国中央军事委员会主席邓小平的提请，决定撤销赵紫阳的中华人民共和国中央军事委员会副主席职务。
