Title: The 35th World Archery Championships Conclude
Author: Shi Guangyao
Date: 1989-07-09
Edition: 4

The 35th World Archery Championships concluded this afternoon in Lausanne. The Soviet men's team and the South Korean women's team won the men's and women's team titles respectively, while the Chinese women's team finished in sixth place.

### 第三十五届世界射箭锦标赛结束
施光耀
1989-07-09
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    第三十五届世界射箭锦标赛结束
    新华社洛桑7月8日电（记者施光耀）第三十五届世界射箭锦标赛今天下午在洛桑降下帷幕。苏联男队和南朝鲜女队分别赢得男、女团体冠军，中国女队名列第六。
