Title: Weipingbin, Head of the Military Affairs Department of Shandong Province's Xintai City, Leads Workers to Establish Enterprises, Achieving Remarkable Results
Author: Lǐ Jiànpíng
Date: 1989-07-16
Page: 5

Weipingbin, the head of the Military Affairs Department of Shandong Province's Xintai City, led workers to establish enterprises, achieving remarkable results in supporting the military through labor. He was awarded the title of Advanced Worker of the entire province. The picture shows a Young Pioneer tying a red scarf around his neck.
