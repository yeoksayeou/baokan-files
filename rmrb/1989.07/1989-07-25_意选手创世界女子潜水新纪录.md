Title: Italian Athlete Sets New World Record for Women's Freediving
Author: Luojinbiao
Date: 1989-07-25
Edition: 4

Italian freediver Patricia Majolica set a new world record for women's freediving today morning in the Bay of Biancavilla, Syracuse, Sicily. She reached a depth of 47 meters using only fins and holding her breath. The dive took a total of 2 minutes and 10 seconds, with 1 minute spent descending and 1 minute and 10 seconds spent ascending. This record broke the previous one set by her sister, Rossana Majolica, in 1981, which was 45 meters.

The Majolica family is known as a "freediving dynasty." Their father, Enzo Majolica, set a world record for men's freediving at 101 meters last July.

### 意选手创世界女子潜水新纪录
罗晋标
1989-07-25
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    意选手创世界女子潜水新纪录
    本报罗马7月25日电　记者罗晋标报道：今天上午，意大利潜水女将帕特里奇亚·马约尔卡，在西西里岛锡拉库萨市白泉海湾上，创造了47米的世界女子潜水新纪录（只带脚蹼的憋气潜水），她共用了2分10秒，其中下潜用了1分，上浮用了1分10秒。这个纪录打破了由她的妹妹罗莎娜·马约尔卡在1981年创造的45米纪录。
    马约尔卡是个“潜水世家”。父亲恩佐·马约尔卡在去年7月创造了101米的世界男子潜水纪录。
