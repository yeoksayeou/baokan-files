Title: Soviet and Hungarian Leaders Discuss Reform Issues
Author:
Date: 1989-07-26
Edition: 3

Soviet leader Gorbachev and Hungarian leaders Németh and Grósz discussed issues related to socialist reform in the current stage and cooperation between the two countries in Moscow on the 25th.

### 苏匈领导讨论改革问题

1989-07-26
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏匈领导讨论改革问题
    苏联领导人戈尔巴乔夫和匈牙利领导人涅尔什、格罗斯25日在莫斯科讨论了现阶段社会主义改革问题和两国合作问题。
