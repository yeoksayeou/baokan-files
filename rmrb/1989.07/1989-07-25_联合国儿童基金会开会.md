Title: UNICEF Holds Meeting
Author:
Date: 1989-07-25
Edition: 3

The United Nations Children's Fund (UNICEF) held its 19th Representative Assembly in Paris on July 24, calling for strengthened efforts in the prevention and treatment of childhood diseases. Over 4,000 experts from 80 countries attended the meeting.

### 联合国儿童基金会开会

1989-07-25
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    联合国儿童基金会开会
    联合国儿童基金会24日在巴黎举行第19届代表大会，呼吁加强儿童疾病的防治和护理工作。来自80个国家的4000多名专家与会。
