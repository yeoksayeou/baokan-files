Title: The Central Committee of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League Decides to Implement the Spirit of the Fourth Plenum
Author:
Date: 1989-07-09
Edition: 2

The fourth plenary session of the ninth central committee of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League was held in Beijing from July 8 to 9. The main topic of the meeting was to study the documents of the 13th Central Committee of the Communist Party of China's Fourth Plenum and the important speeches of Comrade Deng Xiaoping, and to discuss how to combine the actual situation of the League to seriously implement and execute them.

The meeting was presided over by Cai Zemin, Chairman of the Central Committee of the League. The meeting believed that the Fourth Plenum of the Communist Party of China was held at an opportune time and was very successful, in line with the hearts of the Party and the people, and was an important meeting of epoch-making significance. The League, as a close friend of the Communist Party of China, firmly supports the important decisions and important speeches of the Fourth Plenum and Comrade Deng Xiaoping, and will combine its own actual situation to firmly implement and execute them.

The meeting decided: First, to continue to seriously study the spirit of the Fourth Plenum and the important speeches of Comrade Deng Xiaoping, to carry out patriotic education among the members of the League, and through a deep understanding of modern Chinese history and national conditions, to recognize the importance of the leadership of the Communist Party, the inevitability of socialism, the necessity of reform and opening up, and the correctness of "one country, two systems, peaceful reunification," to improve the political quality of the members. Second, to adhere to the system of multi-party cooperation and political consultation under the leadership of the Communist Party, to actively participate in politics and policy-making, and to especially make efforts to assist the government in grasping the "four major tasks" proposed by the Fourth Plenum at present. Third, to make use of the advantage that the members of the League have extensive contacts with compatriots inside and outside Taiwan, to do a good job in overseas liaison work, to help compatriots from Taiwan correctly understand the situation on the mainland, to eliminate various misunderstandings and doubts, to continue to come to the mainland for family visits, tourism, investment, and business, to develop exchanges across the Taiwan Strait, and to make new contributions to the prosperity and strength of the motherland and peaceful reunification.

### 台盟中常会作出贯彻四中全会精神决定  搞好海外联谊　消除台胞疑虑

1989-07-09
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    台盟中常会作出贯彻四中全会精神决定
    搞好海外联谊　消除台胞疑虑
    本报北京7月9日讯　台湾民主自治同盟7月8日至9日在北京举行了第四届第九次中央常务委员扩大会议。会议中心议题，是学习中共十三届四中全会文件和邓小平同志重要讲话，研究如何结合台盟实际认真贯彻执行。
    会议由台盟中央主席团主席蔡子民主持。会议认为，中共四中全会开得很及时，非常成功，符合党心、民心，是继往开来的一次具有划时代意义的重要会议。台盟作为中国共产党的诤友，坚决拥护四中全会的各项重要决定和邓小平同志的重要讲话，并结合自身实际，坚决贯彻执行。
    会议决定：第一，继续认真学习四中全会精神和邓小平同志重要讲话，在盟员中进行爱国主义教育，通过对中国近代史和国情的深刻了解，认识共产党领导的重要性、社会主义的必然性、改革开放的必要性、“一国两制、和平统一”的正确性，提高盟员的政治素质。第二，坚持共产党领导的多党合作和政治协商制度，积极参政议政，在当前特别要尽力协助政府抓好四中全会提出的“四件大事”。第三，利用盟员与台湾岛内外同胞有广泛联系的优势，做好海外联谊工作，帮助台胞正确了解大陆形势，消除各种误解和疑虑，继续来大陆探亲旅游、投资经商，发展两岸交流，为祖国的繁荣富强与和平统一作出新贡献。
