Title: Meeting Between Soviet and Mongolian Leaders
Author:
Date: 1989-07-27
Page: 3

Soviet leader Gorbachev met with Mongolian leader Batmonkh on July 26. The two sides exchanged information on the reform situations in their respective countries and discussed international developments.

### 苏联蒙古领导人会晤

1989-07-27
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏联蒙古领导人会晤
    苏联领导人戈尔巴乔夫26日会晤了正在苏联访问的蒙古领导人巴特蒙赫，双方相互通报了本国的改革情况并就国际形势交换了意见。
