Title: China's Salt Resources
Author: Sūn Lái Xìn
Date: 1989-07-22
Page: 8

Sea Salt
From the mouth of the Yalu River in the north to the Beilun River in the south, almost everywhere in China can produce sea salt. The main sea salt producing regions in Liaoning, Hebei, Tianjin, Shandong, Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Fujian, and Hainan account for 84% of the country's total production. Currently, China's sea salt production capacity has reached over 17 million tons.

Lake Salt
China has discovered over 800 lake salt deposits. The proven lake salt reserves amount to 100 billion tons, mainly distributed in the arid regions of Xinjiang, Tibet, Qinghai, Inner Mongolia, Shaanxi, and Shanxi in the north and west. The Qaidam Salt Lake in Qinghai is the largest salt lake in China.

Rock Salt
Rock salt is often buried underground at depths of 300 to 2000 meters, with only the Aksu salt mines in Xinjiang and a few other places exposed on the surface. Provinces like Yunnan, Sichuan, Hubei, and Hunan have adopted water flushing methods to mine rock salt. The rock salt resources in Jiangsu, Shandong, and Anhui remain undeveloped and unused.

Underground Brine
Natural brine buried underground at depths ranging from tens to thousands of meters contains not only sodium chloride but also potassium, iodine, boron, and lithium. The main distribution areas are the Laizhou Bay in Shandong, western Hubei, and central and western Sichuan.

Brine Lakes
Brine lakes, which are primarily composed of brine, are mainly distributed in Tibet, Xinjiang, Inner Mongolia, and northern Shaanxi.

### 我国的盐资源
孙来信
1989-07-22
第8版(每周文摘)
专栏：

    我国的盐资源
    海盐　我国北起鸭绿江口，南到北仑河口，几乎到处都可晒盐。辽、冀、津、鲁、苏、浙、闽、琼等主要海盐产区产量占全国84％。目前，我国海盐生产能力已达1700多万吨。
    湖盐　我国已发现湖盐矿800多处。现已探明的湖盐储量达1000亿吨，主要分布在新、藏、青、内蒙古、陕、晋等北部和西部气候干旱地带。青海察尔汗盐湖是我国最大的盐湖。
    岩盐　岩盐多埋藏在地下300—2000米处，只有新疆阿克苏等地盐矿出露地表。云、川、鄂、湘等省已采用水浇法开采岩盐矿。苏、鲁、皖等省岩盐资源尚未开发利用。
    地下卤水　埋藏地下数10至千余米的天然卤水除氯化钠外还有钾、碘、硼、锂等。主要分布在山东莱州湾、湖北西部和四川的中部、西部地区。
    卤水湖　卤水湖是以卤水为主的盐湖，主要分布在藏、新、内蒙古、陕北等地区。
                    （1989年第4期《地球》孙来信文）
