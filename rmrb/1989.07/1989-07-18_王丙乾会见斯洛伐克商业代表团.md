Title: Wang Bingqian Meets with Slovakian Business Delegation
Author:
Date: 1989-07-18
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 18th.

State Councilor and Minister of Finance Wang Bingqian met with a Slovakian business delegation led by Minister of Commerce and Tourism Nagy on July 17th. The delegation arrived in Beijing on the 15th to participate in the Beijing International Trade Fair and conduct economic and trade activities. The guests will also visit Shanghai before returning home on the 22nd.

### 王丙乾会见斯洛伐克商业代表团

1989-07-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    王丙乾会见斯洛伐克商业代表团
    新华社北京7月18日电　国务委员兼财政部长王丙乾17日在这里会见了由商业和旅游部长纳吉率领的斯洛伐克商业代表团。代表团15日抵京，前来参加北京国际博览会并进行经贸活动。客人们还将前往上海访问，然后于22日回国。
