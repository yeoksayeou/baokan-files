Title: Wimbledon Tennis Tournament Enters Final Stage, Veteran McEnroe Loses Chance to Reach Finals
Author: Zhang Mingde
Date: 1989-07-08
Edition: 4

The Wimbledon Tennis Tournament has entered its final stage. Veteran player John McEnroe missed his chance to reach the finals today.

According to a report from Xinhua News Agency in London on July 7th, the defending champion Stefan Edberg defeated American star John McEnroe in the men's semifinals at Wimbledon, shattering McEnroe's dream of winning his fourth Wimbledon title.

The second-seeded Swedish player, Edberg, had a tough battle before finally defeating the fifth-seeded McEnroe. The set scores were 7-5, 7-6, and 7-6. Edberg will now face the winner of the match between Lendl and Becker for the championship.

The women's singles title at this year's Wimbledon will be contested between West Germany's Steffi Graf and American veteran Martina Navratilova.

### 温布尔登网球赛进入决战  老将麦肯罗痛失决赛权
张明德
1989-07-08
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    温布尔登网球赛进入决战
    老将麦肯罗痛失决赛权
    据新华社伦敦7月7日电（记者张明德）上届冠军埃德贝里今天在温布尔登网球赛男子半决赛中战胜美国名将麦肯罗，击碎了他第4次获得温布尔登冠军的理想。
    2号种子、瑞典的埃德贝里今天经过激烈的苦战才征服了5号种子麦肯罗。3局的比分是7∶5、7∶6和7∶6。埃德贝里将与伦德尔与贝克尔之间的胜者争夺冠军。
    本届温布尔登网球赛的女子单打冠军将由联邦德国的格拉芙和美国老将纳夫拉蒂诺娃争夺。
