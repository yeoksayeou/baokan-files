Title: A Large U.S. Airliner Crashes, At Least 115 Dead
Author: Cang Lide
Date: 1989-07-20
Page: 3

A large U.S. airliner crashes, at least 115 dead

	BEIJING, July 19 (Xinhua) -- A DC-10 jumbo jet carrying 293 passengers and crew crashed today in Iowa. Initial reports indicate that at least 115 people were killed.

	The United Airlines Flight 232 was en route from Denver to Philadelphia when the crash occurred. According to reports, the second engine at the rear of the plane exploded shortly after takeoff, causing the hydraulic system to fail and the plane to lose control. The pilot attempted an emergency landing at the Sioux City airport in Iowa. The plane crashed about one kilometer from the runway, flipped over, and caught fire, breaking into several pieces.

	The U.S. Federal Aviation Administration has dispatched investigators to the scene.

	So far, 115 people are confirmed dead, and 178 have survived.

### 美一大型客机坠毁  至少有一百一十五人遇难
仓立德
1989-07-20
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美一大型客机坠毁
    至少有一百一十五人遇难
    本报华盛顿7月19日电  记者仓立德报道：一架载有293名乘客和机组人员的DC—10大型客机今天下午在艾奥瓦州失事。据刚刚收到的消息称，至少有115人遇难。
    这架美国联合航空公司232号航班客机是从丹佛城飞往费城途中失事的。据悉飞机起飞后，位于尾部的第二号发动机爆炸，致使液压系统失灵，整个飞机失去控制，飞行员试图在艾奥瓦州苏城机场紧急降落。飞机在离跑道约1公里处翻身起火，机身碎成几段。
    事故发生后，美国航空管理当局已派员前往现场调查。
    据悉，到目前为止，有115人遇难，另有178人生还。
