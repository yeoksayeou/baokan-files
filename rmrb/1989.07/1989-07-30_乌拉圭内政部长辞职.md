Title: Uruguay's Interior Minister Resigns
Author:
Date: 1989-07-30
Page: 3

Uruguay's Interior Minister Antonio Macchiarulo submitted his resignation to the parliament on the evening of the 27th, citing his involvement in the sudden death of a detained youth.

### 乌拉圭内政部长辞职

1989-07-30
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    乌拉圭内政部长辞职
    因涉嫌对一名被捕青年的突然死亡负有负责，乌拉圭内政部长安东尼奥·马切萨诺27日晚向议会提出辞呈。
