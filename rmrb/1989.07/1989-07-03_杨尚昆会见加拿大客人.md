Title: Yang Shangkun Meets Canadian Visitor
Author:
Date: 1989-07-03
Edition: 1

Yang Shangkun Meets Canadian Visitor

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 3rd — State Chairman Yang Shangkun met with Professor Guo Huangui of the Political Science Department at Simon Fraser University in Canada this morning in the Great Hall of the People.

Yang Shangkun warmly welcomed Professor Guo Huangui’s visit.

Yang Shangkun briefed the guest on the situation in Beijing regarding the suppression of the turmoil and the counter-revolutionary rebellion.

Yang Shangkun said, “The situation in the country has now stabilized, which is the unanimous wish of the broad masses of the people. This victory came at a great cost, but the measures we have taken have always been very cautious, restrained, and tolerant, which is evident to all.”

Yang Shangkun inquired about the situation of students studying abroad. He said, “I hope that after Professor Guo Huangui returns, he will share what he has personally seen and heard with the Chinese students and other friends there. I believe that Chinese students and foreign friends will gradually understand. We will continue to move forward. Our country, with a population of 1.1 billion, has great hope.”

### 杨尚昆会见加拿大客人

1989-07-03
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    杨尚昆会见加拿大客人
    新华社北京7月3日电　国家主席杨尚昆今天上午在人民大会堂会见加拿大西蒙·弗雷泽大学政治系教授郭焕圭。
    杨尚昆对郭焕圭教授来访表示热情欢迎。
    杨尚昆向客人介绍了北京制止动乱和平息反革命暴乱的情况。
    杨尚昆说，现在国内的局势已经稳定，这是广大人民群众一致期望的。取得这场斗争的胜利，付出了很大的代价，但我们所采取的办法一直非常谨慎、克制、容忍，这是有目共睹的。
    杨尚昆关心地询问了在国外的留学生情况。他说，希望郭焕圭教授回去后，把亲自看到、听到的实际情况，向在那里的中国留学生和其他朋友谈谈。相信中国留学生和外国朋友会逐渐了解的。他说，我们将继续前进。我们这个拥有11亿人口的国家是大有希望的。
