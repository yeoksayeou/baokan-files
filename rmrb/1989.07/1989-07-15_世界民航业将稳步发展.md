Title: The Global Aviation Industry Will Develop Steadily
Author:
Date: 1989-07-15
Page: 3

International Labour Office, Geneva, July 14th.

A research report released by the International Labour Office on July 14 indicates that the global aviation industry will develop steadily over the next 10 years.

The report predicts that from now until the year 2000, the number of jet aircraft in the world will increase by approximately 57%, and the number of passengers will increase by about 5.3% annually.

According to the data provided in the report, from 1970 to 1986, the passenger volume of airlines worldwide increased by an average of 7.1% per year. It is estimated that in the next 10 years, the passenger volume in the Asia-Pacific region will increase by 7% annually, while the passenger volume in the Europe-America region will increase by less than 5% annually.

### 世界民航业将稳步发展

1989-07-15
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    世界民航业将稳步发展
    新华社日内瓦7月14日电  国际劳工局14日发表的一份研究报告表明，在今后10年中，世界民航事业将稳步发展。
  报告预计，从现在起到2000年，全世界民航喷气式飞机将增加约57％；旅客人数将每年增加约5.3％。
    据报告提供的数字，在1970年至1986年，世界各航空公司的客运量平均每年增加7.1％。预计在今后10年中亚太地区的客运量将每年增加7％，欧美地区的客运量将每年增加不到5％。
