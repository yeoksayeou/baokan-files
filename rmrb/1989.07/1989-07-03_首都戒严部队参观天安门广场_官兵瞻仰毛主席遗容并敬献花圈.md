Title: Capital Martial Law Troops Visit Tiananmen Square
Author:
Date: 1989-07-03
Edition: 2

Capital martial law troops from a certain unit visited the Chairman Mao Memorial Hall this morning, where they paid their respects to Chairman Mao's remains and presented a wreath. The inscription on the wreath read: "The great leader Chairman Mao Zedong is immortal."

Many of the soldiers from the martial law troops were visiting Beijing for the first time. They were excited to hear that they would be going to Tiananmen Square for a tour. Early this morning, filled with the joy of victory, they marched in orderly ranks to Tiananmen Square with reverence in their hearts. After paying their respects to Chairman Mao Zedong's remains, they also visited Chairman Mao Zedong's former residence, the Great Hall of the People, Tiananmen Square, and the Forbidden City. This activity was organized and arranged by the General Political Department. It is reported that the martial law troops will continue to visit and tour in batches.

### 首都戒严部队参观天安门广场  官兵瞻仰毛主席遗容并敬献花圈

1989-07-03
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    首都戒严部队参观天安门广场
    官兵瞻仰毛主席遗容并敬献花圈
    新华社北京7月3日电  首都戒严部队某部官兵今天上午来到毛主席纪念堂，瞻仰了毛主席遗容，并敬献了花圈。花圈的挽联上写着：“伟大领袖毛泽东主席永垂不朽”。
    戒严部队某部许多官兵是第一次来到北京，他们听说去天安门广场参观都非常兴奋。今天一早，他们怀着胜利的喜悦，排着整齐的队列，以仰慕的心情，来到天安门广场。他们在瞻仰了毛泽东主席遗容后，还参观了毛泽东主席故居、人民大会堂、天安门广场和故宫。这一活动是总政治部组织安排的。据悉，戒严部队还将分期分批瞻仰和参观。
