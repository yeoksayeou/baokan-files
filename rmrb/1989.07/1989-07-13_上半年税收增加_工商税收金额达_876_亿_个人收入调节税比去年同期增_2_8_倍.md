Title: Tax Revenue Increases in the First Half of the Year
Author: Wang Jinhu
Date: 1989-07-13
Edition: 1

    Tax Revenue Increases in the First Half of the Year

    Industrial and Commercial Tax Revenue Reaches 8.76 Billion Yuan
    Personal Income Adjustment Tax Increases 2.8 Times Compared to the Same Period Last Year

    BEIJING, July 13 (Xinhua) — Reporter Wang Jinhu reports: This year’s first half tax revenue task has been completed satisfactorily. From January to June, the cumulative industrial and commercial tax revenue nationwide reached 8.76 billion yuan, completing 49.7% of the annual plan, an increase of 16.1% compared to the same period last year, and higher than the growth rate of industrial output value in the first half of the year. There has been a significant increase in personal income adjustment tax, individual, industrial and commercial household, and private enterprise tax revenue.

    According to statistics, the cumulative personal income adjustment tax collected in the first half of the year reached 182.42 million yuan, completing 60.8% of the annual plan, an increase of 2.8 times compared to the same period last year. Among them, Guangdong Province collected 22.83 million yuan in personal income adjustment tax, Liaoning Province 21.08 million yuan, and Beijing, Shanghai, Hebei, Jiangsu, and Fujian collected over 100 million yuan each.

### 上半年税收增加  工商税收金额达876亿  个人收入调节税比去年同期增2.8倍
王锦鹄
1989-07-13
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    上半年税收增加
    工商税收金额达876亿
    个人收入调节税比去年同期增2.8倍
    本报北京7月13日讯　记者王锦鹄报道：今年上半年，我国税收任务完成较好。1至6月份，全国工商税收累计达876亿元，完成年计划49.7％，比去年同期增长16.1％，高于上半年工业产值增长幅度。个人收入调节税、个体、工商户和私人企业税收有明显增长。
    据统计，上半年个人收入调节税征收入库累计达18242万元，完成年计划60.8％，比去年同期增长2.8倍。其中，广东省征收个人收入调节税2283万元，辽宁省2108万元，超过1000万元的有北京、上海、河北、江苏、福建5省市。
