Title: Polish Research Ship Seeks Manganese Deposits in the Pacific
Author:
Date: 1989-07-07
Page: 7

A scientific research vessel named the "Copernicus" recently departed from the Polish port city of Gdynia, heading towards the Pacific Ocean to search for manganese deposits. On board are scientists from Mexico, East Germany, and Poland. They will also conduct geological and geophysical surveys of the Pacific Ocean.

### 波考察船大海找锰矿

1989-07-07
第7版(国际专页)
专栏：

    波考察船大海找锰矿
    一艘命名为“哥白尼号”的科学考察船最近离开波兰海港城市格丁尼亚，前往太平洋寻找锰矿床。乘船前往的包括墨西哥、民主德国和波兰科学家。他们还将对太平洋进行地球物理和地质考察。
