Title: U.S. and Soviet Naval Ships Conduct Mutual Visits
Author:
Date: 1989-07-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, July 19th

	The Pentagon spokesman, Pete Williams, announced on the 18th that three U.S. naval ships will visit the Soviet port of Sevastopol on the Black Sea from August 4th to 8th.

	Currently, three Soviet naval ships are en route to the United States and will arrive at Norfolk, Virginia, on the Atlantic coast on the 21st of this month for a five-day visit.

### 美苏军舰进行互访

1989-07-19
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美苏军舰进行互访
    美国国防部发言人皮特·威廉斯18日宣布，三艘美国军舰将于8月4日至8日访问苏联黑海沿岸的塞瓦斯托波尔港。
    目前，三艘苏联军舰正在前往美国访问的途中，它们将于本月21日到达美国大西洋沿岸弗吉尼亚州的诺福克港进行为期5天的访问。
