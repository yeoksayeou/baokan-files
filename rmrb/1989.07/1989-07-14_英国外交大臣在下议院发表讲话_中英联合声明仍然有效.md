Title: British Foreign Secretary Speaks in the House of Commons: The Sino-British Joint Declaration Remains Valid
Author:
Date: 1989-07-14
Edition: 1

British Foreign Secretary Geoffrey Howe said on July 13 in the British House of Commons that recent events in China have not invalidated the Sino-British Joint Declaration on the Hong Kong issue.

Howe said, “The Joint Declaration has been and remains a good agreement, and Hong Kong residents still hold this view.”

China and Britain signed this Joint Declaration in 1984. The declaration stipulates that China will resume exercising sovereignty over Hong Kong in 1997.

Howe said, “The Joint Declaration is a model at any time. The events in China have neither invalidated it nor changed the conditions on which it is based.”

Howe also emphasized the need to restore confidence in Hong Kong, saying, “What is lacking today and must be restored is confidence – confidence that China will honor the agreement after 1997.”

Howe added that such confidence, of course, cannot be restored overnight and will require time and effort.

Howe recently visited Hong Kong and welcomed the recent statement by the Chinese government on the Hong Kong issue. He said, “China reiterated its obligations under the Joint Declaration, and we welcome this.”

Howe, in rejecting calls from certain Western countries for sanctions against China, said, “The British government does not accept the argument that a package of sanctions should be taken against China.”

### 英国外交大臣在下议院发表讲话  中英联合声明仍然有效

1989-07-14
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    英国外交大臣在下议院发表讲话
    中英联合声明仍然有效
    新华社伦敦7月13日电　英国外交大臣杰弗里·豪13日在英国下议院发表讲话时说，最近在中国发生的事情并未使中英关于香港问题的联合声明失去效力。
    豪说，“联合声明过去和现在都是一个很好的协议，香港居民仍持这种看法。”
    中国和英国于1984年签订了这项联合声明。声明中规定中国将于1997年恢复对香港行使主权。
    豪说，“联合声明无论在什么时期都是一个范本。中国发生的事情既未使其无效，也未改变它所依据的条件。”
    豪同时还强调需要在香港恢复信心，他说，“今天所缺乏的和必须得到恢复的就是信心——对中国将在1997年以后遵守协议的信心。”
    豪补充说，这种信心当然不可能在一夜之间就得到恢复，这还需要时间和进行努力。
    豪最近访问了香港，他对中国政府最近就香港问题发表的声明表示欢迎。他说，“中国重申了它对联合声明所承担的义务，我们对此表示欢迎。”
    豪在对某些西方国家呼吁对中国进行制裁表示拒绝时说，“英国政府不接受那种应对中国采取一揽子制裁措施的论点。”
