Title: Foreign Affairs Briefing
Author:
Date: 1989-07-09
Edition: 2

The China Association for International Friendship and the China-Mongolia Friendship Association held a banquet on July 8 at the Beijing Hotel to celebrate the 68th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution. Mongolian diplomatic representatives were invited to attend.

The Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Ni Zhifu, met on July 8 with a delegation from the Soviet Union's industrial trade unions led by Kosyukov, a member of the Presidium of the Central Council of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions and Chairman of the Central Committee of the Trade Union of Metallurgical Workers of the USSR.

The third meeting of the Standing Working Group on Energy Cooperation under the Sino-Soviet Economic, Trade, and Scientific and Technological Cooperation Commission was held in Beijing from June 24 to July 8. The meeting discussed the implementation of the decisions of the Sino-Soviet Economic, Trade, and Scientific and Technological Cooperation Commission on energy cooperation by both sides of the Standing Working Group, reviewed the work done since the second meeting last year, and explored new areas for expanding cooperation in the energy sector.

On July 6, the two sides signed a meeting summary in Beijing. Huang Yicheng, Minister of the Energy Department, attended the signing ceremony and met with the entire Soviet delegation. The Soviet delegation departed Beijing on July 8 to return home.

China and the German Democratic Republic signed the minutes of the fifth session of the Economic, Trade, and Scientific and Technological Cooperation Commission in Beijing on July 7. The Chinese side's chairman, Qin Zhida, and the East German Minister of Chemistry, Günter Wischnewski, signed the minutes as the German side's chairman.

### 外事简讯

1989-07-09
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲对外友协和中蒙友协8日在北京饭店举行宴会，庆祝蒙古人民革命68周年。蒙古驻华使节应邀出席。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长、中华全国总工会主席倪志福8日会见了以全苏工会中央理事会主席团委员、苏联冶金工业工人工会中央委员会主席科斯丘科夫为团长的苏联产业工会代表团。
    ▲中苏经济、贸易和科技合作委员会动力合作常设工作组第三次会议6月24日至7月8日在北京举行。会议讨论了常设工作组中苏双方执行中苏经济、贸易和科技合作委员会关于动力合作方面的决议的情况，回顾了去年第二次会议以来的工作，并商讨了双方扩大动力方面合作的新的领域。
    7月6日，中苏双方在北京签署了一项会议纪要。能源部部长黄毅诚参加了签字仪式并会见了苏方代表团全体成员。苏方代表团8日离京回国。
    ▲中国和德意志民主共和国7月7日在北京签署了经济、贸易、科技合作委员会第5届会议会谈纪要。委员会中方主席秦仲达和民德化工部长、德方主席京特·维朔夫斯基在纪要上签了字。
          （据新华社）
