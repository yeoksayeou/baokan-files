Title: The Ministerial Council of the OAU Opens
Author:
Date: 1989-07-18
Edition: 3

Foreign ministers of member states of the Organization of African Unity gathered in Addis Ababa on July 17 to hold the organization's biannual ministerial council meeting. They discussed major issues facing Africa.

### 非统部长理事会议开幕

1989-07-18
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    非统部长理事会议开幕
    非洲统一组织各国外长17日云集亚的斯亚贝巴，召开该组织一年二次的部长理事会会议，讨论非洲面临的重大问题。
