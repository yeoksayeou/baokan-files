Title: U.S. Trade Deficit Surges
Author:
Date: 1989-07-19
Edition: 3

The U.S. trade deficit surged sharply in May, reaching $10.2 billion, the highest monthly deficit for the first five months of 1989. Based on the trade deficit for the first five months of this year, it is estimated that the annual trade deficit for 1989 will exceed $111 billion.

The Commerce Secretary stated that the trade deficit in May was disappointing. During this month, imports increased by 4.3%, while exports only rose by 0.9% compared to the previous month.

### 美国外贸赤字急剧上升

1989-07-19
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美国外贸赤字急剧上升
    本报华盛顿7月18日电　据美国商务部今天发表的报告，美国今年5月的外贸逆差急剧上升，达到102亿美元，是1989年头5个月中外贸赤字最高的一个月。据估计，以今年头5个月的外贸逆差折合年率，1989年全年的外贸赤字将超过1110亿美元。
    商务部长说，5月份的外贸赤字令人失望。在这个月里，进口增加了4.3％，而出口只比上月增加0.9％。
