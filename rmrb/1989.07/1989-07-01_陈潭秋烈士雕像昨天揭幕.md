Title: Statue of Martyr Chen Tanqiu Unveiled
Author: Zeng Kun
Date: 1989-07-01
Page: 2

The statue of Chen Tanqiu, one of the founders of the Communist Party of China, was unveiled today at the Revolutionary Martyrs' Cemetery in Urumqi.

Wang Enmao, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Chairman of the Advisory Committee of the Communist Party of New China's Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, Song Hanliang, Secretary of the Autonomous Region Party Committee, and Gao Huanchang, Commander of the Xinjiang Military Region, unveiled the three-meter-tall marble bust.

Chen Tanqiu was secretly executed by the warlord Sheng Shicai in Xinjiang on September 27, 1943.

### 陈潭秋烈士雕像昨天揭幕
曾坤
1989-07-01
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    陈潭秋烈士雕像昨天揭幕
    本报乌鲁木齐七月一日电　记者曾坤报道：中国共产党创始人之一陈潭秋同志的雕像，今天在乌鲁木齐革命烈士陵园落成。
    全国政协副主席、中共新疆维吾尔自治区顾问委员会主任王恩茂，自治区党委书记宋汉良，新疆军区司令员高焕昌等为这座高三米的汉白玉半身雕像揭幕。
    陈潭秋同志一九四三年九月二十七日在新疆被军阀盛世才秘密杀害。
