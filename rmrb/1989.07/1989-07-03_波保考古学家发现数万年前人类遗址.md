Title: Polish and Bulgarian Archaeologists Discover Human Sites from Tens of Thousands of Years Ago
Author:
Date: 1989-07-03
Edition: 3

Polish and Bulgarian archaeologists have discovered human sites dating back 40,000 to 80,000 years in Poland and the Balkan region over the past few years.

According to a report by the Polish News Agency, Polish and Bulgarian scientists have found 40,000-year-old human remains in caves in the Balkans. In the Krakow region of Poland, they discovered human sites from around 23,000 to 17,000 years ago, where people lived by hunting. They also found shelters built by humans 80,000 years ago near Zwolen for hunting purposes.

### 波保考古学家发现数万年前人类遗址

1989-07-03
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    波保考古学家发现数万年前人类遗址
    新华社华沙6月30日电　据波通社报道，近几年，波兰和保加利亚的考古学家曾在波兰和巴尔干地区发现了4万甚至8万年前的人类遗址。
    波、保两国科学家在巴尔干的岩洞中曾发现4万年前的人类遗迹。在波兰的克拉科夫地区，他们找到了大约2.3万—1.7万年前靠狩猎为生的人类遗址。他们还在兹沃伦附近发现了8万年前人类营造的用于打猎的掩蔽体。
