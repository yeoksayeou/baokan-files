Title: Assad and Ershad Send Congratulatory Letters to Jiang Zemin on His Appointment as CCP General Secretary
Author:
Date: 1989-07-07
Edition: 3

	Assad and Ershad Send Congratulatory Letters to Jiang Zemin on His Appointment as CCP General Secretary

	New China News Agency, Beijing, July 7th: On June 28th, Hafez al-Assad, General Secretary of the Arab Socialist Ba'ath Party and President of the Syrian Arab Republic, sent a letter to Jiang Zemin to congratulate him on his election as the General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

	The congratulatory message said: "On the occasion of your election as the General Secretary of the Communist Party of China, I am pleased to extend my sincere congratulations to you on behalf of myself and the leadership of the Arab Socialist Ba'ath Party, and I wish you good health and success.

	"I would like to take this opportunity to express my belief that the friendly and cooperative relations between the Syrian Arab Republic and the People's Republic of China will continue to develop, as this is in the mutual interests of our two peoples, conducive to world peace and justice, and beneficial to the rights of all peoples, especially the right to self-determination, and in line with the principle of non-interference in the internal affairs of other countries.

	"I wish the friendly people of China continuous progress and success under the leadership of the Communist Party of China."

	New China News Agency, Beijing, July 7th: On July 3rd, H.M. Ershad, President of the People's Republic of Bangladesh and Chairman of the Bangladesh National Party, sent a letter to Jiang Zemin to congratulate him on his appointment as the General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

	The congratulatory message said: "I believe that during your tenure, the close and friendly relations that already exist between China and Bangladesh will be further strengthened and consolidated for the benefit of our two countries and peoples, and for peace and stability in the region.

	"I wish you good health, happiness, longevity, and success in your work. I wish the friendly people of China peace, progress, and prosperity under your wise leadership. Please accept my highest regards."

### 阿萨德和艾尔沙德分别致函  祝贺江泽民任中共中央总书记

1989-07-07
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    阿萨德和艾尔沙德分别致函
    祝贺江泽民任中共中央总书记
    新华社北京7月7日电  阿拉伯复兴社会党总书记、阿拉伯叙利亚共和国总统哈菲兹·阿萨德6月28日致函江泽民，祝贺他当选为中国共产党中央委员会总书记。
    贺信说：“在您当选为中国共产党总书记之际，我高兴地以我个人的名义和阿拉伯复兴社会党领导的名义，向您致以最诚挚的祝贺，并祝愿您健康和成功。
    “我愿借此机会表示，我深信阿拉伯叙利亚共和国和中华人民共和国之间的友好和合作关系将不断得到发展，因为这有利于我们两国人民的相互利益，有利于世界和平和正义事业，有利于确保各国人民的权利特别是自决权，有利于不干涉别国内部事务的原则。
    “祝愿友好的中国人民在中国共产党的领导下，不断进步并取得成功。”
    新华社北京7月7日电  孟加拉人民共和国总统、民族党主席侯赛因·穆罕默德·艾尔沙德3日致函江泽民，祝贺他出任中国共产党中央委员会总书记。
    贺信说：“我相信，在您的任期内，为了我们两国、两国人民的利益和为了本地区的和平与稳定，中孟两国业已存在的密切、友好关系将进一步得到加强与巩固。
    “祝愿阁下健康、幸福、长寿、工作顺利，祝友好的中国人民在您的英明领导下和平、进步、繁荣。谨致崇高敬意。”
