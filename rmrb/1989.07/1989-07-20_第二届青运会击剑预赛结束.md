Title: The Second National Youth Games Fencing Preliminaries Conclude
Author: Wáng Kē
Date: 1989-07-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, July 18th.

The preliminaries for the fencing events at the Second National Youth Games concluded yesterday in Shenyang.

Jiangsu, Liaoning, and Hubei teams secured the top three positions in the men's foil team event, respectively. Hubei, Liaoning, and Jiangsu teams took the top three spots in the men's sabre team event. Jiangsu, Liaoning, and Guangdong teams claimed the top three positions in the men's épée team event. Beijing, Shanghai, and Liaoning teams won the top three places in the women's foil team event.

Wang Zhiming (Liaoning), Cheng Denghuai (Hubei), and Tao Hanxiang (Shanghai) finished in the top three in the men's foil individual event. Xiao Hua (Jiangsu), Di Xiaohui (Beijing), and Liu Ying (Liaoning) secured the top three spots in the women's foil individual event. Wang Liqian (Jiangsu), Wang Xuewen (Liaoning), and Li Yi (Jiangsu) finished in the top three in the men's sabre individual event. Wei Jinpeng (Guizhou), Sun Ge (Guangdong), and Wang Yihao (Shanghai) took the top three positions in the men's épée individual event.

### 第二届青运会击剑预赛结束
王珂
1989-07-20
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    第二届青运会击剑预赛结束
    新华社沈阳7月18日电　（记者王珂）第二届全国青少年运动会击剑预赛昨天在沈阳结束。
    江苏、辽宁、湖北队分别获得男子花剑团体前三名，湖北、辽宁、江苏队分别获得男子佩剑团体前三名，江苏、辽宁、广东队分别获得男子重剑团体前三名，北京、上海、辽宁队分别获得女子花剑团体前三名。
    王志军（辽宁）、程登怀（湖北）、陶汉翔（上海）分获男子花剑个人赛前三名，肖华（江苏）、邸晓徽（北京）、刘迎（辽宁）分获女子花剑个人赛前三名，王礼千（江苏）、王学文（辽宁）、李义（江苏）分获男子佩剑个人赛前三名，魏金鹏（贵州）、孙戈（广东）、王翼豪（上海）分获男子重剑个人赛前三名。
