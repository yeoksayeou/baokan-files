Title: M. Rakowski
Author: Tang Deqiao
Date: 1989-07-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 29th.

M. Rakowski

Mieczysław Rakowski, a member of the Polish United Workers' Party Central Committee Political Bureau and Prime Minister of the Polish government, was elected as the First Secretary of the Central Committee at the Central Committee Plenum of the Polish United Workers' Party held on July 29th (see page one for details).

Rakowski was born in December 1926 in a peasant family in the Bydgoszcz province of Poland. He graduated from the Warsaw Academy of Social Sciences in 1956 and obtained a Ph.D. in history in 1957. During World War II, he worked as a worker in the Southern Polish Locomotive and Carriage Repair Shop. From 1945 to 1949, he served in the Polish People's Army and joined the Polish Workers' Party (later renamed the Polish United Workers' Party) in 1946.

Rakowski has served as the deputy editor and editor of the influential Polish magazine "Political Weekly" and has also served as the chairman of the Polish Journalists Association. He was elected as a member of the Polish Parliament in 1972 and as a member of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party in 1975.

In 1981, he became the Deputy Prime Minister, in 1985 he served as the Deputy Speaker of the Polish Parliament, in 1987 he was elected as a member of the Political Bureau of the Polish United Workers' Party Central Committee, and in 1988 he was elected as the Secretary of the Central Committee. In September 1988, he took office as the Prime Minister of Poland. Rakowski advocates for socialist reform and innovation.

Rakowski has visited many countries in Europe, Asia, and Africa. In 1960, he led a delegation of Polish journalists to visit China.

### 米·拉科夫斯基
汤德乔
1989-07-30
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：人物介绍

    米·拉科夫斯基
    波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员、政府总理米奇斯瓦夫·拉科夫斯基在波兰统一工人党7月29日举行的中央全会上当选中央第一书记（消息见一版）。
    拉科夫斯基1926年12月1日生于波兰比得哥什省的一个农民家庭。1956年毕业于华沙社会科学学院，1957年获历史学博士学位。第二次世界大战期间，他曾在波兰南机车车辆修配厂当工人。1945—1949年他在波兰人民军服役，1946年加入波兰工人党（后改为波兰统一工人党）。
    拉科夫斯基曾担任波兰有影响的《政治周刊》副主编、主编，兼任波兰记协主席，1972年当选波兰议员，1975年当选波兰统一工人党中央委员。
    1981年他出任政府副总理，1985年担任波兰议会副议长，1987年当选波兰工人党中央政治局委员，1988年当选中央书记，1988年9月出任波兰政府总理。拉科夫斯基主张社会主义革新和改革。
    拉科夫斯基访问过欧、亚、非洲的许多国家。1960年曾率领波兰新闻工作者代表团访问过中国。（新华社华沙7月29日电）
                                汤德乔
