Title: The Security Council Focuses on the Cyprus Issue and Calls for Easing Tensions Between Greek and Turkish Communities
Author: Liú Kāichéng
Date: 1989-07-25
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, United Nations, July 24th.

The Security Council today called on the Greek and Turkish communities in Cyprus to exercise restraint and take urgent steps to ease the current tense situation in Cyprus.

The Security Council held a consultation meeting this morning to discuss the incident on July 19th where members of the Greek and Turkish communities entered the United Nations buffer zone. After the meeting, the Security Council President, Pejic, issued a statement on behalf of all members of the Security Council, expressing "deep concern" over the tense situation in Cyprus caused by the incident.

The statement called on the Greek and Turkish communities to strictly adhere to the role of the United Nations buffer zone and urged the Turkish community to immediately release the Greek individuals taken from the buffer zone.

The Security Council pointed out that easing tensions between the Greek and Turkish communities is essential to create a suitable atmosphere for the upcoming inter-communal talks aimed at resolving the Cyprus issue.

### 安理会关注塞浦路斯问题  呼吁希土两族缓和紧张局势
刘开宸
1989-07-25
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    安理会关注塞浦路斯问题
    呼吁希土两族缓和紧张局势
    本报联合国7月24日电  记者刘开宸报道：联合国安理会今天要求塞浦路斯希、土两族尽量克制并采取紧急步骤以缓和目前塞浦路斯的紧张局势。
    安理会今天上午举行磋商会，讨论了希、土两族在7月19日冲进联合国缓冲区事件。会后，安理会本月主席佩伊奇发表声明，代表安理会全体理事国，对此事件引起的塞浦路斯紧张局势，表示“深为关切”。
    声明要求希、土两族严格遵守联合国缓冲区的作用，并呼吁塞土族立即释放从缓冲区抓走的希族人。
    安理会指出，希、土两族缓和紧张关系才可为即将举行的解决塞浦路斯问题的两族会谈创造合适的气氛。
