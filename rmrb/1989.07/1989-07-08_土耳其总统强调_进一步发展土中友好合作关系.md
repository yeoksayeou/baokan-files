Title: Turkish President Emphasizes Further Development of Turkey-China Friendly Cooperation
Author: Lǐ Xiándé
Date: 1989-07-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Istanbul, July 7th.

Turkish President Evren today told Xinhua News Agency reporters in Istanbul that further developing friendly cooperation between Turkey and China is in the fundamental interests of the peoples of both countries.

Evren said that since 1982, mutual visits by leaders of the two countries have promoted cooperation between Turkey and China, and he expressed satisfaction with this. He said that as long as the leaders of the two countries make every effort and work actively, cooperation between Turkey and China in all fields will certainly achieve further development.

He said that the friendly cooperation between Turkey and China, especially in the economic and trade fields, has great potential, and both sides should strive to tap this potential.

In 1982, the total trade volume between Turkey and China was less than $25 million, while in 1988, the total trade volume had reached $290 million.

### 土耳其总统强调  进一步发展土中友好合作关系
李贤德
1989-07-08
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    土耳其总统强调
    进一步发展土中友好合作关系
    新华社伊斯坦布尔7月7日电　（记者李贤德）土耳其总统埃夫伦今天在这里对新华社记者发表谈话时强调，进一步发展土中两国友好合作关系，符合两国人民的根本利益。
    埃夫伦说，1982年以来两国领导人的互访促进了土中合作，他对此表示满意。他说，只要两国领导人尽一切努力，积极工作，土中两国在各个领域的合作必将获得进一步发展。
    他说，土中友好合作关系，特别是经贸关系方面，存在巨大潜力，双方应努力挖掘这种潜力。
    1982年土中两国贸易总额不超过2500万美元，1988年贸易总额已达2.9亿美元。
