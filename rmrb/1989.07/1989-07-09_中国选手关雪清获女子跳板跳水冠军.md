Title: Chinese Athlete Guan Xueqing Wins Women's Springboard Diving Championship
Author: Sang Ya Xiong
Date: 1989-07-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, July 8th.

Chinese athlete Guan Xueqing won the women's springboard diving championship with a score of 304 points in the Volksbank Cup International Diving Competition held here today.

In today's competition, American veteran Ferguson won the men's springboard diving championship.

### 中国选手关雪清获女子跳板跳水冠军
桑亚雄
1989-07-09
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    中国选手关雪清获女子跳板跳水冠军
    新华社维也纳7月8日电　（桑亚雄）中国选手关雪清今天在这里举行的大众银行杯国际跳水赛中，以304分的成绩夺得女子跳板跳水冠军。
    在今天的比赛中，美国老将弗格森夺得男子跳板跳水的冠军。
