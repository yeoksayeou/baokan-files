Title: Argentina Willing to Reopen Falklands Talks, British Government Welcomes Statement
Author:
Date: 1989-07-11
Edition: 3

Argentina is willing to reopen talks with Britain over the Falkland Islands, and the British government has welcomed the statement. The British Foreign Office spokesman said today that Britain is studying the remarks made by Argentine President Carlos Menem.

Menem said yesterday that Argentina is prepared to resume talks with Britain without discussing the sovereignty of the Malvinas (Falkland) Islands, in order to restore normal relations between the two countries. In 1982, Britain and Argentina fought a 10-week war over the islands. Britain currently occupies the islands.

### 阿根廷愿重开马岛谈判  英国政府发言表示欢迎

1989-07-11
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    阿根廷愿重开马岛谈判  英国政府发言表示欢迎
    新华社伦敦7月10日电  英国今天对阿根廷新总统梅内姆关于准备恢复英阿两国谈判的讲话表示欢迎，认为这为两国关系正常化开辟了前景。
    英外交部发言人说，英国政府正在研究梅内姆的讲话。
    梅内姆昨天表示，阿根廷准备同英国在不讨论马尔维纳斯群岛（福克兰群岛）主权问题的情况下，同英国重开谈判，以便恢复两国关系。1982年，英阿曾为马岛主权问题进行了为期10周的战争。英国目前占领该群岛。
