Title: Renowned Pediatric Expert Fan Quan's Funeral Held
Author:
Date: 1989-07-17
Edition: 2

Renowned pediatric expert Fan Quan's funeral was held on July 15 in Tianjin. Fan Quan, who dedicated his life to China's pediatric medicine, was a renowned medical expert, a member of the National People's Congress, and the honorary chairman of the China Democratic League in Tianjin.

Fan Quan, who lived to be 82, had a deep understanding of pediatric endocrinology and metabolic diseases, particularly excelling in research related to fluid and electrolyte balance. The "Fan Quan Fluid Therapy Protocol" is widely used. He actively participated in politics and policy-making during his tenure as vice chairman of the Tianjin Municipal People's Congress and vice chairman of the Tianjin Municipal Political Consultative Conference. CPC Politburo Standing Committee member Li Ruihuan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Lu Jiasi, and others sent wreaths.

### 著名儿科专家范权追悼会举行

1989-07-17
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    著名儿科专家范权追悼会举行
    本报天津7月17日电　将一生献给我国儿科事业的著名医学专家、全国人大代表、农工民主党天津市名誉主委范权同志追悼会15日在天津举行。
    享年82岁的范权对儿科内分泌和新陈代谢疾患有较深造诣，尤其擅长有关液体电解质平衡方面的研究，“范氏输液常规”被广泛采用。他曾在市人大副主任、市政协副主席岗位上积极参政议政。中共中央政治局常委李瑞环、全国政协副主席卢嘉锡等送了花圈。
