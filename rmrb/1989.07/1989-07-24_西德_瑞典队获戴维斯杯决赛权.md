Title: West Germany and Sweden Secure Davis Cup Final Spots
Author: Xǔ Fúruì
Date: 1989-07-24
Page: 4

Column: Sports

Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, July 23rd.

In the Davis Cup tennis semi-finals held in Båstad, Sweden, today, Sweden defeated Yugoslavia 3-1 to secure a spot in the final.

According to another report, in the other semi-final held in Munich, West Germany, West Germany defeated the United States to also secure a spot in the final.

Sweden will compete against West Germany for this year's Davis Cup.

### 西德　瑞典队获戴维斯杯决赛权
许福瑞
1989-07-24
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    西德　瑞典队获戴维斯杯决赛权
    新华社斯德哥尔摩七月二十三日电　（记者许福瑞）在瑞典博斯塔德进行的戴维斯杯网球赛半决赛今天下午结束，瑞典队以三比一胜南斯拉夫队，取得决赛权。
    另据报道，在联邦德国慕尼黑进行的另一场半决赛中，联邦德国队战胜美国队，取得了决赛权。
    瑞典队将同联邦德国队争夺今年的戴维斯杯。
