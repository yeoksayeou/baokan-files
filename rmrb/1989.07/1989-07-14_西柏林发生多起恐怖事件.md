Title: Multiple Terrorist Incidents Occur in West Berlin
Author:
Date: 1989-07-14
Edition: 3

	West Berlin has recently experienced multiple terrorist incidents. In the early hours of the 12th, a 28-year-old taxi driver was shot and injured. The perpetrator, after being apprehended, confessed that their motive for the crime was "long-term financial difficulties."

		(This newspaper's special report)

### 西柏林发生多起恐怖事件

1989-07-14
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    西柏林发生多起恐怖事件
    西柏林近日发生多起恐怖事件。12日凌晨，一名28岁的出租汽车司机遭枪击负伤。凶手被抓获后供认，他们的作案原因是“长期缺钱”。
            （本报专电）
