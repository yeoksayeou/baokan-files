Title: The Global Information Market Thrives
Author:
Date: 1989-07-24
Page: 3

According to data provided by the Swiss International Data Corporation on July 20, the global information market's sales in 1988 increased by 12% compared to the previous year, reaching $121 billion. In terms of product categories, personal computers accounted for 39% of the total sales, mid-range and small computers accounted for 37%, and mainframe computers accounted for 24%.

### 世界信息市场兴旺

1989-07-24
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    世界信息市场兴旺
    据瑞士国际数据公司20日提供的数字，1988年，世界信息市场销售额比上一年增加12％，达到1210亿美元。以产品分类，个人电脑的销售额占销售总额的39％，中、小型计算机占37％，大型计算机占24％。
