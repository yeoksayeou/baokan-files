Title: The Seventh Golden Eagle Awards Announced
Author: Wú Kèqiáng  Zhū Guóxián
Date: 1989-07-29
Edition: 4

The Seventh Golden Eagle Awards for Television were announced today in Hangzhou.

The television series "The Last Emperor," produced by the China Television Drama Production Center, won the Outstanding Television Serial Drama Award with a high number of votes. "Family, Spring, Autumn" (co-produced by the Shanghai Television Drama Production Center and Sichuan Television) and "Grand Hotel" (Shanghai Television Drama Production Center), both adapted from famous writer Ba Jin's works, also won the Outstanding Television Serial Drama Award.

The three television dramas that won the Outstanding Television Single-Drama Award are "Eighteen-Year-Old Man" (Shanghai Television Drama Production Center), "The Pigeon" (Shanghai Television Drama Production Center), and "Hepatitis A 1988" (Zhejiang Television Drama Production Center).

"The Three and One" (Shanghai Television Drama Production Center) won the Outstanding Children's Television Drama Award. "Zhu Xi and Li Niang" (Anhui Television Station) won the Outstanding Television Opera Award.

Young actor Chen Daoming won the Best Actor Award, and Xu Ya won the Best Actress Award. Chen Yude won the Best Supporting Actor Award. Beijing People's Art Theatre actress Zhu Lin won the Best Supporting Actress Award.

The special awards went to "Master's Spirit" (co-produced by CCTV, Heilongjiang Film Studio, and the Children's Art Theatre) and "Tiger Man" (Sichuan Television).

### 第七届电视金鹰奖揭晓
吴克强  朱国贤
1989-07-29
第4版(综合)
专栏：

    第七届电视金鹰奖揭晓
    新华社杭州7月28日电　（记者吴克强、朱国贤）第七届大众电视金鹰奖今天在杭州揭晓。
    由中国电视剧制作中心拍摄的《末代皇帝》以高票数当选优秀电视连续剧奖。根据著名作家巴金的作品改编的《家·春·秋》（上海电视剧制作中心和四川电视台合拍）和《大酒店》（上海电视剧制作中心）也同时获优秀电视连续剧奖。
    获优秀电视单剧奖的3部电视剧是上影电视部的《十八岁男子汉》、《花鸽子》（上海电视剧制作中心）和《甲肝1988》（浙江电视剧制作中心）。
    获优秀电视儿童剧奖的是《三个和一个》
  （上海电视剧制作中心）。获优秀电视戏曲片奖的是《朱熹和丽娘》（安徽电视台）。
    获最佳男主角奖的是青年演员陈道明，获最佳女主角奖的是徐娅。陈裕德获最佳男配角奖。北京人艺演员朱琳获最佳女配角奖。
    获特别奖的是《师魂》（中央电视台、龙江电影制片厂、儿童艺术剧院合拍）和《男子汉虎虎》（四川电视台）。
