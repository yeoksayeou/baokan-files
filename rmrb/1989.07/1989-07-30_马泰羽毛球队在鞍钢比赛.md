Title: The Malaysian and Thai Badminton Teams Compete at Anshan Steel
Author: Wáng Zhōngqí
Date: 1989-07-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, July 30th

At the invitation of the Chinese Badminton Association, a delegation of over 30 young badminton players from Malaysia and Thailand visited Anshan Steel for a two-day friendly competition.

In the evening of the 28th, the Liaoning youth men's team and the Malaysian youth men's team engaged in a three-hour intense match, resulting in a narrow victory for the home team with a score of 4-3.

Last night, the Thai youth men's team faced the combined youth men's team from Liaoning and Anshan Steel in a fierce two-hour battle. The home team emerged victorious with a score of 6-3.

### 马泰羽毛球队在鞍钢比赛
王忠奇
1989-07-30
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    马泰羽毛球队在鞍钢比赛
    新华社沈阳七月三十日电  （通讯员王忠奇）应中国羽毛球协会邀请，马来西亚和泰国青年羽毛球队一行三十多人来鞍钢进行为期两天的访问比赛。
    在二十八日晚间的比赛中，辽宁青年男子队和马来西亚青年男子队，进行了三个多小时的对抗赛，结果以四比三险胜。
    昨晚，泰国青年男子队和辽宁、鞍钢青年男子联队经过两个多小时的激烈拚搏。结果主队以六比三的成绩，战胜了客队。
