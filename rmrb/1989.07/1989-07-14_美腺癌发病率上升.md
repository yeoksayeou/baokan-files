Title: Esophageal Adenocarcinoma Incidence on the Rise in the U.S.
Author:
Date: 1989-07-14
Edition: 3

According to a new survey, the incidence of a rare type of esophageal cancer, adenocarcinoma, has increased fivefold compared to the past, and men are more affected than women.

Paul Hesketh, a cancer specialist from Boston University School of Medicine who conducted the survey, said that adenocarcinoma previously accounted for less than 5% of esophageal cancers, but now it has risen to 30%.

### 美腺癌发病率上升

1989-07-14
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美腺癌发病率上升
    据新华社北京7月14日电　据外电报道，美国一项新的调查发现，食道癌中一种不常见的腺癌的发病率比过去要高5倍，而且男性多于女性。
    进行这项调查的波士顿大学医学院的肿瘤学家保罗·赫斯基思博士说，腺癌以前在食道癌中所占比例不足5％，而现已达到30％。
