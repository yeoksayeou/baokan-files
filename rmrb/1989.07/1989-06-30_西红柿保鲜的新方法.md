Title: New Method for Tomato Preservation
Author:
Date: 1989-06-30
Edition: 3

    New Method for Tomato Preservation
    Xinhua News Agency, London, June 29th - According to a report from the magazine *New Scientist*, American scientists have recently invented a new method for preserving tomatoes, which can extend the shelf life of ripe tomatoes from 4-5 days to 10-12 days.

    Biologists at Cornell University in New York discovered that a variety of tomato from Brazil remains fresh after ripening. They found that this variety contains three times more putrescine than ordinary tomatoes.

    They placed ordinary tomatoes in a sealed container filled with a putrescine solution. When the air in the sealed container was removed, the putrescine seeped into the tomatoes. Using this vacuum infiltration method, they successfully extended the shelf life of the tomatoes by more than onefold.

    American scientists hope to isolate the special genes from the Brazilian tomatoes to develop new varieties of tomatoes using genetic engineering technology.

### 西红柿保鲜的新方法

1989-06-30
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    西红柿保鲜的新方法
    新华社伦敦6月29日电  据《新科学家》杂志报道，美国科学家最近发明了一种西红柿保鲜的新方法，可以使成熟西红柿的保鲜时间从4—5天增加到10—12天。
    纽约康奈尔大学的生物学家发现，巴西有一种西红柿，成熟后不易腐烂。他们研究后得知，这种西红柿中含的丁二胺为普通西红柿的3倍。
    他们把普通西红柿放在盛有丁二胺溶液的密封容器中，当把密封容器中的空气抽出时，丁二胺渗进西红柿中。他们用这种真空渗透法使丁二胺渗入西红柿，从而使其保鲜时间延长1倍以上。
    美国科学家希望从巴西西红柿中分离出特殊的基因，以便用遗传工程技术研究出西红柿的新品种。
