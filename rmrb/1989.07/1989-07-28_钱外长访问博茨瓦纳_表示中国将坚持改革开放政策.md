Title: Chinese Foreign Minister Visits Botswana and Expresses China's Commitment to Reform and Opening Up
Author: Chen Qimin, Zhang Yongxing
Date: 1989-07-28
Edition: 3

Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen met with Botswana's Vice President Masire today in Gaborone. He stated that the Chinese government will not yield to any external pressure and will continue to adhere to the policy of reform and opening up, while developing friendship with the Third World.

Foreign Minister Qian praised Botswana's realistic foreign policy and its opposition to apartheid.

Vice President Masire highly praised the friendly relations between Botswana and China, and expressed satisfaction with the economic and technological cooperation between the two countries.

At tonight's welcoming banquet hosted by Botswana's Minister of Foreign Affairs Chipe, Foreign Minister Qian said that the Chinese government is pleased to see the trend towards peace in Southern Africa. The Chinese government will continue to support the frontline states in Southern Africa in their struggle against South Africa's apartheid policy.

Minister Chipe highly praised China's independent foreign policy based on the five principles of peaceful coexistence.

### 钱外长访问博茨瓦纳  表示中国将坚持改革开放政策
陈启民  张永兴
1989-07-28
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    钱外长访问博茨瓦纳
    表示中国将坚持改革开放政策
    新华社哈博罗内7月27日电　（记者陈启民、张永兴）钱其琛外长今天在这里会见博茨瓦纳副总统穆西时指出，中国政府不会屈服于任何外来压力，它将一如既往地坚持改革开放政策，发展同第三世界的友谊。
    钱外长对博茨瓦纳实行现实的外交政策和反对种族隔离政策表示赞赏。
    穆西副总统高度赞扬了博中两国的友好关系，对两国间的经济技术合作表示满意。
    在今晚博茨瓦纳外交部长切佩举行的欢迎宴会上，钱其琛外长说，中国政府高兴地看到南部非洲走向和平的趋势。中国政府将坚持不懈地支持南部非洲前线国家反对南非推行种族隔离政策的斗争。
    切佩外长高度赞扬了建立在四项基本原则上的独立自主的中国外交政策。
