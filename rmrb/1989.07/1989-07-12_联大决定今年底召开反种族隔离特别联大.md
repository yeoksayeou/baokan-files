Title: The UN General Assembly Decides to Hold a Special Session on Apartheid by the End of the Year
Author: Liú Kāi Chén
Date: 1989-07-12
Edition: 3

The UN General Assembly today unanimously decided that the "Special Session on Apartheid and Its Destructive Impact in Southern Africa" will be held from December 12 to 14 this year.

The General Assembly reconvened today at the urgent request of the Chairman of the Special Committee Against Apartheid to discuss "The Issue of South Africa's Apartheid Policy." The Special Committee Against Apartheid proposed specific dates for the special session on apartheid, which was passed unanimously without a vote.

The General Assembly had decided last December to hold this special session before the 44th session of the General Assembly this year.

### 联大决定今年底召开反种族隔离特别联大
刘开宸
1989-07-12
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    联大决定今年底召开反种族隔离特别联大
    本报联合国7月11日电　记者刘开宸报道：第43届联合国大会今天一致决定“关于种族隔离及其在南部非洲破坏性影响的特别联大”于今年12月12日至14日召开。
    联大应反对种族隔离委员会主席的紧急要求于今天复会，审议“南非政府的种族隔离政策问题”。会上，种族隔离委员会提出了关于召开种族隔离特别联大具体日期的建议，未经投票获一致通过。
    联大曾在去年12月决定在今年第44届联大之前召开此特别联大。
