Title: Gorbachev Arrives in Paris, Mitterrand Welcomes at Airport
Author: Zhang Qihua
Date: 1989-07-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, July 4th.

Soviet leader Gorbachev, accompanied by his wife, arrived in Paris at 11:55 AM local time today. French President Mitterrand and his wife were at the airport to welcome them.

At the welcome ceremony held at the airport, President Mitterrand expressed his pleasure in having the opportunity to discuss issues related to world peace and development with Gorbachev. He said, "We will discuss Franco-Soviet relations and the future of Europe." Gorbachev stated that the Soviet Union is willing to continue the work that has already begun.

### 戈尔巴乔夫抵巴黎密特朗到机场欢迎
张启华
1989-07-04
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    戈尔巴乔夫抵巴黎密特朗到机场欢迎
    本报巴黎7月4日电　记者张启华报道：苏联领导人戈尔巴乔夫偕夫人今天中午11时55分（当地时间）到达巴黎，法国总统密特朗和夫人前往机场迎接。
    在机场举行的欢迎仪式上，密特朗总统表示，他为能有机会同戈尔巴乔夫讨论涉及世界和平以及发展的问题而感到高兴。他说，“我们将讨论法苏关系，讨论欧洲的前途”。戈尔巴乔夫表示，苏联愿意将已经开始的工作继续进行下去。
