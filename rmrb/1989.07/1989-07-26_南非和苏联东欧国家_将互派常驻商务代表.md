Title: South Africa and Soviet Eastern European Countries to Exchange Resident Business Representatives
Author:
Date: 1989-07-26
Edition: 3

South Africa and Soviet Eastern European countries are to exchange resident business representatives.

Xinhua News Agency, Gaborone, July 24th - According to news from South Africa today, South Africa is negotiating with diplomats from the Soviet Union, Hungary, and Poland to exchange business representatives.

The South African "Star" reported on Monday that Soviet officials and South African businessmen had held a secret meeting in London.

The report said that the Soviet Union, Poland, and Hungary will send business representatives to reside in Pretoria within 18 months.

A spokesperson for the South African Foreign Ministry made no comment on the report.

The spokesperson confirmed that South Africa's trade volume with Eastern European countries has recently increased.

### 南非和苏联东欧国家  将互派常驻商务代表

1989-07-26
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    南非和苏联东欧国家
    将互派常驻商务代表
    新华社哈博罗内7月24日电　据今天来自南非的消息说，南非正和苏联、匈牙利和波兰的外交官员商谈互派商务代表事宜。
    南非《星报》星期一报道，苏联官员和南非企业家曾在伦敦秘密会晤。
    报道说，苏、波、匈三国将在18个月内派商务代表常驻比勒陀利亚。
    南非外交部发言人没有对此报道作任何评论。
    这位发言人证实，南非同东欧国家的贸易额最近有所增长。
