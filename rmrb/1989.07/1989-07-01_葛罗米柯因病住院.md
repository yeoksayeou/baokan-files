Title: Gromyko Hospitalized Due to Illness
Author:
Date: 1989-07-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 30th.

Former Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Gromyko, underwent surgery recently due to a vascular condition and is currently receiving treatment in the hospital.

On June 30th, the First Deputy Director of the Press Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Gremitsky-Khe, stated at a press conference that Gromyko will turn 80 years old on July 18th. Considering his condition and age, his post-operative state is "satisfactory."

Gromyko was relieved of his duties as a member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the CPSU in September 1988 and retired the following day. He was also removed from his position as Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.

### 葛罗米柯因病住院

1989-07-01
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    葛罗米柯因病住院
    新华社莫斯科6月30日电　原苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席葛罗米柯因脉管病发作于最近作了手术，目前正在医院治疗。
    苏联外交部新闻局第一副局长格列米茨基赫30日在新闻发布会上说，7月18日葛罗米柯将满80周岁，从他的病情和年龄看手术后的状况“令人满意”。
    葛罗米柯在1988年9月30日被解除了苏共中央政治局委员职务，并退休。次日他又被解除苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席职务。
