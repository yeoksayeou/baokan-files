Title: The Moon is Gradually Moving Away from the Earth
Author:
Date: 1989-07-20
Edition: 3

The Moon is gradually moving away from the Earth. According to a report by Xinhua News Agency in Washington on July 18, American scientists have discovered after 20 years of observation and analysis that the Moon is moving away from the Earth at a rate of 1.5 inches per year. However, the Moon will never completely escape the Earth's influence.

The head of the Geodesy Applications Group at NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory, Joan Dickey, said that periodic high-speed laser data obtained from the laser retroreflector on the Moon indicates that the Moon may have a fluid core. This data also reaffirms the theory that the length of each day on Earth is actually not the same. Twenty years ago, when the Apollo 11 astronauts first landed on the Moon, they set up a laser retroreflector and other instruments on the lunar surface.

### 月球正缓慢偏离地球

1989-07-20
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    月球正缓慢偏离地球
    新华社华盛顿7月18日电　美国科学家根据20年的考察和分析后发现，月球正以每年1.5英寸的速度偏离地球，但月球永远摆脱不了地球的影响。
    美国宇航局喷气推进实验室负责大地测量学应用组的琼·迪基说，定期从月球上的激光测距后向反射器获得的高速激光资料表明，月球可能有流体核心。这些资料还再次肯定了地球上每天的时间长度实际上是不一样的理论。20年前“阿波罗11号”宇航员首次登上月球时，在月球表面设置了激光测距后向反射器等仪器。
