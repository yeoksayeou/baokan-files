Title: A Painting by French Artist Gauguin Stolen
Author:
Date: 1989-07-26
Edition: 3

A painting by the French artist Gauguin, valued at over 600,000 pounds, was recently stolen from a cargo warehouse at London's Heathrow Airport, according to a report by the British Broadcasting Corporation on July 25.

### 法画家高更一名画被盗

1989-07-26
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    法画家高更一名画被盗
    据英国广播公司7月25日报道，法国艺术家高更的一幅价值60多万英镑的水彩画日前在英国希思罗机场货仓被窃。
            （本报专电）
