Title: Spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Reiterates China's Commitment to the Sino-British Joint Declaration and Refuses to Allow Hong Kong to Be Used as a Base for Subverting the Central People's Government
Author:
Date: 1989-07-08
Edition: 1

The spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs reiterated China's policies towards Hong Kong today in response to journalists' questions.

A journalist asked: British Foreign Secretary Geoffrey Howe stated on July 4 in Hong Kong that China's "suppression of the democratic movement" has greatly undermined the confidence of Hong Kong people. What is your comment on this?

The spokesperson said: Some people in Hong Kong do not fully understand the true situation regarding China's suppression of the counter-revolutionary riots, leading to some misunderstandings, which is understandable. Once they understand the true situation, these misunderstandings will be dispelled. Recently, General Secretary Jiang Zemin and Director Ji Pengfei reiterated that China's policies towards Hong Kong, formulated in accordance with the "one country, two systems" concept, will not change. The Chinese government will steadfastly fulfill all the obligations undertaken in the Sino-British Joint Declaration. We will not impose socialist policies on the Hong Kong region, nor will the Hong Kong region interfere with or attempt to change the socialist system on the mainland. It is even more unacceptable to allow Hong Kong to be used as a base for subverting the central people's government. It must be pointed out that the British government has an unshirkable responsibility to seriously fulfill all the obligations in the Sino-British Joint Declaration and to maintain Hong Kong's prosperity and stability during the transitional period. We also hope that the British government will adopt a responsible attitude and create a favorable atmosphere and environment for continued cooperation between China and Britain.

### 外交部发言人重申  中国政府信守中英联合声明  不允许利用香港作颠覆中央人民政府基地

1989-07-08
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    外交部发言人重申
    中国政府信守中英联合声明
    不允许利用香港作颠覆中央人民政府基地
    新华社北京7月8日电　外交部发言人今天在回答记者提问时重申了中国政府对香港的方针政策。
    有记者问：英国外交大臣杰弗里·豪7月4日在香港表示，中国“镇压民主运动”使港人的信心受到极大的损害。你对此有何评论？
    这位发言人说：香港有些人对中国政府平息反革命暴乱的真实情况不了解，或不完全了解，产生了一些误解，这是可以理解的。在他们了解了真实情况后，误解是会消除的。最近江泽民总书记和姬鹏飞主任重申，中国政府按照“一国两制”构想所制定的关于香港问题的一系列方针政策是不会改变的，中国政府将始终不渝地信守中英联合声明所承担的各项义务。我们不会把社会主义的政策推行到香港地区，香港地区也不应干预或企图改变内地的社会主义制度，更不能允许利用香港作为颠覆中央人民政府的基地。必须指出，英国政府对于认真履行中英联合声明的各项义务，保持香港过渡时期的繁荣和稳定有着不可推卸的责任。我们还希望英国政府能够切实采取负责任的态度，创造有利于中英双方继续合作的气氛和环境。
