Title: Gorbachev and Rajiv Gandhi Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1989-07-16
Edition: 3

Soviet-Indian Relations to Continue Developing with China

MOSCOW, July 15 (Xinhua) -- Indian Prime Minister Rajiv Gandhi arrived here today for a one-day friendly working visit.

According to TASS, Soviet leader Mikhail Gorbachev held talks with Rajiv Gandhi on major issues in the current world situation.

Both sides noted that positive trends are growing in Europe, including arms control talks and the reduction of military confrontation. Gorbachev and Rajiv Gandhi expressed their critical views on certain opinions and ideas from Western political circles.

Both sides also discussed economic relations in the Asia-Pacific region and the world, as well as the establishment of a new world economic order. They expressed the belief that truly solving problems of global significance can only be achieved with the active participation of the United Nations and the internationalization of these issues.

Both sides pointed out that the Soviet Union and India have similar stances on the events occurring in China, expressing their "determination to continue developing friendly neighborly relations with this great country."

Gorbachev and Rajiv Gandhi also discussed bilateral economic, technological, and military cooperation, and exchanged information on domestic reforms.

Rajiv Gandhi arrived here after participating in the celebrations of the 200th anniversary of the French Revolution and will depart tomorrow morning.

### 戈氏与拉·甘地会谈  苏印将继续发展对华关系

1989-07-16
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    戈氏与拉·甘地会谈
    苏印将继续发展对华关系
    新华社莫斯科7月15日电  印度总理拉·甘地今天抵达这里进行为期1天的友好工作访问。
    据塔斯社报道，苏联领导人戈尔巴乔夫同拉·甘地就当前世界形势的主要问题进行了会谈。
    双方注意到，欧洲的积极倾向正在增长，其中包括裁军谈判和减少军事对抗。戈尔巴乔夫和拉·甘地对西方政界的某些观点和观念表示了他们批评性的意见。
    双方还讨论了亚太地区和世界的经济关系以及建立世界经济新秩序的问题。他们表示相信，真正解决具有世界意义的问题只能在联合国积极参加和使这些问题国际化的情况下才能做到。
    双方指出，苏印对中国发生的事件立场相似，表示“决心继续发展同这一伟大国家的睦邻关系”。
    戈尔巴乔夫和拉·甘地还讨论了双边经济、科技关系以及军事合作方面的问题，并就各自国内的变革交换了信息。
    拉·甘地是在参加法国大革命200周年庆祝活动后来到这里进行访问的，他将于明晨离开。
