Title: Panamanian Militia Protests U.S. Show of Force in Panama
Author:
Date: 1989-07-26
Edition: 3

Panamanian militia members of the "Dignity Battalion" held a peaceful demonstration in front of the U.S. Clayton Military Base in the Panama Canal Zone on July 25, protesting the latest "threats and provocations" by the U.S. military.

These militiamen, trained by the Panamanian Defense Forces, chanted slogans such as "Down with American imperialism" and "U.S. Southern Command, get out."

In the early hours of July 25, a contingent of U.S. troops was transferred from the Clayton Base to the Gamboa Military Base. During this military maneuver, the U.S. military deployed tanks, helicopters, and fighter jets, intimidating Panama.

Recently, U.S. forces stationed in Panama have conducted a series of military exercises. The Panamanian government has accused the U.S. military of violating the 1977 Panama Canal Treaties signed by the United States and Panama.

### 巴拿马民兵示威抗议美对巴耀武扬威

1989-07-26
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    巴拿马民兵示威抗议美对巴耀武扬威
    新华社巴拿马城7月25日电　巴拿马民兵“尊严大队”成员25日在巴拿马运河区美国克莱顿军事基地前举行和平示威，抗议美军新的“恐吓和挑衅行动”。
    这些由巴拿马国防军训练 的民兵们高呼“打倒美帝国主义”和“美国南方司令部滚出去”等口号。
    25日凌晨，一批美军从克莱顿基地调往甘博亚军事基地。
    在这次军事调动中，美军还出动了坦克、直升飞机和战斗机，对巴拿马进行恫吓。
    最近，美国驻巴拿马军队进行了一系列军事演习。巴拿马政府指责美军的这些行动违背了美国和巴拿马政府1977年签署的新巴拿马运河条约。
