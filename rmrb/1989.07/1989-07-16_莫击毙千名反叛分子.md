Title: Mozambican Forces Kill Over a Thousand Rebel Fighters
Author:
Date: 1989-07-16
Edition: 3

Mozambican radio reported on July 15 that in the past three months, the Mozambican government forces have killed 1,040 anti-government armed fighters and captured 117 others. The broadcast said that among them, 334 anti-government elements were killed by the government forces in the central province of Sofala.

### 莫击毙千名反叛分子

1989-07-16
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    莫击毙千名反叛分子
    据新华社马普托7月15日电　莫桑比克电台15日广播说，在过去的3个月里，莫桑比克政府军击毙1040名反政府武装分子，俘虏117人。广播说，其中334名反政府人员是政府军在中部的苏法拉省打死的。
