Title: A Romanian Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-07-18
Edition: 2

A Romanian exhibition of automation equipment and instruments opened in Beijing on the morning of July 18. The exhibition showcases over eighty representative instruments, meters, and devices used in automatic control systems from Romania. This is the first specialized industrial product exhibition held by Romania in China. It will be beneficial for promoting further cooperation between China and Romania in the field of high technology.

### 罗马尼亚一展览会在京开幕

1989-07-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    罗马尼亚一展览会在京开幕
    本报讯　罗马尼亚自动化设备和仪表展览会7月18日上午在北京开幕。展览会展出罗马尼亚用于自动控制系统的具有代表性的仪器、仪表及设备八十余台、件。这是罗马尼亚首次在华举办的专业性工业产品展览会。它将有利于促进中罗两国在高技术领域的合作进一步发展。
