Title: Central Office and Central Liaison Department Issue Announcement
Author:
Date: 1989-07-19
Edition: 1

The Central Office of the Communist Party of China and the Central Committee's International Liaison Department issued an announcement on July 19, expressing heartfelt thanks to political parties and leaders, heads of state and government, friendly organizations, and individuals from various countries for their congratulations on Jiang Zemin's election as the General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee. The full text of the announcement is as follows:

	At the Fourth Plenary Session of the Thirteenth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Jiang Zemin was elected as the General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee. Many political parties and their leaders, heads of state and government, friendly organizations, and individuals from various countries have sent congratulatory messages and letters to the CPC Central Committee and Jiang Zemin, expressing warm congratulations and best wishes. On behalf of the CPC Central Committee and Jiang Zemin, the Central Office of the Communist Party of China and the Central Committee's International Liaison Department extend our heartfelt thanks.

### 中办和中联部发表公告  感谢海外各界来电函  祝贺江泽民任总书记

1989-07-19
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    中办和中联部发表公告
    感谢海外各界来电函  祝贺江泽民任总书记
    新华社北京7月19日电　中共中央办公厅和中共中央对外联络部7月19日发表公告，表示衷心感谢一些国家的政党及其领导人、国家元首和政府首脑、友好团体和友好人士对江泽民同志当选中共中央总书记的祝贺。公告全文如下：
    在中国共产党十三届四中全会上，江泽民同志当选为中共中央总书记，承蒙很多国家的政党及其领导人、国家元首和政府首脑、友好团体和友好人士，向中共中央和江泽民同志发来贺电贺信，表示热烈祝贺和良好祝愿。中共中央办公厅、中共中央对外联络部受中共中央和江泽民同志的委托，谨表示衷心的感谢。
