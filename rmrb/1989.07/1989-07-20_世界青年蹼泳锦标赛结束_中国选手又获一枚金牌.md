Title: The First World Youth Finswimming Championship Concludes
Author: Hóu Fèngjīn
Date: 1989-07-20
Edition: 4

The First World Youth Finswimming Championship Concludes
Chinese Athlete Wins Another Gold Medal

BEIJING, July 18 (Xinhua) — The first World Youth Finswimming Championship, which lasted for three days, concluded today in Dunakeszi, Hungary. In the final day of competition, Chinese athlete Cui Min won the gold medal in the women's 50-meter apnea swimming event with a time of 18.99 seconds. In the other six events, the champions were all Soviet athletes.

Additionally, in today's competition, Chinese athlete Cheng Chao won the silver medal in the women's 1500-meter finswimming event, Cui Min won the bronze medal in the women's 100-meter finswimming event, and Li Ze won the bronze medal in the men's 400-meter finswimming event.

The championship began on July 16, with 123 male and female athletes from 15 countries participating. In the 20 events, apart from the two gold medals won by Chinese athlete Cui Min, the remaining 18 gold medals were all taken by Soviet athletes.

### 世界青年蹼泳锦标赛结束  中国选手又获一枚金牌
侯凤菁
1989-07-20
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    世界青年蹼泳锦标赛结束
    中国选手又获一枚金牌
    新华社布达佩斯7月18日电　（记者侯凤菁）历时3天的第一届世界青年蹼泳锦标赛今天在多瑙新城结束。在最后一天比赛中，中国选手崔敏以18秒99的成绩夺得女子50米屏气潜泳冠军。在其它6个项目的比赛中，冠军全部被苏联选手夺走。
    另外，在今天的比赛中，中国选手程超获女子1500米蹼泳第二名，崔敏获女子100米蹼泳第三名，李泽获男子400米蹼泳第三名。
    这届锦标赛是16日开始的，共有15个国家的123名男、女选手参加。在总共20个项目的比赛中，除中国选手崔敏获两项冠军外，其他18个项目的金牌全被苏联选手夺得。
