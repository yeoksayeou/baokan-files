Title: Ms. Chen Li Hua of Hong Kong Donates Cars to the Asian Games
Author: Liu Xin
Date: 1989-07-04
Edition: 4

Hong Kong's Chen Li Hua, chairperson of the Fuhua Enterprise Company, donated three cars to the upcoming 11th Asian Games in Beijing to show her support. At the donation ceremony held on July 3, Liu Yu Ling, the executive vice-secretary of the Asian Games Foundation, presented Chen Li Hua with an honorary certificate.

Chen Li Hua is a Hong Kong businesswoman and a dedicated social activist. She has shown great concern for the economic development and social progress of the mainland, and has made numerous donations to educational, cultural institutions, and the China Population Welfare Foundation. Chen is particularly interested in the Asian Games to be held in Beijing and has expressed her desire to contribute to the preparations for this international sports event.

### 香港陈丽华女士向亚运会捐赠小轿车
刘新
1989-07-04
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

    香港陈丽华女士向亚运会捐赠小轿车
    本报讯　香港富华企业公司董事长陈丽华女士为表示对即将在北京举办的第十一届亚运会的支持，赠给亚运会三辆小轿车。在7月3日举行的赠车仪式上，亚运会基金会常务副秘书长刘玉令同志向陈丽华颁发了荣誉证书。
    陈丽华女士是一位香港企业家，同时又是一位热心的社会活动家。她关心大陆的经济建设和社会发展，曾多次向大陆的教育、文化界和中国人口福利基金会等单位捐款捐物。陈女士对在北京举办的亚运会非常关注，表示要为这次国际体育盛会的准备工作做出贡献。
    （刘新）
