Title: 30 Cities Conduct Product Quality Inspections
Author: Lǐ Hóngwěi
Date: 1989-07-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, July 29th.

With the approval of the State Council, the National Bureau of Technical Supervision will, in collaboration with relevant departments, conduct inspections on five types of products sold in 30 cities, including Beijing, Tianjin, and Shanghai.

This was announced today in Harbin by a responsible official from the National Bureau of Technical Supervision.

It is understood that the timing and specific types of products for this inspection will be kept confidential. For units found to be producing or selling substandard goods, in addition to being dealt with according to relevant regulations, their names will also be published through media outlets.

### 三十市开展商品质量抽查  抽查时间和品种事先保密
李洪伟
1989-07-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    三十市开展商品质量抽查
    抽查时间和品种事先保密
    新华社哈尔滨7月29日电　（实习生李洪伟）经国务院批准，国家技术监督局将会同有关部门，对京津沪等30个城市所经营的5种商品进行抽查。
    这是国家技术监督局负责人今天在哈尔滨宣布的。
    据了解，这次对抽查的时间和商品的品种实行保密；对查出的生产、经营伪劣商品的单位，除按照有关法规处理外，还要通过新闻单位公布企业的名称。
