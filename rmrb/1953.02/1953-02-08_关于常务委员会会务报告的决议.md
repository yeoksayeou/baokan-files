Title: Resolution on the Work Report of the Standing Committee
Author:
Date: 1953-02-08
Page: 1

    The Fourth Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference unanimously approved the work report of the Standing Committee presented by Vice Chairman Chen Shutong, and resolved: In preparation for convening the Second Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the Standing Committee is specifically entrusted to carry out all preparatory work and convocation work; and it was decided to expand the number of Standing Committee members, adding twenty-three additional Standing Committee members.

### 关于常务委员会会务报告的决议

1953-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

    关于常务委员会会务报告的决议
    中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第四次会议一致同意陈叔通副主席代表常务委员会提出的关于会务的报告，并决议：为准备召开中国人民政协第二届全体会议，特委托常务委员会进行全部准备工作和召集工作；并决定扩大常务委员名额，增选常务委员二十三人。
