Title: Philippine Liberation Army Wins Battle
Author:
Date: 1953-02-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Manila dispatch: The Associated Press reported from Manila that the Philippine People's Liberation Army defeated a Quirino puppet army unit on the 14th at the foot of Mount Sampalevit near Nagataring City, Basilan Province. The Quirino puppet army's communique admitted that the Liberation Army wounded ten Quirino soldiers.

### 菲律宾解放军出击获胜

1953-02-22
第4版()
专栏：

    菲律宾解放军出击获胜
    【新华社讯】美联社马尼拉消息透露：菲律宾人民解放军十四日在蜂牙丝兰省网牙沓林城附近三描礼示山脚打败了一支季里诺傀儡军部队。季里诺傀儡军的公报承认：解放军打伤了季里诺军十人。
