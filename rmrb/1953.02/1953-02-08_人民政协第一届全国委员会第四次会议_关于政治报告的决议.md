Title: Resolution of the Fourth Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference on the Political Report
Author:
Date: 1953-02-08
Page: 1

The Fourth Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference unanimously approved the political report made by Vice Chairman Zhou Enlai. The meeting considers that the main tasks of the Chinese people at present are: (1) to continue to strengthen the struggle to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea, strengthen national defense, and smash any new aggressive conspiracies by the U.S. aggressors against Korea and our country, in order to strive for a reasonable peaceful settlement of the Korean issue and other related issues in the Far East; (2) to concentrate efforts, increase production, practice strict economy, and complete and overfulfill the 1953 economic construction plan, national defense construction plan, and social and cultural construction plan, creating a good start for the country's first Five-Year Plan; (3) to actively prepare for the elections of people's congresses at all local levels and the National People's Congress, and to realize the further democratization of the country. The meeting calls on all nationalities, democratic classes, democratic parties, people's organizations, patriotic democratic人士 from all walks of life, and overseas Chinese to unite more closely under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong, the Chinese Communist Party, and the Central People's Government, and to mobilize together to strive for the victorious completion of the above three major tasks.

### 人民政协第一届全国委员会第四次会议  关于政治报告的决议

1953-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

    人民政协第一届全国委员会第四次会议
    关于政治报告的决议
    中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第四次会议一致同意周恩来副主席所作的政治报告。会议认为，中国人民目前的主要任务是：（一）继续加强抗美援朝斗争，加强国防力量，粉碎美国侵略者对于朝鲜和我国的任何新的侵略阴谋，以争取朝鲜问题和远东其他有关问题的合理的和平解决；（二）集中力量，增加生产，厉行节约，完成和超额完成一九五三年的经济建设计划、国防建设计划和社会文化建设计划，为国家建设的第一个五年计划造成良好的开端；（三）积极准备地方各级人民代表大会和全国人民代表大会的选举，实现国家的进一步的民主化。会议号召全国各民族、各民主阶级、各民主党派、各人民团体、各界爱国民主人士和国外华侨，在毛泽东主席、中国共产党和中央人民政府的领导之下，结成更巩固的人民民主统一战线，一致动员起来，为胜利地完成上述三大任务而奋斗。
