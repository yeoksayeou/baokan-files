Our Great Motherland
The large-scale, focused, and methodical petroleum geological exploration work vigorously carried out in the Northwest region has laid the foundation for the construction of China's future petroleum industry. This is the location of the newly discovered oil field in northern Shaanxi. Geological exploration personnel are here surveying the structure of the oil-bearing strata.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-02-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在西北地区大力进行的有重点、有步骤的石油地质探勘工作，给我国今后石油工业的建设奠定了基础。这是陕北最近被发现的新油田的所在地。地质探勘人员正在这里探测油层的构造。
