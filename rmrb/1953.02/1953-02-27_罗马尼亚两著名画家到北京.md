Title: Two Famous Romanian Painters Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-02-27
Page: 1

    Two Famous Romanian Painters Arrive in Beijing
   [Xinhua News Agency, February 26th] Famous Romanian People's Republic painters Macovei Lisea (female) and Taru Eugene arrived in Beijing by plane on the 26th. They were sent by the Romanian Fine Arts Union to visit our country in accordance with this year's cooperation plan under the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement. Macovei Lisea is the secretary of the Union. Hong Shen, Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government, Ye Qianyu, Vice Chairman of the All-China Artists Association, Yu Feng, Secretary-General, Professor Wu Zuoren, a painter, and representatives from the Art Administration Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government went to the airport to welcome them.
    An Ding, Counselor of the Romanian People's Republic Embassy in China, and Meng Tiannu, First Secretary, also went to welcome them.

### 罗马尼亚两著名画家到北京

1953-02-27
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚两著名画家到北京
   【新华社二十六日讯】罗马尼亚人民共和国名画家马科威·里霞（女）及塔鲁·尤金于二十六日乘机抵达北京。他们是根据中罗文化合作协定本年度合作计划由罗马尼亚造型艺术联合会派来我国进行访问的。马科威·里霞是该联合会的书记。前往机场欢迎的有中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深，中华全国美术工作者协会副主席叶浅予、秘书长郁风、画家吴作人教授及中央人民政府文化部艺术事业管理局代表等。
    罗马尼亚人民共和国驻华大使馆参赞安定及一等秘书孟天努等亦前往欢迎。
