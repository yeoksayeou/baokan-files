Title: National Committee of the CPPCC Nationalities Affairs Group Invites Head of Tibetan Delegation to Report on Impressions of Visiting the Country
Author:
Date: 1953-02-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency: The Nationalities Affairs Group of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference invited Liu Xia·Tudeng Taba, head of the Tibetan delegation, on the 23rd to report on the construction situation in Tibet after its peaceful liberation and the delegation's impressions of visiting various parts of the country.

    When reporting on his impressions of visiting the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Liu Xia·Tudeng Taba pointed out that since the implementation of regional autonomy in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, national unity has become more consolidated, the economy and culture have been developing day by day, religious beliefs have been protected, and the people are living a happy life. He said, "The path to happiness that the people of Inner Mongolia are on today is the path that the people of Tibet must take."

    Liu Xia·Tudeng Taba reported with great excitement on the situation of the motherland's industrial construction and development that they saw in the Northeast; he reported on the prosperous situation of industry and commerce in major cities such as Tianjin and Shanghai.

    Liu Xia·Tudeng Taba reported on his impressions of visiting the Northwest. He said that the Northwest region and Tibet have historical and close ties. We have personally seen that the policy of protecting religious beliefs is being correctly implemented throughout the motherland.

    Liu Xia·Tudeng Taba finally stated that after they return to Tibet, they will convey the brilliant achievements of the motherland's construction to the Tibetan people, further unite the various ethnic groups in Tibet, and work hard under the leadership of Chairman Mao to build a new Tibet in the motherland.

    Also, Liu Xia·Tudeng Taba, head of the Tibetan delegation, on behalf of the Dalai Lama, and Deputy Head Danba Rijie, on behalf of the Panchen Erdeni, presented gifts to Li Weihan, Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, on the 24th.

    Liu Xia·Tudeng Taba, on behalf of the Dalai Lama, and Danba Rijie, on behalf of the Panchen Erdeni, successively presented Hada scarves and Tibetan gifts such as deer antlers, Tibetan incense, pulu fabric, and wool rugs.

### 人民政协全国委员会民族事务组  邀请西藏致敬团团长报告访问全国观感

1953-02-26
第1版()
专栏：

    人民政协全国委员会民族事务组
    邀请西藏致敬团团长报告访问全国观感
    据新华社讯：中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会民族事务组，在二十三日邀请西藏致敬团团长柳霞·土登塔巴报告西藏和平解放后的建设情况和该团访问全国各地的观感。
    柳霞·土登塔巴报告访问内蒙古自治区的观感时指出：内蒙古自治区自从实行了区域自治后，民族团结更加巩固，经济、文化日益发展，宗教信仰得到保护，人民过着愉快的生活。他说：“内蒙古人民今天的幸福道路，就是西藏人民要走的道路。”
    柳霞·土登塔巴以极兴奋的心情报告了他们在东北所看到的祖国工业建设和发展的情形；报告了参观天津、上海等各大城市工商业的繁荣情形。
    柳霞·土登塔巴报告访问西北的观感。他说：西北地区和西藏有着历史的亲密联系。我们亲眼看到保护宗教信仰政策在祖国各地都正确执行着。
    柳霞·土登塔巴最后表示：他们回到西藏后，要把祖国建设的辉煌成就传达给藏族人民，进一步团结西藏各族人民，在毛主席的领导下，为建设祖国的新西藏而努力。
    又讯：西藏致敬团团长柳霞·土登塔巴代表达赖喇嘛、副团长丹巴日杰代表班禅额尔德尼在二十四日向中央人民政府民族事务委员会李维汉主任委员献礼。
    柳霞·土登塔巴代表达赖喇嘛、丹巴日杰代表班禅额尔德尼先后献上哈达和西藏的鹿茸、藏香、氆氇、绒褥等礼品。
