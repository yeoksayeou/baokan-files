Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station to Hold Spring Festival Broadcast Programs
Author:
Date: 1953-02-13
Page: 1

The Central People's Broadcasting Station will hold Spring Festival broadcast programs from the evening of February 13th to February 16th. It has invited Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea, Chen Yi, Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Central People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Li Siguang, Minister of the Central People's Government's Ministry of Geology, Lü Zhengcao, Deputy Minister of the Central People's Government's Ministry of Railways, and Cheng Zihua, Acting Director of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives, to speak to the Chinese People's Volunteers, the border troops of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, geological exploration personnel across the country, railway survey and design staff across the country, and cooperative cadres across the country, respectively. At the same time, famous national opera actors Mei Lanfang, Cheng Yanqiu, Zhou Xinfang, Gai Jiaotian, Chang Xiangyu, Yuan Xuefen, Hao Shouchun, Tan Xiaopei, Xiao Changhua, Jiang Miaoxiang, Ma Lianliang, Li Shaochun, Ye ShengLan, Li Hezeng, Gao Yuanjun and others will be invited to perform beautiful programs.

### 中央人民广播电台  举办春节广播节目

1953-02-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民广播电台
    举办春节广播节目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台将从二月十三日晚起到二月十六日止，举办春节广播节目，邀请中国人民抗美援朝总会主席郭沫若，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部文化部部长陈沂，中央人民政府地质部部长李四光，中央人民政府铁道部副部长吕正操，中华全国合作社联合总社代理主任程子华等分别向中国人民志愿军、中国人民解放军边防部队、全国地质勘探人员、全国铁路勘测设计员工、全国合作社干部讲话；同时邀请全国著名戏曲演员梅兰芳、程砚秋、周信芳、盖叫天、常香玉、袁雪芬、郝寿臣、谭小培、萧长华、姜妙香、马连良、李少春、叶盛兰、李和曾、高元钧等演唱优美节目。
