Title: Railway Minister Teng Daiyuan Hosts Banquet for Korean Railway Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1953-02-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 6th: Railway Minister Teng Daiyuan of the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government held a banquet at 7:30 PM on the 6th to welcome the entire delegation of the Korean Democratic People's Republic Railway Art Troupe to China, led by Lee Chung-koo, Vice-Minister of the Ministry of Railways of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

Attending the banquet were Zhao Jianmin and Shi Zhiren, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government, Liang Yongfu, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the China Railway Trade Union, Chen Chunsen, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the Political Department, and other responsible persons.

Invited to accompany were Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for Resistance Against America and Aid Korea, Zhao Feng, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, He Ying, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, among others. Kwon O-jik, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, and all diplomats from the embassy were also invited to accompany.

In their welcoming speeches, Minister Teng Daiyuan of the Ministry of Railways, Liang Yongfu, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the China Railway Trade Union, Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the Committee for Resistance Against America and Aid Korea, and Zhao Feng, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Culture, all unanimously pointed out that the performance of the Korean Democratic People's Republic Railway Art Troupe in China this time will further enhance the unbreakable fighting friendship between the Chinese and Korean people. After speeches by Head of Delegation Lee Chung-koo and Kwon O-jik, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, famous Korean singers Kim Shin-ryun, Lee Bo-won, and Baek Rak-cheon sang famous Chinese and Korean songs such as "Erlang Mountain," an excerpt from "The White Haired Girl," "People All Over the World Share One Heart," and "Arirang."

The banquet was filled with an atmosphere of cordiality and friendship, with guests frequently raising their glasses to toast the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, the great leader of the Chinese people, General Kim Il-sung, the respected leader of the Korean people, and Chairman Stalin, the great leader and teacher of the people of the world!

The banquet ended at ten o'clock.

### 铁道部滕代远部长欢宴朝鲜铁道艺术团

1953-02-07
第1版()
专栏：

    铁道部滕代远部长欢宴朝鲜铁道艺术团
    【新华社六日讯】中央人民政府铁道部部长滕代远于六日下午七时半举行宴会，欢迎以朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁铁道省副相李青求为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访华铁道艺术团全体团员。
    出席宴会的有中央人民政府铁道部副部长赵健民、石志仁，中国铁路工会全国委员会副主席梁永福、政治部宣传部副部长陈春森等负责人。
    应邀作陪的有中国人民抗美援朝总会秘书长刘贯一，中央人民政府文化部办公厅副主任赵沨、外交部亚洲司副司长何英及北京市副市长吴晗等多人。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使权五稷及大使馆全体外交官也应邀作陪。
    铁道部滕代远部长、中国铁路工会全国委员会副主席梁永福、抗美援朝总会秘书长刘贯一、文化部办公厅副主任赵沨等致欢迎词中，均一致指出此次朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访华铁道艺术团在我国演出，将进一步增强中、朝人民间的牢不可破的战斗的友谊。李青求团长及朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使权五稷致答词后，由朝鲜名歌唱家金信莲、李宝源、白乐天唱“二郎山”“白毛女”插曲、“全世界人民心一条”“阿里浪”等中朝著名歌曲。
    宴会洋溢着亲切友好的空气，宾主频频举杯祝贺中国人民伟大的领袖毛泽东主席，朝鲜人民敬爱的领袖金日成将军和全世界人民的伟大的领袖和导师斯大林主席的健康！
    宴会至十时始毕。
