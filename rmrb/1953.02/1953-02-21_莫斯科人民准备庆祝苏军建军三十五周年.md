Title: Muscovites Prepare to Celebrate the 35th Anniversary of the Founding of the Soviet Army
Author:
Date: 1953-02-21
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 20th: According to TASS Moscow, Muscovites are preparing to celebrate the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army (February 23rd). Clubs, cultural palaces, and libraries are actively preparing to host concerts, lectures, and report meetings. A grand book exhibition to celebrate this anniversary has opened at the Lenin Library. The exhibition features works by Lenin and Stalin, as well as books and pamphlets about outstanding Soviet generals and heroes of the Great Patriotic War. Some books are specifically dedicated to explaining how the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Lenin, and Stalin organized the Soviet Army and Navy. To celebrate this significant holiday, Moscow's theaters will stage plays about the history of the Soviet Army. The Moscow Theater of Drama and Comedy will stage Le. Rudoy's play "Eternal Glory," which depicts the exploits of the Far Eastern guerrillas in their fight against American and Japanese invaders during the Civil War. The Moscow Soviet Theater will stage Bill-Belotserkovsky's play "Storm," which depicts the heroism and perseverance of the Red Guards and sailors during the Civil War. In addition, Moscow's theaters will also stage plays about the Great Patriotic War and the Soviet Army's mission of liberation. Muscovites are extremely interested in films that depict the great feats of Soviet soldiers during the Civil War and the Great Patriotic War. On the 23rd, approximately twenty of the best Soviet films will be shown in cinemas throughout the capital, including "The Fall of Berlin," "The Battle of Stalingrad," "Unforgettable 1919," "Chapayev," "The Defence of Tsaritsyn," and "Meeting on the Elbe." Moscow Radio has also arranged special broadcasts for Soviet Army Day. Soviet musicians, writers, and poets will broadcast their latest works on the radio.

### 莫斯科人民准备庆祝苏军建军三十五周年

1953-02-21
第4版()
专栏：

    莫斯科人民准备庆祝苏军建军三十五周年
    【新华社二十日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：莫斯科人民正在准备庆祝苏军建军三十五周年纪念日（二月二十三日）。各俱乐部、文化宫和图书馆正在纷纷准备举办音乐会、讲演和报告会。为庆祝这个纪念日而举办的一个盛大的书籍展览会，已在列宁图书馆开幕。展览会上陈列的书籍有列宁和斯大林的著作，以及关于卓越的苏军将领和伟大卫国战争中英雄们的书籍和小册子。还有一些书籍专门说明苏联共产党、列宁和斯大林组织苏联陆军和海军的经过。为了庆祝这个重大的节日，莫斯科各剧院将上演关于苏军战史的剧本。莫斯科戏剧和喜剧院将上演勒·鲁多伊的剧本“永不磨灭的光辉”，这个剧本描写内战时期远东游击队和美日侵略者作战的功绩。莫斯科苏维埃剧院将上演比尔—别洛泽尔科夫斯基的剧本“暴风雨”，这个剧本描写赤卫队和水兵在内战期间的英勇气概和坚韧不拔的精神。此外，莫斯科的剧院还将上演关于卫国战争和苏军解放使命的剧本。莫斯科人对于表现苏军战士在内战和伟大卫国战争期间伟大功勋的影片极感兴趣。在二十三日那一天，约有二十部最好的苏联影片将在首都各电影院放映，其中包括攻克柏林、斯大林格勒大会战、难忘的一九一九年、夏伯阳、保卫察里津、易北河会师等。莫斯科电台也为苏军建军节安排了特别广播节目。苏联音乐家、作家和诗人将在电台上广播他们最新的作品。
