Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Issues Notice Commemorating the 30th Anniversary of the "February 7th" Incident

Author:

Date: 1953-02-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 1st—The All-China Federation of Trade Unions has issued a notice regarding the commemoration of the 30th anniversary of the "February 7th" Incident. The notice stipulates: Trade union organizations at all levels should conduct extensive publicity among workers and staff members before and after "February 7th." In addition to publicizing the course and significance of the "February 7th" Great Strike, they should also publicize the construction work of the motherland over the past three years, the great achievements of the Resist America, Aid Korea campaign and the victories on the Korean front, as well as the content and significance of the three major tasks of the state in 1953. On "February 7th," all regions should hold commemorative meetings in various forms. Cultural Palaces and clubs in factories and mines can organize lecture meetings. Railway trade union organizations in Jiang'an, Zhengzhou, Changxindian, and other places should take appropriate forms to solemnly commemorate the occasion. Around the Spring Festival, trade union organizations at all levels should call on and organize workers and staff members to support the People's Army and provide preferential treatment to the families of martyrs, families of servicemen, disabled revolutionary soldiers, and families of employees going to Korea.

### 中华全国总工会发出通知  纪念“二七”三十周年

1953-02-02
第1版()
专栏：

    中华全国总工会发出通知
    纪念“二七”三十周年
    【新华社一日讯】中华全国总工会发出关于纪念“二七”三十周年的通知。通知规定：各级工会组织在“二七”前后应在工人、职员群众中进行广泛的宣传，宣传内容除“二七”大罢工的经过和意义外，还应宣传三年多来祖国的建设工作、抗美援朝的伟大成就和朝鲜前线的胜利以及一九五三年国家三大任务的内容和意义。在“二七”当天，各地应召开不同形式的纪念会。各厂、矿文化宫和俱乐部可组织讲演会。江岸、郑州、长辛店等地铁路工会组织，应采取适当形式，郑重纪念。在春节前后，各级工会组织应号召并组织工人、职员群众进行拥护人民军队和优待烈士家属、军人家属及革命残废军人以及赴朝员工家属的工作或活动。
