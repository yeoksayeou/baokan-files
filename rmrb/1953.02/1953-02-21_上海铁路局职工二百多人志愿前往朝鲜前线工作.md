Title: More Than Two Hundred Shanghai Railway Bureau Employees Volunteer to Work on the Korean Front

Author:

Date: 1953-02-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, February 19th: Two hundred and sixty-seven employees of the Shanghai Railway Bureau volunteered to go to the Korean front to participate in transportation work and departed on February 8th. Before their departure, the Shanghai Railway Bureau held a farewell meeting. Cai Jinhai, a veteran worker who participated in the "February 7th" revolutionary struggle, encouraged the comrades participating in the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea to carry forward the heroic fighting spirit of the revolutionary martyrs to defend the motherland. The employees' family members all encouraged their husbands and sons to devote all their energy to doing a good job in transportation work on the Korean front. At the farewell meeting, the employees who volunteered to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea pledged to overcome all difficulties and resolutely complete the transportation tasks.

### 上海铁路局职工二百多人志愿前往朝鲜前线工作

1953-02-21
第1版()
专栏：

    上海铁路局职工二百多人志愿前往朝鲜前线工作
     【新华社上海十九日电】上海铁路局职工二百六十七人志愿前往朝鲜前线参加运输工作，已在二月八日出发。行前上海铁路局曾举行欢送大会。参加“二七”革命斗争的老工人蔡金海鼓励参加抗美援朝的同志们发扬革命先烈英勇斗争的精神，保卫祖国。职工家属都鼓励自己的丈夫、儿子以全部精力来做好朝鲜前线的运输工作。在欢送大会上，志愿抗美援朝的职工们，保证克服一切困难，坚决完成运输任务。
