    Our Great Motherland
    In order to meet the needs of basic construction, the Beijing Repair Plant of the Central Ministry of Water Resources Engineering Bureau trial-produced the Heping (Peace) drilling machine, and successfully tested it in the Guanting Reservoir and the Yellow River water conservancy project. These engineers and technicians are checking the specifications of the newly produced Heping drilling machine.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-02-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中央水利部工程总局北京修配厂为了适应基本建设的需要，试制和平钻探机，并在官厅水库和黄河水利工程中试用成功。这几位工程师和技术人员正在检查新出厂的和平钻探机的规格。
