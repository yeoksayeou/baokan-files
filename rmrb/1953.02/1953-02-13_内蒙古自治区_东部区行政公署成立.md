Title: Establishment of the Eastern District Administrative Office of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region
Author:
Date: 1953-02-13
Page: 1

    Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region
    Establishment of the Eastern District Administrative Office
    [Xinhua News Agency, Gui Sui, 12th] To strengthen leadership in the eastern region of Inner Mongolia and to meet the needs of large-scale national construction, the Eastern District Administrative Office of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, with the approval of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government, was officially established in Ulanhot on February 1st and has begun operations.
    The Eastern District Administrative Office of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region is a local national democratic united government under the direct leadership of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. Its jurisdiction includes four leagues: Hulunbuir, Hinggan, Jirem, and Ju Ud, totaling thirty banners, counties, and cities, with a population of 1.26 million people from various ethnic groups including Mongol, Han, Daur, Solon, Korean, Hui, and Oroqen. The area covers approximately 410,000 square kilometers.

### 内蒙古自治区  东部区行政公署成立

1953-02-13
第1版()
专栏：

    内蒙古自治区
    东部区行政公署成立
    【新华社归绥十二日电】为加强内蒙古东部地区的领导，以适应国家大规模建设的需要，内蒙古自治区东部区行政公署经中央人民政府政务院批准，已于二月一日在乌兰浩特市正式成立，并开始办公。
    按内蒙古自治区东部行政公署，是在内蒙古自治区人民政府直接领导下的一级地方民族民主联合政府。所辖范围包括：呼纳、兴安、哲里木、昭乌达等四个盟，计三十个旗、县、市，共有蒙、汉、达呼尔、索伦、朝鲜、回、鄂伦春等各民族人口一百二十六万。面积约四十一万多平方公里。
