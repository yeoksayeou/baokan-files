Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Sends Telegram to Stalin, Foreign Minister Zhou to Vyshinsky, Commander-in-Chief Zhu to Vasilevsky Congratulating on the 35th Anniversary of the Founding of the Soviet Army
Author: 
Date: 1953-02-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Report: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government, Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhou Enlai, and Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army have sent telegrams to Chairman Stalin of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Socialist Republics, Minister of Foreign Affairs Vyshinsky, and Marshal Vasilevsky of the Ministry of Military Affairs, respectively, congratulating them on the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Armed Forces. The telegrams are as follows:

    Chairman Mao Zedong's Congratulatory Telegram to Chairman Stalin

    To Comrade Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Socialist Republics:

    On the occasion of the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Armed Forces of the Soviet Socialist Republics, please accept my heartfelt congratulations, Comrade Chairman. I wish the powerful Soviet Armed Forces even greater achievements in the cause of safeguarding world peace and security.

    Mao Zedong
    February 21, 1953, Beijing

    Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's Congratulatory Telegram to Foreign Minister Vyshinsky

    To Comrade Vyshinsky, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Socialist Republics:

    On the occasion of the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Armed Forces of the Soviet Socialist Republics, I extend my warm congratulations to you, Comrade Minister.

    Zhou Enlai
    February 21, 1953, Beijing

    Commander-in-Chief Zhu De's Congratulatory Telegram to Marshal Vasilevsky

    To Marshal Vasilevsky, Minister of Military Affairs of the Soviet Socialist Republics:

    On the commemorative festival of the 35th anniversary of the founding of the great Soviet Army, I wholeheartedly express my congratulations to the incomparably strong Soviet Army, the pillar of world peace, and on behalf of all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, I extend my infinite and highest respect to you and all comrades of the Soviet Army.

    The Chinese People's Liberation Army is systematically studying Soviet military science and all advanced military experience, striving to build itself into a completely modernized army, to safeguard the security of its motherland, and to safeguard world peace together with the Soviet Union and the people of the world. Long live the increasingly consolidated and developed friendly cooperation between the two major allied countries of China and the Soviet Union, the greatest guarantee of world peace!

    Zhu De, Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
    February 21, 1953, Beijing

### 祝贺苏联建军三十五周年毛泽东主席致电斯大林周外长朱总司令电维辛斯基华西列夫斯基

1953-02-23
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺苏联建军三十五周年毛泽东主席致电斯大林周外长朱总司令电维辛斯基华西列夫斯基
    据新华社讯：我中央人民政府毛泽东主席、外交部周恩来部长和中国人民解放军朱德总司令，分别致电苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议主席斯大林、外交部部长维辛斯基和军事部部长华西列夫斯基元帅，祝贺苏联武装部队建军三十五周年。电文如下：
    毛泽东主席致斯大林主席贺电
    苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议主席
    斯大林同志：
    值此苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟武装部队建军三十五周年之际，主席同志，请接受我衷心的祝贺。祝强大的苏联武装部队在维护世界和平与安全的事业中获得更进一步的成就。
    毛泽东
    一九五三年二月二十一日 　北京
    周恩来外长致维辛斯基外长贺电
    苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟外交部部长
    维辛斯基同志：
    值此苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟武装部队建军三十五周年之际，谨向您，部长同志，致以热烈的祝贺。
    周恩来
    一九五三年二月二十一日　北京
    朱德总司令致华西列夫斯基元帅贺电
    苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟军事部部长
    华西列夫斯基元帅：
    在伟大的苏联军队建军三十五周年纪念节，我衷心地为世界和平支柱——坚强无比的苏联军队表示庆贺，谨代表中国人民解放军全体指挥员、战斗员，向您和苏联军队的全体同志致以无限崇高的敬意。
    中国人民解放军正在有系统地学习苏联的军事科学和一切先进军事经验，努力建设自己成为完全现代化的军队，以维护自己祖国的安全，并与苏联和全世界人民一道维护世界的和平。作为世界和平最大保障的中苏两大盟国日益巩固与发展的友好合作万岁！
    中国人民解放军总司令　朱德
    一九五三年二月二十一日　北京
