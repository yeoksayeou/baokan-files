Title: US Embassy in Burma Supports Chiang Kai-shek Bandits Fleeing to Burma; Burmese Democratic Parties Demand Closure of US Embassy
Author:
Date: 1953-02-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, 11th. TASS reported in the "Workers' Daily": Burmese democratic parties are demanding the closure of the US embassy in Rangoon because it supports the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang in Burma. The Burmese Workers' and Peasants' Party issued a statement saying that the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang in Burma "has taken aggressive actions within Burmese territory, clearly undermining Burmese sovereignty."

### 美国驻缅大使馆支持逃缅蒋匪  缅民主党派要求封闭美使馆

1953-02-19
第4版()
专栏：

    美国驻缅大使馆支持逃缅蒋匪
    缅民主党派要求封闭美使馆
    【新华社十三日讯】　塔斯社伦敦十一日讯：据“工人日报”报道：缅甸各民主党派要求封闭仰光的美国大使馆，因为它支持在缅甸的蒋介石匪帮。缅甸工农党发表声明说：在缅甸的蒋介石匪帮“在缅甸领土内采取侵略行动，显然破坏了缅甸的主权”。
