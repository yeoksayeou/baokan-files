    Our Great Motherland
    In order to supply a large quantity of machinery and tools for construction projects, the Beijing Jianhua Iron Factory, directly under the Central Construction Engineering Company, has fully started operations. This is the scene of workers rushing to install machines before the start of work.

    我们伟大的祖国
