Title: Xinjiang Pastoral Area Visiting Group Returns to Xi'an
Author:
Date: 1953-02-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 19th. The Xinjiang Province Pastoral Area Representative Visiting Group, consisting of sixty-three people led by the head of the group, Batihan (Kazak), and the deputy head of the group, Su Shiqing (Han), returned to Xi'an from Hankou on February 18th. Those who welcomed them at the station included Ma Fuchen (Hui), Vice Chairman of the Qinghai Provincial People's Government, Xue Xiangchen and Mu Huaxi (Kazak), members of the Northwest Administrative Committee's Ethnic Affairs Commission, and Luo Sang, Director of the Panchen Lama's Office in Xi'an, totaling more than sixty people.
    The Xinjiang Province Pastoral Area Representative Visiting Group departed from Xi'an on October 17th last year to visit various places. Over the past four months, they have visited the eastern pastoral areas of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and the great constructions and historical sites in major cities in Northeast, North, East, and Central South China, and traveled through the magnificent mountains and rivers of the motherland. When the visiting group was in the capital, they presented a flag and gifts to Chairman Mao to pay their respects and were entertained at banquets by the central leaders.
    During this visit to the eastern pastoral areas of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, the visiting group learned a lot of advanced experience in developing animal husbandry production. They unanimously stated that after returning to Xinjiang, they would better develop Xinjiang's animal husbandry and rapidly build a new Xinjiang for the motherland.

### 新疆牧区参观团回西安

1953-02-20
第1版()
专栏：

    新疆牧区参观团回西安
    【新华社西安十九日电】新疆省牧区代表参观团一行六十三人，在团长巴提汉（哈萨克族）、副团长苏史青（汉族）率领下，于二月十八日由汉口返抵西安。到车站欢迎的有青海省人民政府副主席马辅臣（回族）、西北行政委员会民族事务委员会委员薛向晨、穆华希（哈萨克族）及班禅驻西安办事处处长罗桑等共六十多人。
    新疆省牧区代表参观团在去年十月十七日由西安出发，到各地参观。四个月来，他们参观了内蒙古自治区东部牧区和东北、华北、华东、中南各地大城市的伟大建设及名胜古迹，游历了祖国的壮丽山河。参观团在首都时，曾向毛主席献旗献礼致敬，并受到中央各首长的设宴招待。
    参观团这次在内蒙古自治区东部牧区参观时，学习了很多发展畜牧业生产的先进经验，他们一致表示：回到新疆后要更好地发展新疆的畜牧事业，迅速建设祖国的新新疆。
