Title: Korean Railway Art Troupe Leaves Beijing for Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1953-02-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 19th:

    The visiting Korean Democratic People's Republic Railway Art Troupe, after concluding its performance activities in the capital, left Beijing for Tianjin on the morning of the 19th. More than 300 people, including responsible cadres from the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government and the China Railway Trade Union, went to the station to see them off.
    During its stay in Beijing, the Korean Railway Art Troupe held a total of eleven performances, offering comfort to railway workers who had participated in the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea and their families, and entertaining workers from the Changxindian Railway Factory and the Beijing Railway Administration, as well as people from the Beijing literary and art circles and cadres from government organizations. The performances of the art troupe were warmly welcomed by the audience.
    During its stay in Beijing, the art troupe also visited famous historical sites in the capital and held discussions with people from the capital's literary and art circles.

### 朝鲜铁道艺术团离京去天津

1953-02-20
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜铁道艺术团离京去天津
    【新华社十九日讯】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访华铁道艺术团结束在首都的演出活动后，已在十九日上午离京去天津。前往车站欢送的有中央人民政府铁道部和中国铁路工会的负责干部等共三百多人。
    朝鲜铁道艺术团在京期间共举行了十一次演出，慰问曾赴朝鲜参加抗美援朝斗争的铁路职工和他们的家属，招待长辛店铁路工厂和北京铁路管理局职工、北京文艺界人士和机关干部。艺术团的表演受到了观众的热烈欢迎。
    艺术团在京期间还游览了首都名胜古迹，并和首都文艺界人士举行了座谈。
