Title: My Red Cross Delegation Continues to Hold Second and Third Formal Meetings with Japanese Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1953-02-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, dated 24th: Regarding the issue of assisting Japanese nationals who wish to return to their home country, the delegation of the Chinese Red Cross and the delegation composed of the Japanese Red Cross, the Japanese Peace Liaison Committee, and the Japan-China Friendship Association held their second and third formal meetings on the 20th and 23rd respectively. The meetings proceeded smoothly.

### 我红十字会代表团与日本代表团继续举行第二、三次正式会议

1953-02-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我红十字会代表团与日本代表团继续举行第二、三次正式会议
    【新华社二十四日讯】关于协助愿意回国的日侨归国问题，中国红十字会代表团与日本红十字会，日本和平联络委员会和日中友好协会三团体组成的代表团，在二十日和二十三日继续举行第二次和第三次正式会议，会议进行顺利。
