Title: Japanese Delegation Arrives in Beijing to Discuss the Repatriation of Japanese Nationals

Author:

Date: 1953-02-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 31st—A Japanese delegation composed of representatives from the Japanese Red Cross Society, the Japan-China Friendship Association, and the Japanese Peace Liaison Committee arrived in Beijing by train from Guangzhou at 11:07 PM on January 31st. The delegation is here to discuss specific issues, including ships for assisting the repatriation of Japanese nationals, with the Chinese Red Cross Society. The delegation includes seven representatives: Tadatsugu Shimazu (head of the delegation) and Tadao Kudo from the Japanese Red Cross Society; Kanzo Uchiyama and Toshio Kashima from the Japan-China Friendship Association; Yoshitaro Hirano, Masaharu Hatanaka, and Ms. Tomi Takara from the Japanese Peace Liaison Committee; and six staff members. Greeting them at the station were Wu Yunfu, Executive Director of the Chinese Red Cross Society; Lin Shixiao and Ni Feijun, Deputy Secretaries-General; Ji Feng, Director of the Liaison Department; Zhao Anbo, Advisor; and Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace. Also present to welcome them were Koichi Saionji and Kanemon Nakamura, Japanese representatives attending the World People's Peace Congress currently in Beijing, and Kazuo Suzuki, Permanent Director of the Japan-China Trade Promotion Association.

### 来华接洽日侨回国问题  日本代表团抵北京

1953-02-01
第1版()
专栏：

    来华接洽日侨回国问题
    日本代表团抵北京
    【新华社三十一日讯】由日本红十字会、日中友好协会、日本和平联络委员会三团体组成的前来和中国红十字会洽商协助日侨回国的来华船只及其他具体问题的日本代表团于一月三十一日晚十一时零七分乘火车由广州抵达北京。代表团包括日本红十字会岛津忠承（团长）、工滕忠夫，日中友好协会内山完造、加岛敏雄，日本和平联络委员会平野义太郎、畑中政春及高良富女士等七名代表和工作人员六人。到车站迎接的有中国红十字会总会常务理事伍云甫，副秘书长林士笑、倪斐君，联络部部长纪锋，顾问赵安博及中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一等人。前往欢迎的还有现在北京的出席世界人民和平大会的日本代表西园寺公一、中村翫右卫门和日中贸易促进会常任理事铃木一雄等三人。
