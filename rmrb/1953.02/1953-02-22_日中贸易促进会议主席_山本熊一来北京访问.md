Title: Chairman of the Japan-China Trade Promotion Council, Yamamoto Kumao, Visits Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-02-22
Page: 1

    Chairman of the Japan-China Trade Promotion Council
    Yamamoto Kumao Visits Beijing
    (Xinhua News Agency, February 21st) Yamamoto Kumao, Chairman of the Japan-China Trade Promotion Council, arrived in Beijing by train on the evening of February 21st for a visit. He was accompanied by his secretary, Masamoto Morioka.
    Greeting him at the station were: Ji Chaoding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and Xie Xiaonai, Director of the Liaison Department.
    Also greeting him at the station were: Kazuo Suzuki, Executive Director of the Japan-China Trade Promotion Association, currently in Beijing; and Koichi Saionji and Kan'emon Nakamura, Japanese representatives attending the World People's Peace Congress.
    Yoshitaro Hirano, Deputy Head of the Japanese delegation composed of representatives from the Japanese Red Cross Society, the Japanese Peace Liaison Committee, and the Japan-China Friendship Association, who are in Beijing to discuss the issue of assisting Japanese nationals to return to their country with the Chinese Red Cross Society, also greeted him at the station on behalf of Tadao Kudo, Masaharu Hatanaka, and Toshio Kashima.

### 日中贸易促进会议主席  山本熊一来北京访问

1953-02-22
第1版()
专栏：

    日中贸易促进会议主席
    山本熊一来北京访问
    【新华社二十一日讯】日本日中贸易促进会议主席山本熊一在二十一日晚乘火车抵达北京访问，同来的有山本熊一的秘书森冈政元。
    到车站迎接的有：中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎和联络部部长谢筱乃。
    到车站迎接的还有：现在北京的日本中日贸易促进会常务理事铃木一雄，出席世界人民和平大会的日本代表西园寺公一、中村翫右卫门。
    由日本红十字会、日本和平联络委员会和日中友好协会三团体组成的在北京和中国红十字会商谈协助日侨归国问题的日本代表团副团长平野义太郎，代表工藤忠夫、畑中政春、加岛敏雄等，也到车站迎接。
