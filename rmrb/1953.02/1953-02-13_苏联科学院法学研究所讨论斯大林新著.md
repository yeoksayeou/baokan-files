Title: Soviet Academy of Sciences Institute of Law Discusses Stalin's New Book
Author:
Date: 1953-02-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency Report: The Institute of Law of the Soviet Academy of Sciences held a scientific conference from the 4th to the 7th to discuss Stalin's new book "Economic Problems of Socialism in the USSR", Stalin's speech at the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and the various resolutions of the 19th Party Congress.

The conference was opened by Professor P. E. Orlovsky, Director of the Institute of Law, who presided and listened to and discussed the following reports: G. I. Fedkin's "The Role of the Soviet State and Jurisprudence in Utilizing the Basic Economic Laws of Socialism Discovered by Stalin", A. V. Karass's "The Issue of Socialist Ownership by the State in Stalin's Work 'Economic Problems of Socialism in the USSR'", I. V. Pavlov's "The Legal Form of Gradually Raising Collective Farm Ownership to the Level of Ownership by the Whole People", A. Ya. Bashersternik's "The Issue of the Right to Work in the Soviet Union in Light of Stalin's Instructions on Preparing the Basic Prerequisites for the Transition to Communism", and I. A. Korovin's "International Law Serving Peace".

Attendees listened attentively to the speech by Academician A. Ya. Vyshinsky, who spoke in detail about the historical significance of Stalin's work "Economic Problems of Socialism in the USSR" and Stalin's speech at the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union for the development of science, and especially for the development of jurisprudence.

### 苏联科学院法学研究所讨论斯大林新著

1953-02-13
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联科学院法学研究所讨论斯大林新著
    据新华社讯：苏联科学院法学研究所四日至七日举行科学会议，讨论斯大林的新著“苏联社会主义经济问题”、斯大林在苏联共产党第十九次代表大会上的演说和第十九次党代表大会的各项决议。
    会议由法学研究所所长普·耶·奥尔洛夫斯基教授主持开幕，并听取和讨论了下列报告：格·依·费德金的“苏维埃国家和法学在利用斯大林发现的社会主义基本经济法则中的作用”、阿·维·卡拉斯的“斯大林著作‘苏联社会主义经济问题’中的国家的社会主义所有权问题”、依·维·巴夫洛夫“把集体农庄所有制逐步提高到全民所有制水平的法律形式”、阿·耶·巴谢尔斯特尼克“根据斯大林关于准备过渡到共产主义的基本先决条件的指示来看苏联的劳动权问题”、依·阿·科罗文“为和平服务的国际法”。
    到会者注意地听取了科学院院士安·扬·维辛斯基的演说，他详细地谈到了斯大林的著作“苏联社会主义经济问题”和斯大林在苏联共产党第十九次代表大会上的演说，对于科学的发展、特别是对于法学的发展的历史意义。
