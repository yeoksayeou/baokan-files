Title: Beijing Organizes慰问团 to Visit Hospitals to Comfort Volunteer Army and PLA Recuperating Soldiers

Author:

Date: 1953-02-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Report: On the eve of the Spring Festival, the Beijing Municipal People's Government and the Beijing Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association jointly organized a 慰问团 of over 250 people to visit the wounded and sick soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Chinese People's Liberation Army recuperating in various hospitals in Beijing. Participants in this 慰问团 included Xue Zizheng, Secretary-General of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; the directors of the Beijing Municipal People's Government's Housing Administration Bureau, Labor Bureau, Education Bureau, and other bureaus; Qian Duansheng, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee; and Li Leguang, Secretary-General of the Beijing Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association. Representatives from the Chinese People's Resist America, Aid Korea General Association, various democratic parties in Beijing, and various people's organizations also participated.

The 慰问团 was divided into seven smaller groups to visit the hospitals separately. The visits from the 慰问团 greatly encouraged the recuperating soldiers. Whenever members of the 慰问团 arrived in a ward, they were warmly welcomed. The recuperating soldiers excitedly told the 慰问团 members how their health was improving day by day under the meticulous care of the people across the country. All the recuperating soldiers unanimously expressed their determination to recuperate well so that they could return to the front lines of Resist America, Aid Korea and defending the country as soon as possible.

### 北京市组织慰问团到各医院慰问志愿军和解放军休养员

1953-02-13
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市组织慰问团到各医院慰问志愿军和解放军休养员
    据新华社讯：春节前夕，北京市人民政府和北京市抗美援朝分会，联合组织了二百五十多人的慰问团，慰问在北京各医院休养的中国人民志愿军和中国人民解放军伤病员。参加这一慰问团的，有北京市人民政府秘书长薛子正，北京市人民政府房产管理局、劳动局、教育局等局局长；北京市各界人民代表会议协商委员会副主席钱端升；北京市抗美援朝分会秘书长李乐光等人。抗美援朝总会及北京市各民主党派、各人民团体也派代表参加。
    慰问团共分七个慰问团，分头到各医院去慰问。慰问团的慰问，对休养员有莫大的鼓舞。每当慰问团员到达一病房时，都受到热烈的欢迎，休养员们兴奋地告诉慰问团员们，他们的身体是怎样在全国人民无微不至的关怀下日益健康起来的。所有的休养员一致表示：要更好地安心休养，以便提早回到抗美援朝和保卫国防的前线上去。
