Title: Burmese Peace Delegates Leave Guangzhou for Home

Author:

Date: 1953-02-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, February 10th. After attending the World People's Peace Congress, Burmese delegate Tekin Chit Maung and others, who were invited by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace to visit China, left Guangzhou on the 10th to return home. Among those who went to the station to see them off were Zhang Zicun, vice chairman of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and others.
