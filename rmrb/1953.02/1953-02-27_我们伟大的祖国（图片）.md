Author:
Date: 1953-02-27

    Our Great Motherland
    Workers at the Lüda Railway Factory manufactured many locomotives suitable for use in forest areas in order to cooperate with the construction of the Northeast Forest Railway. This is a newly manufactured locomotive, which will soon be running in the vast forest area, transporting timber to meet the needs of national construction.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-02-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    旅大区铁路工厂的工人们，为了配合东北森林铁路的修筑，制造了许多适合于林区使用的机车。这是新制成的机车，它很快就将行驶在广阔的林区中，运输木材，供应国家建设的需要。
