Title: The Soviet Ministry of Military Affairs and the Ministry of Navy Held Separate Congresses to Celebrate the 35th Anniversary of the Founding of the Soviet Army and Navy
Author:
Date: 1953-02-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 22nd, Moscow dispatch: TASS, Moscow, February 22nd: The Soviet Ministry of Military Affairs, together with representatives of the Party organization, Soviets, and mass organizations of the Soviet capital, held a celebration meeting in Moscow on the 21st to celebrate the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy. The Central Soviet Army Theater was festively decorated for the occasion. On the rostrum, between red flags and flowers, hung portraits of Lenin and Stalin, the founders of the Soviet Armed Forces. Infantrymen, sailors, artillerymen, pilots, tankmen, and cavalrymen stood at attention under their flags, forming a guard of honor. Among those present were: junior officers, senior officers, generals, Stakhanovite workers from Moscow enterprises, Party and Soviet workers, and figures from the scientific and cultural circles. The celebration meeting was opened by Soviet Marshal V.D. Sokolovsky. The meeting elected the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, headed by Stalin, as the honorary presidium. The Minister of the Soviet Ministry of Military Affairs, Soviet Marshal A.M. Vasilevsky, gave a report on the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy. Those attending the celebration meeting passed a congratulatory telegram to Comrade Stalin with great enthusiasm.
    Xinhua News Agency, February 22nd, Moscow dispatch: TASS, Moscow, February 22nd: The Soviet Ministry of the Navy held a celebration meeting in Moscow on the 21st to celebrate the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy. In the Column Hall of the Trade Union Building, which had been festively decorated, naval junior officers, senior officers and generals, as well as representatives of the Party organization, Soviets and mass organizations of the Soviet capital, gathered together. The meeting was opened by the Minister of the Soviet Navy, Vice-Admiral N.G. Kuznetsov. The meeting elected the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, headed by Comrade Stalin, the great leader of the Soviet people and the working people of the world, and the brilliant helmsman, as the honorary presidium of the meeting. Vice-Admiral N.I. Vinogradov gave a report on the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy. When Vinogradov cheered for the heroic Soviet people and their armed forces, for the great Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet people's own Soviet government, and for the beloved leader and brilliant helmsman Comrade Stalin, a storm of prolonged applause resounded in the hall. In a surge of great patriotic enthusiasm, those attending the festive event passed a telegram of salute to Comrade Stalin.

### 苏联军事部和海军部分别举行大会庆祝苏联陆海军建军三十五周年

1953-02-23
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联军事部和海军部分别举行大会庆祝苏联陆海军建军三十五周年
    【新华社二十二日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十二日讯：苏联军事部二十一日会同苏联首都党组织、苏维埃和群众团体的代表，在莫斯科举行了苏联陆海军建军三十五周年庆祝大会。中央苏军剧院为这个节日盛装起来。在主席台上，在红旗和花丛间挂着苏联武装部队的缔造者列宁和斯大林的肖像。步兵、海员、炮兵、飞行员、坦克手和骑兵在军旗下肃立，作仪仗队。到会的人中有：尉官、校官、将军，莫斯科企业的斯塔哈诺夫式工作者，党和苏维埃工作者，科学和文化界人士。庆祝大会由苏联元帅维·德·索科洛夫斯基主持开幕。大会选举以斯大林为首的苏联共产党中央委员会主席团为名誉主席团。苏联军事部部长、苏联元帅阿·麦·华西列夫斯基作了关于苏联陆海军建军三十五周年的报告。参加庆祝大会的人以极大的热情通过了致斯大林同志的贺电。
    【新华社二十二日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十二日讯：苏联海军部二十一日在莫斯科举行了苏联陆海军建军三十五周年庆祝大会。在披上了节日盛装的工会大厦圆柱大厅里，海军的尉官、校官和将军们以及苏联首都的党组织、苏维埃和群众团体的代表们齐集一堂。大会由苏联海军部长、海军中将纳·格·库兹湼佐夫主持开幕。大会选举以苏联人民和全世界劳动人民的伟大领袖、天才的舵手斯大林同志为首的苏联共产党中央委员会主席团为大会的名誉主席团。海军中将纳·伊·维诺格拉多夫作了关于苏联陆海军建军三十五周年的报告。当维诺格拉多夫为英勇的苏联人民和他们的武装部队、为伟大的苏联共产党和苏联人民自己的苏维埃政府、为敬爱的领袖和天才的舵手斯大林同志而欢呼的时候，大厅里响起了暴风雨般长时间的掌声。在高涨的伟大爱国热情中，参加节日盛会的人通过了向斯大林同志致敬的电报。
