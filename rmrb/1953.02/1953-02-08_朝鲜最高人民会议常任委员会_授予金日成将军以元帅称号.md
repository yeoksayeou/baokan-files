Title: The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea Confers the Title of Marshal on General Kim Il-sung
Author:
Date: 1953-02-08
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 7th: The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued a decree on February 7th, conferring the title of Marshal on Kim Il-sung, Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army, and the title of Vice-Marshal on Choe Yong-gon, Minister of National Defense. The decree stated that due to the outstanding achievements of Kim Il-sung, Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army, in the anti-Japanese national liberation struggle and in the cause of creating, strengthening, and developing the Korean People's Army, and due to his brilliant achievements in the just Fatherland Liberation War against the armed aggression of US and British imperialism, the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea specially confers the title of Marshal of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea on the glorious occasion of the fifth anniversary of the founding of the Korean People's Army. Due to the meritorious service of Choe Yong-gon, Minister of National Defense of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, in strengthening the Korean People's Army and in the Fatherland Liberation War, the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea specially confers the title of Vice-Marshal of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

### 朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会  授予金日成将军以元帅称号

1953-02-08
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会
    授予金日成将军以元帅称号
    【新华社平壤七日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会在二月七日发布政令，授予朝鲜人民军最高司令官金日成以元帅称号，授予民族保卫相崔庸健以次帅称号。政令称，由于朝鲜人民军最高司令官金日成在抗日民族解放斗争中和创建、加强与发展朝鲜人民军的事业中，建立了特出的功勋，并在反对美、英帝国主义武装侵略者的正义的祖国解放战争中，以卓越的指挥建立了光辉的功勋，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会特在光荣的朝鲜人民军建军五周年之际，授予朝鲜民主主义人民共和国元帅称号。由于朝鲜民主主义人民共和国民族保卫相崔庸健在加强朝鲜人民军的事业中和祖国解放战争中建立了功勋，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会特授予朝鲜民主主义人民共和国次帅称号。
