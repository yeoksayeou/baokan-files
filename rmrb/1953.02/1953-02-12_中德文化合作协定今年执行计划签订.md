Title: This Year's Implementation Plan for the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement Signed
Author:
Date: 1953-02-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 11th: The governments of the People's Republic of China and the German Democratic Republic signed the 1953 implementation plan for the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement in Berlin on February 9th. Fan Changjiang, Deputy Secretary-General of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the State Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, signed on behalf of the Chinese side, and Wang Daier, Director of the Education, Science, and Art Committee of the Government of the German Democratic Republic, signed on behalf of the German side.

Both Fan Changjiang and Wang Daier delivered speeches at the signing ceremony, pointing out that the cultural exchange between the People's Republic of China and the German Democratic Republic has been specified and concretized due to the signing of the implementation plan, becoming a defined component of the lives of the two peoples. This is of great significance for enhancing the friendly relations between the two countries, and at the same time, it plays an important role in further uniting the two peoples and strengthening the struggle to defend world peace.

### 中德文化合作协定今年执行计划签订

1953-02-12
第1版()
专栏：

    中德文化合作协定今年执行计划签订
    【新华社十一日讯】中华人民共和国政府与德意志民主共和国政府已于二月九日在柏林签订中德文化合作协定一九五三年执行计划。代表中国方面签字的为中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会副秘书长范长江，代表德国方面签字的为德意志民主共和国政府教育、科学与艺术委员会主任汪戴尔。
    范长江及汪戴尔均曾在签字典礼上致词，致词中指出：中华人民共和国与德意志民主共和国之间的文化交流已由于签订执行计划而具体化，成为两国人民生活中确定的组成部分。这对于增进两国间的友好关系有重要意义，同时对于进一步团结两国人民加强保卫世界和平的斗争也有重要作用。
