Our great motherland.
Our country's scientific research work is increasingly closely serving production and construction. This is the Timber Rational Utilization Group of the Civil Engineering and Architectural Research Office of the Changchun Comprehensive Research Institute of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. The staff here are conducting tests on the properties of various building timbers.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-02-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国科学研究工作正日益紧密地为生产建设服务。这是中国科学院长春综合研究所土木建筑研究室的木材合理利用组。这里的工作人员正对各种建筑木材的性能进行试验。
