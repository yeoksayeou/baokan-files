Title: Mao Zedong's Speech Published in Newspapers of Poland, India, Pakistan, and Ceylon
Author:
Date: 1953-02-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 11th—According to the Polish Press Agency, Warsaw: Polish newspapers on the 10th prominently published on their front pages the resolutions adopted by the Fourth Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and the important speech delivered by Chairman Mao Zedong at the closing meeting.
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, February 11th: Indian newspapers on the 9th published Chairman Mao Zedong's speech at the closing meeting of the Fourth Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference under full-page banner headlines on the front page. Newspapers in Pakistan and Ceylon also published Chairman Mao's speech in the most important positions on the 9th.

### 波兰、印度、巴基斯坦、锡兰报纸刊载毛主席讲话

1953-02-12
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰、印度、巴基斯坦、锡兰报纸刊载毛主席讲话
    【新华社十一日讯】据波兰通讯社华沙讯：波兰各报十日以首页显著地位刊载了中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第四次会议所通过的各项决议，和毛泽东主席在闭幕会议上发表的重要讲话。
    【新华社新德里十一日电】印度各地报纸九日都在第一版全页横栏标题下刊载毛泽东主席在中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第四次会议闭幕会上的讲话。巴基斯坦和锡兰的报纸九日也在最重要的位置发表了毛主席的讲话。
