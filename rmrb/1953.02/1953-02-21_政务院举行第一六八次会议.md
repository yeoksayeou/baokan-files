Title: Government Administration Council Holds 168th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-02-21
Page: 1

    The Government Administration Council Holds 168th Meeting

    [Xinhua News Agency, 20th] The 168th session of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 20th. The meeting listened to and approved the report by Hu Yuzhi, Director of the Central People's Government Publishing Administration, on the proceedings of the Second National Publishing Administrative Conference convened by the Publishing Administration. The meeting also adopted several personnel appointments and removals and submitted personnel appointments and removals to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第一六八次会议

1953-02-21
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一六八次会议
    【新华社二十日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百六十八次政务会议在二十日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府出版总署胡愈之署长所作的关于出版总署召开第二届全国出版行政会议经过的报告。会议并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
