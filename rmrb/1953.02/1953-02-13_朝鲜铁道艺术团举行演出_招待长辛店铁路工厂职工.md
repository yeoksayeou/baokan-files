Title: Korean Railway Art Troupe Performs for Workers of Changxindian Railway Factory
Author:
Date: 1953-02-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. The Korean Democratic People's Republic's visiting Railway Art Troupe continued its performances on the evening of the 12th, entertaining the workers of the Changxindian Railway Factory. Before the start of the performance, Li Qingqiu, head of the Korean Railway Art Troupe, presented a dedication and a flag to the Changxindian Railway Factory. Gao Xiaoting, chairman of the Changxindian Locomotive and Rolling Stock Repair Factory Trade Union, delivered a reply speech on behalf of all the workers of the Changxindian Railway Factory and presented a gift to the Korean Railway Art Troupe.

At the performance, the audience responded with enthusiastic applause for every program performed by the Korean Railway Art Troupe.

### 朝鲜铁道艺术团举行演出  招待长辛店铁路工厂职工

1953-02-13
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜铁道艺术团举行演出  招待长辛店铁路工厂职工
    【新华社十二日讯】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访华铁道艺术团在十二日晚继续举行演出，招待长辛店铁路工厂职工。演出开始前，朝鲜铁道艺术团团长李青求向长辛店铁路工厂献词、献旗。长辛店机车车辆修理工厂工会主席高晓亭代表长辛店铁路工厂全体职工致答词并向朝鲜铁道艺术团献礼。
    演出会上，观众对朝鲜铁道艺术团表演的每一个节目均报以热烈的鼓掌。
