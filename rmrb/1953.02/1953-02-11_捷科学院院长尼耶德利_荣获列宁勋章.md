Title: Czech Academy of Sciences President Nejedlý Awarded the Order of Lenin
Author:
Date: 1953-02-11
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th: TASS, Moscow: February 10th is the 75th birthday of Academician Zdeněk Nejedlý, President of the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences and Chairman of the Czechoslovak-Soviet Friendship Association. The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union announced on the 9th that he was awarded the Order of Lenin in recognition of his outstanding contributions to strengthening Czechoslovak-Soviet friendship and cultural cooperation.

### 捷科学院院长尼耶德利  荣获列宁勋章

1953-02-11
第4版()
专栏：

    捷科学院院长尼耶德利
    荣获列宁勋章
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：二月十日是捷克斯洛伐克科学院院长、捷苏友好协会主席兹德尼克·尼耶德利院士的七十五岁寿辰，苏联最高苏维埃主席团特于九日宣布授给他列宁勋章，褒奖他在加强捷苏友好和文化合作方面的卓越贡献。
