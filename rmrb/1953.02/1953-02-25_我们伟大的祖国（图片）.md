Our Great Motherland
The single-row towing method has been gradually promoted in China's inland river transportation. It will become the driving force for reforming China's inland river transportation. This is the situation of the "Badong Wheel" of the Hubei Provincial Inland River Navigation Administration carrying out the single-row towing method.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-02-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    一列式拖运法已在我国内河航运中逐渐推广起来。它将成为改革我国内河航运的动力。这是湖北省内河航运管理局的“巴东轮”正在进行一列式拖运法的情形。
