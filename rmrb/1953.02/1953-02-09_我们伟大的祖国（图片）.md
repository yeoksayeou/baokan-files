Author:
Date: 1953-02-09

    Our Great Motherland
    In order to meet the needs of the people in the Northwest for cultural life, a new paper mill has recently been built in the Northwest Region. This is the staff of the Lanzhou People's Machinery Factory, who are actively supporting the construction of the paper mill. They are assembling digesters for the paper mill.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为了满足西北人民文化生活的需要，最近西北区新建一个造纸厂。这是积极支援造纸厂建设的兰州人民机器厂的职工。他们正在为造纸厂装配蒸球。
