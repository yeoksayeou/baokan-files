Title: Czechoslovak Laboring People Warmly Celebrate the Fifth Anniversary of People's Victory Day
Author:
Date: 1953-02-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague: Czechoslovak laboring people warmly celebrated the fifth anniversary of People's Victory Day (February 25). TASS, Prague: The people of Prague, the capital of Czechoslovakia, held a celebration meeting for People's Victory Day on the 24th at the Smetana Theatre. Republic President Klement Gottwald, government personnel headed by Premier Antonín Zápotocký, National Assembly Chairman Oldřich John, and members of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party attended the meeting. When they appeared on the presidium of the venue, those present responded with stormy applause. At the meeting, Deputy Prime Minister Viliam Široký made a report, narrating the great significance of People's Victory Day. Then, Minister of Health Josef Plohar delivered a speech. A grand concert was held after the meeting.
    Czechoslovak newspapers published editorials and articles on the 25th celebrating the festival.

### 捷劳动人民热烈庆祝人民胜利日五周年

1953-02-27
第4版()
专栏：

    捷劳动人民热烈庆祝人民胜利日五周年
    据新华社讯：捷克斯洛伐克劳动人民热烈庆祝人民胜利日（二十五日）五周年。据塔斯社布拉格讯：捷克斯洛伐克首都布拉格人民二十四日在斯米塔·娜剧院举行了庆祝人民胜利日的大会。共和国总统克·哥特瓦尔德、以安·萨波托斯基总理为首的政府人员、国民议会主席奥·约翰和捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会委员们参加了这次大会。当他们在会场的主席台上出现时，到会者报以暴风雨般的鼓掌。会上，副总理威·西罗基作了报告，叙述了人民胜利日的巨大意义。接着，卫生部部长约·普洛伊哈发表了演说。会后举行了盛大的音乐会。
    捷克斯洛伐克的报纸在二十五日纷纷发表庆祝节日的社论和文章。
