Title: Chinese Medical Delegation Arrives in Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1953-02-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, February 22nd:  The Chinese medical delegation, led by Gong Naiquan, with members Zhong Huilan, Wu Chaoren, and Zhang Tiemin, arrived in Lahore on the 18th to attend the Second All-Pakistan Medical Conference, which opened on February 19th.

On the 17th, the delegation arrived in Karachi, where they were welcomed at the airport by Jilani Shah, Chairman of the Pakistan Medical Association, Manuwal Ali, Vice Chairman, and Loginov, Cultural Secretary of the Soviet Embassy in Pakistan. Zheng Weizhi, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Pakistan, also greeted them at the airport.

### 我国医师代表团到巴基斯坦

1953-02-23
第4版()
专栏：

    我国医师代表团到巴基斯坦
    【新华社新德里二十二日电】应邀参加二月十九日开幕的全巴基斯坦第二届医学会议的中国医师代表团团长宫乃泉，团员钟惠澜、吴朝仁、张铁民等一行四人，在十八日到达拉合尔。
    代表团在十七日到达喀喇蚩，到飞机场欢迎的有：巴基斯坦医学协会主席杰拉·夏、副主席马奴瓦·阿里及苏联驻巴基斯坦大使馆文化秘书洛吉诺夫等。我国驻巴基斯坦大使馆参赞郑为之也到机场迎接。
