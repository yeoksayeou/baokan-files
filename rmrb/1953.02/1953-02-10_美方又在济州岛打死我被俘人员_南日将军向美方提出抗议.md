Title: The US side again killed our captured personnel on Jeju Island. General Nan Ri protested to the US side.
Author:
Date: 1953-02-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 8th. On February 1st, the US side again killed one of our captured personnel in the Jeju Island POW camp. On the 8th, General Nan Ri, chief representative of our delegation to the Korean armistice talks, lodged a serious protest with the other side, pointing out that the other side can in no way escape full responsibility for the war crime of massacring prisoners of war.

### 美方又在济州岛打死我被俘人员  南日将军向美方提出抗议

1953-02-10
第1版()
专栏：

    美方又在济州岛打死我被俘人员
    南日将军向美方提出抗议
    【新华社开城八日电】二月一日美方在济州岛战俘营中又打死我方被俘人员一人。八日，朝鲜停战谈判我方代表团首席代表南日将军向对方提出严重抗议，指出对方决不能逃脱屠杀战俘的战争罪行的完全责任。
