Title: Premier Zhou and Ambassador Pan Youxin Arrive in Lushun-Dalian to a Warm Welcome
Author:
Date: 1953-02-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Dalian, February 22nd: On the eve of the 35th anniversary of the Soviet Army Day, February 22nd, Zhou恩来, Premier of the State Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People' s Government of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Dalian by plane to lead the delegation of the Central People's Government to visit the Soviet Army in the Lushunkou area. The delegation members who accompanied him were: Xiao Jingguang, Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy; Liu Yalou, Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force; Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Central People's Revolutionary Military Commission; and Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government.

Soviet Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Pan Youxin, arrived in Dalian on the same plane.

Senior generals of the Soviet troops stationed in Lushunkou were at the airport to welcome them.

Also at the airport to welcome them were Ouyang Qin, Mayor of Dalian and Secretary of the Dalian Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China; Chen Bocun, Second Secretary of the Dalian Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China; Li Lin and Qiao Chuanjue, Deputy Mayors of Dalian; and Kong Xianglin, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Dalian Municipal People's Government, and others.

Soviet Consul General in Dalian, Morozov, was also at the airport to welcome them.

Premier Zhou's arrival in Dalian was warmly welcomed by the Soviet troops stationed in the Lushunkou area.

Soviet and Chinese national flags and huge portraits of Stalin and Mao Zedong were hung at the airport. After Premier Zhou got off the plane, the band played the national anthems of China and the Soviet Union. Premier Zhou reviewed the Soviet honor guard.

The delegation of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China to visit the Soviet Army in the Lushunkou area is accompanied by five art troupes. These art troupes will begin performing for the Soviet troops stationed in the Lushunkou area on February 22nd.

### 周总理和潘友新大使等抵旅大时受热烈欢迎

1953-02-23
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理和潘友新大使等抵旅大时受热烈欢迎
    【新华社大连二十二日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府慰问驻旅顺口地区苏军代表团团长、中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来在苏联建军节三十五周年前夕——二月二十二日乘飞机到达旅大市。同来的有代表团代表：中国人民解放军海军司令员萧劲光，中国人民解放军空军司令员刘亚楼，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华，中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权。
    同机抵达旅大市的有苏联驻我国特命全权大使潘友新。
    到机场欢迎的有苏军驻旅顺口部队的高级将领。
    到机场欢迎的还有旅大市人民政府市长、中国共产党旅大市委员会书记欧阳钦，中国共产党旅大市委员会第二书记陈伯村，旅大市人民政府副市长李林、乔传珏，旅大市人民政府外事处处长孔祥林等。
    到机场欢迎的还有苏联驻旅大市总领事莫罗佐夫。
    周总理抵达旅大时，受到驻旅顺口地区苏军的热烈欢迎。
    机场上悬挂了苏中两国国旗和斯大林、毛泽东的巨幅画像。周总理下机后，乐队高奏中苏两国国歌。周总理检阅了苏军仪仗队。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府慰问驻旅顺口地区苏军代表团随带有五个文艺工作团。这些文艺工作团从二十二日起，就分别在旅顺口地区苏军防地作慰问演出。
