Title: Korean Ambassador Kwon O-jik Holds Reception Celebrating Army Day

Author:
Date: 1953-02-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 8th: To commemorate the fifth anniversary of the founding of the Korean People's Army on "February 8th," Kwon O-jik, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, held a celebratory reception in Beijing on the evening of the 8th, with a performance by the Railway Art Troupe of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea visiting China.

Among those attending the reception were: Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Central People's Government People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department; Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office; and senior generals from the Army, Navy, and Air Force.

Also attending the reception were: Teng Daiyuan, Minister of the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government; Zhu Xuefan, Minister of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture; Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade; as well as heads of various departments of the Central People's Government, heads of various people's organizations, and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, totaling more than 800 people.

Heads of diplomatic missions and diplomats from various countries stationed in China were also invited to attend the reception.

The Railway Art Troupe of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea visiting China performed many wonderful musical and dance programs at the reception. Each program was warmly welcomed by the audience and received continuous applause.

### 朝鲜权五稷大使举行庆祝建军节招待晚会

1953-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜权五稷大使举行庆祝建军节招待晚会
    【新华社八日讯】为纪念“二·八”朝鲜人民军建军五周年，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使权五稷特于八日晚在北京举行庆祝招待晚会，由朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访华铁道艺术团表演节目。
    出席晚会的有：中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长张宗逊、总政治部副主任萧华、办公厅主任萧向荣，以及陆、海、空军高级将领。
    出席晚会的还有：中央人民政府铁道部部长滕代远、邮电部部长朱学范、外交部副部长章汉夫、文化部副部长丁西林、对外贸易部副部长雷任民，以及中央人民政府各部门首长、各人民团体负责人和北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等八百多人。
    各国驻华使节和外交官也应邀出席晚会。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访华铁道艺术团在晚会中演出了许多精彩的音乐舞蹈节目。每一个节目都受到观众的热烈欢迎，博得不断的掌声。
