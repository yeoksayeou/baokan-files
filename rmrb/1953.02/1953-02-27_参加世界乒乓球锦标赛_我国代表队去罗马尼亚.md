Title: Our National Team Heads to Romania to Participate in the World Table Tennis Championships
Author:
Date: 1953-02-27
Page: 4

    Participating in the World Table Tennis Championships
    Our National Team Heads to Romania
   [Xinhua News Agency, February 24th] The Chinese Table Tennis Delegation, a group of ten people led by Yan Fumin, Secretary of the All-China Sports Federation, departed on the 19th for Romania to participate in the 20th World Table Tennis Championships, which will be held in Bucharest from March 20th to 29th. On their way to Romania, the delegation will visit Czechoslovakia and Hungary for friendly matches.

### 参加世界乒乓球锦标赛  我国代表队去罗马尼亚

1953-02-27
第4版()
专栏：

    参加世界乒乓球锦标赛
    我国代表队去罗马尼亚
   【新华社二十四日讯】中国乒乓球代表队一行十人，由中华全国体育总会秘书晏福民领队，在十九日前往罗马尼亚参加将于三月二十日到二十九日在布加勒斯特举行的第二十届世界乒乓球锦标赛。该代表队在去罗马尼亚途中将访问捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利作友谊比赛。
