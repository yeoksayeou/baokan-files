Title: Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej Receives Chinese Cultural and Educational Delegation
Author:
Date: 1953-02-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, February 24th: Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Romania, received the entire delegation of the People's Republic of China's Cultural and Educational Delegation, headed by Wei Que and Peng Kang, at 6:00 PM on the 20th. Present at the meeting were Chisinevschi and Apostol, Vice-Chairmen of the Council of Ministers of Romania, Minister of Foreign Affairs Bujor, and Miron Constantinescu, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party. Wang Youping, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Romania, accompanied the delegation. After the meeting, Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej hosted a banquet for the entire delegation.

### 乔治乌—德治接见我文教考察团

1953-02-26
第1版()
专栏：

    乔治乌—德治接见我文教考察团
    【新华社布加勒斯特二十四日电】罗马尼亚部长会议主席乔治乌—德治，在二十日下午六时接见以韦悫、彭康为首的中华人民共和国文化教育考察团全团人员。接见时在座的有罗马尼亚部长会议副主席基辛涅夫斯基、阿波斯托尔，外交部部长布吉奇，罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会书记米隆·康斯坦丁尼斯库。中华人民共和国驻罗马尼亚大使王幼平陪同考察团进见。接见以后，乔治乌—德治欢宴考察团全团人员。
