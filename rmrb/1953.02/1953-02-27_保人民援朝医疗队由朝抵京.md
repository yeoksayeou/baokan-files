Title: Bulgarian People's Aid Korea Medical Team Arrives in Beijing from Korea
Author:
Date: 1953-02-27
Page: 1

    Bulgarian People's Aid Korea Medical Team Arrives in Beijing from Korea
    [Xinhua News Agency, February 26th] The Bulgarian People's Aid Korea Medical Team, a group of sixteen people, arrived in Beijing from Korea on the morning of February 26th. Those who welcomed them at the station included: Wu Maosun, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Anti-American Aid Korea General Association; Zeng Zhaosheng, Director of the General Office; Xu Baoju, Minister of the Organization Department; Tan Tiwu and Mo Yichang, Deputy Ministers of the Organization Department; and Tang Mingzhao, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department; Fang Shishan, Secretary-General of the Chinese Medical Association. Personnel from the Bulgarian and Korean Embassies in China also welcomed them at the station.

### 保人民援朝医疗队由朝抵京

1953-02-27
第1版()
专栏：

    保人民援朝医疗队由朝抵京
    【新华社二十六日讯】保加利亚人民援朝医疗队一行十六人在二十六日上午由朝鲜抵京，到车站欢迎的有：中国人民抗美援朝总会副秘书长吴茂荪、办公厅主任曾昭生、组织部部长许宝驹、组织部副部长谭惕吾、莫艺昌、联络部副部长唐明照，中华医学会秘书长方石珊。保加利亚和朝鲜驻我国大使馆人员也到站欢迎。
