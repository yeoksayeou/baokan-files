Title: Vyshinsky Arrives in New York to Attend UN General Assembly

Author: 
Date: 1953-02-25
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, New York, February 24th: According to TASS, New York, February 24th: The head of the Soviet delegation and Soviet Foreign Minister, Vyshinsky, arrived in New York on the "Queen Mary" on the 23rd to attend the resumed second half of the Seventh Session of the United Nations General Assembly, scheduled to reconvene on the 24th. The head of the delegation of the Byelorussian Soviet Socialist Republic to the Seventh Session of the United Nations General Assembly and Minister of Foreign Affairs of Byelorussia, Kiselev, and the head of the delegation of the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic to the Seventh Session of the United Nations General Assembly and Minister of Foreign Affairs of Ukraine, Baranovsky, also arrived in New York on the same ship.
    Among those who went to welcome Vyshinsky were the Soviet Union's Permanent Representative to the United Nations and Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Zorin, the Soviet Ambassador to the United States, Zarubin, personnel of the Soviet delegation to the United Nations, and journalists.

### 出席联合国大会维辛斯基等到纽约

1953-02-25
第1版()
专栏：

    出席联合国大会维辛斯基等到纽约
    【新华社二十四日讯】据塔斯社纽约二十四日讯：苏联代表团团长、苏联外交部部长维辛斯基在二十三日乘“玛利皇后”号轮船到达纽约，出席定于二十四日复会的联合国第七届大会后半期会议。白俄罗斯苏维埃社会主义共和国出席联合国第七届大会代表团团长、白俄罗斯外交部部长基谢列夫，乌克兰苏维埃社会主义共和国出席联合国第七届大会代表团团长、乌克兰外交部部长巴拉诺夫斯基也乘同船到纽约。
    前往迎接维辛斯基等人的有苏联驻联合国常任代表、苏联外交部副部长佐林，苏联驻美国大使查鲁宾，苏联驻联合国代表团的人员及新闻记者等。
