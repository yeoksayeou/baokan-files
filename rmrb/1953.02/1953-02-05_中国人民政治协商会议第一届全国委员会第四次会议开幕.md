Title: The First Session of the Fourth Meeting of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Opens

Author:

Date: 1953-02-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 4th:

    The First Session of the Fourth Meeting of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference opened in Beijing on the afternoon of the 4th.

    One hundred and forty-five members of the National Committee attended today's meeting.

    Those present at the meeting as observers included members of the Central People's Government Committee, responsible persons from the various ministries, commissions, administrations, and associations of the Central People's Government, and members of the Tibetan delegation, totaling forty-nine people.

    At the meeting, Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, delivered a political report; Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the People's Political Consultative Conference, gave a report on the work of the conference; and Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Deputy Head of the Chinese Delegation to the World People's Peace Congress, gave a report on the process and achievements of the World People's Peace Congress.

    Tomorrow's meeting will be for group discussions. (Attached picture)

    Mao Zedong, Chairman of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, announced the opening of the fourth meeting of the National Committee. Seated on the rostrum were: (from left) Zhou Enlai, Li Jishen, Chen Shutong, Guo Moruo, Vice Chairmen of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and Secretary-General Li Weihan. Xinhua News Agency reporter Qi Guanshan

### 中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第四次会议开幕

1953-02-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第四次会议开幕
    【新华社四日讯】中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第四次会议四日下午在北京开幕。
    出席今日会议的有全国委员会委员一百四十五人。
    列席会议的有中央人民政府委员会委员，中央人民政府各院、委、署、会负责人，西藏致敬团人员等共四十九人。
    会上，中国人民政协全国委员会周恩来副主席作了政治报告，人民政协全国委员会陈叔通副主席作了关于会务的报告，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席、出席世界人民和平大会的中国代表团副团长郭沫若作了关于世界人民和平大会的经过和成就的报告。
    明日会议是进行分组讨论。（附图片）
    中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会主席毛泽东宣布全国委员会第四次会议开幕。坐在主席台上的是：（自左起）中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会副主席周恩来、李济深、陈叔通、郭沫若，秘书长李维汉。新华社记者齐观山摄
