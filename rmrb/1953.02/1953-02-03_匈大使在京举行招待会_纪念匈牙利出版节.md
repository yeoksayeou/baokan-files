Title: Hungarian Ambassador Holds Reception in Beijing to Commemorate Hungarian Publishing Festival
Author:
Date: 1953-02-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, February 2nd—On February 1st, the Hungarian Publishing Festival, the Hungarian People's Republic Ambassador to China, Száffránkó, held a reception in Beijing on the 2nd. The reception was for Chinese and foreign journalists and publishing workers in the capital. At the meeting, Mr. Duli, an attaché of the Hungarian Embassy in China, introduced the situation of the Hungarian news and publishing industry's struggle for peace, democracy, and socialism. Duli said that February 1st was the 11th anniversary of the founding of "Free People's Daily," the organ of the Hungarian Working People's Party, and he focused on the development of the "Free People's Daily." Ambassador Száffránkó then answered questions raised by the attendees. After the meeting, Hungarian documentary films "Heroic Youth," "Revenge of Waste Products," and "Semmelweis" were shown.

### 匈大使在京举行招待会  纪念匈牙利出版节

1953-02-03
第4版()
专栏：

    匈大使在京举行招待会
    纪念匈牙利出版节
    【新华社二日讯】二月一日是匈牙利出版节，匈牙利人民共和国驻华大使夏法朗柯二日在北京举行招待会。招待首都中外新闻出版工作者。会上，匈牙利驻华大使馆随员杜力介绍了关于匈牙利新闻出版事业为和平、民主和社会主义而斗争的情况。杜力说：二月一日是匈牙利劳动人民党机关报“自由人民报”创刊十一周年纪念日，他着重介绍了“自由人民报”的发展情形。夏法朗柯大使接着答复了到会者提出的问题。会后并放映了匈牙利纪录电影片“英勇的青年”，“废品的报复”和“赛迈尔魏斯”。
