Title: The CCP Fujian Provincial Committee Disciplinary Inspection Commission Investigates the Suppression of Criticism by Sun Anguo and Others
Author: 
Date: 1953-02-23
Page: 2

Response to People's Daily Readers' Criticism and Suggestions

    The CCP Fujian Provincial Committee Disciplinary Inspection Commission Investigates the Suppression of Criticism by Sun Anguo and Others

    A letter from the Secretariat of the CCP Fujian Provincial Committee: The incident of Sun Anguo, former head of the Civil Affairs Section of Dehua County in our province, and others suppressing criticism, as exposed in the "Readers' Letters" page on January 29, has been investigated and handled by the Provincial Committee Disciplinary Inspection Commission. The results will be communicated to you after processing is completed.

### 中共福建省委纪律检查委员会检查孙安国等压制批评事件

1953-02-23
第2版()
专栏：对人民日报读者批评建议的反应

    中共福建省委纪律检查委员会检查孙安国等压制批评事件
    中共福建省委秘书处来信：一月二十九日“读者来信专页”揭发的我省德化县前民政科长孙安国等压制批评的事件，省委纪律检查委员会已着手调查处理。其结果候处理完毕后告诉你们。
