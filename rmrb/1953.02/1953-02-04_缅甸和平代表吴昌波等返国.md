Title: Burmese Peace Representative U Chan Htoon and Others Return Home
Author:
Date: 1953-02-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 3rd—The Burmese representatives, U Chan Htoon and his party of five, who attended the Vienna World People's Peace Congress and were invited by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace to visit our country, arrived in Guangzhou on January 31. Among them, U Chan Htoon, U Thein, U Yin and U Ba Laung left Guangzhou on the 3rd to return home via Hong Kong. Guo Qiaoran, Deputy Secretary-General of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and others went to the station to welcome and see them off.

### 缅甸和平代表吴昌波等返国

1953-02-04
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸和平代表吴昌波等返国
    【新华社广州三日电】出席维也纳世界人民和平大会后、应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请前来我国参观访问的缅甸代表吴昌波等一行五人，一月三十一日到达广州；其中吴昌波、吴登、吴迎和吴巴拉昂四人在三日离广州取道香港返国。前往车站欢迎和欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广州市分会副秘书长郭翘然等多人。
