Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-02-03
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    In order to cultivate engineering geology personnel urgently needed for railway construction, the Ministry of Railways transferred more than 40 technical cadres engaged in surveying and designing new railway lines from across the country and organized a demonstration team for engineering geological surveys. Under the guidance of Soviet experts, they conducted more than ten days of surveying and practice in the Northwest. This is a scene of engineering personnel inspecting the terrain on the Qinling Mountains.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-02-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为了培养铁路建设中急需的工程地质人员，铁道部调集了全国铁路新线勘测设计技术干部四十多人，组织工程地质勘测示范队，在苏联专家指导下，在西北进行了十几天的勘测实习。这是工程人员正在秦岭上察看地形的情形。
