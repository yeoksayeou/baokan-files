Title: Henan Province Huai River General Headquarters is Investigating and Handling the Problem of Accumulated Cement

Author:
Date: 1953-02-20
Page: 2

A Response to Readers' Criticisms and Suggestions in the People's Daily

    Henan Province Huai River General Headquarters is Investigating and Handling the Problem of Accumulated Cement
    The Henan Province Huai River General Headquarters sent a telegram on February 9th stating: The matter of our department's accumulation of cement, as criticized in the People's Daily on February 1st, is currently under investigation and handling. A detailed report will be sent soon.

### 河南省治淮总指挥部正检查处理积压水泥问题

1953-02-20
第2版()
专栏：对人民日报读者批评建议的反应

    河南省治淮总指挥部正检查处理积压水泥问题
    河南省治淮总指挥部在二月九日来电报说：二月一日人民日报批评我部积压水泥事正检查处理中，不日将详细报告送上。
