Title: Bulgarian Parliament Approves This Year's Budget
Author:
Date: 1953-02-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, February 12th: The People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Bulgaria held its seventh session in Sofia from February 1st to 10th. Finance Minister Kiril Lazarov delivered a report on the 1953 national budget at the meeting. Lazarov pointed out that the 1953 budget is a budget for peaceful socialist construction, guaranteeing the continued development of Bulgaria's industrial and agricultural construction and the continued improvement of the people's living standards. The budget stipulates that the total revenue for the year is 19,020,992,734 leva, while the expenditure is 17,972,992,734 leva, with revenue exceeding expenditure by more than one billion leva. Seventy percent of the budget revenue comes from state-owned industries, and taxes paid by the people account for only 6.6 percent of the total revenue. Expenditures for national economic construction investment and social and cultural facilities account for 77.8 percent of the total budget expenditure.

    During the discussion of the budget report, deputies spoke one after another, pointing out the great significance of the budget for Bulgaria's socialist construction. Vulko Chervenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Bulgaria, spoke at the meeting on the 9th, explaining the various tasks of the Bulgarian people in 1953, and was warmly welcomed by all the deputies. The meeting finally unanimously approved the 1953 national budget.

    The meeting also passed the Cooperative Law, the People's Assembly Election Law and other resolutions.

### 保议会通过今年预算

1953-02-13
第4版()
专栏：

    保议会通过今年预算
    【新华社十二日讯】索非亚消息：保加利亚人民共和国人民议会从二月一日到十日在索非亚举行了第七次会议。财政部长基·拉扎罗夫在会上作了关于一九五三年国家预算的报告。拉扎罗夫指出：一九五三年预算是一个和平的社会主义建设的预算，它保证着保加利亚工农业建设的继续发展和人民生活水平的继续提高。预算中规定，全年收入为一百九十亿二千零九十九万二千七百三十四利瓦保币，支出为一百七十九亿七千二百九十九万二千七百三十四利瓦，收入超过支出十亿余利瓦。预算收入中百分之七十来自国营工业，人民缴纳的税额仅占全部收入的百分之六点六。用于国民经济建设投资和社会文化设施方面的经费，占预算总支出的百分之七十七点八。
    在讨论预算报告时，议员们纷纷发言，指出了预算对于保加利亚社会主义建设的巨大意义。保加利亚部长会议主席维尔科·契尔文科夫在九日会议上发言，说明了保加利亚人民一九五三年的各项任务，受到了全体议员的热烈欢迎。会议最后一致通过了一九五三年的国家预算。
    会议还通过了合作社法、人民议会选举法及其他决议。
