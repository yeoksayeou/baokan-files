Title: Indonesian Peace Representative Modito and Others Return Home After Visiting China at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1953-02-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 17th. After attending the Vienna World People's Peace Congress, the Indonesian representatives, Modito and his party of four, who visited China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, arrived in Guangzhou on the 14th and left Guangzhou on the 16th via Hong Kong to return home. Among those who went to the station to see them off were Pan Fozhang, a member of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and many others.

### 应邀来我国访问后  印尼和平代表莫迪多等返国

1953-02-19
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国访问后
    印尼和平代表莫迪多等返国
    【新华社广州十七日电】　出席维也纳世界人民和平大会后应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来我国访问的印度尼西亚代表莫迪多等一行四人，于十四日到达广州，并已于十六日离广州取道香港返国。前往车站欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广州市分会委员潘佛章等多人。
