Title: Korean Railway Art Troupe Holds Performance in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-02-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 10th:

    The "Performance by the Korean Democratic People's Republic Visiting Chinese Railway Art Troupe," sponsored by the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government, opened on the 10th at the Beijing Youth Palace Theater.

    Attending the performance were Wang Shijie, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government, responsible cadres of the Political Department of the Ministry of Railways and the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union, and more than 1,000 railway workers. Song Xingzhe, Counselor of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, and many embassy staff members also attended the performance.

    Vice Minister Wang Shijie delivered a speech on behalf of the Ministry of Railways and railway workers across the country, warmly welcoming the Korean Democratic People's Republic Visiting Chinese Railway Art Troupe to perform in our country. Li Qingqiu, head of the art troupe, also spoke at the meeting.

    The songs and dances performed by the art troupe were warmly welcomed by the audience in every program.

    The programs performed by the art troupe in our country are divided into dance, chorus, solo, etc. These programs all demonstrated the heroic spirit of the fighting Korean railway workers and the superior artistic traditions of the Korean people.

### 朝鲜铁道艺术团在京举行表演会

1953-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜铁道艺术团在京举行表演会
    【新华社十日讯】中央人民政府铁道部主办的“朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访华铁道艺术团演出”，十日在北京青年宫剧场开幕。
    出席这次演出会的有中央人民政府铁道部副部长王世泰、铁道部政治部及中国铁路工会全国委员会负责干部、铁路职工等共一千多人。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使馆参赞宋性澈及大使馆人员多人也出席了这次演出会。
    王世泰副部长代表铁道部和全国铁路职工致词，对朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访华铁道艺术团来我国演出表示热烈欢迎。艺术团团长李青求也在会上讲了话。
    艺术团演出的歌唱、舞蹈，每一节目都受到观众的热烈欢迎。
    艺术团在我国表演的节目分舞蹈、合唱、独唱等。这些节目都表现了战斗的朝鲜铁路职工的英勇气魄和朝鲜人民的优越的艺术传统。
