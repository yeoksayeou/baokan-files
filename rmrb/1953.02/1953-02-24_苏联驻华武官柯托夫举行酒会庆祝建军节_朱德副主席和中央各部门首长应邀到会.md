Title: Soviet Military Attaché Kotov Holds Reception to Celebrate Army Day; Vice Chairman Zhu De and Heads of Central Departments Attend Upon Invitation
Author:
Date: 1953-02-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd: The Soviet Military Attaché to China, Lieutenant General Kotov, held a reception in Beijing on the afternoon of the 23rd to celebrate the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army.

Those invited to attend were: Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government and Vice Chairman of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government; Gao Gang, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government and Vice Chairman of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Cheng Qian, Vice Chairman of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Dong Biwu and Guo Moruo, Vice Premiers of the Government Administration Council; Zhang Zhiran, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court; Huang Kecheng and Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chiefs of General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office; Chen Xilian, Commander of the Artillery of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Xu Guangda, Commander of the Armored Forces; Zhou Shidi, Commander of the Air Defense Forces; Chang Qiankun, Deputy Commander of the Air Force; Luo Shunchu, Deputy Commander of the Navy; Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; as well as senior officers of the Army, Navy, and Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, heads of various departments of the Central People's Government, responsible persons of various democratic parties and people's organizations, and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, totaling more than 400 people.

Also invited to attend were diplomatic envoys and military attachés from various countries stationed in China.

At the reception, Lieutenant General Kotov delivered a speech, saying: Our motherland is greeting the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy in an environment of creative and peaceful labor, and in an environment where the unbreakable friendship between the Soviet and Chinese peoples is increasingly consolidated and developed. The Soviet Army and Navy have been, are, and will continue to be an indestructible fortress and reliable protector of the interests of the Soviet state.

Vice Chairman Zhu De then spoke, expressing, on behalf of the Chinese people and the Chinese People's Liberation Army, heartfelt gratitude to Marshal Stalin and the heroic Soviet Army for their warm assistance to the cause of the liberation of our people. He said: The Soviet Army is the most advanced and most powerful armed force in the world. Our Chinese People's Liberation Army must strive to humbly learn from the Soviet Army and accept the advanced Soviet military science and all advanced military experience to accelerate regularization and modernization. The people and armies of China and the Soviet Union have been linked by the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship,Alliance and Mutual Assistance in the great cause of jointly defending world peace. The strong unity of the people and armies of China and the Soviet Union is invincible. If the imperialists dare to risk launching a new war, they will surely suffer a complete defeat.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere. At the reception, the hosts and guests repeatedly toasted to the friendship between the peoples and armies of China and the Soviet Union, to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, the great leader of the Chinese people and the creator and leader of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and to the health of the world's working people's leader, the inspirer and organizer of the victories of the Soviet Army—the great Stalin.

### 苏联驻华武官柯托夫举行酒会庆祝建军节  朱德副主席和中央各部门首长应邀到会

1953-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联驻华武官柯托夫举行酒会庆祝建军节
    朱德副主席和中央各部门首长应邀到会
    【新华社二十三日讯】为庆祝苏联建军三十五周年，苏联驻我国大使馆武官柯托夫中将，二十三日下午在北京举行酒会。
    应邀到会的有：中央人民政府副主席、人民革命军事委员会副主席朱德，中央人民政府副主席李济深，中央人民政府副主席、人民革命军事委员会副主席高岗，人民革命军事委员会副主席程潜，人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻，政务院副总理董必武、郭沫若，最高人民法院副院长张志让，人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长黄克诚、张宗逊，办公厅主任萧向荣，中国人民解放军炮兵司令员陈锡联，装甲兵司令员许光达，防空司令员周士第，空军副司令员常乾坤，海军副司令员罗舜初，中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫，以及中国人民解放军陆海空军高级军官、中央人民政府各部门首长和各民主党派、人民团体负责人、北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等共四百余人。
    应邀到会的还有各国驻华使节和武官。
    会上，柯托夫中将致词说：我们的祖国是在创造性的和平劳动的环境中、在苏中两国人民牢不可破的友谊日益巩固和发展的环境中迎接苏联陆海军建军三十五周年的。苏联陆军和海军过去是、现在是、将来也是苏联国家利益的不可摧毁的堡垒和可靠的保护者。
    朱德副主席接着代表中国人民和中国人民解放军向热诚援助我国人民解放事业的斯大林大元帅和英雄的苏联军队表示衷心的感谢。他说：苏联军队是世界上最先进、最强大的武装力量。我们中国人民解放军必须努力虚心地向苏联军队学习，接受苏联先进的军事科学和一切先进的军事经验以加速正规化、现代化建设。中苏两国人民及其军队已由中苏友好同盟互助条约联结在共同保卫世界和平的伟大事业中。中苏两国人民及其军队的坚强团结是无敌的。如果帝国主义者敢于冒险发动新的战争，必将遭受彻底的失败。
    酒会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。会上，宾主频频为中苏两国人民和两国军队的友谊，为中国人民伟大的领袖、中国人民解放军的创造者和领导者毛泽东主席的健康，为世界劳动人民的领袖、苏联军队胜利的鼓舞者和组织者——伟大的斯大林的健康而干杯。
