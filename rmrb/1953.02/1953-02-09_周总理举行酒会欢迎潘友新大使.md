Title: Premier Zhou Holds Reception to Welcome Ambassador Panyushkin

Author: 
Date: 1953-02-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 8th: Premier Zhou Enlai, concurrently Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, held a reception in Beijing on the afternoon of the 8th to welcome the newly appointed Soviet Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Panyushkin.

Ambassador Panyushkin and all diplomatic personnel of the Soviet Embassy were invited to the reception.

Those present included: Secretary-General of the Central People's Government Lin Boqu, Vice Premiers of the Government Administration Council Dong Biwu and Guo Moruo, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court Zhang Zhizhiang, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Xiao Hua, members of the Central People's Government, members of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, heads of various ministries, commissions, and administrations under the Central People's Government, heads of people's organizations, well-known figures from cultural, scientific, and artistic circles, and Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, totaling over 200 people.

At the reception, Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai delivered a welcome speech, expressing his respect for Ambassador Panyushkin's consistent efforts in strengthening the friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union. Ambassador Panyushkin then delivered a reply, expressing his gratitude.

During the reception, the hosts and guests frequently toasted the great friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union, the health of Comrade Stalin, the leader of the working people of the world and standard-bearer of peace, and the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, the great leader of the Chinese people.

### 周总理举行酒会欢迎潘友新大使

1953-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理举行酒会欢迎潘友新大使
    【新华社八日讯】我中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来，八日下午在北京举行酒会，欢迎新任苏联驻我国特命全权大使潘友新。
    潘友新大使和苏联大使馆全体外交人员都应邀到会。
    出席作陪的有：中央人民政府秘书长林伯渠，政务院副总理董必武、郭沫若，最高人民法院副院长张志让，人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华，中央人民政府委员，人民革命军事委员会委员，及中央人民政府各部、会、署首长，人民团体负责人，文化、科学、艺术界著名人士和北京市人民政府市长彭真等二百余人。
    会上，周恩来总理兼外交部长致欢迎词，对潘友新大使一向在加强中苏两国人民友谊方面的努力表示敬意。接着，潘友新大使致答词表示感谢。
    会上，宾主频频为中苏两国人民伟大的友谊，为全世界劳动人民的领袖、和平旗手斯大林主席的健康，为中国人民伟大的领袖毛泽东主席的健康而干杯。
