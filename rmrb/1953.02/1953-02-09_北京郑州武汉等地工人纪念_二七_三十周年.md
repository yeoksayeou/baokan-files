Title: Workers in Beijing, Zhengzhou, Wuhan and Other Places Commemorate the 30th Anniversary of the "February 7th" Strike
Author:
Date: 1953-02-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency Report: Workers in Beijing, Zhengzhou, Wuhan, Xi'an, Fuzhou and other places held grand gatherings on the 7th to commemorate the 30th anniversary of the "February 7th" Strike.

　　More than 1,000 employees of the Beijing Transportation Branch of the Beijing Railway Administration held a commemorative meeting on the 7th. Liu Rong, who was elected as the workers' representative of the Beijing-Suiyuan Railway during the "February 7th" Strike, reported on the course of the "February 7th" struggle. The Beijing Railway Administration also organized a慰问団 (condolence group) to visit the families of the "February 7th" martyrs in Baoding, Changxindian, Zhangjiakou and other places on February 4th.

　　More than 1,500 people, including family members of the "February 7th" martyrs, veteran workers of the "February 7th" strike, and representatives from all walks of life in Zhengzhou, solemnly held a commemorative meeting for the 30th anniversary of the "February 7th" Strike and a ceremony to celebrate the opening of the newly built "February 7th" Memorial Hall on the 7th. Song Zhihe, the mayor of the Zhengzhou Municipal People's Government, on behalf of the people of the city, expressed condolences to the martyrs who sacrificed in the "February 7th" struggle, extended cordial greetings to the family members of the martyrs present, and paid respects to the veteran workers of the "February 7th" strike. Then, Lin Maoxiang, a veteran worker of the "February 7th" strike and the Minister of the Living Quarters Department of the Zhengzhou District Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union, reported on his experience in participating in the heroic "February 7th" struggle at the meeting. After the meeting, the slide show "History of the February 7th Struggle" and the movie "The Road to Light" were shown.

　　In the Jiang'an area of Hankou, one of the birthplaces of the "February 7th" Strike, more than 600 railway worker representatives and veteran workers of the "February 7th" Strike held a commemorative meeting. The workers paid their respects to the "February 7th" martyrs such as Lin Xiangqian and Shi Yang. Various factories in the Jiang'an area also held small commemorative meetings, forums, and get-togethers for young and old workers. The Hankou Branch of the Chinese Railway Trade Union also extended condolences to the families of the "February 7th" martyrs. In order to educate the younger generation, the Wuhan Federation of Trade Unions specially invited labor models and advanced workers from Wuhan to hold a forum on the 7th, and invited Wu Dongshan and Wan Nengshan, veteran workers of the "February 7th" Strike in the Jiang'an area, to report on the history of the "February 7th" struggle.

　　The Xi'an Federation of Trade Unions and the Xi'an Railway Branch Trade Union respectively held commemorative meetings. More than 1,300 worker representatives from the Xi'an railway system attended the commemorative meeting held by the Xi'an Railway Branch Trade Union. At the meeting, the worker representatives expressed that they would commemorate the "February 7th" Strike with practical actions. Liu Yuting, an old fitter who participated in the workers' picket line of the "February 7th" Strike, said that he would write a letter to his son on the Korean front, telling him to defend the motherland well and strive for merit.

　　At the commemorative meeting held by the Fujian Provincial Federation of Trade Unions and the Fuzhou Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, Lin Xianglin, the younger brother of martyr Lin Xiangqian, and Lin Guankang, the son of Lin Xiangqian, both spoke on stage. They both expressed their gratitude to the People's Government, the trade union, and the broad masses of the people for their concern for the families of the martyrs.

### 北京郑州武汉等地工人纪念“二七”三十周年

1953-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    北京郑州武汉等地工人纪念“二七”三十周年
    据新华社讯：北京、郑州、武汉、西安、福州等地工人七日集会隆重纪念“二七”三十周年。
    北京铁路管理局北京运输分局一千多名职工七日举行纪念大会，由“二七”时被选为京绥线工人代表的柳荣报告“二七”斗争经过。北京铁路管理局还组织了慰问团，于二月四日分赴保定、长辛店、张家口等地，慰问“二七”烈士家属。
    郑州“二七”烈士家属、“二七”老工人及各界人民代表一千五百余人，于七日在新建成的“二七”纪念堂隆重举行纪念“二七”三十周年及庆祝“二七”纪念堂开幕典礼大会。郑州市人民政府市长宋致和代表全市人民向在“二七”斗争中牺牲的烈士致哀悼，向到会的烈士家属致亲切的慰问，向“二七”老工人致敬礼。接着“二七”老工人、中国铁路工会郑州区委员会生活住宅部部长林茂湘在会上报告了参加“二七”英勇斗争的经过。会后放映了“二七斗争史”幻灯片和电影“光明之路”。
    在“二七”大罢工斗争策源地之一的汉口江岸地区，六百多个铁路工人代表和“二七”老工人举行了纪念大会。工人们祭吊了林祥谦和施洋等“二七”先烈。江岸地区各厂也都分别举行了小型纪念会、座谈会以及青年工人、老年工人的联欢会。中国铁路工会汉口分区委员会并慰问了“二七”烈士的家属。武汉市总工会为了教育年轻一代，在七日特邀集了武汉市劳动模范和先进工作者举行座谈会，请江岸地区“二七”老工人吴东山、万能山报告“二七”斗争史。
    西安市总工会、西安铁路分区工会分别召开了纪念大会。在西安铁路分区工会召开的纪念大会上，到有西安铁路系统的工人代表一千三百多人。会上，工人代表们表示要以实际行动来纪念“二七”。参加“二七”大罢工工人纠察队的老钳工刘玉亭表示要写信给在朝鲜前线的儿子，叫他好好保卫祖国，争取立功。
    在福建省总工会暨福州市总工会举行的纪念大会上，林祥谦烈士的胞弟林祥麟和儿子林冠康都上台讲话。他们都表示感谢人民政府、工会和广大人民对烈士家属的关怀。
