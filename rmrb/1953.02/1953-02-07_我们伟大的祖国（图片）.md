Author: 
Date: 1953-02-07

    Our Great Motherland
    Today is the 30th anniversary of the "February 7th" Incident. The workers of the Beijing-Hankou Railway are inheriting and carrying forward the glorious revolutionary tradition, and are working hard for the country's large-scale economic construction. This is a model production team at the Changxindian Locomotive and Rolling Stock Repair Factory. They are enthusiastically discussing and formulating new production plans.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-02-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    今天是“二七”三十周年纪念日。京汉铁路的工人们继承和发扬着光荣的革命传统，正为国家大规模的经济建设而努力。这是长辛店机车车辆修理厂的一个模范生产小组。他们正在热烈地讨论制订新的生产计划。
