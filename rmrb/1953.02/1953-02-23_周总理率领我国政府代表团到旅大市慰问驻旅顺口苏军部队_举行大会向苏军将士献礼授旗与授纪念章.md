Title: Premier Zhou Leads Chinese Government Delegation to Lüda City to Comfort Soviet Troops Stationed in Lushunkou; Grand Meeting Held to Present Gifts, Flags, and Commemorative Medals to Soviet Soldiers

Author: 
Date: 1953-02-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Dalian, February 22nd: The delegation of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China to Comfort Soviet Troops Stationed in the Lushunkou Area solemnly held a grand meeting in Lüda City on the afternoon of February 22nd to celebrate the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and to comfort the Soviet troops stationed in the Lushunkou area.

On the rostrum, magnificent portraits of Chairman Stalin and Chairman Mao Zedong were hung, along with the national flags of China and the Soviet Union. Large red banners explaining the profound friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union were hung around the meeting venue.

The presidium of the meeting included: Zhou Enlai, head of the delegation of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China to Comfort Soviet Troops Stationed in the Lushunkou Area, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; delegation members: Xiao Jingguang, Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy; Liu Yalou, Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force; Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Soviet Ambassador to China, P. Yudin; senior generals of the Soviet troops stationed in the Lushunkou area; the mayor of Lüda City; the president of the Lüda City Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; and the Soviet Consul General in Lüda City.

Soviet personnel attending the meeting included representatives of officers and soldiers of all levels of the Soviet troops stationed in the Lushunkou area. Chinese participants included cadres from various organs and units in Lüda City, representatives from various democratic parties and people's organizations, model workers, and figures from the cultural circles. Over 1,500 people attended the meeting.

The meeting began with the orchestra playing the national anthems of the Soviet Union and China.

When the members of the presidium took their seats, all attendees gave a stormy applause and stood up in salute.

Deputy Director Xiao Hua presided over the meeting and delivered the opening remarks.

Premier Zhou Enlai first spoke at the meeting (the full text of the speech will be published separately).

Ambassador P. Yudin and the commander of the Soviet troops stationed in the Lushunkou area also spoke at the meeting successively.

These speeches, which fully demonstrated the permanent and unbreakable friendship between the two great powers of China and the Soviet Union and their determination to jointly defend the Far East and world peace, were repeatedly met with cheers and enthusiastic applause from the attendees.

The delegation presented gifts to the Soviet troops at the meeting and held a ceremony to award flags and commemorative medals. Premier Zhou Enlai, representing Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government, presented the banner to the commander of the Soviet troops stationed in the Lushunkou area to the sound of music, and, representing Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government People's Revolutionary Military Commission, presented the Sino-Soviet Friendship Commemorative Medal to the Soviet soldiers.

The banner, signed by Chairman Mao Zedong, was inscribed with: "The close unity of the great peoples and armed forces of the Soviet Union and China is a reliable guarantee for peace in the Far East and the world."

The meeting proceeded in an extremely warm and friendly atmosphere. Throughout the meeting, the attendees continuously shouted: "Stalin—Mao Zedong, Stalin—Mao Zedong!"

The meeting concluded with twenty young Chinese female cultural workers presenting flowers to the Soviet soldiers.

After the meeting, Premier Zhou Enlai hosted a grand reception at 6:00 p.m. to entertain senior generals of the Soviet troops stationed in the Lushunkou area, officers of all ranks, and representatives of the soldiers. Ambassador P. Yudin and the Soviet Consul General in Lüda City also attended the reception. Those accompanying at the reception included: Ouyang Qin, Mayor of Lüda City and Secretary of the Lüda City Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Chen Bocun, Second Secretary of the Lüda City Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Qiao Chuanjue, Deputy Mayor of Lüda City and President of the Lüda City Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; and people from all walks of life.

Simultaneously with the reception, the Soviet troops stationed in the Lushunkou area held celebration meetings in four locations to celebrate the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army. Representatives of the delegation of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China to Comfort Soviet Troops Stationed in the Lushunkou Area, Xiao Jingguang, Liu Yalou, Xiao Hua, and Wu Xiuquan, attended these four celebration meetings respectively to express their regards.

In the evening, the five cultural work troupes led by our Central People's Government delegation to Comfort Soviet Troops Stationed in the Lushunkou Area held drama, music, and dance performances in five locations to comfort the Soviet troops.

### 周总理率领我国政府代表团到旅大市慰问驻旅顺口苏军部队  举行大会向苏军将士献礼授旗与授纪念章

1953-02-23
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理率领我国政府代表团到旅大市慰问驻旅顺口苏军部队
    举行大会向苏军将士献礼授旗与授纪念章
    【新华社大连二十二日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府慰问驻旅顺口地区苏军代表团二十二日下午在旅大市隆重举行庆祝苏联建军三十五周年、慰问驻旅顺口地区苏军部队大会。
    大会主席台上庄严地悬挂着斯大林主席、毛泽东主席的巨幅画像和中苏两国国旗。会场建筑物四周挂着说明中苏两国人民深厚友谊的大幅红布标语。
    大会主席团包括：中华人民共和国中央人民政府慰问驻旅顺口地区苏军代表团团长、中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，代表团代表：中国人民解放军海军司令员萧劲光、中国人民解放军空军司令员刘亚楼、中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华、中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权，苏联驻我国大使潘友新，苏军驻旅顺口地区部队高级将领，旅大市人民政府市长，以及旅大市中苏友好协会会长，苏联驻旅大市总领事等。
    出席大会的苏联方面人士，有苏军驻旅顺口地区部队各级军官和战士代表；中国方面，有旅大市各机关、部队的干部，各民主党派和各人民团体代表，模范劳动者，文化界人士等。出席大会的共一千五百多人。
    大会在乐队高奏苏中两国国歌声中开始。
    当主席团就位时，全体到会者报以暴风雨般的掌声，并一致起立致敬。
    大会由萧华副主任主持并致开会词。
    周恩来总理在大会上首先讲话（讲词全文另发）。
    潘友新大使和驻旅顺口地区苏军指挥官也在会上相继讲了话。
    这些充分显示中苏两大强国的永久的、牢不可破的友谊和共同保卫远东与世界和平的决心的讲话，都一再受到到会者的欢呼和热烈的掌声。
    代表团在大会上向苏军部队献礼，并举行授旗与授纪念章的仪式。周恩来总理在乐声中代表中央人民政府毛泽东主席将锦旗授给驻旅顺口地区的苏军指挥官，并代表中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会毛泽东主席将中苏友谊纪念章授给苏军将士。
    毛泽东主席署名的锦旗上缀着：“伟大的苏中两国人民及其武装力量的亲密团结是远东及世界和平的可靠保障”。
    大会一直在极为热烈友好的空气中进行，全体到会者在大会进行时间内，曾不断欢呼：“斯大林—毛泽东，斯大林—毛泽东！”
    大会最后由二十个年青的中国女文艺工作者向苏军将士献花。
    大会后，周恩来总理在下午六时，接着举行盛大的招待会，招待驻旅顺口地区苏军部队高级将领，和苏军部队各级军官以及战士代表。潘友新大使和苏联驻旅大市总领事也出席了招待会。在招待会上作陪的有：旅大市人民政府市长、中国共产党旅大市委员会书记欧阳钦，中国共产党旅大市委员会第二书记陈伯村，旅大市人民政府副市长、旅大市中苏友好协会会长乔传珏以及各界人士等。
    与招待会同时，驻旅顺口地区的苏军部队，分别在四处举行庆祝大会，庆祝苏联建军三十五周年。中华人民共和国中央人民政府慰问驻旅顺口地区苏军代表团代表萧劲光、刘亚楼、萧华、伍修权曾分别参加了这四个庆祝会，进行慰问。
    晚间，我中央人民政府慰问驻旅顺口地区苏军代表团所带领的五个文艺工作团，分别在五处举行戏剧、音乐、舞蹈表演会，慰问苏军部队。
