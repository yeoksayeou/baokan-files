Title: Stalin Receives Dr. Kitchlew and Receives Indian Ambassador Menon
Author: 
Date: 1953-02-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 18th: J.V. Stalin received Dr. Saifuddin Kitchlew, Chairman of the All-India Peace Council, on the 17th.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 18th: J.V. Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, received the Indian Ambassador K.P.S. Menon on the 17th. Present at the meeting was Ya. A. Malik, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR.

### 斯大林接见克其鲁并接见印度大使梅农

1953-02-19
第4版()
专栏：

    斯大林接见克其鲁并接见印度大使梅农
    【新华社十八日讯】　塔斯社莫斯科十八日讯：约·维·斯大林十七日接见了全印和平理事会主席赛福丁·克其鲁博士。
    【新华社十八日讯】　塔斯社莫斯科十八日讯：苏联部长会议主席约·维·斯大林十七日接见了印度大使克·普·斯·梅农。接见时在座的有苏联外交部副部长雅·阿·马立克。
