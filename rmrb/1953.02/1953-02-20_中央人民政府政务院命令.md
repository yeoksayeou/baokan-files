Date: 1953-02-20
Page: 1

    Order of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government
    The "Measures for the Payment of Unsettled Deposits in Silver Shops and Money Shops Before Liberation" of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government has been approved by the 166th meeting of the Government Administration Council. It is hereby promulgated for implementation.
        This order.
                                  Premier Zhou Enlai
                               February 20, 1953
