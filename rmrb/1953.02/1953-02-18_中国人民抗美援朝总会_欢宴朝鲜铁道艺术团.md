Title: China People's Anti-American and Aid-Korea General Association Holds Banquet for Korean Railway Art Troupe
Author: 
Date: 1953-02-18
Page: 1
    
    Xinhua News Agency, 17th: The China People's Anti-American and Aid-Korea General Association held a banquet on the evening of the 17th to welcome all members of the Korean Democratic People's Republic visiting Chinese Railway Art Troupe, headed by leader Li Qingqiu. Chen Shutong, Zhang Xiruo, Luo Longji, Xu Baoju, Xu Deheng, Liu Guanyi, Wu Maosun, Cui Yueli and others from the China People's Anti-American and Aid-Korea General Association attended as hosts. Zhou Yang, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, Zhao Jianmin, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Railways, Chen Yi, Minister of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Military Commission, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, He Ying, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and cultural and artistic figures Lao She, Ai Qing, Cao Yu, Zhao Feng, Xu Xiaobing and others were invited to attend. The Korean Ambassador to China, Quan Wu Ji, and embassy staff were also invited.
    At the banquet, Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the China People's Anti-American and Aid-Korea General Association, delivered a welcoming speech first, and leader Li Qingqiu and Ambassador Quan Wu Ji also spoke successively. During the banquet, guests frequently toasted the great victory of the Chinese and Korean people in resisting the American aggressors and the eternal friendship between the Chinese and Korean people.
    The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 中国人民抗美援朝总会  欢宴朝鲜铁道艺术团

1953-02-18
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民抗美援朝总会
    欢宴朝鲜铁道艺术团
    【新华社十七日讯】中国人民抗美援朝总会在十七日晚举行宴会，欢迎以李青求团长为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访华铁道艺术团全体人员。出席作陪的有中国人民抗美援朝总会陈叔通、张奚若、罗隆基、许宝驹、许德珩、刘贯一、吴茂荪、崔月犁等人。应邀作陪的还有中央人民政府文化部副部长周扬、铁道部副部长赵健民、军委总政治部文化部部长陈沂、北京市副市长吴晗、外交部亚洲司副司长何英及文化艺术界人士老舍、艾青、曹禺、赵沨、徐肖冰等人。朝鲜驻我国大使权五稷和使馆人员也应邀出席。
    在宴会上，中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席陈叔通首先致欢迎词，李青求团长和权五稷大使也相继讲了话。席间宾主频频为中朝两国人民抗击美国侵略军的伟大胜利和中朝两国人民永恒的友谊而干杯。
    宴会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
