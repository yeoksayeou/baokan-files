    Our Great Motherland
    This is a man-made cave. Geological exploration personnel found the ore body boundary here. They are about to use drilling rigs to tap the rocks, preparing to meet the iron ore that has been buried for tens of millions of years.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-02-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    这是一个人工开凿的岩洞。地质钻探人员在这里找到了矿体界线。他们就要用钻机叩打岩石，准备和那些被埋藏了千万年的铁矿会面。
