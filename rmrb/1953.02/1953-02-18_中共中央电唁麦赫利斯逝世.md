Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Condolences on Mekhlis's Death
Author:
Date: 1953-02-18
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, February 17th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, expressing condolences on the death of Comrade Mekhlis, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. The text is as follows:
    To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union:
    We are grieved to learn of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Lev Zakharovich Mekhlis, an outstanding activist of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, a Soviet statesman, and a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. We extend our condolences.
                            The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
                                February 17th

### 中共中央电唁麦赫利斯逝世

1953-02-18
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央电唁麦赫利斯逝世
    【新华社十七日讯】中国共产党中央委员会致电苏联共产党中央委员会，哀悼苏联共产党中央委员麦赫利斯同志的逝世。文如下：苏联共产党中央委员会：
    惊闻苏联共产党卓越的活动家、苏维埃的政治家，苏联共产党中央委员会委员列夫·扎哈罗维奇·麦赫利斯同志不幸逝世，谨致哀悼。
                            中国共产党中央委员会
                                二月十七日
