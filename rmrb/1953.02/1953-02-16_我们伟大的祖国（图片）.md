Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-02-16
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    On the morning of this year's Spring Festival, a partial solar eclipse could be seen in vast areas of our country. On this day, schools and Communist Youth League organizations in various places organized small-scale solar eclipse observations to observe the initial and final phases of the solar eclipse and conduct widespread science education. This is a scene of teachers and Young Pioneers from Dongtiejiang Hutong Elementary School in Beijing observing the solar eclipse in the morning.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-02-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    今年春节的早晨，我国广大地区都可看到日偏食。各地学校和青年团组织，在这一天组织了小规模的日食观测，观测日食初亏、复圆的情况，进行了一次广泛的科学教育。这是北京东铁匠胡同小学的老师和少年儿童队员在早晨观测日食的情形。
