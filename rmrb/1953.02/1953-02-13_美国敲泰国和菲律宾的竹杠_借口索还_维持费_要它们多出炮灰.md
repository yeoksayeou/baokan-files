Title: The United States Gouges Thailand and the Philippines, Demanding More Cannon Fodder Under the Pretext of Reclaiming "Maintenance Fees"
Author:
Date: 1953-02-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 10th: The "Today's News" correspondent in Bangkok reports that the US government is demanding that the Thai Phibunsongkhram government and the Philippine Quirino government repay the maintenance costs for the Thai and Philippine troops in Korea. The US government believes that the cost for Thailand in this regard is 7.5 million US dollars, and for the Philippines, it is 9 million US dollars.

When making this request to Phibunsongkhram and Quirino, the US government particularly emphasized that they could repay the debts owed to the US in the following way: each of the two countries should send no less than 15,000 more troops to Korea.

### 美国敲泰国和菲律宾的竹杠  借口索还“维持费”要它们多出炮灰

1953-02-13
第4版()
专栏：

    美国敲泰国和菲律宾的竹杠
    借口索还“维持费”要它们多出炮灰
    【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社喀喇蚩十日讯：“今天报”驻曼谷记者报道，美国政府要求泰国銮披汶政府和菲律宾季里诺政府偿还在朝鲜的泰国部队和菲律宾部队的维持费。美国政府认为泰国在这方面的费用是七百五十万美元，菲律宾是九百万美元。
    美国政府在向銮披汶和季里诺提出这一要求时特别强调：他们可以用下面的办法来偿还所欠美国的债务：这两个国家各自再派遣不少于一万五千名的部队去朝鲜。
