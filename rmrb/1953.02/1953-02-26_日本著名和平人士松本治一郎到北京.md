Title: Prominent Japanese Peace Advocate Matsumoto Jiichiro Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-02-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 25th:

    Matsumoto Jiichiro, a prominent Japanese peace advocate, and his secretary, a party of three, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 25th for a visit at the invitation of the China Peace Committee. Matsumoto Jiichiro was the head of the Japanese delegation to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. Due to obstruction by the Yoshida government, he was unable to attend the conference, but he was elected Vice-Chairman of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee at the conference.

    Those welcoming him at the airport included: Liu Guan-yi, Secretary-General of the China Peace Committee; Tang Mingzhao, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department; Louis Wheaton (American), Deputy Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee; Louis Ayre (New Zealand), a member of the committee; Saionji Kinkazu and Nakamura Ganemon, representatives of Japan attending the World People's Peace Congress; and Bai Shuling, a New Zealand representative attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference.

    Also welcoming him at the airport were Hirano Yoshitaro, Deputy Head of the Japanese delegation composed of three groups from the Japanese Red Cross Society, the Japanese Peace Liaison Committee, and the Japan-China Friendship Association, who came to Beijing to discuss assisting Japanese nationals to return to their country with the Chinese Red Cross Society; Kora Tomi; representatives Uchiyama Kanzo, Hatanaka Masaharu, and Kashima Toshio; Yamamoto Kumaichi, Chairman of the Japan-China Trade Promotion Conference, who is currently in Beijing; and Suzuki Kazuo, Managing Director of the Japan-China Trade Promotion Association.

    Matsumoto Jiichiro gave a speech at the airport, expressing his great excitement that he was finally able to visit Beijing after overcoming the obstruction of the Yoshida government. Subsequently, Liu Guan-yi and Louis Wheaton warmly welcomed Matsumoto Jiichiro on behalf of the China Peace Committee and the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, respectively. They both expressed their willingness to work together with the Japanese people to consolidate peace in Asia and the world. Hirano Yoshitaro also spoke, expressing his welcome to Matsumoto Jiichiro.

### 日本著名和平人士松本治一郎到北京

1953-02-26
第1版()
专栏：

    日本著名和平人士松本治一郎到北京
    【新华社二十五日讯】日本著名和平人士松本治一郎和他的秘书一行三人，应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的邀请，在二十五日乘飞机到达北京访问。松本治一郎是日本出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议代表团团长，因吉田政府阻挠，未能出席会议，在会上他被选为亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副主席。
    到机场欢迎的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一、国际联络部副部长唐明照，亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长路易斯·惠吞
    （美国）、委员路易·艾黎（新西兰），日本出席世界人民和平大会代表西园寺公一和中村玩右卫门，新西兰列席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议代表白书玲。
    到机场欢迎的还有由日本红十字会、日本和平联络委员会和日中友好协会三团体组成的来北京与中国红十字会商谈协助日侨归国问题的日本代表团副团长平野义太郎、高良富、代表内山完造、畑中政春、加岛敏雄，现在北京的日本日中贸易促进会议主席山本熊一，日本中日贸易促进会常务理事铃木一雄等人。
    松本治一郎在机场发表谈话，他对他克服吉田政府的阻挠，终能到达北京访问，表示非常兴奋。接着，刘贯一和路易斯·惠吞分别代表中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会热烈欢迎松本治一郎，他们一致表示愿和日本人民一起努力，来巩固亚洲和世界和平。平野义太郎也讲了话，表示欢迎松本治一郎。
