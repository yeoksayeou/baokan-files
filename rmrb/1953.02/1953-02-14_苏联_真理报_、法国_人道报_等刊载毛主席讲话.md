Title: Soviet "Pravda," French "L'Humanité," and Others Publish Chairman Mao's Speech
Author:
Date: 1953-02-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 13th dispatch: According to TASS, Moscow: "Pravda" published on the 9th a New China News Agency report on Chairman Mao Zedong's speech at the closing session of the Fourth Meeting of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, February 13th: The French "L'Humanité" and the Belgian "Red Flag" both published Chairman Mao Zedong's speech at the closing session of the Fourth Meeting of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in prominent positions on the 9th.

### 苏联“真理报”、法国“人道报”等刊载毛主席讲话

1953-02-14
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联“真理报”、法国“人道报”等刊载毛主席讲话
    【新华社十三日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：“真理报”九日刊载了新华通讯社关于毛泽东主席在中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第四次会议闭幕会上讲话的消息。
    【新华社布拉格十三日电】法国“人道报”和比利时“红旗报”都在九日以显著地位刊载毛泽东主席在中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第四次会议闭幕会上的讲话。
