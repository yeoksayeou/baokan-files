Title: Government Administration Council Holds 169th Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1953-02-28
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, February 27th: The 169th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 27th. The meeting listened to the summary report of the work of the People's Republic of China's cultural delegation to Poland, given by the head of the delegation, Shen Yanbing. The meeting also adopted various appointment and removal proposals and proposals to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval of appointments and removals.

### 政务院举行第一六九次会议

1953-02-28
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一六九次会议
    【新华社二十七日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百六十九次政务会议在二十七日下午举行。会议听取了中华人民共和国访问波兰文化代表团沈雁冰团长所作的该团工作总结报告。会议并通过了各项任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案。
