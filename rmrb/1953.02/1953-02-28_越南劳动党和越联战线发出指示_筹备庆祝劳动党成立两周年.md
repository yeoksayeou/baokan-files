Title: The Vietnam Workers' Party and the Viet Lien Front Issue Instructions to Prepare for the Celebration of the Second Anniversary of the Founding of the Workers' Party

Author: 
Date: 1953-02-28
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, February 27th: According to the Vietnam News Agency, March 3rd is the second anniversary of the founding of the Vietnam Workers' Party, the Vietnam National United Front, and the Joint National United Front of Vietnam, Cambodia, and Laos. The Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party and the National Committee of the Vietnam National United Front have decided to prepare for the celebration.
    According to the instructions of the Workers' Party and the Vietnam National United Front, on March 3rd, the organizations of the Workers' Party and the Vietnam National United Front at all levels will report to the people on their work in 1952, the implementation of policies, and explain to the people the current situation and the tasks for 1953.

### 越南劳动党和越联战线发出指示  筹备庆祝劳动党成立两周年

1953-02-28
第4版()
专栏：

    越南劳动党和越联战线发出指示
    筹备庆祝劳动党成立两周年
    【新华社二十七日讯】据越南通讯社讯：三月三日是越南劳动党、越南国民联合战线和越南、高棉、寮国三国联合民族统一战线成立两周年纪念日，越南劳动党中央委员会和越南国民联合战线全国委员会已决定筹备庆祝。
    根据劳动党和越南国民联合战线的指示，在三月三日那一天，劳动党和越南国民联合战线的各级组织将向人民报告他们在一九五二年的工作、执行政策的情况并向人民解释目前的形势和一九五三年的任务。
