Title: Soviet Ambassador Panyushkin Holds Reception
Author:
Date: 1953-02-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 14th: Soviet Ambassador to China Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary Panyushkin held a reception in Beijing at 5:00 PM on February 14th, celebrating the third anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance.

Those invited to attend were: Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Li Jishen, and Gao Gang; Secretary-General of the Central People's Government Lin Boqu; Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government Zhou Enlai; Vice Premiers Dong Biwu, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, and Deng Xiaoping; Vice President of the Supreme People's Court Zhang Zhizhiang; Deputy Chief Procurator of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Lan Gongwu; as well as heads of various departments of the Central People's Government, responsible persons of various democratic parties and people's organizations, and Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Peng Zhen, and over five hundred others.

Also invited to attend were diplomatic envoys of various countries to China.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere. Guests frequently toasted to the unbreakable friendship between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union, to the health of the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao Zedong, and to the health of the leader of the world's working people and the best friend of the Chinese people, Chairman Stalin.

### 苏联大使潘友新举行酒会

1953-02-16
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联大使潘友新举行酒会
    【新华社十四日讯】为庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订三周年，苏联驻我国特命全权大使潘友新十四日下午五时在北京举行酒会。
    应邀到会的有：中央人民政府副主席朱德、李济深、高岗，中央人民政府委员会秘书长林伯渠，中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来，副总理董必武、郭沫若、黄炎培、邓小平，最高人民法院副院长张志让，最高人民检察署副检察长蓝公武，以及中央人民政府各部门首长，各民主党派、各人民团体负责人，和北京市人民政府市长彭真等五百多人。
    应邀到会的还有各国驻华使节。
    酒会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。会上宾主频频为中苏两国人民牢不可破的友谊，为中国人民伟大领袖毛泽东主席的健康，为世界劳动人民的领袖、中国人民最好的朋友斯大林主席的健康而干杯。
