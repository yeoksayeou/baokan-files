Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Lays Wreath at Soviet Army Martyrs Monument in Lushun-Dalian / Soviet Armed Forces Command in Lushunkou Region Holds Celebration Banquet
Author:
Date: 1953-02-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Dalian, February 23rd. On the morning of February 23rd, the Soviet Army Day, Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Administration Council of the Central People's Government, representing Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government and the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, extended condolences to the delegation of the Soviet Army stationed in the Lushunkou region and laid a wreath at the Soviet Army Martyrs Monument in Dalian City. Accompanying him were delegation representatives: Xiao Jingguang, Commander of the Navy of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Liu Yalou, Commander of the Air Force, Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Central Military Commission of the Central People's Government, and Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government.

Also participating in the wreath-laying ceremony were: Pan Youxin, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to China, senior generals of the Soviet troops stationed in the Lushunkou region, and Morozov, Consul General of the Soviet Union in Dalian City, and others.

Also participating in the wreath-laying ceremony were: Ouyang Qin, Mayor of Dalian City, Secretary of the Dalian City Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chen Bocun, Second Secretary of the Dalian City Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Qiao Chuanjue, Vice Mayor of Dalian City and President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and others.

Xinhua News Agency, Dalian, February 23rd. The command of the Soviet armed forces stationed in the Lushunkou region held a celebration banquet in the afternoon of February 23rd, the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy.

Invited to attend the banquet were Zhou Enlai, head of the Central People's Government delegation to express condolences to the Soviet troops stationed in the Lushunkou region, representatives Xiao Jingguang, Liu Yalou, Xiao Hua, Wu Xiuquan, and heads of the Dalian municipal party and government Ouyang Qin, Chen Bocun, Qiao Chuanjue, and others.

Also attending the banquet were Pan Youxin, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to China, and Morozov, Consul General of the Soviet Union in Dalian City, and others.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests repeatedly cheered and frequently raised their glasses to toast the close and unbreakable friendship between the people and armed forces of the Soviet Union and China, and to the health of Comrade Stalin and Comrade Mao Zedong.

The banquet proceeded in an atmosphere of close friendship.

A joint Soviet-Chinese soiree performance was held after the banquet.

### 周恩来总理向旅大苏军烈士纪念塔献花圈  苏联驻旅顺口地区武装部队指挥部举行庆祝宴会

1953-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理向旅大苏军烈士纪念塔献花圈
    苏联驻旅顺口地区武装部队指挥部举行庆祝宴会
    【新华社大连二十三日电】中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理在苏军建军节日——二月二十三日上午，代表中央人民政府毛泽东主席和中华人民共和国中央人民政府慰问驻旅顺口地区苏军代表团，向旅大市苏军烈士纪念塔敬献花圈。随同前往的，有代表团代表：中国人民解放军海军司令员萧劲光，空军司令员刘亚楼，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华，中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权。
    参加献花圈仪式的，有：苏联驻我国特命全权大使潘友新，驻旅顺口地区苏军部队高级将领，以及苏联驻旅大市总领事莫罗佐夫等。
    参加献花圈仪式的，还有：旅大市人民政府市长、中国共产党旅大市委员会书记欧阳钦，中国共产党旅大市委员会第二书记陈伯村，旅大市人民政府副市长、中苏友好协会会长乔传珏等。
    【新华社大连二十三日电】苏联驻旅顺口地区的武装部队指挥部，在苏联陆海军建军三十五周年节日——二月二十三日下午举行庆祝宴会。
    应邀出席宴会的有我中央人民政府慰问驻旅顺口地区苏军代表团团长周恩来，代表萧劲光、刘亚楼、萧华、伍修权，旅大市党政负责人欧阳钦、陈伯村、乔传珏等。
    出席宴会的有苏联驻我国特命全权大使潘友新，苏联驻旅大市总领事莫罗佐夫等。
    席间，宾主一再欢呼，频频举杯祝贺苏中两国人民及其武装部队的亲密的牢不可破的友谊，祝贺斯大林同志和毛泽东同志的健康。
    宴会在亲密友好的空气中进行。
    宴会后举行了苏中联欢演出晚会。
