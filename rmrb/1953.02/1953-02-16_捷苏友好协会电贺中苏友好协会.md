Title: Czechoslovak-Soviet Friendship Association Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
Author: 
Date: 1953-02-16
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association headquarters received a congratulatory telegram from the Czechoslovak-Soviet Friendship Association on the third anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance. The telegram said: On behalf of all members of our association, we congratulate you on the third anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance. Thanks to the brotherly and selfless assistance of the Soviet Union, the Chinese people are achieving greater and greater success in the cause of socialist construction and in the cause of striving for peace. Long live the Chinese people!

### 捷苏友好协会电贺中苏友好协会

1953-02-16
第2版()
专栏：

    捷苏友好协会电贺中苏友好协会
    【新华社十五日讯】中苏友好协会总会收到捷苏友好协会祝贺中苏友好同盟互助条约签订三周年的贺电。贺电说：谨代表本会全体会员向你们祝贺中苏友好同盟互助条约签订三周年。中国人民由于苏联兄弟般无私的援助而在社会主义建设的事业中、在争取和平的事业中获得越来越大的成就。中国人民万岁！
