Title: Korean Railway Art Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-02-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 5th. At the invitation of the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government, the entire 131-member "Korean Democratic People's Republic Visiting China Railway Art Troupe," led by the head of the troupe, Li Qingqiu, Deputy Minister of Railways of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, arrived in Beijing on the 5th.

People from all walks of life in the capital and railway employees welcomed the arrival of the Korean Railway Art Troupe with great excitement. Those welcoming at the station included Lu Zhengcao, Vice Minister of Railways of the Central People's Government, and representatives from the Chinese People's Committee for Resisting America and Aiding Korea, the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council, the Political Department of the Ministry of Railways, the Chinese Railway Trade Union and other units, as well as employees from various units of the Beijing Railway Administration, and students from railway worker schools, totaling more than 1,000 people. Also welcoming at the station were many Chinese railway workers who had participated in the War to Resist America and Aid Korea in Korea. Kwon O-jik, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, and embassy staff also came to the station to welcome the delegation.

When the special train carrying the art troupe arrived, cheers erupted at the station. Vice Minister Lu Zhengcao and the welcoming masses stepped forward to express their warm welcome to the Korean Railway Art Troupe, and the young pioneers presented flowers to the members of the art troupe. A grand welcoming ceremony was also held at the station. Vice Minister Lu Zhengcao delivered a welcoming speech, and Head of the Troupe Li Qingqiu delivered a reply. They both believed that this visit by the art troupe would further strengthen the fighting friendship and cultural and artistic exchange and cooperation between the peoples of China and Korea. ### 朝鲜访华铁道艺术团到达北京

1953-02-06
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜访华铁道艺术团到达北京
    【新华社五日讯】应中央人民政府铁道部邀请来我国访问的“朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访华铁道艺术团”全体人员一百三十一人，由团长、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁铁道省副相李青求率领，在五日到达北京。
    首都各界人士和铁路员工以极大的兴奋欢迎朝鲜铁道艺术团的到来。在车站欢迎的有中央人民政府铁道部副部长吕正操和中国人民抗美援朝总会，中央人民政府文化部、外交部、政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局、铁道部政治部，中国铁路工会等单位的代表，以及北京铁路管理局各单位的员工，铁路职工学校学生等一千多人。在车站欢迎的还有曾经赴朝鲜参加抗美援朝工作的我国铁路职工多人。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使权五稷和使馆人员也到车站欢迎。
    当艺术团所乘的专车到达时，车站上激起了一片欢呼声，吕正操副部长和欢迎群众即向前对朝鲜铁道艺术团表示热烈的欢迎，少年儿童队员们也纷纷向艺术团人员献花。车站上还举行了隆重的欢迎仪式。吕正操副部长致欢迎词，李青求团长致答词，他们都认为这次艺术团的访问，将进一步加强中朝两国人民的战斗友谊和文化艺术的交流与合作。（附图片）
