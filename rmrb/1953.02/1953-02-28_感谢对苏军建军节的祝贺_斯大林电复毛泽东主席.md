Title: Stalin Cables Chairman Mao Zedong, Thanking Him for Greetings on Soviet Army Day
Author: 
Date: 1953-02-28
Page: 1

    Thanking Him for Greetings on Soviet Army Day
    Stalin Cables Chairman Mao Zedong
    Xinhua News Agency, February 27th: Comrade J.V. Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Socialist Republics, cabled Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China on the 26th, expressing his gratitude for the telegram congratulating the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy. The reply telegram is as follows:
    To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:
    Please accept my heartfelt thanks, Comrade Chairman, for your congratulations on the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy.
    J. Stalin
    Moscow, February 26, 1953

### 感谢对苏军建军节的祝贺  斯大林电复毛泽东主席

1953-02-28
第1版()
专栏：

    感谢对苏军建军节的祝贺
    斯大林电复毛泽东主席
    【新华社二十七日讯】苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议主席斯大林在二十六日复电我中央人民政府毛泽东主席，对去电祝贺苏联陆海军建军三十五周年表示感谢。复电如下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东同志：
    当苏联陆海军建军三十五周年之际，承蒙祝贺，主席同志，请接受我衷心的谢意。
    约·斯大林
    一九五三年二月二十六日  莫斯科
