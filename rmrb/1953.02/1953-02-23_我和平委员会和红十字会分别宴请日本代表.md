Title: China Peace Committee and Red Cross Host Japanese Representatives Separately
Author:
Date: 1953-02-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 22nd:
    Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, hosted a banquet on the afternoon of the 19th for Hirano Yoshitaro and Hatanaka Masaharu, representatives of the Japan Peace Liaison Committee; Utsuyama Kanzo and Kashima Toshio, representatives of the Japan-China Friendship Association; and Ms. Takara Tomi, who had come to Beijing to participate in the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, along with their staff members Iwamura Michio, Hiragaki Miyoshi, and Sakurai Zenichi. Also invited were Saionji Kinkazu and Nakamura Gan'emon, Japanese representatives to the Vienna World People's Peace Congress. Present as guests were Liu Ningyi, Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, and Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace. The hosts and guests frequently raised their glasses to toast Asian peace and the solidarity and friendship between the Chinese and Japanese people. After the meeting, the Japanese film "A Female Miner," brought by Hirano Yoshitaro, Hatanaka Masaharu, and others, was screened.
    On the same day, Hu Lansheng, Vice President of the Chinese Red Cross Society, hosted a banquet for Shimazu Tadanari, President of the Japanese Red Cross Society, Kudo Tadao, representative of the Japanese Red Cross Society, and Lin Yuichi, Nakamura Masayuki, and Himi Yutaro, staff members of the Japanese Red Cross Society delegation. Present as guests were Wu Yunfu, Lin Shixiao, Ni Feijun, and Ji Feng, representatives of the Chinese Red Cross Society delegation.

### 我和平委员会和红十字会分别宴请日本代表

1953-02-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我和平委员会和红十字会分别宴请日本代表
    【新华社二十二日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，在十九日下午宴请日本和平联络委员会代表平野义太郎、畑中政春，日中友好协会代表内山完造、加岛敏雄和曾来北京参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的高良富女士，以及他们的随员岩村三千夫、平垣美代司、樱井善一等人。被邀请的还有出席维也纳世界人民和平大会的日本代表西园寺公一和中村玩右卫门。出席作陪的有亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会秘书长刘宁一和中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一。宾主双方频频起立为亚洲和平及中日两国人民的团结与友好而干杯。会后放演平野义太郎、畑中政春等带来的日本影片“一个女矿工”。
    同日，中国红十字会副会长胡兰生宴请日本红十字会会长岛津忠承、日本红十字会代表工藤忠夫及日本红十字会代表团工作人员林佑一、中村昌行、冰见佑太郎等人。出席作陪的有中国红十字会代表团代表伍云甫、林士笑、倪斐君、纪锋等人。
