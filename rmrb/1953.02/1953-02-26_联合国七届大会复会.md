Title: UN Seventh General Assembly Resumes
Author:
Date: 1953-02-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New York, February 25th: The second half of the United Nations Seventh General Assembly resumed in New York on the afternoon of February 24th.

The President of the Assembly, Pearson, delivered a short speech at the meeting, which lasted about ten minutes. He read a congratulatory letter from US President Eisenhower to the Assembly and briefly reported on the agenda for the second half of the session.

Pearson announced that the Political Committee would begin its work on February 25th and would continue to discuss the Korean question.

### 联合国七届大会复会

1953-02-26
第1版()
专栏：

    联合国七届大会复会
    【新华社二十五日讯】塔斯社纽约二十五日讯：联合国第七届大会后半期会议已于二月二十四日下午在纽约复会。
    大会主席皮尔逊在历时约十分钟的会议上发表了简短的演说。他宣读了美国总统艾森豪威尔给大会的贺信，并且简单地报告了后半期会议的议程。
    皮尔逊宣布：政治委员会将在二月二十五日开始工作，并将继续讨论朝鲜问题。
