Title: Soviet Military Minister and Naval Minister Issue Orders on the 35th Anniversary of the Founding of the Soviet Army and Navy
Author:
Date: 1953-02-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 23rd: TASS Moscow - Soviet Military Minister (formerly translated as Soviet Army Minister), Soviet Marshal Vasilevsky, issued the following order on February 23rd, the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy:
    Comrades soldiers and sergeants of the Army! Comrades officers, colonels, and generals of the Army!
    Today, Soviet soldiers, together with all the working people of our country, are celebrating the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy.
    The Soviet Army, established by our great leaders Lenin and Stalin under the leadership of the Communist Party, has traversed a glorious fighting path, imprinting its banner with indelible brilliance during the Civil War and the Great Patriotic War.
    Today, as the Soviet people are victoriously carrying out the solemn task of communist construction stipulated by the 19th Party Congress, our army, as before, is reliably defending their constructive labor and vigilantly protecting the national interests of the Soviet Union.
    On this 35th anniversary of the establishment of the Soviet Armed Forces, I salute and congratulate the soldiers, sergeants, officers, colonels, and generals of the Soviet Army.
    I wish all personnel of the Soviet Army further achievements in combat and political training, in raising vigilance, and in strengthening combat readiness.
    To commemorate this festival, I order:
    Today, February 23, 1953, in Moscow, the capital of our motherland, in the capitals of the various Union republics, in Kaliningrad, Lviv, Khabarovsk, Vladivostok, and in the hero cities of Leningrad, Stalingrad, Sevastopol, and Odessa, let twenty salvoes be fired in each city.
    Long live our powerful socialist motherland!
    Long live the heroic Soviet people and their brave armed forces!
    Long live the Soviet government!
    Long live the great Party of Lenin and Stalin, the inspirer and organizer of all our victories!
    Glory to our wise leader and teacher, the genius helmsman, the great Stalin!
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 23rd: TASS Moscow - Soviet Naval Minister Kuznetsov issued the following order on February 23rd, the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy:
    Comrades sailors and sergeants of the Navy! Comrades officers, colonels, and generals of the Navy!
    Today, the Soviet Navy personnel, together with the entire Soviet people, are celebrating the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy.
    The Soviet people are celebrating this festival with new achievements in the economy, culture, and strengthening all aspects of the active defense of our great motherland.
    Naval personnel, executing the instructions of our great leader and teacher Comrade Stalin and the resolutions of the 19th Party Congress, are tirelessly enhancing the combat readiness of warships and troops, vigilantly defending the maritime defenses of our motherland.
    The task of naval soldiers is to continue to persistently increase their military and political knowledge, to continuously improve their combat skills, to master modern equipment, to expand the ranks of personnel with outstanding achievements in combat and political training, to enhance vigilance, and to strengthen military discipline and organization.
    Comrades sailors and sergeants! Comrades officers, colonels, and generals of the Navy!
    On this 35th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy, I salute and congratulate you, and wish you further achievements in combat and political training.
    Long live the Soviet Army and Navy!
    Long live the heroic Soviet people!
    Long live our own Soviet government!
    Long live the great Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the inspirer and organizer of all our victories!
    Long live our dear leader and teacher, the genius commander, the great Stalin!

### 苏联陆海军建军三十五周年纪念日苏联军事部长和海军部长发布命令

1953-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联陆海军建军三十五周年纪念日苏联军事部长和海军部长发布命令
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联军事部（前译苏联陆军部）部长、苏联元帅华西列夫斯基在二十三日苏联陆海军建军三十五周年纪念日发布命令如下：陆军战士和士官同志们！陆军尉官、校官和将军同志们！
    今天，苏联战士同我国所有的劳动人民一起庆祝苏联陆海军建军三十五周年。
    由我们的伟大领袖列宁和斯大林建立起来的苏联军队在共产党的领导下，经历了光荣的战斗道路，在内战时期和在伟大的卫国战争时期，使它的军旗带上了不可磨灭的光辉。
    今天，当苏联人民正在胜利地执行第十九次党代表大会所规定的共产主义建设的庄严任务时，我们的军队，像以前一样，正在可靠地保卫着他们的建设性劳动，并警惕地保护着苏联的国家利益。
    在这苏联武装部队建立三十五周年的时候，我向苏联陆军战士、士官、尉官、校官和将军们致敬和祝贺。
    祝苏联陆军的全体人员在战斗和政治训练方面，在提高警惕和加强战斗准备方面获得进一步的成就。
    为了纪念这个节日，我命令：
    今天，一九五三年二月二十三日，在我们祖国的首都莫斯科，在各加盟共和国的首都，在加里宁格勒、罗夫、伯力、海参崴，在列宁格勒、斯大林格勒、塞瓦斯托波尔和敖德萨等英雄城市，各鸣放礼炮二十响。
    我们强大的社会主义祖国万岁！
    英雄的苏联人民和他们的勇敢的武装部队万岁！
    苏维埃政府万岁！
    我们一切胜利的鼓舞者和组织者——伟大的列宁和斯大林党万岁！
    我们英明的领袖和导师、天才的舵手、伟大的斯大林光荣！
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联海军部部长库兹涅佐夫在二十三日苏联陆海军建军三十五周年纪念日发布命令如下：海军战士和士官同志们！海军尉官、校官和将军同志们！
    今天，苏联海军人员和苏联全体人民一道庆祝苏联陆海军建军三十五周年。
    苏联人民以经济、文化和加强我们伟大祖国积极防御各方面的新成就来庆祝这个节日。
    海军人员们执行着我们伟大的领袖和导师斯大林同志的指示和第十九次党代表大会的决议，不倦地增强着军舰和部队的战斗准备，警觉地捍卫我们祖国的海防。
    海军战士的任务是继续坚持地增加他们的军事和政治知识，使他们的战斗技术日益完善，掌握现代的设备，扩大在战斗与政治训练方面有优异成绩的人员的行列，提高警惕性，加强军纪和组织。
    海军战士和士官同志们！海军尉官、校官和将军同志们！
    在这苏联陆海军建军三十五周年的时候，我向你们致敬和祝贺，祝你们在战斗和政治训练方面获得更进一步的成绩。
    苏联陆海军万岁！
    英雄的苏联人民万岁！
    我们自己的苏维埃政府万岁！
    我们一切胜利的鼓舞者和组织者、伟大的苏联共产党万岁！
    我们亲爱的领袖和导师、天才的统帅、伟大的斯大林万岁！
