Title: Beirut Visits Polish Coal Mining Area
Author:
Date: 1953-02-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 3rd: Bolesław Bierut, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic, recently visited the Silesian coal mining area. He spoke at a gathering of Polish United Workers' Party activists, industrial leaders, and miners in the Katowice coal industry, discussing the increase in Polish coal production and the current tasks of Polish coal miners. He said: In 1947, Poland's coal production was 59 million tons, but in 1952 it reached 84.5 million tons. People's Poland has not only restored its coal mining industry but has also further developed it, investing huge sums of money in this area every year. The output of coal mining equipment has almost tripled compared to 1947. The degree of mechanization in coal mining is also increasing. Bierut's speech was warmly welcomed by the miners.

### 贝鲁特访波兰煤矿区

1953-02-13
第4版()
专栏：

    贝鲁特访波兰煤矿区
    【新华社讯】据塔斯社华沙三日讯：波兰人民共和国部长会议主席波·贝鲁特最近访问了西里西亚煤矿区。他在卡托维兹采煤工业的波兰统一工人党活动分子、工业负责人和矿工的集会上发表了讲话，他谈到了波兰煤产量的增加和波兰煤矿工人当前的任务。他说：一九四七年，波兰煤产量为五千九百万吨，但一九五二年到达了八千四百五十万吨。人民波兰不仅恢复了它的采煤工业，而且进一步发展了它，每年在这方面投下了巨额款项。采煤设备的产量，与一九四七年相比，差不多增加了三倍。采煤机械化的程度也在提高了。贝鲁特的讲话受到了矿工的热烈欢迎。
