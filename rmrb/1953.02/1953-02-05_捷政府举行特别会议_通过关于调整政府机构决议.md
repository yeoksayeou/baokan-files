Title: Czechoslovak Government Holds Special Meeting, Passes Resolution on Adjusting Government Structure
Author:
Date: 1953-02-05
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, February 4th: TASS reports that the Czechoslovak News Agency reported that the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic held a special meeting in Prague on January 31st, chaired by Prime Minister Antonín Zápotocký. At this meeting, in accordance with the Constitution and with the consent of the President of the Republic, Klement Gottwald, a resolution was passed concerning adjustments to the government structure.

    The resolution stipulates the establishment of a government presidium within the new government structure, consisting of the Prime Minister and Deputy Prime Ministers. During recesses of government meetings, the presidium will perform day-to-day work and will regularly guide and supervise the work of government officials. The meeting, in accordance with the provisions of the new government structure, replaced a number of government officials.

    President of the Republic Gottwald has appointed the following six individuals as Deputy Prime Ministers: Minister of National Security Karel Bacílek; Minister of National Defense, Army General Alexej Čepička; Minister of Information and Culture Václav Kopecký; Minister of Education, Science and Arts Zdeněk Nejedlý; Member of the National Assembly Antonín Novotný; and Member of the National Assembly Jindřich Uher. Former Deputy Prime Ministers Viliam Široký, Jaromír Dolanský, and Zdeněk Fierlinger remain in their posts. President Gottwald also relieved Široký of his position as Minister of Foreign Affairs and Fierlinger of his position as Director of the Office for Religious Affairs, appointing Member of the National Assembly Václav David as Minister of Foreign Affairs. The former Chairman of the Administrative Committee of the Slovak National Council, Július Ďuriš, was reassigned as Minister of Forestry and Timber Industry, and the former Judicial Commissioner of the Administrative Committee, Rudolf Strechaj, was reassigned as Chairman of the Administrative Committee. Former Czechoslovak Deputy Prime Minister Josef Krosnář was reassigned as Minister of Building Materials, and the former Minister of Heavy Machine Industry, Július Maurer, was reassigned to a special post with ministerial rank. In addition, the President also appointed several new ministers in accordance with the new government structure.

    The newly appointed government officials were sworn into office on the 2nd.

### 捷政府举行特别会议  通过关于调整政府机构决议

1953-02-05
第4版()
专栏：

    捷政府举行特别会议
    通过关于调整政府机构决议
    【新华社四日讯】据塔斯社布拉格讯：捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道，捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府一月三十一日在布拉格举行特别会议，由总理安托宁·萨波托斯基担任主席。这次会议根据宪法并经共和国总统克·哥特瓦尔德的同意，通过了关于调整政府机构的决议。
    决议规定在新的政府机构内成立一个由总理和副总理组成的政府主席团。在政府会议闭会期间，主席团将执行日常工作，并将经常指导和监督政府官员们的工作。会议根据新的政府机构的规定，更换了若干政府官员。
    共和国总统哥特瓦尔德已任命下列六人为副总理：国家保安部部长卡雷尔·巴契列克；国防部部长陆军上将阿列克谢·契皮茨卡；宣传与文化部部长瓦茨拉夫·柯别茨基；教育科学艺术部部长兹德尼克·尼耶德利；国民议会议员安托宁·诺沃提尼；国民议会议员英德里赫·乌吉尔。前任副总理威廉·西罗基、雅罗米尔·杜兰斯基和兹德尼克·费林格仍然留任。哥特瓦尔德总统并免除了西罗基所任外交部部长和费林格所任宗教事务局局长的职务，任命国民议会议员瓦茨拉夫·戴维为外交部部长。原任斯洛伐克民族议会行政委员会主席尤利乌斯·杜里斯，改任森林与木材工业部部长，原任行政委员会司法委员鲁道夫·斯特列查尔改任行政委员会主席。原任捷克斯洛伐克副总理约瑟夫·基斯里改任建筑材料部部长，原任重机器工业部部长尤利乌斯·摩勒改任部长衔的特别职务。此外，总统还根据新的政府机构的规定任命了新部长数人。
    新任命的政府官员已在二日宣誓就职。
