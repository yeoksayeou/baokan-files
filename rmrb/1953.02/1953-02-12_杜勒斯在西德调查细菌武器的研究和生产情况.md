Title: Dulles Investigates Research and Production of Bacteriological Weapons in West Germany
Author:
Date: 1953-02-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, February 11th: According to a Deutsche Press Agency report from Bonn: During his recent visit to Bonn, in addition to urging the West German puppet government to expedite the ratification of the "European Army" treaty for the re-establishment of the West German army, U.S. Secretary of State Dulles also investigated West Germany's research and production of bacteriological weapons. He studied the documents presented by Adenauer, including the secret report from the Biochemical Institute of the University of Tübingen on the ongoing research of new bacillus culture methods, and the reports from the Farben Company's "Hygiene Institute" in Wuppertal, the Bernhard Nocht Institute in Hamburg, and the Institute for Chemical, Technical, Biological, and Educational Research in Weyerhof near Kirchheim-Bolanden in the Palatinate region. Dulles instructed Adenauer to immediately forward these documents to Dodge, the director of the "American Biological Warfare Committee" office in Munich.

### 杜勒斯在西德调查细菌武器的研究和生产情况

1953-02-12
第4版()
专栏：

    杜勒斯在西德调查细菌武器的研究和生产情况
    【新华社十一日讯】据德意志通讯社波恩消息：美国国务卿杜勒斯在最近访问波恩期间，除督促西德傀儡政府使重建西德军队的“欧洲军”条约迅速获得通过以外，同时还调查了西德关于细菌武器的研究和生产。他研究了阿登纳所提出的文件，包括正在研究新的杆菌培养法的图宾根大学生物化学研究所的秘密报告和窝伯塔尔的法本公司“卫生研究所”的报告，汉堡柏恩哈特·诺赫特研究所的报告，法尔兹区吉尔希海姆·波兰登附近的威厄尔豪夫的化学、技术、生物、教育研究所的报告。杜勒斯指示阿登纳把这些文件立即送交“美国生物战委员会”慕尼黑办事处处长道奇。
