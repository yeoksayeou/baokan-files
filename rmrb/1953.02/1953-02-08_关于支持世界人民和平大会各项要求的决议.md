Title: Resolution on Supporting the Various Demands of the World People's Peace Congress
Author:
Date: 1953-02-08
Page: 1
    
    Resolution on Supporting the Various Demands of the World People's Peace Congress

    The Fourth Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, having heard the report by Deputy Head of the Chinese Delegation to the World People's Peace Congress, Guo Moruo, regarding the proceedings and achievements of the World People's Peace Congress, unanimously expressed its agreement and decided to firmly support the various demands of the World People's Peace Congress.

    This conference calls on the people of the entire nation to unite as one, and to join with international friends, to strive together to realize the World People's Peace Congress's just demands regarding the conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact and other matters.

### 关于支持世界人民和平大会各项要求的决议

1953-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

    关于支持世界人民和平大会各项要求的决议
    中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第四次会议，对出席世界人民和平大会中国代表团郭沫若副团长关于世界人民和平大会的经过和成就的报告，一致表示同意，并决定坚决支持世界人民和平大会的各项要求。
    本会议号召全国人民团结一致，并联合国际友人，为实现世界人民和平大会关于缔结五大国和平公约和其他各项正义要求而共同奋斗。
