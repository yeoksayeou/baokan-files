Title: Korean and Chinese Troops Annihilate Four Enemy Platoons in Shangganling and Other Areas
Author: 
Date: 1953-02-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, February 25th. The Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers annihilated two reinforced platoons of the enemy in the battle in the Shangganling area north of Jinhua on the central Korean front.
	At 2:30 AM on the 17th, a crack night attack team of our army completely annihilated a reinforced platoon of the defending enemy, the ROK (Republic of Korea) 9th Division, in a surprise attack in front of the right side of Shangganling.
	On the night of the 17th and in the following days, the enemy troops continuously harassed our army positions on the left side of Shangganling with forces ranging from a squad to a platoon, but each harassment was repelled by our army. At dusk on the 20th, an enemy platoon stealthily approached within ten meters of our army positions on the left side of Shangganling and was repelled by our army's sudden gunfire. The enemy suffered more than twenty casualties.
	At 1:00 AM on the 22nd, our army's night attack team unexpectedly charged onto an enemy position on the left side of Shangganling. After fifteen minutes of fierce fighting, they annihilated another reinforced platoon of the defending enemy, the ROK 9th Division, and captured one light machine gun and four automatic rifles. In this battle, our army suffered only one minor injury and consumed one hand grenade, twelve grenades, and fifty submachine gun bullets.
	In the vicinity of Yanli, northwest of Wendengli on the eastern front, our army annihilated two platoons of the defending enemy, the ROK 3rd Division, on the 12th and 13th.
	In the six days from February 17th to 22nd, our army's frontline mobile artillery fire killed or wounded a total of 527 enemy soldiers. Our army's frontline anti-aircraft artillery fire shot down eight enemy aircraft and damaged eleven enemy aircraft.

### 朝中部队在上甘岭等地歼敌四个排

1953-02-26
第1版()
专栏：

    朝中部队在上甘岭等地歼敌四个排
    【新华社朝鲜前线二十五日电】朝中人民部队又在朝鲜中部战线金化以北上甘岭地区的战斗中，歼灭了敌人两个加强排。
    十七日二时三十分，我军一支精干的夜袭队在上甘岭右边高地前面进行的袭击战中全歼守敌李伪军第九师一个加强排。
    在十七日晚上和以后的几天中，敌军曾以一个班至一个排的兵力不断向上甘岭左边高地我军阵地窜扰，但每次窜扰都被我军击退。二十日黄昏，敌人一个排偷偷摸到上甘岭左边高地我军阵地前十多公尺处，被我军突发的枪火击退。敌军伤亡了二十多人。
    二十二日一时，我军的夜袭队又出敌不意地冲上了上甘岭左边的一个敌人阵地，激战十五分钟，又歼灭守敌李伪军第九师一个加强排，并缴获轻机枪一挺，自动步枪四支。这次战斗，我军仅轻伤一人，消耗手雷一个，手榴弹十二个，冲锋枪子弹五十发。
    在东线文登里西北边岩里附近，我军在十二、十三两日先后歼灭守敌李伪军第三师两个排。
    二月十七日到二十二日的六天中，我军前线游动炮火共击毙击伤敌军五百二十七名。我军前线高射炮火共击落敌机八架，击伤敌机十一架。
