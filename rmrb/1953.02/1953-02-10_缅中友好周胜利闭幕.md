Title: Sino-Burmese Friendship Week Victoriously Concludes

Author: 
Date: 1953-02-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, February 9th: The Sino-Burmese Friendship Week, which began on January 25th, victoriously concluded on January 31st. During the Sino-Burmese Friendship Week, the Burma-China Friendship Association held five seminars and reports, eight film evenings in Rangoon, and held a New China cultural relics and picture exhibition in five districts of the city. People from all walks of life in Burma enthusiastically participated in the activities of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Week. More than 20,000 citizens visited the exhibition. At various seminars, well-known figures from the cultural, academic, artistic, women's, and youth circles expressed their willingness to work hard to enhance the friendship between the peoples of Burma and China. The famous writer Thakin Kodaw Hmaing, who had visited New China and was over eighty years old, personally participated in various seminars and vividly introduced the situation in New China at the meetings. He said: "For the friendship between the peoples of Burma and China, and for the peace of mankind, I am willing to contribute all my energy."

Dr. Htin Aung, President of the Burma-China Friendship Association, said: "The reason why everyone is participating so enthusiastically in the Sino-Burmese Friendship Week is because they believe that strengthening cultural exchanges between China and Burma and consolidating the friendship between China and Burma is urgently needed at present. The liberated Chinese people have embarked on a happy path; New China is worthy of our research and study. The brilliant achievements of New China in construction have strengthened the confidence of the Asian people in their struggle for peace."

### 缅中友好周胜利闭幕

1953-02-10
第4版()
专栏：

    缅中友好周胜利闭幕
    【新华社九日讯】仰光消息：从一月二十五日开始的缅中友好周已经在一月三十一日胜利闭幕。在缅中友好周期间，缅中友好协会在仰光举行了五次座谈会和报告会、八次电影晚会，并在市内五个地区举行了新中国文物图片展览会。缅甸各界人士热烈地参加了缅中友好周的活动。参观展览会的市民群众有两万多人。在各个座谈会上，文化、学术、艺术、妇女和青年各界知名人士都表示要为增进缅中两国人民的友谊而努力。曾经访问过新中国的八十多岁的名作家德钦哥都迈亲自参加了各个座谈会，并在会上生动地介绍了新中国的情况。他说：“为了缅中两国人民的友谊，为了人类的和平，我愿意贡献我的全部精力。”
    缅中友好协会主席信波博士说：“大家这样热烈地参加缅中友好周，是因为他们认为：加强中缅两国文化的交流和巩固中缅两国的友谊是目前迫切需要的。解放了的中国人民走上了幸福的道路；新中国是值得我们研究和学习的。新中国在建设方面的光辉成就，加强了亚洲人民为和平事业而斗争的信心。”
