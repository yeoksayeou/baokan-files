Title: Soviet Newspapers Pravda, Izvestia, and Red Star Publish Vice Chairman Zhou Enlai's Political Report; Indian Newspapers Publish Summaries of the Report
Author:
Date: 1953-02-09
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, Eighth Dispatch from TASS: Pravda, Izvestia, and Red Star all published Vice Chairman Zhou Enlai's political report, delivered on the 4th at the Fourth Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), in their February 7th editions. The newspapers also reported on the proceedings of the meeting.
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, Sixth Dispatch: Newspapers throughout India prominently featured summaries of the political report delivered by Vice Chairman Zhou Enlai of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) at the Fourth Session of the First National Committee of the CPPCC on February 4th, as their top headlines on the 5th.

### 苏联真理报消息报和红星报  刊载周恩来副主席政治报告  印度各地报纸刊载了报告摘要

1953-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联真理报消息报和红星报
    刊载周恩来副主席政治报告
    印度各地报纸刊载了报告摘要
    【新华社八日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：真理报、消息报、红星报都在七日刊载了中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席周恩来四日在中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第四次会议上所作的政治报告。各报并报道了会议进行的情况。
    【新华社新德里六日电】印度各地报纸在五日都用头条新闻位置，刊载了中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席周恩来四日在中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第四次会议上所作的政治报告的摘要。
