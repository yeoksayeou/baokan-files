Title: Chairman Stalin Receives the Argentine Ambassador
Author: 
Date: 1953-02-09
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 8th: TASS reported from Moscow on the 7th that the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, J.V. Stalin, received the Ambassador of the Argentine Republic, L. Bravo, on the 7th. Present at the reception was the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, A.Y. Vyshinsky.

### 斯大林主席接见阿根廷大使

1953-02-09
第4版()
专栏：

    斯大林主席接见阿根廷大使
    【新华社八日讯】塔斯社莫斯科七日电：苏联部长会议主席约·维·斯大林于七日接见阿根廷共和国大使勒·布拉沃。接见时在座的有苏联外交部部长安·扬·维辛斯基。
