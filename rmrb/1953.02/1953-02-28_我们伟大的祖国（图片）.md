Our Great Motherland
On the front lines of the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea, Chinese women have made many indelible contributions over the past two years, and countless heroes and models have emerged. This is a scene of female cadres of a certain unit of the Chinese People's Volunteers carrying various comfort items and representing the leaders of the unit to visit and comfort the soldiers at the front lines.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-02-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在抗美援朝斗争的前线，我国妇女二年多以来立下了许多不可磨灭的功绩，涌现出无数的功臣和模范。这是志愿军某部的女干部，携带着各种慰问品，代表部队首长到前沿慰问战士的情形。
