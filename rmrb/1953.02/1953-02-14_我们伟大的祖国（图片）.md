Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-02-14
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Today is the third anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance. Over the past three years, thanks to the selfless assistance of the Soviet Union, our country's infrastructure construction has made significant progress. This is the newly installed Soviet-style large-scale turbine generator in the Fuxin Power Plant. Wang Shuren, the head of the turbine division, is inspecting the operation of the generator.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-02-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    今天是中苏友好同盟互助条约签订三周年纪念日。三年来，由于苏联无私的帮助，我国的基本建设有了重大的发展。这是阜新发电厂新安装的苏联最新式的大型透平发电机。透平分场班长王树仁正在检查发电机运转的情形。
