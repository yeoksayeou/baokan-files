Title: "Strive for Lasting Peace, Strive for People's Democracy!" Publishes Chairman Mao's Speech and Vice Chairman Zhou's Report
Author:
Date: 1953-02-19
Page: 1
　
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 18th—The latest issue of "Strive for Lasting Peace, Strive for People's Democracy!" newspaper prominently published the full text of Chairman Mao Zedong's speech at the Fourth Meeting of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Vice Chairman Zhou Enlai's political report at the meeting, and information about the proceedings of the meeting.

"Strive for Lasting Peace, Strive for People's Democracy!" also published an editorial entitled "The Indestructible Friendship of the Great Soviet and Chinese Peoples" to celebrate the third anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance.

### “争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”  刊载毛主席的讲话和周副主席的报告

1953-02-19
第1版()
专栏：

    “争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”
    刊载毛主席的讲话和周副主席的报告
    【新华社布拉格十八日电】　最近一期“争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”报以显著地位刊载了毛泽东主席在中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第四次会议上的讲话、和周恩来副主席在会上作的政治报告全文以及会议的进行情况。
    “争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”并以“伟大的苏联和中国人民的牢不可破的友谊”为题发表社论，庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订三周年。
