Title: Suggest Local People's Broadcasting Stations Relay Live Recordings of Public Trials of Corrupt Elements
Author: Wang Hongjun, Gu Yufen, Tao Cheng
Date: 1952-01-24
Page: 2

Column: Reader's Letters

    Suggest Local People's Broadcasting Stations Relay Live Recordings of Public Trials of Corrupt Elements

    Editor: In order to further and more deeply carry out the movement against corruption, waste, and bureaucracy, we suggest that the People's Broadcasting Stations in all parts of the country relay live broadcasts or recordings of public trials of major corrupt elements. In order to prevent corrupt elements from hiding, dispersing, or transferring stolen goods, we suggest that the relevant units immediately organize guard groups or pickets to monitor the sabotage activities of these corrupt elements.
              Wang Hongjun Gu Yufen Tao Cheng

### 建议各地人民广播电台转播公审贪污分子的实况录音
王鸿钧　顾愈奋　陶城
1952-01-24
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    建议各地人民广播电台转播公审贪污分子的实况录音
    编辑同志：为了更深入地开展反贪污、反浪费、反官僚主义运动，我们建议：全国各地的人民广播电台转播有关公审大贪污分子大会的实况或实况录音。为了防止贪污分子隐藏或分散、转送赃物，我们建议：各有关单位立即组织保卫组或纠察队，以监视这些贪污分子的破坏行动。
              王鸿钧　顾愈奋　陶　城
