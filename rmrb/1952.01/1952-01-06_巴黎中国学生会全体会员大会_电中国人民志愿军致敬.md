Title: All-Member Congress of the Chinese Students Association in Paris Sends Respects to the Chinese People's Volunteers

Author:
Date: 1952-01-06
Page: 4

All-Member Congress of the Chinese Students Association in Paris
Telegram of Respects to the Chinese People's Volunteers

Dear Commanders and Fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteers:

You, the most outstanding sons and daughters of the Chinese nation, the most courageous soldiers, bearing the glorious title of the Chinese People's Volunteers, have appeared on the Korean battlefield, fighting shoulder to shoulder with the Korean People's Army, striking at the brutal invading armies of the imperialist camp, and safeguarding the security of the motherland and world peace. This high degree of patriotism and internationalism constantly inspires us, overseas compatriots. We are determined to actively support the motherland's movement to resist America, aid Korea, defend our homes, and defend our country by working hard to strengthen our own work.

Today, at the All-Member Congress of the Chinese Students Association in Paris, we represent all patriotic students in France and extend to you our highest respects.

		All-Member Congress of the Chinese Students Association in Paris
				Paris, December 9, 1951

### 巴黎中国学生会全体会员大会  电中国人民志愿军致敬

1952-01-06
第4版()
专栏：

    巴黎中国学生会全体会员大会
    电中国人民志愿军致敬亲爱的中国人民志愿军全体指战员：
    你们这一队中华民族最优秀的儿女，最勇敢的战士，以中国人民志愿军的光荣称号，出现在朝鲜战场，和朝鲜人民军并肩作战，打击了帝国主义阵营强暴的侵略军队，保卫了祖国的安全与世界的和平。这种高度的爱国主义和国际主义的精神时时刻刻鼓舞着我们在海外的侨胞。我们决心以努力加强我们自己的工作，来积极支援祖国的抗美援朝保家卫国运动。
    今天，在巴黎中国学生会全体大会上，我们代表全体留法爱国同学，向你们致最崇高的敬礼。
        巴黎中国学生会全体会员大会
                一九五一年十二月九日巴黎
