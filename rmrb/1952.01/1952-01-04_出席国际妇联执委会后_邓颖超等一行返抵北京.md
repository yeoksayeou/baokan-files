Title: Deng Yingchao and Entourage Return to Beijing After Attending the Executive Committee Meeting of the International Democratic Women's Federation
Author: 
Date: 1952-01-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 2nd. Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation and Chinese Executive Committee member of the International Democratic Women's Federation, along with a staff of four, returned to Beijing on the 2nd after attending the Executive Committee meeting of the International Democratic Women's Federation held in Berlin. Yang Zhihua, Luo Shuzhang, Shen Zijiu, Kang Keqing, and Liu Wangliming, standing committee members of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Liu Qingyang, executive committee member, and Zhang Xiaomei, chairman of the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, welcomed them at the station. Dong Yueqian, Director of the International Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was also at the station to welcome them.

### 出席国际妇联执委会后  邓颖超等一行返抵北京

1952-01-04
第4版()
专栏：

    出席国际妇联执委会后
    邓颖超等一行返抵北京
    【新华社二日讯】出席在柏林召开的国际民主妇女联合会执行委员会的中国执行委员、中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超及工作人员一行四人，已在二日回到北京。到车站欢迎的有中华全国民主妇女联合会常务委员杨之华、罗叔章、沈兹九、康克清、刘王立明，执行委员刘清扬，北京市民主妇女联合会主席张晓梅等。外交部国际司司长董越千也到站欢迎。
