Title: Central Council Member of the China Association for Promoting Democracy, Xie Renbing, Passes Away in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1952-01-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, January 30th: Xie Renbing, Central Council Member of the China Association for Promoting Democracy, Director of the Shanghai Branch, Member of the East China Military and Political Committee, Member of the Shanghai People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee, and Manager of the Commercial Press, passed away on the morning of January 27th at 3:00 AM due to a cerebral hemorrhage. Ma Yinchu, Wu Kejian, Pan Hannian, Sheng Pihua, Shen Baowen, Shen Zhiyuan, and others formed a funeral committee. A public memorial service was held at the International Funeral Parlor at 2:00 PM on the 29th, and cremation took place at the Jing'an Temple Cemetery at 4:00 PM.

### 民主促进会中央理事  谢仁冰在上海逝世

1952-01-31
第1版()
专栏：

    民主促进会中央理事
    谢仁冰在上海逝世
    【新华社上海三十日电】中国民主促进会中央理事兼上海市分会主任理事、华东军政委员会委员、上海市各界人民代表会议协商委员会委员、商务印书馆经理谢仁冰患脑出血症，二十七日上午三时逝世。当由马寅初、吴克坚、潘汉年、盛丕华、申葆文、沈志远等组织治丧委员会，于二十九日下午二时在万国殡仪馆举行公祭，并于四时在静安寺公墓火葬。
