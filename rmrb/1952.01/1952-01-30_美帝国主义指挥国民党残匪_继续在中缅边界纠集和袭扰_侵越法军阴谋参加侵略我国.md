Title: American Imperialism Commands Kuomintang Remnant Bandits to Continue Gathering and Harassing on the Sino-Burmese Border; French Aggressor Troops in Vietnam Conspire to Participate in the Invasion of Our Country
Author:
Date: 1952-01-30
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 29th: American imperialism continues to support the Kuomintang remnant bandits in carrying out activities on the Sino-Burmese border, attacking border areas of Yunnan Province. According to a TASS dispatch from Rangoon: Batches of Kuomintang troops, commanded by American officers, pass through the Thai border every day to join the Kuomintang Li Mi bandit troops in the Kengtung (Burma) area.
    A London dispatch in the "Bangkok Post" quoted Burmese official representatives as saying: Kuomintang troops are constantly passing through Thai territory, along the highway to Kengtung, to reinforce the Kuomintang troops in Kengtung. A Burmese spokesman said: 10,000 Kuomintang troops who fled to Burma are using Kengtung as a base to attack border areas of Yunnan Province.
    Li Mi has also gone to Taiwan, Hong Kong, and Bangkok to recruit new soldiers.
    Xinhua News Agency, 29th: According to a TASS dispatch from Paris on the 29th: The Paris "l'Humanité" reported that the French Minister for Relations with the Associated States of Indochina, Letourneau, after arriving in Hanoi, Indochina, on January 27, met with General Valluy, the acting French High Commissioner in Indochina, and General Salan, the Commander-in-Chief of the French forces in Indochina, to discuss plans for invading the People's Republic of China.
    "l'Humanité" also said that Letourneau told the two generals that 2,160 French officers and soldiers would sail from Marseille to Indochina on the cargo ship "Skaugum."

### 美帝国主义指挥国民党残匪  继续在中缅边界纠集和袭扰  侵越法军阴谋参加侵略我国

1952-01-30
第1版()
专栏：

    美帝国主义指挥国民党残匪
    继续在中缅边界纠集和袭扰
    侵越法军阴谋参加侵略我国
    【新华社二十九日讯】美帝国主义继续支持国民党残匪在中缅边界进行活动，袭击我云南省边界地区。据塔斯社仰光讯：由美国军官指挥的成批的国民党军队，每日通过泰国国境前往参加景栋（缅甸）地方的国民党李弥匪军。
    “曼谷邮报”上的伦敦消息引证缅甸官方代表的话说：国民党军不断地通过泰国领土，沿着通往景栋的公路前往增援景栋的国民党军队。缅甸发言人说：一万名逃往缅甸的国民党军正以景栋作基地，袭击云南省边界地区。
    李弥并曾赴台湾、香港和曼谷招募新兵。
    【新华社二十九日讯】据塔斯社巴黎二十九日电：巴黎“人道报”报道：法国印度支那联邦成员国关系部长勒都尔诺在一月二十七日到达印度支那的河内以后，曾与法国代理驻印度支那高级专员瓦吕将军和驻印度支那法军总司令萨兰将军会谈，讨论侵略中华人民共和国的计划。
    “人道报”并说，勒都尔诺曾告诉这两个将军：法军官兵二千一百六十人，将从马赛乘“斯科根号”货船开往印度支那。
