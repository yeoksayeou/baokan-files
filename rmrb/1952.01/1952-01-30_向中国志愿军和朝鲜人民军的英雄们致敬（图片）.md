Title: Saluting the Heroes of the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean People's Army (Pictures)
Author: Lin Yang Liu Feng Meng Zhaoduan Qi Guanshan Liu Dong'ao Cao Xinghua Que Wen
Date: 1952-01-30
Page: 5

Column:

　　Saluting the Heroes of the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean People's Army
    Commander Peng Dehuai personally came to see off the returning delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteers before they left Korea.
    On January 23rd, Vice Chairman Zhu De and Premier Zhou Enlai met with all the representatives of the returning delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteers and the visiting delegation of the Korean people at Huairen Hall. This is the scene of the representatives warmly applauding when Vice Chairman Zhu De and Premier Zhou Enlai entered the venue.
    The returning delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteers and the visiting delegation of the Korean people, a total of 270 people, were warmly welcomed by representatives from all walks of life in the capital when they arrived in Beijing on January 18th. This is the scene of Li Xuesan, head of the returning delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteers, leading all the representatives out of the station amidst the applause of the welcoming crowd.
    Li Xuesan (left), head of the returning delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteers, and Hong Chunzhe (right), head of the visiting delegation of the Korean people, accepted flowers from members of the Young Pioneers at the Beijing Railway Station.
    Li Xuesan, head of the returning delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteers, read out Commander Peng Dehuai's letter of thanks to the people of the whole country at the welcome meeting for all circles of people in Beijing.
    Chen Yan (left), representative of the returning delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteers and model telegraph operator, and Xu Jinyu (right), representative of the visiting delegation of the Korean people and model medical worker, extended warm greetings to the wounded and sick soldiers. They are reading Commander Peng Dehuai's letter of sympathy to the wounded and sick soldiers.
    Representatives of the returning delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteers and the visiting delegation of the Korean people visited the family of martyr Zhang Zongpu in Xiaohongmen Village, District 11, Beijing. Martyr Zhang Zongpu gloriously sacrificed his life in the battle to break through the 38th parallel.
    Representatives of the returning delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteers and the visiting delegation of the Korean people visited the family of Zhao Guangbin, a special-class meritorious hero on the Korean front, in District 13, Beijing.
    At the welcome meeting for all circles of people in Beijing for the returning delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteers and the visiting delegation of the Korean people, the working masses enthusiastically requested the Korean representatives to sign their names as souvenirs.
    The people of Xiaohongmen Village, District 11, Beijing, held a report meeting to welcome the returning delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteers and the visiting delegation of the Korean people. The Korean representatives reported on the heroic deeds of the Volunteers and the Korean People's Army at the meeting.
    The Chinese people love the heroic sons and daughters of the motherland, and also love the heroic friends of Korea. The members of the Chinese Young Pioneers, in order to express their respect and sympathy for the heroic Korean friends, used their pens to express their mutual friendship with the female heroes of the visiting delegation of the Korean people.
    The representatives of the returning delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteers and the visiting delegation of the Korean people attended the welcome meeting of all teachers and students of Peking University, and the representatives reported on the heroic deeds on the front lines at the meeting. After the report, the students enthusiastically saw the representatives off to the bus and shook hands tightly with the representatives to say goodbye.
    The representatives of the returning delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteers and the visiting delegation of the Korean people were warmly welcomed by all the teachers and students at Yenching University. The students felt especially close when they saw them, and everyone happily lifted the representatives high. ★ The photos in this issue of the pictorial were taken by Lin Yang, Liu Feng, and Meng Zhaoduan of the People's Liberation Army Pictorial, Qi Guanshan and Liu Dong'ao of the News Photography Bureau, Cao Xinghua, and Que Wen of this newspaper ★

### 向中国志愿军和朝鲜人民军的英雄们致敬（图片）
林扬  刘峰  孟昭端  齐观山  刘东鳌  曹兴华  阙文
1952-01-30
第5版()
专栏：

　　向中国志愿军和朝鲜人民军的英雄们致敬
    中国人民志愿军归国代表团在离开朝鲜归国前，彭德怀司令员亲自来欢送他们。
    一月二十三日，朱德副主席、周恩来总理在怀仁堂接见了中国人民志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团的全体代表们。这是朱德副主席和周恩来总理走进会场时，代表们热烈鼓掌欢迎的情形。
    中国人民志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团一行二百七十人，在一月十八日到达北京时，受到首都各界人民代表的热烈欢迎。这是中国人民志愿军归国代表团团长李雪三率领全体代表在群众欢迎的掌声中走出车站时的情形。
    中国人民志愿军归国代表团团长李雪三（左）、朝鲜人民访华代表团团长洪淳哲（右）等，在北京车站接受了少年儿童队队员的献花。
    中国人民志愿军归国代表团团长李雪三在北京市各界人民欢迎大会上，向参加大会的代表们宣读彭德怀司令员给全国人民的答谢词。
    中国人民志愿军归国代表团代表、模范报务工作者陈炎（左）和朝鲜人民访华代表团代表、模范医务工作者许金玉（右），向伤病员致热烈的慰问。她们正在给伤病员读彭德怀司令员的慰问信。
    中国人民志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团的代表们、慰问了北京市十一区小红门村张宗普烈士的家属。张宗普烈士是在突破三八线战役时光荣牺牲的。
    中国人民志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团的代表们，到北京市十三区慰问了在朝鲜前线的特等功臣赵光斌的家属。
    在北京市各界人民欢迎中国人民志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团大会上，工人群众热烈地要求朝鲜代表给他们签名留念。
    北京市十一区小红门村人民群众举行了欢迎中国人民志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团报告大会。朝鲜代表在大会上向群众报告了志愿军和朝鲜人民军的英雄事迹。
    中国人民热爱祖国的英雄子弟，也热爱朝鲜的英雄友人。中国少年儿童队队员们，为了表示对朝鲜英雄友人的尊敬和慰问，就和朝鲜人民访华代表团的女英雄用笔来表达相互间的友情。
    中国人民志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团的代表们参加了北京大学全体师生的欢迎大会，代表们并在大会上报告了前线的英雄事迹。报告结束后，同学们热烈地欢送代表们上车，并和代表们紧紧地握手道别。
    中国人民志愿军归国代表团代表和朝鲜人民访华代表团代表在燕京大学受到了全体师生的热烈欢迎。同学们看见他们就感到特别亲切，大家都高兴得把代表们高高举起。★　本期画刊照片是由解放军画报社林扬、刘峰、孟昭端，新闻摄影局齐观山、刘东鳌、曹兴华和本报阙文摄　★
