Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-01-04
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The Chongqing to Neijiang section of the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway was opened to traffic on December 7th of last year. 100,000 people in Neijiang participated in the grand opening ceremony. The Young Pioneers of Chongqing also brought flowers and banners to Neijiang to present to the railway workers. This is the scene when they departed.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-01-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    成渝铁路重庆至内江段已于去年十二月七日通车。内江有十万人参加盛大的通车典礼。重庆市的少年儿童队员也带着鲜花和锦旗到内江去献给铁路工作人员。这是他们出发时的情景。
