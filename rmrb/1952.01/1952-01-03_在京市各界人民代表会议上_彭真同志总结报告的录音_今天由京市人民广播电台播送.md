Title: Recording of Comrade Peng Zhen's Summary Report at the Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference to be Broadcast Today by Beijing People's Broadcasting Station
Author: 
Date: 1952-01-03
Page: 1

    At the Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference
    Recording of Comrade Peng Zhen's Summary Report
    To be Broadcast Today by Beijing People's Broadcasting Station
    [This newspaper reports] The Beijing People's Broadcasting Station has decided to broadcast the recording of Chairman Peng Zhen of the Beijing Municipal Consultative Committee's summary report on increasing production and practicing economy, opposing corruption, opposing waste, and opposing bureaucracy at the Third Session of the Third Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference today (the 3rd) from 12:30 to 13:35. Citizens from all walks of life are encouraged to organize listening at the scheduled time. The station's wavelengths are 850 kHz and 1080 kHz. Stations 2, 3, and 4 of the station will broadcast simultaneously on 750 kHz, 920 kHz, and 1320 kHz.
    Tomorrow (the 4th) morning from 8:15 to 9:20, the station will rebroadcast it once.

### 在京市各界人民代表会议上  彭真同志总结报告的录音  今天由京市人民广播电台播送

1952-01-03
第1版()
专栏：

    在京市各界人民代表会议上
    彭真同志总结报告的录音
    今天由京市人民广播电台播送
    【本报讯】北京市人民广播电台决定在今天（三日）十二时三十分至十三时三十五分，播送北京市协商委员会彭真主席在北京市第三届第三次各界人民代表会议上关于增产节约，反对贪污，反对浪费，反对官僚主义问题的总结报告的录音，希望各界市民准时组织收听。该台波长为八五○千周和一○八○千周，该台二、三、四台用七五○千周，九二○千周，一三二○千周同时联播。
    明（四）日上午八时十五分至九时二十分，该台将重播一次。
