Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-01-19
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation and the Korean People's Delegation to China arrived in the capital yesterday. The people of the whole country are welcoming the heroic sons and daughters of the motherland and the heroic Korean friends from the Korean front with reverence. This is a scene of Li Xuesan, head of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation, leading the entire delegation arriving at Beijing Railway Station and being warmly welcomed by representatives from all walks of life.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-01-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国人民志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团已于昨日到达首都。全国人民正以崇敬的心情欢迎来自朝鲜前线的祖国英雄的儿女和英雄的朝鲜友人。这是中国人民志愿军归国代表团团长李雪三率领全团到达北京车站时受到各界代表热烈欢迎的情形。
