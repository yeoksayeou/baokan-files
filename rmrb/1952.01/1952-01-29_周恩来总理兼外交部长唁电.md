Title: Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Sends Condolences
Author:
Date: 1952-01-29
Page: 1

To Comrade Lamsuren, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic:

Deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Marshal Choibalsan, the great leader of the Mongolian people and the great friend of the Chinese people—Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic. I extend my deepest condolences to you, the government of the Mongolian People's Republic, and the Mongolian people. Marshal Choibalsan's immortal contributions to the creation of the Mongolian People's Republic, the opposition to imperialism, and the cause of defending peace will forever live in the hearts of peace-loving people around the world.

　　Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of The People's Republic of China
　　January 27, 1952, in Beijing

### 周恩来总理兼外交部长唁电

1952-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理兼外交部长唁电蒙古人民共和国外交部部长拉姆苏伦同志：惊悉蒙古人民伟大的领袖，中国人民伟大的朋友——蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席乔巴山元帅不幸逝世，谨向您、蒙古人民共和国政府和蒙古人民致深切的哀悼。乔巴山元帅为创建蒙古人民共和国、反对帝国主义、保卫和平事业中的不朽功勋将永远活在全世界爱好和平人民的心中。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长　周恩来
    一九五二年一月二十七日于北京
