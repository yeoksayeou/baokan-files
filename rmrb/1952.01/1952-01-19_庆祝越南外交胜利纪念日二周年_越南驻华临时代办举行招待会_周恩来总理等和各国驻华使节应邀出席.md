Title: Vietnamese Chargé d'Affaires in China Holds Reception to Celebrate the Second Anniversary of the Victory of Vietnamese Diplomacy; Premier Zhou Enlai and Other Heads of Diplomatic Missions in China Invited to Attend
Author:
Date: 1952-01-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 18th:

To celebrate the second anniversary of the victory of Vietnamese diplomacy, the Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Representation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, Zhou Liang, held a reception in Beijing on the evening of the 18th.

Those invited to attend included Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Guo Moruo of the State Council of the Central People's Government, President of the Supreme People's Court Shen Junru, Deputy Director of the General Office of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Zhu Zaoguan, heads of various departments of the Central People's Government and leaders of people's organizations Li Lisan, Shen Yanbing, Ma Xulun, Zhang Hanfu, Yao Yilin, Zeng Zhaolun, Yu Xinqing, Feng Wenbin, and Deputy Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, totaling more than 130 people.

Also invited to attend were the heads of diplomatic missions in China from various countries, including Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, Czechoslovak Ambassador Weiskopf, Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic König, Mongolian Ambassador Jalkhasaikhan, Hungarian Ambassador Saffronko, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Burmese Ambassador U Law Maung, Pakistani Ambassador Raza, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy Isha Mahdi, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy Do Ding, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indian Embassy Kour, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Korean Embassy Choi Young, and diplomatic personnel from various embassies.

The reception was held in a friendly atmosphere.

### 庆祝越南外交胜利纪念日二周年  越南驻华临时代办举行招待会  周恩来总理等和各国驻华使节应邀出席

1952-01-19
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝越南外交胜利纪念日二周年
    越南驻华临时代办举行招待会
    周恩来总理等和各国驻华使节应邀出席
    【新华社十八日讯】越南民主共和国驻我国代表团临时代办周亮为庆祝越南民主共和国外交胜利纪念日二周年，十八日晚在北京举行招待会。
    应邀出席的有中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来、副总理郭沫若，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，人民革命军事委员会办公厅副主任朱早观，中央人民政府各部门首长和人民团体负责人李立三、沈雁冰、马叙伦、章汉夫、姚依林、曾昭抡、余心清、冯文彬及北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等一百三十多人。
    应邀出席的各国驻华使节有苏联大使罗申、捷克斯洛伐克大使魏斯柯普夫、罗马尼亚大使鲁登科、德意志民主共和国外交使团团长柯尼希、蒙古大使贾尔卡赛汗、匈牙利大使夏法朗柯、保加利亚大使彼得科夫、缅甸大使吴拉茂、巴基斯坦大使罗查、印度尼西亚大使馆临时代办伊沙·玛蒂、波兰大使馆临时代办道定、印度大使馆临时代办高尔、朝鲜大使馆临时代办崔英及各使馆外交人员等。
    招待会在友好的气氛中进行。
