Title: Telegram of Condolence from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1952-01-29
Page: 1

To the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, dear comrades:

    We are deeply saddened to learn of the passing of Comrade Choibalsan, the leader of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the Mongolian working people, the creator of the Mongolian People's Republic, and a good friend of the Chinese people. We extend our deepest condolences.

    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
    January 27, 1952

### 中国共产党中央委员会唁电

1952-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中国共产党中央委员会唁电蒙古人民革命党中央委员会亲爱的同志们：
    惊悉蒙古人民革命党和蒙古劳动人民的领袖，蒙古人民共和国的创造者和中国人民的良友乔巴山同志逝世，谨致沉痛的哀悼。
    中国共产党中央委员会
    一九五二年一月二十七日
