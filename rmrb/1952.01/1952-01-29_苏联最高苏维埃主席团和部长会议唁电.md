Title: Condolences from the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet and the Council of Ministers of the USSR

Author:

Date: 1952-01-29
Page: 1

Condolences from the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet and the Council of Ministers of the USSR to the Presidium of the Great People's Khural and the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic:

    The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR and the Council of Ministers of the USSR, upon hearing the news of the death of Marshal Choibalsan, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, express their deepest condolences to the government and people of the Mongolian People's Republic.

    Comrade Choibalsan was a loyal friend of the Soviet people and a fervent fighter for the development and consolidation of the friendship between the Mongolian People's Republic and the Soviet Union, a firm guarantee of the freedom and independence of the Mongolian people.

    The people of all nationalities of the Soviet Union, together with the Mongolian people, deeply mourn the death of Marshal Choibalsan, the glorious leader of the Mongolian people and the indomitable founder of the people's democratic Mongolian state.

    The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, the Council of Ministers of the USSR

### 苏联最高苏维埃主席团和部长会议唁电

1952-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃主席团和部长会议唁电蒙古人民共和国大呼拉尔主席团和部长会议：
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团和苏联部长会议闻悉蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席乔巴山元帅逝世噩耗，特向蒙古人民共和国政府和人民致深切吊唁。
    乔巴山同志是苏联人民的忠诚的朋友，是发展和巩固蒙古人民共和国与苏联之间的友谊的热诚的战士，这种友谊是保障蒙古人民自由和独立的牢固的保证。
    苏联各族人民和蒙古人民一同对蒙古人民的光荣领袖、人民民主的蒙古国家的不屈不挠的缔造者乔巴山元帅的逝世深感哀悼。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团、苏联部长会议
