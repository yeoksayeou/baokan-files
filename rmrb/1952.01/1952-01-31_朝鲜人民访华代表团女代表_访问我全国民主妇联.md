Title: Female Representatives of the Korean People's Delegation to China Visit the All-China Democratic Women's Federation
Author: 
Date: 1952-01-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 30th. Female representatives of the Korean People's Delegation to China visited the All-China Democratic Women's Federation on the 29th. Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, Li Dequan, and heads of various departments attended the reception.

	Deng Yingchao first extended a warm welcome on behalf of the All-China Women's Federation and women across the country to the outstanding representatives of Korean women—the female representatives of the Korean People's Delegation to China—and expressed the highest respect to the Korean people and soldiers and Korean women who are fighting bravely for the independence of their motherland. Deng Yingchao then introduced the situation and main experiences of Chinese women's participation in the anti-corruption, anti-waste, and anti-bureaucracy movement, the implementation of the Marriage Law, and participation in various work such as study and production. At the reception, Li Dequan introduced specific examples of Chinese women's participation in the Resist America, Aid Korea movement and the work of women in minority areas. Finally, Kang Keqing, Minister of Children's Welfare Department of the All-China Women's Federation, and Zeng Xianzhi, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Women's Federation, respectively introduced the situation of China's children's welfare work and the preferential treatment work for families of revolutionary martyrs and military personnel to the Korean female representatives.

	The reception was conducted in a cordial and friendly atmosphere. The female representatives of the Korean People's Delegation to China stated that they would bring back the valuable experience of Chinese women's work to Korea.

### 朝鲜人民访华代表团女代表  访问我全国民主妇联

1952-01-31
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民访华代表团女代表
    访问我全国民主妇联
    【新华社三十日讯】朝鲜人民访华代表团的女代表在二十九日访问了中华全国民主妇女联合会。全国妇联副主席邓颖超、李德全和各部负责人都出席招待。
    邓颖超首先代表全国妇联和全国妇女向朝鲜妇女的优秀代表——朝鲜人民访华代表团的全体女代表表示热烈的欢迎，向为祖国的独立而英勇战斗的朝鲜军民和朝鲜妇女表示崇高的敬意。接着邓颖超介绍了中国妇女参加反贪污、反浪费、反官僚主义运动，贯彻婚姻法以及参加学习、生产等各种工作的情况和主要的经验。李德全在招待会上介绍了中国妇女参加抗美援朝运动的具体事例和少数民族地区的妇女工作。最后全国妇联儿童福利部部长康克清和全国妇联副秘书长曾宪植分别向朝鲜女代表介绍了中国的儿童福利工作和烈属、军属优抚工作的情况。
    招待会在亲密友好的气氛中进行。朝鲜人民访华代表团女代表表示要把中国妇女工作的宝贵经验带回到朝鲜去。
