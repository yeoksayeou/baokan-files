    Our Great Motherland
    The great Chinese People's Volunteers are not only a heroic fighting force, but also a model of cherishing and saving the motherland's resources. This spirit of the Volunteers will be a profound education for the people of the whole country and will inspire the people of the whole country to more resolutely carry out the great struggle against corruption, waste, and bureaucracy. This is a picture of soldiers of a certain unit of the Volunteers making a thrift plan at the front.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-01-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    伟大的中国人民志愿军不但是一支英雄的战斗部队，而且是爱护和节约祖国物资的模范。志愿军的这种精神对于全国人民将是一种深刻的教育，将会鼓舞全国人民更坚决地进行反贪污、反浪费、反官僚主义的伟大斗争。这是志愿军某部战士正在前线订立节约计划的情形。
