Title: Tianjin and Guangzhou Actively Carry Out Activities to Support the Army and Give Preferential Treatment to Families of Servicemen

Author: 
Date: 1952-01-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency report:

    Tianjin and Guangzhou Actively Carry Out Activities to Support the Army and Give Preferential Treatment to Families of Servicemen
    [Our Newspaper News] As the Spring Festival approaches, Tianjin and Guangzhou are actively carrying out work to support the army and give preferential treatment to families of servicemen. Huang Jing, Mayor of Tianjin, Zhou Shutao, Deputy Mayor, and the Tianjin Municipal People's Circles Comfort Group wrote letters to express their solicitude to the Chinese People's Volunteers and the People's Liberation Army wounded and sick soldiers, revolutionary disabled soldiers, families of revolutionary martyrs, families of revolutionary servicemen, and families of revolutionary workers, and called on them to actively participate in the struggle against corruption, waste, and bureaucracy. Deputy Mayor Zhou Shutao, Chairman of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions Huang Huoqing, and model workers, etc., carried comfort letters and comfort goods to visit the wounded and sick soldiers of the Volunteers. Tianjin also plans to conduct a comprehensive inspection of preferential treatment work after the Spring Festival.
    The Guangzhou Municipal People's Government will send Spring Festival congratulatory letters to the garrison troops, and the Municipal People's Government Committee, the Municipal People's Representative Conference, and the Municipal Consultative Committee will form a comfort team to visit wounded and sick soldiers and revolutionary disabled soldiers at hospitals and revolutionary disabled soldier schools, and issue comfort money. The Municipal People's Government also allocated a portion of grain and funds as Spring Festival subsidies for families of martyrs and families of servicemen.
    Tianjin and Guangzhou will hold joint Spring Festival celebration forums for families of martyrs, families of servicemen, and families of workers during the Spring Festival.

### 天津和广州两市积极展开拥军优属

1952-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    天津和广州两市
    积极展开拥军优属
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：春节将临，天津和广州两市积极展开拥军优属工作。天津市人民政府市长黄敬、副市长周叔韬和天津市各界慰问团分别写信慰问人民志愿军和人民解放军伤病员、革命残废军人、革命烈士家属、革命军人家属、革命工作人员家属，并号召他们积极参加反贪污、反浪费、反官僚主义斗争。周叔韬副市长、市总工会黄火青主席和劳动模范等，携带慰问信和慰问品，慰问志愿军伤病员。天津市并计划在春节后普遍检查一次优抚工作。
    广州市人民政府将发出贺春节信慰问驻军，并由市人民政府委员会、市各界人民代表会议、市协商委员会组成慰问队，分赴医院、革命残废军人学校向伤病员、革命残废军人进行慰问，和发给慰问金。市人民政府并拨出一部分粮款，作为对烈属、军属的春节补助。
    天津和广州两市都将在春节期间举行烈属、军属、工属联欢座谈会。
