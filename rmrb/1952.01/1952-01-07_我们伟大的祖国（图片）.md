Author:
Date: 1952-01-07
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    This is Li Xikui, a shunting worker at the Shenyang Station of the China Changchun Railway Administration. He is shunting vehicles. The shunting method he created can improve the efficiency of shunting operations and shorten the dwell and transfer time of vehicles at the station. Currently, shunting personnel at major railway stations across the country are widely learning and applying this new shunting method.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-01-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    这是中国长春铁路管理局沈阳站调车员李锡奎，他正在调动车辆。他所创造的调车法，可以提高调车工作效率，缩短车辆在车站的停留时间和中转时间。目前，全国铁路各主要车站的调车工作人员，都在广泛地学习和运用着这个新的调车法。
