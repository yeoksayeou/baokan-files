    Our Great Motherland
    The People's Liberation Army soldiers stationed in Xinjiang are participating in large-scale production and construction work with great patriotic enthusiasm. This is Comrade Zhang Diyuan, a female tractor driver of the People's Liberation Army. She is happily driving a tractor at a farm directly under the Xinjiang Military Region.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    驻新疆的人民解放军战士们，正以伟大的爱国主义热情，参加大规模的生产建设工作。这是解放军的女拖拉机手张迪源同志，她正在新疆军区直属农场上愉快地驾驶着拖拉机。
