Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions and Trade Unions of Various Countries Exchange New Year Greetings
Author:
Date: 1952-01-03
Page: 4

Telegram of Greetings from the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions
To the All-China Federation of Trade Unions:

    On the occasion of the New Year, we extend our warm congratulations to you. We wish the working people of the People's Republic of China greater achievements in building a New Democratic China and in the struggle to defend and consolidate lasting peace.
           All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions
        December 31st

Telegram of Greetings from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea
To the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea:

    On the occasion of New Year's Day 1952, we extend our warm congratulations to all the working people of Korea. The working classes of our two countries, China and Korea, must unite more closely to win greater victories in our common great cause of opposing the aggression of American imperialism and protecting peace in Asia and the world.
                    All-China Federation of Trade Unions
                    December 31st, 1951

Telegram of Greetings from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to the Vietnam General Confederation of Labour
To the Vietnam General Confederation of Labour:

    On the occasion of New Year's Day 1952, we extend our warm congratulations to the Vietnamese working class and all the people. We wish you brilliant victories in the struggle against imperialist wars of aggression and in defending peace in Asia and the world.
            All-China Federation of Trade Unions
         December 31st, 1951

Telegram of Greetings from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to the Trade Unions of Mongolia
To the Central Council of Trade Unions of Mongolia:

    On the occasion of New Year's Day 1952, we extend our warm congratulations to all the working people of Mongolia. We wish you further achievements in economic construction and in the cause of defending world peace.
          All-China Federation of Trade Unions
        December 31st, 1951

Telegram of Greetings from the Food and Tobacco Workers' International to the National Committee of the Food Industry Workers' Union
To Comrade Yuan Zhihe, Chairman of the China Food Industry Workers' Union:

    We extend brotherly New Year's greetings to the food workers of China and wish you new achievements.
    Dietchev, General Secretary of the Food and Tobacco Workers' International
                    December 29th, 1951

Telegram of Greetings from the Chinese Construction Workers' Union to the Building Workers' International
    Dear Comrades: On the occasion of the New Year 1952, on behalf of the Chinese construction workers, we extend our warm congratulations to our trade union international and the construction worker brothers around the world under its leadership, and wish them more brilliant achievements in strengthening the indestructible friendship and unity among the working people of the world and in defending world peace.
                    National Committee of the Chinese Construction Workers' Union
                                   January 1st

Telegram of Greetings from the Railway Workers' Union of China to the Land and Air Transport Workers' International
To Comrade Troa, Chairman, and Comrade Moraru, General Secretary of the Land and Air Transport Workers' International:

    On the occasion of New Year's Day 1952, on behalf of the Chinese railway workers, we extend our warm congratulations to you and wish our trade union international greater achievements in uniting land and air transport workers around the world and in the cause of defending the vital interests of the working class and lasting world peace.
        Li Jiebo, Chairman of the National Committee of the Railway Workers' Union of China
                                    January 1st

Telegram of Greetings from the Preparatory Committee of the Chemical Workers' Union of China to the Chemical Workers' International
Dear Comrades:

    On the occasion of the New Year 1952, on behalf of the Chinese chemical workers, we extend our heartfelt congratulations to the trade union international, and wish our trade union international more glorious achievements in safeguarding the social and economic interests of chemical workers around the world, strengthening international workers' solidarity, and striving for lasting world peace.
            Jiang Tao, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Chemical Workers' Union of China

Telegram of Greetings from the General Confederation of Labour of Romania to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions
To the All-China Federation of Trade Unions:

    On the occasion of the New Year, we extend our warmest fraternal congratulations to you. The workers of the Romanian People's Republic are watching with great pleasure the achievements of the great Chinese people in building a New China. We hope that 1952 will bring new achievements to the workers of the People's Republic of China in the struggle to defend peace in the Far East and to strengthen the great camp of peace, democracy, and socialism headed by the great Soviet Union.
    Apostol, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the General Confederation of Labour of the Romanian People's Republic
                December 29th, 1951

### 全国总工会和各国工会互贺新年

1952-01-03
第4版()
专栏：

    全国总工会和各国工会互贺新年
    全苏工会中央理事会给我全国总工会的贺电中华全国总工会：
    值此新年之际，我们特向你们致以热烈的祝贺。祝中华人民共和国的劳动人民在建设新民主主义中国及保卫和巩固持久和平的斗争中获得更伟大的成就。
           全苏工会中央理事会
        十二月三十一日
    我全国总工会给朝鲜职业总同盟的贺电朝鲜职业总同盟：
    欣逢一九五二年元旦，谨向朝鲜全体劳动人民致热烈的祝贺。我们中朝两国的工人阶级要更加亲密的团结一致，使我们共同反对美帝国主义的侵略、保护亚洲与世界和平的伟大事业获得更大的胜利。
                    中华全国总工会
                    一九五一年十二月三十一日
    我全国总工会给越南总工会的贺电越南总工会：
    欣逢一九五二年元旦，谨向越南工人阶级和全体人民致热烈的祝贺。祝你们在反对帝国主义侵略战争和保卫亚洲与世界和平的斗争中获得辉煌的胜利。
            中华全国总工会
         一九五一年十二月三十一日
    我全国总工会给蒙古工会的贺电蒙古工会中央理事会：
    欣逢一九五二年元旦，谨向蒙古全体劳动人民致热烈的祝贺。祝你们在经济建设和保卫世界和平的事业中获得进一步的成就。
          中华全国总工会
        一九五一年十二月三十一日
    食品、烟草工人工会国际给我食品工业工会全国委员会的贺电中国食品工业工会主席袁致和同志：
    谨向中国食品工人致以兄弟般的新年祝贺，并祝你们获得新的成就。
    食品、烟草工人工会国际总书记  迪契也夫
                    一九五一年十二月二十九日
    我建筑工会给建筑工人工会国际的贺电
    亲爱的同志们：值此一九五二年新年之际，谨代表中国建筑工人向我们工会国际及其领导下的全世界建筑工人兄弟们致以热烈的祝贺，并预祝在加强全世界劳动人民之间不可摧毁的友谊与团结中以及保卫世界和平中赢得更辉煌的成就。
                    中国建筑工会全国委员会
                                   元月一日
     我国铁路工会给陆空运输工人工会国际的贺电陆空运输工人工会国际主席特罗阿总书记莫拉鲁同志：
    欣逢一九五二年元旦，谨代表中国铁路员工向你们致以热烈的祝贺，并祝我们的工会国际在团结全世界陆空运输工人、为保卫工人阶级切身利益及世界持久和平的事业中获得更伟大的成就。
        中国铁路工会全国委员会主席李颉伯
                                    元月一日
    我化学工会筹委会给化学工人工会国际的贺电亲爱的同志们：
    值此一九五二年新年之际，谨代表中国化学工人向工会国际致以衷心的祝贺，祝贺我工会国际在保卫全世界化学工人社会经济利益，加强国际工人团结和争取世界持久和平中有更光辉的成就。
            中国化学工会筹备委员会主任江涛
    罗马尼亚总工会给我全国总工会的贺电中华全国总工会：
    值此新春佳节，我们向你们致以最热烈的兄弟的庆贺。罗马尼亚人民共和国的工人以非常愉快的心情注视着伟大的中国人民在建设新中国工作中的成就。我们希望一九五二年将给中华人民共和国的工人，在保卫远东的和平，加强以伟大的苏联为首的和平民主与社会主义的巨大阵营的斗争上带来新的成就。
    罗马尼亚人民共和国总工会执行委员会
                        主席阿波斯托尔
                一九五一年十二月二十九日
