Date: 1952-01-14

Our Great Motherland

The People's Liberation Army stationed in Xinjiang has begun large-scale agricultural production and other construction work. It is estimated that this year's grain harvest will reach 108 million jin. All officers and soldiers are determined to work hard to establish mechanized farms and complete higher production increase plans. They are using tractors to turn barren wilderness into fertile fields.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-01-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    驻在新疆的人民解放军开始了大规模的农业生产和其他建设工作。预计今年收获粮食可达一亿零八百万斤。全体指战员决心为创办机械化农场和完成更高的增产计划而努力。他们正在用拖拉机把荒凉的原野变成肥沃的田园。
