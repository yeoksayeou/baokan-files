Title: Tenth Anniversary of the Founding of the Polish Workers' Party - The CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulations to the Polish United Workers' Party
Author:
Date: 1952-01-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 28th: January 28th marks the tenth anniversary of the founding of the Polish Workers' Party. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China has sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party to express congratulations. (Note: The Polish Workers' Party merged with the Polish Socialist Party in December 1948 to form the Polish United Workers' Party.) The telegram is as follows:

To the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party:

    On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the Polish Workers' Party, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends its warmest congratulations to you!

    The ten years of the Polish Workers' Party have been glorious years of continuous growth. Under the leadership of your party, the Polish people have achieved a historic victory in the struggle for national and social liberation. Under the leadership of your party, the solid alliance established by the Polish people with the great Soviet Union and the close friendly cooperation established with various people's democratic countries have become a huge force protecting the cause of world peace.

    The Chinese people watch with wholehearted and warm sympathy every victory of the Polish people in the cause of peaceful construction.

    We respectfully wish your party, under the leadership of Comrade Beirut, further success in leading the Polish people on the road to socialism and in the cause of defending world peace!

    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
    January 27, 1952, Beijing.

### 波兰工人党成立十周年纪念  中共中央电波兰统一工人党祝贺
中国共产党中央委员会
1952-01-29
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰工人党成立十周年纪念
    中共中央电波兰统一工人党祝贺
    【新华社二十八日讯】一月二十八日为波兰工人党成立十周年纪念日，中国共产党中央委员会特致电波兰统一工人党中央委员会祝贺。（按：波兰工人党在一九四八年十二月和波兰社会党合并成立了波兰统一工人党）电文如下：
    波兰统一工人党中央委员会：
    兹逢波兰工人党成立十周年之际，中国共产党中央委员会特向你们致热烈的祝贺！
    波兰工人党的十年是不断壮大的光荣的十年。在你们党的领导之下，波兰人民在争取民族解放和社会解放的斗争中获得了历史的胜利。在你们党的领导下，波兰人民与伟大的苏联所建立的坚固的同盟及与各人民民主国家所建立的亲密的友好合作，成了保护世界和平事业的巨大力量。
    中国人民以满腔热烈的同情注视着波兰人民在和平建设事业上的每一胜利。
    敬祝你们党在贝鲁特同志领导下，在领导波兰人民走向社会主义的途中和保卫世界和平的事业中获得进一步的成功！
    中国共产党中央委员会
    一九五二年一月二十七日，北京。
