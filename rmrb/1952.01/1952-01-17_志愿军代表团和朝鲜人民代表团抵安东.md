Title: Volunteer Army Delegation and Korean People's Delegation Arrive in Andong
Author:
Date: 1952-01-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Andong, 16th. The Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation and the Korean People's Delegation to China, comprising over 260 people, arrived in Andong on the 13th of this month. Representatives from the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea, the General Political Department of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission, and various circles in the Northeast went to welcome them. People from all walks of life in Liaodong and Andong held a welcome reception on the 15th. The two delegations will travel to Beijing within the next few days.

The Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation is led by Li Xuesan, the political commissar of a certain unit of the Volunteer Army. The representatives include many outstanding combat heroes, model workers, military commanders with remarkable achievements, staff officers, political workers, logistics personnel, medical workers, and literary and art workers. Among them are Mao Zhangmiao, the battalion chief of staff of a certain unit and a combat hero famous throughout the army; Yan Chengen, the political instructor of the "Tiandeshan Heroic Company," who defended Tiandeshan on the east bank of the Linjin River with a company of soldiers, resisting two regiments of enemy troops in a bloody battle for three days and three nights, annihilating more than 970 enemy soldiers; and Bian Ronghui, the "Wanli Hao" (Ten Thousand Li) truck driver who has driven safely for more than 20,000 kilometers since entering Korea and has always completed his transportation tasks. The delegation also includes 28 female representatives, including Yu Xiangui, a model nurse who has received more than ten major and minor merits and has been awarded the Military Service Medal of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; Xie Xiumei, a female literary and art worker who worked extensively in the company and bravely rescued the wounded regardless of enemy aircraft strafing; and Chen Yan, a famous model radio operator and People's Hero.

The Korean People's Delegation to China is composed of outstanding representatives from all aspects of Korean society. Among them are Nam Man-su, a "Hero of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea" who commanded a battalion and shot down 92 American aircraft in one year; Kang Bo, a "Hero of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea" who used torpedoes to destroy more than 20 American warships; and Li Wu-jun, a "Hero of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea" who shot down four American aircraft with a heavy machine gun in one month. The delegation also includes Park Si-hyung, a professor of history at Kim Il-sung University, and other representatives from all walks of life.

### 志愿军代表团和朝鲜人民代表团抵安东

1952-01-17
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军代表团和朝鲜人民代表团抵安东
    【新华社安东十六日电】中国人民志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团一行二百六十多人，已在本月十三日到达安东。中国人民抗美援朝总会、中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部和东北各界曾派代表前往迎接。辽东和安东各界人民在十五日举行了欢迎晚会。两个代表团将于日内前往北京。
    中国人民志愿军归国代表团由团长、志愿军某部政治委员李雪三率领，代表中包括许多出色的战斗英雄、模范工作者、功绩卓著的军事指挥员、参谋人员、政治工作人员、后勤工作人员、医务工作人员和文艺工作者。其中有闻名全军的战斗英雄某部营参谋长毛张苗，有在临津江东岸天德山以一连兵力抵抗两团敌军，血战三昼夜，歼敌九百七十多人的“天德山英雄连”的政治指导员阎成恩，有入朝年来安全行车两万多公里次次完成运输任务的“万里号”汽车司机卞溶晖。代表团中还有二十八名女代表，其中有立过十多次大功和小功并荣获朝鲜民主主义人民共和国军功章的模范护士于宪贵，有深入连队工作和不顾敌机扫射舍身抢救伤员的女文艺工作者解秀梅，有著名的模范报务工作者人民英雄陈炎等。
    朝鲜人民访华代表团由朝鲜各方面的优秀代表人物组成。有指挥一个营一年间击落美机九十二架的“朝鲜人民共和国英雄”南万寿，有用鱼雷破坏美舰二十多艘的“朝鲜人民共和国英雄”康波，有在一月内用重机枪击落美机四架的“朝鲜人民共和国英雄”李五俊等。代表团里还有金日成大学历史学教授朴时亨及其他各界代表。
