Title: Speech by Indian Chargé d'Affaires ad interim, Mr. K.B. Lall
Author:
Date: 1952-01-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 26th January: Mr. K.B. Lall, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indian Embassy in China, delivered a speech at the reception celebrating the second anniversary of the Republic of India. The full text is as follows:

    Vice Chairman Zhu De, Vice Chairman Li Jishen, Premier Zhou Enlai, Leaders of the Diplomatic Corps, Esteemed Colleagues, Fellow Asians, Ladies and Gentlemen:

    We are deeply honored that you are here with us today to celebrate our Republic Day. This day is not only a day of joy for us, but also a day for renewed efforts for peace and freedom in Asia and the world, and for friendship and goodwill among all mankind.

    In this great task, we are pleased to think of the great achievements of our great neighbor, the People's Republic of China, and her great leader, Chairman Mao Zedong. Chairman Mao is one of the greatest leaders in Asia and the world. He has elevated the status of Asia. We, the people of India, consider him a great friend.

    Salute to Chairman Mao Zedong!

### 印度临时代办高尔致词

1952-01-29
第4版()
专栏：

    印度临时代办高尔致词
    【新华社二十六日讯】印度驻华大使馆临时代办高尔在庆祝印度共和国国庆二周年招待会上致词，全文如下：
    朱德副主席，李济深副主席，周恩来总理，各位外交团领袖，敬爱的同僚们，亚洲同胞们，诸位来宾：
    诸位今天来到这儿与我们在一起庆祝我们的共和国国庆日，我们感到非常荣幸。这个日子对我们不仅是一个欢欣的日子，而且也是重新为亚洲与世界的和平和自由以及为全人类的友谊和亲善而努力的一个日子。
    在这巨大的任务中，我们愉快的想到我们伟大的邻邦中华人民共和国的盛大的成就以及她的伟大领袖毛泽东主席。毛主席是亚洲以及世界上最伟大领袖之一。他提高了亚洲的地位。我们印度人民认为他是一个伟大的朋友。
    谨向毛泽东主席致敬！
