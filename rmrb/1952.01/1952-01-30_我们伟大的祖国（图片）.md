    Our Great Motherland
    The Chinese People's Volunteers welcomed the New Year with glorious victories on the Korean front. This is a scene of the New Year's celebration where a performance troupe of the Chinese People's Volunteers is performing and singing new operas on the front lines, extending their greetings to the heroic anti-aircraft artillery units of the Volunteers.

专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国人民志愿军在朝鲜前线以光辉的胜利来迎接新年。这是志愿军某部文工团新年在阵地上说唱新洋片，慰问英雄的志愿军高射炮部队时的情景。
