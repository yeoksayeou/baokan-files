Author:
Date: 1952-01-31

    Our Great Motherland
    This is the arrow tower outside the Zhengyangmen Gate in Beijing. The arrow tower area is Beijing's commercial district. In the three years since Beijing's liberation, due to the gradual improvement of the national economy and the general increase in people's purchasing power, commerce in this area is more prosperous than before liberation.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-01-31
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    这是北京正阳门外的箭楼。箭楼附近是北京的商业区。北京解放三年来，由于国家经济情况的逐步好转和人民购买力的普遍提高，所以这一带的商业比解放以前更为繁荣。
