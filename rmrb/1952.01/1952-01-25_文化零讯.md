    Cultural News Briefs
    ▲The Shanghai United Film Studio was officially established on the 20th. This is another state-owned film studio after the Northeast Film Studio, Beijing Film Studio, and Shanghai Film Studio, which are under the Central Film Bureau. The Shanghai United Film Studio was reorganized from the Shanghai joint state-private enterprise Changjiang Film Company and eight private film companies: Kunlun, Wenhua, Guotai, Datong, Daguangming, Dachina, and Huaguang. Yu Ling is the director, and Ye Yiqun and Wu Bangfan are the deputy directors. After its establishment, the studio will strengthen the ideological leadership of future film production. All staff members will improve their political and professional levels by studying Marxism-Leninism, participating in mass life and struggles, and linking with reality. In terms of systems, democratic reforms will be carried out in a planned and step-by-step manner; among employees, ideological remolding movements will be launched; in terms of productions, Mao Zedong's literary and art line will be resolutely implemented to better serve workers, peasants, and soldiers.
    ▲The Shanghai Science and Technology Popularization Association was officially established on the 20th after more than a year of preparation. Over the past year, the association has united scientific, technological, cultural, and educational workers to engage in science and technology popularization work to serve workers, peasants, and soldiers, and has gained some work experience and achievements. It has also developed one thousand members, including scientific experts, professors, engineers, and technicians, as well as a small number of worker cultural and educational cadres, other intellectuals, and advanced workers.

### 文化零讯

1952-01-25
第3版()
专栏：文化零讯

    文化零讯
    ▲上海联合电影制片厂已于二十日正式成立。这是继中央电影局所属的东北电影制片厂、北京电影制片厂、上海电影制片厂之后的又一个公营的电影制片厂。上海联合电影制片厂是由上海公私合营的长江影片公司，私营昆仑、文华、国泰、大同、大光明、大中华、华光八家影片公司改组成立的。由于伶任厂长，叶以群、吴邦藩任副厂长。该厂成立后，将加强今后电影制作的思想领导。全厂工作人员将通过马列主义学习、参加群众生活与斗争，联系实际，提高政治和业务水平。在制度方面，将有计划、有步骤地进行民主改革；在职工之间，展开思想改造运动；在出品方面，坚决贯彻毛泽东文艺路线，以更好地为工、农、兵服务。
    ▲上海市科学技术普及协会经过一年多筹备，于二十日正式成立。一年多来，该会团结科学技术文教工作者，从事科学技术普及工作，为工农兵服务，已经获得一些工作经验和成绩，并发展了一千个会员，其中包括有科学专家、教授、工程师和技术人员以及少数工人文教干部、其他知识分子和先进工作者。
