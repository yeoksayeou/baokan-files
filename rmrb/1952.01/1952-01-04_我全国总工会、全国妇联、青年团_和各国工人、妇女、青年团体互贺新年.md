The Mongolian Central Council of Trade Unions' congratulatory telegram to the Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions:

To Chairman Chen Yun of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions:

Happy New Year. I wish the Chinese trade unions and the friendly Chinese people greater achievements in strengthening their country, consolidating the camp of peace and democracy, and in the cause of national independence.

The congratulatory telegram from the French General Confederation of Labor to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions:

Dear Comrades:

On the occasion of the New Year, we would like to extend our warmest congratulations to you and all the workers in China!

World peace is under threat, but French workers firmly believe that the united action of the people of the world will surely stop the evil conspiracies of the American imperialists and their servants, and will force them to stop the aggressive war in Korea.

French workers greatly admire the help given by your heroic volunteers to the heroic people of Korea, which has constantly defeated the foreign intervention forces.

After achieving a great victory in history, the Chinese people, under the leadership of Comrade Mao Zedong, are moving towards social progress.

Dear Comrades, on behalf of the French workers, we wish you even greater achievements in realizing your already planned great cause, such as completing the national land reform and developing the national economy, so as to ensure the rapid development of China's economic construction and strengthen the forces of the peace camp.

With brotherly greetings,

General Secretary of the French General Confederation of Labor, F. Frachon
Le Lippe
December 18, 1951

The congratulatory telegram from the Polish Youth League to the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League:

Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, dear friends:

On the occasion of the New Year, I would like to extend to you and all the heroic Chinese youth a warm and fraternal greeting. Polish youth are watching with great interest and deep sympathy the outstanding achievements made by the Chinese people and youth under the leadership of Chairman Mao in the cause of peaceful construction. These achievements have dealt a heavy blow to the war-mongers, greatly consolidated the camp of peace, and also encouraged the people of colonial and dependent countries to strengthen their struggle for freedom and democracy. The Chinese People's Volunteers are victoriously assisting the Korean brothers who are suffering from the aggression of American imperialism, and the Polish youth fully support their gratifying struggle. Dear friends! On this New Year of 1952, I wish you new achievements in carrying out peaceful construction and in the cause of striving for the final victory over the American aggressors.

Long live the World Democratic Youth League, the organizer and leader of the youth of the world in the struggle for peace!

Long live Mao Zedong, the great leader of the Chinese people!

Long live the great Stalin, the leader of the world peace camp!

第4版()
