Title: Our Country's Cultural Delegation Returns to Guangzhou After Visiting India and Burma
Author:
Date: 1952-01-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, January 14th: The Chinese cultural delegation of twenty-one people, which visited India and Burma, returned to Guangzhou from Hong Kong at 6:30 PM on the 13th. Du Guoxiang, Director of the Cultural and Educational Department of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, and Zhu Guang and Liang Guang, Deputy Mayors of the Guangzhou Municipal People's Government, went to the station to welcome them.

### 访问印度和缅甸后  我国文化代表团返抵广州

1952-01-15
第1版()
专栏：

    访问印度和缅甸后
    我国文化代表团返抵广州
    【新华社广州十四日电】我国派往印度、缅甸访问的文化代表团一行二十一人，已在十三日下午六时半自香港返抵广州。广东省人民政府文教厅厅长杜国庠、广州市人民政府副市长朱光、梁广等前往车站欢迎。
