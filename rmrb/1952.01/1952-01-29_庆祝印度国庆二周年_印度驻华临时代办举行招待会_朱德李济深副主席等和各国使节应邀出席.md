Title: India's Chargé d'Affaires in China Holds Reception to Celebrate the Second Anniversary of Indian National Day; Vice Chairmen Zhu De, Li Jishen and Diplomatic Envoys from Various Countries Attend Upon Invitation
Author:
Date: 1952-01-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 26th: Mr. Gaul, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Republic of India in our country, held a reception at 6:00 PM today to celebrate the second anniversary of the National Day of the Republic of India.

Invited to attend were Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De and Li Jishen, Premier of the State Administration Council and concurrently Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Huang Yanpei, Acting Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Nie Rongzhen, President of the Supreme People's Court Shen Junru, heads of various departments of the Central People's Government, heads of various people's organizations, and Ding Xilin, head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation and Li Yimang, deputy head, who recently returned from a visit to India, totaling more than 300 people.

Diplomatic envoys to China from various countries who were invited to attend included Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, Czechoslovak Ambassador Weiskopf, Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Mongolian Ambassador Jalkhasaikhan, Hungarian Ambassador Szafranko, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Burmese Ambassador U Hla Maung, Pakistani Ambassador Raza, Swiss Minister Zutter, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy Isa Matti, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Delegation Zhou Liang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Danish Legation Meistrup, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Swedish Embassy Bevin, First Secretary of the Polish Embassy Gura, and diplomatic officials from various embassies, as well as the representatives of the British and Norwegian governments for negotiations and personnel from the office of the Netherlands government's negotiating delegation.

Mr. Gaul delivered a speech at the reception. Following him, Vice Chairman Zhu De of our Central People's Government delivered a congratulatory speech. During the reception, the hosts and guests toasted to the health of the heads of state of both countries.

The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 庆祝印度国庆二周年  印度驻华临时代办举行招待会  朱德李济深副主席等和各国使节应邀出席

1952-01-29
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝印度国庆二周年
    印度驻华临时代办举行招待会
    朱德李济深副主席等和各国使节应邀出席
    【新华社二十六日讯】印度共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办高尔，为庆祝印度共和国国庆二周年，在今日下午六时举行招待会。
    应邀出席的有中央人民政府副主席朱德、李济深，政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来，副总理黄炎培，人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，中央人民政府各部门首长，各人民团体负责人及新近访问印度归来的中国文化代表团团长丁西林、副团长李一氓等三百多人。
    应邀出席的各国驻华使节有苏联大使罗申、捷克斯洛伐克大使魏斯柯普夫、罗马尼亚大使鲁登科、蒙古大使贾尔卡赛汗、匈牙利大使夏法朗柯、保加利亚大使彼得科夫、缅甸大使吴拉茂、巴基斯坦大使罗查、瑞士公使任佐立、印度尼西亚大使馆临时代办伊沙·玛蒂、越南代表团临时代办周亮、丹麦公使馆临时代办迈思妥、瑞典大使馆临时代办贝凡、波兰大使馆一等秘书古拉和各使馆外交官员以及英国、挪威政府的谈判代表和荷兰政府谈判代表办公处的人员。
    高尔在招待会上首先致词。接着，我中央人民政府朱德副主席致祝词。在招待会进行中，宾主曾互为两国元首的健康而举杯。
    招待会在友好的气氛中进行。
