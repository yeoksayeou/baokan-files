Title: Marshal Rokossovsky, Polish Minister of National Defense, Sends New Year Greetings to Commander-in-Chief Zhu De
Author:
Date: 1952-01-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 4th—Marshal Rokossovsky, Polish Minister of National Defense, sent a telegram to Commander-in-Chief Zhu De to congratulate him on the New Year. The telegram is as follows: To Comrade General Zhu De, Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army:
    On the occasion of the New Year of 1952, on behalf of the Polish People's Army and myself, I extend my most sincere respects to you, and wish your army, which defends the freedom of the people and world peace, further success in strengthening its combat strength and improving its fighting will.
      Minister of National Defense Polish Marshal Rokossovsky

### 波兰国防部长罗科索夫斯基元帅  致电朱德总司令祝贺新年

1952-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰国防部长罗科索夫斯基元帅
    致电朱德总司令祝贺新年
    【新华社四日讯】波兰国防部长罗科索夫斯基元帅致电朱德总司令，祝贺新年。电文如下。中国人民解放军总司令朱德将军同志：
    当此一九五二年新年之际，我代表波兰人民军和我个人，向您致以最衷心的敬意，并祝您的保卫人民自由及世界和平的军队，在加强战斗力量和提高战斗意志的工作中获得更进一步的成功。
      国防部长  波兰元帅  罗科索夫斯基
