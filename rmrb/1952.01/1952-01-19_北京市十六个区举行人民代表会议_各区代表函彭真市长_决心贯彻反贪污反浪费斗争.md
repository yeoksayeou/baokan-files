Title: Sixteen Districts of Beijing Hold People's Representative Meetings; Representatives from Each District Write to Mayor Peng Zhen, Resolving to Implement the Struggle Against Corruption and Waste

Author:
Date: 1952-01-19
Page: 1

    【Beijing News Agency News】The sixteen districts under the jurisdiction of Beijing have all held people's representative meetings of various circles. At the meetings, representatives from each district wrote letters to Mayor Peng Zhen, expressing their determination to carry out the anti-corruption, anti-waste, and anti-bureaucracy movement among the broad masses. The letter from all representatives of the First District stated: "We deeply hate and despise the phenomenon of corrupt elements stealing state property. This turbid social atmosphere left over from the reactionary class must not be allowed to continue. We must, according to your instructions, 'besiege it and eliminate it.' We guarantee to you that all representatives, upon returning to factories, shops, government offices, schools, and streets, will mobilize and inspire people from all walks of life to actively participate in this movement, eliminate all concerns, boldly report corrupt elements who steal state property, expose waste, and correct bureaucratic work styles, in order to eliminate all obstacles to the development of the production increase and economy drive." The representatives of the Ninth District said in their letter to Mayor Peng Zhen: "We assure you that we will carry out the anti-corruption, anti-waste, and anti-bureaucracy movement among people from all walks of life, and turn this movement into a broad, mass movement, carrying it out vigorously, resolutely, and thoroughly." The representatives of the Third District and other districts also wrote to Mayor Peng, making the same guarantees.

### 北京市十六个区举行人民代表会议  各区代表函彭真市长  决心贯彻反贪污反浪费斗争

1952-01-19
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市十六个区举行人民代表会议
    各区代表函彭真市长
    决心贯彻反贪污反浪费斗争
    【北京市新闻处讯】北京市所属十六个区都分别召开了各界人民代表会议，会上，各区代表纷纷给彭真市长写信，表示他们决心把反贪污、反浪费、反官僚主义运动贯彻到广大群众中去。第一区全体代表的信上说：“我们非常憎恶和卑视那些贪污分子盗窃国家财产的现象，这种反动阶级遗留下来的恶浊的社会空气，绝不能允许继续存在。我们要按照您指示的话：‘要向它围剿，消灭它’。向您保证：我们全体代表回到工厂、商店、机关、学校、街道后，一定要动员和启发各界人民来积极参加这一运动，破除一切顾虑，大胆地检举偷盗国家财产的贪污分子，揭发浪费现象，纠正官僚主义作风，为开展增产节约运动铲除一切阻碍。”第九区全体代表在给彭真市长的信上说：“我们向您保证将反贪污、反浪费、反官僚主义运动贯彻到各界人民中去，把这个运动形成广大的、群众性的运动，大张旗鼓地、雷厉风行地坚决进行到底。”第三区和其他各区的全体代表也上书彭市长，提出了同样的保证。
