Title: The State Council's Political Affairs Conference was Expanded Yesterday. Premier Zhou Gave Clear Instructions on the Direction, Policies, and Steps of the Anti-Corruption, Anti-Waste, and Anti-Bureaucracy Movement.
Author:
Date: 1952-01-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 11th—The 119th Political Affairs Conference of the Central People's Government State Council was expanded yesterday afternoon. Those attending the meeting included the Vice Chairmen and Deputy Secretaries-General of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the Acting Chief of General Staff and Deputy Chiefs of General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, the Deputy Director of the General Political Department and the heads of departments under the Military Commission, the President and Vice Presidents and Secretary-General of the Supreme People's Court, the Deputy Procurator-General and Secretary-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, the responsible persons of the commissions, ministries, committees, academies, administrations, and banks directly under the State Council, the responsible persons of the Central People's Government's Economy and Austerity Inspection Committee and the Beijing Economy and Austerity Inspection Committee, and the responsible persons of political parties and organizations, totaling over one hundred people. The meeting listened to and discussed the report made by Bo Yibo, Director of the Central People's Government's Economy and Austerity Inspection Committee, on the anti-corruption, anti-waste, and anti-bureaucracy movement in the units directly under the Central People's Government. At the meeting, Premier Zhou Enlai gave clear instructions on the direction, policies, steps, and several key issues in the development of the anti-corruption, anti-waste, and anti-bureaucracy movement. In addition, the meeting also passed several cases of appointments and removals.

### 政务院政务会议昨天扩大举行  周总理对反贪污反浪费反官僚主义运动的方向政策步骤等作了明确指示

1952-01-12
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院政务会议昨天扩大举行
    周总理对反贪污反浪费反官僚主义运动的方向政策步骤等作了明确指示
    【新华社十一日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百一十九次政务会议在十一日下午扩大举行。列席这次会议的有中国人民政协全国委员会副主席、副秘书长，人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长、副总参谋长、总政治部副主任及军委所属各部门首长，最高人民法院正副院长、秘书长，最高人民检察署副检察长、秘书长，政务院所属各委、部、会、院、署、行正副负责人，中央人民政府节约检查委员会和北京市节约检查委员会负责人，以及党派、团体负责人等共一百余人。会议听取和讨论了中央人民政府节约检查委员会薄一波主任所作的关于中央人民政府直属各单位反贪污、反浪费、反官僚主义运动的报告。会上，周恩来总理对反贪污、反浪费、反官僚主义运动的方向、政策、步骤和运动发展中的几个关键问题作了明确指示。此外会议并通过任免案多起。
