Title: Government Administration Council Holds 121st Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-01-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, January 25th—The 121st Government Administration Council meeting of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 25th. The meeting listened to and approved the report by Bo Yibo, Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council, on the handling of production by government organs.

### 政务院举行第一二一次会议

1952-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一二一次会议
    【新华社二十五日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百二十一次政务会议在二十五日下午举行。会议听取和批准了政务院财政经济委员会薄一波副主任所作的关于机关生产的处理问题的报告。
