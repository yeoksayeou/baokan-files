Title: Announcement of the Ministry of Finance of the Central People's Government: Second Principal Repayment Lottery for the First Victory Bonds
Author:
Date: 1952-01-26
Page: 1

    Announcement of the Ministry of Finance of the Central People's Government
    Second Principal Repayment Lottery for the First Victory Bonds
    (Xinhua News Agency, 25th) The Ministry of Finance of the Central People's Government announces the schedule for the second principal repayment lottery for the 1950 First People's Victory Indexed Bonds.

    According to Article 5 of the bond regulations, the second principal repayment date for this series of bonds is March 31, 1952. The public lottery will be held in advance at the Chang'an Grand Theater in Xidan, Beijing, at 1:30 PM on February 1, 1952. Bond tickets drawn in the lottery will be redeemed for principal starting from March 31, and the second installment of interest will be paid simultaneously.

    Minister Bo Yibo, January 25, 1952

### 中央人民政府财政部公告  第一期胜利公债举行第二次还本抽签

1952-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府财政部公告
    第一期胜利公债举行第二次还本抽签
    【新华社二十五日讯】中央人民政府财政部为一九五零年第一期人民胜利折实公债第二次还本定期抽签公告。
    本期公债依照公债条例第五条规定，一九五二年三月三十一日为第二次还本之期。兹定于一九五二年二月一日下午一时半在北京西单长安大戏院先期公开举行抽签，凡中签债票，即自三月三十一日起，开始兑付本金，并同时支付第二期应付利息。
    部长　薄一波　一九五二年一月二十五日
