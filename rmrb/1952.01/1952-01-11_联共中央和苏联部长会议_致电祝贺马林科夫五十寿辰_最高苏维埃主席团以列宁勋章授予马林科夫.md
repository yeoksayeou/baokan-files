Title: Telegram of Congratulations from the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union on Malenkov's Fiftieth Birthday; Presidium of the Supreme Soviet Awards Malenkov the Order of Lenin
Author: Shvernik, Gorkin
Date: 1952-01-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 8th, TASS Dispatch: January 8th marks the fiftieth birthday of Comrade Malenkov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks) and Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union. Below is the congratulatory telegram from the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks) and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union to him:

“To Comrade Georgy Maximilianovich Malenkov: On the occasion of your fiftieth birthday, the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks) and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union warmly congratulate you, the faithful student of Lenin and comrade-in-arms of Stalin, an outstanding leader of the Communist Party and the Soviet state.

Your conscious life has been devoted to the great cause of the Party of Lenin and Stalin, to the struggle for the victory of communism.

As a faithful son of the Soviet people, you have set an inspiring example of serving the people with all your activities, and have completed the glorious tasks entrusted to you by the Communist Party.

You are a faithful follower of our great teachers Lenin and Stalin, and by performing the most responsible tasks in various positions in the Party and state activities, you have created, with your characteristic energy and tenacity, the victory of our motherland over the enemies of mankind in the Great Patriotic War.

We congratulate you, our friend and comrade, dear Georgy Maximilianovich, and wish you good health and long life, and more contributions to our great socialist motherland and to communism.

The Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks), The Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union"

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 8th, TASS Dispatch: On January 7th, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union issued a decree awarding G.M. Malenkov the Order of Lenin. The full text of the decree is as follows:

"In recognition of Comrade Malenkov's outstanding contributions to the Communist Party and the Soviet people, on the occasion of the fiftieth birthday of Comrade Malenkov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks) and Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Georgy Maximilianovich Malenkov is hereby awarded the Order of Lenin.

Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union: Shvernik
Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union: Gorkin"

### 联共中央和苏联部长会议  致电祝贺马林科夫五十寿辰  最高苏维埃主席团以列宁勋章授予马林科夫
什维尔尼克  高尔金
1952-01-11
第4版()
专栏：

    联共中央和苏联部长会议
    致电祝贺马林科夫五十寿辰
    最高苏维埃主席团以列宁勋章授予马林科夫
    【新华社八日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：一月八日，是苏联共产党（布）中央委员会书记、苏联部长会议副主席马林科夫五十岁寿辰。下面是苏联共产党（布）中央委员会和苏联部长会议给他的贺电：“格奥尔基·马克西米里安诺维奇·马林科夫同志：欣逢你的五十岁寿辰，苏联共产党（布）中央委员会和苏联部长会议热诚地祝贺你，列宁的忠实的学生和斯大林的战友，共产党和苏维埃国家杰出的领袖。
    你的有觉悟的一生都是致力于列宁与斯大林的党的伟大事业，致力于争取共产主义胜利的斗争。
    作为苏联人民的忠实的儿子，你以你的全部活动树立了令人鼓舞的为人民服务的榜样，完成了共产党交给你的光荣任务。
    你是我们的伟大导师列宁和斯大林的忠实的追随者，由于在党和国家活动的各种职位上执行最负责的任务，在伟大的卫国战争中以你所特有的精力和刚毅创造了我们祖国对于人类的敌人的胜利。
    我们祝贺你，我们的朋友和同志，亲爱的格奥尔基·马克西米里安诺维奇，健康长寿，对我们伟大的社会主义祖国、对共产主义有更多的贡献。
    苏联共产党（布）中央委员会，苏联部长会议”
    【新华社八日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：一月七日，苏联最高苏维埃主席团颁发命令，以列宁勋章授予格·马·马林科夫。命令全文如下：
    “值此苏联共产党（布）中央委员会书记、苏联部长会议副主席马林科夫同志五十寿辰之际，鉴于马林科夫同志对共产党及苏联人民的卓越贡献，特以列宁勋章授予格奥尔基·马克西米里安诺维奇·马林科夫。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席  什维尔尼克
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书  高尔金”
