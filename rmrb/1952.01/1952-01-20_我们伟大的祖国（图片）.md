Author:
Date: 1952-01-20
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The young men and women of the Sani ethnic group in Yunnan Province are mostly good at singing and dancing. Their songs and dances mostly sing about their history, love, harvest, and life after liberation. These two are Sani girls from Guishan District, Yunnan Province. They are holding pipa and playing the song "Chairman Mao is the Red Sun of all Ethnic Groups."

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    云南省撒尼族的男女青年大都是能歌善舞的。他们的歌舞多半是歌唱他们的历史、爱情、丰收和解放后的生活的。这两位是云南省圭山区的撒尼族少女，她们正抱着琵琶，弹奏“毛主席是各民族的红太阳”的歌曲。
