Title: Foreign Envoys to China Visited the Central People's Government on New Year's Day to Sign and Congratulate Chairman Mao Zedong on the New Year

Author:
Date: 1952-01-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 1st -- On New Year's Day, 1952, foreign envoys to China visited the Central People's Government to sign their names and congratulate Chairman Mao Zedong on the New Year. Those who went to offer their congratulations included: Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, Czechoslovak Ambassador Weiskopf, Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic Koenig, Mongolian Ambassador Jalkhjavyn, Hungarian Ambassador Szafrankó, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Burmese Ambassador U Myat Kyaw, Pakistani Ambassador Raza, Swiss Minister Zollinger, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy Ishaq Matti, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy Dowding, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indian Embassy Kher, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Delegation Zhou Liang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Korean Embassy Choi Young, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Danish Legation Meinstorp, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Swedish Embassy Befvenstedt, and diplomatic officials from various embassies.

Those present to receive them were: Yu Xinqing, Director of the Bureau of Ceremonies of the Central People's Government, Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wei Baoshan, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department.

### 各国驻华使节元旦赴中央人民政府签名  向毛泽东主席祝贺新年

1952-01-03
第1版()
专栏：

    各国驻华使节元旦赴中央人民政府签名
    向毛泽东主席祝贺新年
    【新华社一日讯】一九五二年元旦各国驻华使节赴中央人民政府签名，向毛泽东主席祝贺新年。前往祝贺者有：苏联大使罗申、捷克斯洛伐克大使魏斯柯普夫、罗马尼亚大使鲁登科、德意志民主共和国外交使团团长柯尼希、蒙古大使贾尔卡赛汗、匈牙利大使夏法朗柯、保加利亚大使彼得科夫、缅甸大使吴拉茂、巴基斯坦大使罗查、瑞士公使任佐立、印度尼西亚大使馆临时代办伊沙·玛蒂、波兰大使馆临时代办道定、印度大使馆临时代办高尔、越南代表团临时代办周亮、朝鲜大使馆临时代办崔英、丹麦公使馆临时代办迈思妥、瑞典大使馆临时代办贝凡暨各使馆外交官员。
    在场接待者有：中央人民政府典礼局局长余心清、外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航及交际处副处长魏宝善。
