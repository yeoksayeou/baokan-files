Title: Central Film Bureau and Other Units' Employees and National Film Workers Issue Statement Protesting the British Hong Kong Government's Atrocity of Expelling Our Film Workers
Author:
Date: 1952-01-17
Page: 1

(Xinhua News Agency, 16th) The Central Film Bureau, Northeast Film Studio, and other units' employees, along with national film workers, issued a written statement protesting the British Hong Kong government's unreasonable expulsion of our Hong Kong film workers. The statement is as follows:

Our Hong Kong film artists Sima Wensen, Qi Wenshao, Ma Guoliang, Liu Qiong, Shu Shi, Yang Hua, Shen Ji, Di Fan, and others have recently been unreasonably expelled by the British Hong Kong government. We extend our sincere greetings to Mr. Sima Wensen, Mr. Qi Wenshao, and others with infinite concern. We believe that this violent and barbaric act by the British Hong Kong government is a further blatant manifestation of its consistent obstruction, sabotage, and suppression of the patriotic movements of our compatriots in the past. We, all film workers, together with the people of the whole country, express our utmost indignation and lodge a solemn protest with the British Hong Kong government! The British imperialists must bear all the consequences caused by this unreasonable atrocity! Central Film Bureau, Northeast Film Studio, Beijing Film Studio, Shanghai Film Studio, Film Script Writing Institute, China Film Management Company, China Film Equipment Company, Film School, Shanghai United Film Studio, all employees and national film workers.

January 16th

### 中央电影局等单位职工和全国电影工作者发表声明  抗议香港英政府驱逐我电影工作者的暴行

1952-01-17
第1版()
专栏：

    中央电影局等单位职工和全国电影工作者发表声明
    抗议香港英政府驱逐我电影工作者的暴行
    【新华社十六日讯】中央电影局、东北电影制片厂等单位的全体职工及全国电影工作者发表书面声明，抗议香港英国政府无理驱逐我国香港电影工作者的暴行。声明如下：
    我国香港电影艺术工作者司马文森、齐闻韶、马国亮、刘琼、舒适、杨华、沈寂、狄梵等八人近遭香港英国政府无理驱逐，我们以无限关怀的心情谨向司马文森、齐闻韶等先生遥致亲切的慰问。我们认为：香港英国政府这种狂暴的野蛮行为，是过去对于我国同胞的爱国运动一贯地阻挠、破坏和压制的进一步露骨的表现，我们全体电影工作者和全国人民一起，万分愤怒地向香港英国政府提出严正的抗议！英帝国主义者要对这种无理暴行所招致的后果担负一切责任！中央电影局、东北电影制片厂、北京电影制片厂、上海电影制片厂、电影剧本创作所、中国影片经理公司、中国电影器材公司、电影学校、上海联合电影制片厂全体职工及全国电影工作者。
    一月十六日
