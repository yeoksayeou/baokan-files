Title: Korean Goodwill Tour to China Departs for Visit

Author:

Date: 1952-01-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 10th. A goodwill tour to China comprised of representatives from all circles of the Korean people, totaling 81 individuals, led by the head of the delegation, Hong Sun-cheol, and deputy head Kim Chao-kyu, departed from here on the 7th for Beijing.

The head of the delegation, Hong Sun-cheol, is a representative of the Korean People's Army. The deputy head, Kim Chao-kyu, is the editor-in-chief of the Korean "Literature and Art" magazine. The members include Kim Chang-jun, Secretary-General of the Korean Fatherland Front, as well as representatives from various democratic parties and social organizations, combat heroes from the People's Army, model workers, model farmers, cultural workers, educational workers, and journalists.

The goodwill tour plans to visit major cities and rural areas in China in groups over a period of three months to introduce the heroic resistance of the Korean military and civilians to the Chinese people. The tour will also observe the Chinese people's increased production in support of the front lines in the Resist-America, Aid-Korea movement, as well as the achievements in political, economic, and cultural construction. This is in order to conduct extensive and in-depth propaganda to the Korean people after returning home, and to further consolidate the militant solidarity between the peoples of China and Korea.

### 朝鲜访华观光团  启程来我国访问

1952-01-11
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜访华观光团
    启程来我国访问
    【新华社平壤十日电】朝鲜各界人民代表组成的访华观光团一行八十一人，在团长洪淳哲、副团长金朝奎率领下，已于七日离此首途赴北京。
    观光团团长洪淳哲是朝鲜人民军代表，副团长金朝奎是朝鲜“文学艺术”杂志主笔，团员中包括朝鲜祖国战线书记长金昌俊和各民主政党、社会团体的代表、人民军战斗英雄、模范工人、模范农民、文化工作者、教育工作者以及新闻记者等。
    观光团计划以三个月的时间分组访问我国各大城市与农村，向我国人民介绍朝鲜军民英勇抗战的情形。观光团同时将参观我国人民在抗美援朝运动中增加生产支援前线的情形和政治、经济、文化建设的成就，以便在回国后向朝鲜人民进行广泛和深入的宣传，进一步巩固中朝两国人民的战斗团结。
