Title: Tianjin Sancheng Painting Factory Should Not Produce New Year Pictures Promoting Feudal Ideas
Author: Yu Wenxiao, Fang Jifeng
Date: 1952-01-20
Page: 2

    Tianjin Sancheng Painting Factory
    Should Not Produce New Year Pictures Promoting Feudal Ideas
    Editor: Among the New Year pictures sold in the market in Fengning County, some depict new things, but some actually promote feudal ideas. Some simply put a new name on old New Year pictures. For example, some of the paintings produced by the Tianjin Sancheng Painting Factory: Originally "Liu Bei Marries Sun Quan [lit. Liu Bei Recruits a Son-in-Law]", but now the title is forcibly changed to "Marriage Autonomy." This method of changing the soup but not the medicine is wrong. Can the marriage recruitment of feudal monarchs be interpreted as marriage autonomy in the new society? I believe that the literary and artistic works of New China should serve politics. The Tianjin Sancheng Painting Factory is wrong to do this.
    Yu Wenxiao, Fang Jifeng, Fengshan Complete Primary School, Fengning County, Rehe Province

### 天津三成制画厂  不该制作宣传封建思想的年画
于文小  方继丰
1952-01-20
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    天津三成制画厂
    不该制作宣传封建思想的年画
    编辑同志：在丰宁县市上出卖的年画中，有些画的内容是新事物，但有些画实际上是宣传封建思想的。有的把旧年画按上个新名词。如天津三成制画厂出品的一些画：本来是“刘备招亲”，现在强拉硬扯地把标题改为“婚姻自主”。这种换汤不换药的办法是不对的。难道封建王侯的招亲能被解释为新社会的婚姻自主吗？我认为新中国的文艺作品，都应该为政治服务。天津三成制画厂这样做法是不对的。
    热河省丰宁县凤山完全小学校　于文小　方继丰
