Title: Chairman Popova of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee Sends New Year's Greetings to Soong Ching Ling
Author:
Date: 1952-01-04
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: Chairman Popova of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee sent a New Year's telegram to Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling of the Central People's Government:
    Madam Soong Ching Ling:
    Please accept my heartfelt New Year's greetings. I wish you good health and success in your endeavors to secure the happiness of the Chinese people and consolidate the camp of peace and democracy. Nina Popova

### 苏联妇女反法西斯委员会主席  波波娃致电宋庆龄贺年

1952-01-04
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联妇女反法西斯委员会主席
    波波娃致电宋庆龄贺年
    【新华社讯】苏联妇女反法西斯委员会主席波波娃致中央人民政府副主席宋庆龄的新年贺电：宋庆龄夫人：
    请接受我衷心的新年祝贺，祝您健康，并祝在谋取中国人民的幸福、巩固和平与民主阵营的事业中获得成功。  尼娜·波波娃
