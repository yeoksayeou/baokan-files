Title: Former Soviet People's Commissar for Foreign Affairs Litvinov Dies; Soviet Foreign Ministry Issues Obituary
Author:
Date: 1952-01-03
Page: 4

    Former Soviet People's Commissar for Foreign Affairs
    Litvinov Dies
    Soviet Foreign Ministry Issues Obituary
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 2nd] TASS Moscow, 2nd: The Pravda published the obituary of Maxim Litvinov, former Soviet People's Commissar for Foreign Affairs, by the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
    On December 31, 1951, after a long illness, Maxim Maksimovich Litvinov - an old Bolshevik member, an outstanding Soviet diplomat - passed away.
    Litvinov was born in 1876 in a clerk's family in the city of Bialystok. In 1898, Litvinov joined the Russian Social Democratic Labor Party. In 1903, after the Second Congress of the Russian Social Democratic Labor Party, he joined the Bolshevik Party and actively participated in the party's work.
    From 1902 to 1918, except for two years in between, he was in exile in Britain.
    After the Great October Socialist Revolution, from 1918 onwards, Litvinov worked in the People's Commissariat for Foreign Affairs. In 1921, Litvinov was appointed Deputy People's Commissar for Foreign Affairs, and he participated in the Genoa and Hague Conferences. From 1927 to 1930, Litvinov led the Soviet delegation to the Preparatory Commission for the Disarmament Conference in Geneva, and represented the Soviet government in proposing general disarmament. From 1930 to 1939, Litvinov served as the Soviet People's Commissar for Foreign Affairs. In 1932, he led the Soviet delegation to the Disarmament Conference organized by the League of Nations, and in 1933 he led the Soviet delegation to the London World Economic Conference. In 1933, Litvinov, representing the Soviet government, signed conventions with several countries defining the aggressor. From 1934 to 1938, Litvinov was the Soviet representative to the Council of the League of Nations.
    Litvinov was elected as a member of the Soviet Central Executive Committee and as a representative to the first and second convocations of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.
    After the outbreak of the Great Patriotic War, Litvinov was appointed Soviet Ambassador to the United States of America. From 1943 to 1946, Litvinov served as Deputy People's Commissar for Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union.
    In recent years, Litvinov retired due to serious illness.
    Litvinov was awarded the Order of Lenin, the Order of the Red Banner of Labor, and the medal "For Valiant Labor" during the Great Patriotic War of 1941-1945.

### 苏联前外交人民委员长  李维诺夫病逝  苏联外交部发出讣告

1952-01-03
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联前外交人民委员长
    李维诺夫病逝
    苏联外交部发出讣告
    【新华社二日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二日讯：真理报登载苏联外交部关于前苏联外交人民委员长马克西姆·李维诺夫逝世的讣告。
    一九五一年十二月三十一日，在长期卧病之后，马克西姆·马克西莫维奇·李维诺夫——老布尔什维克党员、杰出的苏联外交家——逝世了。
    李维诺夫在一八七六年生于贝罗斯托克城一个职员的家庭里。一八九八年，李维诺夫加入俄国社会民主工党。一九○三年，在俄国社会民主工党第二次代表大会之后，他加入了布尔什维克党，并积极参加党的工作。
    一九○二年到一九一八年，除了其间的两年之外，他一直流亡在英国。
    在伟大的十月社会主义革命之后，从一九一八年起，李维诺夫即在外交人民委员会工作。一九二一年，李维诺夫被任命为外交人民副委员长，他参加了热那亚和海牙会议。一九二七年至一九三○年间，李维诺夫率领苏联代表团出席日内瓦裁军会议筹备委员会，并代表苏联政府建议普遍裁减军备。从一九三○年到一九三九年，李维诺夫任苏联外交人民委员长。一九三二年，他率领苏联代表团出席国际联盟组织的裁军会议，一九三三年率领苏联代表团出席伦敦世界经济会议。一九三三年，李维诺夫代表苏联政府，与若干国家签订了确定侵略者定义的公约。从一九三四年到一九三八年，李维诺夫是苏联出席国际联盟理事会的代表。
    李维诺夫曾当选为苏联中央执行委员会委员，并曾当选为第一届和第二届苏联最高苏维埃的代表。
    伟大的卫国战争发生后，李维诺夫被任命为苏联驻美利坚合众国的大使。一九四三年到一九四六年，李维诺夫任苏联外交人民副委员长。
    近几年来，李维诺夫因患重病，便告退休。
    李维诺夫曾获得列宁勋章、劳动红旗勋章、一九四一—一九四五年伟大卫国战争期间的“光荣劳动”奖章。
