Title: International Federation of Women General Secretary Madame Vaillant-Couturier Sends Telegram to Comrade Cai Chang Congratulating on the New Year
Author: Wayang—Guli Jiu
Date: 1952-01-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency

    International Federation of Women General Secretary Madame Vaillant-Couturier
    Sends Telegram to Comrade Cai Chang Congratulating on the New Year
    [Xinhua News Agency] Madame Vaillant-Couturier, General Secretary of the Women's International Democratic Federation, sent a telegram to Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, congratulating her on the New Year. The telegram reads as follows:
    To Comrade Cai Chang, All-China Democratic Women's Federation:
    The Secretariat of the Women's International Democratic Federation sincerely wishes you a happy new year and pays tribute to the heroic Chinese mothers who have endured sacrifices to aid the Korean people and defend China's independence. The concerted action of women all over the world will surely contribute to ending the Korean War and safeguarding world peace and human happiness.
                        Vaillant-Couturier

### 国际妇联总书记古久里夫人  致电蔡畅同志祝贺新年
瓦扬—古久里
1952-01-08
第4版()
专栏：

    国际妇联总书记古久里夫人
    致电蔡畅同志祝贺新年
    【新华社讯】国际民主妇女联合会总书记瓦扬—古久里夫人致电中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅，祝贺新年。电文如下：中华全国民主妇女联合会蔡畅夫人：
    国际民主妇女联合会书记处衷心向您祝贺新年，并向为援助朝鲜人民、保卫中国独立而忍受牺牲的英勇的中国母亲们致敬。全世界妇女的一致行动，对于结束朝鲜战争、保卫世界和平与人类幸福必将有所贡献。
                        瓦扬—古久里
