Title: Beijing Municipal Committee for Practicing Economy and Counteracting Waste Begins Inspection of Bribery, Tax Evasion, and Other Behaviors in the Industrial and Commercial Sectors; People's Reporting Boxes and Mass Reporting Reception Stations Have Been Universally Established in the Urban Area

Author:

Date: 1952-01-12
Page: 1

    Beijing Municipal Committee for Practicing Economy and Counteracting Waste
    Today Begins Inspection of Bribery, Tax Evasion, and Other Behaviors in the Industrial and Commercial Sectors
    People's Reporting Boxes and Mass Reporting Reception Stations Have Been Universally Established in the Urban Area
    [This newspaper's report] The Beijing Municipal Committee for Practicing Economy and Counteracting Waste today began conducting inspections in the industrial and commercial sectors regarding behaviors such as bribery, tax evasion, fraud, and theft of public property. All personnel responsible for conducting the inspections hold special certificates signed and sealed by the committee.
    [Beijing News Agency Dispatch] The Beijing Municipal Committee for Practicing Economy and Counteracting Waste has universally established people's reporting boxes and mass reporting reception stations at the entrances of various organs and organizations in the urban area. People from all walks of life are welcome to report, in writing or verbally, all illegal activities such as corruption, waste, extortion, blackmail, accepting bribes, offering bribes, evading taxes, using inferior materials and reducing workmanship, stealing state assets, and stealing state secrets. The committee has agreed with the Beijing Post Office that all mailboxes and post office boxes throughout the city will collect reporting letters free of charge, simply by writing "Deliver to Beijing Post Office Box 100."

### 北京市节约检查委员会  今天开始检查工商界中行贿偷税等行为  并已在市区普遍设立人民检举箱和群众检举接待站

1952-01-12
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市节约检查委员会
    今天开始检查工商界中行贿偷税等行为
    并已在市区普遍设立人民检举箱和群众检举接待站
    【本报讯】北京市节约检查委员会今日开始在工商界进行有关行贿、偷税、欺诈、盗窃公共财物等行为的检查，凡负责前往检查人员，均持有该会签名盖章的专用证件。
    【北京市新闻处讯】北京市节约检查委员会已在市区各机关团体驻地门口普遍设立人民检举箱和群众检举接待站，欢迎各界人民对一切贪污、浪费、敲诈、勒索、受贿、行贿、偷税、偷工减料、盗窃国家资财及盗窃国家机密等不法行为，进行书面或口头检举。该会已与北京邮局约定：所有全市各邮筒、邮箱，一律免费代收检举信件，只须写明“寄交北京邮政信箱一百号”即可。
