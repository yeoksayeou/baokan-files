Title: State Council Holds 118th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-01-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 4th. The 118th meeting of the State Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 4th. The meeting listened to and approved the report made by Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, on the Second Plenary Session of the Academy. The meeting also approved several motions regarding appointments and removals, and motions to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval of appointments.

### 政务院举行第一一八次会议

1952-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一一八次会议
    【新华社四日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百一十八次政务会议于四日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中国科学院郭沫若院长所作的中国科学院关于第二次院务会议的报告。会议并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。
