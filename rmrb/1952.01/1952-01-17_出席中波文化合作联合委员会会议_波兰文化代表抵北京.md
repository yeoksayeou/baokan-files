Title: Polish Cultural Representatives Arrive in Beijing to Attend Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Joint Committee Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-01-17
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 16th
    Polish Cultural Representatives Arrive in Beijing to Attend Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Joint Committee Meeting
    [Xinhua News Agency, 16th] The Polish Republic government has specially dispatched Mr. Winder and three other representatives to China to attend the first meeting of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Joint Committee, which will be held in Beijing to formulate specific plans for Sino-Polish cultural cooperation in 1952. Mr. Winder and others arrived in Beijing by train at 10 p.m. on the 16th. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Mr. Zeng Zhaolun, Deputy Minister of Education of the Central People's Government, Mr. Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Mr. Xu Yixin, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department, Mr. Hong Shen, Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Government Administration Council's Cultural and Educational Committee, and Mr. Wei Baoshan, Deputy Director of the Reception Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Peng Mingzhi, China's ambassador to Poland, Counselor Li Enqiu, and Do Ding, Chargé d'affaires of the Polish Embassy in China, and news commissioner Luo Buman also went to welcome them.
    Note: The Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Joint Committee was established in accordance with Article 3 of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement signed in April 1951.
    [Xinhua News Agency, 16th] The list and resumes of the Polish representatives attending the first meeting of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Joint Committee are as follows:
    Chief Representative: Winder, current Secretary-General of the Polish Republic's Foreign Cultural Cooperation Committee.
    Representative: Barbarg, current Director of the Polish Republic's Ministry of Education.
    Representative: Wierzejski, current Director of the Polish Republic's Ministry of Culture.
    Representative: Kowalczyk, current Chief of the Cultural Liaison Section of the News and Intelligence Department of the Polish Republic's Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 出席中波文化合作联合委员会会议  波兰文化代表抵北京

1952-01-17
第4版()
专栏：

    出席中波文化合作联合委员会会议
    波兰文化代表抵北京
    【新华社十六日讯】为出席将于北京召开之中波文化合作联合委员会第一次会议以制订一九五二年度中波文化合作具体计划，波兰共和国政府特派遣温德先生等四代表来华。温德先生等已于十六日下午十时乘车抵京。前往车站欢迎的有我中央人民政府教育部曾昭抡副部长、外交部办公厅阎宝航副主任、苏联东欧司徐以新副司长、政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局洪深局长、外交部交际处魏宝善副处长等。现在国内之我国驻波兰大使彭明治、参赞李恩求及波兰驻华大使馆临时代办道定、新闻专员罗步曼等亦前往欢迎。
    按：中波文化合作联合委员会系根据一九五一年四月签订之中波文化合作协定第三条成立。
    【新华社十六日讯】出席中波文化合作联合委员会第一次会议的波兰代表名单及简历如下：
    首席代表：温德，现任波兰共和国对外文化合作委员会秘书长。
    代表：巴尔巴格，现任波兰共和国教育部司长。
    代表：维尔雪克，现任波兰共和国文化部司长。
    代表：柯娃列柯娃，现任波兰共和国外交部新闻情报司文化联络科科长。
