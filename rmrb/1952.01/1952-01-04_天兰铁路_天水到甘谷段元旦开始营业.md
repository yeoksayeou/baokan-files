Title: Tianlan Railway: Tianshui to Gangu Section Opens for Business on New Year's Day
Author:
Date: 1952-01-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 2nd. The 65-kilometer Tianshui to Gangu section of the Tian (shui) Lan (zhou) Railway opened for business on New Year's Day. Another 65 kilometers of railway west of Gangu was completed on December 27th of last year and will soon be open for business.

### 天兰铁路  天水到甘谷段元旦开始营业

1952-01-04
第1版()
专栏：

    天兰铁路
    天水到甘谷段元旦开始营业
    【新华社西安二日电】天（水）兰（州）铁路天水到甘谷段六十五公里已在元旦日开始营业。甘谷以西的铁路已有六十五公里在去年十二月二十七日修通，不久就可营业。
