Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-01-06
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    After the land reform was carried out in the newly liberated areas, the political consciousness of women rapidly increased, and they universally demanded to study the Marriage Law. This is a group of women in the reading group of Yuelai Township, Jiangbei County, eastern Sichuan, who are studying news about the Marriage Law in the newspaper.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在新解放区进行了土地改革之后，妇女群众的政治觉悟迅速提高，普遍要求学习婚姻法。这是四川东部江北县悦来乡读报小组的妇女们，她们正在学习报纸上有关婚姻法的新闻。
