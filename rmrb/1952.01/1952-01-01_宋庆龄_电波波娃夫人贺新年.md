Title: Soong Ching-ling, Madam Popova Send New Year's Greetings
Author: 
Date: 1952-01-01
Page: 4

    Soong Ching-ling
    Madam Popova Send New Year's Greetings
    [Xinhua News Agency] Soong Ching-ling, Honorary Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation and Chairman of the Chinese People's National Committee for the Defense of Children, sent a telegram to Madam Popova, Chairman of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, on December 30 to congratulate her on the New Year. The telegram reads as follows: Madam Popova:
    Happy New Year, and I wish you and the Soviet sisters greater success in the struggle to defend world peace.
                            Soong Ching-ling

### 宋庆龄  电波波娃夫人贺新年
宋庆龄
1952-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄
    电波波娃夫人贺新年
    【新华社讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会名誉主席、中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会主席宋庆龄在十二月三十日致电苏联妇女反法西斯委员会主席波波娃夫人，祝贺新年。电文如下：波波娃夫人：
    敬贺新年，并祝您和苏联姊妹们在保卫世界和平的斗争中获得更大的成功。
                            宋庆龄
