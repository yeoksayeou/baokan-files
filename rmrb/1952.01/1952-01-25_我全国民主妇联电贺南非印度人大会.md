Title: All-China Democratic Women's Federation Sends Congratulatory Message to South African Indian Congress

Author:

Date: 1952-01-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 24th. The All-China Democratic Women's Federation sent a congratulatory telegram to the South African Indian Congress. The text of the telegram is as follows: Dr. Dadoo, Chairman of the South African Indian Congress:

    We extend warm congratulations to the 20th Annual Conference of the South African Indian Congress and wish you greater victory in your just struggle for basic rights and against racial discrimination and exploitation.

            All-China Democratic Women's Federation

### 我全国民主妇联电贺南非印度人大会

1952-01-25
第4版()
专栏：

    我全国民主妇联电贺南非印度人大会
    【新华社二十四日讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会电贺南非印度人大会，电文如下：南非印度人大会主席达杜博士：
    向南非印度人大会第二十届年会致以热烈祝贺，祝你们在争取基本权利、反对种族歧视与剥削的正义斗争中，获得更大的胜利。
            中华全国民主妇女联合会
