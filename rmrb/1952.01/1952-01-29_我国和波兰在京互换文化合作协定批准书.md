Title: China and Poland Exchange Instruments of Ratification of Cultural Cooperation Agreement in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1952-01-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, January 26th—The ceremony for the exchange of instruments of ratification of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the governments of China and Poland was held in Beijing on January 25th. Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Wu Xiuquan, and Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Republic of Poland in China, Dao Ding, signed the exchange of ratification certificates on behalf of the governments of China and Poland.

The Chinese side participating in the ceremony included: Minister of Culture of the Central People's Government, Shen Yanbing; Chinese Ambassador to Poland, Peng Mingzhi; Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council, Hong Shen; Deputy Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Xu Yixin; Chief Secretary of the Treaty Committee, Dong Xibai; Counselor of the Government Administration Council's Cultural and Educational Committee, Zhao Feng; Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Poland, Li Enqiu; and Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Wei Baoshan.

The Polish side included: Secretary-General of the Committee for Foreign Cultural Cooperation, Wende; Director of the Ministry of Education, Balbag; Director of the Ministry of Culture, Wierszyk; Chief of the Cultural Liaison Section of the News and Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Kowalewko; First Secretary of the Embassy in China, Baira; Commercial Attaché, Gushiduo; and News Attaché, Lu Buman.

The Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement was signed in Warsaw on April 3, 1951, and was approved by the Central People's Government Committee on November 5, 1951, and by the President of the Republic of Poland on December 4, 1951.

### 我国和波兰在京互换文化合作协定批准书

1952-01-29
第4版()
专栏：

    我国和波兰在京互换文化合作协定批准书
    【新华社二十六日讯】中波两国政府互换中波文化合作协定批准书仪式已于一月二十五日在北京举行。由中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权与波兰共和国驻华大使馆临时代办道定代表中波两国政府签署互换批准证书。参加仪式者中国方面有：中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰、我国驻波兰大使彭明治、政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深、外交部苏联东欧司副司长徐以新、条约委员会主任秘书董希白、政务院文化教育委员会参事赵沨，我国驻波兰大使馆参赞李恩求、外交部交际处副处长魏宝善；波兰方面有：对外文化合作委员会秘书长温德、教育部司长巴尔巴格、文化部司长维尔雪克、外交部新闻情报司文化联络科科长柯娃列柯娃及驻华大使馆一等秘书百拉、商务专员顾士多、新闻专员罗步曼。中波文化合作协定系于一九五一年四月三日签订于华沙、并经我中央人民政府委员会于一九五一年十一月五日批准和波兰共和国总统于一九五一年十二月四日批准。
