Title: World's Peace-Loving People Warmly Support the Korean People Over the Past Year
Author: 
Date: 1952-01-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 16th—Over the past year, the Korean people, who are engaged in a heroic struggle against the American aggressors to defend their country's independence, freedom, and world peace, have received warm support from peace-loving people around the world.

　　In the past year, the Korean people have received more than 16 million items of supplies from the people of fraternal countries. Among them, the Soviet people sent 787 boxes of medicines and a large number of film projectors, films, food, and clothing. The Chinese people sent more than 360,000 sets of cotton-padded clothes,慰问 money equivalent to more than 64 million Korean won, 192 train cars of grain, 150,000 pairs of shoes, and more than 1.279 million boxes of other supplies. The people of fraternal countries such as Poland, Bulgaria, Romania, Hungary, the German Democratic Republic, Czechoslovakia, and Albania sent a large number of medicines, medical equipment, ambulances, clothes, fabrics, blankets,慰问 money, as well as food and daily necessities. The people of the Mongolian People's Republic sent 10,000 leather overcoats, 10,000 sets of cotton-padded clothes, and 10,000 pairs each of leather boots, leather shoes, and leather gloves. The Mongolian People's delegation that recently came to Korea also brought a large number of clothes and meat as gifts. In addition, the Korean people also received a batch of medicines from the World Federation of Democratic Youth. The French people, despite the obstruction of the reactionaries, also sent a batch of medicines to the Korean people. The Austrian people sent a sum of慰问 money.

　　These relief supplies and慰问 money, which have traveled thousands of miles and carry the expectations and affection of tens of millions of peace-loving people, have been promptly distributed to the working people and refugees in various parts of Korea under the unified distribution of the Korean War Zone Disaster Relief Committee. Those who received the relief supplies and慰问 money sincerely thanked the sincere assistance of the peace-loving people all over the world. They unanimously stated that, with the support of the peace-loving people all over the world, they will fight to the end for the victory of the anti-aggression war and the defense of world peace.

### 一年来世界爱好和平人民  热烈支援朝鲜人民

1952-01-17
第4版()
专栏：

    一年来世界爱好和平人民
    热烈支援朝鲜人民
    运抵朝鲜的物资达千余万件
    【新华社平壤十六日电】为保卫祖国的独立自由和世界和平而与美国侵略者进行英勇斗争的朝鲜人民，在一九五一年中得到了全世界爱好和平的人民的热烈支援。
    在过去一年中，朝鲜人民收到各兄弟国家人民送来的物资有一千六百多万件。其中，苏联人民送来的物资有七百八十七箱医药品和大批的电影放映机、影片、食品、衣物等。中国人民送来的物资有三十六万多套棉衣、折合朝鲜币六千四百余万元的慰问金、一百九十二车皮的粮食、十五万双鞋以及一百二十七万九千多箱的其他物资。波兰、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、德意志民主共和国、捷克斯洛伐克、阿尔巴尼亚等兄弟国家人民，送来了大批医药品、医疗器具、救护车、衣服、布匹、毯子、慰问金以及食品、日用品等。蒙古人民共和国人民送来的物资有皮大衣一万件、棉衣一万套、皮靴、皮鞋和皮手套各一万双。最近来朝鲜的蒙古人民代表团又带来了大批衣服和肉类等礼物。此外，朝鲜人民并收到世界民主青年联盟送来的一批医药品。法国人民不顾反动派的阻挠也给朝鲜人民送来了一批医药品。奥地利人民送来了一笔慰问金。
    这些历经千山万水、带着成千万爱好和平人民的期望和情意的救济物资和慰问金，在朝鲜战区灾民救济委员会的统一分配下，已及时地发给了朝鲜各地的劳动人民和难民。领到救济物资和慰问金的人们衷心感谢全世界爱好和平的人民的真挚援助。他们一致表示：要在全世界爱好和平人民的支持声援下，为争取反侵略战争的胜利、保卫世界和平而奋斗到底。
