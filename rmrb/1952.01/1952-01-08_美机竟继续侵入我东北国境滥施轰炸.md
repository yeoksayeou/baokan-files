Title: U.S. Aircraft Continue to Invade Our Northeast Border and Indiscriminately Bomb
Author:
Date: 1952-01-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Andong, 7th. U.S. Air Force planes continue to intrude into our Northeast border and indiscriminately bomb. At 2:53 PM on the 6th, eight U.S. F-84 aircraft intruded into the airspace over Ji'an County, Liaodong Province, dropping fourteen bombs (three of which did not explode). Eleven of the bombs landed at Ji'an Station, injuring three of our station staff and destroying 370 meters of railway track and some other materials. This group of aircraft intruded into the airspace over Shizhuzi in Kuandian County at 3:07 PM, and then flew towards Korea.

### 美机竟继续侵入我东北国境滥施轰炸

1952-01-08
第1版()
专栏：

    美机竟继续侵入我东北国境滥施轰炸
    【新华社安东七日电】美国空军飞机继续侵入我国东北国境，滥施轰炸。六日下午二时五十三分，美国F八四式飞机八架侵入我辽东省辑安县上空，投弹十四枚（有三枚未炸），其中十一枚炸弹落于辑安车站，炸伤我车站工作人员三名，炸毁铁轨三百七十公尺及其他物资一部。这批飞机在下午三时零七分时又侵入宽甸县属石柱子上空，随后向朝鲜方面飞去。
