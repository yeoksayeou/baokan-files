    Our Great Motherland
    The Lü Dongyang Mutual Aid Team is an outstanding model mutual aid team in the northern Anhui region. Responding to Chairman Mao's call to "get organized," and adhering to the principles of voluntary mutual benefit and not neglecting both agriculture and sideline occupations, they have continuously achieved victories in production competitions and other work. This is the sideline occupation group of the Lü Dongyang Mutual Aid Team, and they are drying cellophane noodles.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-01-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    吕东扬互助组是皖北地区出色的模范互助组。他们响应毛主席“组织起来”的号召，在自愿两利和农业副业两不误的方针下，在生产竞赛和其他工作中不断地取得了胜利。这是吕东扬互助组副业小组的组员，他们正在晒粉丝。
