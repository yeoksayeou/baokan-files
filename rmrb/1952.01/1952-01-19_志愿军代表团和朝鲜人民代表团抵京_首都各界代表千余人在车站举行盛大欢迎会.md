Title: Volunteer Army Delegation and Korean People's Delegation Arrive in Beijing; Over a Thousand Representatives from All Circles in the Capital Hold a Grand Welcome Ceremony at the Station
Author:
Date: 1952-01-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th Dispatch: The Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation and the Korean People's Visiting Delegation, a total of 270 people, arrived in Beijing at 1:20 PM on the 18th.

Those who went to the station to welcome them included Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for Resisting America and Aiding Korea; Vice-Chairman Peng Zhen; and Standing Committee members Shen Junru, Shao Lizi, Ma Xulun, Ma Yinchu, Zhang Xiruo, Xu Deheng, Xu Baoju, and Peng Zemin; Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government's Revolutionary Military Commission; Yu Xinqing, Director of the Bureau of Ceremonies of the Central People's Government; Nie Zhen, Chairman of the North China General Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association; Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; Zhang Xiaomei, Chairman of the Beijing Municipal Democratic Women's Federation; representatives of various democratic parties and people's organizations; representatives of workers, troops, government organs, and schools; and Young Pioneers, totaling approximately 1,200 people. The welcomers also held a grand welcome ceremony at the station. Choi Young, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, and all embassy staff also attended the welcome ceremony at the station. At the welcome ceremony, Li Xuesan, head of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation, and Hong Sun-chol, head of the Korean People's Visiting Delegation, and Kim Cho-kyu, the Deputy Head of the delegation, received flowers from the Young Pioneers and letters of greeting from various sectors.

Guo Moruo delivered a welcome speech at the welcome ceremony, saying: "On behalf of all the Chinese people, I warmly welcome the arrival of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation and the Korean People's Visiting Delegation. At the same time, on behalf of all the Chinese people, I extend our highest and heartfelt respects to the invincible and heroic Chinese People's Volunteer Army comrades who are aiding Korea and defending the motherland, and to the heroic Korean people who are bravely resisting the American aggressors in extremely difficult circumstances to defend their country." After describing the great victories achieved by the Chinese and Korean people's troops fighting shoulder to shoulder for more than a year, he said: "Our people nationwide will work even harder in production, practice strict economy, and more powerfully support the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army, in order to achieve a peaceful resolution to the Korean issue."

Li Xuesan, head of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation, said in his reply: "The task of this Volunteer Army Returning Delegation in returning to the motherland is to thank the people of our motherland on behalf of all the Volunteer Army comrades for their full support, to report to the people of the motherland on the recent combat situation on the Korean front, and also to represent the leaders and all comrades of the Volunteer Army in extending our regards to the Volunteer Army wounded and sick comrades who have returned to the country for recuperation, and to bring the enthusiastic situation of the people of the motherland in resisting America, aiding Korea, increasing production, and practicing economy to the front lines." He continued: "Since Chairman Mao issued the call to increase production and practice economy, our Volunteer Army troops have also responded enthusiastically. The broad masses of commanders, combatants, and logistics personnel have conducted discussions during tense combat breaks, on the positions, in the fortifications, and on the transportation lines. Many units have also formulated plans for each person to save money this year, and donate the value saved to airplanes and artillery, in order to cooperate with the current vigorous increase production and practice economy movement in the motherland. Everyone is determined to respond to Chairman Mao's call with combat victories and practical actions of thrift, and to repay the support of the people of the motherland."

Hong Sun-chol, head of the Korean People's Visiting Delegation, said in his reply: "This time, our Korean People's Visiting Delegation, under the deep concern of the Communist Party of China, the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, and its great leader Chairman Mao Zedong, has come to the great People's Republic of China, the country of 475 million brotherly people. It is even more significant that our visiting delegation comes to China at a time when our Chinese brothers are carrying out a vigorous great movement of resisting America, aiding Korea, increasing production, and practicing economy under the correct leadership of the Communist Party of China and the Central People's Government. Our visiting delegation has come to your country in order to further consolidate the unity between the people of Korea and China, and to further consolidate and develop our deep combat friendship in the just struggle against our common enemy—the American aggressors." ### 志愿军代表团和朝鲜人民代表团抵京  首都各界代表千余人在车站举行盛大欢迎会

1952-01-19
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军代表团和朝鲜人民代表团抵京
    首都各界代表千余人在车站举行盛大欢迎会
    【新华社十八日讯】中国人民志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团一行二百七十人，在十八日下午一时二十分到达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有中国人民抗美援朝总会主席郭沫若、副主席彭真及常务委员沈钧儒、邵力子、马叙伦、马寅初、张奚若、许德珩、许宝驹、彭泽民，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华，中央人民政府典礼局局长余心清，华北抗美援朝总分会主席聂真，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，北京市民主妇女联合会主席张晓梅，各民主党派的代表，各人民团体的代表，工人、部队、机关、学校的代表和少年儿童队队员等约一千二百人。欢迎者并在车站举行了盛大的欢迎会。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办崔英和大使馆全体人员亦到车站参加欢迎会。欢迎会上，中国人民志愿军归国代表团团长李雪三、朝鲜人民访华代表团团长洪淳哲、副团长金朝奎接受了少年儿童队队员的献花和各方面的慰问信。
    郭沫若在欢迎会上致欢迎词说：“我代表全中国人民热烈地欢迎中国人民志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团的到来，同时我代表全中国人民向援助朝鲜和捍卫祖国的、无敌的、英雄的中国人民志愿军同志致崇高的衷心的敬礼，向在极其艰苦环境中奋勇抗击美国侵略军为保卫祖国而战的英雄的朝鲜人民致崇高的衷心的敬礼。”他在叙述中朝人民部队一年多来并肩作战取得的伟大胜利后说：“我们全国人民将更加努力生产，厉行节约，更有力地支援中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军，以取得朝鲜问题的和平解决。”
    中国人民志愿军归国代表团团长李雪三致答词说：“这次志愿军归国代表团回到祖国来的任务，就是代表志愿军全体同志感谢全力支援我们的祖国人民，向祖国人民报告最近以来的朝鲜前线的斗争情况，还要代表志愿军首长和全体同志，慰问回国休养的志愿军伤病员同志，并把祖国人民抗美援朝、增产节约的热烈情形，带到前线去。”他接着说：“自毛主席提出增产节约的号召以后，我们志愿军部队也热烈地响应了。广大的指挥员、战斗员、后勤工作人员，在紧张的战斗空隙里，在阵地上、工事里和运输线上，都进行了讨论。许多部队还订出今年每人的节约计划，以节约所值捐献飞机、大炮，来配合祖国目前正在开展着的轰轰烈烈的增产节约运动。大家决心以战斗的胜利和节约的实际行动来响应毛主席的号召，和答谢祖国人民的支援。”
    朝鲜人民访华代表团团长洪淳哲致答词说：“这次我们朝鲜人民访华代表团，在中国共产党、中华人民共和国中央人民政府及其伟大的领袖毛泽东主席的深切关怀下，来到了四亿七千五百万兄弟般的人民国家——伟大的中华人民共和国。正当中国兄弟们在中国共产党和中央人民政府正确的领导下，开展着轰轰烈烈的抗美援朝、增产节约的伟大运动的时候，我们访华代表团来到中国，这个意义更是巨大的。我们访华代表团来到贵国，是为了朝中两国人民的团结更加巩固，为了在反对共同敌人——美国侵略者的正义斗争中，使我们深厚的战斗友谊，更加巩固和发展。”（附图片）
    中国人民志愿军归国代表团团长李雪三（左）、朝鲜人民访华代表团团长洪淳哲（中）、副团长金朝奎（右）等在北京车站接受各界代表的热烈欢迎
