Title: Representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Delegation and the Korean People's Delegation Report Heroic Deeds from the Front to People from All Walks of Life in the Capital; Visit Hospitals to Comfort Wounded and Sick Soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the People's Liberation Army

Author:

Date: 1952-01-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 23rd: Representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation and the Korean People's Delegation to China reported to workers, troops, government employees, students, women, industrialists, and merchants in Beijing on the 21st and 22nd about the heroic deeds of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army in fighting the American aggressors on the Korean front. Approximately 12,000 people attended.

The representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation reported at the meeting the brilliant achievements of the Volunteer Army on the Korean front over the past year. They especially mentioned: Over the past year, we have learned a set of experiences to defeat the American aggressors. This is a valuable experience we have gained with blood and sweat. We will use these experiences more accurately and flexibly to strike the enemy more forcefully. The Volunteer Army representatives also reported on the enthusiastic campaign among the soldiers to increase production and practice strict economy. One Volunteer Army representative stated on the spot that he would save two sets of shirts and other supplies for the motherland this year. He was warmly cheered by the audience.

The representatives of the Korean People's Delegation to China reported the heinous crimes committed by the American aggressors on Korean soil. They said: From the beginning of the Korean War to the incomplete statistics of January 1951, the enemy had destroyed more than 610,000 factories, enterprises, schools, hospitals, and residences in Korea. They also reported the great achievements they had made in the past year and their heroic deeds in battle. They introduced the close relationship between the Volunteer Army and the Korean people. They said: The Volunteer Army has saved a large amount of military grain to relieve the disaster victims in the Korean war zone and actively help the people in the rear with spring plowing, weeding, and autumn harvesting. The people in the rear of Korea also actively help the Volunteer Army comrades. Many Korean women enthusiastically compete to sew clothes and mend shoes for the Volunteer Army.

The people from all walks of life in Beijing love the representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation and the Korean People's Delegation to China with a respectful heart. When the representatives appeared in front of the audience, the audience immediately rushed up to shake hands with them, exchange commemorative badges with them, and ask them to sign their names.

Xinhua News Agency, January 23rd: Representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation and the Korean People's Delegation to China visited various hospitals in batches on the 23rd to comfort the wounded and sick soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

They held consolation meetings in the hospitals and distributed Commander Peng's letter to the wounded and sick comrades of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army returning home for recuperation and the condolence message from Hong Sun-chul, head of the Korean People's Delegation to China, to the wounded of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army.

Hong Sun-chul, head of the Korean People's Delegation to China, delivered a condolence message to the wounded and sick soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army at the consolation meeting, saying: "We Korean people will never forget the noble blood you shed on Korean soil and the immortal feats you have established. Our descendants will carry on your noble honor with deep gratitude forever."

### 志愿军代表团和朝鲜人民代表团的代表  向首都各界报告前线英勇事迹  分赴各医院慰问志愿军和解放军伤病员

1952-01-24
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军代表团和朝鲜人民代表团的代表
    向首都各界报告前线英勇事迹
    分赴各医院慰问志愿军和解放军伤病员
    【新华社二十三日讯】中国人民志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团的代表，于二十一日和二十二日，在北京市向工人、部队、机关工作人员、学生、妇女、工商业者等，报告了中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军在朝鲜前线跟美国侵略军作战的英勇事迹。听众共约一万二千人。
    中国人民志愿军归国代表团的代表在报告会上报告了志愿军一年多来在朝鲜前线所获得的辉煌战绩。他们特别说到：一年多来，我们已学到了战胜美国侵略军的一套经验。这是我们用血和汗换来的宝贵经验，我们将更准确地灵活地运用这些经验，更有力地打击敌人。志愿军代表并报告了战士们热烈展开增加生产，厉行节约运动的情况。一个志愿军代表当场表示要在今年为祖国节省两套衬衣，以及其它用品。他受到听众们的热烈欢呼。
    朝鲜人民访华代表团的代表报告了美国侵略军在朝鲜土地上所犯下的滔天罪行。他们说：从朝鲜战争开始到一九五一年一月的不完全统计，敌人已破坏朝鲜六十一万栋以上的工厂、企业、学校、医院和住宅。他们并报告了一年多来所获得的伟大战绩，以及在战斗中的英勇事迹。他们介绍了志愿军和朝鲜人民的亲密关系。他们说：志愿军曾节约大批军粮，救济朝鲜战区的灾民，并积极帮助后方人民春耕、除草和秋收。朝鲜后方的人民也积极帮助志愿军同志。许多朝鲜妇女热情地争着给志愿军缝衣补鞋。
    北京市各界人民以崇敬的心情，热爱着中国人民志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团的代表。当代表们出现在听众面前时，听众们即一拥而上，争着和他们握手，和他们互换纪念章，请他们签名。
    【新华社二十三日讯】中国人民志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团的代表，二十三日分批到各医院去慰问中国人民志愿军和中国人民解放军的伤病员。
    他们在医院里举行了慰问大会，散发了志愿军彭司令员告中国人民志愿军回国休养的伤病员同志书和朝鲜人民访华代表团团长洪淳哲对中国人民志愿军伤员的慰问词。
    朝鲜人民访华代表团团长洪淳哲，在慰问大会上向中国人民志愿军的伤病员致慰问词说：“我们朝鲜人民永远不会忘记你们在朝鲜疆土上所流的高贵的鲜血和你们树立的不朽的功勋。我们子孙万代将会带着深厚的感谢，把你们高贵的荣誉，永远地传颂着。”
