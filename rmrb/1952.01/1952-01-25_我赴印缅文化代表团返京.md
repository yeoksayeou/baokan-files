Title: Our Cultural Delegation to India and Burma Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-01-25
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, January 24th. The members of the Chinese Cultural Delegation to India and Burma, led by head of the delegation Ding Xilin and deputy head Li Yimang, victoriously completed their mission to visit India and Burma and returned to Beijing on the 24th. Those who came to the station to welcome them included: Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Yan Baohang, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Culture Sha Kefu, Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Government Administration Council's Committee of Cultural and Educational Affairs Hong Shen, Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Chen Yi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression Wu Maosun, and more than 50 others. Burmese Ambassador U Hla Maw, Indian Embassy Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Gore, and all the staff of the Indian and Burmese embassies also came to the station to welcome them. The Chinese cultural delegation left Beijing on September 20th last year and arrived in India on October 28th. After conducting a six-week friendly visit to India, they conducted a nineteen-day friendly visit to Burma. During the delegation's visit to India and Burma, they were warmly welcomed by the governments and peoples of both countries. This trip by the delegation made an important contribution to promoting the friendship and cultural relations between the Chinese and Indian/Burmese peoples.

### 我赴印缅文化代表团返京

1952-01-25
第4版()
专栏：

    我赴印缅文化代表团返京
    【新华社二十四日讯】胜利地完成了访问印缅任务的中国赴印缅文化代表团人员，在团长丁西林、副团长李一氓的率领下，于二十四日返抵北京。到车站欢迎的有：外交部副部长章汉夫、外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航、文化部办公厅主任沙可夫、政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深、人民革命军事委员会总政治部文化部部长陈沂、中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会副秘书长吴茂荪等五十余人。缅甸大使吴拉茂、印度大使馆临时代办高尔以及印度、缅甸两国大使馆全体馆员也到车站欢迎。中国文化代表团在去年九月二十日离京，十月二十八日抵印，在印度进行了六星期的友好访问以后，又在缅甸进行了十九天的友好访问。代表团在印、缅两国访问期间曾受到印、缅两国政府和人民的热烈欢迎。代表团此行对促进中印、中缅人民的友谊和文化关系有重要的贡献。
