    Our Great Motherland
    The Yangko dance is becoming increasingly popular and improved in various parts of our country. During festivals, people often happily dance the Yangko. This is a scene of the Yangko team from the Central Academy of Drama in Beijing singing and dancing in Tiananmen Square.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    秧歌舞在我国各地日益普及和提高了。每逢节日，人们常常愉快地扭起秧歌舞来。这是北京中央戏剧学院的秧歌队正在天安门前载歌载舞前进的情形。
