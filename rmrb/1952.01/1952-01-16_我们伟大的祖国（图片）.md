Our great motherland

The militia work in various places is developing day by day, which is very helpful for consolidating local public security. The Northeast Military Region is implementing a universal militia system in a planned, step-by-step, and focused manner, bringing the Northeast people's armed construction into a new stage and further consolidating the public security in the Northeast. This is the situation of the militia in Changdian District, Kuandian County, Liaodong Province, actively engaged in military training.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-01-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    各地民兵工作日益发展，这对于巩固地方治安很有作用。东北军区正在有计划、有步骤、有重点地实行普遍的民兵制度，使东北人民武装建设进入新的阶段，更进一步巩固了东北的治安。这是辽东省宽甸县长甸区民兵，正在积极从事军事训练的情形。
