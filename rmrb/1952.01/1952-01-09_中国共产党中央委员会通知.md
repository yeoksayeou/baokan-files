    Circular of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China approves all the resolutions of the Second Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League and Comrade Feng Wenbin's report on the "Current Situation and Work of the Communist Youth League" to this session. It is hoped that the Party committees at all levels will lead the local Communist Youth League committees to conscientiously discuss and implement the contents of these resolutions and reports.
                    Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, January 4, 1952

### 中国共产党中央委员会通知

1952-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

    中国共产党中央委员会通知
    中国共产党中央委员会同意青年团中央委员会第二次全体会议的各项决议及冯文彬同志向这次会议所作的“青年团的目前情况与工作”的报告。希望各级党委领导各地青年团团委认真讨论和贯彻实现这些决议和报告的内容。
                    中国共产党中央委员会一九五二年一月四日
