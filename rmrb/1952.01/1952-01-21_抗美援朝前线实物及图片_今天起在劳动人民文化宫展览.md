Author:
Date: 1952-01-21
Page: 1

    Exhibits of Frontline Supplies and Photos from the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea Open Today at the Palace of Culture for the Working People
    [This newspaper dispatch] The exhibition of frontline supplies and photos from the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea, organized by the China People's Committee to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea, will open today (January 21st) at the Beijing Palace of Culture for the Working People. The exhibits were recently brought back from the Korean front by the Chinese People's Volunteer Army delegation. The exhibits include many utensils, combat tools, bedding, food, self-made food and beverage utensils, cultural and recreational equipment used by frontline soldiers in their battlefield life, as well as soldiers' works and battlefield photos. These objects reflect the great Volunteer Army soldiers' spirit of revolutionary heroism of self-sacrifice, hard struggle, and overcoming all difficulties, and will have a great educational and inspiring effect on the nationwide movement of increasing production and practicing economy and opposing corruption, waste, and bureaucracy that is currently underway. The exhibits will soon be taken to various places for exhibition by the Volunteer Army delegation.

### 抗美援朝前线实物及图片  今天起在劳动人民文化宫展览

1952-01-21
第1版()
专栏：

    抗美援朝前线实物及图片
    今天起在劳动人民文化宫展览
    【本报讯】中国人民抗美援朝总会举办的抗美援朝前线实物及图片展览，今天（二十一日）开始在北京市劳动人民文化宫展出。这次展览的实物和图片是由中国人民志愿军归国代表团最近从朝鲜前线带回来的。展览品包括前线战士们在战地生活中的许多用具、作战工具、被服、食物、自己制造的饮食器具、文化娱乐器具以及战士的作品和战场照片等。这些实物反映了伟大的志愿军战士自我牺牲、艰苦奋斗、克服一切困难的革命英雄主义精神，对于全国正在开展的增产节约和反贪污、反浪费、反官僚主义运动，具有很大的教育和鼓舞作用。展览品不久将由志愿军归国代表团分别带往各地展览。
