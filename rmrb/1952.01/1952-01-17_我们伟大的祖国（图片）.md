Author:
Date: 1952-01-17

    Our Great Motherland
    The advanced production team led by Liu Lifu, a machine-building worker and member of the Communist Youth League in Dalian, has been averaging 265 percent of its monthly production quota for the past year, with all products meeting quality standards. The outstanding achievements of the Liu Lifu team have attracted widespread attention in factories throughout Dalian. The picture shows the Liu Lifu team holding a technical research meeting.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-01-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    旅大市机器制造工人、青年团员刘立富所领导的先进生产小组，一年来，每月平均完成生产任务百分之二百六十五，产品质量全部合乎规格。刘立富小组的卓越成绩已经引起了旅大市各工厂的普遍重视。这是刘立富小组在开技术研究会的情形。
