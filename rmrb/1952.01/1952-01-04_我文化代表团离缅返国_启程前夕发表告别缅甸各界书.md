Title: Our Cultural Delegation Returns Home from Burma; Issues Farewell Letter to All Circles of Burma Before Departure
Author:
Date: 1952-01-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 3rd: After a nineteen-day visit to Burma, the Chinese Cultural Delegation boarded a ship on December 28th to return home. U Tun Shein, Permanent Secretary of the Burmese Government's Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Tekin Ba Yu, Vice Chairman of the Burma-China Friendship Association, and Yao Zhongming, our Ambassador to Burma, along with more than 1,500 others, went to the dock to see them off.

The delegation held a reception before leaving Burma to bid farewell to all circles in Burma. Burmese Federal Prime Minister Tekin Nu, Burmese Government Education Minister U Dan Wang, and well-known writer Tekin Ko Tu Mai, among more than 300 people, attended the reception.

The delegation issued a farewell letter, stating: "The friendly relations between the peoples of China and Burma have seen further new development due to the visit of the Burmese People's Observation Delegation to our country and the visit of the Chinese Cultural Delegation to Burma. This deep friendship between the Chinese and Burmese peoples not only greatly helps the two peoples in building their own cultures, but also makes an important contribution to defending peace in Asia and the world."

### 我文化代表团离缅返国  启程前夕发表告别缅甸各界书

1952-01-04
第4版()
专栏：

    我文化代表团离缅返国
    启程前夕发表告别缅甸各界书
    【新华社三日讯】仰光消息：中国文化代表团在缅甸访问了十九天以后，已在十二月二十八日乘轮回国，缅甸政府外交部常任秘书宇吞盛、缅中友好协会副主席德钦巴宇以及我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明等一千五百多人都到码头欢送。
    代表团在离开缅甸的前夕曾举行招待会，向缅甸各界告别，出席招待会的有缅甸联邦总理德钦努、缅甸政府教育部长宇丹旺、著名作家德钦哥都迈等三百多人。
    代表团发表了告别书，指出：“中缅两国人民的友好关系，由于缅甸人民观礼代表团的访问我国，和中国文化代表团的访问缅甸，已经有了进一步的新的发展。中缅人民的这种深厚友情，不仅对两国人民在建设自己的文化上有很大的帮助，而且对保卫亚洲以及世界的和平亦具有重要贡献。”
