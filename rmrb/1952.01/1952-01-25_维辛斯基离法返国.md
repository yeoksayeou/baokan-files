Title: Vyshinsky Departs France to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1952-01-25
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, January 24th: According to TASS, Vyshinsky, head of the Soviet delegation to the Sixth Session of the United Nations General Assembly and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, left Paris on the 22nd to return to Moscow.
    Those who came to the station to see Vyshinsky off included: Malik, Deputy Head of the Soviet delegation, and other members and staff of the Soviet delegation, the heads and members of the Ukrainian, Belarusian, Polish, and Czechoslovakian delegations, the Soviet Ambassador to France, Pavlov, diplomatic representatives of various People's Democracies, representatives of the United Nations Secretariat, and representatives of the French Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 维辛斯基离法返国

1952-01-25
第4版()
专栏：

    维辛斯基离法返国
    【新华社二十四日讯】据塔斯社巴黎讯：苏联出席联合国第六届大会代表团团长、苏联外交部部长维辛斯基已在二十二日离巴黎返莫斯科。
    到车站欢送维辛斯基的有：苏联代表团副团长马立克和苏联代表团其他团员和工作人员，乌克兰、白俄罗斯、波兰和捷克斯洛伐克各国代表团的团长和团员，苏联驻法大使巴甫洛夫，各人民民主国家驻法外交代表，联合国秘书处的代表，法国外交部的代表等。
