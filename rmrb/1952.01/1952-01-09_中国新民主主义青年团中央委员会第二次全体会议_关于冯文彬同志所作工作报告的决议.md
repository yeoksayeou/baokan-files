Title: Resolution of the Second Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League on the Work Report by Comrade Feng Wenbin
Author:
Date: 1952-01-09
Page: 1

    The Second Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League
    Resolution on the Work Report by Comrade Feng Wenbin
    (Adopted by the Second Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Youth League on November 26, 1951)
    The Second Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League unanimously approved the report "The Current Situation and Work of the Youth League" made by Comrade Feng Wenbin, and called on all members to strive to realize the various tasks put forward in the report.

### 中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会第二次全体会议  关于冯文彬同志所作工作报告的决议

1952-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

    中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会第二次全体会议
    关于冯文彬同志所作工作报告的决议
    （一九五一年十一月二十六日青年团中央委员会第二次全体会议通过）
    中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会第二次全体会议，一致同意冯文彬同志所作的“青年团的目前情况与工作”的报告，并号召全体团员努力实现报告中提出来的各项任务。
