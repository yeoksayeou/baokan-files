Title: Mongolian People's Delegation Arrives in Beijing After Visiting Korea
Author:
Date: 1952-01-29
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, January 26th) The Mongolian People's Delegation, having completed its mission to visit Korea, arrived in Beijing by train today. The delegation, led by Damba Dashi, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Chairman of the Great Hural, will stay in Beijing for several days before returning home.

Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the China People's Committee for Resisting America and Aiding Korea, Secretary-General Liu Guanyi, and representatives from the China New Democratic Youth League and the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation went to the station to welcome them.

Jalkasuren, Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic, Choi Young, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Korean Embassy, and personnel from the Mongolian Embassy were also at the station to welcome them.

(Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, January 25th) The Mongolian People's Delegation, which came to Korea to support the just struggle of the Korean people, left here on the 22nd to return home after a seventeen-day visit, having arrived in Pyongyang on the 6th.

The Mongolian People's Delegation brought with them over 500 tons of meat and 5,000 sets of leather clothing, gifts from the Mongolian people to the Korean people. During their stay in Korea, they visited combat heroes of the Korean People's Army and model workers, farmers, youths, and women, and observed firsthand the bloody atrocities committed by the American aggressors on Korean soil. The delegation also met with Premier Kim Il-sung of the Korean Cabinet and Chairman Kim Tu-bong of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, conveying the greetings of the Mongolian people to them.

Before the delegation returned home, Park Jung-ai, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, presented a silk banner on behalf of the Korean Workers' Party to Damba Dashi, head of the Mongolian People's Delegation and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, asking him to forward it to the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party. The delegation also received gifts from General Kim Il-sung to the Government of the Mongolian People's Republic and Marshal Choibalsan, leader of the Mongolian people, as well as many gifts from the Korean people to the Mongolian people.

On the 21st, the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea awarded the Second Class National Flag Order to Genzen Darjaa, a member of the Mongolian People's Delegation and representative of Mongolian workers, in recognition of his contributions to strengthening the unity and friendship between the two peoples and assisting the just war of the Korean people.

The delegation expressed gratitude for the warm hospitality of the Korean people and stated that upon returning home, they would widely introduce the heroic struggle of the Korean people to the Mongolian people and further support the Korean people's anti-aggression struggle.

### 蒙古人民代表团  访问朝鲜后抵达北京

1952-01-29
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民代表团
    访问朝鲜后抵达北京
    【新华社二十六日讯】蒙古人民代表团在完成访问朝鲜的任务后，于今日乘车抵达北京。代表团在团长、蒙古人民革命党中央委员会书记、大呼拉尔主席达姆巴·大辛的率领下，将在北京勾留数日后返国。
    赴车站欢迎的有中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席陈叔通、秘书长刘贯一以及中国新民主主义青年团和北京市民主妇女联合会的代表等人。
    蒙古人民共和国大使贾尔卡赛汗和朝鲜大使馆临时代办崔英以及蒙古大使馆的人员，亦到车站欢迎。
    【新华社平壤二十五日电】为声援朝鲜人民的正义斗争而来朝鲜的蒙古人民代表团，自六日抵达平壤后，经过十七天的参观访问，已经在二十二日离此返国。
    蒙古人民代表团此行曾随带了蒙古人民送给朝鲜人民的五百多吨肉类和五千多套皮衣的礼物。他们在朝鲜期间，慰问了朝鲜人民军的战斗英雄和工人、农民、青年、妇女中的模范，并实地观察了美国侵略者在朝鲜土地上所制造的血腥暴行。代表团并曾先后谒见朝鲜内阁首相金日成和朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉，代表蒙古人民向他们致敬。
    在代表团返国前，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会书记朴正爱曾代表朝鲜劳动党以锦旗一面交给蒙古人民代表团团长、蒙古人民革命党中央委员会书记达姆巴·大辛，请他转致蒙古人民革命党。代表团并收到金日成将军送给蒙古人民共和国政府和蒙古人民领袖乔巴山元帅的礼物以及朝鲜人民送给蒙古人民的许多礼物。
    朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会在二十一日以二级国旗勋章授予蒙古人民代表团团员、蒙古工人代表根兹恩·达鲁左阿，以感谢他在加强两国人民的团结友谊和援助朝鲜人民正义战争的工作中的贡献。
    代表团对朝鲜人民的热情款待表示感谢，并表示在回国后要普遍地向蒙古人民介绍朝鲜人民的英勇斗争事迹，进一步支援朝鲜人民的反侵略斗争。
