Title: Brazilian Radio and Television Workers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-06
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, May 5th: The Brazilian radio and television workers delegation, invited by Beijing Radio to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The delegation members are: head of the delegation, Oduvaldo Vianna; members Deucélia Vianna, Túlio Lemos, and Rubens Galvão. Jin Zhao, head of Beijing Radio, and Wang Jiabin, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, were among those who welcomed them at the airport. Another member of the delegation, José Morais, is on his way to China.

### 巴西广播电视工作者代表团到京

1960-05-06
第6版()
专栏：

    巴西广播电视工作者代表团到京
    新华社5日讯　应北京广播电台邀请来中国访问的巴西广播电视工作者代表团，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团成员有团长奥杜瓦尔多·维阿纳，团员德乌丝赛丽阿·维阿娜、图里奥·莱摩斯、鲁本斯·加尔瓦奥。前往机场欢迎的有北京广播电台负责人金照、中国拉丁美洲友好协会副总干事王家宾等。代表团另一成员何塞·莫拉伊斯正在来华途中。
