Title: Abundant and Diverse Celebration Activities of Shenjiamen Fishermen
Author:
Date: 1960-05-04
Page: 4

Shenjiamen Fishermen Celebrate with Rich and Colorful Activities

(Reported by this newspaper) Shenjiamen, a famous fishing port in Zhoushan County, Zhejiang Province, launched a variety of colorful celebration activities on May 1st to enjoy the "May Day" International Labor Day.

That morning, groups of fishermen, dressed in festive attire, came from fishing boats and islands to the streets to participate in various celebration activities with workers, cadres, residents, and students from the town.

More than a thousand people from all walks of life in the town held a celebration meeting, where representatives of fishermen, workers, and farmers reported on new production achievements.

Some fishermen from various communes in Zhoushan and some fishermen from Fujian Province who were fishing in Zhoushan, celebrating the "May Day" festival in Shenjiamen fishing port, warmly celebrated the unprecedented bumper harvest of this year's spring fishing season, and expressed their determination to further boost their efforts and strive for an even greater harvest in the summer fishing season.

### 沈家门渔民庆祝活动丰富多彩

1960-05-04
第4版()
专栏：

    沈家门渔民庆祝活动丰富多彩
    本报讯　我国著名渔港浙江舟山县沈家门5月1日开展丰富多彩的各项庆祝活动，尽情地欢度“五一”国际劳动节。
    这天清早，成群结队的渔民，换上了节日盛装，从渔船上、渔岛上来到街头，和镇上的工人、干部、居民、学生一起，参加各项庆祝活动。
    镇上各界人民一千多人举行了庆祝大会，渔民、工人、农民代表在会上汇报了新的生产成绩。
    在沈家门渔港欢度“五一”节的舟山各公社的部分渔民和正在舟山捕鱼的福建省部分渔民，热烈庆祝今年春汛生产空前大丰收，并纷纷表示决心，要进一步鼓足干劲，争取夏汛更大的丰收。
