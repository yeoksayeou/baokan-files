Title: Ochab and Others Receive CCP Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1960-05-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 11th. Ochab, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, and Kliszko, received the Chinese Communist Party delegation headed by Bo Yibo in Warsaw today at noon, and the two sides held cordial talks.

Present at the meeting were Jarosinski, Secretary of the Central Committee of the United Workers' Party, Czesak, Minister of Liaison, and Titkov, First Secretary of the Warsaw Municipal Party Committee. Wang Bingnan, Chinese Ambassador to Poland, was also present.

### 奥哈布等接见中共代表团

1960-05-13
第5版()
专栏：

    奥哈布等接见中共代表团
    新华社华沙11日电　波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记奥哈布和克利什科，今天中午在华沙接见了以薄一波为首的中国共产党代表团，双方并进行了亲切的会谈。
    会谈时在座的，有统一工人党中央委员会书记雅罗辛斯基、联络部长切萨克、华沙市委第一书记蒂特科夫。中国驻波兰大使王炳南也在座。
