Title: Our Volleyball Team Competes with the Soviet "Petrel" Team
Author:
Date: 1960-05-17
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 15th. TASS reports: The Chinese men's and women's volleyball teams held friendly matches with the local "Petrel" Sports Association team in Odessa on the 14th. As a result of the match, the Chinese women's team won and the men's team lost.

### 我排球队同苏联“海燕”队比赛

1960-05-17
第7版()
专栏：

    我排球队同苏联“海燕”队比赛
    据新华社莫斯科15日电　据塔斯社报道：中国男、女排球队14日在敖德萨同当地“海燕”体育协会队举行了友谊比赛。比赛结果，中国队女胜男败。
