Title: Iraqi Trade Delegation Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-28
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, May 27th—The Iraqi Republic government trade delegation, headed by Iraqi Minister of Commerce Abdul Latif al-Shawwaf, left Beijing by plane today.
    Those who went to the airport to see them off included Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, Lu Xuzhang, Vice Minister, Liu Xiwen, Director of the Fourth Bureau, Qiao Peixin, Vice President of the People's Bank of China, and Gong Dafei, Deputy Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
    Fadil, the Iraqi Republic's ambassador to China, and others also went to the airport to see them off.

### 伊拉克贸易代表团离京

1960-05-28
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克贸易代表团离京
    新华社27日讯　以伊拉克商业部部长阿卜杜勒·拉蒂夫·沙瓦夫为首的伊拉克共和国政府贸易代表团一行四人，今天乘飞机离开北京。
    前往机场送行的有对外贸易部部长叶季壮，副部长卢绪章，第四局局长刘希文，中国人民银行副行长乔培新，外交部西亚非洲司副司长宫达非等人。
    伊拉克共和国驻华大使法迪尔等也到机场送行。
