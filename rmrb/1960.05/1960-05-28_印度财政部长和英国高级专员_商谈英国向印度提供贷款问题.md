Title: Indian Finance Minister and British High Commissioner Discuss British Loans to India
Author:
Date: 1960-05-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 26th: According to today’s “Statesman” report, Indian Finance Minister Desai held talks with the British High Commissioner to India on the issue of Britain providing new loans to India for the final year of India's Second Five-Year Plan.

The newspaper said that in the past two years, Britain has given India an average of 500 million rupees annually, and has provided India with the same amount this year.

### 印度财政部长和英国高级专员  商谈英国向印度提供贷款问题

1960-05-28
第4版()
专栏：

    印度财政部长和英国高级专员
    商谈英国向印度提供贷款问题
    新华社新德里26日电　据“政治家报”今天报道，印度财政部长德赛同英国驻印度的高级专员就英国为印度第二个五年计划最后一年提供新贷款的问题举行了会谈。
    这家报纸说，在过去两年中英国平均每年给印度五亿卢比，今年也给了印度同样数目的款项。
