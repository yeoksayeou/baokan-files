Title: African Workers Support Japanese Workers' Patriotic and Just Struggle
Author:
Date: 1960-05-29
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, 28th. The delegation of the National Workers' Union of the Congo, which is visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, today sent a telegram to the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan in the name of the National Workers' Union of the Congo, guaranteeing that the National Workers' Union of the Congo will firmly stand on the side of the Japanese working class in the General Council's just struggle against the U.S.-Japan reactionaries.
    The telegram to the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan stated: "At a time when the Japanese people are facing difficult times caused by Kishi Nobusuke, the National Workers' Union of the Congo assures the Japanese working class of complete solidarity. We encourage the General Council to continue its struggle against the maintenance of U.S. military bases in Japan. We stand firmly by your side."
    The delegation of the National Workers' Union of the Congo also sent a protest telegram to the Kishi Nobusuke group today, stating: "The National Workers' Union of the Congo strongly protests the infringement of democracy in the passage of the U.S.-Japan military alliance treaty.
    We believe that the maintenance of U.S. military bases in Japan is a serious threat to the Far East.
    We, together with other peace-loving workers' organizations, support the actions of the General Council."
    Xinhua News Agency, 28th. Omar, chairman of the Zanzibar and Pemba Federation of Labour, and Ali Sultan Issa, organizing secretary of the Zanzibar Federation of Labour, who are visiting China, today sent a telegram to the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, expressing their firm support for the General Council's actions in exposing the traitorous policies of Kishi Nobusuke and mobilizing the entire Japanese working class and people to defend world peace.
    Omar and Ali Sultan Issa's telegram stated: "We firmly support the General Council's actions in exposing the traitorous policies of Kishi Nobusuke and mobilizing the entire Japanese working class and people to defend world peace. We will do everything in our power to promote your victory. Kishi Nobusuke is bound to end up like Syngman Rhee. Only through struggle can we reach our final goal. With brotherly congratulations, we wish you victory."
    Omar and Ali Sultan Issa also sent a telegram to Japanese Prime Minister Kishi Nobusuke today, saying:
  "We, the representatives of the workers of Zanzibar, resolutely protest against the passage of the U.S.-Japan Security Treaty by your unrepresentative government. We firmly believe that your traitorous policies will be defeated by the Japanese people. We further declare that your actions are contrary to the wishes of the people of Asia and Africa and endanger world peace."
    Xinhua News Agency, 28th. Mohammed Dahan, technical advisor of the Somaliland African Independent Trade Union Federation and political secretary of the Somaliland Democratic League Party, who is visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, today sent a telegram to the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, supporting their just struggle against the U.S.-Japan military alliance treaty.
    Mohammed Dahan's telegram stated: "The Somaliland working class, fighting for world peace and world prosperity, condemns the messengers of war—U.S. imperialism. The Somaliland working class strongly supports your struggle for world peace."
    Mohammed Dahan also sent a telegram to the Japanese Kishi Nobusuke government today, saying: "The Somaliland Trade Union Federation, the Democratic League Party delegation strongly protests the U.S.-Japan military alliance, which means a declaration of war on China and the world's desire for peace."

### 非洲工人支持日本工人爱国正义斗争  刚果全国工人联合会代表团说：美国在日本的军事基地是对远东的威胁  桑给巴尔和奔巴工会领袖说：岸信介的卖国政策必将被日本人民所击溃  索马里兰工会领袖说：日美军事联盟是对全世界要求和平的愿望的宣战

1960-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

    非洲工人支持日本工人爱国正义斗争
    刚果全国工人联合会代表团说：美国在日本的军事基地是对远东的威胁
    桑给巴尔和奔巴工会领袖说：岸信介的卖国政策必将被日本人民所击溃
    索马里兰工会领袖说：日美军事联盟是对全世界要求和平的愿望的宣战
    新华社28日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请正在我国访问的刚果全国工人联合会代表团，今天在北京以刚果全国工人联合会的名义致电日本工会总评议会，保证在总评反对美日反动派的正义斗争中，刚果全国工人联合会坚决站在日本工人阶级一边。
    这份致日本工会总评议会的电报说：“当日本人民面临由岸信介造成的困难时期的时候，刚果全国工人联合会向日本工人阶级保证完全的团结。我们鼓励总评继续为反对美国在日本维持军事基地而进行的斗争。我们坚决站在你们一边。”
    刚果全国工人联合会代表团今天还在给岸信介集团的抗议电中说：“在通过日美军事同盟条约的时候，刚果全国工人联合会强烈抗议对民主的侵犯。
    我们认为，维持在日本的美国军事基地就是对远东的严重威胁。
    我们和其他爱好和平的工人组织一起，支持总评的行动。”
    新华社28日讯　正在我国访问的桑给巴尔和奔巴劳工联合会主席欧马、桑给巴尔劳工联合会组织书记阿里·苏勒丹·伊萨今天打电报给日本工会总评议会，表示坚决支持总评揭露岸信介卖国政策和动员全日本工人阶级和人民保卫世界和平的行动。
    欧马和阿里·苏勒丹·伊萨的电报说：“我们坚决支持总评揭露岸信介卖国政策和动员全日本工人阶级和人民保卫世界和平的行动。我们将尽一切努力促使你们胜利。岸信介必然要走李承晚的下场。只有通过斗争我们才能达到最后目标。致以兄弟般的祝贺，祝你们胜利。”
    欧马和阿里·苏勒丹·伊萨今天还打电报给日本首相岸信介说：
  “我们桑给巴尔工人代表坚决抗议你们无代表性的政府通过日美安全条约。我们坚信你们的卖国政策将被日本人民所击溃。我们进一步宣布你们的行动是违背亚洲、非洲全体人民的意愿并危害世界和平。”
    新华社28日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请来我国访问的索马里兰非洲人独立工会联合会技术顾问、索马里兰民主联盟党政治书记穆罕默德·达汉今天打电报给日本工会总评议会，支持他们反对日美军事同盟条约的正义斗争。
    穆罕默德·达汉的电报说：“为争取世界和平和世界繁荣而斗争的索马里兰工人阶级谴责战争使者——美帝国主义。索马里兰工人阶级大力支持你们的争取世界和平的斗争。”
    穆罕默德·达汉今天还打电报给日本岸信介政府说：“索马里兰工会联合会、民主联盟党代表团强烈抗议日美军事联盟，这种联盟意味着对中国和全世界要求和平的愿望的宣战。”
