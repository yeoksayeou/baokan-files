Title: Liao Chengzhi Hosts a Banquet for Habi and Abdillahi
Author:
Date: 1960-05-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, May 5th. Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, hosted a banquet today to welcome Mohammed Habi, former Prime Minister of French Somaliland and Chairman of the Democratic Union, and Ali Abdillahi, Secretary-General, who are currently visiting our country.

Present at the banquet were Liu Ningyi and Bao Erhan, Vice-Chairmen of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, as well as Qu Tangliang, Zhu Ziqi, Wang Mingyuan, Zheng Senyu, Wu Xiaoda, and other figures from various circles.

### 廖承志设宴招待哈比和阿卜迪拉西

1960-05-06
第6版()
专栏：

    廖承志设宴招待哈比和阿卜迪拉西
    新华社5日讯　中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志今天设宴欢迎正在我国访问的法属索马里兰前总理、民主联盟主席穆罕默德·哈比及总书记阿里·阿卜迪拉西。
    出席宴会作陪的有中国亚非团结委员会副主席刘宁一、包尔汉，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，以及区棠亮、朱子奇、王明远、郑森禹、吴晓达等各界人士。
