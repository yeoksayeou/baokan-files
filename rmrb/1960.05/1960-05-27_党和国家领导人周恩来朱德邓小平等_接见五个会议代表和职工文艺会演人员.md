Title: Party and State Leaders Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Deng Xiaoping, and Others Receive Representatives of Five Conferences and Performers of the Workers' Literary and Art Performance

Author:
Date: 1960-05-27
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, May 26th. Party and state leaders Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Deng Xiaoping, Peng Zhen, He Long, Li Xiannian, Lu Dingyi, Wang Jiaxiang, and Luo Ruiqing, this afternoon received representatives attending the All-Army Cultural and Educational Activists Representative Conference of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Political Work Conference of Artillery Academies, and the Activists Representative Conference of the Air Force Directly Subordinate Organs; all personnel participating in the National Workers' Literary and Art Performance; representatives attending the First National Congress of the Blind and Deaf-Mute; and representatives attending the Second National Congress of Advanced Collectives and Advanced Workers of the National Customs.

### 党和国家领导人周恩来朱德邓小平等  接见五个会议代表和职工文艺会演人员

1960-05-27
第2版()
专栏：

    党和国家领导人周恩来朱德邓小平等
    接见五个会议代表和职工文艺会演人员
    新华社26日讯　党和国家的领导人周恩来、朱德、邓小平、彭真、贺龙、李先念、陆定一、王稼祥、罗瑞卿，今天下午接见了出席中国人民解放军全军文化教育积极分子代表大会、炮兵院校政治工作会议、空军直属机关积极分子代表大会的代表；参加全国职工文艺会演的全体人员；出席第一届全国盲人聋哑人代表会议以及全国海关第二次先进集体和先进工作者代表会议的代表。
