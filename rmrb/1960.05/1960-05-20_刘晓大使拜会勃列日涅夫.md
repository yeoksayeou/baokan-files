Title: Ambassador Liu Xiao Calls on Brezhnev
Author: 
Date: 1960-05-20
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 19th: TASS, Moscow, May 19th: The Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, Liu Xiao, today paid a visit to the Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Brezhnev. The meeting took place in the Kremlin.

### 刘晓大使拜会勃列日涅夫

1960-05-20
第5版()
专栏：

    刘晓大使拜会勃列日涅夫
    新华社19日讯　塔斯社莫斯科19日讯：中华人民共和国驻苏联大使刘晓今天拜会了苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席勃列日涅夫。会见在克里姆林宫举行。
