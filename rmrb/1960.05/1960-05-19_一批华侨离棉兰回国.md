Title: A Batch of Overseas Chinese Depart from Medan to Return to China
Author:
Date: 1960-05-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, May 17th: A delayed message received here from Medan says that the "Hai Huang" ship, sent by the Chinese government to pick up overseas Chinese returning to China, left Belawan port in Medan on the afternoon of the 14th, carrying a group of impoverished and displaced overseas Chinese back to the country.

More than four thousand overseas Chinese went to the dock to see them off, and the atmosphere was very warm. Among those who went to the dock to see them off were Saleh Umar, Chairman of the Indonesia-China Friendship Association of Medan, Rumiyati, Chairman of the Indonesian Women's Association of Medan, and other Indonesian friends.

### 一批华侨离棉兰回国

1960-05-19
第4版()
专栏：

    一批华侨离棉兰回国
    新华社雅加达17日电　这里从棉兰收到的迟到消息说，中国政府派去接运华侨回国的“海皇号”轮船，在14日下午载着一批贫苦和流离失所的华侨，离开棉兰的勿拉湾港回国。
    有四千多名华侨到码头为这批华侨送行，气氛非常热烈。到码头送行的还有棉兰的印度尼西亚—中国友好协会主席萨勒·乌马尔、棉兰印度尼西亚妇女协会主席鲁米娅蒂和其它的印度尼西亚朋友们。
