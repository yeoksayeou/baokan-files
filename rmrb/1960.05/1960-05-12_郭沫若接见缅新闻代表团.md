Title: Guo Moruo Receives Burmese News Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-05-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 11th May. Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received the entire Burmese journalist delegation headed by U Aung Than this morning. Present at the reception were Lian Guan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Zhu Muzhi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, Li Bingquan, Secretary of the Secretariat, and Shao Zonghan, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 郭沫若接见缅新闻代表团

1960-05-12
第3版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见缅新闻代表团
    新华社11日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若今天上午接见了以吴昂丹为首的缅甸新闻工作者代表团全体团员。接见时在座的，有人大常务委员会副秘书长连贯，中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席朱穆之，书记处书记李炳泉和外交部新闻司副司长邵宗汉。
