Title: Czechoslovak Friendship Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-05-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, May 23rd: The Czechoslovak Friendship Delegation, consisting of six members and led by Josef Obavesky, which came to China at the invitation of the Sino-Czechoslovak Friendship Association to participate in "China-Czechoslovakia Friendship Week" activities, left Beijing by plane today to return home.

### 捷克斯洛伐克友好代表团回国

1960-05-24
第5版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克友好代表团回国
    据新华社23日讯　应中国捷克斯洛伐克友好协会邀请来我国参加“中捷友好周”活动的捷克斯洛伐克友好代表团一行六人，在团长约瑟夫·奥巴夫斯基率领下，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
