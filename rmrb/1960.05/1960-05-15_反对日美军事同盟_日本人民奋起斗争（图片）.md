Title: The Japanese People Rise Up in Struggle Against the U.S.-Japan Military Alliance (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1960-05-15
Page: 3
    Against the U.S.-Japan Military Alliance
    The Japanese People Rise Up in Struggle (Pictures)
    Farmers drive their tillers to participate in the demonstration. The slogan boards made of straw mats read: "Oppose the 'Security Treaty'!"
    Workers come to demonstrate in front of the Diet, demanding "Dissolve the Diet! Shinsuke Kishi resign!"
    Women actively participate in the action. They hold up slogan boards that say: "Oppose the 'Security Treaty'!" and demonstrate in front of the Diet.
    Christians also join the ranks of the anti-American patriotic struggle. The slogan board reads: "We oppose the new U.S.-Japan 'Security Treaty'."
    Children also follow their heroic mothers to participate in this great national struggle. Their headscarves read: "Unity!"
    Buddhists also take to the streets to express their desire for peace and opposition to wars of aggression.

### 反对日美军事同盟  日本人民奋起斗争（图片）

1960-05-15
第3版()
专栏：

    反对日美军事同盟
    日本人民奋起斗争（图片）
    农民们开着耕种机参加示威。用草席做成的标语牌上写着：“反对‘安全条约’”。
    工人们来到国会前示威，他们要求“解散国会！岸信介辞职！”。
    妇女们积极参加行动。她们拿着：“反对‘安全条约’！”的标语牌在国会门前示威。
    基督教徒也投入反美爱国斗争的行列。标语牌上写着：“我们反对新日美‘安全条约’”。
    孩子们也跟着英勇的妈妈参加这个全民性的伟大斗争。他们的头巾上写着：“团结！”
    佛教徒也走上街头，表达自己的要求和平反对侵略战争的愿望。
