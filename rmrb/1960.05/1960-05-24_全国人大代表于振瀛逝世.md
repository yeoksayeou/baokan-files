Title: NPC Delegate Yu Zhenying Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1960-05-24
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd. Mr. Yu Zhenying, a delegate to the National People's Congress and a Standing Committee member of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, passed away due to illness at 6:34 PM on May 22nd, 1960 in Beijing.

Yu Zhenying was a native of Pingli County, Shaanxi Province, and was 60 years old. His remains were cremated on the afternoon of the 23rd.

The Funeral Committee for Mr. Yu Zhenying was established on the 22nd. A public memorial ceremony will be held at Jialing Temple at 10:00 AM on May 25th, after which the remains will be moved to the Babaoshan Revolutionary Cemetery.

The list of members of the Funeral Committee for Mr. Yu Zhenying is as follows: He Xiangning, Wang Kunlun, Deng Baoshan, Liu Zhongrong, Zhu Yunshan, Li Weihan, Li Chengzhi, Chen Cisheng, Chen Shaoxian, Wu Maosun, Yu Xinqing, Wu Hexuan, Wu Xinyu, Zhang Su, Zhang Zhizhong, Qu Wu, Shao Lizi, Zhou Xinmin, Hou Wailu, Gao Chongmin, Xu Bing, Liang Airan, Guo Jinjie, Cao Mengjun, Mei Gongbin, Peng Youjin, Cheng Qian, Xiong Kewu, Cai Tingkai.

### 全国人大代表于振瀛逝世

1960-05-24
第7版()
专栏：

    全国人大代表于振瀛逝世
    新华社23日讯　全国人民代表大会代表、中国国民党革命委员会中央常务委员于振瀛先生，因病于1960年5月22日下午六时三十四分在北京逝世。
    于振瀛是陕西省平利县人，享年六十岁。遗体已于23日下午举行火葬。
    于振瀛先生治丧委员会已于22日成立。定于5月25日上午十时在嘉兴寺举行公祭，公祭后移灵至八宝山革命公墓。
    于振瀛先生治丧委员会名单如下：何香凝、王昆仑、邓宝珊、刘仲容、朱蕴山、李维汉、李澄之、陈此生、陈劭先、吴茂荪、余心清、武和轩、武新宇、张苏、张治中、屈武、邵力子、周新民、侯外庐、高崇民、徐冰、梁蔼然、郭荩阶、曹孟君、梅龚彬、彭友今、程潜、熊克武、蔡廷锴。
