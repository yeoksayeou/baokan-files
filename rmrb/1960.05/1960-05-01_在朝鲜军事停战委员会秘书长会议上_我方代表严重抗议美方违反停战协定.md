Title: At the Secretary Meeting of the Korean Military Armistice Commission, Our Representative Strongly Protested Against the US Violation of the Armistice Agreement
Author:
Date: 1960-05-01
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 30th - According to the Korean Central News Agency from Kaesong, Colonel Lin Qijie, Secretary of the Korean-Chinese side of the Korean Military Armistice Commission, strongly protested at today's secretary meeting against the recent actions of the US side that violated the armistice agreement.
    The Secretary of the Korean-Chinese side pointed out that on April 23, a US L-19 military aircraft intruded into the airspace 3 kilometers west of Longshan-ri in the Korean-Chinese side's area for reconnaissance. On April 27, two US F-86 military jet aircraft intruded into the airspace 7 kilometers south of Baichuan in the Korean-Chinese side's area, circled, and then flew towards Daqing Island. The continuous intrusion of US military aircraft into the airspace over the Korean-Chinese side's area for reconnaissance activities is a hostile act against the Korean-Chinese side in violation of the armistice agreement.
    The Secretary of the Korean-Chinese side pointed out that, not only that, but on April 23, three US civil police officers fired dozens of carbine bullets in the direction of the Korean-Chinese side's area, one of whom intruded 120 meters into the Korean-Chinese side's part of the demilitarized zone before returning to the US side's area. On the same day, the US civil police engaged in several similar hostile acts.
    The Secretary of the Korean-Chinese side demanded that the US side conduct a responsible investigation into the above-mentioned violations of the armistice agreement, punish the personnel involved in the above-mentioned incidents, and take measures to ensure that similar incidents do not occur again.

### 在朝鲜军事停战委员会秘书长会议上  我方代表严重抗议美方违反停战协定

1960-05-01
第6版()
专栏：

    在朝鲜军事停战委员会秘书长会议上
    我方代表严重抗议美方违反停战协定
    指责美方飞机接连侵入我方上空进行侦察活动
    新华社平壤30日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社开城消息，朝鲜军事停战委员会朝中方面秘书长林箕济上校今天在双方秘书长会议上严重抗议美方最近违反停战协定的行为。
    朝中方面秘书长指出，4月23日，美方L—19型军用飞机一架侵入朝中方面地区龙山里以西三公里的上空进行侦察。4月27日，美方F—86军用喷气式飞机两架侵入朝中方面地区白川以南七公里上空盘旋后飞向大青岛方向。美方军用飞机接连侵入朝中方面地区上空进行侦察活动，是违反停战协定的对朝中方面的敌对行为。
    朝中方面秘书长指出，不仅如此，4月23日，美方三名民政警察向朝中方面地区方向发射了数十发卡宾枪子弹，其中一名侵入了非军事区朝中方面部分一百二十米，以后回到美方地区。同一天，美方的民政警察进行了数次类似的敌对行为。
    朝中方面秘书长要求美方对上述违反停战协定的事件进行负责的调查，处罚同上述事件有关的人员，并且采取措施保证不再发生类似事件。
