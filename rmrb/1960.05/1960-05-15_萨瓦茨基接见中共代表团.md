Title: Zawadzki Receives Chinese Communist Party Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-05-15
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, May 12th. Polish People's Republic State Council Chairman Zawadzki today received the Chinese Communist Party delegation headed by Bo Yibo.
    Present at the meeting were Polish Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Jaroszewicz, and Polish United Workers' Party Central Liaison Minister Czesak. Chinese Ambassador to Poland Wang Bingnan was also present.

### 萨瓦茨基接见中共代表团

1960-05-15
第5版()
专栏：

    萨瓦茨基接见中共代表团
    新华社华沙12日电　波兰人民共和国国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基今天接见了以薄一波为首的中国共产党代表团。
    接见时在座的有波兰部长会议副主席雅罗谢维奇、波兰统一工人党中央联络部长切萨克。中国驻波兰大使王炳南也在座。
