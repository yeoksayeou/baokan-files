Title: Swedish Military Delegation Visits Hangzhou
Author: 
Date: 1960-05-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency: The military delegation of the Kingdom of Sweden, headed by Major General Ollman, visited People's Liberation Army troops, schools, local factories, and people's communes in Wuhan and Shanghai. On the afternoon of the 15th, they took a special plane to Hangzhou for a visit.

### 瑞典军事代表团去杭州访问

1960-05-18
第5版()
专栏：

    瑞典军事代表团去杭州访问
    据新华社讯　以奥尔曼少将为首的瑞典王国军事代表团，在武汉、上海参观访问了人民解放军部队、学校、地方工厂和人民公社后，15日下午乘专机去杭州访问。
