Title: Soviet Academy of Medical Sciences Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-27
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, May 26th. A delegation from the Soviet Academy of Medical Sciences, headed by Academician V.D. Timakov, First Vice-President of the Soviet Academy of Medical Sciences, arrived in Beijing by plane today. They are visiting China at the invitation of the Ministry of Health.

Huang Chia-ssu, President of the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences, and Shen Chi-chen, Vice-President, welcomed them at the airport.

### 苏医学科学院代表团到京

1960-05-27
第2版()
专栏：

    苏医学科学院代表团到京
    新华社26日讯　以苏联医学科学院第一副院长弗·得·季马科夫院士为首的苏联医学科学院代表团一行三人，今天乘飞机到达北京。他们是应卫生部的邀请来我国访问的。
    中国医学科学院院长黄家驷、副院长沈其震等人到机场欢迎。
