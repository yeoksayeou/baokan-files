Title: He Long Returns the Visit to Krim Belkacem
Author:
Date: 1960-05-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 14th: Vice Premier He Long returned the visit this afternoon to Krim Belkacem, head of the delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs. Algerian guests present at the return visit were: Boussouf Abdelhafid and Francis Ahmed.

Also present at the return visit were Ji Pengfei, Li Qiang, Zhang Aiping, He Ying, and Yu Peiwen.

### 贺龙回访克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆

1960-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    贺龙回访克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆
    新华社14日讯　贺龙副总理今天下午回访了阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团团长、副总理兼外交部长克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆。回访时在座的阿尔及利亚贵宾有：布苏夫·阿卜杜勒哈菲德和弗朗西斯·艾哈迈德。
    回访时在座的有姬鹏飞、李强、张爱萍、何英、俞沛文。
