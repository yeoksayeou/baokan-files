Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Other Units Hold Concert to Commemorate the 120th Anniversary of Tchaikovsky's Birth
Author:
Date: 1960-05-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Chinese Musicians Association, and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association jointly held a concert tonight to commemorate the 120th anniversary of the birth of the great Russian composer Pyotr Ilyich Tchaikovsky.

　　After Ma Sicong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Musicians Association and composer, delivered a speech at the meeting, the Central Broadcasting Orchestra, the Chinese People's Liberation Army Military Band Orchestra, and the Lanzhou Film Studio Orchestra performed the composer's "Fifth Symphony" and "Italian Capriccio" and other works. The conductor was Soviet expert V. Yesipov.

　　Among those attending the concert were Lü Ji, Chairman of the Chinese Musicians Association, Secretariat Secretaries Wang Yuanfang, Yang Yinliu, Jiang Dingxian, Yu Yixuan, Go Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Gao Zhendong, Secretary-General of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, musicians Lao Zhicheng, Li Delun, Jiang Yan, Liu Shikun, and more than 600 music lovers.

　　Kabralov and Sang Feilou, First Secretaries of the Soviet Embassy in China, Akosyuk, Secretary of the All-Union Composers Association who is visiting our country, and Soviet self-funded travelers were also invited to attend the concert.

### 中苏友协等单位举行音乐会  纪念柴可夫斯基诞生120周年

1960-05-09
第6版()
专栏：

    中苏友协等单位举行音乐会
    纪念柴可夫斯基诞生120周年
    新华社7日讯　中苏友好协会总会、中国音乐家协会和北京市中苏友好协会，今晚联合举行了纪念伟大俄罗斯作曲家彼·伊·柴可夫斯基诞生一百二十周年音乐会。
    中国音乐家协会副主席、作曲家马思聪在会上致词以后，由中央广播乐团管弦乐队、中国人民解放军军乐团管弦乐队和兰州电影制片厂乐团演出了作曲家的“第五交响乐”、“意大利随想曲”等作品。演出指挥是苏联专家弗·耶西鲍夫。
    出席音乐会的，有中国音乐家协会主席吕骥，书记处书记王元方、杨荫浏、江定仙、喻宜萱，中苏友好协会总会副秘书长戈宝权，北京市中苏友好协会秘书长高振东，音乐界人士老志诚、李德伦、蒋炎、刘诗昆等，以及音乐爱好者共六百多人。
    苏联驻中国大使馆一等秘书卡布拉洛夫、桑飞楼，正在我国访问的全苏作曲家协会书记阿科休克和苏联自费旅行者，也应邀出席了音乐会。
