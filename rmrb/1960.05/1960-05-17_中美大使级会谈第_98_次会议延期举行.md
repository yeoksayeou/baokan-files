Title: 98th Meeting of Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Postponed
Author:
Date: 1960-05-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, May 16th: The 98th meeting of the Sino-U.S. ambassadorial talks, originally scheduled for May 17th, has been postponed to June 7th (Tuesday) by mutual agreement due to administrative reasons.

### 中美大使级会谈第98次会议延期举行

1960-05-17
第5版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈第98次会议延期举行
    新华社华沙16日电　原定于5月17日举行的中美大使级会谈第九十八次会议，因行政性原因经双方协议，延期至6月7日（星期二）举行。
