Title: May the Friendship Between the Chinese and Czechoslovak Peoples Last Forever! Beijing Xinhua Printing Plant Solemnly Celebrates the 15th Anniversary of the Liberation of Czechoslovakia. Czechoslovak Film Week Opens in Eight Major Cities Including Beijing and Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1960-05-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, May 5th: More than 1,000 employees of the Beijing Xinhua Printing Plant held a grand meeting tonight to celebrate the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia. The employees beat gongs and drums, and set off firecrackers to welcome Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Bušiak, and the Czechoslovak friendship delegation headed by Mr. Obaufsky, who came to attend the celebration. Also attending today's meeting were Mr. Štekyrna, Counselor of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, and Mr. Situ Huimin, Secretary-General of the Sino-Czechoslovak Friendship Association.

    Zhou Yongsheng, Director of the Beijing Xinhua Printing Plant, spoke first at the meeting. He said, "Today, all the employees of our Beijing Xinhua Printing Plant are celebrating this great historical festival together with the distinguished guests from Czechoslovakia with unparalleled excitement, and congratulate Czechoslovakia on the great achievements it has made in all aspects of socialist construction over the past fifteen years." At the meeting, Zhou Yongsheng also extended festive congratulations to the Czechoslovak people through the distinguished guests, and wished the deep friendship and close cooperation between the Chinese and Czechoslovak peoples would develop day by day.

    Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Bušiak, was invited to speak at the meeting. After introducing the construction achievements of Czechoslovakia in the fifteen years since its liberation, he said, "The Czechoslovak people are watching with admiration the tremendous economic, cultural, and political development of China since its liberation, especially during the Great Leap Forward. We believe that your achievements are also our achievements, and we are happy for your achievements." His speech was warmly welcomed by all the employees present.

    Representatives of the employees of the Beijing Xinhua Printing Plant read out a congratulatory message celebrating the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia at the meeting. Zhou Yongsheng presented the distinguished guests with picture albums and other books and periodicals printed by the factory. Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Bušiak, presented a delicate glass vase to the factory employees.

    After the meeting, the Beijing Xinhua Printing Plant's amateur cultural troupe performed literary and artistic programs.

Xinhua News Agency, May 6th: To celebrate the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia, the Ministry of Culture, the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and the Sino-Czechoslovak Friendship Association will jointly host "Czechoslovak Film Week" from May 7th. The film week will be held in eight cities including Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Kunming, Fushun, Harbin, Lanzhou, and Xi'an, showing the Czechoslovak feature films "Black Camp," "The Fabulous World of Jules Verne," "The Good Soldier Švejk" (Part II), and the documentary "A Day in Czechoslovakia."

    Today, the opening ceremony of "Czechoslovak Film Week" was held in the capital. More than 1,000 people attended the opening ceremony, including Xia Yan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture, Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Cai Chusheng, Chairman of the China Film Workers Association, Situ Huimin, Secretary-General of the Sino-Czechoslovak Friendship Association, Li Mu, Deputy Director of the Beijing Film Bureau, and people from the capital's film and literary circles.

    Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Bušiak, and Mr. J. Obaufsky, head of the Czechoslovak friendship delegation that came to participate in the Sino-Czechoslovak Friendship Week activities, attended the opening ceremony. Diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries to China also attended the opening ceremony.

    Xia Yan, President of the Sino-Czechoslovak Friendship Association and Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture, delivered a speech at the opening ceremony, warmly congratulating Czechoslovakia on the brilliant achievements it has made in the great cause of building socialism over the past fifteen years. He said that the glorious banner of Marxism-Leninism illuminates the path for us to move forward together. With the increasing development of socialist construction and the increasing enhancement of friendly cooperation between China and Czechoslovakia, the cultural exchanges between our two countries have also been further developed. Excellent Czechoslovak films are welcomed by the Chinese people. Our people are greatly encouraged by seeing the heroic image of the Czechoslovak people on the screen. It is believed that through the screening of this film week, the mutual understanding and friendly cooperation between the Chinese and Czechoslovak peoples will be further promoted.

    Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Bušiak, said in his speech at the opening ceremony that the Czechoslovak film art, which is in the hands of the working people and is developing vigorously, is increasingly becoming an effective tool for raising the ideological awareness of the masses and transforming the old capitalist ideas. It is a powerful weapon today in the struggle against imperialism and in defense of the freedom of the working people and world peace. Ambassador Bušiak said that the ever-developing cultural cooperation between China and Czechoslovakia is an important factor in the common struggle of the two countries and the socialist camp in building socialism and communism. It is believed that this film week will make a valuable contribution to increasing the friendship between China and Czechoslovakia.

### 中捷两国人民友谊万古长青  北京新华印刷厂隆重庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放十五周年  北京上海等八大城市举办的捷克斯洛伐克电影周开幕

1960-05-07
第6版()
专栏：

    中捷两国人民友谊万古长青
    北京新华印刷厂隆重庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放十五周年
    北京上海等八大城市举办的捷克斯洛伐克电影周开幕
    新华社5日讯　北京新华印刷厂一千多名职工今晚举行大会，隆重庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放十五周年。职工们敲锣打鼓、鸣放鞭炮，夹道欢迎前来参加庆祝会的捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使布希尼亚克，和以奥巴夫斯基团长为首的捷克斯洛伐克友好代表团。参加今天大会的还有捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆参赞石杰基那和中捷友好协会秘书长司徒慧敏。
    北京新华印刷厂厂长周永生首先在大会上讲话，他说：“我们北京新华印刷厂全体职工今天以无比兴奋的心情同捷克斯洛伐克贵宾们共同庆祝这一具有伟大历史意义的节日，祝贺捷克斯洛伐克十五年来在社会主义建设各方面所取得的巨大成就。”周永生在会上还向贵宾并通过贵宾们向捷克斯洛伐克人民致以节日的祝贺，并祝贺中捷两国人民之间深厚友谊和亲密合作的日益发展。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使布希尼亚克应邀在会上讲话，他介绍了捷克斯洛伐克解放十五年来的建设成就以后说：“捷克斯洛伐克人民怀着赞美的心情注视着中国解放以来，特别是在大跃进期间经济、文化和政治的巨大发展，我们认为，你们的成就也就是我们的成就，为你们的成就感到高兴。”他的讲话受到全场职工的热烈欢迎。
    北京新华印刷厂职工代表在会上宣读了庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放十五周年的贺词。周永生用本厂印刷的画报等书刊赠送给贵宾。捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使布希尼亚克把一支精致的玻璃花瓶赠送给工厂职工。
    会后，北京新华印刷厂职工业余文工团演出了文艺节目。
    新华社6日讯　为庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放十五周年，文化部、对外文化联络委员会和中捷友好协会从5月7日起联合举办“捷克斯洛伐克电影周。”电影周将在北京、上海、广州、昆明、抚顺、哈尔滨、兰州、西安等八个城市举行，上映捷克斯洛伐克故事片“黑营”、“毁灭的发明”、“好兵帅克”（下集）和纪录片“在捷克斯洛伐克的一天”。
    今天，首都举行了“捷克斯洛伐克电影周”的开幕式。参加开幕式的有文化部副部长夏衍、外交部副部长罗贵波、对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥、中国电影工作者联谊会主席蔡楚生、中捷友好协会秘书长司徒慧敏、北京市电影局副局长李牧及首都电影、文艺界人士等千余人。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使布希尼亚克和约·奥巴夫斯基团长率领前来参加中捷友好周活动的捷克斯洛伐克友好代表团，出席了开幕式。出席开幕式的还有各国驻华使节和外交官员。
    中捷友好协会会长、文化部副部长夏衍在开幕式上致词，他热烈祝贺捷克斯洛伐克十五年来在建设社会主义伟大事业上取得的辉煌成就。他说，马克思列宁主义的光辉旗帜照耀着我们共同前进的道路。随着中捷两国社会主义建设的日益发展和友好合作的日益增强，我们两国的文化交流也获得了进一步的发展。捷克斯洛伐克的优秀影片受到中国人民的欢迎。我国人民在银幕上看到捷克斯洛伐克人民的英雄形象，得到很大的鼓舞。相信通过这次电影周的映出，将会更加促进中捷两国人民之间的相互了解和友好合作。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使布希尼亚克在开幕式上讲话说：掌握在劳动人民手中的，正在蓬勃发展的捷克斯洛伐克电影艺术，日益成为提高群众思想觉悟、改造旧的资本主义思想的有效工具。它是今天向帝国主义做斗争，是保卫劳动人民自由和世界和平的有力武器。布希尼亚克大使说，捷中两国之间日益发展的文化合作，是两国，也是社会主义阵营在建设社会主义、共产主义共同斗争中的重要因素。相信这次电影周将为增加捷中两国之间的友谊做出宝贵的贡献。
