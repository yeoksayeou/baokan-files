Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends a Message of Support for the Anti-American Struggle of Peruvian Workers
Author: 
Date: 1960-05-17
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 16th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a message to the Peruvian Workers' Confederation, supporting the just struggle of Peruvian workers.

The telegram said: Chinese workers express their utmost indignation at the crimes of American monopoly capitalists in massacring Peruvian farmers and extend their deep condolences to the victimized farmers. Chinese workers fully sympathize with and support the struggle of Peruvian workers against the exploitation and plunder of American imperialism, for democratic rights, and to defend national sovereignty, and deeply believe that your just struggle will surely achieve final victory.

### 我全国总工会致电  声援秘鲁工人的反美斗争

1960-05-17
第6版()
专栏：

    我全国总工会致电
    声援秘鲁工人的反美斗争
    新华社16日讯　中华全国总工会致电秘鲁工人联合会，支援秘鲁工人的正义斗争。
    电报说：中国工人对美国垄断资本家屠杀秘鲁农民的罪行表示无比愤慨，并对受害农民致以沉痛的哀悼。中国工人完全同情和支持秘鲁工人反对美帝国主义剥削和掠夺，争取民主权利和保卫国家主权的斗争，并深信你们的正义斗争一定取得最后胜利。
