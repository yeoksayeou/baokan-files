Title: Premier Zhou Receives Representatives of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1960-05-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 13th: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi received the representatives of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association headed by the Vice President of the Association, Tran Huy Lieu, at 4:00 PM today (local time) and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam He Wei, and some other accompanying comrades.

### 周总理接见越中友协代表

1960-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见越中友协代表
    新华社河内13日电　周恩来总理和陈毅副总理今天下午四时（当地时间）接见了以越中友好协会副会长陈辉燎为首的越中友协代表，同他们进行了亲切、友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有外交部副部长章汉夫、中国驻越南大使何伟和部分其他随行同志。
