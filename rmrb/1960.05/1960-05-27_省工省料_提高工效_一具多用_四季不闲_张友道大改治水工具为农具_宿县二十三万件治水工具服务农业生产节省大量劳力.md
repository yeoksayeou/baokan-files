Title: Saving Labor and Materials, Improving Efficiency, Multi-Purpose, Useful in All Seasons: Zhang Youdao Greatly Modifies Water Control Tools into Agricultural Implements; Su County Uses 230,000 Water Control Tools to Serve Agricultural Production, Saving a Large Amount of Labor

Author: 
Date: 1960-05-27
Page: 6

　　本报讯　安徽省著名的治水标兵张友道领导的青年突击队，把治水工具分别运用在农业生产上，掀起了一个农业生产的高工效运动。中共宿县县委全面推广了这个队的经验，截至4月底，全县二十三万件治水工具已基本用于农业生产。仅4月份的统计，就节省了一百四十多万个劳动日。
　　治水工具有的可以直接用于农业生产，有的只要“位置移动，部件分家”，经过小改巧改，就能用于农业生产。如在田间运输方面，治水的各种运土车都能用，运土的铁木轨道也能在田间铺设，使用滑车运输。无轨滑车稍加改装，可用于撒肥；牵引滑车配上一个移行器，就改成了牵引犁。如运肥滑车两人操作，一天能运肥四万斤，比人工挑肥工效提高十四倍，比牛车运肥工效高五倍。张友道突击队运用到农业生产上的二百五十件大小治水工具，在春季生产中可节省五千一百个劳动日。
　　治水工具转用于农业生产，花工、花材料都不多，同时发挥了一具多用的效益。如张友道突击队改制的九种十五件大型治水工具，只用了两天时间，十八个工。据木工反映：制造一部撒肥车，至少要四个工，现在用无轨滑车改为撒肥车，只用了半个工，木料用得也很少。
　　治水工具改用于农业后，将来不仅仍能用来治水，而且还越改越精巧。例如龚培兰把动力滑车改成动力牵引犁以后，在操纵台上安装了移行器，就便于移动，若用于治水，即可扩大卸土范围，由卸土一条线变成一大片。
　　为了把治水工具迅速的用于农业生产，中共宿县县委数次召开公社党委书记会议，作了专门研究部署，组织社队干部到张友道突击队参观学习，并从各工厂派出一百多名技术人员，分赴各社传授技术经验。符力公社在参观张友道突击队后，一天内就把五百九十部提水辘轳，改为人畜两用的水车，工效比一般龙骨水车提高四倍，并将一千五百多米的水利工地运土轨道搬家，铺设在田间，便利运肥。全县共增加了七万二千多辆各种运肥车和二十多公里的运肥轨道，大大加快了运肥速度。

　　This newspaper reports that the Youth Shock Brigade, led by Zhang Youdao, a famous water control model worker in Anhui Province, has applied water control tools to agricultural production, launching a high-efficiency movement in agricultural production. The Su County Committee of the Chinese Communist Party has comprehensively promoted the team's experience. As of the end of April, 230,000 water control tools in the county have been basically used in agricultural production. Statistics for April alone show that more than 1.4 million labor days were saved.
　　Some water control tools can be directly used for agricultural production, while others can be used for agricultural production with slight and clever modifications by "moving positions and separating parts." For example, in terms of field transportation, various soil transport vehicles for water control can be used, and iron and wood tracks for soil transport can also be laid in the field for trolley transport. Trackless trolleys can be used for fertilizing with slight modifications; traction trolleys equipped with a shifting device can be converted into traction plows. For example, a fertilizer transport trolley operated by two people can transport 40,000 catties of fertilizer a day, which is 14 times more efficient than manual fertilizer carrying and 5 times more efficient than oxcart fertilizer transport. The 250 large and small water control tools used by the Zhang Youdao Shock Brigade in agricultural production can save 5,100 labor days in spring production.
　　Converting water control tools for agricultural production does not require much labor or materials, and at the same time, it gives full play to the benefits of multi-purpose tools. For example, the nine types of fifteen large water control tools modified by the Zhang Youdao Shock Brigade only took two days and eighteen workdays. According to carpenters, it takes at least four workdays to manufacture a fertilizer spreader, but now it only takes half a workday to convert a trackless trolley into a fertilizer spreader, and very little wood is used.
　　After water control tools are modified for agricultural use, they can not only still be used for water control in the future, but also become more and more sophisticated. For example, after Gong Peilan converted a power trolley into a power traction plow, she installed a shifting device on the operating table to facilitate movement. If used for water control, it can expand the soil unloading range, changing from a line of soil unloading to a large area.
　　In order to quickly apply water control tools to agricultural production, the Su County Committee of the Chinese Communist Party held several meetings of commune party committee secretaries, made special studies and deployments, organized cadres from communes and teams to visit and study the Zhang Youdao Shock Brigade, and dispatched more than 100 technicians from various factories to go to various communes to impart technical experience. After visiting the Zhang Youdao Shock Brigade, Fuli Commune converted 590 water lifting windlasses into waterwheels for both human and animal use in one day, which is four times more efficient than ordinary dragon bone waterwheels, and moved more than 1,500 meters of soil transport tracks from water conservancy sites and laid them in the fields to facilitate fertilizer transport. The county added more than 72,000 various fertilizer transport vehicles and more than 20 kilometers of fertilizer transport tracks, greatly speeding up fertilizer transport.
