Title: Polish "Guards" Basketball Team Holds Friendly Match in Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-05-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, May 5th: The Polish "Guards" men's basketball team held a friendly match with the Zhejiang men's basketball team in Hangzhou on the evening of the 4th. The Zhejiang team won with a score of 62 to 59.

In this game, both sides played excellently. The score was tied eleven times. The Polish "Guards" team only lost to the Zhejiang team by three points at the end.

The Polish team arrived in Hangzhou on the 3rd. They visited scenic spots and historical sites around West Lake.

### 波“近卫军”篮球队在杭州举行友谊赛

1960-05-06
第4版()
专栏：

    波“近卫军”篮球队在杭州举行友谊赛
    新华社杭州5日电　波兰“近卫军”男子篮球队4日晚在杭州同浙江男子篮球队举行友谊比赛，浙江队以六十二比五十九获胜。
    在这场球赛中，双方都打得很出色，曾经十一次打成平局，直到最后波兰“近卫军”队才以三分之差败给了浙江队。
    波兰队是在3日到杭州的，他们曾经游览了西湖名胜古迹。
