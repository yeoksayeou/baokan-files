Title: National Workers' Table Tennis Team Championships Conclude
Author:
Date: 1960-05-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 16th: The 1960 National Workers' Table Tennis Team Championships for men and women concluded on the 15th. The Shanghai men's and women's teams both won the team championships with a record of five wins and no losses. The Guangdong men's and women's teams both won second place. The men's teams ranking third to sixth were Shandong, Liaoning, Fujian, and Beijing; the women's teams ranking third to sixth were Liaoning, Jilin, Sichuan, and Heilongjiang.

### 全国职工乒乓球男女团体赛结束

1960-05-17
第4版()
专栏：

    全国职工乒乓球男女团体赛结束
    据新华社上海16日电　1960年全国职工乒乓球比赛男女团体赛已在15日结束。上海男女队以五战五胜的成绩，双双获得团体赛冠军。广东男女队双获亚军。男子第三到第六的名次是山东、辽宁、福建和北京队；女子第三到第六的名次是辽宁、吉林、四川和黑龙江队。
