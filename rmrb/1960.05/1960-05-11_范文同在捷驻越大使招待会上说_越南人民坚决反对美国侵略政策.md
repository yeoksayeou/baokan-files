Title: Pham Van Dong Says at Czechoslovak Ambassador's Reception that the Vietnamese People Firmly Oppose the U.S. Aggression Policy
Author:
Date: 1960-05-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 9th. Premier Pham Van Dong of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam announced tonight at a reception held by Czechoslovak Ambassador to Vietnam, Frýdek, to celebrate the 15th anniversary of Czechoslovakia's liberation, that the Vietnamese people, together with peace-loving people all over the world, strongly condemn the provocative act of U.S. imperialist reconnaissance planes intruding into Soviet airspace. The Vietnamese people resolutely oppose the U.S. imperialist aggression and provocation in various parts of the world, especially in the Far East and Southeast Asia.

Premier Pham Van Dong said that the Vietnamese people are determined to fight against the U.S. imperialist policy of aggression in South Vietnam. The Vietnamese people continue to denounce to world public opinion the conspiracy of U.S. imperialism and its lackeys to expand the U.S. military advisory group in South Vietnam in order to openly import U.S. troops into South Vietnam and intensify the danger of war.

Le Duan, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, Vice Premiers Phan Ke Toai and Pham Hung, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly Ton Duc Thang, heads of various government departments, and diplomatic envoys of various countries to Vietnam attended tonight's reception.

### 范文同在捷驻越大使招待会上说  越南人民坚决反对美国侵略政策

1960-05-11
第6版()
专栏：

    范文同在捷驻越大使招待会上说
    越南人民坚决反对美国侵略政策
    据新华社河内9日电　越南民主共和国总理范文同今天晚上在捷克斯洛伐克驻越南大使傅尔德为庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放十五周年而举行的招待会上宣布，越南人民同世界爱好和平人民一起，强烈谴责美帝国主义的侦察机侵入苏联领空的挑衅行为。越南人民坚决反对美帝国主义在世界各地，特别是在远东和东南亚的侵略和挑衅行为。
    范文同总理说，越南人民坚决进行斗争，反对美帝国主义在南越的侵略政策。越南人民继续向世界舆论控诉美帝国主义及其走狗扩大在南越的美国军事顾问团以便实现公开向南越输入美国军队，加强战争危险的阴谋。
    越南劳动党中央委员会政治局委员黎笋，副总理潘继遂和范雄，国会常务委员会委员长孙德胜，政府各部门负责人以及各国驻越南外交使节出席了今晚的招待会。
