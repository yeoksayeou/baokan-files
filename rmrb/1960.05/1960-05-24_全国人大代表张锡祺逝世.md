Title: NPC Deputy Zhang Xiqi Passes Away
Author: 
Date: 1960-05-24
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Hefei, 22nd. Zhang Xiqi, Deputy to the National People's Congress, member of the Anhui Provincial People's Committee, Chairman of the Anhui Provincial Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, Chairman of the Anhui Provincial Association for Science and Technology, and President of Anhui Medical College, passed away in Hefei at 9 p.m. on May 19th due to long-term heart disease, hypertension, and other illnesses, despite extensive medical treatment.

Zhang Xiqi was from Fujian Province and was 62 years old. The funeral committee was established on the 20th.

### 全国人大代表张锡祺逝世

1960-05-24
第7版()
专栏：

    全国人大代表张锡祺逝世
    新华社合肥22日电　全国人民代表大会代表、安徽省人民委员会委员、中国农工民主党安徽省委员会主任委员、安徽省科学技术协会主任委员、安徽医学院院长张锡祺，因长期患心脏病、高血压病等症，经多方医治无效，于5月19日晚九时在合肥逝世。
    张锡祺是福建省人，享年六十二岁。治丧委员会已经在20日成立。
