Title: Burhan Meets with Habi and Abdillahi
Author:
Date: 1960-05-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, May 5th. Burhan, Vice-President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, met with Mohamed Habi, former Prime Minister of French Somaliland and President of the Democratic Union, and Ali Abdillahi, Secretary-General, this morning for a cordial conversation.

　　Vice-President Burhan, on behalf of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, presented the guests with color documentary films "Celebrating Ten Years" and "Holding High the Banner of the Bandung Conference, Supporting the Struggle of the African People."

### 包尔汉会见哈比和阿卜迪拉西

1960-05-06
第6版()
专栏：

    包尔汉会见哈比和阿卜迪拉西
    新华社5日讯　中国非洲人民友好协会副会长包尔汉今天上午会见了法属索马里兰前总理、民主联盟主席穆罕默德·哈比和总书记阿里·阿卜迪拉西，进行了亲切的谈话。
    包尔汉副会长代表中国非洲人民友好协会向客人赠送了彩色纪录片“欢庆十年”和纪录片“高举万隆会议旗帜，支持非洲人民斗争”各一部。
