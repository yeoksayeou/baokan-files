Title: Resolutely Follow the Path of Peace, Independence, Democracy and Neutrality: All-China Youth Federation Sends Telegram to the Tenth Congress of the Japan Youth Organizations Council Supporting Patriotic Struggle
Author:
Date: 1960-05-13
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The All-China Youth Federation sent a telegram to the Tenth National Congress of the Japan Youth Organizations Council, which will be held on the 13th, supporting their struggle against the Japan-U.S. "Security Treaty."

In the telegram, the All-China Youth Federation expressed sympathy and support for the efforts made by the Japan Youth Organizations Council in recent years in safeguarding peace, banning atomic and hydrogen bombs, recovering Okinawa under U.S. military occupation, preventing the revision of the Japan-U.S. "Security Treaty," and restoring Sino-Japanese diplomatic relations.

The All-China Youth Federation's telegram said: "Recently, the struggle of the Japanese people and youth to prevent the ratification of the new Japan-U.S. 'Security Treaty' has entered a new high tide. This massive struggle once again powerfully demonstrates that the Japanese people and youth will never allow the United States to revive militarism, never allow the U.S.-Japan reactionaries to push them into a new war of aggression, and resolutely demand to follow the path of peace, independence, democracy, and neutrality. We wish your congress new achievements in its efforts to defend world peace and oppose the Japan-U.S. 'Security Treaty.'"

### 坚决走和平独立民主中立道路  全国青联电日本青年团协议会十次大会支持爱国斗争

1960-05-13
第2版()
专栏：

    坚决走和平独立民主中立道路 
    全国青联电日本青年团协议会十次大会支持爱国斗争
    新华社讯　中华全国青年联合会致电将于13日举行的日本青年团协议会第十次全国大会，支持他们反对日美“安全条约”进行的斗争。
    全国青联在电报中对日本青年团协议会几年来在维护和平、禁止原子弹氢弹、收复在美军占领下的冲绳、阻止修改日美“安全条约”及恢复中日邦交斗争中所作的努力表示同情和支持。
    全国青联的电报说：“最近，日本人民和青年阻止批准新日美‘安全条约’的斗争又进入了一个新的高潮。这个声势浩大的斗争，再一次有力地说明了日本人民和青年决不允许美国复活军国主义，决不允许美日反动派把他们推向一次新的侵略战争，坚决要求走和平、独立、民主、中立的道路。谨祝你们的大会在保卫世界和平、反对日美‘安全条约’的努力中取得新的成就。”
