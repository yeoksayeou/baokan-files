Title: Chairman Tsedenbal's Welcome Speech at Ulaanbaatar Airport
Author:
Date: 1960-05-28
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, May 27th: The full text of the welcome speech by Tsedenbal, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, at Ulaanbaatar Airport is as follows:

    Dear Comrade Zhou Enlai, esteemed guests from the People's Republic of China, dear friends:
    Today, we welcome with extreme excitement the distinguished guest from our great neighbor, the People's Republic of China—the Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, our dear friend, the respected Comrade Zhou Enlai.
    On behalf of the government of the Mongolian People's Republic and the Mongolian people, I extend my heartfelt respects to our dear guest, one of the outstanding leaders of the People's Republic of China, the respected Comrade Premier Zhou Enlai, and to the Chinese state activists participating in this friendly visit.
    The Mongolian and Chinese peoples are members of the united and powerful socialist camp led by the Soviet Union. The noble and consistent goal of building socialism and communism tightly binds our two peoples together.
    Under the banner of invincible Marxism-Leninism, our two peoples are achieving even greater new successes year by year in realizing this just cause.
    The Mongolian people are constantly watching their great friend—the Chinese people—under the wise leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party headed by Comrade Mao Zedong. We are delighted and feel proud of the great leap forward in the cause of building socialism and the outstanding contributions made to consolidating peace in Asia and the world.
    Today, we note with satisfaction that the unbreakable friendly relations between our two countries, formed in accordance with the great principles of proletarian internationalism, are being steadily consolidated and developed for the benefit of the two peoples.
    Respected Premier, it has been almost six years since your first visit to our country. Since then, tremendous changes have taken place both in our domestic situation and on the international stage. During this period, our strong socialist camp has been further consolidated and developed.
    Relying on the ever-increasing fraternal assistance of the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and other fraternal socialist countries, the Mongolian people, under the leadership of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, have achieved gratifying and wonderful successes in the political, economic, and cultural life of our country.
    We are very pleased to be able to introduce you to the life of our working people. We sincerely welcome you to make a wonderful visit to our country.
    We firmly believe that your visit will add a new and glorious page to the history of the fraternal friendship and cooperation between our two peoples, and will make an important contribution to the cause of consolidating the unity of the peoples in the socialist camp headed by the great Soviet Union.
    Long live the unbreakable friendship between Mongolian and Chinese peoples!
    Long live world peace!
    Dear guests, we welcome you!

### 泽登巴尔主席在乌兰巴托机场上的欢迎词

1960-05-28
第2版()
专栏：

    泽登巴尔主席在乌兰巴托机场上的欢迎词
    新华社乌兰巴托27日电　蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席泽登巴尔在乌兰巴托机场的欢迎词，全文如下：亲爱的周恩来同志，来自中华人民共和国的尊敬的贵宾
  们，亲爱的朋友们：
    今天，我们以极其兴奋的心情欢迎从我们伟大邻邦中华人民共和国来的贵宾——中华人民共和国国务院总理、我们的亲爱的朋友尊敬的周恩来同志。
    我代表蒙古人民共和国政府、蒙古人民向我们亲爱的贵宾、中华人民共和国杰出的领导人之一，尊敬的周恩来总理同志以及参加这次友好访问的中国国家活动家们致以衷心的敬意。
    蒙中人民是以苏联为首的牢不可破的强大社会主义阵营各国人民团结大家庭中的成员。建设社会主义和共产主义的崇高一致的目标，把我们两国人民紧紧地连在一起。
    我们两国人民在战无不胜的马克思列宁主义旗帜下，在实现这个正义的事业中，正在逐年取得更加巨大的新成就。
    蒙古人民每时每刻都在注视着他们的伟大朋友——中国人民在中国共产党和以毛泽东同志为首的中国共产党中央委员会的英明领导下，在建设社会主义的事业中所进行的巨大跃进和对巩固亚洲和世界和平所作出的卓越贡献，我们对此感到喜悦和自豪。
    今天，值得我们满意地指出的是，按照无产阶级国际主义伟大原则结成的我们两国间的牢不可破的友好关系，正在为了两国人民的利益坚定不移地巩固和发展着。
    尊敬的总理同志，您第一次在我国进行访问以来，已经将近六年了。从那时起，不论我国的国内情况和在国际舞台上，都发生了巨大的变化。在这期间，我们强大的社会主义阵营有了进一步的巩固和发展。
    蒙古人民依靠苏联、中华人民共和国和各兄弟社会主义国家的不断增长着的兄弟般的援助，在蒙古人民革命党的领导下，在本国的政治、经济和文化生活中取得了令人欢欣鼓舞的美好成就。
    我们感到非常高兴，能够向您介绍我国劳动人民的生活情况。我们衷心地欢迎你们在我国进行美好的访问。
    我们坚信，您的这次访问，将在我们两国人民的兄弟友谊和合作的历史上，增添新的、光辉的一页，并且将对巩固以伟大苏联为首的社会主义阵营各国人民的团结事业作出重要的贡献。
    蒙中人民的牢不可破的友谊万岁！
    世界和平万岁！
    敬爱的贵宾们，我们欢迎你们！
