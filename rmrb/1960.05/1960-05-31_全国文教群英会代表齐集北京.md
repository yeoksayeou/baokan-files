Title: National Representatives of Outstanding Individuals in Culture and Education Gather in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 30th — Representatives from all over the country attending the National Congress of Advanced Units and Advanced Workers in Socialist Construction in the fields of education, culture, health, sports, and journalism have all arrived in Beijing. They have completed their registration for the conference and have been warmly welcomed by the reception units in the capital.

Preparations for the conference are basically complete.

### 全国文教群英会代表齐集北京

1960-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

    全国文教群英会代表齐集北京
    新华社30日讯　出席全国教育和文化、卫生、体育、新闻方面社会主义建设先进单位和先进工作者代表大会的各地代表团，全部到达北京。他们已经向大会报到完毕，受到首都各接待单位的热情欢迎。
    大会的筹备工作已经基本就绪。
