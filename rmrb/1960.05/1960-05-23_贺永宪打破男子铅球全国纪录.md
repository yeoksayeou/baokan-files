Title: He Yongxian Breaks National Men's Shot Put Record
Author:
Date: 1960-05-23
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd: He Yongxian, the national record holder for men's shot put in China, broke the national record of 15.33 meters with a throw of 15.84 meters at the Beijing Higher Education Institution Track and Field Meet on the afternoon of the 21st.

### 贺永宪打破男子铅球全国纪录

1960-05-23
第7版()
专栏：

    贺永宪打破男子铅球全国纪录
    据新华社22日讯　我国男子铅球全国纪录保持者贺永宪，21日下午在北京市高等院校田径运动会上，以十五米八四的成绩打破了十五米三三的全国纪录。
