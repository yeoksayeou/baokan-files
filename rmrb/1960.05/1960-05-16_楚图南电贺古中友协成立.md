Title: Chu Tunan Sends Congratulatory Telegram on the Establishment of the Cuba-China Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1960-05-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries and the China-Latin America Friendship Association, sent a telegram on the 12th to congratulate the establishment of the Cuba-China Friendship Association.

The telegram stated that the establishment of the Cuba-China Friendship Association marks a further development of the friendly solidarity and cultural exchange between the Cuban people and the Chinese people. The Chinese people will always stand unswervingly with the Cuban people and resolutely support the Cuban people's just struggle to safeguard national independence, fight for democracy, and oppose U.S. imperialism. Victory will surely belong to the heroic Cuban people!

The telegram concluded by wishing the Cuban revolution continuous victories in its just struggle against U.S. imperialist aggression, and wishing the friendship and cultural exchange between the Chinese and Cuban peoples would grow day by day.

### 楚图南电贺古中友协成立

1960-05-16
第4版()
专栏：

    楚图南电贺古中友协成立
    新华社15日讯　中国人民对外文化协会会长、中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南，12日致电祝贺古巴中国友好协会成立。
    电报说，古巴中国友好协会的成立，标志着古巴人民与中国人民之间的友好团结和文化交流进一步发展。中国人民将始终不渝地和古巴人民站在一起，坚决支持古巴人民为维护民族独立、争取民主、反对美帝国主义的正义斗争。胜利一定属于英雄的古巴人民！
    电报最后祝古巴革命在反对美帝国主义侵略的正义斗争中不断地从胜利走向胜利，祝中国人民和古巴人民之间的友谊与文化交流日益增进。
