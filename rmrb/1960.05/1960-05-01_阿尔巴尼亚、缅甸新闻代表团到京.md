Title: Albanian and Burmese News Delegations Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th — The Albanian journalists' delegation, invited to visit our country, led by Ajet Simixhiu, Director of the Albanian Telegraph Agency, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

Xinhua News Agency, 30th — The Burmese journalists' delegation, invited by the All-China Journalists Association to visit our country, led by U Aung Than, Chairman of the Burmese Journalists Association, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

### 阿尔巴尼亚、缅甸新闻代表团到京

1960-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚、缅甸新闻代表团到京
    据新华社30日讯　应邀来我国访问的阿尔巴尼亚新闻工作者代表团，在团长、阿尔巴尼亚通讯社社长阿耶特·西米季乌率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    据新华社30日讯　应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请前来我国访问的缅甸新闻工作者代表团，在缅甸新闻工作者协会主席吴昂丹率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
