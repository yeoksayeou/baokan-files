Title: The Wish for Peaceful Reunification of the Motherland Will Surely Be Realized
Author:
Date: 1960-05-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, 29th. 150,000 people from all walks of life in Chengdu held a rally this afternoon to enthusiastically support the patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people.

Li Dazhang, Secretary of the Sichuan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Mi Jianshu, Secretary of the Chengdu Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and responsible persons of provincial and municipal organs, democratic parties, and people's organizations attended today's meeting.

Kang Nai'er, member of the Sichuan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Chairman of the Sichuan Provincial Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, spoke on behalf of the Sichuan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China. He said that the deep suffering of the South Korean people was caused by US imperialism. US imperialism is the mortal enemy of the Korean people and the culprit that endangers peace in Asia and the world. He said that we fully support the call and propositions put forward by the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party on April 11. The US aggressor troops must get out of South Korea and let the Korean people realize their wish for peaceful reunification of the motherland.

Following him, responsible persons of mass organizations such as the Sichuan Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, the Sichuan Provincial Committee of the Communist Youth League, and the Sichuan Provincial Women's Federation, as well as responsible persons of the Sichuan Provincial organizations of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, the China Democratic League, and the China National Democratic Construction Association, and representatives of workers, peasants, students, urban people's communes, and industrial and commercial circles also spoke. The congress passed a telegram supporting the patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people amidst warm applause.

### 和平统一祖国的愿望一定实现

1960-05-01
第4版()
专栏：

    和平统一祖国的愿望一定实现
    成都
    新华社成都29日电　成都市各界人民十五万人，今天下午举行集会，热烈支援南朝鲜人民的爱国正义斗争。
    中共四川省委书记李大章、中共成都市委书记米建书和省市机关、民主党派、人民团体的负责人参加了今天的大会。
    中共四川省委委员、和大四川省分会副主席康乃尔代表中共四川省委首先在大会上讲话。他说，南朝鲜人民深重的灾难是美帝国主义一手造成的。美帝国主义是朝鲜人民的死敌，是危害亚洲和世界和平的罪魁。他说，我们完全支持朝鲜劳动党中央委员会在4月11日所提出的号召和主张。美国侵略军必须滚出南朝鲜，让朝鲜人民实现和平统一祖国的愿望。
    接着讲话的有四川省工会联合会、共青团四川省委、四川省妇联等群众团体的负责人，和民革、民盟、民建四川省地方组织的负责人以及工人、农民、学生、城市人民公社、工商界的代表等。大会在热烈掌声中通过了支援南朝鲜人民爱国正义斗争的通电。
