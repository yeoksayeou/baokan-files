Title: Reaping and Planting Simultaneously (Picture)
Author: Chen Jie, Huang Luheng
Date: 1960-05-26
Page: 6

    Reaping and Planting Simultaneously
    Summer harvesting and summer planting work in various parts of Sichuan are achieving successive victories. A moving scene of "a field of yellow in the morning, a pool of water at noon, and rows of seedlings in the afternoon" has appeared everywhere. This is a scene of members of the Liemian Commune in Wusheng County rushing to transplant mid-season rice after harvesting wheat.

　　　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Chen Jie and Huang Luheng photographed
