Title: African and Costa Rican Women Representatives Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-05-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency dispatch:

    Six women representatives from Basutoland, Sierra Leone, and Tanganyika in Africa, and Costa Rica, who were invited by the All-China Women's Federation to visit China and participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 15th to return to their countries.

### 非洲和哥斯达黎加妇女代表返国

1960-05-18
第5版()
专栏：

    非洲和哥斯达黎加妇女代表返国
    据新华社讯　应全国妇女联合会邀请来我国参观访问并参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝活动的非洲巴苏陀兰、塞拉勒窝内、坦噶尼喀的妇女代表以及哥斯达黎加的妇女代表共六人，15日上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
