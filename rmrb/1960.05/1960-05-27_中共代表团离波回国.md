Title: CCP Delegation Leaves Poland for Home

Author:

Date: 1960-05-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, May 26th: The Chinese Communist Party delegation headed by Bo Yibo left Warsaw by plane this morning to return home.

Those who saw the delegation off at the airport included Polish United Workers' Party Central Political Bureau members Jędrychowski and Kliszko, United Workers' Party Central Committee Secretariat Secretary Jarosiński, Party Central Committee Minister of Science and Education Wierzbicki, Minister of Communications Czesak, and United Workers' Party Warsaw City Committee First Secretary Titkow. Chinese Ambassador to Poland Wang Bingnan also saw the delegation off at the airport.

On the evening of the 25th, Ambassador Wang Bingnan held a farewell banquet for the CCP delegation. Jędrychowski, Kliszko, Ochab, Jarosiński, Jaroszewicz and others attended the banquet.

### 中共代表团离波回国

1960-05-27
第4版()
专栏：

    中共代表团离波回国
    新华社华沙26日电　以薄一波为首的中国共产党代表团，今天上午乘飞机离开华沙返国。
    到机场送行的有波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员英德里霍夫斯基、克利什科，统一工人党中央委员会书记处书记雅罗辛斯基，党中央委员会科学和教育部长维尔布兰，联络部长切萨克，统一工人党华沙市委员会第一书记蒂特科夫。中国驻波兰大使王炳南也到机场送行。
    25日晚上，王炳南大使曾为中共代表团举行饯行宴会。英德里霍夫斯基、克利什科、奥哈布、雅罗辛斯基、雅罗谢维奇等人出席了宴会。
