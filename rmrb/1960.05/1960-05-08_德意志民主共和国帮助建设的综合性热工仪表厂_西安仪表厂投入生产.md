Title: Comprehensive Thermal Instrument Factory Built with Assistance from the German Democratic Republic - Xi'an Instrument Factory Goes into Production
Author:
Date: 1960-05-08
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an. The state-owned Xi'an Instrument Factory has been completed and recently held an opening ceremony, officially commencing production.

At the opening ceremony, Zhang Ce, Secretary of the Shaanxi Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and First Secretary of the Xi'an Municipal Committee, spoke on behalf of the Provincial Committee and the people of the province, expressing warm gratitude to the government of the German Democratic Republic for providing comprehensive technical assistance in the construction of this factory, and to the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic and the German experts who came from afar to attend the factory's opening ceremony and diligently assisted in the factory's construction.

The head of the delegation of the German Democratic Republic attending the opening ceremony of the Xi'an Instrument Factory, Mr. Weining, warmly congratulated the completion and commissioning of the instrument factory, and presented a banner on behalf of the employees of related enterprises in the German Democratic Republic.

The construction of the Xi'an Instrument Factory took two years and four months. It is a magnificent, technologically advanced, and comprehensive thermal instrument factory. It produces various instruments used in metallurgy, electric power, petroleum, chemical industry, and other sectors, serving the mechanization, automation, and scientific research of China's industrial and agricultural production.

The construction of the Xi'an Instrument Factory has received comprehensive assistance from the German Democratic Republic. Dozens of factories and enterprises have provided technical data and advanced machinery and equipment to the Xi'an Instrument Factory, and have helped train technical and management cadres. The Party and government of the German Democratic Republic also sent outstanding experts in civil engineering, equipment, installation, production, technology, and product design to assist in the factory's construction and production preparation work.

### 德意志民主共和国帮助建设的综合性热工仪表厂  西安仪表厂投入生产

1960-05-08
第2版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国帮助建设的综合性热工仪表厂
    西安仪表厂投入生产
    据新华社西安电　国营西安仪表厂已经建成，最近举行开工典礼，正式投入生产。
    在开工典礼大会上，中共陕西省委书记、西安市委第一书记张策在讲话中，代表省委和全省人民向给予这个厂的建设以全面技术援助的德意志民主共和国政府，向远道而来参加工厂开工典礼的德意志民主共和国政府代表团和辛勤帮助建厂的德国专家热烈致谢。
    参加西安仪表厂开工典礼的德意志民主共和国代表团团长威宁热情祝贺仪表厂的建成和投入生产，并代表德意志民主共和国有关企业职工赠送了锦旗。
    西安仪表厂的建设历时两年零四个月，是一座规模宏伟，技术先进，综合性的热工仪表厂。它生产冶金、电力、石油、化工等部门所用的各种仪表，为我国工农业生产的机械化、自动化和科学研究工作服务。
    西安仪表厂的建设，得到德意志民主共和国全面援助，几十个工厂企业为西安仪表厂提供了技术资料和先进的机器设备，并帮助培训了技术干部和管理干部。德意志民主共和国党和政府还派出土建、设备、安装、生产、工艺及产品设计等方面的优秀专家，帮助这个厂的建设和生产准备工作。
