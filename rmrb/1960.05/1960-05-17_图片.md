Title: Pictures

Author:

Date: 1960-05-17
Page: 2

    Fifteen thousand people from all walks of life in Fuzhou City, on the front lines of national defense, held a rally and demonstration on May 14, resolutely supporting the just struggle of the Japanese people against the U.S.-Japan military alliance treaty. This is a photograph of militiamen angrily shouting slogans.
                              Xinhua News Agency reporter Xiang Zhi (Fax photo)
    Residents of Yaoshui Lane in Shanghai's Putuo District, who were blockaded by Japanese imperialism for more than ten days in 1937, cut off from the outside world and starved to death twenty-seven residents, signed their names, resolutely opposing U.S. imperialism's revival of Japanese militarism.
                                     Xinhua News Agency reporter Cui Shengli
    On May 14, 600,000 people from all walks of life in Guangzhou City held a rally and demonstration to support the Japanese people's opposition to the U.S.-Japan military alliance treaty. Workers participating in the demonstration shouted: Oppose the U.S.-Japanese reactionaries' conspiracy to plan a new war!
                            Xinhua News Agency reporter Cai Zhongzhi (Fax photo)
    Workers of the Shanghai State-Owned Cotton Mill No. 2 gathered in front of the martyr Gu Zhenghong's memorial statue on May 15 to support the Japanese people's struggle against the U.S.-Japan military alliance treaty. The predecessor of Shanghai State-Owned Cotton Mill No. 2 was the Shanghai Japanese-owned Naigaiwata Cotton Mill No. 7. Gu Zhenghong, a worker at this factory, was killed by Japanese imperialists on this day thirty-five years ago. Subsequently, the "May Thirtieth" anti-imperialist patriotic movement broke out in Shanghai.
                            Xinhua News Agency reporter Yang Putao (Fax photo)
    On May 12, 200,000 people of all ethnic groups and from all walks of life in Lanzhou City, Gansu Province, held a large-scale rally and demonstration, resolutely supporting the Japanese people's just struggle against the U.S.-Japan military alliance treaty.
                                       Huang Duidai (Xinhua News Agency draft)

### 图片

1960-05-17
第2版()
专栏：

    海防前线的福州市各界人民十五万人，5月14日，举行集会和游行示威，坚决支持日本人民反对日美军事同盟条约的正义斗争。这是民兵们在愤怒地高呼口号。　
                              新华社记者　相知摄（传真照片）
    1937年5月27日，曾被日本帝国主义封锁在里弄十多天，与外隔绝无东西可吃，饿死了二十七名居民的上海普陀区药水弄居民纷纷签名，坚决反对美帝国主义复活日本军国主义。
                                     　新华社记者　崔生联摄
    5月14日，广州市各界六十万人集会示威，支援日本人民反对日美军事同盟条约。参加示威的工人们高呼：反对美日反动派策划新战争的阴谋！　 
                            新华社记者　蔡忠植摄（传真照片）
    上海国棉二厂的工人5月15日在顾正红烈士纪念像前集会，支持日本人民反对日美军事同盟条约的斗争。上海国棉二厂的前身是上海日商内外棉纺第七厂。这个工厂的工人顾正红就是在三十五年前的今天被日本帝国主义者杀害的。随后，在上海爆发了“五卅”反帝爱国运动。
                            新华社记者　杨溥涛摄（传真照片）
    5月12日，甘肃省兰州市各族各界二十万人举行大规模的集会和示威游行，坚决支持日本人民反对日美军事同盟条约的正义斗争。
                                       黄对岱摄（新华社稿）
