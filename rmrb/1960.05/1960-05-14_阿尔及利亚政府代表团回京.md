Title: Algerian Government Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 13th. The Algerian Provisional Government delegation led by Deputy Prime Minister Krim Belkacem, returned to Beijing today after visiting the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to continue their visit in our country.
    The distinguished Algerian guests, accompanied by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei and others, visited Shijiazhuang today.

### 阿尔及利亚政府代表团回京

1960-05-14
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚政府代表团回京
    新华社13日讯　由克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆副总理率领的阿尔及利亚临时政府代表团，在访问朝鲜民主主义人民共和国之后，今天返回北京，继续在我国进行访问。
    阿尔及利亚的贵宾们，今天由外交部副部长姬鹏飞等陪同，在石家庄进行了参观访问。
