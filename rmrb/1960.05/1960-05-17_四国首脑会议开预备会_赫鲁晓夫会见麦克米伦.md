Title: Four-Power Summit Preparatory Meeting Held; Khrushchev Meets with Macmillan
Author:
Date: 1960-05-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, May 16th, dispatch from Paris: According to TASS, the preparatory meeting for the summit conference of the four powers, the Soviet Union, France, the United States, and the United Kingdom, was held today at 11:00 AM Paris time in the French Cabinet meeting room at the Élysée Palace.

The Soviet side participating in the meeting included Chairman of the Council of Ministers Khrushchev, Foreign Minister Gromyko, and Defense Minister Malinovsky. The French side included President De Gaulle, Prime Minister Debré, and Foreign Minister Couve de Murville. The American side included President Eisenhower, Secretary of State Herter, and Defense Secretary Gates. The British side included Prime Minister Macmillan and Foreign Secretary Lloyd. The preparatory meeting began at 11:00 AM sharp.

Xinhua News Agency, May 15th, dispatch from Paris: TASS reports that Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, visited British Prime Minister Macmillan at the British Embassy on the 15th.

Accompanying Khrushchev on the visit were Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko, Defense Minister Malinovsky, and Soviet Ambassador to the United Kingdom, Soldatov.

### 四国首脑会议开预备会  赫鲁晓夫会见麦克米伦

1960-05-17
第5版()
专栏：

    四国首脑会议开预备会
    赫鲁晓夫会见麦克米伦
    新华社16日讯　据塔斯社巴黎讯：苏、法、美、英四大国首脑会议预备会议今天上午巴黎时间十一时在爱丽舍宫法国内阁会议厅开会。
    参加会议的苏联方面有部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫、外交部长葛罗米柯、国防部长马利诺夫斯基。法国方面是总统戴高乐、总理德勃雷和外交部长德姆维尔。美国方面是总统艾森豪威尔、国务卿赫脱和国防部长盖茨。英国方面是首相麦克米伦和外交大臣劳埃德。预备会议在十一点整开始。
    新华社15日讯　塔斯社巴黎讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫15日前往英国大使馆拜会英国首相麦克米伦。
    陪同赫鲁晓夫前往拜会的有苏联外交部长葛罗米柯、国防部长马利诺夫斯基和苏联驻英国大使索尔达托夫。
