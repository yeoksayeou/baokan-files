Title: USSR Russian Minister of Social Security Muravyeva Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:
Date: 1960-05-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 13th - Muravyeva, Minister of Social Security of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic of the USSR, and Filimonova, Director of the Department of Large Families Mothers, who came to China for a friendly visit at the invitation of Minister of Internal Affairs Qian Ying, concluded their visits to Beijing, Shanghai and other places and left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.  Those who saw them off at the airport included Qian Ying, Minister of Internal Affairs, Wang Yifu and Chen Qiyuan, Vice Ministers. Antonov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 苏联俄罗斯社会保障部长  穆拉维耶娃离京回国

1960-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联俄罗斯社会保障部长
    穆拉维耶娃离京回国
    新华社13日讯　应内务部部长钱瑛邀请来我国进行友好访问的苏联俄罗斯苏维埃联邦共和国社会保障部部长穆拉维耶娃，和随同前来的多子女母亲管理局局长菲里莫诺娃，结束了在北京、上海等地的访问，今天早晨乘飞机离开北京回国。到机场欢送的，有内务部部长钱瑛，副部长王一夫、陈其瑗等。苏联驻华大使馆参赞安东诺夫也到机场送行。
