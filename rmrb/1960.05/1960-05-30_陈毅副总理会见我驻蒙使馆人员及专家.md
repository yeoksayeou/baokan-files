Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Staff of Chinese Embassy in Mongolia and Experts
Author:
Date: 1960-05-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, May 29th. Vice Premier Chen Yi met today at the Chinese Embassy in Mongolia with all the comrades of the Embassy, representatives of Chinese experts, technicians, and employees working in the Mongolian People's Republic, as well as representatives of overseas Chinese.

    Vice Premier Chen Yi had cordial conversations with them.

### 陈毅副总理会见我驻蒙使馆人员及专家

1960-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理会见我驻蒙使馆人员及专家
    新华社乌兰巴托29日电　陈毅副总理今天在中国驻蒙古大使馆会见了大使馆全体同志、在蒙古人民共和国工作的中国专家、技术人员和员工的代表，以及华侨代表。
    陈毅副总理同他们进行了亲切的谈话。
