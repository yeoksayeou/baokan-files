Title: Brazilian Agricultural Economist Borges and Wife Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-05-25
Page: 4
　
    Xinhua News Agency, May 24th. The Brazilian agricultural economist Pembe Votore Akseori Borges and his wife, who were invited by the Chinese Latin American Friendship Association to visit our country, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 23rd to return home.

### 巴西农业经济学家  波热斯和夫人回国

1960-05-25
第4版()
专栏：

    巴西农业经济学家
    波热斯和夫人回国
    新华社24日讯　应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请来我国访问的巴西农业经济学家彭贝渥·阿克孝里·波热斯夫妇，已经在23日上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
