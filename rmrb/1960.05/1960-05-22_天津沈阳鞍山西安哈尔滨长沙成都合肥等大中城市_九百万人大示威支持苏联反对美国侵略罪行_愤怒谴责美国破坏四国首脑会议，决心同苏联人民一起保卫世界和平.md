Title: People of Tianjin, Shenyang, Anshan, Xi'an, Harbin, Changsha, Chengdu, Hefei, and Other Major Cities Hold a Massive Demonstration of Nine Million People Supporting the Soviet Union and Opposing American Aggression and Crimes; Angrily Condemning the United States for Sabotaging the Four-Power Summit, Determined to Defend World Peace Together with the Soviet People
Author:
Date: 1960-05-22
Page: 1

Unite, people of the world, and overthrow American imperialism!
Tianjin, Shenyang, Anshan, Xi'an, Harbin, Changsha, Chengdu, Hefei, and Other Major Cities
Nine Million People Hold a Massive Demonstration Supporting the Soviet Union and Opposing American Aggression and Crimes
Angrily Condemning the United States for Sabotaging the Four-Power Summit, Determined to Defend World Peace Together with the Soviet People

(Xinhua News Agency Report) Massive rallies and demonstrations supporting the just position of the Soviet Union and opposing the United States' sabotage of the Four-Power Summit were held in major and medium-sized cities in China, including Tianjin, Shenyang, Anshan, Xi'an, Harbin, Changsha, Chengdu, and Hefei. The number of people who participated in these rallies and demonstrations reached 9,080,000.

In Tianjin, two million people held a massive parade and demonstration. Liu Zihou, Secretary of the Hebei Provincial Party Committee of the Communist Party of China, spoke at the rally. He first represented the 43 million people of the entire province in expressing their firm support for the Soviet Union's just position and extreme indignation at the American aggression and provocation against the Soviet Union. Representatives from all walks of life also spoke. Their speeches repeatedly aroused warm applause. From the central venue to other venues, the cry of "Unite, people of the world, and overthrow American imperialism" resounded everywhere.

In Liaoning Province, grand rallies and demonstrations were held in Shenyang, Anshan, Andong, and Yingkou. In Shenyang, 1.5 million people participated; in Anshan, 800,000 people participated; and in Andong and Yingkou, 380,000 people participated. In Shenyang, Huang Huoqing, First Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Party Committee of the Communist Party of China, vehemently condemned the aggressive and provocative acts of American imperialism. His speech was repeatedly welcomed by the masses. In Anshan, Meng Tai, National Model Worker and Deputy Director of the Anshan Iron and Steel Works, said: "If American imperialism insists on aggressive war, it is like an egg hitting a rock, seeking its own death. Our steelworkers are determined to produce more high-quality steel to strengthen the socialist camp's power. If American imperialism dares to launch a war, we will smash their heads!"

In Shaanxi Province, rallies and parades were held in Xi'an, Xianyang, Baoji, Tongchuan, Yulin, Yan'an, Hanzhong, Ankang, and Shangxian. More than 1.7 million people participated in these locations. In Xi'an, Zhang Ce, Secretary of the Shaanxi Provincial Party Committee of the Communist Party of China, spoke. In these places, the masses who gathered and marched expressed their determination to stand with the great Soviet people, resolutely fight, completely eliminate imperialism, and defend world peace. While rallies were being held, even more people listened to the radio broadcasts; in Hanzhong, Shangxian, and Baoji alone, more than 1.3 million people listened in.

In Harbin, large banners were hung on all the main buildings of the city early in the morning. Near noon, over a million workers, farmers, students, cadres, etc., converged into a massive torrent, pouring into the rally venue. Ouyang Qin, First Secretary of the Heilongjiang Provincial Party Committee of the Communist Party of China, representing the people of the province, expressed firm support for the Soviet Union's just position and a determination to smash the aggressive schemes of American imperialism.

In Changsha, Hunan Province, 800,000 people participated in the rally and parade. Those who attended the meeting were filled with righteous indignation and irrepressible anger, harboring deep hatred for the aggressive and provocative acts of American imperialism. Zhou Hui, Secretary of the Hunan Provincial Party Committee of the Communist Party of China, Cheng Qian, Vice Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, and people from all walks of life spoke. They unanimously expressed their support for the Soviet Union's just position and their firm opposition to the aggression and provocation of American imperialism.

In Chengdu, 500,000 people from all walks of life held a massive rally and demonstration today. Li Daizhang, Secretary of the Sichuan Provincial Party Committee of the Communist Party of China and Governor of Sichuan Province, spoke at the mass rally in the city center square. He said that the people of Sichuan firmly support the Soviet Union's solemn stance and oppose the American imperialist crime of sabotaging the Four-Power Summit. After the meeting, 500,000 people held demonstration parades in different districts.

In Hefei, Anhui Province, a rally and parade of 400,000 people was held today. Gui Linqi, Secretary of the Anhui Provincial Party Committee of the Communist Party of China, spoke. Flags were like a forest in the square, and people waved their fists and shouted slogans with fervor. Liang Yongzhen, a sixty-year-old grandmother, composed a poem: "American imperialism is truly hateful, creating wars to harm the people; you sabotage the Four-Power Conference, yet falsely accuse others of being responsible; no matter how you change your tricks, the people have long seen your ugly face; if we don't overthrow you one day, we will not stop fighting for a moment."

### 天津沈阳鞍山西安哈尔滨长沙成都合肥等大中城市  九百万人大示威支持苏联反对美国侵略罪行  愤怒谴责美国破坏四国首脑会议，决心同苏联人民一起保卫世界和平

1960-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

    全世界人民团结起来，打倒美帝国主义！
    天津沈阳鞍山西安哈尔滨长沙成都合肥等大中城市
    九百万人大示威支持苏联反对美国侵略罪行
    愤怒谴责美国破坏四国首脑会议，决心同苏联人民一起保卫世界和平
    本报讯　据新华社消息：我国天津、沈阳、鞍山、西安、哈尔滨、长沙、成都、合肥等大中城市举行支持苏联正义立场、反对美国破坏四国首脑会议的集会和示威。参加这些地方集会和示威的群众达九百零八万人。
      在天津，二百万人举行了声势浩大的游行和示威。中共河北省委书记刘子厚在大会上作了讲话，他首先代表全省四千三百多万人民表示坚决支持苏联的正义立场，对美国对苏联的侵略和挑衅表示极大的愤慨。各界代表也都讲了话。他们的讲话都一再激起热烈的掌声。从中心会场到其他会场，到处高呼“全世界人民团结起来，打倒美帝国主义。”
    在辽宁省的沈阳、鞍山、安东和营口等地都举行了盛大的集会和示威。在沈阳，参加的群众有一百五十万，在鞍山，参加的群众有八十万，在安东和营口，参加的群众有三十八万。在沈阳，中共辽宁省委第一书记黄火青，十分愤慨地谴责了美帝国主义的侵略和挑衅行为。他的讲话，一再受到群众的欢迎。在鞍山，全国劳动模范、鞍钢炼铁厂副厂长孟泰说：“美帝国主义如果坚持侵略战争，就像鸡蛋往石头上碰，是自找死亡，我们钢铁工人坚决要多炼出优质钢铁，加强社会主义阵营的强大力量，美帝国主义胆敢发动战争，我们就要打碎它们的脑袋！”
    在陕西省，西安市和咸阳、宝鸡、铜川、榆林、延安、汉中、安康商县都举行了集会和游行。这些地方参加的群众有一百七十多万人。在西安，中共陕西省委书记张策作了讲话。在这些地方，集会和游行的群众都表示要同伟大的苏联人民在一起，坚决斗争，彻底消灭帝国主义、保卫世界和平。在集会举行的同时，各地收听广播的更多，仅汉中、商县和宝鸡三个地区收听的就达一百三十多万人。
    在哈尔滨，一早，全市的主要建筑物上都挂起了大幅标语。将近中午，一百多万工人、农民、学生、机关干部等，汇成一支巨流，涌进大会会场。中共黑龙江省委第一书记欧阳钦代表全省人民表示坚决支持苏联的正义立场，坚决粉碎美帝国主义的侵略阴谋。
    湖南省长沙市，有八十万人参加了集会和游行。参加大会的人们，个个义愤填胸，怒不可抑，对美帝国主义的侵略和挑衅行为抱有切齿的痛恨。中共湖南省委书记周惠、中国国民党革命委员会副主席程潜以及各界人士都作了讲话。他们一致表示支持苏联的正义立场，坚决反对美帝国主义的侵略和挑衅。
    在成都，各界人民五十万人今天举行声势浩大的集会和示威。中共四川省委书记、四川省省长李大章在市中心广场的群众大会上作了讲话，他说，四川人民坚决支持苏联的严正立场，反对美帝国主义破坏四国首脑会议的侵略罪行。会后，五十万人分区举行了示威游行。
    安徽省合肥市，今天举行了四十万人的集会和游行。中共安徽省委书记桂林栖作了讲话。广场上旗帜如林，人们挥着拳头，激昂地高呼口号。六十岁的老奶奶梁永珍编了一首诗：“美帝国主义真可恨，制造战争害人民，四国会议你破坏，反叫别人负责任；不管你把戏怎么变，你的鬼脸人民早看清，一天不把你打倒，我们一刻也不停止斗争。”（附图片）
    5月21日，天津市人民集会示威，支持苏联正义立场，严厉谴责美帝国主义破坏四国首脑会议。图为大会会场一角。新华社记者
