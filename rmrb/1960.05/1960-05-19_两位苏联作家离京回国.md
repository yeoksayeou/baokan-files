Title: Two Soviet Writers Leave Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-05-19
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, May 18th. The Soviet writers Ivan Konstantinovich Talba and Stepan Ivanovich Oleinik, who came to China for a visit according to the 1959 implementation plan of the Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home after visiting Beijing, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Changsha, Wuhan and other cities.
　　Wang Yafan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Writers Association, and Chen Bingyi, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of "World Literature" magazine, were at the airport to see them off.

### 两位苏联作家离京回国

1960-05-19
第5版()
专栏：

    两位苏联作家离京回国
    新华社18日讯　根据中苏文化合作协定1959年执行计划来我国访问的苏联作家依万·康斯坦丁诺维奇·塔尔巴和斯切潘·依万诺维奇·奥列尼克，在访问了北京、南京、上海、杭州、广州、长沙、武汉等城市后，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的有中国作家协会副秘书长王亚凡，“世界文学”杂志副主编陈冰夷。
