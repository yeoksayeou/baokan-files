Title: Togolese Workers' Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. A delegation of two from the National Union of Togolese Workers, led by Jean Sinette, General Secretary of the Togolese Meteorological Workers' Union, arrived in Beijing by plane today. They are visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

### 多哥工人联合会代表团到京

1960-05-13
第4版()
专栏：

    多哥工人联合会代表团到京
    新华社12日讯　多哥工人全国联合会代表团一行二人，在团长、多哥气象工作者工会总书记让·西奈特率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中华全国总工会邀请来我国访问的。
