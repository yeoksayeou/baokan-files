Title: Liao Chengzhi and Others Said at the Farewell for the Japan-China Friendship Association Delegation: The Chinese People Will Forever Support the Japanese People's Struggle. Akatsu Masuzo Said: We Will Never Allow US Imperialist Aggression and the Revival of Japanese Militarism.
Author:
Date: 1960-05-12
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China, held a banquet at noon today to bid farewell to the Japan-China Friendship Association delegation headed by Akatsu Masuzo and Japanese journalists including Ito Takeo.

Yang Hansheng delivered a farewell speech at the banquet. He expressed his hope that the Japanese friends would convey to the Japanese people the enthusiasm and determination of the Chinese people to support the Japanese people's struggle against the Japan-US military alliance treaty and for Japan's independence, democracy, peace, and neutrality. He proposed a toast to the complete victory of the Japanese people's struggle and to the militant friendship between the Chinese and Japanese people.

Head of Delegation Akatsu Masuzo said in his speech that their participation in the mass rally of over one million people in Beijing and the mass rally of 800,000 people in Tianjin, as well as everything they had seen in China, had deeply convinced them that the Chinese people firmly supported the Japanese people's struggle to the end. He expressed his heartfelt gratitude for this support.

Akatsu Masuzo stated that upon returning home, they would not only convey to the Japanese people the enthusiasm and determination of the Chinese people to support the Japanese people, but would also concentrate all their efforts on opposing the Japan-US military alliance treaty and the revival of Japanese militarism in the future. He firmly said, "We will never allow US imperialist aggression, and we will never allow the revival of Japanese militarism."

Li Dequan said in her speech that the Chinese and Japanese people would unite together to fight to the end to defend world peace. She said that they had visited Japan twice and would never forget the Japanese people's love for the Chinese people.

Liao Chengzhi emphasized in his speech that the friendship between the Chinese and Japanese people was a fighting friendship forged against a common enemy. He stated: "China's attitude towards Kishi Nobusuke is firm, and the Chinese people's support for the Japanese people's struggle is also forever firm." He thanked the friends of the Japan-China Friendship Association for their contributions to enhancing the friendly relations between the Chinese and Japanese people. He wished the Japanese friends greater development and greater achievements in their future work.

The speeches of the hosts and guests were warmly welcomed by people from both China and Japan.

Chinese figures from various fields including Cai Tingkai, Mei Lanfang, Lü Ji, Xie Nanguang, Zhao Anbo, Zhao Puchu, Ding Tuo, Sun Pinghua, Xiao Xiangqian, Peng Yan, Zheng Senyu, Kin Koaki, Wu Xuewen, and Lin Lin, attended the banquet.

Japanese peace figure in Beijing, Kinkazu Saionji, was invited to attend the banquet.

### 廖承志等在欢送日中友协代表团时说  中国人民永远支持日本人民斗争  赤津益造说绝不容许美帝国主义侵略和日本军国主义复活

1960-05-12
第2版()
专栏：

    廖承志等在欢送日中友协代表团时说
    中国人民永远支持日本人民斗争
    赤津益造说绝不容许美帝国主义侵略和日本军国主义复活
    新华社11日讯　中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙和中国红十字会总会会长李德全今天中午举行宴会，欢送以赤津益造为首的日中友好协会访华代表团和伊藤武雄等日本新闻界人士。
    阳翰笙在宴会上致欢送词。他希望日本朋友们把中国人民支持日本人民反对日美军事同盟条约和争取日本独立、民主、和平、中立的斗争的热情和决心，告诉日本人民。他提议为日本人民斗争的彻底胜利，为中日两国人民的战斗友谊，干杯。
    赤津益造团长在致词中说，他们这次在中国参加了北京一百多万人大集会和天津八十万人大集会，以及他们看到的一切，使他们深信中国人民对日本人民的斗争是坚决支持到底的。他衷心感谢这种支持。
    赤津益造表示，他们回国后不仅要把中国人民支持日本人民的热情和决心，详细告诉日本人民，而且今后将集中全力反对日美军事同盟条约和反对日本军国主义的复活。他坚定地说，我们绝不容许美帝国主义的侵略，我们也绝不容许日本军国主义复活。
    李德全在致词中说，中日两国人民团结一起为保卫世界和平奋斗到底。她说，我们曾两次访问日本，我们永远不会忘记日本人民对中国人民的热爱。
    廖承志在致词中强调指出，中日两国人民的友谊是为反对共同敌人而结成的战斗友谊。他表示：中国对岸信介的态度是坚决的，中国人民支持日本人民的斗争也是永远坚决的。他感谢日中友好协会的朋友们为增进中日两国人民友好关系方面所作的贡献。他祝日本朋友们今后在工作中获得更大的发展和更大的成就。
    宾主的讲话，受到中日两国人士的热烈欢迎。
    中国各方面的人士蔡廷锴、梅兰芳、吕骥、谢南光、赵安博、赵朴初、丁拓、孙平化、萧向前、彭炎、郑森禹、金子明、吴学文、林林等，出席了宴会。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一应邀出席了宴会。
