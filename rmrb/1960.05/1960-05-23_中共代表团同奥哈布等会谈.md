Title: CCP Delegation Holds Talks with Ochab and Others
Author: 
Date: 1960-05-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, May 21st: The Chinese Communist Party delegation, headed by Bo Yibo, held talks with Ochab, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, and Kliszko in Warsaw on the 21st.

Present at the talks were Jarosinski, Secretary of the Central Committee of the United Workers' Party, and ministers of various departments of the Central Committee. Wang Bingnan, Chinese Ambassador to Poland, was also present.

On the same day, Jędrychowsk, Chairman of the Polish-Chinese Friendship Association and member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, received the delegation and had a cordial conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Stawinski, member of the Board of Directors of the Polish-Chinese Friendship Association and Minister of Light Industry, Leśz, Minister of Domestic Trade, Wende, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Democratic Party, and Ambassador Wang Bingnan.

The CCP delegation returned to Warsaw on the morning of the 21st after visiting Krakow, Wrocław, Katowice, and Gdańsk. The delegation will continue to visit Warsaw and other places.

### 中共代表团同奥哈布等会谈

1960-05-23
第5版()
专栏：

   中共代表团同奥哈布等会谈
    新华社华沙21日电　以薄一波为首的中国共产党代表团，21日在华沙同波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员、书记处书记奥哈布、克利什科举行了会谈。
    会谈时在座的有统一工人党中央委员会书记处书记雅罗辛斯基以及党中央各部的部长们。中国驻波兰大使王炳南也在座。
    当天，波中友好协会主席、波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员英德里霍夫斯基接见了代表团，并同代表团进行了亲切的交谈。
    接见时在座的有波中友好协会理事、轻工业部部长斯塔文斯基，国内贸易部部长莱什，民主党中央委员会副主席温德等人，在座的还有王炳南大使。
    中共代表团是在参观了克拉科夫、弗罗茨瓦夫、卡托维茨、格但斯克等城市之后于21日上午返回华沙的。代表团将继续在华沙等地参观访问。
