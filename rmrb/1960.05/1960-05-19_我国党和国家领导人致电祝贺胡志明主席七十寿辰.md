Our Party and State Leaders Send Telegram to Congratulate President Ho Chi Minh on His Seventieth Birthday

Xinhua News Agency, May 18th. Our Party and state leaders today sent a telegram to President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Viet Nam, congratulating him on his seventieth birthday. The telegram reads as follows:

To Comrade Ho Chi Minh, Chairman of the Central Committee and General Secretary of the Viet Nam Workers' Party, President of the Democratic Republic of Viet Nam,

On the occasion of your seventieth birthday, we, on behalf of the Chinese Communist Party , the government of People's Republic of China and the Chinese people extend to you--the founder and leader of the Viet Nam Workers' Party, the most beloved leader of the Vietnamese people, an outstanding fighter of the international communist movement, and the closest friend of the Chinese people, our warm and fraternal congratulations.

For decades, with all your energy and rich experience in revolutionary struggle, you have carried out a long-term, heroic and unyielding struggle for the complete liberation of the Vietnamese working people, for the cause of peace, unification, independence and democracy in Viet Nam, and have achieved great victories. Your outstanding contribution to the revolutionary cause of the Vietnamese people, the international communist movement and the workers' movement, as well as to world peace, has made you loved not only by the Vietnamese people, but also by the people of China and all countries in the world.

We sincerely congratulate you on your health and longevity. We wish you greater and newer achievements in leading the Vietnamese people to build socialism, strive for the peaceful unification of Viet Nam, oppose the war and aggression policies of U.S. imperialism, and defend peace in the Far East and the world.

Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party: Mao Zedong
Chairman of the People's Republic of China: Liu Shaoqi
Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China: Zhu De
Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China: Zhou Enlai

May 18, 1960

### 我国党和国家领导人致电祝贺胡志明主席七十寿辰

1960-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我国党和国家领导人致电祝贺胡志明主席七十寿辰
    新华社18日讯　我国党和国家领导人今天打电报给越南民主共和国胡志明主席，祝贺他的七十寿辰。电文如下：越南劳动党中央委员会主席兼总书记、越南民主共和国主席，敬爱的胡志明同志：
    欣逢你七十寿辰之际，我们谨代表中国共产党、中华人民共和国政府和中国人民向你——越南劳动党的创始者和领导者、越南人民最敬爱的领袖、国际共产主义运动杰出的战士和中国人民最亲密的朋友致热忱的、兄弟般的祝贺。　
    数十年来，你以自己的全部精力和丰富的革命斗争经验，为越南劳动人民的彻底解放，为越南和平、统一、独立和民主的事业进行了长期的英勇不屈的斗争，并且取得了巨大的胜利。你对越南人民革命事业、国际共产主义运动和工人运动以及世界和平所作的卓越贡献，使你不仅受到越南人民，而且受到中国和世界各国人民的敬爱。
    我们衷心地祝贺你健康和长寿。祝你在领导越南人民建设社会主义、争取越南和平统一、反对美帝国主义的战争政策和侵略政策和保卫远东和世界和平的斗争中取得更大更新的成就。
    中国共产党中央委员会主席  毛泽东
    中华人民共和国主席  刘少奇
    中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长　朱德
    中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                                    一九六○年五月十八日（附图片）
