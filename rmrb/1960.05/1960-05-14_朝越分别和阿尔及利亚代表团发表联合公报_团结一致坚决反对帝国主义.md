Title: 朝越 separately issued joint communiques with the Algerian delegation: Unite and resolutely oppose imperialism
Author:
Date: 1960-05-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 12th: The joint communique issued here today by the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria emphasized the need to maintain a high degree of vigilance against the conspiracies of the imperialists and to further strengthen the unity of the Asian and African peoples in the struggle against imperialism and colonialism. The communique said that the Korean government delegation highly appreciates the heroic struggle of the Algerian people against French colonialism and for national independence and freedom, and expressed its full support.

The communique said: The Algerian delegation highly appreciates the achievements of the Korean people in overcoming imperialism and in socialist construction. The Korean issue must be resolved peacefully by the Korean people themselves on a democratic basis without foreign interference. The Algerian delegation also expressed its full support for the struggle of the South Korean people against fascist rule.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 6th: A joint communique issued here today by the delegation of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria stated that the delegations of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria believe that the unity of Asian and African countries in the struggle against colonialism and in defense of world peace has repeatedly defeated the aggressive actions of imperialism.

The communique said that in Africa, with the resistance of the Algerian people, many countries have raised the banner of national revolution. Both delegations expressed their firm support for the struggle of other African countries against colonialism. The communique said that the delegation of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam condemns the French colonialists' conspiracy to split the territory of Algeria and condemns the members of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization, especially the United States, for supporting the colonialists in engaging in a colonial war against the Algerian people. The Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Vietnamese people will continue to thoroughly support the heroic struggle of the Algerian people until final victory. The delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria is pleased with the achievements of the Vietnamese people in building their motherland since the restoration of peace in Indochina. The delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria fully sympathizes with the desire of the Vietnamese people to reunify their motherland and believes that the reunification of Vietnam must be realized on the basis of strict implementation of the Geneva Accords.

### 朝越分别和阿尔及利亚代表团发表联合公报  团结一致坚决反对帝国主义

1960-05-14
第5版()
专栏：

    朝越分别和阿尔及利亚代表团发表联合公报
    团结一致坚决反对帝国主义
    据新华社平壤12日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和阿尔及利亚共和国的临时政府代表团今天在这里发表的联合公报强调，必须对帝国主义者的阴谋保持高度的警惕，并在反对帝国主义和殖民主义的斗争中进一步加强亚非人民的团结。公报说，朝鲜政府代表团对阿尔及利亚人民反对法国殖民主义、争取民族独立和自由的英勇斗争给予高度的评价，并表示完全的支持。
    公报说：阿尔及利亚代表团对朝鲜人民战胜帝国主义，并在社会主义建设中取得的成就，给予高度的评价。朝鲜问题必须在没有外国干涉的情况下由朝鲜人民自己在民主的基础上和平解决。阿尔及利亚代表团并表示完全支持南朝鲜人民反对法西斯统治的斗争。
    据新华社河内6日电　此间今天公布越南民主共和国政府代表团和阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团发表的联合公报说，越南民主共和国政府代表团和阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团认为，亚非各国在反对殖民主义和保卫世界和平斗争中的团结曾经一再击败了帝国主义的侵略行动。
    公报说，在非洲，随着阿尔及利亚人民的抗战，许多国家已经高举了民族革命旗帜。双方代表团表示它们坚决支持非洲其它各国反对殖民主义的斗争。公报说，越南民主共和国政府代表团谴责法国殖民者分裂阿尔及利亚国土的阴谋，谴责北大西洋军事集团各成员国、特别是美国支持殖民者从事与阿尔及利亚人民为敌的殖民地战争的行为。越南民主共和国政府和越南人民将继续彻底支持阿尔及利亚人民英勇斗争，直到取得最后胜利。阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团对越南人民在印度支那和平恢复以来在建设祖国事业中取得的成就感到高兴。阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团完全同情越南人民统一祖国的愿望，并认为越南的统一必须在严正履行日内瓦协议的基础上实现。
