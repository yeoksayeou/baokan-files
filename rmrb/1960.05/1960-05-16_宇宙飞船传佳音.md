Title: Good News from Spaceship

Author:

Date: 1960-05-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 15th: TASS, Moscow, May 15th: Signals transmitted by the spaceship launched by the Soviet Union into Earth orbit today have been recorded. Moscow Radio broadcast these signals.

### 宇宙飞船传佳音

1960-05-16
第5版()
专栏：

    宇宙飞船传佳音
    新华社莫斯科15日电　据塔斯社莫斯科15日讯：苏联今天向地球卫星轨道发射的宇宙飞船传来的讯号已被录制下来。莫斯科广播电台转播了这些讯号。
