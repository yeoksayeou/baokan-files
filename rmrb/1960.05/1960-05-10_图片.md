On May 5th, in Ankara, the capital of Turkey, about four thousand students, staff, and civil servants held a demonstration. Demonstrators blocked the car of Turkish dictator Prime Minister Menderes on Atatürk Boulevard in Ankara, waving their fists at him and demanding his resignation. The picture shows Menderes surrounded by the masses.
