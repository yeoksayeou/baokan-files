Title: Comrade Zhu De and Comrade Deng Xiaoping Host a Banquet for Danish Communist Party Chairman Jespersen
Author:
Date: 1960-05-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 24th.  Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, and Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary, hosted a banquet at noon today for Danish Communist Party Chairman Jespersen and Mrs. Jespersen, and held cordial talks.

    Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau, Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat, Liu Lantao, Alternate Secretary, Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee, and Li Qixin and Zhang Xiangshan, responsible staff members of the CPC Central Committee organs, also attended the banquet and talks.
    In recent days, Comrade Jespersen and his entourage visited the state-owned Beijing No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory, Erlong Road People's Commune, the Palace Museum, the National Cultural Palace, and toured the city and the Summer Palace. Comrade Jespersen and his entourage have left for other places today for visits. (Attached picture)
    Zhu De and Deng Xiaoping host a banquet for Danish Communist Party Chairman Knud Jespersen. The second person from the right is Knud Jespersen, and the fourth person is Mrs. Knud Jespersen.
        Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Changyong

### 朱德同志和邓小平同志欢宴丹共主席耶斯佩森

1960-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德同志和邓小平同志欢宴丹共主席耶斯佩森
    新华社24日讯　中共中央副主席朱德、总书记邓小平，今天中午设宴招待丹麦共产党主席耶斯佩森和耶斯佩森夫人，并且进行了亲切的会谈。
    参加宴会和会谈的，还有政治局委员彭真、书记处书记王稼祥、候补书记刘澜涛、中央委员刘宁一以及中共中央机关负责工作人员李启新、张香山等人。
    连日来，耶斯佩森同志一行在北京参观了国营北京第二棉纺织厂、二龙路人民公社、故宫博物院、民族文化宫，并看了市容，游览了颐和园。耶斯佩森同志一行已在今天去外地参观访问。（附图片）
    朱德、邓小平宴请丹共主席克努德·耶斯佩森。右起第二人是克努德·耶斯佩森、第四人是克努德·耶斯佩森夫人。
        新华社记者　李长永摄
