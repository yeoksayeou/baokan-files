Title: CCP Delegation Arrives in Poland
Author:
Date: 1960-05-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, May 10th: The Chinese Communist Party delegation, headed by Bo Yibo, arrived in Warsaw by plane this evening for a visit at the invitation of the Polish United Workers' Party.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included: Kliszko, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party; Jarosinski, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee; Wierblan, Minister of the Science and Education Department of the Central Committee; Czesak, Minister of the Liaison Department of the Central Committee, and others.

Diplomatic officials from the Chinese Embassy in Poland were also present to welcome the delegation.

### 中共代表团抵达波兰

1960-05-12
第3版()
专栏：

    中共代表团抵达波兰
    新华社华沙10日电　以薄一波为首的中国共产党代表团，应波兰统一工人党的邀请，今晚乘飞机到华沙访问。
    到机场欢迎的有：波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记克利什科，中央书记处书记雅罗辛斯基，党中央科学教育部部长维尔布兰，党中央联络部长切萨克等。
    前往欢迎的还有我国驻波兰大使馆的外交官员。
