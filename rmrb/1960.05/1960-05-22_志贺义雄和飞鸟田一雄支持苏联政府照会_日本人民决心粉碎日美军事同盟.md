Title: Shiga Yoshio and Asukata Ichio Support Soviet Government Note: Japanese People Determined to Smash the Japan-U.S. Military Alliance
Author:
Date: 1960-05-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 21st: Yoshio Shiga, leader of the Japanese Communist Party's Diet caucus, stated today, "We must overthrow the Kishi Nobusuke government and dissolve the Diet in order to smash the newly signed (Japan-U.S.) military alliance treaty."
    Shiga Yoshio expressed support for the Soviet government's note to the Japanese government, urging the Japanese government to pay attention to the dangerous consequences caused by American bases in Japan. He said that from the perspective of international law, the Soviet note is justified, as international law stipulates respect for national sovereignty and territorial integrity.
    Shiga Yoshio then said, "The vast majority of the Japanese people are demanding the withdrawal of U-2 jet aircraft from Japan. Opposition parties have already requested the Diet to take measures to remove these aircraft. The people of Kanagawa Prefecture, where the Black Jet aircraft are stationed, held demonstrations demanding the withdrawal of U-2 jet aircraft from Japan."
    Shiga Yoshio pointed out Kishi Nobusuke's aggressive intentions. He said, "Kishi Nobusuke echoed (U.S. Secretary of State) Herter's words, saying that the U-2 jet aircraft in Japan are used for meteorological observations. Kishi Nobusuke even said that if the U-2 aircraft base in Japan is attacked, Article 5 of the new Japan-U.S. 'Security Treaty' will be invoked. These words of Kishi Nobusuke confirm his aggressive intentions."
    Ichio Asukata, a member of the Japanese Socialist Party in the Diet, also expressed support for the Soviet note. He said, "I cannot help but doubt what nationality Kishi Nobusuke belongs to, because he is so enthusiastic about defending the American spy jet."
    Asukata Ichio pointed out, "Under the rule of this government, which lacks independence, the Japanese people cannot live in peace."

### 志贺义雄和飞鸟田一雄支持苏联政府照会  日本人民决心粉碎日美军事同盟

1960-05-22
第4版()
专栏：

    志贺义雄和飞鸟田一雄支持苏联政府照会
    日本人民决心粉碎日美军事同盟
    新华社21日讯　东京消息：日本共产党国会党团领袖志贺义雄今天发表谈话强调说，“我们必须推翻岸信介政府，解散国会，以便粉碎刚签订的（日美）军事同盟条约。”
    志贺义雄表示支持苏联政府给日本政府的要日本政府注意日本的美国基地所引起的危险后果的照会。他说，从国际法的观点看来，苏联的照会是正当的，国际法规定尊重国家主权和领土完整。
    志贺义雄接着说，“绝大多数日本人民都要求U—2型喷气式飞机撤出日本。反对党已经要求国会采取措施使这种飞机撤走。黑色喷气式飞机驻扎的神奈川县人民举行了示威，要求U—2型喷气式飞机撤出日本。”
    志贺义雄指出了岸信介的侵略意图。他说，“岸信介附和（美国国务卿）赫脱的话说，在日本的U—2型喷气式飞机是用来进行气象观察的。岸信介甚至说，如果设在日本的U—2型飞机基地遭到攻击，将引用新日美‘安全条约’第五条。岸信介的这些话证实了他的侵略意图。”
    日本社会党国会议员飞鸟田一雄也表示支持苏联的照会。他说，“我不能不怀疑岸信介属于什么国籍，因为他很热心地为美国的间谍喷气式飞机辩解”。
    飞鸟田一雄指出，“在这种缺乏独立精神的政府统治下，日本人民不能安心生活。”
