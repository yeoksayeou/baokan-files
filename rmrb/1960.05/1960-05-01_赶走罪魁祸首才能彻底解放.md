Title: Only by Driving Away the Culprit Can We Achieve Complete Liberation
Author: 
Date: 1960-05-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 29th April: Two hundred thousand people from all walks of life in Harbin held a rally this afternoon to support the patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people.

Among those attending the meeting were Qiang Xiaochu and Wang Yilun, Secretaries of the Heilongjiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, leading figures from the Harbin municipal Party and government, leaders of local organizations of various democratic parties, and leaders of various people's organizations. Korean interns in Harbin also attended the meeting. Also attending were the Soviet Consul General in Harbin and all staff members of the consulate, as well as Soviet experts in Harbin.

The meeting began at 3 p.m. Ren Zhongyi, Alternate Secretary of the Heilongjiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and First Secretary of the Harbin Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, spoke at the meeting. He emphasized that in order to save its colonial rule in South Korea, U.S. imperialism is stepping up its interference in the patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people. The South Korean people have clearly seen that U.S. imperialism is the culprit that enslaves the South Korean people, and only by driving out U.S. imperialism can South Korea achieve complete liberation. On behalf of the people of Heilongjiang Province and Harbin City, Ren Zhongyi firmly supported the patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people, and firmly supported the appeal to the South Korean people in the letter from the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party to the South Korean people and the statement of the joint meeting of leaders of Korean political parties and social organizations, believing that these appeals expressed the wishes of all Korean patriots and pointed out the correct path for the struggle of the South Korean people. In his speech, Ren Zhongyi also expressed his firm support for the just struggle of the Japanese people against the Japan-U.S. military alliance treaty.

Subsequently, the heads of local organizations of various democratic parties and people's organizations, as well as representatives of workers, members of urban and suburban rural people's communes, and militia representatives, spoke at the meeting, unanimously expressing their firm support for the patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people.

Qiqihar, Jiamusi, Mudanjiang and other cities in Heilongjiang Province also held rallies this afternoon to support the patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people.

### 赶走罪魁祸首才能彻底解放

1960-05-01
第4版()
专栏：

    赶走罪魁祸首才能彻底解放
    哈尔滨
    新华社哈尔滨29日电　哈尔滨各界人民二十万人今天下午举行了支援南朝鲜人民爱国正义斗争的大会。 
    参加大会的有中共黑龙江省委书记处书记强晓初、王一伦，哈尔滨市的党政负责人、各民主党派地方组织和各人民团体的负责人。在哈尔滨的朝鲜实习生也参加了大会。参加大会的还有苏联驻哈尔滨总领事和领事馆全体工作人员，以及在哈尔滨的苏联专家。
    大会在下午三时开始。中共黑龙江省委书记处候补书记、中共哈尔滨市委第一书记任仲夷在会上讲了话。他强调指出，美帝国主义为了挽救它在南朝鲜的殖民统治，正在加紧干涉南朝鲜人民的爱国正义斗争。南朝鲜人民已经清楚地看到，美帝国主义是奴役南朝鲜人民的罪魁祸首，只有把美帝国主义赶出去，南朝鲜才能获得彻底解放。任仲夷代表黑龙江省和哈尔滨市人民，坚决支持南朝鲜人民的爱国正义斗争，坚决支持朝鲜劳动党中央委员会告南朝鲜人民书和朝鲜各政党、社会团体领袖联席会议声明中向南朝鲜人民发出的号召，认为这些号召表达了朝鲜一切爱国者的心愿，给南朝鲜人民的斗争指出了正确的道路。任仲夷在讲话中还表示坚决支持日本人民反对日美军事同盟条约的正义斗争。
    接着，各民主党派地方组织和各人民团体的负责人，以及工人、城市和郊区农村人民公社的社员、民兵代表等，都在会上讲了话，一致表示坚决支持南朝鲜人民的爱国正义斗争。
    黑龙江省齐齐哈尔、佳木斯、牡丹江等城市，今天下午都举行了支援南朝鲜人民爱国正义斗争大会。
