Title: Czechoslovakian Men's and Women's Archery Teams Hold Friendly Matches in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1960-05-24
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, May 23rd. The Czechoslovakian men's and women's archery teams held friendly matches with the Shanghai men's and women's archery teams in Shanghai on the 21st and 22nd. As a result, the visiting male athletes won the first and second places in the single-round archery all-around event, while Shanghai athletes won the third and fourth places; the visiting female athletes won the first and fourth places, while the second and third places were won by Shanghai athletes.
The Czechoslovakian men's and women's archery teams are scheduled to leave Shanghai on the 26th to return to Beijing, and then visit and compete in Hohhot.

### 捷男女射箭队在上海举行友谊赛

1960-05-24
第5版()
专栏：

    捷男女射箭队在上海举行友谊赛
    据新华社上海23日电　捷克斯洛伐克男、女射箭队21日和22日在上海同上海市男、女射箭队举行友谊比赛。结果，客队男选手获得单轮射箭全能的第一、二名，上海选手得第三、四名；客队女子选手获得第一名和第四名，第二、三名由上海选手获得。
    捷克斯洛伐克男、女射箭队预定在26日离上海回北京，然后到呼和浩特访问比赛。
