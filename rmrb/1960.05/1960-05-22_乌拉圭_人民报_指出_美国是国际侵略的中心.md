Title: Uruguayan "People's Paper" Points Out that the United States is the Center of International Aggression
Author:
Date: 1960-05-22
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 20th Dispatch: According to TASS, Uruguay's "People's Paper" stated in an editorial on the 18th entitled "American Conspiracies Sabotage the Summit Conference" that the failure of the summit conference "is a direct result of the provocative actions of the American militarist clique." The newspaper wrote, "The current arrogant and shameless leadership group in the United States remains the center of international aggression."

### 乌拉圭“人民报”指出  美国是国际侵略的中心

1960-05-22
第3版()
专栏：

    乌拉圭“人民报”指出
    美国是国际侵略的中心
    据新华社20日讯　据塔斯社消息：乌拉圭“人民报”18日在一篇题为“美国阴谋破坏最高级会议”的社论中指出，最高级会议的失败，“是美国军国主义集团挑衅行动的直接结果”。报纸写道，“美国现时的狂妄而无耻的领导集团，仍然是国际侵略的中心。”
