Title: Khrushchev-De Gaulle Talks; Eisenhower and Macmillan Arrive in Paris Separately; German Democratic Republic Submits Memorandum to Four Powers
Author:
Date: 1960-05-16
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, May 15th - According to TASS, Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Khrushchev held talks with French President De Gaulle at the Élysée Palace on May 15th.

    The Soviet side participating in the talks also included Foreign Minister Gromyko, Defense Minister Malinovsky, and Soviet Ambassador to France Vinogradov. The French side included Prime Minister Debré, Foreign Minister Couve de Murville, and French Ambassador to the Soviet Union Dejean.

    Xinhua News Agency, May 15th - U.S. President Eisenhower quietly left Washington last night and arrived in Paris today to attend the upcoming East-West summit.

    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, May 15th - British Prime Minister Macmillan arrived here today from London to attend the East-West summit scheduled for tomorrow.

    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, May 15th - On the eve of the East-West summit, frequent talks were held among Western diplomats here.

    U.S. Secretary of State Herter and British Foreign Secretary Lloyd, who have already arrived in Paris, held talks with French Foreign Minister Couve de Murville on May 14th. West German Foreign Minister Brentano also participated in the talks. According to a spokesperson for the meeting, the Western foreign ministers reiterated the Western countries' stubborn stance on West Berlin and the German question, "demanding the reunification of Germany on the basis of self-determination, while also maintaining the rights of the Allies in West Berlin and the interests of the people of West Berlin."

    West German Chancellor Adenauer also arrived in Paris on May 14th to participate in the four-power Western summit scheduled for tonight to finalize the West's strategy at the summit.

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, May 15th - According to TASS, the government of the German Democratic Republic has submitted to the governments of the Soviet Union, the United States, Great Britain, and France a "Memorandum of the Government of the German Democratic Republic on the Meeting of Heads of State and Government to be held in Paris in May 1960."

    The memorandum clarifies the views of the German Democratic Republic on the main issues to be discussed at the summit, which are: the question of complete disarmament, the question of preparing a peace treaty with the two Germanies, and the settlement of the West Berlin question.

### 赫鲁晓夫戴高乐会谈  艾森豪威尔和麦克米伦分别抵达巴黎  德意志民主共和国向四国提出备忘录

1960-05-16
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫戴高乐会谈
    艾森豪威尔和麦克米伦分别抵达巴黎
    德意志民主共和国向四国提出备忘录
    新华社15日讯　据塔斯社巴黎讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫15日在爱丽舍宫同法国总统戴高乐举行了会谈。
    苏联方面参加会谈的还有外交部长葛罗米柯、国防部长马利诺夫斯基和苏联驻法国大使维诺格拉多夫，法国方面参加会谈的有：总理德勃雷、外交部长德姆维尔和法国驻苏联大使德让。
    据新华社15日讯　美国总统艾森豪威尔昨天晚上悄悄地离开了华盛顿，并且在今天到达巴黎参加即将举行的东西方最高级会议。
    新华社巴黎15日电　英国首相麦克米伦今天从伦敦到达这里出席定明天举行东西方政府首脑会议。
    据新华社巴黎15日电　在东西方最高级会议举行前夕，这里的西方外交官之间进行了频繁的会谈。
    已经到达巴黎的美国国务卿赫脱和英国外交大臣劳埃德14日同法国外交部长德姆维尔举行了会谈，参加会谈的还有西德外交部长勃伦塔诺。据会议发言人后来宣布，西方外长在会上重申了西方国家对西柏林和德国问题的顽固立场，“要求在自决的基础上重新统一德国，同时还要保持盟国在西柏林的权利和西柏林人民的利益”。
    西德总理阿登纳14日也到达巴黎，参加定于今天晚上举行的最后制定西方在最高级会议上的策略的西方四国首脑会议。
    新华社15日讯　据塔斯社柏林15日讯：德意志民主共和国政府向苏联、美国、英国和法国政府提交了“德意志民主共和国政府关于1960年5月在巴黎举行的国家和政府领导人会议的备忘录”。
    备忘录阐明了德意志民主共和国对最高级会议要讨论的主要问题的观点，这些问题是：全面裁军问题、准备同两个德国缔结和约的问题和解决西柏林问题。
