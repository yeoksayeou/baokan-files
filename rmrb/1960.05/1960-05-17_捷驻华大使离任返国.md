Title: Czechoslovak Ambassador to China Departs for Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-05-17
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Bušek and his wife, left by plane this morning to return home at the end of his term.

	Yu Zhan, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department, went to the airport to see them off.

	Diplomatic envoys from various countries to China and diplomatic officials from the Czechoslovak Embassy in China also went to the airport to see them off.

### 捷驻华大使离任返国

1960-05-17
第7版()
专栏：

    捷驻华大使离任返国
    新华社16日讯　捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻中国大使布希尼亚克和夫人，今天上午乘飞机离任返国。
    前往机场送行的有外交部苏欧司副司长余湛和礼宾司副司长马振武。
    各国驻华使节和捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆的外交官员也到机场送行。
