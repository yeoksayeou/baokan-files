Title: Central Committee Leaders of the CCP Receive Visiting Delegations of Various Ethnic Groups
Author:
Date: 1960-05-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th May: Zhu De and Lin Biao, Vice Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary, He Long and Tan Zhenlin, members of the Political Bureau, and Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat, this afternoon received the visiting delegations of various ethnic groups from Inner Mongolia, Tibet, Guangxi, Sichuan, and Guizhou, who came to Beijing to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations, and the representatives attending the National Institute for Nationalities Presidents Conference.

### 中共中央领导人接见各民族参观团

1960-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人接见各民族参观团
    新华社7日讯　中共中央副主席朱德、林彪，总书记邓小平，政治局委员贺龙、谭震林，书记处书记王稼祥，今天下午接见了前来北京参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝活动的内蒙古、西藏、广西、四川、贵州的各民族参观团，和出席全国民族学院院长会议的代表。
