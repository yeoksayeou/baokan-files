Title: U.S. Drives Southeast Asia Group to Prepare for War; Military Advisors from Member States Meet in Washington to Plan Military Expansion
Author:
Date: 1960-05-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Washington dispatch: The military advisors of the member states of the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization, controlled by the United States, began a meeting in Washington on the 25th to discuss the issue of this aggressive military bloc intensifying the creation of tensions and further expanding armaments in the Far East.

According to reports, the specific issues to be discussed at this three-day meeting include: reviewing the so-called "improved operational plans" for this group to conduct "limited wars" in the Southeast Asian region, formulating plans for future joint military exercises, and studying the issue of further strengthening the "coordination" of the armed forces of the member states. In addition, the meeting will once again discuss the issue of intervening in the national democratic movements of Southeast Asian countries under the guise of opposing "subversive activities."

### 美国驱策东南亚集团备战  各成员国军事顾问在华盛顿开会策划扩军

1960-05-28
第5版()
专栏：

    美国驱策东南亚集团备战
    各成员国军事顾问在华盛顿开会策划扩军
    据新华社讯　华盛顿消息：美国控制的东南亚条约组织各成员国的军事顾问25日开始在华盛顿举行会议，讨论这个侵略性军事集团在远东加紧制造紧张局势和进一步扩充军备的问题。
    据报道，这个预定开三天的会议将要讨论的具体问题包括：审查这个集团在东南亚地区进行“有限战争”的所谓“改进了的作战计划”，拟订今后举行联合军事演习的方案以及研究进一步加强各成员国的武装部队的“配合”问题。此外，会议还要在所谓反对“颠覆活动”的幌子下再一次讨论干预东南亚国家的民族民主运动的问题。
