Title: "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" Published in Chile
Author:
Date: 1960-05-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Santiago, May 25th: According to the Chilean "El Siglo" newspaper, the Spanish translation of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong," based on the English translation, has recently been published here. The selection consists of two volumes, including Mao Zedong's major essays and speeches written between 1926 and 1945.

The publisher introduced the selection by saying, "This brilliant politician, philosopher, and outstanding military strategist led the Chinese people to victory after a long period of struggle."

### 智利出版“毛泽东选集”

1960-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

    智利出版“毛泽东选集”
    新华社圣地亚哥25日电　据智利“世纪报”报道：根据英译本转译成西班牙文的“毛泽东选集”不久前已在这里出版。选集共分两卷，包括毛泽东同志在1926年到1945年期间写的主要论文和演说稿。
    出版者介绍这个选集时说，“这位天才的政治家、哲学家和卓越的军事家领导中国人民经过长期的斗争，取得了胜利。”
