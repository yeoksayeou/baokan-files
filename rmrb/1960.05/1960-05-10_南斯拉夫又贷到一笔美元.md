Title: Yugoslavia Obtains Another Dollar Loan
Author:
Date: 1960-05-10
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, 8th. Yugoslavia has received another loan from the United States. According to reports in the newspapers here on the 7th, the U.S. Government's Development Loan Fund has granted Yugoslavia a loan of $14.8 million to purchase diesel locomotives for railway transportation. As is well known, Yugoslavia received a total of $51.5 million in loans from the United States last year.

### 南斯拉夫又贷到一笔美元

1960-05-10
第5版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫又贷到一笔美元
    新华社贝尔格莱德8日电　南斯拉夫又从美国得到一笔贷款。据7日这里的报纸报道，美国政府开发贷款基金会给南斯拉夫一千四百八十万美元的贷款，以购买用于铁路交通的柴油机车。众所周知，南斯拉夫去年从美国得到了总数达五千一百五十万美元的贷款。
