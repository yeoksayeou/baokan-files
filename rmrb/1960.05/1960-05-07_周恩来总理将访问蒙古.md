Title: Premier Zhou Enlai to Visit Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1960-05-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, will pay a friendly visit to the Mongolian People's Republic in late May at the invitation of Comrade Yu. Tsedenbal, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic.

### 周恩来总理将访问蒙古

1960-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理将访问蒙古
    新华社6日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志应蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席尤·泽登巴尔同志的邀请，将在5月下旬到蒙古人民共和国进行友好访问。
