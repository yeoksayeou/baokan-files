Title: All-China Women's Federation and Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association Host Banquet for Guests from Ten Latin American Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-05-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, May 18th: The All-China Women's Federation and the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association jointly held a banquet on the evening of May 17th to welcome women's delegations and representatives from ten countries—Brazil, Cuba, Colombia, Honduras, Martinique, Paraguay, Peru, Puerto Rico, Uruguay, and Venezuela—who came to China for a visit and to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations.

Li Dequan, Vice-President of the All-China Women's Federation, and Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, delivered speeches at the banquet, expressing the profound friendship and close unity between the Chinese people and women and the people and women of Latin America, and wishing the national democratic movements of the Latin American countries greater victories.

A. G. de Montoya from Colombia, representing all the foreign guests, delivered a speech expressing deep gratitude to the Chinese hosts for their warm hospitality and stated that upon returning home, they "will selflessly and enthusiastically fight for national independence and against imperialism and all oppressors."

The women's delegations and representatives from the ten Latin American countries left Beijing on May 18th to return to their countries.

### 全国妇联和中国拉丁美洲友协设宴  招待拉丁美洲十国客人

1960-05-19
第5版()
专栏：

    全国妇联和中国拉丁美洲友协设宴
    招待拉丁美洲十国客人
    据新华社18日讯　全国妇女联合会和中国拉丁美洲友好协会17日晚上联合举行宴会，招待前来我国参观访问和参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝活动的来自巴西、古巴、哥伦比亚、洪都拉斯、马提尼克、巴拉圭、秘鲁、波多黎各、乌拉圭、委内瑞拉十个国家的妇女代表团和代表。
    全国妇联副主席李德全和中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南先后在宴会上致词，表达中国人民和妇女同拉丁美洲人民和妇女之间的深厚友情和亲密团结，并祝愿拉丁美洲各国人民的民族民主运动取得更大胜利。
    来自哥伦比亚的阿·格·德蒙托亚，代表所有外宾致词，对中国主人的盛情招待表示深切的感谢，并表示回国以后，“将忘我热情地为争取民族独立、反对帝国主义和一切压迫者而斗争”。
    拉丁美洲十个国家的妇女代表团和代表已于18日离北京返国。
