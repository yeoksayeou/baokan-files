Title: Cambodian Regency Council Hosts Banquet for Premier Zhou; Cambodian Queen and Regency Council Receive Premier Zhou; Premier Zhou Calls on Prince Sihanouk and Prime Minister Pho Proeung
Author:
Date: 1960-05-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, May 5th: The Chairman of the Cambodian Regency Council, Prince Sisowath Monireth, held a banquet at 8:30 PM (local time) today to express a grand and warm welcome to Premier Zhou Enlai.

Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and several other members of Premier Zhou's entourage also attended the banquet.

Also present at the banquet were Regency Council members Zhang Qiang, Pitu de Monteiro, Speaker of the National Assembly Chuoei, Prime Minister Pho Proeung, First Deputy Prime Minister Nhiek Tioulong, Second Deputy Prime Minister Tep Phan, Commander-in-Chief of the Royal Armed Forces Lieutenant General Lon Nol, Director of the Royal Household Affairs Office Sang Yun, and other senior Cambodian officials.

Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia Wang Youping, Cambodian Ambassador to China Leng Ngeth, and diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed here also attended the banquet.

The banquet was held in a friendly and harmonious atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, May 5th: The Cambodian Queen and the Regency Council received Premier Zhou Enlai at the Throne Hall of the Royal Palace at 5:00 PM today.

Accompanying Premier Zhou were Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and other principal members of the entourage. Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia Wang Youping was also present at the reception.

Also present at the reception were: Chairman of the Cambodian Regency Council Prince Sisowath Monireth, the two members of the Regency Council, Zhang Qiang and Pitu de Monteiro, as well as Cambodian Second Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Tep Phan, Director of the Royal Household Affairs Office Sang Yun, and Cambodian Ambassador to Beijing Leng Ngeth.

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, May 5th: Premier Zhou Enlai visited Prince Sihanouk at his Chamkarmon Villa at 11:30 AM (local time) this morning.

Accompanying Premier Zhou Enlai were Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and other members of the entourage.

Also present at the meeting was Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia Wang Youping.

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, May 5th: Premier Zhou Enlai visited Cambodian Prime Minister Pho Proeung at the Cambodian Prime Minister's Office at 12:20 PM (local time) today.

Accompanying Premier Zhou Enlai were: Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and other members of the entourage.

Also present at the meeting was Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia Wang Youping.

On the Cambodian side were: Minister of State for National Education, Representative to the Prime Minister's Office Xie Zhenguo and other senior officials of the Cambodian Cabinet.

### 柬摄政委员会宴请周总理  柬埔寨王后和摄政委员会接见周总理  周总理拜会西哈努克亲王和福·波伦首相

1960-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

    柬摄政委员会宴请周总理
    柬埔寨王后和摄政委员会接见周总理
    周总理拜会西哈努克亲王和福·波伦首相
    新华社金边5日电　柬埔寨摄政委员会主席西索瓦特·莫尼勒亲王在今天二十点三十分（当地时间）举行宴会，对周恩来总理表示盛大的和热诚的欢迎。
    副总理陈毅，外交部副部长章汉夫和周总理的其它几位随行人员也出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的还有摄政委员会委员张强、皮图·德蒙特里奥，国民议会议长舒埃，首相福·波伦，第一副首相涅刁隆，第二副首相泰潘，皇家军队总司令朗诺少将，皇廷事务办公厅主任桑云和其它的柬埔寨高级官员。
    中国驻柬埔寨大使王幼平和柬埔寨驻中国大使兰·涅特以及各国驻这里的外交使节也出席了宴会。
    宴会是在友好和融洽的气氛中进行的。
    新华社金边5日电　柬埔寨王后和摄政委员会今天下午五时在国王御座宝殿接见了周恩来总理。
    陪同周总理的有副总理陈毅、外交部副部长章汉夫和其他的主要随行人员。接见时，中国驻柬埔寨大使王幼平也在座。
    接见时在座的还有：柬埔寨摄政委员会主席西索瓦特·莫尼勒亲王，摄政委员会的两位委员张强和皮图·德蒙特里奥，以及柬埔寨第二副首相兼外交大臣泰潘，皇廷事务办公厅主任桑云，和柬埔寨驻北京大使兰·涅特。
    新华社金边5日电　周恩来总理今天上午十一时三十分（当地时间）到西哈努克亲王的桑园别墅拜会了西哈努克亲王。
    陪同周恩来总理前往的有副总理陈毅、外交部副部长章汉夫和其他随行人员。
    拜会时在座的还有中国驻柬埔寨大使王幼平。
    新华社金边5日电　周恩来总理今天中午十二时二十分（当地时间）到柬埔寨首相府拜会了柬埔寨首相福·波伦。
    陪同周恩来总理前往的有：副总理陈毅、外交部副部长章汉夫和其他随行人员。
    拜会时在座的还有中国驻柬埔寨大使王幼平。
    柬埔寨方面在座的有：国民教育国务大臣、驻首相府代表谢振国和柬埔寨内阁的其他高级官员。
