Title: China-Latin America Friendship Association and Other Organizations Send Telegrams to Express Condolences and Concern to the People of Chile
Author:
Date: 1960-05-28
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, May 27th: Chu Tunan, President of the China-Latin America Friendship Association, and the All-China Women's Federation, the All-China Youth Federation, and the All-China Students' Federation, today sent telegrams to the Chile-China Cultural Association, the Chilean Women's League, the Chilean Communist Youth League, and the Chilean Student Union, respectively, to express condolences and concern to the people of Chile regarding the strong earthquake in Chile.

### 中国拉丁美洲友协等团体致电  向智利人民表示慰问和关怀

1960-05-28
第5版()
专栏：

    中国拉丁美洲友协等团体致电
    向智利人民表示慰问和关怀
    新华社27日讯　中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南和中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会、中华全国青年联合会、中华全国学生联合会，今天分别致电智利中国文化协会、智利妇女联盟、智利共产主义青年联盟和智利大学生联合会，对智利发生强烈地震向智利人民表示慰问和关怀。
