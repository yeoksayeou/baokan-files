Title: Khrushchev Receives Envoys from Socialist Countries to France
Author:
Date: 1960-05-18
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, May 17th, TASS dispatch: Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers Khrushchev received the diplomatic envoys to France from the people's democratic countries on May 16th: Albania—Ramani, Bulgaria—Kamenov, Hungary—Vincze, Poland—Gajewski, Romania—Bălănescu, Czechoslovakia—Pleško and held discussions with them regarding the Four Power Summit. Taking part in the conversation were: Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko, Soviet Defense Minister Malinovsky, Soviet Ambassador to France Vinogradov.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见社会主义国家驻法使节

1960-05-18
第2版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见社会主义国家驻法使节
    新华社17日讯　塔斯社巴黎16日讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫5月16日接见了人民民主国家驻法国的外交使节：阿尔巴尼亚——拉马尼、保加利亚——卡麦诺夫、匈牙利——温契、波兰——加耶夫斯基、罗马尼亚——巴拉奈斯库、捷克斯洛伐克——普勒斯科特，并且同他们就四国首脑会议问题进行了交谈。参加这次谈话的还有：苏联外交部长葛罗米柯，苏联国防部长马利诺夫斯基，苏联驻法大使维诺格拉多夫。
