Title: Iraqi Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-05-21
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, May 19th. The Iraqi Republic Government Trade Delegation, led by the Iraqi Minister of Commerce, Abdul Latif al-Shawaf, arrived in Beijing by plane today.
    Those who went to the airport to welcome them included the Minister of Foreign Trade, Ye Jiuzhuang, Vice Minister Lu Xuzhang, Vice President of the People's Bank of China, Qiao Peixin, and Deputy Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Gong Dafei.
    The Ambassador of the Republic of Iraq to China, Fadil, and others also went to the airport to greet them.

### 伊拉克政府贸易代表团到京

1960-05-21
第6版()
专栏：

    伊拉克政府贸易代表团到京
    新华社19日讯　伊拉克共和国政府贸易代表团一行四人在伊拉克商业部部长阿卜杜勒·拉蒂夫·沙瓦夫率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有我对外贸易部部长叶季壮，副部长卢绪章，中国人民银行副行长乔培新，外交部西亚非洲司副司长宫达非等人。
    伊拉克共和国驻华大使法迪尔等也到机场迎接。
