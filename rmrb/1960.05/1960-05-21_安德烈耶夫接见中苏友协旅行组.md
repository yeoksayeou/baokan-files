Title: Andreyev Receives Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Travel Group
Author:
Date: 1960-05-21
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 19th: Andreyev, Chairman of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Council, received the members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association activist professional travel group led by Chen Shaokuan, a representative of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, on the afternoon of the 18th.

### 安德烈耶夫接见中苏友协旅行组

1960-05-21
第6版()
专栏：

    安德烈耶夫接见中苏友协旅行组
    据新华社莫斯科19日电　苏中友协理事会主席安德烈耶夫18日下午接见了由中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会代表陈绍宽率领的中苏友协积极分子专业旅行组成员。
