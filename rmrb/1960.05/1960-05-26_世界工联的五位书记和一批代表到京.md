Title: The Five Secretaries of the World Federation of Trade Unions and a Group of Representatives Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-26
Page: 2
    Xinhua News Agency, May 25th. The Secretaries of the World Federation of Trade Unions, Grassi (Italy), Teodorescu (Romania), Marcel Braud (France), Sugiri (Indonesia), and Zacharia (Sudan), who will attend the World Federation of Trade Unions Council meeting in Beijing, as well as representatives from Burma, Portuguese Guinea, Ubangi-Shari in Equatorial Africa, Argentina, Chile, Colombia, and Peru, and 38 staff members of the World Federation of Trade Unions, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. Liu Changsheng, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Li Zaiwen, Secretary of the Secretariat, and Ma Chungu, China's representative to the World Federation of Trade Unions, welcomed them at the airport.

    Prior to this, council members and representatives and staff from Hungary, Mongolia, Indonesia, Ceylon, Iraq, Sudan, Cameroon, Madagascar, Costa Rica, and Australia had already arrived in Beijing.

### 世界工联的五位书记和一批代表到京

1960-05-26
第2版()
专栏：

    世界工联的五位书记和一批代表到京
    新华社25日讯　出席将在北京举行的世界工会联合会理事会议的世界工联书记葛拉西（意大利）、特奥多雷斯库（罗马尼亚）、马塞尔·布拉（法国）、苏吉里（印度尼西亚）、扎卡里亚（苏丹），以及缅甸、葡属几内亚、赤道非洲乌班吉沙立、阿根廷、智利、哥伦比亚、秘鲁的代表和世界工联工作人员三十八人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜、书记处书记栗再温、中国驻世界工联书记马纯古等。
    在这以前，已到达北京的有匈牙利、蒙古、印度尼西亚、锡兰、伊拉克、苏丹、喀麦隆、马达加斯加、哥斯达黎加、澳大利亚的理事和代表和工作人员。
