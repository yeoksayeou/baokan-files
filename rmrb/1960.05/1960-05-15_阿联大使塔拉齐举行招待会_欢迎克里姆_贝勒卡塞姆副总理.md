Title: 阿联大使塔拉齐举行招待会 欢迎克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆副总理 (UAE Ambassador Tarazi Holds Reception to Welcome Deputy Prime Minister Krim Belkacem)
Author:
Date: 1960-05-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. Arab Republic United Arab Republic Ambassador Tarazi held a reception at the embassy this afternoon to welcome the delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, headed by Deputy Prime Minister Krim Belkacem, to visit China.

Vice Premier He Long attended the reception and proposed a toast at the reception.

Ambassador Tarazi first delivered a speech at the reception. He said that the delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria represents the people of a self-respecting Arab country. He said that for six years, the Algerian people have stood firmly against the aggression of imperialism. The final victory will belong to the Algerian people who are fighting for the freedom and independence of their motherland. He proposed a toast to the Algerian people's struggle against imperialism and victory, to the friendship between the peoples of Arab countries and the Chinese people.

In his speech, Deputy Prime Minister Krim Belkacem talked about the Algerian people's nearly six years of unyielding struggle for freedom and national independence. He said that the attitude of the great Chinese people in resolutely supporting the Algerian people is in complete accordance with the Bandung Conference's principles on eliminating imperialism and implementing peaceful coexistence. He proposed a toast to the long live Asian-African solidarity, to the long live friendship between the Chinese people and the Arab people.

Vice Premier He Long proposed a toast, to the victory of the heroic Algerian people's struggle against French colonialism and for national independence, to the development of friendship between the Chinese and Arab peoples, to the unity of the Arab peoples, to the great unity of the Asian and African peoples and the people of the world, and to the continuous victory of the sacred cause of opposing imperialist war policies and safeguarding world peace!

The head of the diplomatic corps stationed in Beijing, Afghan Ambassador Samad, proposed a toast to the complete freedom and independence of the Algerian people.

Among those invited to attend the reception were Vice Premier of the State Council Luo Ruiqing, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Shen Junru and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Burhan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association Liu Changsheng, and people from all walks of life.

Envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries to China were also invited to attend the reception.

### 阿联大使塔拉齐举行招待会  欢迎克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆副总理

1960-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    阿联大使塔拉齐举行招待会
    欢迎克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆副总理
    新华社14日讯　阿拉伯联合共和国驻中国大使塔拉齐，为欢迎以克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆副总理为首的阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团访问中国，今天下午在大使馆举行招待会。
    贺龙副总理出席了招待会，并且在招待会上祝酒。
    塔拉齐大使首先在招待会上致词。他说，阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团代表着一个自尊的阿拉伯国家的人民。他说，六年以来，阿尔及利亚人民坚强地站在反对帝国主义的进攻的前面。最后的胜利将归于为争取祖国的自由和独立而进行斗争的阿尔及利亚人民的。他提议为阿尔及利亚人民对帝国主义的斗争和胜利，为阿拉伯各国人民之间和中国人民之间的友谊，干杯。
    克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆副总理在致词中谈到阿尔及利亚人民为了争取自由和民族独立，已经进行了将近六年的不屈不挠的斗争，他说，伟大的中国人民坚决支持阿尔及利亚人民的态度，是完全符合万隆会议关于消除帝国主义和实行和平共处的原则的。他提议为亚非团结万岁，为中国人民和阿拉伯人民之间的友谊万岁，干杯。
    贺龙副总理在祝酒时提议，为英勇的阿尔及利亚人民反对法国殖民主义、争取民族独立斗争的胜利，为中国人民和阿拉伯人民友谊的发展，为阿拉伯各国人民的团结，为亚非人民和全世界人民的大团结，为反对帝国主义战争政策，维护世界和平的神圣事业不断取得胜利，干杯！
    驻北京外交使团团长、阿富汗驻中国大使萨马德在祝酒中提议，为阿尔及利亚人民完全的自由和独立，干杯。
    应邀参加招待会的，还有国务院副总理罗瑞卿，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长沈钧儒、陈叔通，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，中国非洲人民友好协会会长刘长胜，以及各方面的人士。
    各国驻中国的使节和外交官员也应邀出席了招待会。
