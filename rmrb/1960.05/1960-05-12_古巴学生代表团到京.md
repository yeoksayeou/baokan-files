Title: Cuban Student Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 11th – A six-member Cuban student delegation, led by Luis Soto Andrade, Vice Secretary-General of the Cuban University Students Federation, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

### 古巴学生代表团到京

1960-05-12
第3版()
专栏：

    古巴学生代表团到京
    新华社11日讯　古巴学生代表团一行六人，在团长、古巴大学生联合会副总书记路易斯·索托·安得拉加率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
