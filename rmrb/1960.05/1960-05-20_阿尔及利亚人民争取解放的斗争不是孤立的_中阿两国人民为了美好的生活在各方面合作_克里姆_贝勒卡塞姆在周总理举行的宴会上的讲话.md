Title: The Algerian People's Struggle for Liberation is Not Isolated: The People of China and Algeria Cooperate in All Aspects for a Better Life - Krim Belkacem's Speech at the Banquet Hosted by Premier Zhou

Author: 
Date: 1960-05-20
Page: 3

Your Excellency Premier Zhou Enlai, Honorable Ministers, Dear Friends:

Our visit to your country is coming to an end. China is a great civilized country undergoing a socialist revolution.

Everything we have seen fills us with admiration. We have witnessed the achievements of the Great Leap Forward. These achievements in all areas are significant, and our time is limited, so we have only seen a small part. However, this is already enough to be a model for us to learn from, and it is also enough for imperialism to forever remember that the Chinese people, who have stood up and are working bravely and enthusiastically, are free and invincible. We thank the Chinese people for their deep concern for the Algerian war of liberation. The Algerian struggle is a struggle of a small country against a powerful enemy. But the struggle of the people of this small country is encouraged by the historically significant unity of the peoples of all countries against imperialism.

Our talks with the leaders of the powerful Chinese people were conducted entirely under the guidance of this spirit of unity. The Algerian people warmly thank the Chinese people for the practical assistance they have given them, and for the material sacrifices the Chinese people are currently making, so that their brothers, the Algerian people, can also live freely. The Algerian people will cherish the sacrifices made by the Chinese people for them. This help is precious to the Algerian people and proves that the Algerian people are not isolated in their struggle for liberation. Algeria will not forget its friends in combat and their support in today's fighting Algeria after independence. Happy days are coming, and we will cooperate in all aspects for the best life of our two peoples.

I wholeheartedly shout: Long live the great and glorious Chinese people! Long live our unity and friendship!

I propose that everyone raise their glasses to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, to the health of Premier Zhou Enlai, and to the health of all friends present today!

### 阿尔及利亚人民争取解放的斗争不是孤立的  中阿两国人民为了美好的生活在各方面合作  克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆在周总理举行的宴会上的讲话

1960-05-20
第3版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚人民争取解放的斗争不是孤立的
    中阿两国人民为了美好的生活在各方面合作
    克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆在周总理举行的宴会上的讲话国务院总理周恩来阁下，各位部长先生，亲爱的朋友们：
    我们在贵国的访问快要结束了。中国是一个文明的、正在进行社会主义革命的伟大国家。
    我们所看到的一切使我们非常钦羡。我们看到了大跃进的成就。这些各方面的成就很大，而我们的时间有限，所以我们看到的只是很少的一部分。但是，这已经足以成为我们学习的榜样，这也足以使得帝国主义永远记住：站立起来并勇敢而热情地从事劳动的中国人民，是自由的、不可战胜的。我们感谢中国人民对阿尔及利亚解放战争的深切关怀。阿尔及利亚的斗争是一场小国的人民对强大敌人进行的斗争。但是这个小国人民的斗争从反对帝国主义的各国人民的具有历史意义的团结中得到了鼓舞。
    我们和强大的中国人民的领导者所举行的会谈完全是在这种团结的精神指导下进行的。阿尔及利亚人民热烈感谢中国人民给予他们的切实的援助，感谢中国人民目前在物质上所作的牺牲，他们这样做是为了使他们兄弟的阿尔及利亚人民也能够自由地生活。阿尔及利亚人民将非常珍视中国人民为他们作出的牺牲。这种帮助对阿尔及利亚人民是珍贵的，而且证明，阿尔及利亚人民在争取解放的斗争中并不是孤立的。阿尔及利亚在获得独立后将不会忘记自己今天在战斗中的阿尔及利亚的朋友们以及他们的支持。幸福的日子即将到来，为了我们两国人民最美好的生活，我们将在各个方面进行合作。
    我衷心地高呼：伟大的光荣的中国人民万岁！我们的团结和友谊万岁！
    我建议大家举杯，为毛泽东主席的健康，为周恩来总理的健康，为今天到会各位朋友的健康干杯！
