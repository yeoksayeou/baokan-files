Title: Gomulka Receives Chinese Communist Party Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-05-24
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, May 22nd: Gomulka, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, received the Chinese Communist Party delegation headed by Bo Yibo today and had a cordial conversation with the delegation.

    Present at the meeting were members of the Political Bureau and Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, including Ochab and Kliszko, as well as members of the Political Bureau, Indrzejczyk and Loga-Sowiński, and Secretary of the Secretariat, Jaroszyński.

    Wang Bingnan, Chinese Ambassador to Poland, was also present.

### 哥穆尔卡接见中共代表团

1960-05-24
第5版()
专栏：

    哥穆尔卡接见中共代表团
    新华社华沙22日电　波兰统一工人党中央第一书记哥穆尔卡今天接见了以薄一波为首的中国共产党代表团，并同代表团进行了亲切的谈话。
    接见时在座的有波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员、书记处书记奥哈布、克利什科，中央政治局委员英德里霍夫斯基、洛加—索文斯基、中央书记处书记雅罗辛斯基等人。
    中国驻波兰大使王炳南也在座。
