Where Does the Fragrance Come From?

　　Essential oil is a type of natural fragrance, and the vast majority of it is extracted from fragrant plants. Fragrant plants all contain aromatic substances. Due to the different types of fragrant plants, the location of aromatic substances also varies. Some exist in the flowers, leaves, stems, roots, seeds, and kernels of plants, while others exist in the bark and peels of fruits. Aromatic substances existing in seeds and kernels (fruits) include coriander seeds, litsea cubeba, and peppercorns; those existing in leaves and stems include geranium, mint, and agastache; those existing in flowers include roses, jasmine, and tuberose; those existing in leaves include violets, white orchid leaves, and bitter orange leaves; those existing in rhizomes include vetiver, iris, and calamus; and those existing in tree bark and trunks include sandalwood, cypress, and sassafras. To identify fragrant plants, simply break or crush the leaves or bark of the plant. When the plant's oil cells are damaged, you can smell a fragrance.

　　Fragrances and perfumes have a wide range of uses and are indispensable raw materials for industries such as food, cosmetics, hygiene, and medicine. (Lu Liang)

### 哪里来的香味？
卢良
1960-05-31
第5版()
专栏：小常识

    哪里来的香味？
    芳香油是天然香料的一种，绝大部分都是从香料植物里提炼出来的。香料植物都含有芳香物质。由于香料植物种类不同，芳香物质存在的部位也不同。有的存在于植物的花、叶、茎、根、籽、仁里，有的存在于树皮和果皮之中。芳香物质存在于籽仁（果实）中的，有胡荽子、山苍子、花椒等；存在于叶茎中的，有香叶天竹葵、薄荷、藿香；存在于花朵中的，有玫瑰、茉莉、晚香玉等；存在于叶子里的，有紫罗兰、白兰叶、玳玳叶等；存在于根茎中的，有岩兰草、弋尾、菖蒲等；存在于树皮树干的，有檀香、柏木、黄樟等。要识别香料植物，只要把植物的叶或皮折断、弄碎，植物的油细胞受到破坏，就可以闻到一股香味。
    香料、香精的用途很广，它是食品、化妆品、卫生、医药等工业不可缺少的原料。（卢良）
