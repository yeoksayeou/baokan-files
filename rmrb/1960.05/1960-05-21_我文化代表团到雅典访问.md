Title: Chinese Cultural Delegation Visits Athens
Author:
Date: 1960-05-21
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Athens, May 20th: A Chinese cultural delegation led by Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries of China, arrived in Athens on the 16th.

　　Spilios Melas, Beata Kitsikis, and Dimitrios Papaspirou, responsible persons of the Greece-China Friendship Association, welcomed them at the airport. People from all walks of life in Greece who have visited China in recent years also welcomed them at the airport.

### 我文化代表团到雅典访问

1960-05-21
第6版()
专栏：

   我文化代表团到雅典访问
    新华社20日讯　雅典消息：由中国对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥率领的中国文化代表团已经在16日到达雅典。
    希腊—中国友好协会的负责人斯皮罗斯·梅拉斯、贝阿塔·基齐基斯和吉米特里奥斯·帕巴斯皮鲁到机场欢迎他们。到机场欢迎他们的还有在最近几年中访问过中国的希腊各界人士。
