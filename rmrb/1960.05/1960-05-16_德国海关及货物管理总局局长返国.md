Title: Director of the German Customs and Commodity Management Bureau Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-05-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, May 15th: Anton Ruh, Director of the Customs and Commodity Management Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign and Intra-German Trade of the German Democratic Republic, and his party of three, who were invited to visit China, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.

Lin Haiyun, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and Yin Zhiyue, Deputy Director of the General Administration of Customs of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, were at the airport to see them off.

Norbert, Commercial Counselor of the German Democratic Republic in China, was also at the airport to see them off.

### 德国海关及货物管理总局局长返国

1960-05-16
第5版()
专栏：

    德国海关及货物管理总局局长返国
    据新华社15日讯　应邀来我国参观访问的德意志民主共和国对外及东西德贸易部海关及货物管理总局局长安东·鲁等一行三人，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    前往机场欢送的，有对外贸易部副部长林海云、对外贸易部海关总署副署长殷之钺等。
    德意志民主共和国驻中国商务参赞诺伯特也到机场送行。
