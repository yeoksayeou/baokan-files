Title: Celebrating the 15th Anniversary of Czechoslovakia's Liberation, Many Cities in China Hold "Sino-Czech Friendship Week"
Author:
Date: 1960-05-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. To celebrate the great festival of the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia on May 9th, many important cities in China will launch "Sino-Czech Friendship Week" activities from May 5th to 11th to warmly celebrate this glorious festival of the Czechoslovak people.

During the "Sino-Czech Friendship Week," the working people of Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Kunming, Fushun, Harbin, Lanzhou, Xi'an and other cities will widely hold celebrations, get-togethers and other celebratory activities, and will also hold a Czechoslovak Film Week. The "Celebration Meeting for the 15th Anniversary of the Liberation of Czechoslovakia," sponsored by the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Sino-Czech Friendship Association, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Youth Federation, the All-China Women's Federation, and the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, will be grandly held in the capital on May 8th.

The Sino-Czech Friendship Farm in Huanghua County, Hebei Province, and some places where Czechoslovak experts work will also hold celebrations.

### 庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放十五周年  我国许多城市举行“中捷友好周”

1960-05-06
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放十五周年
    我国许多城市举行“中捷友好周”
    新华社4日讯　5月9日是捷克斯洛伐克解放十五周年的伟大节日，从5月5日到11日，我国许多重要城市将开展“中捷友好周”的活动，热烈庆祝捷克斯洛伐克人民的这一光辉节日。
    在“中捷友好周”期间，北京、上海、广州、昆明、抚顺、哈尔滨、兰州、西安等城市的劳动人民将广泛举行庆祝会、联欢会等庆祝活动，并将举行捷克斯洛伐克电影周。由对外文化联络委员会、中捷友好协会、全国总工会、全国青联、全国妇联、全国文联主办的“捷克斯洛伐克解放十五周年庆祝大会”将于5月8日在首都隆重举行。
    河北省黄骅县中捷友谊农场及一些有捷克斯洛伐克专家工作的地方也将举行庆祝活动。
