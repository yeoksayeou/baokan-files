Title: Pictures

Author: 
Date: 1960-05-15
Page: 4

The Chinese Communist Party delegation, headed by Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and the Vietnamese Labor Party delegation, headed by Ho Chi Minh, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party, held talks in Hanoi on May 12.
Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council, held a banquet at the Chinese Embassy in Vietnam on May 13 to thank Vietnam for its warm reception. The picture shows Chairman Ho Chi Minh and Premier Zhou Enlai at the banquet. All these photos were taken by Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian (faxed photos)

On May 12, Premier Zhou visited the Renzheng Township Agricultural Production Cooperative in the suburbs of Hanoi and was warmly welcomed by the farmers of the entire township. The picture shows farmers giving Premier Zhou bananas, pineapples, etc. that they produced.

Chairman Ho Chi Minh and Premier Zhou Enlai on the rostrum of the mass rally in Hanoi.

Right picture: Premier Zhou Enlai with primary school students when he visited Renzheng Township Primary School in the suburbs of Hanoi on May 12.

Bottom picture: Premier Zhou Enlai with students when he visited the Hanoi University of Technology on May 12.
