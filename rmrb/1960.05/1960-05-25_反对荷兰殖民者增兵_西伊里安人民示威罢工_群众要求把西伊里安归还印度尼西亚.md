Title: West Irian People Demonstrate and Strike Against Dutch Colonialists Increasing Troops; Masses Demand Return of West Irian to Indonesia
Author:
Date: 1960-05-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 20th. According to an Antara News Agency report from the Moluccas on the 20th, official sources from Dutch-occupied West Irian say that since the Netherlands attempted to strengthen its military forces in West Irian, the local people have launched a struggle against the Dutch sending more troops.

The struggle reached a climax in Serui, Sorong, Biak Island, and Merauke in West Irian in February and March of this year. People in these places held mass demonstrations and workers in Dutch oil companies and other enterprises went on strike to oppose the Dutch occupation of West Irian and demand the return of West Irian to Indonesia.

The report also revealed that the Dutch authorities are building airports in Biak Island, Merauke, and Sorong in an attempt to strengthen their military power in West Irian.

### 反对荷兰殖民者增兵  西伊里安人民示威罢工  群众要求把西伊里安归还印度尼西亚

1960-05-25
第4版()
专栏：

    反对荷兰殖民者增兵
    西伊里安人民示威罢工
    群众要求把西伊里安归还印度尼西亚
    新华社雅加达20日电　据20日安塔拉通讯社马鲁古消息，来自荷兰占领的西伊里安的官方消息说，自从荷兰企图加强它在西伊里安的军事力量以来，当地人民展开了反对荷兰派遣更多的军队的斗争。
    在西伊里安的塞鲁伊、梭弄、别克岛和马老奇，在今年2月和3月达到斗争的高潮。这些地方人民举行群众示威，在荷兰石油公司等企业的职工举行罢工等，来反对荷兰占领西伊里安，要求把西伊里安归还印度尼西亚。
    消息还透露说，荷兰当局正在修建在别克岛、马老奇和梭弄的飞机场，企图加强西伊里安的军事力量。
