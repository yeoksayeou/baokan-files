Author:
Date: 1960-05-30
Page: 1

Notice from the Comrade Lin Boqu Funeral Committee

    Comrade Lin Boqu's memorial hall is set up in the Palace of the Working People's Culture. Public mourning will be held from 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM on May 30, from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM on May 31, and from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM on June 1. A public memorial service will be held on June 2.

    The Funeral Committee's office is located in the Palace of the Working People's Culture. Telephone numbers: 39-6133, 39-6233.
