Title: China and Iraq Sign Trade and Payment Agreement
Author:
Date: 1960-05-26
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, May 25th. The governments of the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Iraq signed a trade and payment agreement between the two countries in Beijing this afternoon. Representing the government of the People's Republic of China was Ye Ji-zhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, and representing the government of the Republic of Iraq was Abdul Latif Shawwaf, head of the Iraqi government trade delegation and Minister of Commerce of the Republic of Iraq.

    Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, attended the signing ceremony. The Chinese side attending the signing ceremony included Lu Xuzhang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Qiao Peixin, Vice President of the People's Bank of China, Gong Dafei, Deputy Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others. The Iraqi side attending the signing ceremony included Fadil, the Iraqi Ambassador to China, and all members of the Iraqi government trade delegation.

### 中伊签订贸易和支付协定

1960-05-26
第2版()
专栏：

    中伊签订贸易和支付协定
    新华社25日讯　中华人民共和国政府和伊拉克共和国政府今天下午在北京签订了两国贸易和支付协定。代表中华人民共和国政府签字的是对外贸易部部长叶季壮，代表伊拉克共和国政府签字的是伊拉克政府贸易代表团团长、伊拉克共和国商业部部长阿卜杜勒·拉蒂夫·沙瓦夫。
    中华人民共和国国务院副总理李先念参加了签字仪式。中国方面参加签字仪式的，还有对外贸易部副部长卢绪章、中国人民银行副行长乔培新、外交部西亚非洲司副司长宫达非等。伊拉克方面参加签字仪式的，有伊拉克共和国驻华大使法迪尔和伊拉克政府贸易代表团的全体团员。
