Title: Discussing Matters Concerning the Presentation of Credentials: Polish New Ambassador to China, Knothe, Meets with Luo Guibo
Author:
Date: 1960-05-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Jerzy Knothe, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Polish People's Republic to China, met with Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of our country, this morning to discuss matters concerning the presentation of credentials.

Present at the meeting were Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Yu Zhan, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet Union and Eastern Europe.

### 商谈递交国书事宜  波兰新任驻华大使克诺泰会见罗贵波

1960-05-10
第6版()
专栏：

    商谈递交国书事宜
    波兰新任驻华大使克诺泰会见罗贵波
    新华社9日讯　波兰人民共和国新任驻中国特命全权大使耶日·克诺泰，今天上午会见了我国外交部副部长罗贵波，商谈递交国书事宜。
    会见时在座的有外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文，苏联东欧司副司长余湛。
