Title: Saitama Prefecture People Decide to Hold Demonstration
Author:
Date: 1960-05-27
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo: The "Saitama Prefecture People's Meeting to Prevent the Revision of the Security Treaty" decided on the 18th to hold a demonstration of thousands of people when US President Eisenhower arrives in this area to oppose his visit and to protest the US-Japan military alliance treaty to him.
    According to Eisenhower's schedule for visiting Japan, he will play golf at a club in Kasumigaseki, Kawagoe City, Saitama Prefecture on June 21. The people of Saitama Prefecture have decided to hold a demonstration near the golf course on that day.

### 埼玉县人民决定举行示威

1960-05-27
第3版()
专栏：

    埼玉县人民决定举行示威
    据新华社讯　东京消息：日本“埼玉县人民阻止修改安全条约会议”18日决定在美国总统艾森豪威尔到这个地方的时候，举行数千人的示威游行，反对他的访问，并就日美军事同盟条约向他提出抗议。
    按照艾森豪威尔访问日本的日程，他将在6月21日到埼玉县内川越市霞关的一个俱乐部里打高尔夫球。埼玉县人民决定那一天就在高尔夫球场附近举行示威游行。
