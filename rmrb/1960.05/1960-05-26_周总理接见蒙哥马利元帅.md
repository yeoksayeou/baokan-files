Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Field Marshal Montgomery
Author:
Date: 1960-05-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 25th: Premier Zhou Enlai received British Field Marshal Montgomery, who is currently visiting our country, this morning.

    Vice Premier He Long was present at the meeting.
    
Xinhua News Agency, May 25th: Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jiuzhuang received British Field Marshal Montgomery this afternoon.

Xinhua News Agency, May 25th: The British Chargé d'Affaires in China, Mr. Shipton, hosted a banquet at the Chargé d'Affaires' office this evening in honor of Field Marshal Montgomery's visit to China.

    Vice Premier He Long, Vice Premier of the State Council and Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, General Luo Ruiqing, and Vice Premier of the State Council Xi Zhongxun were invited to attend the banquet.

    Also present at the banquet were Vice Foreign Minister Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of National Defense General Wang Shusheng, Director of the All-China Sports Federation Li Da, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Lin Haiyun, and Vice Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, among others.

    Today, Field Marshal Montgomery also watched the parachute jumping performance and glider flying performance organized by the National Defense Sports Association, and visited the Beijing No. 2 Cotton Textile Mill. (Picture Attached)

### 周总理接见蒙哥马利元帅

1960-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见蒙哥马利元帅
    新华社25日讯　周恩来总理今天上午接见了正在我国访问的英国蒙哥马利元帅。
    接见时，国务院副总理贺龙元帅在座。
    新华社25日讯　对外贸易部部长叶季壮，今天下午接见了英国蒙哥马利元帅。
    新华社25日讯　英国驻中国代办施棣华为蒙哥马利元帅访问中国，今晚在代办处举行宴会。
    国务院副总理贺龙元帅，国务院副总理、中国人民解放军总参谋长罗瑞卿大将，国务院副总理习仲勋应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的，还有外交部副部长罗贵波，国防部副部长王树声大将，中国人民国防体育协会主任李达上将，对外贸易部副部长林海云，北京市副市长吴晗等。
    今天蒙哥马利元帅还观看了国防体育协会组织的跳伞表演和滑翔机飞行表演，参观了北京第二棉纺织厂。（附图片）
