Title: The Second National Assembly Election in Vietnam is Held Warmly, President Ho Chi Minh Elected as National Assembly Representative
Author:
Date: 1960-05-11
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 10th: The Democratic Republic of Vietnam held its second National Assembly election on May 8th.

According to Hanoi newspapers on May 10th: The work of electing representatives to the Second National Assembly in Hanoi, the capital of Vietnam, has been successfully concluded. The beloved leader of the Vietnamese people, President Ho Chi Minh, has been elected as a representative to the Second National Assembly.

Also elected as National Assembly representatives were Le Duan, Truong Chinh, Pham Hung, Hoang Quoc Viet, and twenty-five others.

This National Assembly election was conducted in an extremely warm atmosphere. On the 8th, Hanoi was decorated with lanterns and colorful flags hung all over the streets and alleys. At the crack of dawn, male and female voters dressed in festive attire eagerly came to the polling stations to vote.

In Hanoi, 380,238 voters participated in the election, accounting for 99.85% of the city's total voters. There were more than 200 polling stations, and 100% of the voters participated in the voting.

The election results were announced by each electoral unit on the evening of May 9th.

In Hai Phong, Thai Binh, Hai Ninh, and other places, the voter participation rate also reached 99% to 100% of all voters.

President Ho Chi Minh, the beloved leader of the Vietnamese people, voted at the Nguyen Khuyen School polling station in Hanoi's first constituency on the morning of the 8th. When President Ho arrived, the crowd waiting at the polling station burst into thunderous applause and cheers. They jumped and shouted "Long live President Ho!" "Long live President Ho!" again and again. The entire polling station was filled with endless joy.

President Ho presented his voter certificate to the election group, wrote down the candidates he approved of on the ballot, and then walked to the ballot box to cast the first vote at this polling station. Following him was Tran Danh Tuyen, Secretary of the Hanoi Municipal Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party.

Le Duan, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, Pham Van Dong, Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Vice-Premiers Truong Chinh, Pham Hung, and others also voted at their respective polling stations on the 8th.

### 越南第二届国会选举热烈举行  胡志明主席当选为国会代表

1960-05-11
第7版()
专栏：

    越南第二届国会选举热烈举行
    胡志明主席当选为国会代表
    据新华社河内10日电　越南民主共和国8日举行了第二届国会选举。
    据河内报纸10日报道：越南首都河内市选举第二届国会代表的工作已经胜利结束。越南人民的敬爱领袖胡志明主席已经当选为第二届国会代表。
    当选为国会代表的还有黎笋、长征、范雄、黄国越等二十九人。
    这次国会选举是在极为热烈的气氛中进行的。在8日，河内市到处张灯结彩，大街小巷挂满彩色缤纷的旗帜。天刚蒙蒙亮，穿着节日盛装的男女选民就踊跃来到投票站投票。
    在河内市，有三十八万零二百三十八名选民参加了选举，占全市选民总数的99.85％，有二百多个投票站，百分之百的选民参加了投票。
    各个选举单位在5月9日晚间公布了选举结果。
    海防、太平、海宁等地参加投票的选民也达到了全部选民的99％到100％。
    越南人民敬爱的领袖胡志明主席8日早晨到河内第一选区阮荐学校投票站投票。当胡主席到来时，伫候在投票站的群众响起了暴风雨般的掌声和欢呼声。他们跳跃着，一阵又一阵地高呼“胡主席万岁！”“胡主席万岁！”整个投票站笼罩着无尽的欢乐的气氛。
    胡主席向选举小组人员缴验了选民证，在选票上写下他同意的候选人，然后走向票箱投下这个投票站的第一票。随着他投票的是越南劳动党河内市委书记陈名宣。
    越南劳动党中央委员会政治局委员黎笋，越南民主共和国总理范文同，副总理长征、范雄等8日都在所属投票站投了票。
