Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Soviet Artists Performance Troupe, Ending Performances in Beijing and Heading to Other Regions for Visits
Author:
Date: 1960-05-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, May 20th: Vice Premier Chen Yi watched the Soviet Artists Performance Troupe's final performance in Beijing tonight at the Tianqiao Theater. During the intermission, he received the troupe's leader, Novikov, and the artists.

The troupe consists of fifteen members. During the cordial conversation with the Soviet artists, Vice Premier Chen Yi praised their efficient organization. Although small in number, their program was rich and wonderful, and their performance skills were excellent. Vice Premier Chen Yi said that this is something Chinese art workers should learn from.

The performance troupe will soon visit and perform in cities such as Zhengzhou, Shanghai, and Hangzhou.

### 陈毅副总理  接见苏联艺术家表演团表演团  结束在京演出将去各地访问

1960-05-21
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理
    接见苏联艺术家表演团表演团
    结束在京演出将去各地访问
    据新华社20日讯　苏联艺术家表演团今晚在天桥剧场举行在北京的最后一场演出。陈毅副总理观看了演出，并在演出休息时接见了表演团领队诺维科夫和艺术家们。
    这个表演团共由十五人组成。陈毅副总理和苏联艺术家们亲切谈话时，赞扬他们组织精干，人虽少但节目丰富精采，表演技巧很好。陈毅副总理说这方面值得中国艺术工作者们学习。
    表演团即将去郑州、上海、杭州等城市进行访问演出。
