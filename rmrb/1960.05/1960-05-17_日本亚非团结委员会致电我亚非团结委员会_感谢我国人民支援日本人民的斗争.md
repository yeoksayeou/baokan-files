Title: The Japanese Asian-African Solidarity Committee Sends a Telegram to Our Asian-African Solidarity Committee, Thanking Our People for Supporting the Japanese People's Struggle
Author:
Date: 1960-05-17
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 16th. The Japanese Asian-African Solidarity Committee sent a telegram to the Chinese Asian-African Solidarity Committee, thanking the Chinese people for the million-person rally held in Beijing to support the Japanese people's opposition to the Japan-US "Security Treaty." The telegram said: "At this decisive moment, it most powerfully encourages our struggle. Let us continue the struggle against our common enemy—US imperialism and Japanese militarism. Long live our eternal solidarity."

### 日本亚非团结委员会致电我亚非团结委员会  感谢我国人民支援日本人民的斗争

1960-05-17
第2版()
专栏：

    日本亚非团结委员会致电我亚非团结委员会
    感谢我国人民支援日本人民的斗争
    新华社16日讯　日本亚非团结委员会致电中国亚非团结委员会，感谢我国人民在北京举行的支援日本人民反对日美“安全条约”的一百万人的大会。电报里说：“在这决定性的时刻，它最有力地鼓舞我们的斗争。让我们继续进行反对我们的共同敌人——美帝国主义和日本军国主义的斗争。我们永恒的团结万岁。”
