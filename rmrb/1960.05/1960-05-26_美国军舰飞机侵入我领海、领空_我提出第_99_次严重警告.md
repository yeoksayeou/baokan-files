Title: American Warships and Aircraft Invade Our Territorial Waters and Airspace; I Issue the 99th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-05-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 25th: On May 24th, from 19:14 to 21:09, an American warship intruded into the waters of the Dongyin and Haitan areas of Fujian Province. From 01:12 to 02:14 on the 25th, another American warship again intruded into the aforementioned waters. From 09:57 to 10:10 on the 25th, an American military aircraft intruded into the airspace over the Shantou to Pinghai area of Guangdong Province. Regarding these military provocations by American warships and military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 99th serious warning.

### 美国军舰飞机侵入我领海、领空  我提出第99次严重警告

1960-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

    美国军舰飞机侵入我领海、领空
    我提出第99次严重警告
    新华社25日讯　5月24日十九时十四分至二十一时零九分，美国军舰一艘侵入我福建省东引、海坛地区海域。25日一时十二分至二时十四分，又有美国军舰一艘再次侵入我上述海域。25日九时五十七分至十时十分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省汕尾至平海地区领空。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅行为，我外交部发言人奉命提出第九十九次严重警告。
