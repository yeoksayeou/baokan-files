Title: President of Vietnam-China Friendship Association, Pei Xian, Dies; Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association Sends Condolences
Author:
Date: 1960-05-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency report: Mr. Pei Xian, member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front, President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, member of the Vietnamese Committee for the Defense of World Peace, and renowned Sinologist, unfortunately passed away at 5:00 PM on May 19th at the Vietnam-Soviet Friendship Hospital in Hanoi at the age of seventy-three.

The China-Vietnam Friendship Association sent a telegram to the Vietnam-China Friendship Association on the 20th to express condolences on the death of Pei Xian, President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association.

The telegram said: "Comrade Pei Xian dedicated his life to continuously developing the friendship between the people of China and Vietnam, made significant contributions, and we will forever remember him."

Yang Xiufeng, President of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association, sent a telegram to Pei Xian's family to express condolences.
