Title: Kazakh Men's Basketball Team Holds First Friendly Match in China
Author: 
Date: 1960-05-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 11th: The men's basketball team of the Kazakh Republic Sports Academy of the Soviet Union held its first friendly match in China tonight against the Beijing Youth Men's Basketball Team. The Beijing Youth Team won with a score of 58 to 56. At the end of the first half, the Kazakh team led with a score of 39 to 28.

Approximately six thousand spectators watched this intense match which was tied eight times. After watching the game, Vice Premier of the State Council He Long, Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, and others met with the athletes from both sides on the court and took pictures with them.

The Kazakh team displayed steady offensive and defensive skills tonight. They made full use of the stipulated thirty seconds of ball control time when attacking, taking steady steps and achieving a high success rate. Despite the Beijing Youth Team changing defensive tactics multiple times, they remained calm and methodical.

### 哈萨克男子篮球队  举行访华第一场友谊赛

1960-05-12
第3版()
专栏：

    哈萨克男子篮球队
    举行访华第一场友谊赛
    据新华社11日讯　苏联哈萨克共和国体育学院男子篮球队今晚同北京青年男子篮球队举行访华第一场友谊比赛，结果北京青年队以五十八比五十六获胜。上半场结束时哈萨克队曾以三十九比二十八领先。
    约六千名观众观看了这场曾经八次打成平局的激烈比赛。国务院副总理贺龙，北京市市长彭真等在观看了球赛后，曾到球场上接见了双方运动员，并且同他们在一起照了像。
    哈萨克队今晚表演了稳健的进攻和防守技巧。他们进攻时充分利用规定的三十秒的控制球时间，稳扎稳打，成功率很高。尽管北京青年队多次变换防守战术，他们也不慌不忙，打来头头是道。
