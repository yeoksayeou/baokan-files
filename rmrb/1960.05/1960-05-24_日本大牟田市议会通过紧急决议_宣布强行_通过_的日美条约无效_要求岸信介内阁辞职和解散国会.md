Title: Omuta City Council in Japan Passes Emergency Resolution: Declares Forced "Passage" of U.S.-Japan Treaty Invalid; Demands Resignation of Nobusuke Kishi Cabinet and Dissolution of the Diet
Author:
Date: 1960-05-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, May 23rd: According to today's "Akahata" report, the Omuta City Council in Japan held a special meeting on the 21st and passed an emergency resolution. The resolution declares that the Diet's House of Representatives' forced "passage" of the new U.S.-Japan "Security Treaty" and the decision to extend the Diet session are both invalid. It also demands that the Nobusuke Kishi Cabinet immediately step down and dissolve the Diet.

This is the first Japanese city council to take action demanding Kishi's resignation since Kishi forced the House of Representatives to "pass" the new "Security Treaty".

Omuta is a coal mining industrial center in Kyushu, Japan, and half of its citizens are coal miners.

### 日本大牟田市议会通过紧急决议  宣布强行“通过”的日美条约无效  要求岸信介内阁辞职和解散国会

1960-05-24
第4版()
专栏：

    日本大牟田市议会通过紧急决议  
    宣布强行“通过”的日美条约无效
    要求岸信介内阁辞职和解散国会
    新华社23日讯　东京消息：据“赤旗报”今天报道，日本大牟田市议会在21日举行临时会议，通过一项紧急决议，宣布国会众议院强行“通过”的新日美“安全条约”和延长国会会期的决定都是无效的，并且要求岸信介内阁立即下台，解散国会。
    这是岸信介强使众议院“通过”新“安全条约”以后采取要求岸信介下台的行动的第一个日本市议会。
    大牟田是日本九州的煤矿工业中心，市民的一半是煤矿工人。
