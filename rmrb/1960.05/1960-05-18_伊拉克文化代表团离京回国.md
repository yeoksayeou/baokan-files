Title: Iraqi Cultural Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-05-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency: The Iraqi cultural delegation led by Dr. Mahdi al-Matsumi left Beijing by plane on the morning of May 16 to return to their country.

### 伊拉克文化代表团离京回国

1960-05-18
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克文化代表团离京回国
    据新华社讯　以马赫迪·马特苏米博士为首的伊拉克文化代表团于16日上午乘飞机离北京返国。
