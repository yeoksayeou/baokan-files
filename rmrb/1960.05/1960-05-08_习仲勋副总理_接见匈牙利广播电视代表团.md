Title: Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun Receives Hungarian Radio and Television Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-05-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 7th -- Vice Premier of the State Council, Xi Zhongxun, received this afternoon the Hungarian Radio and Television Delegation headed by Laszlo Gacs, Chairman of the Hungarian Radio and Television Committee, and all members of the delegation, and had a cordial conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Mei Yi, Director of the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau, Jin Zhao, Deputy Director, Zuo Ying, Deputy Dean of the Broadcasting Institute, and others. Szigeti, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Hungarian Embassy in China, was also present.

### 习仲勋副总理  接见匈牙利广播电视代表团

1960-05-08
第5版()
专栏：

    习仲勋副总理
    接见匈牙利广播电视代表团
    新华社7日讯　国务院副总理习仲勋今天下午接见了匈牙利广播电视代表团团长、匈牙利广播与电视委员会主席拉斯罗·加奇和代表团全体团员，并且进行了亲切的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有广播事业局局长梅益，副局长金照，广播学院副院长左英等。匈牙利驻中国大使馆临时代办西格蒂也在座。
