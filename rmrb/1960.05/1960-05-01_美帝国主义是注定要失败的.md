Title: American Imperialism is Doomed to Fail

Author: 
Date: 1960-05-01
Page: 4

    Kunming
    Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 30th - People from all walks of life in Yunnan Province and Kunming City held a meeting of 50,000 people yesterday to support the patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people.
    Liu Zhuopu, Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Governor of Yunnan Province, spoke at the meeting on behalf of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China. He said that fifteen years of painful life have made the South Korean people increasingly aware that the colonial rule of US imperialism in South Korea is the root cause of all the disasters suffered by the South Korean people. The struggle of the South Korean people has dealt a heavy blow to US imperialism. All the aggressive and interventionist activities of US imperialism are doomed to failure. On behalf of the people of the province, he expressed his firm support for the patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people and his firm support for the struggle of the Korean people for the reunification of their motherland. He also expressed his firm support for the just struggle of the Japanese people against the Japan-US "Security Treaty".
    Subsequently, responsible persons of various people's organizations, representatives of local organizations of various democratic parties, and representatives of workers, peasants, and students spoke at the meeting. They unanimously expressed their solidarity with the Korean people and their firm support for the patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people.
    Workers, peasants, students, and members of urban people's communes from twenty-one ethnic groups, including Han, Yi, Dai, and Jingpo, participated in the meeting. Wu Wenxiang, the Vietnamese Consul in Kunming, also attended the meeting. A demonstration parade was held after the meeting.

### 美帝国主义是注定要失败的

1960-05-01
第4版()
专栏：

    美帝国主义是注定要失败的
    昆明
    新华社昆明30日电　云南省和昆明市各界人民五万人昨天举行大会，支援南朝鲜人民的爱国正义斗争。
    中共云南省委书记处书记、云南省副省长刘卓甫代表中共云南省委在大会上讲话。他说，十五年的惨痛的生活，使南朝鲜人民日益看清了美帝国主义对南朝鲜的殖民统治，是南朝鲜人民遭受一切灾难的根源。南朝鲜人民的斗争，给了美帝国主义以沉重的打击。美帝国主义的一切侵略和干涉活动，是注定要失败的。他代表全省人民表示坚决支持南朝鲜人民的爱国正义斗争，坚决支持朝鲜人民争取祖国统一的斗争。他还表示坚决支持日本人民反对日美“安全条约”的正义斗争。
    接着在大会上讲话的，有各人民团体的负责人，各民主党派地方组织的代表以及工人、农民、学生的代表等。他们一致表示和朝鲜人民站在一起，坚决支援南朝鲜人民的爱国正义斗争。
    参加大会的有汉、彝、傣、景颇等二十一个民族的工人、农民、学生和城市人民公社的社员。越南驻昆明领事武文享也参加了大会。会后举行了示威游行。
