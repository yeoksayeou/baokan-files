Title: Friends from Over Sixty Countries and Regions Celebrate "May Day" Together; State Council Holds Grand Banquet to Entertain Foreign Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-05-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 30th: The State Council of the People's Republic of China held a grand banquet in the banquet hall of the Great Hall of the People tonight to entertain foreign guests from over sixty countries and regions around the world. More than three thousand Chinese and foreign guests of all colors and speaking various languages gathered tonight to warmly celebrate the "May Day" International Labor Day.

Foreign guests from Asia, Africa, Latin America and other regions at the forefront of the anti-imperialist and anti-colonialist struggle were warmly welcomed at tonight's banquet. Vice Premier He Long praised in his speech that the people of Asia, Africa and Latin America have achieved a series of victories in the struggle against imperialism and colonialism, and the Chinese people have always regarded their struggle as our own and given firm support.

Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Song Qingling and Dong Biwu, Vice Chairmen of the People's Republic of China, Lin Biao, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and other party and state leaders attended tonight's banquet.

The banquet started at seven o'clock. Chairman Zhu De and others accompanied Algerian Deputy Prime Minister Krim Belkacem, Blas Roca, General Secretary of the Popular Socialist Party of Cuba, and Habib, Chairman of the Democratic Union of French Somaliland and former Prime Minister, and other distinguished guests into the banquet hall amidst joyful music. At this time, stormy applause rang out in the hall, warmly welcoming them.

After the banquet began, Vice Premier He Long delivered a speech, warmly extending festive congratulations to guests from various countries (full text to be published separately). His speech was warmly welcomed by the people, and stormy applause rang out throughout the venue.

He Long said that the cause of liberation of the working people and oppressed nations around the world, and the cause of the people of the world to strive for and defend world peace have made great progress. Now, we are witnessing a beautiful and bright prospect. As long as the people of the world further strengthen unity and continue to fight, we will surely win greater victories. He emphasized: The Chinese people have always firmly supported the patriotic and just struggle of the people of Asian, African and Latin American countries.

Seated at today's main table was Algerian Deputy Prime Minister Krim Belkacem, who had just arrived in Beijing before the banquet. When He Long pointed out in his speech that the heroic Algerian people had set a clear banner for the African people's struggle for liberation, the audience responded with warm applause.

During the banquet, leaders such as He Long, Vice Premier Li Fuchun, and Vice Chairman Peng Zhen went to the tables of the foreign guests to propose toasts, congratulating the further development of friendship between the Chinese people and the people of all countries, and congratulating the further strengthening of the unity of the people of all countries.

Also present at the banquet were: Vice Premiers of the State Council Bo Yibo, Luo Ruiqing, and Xi Zhongxun, Alternate Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Liu Lantao and Hu Qiaomu, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Lin Boqu, Luo Ronghuan, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Chen Shutong, Saifudin, Cheng Qian, He Xiangning, and Lin Feng. Heads of various departments in Beijing, representatives of many returned overseas Chinese, and representatives of ethnic minorities also attended the banquet.

### 同来自六十多个国家和地区的朋友共庆“五一”节  国务院举行盛宴招待外宾

1960-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

    同来自六十多个国家和地区的朋友共庆“五一”节
    国务院举行盛宴招待外宾
    贺龙副总理讲话说：当前形势无限好，世界人民反帝国主义斗争必胜，中国一贯坚决支持亚、非和拉丁美洲人民的正义斗争
    据新华社30日讯　中华人民共和国国务院今晚在人民大会堂宴会厅举行盛大宴会，招待来自世界各地六十多个国家和地区的外宾。三千多位有着各种肤色、操着各种语言的中外来宾，今晚欢聚一堂，热烈庆祝“五一”国际劳动节。
    来自反帝国主义、反殖民主义斗争前线的亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲等地区的外宾，在今晚宴会上，受到人们热烈的欢迎。贺龙副总理在讲话时赞扬说，亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲人民在反对帝国主义、反对殖民主义斗争中，已经取得了一系列的胜利，中国人民一向把他们的斗争看作是我们自己的斗争，并且给予坚决的支持。
    中共中央副主席、人大常委会委员长朱德，中华人民共和国副主席宋庆龄、董必武，中共中央副主席林彪，中共中央总书记邓小平等党和国家的领导人，出席了今晚的宴会。
    宴会在七时开始。朱德委员长等陪同阿尔及利亚副总理克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆，古巴人民社会党总书记布拉斯·罗加，法属非洲索马里兰民主联盟主席、前总理哈比等贵宾，在欢乐的乐曲声中走进宴会厅，这时大厅里响起暴风雨般的掌声，热烈欢迎他们。
    宴会开始后，贺龙副总理讲了话，他向各国来宾热烈地致节日的祝贺（全文另发）。他的讲话，受到人们热烈的欢迎，全场不断响起暴风雨般的掌声。
    贺龙说，全世界劳动人民和被压迫民族的解放事业，全世界人民争取和保卫世界和平的事业，有了巨大的发展。现在我们面前正展现出一片美好、光明的景象。只要全世界人民进一步加强团结，再接再厉进行斗争，我们就一定会赢得更大胜利。他强调地指出：中国人民一贯坚决支持亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲各国人民的爱国正义斗争。
    在今天的主宾席上，坐着在宴会前刚刚到达北京的阿尔及利亚副总理克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆。当贺龙在讲话中指出英勇的阿尔及利亚人民，为非洲人民争取解放的斗争树立了鲜明的旗帜的时候，全场报以热烈的掌声。
    在宴会进行过程中，贺龙、李富春副总理，彭真副委员长等领导人，分别到外宾们的席前敬酒，祝贺中国人民和各国人民的友谊进一步发展，祝贺世界各国人民的团结进一步加强。
    出席宴会的还有：国务院副总理薄一波、罗瑞卿、习仲勋，中共中央委员会书记处候补书记刘澜涛、胡乔木，人大常委会副委员长林伯渠、罗荣桓、沈钧儒、郭沫若、陈叔通、赛福鼎、程潜、何香凝、林枫。政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，国防委员会副主席张治中。
    出席宴会的还有党中央和国务院各部的部长、副部长，以及各人民团体的负责人，他们当中有刘宁一、廖承志、叶季壮、伍修权、罗贵波、刘长胜等。北京市各部门的负责人，许多归国华侨代表和少数民族的代表，也出席了宴会。
