Title: He Long Meets with Krim Belkacem
Author:
Date: 1960-05-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 14th: Vice Premier of the State Council He Long and the head of the delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Krim Belkacem, continued their talks this afternoon in a friendly atmosphere. The Chinese side participating in the talks included Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang, and others. The Algerian side participating in the talks included Minister of Armaments and Liaison Boussouf Abdelhafid, and Minister of Finance and Economic Affairs Francis Ahmed.

### 贺龙同克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆会谈

1960-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    贺龙同克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆会谈
    新华社14日讯　国务院副总理贺龙同阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团团长、副总理兼外交部长克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆，今天下午在友好气氛中继续举行会谈。中国方面参加会谈的有外交部副部长姬鹏飞，对外贸易部副部长李强等。阿尔及利亚方面参加会谈的有：军备和联络部部长布苏夫·阿卜杜勒哈菲德，财政和经济事务部部长弗朗西斯·艾哈迈德。
