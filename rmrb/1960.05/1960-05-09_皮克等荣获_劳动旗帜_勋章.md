Title: Pieck and Others Awarded "Banner of Labor" Medal
Author:
Date: 1960-05-09
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 8th. On the eve of the 15th anniversary of the German people's liberation from fascist enslavement, the Presidium of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic awarded the "Banner of Labor" medal to a number of individuals who immediately engaged in leadership work in the "League of Anti-Fascist Democratic Parties and Mass Organizations" after the liberation.
    Those awarded this medal include Pieck, Ulbricht, and Grotewohl.

### 皮克等荣获“劳动旗帜”勋章

1960-05-09
第6版()
专栏：

    皮克等荣获“劳动旗帜”勋章
    新华社柏林8日电　德意志民主共和国人民议院主席团在德国人民从法西斯奴役下解放十五周年前夕，向一些在解放之后立刻在“反法西斯民主党派和群众组织联盟”中从事领导工作的人物颁发了“劳动旗帜”勋章。
    获得这一勋章的有皮克、乌布利希和格罗提渥等。
