Title: Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun Receives Bulgarian-Chinese Friendship Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-05-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, May 27th. Vice Premier of the State Council, Xi Zhongxun, received all members of the Bulgarian-Chinese Friendship Delegation headed by Ivan Yotov this afternoon and had a cordial conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Nie Zhen, Vice President of the China-Bulgaria Friendship Association, and Luo Jun, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Panchevski, was also present.

### 习仲勋副总理接见保中友好代表团

1960-05-28
第5版()
专栏：

    习仲勋副总理接见保中友好代表团
    新华社27日讯　国务院副总理习仲勋今天下午接见了以伊万·约托夫为首的保加利亚中国友好代表团全体成员，并且进行了亲切的谈话。
    接见时在座的有中国保加利亚友好协会副会长聂真，对外文化联络委员会副主任罗俊等。
    保加利亚驻中国大使潘切夫斯基也在座。
