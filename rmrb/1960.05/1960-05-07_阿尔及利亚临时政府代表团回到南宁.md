Title: Algerian Provisional Government Delegation Returns to Nanning
Author:
Date: 1960-05-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, 6th May. The delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, led by Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Krim Belkacem, arrived in Nanning by special plane this afternoon after visiting the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, to continue its visit in our country.

　　When the plane carrying the distinguished guests slowly landed at Nanning Airport, which was adorned with the flags of China and Algeria, the distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by Lu Shaowu, Vice Chairman of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, Mo Naiqun, Vice Chairman of the Guangxi Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Li Tongwen, Deputy Secretary-General of the People's Committee of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region. Young Pioneers from Nanning presented flowers to the distinguished guests.

### 阿尔及利亚临时政府代表团回到南宁

1960-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚临时政府代表团回到南宁
    新华社南宁6日电　由克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆副总理兼外交部长率领的阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团，在访问越南民主共和国后，今天下午乘专机到达南宁，继续在我国访问。
    当载着贵宾的飞机徐徐降落在飘扬着中国和阿尔及利亚国旗的南宁机场时，贵宾们受到了广西僮族自治区副主席卢绍武、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广西分会副主席莫乃群、广西僮族自治区人民委员会副秘书长李同文等人的热烈欢迎。南宁市少先队员向贵宾们献了鲜花。
