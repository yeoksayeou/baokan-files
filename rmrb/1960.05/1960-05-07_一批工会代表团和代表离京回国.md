Title: A Batch of Trade Union Delegations and Representatives Leave Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1960-05-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. A delegation from the Cuban Workers' Confederation, and delegations from the Brazilian National Civil Servants' Union and the Brazilian National Bank Employees' Union, who were invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations and visit China, left Beijing by plane today to return home.

Prior to this, representatives of the Gabonese Trade Union Confederation and representatives of the Chadian Trade Union Confederation had visited various parts of China and participated in the "May Day" celebrations in Beijing, and had already left Beijing to return home.

### 一批工会代表团和代表离京回国

1960-05-07
第4版()
专栏：

    一批工会代表团和代表离京回国
    新华社6日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝活动和访问的古巴工人联合会代表团、巴西全国公务员工会和巴西全国银行职员联合会代表团，今天乘飞机离开北京返国。
    在此以前，加蓬工会联合会代表、乍得工会联合会代表在我国各地参观访问并在北京“五一”参加庆祝活动以后，已离京返国。
