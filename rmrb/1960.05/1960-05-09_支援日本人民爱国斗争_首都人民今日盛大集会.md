Title: Support the Patriotic Struggle of the Japanese People - Grand Rally Held by the People of the Capital Today
Author:
Date: 1960-05-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th - People from all walks of life in the capital will hold a grand rally on the 9th to support the Japanese people in their opposition to the Japan-U.S. military alliance treaty. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the live proceedings of the meeting from 2:50 PM on the 9th, using the frequency of its first program. Beijing Television will also broadcast it simultaneously.

### 支援日本人民爱国斗争  首都人民今日盛大集会

1960-05-09
第1版()
专栏：

    支援日本人民爱国斗争
    首都人民今日盛大集会
    新华社8日讯　首都各界人民将在9日举行盛大集会，支援日本人民反对日美军事同盟条约。中央人民广播电台将从9日下午二点五十分开始，用第一套节目的频率转播大会的实况。北京电视台也将同时转播。
