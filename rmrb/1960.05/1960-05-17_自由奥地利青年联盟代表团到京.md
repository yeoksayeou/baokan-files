Title: Delegation of the League of Free Austrian Youth Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-17
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, May 15th: At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, the delegation of the League of Free Austrian Youth, led by Karl Dontel, Chairman of the League of Free Austrian Youth, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The delegation consists of five members.

### 自由奥地利青年联盟代表团到京

1960-05-17
第7版()
专栏：

    自由奥地利青年联盟代表团到京
    据新华社15日讯　应共青团中央邀请来我国访问的自由奥地利青年联盟代表团一行五人，在团长、自由奥地利青年联盟主席卡尔·顿特尔率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
