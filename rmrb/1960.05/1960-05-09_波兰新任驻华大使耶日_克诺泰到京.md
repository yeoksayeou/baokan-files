Title: Polish New Ambassador to China, Jerzy Knothe, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-09
Page: 6

    Polish New Ambassador to China
    Jerzy Knothe Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, May 8th. Jerzy Knothe, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Polish People's Republic to China, arrived in Beijing by plane today. Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Yu Zhan, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs, were at the airport to welcome him.
    Fularato, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China, and embassy staff also welcomed him at the airport.

### 波兰新任驻华大使耶日·克诺泰到京

1960-05-09
第6版()
专栏：

    波兰新任驻华大使
    耶日·克诺泰到京
    新华社8日讯　波兰人民共和国新任驻中国特命全权大使耶日·克诺泰今天乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有：外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文，苏联东欧司副司长余湛。
    波兰驻中国大使馆临时代办傅拉托和使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
