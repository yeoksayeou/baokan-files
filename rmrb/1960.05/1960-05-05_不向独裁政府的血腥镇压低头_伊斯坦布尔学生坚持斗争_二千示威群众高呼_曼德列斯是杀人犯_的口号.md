Title: Refusing to Bow to the Dictatorial Government's Bloody Suppression, Istanbul Students Persist in Struggle, Two Thousand Demonstrators Chant "Menderes is a Murderer"
Author:
Date: 1960-05-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Istanbul dispatch: Students in Istanbul, a major city in western Turkey, defying the bloody suppression of the Menderes government, staged multiple demonstrations in the city again on the 3rd.

Approximately two thousand students marched on Istanbul's Independence Avenue, shouting slogans: "Freedom!" "Menderes is a murderer!" Students also demonstrated near Taksim Square in the city center. The army forcibly dispersed the student ranks and arrested some more students.

At 8:00 PM, a curfew was imposed throughout the city. Turkish soldiers patrolled the deserted streets in groups of three. Only the army's heavy tanks rumbled towards the old Dolmabahçe Palace, where Turkish Foreign Minister Zorlu, amidst an atmosphere of grimness, held a banquet for the foreign ministers of the North Atlantic aggressive bloc member states who were in Istanbul for a meeting.

### 不向独裁政府的血腥镇压低头  伊斯坦布尔学生坚持斗争  二千示威群众高呼“曼德列斯是杀人犯”的口号

1960-05-05
第5版()
专栏：

    不向独裁政府的血腥镇压低头
    伊斯坦布尔学生坚持斗争
    二千示威群众高呼“曼德列斯是杀人犯”的口号
    新华社4日讯　伊斯坦布尔消息：土耳其西部大城市伊斯坦布尔的学生不顾曼德列斯政府的血腥镇压，3日在这个城市又举行了多次示威游行。
    大约二千名学生在伊斯坦布尔的独立大街上游行，他们高呼口号：“自由！”“曼德列斯是杀人犯！”在市中心的塔克辛广场附近也有学生举行示威。军队强迫解散学生的队伍，并且又逮捕了一些学生。
    晚上八时，全市就开始实施戒严。土耳其军队三人一组，在阒无行人的街道上巡逻。只有军队的重型坦克隆隆驶往古老的多耳马巴契宫，那里土耳其外交部长佐鲁在一片肃杀的气氛中为到伊斯坦布尔开会的北大西洋侵略集团成员国的外交部长们举行了一个宴会。
