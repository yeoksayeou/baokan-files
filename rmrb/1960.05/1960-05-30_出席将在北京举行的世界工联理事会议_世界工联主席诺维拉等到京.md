Title: WFTU President Novella and Others Arrive in Beijing to Attend WFTU Executive Committee Meeting to Be Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 29th - Novella (Italy), President of the World Federation of Trade Unions (WFTU), Vice-Presidents Benoît Frachon (France), Herbert Warnke (German Democratic Republic), Dange (India), František Zupka (Czechoslovakia), Loga-Sowiński (Poland), and Enrique Pastorino (Uruguay) arrived in Beijing by plane today to attend the WFTU Executive Committee meeting to be held in Beijing. They were warmly welcomed by Liu Ningyi, President of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Vice-Presidents Liu Changsheng, Zhu Xuefan, Li Jiebo, and Ma Chungu, Secretary of the Chinese Mission to the WFTU.
    Diplomatic envoys and officials from relevant countries' embassies in China also welcomed them at the airport today.
    Board members and representatives from the Soviet Union, the German Democratic Republic, Romania, Bulgaria, Albania, France, Italy, and other countries also arrived in Beijing on the same plane.
    Prior to this, Vicente Lombardo Toledano (Mexico), Vice President of the WFTU, Pyotr Pimenov (USSR), and Pierre Gensous (France); as well as WFTU officials, representatives of the Trade Unions International of Public and Allied Employees, Trade Unions International of Miners, Trade Unions International of Workers in Commerce; Trade Unions International of Transport, Port and Fishery Workers, Trade Unions International of Textile Garment Leather and Fur Workers, Trade Unions International of Building Wood and Building Materials Industries, Trade Unions International of Agricultural Forestry Workers, Trade Unions International of Food Tobacco Hotel and Allied Industries Workers, Trade Unions International of Teachers,Trade Unions International of Chemical, Oil & Allied Workers, Trade Unions International of Metal & Engineering Industries Workers; board members and representatives from the Soviet Union, Bulgaria, Romania, Hungary, the Democratic People's Republic Of Korea, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Mongolia, the German Democratic Republic, Albania, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Iran, Lebanon, Ivory Coast, Cuba, Haiti, the Dominican Republic, Ecuador, Italy, Austria, Luxembourg, and Spain; and WFTU staff had already arrived in Beijing.

### 出席将在北京举行的世界工联理事会议  世界工联主席诺维拉等到京

1960-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

    出席将在北京举行的世界工联理事会议
    世界工联主席诺维拉等到京
    新华社29日讯　出席将在北京举行的世界工会联合会理事会议的世界工联主席诺维拉（意大利），副主席贝诺瓦·弗拉商（法国）、赫伯特·瓦恩克（德意志民主共和国）、丹吉（印度）、弗朗齐歇克·苏普卡（捷克斯洛伐克）、洛加—索文斯基（波兰）、恩里克·帕斯塔里诺（乌拉圭），今天乘飞机到达北京，受到中华全国总工会主席刘宁一，副主席刘长胜、朱学范、李颉伯，中国驻世界工联书记马纯古等的热烈欢迎。
    有关国家驻中国使节和外交官员，今天也到机场欢迎。
    同机到达北京的还有苏联、德意志民主共和国、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚、法国、意大利等国家的理事和代表。
    在这以前，已到达北京的有世界工联副主席维森特·隆巴多·托列达诺（墨西哥），世界工联书记别列金（苏联）、卡萨台（意大利）；以及世界工联公务员、公共事业与有关工人工会国际，矿业工人工会国际，商业工人工会国际，运输、港口与渔业工人工会国际，纺织、服装、皮革与皮毛工人工会国际，建筑、木材与建筑材料工人工会国际，农林工人工会国际，食品、烟草工业、旅馆与餐馆工人工会国际，教育工作者工会国际，化学、石油与有关工人工会国际，五金机器工人工会国际的代表；苏联、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、蒙古、德意志民主共和国、阿尔巴尼亚、越南民主共和国、伊朗、黎巴嫩、象牙海岸、古巴、海地、多米尼加、厄瓜多尔、意大利、奥地利、卢森堡、西班牙的理事和代表，以及世界工联工作人员。
