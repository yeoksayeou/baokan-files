Title: To Celebrate the 15th Anniversary of the Liberation of Czechoslovakia, Novotný Holds a Grand Reception
Author:
Date: 1960-05-11
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 9th. To celebrate the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia by the Soviet Army, Novotný, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and President of the Republic, held a grand reception in Prague Palace tonight.

    Attending the reception were Czechoslovak Communist Party and state leaders such as Fierlinger and Široký.

    The Soviet delegation led by Podgorny also attended the reception at invitation.

### 庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放十五周年  诺沃提尼举行盛大招待会

1960-05-11
第7版()
专栏：

    庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放十五周年
    诺沃提尼举行盛大招待会
    新华社布拉格9日电　捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会第一书记、共和国总统诺沃提尼今晚在布拉格宫举行盛大招待会，庆祝苏军解放捷克斯洛伐克十五周年。
    出席招待会的有费林格、西罗基等捷克斯洛伐克共产党和国家领导人。
    由波德戈尔内率领的苏联代表团也应邀出席了招待会。
