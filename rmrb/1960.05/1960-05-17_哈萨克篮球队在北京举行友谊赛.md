Title: Kazakh Basketball Team Holds Friendly Matches in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-17
Page: 7
　
    Combined reports from our newspaper: In a friendly match held tonight at the Beijing Gymnasium, the Chinese "Pioneer" Sports Association men's basketball team won against the Kazakh Republic Sports Institute men's basketball team with a score of 75 to 49.
    On the 12th, the Chinese People's Liberation Army "August 1st" men's basketball team defeated the Soviet Kazakh Republic Sports Institute men's basketball team with a score of 73 to 55. In the first half, the "August 1st" team led with a score of 37 to 27.
    On the 14th, the Soviet Kazakh Republic Sports Institute men's basketball team had a friendly match with the Beijing men's basketball team. As a result, the Beijing team won with a score of 67 to 47. [Beijing led] twelve to twenty. The visiting team is about to visit Tianjin, where it is scheduled to hold a friendly match with the Hebei men's basketball team on the 17th.

### 哈萨克篮球队在北京举行友谊赛

1960-05-17
第7版()
专栏：

    哈萨克篮球队在北京举行友谊赛
    本报综合报道　中国“前卫”体育协会男子篮球队今晚在北京体育馆举行的一场友谊比赛中，以七十五对四十九的比数赢了哈萨克共和国体育学院男子篮球队。
    12日，中国人民解放军“八一”男子篮球队以七十三比五十五战胜了苏联哈萨克共和国体育学院男子篮球队。上半场“八一”队以三十七比二十七领先。
    14日，苏联哈萨克共和国体育学院男子篮球队，曾同北京男子篮球队友谊比赛。结果，北京队以六十七比四十七获胜。十二比二十领先。客队即将去天津访问，预定17日在那里同河北男子篮球队举行友谊赛。
