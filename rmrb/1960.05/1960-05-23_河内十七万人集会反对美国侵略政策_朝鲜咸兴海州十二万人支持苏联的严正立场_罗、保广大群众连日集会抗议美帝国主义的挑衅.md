Title: 170,000 People in Hanoi Rally Against US Aggression Policy; 120,000 People in Hamhung and Haeju, Korea, Support the Soviet Union's Solemn Position; Broad Masses in Romania and Bulgaria Hold Daily Rallies to Protest US Imperialist Provocations
Author:
Date: 1960-05-23
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 22nd – 170,000 people from all walks of life in Hanoi held a large-scale rally this morning in Ba Dinh Square, strongly opposing US imperialism's sabotage of the Four-Power Summit.

The rally was organized by the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front, and the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace.

Prime Minister Pham Van Dong, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party Truong Chinh, Pham Hung, Hoang Van Hoan, Deputy Prime Minister Phan Ke Toai, Chairman of the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace Le Dinh Tham, and leaders of various mass organizations attended the rally. Soviet Ambassador to Vietnam Sokolov and diplomatic envoys from various socialist countries in Vietnam also attended the rally.

Early this morning, cadres, workers, suburban farmers, students, and residents flocked to Ba Dinh Square. They held up placards and chanted slogans such as: "Strongly oppose US imperialism's sabotage of the summit meeting!" "Warmly support the Soviet Union's resolute and correct stance against US imperialism's policy of war preparations!" "US imperialism get out of South Vietnam!"

A member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party delivered a report at the rally. After the rally, a large-scale demonstration was held.

Eisenhower is a Representative of the Warmongers

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 22nd – Newspapers here reported today: A total of 120,000 people in Hamhung and Haeju cities in northern Korea held rallies and demonstrations on the 21st, supporting the Soviet government's solemn position on the Four-Power Summit and condemning US imperialism for sabotaging the Four-Power Summit.

An 80,000-person rally was held in Hamhung City. Banners held by the masses read: "US imperialism cannot escape the serious responsibility for sabotaging the summit conference!" "US imperialism is the irreconcilable enemy of the Korean people!" "US imperialism get out of South Korea, Taiwan, South Vietnam, Turkey, and the Philippines!" and other slogans.

Kim Tae-ryong, Chairman of the Hamhung City Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, and representatives from all walks of life spoke at the meeting. They emphasized that the US imperialist aggression against the Soviet Union had undermined the Four-Power Summit, that US imperialism was the mastermind behind escalating international tensions and disrupting world peace, and that Eisenhower was a representative of the warmongers.

At the 40,000-person rally held in Haeju City, Han Young-il, Chairman of the Haeju City Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, and representatives from all walks of life strongly condemned US imperialism's crimes in sabotaging the Four-Power Summit and expressed support and solidarity with the Japanese people's struggle against the Kishi Nobusuke group.

US Aggression Proves It Is an Enemy of Peace

According to Xinhua News Agency, 22nd – The "Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej" Clothing Factory, the "August 23rd" Metallurgical Factory in Bucharest, the capital of Romania, and the "May 1st" Factory in Ploiesti City, all held mass rallies on the afternoon of the 21st, each attended by thousands of workers.

At the mass rally held at the "August 23rd" Metallurgical Factory, worker Iliana said that the US government had been prepared from the beginning to sabotage the great power summit, which proves that it is an enemy of peace. The resolution passed by the conference stated: "The Romanian people, united around the Romanian Workers' Party and the government, are determined to do everything possible to defend peace, heighten vigilance, and expose and repel any encroachment by the enemy."

In Bulgaria, many factories, villages, government agencies, schools, and neighborhoods have held mass rallies in recent days. In the Silistra region alone, more than 150 rallies were held on the 19th alone.

At these rallies, the working people of various places voiced a unanimous and just call: Resolutely protest the US imperialists, the culprits who sabotaged the Paris Four-Power Summit by sending military reconnaissance planes to invade the Soviet Union! Fully support the Soviet Union's just position against US aggression!

The employees of the Sliven "Dimitrov" Textile United Factory, one of Bulgaria's largest textile enterprises, unanimously passed a telegram to the Soviet Embassy in Bulgaria at their rally, angrily condemning the crimes of US imperialism.

### 河内十七万人集会反对美国侵略政策  朝鲜咸兴海州十二万人支持苏联的严正立场  罗、保广大群众连日集会抗议美帝国主义的挑衅

1960-05-23
第3版()
专栏：

    河内十七万人集会反对美国侵略政策
    朝鲜咸兴海州十二万人支持苏联的严正立场
    罗、保广大群众连日集会抗议美帝国主义的挑衅
    新华社河内22日电　河内各界十七万人，今天早晨在巴亭广场举行大规模的集会，强烈反对美帝国主义破坏四国首脑会议。
    这次大会是由越南劳动党中央委员会、越南祖国战线中央委员会、越南保卫世界和平委员会主持的。
    范文同总理，越南劳动党中央政治局委员长征、范雄、黄文欢，副总理潘继遂，越南保卫世界和平委员会主席黎廷探和各群众团体的负责人参加了大会。苏联驻越南大使苏科洛夫和各社会主义国家驻越南的外交使节也参加了大会。
    今天一清早，机关干部、工人、市郊农民、学生和居民涌向巴亭广场。他们举着标语牌，振臂高呼：“激烈反对美帝国主义破坏最高级会议！”“热烈支持苏联的坚决和正确的立场，反对美帝国主义的备战政策！”“美帝国主义滚出南越”。
    越南劳动党中央政治局委员在大会上作了报告。大会结束以后，举行了大规模的示威游行。
    艾森豪威尔是战争贩子的代表
    新华社平壤22日电　这里的报纸今天报道：朝鲜北部咸兴市和海州市21日共有十二万人举行大会和示威，支持苏联政府对四国首脑会议采取的严正立场，谴责美帝国主义破坏四国首脑会议。
    咸兴市举行了八万人的大会。群众举着的横幅上写着“美帝国主义逃避不了破坏首脑会议的严重责任！”“美帝国主义是朝鲜人民的不共戴天的敌人！”“美帝国主义从南朝鲜、台湾、南越、土耳其、菲律宾滚出去！”等标语口号。
    朝鲜劳动党咸兴市委员会委员长金泰龙和各界代表在会上讲了话。他们强调指出，美帝国主义对苏联进行的侵略行为破坏了四国首脑会议，美帝国主义是加剧国际紧张局势、扰乱世界和平的主谋者，艾森豪威尔是战争贩子的代表。
    在海州市举行的四万人的大会上，朝鲜劳动党海州市委员会委员长韩英一和各界代表在讲话中强烈谴责美帝国主义破坏四国首脑会议的罪行，并对日本人民反对岸信介集团的斗争表示支持和声援。美国的侵略行动证明它是和平的敌人
    据新华社22日讯　罗马尼亚首都布加勒斯特“乔治乌—德治”被服厂、“8月23日”冶金工厂和普罗什蒂城的“五一”工厂都在21日下午举行了各有数千名工人参加的群众大会。
    在“8月23日”冶金工厂举行的群众大会上，工人伊莱亚纳说，美国政府一开始就准备破坏大国首脑会谈，这证明它是和平的敌人。大会通过的决议说：“团结在罗马尼亚工人党和政府周围的罗马尼亚人民决心全力保卫和平，提高警惕，揭露和击退敌人的任何侵犯行动。”
    保加利亚的许多工厂、农村、机关、学校、街区连日来都举行了群众集会。单是西利斯特拉一个州在19日一天内就举行了一百五十多次集会。
    各地劳动人民在这些集会上发出了一致的正义呼声：坚决抗议破坏巴黎四国首脑会议的祸首——派军用侦察飞机侵犯苏联的美帝国主义者！完全支持苏联反对美国侵略的正义立场！
    保加利亚最大纺织企业之一——斯利文“季米特洛夫”纺织联合工厂的职工们在集会上还一致通过了打给苏联驻保加利亚大使馆的电报，愤怒谴责美帝国主义的罪行。
