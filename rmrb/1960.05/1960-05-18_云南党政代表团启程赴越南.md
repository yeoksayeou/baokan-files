Title: Yunnan Party and Government Delegation Departs for Vietnam
Author: 
Date: 1960-05-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming Telegram: The Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee dispatched a Yunnan Provincial Party and Government Delegation, which departed from Kunming by car on the afternoon of the 15th to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to participate in the celebrations for the fifth anniversary of the establishment of the Vietnamese Thai-Meo Autonomous Region.

### 云南党政代表团启程赴越南

1960-05-18
第5版()
专栏：

    云南党政代表团启程赴越南
    据新华社昆明电　中共云南省委员会、云南省人民委员会派出云南省党政代表团，于15日下午乘汽车离开昆明前往越南民主共和国，参加越南傣族—苗族自治区成立五周年庆祝活动。
