Title: Czechoslovakian Archery Teams Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-17
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, May 15th. The Czechoslovakian men's and women's archery teams, a group of six led by the Chairman of the Czechoslovakian Archery Association, Jiří Baštař, arrived in Beijing by plane today at our invitation to visit and compete in our country. The guest team is scheduled to hold their first friendly match in China with the Chinese People's Liberation Army men's and women's archery teams in Beijing on the 17th and 18th.

### 捷克斯洛伐克男女射箭队到京

1960-05-17
第7版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克男女射箭队到京
    据新华社15日讯　应邀来我国访问比赛的捷克斯洛伐克男女射箭队一行六人，今天由捷克斯洛伐克射箭协会主席伊里·巴什塔日率领乘飞机到达北京。客队预定17日和18日在北京同中国人民解放军男女射箭队举行访华第一次友谊比赛。
