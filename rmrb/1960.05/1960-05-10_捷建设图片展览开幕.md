Title: Czechoslovak Construction Picture Exhibition Opens
Author:
Date: 1960-05-10
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, May 9th: To celebrate the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia, the "Exhibition of Achievements in Socialist Construction of the Czechoslovak Republic over the Past Fifteen Years," sponsored by the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Sino-Czechoslovak Friendship Association, opened today at the Water Pavilion in Zhongshan Park, Beijing.

    Xia Yan, President of the Sino-Czechoslovak Friendship Association and Vice Minister of Culture, Geng Biao, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Luo Jun, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Feng Jiping, Vice Mayor of Beijing, and others attended the opening ceremony.

    Jan Bušniak, Ambassador of the Czechoslovak Republic to China, and embassy staff, Josef Obáfski, head of the Czechoslovak Friendship Delegation, and members of the delegation attended the opening ceremony. Diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries to China also attended the opening ceremony.

### 捷建设图片展览开幕

1960-05-10
第2版()
专栏：

    捷建设图片展览开幕
    据新华社9日讯　为庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放十五周年，由对外文化联络委员会和中捷友好协会主办的“捷克斯洛伐克共和国十五年社会主义建设成就图片展览”，今天在首都中山公园水榭举行开幕式。
    中捷友好协会会长、文化部副部长夏衍，外交部副部长耿飚，对外文化联络委员会副主任罗俊，北京市副市长冯基平等出席了开幕式。
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻中国大使扬·布希尼亚克和使馆工作人员，捷克斯洛伐克友好代表团团长约瑟夫·奥巴夫斯基和代表团成员出席了开幕式。出席开幕式的还有各国驻华使节和外交官员。
