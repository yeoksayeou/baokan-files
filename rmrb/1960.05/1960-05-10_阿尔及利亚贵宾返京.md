Title: Algerian Guests Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 9th: The delegation of the Provisional Government of Algeria, led by Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Krim Belkacem, returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon from Wuhan. They were accompanied by Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of China, and He Ying, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department. Those who greeted them at the airport included Li Qiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and He Gongkai, Deputy Director of the West Asia and Africa Department.

The delegation arrived in Wuhan by special plane from Guangzhou yesterday morning. In the afternoon, accompanied by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei and Zhang Tixue, Governor of Hubei Province, the delegation visited the Yangtze River Bridge and the Wuhan Iron and Steel Corporation in the rain. In the evening, Zhang Tixue hosted a banquet to entertain the distinguished guests, with Chen Yixin, Deputy Governor of Hubei Province, and Liu Huinong, Mayor of Wuhan, in attendance. At the banquet, Zhang Tixue, on behalf of the people of Hubei Province and Wuhan City, expressed a warm welcome to the Algerian guests. He said: The Chinese people have always firmly supported the Algerian people's revolutionary struggle for national liberation and firmly believe that the just struggle of the Algerian people will achieve final victory. Vice Premier Krim Belkacem spoke amidst warm applause. He first praised the industrial construction he saw in Wuhan, saying: The Chinese friends have shattered the superstition of imperialist technological superiority. The Algerian people, who have persisted in the national liberation struggle for six years, are watching with admiration the efforts of the 650 million Chinese people in socialist construction and in building a country that serves the people. He said: When we see the victory of the Chinese revolution, we are even more determined to liberate our motherland. The Algerian people are the fighting brothers of the great Chinese people, and we will always be united. After the banquet, the guests watched the large-scale revolutionary historical opera "Honghu Red Guards."

This morning, accompanied by Liu Huinong, Mayor of Wuhan, the guests visited a military school.

### 阿尔及利亚贵宾返京

1960-05-10
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚贵宾返京
    新华社9日讯　由克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆副总理兼外交部长率领的阿尔及利亚临时政府代表团，今天下午由武汉乘飞机回到北京。我国外交部副部长姬鹏飞、西亚非洲司司长何英陪同前来。到机场迎接的有对外贸易部副部长李强、外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文和西亚非洲司副司长何功楷等人。
    代表团是在昨天上午由广州乘专机到达武汉的。下午，代表团由外交部副部长姬鹏飞和湖北省省长张体学等人陪同，冒雨参观了长江大桥和武钢。晚上，张体学设宴招待贵宾，湖北省副省长陈一新、武汉市市长刘惠农出席作陪。张体学在宴会上代表湖北省和武汉市人民向阿尔及利亚贵宾表示热烈欢迎。他说：中国人民一贯坚决支持阿尔及利亚人民争取民族解放的革命斗争，并且深信阿尔及利亚人民的正义斗争一定会取得最后胜利。克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆副总理在热烈的掌声中讲话。他首先称赞在武汉所看到的工业建设，他说：中国朋友们已经摧毁了帝国主义技术优越的迷信，坚持了民族解放斗争六年的阿尔及利亚人民怀着敬佩的心情，注视着六亿五千万中国人民为社会主义建设、为建立一个服务于人民的国家所作的努力。他说：当我们看到中国革命的胜利，我们更决心要解放我们的祖国，阿尔及利亚人民是伟大中国人民的战斗兄弟，我们将永远团结一致。宴会后，贵宾们观看了大型革命历史歌剧“洪湖赤卫队”。
    今天上午，贵宾们在武汉市市长刘惠农陪同下，参观了一所军事学校。
