Title: Chinese Association for Science and Technology Delegation Visits Poland
Author:
Date: 1960-05-11
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 10th. At the invitation of Mr. Fritzehovsky, Chairman of the Polish Association for the Popularization of Knowledge, a three-member delegation from the Chinese Association for Science and Technology, led by Mr. Ding Xilin, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese Association for Science and Technology, left Beijing by plane this morning for Warsaw to attend the Third National Congress of the Polish Association for the Popularization of Knowledge, which will be held from May 13th to 15th.

### 我国科协代表团赴波兰

1960-05-11
第6版()
专栏：

    我国科协代表团赴波兰
    新华社10日讯　应波兰知识普及协会主席芙里采霍夫斯基的邀请，中国科学技术协会代表团一行三人，在中国科学技术协会副主席丁西林率领下，今晨由北京乘飞机前往华沙，参加波兰知识普及协会将在5月13日至15日举行的第三次全国代表大会。
