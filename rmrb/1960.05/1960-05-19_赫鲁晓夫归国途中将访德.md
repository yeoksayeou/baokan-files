Title: Khrushchev to Visit Germany on His Way Home
Author: 
Date: 1960-05-19
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, May 18th, TASS dispatch: According to reports from the German News Agency: The government of the German Democratic Republic has invited Soviet Premier Khrushchev to visit the German Democratic Republic on his way back from Paris.
    Khrushchev said in a conversation with reporters in Paris on May 17 that he would stop in Berlin on his way back to Moscow to meet with his friends Walter Ulbricht, Otto Grotewohl and others.

### 赫鲁晓夫归国途中将访德

1960-05-19
第2版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫归国途中将访德
    新华社18日讯　塔斯社柏林17日讯：据德意志通讯社报道：德意志民主共和国政府邀请苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫在他从巴黎回国的途中，到德意志民主共和国进行访问。
    赫鲁晓夫5月17日在巴黎同记者谈话时说，他在回莫斯科的时候将在柏林停留，以便同他的朋友瓦尔特·乌布利希、奥托·格罗提渥等人会晤。
