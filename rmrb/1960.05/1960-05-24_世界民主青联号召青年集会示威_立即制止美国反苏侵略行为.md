Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Calls on Youth to Assemble and Demonstrate: Immediately Stop US Anti-Soviet Aggressive Acts
Author:
Date: 1960-05-24
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, May 20th: The Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth held a press conference here on the 20th, issuing a statement and a letter to the youth of the world, condemning the US imperialist bloc for sabotaging the Four-Power Summit Conference.

    The statement emphasized that the US government and Eisenhower should be held responsible for the failure of the summit conference. The statement said that the World Federation of Democratic Youth, representing 87 million members, resolutely supports the position of Soviet youth and the Soviet government, and demands that such acts of aggression against the Soviet Union be stopped immediately. The World Federation of Democratic Youth calls on the youth of all countries to strengthen the struggle against imperialism and for peace and independence, and to protest against the current policies of the United States through gatherings, marches, and other means.

### 世界民主青联号召青年集会示威  立即制止美国反苏侵略行为

1960-05-24
第3版()
专栏：

    世界民主青联号召青年集会示威
    立即制止美国反苏侵略行为
    据新华社布达佩斯电　世界民主青年联盟书记处20日在这里举行的记者招待会上发表声明和告世界青年书，谴责美国帝国主义集团破坏四国首脑会议。
    声明强调指出，美国政府和艾森豪威尔应对首脑会议不能举行负责。声明说，世界民主青年联盟代表八千七百万会员，坚决支持苏联青年和苏联政府的立场，要求立即制止这种反对苏联的侵略行为。世界民主青年联盟号召各国青年加强反对帝国主义和争取和平与独立的斗争，用集会、游行等各种方式来抗议美国的现行政策。
