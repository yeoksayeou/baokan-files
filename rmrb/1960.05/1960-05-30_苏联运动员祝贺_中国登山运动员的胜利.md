Title: Soviet Athletes Congratulate Chinese Mountaineers on Their Victory
Author:
Date: 1960-05-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 28th. TASS reports: Abalkov, a well-known Soviet mountaineer and old friend of Chinese mountaineers, wrote an article in "Soviet Russia" today congratulating the Chinese mountaineering team on their heroic feat of successfully ascending Mount Everest from the north slope. He said that all Soviet athletes are sincerely happy for the outstanding achievements of their Chinese brothers.

He pointed out that Chinese mountaineering has only been in existence for five years and has already solved the most complex problem of climbing Mount Everest, which is extremely remarkable. He wrote that the love of labor led the Chinese athletes to victory.

### 苏联运动员祝贺  中国登山运动员的胜利

1960-05-30
第2版()
专栏：

    苏联运动员祝贺
    中国登山运动员的胜利
    新华社莫斯科28日电　据塔斯社报道：中国登山运动员的老朋友苏联著名的登山运动员阿巴拉科夫今天在“苏维埃俄罗斯报”上撰文祝贺中国登山队胜利地从北坡登上了珠穆朗玛峰的壮举。他说全体苏联运动员都衷心地为中国兄弟们所获得的卓越成就而高兴。
    他指出，中国登山运动总共只有五年的历史，就已经一举解决了像攀上珠穆朗玛高峰这样最为复杂的难题，这是极为了不起的事。他写道，对劳动的热爱使中国运动员取得了胜利。
