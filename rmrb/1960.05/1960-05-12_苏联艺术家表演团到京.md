Title: Soviet Artists Performing Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-12
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 11th: The Soviet Artists Performing Troupe, a group of fifteen people led by A.G. Novikov, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a one-and-a-half-month visit and performance tour in China, according to the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

The Soviet Artists Performing Troupe is composed of outstanding musicians, dancers, and soloists from various ethnic groups in the Soviet Union. Among them is Lidia Krubinnina, People's Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, who visited China with the ballet troupe of the Novosibirsk Opera and Ballet Theatre in 1957. There are also three Honored Artists from the Russian, Uzbek, and Kyrgyz Republics, as well as some international competition winners.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Luo Jun, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Ge Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, as well as Tie Dietelin, First Secretary of the Soviet Embassy in China, Ablova, Second Secretary, and others.

### 苏联艺术家表演团到京

1960-05-12
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联艺术家表演团到京
    新华社11日讯　根据中苏文化合作协定1960年执行计划前来我国作一个半月访问演出的苏联艺术家表演团一行十五人，在阿·格·诺维科夫率领下今天乘飞机到达北京。
    苏联艺术家表演团是由苏联各民族优秀的音乐、舞蹈、独奏等演员组成的，其中有1957年随新西伯利亚歌剧芭蕾舞剧院芭蕾舞团来中国访问演出的俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国人民演员李吉娅·克鲁宾妮娜，还有俄罗斯、乌兹别克、吉尔吉斯共和国的三位功勋演员以及一些国际比赛获奖者。
    前往机场迎接的有对外文化联络委员会副主任罗俊，中苏友好协会总会副秘书长戈宝权，以及苏联驻中国大使馆一等秘书铁迭林、二等秘书阿布洛娃等。
