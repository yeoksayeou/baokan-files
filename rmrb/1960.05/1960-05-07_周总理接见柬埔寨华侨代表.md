Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Representatives of Overseas Chinese in Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1960-05-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, May 6th: Premier Zhou Enlai received two hundred representatives of overseas Chinese here at the Chinese Embassy at 5:30 pm today (local time), and took pictures with them.

Accompanying Premier Zhou Enlai in receiving the representatives of overseas Chinese were Vice Premier Chen Yi, Deputy Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, and Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia Wang Youping.

### 周总理接见柬埔寨华侨代表

1960-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见柬埔寨华侨代表
    新华社金边6日电　周恩来总理今天下午五点三十分（当地时间）在这里的中国大使馆接见了两百名华侨代表，并且同他们一起照了像。
    陪同周恩来总理接见华侨代表的，有副总理陈毅，外交部副部长章汉夫和中国驻柬埔寨大使王幼平。
