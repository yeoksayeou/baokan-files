Title: Indian "Blitz" Weekly Reveals: US Planes Have Been Conducting Espionage Activities in Chinese Areas Adjacent to India

Author:

Date: 1960-05-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 14th. The "Blitz" weekly, published today, carried a news item revealing that in recent months, American spy planes have been flying over the Sino-Indian border and the skies of Nepal, Bhutan, and Sikkim.

The news said that the extra-parliamentary groups of the People's Socialist Party, the Freedom Party, and the Indian People's League had raised a series of questions in parliament, trying hard to attribute these airspace violations to China. The news said, "Now authoritative sources have revealed that all these airspace violations are part of American espionage activities."

The news said, "According to these sources, the main purpose of these violations of Indian airspace is not to provoke India, but to conduct espionage activities over the Chinese areas bordering India."

The news also said that the number of these spy flights increased last year, especially during and after the Tibetan rebellion.

### 印度“闪电”周报揭露  美机一直在毗邻印度的中国地区进行间谍活动

1960-05-15
第3版()
专栏：

    印度“闪电”周报揭露
    美机一直在毗邻印度的中国地区进行间谍活动
    据新华社新德里14日电　今天出版的“闪电”周报刊载的一则消息揭露，最近几个月以来，美国间谍飞机一直在中印边境和尼泊尔、不丹、锡金上空飞行。
    消息说，人民社会党、自由党、印度人民同盟的院外集团在议会提出一系列问题竭力企图把这些侵犯领空的事件说成是由中国引起的，消息说，“现在权威人士已透露，所有这些侵犯领空的行动都是美国间谍活动的一部分。”
    消息说，“据这些人士认为，这几次侵犯印度领空的主要目的不是为了向印度挑衅，而是在同印度接壤的中国地区的上空进行间谍活动。”
    消息又说，这些间谍飞行的次数去年有所增加，特别是在西藏叛乱期间和叛乱以后情况更是如此。
