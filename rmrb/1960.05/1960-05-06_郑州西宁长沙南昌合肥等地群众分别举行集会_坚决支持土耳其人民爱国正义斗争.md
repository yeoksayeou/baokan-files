Title: Mass rallies held in Zhengzhou, Xining, Changsha, Nanchang, Hefei and other places to resolutely support the Turkish people's patriotic and just struggle
Author:
Date: 1960-05-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, May 5th. Youth and people in Zhengzhou, Xining, Changsha, Nanchang, Hefei, Chengdu, Hangzhou and other places held grand rallies yesterday to support the patriotic and just struggle of the Turkish people.
    More than 1,700 young people and people from all walks of life in Zhengzhou City held a rally. Zhao Fengqi, secretary of the Henan Provincial Committee of the Communist Youth League, spoke at the meeting. He pointed out that the current situation in Turkey is similar to the situation in our country during the "May Fourth" Movement forty-one years ago. At that time, the forces of imperialism and feudalism were very strong, but they were finally crushed by the awakened masses. All imperialists and reactionary governments will suffer the same fate. At a rally held by more than 1,000 young people and representatives from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Xining City, Liu Libin, secretary of the Qinghai Provincial Committee of the Communist Youth League, pointed out that the Turkish people's struggle against U.S. imperialism and its running dog Menderes' dictatorial rule is another heavy blow to U.S. imperialism's policy of aggression and war. It once again proves the truth that no matter what cruel suppression and deception methods U.S. imperialism and its running dogs use, the struggle of all oppressed nations and peoples for national independence and democracy cannot be stopped. Two thousand six hundred young people and people from all walks of life in Changsha City held a rally. Zhang Delong, secretary of the Hunan Provincial Committee of the Communist Youth League, spoke at the meeting, expressing firm support for the patriotic and just struggle of the Turkish people. More than 3,000 young people in Nanchang City held a rally. Ren Siyi, deputy secretary of the Nanchang Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, stated in his speech at the meeting that the youth of Jiangxi, with their glorious revolutionary tradition, firmly stand on the side of the Turkish people. Representatives of trade unions, women's federations, democratic parties, people's organizations, workers, peasants, and students in these places also spoke at the meeting, expressing firm support for the heroic struggle of the Turkish people against U.S. imperialism and its running dog Menderes reactionary clique. More than 1,500 people in Hefei City held a rally. Ding Hao, secretary of the Anhui Provincial Committee of the Communist Youth League, delivered a speech at the meeting, resolutely supporting the patriotic and just struggle of the Turkish people. At a rally held by more than 1,600 young people from all walks of life in Chengdu City, Bai Shangwu, deputy secretary of the Sichuan Provincial Committee of the Communist Youth League, spoke. At a rally held by more than 2,000 people in Hangzhou City, Li Peizeng, deputy secretary of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Communist Youth League, spoke.

### 郑州西宁长沙南昌合肥等地群众分别举行集会  坚决支持土耳其人民爱国正义斗争

1960-05-06
第4版()
专栏：

    郑州西宁长沙南昌合肥等地群众分别举行集会
    坚决支持土耳其人民爱国正义斗争
    新华社5日讯　郑州、西宁、长沙、南昌、合肥、成都、杭州等地的青年和群众昨天举行了盛大集会，支持土耳其人民的爱国正义斗争。
    郑州市青年和各界人民一千七百多人举行了集会。共青团河南省委书记赵凤岐在会上讲了话，他指出：当前的土耳其情况正和四十一年前我国“五四”运动时的情况相似，那时帝国主义和封建势力的力量很强大，但终于为觉醒了的人民群众所摧垮。一切帝国主义和反动政府都要遭到同样的命运。在西宁市一千多名青年和各族各界人民代表举行的集会上，共青团青海省委书记刘力彬指出，土耳其人民反对美帝国主义及其走狗曼德列斯独裁统治的斗争，是对美帝国主义侵略政策和战争政策的又一沉重打击。它再一次地证明了这一个真理：无论美帝国主义和他的走狗们使用什么残酷的镇压和欺骗手段，一切被压迫民族人民争取民族独立和民主的斗争，都是阻挡不住的。长沙市有二千六百名青年和各界人民举行了集会。共青团湖南省委书记张德隆在会上讲话，表示坚决支持土耳其人民的爱国正义斗争。南昌市有三千多名青年举行了集会，共青团南昌市委副书记任斯贻在大会上的讲话中表示，富有光荣革命传统的江西青年，坚决站在土耳其人民一边。这些地方的工会、妇联和各民主党派、各人民团体以及工人、农民、学生的代表也都在会上讲话，表示坚决支持土耳其人民反对美帝国主义及其走狗曼德列斯反动集团的英勇斗争。合肥市有一千五百多人举行集会。共青团安徽省委书记丁浩在大会上发表了坚决支持土耳其人民爱国正义斗争的讲话。在成都市各界青年一千六百多人举行的集会上，共青团四川省委副书记白尚武讲了话。在杭州市两千多人举行的集会上，共青团浙江省委副书记李培增讲了话。
    在这些集会上，群众情绪激昂，他们不断高呼：“坚决支持土耳其人民的爱国正义斗争！”“坚决反对美帝国主义及其走狗奴役和屠杀土耳其爱国人民！”“美帝国主义从土耳其滚出去！从南朝鲜滚出去！从南越滚出去！从日本滚出去！从菲律宾滚出去！从我国台湾滚出去！”“世界人民团结起来！打倒帝国主义！打倒殖民主义！”等口号。
