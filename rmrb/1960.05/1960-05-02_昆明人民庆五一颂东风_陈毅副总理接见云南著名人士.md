Title: Kunming People Celebrate May Day and Praise the East Wind; Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Prominent Figures from Yunnan
Author:
Date: 1960-05-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, May 1st: The 300,000 working people of Kunming City today celebrated International Labor Day with extreme joy, singing and dancing. Chen Yi, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Premier of the State Council, celebrated the festival with people of all ethnic groups and all walks of life in the city.

At around 10:00 a.m., Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu, accompanied by Yan Hongyan, First Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Yu Yichuan, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Provincial Party Committee and Governor, Zhao Zengyi, First Secretary of the Kunming Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Pan Shuoduan, Mayor, and other responsible comrades from the province and city, arrived at the celebration venue of the Tuodong People's Commune in the urban area—the Tuodong Stadium. At this time, the entire stadium of more than 30,000 people erupted in cheers, and the commune members waved the flowers in their hands to express their warm welcome.

Yao Jisui, Secretary of the Panlong District Committee of the Communist Party of China, spoke at the celebration rally, expressing a warm welcome to Vice Premier Chen Yi and others. After narrating the tremendous achievements made by the people's commune in this city since its establishment, he pointed out: It is of great significance to celebrate May Day today, under the excellent situation of the East Wind further prevailing over the West Wind and the unprecedented strengthening of international proletarian solidarity, and in today's world where the forces of peace are unprecedentedly strong and the struggle of the colonial and semi-colonial people for independence is unprecedentedly developed. Representatives of the commune members also unanimously expressed their support for the patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people and the struggle of the Japanese people against the Japan-U.S. military alliance treaty, and supported the national democratic movements of the people of Africa and Latin America.

At noon, people poured out of dozens of celebration venues into the streets and held a massive zonal parade. The parade marched mightily through the streets and alleys of the city. People carried countless banners, shouting in unison for the great victory of the General Line, the Great Leap Forward, and the People's Commune, and hailing the great unity of the world's proletariat and the socialist camp, and the great unity of the people of the world.

At 4:00 p.m., Vice Premier Chen Yi and others came to Yuantong Park and participated in a joint celebration with people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life.

This afternoon, Vice Premier Chen Yi also received the responsible persons of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, various democratic parties, people's organizations, and well-known figures from all walks of life at the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee.

### 昆明人民庆五一颂东风  陈毅副总理接见云南著名人士

1960-05-02
第2版()
专栏：

    昆明人民庆五一颂东风
    陈毅副总理接见云南著名人士
    据新华社昆明1日电　昆明市三十万劳动人民今天怀着极为欢乐的心情，载歌载舞地欢庆“五一”国际劳动节。中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅在昆明和全市各族各界群众一起欢度佳节。
    上午十时许，陈毅副总理、章汉夫副部长等，在中共云南省委第一书记阎红彦，省委书记处书记、省长于一川，中共昆明市委第一书记赵增益和市长潘朔端等省市负责同志陪同下，来到了市区拓东人民公社的庆祝会场——拓东体育场。这时全场三万多人欢声雷动，社员们摇动手上的鲜花，表示热烈欢迎。
    中共盘龙区委书记姚继隋在庆祝大会上讲话，对陈毅副总理等表示热烈欢迎。他在叙述了这个城市人民公社建立以来取得的巨大成就以后指出：在东风进一步压倒西风无产阶级的国际团结空前加强的大好形势下，在世界和平力量空前强大、殖民地半殖民地人民争取独立斗争空前发展的今天，来庆祝“五一”节，具有十分重大的意义。社员代表们在讲话中，也一致表示支持南朝鲜人民的爱国正义斗争和日本人民反对日美军事同盟条约的斗争，支持非洲和拉丁美洲人民的民族民主运动。
    中午，人们从几十个庆祝场所涌向街头，举行了声势浩大的分区游行。游行队伍浩浩荡荡地走遍了全市的大街小巷，人们抬着无数的标语牌，同声欢呼总路线、大跃进和人民公社的伟大胜利，欢呼全世界无产阶级和社会主义阵营的伟大团结，和全世界人民的大团结。
    下午四时，陈毅副总理等来到园通公园，参加了各族各界群众的联欢活动。
    今天下午，陈毅副总理还在云南省人民委员会接见了云南省政协、各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人以及各界著名人士。
