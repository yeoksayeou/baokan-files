Title: Celebrating "May Day" More Widely and Enthusiastically (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1960-05-01
Page: 4

    The young workers of the "Double-Chemical" Youth Shock Brigade of the Beijing Automobile Manufacturing Plant, an advanced collective from the National Congress of Heroes, are studying the article commemorating the 90th anniversary of the birth of the great proletarian revolutionary teacher Lenin - "Long Live Leninism." This theory study group has been praised by the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League. (Photo by Gu Dehua, Xinhua News Agency)

    Members of various handicraft mutual aid groups in Lhasa, Tibet, manufactured more than 1,000 kinds of production tools and daily necessities in April as a gift to "May Day." Here, they are carrying new products to report the good news to the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region.
                          (Photo by Lin Ying, Xinhua News Agency draft, Fax photo)

    Workers at the No. 9 Blast Furnace of Anshan Iron and Steel Company have launched a production competition to exceed a utilization coefficient of 3.0 in order to greet "May Day." To ensure the normal operation of the blast furnace, the workers are inspecting and repairing it at high altitude while continuing production. (Photo by Miao Ming, Xinhua News Agency)

    Beijing's Jingshan Park will be a paradise for children on May 1st. The Beijing Children's Palace will hold an arts and crafts exhibition reflecting the themes of "Long Live the Three Red Banners" and "Work-Study Program." Here, Young Pioneers from the art group of the Children's Palace are completing the final oil paintings for the exhibition.
                (Photo by Gu Dehua, Xinhua News Agency)

    Students from the Guangzhou Overseas Chinese Supplementary School, who returned from Indonesia this year, are enthusiastically practicing singing and dancing to prepare for their first "May Day" after returning to the country.
                    (Photo by Huang Chihe, Xinhua News Agency, Fax photo)

    The Dongfeng, Shuguang, and Suburb People's Communes in the suburbs of Yuci City, Shanxi Province, jointly built eight high-lift irrigation stations at the fastest speed to present a gift to the "May 1st" International Labor Day, turning 3,600 mu of dry land into irrigated land. The high-lift irrigation station adopts the method of concentrating the use of forty waterwheels instead of water pumps, and after testing, the effect is very good. This is the fourth completed centralized waterwheel drawing water for irrigation.
　  (Photo by Hua Ai, Xinhua News Agency)

### 更广泛更热烈地庆祝“五一”节（图片）

1960-05-01
第4版()
专栏：

    更广泛更热烈地庆祝“五一”节
    全国群英会先进集体、北京汽车制造厂双化青年突击队的青年工人们，正在学习纪念伟大的无产阶级革命导师列宁诞生九十周年的文章——列宁主义万岁。这个理论学习小组曾受到共青团北京市委员会的表扬。　新华社记者　顾德华摄
    西藏拉萨市各手工业互助组的组员们，在4月份内制造了一千余种生产工具和生活用品，向“五一”献礼。这是他们抬着新产品向西藏自治区筹备委员会报喜。
                          林　莹摄　新华社稿　（传真照片）
    鞍钢九号高炉工人掀起了一个在利用系数方面决心超过三点零的生产竞赛，迎接“五一”节。为了保证高炉正常运转，工人们一边生产一边在高空检修。　新华社记者　苗　明摄
    5月1日北京景山公园是孩子们的乐园，北京少年宫将举办反映三面红旗万万岁、勤工俭学等内容的工艺美术展览会。这是少年宫美术组的少先队员正在完成展览会上的最后几幅油画。
                新华社记者　顾德华摄
    今年从印度尼西亚归国的广州归国华侨补习学校的学生，在热情地练习歌舞，准备迎接归国后的第一个“五一”。
                    新华社记者　黄炽和摄（传真照片）
    山西省榆次市郊东风、曙光、郊区三个人民公社，为向“五一”国际劳动节献礼，以最快速度联合修起了八个高灌站，使三千六百亩旱原变为水地。高灌站，采取将四十部水车集中使用代替水泵的办法，经过试验，效果很好。这是已建成的第四台集中水车正在抽水浇地。
　  新华社记者　花　皑摄
