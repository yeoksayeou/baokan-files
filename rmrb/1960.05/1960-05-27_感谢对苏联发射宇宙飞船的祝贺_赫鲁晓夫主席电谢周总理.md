Title: Chairman Khrushchev Sends Telegram to Premier Zhou to Thank Him for Congratulations on the Launch of the Soviet Spaceship
Author:
Date: 1960-05-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 26th. Chairman Khrushchev of the Soviet Council of Ministers sent a telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai on the 25th, thanking him for his warm congratulations on the launch of the Soviet satellite spaceship. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

Beijing, Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China,

Dear Comrade Zhou Enlai:

On behalf of the Soviet people and the Soviet government, I extend my heartfelt thanks to you, and through you to the government of the People's Republic of China and the entire Chinese people, for your warm congratulations on the launch of the Soviet satellite spaceship. This great victory of the Soviet people in conquering outer space is a new and powerful proof that socialist countries are devoting their creative efforts to peaceful construction and ensuring human progress. It is very clear that the new achievements of Soviet science and technology serve the cause of peace and socialism, and socialism opens up broad possibilities for the realization of all the good things that human genius can create. The launch of the satellite spaceship shows that the strength of socialism is constantly growing, and that the strength of socialism can defend peace and, if the imperialist adventurers dare to launch a new war, can stop them.

I wish the fraternal Chinese people new victories in the great and noble cause of socialist and communist construction.

                                              N. Khrushchev
                                    May 25, 1960
                                  Moscow, Kremlin

### 感谢对苏联发射宇宙飞船的祝贺  赫鲁晓夫主席电谢周总理

1960-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

    感谢对苏联发射宇宙飞船的祝贺
    赫鲁晓夫主席电谢周总理
    新华社26日讯　苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫25日打电报给周恩来总理，感谢他对苏联发射卫星式宇宙飞船的热烈祝贺。电报全文如下：北京中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志亲爱的周恩来同志：
    我谨代表苏联人民和苏联政府向你，并且通过你向中华人民共和国政府和全体中国人民表示衷心的谢意，感谢你们对苏联发射卫星式宇宙飞船的热烈祝贺。苏联人民在征服宇宙空间方面的这一伟大胜利，是社会主义国家把创造性的努力用于和平建设和保证人类进步的新的有力的证明。十分明显，苏联科学技术的新成就是为和平事业和社会主义事业服务的，社会主义为人类天才所能创造的一切美好事物的实现，开辟了广泛的可能性。卫星式宇宙飞船的发射表明：社会主义力量在不断增长，社会主义的力量是能够保卫住和平的，如果帝国主义冒险分子胆敢发动新战争，社会主义的力量能够制止他们。
    祝兄弟的中国人民在社会主义和共产主义建设的伟大和崇高的事业中取得新的胜利。
                                              尼·赫鲁晓夫
                                    1960年5月25日
                                  于莫斯科，克里姆林宫
