Title: Two Polish Guests Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 14th — According to the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement, Jasinski, a Polish drama theorist and director of the Central Administration of Drama of the Polish Ministry of Culture and Art, and Julandt, vice chairman of the Polish Writers Association, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to China.

### 两位波兰客人到京

1960-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    两位波兰客人到京
    新华社14日讯　根据中波文化合作协定1960年执行计划，来华访问的波兰戏剧理论家、波兰文化艺术部戏剧中央管理局局长雅辛斯基和波兰作家协会副主席尤兰道特，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
