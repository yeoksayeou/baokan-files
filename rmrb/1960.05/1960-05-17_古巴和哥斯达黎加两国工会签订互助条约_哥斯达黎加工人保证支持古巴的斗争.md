Title: Cuba and Costa Rica Sign Mutual Aid Treaty; Costa Rican Workers Pledge Support for Cuba's Struggle
Author:
Date: 1960-05-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, San José, 14th. The Cuban Workers' Confederation and the General Confederation of Workers of Costa Rica yesterday signed a mutual aid treaty in San José, guaranteeing that the two trade unions mutually undertake obligations to defend the social, political, and economic achievements of the Cuban Revolution and the democratic demands of the Costa Rican people.

The treaty stipulates that in the event of imperialist aggression against Cuba, the General Confederation of Workers of Costa Rica will mobilize the workers and agricultural workers belonging to the General Confederation to defend the fraternal people of Cuba.

This mutual aid treaty was signed by Miguel Reyes, Secretary General of the Cuban Tobacco Workers' Union and member of the friendly delegation sent by the Cuban "26th of July Movement" currently visiting Costa Rica, on behalf of the Cuban Workers' Confederation, and Gonzalo Cierra, Secretary General of the General Confederation of Workers of Costa Rica.

### 古巴和哥斯达黎加两国工会签订互助条约  哥斯达黎加工人保证支持古巴的斗争

1960-05-17
第6版()
专栏：

    古巴和哥斯达黎加两国工会签订互助条约
    哥斯达黎加工人保证支持古巴的斗争
    新华社14日讯　圣约瑟消息：古巴工人联合会和哥斯达黎加总工会昨天在圣约瑟签署的一项互助条约中，保证两个工会互相承担义务，保卫古巴革命的社会、政治、经济成果和哥斯达黎加人民的民主要求。
    条约规定，在帝国主义侵略古巴的情况下，哥斯达黎加总工会将动员参加总工会的工人和农业工人保卫古巴的兄弟人民。
    这项互助条约是由目前正在哥斯达黎加访问的古巴“7月26日运动”派遣的友好代表团成员、古巴烟草工人工会总书记米格尔·雷耶斯代表古巴工人联合会，同哥斯达黎加总工会总书记冈萨洛·西埃拉签署的。
