Title: Tokyo in May
Author: Cheng Guangrui
Date: 1960-05-25
Page: 8

Tokyo in May, a May of conspiracies, pouring rain, pitch-black night. A farce is staged in the Diet, with Kishi Nobusuke “passing” the new “Security Treaty” single-handedly! This treaty is darker than the night, this treaty is a bloody deed of betrayal! It betrays the tears of widows, the blood of orphans, the cherry blossoms on the Japanese coast, the white snow on the summit of Mount Fuji... This treaty is darker than the night, it is a page from the American war order! It also wants to sell the sunshine of Asian peace, to sell the bright moon of quiet nights on the Pacific Ocean...

Tokyo in May, a May of struggle, flags filling the city, songs filling the streets; How can the pitch-black night conceal the fiery red flags? How can the pouring rain extinguish the songs of anger! In the Diet, a handful of demons and monsters carry out a conspiratorial vote, outside the Diet, the fighting spirit of millions of people is as strong as steel; Inside the Diet, they shout "Passed!" under the protection of bayonets, outside the Diet, the roar of millions of people shakes the mountains!

"New 'Security Treaty', spies in the black sky, 'Honest John', 'Hawk', 'Sidewinder', together with the American devils and Kishi Nobusuke... Throw all of this into the sea to feed the fish and turtles!" This roar commands the heavy rain to stop, this roar will tear the night! When dawn breaks, let us see the wonder of the sunrise, at the foot of Mount Fuji, cherry blossoms like the sea, the morning sun like blood.

### 东京的五月
程光锐
1960-05-25
第8版()
专栏：

    东京的五月
    程光锐东京的五月，阴谋的五月，倾盆的雨，漆黑的夜，国会里上演一幕丑剧，岸信介只手“通过了”新“安全条约”！这条约比黑夜还黑，这条约是一张血腥的卖身契！它出卖了寡妇的泪，孤儿的血，日本海岸的樱花，富士山头的白雪……这条约比黑夜还黑，是美国战争订货单的一页！它还要出卖亚洲和平的阳光，出卖太平洋上静夜的明月……东京的五月，斗争的五月，旗帜满城，歌声满街；漆黑的夜，怎能把火红的旗帜掩遮？倾盆的雨，怎能把愤怒的歌声浇灭！国会里，一小撮鬼怪进行阴谋的表决，国会外，千百万人民的斗志如钢似铁；国会里，在刺刀保护下喊一声“通过”，国会外，千百万人民的吼声震撼山岳！“新‘安全条约’，黑色的空中间谍，‘诚实的约翰’、‘鹰隼’、‘响尾蛇’，连同美国佬和岸信介……这一切，统统扔下大海，去喂鱼鳖！”这吼声喝令大雨停歇，这吼声要把黑夜撕裂！待天明，且看日出的奇景，富士山下，樱花如海，朝阳如血。
