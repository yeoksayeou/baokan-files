Title: Swedish Workers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 14th. A five-member Swedish workers delegation, led by Sten Schuberg, chairman of the Swedish Postal and Telegraph Workers' Union, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. They are visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

### 瑞典工人代表团到京

1960-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典工人代表团到京
    新华社14日讯　瑞典工人代表团一行五人，在团长、瑞典邮电工会主席斯坦·修贝率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中华全国总工会邀请来我国访问的。
