Title: After Participating in the Socialist Public Security Sports Organization Basketball Game, a Group of Guests from Poland, East Germany and Hungary Left Beijing to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-05-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 8th - After participating in the first Men's Basketball Competition of the Socialist Countries' Public Security Sports Organizations, the Polish "Guard" Men's Basketball Team, the observer Kirst from the German Democratic Republic "Dynamo" Sports Association, and Herschel, concluded their visits in various parts of our country and returned home by plane from Beijing today. After the competition, the Polish "Guard" team held four friendly matches with the "August 1st" Men's Basketball Team, Shanghai "Pioneer" Team, Shanghai Team, and Zhejiang Team in Beijing, Shanghai, and Hangzhou respectively. As a result, the visiting team won against the Shanghai "Pioneer" Team with a score of eighty-nine to eighty-one, and lost to the home team in the other three games.

Xinhua News Agency, 10th - The observer Horvat Goresi from the Hungarian "Dozsa" Sports Association and the Hungarian international basketball referee Wenzai, left Beijing by plane today to return home after completing their visit in our country. Mei Bin, Deputy Director of the National Committee of the Chinese "Pioneer" Sports Association, and others went to the airport to see them off.

### 参加社会主义公安体育组织篮球赛后  波德匈一批客人离北京返国

1960-05-11
第6版()
专栏：

    参加社会主义公安体育组织篮球赛后  
    波德匈一批客人离北京返国
    据新华社8日讯　参加社会主义国家公安体育组织第一届男子篮球比赛的波兰“近卫军”男子篮球队、德意志民主共和国“狄纳莫”体育协会观察员基尔斯特和赫歇尔，结束了在我国各地的访问以后，今天由北京乘飞机回国。波兰“近卫军”队，在比赛结束后曾在北京、上海、杭州分别同“八一”男子篮球队、上海“前卫”队、上海队、浙江队举行了四场友谊比赛。结果，客队以八十九比八十一胜上海“前卫”队，其他三场比赛都败给主队。
    据新华社10日讯　匈牙利“杜沙”体育协会观察员霍瓦特·戈勒希和匈牙利篮球国际裁判文蔡，结束在我国的访问后，今天乘飞机离北京回国。中国“前卫”体育协会全国委员会副主任梅彬等人到机场欢送他们。
