Title: Li Mingqiang to Visit Romania for Performances
Author:
Date: 1960-05-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, May 15th: Chinese young pianist Li Mingqiang arrived in Romania by train from Budapest, the capital of Hungary, on the 14th for a two-week visit and performance tour.

Original: 


### 李名强到罗访问演出

1960-05-18
第5版()
专栏：

    李名强到罗访问演出
    据新华社布加勒斯特15日电　中国青年钢琴家李名强，14日从匈牙利首都布达佩斯乘火车到罗马尼亚作为期两周的访问演出。
