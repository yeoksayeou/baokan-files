Title: Swedish Kingdom Military Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, May 5th. The military delegation of the Kingdom of Sweden, invited by our Ministry of National Defense for a friendly visit to our country, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The military delegation, headed by Major General Ållman, Chief of the Military Command of the Swedish Ministry of National Defense, consists of three members. The members are Lieutenant Colonel Wallin and Major Bondeson.

General Luo Ruiqing, Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, welcomed the delegation at the airport.

Also present at the airport to welcome the delegation were General Yang Chengwu, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General Liu Shao Wen, Major General Xie Fang, Air Force Major General Wang Zhitao, Navy Major General Fu Jize, and Wang Jin, Deputy Director of the West European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Military attachés of various countries' embassies in China also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

### 瑞典王国军事代表团到京

1960-05-06
第6版()
专栏：

    瑞典王国军事代表团到京
    新华社5日讯　应我国国防部邀请来我国作友好访问的瑞典王国军事代表团，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。以瑞典王国国防部军令部部长奥尔曼少将为首的军事代表团成员共三人。团员有瓦林中校、邦德逊少校。
    中国人民解放军总参谋长罗瑞卿大将到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的还有杨成武上将、萧向荣中将、刘少文中将、解方少将、王智涛空军少将、傅继泽海军少将以及外交部西欧司副司长王进等。
    各国驻华大使馆武官也到机场欢迎。
