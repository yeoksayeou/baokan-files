Title: Premier Zhou Receives Representatives of Overseas Chinese in Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1960-05-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 13th: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi received representatives of local overseas Chinese at the Presidential Palace this afternoon. Vice Premier Chen Yi also spoke to the representatives of the overseas Chinese.

At the end of the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi took a group photo with the representatives of the overseas Chinese as a memento.

### 周总理接见越南华侨代表

1960-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见越南华侨代表
    新华社河内13日电　周恩来总理、陈毅副总理今天下午在主席府接见了当地华侨代表，陈毅副总理并向华侨代表讲了话。
    接见结束时，周恩来总理、陈毅副总理同华侨代表合影留念。
