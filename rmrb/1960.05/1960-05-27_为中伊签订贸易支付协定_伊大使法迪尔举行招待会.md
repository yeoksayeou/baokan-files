Title: Iraqi Ambassador Fadil Holds Reception for the Signing of Trade and Payment Agreement Between China and Iraq
Author:
Date: 1960-05-27
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, May 26th - Iraqi Ambassador to China, Fadil, held a reception at the Xinqiao Hotel tonight to celebrate the visit of the Iraqi government trade delegation to our country and the signing of the trade and payment agreement between China and Iraq.
    Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jiuzhuang, Minister of Commerce Yao Yilin, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Geng Biao, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Lu Xuzhang, Vice President of the People's Bank of China Qiao Peixin, Vice President of the Sino-Iraqi Friendship Association Luo Jun, Vice Presidents of the Chinese-African Peoples' Friendship Association Liu Zizai and Zhang Tiesheng, and Vice Mayor of Beijing Le Songsheng attended the reception.
    Ambassador Fadil and Minister Ye Jiuzhuang spoke at the reception.
    All members of the Iraqi government trade delegation, headed by Iraqi Minister of Commerce Abdul Latif Shawwaf, attended the reception.
    Also attending the reception were diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China.

### 为中伊签订贸易支付协定  伊大使法迪尔举行招待会

1960-05-27
第2版()
专栏：

    为中伊签订贸易支付协定
    伊大使法迪尔举行招待会
    新华社26日讯　伊拉克共和国驻中国大使法迪尔，为伊拉克政府贸易代表团访问我国、中国伊拉克两国签订贸易和支付协定，今晚在新侨饭店举行招待会。
    对外贸易部部长叶季壮，商业部部长姚依林、外交部副部长耿飚，对外贸易部副部长卢绪章，中国人民银行副行长乔培新，中伊友好协会副会长罗俊，中国非洲人民友好协会副会长刘子载、张铁生，北京市副市长乐松生等出席了招待会。
    法迪尔大使和叶季壮部长在招待会上讲了话。
    以伊拉克商业部部长阿卜杜勒·拉蒂夫·沙瓦夫为首的伊拉克政府贸易代表团全体成员出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的，还有各国驻华使节和外交官员。
