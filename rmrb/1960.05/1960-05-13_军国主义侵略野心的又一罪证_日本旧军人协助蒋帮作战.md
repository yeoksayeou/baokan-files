Title: Another Criminal Evidence of Japanese Militaristic Aggression Ambition: Former Japanese Soldiers Assisted Chiang Gang in Combat
Author:
Date: 1960-05-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, May 12th: "Mainichi Shimbun" reported today that the Ehime Prefecture Federation of the Japan Socialist Party revealed on the 11th that former Japanese soldiers had assisted the Chiang Kai-shek clique's army in combat on Jinmen (Quemoy) and Mazu (Matsu).

According to the revelation, under the instruction of the US Central Intelligence Agency, twenty former Japanese army soldiers, including Nobuyoshi Ichinose (a graduate of the Japanese Army University and a lieutenant colonel in the Imperial Japanese Army) living in Imabari City, participated in the combat command of the Taiwan Chiang army's landing on Jinmen and Mazu during the period from 1950 to 1953.

According to reports, the Socialist Party attaches great importance to this issue and has decided to raise it in the Special Committee on the "Security Treaty" of the House of Representatives to investigate the Japanese government.

### 军国主义侵略野心的又一罪证  日本旧军人协助蒋帮作战

1960-05-13
第3版()
专栏：

    军国主义侵略野心的又一罪证
    日本旧军人协助蒋帮作战
    新华社12日讯　东京消息：据“每日新闻”今天报道，日本社会党爱媛县联合会11日揭露，日本旧军人曾经在金门、马祖协助蒋介石集团的军队作战。
  据揭露，在美国中央情报局的指示下，居住在今治市的市坡信义（他是日本陆军大学毕业生、日本帝国陆军中校）等二十名旧日本陆军军人，在1950年到1953年期间参加了台湾蒋军登陆金门、马祖的作战指挥。
    据报道，社会党重视这个问题，决定将在众议院“安全条约”特别委员会提出这个问题，追究日本政府。
