Title: Cuba Decides to Establish Foreign Trade Bank
Author:
Date: 1960-05-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Havana - Cuban President Dorticos has approved the decision to establish the Cuban Foreign Trade Bank.

The Cuban Foreign Trade Bank will be a lending institution responsible for developing and guiding Cuba's foreign trade. Its responsibilities include expanding foreign markets, developing Cuban export varieties, and managing the national import and export industries.

### 古巴决定成立对外贸易银行

1960-05-03
第5版()
专栏：

    古巴决定成立对外贸易银行
    据新华社哈瓦那电　古巴总统多尔蒂科斯已经批准成立古巴对外贸易银行的决定。
    古巴对外贸易银行将是一个负责发展和指导古巴对外贸易的贷款机构，它的职责包括扩大国外市场、发展古巴出口品种，同时负责全国进出口业的管理工作。
