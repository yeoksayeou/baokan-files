Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Singapore Trades Union Congress Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-05-22
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 21st. Vice Premier Chen Yi received the entire delegation of the Singapore Trades Union Congress, headed by Devan Nair, this morning. Present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, Zhu Xuefan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others.

### 陈毅副总理接见新加坡职工总会代表团

1960-05-22
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见新加坡职工总会代表团
    新华社21日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了以德万·奈尔为首的新加坡职工联合总会代表团全体团员。接见时在座的有中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志，中华全国总工会副主席朱学范等。
