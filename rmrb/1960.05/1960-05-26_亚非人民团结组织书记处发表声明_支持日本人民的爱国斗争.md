Title: Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Organization Secretariat Issues Statement Supporting the Patriotic Struggle of the Japanese People
Author:
Date: 1960-05-26
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 24th – The Secretariat of the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Organization issued a statement here today, saying that the people of Asian and African countries are indignant at the US-Japanese reactionaries' attempt to bring the new Japan-US "Security Treaty" into effect.

The statement expressed support for "the Japanese people's patriotic struggle to prevent the revision of the Japan-US Security Treaty, and hopes that they will move in the direction of abolishing this treaty."

The statement welcomed the joint statement issued earlier by the trade unions of China, Japan, and North Korea. This joint statement called on workers of the three countries to support the struggle of the people of Japan and South Korea, drive the US imperialists out of the Western Pacific, and support the struggle of the people of African countries for national independence.

### 亚非人民团结组织书记处发表声明  支持日本人民的爱国斗争

1960-05-26
第3版()
专栏：

    亚非人民团结组织书记处发表声明
    支持日本人民的爱国斗争
    据新华社开罗24日电　亚非人民团结组织书记处今天在这里发表声明说，亚非各国人民对于企图使新日美“安全条约”生效的美日反动派表示愤慨。
    声明表示支持“日本人民阻止修改日美安全条约的爱国斗争，并且希望他们向着废除这项条约的方向迈进。”
    声明欢迎中国、日本和朝鲜三国工会前些时候发表的联合声明。这项联合声明号召三国工人支持日本和南朝鲜人民的斗争，把美帝国主义者赶出西太平洋，并且支持非洲各国人民争取民族独立的斗争。
