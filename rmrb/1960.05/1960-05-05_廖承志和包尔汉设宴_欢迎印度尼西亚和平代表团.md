Title: Liao Chengzhi and Burhan Host Banquet to Welcome Indonesian Peace Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-05-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the China Peace Committee and Burhan, Chairman of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the visiting Indonesian Peace Delegation.

Those attending the banquet tonight included: Qu Tangliang, Huang Jiasi, Zhou Erfu, Zhang Jie, Ding Tuo, Fang Ming, Peng Wen, Li Qiong, Wang Mingyuan, Zheng Senyu, Zhu Ziqi and other people from all walks of life.

### 廖承志和包尔汉设宴  欢迎印度尼西亚和平代表团

1960-05-05
第6版()
专栏：

    廖承志和包尔汉设宴
    欢迎印度尼西亚和平代表团
    新华社4日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志、中国印度尼西亚友好协会会长包尔汉，今晚设宴欢迎正在我国访问的印度尼西亚和平代表团。
    出席今晚宴会的有：区棠亮、黄家驷、周而复、张杰、丁拓、方明、彭文、李琼、王明远、郑森禹、朱子奇等各界人士。
