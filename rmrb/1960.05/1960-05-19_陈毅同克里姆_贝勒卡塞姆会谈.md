Title: Chen Yi Holds Talks with Krim Belkacem
Author:
Date: 1960-05-19
Page: 1

    Chen Yi Holds Talks with Krim Belkacem
    Xinhua News Agency, May 18th: Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs, held talks with Krim Belkacem, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, this afternoon in a friendly atmosphere. On the Algerian side, Boumahdi, Minister of Armaments and Liaison, and Francis Ahmed, Minister of Finance and Economic Affairs, participated in the talks. On the Chinese side, Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Li Qiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and others participated in the talks.

### 陈毅同克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆会谈

1960-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅同克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆会谈
    新华社18日讯　国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅，同阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府副总理兼外交部长克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆，今天下午在友好的气氛中举行了会谈。阿尔及利亚方面参加会谈的，有军备和联络部部长布苏夫·阿卜杜勒哈菲德，财政和经济事务部部长弗朗西斯·艾哈迈德。中国方面参加会谈的，有外交部副部长姬鹏飞，对外贸易部副部长李强等。
