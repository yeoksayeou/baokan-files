Title: U.S. Military Aircraft Intrudes into China's Airspace to Provoke: 95th Serious Warning Issued
Author:
Date: 1960-05-12
Page: 2

    U.S. Military Aircraft Intrudes into China's Airspace to Provoke
    I Issue the 95th Serious Warning

    Xinhua News Agency, 11th - At 11:58 to 12:06 on May 11th, a U.S. Navy patrol plane intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 95th serious warning.

### 美国军用飞机侵入我国领空挑衅  我提出第95次严重警告

1960-05-12
第2版()
专栏：

    美国军用飞机侵入我国领空挑衅
    我提出第95次严重警告
    新华社11日讯　5月11日十一时五十八分到十二时零六分，美国海军巡逻机一架侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第九十五次严重警告。
