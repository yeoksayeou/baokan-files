Title: Hungarian Communist Youth League Central Art Troupe Leaves Beijing for the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1960-05-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The Hungarian Communist Youth League Central Art Troupe concluded its visit and performances in our country and left Beijing by plane this morning for the Soviet Union.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Luo Jun, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Yang Haibo, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and literary and art workers from the capital.

The Hungarian artists arrived in our country on March 31st. After visiting and performing in Jinan, Nanjing, Shanghai, Tianjin and other places, they returned to Beijing on April 29th to participate in the various celebration activities of the "May Day" festival, and gave two performances at the Asia-Africa Student Sanatorium and the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, which were warmly welcomed.

### 匈牙利共青团中央艺术团离北京去苏联

1960-05-07
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利共青团中央艺术团离北京去苏联
    新华社6日讯　匈牙利共产主义青年团中央艺术团结束了在我国的访问演出，今天上午乘飞机离开北京去苏联。
    到机场欢送的，有对外文化联络委员会副主任罗俊、共青团中央书记处书记杨海波，以及首都的文艺工作者。
    匈牙利艺术家们是在3月31日来到我国的。他们在济南、南京、上海、天津等地进行访问演出以后，在4月29日回到北京，参加了“五一”节的各项庆祝活动，并且在亚非学生疗养院和共青团中央机关演出了两场，受到了热烈的欢迎。
