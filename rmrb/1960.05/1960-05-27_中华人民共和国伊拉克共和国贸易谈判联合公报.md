Title: Joint Communiqué on Trade Negotiations between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Iraq
Author:
Date: 1960-05-27
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, May 27th: A joint communiqué on trade negotiations between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Republic of Iraq was signed in Beijing on the afternoon of May 25th.  Ye Ji-zhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, signed the communiqué on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China, and Abdul Latif Shawwaf, Minister of Commerce of the Republic of Iraq and head of the government trade delegation, signed on behalf of the Government of the Republic of Iraq.

The full text of the Joint Communiqué on Trade Negotiations between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Iraq is as follows:

At the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, the trade delegation of the Government of the Republic of Iraq arrived in Beijing on May 19, 1960, and held discussions with the Government of the People's Republic of China on trade and payment agreements between China and Iraq. The 1960-1961 China-Iraq Trade Agreement and Payment Agreement were signed in Beijing on May 25, 1960. Ye Ji-zhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, signed on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China, and Abdul Latif Shawwaf, Minister of Commerce and head of the trade delegation, signed on behalf of the Government of the Republic of Iraq.

According to the attached list of goods to the aforementioned trade agreement, China will export cotton cloth, silk, steel, various machines, and daily necessities to Iraq; Iraq will export dates, cotton, hides, crude oil and petroleum products, and cement to China.

During the negotiations, both sides frankly and fully exchanged views on trade issues between the two countries, which not only enhanced mutual understanding but also contributed to the further development of trade between the two countries.

The negotiations were conducted in a friendly and harmonious atmosphere throughout. Both sides expressed satisfaction with the results of the negotiations, believing that the signing of the 1960-1961 China-Iraq Trade Agreement and Payment Agreement marks the consolidation and development of economic and trade relations between China and Iraq, as well as the enhancement of friendship between the two governments and peoples.

### 中华人民共和国伊拉克共和国贸易谈判联合公报

1960-05-27
第2版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国伊拉克共和国贸易谈判联合公报
    新华社27日讯　中华人民共和国政府和伊拉克共和国政府25日下午在北京签订了贸易谈判联合公报。代表中华人民共和国政府在公报上签字的是对外贸易部部长叶季壮，代表伊拉克共和国政府签字的是伊拉克共和国商业部部长、政府贸易代表团团长阿卜杜勒·拉蒂夫·沙瓦夫。
    中华人民共和国和伊拉克共和国贸易谈判联合公报全文如下：
    伊拉克共和国政府贸易代表团应中华人民共和国政府的邀请于1960年5月19日抵达北京，和中华人民共和国政府就中、伊两国的贸易、支付协定进行了商谈。并且已经于1960年5月25日在北京签订了1960—1961年中、伊贸易协定和支付协定。代表中华人民共和国政府签字的是对外贸易部部长叶季壮，代表伊拉克共和国政府签字的是商业部部长、贸易代表团团长阿卜杜勒·拉蒂夫·沙瓦夫。
    根据上述贸易协定所附货单，中国将对伊拉克出口棉布、丝绸、钢材、各种机器和日用百货等；伊拉克将对中国出口椰枣、棉花、皮张、原油及石油产品、水泥等。
    在谈判中双方就两国的贸易问题坦率地、充分地交换了意见，这不仅增进了彼此间的相互了解，而且有助于两国间贸易的进一步发展。
    谈判始终是在友好和融洽的气氛中进行的。双方对谈判的结果表示满意，认为1960—1961年的中、伊贸易协定和支付协定的签订标志着中、伊两国的经济贸易的巩固和发展，以及两国政府和人民间的友谊的增进。
