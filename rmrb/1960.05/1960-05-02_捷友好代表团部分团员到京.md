Title: Part of Czechoslovak Friendship Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-02
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, May 1st: Part of the Czechoslovak Friendship Delegation, invited by the Sino-Czechoslovak Friendship Association to participate in the Sino-Czechoslovak Friendship Week activities and pay a friendly visit to our country, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The members included: Mi. Vasik, Deputy Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak National Front; E. Skrlpkova, Chairman of the Prague State Peace Committee; and J. Novotny, Director of the Czech-Chinese Friendship Factory. Those who welcomed them at the airport included Cui Yitian, Vice President of the Sino-Czechoslovak Friendship Association, Si Tu Huimin, Secretary-General, as well as representatives from the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the First Ministry of Machine Building.
Also at the airport to welcome them were J. Obafsky, head of the Czechoslovak Friendship Delegation, who is already in Beijing, and Lehman, Counselor of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China.

### 捷友好代表团部分团员到京

1960-05-02
第3版()
专栏：

    捷友好代表团部分团员到京
    新华社1日讯　应中捷友好协会邀请来我国参加中捷友好周活动并进行友好访问的捷克斯洛伐克友好代表团部分团员：捷克斯洛伐克民族阵线中央副书记米·瓦西克，布拉格州和平委员会主席埃·斯克尔普柯娃，捷中友谊工厂厂长约·诺沃提尼，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的，有中捷友好协会副会长崔义田，秘书长司徒慧敏，以及对外文委、第一机械工业部的代表等。
    已经在北京的捷克斯洛伐克友好代表团团长约·奥巴夫斯基和捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆参赞雷曼，也到机场欢迎。
