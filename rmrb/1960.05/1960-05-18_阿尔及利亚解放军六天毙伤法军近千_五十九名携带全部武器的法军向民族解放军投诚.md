Title: Algerian Liberation Army Kills or Wounds Nearly a Thousand French Soldiers in Six Days; Fifty-Nine French Soldiers, Carrying All Their Weapons, Surrender to the National Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1960-05-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 16th. The Algerian National Liberation Army headquarters announced in a military communique here today that the Algerian National Liberation Army killed 610 French soldiers and wounded 355 between the 7th and 12th of this month. In addition, 59 French soldiers, carrying all their weapons, surrendered to the Algerian National Liberation Army.

The communique said that during this period, the Algerian forces ambushed enemy convoys 18 times, engaged the enemy in 22 encounters, and launched 43 attacks on enemy strongholds. Its demolition squads also carried out 37 operations in various parts of Algeria, and commando units launched 48 attacks. In these battles, the Algerian National Liberation Army destroyed 40 French armored vehicles, blew up two French trains, shot down nine French aircraft, and captured many weapons and medical supplies.

### 阿尔及利亚解放军六天毙伤法军近千  五十九名携带全部武器的法军向民族解放军投诚

1960-05-18
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚解放军六天毙伤法军近千
    五十九名携带全部武器的法军向民族解放军投诚
    新华社开罗16日电　阿尔及利亚民族解放军总部16日在这里发表军事公报说，阿尔及利亚民族解放军在本月7日到12日期间击毙法国士兵六百一十名，打伤三百五十五名。此外，有五十九名携带全部武器的法国士兵向阿尔及利亚民族解放军投诚。
    公报说，在此期间，阿尔及利亚部队对敌人的运输队进行了十八次伏击，同敌人进行了二十二次遭遇战，向敌人的据点发动攻击四十三次。它的爆破部队还在阿尔及利亚各地采取了三十七次行动，突击部队发动攻击四十八次。在这些战斗中，阿尔及利亚民族解放军摧毁法军装甲车四十辆，炸毁法军火车两列，击落法军飞机九架，并缴获许多武器和医药用品。
