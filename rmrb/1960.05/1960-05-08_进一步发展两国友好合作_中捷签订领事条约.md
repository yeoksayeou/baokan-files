Title: Further Develop Friendly Cooperation Between the Two Countries: China and Czechoslovakia Sign Consular Treaty
Author:
Date: 1960-05-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 7th May. Communiqué on the talks regarding the Consular Treaty between the People's Republic of China and the Czechoslovak Republic.

    The delegations of the People's Republic of China and the Czechoslovak Republic held talks in Prague from May 3rd to 7th, 1960 regarding the conclusion of a Consular Treaty between China and Czechoslovakia. The talks were conducted in an atmosphere of traditional friendship and full understanding.

    The Consular Treaty between China and Czechoslovakia stipulates the mutual cooperative relations between the two countries in consular matters, which will inevitably further develop the friendly cooperation between the two countries.

    The head of the Chinese delegation participating in the talks was Cao Ying, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Czechoslovakia. The head of the Czechoslovak delegation was Dr. Antonín Gregor, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Czechoslovak Republic. The Chairman of the People's Republic of China appointed Cao Ying, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Czechoslovakia, and the President of the Czechoslovak Republic appointed Václav David, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Czechoslovak Republic, to sign the treaty; the signing ceremony was held in Prague on May 7th, 1960.

### 进一步发展两国友好合作  中捷签订领事条约

1960-05-08
第4版()
专栏：

    进一步发展两国友好合作
    中捷签订领事条约
    新华社布拉格7日电　中华人民共和国和捷克斯洛伐克共和国领事条约会谈公报。
    中华人民共和国代表团和捷克斯洛伐克共和国代表团于1960年5月3日至7日在布拉格举行了关于缔结中捷领事条约的会谈。会谈是在传统友谊和充分谅解的气氛中进行的。
    中捷领事条约规定了两国在领事方面的相互合作关系，它必将使两国间的友好合作进一步发展。
    参加会谈的中国代表团团长是中华人民共和国驻捷克斯洛伐克特命全权大使曹瑛，捷克斯洛伐克代表团团长是捷克斯洛伐克共和国外交部第一副部长安托宁·格里哥尔博士。中华人民共和国主席特派中华人民共和国驻捷克斯洛伐克特命全权大使曹瑛，捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统特派捷克斯洛伐克共和国外交部长瓦茨拉夫·戴维在条约上签字；签字仪式已于1960年5月7日在布拉格举行。
