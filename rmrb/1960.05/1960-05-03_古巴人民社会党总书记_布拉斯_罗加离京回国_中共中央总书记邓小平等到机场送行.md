Title: First Secretary of the People's Socialist Party of Cuba, Blas Roca, Leaves Beijing for Home; General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, Deng Xiaoping, and others See Him Off at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1960-05-03
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, May 2nd. Blas Roca, First Secretary of the People's Socialist Party of Cuba, and his wife, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home after visiting China at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

    Those who saw him off at the airport were: Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee; Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau; Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat; Wu Xiuquan, member of the Central Committee; and Li Qixin, responsible staff member of the CPC Central Committee organs.

    Last night, Comrade Deng Xiaoping hosted a banquet in honor of Comrade Roca and his wife.

    During their visit to China, Blas Roca and his wife met Chairman Mao Zedong in Tianjin. In Beijing, they visited people's communes and factories, and viewed the Cuban Revolution photo exhibition held at the Working People's Cultural Palace and buildings such as the Great Hall of the People and the Beijing Railway Station. On May 1st International Labor Day, they met with the working people of the capital in Beihai Park and Zhongshan Park and were warmly welcomed. (Attached picture)

    General Secretary Deng Xiaoping shakes hands with General Secretary Roca to say goodbye. (Photo by Li Changyong, Xinhua News Agency)

### 古巴人民社会党总书记  布拉斯·罗加离京回国  中共中央总书记邓小平等到机场送行

1960-05-03
第1版()
专栏：

    古巴人民社会党总书记
    布拉斯·罗加离京回国
    中共中央总书记邓小平等到机场送行
    新华社2日讯　古巴人民社会党总书记布拉斯·罗加和他的夫人，应中国共产党中央委员会的邀请在我国参观访问后，已于今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场送行的有：中共中央总书记邓小平，政治局委员彭真，书记处书记王稼祥，中央委员伍修权，中共中央机关负责工作人员李启新等。
    昨晚，邓小平同志设宴为罗加同志和他的夫人饯行。
    布拉斯·罗加和他的夫人在我国访问期间，曾在天津会见毛泽东主席。他们在北京曾访问了人民公社、工厂，参观了在劳动人民文化宫举行的古巴革命图片展览会和人民大会堂、北京火车站等建筑物。“五一”国际劳动节，他们曾到北海公园和中山公园会见首都劳动人民，受到了热烈欢迎。（附图片）
    邓小平总书记和罗加总书记握手告别　新华社记者　李长永摄
