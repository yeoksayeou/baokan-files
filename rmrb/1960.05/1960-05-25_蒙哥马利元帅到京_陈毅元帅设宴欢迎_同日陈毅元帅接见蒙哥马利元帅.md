Title: Marshal Montgomery Arrives in Beijing, Marshal Chen Yi Hosts a Banquet in Welcome. Marshal Chen Yi Receives Marshal Montgomery on the Same Day.
Author:
Date: 1960-05-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 24th. British Marshal Montgomery, who is visiting our country, arrived in Beijing by plane from Guangzhou at noon today.

Those who welcomed him at the airport included General Wang Shusheng, Vice Minister of National Defense; Huang Zhong, Deputy Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission; Major General Wang Xinggang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense; Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Xin Yi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee; and Major General Luo Wenfang, Director of the Beijing National Defense Sports Association.

Mr. S. E. Rivers, British Chargé d'affaires in China, was at the airport to welcome him.

General Li Da, Director of the Chinese People's National Defense Sports Association, accompanied Montgomery from Guangzhou to Beijing today.

This afternoon, Montgomery visited the Beijing Railway Station, the National Cultural Palace, and other new buildings in Beijing.

Xinhua News Agency, 24th. Marshal Chen Yi held a banquet this evening to welcome British Marshal Montgomery.

Marshal He Long attended the banquet.

Also present at the banquet were Fu Zuoyi, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council; Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade; Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; General Wang Shusheng, Vice Minister of National Defense; General Li Da, Director of the Chinese People's National Defense Sports Association; Huang Zhong, Deputy Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission; Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Wu Han, Vice Mayor of Beijing; Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Song Zhi Guang, Deputy Director of the Department of West European Affairs.

Mr. S. E. Rivers, British Chargé d'affaires in China, was invited to attend the banquet.

Xinhua News Agency, 24th. Marshal Chen Yi of the People's Republic of China received British Marshal Montgomery, who is currently visiting our country, this afternoon. (Attached picture)

### 蒙哥马利元帅到京  陈毅元帅设宴欢迎  同日陈毅元帅接见蒙哥马利元帅

1960-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙哥马利元帅到京  陈毅元帅设宴欢迎
    同日陈毅元帅接见蒙哥马利元帅
    新华社24日讯　来我国访问的英国蒙哥马利元帅，今天中午乘飞机由广州到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有国防部副部长王树声大将，体育运动委员会副主任黄中，国防部办公厅副主任王兴纲少将，外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文，北京市人民委员会副秘书长辛毅，北京市国防体育协会主任罗文坊少将等。
    英国驻中国代办施棣华到机场欢迎。
    中国人民国防体育协会主任李达上将今天陪同蒙哥马利由广州到达北京。
    今天下午，蒙哥马利参观了北京车站、民族文化宫和北京的其他新建筑。
    新华社24日讯　陈毅元帅今晚举行宴会，欢迎英国蒙哥马利元帅。
    贺龙元帅出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的，还有国防委员会副主席傅作义，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长罗贵波，国防部副部长王树声大将，中国人民国防体育协会主任李达上将，体育运动委员会副主任黄中，中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸，北京市副市长吴晗，外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文，西欧司副司长宋之光等。
    英国驻中国代办施棣华应邀出席了宴会。
    新华社24日讯　中华人民共和国元帅陈毅今天下午接见了正在我国访问的英国蒙哥马利元帅。（附图片）
