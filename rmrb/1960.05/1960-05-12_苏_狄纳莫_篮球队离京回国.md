Title: Soviet "Dynamo" Basketball Team Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-05-12
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 11th: The Soviet "Dynamo" men's basketball team, which participated in the first men's basketball competition of socialist countries' public security sports organizations, left Beijing by plane today to return home after ending its visit to our country.

### 苏“狄纳莫”篮球队离京回国

1960-05-12
第3版()
专栏：

    苏“狄纳莫”篮球队离京回国
    新华社11日讯　参加社会主义国家公安体育组织第一届男子篮球比赛大会的苏联“狄纳莫”男子篮球队，结束了在我国的访问后，今天乘飞机离北京回国。
