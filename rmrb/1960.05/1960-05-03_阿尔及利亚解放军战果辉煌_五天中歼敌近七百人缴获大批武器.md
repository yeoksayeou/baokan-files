Title: Algerian Liberation Army Achieves Brilliant Victories: Nearly 700 Enemies Annihilated and Large Quantities of Weapons Seized in Five Days
Author:
Date: 1960-05-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, May 1st. The Algerian National Liberation Army headquarters here announced today that the National Liberation Army killed 448 French colonial soldiers and wounded 242 between April 25th and 29th.

During this period, Algerian forces engaged the enemy in seventeen locations and launched twenty-four attacks on enemy military positions. They also ambushed enemy convoys thirty-one times.

The communiqué said that thirty-eight French armored vehicles were destroyed, and four French military aircraft were shot down. The Algerians also seized 505 weapons of various types. Forty French soldiers surrendered to the Algerian army with their weapons.

### 阿尔及利亚解放军战果辉煌  五天中歼敌近七百人缴获大批武器

1960-05-03
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚解放军战果辉煌
    五天中歼敌近七百人缴获大批武器
    新华社开罗1日电　据这里的阿尔及利亚民族解放军总部今天宣布：民族解放军在4月25日到29日打死法国殖民军四百四十八人，打伤二百四十二人。
    在这一期间，阿尔及利亚军队曾在十七个地方同敌人交战，并向敌军的军事阵地发动了二十四次进攻。他们还对敌军的车队进行了三十一次伏击。
    公报说，有三十八辆法国装甲车遭到破坏，四架法国军用飞机被击落。阿尔及利亚人还缴获了五百零五件各种类型的武器。有四十名法国士兵带着武器向阿尔及利亚军投诚。
