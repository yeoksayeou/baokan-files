Title: Montgomery Returns Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-05-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 28th: British Field Marshal Montgomery arrived in Guangzhou from Shanghai by special plane this morning and transferred to a train via Shenzhen to return home via Hong Kong.

    Among those who went to the station to see him off were Zhu Guang, the Mayor of Guangzhou, as well as General Li Da, Director of the Chinese National Defense Sports Association, and Rong Gaotang, Deputy Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, who accompanied Field Marshal Montgomery to Guangzhou.

    Also present at the station to see him off were Liang Xiang, Deputy Mayor of Guangzhou, Major General Xiao Yuanli, Major General Liu Xinglong, Major General Huang Yiping, Rear Admiral Qi Yong, Air Force Major General Zhang Xisan, leading members of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Guangzhou, Wang Jinchuan, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Guangdong Provincial People's Committee, and Lu Chengzhang, Deputy Director of the Guangdong Provincial National Defense Sports Association.

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 28th: British Field Marshal Montgomery, accompanied by General Li Da, Director of the Chinese National Defense Sports Association, and Rong Gaotang, Deputy Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, left Shanghai by plane for Guangzhou this morning.

    Among those who saw him off at the airport were Cao Diqiu, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, Major General Xiong Yingtang, Director of the Shanghai National Defense Sports Association and Deputy Commander of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Zhang Yaohui, Deputy Secretary-General of the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee, and Lu Boming, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office.

    Mr. Wright, Counselor of the British Chargé d'Affaires Office in China, also saw him off at the airport.

### 蒙哥马利元帅回国

1960-05-29
第2版()
专栏：

    蒙哥马利元帅回国
    新华社广州28日电　英国蒙哥马利元帅今天上午从上海乘专机抵达广州，并转乘火车经深圳取道香港回国。
    前往车站欢送的有广州市市长朱光，以及陪同蒙哥马利元帅到广州的中国人民国防体育协会主任李达上将，国家体育运动委员会副主任荣高棠等。
    前往车站欢送的还有广州市副市长梁湘，中国人民解放军驻广州部队首长萧元礼少将，刘兴隆少将，黄一平少将，齐勇海军少将，张西三空军少将，广东省人民委员会外事处处长王锦川，广东省国防体育协会副主任卢承章等。
    新华社上海28日电　英国蒙哥马利元帅今天早晨由中国人民国防体育协会主任李达上将、体育运动委员会副主任荣高棠陪同，乘飞机离开上海前往广州。
    到机场欢送的有，上海市副市长曹荻秋，上海市国防体育协会主任、中国人民解放军上海警备区副司令员熊应堂少将，上海市人民委员会副秘书长张耀辉、外事处处长卢伯明等。
    英国驻中国代办处驻上海参赞赖伟德也到机场送行。
