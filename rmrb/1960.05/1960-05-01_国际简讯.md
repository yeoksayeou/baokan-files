International Briefs
    U.S. Living Costs Rise in March
    Washington Dispatch: The U.S. Labor Department announced on the 25th that the U.S. living cost index for March broke the February record and reached 125.7% of the average consumer price index from 1947 to 1949.
    Iranian City of Lar Destroyed by Earthquake
    Tehran Dispatch: The city of Lar, near the Persian Gulf in southern Iran, was hit by a major earthquake on the 24th. According to a spokesman for the Iranian Red Lion and Sun Society, 97% of the city has been destroyed.

### 国际简讯

1960-05-01

专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
  美国三月份生活费用上涨
    华盛顿讯　美国劳工部25日宣布，美国3月份的生活费用指数打破了2月份的最高纪录，而达到1947年到1949年的消费价格平均指数的125.7％。
    伊朗拉尔城毁于地震
    德黑兰讯　伊朗南部波斯湾附近的拉尔城在24日发生了大地震。据伊朗红狮子与太阳会发言人说，这个城市的97％已被摧毁。
