Title: More Than Two Thousand Soviet Advanced Producers Awarded
Author:
Date: 1960-05-31
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 28th (TASS): More than two thousand advanced Soviet producers were awarded the "Hammer and Sickle" gold medal, the Order of Lenin, the "Labor Valor" medal, and the "Labor Excellence" medal by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR. The award recipients are all advanced producers who have made outstanding achievements in production and have demonstrated pioneering spirit in the competition for the title of Communist Labor Brigade and Shock Worker. The award ceremony for advanced individuals in the competition for the title of Communist Labor Brigade and Shock Worker was held on the morning of the 28th. The awards were presented by A.A. Brezhnev, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, and others.

### 苏联两千多名先进生产者获奖

1960-05-31
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联两千多名先进生产者获奖
    新华社28日讯　塔斯社莫斯科28日讯：苏联两千多名先进生产者荣获苏联最高苏维埃主席团奖给的“镰刀和锤子”金质奖章、列宁勋章、“劳动英勇”奖章和“劳动出色”奖章。获奖者都是生产成绩卓著和在争取共产主义劳动队和突击手称号的竞赛中表现了首创精神的先进生产者。争取共产主义劳动队和突击手称号竞赛先进者会议于28日上午举行了授奖仪式，主持发奖的人是苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席勃列日涅夫等人。
