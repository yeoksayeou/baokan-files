Title: Prestes, General Secretary of the Brazilian Communist Party, Writes to Celebrate International Labor Day on "May Day"
Author: 
Date: 1960-05-03
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Rio de Janeiro, 29th -- Prestes, General Secretary of the Brazilian Communist Party, said in an article published in the "New Orientation" weekly today celebrating International Labor Day on "May Day" that the Brazilian workers' movement will make more contributions to the solidarity of the international proletariat.
    In his article, Prestes talked about the victories that Brazilian workers have achieved and the unity between them. Prestes said, "In celebrating 'May Day,' this day of solidarity, workers have protested against the racial massacres in the Union of South Africa, protested against the bloody rule in South Korea, and expressed their support for the fraternal Cuban people and the Fidel Castro revolutionary government. Workers will also make every effort to strive for positive results from the upcoming summit conference, so that international tensions can be further eased."

### 巴西共产党总书记普列斯特斯撰文  庆祝“五一”国际劳动节

1960-05-03
第6版()
专栏：

    巴西共产党总书记普列斯特斯撰文
    庆祝“五一”国际劳动节
    新华社29日讯　里约热内卢消息：巴西共产党总书记普列斯特斯今天在“新方针”周刊上发表的庆祝“五一”国际劳动节的文章中说，巴西工人运动将对国际无产者的团结作出更多的贡献。
    普列斯特斯在文章中谈到巴西劳动者已经取得的胜利和他们之间的团结。普列斯特斯说，“在庆祝‘五一’这个团结日的时候，劳动者纷纷抗议南非联邦的种族屠杀，抗议南朝鲜的血腥统治，并且表示支持兄弟的古巴人民和菲德尔·卡斯特罗革命政府。劳动者还将作出一切努力，以争取即将召开的最高级会议获得积极的成果，使国际紧张局势得到进一步的缓和。”
