Title: Ambassador Ke Hua Lays Wreath at Guinean Martyrs Monument
Author:
Date: 1960-05-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, May 20th: Chinese Ambassador to Guinea, Ke Hua, accompanied by Sana, Acting Chief of Protocol of the Guinean Ministry of Foreign Affairs, laid a wreath at the Guinean Martyrs Monument in Deboff on the morning of May 20th at 10:00 am. The inscription on the wreath read: "Dedicated to all the martyrs who sacrificed themselves in Guinea's struggle against colonialism."

### 柯华大使向几内亚烈士纪念碑献花圈

1960-05-23
第5版()
专栏：

    柯华大使向几内亚烈士纪念碑献花圈
    据新华社科纳克里20日电　中国驻几内亚大使柯华20日上午十时在几内亚外交部代理礼宾司司长萨纳的陪同下，前往德鲍夫的几内亚烈士纪念碑献花圈。花圈上写着：“献给几内亚反对殖民主义斗争中牺牲的全体烈士”。
