Title: At the Invitation of the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee, Two Kenyan Guests Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-21
Page: 6

    At the Invitation of the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee, Two Kenyan Guests Arrive in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, 19th - Odhiambo Okelo, Director of the Kenyan Office in Cairo, and Abdalla Hassan Bashir, a member of the office, arrived in Beijing this afternoon. They are visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee.

### 应我国亚非团结委员会邀请  两位怯尼亚客人到京

1960-05-21
第6版()
专栏：

    应我国亚非团结委员会邀请
    两位怯尼亚客人到京
    新华社19日讯　怯尼亚驻开罗办事处主任奥迪安博·奥凯洛和办事处成员阿卜达拉·哈桑·巴希尔，今天下午到达北京。他们是应中国亚非团结委员会的邀请前来我国进行访问的。
