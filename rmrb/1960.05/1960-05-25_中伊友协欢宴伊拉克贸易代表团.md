Title: Sino-Iraqi Friendship Association Hosts Banquet for Iraqi Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-05-25
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, May 24th: The China-Iraq Friendship Association hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the Iraqi government trade delegation. Luo Jun, Vice President of the China-Iraq Friendship Association, and Abdul Latif al-Shawwaf, head of the Iraqi government trade delegation, spoke at the banquet, wishing the friendly relations between the Chinese and Iraqi people would continue to develop.

Those attending the banquet included Lu Xuzhang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Sa Kongliao, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission; Zhou Erfu, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Gong Dafei, Deputy Director of the West Asian and African Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Beijing directors of the China-Iraq Friendship Association. The Iraqi Ambassador to China, Fadil, was also invited to attend the banquet.

### 中伊友协欢宴伊拉克贸易代表团

1960-05-25
第4版()
专栏：

    中伊友协欢宴伊拉克贸易代表团
    新华社24日讯　中国伊拉克友好协会今晚设宴欢迎伊拉克政府贸易代表团。中国伊拉克友好协会副会长罗俊和伊拉克政府贸易代表团团长阿卜杜勒·拉蒂夫·沙瓦夫在宴会上先后讲话，互祝中伊两国人民的友好关系日益发展。
    参加宴会的有对外贸易部副部长卢绪章，民族事务委员会副主任萨空了，中国人民对外文化协会秘书长周而复，外交部西亚非洲司副司长宫达非和中伊友协在京理事等。伊拉克驻华大使法迪尔也应邀参加了宴会。
