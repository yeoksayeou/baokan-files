Title: Nuclear Test Ban Conference Resumes
Author:
Date: 1960-05-29
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 28th. The conference of the Soviet Union, the United States and Britain on the cessation of nuclear weapons tests resumed in Geneva on the 27th after a short recess.

### 不继续核试验会议复会

1960-05-29
第3版()
专栏：

    不继续核试验会议复会
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社日内瓦讯：苏、美、英三国不继续核武器试验会议在经过短时期的休会以后27日在日内瓦复会。
