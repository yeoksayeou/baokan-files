Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions and All-China Women's Federation Send Telegrams Supporting the Struggle of the People of the Congo and Northern Rhodesia

Author: 
Date: 1960-05-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 13th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions today sent telegrams to the Congolese National Workers' Union and the Northern Rhodesian Trade Union Congress, supporting their struggle against colonialism.

In its telegram to the Congolese National Workers' Union, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions said: "The Chinese working class and people express their highest respect and firm support for the Congolese people's demonstrations against the Belgian colonialists' divisive conspiracies and the heroic struggle of the workers' strike in the Elisabethville area. Imperialism and colonialism have been opposed by the people of the world. As long as the Congolese people further unite and persist in the struggle, they will surely achieve complete and thorough independence."

In its telegram to the Northern Rhodesian Trade Union Congress and the workers in the Ndola Kitwe copper mining area, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions stated that the Chinese working class and people are extremely angry at and strongly protest the British colonialists' unreasonable suppression and arrest of Northern Rhodesian workers and people. The telegram stated: "The Chinese people fully sympathize with and support the struggle of African workers and people against imperialism. We are convinced that the people of all countries, united in a common struggle, will achieve final victory."

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. The All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China today sent telegrams to the people of the Congo and Northern Rhodesia, resolutely supporting their anti-colonialist struggle.

In its telegram to the Congolese people, the All-China Women's Federation strongly protested and severely condemned the Belgian colonial authorities' frantic suppression of the Congolese people. The telegram stated: "The days of the total collapse of colonial rule in Africa are not far off. As long as the Congolese people persist in their unremitting struggle, they will surely achieve the final victory in their struggle against colonialism and for national independence."

In its telegram to the people of Northern Rhodesia, the All-China Women's Federation expressed its firm support for the just struggle of the people and women of Northern Rhodesia against colonialism and for national independence, and expressed its belief that the people of Northern Rhodesia, like the people of other African countries, will certainly be able to drive out imperialism and achieve the final victory in the struggle for national independence and freedom.

### 我国全总和妇联发出电报  支持刚果和北罗得西亚人民的斗争

1960-05-16
第4版()
专栏：

    我国全总和妇联发出电报
    支持刚果和北罗得西亚人民的斗争
    新华社13日讯　中华全国总工会今天分别打电报给刚果全国工人联合会和北罗得西亚工会大会，支持他们反对殖民主义的斗争。
    全总在致刚果全国工人联合会的电报中说：“中国工人阶级和人民对刚果人民反对比利时殖民者分化阴谋的示威和伊利沙伯维尔地区工人罢工的英勇斗争表示崇高的敬意和坚决的支持，帝国主义和殖民主义已遭受全世界人民的反对，只要刚果人民进一步团结起来、坚持斗争，一定会取得完全的彻底的独立。”
    全总在致北罗得西亚工会大会和恩多拉基特威铜矿地区工人的电报中说，中国工人阶级和人民对英国殖民主义者无理镇压和逮捕北罗得西亚工人和人民的野蛮行为表示非常愤怒并提出严重抗议。电报说：“中国人民完全同情和支持非洲工人和人民反对帝国主义的斗争。确信在共同斗争中团结起来的各国人民一定会取得最后的胜利。”
    新华社14日讯　中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会今天分别打电报给刚果和北罗得西亚的人民，坚决支持他们的反殖民主义斗争。
    全国妇联在致刚果人民的电报中强烈抗议并严厉谴责了比利时殖民当局对刚果人民的疯狂镇压。电报说：“殖民统治在非洲总崩溃的日子已经不远了，只要刚果人民坚持不懈地斗争，一定会取得反对殖民主义争取民族独立的最后胜利。”
    全国妇联在致北罗得西亚人民的电报中，表示坚决支持北罗得西亚人民和妇女反对殖民主义争取民族独立的正义斗争，并且相信北罗得西亚人民将和非洲各国人民一样，一定能够把帝国主义赶出去，取得争取民族独立和自由的斗争的最后胜利。
