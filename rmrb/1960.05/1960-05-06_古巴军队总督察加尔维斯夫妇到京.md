Title: Cuban Army Inspector General Gálvez and Wife Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, May 5th: Cuban Republic Army Inspector General Gálvez and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane today. They are in China for a friendly visit at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General, and Yao Zhen, Vice President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association.

### 古巴军队总督察加尔维斯夫妇到京

1960-05-06
第6版()
专栏：

    古巴军队总督察加尔维斯夫妇到京
    新华社5日讯　古巴共和国军队总督察加尔维斯和他的夫人，在今天乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中国人民外交学会的邀请来我国进行友好访问的。
    到机场欢迎的，有中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之，副秘书长吴晓达，中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长姚溱等。
