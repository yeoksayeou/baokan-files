Title: Indian National Congress President Reddy Again Slanders Our Country with "Aggression"
Author:
Date: 1960-05-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 20th: According to the Press Trust of India, Indian National Congress President Reddy, speaking at a rally in Kurd, Orissa on the evening of the 17th, once again slandered China. He incited that defending India's northern borders against "Chinese aggression" was not a matter for any political party, but a matter for the entire nation.

Mohammad Ibrahim, Chairman of the Central Special Executive Committee of the All-India Muslim League, also repeated the slanderous remarks about "Chinese encroachment on Indian borders" in Madras on the 17th. He absurdly said that "the Chinese threat to India is increasing."

### 印度国大党主席雷迪又诬蔑我国“侵略”

1960-05-22
第5版()
专栏：

    印度国大党主席雷迪又诬蔑我国“侵略”
    新华社新德里20日电　据印度报业托辣斯报道，印度国大党主席雷迪17日晚上在奥里萨邦库尔德路德的一次集会上讲话时，再次诬蔑中国。他煽动说，保卫印度北部边界对付“中国侵略”不是任何政党的问题，而是整个民族的问题。
    印度南部穆斯林代表大会中央特别执行委员会主席穆罕默德·易卜拉欣17日在马德拉斯也重复“中国人侵犯印度边界”的谰言。他十分荒唐地说，“中国人对印度的威胁越来越大”。
