Title: To the Miners of Miike
Author: Tóng Sōng
Date: 1960-05-28
Page: 8

To the Miners of Miike

Comrades miners of Miike, you stand on the front line of the battle. You are developing the fire from underground, igniting endless heat. Your indignation is like the coal mine explosives, roaring at the Kishi Nobusuke clique! Wearing willow helmets and headlamps, fighting against the violent police... When one Kubo Kiyoshi falls, thousands upon thousands of Japanese people stand up, determined to fight until final victory in the battle that decides the fate of the Japanese people. Chinese miners are your rearguard, united as iron and steel. Fight on, people of Japan, fight on, comrades miners of Miike!

### 寄给三池的矿工
童松
1960-05-28
第8版()
专栏：

    寄给三池的矿工
    辽宁抚顺　童松三池的矿工兄弟们，你们站在战斗的前线。你们在开发地下的火种，把无穷的热力点燃。你们的义愤如同采掘的煤炮，在向岸信介集团怒吼！头戴着柳盔和头灯，在和行凶的警察搏斗……一个久保清倒下去了，千千万万的日本人民站起来了，决定日本人民命运的战斗，不获得最后胜利决不罢休。中国矿工是你们的后盾，如铁如钢团结在一起。战斗吧，日本的人民，战斗吧，三池的矿工兄弟！
