Title: Chinese Communist Party Delegation Holds Talks with Jędrychowski
Author:
Date: 1960-05-26
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, May 24th: The Chinese Communist Party delegation headed by Bo Yibo held talks on the 24th with Jędrychowski, Chairman of the Polish State Planning Commission and member of the Political Bureau of the Polish United Workers' Party Central Committee.

Participating in the talks on the Polish side were Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers Jaroszewicz and First Vice-Chairman of the State Planning Commission Gede, among others. On the Chinese side were Li Baohua and Liu Xiufeng. The Chinese Ambassador to Poland, Wang Bingnan, was also present.

The talks were conducted in a friendly atmosphere, and both sides exchanged views on further strengthening economic cooperation between China and Poland.

### 中共代表团同英德里霍夫斯基会谈

1960-05-26
第2版()
专栏：

    中共代表团同英德里霍夫斯基会谈
    新华社华沙24日电　以薄一波为首的中国共产党代表团，24日和波兰国家计划委员会主席、波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员英德里霍夫斯基举行了会谈。
    参加会谈的，波兰方面有部长会议副主席雅罗谢维奇、国家计划委员会第一副主席盖德等；中国方面有李葆华、刘秀峰。中国驻波兰大使王炳南也在座。
    会谈是在友好的气氛中进行的，双方就进一步加强中波两国的经济合作问题交换了意见。
