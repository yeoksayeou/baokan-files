Title: Soviet Kazakh Republic Men's Basketball Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-09
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, 8th -- The Soviet Kazakh Republic Institute of Physical Culture men's basketball team, a group of twelve led by the institute's president Mukhamedyanov, arrived in Beijing by plane today. They will play their first friendly match in China against the Beijing Youth Men's Basketball Team on the 11th in Beijing.
    The visiting team will play a total of eight friendly matches with Chinese basketball teams in Beijing, Tianjin, Shenyang and other places. The other three matches in Beijing are scheduled as follows:
    12th against the Chinese People's Liberation Army "August 1st" Team;
    14th against the Beijing Team;
    15th against the Chinese "Pioneer" Sports Association Team.

### 苏联哈萨克共和国男子篮球队到京

1960-05-09
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联哈萨克共和国男子篮球队到京
    据新华社8日讯　苏联哈萨克共和国体育学院男子篮球队一行十二人，今天由这个学院的院长穆哈麦江诺夫率领，乘飞机到达北京。他们将于11日在北京同北京青年男子篮球队举行访华第一场友谊比赛。
    客队这次将在北京、天津、沈阳等地，同我国的篮球队共举行八场友谊比赛。在北京的其他三场比赛确定为：
    12日对中国人民解放军“八一”队；
    14日对北京队；
    15日对中国“前卫”体育协会队。
