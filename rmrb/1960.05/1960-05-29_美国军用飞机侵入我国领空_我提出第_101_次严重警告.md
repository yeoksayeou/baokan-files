Title: U.S. Military Aircraft Intrudes into China's Airspace; I Issue the 101st Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-05-29
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, May 28th: On May 28th, from 10:05 to 10:20, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over the Shantou to Daya Bay area of Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. military aircraft, a spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 101st serious warning.

### 美国军用飞机侵入我国领空  我提出第101次严重警告

1960-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

    美国军用飞机侵入我国领空
    我提出第101次严重警告
    新华社28日讯　5月28日十时零五分至十时二十分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省汕尾至大亚湾地区领空，对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅行为，我外交部发言人奉命提出第一百零一次严重警告。
