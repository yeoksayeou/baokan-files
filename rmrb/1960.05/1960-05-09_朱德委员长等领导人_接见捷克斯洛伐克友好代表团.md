Title: Chairman Zhu De and Other Leaders Receive Czechoslovakian Friendship Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-05-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, May 8th: Chairman Zhu De, Vice Chairman Peng Zhen, and Vice Premier He Long received all members of the Czechoslovakian Friendship Delegation headed by Chairman Josef Opatovsky this evening. The hosts and guests engaged in a cordial and friendly conversation.

Present at the reception were Zhang Xiruo, Xia Yan, Luo Guibo, Zhang Zhixiang, and others.

Also present at the reception was the Czechoslovakian Ambassador to China, Bušiňák. (Attached picture)
	Chairman Zhu De receives Josef Opatovsky, the head of the Czechoslovakian Friendship Delegation, and all members of the delegation. The picture shows the scene at the reception. The first person on the left is Josef Opatovsky, and the first person on the right is Ambassador Bušiňák. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Changyong)

### 朱德委员长等领导人  接见捷克斯洛伐克友好代表团

1960-05-09
第6版()
专栏：

    朱德委员长等领导人
    接见捷克斯洛伐克友好代表团
    新华社8日讯　朱德委员长、彭真副委员长和贺龙副总理今天晚上接见了以约瑟夫·奥巴夫斯基团长为首的捷克斯洛伐克友好代表团全体成员，宾主进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有张奚若、夏衍、罗贵波、张致祥等。
    接见时，捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使布希尼亚克也在座。（附图片）
