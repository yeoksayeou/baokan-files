Title: Mourning the Great Warrior of the Chinese People: All Circles in the Capital Pay Respects to Comrade Lin Boqu; Zhu De, Dong Biwu, and More Than 10,000 People from All Walks of Life Offer Condolences at Comrade Lin Boqu's Funeral Hall
Author:
Date: 1960-05-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 30th: More than 10,000 people from all walks of life in the capital went to the Working People's Cultural Palace today to mourn Comrade Lin Boqu, the great warrior of the Chinese people.

    Comrade Lin Boqu's funeral hall was set up in the main hall of the Working People's Cultural Palace. A white cloth banner hung in the center of the hall, displaying Comrade Lin Boqu's portrait. Black gauze draped down on both sides of the portrait, and evergreen potted pines were placed below. In front of the portrait on the altar rested Comrade Lin Boqu's ashes, covered with the flag of the Chinese Communist Party. The hall was filled with wreaths inside and out.

    Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Dong Biwu, Member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and others came to the Working People's Cultural Palace today to pay their respects before Comrade Lin Boqu's portrait.

    Starting at 3 p.m., responsible persons and cadres from various departments of the CPC Central Committee, state organs, people's organizations, and democratic parties, officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, responsible persons and cadres from Beijing, as well as workers, peasants, and students, all wearing black纱 and white paper flowers, silently walked past Comrade Lin Boqu's portrait amid mournful music. They mourned Comrade Lin Boqu, who was infinitely loyal to the Party and the people throughout his life and dedicated his life's energy to the great cause of communism.

    During the mourning period, some members and alternate members of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, responsible persons from various departments of the Party Central Committee and state organs, some members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, senior officers and generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, responsible persons from the Party and government of Beijing, and responsible persons from Party and government of higher education institutions in Beijing, took turns keeping vigil.

### 悼念中国人民的伟大战士  首都各界吊唁林伯渠同志  朱德、董必武和各界人民一万多人到林伯渠同志灵堂致哀

1960-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

    悼念中国人民的伟大战士
    首都各界吊唁林伯渠同志
    朱德、董必武和各界人民一万多人到林伯渠同志灵堂致哀
    新华社30日讯　首都各界人民一万多人，今天前往劳动人民文化宫，吊唁中国人民的伟大战士林伯渠同志。
    林伯渠同志灵堂设在劳动人民文化宫大殿。灵堂正中的白色布幔上，悬挂着林伯渠同志的遗像，遗像两旁垂着黑纱，下面布满了常青的盆松。遗像前灵台上安置着林伯渠同志的骨灰盒，上面复盖着中国共产党党旗；灵堂内外放满了花圈。
    中共中央副主席、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德，中共中央政治局委员、中华人民共和国副主席董必武等，今天来到劳动人民文化宫，在林伯渠同志遗像前致哀。
    从下午三时开始，中共中央各部门、国家机关各部门、各人民团体、各民主党派的负责人和机关干部，中国人民解放军的军官和士兵，北京市的负责人和机关干部以及工人、农民和学生，都佩戴着黑纱、白色纸花，在哀乐声中，默默地从林伯渠同志的遗像前走过，悼念一生对党、对人民无限忠诚，把毕生的精力贡献于伟大的共产主义事业的林伯渠同志。
    在吊唁的时候，中国共产党中央委员会的部分委员和候补委员，党中央机关各部门和国家机关各部门的负责人，全国人民代表大会常委会和政协全国委员会常委会的部分委员，中国人民解放军的高级军官和将军们，北京市党、政负责人，北京市高等院校的党政负责人，先后轮流守灵。
