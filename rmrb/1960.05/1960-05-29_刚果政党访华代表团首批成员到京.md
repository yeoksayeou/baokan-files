Title: First Group of Congolese Political Party Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, May 28th—The first group of members of the Congolese political party delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. They were led by Alphonse Makwambala, Chairman of the Political Bureau of the People's Party, and Arthur Mayamba, General Chairman of the Bayanzi Alliance.

The Congolese political party delegation includes key figures from nationalist parties such as the People's Party, the Congolese General Alliance, the African Solidarity Center, and the Bayanzi Alliance. The head of the delegation and other members will arrive in Beijing soon.

### 刚果政党访华代表团首批成员到京

1960-05-29
第3版()
专栏：

    刚果政党访华代表团首批成员到京
    据新华社28日讯　应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的刚果政党访华代表团首批人员，由人民党政治局主席阿方斯·马克万巴拉，巴阳齐联盟总主席阿图尔·玛扬巴率领下，于今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    刚果政党访华代表团包括人民党、刚果总联盟、非洲团结中心、巴阳齐联盟等民族主义政党的主要负责人。访华代表团的团长和其他成员，将在最近到达北京。
