Title: Albanian Labor Youth League Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 9th: At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, the Albanian Labor Youth League delegation, led by Jolekia Bremti, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Youth League, arrived in Beijing on the morning of the 8th for a visit to our country.

Liang Buting, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and Parifti, the Albanian Ambassador to China, and others welcomed the delegation at the airport.

That evening, the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League held a welcoming banquet for the delegation. Luo Yi, Hu Keshi, and Liang Buting, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, attended the banquet.

### 阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟代表团到京

1960-05-10
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟代表团到京
    新华社9日讯　应中国共产主义青年团中央委员会邀请来我国访问的阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟代表团，在团长、阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟中央委员会书记处书记约勒基亚·布列姆提率领下，8日上午到达北京。
    共青团中央书记处书记梁步庭、阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使帕里夫蒂等到机场欢迎代表团。
    当晚，共青团中央书记处为代表团举行了欢迎宴会。共青团中央书记处书记罗毅、胡克实、梁步庭等参加了宴会。
