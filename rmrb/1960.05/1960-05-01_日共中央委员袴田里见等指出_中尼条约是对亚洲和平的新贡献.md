Title: JCP Central Committee Member Satomi Hakamada and Others Point Out That the Sino-Nepalese Treaty Is a New Contribution to Asian Peace
Author:
Date: 1960-05-01
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 30th. Japanese Communist Party Central Committee member Satomi Hakamada and Japan Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee representative Kenzo Nakajima told Japanese reporters today that the signing of the Sino-Nepalese Treaty of Peace and Friendship is another example of implementing the Bandung spirit.
    Hakamada Satomi said that the Sino-Nepalese Treaty of Peace and Friendship is a new contribution to Asian peace following the Sino-Burmese Treaty of Friendship and Mutual Non-Aggression signed in January this year. He concluded by saying that the Japanese people will support the Chinese government's recent proposal for countries in the Asian and Pacific region to conclude a peace pact through actions against the Diet's ratification of the new Japan-U.S. "Security Treaty."
    Kenzo Nakajima emphasized that the Sino-Nepalese Treaty of Peace and Friendship has set a brilliant example for resolving all international disputes in Asia, and has taken an important step forward in the work of establishing a peace zone including the entire Asia and Africa.

### 日共中央委员袴田里见等指出  中尼条约是对亚洲和平的新贡献

1960-05-01
第5版()
专栏：

    日共中央委员袴田里见等指出
    中尼条约是对亚洲和平的新贡献
    新华社30日讯　东京消息：日本共产党中央委员会委员袴田里见和日本亚非团结委员会代表委员中岛健藏今天对日本记者说，中尼和平友好条约的签订，是贯彻万隆精神的又一范例。
    袴田里见说，中尼和平友好条约是继今年1月签订的中缅友好和互不侵犯条约之后对亚洲和平的新贡献。他最后说，日本人民将用反对国会批准新的日美“安全条约”的行动来支持中国政府最近提出的亚洲和太平洋地区各国缔结和平公约的建议。
    中岛健藏强调说，中尼和平友好条约为解决亚洲的一切国际争端树立了光辉的榜样，并且在建立包括整个亚非两洲在内的和平地区的工作方面向前迈进了重要的一步。
