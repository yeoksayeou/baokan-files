Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Hosts Banquet in Honor of Premier Zhou

Author:

Date: 1960-05-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 13th: Chairman Ho Chi Minh hosted a banquet at the Presidential Palace tonight to welcome Premier Zhou Enlai.

Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu, all accompanying Chinese comrades, and Comrade He Wei, the Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam, were invited to attend.

Vietnamese Party and government leaders present included Comrades Le Duan, Pham Van Dong, Truong Chinh, Le Duc Tho, Pham Hung, Nguyen Chi Thanh, Hoang Van Hoan, Nguyen Duy Trinh, Le Thanh Nghi, and Ung Van Khiem. The hosts and guests chatted and laughed heartily like one big family. After the banquet, the hosts and guests enjoyed a wonderful artistic performance together.

### 胡志明主席设宴欢迎周总理

1960-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席设宴欢迎周总理
    新华社河内13日电　胡志明主席今晚在主席府设宴欢迎周恩来总理。
    陈毅副总理、章汉夫副部长和随行的全体中国同志以及中国驻越南大使何伟等同志，都应邀出席。
  出席作陪的越南党政领导同志，有黎笋、范文同、长征、黎德寿、范雄、阮志清、黄文欢、阮维祯、黎清毅、雍文谦等同志。宾主就像一家人那样尽情地谈笑。宴会以后，宾主一起欣赏了精彩的艺术表演。
