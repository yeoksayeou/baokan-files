Title: Mongolian and Romanian Basketball Teams Compete Against Chinese Teams in Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1960-05-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, May 1st. The Mongolian People's Republic Public Security Men's Basketball Team and the Romanian "Dinamo" Men's Basketball Team played friendly matches against the Hubei "Avant-garde" Team and the Hubei Team respectively in Wuhan this afternoon.

　　Wuhan audiences, who are joyfully celebrating "May Day" International Labor Day, filled the Wuhan Gymnasium to watch these two games. The results of the matches were: the two visiting teams lost to the home teams with scores of 55 to 100 and 81 to 91 respectively.

　　The two visiting teams will leave Wuhan for Guangzhou on the 2nd.

### 蒙、罗篮球队在武汉同中国队比赛

1960-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙、罗篮球队在武汉同中国队比赛
    新华社武汉1日电　蒙古人民共和国公安男子篮球队和罗马尼亚“狄纳莫”男子篮球队，今天下午在武汉分别与湖北“前卫”队和湖北队进行友谊比赛。
    正在欢度“五一”国际劳动节的武汉观众坐满了武汉体育馆，观看这两场比赛。比赛结果是：两个客队分别以五十五比一百和八十一比九十一败给主队。
    两客队2日将离武汉赴广州。
