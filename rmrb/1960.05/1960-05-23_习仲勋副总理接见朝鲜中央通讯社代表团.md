Title: Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun Receives Delegation from the Korean Central News Agency
Author:
Date: 1960-05-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, May 21st – Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun received the delegation from the Korean Central News Agency, led by Ko Song-chun, this morning and had a cordial conversation with them.

Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd – The delegation from the Korean Central News Agency, which visited our country at the invitation of Xinhua News Agency, left Beijing by plane this morning to return to their country.

Xinhua News Agency and KCNA signed an agreement on the exchange of news on the afternoon of the 21st.

### 习仲勋副总理接见朝鲜中央通讯社代表团

1960-05-23
第5版()
专栏：

    习仲勋副总理接见朝鲜中央通讯社代表团
    新华社21日讯　习仲勋副总理今天上午接见了以高成淳为首的朝鲜中央通讯社代表团，并同他们作了亲切的交谈。
    新华社22日讯　应新华通讯社邀请来我国访问的朝鲜中央通讯社代表团今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    新华社和朝中社在21日下午签订了关于交换新闻的协定。
