Title: White House Press Secretary Announces Eisenhower's Visit to Japan Will Proceed as Planned
Author:
Date: 1960-05-25
Page: 3

White House Press Secretary Announces Eisenhower's Visit to Japan Will Proceed as Planned

(Reported by our newspaper) Despite the Japanese people's strong opposition to U.S. President Eisenhower's visit to Japan, the U.S. government has indicated that it is not prepared to change Eisenhower's planned visit to Japan. According to the U.S. Information Service, White House Press Secretary Hagerty said at a press conference on the 23rd that Eisenhower's plans to visit Japan and South Korea "will not be affected by the cancellation of his visit to the Soviet Union."

### 白宫新闻秘书宣布艾森豪威尔访日计划不变

1960-05-25
第3版()
专栏：

    白宫新闻秘书宣布艾森豪威尔访日计划不变
    本报讯　尽管日本人民坚决反对美国总统艾森豪威尔访问日本，美国政府仍然表示不准备改变艾森豪威尔的访日计划。据美国新闻处报道，白宫新闻秘书哈格蒂在23日举行的记者招待会上说，艾森豪威尔访问日本和南朝鲜的计划，“不会由于取消了他对苏联的访问而受到影响”。
