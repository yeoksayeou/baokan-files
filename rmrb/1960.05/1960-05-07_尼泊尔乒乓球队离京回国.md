Title: Nepalese Table Tennis Team Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-05-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th: The Nepalese table tennis team left Beijing by plane this morning to return home after a three-week visit to our country. Zhang Lianhua, Secretary-General of the All-China Sports Federation, and table tennis players from Beijing went to the airport to see them off.

Team leader Bandari, before leaving Beijing, spoke about his impressions of his visit to China. He said that during this visit, they participated in the "May 1st" International Labor Day celebrations, visited people's communes, factories, universities, and toured scenic spots and historical sites in various places. He said that all this left him with unforgettable impressions. He believes that China has provided various conditions for the development of sports, and China's sports, which have already achieved great achievements, will surely have further development.

### 尼泊尔乒乓球队离京回国

1960-05-07
第4版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔乒乓球队离京回国
    据新华社6日讯　尼泊尔乒乓球队在我国访问了三个星期以后，今晨乘飞机离北京回国。中华全国体育总会秘书长张联华和北京市的乒乓球选手曾到机场欢送他们。
    乒乓球队领队班达里在离北京前谈到这次访华观感时说，这次访问，他们参加了“五一”国际劳动节的庆祝活动，参观了人民公社、工厂、大学和游览了各地的名胜古迹。他说，这一切都给他留下了难以忘怀的印象。他认为，中国为发展体育运动提供了各种条件，中国已经取得巨大成就的体育运动一定会有更进一步的发展。
