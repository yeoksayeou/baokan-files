Title: Food Riot in Nagpur, India; Municipal Warehouse Burned Down
Author:
Date: 1960-05-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 6th - According to a report today from the Press Trust of India, crowds in Nagpur, formerly part of Bombay State, India, looted 1,700 bags of grain from five warehouses. A municipal warehouse storing goods worth 300,000 rupees was burned to the ground. Dozens of cloth shops were either burned or looted.

The news agency said that two post offices have been burned twice. Wholesale business in the city has not yet recovered.

### 印度那格浦尔发生抢粮事件  市政机构货仓被烧毁

1960-05-08
第5版()
专栏：

    印度那格浦尔发生抢粮事件
    市政机构货仓被烧毁
    新华社新德里6日电　据印度报业托辣斯今天报道，印度原属孟买邦的那格浦尔市群众从五个仓库里抢走了一千七百袋粮食。市政机构的一座存放三十万卢比货物的仓库被烧成灰烬。几十家布店有的被烧了，有的被抢了。
    这家通讯社说，有两家邮局已经两次被烧。这个城市的批发业务还没有恢复。
