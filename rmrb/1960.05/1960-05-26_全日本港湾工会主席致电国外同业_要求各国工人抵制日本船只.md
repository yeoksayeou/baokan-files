Title: Chairman of the All-Japan Harbor Workers' Union Calls Foreign Counterparts, Demanding Workers of All Countries Boycott Japanese Ships
Author:
Date: 1960-05-26
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, May 25th: Kenta Tomitaro, Chairman of the All-Japan Harbor Workers' Union, sent telegrams to relevant unions abroad this afternoon, requesting them not to load or unload Japanese ships in protest against the Kishi Nobusuke government and in support of the Japanese working class's struggle against the ratification of the new Japan-U.S. "Security Treaty."

The telegram pointed out that U.S. imperialism and Kishi Nobusuke are the common enemies of the people of all countries in Asia and the Pacific region.

### 全日本港湾工会主席致电国外同业  要求各国工人抵制日本船只

1960-05-26
第3版()
专栏：

    全日本港湾工会主席致电国外同业
    要求各国工人抵制日本船只
    新华社25日讯　东京消息：全日本港湾工会主席兼田富太郎今天下午打电报给国外的有关工会，要求它们不给日本船只装货和卸货，以抗议岸信介政府和支持日本工人阶级反对批准新日美“安全条约”的斗争。
    电报指出，美帝国主义和岸信介是亚洲和太平洋地区各国人民的共同敌人。
