Title: Premier Zhou Receives Iraqi Government Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-05-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 25th May. Premier Zhou Enlai received all members of the Iraqi Government Trade Delegation headed by Minister of Commerce Abdul Latif al-Shawwaf this afternoon and had a friendly conversation with them. Present at the reception were Lu Xuzhang, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade; Liu Xiwu, Director of the Fourth Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade; and He Ying, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. The Iraqi Ambassador to China, Fadil, was also present at the reception.

### 周总理接见伊拉克政府贸易代表团

1960-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见伊拉克政府贸易代表团
    新华社25日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了以商业部长阿卜杜勒·拉蒂夫·沙瓦夫为首的伊拉克政府贸易代表团的全体成员，并且同他们进行了友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有对外贸易部副部长卢绪章，对外贸易部第四局局长刘希文，外交部西亚非洲司司长何英。接见时，伊拉克驻中国大使法迪尔也在座。
