Title: Wu Lengxi Hosts Banquet for Albanian News Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-05-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Wu Lengxi, Editor-in-Chief of the People's Daily and Director of the Xinhua News Agency, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the Albanian News Delegation headed by Ajet Simixhiu, Director of the Albanian Telegraph Agency.

Present at the banquet was Muhamet, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Albanian Embassy in China.

Among those present were Zhu Muzhi, Deputy Director of the Xinhua News Agency, Hu Jiwei, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the People's Daily, Bao Zhijing, Director of the Press and Publication Department of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and leading members of the press circles in the capital.
