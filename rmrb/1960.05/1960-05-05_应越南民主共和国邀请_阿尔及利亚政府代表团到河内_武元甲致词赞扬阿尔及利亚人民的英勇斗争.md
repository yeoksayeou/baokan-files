Title: At the invitation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Algerian Government Delegation arrives in Hanoi. Vo Nguyen Giap delivers a speech praising the heroic struggle of the Algerian people.
Author: 
Date: 1960-05-05
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 4th May. The delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, led by Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Krim Belkacem, arrived in Hanoi by plane this morning. The delegation will visit Vietnam for three days at the invitation of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

When the plane carrying the delegation headed by Deputy Prime Minister Krim Belkacem slowly landed at the airport, which was decorated with the national flags of Algeria and Vietnam, Deputy Prime Ministers Vo Nguyen Giap and Phan Ke Toai of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly Ton Duc Thang, and Deputy Chairman Hoang Van Hoan stepped forward to welcome the distinguished guests.

In his welcoming speech at the airport, Vo Nguyen Giap warmly praised the heroic spirit shown by the brave Algerian people in their struggle against French imperialism, which is supported by US imperialism and the North Atlantic bloc. Vo Nguyen Giap said that the Vietnamese people are always watching the struggle of the Algerian people and are extremely excited about the victory of the Algerian people. He said that Algeria will surely be independent.

Vo Nguyen Giap expressed his belief that the visit of the delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria will further strengthen the close friendship between the two peoples and contribute to the unity of the people of Asian and African countries, the struggle for national independence, and the cause of defending world peace.

In his speech, Deputy Prime Minister Krim Belkacem said that Vietnam and Algeria have the same experience and have also carried out heroic struggles against the French colonialists. He said that the Vietnamese and Algerian people have made decisive contributions in the struggle against French colonialism. He said that this is the glory of the Vietnamese and Algerian people.

The speeches of Vo Nguyen Giap and Krim Belkacem were constantly interrupted by the warm applause of the welcoming masses at the airport.

Among those who went to the airport to welcome the Algerian delegation were ministers and deputy ministers of government departments, heads of people's organizations, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

From Gia Lam Airport to the State Guest House, tens of thousands of Hanoi citizens lined the streets holding the national flags of Vietnam and Algeria, warmly welcoming the distinguished Algerian guests.

### 应越南民主共和国邀请  阿尔及利亚政府代表团到河内  武元甲致词赞扬阿尔及利亚人民的英勇斗争

1960-05-05
第6版()
专栏：

    应越南民主共和国邀请
    阿尔及利亚政府代表团到河内
    武元甲致词赞扬阿尔及利亚人民的英勇斗争
    新华社河内4日电　由克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆副总理兼外交部长率领的阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团今天上午乘飞机到达河内。代表团应越南民主共和国政府的邀请，将在越南访问三天。
    当以克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆副总理为首的代表团乘坐的飞机徐徐降落在挂着阿尔及利亚和越南两国国旗的机场上时，越南民主共和国政府副总理武元甲、潘继遂，国会常务委员会委员长孙德胜、副委员长黄文欢等走上前去欢迎贵宾。
    武元甲在机场上致欢迎词时，热烈赞扬英勇的阿尔及利亚人民在反抗受到美帝国主义和北大西洋集团支持的法国帝国主义的斗争中所表现的英勇精神。武元甲说，越南人民时刻都在注视着阿尔及利亚人民的斗争，对阿尔及利亚人民的胜利感到无比的兴奋。他说，阿尔及利亚一定会独立。
    武元甲表示相信，阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团的访问，将进一步加强两国人民之间的亲密友谊，为亚非各国人民的团结、为民族独立斗争和保卫世界和平事业作出贡献。
    克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆副总理在讲话时说，越南和阿尔及利亚有着相同的遭遇，同样对法国殖民者进行了英勇的斗争。他说，越南人民和阿尔及利亚人民在反对法国殖民主义的斗争中作出了决定性的贡献。他说，这是越阿两国人民的光荣。
    武元甲和克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆的讲话，不断被机场上欢迎群众的热烈掌声所打断。
    前往机场欢迎阿尔及利亚代表团的还有政府各部正副部长、人民团体负责人以及各国驻越南民主共和国的外交使节等。
    从嘉林机场到迎宾馆，河内市民成万人手持越、阿两国国旗排列在道路两旁，热烈欢迎阿尔及利亚贵宾。
