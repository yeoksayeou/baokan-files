Title: Foreign Trade Union Delegations and Representatives Arrive in Beijing to Attend China's "May Day" Celebrations Upon Invitation
Author:
Date: 1960-05-02
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, May 1st – The Albanian Trade Union Delegation headed by Fangjel Meheli, member of the Presidium of the Central Council of Albanian Trade Unions; the All-India Trade Union Congress Delegation headed by Mrs. Krishnan, Vice-President of the All-India Trade Union Congress of India; the Chilean Workers' United Trade Union Delegation headed by Balas Celestino, National Councilor of the Chilean Workers' United Trade Union and Secretary of the Railway Workers' Union, and Portugarl Linalco; the Madagascar Agricultural and Livestock Union Delegation headed by Lasarasaka; Fall Aliou, representative of the Rural Development Mutual Company Trade Union of the Republic of Senegal; and Muhammad Marzuki, Deputy Head of the Indonesian Workers' Delegation and some of the delegation's representatives, arrived in Beijing by plane on April 30th. The Cameroon General Confederation of Labor Delegation headed by Monha Antoana, member of the Steering Committee of the Cameroon General Confederation of Labor, and the Iranian United Trade Union Central Council Delegation headed by Emir-Hsieh, arrived in Beijing by plane on May 1st. They were all invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to come to China to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations and visits. In addition, the Indonesian Railway Workers' Union Delegation headed by Rubis, First Deputy Secretary of the Indonesian Railway Workers' Union, which was invited by the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union to come to China to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations and visits, also arrived in Beijing by plane on April 30th.

### 应邀参加我国“五一”庆祝活动  一批外国工会代表团和代表到京

1960-05-02
第3版()
专栏：

    应邀参加我国“五一”庆祝活动
    一批外国工会代表团和代表到京
    新华社1日讯　以阿尔巴尼亚工会中央理事会主席团委员方捷尔·麦黑里为首的阿尔巴尼亚工会代表团、以全印工会大会副主席克里希南夫人为首的印度全印工会大会代表团、以智利工人统一工会全国理事、铁路工人工会书记巴拉斯·塞莱斯蒂诺，波尔图加耳·利纳尔科为首的智利工人统一工会代表团、以拉萨拉萨卡为首的马达加斯加农业和畜牧业工会代表团，塞内加尔共和国农村发展互助公司工会代表法尔·阿里乌和印度尼西亚工人代表团副团长穆罕默德·玛尔朱基以及代表团的部分代表，4月30日乘飞机到达北京。以喀麦隆劳工总联合会指导委员会委员蒙哈·安托阿纳为首的喀麦隆工人总联合会代表团，以艾米尔—歇兹为首的伊朗统一工会中央理事会代表团，在1日乘飞机到达北京。他们都是应中华全国总工会邀请，前来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝活动和访问的。此外应中国铁路工会全国委员会邀请，前来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝活动和访问的以印度尼西亚铁路工人工会第一副书记鲁比斯为首的印度尼西亚铁路工人工会代表团，也在4月30日乘飞机到达北京。
