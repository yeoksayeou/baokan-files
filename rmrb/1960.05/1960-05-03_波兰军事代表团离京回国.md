Title: Polish Military Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1960-05-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 2nd. The Polish Military Delegation, headed by General Bozhkovsky, concluded its visit to China and left Beijing by plane for home on the 2nd.

    Luo Ruiqing, Vice Premier of the State Council and Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, went to the airport to see the distinguished guests off. Members of the song and dance troupes of the Army, Navy, and Air Force presented flowers to the distinguished guests.

    General Bozhkovsky and all members of the delegation shook hands with those who came to see them off at the airport, reluctant to part. The Tu-104 plane carrying the guests took off at 7 o'clock in the bright sunshine.

    Among those who saw the distinguished guests off at the airport were Senior General Wang Shusheng, Senior General Xiao Hua, Senior General Peng Shaohui, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General Qiu Huizuo, Lieutenant General Zhang Lingbin, Vice Admiral Liu Daosheng, Lieutenant General Liu Shaowen, Air Force Major General Wang Zhitao and other senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

    Vice Foreign Minister Luo Guibo also went to the airport to see them off.

    Also at the airport to see them off were Furato, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China, Lieutenant Colonel Shimanski, Acting Military Attaché, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to China.

### 波兰军事代表团离京回国

1960-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰军事代表团离京回国
    新华社2日讯　以博齐沃夫斯基上将为首的波兰军事代表团，结束在我国的参观访问，2日由北京乘飞机回国。
    国务院副总理、中国人民解放军总参谋长罗瑞卿大将到机场欢送贵宾。陆、海、空军文工团的团员们向贵宾们献了花。
    博齐沃夫斯基上将和代表团全体人员，在机场上同送行者握手，依依惜别。客人们乘坐的图——一○四飞机，七时在灿烂的阳光中起飞。
    到机场欢送贵宾的，还有王树声大将，萧华上将、彭绍辉上将、萧向荣中将，丘会作中将、张令彬中将，刘道生海军中将，刘少文中将、王志涛空军少将等中国人民解放军高级将领。
    外交部副部长罗贵波也到机场欢送。
    到机场欢送的还有波兰驻我国大使馆临时代办傅拉托、代理武官席曼斯基中校以及各国驻华外交官员。
