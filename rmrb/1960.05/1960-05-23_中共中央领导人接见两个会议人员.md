Title: Central Committee Leaders of the Chinese Communist Party Receive Personnel from Two Meetings
Author:
Date: 1960-05-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd: Zhu De and Lin Biao, Vice-Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; and Peng Zhen, Chen Yi, He Long, and Li Xiannian, members of the Political Bureau, this afternoon received the representatives attending the National Technical Innovation and Technical Revolution Performance Conference of the financial and trade departments, and all the personnel participating in the National Textile Industry Department and Bureau Chiefs Conference.

### 中共中央领导人接见两个会议人员

1960-05-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人接见两个会议人员
    新华社22日讯　中共中央副主席朱德、林彪，政治局委员彭真、陈毅、贺龙、李先念，今天下午接见了出席全国财贸部门技术革新和技术革命表演大会的代表，和参加纺织工业全国厅局长会议的全体人员。
