Title: Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of Albania, Haxhi Lleshi, to Visit Our Country
Author: 
Date: 1960-05-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 21st. Comrade Haxhi Lleshi, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania, will pay a friendly visit to the People's Republic of China soon at the invitation of Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China. (Attached picture)
    Right picture: Chairman Knud Jespersen (front left) walks out of the airport accompanied by Vice Chairman Zhu De.
          Xinhua News Agency reporter Xue Yin

### 阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团主席  哈奇·列希将来我国访问

1960-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

   阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团主席
   哈奇·列希将来我国访问
    新华社21日讯　阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团主席哈奇·列希同志，应中华人民共和国主席刘少奇同志的邀请，将于最近到中华人民共和国进行友好访问。（附图片）
    右图：克努德·耶斯佩森主席（前左）在朱德副主席陪同下步出机场。
          新华社记者　雪印摄
