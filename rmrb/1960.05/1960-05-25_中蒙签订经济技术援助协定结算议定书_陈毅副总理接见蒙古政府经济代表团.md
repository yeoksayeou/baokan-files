Title: China and Mongolia Sign Protocol on Settlement of Economic and Technical Assistance Agreement; Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Mongolian Government Economic Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-05-25
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, May 24th -- The governments of the People's Republic of China and the Mongolian People's Republic held negotiations in Beijing regarding the final settlement of the "Agreement on Economic and Technical Assistance from the People's Republic of China to the Mongolian People's Republic" signed on August 29, 1956. They reached a satisfactory agreement in a friendly atmosphere, and a signing ceremony for the final settlement protocol was held on the 23rd.

According to the provisions of the 1956 agreement on economic and technical assistance from the People's Republic of China to the Mongolian People's Republic, China had already helped the Mongolian People's Republic build seventeen projects, including a woolen textile factory, a glass factory, a thermal power plant, a paper mill, and water conservancy projects, and supplied various tools to the Mongolian People's Republic during the period from 1956 to 1959. The final settlement showed that the expenses incurred by China in fulfilling all the obligations stipulated in the above-mentioned agreement were offset by the amount of assistance stipulated in the agreement, leaving a balance. Both parties agreed that the Mongolian People's Republic would propose new projects within the balance, and China would continue to provide technical assistance.

Signing the protocol on behalf of the Chinese side was Li Qiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China; signing on behalf of the Mongolian side was Sharrab, head of the Mongolian Government Economic Delegation and Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China.

Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi attended the signing ceremony.

Also present at the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Liu Mingfu, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission, and others; on the Mongolian side were Tseven, member of the Mongolian Government Economic Delegation and Vice Chairman of the State Planning Commission, Aiden-Sureng, First Deputy Minister of Industry, Purbadordzhi, Director of the Bureau of the Committee for Foreign Economic Relations, and Dorzhi, Economic and Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the Mongolian People's Republic in China.

Xinhua News Agency, May 24th -- Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi received all members of the Mongolian Government Economic Delegation headed by Ambassador Sharrab on the afternoon of the 23rd and had a cordial and friendly conversation.

Present at the reception were Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, Li Qiang, Vice Minister, Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Liu Mingfu, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission, and others. Dorzhi, Economic and Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the Mongolian People's Republic in China, was also present at the reception.

### 中蒙签订经济技术援助协定结算议定书  陈毅副总理接见蒙古政府经济代表团

1960-05-25
第4版()
专栏：

    中蒙签订经济技术援助协定结算议定书
    陈毅副总理接见蒙古政府经济代表团
    新华社24日讯　中华人民共和国政府和蒙古人民共和国政府在北京进行了关于双方在1956年8月29日签订的“中华人民共和国给予蒙古人民共和国经济和技术援助的协定”最后结算的谈判，并且在友好的气氛中圆满达成协议，23日举行了最后结算议定书的签字仪式。
    根据1956年中华人民共和国给予蒙古人民共和国经济和技术援助的协定规定，中国已经在1956年至1959年期间帮助蒙古人民共和国建设了毛纺织厂、玻璃厂、热电站、造纸厂和水利工程等十七个工程项目，并供应了蒙古人民共和国各种工具。最后结算表明，中国为完成上述协定规定的全部义务所支出的费用与这个协定规定的援助金额相抵，尚有一部分余额。双方议定：蒙古人民共和国将在余额范围内提出新的项目，由中国继续提供技术援助。
    代表中国方面在议定书上签字的是中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长李强；代表蒙古方面在议定书上签字的是蒙古人民共和国政府经济代表团团长、蒙古人民共和国驻中国大使沙拉布。
    国务院副总理陈毅参加了签字仪式。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面还有：对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，国家计划委员会副主任刘明夫等；蒙古方面有：蒙古人民共和国政府经济代表团团员、国家计划委员会副主席策文，工业部第一副部长艾登苏伦，对外经济联络委员会局长普日布道吉以及蒙古人民共和国驻中国大使馆经济及商务参赞道尔吉。
    新华社24日讯　国务院副总理陈毅23日下午接见了以沙拉布大使为首的蒙古人民共和国政府经济代表团全体成员，并且进行了亲切友好的交谈。
    接见时在座的，有对外贸易部部长叶季壮，副部长李强，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，国家计划委员会副主任刘明夫等。接见时，蒙古人民共和国驻中国大使馆经济及商务参赞道尔吉也在座。
