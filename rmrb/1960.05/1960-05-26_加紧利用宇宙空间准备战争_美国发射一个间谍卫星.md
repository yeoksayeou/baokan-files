Title: Intensifying the Use of Outer Space to Prepare for War: U.S. Launches a Spy Satellite
Author:
Date: 1960-05-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 25th: Cape Canaveral (United States) News: In order to intensify its insidious aim of using outer space for espionage activities, the U.S. Air Force launched another "Midas II" military reconnaissance satellite on May 24. Two hours after the launch, they announced that the satellite had entered orbit around the Earth. However, due to the failure of the ground calculation center, the parameters of the satellite's orbit, such as perigee and apogee, could not be obtained for a long time. Previously, the U.S. Air Force launched the "Midas I" satellite for the same purpose on February 26, but it failed.

The "Midas II" satellite is equipped with reconnaissance instruments such as infrared rays and is specifically used to collect military intelligence.

### 加紧利用宇宙空间准备战争  美国发射一个间谍卫星

1960-05-26
第4版()
专栏：

    加紧利用宇宙空间准备战争
    美国发射一个间谍卫星
    据新华社25日讯　卡纳维拉尔角（美国）消息：为了加紧利用宇宙空间从事间谍活动的阴险目的，美国空军在5月24日又发射了一个军事侦察用的人造卫星“米达斯二号”。发射后两小时，他们宣布这个卫星已经进入绕地球运行的轨道。但是，由于地面计算中心失灵，这个卫星运行的近地点、远地点等参数久久未能获得。在这以前，美国空军在2月26日曾发射同样目的的“米达斯一号”卫星，但遭到失败。
    “米达斯二号”卫星上装着红外线等侦察仪器，是专门用来收集军事情报的。
