Title: Spaceship遨游太空 Many Regions Can See the Spaceship with the Naked Eye
Author:
Date: 1960-05-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, May 17th: TASS reports: As of 20:16 Moscow time on May 16th (01:16 Beijing time on May 17th), the Soviet satellite spaceship had orbited the Earth twenty-eight times. All Soviet measuring and receiving stations continue to observe the satellite spaceship regularly, receiving scientific intelligence regarding the operation of the instruments installed in the satellite spaceship's cabin. Analysis of available scientific measurement data indicates that all basic parameters are maintained within the specified standard range in the experimental cabin used to study the conditions for human spaceflight. The last stage of the carrier rocket is now orbiting along a path very close to the orbit of the satellite spaceship. The satellite spaceship can be seen with the naked eye before sunrise in the region between 35 and 49 degrees north latitude, and after sunset in the region between 56 and 70 degrees south latitude.

Xinhua News Agency, May 17th, Moscow dispatch from TASS: The Soviet satellite spaceship and the last stage of its carrier rocket will pass over Beijing at 02:56 early morning on the 18th (Beijing time).

The region between 35 and 49 degrees north latitude can be seen with the naked eye before sunrise.

### 宇宙飞船遨游太空  许多地区可以用肉眼看到飞船

1960-05-18
第5版()
专栏：

    宇宙飞船遨游太空
    许多地区可以用肉眼看到飞船
    据新华社17日讯　据塔斯社报道：到莫斯科时间16日二十时十六分（北京时间17日一时十六分），苏联卫星式飞船已经围绕地球运行了二十八周。苏联所有测量站和接收站继续对卫星式飞船进行经常的观察，接收有关安装在卫星式飞船舱内的仪器工作情况的科学情报。对现有科学测量数据的分析表明，在用来研究人在宇宙空间飞行条件的实验舱内，所有基本参数都保持在规定标准范围以内。运载火箭的最后一级现在沿着一条同卫星式飞船轨道极相接近的轨道运行。北纬三十五度到四十九度地区在日出前、南纬五十六度到七十度地区在日落后，可以用肉眼看到卫星式飞船。
    新华社17日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联卫星式宇宙飞船及其运载火箭最后一级18日凌晨二时五十六分（北京时间）将经过北京。
    北纬三十五度到四十九度地区在日出前可以用肉眼看到。
