Title: Danish Communist Party Chairman Jespersen Arrives in Beijing; Comrades Zhu De, Peng Zhen, and Others Welcome Him at the Airport
Author: 
Date: 1960-05-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 21st: Knud Jespersen, Chairman of the Danish Communist Party, and his wife, along with three accompanying personnel, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. They are here for a visit to our country.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee; Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau; Liu Lantao, alternate secretary of the Secretariat; and Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee, among others.

### 丹共主席耶斯佩森到京  朱德、彭真等同志到机场欢迎

1960-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

    丹共主席耶斯佩森到京
    朱德、彭真等同志到机场欢迎
    新华社21日讯　应中国共产党中央委员会邀请来我国访问的丹麦共产党主席克努德·耶斯佩森和夫人以及随员一行三人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有中共中央副主席朱德，政治局委员彭真，书记处候补书记刘澜涛，中央委员刘宁一等人。
