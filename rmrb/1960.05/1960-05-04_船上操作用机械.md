Title: Onboard Mechanization
Author:
Date: 1960-05-04
Page: 2

    On the Shipping Front

    The entire staff of Wooden Sailboat No. 304 of the Shenjiamen Transportation Station in Zhoushan, Zhejiang Province, carried out a major innovation campaign and, after three days of hard work, realized 120 innovations. Now, with the exception of rowing, all operations on board, including raising the sails, winding the anchor, steering, opening the hold, and pumping water, have been mechanized or semi-mechanized.

### 船上操作用机械

1960-05-04
第2版()
专栏：海运线上

    船上操作用机械
    浙江省舟山沈家门运输站三○四号木帆船全体员工，大闹革新，奋战三天，实现革新一百二十项。现在，船上除摇橹以外，升篷、车锚、掌舵、起舱、抽水等全部实现了机械化、半机械化操作。
