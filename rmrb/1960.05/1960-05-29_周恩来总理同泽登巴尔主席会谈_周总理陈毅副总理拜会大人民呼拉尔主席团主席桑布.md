Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Holds Talks with Chairman Tsedenbal; Premier Zhou and Vice Premier Chen Yi Call on Sambuu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural

Author: 
Date: 1960-05-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, May 28th: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, held talks with Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, Tsedenbal, this morning at the Mongolian Government Building.

Participating in the talks from the Chinese side were: Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission Liu Mingfu, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs and Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong, Deputy Director of the Premier’s Office of the State Council Luo Qingchang, Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Chen Shuliang, and Director of the Complete Plant Equipment Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade Chang Yanqing. Participating in the talks from the Mongolian side were: First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Molomjamts, Chairman of the State Planning Commission Ragchaa, Minister of Foreign Affairs Shagdarjav, Minister of Foreign Trade Gombojab, Minister of Industry Damdin, Chairman of the Committee for Foreign Economic Relations Balzhid, and Mayor of Ulaanbaatar Bata.

Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia Xie Fusheng and Mongolian Ambassador to China Sharav also participated in the talks.

The talks were conducted in a cordial and friendly atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, May 28th: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi called on Sambuu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, this morning.

Present during the call were: Vice Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural Baljinniyam, members Damdinsuren, Hashgambai, Chairman of the Great People's Hural Zhalkhasaikhan, Minister of Foreign Affairs Shagdarjav, and Mongolian Ambassador to China Sharav. Present from the Chinese side were: Ji Pengfei, Li Qiang, Liu Mingfu, Han Nianlong, Luo Qingchang, Chen Shuliang, Chang Yanqing, and Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia Xie Fusheng.

### 周恩来总理同泽登巴尔主席会谈  周总理陈毅副总理拜会大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布

1960-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理同泽登巴尔主席会谈
    周总理陈毅副总理拜会大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布
    新华社乌兰巴托28日电　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席泽登巴尔今天上午在蒙古政府大厦举行会谈。
    中国方面参加会谈的有：国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅、外交部副部长姬鹏飞、对外贸易部副部长李强、国家计划委员会副主任刘明夫、外交部部长助理兼办公厅主任韩念龙、国务院总理办公室副主任罗青长、外交部第二亚洲司司长陈叔亮和对外贸易部成套设备局局长常彦卿。蒙古方面参加会谈的有：部长会议第一副主席莫洛姆扎木茨、国家计划委员会主席拉格查、外交部长沙格达尔苏伦、对外贸易部长贡布扎布、工业部长达姆丁、对外经济联络委员会主席巴尔日德和乌兰巴托市市长巴塔。
    中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生和蒙古驻中国大使沙拉布也参加了会谈。
    会谈是在亲切和友好的气氛中进行的。
    新华社乌兰巴托28日电　周恩来总理、陈毅副总理今天上午拜会了蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布。
    拜会时在座的有：大人民呼拉尔主席团副主席巴尔吉尼雅姆、委员达木丁苏伦、哈什甘巴伊，大人民呼拉尔主席贾尔卡赛汗，外交部长沙格达尔苏伦，蒙古驻中国大使沙拉布。中国方面在座的有：姬鹏飞、李强、刘明夫、韩念龙、罗青长、陈叔亮、常彦卿，以及中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生。
