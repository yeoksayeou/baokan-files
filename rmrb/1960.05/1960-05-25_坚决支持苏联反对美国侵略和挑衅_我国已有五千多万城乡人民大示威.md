Title: Resolutely Support the Soviet Union Against American Aggression and Provocation; Over 50 Million Urban and Rural People in Our Country Hold Massive Demonstrations
Author:
Date: 1960-05-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 24th: By the 23rd, over 53 million people in urban and rural areas of our country had held rallies and demonstrations to support the Soviet government's just stand against the American imperialist aggression, and severely condemned the American imperialist sabotage of the Four-Power Summit Conference. Unprecedented rallies and demonstrations were held in Beijing, Shanghai, provincial capitals, autonomous region capitals, and various important cities. Many medium and small cities, factories, mines, schools, rural people's communes, and various units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army also held rallies and demonstrations one after another. In the three days from the 20th to the 22nd, more than 25 million people in 31 major cities held rallies and demonstrations, including 3.2 million in Beijing, more than 3 million in Shanghai, and 2 million in Tianjin. The number of people participating in rallies and demonstrations in Wuhan, Chongqing, and Shenyang all exceeded 1.5 million. Harbin, Nanjing, and Lushun-Dalian also had more than 1 million people participating in demonstrations and rallies. Many cities held city-wide demonstrations and rallies. In Lhasa, a city with a population of over 50,000, 35,000 people held demonstrations and rallies. In Shashi, Hubei Province, with a population of over 100,000, 80,000 people held demonstrations and rallies. This powerful demonstration and rally held to support the Soviet government's just stand against American imperialist aggression has spread to every corner of our country. From Heihe, a border town in the extreme north of our country bordering the great ally Soviet Union, to Xiamen on the front line of Fujian in the south, and the remote Lesser Khingan Mountains forest area, there were rallies and demonstrations held by thousands or even hundreds of thousands of people. The first secretary or secretary of the local Chinese Communist Party organizations, the heads of the government, various democratic parties, and people's organizations, and representatives of workers, farmers, intellectuals, and militia all spoke at the local rallies. They severely condemned the American imperialist war provocations and the crime of sabotaging the Four-Power Summit Conference, and fully supported the just stand of the Soviet government. They pointed out that American imperialism's aggression and provocation against the Soviet Union is an aggression and provocation against China and the entire socialist camp, and any such aggression and provocation will be met with a devastating counterattack. The people participating in the demonstrations and rallies shouted slogans such as "Support the Soviet government's solemn stand against the American imperialist aggression!", "People of the world unite, strengthen the struggle, oppose the imperialist war forces headed by the United States, and defend world peace!", "Long live the unbreakable friendship and unity between the Chinese and Soviet people!", expressing their common hatred for the enemy with the Soviet people, and also fully expressing the firm determination of the 650 million people of our country to oppose American imperialist aggression and war provocations and defend world peace.

This newspaper reports, according to Xinhua News Agency: While the people of major cities across the country held powerful demonstrations, in recent days, people from all walks of life in Hohhot and Urumqi, as well as many small and medium-sized cities in Liaoning, Anhui, Jilin, Hunan, Hubei, Guangxi and other provinces (regions), have also held large-scale rallies and demonstrations, resolutely supporting the Soviet Union's struggle against American imperialist aggression and provocation, and angrily condemning American imperialism's crime of sabotaging the Four-Power Summit Conference.

In Hohhot, the capital of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, on the 23rd, 400,000 people of all ethnic groups from the city to the suburbs held rallies in dozens of locations. On the same day, in Baotou, an iron and steel industrial city, more than 200,000 people of all ethnic groups also held rallies and demonstrations.

In Urumqi, the capital of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, 250,000 people of all ethnic groups held rallies and demonstrations in the rain on the 23rd.

In Fushun, Jinzhou, Benxi, Fuxin, Chaoyang and other cities and their respective counties in Liaoning Province, rallies and demonstrations were held separately on the 21st, 22nd, and 23rd, with a total participation of 1.63 million people.

In Anhui Province, on the 22nd, rallies and demonstrations were held in six cities including Bengbu, Wuhu, Anqing, Huainan, Ma'anshan, and Tongling, with a total of 740,000 people.

In Jilin, Yanji, Liaoyuan, Siping, Baicheng, Tonghua, Hunjiang and other seven small and medium-sized cities in Jilin Province, 640,000 people have held rallies and parades in recent days. 7 million people in the province's county towns and people's communes also listened to live broadcasts of the rallies held in Changchun, Jilin, Yanji and other cities.

In Hengyang, Zhuzhou, and Xiangtan in Hunan Province, 640,000 people held rallies and parades in the rain on the 22nd.

In Huangshi, Shashi, Yichang and Xiangfan in Hubei Province, a total of 260,000 people held rallies and demonstrations separately.

In Liuzhou, Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, 170,000 people participated in rallies and demonstrations on the 23rd.

Heihe Town, Heilongjiang Province, which faces the Soviet Union across the river in the north of our country, also held a rally and demonstration attended by more than 10,000 people on the evening of the 22nd.

### 坚决支持苏联反对美国侵略和挑衅  我国已有五千多万城乡人民大示威

1960-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

    坚决支持苏联反对美国侵略和挑衅
    我国已有五千多万城乡人民大示威
    新华社24日讯　到23日止，我国广大城乡已经有五千三百多万人举行集会和示威游行，支持苏联政府反对美帝国主义侵略罪行的正义立场，严厉谴责美帝国主义破坏四国政府首脑会议。北京、上海，各省省会，各自治区首府，各个重要城市都举行了规模空前的集会和示威，许多中、小城市、工厂、矿山、学校，农村人民公社，中国人民解放军的各部队，也都先后分别举行了集会和示威。在20日到22日的三天中，就有三十一个大城市的二千五百多万人举行了集会和示威，其中北京有三百二十万人、上海有三百多万人、天津有二百万人。武汉、重庆、沈阳参加集会和示威的人数都在一百五十万人以上。哈尔滨、南京、旅大也有一百万人以上参加了示威和集会。许多城市都是倾城举行示威和集会，五万多人口的拉萨市有三万五千人举行示威和集会，有十多万人口的湖北省沙市市，有八万人举行示威和集会。这一次为支持苏联政府反对美帝国主义侵略的正义立场而举行的强大示威和集会，遍及我国的各个角落。从我国极北方与伟大盟邦苏联毗邻的边境城镇黑河，到南方福建前线的厦门，以及边远的小兴安岭林区，都有成千上万以至几十万人举行的集会和示威。各地中国共产党组织的第一书记或书记，政府、各民主党派、人民团体的负责人，工人、农民、知识分子、民兵的代表，都在当地举行的集会上讲了话。他们严厉谴责美帝国主义的战争挑衅和破坏四国政府首脑会议的罪行，完全支持苏联政府的正义立场。他们指出，美帝国主义对苏联的侵略和挑衅，就是对中国和整个社会主义阵营的侵略和挑衅，任何这种侵略和挑衅都将遭到毁灭性的回击。参加示威和集会的人们高呼“拥护苏联政府对美帝国主义侵略罪行的严正立场！”“全世界人民团结起来，加强斗争，反对以美国为首的帝国主义战争势力，保卫世界和平！”“中苏两国人民牢不可破的友谊和团结万岁！”等口号，表示他们与苏联人民同仇敌忾的心情，也充分表达了我国六亿五千万人民为反对美帝国主义的侵略和战争挑衅、保卫世界和平的坚强决心。
    本报讯　据新华社消息：和全国各大中城市人民举行强大示威的同时，近几天来，呼和浩特、乌鲁木齐二市以及辽宁、安徽、吉林、湖南、湖北、广西等省（区）的许多中小城市的各界人民，也举行了大规模的集会和示威游行，坚决支持苏联反对美帝国主义侵略和挑衅的斗争，愤怒谴责美帝国主义破坏四国首脑会议的罪行。
    内蒙古自治区首府呼和浩特市，23日，从城市到郊区，有四十万各族人民分成几十处举行了集会。同一天，在钢铁工业城市——包头市，也有二十多万各族人民举行了集会和示威。
    新疆维吾尔自治区首府乌鲁木齐市各族人民二十五万人，23日冒雨举行集会和示威。
    辽宁省抚顺、锦州、本溪、阜新、朝阳等市及所属各县，在21、22、23日分别举行集会示威，参加人数共达一百六十三万人。
    安徽省22日举行集会示威的，有蚌埠、芜湖、安庆、淮南、马鞍山、铜陵等六个城市，共七十四万人。
    吉林省吉林、延吉、辽源、四平、白城、通化、浑江等七个中小城市，连日来有六十四万人集会游行。全省各县城和人民公社还有七百万人收听了长春、吉林、延吉等市人民集会的实况广播。
    湖南省衡阳、株洲、湘潭三市，六十四万人于22日冒雨集会游行。
    湖北省黄石、沙市、宜昌和襄樊四市，共有二十六万人分别举行集会示威。
    广西僮族自治区柳州市，23日参加集会示威的有十七万人。
    我国北部与苏联隔江相望的黑龙江省黑河镇，在22日晚上也举行了有一万多人参加的集会和示威。
