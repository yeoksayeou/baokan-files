Title: Asian Trade Union Representatives Visiting China Travel to Various Locations After Visiting Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: Asian trade union delegations and trade union representatives, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations in China, have departed for various locations to continue their visits after touring Beijing.

The guests received a warm welcome during their visit to Beijing. The Japan General Council of Trade Unions (Sohyo) delegation, upon seeing all the facilities at the Beijing Railway Station designed for the convenience of passengers, praised, "Such thoughtful consideration for passengers can only be achieved in a socialist country."

The Indonesian Workers' Delegation and the Indian All India Trade Union Congress Delegation visited the Beijing No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory, touring various workshops and workers' living areas. That evening, the factory workers invited the Indonesian and Indian guests to attend their weekend party. At the party, after the guests enjoyed songs and dances written and performed by the workers, they took to the stage and sang "The Internationale" and "Struggling for Freedom" among other songs. Sudjono, the head of the Indonesian Workers' Delegation, addressed the workers, saying, "The friendship between the Chinese people and the Indonesian people is profound, and no force can destroy our friendship and unity."

When the Trade Union Congress of Burma Delegation visited the Central Institute for Nationalities, students of various ethnic groups, dressed in their national costumes, gathered along the roadside to warmly welcome the guests.

### 访问我国的亚洲各国工会代表  在北京参观后赴各地访问

1960-05-08
第5版()
专栏：

    访问我国的亚洲各国工会代表
    在北京参观后赴各地访问
    据新华社7日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝活动的亚洲各国工会代表团和工会代表，在北京参观访问后，已陆续前往各地参观访问。
    客人们在北京参观访问时，受到了热烈欢迎。日本总评访华代表团在北京车站看到一切设备都是为旅客方便着想，他们称赞说：“为旅客想的这样周到，只有社会主义国家才能办得到。”
    印度尼西亚工人代表团和印度全印工会大会代表团，曾到北京第二棉纺织厂参观了各个车间和工人生活区。当天晚上，这个工厂的工人邀请印度尼西亚和印度客人们参加了他们举办的周末晚会。在晚会上，客人们欣赏了工人们自编自演的歌舞以后，还先后上台演唱了“国际歌”、“为自由而斗争”等歌曲。印度尼西亚工人代表团团长苏德佐向工人们讲了话。他说：“中国人民和印度尼西亚人民之间的友谊是深厚的，任何力量都破坏不了我们之间的友谊团结。”
    当缅甸联邦工会总会代表团到中央民族学院参观时，穿着民族服装的各族学生们聚集在路旁热烈欢迎客人。
