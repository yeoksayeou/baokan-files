Title: Premier Zhou Will Visit Vietnam
Author: 
Date: 1960-05-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, at the invitation of Premier Pham Van Dong of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, will pay a friendly visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam from May 9th to 12th. Accompanying him on the visit will be Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Marshal Chen Yi, and others.

### 周总理将访问越南

1960-05-02
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理将访问越南
    新华社1日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来应越南民主共和国政府总理范文同的邀请，将于5月9日至12日，在越南民主共和国作友好访问。同行访问的有国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅等。
