Title: Polish "Guards" Basketball Team Plays in Shanghai with One Win and One Loss
Author:
Date: 1960-05-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, May 2nd. The Polish "Guards" men's basketball team concluded its friendly matches in Shanghai today. During the two games held in Shanghai, they defeated the Shanghai "Pioneer" men's basketball team on April 30th with a score of 89 to 81, and lost to the Shanghai men's basketball team tonight with a score of 75 to 89. More than 7,000 spectators watched these two exciting matches.

On April 30th, Shanghai Vice Mayor Jin Zhonghua met with the head of the visiting team, Sienkiewicz.

The Polish "Guards" men's basketball team arrived in Shanghai on April 29th and is scheduled to leave Shanghai for Hangzhou on May 3rd.

### 波“近卫军”篮球队在上海比赛一胜一败

1960-05-04
第4版()
专栏：

    波“近卫军”篮球队在上海比赛一胜一败
    据新华社上海2日电　波兰“近卫军”男子篮球队今天结束了在上海举行的友谊比赛。在上海期间举行的两场比赛中，4月30日以八十九比八十一胜上海“前卫”男子篮球队，今晚以七十五比八十九败给上海市男子篮球队。共有七千多观众观看了这两场精彩的比赛。
    4月30日，上海市副市长金仲华接见了客队领队谢恩凯维奇。
    波兰“近卫军”男子篮球队是4月29日到上海的，预定在5月3日离上海去杭州。
