Title: Indian "People's Century" Weekly Editorial Points Out Nehru's Attacks on China Benefit Imperialism
Author:
Date: 1960-05-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 14th: The latest issue of the Urdu-language "People's Century" weekly published an editorial strongly criticizing Nehru's recent attacks on China at a press conference in London.

The editorial stated: "What Nehru said in London will only benefit the imperialist warmongers, benefit the enemies of India and China, and benefit the enemies of freedom and progress in Asian countries."

The weekly stated, "Nehru's argument means that any country that increases its population and develops its industry will become a threat to its neighbors, but Nehru probably wouldn't use the same words to talk about India. So, how can it be said that China's large population and huge industrial development will be a danger to Asia and the world?"

The weekly considers this argument to be a "muddled and reactionary argument." It said, "Imperialism and its agents in Asia have always used this argument to maintain their colonial rule over Asian countries. All Asians, Africans, and all justice-loving people around the world know that it is imperialism that threatens the peace and freedom of all countries, and nothing else."

The editorial described how Western imperialist countries have frantically engaged in colonial plunder for two hundred years, turning Asian and African countries into ruins. The editorial said, "American and British imperialism has today established military bases in South Korea, South Vietnam, Japan, Pakistan, Iran, Turkey, and Taiwan. These military bases have become a threat to China, India, and all of Asia. Nehru said nothing about this danger, but he saw danger in China's growing population and its industrial progress."

The newspaper said: "The truth is that an increase or decrease in a country's population or an increase in its industrial potential does not threaten peace. What poses a danger to peace is the existence of capitalism, its monopoly aspects, and the imperialist system. The populations of many countries are larger than those of Britain, Belgium, the Netherlands, Germany, France, or the United States, but it is these Western countries with smaller populations that enslave other countries for their own benefit and are the culprits of two world wars. From a population perspective, the Soviet Union ranks third in the world, and its industrial development is faster than any other country, but today this country is the most powerful defender and protector of peace."

The editorial said, "Socialism does not need to attack other countries or enslave other countries for plunder. China has also established the same social system, and China's population and industrial growth will not bring any danger to Asia or the world. On the contrary, it will only strengthen peace in Asia and the world."

The newspaper said: "We believe that it is wrong to describe the Sino-Indian border dispute as Chinese aggression." The editorial said that the disputes between China and India "should be resolved through friendly negotiations."

### 印度“人民世纪”周报社论指出  尼赫鲁攻击中国有利帝国主义

1960-05-15
第5版()
专栏：

    印度“人民世纪”周报社论指出
    尼赫鲁攻击中国有利帝国主义
    新华社新德里14日电　最近一期的乌尔都文“人民世纪”周报发表社论，强烈抨击尼赫鲁最近在伦敦举行的记者招待会上对中国进行的攻击。
    社论说：“尼赫鲁在伦敦所说的话只会有利于帝国主义战争贩子，有利于印度和中国的敌人，有利于亚洲国家自由和进步的敌人。”
    这家周报说，“尼赫鲁提出的论点意味着，凡是增加本国人口和发展本国工业的国家就会成为它们的邻邦的威胁，但是尼赫鲁大概不会用同样的话来谈印度。那么，怎么能说中国众多的人口和工业的巨大发展会成为亚洲和世界的危险呢？”
    这家周报认为这个论点是“糊涂而反动的论点”。它说，“帝国主义及其在亚洲的代理人一直就用这种论点来维持其对亚洲国家的殖民统治。所有的亚洲人、非洲人以及全世界一切爱好正义的人们都知道，威胁各国的和平和自由的正是帝国主义而不是其他的东西。”
    社论叙述了两百年来西方帝国主义各国如何疯狂地从事于殖民主义的掠夺而使亚非各国变成废墟。社论说：“美英帝国主义今天在南朝鲜、南越、日本、巴基斯坦、伊朗、土耳其和台湾建立了军事基地，这些军事基地已经成为对中国、印度和全亚洲的威胁。尼赫鲁对于这种危险一声不吭，但是他却从中国增长的人口和它工业的进步中看到了危险。”
    这家报纸说：“真实的情况是，一个国家人口的增减或者工业潜力的增长是不会威胁到和平的。所以会对和平发生危险，乃是由于资本主义及其垄断的方面和帝国主义制度的存在。许多国家的人口都大于英国、比利时、荷兰、德国、法国或者美国，但是却是这些人口较少的西方国家为了它们自己的利益来奴役其他的国家，并且成为两次世界大战的祸首。从人口的角度来看，苏联居于世界第三位，它的工业发展比其他国家都快，但是今天这个国家却是和平最有力的维护者和保卫者。”
    社论说，“社会主义是不需要进攻别的国家或者为了掠夺而奴役别的国家的。中国也已经建立了同样的社会制度，中国的人口和工业的增长不会给亚洲或者世界带来任何危险，相反，它只会加强亚洲和世界的和平。”
    报纸说：“我们认为把中印边境分歧说成是中国的侵略是不对的。”社论说，中国和印度之间的分歧“应该通过友好的谈判加以解决。”
