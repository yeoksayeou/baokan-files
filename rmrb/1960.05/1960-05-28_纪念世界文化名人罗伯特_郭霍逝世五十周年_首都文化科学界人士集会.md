Title: Capital Cultural and Scientific Circles Gather to Commemorate the Fiftieth Anniversary of the Death of World Cultural Figure Robert Koch
Author:
Date: 1960-05-28
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, May 27th: More than 400 people from cultural and scientific circles in the capital gathered today to commemorate the fiftieth anniversary of the death of Robert Koch, a world cultural figure and famous German doctor.

    Fu Lianzhang, President of the Chinese Medical Association, said in his opening remarks that Robert Koch was a famous German doctor and microbiologist who made outstanding contributions to medicine and microbiology, saved countless patients, ensured the health of more people, and benefited mankind. He said that today's commemoration of the fiftieth anniversary of Robert Koch's death will not only promote cultural and scientific exchanges between China and Germany, but also embody the friendly cooperation between the Chinese and German people and the significance of defending world peace. He said that Chinese medical workers should learn from Dr. Robert Koch's spirit of enthusiastically imparting knowledge and pass on medical and scientific knowledge to the broad masses of the people.

    Xie Shaowen, Executive Director of the Chinese Society for Microbiology, gave a report at the meeting entitled "Some Advances in Medical Microbiology from the Perspective of Robert Koch's Contributions."

    Winning, Counselor of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China, Vladimir Timakov, Academician and head of the delegation from the Soviet Academy of Medical Sciences currently visiting China, as well as other members of the delegation, were invited to attend the commemoration. Winning and Academician Timakov spoke at the meeting.

    Also present at the commemoration were Cui Yitian, Vice Minister of Health and Vice President of the Chinese Medical Association; Qian Xinzhong, Vice Minister of Health; Mao Yisheng, Vice Chairman of the China Association for Science and Technology and Chairman of the Beijing Association for Science and Technology; Huang Jiasi, Vice Chairman of the China Association for Science and Technology; Zhou Erfu, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Shen Qizhen, Director of the Sino-German Friendship Association; and Wu Maosun, Standing Committee Member of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace.

### 纪念世界文化名人罗伯特·郭霍逝世五十周年  首都文化科学界人士集会

1960-05-28
第5版()
专栏：

    纪念世界文化名人罗伯特·郭霍逝世五十周年
    首都文化科学界人士集会
    据新华社27日讯　首都文化科学界人士四百多人在今天集会，纪念世界文化名人、德国著名医生罗伯特·郭霍逝世五十周年。
    中华医学会会长傅连暲在开幕词中说，罗伯特·郭霍是德国的一位著名的医生、微生物学家，在医学和微生物学方面作出了卓越的贡献，拯救了无数病人，保证了更多人健康，造福于人类。他说，今天举行的罗伯特·郭霍逝世五十年纪念会，不仅起到促进中德两国文化科学交流的作用，同时也体现了中德两国人民的友好合作与保卫世界和平的意义。他说，中国医务工作者要学习罗伯特·郭霍医生热心传授的精神，把医学科学知识传授给广大人民群众。
    中国微生物学会常务理事谢少文在会上作了“从罗伯特·郭霍的贡献来看医用微生物学的某些进展”的报告。
    德意志民主共和国驻中国大使馆参赞温宁、正在我国访问的苏联医学科学院代表团团长弗·得·季马科夫院士以及代表团其他成员，都应邀出席了纪念会。温宁和季马科夫院士在会上讲了话。
    出席纪念会的，还有卫生部副部长、中华医学会副会长崔义田，卫生部副部长钱信忠，中华人民共和国科学技术协会副主席、北京市科学技术协会主席茅以升，中华人民共和国科学技术协会副主席黄家驷，中国人民对外文化协会秘书长周而复，中德友好协会理事沈其震，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员吴茂荪等人。
