Title: North Korea and East Germany Recognize the Republic of Togo; Premier Kim Il-sung and President Pieck Send Congratulatory Telegrams
Author:
Date: 1960-05-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, April 30th: According to a report by the Korean Central News Agency on April 29th, Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, announced in a congratulatory telegram to Togo's Prime Minister Olympio that North Korea recognizes the Republic of Togo.

Premier Kim Il-sung, on behalf of the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, warmly congratulated Togo on declaring its independence.

The telegram stated that all the people of North Korea, who have consistently supported and sympathized with the African people's struggle against imperialism and colonialism, wholeheartedly congratulate the people of Togo on the brilliant achievements they have made in defending peace and consolidating independence. The telegram concluded by expressing confidence that the friendship and cooperation between the peoples of the two countries will be strengthened and developed.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, April 29th: Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, sent a telegram to Togo's Prime Minister Olympio, congratulating Togo on its independence.

The telegram stated that the German Democratic Republic recognizes Togo as an independent and sovereign republic.

### 朝德承认多哥共和国  金日成首相皮克总统致电祝贺

1960-05-03
第6版()
专栏：

    朝德承认多哥共和国
    金日成首相皮克总统致电祝贺
    新华社平壤4月30日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社4月29日报道，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国首相金日成在致多哥总理奥林匹欧的贺电中宣布朝鲜承认多哥共和国。
    金日成首相代表朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府热烈祝贺多哥宣布独立。
    贺电说，一贯支持和同情非洲人民反对帝国主义和殖民主义斗争的全体朝鲜人民衷心祝贺多哥人民在保卫和平和巩固独立的工作中所取得的辉煌成就。贺电最后表示确信，两国人民的友谊和合作将会得到加强和发展。
    新华社柏林29日电　德意志民主共和国总统皮克致电多哥总理奥林匹欧，祝贺多哥独立。
    电文说，德意志民主共和国承认多哥是一个独立自主的共和国。
