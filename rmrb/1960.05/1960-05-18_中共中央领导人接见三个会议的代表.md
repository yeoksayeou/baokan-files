Title: Central Committee Leaders of the CCP Receive Representatives of Three Conferences
Author:
Date: 1960-05-18
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, 17th. Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, Lin Biao, Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary, Peng Zhen, Luo Ronghuan, Li Fuchun, He Long, members of the Political Bureau, Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat, and Luo Ruiqing, member of the Central Committee, received this afternoon all the representatives attending the First Armored Forces Staff Work Conference of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Third Advanced Collective Activists Representatives Conference of the Beijing Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and the National Rural Statistical Work Experience Exchange Conference.

### 中共中央领导人接见三个会议的代表

1960-05-18
第2版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人接见三个会议的代表
    新华社17日讯　中共中央副主席朱德、林彪，总书记邓小平，政治局委员彭真、罗荣桓、李富春、贺龙，书记处书记王稼祥，中央委员罗瑞卿，今天下午接见了出席中国人民解放军装甲兵第一届参谋工作会议，中国人民解放军北京空军第三届先进集体积极分子代表大会，以及全国农村统计工作经验交流大会的全体代表。
