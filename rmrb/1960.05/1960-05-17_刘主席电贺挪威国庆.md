Title: Chairman Liu Sends Congratulations on Norwegian National Day
Author:
Date: 1960-05-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 16th

    Chairman Liu Sends Congratulations on Norwegian National Day
    Xinhua News Agency, May 16th - Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, sent a telegram today to the King of Norway congratulating him on Norwegian National Day. The telegram reads as follows:

    To His Majesty King Olav V of the Kingdom of Norway:

    On the occasion of the National Day of the Kingdom of Norway, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to Your Majesty and the people of your country on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish Norway prosperity and its people happiness.

                   Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, May 16, 1960

### 刘主席电贺挪威国庆

1960-05-17
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席电贺挪威国庆
    新华社16日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇今天打电报给挪威国王祝贺挪威国庆，电文如下：挪威王国国王奥拉夫五世陛下：
    在挪威王国国庆的时候，我谨代表中国人民并且以我个人的名义，向陛下和贵国人民表示诚挚祝贺。祝挪威国家繁荣，人民幸福。
                   中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇　1960年5月16日
