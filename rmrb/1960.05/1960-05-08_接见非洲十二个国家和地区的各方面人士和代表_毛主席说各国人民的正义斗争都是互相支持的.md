Title: Chairman Mao Meets with People and Representatives from Various Circles in Twelve African Countries and Regions: Chairman Mao Says That the Just Struggles of All Countries' People Support Each Other

Author: 
Date: 1960-05-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, 7th. Chairman Mao Zedong met this afternoon in Zhengzhou with social activists, peace figures, and trade union, youth, and student delegations and representatives from twelve African countries and regions who are currently visiting China. They are: social activists and peace figures from Somaliland, Senegal, Sierra Leone, and Sudan; the delegation of the General Union of Algerian Workers, the delegation of the National Union of Congolese Workers, the delegation of the Guinean Confederation of Workers' Unions, the delegation of the General Confederation of Labour of Cameroon, the delegation of the Madagascar Trade Union, the delegation of the Trade Union Federation of Mali, the delegation of the Moroccan Labour Union, the delegation of the Zanzibar Federation of Labour, and trade union representatives from Senegal, and representatives of the African Independence Trade Union Federation of French Somaliland; the Algerian Youth Delegation, the Algerian Student Delegation, the Cameroonian Youth Delegation, the Zanzibar Youth Delegation, and the Congolese Youth Delegation.

    During the meeting, each delegation made enthusiastic speeches, introducing the various sufferings they had endured under imperialist oppression and exploitation for many years; introducing the situation of their struggle against imperialism and colonialism; expressing their determination and confidence in striving for complete victory, and expressing their deep feelings for the Chinese people and their respect and love for Chairman Mao.

    Chairman Mao, on behalf of the 650 million Chinese people, expressed complete sympathy and full support for the heroic struggle of the African people against imperialism and colonialism. Chairman Mao also expressed sympathy and support for the patriotic and just struggles of the South Korean and Turkish people against US imperialism and its running dogs. The Chairman believes that the struggle of the South Korean and Turkish people shows that the storm of struggle of the oppressed people of Asian countries against imperialism and its running dogs will have a greater upsurge. This struggle will be a support for the just struggle of the African people, the Latin American people, and the people of the world. The Chairman said that the just struggles of the people of all countries in the world support each other. Chairman Mao thanked the African friends for their deep friendship with the Chinese people, congratulated the great unity of the Chinese and African people, and was confident that in the common struggle against imperialism and colonialism, final victory will be achieved.

    Yang Shangkun, alternate member of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, and Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and Vice-Chairman of the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee, were present at the meeting. (Attached picture)

### 接见非洲十二个国家和地区的各方面人士和代表  毛主席说各国人民的正义斗争都是互相支持的

1960-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

    接见非洲十二个国家和地区的各方面人士和代表
    毛主席说各国人民的正义斗争都是互相支持的
    他代表中国人民完全同情和支持非洲、南朝鲜、土耳其人民的正义斗争。并认为亚洲各国受压迫人民反对帝国主义及其走狗的斗争风暴将会有更大的兴起。
    新华社郑州7日电　毛泽东主席今天下午在这里接见了正在我国参观访问的来自非洲十二个国家和地区的社会活动家、和平人士以及工会、青年、学生代表团和代表。他们是：索马里兰、塞内加尔、塞拉勒窝内、苏丹的社会活动家和和平人士；阿尔及利亚工人总联合会代表团、刚果全国工人联合会代表团、几内亚工人工会联合会代表团、喀麦隆劳工总联合会代表团、马达加斯加工会代表团、马里联邦工会联合会代表团、摩洛哥劳联代表团、桑给巴尔劳工联合会代表团和塞内加尔工会代表、法属索马里兰非洲人独立工会联合会代表；阿尔及利亚青年代表团、阿尔及利亚学生代表团、喀麦隆青年代表团、桑给巴尔青年代表团和刚果青年代表。
    在接见中，各个代表团都作了热烈的发言，介绍了他们多少年来在帝国主义压迫和榨取下所遭受的种种苦难；介绍了他们反对帝国主义、殖民主义斗争的情况；表达了他们争取彻底胜利的决心和信心，并且表达了他们对于中国人民的深厚感情和对于毛泽东主席的敬爱。
    毛泽东主席代表中国六亿五千万人民对于非洲人民反对帝国主义、殖民主义的英勇斗争，表示完全同情和完全支持。毛泽东主席还表示同情和支持南朝鲜人民、土耳其人民反对美帝国主义及其走狗的爱国正义斗争。主席认为：南朝鲜人民和土耳其人民的这种斗争，表明亚洲各国受压迫人民反对帝国主义及其走狗的斗争风暴，将会有更大的兴起。这个斗争对于非洲人民、拉丁美洲人民以及全世界人民的正义斗争都将是一种支持。主席说，全世界各国人民的正义斗争，都是互相支持的。毛泽东主席感谢非洲朋友们对于中国人民的深厚友情，祝贺中国人民和非洲人民的伟大团结，并且确信在反对帝国主义、殖民主义的共同斗争中，一定会取得最后的胜利。
    今天陪同接见的有中共中央书记处候补书记杨尚昆，中华全国总工会主席、中国亚非团结委员会副主席刘宁一。（附图片）
