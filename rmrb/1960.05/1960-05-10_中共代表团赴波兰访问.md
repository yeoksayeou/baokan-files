Title: CCP Delegation Visits Poland
Author:
Date: 1960-05-10
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The Chinese Communist Party delegation, led by Bo Yibo, and including members Li Baohua, Liu Xiufeng, Huo Shilian, Xu Daoqi and five staff members, left Beijing by plane today for Warsaw at the invitation of the Polish United Workers' Party.
    Those who saw them off at the airport included: Li Fuchun, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee; Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat; Wu Xiuquan, member of the Central Committee; Gong Zirong, responsible staff member of the CPC Central Committee; Sun Zhiyuan and Zhang Guojian, Deputy Directors of the State Economic Commission; Lai Jifa and Song Yuhe, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Construction Engineering, and others.
    The newly appointed Polish Ambassador to China, Jerzy Knothe, and other diplomats from the embassy also saw them off at the airport.

### 中共代表团赴波兰访问

1960-05-10
第6版()
专栏：

    中共代表团赴波兰访问
　　新华社9日讯　应波兰统一工人党邀请赴波兰访问的中国共产党代表团团长薄一波，团员李葆华、刘秀峰、霍士廉、许道琦和随员五人，今天乘飞机离开北京前往华沙。
    到机场送行的有：中共中央政治局委员李富春，书记处书记王稼祥，中央委员伍修权，中共中央机关负责工作人员龚子荣，国家经济委员会副主任孙志远、张国坚，建筑工程部副部长赖际发、宋裕和等。
    波兰新任驻华大使耶日·克诺泰及大使馆其他外交官也到机场送行。
