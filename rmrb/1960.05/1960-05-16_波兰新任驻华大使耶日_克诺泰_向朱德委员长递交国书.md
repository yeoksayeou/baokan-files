Title: Polish Newly Appointed Ambassador to China, Jerzy Knothe, Presents Credentials to Chairman Zhu De
Author: 
Date: 1960-05-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 15th - The newly appointed Ambassador of Poland to China, Jerzy Knothe, accompanied by all diplomatic officials of the embassy, presented his credentials to Chairman Zhu De this afternoon.

Present at the presentation ceremony were: Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and East European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Yu Zhan, and Deputy Director of the Protocol Department Ma Zhenwu.

In his speech, Ambassador Jerzy Knothe said: The friendship between the People's Republic of Poland and the People's Republic of China is based on the solid and lasting foundation of common ideas and common goals. Our thought is the immortal thought of Marxism-Leninism, and our goal is to build socialism and build a happy and beautiful life for the working people of our two countries. The great idea of proletarian internationalism unites us together. Our friendship and increasingly developing cooperation in all aspects of political, social, economic and cultural life serve the great cause of the unity and strength of the entire socialist camp, the cause of all progressive humanity, and world peace.

Chairman Zhu De said in his reply: Poland and China are members of the great socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union. Our common thought is the brilliant Marxism-Leninism, and our common cause is to realize the great ideal of communism. It is this great common thought and common cause which closely unites the people of our two countries. The friendship and cooperation between our two countries will surely be consolidated and developed day by day in the future. This will help the development of the socialist construction of our two countries and the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and will contribute to the struggle against the imperialist policy of war and aggression headed by the United States, to the defense of world peace and the lofty cause of human progress.

After accepting the credentials, Chairman Zhu De had a cordial and friendly conversation with Ambassador Jerzy Knothe.

### 波兰新任驻华大使耶日·克诺泰  向朱德委员长递交国书

1960-05-16
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰新任驻华大使耶日·克诺泰
    向朱德委员长递交国书
    新华社15日讯　波兰新任驻中国大使耶日·克诺泰，今天下午偕同大使馆全体外交官员，向朱德委员长递交国书。
    递交国书时在场的有：外交部副部长罗贵波，外交部苏联东欧司副司长余湛、礼宾司副司长马振武。
    耶日·克诺泰大使在致颂词中说：波兰人民共和国和中华人民共和国之间的友谊是建立在共同思想、共同目标的坚固持久的基础上的。我们的思想是不朽的马克思列宁主义思想，我们的目标是建设社会主义，建设我们两国劳动人民的幸福和美好生活。无产阶级国际主义的伟大思想，把我们联结在一起。我们的友谊和在政治生活、社会生活、经济和文化生活的一切方面越来越发展着的合作，服务于整个社会主义阵营的团结和强大的伟大事业，服务于整个进步人类的事业，服务于世界和平。
    朱德委员长在致答词中说：波兰和中国是以苏联为首的伟大的社会主义阵营的成员。我们共同的思想是光辉的马克思列宁主义，我们共同的事业是实现共产主义的伟大理想。正是这种伟大的共同思想和共同事业把我们两国人民紧密地团结起来。我们两国之间的友谊和合作今后必将日益巩固和发展。这将有助于我们两国的社会主义建设事业的发展和以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结，并将为反对以美国为首的帝国主义战争侵略政策、保卫世界和平的斗争和人类进步的崇高事业作出贡献。
    朱德委员长在接受国书以后，同耶日·克诺泰大使进行了亲切友好的谈话。
