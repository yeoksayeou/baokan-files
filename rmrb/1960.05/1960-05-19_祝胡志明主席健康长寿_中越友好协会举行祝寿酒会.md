Title: China-Vietnam Friendship Association Holds Reception to Wish Chairman Ho Chi Minh a Long and Healthy Life
Author:
Date: 1960-05-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 18th. The China-Vietnam Friendship Association held a reception tonight to celebrate the 70th birthday of President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Among those attending the reception were Yang Xiufeng, President of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association; Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Luo Jun, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Feng Jiping, Vice Mayor of Beijing; Xia Yan and Xu Zhizhen, council members of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association; and Lin Haiyun, Huang Zhong, Zhang Kai, Shu Sheyu, and other responsible persons from relevant departments, totaling more than 200 people.

Tran Trong Quat, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, embassy staff, Vietnamese experts, students, and interns in Beijing, and Vietnamese friends who are currently visiting our country were all invited to attend the reception.

Yang Xiufeng, President of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association, delivered a congratulatory speech at the reception. He said that Chairman Ho Chi Minh's outstanding contributions to the revolutionary cause of the Vietnamese people, the international communist movement and the workers' movement, world peace, and the revolutionary friendship between the people of China and Vietnam are deeply respected by the Chinese people. We warmly congratulate Chairman Ho Chi Minh on his health and longevity, and congratulate him on achieving greater and newer successes in leading the Vietnamese people in building socialism, striving for the peaceful reunification of Vietnam, opposing the war and aggression policies of U.S. imperialism, and defending peace in Asia and the world.

Tran Trong Quat, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, said in his speech that Chairman Ho Chi Minh has led the Vietnamese people to achieve great successes in socialist construction. In the future, the Vietnamese people will create newer achievements and launch a wave of learning from Chairman Ho Chi Minh's style to celebrate his 70th birthday. Finally, he proposed a toast to the health of Chairman Ho Chi Minh and Chairman Mao Zedong.

After the speeches, Yang Xiufeng, on behalf of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association, requested Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Tran Trong Quat to forward a red satin banner embroidered with a large golden character for "Longevity" to Chairman Ho Chi Minh.

The reception was filled with an atmosphere of joy and friendship. Everyone frequently toasted to the health and longevity of Chairman Ho Chi Minh and the brotherly friendship between the people of China and Vietnam.

After the reception, the documentary film "Chairman Ho Chi Minh" was shown.

### 祝胡志明主席健康长寿  中越友好协会举行祝寿酒会

1960-05-19
第4版()
专栏：

    祝胡志明主席健康长寿
    中越友好协会举行祝寿酒会
    新华社18日讯　中国越南友好协会今晚举行酒会，庆祝越南民主共和国胡志明主席七十寿辰。
    出席酒会的，有中越友好协会会长杨秀峰，外交部副部长罗贵波，对外文委副主任罗俊，北京市副市长冯基平，中越友协理事夏衍、许之桢和有关部门负责人林海云、黄中、张凯、舒舍予等二百多人。
    越南民主共和国驻华大使馆临时代办陈仲括和大使馆工作人员，越南在北京的专家、留学生、实习生，和正在我国访问的越南朋友等，都应邀出席了酒会。
    中越友协会长杨秀峰在酒会上致祝词。他说，胡志明主席对越南人民的革命事业、国际共产主义运动和工人运动及世界和平，对中越两国人民之间革命友谊所作的卓越贡献受到中国人民衷心的尊敬。我们热烈地祝贺胡志明主席健康长寿，祝贺他在领导越南人民建设社会主义、争取越南和平统一、反对美帝国主义的战争政策和侵略政策、保卫亚洲和世界和平的事业中，取得更大更新的成就。
    越南驻华大使馆临时代办陈仲括在讲话中说，胡志明主席领导越南人民进行社会主义建设取得了伟大成就。越南人民今后要创造更新的成绩和掀起学习胡志明主席作风的热潮，来祝贺他的七十寿辰。最后他提议为胡志明主席和毛泽东主席的健康干杯。
    讲话以后，杨秀峰代表中越友协，请陈仲括临时代办把一面镶着斗大的金色“寿”字的红缎寿幛，转送胡志明主席。
    酒会上充满了一片欢乐友谊的气氛，大家频频为胡志明主席的健康长寿和中越两国人民兄弟般的友谊而干杯。
    会后放映了纪录影片“胡志明主席”。
