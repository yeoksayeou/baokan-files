Title: Czechoslovak Exhibition Opens in Moscow, Novotný and Khrushchev Speak at Opening Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1960-05-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 3rd: The "Czechoslovakia in 1960" exhibition, held to commemorate the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia, grandly opened today in Moscow's Sokolniki Park.

Attending the opening ceremony were the Czechoslovak party and government delegation headed by Novotný and Soviet Communist Party and state leaders Khrushchev, Voroshilov, and others.

Novotný and Khrushchev both spoke at the opening ceremony.

The Czechoslovak party and government delegation, headed by Novotný, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and President of the Republic, arrived in Moscow on the 2nd.

The delegation is here to attend the opening ceremony of the "Czechoslovakia in 1960" exhibition, which opened in Moscow on the 3rd.

### 捷展览会在莫斯科开幕  诺沃提尼和赫鲁晓夫在开幕式上讲话

1960-05-05
第6版()
专栏：

    捷展览会在莫斯科开幕
    诺沃提尼和赫鲁晓夫在开幕式上讲话
    新华社莫斯科3日电　为纪念捷克斯洛伐克解放十五周年而举办的“1960年的捷克斯洛伐克”展览会今天在莫斯科的索科尼基公园隆重开幕。
    出席开幕式的，有以诺沃提尼为首的捷克斯洛伐克党政代表团和苏联共产党和国家的领导人赫鲁晓夫、伏罗希洛夫等。
    诺沃提尼和赫鲁晓夫都在开幕式上讲了话。
    以捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会第一书记、共和国总统诺沃提尼为首的捷克斯洛伐克党政代表团，2日抵达莫斯科。
    代表团是前来参加3日在莫斯科开幕的“1960年的捷克斯洛伐克”展览会开幕式的。
