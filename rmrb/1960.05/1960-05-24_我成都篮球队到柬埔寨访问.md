Title: Chengdu Basketball Team Visits Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1960-05-24
Page: 5
　
Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, May 22nd – The Chinese Chengdu Basketball Team, led by Li Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Sichuan Provincial Branch of the All-China Sports Federation, arrived here this morning at the invitation of the Cambodian Sports Association. The Chinese basketball team is a return visit for the Cambodian national basketball team's visit to China last July and August.

The Chinese basketball team will visit here for three weeks and conduct friendly matches with Cambodian basketball players and overseas Chinese basketball players.

### 我成都篮球队到柬埔寨访问

1960-05-24
第5版()
专栏：

    我成都篮球队到柬埔寨访问
    新华社金边22日电　由中华全国体育总会四川省分会副主席李镇率领的中国成都篮球队应柬埔寨体育协会的邀请，今天上午到达这里。中国篮球队是对柬埔寨国家篮球队去年7、8月间访问中国的回访。
    中国这个篮球队将在这里访问三个星期，同柬埔寨篮球运动员和华侨篮球运动员进行友谊比赛。
