Title: Thoroughly Expose Eisenhower's Peace Disguise: Shanxi People's Representatives and CPPCC Members, and Heads of Various People's Organizations in Inner Mongolia, Issue Statements Supporting Khrushchev's Declaration
Author:
Date: 1960-05-21
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Taiyuan, May 20th: The representatives of the Shanxi Provincial People's Congress and the members of the Shanxi Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) resolutely support the statement made by Chairman Khrushchev of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union at the preliminary meeting of the Four Power Summit, and sternly condemn the aggression and provocation of the socialist camp by U.S. imperialism.

    Bai Qingshan, Provincial People's Representative, Model Worker, and Head of the Equipment and Power Section of the Taishan Mining Machinery Factory, said: "The face of U.S. imperialism, pretending peace but actually preparing for war, has been thoroughly exposed. I fully support Khrushchev's just statement."

    Guo Yuhui, member of the Shanxi Provincial Committee of the CPPCC and a nationally renowned agricultural model worker, said: "We oppose war, but we are never afraid of aggression. If U.S. imperialism dares to be so crazy as to launch a war, it will never end well."

    Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, May 20th: Guan Buzhabu, Vice Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Federation of Trade Unions, Sha Ti, Deputy Secretary of the Inner Mongolia Committee of the Communist Youth League, Yun Qing, Deputy Director of the Inner Mongolia Women's Federation, Li Shibei, Vice Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Committee of the CPPCC, and Sun Lanfeng, Vice Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, jointly issued written statements on the 19th, resolutely supporting the statement made by Chairman Khrushchev of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union at the preliminary meeting of the Four Power Summit.

    The joint statement issued by Guan Buzhabu and others stated that Chairman Khrushchev's statement on May 16 regarding the intrusion of the American U-2 aircraft into the Soviet Union for espionage activities is completely correct. The people of all ethnic groups in Inner Mongolia, together with the people of the whole country, express their resolute support for this completely reasonable and just position of the Soviet Union.

### 彻底揭穿艾森豪威尔和平伪装  山西人民代表和政协委员内蒙古各人民团体负责人发表谈话支持赫鲁晓夫声明

1960-05-21
第2版()
专栏：

    彻底揭穿艾森豪威尔和平伪装
    山西人民代表和政协委员内蒙古各人民团体负责人发表谈话支持赫鲁晓夫声明
    据新华社太原20日电　山西省人民代表大会的代表和政协山西省委员会的委员们坚决支持苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫在四国首脑会议预备会议上的声明，严厉谴责美帝国主义对社会主义阵营的侵略和挑衅。
    省人民代表、劳动模范、太山矿山机器厂设备动力科科长白清山说：美帝国主义假和平真备战的面目已经彻底被揭露。我完全支持赫鲁晓夫的严正声明。
    政协山西省委员会委员、全国著名的农业劳动模范郭玉惠说：“我们反对战争，但决不害怕侵略，如果美帝国主义竟敢疯狂到胆敢发动战争，它就决不会有好下场。”
    据新华社呼和浩特20日电　内蒙古总工会副主席官布扎布、共青团内蒙古委员会副书记沙梯、内蒙古妇女联合会副主任云清、政协内蒙古委员会副主席李世杯、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会内蒙古分会副主席孙兰峰，19日联合发表书面谈话，坚决支持苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫在四国首脑会议预备会议上的声明。
    官布扎布等人发表的联合谈话说，赫鲁晓夫主席5月16日关于美国U—2式飞机侵入苏联进行间谍活动的声明是完全正确的。内蒙古各族各界人民同全国人民一起，对苏联这一完全合理的严正立场，表示坚决的支持。
