Title: Afghan Ambassador to China Holds Reception to Celebrate Afghanistan's Independence Day
Author:
Date: 1960-05-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, May 27th: Afghan Ambassador to China, Mr. Mohammad Yahya Maroofi Samad, held a reception at the embassy this afternoon to celebrate the Independence Day of the Kingdom of Afghanistan.

Vice Premiers of the State Council, He Long, Li Xiannian, and Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Shen Junru and Guo Moruo, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Geng Biao, were all invited to attend the reception.

Ambassador Samad delivered a speech first, welcoming the guests to the reception. He said that the Independence Day of the Kingdom of Afghanistan is a pleasant and auspicious day. He proposed a toast to Chairman Liu Shaoqi, to the prosperity and happiness of the Chinese people, to the continuous friendly relations between Afghanistan and China, and to peace and friendship between all countries.

In his speech, Vice Premier He Long, on behalf of the Chinese government and people, extended warm congratulations to the government and people of the Kingdom of Afghanistan. Vice Premier He Long praised the government of the Kingdom of Afghanistan for consistently pursuing a policy of peace and neutrality, and for making unremitting efforts and positive contributions to promoting the Bandung spirit, adhering to Asian-African solidarity, and safeguarding world peace. He said that the traditional friendship between China and Afghanistan is being consolidated and developed on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. The friendly cooperative relations between China and Afghanistan will surely achieve new developments in the future. The Chinese people are pleased with the efforts and achievements made by the Afghan people in developing their national economy. Vice Premier He Long concluded by proposing a toast to the health of His Majesty King Mohammed Zahir Shah of the Kingdom of Afghanistan.

Also attending the reception were people from all walks of life in the capital and envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries to China.

### 庆祝阿富汗独立纪念日  阿富汗驻华大使举行招待会

1960-05-28
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝阿富汗独立纪念日
    阿富汗驻华大使举行招待会
    新华社27日讯　阿富汗驻中国大使萨马德今天下午在大使馆举行招待会，庆祝阿富汗王国独立纪念日。
    国务院副总理贺龙、李先念、习仲勋，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长沈钧儒、郭沫若，外交部副部长耿飚等，都应邀出席了招待会。
    萨马德大使首先致词，欢迎客人们出席招待会。他说，阿富汗王国独立纪念日是一个愉快和吉祥的日子。他提议，为刘少奇主席，为中国人民的繁荣和幸福，为阿富汗和中国之间的持续的友好关系，为各国间的和平和友谊，干杯。
    贺龙副总理在致词时代表中国政府和人民向阿富汗王国政府和人民致以热烈的祝贺。贺龙副总理赞扬阿富汗王国政府一贯奉行和平中立政策，对发扬万隆精神、坚持亚非团结和维护世界和平作出了不懈的努力和积极的贡献。他说，中阿两国的传统友谊在和平共处五项原则的基础上正在日益巩固和发展。中阿两国的友好合作关系今后必将获得新的发展。中国人民对阿富汗人民在发展民族经济方面的努力和所取得的成就感到高兴。贺龙副总理最后提议为阿富汗王国国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿陛下的健康干杯。
    出席招待会的，还有首都各方面的人士和各国驻中国的使节和外交官员。
