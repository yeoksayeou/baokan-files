Title: CPI Secretary Ramamurti Condemns U.S. Sabotage of Four-Power Conference
Author:
Date: 1960-05-25
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 23rd. CPI Secretariat Secretary Ramamurti, speaking at a mass meeting of port and dock workers in Madras on the 22nd, condemned the United States for sabotaging the four-power summit conference.

    In condemning the provocative act of the United States in sending spy planes to invade Soviet airspace, Ramamurti said, "If such a challenge can be made to the Soviet Union with impunity, what would the Americans not do to smaller countries without adequate means of self-defense?"
    Ramamurti said, "Therefore, the issue is not merely a matter of offending the self-esteem and dignity of the Soviet Union, but a matter of respecting the fundamental principle of the sovereignty of other countries (whether large or small). Khrushchev's refusal to yield to the conspiracy of the United States is, in effect, also safeguarding the honor and self-esteem of smaller countries from being undermined by the shameless acts of the United States."

### 印共书记处书记拉马穆蒂  斥美国破坏四国会议

1960-05-25
第4版()
专栏：

    印共书记处书记拉马穆蒂
    斥美国破坏四国会议
    新华社新德里23日电　印度共产党书记处书记拉马穆蒂22日在马德拉斯举行的港口和码头工人群众大会上讲话时，谴责美国破坏了四国首脑会议。
    拉马穆蒂在谴责美国派遣间谍飞机侵犯苏联领空的挑衅行为时说，“如果可以对苏联进行这种挑战而不受到惩罚的话，美国人对于没有充分自卫手段的较小国家还有什么干不出来的事呢。”
    拉马穆蒂说，“因此，涉及的问题不仅仅是触犯苏联的自尊和尊严的问题，而是尊重其他国家（不论这个国家是大是小）主权的基本原则的问题。赫鲁晓夫拒绝向美国的阴谋让步，实际上也是维护较小国家的荣誉和自尊，使它们不受美国的厚颜无耻的行为所损害。”
