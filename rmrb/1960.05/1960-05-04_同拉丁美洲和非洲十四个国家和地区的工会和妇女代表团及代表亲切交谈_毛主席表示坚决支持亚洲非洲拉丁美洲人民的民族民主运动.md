Title: Cordial Conversation with Trade Union and Women's Delegations and Representatives from Fourteen Countries and Regions in Latin America and Africa: Chairman Mao Expressed Firm Support for the National Democratic Movement of the Peoples of Asia, Africa, and Latin America

Author:

Date: 1960-05-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 3rd. Chairman Mao Zedong met here this afternoon with trade union and women's delegations and representatives from fourteen countries and regions in Latin America and Africa who are currently visiting China. They are: the Cuban Workers' Confederation Delegation, the Brazilian National Public Servants Union and the Brazilian National Bank Workers Union Delegation; the women's delegations from Sierra Leone and Tanganyika and Basutoland women representatives; the women's delegations from Cuba, Venezuela and Uruguay and women representatives from Brazil, Colombia, Peru, Paraguay, Costa Rica, Honduras, Puerto Rico, Martinique.

Chairman Mao Zedong had a cordial conversation with these friends, expressing the firm support of the 650 million Chinese people for the national democratic movements currently being carried out by the people of Cuba and the entire Asia, Africa, and Latin America, thanking them for their support and assistance to New China, and pointing out: Our common enemy is U.S. imperialism, and we are all standing on the same front. We need to unite and support each other. Chairman Mao Zedong said that all the people of the world, including the American people, are our friends. He also expressed his support for the upcoming Four-Power Summit Conference. The friends from these countries also spoke about the current situation in their respective countries regarding the struggle against imperialism, for the attainment and maintenance of national independence and democracy, and expressed their determination and confidence to overcome all difficulties and strive for final victory.

After seeing Chairman Mao Zedong, the friends from various countries stepped forward to shake hands cordially with Chairman Mao and took photos with him. After the conversation, they enthusiastically and reluctantly sang the newly learned song "The East is Red" to bid farewell to Chairman Mao. Chairman Mao also personally escorted the guests to the door and waved goodbye to them.

Also present at the meeting today were Liu Changsheng, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Cao Mengjun, member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation. (Attached picture)

### 同拉丁美洲和非洲十四个国家和地区的工会和妇女代表团及代表亲切交谈  毛主席表示坚决支持亚洲非洲拉丁美洲人民的民族民主运动

1960-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

    同拉丁美洲和非洲十四个国家和地区的工会和妇女代表团及代表亲切交谈
    毛主席表示坚决支持亚洲非洲拉丁美洲人民的民族民主运动
    他指出，我们共同的敌人是美帝国主义，我们大家都是站在一条战线上，大家需要互相团结互相支持。全世界人民包括美国人民都是我们的朋友。他表示支持即将召开的四国首脑会议。
    新华社济南3日电　毛泽东主席今天下午在这里接见了正在我国参观访问的来自拉丁美洲和非洲共十四个国家和地区的工会和妇女代表团及代表。他们是：古巴工人联合会代表团、巴西全国公务员工会和巴西全国银行职工联合会代表团；塞拉勒窝内和坦噶尼喀的妇女代表团及巴苏陀兰妇女代表；古巴、委内瑞拉和乌拉圭的妇女代表团及巴西、哥伦比亚、秘鲁、巴拉圭、哥斯达黎加、洪都拉斯、波多黎各、马提尼克的妇女代表。
    毛泽东主席和这些朋友们进行了亲切的交谈，向他们表达了中国六亿五千万人民对古巴以及整个亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲人民目前所进行的民族民主运动的坚决支持，感谢他们对新中国的支持和帮助，并指出：我们共同的敌人是美帝国主义，我们大家都是站在一条战线上，大家需要互相团结互相支持。毛泽东主席说，全世界人民包括美国人民都是我们的朋友。他在谈话中还表示对即将召开的四国首脑会议的支持。这些国家的朋友们也都谈了各自国家当前的反对帝国主义、争取和维护民族独立及民主的斗争情况，并表示了克服一切困难、争取最后胜利的决心和信心。
    各国朋友们见到毛泽东主席后，纷纷上前同毛主席亲切地握手，并和毛主席合影。谈话后他们又热情地、依依不舍地唱着刚刚学会的“东方红”歌曲，向毛主席告别，毛主席也亲自将客人们送到门口，向他们挥手告别。
    今天陪同接见的有中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜，中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会主席团委员曹孟君。（附图片）
