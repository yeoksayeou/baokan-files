Title: Pyongyang, Hanoi, and Ulaanbaatar Hold Rallies and Parades to Celebrate "May Day," Firmly Supporting the Heroic Struggle of the South Korean People
Author:
Date: 1960-05-02
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, May 1st: More than 200,000 people in Pyongyang City held a grand rally and parade today to celebrate the "May Day" International Labor Day.

On the rostrum at the rally were Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of Korea, and Choi Yong-kun, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, and other party and state leaders.

Kim Chang-man, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, spoke at the rally. Kim Chang-man extended festive greetings to all working people and praised their labor achievements.

Kim Chang-man warmly praised the recent heroic struggle of the South Korean people against the U.S.-Syngman Rhee clique.

He called on the South Korean people to continue their tenacious struggle, drive out the U.S. troops, completely abolish the Syngman Rhee puppet regime, dissolve the South Korean bogus "National Assembly," and punish the traitor Syngman Rhee.

The parade began at 9:30. The huge banners carried by the parading masses read: "Increase Production and Construction to Support the Struggle of the South Korean People!" "U.S. Aggressor Troops Get Out of South Korea!" "Settle Accounts with Syngman Rhee's Fascist Rule!" "U.S. Aggressor Troops Get Out of China's Taiwan!" "U.S. Aggressor Troops Get Out of Japan!" "Support the Japanese People's Struggle Against the Japan-U.S. 'Security Treaty'!" The parading masses also held up cartoons satirizing the heavy blows suffered by the U.S. imperialism and the Syngman Rhee clique at the hands of the South Korean people. The parade demonstrated the enthusiastic support of the people of North Korea for the struggle of the South Korean people against the U.S.-Syngman Rhee clique.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 1st: 150,000 people from all walks of life in Hanoi City held a grand rally and parade at Ba Dinh Square this morning to solemnly celebrate "May Day" International Labor Day.

On the reviewing stand were President Ho Chi Minh, Ton Duc Thang, Le Duan, Pham Van Dong, Truong Chinh, Phan Ke Toai, Vo Nguyen Giap, Pham Hung and other party and state leaders.

Premier Pham Van Dong delivered a speech at the rally. He strongly condemned the U.S. imperialist policies of aggression and arms expansion. He called on all the people of Vietnam to resolutely struggle against the U.S. imperialism and its lackey Ngo Dinh Diem clique for their brutal violation of the Geneva Accords and turning South Vietnam into a new-style colony and military base of the U.S. imperialism.

Premier Pham Van Dong called on the people of South Vietnam to strengthen unity and struggle against the U.S. imperialist and its lackeys' policies of aggression and arms expansion, against suppression and persecution, demanding democratic and free rights, and demanding the implementation of the Geneva Accords.

He said that with the support of peace-loving people all over the world, the just struggle of the Vietnamese people for the unification of their motherland will surely be victorious.

In his speech, Pham Van Dong expressed support for the heroic struggle of the South Korean people against the U.S. imperialism and its lackey Syngman Rhee.

After Premier Pham Van Dong's speech, the grand parade began. After the parade, President Ho Chi Minh gave a brief speech calling on people from all walks of life to actively participate in the second National Assembly election to be held on May 8.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, May 1st: The working people of Ulaanbaatar held a grand rally and mass parade in Sukhbaatar Square today to celebrate "May Day" International Labor Day. Mongolian party and state leaders Tsedenbal, Molomjamts, Baljinnnyam, Dugersuren and others attended the celebration.

Baljinnnyam, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Deputy Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural, spoke at the rally. He said: The Mongolian working people are comprehensively carrying out socialist construction and striving to fulfill the tasks of the final year of the three-year plan ahead of schedule and with overfulfilled targets.

Speaking about the international situation, Baljinnnyam said that the imperialist colonial system is increasingly collapsing. The Mongolian people firmly support the large-scale and heroic struggle recently launched by the South Korean people against the reactionary rule of the U.S. imperialism and the Syngman Rhee clique.

Baljinnnyam pointed out that the international imperialist reactionary forces still exist. He condemned the new U.S.-Japan "Security Treaty" for further aggravating the international tensions.

After Baljinnnyam's speech, the troops of the Mongolian People's Army marched past the rostrum, receiving the review of the Mongolian leaders. This was followed by a mighty mass parade with red flags and colored flags held high and bouquets in hand.

### 平壤、河内、乌兰巴托人民集会游行庆祝“五一”节  坚决支持南朝鲜人民的英勇斗争

1960-05-02
第3版()
专栏：

    平壤、河内、乌兰巴托人民集会游行庆祝“五一”节
    坚决支持南朝鲜人民的英勇斗争
    一致谴责美帝国主义的侵略政策　要求美国军队从亚洲滚出去
    据新华社平壤1日电　平壤市二十多万人民今天举行盛大集会和游行，欢庆“五一”国际劳动节。
    在大会主席台上的有，朝鲜内阁首相金日成、最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健等党和国家的领导人。
    朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长金昌满在大会上讲了话。金昌满向全体劳动人民致节日的祝贺，赞扬他们创造的劳动成就。
    金昌满热烈赞扬了南朝鲜人民最近进行的反对美国—李承晚集团的英勇斗争。
    他号召南朝鲜人民继续进行顽强的斗争，赶走美国军队，全面废除李承晚傀儡政权，解散南朝鲜伪“国会”，惩办卖国贼李承晚。
    九时半开始游行。游行群众抬着的巨大的横幅上写着：“增产建设支援南朝鲜人民的斗争！”“美国侵略军从南朝鲜滚出去！”“清算李承晚的法西斯统治！”“美国侵略军从中国的台湾滚出去！”“美国侵略军从日本滚出去！”“支持日本人民反对日美‘安全条约’的斗争！”游行群众还举着一些讽刺美帝国主义和李承晚集团遭到南朝鲜人民的沉重打击的漫画。游行表现了朝鲜北部人民对南朝鲜人民反对美国—李承晚集团的斗争的热烈支持。
    据新华社河内1日电　河内市各界人民十五万人今天上午在巴亭广场举行盛大集会和游行，隆重庆祝“五一”国际劳动节。
    在检阅台上的有，胡志明主席、孙德胜、黎笋、范文同、长征、潘继遂、武元甲、范雄等党和国家领导人。
    范文同总理在会上发表了讲话。他强烈谴责美帝国主义的侵略、备战政策。他号召越南全体人民坚决斗争，反对美帝国主义及其走狗吴庭艳集团粗暴地违反日内瓦协议、把南越变成美帝国主义的新式殖民地和军事基地。
    范文同总理号召南越人民加强团结、斗争，反对美帝国主义及其走狗的侵略和备战政策；反对镇压、迫害行动；要求民主自由权利；要求履行日内瓦协议。
    他说，在全世界爱好和平人民的支持下，越南人民为统一祖国而进行的正义斗争一定会取得胜利。
    范文同在讲话中表示支持南朝鲜人民反对美帝国主义及其走狗李承晚的英勇斗争。
    范文同总理讲话后，盛大的游行开始。游行结束后，胡志明主席在简短讲话中号召各界人民踊跃参加即将于5月8日举行的第二届国会选举。
    据新华社乌兰巴托1日电　乌兰巴托劳动人民今天在苏赫巴托尔广场举行盛大集会和群众游行，庆祝“五一”国际劳动节。蒙古党和国家领导人泽登巴尔、莫洛姆扎木茨、巴尔吉尼雅姆、杜格苏伦等人出席了庆祝大会。
    蒙古人民革命党中央政治局委员、大人民呼拉尔主席团副主席巴尔吉尼雅姆在会上讲了话。他说：蒙古劳动人民正在全面开展社会主义建设，争取提前和超额完成三年计划最后一年的任务。
    巴尔吉尼雅姆在谈到国际形势时说，帝国主义殖民体系正日趋崩溃。蒙古人民坚决支持南朝鲜人民最近展开的反对美帝国主义和李承晚集团反动统治的大规模的英勇斗争。
    巴尔吉尼雅姆指出，目前国际帝国主义反动势力仍然存在。他指责新的美日“安全条约”进一步加剧了国际紧张局势。
    巴尔吉尼雅姆讲话后，蒙古人民军部队走过主席台，接受蒙古领导人的检阅。随后是高举红旗彩旗、手持花束的浩浩荡荡的群众游行队伍。
