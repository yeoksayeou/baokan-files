Title: Deep Sympathy, Resolute Support
Author:
Date: 1960-05-01
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 29th—More than 60,000 people from all walks of life in Nanjing gathered and paraded this afternoon, resolutely supporting the patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people. The meeting began at 3:00 p.m. Peng Chong, alternate secretary of the Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and first secretary of the Nanjing Municipal Committee, spoke at the meeting. He said: The people of Jiangsu Province and Nanjing City, like the people of the whole country, express great indignation at the bloody atrocities of U.S. imperialism and the Syngman Rhee clique in massacring and suppressing South Korean demonstrators, and express deep sympathy and resolute support for the patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people. Peng Chong said that as the patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people achieved successive victories, the heroic Japanese people also launched a new upsurge in the struggle against the Japan-U.S. "Security Treaty." This is a new development in the Japanese people's anti-U.S. struggle. We resolutely support the just struggle of the Japanese people.
    Subsequently, representatives from all walks of life spoke at the meeting, unanimously expressing their resolute support for the patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people.

### 深切的同情　坚决的支持

1960-05-01
第4版()
专栏：

    深切的同情　坚决的支持
    南京
    新华社南京29日电　南京市各界人民六万多人，今天下午集会游行坚决支持南朝鲜人民的爱国正义斗争。大会在下午三时开始，中共江苏省委候补书记、南京市委第一书记彭冲在大会上讲话。他说：江苏全省及南京市人民和全国人民一样，对美帝国主义和李承晚集团屠杀和镇压南朝鲜示威群众的血腥暴行，表示极大的愤怒，对南朝鲜人民的爱国正义斗争表示深切的同情和坚决的支持。彭冲说，在南朝鲜人民的爱国正义斗争取得节节胜利时，英勇的日本人民也掀起了反对日美“安全条约”斗争的新高潮，这是日本人民反美斗争的新发展。我们坚决支持日本人民的正义斗争。
    接着，各界代表先后在会上讲话，他们一致表示坚决支持南朝鲜人民的爱国正义斗争。
