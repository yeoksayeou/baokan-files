Title: The Chinese and Soviet People Condemn American Aggression; Capital to Hold Grand Rally Today Supporting the Soviet Union's Just Position
Author:
Date: 1960-05-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency report: People from all walks of life in Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City, Shaanxi Province and Xi'an City, and Chongqing City have expressed their full support for the Soviet government's statement and strongly condemned the United States for insisting on aggression and undermining the Four-Power Conference.
    After the Soviet government's statement was transmitted to Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City, people from all walks of life issued statements expressing their determination to stand with the Soviet people forever, overthrow American imperialism, and defend world peace.
  The Shaanxi Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference held a meeting on May 19. Attendees pointed out that the Soviet government's statement was entirely a just voice and expressed their firm support for the statement. The 1,600 representatives attending the Shaanxi Provincial Conference of Advanced Collectives and Advanced Workers in Education, Culture, Science, Health, and Sports, currently being held in Xi'an, wrote a letter to the newspaper expressing their support for the Soviet Union's solemn position.
    Many factories and mines in Chongqing City and more than 180 reading groups in the Sino-Soviet Friendship People's Commune in the suburbs held discussions to support the Soviet government's statement and strongly condemn the criminal activities of American imperialism. (Detailed reports on page 5)
    Xinhua News Agency report: Representatives from all walks of life in Moscow held a conference at the Tchaikovsky Conservatory on the 18th, unanimously supporting Comrade Khrushchev's statement and strongly condemning the US imperialist crime of violating Soviet airspace. At the meeting, Soviet Peace Defense Committee Chairman Tikhonov, All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions Secretary Nikolayeva, and others spoke. They pointed out that Khrushchev's statement exposed the deceptive tricks of the American ruling clique in deceiving the people of all countries, and this statement has received unanimous support from the Soviet Union and the people of the world. (Detailed reports on page 5)
    Xinhua News Agency, May 19th: People from all walks of life in the capital will hold a grand rally on the 20th to support the Soviet Union's just position and oppose US imperialism's sabotage of the Four-Power Summit. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will begin broadcasting the live proceedings of the conference at 2:50 PM on the 20th, using the frequency of the first program. Beijing Television will also broadcast simultaneously.

### 中苏人民纷纷谴责美国侵略  首都今日举行声势浩大集会支持苏联正义立场

1960-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏人民纷纷谴责美国侵略  
    首都今日举行声势浩大集会支持苏联正义立场
    本报讯　据新华社消息：广东省和广州市、陕西省和西安市以及重庆市的各界人民，纷纷表示全力支持苏联政府的声明，严厉谴责美国坚持侵略和破坏四国会议。
    当苏联政府的声明传到广东省和广州市后，各界人民纷纷发表谈话，表示将和苏联人民永远站在一起，打倒美帝国主义和保卫世界和平。
  政协陕西省西安市委员会在5月19日举行了会议，到会者指出苏联政府的声明完全是正义的声音，表示坚决支持这个声明。正在西安召开的陕西省教育、文化、科学、卫生、体育等方面的先进集体和先进工作者代表会议的一千六百名代表，给报纸写了一封信，表示支持苏联的严正立场。
    重庆市的许多厂矿和市郊中苏友好人民公社的一百八十多个读报组，都举行了座谈会，支持苏联政府的声明，严厉谴责美帝国主义的罪恶活动。（详细报道见第五版）
    本报讯　据新华社消息：莫斯科各界代表18日在柴可夫斯基音乐厅举行大会，一致拥护赫鲁晓夫同志的声明，严厉谴责美帝国主义侵犯苏联领空的罪行。在会上，苏联保卫和平委员会主席吉洪诺夫、全苏工会中央理事会书记尼古拉耶娃等都作了讲话。他们指出赫鲁晓夫的声明，向全世界揭露了美国统治集团欺骗各国人民的诡诈把戏，这一声明受到了苏联和全世界人民的一致支持。（详细报道见第五版）
    新华社19日讯　首都各界人民将在20日举行声势浩大的集会，支持苏联正义立场、反对美帝国主义破坏四国首脑会议。中央人民广播电台将从20日下午两点五十分开始，用第一套节目的频率转播大会的实况。北京电视台也将同时转播。
