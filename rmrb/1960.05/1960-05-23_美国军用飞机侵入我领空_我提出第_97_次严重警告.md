Title: U.S. Military Aircraft Intrudes into Our Airspace; We Issue the 97th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-05-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd: On May 21st, from 12:32 to 12:45, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over Shanwei and Sanmen Island in Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. military aircraft, a spokesman for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 97th serious warning.

### 美国军用飞机侵入我领空  我提出第97次严重警告

1960-05-23
第1版()
专栏：

    美国军用飞机侵入我领空
    我提出第97次严重警告
    新华社22日讯　5月21日十二时三十二分至四十五分，美国军用飞机一架侵入我广东省的汕尾、三门岛上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第九十七次严重警告。
