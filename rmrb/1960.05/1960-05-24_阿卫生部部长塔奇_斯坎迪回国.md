Title: Albanian Minister of Health Taci Skandi Returns Home
Author: 
Date: 1960-05-24
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 23rd: Albanian Minister of Health Taci Skandi and his entourage concluded their friendly visit to our country and left Beijing by plane today to return home.

### 阿卫生部部长塔奇·斯坎迪回国

1960-05-24
第5版()
专栏：

    阿卫生部部长塔奇·斯坎迪回国
    新华社23日讯　阿尔巴尼亚卫生部部长塔奇·斯坎迪和随行人员，结束了在我国的友好访问，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
