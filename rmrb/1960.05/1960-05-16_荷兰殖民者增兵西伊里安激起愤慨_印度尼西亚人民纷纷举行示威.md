Title: Dutch Colonialists' Troop Increase in West Irian Arouses Indignation - Indonesian People Hold Demonstrations One After Another
Author:
Date: 1960-05-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 14th - Following Jakarta and Bandung, people in Yogyakarta and Surabaya, Indonesia, also held demonstrations on the 13th to express their indignation at the Dutch colonialists' increase of troops in West Irian.
　　The Indonesian "People's Daily" reported today that in Yogyakarta, the cultural center of Indonesia, approximately 2,500 students and youths held a demonstration filled with anti-imperialist, anti-colonialist and patriotic spirit. They held up signs with slogans such as "Drive the Dutch out" and "Recover West Irian," chanting slogans and singing war songs. Slogans expressing the Indonesian people's determination to recover West Irian were pasted on some of the city's streets, trees, and cars.
　　The demonstrating students and youths also issued a statement expressing their readiness to leave school and drive the Dutch imperialists out of West Irian.
　　In Surabaya, East Java, Indonesia's second largest city, more than 10,000 people also held a large-scale demonstration on the 13th. They held up signs opposing the Dutch imperialists' aggressive actions and demanding that the people unite to deal with the Dutch imperialists.

### 荷兰殖民者增兵西伊里安激起愤慨  印度尼西亚人民纷纷举行示威

1960-05-16
第4版()
专栏：

    荷兰殖民者增兵西伊里安激起愤慨    
    印度尼西亚人民纷纷举行示威
    新华社雅加达14日电　印度尼西亚日惹和泗水人民继雅加达和万隆之后13日也举行了示威，表示他们对荷兰殖民主义者在西伊里安增加军队一事的愤慨。
    印度尼西亚“人民日报”今天报道，在印度尼西亚文化中心日惹，大约有两千五百名学生和青年举行了充满反对帝国主义，反对殖民主义和爱国精神的示威。他们高举上面写着“把荷兰赶出去”、“收复西伊里安”字样的标语牌，高呼口号和唱着战歌。市内的一些大街上、树上和汽车上都贴着表示印度尼西亚人民决心收复西伊里安的标语。
    示威学生和青年还发表声明，表示他们准备离开学校把荷兰帝国主义者赶出西伊里安。
    在印度尼西亚第二大城市东爪哇的泗水，13日也有一万多人举行大规模的示威。他们高举反对荷兰帝国主义者侵略行动和要求人民团结起来对付荷兰帝国主义者的标语牌。
