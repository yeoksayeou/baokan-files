Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Visits Mongolian Industrial United Factory
Author:
Date: 1960-05-29
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, May 28th: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi, accompanied by Chairman Tsedenbal and First Vice Chairman Molomjamts, visited the shoe factory and woolen textile factory of the Mongolian Industrial United Factory in Ulaanbaatar this afternoon. They were warmly welcomed by the factory workers.
	Today, the factory's buildings, workshops, and machines were adorned with Chinese and Mongolian flags and banners welcoming Premier Zhou and the distinguished Chinese guests. As Premier Zhou arrived at the factory, the workers lined the streets, cheering and paying their respects to Premier Zhou and the other Chinese guests. Two female workers presented Premier Zhou with flowers.
	Premier Zhou Enlai visited the various production workshops of the shoe factory and woolen textile factory, carefully observing the production situation and various products, and engaging in friendly conversations with the workers. In the shoe factory, Premier Zhou praised female worker Leham for her excellent work as he watched her skillfully operate the machine. Leham, along with all the workers in the factory, launched a socialist labor emulation campaign to welcome Premier Zhou, completing 130% of the May production target ahead of schedule.
	In the woolen textile factory, Director Oidovdorj introduced the factory's situation, saying that all the factory workers, with high labor enthusiasm, launched a production competition to welcome the distinguished Chinese guests, completing the production plan ahead of schedule on the 23rd. He expressed his gratitude to the Chinese people for their help in building the factory. Subsequently, Premier Zhou visited the various workshops that had just started production this year. The director of the spinning workshop, Padarch, presented Premier Zhou with the mobile red banner they had won in the socialist emulation campaign. He said, "This is the honor our workshop has earned in labor production to welcome the respected Premier." Premier Zhou Enlai, Chairman Tsedenbal, and others took photos with the workers in the workshop. Premier Zhou presented gifts to the factory.
	While visiting the woolen textile factory, Premier Zhou also met with the Chinese technicians working there, encouraging them to humbly learn from the Mongolian workers and do their job well.
	Premier Zhou and Vice Premier Chen Yi came to the finished product workshop, where various colorful woolens and blankets were displayed. Both Premier Zhou and Vice Premier Chen Yi praised these high-quality products.
	As Premier Zhou bid farewell to the factory workers, the director pointed to the rockery and green trees in the courtyard, saying that these were newly built and cultivated by the workers during their spare time to welcome Premier Zhou.
	The factory workers enthusiastically lined the streets to bid farewell to Premier Zhou.
	The Industrial United Factory is one of the earliest and largest factories established in Mongolia, with the shoe factory and woolen textile factory being newly built recently.
	This morning, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi, accompanied by Chairman Tsedenbal and Vice Chairman Maidar, visited the city of Ulaanbaatar.

### 周总理参观蒙古工业联合工厂

1960-05-29
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理参观蒙古工业联合工厂
    新华社乌兰巴托28日电　周恩来总理、陈毅副总理今天下午由泽登巴尔主席、莫洛姆扎木茨第一副主席陪同，在乌兰巴托参观了蒙古工业联合工厂的皮鞋厂和毛纺织厂，受到工厂职工极为热烈的欢迎。
    今天，在工厂的厂房、车间和机器上，到处挂着和插着中蒙两国国旗和欢迎周总理、欢迎中国贵宾的标语。当周总理来到工厂时，职工们夹道欢呼，向周总理和其他中国客人致敬。两个女职工把鲜花献给周总理。
    周恩来总理到皮鞋厂和毛纺织厂的各个生产车间，仔细地参观了生产情况和各种产品，并同工人们亲切谈话。在皮鞋厂，周总理看着女工勒哈姆熟练操纵机器，称赞她的工作很好。勒哈姆和全厂工人一起，为迎接周总理而开展社会主义竞赛，以130％提前超额完成了5月份的生产任务。
    在毛纺织厂，厂长奥伊道布道尔吉介绍工厂情况时说，全厂职工为迎接中国贵宾，以高涨的劳动热情，开展生产竞赛，在23日提前完成了生产计划。他对中国人民帮助建设这个工厂，表示感谢。随后，周总理到今年才投入生产的各个车间参观。纺纱车间主任帕达尔奇把他们在社会主义竞赛中获得的优胜流动红旗献给周总理。他说，这是我们车间为迎接尊敬的总理，在劳动生产中获得的荣誉。周恩来总理和泽登巴尔主席等在车间里同工人们一起照了相。周总理向工厂赠送了礼品。
    周总理在毛纺织厂参观时，还会见了在这里工作的中国技术人员们，勉励他们虚心向蒙古工人学习，作好工作。
    周总理和陈毅副总理来到成品车间，这里陈列着色泽鲜艳的各种毛呢、毛毯。周总理和陈毅副总理都称赞这些质量很好的产品。
    当周总理向工厂职工告别时，厂长指着院子里的假山、绿树说，这些都是职工们为迎接周总理，利用工余时间新近修建和培植的。
    工厂职工们热情地夹道欢送了周总理。
    工业联合工厂是蒙古建立最早和最大的工厂之一，其中的皮鞋厂和毛纺织厂是最近新建的。
    今天上午，周恩来总理、陈毅副总理由泽登巴尔主席、迈达尔副主席陪同，参观了乌兰巴托市市容。
