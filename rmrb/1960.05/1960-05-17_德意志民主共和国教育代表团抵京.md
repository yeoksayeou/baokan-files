Title: GDR Education Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-17
Page: 7

    GDR Education Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, May 15th: According to the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement, the German Democratic Republic Education Delegation, consisting of six members and led by Margot Honecker, Deputy Minister of the National Education Ministry, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 15th.

### 德意志民主共和国教育代表团抵京

1960-05-17
第7版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国教育代表团抵京
    据新华社15日讯　根据中德文化合作协定1960年执行计划的规定，德意志民主共和国教育代表团一行六人，在国民教育部副部长马戈特·霍内克率领下，已在15日下午到达北京。
