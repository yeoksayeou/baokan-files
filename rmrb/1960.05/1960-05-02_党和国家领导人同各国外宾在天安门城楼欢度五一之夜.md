Title: Party and State Leaders Celebrate May Day Evening with Foreign Guests on Tiananmen Rostrum
Author:
Date: 1960-05-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 1st May. On the joyous "May Day" evening, Party and State leaders Zhu De, Lin Biao, Deng Xiaoping, Dong Biwu, Peng Zhen, He Long, Li Fuchun, Guo Moruo, and others cordially met foreign guests from over sixty countries and regions across five continents on the Tiananmen Rostrum, extending warm festive greetings to them and, through them, to the working people of the world.

	As evening fell, Tiananmen Square was completely immersed in a sea of joy. The majestic Tiananmen Rostrum was adorned with red flags, and eight large red palace lanterns shone brightly. Countless electric lights镶 on the buildings surrounding the square and silver searchlight beams interwoven in the sky above Tiananmen reflected each other. On the Tiananmen Rostrum, Party and State leaders, together with the Provisional Government Delegation of the Republic of Algeria headed by Krim Belkacem, the General Secretary of the People’s Socialist Party of Cuba Blas Roca and his wife, the Polish Military Delegation headed by Bożywoovski, the President of the Democratic Union of French Somaliland and former Prime Minister Habibi, foreign guests from countries and regions such as the Soviet Union, North Korea, Japan, Congo, and Brazil who were invited to visit China, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to China, watched the mass celebration and colorful fireworks and firecrackers in Tiananmen Square.

	After the first batch of fireworks and firecrackers were set off, Party and State leaders came to both sides of the Tiananmen Rostrum and cordially met with all the foreign guests. They shook hands with the foreign guests one by one, exchanging warm congratulations. Some foreign guests cheered excitedly during the meeting. The Tiananmen Rostrum was filled with a warm atmosphere of unity and friendship between the Chinese people and the people of all countries from beginning to end.

### 党和国家领导人同各国外宾在天安门城楼欢度五一之夜

1960-05-02
第1版()
专栏：

    党和国家领导人同各国外宾
    在天安门城楼欢度五一之夜
    新华社1日讯　欢乐的“五一”之夜，党和国家的领导人朱德、林彪、邓小平、董必武、彭真、贺龙、李富春、郭沫若等，在天安门城楼亲切会见了来自五大洲六十多个国家和地区的外宾，向他们并通过他们向全世界劳动人民致以热烈的节日祝贺。
    入晚，天安门广场完全沉浸在欢乐的海洋里。巍峨的天安门城楼上红旗招展，八盏大红宫灯闪耀着光辉。镶在广场周围建筑物上的无数电灯，和交织在天安门上空的银色探照灯光柱，互相辉映。在天安门城楼上，党和国家的领导人，同以克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆为首的阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团，古巴人民社会党总书记布拉斯·罗加和夫人，以博齐沃夫斯基为首的波兰军事代表团，法属索马里兰民主联盟主席、前总理哈比，应邀来我国访问的苏联、朝鲜、日本、刚果、巴西等国家和地区的外宾，以及各国驻华使节们，一起观看了天安门广场上群众的大联欢和五彩缤纷的礼花和焰火。
    燃放第一批礼花和焰火之后，党和国家领导人来到天安门城楼两侧，同所有的外宾亲切会见。他们和外宾们一一握手，互致热情的祝贺，有的外宾在会见时激动地欢呼起来。天安门城楼上自始至终洋溢着中国人民和各国人民团结、友好的热烈气氛。
