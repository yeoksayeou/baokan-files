Title: Liu Changsheng Receives Thiam Baidy Ly
Author:
Date: 1960-05-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 22nd - Liu Changsheng, President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, received Thiam Baidy Ly, member of the Central Committee of the African Independence Party, Secretary-General of the West African-Asian Solidarity Committee, and guest from Senegal, this afternoon, and had a cordial conversation with him.

Liu Changsheng, on behalf of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, presented the documentary film "Hold High the Banner of the Bandung Conference and Support the Struggle of the African People" to the guest.

### 刘长胜接见提迪安·巴依迪·黎

1960-05-23
第5版()
专栏：

    刘长胜接见提迪安·巴依迪·黎
    新华社22日讯　中国非洲人民友好协会会长刘长胜今天下午接见了非洲独立党中央委员、西非亚非团结委员会秘书长、塞内加尔客人提迪安·巴依迪·黎，并且同他进行了亲切的谈话。
    刘长胜代表中国非洲人民友好协会向客人赠送了纪录影片“高举万隆会议旗帜，支持非洲人民斗争。”
