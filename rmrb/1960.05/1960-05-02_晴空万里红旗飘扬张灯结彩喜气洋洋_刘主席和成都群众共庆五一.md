Title: Clear Sky, Red Flags Flying, Lanterns and Decorations, Overflowing with Joy: Chairman Liu Celebrates May Day with the People of Chengdu
Author:
Date: 1960-05-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, May 1st: Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman of the People's Republic of China, celebrated International Labor Day "May 1st" today with people from all walks of life in Sichuan Province and Chengdu.

When Chairman Liu Shaoqi arrived at the venue, the whole place erupted in cheers. Chairman Liu continuously waved to the jubilant masses, and the cheers and thunderous applause lasted for a long time. The masses carried charts and models of various production records, reporting their production and work achievements to Chairman Liu Shaoqi.

Also participating in the celebration with Chairman Liu Shaoqi were Li Dazhang, Secretary of the Sichuan Provincial Party Committee and Governor of Sichuan Province; Liao Zhigao, Chen Gang, Du Xinyuan, and Zhao Cangbi, Secretaries of the Provincial Party Committee; Liao Jingdan, First Secretary of the Chengdu Municipal Party Committee; and responsible persons of the provincial and municipal local organizations of various democratic parties and people's organizations.

Today, the sky was clear, and red flags were flying. Chengdu was decorated with lanterns and colorful streamers everywhere, overflowing with joy.

A total of 500,000 people in the city held celebrations in eight districts. The celebration meeting attended by Chairman Liu Shaoqi included more than 30,000 people, including workers, farmers, soldiers, government cadres, students, and residents. At the meeting, Peng Guangwei, Chairman of the Sichuan Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, and representatives of workers, farmers, and students spoke.

Chairman Liu Shaoqi had cordial conversations with advanced representatives of workers and farmers from Sichuan Province and Chengdu who participated in the celebration. He inquired about their work and living conditions.

After the meeting, 500,000 people in the city, holding red flags and shouting slogans, held celebratory parades in more than a dozen routes. The whole city was immersed in a warm and joyous atmosphere from morning to night.

On the eve of "May 1st," Chairman Liu Shaoqi also attended the Sichuan Province and Chengdu people's celebration of the "May 1st" International Labor Day evening party. Before the meeting, Chairman Liu Shaoqi met with the responsible persons of the leading organs of the Party, government, and army in Sichuan Province and Chengdu, and Soviet and Hungarian experts in Chengdu.

### 晴空万里红旗飘扬张灯结彩喜气洋洋  刘主席和成都群众共庆五一

1960-05-02
第2版()
专栏：

    晴空万里红旗飘扬张灯结彩喜气洋洋
    刘主席和成都群众共庆五一
    据新华社成都1日电　中共中央副主席、中华人民共和国主席刘少奇，今天同四川和成都市的各界人民一起庆祝“五一”国际劳动节。
    刘少奇主席来到会场时，全场欢声雷动。刘主席不断向欢腾的群众挥手致意，全场欢呼声和暴风雨般的掌声经久不息。群众抬着各种生产纪录的图表、模型，向刘少奇主席汇报他们生产、工作的成绩。
    和刘少奇主席一起参加庆祝大会的，有中共四川省委书记、四川省省长李大章，省委书记廖志高、陈刚、杜心源、赵苍璧，中共成都市委第一书记廖井丹以及各民主党派省市地方组织和各人民团体的负责人。
    今天，万里晴空，红旗飘扬。成都市处处张灯结彩，喜气洋洋。
    全市共有五十万人分八个区举行了庆祝活动。刘少奇主席参加的庆祝会，包括工人、农民、部队、机关干部、学生、居民共三万多人。会上，四川省工会联合会主席彭光伟，以及工人、农民、学生的代表讲了话。
    刘少奇主席和参加庆祝大会的四川省、成都市工人、农民先进代表人物进行了亲切的谈话。询问了他们的工作和生活情况。
    大会结束后，全市五十万群众举着红旗，高呼口号，分成十几路举行了庆祝游行。整个城市从早到晚沉浸在热烈欢腾的气氛里。
    “五一”前夕，刘少奇主席还参加了四川省、成都市各界人民庆祝“五一”国际劳动节晚会。会前，刘少奇主席接见了四川省、成都市党、政府、军队领导机关的负责人，和在成都的苏联、匈牙利专家。
