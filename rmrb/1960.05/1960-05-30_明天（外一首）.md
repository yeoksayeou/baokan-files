Title: Tomorrow (and another poem)
Author: Molihe Jiefudaiite
Date: 1960-05-30
Page: 8

    Tomorrow (and another poem)
    [Turkish] Molihe Jiefudaiite All hope rests on tomorrow. Joy trembles in my heart. Just a moment more, and see—the sun will shine all over the world.

  Telegraph to the poet Jiahede Saiteke No, you cannot rest. As long as you close your eyes, our poor Turkey will sound in your heart. You get out of bed and continue writing. Now, my brother, you are no longer yourself. You are like a telegraph at night, continuously receiving sounds from the vast darkness and sending them to all parts of the world. When Turkey is still in tears, you cannot, nor should you, rest. Everything in the world must change, you should continue to work hard. Let your words echo again and again, like aviation signals in the fog, throughout the country, until dawn breaks in the East.
              (Translated by Yao Wan)

### 明天（外一首）
默里赫·杰甫代特
1960-05-30
第8版()
专栏：

    明天（外一首）
    〔土耳其〕　默里赫·杰甫代特 一切希望寄托在明天。欢乐颤动在我的心间。只要再过片刻呀，看——太阳就会把世界照遍。
  电报机致诗人贾赫德·塞特克不，你不能够安寝。只要你一合上眼睛，我们可怜的土耳其，就会在你心中发出声音。你下得床来继续写作。如今，我的兄弟，你不再是你自己。你像夜间的电报机，从茫茫的黑暗里，不间断地收录下了声音，又把它发向世界各地，当土耳其还噙着泪水，你不能够，也不应该休息。世界上的一切必须改变，你应当继续努力。要让你的话一遭又一遭，像雾中的航空信号，响彻在国土上，一直到东方破晓。
              （姚万译）
