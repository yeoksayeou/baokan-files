Title: US Imperialism is the Common Enemy of People All Over the World
Author:
Date: 1960-05-01
Page: 4

Changsha
Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, 30th - Seventy thousand people from all walks of life in Hunan Province and Changsha City gathered and paraded yesterday afternoon to support the patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people.

    Tan Yu bao, Secretary of the Hunan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, spoke at the meeting. He said that the struggle of the South Korean people against the fascist rule of the US-backed Syngman Rhee clique and for national liberation and democratic freedom is a part of the world's anti-imperialist struggle. He strongly condemned the US imperialism for interfering in the internal affairs of South Korea and assisting the Syngman Rhee traitorous clique in slaughtering the South Korean people, pointing out that US imperialism is the most ferocious, barbaric, and brutal enemy of the Korean people and the people of the world. He said that only by driving US imperialism out of South Korea can the South Korean people get rid of poverty and enslavement.
    Subsequently, the heads of various people's organizations and local organizations of democratic parties, as well as representatives of workers, peasants, militiamen, urban commune members, and students, spoke one after another. They unanimously condemned the crimes of US imperialism and the reactionary clique in slaughtering the South Korean people, and expressed their firm support for the patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people.

### 美帝国主义是全世界人民的公敌

1960-05-01
第4版()
专栏：

    美帝国主义是全世界人民的公敌
    长沙
    新华社长沙30日电　湖南省和长沙市各界人民七万人29日下午集会游行，支援南朝鲜人民的爱国正义斗争。
    中共湖南省委书记谭余保在大会上讲话。他说，南朝鲜人民为反对美李集团法西斯统治、争取民族解放和民主自由进行的斗争，是全世界反帝斗争的一部分。他强烈谴责美帝国主义干涉南朝鲜内政、帮助李承晚卖国集团屠杀南朝鲜人民的暴行，指出美帝国主义是朝鲜人民和全世界人民最凶恶、最野蛮、最残暴的敌人。他说，只有把美帝国主义从南朝鲜赶出去，南朝鲜人民才能摆脱贫困和奴役。
    接着，各人民团体和各民主党派地方组织的负责人，以及工人、农民、民兵、城市公社社员和学生代表等相继讲话。他们一致谴责美帝和反动集团屠杀南朝鲜人民的罪行，表示坚决支援南朝鲜人民的爱国正义斗争。
