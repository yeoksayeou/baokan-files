Title: U.S. Military Aircraft Intrudes into Our Airspace; 98th Serious Warning Issued
Author:
Date: 1960-05-24
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, 23rd -- On May 22nd, from 14:28 to 14:50, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into our airspace over the Shantou to Sanmen Island area of Guangdong Province. From 09:20 to 09:30 on May 23rd, a U.S. naval reconnaissance patrol aircraft intruded into the airspace above Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, a spokesman for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 98th serious warning.

### 美国军用飞机侵入我领空  我提出第98次严重警告

1960-05-24
第1版()
专栏：

    美国军用飞机侵入我领空
    我提出第98次严重警告
    新华社23日讯　5月22日十四时二十八分至五十分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省汕头至三门岛地区领空。5月23日九时二十分至三十分，美国海军侦察巡逻机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第九十八次严重警告。
