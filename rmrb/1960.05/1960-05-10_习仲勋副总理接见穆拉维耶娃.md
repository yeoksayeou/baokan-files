Title: Xi Zhongxun, Vice Premier, Receives Muravyeva
Author:
Date: 1960-05-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, May 9th: Vice Premier of the State Council, Xi Zhongxun, received Muravyeva, the Minister of Social Security of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, and Firimonova, the Director of the Bureau for Mothers with Multiple Children who accompanied her, this morning.

Present at the reception were Qian Ying, Minister of Internal Affairs, and Wang Yifu, Vice Minister.

Also present at the reception was Antonov, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China.

### 习仲勋副总理接见穆拉维耶娃

1960-05-10
第6版()
专栏：

    习仲勋副总理接见穆拉维耶娃
    新华社9日讯　国务院副总理习仲勋今天上午接见了俄罗斯苏维埃联邦共和国社会保障部部长穆拉维耶娃和随同前来的多子女母亲管理局局长菲里莫诺娃。
    接见时在座的有内务部部长钱瑛，副部长王一夫。
    接见时，苏联驻华大使馆临时代办安东诺夫也在座。
