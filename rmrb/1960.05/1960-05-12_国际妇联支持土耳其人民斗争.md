Title: International Federation of Women Supports Turkish People's Struggle
Author:
Date: 1960-05-12
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, May 10th: The Women's International Democratic Federation yesterday sent a telegram to Turkish women, supporting the Turkish people's struggle. The telegram expressed solidarity with the Turkish people's struggle against military bases and for improved living standards.

### 国际妇联支持土耳其人民斗争

1960-05-12
第3版()
专栏：

    国际妇联支持土耳其人民斗争
    新华社柏林10日电　国际民主妇女联合会昨天打电报给土耳其妇女，支持土耳其人民的斗争。电报表示声援土耳其人民反对军事基地和争取改善生活的斗争。
