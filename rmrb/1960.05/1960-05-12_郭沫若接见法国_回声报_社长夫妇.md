Title: Guo Moruo Receives French "Les Échos" Publisher and Wife
Author:
Date: 1960-05-12
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, received Émile Servan-Schreiber, publisher of the French "Les Échos," and his wife, who are currently visiting China, this morning. Zhou You, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Journalists Association, was present at the meeting.

### 郭沫若接见法国“回声报”社长夫妇

1960-05-12
第3版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见法国“回声报”社长夫妇
    新华社10日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席、中国科学院院长郭沫若，今天上午接见了正在我国访问的法国“回声报”社长埃米尔·塞文—希莱伯和他的夫人。接见时在座的有中华全国新闻工作者协会书记处书记周游。
