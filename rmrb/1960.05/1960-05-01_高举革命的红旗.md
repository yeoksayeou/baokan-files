Title: Raise High the Revolutionary Red Flag
Author: Aiding
Date: 1960-05-01
Page: 8

    Raise High the Revolutionary Red Flag
    Aiding
  "May Day" is a great festival, a festival of struggle for the international working class, and a revolutionary festival for the working people of the world! In commemorating "May Day,"
  we must carry forward the tradition of struggle,
  we must carry forward the spirit of revolution;
  we must raise high the revolutionary red flag! Revolution is the locomotive of history, revolution promotes the death of reactionaries, revolution promotes the growth of new forces, revolution will transform the whole world into communism! The road we have traveled is the road of revolution, and we have created historical miracles in the revolution! One revolution overthrew the three great enemies, and another revolution established socialism! Now,
  someone is making shameless clamors,
  saying that it is possible to only want "peace" and not
    revolution;
  this is modern revisionism! This is truly an unbelievable absurdity! With imperialism still existing in the world, how can we abandon revolution? The enemies of revolution oppress the people of revolution, and the revolutionary people must use revolutionary action to deal with the enemies of revolution! "It is better to stand up and fight than to sit and die!" This is the voice of the South Korean people, and this is
  a truth! Let the facts refute modern revisionists,
  the people of Korea, the people of Japan, the people of Africa, the people of the Americas, revolutionary storms are sweeping through every corner of the world! We want to cheer,
  we want to sing praises,
    we want to support,
      we want to assist! Revolution promotes the Great Leap Forward in our country, and revolution also connects us with the oppressed
  people of the whole world! We want to share fate and
  breathe together with the people of the whole world! Revolution is eternal,
  the revolutionary red flag will never fall;
    Long live world revolution!
      Long live "May Day"!

### 高举革命的红旗
艾汀
1960-05-01
第8版()
专栏：

    高举革命的红旗
    艾汀“五一”，是伟大的节日，是国际工人阶级的斗争节日，是世界劳动人民的革命节日！纪念“五一”，
  我们要发扬斗争的传统，
  我们要发扬革命的精神；
  我们要高举革命的红旗！革命是历史的火车头，革命促进反动派的死亡，革命促进新生力量的成长，革命要把全世界变成为共产主
  义！我们走过的道路是革命的道
  路，我们在革命中创造了历史的奇
  迹！一个革命把三大敌人打倒，又一个革命把社会主义建立！现在，
  有人发出无耻的叫嚣，
  说是可以只要“和平”，不
    要革命；
  这就是现代修正主义者！这真是天下奇谈，这真是荒谬绝伦！世界上还有帝国主义存在，怎能抛掉革命？革命的敌人压迫着革命的人
  民，革命的人民就要用革命行动来
  对付革命的敌人！“坐着死不如站起来战斗！”这是南朝鲜人民的声音，这是
  一条真理！让事实来反驳现代修正主义
  者，朝鲜的人民，日本的人民，非
  洲的人民，美洲的人民，世界上各个角落到处都掀起了革命的风暴！我们要欢呼，
  我们要歌颂，
    我们要赞助，
      我们要支援！革命推动我们国家的大跃进，革命又把我们同全世界被压迫
  的人民联系在一起！我们要同全世界的人民，同命
  运，共呼吸！革命是永生的，
  革命的红旗永远不落；
    世界革命万岁！
      “五一”万岁！
