Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives the Inspector General of the Cuban Army
Author:
Date: 1960-05-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 24th. Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi received Major William Gálvez Rodríguez, Inspector General of the Cuban Army, who is currently visiting China, and his wife this afternoon, and held friendly talks with them.

Present at the reception were Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General, and Shen Jian, Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. (Attached picture)

Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi receive Major William Gálvez Rodríguez, Inspector General of the Cuban Army, and his wife.

### 周总理接见古巴军队总督察

1960-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见古巴军队总督察
    新华社24日讯　周恩来总理和陈毅副总理今天下午接见了正在我国访问的古巴军队总督察威廉·加尔维斯·罗德里格斯少校和他的夫人，并同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之、副秘书长吴晓达，外交部美澳司司长申健。（附图片）
