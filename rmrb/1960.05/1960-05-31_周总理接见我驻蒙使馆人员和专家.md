Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Personnel of Our Embassy in Mongolia and Experts
Author: 
Date: 1960-05-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, May 30th: Premier Zhou Enlai met with all the personnel of the Chinese Embassy in Mongolia and representatives of Chinese experts and employees working in Mongolia at 6:30 PM today at the Chinese Embassy in Mongolia, and had cordial talks with them. Vice Premier Chen Yi also attended the meeting.

### 周总理接见我驻蒙使馆人员和专家

1960-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见我驻蒙使馆人员和专家
    新华社乌兰巴托30日电　周恩来总理今天下午六时半在中国驻蒙古大使馆接见了大使馆全体人员和在蒙古工作的中国专家、员工的代表，并同他们进行了亲切的谈话。陈毅副总理也参加了接见。
