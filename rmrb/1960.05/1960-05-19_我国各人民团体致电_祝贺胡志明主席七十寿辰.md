Title: Chinese People's Organizations Send Telegrams to Congratulate President Ho Chi Minh on His Seventieth Birthday
Author:
Date: 1960-05-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Hu Yaobang, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee and Chairman of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and Yang Xiufeng, President of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association, sent telegrams to President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on May 18 to warmly congratulate him on his seventieth birthday.

Liu Ningyi's congratulatory telegram said, "You are the loyal son and long-tested leader of the Vietnamese people. Under the leadership of the Vietnamese Labor Party and yourself, the Vietnamese people have carried out unremitting revolutionary struggles, successively achieving victory in the August Revolution, establishing the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and achieving victory in the nine-year War of Resistance. They have carried out socialist transformation and socialist construction in the completely liberated North Vietnam, achieving great successes, and are continuing to strive to build a peaceful, unified, independent, democratic, and prosperous Vietnam. The Vietnamese people have made and are making important contributions to strengthening the power of the socialist camp, opposing imperialism, and defending world peace."

"We wish you good health and a long life. We wish the Vietnamese people under your leadership even greater achievements in future construction."

Hu Yaobang's congratulatory telegram said: "On the occasion of your seventieth birthday, I would like to extend to you, on behalf of the members of the Chinese Communist Youth League and all Chinese youth, the most sincere and warm congratulations. I wish you eternal health and a long life.

Chinese youth have infinite love and high respect for you—the most beloved leader of the Vietnamese people and youth and an outstanding fighter in the international communist movement. You have made glorious contributions to the victory of the Vietnamese people's revolution and socialist construction, as well as the international communist movement. Chinese youth will unite closely with their fraternal Vietnamese youth to fight together for building socialism and communism, opposing imperialism, and defending world peace."

Cai Chang's congratulatory telegram said: "On the occasion of your seventieth birthday, I extend to you, on behalf of the Chinese women, the warmest congratulations and the highest respect.

I sincerely wish you greater victory in leading the Vietnamese people in the struggle to build socialism and strive for national reunification. And I wish you a long life."

Guo Moruo's congratulatory telegram said: "On the occasion of your seventieth birthday, we would like to extend to you our highest respect and warmest congratulations.

The entire Chinese people admire your outstanding contributions over the decades to the liberation of the Vietnamese people and to the progress of mankind and world peace. We wish the fraternal Vietnamese people, under your wise leadership, will advance from victory to victory and achieve even more brilliant achievements in the great cause of building socialism, striving for national reunification, safeguarding Asian and world peace, and promoting human progress.

We wish you a long life."

Yang Xiufeng's congratulatory telegram said: "On the occasion of your seventieth birthday, you—the most beloved leader of the Vietnamese people and the closest friend of the Chinese people—I would like to extend to you the most sincere and heartfelt congratulations. For decades, you have waged unremitting struggles for the complete liberation of the Vietnamese working people and for Vietnam's national independence and socialist cause, and have achieved great victories. The Chinese people have infinite respect and love for you. We sincerely wish you good health and longevity, and wish you greater victory in leading the Vietnamese people in building socialism, striving for peaceful reunification of Vietnam, and defending the cause of Asian and world peace."

### 我国各人民团体致电  祝贺胡志明主席七十寿辰

1960-05-19
第4版()
专栏：

    我国各人民团体致电
    祝贺胡志明主席七十寿辰
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：中华全国总工会主席刘宁一，中国共产主义青年团中央委员会第一书记胡耀邦，中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会主席蔡畅，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席、中国文学艺术界联合会主席郭沫若，中国越南友好协会会长杨秀峰18日致电越南民主共和国胡志明主席热烈祝贺他的七十寿辰。
    刘宁一的贺电说，您是越南人民的忠实儿子和久经考验的领袖。越南人民在越南劳动党和您的领导下，进行了坚持不懈的革命斗争，先后取得了八月革命的胜利，成立了越南民主共和国，取得了九年抗战的胜利，在完全解放了的越南北方进行社会主义改造和社会主义建设，取得了伟大的成就，并正在为建成一个和平、统一、独立、民主和富强的越南而继续奋斗。越南人民已经并且正在对增强社会主义阵营的威力、反对帝国主义和保卫世界和平做出了重要的贡献。
    祝您身体健康、万寿无疆，祝越南人民在您的领导下，在今后的建设中取得更重大的成就。
    胡耀邦的贺电说：“在您七十寿辰到来的时候，我谨代表中国共青团员和全中国青年向您致以最衷心的热烈祝贺，祝您永远健康，万寿无疆。
    中国青年对您——越南人民和青年最敬爱的领袖和国际共产主义运动的杰出战士，怀着无限的热爱和崇高的敬意。您为越南人民革命和社会主义建设事业以及国际共产主义运动的胜利作出了光荣的贡献。中国青年将紧密地和兄弟的越南青年团结在一起，为建设社会主义和共产主义、反对帝国主义和保卫世界和平而共同奋斗。”
    蔡畅的贺电说：“在您七十大庆的时候，我代表中国妇女向您致以最热烈的祝贺和崇高的敬意。
    谨祝您在领导越南人民建设社会主义和争取祖国统一的斗争中取得更大的胜利。并祝您万寿无疆。”
    郭沫若的贺电说：“欣逢您七十寿诞之期，我们谨向您致最崇高的敬意和最热烈的祝贺。
    全中国人民都敬仰您几十年来为越南人民的解放、为人类进步和世界和平所作的卓越贡献。祝兄弟的越南人民在您的英明领导下，从胜利走向胜利，在建设社会主义和争取祖国统一、维护亚洲和世界和平及促进人类进步的伟大事业中，获得更加辉煌的成就。
    敬祝您万寿无疆。”
    杨秀峰的贺电说，欣逢您——越南人民最敬爱的领袖、中国人民最亲密的朋友七旬大庆之际，谨向您致以最热诚最真挚的祝贺。几十年来，您为越南劳动人民的彻底解放，为越南的民族独立和社会主义事业，进行了坚持不懈的斗争，并且取得了巨大的胜利。中国人民对您怀着无限的敬意和爱戴。我们衷心地祝您健康长寿，祝您在领导越南人民建设社会主义、争取越南和平统一、保卫亚洲和世界和平的事业中取得更大的胜利。
