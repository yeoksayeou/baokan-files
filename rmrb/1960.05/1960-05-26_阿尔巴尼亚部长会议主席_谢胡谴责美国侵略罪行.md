Title: Albanian Chairman of the Council of Ministers Shehu Condemns US Aggression

Author: 
Date: 1960-05-26
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, May 24th: Albanian Chairman of the Council of Ministers Shehu condemned the aggressive acts of US imperialism against the Soviet Union at a rally in the Erseka region on May 23rd, commemorating the seventeenth anniversary of the establishment of a local guerrilla force. Shehu said that this behavior led to the collapse of the four-power summit conference.

    Referring to Khrushchev's statement at the preliminary meeting of the Great Powers Summit, Shehu said that the mask of US imperialism had been torn off, and its true and sinister face had been exposed to all.

### 阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席  谢胡谴责美国侵略罪行

1960-05-26
第2版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席
    谢胡谴责美国侵略罪行
    新华社地拉那24日电　阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席谢胡23日在爱尔赛卡区人民纪念当地的一个游击队建立十七周年的大会上，谴责美帝国主义对苏联的侵略行为。谢胡说，这种行为导致了四国政府首脑会议的垮台。
    谢胡在谈到赫鲁晓夫在大国首脑会议预备会议上的声明时说，美帝国主义的假面具被撕开了，它的真正的凶恶嘴脸已暴露于众目之下。
