Title: Sino-Czech Friendship Association Holds Banquet to Welcome Czechoslovak Friendship Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-05-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 6th

The Sino-Czech Friendship Association held a banquet at noon today to welcome the Czechoslovak Friendship Delegation headed by J. Obaufsky. Those present at the banquet included Xia Yan, President of the Sino-Czech Friendship Association, Vice Presidents Cui Yitian, Secretary General Situ Huimin, and Directors Zhang Pei, Zhou You, Guo Rong, and Yu Yixuan.

Also present at the banquet were Luo Jun, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Xi Zhanyuan, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

Czechoslovak Ambassador to China Bušiňák, Counsellor Šťejkina, and Lehman were also invited to attend the banquet.

### 中捷友好协会举行宴会  欢迎捷克斯洛伐克友好代表团

1960-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

    中捷友好协会举行宴会
    欢迎捷克斯洛伐克友好代表团
    新华社6日讯　中捷友好协会今天中午设宴欢迎以约·奥巴夫斯基为首的捷克斯洛伐克友好代表团。出席宴会的有中捷友协会长夏衍，副会长崔义田，秘书长司徒慧敏，理事张沛、周游、郭荣、喻宜萱。
    出席宴会的还有对外文委副主任罗俊，全国总工会书记处书记郗占元等。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使布希尼亚克，参赞石杰基那、雷曼也应邀出席了宴会。
