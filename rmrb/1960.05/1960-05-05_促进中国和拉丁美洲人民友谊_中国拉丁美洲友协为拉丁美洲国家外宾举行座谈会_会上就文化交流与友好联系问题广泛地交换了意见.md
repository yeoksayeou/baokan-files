Title: Promoting Friendship Between the Chinese and Latin American Peoples: The China-Latin America Friendship Association Holds a Symposium for Foreign Guests from Latin American Countries; Broad Exchange of Views on Cultural Exchange and Friendly Relations at the Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-05-05
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The China-Latin America Friendship Association held a symposium today for foreign guests from Latin American countries who are currently visiting China. These included the Peruvian Cultural Delegation, Brazilian scientists Guerreiro Ramos, Enzman Cavalcanti, Mario Schemberg, the Argentinean renowned lawyer Rodolfo Araoz Alfaro and his wife, the Brazilian agricultural economist Pombeu Accioli Borges and his wife, the Ecuadorian philosophy professor Dr. Medina, and the Chilean painter Julio Escámez. At the meeting, broad exchanges of views were conducted on promoting cultural exchange and friendly relations between the Chinese people and the peoples of various Latin American countries.

The symposium was presided over by Chu Tunan, President of the China-Latin America Friendship Association. Wu Defeng, Zhang Zhixiang, Xiao San, Zhou Erfu, Xi Fengzhou, Hua Junwu, Hu Chuankui, Wang Yuanfang, and other figures from various circles in the capital attended the symposium.

### 促进中国和拉丁美洲人民友谊  中国拉丁美洲友协为拉丁美洲国家外宾举行座谈会  会上就文化交流与友好联系问题广泛地交换了意见

1960-05-05
第6版()
专栏：

    促进中国和拉丁美洲人民友谊
    中国拉丁美洲友协为拉丁美洲国家外宾举行座谈会
    会上就文化交流与友好联系问题广泛地交换了意见
    新华社4日讯　中国拉丁美洲友好协会今天为正在我国访问的秘鲁文化代表团，巴西科学家格雷罗·雷摩斯，恩兹曼·卡瓦尔康蒂，马里奥·谢姆堡，阿根廷著名律师罗多尔夫·阿拉奥斯·阿尔法罗夫妇，巴西农业经济学家彭贝渥·阿克孝里·鲍热斯夫妇，厄瓜多尔哲学教授梅第纳博士和智利画家胡利奥·爱斯卡麦兹等拉丁美洲国家的外宾举行了座谈会，会上就促进中国人民和拉丁美洲各国人民的文化交流与友好联系广泛地交换了意见。
    座谈会由中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南主持，首都各界人士吴德峰、张致祥、萧三、周而复、席凤洲、华君武、胡传揆、王元方等参加了座谈。
