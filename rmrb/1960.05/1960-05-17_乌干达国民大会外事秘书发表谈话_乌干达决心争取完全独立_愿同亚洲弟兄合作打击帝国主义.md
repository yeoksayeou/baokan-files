Title: Uganda National Congress Foreign Affairs Secretary Makes Statement: Uganda Determined to Strive for Complete Independence; Willing to Cooperate with Asian Brothers to Combat Imperialism
Author: 
Date: 1960-05-17
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, May 13th: John Kale, Foreign Affairs Secretary of the Uganda National Congress, said at a press conference in Cairo on the 13th that the Uganda National Congress reiterates its determination to achieve complete independence for Uganda no later than 1961. He said that the people of Uganda are not begging for independence, but are only determined to take back the independence from those who forcibly stole it from them.

Kale said that the Uganda National Congress demands free and universal elections in September 1960, and then the establishment of a national government to negotiate with Britain on the basis of complete independence in early 1961.

Kale said that imperialists and colonialists know that the solidarity of the Asian and African peoples is a force determined to eliminate imperialism and colonialism. He said that when imperialists try to prolong their rule in Africa, "we should cooperate with our Asian brothers in order to give imperialism a fatal blow."

He went on to say, "We wholeheartedly welcome cooperation and friendship on the basis of mutual benefit."

Kale said that the Uganda National Congress calls on all African freedom fighters to unite with the Asian-African Solidarity Movement to promote the success of their struggle for freedom and independence. He reminded people to beware of neo-colonialist countries. He said that these countries, on the one hand, claim to be friendly to African countries, but on the other hand, are strengthening their economic enslavement of Africa.

### 乌干达国民大会外事秘书发表谈话  乌干达决心争取完全独立  愿同亚洲弟兄合作打击帝国主义

1960-05-17
第6版()
专栏：

    乌干达国民大会外事秘书发表谈话
    乌干达决心争取完全独立
    愿同亚洲弟兄合作打击帝国主义
    新华社开罗13日电　乌干达国民大会外事秘书约翰·凯耳13日在开罗的一次记者招待会上说，乌干达国民大会重申，它决心要乌干达最迟在1961年获得完全的独立。他说，乌干达人民不是在乞求独立，而只是决心从那些强行从他们那里抢走独立的人手中把独立夺回来。
    凯耳说，乌干达国民大会要求在1960年9月进行自由的普选，然后建立民族政府，以便在1961年初完全独立的基础上同英国进行谈判。
    凯耳说，帝国主义者和殖民主义者知道，亚非人民的团结是一个决心消灭帝国主义和殖民主义的力量。他说，当帝国主义者企图延长他们在非洲的统治的时候，“我们应该同我们的亚洲弟兄合作，以便给予帝国主义以致命的打击。”
    他接着说，“我们衷心地欢迎在互利的基础上的合作和友谊”。
    凯耳说，乌干达国民大会号召全非洲的自由战士与亚非团结运动结合以促进他们争取自由和独立的斗争的成功，他提醒人们提防新殖民主义国家。他说，这些国家一方面自称同非洲国家友好，但是另一方面却在加强它们对非洲的经济奴役。
