Title: Argentine Cultural Friendship Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, May 5th. At the invitation of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, the four members of the Argentine Cultural Friendship Delegation arrived in Beijing by plane today. Yao Zhen, Vice President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, and others welcomed them at the airport.

The four Argentine guests are: Argentine writer Carlos Daudet, renowned surgeon Ernesto Malbec, renowned dentist José Chudnovsky and his wife, pediatric dentist Ana Chudnovsky.

### 阿根廷文化友好代表团成员到京

1960-05-06
第6版()
专栏：

    阿根廷文化友好代表团成员到京
    新华社5日讯　应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请来我国访问的阿根廷文化友好代表团成员一行四人今天乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长姚溱等。
    这四位阿根廷客人是：阿根廷作家卡洛斯·达乌德特、著名外科医生埃尔内斯托·马耳贝克、著名牙科医生何塞·楚德诺夫斯基和他的夫人、小儿口腔医生安娜·楚德诺夫斯基。
