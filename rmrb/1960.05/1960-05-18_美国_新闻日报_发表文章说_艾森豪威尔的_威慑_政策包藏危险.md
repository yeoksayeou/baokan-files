Title: U.S. "Newsday" Publishes Article Saying Eisenhower's "Deterrence" Policy Contains Dangers
Author:
Date: 1960-05-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 17th Dispatch, TASS: The American "Newsday" published an editorial severely criticizing President Eisenhower and his policies.

The newspaper wrote: "When a person's actions are abnormal, then no one will be surprised if he says some crazy things. To be more precise, when a country pursues an abnormal international policy, it is not surprising if the head of that country says some crazy things. In other words, if the head of a country says some crazy things, then this is enough to show how abnormal the government's policy is."

The newspaper continued: "When President Eisenhower attempted to defend his foolish dispatch of spy planes to violate Soviet airspace, he said, 'The security of the whole world requires' that 'our means of deterrence must not be threatened at any time.' How confused are these words and this idea! It must be known that this was part of a speech carefully prepared for a press conference."

The newspaper criticized the United States for pursuing a "deterrence" policy. It pointed out that this policy contains enormous dangers.

### 美国“新闻日报”发表文章说  艾森豪威尔的“威慑”政策包藏危险

1960-05-18
第5版()
专栏：

    美国“新闻日报”发表文章说
    艾森豪威尔的“威慑”政策包藏危险
    新华社17日讯　据塔斯社纽约16日讯：美国“新闻日报”刊登了一篇编辑部的文章，严厉抨击艾森豪威尔总统和他的政策。
    这家报纸写道：“当一个人的行动不正常的时候，那末他说一些疯话谁也不会感到奇怪的。更正确一点说，当一个国家执行不正常的国际政策的时候，这一国家的首脑就是说了一些疯话，也不会令人感到奇怪。换句话说，如果一个国家的首脑说了一些疯话，那末，这就足以说明这一政府的政策是多么的不正常。”
    报纸接着说：“艾森豪威尔总统企图为自己愚蠢地派遣间谍飞机侵犯苏联领空进行辩护时说，‘全世界的安全要求’‘我们的威慑手段在任何时候都不能遭到威胁’。说这些话和这种想法是多么的混乱啊！须知道这是为记者招待会所精心准备的发言的一部分。”
    这家报纸抨击美国执行“威慑”政策。它指出，这种政策包藏着巨大的危险。
