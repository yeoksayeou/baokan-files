Title: National Gymnastics Championships for Master Athletes Begin
Author:
Date: 1960-05-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency report: The 1960 National Gymnastics Championships for Master Athletes, with 301 male and female athletes participating, began on the afternoon of the 14th at the Shichahai Gymnasium in Beijing.

Nearly half of the athletes participating in this competition are newcomers who have emerged in the past year or two, including 66 male and female athletes who are participating in a national competition for the first time. Therefore, this championship is actually a major review of the new forces in China's gymnastics movement.

Due to the large number of participants (136 men and 165 women), the championships will last until the 21st.

### 全国健将级体操锦标赛开始

1960-05-17
第4版()
专栏：

    全国健将级体操锦标赛开始
    据新华社讯　有三百零一名男女选手参加的1960年全国健将级体操锦标赛，14日下午开始在北京什刹海体育馆中举行。
    参加这次锦标赛的选手，近半数都是近一两年来成长起来的新手，其中有六十六名男女选手还都是第一次参加全国性比赛。因而，这次锦标赛实际上是对我国体操运动新生力量的一次大检阅。
    由于参加的选手多（男一百三十六人，女一百六十五人），锦标赛要到21日结束。
