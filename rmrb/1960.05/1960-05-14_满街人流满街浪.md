Title: Streams of People Fill the Streets, Waves Fill the Streets
Author: Chen Guoping
Date: 1960-05-14
Page: 8
Spring City New Song

    Streams of people fill the streets, waves fill the streets,
    Red flags flutter in the spring breeze,
    White doves circle in the blue sky,
    Streams of people fill the streets, waves fill the streets, ah!
    Ten thousand miles of spring breeze, a city-wide carnival.
    "The Fighting People's Commune is established!"
    The drumbeat strikes people's heartstrings;
    A thousand years of ideals are realized today,
    Tears of joy blur so many eyes.
    Lofty green mountains raise their arms in cheers,
    The surging Songhua River sings a passionate hymn.
    The footsteps of history have entered a new era,
    Products revolve around machines faster and faster.
    Thousands of automatic lines sound the advance drum,
    Thousands of hearts are condensed into an iron belief:
    "Hold high the red flag of Mao Zedong Thought and continue the Great Leap Forward."
    Fifty thousand hands are raised to support the blue sky.
      Harbin　Chen Guoping

### 满街人流满街浪
陈国屏
1960-05-14
第8版()
专栏：春城新歌

    满街人流满街浪
    红旗在春风里招展，
    白鸽在蓝空中回旋，
    满街的人流满街的浪呵！
    万里春风，倾城狂欢。
    “奋斗人民公社成立了！”
    鼓点儿敲打着人们的心弦；
    千万年的理想今天一旦实现，
    欢喜的泪花模住了多少双眼。
    巍巍青山举起手臂欢呼，
    滔滔松花江高唱起激情的礼赞。
    历史的足音走进了新的时代，
    产品围绕着机器更快地飞转。
    千万条自动线擂响了进军鼓，
    千万颗心凝成一个铁的信念：
    “高举毛泽东思想红旗持续大跃进。”
    五万双手举起来撑住了蓝天。
      哈尔滨　陈国屏
