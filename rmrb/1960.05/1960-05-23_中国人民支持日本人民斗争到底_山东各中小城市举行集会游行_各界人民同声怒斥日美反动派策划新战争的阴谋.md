Title: The Chinese People Resolutely Support the Japanese People's Struggle to the End. Shandong's Small and Medium-Sized Cities Hold Rallies and Parades. People From All Walks of Life Unanimously Denounce the US-Japan Reactionaries' Conspiracy to Plot a New War.
Author: 
Date: 1960-05-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 21st - The vast people of small and medium-sized cities in Shandong Province, who have suffered deeply from Japanese aggressors, have been angered by the US-Japan reactionaries' conspiracy to plot a new war. In recent days, people from all walks of life in Yantai, Zibo, Changwei, Liaocheng, Dezhou, and other places have held rallies and parades to oppose the US-Japan military alliance and resolutely support the just struggle of the Japanese people.

More than 20,000 people from all walks of life in Yantai City held rallies and parades, angrily waving their fists and flags, and shouting slogans such as "Oppose the US-Japan Military Alliance Treaty" and "Oppose the Revival of Japanese Militarism." Representatives from all walks of life unanimously condemned the new crimes of US imperialism and the Japanese Nobusuke Kishi clique in antagonizing peace-loving people around the world in their speeches, and expressed their firm support for the just struggle of the Japanese people.

More than 50,000 coal miners and people from all walks of life in the coal mining city of Zibo held a rally. They unanimously pointed out that US imperialism is the common enemy of the people of the world and expressed their firm support for the Japanese people's struggle against the US-Japan military alliance. More than 10,000 people in Weifang City who participated in the demonstration parade expressed their willingness to stand with the Japanese people and completely shatter the war conspiracy of the US-Japan reactionaries. A rally was also held in Dezhou.

### 中国人民支持日本人民斗争到底  山东各中小城市举行集会游行  各界人民同声怒斥日美反动派策划新战争的阴谋

1960-05-23
第4版()
专栏：

    中国人民支持日本人民斗争到底
    山东各中小城市举行集会游行
    各界人民同声怒斥日美反动派策划新战争的阴谋
    据新华社济南21日电　深受日本侵略者之害的山东各中小城市广大人民被美日反动派策划新战争的阴谋激怒了。连日来，烟台、淄博、昌潍、聊城、德州等地各界人民纷纷举行集会和游行，反对日美军事同盟，坚决支持日本人民的正义斗争。
    烟台市各界两万多人举行了集会游行，人们愤怒地挥动着拳头和旗帜，高呼：“反对日美军事同盟条约”、“反对复活日本军国主义”等口号。各界代表在讲话中一致谴责美帝国主义和日本岸信介集团敌视全世界和平人民的新罪行，表示坚决支持日本人民的正义斗争。
    煤矿城市淄博市的煤矿工人和各界人民五万多人举行了集会。他们一致指出：美帝国主义是全世界人民的公敌，表示坚决支援日本人民反对日美军事同盟的斗争。潍坊市参加示威游行的一万多群众表示，要和日本人民站在一起，彻底粉碎日美反动派的战争阴谋。德州市也举行了集会。
