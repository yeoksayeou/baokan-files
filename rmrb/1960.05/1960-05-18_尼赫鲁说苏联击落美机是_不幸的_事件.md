Title: Nehru Says Soviet Downing of US Plane was an "Unfortunate" Incident
Author:
Date: 1960-05-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, May 17th: Indian Prime Minister Nehru, in a meeting with Indian reporters in London on May 7th, discussing the incident of the American plane invading Soviet airspace for espionage activities, actually said that the Soviet downing of an American plane was "unfortunate" and that the "psychological atmosphere" of the summit meeting was "somewhat damaged." Nehru also said at a luncheon held by the Foreign Press Association in London on May 9th that this matter would not have any major impact on the summit meeting.

After concluding the Commonwealth Prime Ministers' Conference, Nehru passed through Frankfurt, West Germany, on May 16th. He met with Knappstein, a senior official of the West German Foreign Ministry, at the airport. Later, this West German official said, "On the German question, Nehru is a friend of ours (West Germany)."

### 尼赫鲁说苏联击落美机是“不幸的”事件

1960-05-18
第4版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁说苏联击落美机是“不幸的”事件
    新华社17日讯　伦敦消息：印度总理尼赫鲁5月7日在伦敦接见印度记者谈到美国飞机侵犯苏联领空进行间谍活动的事件时居然说，苏联击落一架美国飞机的事件是“不幸的”，最高级会议的“心理气氛多少受到了损害”。尼赫鲁还在5月9日在伦敦外国记者协会举行的午餐会上说，这件事对最高级会议不会有什么重大的影响。
    尼赫鲁16日在结束了英联邦总理会议后经过了西德的法兰克福。他在机场上会见了西德外交部高级官员克纳普施太因。后来这位西德官员说，“在德国问题上，尼赫鲁是我们（西德）的朋友。”
