Title: Earthquake Strikes Southern Albania; Government Establishes Special Agency to Properly Resettle Victims
Author:
Date: 1960-05-30
Page: 7

    Earthquake Strikes Southern Albania
    Government Establishes Special Agency to Properly Resettle Victims
    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, May 27th: A severe earthquake struck the city of Korçë in southern Albania on the morning of May 26th. According to current statistics, six people have died, ninety-five have been injured, and five hundred houses have been destroyed in Korçë and the surrounding area. Following this event, the Albanian Council of Ministers immediately established a committee headed by Peçi, Chairman of the State Control Commission, and took emergency measures to rescue the injured residents.
    Currently, twenty-five seriously injured residents have been sent to hospitals for treatment. Residents whose homes were damaged have been properly resettled in schools and tents. All state-owned shops in Korçë have also immediately resumed normal operations to ensure the supply of food and necessities.

### 阿尔巴尼亚南部发生地震  政府成立专门机构妥善安置灾民

1960-05-30
第7版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚南部发生地震
    政府成立专门机构妥善安置灾民
    新华社地拉那27日电　阿尔巴尼亚南部科尔察城26日清晨发生严重的地震。根据迄至目前的统计，科尔察城和附近地区已有六人死亡，九十五人受伤，五百所房被震毁。在发生了这一情况以后，阿尔巴尼亚部长会议立即成立了由国家监察委员会主席佩奇为首的委员会，并采取紧急措施，抢救受伤的居民。
    现在，二十五名重伤居民已被送往医院治疗。房屋遭到损毁的居民，都已妥善地安置在学校和帐篷内居住。科尔察所有的国营商店也都立即开始了正常营业，以保证供应食品和必需品。
