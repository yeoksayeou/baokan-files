Title: Casablanca International Exposition Opens; Moroccan King Visits Chinese Pavilion
Author:
Date: 1960-05-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Casablanca, April 28th: The 16th Casablanca International Exposition opened here today, presided over by the King of Morocco, Mohammed V. High-ranking Moroccan government officials and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Morocco attended the opening ceremony of the exposition.

Twenty-one countries are participating in this year's exposition, including Morocco, China, Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, Hungary, Poland, Iraq, Tunisia, and the United Kingdom.

The King of Morocco, accompanied by the Chinese Ambassador to Morocco, Bai Ren, and the head of the Chinese delegation to the exposition, Zhang Tingyu, visited the Chinese pavilion. The King expressed great interest upon seeing the "Red Flag" automobile at the entrance of the Chinese pavilion. When Ambassador Bai Ren told the King that among the exhibits were tea seedlings planted by Chinese tea-planting experts on Moroccan soil, the King said, "I will come and have a good look later." The Moroccan Prime Minister said after the visit, "The Chinese pavilion is really good."

Prior to the opening of the exposition, local newspapers published news about the Chinese pavilion. "Le Petit Marocain" (The Little Moroccan) said in its introduction to the Chinese pavilion that among the more than two thousand exhibits were precision machine tools, textile machines, automobiles, trucks, agricultural machines, and clothing, which demonstrated the achievements of the Chinese people in the development of the national economy.

The exposition is scheduled to end on May 15th.

### 卡萨布兰卡国际博览会开幕  摩洛哥国王参观中国馆

1960-05-01
第5版()
专栏：

    卡萨布兰卡国际博览会开幕
    摩洛哥国王参观中国馆
    新华社卡萨布兰卡28日电　第十六届卡萨布兰卡国际博览会在摩洛哥国王穆罕默德五世主持下，今天在这里开幕。摩洛哥政府高级官员和各国驻摩洛哥外交使节都出席了博览会的开幕典礼。
    参加今年博览会的有二十一个国家，其中包括：摩洛哥、中国、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、匈牙利、波兰、伊拉克、突尼斯、英国等国。
    摩洛哥国王在中国驻摩洛哥大使白认和参加博览会的中国代表团团长张廷禹的陪同下参观了中国馆。国王在中国馆入口处看到“红旗”牌汽车时，表示很感兴趣。当白认大使告诉国王，在展览品中有中国植茶专家在摩洛哥土地上种植的茶树苗时，国王说，“我以后还要来好好看一看”。摩洛哥首相在参观以后说，“中国馆实在是好。”
    在博览会开幕之前，当地报纸就刊载了关于中国馆的消息。“小摩洛哥人报”在介绍中国馆时说，在两千多件展品中，有精密工作母机、纺织机、汽车、卡车、农业机器和服装等，这表明中国人民在国民经济发展方面取得的成就。
    博览会预定在5月15日结束。
