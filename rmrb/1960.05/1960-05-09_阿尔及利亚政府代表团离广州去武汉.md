Title: Algerian Government Delegation Leaves Guangzhou for Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1960-05-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, May 8th: The delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, led by Deputy Prime Minister Krim Belkacem, left Guangzhou by plane this morning for a visit to Wuhan. Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of China, and He Ying, Director of the Department of West Asian and African Affairs, accompanied them.

    Chen Yu, Governor of Guangdong Province, Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou, and Major General Liu Xinglong, Head of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Guangzhou, were at the airport to greet the distinguished guests.

### 阿尔及利亚政府代表团离广州去武汉

1960-05-09
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚政府代表团离广州去武汉
    新华社广州8日电　由克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆副总理率领的阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团，今天上午乘飞机离开广州赴武汉访问。我国外交部副部长姬鹏飞、西亚非洲司司长何英陪同前往。
    广东省省长陈郁，广州市市长朱光，中国人民解放军驻广州部队首长刘兴隆少将等到机场欢送贵宾。
