Title: Our Youth Gymnastics Team to Hungary for International Competition
Author:
Date: 1960-05-18
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 15th. A six-member Chinese youth gymnastics team, both male and female, left Beijing by plane today for Hungary, invited to participate in the International Individual Gymnastics Championships held in Budapest from May 20th to 22nd.

### 我青年体操队去匈参加国际比赛

1960-05-18
第5版()
专栏：

    我青年体操队去匈参加国际比赛
    据新华社15日讯　中国青年男女体操队一行六人，今天由北京乘飞机前往匈牙利，应邀参加5月20日到22日在布达佩斯举行的国际个人体操锦标赛。
