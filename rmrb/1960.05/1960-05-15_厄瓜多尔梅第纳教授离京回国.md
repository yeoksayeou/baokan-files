Title: Ecuadorian Professor Medina Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-05-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 10th: Ecuadorian Professor Manuel Medina, who visited China at the invitation of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, concluded his visit to China and left Beijing by plane this morning to return to his country. Zhu Boshen, Standing Director of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, saw him off at the airport.

### 厄瓜多尔梅第纳教授离京回国

1960-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    厄瓜多尔梅第纳教授离京回国
    新华社10日讯　应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请来我国访问的厄瓜多尔教授马奴埃尔·梅第纳，结束了在我国的访问，今天上午乘飞机离开北京返国。中国拉丁美洲友好协会常务理事朱伯深到机场送行。
