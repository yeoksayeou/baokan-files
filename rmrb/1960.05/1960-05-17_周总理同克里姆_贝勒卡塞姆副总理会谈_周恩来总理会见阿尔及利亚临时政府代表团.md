Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Holds Talks with Deputy Prime Minister Krim Belkacem; Premier Zhou Enlai Meets with Delegation of the Provisional Government of Algeria
Author:
Date: 1960-05-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, May 16th: Premier Zhou Enlai held talks with Krim Belkacem, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, in Wuhan this afternoon.

Participating in the talks on the Algerian side were: Boussouf Abdelhafid, Minister of Armaments and Liaison; and Francis Ahmed, Minister of Finance and Economic Affairs. On the Chinese side were: Chen Yi, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs; Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; and Li Qiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and others.

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, May 16th: Premier Zhou Enlai met with Krim Belkacem, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, and the members of the delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria he led, in Wuhan this afternoon: Boussouf Abdelhafid, Minister of Armaments and Liaison; Francis Ahmed, Minister of Finance and Economic Affairs; Belehoucine Mabrouk, Deputy Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Advisors Jawi Muhammad, Benabdelmoumen Ali, Rouchdi Halim, Hamidi Hassan Kamal, and Hafid Karim; News Commissioner Demagratrous Rabi; and Attache of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ahamani Abdelaziz.

Participating in the meeting were Chen Yi, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs; Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Li Qiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; He Ying, Director of the West Asian and African Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Li Chao, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Hubei Provincial People's Committee.

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, May 15th: The delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, headed by Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Krim Belkacem, arrived in Wuhan today by special train from Beijing.

Accompanying the distinguished guests to Wuhan were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang, Director of the West Asian and African Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs He Ying, and Director of the Protocol Department Yu Peiwen.

Welcoming the distinguished guests at the station were Wang Haishan, Vice Governor of Hubei Province; Liu Huinong, Mayor of Wuhan; Major General Zhang Guangcai, head of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Wuhan; Cai Shubin, Vice Chairman of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Li Bogang, Vice Chairman of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and others.

### 周总理同克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆副总理会谈  周恩来总理会见阿尔及利亚临时政府代表团

1960-05-17
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理同克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆副总理会谈  
    周恩来总理会见阿尔及利亚临时政府代表团
    新华社武汉16日电　周恩来总理今天下午在武汉同阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府副总理兼外交部长克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆举行了会谈。
    参加会谈的，阿尔及利亚方面有军备和联络部长布苏夫·阿卜杜勒哈菲德，财政和经济事务部长弗朗西斯·艾哈迈德。中国方面有副总理兼外交部长陈毅，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，对外贸易部副部长李强等。
    新华社武汉16日电　周恩来总理今天下午在武汉会见了阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府副总理兼外交部长克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆和他率领的阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团成员：军备和联络部长布苏夫·阿卜杜勒哈菲德，财政和经济事务部长弗朗西斯·艾哈迈德，外交部副秘书长贝勒侯赛因·马卜鲁克，顾问贾维·穆罕默德、本阿卜杜勒穆迈迈·阿里、鲁什迪·哈利姆、哈米迪·哈桑·卡迈勒、哈菲德·卡里姆，新闻专员德马格拉特鲁斯·拉比，外交部随员阿哈马尼·阿卜杜勒阿齐兹。
    参加会见的，有副总理兼外交部长陈毅，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，对外贸易部副部长李强，外交部西亚非洲司司长何英，外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文，湖北省人民委员会外事处副处长李超。
    新华社武汉15日电　以克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆副总理兼外交部长为首的阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团，今日由北京乘专车到达武汉。
    陪同贵宾们前来武汉的，有外交部副部长姬鹏飞、对外贸易部副部长李强、外交部西亚非洲司司长何英和礼宾司司长俞沛文。
    在车站欢迎贵宾们的，有湖北省副省长王海山、武汉市市长刘惠农、中国人民解放军驻武汉部队首长张广才少将、湖北省政协副主席蔡书彬、武汉市政协副主席李伯刚等。
