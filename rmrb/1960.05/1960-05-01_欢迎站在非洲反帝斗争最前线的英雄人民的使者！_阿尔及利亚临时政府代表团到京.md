Title: Welcome the Envoys of the Heroic People Standing at the Forefront of Africa's Anti-Imperialist Struggle! Delegation of the Provisional Government of Algeria Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-01
Page: 1

The envoys of the heroic people standing at the forefront of Africa's anti-imperialist struggle are welcomed!
Delegation of the Provisional Government of Algeria Arrives in Beijing
Vice Premier He Long and others welcomed them at the airport, expressing that the Chinese people have always firmly supported the just struggle of the Algerian people.
Vice Premier Krim Belkacem said that the Algerian people will fight more firmly until they achieve national independence.

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - The friendly envoys of the fighting Algerian people - Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Krim Belkacem and the delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria led by him, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a friendly visit to China at the invitation of our government. In his speech at the Capital Airport, Vice Premier Krim Belkacem solemnly declared: The determination of the Algerian people to resist the French colonial army supported by the Atlantic Group, especially the rulers of the United States, is firmer than ever. We will continue to fight until we achieve national independence.

Vice Premier of the State Council He Long and other national leaders went to the airport to warmly welcome the distinguished Algerian guests. In his welcoming speech, Vice Premier He Long enthusiastically praised the Algerian people for standing at the forefront of the African continent in the struggle against imperialism and setting a brilliant example for the African people's national independence struggle with their heroic spirit of perseverance and struggle. He said: "The Chinese people have always resolutely supported the Algerian people's struggle for national liberation," "The heroic and tenacious struggle of the Algerian people will surely achieve final victory."

Today, the national flags of China and Algeria fluttered high in the sky above the Capital Airport. At 5:00 p.m., Vice Premier Krim Belkacem, members of the delegation: Minister of Armaments and Liaison Boussouf Abdelhafid, Minister of Finance and Economic Affairs Francis Ahmed, Deputy Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Belelhoucine Mablouk, advisors Djawi Mohamed, Benabdelmoumen Ali, Rouchdi Halim, Hamidi Hassan Kamel, Hafid Karim, Benhabyles Abdelmalek, Information Commissioner Demaghlatrous Rabah, and Attache of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Ahamani Abdelaziz, stepped off the plane amidst applause. At this time, Vice Premier of the State Council He Long, Vice Premier of the State Council and Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army General Luo Ruiqing, Vice Premier of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairman Cheng Qian of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Vice Chairman Bao Erhan of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Vice Chairmen Zhang Zhizhong and Fu Zuoyi of the National Defense Council, Chairman Liao Chengzhi of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo and Ji Pengfei, and others, approached the plane to welcome them and warmly shook hands with the distinguished guests.

After the band played the national anthems of Algeria and China, Vice Premier Krim Belkacem, accompanied by Vice Premier He Long, reviewed the army honor guard. A group of Young Pioneers presented flowers to all the distinguished guests.

Vice Premier He Long and Vice Premier Krim Belkacem spoke at the airport (full text on page two). Their speeches won round after round of warm applause from Chinese and foreign people at the airport.

After the welcoming ceremony, Vice Premier Krim Belkacem and other distinguished guests met with more than 1,000 welcoming people and armed male and female militia队伍 waiting at the airport. When Vice Premier Krim Belkacem walked in front of the neatly arranged militia队伍, the heroic militiamen saluted the representatives of the heroic Algerian people with their guns. Vice Premier Krim Belkacem waved to them and shook hands cordially with the male and female militiamen. In the welcoming line, people beat gongs and drums, applauded enthusiastically, and shouted slogans: "Resolutely support the just struggle of the Algerian people!" "Long live the unity of the Asian and African people!" "Salute to the heroic Algerian soldiers and civilians who are fighting bravely!"

Also present at the airport today were heads of government departments and democratic parties, heads of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association and other people's organizations, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, heads of Beijing Municipality, and people from all walks of life in the capital.

Diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China, members of the Algerian trade union delegation, youth delegation, and student delegation who are in Beijing, also came to the airport to welcome them today. (Attached picture)

### 欢迎站在非洲反帝斗争最前线的英雄人民的使者！  阿尔及利亚临时政府代表团到京

1960-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

    欢迎站在非洲反帝斗争最前线的英雄人民的使者！
    阿尔及利亚临时政府代表团到京
    贺龙副总理等到机场迎接，表示中国人民一贯坚决支持阿人民的正义斗争
    克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆副总理说，阿人民将更坚定地战斗，直到获得民族独立
    据新华社30日讯　战斗的阿尔及利亚人民的友好使者——克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆副总理兼外交部长和由他率领的阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团，应我国政府邀请来中国进行友好访问，于今天下午乘飞机到达北京。克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆副总理在首都机场的讲话中庄严宣称：阿尔及利亚人民抗击由大西洋集团特别是美国统治者所支持的法国殖民军队的决心，比过去任何时候更加坚定，我们将继续战斗，直到获得民族独立。
    国务院副总理贺龙等国家领导人前往机场热烈欢迎阿尔及利亚贵宾。贺龙副总理在欢迎讲话中热情赞扬阿尔及利亚人民，站在非洲大陆反对帝国主义斗争最前线，以不屈不挠、坚持斗争的英雄气概给非洲人民的民族独立斗争树立了光辉的榜样。他说：“中国人民一贯坚决支持阿尔及利亚人民争取民族解放的斗争”，“阿尔及利亚人民的英勇顽强的斗争一定会取得最后胜利”。
    今天，中国和阿尔及利亚的国旗，在首都机场上空迎风高高飘扬。下午五时，克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆副总理，代表团成员：军备和联络部长布苏夫·阿卜杜勒哈菲德，财政和经济事务部长弗朗西斯·艾哈迈德，外交部副秘书长贝勒侯赛因·马卜鲁克，顾问贾维·穆罕默德，本阿卜杜勒穆迈迈·阿里，鲁什迪·哈利姆，哈米迪·哈桑·卡迈勒，哈菲德·卡里姆，本哈比莱斯·阿卜杜勒马利克，新闻专员德马格拉特鲁斯·拉比，外交部随员阿哈马尼·阿卜杜勒阿齐兹，在一片掌声中走下飞机。这时，国务院副总理贺龙，国务院副总理、中国人民解放军总参谋长罗瑞卿大将，国务院副总理习仲勋，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长程潜，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，国防委员会副主席张治中、傅作义，中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长罗贵波、姬鹏飞等，都走近飞机旁迎接，同贵宾们一一亲切握手致意。
    乐队高奏阿尔及利亚和中国国歌后，克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆副总理由贺龙副总理陪同，检阅了陆军仪仗队。一群少年先锋队员向全体贵宾献了鲜花。
    贺龙副总理和克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆副总理在机场上讲了话（全文见第二版）。他们的讲话博得了在机场上的中外人士的一阵又一阵的热烈鼓掌欢迎。
    欢迎仪式以后，克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆副总理等贵宾，同迎候在机场上的一千多欢迎群众和武装的男女民兵队伍见面。当克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆副总理走到队容严整的民兵队伍前面时，雄赳赳的民兵们持枪向英雄的阿尔及利亚人民的代表们致敬。克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆副总理招手向他们致意，并且和男女民兵们亲切握手。欢迎行列里，人们敲打锣鼓，热烈鼓掌，振臂高呼口号：“坚决支持阿尔及利亚人民的正义斗争！”“亚非人民团结万岁！”“向英勇抗战的阿尔及利亚军民致敬！”
    今天到机场欢迎的，还有政府各部门、各民主党派的负责人，中国非洲人民友好协会和其他人民团体的负责人，中国人民解放军高级将领，北京市的负责人，和首都各方面的人士。
    各国驻中国的使节和外交官员，正在北京的阿尔及利亚工会代表团、青年代表团和学生代表团的成员，今天也到机场欢迎。（附图片）
