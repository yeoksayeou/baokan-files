Title: Our Mountaineering Association Sends Telegram of Congratulations to Mountaineering Team Members
Author:
Date: 1960-05-29
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, May 27th: The Chinese Mountaineering Association today sent a telegram to the Chinese Mountaineering Team, congratulating them on their successful ascent of Mount Everest's summit.

The telegram stated: "We are delighted to hear the good news of your ascent to the world's highest peak, Mount Everest. The Chinese Mountaineering Association extends to you our warmest congratulations and heartfelt regards.

Under the glorious illumination of the red flag of Mao Zedong Thought, you have fully demonstrated the revolutionary vigor and excellent qualities of courage, tenacity, perseverance, and decisiveness. You have greatly promoted the communist spirit of mutual assistance and mutual encouragement, overcome the extreme altitude hypoxia, conquered the severe cold of minus forty degrees Celsius, endured all kinds of hardships, and overcome all difficulties, finally achieving a glorious victory.

Comrades! Your victory has written a brilliant page in the history of mountaineering in our country and made a tremendous contribution to the world mountaineering movement. Let us, under the leadership of the Party and Chairman Mao, continue our efforts, forge ahead courageously, and further develop the mass mountaineering movement in our country, so that this movement can better serve the cause of socialist construction."

### 我登山协会电贺登山队员

1960-05-29
第2版()
专栏：

    我登山协会电贺登山队员
    新华社27日讯　中国登山协会今天打电报给中国登山队，祝贺他们成功地攀登上了珠穆朗玛峰顶峰。
    电报中说：欣闻你们登上世界最高峰——珠穆朗玛峰的捷报，中国登山协会谨向你们致以最热烈的祝贺和衷心的慰问。
    你们在毛泽东思想红旗的光辉照耀下，充分发挥了革命干劲与勇敢、顽强、坚毅、果断的优良品质，大大发扬了互相帮助、互相激励的共产主义精神，克服了高山极度缺氧的困难，战胜了摄氏零下四十度的严寒，历尽艰险，排除万难，终于取得了光辉的胜利。
    同志们！你们的胜利在我国登山运动史上，写下了辉煌的一页，对世界登山运动也作出了巨大的贡献。让我们在党和毛主席的领导下，再接再厉，奋勇前进，进一步开展我国的群众性登山运动，使这一运动更好地为社会主义建设事业服务。
