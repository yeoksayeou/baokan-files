Title: National Track Cycling Championships Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency report: The 1960 National Track Cycling Championships began on May 15th at the Beijing Velodrome.

This year's championships are larger than previous ones, with 108 male athletes and 99 female athletes from fifteen provinces and cities across the country, as well as sixteen units including the Chinese People's Liberation Army participating, competing in fifteen events. The championships will conclude on the 20th.

### 全国自行车场地锦标赛在京举行

1960-05-17
第4版()
专栏：

    全国自行车场地锦标赛在京举行
    据新华社讯　1960年全国自行车场地锦标赛，本月15日在北京赛车场开始举行。
    这次锦标赛比过去的规模都大，全国有十五个省、市及中国人民解放军等十六个单位的一百零八名男选手和九十九名女选手参加，将举行十五个项目的比赛。这次锦标赛将在20日结束。
