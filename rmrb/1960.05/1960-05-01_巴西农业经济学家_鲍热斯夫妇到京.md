Title: Brazilian Agricultural Economist and Wife Borges Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th—Brazilian agricultural economist Pemberto Accioly Borges and his wife, Maria Jose Cruz Marcondes, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese Latin American Friendship Association.

### 巴西农业经济学家  鲍热斯夫妇到京

1960-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    巴西农业经济学家
    鲍热斯夫妇到京
    新华社30日讯　应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请前来我国访问的巴西农业经济学家彭贝渥·阿克孝里·鲍热斯和他的夫人玛丽亚·何塞·克鲁斯·马康德斯在今天乘飞机到达北京。
