Title: Chairman Mao Meets with Danish Communist Party Chairman Jespersen
Author:
Date: 1960-05-29
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, May 28th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China met with Comrade Knud Jespersen, Chairman of the Danish Communist Party, and his wife in Shanghai on the afternoon of the 28th, and had a cordial conversation. Present at the meeting were Kang Sheng, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat. (Attached picture)
    Chairman Mao shaking hands with Knud Jespersen, Chairman of the Danish Communist Party.
                        Xinhua News Agency reporter Chen Juanmei (fax photo)

### 毛主席会见丹共主席耶斯佩森

1960-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席会见丹共主席耶斯佩森
    新华社上海28日电　28日下午，中国共产党中央委员会毛泽东主席在上海会见了丹麦共产党主席克努德·耶斯佩森同志和他的夫人，进行了亲切的交谈。会见时在座的有中共中央政治局候补委员康生、书记处候补书记杨尚昆。（附图片）
