Title: General Luo Ruiqing Feted the Polish Military Delegation; Bozhiwovsky Said He Would Bring the Friendship of the Chinese People to the Polish People's Army
Author:
Date: 1960-05-02
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, May 1st. Deputy Prime Minister of the State Council and Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, General Luo Ruiqing, held a banquet today to bid farewell to the Polish military delegation headed by General Bozhiwovsky.

    In his speech at the banquet, General Luo Ruiqing, on behalf of the Chinese people and all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, thanked the Polish military delegation headed by General Bozhiwovsky for their friendly visit to our country and thanked them for bringing the profound friendship of the Polish people.

    General Luo Ruiqing said that our great socialist camp, with a population of one billion and geographically connected, stands as majestic as Mount Tai in the world; the immortal Marxist-Leninist thought and the principle of proletarian internationalism closely unite the people of our countries, and we are advancing rapidly towards the common goal of socialism and communism. No reactionary force in the world can stop our progress. Strengthening the unity of socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union and strengthening the unity of the international working class are the main guarantees for our victory in socialist construction and for easing international tensions and safeguarding world peace. The brotherly unity of the Soviet Union, China, Poland and other socialist countries is in the fundamental interests of the people of socialist countries and the people of the whole world. We cherish the unity of our socialist camp as we cherish our own eyes. The eternal and unbreakable brotherly friendship between the Chinese and Polish people will forever radiate brilliantly.

    General Bozhiwovsky, head of the Polish military delegation, then said that we would bring the warm friendship and hearts of the great and glorious Chinese people and the Chinese People's Liberation Army to the Polish People's Army.

    General Bozhiwovsky said that during the visit, we had the opportunity to understand the great achievements made by the Chinese people in building socialism on their beautiful land under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party headed by Comrade Mao Zedong. We also understand that the Chinese people's armed forces are able to protect the Chinese people in building socialism peacefully. We are happy and proud of this. He said that although our two countries are thousands of miles apart, the immortal Marxist-Leninist thought unites us.

    The banquet was filled with a joyful, united and friendly atmosphere. The hosts and guests frequently raised their glasses to congratulate the growing strength of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union and the unbreakable friendship between the people and armies of China and Poland.

    Those present at today's banquet included General Wang Shusheng, General Xiao Hua, General Peng Shaohui, General Yang Yong, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General Qiu Huizuo, Lieutenant General Mo Wenhua, Lieutenant General Liu Shaowen, Lieutenant General Tan Jiasu of the Air Force, Lieutenant General Yuan Ziqin, Lieutenant General Zhang Chiming, and Rear Admiral Zhang Xuesi of the Navy, and other senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

    Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo attended the banquet.

    Also invited to attend were Furato, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China, and Lieutenant Colonel Ximanski, acting military attache. Military attaches from various countries stationed in China were also invited to attend the banquet.

    Today, the Polish military delegation, accompanied by General Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and General Peng Shaohui, Deputy Chief of General Staff, also participated in the grand garden party held by the people of Beijing in the Summer Palace to celebrate the "May 1st" International Labor Day.

### 罗瑞卿大将欢送波兰军事代表团  博齐沃夫斯基说要把中国人民的友谊带给波兰人民军

1960-05-02
第3版()
专栏：

    罗瑞卿大将欢送波兰军事代表团
    博齐沃夫斯基说要把中国人民的友谊带给波兰人民军
    新华社1日讯　国务院副总理、中国人民解放军总参谋长罗瑞卿大将今天举行宴会，欢送以博齐沃夫斯基上将为首的波兰军事代表团。
    罗瑞卿大将在宴会上致词，代表中国人民和中国人民解放军全体指战员，感谢以博齐沃夫斯基上将为首的波兰军事代表团在我国的友好访问，感谢他们带来波兰人民的深厚友谊。
    罗瑞卿大将说，我们伟大的社会主义阵营，拥有十亿人口，地理上连成一片，像泰山一样地巍然屹立于世界；不朽的马克思列宁主义思想和无产阶级国际主义原则，把我们各国人民紧密地团结在一起，向着共同的目标——社会主义和共产主义，突飞猛进。世界上任何反动力量也阻挡不住我们的前进。加强以苏联为首的社会主义各国的团结，加强国际工人阶级的团结，是我们取得社会主义建设的胜利和缓和国际紧张局势、维护世界和平的主要保证。苏联、中国、波兰及其他社会主义国家的兄弟般的团结，是社会主义各国人民和全世界人民的根本利益所在。我们像爱护自己眼珠一样地爱护我们社会主义阵营的团结。中波两国人民永恒的、牢不可破的兄弟友谊，永远放射着灿烂的光辉。
    波兰军事代表团团长博齐沃夫斯基上将接着讲话说，我们要把伟大的光辉的中国人民和中国人民解放军的热烈的友谊和心意带给波兰人民军。
    博齐沃夫斯基上将说，在访问期间，我们有机会了解了中国人民在以毛泽东同志为首的中国共产党的领导下，在美丽的国土上建设社会主义所取得的巨大成就。我们也了解了中国人民武装力量能够保卫中国人民平安地建设社会主义。为此，我们感到高兴，感到骄傲。他说，我们两国之间相距数千里，但是不朽的马克思列宁主义思想把我们团结在一起。
    席间，洋溢着一片喜气洋洋、团结、友爱的深情厚意。宾主频频为以苏联为首的社会主义阵营日益强大，为中波两国人民和军队的牢不可破的友谊举杯祝贺。
    出席今天宴会的有，王树声大将、萧华上将、彭绍辉上将、杨勇上将、萧向荣中将、丘会作中将、莫文骅中将、刘少文中将、谭家述空军中将、袁子钦中将、张池明中将、张学思海军少将等中国人民解放军高级将领。
    外交部副部长罗贵波出席了宴会。
    应邀出席的还有波兰驻中国大使馆临时代办傅拉托和代理武官席曼斯基中校。各国驻华武官也应邀出席了宴会。
    今天，波兰军事代表团，由中国人民解放军总政治部副主任萧华上将、副总参谋长彭绍辉上将陪同，还参加了北京市人民在颐和园举行的庆祝“五一”国际劳动节盛大游园会。
