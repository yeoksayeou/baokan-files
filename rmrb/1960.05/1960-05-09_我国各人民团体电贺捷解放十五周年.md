Title: Chinese People's Organizations Send Congratulatory Telegrams on the 15th Anniversary of Czechoslovakia's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1960-05-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 8th: The Sino-Czechoslovakia Friendship Association, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the All China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Women's Federation, and the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League sent telegrams on the 6th and 7th to the Central Committee of the National Front of the Czechoslovak Republic and relevant people's organizations, warmly congratulating them on the 15th anniversary of Czechoslovakia's liberation.

### 我国各人民团体电贺捷解放十五周年

1960-05-09
第4版()
专栏：

    我国各人民团体电贺捷解放十五周年
    据新华社8日讯　中国捷克斯洛伐克友好协会、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中华全国总工会、中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会和中国共产主义青年团中央委员会6日和7日分别打电报给捷克斯洛伐克共和国民族阵线中央委员会和有关人民团体，热烈祝贺捷克斯洛伐克解放十五周年。
