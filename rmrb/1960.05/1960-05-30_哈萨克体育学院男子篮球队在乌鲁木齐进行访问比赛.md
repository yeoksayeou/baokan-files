Title: Kazakh Institute of Physical Education Men's Basketball Team Visits Urumqi for Matches
Author:
Date: 1960-05-30
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, May 29th: The men's basketball team from the Kazakh Republic Institute of Physical Education of the Soviet Union arrived in Urumqi on May 23rd, after visiting Beijing and Tianjin.

    During their stay in Urumqi, the visiting team played two friendly matches with the Gansu Youth Men's Basketball Team and the Xi'an Institute of Physical Education Men's Basketball Team. The visiting team won with scores of 85-46 and 83-51, respectively. On the 27th, the visiting team also played an exhibition match with the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Men's Basketball Team.

### 哈萨克体育学院男子篮球队在乌鲁木齐进行访问比赛

1960-05-30
第7版()
专栏：

    哈萨克体育学院男子篮球队在乌鲁木齐进行访问比赛
    据新华社乌鲁木齐29日电　苏联哈萨克共和国体育学院男子篮球队，访问北京、天津后，于5月23日到达乌鲁木齐。
    客队在乌鲁木齐逗留期间，先后与甘肃青年男子篮球队和西安体育学院男子篮球队进行了两场友谊比赛。客队分别以八十五比四十六、八十三比五十一的比分获胜。27日，客队又与新疆维吾尔自治区男子篮球代表队作了一场表演赛。
