Title: Expose the True Face of US Imperialism's Opposition to Peace: Romanian and Bulgarian People Rally to Condemn American Aggressors; International Students in the Soviet Union Hold Meeting to Denounce US Plane's Provocations Against the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1960-05-29
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 27th Dispatch: The working people of Romania and Bulgaria have continued to condemn the US imperialists' provocations against the Soviet Union and their sabotage of the Four-Power Summit Conference in recent days. People pointed out that the sheep's clothing worn by the US imperialists has been torn off.

    The Romanian working people have continued to hold protest rallies recently. At a meeting held on the 26th by more than two thousand workers at the Kirov Shoe Factory in Bucharest, worker Constantin Constantinescu said that the US spy plane's aggressive provocations against the Soviet Union have made it clear to people that the US government is against socialist countries and peace. He said that the US government is implementing the policies of monopoly capitalists and arms dealers. This policy is doomed to failure. Engineer Marinescu also expressed his support for the Soviet Union's solemn stance at the meeting. He said that the deceptive sheep's clothing worn by US imperialism has been torn off.

    The employees of the Kirov Shoe Factory unanimously passed a resolution, firmly protesting US imperialism's aggressive activities against the Soviet Union and fully supporting the Soviet Union's position. The resolution said: "The peace-loving forces of the world can shatter any despicable conspiracy of the imperialists to endanger peace."

    Protest rallies were also held in Hunedoara, Reşiţa, Craiova, and other places. On the 25th, more than seven thousand workers from the Reşiţa Metallurgical Combine held a rally to condemn the United States for escalating the international situation. Worker Francisc said at the meeting: "The unity of the socialist camp is the main guarantee for defending world peace. We workers will do everything possible to complete the great plan formulated by the Party for the prosperity of the motherland in order to contribute to strengthening the forces of peace." On this day, more than 1,200 employees of the Roman Steel Pipe Plant also held a rally to protest against the American aggressors.

    According to Sofia newspapers on the 27th, youth in the Bulgarian capital held a rally, angrily protesting against the US imperialists' crimes of aggression and provocation. The youth participating in the rally held up eye-catching signs: "US imperialists are shameless!" "The United States has sabotaged the Paris Conference!" "We demand peace, not playing with peace!"

    Ganev, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Dimitrov Communist Youth League of Bulgaria, said at the meeting that the US imperialist elements had sabotaged the Paris Four-Power Summit Conference. Bulgarian youth angrily condemned the shameless acts of US provocateurs and supported the Soviet Union's peace-loving, frank, and firm policy.

    Alena Daneva, the leader of a Communist Youth League labor shock brigade at the "Blagoev" printing factory, university student Dimo Yordanov, and World Federation of Democratic Youth Secretary Dala Taufik, who is visiting Bulgaria, also spoke at the meeting, unanimously protesting against the crimes of the US imperialist aggressors in sabotaging the Four-Power Summit Conference.

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 28th Dispatch: TASS Moscow, 27th Dispatch: Foreign students studying at Moscow universities today sent a protest telegram to UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld stating that the failure of the Paris Four-Power Summit Conference was caused by the US government's aggressive policies.

    This protest telegram was unanimously passed by students and graduate students from Asian, African, European, and Latin American countries at a mass meeting held at Moscow University today.

    The telegram said that we see very clearly that the provocative flight of the US plane not only violated the sovereignty of the Soviet Union, but also threatened the sovereignty of other countries and violated the UN Charter.

    The participants in the mass meeting also passed a telegram to Soviet government leader Khrushchev, expressing their support for his statement at the preparatory meeting of the Paris Summit Conference. The telegram pointed out that this statement exposed to the people of the world the hypocritical faces of those who sabotaged the Paris Conference with their actions.

### 揭穿美帝国主义反对和平的真面目  罗保人民集会声讨美国侵略者  各国留苏学生举行大会谴责美机对苏联的挑衅

1960-05-29
第3版()
专栏：

    揭穿美帝国主义反对和平的真面目
    罗保人民集会声讨美国侵略者
    各国留苏学生举行大会谴责美机对苏联的挑衅
    据新华社27日讯　罗马尼亚和保加利亚的劳动人民近几天来继续谴责美帝国主义者对苏联进行挑衅和破坏四国政府首脑会议的罪行。人们指出：美帝国主义者披在身上的羊皮已经被扯下来了。
    罗马尼亚劳动人民最近继续举行抗议集会。在布加勒斯特基洛夫制鞋厂两千多工人26日举行的集会上，工人康斯坦丁尼斯库说，美国间谍飞机对苏联进行侵略挑衅，使人们看清了美国政府反对社会主义国家与和平的侵略面目。他说，美国政府执行着垄断资本家和军火商的政策。这种政策是注定要失败的。工程师马里尼斯库也在会上表示支持苏联的严正立场。他说，披在美帝国主义身上的骗人的羊皮已经给揭下来了。
    基洛夫制鞋厂职工最后一致通过决议，坚决抗议美帝国主义对苏联的侵略活动，并完全支持苏联的立场。决议说：“世界爱好和平的力量能够粉碎帝国主义者危害和平的任何卑鄙的阴谋”。
    在洪尼多阿拉、累希察、克拉约发等地也举行了抗议大会。累希察冶金联合企业七千多名工人25日举行集会，谴责美国加剧国际局势。工人弗兰西斯克在会上说：“社会主义阵营的团结是保卫世界和平的主要保证。我们工人将尽一切努力完成党为祖国的繁荣所制定的伟大计划，以便为增强和平力量作出贡献”。这一天，罗曼钢管厂的一千二百多名职工也举行了抗议美国侵略者的集会。
    据索非亚的报纸27日报道，保加利亚首都青年举行集会，愤怒抗议美帝国主义的侵略挑衅罪行。参加集会的青年们高举着醒目的标语牌：“美帝国主义者无耻！”“美国破坏了巴黎会议！”“我们要求的是和平，不是玩弄和平！”
    保加利亚季米特洛夫共产主义青年团中央书记加涅夫在集会上讲话说，美帝国主义分子破坏了巴黎四国政府首脑会议。保加利亚青年愤怒斥责美国挑衅分子的无耻行为并且支持苏联的爱好和平的坦率而坚毅的政策。
    “布拉戈耶夫”印刷厂的一个共青团劳动突击队队长艾伦娜·达内娃、大学生迪莫·约尔达诺夫以及正在保加利亚访问的世界民主青年联盟书记达拉·陶菲克都在会上先后讲了话，他们一致抗议美帝国主义侵略者破坏四国政府首脑会议的罪行。
    据新华社28日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科27日讯：在莫斯科高等学校学习的外国留学生今天向联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德拍了一份抗议电报说，巴黎四国政府首脑会议没有能够举行是由于美国政府的侵略政策所造成的。
    这份抗议电报是由来自亚洲、非洲、欧洲和拉丁美洲国家的留学生和研究生今天在莫斯科大学召开的群众大会上一致通过的。
    电报中说，我们看得非常清楚，美国飞机的挑衅性飞行不仅仅侵犯了苏联的主权，而且是对其他国家主权的威胁和对联合国宪章的破坏。
    群众大会的参加者还通过了致苏联政府首脑赫鲁晓夫的电报，表示支持他在巴黎政府首脑会议预备会议上的声明。电报中指出，这个声明向全世界人民揭露了那些以他们的行动破坏了巴黎会议的人的伪善面目。
