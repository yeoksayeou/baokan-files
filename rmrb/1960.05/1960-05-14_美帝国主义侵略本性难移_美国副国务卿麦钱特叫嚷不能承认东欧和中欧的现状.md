Title: U.S. Imperialism's Aggressive Nature is Hard to Change: U.S. Deputy Undersecretary of State Merchant Clamors About Not Recognizing the Status Quo in Eastern and Central Europe
Author:
Date: 1960-05-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 11th: U.S. Deputy Undersecretary of State Merchant, responsible for political affairs, reiterated yesterday that the United States wants to interfere in the internal affairs of Eastern European socialist countries, clamoring that the United States cannot "acquiesce to the status quo in Eastern and Central Europe."

Merchant made this shameless clamor when he received a delegation from the so-called "American Friends of Captive Nations," composed of U.S. bipartisan members of Congress. This delegation requested Merchant that the United States raise the so-called "Eastern European issue" at the summit conference to provoke the socialist camp.

### 美帝国主义侵略本性难移  美国副国务卿麦钱特叫嚷不能承认东欧和中欧的现状

1960-05-14
第5版()
专栏：

    美帝国主义侵略本性难移
    美国副国务卿麦钱特叫嚷不能承认东欧和中欧的现状
    新华社11日讯　纽约消息：负责政治事务的美国副国务卿麦钱特昨天重申美国要干涉东欧社会主义国家的内政，叫嚣说美国不能“默认东欧和中欧的现状”。
    麦钱特这种无耻叫嚷是在接见一个由美国两党议员参加组成的所谓“被奴役国家美国友人协会”代表团的时候发出的。这个代表团向麦钱特要求美国在最高级会议上提出所谓“东欧问题”来向社会主义阵营挑衅。
