Title: Premier Zhou and Vice Premier Chen Yi Lay Wreath at the Sukhe-Bator—Choibalsan Mausoleum
Author:
Date: 1960-05-28
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, 27th. Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi, accompanied by Molomjamts, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, laid a wreath at the Sukhe-Bator—Choibalsan Mausoleum at 2:00 p.m. today. The wreath was inscribed with "Comrades Sukhe-Bator—Choibalsan Live Forever." All members of Premier Zhou Enlai's entourage and Xie Fangsheng, the Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, accompanied them.

### 周总理陈毅副总理向苏赫巴托尔——乔巴山陵墓献花圈

1960-05-28
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理陈毅副总理
    向苏赫巴托尔——乔巴山陵墓献花圈
    新华社乌兰巴托27日电　周恩来总理、陈毅副总理由蒙古人民共和国部长会议第一副主席莫洛姆扎木茨陪同，今天下午二时向苏赫巴托尔—乔巴山陵墓献了花圈，花圈上写着“苏赫巴托尔—乔巴山同志永垂不朽”。周恩来总理的全体随行人员和中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生都随同前往。
