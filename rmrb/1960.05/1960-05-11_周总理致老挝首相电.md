Title: Premier Zhou's Telegram to the Prime Minister of Laos
Author:
Date: 1960-05-11
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 9th: Premier Zhou Enlai, while passing over Laos on a special plane, sent a telegram to the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Laos to express his regards. The text of the telegram is as follows:
    To His Excellency the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Laos:
    As I fly over your country's airspace once again, I would like to extend my greetings to your government and people.
                        Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                                        Morning of May 9, 1960

### 周总理致老挝首相电

1960-05-11
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理致老挝首相电
    新华社河内9日电　周恩来总理乘坐专机路过老挝上空时，致电老挝王国政府首相致意。电文如下：老挝王国政府首相阁下：
    当我再次飞越贵国领空的时候，谨向贵国政府和人民致意。
                        中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                                        1960年5月9日上午
