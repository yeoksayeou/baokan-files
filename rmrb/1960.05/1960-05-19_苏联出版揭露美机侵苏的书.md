Title: Soviet Union Publishes Book Exposing American Aircraft Intrusion into Soviet Territory
Author:
Date: 1960-05-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 18th. TASS reports: A book titled "Thoroughly Expose the Aggressors! A Record of American Aircraft's Provocative Intrusion into Soviet Airspace" has been published in Moscow.
    After reading the material published in this book, readers will understand the inside story of one of the biggest international provocations—the piratical intrusion of American spy planes into Soviet airspace.

### 苏联出版揭露美机侵苏的书

1960-05-19
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联出版揭露美机侵苏的书
    新华社莫斯科18日电　据塔斯社报道，“彻底揭露侵略者！美国飞机挑衅性侵犯苏联领空记实”一书已在莫斯科出版。
    读者在看了这本书上发表的材料以后，就会了解到最大的国际挑衅之一——美国间谍飞机对苏联领空的强盗式侵犯的底细。
