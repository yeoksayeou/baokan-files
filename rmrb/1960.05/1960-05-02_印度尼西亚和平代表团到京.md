Title: Indonesian Peace Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, May 1st. The Indonesian Peace Delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace to participate in the "May Day" celebrations and to pay a visit to our country, arrived in Beijing by plane last night. The delegation includes: Head of the Delegation Sukino, Deputy Head of the Delegation Islan, and members Sulodo, Mrs. Sukiato, and Makun Mangukusuma. Zhu Ziqi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and others welcomed the guests at the airport.

### 印度尼西亚和平代表团到京

1960-05-02
第3版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚和平代表团到京
    新华社1日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来我国参加“五一”节庆祝活动和进行访问的印度尼西亚和平代表团团长苏基诺、副团长依斯兰和团员苏洛多、苏基亚多夫人、马贡·曼古古苏玛一行五人，昨晚乘飞机到达北京。中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副秘书长朱子奇等人到机场欢迎客人。
