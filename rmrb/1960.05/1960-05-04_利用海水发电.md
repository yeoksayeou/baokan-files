Title: Generating Electricity Using Seawater

Author: 
Date: 1960-05-04
Page: 2

Maritime Transport Line

    Generating Electricity Using Seawater
    The crew of the "Jia Bo No. 2" barge, belonging to the Storage and Transportation Section of the Guangdong Provincial Department of Commerce, utilized the power of the water flow generated by the ship's movement to generate electricity. This solved the problem of lighting for the entire ship, reduced the burden on the power diesel engine, and saved diesel fuel.

### 利用海水发电

1960-05-04
第2版()
专栏：海运线上

    利用海水发电
    广东省商业厅储运科所属一艘驳船——加驳二船，船员们利用船前进时水流的力量发电，解决了全船的照明用电，减轻了动力柴油机的负担，并可节省柴油。
