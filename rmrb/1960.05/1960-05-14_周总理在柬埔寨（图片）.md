Title: Premier Zhou in Cambodia (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1960-05-14
Page: 4

On May 8th, Premier Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister Pho Proeung exchanged documents after signing the Sino-Cambodian Joint Communiqué.
                           All photos by Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian
On May 8th, Premier Zhou Enlai held a banquet in Phnom Penh, expressing gratitude for the warm hospitality and enthusiastic welcome of the Cambodian hosts. The picture shows Premier Zhou Enlai, Chairman of the Regency Council Sisowath Monireth, and Prince Norodom Sihanouk at the banquet.
Top Image: On May 6th, Premier Zhou Enlai cut the ribbon for the opening of the Cambodian Royal Radio Station. This radio station was a gift from Premier Zhou Enlai to Prince Sihanouk during his previous visit to Cambodia. Second from the right in the front row is Prince Sihanouk.
Second Image: On May 7th, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi, accompanied by Prince Sihanouk, visited the Kampong Cham Cambodian Royal Textile Factory, which was built with Chinese assistance.
Left Image 1: On May 6th, Premier Zhou Enlai received representatives of overseas Chinese at the Chinese Embassy in Cambodia. The picture shows overseas Chinese representatives presenting a banner to Premier Zhou.
Left Image 2: On May 6th, the Khmer Royal Socialist Youth League held a grand rally to welcome Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi. Premier Zhou Enlai, accompanied by Prince Sihanouk, received a warm welcome as he entered the venue.

### 周总理在柬埔寨（图片）

1960-05-14
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理在柬埔寨（图片）
    5月8日，周恩来总理和福·波伦首相在中柬两国联合声明上签字后，交换文件。
                           本组照片均为新华社记者　杜修贤摄
    5月8日，周恩来总理在金边举行宴会，对柬埔寨主人的殷勤招待和热烈欢迎表示谢意。图为周恩来总理、摄政委员会主席西索瓦特·莫尼勒、西哈努克亲王殿下在宴会上。
    上一图：5月6日，周恩来总理为柬埔寨皇家电台开幕剪彩。这个电台是周恩来总理在上次访问柬埔寨时赠送给西哈努克亲王的礼物。前排右起第二人是西哈努克亲王。
    上二图：5月7日，周恩来总理、陈毅副总理在西哈努克亲王陪同下参观由中国帮助建设的磅湛柬埔寨皇家纺织厂。
    左一图：5月6日，周恩来总理在中国驻柬埔寨大使馆接见华侨代表。图为华侨代表向周总理赠旗。
    左二图：5月6日，高棉王家社会主义青年团为欢迎周恩来总理和陈毅副总理举行了盛大的集会。周恩来总理在西哈努克亲王陪同下进入会场时受到热烈的欢迎。
