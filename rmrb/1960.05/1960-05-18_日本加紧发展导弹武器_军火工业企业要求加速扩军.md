Title: Japan Intensifies Development of Missile Weapons; Munitions Industry Enterprises Demand Accelerated Military Expansion
Author:
Date: 1960-05-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 14th: Under the support of U.S. imperialism, the Japanese militarists, who are seriously endangering peace in Asia and the world, are intensifying Japan's nuclear armament.

According to a report in the Japanese Economic Newspaper on the 13th, the Defense Production Committee of the Japan Federation of Economic Organizations (composed of Japanese monopoly capital, the core leading Japan's military production) recently submitted a report to the Defense Agency. It demanded that the Nobusuke Kishi government produce various missiles worth 168.788 billion yen in Japan during the ten-year period from 1961 to 1970, and at the same time allocate 397.85 billion yen from state finances for the production and repair of aircraft.

The report also demanded that the Nobusuke Kishi government allocate more military expenses from the national treasury and take special measures to "protect secrets."

This report, full of gunpowder and advocating Japan's nuclear armament, puts forward a large-scale missile manufacturing plan. It plans to produce 100 to 150 "Nike-Hercules" missiles, 100 to 200 "Hawk" missiles, 20 "Bomarc" missiles, and 400 "Sidewinder" missiles annually.

In addition to planning to manufacture missiles, the Defense Production Committee of the Japan Federation of Economic Organizations also suggested in the report that the Nobusuke Kishi government purchase a large number of missiles from foreign countries. The plan proposed in the report is that Japan should allocate 237.6 billion yen for the purchase of missiles during the ten-year period from 1961 to 1970.

### 日本加紧发展导弹武器  军火工业企业要求加速扩军

1960-05-18
第3版()
专栏：

    日本加紧发展导弹武器
    军火工业企业要求加速扩军
    新华社14日讯　东京消息：在美帝国主义的扶植下，严重危害亚洲与世界和平的日本军国主义者正在使日本加紧核武装。
    据日本经济新闻13日报道，日本经济团体联合会防卫生产委员会（由日本垄断资本组成，领导日本军需生产的核心）最近向防卫厅提出一项报告书。要求岸信介政府从1961年到1970年的十年期间，在日本生产价值一千六百八十七亿八千万日元的各种导弹，同时从国家财政里拨出三千九百七十八亿五千万日元做为飞机的生产和修理费用。
    这项报告还要求岸信介政府从国库里拨出更多的军事费用和采取什么“保护机密”的特制措施。
    这项充满火药味和鼓吹日本核武装的报告书，提出了一个数字庞大的制造导弹的计划。它拟定每年生产“奈克—赫尔克里”式导弹一百至一百五十颗，“鹰”式导弹一百至二百颗，“鲍马克”式导弹二十颗，“响尾蛇”式导弹四百颗等等。
    日本经济团体联合会防卫生产委员会除了策划制造导弹外，还在报告书里建议岸信介政府从外国购买大批的导弹。报告书提出的计划是：日本从1961年到1970年的十年期间，要为购买导弹拨出二千三百七十六亿日元。
