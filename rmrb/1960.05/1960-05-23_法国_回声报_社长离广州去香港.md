Title: President of French "Les Echos" Leaves Guangzhou for Hong Kong
Author:
Date: 1960-05-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, May 21st: Mr. and Mrs. Émile Servan-Schreiber, President of the French newspaper "Les Echos," who were invited by the All-China Journalists Association to visit our country, left Guangzhou by train for Hong Kong today.

### 法国“回声报”社长离广州去香港

1960-05-23
第5版()
专栏：

    法国“回声报”社长离广州去香港
    据新华社广州21日电　应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请前来我国访问的法国“回声报”社长埃米尔·塞文—希莱伯夫妇，今天乘火车离开广州去香港。
