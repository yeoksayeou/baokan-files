Title: Dutch Chargé d'Affaires Holds Reception to Celebrate Dutch Queen's Birthday and National Day
Author:
Date: 1960-05-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, May 5th. Dutch Chargé d'Affaires to China, C.H.A. Plug, held a reception this afternoon to celebrate the birthday of Queen Juliana of the Netherlands and the Dutch National Day.

Han Nianlong, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, and others attended the reception. Diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries to China were also invited to attend the reception.

### 庆祝荷兰女王诞辰和国庆日  荷兰驻华代办举行酒会

1960-05-06
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝荷兰女王诞辰和国庆日
    荷兰驻华代办举行酒会
    新华社5日讯　荷兰王国驻中国代办柯大伟，今天下午举行酒会，庆祝荷兰女王朱丽安娜的诞辰和荷兰国庆日。
    外交部部长助理韩念龙等出席了酒会。应邀出席酒会的还有各国驻中国外交使节和外交官员。
