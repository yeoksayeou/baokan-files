Title: Soviet Artists Performance Troupe's First Performance in Beijing; Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun and Others Attended and Watched the Performance
Author:
Date: 1960-05-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, May 14th: The Soviet Artists Performance Troupe held its first performance in China tonight in Beijing.

Vice Premier of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Minister Xia Yan, Deputy Directors of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan and Luo Jun, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Go Baoquan, and famous figures from the literary and art circles such as Zhao Feng and Hu Guogang attended and watched the performance.

Counsellor of the Soviet Embassy in China, Antonov, and diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries in China also attended tonight's performance.

The Soviet artists performed a total of fourteen programs, including ballet excerpts, instrumental solos, vocal solos, folk dances, and soft gymnastics.

Among the artists performing tonight was Li Jiya Krubinnina, People's Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, who is familiar to Chinese audiences. She visited China in 1957 with the Novosibirsk Opera and Ballet Theatre and was warmly welcomed by Chinese audiences. Tonight she performed excerpts from the ballets "Don Quixote" and "The Stone Flower."

Aleksandra Yakovenko, Honored Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic and soprano, sang Russian and Ukrainian folk songs and the Chinese song "Nightingale, You Beautiful Singer."

The performance also included Uzbek and Horezmi folk dances by Klara Yushupova, Honored Artist of the Uzbek Soviet Socialist Republic, and a male solo by Zafar Chabaradaev, Honored Artist of the Kyrgyz Soviet Socialist Republic, winner of the 6th World Youth Festival.

Each program was met with enthusiastic applause and requests for encores, and the Soviet artists took bows again and again.

The performance ended with the singer Yuri Karkin singing Moscow-Beijing. The entire audience stood up and applauded warmly for a long time.

During the intermission, Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun and the heads of various departments present met with all the members of the performance troupe, and at the end of the performance, they went on stage to congratulate them on their successful performance.

According to the 1960 execution plan of the Sino-Soviet Cultural Agreement, the Soviet Artists Performance Troupe will be visiting and performing in China for a period of six weeks.

### 苏艺术家表演团在京首次演出  习仲勋副总理等出席观看演出

1960-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    苏艺术家表演团在京首次演出
    习仲勋副总理等出席观看演出
    新华社14日讯　苏联艺术家表演团今晚在北京举行访华首次演出。
    国务院副总理习仲勋，文化部部长沈雁冰、副部长夏衍，对外文化联络委员会副主任楚图南、罗俊，中苏友好协会总会副秘书长戈宝权，著名文艺界人士赵沨、胡果刚等出席观看演出。
    苏联驻华大使馆参赞安东诺夫和各国驻华使节、外交官员也出席观看了今晚的演出。
    苏联艺术家们一共表演了十四个节目，包括芭蕾舞片断、器乐独奏、独唱、民间舞蹈、柔软体操等。
    今晚演出的艺术家们当中，有中国观众熟悉的俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国人民演员李吉娅·克鲁宾妮娜，她在1957年曾随新西伯利亚歌剧芭蕾舞剧院来我国访问演出，受到我国观众热烈欢迎。今晚她表演了舞剧“堂·吉呵德”和“宝石花”中的片断。
    俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国功勋演员、女高音歌唱家亚历山大·雅科文卡演唱了俄罗斯和乌克兰的民歌和中国歌曲“百灵鸟，你这美好的歌手”。
    演出的节目中还有乌兹别克苏维埃社会主义共和国功勋演员克拉拉·尤舒波娃的乌兹别克和贺列兹姆斯基民间舞蹈，以及第六届世青节获奖者、吉尔吉斯苏维埃社会主义共和国功勋演员扎法尔·恰巴尔达也夫的男声独唱。
    每个节目都受到热烈鼓掌要求重演一遍，苏联艺术家们一次又一次地谢幕。
    演出以歌唱家尤里·卡尔金演唱莫斯科—北京结束。全场起立热烈地长时间地鼓掌。
    在演出休息时，习仲勋副总理和出席的各部门负责人接见了表演团全体人员，并在演出结束时，上台祝贺他们演出成功。
    苏联艺术家表演团根据中苏文化协定1960年执行计划，将在我国作为期六周的访问演出。
