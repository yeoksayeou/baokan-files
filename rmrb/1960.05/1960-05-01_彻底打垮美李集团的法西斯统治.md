Title: Thoroughly Overthrow the Fascist Rule of the US-Syngman Rhee Clique

Author: An Dong

Date: 1960-05-01

Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, An Dong, 30th -- People in An Dong city, who face North Korea across the river and jointly resisted American aggression, held a rally and demonstration parade with 100,000 participants yesterday afternoon, resolutely supporting the South Korean people's patriotic and just struggle.

Wang He, First Secretary of the An Dong Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and other party and government leaders, as well as those in charge of local organizations of people's organizations and democratic parties, participated in the conference.

The conference began at 1:30 p.m. Wang He, First Secretary of the An Dong Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, spoke first at the conference. He said that the two million people in the An Dong area expressed their utmost indignation and severe condemnation of the bloody atrocities of the US-Syngman Rhee clique in massacring and suppressing the South Korean people, and expressed their highest respect and firm support for the South Korean people who are fighting heroically for national liberation, democratic freedom and the right to survival. He said that the days of US imperialism are getting worse and worse. No matter how hard US imperialism struggles, history will not be shifted by the will of imperialism and reactionaries. Imperialism and all reactionaries are bound to fail, and the oppressed people are bound to win.

Subsequently, heads of various people's organizations and local organizations of democratic parties, as well as representatives of workers, peasants, and students, spoke at the conference. Finally, the conference adopted a telegram supporting the South Korean people's patriotic and just struggle.

After the meeting, 100,000 people held a massive demonstration parade on three routes.

### 彻底打垮美李集团的法西斯统治

1960-05-01
第4版()
专栏：

    彻底打垮美李集团的法西斯统治
    安东
    新华社安东30日电　和朝鲜人民隔江相望并且共同抵抗过美国侵略的安东市人民，昨天下午举行了有十万人参加的集会和示威游行，坚决支持南朝鲜人民的爱国正义斗争。
    中共安东市委第一书记王鹤等党政领导人和各人民团体、各民主党派地方组织的负责人都参加了大会。
    大会在下午一点半开始。中共安东市委第一书记王鹤首先在大会上讲话。他说，我们安东市地区两百万人民，对美李集团屠杀和镇压南朝鲜人民血腥暴行表示万分愤怒和严厉谴责，对正在为争取民族解放、民主自由和生存权利而英勇斗争的南朝鲜人民表示崇高的敬意和坚决的支援。他说，美帝国主义的日子越来越不好过了，不管美帝国主义如何挣扎，但历史总是不以帝国主义和反动派的意志为转移，帝国主义和一切反动派必然要失败，被压迫的人民一定要胜利。
    接着在大会上讲话的，还有各人民团体和各民主党派地方组织的负责人，以及工人、农民、学生代表等。大会最后通过了支援南朝鲜人民爱国正义斗争的通电。
    会后，十万群众分三路举行了声势浩大的示威游行。
