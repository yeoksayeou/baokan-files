Title: Sambuu Returns to Ulaanbaatar After Visiting the Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, and Germany
Author:
Date: 1960-05-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency report: According to Xinhua News Agency, after a friendly visit of nearly a month to the Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, and the German Democratic Republic, Sambuu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia, and his entourage returned to Ulaanbaatar by plane on the 8th.

Sambuu and his entourage spent eight days on a friendly visit to the Soviet Union. In addition to Moscow, he also visited Kiev and Minsk.

During his visits to Poland, Czechoslovakia, and the German Democratic Republic, Sambuu held cordial and friendly talks with the leaders of the fraternal countries. In these talks, both sides were in complete agreement on further developing friendly cooperation, strengthening the unity of the socialist camp, the assessment of the current international situation, and the ways to solve the most important international problems.

### 访问苏、波、捷、德后  桑布回到乌兰巴托

1960-05-11
第6版()
专栏：

    访问苏、波、捷、德后
    桑布回到乌兰巴托
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布和他的随行人员在苏联、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克和德意志民主共和国进行了为期近一个月的友好访问以后，于8日坐飞机回到乌兰巴托。
    桑布及其随行人员在苏联共进行了八天的友好访问。除莫斯科外，他还访问了基辅和明斯克等地。
    桑布在访问波兰、捷克斯洛伐克和德意志民主共和国期间，曾与各兄弟国家领导人举行了亲密友好的会谈。在这些会谈中，双方对进一步发展友好合作关系、加强社会主义阵营的团结、对当前国际形势的估计，以及对解决最重要的国际问题的途径的看法是完全一致的。
