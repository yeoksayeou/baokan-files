Title: Zhu Muzhi Hosts a Banquet for the Burmese Journalists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-05-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. Zhu Muzhi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, hosted a banquet this afternoon in honor of the Burmese Journalists Delegation headed by U Aung Dan, Chairman of the Burmese Journalists Association.

Attending as guests were Li Bingquan, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Journalists Association; Shao Zonghan, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Xu Hongjiu, Secretary-General of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association; and leading figures from the capital's journalistic circles. U Yong Lin, Second Secretary of the Burmese Embassy in China, was also invited to the banquet.

### 朱穆之设宴招待缅甸新闻工作者代表团

1960-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

    朱穆之设宴招待缅甸新闻工作者代表团
    新华社2日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席朱穆之今午设宴招待以缅甸新闻工作者协会主席吴昂丹为首的缅甸新闻工作者代表团。
    作陪的有：中华全国新闻工作者协会书记处书记李炳泉、外交部新闻司副司长邵宗汉、中缅友协秘书长徐宏九及首都新闻界负责人。缅甸驻华大使馆二等秘书吴容林也应邀出席了宴会。
