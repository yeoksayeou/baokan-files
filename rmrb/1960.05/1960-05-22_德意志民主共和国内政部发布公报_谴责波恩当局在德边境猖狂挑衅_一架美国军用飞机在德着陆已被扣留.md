Title: German Democratic Republic Ministry of Interior Issues Communiqué Condemning Bonn Authorities' Rampant Provocations on German Border; U.S. Military Aircraft Landing in Germany Detained
Author:
Date: 1960-05-22
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, May 19th: A communiqué released by the Press Office of the Ministry of Interior of the German Democratic Republic stated that the recent new provocations launched by West Germany on the western border of the German Democratic Republic show that the Bonn authorities are in no way willing to lag behind the American aerial bandits. From January to March 1960, a total of 520 provocative incidents occurred.

The communiqué said that the Bonn authorities are attempting to create new tension and undermine relations between the two Germanys by creating incidents on the western border of the German Democratic Republic.

The communiqué concluded that the Bonn authorities should bear full responsibility for all the consequences arising from these incidents.
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, May 21st: TASS Berlin, May 21st: According to reports from qualified sources by the German News Agency: On May 20th, a U.S. military aircraft landed on the territory of the German Democratic Republic. All personnel on the aircraft have been taken into custody.

### 德意志民主共和国内政部发布公报  谴责波恩当局在德边境猖狂挑衅  一架美国军用飞机在德着陆已被扣留

1960-05-22
第3版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国内政部发布公报
    谴责波恩当局在德边境猖狂挑衅
    一架美国军用飞机在德着陆已被扣留
    新华社柏林19日电　德意志民主共和国内政部新闻处发布的公报说，最近西德在德意志民主共和国西部边界发动的新的挑衅表明，波恩当局在各方面都不甘落在美国空中强盗的后面。从1960年1月到3月，共发生了五百二十起挑衅事件。
    公报说，波恩当局企图通过在德意志民主共和国西部边境制造事件来制造新的紧张，破坏两个德国之间的关系。
    公报最后说，波恩当局应对由这些事件而造成的一切后果负完全的责任。
    新华社柏林21日电　塔斯社柏林21日讯：德意志通讯社根据有资格方面提供的情况报道说：5月20日，有一架美国军用飞机在德意志民主共和国领土上着陆。飞机上的全部人员已被看管起来。
