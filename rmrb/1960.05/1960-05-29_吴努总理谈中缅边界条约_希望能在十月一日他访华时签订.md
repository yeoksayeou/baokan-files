Title: Premier U Nu Talks About Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty, Hoping to Sign it During His Visit to China on October 1st
Author:
Date: 1960-05-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 27th - Burmese Prime Minister U Nu said at a press conference here on the 24th that he hoped the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty could be signed on October 1st this year when he visits Beijing.

He said that he had talked with Premier Zhou Enlai during Premier Zhou Enlai's visit to Burma. Premier Zhou Enlai also expressed his agreement. U Nu also said that the Burmese and Chinese sides are now discussing how to hold a meeting of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee as soon as possible.

### 吴努总理谈中缅边界条约  希望能在十月一日他访华时签订

1960-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

    吴努总理谈中缅边界条约
    希望能在十月一日他访华时签订
    新华社仰光27日电　缅甸总理吴努24日在这里举行的一次记者招待会上说，他希望中缅边界条约能在今年10月1日他访问北京时签订。
    他说，在周恩来总理访问缅甸时，他曾经和周恩来总理谈过。周恩来总理也表示同意。吴努还说，现在缅中双方正在商谈，使中缅边界联合委员会尽早举行会议。
