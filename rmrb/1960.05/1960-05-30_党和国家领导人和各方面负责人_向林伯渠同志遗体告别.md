Title: Party and State Leaders and Responsible Persons from Various Fields Pay Last Respects to Comrade Lin Boqu's Remains
Author:
Date: 1960-05-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 29th. Party and state leaders and responsible persons from various fields went to the Beijing Hospital today to pay their last respects to the remains of Comrade Lin Boqu.

The remains of Comrade Lin Boqu were surrounded by flowers and greenery. In front was a wreath presented by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

At 4:40 PM, Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee and Chairman of the People's Republic of China, arrived at Beijing Hospital. He stood in silent tribute before the serene countenance of Comrade Lin Boqu.

At 5:45 PM, Soong Ching Ling, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, arrived at Beijing Hospital to pay her respects to Comrade Lin Boqu's remains.

Party and state leaders who went to pay their respects included Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee; Peng Zhen, Luo Ronghuan, Li Fuchun, Peng Dehuai, Liu Bocheng, He Long, and Li Xiannian, members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee; Zhang Wentian and Lu Dingyi, alternate members; Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee; Liu Lantao and Hu Qiaomu, alternate secretaries; Deng Zihui, Luo Ruiqing, and Xi Zhongxun, Vice Premiers of the State Council; Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Saifudin, He Xiangning, and Lin Feng, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Li Siguang, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Ye Jianying, Zhang Zhizhong, and Fu Zuoyi, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council; Xie Juezai, President of the Supreme People's Court, and others.

Also present to pay their respects were members and alternate members of the CPC Central Committee in Beijing and responsible persons of central departments, responsible persons of government departments, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, responsible people from various democratic parties and people's organizations, members of the Standing Committees of the National People's Congress and the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, responsible persons of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPC and the Beijing Municipal People's Committee, and responsible persons of various provinces and autonomous regions in Beijing.

### 党和国家领导人和各方面负责人  向林伯渠同志遗体告别

1960-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

    党和国家领导人和各方面负责人
    向林伯渠同志遗体告别
    新华社29日讯　党和国家的领导人和各方面的负责人，今天先后到北京医院向林伯渠同志的遗体告别。
    林伯渠同志的遗体周围布满鲜花和绿草。前面放着中国共产党中央委员会敬献的花圈。
    下午四时四十分，中共中央副主席、中华人民共和国主席刘少奇来到北京医院。他在林伯渠同志安详的遗容前面，肃立致哀。
    下午五时四十五分，中华人民共和国副主席宋庆龄来到北京医院，向林伯渠同志遗体致哀告别。
    前往告别的党和国家领导人，有中共中央总书记邓小平，中共中央政治局委员彭真、罗荣桓、李富春、彭德怀、刘伯承、贺龙、李先念，候补委员张闻天、陆定一，中央书记处书记王稼祥，候补书记刘澜涛、胡乔木，国务院副总理邓子恢、罗瑞卿、习仲勋，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、赛福鼎、何香凝、林枫，政协全国委员会副主席李四光，国防委员会副主席叶剑英、张治中、傅作义，最高人民法院院长谢觉哉等。
    前往告别的，还有在北京的中共中央委员、候补委员和中央各部门的负责人，政府各部门的负责人，中国人民解放军的高级将领，各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人，人大常委会和政协全国委员会常委会的委员，中共北京市委和北京市人民委员会的负责人，以及在北京的各省、自治区的负责人等。
