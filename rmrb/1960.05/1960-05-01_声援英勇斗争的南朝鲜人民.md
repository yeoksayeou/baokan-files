Title: In Support of the Heroic Struggle of the South Korean People
Author:
Date: 1960-05-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, 29th—Today, 100,000 people from all walks of life in Jiangxi Province and Nanchang City gathered at Nanchang Bayi Square this afternoon and held a grand parade afterwards to support the patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people.
    At noon today, 100,000 workers, farmers, students, citizens, and militiamen, holding high banners and cartoon placards, and shouting slogans such as "Yankee imperialists get out of South Korea! Get out of our territory of Taiwan! Get out of Japan! Get out of South Vietnam!" flocked to the "Bayi" Square from all directions.
    The meeting began at 2:30 p.m. Guo Guangzhou, Alternate Secretary of the Jiangxi Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and First Secretary of the Nanchang Municipal Committee, spoke first at the meeting. He heartily congratulated the South Korean people on the initial victory of their patriotic and just struggle, expressed the firm support of the people of Jiangxi Province for the patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people, and severely condemned the heinous crimes committed by U.S. imperialism in South Korea. He also expressed firm support for the just struggle of the Japanese people against the Japan-U.S. "Security Treaty."
    Subsequently, representatives of the Jiangxi Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, the Women's Federation, the Communist Youth League, and local organizations of democratic parties, as well as representatives of workers, farmers, and religious circles, spoke, unanimously expressing severe condemnation of the heinous crimes of the U.S.-Syngman Rhee clique and firm support for the just struggle of the South Korean people.
    After the meeting, 100,000 people, waving colorful flags and banners, formed twelve columns and marched towards the city center, holding a grand parade.

### 声援英勇斗争的南朝鲜人民

1960-05-01
第4版()
专栏：

    声援英勇斗争的南朝鲜人民
    南昌
    新华社南昌29日电　江西省和南昌市各界十万人民今天下午在南昌八一广场集会，会后并举行盛大游行，声援南朝鲜人民的爱国正义斗争。
    今天中午，十万工人、农民、学生、市民和民兵，高举着横幅标语和漫画牌，高呼着“美帝国主义从南朝鲜滚出去！从我国领土台湾滚出去！从日本滚出去！从南越滚出去！”等口号，从四面八方拥向“八一”广场。
    大会在下午二时半开始。中共江西省委候补书记、南昌市委第一书记郭光洲首先在会上讲话。他衷心祝贺南朝鲜人民爱国正义斗争所取得的初步胜利，代表江西全省人民表示坚决支持南朝鲜人民的爱国正义斗争，并严厉谴责美帝国主义在南朝鲜犯下的滔天罪行。他还表示坚决支持日本人民反对日美“安全条约”的正义斗争。
    接着，江西省工会、妇联、共青团和民主党派地方组织代表以及工人、农民、宗教界代表讲了话，他们一致表示严厉谴责美李集团的滔天罪行，坚决支持南朝鲜人民的正义斗争。
    大会结束后，十万群众挥舞着五颜六色的旗帜和标语，列成十二路纵队向市区进发，举行了盛大的游行。
