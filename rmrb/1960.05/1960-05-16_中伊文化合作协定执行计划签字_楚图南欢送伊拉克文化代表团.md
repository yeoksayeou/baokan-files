Title: Sino-Iraqi Cultural Cooperation Agreement Implementation Plan Signed, Chu Tunan Hosts Farewell for Iraqi Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-05-16
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, 15th Dispatch: The 1960 Implementation Plan of the Cultural Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Republic of Iraq was signed in Beijing this morning.
    Luo Jun, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Dr. Mahdi al-Matsumi, head of the Iraqi cultural delegation, signed the implementation plan on behalf of their respective governments.
    Xinhua News Agency, 15th Dispatch: Chu Tunan, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, held a banquet at noon today to bid farewell to the Iraqi cultural delegation headed by Dr. Mahdi al-Matsumi.

### 中伊文化合作协定执行计划签字  楚图南欢送伊拉克文化代表团

1960-05-16
第5版()
专栏：

    中伊文化合作协定执行计划签字
    楚图南欢送伊拉克文化代表团
    据新华社15日讯　中华人民共和国政府和伊拉克共和国政府文化合作协定1960年执行计划，今天上午在北京签字。
    对外文化联络委员会副主任罗俊，伊拉克文化代表团团长马赫迪·马特苏米博士，分别代表两国政府在执行计划上签字。
    据新华社15日讯　对外文化联络委员会副主任楚图南今天中午举行宴会，欢送以马赫迪·马特苏米博士为首的伊拉克文化代表团。
