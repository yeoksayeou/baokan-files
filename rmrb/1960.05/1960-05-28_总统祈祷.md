Title: President's Prayer
Author: Wang Xiaowei, Sun Qingquan
Date: 1960-05-28
Page: 8

President's Prayer (Danxian)
Tianjin Sundries Export Company Wang Xiaowei Sun Qingquan (Prelude)
　　U.S. imperialism is too insidious,
　　Assembling aggressive blocs in the world.
　　Colluding with reactionary lackeys, acting in cahoots,
　　Poisoning the people to seek hegemony in the world. (Peaceful Years)
　　Eisenhower, in power in the United States,
　　Aggresses everywhere, trying to make a fortune,
　　He is the number one capitalist boss,
　　His heart is not satisfied unless he swallows the world.
　　Looking for lackeys everywhere, seizing every opportunity,
　　Syngman Rhee of South Korea is one of them,
　　Japanese Prime Minister Nobusuke Kishi, Ngo Dinh Diem of South Vietnam,
　　Piecing together aggressive blocs.
　　Speaking of Nobusuke Kishi, this puppet regime,
　　Is the mortal enemy of the Japanese people,
　　Is the apple of the eye of the U.S. imperialists,
　　Eisenhower colludes with him,
　　To sign a military alliance treaty,
　　To revive Japanese militarism. (Southern Gong and Northern Drum)
　　Nobusuke Kishi forces a smile,
　　Respectfully calls out "Great President, my good backer,"
　　Alas! Recent developments frighten me,
　　Our power politics are really a bit precarious,
　　Great President, please take a look,
　　These volcanoes are about to erupt. (Four-Beat Tune)
　　Eisenhower's heart is filled with sorrow upon hearing this,
　　Thousands of words of bitterness in his heart.
　　Really hoping that you can achieve your wishes,
　　Who would have thought of wasting millions of dollars on Japan?
　　Li Qipeng was beaten to death, how tragic,
　　We should go to offer condolences and burn paper money.
　　The two of them, hand in hand, came to the White House courtyard,
　　To pray before God. (Bangzi Buddha)
　　Eisenhower, kneels before the building,
　　His heart trembling, he prays.
　　My Nobusuke Kishi! The big parades and demonstrations frighten me.
　　Syngman Rhee, Ngo Dinh Diem are my partners,
　　Partners! This time we might be finished!
　　Alas... hai hei hai alas...
　　Believer Eisenhower, bathes and purifies himself,
　　Respectfully asks God to descend to earth!
　　Bless me to dominate the world, maintain my trampling of the people,
　　Bestow fortune on believers, grant believers a few more years of life. (Flowing rhythm fast storytelling)
　　Suddenly hearing loud shouts from all directions,
　　It turns out to be hundreds of millions of people shouting angrily, burning flames of hatred everywhere,
　　Seeing demonstrating masses, holding up signs, waving their iron fists, telling you to get out, guaranteeing you no burial place, leaving a foul reputation for ten thousand years.

### 总统祈祷
王晓为　孙清泉
1960-05-28
第8版()
专栏：

    总统祈祷〔单弦〕
    天津杂品出口公司　王晓为　孙清泉（曲头）
  美帝国主义太阴险，
  在世界上拼凑侵略集团。
  勾结那反动派走狗，狼狈为奸，
  毒害人民在世界上称霸权。（太平年）
  艾森豪威尔，在美国掌政权，
  到处侵略想大发财源，
  他是个资本家头号的大老板，
  不独吞世界他的心不甘。
  到处找走狗，有空子他就钻，
  南朝鲜的李承晚就是他的一
    员，
  日本首相岸信介，南越的吴庭
      艳，
  东拼西凑搞侵略集团。
  单说岸信介这个傀儡政权，
  是日本人民的死敌，
  是美帝的心肝，
  艾森豪威尔跟他勾串合谋，
  要把军事同盟条约签，
  把日本军国主义来个死灰复
      燃。（南锣北鼓）
  岸信介强带笑颜，
  尊声大总统，我的好靠山，
  唉呀！近来形势，我的心惊肉
      战，
  咱的实力政策真有点悬，
  大总统，您快看一看，
  这些处火山都要喷火焰。（四板腔）
  艾森豪威尔听此话，心中悲惨，
  千言万语他苦在心间。
  实指望，盼你们达到心愿，
  谁料想为日本浪费百万美元。
  李起鹏被打死，惨也不惨，
  咱应该去吊孝烧化纸钱。
  他二人，手拉手，来到白宫的
    大院，
  要在上帝面前祷告一番。（梆子佛）
  艾森豪威尔，跪倒在楼前，
  心惊肉跳来祈祷一番。
  我的岸信介！大游行大示威我
    的心里光打战。
  李承晚、吴庭艳我的伙计，
  伙计们哪！这回咱们恐怕要完
      蛋哪！
  唉……嗐嘿嗐唉……。
  信徒艾森豪威尔，沐浴净身，
    恭请上帝降临凡哪！
  保佑我独霸世界，维护我把人
      民摧残，
  赐福信徒大发财源，赐寿信徒
      再活几年。（流水板快书）
  猛听得四面八方高声喊，
  原来是亿万人民连声怒吼，到
    处燃烧愤恨的火焰，
  但得见示威的群众，高举标语，
    挥动铁拳说叫你滚蛋，管叫
    你死无葬身之处，遗臭万年。
