Title: State Council Appointment and Removal List
Author: 
Date: 1960-05-22
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The State Council appointed and removed a number of personnel from state organs, universities and colleges, and embassies abroad on March 28 and April 29.

The list of appointments and removals on March 28 is as follows:

　　Appointed Cui Qun as Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

　　Removed Cui Qun from the position of Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia.

The list of appointments and removals on April 29 is as follows:

　　Appointed Liu Xinquan as Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs;
　　Yuan Yeli as Vice Minister of Aquatic Products;
　　Xu Binzhou as Vice Minister of Agricultural Machinery;
　　Guang Fuzhao as Vice Minister of Geology;
　　Kong Xiangzhen as Vice Minister of Light Industry;
　　Peng De and Yu Mei as Vice Ministers of Communications;
　　Wei Zhenwu as Vice Minister of Agriculture;
　　Zhang Zhao as Vice Minister of Forestry;
　　Cui Yaoxian as Vice President of the People's University of China;
　　Li Sen as Vice President of Beijing Institute of Technology;
　　Zhou Nan and Zhang Su as Vice Presidents of Nanjing Aeronautical Institute;
　　Li Jingyu as President of Beijing Institute of Agricultural Mechanization;
　　Wang Peng as Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the People's Republic of Bulgaria;
　　Wang Ping as Cultural Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the German Democratic Republic;
　　Liu Xianglun as Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Democratic People's Republic of Korea;
　　Wang Tao as Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

　　Removed Song Minzhi from the position of Vice President of Harbin Institute of Technology;
　　Xu Juefei from the position of President of Beijing Institute of Agricultural Mechanization;
　　Wen Pengjiu from the position of Cultural Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the German Democratic Republic;
　　Gao Shirong from the position of Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Czechoslovak Republic;
　　Wang Zhuozhi from the position of Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the People's Republic of Bulgaria;
　　Du Yun from the position of Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Republic of India;
　　Xie Shoutian from the position of Commercial Counselor of the Office of the Chargé d'Affaires of the People's Republic of China in the United Kingdom.
