Title: Let Kinmen Compatriots and Chiang Troops Enjoy "May Day"
Author:
Date: 1960-05-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 30th -- A spokesman for the Fujian Front Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army announced: May 1st is International Labor Day. In order to allow compatriots and Chiang Kai-shek troops on the islands of Greater Kinmen, Lesser Kinmen, Greater Tan, and Lesser Tan to celebrate this great world labor people's holiday, the artillery units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army on the Fujian Front have been ordered to cease shelling on May 1st and have already broadcast this on the front lines on the afternoon of April 30th.

### 让金门岛同胞和蒋军欢度“五一”节  福建前线停止炮击

1960-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    让金门岛同胞和蒋军欢度“五一”节
    福建前线停止炮击
    新华社福建前线30日电　中国人民解放军福建前线司令部发言人宣称：5月1日，是国际劳动节。为了让大金门、小金门、大担、二担等岛屿上的同胞和蒋军官兵欢度这个伟大的世界劳动人民的节日，福建前线中国人民解放军炮兵部队，奉命在5月1日停止炮击，并且已经在4月30日下午在前线作了广播。
