Title: Khrushchev to Visit Ghana
Author:
Date: 1960-05-10
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 6th. TASS reports: Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Khrushchev accepted the invitation of Ghanaian Prime Minister Nkrumah and will visit Ghana at a time he deems appropriate. The exact date of the visit is to be determined later.
    Khrushchev received the Ghanaian parliamentary delegation currently visiting the Soviet Union at the Kremlin in Moscow on the 6th. Nkrumah's aforementioned invitation was extended to Khrushchev by the head of the delegation, Kozyjo, during this reception.

### 赫鲁晓夫将访问加纳

1960-05-10
第6版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫将访问加纳
    新华社莫斯科6日电　据塔斯社报道：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫接受加纳总理恩克鲁玛的邀请，将于他认为适当的时候访问加纳。确切的访问日期有待以后确定。
    赫鲁晓夫6日在莫斯科克里姆林宫接见了目前正在苏联访问的加纳议会代表团。恩克鲁玛的上述邀请，就是在这次接见时由代表团团长科齐约向赫鲁晓夫提出来的。
