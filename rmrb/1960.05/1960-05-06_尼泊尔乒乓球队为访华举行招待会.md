Title: Nepalese Table Tennis Team Holds Reception for Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1960-05-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 5th. The Nepalese Table Tennis Team held a reception in Beijing today for their visit to China.

Rong Gaotang, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation, Zhang Lianhua, Secretary-General, Zhang Yihua, Secretary-General of the Sino-Nepalese Friendship Association, Zhang Bingguang, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation and Chairman of the Beijing Students' Federation, Yi Liwen, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the China Women Magazine, and China's famous table tennis players Rong Guotuan, Qiu Zhonghui and others were invited to attend the reception.

Nepalese students in Beijing were also invited to attend the reception.

The reception was presided over by Shanta Ram Bhandari, the leader of the Nepalese Table Tennis Team. At the beginning of the reception, he said that as the first Nepalese sports delegation to visit China, they were warmly welcomed as soon as they set foot on Chinese soil. He expressed his heartfelt thanks to the hosts on behalf of the Nepalese athletes and the Nepalese Table Tennis Association. Bhandari also expressed his hope that Chinese athletes would visit Nepal in his speech.

Rong Gaotang then spoke. He said that during the Nepalese Table Tennis Team's three-week visit to China, they brought the deep friendship of the Nepalese people to the Chinese people. At the same time, through friendly matches, the Nepalese table tennis players' good skills and excellent style have left an unforgettable impression on the Chinese people, and have contributed to further promoting the traditional friendship between the two countries.

At the reception, the leader of the Nepalese Table Tennis Team, Bhandari, the captain, Mrs. Kamal Rana, Vice-Chairman of the Nepalese Upper House, the captain, Ji. P. Lohani, and the male and female players of the table tennis team, had cordial conversations with the Chinese guests.

### 尼泊尔乒乓球队为访华举行招待会

1960-05-06
第4版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔乒乓球队为访华举行招待会
    新华社5日讯　尼泊尔乒乓球队为访问中国，今天在北京举行招待会。
    中华全国体育总会副主席荣高棠、秘书长张联华，中国尼泊尔友好协会总干事张亦桦，全国学联副主席、北京市学联主席张炳光，中国妇女杂志社副社长易理文，以及我国著名乒乓球选手容国团、丘钟惠等人应邀出席了招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的，还有在北京的尼泊尔学生等。
    招待会由尼泊尔乒乓球队领队山塔·拉姆·班达里主持。他在招待会开始时讲话说，他们做为第一个访问中国的尼泊尔体育代表队，刚一踏上中国的国土，就受到了热烈的欢迎。他代表尼泊尔运动员和尼泊尔乒乓球协会，向主人表示衷心的感谢。班达里在讲话中还希望中国运动员到尼泊尔访问。
    荣高棠接着讲话。他说，尼泊尔乒乓球队在我国三个星期的访问中，带来了尼泊尔人民对中国人民的深厚友情。同时，通过友谊比赛，尼泊尔乒乓球选手的良好技术和优秀的作风，都给中国人民留下了难以忘怀的印象，并且对于进一步促进两国传统的友谊作出了贡献。
    在招待会上，尼泊尔乒乓球队领队班达里，队长、尼泊尔上议院副议长卡玛尔·拉纳夫人，队长吉·普·洛哈尼和乒乓球队的男女选手们，分别同中国客人们进行了亲切的交谈。
