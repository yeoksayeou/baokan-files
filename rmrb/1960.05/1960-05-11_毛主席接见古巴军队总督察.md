Title: Chairman Mao Receives the Inspector General of the Cuban Army
Author:
Date: 1960-05-11
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, May 10th: Chairman Mao Zedong met here this afternoon with Major William Gálvez Rodríguez, Inspector General of the Cuban Army, and his wife, who are currently visiting China, and had a cordial conversation with them. Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, was present at the meeting.
    (Attached picture)
    Above: Chairman Mao Zedong met with Major William Gálvez Rodríguez, Inspector General of the Cuban Army, and his wife in Zhengzhou on May 10th.
                          Xinhua News Agency reporter Chen Juanmei (fax photo)

### 毛主席接见古巴军队总督察

1960-05-11
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见古巴军队总督察
    新华社郑州10日电　毛泽东主席今天下午在这里接见了正在我国参观访问的古巴军队总督察威廉·加尔维斯·罗德里格斯少校和他的夫人，并和他们进行了亲切的谈话。接见时在座的有中共中央书记处候补书记杨尚昆。
    （附图片）
    上图：毛泽东主席5月10日在郑州接见古巴军队总督察威廉·加尔维斯·罗德里格斯少校夫妇。
                          新华社记者　陈娟美摄（传真照片）
