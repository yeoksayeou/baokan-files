Title: Danish Minister of Ecclesiastical Affairs, Mrs. Bodil Koch, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-30
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, May 29th. At the invitation of Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Danish Minister of Ecclesiastical Affairs, Mrs. Bodil Koch, along with her husband Professor Koch, daughter Marianne, and private secretary Niels Holm, arrived in Beijing by plane today. Mrs. Bodil Koch will be visiting China for three weeks.

Tonight, Minister Shen Yanbing held a banquet to welcome the guests.

### 丹麦宗教事务大臣博迪尔·科克夫人到京

1960-05-30
第7版()
专栏：

    丹麦宗教事务大臣博迪尔·科克夫人到京
    据新华社29日讯　应文化部部长沈雁冰的邀请，丹麦宗教事务大臣博迪尔·科克夫人及其丈夫科克教授、女儿玛丽安娜、私人秘书尼尔斯·荷尔姆，在今天乘飞机到达北京。博迪尔·科克夫人将在我国作为期三周的参观访问。
    今晚，沈雁冰部长举行宴会欢迎客人。
