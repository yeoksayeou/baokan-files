Title: Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun Receives Hungarian Legal Workers Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-05-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, May 18th: Vice Premier of the State Council, Xi Zhongxun, received Dr. Andor Ferencz, Deputy Minister of Justice of the Hungarian People's Republic, and all members of the Hungarian legal workers delegation led by him this afternoon, who are currently visiting our country, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Wu Defeng, Vice President and First Secretary of the Secretariat of the China Political Science and Law Society, and Vice Presidents Zhang Zhiran and Zhang Youyu.

Also present at the reception was Sigeti, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Hungarian Embassy in China.

### 习仲勋副总理  接见匈法律工作者代表团

1960-05-19
第5版()
专栏：

    习仲勋副总理
    接见匈法律工作者代表团
    新华社18日讯　国务院副总理习仲勋今天下午接见了正在我国访问的匈牙利人民共和国司法部副部长安多·费伦茨博士和由他率领的匈牙利法律工作者代表团全体团员，并同他们进行了亲切友好的交谈。
    接见时在座的，有中国政治法律学会副会长、书记处第一书记吴德峰，副会长张志让、张友渔等。
    接见时，匈牙利驻中国大使馆临时代办西格蒂也在座。
