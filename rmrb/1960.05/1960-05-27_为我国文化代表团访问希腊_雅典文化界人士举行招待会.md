Title: Reception Held by Athens Cultural Figures for Visit of Chinese Cultural Delegation to Greece
Author:
Date: 1960-05-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 25th. Athens dispatch: Athens cultural figures held a reception on May 23rd for the Chinese Cultural Delegation currently visiting Greece.

The Chinese Cultural Delegation is led by Zhang Zhixiang, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. Over one hundred members from the Greek National Writers Union, the Writers Association, and the Young Writers Union attended the reception. The President of the National Writers Union and the head of the Chinese delegation delivered speeches at the reception.

On May 21st, members of the delegation visited Thessaloniki, the second largest city in Greece. In the afternoon, they paid a courtesy call to the mayor of the city.

### 为我国文化代表团访问希腊  雅典文化界人士举行招待会

1960-05-27
第4版()
专栏：

    为我国文化代表团访问希腊
    雅典文化界人士举行招待会
    新华社日内瓦25日电　雅典消息：雅典文化界人士5月23日为正在访问希腊的中国文化代表团举行招待会。
    中国文化代表团是由中国人民对外文化协会副会长张致祥率领的。希腊全国作家联合会、作家协会和青年作家联合会的一百多名会员出席了招待会。全国作家联合会会长和中国代表团团长在招待会上讲了话。
    代表团团员5月21日访问了希腊第二大城市萨罗尼加。他们在下午拜会了这个城市的市长。
