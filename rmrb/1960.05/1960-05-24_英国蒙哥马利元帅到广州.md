Title: British Field Marshal Montgomery Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-05-24
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, May 23rd: British Field Marshal Montgomery, who is visiting our country, arrived in Guangzhou by train from Shenzhen this afternoon.

Those who welcomed him at the station included General Li Da, Director of the All-China Athletic Federation, Liang Xiang, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou, Wang Jinchuan, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Guangdong Provincial People's Committee, and Zeng Xijiu, Deputy Director of the Guangdong Provincial Athletic Federation.

This afternoon, Field Marshal Montgomery, accompanied by General Li Da, Wang Jinchuan, and others, visited a militia training session at a factory in Guangzhou.

In the evening, Zhu Guang, the Mayor of Guangzhou, hosted a banquet to welcome Field Marshal Montgomery. General Li Da, Liang Xiang, Wang Jinchuan, and others attended as guests.

### 英国蒙哥马利元帅到广州

1960-05-24
第5版()
专栏：

    英国蒙哥马利元帅到广州
    新华社广州23日电　来我国访问的英国蒙哥马利元帅，今天下午从深圳乘火车到达广州。
    到车站欢迎的，有中国人民国防体育协会主任李达上将，广州市副市长梁湘，广东省人民委员会外事处处长王锦川，广东省国防体育协会副主任曾席久等。
    下午，蒙哥马利元帅在李达上将和王锦川等陪同下，参观了广州市一个工厂的民兵训练。
    晚上，广州市市长朱光设宴欢迎蒙哥马利元帅。李达上将、梁湘、王锦川等出席作陪。
