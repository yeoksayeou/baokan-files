Title: Khrushchev Visits Macmillan and De Gaulle
Author:
Date: 1960-05-19
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, May 18th, TASS, Paris dispatch: On May 18th, before leaving Paris, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, visited British Prime Minister Macmillan.
    During the meeting, the Soviet side present were Gromyko, Malinovsky, and the Ambassador to the United Kingdom, Soldatov; the British side present were Lloyd, Miller, and the Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Reilly.
    Xinhua News Agency, May 18th, TASS, Paris dispatch: On May 18th, before leaving Paris, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, visited French President De Gaulle.
    The Soviet side present were Gromyko, Malinovsky, and the Soviet Ambassador to France, Vinogradov.

### 赫鲁晓夫拜会麦克米伦和戴高乐

1960-05-19
第2版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫拜会麦克米伦和戴高乐
    新华社18日讯　塔斯社巴黎讯：18日，苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫在离开巴黎前拜会了英国首相麦克米伦。
    会见时，苏联方面在座的有葛罗米柯、马利诺夫斯基和驻英国大使索尔达托夫；英国方面在座的有劳埃德、米勒、驻苏联大使赖利。
    新华社18日讯　塔斯社巴黎讯：18日，苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫在离开巴黎前拜会了法国总统戴高乐。
    苏联方面在场的有葛罗米柯、马利诺夫斯基和苏联驻法国大使维诺格拉多夫。
