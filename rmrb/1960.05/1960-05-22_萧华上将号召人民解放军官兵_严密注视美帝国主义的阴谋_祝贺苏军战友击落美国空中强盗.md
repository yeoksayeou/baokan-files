Title: General Xiao Hua Calls on Officers and Soldiers of the People's Liberation Army to Closely Watch the Conspiracies of U.S. Imperialism; Congratulates Soviet Comrades for Shooting Down American Aerial Bandits
Author:
Date: 1960-05-22
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 21st: General Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, today at the Representative Conference of Active Cultural and Educational Elements of the People's Liberation Army, angrily condemned the U.S. aggressors' crimes of aggression and provocation against the Soviet Union, and condemned U.S. imperialism's shameless sabotage of the Four-Power Summit and creation of international tension. He stated that the Chinese people and all officers and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army resolutely support the Soviet government's solemn position, fully support the series of solemn measures taken by the Soviet government against the provocative crimes of the U.S. aggressors, and warmly congratulate the Soviet comrades for their brilliant victory in shooting down an American aerial bandit in one fell swoop. He called on the vast number of officers and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army to raise their vigilance and closely watch the war conspiracies of U.S. imperialism.

### 萧华上将号召人民解放军官兵  严密注视美帝国主义的阴谋  祝贺苏军战友击落美国空中强盗

1960-05-22
第2版()
专栏：

    萧华上将号召人民解放军官兵
    严密注视美帝国主义的阴谋
    祝贺苏军战友击落美国空中强盗
    据新华社21日讯　中国人民解放军总政治部副主任萧华上将，今天在人民解放军文化教育积极分子代表大会上愤怒谴责美国侵略者对苏联的侵略和挑衅罪行，谴责美帝国主义无耻破坏四国政府首脑会议，制造国际紧张局势。他表示，中国人民和人民解放军全体官兵坚决支持苏联政府的严正立场，完全拥护苏联政府对美国侵略者的挑衅罪行所采取的一系列的严正措施，热烈祝贺苏军战友一举击落美国空中强盗的光辉胜利。他号召人民解放军广大官兵提高警惕，严密注视美帝国主义的战争阴谋。
