Title: Korean and Algerian Delegations Hold Talks; Vice Premier Kim Il Says Compromise with Imperialist Robbers is Unacceptable; Krim Belkacem Says Korea Will Surely Achieve Reunification
Author:
Date: 1960-05-12
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 11th May: The government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, headed by Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Krim Belkacem, which is currently visiting Korea, held talks in Pyongyang today.

    The Korean side participating in the talks included: First Vice Premier of the Cabinet Kim Il, Vice Premier of the Cabinet Li Zhouyuan, Minister of Foreign Affairs Park Sung-chul, and Chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army Kim Chang-bong.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 11th May: First Vice Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Kim Il, held a grand banquet tonight to welcome the delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, headed by Deputy Prime Minister Krim Belkacem, which is visiting Korea.

    Kim Il first delivered a speech at the banquet. He once again extended a warm welcome to the delegation and expressed his deep sympathy and support for the Algerian people who are waging a heroic struggle against French colonialists.

    He said, "Compromising with imperialist robbers is unacceptable. Only by fighting them and giving them a decisive blow. The people of our two countries have stood up and dealt a great blow to the bluffing enemy, exposing the powerlessness of the imperialists in the face of the people who have stood up."

    When Deputy Prime Minister Krim Belkacem delivered a speech at the banquet, he warmly praised the heroic struggle of the Korean people against US imperialism in the Korean War of National Liberation and the great achievements they have made in post-war reconstruction. He said that we were amazed and admired by the high level of industrial development and the wide variety of products in Korea. Krim Belkacem pointed out that the just struggle of the South Korean people against the US-Li clique is surging, and the Korean people will surely be able to achieve the reunification of their motherland.

### 朝鲜阿尔及利亚两国代表团会谈  金一副首相说对帝国主义强盗妥协是不行的  克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆说朝鲜一定能实现统一

1960-05-12
第3版()
专栏：

    朝鲜阿尔及利亚两国代表团会谈
    金一副首相说对帝国主义强盗妥协是不行的
    克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆说朝鲜一定能实现统一
    据新华社平壤11日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团和正在朝鲜访问的以克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆副总理兼外交部长为首的阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团今天在平壤举行了会谈。
    朝鲜方面参加会谈的有：朝鲜内阁第一副首相金一、内阁副首相李周渊、外务相朴成哲、朝鲜人民军总参谋长金昌奉。
    据新华社平壤11日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁第一副首相金一今天晚上举行盛大宴会，欢迎来朝鲜访问的以克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆副总理为首的阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团。
    金一首先在宴会上致词。他再次对代表团表示热烈的欢迎，并且对为反对法国殖民主义者进行英勇战斗的阿尔及利亚人民表示深切的同情和支持。
    他说，“对帝国主义强盗妥协是不行的。只有同他们进行战斗，给他们以决定性的打击。我们两国人民都站起来了，并给予虚张声势的敌人以巨大的打击，暴露了帝国主义者在站起来了的人民面前是无力的。”
    克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆副总理在宴会上致词时，热烈地赞扬了朝鲜人民在朝鲜卫国战争中进行的反对美帝国主义的英勇战斗和他们在战后恢复建设中取得的巨大成就。他说，朝鲜具有的高度的工业水平和多种多样的产品，使我们感到惊讶和敬佩。克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆指出，南朝鲜人民正在进行的反对美李集团的正义斗争正在高涨，朝鲜人民一定能够实现祖国的统一。
