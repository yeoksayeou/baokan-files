Title: The Newest and Most Beautiful Picture
Author: Gao Yun
Date: 1960-05-04
Page: 8

    The Newest and Most Beautiful Picture
    Dalian Shipyard Gao Yun
  Automated line,
      Automated line... like the Milky Way in the sky, like the clear spring in the mountain stream,
  —A jade belt,
      Cutting across the workshop.
  Workpieces converge into a river,
      Rolling forward.
  Steel—
          Silvery light dazzling,
  Copper—
          Golden light brilliant.
  Golden waves and silver waves are churning,
  How delightful by the golden and silver riverbanks.
  Apprentices—
          Are laughing,
  Masters—
        Are praising.
    Sing, dance!
    A continuous stream appears in the workshop,
        —Forever spring! Automated line,
    Automated line... a delicate silver chain, a wondrous string,
    It is clanging,
    Plucking at our heartstrings. We are at the console,
        Pressing the buttons; We are beside the automated line,
        Touring the workshop. Each machine tool,
    Is like a blue gem; The automated line is
    The silver thread that runs through the gems. East—
        Raw materials piled high, West—
        Products like mountains.
    Ahead is,
    The avenue lit red by the morning glow,
    Ahead is,
    The magnificent and solemn palace... Automated line,
    Automated line, The Party's
    Golden light—
        Is your warp, The masses'
    Infinite wisdom—
        Is your weft, Woven into
    Woven into
  The newest and most beautiful picture!

### 最新最美的画面
高云
1960-05-04
第8版()
专栏：

    最新最美的画面
    大连造船厂　高云
  自动线，
      自动线……像天上的银河，像山涧的清泉，
  ——一条玉带，
      横划车间。
  工件汇成河流，
      滚滚向前。
  钢的——
          银光夺目，
  铜的——
          金光灿烂。
  金波银浪在翻腾，
  金银河畔好喜欢。
  徒弟——
          在欢笑，
  师傅——
        在赞叹。
    唱吧，跳吧！
    车间出现长流水，
        ——永远是春天！自动线，
    自动线……一条精美的银链，一条奇异的琴弦，
    它在叮当作响，
    弹动着我们的心弦。我们在操纵台上，
        按动着电钮；我们在自动线旁，
        巡回着车间。台台机床，
    宛如蓝色的宝石；自动线哪它就是
    贯穿宝石的银线。东面——
        原料成堆，西面——
        产品成山。
    前面是，
    朝霞映红的大道，
    前面是，
    瑰丽庄严的宫殿……自动线哪
    自动线，党的
    金色光芒——
        是你的经丝，群众的
    无穷智慧——
        是你的纬线，织成了
    织成了
  最新最美的画面！
