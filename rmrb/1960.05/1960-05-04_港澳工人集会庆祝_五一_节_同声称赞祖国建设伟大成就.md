Title: Hong Kong and Macao Workers Gather to Celebrate "May Day," Praising the Great Achievements of National Construction

Author:

Date: 1960-05-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, May 2nd: Workers in Hong Kong and Macao have been gathering in recent days to warmly celebrate the "May Day" International Labor Day.

　　Hong Kong workers from all industries held a grand gathering at 1:00 PM on May 1st to celebrate "May Day." Xu Zhencai, Chairman of the Assembly, delivered the opening speech, saying: Last year, our country completed the main targets of the Second Five-Year Plan three years ahead of schedule. Recently, the Second Session of the Second National People's Congress approved the 1960 National Economic Development Plan and decided to strive to complete the National Agricultural Development Program two or three years ahead of schedule and vigorously promote urban people's communes. Therefore, celebrating "May Day" International Labor Day today makes us particularly excited and encouraged. Li Sheng, Director of the Hong Kong and Kowloon Federation of Trade Unions, also spoke at the meeting.

　　The Hong Kong and Kowloon Federation of Trade Unions held a tea party for representatives of various trade unions on the evening of April 30th to celebrate the festival. In addition, more than sixty trade unions from various industries also held tea parties, dinners, recreational gatherings, film screenings, and sports competitions to celebrate.

　　Workers from all industries in Macao held a celebration meeting at the Workers' Recreation Center on April 29th. Liang Pei, Chairman of the Assembly, said at the meeting that every construction achievement of our country gives great encouragement to Macao workers. The Macao Federation of Trade Unions held a hometown photo exhibition and a recreational evening party on May 1st.

### 港澳工人集会庆祝“五一”节  同声称赞祖国建设伟大成就

1960-05-04
第4版()
专栏：

    港澳工人集会庆祝“五一”节
    同声称赞祖国建设伟大成就
    新华社香港2日电　香港和澳门的工人连日集会，热烈庆祝“五一”国际劳动节。
    香港各业工人1日下午一时举行庆祝“五一”的盛大集会，大会主席徐振材致开会词说：去年我国提前三年完成了第二个五年计划的主要指标，最近，第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议又通过了1960年国民经济发展计划，决定要争取提前两三年完成全国农业发展纲要，大搞城市人民公社，因此，今天庆祝“五一”国际劳动节，特别使我们兴奋鼓舞。港九工会联合会理事长李生也在会上讲了话。
    港九工会联合会在4月30日晚上举行了各工会代表茶叙会，庆祝节日。此外，六十多个各业工会也分别举行了茶叙会、餐会、游艺会、电影欣赏会和体育比赛等庆祝活动。
    澳门各业工人4月29日在工人康乐馆举行了庆祝大会。大会主席梁培在会上说，我国每一项建设成就，都给澳门工人以很大的鼓舞。澳门工会联合总会在1日举行了家乡图片展览和游艺晚会。
