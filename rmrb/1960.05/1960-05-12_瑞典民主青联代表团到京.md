Title: Swedish Democratic Youth League Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 11th. The four-member delegation of the Swedish Democratic Youth League, led by Rolf Utberg, Chairman of the Swedish Democratic Youth League, arrived in Beijing by plane today. They are visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation.

### 瑞典民主青联代表团到京

1960-05-12
第3版()
专栏：

    瑞典民主青联代表团到京
    新华社11日讯　瑞典民主青年联盟代表团一行四人，在团长、瑞典民主青年联盟主席鲁耳夫·乌特堡率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中华全国青年联合会的邀请来我国访问的。
