Title: The Flames of Struggle Against U.S. Imperialism and its Running Dogs Spread Throughout the Country: 2 Million People Demonstrate Simultaneously Across Japan; 90,000 Workers in Tokyo Demonstrate at the U.S. Embassy
Author:
Date: 1960-05-28
Page: 1

    The Flames of Struggle Against U.S. Imperialism and its Running Dogs Spread Throughout the Country
    2 Million People Demonstrate Simultaneously Across Japan
    90,000 Workers in Tokyo Demonstrate at the U.S. Embassy
    (Reported by our newspaper) Xinhua News Agency reported: Two million Japanese people held large-scale rallies and demonstrations simultaneously on the 26th in various parts of Japan, opposing the ratification of the new "Security Treaty" and demanding the immediate resignation of the Nobusuke Kishi cabinet and the dissolution of the Diet.
          (Detailed report on page three)
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, May 27th: Nearly 90,000 workers demonstrating in front of the Japanese Diet went to the U.S. Embassy at around 8 p.m. yesterday to hold a demonstration.
    The demonstrators included workers from the National Railway Workers' Union, the All-Japan Telecommunication Workers' Union, the All Monopoly Corporation Workers' Union, the Japan Federation of Steel Industry Workers' Unions, the Coal Miners' Union, and the Japan Federation of Newspaper Workers' Unions. The demonstrators also included members of the Socialist Party and the Communist Party, and members of various peace organizations.
    During the demonstration, they shouted: "Eisenhower, don't come to Japan!", "Yankee go home!", "Take back the Black Jets!", "Return Okinawa!".
    As the demonstrators passed through the nearly twenty layers of cordon set up by the Japanese police in front of the U.S. Embassy, they held an "S"-shaped demonstration. Many American military police wearing white helmets hid in the dark alleys outside the embassy. Then the 90,000 demonstrators held a street demonstration until the parade disbanded in an orderly manner at Shimbashi Station.

### 反对美帝国主义及其走狗的斗争烽火燃遍全国  日本各地二百万人同时示威  东京九万工人到美国大使馆示威

1960-05-28
第1版()
专栏：

    反对美帝国主义及其走狗的斗争烽火燃遍全国
    日本各地二百万人同时示威
    东京九万工人到美国大使馆示威
    本报讯　据新华社消息：日本各地共有二百万人民在26日同时举行声势浩大的大会和示威游行，反对批准新“安全条约”，要求岸信介内阁立即下台和解散国会。
          （详细报道见第三版）
    新华社27日讯　东京消息：在日本国会前示威的将近九万名工人在昨天晚上八点钟左右前往美国大使馆举行示威。
    示威者中包括国营铁道工会、全国电气通讯工会、全专卖工会、钢铁产业工会联合会、煤矿工会、新闻工作者联合会所属的工人。示威者中还有社会党和共产党的党员，各个和平团体的成员。
    他们在举行示威的时候高呼：“艾森豪威尔不要到日本来！”，“美国兵滚回去！”，“把黑色喷气机拿回去！”，“收回冲绳！”。
    当示威者通过由日本警察在美国大使馆前面布设的将近二十层的警戒线的时候，他们举行了“S”字形的示威游行。很多戴着白色钢帽的美国宪兵躲在大使馆外面的黑巷里。接着这九万名示威者又在大街上举行示威游行，直到新桥火车站游行队伍才秩序井然地解散。
