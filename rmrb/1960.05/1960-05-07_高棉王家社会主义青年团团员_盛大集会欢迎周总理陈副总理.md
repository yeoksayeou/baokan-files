Title: Members of the Khmer Royal Socialist Youth League Hold Grand Rally to Welcome Premier Zhou and Vice Premier Chen
Author:
Date: 1960-05-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 6th – Thousands of members of the Khmer Royal Socialist Youth League gathered this morning in the Royal Palace Square in Phnom Penh to participate in a grand rally to welcome Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi.

    Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi both spoke at the meeting. They encouraged the Cambodian youth to work hard to build their motherland and to maintain their country's national independence and policy of peace and neutrality. Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi assured them that their efforts could always count on the support of the Chinese youth.

    Representatives of the Khmer Royal Socialist Youth League said in their speeches that the contact between the youth of Cambodia and China would be a good demonstration of their shared desire for peace and progress.

    Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and other members of Premier Zhou Enlai's entourage also attended the welcoming rally. Also attending the rally were Prince Sihanouk, Prime Minister Pho Proeung, and other senior Cambodian officials. The Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia and the Cambodian Ambassador to China also attended the welcoming rally.

    After the speeches, members of the Khmer Royal Socialist Youth League performed group callisthenics for the distinguished Chinese guests.

    While the welcoming rally was being held, the streets around the Royal Palace Square were filled with citizens of Phnom Penh, who had arrived early to welcome Premier Zhou Enlai.

### 高棉王家社会主义青年团团员  盛大集会欢迎周总理陈副总理

1960-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

    高棉王家社会主义青年团团员
    盛大集会欢迎周总理陈副总理
    新华社金边6日电　高棉王家社会主义青年团的数以千计的团员今天上午会合在金边的王宫广场，参加为欢迎周恩来总理和陈毅副总理而举行的一次盛大的集会。
    周恩来总理和陈毅副总理都在会上讲了话。他们鼓励柬埔寨青年努力建设自己的祖国，并维护他们国家的民族独立和和平中立政策。周恩来总理和陈毅副总理向他们保证，他们的这种努力始终能指望得到中国青年的支持。
    高棉王家社会主义青年团的代表在讲话中说，柬埔寨和中国两国青年之间的接触将是他们一致希望和平和进步的良好证明。
    外交部副部长章汉夫和周恩来总理的其他随行人员也参加了这次欢迎大会。参加大会的还有西哈努克亲王、福·波伦首相和柬埔寨的其他高级官员。中国驻柬埔寨大使和柬埔寨驻中国大使也参加了欢迎大会。
    在讲话完毕后，高棉王家社会主义青年团的团员们为中国贵宾们表演了男女集体操。
    在举行欢迎大会时，王宫广场周围的街道上挤满了金边的市民，他们是一早就到这里来欢迎周恩来总理的。
