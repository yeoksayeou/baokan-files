Title: China Donates 10,000 Tons of Rice to Guinea
Author:
Date: 1960-05-06
Page: 6

    President Touré stated upon receiving the gift that China's assistance contributes to the common struggle of the two peoples against imperialism.
    Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, 4th - Chinese Ambassador to Guinea, Ke Hua, paid a courtesy call on the President of the Republic of Guinea, Sékou Touré, at the Presidential Palace on the afternoon of the 3rd.
    During the visit, Ambassador Ke Hua, on behalf of the government of the People's Republic of China, presented 10,000 tons of rice to the government of the Republic of Guinea, to express the friendship of the Chinese government and people to the Guinean government and people.
    President Sékou Touré accepted the gift on behalf of the Guinean government and people. He said that the Guinean government and people were honored by the assistance from the Chinese government and people, and expressed sincere gratitude. The President expressed his belief that this assistance would further contribute to strengthening the common struggle of the two peoples against imperialism.

### 我国赠送几内亚万吨大米

1960-05-06
第6版()
专栏：

    我国赠送几内亚万吨大米
  杜尔总统在接受礼物时表示，中国的援助有助于两国人民反对帝国主义的共同斗争
    新华社科纳克里4日电　中国驻几内亚大使柯华3日下午在总统府拜会了几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔。
    在拜会时，柯华大使以中华人民共和国政府的名义向几内亚共和国政府赠送一万吨大米，以表示中国政府和人民对几内亚政府和人民的友谊。
    塞古·杜尔总统代表几内亚政府和人民接受这份礼物。他说，几内亚政府和人民对中国政府和人民的援助感到荣幸，并且表示真诚的感谢。总统表示相信，这种援助将进一步有助于加强两国人民反对帝国主义的共同斗争。
