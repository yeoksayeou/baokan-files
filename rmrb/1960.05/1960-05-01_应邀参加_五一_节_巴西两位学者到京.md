Title: Brazilian Scholars Arrive in Beijing for May Day Celebrations

Author:

Date: 1960-05-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - At the invitation of the China Association for Science and Technology, Brazilian chemist Professor Enzio Cavalcanti and physicist Professor Mario Schemberg arrived in Beijing by plane today to participate in the "May Day" celebrations and visit China.

### 应邀参加“五一”节  巴西两位学者到京

1960-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    应邀参加“五一”节
    巴西两位学者到京
    新华社30日讯　应中华人民共和国科学技术协会邀请来我国参加“五一”庆祝活动和访问的巴西化学家恩兹曼·卡瓦尔康蒂教授、物理学家马里奥·谢姆堡教授，今天乘飞机到达北京。
