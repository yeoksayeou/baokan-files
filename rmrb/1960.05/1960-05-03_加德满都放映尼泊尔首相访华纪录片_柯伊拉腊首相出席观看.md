Title: Kathmandu Screens Documentary on Nepalese Prime Minister's Visit to China; Prime Minister Koirala Attends Screening
Author:
Date: 1960-05-03
Page: 6

    Kathmandu Screens Documentary on Nepalese Prime Minister's Visit to China
    Prime Minister Koirala Attends Screening
    Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, May 1st – A news documentary about Prime Minister Koirala's visit to China was screened in Kathmandu, the capital of Nepal, today. This film was presented to the Nepalese Prime Minister by Premier Zhou Enlai during his visit to Kathmandu.
    Prime Minister Koirala, along with Interior Minister Upretya and Transportation Minister Ganesh Man Singh, who accompanied him on his visit to China, the speakers of both houses of parliament, several ministers, and senior officials, attended the screening. Ye Cheng-zhang, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Nepal, accompanied them.
    Nepalese friends watched the film with great interest. They said that the film demonstrated the further strengthening of friendship between China and Nepal.

### 加德满都放映尼泊尔首相访华纪录片  柯伊拉腊首相出席观看

1960-05-03
第6版()
专栏：

    加德满都放映尼泊尔首相访华纪录片
    柯伊拉腊首相出席观看
    新华社加德满都1日电　尼泊尔首都加德满都今天放映了一部关于柯伊拉腊首相访问中国的新闻纪录片。这部影片是周恩来总理访问加德满都时送给尼泊尔首相的。
    柯伊拉腊首相以及曾经随同他访问过中国的内政大臣乌帕德亚亚和交通大臣加奈什曼·辛格，议会两院的议长，几位部长和高级官员都出席观看了这部影片。中国驻尼泊尔大使馆参赞叶成章陪同他们一起观看。
    尼泊尔朋友们以极大的兴趣观看了这部影片。他们说，这部影片表明了尼中两国友谊的进一步加强。
