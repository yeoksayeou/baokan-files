Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives the Albanian Minister of Health
Author:
Date: 1960-05-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, May 21st: Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Albanian Minister of Health, Taci Skendo, and his entourage this afternoon and had a cordial and friendly conversation.

Present at the reception were Li Dequan, Minister of Health; He Biao and Cui Yitian, Vice Ministers; Yu Zhan, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.

Also present at the reception was the Albanian Ambassador to China, Prifti.

### 陈毅副总理接见阿尔巴尼亚卫生部长

1960-05-22
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见阿尔巴尼亚卫生部长
    新华社21日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见了阿尔巴尼亚卫生部长塔齐·斯坎迪和他的随行人员，进行了亲切友好的谈话。
  接见时在座的，有卫生部部长李德全，副部长贺彪、崔义田，外交部苏联东欧司副司长余湛等。
    接见时，阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使帕里夫蒂也在座。
