Title: U.S. Aircraft Again Intrudes into Our Xisha Islands; I Issue 96th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-05-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 13th. On May 13th, from 11:20 to 11:26, a U.S. Navy patrol plane intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island and Shidao Island in our Xisha Islands, Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, a spokesperson for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 96th serious warning.

### 美国飞机又侵入我西沙群岛  我提出第96次严重警告

1960-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    美国飞机又侵入我西沙群岛
    我提出第96次严重警告
    新华社13日讯　5月13日十一时二十分至十一时二十六分，美国海军巡逻机一架侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛、石岛上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第九十六次严重警告。
