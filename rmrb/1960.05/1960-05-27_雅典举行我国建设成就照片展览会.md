Title: Photo Exhibition of China's Construction Achievements Held in Athens
Author:
Date: 1960-05-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 25th: A photo exhibition of the achievements of the People's Republic of China in the past decade, organized by the Greece-China Friendship Association, opened in Athens on the evening of the 24th.

Greek parliamentarians and prominent figures from all walks of life attended the opening ceremony. They showed great interest in China's achievements and expressed their belief that China would catch up with the most developed countries in the world before long. The Chinese cultural delegation, led by Zhang Zhixiang, vice president of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, who is currently visiting Athens, also attended the opening ceremony of the exhibition.

### 雅典举行我国建设成就照片展览会

1960-05-27
第4版()
专栏：

    雅典举行我国建设成就照片展览会
    新华社日内瓦25日电　雅典消息：希腊—中国友好协会举办的中华人民共和国十年来成就照片展览会，24日晚上在雅典开幕。
    出席展览会开幕式的有希腊议员和各界知名人士。他们对中国的成就表现出巨大的兴趣，并且表示相信中国不要很久将赶上世界上最发达的国家。正在雅典访问的、由中国人民对外文化协会副会长张致祥率领的中国文化代表团，也出席了展览会的开幕式。
