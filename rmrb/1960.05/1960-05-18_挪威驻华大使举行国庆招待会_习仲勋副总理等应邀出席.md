Title: Norwegian Ambassador to China Holds National Day Reception, Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun and Others Attend
Author:
Date: 1960-05-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 17th - The Ambassador of the Kingdom of Norway to China, Mr. Dons, held a reception at the embassy tonight to celebrate Norwegian National Day.

Vice Premier of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo, and others were invited to attend the reception. Also present at the reception were diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China.

### 挪威驻华大使举行国庆招待会  习仲勋副总理等应邀出席

1960-05-18
第5版()
专栏：

    挪威驻华大使举行国庆招待会
    习仲勋副总理等应邀出席
    新华社17日讯　挪威王国驻中国大使唐斯今晚在大使馆举行招待会，庆祝挪威国庆。
    国务院副总理习仲勋，外交部副部长罗贵波等应邀出席了招待会。出席招待会的还有各国驻中国的使节和外交官员。
