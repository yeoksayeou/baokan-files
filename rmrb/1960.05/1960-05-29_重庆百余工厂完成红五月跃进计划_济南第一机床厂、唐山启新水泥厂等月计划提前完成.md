Title: Over a Hundred Factories in Chongqing Complete Red May Leap Forward Plan; Jinan No. 1 Machine Tool Plant, Tangshan Qixin Cement Plant, and Others Fulfill Monthly Plans Ahead of Schedule
Author: 
Date: 1960-05-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, May 28th: As of the 26th, one hundred and seven factories in Chongqing had completed their Red May Leap Forward plans ahead of schedule.

    As soon as May began, workers in Chongqing factories put forward the ambitious slogan of "Turning Red May into a Leap Forward Month" and launched enthusiastic production campaigns, experience summaries, and exchange competitions, which continuously raised production levels. After Chongqing Crane Factory vigorously promoted mass movements and disseminated advanced casting and cutting experiences, it completed the entire month's plan six days ahead of schedule, exceeding it by 18%. At the beginning of May, Heibuzi Oil Refinery mobilized workers to engage in the comprehensive utilization of raw materials, thereby enabling the entire factory to complete the monthly plan twenty days ahead of schedule, exceeding it by 16.43%.

    Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, May 28th: Jinan No. 1 Machine Tool Plant has comprehensively completed its May production plan five days ahead of schedule. As of the 26th, the total output value and overall labor productivity have both fulfilled the monthly plan by 100%; product output and profits turned over to the state have completed 130.55% and 103.99% of the monthly plan, respectively; product costs have been reduced by 20% compared to the plan; and product quality has all reached national standards, while also ensuring safe production.

    Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, May 28th: Workers at the Tangshan Qixin Cement Plant completed the May national production plan four days and sixteen hours ahead of schedule on the 27th. Compared with April, the average daily cement output increased by 8.9%, and the quality also improved significantly.

    Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, May 27th: Tianjin No. 1 Steel Rolling Plant completed the May production plan ahead of schedule on the 25th. Compared with April, the plant's steel output increased by more than 33%.

    Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, May 27th: Tianjin Textile Machinery Factory comprehensively completed the May national production plan on the 25th. The total output value increased by 6% compared with April, product output increased by 3.5%, labor productivity increased by 6%, and costs decreased by 25% compared with the same period last year. ### 重庆百余工厂完成红五月跃进计划  济南第一机床厂、唐山启新水泥厂等月计划提前完成

1960-05-29
第5版()
专栏：

    重庆百余工厂完成红五月跃进计划
    济南第一机床厂、唐山启新水泥厂等月计划提前完成
    据新华社重庆28日电　重庆市到26日为止，已有一百零七个工厂提前完成了红5月的跃进计划。
    重庆市各厂职工一跨进5月份就提出“要把红5月变成飞跃月”的豪迈口号，并且纷纷展开热火朝天的生产大会战、经验大总结、大交流的竞赛活动，使生产水平不断上升。重庆起重机厂大搞群众运动，推广先进的铸造和切削经验后，提前六天超额18％完成了全月计划。黑不子炼油厂5月份一开始，就发动职工大搞原料材料综合利用，因而使全厂提前二十天超额16.43％完成了月计划。
    据新华社济南28日电　济南第一机床厂已提前五天全面完成5月份生产计划。到26日止，总产值、全员劳动生产率，都百分之百的完成月计划；产品产量和上缴利润，分别完成月计划的130.55％和103.99％；产品成本比计划降低20％；产品质量全部达到国家规定标准，并且做到了安全生产。
    据新华社天津28日电　唐山启新水泥厂职工在27日提前四天零十六小时，完成了5月份国家生产计划。与4月份比较，水泥平均日产量增长8.9％，质量也有显著的提高。
    据新华社天津27日电　天津市第一轧钢厂在25日提前完成了5月份生产计划。同4月份比较，全厂钢材产量提高33％以上。
    据新华社天津27日电　天津纺织机械厂已在25日全面完成了5月份国家生产计划。总产值比4月份提高6％，产品产量提高3.5％，劳动生产率提高6％，成本比去年同期降低25％。（附图片）
