Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Lays Wreath at Martyrs' Cemetery in Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1960-05-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 10th: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi, accompanied by Premier Pham Van Dong of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and others, laid a wreath at the Mai Dich Martyrs' Cemetery at 4:00 PM today (local time). The inscription on the wreath read: "Eternal Glory to the Revolutionary Martyrs of the Vietnamese People!" Also present at the Martyrs' Cemetery today were Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu and other accompanying personnel, as well as Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam He Wei. Accompanying them were also Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Ung Van Khiem, Vietnamese Ambassador to China Tran Tu Binh, and Chairman of the Hanoi Municipal Administrative Committee Tran Duy Hung, among others.

### 周总理在河内向烈士墓献花圈

1960-05-11
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理在河内向烈士墓献花圈
    新华社河内10日电　周恩来总理和陈毅副总理，由越南民主共和国范文同总理等陪同，在今天下午四时（当地时间）向梅役烈士墓献了花圈。在花圈上写着：“越南人民的革命烈士永垂不朽！”今天随同前往烈士墓的，有章汉夫副部长和其他随行人员，以及中国驻越南大使何伟。陪同前往的还有越南民主共和国外交部副部长雍文谦、越南驻中国大使陈子平和河内市行政委员会主席陈维兴等。
