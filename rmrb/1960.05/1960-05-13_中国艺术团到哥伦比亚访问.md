Title: Chinese Art Troupe Visits Colombia
Author:
Date: 1960-05-13
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Bogotá, May 11th: The entire Chinese Art Troupe has arrived in Colombia in batches after their visit and performances in Venezuela. The last group of the art troupe, led by head of the troupe Chen Zhongjing, arrived in Bogotá this afternoon and was warmly welcomed at Dorado Airport.

Congresswoman Ofelia Uribe, President of the Colombia-China Friendship Association, delivered a welcoming speech on behalf of the Colombia-China Friendship Association, saying, "The arrival of the Chinese Art Troupe will strengthen the friendly and cultural relations between the Chinese and Colombian people."

In his reply, Chen Zhongjing, head of the Chinese Art Troupe, expressed his gratitude to the Colombian people for their welcome to the art troupe and said he was honored to participate in the celebrations of the 150th anniversary of Colombia's independence.

### 中国艺术团到哥伦比亚访问

1960-05-13
第5版()
专栏：

    中国艺术团到哥伦比亚访问
    据新华社波哥大11日电　中国艺术团全体团员在委内瑞拉访问演出后已经先后到达哥伦比亚。艺术团最后一批人员今天下午在团长陈忠经率领下到达波哥大，在多拉多机场受到热烈的欢迎。
    哥伦比亚—中国友好协会主席女议员奥费莉亚·乌里维代表哥伦比亚—中国友好协会致欢迎词说，“中国艺术团的来到将加强中国人民和哥伦比亚人民之间的友好和文化关系”。
    中国艺术团团长陈忠经在致答词时对哥伦比亚人士给予艺术团的欢迎表示感谢，并对有机会参加庆祝哥伦比亚独立一百五十周年的活动表示荣幸。
