Title: German National Front Issues Appeal on Liberation Day: West German Militarism Must Be Fundamentally Eradicated
Author: 
Date: 1960-05-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, May 4th: TASS Berlin, May 3rd dispatch: May 8th marks the fifteenth anniversary of the German people's liberation from Hitler's fascist rule. To this end, the National Council of the National Front of the German Democratic Republic issued an appeal to the people.

The appeal first extended congratulations to the heroic people of the Soviet Union and the anti-Hitler alliance countries who defeated Hitler.

The appeal said that we deeply cherish the unbreakable friendship between the German and Soviet peoples. The Soviet people are helping us realize the ideals of the best sons and daughters of the German nation—peace, democracy, and socialism.

The appeal said that after the defeat of Hitler, under the leadership of the united working class, we established the German Democratic Republic—the first worker-peasant state in German history, the first peaceful Germany, the hope of the German nation, and the guarantee of a peaceful and happy future for the German nation.

The National Council of the National Front of the German Democratic Republic pointed out that the actions of the West German ruling clique threaten the security and peace of Europe and the survival of the German nation.

The appeal emphasized that solving the German question means fundamentally eradicating German militarism.

### 德全国阵线就解放日发出呼吁书  必须根本铲除西德军国主义

1960-05-05
第6版()
专栏：

    德全国阵线就解放日发出呼吁书
    必须根本铲除西德军国主义
    新华社柏林4日电　据塔斯社柏林3日讯：5月8日是德国人民自希特勒法西斯统治下获得解放的十五周年。为此，德意志民主共和国全国阵线全国委员会向人民发表呼吁书。
    呼吁书首先向击败希特勒的苏联和反希特勒同盟各国的人民英雄表示祝贺。
    呼吁书说，我们非常珍视德苏人民之间的牢不可破的友谊。苏联人民正在帮助我们实现德意志民族优秀儿女的理想——和平、民主和社会主义。
    呼吁书说，在击败希特勒之后，在团结一致的工人阶级的领导下，我们建立了德意志民主共和国——德国历史上的第一个工农国家，它是第一个和平的德国，它是德意志民族的希望，是德意志民族和平与幸福的未来的保证。
    德意志民主共和国全国阵线全国委员会指出，西德统治集团的行为威胁着欧洲的安全与和平，威胁着德意志民族的生存。
    呼吁书强调指出，解决德国问题就意味着根本铲除德国军国主义。
