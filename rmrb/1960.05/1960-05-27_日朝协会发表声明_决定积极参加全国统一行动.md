Title: Japan-China Friendship Association Issues Statement: Decides to Actively Participate in Nationwide Unified Action
Author:
Date: 1960-05-27
Page: 3

    Japan-China Friendship Association Issues Statement
    Decides to Actively Participate in Nationwide Unified Action
    The Japan-China Friendship Association issued a statement on the 24th, resolutely declaring that it will launch a struggle to prevent the final ratification of the new "Security Treaty," demanding that the Nobusuke Kishi cabinet immediately step down and dissolve the Diet. The statement also indicated that it will actively participate in the nationwide unified action led by the "National Conference to Prevent Revision of the Security Treaty."

### 日朝协会发表声明  决定积极参加全国统一行动

1960-05-27
第3版()
专栏：

    日朝协会发表声明
    决定积极参加全国统一行动
    日朝协会24日发表声明，坚决表示将展开斗争，阻止新“安全条约”的最后批准，要求岸信介内阁立即下台并解散国会。声明还表示，它将积极参加由“阻止修改安全条约国民会议”主持的全国统一行动。
