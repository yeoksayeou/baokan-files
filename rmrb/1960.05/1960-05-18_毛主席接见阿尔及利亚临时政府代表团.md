Title: Chairman Mao Receives Delegation from the Provisional Government of Algeria
Author:
Date: 1960-05-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, May 17th: Chairman Mao Zedong received the delegation from the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, headed by Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Krim Belkacem, here this afternoon and had a cordial conversation with them.

The members of the Algerian Provisional Government delegation who participated in the reception were: Minister of Armaments and Liaison, Boussouf Abdelhafid; Minister of Finance and Economic Affairs, Francis Ahmed; Deputy Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Belehouane Mabrouk; Advisors, Jawi Mohammed, Benabdelmoumen Ali, Rouchdi Halim, Hamidi Hassan Kamel, Hafid Karim; News Commissioner, Demaglatrous Rabbi; Attache of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ahamani Abdelaziz.

Also present at the reception were Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Li Qiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, He Ying, Director of the Department of West Asian and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 毛主席接见阿尔及利亚临时政府代表团

1960-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见阿尔及利亚临时政府代表团
    新华社武汉17日电　毛泽东主席今天下午在这里接见了以副总理兼外交部长克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆为首的 阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团，并进行了亲切的交谈。
    参加接见的阿尔及利亚临时政府代表团的成员有：军备和联络部长布苏夫·阿卜杜勒哈菲德，财政和经济事务部长弗朗西斯·艾哈迈德，外交部副秘书长贝勒侯赛因·马卜鲁克，顾问贾维·穆罕默德、本阿卜杜勒穆迈迈·阿里、鲁什迪·哈利姆、哈米迪·哈桑·卡迈勒、哈菲德·卡里姆，新闻专员德马格拉特鲁斯·拉比，外交部随员阿哈马尼·阿卜杜勒阿齐兹。
    陪同接见的有中共中央书记处候补书记杨尚昆，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，对外贸易部副部长李强，外交部西亚非洲司司长何英，外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文。
