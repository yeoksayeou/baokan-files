Title: Soviet Kazakh Institute of Physical Education Men's Basketball Team Holds Friendly Match with Hebei Province Men's Basketball Team
Author:
Date: 1960-05-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, May 18th: The Soviet Kazakh Institute of Physical Education men's basketball team held a friendly match with the Hebei Province men's basketball team in Tianjin on the evening of the 17th. The visiting team won with a score of 63 to 57.
　　
The visiting team will also play their second friendly match of their visit to Tianjin against the Nanjing Military Region men's basketball team on the 19th.

### 苏联哈萨克体育学院男子篮球队  同河北省男子篮球队举行友谊赛

1960-05-19
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联哈萨克体育学院男子篮球队
    同河北省男子篮球队举行友谊赛
    据新华社天津18日电　苏联哈萨克体育学院男子篮球队17日晚在天津与河北省男子篮球队进行友谊比赛，结果客队以六十三比五十七获胜。
    客队在19日还将与南京部队男子篮球队进行来津访问的第二场友谊比赛。
