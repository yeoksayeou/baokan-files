Title: Vietnamese Embassy in China Sends Letter of Gratitude to All Circles in China, Thanking Them for Congratulating President Ho Chi Minh on His Birthday
Author:
Date: 1960-05-30
Page: 7

The Vietnamese Embassy in China Sends a Letter to Our Country Expressing Gratitude for the Congratulations on President Ho Chi Minh's Birthday
This newspaper reports that the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China sent a letter to this newspaper expressing gratitude to the Chinese people for their congratulations on President Ho Chi Minh's 70th birthday. The letter is as follows:

During the celebration of President Ho Chi Minh's 70th birthday, workers, factories, mines, urban and rural people's communes, the Chinese People's Liberation Army, cadres, teachers and students of various universities and colleges, various organizations and Vietnamese nationals in various parts of China entrusted the Vietnamese Embassy to forward congratulatory telegrams, letters and various gifts to President Ho Chi Minh. The Vietnamese Embassy has forwarded all congratulatory letters, telegrams and gifts one by one. Now, on behalf of President Ho Chi Minh, the Vietnamese Embassy would like to express its most sincere gratitude to the above-mentioned congratulators.

                                    Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China

### 越南驻华大使馆来函向我国各界致意  感谢对胡志明主席寿辰的祝贺

1960-05-30
第7版()
专栏：

    越南驻华大使馆来函向我国各界致意
    感谢对胡志明主席寿辰的祝贺
    本报讯　越南民主共和国驻华大使馆致函本报表示感谢我国人民对胡志明主席七十寿辰的祝贺。来函如下：
    在庆贺胡志明主席七十大寿期间，中国各地工人、厂矿、城乡人民公社、中国人民解放军、干部、各院校师生、各机关团体和越南侨民等纷纷托越南大使馆将贺电贺信以及各种礼物转赠给胡志明主席。越南大使馆已经将所有的贺寿函电及礼物一一照转。现奉胡志明主席之命，越南大使馆谨向上述祝寿者致以最真诚的感谢。
                                    越南民主共和国驻华大使馆
