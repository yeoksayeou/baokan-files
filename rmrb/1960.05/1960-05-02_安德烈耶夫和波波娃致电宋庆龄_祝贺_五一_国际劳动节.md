Title: Andreyev and Popova Send Telegram to Soong Ching Ling to Congratulate "May Day" International Labor Day
Author:
Date: 1960-05-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, May 1st—Soong Ching Ling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, today received a telegram of congratulations on "May Day" International Labor Day from Andreyev, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, and Popova, Chairman of the Presidium of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. In their telegram, they congratulated the Chinese people on their new achievements in the cause of building socialism and congratulated the unbreakable friendship and brotherly cooperation between the Soviet and Chinese people on their increasing consolidation and development.

### 安德烈耶夫和波波娃致电宋庆龄  祝贺“五一”国际劳动节

1960-05-02
第3版()
专栏：

    安德烈耶夫和波波娃致电宋庆龄
    祝贺“五一”国际劳动节
    据新华社1日讯　中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄今天收到了苏联苏中友好协会理事会主席安德烈耶夫和苏联对外友好和文化联系协会联合会主席团主席波波娃发来的祝贺“五一”国际劳动节的电报。他们在电报中祝贺中国人民在建设社会主义的事业中获得新的成就，祝贺苏中两国人民牢不可破的友谊与兄弟般的合作日益巩固和发展。
