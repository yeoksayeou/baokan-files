Title: Hungarian-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-05-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 2nd. The five-member delegation of the Hungarian-Chinese Friendship Association, led by Pataki János, member of the Presidium of the Hungarian-Chinese Friendship Association, who came to China at the invitation of the Chinese-Hungarian Friendship Association to participate in the "Sino-Hungarian Friendship Week" activities, left Beijing by plane today to return to their country. Xiao San, Vice President of the Sino-Hungarian Friendship Association, Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, and others went to the airport to see them off. Szigeti, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Hungarian Embassy in China, also went to the airport to see them off.

During their stay in China, in addition to participating in the "Sino-Hungarian Friendship Week" activities, the delegation of the Hungarian-Chinese Friendship Association also paid friendly visits to Luoyang, Chengdu, Chongqing, Wuhan, Shanghai and other cities, and spent the "May Day" International Labor Day in China.

### 匈中友协代表团离京回国

1960-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

    匈中友协代表团离京回国
    新华社2日讯　应中国匈牙利友好协会邀请来我国参加“中匈友好周”活动的匈牙利中国友好协会代表团一行五人，在团长、匈中友协主席团委员帕塔基·亚诺什率领下，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。前往机场欢送的有中匈友协副会长萧三，对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥等。匈牙利驻华大使馆临时代办西格蒂也到机场送行。
    匈中友协代表团在我国期间，除参加了“中匈友好周”活动外，还到洛阳、成都、重庆、武汉、上海等城市作了友好访问，并在我国欢度了“五一”国际劳动节。
