Title: Turkish Army Launches Coup, Overthrows Menderes's Traitorous Government
Author:
Date: 1960-05-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Turkish army launched a coup on the morning of the 27th, overthrowing the traitorous Menderes government. The Turkish army has taken over military and civil administration throughout the country.

According to reports, Prime Minister Menderes and his Minister of Finance were arrested by the army in Kütahya and have been taken to the capital, Ankara. Also under military guard are the Turkish President, Speaker of the National Assembly, Minister of Defense, Minister of the Interior, Commander of the Turkish Ground Forces, other ministers, and provincial governors. ### 土耳其陆军发动政变  推翻曼德列斯卖国政府

1960-05-28
第1版()
专栏：

    土耳其陆军发动政变
    推翻曼德列斯卖国政府
    本报讯　据新华社消息：土耳其陆军在27日清晨发动政变，推翻了卖国的曼德列斯政府。土耳其军队已经接管了全国的军事和民政管理权。
    据报道，曼德列斯总理和他的财政部长一起在库塔耶城被军队逮捕并已押到首都安卡拉。被军队看管的还有土耳其总统、国民议会议长、国防部长、内政部长、土耳其地面部队司令、其他部长和各省省长。（详细报道见第四版）
