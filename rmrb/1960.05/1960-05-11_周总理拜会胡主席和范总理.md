Title: Premier Zhou Pays Respects to President Ho and Premier Pham
Author:
Date: 1960-05-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 10th: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice-Premier Chen Yi paid respects to President Ho Chi Minh and Premier Pham Van Dong this morning.

During the visit, President Ho Chi Minh, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and the entire Vietnamese people, extended the warmest welcome to Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice-Premier Chen Yi, and all members of Premier Zhou Enlai's entourage.

Premier Zhou Enlai conveyed the greetings of Comrade Mao Zedong, Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Comrade Zhu De, and other leading comrades of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party to President Ho Chi Minh and Premier Pham Van Dong, and paid tribute to President Ho Chi Minh and Premier Pham Van Dong on behalf of the Chinese government and people.

Present at the meeting on the Vietnamese side were: Le Duan, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Labor Party, Deputy Premiers Truong Chinh, Phan Ke Toai, Pham Hung, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly Ton Duc Thang, Vice Chairman Hoang Van Hoan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Ung Van Khiem, Nguyen Duc Duong, and Vietnamese Ambassador to China Tran Tu Binh.

Present on the Chinese side were: Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu and all members of Premier Zhou Enlai's entourage, as well as Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam He Wei.

### 周总理拜会胡主席和范总理

1960-05-11
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理拜会胡主席和范总理
    新华社河内10日电　周恩来总理和陈毅副总理今天上午拜会了胡志明主席和范文同总理。
    在拜会的时候，胡志明主席代表越南劳动党中央、越南民主共和国政府和全体越南人民，向周恩来总理、陈毅副总理和周恩来总理的全体随行人员表示最热烈的欢迎。
    周恩来总理向胡志明主席和范文同总理转达了毛泽东同志、刘少奇同志、朱德同志和中国共产党中央的其他领导同志的问候，并且代表中国政府和人民向胡志明主席和范文同总理致敬。
    拜会时，越南方面在座的有：劳动党中央政治局委员黎笋，副总理长征、潘继遂、范雄，国会常务委员会委员长孙德胜，副委员长黄文欢，外交部副部长雍文谦、阮德扬和越南驻中国大使陈子平。
    中国方面在座的有：章汉夫副部长和周恩来总理的全体随行人员，以及中国驻越南大使何伟。
