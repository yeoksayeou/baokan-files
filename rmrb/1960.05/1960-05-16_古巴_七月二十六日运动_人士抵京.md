Title: Cuban "July 26th Movement" Personnel Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-16
Page: 5

    Cuban "July 26th Movement" Personnel Arrive in Beijing
    (Xinhua News Agency, May 15th) At the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, a group of three people from the Cuban "July 26th Movement" arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to our country.
    They are Fernando López Muñío, Director of Secondary Education Department of the Cuban Ministry of Education; Fernando Luis, Mayor of Santiago de Cuba; and Manuel Susarte, Financial Secretary of the "July 26th Movement".
    Welcoming the Cuban guests at the airport were Wu Maosun, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General, and others.

### 古巴“七月二十六日运动”人士抵京

1960-05-16
第5版()
专栏：

    古巴“七月二十六日运动”人士抵京
    据新华社15日讯　应中国人民外交学会邀请前来我国访问的古巴“7月26日运动”人士一行三人，于今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    他们是，古巴教育部中等教育司司长费尔南多·洛佩斯·穆尼奥，古巴圣地亚哥市市长费尔南多·路易斯，“7月26日运动”财务秘书曼努埃尔·苏萨耳迪。
    在机场欢迎古巴客人们的有，中国人民外交学会秘书长吴茂荪、副秘书长吴晓达等。
