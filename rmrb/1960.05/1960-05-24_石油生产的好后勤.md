Title: Good Logistics for Petroleum Production
Author: 
Date: 1960-05-24
Page: 6

Good logistics for petroleum production,
Petroleum production leaps forward,
Commercial work serves as logistics:
Food and drink are supplied,
Also providing haircuts and mending clothes.
Petroleum production increases daily,
Commercial comrades deserve credit.
Workers of Dushanzi Refinery

### 石油生产的好后勤

1960-05-24
第6版()
专栏：

    石油生产的好后勤
    石油生产大跃进，
    商业工作当后勤：
    吃的喝的供应上，
    还给理发补衣裳。
    石油生产日日高，
    商业同志有功劳。
    独山子炼油厂工人
