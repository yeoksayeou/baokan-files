Title: Hu Yuzhi Hosts Banquet for Visiting Congolese Party Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1960-05-31
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 30th — Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the first group of members of the Congolese Party Delegation, led by Alphonse Makwambala, Chairman of the Political Bureau of the People's Party, and Arthur Mayamba, General Chairman of the Bayanzi Alliance.

In his speech, Hu Yuzhi expressed his respect for the heroic Congolese people who are fighting against imperialism and colonialism. He said, "We are very happy to know that the Congo will be independent on June 30. We warmly congratulate the Congo on its upcoming independence."

Hu Yuzhi said, "The Chinese people, who won their victory after a long struggle against imperialism and colonialism, regard the struggle of the Congolese people is their own struggle, and the victory of the Congolese people as their own victory."

Alphonse Makwambala, Chairman of the Political Bureau of the People's Party, pointed out in his speech that the era of imperialism has passed, "Although it wears all kinds of masks, it will still be seen through by the people." He said that the awakened Congolese people must fight to the end for the complete independence of their country.

Arthur Mayamba, General Chairman of the Bayanzi Alliance, said that the Congolese people regard the Chinese people as their brothers, and the fraternal friendship between the Congolese people and the Chinese people cannot be separated by any force.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests frequently toasted to the liberation of the Congo and the fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Congolese people.

Among those present were: Li Dequan, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity; Zhang Tiesheng, Vice President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association; Ding Xilin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; and Wang Yunsheng, Vice Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association. Mei Gongbin, Deputy Secretary-General of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, also attended the banquet.

### 胡愈之欢宴刚果政党访华代表

1960-05-31
第3版()
专栏：

    胡愈之欢宴刚果政党访华代表
    据新华社30日讯　中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之今晚设宴欢迎由人民党政治局主席阿方斯·马克万巴拉和巴阳齐联盟总主席阿图尔·玛扬巴率领的刚果政党访华代表团首批团员。
    胡愈之在会上致词，向正在进行反对帝国主义、反对殖民主义斗争的英勇的刚果人民表示敬意。他说：“我们非常高兴地知道刚果将在6月30日独立。我们热烈地预先祝贺刚果独立。”
    胡愈之说：“经过长期的反帝反殖民主义斗争才获得胜利的中国人民，把刚果人民的斗争看作是自己的斗争，把刚果人民的胜利看作是自己的胜利。”
    人民党政治局主席阿方斯·马克万巴拉在致词中指出，帝国主义的时代已经过去了，“尽管它戴着各式各样的假面具，它也还是要为人民识破的”。他说，觉醒了的刚果人民，一定要为争取本国的完全独立而斗争到底。
    巴阳齐联盟总主席阿图尔·玛扬巴说，刚果人民把中国人民看作是他们的兄弟，刚果人民和中国人民的兄弟友谊是任何力量也分不开的。
    席间，宾主频频为刚果的解放和中国刚果两国人民间的兄弟友谊而干杯。
    出席作陪的有：中国亚非团结委员会副主席李德全，中国非洲人民友好协会副会长张铁生，中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林，中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席王芸生等。中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副秘书长梅龚彬也出席了宴会。
