Title: Celebrating the 15th Anniversary of the Liberation of Czechoslovakia: Shenyang No. 2 Machine Tool Plant Named "Sino-Czech Friendship Factory"; Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun and Ambassador Bušniak Praised the Further Development of Sino-Czech Friendship
Author:
Date: 1960-05-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, May 4th.  Vice Premier of the State Council Xi Zhongxun officially named the Shenyang No. 2 Machine Tool Plant as the "Sino-Czech Friendship Factory" at a grand meeting held this morning in the Shenyang No. 2 Machine Tool Plant to celebrate the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia and the naming ceremony of the Sino-Czech Friendship Factory.

Czechoslovak Ambassador to China Bušniak and all members of the Czechoslovak friendship delegation headed by Obafsky arrived in Shenyang from Beijing yesterday on the same flight as Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun to attend the ceremony.  Also attending the meeting from Beijing were Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building Wang Daohan, President of the Sino-Czech Friendship Association Xia Yan, and Deputy Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Yu Zhan, among others.  Huang Huoqing, First Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and other leaders of the Liaoning Province and Shenyang City Party and government, as well as leaders of various people's organizations, attended the meeting. Czechoslovak experts working in Shenyang also attended the meeting.

The naming ceremony was presided over by Liu Baotian, Deputy Governor of Liaoning Province and Mayor of Shenyang.

In his speech, Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun first announced the naming of the Shenyang No. 2 Machine Tool Plant as the Sino-Czech Friendship Factory. In his speech, Xi Zhongxun warmly praised the brilliant achievements made by the Czechoslovak people in all aspects of socialist construction under the correct leadership of the Czechoslovak Communist Party over the past fifteen years, and expressed the warmest congratulations to the Czechoslovak government on behalf of the Chinese government and people. He especially praised the advanced level of Czechoslovakia's machine industry and the Kovost factory named "Czech-Chinese Friendship Factory". Xi Zhongxun expressed his firm belief that the "Sino-Czech Friendship People's Commune" named in our country in the past, as well as the "Czech-Chinese Friendship Agricultural Cooperative", the "Czech-Chinese Friendship Factory" and today's "Sino-Czech Friendship Factory" named in Czechoslovakia, will make contributions to further consolidating and developing the friendly and cooperative relations between China and Czechoslovakia.

Ambassador Bušniak then spoke. He believed that the People's Republic of China named one of its important factories as the "Sino-Czech Friendship Factory," proving how important the Chinese people place Sino-Czech friendship. He hoped that the Sino-Czech Friendship Factory would better complete its national plan and that all Chinese people would live better and better lives.

The speeches of Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun and Ambassador Bušniak were interrupted by warm applause from time to time. Subsequently, Novotny, a member of the Czechoslovak Friendship Delegation and the director of the "Czech-Chinese Friendship Factory", and Zou Jiahua, the acting director of our country's "Sino-Czech Friendship Factory", spoke successively. They both expressed on behalf of the factory workers that they would contribute to the strong unity of Sino-Czech friendship and the socialist camp.

Ambassador Bušniak, the director of the Czech-Chinese Friendship Factory, and the acting director of the Sino-Czech Friendship Factory also exchanged gifts at the ceremony. When the directors of the two friendship factories warmly embraced, the workers of the entire factory cheered and the sound lasted for a long time. Afterwards, the distinguished guests visited some workshops of the Sino-Czech Friendship Factory and watched the workers' technical performances.

In the evening, Huang Huoqing, First Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, held a banquet to celebrate the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia and the naming ceremony of the Sino-Czech Friendship Factory. The banquet was filled with a fraternal and friendly atmosphere.

### 庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放十五周年  沈阳第二机床厂命名“中捷友谊工厂”  习仲勋副总理和布希尼亚克大使盛赞中捷友谊进一步发展

1960-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放十五周年
    沈阳第二机床厂命名“中捷友谊工厂”
    习仲勋副总理和布希尼亚克大使盛赞中捷友谊进一步发展
    据新华社沈阳4日电　国务院副总理习仲勋今天上午在沈阳第二机床厂举行的庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放十五周年、中捷友谊工厂命名典礼大会上，正式命名沈阳第二机床厂为“中捷友谊工厂”。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使布希尼亚克、以奥巴夫斯基为首的捷克斯洛伐克友好代表团全体成员，昨天和习仲勋副总理同机从北京来到沈阳参加典礼。从北京前来参加大会的还有第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵、中捷友好协会会长夏衍和外交部苏联东欧司副司长余湛等人。中共辽宁省委第一书记黄火青等辽宁省和沈阳市党和政府负责人与各人民团体的负责人出席了大会。出席大会的还有在沈阳帮助工作的捷克斯洛伐克的专家们。
    命名典礼由辽宁省副省长、沈阳市长刘宝田主持。
    习仲勋副总理在讲话时首先宣布把沈阳第二机床厂命名为中捷友谊工厂。习仲勋在讲话中热烈地赞扬了捷克斯洛伐克人民十五年来在捷克斯洛伐克共产党的正确领导下，在社会主义建设各个方面所创造的辉煌成就，并代表中国政府和人民向捷克斯洛伐克政府表示最热烈的祝贺。他特别赞扬了捷克斯洛伐克的机械工业和以“捷中友谊工厂”命名的科沃斯特工厂的先进水平。习仲勋表示深信，过去在我国命名的“中捷友谊人民公社”和在捷克斯洛伐克命名的“捷中友谊农业合作社”、“捷中友谊工厂”以及今天命名的“中捷友谊工厂”，将在进一步巩固和发展中捷两国友谊和合作关系中作出贡献。
    布希尼亚克大使接着讲话。他认为，中华人民共和国把重要的工厂之一命名为“中捷友谊工厂”，证明了中国人民把捷中友谊放在多么重要的地位。他希望中捷友谊工厂更好地完成国家计划，并且希望全体中国人民生活得越来越美好。
    习仲勋副总理和布希尼亚克大使的讲话，不时为热烈的掌声所打断。随后，捷克斯洛伐克友好代表团团员、“捷中友谊工厂”厂长诺沃提尼和我国“中捷友谊工厂”代厂长邹家华先后讲话。他们都代表本厂职工表示要为中捷友谊和社会主义阵营的坚强团结贡献力量。
    布希尼亚克大使、捷中友谊工厂厂长和中捷友谊工厂代厂长还在典礼上互相赠送礼物。当两个友谊厂的厂长热烈拥抱时，全厂工人欢声雷动，经久不息。以后，贵宾们参观了中捷友谊工厂的一些车间，并观看了工人的技术表演。
    傍晚，中共辽宁省委第一书记黄火青举行宴会，庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放十五周年和中捷友谊工厂举行命名典礼。宴会上充满了兄弟般的友好气氛。
