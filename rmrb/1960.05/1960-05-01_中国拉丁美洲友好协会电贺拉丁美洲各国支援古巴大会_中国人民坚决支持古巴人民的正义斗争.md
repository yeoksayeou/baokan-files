Title: China-Latin America Friendship Association Cables Congratulations to Latin American Countries' Support Cuba Conference; Chinese People Firmly Support the Just Struggle of the Cuban People
Author:
Date: 1960-05-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 30th -- The China-Latin America Friendship Association yesterday sent a cable of congratulations to the Latin American Countries' Support Cuba Conference held on April 30th in Havana, the capital of Cuba.
	The telegram said, "The Chinese people firmly stand together with the Latin American people and support the just struggle of the Cuban Revolutionary Government and the heroic Cuban people to safeguard national independence and oppose the aggression of US imperialism. Victory surely belongs to the Cuban people.”

### 中国拉丁美洲友好协会电贺拉丁美洲各国支援古巴大会  中国人民坚决支持古巴人民的正义斗争

1960-05-01
第5版()
专栏：

    中国拉丁美洲友好协会电贺拉丁美洲各国支援古巴大会
    中国人民坚决支持古巴人民的正义斗争
    新华社30日讯　中国拉丁美洲友好协会昨天致电祝贺4月30日在古巴首都哈瓦那举行的拉丁美洲各国支援古巴大会。
  电报说，“中国人民坚决和拉丁美洲人民站在一起，支持古巴革命政府和英雄的古巴人民为维护民族独立反对美帝国主义侵略而进行的正义斗争。胜利一定属于古巴人民。”
