Title: Soviet Scientific Achievements Strengthen the Forces of Peace; Soong Ching Ling and Guo Moruo Send Telegrams Congratulating the Soviet Union on the Successful Launch of a Spaceship

Author:
Date: 1960-05-17
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 16th May. Soong Ching Ling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, sent a telegram to Popova, Chairman of the Presidium of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Andreyev, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, congratulating the Soviet Union on the successful launch of a satellite spaceship. The telegram reads as follows:

Dear Comrade Popova: Dear Comrade Andreyev:

We are delighted to learn that the Soviet Union successfully launched a satellite spaceship on May 15. This successful launch of a satellite spaceship is another new and significant achievement made by the Soviet Union in the great undertaking of conquering outer space, following the launch of three artificial Earth satellites, three cosmic rockets, and two multi-stage ballistic rockets. It shows that the day when mankind boards a spaceship is greatly approaching. On behalf of all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, I extend our heartfelt congratulations to you, and through you, to the great Soviet people, the outstanding Soviet workers, designers, and scientists.

　　　　　　　　　　President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Soong Ching Ling
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　May 16, 1960

Xinhua News Agency, 16th May. Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, sent a telegram to Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, congratulating the Soviet Union on the successful launch of a satellite spaceship. The telegram reads as follows:

Academician Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences:

I am delighted to learn that the Soviet Union successfully launched a satellite spaceship on May 15. On behalf of the Chinese scientific community, I extend my sincerest congratulations to you and, through you, to the Soviet scientific community.

The Soviet Union's launch of this 4.5-ton spaceship demonstrates that the Soviet Union is not far from realizing human flight in space; this fact once again proves that the Soviet Union has left the capitalist countries far behind in rocket technology and space travel.

The Soviet Union is committed to the scientific cause of benefiting mankind and has made increasingly great achievements, once again demonstrating the unparalleled superiority of the socialist system.

Every achievement of the Soviet Union in science and technology strengthens the socialist camp and the cause of world peace, and is a powerful encouragement to peace-loving people all over the world. We believe that in the near future, the Soviet Union will certainly make even greater scientific achievements for mankind.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Guo Moruo
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　May 16, Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, 16th May. Li Siguang, Chairman of the Scientific and Technological Association of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram to Semenov, Chairman of the Board of the All-Union Society for Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge, Golikov, Chairman of the Trade Union of Workers in Higher Education and Scientific Institutions of the Soviet Union, and Shiryanov, Chairman of the All-Union Scientific and Technological Association, congratulating the Soviet Union on the successful launch of a satellite spaceship. The telegram reads as follows:

Dear Comrades:

We are delighted to learn that the Soviet Union successfully launched a satellite spaceship into Earth orbit on May 15, 1960. Please allow me, on behalf of all members of the Scientific and Technological Association of the People's Republic of China, to extend our warm congratulations to you and, through you, to the Soviet scientists, engineers, and all the Soviet people.

The successful launch of this satellite spaceship by the Soviet Union is another great contribution made by Soviet scientists, engineers, and the Soviet people, under the leadership of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet government, to the great cause of conquering outer space for the noble purpose of human peace and progress, bringing mankind a big step closer to entering the era of spaceflight. We wish you even greater success.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　Chairman of the Scientific and Technological Association of the People's Republic of China, Li Siguang
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　May 16, 1960

### 苏联科学成就加强了和平力量  宋庆龄和郭沫若等电贺苏联发射宇宙飞船成功

1960-05-17
第7版()
专栏：

    苏联科学成就加强了和平力量
    宋庆龄和郭沫若等电贺苏联发射宇宙飞船成功
    新华社16日讯　中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄致电苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会理事会主席波波娃、苏中友好协会理事会主席安德烈耶夫，祝贺苏联成功地发射了卫星式宇宙飞船。电文如下：亲爱的波波娃同志：亲爱的安德烈耶夫同志：
    欣悉苏联于5月15日成功地发射了一个卫星式宇宙飞船。这一卫星式宇宙飞船的成功发射，是苏联继发射三个人造地球卫星、三个宇宙火箭和两个多级弹道火箭之后，在征服宇宙空间的伟大事业中所取得的又一次新的巨大的成就，它说明人类登上宇宙飞船的日子大大临近了。谨代表中苏友好协会全体会员，向您们，并通过您们，向伟大的苏联人民、杰出的苏联工人、设计师、科学家们，表示衷心的祝贺。
                          中苏友好协会总会会长　宋庆龄
                                              1960年5月16日
    新华社16日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若致电苏联科学院院长?斯米扬诺夫，祝贺苏联成功地发射了卫星式宇宙飞船，电文如下：苏联科学院院长?斯米扬诺夫院士：
    欣闻苏联在5月15日成功地发射了卫星式宇宙飞船，我谨代表中国科学界向您并通过您向苏联科学界致以最诚挚的庆贺。
    苏联这次发射重达四吨半的宇宙飞船，说明苏联实现人在宇宙间飞行已为期不远；这个事实再一次证明苏联在火箭技术和宇宙航行方面已经把资本主义国家抛在更遥远的后面了。
    苏联致力于造福人类的科学事业，作出了越来越伟大的成就，再一次显示了社会主义制度无比的优越性。
    苏联科学技术上的每一项成就，都为社会主义阵营、为世界和平事业增加了力量，对全世界爱好和平的人民是有力的鼓舞。我们相信在不久的将来，苏联一定能为人类作出更伟大的科学成就。
                                中国科学院院长
                                                  郭沫若
                                             5月16日于北京
    新华社16日讯　中华人民共和国科学技术协会主席李四光致电苏联政治和科学知识普及协会理事会主席谢苗诺夫、苏联高等学校和科学机关工作者工会主席戈里夫柯夫、全苏科学技术协会主席西鲁扬诺夫，庆贺苏联成功地发射了卫星式宇宙飞船。电文如下：亲爱的同志们：
    欣悉苏联于1960年5月15日向地球卫星轨道成功地发射了卫星式宇宙飞船，请允许我代表中华人民共和国科学技术协会全体会员向您们并通过您们向苏联科学家、工程师和苏联全体人民致以热烈的祝贺。
    苏联这次发射卫星式宇宙飞船的成功是苏联科学家、工程师和苏联人民在苏联共产党和苏联政府的领导下，为了人类和平、进步的崇高目的，在征服宇宙空间的伟大事业中又作出了一次巨大的贡献，使人类进入宇宙飞行的时代又提前了一大步。预祝您们获得更大成功。
                    中华人民共和国科学技术协会主席　李四光
                                             1960年5月16日
