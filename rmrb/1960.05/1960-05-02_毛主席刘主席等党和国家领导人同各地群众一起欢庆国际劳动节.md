Title: Chairman Mao, Chairman Liu and Other Party and State Leaders Celebrate International Labor Day with People from All Over the Country
Author:
Date: 1960-05-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 1st. Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yun, and Vice Premier Chen Yi, today celebrated "May Day" International Labor Day with the broad masses in Tianjin, Chengdu, Guiyang, Shanghai, and Kunming respectively.

    Chairman Mao Zedong attended the mass rally celebrating the festival in the central square of Tianjin. Fifty thousand people including workers, peasants, urban people's commune members, students, representatives of the armed forces and people from all walks of life in Tianjin participated in the rally. The masses participating in the rally enthusiastically cheered "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Long live the Chinese Communist Party!"

    Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Part of China and Chairman of the People's Republic of China, participated in the celebration rally held by people from all walks of life in Sichuan Province and Chengdu, and had cordial conversations with advanced figures from various fronts who participated in the rally.

    Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Part of China and Premier of the State Council, participated in the celebration rally at NanChang Square, the central venue for the "May Day" festival gathering in Guiyang.

    Chen Yun, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Part of China and Vice Premier of the State Council, went to the Shanghai No. 1 Steel Plant to participate in the celebration meeting held by Shanghai steel workers and people from all walks of life in the Wusong District.

    Chen Yi, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Premier of the State Council, participated in the celebration rally in Kunming.

    Chairman Mao and other Party and state leaders participated in "May Day" celebration activities in various places, which greatly encouraged the local masses. The masses in various places expressed that they would create greater achievements on all fronts, strive to complete half of the annual plan in the first half of the year, and make greater contributions to the socialist construction of the motherland. ### 毛主席刘主席等党和国家领导人同各地群众一起欢庆国际劳动节

1960-05-02
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席刘主席等党和国家领导人
    同各地群众一起欢庆国际劳动节
    新华社1日讯　毛泽东主席、刘少奇主席、周恩来总理、陈云副总理、陈毅副总理，今天分别在天津、成都、贵阳、上海、昆明同广大群众一起度过了“五一”国际劳动节。
    毛泽东主席在天津出席了在中心广场举行的庆祝节日的群众大会。天津市的工人、农民、城市人民公社社员、学生、部队代表以及各界人民共五万人参加了大会。参加大会的群众热烈欢呼
  “毛主席万岁！”“中国共产党万岁！”。
    中共中央副主席、中华人民共和国主席刘少奇在成都参加了四川省和成都市各界人民举行的庆祝大会，并且和参加大会的各个战线的先进人物进行了亲切的谈话。
    中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来在贵阳庆祝“五一”节集会的中心会场——南厂广场参加了庆祝大会。
    中共中央副主席、国务院副总理陈云在上海到上钢一厂参加了上海钢铁工人和吴淞区各界人民举行的庆祝会。
    中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅在昆明参加了庆祝大会。
    毛主席等党和国家领导人在各地参加“五一”节庆祝活动，使当地群众受到极大鼓舞，各地群众纷纷表示要在各个战线上创造更大的成绩，力争上半年完成全年计划的一半，为祖国的社会主义建设作出更大贡献。（附图片）
