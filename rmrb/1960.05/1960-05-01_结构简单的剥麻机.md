Title: Simple Structure Hemp Stripping Machine
Author:
Date: 1960-05-01
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency report:

    The farm tool factory of Yangxin County, Hubei Province—a major ramie producing area—has successfully created a 59-121 model powered hemp stripping machine with a simple structure. Using this hemp stripping machine to strip hemp is more than ten times more efficient than manual stripping.
    This powered hemp stripping machine consists of four main components. Its core part is two pairs of roller-type stripping wheels and an eccentric shaft. After fresh hemp stalks are fed from the hopper, the stripping wheels crush the hemp stalks, remove the hemp skin, and the white hemp is taken out.

### 结构简单的剥麻机

1960-05-01
第7版()
专栏：

    结构简单的剥麻机
    湖北省主要苎麻产区——阳新县的农具厂，创造成功了一种结构简单的五九—一二一型动力剥麻机。用这种剥麻机剥麻，比手工剥麻效率提高十倍以上。
    这种动力剥麻机由四个主要部件构成。其核心部分为两对滚筒式的剥麻轮和偏心轴，鲜麻秆从进料斗喂进后，剥麻轮就碎烂麻秆，除去麻皮，取出即成白麻。　（据新华社讯）
