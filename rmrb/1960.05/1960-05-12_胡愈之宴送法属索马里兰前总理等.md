Title: Hu Yuzhi Hosts Farewell Banquet for Former Prime Minister of French Somaliland and Others

Author: 
Date: 1960-05-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, hosted a banquet on the 10th to bid farewell to Mohamed Harbi, former Prime Minister of French Somaliland and Chairman of the Democratic Union, and Ali Abdillahi, Secretary-General, who were visiting China. Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, and Bao Erhan, Vice President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, attended the banquet today. The guests have concluded their visit to China and will leave our country on the 11th.

### 胡愈之宴送法属索马里兰前总理等

1960-05-12
第3版()
专栏：

    胡愈之宴送法属索马里兰前总理等
    新华社11日讯　中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之10日设宴欢送在我国访问的法属索马里兰前总理、民主联盟主席穆罕默德·哈比和总书记阿里·阿卜迪拉西。参加今天宴会的有中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志，中国非洲人民友好协会副会长包尔汉等。客人们已结束在我国的访问，将于11日离开我国。
