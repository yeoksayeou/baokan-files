Title: Xiao San and Chinese Journalists Delegation Arrive in Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1960-05-16
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 14th - Chinese writer Xiao San arrived in Hanoi by train on the afternoon of May 14th. He is visiting Vietnam under the 1960 Sino-Vietnamese cultural cooperation plan.

Nguyen Cong Hoan, head of the Vietnamese Writers Association, and others welcomed him at the station.

The Chinese journalists delegation, consisting of three people led by the head of the delegation Yang Dan, arrived in Hanoi on the 13th, also under the cultural cooperation plan.

### 萧三和我新闻工作者代表团到河内

1960-05-16
第5版()
专栏：

    萧三和我新闻工作者代表团到河内
    新华社河内14日电　中国作家萧三14日下午乘火车到达河内。他是根据1960年中越两国文化合作计划前来越南进行访问的。
    到车站欢迎的有越南作家协会负责人阮公欢等。
    根据这项文化合作计划前来越南访问的中国新闻工作者代表团一行三人在团长杨丹率领下已于13日到达河内。
