Title: Sukarno Arrives in Tokyo
Author:
Date: 1960-05-25
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, May 24th: Indonesian President Sukarno arrived in Tokyo today after visiting the United States. He is the first foreign president to visit Japan after Nobusuke Kishi used violence to force the Lower House to pass the US-Japan military alliance treaty.

    The news said that Sukarno will stay in Japan for ten days as Nobusuke Kishi's "personal guest."

### 苏加诺到东京

1960-05-25
第4版()
专栏：

    苏加诺到东京
    新华社24日讯　东京消息：印度尼西亚总统苏加诺在访问美国后，今天到达东京。他是岸信介使用暴力强使下议院通过美日军事同盟条约以后到日本访问的第一个外国总统。
    消息说，苏加诺将作为岸信介的“私人客人”，在日本逗留十天。
