Title: Australian-Chinese Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, May 9th. The Australian-Chinese Association delegation, consisting of three members, arrived in Beijing by train on the 8th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

    Zhu Boshen, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and others were present at the station to welcome them.

### 澳中协会代表团到京

1960-05-10
第6版()
专栏：

    澳中协会代表团到京
    新华社9日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国访问的澳中协会代表团一行三人，8日乘火车到达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有中国人民对外文化协会副秘书长朱伯深等。
