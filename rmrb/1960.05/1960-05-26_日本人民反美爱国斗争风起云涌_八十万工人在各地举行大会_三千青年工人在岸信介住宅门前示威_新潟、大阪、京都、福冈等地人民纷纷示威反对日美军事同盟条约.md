Title: Japanese People's Anti-American Patriotic Struggle Rises Vigorously; 800,000 Workers Hold Rallies in Various Places; 3,000 Young Workers Demonstrate in Front of Nobusuke Kishi's Residence; People in Niigata, Osaka, Kyoto, Fukuoka, and Other Places Demonstrate Against the Japan-U.S. Military Alliance Treaty
Author: 
Date: 1960-05-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Japanese people are continuing to launch vigorous actions to oppose the Kishi Nobusuke clique's illegal "passage" of the Japan-U.S. military alliance treaty.

On the 25th, 800,000 workers belonging to the All-Japan Council of Trade Unions held workplace rallies throughout the country, demanding the resignation of the Kishi Nobusuke Cabinet and the dissolution of the Diet.

The General Council of Trade Unions of Japan also decided at a representatives' meeting held on the afternoon of the 24th to hold a nationwide general strike on June 4th.

On the evening of the 25th, more than 3,000 young workers in Tokyo held a rally in Miyamoto Park, demanding the abolition of the new Japan-U.S. "Security Treaty" and passing a resolution opposing Eisenhower's visit to Japan. After the meeting, the workers held a demonstration in front of Nobusuke Kishi's private residence for more than an hour and a half. They shouted: "We do not recognize the 'Security' Treaty!" "Kishi Nobusuke, get out of Japan!" "Dissolve the Diet now!"

From the 20th to the 24th, the people of Niigata, Osaka, Kyoto, Kanagawa, Fukuoka, Kumamoto, Tokushima, and other places also held large-scale demonstrations and parades against the Japan-U.S. military alliance treaty. On the 24th, Niigata Prefecture had 120,000 workers and citizens holding protest rallies in 27 locations throughout the prefecture.

### 日本人民反美爱国斗争风起云涌  八十万工人在各地举行大会　三千青年工人在岸信介住宅门前示威  新潟、大阪、京都、福冈等地人民纷纷示威反对日美军事同盟条约

1960-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

    日本人民反美爱国斗争风起云涌
    八十万工人在各地举行大会　三千青年工人在岸信介住宅门前示威
    新潟、大阪、京都、福冈等地人民纷纷示威反对日美军事同盟条约
    本报讯　据新华社消息：日本人民继续展开强有力的行动，反对岸信介集团非法“通过”日美军事同盟条约。
    25日，全日本工会会议所属的八十万工人在全国各地举行工作场所大会，要求岸信介内阁总辞职和解散国会。
    日本工会总评议会在24日下午举行的代表会议也决定在6月4日举行全国总罢工。
    25日晚上，东京三千多名青年工人在宫本公园举行大会，要求废除新日美“安全条约”，并且通过了反对艾森豪威尔访问日本的决议。会后，工人们在岸信介私人住宅门前举行了一小时半以上的示威。他们高呼：“我们不承认‘安全’条约！”“岸信介滚出日本去！”“现在就解散国会！”
    从20日到24日，新潟、大阪、京都、神奈川、福冈、熊本、德岛等地的人民，也举行了反对日美军事同盟条约的大规模示威游行。新潟县24日就有十二万名工人和市民在全县二十七个地方举行了抗议大会。
