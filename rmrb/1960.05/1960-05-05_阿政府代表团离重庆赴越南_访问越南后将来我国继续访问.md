Title: Algerian Government Delegation Leaves Chongqing for Vietnam; Will Visit China Again After Visiting Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1960-05-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 4th. The Algerian Republic Provisional Government delegation, headed by Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Krim Belkacem, and accompanied by Vice Foreign Minister Ji Pengfei, arrived in Chongqing yesterday by plane from Beijing via Xi'an.

Shaanxi Province Vice Governor Li Qiming and Xi'an Mayor Liu Geng welcomed and saw off the delegation at the airport upon their arrival and departure from Xi'an.

The delegation was warmly welcomed by the responsible persons of the local People's Committee upon arrival in Chongqing, and in the evening attended a welcome banquet given by the Municipal People's Committee and enjoyed Sichuan opera.

The delegation left Chongqing this morning to visit the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. After visiting the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the delegation will continue its visit to our country.

### 阿政府代表团离重庆赴越南  访问越南后将来我国继续访问

1960-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

    阿政府代表团离重庆赴越南
    访问越南后将来我国继续访问
    新华社重庆4日电　以克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆副总理兼外交部长为首的阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团，在外交部副部长姬鹏飞陪同下，于昨天由北京乘飞机经西安到达重庆。
    陕西省副省长李启明，西安市市长刘庚在代表团到达和离开西安的时候，都到机场欢迎和欢送。
    代表团到达重庆的时候，受到当地人民委员会负责人的热烈欢迎，当晚还出席了市人民委员会举行的欢迎宴会，并欣赏了川剧。
    代表团已经在今天上午离开重庆，前往越南民主共和国访问。代表团在访问越南民主共和国以后，仍将到我国继续进行访问。
