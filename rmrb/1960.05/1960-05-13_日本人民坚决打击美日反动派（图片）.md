Title: The Japanese People Resolutely Strike at the US-Japanese Reactionaries (Photo)
Author:
Date: 1960-05-13

Japanese People Resolutely Strike at the US-Japanese Reactionaries (Photo)
    Top: The demonstration procession from all walks of life in Tokyo carries grotesque models of Nobusuke Kishi, parading in front of the Japanese Diet.
    Left: On May 12, 4.6 million Japanese workers held a general strike to oppose the US-Japan treaty.
    Bottom: On April 26, various regions in Japan launched the fifteenth unified action to prevent the ratification of the new US-Japan "Security Treaty." This is a scene of students fighting with the police on the streets of Tokyo.
  Top: A marching crowd from Nagano Prefecture, holding a large banner with the slogan "Down with the Kishi Nobusuke Government," demonstrates on the streets of Tokyo.　　　　Xinhua News Agency draft

### 日本人民坚决打击美日反动派（图片）

1960-05-13
第3版()
专栏：

    日本人民坚决打击美日反动派（图片）
    上：东京各界的示威游行队伍，抬着丑态百出的岸信介的模型在日本国会前行进。
    左：5月12日，日本四百六十万工人举行大罢工反对日美条约。
    下：4月26日，日本各地展开了阻止批准新日美“安全条约”的第十五次统一行动。这是学生们在东京街道上和警察进行斗争。
  上：来自长野县的群众游行队伍，高举“打倒岸信介政府”的横幅大标语，在东京大街上示威。　　　　新华社稿
