Title: Guo Moruo Receives Indonesian Peace Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-05-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 7th - Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, received the Indonesian Peace Delegation led by Sukino, Vice Chairman of the Indonesian Peace Committee, this morning. Qu Tangliang and Wang Mingyuan were present at the reception.

### 郭沫若接见印度尼西亚和平代表团

1960-05-08
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见印度尼西亚和平代表团
    新华社7日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若今天上午接见了印度尼西亚和平委员会副主席苏基诺率领的印度尼西亚和平代表团。接见时在座的有区棠亮、王明远。
