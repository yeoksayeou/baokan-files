Title: Premier Zhou Enlai's Talks with Montgomery
Author:
Date: 1960-05-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 26th: Premier Zhou Enlai held talks with British Field Marshal Montgomery this afternoon.

Present at the meeting were Vice Premier Marshal He Long and Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Marshal Chen Yi.

### 周总理同蒙哥马利谈话

1960-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理同蒙哥马利谈话
    新华社26日讯　周恩来总理今天下午同英国蒙哥马利元帅进行了谈话。
    在座的有副总理贺龙元帅和副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅。
