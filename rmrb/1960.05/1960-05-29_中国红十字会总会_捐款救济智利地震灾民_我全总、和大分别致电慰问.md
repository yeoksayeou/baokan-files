Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions and China Peace Committee Send Condolences

Author:

Date: 1960-05-29
Page: 3

    China Red Cross Society
    Donates Funds to Aid Chilean Earthquake Victims
    All-China Federation of Trade Unions and China Peace Committee Send Condolences Respectively
    Xinhua News Agency, May 28th: The Red Cross Society of China today donated 25,000 yuan in Renminbi to the Chilean Red Cross to aid victims of the Chilean earthquake.
    The League of Red Cross Societies issued a circular after the earthquake in Chile, calling on national Red Cross societies to donate funds to aid the victims.
    Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China, also sent a telegram to Dr. Inostroza, President of the Chilean Red Cross, on May 27, expressing condolences to the victims of the earthquake in Chile.
    Xinhua News Agency, May 28th: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the China Peace Committee yesterday and today sent telegrams to the Chilean Workers' United Confederation and the Chilean Peace Committee respectively, expressing concern and condolences to the victims of the earthquake in Chile.

### 中国红十字会总会  捐款救济智利地震灾民  我全总、和大分别致电慰问

1960-05-29
第3版()
专栏：

    中国红十字会总会
    捐款救济智利地震灾民
    我全总、和大分别致电慰问
    据新华社28日讯　中国红十字会总会今天捐款人民币二万五千元给智利红十字会，救济智利地震灾民。
    红十字会协会在智利遭到地震后曾发出通函，呼吁各国红十字会捐款救济灾民。
    中国红十字会总会会长李德全27日还打电报给智利红十字会会长伊诺斯特罗萨博士，向智利遭受地震的灾民表示慰问。
    新华社28日讯　中华全国总工会和中国人民保卫世界和平委员会昨天和今天分别打电报给智利工人统一工会和智利和平委员会，对智利遭到地震的灾民表示关怀和慰问。
