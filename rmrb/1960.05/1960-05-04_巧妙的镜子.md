Seafaring Line

    Clever Mirror
    Yang Xiaozhao, a young boatman at the Dinghai Transportation Station, boldly researched and created a navigational reflecting mirror. By using the reflecting mirror, boats can observe the surrounding scenery, compass, and clock from inside the cabin or the captain's bunk, ensuring the safe navigation of the vessel.

### 巧妙的镜子

1960-05-04
第2版()
专栏：海运线上

    巧妙的镜子
    定海交通运输站青年船工杨孝造大胆钻研，创造了一种航行反光镜，船只使用反光镜可以在船舱里或者在老大的铺位上看到四周的景物、指南针和时钟等，可保证船只安全航行。
