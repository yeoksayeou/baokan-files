Title: Colombian Petroleum Workers Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-05-05
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 4th. A delegation from the Colombian Petroleum Workers Union, headed by Rafael Berlaes, vice chairman of the Colombian Petroleum Workers Union, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.
    They are visiting China at the invitation of the National Committee of the Chinese Petroleum Trade Union.

### 哥伦比亚石油工人联合会代表团到京

1960-05-05
第6版()
专栏：

    哥伦比亚石油工人联合会代表团到京
    新华社4日讯　以哥伦比亚石油工人联合会副主席拉法埃尔·贝拉埃斯为首的哥伦比亚石油工人联合会代表团，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    他们是应中国石油工会全国委员会邀请，来我国参观访问的。
