Title: Premier Zhou's Telegram to Prince Sihanouk and Prime Minister Phoumi Nosavan
Author:
Date: 1960-05-11
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 9th May. The full text of Premier Zhou Enlai's telegram of thanks to Prince Norodom Sihanouk and Prime Minister Phoumi Nosavan is as follows:

Your Royal Highness Prince Sihanouk,
Your Excellency Prime Minister Phoumi Nosavan,

As I am about to leave the land of the fraternal Cambodian people, please allow me, on behalf of myself, Marshal Chen Yi, and all members of the delegation, to express our gratitude to Your Highness and Your Excellency, and please convey our thanks to Her Majesty the Queen, His Royal Highness Prince Monireth, Chairman of the Regency Council, the Royal Government, and all the Cambodian people.

I wish the Kingdom of Cambodia prosperity, its people happiness, and the continuous development and consolidation of the friendship between the Chinese and Cambodian peoples.

                                                     Zhou Enlai
                                            Morning of May 9, 1960

### 周总理致西哈努克亲王和波伦首相电

1960-05-11
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理致西哈努克亲王和波伦首相电
    新华社金边9日电　周恩来总理致西哈努克亲王和福·波伦首相的感谢电，全文如下：尊敬的西哈努克亲王殿下，尊敬的福·波伦首相阁下：
    当我就要离开兄弟般的柬埔寨人民的国土的时候，请允许我以我本人、陈毅元帅和代表团全体成员的名义，向殿下和阁下致谢，并请转达我们对王后陛下、摄政委员会主席莫尼勒亲王殿下、王国政府和全体柬埔寨人民的谢意。
  祝柬埔寨王国繁荣，人民幸福，中柬两国人民的友谊不断发展和巩固。
                                                     周恩来
                                            1960年5月9日上午
