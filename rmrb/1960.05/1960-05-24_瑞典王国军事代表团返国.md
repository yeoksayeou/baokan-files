Title: Swedish Royal Military Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-05-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, May 18th: The Swedish Royal Military Delegation, headed by Major General Ahlmann, Chief of the Swedish Ministry of Defence's Military Order Department, left Guangzhou by train for Shenzhen on the morning of the 18th after concluding its visit to China, and will return home via Hong Kong.

### 瑞典王国军事代表团返国

1960-05-24
第5版()
专栏：

    瑞典王国军事代表团返国
    据新华社广州电　以瑞典王国国防部军令部部长奥尔曼少将为首的瑞典王国军事代表团，在结束对我国访问后，已于18日上午由广州乘火车去深圳，经香港回国。
