Title: The Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front, and the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace Issue a Message to the People: Strongly Condemning the American Robber's Crimes of Aggression
Author: 
Date: 1960-05-24
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 21st: The Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front, and the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace, in a message to the people issued today, strongly condemned the crimes of U.S. imperialism in sending aircraft to invade Soviet airspace and sabotaging the four-power summit conference.

    The message to the people stated, "We cannot remain indifferent and lose our vigilance in the face of the provocative actions of the enemy of the world's people and the Vietnamese people—U.S. imperialism. They are stepping up their efforts to undermine world peace and the cause of our people's struggle for the reunification of our country." The message to the people warmly supports the correct stance taken by Khrushchev and the Soviet government.

### 越南劳动党中央委员会  越南祖国战线中央委员会  越南保卫世界和平委员会发表告人民书  强烈谴责美国强盗侵略罪行

1960-05-24
第3版()
专栏：

    越南劳动党中央委员会  越南祖国战线中央委员会  越南保卫世界和平委员会发表告人民书
    强烈谴责美国强盗侵略罪行
    据新华社河内21日电　越南劳动党中央委员会、越南祖国战线中央委员会和越南保卫世界和平委员会，在今天发表的告人民书中，强烈谴责美帝国主义派遣飞机侵入苏联领空、破坏四国首脑会议的罪行。
    告人民书说，“我们不能在世界人民和越南人民的敌人——美帝国主义的挑衅行动面前置若罔闻和失掉警惕。它们正在加紧破坏世界和平，破坏我国人民为统一祖国而斗争的事业。”告人民书热烈支持赫鲁晓夫和苏联政府所采取的正确立场。
