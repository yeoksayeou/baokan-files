Title: Zhang Yun Receives Hong Kong and Macao Women's "May Day" Visiting Group
Author:
Date: 1960-05-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Zhang Yun, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, received the Hong Kong and Macao Women's "May Day" Visiting Group this morning. Wu Qing, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation, and others were present at the reception.

Zhang Yun had a cordial conversation with Su Wuzi, head of the visiting group, and the members.

### 章蕴接见港澳妇女“五一”参观团

1960-05-08
第5版()
专栏：

    章蕴接见港澳妇女“五一”参观团
    新华社7日讯　全国妇联副主席章蕴今天上午接见了港澳妇女“五一”参观团。陪同接见的有全国妇联书记处书记吴綪等。
    章蕴同参观团团长苏务滋和团员们进行了亲切的谈话。
