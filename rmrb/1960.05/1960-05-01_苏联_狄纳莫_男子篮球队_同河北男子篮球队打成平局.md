Title: The Soviet "Dynamo" Men's Basketball Team Draws with the Hebei Men's Basketball Team
Author:
Date: 1960-05-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 30th - The Soviet "Dynamo" Men's Basketball Team held a friendly match with the Hebei Province Men's Basketball Team in Tianjin on the evening of the 29th, resulting in a 63-63 tie. This wonderful match repeatedly won warm applause from more than six thousand spectators.

The game saw eleven ties, with the score remaining very close throughout. At the end of the first half, the Hebei team was leading by six points, 34-28.

### 苏联“狄纳莫”男子篮球队  同河北男子篮球队打成平局

1960-05-01
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联“狄纳莫”男子篮球队
    同河北男子篮球队打成平局
    新华社天津30日电　苏联“狄纳莫”男子篮球队29日晚在天津同河北省男子篮球队举行了一场友谊比赛，结果以六十三比六十三打成平局。这场精采的比赛，不断博得了六千多名观众的热烈掌声。
    这场比赛，曾出现十一次平局，比分始终很接近。上半场结束时，河北队曾以三十四比二十八领先六分。
