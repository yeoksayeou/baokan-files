Title: Heart - Bomb
Author: Shāní Huá Móu
Date: 1960-05-27
Page: 8

Column: Poetry Leaflet

　　Heart - Bomb
　　(Dong Nationality) Shāní Huá Móu
　　Billions of hearts, like angry bombs,
　　Billions of hands, like dense forests.
　　We can no longer tolerate the heinous acts of U.S. imperialism,
　　The tyranny of Syngman Rhee,
　　The iron heels of the invaders trampling.
　　Moscow, Pyongyang, Beijing,
　　Everywhere is sending out roars of anger:
　　U.S. imperialism get out of South Korea,
　　Korea forever belongs to the Korean people!

### 心——炸弹
沙尼华谋
1960-05-27
第8版()
专栏：诗传单

    心——炸弹
    侗族　沙尼华谋亿万颗心，如同愤怒的炸弹，亿万双手，如同密茂的森林。美帝的凶行，李承晚的暴政，我们再不堪容忍，侵略者的铁蹄蹂躏。莫斯科、平壤、北京，到处都发出怒吼的声音：美帝国主义滚出南朝鲜，朝鲜永远属于朝鲜人！
