Title: U.S. Military Aircraft Intrudes into Airspace Over Xisha Islands; 102nd Serious Warning Issued

Author:

Date: 1960-05-31
Page: 1

    U.S. Military Aircraft Intrudes into Airspace Over Xisha Islands
    102nd Serious Warning Issued
    Xinhua News Agency, May 30th: On May 30th, a U.S. Navy patrol plane twice intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island and Xisha Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province, once between 12:33 and 12:45 and again between 13:18 and 13:25. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, a spokesman for the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 102nd serious warning.

### 美国军用飞机侵入我西沙群岛上空  我提出第102次严重警告

1960-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

    美国军用飞机侵入我西沙群岛上空
    我提出第102次严重警告
    新华社30日讯　5月30日，美国海军巡逻飞机一架，于十二时三十三分至四十五分和十三时十八分至二十五分先后两次侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛、西沙洲地区上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第一百零二次严重警告。
