Title: May the Fraternal Friendship Between the Chinese and Vietnamese Peoples Last Forever: Vietnamese Newspapers Publish Editorials Warmly Welcoming Premier Zhou's Visit
Author:
Date: 1960-05-10
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 9th: Today, Hanoi's major newspapers – "People's Daily," "Capital Hanoi Daily," "New Era Daily," and "Vanguard Daily" – all published editorials welcoming Premier Zhou Enlai's visit to Vietnam.
    The "People's Daily" editorial said that the Vietnamese people are particularly happy and delighted to welcome Premier Zhou Enlai, the respected representative of the Chinese people, and his entourage during such a joyous time when the Vietnamese people have just victoriously held the second National Assembly elections. The editorial warmly welcomed the achievements made by Premier Zhou Enlai during his visits to four other Asian countries, and believes that Premier Zhou Enlai's visits in the past few weeks have further clarified China's peaceful foreign policy. The editorial pointed out that the good results of Premier Zhou Enlai's visits to the four Asian countries have made the Asian people and all peace-loving people in the world feel elated and encouraged. The "Capital Hanoi Daily" editorial said that Premier Zhou Enlai's visit to Vietnam greatly encourages the Vietnamese people who are smoothly carrying out socialist construction and striving for the unification of their motherland. In talking about China's construction achievements, the editorial said that China's continued leap forward is an incomparably important contribution to defending peace in Asia and the world. The editorial talked about the peaceful foreign policy consistently pursued by the Chinese government, and said that China's international status has become higher and more consolidated.
    The "New Era Daily" editorial said that China is Vietnam's true brother who shares weal and woe. In the process of striving for liberation and building the motherland, the Vietnamese people have continuously received the full support and selfless assistance of the Chinese people. The editorial said that Premier Zhou's visit this time will further strengthen Sino-Vietnamese friendship and encourage the people of the two countries to make more contributions to the common cause of the socialist camp and the peace-loving people of the world.
    The above three newspapers also published the news of Premier Zhou Enlai's arrival in Hanoi on the prominent first page today, as well as the photos and resumes of Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi. The "People's Daily" had a striking banner headline at the top of the first page, "The Friendship Between Vietnam and China Will Last Forever." The "New Era Daily" and "Capital Hanoi Daily" printed slogans in Chinese and Vietnamese on the first page, "Warmly Welcome Premier Zhou Enlai's Visit to Vietnam" and "The Friendship Between Vietnam and China Will Last Forever."
    The "People's Daily" also published an article today by Bui Ky, President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, entitled "Warmly Welcome Premier Zhou Enlai's Visit to Vietnam," and published letters from workers in some Vietnamese factories to Premier Zhou. The letters all mentioned that the achievements they have made in production are inseparable from China's assistance.
    The "Vanguard Daily," the organ of the Vietnam Labor Youth League, published an editorial today entitled "China – Our Close and Great Friend," welcoming Premier Zhou Enlai's visit to Vietnam.
    After outlining the mutual support between China and Vietnam in the revolution over the past decades and China's selfless assistance to Vietnam in recent years, the editorial said, "China's assistance to Vietnam is incomparably great, and the Chinese people have a vast friendship with the Vietnamese people."
    After pointing out the efforts made by the Chinese government over the years for the victory of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and Premier Zhou's recent visits to four Asian countries, the editorial said, "Vietnamese youth and people warmly welcome and firmly support this peaceful effort by the Chinese government." The editorial also said, "The achievements of the Chinese people have been strongly encouraging the Vietnamese people to strive for the cause of building a socialist North and striving for the unification of the motherland."

### 中越人民兄弟友谊万古长青  越南报纸发表社论热烈欢迎周总理访问

1960-05-10
第6版()
专栏：

    中越人民兄弟友谊万古长青
    越南报纸发表社论热烈欢迎周总理访问
    新华社河内9日电　今天，河内的主要报纸——“人民报”、“首都河内报”、新时代报”和“前锋报”都发表社论，欢迎周恩来总理访问越南。
    “人民报”的社论说，越南人民感到特别高兴和愉快的是，能够在越南人民刚刚胜利地举行了第二届国会选举这样欢腾的日子里，来迎接中国人民敬爱的代表——周恩来总理和他的随行人员。社论对周恩来总理在访问其他四个亚洲国家中所取得的成就表示热烈的欢迎，并且认为周恩来总理过去几周的访问进一步阐明了中国的和平外交政策。社论指出，周恩来总理访问亚洲四国的良好结果使亚洲人民和世界上一切爱好和平的人们感到欢欣鼓舞。“首都河内报”的社论说，周恩来总理这次访问越南，大大地鼓舞着正在顺利进行社会主义建设和争取祖国统一的斗争的越南人民。社论在谈到中国的建设成就时说，中国的持续跃进是对保卫亚洲和世界和平的无比重大的贡献。社论谈到了中国政府一贯奉行的和平外交政策，并且说，中国在国际上的地位已经愈来愈高和愈来愈巩固。
    “新时代报”的社论说，中国是越南的真正的同甘共苦的兄弟。越南人民在争取解放和建设祖国的过程中，不断地得到中国人民的全力支持和无私帮助。社论说，周总理这一次的访问将进一步加强越中友谊，并鼓舞两国人民为社会主义阵营和世界爱好和平人民的共同事业作出更多的贡献。
    以上三家报纸今天还在第一版显著地位，登载了周恩来总理将要到达河内的消息以及周恩来总理和陈毅副总理的照片和简历。“人民报”第一版上方登着醒目的通栏大字标题“越、中两国之间的友谊万古长青”。“新时代报”和“首都河内报”用中、越两国文字在第一版登着“热烈欢迎周恩来总理访问越南”、“越中两国之间的友谊万古长青”的标语。
    “人民报”今天还刊登了越中友好协会会长裴?写的“热烈欢迎周恩来总理访问越南”的文章，并且发表了越南一些工厂的职工给周总理的信。信中都提到他们在生产中所取得的成就是同中国的援助分不开的。
    越南劳动青年团机关报“前锋报”今天发表题为“中国——我们亲密而伟大的朋友”的社论，欢迎周恩来总理访问越南。
    社论在概述中越两国几十年来在革命中互相支援和中国几年来对越南的无私援助之后说，“中国对越南的援助无比伟大，中国人民同越南人民有着浩海般的友谊。”
    社论在指出中国政府几年来为和平共处五项原则的胜利所作的努力和最近周总理对亚洲四国的访问之后说，“越南青年和人民热烈欢迎并坚决支持中国政府的这种和平努力。”社论还说，“中国人民的成就一直有力地鼓舞着越南人民为建设社会主义的北方和争取统一祖国的斗争事业而奋斗。”
