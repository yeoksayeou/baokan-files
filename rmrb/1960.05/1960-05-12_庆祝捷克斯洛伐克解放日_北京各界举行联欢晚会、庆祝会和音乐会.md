Title: Beijing Circles Hold Get-Together Parties, Celebration Meetings, and Concerts to Celebrate Czechoslovakia's Liberation Day
Author:
Date: 1960-05-12
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 11th -- Beijing University and the Beijing Stamp Factory held get-together parties and celebration meetings on the 10th to celebrate the 15th anniversary of Czechoslovakia's liberation.

    Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Bušiňák, participated in the get-together party at Beijing University. Also participating in the party were Zhang Chao, Director of the Council of the Sino-Czechoslovak Friendship Association and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and international students studying at Beijing University. Czechoslovak students studying at the Agricultural University also came to participate in the party.

    At the celebration meeting at the Beijing Stamp Factory, Factory Director Zhao Xingmin, on behalf of all the factory's employees, extended festive greetings to the distinguished Czechoslovak guests and the Czechoslovak people, and expressed gratitude to the Czechoslovak experts who had helped build the factory. The head of the Czechoslovak Friendship Delegation, Obafsky, delegation member Vasik, and Counselor of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, Šťěkiňa, participated in the celebration meeting and gave speeches at the meeting.

    Xinhua News Agency, 11th -- The Sino-Czechoslovak Friendship Association and the Chinese Musicians Association jointly held a concert at the Capital Theater in Beijing tonight to celebrate the 15th anniversary of Czechoslovakia's liberation.

    Lu Ji, Chairman of the Chinese Musicians Association, said in his speech that since Czechoslovakia's liberation, the people's musical cause has made great progress under the leadership of the Communist Party. Musical creation has not only carried forward the excellent national traditions, but has also achieved great achievements in reflecting the real life of the people in socialist construction.

    At the concert, the Central Philharmonic Orchestra performed Dvořák's "New World Symphony" and works by modern Czechoslovak composers.

    Among those attending the concert were Xia Yan, President of the Sino-Czechoslovak Friendship Association and Vice Minister of Culture, Ren Hong, Director of the Sino-Czechoslovak Friendship Association, Yang Yinliu, Li Yuanqing, and Li Delun, Directors of the Chinese Musicians Association, as well as more than 1,000 people from all walks of life in the capital.

    Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Bušiňák, and other embassy staff attended the concert.

### 庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放日  北京各界举行联欢晚会、庆祝会和音乐会

1960-05-12
第3版()
专栏：

    庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放日
    北京各界举行联欢晚会、庆祝会和音乐会
    新华社11日讯　北京大学和北京邮票工厂10日举行联欢晚会和庆祝会，庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放十五周年。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使布希尼亚克参加了北京大学的联欢晚会。参加晚会的还有中捷友好协会理事、共青团中央书记处书记张超和在北京大学学习的各国留学生。在农业大学学习的捷克斯洛伐克留学生也前来参加晚会。
    在北京邮票工厂的庆祝大会上，工厂厂长赵醒民代表全厂职工向捷克斯洛伐克贵宾和捷克斯洛伐克人民致以节日的祝贺，并且向曾经帮助建厂的捷克斯洛伐克专家表示感谢。捷克斯洛伐克友好代表团团长奥巴夫斯基、团员瓦西克和捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆参赞石杰基那参加了庆祝大会，并且在会上讲了话。
    据新华社11日讯　中捷友好协会和中国音乐家协会今晚在首都北京剧场联合举行音乐会，庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放十五周年。
    中国音乐家协会主席吕骥在会上致词说，捷克斯洛伐克解放以来，人民的音乐事业在共产党的领导下有了巨大的发展，音乐创作不仅发扬了优秀的民族传统，而且在反映人民在社会主义建设中的现实生活方面也取得了巨大成就。
    会上，中央乐团演出了捷克斯洛伐克古典作曲家德沃夏克的“新大陆交响乐”以及现代捷克斯洛伐克作曲家的作品。
    出席音乐会的有，中捷友好协会会长、文化部副部长夏衍，中捷友好协会理事任虹，中国音乐家协会理事杨荫浏、李元庆、李德伦，以及首都各界人士共一千多人。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使布希尼亚克和使馆其他人员出席了音乐会。
