Title: Two Hungarian Educators Arrive in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-05-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd: Jalo Miklos, Director of the Children and Youth Protection Department of the Hungarian Ministry of Culture and Education, and Fökédy József, Deputy Director of the Education Department of the Ministry of Culture and Education, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon to conduct an inspection in our country according to the 1960 execution plan of the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 匈牙利两位教育工作者到京

1960-05-24
第5版()
专栏：

    匈牙利两位教育工作者到京
    新华社22日讯　根据中匈文化合作协定1960年执行计划来我国考察的匈牙利文教部儿童和青年保护司司长亚洛·米克洛什和文教部教育司副司长弗凯德·约瑟夫，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
