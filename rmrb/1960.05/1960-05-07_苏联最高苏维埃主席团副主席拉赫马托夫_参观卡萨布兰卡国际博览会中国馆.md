Title: Deputy Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Rakhmatov, Visits the Chinese Pavilion at the Casablanca International Exposition
Author:
Date: 1960-05-07
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Casablanca, May 5th. Mirzo Rakhmatov, Deputy Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Tajik Soviet Socialist Republic, who is currently visiting Morocco, visited the Chinese Pavilion at the Casablanca International Exposition on the morning of the 4th. He was accompanied by the Soviet Ambassador to Morocco and the President of the Casablanca International Exposition.

Zhang Tingyu, head of the Chinese delegation, accompanied the distinguished guests on their visit and provided them with detailed introductions. After the visit, the distinguished guests expressed their wishes for the Chinese people to achieve even greater accomplishments.

### 苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席拉赫马托夫  参观卡萨布兰卡国际博览会中国馆

1960-05-07
第7版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席拉赫马托夫
    参观卡萨布兰卡国际博览会中国馆
    新华社卡萨布兰卡5日电　正在摩洛哥访问的苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席、苏联塔吉克苏维埃社会主义共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席米尔佐·拉赫马托夫在4日午前参观了卡萨布兰卡国际博览会的中国馆。陪同他参观的有苏联驻摩洛哥大使和卡萨布兰卡国际博览会会长。
    中国代表团团长张廷禹陪同贵宾们参观，并向他们作了详细的介绍。在参观后，贵宾表示祝中国人民取得更大的成就。
