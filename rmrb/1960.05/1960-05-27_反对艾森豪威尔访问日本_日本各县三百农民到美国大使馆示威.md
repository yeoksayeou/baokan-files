Title: Protesting Eisenhower's Visit to Japan: Three Hundred Farmers from Various Japanese Prefectures Demonstrate at the American Embassy
Author:
Date: 1960-05-27
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 26th - Three hundred farmers from prefectures including Ibaraki, Nagano, Niigata, Fukushima, Yamanashi, Yamagata, Akita, and Oita, waving straw flags, demonstrated at the U.S. Embassy in Japan this afternoon to oppose U.S. President Eisenhower's visit to Japan and demand the withdrawal of U-2 spy planes from Japan. They are all members of the All-Japan Farmers Association Federation and came to Tokyo to participate in today's large demonstration against the new Japan-U.S. "Security Treaty."

Four farmer representatives, including Yasushi Noze, chairman of the All-Japan Farmers Association Federation, met with representatives of the U.S. Embassy and delivered a letter of protest.

Afterwards, they went to Hibiya Park to join other demonstrators in protesting against the Kishi Nobusuke clique's forcible "passage" of the Japan-U.S. military alliance treaty in the House of Representatives.

### 反对艾森豪威尔访问日本  日本各县三百农民到美国大使馆示威

1960-05-27
第3版()
专栏：

    反对艾森豪威尔访问日本
    日本各县三百农民到美国大使馆示威
    新华社26日讯　东京消息：来自茨城、长野、新潟、福岛、山梨、山形、秋田、大分等县的三百名农民挥舞着草制旗帜，今天下午到美国驻日本大使馆去举行示威，反对美国总统艾森豪威尔访问日本，并要求美国U—2型间谍飞机撤出日本。他们全都是全日本农民协会联合会的会员，是到东京来参加今天举行的反对新日美“安全条约”的大示威的。
    四位农民代表，包括全日本农民协会联合会会长野汫胜，会见了美国大使馆的代表并递交了抗议信。
    然后，他们到日比谷公园去，同其它的示威者汇合起来，抗议岸信介集团在众议院强行“通过”日美军事同盟条约。
