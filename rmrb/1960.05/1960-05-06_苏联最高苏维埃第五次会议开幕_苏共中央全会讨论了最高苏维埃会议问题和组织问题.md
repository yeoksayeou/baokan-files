Title: Opening of the Fifth Session of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union; Plenary Session of the CPSU Central Committee Discussed Issues of the Supreme Soviet Session and Organizational Matters
Author: 
Date: 1960-05-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, May 5th, Moscow dispatch: TASS Moscow dispatch: The Fifth Session of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union opened at 10:00 AM (Moscow time) today in the Kremlin, Moscow.

The Soviet of the Union and the Soviet of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet first held separate meetings. The two houses of the Supreme Soviet adopted the following agenda for this session:

    Abolition of taxes on workers and employees;
    On measures to complete the transition of all workers and employees to a shortened workday in 1960;
    Approval of the decrees of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.

Subsequently, the two houses of the Supreme Soviet held a joint session at 11:00 AM. Nikita Khrushchev, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, delivered a report on the first item on the agenda, "Abolition of Taxes on Workers and Employees."

Foreign diplomatic envoys to the Soviet Union, as well as Soviet and foreign journalists, were present at the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, May 5th, Moscow dispatch: TASS Moscow, May 5th: The communiqué of the plenary session of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union held on May 4th was officially released here. The communiqué stated:

    The plenary session discussed the issues of the Supreme Soviet session and organizational matters.
    The plenary session elected A. Kosygin, N. Podgorny, and D. Polyansky as members of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee.
    The plenary session relieved A. Aristov, member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee, and P. Pospelov, alternate member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee, of their duties as secretaries of the CPSU Central Committee, so that they could concentrate on their work in the Bureau of the Russian Federation of the CPSU Central Committee.
    The plenary session relieved N. Ignatov, member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee, of his duties as secretary of the CPSU Central Committee, because he was appointed Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union.
    The plenary session relieved Ye. Furtseva, member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee, of her duties as secretary of the CPSU Central Committee, because she was appointed Minister of Culture of the Soviet Union.
    The plenary session elected V. Kozlov, member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee, as secretary of the CPSU Central Committee.
    The plenary session relieved N. Belyayev of his membership in the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee.
    The plenary session relieved A. Kirichenko of his membership in the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee and his duties as secretary of the CPSU Central Committee.

Xinhua News Agency, May 5th, Moscow dispatch: TASS Moscow, May 5th: In view of the transfer of work, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union relieved V. Kozlov of his post as First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union (at the plenary session of the CPSU Central Committee held on May 4th, Kozlov was elected as Secretary of the Central Committee).

A. Kosygin was appointed First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, relieving him of his post as Chairman of the State Planning Committee of the Soviet Union.

N. Ignatov was appointed Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union (therefore, this plenary session of the CPSU Central Committee relieved N. Ignatov of his duties as Secretary of the Central Committee).

Ye. Furtseva was appointed Minister of Culture of the Soviet Union.

V. Novikov was appointed as Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the State Planning Committee of the USSR (Novikov was formerly Deputy Chairman and Chairman of the State Planning Committee of the Council of Ministers of the Russian Federation).

In view of the transfer of work, N. Mikhailov was relieved of his post as Minister of Culture of the Soviet Union.

### 苏联最高苏维埃第五次会议开幕  苏共中央全会讨论了最高苏维埃会议问题和组织问题

1960-05-06
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃第五次会议开幕
    苏共中央全会讨论了最高苏维埃会议问题和组织问题
    新华社5日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃第五次会议，今天上午十时（莫斯科时间）在莫斯科克里姆林宫开幕。
    苏联最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院首先分别举行了会议。两院会议通过这次会议的议程如下：
    取销工人和职员的赋税；
    关于在1960年完成全体工人和职员过渡到缩短工作日的措施；
    批准苏联最高苏维埃主席团的命令。
    接着，苏联最高苏维埃两院在上午十一时举行联席会议。苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫在会上就第一项议程“取销工人和职员的赋税”作了报告。
    各国驻苏联外交使节以及苏联和外国的新闻记者列席了会议。
    新华社5日讯　塔斯社莫斯科5日电：此间正式发表了苏联共产党中央委员会5月4日举行的全体会议的公报。公报中说：
    全会讨论了苏联最高苏维埃会议的问题和组织问题。
    全会选出阿·柯西金、尼·波德戈尔内和德·波利扬斯基为苏共中央主席团委员。
    全会免除了苏共中央主席团委员阿·阿里斯托夫和苏共中央主席团候补委员彼·波斯别洛夫所担任的苏共中央书记的职务，使他们能够集中精力从事苏共中央俄罗斯联邦局的工作。
    全会免除了苏共中央主席团委员尼·伊格纳托夫的苏共中央书记的职务，因为他被任命为苏联部长会议副主席。
    全会免除了苏共中央主席团委员叶·福尔采娃的苏共中央书记的职务，因为她被任命为苏联文化部部长。
    全会选出苏共中央主席团委员弗·科兹洛夫为苏共中央书记。
    全会免除了尼·别利亚耶夫的苏共中央主席团委员的职务。
    全会免除了阿·基里钦科的苏共中央主席团委员和苏共中央书记的职务。
    新华社5日讯　塔斯社莫斯科5日电：鉴于工作的调动，苏联最高苏维埃主席团免除弗·科兹洛夫的苏联部长会议第一副主席的职务（在5月4日举行的苏共中央全会上，科兹洛夫当选为中央委员会书记）。
    阿·柯西金被任命为苏联部长会议第一副主席，免除他的苏联国家计划委员会主席的职务。
    尼·伊格纳托夫被任命为苏联部长会议副主席（因此，这次苏共中央全会免除了尼·伊格纳托夫的中央委员会书记的职务）。
    叶·福尔采娃被任命为苏联文化部部长。
    弗·诺维科夫被任命为苏联部长会议副主席和苏联国家计划委员会主席（诺维科夫原任俄罗斯联邦部长会议副主席和国家计划委员会主席）。
    鉴于工作的调动，免除尼·米哈伊洛夫的苏联文化部部长的职务。
