Title: Without the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, It Is Impossible to Establish a Socialist State
Author:
Date: 1966-09-06
Edition: 5

Mali's Director of Physical Culture, Ben Gorro Koulibaly, said: "Mao Zedong Thought is an invaluable common treasure of the world's proletariat." He said: "The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China is an unprecedented and unique socialist cultural revolution in human history. Without this cultural revolution, it is impossible to establish a genuine socialist state. Only those with socialist ideology can create socialist culture."

Ben Gorro Koulibaly is leading the Mali national football team on a visit to China.

He said: "We fully agree with Chairman Mao's truth that class struggle exists universally in socialist society. If people are not vigilant against old ideas and old culture and do not resolutely fight against them, it will be very difficult for China to transition to a communist society. The great significance of Chairman Mao personally leading the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution lies here. We believe that under the brilliant guidance of Mao Zedong Thought, the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China will surely achieve thorough victory, enabling China to rid itself of all reactionary culture and old ideas, and ultimately build a communist society where there is no exploitation of man by man."

This Mali physical culture official enthusiastically praised the Chinese people and athletes for studying Chairman Mao's works in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, saying: "The Chinese people and athletes carry the 'Quotations from Chairman Mao' with them as a precious book, because this book is an inexhaustible source of strength for them to overcome various difficulties on their forward march."

### 没有无产阶级文化大革命  就不能确立社会主义国家  马里体育专员说中国文化大革命必将取得胜利

1966-09-06
第5版()
专栏：

    没有无产阶级文化大革命
    就不能确立社会主义国家
    马里体育专员说中国文化大革命必将取得胜利
    据新华社三日讯　马里共和国体育专员本戈罗·库利巴利说：“毛泽东思想是世界无产阶级无可估计的共同财富。”他说：“中国无产阶级文化大革命是人类历史上从未有过的，也是唯一的社会主义文化大革命，如果没有这个文化大革命，就不能确立一个真正的社会主义国家。社会主义的文化，只有具有社会主义思想的人才能创造。”
    本戈罗·库利巴利是率领马里国家足球队来中国访问的。
    他说：“我们非常赞成毛主席提出的这样一个真理，那就是在社会主义社会中普遍存在着阶级斗争。假使人们对旧思想、旧文化丧失警惕，不对它们进行坚决斗争，要使中国过渡到共产主义社会将是很困难的。毛主席亲自领导无产阶级文化大革命的伟大意义就在这里。我们相信，中国人民在毛泽东思想的光辉照耀下，中国的无产阶级文化大革命必将取得彻底胜利，使中国摆脱一切反动的文化和一切旧思想，把中国最终建成没有人压迫人、没有人剥削人的共产主义社会。”
    这位马里体育专员热情地赞扬中国人民和中国运动员在文化大革命运动中活学活用毛主席著作时说：“中国人民和运动员把《毛主席语录》当作随身携带的宝书，因为这本书是他们在前进的道路上克服各种困难的取之不尽、用之不竭的力量的源泉。”
