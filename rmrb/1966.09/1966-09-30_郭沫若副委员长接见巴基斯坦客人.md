Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Receives Pakistani Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-09-30
Edition: 4

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Chairman of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries Guo Moruo received Secretary-General of the Pakistan Writers' Association Jamiluddin Ali and his wife this afternoon. He had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见巴基斯坦客人

1966-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见巴基斯坦客人
    新华社二十九日讯　人大常委会副委员长、全国文联主席郭沫若，今天下午接见巴基斯坦作家同人会秘书长贾米路丁·阿里和夫人，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
