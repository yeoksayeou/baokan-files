Title: Farmers in Qidong County Fight Drought to Achieve Bumper Harvest
Author:
Date: 1966-09-05
Edition: 3

	Farmers in Qidong County, Jiangsu Province, are fighting a drought to achieve a bumper harvest. According to Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, September 2nd, the 580,000 mu of cotton in Qidong County is growing well due to meticulous management. In the previous period, there was a drought with no rain. The Communist Party Committee of Qidong County instructed all communes and production brigades to seriously study the works of Chairman Mao and to actively apply them in the anti-drought struggle.

	The broad masses of cadres and farmers have established the idea of farming for revolution, their spirits are high, and they are fully engaged in the anti-drought struggle to achieve a bumper harvest. Many leading cadres from the county, district, commune, and brigade levels have gone deep into the production front lines. Personnel from various enterprises have also brought their bedding, "Quotations from Chairman Mao," and tools to the production teams to support the anti-drought efforts. Commercial personnel have delivered goods to the doorsteps, directly supporting agricultural production.

### 启东县社员抗干旱夺丰收

1966-09-05
第3版()
专栏：

    启东县社员抗干旱夺丰收
    据新华社南京二日电　江苏省启东县五十八万亩棉花，由于精细管理，长势很好。前一时期，天旱不雨，中共启东县委指示各社队，认真学习毛主席著作，并在抗旱斗争中活学活用。
    广大干部社员树立了为革命种田的思想，精神振奋，全力投入抗旱斗争，夺取丰收。县、区、公社和大队的许多负责干部，都深入到生产第一线。各企业单位的人员，也扛着铺盖，带着《毛主席语录》和工具，到生产队支援抗旱。商业人员送货上门，直接支援农业生产。
