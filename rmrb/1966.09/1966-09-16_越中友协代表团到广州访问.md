Title: Delegation of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association Visits Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1966-09-16
Edition: 5

A delegation of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, led by Sun Guanguo, arrived in Guangzhou by plane from Nanchang on the 13th.

Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Acting Governor of Guangdong Province Lin Liming hosted a banquet to welcome the delegation that day. In his speech at the banquet, Lin Liming expressed his wishes for the fraternal Vietnamese people to achieve greater victories in their struggle against American imperialism and for national salvation. Sun Guanguo said, "During our visit to China, we have witnessed a great proletarian cultural revolution underway, sweeping away the remnants of feudalism and capitalism, and establishing a new proletarian culture. We are delighted by China's magnificent achievements. We wish China even greater successes in the future."

On the 14th, more than a thousand people from various sectors in Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City, including Red Guards, held a grand gathering to celebrate the 21st anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Before visiting Guangzhou, the delegation visited the "August 1st" Uprising Memorial Hall in Nanchang and toured Jinggangshan. The people of Jinggangshan also held a celebration for the 21st anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

### 越中友协代表团到广州访问

1966-09-16
第5版()
专栏：

    越中友协代表团到广州访问
    据新华社广州十四日电　由孙光阀率领的越中友协代表团，十三日从南昌乘飞机到达广州。
    中共广东省委书记处书记、广东省代省长林李明当天设宴欢迎代表团。林李明在宴会上讲话，他祝愿兄弟的越南人民在抗美救国斗争中取得更大的新的胜利。孙光阀说，我们在中国访问期间，中国正在进行一场无产阶级文化大革命，扫掉封建主义和资本主义遗留的残迹，建立无产阶级新文化。我们为中国取得的辉煌成就感到欢欣鼓舞。他祝中国今后取得越来越大的成就。
    十四日，广东省、广州市各界和红卫兵一千多人隆重集会，热烈庆祝越南民主共和国成立二十一周年。
  在访问广州前，代表团在南昌参观了“八一”起义纪念馆，访问了井冈山。井冈山各界人民也为越南民主共和国成立二十一周年举行了庆祝集会。
