Title: Delegation of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association Visits Other Regions
Author:
Date: 1966-09-07
Edition: 5

A delegation of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, led by Sun Guanguo, departed Beijing by plane this morning to visit and tour other regions.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Yang Xiufeng, Zhang Ziyu, Chu Tuonan, and other relevant officials.

Lai Chung Shi, Counselor of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, and Nguyen Minh Phuong, Acting Head of the Vietnam National Liberation Front's Permanent Delegation to China, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 越中友协代表团去外地参观访问

1966-09-07
第5版()
专栏：

    越中友协代表团去外地参观访问
    新华社六日讯　由孙光阀率领的越中友协代表团今天上午乘飞机离开北京，去外地参观访问。
    到机场欢送的，有杨秀峰、张奚若、楚图南等有关方面人士。
    越南驻中国大使馆参赞黎终始、越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代理团长阮明芳等也到机场欢送。
