Premier Zhou Receives Visiting Delegation from Japan's Liberal Democratic Party

Xinhua News Agency, September 12th: Premier Zhou Enlai this evening received members of the visiting delegation from Japan's Liberal Democratic Party to China, including Furui Jizō, Kosaka Zen'ichirō, Esaki Masami, Fukuda Takeo, Kuranari Masayoshi, Sakamura Kichimasa, Kujiraoka Heisuke, Kawano Michiaki, Ōkubo Shinsei, Shimonishi Bun'ō, and Kamihata Yoshihiko. Premier Zhou Enlai had a friendly conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi, Wang Xiaoyun, and Xiao Jinguang, among others. (Photo attached)

### 周总理接见日本自由民主党议员访华团

1966-09-13
第5版()
专栏：

    周总理接见日本自由民主党议员访华团
    新华社十二日讯　周恩来总理今晚接见日本自由民主党国会议员访华团成员古井喜实、小坂善太郎、江崎真澄、福田一、仓成正、坂村吉正、鲸冈兵助、川野三晓、大久保任晴、下西文雄、上和田义彦。周恩来总理同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有廖承志、王晓云、萧向前等。（附图片）
