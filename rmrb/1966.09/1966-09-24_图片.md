Title: Exhibition of Chinese Artworks in Bucharest
Author:
Date: 1966-09-24
Edition: 5

During the exhibition of Chinese artworks in Bucharest, the Romanian people expressed their boundless love for Chairman Mao. The image shows some viewers standing in front of the oil painting "On the Eve of the Decisive Battle," gazing for a long time at the lofty image of Chairman Mao.
