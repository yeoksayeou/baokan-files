Title: Kunming Automobile Transport Station Achieves High Volume, Speed, Quality, and Efficiency
Author:
Date: 1966-09-12
Edition: 2

Kunming Automobile Transport Station has achieved remarkable results in its transportation work for the first eight months of this year. The station has exceeded the state's plan for cargo volume and material turnover. Labor productivity has increased by 11.7 percent compared to the same period last year.

### 昆明汽车运输总站运输多快好省

1966-09-12
第2版()
专栏：

    昆明汽车运输总站运输多快好省
    据新华社昆明十一日电  昆明汽车运输总站今年前八个月的运输工作，做得又多又快又好又省。货运量、物资周转量等都超额完成了国家计划，劳动生产率比去年同期提高百分之十一点七。
