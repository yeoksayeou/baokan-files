Title: Excerpt from the Communiqué of the Eleventh Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1966-09-24
Edition: 5

	Mao Zedong is the greatest Marxist-Leninist of contemporary times. Mao Zedong has brilliantly, creatively, and comprehensively inherited, defended, and developed Marxism-Leninism, elevating it to a new stage. Mao Zedong Thought is the Marxism-Leninism of the era when imperialism is heading towards comprehensive collapse and socialism is advancing towards worldwide victory.

### 摘自《中国共产党第八届中央委员会第十一次全体会议公报》

1966-09-24
第5版()
专栏：

      毛泽东同志是当代最伟大的马克思列宁主义者。毛泽东同志天才地、创造性地、全面地继承、捍卫和发展了马克思列宁主义，把马克思列宁主义提高到一个崭新的阶段。毛泽东思想是在帝国主义走向全面崩溃，社会主义走向全世界胜利的时代的马克思列宁主义。
      摘自《中国共产党第八届中央委员会第十一次全体会议公报》
