Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Chairman Kang Sheng Receive Artists from the Japanese Progressive Theater
Author:
Date: 1966-09-19
Edition: 4

	Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Chairman Kang Sheng received the leader of the Japanese Progressive Theater, Kawaharasaki Chojuro, deputy leaders Nakamura Tan'emon and Kawaharasaki Shizuka, as well as the main actors of the troupe, and had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.

	Present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi, Song Yiping, Zhou Yiping, and others.

	After the reception, Premier Zhou and Vice Chairman Kang and others watched a preview performance of the play "Five-Storied Pagoda" by the artists of the Progressive Theater. (Photo attached)

	Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Chairman Kang Sheng receive the leader of the Japanese Progressive Theater, Kawaharasaki Chojuro (fifth from the right), deputy leaders Nakamura Tan'emon (second from the right) and Kawaharasaki Shizuka (sixth from the right), as well as the main actors of the troupe. ### 周恩来总理和康生副委员长接见日本前进座剧团艺术家

1966-09-19
第4版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理和康生副委员长接见日本前进座剧团艺术家
    新华社十八日讯　周恩来总理和康生副委员长，今晚接见日本前进座剧团团长河原崎长十郎，副团长中村翫右卫门、河原崎静江，以及剧团的主要演员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有廖承志、宋一平、周一萍等。
    接见以后，周总理和康生副委员长等观看了前进座艺术家预演话剧《五重塔》。（附图片）
