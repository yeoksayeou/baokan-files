Title: Pakistani Friendship Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-09-29
Edition: 6

A Pakistani friendship delegation led by A. Munaim Khan, the Governor of East Pakistan, arrived in Beijing by plane tonight. The delegation was invited by Vice Premier Chen Yi to participate in the National Day celebrations.

Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Chairman Guo Moruo, and over a thousand Beijing residents warmly welcomed the delegation at the airport. Also present at the airport to welcome the delegation were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Liu Xiao and other relevant officials. Pakistani Ambassador to China Sultan Muhammad Khan was also at the airport to greet the delegation.

The Pakistani dignitaries and Tanzanian dignitaries arrived in Beijing on the same plane on September 28 and were escorted out of the airport by Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Chairman Guo Moruo, and others.

### 巴基斯坦友好代表团到京  陈毅副总理和郭沫若副委员长前往机场迎接

1966-09-29
第6版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦友好代表团到京
    陈毅副总理和郭沫若副委员长前往机场迎接
    据新华社二十八日讯　由东巴基斯坦省督阿·穆奈姆·汗率领的巴基斯坦友好代表团，应陈毅副总理的邀请，前来我国参加国庆庆祝活动，今天晚上乘飞机到达北京。
    陈毅副总理、郭沫若副委员长和首都群众一千多人，到机场热烈欢迎。到机场欢迎的，还有外交部副部长刘晓等有关方面负责人。巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆罕默德·汗也到机场迎接。（附图片）
    巴基斯坦贵宾和坦桑尼亚贵宾九月二十八日同机到达北京，由陈毅副总理、郭沫若副委员长等陪同步出机场。　新华社记者摄
