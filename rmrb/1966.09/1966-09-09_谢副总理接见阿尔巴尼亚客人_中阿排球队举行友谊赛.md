Title: Vice Premier Xie Meets Albanian Guests and Chinese and Albanian Volleyball Teams Hold Friendly Match
Author:
Date: 1966-09-09
Edition: 4

Vice Premier Xie Meets Albanian Guests

	According to a report from Xinhua News Agency on the 8th, Vice Premier Xie Fuzhi this evening met with Vasile Afërdita, Vice Chairman of the "Dinamo" Sports Club of Albania, and the entire Albanian Ministry of Interior "Dinamo" men's volleyball team led by him, as well as Albanian sculptor Andrea Mana, who is currently visiting our country. He had a very cordial and friendly conversation with them.

	Present at the meeting were Yang Qicheng, Qiao Guanhua, Huang Zhong, and Chen Bo.

	Albanian Ambassador to China Vasili Natanaeli was also present at the meeting.

	After the meeting, Vice Premier Xie Fuzhi hosted a banquet to entertain the Albanian guests.

	The Albanian guests arrived in Beijing on the 5th.

Chinese and Albanian Volleyball Teams Hold Friendly Match

	According to a report from Xinhua News Agency on the 7th, today, more than 15,000 people in the capital, filled with revolutionary friendship, watched the friendly match between the Albanian Ministry of Interior "Dinamo" men's volleyball team and the Beijing men's volleyball team at the Beijing Workers' Gymnasium. The result of the match was a 3-1 victory for the home team.

### 谢副总理接见阿尔巴尼亚客人  中阿排球队举行友谊赛

1966-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

    谢副总理接见阿尔巴尼亚客人
    中阿排球队举行友谊赛
    据新华社八日讯　谢富治副总理今晚接见了阿尔巴尼亚“狄纳莫”体育俱乐部委员会副主席瓦西卡·阿费卓列以及由他率领的阿尔巴尼亚内务部“狄纳莫”男子排球队全体同志和正在我国访问的阿尔巴尼亚雕塑家安德烈阿·马纳，并且同他们进行了极为亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有杨奇清、乔冠华、黄中和陈播等。
    接见时，阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使瓦西里·纳塔奈利也在座。
    接见后，谢富治副总理设宴招待了阿尔巴尼亚客人。
    阿尔巴尼亚客人是五日到京的。
    据新华社七日讯　今天下午，首都一万五千多人满怀着革命的情谊，在北京工人体育馆观看了阿尔巴尼亚内务部“狄纳莫”男子排球队同北京男子排球队进行的友谊比赛。比赛结果，主队以三比一获胜。
