Title: Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Three U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-09-08
Edition: 5

Vietnamese military and civilians shot down three U.S. aircraft.

	NEW DELHI, Sept. 7 (Xinhua) — It was announced here today that on September 6, the military and civilians of Quang Ninh Province in northern Vietnam shot down one invading U.S. jet aircraft.

	According to additional information, the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province shot down a total of four invading U.S. aircraft on September 1 and 5 (including two that have already been announced).

### 越南军民击落美机三架

1966-09-08
第5版()
专栏：

    越南军民击落美机三架
    新华社河内七日电　据这里七日公布：越南北方广宁省军民六日击落入侵的美国喷气式飞机一架。
    另据补充消息，广平省军民在九月一日和五日共击落入侵的美国强盗飞机四架（其中两架已公布）。
