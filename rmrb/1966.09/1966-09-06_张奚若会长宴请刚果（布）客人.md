Title: Chairman Zhang Qirui Hosts Dinner for Guests from Congo (Brazzaville)
Author:
Date: 1966-09-06
Edition: 6

Chairman Zhang Qirui of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs hosted a dinner this evening to warmly welcome Mr. Aubert Lendja, a member of the Central Committee and Political Bureau of the National Revolutionary Movement of Congo (Brazzaville).

Both Zhang Qirui and Aubert Lendja delivered speeches at the dinner, praising the growing friendship between the peoples of China and Congo (Brazzaville). Zhang Qirui stated, "We firmly believe that the combat friendship between the peoples of China and Congo (Brazzaville) will continue to strengthen and develop in our common struggle against imperialism and old and new colonialism."

In his speech, Aubert Lendja condemned imperialism's attempts to strangle the independence of Congo (Brazzaville). He expressed full support for the Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. imperialist aggression and for the Chinese people's struggle to liberate Taiwan, which is sacred Chinese territory occupied by U.S. imperialism.

### 张奚若会长宴请刚果（布）客人

1966-09-06
第6版()
专栏：

    张奚若会长宴请刚果（布）客人
    据新华社五日讯　中国人民外交学会会长张奚若今晚举行宴会，热烈欢迎刚果（布）全国革命运动中央委员会委员、政治局委员奥贝·伦达。
    张奚若和奥贝·伦达先后在宴会上讲话，称颂中国和刚果（布）两国人民的战斗友谊不断增长。张奚若说，我们深信，中国刚果（布）两国人民的战斗友谊一定会在反对帝国主义和新、老殖民主义的共同斗争中日益巩固和发展。
    奥贝·伦达在讲话中谴责帝国主义千方百计地企图扼杀刚果（布）的独立。他表示毫无保留地支持越南人民反对美帝国主义侵略的斗争，支持中国人民解放被美帝国主义霸占的中国的神圣领土台湾。
