Title: Nanning's Industrial Production Sees Significant Growth
Author:
Date: 1966-09-19
Edition: 3

Nanning's industrial and transportation enterprises in Guangxi have ensured comprehensive leaps in industrial production while focusing on revolution and promoting production during the socialist education movement and the deepening of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

From January to August, the city's total industrial output value increased by 30 percent compared to the same period last year. The production of 54 types of products saw significant growth, and product quality has also improved. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 南宁工业生产大幅度增长

1966-09-19
第3版()
专栏：

    南宁工业生产大幅度增长
    广西南宁市工业交通企业在社会主义教育运动和深入开展的无产阶级文化大革命运动中，抓革命，促生产，保证了工业生产全面跃进。
      一至八月，全市工业总产值比去年同期提高百分之三十，五十四种产品产量大幅度增长，产品质量也有所提高。　（据新华社）
