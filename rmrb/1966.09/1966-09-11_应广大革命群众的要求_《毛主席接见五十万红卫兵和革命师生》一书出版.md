Title: In Response to the Broad Masses of Revolutionary People's Request, the Book "Chairman Mao Receives 500,000 Red Guards and Revolutionary Students" is Published
Author:
Date: 1966-09-11
Edition: 1

In response to the broad masses of revolutionary people's request, the book "Chairman Mao Receives 500,000 Red Guards and Revolutionary Students" is published.

	New China News Agency, September 10th: Our great teacher, great leader, great supreme commander, and great helmsman Chairman Mao, along with other central leaders, on August 31st, in the majestic Tiananmen Square of the capital, received 500,000 Red Guards and revolutionary students from all over the country and the capital. This greatly boosted the morale of the broad revolutionary masses. The People's Publishing House, in response to the broad revolutionary masses' request, has published the book "Chairman Mao Receives 500,000 Red Guards and Revolutionary Students." The contents include: two pages of photographs of Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Comrade Lin Biao greeting the revolutionary students, and Comrade Lin Biao's speech at the rally. It also includes the news of Chairman Mao receiving the Red Guards and revolutionary students, the speeches of Comrade Lin Biao and Comrade Zhou Enlai, and the "People's Daily" report "Carry the Spark of Revolution to the Entire Country." This book will be available for purchase at the Beijing Xinhua Bookstore starting from the 11th and will be printed in large quantities across the country.

### 应广大革命群众的要求  《毛主席接见五十万红卫兵和革命师生》一书出版

1966-09-11
第1版()
专栏：

    应广大革命群众的要求
    《毛主席接见五十万红卫兵和革命师生》一书出版
    新华社十日讯　我们伟大导师，伟大领袖，伟大统帅，伟大舵手毛主席，和中央其他领导同志，八月三十一日在首都雄伟的天安门广场，接见来自全国各地和首都的五十万名红卫兵和革命师生，极大地鼓舞了广大革命群众的斗志。人民出版社根据广大革命群众的要求，出版了《毛主席接见五十万红卫兵和革命师生》一书。内容有：毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪同志向革命师生致意和林彪同志在大会上讲话的像页两幅，毛主席接见红卫兵和革命师生的消息，林彪同志和周恩来同志的讲话，并附有《人民日报》的通讯《把革命的火种带到全国去》。这本书从十一日起在北京新华书店发行，并将在全国各地大量印行。
