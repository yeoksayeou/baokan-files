Title: Albanian Petroleum Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-09-06
Edition: 6

At the invitation of the Chinese government, an Albanian petroleum delegation led by Zenel Hamiti, Deputy Minister of the People's Republic of Albania's Ministry of Industry and Mining, arrived in Beijing by airplane this morning.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included Li Yingji, Tang Ke, Zhang Dingyi, and other relevant officials.

Vasili Natanaeli, the Albanian Ambassador to China, also went to the airport to greet the delegation.

### 阿尔巴尼亚石油代表团到京

1966-09-06
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚石油代表团到京
    新华社五日讯　应邀前来我国进行友好访问的阿尔巴尼亚石油代表团，在团长、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国工矿部副部长泽内尔·哈米蒂率领下，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有李应吉、唐克、张定一等有关方面负责人。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使瓦西里·纳塔奈利也到机场迎接。
