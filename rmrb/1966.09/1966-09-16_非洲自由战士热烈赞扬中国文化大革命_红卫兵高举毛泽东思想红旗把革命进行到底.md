Title: African Freedom Fighters Warmly Praise China's Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1966-09-16
Edition: 5

African friends warmly praise China's proletarian cultural revolution. In the world revolution's overall struggle, the cultural revolution is a necessary step.

The representative of the Basutoland Congress Party in Cairo, Mapafani, told a Xinhua News Agency reporter, "The Cultural Revolution guided by Mao Zedong Thought is a necessary step in the revolution to transform a world of human exploitation and in the overall struggle against imperialism led by the United States."

Mapafani pointed out, "Chairman Mao, unlike all the fake communists and self-proclaimed Marxist-Leninists, correctly points out that class struggle exists in socialist countries."

He said, "The Red Guards, as the vanguard of the cultural revolution, are all young people. After studying Chairman Mao's works, they rise up to rebel against old ideas and old customs. They are striving to create a new society without superstition, oppression, and exploitation."

Other freedom fighters from Basutoland in Cairo praised the Chinese Red Guards as excellent successors of the Chinese revolution. They emphasized that imperialism hopes to achieve a peaceful evolution among the younger generation in China, but Chinese youth are determined to hold high the red flag of Mao Zedong Thought and carry the revolution through to the end, making imperialism's vain dream impossible to realize.

A freedom fighter from Azania in Cairo asked a Xinhua News Agency reporter to convey his respect to the Chinese Red Guards. The cultural revolution unfolding in China is an important development of Marxism-Leninism.

The office of the Swaziland Progressive Party in Cairo issued a statement saying that the cultural revolution launched by the broad masses of the Chinese people, holding high the great red flag of Mao Zedong Thought, is the most important development of Marxism-Leninism and a heavy blow to all imperialists, revisionists, and reactionaries in the world.

The statement emphasized, "Mao Zedong Thought is the Marxism-Leninism of the new era where imperialism and revisionism head towards final collapse and socialism towards world victory. Mao Zedong Thought will definitely illuminate the world."

The statement refuted the reactionaries' slanders against the Chinese Red Guards. The statement said, "The young generation in China is determined to defend Marxism-Leninism and more effectively follow and apply the thoughts of Chairman Mao." With the great Mao Zedong Thought, people can achieve thorough liberation.

A young worker from Zanzibar told a Xinhua News Agency reporter, "The Cultural Revolution in China is an unprecedented great revolutionary movement. This is a new development of Marxism-Leninism principles."

He emphasized, "This cultural revolution is being carried out under the direct leadership of Mao Zedong, the wise successor of Marx and Lenin. We compare Chairman Mao's thoughts to the red sun rising from the east, which will illuminate the world." He said, "With the great Mao Zedong Thought, no demon or ghost can block the people from achieving their thorough liberation."

### 非洲自由战士热烈赞扬中国文化大革命  红卫兵高举毛泽东思想红旗把革命进行到底

1966-09-16
第5版()
专栏：

    非洲自由战士热烈赞扬中国文化大革命
    红卫兵高举毛泽东思想红旗把革命进行到底
    据新华社十五日讯　非洲各国朋友热烈赞扬中国无产阶级文化大革命。在世界革命的总斗争中文化大革命是必要步骤
    巴苏陀兰大会党驻开罗代表马帕法尼对新华社记者发表谈话说，以毛泽东思想为指导的中国文化大革命，“是进行改造人剥削人的世界的革命和反对以美国为首的帝国主义的总斗争所必须采取的步骤”。
    马帕法尼指出，“毛主席与一切假共产党人和自封的马克思列宁主义者不同，他正确地指出，社会主义国家中存在着阶级斗争。”
    他说：“作为文化革命先锋的红卫兵，都是青年人，他们学习了毛主席的著作后，就起来造旧思想和旧风俗的反。他们正在努力创造一个没有迷信、压迫和剥削的新社会。”
    在开罗的巴苏陀兰的其他自由战士赞扬中国红卫兵是中国革命的很好的接班人。他们强调指出，帝国主义希望在中国年青一代中间实现和平演变，然而中国青年决心高举毛泽东思想红旗，把革命进行到底，帝国主义的这种虚妄梦想是不可能实现的。
    阿扎尼亚的一位自由战士在开罗要求新华社记者把他的敬意转达给中国红卫兵。中国开展的文化大革命是马列主义的重要发展
    斯威士兰进步党驻开罗办事处发表声明说，中国广大人民群众高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗所开展的文化大革命，是马克思列宁主义最重要的发展，是对全世界一切帝国主义者、修正主义者和反动派的沉重打击。
    声明强调说：“毛泽东主席的思想是帝国主义和修正主义走向最后崩溃、社会主义走向全世界胜利的新时代的马克思列宁主义。毛泽东思想一定会照亮全世界。”
    声明驳斥了反动派对中国红卫兵的诽谤。声明说：“中国的青年一代决心捍卫马克思列宁主义，更有效地遵循和运用毛泽东主席的思想。”有了伟大的毛泽东思想人民就能取得彻底解放
    桑给巴尔的一位青年工作者对新华社记者发表谈话说：“中国文化大革命是史无前例的伟大革命运动。这是马克思列宁主义原则的新发展。”
    他强调指出：“这场文化大革命是在马克思和列宁的英明继承者毛泽东主席的直接领导下进行的。我们把毛主席思想比作红太阳，它从东方升起，将照亮全世界。”他说：“有了伟大的毛泽东思想，任何妖魔鬼怪都阻挡不了人民取得自己的彻底解放。”
