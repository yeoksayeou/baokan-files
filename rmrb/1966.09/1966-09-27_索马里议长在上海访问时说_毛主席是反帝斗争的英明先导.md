Title: Somali Speaker Says Chairman Mao is the Wise Leader of the Anti-Imperialist Struggle
Author:
Date: 1966-09-27
Edition: 6

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, September 25th: Somali National Assembly Speaker Sheikh Mukhtar Muhammad Hussein, during his visit here, enthusiastically praised Chairman Mao Zedong. He said: Chairman Mao Zedong is the wise leader of the anti-imperialist struggle. He is serving the people of Asia, Africa, and Latin America, and the people of the world. The Speaker also wished the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao Zedong, longevity.

	Sheikh Mukhtar Speaker made these remarks at a banquet held by Shanghai Mayor Cao Qiqiao for him and the Somali National Assembly Friendship Delegation he led on September 22nd. At the banquet, he spoke about his impressions of visiting China and said that the Chinese people firmly grasp Mao Zedong Thought and have a high level of political awareness.

	Cao Qiqiao, in his speech at the banquet, expressed the most resolute support for the anti-imperialist and anti-colonial struggles of the Somali people and the peoples of various African countries.

	The Somali National Assembly Friendship Delegation arrived in Shanghai by special plane from Guangzhou in the morning of September 22nd. Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Zhou Jianren and Mayor Cao Qiqiao and other leaders welcomed them warmly at the airport.

	The guests in Shanghai visited factories, rural people's communes, and hospitals. Sheikh Mukhtar Speaker, during his visit, enthusiastically praised the progress and achievements made by the Chinese people, saying that these were the results of acting according to the instructions of the Communist Party of China and Chairman Mao Zedong. After visiting the Sixth People's Hospital of Shanghai, he said, "What we have seen today in China's achievements in the field of medicine is something that no other country in the world can achieve. These achievements make the people of Asia and Africa proud."

### 索马里议长在上海访问时说  毛主席是反帝斗争的英明先导

1966-09-27
第6版()
专栏：

    索马里议长在上海访问时说
    毛主席是反帝斗争的英明先导
    据新华社上海二十五日电　索马里国民议会议长谢赫·穆克塔尔·穆罕默德·侯赛因在这里访问的时候热情赞扬毛泽东主席。他说：毛泽东主席是反帝斗争的英明先导，他是为亚非拉人民和世界人民服务的。议长还祝中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席万寿无疆。
    谢赫·穆克塔尔议长是二十二日在上海市市长曹荻秋为他和由他率领的索马里国民议会友好代表团举行的宴会上这样说的。他在宴会上谈到他访问中国的观感时还说，中国人民紧紧掌握了毛泽东思想，具有高度的政治觉悟。
    曹荻秋在宴会上讲话的时候表示最坚决地支持索马里人民和非洲各国人民的反帝反殖斗争。
    索马里国民议会友好代表团，是二十二日上午从广州乘专机到达上海的。人大常委会副委员长周建人和曹荻秋等负责人曾到机场热烈欢迎。
    客人们在上海参观了工厂、农村人民公社和医院等。谢赫·穆克塔尔议长在参观中热情赞扬中国人民所取得的进步和成就，是按照中国共产党和毛泽东主席指示办事的结果。他在参观上海市第六人民医院后说，我们今天所看到的中国在医学方面取得的成就，是世界上任何一国不可能达到的。这种成就，使亚非人民自豪。
