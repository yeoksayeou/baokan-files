Title: Prince Sihanouk Wires Congratulations on the Asian New Forces Games
Author:
Date: 1966-09-22
Edition: 5

Prince Norodom Sihanouk of Cambodia, in a message of congratulations to the first Asian New Forces Games to be held in Phnom Penh, said that the Games "will demonstrate the unity of the countries and peoples who are uncompromisingly fighting against the imperialist ambitions of American imperialism to dominate the world."

The message said: "The first Asian New Forces Games will solemnly demonstrate the unity and strength of the Asian peoples determined to advance along the road of progress, while certain big powers presume that only they can progress."

The message said: "I fervently hope that the peoples of the various Asian countries will send their respective athletes as representatives to participate in these Games, which will be dedicated to our fraternal unity."

The message expressed that Cambodia will make every effort to ensure the success of the first Asian New Forces Games in terms of organizational work. The message also expressed the hope that these Games will help promote the unity of the peoples of the various Asian countries in their struggle against imperialism.

### 西哈努克亲王电贺亚洲新运会  希望新运会促进亚洲人民在反帝斗争中的团结

1966-09-22
第5版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王电贺亚洲新运会
    希望新运会促进亚洲人民在反帝斗争中的团结
    据新华社金边二十一日电　柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王在给即将在金边举行的第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会的贺电中指出，这次运动会“将显示那些毫不妥协地同美帝国主义统治世界的野心进行斗争的国家和人民的团结。”
    贺电说：“第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会将庄严地显示决心在进步的道路上前进的亚洲人民的团结和力量，而某些大国竟然自以为只有它们才能进步。”
    贺电说：“我热烈希望，亚洲各国人民将派出他们各自的运动员作为代表参加这次运动会，这次运动会将致力于我们的兄弟般的团结。”
    贺电表示，柬埔寨将作出一切努力，从组织工作方面保证第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会的成功。贺电表示希望，这次运动会将有助于促进亚洲各国人民在反对帝国主义斗争中的团结。
