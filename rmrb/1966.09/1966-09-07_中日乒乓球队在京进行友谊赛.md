Title: Chinese and Japanese Ping-Pong Teams Hold Friendly Match in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-09-07
Edition: 6

Japanese men's and women's ping-pong teams held their first friendly match in China at the Beijing Workers' Gymnasium this evening against the Chinese men's and women's ping-pong teams. The Chinese men's and women's teams won with scores of 5-1 and 3-1, respectively.

In the men's team competition, Chinese player Zhang Xiaolin defeated Japanese men's singles champion Nagatani Nobuyuki with a score of 2-0, and also defeated Fukushima with a score of 2-0.

Chinese women's team members Li Li and Li Huiming both defeated Japanese players Yamazaki Kyoko and Morisawa Sachiko with scores of 2-0.

### 中日乒乓球队在京进行友谊赛

1966-09-07
第6版()
专栏：

    中日乒乓球队在京进行友谊赛
    据新华社六日讯　日本男、女乒乓球队今晚在北京工人体育馆同中国男、女乒乓球队进行访华首场友谊比赛。中国男女队分别以五比一和三比一的优势取得了胜利。
    在男子团体赛中，中国选手张燮林以二比○赢了日本男子单打冠军长谷川信彦，又以二比○的绝对优势战胜了福岛。
    中国女队的李力和李赫男分别以二比○赢了日本队的山中教子和森泽幸子。
