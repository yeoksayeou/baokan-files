Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Austrian Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-09-20
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi met with Austrian guests today.

New China News Agency, September 19th: Vice Premier Chen Yi met with Rudolf Sellinger, Chairman of the Austrian Federal Economic Chamber and Vice Chairman of the Austrian People's Party, and the delegation of the Austrian Federal Economic Chamber led by him this morning. They had a friendly conversation.

Attending the meeting were Nan Hanchen and others.

Schmidt, the representative of the Austrian Federal Economic Chamber in Beijing, was also present. (Photo attached)

The photo shows a group photo taken during the meeting. (Photo by New China News Agency reporter)

### 陈毅副总理接见奥地利客人

1966-09-20
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见奥地利客人
    新华社十九日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了奥地利联邦商会主席、奥地利人民党副主席鲁道夫·赛林格，和由他率领的奥地利联邦商会代表团成员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有南汉宸等。
    奥地利联邦商会驻北京代表施密特也在座。（附图片）
   图为接见时合影。　新华社记者摄
