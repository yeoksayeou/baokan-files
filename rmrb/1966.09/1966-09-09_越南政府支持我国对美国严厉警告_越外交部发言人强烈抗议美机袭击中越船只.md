Title: Vietnamese Government Supports China's Severe Warning to the United States
Author:
Date: 1966-09-09
Edition: 4

Vietnamese Government Supports China's Severe Warning to the United States

Vietnamese Foreign Ministry Spokesperson Strongly Protests U.S. Aircraft Attack on Chinese-Vietnamese Ships

According to Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 8: The spokesperson for the Foreign Ministry of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam issued a statement on September 8, strongly protesting the recent frequent bombings and strafing of Vietnamese ships by U.S. bandit aircraft. Furthermore, on August 29, they brazenly attacked a Chinese cargo ship sailing in the Gulf of Tonkin.

The statement said: "The aforementioned war crimes by the United States are extremely arrogant violations of the sovereignty of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and blatantly violate the principles of international law regarding free trade and free passage in the territorial waters of various countries, obstructing the entry and exit of foreign merchant ships from the ports of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam."

The statement pointed out that the U.S. aircraft's attack on Chinese ships is a new and serious step in the U.S. conspiracy to provoke war against the People's Republic of China.

The statement said: "The Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam once again strongly protests against the aforementioned criminal actions by the United States and declares that the United States must bear full responsibility for all consequences arising therefrom."

The statement said that the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam fully supports the severe warning and correct stance of the spokesperson for the Ministry of National Defense of the People's Republic of China regarding the U.S. attack on two Chinese cargo ships. The statement demands that the United States must stop attacking Vietnamese ships and must cease all provocations, obstructions, threats, and bombings of foreign merchant ships entering and exiting the ports of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

### 越南政府支持我国对美国严厉警告  越外交部发言人强烈抗议美机袭击中越船只

1966-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

    越南政府支持我国对美国严厉警告
    越外交部发言人强烈抗议美机袭击中越船只
    据新华社河内八日电　越南民主共和国外交部发言人八日发表声明，强烈抗议美国强盗飞机最近不断轰炸和扫射越南的船只，并且在八月二十九日悍然袭击在北部湾海域行驶的中国货船。
    声明说：“美国的上述战争罪行，极其蛮横地侵犯了越南民主共和国的主权，粗暴地违反了国际法关于在各国领海上进行自由贸易和自由来往的原则，阻挠各国商船出入越南民主共和国港口。”
    声明指出，美国飞机对中国船只的袭击是美国对中华人民共和国进行战争挑衅阴谋的一个新的严重步骤。
    声明说：“越南民主共和国政府再一次强烈抗议美国的上述罪恶行动，并且宣布，美国必须对由此而引起的一切后果承担全部责任。”
    声明说，越南民主共和国政府完全支持中华人民共和国政府国防部发言人就美国对中国的两艘货船发起攻击一事提出的严厉警告和采取的正确立场。声明要求美国必须停止袭击越南船只，必须停止对出入越南民主共和国港口的外国商船的挑衅、阻挠、威胁和轰炸的一切行动。
