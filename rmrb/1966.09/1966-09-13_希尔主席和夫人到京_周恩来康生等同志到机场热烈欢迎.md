Chairman Hill and His Wife Arrive in Beijing
Comrade Zhou Enlai, Kang Sheng and Other Comrades Warmly Welcome Them at the Airport

Xinhua News Agency, September 12th: Chairman E.F. Hill of the Communist Party of Australia (Marxist-Leninists) and his wife, Jo Hill, arrived in Beijing by plane today in the afternoon. They were invited by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to visit China on a friendly tour.

Comrade Zhou Enlai, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Comrade Kang Sheng, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and his wife Cao Yiyou, Comrade Liu Ningyi, a member of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Comrade Zhao Yimin, an alternate member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and other comrades warmly welcomed them at the airport. Young Pioneers presented fresh flowers to Chairman Hill and his wife.

### 希尔主席和夫人到京  周恩来康生等同志到机场热烈欢迎

1966-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

    希尔主席和夫人到京
    周恩来康生等同志到机场热烈欢迎
    新华社十二日讯  澳大利亚共产党（马克思列宁主义者）主席爱·弗·希尔同志和夫人乔·希尔，应中国共产党中央委员会邀请来我国进行友好访问，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    中共中央政治局常委周恩来同志，中共中央政治局常委康生同志和夫人曹轶欧，中共中央书记处书记刘宁一，中共中央候补委员赵毅敏等同志，到机场热烈欢迎。少先队员向希尔主席和夫人献了鲜花。
