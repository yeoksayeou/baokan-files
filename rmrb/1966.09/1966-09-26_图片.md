Title: Medical Cadets from a Certain Military Veterinary Academy Participate in Labor and Promote Mao Zedong Thought
Author:
Date: 1966-09-26
Edition: 2

Cadets and faculty from a certain military veterinary academy of the People's Liberation Army came to the Yonghong Sino-Romanian Friendship People's Commune to participate in labor. During their rest time, they visited poor and lower-middle peasants, promoting Mao Zedong Thought. This is a scene of the cadets presenting Chairman Mao's quotations to the poor peasant and military family member Wang Yuzhen, studying Chairman Mao's quotations together.
