Title: Vice Premier Chen Meets with Delegation from the France-China Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1966-09-02
Edition: 3

Vice Premier Chen Yi met with a delegation from the France-China Friendship Association this morning. The delegation was led by Marcel Serruys, Vice Chairman of the French Association for Friendship with China. They engaged in friendly discussions. The delegation arrived in Beijing on August 28.

### 陈副总理接见法中友协代表团

1966-09-02
第3版()
专栏：

    陈副总理接见法中友协代表团
    据新华社一日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今天上午接见由法国法中友好协会副主席马赛尔·赛里雪罗率领的法中友协访华代表团，同他们进行了友好的谈话。代表团是八月二十八日到北京的。
