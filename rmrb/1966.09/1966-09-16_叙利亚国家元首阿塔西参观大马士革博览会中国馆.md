Title: Syrian Head of State Atassi Visits the Chinese Pavilion at the Damascus Fair
Author:
Date: 1966-09-16
Edition: 5

Syrian Head of State Nureddin Atassi visited the Chinese pavilion at the 13th Damascus International Fair on the evening of September 10, accompanied by Chinese Ambassador to Syria Chen Tan.

When Atassi saw a photo of Chairman Mao swimming in the Yangtze River, he said, "Chairman Mao not only has good health but also a great and strong mind."

After the visit, Atassi wrote in the guest book:

"I am honored to see the great achievements of the friendly Chinese people in socialist construction.

"I am proud of the Chinese people's resolute opposition to imperialism and colonialism.

"Long live the friendship between the Syrian and Chinese peoples in their common struggle against imperialism and colonialism!"

### 叙利亚国家元首阿塔西参观大马士革博览会中国馆

1966-09-16
第5版()
专栏：

    叙利亚国家元首阿塔西
    参观大马士革博览会中国馆
    新华社大马士革电　叙利亚国家元首努尔丁·阿塔西十日晚上在中国驻叙利亚大使陈坦陪同下参观了第十三届大马士革国际博览会的中国馆。
    当阿塔西看到毛主席在长江中游泳的照片时说：毛主席不但身体健康，而且有伟大而坚强的思想。
    参观后，阿塔西在留言簿上写道：
    我荣幸地看到友好的中国人民在社会主义建设中取得的伟大成就。
    我为中国人民坚决反对帝国主义和殖民主义而感到自豪。
    叙、中两国人民在反对帝国主义和殖民主义的共同斗争中的友谊万岁！
