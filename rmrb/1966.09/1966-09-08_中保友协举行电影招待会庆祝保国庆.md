Title: The China-Bulgaria Friendship Association Holds a Film Screening to Celebrate Bulgaria's National Day
Author:
Date: 1966-09-08
Edition: 4

The China-Bulgaria Friendship Association held a film screening in the afternoon today, showing the Chinese large-scale art documentary "Song of Reclamation," to celebrate Bulgaria's National Day. Bulgarian Ambassador to China Stoichev and diplomatic officials were invited to attend the screening.

### 中保友协举行电影招待会庆祝保国庆

1966-09-08
第4版()
专栏：

    中保友协举行电影招待会庆祝保国庆
    新华社七日讯　中国保加利亚友好协会今天下午举行电影招待会，放映中国大型艺术纪录片《军垦战歌》，庆祝保加利亚国庆。保加利亚驻中国大使斯托伊切夫和外交官员应邀出席了招待会。
