Title: The People's Demand: The Simplified Chinese Edition of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" Will Be Mass-Produced
Author:
Date: 1966-09-04
Edition: 1

The People's Publishing House, in response to the widespread demand of the masses and with the approval of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, has decided to publish a simplified Chinese edition of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong." The typesetting for this edition has been completed. A large number of printing plates have been sent to various parts of the country, and mass production has begun.

The typesetting, proofreading, and plate-making work for this new edition has been strongly supported by the cadres and workers of relevant publishing, printing, and plate-making units in Beijing and Shanghai. This honorable political task was successfully completed in just two months.

### 根据广大群众的要求  《毛泽东选集》简体字横排本将大量出版

1966-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

    根据广大群众的要求
    《毛泽东选集》简体字横排本将大量出版
    新华社三日讯　人民出版社根据广大群众的要求，经中共中央批准，决定出版的《毛泽东选集》简体字横排本，已经完成制版任务。大批纸型已经陆续发往全国各地，开始大量印行。
    新排本的排校制型工作，得到北京、上海有关出版、印刷和制型单位广大干部和工人的大力支援，在短短两个月内就胜利完成了这项光荣的政治任务。
