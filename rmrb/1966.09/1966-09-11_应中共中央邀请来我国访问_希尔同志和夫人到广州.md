Title: Hill and Wife Arrive in Guangzhou on Invitation of CPC Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1966-09-11
Edition: 1

At the invitation of the CPC Central Committee, Comrade E.F. Hill, Chairman of the Australian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninists), and his wife arrived in Guangzhou by train this afternoon for a friendly visit to China.

Comrade Zhao Ziyang, First Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the CPC, and Comrade Lin Liming, Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the CPC, and others, warmly welcomed the guests at the station. Young Pioneers presented flowers to the distinguished guests. Tonight, Zhao Ziyang held a banquet to warmly welcome Comrade Hill and his wife.

### 应中共中央邀请来我国访问  希尔同志和夫人到广州

1966-09-11
第1版()
专栏：

    应中共中央邀请来我国访问
    希尔同志和夫人到广州
    新华社广州十日电　澳大利亚共产党（马克思列宁主义者）主席爱·弗·希尔和夫人，应中共中央邀请来我国进行友好访问，今天下午乘火车到达广州。
    中共广东省委第一书记赵紫阳、中共广东省委书记处书记林李明等，到车站热烈欢迎。少先队员向贵宾献了鲜花。今晚，赵紫阳举行宴会热烈欢迎希尔同志和夫人。
