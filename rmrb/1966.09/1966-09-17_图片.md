Title: Chairman Mao, We Have Come from All Directions
Author: 
Date: 1966-09-17
Edition: 4

Chairman Mao, today, we have come from all over the country to be by your side. We feel incredibly excited and incredibly fortunate. We will forever read your books, listen to your words, and act according to your instructions. We will follow you in making revolution for the rest of our lives!

    Photograph by our reporter (All photos on this page, except those taken by our reporter, are by Xinhua News Agency reporters)
