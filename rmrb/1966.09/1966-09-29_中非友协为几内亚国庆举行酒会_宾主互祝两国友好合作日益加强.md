Title: The China-Africa Friendship Association Holds a Reception for Guinea's National Day
Author:
Date: 1966-09-29
Edition: 6

The China-Africa Friendship Association held a reception today to celebrate the national day of the Republic of Guinea. The Chargé d'Affaires of the Guinea Embassy in China, Kamara Fouti Issiaka, said in his speech, "We extend our warm and fraternal congratulations to the heroic Chinese people for the tremendous achievements made under the leadership of the Communist Party of China and the revered Chairman Mao Zedong, despite the most arduous conditions."

Chargé d'Affaires Kamara also said that the friendship between Guinea and China is based on a firm and uncompromising desire to fight against imperialism and colonialism. Therefore, the friendly cooperative relationship between the two countries is becoming increasingly stronger.

Vice Chairman of the China-Africa Friendship Association, Ding Xilin, spoke first at the reception. He discussed the new developments in the current African national liberation movement, pointing out that the peoples of China and Guinea will further join hands and advance side by side in completing their common tasks of anti-imperialism, anti-colonialism, and building their own countries. The wife of the Guinea Ambassador to China, Kamara Segu, and the Guinea writer N'K. Koundet, who is visiting Beijing, were also invited to attend the reception.

### 中非友协为几内亚国庆举行酒会  宾主互祝两国友好合作日益加强

1966-09-29
第6版()
专栏：

    中非友协为几内亚国庆举行酒会
    宾主互祝两国友好合作日益加强
    据新华社二十八日讯  中非友协今天为几内亚共和国国庆举行了酒会。几内亚驻中国大使馆临时代办卡马拉·福台·伊西阿卡在讲话中说，“我们向英雄的中国人民在中国共产党和崇敬的毛泽东主席领导下，在最艰苦的条件下所取得的超越我们时代的巨大进展，表示热烈的、兄弟般的祝贺”。
    卡马拉临时代办还说，几中两国之间的友谊建立在坚决地、毫不妥协地反帝、反殖的共同愿望的基础之上，因此，几中两国的友好合作关系正在日益加强。
    中非友协副会长丁西林在酒会上首先讲话，他谈到当前非洲民族解放运动的新发展，指出中几两国人民在今后完成反帝、反殖以及建设自己国家的共同任务中，必将进一步携起手来，并肩前进。几内亚驻中国大使卡马拉·塞古的夫人，正在北京访问的几内亚作家聂·卡·孔德特也应邀出席了酒会。
