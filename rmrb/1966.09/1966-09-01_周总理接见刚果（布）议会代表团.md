Title: Premier Zhou Receives the Congolese (Brazzaville) Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-09-01
Edition: 3

Premier Zhou today received the President of the National Assembly of the Congo (Brazzaville) Mouyabi Andre George and his wife, as well as the entire delegation of the Congolese (Brazzaville) Parliament led by him. They had a friendly and cordial conversation.

Attending the reception were Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Liu Ningyi and his wife Li Shuying, Deputy Secretary-General Lian Guan, Deputy Director of the African Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Xie Feng, and others.

The Charge d'Affaires of the Congolese (Brazzaville) Embassy in China, Jean Daniel Bakula, was also present at the reception.

### 周总理接见刚果（布）议会代表团

1966-09-01
第3版()
专栏：

    周总理接见刚果（布）议会代表团
    新华社三十一日讯周恩来总理今天下午接见刚果（布）国民议会议长穆亚比·安德烈·乔治和夫人，以及由他率领的刚果（布）议会代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有人大常委会副委员长刘宁一和夫人李淑英，副秘书长连贯，外交部非洲司副司长谢丰等。
    接见时，刚果（布）驻中国大使馆临时代办让·丹尼尔·巴库拉也在座。
