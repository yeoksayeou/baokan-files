Title: U.S. Warships and Aircraft Intrude into China's Guangdong Territorial Waters and Airspace
Author:
Date: 1966-09-27
Edition: 6

U.S. warships and aircraft intrude into China's Guangdong territorial waters and airspace
China issues the 412th serious warning

	NEW YORK, Sept. 25 (Xinhua) -- At 8:43 a.m. to 12:12 p.m. on Sept. 25, a U.S. warship intruded into China's territorial waters off Pinghai and Shanwei in Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 10:00 a.m. to 10:11 a.m., a U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over Wenchang County, northeast of Hainan Island, Guangdong Province. From 1:00 p.m. to 1:28 p.m. on the same day, another U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island and Dong Island in the Xisha Islands, Guangdong Province. In response to these military provocations by U.S. warships and aircraft, the spokesperson for China's Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the Ministry, issued the 412th serious warning.

### 美舰美机侵入我广东领海领空  我提出第四百一十二次严重警告

1966-09-27
第6版()
专栏：

    美舰美机侵入我广东领海领空
    我提出第四百一十二次严重警告
    新华社二十五日讯　九月二十五日八时四十三分至十二时十二分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我广东省平海、汕尾以南地区领海。同日十时至十时十一分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省海南岛文昌县东北地区领海上空。同日十三时至十三时二十八分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛、东岛地区领空。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百一十二次严重警告。
