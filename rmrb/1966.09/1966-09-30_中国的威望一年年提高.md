Title: China's Prestige Grows Year by Year
Author: Fēnguǒ Sīdēng
Date: 1966-09-30
Edition: 5

All the honest and peace-loving people of the world are cheering for China's great revolution. No lies, distortions, or deceptions can erase the achievements of the People's Republic of China. Despite the modern revisionists launching an anti-China campaign, the prestige of the People's Republic of China is growing year by year.

### 中国的威望一年年提高
芬果司登
1966-09-30
第5版()
专栏：国外来信

    中国的威望一年年提高
    全世界所有正直的爱好和平的人民都为中国伟大的革命而欢呼。任何谎言、歪曲和欺骗都不能抹煞人民中国的成就。尽管现代修正主义发起反华运动，而人民中国的威望却在一年年地提高。
            〔美国〕芬果司登
