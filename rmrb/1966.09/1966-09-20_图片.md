Title: Beijing's Chaoyang District: Enthusiastic Study of Chairman Mao's Works
Author:
Date: 1966-09-20
Edition: 4

Beijing's Chaoyang District, Wa Li People's Commune, Longwangtan Production Brigade's active participants in the study of Chairman Mao's works, Wang Hai (right) and Chen Xiuying (left), have obtained a copy of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong." They treasure it as a precious gem and immediately begin to read it.
