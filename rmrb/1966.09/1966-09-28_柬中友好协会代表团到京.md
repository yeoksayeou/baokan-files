Title: Delegation of the Cambodia-China Friendship Association Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-09-28
Edition: 4

At the invitation of the China-Cambodia Friendship Association, a delegation from the Cambodia-China Friendship Association, led by its chairman, Lan Neth, arrived in Beijing by airplane on the 27th. Vice-chairmen Zhang Qixian and Zhou Yiping of the China-Cambodia Friendship Association went to the airport to warmly welcome the Cambodian guests.

### 柬中友好协会代表团到京

1966-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

    柬中友好协会代表团到京
    据新华社讯　应中柬友好协会邀请来我国进行友好访问并参加国庆活动的柬中友好协会代表团，在团长、柬中友好协会主席兰·涅特率领下，二十七日乘飞机到达北京。中柬友协副会长张茜、周一萍等前往机场热烈欢迎柬埔寨客人。
