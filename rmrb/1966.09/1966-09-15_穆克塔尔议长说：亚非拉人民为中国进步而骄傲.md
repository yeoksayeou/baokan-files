Title: Chairman Mukhtar: Asian, African, and Latin American Peoples Are Proud of China's Progress
Author:
Date: 1966-09-15
Edition: 5

Chairman Mukhtar: Asian, African, and Latin American Peoples Are Proud of China's Progress

According to Xinhua News Agency, on the 14th, Chairman Sheikh Mukhtar Muhammad Hussein of the Somali National Assembly said this afternoon during his visit to the Beijing Second Machine Tool Factory: "The peoples of Asia, Africa, and Latin America are proud of the progress made by the Chinese people. They regard your achievements as their own achievements."

He said that currently, many peoples in the Asia, Africa, and Latin America regions are still suffering from the oppression of imperialism and colonialism, and now the only help they can find is from China. He also praised the Chinese people for insisting on self-reliance in national construction and wished the Chinese workers and the peoples of Asia, Africa, and Latin America to march forward together.

The friendly delegation of the Somali National Assembly also visited the Beijing Electron Tube Factory this morning. After the visit, they expressed admiration for the spirit of Chinese workers who are fully dedicated to promoting production and improving technology.

Yesterday morning, the Somali guests, accompanied by Vice Chairman Yang Mingxuan of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, visited the Beijing Institute of Physical Education and watched a gymnastics performance.

### 穆克塔尔议长说：亚非拉人民为中国进步而骄傲

1966-09-15
第5版()
专栏：

    穆克塔尔议长说：亚非拉人民为中国进步而骄傲
    据新华社十四日讯　索马里国民议会议长谢赫·穆克塔尔·穆罕默德·侯赛因今天下午参观北京第二机床厂时说：“亚非拉人民对中国人民取得的进步感到骄傲。因为他们把你们的成绩看作是他们自己的成绩。”
    他说，现在亚非拉地区还有许多人民在遭受帝国主义和殖民主义的奴役，而现在唯一可以找到帮助他们的就是中国。他还赞扬中国人民在国家建设中坚持自力更生，并祝愿中国工人和亚非拉人民共同前进。
    索马里国民议会友好代表团今天上午还参观了北京电子管厂，参观后他们对中国工人全力促进生产和改进技术的精神表示敬佩。
    昨天上午，索马里客人还在人大常委会副委员长杨明轩陪同下，参观了北京体育学院，观看了体操表演。
