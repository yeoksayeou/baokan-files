Title: PLA Soldiers Follow Chairman Mao's Instructions, Maintaining and Carrying Forward the Glorious Tradition of Being Both a Fighting Force, a Work Team, and a Production Team
Author:
Date: 1966-09-28
Edition: 3

The People's Liberation Army of China has always adhered to and carried forward the glorious tradition of being a fighting force, a work team, and a production team under the guidance of Chairman Mao. This is a scene of soldiers from a unit of the Guangzhou Military Region studying Chairman Mao's works during their production and rest time.
