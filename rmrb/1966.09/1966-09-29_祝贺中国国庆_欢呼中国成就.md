Title: Celebrate China's National Day and Hail China's Achievements
Author:
Date: 1966-09-29
Edition: 6

Albanian people in various places hold celebrations; Hanoi workers launch labor competition

	PHOTO: Albanian workers celebrate China's National Day.

	PHOTO: Vietnamese workers compete in labor to celebrate China's National Day.

	PHOTO: Cambodian newspaper praises China as an unshakable fortress and a reliable friend of the people of all countries.

	From New China News Agency, Tirana, September 28

	Albania has been holding various activities nationwide since September 26 to enthusiastically celebrate the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

	The famous Skrapar Electric Wire Factory held a celebration meeting on September 26. Attending the celebration were the First Secretary of the Skrapar District Party Committee, Kostas, the Economic Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, Li Mingshan, and Chinese experts working in Skrapar.

	Factory Director Dibra, in his speech, enthusiastically praised the great thought of Mao Zedong. He pointed out that the Chinese people, armed with Mao Zedong Thought, are a great example for all the people of the world. No force in the world can stop the advance of the Chinese giant. Dibra said that the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China has shattered all the dreams of the imperialists and modern revisionists to restore capitalism in China. He said, "We are particularly pleased with the great achievements of the Chinese people and are greatly inspired by them."

	The workers present repeatedly shouted slogans such as "Enver-Hoxha and Mao Zedong," "Friendship is guaranteed," and so on.

	The members of the Rembe Agricultural Production Cooperative in Korça District and the teachers and students of the "November 8th" 12-year school also held celebration meetings on September 26.

	From New China News Agency, Hanoi, September 28

	In recent days, workers in many factories and enterprises in Hanoi, the capital of Vietnam, have launched labor competitions to celebrate the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

	Despite frequent harassment and attacks by American aggressor planes on Hanoi, the workers have not been intimidated by the American aggressors. On September 20, the responsible person of the Dong Xuan Knitting Factory issued a call to all workers to launch a labor competition to celebrate China's National Day. Subsequently, various workshops and production teams discussed and set specific targets, determined to achieve the best results to welcome China's National Day. Every day, the factory's wired broadcast continuously reports on the great achievements of the Chinese people under the leadership of the great leader Chairman Mao in various fields.

	The Red River Stationery Factory launched a labor competition to celebrate China's National Day in September. The workers of the Hai Phong Food Processing and Candy Factory, in addition to focusing on production, also actively participate in political study, improve political awareness, and respond to Chairman Ho Chi Minh's call to fight to the end with courage and sacrifice. In addition, the workers of the Thinh Phat, Lien Thong, and Tai Nguyen rubber shoe factories, as well as the Hanoi Pharmaceutical Factory and the Phuong Dong Chemical Factory, are also launching labor competitions to celebrate China's National Day.

	From New China News Agency, Phnom Penh, September 28

	The Cambodian newspaper "Cambodia News" published an editorial titled "Unbreakable Friendship" on September 27, enthusiastically celebrating the upcoming National Day of the People's Republic of China. The editorial said: "For all those who believe that China's liberation is their own hope for liberation, October is also their festival." The editorial pointed out that China is an unshakable fortress. China is the hope of the people who seek freedom. China opposes all aggression and is a reliable friend and strong backing for its neighboring countries that have suffered aggression. The editorial reaffirmed Cambodia's friendship with China. The editorial said: "We trust China. China is a great country with unlimited prestige, and we will always love, respect, and admire China."

### 祝贺中国国庆　欢呼中国成就

1966-09-29
第6版()
专栏：

    祝贺中国国庆　欢呼中国成就
    阿尔巴尼亚各地群众举行庆祝活动　越南首都职工展开劳动竞赛
    柬报说中国是坚不可摧的堡垒，是各国人民的可靠朋友
    据新华社地拉那二十八日电　阿尔巴尼亚全国各地从二十六日起举办各种活动，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立十七周年。
    著名的斯库台电线厂全体职工二十六日举行了庆祝集会。参加庆祝会的有劳动党斯库台区委员会第一书记科斯塔、中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆经济参赞李鸣山以及在斯库台工作的中国专家。
    电线厂厂长迪布拉在讲话中热烈赞扬了伟大的毛泽东思想。他指出，用毛泽东思想武装起来的中国人民是全世界人民的伟大榜样。世界上没有任何力量能够阻挡中国巨人前进。迪布拉说，中国无产阶级文化大革命粉碎了帝国主义者、现代修正主义者企图在中国复辟资本主义的一切梦想。他说，我们对中国人民取得的伟大成就感到特别的高兴，并从中得到了巨大鼓舞。
    与会的职工们一再高呼“恩维尔—毛泽东”、“友谊是保证”等口号。
    科尔察区雷姆贝农业生产合作社社员和“十一月八日”十二年制的学校的师生也在二十六日分别举行了庆祝会。
    据新华社河内二十八日电　越南首都河内市许多工厂和企业的广大职工，最近纷纷发起劳动竞赛，以庆祝中华人民共和国建国十七周年。
    尽管美国强盗飞机经常对河内进行骚扰和袭击，职工们并没有被美国强盗所吓倒。九月二十日，冬春针织厂负责人向全体职工发出了开展向中国国庆节献礼竞赛的号召。接着，各车间、各生产组纷纷进行讨论，人人订出具体的指标，决心以最好的成绩来迎接中国国庆。每天，工厂里的有线广播还不断地播送中国人民在伟大的领袖毛主席领导下，各方面取得的伟大成就。
    红河文具厂进入九月份就掀起了向中国国庆节献礼的劳动竞赛运动。海州面食加工和糖果厂的职工们在竞赛中，除搞好生产外，还积极参加政治学习，提高政治思想认识，以实际行动响应胡志明主席的战斗号召，不怕艰苦、牺牲，坚决战斗到最后胜利。此外，至行、利盛、再生等胶鞋厂和河内制药厂、前锋化工厂等厂职工也正在开展庆祝中国国庆的劳动竞赛。
    据新华社金边二十八日电　《柬埔寨电讯报》二十七日发表题为《牢不可破的友谊》的社论，热烈祝贺即将来临的中华人民共和国国庆。社论说：“对于所有认为中国的解放就是自己解放的希望的人来说，十月也是他们的节日。”社论指出，中国是坚不可克的堡垒。中国是那些要求自由的人民的希望。中国反对一切侵略，是它的遭受侵略的邻国的可靠朋友，是它们的坚强后盾。社论重申柬埔寨对中国的友谊。社论说：“我们信赖中国。中国是个伟大的，享有无限威望的国家，我们永远热爱、尊敬和钦佩中国。”
