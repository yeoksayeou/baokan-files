Title: Liao Chengzhi Hosts Dinner for Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-09-02
Edition: 3

Liao Chengzhi hosted a dinner tonight to welcome a delegation from the Japanese Liberal Democratic Party, including members of the Japanese Diet such as Koi Jizhi, Xiao Xian Tai, Jiang Qi Cheng, and Fudai Yi, among others.

### 廖承志宴请日本客人

1966-09-02
第3版()
专栏：

    廖承志宴请日本客人
    据新华社一日讯　廖承志今晚设宴招待日本自由民主党国会议员访华团古井喜实、小坂善太郎、江崎真澄、福田一等日本人士。
