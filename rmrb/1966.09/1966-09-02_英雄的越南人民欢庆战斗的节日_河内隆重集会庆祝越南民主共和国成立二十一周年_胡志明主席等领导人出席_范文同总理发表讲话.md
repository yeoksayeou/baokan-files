Title: Heroic Vietnamese People Celebrate the Battle Day
Author:
Date: 1966-09-02
Edition: 3

Hanoi held a grand rally to celebrate the 21st anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Chairman Ho Chi Minh and other leaders attended. Prime Minister Pham Van Dong delivered a speech.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 1, more than a thousand people from all walks of life in Hanoi gathered in a spirit of struggle and victory to celebrate the 21st anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam (September 2).

Chairman Ho Chi Minh and other Vietnamese party and government leaders, including Le Duan, Le Duong, Pham Van Dong, Vo Nguyen Giap, Nguyen Duy Trinh, Ton Duc Thang, and Phan Khac Su, attended the meeting.

Chinese Ambassador Zhu Qifeng and other foreign diplomats stationed in Vietnam were also invited to attend the meeting.

Prime Minister Pham Van Dong spoke at the meeting. He, on behalf of the Vietnamese Labor Party and the government, warmly praised the great victories achieved by the military and people of South and North Vietnam in the anti-American national salvation struggle.

Prime Minister Pham Van Dong strongly condemned the U.S. imperialists for intensifying and expanding the war of aggression against Vietnam while playing the "peace talk" trick. He emphasized that the U.S. imperialists must stop the war of aggression against Vietnam and withdraw all U.S. troops and their mercenaries from Vietnam, and then peace will be immediately restored. Prime Minister Pham Van Dong reaffirmed the four-point proposal of the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the five-point declaration of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam.

Prime Minister Pham Van Dong said that Chairman Ho's call for resistance against the Americans and national salvation has been brilliantly, strongly, and deeply embodied the will of the Vietnamese people nationwide. This is the action program for the heroic Vietnamese nation's struggle for national salvation against the Americans!

Prime Minister Pham Van Dong called on all Vietnamese people to enthusiastically respond to Chairman Ho's sacred call for resistance against the Americans and national salvation issued on July 17, and to be determined to fight for the complete victory of the anti-American national salvation struggle for the independence, freedom, and socialism of the motherland.

After Prime Minister Pham Van Dong's speech, the entire audience enthusiastically shouted battle slogans.

The celebration rally ended with the entire audience singing the battle song "Liberate the South."

According to Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 1, Prime Minister Pham Van Dong of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam held a reception in Hanoi to warmly celebrate the 21st anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Chairman Ho Chi Minh and other Vietnamese party and government leaders, including Le Duan, Le Duong, Vo Nguyen Giap, Nguyen Duy Trinh, Nguyen Chi Thanh, and Phan Khac Su, attended the reception.

Attending the reception were also the combat heroes of the Vietnamese People's Army and labor models.

Chinese Ambassador Zhu Qifeng and other foreign diplomats stationed in Vietnam were also invited to attend the reception.

### 英雄的越南人民欢庆战斗的节日  河内隆重集会庆祝越南民主共和国成立二十一周年  胡志明主席等领导人出席　范文同总理发表讲话

1966-09-02
第3版()
专栏：

    英雄的越南人民欢庆战斗的节日
    河内隆重集会庆祝越南民主共和国成立二十一周年
    胡志明主席等领导人出席　范文同总理发表讲话
    据新华社河内一日电　河内市各界人民一千多人，在战斗和胜利的气氛中隆重集会，热烈庆祝越南民主共和国成立二十一周年（九月二日）。
    胡志明主席和黎笋、长征、范文同、范雄、武元甲、阮维桢、孙德胜、潘继遂等越南党政领导人出席了大会。
    中国大使朱其文和各国驻越南的使节也应邀出席了大会。
    范文同总理在会上讲了话。他代表越南劳动党和政府热烈赞扬越南南方和北方军民在抗美救国斗争中取得的伟大胜利。
    范文同总理严厉谴责美帝国主义一面极力加强和扩大侵略越南战争，一面玩弄“和谈”骗局。他强调指出：美帝国主义必须停止侵略越南的战争，必须把美国军队和它的帮凶军全部撤出越南，这样和平就会立即恢复。范文同总理重申越南民主共和国政府的四项主张和越南南方民族解放阵线的五点声明。
    范文同总理说，胡主席的抗美救国战斗号召，已经光辉地强烈地和深刻地体现了越南全国人民的意志。这是英雄的越南民族的抗美救国事业的行动纲领！
    范文同总理号召全体越南人民热烈响应胡主席七月十七日发出的抗美救国神圣号召，为争取祖国的独立、自由和社会主义，而决心战斗到取得抗美救国斗争的完全胜利。
    在范文同总理讲话后，全场热烈高呼战斗口号。
    庆祝大会在全场高唱《解放南方》的战歌声中结束。
    新华社河内一日电　越南民主共和国总理范文同在河内举行招待会，热烈庆祝越南民主共和国成立二十一周年。
    胡志明主席和黎笋、长征、范雄、武元甲、阮维桢、黄文欢、潘继遂等越南党政领导人出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的还有越南人民军的战斗英雄和劳动模范。
    中国大使朱其文和各国驻越南的使节也应邀出席了招待会。
