Title: New Zealand Communist Party General Secretary Wilcox Enthusiastically Hails the Chinese Cultural Revolution as Benefiting the World's People
Author:
Date: 1966-09-15
Edition: 5

New Zealand Communist Party General Secretary Wilcox enthusiastically hails the Chinese Cultural Revolution as benefiting the world's people. The revolutionary actions of the Red Guards are a great thing for both China and the world.

WELLINGTON, Sept. 14 (Xinhua) — In the September 14 issue of the New Zealand Communist Party's organ, the weekly "People's Voice," General Secretary Wilcox issued a statement enthusiastically hailing the great historical significance of the Chinese proletarian cultural revolution and warmly praising the revolutionary actions of the Red Guards as "a great thing."

The statement said: "All class-conscious workers and genuine socialists will welcome the great developments now taking place in China. The movement of Chinese revolutionary youth who make up the Red Guards is a movement to consolidate the socialist revolution and to thoroughly eliminate capitalist remnants."

The statement sternly refuted the slander and vilification of the Red Guards' activities and pointed out: "The Red Guards are at the forefront of the great proletarian cultural revolution now under way in China." Their revolutionary actions "are a great thing for both China and the world."

The statement pointed out: "The purpose of this cultural revolution is completely in line with the basic principles of Marxism-Leninism. These purposes are in the interests of the working people of the whole world.

"Therefore, the cultural revolution not only faces opposition and attacks from imperialism but also from the running dogs of imperialism, the Soviet revisionists and their followers abroad. These people have abandoned the basic principles of Marxism-Leninism themselves but are busy attacking China's cultural revolution and the Red Guards movement.

"But any class-conscious worker can judge who is right and who is wrong based on the evidence at hand."

Wilcox pointed out: "Chinese youth are full of revolutionary enthusiasm. Millions of young people are throwing themselves into the movement to strengthen socialism. The nature and scale of this movement are unprecedented in history."

The statement said: "What about the situation in the Soviet Union and in those socialist countries whose leaders follow the revisionists? Are not the young people in those countries being encouraged more and more to imitate capitalist ways? In the economic life of those countries, the trend is toward a capitalist retrogression; similarly, capitalist ideas, methods, and habits are becoming more and more serious in their cultural life."

The statement said: "In China, it is the opposite. This is something that everyone who cherishes socialism must welcome." The statement emphasized: "The great proletarian cultural revolution in China and its active Red Guards have great historical significance because this is a guarantee that China's future will forever maintain a socialist character. "This is also a guarantee that world socialism will have a strong and permanent base in the future, no matter what happens."

### 新共总书记威尔科克斯热烈欢呼  中国文化革命符合世界人民利益  红卫兵的革命行动对中国和全世界都是一件大好事

1966-09-15
第5版()
专栏：

    新共总书记威尔科克斯热烈欢呼
    中国文化革命符合世界人民利益
    红卫兵的革命行动对中国和全世界都是一件大好事
    新华社惠灵顿十四日电　新西兰共产党总书记威尔科克斯在十四日一期的新共机关报《人民之声》周报上发表声明，热烈欢呼中国无产阶级文化大革命具有“巨大的历史意义”，并且热烈赞扬红卫兵的革命行动是“一件大好事”。
    声明说：“一切有阶级觉悟的工人和真正的社会主义者，都将欢呼现在正在中国发生的社会主义的伟大事态发展。组成红卫兵的中国革命青年的运动，是巩固社会主义革命和彻底根除资本主义残余的一个运动。”
    声明严厉驳斥了那些对红卫兵活动的造谣和诽谤，并且指出：“红卫兵站在目前正在中国进行的伟大的工人阶级文化大革命的最前列。”他们的革命行动
  “对中国和对全世界都是一件大好事”。
    声明指出：“这场文化革命的目的同马克思列宁主义的基本原则是完全一致的。这些目的是符合全世界劳动人民的利益的。
      “因此，文化革命不仅遭到帝国主义的反对和攻击，而且遭到帝国主义的走卒苏联修正主义者及其国外追随者的反对和攻击。这些人自己抛弃了马克思列宁主义的基本原则，却忙于攻击中国的文化革命和红卫兵运动。
    “但是，任何一个有阶级觉悟的工人都能够根据手头的证据判断出谁是谁非。”
    威尔科克斯指出：“中国青年充满革命激情。千百万的青年正在投身于加强社会主义的运动。这个运动的性质和规模是史无前例的。”
    声明说：“在苏联，以及在那些其领导人追随修正主义者的社会主义国家里，情况又是怎么样呢？那些国家的青年人难道不是正被鼓励去越来越多地模仿资本主义的一套吗？”“在这些国家的经济生活中，趋势是向资本主义倒退；同样，在它们的文化生活中，资本主义思想、方法和习惯也变得越来越严重。”
    声明说：“而在中国恰恰相反。这是一切珍视社会主义的人必须加以欢迎的。”声明强调指出：“中国的伟大的工人阶级的文化大革命及其红卫兵积极分子具有巨大的历史意义，因为这是使中国的前途永远保持社会主义性质的一个保证。”“这也是使世界社会主义在将来不管发生什么事情都能有一个强大的永久基地的保证。”
