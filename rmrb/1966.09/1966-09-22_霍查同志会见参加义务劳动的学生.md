Comrade Enver Hoxha Meets Students Participating in Voluntary Labor

Tirana, September 21 (New China News Agency) - According to a report by the Albanian News Agency, on the afternoon of the 20th, Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor, met with a group of young students from primary and secondary schools who participated in the summer voluntary labor and had a very cordial conversation with them. Present at the meeting were Ramiz Alia, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor, Adil Karicaku, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Union of Albanian Working Youth, and Agim Saraçaku, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Union of Albanian Working Youth.

The young people reported to Comrade Enver Hoxha on the achievements and education they received during the summer voluntary labor.

Comrade Hoxha expressed satisfaction with the work of the young people and, through them, congratulated all the young people in the country on their completed work, hoping that they would achieve greater successes in the future organized voluntary labor. Comrade Hoxha also gave very valuable suggestions to the young people on the issues of study, labor, and education.

### 霍查同志会见参加义务劳动的学生

1966-09-22
第5版()
专栏：

    霍查同志会见参加义务劳动的学生
    新华社地拉那二十一日电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央第一书记恩维尔·霍查同志，二十日下午在党中央大厦会见了一批参加今年夏季义务劳动的大中学校的青年学生，并与他们进行了十分亲切的谈话。会见时在场的有，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、中央书记拉·阿利雅，劳动青年联盟中央第一书记阿·米洛和劳动青年联盟中央书记阿·萨拉库。
    青年们向恩维尔·霍查同志汇报了在夏季义务劳动中，他们所取得的成绩和受到的教育。
    霍查同志对青年们的劳动表示满意，并通过他们向全国所有青年祝贺他们所完成的工作，希望他们在将来组织的义务劳动中取得更大的成就。霍查同志还向青年们就青年的学习、劳动和教育问题提出了十分宝贵的建议。（附图片）
