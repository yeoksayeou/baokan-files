Title: I Love Chairman Mao
Author: 
Date: 1966-09-27
Edition: 5

I love the great Chairman Mao.
My name is Jones. I am almost fifteen years old. I love China, I love the great Communist Party of China, and I love the great leader of the Chinese people, Comrade Mao Zedong. I spend most of my spare time reading books about Comrade Mao Zedong and his great works.

I think he is the greatest philosopher in history. I love Chairman Mao and the great founder of communism, Karl Marx. Recently, I read a biography of Comrade Mao Zedong and saw how great a person he is. I live in Wales. I hope that Wales will achieve communism. Of course, this will take a long time. But I firmly believe that one day the whole world will achieve communism.

Long live Chairman Mao and the Communist Party of China!

		Wales, Jones

### 我热爱伟大的毛主席
琼斯
1966-09-27
第5版()
专栏：国外来信

    我热爱伟大的毛主席
    我的名字叫琼斯，快十五岁了。我热爱中国，热爱中国伟大的党，热爱中国人民伟大的领袖毛泽东同志。我把课余的大部分时间用来阅读有关介绍毛泽东同志的书和他的伟大的著作。
    我认为他是历史上最伟大的哲学家。我热爱毛主席和共产主义伟大的创始人卡尔·马克思。最近，我读了一本毛泽东同志的传略，从中看到他是一位多么伟大的人物。我住在威尔士。我希望威尔士实现共产主义。当然，这还要经过一段长时间。但是，我深深知道终会有那么一天，整个世界都将会实现共产主义。
    毛主席和中国共产党万岁！
                              英国　琼斯
