Title: Economic Delegation from Congo (Brazzaville) Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-09-28
Edition: 4

The economic delegation from the government of Congo (Brazzaville), led by the Minister of National Construction, Claude Dakosta, arrived in Beijing by plane this evening. The delegation is in China at the invitation of the Foreign Economic Liaison Commission to participate in National Day celebrations and conduct a friendly visit.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included the Director of the Foreign Economic Commission, Fang Yi, and relevant officials such as Yang Lin, Li Jingzhao, and Xie Feng.

The Charge d'Affaires of the Congolese (Brazzaville) Embassy in China, Daniel Bakula, and the member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Congolese National Revolutionary Movement, Aubert Lounda, who is currently visiting China, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 刚果（布）政府经济代表团到京

1966-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

    刚果（布）政府经济代表团到京
    新华社二十七日讯　刚果（布）政府经济代表团，在团长刚果（布）国家建设部部长达科斯塔·克劳德率领下，今晚乘飞机到达北京。代表团是应对外经济联络委员会邀请来我国参加国庆活动并进行友好访问的。
    到机场欢迎的，有对外经委主任方毅，以及有关方面的负责人杨琳、李景昭、谢丰等。
    刚果（布）政府驻中国大使馆临时代办丹尼尔·巴库拉以及正在我国访问的刚果（布）全国革命运动中央委员会政治局委员奥贝·伦达也到机场迎接。
