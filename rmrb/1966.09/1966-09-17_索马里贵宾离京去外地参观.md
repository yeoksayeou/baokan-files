Title: Somali Guests Depart Beijing for Tours Outside the Capital
Author:
Date: 1966-09-17
Edition: 5

Somali National Assembly President Sheikh Mukhtar Muhammad Hussein, along with the friendly delegation of the Somali National Assembly he leads, departed Beijing by special plane this morning to visit and tour outside the capital, accompanied by Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo. Those who went to the airport to see them off included Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Yang Mingxuan, Committee members Nan Hanchen and Xie Fumin, and Vice Secretary-General Lian Guan.

### 索马里贵宾离京去外地参观

1966-09-17
第5版()
专栏：

    索马里贵宾离京去外地参观
    新华社十六日讯　索马里国民议会议长谢赫·穆克塔尔·穆罕默德·侯赛因，以及由他率领的索马里国民议会友好代表团，由人大常委会副委员长郭沫若陪同，今天上午乘专机离开北京去外地参观访问。到机场欢送的，有人大常委会副委员长杨明轩，委员南汉宸、谢扶民，副秘书长连贯等。
