Title: Comrade Gribat Leaves Beijing for Visits to Shanghai and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1966-09-05
Edition: 1

Comrade Gribat of the Central Committee of the Belgian Communist Party and his wife left Beijing by plane today to visit Shanghai and other places in a friendly tour, and then return to their country.

Comrade Kang Sheng, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Comrade Liu Ningyi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Comrade Zhao Yimin, alternate member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and other comrades went to the airport to see them off.

Young Pioneers presented flowers to Comrade Gribat and his wife.

### 格里巴同志离京去上海等地访问  康生等同志到机场欢送

1966-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    格里巴同志离京去上海等地访问
    康生等同志到机场欢送
    新华社四日讯　比利时共产党中央委员会书记格里巴同志和夫人，今天乘飞机离开北京，前往上海等地进行友好访问，然后回国。
    中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记康生，中共中央书记处书记刘宁一、中共中央候补委员赵毅敏等同志到机场欢送。
    少年先锋队队员向格里巴同志和夫人献了花。
