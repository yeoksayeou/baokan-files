Author:
Date: 1966-09-05
Edition: 4

Friendly Exchanges

	△ The Consul-General of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming, Yang Wenzhong, and the Consul-General in Guangzhou, Du Wenmin, along with their spouses, held banquets and receptions on the 1st and 2nd respectively to celebrate the 21st anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

	Leaders from Yunnan and Guangdong provinces were invited to attend the banquets and receptions.

	△ The Chinese Youth Delegation concluded their three-week visit to Japan, returning home on the 2nd by plane from Tokyo, carrying with them the fighting friendship of the Japanese people and youth.

	Those who went to Haneda Airport to see them off included the Chairman of the Socialist Youth League of Japan, Shendian Zhao, as well as representatives from the Japan-China Friendship Association, the Japan Youth Federation, the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, and friends from the Socialist Party.

	△ According to the relevant agreements between the Chinese and Nepalese governments, the Chinese portion of the construction team that went to Nepal to help build the Kathmandu-Kodari Highway has returned to the motherland on the 1st after completing their assigned tasks on schedule. They were warmly welcomed by representatives of the Party, government, and military leadership of the Tibet Autonomous Region and by the local people at the Sino-Nepalese Friendship Bridge.

	△ At the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Robert Lunda, a member of the Central Committee and Political Bureau of the National Revolutionary Movement of Congo (Brazzaville), arrived in Beijing by plane on the 4th.

### 友好往来

1966-09-05
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    △越南民主共和国驻昆明总领事杨文忠、驻广州总领事杜文敏和夫人，于一日和二日晚分别举行宴会和酒会，庆祝越南民主共和国成立二十一周年。
    云南和广东省的领导同志应邀出席了宴会和酒会。
    △中国青年代表团结束在日本的三周访问，满载日本人民和青年的战斗友谊，二日乘飞机离开东京回国。
    前往羽田机场欢送的有日本社会主义青年同盟委员长深田肇以及日中友好协会、日本青年团协议会、日本工会总评议会等团体的负责人和社会党的朋友。
    △根据中尼两国政府的有关协议去尼泊尔帮助修筑加德满都—科达里公路的中国部分施工人员，在按期完成所担负的任务后已经于一日回到祖国。他们在中尼边界友谊桥上受到我国西藏地区党、政府、驻军领导机关的代表和人民群众的热烈欢迎。
    △应中国人民外交学会邀请，刚果（布）全国革命运动中央委员会委员、政治局委员罗伯尔·伦达于四日乘飞机到达北京。
          （据新华社）
