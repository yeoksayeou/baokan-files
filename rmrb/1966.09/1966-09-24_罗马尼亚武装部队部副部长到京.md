Title: Deputy Minister of the Romanian Armed Forces Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-09-24
Edition: 5

Romanian Deputy Minister of the Armed Forces General Ionela Vasile and his wife, at the invitation of China, arrived in Beijing today for a vacation.

Vice Minister of National Defense Xiao Jingguang, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Liberation Army Peng Shaohui, and relevant department heads Liu Daosheng, Cao Liwei, Jia Ruoyu, Pan Zhenwu, Yuan Huabing, and Lin Yongyi went to the airport to welcome them.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them also included Romanian Ambassador to China Aureliu Dumitrescu, Military Attaché Colonel Soltescu, and his wife.

### 罗马尼亚武装部队部副部长到京

1966-09-24
第5版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚武装部队部副部长到京
    新华社二十三日讯  罗马尼亚社会主义共和国武装部队部副部长约奈尔·华西列中将及夫人应邀来我国休假于今天到达北京。
    国防部副部长萧劲光，中国人民解放军副总参谋长彭绍辉以及有关部门负责人刘道生、曹里怀、贾若瑜、潘振武、苑化冰、林涌一等前往机场迎接。
    前往机场迎接的还有罗马尼亚驻华大使奥列尔·杜马、武官索列斯库上校及夫人。
