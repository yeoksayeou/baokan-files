	Chairman Mao's Quotations
	The fundamental revolutionary force is the workers and peasants. The leading class of the revolution is the proletariat. Without this fundamental revolutionary force and without the leadership of the proletariat, it is impossible to complete the democratic revolution against imperialism and feudalism.

							From "The May Fourth Movement"

### 毛主席语录

1966-09-15
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    根本的革命力量是工农，革命的领导阶级是工人阶级。如果离开了这种根本的革命力量，离开了工人阶级的领导，要完成反帝反封建的民主革命是不可能的。
                                        《五四运动》
