Title: Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Two U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-09-13
Edition: 4

Vietnamese military and civilians shoot down two U.S. aircraft

New China News Agency, Hanoi, September 11 — According to a report released here on September 11: On the evening of the 10th, the military and civilians of the Vietnamese provinces of Haiphong and Haiyang shot down two invading U.S. aggressor aircraft, damaged several others, and captured a live U.S. air pirate.

### 越南军民击落美机两架

1966-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

    越南军民击落美机两架
    新华社河内十一日电　据这里十一日公布：越南北方河北、海阳两省军民，十日晚上击落入侵的美国强盗飞机两架，击伤多架，并且活捉了美国飞贼。
