Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1966-09-25
Edition: 5

Friendly Exchanges

	△ The delegation of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association led by Sun Guanguo concluded their friendly visit to our country and departed from Nanning for their home country on the 19th.

	△ At the invitation of the Romanian Academy of Sciences, a delegation from the Chinese Academy of Sciences, led by Vice President Zhu Kezhen, consisting of four members, departed by plane from Beijing on the 23rd to Romania to participate in the centenary celebrations of the Romanian Academy of Sciences.

	△ The Chinese Folk Song and Dance Troupe, led by Yu Wenhui, arrived in Cairo by plane on the evening of the 20th. The troupe is visiting and performing in accordance with the execution plan of the cultural cooperation agreement between China and the United Arab Republic.

### 友好往来

1966-09-25
第5版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    △由孙光阀率领的越中友协代表团结束了在我国的友好访问，十九日离南宁回国。
    △应罗马尼亚科学院邀请，以中国科学院副院长竺可桢为首的中国科学院代表团一行四人，二十三日乘飞机离开北京前往罗马尼亚参加罗马尼亚科学院一百周年庆祝活动。
    △由禹文淮率领的中国民间歌舞团二十日晚间乘飞机到达开罗。歌舞团是根据中国和阿联的文化合作协定执行计划前来访问演出的。
