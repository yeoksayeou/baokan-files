Title: Chairman Mao, the Great Helmsman
Author:
Date: 1966-09-28
Edition: 6

	Sailing the seas relies on the helmsman!
	Navigating treacherous rapids,
	Rely on the helmsman.
	Crossing swift currents,
	Rely on the helmsman.
	Avoiding hidden reefs,
	Rely on the helmsman.
	Marching towards victory,
	Rely on the helmsman.
	Respected Chairman Mao,
	You are our greatest helmsman!

	In those days of dark clouds,
	In those years of gunfire,
	Chairman Mao raised his mighty hand,
	And wrote the four volumes of his magnum opus,
	Issuing the call to battle,
	Paving the way for revolution,
	The thunder roars across the land!
	We, the workers, peasants, and soldiers,
	Follow Chairman Mao,
	Wielding scythes,
	Swinging hoes,
	Overthrowing the three great mountains,
	And banishing poverty and ignorance.
	Where does our strength come from?
	From Chairman Mao—
	Our great teacher,
	Our great leader,
	Our great commander,
	Our great helmsman!

	Today,
	Our beloved Chairman Mao,
	Has received the million-strong cultural revolution army,
	And shaken hands with the million revolutionary masses.
	Our hearts,
	Separated by thousands of mountains and rivers,
	Fly to Tiananmen Gate;
	Our hands,
	Stretched across thousands of miles,
	Reach out to Chairman Mao!
	Chairman Mao, Chairman Mao,
	You have shaken hands with the cultural revolution army of the capital,
	And that is shaking hands with us.
	Our hearts, as poor and lower-middle peasants, surge with hot blood,
	Our eyes, as poor and lower-middle peasants, well up with hot tears.
	Our hands,
	Are full of strength;
	Our hands,
	Are warm.
	We will use these iron hands,
	To transform the world,
	And remake the Earth!

	Chairman Mao, the great helmsman,
	We follow you in revolution,
	Daring to change the heavens and the earth,
	Clearing the way forward!
	Command the river,
	And the river parts;
	Command the mountain,
	And the mountain bows.
	On the path to Dazhai, we press on,
	Spreading brocade across the land!

	Chairman Mao, the great helmsman,
	You guide us,
	To beat the drums of the cultural revolution,
	To raise our iron fists,
	With whips in hand,
	To fiercely attack the ghosts and snakes,
	And engage in fierce struggle with the class enemy!
	To smash the old world,
	To make it fall on its face,
	To smash the old world,
	To make it crumble,
	Let the red flag fly high,
	Let the red light shine across the continents!

	Following Chairman Mao,
	To create a fiery new world,
	Stride forward,
	March! March!! March!!!

### 毛主席，伟大的舵手（对口词）
河北省农民业余演出队
1966-09-28
第6版()
专栏：

    毛主席，伟大的舵手（对口词）
    河北省农民业余演出队
    大海航行靠舵手！
    闯险滩，
    靠舵手。
    过急流，
    靠舵手。
    绕暗礁，
    靠舵手。
    走向胜利，
    靠舵手。
    敬爱的毛主席，
    就是我们最伟大的舵手！
    在那乌云密布的岁月，
    在那炮火纷飞的年头，
    毛主席挥巨手，
    写下了雄文四卷，
    发出了战斗的号令，
    开辟了革命的大路，
    万里江山风雷吼！
    我们工农兵群众，
    跟着毛主席，
    抡起铡刀，
    挥起锄头，
    把三座大山推翻，
    还要把一穷二白赶走。
    力量的源泉来自何处？
    来自毛主席——
    我们伟大的导师，
    我们伟大的领袖，
    我们伟大的统帅，
    我们伟大的舵手！
    今天，
    咱敬爱的毛主席，
    接见了百万文化革命大军，
    紧握了百万双革命群众的手。
    我们的心，
    隔着千山万水，
    飞向天安门；
    我们的手，
    隔着千里万里，
    伸向毛主席！
    毛主席呀，毛主席，
    您和首都文化革命大军握了手，
    就是握了我们的手。
    咱贫下中农心里热血涌，
    咱贫下中农眼里热泪流。
    咱手上，
    都是劲儿；
    咱手上，
    热呼呼。
    我们要用这双钢铁手，
    改造世界，
    重整地球！
    毛主席，伟大的舵手，
    我们跟着您闹革命，
    敢教日月换新天，
    披荆斩棘向前走！
    指河，
    河让路。
    指山，
    山低头！
    大寨路上不停步，
    江山万里铺锦绣！
    毛主席，伟大的舵手，
    指引我们，
    擂响文化大革命的战鼓，
    挥起铁拳，
    长缨在手，
    向牛鬼蛇神猛开火，
    同阶级敌人大搏斗！
    把旧世界，
    打它个人仰马翻，
    把旧世界，
    砸它个落花流水，
    让红旗漫天飘，
    让红光照五洲！
    跟着毛主席，
    开创火红的新世界，
    大步向前，
    走！走！！走！！！
