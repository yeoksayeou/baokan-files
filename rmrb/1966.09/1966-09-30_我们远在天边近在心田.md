Title: We Are Far Away but Close at Heart
Author: Duoluolieshi
Date: 1966-09-30
Edition: 5

I am so excited to hear the voice of China. My heart is filled with immense joy! I cannot express it in words. But I want to say, we are far away, but close at heart! I am sleepless. I think of China, of the past, present, and future. I wonder what China will be like twenty years from now? What will the new generation be like?

### 我们远在天边近在心田
多罗列士
1966-09-30
第5版()
专栏：国外来信

    我们远在天边近在心田
    我是多么激动地在倾听中国的声音。我心里感到无比兴奋！我无法用言语来表达。但是我想，我们远在天边，近在心田！我失眠了。我想起中国，想起过去、现在和将来，我想二十年后中国将会怎样？新的一代将会怎样？
                      〔乌拉圭〕多罗列士
