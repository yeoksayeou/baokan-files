Title: The People of Daqing Will Forever Follow Chairman Mao
Author: Dàqīng Yóutián Gōngrén, Jiāshǔ Yùyè Yǎnchù Duì
Date: 1966-09-28
Edition: 6

The mightiest power is that of Mao Zedong Thought,
Revolutionary people are full of vigor,
The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution surges forward,
Daqing workers charge ahead, riding on horses into battle.

Revolutionary big-character posters, one after another,
Expose all the monsters and demons in broad daylight.

Daqing workers wield the mighty cudgel,
Grasp the black-hearted wolves who oppose the Party and socialism,
Strike! Strike! Strike!
If the monsters and demons try to stage a comeback,
We will raise our iron fists and smash them down!
If the monsters and demons try to rebel,
We will raise our iron fists and smash them down!
Anyone who opposes the Party Central Committee,
We will raise our iron fists and smash them down!
Anyone who opposes Chairman Mao,
We will raise our iron fists and smash them down!

We vow to defend the dictatorship of the proletariat,
We vow to defend the socialist mountains and rivers!

The Daqing Campaign
Is to take Mao Zedong Thought as the guiding principle for all work,
Is to start with the "Two Articles," with class struggle and the struggle between the two lines as the key,
To learn from the People's Liberation Army, to carry out revolutionization.

The victory of the Daqing Campaign is the victory of Mao Zedong Thought.
The great red flag of Mao Zedong Thought flies in the wind,
Victorious news spreads far and wide.
Daqing workers, with high morale,
Fight stronger with each passing year in the six-year campaign,
Industry and agriculture both leap forward,
Daqing Oilfield is thriving.

Mao Zedong Thought is the highest directive for all our work,
We must
Strive to study Mao Zedong Thought,
Faithfully carry out Mao Zedong Thought,
Eagerly propagate Mao Zedong Thought,
Bravely defend Mao Zedong Thought.

With Mao Zedong Thought as the commander,
Build the troops with the Four Modernizations:
Highly proletarian,
Revolutionary,
Combat-ready,
Modern.

With a good troop armed with Mao Zedong Thought,
No difficulty is too great.

With Mao Zedong Thought as the commander,
Smash all old frameworks.

Be builders and defenders of a new world,
Break the old and establish the new to create a new world.

With Mao Zedong Thought as the commander,
Grasp the big wrench to drill the wells,
Pick up the hoe to farm the land,
Pick up the rifle to kill the enemy,
Pick up the pen to write articles,
Fight down all monsters and demons.

With Mao Zedong Thought as the commander,
Both industry and agriculture flourish,
Strive to make the oilfield a great school of Mao Zedong Thought,
Everyone's thoughts become revolutionary.
Everyone reads Chairman Mao's books,
Work hard on the written word.

Study and apply dialectics,
Remember the principle of "two into one."

Mao Zedong Thought guides us,
To forge a revolutionary path!

Chairman Mao said:
Workers should take work as their main task, but also learn military affairs, politics, and culture. They should also carry out socialist education, criticize the bourgeoisie. In places with conditions, they should also engage in agricultural and sideline production, such as Daqing Oilfield.

It is Mao Zedong Thought—
That points out the direction for us to advance;
It is Mao Zedong Thought,
That arms our petroleum workers;
It is Mao Zedong Thought,
That gives us boundless strength;
It is Mao Zedong Thought,
That nurtures our continuous growth.

Every victory of Daqing Oilfield,
Shines with the brilliance of Mao Zedong Thought.

Every step forward by Daqing workers,
Is the strength given to us by the Party.

Daqing—is the Party's Daqing;
Daqing—is the people's Daqing of the whole nation.

Following Chairman Mao,
Following the Communist Party,
We dare to climb mountains of knives and walk through seas of fire,
We dare to be self-reliant and strive for strength,
Daqing workers keep Chairman Mao's teachings in mind,
Strive for the people, strive for the country,
Stand on the great oilfield,
Keep the world revolution in mind.

The seas churn with clouds and waves,
The continents shake with thunder and lightning,
We will sweep away all harmful creatures, leaving none behind.

We will forever follow Chairman Mao,
Dare to face great winds and waves.
We will forever follow Chairman Mao,
Sweep away all class enemies.
We will forever follow Chairman Mao,
Dare to create any miracle in the world.
We will forever follow Chairman Mao,
March forward with heads held high.

Chairman Mao, we,
Love you endlessly, are loyal to you endlessly,
Admire you endlessly, believe in you endlessly.

We sincerely wish,
Our most beloved leader Chairman Mao,
Eternal health, eternal life!

We enthusiastically cheer,
Our most beloved leader Chairman Mao,
Long live! Long live!
Long live Chairman Mao! Long live! Ten thousand years!

### 大庆人永远跟着毛主席
大庆油田工人、家属业余演出队
1966-09-28
第6版()
专栏：

    大庆人永远跟着毛主席
    大庆油田工人、家属业余演出队
    毛泽东思想威力最大，
    革命人民意气风发，
    无产阶级文化大革命运动汹涌澎湃，
    大庆工人冲锋陷阵跨战马。
    革命的大字报，你一张，我一张，
    把一切牛鬼蛇神统统暴露在光天化日之下。
    大庆工人奋起千钧棒，
    揪住反党反社会主义的黑心狼，
    打！打！打！
    牛鬼蛇神想变天，
    我们举起铁拳打倒他！
    牛鬼蛇神想造反，
    我们举起铁拳打倒他！
    谁要反对党中央，
    我们举起铁拳打倒他！
    谁要反对毛主席，
    我们举起铁拳打倒他！
    誓死保卫无产阶级专政，
    誓死保卫社会主义江山！
    大庆会战，
    就是以毛泽东思想为一切工作的指导方针，
    就是靠“两论”起家，以阶级斗争、两条道路斗争为纲，大学解放军，大搞革命化。
    大庆会战的胜利，就是毛泽东思想的胜利。
    毛泽东思想伟大红旗迎风飘扬，
    胜利的捷报传遍四方。
    大庆工人，斗志昂扬，
    六年会战，越战越强，
    工业农业，双双跃进，
    大庆油田，一片兴旺景象。
    毛泽东思想是我们各项工作的最高指示，
    我们要
    努力学习毛泽东思想，
    忠实贯彻毛泽东思想，
    热情宣传毛泽东思想，
    勇敢捍卫毛泽东思想。
    毛泽东思想把帅挂，
    队伍建设抓四化：
    非常无产阶级化，
    革命化，
    战斗化，
    现代化。
    有了毛泽东思想武装起来的好队伍，
    天大的困难都不怕。
    毛泽东思想把帅挂，
    把旧的框框全打垮。
    作新世界的建设者、保卫者，
    破旧立新创天下。
    毛泽东思想把帅挂，
    紧握大钳把井打，
    拿起锄头能种地，
    拿起枪杆把敌杀，
    拿起笔杆写文章，
    把牛鬼蛇神全斗垮。
    毛泽东思想把帅挂，
    亦工亦农开红花，
    努力把油田办成毛泽东思想大学校，
    人人思想革命化。
    人人都读毛主席书，
    用字上面狠下功夫。
    大学大用辩证法，
    一分为二牢记住。
    毛泽东思想指引咱，
    要闯出一条革命路！
    毛主席说：
    工人以工为主，也要兼学军事、政治、文化。也要搞社会主义教育运动，也要批判资产阶级。在有条件的地方，也要从事农副业生产，例如大庆油田那样。
    是毛泽东思想——
    给我们指出了前进的方向；
    是毛泽东思想，
    把我们石油工人武装；
    是毛泽东思想，
    给了我们无穷的力量；
    是毛泽东思想，
    哺育我们不断成长。
    大庆油田的每个胜利，
    都闪耀着毛泽东思想的光芒。
    大庆工人每前进一步，
    都是党给我们的力量。
    大——庆，是党的大庆；
    大——庆，是全国人民的大庆。
    跟着毛主席，
    跟着共产党，
    刀山敢上，火海敢闯，
    自力更生，奋发图强，
    大庆工人，把毛主席的教导牢记心上，
    为人民争气，为祖国争光，
    脚踏大油田，
    把世界革命记心上。
    四海翻腾云水怒，
    五洲震荡风雷激，
    要扫除一切害人虫，全无敌。
    我们永远跟着毛主席，
    敢顶大风和大浪。
    我们永远跟着毛主席，
    把阶级敌人一扫光。
    我们永远跟着毛主席，
    什么人间奇迹都敢创。
    我们永远跟着毛主席，
    昂首阔步奔前方。
    毛主席，我们对您，
    无限热爱，无限忠诚，
    无限崇拜，无限信仰。
    我们衷心的祝愿，
    最敬爱的领袖毛主席，
    永远健康，万寿无疆！
    我们热烈欢呼，
    最敬爱的领袖毛主席，
    万岁！万岁！
    毛主席万岁！万岁！万万岁！
            （《大庆人永远跟着毛主席》原作较长，这里选登的是其中一部分）
