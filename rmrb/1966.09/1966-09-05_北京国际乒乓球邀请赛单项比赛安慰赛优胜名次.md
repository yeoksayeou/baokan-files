Title: Winners of the Consolation Matches in the Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational Tournament
Author:
Date: 1966-09-05
Edition: 2

The winners of the consolation matches in the five individual events of the 1966 Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational Tournament are as follows:

Men's Singles: Champion Zhang Xueliang (China), Runner-up Li Fuyong (originally named Li Furong, China), Third Place Zhou Lanxi (China) and Su Guoxi (China)

Women's Singles: Champion Lin Huicheng (China), Runner-up Li Li (originally named Li Li, China), Third Place Yamazaki Kyoko (Japan) and Zheng Minzhi (China)

Men's Doubles: Champions Li Fuyong and Lu Jufang (China), Runners-up Zhang Xueliang and Li Jingguang (China), Third Place Fukushima Manji and Hasegawa Nobuhiko (Japan) and Zhou Lanxi and Su Guoxi (China)

Women's Doubles: Champions Yamazaki Kyoko and Fukaoka Naoko (Japan), Runners-up Lin Huicheng and Zheng Minzhi (China), Third Place Hirota Sachiko, Morisawa Sachiko (Japan) and Li Heming and Li Li (China)

Mixed Doubles: Champions Li Jingguang and Li Heming (China), Runners-up Li Fuyong and Li Li (China), Third Place Fukushima Manji and Morisawa Sachiko (Japan) and Pak Sin-il and Kim Bang-j (North Korea)

Men's Consolation Match: Champion Kiyomoto Hajime (Japan), Runner-up Kim Eng (Cambodia)

Women's Consolation Match: Champion Nguyen Thi Mai (Vietnam), Runner-up La Pha (Cambodia)

(Photo attached)

The outstanding male and female athletes from various countries who participated in the 1966 Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational Tournament, along with the winners of the five individual events and the men's and women's consolation matches, pose for a group photo after receiving flowers.

### 北京国际乒乓球邀请赛单项比赛安慰赛优胜名次

1966-09-05
第2版()
专栏：

    北京国际乒乓球邀请赛单项比赛安慰赛优胜名次
    新华社四日讯　一九六六年北京国际乒乓球邀请赛五个单项比赛和安慰赛优胜名次：
    男子单打：冠军庄则栋（中国），亚军李富勇（原名李富荣，中国），第三名周兰荪（中国）和苏国熙（中国）
    女子单打：冠军林慧卿（中国），亚军李力（原名李莉，中国），第三名山中教子（日本）和郑敏之（中国）
    男子双打：冠军李富勇、陆巨芳（中国），亚军庄则栋、李景光（中国），第三名福岛万治、长谷川信彦（日本）和周兰荪、苏国熙（中国）
    女子双打：冠军山中教子、深津尚子（日本），亚军林慧卿、郑敏之（中国），第三名广田佐枝子，森泽幸子（日本）和李赫男、李力（中国）
    混合双打：冠军李景光、李赫男（中国），亚军李富勇、李力（中国），第三名福岛万治、森泽幸子（日本）和朴信一、金邦子（朝鲜）
    男子安慰赛：冠军键本肇（日本），亚军金英尼（柬埔寨）
    女子安慰赛：冠军阮氏梅（越南），亚军拉·帕花（柬埔寨）
