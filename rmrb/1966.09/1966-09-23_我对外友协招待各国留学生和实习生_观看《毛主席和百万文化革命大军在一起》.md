Title: The Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries Hosts Foreign Students and Interns to Watch "Chairman Mao and the Million-strong Cultural Revolution Army"
Author:
Date: 1966-09-23
Edition: 4

Various foreign students and interns in Beijing watched the large-scale color documentary "Chairman Mao and the Million-strong Cultural Revolution Army" on the evenings of September 21 and 22. They expressed their boundless love and admiration for the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao. Every time Chairman Mao appeared on the screen, they applauded enthusiastically. Some foreign students and interns, before the film screening, studied "Quotations from Chairman Mao" with Chinese students and sang "The East is Red" and "The Ship Sails on the Sea with the Helmsman."

This film was screened by the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries to welcome foreign students and interns in Beijing. The president of the association, Chu Tu-nan, attended the reception.

### 我对外友协招待各国留学生和实习生  观看《毛主席和百万文化革命大军在一起》

1966-09-23
第4版()
专栏：

    我对外友协招待各国留学生和实习生
    观看《毛主席和百万文化革命大军在一起》
    新华社二十二日讯　各国在北京的留学生和实习生，二十一日和二十二日晚上，观看了大型彩色纪录片《毛主席和百万文化革命大军在一起》，他们对中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席表示无比热爱，无比敬仰。每当毛主席在银幕上出现的时候，他们都报以热烈的掌声。有的外国留学生和实习生在电影放映前，还和中国学生一起学习《毛主席语录》，高唱《东方红》和《大海航行靠舵手》。
    这部影片是中国人民对外文化友好协会为招待各国在京留学生、实习生而放映的。对外友协会长楚图南出席了招待会。
