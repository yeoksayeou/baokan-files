Title: Japanese People Protest Against U.S. Nuclear Submarine Entering Yokosuka
Author:
Date: 1966-09-07
Edition: 6

	Japanese citizens from Tokyo and Kanagawa Prefecture, numbering 9,000, held a rally on the evening of September 5 in a park near the U.S. naval base in Yokosuka. The rally was to protest against U.S. aggression in Vietnam and the docking of the U.S. nuclear submarine "Sea Dragon" at Yokosuka port. The rally unanimously passed a resolution of protest addressed to the governments of the United States and Japan. After the rally, participants marched to the front of the U.S. military base, chanting slogans such as "Nuclear submarines, go back!"

### 日本人民示威抗议美国核潜艇闯进横须贺

1966-09-07
第6版()
专栏：

    日本人民示威抗议美国核潜艇闯进横须贺
    据新华社六日讯　日本东京和神奈川县的九千公众，九月五日晚上在靠近横须贺美国海军基地的临海公园举行大会，抗议美国侵略越南和美国核潜艇“海龙号”进驻横须贺港口。大会一致通过了给美国和日本两国政府的抗议信。大会结束后，与会者到美军基地前示威游行，高呼“核潜艇滚回去”等口号。
