Title: Decision of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1966-09-13
Edition: 2

The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution is aimed at revolutionizing people's thinking, thereby making all work done more, faster, better, and more economically. As long as the masses are fully mobilized and arrangements are properly made, it is possible to ensure that the cultural revolution and production will not be neglected, and to ensure high quality in all work.

### 《中国共产党中央委员会关于无产阶级文化大革命的决定》

1966-09-13
第2版()
专栏：

    无产阶级文化大革命，就是为的要使人的思想革命化，因而使各项工作做得更多、更快、更好、更省。只要充分发动群众，妥善安排，就能够保证文化革命和生产两不误，保证各项工作的高质量。
