Title: Swift and Decisive: South Vietnamese Liberation Army Inflicts Heavy Casualties on US and Puppet Forces
Author:
Date: 1966-09-14
Edition: 5

The South Vietnamese Liberation Army has demonstrated its prowess in close combat, inflicting heavy casualties on US and puppet forces.

In the province of Binh Dinh: Half an hour to annihilate an enemy regiment

The armed forces of the South Vietnamese Liberation Army in Binh Dinh Province, on the morning of the 8th, engaged in a 28-minute close combat and nearly annihilated an enemy regiment composed of two battalions, killing, wounding, and capturing nearly 600 enemy troops.

On the evening of the 7th, the liberation forces launched a fierce attack on the enemy stationed in the Se Loc area, about 6 kilometers northeast of Phu My. They annihilated a company of the puppet 40th Regiment. At the same time, the liberation forces also shelled the enemy stationed in the My Da area south of Phu My County, annihilating a security company of the puppet forces.

After suffering heavy losses, the American aggressors and their lackeys hastily dispatched a regiment composed of two battalions of the puppet 41st Regiment to reinforce the area on the morning of the 8th. By noon, the enemy had fallen into an ambush set by the liberation forces between Phu My and Phu Gi. From the start of the battle, the liberation forces divided the enemy and annihilated them one by one. The enemy regiment was in complete disarray, fleeing in all directions. The liberation forces heavily encircled the enemy while fiercely charging and killing, engaging in hand-to-hand combat and annihilating a large number of enemy troops.

In the province of Tuyen Duc: Annihilating a US Army battalion and heavily damaging two others

According to a supplementary report from the South Vietnamese Liberation News Agency, on August 25, the armed forces of South Vietnam's liberation army fought a brilliant annihilation battle in the Bac Gia district of Tuyen Duc Province.

The report said that in the fierce battle that lasted 12 hours in the Bung Trang area of Bac Gia district, the liberation forces demonstrated their strength in close combat, rendering US aircraft and artillery ineffective, and repelling 34 frantic enemy attacks. They annihilated a battalion of the US 1st Infantry Division's 2nd Brigade and heavily damaged two other battalions, killing and wounding more than 700 US invaders, including one US major and two captains. They destroyed 14 tanks, shot down four helicopters, and seized a large amount of weapons and military supplies.

In the province of Binh Duong: Eliminating nearly 300 US invaders

According to a report from the South Vietnamese Liberation News Agency, the armed forces of the South Vietnamese Liberation Army have recently achieved significant victories in their counterattacks against US and puppet forces and their accomplices.

The liberation forces in Binh Duong Province, from August 21 to 29, eliminated 364 enemy troops in their counterattacks against the US and puppet forces' "sweeping operations," including nearly 300 US invaders. They destroyed 10 military vehicles, two cannons, and shot down one helicopter.

The report said that on August 17 and 18, the Australian accomplice forces suffered heavy losses, with more than 700 casualties in the Thua Tien and Long Thanh areas. On August 21, under the cover of US air and artillery support, the US 173rd Airborne Brigade, the puppet 10th Division, and multiple battalions of New Zealand accomplice forces launched another "sweeping operation" in the Chuong My and Chuong Ngai districts of Binh Duong Province. The military and civilians of Chuong My and Chuong Ngai districts rose up to bravely counterattack, launching fierce assaults on the enemy everywhere and inflicting heavy losses.

### 猛打猛冲　速战速决　围点打援　各个歼灭  南越解放军发挥近战威力痛歼美伪军

1966-09-14
第5版()
专栏：

    猛打猛冲　速战速决　围点打援　各个歼灭
    南越解放军发挥近战威力痛歼美伪军
    在平定省：半小时歼灭敌军一个战团
    南越平定省解放武装力量，八日上午经过二十八分钟的近战，几乎全歼敌军由两个营的兵力组成的一个战团，共打死打伤和俘虏敌人近六百名。
    七日晚上，解放武装力量向驻扎在蓬山东北六公里世禄地区的敌军发动猛烈袭击，消灭伪军第四十团的一个连。同时，解放武装力量还炮击驻扎在符美县南面芽达地区的敌人，消灭伪军一个保安连。
    美国强盗及其走狗遭到沉重打击后，于八日上午慌忙出动由伪军第四十一团的两个营组成的一个战团，企图增援。当天中午，敌人陷入了解放武装力量在符美和符吉之间设置的伏击阵地，解放武装力量从战斗一开始，就把敌人分割开来，各个予以歼灭。敌人整个战团混乱不堪，争相逃窜。解放武装力量一面重重包围敌人，一面猛冲猛杀，展开肉搏战，消灭大批敌人。
    在土龙木省：全歼美军一营重创两营
    据越南南方解放通讯社补充报道：越南南方解放武装力量八月二十五日在土龙木省边葛县打了一场漂亮的歼灭战。
    报道说，在边葛县的蓬庄地区进行的这场连续十二个小时的激烈战斗中，解放武装力量发挥近战的威力，使美国飞机大炮失去了作用，粉碎了敌人三十四次疯狂进攻，全歼美国第一步兵师第二旅一个营和重创两个营，打死打伤美国侵略军七百多名，其中包括美军少校一名、上尉两名，击毁坦克十四辆，击落直升飞机四架，缴获大批武器和军用物资。
    在巴地省：消灭美国侵略军近三百名
    据越南南方解放通讯社报道，南越解放武装力量最近在反击美伪军和帮凶军的战斗中接连获得重大战果。
    巴地省解放武装力量，八月二十一日到二十九日，在反击美伪军的“扫荡”中，消灭敌人三百六十四名，其中有美国侵略军近三百名，击毁军车十辆、大炮两门，击落直升飞机一架。
    报道说，在八月十七日和十八日，澳大利亚帮凶军在土山和隆新地区遭到巴地省解放军沉重打击，伤亡七百多名以后，美国侵略军第一七三空降旅以及伪军第十师和新西兰帮凶军多营，在美国空军、炮兵的掩护下，于八月二十一日又向巴地省川木县和珠德县进行“扫荡”。川木、珠德两县军民奋起英勇反击，到处猛烈袭击敌人，给予沉重打击。　（据新华社讯）（附图片）
