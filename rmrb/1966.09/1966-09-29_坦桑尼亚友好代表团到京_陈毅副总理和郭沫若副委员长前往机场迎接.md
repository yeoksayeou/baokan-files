Title: Tanzanian Friendship Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-09-29
Edition: 6

At the invitation of the Chinese government, a Tanzanian friendship delegation, led by Kitunga, the governor of Tanzania's Coastal Province, arrived in Beijing by airplane this evening.

Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Chairman Guo Moruo, and more than a thousand people from the capital went to the airport to warmly welcome the guests. Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Liu Xiao and other relevant officials. Tanzanian Ambassador to China Waziri Juma also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 坦桑尼亚友好代表团到京  陈毅副总理和郭沫若副委员长前往机场迎接

1966-09-29
第6版()
专栏：

    坦桑尼亚友好代表团到京
    陈毅副总理和郭沫若副委员长前往机场迎接
    据新华社二十八日讯　应邀来我国参加国庆活动和进行友好访问的坦桑尼亚友好代表团，在坦桑尼亚海岸省省长基顿杜的率领下，今天晚上乘飞机到达北京。
    陈毅副总理、郭沫若副委员长和首都的一千多人，到机场热烈欢迎客人。前往机场欢迎的，还有外交部副部长刘晓等有关方面负责人。坦桑尼亚驻中国大使瓦齐里·朱马也到机场迎接。
