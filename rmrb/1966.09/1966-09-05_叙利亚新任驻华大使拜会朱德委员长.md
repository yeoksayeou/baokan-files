Title: Syria's New Ambassador to China Calls on Chairman Jude
Author:
Date: 1966-09-05
Edition: 4

Syria's new ambassador to China, Sadik, paid a courtesy call on Chairman Jude of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress this afternoon. Chairman Jude had a friendly conversation with him.

### 叙利亚新任驻华大使拜会朱德委员长

1966-09-05
第4版()
专栏：

    叙利亚新任驻华大使拜会朱德委员长
    新华社四日讯　叙利亚新任驻中国大使萨迪克今天下午拜会了全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德。朱德委员长同他进行了友好的谈话。
