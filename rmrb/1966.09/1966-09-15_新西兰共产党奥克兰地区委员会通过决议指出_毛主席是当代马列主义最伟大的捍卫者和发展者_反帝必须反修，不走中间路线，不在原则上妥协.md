Title: Auckland District Committee of the New Zealand Communist Party Passes Resolution
Author:
Date: 1966-09-15
Edition: 5

The Auckland District Committee of the New Zealand Communist Party passed a resolution at a recent meeting, praising Chairman Mao as the "greatest defender and developer of Marxism-Leninism in the contemporary world," and emphasizing that opposing imperialism must include opposing revisionism.

The resolution fully supports the statement made by New Zealand Communist Party General Secretary Wilcock on August 3, on behalf of the New Zealand Communist Party Central Committee, to celebrate the 45th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese Communist Party.

The resolution states that Wilcock's statement is "another contribution by the New Zealand Communist Party's national leadership to the anti-revisionist struggle in New Zealand and internationally, because revisionism remains the primary danger in the world communist movement. The statement correctly assesses the great leading role played by the Chinese Communist Party, headed by the greatest defender and developer of contemporary Marxism-Leninism, Mao Zedong, in this struggle."

The resolution points out: "The true Marxist-Leninists in the world, with the Chinese Communist Party as the vanguard, not only expose the treacherous theories and despicable actions of the revisionist followers of Khrushchev, but also show that to oppose imperialism, one must wage an uncompromising struggle against revisionism, unyielding, not taking the middle road, and not compromising on principles."

### 新西兰共产党奥克兰地区委员会通过决议指出  毛主席是当代马列主义最伟大的捍卫者和发展者  反帝必须反修，不走中间路线，不在原则上妥协

1966-09-15
第5版()
专栏：

    新西兰共产党奥克兰地区委员会通过决议指出
    毛主席是当代马列主义最伟大的捍卫者和发展者
    反帝必须反修，不走中间路线，不在原则上妥协
    新华社惠灵顿十三日电　新西兰共产党奥克兰地区委员会在最近举行的一次会议上通过决议，赞扬毛主席是“当代马克思列宁主义最伟大的捍卫者和发展者”，强调反对帝国主义必须反对修正主义。
    决议表示完全拥护新共总书记威尔科克斯同志八月三日代表新共全国书记处为庆祝中国共产党成立四十五周年而发表的声明。
    决议说，威尔科克斯同志的声明，是新共全国领导机构“对新西兰和国际反修斗争的又一贡献，因为修正主义仍然是世界共产主义运动中的主要危险。声明正确估计了以当代马克思列宁主义最伟大的捍卫者和发展者毛泽东为首的中国共产党，在这场斗争中所起的伟大领导作用。”
    决议指出：“以中国共产党为先锋队的世界上真正的马克思列宁主义者，不仅揭露了赫鲁晓夫及其修正主义追随者的背叛理论和卑鄙勾当，他们还表明，要反对帝国主义，就不能不对修正主义进行毫不留情的斗争，毫不动摇，不走中间路线，不在原则上妥协。”（附图片）
