Title: South Vietnamese Liberation Army Bombards Enemy Airfield, Destroys Ten Enemy Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-09-12
Edition: 6

	South Vietnamese Liberation Army Bombards Enemy Airfield, Destroys Ten Enemy Aircraft

	Hanoi, September 9 — According to a report from the Liberation Communications Agency of South Vietnam, on the night of August 28, the armed forces of the South Vietnamese Liberation Army, with the support of the local people, launched a fierce artillery attack on the enemy's airfield in Vung Tau Province. In one swift action, they destroyed ten enemy transport planes and helicopters, injuring many American aggressors and puppet soldiers.

### 南越解放军炮击敌军机场  击毁敌机十架

1966-09-12
第6版()
专栏：

    南越解放军炮击敌军机场
    击毁敌机十架
    新华社河内九日电  据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方解放武装力量八月二十八日夜晚在当地人民的支援下，猛烈炮击永隆省敌军机场，一举击毁敌人运输机和直升飞机十架，打伤许多美国侵略军和伪军。
