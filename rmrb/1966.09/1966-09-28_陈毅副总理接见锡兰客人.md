Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Sri Lankan Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-09-28
Edition: 4

Vice Premier Chen Yi today met with Mrs. Senaratne and Mr. Senaratne of Sri Lanka, who are currently visiting China. He had a friendly conversation with them.

### 陈毅副总理接见锡兰客人

1966-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见锡兰客人
    新华社二十七日讯　陈毅副总理今天接见正在我国访问的锡兰塞纳拉特那夫人和塞纳拉特那先生，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
