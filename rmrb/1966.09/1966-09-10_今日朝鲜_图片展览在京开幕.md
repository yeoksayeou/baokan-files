Title: "Today's Korea" Photo Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-09-10
Edition: 5

"The 'Today's Korea' photo exhibition opened today at the China Art Museum. The exhibition features over 180 photographs that reflect the construction achievements of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the heroic struggle of the people of South Korea. The exhibition is organized by the China International Cultural Exchange Association and the China-Korea Friendship Association in accordance with the implementation plan of the Sino-Korean Cultural Cooperation Agreement."

### “今日朝鲜”图片展览在京开幕

1966-09-10
第5版()
专栏：

    “今日朝鲜”图片展览在京开幕
    据新华社九日讯　“今日朝鲜”图片展览，今天在中国美术馆正式展出。展出的一百八十多幅图片，反映朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的建设成就，反映了南朝鲜人民的英勇斗争。展览会是对外文委、中朝友协根据中朝文化合作协定执行计划而举办的。
