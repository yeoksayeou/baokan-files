China's Ministry of Culture Donates a Special Piano to Burma
The Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China donated a specially made grand piano to the Ministry of Culture of the Union of Burma on September 9.

In the afternoon of September 9, a ceremony was held in Rangoon to present the gift. Chinese Ambassador to Burma, Geng Biao, on behalf of the Chinese government, handed over the piano to U Tin Tun, Deputy Secretary of the Union Ministry of Culture.

China's Red Cross Sends Medicines to Nepal
The Chinese Red Cross Society, in order to help the victims of the recent earthquake in western Nepal, donated 50,000 milliliters of cholera-typhoid-paratyphoid-tetanus pentavalent vaccine to the Nepal Red Cross Society.

The medicines have been handed over by Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, Yang Gongsu, to Princess Prinsip Shah, Chairperson of the Nepal Red Cross Society.

Princess Prinsip Shah expressed her gratitude for the concern shown by the Chinese people for the Nepalese people.

Egyptian Sailors Refuse to Transport U.S. War Supplies to Vietnam
According to a recent report by the Cairo-based newspaper *Al-Jumhuriya*, some sailors from the United Arab Republic refused to transport military supplies for the U.S. aggressors. The report stated that five sailors working on a Greek ship discovered that the ship was transporting military supplies to the U.S. aggressors in South Vietnam, and they refused to work.

    我文化部送缅甸一架特制钢琴
    中华人民共和国文化部九日赠给缅甸联邦文化部一架特制的大钢琴。
    九日下午在仰光举行了赠礼仪式。中国驻缅甸大使耿飚代表中国政府把钢琴交给了联邦文化部副秘书吴登丹。
  我红十字会送尼泊尔一批药品
    中国红十字会为救济尼泊尔西部最近地震中的受难者，把五万毫升霍乱伤寒—副伤寒甲、乙、破伤风类毒素五联制剂赠送给尼泊尔红十字会。
    这批药品已经由中国驻尼泊尔大使杨公素转交给尼泊尔红十字会主席普林塞普·沙阿公主。
    普林塞普·沙阿公主表示感谢中国人民对尼泊尔人民的关怀。
  阿联海员拒运美国侵越军需品
    据开罗《共和国报》最近报道，阿拉伯联合共和国的一些船员拒绝为美国侵略军运输军用物资。报道说，五名在希腊货船上工作的阿联船员，发觉这只船是给在南越的美国侵略军运输军需品时，他们就拒绝工作。
                      （据新华社）
