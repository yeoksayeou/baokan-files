Title: Speech at the National Propaganda Work Conference of the Communist Party of China
Author: Mao Zedong
Date: 1966-09-11
Edition: 3

In our country, the struggle to consolidate the socialist system, the struggle between socialism and capitalism to see who will prevail, will still take a very long historical period. However, we should all see that this new socialist system will definitely be consolidated.

### 《在中国共产党全国宣传工作会议上的讲话》
毛泽东
1966-09-11
第3版()
专栏：

    在我国，巩固社会主义制度的斗争，社会主义和资本主义谁战胜谁的斗争，还要经过一个很长的历史时期。但是，我们大家都应该看到，这个社会主义的新制度是一定会巩固起来的。
    毛泽东
    《在中国共产党全国宣传工作会议上的讲话》
