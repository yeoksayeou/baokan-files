Title: To Meet the People's Strong Desire to Study Mao Zedong Thought
Author:
Date: 1966-09-22
Edition: 5

To meet the strong desire of the people of Peru to study and master Mao Zedong Thought, the Communist Party of Peru has decided to publish a Spanish edition of the works of Chairman Mao.

In recent years, Mao Zedong Thought has been widely spread among the workers, peasants, and revolutionary intellectuals of Peru. Peruvian revolutionaries, especially revolutionary youth, are eager to study the works of Mao Zedong. They believe that Mao Zedong Thought is the highest level of Marxism-Leninism in the contemporary world and is the infallible guide for the Peruvian revolution.

The Communist Party of Peru is about to publish the following works of Mao Zedong: "Analysis of the Classes in Chinese Society," "Report on an Investigation of the Peasant Movement in Hunan," "The Spark Will Become a Flame," "Rectify the Party's Style of Work," "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship," "On Practice," and "On Contradiction."

### 为满足人民学习毛泽东思想的强烈愿望  秘共决定出版毛主席著作

1966-09-22
第5版()
专栏：

    为满足人民学习毛泽东思想的强烈愿望
    秘共决定出版毛主席著作
    新华社二十一日讯  利马消息：为了满足秘鲁人民要求学习和掌握毛泽东思想的强烈愿望，秘鲁共产党已决定出版毛泽东著作的西班牙文版。
    近几年来，毛泽东思想在秘鲁工人、农民和革命知识分子中间得到广泛的传播。秘鲁革命者，特别是革命青年，迫切要求学习毛泽东著作。他们认为，毛泽东思想是当代最高水平的马克思列宁主义，是秘鲁革命战无不胜的指南。
    秘鲁共产党即将出版的毛泽东著作有：《中国社会各阶级的分析》、《湖南农民运动考察报告》、《星星之火，可以燎原》、《关于纠正党内的错误思想》、《中国革命和中国共产党》、《实践论》和《矛盾论》等。
