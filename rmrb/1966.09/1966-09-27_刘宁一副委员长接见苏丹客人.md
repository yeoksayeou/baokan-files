Title: Vice Chairman Liu Ning Meets Sudanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-09-27
Edition: 6

Vice Chairman Liu Ning of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress received Sudanese National Assembly member, Central Executive Committee member of the National Union Party, and former Minister of State Muhammad Jubara Awad this afternoon and had a friendly conversation with him.

Present at the reception were Vice Secretary-General Lian Guan of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and others.

### 刘宁一副委员长接见苏丹客人

1966-09-27
第6版()
专栏：

    刘宁一副委员长接见苏丹客人
    新华社二十六日讯　人大常委会副委员长刘宁一今天下午接见苏丹制宪议会议员、民族联合党中央执委、前任内阁国务部长穆罕默德·朱巴拉·阿瓦德，同他进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有人大常委会副秘书长连贯等。
