Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1966-09-27
Edition: 6

Friendly Exchanges

	△ At the invitation of Vice Minister Wang Xinyuan of the First Ministry of Light Industry, the Director of the Office of the Ministry of Industry of Cambodia, Dith Dan, and Deputy Director of the Glass Factory, Nouvane, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 25th.

	△ The delegation of the Cambodia-China Friendship Association, consisting of three members led by President D. Bujok, arrived in Beijing on the evening of the 24th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries to participate in our national day activities.

	△ Professors Chahabi Lal Gamirel and Uma Prasad Shrestha of Nepal, at the invitation of our Association for Science and Technology, arrived in Beijing on the 24th for a friendly visit to our country.

	△ The delegation of the Icelandic-Chinese Cultural Association, consisting of writer Sigurdur Robertson and radio reporter Stefan Jonsson, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 25th at the invitation to participate in our national day activities and conduct a friendly visit to our country.

	△ Professor of Physiology at the Karolinska Institute in Sweden, Von Euler, and his wife, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 25th at the invitation of the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences for a visit to our country.

	△ The delegation of the University of Mexico, consisting of five members led by President Luis Quintero, arrived in Beijing by plane in the afternoon of the 26th at the invitation of the China-Latin America Friendship Association to participate in our national day activities. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 友好往来

1966-09-27
第6版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    △应第一轻工业部副部长王新元邀请来我国访问的柬埔寨工业部办公厅主任狄丹和玻璃厂副厂长纳望努，于二十五日乘飞机到达北京。
    △应中国人民对外文化友好协会邀请来参加我国庆活动的比中协会代表团一行三人，在团长德·布若克率领下，已于二十四日晚到达北京。
    △尼泊尔查哈比·拉尔·加米雷尔和乌马·普拉萨德·什雷斯塔教授，应我科学技术协会的邀请前来我国进行友好访问，已在二十四日到达北京。
    △应邀来我国参加国庆活动和进行友好访问的，由作家西古尔·罗伯特逊和国家广播电台记者斯蒂凡·荣逊组成的冰岛中国文化协会代表团，二十五日乘飞机到达北京。
    △瑞典卡洛琳斯卡医学院生理学教授冯·艾勒和夫人应中国医学科学院邀请来我国访问，二十五日乘飞机到达北京。
    △应中拉友协邀请来我国参加国庆活动的墨西哥大学代表团一行五人，在团长路易斯·金塔尼利亚博士率领下，于二十六日下午乘飞机到达北京。（据新华社）
