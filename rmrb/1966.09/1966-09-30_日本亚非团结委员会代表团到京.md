Title: Delegation of the Japan Asia-Africa Solidarity Committee Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-09-30
Edition: 4

A delegation from the Japan Asia-Africa Solidarity Committee, led by Sakamoto Tokumatsu and Kai Seima, arrived in Beijing today by airplane. They were invited to participate in our national day celebrations.

Liao Chengzhi and other relevant officials welcomed them warmly at the airport. Among those present to greet the delegation were the Japanese peace activist Nishizonoji Koichi, who is currently in Beijing.

### 日本亚非团结委员会代表团到京

1966-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

    日本亚非团结委员会代表团到京
    新华社二十九日讯　由坂本德松和甲斐静马率领的日本亚非团结委员会访华代表团，应邀前来我国参加国庆活动，今天晚上乘飞机到达北京。
    廖承志等有关方面负责人到机场热烈欢迎。前来迎接的还有在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一。
