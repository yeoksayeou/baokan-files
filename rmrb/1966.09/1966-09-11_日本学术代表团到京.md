Title: Japanese Academic Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-09-11
Edition: 5

A Japanese academic delegation led by Professor Emeritus Hiroshi Kawasumi of Tokyo University, consisting of nine members, arrived in Beijing on September 9 at the invitation of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. Tonight, Qin Lisheng, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, hosted a banquet to welcome the guests.

### 日本学术代表团到京

1966-09-11
第5版()
专栏：

    日本学术代表团到京
    新华社十日讯　以东京大学名誉教授河角广为首的日本学术代表团一行九人，应中国科学院的邀请到中国访问，于九日到达北京。今晚，中国科学院副秘书长秦力生设宴欢迎客人。
