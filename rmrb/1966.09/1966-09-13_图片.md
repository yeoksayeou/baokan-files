Title: Members of the Dongfanghong Commune's Xingwu Brigade's Eleventh Production Team Irrigate Sweet Potato Fields to Combat Drought
Author:
Date: 1966-09-13
Edition: 2

Members of the Dongfanghong Commune's Xingwu Brigade's Eleventh Production Team in Xuyi County, Jiangsu, are irrigating their sweet potato fields to combat the drought, striving for a good autumn harvest.
