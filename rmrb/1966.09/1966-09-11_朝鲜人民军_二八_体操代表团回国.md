Title: The Korean People's Army "28" Gymnastics Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1966-09-11
Edition: 5

The Korean People's Army "28" gymnastics delegation, led by Colonel Huang Zhen, has completed its visit to our country and departed Beijing by plane in the afternoon of the 9th to return home. Those who went to the airport to see them off included Li Wei, Tian Tao, and other relevant department heads. Colonel Choi Jeong-yeong, military attaché of the Korean Embassy in China, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 朝鲜人民军“二八”体操代表团回国

1966-09-11
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民军“二八”体操代表团回国
    新华社九日讯　由黄轸上校率领的朝鲜人民军“二八”体操代表团，已经结束在我国的访问，于九日下午乘飞机离开北京回国。到机场欢送的，有李伟、田涛等有关单位负责人。朝鲜驻中国大使馆武官崔济荣上校等，也前往机场送行。
