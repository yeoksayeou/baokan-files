Title: Delegation of the Secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists' Association Visits Pakistan, Cambodia, and Nepal
Author:
Date: 1966-09-11
Edition: 6

At the invitation of the news circles of Pakistan, Cambodia, and the Nepalese Asia-Africa Solidarity Committee and news circles, the friendly delegation of the Secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists' Association departed from Beijing by plane today to visit the aforementioned countries.

The delegation members include: Saburo Yamazaki (Japan), Karume Jumayi (Tanzania), and Yang Yi (China).

Those who came to the airport to see them off included Supeno, the Indonesian representative of the Secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists' Association, and others.

### 亚非记协书记处代表团应邀访问巴柬尼三国

1966-09-11
第6版()
专栏：

    亚非记协书记处代表团
    应邀访问巴柬尼三国
    新华社十日讯　应巴基斯坦、柬埔寨等国新闻界和尼泊尔亚非团结委员会与新闻界的邀请，亚非新闻工作者协会书记处友好代表团今天乘飞机离开北京，前往上述国家进行友好访问。代表团成员有：杉山市平（日本）、卡荣朱迈勒（坦桑尼亚）和杨翊（中国）。
    到机场送行的有亚非新闻工作者协会书记处印度尼西亚代表苏佩诺等。
