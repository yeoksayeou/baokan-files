Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-09-29
Edition: 6

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo received the fourth batch of Japanese Socialist Party activists led by Eryu Eichi today morning and had a friendly and cordial conversation with them. Present at the meeting were Wang Yinfu and others.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人

1966-09-29
第6版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人
    新华社二十八日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若今天上午接见了以羽根荣一为首的第四批日本社会党活动家代表团，和他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的有王荫圃等。
