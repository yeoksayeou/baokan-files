Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1966-09-16
Edition: 5

	Friendly Exchanges

	△ At the invitation of the Foreign Languages Press, a five-member cultural delegation from Somalia, led by its head, Mohamed Karif Jama, Director of the Education Department, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 15th to conduct a friendly visit and participate in our national day celebrations.

	△ In accordance with the 1966 execution plan of the China-Guinea cultural cooperation agreement, Guinea writer N. K. Koundet and his wife arrived in Beijing on the 15th.

	△ The 13-member delegation of the Korean People's Army, led by team leader Colonel Lee Geon-san, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 15th to participate in the friendly radio telegraphy contest among friendly armies.

	△ At the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association, a four-member delegation of journalists from Guinea, led by Diallo Le Louma, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 15th.

	△ The Vietnam Democratic Republic youth track and field team concluded its friendly visit to our country and departed by train from Nanning on the afternoon of the 12th to return home.

	△ After visiting Beijing and Shanghai, the Mali national football team departed by plane from Guangzhou on the evening of the 10th to return home.

	△ The delegation of the All-China Federation of Agricultural and Forestry Trade Unions, invited to participate in the Ceylon Plantation Workers' Union Conference and the First Session of the All-Ceylon Farmers' Conference, departed by plane from Beijing on the 14th. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 友好往来

1966-09-16
第5版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    △应对外文委邀请前来我国进行友好访问并参加我国庆活动的索马里文化代表团一行五人，在团长、索马里教育部司长穆罕默德·卡里夫·贾马率领下，于十五日乘飞机到达北京。
    △根据中国几内亚文化合作协定一九六六年执行计划，几内亚作家聂·卡·孔德特和夫人十五日到达北京。
    △参加友军无线电快速收发报友谊赛的朝鲜人民军代表队一行十三人，在领队李根产中校率领下，十五日乘飞机到北京。
    △应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请，由迪亚洛·勒卢马率领的几内亚新闻工作者代表团一行四人，十五日乘飞机到北京。
    △越南民主共和国青年田径队结束了在我国的友好访问，十二日下午乘火车离开南宁回国。
    △马里国家足球队访问了北京、上海后于十日晚乘飞机离广州回国。
    △应邀参加锡兰种植园工人工会代表大会和全锡兰农民大会第一届代表大会的中国农林工会代表团，十四日乘飞机离开北京。　（据新华社）
