Title: Fully Exploit the Power of People's War to Annihilate Large Numbers of Enemy Forces
Author:
Date: 1966-09-15
Edition: 5

The military and civilian population of the Central Region of South Vietnam have, in the past year since the launch of the "Decisive Battle to Defeat the American Aggressors" competition in July last year, fully exploited the invincible power of people's war, fought bravely, and achieved brilliant victories.

According to incomplete statistics, from July last year to July this year, the military and civilian population of the Central Region have annihilated a total of 126,000 enemy troops, including nearly 50,000 American aggressors and nearly 10,000 South Korean mercenaries. They have completely annihilated 24 enemy infantry battalions, 3 armored battalions, and 163 companies, including 8 American infantry battalions, 51 companies, and 9 South Korean companies. They have shot down and destroyed 1,565 enemy aircraft, destroyed 400 armored vehicles, 1,000 other military vehicles, and 67 cannons of various calibers, burned 25 million liters of gasoline, and seized nearly 10,000 weapons of various types.

At present, the military and civilian population of the Central Region, inspired by the great battle call of Chairman Ho Chi Minh, are further vigorously carrying out the "Decisive Battle to Defeat the American Aggressors" competition, fighting stronger and stronger, determined to annihilate more enemy forces and achieve even greater victories.

Hanoi, September 14, Xinhua News Agency: According to a report from the Liberation News Agency of South Vietnam, the Liberation Army of South Vietnam launched a fierce attack on two U.S. military construction sites on the outskirts of Saigon on the night of September 13, destroying and severely damaging 55 U.S. military trucks on the spot.

According to reports from foreign news agencies, two units of the Liberation Army raided two construction sites of the American Raymond Morrison and Knudsen Public Works Company between Go Dau and Saigon that night and launched an attack about 5 kilometers northeast of Saigon. In this attack, U.S. truck drivers, sentries, and others were injured or killed.

### 充分发挥人民战争威力　大量消灭敌人有生力量  南越中部中区军民一年歼敌十二万多  五万美国侵略军和一万南朝鲜帮凶军被歼灭

1966-09-15
第5版()
专栏：

    充分发挥人民战争威力　大量消灭敌人有生力量
    南越中部中区军民一年歼敌十二万多
    五万美国侵略军和一万南朝鲜帮凶军被歼灭
    新华社河内十四日电  据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方中部中区军民，在去年七月开展“决战决胜美国侵略强盗”竞赛运动以来的一年中，发挥人民战争的无敌威力，英勇战斗，取得辉煌胜利。
    据不完全统计，从去年七月到今年七月，中部中区军民共歼灭敌人十二万六千名，其中包括美国侵略军近五万名和南朝鲜帮凶军近一万名，全歼敌人二十四个步兵营，三个装甲营和一百六十三个连，其中包括美国侵略军八个步兵营、五十一个连和南朝鲜帮凶军九个连，击落、击毁敌机一千五百六十五架，击毁装甲车四百辆，其它军车一千辆和各种口径的大炮六十七门，烧毁汽油二千五百多万公升，缴获各种武器近一万件。
    目前，中部中区军民在胡志明主席的伟大战斗号召的鼓舞下，正在进一步大力开展“决战决胜美国侵略强盗”的竞赛运动，越战越强，决心消灭敌人更多的有生力量，夺取更加巨大的胜利。
    新华社十四日讯　西贡消息：越南南方解放军十三日夜里猛烈袭击西贡边缘的两个美军工地，当场击毁和严重击坏美国军用卡车五十五辆。
    据外国通讯社报道，解放军的两支部队，在当天夜里冲进了美国雷蒙德·莫里森和克努德森公共工程公司在守德和西贡之间的两个工地，并且在西贡东北约五公里的地方发动了进攻。在这次袭击中，美军的卡车司机、哨兵等都遭到了伤亡。
