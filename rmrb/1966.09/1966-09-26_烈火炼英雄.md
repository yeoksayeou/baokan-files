Title: Forging Heroes in the Furnace of Fire
Author: Wú Hóngyuán
Date: 1966-09-26
Edition: 3

The heroic drilling team,
    Truly wonderful, truly wonderful,
    You are a great furnace,
    You are a great school,
    Mao Zedong Thought is mastered,
    Everyone dares to shoulder heavy burdens.
    Responding to the leader's call,
    Embarking on the revolutionary path,
    Searching for gas and oil for the motherland,
    Enduring wind and rain without complaint!

The heroic drilling team,
    Struggling in the great oil field.
    The derrick stands tall,
    The drilling machine roars, shaking the mountains and rivers;
    With both hands, seize the gas tiger,
    With one heart, develop the great oil field.
    Day and night, toiling tirelessly,
    Revolutionary enthusiasm soars,
    The highest directives are firmly in mind,
    Mountains of knives and seas of fire are mere trifles!

The heroic drilling team,
    Everyone's heart is red,
    Gas wells explode, flames soar,
    Everyone bravely rushes forward;
    United in effort to rescue,
    Even if the flames rage.
    Standing firm at the battle station,
    The nation's wealth is in our hearts;
    Treading through a thousand waves of fire,
    Forging heroes in the furnace of fire!

### 烈火炼英雄
吴洪源
1966-09-26
第3版()
专栏：

    烈火炼英雄
    解放军某部　吴洪源
    英雄的钻井队，
    真是好呀真是好，
    你是一座大熔炉，
    你是一所大学校，
    毛泽东思想学到手，
    人人敢把重担挑。
    响应领袖号召，
    踏上革命大道，
    找气找油为祖国，
    餐风宿露不辞劳！
    英雄的钻井队，
    奋战大油田。
    井架高高竖起来，
    钻机隆隆震山川；
    双手捉住气老虎，
    一心开发大油田。
    日夜艰苦奋战，
    革命干劲冲天，
    最高指示牢记心头，
    刀山火海只等闲！
    英雄的钻井队，
    人人肝胆红，
    气井爆炸起大火，
    个个勇猛向前冲；
    齐心合力去抢救，
    哪怕它烈焰逞凶。
    坚守战斗岗位，
    国家财富记心中；
    踏平火海千层浪，
    烈火当中炼英雄！
