Title: A U.S. Plane Shot Down by Hanoi's Military and Civilians
Author:
Date: 1966-09-16
Edition: 6

A U.S. plane shot down by Hanoi's military and civilians is seen smoking and burning as it crashes. In the upper right, an American pilot is seen parachuting to safety. He was captured immediately after landing.
