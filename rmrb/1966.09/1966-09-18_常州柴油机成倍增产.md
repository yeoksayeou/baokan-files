Title: Diesel Engines in Changzhou See Fold Increase in Production
Author:
Date: 1966-09-18
Edition: 3

	Workers at the Changzhou Diesel Engine Factory in Jiangsu Province have, during the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, studied and applied Chairman Mao's works diligently. Their revolutionary spirit has been greatly invigorated, and their revolutionary willpower has been greatly enhanced, effectively driving the development of production. By the end of August, the factory had already produced 5,000 small horsepower diesel engines, doubling last year's production.

### 常州柴油机成倍增产

1966-09-18
第3版()
专栏：

    常州柴油机成倍增产
    据新华社南京电　江苏常州柴油机厂职工在无产阶级文化大革命中，活学活用毛主席著作，革命精神大振，革命志气大长，有力地推动了生产的发展。到八月底止，全厂已经生产了五千台小马力柴油机，相当于去年产量的两倍。
