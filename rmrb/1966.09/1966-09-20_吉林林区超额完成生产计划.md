Title: Jilin Forestry Region Exceeds Production Plan
Author:
Date: 1966-09-20
Edition: 2

The revolutionary workers of the Jilin Province forestry region have, in the unprecedented Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, actively studied and applied Chairman Mao's works, grasped the revolution, and promoted production, achieving double victories in revolution and timber production. In August, the province's timber production, following the completion of the state plan for each type and specification from January to July, exceeded the state plan by 7.9 percent despite heavy rain and severe water damage.

### 吉林林区超额完成生产计划

1966-09-20
第2版()
专栏：

    吉林林区超额完成生产计划
    吉林省林区广大革命职工，在史无前例的无产阶级文化大革命中，活学活用毛主席著作，抓革命，促生产，取得了革命和木材生产双胜利。八月份全省木材生产，继一至七月月月按品种、规格完成国家计划之后，在雨多、水害严重的情况下，又超额百分之七点九完成了国家计划。
