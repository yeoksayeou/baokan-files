Title: China Council for the Promotion of International Trade Hosts Dinner for Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-09-30
Edition: 4

To celebrate the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade held a reception tonight to welcome Japanese friends from friendly trading companies in Beijing. Chairman of the China-Japan Friendship Association, Liao Chengzhi, and relevant officials attended the reception. Also present were Japanese peace activists in Beijing, such as Nishizonoji Koichi.

At the reception, guests and hosts toasted to the greatness of Mao Zedong Thought, celebrating the great victory of the Chinese Cultural Revolution, and wishing the great teacher, great leader, great commander, and great helmsman, Chairman Mao Zedong, eternal life.

In their toasts, they unanimously expressed their determination to remove all artificial obstacles on the path of Sino-Japanese friendship and the development of Sino-Japanese trade, to thoroughly smash the conspiracies and tricks of U.S. imperialism, the Sato government, and modern revisionism and their various followers, and to strive together for the continuous development of friendship between the peoples of China and Japan and the continuous development of Sino-Japanese friendly trade.

According to a report by Xinhua News Agency on the 28th, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Xiao Fangzhou, hosted a dinner tonight to welcome the delegation of the Japan Federation of Local Assembly Members for the Promotion of International Trade, led by Jihawa Hiro. The delegation arrived in Beijing on the 27th to participate in the National Day celebrations at the invitation of China.

### 我国际贸易促进会宴请日本客人

1966-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

    我国际贸易促进会宴请日本客人
    新华社二十九日讯为庆祝中华人民共和国成立十七周年，中国国际贸易促进委员会今晚举行招待会，招待在京的日本友好商社的朋友。中日友好协会会长廖承志以及有关方面负责人出席了招待会。出席招待会的还有在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一。
    宾主在招待会上纷纷祝酒，颂扬伟大的毛泽东思想，庆贺中国无产阶级文化大革命的伟大胜利，祝愿中国人民的伟大导师、伟大领袖、伟大统帅、伟大舵手毛泽东主席万寿无疆。
    他们在祝酒中还一致表示，要扫除中日友好和中日贸易发展道路上的种种人为障碍，彻底粉碎美帝国主义、佐藤政府和现代修正主义及其形形色色的追随者的阴谋诡计，为中日两国人民间的友谊和中日友好贸易的不断发展，而共同奋斗到底。
    据新华社二十八日讯  中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席萧方洲今晚举行宴会，招待由实川博率领的日本地方议员促进国际贸易联盟代表团。代表团是应邀来我国参加国庆活动，于二十七日到达北京的。
