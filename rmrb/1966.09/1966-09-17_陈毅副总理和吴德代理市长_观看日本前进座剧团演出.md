Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi and Acting Mayor Wu De Watch Performance by Japan's Zengeki Theater Troupe
Author:
Date: 1966-09-17
Edition: 5

	Vice Premier Chen Yi and Acting Mayor of Beijing Wu De watched the performance by the Japanese Zengeki Theater Troupe tonight. They met with the troupe leader Kawaharasaki Chojuro, deputy leader Nakamura Ken'emon, Kawaharasaki Shizuka, and other main performers during the intermission, engaging in a friendly and cordial conversation.

	After the Japanese artists finished their performance, both the stage and the audience sang "The East Is Red" in unison.

	Vice Premier Chen Yi and others went on stage to shake hands with the performers, congratulating them on their successful performance.

	Liao Chengzhi, Nan Hanchen, Song Yiping, Zhou Yiping, and other relevant officials also watched the performance.

### 陈毅副总理和吴德代理市长  观看日本前进座剧团演出

1966-09-17
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理和吴德代理市长
    观看日本前进座剧团演出
    新华社十六日讯　陈毅副总理和北京市代理市长吴德，今晚观看了日本前进座剧团演出的话剧《本牧亭》和《胡套》，并在演出休息时接见了团长河原崎长十郎、副团长中村翫右卫门、河原崎静江和主要演员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    日本艺术家演出结束后，台上台下同声高唱《东方红》。
    陈毅副总理等登上舞台，同演员们亲切握手，祝贺他们演出成功。
    廖承志、南汉宸、宋一平、周一萍等有关方面负责人也观看了演出。
