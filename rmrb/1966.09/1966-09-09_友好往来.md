Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1966-09-09
Edition: 4

A delegation of 28 members from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam's youth football team, led by Pan Angdang, arrived in Beijing by train on the morning of the 8th.

The delegations from 11 countries and regions participating in the 1966 Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational began departing Beijing on the 5th to visit other cities or return home.

At the invitation of the China People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, a delegation from the New Zealand China Association, consisting of Ki McArthur and Di Xiansen, arrived in Beijing by plane in the evening of the 7th.

Meng Jisen, a representative from Tanzania who missed the 12th World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs due to a late arrival, visited China at the invitation of the Asian-African Solidarity Committee. He departed Beijing in the morning of the 8th to return home. Meng Jisen arrived in Beijing on the 3rd. He expressed support for the just stance of the 16-country representatives who withdrew from the World Conference and agreed with their statement.

### 友好往来

1966-09-09
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    越南民主共和国青年足球队一行二十八人，由潘昂当率领，八日上午乘火车到达北京。
    参加一九六六年北京国际乒乓球邀请赛的十一个国家和地区的乒乓球代表团，从五日开始陆续离开北京去外地访问或回国。
    应中国人民对外文化友好协会邀请，由奇·麦克阿瑟和第·夏逊组成的新西兰中国协会访华代表团，七日晚乘飞机到达北京。
    因迟到而未赶上参加第十二届禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会的坦桑尼亚代表孟吉森，应我亚非团结委员会邀请来我国访问后，八日早晨离开北京回国。孟吉森是在三日到达北京的。他对十六国代表退出世界大会的正义立场表示支持，并同意他们发表的声明。
