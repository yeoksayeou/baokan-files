Title: Vice Chairman Dong Biwu Meets with Speaker Mukhtar
Author:
Date: 1966-09-13
Edition: 5

Vice Chairman Dong Biwu today afternoon received Sheikh Mukhtar Muhammad Hussein, Speaker of the Somali National Assembly, and the entire delegation of the Somali National Assembly Friendship Delegation led by him. They had a cordial and friendly conversation.

Present at the reception were Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo and relevant responsible persons Lian Guan and Xie Feng, among others. (Photo attached)

The photo shows a group photo taken during the reception. The sixth person from the left in the front row is Speaker Sheikh Mukhtar Muhammad Hussein. Photographed by Xinhua News Agency reporter.

### 董必武副主席接见穆克塔尔议长

1966-09-13
第5版()
专栏：

    董必武副主席接见穆克塔尔议长
    新华社十二日讯　董必武副主席今天下午接见索马里国民议会议长谢赫·穆克塔尔·穆罕默德·侯赛因，以及由他率领的索马里国民议会友好代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有人大常委会副委员长郭沫若和有关方面负责人连贯、谢丰等。（附图片）
    图为接见时合影。前左第六人是谢赫·穆克塔尔·穆罕默德·侯赛因议长。　　新华社记者摄
