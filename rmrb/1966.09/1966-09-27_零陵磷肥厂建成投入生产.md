Title: Zero Ling Phosphatic Fertilizer Plant Completed and Put into Production
Author:
Date: 1966-09-27
Edition: 2

The phosphatic fertilizer plant in Zero Ling District, Hunan Province, with an annual production capacity of 60,000 tons of calcium-magnesium phosphate fertilizer, has been completed and officially put into production.

The construction of this plant began in May of last year. The masses and cadres participating in the construction held high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, learned from the People's Liberation Army, and learned from Daqing. They fully demonstrated the spirit of self-reliance and striving for progress, achieving more than ten major technological innovations. This ensured the completion of the plant construction quickly, efficiently, and economically.

### 零陵磷肥厂建成投入生产

1966-09-27
第2版()
专栏：

    零陵磷肥厂建成投入生产
    年产六万吨钙镁磷肥的湖南省零陵专区磷肥厂，已经建成并正式投入生产。
    这个厂是去年五月破土动工的。参加施工的群众和干部，高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，大学解放军，大学大庆，充分发挥自力更生、奋发图强的精神，搞成了十多项重大技术革新，保证多快好省地完成了建厂任务。
