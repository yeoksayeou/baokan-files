Title: Learn and Apply in Struggle
Author:
Date: 1966-09-20
Edition: 4

More than 40,000 revolutionary masses in Urumqi joyfully received the "Quotations from Chairman Mao." Overjoyed to have Chairman Mao's book, they vowed to study and apply Chairman Mao's works in struggle, to seize the revolution and promote production, striving for double victories in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and production.

As bookstores began selling the books amidst the sound of gongs and firecrackers, many workers, revolutionary cadres, and youths enthusiastically cheered. Over 30 revolutionary workers from the Fifth Team of the Transportation Company under the Autonomous Region's Transportation Bureau have long carried Chairman Mao's works, traveling back and forth on the transportation lines south of the Tian Shan Mountains. Whenever they arrived at a station, they would study Chairman Mao's works with the station's workers. On this day, after purchasing the "Quotations from Chairman Mao," they were extremely excited. They said, "Chairman Mao's works are our compass. With the great Mao Zedong Thought, we can distinguish between friends and foes and overthrow all ghosts and monsters."

The New China Bookstore also delivered books to rural areas and schools. After receiving the "Quotations from Chairman Mao," members of the Second Production Team of the First Brigade of Hongguang Commune in Urumqi County joyfully sang revolutionary songs and performed items such as "Chairman Mao's Works Are Good." They vowed to follow Chairman Mao's instructions, to seize the revolution and promote production, ensuring double victories in the cultural revolution and production.

### 在斗争中学　在斗争中用

1966-09-20
第4版()
专栏：

    在斗争中学　在斗争中用
    据新华社乌鲁木齐电  乌鲁木齐市四万多革命群众喜得《毛主席语录》。他们得到毛主席的书，无比兴奋，表示要在斗争中活学活用毛主席著作，抓革命，促生产，力争文化大革命和生产双胜利。
    当各书店在锣鼓、鞭炮声中开始售书时，许多工人、革命干部和青少年热烈欢呼。自治区交通厅运输公司第五车队的三十多个革命职工，长期以来带着毛主席著作，在天山南北运输线上往返，他们每到一站，就同站上的职工一起学习毛主席著作。这天他们买到《毛主席语录》后心情十分激动。他们说，毛主席著作是我们前进的方向盘。有了伟大的毛泽东思想，我们就能分清敌我，打倒一切牛鬼蛇神。新华书店还送书到农村和学校去。乌鲁木齐县红光公社一大队第二生产队的社员得到《毛主席语录》后，高兴地唱起革命歌曲，并演出了《毛主席著作就是好》等节目。他们表示要按毛主席指示，抓革命促生产，保证文化革命和生产双胜利。
