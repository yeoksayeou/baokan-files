Title: Study Chairman Mao's Works, Follow Chairman Mao's Words, Act According to Chairman Mao's Instructions, Be a Good Soldier of Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1966-09-26
Edition: 4

In the midst of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, the vast numbers of workers, peasants, and soldiers of our country have taken on the attitude of masters, striding confidently onto the stage of literature and art. This is a group of amateur performers from all over the country rehearsing on the stage of the capital, preparing to perform songs praising the great leader Chairman Mao and the invincible Mao Zedong Thought for the National Day celebration.

The people of all ethnic groups in Urumqi are holding Chairman Mao's works and raising high portraits of Chairman Mao, determined to advance bravely in the direction indicated by Chairman Mao!

The Daqing Oil Field is a great school of Mao Zedong Thought. The people of Daqing are striving to read Chairman Mao's works, wholeheartedly listen to Chairman Mao's words, and resolutely act according to Chairman Mao's instructions, achieving one great victory after another in production and construction.

This is the "Iron Man" Wang Jinxi (center) of Daqing and the workers of the 1205 drilling team, who have created the world's most advanced drilling level, studying the "Quotations from Chairman Mao" together, determined to advance and seize new victories.

The broad ranks of officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army are determined to turn our army into a great school of Mao Zedong Thought, making our army advance in big strides on the road of becoming highly proletarianized and highly combat-ready. This is the political instructor Xu Shouqi of the "Vanguard Boat of the Sea" giving a lecture on Chairman Mao's works to the soldiers.

The broad masses of our militia are studying Chairman Mao's thoughts on people's war, with doubled confidence and vigilance, ready at any time to cooperate with the People's Liberation Army to annihilate the imperialist aggressors and their running dogs in the vast ocean of people's war.

This is the militia of Yantian Brigade, Salt Field Production Team, Shahekou Commune, Bao'an County, Guangdong Province, studying Chairman Mao's works before training.

The people of our country, taking the People's Liberation Army as an example, have launched a new high tide of studying and applying Chairman Mao's works, determined to arm their minds with Mao Zedong Thought, to be critics of the old world and builders of the new world. This is a meeting of the members of Mobei Commune, Xingan County, Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, to share their experiences in studying and applying Chairman Mao's works.

### 读毛主席的书  听毛主席的话 照毛主席的指示办事  做毛主席的好战士（图片）

1966-09-26
第4版()
专栏：

    读毛主席的书  听毛主席的话  照毛主席的指示办事  做毛主席的好战士
    在无产阶级文化大革命的高潮中，我国广大工农兵以主人翁的姿态，昂首阔步走上文艺舞台。这是来自全国各地的工农兵业余演员在首都舞台上排练歌颂伟大领袖毛主席，歌颂战无不胜的毛泽东思想的节目，准备国庆演出。
    乌鲁木齐市各族人民手拿毛主席著作，高举毛主席画像，表示坚决沿着毛主席指引的方向奋勇前进！
    大庆油田是一座毛泽东思想的大学校。大庆人努力读毛主席的书，一心听毛主席的话，坚决按照毛主席的指示办事，在生产建设上取得了一个又一个伟大的胜利。
    这是大庆“铁人”王进喜（中）和创造世界钻井最先进水平的一二○五钻井队工人一起学习《毛主席语录》，决心乘胜前进，夺取新胜利。
    中国人民解放军广大指战员决心把我军办成毛泽东思想的大学校，使我军在非常无产阶级化、非常战斗化的道路上大踏步前进。这是英勇无敌的“海上先锋艇”的指导员徐寿祺正给战士们讲解毛主席著作。
    我国广大民兵努力学习毛主席关于人民战争的思想，正以百倍信心，百倍警惕，随时准备配合人民解放军，把胆敢侵犯我国的美帝国主义及其帮凶消灭在人民战争的汪洋大海里。
    这是广东省宝安县沙头角公社盐田大队民兵在训练前学习毛主席著作。
    我国人民以解放军为榜样，掀起了活学活用毛主席著作的新高潮，决心用毛泽东思想武装头脑，做旧世界的批判者，新世界的建设者。这是广西壮族自治区兴安县漠川公社社员举行活学活用毛主席著作讲用会。                        本版照片均系新华社记者摄
