Title: At the Invitation of the CPC Central Committee, Comrade N. Sanmugathasan Arrives in Beijing to Participate in National Day Activities
Author:
Date: 1966-09-29
Edition: 1

At the invitation of the CPC Central Committee, Comrade N. Sanmugathasan, a member of the Political Bureau and the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Ceylon, arrived in Beijing by plane this evening to participate in our National Day activities and to conduct a friendly visit.

Comrade Tan Zhenlin, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and Comrade Liu Ningyi, member of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, were at the airport to give a warm welcome. Young Pioneers presented fresh flowers to Comrade N. Sanmugathasan.

### 应中共中央邀请参加我国庆活动  纳·桑穆加塔桑同志到京  谭震林刘宁一等同志到机场欢迎

1966-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    应中共中央邀请参加我国庆活动
    纳·桑穆加塔桑同志到京
    谭震林刘宁一等同志到机场欢迎
    新华社二十八日讯　锡兰共产党中央政治局委员、书记处书记纳·桑穆加塔桑同志，应中共中央委员会的邀请来我国参加国庆活动和进行友好访问，今天晚上乘飞机到达北京。
    中共中央政治局委员谭震林同志，中共中央书记处书记刘宁一同志，到机场热烈欢迎。少先队员向纳·桑穆加塔桑同志献了鲜花。
