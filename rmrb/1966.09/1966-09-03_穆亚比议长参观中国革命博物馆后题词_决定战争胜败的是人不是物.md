Title: Speaker Muambi's Inscription After Visiting the Chinese Revolutionary Museum
Author:
Date: 1966-09-03
Edition: 3

Speaker Muambi of the Congolese National Assembly inscribed the following after visiting the Chinese Revolutionary Museum: "I am convinced of Chairman Mao Zedong's great thought: what determines the outcome of a war is people, not things."

Speaker Muambi also wrote that this visit to the Chinese Revolutionary Museum "allowed us to understand the long-term liberation struggle of the heroic Chinese people."

Speaker Muambi and his wife, along with members of the Congolese National Assembly delegation led by him, were accompanied by Vice Chairman Guo Moruo and his wife. They were warmly welcomed by the responsible officials and staff of the Revolutionary Museum during their visit today.

### 穆亚比议长参观中国革命博物馆后题词  决定战争胜败的是人不是物

1966-09-03
第3版()
专栏：

    穆亚比议长参观中国革命博物馆后题词
    决定战争胜败的是人不是物
    新华社二日讯　刚果（布）国民议会议长穆亚比今天参观中国革命博物馆后，在博物馆留言簿上题词，表示“确信毛泽东主席的伟大思想：决定战争胜败的是人，而不是物”。
    穆亚比议长还写道，这次访问中国革命博物馆，“使我们了解到英雄的中国人民所进行的长期的解放斗争的情况”。
    穆亚比议长和夫人以及由他率领的刚果（布）议会代表团的成员，由郭沫若副委员长和夫人陪同，在今天参观的时候，受到革命博物馆负责人和工作人员的热烈欢迎。
