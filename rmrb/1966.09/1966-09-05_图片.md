Title: In the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, Shanghai Shipbuilding Factory's Copper Pipe Bending Team Persists in Studying Chairman Mao's Works for Eight Years
Author:
Date: 1966-09-05
Edition: 3

In the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, the workers of the copper pipe bending team at Shanghai Shipbuilding Factory, who have persisted in studying Chairman Mao's works for eight years, are using Mao Zedong Thought as their weapon. They are determined to carry the Cultural Revolution through to the end and to promote a new production upsurge. This is a scene of the team studying Chairman Mao's works.

	New China News Agency Photographer
