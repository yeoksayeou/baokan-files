Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1966-09-04
Edition: 5

At the invitation of China, a Cambodian swimming, diving, and water polo delegation of 46 people, led by the director of the Cambodian Sports Bureau, Chhum Soun Pan, arrived in Beijing by airplane on the evening of the 3rd.

At the invitation of the Chinese People's Relief Association, the fifth delegation from the Japanese National Rescue Association, led by Saito Yoshinari, arrived in Beijing by airplane on the 2nd.

At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the delegation of the Mauritania Workers' Union, led by the general secretary of the union, Fal Marlik, arrived in Beijing by airplane on the 1st.

At the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs and the Chinese Medical Association, Mrs. Senaratne and Mr. Senaratne from Ceylon arrived in Beijing by airplane on the evening of the 2nd.

(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 友好往来

1966-09-04
第5版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    应邀来我国进行友好访问的柬埔寨游泳、跳水、水球代表团一行四十六人，由柬埔寨体育局办公室主任春梳潘率领，三日晚乘飞机到达北京。
    应中国人民救济总会的邀请来我国进行访问的，由斋藤喜作率领的日本国民救援会代表团第五次访华代表团一行八人，二日乘飞机到达北京。
    应中华全国总工会邀请，毛里塔尼亚工人联合会代表团，在这个联合会的总书记法尔·马立克率领下，一日乘飞机到达北京。
    应中国人民外交学会和中华医学会邀请，锡兰塞纳拉特那夫人和塞纳拉特那先生已在二日晚乘飞机到达北京。
                  （据新华社）
