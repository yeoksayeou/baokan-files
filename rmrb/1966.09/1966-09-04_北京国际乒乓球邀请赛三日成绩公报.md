Author:
Date: 1966-09-04
Edition: 5

Three-Day Results of the Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational

New China News Agency, September 3rd: Results of the three-day competition of the 1966 Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational:

Men's Singles (Round of 16):
	Li Fu Yong (China) defeated Kim Chang Ho (North Korea), 3-2
	Zhou Lan Xun (China) defeated Lu Ju Fang (China), 3-1
	Su Guo Xi (China) defeated Li Jing Guang (China), 3-0
	Zhuang Zedong (China) defeated Fukushima Manji (Japan), 3-0

Men's Doubles (Round of 16):
	Zhuang Zedong and Li Jing Guang (China) defeated Jeong Yang Hong and Kim Yeong Sam (North Korea), 3-0
	Fukushima Manji and Hasegawa Nobuhiko (Japan) defeated Kim Chang Ho and Jeon Deok Ji (North Korea), 3-0
	Zhou Lan Xun and Su Guo Xi (China) defeated Park Sin Il and Kim Kwang Bae (North Korea), 3-0
	Li Fu Yong and Lu Ju Fang (China) defeated S. Kaval and S. Marshe (Lebanon), 3-0

Men's Singles (Semifinals):
	Li Fu Yong (China) defeated Zhou Lan Xun (China), 3-1
	Zhuang Zedong (China) defeated Su Guo Xi (China), 3-0

Women's Singles (Semifinals):
	Lin Hui Qing (China) defeated Yamazaki Kyoko (Japan), 3-0
	Li Li (China) defeated Zheng Min Zhi (China), 3-0
