Romania Appoints Minister of Armed Forces
The State Council of Romania has appointed General Ion Ioniță as the Minister of Armed Forces of Romania.

Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down 11 U.S. Aircraft
The military and civilians of the four provinces of Quảng Ninh, Quảng Bình, Ninh Bình, and Thanh Hóa in North Vietnam shot down 11 U.S. aircraft and captured a U.S. pilot between August 29 and 31.

South Vietnamese People's Armed Forces Ambush U.S. Convoy
The liberation armed forces in the provinces of Quảng Ngãi, Tuyên Dương, and Đà Lạt in South Vietnam have recently been continuously striking the enemy on the transportation lines. In Tuyên Dương Province, the people's armed forces laid a large minefield on National Route 13, ambushing a U.S. convoy, destroying three enemy tanks and armored vehicles, and killing 52 U.S. invaders.

Laotian Patriots Repel Enemy Attacks
In August, the patriotic military and civilians of the province of Xieng Khouang in Laos repulsed multiple attacks by U.S. imperialism and its lackeys on the liberated areas, eliminating 84 enemies, shooting down two enemy aircraft, and seizing various military supplies.

U.S. Uses Thailand to Intensify Invasion of Laos
According to the "Voice of the Thai People" radio station, the United States is using its military base in Thailand, which it is expanding to invade Indochina, to intensify its invasion of Laos. Currently, the United States is strengthening its military deployment in Thailand to dispatch at least three divisions of U.S. combat troops to Laos.

U.S. Expands Military Base in the Philippines
In its "gradual escalation" war of aggression against Vietnam, U.S. imperialism has expanded its military base in the Philippines into a rear base. According to a U.S. military officer, they have begun expanding ammunition depots, aircraft hangars, and runways to cope with the "new situation."

Severe Famine Strikes Andhra Pradesh in India
Severe famine has struck the regions of Anantapur and Nalgonda in Andhra Pradesh, India. In the Kadiri Taluka of Anantapur District, approximately 20,000 people have been forced to relocate, and at least 25 people have starved to death. In Nalgonda District, over 3,000 head of livestock have died due to lack of feed.

### 简明新闻

1966-09-01
第3版()
专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    罗马尼亚任命武装部队部长
    罗马尼亚国务委员会已任命扬·约尼查上将为罗马尼亚武装部队部长。
    越南军民又击落美机十一架
    越南北方广宁、广平、宁平、清化四省军民，在八月二十九日到三十一日三天中，共击落美国飞机十一架，并活捉了美国飞贼。
    南越人民武装截击美国车队
    越南南方广义、土龙木、多乐等省解放武装力量，最近在交通线上不断给敌人以沉重打击。土龙木省人民武装在第十三号公路上大摆地雷阵，截击美国强盗的车队，击毁敌人的坦克和装甲车三辆，打死美国侵略军五十二名。
    老挝爱国军民挫败敌人进攻
    老挝川圹省爱国军民，八月份挫败美帝国主义及其走狗对解放区发动的多次进攻，消灭敌人八十四名，击落敌机两架，并且缴获各种军用物资。
    美国利用泰国加紧侵略老挝
    据“泰国人民之声”电台揭露，美国正利用它在印度支那扩大侵略战争的军事基地泰国，加紧侵略老挝。目前美国正加强在泰国的军事部署，以便从泰国派遣至少三个师的美国战斗部队去老挝。
    美国在菲律宾扩建军事基地
    美帝国主义在侵略越南的“逐步升级”的战争中，已经把它在菲律宾的军事基地扩建成为后方基地。据美国军官透露，他们已开始扩建弹药库、飞机库和跑道，以应付“新的形势”。
    印度安得拉邦发生严重饥荒
    印度安得拉邦的阿南塔普尔和拿尔刚达等地发生了严重的饥荒。在阿南塔普尔县的卡迪里塔鲁卡，大约有两万人因而被迫迁移到其他地方，至少已有二十五人饿死。在拿尔刚达县，有三千多头牲口由于缺乏饲料而死亡。　　　　　（据新华社）
