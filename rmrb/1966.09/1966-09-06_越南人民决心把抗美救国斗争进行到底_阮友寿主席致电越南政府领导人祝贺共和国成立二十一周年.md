Title: The Vietnamese People Are Determined to Carry Out the Struggle Against the U.S. to the End
Author:
Date: 1966-09-06
Edition: 6

The Vietnamese people are determined to carry out the struggle against the U.S. to the end. Nguyen Huu Tho, Chairman of the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, sent a telegram to the leaders of the Vietnamese government to congratulate the 21st anniversary of the founding of the Republic.

New China News Agency, Hanoi, September 5th — According to the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency, Nguyen Huu Tho, Chairman of the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, sent a telegram to the leaders of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on the occasion of the 21st anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. He expressed that the people of South Vietnam and North Vietnam are determined to carry forward the tradition of resolute national struggle, hoist the banner of decisive battle and victory against the U.S. aggressors, and fight for the unification of the motherland.

The telegram said that the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam once again expresses the unshakable will of the people of South Vietnam to fight to the final victory. No matter how much the U.S. bandits expand the war, the people of South Vietnam will resolutely defeat the U.S. bandits and their lackeys.

New China News Agency, Hanoi, September 2nd — The Vietnamese newspaper "People's Daily" published an editorial on the 2nd, warmly celebrating the 21st anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and reiterated that all Vietnamese military and civilians are determined to implement the great battle call of Chairman Ho Chi Minh, and carry out the struggle against the U.S. to complete victory.

The editorial of "People's Daily" said that over the past 21 years, the struggle of the Vietnamese people has been filled with hardships and sacrifices, but it is also a road of heroic struggle and brilliant victories.

The editorial emphasized that the battle call of Chairman Ho Chi Minh to resist the U.S. and save the country is powerfully inspiring the people and soldiers of the whole country to bravely advance, annihilate the invaders, and the Vietnamese people are ready at any time to smash the enemy's new adventurous plans. A high tide of resisting the U.S. and saving the country is surging and sweeping across the country.

The editorial of "People's Army Daily" on the same day said: "The U.S. bandits stubbornly use the conspiracy to divide our country, occupy our South, and bomb and strafe our North. They have violated the most sacred rights of our nation. The hatred and anger of our people are sky-high. We will resolutely fight until there is not a single U.S. aggressor on Vietnamese soil."

### 越南人民决心把抗美救国斗争进行到底  阮友寿主席致电越南政府领导人祝贺共和国成立二十一周年

1966-09-06
第6版()
专栏：

    越南人民决心把抗美救国斗争进行到底
    阮友寿主席致电越南政府领导人祝贺共和国成立二十一周年
    新华社河内五日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团主席阮友寿，在越南民主共和国成立二十一周年的时候，打电报给越南民主共和国政府领导人，表示越南南方和越南北方人民，决心发扬坚强不屈的民族斗争传统，高举决战决胜美国侵略者的旗帜，为统一祖国而斗争。
    电报说，越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团再一次表示越南南方人民决心战斗到最后胜利的坚定不移的钢铁意志。不管美国强盗把战争扩大到什么地步，越南南方人民都坚决打败美国强盗及其走狗。
    新华社河内二日电　越南《人民报》二日发表社论，热烈庆祝越南民主共和国成立二十一周年，并且重申，全体越南军民坚决实现胡志明主席的伟大战斗号召，把抗美救国斗争进行到彻底胜利。
    《人民报》的社论说，二十一年来，越南人民的斗争道路充满了艰苦和牺牲，但也是英勇斗争和取得辉煌胜利的道路。
    社论强调说，胡志明主席的抗美救国战斗号召，正有力地鼓舞着越南全国人民和战士奋勇前进，消灭侵略者，越南人民随时准备粉碎敌人的新冒险计划。一个抗美救国的高潮正在汹涌澎湃、席卷全国。
    《人民军队报》同日发表社论说：“美国强盗顽固地使用分割我国、侵占我国南方和轰炸、扫射我国北方的阴谋。它侵犯了我国民族最神圣的权利。我国人民的仇恨怒火冲天，我们坚决战斗到在越南土地上没有一个美国侵略者的时候为止。”
