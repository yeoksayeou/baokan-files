Title: Vice Premier He Long Meets with the Chinese Table Tennis Team, Calls for a Campaign to Study Chairman Mao's Works in the Sports World
Author:
Date: 1966-09-06
Edition: 2

Vice Premier He Long today met with the entire Chinese table tennis team participating in the 1966 Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational and delivered an important speech.

Vice Premier He Long congratulated the Chinese table tennis team on their outstanding achievements in this invitational tournament. He said, "This is another victory of the great Mao Zedong Thought."

Vice Premier He Long said, "We must launch a campaign to study Chairman Mao's works in the sports world. Not only the table tennis team should strive to learn, but all other sports teams should also study diligently. The first and foremost task is to study Mao Zedong Thought. You should study with questions in mind, learn and apply it actively, and work hard on its application. Only by mastering Mao Zedong Thought can you achieve ideological revolutionization, gain true ability, and become fearless of heaven and earth. Without this, there is no spiritual strength."

Present at the meeting were: Vice Chairman of the State Sports Commission Rong Gao Tang, Huang Zhong, Li Meng Hua, Zhao Zheng Hong, and Chairman of the Chinese Table Tennis Association Chen Xian.

### 贺龙副总理接见我乒乓球代表团时说  体育界要发起大学毛主席著作运动

1966-09-06
第2版()
专栏：

    贺龙副总理接见我乒乓球代表团时说
    体育界要发起大学毛主席著作运动
    只有把毛泽东思想学到手，才能思想革命化，才有本事，才能天不怕，地不怕。没有这个，就没有精神力量。
    新华社五日讯　贺龙副总理今天接见了参加一九六六年北京国际乒乓球邀请赛的中国乒乓球代表团全体成员，并作了重要讲话。
    贺龙副总理祝贺中国乒乓球队在这次邀请赛中取得的优异成绩。他说，这是伟大的毛泽东思想的又一胜利。
    贺龙副总理说，我们要在体育界进一步发起一个大学毛主席著作的运动，不光乒乓球队要努力学，其他运动队也要好好学。千条万条学习毛泽东思想是第一条。你们要带着问题学，活学活用，在用字上狠下功夫。只有把毛泽东思想学到手，才能思想革命化，才有本事，才能天不怕，地不怕。没有这个，就没有精神力量。
    接见时在座的有：国家体委副主任荣高棠、黄中、李梦华、赵正洪，中国乒乓球协会主席陈先等。
