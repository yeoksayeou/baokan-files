Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Somali Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-09-16
Edition: 5

Premier Zhou Enlai received Somali National Assembly Speaker Sheikh Mukhtar Muhammad Hussein and the entire delegation of the Somali National Assembly Friendship Delegation led by him this evening. He had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Liu Ningyi and relevant department heads Lianhuan, Xiefeng, and others. (Photo attached)

The photo shows the group at the reception. The fifth person from the left in the front row is Mukhtar, the Speaker.

### 周恩来总理接见索马里贵宾

1966-09-16
第5版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见索马里贵宾
    新华社十五日讯　周恩来总理今晚接见了索马里国民议会议长谢赫·穆克塔尔·穆罕默德·侯赛因，以及由他率领的索马里国民议会友好代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有人大常委会副委员长刘宁一以及有关方面负责人连贯、谢丰等。（附图片）
    图为接见时合影。前排左起第五人是穆克塔尔议长。
        新华社记者摄
