Title: The Blood Debt Owed by the American Bandits Must Be Repaid in Blood
Author:
Date: 1966-09-12
Edition: 2

	The blood debt owed by the American bandits must be repaid in blood.
	Guangdong's various sectors hold a memorial gathering to mourn the nine martyrs who sacrificed their lives.

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 11th - More than 5,000 representatives from various sectors of Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City held a memorial gathering this morning at the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall to mourn the nine martyrs who sacrificed their lives when the Chinese cargo ship No. 1018 was attacked by American imperialist military aircraft while sailing on a normal route in the Gulf of Tonkin on August 29th. They also condemned the heinous crimes committed by American imperialism against China.

	Vice Minister of Communications Yu Mei, Deputy Governor of Guangdong Province Lin Liming, and Mayor of Guangzhou City Zeng Shen spoke at the meeting. In their speeches, they expressed deep sorrow for the heroic martyrs, extended their warmest condolences to the families of the martyrs, and pointed out that the martyrs were truly outstanding sons of the Chinese people, and their deaths were heavier than the Taihang Mountains. They also angrily pointed out that the blood debt owed by American imperialism to China not only aroused the Chinese people's deep hatred towards American imperialism but also further inspired the Chinese people's resolve to support Vietnam and resist America.

	Lin Xiu Lian, the wife of the deceased martyr Wu Qin, and Li Daqun, the son of Li Siliang, also spoke at the meeting. They angrily condemned the heinous crimes of American imperialism in killing their loved ones and emphasized that the blood debt owed by American imperialism must be repaid in blood. They expressed their determination to follow Chairman Mao in revolution and fight to the end against the American imperialist invaders.

	Representatives from Guangdong Province's water transport and foreign trade sectors, as well as representatives from Guangzhou City's Red Guards and revolutionary students, also spoke at the meeting. They expressed the strongest condemnation and protest against the bloody crimes of American imperialism.

### 美国强盗欠下的血债一定要用血来还  广东各界集会追悼英勇牺牲的九烈士

1966-09-12
第2版()
专栏：

    美国强盗欠下的血债一定要用血来还
    广东各界集会追悼英勇牺牲的九烈士
    据新华社广州十一日电　广东省、广州市各界代表五千多人，今天上午在中山纪念堂举行追悼大会，追悼我国一零一八号货船八月二十九日在北部湾正常航线上航行时遭到美帝国主义军用飞机攻击而英勇牺牲的九名烈士，控诉美帝国主义对我国犯下的滔天罪行。
    交通部副部长于眉，广东省代省长林李明和广州市市长曾生先后在会上讲话。他们在讲话中对光荣牺牲的烈士表示沉痛的哀悼，向烈士家属表示最亲切的慰问，指出烈士们不愧为中国人民的优秀儿子，他们的死比泰山还重。并且愤怒地指出，美帝国主义欠下我国的这一笔血债，不但激起了我国人民对美帝国主义的无比仇恨，而且更激励着我国人民援越抗美的决心。
    死难烈士伍钦的妻子林秀莲，李嗣廉的儿子李大群也在会上讲话。他们愤怒控诉了美帝国主义杀害他们亲人的滔天罪行，并且强调指出，美帝国主义欠下的血债一定要用血来还。他们表示要永远跟着毛主席闹革命，为反对美帝国主义侵略者而斗争到底。
    广东省水运部门、对外贸易部门的代表，以及广州市的红卫兵和革命学生的代表也在会上讲话，他们对美帝国主义的血腥罪行表示最强烈的谴责和抗议。
