Title: Delegation of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association Leaves for Vietnam to Attend Celebrations of Our National Day
Author:
Date: 1966-09-27
Edition: 6

At the invitation of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, a delegation of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association, led by Qu Mengjue, consisting of five members, departed from Beijing by plane this morning to Vietnam to participate in the celebrations of the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China by the Vietnamese people and to conduct a friendly visit.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Yang Xiufeng, Chu Tunan, and other relevant officials.

The Vietnamese Ambassador to China, Chen Ziping, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 应邀参加越南人民庆祝我国国庆活动  中越友协代表团离京去越南

1966-09-27
第6版()
专栏：

    应邀参加越南人民庆祝我国国庆活动
    中越友协代表团离京去越南
    新华社二十六日讯  应越南中国友好协会的邀请，由区梦觉率领的中国越南友好协会代表团一行五人，今天上午乘飞机离开北京前往越南参加越南人民庆祝中华人民共和国成立十七周年活动和进行友好访问。
    到机场送行的，有杨秀峰、楚图南等有关方面负责人。
    越南驻中国大使陈子平也到机场送行。
