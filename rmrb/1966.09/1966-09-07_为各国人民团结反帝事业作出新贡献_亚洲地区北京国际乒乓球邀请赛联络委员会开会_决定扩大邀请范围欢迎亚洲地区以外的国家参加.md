Title: Making New Contributions to the Cause of Unity Among the Peoples of All Countries in Opposing Imperialism
Author:
Date: 1966-09-07
Edition: 6

The Liaison Committee for the Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational in the Asian Region held a meeting today.

	Attending the meeting were representatives from twelve countries and regions that participated in the 1966 Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational. These countries and regions are: Cambodia, Ceylon, China, Japan, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Lebanon, Nepal, Pakistan, Palestine, Syria, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Yemen.

	The meeting was conducted in an atmosphere of unity, friendship, and democratic consultation. The attendees expressed their gratitude to the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao, the Chinese Communist Party, the Chinese government, and the Chinese people for their care and support for this invitational tournament.

	The meeting noted that the 1966 Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational, held from August 22 to September 4, was a resounding success. Through joint training and competition, the athletes from various countries not only exchanged experiences and improved their skills but also further strengthened their unity and anti-imperialist fighting friendship. This was a competition full of friendly atmosphere, fully embodying the spirit of promoting friendship, mutual progress, common development, and unity against imperialism.

	The attendees were pleased to note that since its inception in 1964, the Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational has grown in scale, with more participating countries and an ever-widening influence. Therefore, the meeting decided to expand the invitational range in the future, welcoming countries outside the Asian region to participate, in order to make new contributions to the development of table tennis in various countries around the world and the improvement of technical levels, as well as to strengthen the fighting friendship among the peoples and athletes of various countries in the cause of unity against imperialism.

	The meeting unanimously decided that starting from 1966, the Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational will be held every two years.

### 为各国人民团结反帝事业作出新贡献  亚洲地区北京国际乒乓球邀请赛联络委员会开会  决定扩大邀请范围欢迎亚洲地区以外的国家参加

1966-09-07
第6版()
专栏：

    为各国人民团结反帝事业作出新贡献
    亚洲地区北京国际乒乓球邀请赛联络委员会开会
    决定扩大邀请范围欢迎亚洲地区以外的国家参加
    据新华社六日讯　亚洲地区北京国际乒乓球邀请赛联络委员会今天举行会议。
    出席会议的，有参加一九六六年北京国际乒乓球邀请赛的十二个国家和地区的代表，这些国家和地区是：柬埔寨、锡兰、中国、日本、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、黎巴嫩、尼泊尔、巴基斯坦、巴勒斯坦、叙利亚、越南民主共和国和也门。
    会议是在团结友好、民主协商的气氛中进行的。与会者表示感谢中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席、中国共产党、中国政府和中国人民对这次邀请赛的关怀。
    会议指出，八月二十二日至九月四日举行的一九六六年北京国际乒乓球邀请赛获得了圆满的成功。通过共同练习和比赛等活动，各国运动员不仅交流了经验，提高了水平，而且进一步加强了团结反帝的战斗友谊。这是一次充满友好气氛的比赛，充分体现了增进友好、互相促进、共同发展、团结反帝的精神。
    与会者满意地指出，北京国际乒乓球邀请赛自一九六四年创办以来，规模一次比一次大，参加国越来越多，影响越来越广。因此，会议决定今后将扩大这种邀请赛的邀请范围，欢迎亚洲地区以外的国家参加，以便对促进世界各国乒乓球运动的开展以及技术水平的提高，对加强各国人民和运动员在团结反帝事业中的战斗友谊，作出新的贡献。
    会议一致决定，自一九六六年起，每两年举行一次北京国际乒乓球邀请赛。
