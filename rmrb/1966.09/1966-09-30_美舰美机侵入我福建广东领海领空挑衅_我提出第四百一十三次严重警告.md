Title: U.S. Ships and Planes Intrude into China's Territorial Waters and Airspace in Fujian and Guangdong
Author:
Date: 1966-09-30
Edition: 4

On September 27 at 15:39 until September 28 at 5:37, a U.S. military vessel intruded into China's territorial waters off the eastern region of Minjiang, Fujian Province. On September 28 from 4:31 to 9:18, and on September 29 from 6:08 to 10:00, a U.S. military vessel intruded into China's territorial waters off the southeastern region of Chongwu, Fujian Province. On September 29 from 6:13 to 11:28, another U.S. military vessel intruded into China's territorial waters off the southeastern region of Dongshan Island, Fujian Province. On the same day from 11:45 to 12:16, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into China's airspace over Yongxing Island and Dong Island in the Xisha Islands, Guangdong Province.

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs spokesperson, on behalf of the Chinese government, issued the 413th serious warning in response to the U.S. military vessels and aircraft's provocative actions.

### 美舰美机侵入我福建广东领海领空挑衅  我提出第四百一十三次严重警告

1966-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

    美舰美机侵入我福建广东领海领空挑衅
    我提出第四百一十三次严重警告
    新华社二十九日讯　九月二十七日十五时三十九分至二十八日五时三十七分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省闽江口以东地区领海。二十八日四时三十一分至九时十八分，二十九日六时八分至十时，美国军舰一艘，两次侵入我福建省崇武东南地区领海。二十九日六时十三分至十一时二十八分，又有美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省东山岛东南地区领海。同日十一时四十五分至十二时十六分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛、东岛地区领空。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百一十三次严重警告。
