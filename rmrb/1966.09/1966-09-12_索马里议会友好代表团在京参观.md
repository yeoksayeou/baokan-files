Title: Somali Parliamentary Friendship Delegation Visits Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-09-12
Edition: 5

According to a report by Xinhua News Agency on the 11th, members of the Somali National Assembly Friendship Delegation visited the Hongqi People's Commune in the suburbs of Beijing today. Representative Hajji Yusuf Egala said during the visit, "We are impressed by the progress of the Chinese people under the guidance of Chairman Mao Zedong's policies. The path taken by the Chinese people will be of great help to other countries and nations. We want to learn from you."

Representative Egala also stated that the Somali people stand with the Chinese people in the common struggle against imperialism and colonialism. He wished for the everlasting friendship between the peoples of Somalia and China and long life to Chairman Mao Zedong!

This evening, Speaker Sheikh Mukhtar and members of the Somali National Assembly Friendship Delegation visited the large clay sculpture "The Opium Den."

### 索马里议会友好代表团在京参观

1966-09-12
第5版()
专栏：

    索马里议会友好代表团在京参观
    据新华社十一日讯　索马里国民议会友好代表团的团员，今天访问了北京郊区的红星人民公社。代表团团员哈吉·优素福·埃加勒在参观中说：“中国人民在毛泽东主席制定的方针政策的道路上前进，使我们感到敬佩。中国人民走的道路，对于其他国家和民族会有很大帮助，我们要向你们学习。”
    埃加勒议员还说，索马里人民同中国人民站在一起，共同反对帝国主义和殖民主义。他祝愿索中两国人民友谊万岁！祝毛泽东主席万寿无疆！
    今晚，谢赫·穆克塔尔议长和索马里国民议会友好代表团的成员，参观了大型泥塑《收租院》。
