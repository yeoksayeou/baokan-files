Title: Female Volleyball Players Hold Up Chairman Mao's Portrait and Chant Slogans
Author:
Date: 1966-09-14
Edition: 4

Female volleyball athletes are holding up a portrait of Chairman Mao, holding the "Quotations from Chairman Mao," and before the start of the selection match, they are shouting together with the audience, "Long live the Communist Party of China!" "Long live Chairman Mao!"
