Title: 3,000 Red Guards from Ten Provinces and Cities Meet with Mai Xiande
Author:
Date: 1966-09-04
Edition: 2

	3,000 Red Guards from Ten Provinces and Cities Meet with Mai Xiande

	Mai Xiande greeted the Red Guards and expressed his firm support for their revolutionary actions. The young Red Guards vowed to learn from Mai Xiande's proletarian spirit of steel, to be forever loyal to the Party, the people, Chairman Mao, and Mao Zedong Thought, and to achieve a complete victory in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

	According to a report from Xinhua News Agency in Guangzhou on the 3rd, combat hero Mai Xiande met with over 3,000 Red Guards and revolutionary students from Beijing, Jilin, Jiangxi, and seven other provinces and cities in the afternoon of the 2nd. Mai Xiande greeted the Red Guards and expressed his firm support for their revolutionary actions. The young Red Guards vowed to learn from Mai Xiande's proletarian spirit of steel, to be forever loyal to the Party, the people, Chairman Mao, and Mao Zedong Thought, and to achieve a complete victory in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

	When Mai Xiande entered the venue, the applause was deafening. Slogans and cheers such as "Long live Chairman Mao!" and "Learn from the good soldier of Chairman Mao, Comrade Mai Xiande!" echoed throughout the hall. The Red Guards enthusiastically greeted Mai Xiande, placing Red Guard armbands on his arm and pinning badges with Chairman Mao's portrait on his chest. Mai Xiande spoke at the meeting, saying, "Comrades Red Guards, greetings! I salute you and learn from you! I fully support your revolutionary actions and will resolutely carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution to the end!" He raised his fist and shouted, "Long live the Communist Party of China! Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years! Ten thousand, ten thousand years!" Then, he excitedly led everyone to sing "Sailing the Seas Depends on the Helmsman," expressing their boundless love, respect, and admiration for the great teacher, great leader, great commander, and great helmsman Chairman Mao.

	Leaders from the Central-South Bureau of the Communist Party of China, the Guangzhou Military Region, the Guangdong Provincial Party Committee, and the Guangzhou Municipal Party Committee attended the meeting. Leaders from the Guangzhou Military Region and the Guangdong Provincial Party Committee spoke at the meeting.

	Two Red Guards from Beijing spoke at the meeting, saying, "Comrade Mai Xiande is a good soldier of Chairman Mao. His deep class feelings of boundless love for Chairman Mao have deeply educated us; his loyalty to Chairman Mao and Mao Zedong Thought has always inspired us; his support for us Red Guards has given us great encouragement. We must be like Mai Xiande, forever loyal to the Party, the people, Chairman Mao, and Mao Zedong Thought, and resolutely follow Chairman Mao's teachings, dare to struggle, dare to revolutionize, be good at struggling, be good at revolutionizing, and thoroughly, completely, and unconditionally implement the Sixteen Points, closely follow Chairman Mao, and carry the Cultural Revolution to the end!"

	(Photo attached)

	On August 28, the capital's Red Guards who went to Guangzhou for revolutionary linkage visited combat hero Mai Xiande at the Guangzhou Naval Sanatorium. Mai Xiande and the revolutionary youngsters sang "Sailing the Seas Depends on the Helmsman" loudly. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter)

### 十省市三千红卫兵会见麦贤得

1966-09-04
第2版()
专栏：

    十省市三千红卫兵会见麦贤得
    麦贤得向红卫兵们问好，表示坚决支持红卫兵的革命行动。红卫兵小将们表示，要很好地学习麦贤得的无产阶级硬骨头精神，像麦贤得那样永远忠于党，忠于人民，忠于毛主席，忠于毛泽东思想，夺取无产阶级文化大革命的胜利
    据新华社广州三日电  战斗英雄麦贤得，二日下午在广州会见了来自北京、吉林、江西等十个省市的三千多名红卫兵和革命学生。麦贤得向红卫兵们问好，表示坚决支持红卫兵的革命行动。红卫兵小将们表示，要很好地学习麦贤得的无产阶级硬骨头精神，像麦贤得那样，永远忠于党，忠于人民，忠于毛主席，忠于毛泽东思想，夺取无产阶级文化大革命的彻底胜利。
    当麦贤得进入会场时，掌声雷动。“毛主席万岁”“向毛主席的好战士麦贤得同志学习”等口号声、欢呼声响彻全场。红卫兵们激动地向麦贤得问好，把红卫兵袖章戴在麦贤得的手臂上，把有毛主席像的纪念章佩戴在麦贤得胸前。麦贤得在会上讲了话。他说：“红卫兵同志们，你们好，我向你们致敬，向你们学习！我完全支持你们的革命行动，坚决将无产阶级文化大革命进行到底！”他振臂高呼：“中国共产党万岁！”“毛主席万岁！万岁！万万岁！”接着，他又激动地指挥大家高唱《大海航行靠舵手》，表达他们对伟大导师、伟大领袖、伟大统帅、伟大舵手毛主席无限热爱、无限敬仰、无限崇拜的深情。
    中共中央中南局、人民解放军广州部队、中共广东省委和广州市委的负责人出席了会见大会。广州部队和中共广东省委负责人分别在会上讲了话。
    来自北京的两位红卫兵在会上讲了话，他们说，麦贤得同志是毛主席的好战士，他对毛主席无限热爱的深厚阶级感情，深深地教育了我们；他对毛主席、对毛泽东思想的赤胆忠心，永远激励着我们；他对我们红卫兵的支持，给了我们巨大的鼓舞。我们一定要像麦贤得那样，永远忠于党，忠于人民，忠于毛主席，忠于毛泽东思想，坚决按照毛主席的教导，敢于斗争，敢于革命，善于斗争，善于革命，认真地、全面地、彻底地、不折不扣地贯彻执行十六条，紧紧跟着毛主席，将文化大革命进行到底！
