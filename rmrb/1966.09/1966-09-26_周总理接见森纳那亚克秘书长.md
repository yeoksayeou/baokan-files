Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Secretary-General Senanayake
Author:
Date: 1966-09-26
Edition: 6

Premier Zhou met with Secretary-General Senanayake of the Permanent Bureau of Afro-Asian Writers this evening and had a friendly and cordial conversation with him. Those present at the meeting included Liao Chengzhi and others.

September 25, Premier Zhou met with Secretary-General Senanayake of the Permanent Bureau of Afro-Asian Writers and his wife.

### 周总理接见森纳那亚克秘书长

1966-09-26
第6版()
专栏：

    周总理接见森纳那亚克秘书长
    新华社二十五日讯　周恩来总理今晚接见亚非作家常设局秘书长森纳那亚克先生和夫人，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有廖承志等。（附图片）
    九月二十五日，周恩来总理接见亚非作家常设局秘书长森纳那亚克先生和夫人。
                          新华社记者摄
