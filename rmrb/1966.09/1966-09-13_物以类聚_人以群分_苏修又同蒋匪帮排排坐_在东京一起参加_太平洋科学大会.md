Title: Birds of a feather flock together, people associate with their own kind
Author:
Date: 1966-09-13
Edition: 4

	Birds of a feather flock together, people associate with their own kind.
	The Soviet revisionist leadership clique sat side by side with the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang
	in Tokyo to jointly participate in the "Pacific Science Conference."

	BEIJING, Sept. 12 (Xinhua) -- Tokyo reports: The Soviet revisionist leadership clique sent representatives to sit with the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang, the South Korean puppet clique, and the U.S. imperialist representatives from Aug. 22 to Sept. 10 to participate in the so-called "Pacific Science Conference" held in Tokyo.

	According to reports from Tokyo, the purpose of this conference is to strengthen their "friendship, cooperation, and contact."

	Statistics show that this is the ninth time since last October that Soviet representatives have openly sat with U.S., Taiwan Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang, and South Korean puppet clique representatives in the Southeast Asian region to engage in collusion.

### 物以类聚　人以群分  苏修又同蒋匪帮排排坐  在东京一起参加“太平洋科学大会”

1966-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

    物以类聚　人以群分
    苏修又同蒋匪帮排排坐
    在东京一起参加“太平洋科学大会”
    新华社十二日讯　东京消息：苏联修正主义领导集团派代表从八月二十二日至九月十日同蒋介石匪帮、南朝鲜傀儡集团以及美帝国主义的代表坐在一起参加了在东京举行的所谓“太平洋科学大会”第十一次会议。
    据来自东京的消息说，这次会议的目的在于加强他们彼此间的“友谊合作”和“联系”。
    据统计，这是苏联代表自去年十月以来第九次在东南亚地区同美国、台湾蒋介石匪帮、南朝鲜傀儡集团的代表公然坐在一起勾勾搭搭。
