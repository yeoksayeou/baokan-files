Title: Two Guests from Morocco Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-09-22
Edition: 5

At the invitation of the China-Africa People's Friendship Association, two guests from Morocco, Abdoul Razak, Secretary-General of the Permanent Bureau for the Coordination of Arabization of the Rabat Arab League, and his wife, arrived in Beijing by plane on September 21.

### 摩洛哥两位客人到京

1966-09-22
第5版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥两位客人到京
    新华社二十一日讯　应中国非洲人民友好协会邀请来我国参加国庆活动的摩洛哥拉巴特阿拉伯联盟阿拉伯化协调常务办公室总书记阿卜杜勒·拉济兹和夫人，已在二十一日乘飞机到达北京。
