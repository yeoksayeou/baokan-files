Title: Indonesian Right-Wing Military Regime Openly Begs the US for "Aid"
Author:
Date: 1966-09-05
Edition: 5

Indonesian right-wing military regime openly begs the US for "aid"

According to a report by Xinhua News Agency on the 4th, a message from Jakarta states: The Indonesian right-wing military regime openly begged the US imperialists for economic and military "aid."

According to the early release of the September 5 issue of the American magazine *Newsweek*, when the head of the Indonesian right-wing military regime, Suharto, spoke exclusively to a reporter from this magazine, he said: Indonesia is "willing to accept" US economic "aid." He also said, "We need military aid."

Suharto openly stated that the Indonesian right-wing military regime will sell off the country's resources in exchange for "aid."

### 印尼右派军人政权公开向美帝乞讨“援助”

1966-09-05
第5版()
专栏：

    印尼右派军人政权公开向美帝乞讨“援助”
    据新华社四日讯　雅加达消息：印度尼西亚右派军人政权公开地向美帝国主义乞讨经济和军事“援助”。
    据提前出版的九月五日一期的美国《新闻周刊》报道，印度尼西亚右派军人政权头目苏哈托向这家周刊的记者单独发表谈话时表示：印度尼西亚“乐于接受”美国的经济“援助”。他还说，“我们需要军事援助”。
    苏哈托公开表示，印尼右派军人政权将以出卖国家的资源来换取“援助”。
