Title: The Executive Committee of the Uruguayan Left-Wing Revolutionary Movement Issues a Statement: The Communist Party of China is the Vanguard of the Contemporary Revolutionary Movement, the Cultural Revolution is a Qualitative Leap into a New Stage of the Chinese Socialist Revolution
Author:
Date: 1966-09-11
Edition: 5

	Uruguayan Left-Wing Revolutionary Movement Executive Committee Issues Statement
	The Communist Party of China is the Vanguard of the Contemporary Revolutionary Movement
	The Cultural Revolution is a Qualitative Leap into a New Stage of the Chinese Socialist Revolution

	New China News Agency Montevideo, September 8

	The Executive Committee of the Uruguayan Left-Wing Revolutionary Movement issued a statement, enthusiastically supporting the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution led by Chairman Mao Zedong. The statement praises Chairman Mao Zedong as the greatest Marxist-Leninist of our time.

	The statement says, the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution is a qualitative leap into a new stage of the Chinese socialist revolution. In this new stage, all the remaining bourgeois and other exploiting class ideologies, customs, habits, and morals in Chinese society will be eliminated. "This is a genuine, profound ideological revolution to transform the thinking of hundreds of millions of men and women, to make Marxism-Leninism the worldview of all the Chinese people. This is a gigantic, historic, and unprecedented task."

	The statement points out that this Cultural Revolution aims to destroy the ideological foundations that breed revisionism, ensuring that China will not have the material and ideological bases for a capitalist restoration. This is a highly significant contribution to the advancement of the world revolution.

	The statement says, "The current proletarian cultural revolution in China is the most important aspect of the development of the proletarian revolution and the national liberation movement worldwide. Objective conditions have made the People's Republic of China and the Communist Party of China the vanguard of the contemporary revolutionary movement. The international communist movement is developing, the revolution is deepening, and neither the imperialist murderers of the people of various countries nor the sinister plots of the modern revisionists led by the Soviet leadership can stop this mighty torrent."

	The statement says, "Comrade Mao Zedong is the greatest Marxist-Leninist of our time. The revisionists and other opportunists attack Comrade Mao Zedong with the aim of defaming Marxism-Leninism, which is to collude with imperialism."

	The Executive Committee of the Uruguayan Left-Wing Revolutionary Movement decided to widely publicize the decision of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, adopted on August 8, and to instruct the Propaganda Committee of the Left-Wing Revolutionary Movement to compile analytical materials on this issue for public discussion and education.

### 乌拉圭左派革命运动执委会发表声明  中国共产党是当代革命运动的先锋  文化大革命是中国社会主义革命进入新阶段的质的飞跃

1966-09-11
第5版()
专栏：

    乌拉圭左派革命运动执委会发表声明
    中国共产党是当代革命运动的先锋
    文化大革命是中国社会主义革命进入新阶段的质的飞跃
    新华社蒙得维的亚八日电　乌拉圭左派革命运动执行委员会发表声明，热烈支持中国人民在毛泽东主席领导下所开展的无产阶级文化大革命运动。声明赞扬毛泽东主席是当代最伟大的马克思列宁主义者。
    声明说，无产阶级文化大革命是中国社会主义革命进入新的阶段的质的飞跃，在这个新阶段里，将要消灭中国社会尚存的所有资产阶级和其他剥削阶级的思想、风俗、习惯和道德的残余。这“是一次改造亿万男女的思想、把马克思列宁主义变为全中国人民的世界观的一次真正的深刻的意识形态的革命。这是一项巨大的、历史性的和空前未有的任务”。
    声明指出，这场文化大革命是要摧毁滋长修正主义的思想基础，保证中国不会有资本主义复辟的物质和思想的基础。这是对于世界革命向前发展的一个极为重要的贡献。
    声明说：“中国目前的无产阶级文化革命是全世界范围内的无产阶级革命和民族解放（运动）发展的最重要的一个方面。客观条件已经使中华人民共和国和中国共产党成了当代革命运动的先锋。国际共产主义运动在发展，革命在深入，无论是屠杀各国人民的美帝国主义者，还是以苏联（领导）集团为首的现代修正主义者的不可告人的阴谋诡计，都无法阻挡住这股巨大的洪流。”
    声明说，“毛泽东同志是当代最伟大的马克思列宁主义者。修正主义者和其他机会主义者攻击毛泽东同志，目的是为了诋毁马克思列宁主义，这也就是同帝国主义采取‘联合行动’。”
    乌拉圭左派革命运动执行委员会决定，广泛宣传中共中央八月八日通过的关于无产阶级文化大革命的决定，并且责成左派革命运动宣传委员会编写分析这个问题的公开材料，作为群众教育和讨论的文件。
