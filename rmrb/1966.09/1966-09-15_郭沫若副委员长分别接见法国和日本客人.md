Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Receives French and Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-09-15
Edition: 5

Vice Chairman Guo Moruo of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress received today in the morning the Vice Chairman of the Social Affairs Committee of the French Senate, Roger Lagrand, and the entire delegation of the Social Affairs Committee of the French Senate led by him. They had a friendly conversation.

According to Xinhua News Agency's report on the 14th, Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, received in the afternoon the Japanese academic delegation led by Kawakami Hiroshi. They had a friendly conversation.

### 郭沫若副委员长分别接见法国和日本客人

1966-09-15
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长分别接见法国和日本客人
    据新华社十三日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若今天上午接见了法国参议院社会事务委员会副主席罗歇·拉格朗日，和由他率领的法国参议院社会事务委员会代表团全体成员，和他们进行了友好的谈话。
    据新华社十四日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、中国科学院院长郭沫若，今天下午接见以河角广为首的日本学术代表团，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
