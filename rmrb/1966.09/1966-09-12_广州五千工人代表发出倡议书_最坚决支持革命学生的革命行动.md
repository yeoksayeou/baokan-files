Title: 5,000 Workers' Representatives in Guangzhou Issue an Initiative
Author:
Date: 1966-09-12
Edition: 1

In a recently held conference of worker representatives from Guangzhou's industrial, transportation, urban construction, and financial sectors, 5,000 worker representatives issued an initiative. They called on all revolutionary workers in the city to take on the responsibility of being the main force in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, and to most enthusiastically and resolutely support the revolutionary actions of revolutionary students.

The initiative also called on all workers in Guangzhou to take the "Sixteen Points" formulated and personally directed by Chairman Mao as the guiding principle. They must seriously study, familiarize themselves with, master, and apply the "Sixteen Points," and execute them without any deviation.

The initiative further called for all workers to take the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution as the guiding principle, to seize the revolution and promote production, to stand firm at their production posts with the attitude of masters, and to achieve double victories in the Cultural Revolution and production.

This conference of worker representatives was initiated and held by many advanced collectives, advanced producers (workers), and active learners of Chairman Mao's works in Guangzhou. Many worker representatives from factories and enterprises spoke at the conference, enthusiastically hailing the excellent situation of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution nationwide.

### 广州五千工人代表发出倡议书  最坚决支持革命学生的革命行动

1966-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

    广州五千工人代表发出倡议书
    最坚决支持革命学生的革命行动
    据新华社广州十一日电　在最近召开的广州市工交、城市建设、财贸战线职工代表会议上，五千名工人代表发出倡议书，号召全市革命职工在无产阶级文化大革命中肩负起主力军的责任，最热烈最坚决地支持革命学生的革命行动。
    倡议书还倡议全市工人阶级在文化大革命中一定要以毛主席亲自主持制定的十六条为纲，认真学习十六条，熟悉十六条，掌握十六条，运用十六条，不折不扣地执行十六条。
    倡议书还倡议，一定要以无产阶级文化大革命为纲，抓革命，促生产，以主人翁的姿态坚守生产岗位，夺取文化大革命和生产的双胜利。
    这次职工代表会议是由广州市的许多先进集体、先进生产（工作）者、学习毛主席著作积极分子发起召开的。许多工厂、企业的工人代表在会上发言，他们热烈地欢呼全国无产阶级文化大革命的大好形势。
