    Egyptian President Nasser Visits Tanzania

    Egyptian President Nasser and his entourage arrived in Dar es Salaam by airplane on the afternoon of the 22nd, beginning a five-day state visit to the United Republic of Tanzania.

    U.S. Imperialists Increase Troops in South Vietnam to 311,000

    While the Johnson administration is intensifying its "peace talks" deception, the United States continues to send more troops to South Vietnam, preparing to further escalate the war of aggression against Vietnam. The U.S. military announced on the 22nd that the number of American troops currently in South Vietnam has reached 311,000, an increase of 11,000 from August.

    Thailand Becomes Main U.S. Air Base for Aggression Against Vietnam

    According to a report from Bangkok by the British *Daily Mail* on the 21st, the United States has made Thailand its main air base for invading Vietnam and Laos. The report states that the United States is using seven Thai air bases, with an eighth nearing completion. The U.S. 13th Air Force is the "main force" attacking North Vietnam.

    Belgium Deals Another Blow to the North Atlantic Alliance

    Belgian Defense Minister Charles Bosquet confirmed on the 22nd that Belgium intends to reduce its military "obligations" to the U.S.-controlled North Atlantic Alliance. Belgium complains that its military "obligations" to the North Atlantic Alliance are depleting its resources, as it spends over $440 million annually on this.

### 简明新闻

1966-09-24
第6版()
专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    阿联总统纳赛尔到坦桑尼亚访问
    阿联总统纳赛尔和他的随行人员二十二日下午乘飞机到达达累斯萨拉姆，对坦桑尼亚联合共和国进行为期五天的国事访问。
    美帝在南越炮灰已增至三十一万
    在约翰逊政府加紧推行“和谈”阴谋的同时，美国继续增兵南越，准备进一步扩大侵越战争。美国军方二十二日宣布，目前在南越的美军已达三十一万一千人，比八月份增加一万一千人。
    泰国已成美帝侵越主要空军基地
    据英国《每日邮报》二十一日从曼谷发回的报道透露，美国已把泰国作为它侵略越南和老挝的主要空军基地。报道说，美国正在使用七个泰国空军基地，第八个基地已接近完工。美国第十三航空队是袭击越南北方的“主要力量”。
    比利时给北大西洋集团又一打击
    比利时国防大臣夏尔·博斯维克二十二日证实，比利时打算削减它对美国控制的北大西洋集团的军事“义务”。比利时抱怨它对北大西洋集团的军事“义务”正在耗尽它的资源，因为它每年在这方面要花四亿四千多万美元。
                            （据新华社）
