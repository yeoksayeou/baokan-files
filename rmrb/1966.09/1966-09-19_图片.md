Title: Chairman Mao Meets with Australian Communist Leader
Author:
Date: 1966-09-19
Edition: 4

Chairman Mao Zedong met with representatives from revolutionary students and teachers from all over the country who had come to Beijing on September 15. He also warmly received E. F. Hill, Chairman of the Australian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist), and his wife, Jo Hill, who were visiting China on a friendly mission.
