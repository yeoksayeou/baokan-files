Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-09-04
Edition: 5

Vice Chairman Guo Moruo met with Japanese guests this afternoon. The guests were a delegation of eleven members from the Liberal Democratic Party of Japan, led by Furui Xishizhi, Xiaoshan Shantao, Jiangqi Zhencheng, and Fudatian Yi. Guo engaged in friendly conversation with the delegation.

Present at the meeting were Wang Xiaoyun, Xiao Xiangqian, and other relevant officials.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人

1966-09-04
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人
    新华社三日讯　郭沫若副委员长今天下午接见由古井喜实、小坂善太郎、江崎真澄、福田一等十一人组成的日本自由民主党国会议员访华团的成员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有王晓云、萧向前等有关方面负责人。
