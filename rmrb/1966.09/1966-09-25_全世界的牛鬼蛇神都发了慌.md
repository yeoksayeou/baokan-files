Title: All the Demons and Monsters in the World Are Panicking
Author: Hecun
Date: 1966-09-25
Edition: 5

The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China has caused all the demons and monsters in the world to panic. I have come to realize more clearly how deeply these demons and monsters are hidden. In a country like China, which frequently engages in revolutionary struggles, there are still counter-revolutionary demons and monsters. Then, in other countries, within other so-called Communist Parties, could there be no demons and monsters who wave the "red flag" to oppose the red flag? The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution provides a great lesson for the democratic forces in Japan. Mao Zedong Thought is not only for China but for the whole world.

### 全世界的牛鬼蛇神都发了慌
河村
1966-09-25
第5版()
专栏：国外来信

    全世界的牛鬼蛇神都发了慌
    中国的文化大革命，使全世界的牛鬼蛇神都发了慌。我更清楚了解到，这些牛鬼蛇神是如何深深地潜藏着。像中国这样的国家，经常不断地进行革命斗争，还有反党、反革命的牛鬼蛇神存在。那末，在其他一些国家，在其他一些所谓共产党的内部，难道还能没有打着“红旗”反红旗的修正主义的牛鬼蛇神吗？中国文化大革命给予日本民主势力极大的教训。毛泽东思想不仅是中国的，也是全世界的。　〔日本〕河村
