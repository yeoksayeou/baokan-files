Title: Burmese Writer Du Ama Says: The Cultural Revolution Makes Imperialists Weep
Author:
Date: 1966-09-28
Edition: 5

Burmese writer Du Ama said in an article published in the Burmese newspaper *The People's Daily* on September 24 that the purpose of the Chinese people's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution is to prevent the capitalist restoration that occurred in the Soviet Union from happening in China. "Therefore, this is not something for imperialists to be happy about, but rather something that makes them weep bitterly."

### 缅甸作家杜阿玛说：文化革命使帝国主义痛哭流涕

1966-09-28
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸作家杜阿玛说：
    文化革命使帝国主义痛哭流涕
    缅甸著名作家杜阿玛二十四日在缅甸《人民报》发表文章指出，中国人民进行无产阶级文化大革命的目的，就是要防止今天在苏联发生的资本主义复辟在中国重演，“因此这不是帝国主义值得高兴而是使他们痛哭流涕的事”。
