Title: Diplomatic Corps and Foreign Diplomats Watch "Chairman Mao and the Million-strong Cultural Revolution Army"
Author:
Date: 1966-09-21
Edition: 5

	Diplomatic corps and foreign diplomats stationed in China were invited to a film screening hosted by the China Foreign Affairs Committee this evening. They watched the large-scale color documentary "Chairman Mao and the Million-strong Cultural Revolution Army." The entire audience burst into enthusiastic applause when our great leader Chairman Mao appeared on the screen.

	Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo and Vice Chairman of the China Foreign Affairs Committee Song Yiping attended the film screening.

### 各国驻我国使节和外交官员观看《毛主席和百万文化革命大军在一起》

1966-09-21
第5版()
专栏：

    各国驻我国使节和外交官员
    观看《毛主席和百万文化革命大军在一起》
    新华社二十日讯　各国驻我国的使节和外交官员今天晚上应邀出席对外文委举行的电影招待会，观看了大型彩色纪录影片《毛主席和百万文化革命大军在一起》。当我们伟大的领袖毛主席出现在银幕上时，全场响起了热烈的掌声。
    外交部副部长罗贵波和对外文委副主任宋一平出席了电影招待会。
