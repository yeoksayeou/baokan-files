Title: Speaker Mukhtar Visits Beijing, Praises China's Construction Achievements and Wishes Chairman Mao Eternal Life
Author:
Date: 1966-09-10
Edition: 5

Speaker Mukhtar of the National Assembly of Somalia, Sheikh Mukhtar, visited the Chinese Revolution Museum this afternoon. In his inscription for the museum, he wrote: "We are deeply moved by the Chinese people's struggle against American imperialism, British imperialism, and other imperialisms."

Speaker Mukhtar this morning visited the Beijing No. 3 Cotton Textile Mill. Speaking of his impressions here, he said, "I hope the Chinese people will continue to advance and achieve greater victories in building their country and in the struggle against imperialism." He concluded by wishing "Chairman Mao Zedong eternal life."

Speaker Mukhtar and the members of the Somalia National Assembly Friendship Delegation, led by him, were warmly welcomed by the workers of the factory and the staff of the museum this morning and afternoon, respectively. They were accompanied by Vice Chairman Guo Moruo of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Committee Member Nan Hancheng.

### 穆克塔尔议长在京参观  赞扬我国建设成就　祝毛主席万寿无疆

1966-09-10
第5版()
专栏：

    穆克塔尔议长在京参观
    赞扬我国建设成就　祝毛主席万寿无疆
    新华社九日讯　索马里国民议会议长谢赫·穆克塔尔今天下午参观了中国革命博物馆。他在为博物馆的题词中写道：“我们对中国人民反对美帝国主义、英帝国主义和其他帝国主义的斗争，深为感动。”
    谢赫·穆克塔尔议长今天上午参观了北京第三棉纺织厂。他谈到在这里的观感时，“希望中国人民继续前进，在建设祖国和反帝斗争方面取得更大胜利。”他最后“祝毛泽东主席万寿无疆。” 
    谢赫·穆克塔尔议长和由他率领的索马里议会友好代表团的成员，在今天上午和下午，分别由人大常委会副委员长郭沫若，委员南汉宸陪同参观的时候，受到工厂职工和博物馆工作人员的热烈欢迎。
