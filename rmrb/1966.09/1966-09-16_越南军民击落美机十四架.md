Title: Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down 14 U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-09-16
Edition: 6

In recent days, the Vietnamese military and civilians have bravely countered the savage aerial attacks by U.S. aggressors, shooting down a total of fourteen U.S. aircraft. On the 12th, the military and civilians of Nghe An Province shot down a U.S. "Phantom" aircraft and captured the pilot. On the same day, the military and civilians of Hai Duong Province also shot down a U.S. aircraft. The following morning, the military and civilians of Thanh Hoa Province shot down another U.S. aircraft. On the 14th, the military and civilians of Nghe An Province's Vinh City and Ha Bac Province shot down three U.S. aircraft.

According to additional reports: the military and civilians of Nghe An, Quang Binh, and Vinh Linh regions shot down one U.S. aircraft each on the 2nd and 4th. The military and civilians of Thanh Hoa shot down three U.S. aircraft on the 5th, 6th, and 9th. Additionally, the military and civilians of Nghe An Province and Quang Binh Province shot down one invading U.S. aircraft each on the 7th and 13th.

### 越南军民击落美机十四架

1966-09-16
第6版()
专栏：

    越南军民击落美机十四架
    据新华社河内电　连日来越南北方军民英勇反击美国空中强盗的疯狂肆虐，共击落美机十四架。十二日，义安省军民击落美国“鬼怪式”飞机一架，并活捉飞贼。同日海阳省也击落美机一架。次日清晨，清化省军民又击落美机一架。十四日，义安省荣市和河北省军民击落美机三架。
    另据补充消息：义安、河静和永灵区军民分别在二日和四日击落美机一架。清化军民在五日、六日和九日也击落美机三架。此外，义安省和河静省军民分别在七日和十三日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架。
