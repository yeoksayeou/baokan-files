Title: Song of the Three-Eight Style
Author:
Date: 1966-09-10
Edition: 4

The "Three-Eight Style" is a style created by our great leader Chairman Mao, and it is a style that the People's Liberation Army adheres to and implements. For Red Guards, revolutionary youth, and revolutionary masses to practice the "Three-Eight Style" is an important part of learning from the People's Liberation Army. To ensure that everyone always remembers the "Three-Eight Style" and implements it everywhere, the newspaper, in response to readers' requests, publishes the "Song of the Three-Eight Style" today.

### 三八作风歌（歌曲）

1966-09-10
第4版()
专栏：

    三八作风歌
    编者附记：
    “三八作风”是我们伟大领袖毛主席创立的作风，是人民解放军贯彻实行的作风。红卫兵、革命青少年和革命群众实行“三八作风”是学习解放军的一个重要内容。为了使大家时刻牢记“三八作风”，处处贯彻“三八作风”，本报应读者要求，今天发表“三八作风歌”。
