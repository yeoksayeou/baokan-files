Title: Vietnamese Foreign Ministry Strongly Condemns U.S.-Saigon Puppet's "Election" Farce in South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-09-12
Edition: 6

The Vietnamese Foreign Ministry strongly condemns the U.S. aggressors and their lackeys for plotting to stage a "Constitutional Assembly Election" farce in South Vietnam on September 11.

The statement says, the "Constitutional Assembly Election" farce directed by the U.S. is obviously aimed at providing a "legal" basis for the U.S. to intensify its aggression, consolidate the Saigon puppet army and government, and seize the initiative on the battlefield in South Vietnam. The Johnson administration attempts to use this trick to resist the growing resistance of the American people in order to win the U.S. elections in November.

The statement emphasizes that the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam is the sole genuine representative of the South Vietnamese people. Regarding the U.S. imperialists and their Nguyen Van Thieu-Nguyen Cao Ky clique's "election" farce, the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam has clearly pointed out: All so-called "Constitutional Assemblies" or any "parliaments" created by the U.S.-Saigon puppet clique are tools serving the aggressive and traitorous policies of U.S. imperialism and its lackeys. Therefore, the South Vietnamese people resolutely do not recognize any deceptive "elections" conducted by U.S. imperialism and its lackeys.

The statement says, the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam strongly condemns the so-called "Constitutional Assembly Election" conducted by the U.S. and the Nguyen Van Thieu-Nguyen Cao Ky clique, and declares that it firmly does not recognize any results of this "election."

### 越南外交部严厉谴责美伪在南越制造“大选”丑剧  越南人民决不承认美帝策划的伪“选举”  南越民族解放阵线是南越人民唯一真正代表

1966-09-12
第6版()
专栏：

    越南外交部严厉谴责美伪在南越制造“大选”丑剧
    越南人民决不承认美帝策划的伪“选举”
    南越民族解放阵线是南越人民唯一真正代表
    据新华社河内电　越南民主共和国外交部八日发表声明，严厉谴责美国侵略者及其走狗策划在九月十一日在越南南方上演“制宪议会选举”丑剧的阴谋。
    声明说，美国导演的这次“制宪议会选举”丑剧，显然是为美国继续加强侵略战争、巩固南越伪军和伪政权以及夺取越南南方战场上的主动权而制造“合法”的基础。约翰逊政府妄图以这种伎俩来抵御美国人民日益强烈的反抗，以便在十一月份的美国大选中取胜。
    声明强调说，越南南方民族解放阵线是越南南方人民的唯一真正代表。关于美帝国主义及其阮文绍—阮高其集团的“选举”丑剧，越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团已经明确指出：一切所谓的“制宪议会”或由美伪集团制造的任何一种“议会”，都是为美帝国主义及其走狗的侵略政策和卖国政策效劳的工具。因此，越南南方人民坚决不承认美帝国主义及其走狗进行的任何骗人的“选举”。
    声明说，越南民主共和国政府严厉谴责美国和阮文绍—阮高其集团进行的所谓“制宪议会选举”，并且宣布坚决不承认这一“选举”的任何结果。
