Title: Somali National Assembly Friendship Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-09-30
Edition: 4

The Speaker of the Somali National Assembly, Sheikh Mukhtar Muhammad Hussein, and the friendship delegation of the Somali National Assembly led by him, after visiting southern China, returned to Beijing today by special plane, accompanied by Vice Chairman Zhou Jianren. Vice Chairman Yang Mingxuan and others went to the airport to welcome them.

### 索马里国民议会友好代表团回京

1966-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

    索马里国民议会友好代表团回京
    新华社二十九日讯　索马里国民议会议长谢赫·穆克塔尔·穆罕默德·侯赛因和由他率领的索马里国民议会友好代表团，在我国南方访问以后，在周建人副委员长陪同下，今天乘专机回到北京。杨明轩副委员长等前往机场迎接。
