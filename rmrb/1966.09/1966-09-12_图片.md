Title: Air Force Pilots Study Chairman Mao's Works During Rest Time
Author:
Date: 1966-09-12
Edition: 3

	Pilots from a certain unit of the Beijing Military Region Air Force are utilizing their rest time at the airfield to study the works of Chairman Mao. The photo was taken by a reporter from this newspaper.
