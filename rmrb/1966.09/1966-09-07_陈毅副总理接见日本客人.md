Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-09-07
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi today received members of the visiting delegation from the Liberal Democratic Party of Japan's Diet, including Kojima Yoshizane, Kosaka Zenzo, Ezaki Masumi, Fukuda Ichiro, Kuranari Masashi, Sakamura Kichimasa, Kujiraoka Heisuke, Kawano Mitsuaki, Okubo Ninnin, Shimonishi Fumio, and Kamisato Yoshihiko. He had a friendly talk with them.

Liao Chengzhi and others participated in the reception.

### 陈毅副总理接见日本客人

1966-09-07
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见日本客人
    新华社六日讯　陈毅副总理今天接见了日本自由民主党国会议员访华团的成员古井喜实、小坂善太郎、江崎真澄、福田一、仓成正、坂村吉正、鲸冈兵助、川野三晓、大久保任晴、下西文雄、上和田义彦，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    廖承志等参加了这次接见。
