Title: Brussels People's Theatre Passes Resolution: The Significance of China's Cultural Revolution Is Immeasurable
Author:
Date: 1966-09-06
Edition: 5

Brussels People's Theatre Passes Resolution: The Significance of China's Cultural Revolution Is Immeasurable

	New China News Agency reports: The Brussels People's Theatre Committee recently unanimously passed a resolution, celebrating the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution being carried out by the Chinese people. The full text of the resolution is as follows:

	The Brussels People's Theatre expresses its warmest regards to the Chinese people, led by Comrade Mao Zedong and the Chinese Communist Party, for their outstanding struggle in the current Great Socialist Cultural Revolution.

	It will deeply study the lessons of this Cultural Revolution; the importance of these lessons to the people of the world is immeasurable.

	Long live the Chinese people! Long live the Chinese Communist Party! Long live the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution! Long live Mao Zedong Thought!

### 布鲁塞尔人民剧院通过决议  中国文化革命意义无法估量

1966-09-06
第5版()
专栏：

    布鲁塞尔人民剧院通过决议
    中国文化革命意义无法估量
    新华社讯　比利时布鲁塞尔人民剧院委员会全体成员不久前一致通过一项决议，欢呼中国人民正在进行的无产阶级文化大革命。决议全文如下：
    布鲁塞尔人民剧院热情地向以毛泽东同志为首的中国共产党领导下的中国人民在目前开展的伟大的社会主义文化大革命中所进行的卓越斗争致以敬意。
    它将深入研究这次文化大革命的教益；这些教益对于全世界人民的重要性是无法估量的。
    中国人民万岁！中国共产党万岁！社会主义文化大革命万岁！毛泽东思想万岁！
