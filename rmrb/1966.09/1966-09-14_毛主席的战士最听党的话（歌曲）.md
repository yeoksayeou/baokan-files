Title: Chairman Mao's Soldiers Always Listen to the Party
Author:
Date: 1966-09-14
Edition: 6

    In the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, the broad masses of Red Guards and revolutionary youth have resolved to be like the soldiers of the People's Liberation Army, to read Chairman Mao's books, to listen to Chairman Mao's words, to act according to Chairman Mao's instructions, and to be good soldiers of Chairman Mao. The revolutionary song "Chairman Mao's Soldiers Always Listen to the Party" truly expresses the deep feelings of the soldiers of the People's Liberation Army who love Chairman Mao and the Party, listen to Chairman Mao's words, listen to the Party's words, and dedicate themselves wholeheartedly to the revolution. Now, in response to everyone's request, we are republishing this song.

### 毛主席的战士最听党的话（歌曲）

1966-09-14
第6版()
专栏：

    毛主席的战士最听党的话
    编者的话：在无产阶级文化大革命中，广大的红卫兵和革命青少年，决心要像解放军战士那样，读毛主席的书，听毛主席的话，照毛主席的指示办事，做毛主席的好战士。革命歌曲《毛主席的战士最听党的话》，真实地表达了解放军战士们无限热爱毛主席，热爱党，听毛主席的话，听党的话，一心为革命的深厚的思想感情。现在，我们应大家的要求，重新发表这首歌曲。
