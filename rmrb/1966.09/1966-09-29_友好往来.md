Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1966-09-29
Edition: 6

Friendly Exchanges

	• Technical Advisor Mamadou Talla of the Malian Presidency and his wife, at the invitation of China, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 27th for a friendly visit.

	• The Malian Agricultural Delegation led by Abdoulaye Diop and the General Manager of the Mali-Niger Company, Samba Lamina Traore and his wife, at the invitation of China, arrived in Beijing in the evening of the 27th to participate in National Day activities.

	• Brazilian lawyer Danilo Santos and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane in the evening of the 27th.

	Santos and his wife came to China at the invitation of Hou Fazeng, Wang Zhi, Su Ziping, and Zhang Baosheng, members of the Chinese Exhibition Preparation Group who were illegally detained by the Brazilian authorities, to participate in National Day activities and conduct a friendly visit.

### 友好往来

1966-09-29
第6版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    △马里总统府技术顾问马马杜·塔拉和夫人，应邀来我国进行友好访问，二十七日乘飞机到达北京。
    △由阿布杜拉耶·迪奥科率领的马里农业考察团，和马里尼日尔公司总经理桑巴·拉明纳·特拉奥雷和夫人，应邀来我国参加国庆活动，二十七日晚到达北京。
    △巴西律师达尼卢·桑图斯和夫人，二十七日晚乘飞机到达北京。
    桑图斯夫妇是应曾被巴西当局非法逮捕的中国展览会筹备组成员侯法曾、王治、苏子平，张宝生的邀请，前来我国参加国庆活动并进行友好访问的。　（据新华社）
