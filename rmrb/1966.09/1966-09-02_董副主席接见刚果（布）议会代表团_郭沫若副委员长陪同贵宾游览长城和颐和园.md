Title: Vice Chairman Dong Receives Delegation from the National Assembly of the Congo (Brazzaville)
Author:
Date: 1966-09-02
Edition: 3

Vice Chairman Dong received the delegation from the National Assembly of the Congo (Brazzaville) today in the afternoon. Vice Chairman Guo Moruo accompanied the guests on a tour of the Great Wall and the Summer Palace.

	New China News Agency, September 1st: Vice Chairman Dong Biwu met today in the afternoon with the President of the National Assembly of the Congo (Brazzaville), Muabi Andele Ji, and the entire delegation led by him. They had a cordial and friendly conversation. Those present at the meeting included Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo, Deputy Secretary-General Lian Guan, Zhao Boping, and Deputy Director of the African Affairs Department of the Foreign Ministry, Xie Feng.

	The Chargé d'Affaires of the Congolese (Brazzaville) Embassy in China, Jean Daniel Bakula, was also present.

	According to the New China News Agency, September 1st: The President of the National Assembly of the Congo (Brazzaville), Muabi Andele Ji, and his wife, along with the members of the delegation led by him, were accompanied by Vice Chairman Guo Moruo and his wife Yu Liqun today to visit the Great Wall and the Summer Palace.

	The Chargé d'Affaires of the Congolese (Brazzaville) Embassy in China, Jean Daniel Bakula, and others also joined the delegation for the visit. The delegation visited the Beijing Educational Instruments Factory yesterday.

### 董副主席接见刚果（布）议会代表团  郭沫若副委员长陪同贵宾游览长城和颐和园

1966-09-02
第3版()
专栏：

    董副主席接见刚果（布）议会代表团
    郭沫若副委员长陪同贵宾游览长城和颐和园
    新华社一日讯　董必武副主席今天下午接见刚果（布）国民议会议长穆亚比·安德烈·乔治，和由他率领的刚果（布）议会代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有人大常委会副委员长郭沫若，副秘书长连贯、赵伯平，外交部非洲司副司长谢丰。
    刚果（布）驻中国大使馆临时代办让·丹尼尔·巴库拉也在座。
    据新华社一日讯　刚果（布）国民议会议长穆亚比·安德烈·乔治和夫人，以及由他率领的刚果（布）议会代表团的成员，今天由郭沫若副委员长和夫人于立群陪同，游览了长城和颐和园。
    刚果（布）驻中国大使馆临时代办让·丹尼尔·巴库拉等也随同代表团参观游览。代表团昨天参观了北京教学仪器厂。
