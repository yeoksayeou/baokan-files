Title: Friendly Competition in Rapid Wireless Communication Among Allied Forces to be Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-09-15
Edition: 5

The friendly competition in rapid wireless communication among allied forces will begin on September 21st in Beijing. The participating teams in this friendly competition include representatives from the military forces of China, Vietnam, and North Korea. This competition will further strengthen the friendship and unity among the peoples and military forces of the three countries. It will also facilitate the exchange of experiences and improve the technical level of wireless communication.

	Newswire: September 14th

	The Vietnamese People's Army delegation, consisting of 11 members led by Colonel Huang Cai Long, arrived in Beijing by train today.

	The Vietnamese People's Army delegation was warmly welcomed at the station by relevant officials Han Fu Dong, Qu Pei Yuan, Sun Zheng Hua, and athletes from the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The athletes presented flowers to their Vietnamese comrades from the frontlines of the anti-American struggle.

	Senior Colonel Chen Wen Peng, the military attaché of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, was also present at the station to welcome the delegation.

### 友军无线电快速收发报友谊赛将在京举行

1966-09-15
第5版()
专栏：

    友军无线电快速收发报友谊赛将在京举行
    据新华社十四日讯  友军无线电快速收发报友谊赛，将于九月二十一日开始在北京举行。参加这次友谊赛的有中国、越南、朝鲜三个国家的军队代表队。通过这次友谊赛，将进一步增进三国人民及其军队之间的友谊和团结；相互交流经验，提高无线电收发报的技术水平。
    新华社十四日讯　参加友军无线电快速收发报友谊赛的越南人民军代表队一行十一人，在领队黄才龙中校率领下，今天乘火车到达北京。
    越南人民军代表队在车站上受到了有关方面负责人韩复东、屈培壅、孙正华和中国人民解放军运动员们的热烈欢迎。运动员们向来自反美斗争前线的越南战友献了鲜花。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆武官陈文彭上校也到车站迎接。
