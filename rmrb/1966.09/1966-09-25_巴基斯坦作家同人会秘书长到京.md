Title: Secretary-General of the Pakistan Writers' Association Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-09-25
Edition: 5

At the invitation of the Chinese Writers' Association and the Secretary-General of the Afro-Asian Writers' Bureau, Sennanayake, the Secretary-General of the Pakistan Writers' Association, Jamiluddin Ali, and his wife arrived in Beijing this evening.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included: Vice Chairman of the Chinese Federation of Literary and Art Circles, Xu Guangping, and Secretary-General of the Chinese Writers' Association, Xu Hanru.

The Secretary-General of the Afro-Asian Writers' Bureau, Sennanayake, the Sudanese writer Kair, the Indonesian writer Isa, the Angolan writer Dakru, and the Cameroonian writer Feizi also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 巴基斯坦作家同人会秘书长到京

1966-09-25
第5版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦作家同人会秘书长到京
    新华社二十四日讯  应中国作家协会及亚非作家常设局秘书长森纳那亚克的邀请，巴基斯坦作家同人会秘书长贾米路丁·阿里夫妇今晚到达北京。到机场迎接的有：中国文联副主席许广平，中国作家协会秘书长许翰如等。
    亚非作家常设局秘书长森纳那亚克，苏丹作家凯尔，印尼作家伊萨，安哥拉作家达克鲁斯，喀麦隆作家费则，也到机场迎接。
