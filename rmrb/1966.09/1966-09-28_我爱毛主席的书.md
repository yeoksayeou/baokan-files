Title: I Love Chairman Mao's Book
Author: Meng Xianzong
Date: 1966-09-28
Edition: 3

	I love Chairman Mao's book,
	More than I love my parents;
	Studying it year after year,
	My heart is warm and glowing.
	The revolutionary path is clear,
	Striding forward on the journey.
	Gripping my gun and pen,
	The country will be forever secure.
