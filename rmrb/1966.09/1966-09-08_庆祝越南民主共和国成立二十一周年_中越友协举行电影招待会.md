Title: Celebrating the 21st Anniversary of the Founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-09-08
Edition: 4

The China-Vietnam Friendship Association held a movie screening event tonight to enthusiastically celebrate the 21st anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

The event screened films such as "Heroic Hon Giao Island," which depict the heroic deeds of the military and people of North Vietnam in their struggle against American aggression and for national salvation. The Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, Le Chung Thi, and representatives from the Permanent Representative Office of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam in China, including Le Thong San, attended the event.

### 庆祝越南民主共和国成立二十一周年  中越友协举行电影招待会

1966-09-08
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝越南民主共和国成立二十一周年
    中越友协举行电影招待会
    新华社七日讯　中国越南友好协会今晚举行电影招待会，热烈庆祝越南民主共和国成立二十一周年。
    招待会上放映了反映越南北方军民抗美救国斗争英雄事迹的影片《英雄的昏果岛》等。越南驻中国大使馆临时代办黎终始，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代表黎松山等出席了招待会。
