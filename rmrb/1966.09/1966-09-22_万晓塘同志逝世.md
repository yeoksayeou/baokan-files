Title: Comrade Wan Xiaotang Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1966-09-22
Edition: 2

Comrade Wan Xiaotang, Secretary of the CPC Hebei Provincial Committee, First Secretary of the CPC Tianjin Municipal Committee, First Political Commissar of the Tianjin Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and National People's Congress Representative, passed away on the evening of September 19 due to a heart attack. He was 50 years old.

Comrade Wan Xiaotang joined the Communist Party of China in 1937. He has held various positions, including platoon leader of an anti-Japanese guerrilla unit, county committee secretary, political instructor of a regiment in the Eighth Route Army, department head of a regional committee, director of the Public Security Bureau of the Jilu-Yu Administrative Region, director of the Social Affairs Department of the Jilu-Yu Regional Party Committee, director of the Tianjin Public Security Bureau, deputy mayor of Tianjin, and secretary of the CPC Tianjin Municipal Committee.

Comrade Wan Xiaotang was an outstanding member of our Party. He worked diligently and conscientiously, and despite long-term illness, he continued to work tirelessly, remaining loyal to the Party's cause. His passing is a great loss to the Party.

After Comrade Wan Xiaotang's passing, the First Secretary of the CPC Hebei Provincial Committee, Liu Zichou, along with other responsible comrades from Hebei Province and Tianjin Municipality, paid their respects and bid farewell to his body.

### 万晓塘同志逝世

1966-09-22
第2版()
专栏：

    万晓塘同志逝世
    新华社天津二十一日电　中共河北省委书记处书记、中共天津市委第一书记、中国人民解放军天津警备区第一政治委员、全国人民代表大会代表万晓塘同志，因心脏病暴发，在九月十九日晚逝世。享年五十岁。
    万晓塘同志在一九三七年参加中国共产党，历任抗日游击队中队长，中共县委书记，八路军营教导员，地委部长，冀鲁豫行署公安局局长，冀鲁豫区党委社会部长，天津市公安局长，天津市副市长，中共天津市委书记处书记等职务。
    万晓塘同志，是我党优秀党员。他勤勤恳恳、兢兢业业工作，并且长期带病坚持工作，忠实于党的事业。他的逝世是党的损失。
    万晓塘同志逝世后，中共河北省委第一书记刘子厚以及河北省和天津市的负责同志等曾前往致哀，向遗体告别。
