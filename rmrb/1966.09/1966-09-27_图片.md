Title: Cotton Harvest in Changshu County, Jiangsu Province
Author:
Date: 1966-09-27
Edition: 2

In Changshu County, a major cotton-producing area in Jiangsu Province, the people have achieved significant increases in cotton production for four consecutive years. This year, they have once again secured a substantial increase in yield. The photo shows members of the Fourth Production Brigade, Third Commune, Xushi People's Commune in Changshu County, busy sun-drying newly harvested cotton.
