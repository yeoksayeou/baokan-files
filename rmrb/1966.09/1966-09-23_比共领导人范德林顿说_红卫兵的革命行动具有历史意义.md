Title: Belgian Communist Party Leader Van Der Linden Says the Red Guards' Revolutionary Actions Have Historical Significance
Author:
Date: 1966-09-23
Edition: 4

Belgian Communist Party Political Bureau member Van Der Linden, in a meeting with all party members in the Brussels region, enthusiastically praised China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. He said: "The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China is a great development of Mao Zedong Thought."

He said, "Mao Zedong is a genius successor of Marxism-Leninism," and "The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China has been welcomed by all Marxist-Leninists around the world." He said, "The more the reactionaries attack our Chinese comrades, the more our Chinese comrades feel they are on the right path."

According to a report in the September 9 issue of the Belgian Communist Party's organ, the weekly "Voice of the People," Van Der Linden, who recently visited China, reported on the situation of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China at this meeting.

In his report, Van Der Linden also highly praised the revolutionary actions of the Chinese Red Guards. He said, the Red Guards' revolutionary actions, which have historical significance, "have received the support and encouragement of the Party, revolutionary masses, youth, the People's Liberation Army, peasants, and workers."

Van Der Linden said in his report, through the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, Mao Zedong Thought will become an even more invincible great material force.

He said, the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution will achieve great victories for socialism.

### 比共领导人范德林顿说  红卫兵的革命行动具有历史意义

1966-09-23
第4版()
专栏：

    比共领导人范德林顿说
    红卫兵的革命行动具有历史意义
    据新华社二十一日讯  布鲁塞尔消息：比利时共产党政治局委员范德林顿在比利时共产党布鲁塞尔地区全体党员会议上热烈赞扬中国无产阶级文化大革命，他说：中国的无产阶级文化大革命，是毛泽东思想的巨大发展。
    他说：“毛泽东是马克思列宁主义天才的继承者”，“中国的无产阶级文化大革命受到了全世界马克思列宁主义者的欢迎”。他说，“反动派越是攻击我们的中国同志，中国同志就越是感到走的是康庄大道。”
    据九月九日出版的比利时共产党机关刊物《人民之声》周刊报道，最近刚访问中国的范德林顿在这次会议上报告了中国无产阶级文化大革命的情况。
    范德林顿在报告中还高度评价中国红卫兵的革命行动。他说，红卫兵的具有历史意义的革命行动，“受到党、革命群众、青年、解放军、农民和工人的支持和鼓励。”
    范德林顿在报告中说，通过无产阶级文化大革命，毛泽东思想将更成为锐不可当的巨大物质力量。
    他说，无产阶级文化大革命将使社会主义取得巨大胜利。
