Title: Sri Lankan Communist Party Politburo Member Samugadasan Says Indonesian Revolution Will Rise Again and Achieve Victory
Author: 
Date: 1966-09-05
Edition: 5

New Delhi, September 1—The Ceylon Communist Party Central Committee Politburo member and Secretariat member Samugadasan published an article in the August 22 issue of the Ceylonese *Labour News* and *Worker's News*. The article states that the Indonesian revolution will inevitably rise again and ultimately achieve victory. The article emphasizes: "Where there is oppression, there will be rebellion. This is an inevitable law."

The article points out: The Indonesian revolution is only a temporary setback. The brutal actions and fascist dictatorship of the Indonesian right-wing military regime do not indicate their strength but rather their weakness—they can no longer continue to rule through the deceptive means of so-called parliamentary democracy.

The article states: The Indonesian events once again prove this truth: the reactionaries will never allow the working class to come to power through peaceful means. Peaceful transition is a utopian dream of revisionism.

The article further points out: Suharto's power is entirely derived from the brutal force of the army he commands. The so-called "people's" demonstrations organized by the Indonesian Army cannot deceive anyone. Parliament and the parliamentary system are merely a thin veil covering the naked capitalist dictatorship based primarily on the armed forces.

### 锡共政治局委员桑穆加塔桑发表文章指出  印尼革命必将再次兴起获得胜利  印尼事件表明和平过渡是修正主义的空想

1966-09-05
第5版()
专栏：

    锡共政治局委员桑穆加塔桑发表文章指出
    印尼革命必将再次兴起获得胜利
    印尼事件表明和平过渡是修正主义的空想
    新华社科伦坡一日电  锡兰共产党中央政治局委员、书记处书记桑穆加塔桑在八月二十二日的锡兰《劳工报》和《工人报》上发表文章指出，印度尼西亚的革命必将再次兴起，并且必将获得最后胜利。文章强调指出：“哪里有镇压，哪里就要造反。这是必然的规律。”
    文章指出：印度尼西亚革命只是暂时的退却，印度尼西亚右派军人政权采取残暴行动和实行法西斯专政，这不是表明它们有力量而是表明它们虚弱——不能通过所谓议会民主这种骗人的东西继续进行统治了。
    文章说：印度尼西亚的事件再一次表明这个真理：反动派决不会让工人阶级通过和平手段来当权。和平过渡是修正主义的空想。
    文章还指出：苏哈托的权力完全是从他所指挥的军队的残暴力量获得的。印度尼西亚陆军所组织的所谓“人民”示威欺骗不了任何人。议会和议会制度只是掩盖主要以武装部队为基础的赤裸裸的资本专政的一块薄薄的遮羞布。
