Title: Huainan Power Plant's Electricity Generation Increases Month by Month
Author:
Date: 1966-09-10
Edition: 2

Huainan Power Plant's Electricity Generation Increases Month by Month

According to Xinhua News Agency, Hefei, September 8th: The workers of Huainan Power Plant, armed with the invincible Mao Zedong Thought, have been fighting against the bourgeois representatives during the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, striving for a double harvest in cultural revolution and production.

This summer, the temperature was higher than in previous years, but the entire plant showed an unprecedented good situation in production: the electricity generation in June increased by 7.5% compared to the average of the first five months of this year, and by 6% compared to the same period last year; in July, it increased by 8.2% compared to June; in August, not only was the electricity generation over-fulfilled, but it also set a new record for the highest daily electricity generation since the plant was built. The internal electricity consumption has significantly decreased compared to the same period last year. The coal consumption rate has reached the advanced standard set by the state, and the production cost has been greatly reduced.

### 淮南发电厂发电量月月增长

1966-09-10
第2版()
专栏：

    淮南发电厂发电量月月增长
    据新华社合肥八日电　淮南发电厂职工在无产阶级文化大革命中，以战无不胜的毛泽东思想为武器，向资产阶级代表人物开火，力争文化革命和生产双丰收。
    今年夏天，气温比往年高，但是全厂在生产上却出现了一派前所未有的大好形势：六月份发电量比今年头五个月平均增长百分之七点五，比去年同期增长百分之六；七月份比六月份增长百分之八点二；八月份，不仅超额发电，而且创造了建厂以来日发电量最高纪录。厂内用电比去年同期有显著下降。煤耗率达到国家规定的先进标准，生产成本大大降低。
