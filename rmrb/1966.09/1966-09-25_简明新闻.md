	Brief News

	Military and Civilians in North Vietnam Shoot Down 10 US Planes

	The military and civilians in the provinces of Thanh Hoa, Nam Ha, Ha Bac, and Ha Tinh in North Vietnam shot down 10 invading American aggressor planes from September 21 to 23.

	Fumi Vongvich Accuses US Imperialism of Spraying Chemical Poisons

	Fumi Vongvich, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front and Minister of Information, Propaganda, and Tourism of the Lao National United Government, condemned the recent crime of the US imperialists spraying chemical poisons on the people in the liberated areas of Laos.

	Four Thousand People in Ecuador Hold Anti-US Demonstration

	Approximately four thousand people recently held a demonstration and rally in Quito, the capital of Ecuador, condemning the US aggression in Vietnam and interference in Ecuador's internal affairs. They also protested against the economic policies of the Yarovoy military dictatorship, which are subservient to the US and harm national interests. The demonstrators carried placards reading, "Long live the heroic struggle of the Vietnamese people!" and "Down with US imperialism, the number one enemy of the people of the world!"

	Panamanian Students Punish US Ambassador

	Panamanian students, harboring deep national hatred towards the US imperialists, on September 22, punished the US Ambassador to Panama, Adel, with stones, garbage, and red paint at the University of Panama.

	US Conducts Another Underground Nuclear Test in Nevada

	Washington: The US Atomic Energy Commission announced that the US conducted another underground nuclear weapons test at the Nevada Test Site on September 23.

### 简明新闻

1966-09-25
第6版()
专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    越南北方军民又击落美机十架
    越南北方清化、南河、河北、河静等省军民在二十一日到二十三日三天内，共击落入侵的美国强盗飞机十架。
    冯维希谴责美帝撒放化学毒药
    老挝爱国战线党中央委员会总书记、老挝民族团结政府新闻、宣传和游览部大臣富米·冯维希谴责美帝国主义最近对老挝解放区人民撒放化学毒药的罪行。
    厄瓜多尔四千人举行反美大示威
    厄瓜多尔首都基多约四千人最近举行示威游行和集会谴责美国侵略越南和干涉厄瓜多尔内政，并且抗议耶罗维军事独裁政权执行屈从美国、损害民族利益的经济政策。参加示威的群众高举“越南人民的英勇斗争万岁！”“打倒全世界人民的头号敌人美帝国主义！”等标语牌。
    巴拿马大学生惩罚美驻巴大使
    对美帝怀有深刻民族仇恨的巴拿马大学生，二十二日在巴拿马大学用石块、垃圾和红色油漆惩罚了美国驻巴拿马大使阿德尔。
    美国又在内华达搞地下核试验
    华盛顿消息：据美国原子能委员会宣布，美国二十三日又在内华达试验场进行地下核武器试验。
                            （据新华社讯）
