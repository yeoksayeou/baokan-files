Title: Chinese and Vietnamese Youth Football Teams Play Friendly Match; Vice Premiers He Long and Chen Yi Receive Vietnamese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-09-12
Edition: 5

The youth football team of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam played a friendly match against the Chinese youth football team at the Beijing Workers' Stadium this evening. The match ended in a 1-1 draw.

Vice Premiers He Long and Chen Yi, along with the Chargé d'Affaires of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, Lyh Chung-shih, watched the match this evening. Before the start of the second half, He Long and Chen Yi received the visiting team's leader, Phan Anh Dang, and walked down from the VIP stand to shake hands with the athletes of both teams. They also posed for a group photo together.

### 中越青年足球队进行友谊赛  贺龙陈毅副总理接见越南客人

1966-09-12
第5版()
专栏：

    中越青年足球队进行友谊赛
    贺龙陈毅副总理接见越南客人
    据新华社十一日讯　越南民主共和国青年足球队今晚在北京工人体育场同中国青年足球队进行友谊比赛。比赛结果一比一。
    贺龙、陈毅副总理，越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办黎终始观看了今晚的比赛。下半场比赛开始前，贺龙、陈毅副总理接见了客队领队潘昂当，并且同黎终始走下主席台同双方运动员一一握手，在一起照了像。
