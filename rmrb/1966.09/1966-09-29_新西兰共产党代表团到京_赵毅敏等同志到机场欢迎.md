Title: New Zealand Communist Party Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-09-29
Edition: 1

A delegation from the New Zealand Communist Party, led by Comrade A. Reith, arrived in Beijing by plane tonight. They were invited by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to participate in the National Day celebrations and to conduct a friendly visit to China.

Comrade Zhao Yimin, alternate member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and others went to the airport to welcome them. Young Pioneers presented flowers to all members of the delegation.

### 新西兰共产党代表团到京  赵毅敏等同志到机场欢迎

1966-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    新西兰共产党代表团到京
    赵毅敏等同志到机场欢迎
    新华社二十八日讯　由团长亚·雷特同志率领的新西兰共产党代表团，应中共中央委员会的邀请来我国参加国庆活动和进行友好访问，今天晚上乘飞机到达北京。
    中共中央候补委员赵毅敏同志等前往机场欢迎。少先队员向代表团全体同志献了鲜花。
