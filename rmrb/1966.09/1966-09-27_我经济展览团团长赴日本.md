Title: Chinese Economic Exhibition Delegation Head to Japan
Author:
Date: 1966-09-27
Edition: 6

The People's Republic of China's Economic and Trade Exhibition is soon to open in Kitakyushu, Japan. The delegation head, Xiao Xiangqian, departed Beijing today for Japan.

Those who went to the airport to see him off included the secretary-general of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Zhang Fusheng, and relevant personnel. Representatives from the Japan Friendship Trading Company in Beijing also went to the airport to see him off.

### 我经济展览团团长赴日本

1966-09-27
第6版()
专栏：

    我经济展览团团长赴日本
    新华社二十五日讯　中华人民共和国经济贸易展览会即将在日本北九州开幕。展览团团长萧向前今天离开北京去日本。
    前往机场送行的有中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长张复生及有关人士。在京日本友好商社代表也到机场送行。
