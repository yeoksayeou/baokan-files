Title: Thai Military Leader Admits U.S. Troops Participate in Suppressing Thai People's Armed Struggle
Author:
Date: 1966-09-19
Edition: 5

Thai military leader admits U.S. troops participate in suppressing Thai people's armed struggle

According to a report by Xinhua News Agency on the 18th, a high-ranking military officer of the Thanom–Praphas sellout clique in Bangkok admitted on the 16th for the first time that the invading U.S. troops stationed in Thailand have participated in the reactionary Thai troops' suppression of the Thai people.

According to the Bangkok World News on the 17th, quoting the Thai armed forces chief of staff, Thawit Pongsri, a "Thai-U.S. Joint Special Operations Force" is currently conducting so-called "anti-communist" military activities in various parts of Thailand.

According to a report by the Associated Press on the 17th, the number of U.S. troops stationed in Thailand is estimated to be around 27,000.

### 泰国军事头目公开承认  美军参加镇压泰国人民武装斗争

1966-09-19
第5版()
专栏：

    泰国军事头目公开承认
    美军参加镇压泰国人民武装斗争
    据新华社十八日讯　曼谷消息：泰国他侬—巴博卖国集团的一名高级军官十六日第一次公开承认，驻在泰国的美国侵略军参加了泰国反动部队镇压泰国人民的战斗行动。
    据《曼谷世界报》十七日引述泰国武装部队总参谋长他威·尊拉塞的谈话说，一支“泰—美联合特别作战部队”正在泰国各地进行所谓“剿共”军事活动。
    据美联社十七日报道，驻在泰国的美国军队估计现在共有二万七千人。
