Title: Chairman Mao is the Red Sun in the Hearts of the World's Revolutionary People
Author:
Date: 1966-09-30
Edition: 5

Chairman Mao is the red sun in the hearts of the world's revolutionary people. This is a truth that cannot be denied. In the great era of the world's proletarian revolution, Chairman Mao, as the great leader of the Chinese proletariat and the Chinese people, has led the Chinese revolution to great victories. He has also led the world's revolutionary people in their struggle against imperialism, revisionism, and reactionaries. Chairman Mao's thought is the guiding light for the world's revolutionary people.

The Central Academy of Fine Arts has provided this article.
