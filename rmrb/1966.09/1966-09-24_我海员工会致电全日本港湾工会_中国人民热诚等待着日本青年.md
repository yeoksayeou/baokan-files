Title: Chinese Seamen's Union Cables All-Japan Port Workers' Union
Author:
Date: 1966-09-24
Edition: 6

The All-China Seamen's Union today sent a telegram to the All-Japan Port Workers' Union, strongly protesting against the Sato government's use of violence against the just struggle of Japanese youth and its open arrest of the head of the Japanese Port Workers' Union delegation, Yamamoto Nobuaki.

The telegram said, the Chinese seamen and workers, together with all the Chinese people and youth, have already made all the necessary preparations and are ready at any time to welcome Japanese youth friends to China to participate in the second China-Japan Youth Friendship Grand Festival. Whenever you come, we will welcome you; however many of you come, we will welcome you all. We are eagerly awaiting your arrival.

### 我海员工会致电全日本港湾工会  中国人民热诚等待着日本青年

1966-09-24
第6版()
专栏：

    我海员工会致电全日本港湾工会
    中国人民热诚等待着日本青年
    据新华社二十三日讯　中国海员工会全国委员会今天打电报给全日本港湾工会，强烈抗议佐藤政府对日本青年的正义斗争施加暴力，并且公然一度逮捕日本港湾工会代表团团长山本信晃。
    电报说，中国海员工人同全中国人民和青年，都已经做好一切筹备工作，随时准备欢迎日本青年朋友来华参加第二届中日青年友好大联欢，你们什么时候来，我们就什么时候欢迎，你们来多少，我们就欢迎多少，我们正在热诚地等待你们的到来。
