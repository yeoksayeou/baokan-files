Title: Celebrating the 18th Anniversary of the Founding of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea
Author:
Date: 1966-09-08
Edition: 4

Beijing's Hongxing Sino-Korean Friendship People's Commune held a film evening to celebrate the 18th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The acting charge d'affaires of the Korean Embassy in China, Kim Jae-suk, and other diplomatic officials were invited to attend. Revolutionary workers from the state-owned Beijing Electronic Tube Factory and the state-owned Beijing Second Radio Materials Factory also held a celebration on the 6th.

### 庆祝朝鲜民主主义人民共和国成立十八周年  北京中朝友好公社举行晚会

1966-09-08
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝朝鲜民主主义人民共和国成立十八周年
    北京中朝友好公社举行晚会
    据新华社七日讯　北京红星中朝友好人民公社社员今天举行电影晚会庆祝朝鲜民主主义人民共和国成立十八周年。朝鲜驻中国大使馆临时代办金在淑等外交官员应邀出席。国营北京电子管厂和国营北京第二无线电器材厂的革命职工六日也举行庆祝会。
