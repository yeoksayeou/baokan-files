Title: U.S. Ships and Aircraft Intrude into Our Territorial Waters and Airspace
Author:
Date: 1966-09-04
Edition: 5

	U.S. Ships and Aircraft Intrude into Our Territorial Waters and Airspace
	We Issue the 410th Serious Warning

	New China News Agency, September 3rd: At 4:32 AM to 5:22 AM on September 3rd, one U.S. military vessel intruded into our territorial waters off the southeastern region of Dangeng Island, Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 10:06 AM to 10:09 AM, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace above the northeastern region of Wenchang County, Hainan Island, Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 8:13 AM to 8:22 AM and from 12:34 PM to 1:02 PM, two U.S. military aircraft successively intruded into the airspace over Dong Island and Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands, Guangdong Province. Regarding the military provocations by the U.S. military vessels and aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the ministry, issues the 410th serious warning.

### 美舰美机侵入我领海领空  我提出第四百一十一次严重警告

1966-09-04
第5版()
专栏：

    美舰美机侵入我领海领空
    我提出第四百一十一次严重警告
    新华社三日讯　九月三日四时三十二分至五时二十二分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我广东省担杆列岛东南地区领海。同日十时零六分至十时零九分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省海南岛文昌县东北地区领海上空。同日八时十三分至八时二十二分和十二时三十四分至十三时零二分，美国军用飞机各一架，先后侵入我广东省西沙群岛的东岛、永兴岛地区领空。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百一十一次严重警告。
