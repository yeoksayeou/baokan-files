Title: Chairman Mao's Work "Care for the People's Livelihood, Pay Attention to Work Methods" Published in Tamil in Ceylon
Author:
Date: 1966-09-04
Edition: 5

Chairman Mao's work "Care for the People's Livelihood, Pay Attention to Work Methods" has been translated into Tamil and published in Ceylon.

This book was published by the Colombo People's Press.

### 毛主席著作《关心群众生活，注意工作方法》在锡兰译成泰米尔文出版

1966-09-04
第5版()
专栏：

    毛主席著作《关心群众生活，注意工作方法》在锡兰译成泰米尔文出版
    新华社科伦坡二日电　毛主席著作《关心群众生活，注意工作方法》已被译成泰米尔文在锡兰出版。
    这本书是由科伦坡人民出版社出版的。
