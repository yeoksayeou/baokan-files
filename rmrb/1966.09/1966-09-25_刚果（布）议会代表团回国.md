Title: Delegation of the National Assembly of the Congo (Brazzaville) Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1966-09-25
Edition: 5

The delegation of the National Assembly of the Congo (Brazzaville), led by the President of the National Assembly, Muabi Andre George, and his wife, has concluded their visit to our country. They departed from Shanghai by plane on the evening of the 20th and returned home.

Shanghai Mayor Cao Qiqiao hosted a banquet for the Congolese guests on the 18th. Muabi, the President of the National Assembly, enthusiastically praised the great achievements of the Chinese people under the wise leadership of the Communist Party of China and Chairman Mao in building their own country. He said that the Chinese people not only increased production but also transformed the entire society. The path that the Chinese people are taking is not only the path that the Congolese people should take but also the path that all the people of the world should take.

Cao Qiqiao enthusiastically praised the great achievements of the Congolese people at the banquet.

### 刚果（布）议会代表团回国

1966-09-25
第5版()
专栏：

    刚果（布）议会代表团回国
    据新华社上海电　刚果（布）国民议会议长穆亚比·安德烈·乔治和夫人，以及由他率领的刚果（布）议会代表团，结束了在我国的访问，二十日晚乘飞机离开上海回国。
    上海市市长曹荻秋十八日为刚果（布）客人举行了宴会。穆亚比议长在宴会上热烈赞扬中国人民在中国共产党和毛主席的英明领导下，在建设自己祖国中取得了辉煌的成就。他说，中国人民不仅增加了生产，而且改造了整个社会。中国人民走的道路，不仅是我们刚果（布）人民要走的，也是全世界人民所要走的道路。
    曹荻秋在宴会上热烈赞扬了刚果（布）人民取得的巨大成就。
