Title: Foreign Friends Warmly Praise Our Great Leader
Author:
Date: 1966-09-24
Edition: 5

Foreign friends warmly praise our great leader
Mao Zedong is the helmsman of the world's revolutionary people
Mao Zedong points the way forward

On September 22, Somalian National Assembly President Sheikh Mukhtar visited the site of the Peasant Movement Training Institute, which was established by Comrade Mao Zedong, in Guangzhou. He enthusiastically praised the great leader Mao Zedong and the Chinese revolution led by Mao Zedong.

He wrote in the guest book: "The great thinker and leader Mao Zedong, who guides the correct path and revolutionary goals, has deeply moved me. At that time, Mao Zedong's great thoughts of training revolutionary cadres in this school ignited the first spark and the first torch. From this visit, I understand how the spark of revolution was ignited here and spread like wildfire across all of China. I pay tribute to this heroic and great leader Mao Zedong. Long live Chairman Mao! Long live the revolution led by Chairman Mao, which has brought fruits to the Chinese people! Long live the revolution that will bring the same fruits to the people of the world! Long live the friendship between Somalia and China!"

The brilliance of Mao Zedong Thought illuminates the hearts of people worldwide

Recently, Mahabuti, a representative of the Lao National Civil Servants Federation, shared his impressions of visiting China, saying that China is the center of the world revolution, and Chairman Mao is the teacher, commander, and helmsman of the world's revolutionary people. The brilliance of Mao Zedong Thought illuminates the hearts of people worldwide, and the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China is the result of the brilliance of Mao Zedong Thought.

Mahabuti said: "The oppressed and exploited people of the world regard China as the center of the world revolution." He said: "They all look towards China because China has the brilliant Mao Zedong Thought."

Chairman Mao dedicates himself to the liberation cause of the oppressed people worldwide

Recently, Yusuf Shimi, the leader of the Tanzanian Friendship Delegation, shared his impressions of visiting China, saying: "Chairman Mao is a person of great talent. Chairman Mao loves the people, and the people love and respect Chairman Mao."

Yusuf wore a Chairman Mao badge on his chest. He enthusiastically said: "Chairman Mao is in good health. He likes to meet us, guests from the fighting continent of Africa." He sincerely praised Chairman Mao for dedicating himself to the liberation cause of the Chinese people and the oppressed people of the world.

Chairman Mao is the leader of the oppressed people worldwide

After watching the performance by the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe in Kabul, the capital of Afghanistan, the Afghan people expressed their boundless love and respect for the greatest leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao Zedong. During the performance, whenever the radiant image of Chairman Mao appeared on the screen, the audience would burst into enthusiastic applause. Some audience members, filled with passion, said: "Mao Zedong is the greatest leader of our time, and we respect him very much." "Mao Zedong is not only the leader of 700 million Chinese people but also the leader of the oppressed people worldwide."

### 各国朋友热情歌颂我们伟大的领袖  毛主席是世界革命人民的舵手

1966-09-24
第5版()
专栏：

    各国朋友热情歌颂我们伟大的领袖
    毛主席是世界革命人民的舵手
    毛主席指引着正确的道路
    索马里国民议会议长谢赫·穆克塔尔二十二日在广州参观毛泽东同志主办的农民运动讲习所旧址的时候，热情赞扬伟大领袖毛主席和由毛主席领导的中国革命。
    他在留言簿上题词的时候写道：“指引着正确道路和革命目标的伟大思想家、伟大领袖毛主席所作的工作，使我深受感动。当时毛主席在这座学校培养革命干部的伟大思想，点燃了星星之火和第一把火炬。我从这次参观中，了解到革命的星星之火怎样从这里点燃起来，以燎原之势燃遍全中国。我向这位英雄和伟大的领袖毛泽东主席致敬。让我们欢呼毛主席万岁！由毛主席领导的、中国人民已经享受到革命成果的革命万岁！使世界人民也将要享受到同样成果的革命万岁！索中两国友谊万岁！”
    毛泽东思想的光辉照亮全世界人民的心
    最近访问过中国的老挝全国公务员联合会代表马哈布蒂发表访华观感说，中国是世界革命的中心，毛主席是世界革命人民的导师、统帅和舵手，毛泽东思想的光辉照亮了全世界人民的心，中国无产阶级文化大革命就是毛泽东思想光辉照耀的结果。
    马哈布蒂说：“全世界被压迫、被剥削的人民把中国看成是世界革命的中心。”他说：“他们都面向着中国，因为中国有光辉的毛泽东思想。”
    毛主席献身于全世界被压迫人民解放事业
    最近访问过中国的坦桑尼亚友好代表团团长优素夫·希米德上校，谈到他访问中国的观感时说：“毛主席是一位有伟大天才的人。毛主席热爱人民，人民热爱和尊敬毛主席。”
    优素夫胸前戴着毛主席纪念章。他兴致勃勃地说：“毛主席身体很好。他喜欢接见我们这些从战斗的非洲来的客人。”他衷心地赞扬毛主席“献身于中国人民的解放事业和全世界被压迫人民的解放事业”。
    毛主席是全世界被压迫人民的领袖
    阿富汗人民观看了中国杂技艺术团在阿富汗首都喀布尔的访问演出后，纷纷表达他们对中国人民最伟大的领袖毛泽东主席的无限热爱和敬意。在演出过程中，每当金光四射的毛主席彩色像在天幕上出现时，场内总是响起热烈的掌声。一些观众满怀激情地说：“毛泽东主席是当代最伟大的领袖，我们非常尊敬他。”“毛泽东主席不仅是七亿中国人民的领袖，也是全世界被压迫人民的领袖。”
