Title: Congolese Revolutionary Leader Meets with Red Guards in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-09-23
Edition: 4

On September 12, Aubert Ouandié, a member of the Central Committee and Political Bureau of the National Revolutionary Movement of Congo (Brazzaville), held a discussion with Red Guards in Beijing. The image shows Red Guards presenting a framed quotation of Chairman Mao to the guest.

Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter.
