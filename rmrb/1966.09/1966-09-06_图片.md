Title: Red Guards of Huai De County's Hong Wei Middle School Learn from the PLA
Author: Gao Wanpeng
Date: 1966-09-06
Edition: 2

The Red Guards of the Hong Wei Town's Private First Middle School in Huai De County, Jilin Province, enthusiastically respond to the Party's call to learn from the People's Liberation Army. The image shows the PLA, at the invitation of the Red Guards, explaining the Three Main Rules of Discipline and Eight Points for Attention.
