Title: Chairman Mao Ascends Tiananmen
Author: Li Zhixin
Date: 1966-09-08
Edition: 3

A red sun shines over the universe,
Chairman Mao ascends Tiananmen.
The red five-star badge,
Radiates brilliance, and rainbows fly.
Chairman Mao stands with us,
Warmth of happiness flows through our bodies.
Chairman Mao is our guide,
Your heart is connected with the masses,
Steering the revolution's course,
Leading us forward.
Thousands and thousands of cheers for you,
Long live! Long live! Ten thousand years!
Chairman Mao ascends Tiananmen,
Inspiring millions of revolutionaries,
Stand firm and spirited,
Hold the rifles tightly in hand,
Following the commander Chairman Mao,
Our hearts remain unchanged till the seas dry up and stones crumble!

### 毛主席登上天安门
李知心
1966-09-08
第3版()
专栏：

    毛主席登上天安门
    李知心
    一轮红日照乾坤，
    毛主席登上天安门。
    红色五星红领章，
    光芒万丈彩霞飞。
    毛主席同咱站一起，
    幸福暖流遍全身。
    毛主席是带路人，
    您与群众心连心，
    革命路上把舵掌，
    引导我们向前进。
    千声万声欢呼您，
    万岁！万岁！万万岁！
    毛主席登上天安门，
    鼓舞亿万革命人，
    立场坚定斗志昂，
    手中钢枪握得紧，
    跟着统帅毛主席，
    海枯石烂不变心！
