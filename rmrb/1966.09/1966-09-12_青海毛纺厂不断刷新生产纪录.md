Title: Qinghai Woolen Mill Continually Breaks Production Records
Author:
Date: 1966-09-12
Edition: 2

The workers of the Qinghai Woolen Mill have directed the revolutionary spirit unleashed during the Cultural Revolution into their production efforts. Since June, the entire mill has been thriving, continually breaking production records. The total output value from June to August increased by more than 24 percent compared to the same period last year. The quality has surpassed the national plan indicators, and costs have also been reduced. (Accompanying photo)

### 青海毛纺厂不断刷新生产纪录

1966-09-12
第2版()
专栏：

    青海毛纺厂不断刷新生产纪录
    据新华社西宁十一日电  青海毛纺织厂的职工把文化大革命中焕发出来的革命干劲用到生产上去。六月份以来，全厂生产欣欣向荣，生产纪录不断刷新。六至八月份总产值比去年同期提高了百分之二十四多，质量超过了国家计划指标，成本也有所降低。（附图片）
