Title: The Leader of the Congolese (Brazzaville) Economic Delegation Says at the Welcome Banquet That the Chinese Cultural Revolution Will Certainly Eliminate Bourgeois Thought
Author:
Date: 1966-09-29
Edition: 6

The leader of the Congolese (Brazzaville) economic delegation, Da Costa Claude Antoine, said at the welcome banquet hosted by the Minister of Foreign Economic Relations, Fang Yi, that the Chinese Cultural Revolution will certainly eliminate bourgeois thought and will also repel all attacks by imperialism.

Fang Yi spoke first at the banquet, praising the development of the fraternal friendship between the peoples of China and Congo (Brazzaville) and the economic and technological cooperation between the two countries since the establishment of diplomatic relations.

Da Costa Claude Antoine, in his speech, conveyed the Congolese (Brazzaville) people's fraternal greetings and friendship to the Chinese people. He congratulated the Chinese people on the victories achieved in the proletarian cultural revolution under the guidance of Mao Zedong Thought.

The Chargé d'Affaires of the Congolese (Brazzaville) Embassy in China, Jean Daniel Bakula, was also invited to attend the banquet.

### 刚果（布）经济代表团团长在欢迎宴会上说  中国文化大革命一定能消除资产阶级思想

1966-09-29
第6版()
专栏：

    刚果（布）经济代表团团长在欢迎宴会上说
    中国文化大革命一定能消除资产阶级思想
    据新华社二十八日讯  刚果（布）政府经济代表团团长达·科斯塔·克劳德安托万今晚在对外经委主任方毅举行的欢迎宴会上赞扬说，中国无产阶级文化大革命一定能消除资产阶级思想，同时能打退帝国主义的一切进攻。
    方毅在宴会上首先讲话，他赞扬了中国和刚果（布）建交以来，两国人民的战斗友谊和两国经济技术合作关系的发展。
    达·科斯塔·克劳德安托万团长在讲话中转达了刚果（布）人民对中国人民兄弟般的问候和友情。他祝贺中国人民在毛泽东思想照耀下开展的无产阶级文化大革命所取得的胜利。
    刚果（布）驻中国大使馆临时代办让·丹尼尔·巴库拉应邀出席了宴会。
