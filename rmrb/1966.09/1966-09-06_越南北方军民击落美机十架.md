Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Ten American Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-09-06
Edition: 6

The military and civilians of North Vietnam shot down ten American aircraft.

According to a report from here on the evening of the 5th, the military and civilians of South River Province shot down two invading American aggressor aircraft on the 5th.

According to additional information, the military and civilians of Qui Nhon Province shot down two American aggressor aircraft on September 3rd.

According to a report from Xinhua News Agency in Hanoi on the 4th, the military and civilians of Hanoi, under the great battle call of Comrade Ho Chi Minh, bravely fought continuously on the 4th, using fierce artillery fire to smash the enemy's multiple attacks, and shot down three American aggressor aircraft on the spot. It was announced that the military and civilians of Vinh Phuc, Bac Thai, and Ha Tinh provinces also shot down one American aggressor aircraft each on the 4th, and captured a batch of American pilots.

	Possessing the spirit of decisive battle and decisive victory, and skilled in close combat and night combat, soldiers of the South Vietnamese Liberation Army

		(Vietnam News Agency, Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 越南北方军民击落美机十架

1966-09-06
第6版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民击落美机十架
    新华社河内五日电　据这里五日晚上公布：越南北方南河省军民，五日把入侵的两架美国强盗飞机全部击落。
    另据补充消息，河静省军民九月三日曾击落美国强盗飞机两架。
    据新华社河内四日电　河内市军民在胡主席的伟大战斗号召鼓舞下，四日英勇地进行了连续战斗，以猛烈的炮火粉碎了敌机的多次进攻，当场击落美国强盗飞机三架。据公布，永福、北太、兴安三省的军民，四日也各击落美国强盗飞机一架，并活捉一批美国飞行员。
