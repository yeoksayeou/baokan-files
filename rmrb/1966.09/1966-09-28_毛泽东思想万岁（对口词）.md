Title: Long Live Mao Zedong Thought
Author: 广州部队海上文化工作队
Date: 1966-09-28
Edition: 6

The editor's words: To celebrate the 17th anniversary of the founding of our great People's Republic of China, amateur and professional art troupes have created many performances that sing the praises of our beloved leader Chairman Mao and extol the great, invincible Mao Zedong Thought. These performances express the boundless love and admiration of the people of China and the revolutionary people of the world for Chairman Mao and Mao Zedong Thought. Revolutionary art serves proletarian politics and serves the workers, peasants, and soldiers. To emphasize proletarian politics is to emphasize Mao Zedong Thought. Revolutionary art serving proletarian politics means singing the praises of Mao Zedong Thought. Revolutionary art serving the workers, peasants, and soldiers means singing the praises of our red sun, Chairman Mao, and singing the praises of our lifeblood, Mao Zedong Thought. Here we select a few performances created by amateur and professional art troupes. Let us sing together in praise of our great leader Chairman Mao and enthusiastically extol the great Mao Zedong Thought.

Long Live Mao Zedong Thought

East wind sweeps ten thousand miles,
Battle flags fluttering high.
Four seas in turmoil,
Five continents shaking.

The brilliant Mao Zedong Thought
Illuminates the world;
The great people's war
Terrifies the invading wolves.

Imperialists tremble in fear,
American bandits rave madly.
They think to threaten us with war
To frighten us—it's a dream;
They think to isolate us with slander
To make us lonely—it's a delusion.

We are full of spirit,
Our fighting will is high.
For the prosperity of our motherland,
For the liberation of mankind,
We dare to cross the sea of fire,
We dare to climb the mountain of knives.

Mao Zedong Thought
Is the mightiest weapon;
Mao Zedong Thought
Guides us to victory.

With it, we won the country;
With it, we defend our borders.
Look at the south bank of the Yangtze,
Look at the inside of the Great Wall,
Every household studies "Selected Works of Mao Zedong,"
Every place echoes with songs,
Soldiers and civilians stand shoulder to shoulder,
Busy with production and military training.

On Mount Jinggang, the Red Guards
Raise their arms and cheer:
The sons and daughters of China have great aspirations;
Under the Pagoda Mountain, the militia
Raise their guns and sing:
We love military attire, not red dresses.

In the green cornfields,
Revolutionary descendants dance with red plumes;
Under the coconut trees,
Sisters guard the coast by moonlight.

Liu Huilan's younger sister
Sharpened the steel knife in her hand;
The youth of Liu Village
Put on green military uniforms.

The old soldiers of the Hainan Island Guerrilla Force
Stand tall and proud,
Capturing American pilots alive.

The iron soldier Mai Xiande,
Severely wounded,
Fought bravely against the Kuomintang bandits.

The heroic People's Liberation Army of China
Holds high the red flag to guard the borders.
Cannons raised,
Bullets loaded,
Supporting our Vietnamese brothers,
Preparing to strike the evil American wolves.

Internationalism
Is our duty,
World revolution
Is our lofty ideal.
Our friends are all over the world,
Mao Zedong Thought shines in all directions.

Looking south,
The banks of the River of Virtue are ablaze with gunfire,
Vietnamese brothers and sisters win battles.
Rifles shoot down thieves,
Cannons bombard airfields.
Everywhere are traps,
Everywhere are stakes.
The Americans can hardly move,
The PLA fights stronger and stronger.

Looking east,
Songs of valor echo under Mount Fuji.
Japan wants independence,
Americans, go back!
Americans, go back!
The anti-American tide rises higher and higher.

Looking west,
The African jungle is ablaze,
Gunshots echo in the American valleys.
Breaking the chains,
Chests held high,
Wielding big knives,
Dancing with long spears,
Climbing high mountains,
Occupying villages,
Laying mines,
Blowing up bridges.
Scaring Johnson,
Crying for his father and mother!

Looking north,
Revisionists
Have the face of slaves.
Kneeling before the murderers,
Maliciously slandering revolutionaries!
Shameful traitors
Cannot escape the net,
The great people
Are filled with rage.
We will fight against imperialism and revisionism
To the end,
Mao Zedong
Guides us to victory!

With the villages of the world,
Surround the cities of the world,
With people's war,
Overthrow the old world,
With people's war,
Bury imperialism!

All proletarians of the world
Shout in unison,
All revolutionaries of the world
Sing aloud:
Mao Zedong
Is the Lenin of our time;
Mao Zedong
Is the hope of the world;
Mao Zedong
Is the banner of heroes;
Mao Zedong
Is the sun of mankind.

Armed with Mao Zedong Thought,
Invincible in a hundred battles,
Unconquerable;
Armed with Mao Zedong Thought,
Brave and strong,
Like iron and steel!

Fight, fight,
Defeat the American aggressors;
Advance, advance,
Welcome the liberation of mankind!

Long live! Long live!
The great people's war,
Long live! Long live!
The brilliant Mao Zedong Thought!

    编者的话：为了庆祝我们伟大的中华人民共和国成立十七周年，广大的业余文艺工作者和专业文艺团体，创作了许多歌唱我们敬爱的领袖毛主席、歌颂伟大的战无不胜的毛泽东思想的文艺节目。这些节目，表达了全国人民和全世界革命人民无限热爱毛主席，无限崇拜毛泽东思想的心情。革命的文艺是为无产阶级政治服务的，是为工农兵服务的，突出无产阶级政治，就是突出毛泽东思想。革命文艺为无产阶级政治服务，就要歌颂毛泽东思想。革命文艺要为工农兵服务，就要歌唱我们心中的红太阳毛主席，就要歌颂我们的命根子毛泽东思想。这里选登几个业余和专业文艺工作团体创作的节目。让我们同声歌唱伟大领袖毛主席，热情歌颂伟大的毛泽东思想。
