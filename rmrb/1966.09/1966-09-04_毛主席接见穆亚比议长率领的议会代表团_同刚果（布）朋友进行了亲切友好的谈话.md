Title: Chairman Mao Receives Delegation Led by Speaker Muabi
Author:
Date: 1966-09-04
Edition: 1

Chairman Mao today afternoon received the Speaker of the National Assembly of the People's Republic of the Congo, Muabi Andre George, and his wife, as well as members of the Congolese parliamentary delegation led by him. Chairman Mao had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Kang Sheng and his wife, and Vice Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Lian Guan.

The Charge d'Affaires of the Congolese Embassy in China, Jean Daniel Bakula, was also present.

### 毛主席接见穆亚比议长率领的议会代表团  同刚果（布）朋友进行了亲切友好的谈话

1966-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见穆亚比议长率领的议会代表团
    同刚果（布）朋友进行了亲切友好的谈话
    新华社三日讯　毛泽东主席今天下午接见刚果（布）国民议会议长穆亚比·安德烈·乔治和夫人，以及由他率领的刚果（布）议会代表团的成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有人大常委会副委员长康生和夫人，人大常委会副秘书长连贯。
    刚果（布）驻中国大使馆临时代办让·丹尼尔·巴库拉也在座。
