Title: The Nation Jubilantly Hails Chairman Mao's Everlasting Bond with the Revolutionary People
Author:
Date: 1966-09-03
Edition: 2

The nation jubilantly hails Chairman Mao's everlasting bond with the revolutionary people. Following Chairman Mao's teachings, we dare to revolutionize and are skilled at revolutionizing. We will vigorously carry out the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

According to a Xinhua News Agency report dated September 2nd, Chairman Mao ascended the Tiananmen Gate, and the sky was filled with red rays of dawn. The joyous news of our most beloved great leader receiving 500,000 Red Guards and revolutionary students and teachers in the capital spread to various places, and the whole nation was in a state of jubilation. People across the country enthusiastically hailed Chairman Mao's everlasting bond with the revolutionary people and expressed their firm resolve to respond to Chairman Mao's great call. They will take up the revolutionary iron broom and sweep away all the ghosts and monsters.

In the past two days, cities across the country have been filled with a festive atmosphere. Many Red Guards and revolutionary students and teachers who went to Beijing to be received by Chairman Mao and other central leaders have rushed back to their respective places to bring the revolutionary spark to the entire nation. People across the country have expressed their firm support for the speeches by Comrade Lin Biao and Comrade Zhou Enlai, believing that these two speeches have pointed out the direction for the current Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and that they will definitely follow them. People also expressed that they will definitely study Chairman Mao's works in the struggle, and earnestly, comprehensively, thoroughly, and unconditionally implement the Sixteen Points. They will definitely learn from the People's Liberation Army, dare to revolutionize, and be skilled at revolutionizing, letting the flames of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution burn even more fiercely across the country.

In the cities of Taiyuan, Baoding, and Hohhot in North China, the broad masses of Red Guards, revolutionary students and teachers, and workers and peasants were overjoyed and took to the streets to enthusiastically hail the great leader Chairman Mao's unlimited trust and greatest support for the revolutionary masses. Many Red Guards excitedly said: We have the invincible Mao Zedong Thought, the supreme commander Chairman Mao's leadership, our action guide the Sixteen Points, the billions of revolutionary masses as a strong backing, and the best learning example the People's Liberation Army. We have a heart that is forever loyal to the Party, the people, Chairman Mao, and Mao Zedong Thought. We are not afraid of the twists and turns of the struggle, not afraid of falling, and we are afraid of nothing!

The revolutionary masses in Harbin and Changchun in Northeast China were deeply moved by the news of Chairman Mao receiving revolutionary students and teachers from various places. After hearing the news, the Red Guards and revolutionary students in Harbin immediately held a celebratory parade. More than 3,000 revolutionary students and teachers from Harbin Institute of Technology shouted slogans such as "Long live Chairman Mao" and "Long live the Communist Party of China" as they marched onto the streets. They expressed their determination to study the Sixteen Points well, learn from the People's Liberation Army, and be the vanguard in the Cultural Revolution.

In the cities of Xining, Yinchuan, Lanzhou, and Xi'an in Northwest China, people of various ethnic groups were overjoyed and spread the news to each other after hearing that the great leader Chairman Mao had received Red Guards and revolutionary students and teachers from across the country. They shouted in various ethnic languages: "Long live the invincible Mao Zedong Thought!" "Long live the great leader Chairman Mao, who is most beloved by the people of all ethnic groups!" The broad masses of Red Guards, revolutionary students and teachers, and workers and peasants in Xi'an held meetings. Many young Red Guards said: This is a great joy for all of us Red Guards. With Chairman Mao supporting us, we are not afraid of anything. We will climb mountains of knives and walk through fires to root out all the ghosts and monsters.

In the cities of Nanchang, Jinan, Hefei, Hangzhou, and Fuzhou in East China, the atmosphere was also one of great excitement and the whole city was in a state of turmoil. On September 1st, tens of thousands of Red Guards, revolutionary students and teachers, and workers and peasants in Nanchang carried giant portraits of Chairman Mao wrapped in red silk, Chairman Mao's quotations, and a giant model of the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong," beating drums and gongs, and surging to the reception station of the local party committee to submit reports of joy and declarations of determination.

The joyous news reached the cities of Changsha, Zhengzhou, and Nanning in Central South China, and the broad masses of revolutionary students and teachers and revolutionary masses shouted "Long live Chairman Mao!" thousands of times. In Changsha, a Red Guard who had seen Chairman Mao at the grand rally of a million revolutionary masses in Beijing on August 18th said that Chairman Mao's reception of Red Guards and revolutionary students and teachers from across the country and Beijing had greatly boosted our morale.

In the cities of Guiyang, Kunming, and Lhasa in Southwest China, the revolutionary masses enthusiastically hailed Chairman Mao's reception of revolutionary students and teachers. On September 1st, 50,000 Red Guards and revolutionary students in Guiyang held a grand rally, enthusiastically hailing Chairman Mao's reception of 500,000 Red Guards and revolutionary students and teachers from across the country in the capital, and welcoming comrades from Beijing and other places who came to Guiyang for revolutionary connections. In Kunming, hundreds of propaganda teams composed of Red Guards, revolutionary students and teachers, and revolutionary workers took to the main streets of the city to carry out propaganda activities today. The broad masses of revolutionary students and teachers and revolutionary workers created many new songs and artistic performances praising the great leader Chairman Mao. More than 400 Red Guards and revolutionary students from Lhasa Middle School expressed their determination to Chairman Mao and the Party Central Committee, saying: Our great leader Chairman Mao is always by our side. We are not afraid of heaven or earth, not afraid of hardship or death, and we will definitely declare war on the old world.

### 全国人民热烈欢呼毛主席永远和革命人民心连心  按毛主席教导敢于革命善于革命  把文化大革命轰轰烈烈开展起来

1966-09-03
第2版()
专栏：

    全国人民热烈欢呼毛主席永远和革命人民心连心
    按毛主席教导敢于革命善于革命
    把文化大革命轰轰烈烈开展起来
    据新华社二日讯  毛主席登上天安门，朝霞万丈满天红，我们最敬爱的伟大领袖在首都接见五十万红卫兵和革命师生的喜讯传到各地，举国一片欢腾。各地人民热烈欢呼毛主席永远和革命人民心连心，纷纷表示坚决响应毛主席的伟大号召，奋起千钧棒，拿起革命的铁扫帚，把一切牛鬼蛇神扫得一干二净。
    这两天，全国各城市到处是一派欢腾景象。许多到北京受到毛主席和中央其他领导同志接见的红卫兵和革命师生星夜赶回各地，要把革命的火种带到全国。各地人民表示最坚决地拥护林彪同志和周恩来同志的讲话，认为这两个讲话为当前的无产阶级文化大革命指明了方向，一定坚决照办。人们还表示：一定要在斗争中活学活用毛主席著作，认真地、全面地、彻底地、不折不扣地贯彻执行十六条，一定要学习解放军，敢于革命，善于革命，让无产阶级文化大革命的烈火在全国各地更加猛烈地燃烧起来。
    在华北的太原、保定、呼和浩特等城市，广大红卫兵、革命师生和工农兵群众欢欣鼓舞，纷纷走上街头热烈欢呼伟大领袖毛主席对革命群众无限信任和最大支持。许多红卫兵激动地说：我们有最强大的战无不胜的毛泽东思想，有最高统帅毛主席的领导，有我们行动的指南十六条，有亿万革命群众作坚强后盾，有最好的学习榜样解放军，我们有一颗永远忠于党、忠于人民、忠于毛主席、忠于毛泽东思想的红心，我们不怕斗争的反复，不怕跌跤，什么都不怕！
    东北的哈尔滨和长春市的革命群众，被毛主席接见各地革命师生的喜讯所深深激动。哈尔滨的红卫兵和革命师生听到喜讯后，立即举行庆祝游行。哈尔滨工业大学的三千多名革命师生，高呼“毛主席万岁”、“中国共产党万岁”的口号，走上大街，他们表示一定要很好学习十六条，学习解放军，在文化大革命中当好急先锋。
    西北地区的西宁、银川、兰州、西安等城市的各族革命群众，听到伟大领袖毛主席接见全国各地红卫兵和革命师生的消息后，欢欣若狂，奔走相告，他们用各民族不同的语言高呼：“战无不胜的毛泽东思想万岁！”“各族人民最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席万岁！”西安市的广大红卫兵、革命师生和工农兵群众纷纷举行集会。许多红卫兵小将说：这是我们全体红卫兵的大喜事，有了毛主席给我们撑腰，我们就什么也不怕，上刀山，下火海，也要把一切牛鬼蛇神揪出来。
    华东的南昌、济南、合肥、杭州、福州等城市，也都是万众振奋，全城沸腾。南昌一日有数十万红卫兵、革命师生和工农兵群众，抬着围着红色彩绸的毛主席巨幅画像、毛主席语录牌和《毛泽东选集》的巨大模型，敲锣打鼓，涌到当地党委领导机关接待站递交喜报和决心书。
    喜讯传到中南地区的长沙、郑州、南宁等城市，广大革命师生和革命群众千遍万遍高呼：“毛主席万岁！”在长沙市，曾在八月十八日参加首都百万革命群众庆祝无产阶级文化大革命大会时见到毛主席的一个红卫兵说，毛主席再一次接见来自全国各地和首都的红卫兵和革命师生，大大地给我们撑了腰。
    西南地区的贵阳、昆明、拉萨等城市的革命群众，热烈欢呼毛主席接见革命师生。一日晚上，贵阳市有五万红卫兵和革命师生举行盛大集会，最热烈地欢呼毛主席在首都接见各地五十万红卫兵和革命师生，并欢迎首都和各地到贵阳进行革命串连的同学。在昆明，今天有成百个由红卫兵、革命师生和革命职工组成的宣传队，在市区的各条主要街道上展开宣传活动。广大革命师生和革命职工创作了许多歌颂伟大领袖毛主席的新歌曲和文艺节目。拉萨中学的四百多名红卫兵和革命师生向毛主席和党中央表示决心说：我们伟大的领袖毛主席时时刻刻都在我们身边。我们天不怕，地不怕，不怕苦，不怕死，坚决向旧世界宣战。
