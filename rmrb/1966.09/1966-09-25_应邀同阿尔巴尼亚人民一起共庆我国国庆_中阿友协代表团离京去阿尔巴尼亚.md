Title: Invited to Celebrate China's National Day with the Albanian People
Author:
Date: 1966-09-25
Edition: 5

A delegation from the China-Albania Friendship Association, led by Mu Lin, consisting of six members, departed from Beijing by plane today. They were invited to Albania to celebrate the 17th anniversary of the founding of our country together with the Albanian people and to conduct a friendly visit.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Song Yiping, Zhu Rong, Wang Lei, and other relevant officials.

Also present at the airport to see them off were Albanian Ambassador to China Vasili Nataneli and others.

### 应邀同阿尔巴尼亚人民一起共庆我国国庆  中阿友协代表团离京去阿尔巴尼亚

1966-09-25
第5版()
专栏：

    应邀同阿尔巴尼亚人民一起共庆我国国庆
    中阿友协代表团离京去阿尔巴尼亚
    新华社二十三日讯　由团长穆林率领的中国阿尔巴尼亚友好协会代表团一行六人，今天乘飞机离开北京，应邀前往阿尔巴尼亚，同阿尔巴尼亚人民一起共同庆祝我国成立十七周年，并进行友好访问。
    到机场送行的有宋一平、朱荣、王磊等有关方面负责人。
    到机场送行的，还有阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使瓦西里·纳塔奈利等。
