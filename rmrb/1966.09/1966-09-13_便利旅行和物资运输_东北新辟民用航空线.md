Title: Convenient Travel and Cargo Transportation: New Civil Aviation Routes in Northeast China
Author:
Date: 1966-09-13
Edition: 2

To strengthen the aviation transportation industry in Northeast China, the Civil Aviation Administration of China opened a new civil aviation route from Shenyang to Changchun, Harbin, and Jiamusi on September 12. The schedule for this route is as follows: flights depart from Shenyang to Jiamusi every Monday and Thursday, and return flights from Jiamusi to Shenyang every Tuesday and Friday. The opening of this route not only facilitates the needs of people for air travel, promotes the exchange of materials in Northeast China, but also strengthens the connection between Northeast China and the rest of the country.

### 便利旅行和物资运输  东北新辟民用航空线

1966-09-13
第2版()
专栏：

    便利旅行和物资运输
    东北新辟民用航空线
    新华社沈阳十二日电　为了加强我国东北地区的航空运输事业，中国民用航空总局已于九月十二日开辟了沈阳—长春—哈尔滨—佳木斯民用航空线。这条航线的班期是：每星期一、四由沈阳飞往佳木斯，星期二、五由佳木斯飞回沈阳。这条航线的开辟，不仅便利了人民航空旅行的需要，促进东北地区的物资交流，而且密切了东北地区和内地的联系。
