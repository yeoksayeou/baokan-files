Title: Mao Zedong Thought Shines Brightly
Author: Jiang Zongyang
Date: 1966-09-04
Edition: 6

Soldier of a Certain Unit of the People's Liberation Army

Jiang Zongyang

A: What is the most precious thing in the world?
B: It is the brilliant works of Chairman Mao.
A: What is the most radiant thing in the world?
B: It is the great Mao Zedong Thought.
A: Mao Zedong Thought, the sun of truth.
B: Illuminating the five continents, shining over the four seas.
A: We, the People's Liberation Army,
B: Are armed with Mao Zedong Thought.
A: From small to large, from weak to strong,
B: Invincible in battle, tempered into steel,
A: We defeated the ferocious Japanese devils,
B: And annihilated the running dogs of American imperialism, the Kuomintang bandits.
A: Our great seven hundred million people,
B: Are armed with Mao Zedong Thought.
A: Self-reliance,
B: Striving for strength,
A: Overcoming countless difficulties,
B: Withstanding the surging waves,
A: Creating miracles on earth,
B: The ten thousand li of rivers and mountains are transformed!
A: Look! The red flags of Daqing are flying everywhere,
B: Look! The red flowers of Dazhai are blooming all over.
A: Look! Revolutionary heroes are singing and marching forward,
B: In a mighty surge!
Both: Towards the front!
A: All the revolutionary people of the world,
B: Are armed with Mao Zedong Thought.
A: The masses are awakening,
B: United in seeking liberation.
A: Fires of revolution are burning across Asia, Africa, and Latin America,
B: A mighty anti-American wave is sweeping the world,
Both: The east wind prevails, the west wind declines,
The oppressed peoples will achieve thorough liberation!
A: Mao Zedong Thought,
B: The sun of truth,
A: Scaring away all the ghosts and snakes,
B: Leaving them no place to hide.
A: The class enemies at home and abroad,
B: Shoot poisoned arrows in their dreams, aiming at the sun.
A: Pfft! Daydreaming!
B: Pfft! Foolish wishes!
A: The brilliance of truth cannot be obscured,
B: Who can block the sun's rays!
A: We revolutionary warriors,
B: Will defend Mao Zedong Thought to the death!
A: Meet swords with swords,
B: Meet pens with pens,
A: Annihilate all armed enemies,
B: And wipe out all unarmed enemies!
Both: Let the great red flag of Mao Zedong Thought,
Fly forever, for all eternity!

### 毛泽东思想放光芒（对口词）
姜宗扬
1966-09-04
第6版()
专栏：

    毛泽东思想放光芒（对口词）
    解放军某部战士  姜宗扬甲：世界上什么最宝贵？乙：是光辉的毛主席著作。甲：世界上什么最闪光？乙：是伟大的毛泽东思想。甲：毛泽东思想，真理的太阳。乙：照耀五大洲，四海放光芒。甲：我们人民解放军，乙：靠毛泽东思想来武装。甲：由小到大，从弱到强，乙：战无不胜，百炼成钢，甲：打败了穷凶极恶的日本鬼，乙：消灭了美帝走狗蒋匪帮。甲：我们伟大的七亿人民，乙：靠毛泽东思想来武装。甲：自力更生，乙：奋发图强，甲：战胜了重重困难，乙：顶住了惊涛骇浪，甲：创造了人间奇迹，乙：万里江山换新装！甲：看！大庆的红旗到处飘扬，乙：看！大寨的红花遍地开放。甲：看！革命英雄高歌迈阔步，乙：浩浩荡荡！合：奔向前方！甲：全世界的革命人民，乙：用毛泽东思想武装。甲：群众在觉醒，乙：齐心求解放。甲：亚非拉烽火遍地燃烧，乙：全世界掀起反美巨浪，合：东风浩荡西风衰，
  被压迫人民要得到彻底解放！甲：毛泽东思想，乙：真理的太阳，甲：吓得牛鬼蛇神，乙：无处去躲藏。甲：国内外的阶级敌人，乙：手放毒箭梦想射太阳。甲：呸！白日做梦！乙：呸！痴心妄想！甲：真理的光辉遮不住，乙：太阳的光芒谁能挡！甲：我们革命的战士，乙：誓死捍卫毛泽东思想！甲：刀来刀迎，乙：笔来笔挡，甲：消灭一切拿枪的敌人，乙：也把不拿枪的敌人消灭光！合：让毛泽东思想的伟大红旗，
  千秋万代，永远飘扬！
