Title: Nanchang Rubber Factory Achieves Leap in Both Production and Quality
Author:
Date: 1966-09-14
Edition: 2

In the midst of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, the workers of the Nanchang Rubber Factory have been diligently studying the works of Chairman Mao, using Mao Zedong Thought as the guiding principle for all their work. While actively participating in the cultural revolution, they have also been striving to increase production, ensuring the full completion of the production plan. From January to August this year, the total output value of the factory increased by 14% compared to the same period last year; the production of the main product, tires, increased by 46%; the quality of the products has significantly improved; and the number of new products introduced this year is the highest in recent years.

### 南昌橡胶厂产量质量双跃进  今年增加的新品种超过往年

1966-09-14
第2版()
专栏：

    南昌橡胶厂产量质量双跃进
    今年增加的新品种超过往年
    据新华社南昌十三日电　在无产阶级文化大革命中，南昌橡胶厂的职工努力学习毛主席著作，把毛主席的书当作一切工作的指导方针。他们一面积极投入文化大革命的斗争，一面努力生产，确保生产计划全面完成。全厂一月到八月份的总产值比去年同期增长百分之十四；主要产品轮胎产量比去年同期增长百分之四十六；产品质量显著提高；增加的新品种是近几年来最多的一年。
