Title: Anhui Achieves Bumper Fruit Harvest
Author:
Date: 1966-09-29
Edition: 3

Anhui Province has achieved a rare bumper harvest of fruits this year. The Huizhou snow pears, Dangshan pears, and apples all saw an increase in production of more than one hundred percent compared to last year. Grape production increased by thirty-five percent. Currently, these fruits are being transported in large quantities to various locations to meet the needs of urban and rural populations.

### 安徽水果获得好收成

1966-09-29
第3版()
专栏：

    安徽水果获得好收成
    安徽省今年水果获得近年来少有的好收成。徽州雪梨、砀山梨和苹果，都比去年增产一倍以上。葡萄增产百分之三十五，现在，这些水果正源源运往各地，供应城乡人民需要。　（据新华社讯）
