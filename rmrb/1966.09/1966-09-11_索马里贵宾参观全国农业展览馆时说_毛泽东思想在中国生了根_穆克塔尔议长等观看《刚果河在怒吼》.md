Title: Somali Guests Visit the National Agricultural Exhibition Hall and Say Mao Zedong Thought Has Taken Root in China
Author:
Date: 1966-09-11
Edition: 5

	Somali Guests Visit the National Agricultural Exhibition Hall and Say Mao Zedong Thought Has Taken Root in China
	Muqtar Speaker and Others Watch "The Congo River Roars"

	Xinhua News Agency, September 10th: Chairman of the Somali National Assembly Sheikh Muqtar and members of the Somali National Assembly Friendship Delegation, led by him, visited the National Agricultural Exhibition Hall today. They enthusiastically praised the Chinese peasants, under the guidance of Mao Zedong Thought, for their self-reliance and hard work, and for achieving great developments in agriculture.

	Sheikh Muqtar and other Somali friends showed great interest in visiting the exhibition hall's Dazhai-style agricultural typical exhibition and the Water Conservancy Hall. Sheikh Muqtar wrote in the inscription for the exhibition hall: "The achievements of Chinese peasants in overcoming poverty and improving the land have amazed me. I wish the Chinese peasants, with their current determination, to continue moving forward to realize a brilliant future and a better life for the next generation." He also expressed his gratitude to the Chinese government for sending agricultural technicians to help the Somali people.

	Team member Mustafa shared his thoughts, saying, "What impresses me is that Mao Zedong Thought has taken root in many places, enabling the Chinese people to achieve great successes in all aspects, and agriculture has also developed greatly. The Chinese people have truly applied Mao Zedong Thought to practical struggles!" He also wished for the continuous development of friendship between China and Somalia and long life for Chairman Mao.

	Deputy Chairman Yang Mingxuan expressed heartfelt thanks to the Somali friends for their friendly sentiments.

	Sheikh Muqtar and other Somali friends, accompanied by Deputy Chairman Liu Ningyi, visited the Beijing Wire Factory this afternoon and were warmly welcomed by the workers.

	Xinhua News Agency, September 10th: Chairman of the Somali National Assembly Sheikh Muqtar and the Somali National Assembly Friendship Delegation, led by him, were invited to attend an evening performance and watched the ballet "The Congo River Roars."

	Chairman Zhu De accompanied the Somali guests to watch the performance.

	The Somali friends applauded enthusiastically as they watched this ballet, which reflects the heroic struggle of the Congolese people against American imperialist aggression, fighting for national independence, freedom, and liberation. The performance was met with enthusiastic applause.

	After the performance, Chairman Zhu De and others accompanied Sheikh Muqtar and others onto the stage to shake hands with the performers and offer congratulations.

	This evening performance was held by the China-Africa Friendship Association to welcome the Somali National Assembly Friendship Delegation. Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo, Yang Mingxuan, and Vice Chairman of the China-Africa Friendship Association Nan Hanchen, among others, also accompanied the Somali guests to watch the performance. Somali experts and students studying in Beijing were also invited to attend the evening performance.

### 索马里贵宾参观全国农业展览馆时说  毛泽东思想在中国生了根  穆克塔尔议长等观看《刚果河在怒吼》

1966-09-11
第5版()
专栏：

    索马里贵宾参观全国农业展览馆时说
    毛泽东思想在中国生了根
    穆克塔尔议长等观看《刚果河在怒吼》
    新华社十日讯　索马里国民议会议长谢赫·穆克塔尔和由他率领的索马里国民议会友好代表团的成员，今天在参观全国农业展览馆时，热烈赞扬中国农民在毛泽东思想指引下，发扬自力更生、艰苦奋斗的精神，取得了农业的大发展。
    谢赫·穆克塔尔议长等索马里朋友以极大的兴趣，参观了展览馆的大寨式农业典型展览和水利馆。谢赫·穆克塔尔议长在为展览馆的题词中写道：“中国农民在战胜贫困和改良土地方面所取得的成绩，使我感到惊奇。我祝愿中国农民为实现光辉的未来和下一代的美好生活，按照他们现在的决心继续前进。”他还表示感谢中国政府派农业技术人员去帮助索马里人民。
    代表团团员穆斯塔法在发表自己的感想时说，“使我感到钦佩的是，毛泽东思想在很多地方生了根，使得中国人民在各方面都获得了很大成就，农业也有很大的发展。中国人民真正把毛泽东思想运用到实际斗争中去了！”他还祝中国和索马里友谊不断发展，祝愿毛主席长寿。
    陪同参观的杨明轩副委员长对索马里朋友表达的友好感情，表示衷心的感谢。
    谢赫·穆克塔尔议长和其他索马里朋友，今天下午由刘宁一副委员长陪同，参观了北京钢丝厂，受到职工的热烈欢迎。
    新华社十日讯　谢赫·穆克塔尔议长和由他率领的索马里国民议会友好代表团，今天应邀出席晚会，观看了舞剧《刚果河在怒吼》。
    朱德委员长陪同索马里客人观看了演出。
    索马里朋友们在观看这个反映英雄的刚果人民，为反对美帝国主义侵略，争取民族独立、自由和解放而进行英勇斗争的舞剧时，对演员们的精彩表演，报以一阵阵热烈的掌声。
    演出结束后，朱德委员长等陪同谢赫·穆克塔尔议长等登上舞台，同演员们亲切握手，表示祝贺。
    这个晚会是中非友协为欢迎索马里国民议会友好代表团而举行的。人大常委会副委员长郭沫若、杨明轩，中非友协副会长南汉宸等，也陪同索马里客人观看演出。在北京的索马里专家和留学生也应邀出席了晚会。
