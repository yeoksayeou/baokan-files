Title: Delegation of the Japan-China Friendship Association Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-09-30
Edition: 4

A delegation from the Japan-China Friendship Association, led by the association's vice-chairman Kuroda Juzō and chairman Miyazaki Seiken, arrived in Beijing by plane tonight. They were invited to participate in the National Day celebrations.

Liao Chengzhi and other relevant officials, along with over 400 Red Guards from the capital, warmly welcomed them at the airport. The Japanese peace activist Nishisonoji Kōichi, who is currently in Beijing, also went to the airport to greet them.

### 日中友协代表团到京

1966-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

    日中友协代表团到京
    新华社二十九日讯　由日中友协副会长黑田寿男和日中友协理事长宫崎世民率领的日中友协代表团，应邀前来我国参加国庆活动，今天晚上乘飞机到达北京。
    廖承志等有关方面负责人和首都四百多名红卫兵到机场热烈欢迎。在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也到机场迎接。
