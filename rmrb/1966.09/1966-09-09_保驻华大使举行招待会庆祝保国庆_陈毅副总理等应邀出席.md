Title: Bulgarian Ambassador Hosts Reception to Celebrate Bulgarian National Day
Author:
Date: 1966-09-09
Edition: 4

Bulgarian Ambassador to China Stoichev held a reception this afternoon to celebrate the 22nd anniversary of the Bulgarian Socialist Revolution.

Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Yang Mingxuan, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Gao Chongmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qiao Guanhua, and other relevant officials attended the reception.

Diplomats from various countries' embassies in China also attended the reception.

### 保驻华大使举行招待会庆祝保国庆  陈毅副总理等应邀出席

1966-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

    保驻华大使举行招待会庆祝保国庆
    陈毅副总理等应邀出席
    新华社八日讯　保加利亚驻中国大使斯托伊切夫今天下午举行招待会，庆祝保加利亚社会主义革命二十二周年。
    国务院副总理陈毅，人大常委会副委员长杨明轩，政协副主席高崇民，外交部副部长乔冠华，以及有关方面负责人出席了招待会。
    各国驻中国大使馆官员也出席了招待会。
