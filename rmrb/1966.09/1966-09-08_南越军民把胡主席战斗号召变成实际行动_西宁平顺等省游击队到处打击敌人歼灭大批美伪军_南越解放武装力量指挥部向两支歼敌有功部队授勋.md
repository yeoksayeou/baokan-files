Title: South Vietnamese Military and Civilians Turn Chairman Hu's Battle Call into Action
Author:
Date: 1966-09-08
Edition: 5

South Vietnamese military and civilians have turned Chairman Hu's great battle call into action. Guerrilla forces from provinces such as Xining, Phu Tho, Phu Yen, Sa Dec, and Chu Duc have been striking the enemy everywhere and annihilating large numbers of American and puppet troops.

The Xining Provincial Guerrilla Force in the county of Tra Vinh, from August 29 to September 1 over four days, consecutively smashed a sweep by more than a battalion of American invaders, annihilating nearly 150 American soldiers, shooting down and blowing up five enemy planes, and heavily damaging nine American tanks and armored vehicles.

The local forces and guerrilla units in the county of Le Hong Phong, Phu Tho Province, on August 16, fought a brilliant ambush battle on a highway, annihilating an entire company and platoon of puppet troops in just 40 minutes, seizing a large amount of weapons and military supplies.

The local forces and guerrilla units in Phu Yen Province, on August 20 and 21, consecutively attacked enemy strongholds, annihilating two platoons of puppet troops and seizing more than 30 weapons.

The local forces and guerrilla units in Sa Dec Province, in three attacks in August, annihilated more than 130 enemy troops and sank three enemy ships.

The guerrilla units in the county of Hong Nghe, Chu Duc Province, on the night of August 23, launched a fierce attack on the An Long stronghold, destroying six enemy barracks, blowing up two military vehicles, and annihilating a batch of enemy troops.

According to the Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 7, the South Vietnamese Liberation Forces Command recently decided to award the Third-Class Liberation Military Merit Medal to two liberation army units that have performed outstanding actions.

The first unit, on August 17 and 18, annihilated more than 700 Australian mercenary troops in the areas of Thua Tien and Long Xuyen in Ba Ria Province.

The other unit, on August 25, in the areas of Binh Ba and Loc Ninh in Tuy Hoa Province, in close cooperation with local guerrilla units, annihilated nearly a battalion of American troops.

	South Vietnamese Liberation Army soldiers are training hard, ready to annihilate American invaders at any time.

### 南越军民把胡主席战斗号召变成实际行动  西宁平顺等省游击队到处打击敌人歼灭大批美伪军  南越解放武装力量指挥部向两支歼敌有功部队授勋

1966-09-08
第5版()
专栏：

    南越军民把胡主席战斗号召变成实际行动
    西宁平顺等省游击队到处打击敌人歼灭大批美伪军
    南越解放武装力量指挥部向两支歼敌有功部队授勋
    新华社河内六日电  据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方西宁、平顺、平定、沙沥、朱笃等省解放武装力量，坚决把胡主席的伟大战斗号召变成实际行动，到处打击敌人，最近又歼灭大批美伪军。
    西宁省盏盆县游击队，从八月二十九日到九月一日的四天中，接连粉碎一营多美国侵略军的“扫荡”，歼灭美军近一百五十名，击落和炸毁敌机五架，重创美国坦克和装甲车九辆。
    平顺省黎鸿峰县地方部队和游击队，八月十六日在一条公路上打了一场漂亮的伏击战，四十分钟全歼伪军一个连和一个排，缴获大批武器和军用物资。
    平定省地方部队和游击队在八月二十日和二十一日两天中，连续袭击敌人的据点，全歼伪军两个排，缴获武器三十多件。
    沙沥省地方部队和游击队，在八月份的三次袭击战中，共消灭敌人一百三十多名，击沉敌船三艘。
    朱笃省洪御县游击队，八月二十三日深夜猛烈袭击安隆据点，一举摧毁敌人兵营六座，击毁军车两辆，歼灭敌军一批。
    据新华社河内七日电据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方广南省北部奠盘和维川两县游击队，从八月一日到十九日接连出击，共歼灭美国侵略军一百多名，击落敌人直升飞机一架，击毁和击伤军车二辆。
    新华社河内七日电据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方解放武装力量指挥部，最近决定把三级解放军功勋章分别授予作战有功的两支解放军部队。
    第一支部队曾在八月十七日和十八日，在巴地省土山、隆新两地歼灭澳大利亚帮凶军七百多名。
    另一支部队在八月二十五日在土龙木省蓬庄、罗盖两地，同当地游击队密切配合，歼灭美军近一个营。
