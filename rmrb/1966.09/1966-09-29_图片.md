Title: A Grand Banquet Held to Welcome Overseas Chinese and Compatriots from Hong Kong and Macao
Author:
Date: 1966-09-29
Edition: 6

On September 28, the Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, Xia Chengzhi, held a grand banquet to warmly welcome overseas Chinese and compatriots from Hong Kong and Macao who returned to participate in the celebration of the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. Comrades Tao Zhu, Chen Yi, Ye Jianying, and Xia Chengzhi toasted the overseas Chinese and compatriots from Hong Kong and Macao attending the banquet.

	New China News Agency photographer
