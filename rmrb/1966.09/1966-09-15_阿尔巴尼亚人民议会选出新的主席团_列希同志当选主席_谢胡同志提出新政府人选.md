Title: Albanian People's Assembly Elects New Presidium, Lleshi Elected Chairman, Shehu Proposes New Government Lineup
Author:
Date: 1966-09-15
Edition: 5

The sixth session of the Albanian People's Assembly held its first meeting on September 13th and unanimously approved the new members of the Presidium of the People's Assembly.

The newly elected Presidium is as follows: Chairman H. Lleshi; Vice-Chairmen G. Nushi, R. Marko, and M. Peza; Secretary B. Klosi; Members Enver Hoxha, E. Barhani, H. Cako, K. Ili, M. Fino, P. Piristri, S. Mojsiu, T. Jakova, V. Kaba, and Ch. Spasiu.

Following this, Chairman of the Council of Ministers Shehu delivered an important report to the assembly. In his report, Shehu accepted the assembly's mandate and, based on the opinions of the Party Central Committee, proposed the following lineup for the new government: Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Defense B. Balluku; Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers H. Toska and A. Carçani; Minister of Foreign Affairs N. Nasi; Minister of Internal Affairs K. Haxhiu; Minister of Industry and Mines K. Theodhori; Minister of Agriculture P. Dodbi; Minister of Education and Culture Sh. Dhejana; Minister of Construction H. Dragoti; Minister of Trade Gj. Ngjela; Minister of Communications M. Kirko; Minister of Finance A. Verli; Minister of Health Q. Pishtari; Chairman of the State Planning Commission S. Kolega; Minister without Portfolio Sh. Peçi.

Shehu also suggested in his report the abolition of the Ministry of Justice. After Shehu's report, representatives discussed the report.

On the 12th and 13th, the assembly, after discussions, approved the newly revised Labor Law and Social Insurance Law. The meeting will continue on the morning of the 14th.

### 阿尔巴尼亚人民议会选出新的主席团  列希同志当选主席　谢胡同志提出新政府人选

1966-09-15
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚人民议会选出新的主席团
    列希同志当选主席　谢胡同志提出新政府人选
    新华社地拉那十四日电　阿尔巴尼亚第六届人民议会第一次会议，十三日全体一致通过了人民议会新的主席团的人选。
    新选出的主席团是：主席哈·列希；副主席果·努什、里·马尔科和穆·佩扎；秘书比·克洛西；委员恩维尔·霍查、埃·巴尔哈尼、希·恰科、卡·伊利、米·弗加、皮·皮里斯特里、斯·莫伊修、托·雅科瓦、维·卡博和查·斯巴修。
    接着，部长会议主席谢胡在会上作了重要报告。谢胡在报告中接受大会的委托，并根据劳动党中央的意见，提出了新政府的人选：部长会议副主席兼国防部长贝·巴卢库；部长会议副主席哈·托斯卡和阿·查尔查尼；外交部长奈·纳赛；内务部长卡·哈兹比乌；工业和矿产部长科·蒂奥多西；农业部长皮·多德比巴；教育和文化部长绍·德雅纳；建筑部长希·德拉戈蒂；贸易部长乔·恩杰拉；交通部长米·基尔科；财政部长阿·维尔利；卫生部长齐·皮斯特里；国家计划委员会主席斯·科列加；不管部长谢·佩奇。
    谢胡在报告中还建议取消司法部。谢胡报告后，代表们对报告展开了讨论。
    十二日和十三日会议经过讨论，分别通过了新修改的劳动法和社会保险法。会议将在十四日上午继续举行。
