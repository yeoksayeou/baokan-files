Title: Somali Parliamentary Friendship Delegation Arrives in Shanghai at the Invitation of Chairman Ju De
Author:
Date: 1966-09-08
Edition: 4

At the invitation of Chairman Ju De of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the Chairman of the National Assembly of the Somali Republic, Sheikh Mukhtar Muhammad Hussein, and the Somali National Assembly Friendship Delegation he leads, arrived in Shanghai by plane this evening to conduct a friendly visit to our country.

The flags of China and Somalia were flying at the airport. Vice Mayor Shi Ying of Shanghai was at the airport to warmly welcome the Somali guests. Young people presented flowers to the guests.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation also included Lian Guan, Vice Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, who came specially from Beijing to Shanghai to welcome the guests, as well as responsible officials from various relevant departments in Shanghai.

	Mogadishu, Sept. 7 (Xinhua) - The Chairman of the National Assembly of the Somali Republic, Sheikh Mukhtar Muhammad Hussein, and the National Assembly Friendship Delegation he leads, at the invitation of Chairman Ju De of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China, left Mogadishu on September 1 to come to China for a friendly visit.

	Those who went to the airport to see off the delegation included Somali Prime Minister Abdirizak Haji Hussein and Vice Chairman of the National Assembly and others.

	Chinese Ambassador to Somalia Yang Shouzheng also went to the airport to see off the delegation.

	Before the delegation's departure, Ambassador Yang Shouzheng held a reception on the evening of August 29 to see off the delegation.

### 应朱德委员长邀请来我国访问  索马里议会友好代表团到上海

1966-09-08
第4版()
专栏：

    应朱德委员长邀请来我国访问
    索马里议会友好代表团到上海
    新华社上海七日电　应全国人民代表大会常务委员会朱德委员长的邀请，前来我国进行友好访问的索马里共和国国民议会议长谢赫·穆克塔尔·穆罕默德·侯赛因，以及由他率领的索马里国民议会友好代表团，今天晚上乘飞机到达上海。
    机场上飘扬着中国、索马里两国国旗。上海市副市长石英到机场热烈欢迎索马里贵宾。青年们向贵宾献了花。
    到机场欢迎的，还有专程由北京来上海迎接贵宾的人大常委会副秘书长连贯，以及上海各有关部门的负责人。
    新华社七日讯　摩加迪沙消息：索马里共和国国民议会议长谢赫·穆克塔尔·穆罕默德·侯赛因和他率领的国民议会友好代表团，应我国人大常委会委员长朱德的邀请，在九月一日离开摩加迪沙前来中国进行友好访问。
    到机场欢送代表团的有索马里总理阿卜迪里扎克·哈吉·侯赛因和国民议会副议长等。
    中国驻索马里大使杨守正也到机场欢送。
    代表团出发前，杨守正大使曾在八月二十九日晚上举行招待会欢送。
