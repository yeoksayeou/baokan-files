Title: Signing of the 1966 Implementation Plan for the Sino-Mongolian Cultural Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1966-09-30
Edition: 4

The 1966 Implementation Plan for the Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the People's Republic of China and the Mongolian People's Republic was signed this morning in Beijing.

The Vice Chairman of the State Council's Cultural Group, Shi Huaipei, and the Mongolian Ambassador to China, Dun Zhebengmi, signed the agreement.

### 中蒙文化合作协定今年执行计划签字

1966-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

    中蒙文化合作协定今年执行计划签字
    新华社二十九日讯　中华人民共和国和蒙古人民共和国文化合作协定一九六六年执行计划今天上午在北京签字。
    对外文委副主任史怀璧和蒙古驻中国大使敦·策伯格米德分别在协定上签了字。
