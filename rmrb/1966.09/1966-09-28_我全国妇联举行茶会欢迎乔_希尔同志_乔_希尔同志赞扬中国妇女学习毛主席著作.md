Title: The All-China Women's Federation Holds a Tea Party to Welcome Comrade Joe Hill
Author:
Date: 1966-09-28
Edition: 4

The All-China Women's Federation held a tea party to welcome Comrade Joe Hill. Comrade Joe Hill praised Chinese women for studying Chairman Mao's works.

	NEW CHINA NEWS AGENCY, 27th September — Comrade Joe Hill, Chairman of the Communist Party of Australia (Marxist-Leninists), and his wife, Comrade Joe Hill, attended a welcoming tea party held by the All-China Women's Federation this morning. At the tea party, Comrade Joe Hill said, "Everywhere I go in China, I see women studying Chairman Mao's works. There are tens of thousands of examples of women actively applying Chairman Mao's teachings."

	At the tea party, Vice-Chairman Kang Keqing of the All-China Women's Federation, and First Secretary Luo Qiong and Secretary Yang Yuyu of the Secretariat, enthusiastically introduced the situation of the broad masses of working women in our country actively studying and applying Chairman Mao's works. When Kang Keqing and others talked about how women in our country, regardless of age, have unlimited love, admiration, and faith in Chairman Mao, and how many illiterate people are also striving to master Mao Zedong Thought, Comrade Joe Hill repeatedly expressed her enthusiastic praise.

	Comrade Joe Hill also shared her recent visit to Yan'an at the tea party. She said that during her two-day visit to Yan'an, she was greatly inspired and encouraged. She saw the great power of Mao Zedong Thought in Yan'an and other places.

	At the tea party, the leaders of the All-China Women's Federation presented the guest with a replica of Chairman Mao's recent calligraphy for the magazine "Chinese Women," which reads "Chinese Women," as well as the newly published "Chinese Women" magazine and a set of photographs of active women learners of Chairman Mao's works in China.

### 我全国妇联举行茶会欢迎乔·希尔同志  乔·希尔同志赞扬中国妇女学习毛主席著作

1966-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

    我全国妇联举行茶会欢迎乔·希尔同志
    乔·希尔同志赞扬中国妇女学习毛主席著作
    新华社二十七日讯  澳大利亚共产党（马克思列宁主义者）主席希尔同志的夫人乔·希尔同志，今天上午出席了全国妇联为她举行的欢迎茶会。乔·希尔同志在茶会上说：“我在中国到处都看到妇女们在学习毛主席著作，出现了成千上万的活学活用毛主席著作的典型。”
    在茶会上，全国妇联副主席康克清和书记处第一书记罗琼、书记杨蕴玉，热情地向乔·希尔介绍了我国广大劳动妇女活学活用毛主席著作的情况。当康克清等谈到我国广大劳动妇女无论老幼都无限热爱毛主席，无限崇拜、无限信仰毛泽东思想，许多不识字的人也在努力掌握毛泽东思想时，乔·希尔同志不时表示热情的称赞。
    乔·希尔同志还在茶会上谈了她最近访问延安的感想。她说，她在延安的两天参观访问中受到了很大的启发和鼓舞。她在延安和其它地方的参观，都看到了毛泽东思想的巨大威力。
    茶会上，全国妇联的负责人向客人赠送了毛主席最近为《中国妇女》杂志所题的“中国妇女”四个字的复制品，以及新出版的《中国妇女》杂志和中国劳动妇女学习毛主席著作积极分子的一套照片。
