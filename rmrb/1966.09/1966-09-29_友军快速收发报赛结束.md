Title: Friendly Forces Rapid Radio Transmission Contest Concludes
Author:
Date: 1966-09-29
Edition: 6

The friendly forces radio transmission contest concluded today after six days of intense and fierce competition. A total of 19 athletes from China, Vietnam, and North Korea broke 15 international records, with one athlete tying one international record.

### 友军快速收发报赛结束

1966-09-29
第6版()
专栏：

    友军快速收发报赛结束
    据新华社二十七日讯　友军无线电快速收发报友谊赛经过六天紧张激烈的比赛，今天胜利结束。中国、越南、朝鲜三国运动员共有十九人打破十五项国际纪录，一人平一项国际纪录。
