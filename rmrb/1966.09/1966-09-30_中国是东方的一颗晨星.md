Title: China is a Morning Star in the East
Author: Bùluónǚ
Date: 1966-09-30
Edition: 5

China is a morning star in the East. In the entire world, China is a great banner flying high and waving in the wind. It represents a path that we should follow. It is like a morning star that appears in the East, guiding us in the right direction.

I am opposed to the program of the Khrushchev revisionists. We support your program because it advocates for all the exploited to rise up against the exploiters. It is not just me who agrees with your views, but the vast majority of people.

### 中国是东方的一颗晨星
布鲁诺
1966-09-30
第5版()
专栏：国外来信

    中国是东方的一颗晨星
    在全世界，中国是一面高高升起、迎风飘扬的伟大旗帜。她代表着一条我们应该遵循的道路。她像出现在东方的一颗晨星，给我们指引着方向。
    赫鲁晓夫修正主义者的纲领，我是反对的。我们赞同你们的纲领，因为它支持所有的被剥削者反对剥削者。同意你们意见的不止我一个人，而是绝大部分人。
                        〔意大利〕布鲁诺
