Title: Chairman of the Australian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninists) and His Wife Arrive in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1966-09-11
Edition: 5

On September 10, Chairman of the Australian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninists) Hill (fourth from the left) and his wife (third from the left) arrived in Guangzhou. The photo shows them posing for a group picture at the station. Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter (telephoto).
