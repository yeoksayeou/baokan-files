Title: Celebrating the Sixth Anniversary of the Sino-Guinea Friendship Treaty
Author:
Date: 1966-09-13
Edition: 5

Chinese Ambassador Cai Zemin held a reception to celebrate the sixth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Guinea Friendship Treaty.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, September 11 -- Chinese Ambassador to Guinea Cai Zemin held a reception at the Chinese Embassy in Guinea on the evening of September 10 to celebrate the sixth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Guinea Friendship Treaty.

About one hundred people attended the reception. At the reception, the hosts and guests exchanged warm sentiments about the brotherly friendship between China and Guinea. Guinean artists who had just returned from China were particularly lively, singing songs in Chinese such as "The East is Red," which praises the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao.

Guinean friends expressed their boundless love for Chairman Mao, enthusiastically shouting, "Long live Chairman Mao!" Guinean friend N'diya said, "Mao Zedong Thought is not only a beacon for the Chinese revolution but also a beacon for world revolution."

### 庆祝中几友好条约签订六周年  柴泽民大使举行招待会

1966-09-13
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝中几友好条约签订六周年
    柴泽民大使举行招待会
    据新华社科纳克里十一日电  中国驻几内亚大使柴泽民十日晚上在中国驻几内亚大使馆举行招待会，庆祝中国—几内亚友好条约签订六周年。
    大约一百人参加了招待会。在招待会上，宾主畅叙中国和几内亚之间兄弟般的友谊。刚从中国访问归来的几内亚艺术家十分活跃，他们用中文演唱了歌颂中国人民伟大领袖毛主席的《东方红》等歌曲。
    几内亚朋友无限热爱毛主席，他们激动地高呼“毛主席万岁”。几内亚朋友恩迪亚说：“毛泽东思想不仅是中国革命的灯塔，而且也是世界革命的灯塔。”
