Title: Enhancing Friendship, Mutual Promotion, and Joint Development; Unite to Oppose Imperialism
Author:
Date: 1966-09-09
Edition: 6

On the evening of September 4, the finals of five events in the 1966 Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational were held. The picture shows the scene during the finals at the Beijing Workers' Gymnasium.

	New China News Agency photographer

	The Pakistani table tennis team visited the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Museum and listened to the guide explain Chairman Mao's report to the Red Army University students on the eve of the Anti-Japanese War, summarizing the experiences of the Second Revolutionary Civil War.

	Sports News reporter

	Vietnamese table tennis player Nguyen Thi Mai read "Quotations from Chairman Mao" together with Chinese athletes.

	Sports News reporter

	On September 5, athletes from various countries participating in the Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational, together with Chinese athletes, visited the Capital People's Park and took a group photo.

	Our reporter

	Palestinian athletes held up portraits of Chairman Mao and "Quotations from Chairman Mao" and shouted, "Long live Mao Zedong Thought!"

	Our reporter

	In the car, Syrian table tennis team members held "Quotations from Chairman Mao" and portraits of Chairman Mao and shouted "Long live Chairman Mao! Long live! Long live ten thousand times!" with the Red Guards.

	Sports News reporter

    九月四日晚，一九六六年北京国际乒乓球邀请赛进行了五个单项的决赛。图为正在北京工人体育馆进行决赛时的场面。

    巴基斯坦乒乓球队的队员们参观中国人民革命军事博物馆，倾听解说员介绍毛主席在抗日战争前夕，总结第二次国内革命战争经验，向红军大学学员作报告的情形。　　　　　　体育报记者摄
    越南乒乓球选手阮氏梅同中国选手一起，兴奋地朗读《毛主席语录》。
