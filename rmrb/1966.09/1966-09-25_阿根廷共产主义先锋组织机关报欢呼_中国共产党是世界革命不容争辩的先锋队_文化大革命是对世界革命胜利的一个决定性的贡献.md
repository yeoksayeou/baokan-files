Title: Argentine Communist Vanguard Organization's Organ Publishes Article Hailing the Chinese Communist Party as the Undisputed Vanguard of the World Revolution and the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution as a Decisive Contribution to the Victory of the World Revolution
Author:
Date: 1966-09-25
Edition: 5

	According to a Xinhua News Agency report dated September 24, Buenos Aires: The Argentine Communist Vanguard Organization's organ, "Uncompromising News," recently published an article stating that the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China is an "unprecedented victory in the history of the international communist movement" and "a decisive contribution to the victory of the world revolution."

	The article points out that the Chinese Communist Party, nurtured by Mao Zedong Thought, is the "undisputed and invincible vanguard of the world revolution."

	The article states that the class struggle between the proletariat and the bourgeoisie runs through the entire socialist society. The bourgeoisie will not readily give up their political and economic power; they will also compete with the proletariat in the cultural sphere. Therefore, it is necessary to carry out a socialist revolution in the minds of tens of millions of people to consolidate the political and economic power of the proletariat, prevent the emergence of revisionism, and prevent the restoration of capitalism.

	The article continues, saying that the proletarian cultural revolution being carried out by the Chinese people under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Mao Zedong Thought is precisely aimed at achieving this goal.

	The article points out that the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China is the Chinese people's revolutionary response to the continuous aggression of American imperialism and the betrayal of revisionism. It shows that the Chinese Communist Party, led by Mao Zedong, is determined to strengthen and defend China, the great center of the world revolution, and the great center of struggle against imperialism and revisionism. The article states that the proletarian cultural revolution to uproot revisionism from Chinese soil is a historical necessity for the development of the Chinese revolution and the world revolution. The article points out that the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China is a necessary step taken to resolutely oppose American imperialism and Soviet revisionism and to better and more powerfully support the oppressed nations of Asia, Africa, and Latin America, as well as all the oppressed people of the world.

	The article states that the revolutionary consciousness of the Chinese people is a powerful force that makes the criminals of atomic war and the revisionist traitors tremble.

### 阿根廷共产主义先锋组织机关报欢呼  中国共产党是世界革命不容争辩的先锋队  文化大革命是对世界革命胜利的一个决定性的贡献

1966-09-25
第5版()
专栏：

    阿根廷共产主义先锋组织机关报欢呼
    中国共产党是世界革命不容争辩的先锋队
    文化大革命是对世界革命胜利的一个决定性的贡献
    据新华社二十四日讯  布宜诺斯艾利斯消息：阿根廷共产主义先锋组织机关报《不妥协报》最近发表文章说，中国无产阶级文化大革命是“国际共产主义运动史上的一次前所未有的胜利”，“是对世界革命胜利的一个决定性的贡献”。
    文章指出，用毛泽东思想哺育的中国共产党是“世界革命不容争辩的和战无不胜的先锋队。”
    文章说，社会主义社会自始至终贯穿着无产阶级和资产阶级之间的阶级矛盾和斗争。资产阶级绝不甘心于丧失政治和经济权力，他们还会在文化领域内同无产阶级较量。因此，必须在千千万万人的头脑中进行社会主义革命，以巩固无产阶级的政治和经济权力，防止出现修正主义，防止资本主义复辟。
    文章接着说，中国人民在中国共产党和毛泽东思想指导下正在进行的无产阶级文化革命，正是为了达到这个目的而进行的。
    文章指出，中国无产阶级文化大革命是中国人民对于美帝国主义的不断侵略和修正主义的背叛所作出的革命的回击，它表明了以毛泽东为首的中国共产党决心加强和捍卫中国这个世界革命的伟大中心和这个对帝国主义和修正主义进行不调和斗争的伟大中心。文章说，从中国土地上挖掉修正主义根子的无产阶级文化革命，是中国革命和世界革命的历史发展的需要。文章指出，中国无产阶级文化大革命是中国为了坚决反对美帝国主义和苏联修正主义，为了更好地更有力地支援亚、非、拉被压迫民族以及全世界被剥削者而采取的一个必要步骤。
    文章说，中国人民的革命觉悟是足以使原子战争罪犯和修正主义叛徒发抖的强大力量。
