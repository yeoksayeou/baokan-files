Title: Local Publishing and Printing Departments Implement Revolutionary Measures to Mass-Produce Selected Works and Quotations of Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1966-09-07
Edition: 3

To meet the urgent needs of the broad masses of the people to study the works of Chairman Mao, publishing and printing departments across the country are taking revolutionary measures to mass-produce "Selected Readings from the Works of Mao Zedong" and "Quotations from Chairman Mao." At the same time, relevant departments have decided that starting from September 7th, these two works of Chairman Mao will be sold at new prices: "Selected Readings from the Works of Mao Zedong" Type A, four-point font, two-volume set, priced at 7 jiao; five-point font, priced at 5 jiao; Type B, four-point font, priced at 3 jiao 5 fen; five-point font, priced at 2 jiao 5 fen. "Quotations from Chairman Mao," 64-page plastic-covered edition, priced at 4 jiao 5 fen; paperback edition, priced at 2 jiao 5 fen; the 32-page student edition, priced at 2 jiao.

### 各地出版印刷部门采取革命措施  大量印制毛泽东著作选读和语录

1966-09-07
第3版()
专栏：

    各地出版印刷部门采取革命措施
    大量印制毛泽东著作选读和语录
    新华社六日讯　为了满足广大人民群众学习毛主席著作的迫切需要，各地出版、印刷部门正采取革命措施，大量印制《毛泽东著作选读》和《毛主席语录》。同时，有关部门决定，从九月七日起，这两种毛主席著作按新定价发售：《毛泽东著作选读》甲种本四号字本上下两册售价七角，五号字本售价五角；乙种本四号字本售价三角五分，五号字本售价二角五分。《毛主席语录》六十四开的塑料封套本售价四角五分，平装本售价二角五分；学生用的三十二开本，售价二角。
