Title: Xuchang Region's Flue-Cured Tobacco Production Better Than Last Year
Author:
Date: 1966-09-20
Edition: 2

The renowned flue-cured tobacco production area of Xuchang in Henan Province has seen its cadres and community members hoist high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought. They have displayed a revolutionary spirit of daring to struggle and daring to win, raising tobacco leaf production to a new level.

Currently, the tobacco-growing region of Xuchang is in the peak season for harvesting tobacco leaves. Community members, filled with the joy of a bountiful harvest, are actively engaged in picking and curing the tobacco. Judging from the growth of tobacco in various places, this year's flue-cured tobacco production is expected to be better than last year's.

### 许昌专区烤烟生产比去年好

1966-09-20
第2版()
专栏：

    许昌专区烤烟生产比去年好
    我国著名的烤烟产区河南许昌专区，广大干部和社员高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，发扬敢于斗争、敢于胜利的革命精神，把烟叶生产提高到一个新的水平。
    现在，许昌烟区已进入采摘烟叶的旺季，广大社员满怀丰收的喜悦，正在积极采摘、烘烤。从各地烟草长势看，今年烤烟生产的年景要比去年好。
