Title: Albanian People Commemorate Martyrs' Day
Author:
Date: 1966-05-09
Edition: 5

    Albanian People Commemorate Martyrs' Day
    (Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 6th) The Albanian people nationwide commemorated the annual Martyrs' Day (May 5th) on the 5th. The Albanian people, with a solemn mood, commemorated the heroic martyrs who sacrificed themselves in the national liberation struggle, and were determined to hold up the revolutionary banner even higher and defend the fruits of the revolution.
    On the 5th, people from all walks of life in Tirana went to the Martyrs' Cemetery to lay wreaths. Albanian party and government leaders Alia, Carcani, Tiodosi, etc. laid wreaths at the Martyrs' Cemetery on the morning of the 5th. Wang Jinchuan, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, and diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Tirana also laid wreaths at the Martyrs' Cemetery.
    On the same day, working people in Vlora, Korçë, Gjirokastër and other places also held various activities to commemorate the martyrs. (Photo attached)
    Comrade Shehu, Comrade Kambo and other distinguished Albanian guests visited the wheat fields of the Meirong Brigade in Tonglu County, Zhejiang Province. (Photo by Liu Qingrui, Xinhua News Agency reporter)

### 阿尔巴尼亚人民纪念烈士节

1966-05-09
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚人民纪念烈士节
    新华社地拉那六日电　阿尔巴尼亚全国人民五日纪念了一年一度的烈士节（五月五日）。阿尔巴尼亚人民以庄严肃穆的心情缅怀在民族解放斗争中英勇牺牲的烈士，决心更高地举起革命旗帜，保卫革命的胜利果实。
    五日，地拉那各界人民纷纷前往烈士墓敬献花圈。阿尔巴尼亚党政领导人阿利雅、查尔查尼、蒂奥多西等在五日上午到烈士墓献了花圈。到烈士墓敬献花圈的还有中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办王锦川和各国驻地拉那的外交使节等。
    同一天，发罗拉、科尔察、吉诺卡斯特等地的劳动人民也举行了纪念死难烈士的各种活动。（附图片）
    谢胡同志、卡博同志等阿尔巴尼亚贵宾在浙江桐庐县梅蓉大队的麦田里参观。　新华社记者　刘庆瑞摄
