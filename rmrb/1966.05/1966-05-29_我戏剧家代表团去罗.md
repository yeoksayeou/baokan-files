Title: Our Theatrical Delegation Leaves for Romania
Author:
Date: 1966-05-29
Edition: 6

    Our Theatrical Delegation Leaves for Romania
    Xinhua News Agency, May 28th. According to the 1966 implementation plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, the Chinese Theatrical Delegation, led by Mu Hong and consisting of three members, left by plane today for a visit to Romania.




**Named Entities**


牧虹	Mu Hong
中国戏剧家代表团	Chinese Theatrical Delegation
罗马尼亚	Romania
中罗文化合作协定	Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement
新华社	Xinhua News Agency

### 我戏剧家代表团去罗

1966-05-29
第6版()
专栏：

    我戏剧家代表团去罗
    新华社二十八日讯　根据中罗文化合作协定一九六六年执行计划，由牧虹率领的中国戏剧家代表团一行三人，今天乘飞机前往罗马尼亚访问。
