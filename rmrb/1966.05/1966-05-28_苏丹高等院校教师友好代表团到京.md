Title: Sudanese Higher Education Teachers' Friendship Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-28
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 27th – The Sudanese Higher Education Teachers' Friendship Delegation, invited by the Chinese-African Peoples' Friendship Association to visit our country, arrived in Beijing on the 25th, led by its leader, Muhammad Tawm Tijani.
    Zhang Tiesheng, Vice President of the Chinese-African Peoples' Friendship Association, Ai Wen, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, and others went to the airport to welcome them.

### 苏丹高等院校教师友好代表团到京

1966-05-28
第5版()
专栏：

    苏丹高等院校教师友好代表团到京
    新华社二十七日讯　应中非友协邀请来我国访问的苏丹高等院校教师友好代表团，在团长穆罕默德·陶姆·提加尼率领下，已在二十五日到达北京。
    前往机场迎接的，有中非友协副会长张铁生、对外友协书记处书记艾汶等。
