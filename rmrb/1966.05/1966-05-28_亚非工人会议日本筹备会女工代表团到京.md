Title: Asian-African Workers Conference Japanese Preparatory Committee Women Workers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-05-28
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 26th – The women workers delegation of the Asian-African Workers Conference Japanese Preparatory Committee, led by Takamatsu Tsuyako, Secretary-General of the Japan Red Cross Society Workers' Union Federation, arrived in Beijing by plane today with a group of eighteen people.
    They are visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions for a friendly visit. Wang Zhijie, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others welcomed them at the airport.

 

**Named Entities**

中华全国总工会	Zhonghua Quanguo Zonggonghui (All-China Federation of Trade Unions)
亚非工人会议日本筹备会	Yafei Gongren Huiyi Riben Choubei Hui (Asian-African Workers Conference Japanese Preparatory Committee)
柘枟津伊子	Takamatsu Tsuyako
日本红十字会工会联合会	Riben Hongshizi Hui Gonghui Lianhehui (Japan Red Cross Society Workers' Union Federation)
新华社	Xinhua News Agency
北京	Beijing
王志杰	Wang Zhijie

### 亚非工人会议日本筹备会女工代表团到京

1966-05-28
第5版()
专栏：

    亚非工人会议日本筹备会女工代表团到京
    新华社二十六日讯　由日本红十字会工会联合会总书记柘枟津伊子率领的亚非工人会议日本筹备会访华女工代表团一行十八人，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    他们是应中华全国总工会邀请来我国进行友好访问的。到机场欢迎的，有中华全国总工会书记处书记王志杰等。
