Title: Matsumura Kenzō and Others Arrive in Guangzhou
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-12
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, May 11th -- Matsumura Kenzō, advisor to the Japanese Liberal Democratic Party, and his party of six arrived in Guangzhou this afternoon.
    Zeng Sheng, Mayor of Guangzhou, Luo Peiyuan, Deputy Mayor, and other responsible persons welcomed them at the station. Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji also greeted them at the station.
    Wang Xiaoyun, a member of the Council of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, made a special trip from Beijing to Shenzhen to welcome the guests.
    In the evening, Zeng Sheng held a welcome banquet for Matsumura Kenzō and others.

### 松村谦三等到达广州

1966-05-12
第5版()
专栏：

    松村谦三等到达广州
    新华社广州十一日电　日本自由民主党顾问松村谦三等一行六人，今天下午到达广州。
    广州市市长曾生、副市长罗培元等负责人到车站欢迎。日本和平人士西园寺公一也到车站迎接。
    中国人民外交学会理事王晓云专程从北京前往深圳迎接客人。
    当晚，曾生为松村谦三等举行了欢迎宴会。
