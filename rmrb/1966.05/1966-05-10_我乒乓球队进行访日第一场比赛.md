Title: Our Table Tennis Team Plays First Match in Japan

Author: 
Date: 1966-05-10
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 7th – The Chinese men’s and women’s table tennis teams played their first official friendly match in Tokyo on the 7th. The Chinese men’s team won 5-1, while the women's team lost 3-5. The Chinese men's and women's table tennis teams arrived in Japan on the 5th for a visit.
    The Chinese men’s team consisted of Li Jingguang, Zhou Lansun, and Wang Jiasheng. The Japanese team consisted of Nobuhiko Hasegawa, Hiroshi Takahashi, and Koji Kimura.
    The Chinese women’s team consisted of Liang Lizhen, Li Li, and Li Henan. The Japanese team consisted of Noriko Yamanaka, Sachiko Morisawa, and Hisako Fukatsu.

### 我乒乓球队进行访日第一场比赛

1966-05-10
第5版()
专栏：

    我乒乓球队进行访日第一场比赛
    新华社东京七日电中国男女乒乓球队七日在东京进行了第一场正式友谊比赛。中国男队以五比一获胜，女子队以三比五失利。中国男女乒乓球队是在五日到达日本访问的。
    中国男子队由李景光、周兰荪和王家声上场。日本队出场的是长谷川信彦、高桥浩和木村兴治。
    中国女子队出场的是梁丽珍、李莉和李赫男。日本出场的是山中教子、森泽幸子和深津尚子。
