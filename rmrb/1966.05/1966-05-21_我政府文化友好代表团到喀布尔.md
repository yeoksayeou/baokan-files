Title: Our Government Cultural Friendship Delegation Arrives in Kabul
Author:
Date: 1966-05-21
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, May 20th - The Chinese Government Cultural Friendship Delegation, led by Shi Huaibi, Vice Minister of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, arrived in Kabul by car on the afternoon of the 19th from Kandahar. The delegation arrived in Kandahar by plane on the 18th to begin its friendly visit to Afghanistan.
    The Chinese delegation was welcomed at the airport by Mohammad Karzai, Director of the News and Propaganda Bureau of Kandahar Province, and Zhong Hanjiu, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Afghanistan. The delegation met with Mohammad Anas, the Governor of Kandahar Province, and visited some of the historical sites of this second largest city in Afghanistan. On the evening of the 18th, the Governor had dinner with the Chinese guests.

### 我政府文化友好代表团到喀布尔

1966-05-21
第6版()
专栏：

    我政府文化友好代表团到喀布尔
    新华社喀布尔二十日电　由对外文委副主任史怀璧率领的中国政府文化友好代表团十九日下午乘车从坎大哈到达喀布尔。代表团是十八日乘飞机到达坎大哈开始对阿富汗进行友好访问的。
    中国代表团在机场受到坎大哈省的新闻和宣传局长穆罕默德·卡尔宰和中国驻阿富汗大使馆参赞种汉九的欢迎。代表团会见了坎大哈省省长穆罕默德·阿纳斯，并参观了阿富汗这个第二大城市的一些名胜古迹。十八日晚上，省长同中国客人共进晚餐。
