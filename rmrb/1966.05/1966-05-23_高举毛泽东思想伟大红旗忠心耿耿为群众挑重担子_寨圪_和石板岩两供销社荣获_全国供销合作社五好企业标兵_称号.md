Title: Holding High the Great Red Banner of Mao Zedong Thought, Devotedly Shouldering Heavy Burdens for the Masses: Zhaigeta and Shiban'yan Supply and Marketing Cooperatives Awarded the Title of "National Supply and Marketing Cooperative Five-Good Enterprise Pacesetter"
Author:
Date: 1966-05-23
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 22nd dispatch: The All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives, the Political Department of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives of the CPC, and the National Committee of the National Financial and Trade Union of China held an award ceremony in Beijing on the 20th of this month, awarding the Zhaigeta Supply and Marketing Cooperative of Fushan County, Shanxi Province, and the Shiban'yan Supply and Marketing Cooperative of Lin County, Henan Province, the title of "National Supply and Marketing Cooperative Five-Good Enterprise Pacesetter." They also praised the achievements they have made since their establishment in holding high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, emphasizing politics, and realizing the ideological revolutionization of people and the revolutionization of enterprises, and encouraged them to continue to revolutionize and move forward.
    The employees of the Zhaigeta Supply and Marketing Cooperative and the Shiban'yan Supply and Marketing Cooperative are all filled with class feeling and serve the people wholeheartedly. They are anxious about what the masses are anxious about, worry about what the masses worry about, devote themselves to shouldering heavy burdens for the masses, adhere to and carry forward the revolutionary spirit of "one carrying pole," and do good deeds for the masses. They conscientiously implemented Chairman Mao's general policy of "developing the economy and ensuring supply," and tried every means to help the masses develop agricultural and sideline production, consolidate and develop the collective economy, and establish a close, flesh-and-blood relationship with the masses.
    The All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives, the Political Department of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives of the CPC, and the National Committee of the National Financial and Trade Union of China called on supply and marketing cooperatives at all levels and all employees across the country to take the employees of the Zhaigeta and Shiban'yan Supply and Marketing Cooperatives as examples, conscientiously learn their revolutionary spirit and advanced experience, creatively study and apply Chairman Mao's works, and further carry out the "Five-Good" enterprise and "Six-Good" employee-oriented comparison, learning, catching-up, helping, and surpassing movement, so that "Five-Good" enterprises and "Six-Good" employees can become widespread, better promote the ideological revolutionization of employees, the revolutionization of enterprises, and the revolutionization of institutions, and complete the glorious tasks entrusted to them by the Party and the state.
    At the award ceremony, Liu Rongsheng, political instructor of the Zhaigeta Supply and Marketing Cooperative, and Li Linhuan, political instructor of the Shiban'yan Supply and Marketing Cooperative, spoke respectively. They expressed that they would regard honor as a force for continuous advancement, further highlight politics, hold even higher the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, conscientiously and creatively study and apply Chairman Mao's works, adhere to taking class struggle and the struggle between the two lines as the key, continue to carry forward the revolutionary spirit of "one carrying pole," have the motherland in mind, have the world in sight, better run the people's supply and marketing cooperative, and serve the people wholeheartedly.

### 高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗忠心耿耿为群众挑重担子  寨圪?和石板岩两供销社荣获“全国供销合作社五好企业标兵”称号

1966-05-23
第4版()
专栏：

    高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗忠心耿耿为群众挑重担子
    寨圪?和石板岩两供销社荣获“全国供销合作社五好企业标兵”称号
    新华社二十二日讯　中华全国供销合作总社、中共中华全国供销合作总社政治部、中国财贸工会全国委员会本月二十日在北京举行授奖大会，分别授予山西省浮山县寨圪?供销合作社和河南省林县石板岩供销合作社以“全国供销合作社五好企业标兵”称号，并且表扬他们建社以来，高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，突出政治，在实现人的思想革命化和企业革命化方面所取得的成绩，鼓励他们不断革命，不断前进。
    寨圪?供销合作社和石板岩供销合作社职工，都满怀阶级感情，全心全意为人民服务。他们急群众之急，忧群众之忧，忠心耿耿为群众挑重担子，坚持和发扬“一根扁担”的革命精神，为群众办好事。他们认真地贯彻了毛主席“发展经济，保障供给”总方针，千方百计帮助群众发展农副业生产，巩固和发展集体经济，与群众建立了水乳交融、血肉相连的关系。
    中华全国供销合作总社、中共中华全国供销合作总社政治部和中国财贸工会全国委员会，号召全国各级供销合作社和全体职工要以寨圪?、石板岩供销合作社职工为榜样，认真学习他们的革命精神和先进经验，活学活用毛主席著作，进一步深入开展以“五好”企业、
  “六好”职工为目标的比学赶帮超运动，使“五好”企业、“六好”职工成片成林，更好地促进职工的思想革命化、企业革命化和机关革命化，完成党和国家给予的各项光荣任务。
    在授奖大会上，寨圪?供销合作社政治指导员刘荣升、石板岩供销合作社政治指导员李林洹分别讲了话。他们表示要把荣誉当作不断前进的力量，进一步突出政治，更高地举起毛泽东思想的伟大红旗，认真活学活用毛主席著作，坚持以阶级斗争和两条道路斗争为纲，继续发扬“一根扁担”的革命精神，胸怀祖国，放眼世界，更好地办好人民的供销合作社，全心全意为人民服务。
