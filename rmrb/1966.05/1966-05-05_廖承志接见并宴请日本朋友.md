Title: Liao Chengzhi Receives and Hosts Japanese Friends

Author:

Date: 1966-05-05
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 4th dispatch: Liao Chengzhi, President of the Sino-Japanese Friendship Association, received and hosted a banquet tonight for the Japanese Publishing Delegation headed by Shoichi Noma, the Japanese Sinologist and Educator Delegation headed by Dr. Akihisa Todo, and the Japanese dyeing and printing expert Saburo Ono, and others. Chu Tunan, Zhao Anbo, Lin Lin, Wang Xiaoyun, and others attended the reception and banquet.

### 廖承志接见并宴请日本朋友

1966-05-05
第5版()
专栏：

    廖承志接见并宴请日本朋友
    新华社四日讯　中日友好协会会长廖承志今晚接见并宴请由野间省一为首的日本出版代表团，以藤堂明保博士为首的日本中国语研究者教育者代表团，以及日本印染专家小野三郎等。参加接见和宴会的，有楚图南、赵安博、林林、王晓云等。
