Title: Chairman Boumedienne Congratulates China on Successful Nuclear Test
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-21
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, May 20th. Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the People's Republic of China received a letter written to him on May 12th by Houari Boumedienne, Chairman of the National Revolutionary Council of Algeria and Premier of the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Algeria, congratulating China on the successful nuclear test containing thermonuclear materials.

### 布迈丁主席祝贺我国核试验成功

1966-05-21
第6版()
专栏：

    布迈丁主席祝贺我国核试验成功
    新华社二十日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇收到了阿尔及利亚全国革命委员会主席、阿尔及利亚民主人民共和国政府总理胡阿里·布迈丁五月十二日写给他的一封信，祝贺中国含有热核材料的核试验成功。
