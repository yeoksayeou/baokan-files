Title: Shukairy Chairman Thanks Me for Supporting the Palestinian Liberation Struggle; China is a Base for Anti-Imperialist Struggle, a Fortress of Liberation and Progress

Author:
Date: 1966-05-21
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 19th – Chairman Shukairy of the Palestine Liberation Organization said in a telegram to the Islamic Association of China on the 18th that China is "a base for anti-imperialist struggle, a fortress of liberation and progress in the 20th century."
    Shukairy's telegram was in response to a telegram from the Islamic Association of China on "Palestine Day," expressing firm support for the Palestinian people's liberation struggle. Shukairy said in the telegram, "We are pleased to note that the Chinese government, people, and Muslims support us in opposing imperialism, neo-colonialism, and world Zionism, and support national liberation movements everywhere." He expressed gratitude for the Chinese government and people's sincere assistance to the Palestinian people's just struggle against U.S. imperialism and its tool of aggression, Israel.
    He said, "We deeply respect and are proud of the fruitful traditional friendship between the fighting Palestinian people and the friendly Chinese people."
    Shukairy condemned the U.S.-led imperialism's attempt to restore its influence in the Arab world through the conspiracy of the so-called "Islamic Pact." He said that any evil conspiracy of U.S.-led imperialism will never succeed and will ultimately be defeated.
    He said, "We will continue the struggle against U.S. imperialism and its lackeys, against all forms of colonialism in all parts of the world, until the forces of evil, aggression, exploitation, terror, and oppression are eliminated from the world."

### 舒凯里主席感谢我对巴勒斯坦解放斗争的支持  中国是反帝斗争根据地，解放和进步的堡垒

1966-05-21
第5版()
专栏：

    舒凯里主席感谢我对巴勒斯坦解放斗争的支持
    中国是反帝斗争根据地，解放和进步的堡垒
    新华社开罗十九日电　巴勒斯坦解放组织主席舒凯里十八日在给中国伊斯兰教协会的一份电报中说，中国是“反帝斗争的根据地，是二十世纪解放和进步的堡垒”。
    舒凯里的这份电报是感谢中国伊斯兰教协会就“巴勒斯坦日”打电报给他表示对巴勒斯坦人民解放斗争的坚决支持。舒凯里在电报中说：“我们满意地注意到中国政府、人民和穆斯林支持我们反对帝国主义、新殖民主义和世界犹太复国主义，支持各地的民族解放运动。”他表示感谢中国政府和人民真诚援助巴勒斯坦人民反对美帝国主义及其侵略工具以色列的正义斗争。
    他说：“我们深深尊重斗争中的巴勒斯坦人民和友好的中国人民之间富有成果的传统友谊，并为此感到自豪。”
    舒凯里谴责以美国为首的帝国主义企图通过所谓“伊斯兰条约”的阴谋来恢复它们在阿拉伯世界的势力。他说，以美国为首的帝国主义的任何罪恶阴谋决不会得逞，它们终将被击败。
    他说：“我们将继续进行反对美帝国主义及其走狗、反对全世界一切地方的各种形式的殖民主义的斗争，直到把邪恶、侵略、剥削、恐怖和压迫的势力从世界上消灭掉。”
