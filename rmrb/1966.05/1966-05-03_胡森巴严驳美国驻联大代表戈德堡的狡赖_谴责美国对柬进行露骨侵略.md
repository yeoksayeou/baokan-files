Title: Hu Senba sternly refutes U.S. Representative to the UN Goldberg's sophistry, condemning the U.S. for blatant aggression against Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1966-05-03
Edition: 5

    According to Xinhua News Agency, New York: Cambodian Chief Representative to the United Nations Hu Senba wrote to United Nations Secretary-General U Thant, sternly condemning the "constant threats, provocations, and blatant acts of aggression" by the United States, directly or indirectly, against Cambodia. This letter was published on April 29.
    In his letter, Hu Senba exposed the U.S. authorities for saying nice things while engaging in extremely vile and despicable actions. He said, "Despite official declarations (by the United States) of respect for the sovereignty and neutrality of Cambodia, the United States, under the cover of this pretense, directly or indirectly—through its allies in Saigon and Bangkok and the puppet 'Free Khmer'—is engaging in constant threats, provocations, and blatant acts of aggression against the political system, independence, and territorial integrity of the Kingdom of Cambodia."
    Hu Senba refuted the sophistry of U.S. Ambassador to the United Nations Goldberg regarding U.S. aggression against Cambodia. He said, "I object to the assertion that the U.S. government has not provided any support to the 'Free Khmer.' The opposite is true. According to the confessions of the 'Free Khmer' traitors themselves, who have been captured or surrendered to the Cambodian authorities, the United States has financed, equipped, and trained these mercenaries." In another letter to U Thant published on the same day, Hu Senba also mentioned that Thai troops had fired on Cambodia's Preah Vihear Temple twice, on the 21st and 25th.
    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 2nd—According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on the 2nd, Thai armed forces stationed in the Harle area, under the cover of three warships, fired on the Chine Sa and Chang Yang areas of Cambodia's Koh Kong Province on April 30.
    On April 24, approximately forty Thai armed forces personnel used heavy weapons to fire on a Cambodian outpost in Battambang Province.
    In both of these attacks, the Cambodian defenders resolutely carried out self-defense counterattacks. (Attached picture)
    The Royal Cambodian Armed Forces are stepping up military training, preparing to give a strong blow to the invading enemy.
　Xinhua News Agency

### 胡森巴严驳美国驻联大代表戈德堡的狡赖  谴责美国对柬进行露骨侵略

1966-05-03
第5版()
专栏：

    胡森巴严驳美国驻联大代表戈德堡的狡赖
    谴责美国对柬进行露骨侵略
    据新华社讯　纽约消息：柬埔寨驻联合国首席代表胡森巴写信给联合国秘书长吴丹，严正谴责美国直接或间接地对柬埔寨进行“不断的威胁、挑衅和露骨的侵略行动”。这封信已经在四月二十九日发表。
    胡森巴在信中揭穿美国当局说的是漂亮话，干的却是极其卑鄙龌龊的勾当。他说：“尽管（美国）官方宣称尊重柬埔寨的主权和中立，美国却在这个幌子的掩盖下直接或者间接地——通过它的西贡和曼谷这些同盟者和‘自由高棉’这个傀儡——采取不断的威胁、挑衅和露骨的侵略行动，来反对柬埔寨王国的政治制度、独立和领土完整。”
    胡森巴驳斥了美国驻联合国大使戈德堡对美国侵略柬埔寨的狡赖。他说：“我反对那种说美国政府并没有给‘自由高棉’提供任何支持的说法。事实恰好相反。根据被俘获或者是向柬埔寨当局投诚的那些‘自由高棉’叛徒自己的招供，美国资助、装备和训练了这些雇佣军。”胡森巴在同一天发表的给吴丹的另一封信中还提到，泰国军队在二十一日和二十五日曾两次向柬埔寨的柏威夏寺开火。
    新华社金边二日电　据柬埔寨新闻社二日报道，驻扎在哈勒地区的泰国武装部队四月三十日在三艘军舰的掩护下，向柬埔寨戈公省的奇内萨和昌扬地区进行射击。
    四月二十四日，泰国武装部队约四十人使用重武器射击马德望省的一个柬埔寨哨所。
    在这两次进攻中柬埔寨守卫者坚决进行了自卫反击。（附图片）
    柬埔寨王国军队加紧军事训练，准备给入侵的敌人以有力的打击。
　新华社发
