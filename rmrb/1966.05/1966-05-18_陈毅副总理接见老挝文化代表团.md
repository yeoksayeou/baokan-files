Author:
Date: 1966-05-18
Edition: 6

    Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Lao Cultural Delegation
    (Xinhua News Agency, May 17) Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Lao cultural delegation headed by Outtama Chounlamany this afternoon and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Lian Guan, Lin Lin, Dong Xuelin and other responsible persons from relevant departments.
    On the evening of the 14th, Lian Guan, President of the China-Laos Friendship Association, hosted a banquet in honor of the Lao cultural delegation led by Outtama Chounlamany.
    Madame Quinim Pholsena of Laos and Khanma Phoukhong, head of the Lao Patriotic Front Art Troupe, also attended the banquet. (Attached picture)
    May 17, Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Lao cultural delegation led by Outtama Chounlamany (front left three). Xinhua News Agency reporter Wu Yuanliu

### 陈毅副总理接见老挝文化代表团

1966-05-18
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见老挝文化代表团
    据新华社十七日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见以乌塔马·朱拉马尼为首的老挝文化代表团，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有连贯、林林、董学林等有关方面负责人。
    十四日晚，中国老挝友好协会会长连贯设宴招待由乌塔马·朱拉马尼率领的老挝文化代表团。
    老挝贵宁·奔舍那夫人及老挝爱国战线党文工团团长坎马·富贡也出席了宴会。（附图片）
    五月十七日，陈毅副总理接见由乌塔马·朱拉马尼（前左三）率领的老挝文化代表团。　新华社记者　吴元柳摄
