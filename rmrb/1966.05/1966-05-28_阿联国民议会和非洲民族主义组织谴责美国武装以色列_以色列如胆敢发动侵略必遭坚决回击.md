Title: 阿联国民议会和非洲民族主义组织谴责美国武装以色列  以色列如胆敢发动侵略必遭坚决回击 (United Arab Republic National Assembly and African Nationalist Organizations Condemn the United States for Arming Israel: Israel Will Face Resolute Counterattack If It Dares to Launch Aggression)
Author:
Date: 1966-05-28
Edition: 6

    新华社开罗二十六日电 (Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 26th) The National Assembly of the United Arab Republic (UAR) and the Cairo Office of African Nationalist Organizations strongly condemned the recent arms supply by the United States to Israel.

    The UAR National Assembly passed a resolution on the 24th stating, "These weapons, as well as those obtained by Israel from other sources, will be used for new aggression against Arab territories." The resolution exposed the duplicitous policy of the United States, which on the one hand talks about peace in the Middle East, and on the other hand arms Israel. It said, "This policy will intensify the tense situation in the Middle East, increase the possibility of Israeli aggression against Arab territories, and threaten international security and peace."

    First Vice President of the UAR and Deputy Supreme Commander of the Armed Forces, Amir, said on the 25th that the UAR was not surprised by the US arms supply to Israel, because the US has always supported Israel. He warned that if Israel dares to carry out aggression with the support of imperialism, the UAR will give a resolute blow.

    A joint statement issued in Cairo on the 25th by the Mozambique Revolutionary Committee, the South West Africa National Union, the Zimbabwe African National Union, and the Pan-Africanist Congress of South Africa stated that the US arming of Israel means continuing the enslavement of the Palestinian people and allowing Israel to continue to act as an agent of Western imperialism. This will inevitably be opposed by peace-loving people all over the world.

    The Basutoland Congress Party office in Cairo issued a statement saying: "The US arms supply to Israel proves that the US always talks about peace on the one hand and carries out aggression on the other."

    A statement issued by the Swaziland Progressive Party office said that the US's practice of building Israel into a military base threatens peace in the region.

    The Bechuanaland People's Party office issued a statement calling on all peace-loving people to condemn the US arms deal with Israel and support the Arab people's struggle to recover the occupied Palestine.

    The African Independence Party of Guinea and Cape Verde, the South West Africa People's Organization, the Mozambique Liberation Front, the People's Movement for the Liberation of Angola, and the People's Idea Movement of Equatorial Guinea, along with seven other African nationalist parties, also issued a joint statement condemning the US arming of Israel and supporting the struggle of the Arab people.

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 24th, the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Organization issued a statement here on the 24th condemning the recent US arms supply to Israel and interference in the internal affairs of the Arab people. The statement said that Israel, with the support of US imperialism and other imperialist masters, has committed heinous crimes in the Arab region. The statement concluded by saying that the people of Asia and Africa support the just struggle of the Palestinian people and the Arab countries to achieve the full restoration of the rights of the Palestinian people and to stop imperialist aggression. Arab League Deputy Secretary-General Arif Zahir said on the 24th that the US arms supply to Israel is a violation of the rights of the Arab people.

### 阿联国民议会和非洲民族主义组织谴责美国武装以色列  以色列如胆敢发动侵略必遭坚决回击

1966-05-28
第6版()
专栏：

    阿联国民议会和非洲民族主义组织谴责美国武装以色列
    以色列如胆敢发动侵略必遭坚决回击
    新华社开罗二十六日电  阿联国民议会和非洲民族主义组织驻开罗办事处强烈谴责美国最近向以色列提供武器。
    阿联国民议会二十四日通过的一项决议说：“这些武器以及以色列从别的来源获得的武器，将用于对阿拉伯国土进行新的侵略。”决议揭露了美国一面侈谈中东和平、一面武装以色列的两面派政策。它说：“这种政策将加剧中东地区的紧张局势，增加以色列侵略阿拉伯国土的可能性和威胁国际安全与和平。”
    阿联第一副总统、武装部队最高副司令阿密尔二十五日说，美国向以色列提供武器并不使阿联惊异，因为美国一直在扶植以色列。他警告说，如果以色列胆敢在帝国主义的支持下进行侵略，阿联将给予坚决的打击。
    莫三鼻给革命委员会、西南非洲民族联盟、津巴布韦非洲民族联盟和南非泛非主义者大会二十五日在开罗发表的一项联合声明说，美国武装以色列意味着继续奴役巴勒斯坦人民和使以色列继续充当西方帝国主义的代理人。这必将遭到全世界爱好和平的人民的反对。
    巴苏陀兰大会党驻开罗办事处发表声明说：“美国向以色列提供武器一事证明：美国总是一面侈谈和平，一面进行侵略。”
    斯威士兰进步党办事处发表的一项声明说，美国把以色列建成军事基地的做法威胁着这一地区的和平。
    贝专纳人民党办事处发表的声明要求一切爱好和平的人民谴责美国同以色列进行武器交易，支持阿拉伯人民收复被强占的巴勒斯坦的斗争。
    几内亚和佛得角非洲独立党、西南非洲人民组织、莫三鼻给解放阵线、安哥拉人民解放运动和赤道几内亚人民思想运动等七个非洲民族主义政党也发表联合声明，谴责美国武装以色列并支持阿拉伯人民的斗争。
    据新华社开罗二十四日电亚非人民团结组织书记处二十四日在这里发表声明，谴责美国最近向以色列提供军火，以及干涉阿拉伯人民的内部事务。声明说，以色列在美帝国主义和其他帝国主义主子们的支持下，在阿拉伯地区犯下了滔天罪行。声明最后说，亚非各国人民支持巴勒斯坦人民和阿拉伯各国人民为争取完全恢复巴勒斯坦人民的权利和制止帝国主义侵略而进行的正义斗争。阿拉伯联盟副秘书长阿里夫·查希尔二十四日说，美国向以色列提供军火是对阿拉伯人民的权利的侵犯。
