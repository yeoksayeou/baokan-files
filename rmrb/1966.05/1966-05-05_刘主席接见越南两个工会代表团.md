Title: Chairman Liu Receives Two Vietnamese Trade Union Delegations
Author:
Date: 1966-05-05
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, May 4th – Chairman Liu Shaoqi received this afternoon the delegation from the South Vietnam Liberation Federation of Trade Unions led by Tran Hoai Nam and the delegation from the Vietnam General Federation of Trade Unions led by Hoang Mao. Chairman Liu Shaoqi had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Chen Shaomin and Kang Yonghe, Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.
    Also present were Tran Tu Binh, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, and Nguyen Minh Phuong, Deputy Head of the Permanent Delegation of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front to China.

### 刘主席接见越南两个工会代表团

1966-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席接见越南两个工会代表团
    新华社四日讯　刘少奇主席今天下午接见了由陈怀南率领的越南南方解放工会联合会代表团和由黄茂率领的越南总工会代表团。刘少奇主席同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有：中华全国总工会副主席陈少敏、康永和。
    越南民主共和国驻华大使陈子平、越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团副团长阮明芳也在座。
