Title: Vice Chairman Lin Feng Receives Guests from Ceylon
Author:
Date: 1966-05-04
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 3rd—Vice Chairman Lin Feng received Ceylon China Friendship Association Chairman Aladiahawanna and his wife, and Ceylon Freedom Party Organization Secretary Ronnie de Mel and his wife this morning, and held friendly talks with them.
    Present at the reception were Zhang Zhiran, President of the China-Ceylon Friendship Association, Ma Chungu, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others. Counselor Cruz of the Ceylon Embassy in China was also present.

### 林枫副委员长接见锡兰客人

1966-05-04
第5版()
专栏：

    林枫副委员长接见锡兰客人
    新华社三日讯　林枫副委员长今天上午接见了锡兰中国友好协会主席阿拉季亚瓦纳和夫人、锡兰自由党组织书记洛尼·德麦尔和夫人，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中锡友协会长张志让、中华全国总工会副主席马纯古等。锡兰驻中国大使馆参赞柯鲁斯也在座。
