Title: Romanian Party and Government Delegation Returns Home; Comrades Chen Yi and Li Xiannian See Them Off at the Airport

Author:

Date: 1966-05-14
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, May 13th: Emil Bodnăraş, Member of the Executive Committee of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee, Member of the Standing Presidium of the Central Committee, and First Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and the Romanian Party and Government Delegation led by him, which visited Vietnam, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.
    Comrades Chen Yi and Li Xiannian, Members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice-Premiers of the State Council, as well as Zhao Yimin, Qiao Guanhua, Peng Shaohui, Cui Yueli, and other responsible persons from relevant departments, were at the airport to see them off. Young Pioneers presented flowers to the distinguished guests.
    Also present at the airport were Romanian Ambassador to China Aurel Duma, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China Tran Tu Binh, and Tran Van Thanh, Head of the Permanent Delegation of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front to China. (Photo attached)
    Left Photo: Comrade Bodnăraş (first from right in front) leads the Romanian Party and Government Delegation returning home. Comrades Chen Yi and Li Xiannian, among others, were at the airport to see them off.
                                        Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Jingde

### 罗党政代表团回国  陈毅李先念等同志到机场欢送

1966-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    罗党政代表团回国
    陈毅李先念等同志到机场欢送
    新华社十三日讯　罗马尼亚共产党中央执行委员会委员、中央常设主席团委员、部长会议第一副主席埃米尔·波德纳拉希，和由他率领的访问越南的罗马尼亚党政代表团，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的，有中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅、李先念，以及赵毅敏、乔冠华、彭绍辉、崔月犁等有关方面的负责人。少先队员向贵宾们献了鲜花。
    罗马尼亚驻中国大使奥列尔·杜马，越南民主共和国驻中国大使陈子平，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长陈文成，也到飞机场送行。（附图片）
    左图：波德纳拉希同志（前右二）率领罗马尼亚党政代表团回国。陈毅、李先念等同志到机场欢送。
                                        新华社记者　王敬德摄
