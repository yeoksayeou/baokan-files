Title: Albanian Newly Appointed Ambassador Nataili Calls on Premier Zhou

Author: 
Date: 1966-05-14
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 13th dispatch: Albanian newly appointed Ambassador to China, Vasili Nathanaili, paid a call on Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council this afternoon. Premier Zhou had a friendly conversation with him.



**Named Entities**


阿尔巴尼亚 Albania
瓦西里·纳塔奈利 Vasili Nathanaili
周恩来 Zhou Enlai
国务院 State Council
新华社 Xinhua News Agency

### 阿新任大使纳塔奈利拜会周总理

1966-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    阿新任大使纳塔奈利拜会周总理
    新华社十三日讯　阿尔巴尼亚新任驻华大使瓦西里·纳塔奈利，今天下午拜会国务院总理周恩来。周总理同他进行了友好的谈话。
