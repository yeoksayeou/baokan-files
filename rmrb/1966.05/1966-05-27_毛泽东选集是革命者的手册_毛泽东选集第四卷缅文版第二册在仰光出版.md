Title: Selected Works of Mao Zedong is a Handbook for Revolutionaries; Volume Four, Second Edition of the Burmese Translation of Selected Works of Mao Zedong Published in Yangon
Author:
Date: 1966-05-27
Edition: 5

    Selected Works of Mao Zedong is a Handbook for Revolutionaries
    Volume Four, Second Edition of the Burmese Translation of Selected Works of Mao Zedong Published in Yangon
    Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, 26th – The second volume of the Burmese edition of Volume Four of the Selected Works of Mao Zedong was recently published here.
    The translator is Burmese writer Yan Kyaw, and it is published by the "Flying Pen" bookstore in Yangon.
    Volume Four (First Edition) of the Selected Works of Mao Zedong has already been published earlier.
    The publisher placed an advertisement in the 26th's "Mirror Daily". The advertisement said: "The Selected Works of Mao Zedong is a handbook for revolutionaries. It combines Marxism with the Chinese revolution. It strengthens the revolution of the working people of all countries in the world."

### 毛泽东选集是革命者的手册  毛泽东选集第四卷缅文版第二册在仰光出版

1966-05-27
第5版()
专栏：

    毛泽东选集是革命者的手册
    毛泽东选集第四卷缅文版第二册在仰光出版
    新华社仰光二十六日电　毛泽东选集第四卷缅文版第二册最近在这里出版。
    译者是缅甸作家延觉，由仰光的“飞笔”书店出版。
    毛泽东选集第四卷（第一册）已经在早些时候就出版了。
    出版者在二十六日的《镜报》上登了一则广告。广告说：“毛泽东选集是革命者的手册。它把马克思主义同中国革命结合起来了。它加强了全世界各国劳动人民的革命。”
