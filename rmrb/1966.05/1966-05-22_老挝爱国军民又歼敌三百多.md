Title: Lao Patriotic Armed Forces and People Annihilate Over 300 Enemies Again
Author:
Date: 1966-05-22
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Khang Khay dispatch: According to Radio Pathet Lao, from April 3 to 15, the patriotic armed forces and people of Ban Ang region in Luang Prabang Province, Laos, bravely counterattacked the offensives of bandit commandoes instigated by the United States and its running dogs, and struck at their harassment and sabotage activities. In the battles, they annihilated 200 enemies and seized many guns and military supplies.
    From March 5 to April 3, militiamen and guerrillas in Muang Son District, Sam Neua Province, killed a total of 99 bandits and wounded 16.
    In March, the patriotic armed forces and people of Phongsaly Province annihilated a total of 47 bandits in anti-bandit operations, seizing many weapons and military supplies.

### 老挝爱国军民又歼敌三百多

1966-05-22
第6版()
专栏：

    老挝爱国军民又歼敌三百多
    新华社康开电　据巴特寮电台广播：老挝琅勃拉邦省班昂地区的爱国军民，从四月三日到十五日，英勇反击了美国及其走狗指使的土匪别动队的进攻，打击了他们的骚扰破坏活动，在战斗中歼灭敌人二百名，缴获许多枪支和军用物资。
    桑怒省芒松县民兵游击队从三月五日到四月三日，共打死土匪九十九名，打伤十六名。
    丰沙里省爱国军民三月份在剿匪战斗中，共歼灭土匪四十七名，缴获许多武器和军用物资。
