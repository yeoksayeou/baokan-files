Title: Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the NPC Standing Committee, Receives Japanese Friends
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-10
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 9th -- Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received this afternoon the entire delegation of the Japanese Local Assembly Members' League for the Promotion of International Trade, headed by Miura Hachiro, and the delegation of Japanese Women Local Assembly Members, headed by Gotō Mitsuru, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Zhang Huadong, Yang Yunyu, Zhao Anbo, Li Chuan, and other responsible persons from relevant departments.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本朋友

1966-05-10
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见日本朋友
    新华社九日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若今天下午接见了以三浦八郎为首的日本地方议员促进国际贸易联盟访华代表团和以后藤满为首的日本妇女地方议员代表团的全体人员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有张化东、杨蕴玉、赵安博、李川等有关方面负责人。
