Title: Peruvian Folk Handicraft Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-05-27
Edition: 5

    The “Peruvian Folk Handicraft Exhibition” officially opened today in Beijing’s Zhongshan Park. All the exhibits were brought by the Peruvian journalists delegation who came to China for a friendly visit. They possess a unique national style and folk characteristics. After the exhibition, all these exhibits will be presented to our country to express the Peruvian people's friendship toward the Chinese people.
    The exhibition is jointly sponsored by the Chinese Latin American Friendship Association, the All-China Journalists Association, and the Chinese Artists Association. The heads of these organizations, Chu Tunan, Wang Yunsheng, Liu Kaiqu, Chen Shuliang, as well as audiences from the capital, attended the opening ceremony.
    Antonio Fernández Arce, head of the Peruvian journalists delegation, and members Carlos Ortega, Alberto Arrarte Villanueva, Eduardo Justo, and others also attended the opening ceremony.
    After the opening ceremony, a symposium was held, where the Peruvian friends introduced the development of Peruvian folk handicrafts.

### 秘鲁民间手工艺品展览在京展出

1966-05-27
第5版()
专栏：

    秘鲁民间手工艺品展览在京展出
    新华社二十五日讯　“秘鲁民间手工艺品展览”今天在北京中山公园正式展出。全部展品都是前来我国进行友好访问的秘鲁新闻工作者代表团带来的，它们具有独特的民族风格和民间特色。这些展品在展出结束后，都将赠送我国，表达秘鲁人民对中国人民的友谊。
    展览会是由中国拉丁美洲友好协会、中华全国新闻工作者协会、中国美术家协会联合主办的，这些单位的负责人楚图南、王芸生、刘开渠、陈叔亮以及首都观众出席了开幕式。
    秘鲁新闻工作者代表团团长安东尼奥·费尔南德斯·阿尔塞和团员卡洛斯·奥尔特加、阿尔贝托·阿拉约·比利亚努埃瓦、爱德华多·胡斯托等也出席了开幕式。
    开幕式之后，举行了座谈会，秘鲁朋友在座谈会上介绍了秘鲁民间手工艺品的发展情况。
