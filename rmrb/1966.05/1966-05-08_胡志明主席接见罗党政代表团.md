Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Receives Romanian Party and Government Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-08
Edition: 5

    Hanoi, 7th May (Xinhua) – According to local newspaper reports, Ho Chi Minh, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labour Party and President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, received on the 5th of May, Emil Bodnăraș, Member of the Executive Committee of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee, Member of the Permanent Presidium of the Central Committee, First Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and all members of the Romanian Party and Government Delegation he led.

    On the evening of the 6th, the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labour Party and the Vietnamese government held a banquet to welcome the Romanian Party and Government Delegation.

    Nguyễn Duy Trinh and Emil Bodnăraș spoke at the banquet successively.

### 胡志明主席接见罗党政代表团

1966-05-08
第5版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席接见罗党政代表团
    新华社河内七日电　据这里的报纸报道，越南劳动党中央委员会主席、越南民主共和国主席胡志明，五日接见了罗马尼亚共产党中央执行委员会委员、中央常设主席团委员、政府部长会议第一副主席埃·波德纳拉希以及由他率领的罗马尼亚党政代表团全体成员。
    六日晚上，越南劳动党中央委员会和越南政府举行宴会，欢迎罗马尼亚党政代表团。
    阮维桢和埃·波德纳拉希先后在宴会上讲了话。
