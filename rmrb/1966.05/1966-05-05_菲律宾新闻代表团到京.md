Title: Philippine News Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-05-05
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 4th. A five-member Philippine news delegation, at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs and led by Mr. Jose Cruz, columnist of the *Manila Times*, arrived in Beijing on the 4th. Members of the delegation include Napoleon Rama, political reporter of the *Philippine Free Press* weekly, Ernesto Granada, columnist of the *Manila Chronicle*, and Arian Cristobal, associate editor of the *Realidad* weekly. Mrs. Jose Cruz also accompanied the delegation.
    Wang Yunsheng, Wang Yinpu, Zhang Linsheng, and others were at the airport to greet them.

### 菲律宾新闻代表团到京

1966-05-05
第5版()
专栏：

    菲律宾新闻代表团到京
    新华社四日讯　菲律宾新闻代表团一行五人，应中国人民外交学会邀请，在团长《马尼拉时报》专栏作家何塞·克鲁斯率领下，于四日抵京。代表团团员有《菲律宾自由新闻》周刊政治记者纳波莱昂·拉马，《马尼拉纪事报》专栏作家埃内斯托·格兰纳达，《写实》周刊副编辑阿里安·克里斯托巴。随同前来的还有何塞·克鲁斯夫人。
    前往机场迎接的有王芸生、王荫圃和张林生等。
