Title: Afghan Prime Minister Maiwandwal Receives Chinese Government Cultural Friendship Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-05-25
Edition: 5

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, May 23rd - Afghan Prime Minister Mohammad Hashim Maiwandwal received the Chinese Government Cultural Friendship Delegation led by Shi Huaibi, Deputy Director of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, here on the 22nd. He had a friendly conversation with the Chinese guests.
    Chen Feng, the Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan, was also present at the meeting.

### 阿富汗首相迈万德瓦尔  接见我政府文化友好代表团

1966-05-25
第5版()
专栏：

    阿富汗首相迈万德瓦尔
    接见我政府文化友好代表团
    据新华社喀布尔二十三日电  阿富汗首相穆罕默德·哈希姆·迈万德瓦尔二十二日在这里接见了由中国对外文化联络委员会副主任史怀璧率领的中国政府文化友好代表团。他同中国客人们进行了友好的谈话。
    在接见时，中国驻阿富汗大使陈枫也在座。
