Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions and Others Send Telegrams to Congratulate the 11th Anniversary of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan; Resolutely Support the Struggle of Korean Residents in Japan Against the US-Japan Reactionaries
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-25
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, May 24th: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Women's Federation, and the All-China Youth Federation today sent telegrams to the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan, warmly congratulating the 11th anniversary of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan.

    The telegram said that for eleven years, the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan has united 600,000 Koreans residing in Japan and carried out heroic and unyielding struggles against the US-Japan reactionaries to defend the national and democratic rights of Korean residents in Japan and to achieve the reunification of the fatherland, achieving brilliant accomplishments.

    The telegram reiterated its firm support for the just struggle currently being waged by Korean citizens in Japan against the US imperialism's occupation of South Korea and for the early reunification of the fatherland, to smash the "Japan-South Korea Treaty", to oppose the US-Japan reactionaries' imposition of "South Korean" nationality on Korean residents in Japan and to deprive Korean residents in Japan of their national and democratic educational rights, and expressed brotherly and sisterly respect to the Korean residents in Japan, who have a glorious tradition of struggle, wishing them new and greater achievements in their future struggles.

### 我全总等电贺旅日朝侨总联合会成立十一周年  坚决支持旅日朝侨反对美日反动派的斗争

1966-05-25
第6版()
专栏：

    我全总等电贺旅日朝侨总联合会成立十一周年
    坚决支持旅日朝侨反对美日反动派的斗争
    新华社二十四日讯　中华全国总工会、中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会和中华全国青年联合会今天打电报给旅日朝侨总联合会，热烈祝贺旅日朝侨总联合会成立十一周年。
    电报说，十一年来，旅日朝侨总联合会团结六十万侨居日本的朝鲜人，为保卫旅日朝侨的民族民主权利，为实现祖国的统一，同美日反动派进行了英勇不屈的斗争，取得了辉煌的成就。
    电报重申坚决支持旅日朝鲜公民正在开展着的反对美帝国主义霸占南朝鲜和争取祖国早日统一，粉碎“日韩条约”，反对美日反动派把“韩国”国籍强加给旅日朝鲜公民和剥夺旅日朝侨民族民主教育权利的正义斗争，并向具有光荣斗争传统的旅日朝侨表示兄弟姊妹般的敬意，祝他们在今后的斗争中取得新的更加巨大的成就。
