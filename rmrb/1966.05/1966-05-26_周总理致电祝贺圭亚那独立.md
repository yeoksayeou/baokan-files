Title: Premier Zhou Sends Telegram Congratulating Guyana on its Independence
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-26
Edition: 1

    Premier Zhou Sends Telegram Congratulating Guyana on its Independence
    Xinhua News Agency, May 25th -- Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, today sent a telegram to Prime Minister Forbes Burnham of Guyana, congratulating Guyana on its declaration of independence. The telegram reads:
    Georgetown, Guyana
    Prime Minister Forbes Burnham of Guyana:

    On the occasion of Guyana's declaration of independence, I, on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China, extend warm congratulations to the people of Guyana. I wish your people new achievements in the struggle to safeguard national independence and oppose new and old colonialism.
    Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai
                May 25, 1966
                                    Beijing

### 周总理致电祝贺圭亚那独立

1966-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理致电祝贺圭亚那独立
    新华社二十五日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天打电报给圭亚那总理福贝斯·伯纳姆，祝贺圭亚那宣告独立。电文如下：圭亚那　乔治敦圭亚那总理福贝斯·伯纳姆：
    值圭亚那宣告独立之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民向圭亚那人民表示热烈的祝贺。祝贵国人民在维护民族独立、反对新、老殖民主义的斗争中取得新的成就。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                一九六六年五月二十五日
                                    于北京
