Title: Thai Prime Minister Thanom Visits "Malaysia" to Broker Collusion Between "Malaysia" and the Indonesian Right-Wing Regime
Author:
Date: 1966-05-30
Edition: 6

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Kuala Lumpur, May 27: Thai Prime Minister Thanom Kittikachorn visited "Malaysia" from May 23 to 27 and held talks with "Malaysian" Prime Minister Rahman and Deputy Prime Minister Razak.
    According to reports, in addition to strengthening collusion with "Malaysia," Thanom's visit actively brokered the end of "confrontation" between "Malaysia" and the Indonesian right-wing military regime, and, according to American instructions, launched a conspiracy to expand the "Southeast Asia League."
    A statement issued in Kuala Lumpur on the 27th said that in the talks, Rahman expressed gratitude to Thailand for its efforts to promote a "resolution of the dispute between Malaysia and Indonesia," while Thanom also expressed the hope that they "will achieve a friendly resolution in the current favorable atmosphere." The statement also emphasized intensifying the efforts to make the anti-communist political alliance formed in 1961 by Malaya, the Philippines, and Thailand—the "Southeast Asia League"—an "effective organization." When Thanom arrived in Kuala Lumpur on the 23rd, he told reporters that the main topic of his talks with Rahman was "promoting regional cooperation." He specifically mentioned the need to "strengthen ties between Malaysia, the Philippines, Indonesia, and Thailand."

### 泰国总理他侬到“马来西亚”活动  为“马来西亚”同印尼右派政权勾结进行撮合

1966-05-30
第6版()
专栏：

    泰国总理他侬到“马来西亚”活动
    为“马来西亚”同印尼右派政权勾结进行撮合
    据新华社二十七日讯　吉隆坡消息：泰国总理他侬在二十三日到二十七日访问了“马来西亚”，同“马来西亚”总理拉赫曼和副总理拉扎克举行了会谈。
    据报道，他侬这次访问，除加强同“马来西亚”的勾结以外，还积极为“马来西亚”和印度尼西亚右派军人政权的结束“对抗”进行撮合，并且按照美国的指示展开扩大“东南亚联盟”的阴谋活动。
    二十七日在吉隆坡发表的一项公告说，在会谈中，拉赫曼表示感谢泰国致力于促致“解决马来西亚和印度尼西亚之间的争端”，而他侬也表示希望它们“将在目前的有利气氛中取得友好的解决”。公告还强调要加紧使一九六一年由马来亚、菲律宾和泰国组成的反共政治联盟——“东南亚联盟”——成为一个“有效的组织”。他侬在二十三日到达吉隆坡的时候就对新闻记者说，他同拉赫曼会谈的主要题目是“增进区域合作”。他特别提到要“密切马来西亚、菲律宾、印度尼西亚和泰国之间的联系”。
