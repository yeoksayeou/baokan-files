Title: Emerging Powerlifting Federation Liaison Committee Established in Beijing; China Elected as Chairman; Next Competition to be Held in China in 1968
Author:
Date: 1966-05-26
Edition: 4

    The heads of delegations, representatives, and observers of weightlifting from various countries and regions participating in the 1966 Emerging Powerlifting Invitational Tournament held meetings in Beijing on May 19, 24, and 25.

    After full discussion, the meetings decided to establish an Emerging Powerlifting Competition Liaison Committee to strengthen mutual unity and cooperation and to continue holding Emerging Powerlifting Competitions.

    The meetings unanimously elected China as the chairman of the Emerging Powerlifting Competition Liaison Committee; Albania, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and Pakistan as vice-chairmen, with another vice-chairman to be selected from an African country. The meetings also decided that the other countries and regions participating in this invitational tournament would be members of the Liaison Committee; the Secretary-General of the Liaison Committee would be appointed by the chairman country.

    The meetings decided that the Emerging Powerlifting Competition would be held every two years, with the next competition scheduled to be held in China in 1968.

    The meetings were conducted in an atmosphere of full consultation and solidarity and friendship, and a communiqué was issued after the meetings. With great indignation, the participants condemned the despicable acts of the International Weightlifting and Bodybuilding Federation, which, under the manipulation of US imperialism, obstructed friendly exchanges between weightlifting athletes from various countries and grossly infringed upon the sovereignty of other countries. Many countries' athletes overcame numerous obstacles set up by imperialism and resolutely came to Beijing to participate in the competition, demonstrating a fearless spirit of daring to struggle against imperialism. The participants unanimously agreed that as long as the people and athletes of all countries strengthen unity and persist in struggle, any schemes and tricks of imperialism and old and new colonialism can be crushed.

    The meetings considered that this invitational tournament was conducted in an atmosphere full of friendship and unity. Under the guidance of the spirit of enhancing friendship, promoting each other, developing together, and uniting against imperialism, the participants in the invitational tournament exchanged technical experiences and established deep friendships. Athletes from various countries achieved excellent results in the competition, breaking two world records, equaling one world record, and breaking the records of the first Emerging Forces Games forty-four times in nineteen events. The successful holding of this invitational tournament further promoted the friendship and anti-imperialist solidarity between the people and athletes of the participating countries and made a contribution to the development of weightlifting.

    The meetings were presided over by Zhong Shitong, chairman of the organizing committee of this invitational tournament and chairman of the Chinese Weightlifting Association. The following attended the meetings: Skander Tupja, head of the Albanian delegation; Mihal Cipo, deputy head of the Albanian delegation; Sreng Ti, head of the Cambodian delegation; Ke Leh, deputy head of the Cambodian delegation; Tienne Ranasinghe, head of the Ceylon delegation; Qu Zhiquan, head of the Chinese delegation; Kamoio Nuka, head of the Japanese delegation; Kim In-guk, head of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea delegation; Muhammad Naqi Butt, head of the Pakistani delegation, and Muhammad Ayub Khan, deputy head of the Pakistani delegation; Abdul Karim Shawa, head of the Palestinian delegation; Nizar Nahid, head of the Syrian delegation; Ahmed Gora Ebrahim, representative of Azania (South Africa); Diaso Mamadou and Bah Dirno, observers from Guinea; and Dalamane Sangare, observer from Mali. ### 新兴力量举重赛联络委员会在京成立  推选中国为委员会主席　下次比赛一九六八年在中国举行

1966-05-26
第4版()
专栏：

    新兴力量举重赛联络委员会在京成立
    推选中国为委员会主席　下次比赛一九六八年在中国举行
    新华社二十五日讯　参加一九六六年新兴力量举重邀请赛的各国和地区举重代表团团长、代表、观察员，五月十九日、二十四日和二十五日在北京举行了会议。
    会议经过充分讨论，决定成立新兴力量举重比赛联络委员会，以便加强相互间的团结和合作，继续举办新兴力量举重比赛。
    会议一致推选中国为新兴力量举重比赛联络委员会主席；阿尔巴尼亚、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、巴基斯坦为副主席，另一名副主席将从非洲国家中产生。会议并且决定，参加这次邀请赛的其他国家和地区为联络委员会的委员；联络委员会的秘书长由主席国任命。
    会议确定每两年举行一次新兴力量举重比赛，下一次比赛定一九六八年在中国举行。
    这次会议是在充分协商和团结友好的气氛中进行的，会后并且发表了会议公报。会议的参加者怀着十分愤慨的心情，谴责了国际举重与健身联合会在美帝国主义操纵下阻挠各国举重运动员进行友好来往，粗暴地侵犯别国主权的卑鄙行径。这次有许多国家的运动员冲破了帝国主义设置的重重障碍，毅然来到北京参加比赛，表现了敢于同帝国主义作斗争的大无畏精神。会议的参加者一致认为：只要各国人民和运动员加强团结，坚持斗争，帝国主义和新老殖民主义的任何阴谋诡计都是可以粉碎的。
    会议认为：这次邀请赛是在充满着友好和团结的气氛中进行的。邀请赛的参加者在增进友好、互相促进、共同发展、团结反帝的精神指导下，交流了技术经验，建立了深厚的友谊。各国运动员在比赛中创造了优良的成绩，打破了两项世界纪录、平了一项世界纪录，并且四十四次打破了十九项第一届新兴力量运动会的纪录。这次邀请赛的成功举行，进一步促进了参加比赛的各国人民和运动员之间的友谊和反帝团结，对举重运动的发展作出了贡献。
    会议由这次邀请赛组织委员会主席、中国举重协会主席钟师统主持。出席会议的有：阿尔巴尼亚代表团团长斯坎德·图皮亚、副团长米哈尔·齐波，柬埔寨代表团团长盛提、副团长凯莱，锡兰代表团团长季恩·拉纳辛格，中国代表团团长曲治全，日本代表团团长额贺熏生，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国代表团团长金仁国，巴基斯坦代表团团长穆罕默德·纳基·布特和副团长穆罕默德·阿尤布汗，巴勒斯坦代表团团长阿卜杜勒·卡里姆·沙瓦，叙利亚代表团团长尼扎尔·纳希德，阿扎尼亚（南非）代表艾哈迈德·戈拉·易卜拉欣，几内亚观察员迪亚索·马马杜、巴赫·迪尔诺，马里观察员达拉马内·桑加雷等。（附图片）
