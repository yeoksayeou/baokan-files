Title: Our General Staff Department and General Political Department Host Banquet for Romanian Guests
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-01
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th. The General Staff Department and the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army held a banquet tonight to warmly welcome Romanian People's Army officers and their wives who are here on vacation in our country.
    On the eve of the "May 1st" International Labor Day, Li Tianyou, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and his wife, Xu Liqing, Deputy Director of the General Political Department, and others, gathered together with Lieutenant General Nicolescu Marin, Commander of the Artillery of the Romanian People's Army, and his wife, and other Romanian friends. They talked cordially and repeatedly toasted to the fighting friendship between the people of China and Romania and the two armies.
    Romanian Ambassador to China Aurel Duma and Military Attaché Colonel Solescu and his wife were also invited to the banquet.
    Also gathering with Romanian friends were: Wu Kehua and his wife, Jia Ruoyu and his wife, Pan Zhenwu, Yuan Huabing and his wife, Li Kai and his wife, Wang Pingshui and his wife, etc.

### 我总参谋部和总政治部欢宴罗马尼亚客人

1966-05-01
第6版()
专栏：

    我总参谋部和总政治部欢宴罗马尼亚客人
    新华社三十日讯　中国人民解放军总参谋部和总政治部今晚举行宴会，热烈欢迎来我国休假的罗马尼亚人民军军官和他们的夫人。
    在“五一”国际劳动节的前夕，中国人民解放军副总参谋长李天佑和夫人、总政治部副主任徐立清等，同罗马尼亚人民军炮兵司令尼考列斯库·马林中将和夫人以及其他罗马尼亚朋友欢聚一堂，亲切交谈，他们频频为中罗两国人民和两国军队之间的战斗友谊干杯。
    罗马尼亚驻中国大使奥列尔·杜马和大使馆武官索列斯库上校和夫人，也应邀出席了宴会。
    同罗马尼亚朋友欢聚的还有：吴克华和夫人、贾若瑜和夫人、潘振武、苑化冰和夫人、李开和夫人、王平水和夫人等。
