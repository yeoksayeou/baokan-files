Title: The Indian Government Deserves to Be the Anti-China Vanguard of the US and the Soviet Union - Attacking China's Nuclear Explosion and Begging the US and the Soviet Union for "Nuclear Protection"
Author:
Date: 1966-05-14
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 13th, New Delhi Dispatch: The Indian government, acting as the anti-China vanguard of US imperialism and Soviet revisionism, on one hand, vigorously attacks China's nuclear tests, and on the other hand, takes the opportunity to promote the "prevention of nuclear proliferation" merchandise of the United States and the Soviet Union, serving the conspiracy of the US and the Soviet Union to monopolize nuclear weapons and dominate the world.
    Indian Prime Minister Indira Gandhi said in Parliament on the 10th that China's recent nuclear explosion "poses a serious threat to India's security." Indian Foreign Minister Swaran Singh also attacked China's nuclear test as a "threat to world peace" in a speech to the Indian People's Assembly on the 10th. This Indian Foreign Minister attempted to bring non-nuclear countries under the "nuclear umbrella" of the US and the Soviet Union. He said that the best guarantee for preventing nuclear proliferation is the "guarantee by the major nuclear powers - the United States and the Soviet Union - that the interests of non-nuclear countries will not be harmed by their adherence to the principles of non-proliferation."
    According to Western news agencies, the Indian representative Trivedi, who is attending the 17-nation disarmament conference in Geneva, vigorously attacked China at the meeting on the 10th, requesting the meeting to consider the impact of China's nuclear test. He expressed great anxiety about the situation in which the US and the Soviet Union's monopoly on nuclear weapons was broken. He said: "We must use our energy and determination to prevent this actual and open proliferation and deal with the fundamental problems of the current proliferation threat."
    Trivedi also yearned for the United States and the Soviet Union to reach a dirty deal on "preventing nuclear proliferation." He said, "The Soviet Union and the United States should prepare their draft treaty on the prevention of nuclear weapons proliferation so that an agreement can be reached as soon as possible when the meeting resumes."

### 印度政府不愧是美苏反华急先锋  攻击中国核爆炸乞求美苏“核保护”

1966-05-14
第5版()
专栏：

    印度政府不愧是美苏反华急先锋
    攻击中国核爆炸  乞求美苏“核保护”
    新华社十三日讯　新德里消息：充当美帝国主义和苏联修正主义反华急先锋的印度政府，一面大肆攻击中国的核试验，一面乘机替美国和苏联推销“防止核扩散”的货色，为美苏垄断核武器主宰世界的阴谋效劳。
    印度总理英迪拉·甘地夫人十日在议会上说什么中国最近的核爆炸“构成对印度的安全的严重威胁”。印度外交部长斯瓦兰·辛格十日在印度人民院讲话时，也攻击中国的核试验是“对世界和平的威胁”。这个印度外交部长妄图把非核国家纳入美苏的“核保护伞”。他说，防止核扩散的最好的保证是由“主要的核国家——美国和苏联——保证：非核国家的利益不会由于它们遵守不扩散的原则而受到损害”。
    据西方通讯社报道，正在日内瓦参加十七国裁军会议的印度代表特里维迪十日在会议上大肆攻击中国，要求会议考虑中国核试验的影响。他对美苏垄断核武器的局面被打破表示非常焦急，他说：“我们必须用我们的精力和决心来防止这种实际的、公然进行的扩散，并且处理当前扩散威胁的根本问题。”
    特里维迪还渴望美国和苏联达成“防止核扩散”的肮脏交易，他说：“苏联和美国应当把它们的防止核武器扩散条约草案准备得很完备，以便在会议复会时可以尽快达成协议。”
