Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Guests from Congo (Brazzaville); Ministry of Education and Science and Technology Association Host Banquet for Congo (Brazzaville) Science Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-14
Edition: 6

    Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Guests from Congo (Brazzaville)
    Ministry of Education and Science and Technology Association Host Banquet for Congo (Brazzaville) Science Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, 13th. This morning, Vice Premier Chen Yi received Lévi Makany, Minister of National Education of Congo (Brazzaville), and Professor Lopes Henri, and held cordial and friendly talks with them. Present at the reception were Wang Shuntong, Fang Zhi, and other responsible persons from relevant departments. Bakoula Daniel, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Congo (Brazzaville) Embassy in China, was also present.
    Xinhua News Agency, 12th. The Ministry of Education of the People's Republic of China and the National Committee of the China Association for Science and Technology jointly held a banquet on the evening of the 11th to welcome the Congo (Brazzaville) Science Delegation headed by Lévi Makany, Minister of National Education of Congo (Brazzaville), who are visiting our country. Among those attending the banquet were He Wei, Minister of Education, Zhou Peiyuan, Vice Chairman of the Science and Technology Association, Wang Shuntong, Secretary of the Secretariat, and others. ### 陈毅副总理接见刚果（布）客人  教育部和科协宴请刚果（布）科学代表团

1966-05-14
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见刚果（布）客人
    教育部和科协宴请刚果（布）科学代表团
    新华社十三日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见刚果（布）国民教育部部长列维·马卡尼和洛贝斯·亨利教授，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有王顺桐、方知等有关方面负责人。刚果（布）驻中国大使馆临时代办巴库拉·丹尼尔也在座。
    新华社十二日讯　中华人民共和国教育部和科学技术协会全国委员会十一日晚上联合举行宴会，欢迎来我国访问的以刚果（布）国民育教部部长列维·马卡尼为首的刚果（布）科学代表团。出席宴会的，有教育部部长何伟、科协副主席周培源、书记处书记王顺桐等人。（附图片）
    五月十三日，陈毅副总理接见刚果（布）国民教育部部长列维·马卡尼（右三）和洛贝斯·亨利教授（左三）。　		新华社记者　张　彬摄
