Title: The United States Intensifies Arming of Israel, Creating a Dangerous Situation in the Middle East; Arab Countries Denounce the United States for Encouraging Israeli Aggression
Author:
Date: 1966-05-27
Edition: 6

    (Xinhua News Agency) Government leaders and parliaments of Arab countries have strongly condemned the United States for recently providing Israel with a large number of weapons.

    On the evening of the 24th, UAR Prime Minister Mohieddin said in a speech to the UAR National Assembly: The UAR believes that the United States "providing offensive weapons to Israel will encourage Israel to launch more aggression against Arab countries," thereby creating a dangerous situation in the Middle East.

    He stated that the UAR government is determined to continue strengthening its armed forces to defend the country from foreign aggression.

    Following his speech, the UAR National Assembly unanimously passed a motion to send a telegram to the U.S. Congress protesting the U.S. provision of weapons to Israel.

    The Palestine Trade Union Federation issued a statement on the 21st, pointing out that this new arms deal exposes the anti-Arab policy of the U.S. ruling clique to consolidate the existence of aggressive Israel. The statement called on all revolutionary forces in the Arab world to condemn Israel and the United States for their policy of interference in the region and to strengthen the struggle against this policy.

    Syrian Defense Minister Hafez al-Assad strongly condemned on the 24th the conspiracy to make Arab countries sign "peace agreements" with Israel. He said that there is no peace between the Arab countries and Israel. The Arab people demand the recovery of the lands that have been taken away and are determined to drive out the aggressors.

    The Syrian newspaper *Al-Thawra* published an editorial calling on Arab countries to reconsider their relations with the United States, because the United States itself has confirmed that it harbors aggressive intentions towards Arab countries.

    An Iraqi Foreign Ministry spokesman told a reporter from the Iraqi News Agency on the 22nd: "The Iraqi government believes that the United States' provision of fighter jets and continued provision of heavy weapons to Israel, coupled with the large amount of economic assistance that Israel obtains from the United States in all aspects, will create a very serious situation in this region." "Therefore, the Iraqi government has no choice but to strongly condemn the arming of Israel directed by international groups. This practice will encourage Israel to continue to implement its aggressive actions and policies."

    According to a broadcast by Sana'a Radio on the 22nd, Yemeni Foreign Minister Hassan Makkie condemned Israel, fostered by U.S. imperialism, as a dagger inserted into the heart of the Arab people. He said that the U.S. provision of weapons to Israel will encourage Israel to implement aggressive policies against the Arab people.

    The Sudanese News Agency published a statement on the 24th by Khadr Hamad, a member of the Sudanese Supreme State Council, condemning the U.S. provision of weapons to Israel as a criminal act against the security of Arab countries.

    He called on Arab countries to immediately strengthen their unity to deal with this growing danger.

    The Kuwaiti National Assembly also sent a telegram to the U.S. Congress on the 24th, condemning the U.S. provision of weapons to Israel.

    (Xinhua News Agency, Cairo) The UAR newspaper *Al-Ahram* reported on the 20th that, according to the recent U.S.-Israeli arms deal, the United States has been shipping new weapons to Israel for the past two weeks.

    The newspaper said that this completely secret arms deal included 200 Patton M-48 heavy tanks, 80 F-104 fighter jets, and an unspecified number of F-111 modern fighter jets.

    According to Western news agencies, a U.S. State Department spokesman on the 19th tried to cover up the crime of the United States shipping a large number of weapons to Israel, threatening the security of Arab countries, but had to admit that the United States had agreed to provide Israel with a so-called "limited number of tactical aircraft."

### 美国加紧武装以色列造成中东危险局势  阿拉伯国家怒斥美国鼓励以色列侵略

1966-05-27
第6版()
专栏：

    美国加紧武装以色列造成中东危险局势
    阿拉伯国家怒斥美国鼓励以色列侵略
    据新华社讯　阿拉伯国家的政府领导人和议会纷纷谴责美国最近又向以色列提供大批武器。
    阿联总理毛希丁二十四日晚上在阿联国民议会讲话时说：阿联认为，美国向以色列“提供进攻性武器将鼓励以色列对阿拉伯国家发动更多侵略”，从而造成中东的危险局势。
    他表示阿联政府决心继续加强武装力量，以保卫国家免遭外国侵略。
    在他讲话以后，阿联国民议会一致通过一项动议，决定打电报给美国国会，对美国向以色列提供武器提出抗议。
    巴勒斯坦工会联合会二十一日发表声明指出：这次新的武器交易暴露了美国统治集团企图巩固侵略成性的以色列的存在的反阿拉伯政策。声明号召阿拉伯一切革命力量谴责以色列和美国在这个地区进行干涉的政策，并且加强反对这种政策的斗争。
    叙利亚国防部长哈菲兹·阿萨德二十四日强烈谴责企图使阿拉伯国家同以色列签订“和平协定”的阴谋。他说，在阿拉伯国家和以色列之间没有和平可言。阿拉伯人民要求收复被夺去的土地，决心把侵略者赶出去。
    叙利亚《革命报》发表社论要求阿拉伯国家重新考虑同美国的关系，因为美国自己证实它对阿拉伯国家怀有侵略企图。
    伊拉克外交部发言人二十二日对伊拉克通讯社记者说：“伊拉克政府认为，美国向以色列提供战斗机和继续提供重型武器加上以色列从美国各方面取得它所需要的大笔经济援助，这将在这一地区造成十分严重的局势。”“因此，伊拉克政府不得不强烈谴责由国际集团指使的武装以色列的做法。这种做法将鼓励以色列继续推行它的侵略行动和政策。”
    据萨那电台二十二日广播，也门外交部长哈桑·迈基谴责美帝国主义扶植的以色列是在阿拉伯人民心脏插入一把匕首。他说，美国向以色列提供武器将鼓励以色列对阿拉伯人民实行侵略政策。
    苏丹通讯社二十四日发表苏丹国家最高委员会委员哈德尔·哈马德的一项声明，谴责美国向以色列提供武器是针对阿拉伯国家安全的罪恶行动。
    他要求阿拉伯国家立即加强团结来对付这个日益增长的危险。
    科威特国民议会也在二十四日打电报给美国国会，谴责美国向以色列提供武器。
    据新华社开罗电　阿联《金字塔报》二十日报道，根据美国同以色列最近达成的武器交易，两周来，美国一直在向以色列运送新的武器。
    这家报纸说，这笔完全秘密的军火交易，包括两百辆巴顿式M—48重型坦克，八十架F—104战斗机和数目不详的F—111现代化的战斗机。
    另据西方通讯社报道，美国国务院发言人十九日力图掩饰美国运送大批武器给以色列威胁阿拉伯国家的安全的罪行，但不得不承认美国已经同意向以色列提供所谓“有限数目的战术飞机”。
