Title: Lao Patriotic Armed Forces and People Shoot Down a U.S. Plane
Author:
Date: 1966-05-12
Edition: 6

    New China News Agency, Khang Khay, 11th – According to a Pathet Lao Radio broadcast: Lao patriotic armed forces and people in Xieng Khouang province shot down a U.S. bandit plane on the 1st.
    On that day, U.S. imperialism and its lackeys sent many planes to bomb and strafe villages along Lao Highway 7 and the Khang Khay and Phou Kout areas. The patriotic armed forces and people of Xieng Khouang province fought back heroically, shooting down a U.S. T-28 aircraft and damaging several others.

### 老挝爱国军民击落美机一架

1966-05-12
第6版()
专栏：

    老挝爱国军民击落美机一架
    新华社康开十一日电　据巴特寮电台广播：老挝川圹省爱国军民一日打落美国强盗飞机一架。
    这天，美帝国主义和它的走狗派许多飞机轰炸和扫射老挝第七号公路沿线一带村庄和康开、富科特山地区。川圹省爱国军民英勇还击，打落美国T—28型飞机一架，打伤多架。
