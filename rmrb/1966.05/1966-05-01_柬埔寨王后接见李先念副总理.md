Title: Cambodian Queen Receives Vice Premier Li Xiannian
Author:
Date: 1966-05-01
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 29th April – Queen Sisowath Monivong Kossamak Nearireath of Cambodia received Chinese Vice Premier Li Xiannian of the State Council and Vice Director Wang Daohan of the Commission for Economic Relations with Foreign Countries, who are currently on a friendly visit to Cambodia, on the afternoon of the 29th, and held sincere and friendly talks with them.
    Vice Premier Li Xiannian conveyed to the Queen the greetings of Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Vice Premier Chen Yi. The Queen expressed her gratitude for this and wished Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Vice Premier Chen Yi good health. Present at the reception were Chinese Ambassador Chen Shuliang and his wife. (Photo attached)
    On April 27th, Cambodian Head of State Prince Sihanouk held a banquet in Phnom Penh to entertain Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People’s Republic of China. The picture shows Prince Sihanouk speaking at the banquet. Xinhua News Agency photo.

### 柬埔寨王后接见李先念副总理

1966-05-01
第6版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨王后接见李先念副总理
    新华社金边二十九日电　柬埔寨王后西索瓦·莫尼旺·哥沙曼·尼亚里丽二十九日下午接见了正在柬埔寨进行友好访问的中国国务院副总理李先念和对外经济联络委员会副主任汪道涵，并且同他们进行了诚挚友好的谈话。
    李先念副总理向王后转达了毛泽东主席、刘少奇主席、周恩来总理和陈毅副总理对王后的问候。王后为此表示感谢，并祝毛泽东主席、刘少奇主席、周恩来总理和陈毅副总理身体健康。接见时在座的有中国大使陈叔亮和夫人。（附图片）
    四月二十七日，柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王在金边举行宴会，招待中华人民共和国国务院副总理李先念。图为西哈努克亲王在宴会上讲话。　新华社发
