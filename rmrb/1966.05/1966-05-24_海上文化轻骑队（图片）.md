Title: Maritime Cultural Light Cavalry Team (Picture)
Author: Zhou, Feng, Zhang
Date: 1966-05-24
Edition: 4

    Maritime Cultural Light Cavalry Team
    The Guangzhou Military Region's Maritime Cultural Work Team is a cultural light cavalry team that resolutely implements Chairman Mao's direction for literature and art, and serves the companies and soldiers. Taking performances as the main focus, they provide comprehensive services. They are not only an artistic performance team, but also a political work team, a propaganda team, and a service team. They have been praised by the broad masses of commanders and fighters and are known as "the most welcome people."
    The People's Liberation Army Guangzhou Military Region's Maritime Cultural Work Team active on the island.
    The team members perform "Our Company Has Many Good People and Good Deeds" for the coastal defense soldiers on the beach.
    In addition to performances, the team members explain the exhibition photos to the island fishermen.
                             Photo by Zhou Fengzhang (Xinhua News Agency draft)

### 海上文化轻骑队（图片）
周冯张
1966-05-24
第4版()
专栏：

    海上文化轻骑队
    广州部队海上文化工作队，是一支坚决贯彻毛主席文艺方向，面向连队为兵服务的文化轻骑队。他们以演出为主，全面服务，既是文艺演出队，又是政治工作队、宣传队、服务队，受到了广大指战员的赞扬，被称为“最受欢迎的人”。
    活跃在海岛上的人民解放军广州部队海上文化工作队。
    队员们在海滩上给海防战士演出“咱连好人好事多又多”。
    队员们在演出之余，给海岛渔民讲解展览照片。
                             周冯张摄（新华社稿）
