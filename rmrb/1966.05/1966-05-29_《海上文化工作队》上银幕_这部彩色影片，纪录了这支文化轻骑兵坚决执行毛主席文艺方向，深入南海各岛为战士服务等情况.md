Title: The "Offshore Cultural Work Team" on the Screen: This color film records this cultural light cavalry resolutely implementing Chairman Mao's literary and artistic direction and going deep into the South China Sea islands to serve the soldiers.
Author:
Date: 1966-05-29
Edition: 4

    "Offshore Cultural Work Team" on the Screen
    This color film records this cultural light cavalry resolutely implementing Chairman Mao's literary and artistic direction and going deep into the South China Sea islands to serve the soldiers.
    Xinhua News Agency, 28th—The color documentary film "Offshore Cultural Work Team," which chronicles the Guangzhou Military Region's Offshore Cultural Work Team of the Chinese People's Liberation Army going deep into the islands to serve the soldiers, has been completed by the August First Film Studio and will be shown in the capital starting today.
    The film records the scene of Vice Premier of the State Council He Long and Tao Zhu receiving all members of the Guangzhou Military Region's Offshore Cultural Work Team, praising them for resolutely implementing Chairman Mao's literary and artistic direction, conscientiously implementing Comrade Lin Biao's instructions on literary and artistic work in the army, and inheriting and carrying forward the glorious tradition of the old Red Army propaganda team.
    The film records the situation of this offshore cultural work team, known by the soldiers as a cultural light cavalry, going deep into the South China Sea islands, enthusiastically performing for the soldiers, serving the soldiers, guiding the companies to carry out recreational activities, and actively publicizing Mao Zedong Thought; at the same time, it also records the team members' persistence in studying Chairman Mao's works, exercising and working with the soldiers, learning from the soldiers, striving to transform their world outlook, and resolutely taking the road of integration with workers, peasants, and soldiers.
    The Guangzhou Military Region's Offshore Cultural Work Team is composed of twenty-one people. Since its establishment in March of last year, they have been loyal to Mao Zedong Thought, persisted in implementing the Party's literary and artistic policy, unswervingly highlighted politics, diligently served the troops guarding the South China Sea islands of the motherland, and tempered and transformed themselves in the practice of revolutionary struggle, making remarkable achievements and being warmly welcomed by the broad masses of commanders and fighters.

### 《海上文化工作队》上银幕  这部彩色影片，纪录了这支文化轻骑兵坚决执行毛主席文艺方向，深入南海各岛为战士服务等情况

1966-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

    《海上文化工作队》上银幕
    这部彩色影片，纪录了这支文化轻骑兵坚决执行毛主席文艺方向，深入南海各岛为战士服务等情况
    新华社二十八日讯　记述中国人民解放军广州部队海上文化工作队深入海岛为兵服务的彩色纪录影片《海上文化工作队》，已经由八一电影制片厂拍摄完成，今天起在首都上映。
    影片纪录了国务院副总理贺龙、陶铸接见广州部队海上文化工作队全体队员，称赞他们坚决贯彻毛主席文艺方向，认真执行林彪同志对部队文艺工作的指示，继承和发扬老红军宣传队光荣传统的场面。
    影片纪录了这支被战士称为文化轻骑兵的海上文化工作队，深入南海各岛，热情为战士演出，为战士服务，辅导连队开展文娱活动，积极宣传毛泽东思想的情况；同时还纪录了队员们坚持学习毛主席著作，和战士同操练，同劳动，向战士学习，努力改造自己的世界观，坚决走与工农兵相结合的道路等情况。
    广州部队海上文化工作队由二十一人组成，从去年三月成立以来，他们忠于毛泽东思想，坚持贯彻执行党的文艺方针，坚定不移地突出政治，勤勤恳恳地为守卫祖国南海岛屿的部队服务，并在革命斗争实践中锻炼改造自己，作出了显著成绩，受到部队广大指战员的热烈欢迎。
