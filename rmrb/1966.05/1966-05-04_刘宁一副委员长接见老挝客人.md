Title: Vice Chairman Liu Ning Receives Laotian Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-05-04
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 3rd – Vice Chairman Liu Ning received the Lao Patriotic Forces news delegation led by Mr. Chalern Vongsayanone, Director of the “Voice of Laos” Radio Station, today and held cordial and friendly talks with them. Present at the reception were Lian Guan, Li Wu, Bi Jilong, and other responsible persons from relevant departments.

### 刘宁一副委员长接见老挝客人

1966-05-04
第5版()
专栏：

    刘宁一副委员长接见老挝客人
    新华社三日讯　刘宁一副委员长今天接见了由“老挝之声”电台台长扎伦·翁桑昂率领的老挝爱国力量新闻代表团，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有连贯、李伍、毕季龙等有关方面负责人。
