Title: Chairman Zhang Hanfu Visits Chairman of the Cambodia-China Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1966-05-04
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd dispatch: Zhang Hanfu, President of the China-Cambodia Friendship Association, visited Lan Niete, head of the delegation of the Cambodia-China Friendship Association and Chairman of the Cambodia-China Friendship Association, this afternoon. The host and guest had a cordial and friendly conversation. Ding Xilin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhou Yiping, Vice President of the China-Cambodia Friendship Association, and others were present at the meeting.
    All members of the Cambodia-China Friendship Association delegation were also present.

### 章汉夫会长拜会柬中友协主席

1966-05-04
第5版()
专栏：

    章汉夫会长拜会柬中友协主席
    新华社三日讯　中国柬埔寨友好协会会长章汉夫今天下午拜会了柬中友协代表团团长、柬中友协主席兰·涅特，宾主进行了亲切友好的谈话。拜会时在座的，有中国人民对外文化友好协会副会长丁西林、中柬友协副会长周一萍等。
    柬中友协代表团全体团员也在座。
