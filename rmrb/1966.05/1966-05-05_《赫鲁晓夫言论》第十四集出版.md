Title: 《Khrushchev's Speeches》 Volume 14 Published
Author:
Date: 1966-05-05
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 4th. "Khrushchev's Speeches," Volume 14, has been published by World Knowledge Press and is being distributed throughout the country. This volume includes 115 reports, speeches, letters, answers to reporters' questions, etc., publicly released by Khrushchev from January to March 1960.



**Named Entities**

赫鲁晓夫  Hè Lǔxiǎo fū (Khrushchev)

### 《赫鲁晓夫言论》第十四集出版

1966-05-05
第5版()
专栏：

    《赫鲁晓夫言论》第十四集出版
    新华社四日讯　《赫鲁晓夫言论》第十四集，已由世界知识出版社出版，在全国各地发行。这一集的内容，包括赫鲁晓夫在一九六○年一月至三月期间公开发表的报告、讲话、函电、答记者问等，共一百一十五篇。
