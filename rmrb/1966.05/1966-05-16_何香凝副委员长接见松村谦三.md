Title: He Xiangning, Vice Chairman, Receives Matsumura Kenzo
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-16
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, May 15th -- He Xiangning, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received Matsumura Kenzo, advisor to the Japanese Liberal Democratic Party, and his entourage this afternoon and held friendly talks with them.
    Present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi and his wife Jing Puchun, Liao Mengxing, Wang Xiaoyun, and other relevant personnel.

### 何香凝副委员长接见松村谦三

1966-05-16
第6版()
专栏：

    何香凝副委员长接见松村谦三
    新华社十五日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长何香凝，今天下午接见了日本自由民主党顾问松村谦三和他的随行人员，并且同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有廖承志和夫人经普椿、廖梦醒、王晓云等有关方面人士。
