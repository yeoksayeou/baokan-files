Title: China's Foreign Cultural Committee and Ministry of Culture Host Banquet for Tanzanian Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-05-02
Edition: 3

    According to Xinhua News Agency, the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Ministry of Culture hosted a banquet tonight in honor of the visiting Tanzanian Cultural Delegation.
    Shi Huai'bi, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Joseph Kasella Bantu, head of the delegation, spoke at the banquet. They warmly praised the mutual support and encouragement between China and Tanzania in the struggle against imperialism, old and new colonialism, and their formation of a fighting friendship. In his speech, Head of Delegation Joseph Kasella Bantu said that Tanzania insisted on maintaining its sovereignty in the anti-imperialist struggle and did not yield to imperialism. He said that they were encouraged by Chairman Mao's assertion that imperialism is heading towards its demise. He said that the sympathy of Chinese friends for their anti-imperialist and anti-colonial struggle shows that they are not alone.
    Xu Guangxiao, Vice Minister of Culture, and responsible persons from relevant departments, including Li Menghua, Gong Dafei, and Zhang Tiesheng, attended the banquet.
    Tanzanian Ambassador to China, Wazir Juma, was also invited to attend.

### 我对外文委等宴请坦桑文化代表团

1966-05-02
第3版()
专栏：

    我对外文委等宴请坦桑文化代表团
    据新华社一日讯　对外文化联络委员会和文化部今晚设宴招待来我国访问的坦桑尼亚文化代表团。
    对外文委副主任史怀璧和代表团团长约瑟夫·卡西拉·班图在宴会上讲话，他们热情称颂中坦两国在反对帝国主义、新老殖民主义的斗争中，相互支持、相互鼓舞，结成了战斗的友谊。约瑟夫·卡西拉·班图团长在讲话中说，坦桑尼亚在反帝斗争中坚持维护自己的主权，未向帝国主义屈服。他说，我们受到毛主席关于帝国主义正在走向灭亡的论断的鼓舞。他说，我们反帝反殖斗争有着中国朋友的同情，表明我们不是孤独的。
    文化部副部长徐光霄以及有关方面负责人李梦华、宫达非、张铁生等出席了宴会。
    坦桑尼亚驻中国大使瓦齐里·朱马也应邀出席。
