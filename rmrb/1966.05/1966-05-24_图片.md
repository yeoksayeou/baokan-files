Title: Picture
Author: Lu Haoqing
Date: 1966-05-24
Edition: 5

    At the invitation of the Youth League of the Tanganyika African National Union, a Chinese youth delegation visited Tanzania in early March of this year. They conveyed the deep friendship of Chinese youth to African youth. The picture shows members of the Chinese youth delegation performing the "Bow Dance," which reflects the anti-imperialist history of the Chinese people, for Tanzanian youth.
                              Xinhua News Agency reporter Lu Haoqing
