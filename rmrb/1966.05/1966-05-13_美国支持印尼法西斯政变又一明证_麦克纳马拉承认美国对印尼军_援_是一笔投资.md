Title: Another Clear Evidence of US Support for Indonesian Fascist Coup: McNamara Admits US "Aid" to Indonesian Military Was an Investment
Author:
Date: 1966-05-13
Edition: 6

    Another Clear Evidence of US Support for Indonesian Fascist Coup
    McNamara Admits US "Aid" to Indonesian Military Was an Investment
    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 12th—Washington dispatch: US Secretary of Defense McNamara on the 11th virtually admitted that the US government utilized the Indonesian reactionary military clique to carry out a fascist military coup in Indonesia.
    He was beside himself with glee when testifying before the Senate Foreign Relations Committee on the issue of US foreign "aid," saying that US military "aid" to Indonesia in the past was a good investment. He emphasized that this "aid" enabled the US to maintain "contact" with those Indonesian military leaders who "played a big role in the recent changes of government."

### 美国支持印尼法西斯政变又一明证  麦克纳马拉承认美国对印尼军“援”是一笔投资

1966-05-13
第6版()
专栏：

    美国支持印尼法西斯政变又一明证
    麦克纳马拉承认美国对印尼军“援”是一笔投资
    新华社十二日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国防部长麦克纳马拉十一日实际上承认了美国政府利用印度尼西亚反动军人集团在印度尼西亚搞法西斯军事政变。
    他在参议院外交委员会为美国对外“援助”问题作证的时候得意忘形地说，美国过去对印度尼西亚的军事“援助”是很好的投资。他强调了这种“援助”使得美国能够同那些“在最近的政府变动中起了很大作用”的印度尼西亚军事领袖保持“接触”。
