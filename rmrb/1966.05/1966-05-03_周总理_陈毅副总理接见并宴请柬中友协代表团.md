Title: Premier Zhou and Vice Premier Chen Yi Receive and Host Banquet for Cambodian-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-05-03
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, May 2nd – Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Cambodian-Chinese Friendship Association delegation headed by Lan Nete this morning. Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, and the Cambodian guests held cordial and friendly talks.
    Present at the reception were the responsible persons from various sectors: Zhang Hanfu, Chu Tunan, Ding Xilin, Zhang Qian, Song Yiping, Zhou Yiping, Wang Demao, Huang Shuze, etc.
    Prince Norodom Naradipo, who is studying in Beijing, and Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Cambodian Embassy in China, Li Jun, and his wife were also present.
    After the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi hosted a luncheon in honor of the Cambodian guests.

### 周总理　陈毅副总理接见并宴请柬中友协代表团

1966-05-03
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理　陈毅副总理接见并宴请柬中友协代表团
    新华社二日讯　国务院总理周恩来、副总理陈毅今天上午接见了以兰·涅特为首的柬埔寨中国友好协会代表团。周恩来总理、陈毅副总理和柬埔寨客人进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的各方面负责人有：章汉夫、楚图南、丁西林、张茜、宋一平、周一萍、王德茂、黄树则等。
    在北京学习的柬埔寨王子诺罗敦·纳拉迪波和柬埔寨驻中国大使馆临时代办李军及夫人也在座。
    接见以后，周恩来总理、陈毅副总理设午宴招待柬埔寨客人。
