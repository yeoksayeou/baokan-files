Title: Ceylon Trade Union Federation Delegation Leader Nadongge Delivers Speech: Chinese People Achieve Great Successes Under the Guidance of Mao Zedong Thought; Mao Zedong's Words are a Guide for the Oppressed People of Asia, Africa, and Latin America
Author:
Date: 1966-05-24
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, May 23rd -- T.N. Nadongge, the leader of the Ceylon Trade Union Federation delegation currently visiting China, told a reporter from this agency here: "Mao Zedong's words are a guide for the people and nations oppressed in Asia, Africa, Latin America, and everywhere else. Without Mao Zedong Thought, it is impossible to defeat imperialism and revisionism, and it is impossible to build socialism within China."
    He said that the Ceylon Trade Union Federation delegation and trade union delegations from Asia, Africa, and Latin America all expressed their firm and unwavering opposition to the common enemies of the people of the world—U.S. imperialism and the Soviet Communist Party leadership group, who are despicably following U.S. imperialism. Nadongge said: "We believe that in this struggle, Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought are the most correct guidance."
    Nadongge said: "The Chinese people are fully supporting the revolutionary struggles of the people of Asia, Africa, and Latin America." "The Chinese people's socialist construction is not only for China but also to support the revolutionary struggle against imperialism and modern revisionism. They understand that the revolution is not yet complete and must be carried through to the end."
    He said: "The Chinese people love peace, but if anyone wants to undermine peace, they are always ready to eliminate this force that disrupts peace. The Chinese people understand that as long as imperialism exists in the world, world peace is impossible to achieve."
    Nadongge strongly condemned the Soviet revisionist leadership group's attempt to despicably betray the Vietnamese people's liberation struggle. He said: "The Soviet revisionist leadership group acts as a broker for U.S. imperialism and plays the role of a so-called 'angel of peace.' The Soviet revisionist leadership group's claims that China is hindering Soviet aid to Vietnam are complete lies to deceive the world and to whitewash itself."
    Nadongge expressed his firm belief that the Vietnamese people's anti-American struggle for national salvation will ultimately achieve complete victory. He said: "Facts will once again prove the correctness of Mao Zedong's assertion that imperialism and all reactionaries are paper tigers, and that it is people, not weapons, that determine the outcome of war."
    Speaking about his impressions of his visit to China, Nadongge said that during this visit, he realized that whether in China's past national liberation struggle or in today's socialist revolution and socialist construction, Mao Zedong Thought has demonstrated tremendous power in all historical periods. He warmly praised China's achievements in socialist construction. He said: "The tremendous achievements made by the Chinese people without any foreign aid are fundamentally due to their reliance on Mao Zedong Thought."

### 锡兰工会联合会代表团长纳东格发表谈话  中国人民在毛泽东思想指导下获大成就  毛泽东的话是亚非拉被压迫人民的指针

1966-05-24
第5版()
专栏：

    锡兰工会联合会代表团长纳东格发表谈话
    中国人民在毛泽东思想指导下获大成就  毛泽东的话是亚非拉被压迫人民的指针
    新华社上海二十三日电　正在中国访问的锡兰工会联合会代表团团长蒂·恩·纳东格在这里对本社记者说：“毛泽东的话是亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲和一切被压迫人民和被压迫民族的指针。没有毛泽东思想就不可能战胜帝国主义和修正主义，也不可能在中国国内建设社会主义”。
    他说，锡兰工会联合会代表团和来自亚洲、非洲和拉丁美洲的工会代表团，都表示要坚定不移地反对全世界人民的共同敌人——美帝国主义和卑鄙地追随美帝国主义的苏共领导集团。纳东格说：“我们认为，在这场斗争中，马克思列宁主义、毛泽东思想是最正确的指导。”
    纳东格说：“中国人民是全力支持亚洲、非洲和拉丁美洲人民的革命斗争的。”“中国人民进行社会主义建设不仅是为了中国，而且是为了支持反对帝国主义和现代修正主义的革命斗争。他们懂得革命还没有完成，要把革命进行到底。”
    他说：“中国人民热爱和平，但是如果谁要破坏和平的话，他们就随时准备消灭这种破坏和平的力量。中国人民懂得，只要世界上还存在帝国主义，世界和平是不可能实现的。”
    纳东格强烈谴责了苏联修正主义领导集团企图卑鄙地出卖越南人民解放斗争的阴谋。他说：“苏联修正主义领导集团充当了美帝国主义的掮客，扮演了所谓‘和平天使’的角色。苏联修正主义领导集团说什么中国阻挠苏联对越南的援助，完全是欺骗世界的谎言，是为它自己涂脂抹粉。”
    纳东格表示深信，越南人民的抗美救国斗争终将获得彻底胜利。他说：“事实将再一次证明，毛泽东关于帝国主义和一切反动派都是纸老虎、决定战争胜负的是人而不是武器这一论断的正确性。”
    纳东格谈到访华观感时说，他在这次访问中体会到，不论是在中国过去的民族解放斗争中，还是今天的社会主义革命和社会主义建设中，毛泽东思想在中国各个历史时期都显示出了巨大的威力。他热烈赞扬中国社会主义建设的成就。他说：“中国人民在没有任何外国的援助下，取得的巨大成就，归根结蒂是因为他们依靠了毛泽东思想。”
