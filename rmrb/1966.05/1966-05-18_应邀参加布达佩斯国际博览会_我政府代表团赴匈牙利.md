Title: Our Government Delegation Leaves for Hungary to Attend the Budapest International Fair

Author: 
Date: 1966-05-18
Edition: 6

    In Response to Invitation to Attend Budapest International Fair
    Our Government Delegation Leaves for Hungary
    Xinhua News Agency, 16th. At the invitation of the Hungarian government, the Chinese government delegation, headed by Fan Mu Han, Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building, left Beijing by plane this morning to attend the opening ceremony of the 1966 Budapest International Fair and to visit the Fair.
    Seeing them off at the airport were Sun Youyu, Chen Boqing, Gao Shirong, Xue Tao, and diplomatic officials from the Hungarian Embassy in China.

### 应邀参加布达佩斯国际博览会  我政府代表团赴匈牙利

1966-05-18
第6版()
专栏：

    应邀参加布达佩斯国际博览会
    我政府代表团赴匈牙利
    新华社十六日讯　应匈牙利政府邀请前往参加一九六六年布达佩斯国际博览会开幕式并参观博览会的中国政府代表团，在第一机械工业部副部长范慕韩率领下，今天上午乘飞机离开北京。
    到机场送行的，有孙友余、陈伯清、高仕融、薛涛等，以及匈牙利驻华大使馆外交官员。
