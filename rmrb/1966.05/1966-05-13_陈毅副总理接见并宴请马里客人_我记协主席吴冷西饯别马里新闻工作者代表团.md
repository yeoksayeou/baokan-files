Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives and Hosts a Banquet for Guests from Mali
Author:
Date: 1966-05-13
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Chen Yi today received the delegation of the Mali Federation of Journalists led by Bakari Traore and had a friendly and cordial conversation with him.

After the reception, Vice Premier Chen Yi hosted a banquet for the Mali guests.

Those present at the reception and banquet included Wang Yi, First Secretary of the All-China Journalists Association, Hou Yefeng, Deputy Director of the African Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Chen Weifan, Deputy Director of the News Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Yang Yi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists Association. The Ambassador of Mali to China, Bilama Traore, was also present.

The All-China Journalists Association Chairman Wu Lengxi hosted a banquet this evening to see off the delegation of Mali journalists led by Bakari Traore. The delegation came to attend the recently concluded Fourth Plenary Session (Expanded) of the Secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists Association held in Beijing. Those attending the banquet included Wang Yi, First Secretary of the All-China Journalists Association, and Deng Gang, Secretary.

Also attending the banquet were Supreno (Indonesia), Manuvila (Ceylon), and Yang Yi (China), representatives of the Secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists Association. The Ambassador of Mali to China, Bilama Traore, was also invited to attend.

May 12, Vice Premier Chen Yi received the delegation of the Mali Federation of Journalists led by Bakari Traore (fifth from the right). Third from the right is the Ambassador of Mali to China, Bilama Traore.

### 陈毅副总理接见并宴请马里客人  我记协主席吴冷西饯别马里新闻工作者代表团

1966-05-13
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见并宴请马里客人
    我记协主席吴冷西饯别马里新闻工作者代表团
    新华社十二日讯　陈毅副总理今天接见了马里新闻工作者联合代表团团长巴卡里·特拉奥雷，同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见后，陈毅副总理宴请马里客人。
    接见和宴请时在座的有中华全国新闻工作者协会第一书记王揖，外交部非洲司副司长侯野烽、新闻司副司长陈维帆，亚非新闻工作者协会书记处书记杨翊等。马里驻我国大使比拉马·特拉奥雷也在座。
    新华社十二日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会主席吴冷西今天晚上举行宴会，欢送以巴卡里·特拉奥雷为首的马里新闻工作者代表团，代表团是前来出席不久前在北京召开的亚非新闻工作者协会书记处第四次全体会议（扩大）的。参加宴会的有中华全国新闻工作者协会第一书记王揖和书记邓岗等。
    参加宴会的还有亚非新闻工作者协会书记处代表苏佩诺（印尼）、曼努维拉（锡兰）和杨翊（中国）。马里驻中国大使比拉马·特拉奥雷也应邀出席。（附图片）
    五月十二日，陈毅副总理接见马里新闻工作者联合代表团团长巴卡里·特拉奥雷（右五）。右三是马里驻华大使比拉马·特拉奥雷。
                    新华社记者　郑小箴摄
