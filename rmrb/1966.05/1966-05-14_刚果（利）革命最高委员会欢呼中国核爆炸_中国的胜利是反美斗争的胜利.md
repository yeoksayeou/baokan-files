Title: Congo (Kinshasa) Revolutionary Supreme Council Hails China's Nuclear Explosion; China's Victory is a Victory in the Anti-American Struggle
Author:
Date: 1966-05-14
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 12th May – The Revolutionary Supreme Council of Congo (Kinshasa) sent a telegram to the Chinese Embassy here, congratulating China on the successful nuclear explosion containing thermonuclear materials, and hailed this victory as a victory in the struggle against American imperialism.
    The telegram said: "The Revolutionary Supreme Council of Congo (Kinshasa) has learned with joy of the successful nuclear explosion containing thermonuclear materials by the fraternal Chinese people. We take this opportunity to express our congratulations to the Chinese people, the government, and the Communist Party, who have always been loyal to the principles of Chairman Mao Zedong."
    The telegram said: "The Congolese people hail this new victory and regard it as their own victory, which is also a victory in the struggle against American imperialism, for peace and for the progress of all peoples."

### 刚果（利）革命最高委员会欢呼中国核爆炸  中国的胜利是反美斗争的胜利

1966-05-14
第5版()
专栏：

    刚果（利）革命最高委员会欢呼中国核爆炸
    中国的胜利是反美斗争的胜利
    新华社开罗十二日电　刚果（利）革命最高委员会打电报给这里的中国大使馆，祝贺中国成功地进行了含有热核材料的核爆炸，并欢呼这一胜利是反对美帝国主义斗争的胜利。
    电报说：“刚果（利）革命最高委员会怀着喜悦的心情获悉兄弟的中国人民的含有热核材料的核爆炸成功。我们趁这个机会向始终忠于毛泽东主席原则的中国人民、政府和共产党表示祝贺。”
    电报说：“刚果人民欢呼这一新胜利，并且把它当作自己的胜利，这也就是反对美帝国主义、争取和平和各国人民进步的斗争的胜利。”
