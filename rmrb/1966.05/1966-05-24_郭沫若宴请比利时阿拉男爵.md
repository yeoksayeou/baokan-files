Title: Guo Moruo Hosts a Banquet for Belgian Baron Alain

Author: 
Date: 1966-05-24
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 23rd: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and his wife, Yu Liqun, received and hosted a banquet tonight for Baron Anton Alain, Chairman of the Belgian Action for Peace and Independence of All Peoples and the Belgian Committee in Support of the South Vietnamese People, and his wife. The hosts and guests held cordial and friendly talks.
    Among those attending the reception and banquet were Nan Hanchen and his wife, as well as Song Yiping, Tang Mingzhao, Li Chuwen, and other responsible persons from relevant departments.

### 郭沫若宴请比利时阿拉男爵

1966-05-24
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若宴请比利时阿拉男爵
    新华社二十三日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若和夫人于立群，今晚接见并设宴招待比利时争取和平与各国人民独立行动和比利时声援越南南方人民委员会主席安东·阿拉男爵和夫人。宾主进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    参加接见和宴会的有南汉宸和夫人以及宋一平、唐明照、李储文等有关方面负责人。
