Title: Our Table Tennis Team's First Match in Cambodia

Author:

Date: 1966-05-10
Edition: 5

    Our Table Tennis Team's First Match in Cambodia
    (Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 9th) The Chinese Men's Table Tennis Team, which is in Cambodia for a friendly visit, played its first match here last night against the Royal Cambodian Armed Forces Men's Table Tennis Team. The Chinese team won 5-1. Nearly six thousand spectators watched the match.
    Lieutenant Colonel Buo Sen An, Director of the Technical Bureau of the Cambodian Sports Bureau and Chairman of the Executive Committee of the First Asian Emerging Forces Games, and Chen Shuliang, the Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia, attended and watched the match. An opening ceremony was held before the match began.
    The Chinese players who played were Zhuang Zedong, Ma Jinbao, and Wu Xiaoming. In the six games, the home team's Jiang Chunan defeated Ma Jinbao 2-0 (21-15, 24-22); in the remaining games, the Chinese players won 2-0.
    After the match, Lin Huiqing and Di Qianghua, Zhuang Zedong and Zhang Xielin performed exhibition matches, which were warmly welcomed by the audience.

### 我乒乓球队在柬埔寨首次比赛

1966-05-10
第5版()
专栏：

    我乒乓球队在柬埔寨首次比赛
    新华社金边九日电　应邀到柬埔寨进行友好访问的中国男子乒乓球队，八日晚上在这里同柬埔寨王家武装部队男子乒乓球队进行了首次比赛，中国队以五比一获胜。将近六千名观众观看了这场比赛。
    柬埔寨体育局主管技术局长兼第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会执行委员会主席卜森安中校和中国驻柬埔寨大使陈叔亮出席观看了比赛。比赛开始前举行了开幕式。
    中国队上场的运动员是庄则栋、马金豹和吴小明。在六盘比赛中，主队江楚南以二比○胜马金豹（二十一比十五，二十四比二十二）；其余各盘比赛，中国选手都以二比○获胜。
    比赛结束后，林慧卿和狄蔷华、庄则栋和张燮林进行了表演赛，受到了观众的热烈欢迎。
