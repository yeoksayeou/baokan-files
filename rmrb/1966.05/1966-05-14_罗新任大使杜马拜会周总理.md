Title: Romanian Newly Appointed Ambassador Duma Pays Courtesy Call on Premier Zhou

Author:
Date: 1966-05-14
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, May 13th – The newly appointed Romanian Ambassador to China, Aurel Duma, paid a courtesy call on Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai this afternoon. Premier Zhou held a friendly conversation with him.

### 罗新任大使杜马拜会周总理

1966-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    罗新任大使杜马拜会周总理
    新华社十三日讯　罗马尼亚新任驻华大使奥列尔·杜马，今天下午拜会国务院总理周恩来。周总理同他进行了友好的谈话。
