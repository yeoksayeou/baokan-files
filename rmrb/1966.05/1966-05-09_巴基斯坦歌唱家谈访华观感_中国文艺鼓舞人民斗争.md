Title: Pakistani Singer Discusses Impressions of China Visit: Chinese Art and Literature Inspires People's Struggle
Author:
Date: 1966-05-09
Edition: 5

    Pakistani Singer Discusses Impressions of China Visit
    Chinese Art and Literature Inspires People's Struggle
    According to Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi: Pakistani renowned singer, Mrs. Firdousi, when talking to Pakistani journalists about her impressions of China and Chinese revolutionary culture and art, said: "The great Chinese people's culture and art are entirely for liberating the suffering people, freeing them from all forms of exploitation, oppression, and suppression."
    Mrs. Firdousi was a member of the Pakistani cultural and art delegation that recently visited China. After returning to her country, she spoke to the Pakistan Press Association about her impressions of China. She said that the aim of Chinese artists is to inspire people to be mentally prepared to become strong defenders against the evil forces of imperialism and colonialism. She said that Chinese culture and art are not only a means of entertainment but also a necessary means for mankind to get rid of all evil forces. She also said: "The Chinese people are a courageous nation. They are committed to creating their own destiny and are completely opposed to imperialism and neo-colonialist forces." (Attached picture)
    The picture shows the scene of the Chinese Folk Art Troupe's performance in Mauritania, where they gathered happily with local youths. Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Xianquan

### 巴基斯坦歌唱家谈访华观感  中国文艺鼓舞人民斗争

1966-05-09
第5版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦歌唱家谈访华观感
    中国文艺鼓舞人民斗争
    据新华社拉瓦尔品第电　巴基斯坦著名歌唱家菲尔杜西夫人在向巴基斯坦记者发表访华观感谈到中国的革命的文化艺术时说：“伟大的中国人民的文化艺术完全是为了使苦难的人们得到解放，使他们摆脱一切形式的剥削、压迫和镇压。”
    菲尔杜西夫人是最近访问中国的巴基斯坦文化艺术团的团员，她在回国后向巴基斯坦报联社记者发表了访华观感。她说，中国的艺术家要达到的目的是：鼓舞人们作好精神准备，成为对付帝国主义和殖民主义恶势力的坚强卫士。她说，中国的文化艺术不仅是娱乐的手段，同时是使人类摆脱一切邪恶势力所必需的手段。她还说：“中国人民是一个勇敢的民族，他们正致力于创造自己的命运，并且完全反对帝国主义和新殖民主义势力。”（附图片）
