Title: Vice President Karume Receives Chinese Experts
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-11
Edition: 6

　　新华社桑给巴尔九日电　坦桑尼亚联合共和国第一副总统卡鲁姆九日在这里接见了一批中国专家，并且同他们进行了友好的谈话。这些修配农具和种植甘蔗和烟草的中国专家是最近来到这里帮助桑给巴尔发展农业的。接见时在座的有桑给巴尔农业部长莫约和中国驻桑给巴尔领事刘干。
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Zanzibar, 9th - First Vice President Karume of the United Republic of Tanzania received a group of Chinese experts here on the 9th and had a friendly conversation with them. These Chinese experts, who repair agricultural tools and plant sugarcane and tobacco, recently came here to help Zanzibar develop agriculture. Present at the reception were Minister of Agriculture of Zanzibar, Moyo, and Chinese Consul in Zanzibar, Liu Gan.
