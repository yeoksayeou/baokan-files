Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Belgian Baron Alain

Author: 
Date: 1966-05-23
Edition: 6

    Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Belgian Baron Alain
    Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd—Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi received Baron Anton Alain, Chairman of the Belgian Action for Peace and Independence of All Peoples and the Belgian Committee for Support of the South Vietnamese People, and his wife this evening and had a friendly conversation with them. Present at the reception were Yang Ji and other relevant responsible comrades. (Attached picture)
    May 22nd, Vice Premier Chen Yi received Baron Anton Alain, Chairman of the Belgian Action for Peace and Independence of All Peoples and the Belgian Committee for Support of the South Vietnamese People, and his wife.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian photographed.

### 陈毅副总理接见比利时阿拉男爵

1966-05-23
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见比利时阿拉男爵
    新华社二十二日讯　陈毅副总理兼外交部长今晚接见了比利时争取和平与各国人民独立行动和比利时声援越南南方人民委员会主席安东·阿拉男爵和他的夫人，并同他们进行了友好的谈话。接见时在座的有杨骥等有关方面负责人。（附图片）
    五月二十二日，陈毅副总理接见比利时争取和平与各国人民独立行动和比利时声援越南南方人民委员会主席安东·阿拉男爵和夫人。　
    新华社记者　杜修贤摄
