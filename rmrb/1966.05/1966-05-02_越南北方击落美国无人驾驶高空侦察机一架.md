Title: North Vietnam Shoots Down a U.S. Unmanned High-Altitude Reconnaissance Aircraft

Author:

Date: 1966-05-02
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 30th – It was announced here that the military and civilians of Ha Tay Province in North Vietnam shot down a trespassing U.S. unmanned high-altitude reconnaissance aircraft on the afternoon of April 30th. This is the nineteenth U.S. unmanned high-altitude reconnaissance aircraft shot down by the military and civilians of North Vietnam.

### 越南北方击落美国无人驾驶高空侦察机一架

1966-05-02
第3版()
专栏：

    越南北方击落美国无人驾驶高空侦察机一架
    新华社河内三十日电　据这里公布，越南北方河西省军民四月三十日下午击落入侵的美国无人驾驶高空侦察机一架。这是越南北方军民击落的第十九架美国无人驾驶高空侦察机。
