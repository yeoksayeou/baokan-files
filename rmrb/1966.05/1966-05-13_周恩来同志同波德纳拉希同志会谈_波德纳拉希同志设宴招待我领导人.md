Title: Comrade Zhou Enlai Holds Talks with Comrade Bodnăraș; Comrade Bodnăraș Hosts Banquet for Our Leaders
Author:
Date: 1966-05-13
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 12th. Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee and Premier of the State Council, held talks today with Emil Bodnăraș, head of the Romanian Party and Government Delegation, member of the Executive Committee of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee, member of the Standing Presidium of the Central Committee, and First Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers, who arrived in Beijing after visiting Vietnam.
    The talks were held in a comradely and friendly atmosphere.
    Participating in the talks on the Chinese side were Chen Yi and Li Xiannian, members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premiers of the State Council, Zhao Yimin, alternate member of the CPC Central Committee, Qiao Guanhua, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and others.
    Participating in the talks on the Romanian side were Paul Niculescu-Mizil, member of the Executive Committee of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee and Secretary of the Central Secretariat, Aurel Duma, Romanian Ambassador to China, General Ion Ioniță, member of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee and Deputy Minister of the Armed Forces, Vasile Vlad, alternate member of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee and Director of the Central International Department, and Vasile Gliga, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs.
    Xinhua News Agency, 12th. Emil Bodnăraș, head of the Romanian Party and Government Delegation, member of the Executive Committee of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee, member of the Standing Presidium of the Central Committee, and First Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers, hosted a banquet at the Romanian Embassy in China tonight to entertain our leaders.
    Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee and Premier of the State Council, Chen Yi and Li Xiannian, members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premiers of the State Council, as well as Zhao Yimin, Qiao Guanhua, Lu Ping, and responsible persons from relevant departments, were invited to attend the banquet.
    Comrade Bodnăraș and Comrade Zhou Enlai successively proposed toasts at the banquet, proposing a toast to the profound friendship and cooperation between the two parties, the two countries, and the two peoples of Romania and China.
    Members of the Romanian Party and Government Delegation and Aurel Duma, the Romanian Ambassador to China, attended the banquet.

### 周恩来同志同波德纳拉希同志会谈  波德纳拉希同志设宴招待我领导人

1966-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来同志同波德纳拉希同志会谈
    波德纳拉希同志设宴招待我领导人
    新华社十二日讯　中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来，同访问越南以后来到北京的罗马尼亚党政代表团团长、罗马尼亚共产党中央执行委员会委员、中央常设主席团委员、部长会议第一副主席埃米尔·波德纳拉希，今天举行了会谈。
    会谈是在同志式的友好的气氛中进行的。
    中国方面参加会谈的，有中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅、李先念，中共中央候补委员赵毅敏，外交部副部长乔冠华等。
    罗马尼亚方面参加会谈的，有罗马尼亚共产党中央执行委员会委员、中央书记处书记保罗·尼古列斯库—米齐尔，罗马尼亚驻中国大使奥列尔·杜马，罗马尼亚共产党中央委员、武装部队部副部长扬·约尼策将军，罗马尼亚共产党中央候补委员、中央国际部部长瓦西里·弗拉德，外交部副部长瓦西里·格利加。
    新华社十二日讯　罗马尼亚党政代表团团长、罗马尼亚共产党中央执行委员会委员、中央常设主席团委员、部长会议第一副主席埃米尔·波德纳拉希，今晚在罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆举行宴会，招待我国领导人。
    中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来，中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅、李先念，以及赵毅敏、乔冠华、陆平等有关方面的负责人，应邀出席了宴会。
    波德纳拉希同志和周恩来同志在宴会上先后祝酒，他们提议为罗中两党、两国和两国人民的深厚友谊和合作而干杯。
    罗马尼亚党政代表团团员和罗马尼亚驻中国大使奥列尔·杜马，出席宴会作陪。
