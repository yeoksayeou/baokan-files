Title: Not Ignorance

Author:

Date: 1966-05-04
Edition: 5

    In his written speech on May Day, the head of the Indonesian right-wing military clique, Suharto, spoke at length about the so-called struggle of the workers. He shamelessly shouted: "The direction of our struggle is still to the left."

    In Gogol's *Dead Souls*, there is a twelve- or thirteen-year-old girl who shows Chichikov the way, and she can't even distinguish between left and right. That is due to ignorance. But for this forty-five-year-old general to call right left is simply shameless.

### 并不是无知

1966-05-04
第5版()
专栏：三言两语

    并不是无知
    印度尼西亚右派军人集团的头子苏哈托在“五一”节发表书面讲话，大谈所谓工人的斗争。他居然毫不脸红地叫喊说：“我们斗争的方向仍然是左的。”
    在果戈里的《死魂灵》中，有一个给乞乞科夫带路的十二三岁的小女孩，左右也分不清。那是由于无知。这个四十五岁的将军把右说成是左，却是无耻。
