Title: President Touré Warmly Congratulates Our Country on the Success of the Third Nuclear Test, Praises China's Declaration of Not Being the First to Use Nuclear Weapons as Proof of China's Sincerity for Peace
Author:
Date: 1966-05-13
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 12th. Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Premier Zhou Enlai received a telegram on the 12th from Guinean President Sékou Touré, warmly congratulating China on the successful nuclear explosion containing thermonuclear materials. The full text of the telegram is as follows:
    To His Excellency Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the People's Republic of China, Beijing,
    To His Excellency Premier Zhou Enlai:

    We are delighted to learn of the success of China's nuclear explosion, a new achievement in science. We extend our warm congratulations to the friendly Chinese people, and especially to the Chinese scientists, and commend their efforts to maintain world peace. We are particularly moved by your reiteration of the proposal for a world conference on the complete prohibition of nuclear and thermonuclear weapons. Your declaration of never being the first to use such weapons is to the honor of the Chinese people and proves your sincerity for peace.

    With highest regards.

                        Ahmed Sékou Touré
              Conakry, May 11, 1966

### 杜尔总统热烈祝贺我国第三次核试验成功  赞扬中国不首先使用核武器的声明是中国和平诚意的证明

1966-05-13
第5版()
专栏：

    杜尔总统热烈祝贺我国第三次核试验成功
    赞扬中国不首先使用核武器的声明是中国和平诚意的证明
    新华社十二日讯　刘少奇主席和周恩来总理十二日收到几内亚总统塞古·杜尔热烈祝贺中国成功地进行了含有热核材料的核爆炸的电报。电报全文如下：北京中华人民共和国刘少奇主席阁下，周恩来总理阁下：
    我们衷心高兴地获悉中国核爆炸成功，在科学上取得的新的成就。我们向友好的中国人民，特别向中国科学家们，表示热烈的祝贺，并且赞扬他们为维护世界和平而作出的努力。你们重申关于召开全面禁止核武器和热核武器的世界会议的建议，使我们格外感动。你们关于绝不首先使用这种武器的声明，是中国人民的荣誉，并且证明了你们的和平诚意。
    顺致崇高的敬意。
                        艾哈迈德·塞古·杜尔
              一九六六年五月十一日于科纳克里
