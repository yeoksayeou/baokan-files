Title: Vice Premier Bo Receives Albanian Handicraft Cooperative Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-03
Edition: 1

    Vice Premier Bo Yibo received the entire Albanian Handicraft Cooperative Delegation this morning, led by Kristo Çermeko, Chairman of the Central Union of Albanian Handicraft Cooperatives, and held cordial and friendly talks with them.
    Present at the meeting were Chen Yifan, Vice Minister of the Second Ministry of Light Industry and Director of the All-China Federation of Handicraft Cooperatives, and others.
    Also present was Meleqi Babani, Counselor of the Albanian Embassy in China.

### 薄副总理接见阿尔巴尼亚手工业合作社代表团

1966-05-03
第1版()
专栏：

    薄副总理接见阿尔巴尼亚手工业合作社代表团
    新华社二日讯　薄一波副总理今天上午接见了由阿尔巴尼亚手工业合作社中央联合会主席克里斯托·赛梅尔科率领的阿尔巴尼亚手工业合作社代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有第二轻工业部副部长、中华全国手工业合作总社主任陈一帆等。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使馆参赞梅莱契·巴巴尼也在座。
