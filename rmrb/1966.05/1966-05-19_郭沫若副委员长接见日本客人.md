Title: Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the NPC Standing Committee, Receives Japanese Guests
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-19
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 18th -- Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, this afternoon received all members of the Japanese Okayama Economic Friendship Delegation led by Yoshio Kono and held a friendly conversation with them.
    Xiao Fangzhou was present at the reception.
    Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji, who is in Beijing, was also present.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人

1966-05-19
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人
    新华社十八日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若今天下午接见由鸿上芳雄率领的日本冈山经济友好代表团全体成员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有萧方洲。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也在座。
