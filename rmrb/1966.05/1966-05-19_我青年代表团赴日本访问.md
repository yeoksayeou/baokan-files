Title: Chinese Youth Delegation Visits Japan
Author:
Date: 1966-05-19
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency: At the invitation of the Japan Welcome Chinese Youth Delegation Executive Committee, the Chinese Youth Delegation, consisting of twelve members led by Qian Dawei, Vice Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, left Beijing for Japan on the 15th for a friendly visit. Those who saw them off included Hu Qili, Vice Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, Wen Chi, Director of the China-Japan Friendship Association, and Huang Bocheng, Vice Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation.

### 我青年代表团赴日本访问

1966-05-19
第5版()
专栏：

    我青年代表团赴日本访问
    新华社讯　应日本欢迎中国青年代表团实行委员会邀请，去日本进行友好访问的中国青年代表团一行十二人，在中华全国青年联合会副主席钱大卫率领下，十五日离开北京去日本。前往送行的有全国青联副主席胡启立、中日友协理事文迟、全国学联副主席黄伯诚等。
