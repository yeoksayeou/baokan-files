Title: Nkrumah Sends Congratulatory Telegram on China's Great Achievement in Thermonuclear Matters
Author:
Date: 1966-05-16
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 15th -- Chairman of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi and Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai received identical telegrams from Kwame Nkrumah on May 13th from Conakry, congratulating China on the success of its nuclear explosion containing thermonuclear materials. The text of the telegram is as follows:
      "I have just learned of the great achievements of the Chinese people and scientists in nuclear and thermonuclear matters. I extend my heartfelt congratulations to the Chinese government and people through Your Excellencies. We Africans understand and appreciate the decision of the People's Republic of China never to use nuclear weapons unless for self-defense. China's reaffirmation of its willingness to convene a world conference aimed at the abolition and elimination of all nuclear weapons is a correct action. I wish the Chinese people and government continuous success in all aspects."

### 恩克鲁玛电贺我国在热核方面的伟大成就

1966-05-16
第5版()
专栏：

    恩克鲁玛电贺我国在热核方面的伟大成就
    新华社十五日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇和国务院总理周恩来收到了克瓦米·恩克鲁玛五月十三日从科纳克里分别打给他们的内容相同的电报，祝贺中国含有热核材料的核爆炸成功。电文如下：
      “我刚得悉中国人民和科学家在核和热核方面的伟大成就。我通过阁下向中国政府和人民表示衷心的祝贺。我们非洲人理解和欣赏中华人民共和国关于除非为了自卫永远不使用核武器的决定。中国重申愿意召开一次旨在废除和消灭一切核武器的世界会议是一个正确的行动。我祝中国人民和政府在各方面不断取得成就。”
