Title: Chinese Government Trade Delegation Leaves the UAR for Zambia
Author:
Date: 1966-05-12
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 10th. The Chinese Government Trade Delegation, led by Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, concluded its friendly visit to the UAR and left here by plane on the 10th for Zambia.
    Hamdi, State Secretary of the Ministry of Economy and Foreign Trade of the UAR, and Huang Hua, Chinese Ambassador to the UAR, saw the delegation off at the airport.
    During its visit here, the delegation signed the trade protocol for this year with the UAR.
    The delegation visited factories and industrial facilities in Cairo, Alexandria, and Port Said, as well as historical sites in Luxor.
    When the delegation visited Port Said, Mayor Mohammed Farid Doura held a luncheon in its honor, with Talat Khairy, State Minister in charge of youth affairs, also present.

### 我政府贸易代表团离阿联赴赞比亚

1966-05-12
第5版()
专栏：

    我政府贸易代表团离阿联赴赞比亚
    新华社开罗十日电　由对外贸易部副部长雷任民率领的中国政府贸易代表团结束了对阿联的友好访问，十日乘飞机离开这里前往赞比亚。
    阿联经济和外贸部国务秘书哈姆迪和中国驻阿联大使黄华到机场为代表团送行。
    代表团在这里访问期间，同阿联签订了本年度的贸易议定书。
    代表团参观了开罗、亚历山大和塞得港的工厂和工业设施以及卢克苏尔的古迹。
    代表团在塞得港访问时，市长穆罕默德·法里德·杜兰曾设午宴招待，负责青年事务的国务部长塔拉特·哈伊里也在座。
