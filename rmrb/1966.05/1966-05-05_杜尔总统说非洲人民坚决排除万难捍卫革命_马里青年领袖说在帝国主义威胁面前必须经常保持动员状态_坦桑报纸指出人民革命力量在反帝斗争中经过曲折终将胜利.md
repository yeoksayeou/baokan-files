Title: President Touré Says African People Resolutely Overcome All Difficulties to Defend the Revolution; Malian Youth Leader Says Constant Mobilization Is Necessary in the Face of Imperialist Threats; Tanzanian Newspaper Points Out That People's Revolutionary Forces Will Ultimately Triumph in the Anti-Imperialist Struggle After Twists and Turns
Author:
Date: 1966-05-05
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, 3rd – Guinean President Sékou Touré said at a mass rally here on May Day that the Guinean people will forever oppose the imperialists.

    The Guinean newspaper *Liberté* published President Touré's speech on the 3rd.

    He condemned the neocolonialist conspiracies in Africa. He pointed out that Britain is attempting to undermine the dignity of the Zimbabwean people by negotiating with the Smith racist regime in Southern Rhodesia.

    President Touré pointed out: “However, revolutionary Africa will inevitably overcome all difficulties and triumph over injustice.” He said: "We must prepare ourselves everywhere to strive for the establishment of a free and dignified Africa."

    President Touré called on the Guinean working people to continue to be vigilant in order to defend the revolution.

    Xinhua News Agency, Dar es Salaam – The Tanzanian newspaper *The Nationalist* published an article on the 26th on the occasion of the second anniversary of the founding of the United Republic of Tanzania, saying that the people's revolutionary forces will ultimately triumph in the struggle against the imperialist counter-revolutionary forces after twists, turns, setbacks, and temporary failures. This is the trend of history.

    The article said that today in Africa and Asia, "the people are carrying out revolutions to turn the old into the new, while imperialism is instigating counter-revolutions to turn the new back into the old."

    The article pointed out that today the imperialists are plotting counter-revolutionary conspiracies to resist any fundamental changes that benefit the people and are attempting to continue to make Asian and African countries their colonies.

    The article condemned neocolonialism, saying that the clearer the people understand the true meaning of neocolonialism, the more they hope for fundamental changes that benefit the people of Asian and African countries.

    The article pointed out that the interests of the people and the interests of the imperialists "are completely opposed. It is an illusion to think that these two interests can coexist."

    Xinhua News Agency, Bamako – Gabou Diawara, a member of the National Political Bureau of the Youth Committee of the Sudanese Union of Mali, recently called on Malian youth to be vigilant against imperialist conspiracies in Africa at the first national conference of Malian youth. He pointed out that imperialism has launched a widespread offensive in Africa, attempting to make a comeback and damage the young independent countries.

    He called on Malian youth to mobilize and actively participate in the struggle for agricultural production to defend the revolutionary achievements of the country. He said that the lesson of Ghana must attract the attention of the conscious people of African countries. The despicable coup against President Nkrumah allowed imperialist forces to re-enter Ghana. He emphasized that if we want to survive, we must constantly remain mobilized. In the face of such threats, we will never retreat. The greater the threat, the more determined we must be to overcome and defeat imperialist conspiracies.

### 杜尔总统说非洲人民坚决排除万难捍卫革命  马里青年领袖说在帝国主义威胁面前必须经常保持动员状态  坦桑报纸指出人民革命力量在反帝斗争中经过曲折终将胜利

1966-05-05
第6版()
专栏：

    杜尔总统说非洲人民坚决排除万难捍卫革命
    马里青年领袖说在帝国主义威胁面前必须经常保持动员状态
    坦桑报纸指出人民革命力量在反帝斗争中经过曲折终将胜利
    新华社科纳克里三日电　几内亚总统塞古·杜尔一日在这里举行的“五一”节群众大会上说：几内亚人民将永远反对帝国主义者。
    几内亚《自由报》三日刊载了杜尔总统的演说。
    他谴责新殖民主义在非洲的阴谋活动。他指出，英国企图通过同南罗得西亚的史密斯种族主义政权谈判来破坏津巴布韦人民的尊严。
    杜尔总统指出：“但是，革命的非洲必将排除万难，战胜非正义行为”。他说：“我们必须在各个地方，为争取建立一个自由和有尊严的非洲作好准备。”
    杜尔总统要求几内亚劳动人民为了捍卫革命继续保持警惕。
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆电　坦桑尼亚《民族主义者报》二十六日就坦桑尼亚联合共和国成立两周年发表文章说，人民的革命力量在同帝国主义反革命力量斗争中，经过曲折、挫折和暂时的失败，最终将取得胜利。这是历史的潮流。
    文章说，今天在非洲和亚洲，“人民进行革命使旧的变成新的，而帝国主义策动反革命使新的又变成旧的。”
    文章指出，今天帝国主义者策划反革命阴谋来抵抗任何有利于人民的根本变革，并企图使亚非国家继续成为他们的殖民地。
    文章谴责新殖民主义说，人民对新殖民主义的真正意义了解越清楚，他们就越希望发生有利于亚非国家人民的根本变革。
    文章指出，人民的利益和帝国主义者的利益“是完全对立的。如果以为这两种利益可以共同相处，那是幻想。”
    据新华社巴马科电　马里苏丹联盟党全国政治局青年委员加布·迪亚瓦拉最近在马里青年第一次全国会议上号召马里青年对帝国主义在非洲的阴谋提高警惕。他指出，帝国主义又在非洲发动了广泛的攻势，企图卷土重来，损害年轻的独立国家。
    他号召马里青年动员起来，积极参加农业生产的斗争，保卫国家的革命成果。他说，加纳的教训必须引起有觉悟的非洲各国人民的注意。对恩克鲁玛总统进行的卑鄙政变，使得帝国主义势力重新侵入了加纳。他强调指出，如果我们要生存，我们就必须经常保持动员状态。在这样的威胁面前，我们决不后退。威胁越大，我们战胜和击败帝国主义阴谋的决心就必须更加坚定。
