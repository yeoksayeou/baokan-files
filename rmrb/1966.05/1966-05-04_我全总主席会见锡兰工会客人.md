Title: Chairman of All-China Federation of Trade Unions Meets Ceylonese Trade Union Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-05-04
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 3rd. Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, today met with the Ceylon Trade Union Federation delegation headed by T.N. Nadarajah, who came to China to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations, and the Ceylon Trade Union Federation visiting delegation headed by D. Henry.


**Named Entities**


中华全国总工会   Zhonghua Quanguo Zonggonghui

蒂·恩·纳东格  Ti. En. Nadongge
迪·亨利  Di. Henry

### 我全总主席会见锡兰工会客人

1966-05-04
第5版()
专栏：

    我全总主席会见锡兰工会客人
    新华社三日讯　中华全国总工会主席刘宁一，今天会见了来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝活动的以蒂·恩·纳东格为首的锡兰工会联合会代表团和以迪·亨利为首的锡兰工会联合会访华代表团。
