Title: Vice Premier Chen Receives Two Philippine Delegations; All-China Journalists Association Holds Reception to Welcome Philippine Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-05-10
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Philippine Trade Delegation headed by Hermenegildo B. Marcial this morning and had a friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception was Yong Longgui, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and others.
    Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Philippine News Delegation led by Jose L. Guevara, columnist for the Manila Times, this morning and answered questions they raised.
    Present at the reception were Li Wu, Zhang Linsheng, and others.
    Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The All-China Journalists Association held a reception this evening to welcome the Philippine News Delegation led by Jose L. Guevara, columnist for the Manila Times, and the Philippine Trade Delegation headed by Hermenegildo B. Marcial.
    Wang Yunsheng, Vice Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, and Jose L. Guevara spoke at the reception.
    Among those attending the reception were Deng Gang, Li Wu, Wang Yinpu and other responsible persons from relevant departments.

### 陈副总理接见菲律宾两代表团  我记协举行招待会欢迎菲客人

1966-05-10
第5版()
专栏：

    陈副总理接见菲律宾两代表团
    我记协举行招待会欢迎菲客人
    新华社九日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见以赫内罗索·马西亚尔为首的菲律宾贸易考察代表团，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席勇龙桂等。
    据新华社九日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见由《马尼拉时报》专栏作家何塞·克鲁斯率领的菲律宾新闻代表团，回答了他们提出的问题。
    接见时在座的有李伍、张林生等。
    新华社九日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会今晚举行招待会，欢迎以《马尼拉时报》专栏作家何塞·克鲁斯为首的菲律宾新闻代表团，以赫内罗索·马西亚尔为首的菲律宾贸易考察代表团。
    中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席王芸生和何塞·克鲁斯先后在招待会上讲话。
    出席招待会的，有邓岗、李伍、王荫圃等有关方面负责人。
