Title: Vietnamese North and South People's Organizations Wire Congratulations on China's Nuclear Test: China's Achievements Inspire the Vietnamese People's Anti-American Struggle
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-16
Edition: 5

    According to Xinhua News Agency, May 15th: After our country successfully conducted a nuclear test containing thermonuclear materials, our people's organizations received many congratulatory telegrams from people's organizations in the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and South Vietnam. The State Commission for Science and Technology of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam also sent a telegram to the Chinese Academy of Sciences congratulating it on the success of this nuclear test.
    Hoang Quoc Viet, Chairman of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor, said in his congratulatory telegram that this is an extremely great victory for the Chinese working class and people, a brilliant achievement of the correct leadership of the Chinese Communist Party headed by the esteemed Chairman Mao Zedong, an unparalleled important contribution to strengthening the revolutionary forces of the world, a serious blow to the war policy and nuclear blackmail attempts of US imperialism and its lackeys, and an outstanding contribution to defending peace in Southeast Asia and the world.
    The telegram from the Vietnam Women's Union stated that this nuclear test is a great victory for the Chinese people under the wise leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao in strengthening the country's national defense forces.
    The telegram from the Central Committee of the Vietnam Labor Youth League, the Vietnam Youth Federation, and the Vietnam Student Union stated that this new achievement greatly inspires the national liberation movements in Asia, Africa, and Latin America.
    The telegram from the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace and the Vietnam Asian-African People's Solidarity Committee stated that this new and great achievement by China in the development of nuclear weapons is a great inspiration to the Vietnamese people who are resolutely fighting against US imperialist aggression with the thorough support and full assistance of the increasingly powerful fraternal Chinese people. It strengthens our belief that we will surely achieve final victory in the cause of resisting America and saving the country.
    Tran Dai Nghia, Director of the State Commission for Science and Technology of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, said in his congratulatory telegram that this great achievement of Chinese scientific and technological workers and the entire people is a great contribution to strengthening the power of world forces against imperialism and greatly inspires the Vietnamese people and scientific and technological workers in their struggle against US imperialism and for the independence and unification of their country.
    Pham Xuan Thai, Chairman of the South Vietnam Liberation Federation of Trade Unions, said in his congratulatory telegram that China's new achievements further inspire the working class and working people of South Vietnam in their determination to defeat the US aggressors, liberate South Vietnam, and then peacefully unify the country.
    Nguyen Thi Dinh, Chairman of the South Vietnam Women's Liberation Federation, said in her telegram: "The women and people of South Vietnam are delighted and inspired by this glorious achievement of the fraternal Chinese people and regard it as a powerful encouragement to the women and people of South Vietnam who are waging a fierce struggle against the US aggressors for the liberation of South Vietnam."
    The telegram from Phuong Van Cung, Chairman of the South Vietnam Peace Committee, and Nguyen Ngoc Thuong, Chairman of the South Vietnam Asian-African Solidarity Committee, stated that this new achievement of the Chinese people has powerfully inspired the patriotic struggle of the South Vietnamese people and the struggle of all nations for liberation, while making the imperialists, especially US imperialists, tremble with fear.

### 越南南北方各人民团体电贺我核试验  中国成就是对越南人民反美斗争的鼓舞

1966-05-16
第5版()
专栏：

    越南南北方各人民团体电贺我核试验
    中国成就是对越南人民反美斗争的鼓舞
    据新华社十五日讯　我国成功地进行含有热核材料的核试验后，我国各人民团体收到了越南民主共和国和越南南方各人民团体发来许多贺电。越南民主共和国国家科学技术委员会也致电中国科学院祝贺这次核试验的成功。
    越南总工会主席黄国越的贺电说，这是中国工人阶级和人民的极其伟大的胜利，是以尊敬的毛泽东主席为首的中国共产党正确领导的光辉成就，对加强全世界革命力量是一无比重要的贡献，对美帝国主义及其走狗的战争政策和核讹诈的企图是一严重打击，对保卫东南亚和全世界的和平是一卓越的贡献。
    越南妇女联合会的电报说，这次核试验是中国人民在中国共产党和毛主席的英明领导下为加强国家国防力量的巨大胜利。
    越南劳动青年团中央委员会、越南青年联合会和越南大学生联合会的电报说，这一新成就大大鼓舞了亚洲、非洲和拉丁美洲的民族解放运动。
    越南保卫世界和平委员会、越南亚非人民团结委员会的电报说，中国在发展核武器方面所取得这一新的伟大成就，对于在日益强大的兄弟的中国人民彻底地支持和全力地援助下的、正在坚决战斗反对美帝国主义侵略的越南人民，是一种巨大的鼓舞。它使我们更加坚定对在抗美救国的事业中必将取得最后胜利的信念。
    越南民主共和国国家科学技术委员会主任陈大义的贺电说，中国科学技术工作者和全体人民的这个伟大成就，对加强全世界反对帝国主义力量的威力是一个巨大贡献，并大大鼓舞了在反对美帝国主义、为祖国的独立和统一而斗争中的越南人民和科学技术工作者。
    越南南方解放工会联合会主席范春泰的贺电说，中国的新成就，更加鼓舞了越南南方工人阶级和劳动人民决心战胜美国侵略者、解放越南南方、进而和平统一祖国的斗争。
    越南南方妇女解放联合会主席阮氏定的电报说：“越南南方妇女和人民为兄弟的中国人民的这一光辉成就而感到欢欣鼓舞，并把这看成是对正在为解放越南南方而同美国侵略者进行激烈斗争的越南南方妇女和人民的强有力的鼓舞。”
    越南南方和平委员会主席冯文恭、越南南方亚非团结委员会主席阮玉尚的电报说，中国人民取得的这一新的成就有力地鼓舞了越南南方人民的爱国斗争和各民族争取解放的斗争，而使帝国主义，特别是美帝国主义胆战心惊。
