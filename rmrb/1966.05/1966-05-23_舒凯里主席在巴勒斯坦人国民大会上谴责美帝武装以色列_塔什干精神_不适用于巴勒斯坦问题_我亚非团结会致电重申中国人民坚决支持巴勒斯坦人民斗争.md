Title: Shukairy Condemns US Imperialism for Arming Israel at the Palestinian National Congress; "Spirit of Tashkent" Does Not Apply to the Palestine Question; Our Afro-Asian Solidarity Association Cables Reaffirming Chinese People's Firm Support for Palestinian People's Struggle
Author:
Date: 1966-05-23
Edition: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Gaza, 20th - The Third Palestinian National Congress opened in Gaza on the evening of the 20th. Attending the congress were approximately five hundred delegates from Gaza and various Arab countries.
　　Husni, the United Arab Republic's Administrative Governor of Gaza, spoke at the opening ceremony on behalf of President Nasser. He emphasized that the liberation of Palestine must be achieved through revolution.
　　Ahmed Shukeiri, Chairman of the Palestine Liberation Organization, stated in his speech at the congress that US imperialism is the enemy of the Palestinian people and all Arab people. He stressed the need to take a firm stance against the United States' heinous act of supporting Israel with weapons. He said that just days ago, Israelis wielding American weapons invaded Palestinian villages and plundered their property.
　　He also pointed out that West Germany, at the behest of US imperialism, had supplied Israel with a large quantity of weapons.
　　Shukairy accused US imperialism of providing Israel with atomic reactors.
　　He praised the militant friendship between Palestine and China. He said that China was the first country to support Palestine. Since US imperialism supplies unjust Israel with weapons, why shouldn't our just people accept China's support?
　　In discussing his talks with Soviet Premier Kosygin in Cairo, he said that he told Kosygin that the Tashkent Agreement should not be applied to the Palestine question. He said that since Israel, with the support of US imperialism, has occupied Palestinian territory and driven Palestinians from their homes, the Palestinian cause is the struggle of the Palestinian people to return to their homes.
　　Shukairy condemned the planned "Islamic Pact" as an imperialist conspiracy.
　　In discussing the significant achievements made since the establishment of the Palestine Liberation Organization, he said that twenty thousand Palestinians are currently undergoing military training, and they are taking up arms in preparation for the arrival of the day of liberation.
　　After Shukairy delivered his speech, the congress entered a closed session. Congratulatory messages from the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee and other Afro-Asian solidarity organizations were read at the congress.
　　Xinhua News Agency, 19th - The Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee today sent a telegram to the Third Palestinian National Congress being held in Gaza, reaffirming the Chinese people's firm support for the just struggle of the Palestinian Arab people against US imperialism and its tool of aggression, Israel, demanding to return to their homes, and restore their legitimate rights, and firmly support the just struggle of the Arab countries to oppose imperialism headed by the United States, oppose Zionism, and strive for and safeguard national independence. The telegram expressed complete confidence that all the criminal activities of imperialism and its lackeys and accomplices to undermine the Arab people's struggle will be thoroughly defeated, and final victory will surely belong to the heroic Palestinian Arab people and the Arab countries.

### 舒凯里主席在巴勒斯坦人国民大会上谴责美帝武装以色列  “塔什干精神”不适用于巴勒斯坦问题  我亚非团结会致电重申中国人民坚决支持巴勒斯坦人民斗争

1966-05-23
第6版()
专栏：

    舒凯里主席在巴勒斯坦人国民大会上谴责美帝武装以色列
    “塔什干精神”不适用于巴勒斯坦问题
    我亚非团结会致电重申中国人民坚决支持巴勒斯坦人民斗争
    新华社加沙二十日电　第三次巴勒斯坦人国民大会二十日晚上在加沙开幕。出席大会的有来自加沙和阿拉伯各国的约五百名代表。
    阿联加沙行政长官胡斯尼代表纳赛尔总统在开幕式上讲了话。他着重指出，巴勒斯坦的解放必须通过革命。
    巴勒斯坦解放组织主席舒凯里在大会上讲话时指出，美帝国主义是巴勒斯坦人民和全体阿拉伯人民的敌人。他强调要采取坚定的态度反对美国用武器支持以色列的罪恶行动。他说，几天以前，手里拿着美国武器的以色列人侵入了巴勒斯坦人的村庄，掠夺了他们的财产。
    他还指出，西德应美帝国主义的要求向以色列供应了大量的武器。
    舒凯里指责美帝国主义给以色列提供了原子反应堆。
    他赞扬巴勒斯坦和中国之间的战斗友谊。他说，中国是第一个支持巴勒斯坦的国家。既然美帝国主义向非正义的以色列供应武器，我们正义的人民为什么不接受中国的支持呢？
    他在谈到他同苏联总理柯西金在开罗进行的谈话时说，他对柯西金说，塔什干协议不应当应用于巴勒斯坦问题。他说，既然以色列在美帝国主义的支持下霸占了巴勒斯坦的国土，把巴勒斯坦人赶出了他们的家园，因此巴勒斯坦事业，是巴勒斯坦人民争取返回家园的斗争。
    舒凯里谴责正在策划中的“伊斯兰条约”是帝国主义的阴谋。
    他在谈到巴勒斯坦解放组织成立以来所取得的重大成就时说，有两万巴勒斯坦人正在受军事训练，他们拿起武器准备迎接解放日的到来。
    在舒凯里发表讲话后，大会进入秘密会议。大会上宣读了中国亚非团结委员会以及其他国家亚非团结组织的贺电。
    新华社十九日讯　中国亚非团结委员会今天打电报给正在加沙举行的第三次巴勒斯坦人国民大会，重申中国人民坚决支持巴勒斯坦阿拉伯人民反对美帝国主义及其侵略工具以色列、要求重返家园、恢复合法权利的正义斗争，并坚决支持阿拉伯各国人民反对以美国为首的帝国主义、反对犹太复国主义、争取和维护民族独立的正义斗争。电报表示完全相信，帝国主义及其走狗和合伙者一切破坏阿拉伯人民斗争的罪恶活动，都将遭到彻底失败，最后胜利必将属于英勇的巴勒斯坦阿拉伯人民和阿拉伯各国人民。
