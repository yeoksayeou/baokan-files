Title: Capital Workers and Albanian Friends Gather in Joy: The Evening Radiated the Revolutionary and Militant Friendship of the Chinese and Albanian Peoples

Author:
Date: 1966-05-27
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 25th—Several hundred workers from the capital and nationally renowned advanced producers gathered tonight with their closest comrades-in-arms from the "Land of Eagles"—the comrades of the Albanian Trade Union Delegation led by Petrit Ekmekçiu. Chen Shaomin and Ma Chungu, Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Di Zicai, Secretary of the Secretariat, and Peng Siming, Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, and others attended the soiree.
    The evening radiated the revolutionary and militant friendship of the Chinese and Albanian workers. The capital's workers performed Chinese songs and dances and Albanian songs for their Albanian comrades-in-arms. Chinese and Albanian comrades also joined hands to dance group dances. The children of the Changxindian Locomotive and Rolling Stock Factory's staff and workers' elementary school performed dances and recited poems they had written, such as "The friendship between the Chinese and Albanian people is deep; the hearts of the Chinese and Albanian people are linked." When they presented red scarves to the Albanian comrades, warm applause erupted throughout the venue, and people shouted: "Mao Zedong! Enver!"

### 首都工人和阿尔巴尼亚朋友联欢  晚会上洋溢着中阿两国人民的革命战斗友情

1966-05-27
第5版()
专栏：

    首都工人和阿尔巴尼亚朋友联欢
    晚会上洋溢着中阿两国人民的革命战斗友情
    新华社二十五日讯　首都数百工人和全国著名的先进生产者，今晚同来自“山鹰之国”的最亲密战友——由佩特利特·艾克梅克奇乌率领的阿尔巴尼亚工会代表团的同志们一起联欢。全国总工会副主席陈少敏、马纯古，书记处书记狄子才和北京市总工会主席彭思明等出席了联欢晚会。
    晚会上洋溢着中阿两国工人的革命战斗友情。首都工人为阿尔巴尼亚战友们演出了中国歌舞和阿尔巴尼亚歌曲。中阿两国的同志们还手挽手地跳起集体舞。长辛店机车车辆工厂职工子弟小学的小朋友，为阿尔巴尼亚同志表演了舞蹈，朗诵了自己写的“中阿人民友谊深，中阿人民心连心”的诗。当他们把红领巾献给阿尔巴尼亚同志时，全场响起了热烈的掌声，人们高呼：“毛泽东！恩维尔！”
