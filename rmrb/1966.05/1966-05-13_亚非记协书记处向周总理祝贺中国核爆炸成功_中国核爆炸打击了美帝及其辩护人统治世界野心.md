Title: Afro-Asian Journalists' Association Secretariat Congratulates Premier Zhou on China's Successful Nuclear Explosion; China's Nuclear Explosion Strikes a Blow to the U.S. Imperialists and Their Apologists' Ambition to Dominate the World
Author:
Date: 1966-05-13
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 12th. Premier Zhou Enlai yesterday received a letter from the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association congratulating our country on the success of its third nuclear explosion.
    The congratulatory letter, signed by Djawoto, Secretary-General of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association, said:
    "The Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association extends to Your Excellency, and through you to the Government of the People's Republic of China, its warmest congratulations on the success of your third nuclear explosion.
    "The Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association firmly believes that this brilliant victory you have achieved in the nuclear field is a major step forward in defending China's security and a tremendous encouragement to all the revolutionary struggles being waged today by the oppressed people in many parts of the world.
    "Your great success in exploding the third nuclear bomb shatters the delusion of the U.S. imperialists and their collaborating pseudo-revolutionaries to monopolize nuclear weapons in order to blackmail freedom-loving nations, and at the same time gives tremendous courage and confidence to the people of all countries struggling for their liberation. This is a crushing blow to the U.S. imperialists and their apologists' insane ambition to dominate the world. This is a glorious contribution made by the peace-loving Chinese people to world peace.
    "The Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association fully agrees with the view expressed by the Chinese government that under the circumstances that the U.S. imperialists continue to produce nuclear weapons in large quantities, China has no other choice but to develop its own nuclear weapons to defend itself and to defend world peace.
    "The Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association once again congratulates Your Excellency, your government, the great revolutionary scientists and people of China on this glorious achievement."

### 亚非记协书记处向周总理祝贺中国核爆炸成功  中国核爆炸打击了美帝及其辩护人统治世界野心

1966-05-13
第5版()
专栏：

    亚非记协书记处向周总理祝贺中国核爆炸成功
    中国核爆炸打击了美帝及其辩护人统治世界野心
    新华社十二日讯　周恩来总理昨天收到了亚非新闻工作者协会书记处祝贺我国第三次核爆炸成功的信件。
    由亚非新闻工作者协会总书记查禾多签署的这封贺信说：
    “亚非新闻工作者协会书记处谨向阁下并通过你向中华人民共和国政府最热烈地祝贺你们第三次核爆炸成功。
    “亚非新闻工作者协会书记处完全相信，你们在核方面取得的这个光辉胜利是在保卫中国的安全方面前进了一大步，是对世界许多地方被压迫人民今天所进行的一切革命斗争的巨大鼓舞。
    “你们在爆炸第三颗核弹方面的巨大成功粉碎了美帝国主义者及与他们合作的假革命者为讹诈爱好自由的民族而垄断核武器的妄想，同时使得为争取自己的解放而斗争的各国人民获得巨大的勇气和信心。这是对美帝国主义者及其辩护人统治世界的疯狂的野心的粉碎性打击。这是爱好和平的中国人民对世界和平作出的光辉的贡献。
    “亚非新闻工作者协会书记处完全同意中国政府所表示的观点，即在美帝国主义者继续大量生产核武器的情况下，中国没有别的办法，只得发展自己的核武器以保卫自己，保卫世界和平。
    “亚非新闻工作者协会书记处再次向阁下、你的政府、中国的伟大革命科学家和人民祝贺你们取得的这个光荣的成就。”
