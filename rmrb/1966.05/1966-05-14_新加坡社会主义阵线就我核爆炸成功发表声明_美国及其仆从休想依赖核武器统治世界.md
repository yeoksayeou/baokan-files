Title: Singapore Socialist Front Issues Statement on Successful Chinese Nuclear Explosion: The United States and its Lackeys Cannot Dream of Ruling the World by Relying on Nuclear Weapons

Author:

Date: 1966-05-14
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 12th dispatch from Singapore: The Singapore Socialist Front issued a statement on the 11th saying that the success of China's nuclear explosion not only helps China's national defense, but also makes a great contribution to safeguarding world peace.

    The statement pointed out that since the United States dropped atomic bombs on Hiroshima and Nagasaki, people all over the world have been threatened and blackmailed by American nuclear weapons. Therefore, the success of China's nuclear explosion has encouraged people all over the world to further strengthen the struggle against aggression and for national liberation. At the same time, it shocked the United States and its lackeys, making them realize that they can no longer rely on nuclear weapons to rule the world.

    The statement also said that the people of Singapore do not want nuclear war. When the United States uses various nuclear weapons to threaten other countries, the people of Singapore must unite and struggle to force the United States to remove its current nuclear facilities in Asia and other places.

    According to Western news agencies, Lee Siew Choh, Chairman of the Singapore Socialist Front, said in a statement on the 10th that the success of China's nuclear explosion was a "very good thing."

    He said: "This helps to tell the Americans: they cannot do whatever they want." "It powerfully encourages the people of the world to fight against the United States."

### 新加坡社会主义阵线就我核爆炸成功发表声明  美国及其仆从休想依赖核武器统治世界

1966-05-14
第5版()
专栏：

    新加坡社会主义阵线就我核爆炸成功发表声明
    美国及其仆从休想依赖核武器统治世界
    新华社十二日讯　新加坡消息：新加坡社会主义阵线十一日发表声明说，中国核爆炸的成功不但有助于中国的国防，而且对保卫世界和平也有极大的贡献。
    声明指出，自从美国在广岛和长崎投下原子弹之后，全世界人民都受到美国核武器的威胁和讹诈。因此，中国的核爆炸成功，鼓舞了全世界人民进一步加强反侵略和争取民族解放的斗争。同时也使美国及其仆从感到震惊，使他们知道，他们已经不能依赖核武器来统治这个世界了。
    声明还说，新加坡人民不要核战争。当美国利用各种核武器威胁其它国家的时候，新加坡人民必须团结斗争，迫使美国撤除它目前在亚洲和其它地方的核设备。
    据西方通讯社报道：新加坡社会主义阵线主席李绍祖十日发表谈话说，中国核爆炸成功是一件“非常好的事情”。
    他说：“这有助于告诉美国人：他们不能为所欲为。”“它有力地鼓舞了世界人民反对美国的斗争。”
