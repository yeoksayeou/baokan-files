Title: Resolutely Repulse the Incursion of U.S. Imperialism and Its Running Dogs: Laotian Army and People Annihilate Over 3,000 Enemies and Shoot Down More Than 70 U.S. Aircraft in the First Quarter; Patriotic Army and People in Xieng Khouang and Other Provinces Shot Down Another Nine U.S. Aircraft Last Month
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-06
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Khang Khay, 4th—The Pathet Lao Radio broadcast today that the patriotic army and people of the provinces of Sam Neua and Xieng Khouang in Laos, in the first three months of this year, annihilated a total of 1,979 enemies and shot down 72 U.S. aircraft in repelling the attacks of the United States and its running dog troops.
    In the first three months of this year, U.S. imperialism and the Lao right-wing reactionary group continuously carried out "mopping-up" and nibbling operations against the provinces of Sam Neua and Xieng Khouang. The patriotic army and people of Sam Neua and Xieng Khouang provinces heroically fought back against the enemy and achieved significant victories.
    In Xieng Khouang province, 685 enemies were annihilated, including 572 killed, 87 wounded, and 26 captured; 57 U.S. aircraft were shot down, and dozens were damaged.
    In Sam Neua province, 1,294 enemies were annihilated, including 680 killed, 223 captured, and 391 who surrendered; many were also wounded. Fifteen U.S. aircraft were shot down, and dozens were damaged.

    Xinhua News Agency, Khang Khay, 2nd—The Pathet Lao Radio broadcast on the 2nd that the patriotic army and people in the central and lower Laos regions have achieved brilliant results in their self-defense battles in the first three months of this year, annihilating a total of 577 enemies and seizing a large quantity of weapons and other military supplies.
    During this period, U.S. imperialism and the Lao traitorous running dog clique repeatedly gathered many battalions of troops to attack the liberated areas of central and lower Laos. U.S. imperialists also dispatched aircraft to bomb and strafe, massacring the Lao people. The Lao patriotic armed forces, with the support of the local people, launched seventeen counterattacks, killing a total of 431 enemies, wounding 76, capturing 39, and forcing 31 enemy soldiers to surrender. They also seized 198 pieces of artillery, mortars, recoilless rifles, and other weapons, 4 military vehicles, a large amount of ammunition, and other military supplies.

    Xinhua News Agency, Khang Khay, 3rd—The Pathet Lao Radio broadcast that the patriotic army and people of Luang Prabang and Houei Sai provinces in Laos, in the first three months of this year, destroyed 293 "unity villages" where U.S. imperialism and its running dogs brutally ruled the people, liberating tens of thousands of people.
    In the first three months of this year, the patriotic army and people of these two provinces, in counterattacking the attacks and encroachments of U.S. imperialism and its running dogs, conducted 95 battles of all sizes, annihilating 1,136 enemies, including 445 killed, 158 wounded, 66 captured, and 467 who surrendered; seizing a large number of weapons and military supplies.

    Xinhua News Agency, Khang Khay, 3rd—According to the "Voice of Laos" radio broadcast, local troops and guerrillas in Muong Kham County, Xieng Khouang Province, Laos, shot down two U.S. bandit aircraft on April 18th and 20th.

    Xinhua News Agency, Khang Khay—The Pathet Lao Radio broadcast on April 29th that the patriotic army and people of Sam Neua Province, Laos, on April 23rd, in the battle to repel invading U.S. bandit aircraft, in addition to the two already announced, also shot down a F-4H U.S. aircraft and damaged several others.

    Xinhua News Agency, Khang Khay, 2nd—The Pathet Lao Radio broadcast that the patriotic army and people of Sam Neua Province, Laos, shot down three U.S. bandit aircraft and damaged several others in the two days of April 28th and 29th.

    Xinhua News Agency, Khang Khay—According to the "Voice of Laos" radio broadcast, the air defense forces of the Lao armed forces guarding the Phou Kout Mountain area in Xieng Khouang Province shot down an invading U.S. A4D aircraft and damaged two others on April 28th.

    Xinhua News Agency, Khang Khay, 5th—The Pathet Lao Radio broadcast that the patriotic army and people of Sam Neua Province, Laos, shot down two U.S. F-105 jet fighters on April 30th. (Image attached)
    Women in the Laotian liberated areas are actively engaged in production and support the front. They are also taking up arms, working closely with the Lao People's Liberation Army to defend their homes and the liberated areas. The picture shows Laotian female guerrillas practicing shooting with the help of Lao People's Liberation Army soldiers.

### 坚决反击美帝国主义及其走狗的进犯  老挝军民首季歼敌三千余击落美机七十多架  川圹等省爱国军民上月又击落美机九架

1966-05-06
第5版()
专栏：

    坚决反击美帝国主义及其走狗的进犯
    老挝军民首季歼敌三千余击落美机七十多架
    川圹等省爱国军民上月又击落美机九架
    新华社康开四日电　据巴特寮电台今天广播：老挝桑怒和川圹两省爱国军民今年头三个月在反击美国及其走狗军队的进攻中，共歼灭敌人一千九百七十九名，击落美国飞机七十二架。
    今年头三个月，美帝国主义及老挝右派反动集团不断对桑怒和川圹两省进行“扫荡”和蚕食，桑怒和川圹两省爱国军民英勇反击敌人，取得了重大胜利。
    在川圹省，歼灭敌人六百八十五名，其中打死五百七十二名，打伤八十七名，俘虏二十六名；击落美国飞机五十七架，击伤数十架。
    在桑怒省，歼灭敌人一千二百九十四名，其中打死六百八十名，俘虏二百二十三名，投降三百九十一名，还打伤多名；击落美国飞机十五架，击伤数十架。
    新华社康开二日电　据巴特寮电台二日广播：老挝中寮和下寮地区的爱国军民在今年头三个月的自卫战斗中，取得了辉煌战果，一共歼灭了敌人五百七十七名，并且缴获了大量的武器和其它军用物资。
    在这一期间，美帝国主义和老挝卖国走狗集团曾经一再集结许多营的兵力，向中寮和下寮解放区进攻。美帝国主义者还派遣了飞机进行轰炸扫射，残杀老挝人民。老挝爱国武装力量在当地人民的支持下进行了十七次反击，一共打死了敌人四百三十一名，打伤七十六名，俘虏三十九名，并且迫使敌军三十一名投降。他们还缴获大炮、迫击炮、无后座力炮以及其他武器一百九十八件，军车四辆、大量的弹药和其它军用物资。
    新华社康开三日电　据巴特寮电台广播：老挝琅勃拉邦和会晒两省爱国军民在今年头三个月内，捣毁了美帝国主义和它的走狗残暴统治人民的“团结村”二百九十三个，解放了成万的人民。
    这两省爱国军民在今年头三个月内反击美帝国主义及其走狗的进攻和蚕食中，共进行了大、小战斗九十五次，歼灭敌人一千一百三十六名，其中打死四百四十五名，打伤一百五十八名，俘虏六十六名，投降四百六十七名；缴获大批武器和军用物资。
    据新华社康开三日电　据
  “老挝之声”电台广播：老挝川圹省芒坎县地方部队和游击队四月十八日和二十日两天，击落美国强盗飞机两架。
    新华社康开电　据巴特寮电台四月二十九日广播：老挝桑怒省爱国军民四月二十三日在反击入侵的美国强盗飞机的战斗中，除已公布的打落的两架外，还打落F—4H型美国飞机一架，打伤多架。
    据新华社康开二日电　据巴特寮电台广播：老挝桑怒省爱国军民在四月二十八日和二十九日两天里，打落了美国强盗飞机三架，击伤多架。
    新华社康开电　据“老挝之声”电台广播：守卫在川圹省富科特山地区的老挝武装力量防空部队，四月二十八日打落入侵的美国A4D型飞机一架，打伤两架。
