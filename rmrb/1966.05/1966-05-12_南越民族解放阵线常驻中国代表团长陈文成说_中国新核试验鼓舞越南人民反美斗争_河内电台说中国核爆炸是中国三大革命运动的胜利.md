Title:  Chen Wen Cheng, Head of the Permanent Delegation of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front to China, Says China's New Nuclear Test Encourages the Vietnamese People's Anti-American Struggle; Hanoi Radio Says China's Nuclear Explosion is a Victory of China's Three Great Revolutionary Movements

Author: 
Date: 1966-05-12
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 11th – Chen Wen Cheng, head of the Permanent Delegation of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front to China, wrote a letter on the 10th to the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, saying that China's successful nuclear explosion containing thermonuclear materials is of great significance. It not only strengthens China's powerful national defense but also makes a positive contribution to safeguarding peace in Asia and the world.

    The congratulatory letter said: "We believe that the people of South Vietnam will be extremely happy about the brilliant achievements of the fraternal Chinese people and will regard it as a powerful encouragement to the struggle against U.S. imperialism and its lackeys, aimed at liberating the South, defending the North, and then peacefully reunifying the motherland.

    "We wholeheartedly wish the Chinese people, commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Chinese engineering and technical personnel, and defense workers even greater achievements in their work and in being ready to fight at any time."

    Chen Huai Nan, head of the delegation of the South Vietnam Liberation Federation of Trade Unions, who is visiting Shenyang, also sent a telegram on the 10th to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, warmly congratulating China on the success of its third nuclear test.

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 11th – "Voice of Vietnam" radio station broadcast a commentary on the 10th, warmly congratulating China on the glorious achievement of the successful third nuclear explosion.

    The commentary said that China has successfully conducted another nuclear explosion containing thermonuclear materials. Faced with this particularly significant event, the imperialists headed by the United States are alarmed and frightened. On the contrary, the Vietnamese people and peace-loving people all over the world are very excited and proud.

    The commentary said that this is a new and extremely great achievement of the Chinese people. Only a year and a half have passed since China exploded its first atomic bomb on October 16, 1964, and China has already taken a big step forward in the field of atomic science and technology, successfully carrying out a nuclear explosion containing thermonuclear materials. This is a powerful proof of the superiority of the socialist system. This is the victory of the general line of socialist construction put forward by the Chinese Communist Party headed by Comrade Mao Zedong. This is the victory of the three great revolutionary movements of class struggle, production struggle, and scientific experiment that are flourishing throughout China.

    The commentary pointed out that China's possession of nuclear weapons is a great encouragement to the revolutionary people of all countries who are struggling for liberation. This is a very valuable contribution to the cause of safeguarding world peace.

    The commentary emphasized that China's development of nuclear weapons is for self-defense, to defend the Chinese people, and to smash the U.S. imperialists' nuclear monopoly, so that they cannot continue to use nuclear weapons to intimidate and bully the people of all countries.

    The commentary said that China's successful nuclear explosion is a heavy blow to the U.S. imperialists and their lackeys in the world and in Vietnam. The Vietnamese people regard the achievements of the Chinese people as their own achievements. It strengthens the Vietnamese people's confidence in the inevitable victory of the Vietnamese and world revolutions.

### 南越民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团长陈文成说  中国新核试验鼓舞越南人民反美斗争  河内电台说中国核爆炸是中国三大革命运动的胜利

1966-05-12
第5版()
专栏：

    南越民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团长陈文成说
    中国新核试验鼓舞越南人民反美斗争
    河内电台说中国核爆炸是中国三大革命运动的胜利
    新华社十一日讯　越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长陈文成十日写信给中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和中国亚非团结委员会说，中国成功地进行含有热核材料的核爆炸具有非常重大的意义，它不仅为了加强中国强大的国防力量，而且对保卫亚洲和世界的和平作出积极的贡献。
    贺信说：“我们相信，越南南方人民对兄弟的中国人民取得这个辉煌的成就将感到无比的高兴，并且把它看作是对反对美帝国主义及其走狗，旨在解放南方、保卫北方，进而和平统一祖国的斗争的一种有力的鼓舞。
    “我们衷心地祝中国人民、中国人民解放军指战员、中国工程技术人员和国防工人，在工作中和随时准备战斗中取得更大的成就。”
    正在沈阳访问的越南南方解放工会联合会代表团团长陈怀南，十日也打电报给中华全国总工会热烈祝贺中国第三次核试验成功。
    新华社河内十一日电“越南之声”广播电台十日发表评论，热烈祝贺中国第三次核爆炸成功的辉煌成就。
    评论说，中国又成功地进行了一次含有热核材料的核爆炸。面对这一具有特别重要意义的事件，以美国为首的帝国主义感到惊惶害怕，相反，越南人民和世界爱好和平人民却感到非常兴奋和自豪。
    评论说，这是中国人民的一个新的、极其巨大的成就。从一九六四年十月十六日，中国爆炸第一颗原子弹以来，时间才仅仅过去一年半，中国在原子科学技术领域内，已经向前迈进了一大步，成功地进行了含有热核材料的核爆炸。这是社会主义制度优越性的强有力的证明。这是以毛泽东同志为首的中国共产党提出的社会主义建设总路线的胜利。这是正在中国各地蓬勃发展着的阶级斗争、生产斗争、科学实验三大革命运动的胜利。
    评论指出，中国拥有核武器，是对正在为争取解放而进行斗争的各国革命人民的一个巨大鼓舞。这是对保卫世界和平事业的一个非常宝贵的贡献。
    评论强调说，中国发展核武器是为了自卫，是为了保卫中国人民，中国发展核武器也是为了粉碎美帝国主义的核垄断，使它不能继续用核武器来恫吓和欺侮各国人民。
    评论说，中国这次核爆炸成功，是对美帝国主义及其走狗在全世界以及在越南的侵略的一个沉重打击。越南人民把中国人民的成就看作是自己的成就。它使越南人民加强了对越南和世界革命必胜的信心。
