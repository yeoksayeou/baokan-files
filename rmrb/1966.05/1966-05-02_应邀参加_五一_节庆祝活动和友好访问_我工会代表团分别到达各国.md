Title: Invited to Participate in "May Day" Celebrations and Friendly Visits, Our Trade Union Delegations Arrive in Various Countries

Author: 
Date: 1966-05-02
Edition: 3

    (Xinhua News Agency) Chinese trade union delegations led by He Shumin, Vice Chairman of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Federation of Trade Unions; Li Xuemin, Deputy Secretary-General of the Shanghai Municipal Federation of Trade Unions; Liang Chao, Vice Chairman of the Guangzhou Municipal Federation of Trade Unions; Wang Jun, Chairman of the Harbin Municipal Federation of Trade Unions; He Ying, Vice Chairman of the Changchun Municipal Federation of Trade Unions; Ye Xin, Vice Chairman of the Shandong Provincial Federation of Trade Unions; Chen Guang, Vice Chairman of the Hebei Provincial Federation of Trade Unions; Zhang Xiaoyong, Vice Chairman of the Shaanxi Provincial Federation of Trade Unions; Lu Gang, Vice Chairman of the Hubei Provincial Federation of Trade Unions; Zhao Tuo, Vice Chairman of the Shanxi Provincial Federation of Trade Unions; Zhu Xin, Vice Chairman of the Jiangxi Provincial Federation of Trade Unions; and Mo Zhenqiu, have successively arrived in Albania, Bulgaria, Cuba, the German Democratic Republic, Poland, Romania, Czechoslovakia, the Mongolian People's Republic, the United Arab Republic, Congo (Brazzaville), Guinea, and Morocco, respectively, upon invitation to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations and to conduct friendly visits.

### 应邀参加“五一”节庆祝活动和友好访问  我工会代表团分别到达各国

1966-05-02
第3版()
专栏：

    应邀参加“五一”节庆祝活动和友好访问
    我工会代表团分别到达各国
    据新华社讯　由广西壮族自治区总工会副主席何庶民率领的中国工会代表团，由上海市总工会副秘书长李学民率领的中国工会代表团，由广州市总工会副主席梁超率领的中国工会代表团，由哈尔滨市总工会主席王军率领的中国工会代表团，由长春市总工会副主席贺瑛率领的中国工会代表团，由山东省总工会副主席叶莘率领的中国工会代表团，由河北省总工会副主席陈光率领的中国工会代表团，由陕西省总工会副主席张孝雍率领的中国工会代表团，由湖北省总工会副主席逯刚率领的中国工会代表团，由山西省总工会副主席赵拓率领的中国工会代表团，由江西省总工会副主席朱昕率领的中国工会代表团和由莫振球率领的中国工会代表团，先后分别到达阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、古巴、德意志民主共和国、波兰、罗马尼亚、捷克斯洛伐克、蒙古人民共和国、阿联、刚果（布）、几内亚和摩洛哥，他们是应邀参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝活动和进行友好访问。
