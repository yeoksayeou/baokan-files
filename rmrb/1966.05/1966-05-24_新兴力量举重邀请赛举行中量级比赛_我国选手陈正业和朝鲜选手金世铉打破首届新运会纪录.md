Title: Emerging Forces Invitational Weightlifting Tournament Holds Middleweight Competition; Chinese Athlete Chen Zhengye and North Korean Athlete Kim Se-Hyun Break First Games of the New Emerging Forces Record

Author: 
Date: 1966-05-24
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 23rd -- The 1966 Emerging Forces Invitational Weightlifting Tournament held its fifth weight class event tonight at the Beijing Gymnasium - the middleweight division. Chinese athlete Chen Zhengye, who won the championship, Democratic People's Republic of Korea athlete Kim Se-Hyun, who won the silver medal, and Chinese athlete Xiao Xishan, who participated in the exhibition, achieved total scores of 420 kg, 410 kg, and 417.5 kg, respectively, all breaking the first Games of the New Emerging Forces record of 405 kg in this weight class.
    As they achieved these new scores, music played, and the audience warmly applauded to congratulate them. Young Pioneers from China presented them with flowers.
    Twenty-three-year-old Chen Zhengye competed fiercely with Kim Se-Hyun.
    In the press, Chen Zhengye achieved a score of 140 kg, lifting 15 kg more than Kim Se-Hyun, and breaking the first Games of the New Emerging Forces record of 127.5 kg in this lift.
    In the snatch, Chen Zhengye lifted 120 kg, while Kim Se-Hyun lifted 130 kg, breaking the first Games of the New Emerging Forces record of 120 kg in this lift. At this point, Chen Zhengye's press and snatch scores were only 5 kg more than Kim Se-Hyun's.
    In the clean and jerk, after Chen Zhengye lifted 160 kg, his total score reached 420 kg, breaking not only the first Games of the New Emerging Forces record but also the national record of 412.5 kg.
    In order to surpass Chen Zhengye in total score, Kim Se-Hyun, after clean and jerking 155 kg, requested the barbell weight be increased sharply to 167.5 kg. Although he failed twice in attempting this weight, his "fighting" spirit won people's praise.
    Japanese athlete Kazuyoshi Sato won third place with a total score of 377.5 kg (press 125, snatch 117.5, clean and jerk 135).
    Twenty-three-year-old Xiao Xishan achieved scores of 130 kg in both the press and snatch today, and 157.5 kg in the clean and jerk. Among them, the snatch score not only broke the first Games of the New Emerging Forces record but also the national record of 128.5 kg. His total score also broke the national record.
    Eight athletes participated in tonight's middleweight competition. Although the competition was fierce, the arena was always filled with an atmosphere of unity and friendship. Athletes from various countries often congratulated each other on their new achievements. Athletes who had already competed seized the opportunity to chat cordially and exchange experiences. Japanese athlete Tokio Taniai and others enthusiastically helped Ceylonese athlete M. Sabaratnam prepare for the competition.
    Cambodian athlete Roeung Chantha, who participated in the competition, and Cambodian athlete Chhorn Orn, who participated in the exhibition, both broke Cambodian records today.
    Roeung Chantha's total score reached 270 kg (press 87.5, snatch 77.5, clean and jerk 105), raising the Cambodian record by 10 kg. His press and snatch scores also broke Cambodian records. Chhorn Orn's total score was 260 kg (press 75, snatch 80, clean and jerk 105), with the snatch score breaking the Cambodian record.
    The total scores and press, snatch, and clean and jerk scores of the other four athletes participating in the middleweight competition are:
    Yo. Shahly (Syria) -- 335 kg (95, 107.5, 132.5);
    Bai. Canka (Albania) -- 315 kg (100, 92.5, 122.5);
    A. Belinova (Albania) -- 312.5 kg (92.5, 97.5, 122.5);
    M. Sabaratnam (Ceylon) -- 247.5 kg (70, 75, 102.5).
    The invitational tournament will hold the final three weight classes on the 24th -- light heavyweight, second heavyweight, and heavyweight competitions.

### 新兴力量举重邀请赛举行中量级比赛  我国选手陈正业和朝鲜选手金世铉打破首届新运会纪录

1966-05-24
第5版()
专栏：

    新兴力量举重邀请赛举行中量级比赛
    我国选手陈正业和朝鲜选手金世铉打破首届新运会纪录
    新华社二十三日讯  一九六六年新兴力量举重邀请赛今晚在北京体育馆举行第五个级别——中量级的比赛。获得冠军的我国选手陈正业、获得亚军的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国选手金世铉和参加表演的我国选手萧锡山的总成绩，分别达到了四二○公斤、四一○公斤和四一七·五公斤，都打破了第一届新运会这一级别总成绩四○五公斤的纪录。
    当他们取得这些新成绩时，乐声响起，全场观众热烈鼓掌向他们表示祝贺，我国女少先队员分别向他们献了花。
    二十三岁的陈正业同金世铉争夺得很激烈。
    在推举中，陈正业的成绩达到了一四○公斤，比金世铉多举起了十五公斤，并且刷新了第一届新运会这一级别推举一二七·五公斤的纪录。
    在抓举中，陈正业举起了一二○公斤，而金世铉举起了一三○公斤，刷新了第一届新运会这一级别抓举一二○公斤的纪录。这时，陈正业推举和抓举成绩只比金世铉多五公斤。
    在挺举中，陈正业举起一六○公斤以后，总成绩达到了四二○公斤，不仅打破了第一届新运会纪录，而且打破了四一二·五公斤的全国纪录。
    金世铉为了使总成绩超过陈正业，他在挺举起一五五公斤以后，就要求把杠铃重量猛增加到一六七·五公斤。他虽然两次试举这个重量都没有成功，但是他的“拼”劲博得了人们的赞扬。
    日本选手佐藤和良获得了第三名，总成绩是三七七·五公斤（推举一二五、抓举一一七·五、挺举一三五）。
    二十三岁的萧锡山今天推举和抓举的成绩都是一三○公斤，挺举成绩是一五七·五公斤。其中抓举成绩不仅也打破了第一届新运会的纪录，而且还打破了一二八·五公斤的全国纪录。他的总成绩也打破了全国纪录。
    有八名选手参加了今晚的中量级比赛。比赛虽然很激烈，但是竞赛场上始终充满团结友好的气氛。各国选手经常彼此祝贺对方取得的新成绩。已经比赛完的选手抓紧时机亲切交谈，交流经验。日本选手谷合时雄等还热情地帮助锡兰选手姆·萨巴拉特南准备比赛。
    参加比赛的柬埔寨选手罗昌塔和参加表演的柬埔寨选手纯诺今天都打破了柬埔寨纪录。
    罗昌塔的总成绩达到了二七○公斤（推八七·五、抓七七·五、挺一○五），把柬埔寨纪录提高了十公斤。他的推举和抓举成绩也打破了柬埔寨纪录。纯诺的总成绩是二六○公斤（推七五、抓八○、挺一○五），其中抓举成绩打破了柬埔寨纪录。
    参加中量级比赛的其他四名选手的总成绩和推举、抓举、挺举成绩分别是：
    约·沙赫利（叙利亚）——三三五公斤
  （九五、一○七·五、一三二·五）；
    拜·灿卡（阿尔巴尼亚）——三一五公斤（一○○、九二·五、一二二·五）；
    阿·贝利诺瓦（阿尔巴尼亚）——三一二·五公斤（九二·五、九七·五、一二二·五）；
    姆·萨巴拉特南（锡兰）——二四七·五公斤（七○、七五、一○二·五）。
    邀请赛二十四日将进行最后三个级别——轻重量级、次重量级和重量级的比赛。
