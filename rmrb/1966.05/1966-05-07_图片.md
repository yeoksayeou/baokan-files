Title: Deng Zhiying
Author: Deng Zhiying
Date: 1966-05-07
Edition: 5

    On May 6th, more than 10,000 workers in Shanghai held a grand rally to warmly welcome Chairman Shehu and the Albanian Party and Government Delegation led by him. The Shanghai workers and masses who participated in the welcoming rally expressed the warmest welcome and highest respect to the representatives of the heroic Albanian people.
    Photo by Deng Zhiying (Xinhua News Agency draft, fax photo)
