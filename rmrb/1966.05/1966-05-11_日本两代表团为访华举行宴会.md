Title: Two Japanese Delegations Hold Banquet for Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1966-05-11
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, May 7th – The Japanese Local Assembly Members Promoting International Trade Alliance Delegation headed by Hachiro Miura, and the Japanese Women Local Assembly Members Delegation headed by Mitsuru Goto, jointly held a banquet this evening to entertain people from all walks of life in the capital. Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and other relevant figures including Yang Yunyu, Zhang Huadong, Yong Longgui, Li Xinnong, Wang Xiaoyun, etc., were invited to attend the banquet.

### 日本两代表团为访华举行宴会

1966-05-11
第6版()
专栏：

    日本两代表团为访华举行宴会
    新华社七日讯　以三浦八郎为首的日本地方议员促进国际贸易联盟访华代表团，和以后藤满为首的日本妇女地方议员代表团，今晚联合举行宴会，招待首都各界人士。中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸和其他有关方面人士杨蕴玉、张化东、勇龙桂、李新农、王晓云等应邀出席了宴会。
