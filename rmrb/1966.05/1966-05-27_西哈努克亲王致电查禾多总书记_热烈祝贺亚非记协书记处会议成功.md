Title: Prince Sihanouk Sends Telegram to Secretary-General Thiam Diallo, Warmly Congratulating on the Success of the Secretariat Meeting of the Asian-African Journalists Association

Author:
Date: 1966-05-27
Edition: 6

    Prince Sihanouk Sends Telegram to Secretary-General Thiam Diallo
    Warmly Congratulating on the Success of the Secretariat Meeting of the Asian-African Journalists Association
    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, May 24th (electric dispatch) – According to the Cambodian News Agency on May 24th, Cambodian Head of State Prince Sihanouk sent a telegram to Thiam Diallo, Secretary-General of the Asian-African Journalists Association, on May 22nd, expressing warm congratulations on the great success achieved at the Fourth Plenary Meeting (Enlarged) of the Secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists Association recently held in Beijing.
    The telegram expressed gratitude for the meeting's support for Cambodia's struggle against the aggression and conspiracies of the American imperialists and their running dogs.
    The telegram wished the Asian-African Journalists Association success in its noble actions against imperialism in the future.

### 西哈努克亲王致电查禾多总书记  热烈祝贺亚非记协书记处会议成功

1966-05-27
第6版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王致电查禾多总书记
    热烈祝贺亚非记协书记处会议成功
    新华社金边二十四日电　据柬埔寨新闻社二十四日报道，柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王五月二十二日打电报给亚非新闻工作者协会总书记查禾多，对最近在北京举行的亚非新闻工作者协会书记处第四次全体会议（扩大）获得的巨大成功表示热烈的祝贺。
    电报对会议支持柬埔寨反对美帝国主义者及其走狗的侵略和阴谋的斗争表示感谢。
    电报祝亚非新闻工作者协会今后在反对帝国主义的崇高行动中取得成功。
