Title: Czechoslovak Ambassador to China Hosts National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1966-05-10
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 9th -- Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Krištůfek, held a reception at the embassy this evening to celebrate the National Day of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic.
    Vice Premier of the State Council Ulanhu, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Yang Mingxuan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Gao Chongmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Wang Bingnan, and responsible persons from relevant departments were invited to attend the reception.
    Ambassador Krištůfek and Vice Premier Ulanhu delivered toasts at the reception successively, toasting to the friendship between the peoples of China and Czechoslovakia.
    Diplomatic envoys of various countries to China were also invited to attend the reception.
    On the afternoon of May 5th, Ambassador Krištůfek held a film reception to celebrate the National Day of Czechoslovakia.

### 捷驻华大使举行国庆招待会

1966-05-10
第5版()
专栏：

    捷驻华大使举行国庆招待会
    新华社九日讯　捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使克日斯特克今晚在大使馆举行招待会，庆祝捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国国庆。
    国务院副总理乌兰夫，人大常委会副委员长杨明轩，政协全国委员会副主席高崇民，外交部副部长王炳南以及有关方面负责人，应邀出席了招待会。
    克日斯特克大使和乌兰夫副总理先后在招待会上祝酒，为中捷两国人民的友谊干杯。
    各国驻中国的使节也应邀出席了招待会。
    五日下午，克日斯特克大使为庆祝捷克斯洛伐克国庆举行了电影招待会。
