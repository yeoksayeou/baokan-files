Title: Nan Hanchen Receives and Fetes Philippine Trade Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-09
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 8th. Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, received and hosted a banquet tonight for the entire members of the Philippine Trade Delegation headed by Masial.
    Present at the reception and banquet were Yong Longgui, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Li Xinnong, Deputy Director of the Ministry of Foreign Trade; Xu Wenyi, Deputy Director of the World Knowledge Publishing House; and responsible persons from relevant departments.
    This seven-member delegation came to China for a visit at the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and arrived in Beijing on the 6th.

### 南汉宸接见并宴请菲贸易考察团

1966-05-09
第5版()
专栏：

    南汉宸接见并宴请菲贸易考察团
    新华社八日讯中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸今晚接见并宴请以马西亚尔为首的菲律宾贸易考察团全体成员。
    接见和宴请时在座的，有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席勇龙桂，对外贸易部副局长李新农，《世界知识》副社长许文益等有关方面负责人。
    这个一行七人的考察团，是应中国国际贸易促进委员会邀请来我国访问，在六日到达北京的。
