Title: Northern Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down 18 US Aircraft in Consecutive Days; Hanoi Military and Civilians Shoot Down a US Unmanned High-Altitude Reconnaissance Aircraft

Author:
Date: 1966-05-11
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, Tenth -- According to announcements here on the tenth: Northern Vietnamese military and civilians shot down four invading American bandit aircraft on the tenth.
    On the afternoon of the tenth, multiple batches of American bandit aircraft attacked some residential areas in Yen Bai and Quang Binh provinces, and carried out provocative activities against the coastal areas of Haiphong City and Quang Ninh Province, encountering fierce counterattacks from the military and civilians in various places.
    According to preliminary reports, Yen Bai Province shot down two American aircraft, Quang Binh Province shot down one, and the People's Navy shot down one.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, Ninth -- According to announcements here on the ninth: The military and civilians of Hanoi City, the capital of Vietnam, shot down a US unmanned high-altitude reconnaissance aircraft at noon on the ninth, which had intruded into Hanoi's airspace for reconnaissance activities.
    According to Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, Seventh -- According to announcements here, the military and civilians of Nam Ha and Thai Binh provinces in northern Vietnam each shot down an invading American bandit aircraft on May 6.
    In addition, according to supplementary announcements, at the end of April, Ha Tinh and Thai Binh provinces, and the military and civilians of Me Island in Thanh Hoa Province, each shot down an American aircraft.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, Eighth -- According to announcements here on the evening of the eighth: The military and civilians of Quang Ninh, Bac Thai, and Lang Son provinces in northern Vietnam shot down six invading American bandit aircraft on the eighth and captured American pilots.
    Among them, Quang Ninh Province shot down three enemy aircraft, Bac Thai Province shot down two, and Lang Son Province shot down one.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, Fourth -- According to supplementary announcements here on the fourth, militiamen from Tuan Ket, Trung Thanh, and An Hoa communes in Da Bac district, Hoa Binh province, in northern Vietnam, cooperated on April 29 and shot down an invading American jet aircraft and captured an American pilot alive.
    On April 29, in addition to the three American aircraft already announced, the military and civilians of Quang Ninh Province also shot down another enemy aircraft. On May 1, the military and civilians of this province shot down another American aircraft.

### 越南北方军民连日击落美机十八架  河内军民击落美国无人驾驶高空侦察机一架

1966-05-11
第6版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民连日击落美机十八架
    河内军民击落美国无人驾驶高空侦察机一架
    新华社河内十日电　据这里十日公布：越南北方军民十日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机四架。
    十日下午，美国强盗飞机多批袭击安沛、广平等省一些居民区，并对海防市和广宁省沿海地区进行挑衅活动，遭到各地军民的猛烈还击。
    据初步消息，安沛省击落美国飞机两架，广平省击落一架，人民海军击落一架。
    新华社河内九日电　据这里九日公布：越南首都河内市军民九日中午击落侵入河内领空进行侦察活动的美国无人驾驶高空侦察机一架。
    据新华社河内七日电　据这里公布，越南北方南河、太平两省军民在五月六日各击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架。
    另据补充公布，四月底，河静、太平两省和清化省湄岛军民还各击落美国飞机一架。
    新华社河内八日电　据这里八日晚上公布：越南北方广宁、北太和谅山三省军民，八日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机六架，并俘虏了美国飞行员。
    其中，广宁省击落敌机三架，北太省击落两架，谅山省击落一架。
    据新华社河内四日电　据这里四日补充公布，越南北方和平省沱北县团结、忠诚和安和三个乡的民兵，在四月二十九日协同作战，击落入侵的美国喷气式飞机一架，并活捉美国飞行员。
    四月二十九日，广宁省军民除已公布的击落美机三架外，还击落另一架敌机。五月一日，这个省的军民又击落美机一架。
