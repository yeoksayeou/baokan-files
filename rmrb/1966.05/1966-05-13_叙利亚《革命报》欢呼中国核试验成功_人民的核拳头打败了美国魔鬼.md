Title: Syrian Newspaper "Al-Thawrah" Hails China's Successful Nuclear Test: The People's Nuclear Fist Defeats the American Devil
Author:
Date: 1966-05-13
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, May 11th: The Syrian newspaper "Al-Thawrah" published an editorial on May 11th, hailing China's successful nuclear explosion containing thermonuclear materials. The editorial pointed out that this is a major scientific achievement.
    The editorial said that China possesses nuclear weapons for its own defense.
    The editorial condemned the United States for carrying out nuclear blackmail around the world. The editorial said: China's development of nuclear weapons has given the White House a serious lesson, severely打击 (dǎ jī) [hit hard] the imperialist stubborn mindset that believes people of all countries must submit to the American devil, or be destroyed by nuclear bombs. But the people's fist, especially the nuclear fist, has defeated the American devil.
    The editorial said: China's nuclear explosion will trigger new revolutionary outbreaks in emerging countries, shorten the lifespan of imperialism, and end the suffering of the people.

### 叙利亚《革命报》欢呼中国核试验成功  人民的核拳头打败了美国魔鬼

1966-05-13
第5版()
专栏：

    叙利亚《革命报》欢呼中国核试验成功
    人民的核拳头打败了美国魔鬼
    新华社大马士革十一日电  叙利亚《革命报》十一日发表社论，欢呼中国成功地进行了含有热核材料的核爆炸。社论指出，这是重大的科学成就。
    社论说，中国拥有核武器是为了自己的防御。
    社论谴责美国在全世界进行核讹诈。社论说：中国发展核武器给了白宫严重的教训，沉重打击了那种认为世界各国人民必须向美国魔鬼屈服、不然就要被核弹毁灭的帝国主义顽固脑筋。但是人民的拳头、特别是核拳头打败了美国魔鬼。
    社论说：中国的核爆炸将在新兴国家中引起新的革命爆发，缩短帝国主义的寿命，结束人民的灾难。
