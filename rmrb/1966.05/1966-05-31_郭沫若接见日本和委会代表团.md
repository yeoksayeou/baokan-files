Title: Guo Moruo Receives Delegation from the Japanese Peace Committee

Author: 
Date: 1966-05-31
Edition: 6

    Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Honorary President of the China-Japan Friendship Association, and his wife received and hosted a banquet tonight for the entire members of the Third Delegation of the Japanese Peace Committee to China, led by Kaneko Reigaku and Asahina Yasushi. The hosts and guests held cordial and friendly talks.
    Zhang Xiangshan, Zhao Anbo, Zheng Senyu, Wang Xiaoyun, Sun Fuling and others attended the reception and banquet. Japanese peace activist in Beijing, Kinkazu Saionji and his wife were also present.

### 郭沫若接见日本和委会代表团

1966-05-31
第6版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见日本和委会代表团
    新华社三十日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席、中日友好协会名誉会长郭沫若和夫人今晚接见并设宴招待由金子灵学、朝比奈靖司率领的日本和平委员会第三次访华代表团全体成员。宾主进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    参加接见和宴会的有张香山、赵安博、郑森禹、王晓云、孙孚凌等。在京日本和平人士西园寺公一和夫人也在座。
