Title: Sino-Czech Friendship Association Holds Film Reception to Celebrate Czechoslovak National Day
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-08
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The Chinese-Czechoslovak Friendship Association held a film reception this afternoon to celebrate the National Day of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic.

    Among those present at the reception were Situ Huimin, Secretary-General of the Sino-Czech Friendship Association, and Chen Boqing, Jin Guangzu, Zhou You, Yu Yixuan, and other relevant figures.

    Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Križtek, and diplomatic officials from the embassy were invited to attend the reception. The Chinese film "Jingpo Girl" was screened at the reception.

### 中捷友协为庆祝捷国庆举行电影招待会

1966-05-08
第5版()
专栏：

    中捷友协为庆祝捷国庆举行电影招待会
    新华社七日讯　中国捷克斯洛伐克友好协会为庆祝捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国国庆，今天下午举行电影招待会。
    出席招待会的有中捷友协秘书长司徒慧敏和陈伯清、金光祖、周游、喻宜萱等有关方面人士。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使克日斯特克和大使馆外交官员应邀出席了招待会。招待会上放映了中国影片《景颇姑娘》。
