Title: Head of Japan-China Friendship Association Youth and Women Delegation Says Chinese Youth of the Mao Zedong Era Developed a Spirit of Perpetual Revolution; Japanese Youth Must Master Mao Zedong Thought

Author:
Date: 1966-05-09
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, 8th -- According to a report in the Hong Kong newspaper *Wen Wei Po*, Sugita Hideko, head of the Japan-China Friendship Association Youth and Women Delegation, told Hong Kong newspaper reporters here that Japanese youth must master Mao Zedong Thought.

    This delegation of eighteen people returned to their country via Hong Kong on the evening of the 4th after completing a five-week visit to China. Sugita Hideko said that Chinese youth love their great leader Mao Zedong and are all wholeheartedly studying and applying Mao Zedong Thought. Chinese youth have developed a spirit of fearing neither hardship nor death, and of perpetual revolution. They have ambitious goals and lofty aspirations. Chinese youth have bravely shouldered the arduous task of thoroughly defeating U.S. imperialism's aggression and supporting the revolutionary struggles of people in all countries of the world.

    She said: "We, the young men and women of Japan who live under the control of U.S. imperialism, deeply feel that the Chinese youth who live in the Mao Zedong era are happy." She said, "Chinese youth are a model for Japanese youth and the youth of the whole world. We must learn from Chinese youth and master Mao Zedong Thought."

    She expressed that awakened Japanese youth are determined to unite with Chinese youth in one front, advancing hand in hand and shoulder to shoulder, to wage a resolute struggle against imperialism and reactionaries.

### 日中友协青年妇女代表团长说毛泽东时代中国青年养成永远革命的精神  日本青年一定要把毛泽东思想学到手

1966-05-09
第5版()
专栏：

    日中友协青年妇女代表团长说毛泽东时代中国青年养成永远革命的精神
    日本青年一定要把毛泽东思想学到手
    新华社香港八日电　据香港《文汇报》报道，日中友协青年妇女代表团团长杉田日出子在这里对香港报纸记者说，日本青年一定要把毛泽东思想学到手。
    这个一行十八人的代表团是结束了在我国为时五个星期的访问后，于四日晚经香港回国的。杉田日出子说，中国青年热爱伟大的领袖毛泽东，个个一心活学活用毛泽东思想。中国青年已养成不怕苦、不怕死、永远革命的精神。他们树雄心，立大志，胸中有远大的目标。中国青年已勇敢地担负起彻底打败美帝国主义侵略，支援世界各国人民革命斗争的艰巨任务。
    她说：“我们在美帝国主义控制下生活的日本青年男女，深切地感到，生活在毛泽东时代的中国青年是幸福的。”她说：“中国青年是我们日本青年和全世界青年的模范，我们一定要向中国青年看齐，把毛泽东思想学到手。”
    她表示，日本觉醒的青年决心和中国青年联成一条阵线，手携手，肩并肩前进，为反对帝国主义和反动派进行坚决的斗争。
