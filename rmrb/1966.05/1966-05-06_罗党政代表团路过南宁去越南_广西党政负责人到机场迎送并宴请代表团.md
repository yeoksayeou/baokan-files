Title: Romanian Party and Government Delegation Passes Through Nanning on Its Way to Vietnam; Guangxi Party and Government Leaders Welcome and See Off the Delegation at the Airport and Host a Banquet

Author: 
Date: 1966-05-06
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, May 5th – The Romanian Party and Government Delegation, led by Emil Bodnăraș, Member of the Central Executive Committee of the Romanian Communist Party, Member of the Standing Presidium, and First Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian Socialist Republic, arrived in Nanning by special plane at noon today from Yangon, en route to visit the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at the airport by Qiao Xiaoguang, Second Secretary of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, He Ximing, Secretary of the Autonomous Region Party Committee Secretariat and Vice-Chairman of the Autonomous Region, and other responsible individuals. Young women presented bouquets of flowers to all the distinguished guests of the delegation.
    At noon, Qiao Xiaoguang held a banquet to welcome the Romanian Party and Government Delegation.
    At the banquet, the hosts and guests raised their glasses to jointly wish that the friendship and unity between the Chinese and Romanian Parties and peoples would be further consolidated and developed, and wished the heroic Vietnamese people brilliant victories in their great anti-American and national salvation struggle against American imperialist aggression.
    After a short rest in Nanning, the distinguished guests boarded a special plane to Hanoi, the capital of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. Qiao Xiaoguang, He Ximing, and others saw them off at the airport.
    Present at the airport to welcome and see off the distinguished guests and attending the banquet were also responsible individuals from the Party, government, and military of the Autonomous Region and Nanning City.
    Phan Manh Cu, Consul General of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Nanning, and Deputy Consul Le Quang Dien also went to the airport to welcome and see off the distinguished guests and were invited to attend the banquet.

### 罗党政代表团路过南宁去越南  广西党政负责人到机场迎送并宴请代表团

1966-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

    罗党政代表团路过南宁去越南
    广西党政负责人到机场迎送并宴请代表团
    新华社南宁五日电　由罗马尼亚共产党中央执行委员会委员、常设主席团委员、罗马尼亚社会主义共和国部长会议第一副主席埃米尔·波德纳拉希率领的罗马尼亚党政代表团，在前往越南民主共和国访问途中，今天中午由仰光乘专机到达南宁。贵宾们在机场上受到了中共广西壮族自治区委员会第二书记乔晓光，自治区党委书记处书记、自治区副主席贺希明等负责人的热烈欢迎。女青年向代表团全体贵宾献了鲜花。
    午间，乔晓光为欢迎罗马尼亚党政代表团举行了宴会。
    宾主在宴会上举杯共祝中罗两党和两国人民的友谊和团结进一步巩固和发展，祝英雄的越南人民在反对美帝国主义侵略的伟大抗美救国斗争中取得辉煌的胜利。
    贵宾们在南宁稍事休息后，即乘专机去越南民主共和国首都河内。乔晓光、贺希明等到机场送行。
    今天到机场迎送贵宾并出席宴会作陪的，还有自治区和南宁市党、政府、军队的负责人。
    越南民主共和国驻南宁总领事潘孟居、副领事黎光奠也到机场迎送贵宾，并应邀出席宴会。
