Title: Demonstrations Against Johnson in Long Beach; New York Citizens Hold Anti-Vietnam War Demonstrations to Block US Troops Participating in Parade
Author:
Date: 1966-05-23
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, May 21st Dispatch from Long Beach: More than twenty people in Long Beach, California, demonstrated against U.S. President Johnson on the 20th to protest the American war of aggression in Vietnam.

    On this day, while Johnson was addressing the United Automobile Workers of America National Convention being held in Long Beach via telephone from the White House, demonstrators unfurled banners and placards against the war of aggression in Vietnam inside the venue, which read: "Stop Johnson's War!" "End the Vietnam War!"

    Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd Dispatch from New York: Hundreds of New York citizens held demonstrations on the 21st to oppose the war of aggression against Vietnam during a large-scale parade held by the American authorities for "Armed Forces Day."

    According to reports, many demonstrators broke through the heavy police cordon, sat on the street where the parade was being held, and shouted slogans: "End the Vietnam War!" "Bring our youth home!"

    The demonstrators forced the troops participating in the parade to stop. The authorities dispatched a large number of police and police cars to forcibly drag them away.

### 美国长滩市群众向约翰逊示威  纽约市民举行反侵越示威阻拦参加阅兵的美军

1966-05-23
第6版()
专栏：

    美国长滩市群众向约翰逊示威
    纽约市民举行反侵越示威阻拦参加阅兵的美军
    新华社二十一日讯　长滩消息：美国加利福尼亚州长滩市二十多名群众二十日向美国总统约翰逊示威，反对美国侵略越南的战争。
    这一天，当约翰逊在白宫通过电话向正在长滩举行的美国联合汽车工人工会全国代表大会发表演说时，示威者在会场内打出了反对侵越战争的旗帜和标语牌，上面写着：“停止约翰逊的战争！”“结束越南战争！”
    新华社二十二日讯　纽约消息：纽约市民数百人二十一日在美国当局为“武装部队日”举行大规模阅兵时，举行示威，反对侵略越南的战争。
    据报道，许多示威者冲破警察的重重阻拦，在举行阅兵的大街上席地而坐，并且高呼口号：“结束越南战争！”“让我们的青年回家来！”。
    示威者们迫使参加阅兵的军队停顿下来。当局出动了大批警察、警车把他们强行架走。
