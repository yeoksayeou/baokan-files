Title: Huang Zhen, Chinese Ambassador to France, Holds Reception for "China Pavilion Day" at the Paris Exposition

Author:
Date: 1966-05-22
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 20th. Huang Zhen, Chinese Ambassador to France, and his wife, along with Hu Ming, head of the Chinese Exhibition Delegation participating in the Paris Exposition, held a grand reception inside the China Pavilion on the evening of the 20th for the "China Pavilion Day."

    Nearly one thousand guests attended the reception, including Daniel Mayer, Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the National Assembly; former Prime Minister Pierre Mendès France; Hubert Rousselier, Chairman of the French National Center for Foreign Trade; André Mésier, Chairman of the Paris Exposition; other well-known figures from all walks of life in France; and ambassadors and other diplomatic officials from many countries.

    The reception was filled with a friendly atmosphere.

    The guests raised their glasses to congratulate the Chinese people for creating such beautiful and exquisite arts and crafts.

    Since the opening of the China Pavilion three days ago, it has received more than 77,000 visitors. In the visitor's book, people have written many enthusiastic words of praise.

### 为巴黎博览会“中国馆日”  我驻法大使黄镇举行招待会

1966-05-22
第5版()
专栏：

    为巴黎博览会“中国馆日”
    我驻法大使黄镇举行招待会
    新华社巴黎二十日电　中国驻法国大使黄镇和夫人以及参加巴黎博览会的中国展览团团长胡明，二十日晚上在中国馆内为当天的“中国馆日”举行了盛大的招待会。
    出席招待会的客人将近一千人，其中包括国民议会外交委员会主席丹尼埃·麦耶、前总理皮埃尔·孟戴斯—弗朗斯、法国对外贸易全国中心主席余贝尔·鲁塞利埃和巴黎博览会主席安德烈·麦西埃等法国各界著名人士和许多国家的大使及其他外交官员。
    招待会上洋溢着友好的气氛。
    客人们举杯祝贺中国人民创造了如此美丽精致的工艺美术。
    中国馆开馆三天以来，已接待了七万七千多观众。在观众留言簿上，人们写下了许多热烈赞扬的话语。
