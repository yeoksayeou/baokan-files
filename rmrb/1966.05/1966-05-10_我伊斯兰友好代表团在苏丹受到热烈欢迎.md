Title: My Islamic Friendship Delegation Warmly Welcomed in Sudan
Author:
Date: 1966-05-10
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Khartoum, May 8th. The Chinese Islamic Friendship Delegation led by Zhang Jie left Khartoum on the 7th to visit Port Sudan.

    The delegation arrived in Khartoum on May 1st at the invitation of the Sudanese government.

    The delegation received a warm welcome during its visit to Khartoum. They were received successively by Abdul Majid Geissa, Grand Qadi in charge of Islamic Sharia courts, Hassan Awadalla, Minister of Education, and Khalifa Abbas Obeid, Undersecretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Yu Peiwen, the Chinese Ambassador to Sudan, was also present at the receptions.

    Kamil Bagir, President of the Islamic University, and Hojali Osman, Director of the Sudanese Religious Affairs Bureau, hosted banquets for the delegation. The delegation visited the Omdurman Islamic University, the Al-Alti College, and other institutions.

### 我伊斯兰友好代表团在苏丹受到热烈欢迎

1966-05-10
第5版()
专栏：

    我伊斯兰友好代表团在苏丹受到热烈欢迎
    新华社喀土穆八日电　由张杰率领的中国伊斯兰友好代表团七日离开喀土穆前往苏丹港进行访问。
    代表团是应苏丹政府的邀请于五月一日到达喀土穆的。
    代表团在喀土穆访问期间，受到了热烈欢迎。他们先后受到掌理伊斯兰教堂法的大审判官阿卜杜勒·马吉德·吉塞萨、教育部长哈桑·阿瓦达拉和外交部次长哈利法·阿巴斯·奥贝德的接见。中国驻苏丹大使俞沛文在接见时也在座。
    伊斯兰大学校长卡米勒·巴格尔和苏丹宗教事务局局长霍加利·奥斯曼曾分别设宴招待代表团。代表团参观了恩图曼伊斯兰大学、阿尔提学院等。
