Title: Chairman Mao's Two Works Published in Ceylon
Author:
Date: 1966-05-18
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, May 17th: Tamil versions of Chairman Mao Zedong's works "On New Democracy" and "Combat Liberalism" have been published here. These two works by Chairman Mao were published by the Colombo People's Publishing House and translated by Kumaralingam.

### 毛主席两篇著作在锡兰出版

1966-05-18
第5版()
专栏：

    毛主席两篇著作在锡兰出版
    新华社科伦坡十七日电　毛泽东主席的著作《新民主主义论》和《反对自由主义》的泰米尔文版已经在这里发行。毛主席的这两篇著作由科伦坡人民出版社出版，译者是库马拉林加姆。
