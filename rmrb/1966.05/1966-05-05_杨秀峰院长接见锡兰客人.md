Title: President Yang Xiufeng Receives Guests from Ceylon
Author:
Date: 1966-05-05
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, April 4th – Yang Xiufeng, President of the Supreme People's Court, received Alardiyawana, Chairman of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association, and his wife this afternoon, and held a cordial and friendly conversation with them. Lin Lin, Chen Jin, and other responsible persons from relevant departments were present at the meeting.

### 杨秀峰院长接见锡兰客人

1966-05-05
第5版()
专栏：

    杨秀峰院长接见锡兰客人
    新华社四日讯　最高人民法院院长杨秀峰今天下午接见锡兰中国友好协会主席阿拉季亚瓦纳和夫人，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有林林、陈金等有关方面负责人。
