Title: Guo Moruo Receives and Banquets Japanese Guests
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-21
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, May 20th: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Honorary President of the Sino-Japanese Friendship Association, received and banqueted the visiting delegation of the Japan Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, headed by Minoru Ito, on the 19th. The guests and hosts had a cordial and friendly conversation.



**Named Entities**

郭沫若	Guo Moruo
伊藤实	Ito Minoru
中国人民保卫世界和平委员会	Chinese People's Committee for World Peace
中日友好协会	Sino-Japanese Friendship Association
日本亚非团结委员会	Japan Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity
新华社	Xinhua News Agency

### 郭沫若接见并宴请日本客人

1966-05-21
第6版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见并宴请日本客人
    新华社二十日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席、中日友好协会名誉会长郭沫若十九日接见并宴请以伊藤实为首的日本亚非团结委员会访华代表团。宾主进行了亲切友好的谈话。
