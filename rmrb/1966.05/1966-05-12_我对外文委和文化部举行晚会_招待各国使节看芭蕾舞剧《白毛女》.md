Title: Foreign Culture Commission and Ministry of Culture Host Soiree for Foreign Envoys to See Ballet "The White-Haired Girl"
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-12
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 11th. The Foreign Culture Commission and the Ministry of Culture held a soiree tonight to host foreign envoys to China, diplomatic officials, and their wives to watch the modern ballet "The White-Haired Girl."
    Accompanying them to watch the performance were Shi Huaibi, Vice-Chairman of the Foreign Culture Commission, Xu Guangxiao, Vice-Minister of Culture, and responsible persons from relevant departments such as Hao Deqing, Sun Xiaocun, and Zhang Tiesheng.

### 我对外文委和文化部举行晚会  招待各国使节看芭蕾舞剧《白毛女》

1966-05-12
第5版()
专栏：

    我对外文委和文化部举行晚会
    招待各国使节看芭蕾舞剧《白毛女》
    新华社十一日讯　对外文委和文化部今晚举行晚会，招待各国驻华使节以及外交官员和他们的夫人观看现代芭蕾舞剧《白毛女》。
    陪同观看演出的，有对外文委副主任史怀璧、文化部副部长徐光霄，以及有关方面负责人郝德青、孙晓村、张铁生等。
