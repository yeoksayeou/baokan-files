Title: Iraqi Foreign Ministry Economic Department Director Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1966-05-08
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 4th -- Motawali, Director of the Economic Department of the Iraqi Foreign Ministry, and his wife, who were invited to visit our country, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.
    Du Yu Yun, Assistant Minister of Foreign Trade, and others went to the airport to see them off.

### 伊拉克外交部经济司司长回国

1966-05-08
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克外交部经济司司长回国
    新华社四日讯　应邀来我国访问的伊拉克外交部经济司司长莫特瓦利和夫人，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    前往机场送行的，有对外贸易部部长助理杜毓沄等。
