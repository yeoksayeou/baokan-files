    Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Journalists from Four Nordic Countries

    According to Xinhua News Agency, May 17th, Vice Premier Chen Yi received journalists from Denmark, Finland, Norway, and Sweden this afternoon and answered their questions.

    The journalists were: Holmgaard, International Politics Editor of the Danish "Information"; Paulsen, Feature Editor and Foreign Affairs Writer of the Danish News Agency; Valtasaari, Editor of the Finnish Broadcasting Company; Rautonen, Editor of the Finnish magazine "Help"; Slaattelid, Chairman of the Norwegian Press Association and Editor of "Verdens Gang"; Tollevik, Editor of the Norwegian Bergen "Arbeiderbladet"; Branting, Foreign Affairs Editor of the Swedish "Dagens Nyheter"; and Andersson, Foreign Correspondent of the Swedish Malmö "Arbetet".

    Also present at the reception were Li Wu, Qin Jialin, Bi Jilong, and other responsible persons from relevant departments.

    On the afternoon of the 16th, Qin Jialin, Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, held a reception for journalists from the four Nordic countries of Denmark, Finland, Norway, and Sweden.

    Representatives from the embassies of Denmark, Finland, Norway, and Sweden in China were also invited to attend the reception. (Attached picture)

### 陈毅副总理接见北欧四国新闻工作者

1966-05-18
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见北欧四国新闻工作者
    据新华社十七日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见丹麦、芬兰、挪威、瑞典等北欧四国新闻工作者，回答了他们提出的问题。
    这些新闻工作者是：丹麦《消息报》国际政治版编辑霍尔姆高，丹麦通讯社特写部编辑兼外事撰稿人鲍尔森；芬兰广播公司编辑瓦尔塔沙里，芬兰《帮助》杂志编辑拉乌托宁；挪威新闻协会主席、《世界之路》报编辑斯莱顿，挪威卑尔根《工人报》编辑托勒维克；瑞典《每日新闻》外事编辑勃朗台尔，瑞典马尔摩《劳动报》外事记者安德逊。
    接见时在座的有李伍、秦加林、毕季龙等有关方面负责人。
    十六日下午，外交部新闻司司长秦加林举行酒会，招待丹麦、芬兰、挪威、瑞典等北欧四个国家的新闻工作者。
    丹麦、芬兰、挪威、瑞典四国驻中国大使馆的代表也应邀出席了酒会。（附图片）
    五月十七日，陈毅副总理接见北欧四国新闻工作者。
      新华社记者　吴元柳摄
