Title: Chinese Cultural Friendship Delegation Leaves for Visits to Afghanistan and Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1966-05-16
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, May 13th—The Chinese Government Cultural Friendship Delegation, led by Shi Huaibi, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, left by plane this morning for visits to Afghanistan and Pakistan.  Those who went to the airport to see them off included Ren Yinglun, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and others.
    Also present at the airport to see them off were Durrani, Chargé d'Affaires of the Pakistani Embassy in China, and Mohammad Siddiq, First Secretary of the Afghan Embassy in China.

### 我文化友好代表团赴阿富汗巴基斯坦访问

1966-05-16
第6版()
专栏：

    我文化友好代表团赴阿富汗巴基斯坦访问
    新华社十三日讯　由对外文委副主任史怀璧率领的中国政府文化友好代表团，今天上午乘飞机赴阿富汗和巴基斯坦访问。前往机场送行的有对外文委副主任任映仑等。
    巴基斯坦驻华大使馆公使杜拉尼、阿富汗驻华大使馆一等秘书穆罕默德·西迪克等也前往送行。
