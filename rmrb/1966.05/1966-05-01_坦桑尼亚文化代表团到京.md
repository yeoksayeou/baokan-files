    Tanzanian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, April 30th. The Tanzanian Cultural Delegation, led by its head Joseph Kasella Bantu, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to China at the invitation of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Ministry of Culture. Those who welcomed them at the airport included Shi Huai'bi, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Xu Guangxiao, Vice-Minister of Culture, and Gong Dafei, head of relevant units.
    Tanzanian Ambassador to China Wazir Juma also welcomed them at the airport.

### 坦桑尼亚文化代表团到京

1966-05-01
第6版()
专栏：

    坦桑尼亚文化代表团到京
    新华社三十日讯　由团长约瑟夫·卡西拉·班图率领的坦桑尼亚文化代表团一行四人，应对外文委和文化部邀请来我国访问，今天乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有对外文委副主任史怀璧、文化部副部长徐光霄和有关单位负责人宫达非等。
    坦桑尼亚驻华大使瓦齐里·朱马也到机场欢迎。
