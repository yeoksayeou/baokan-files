Title: China's Youth Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Africa
Author:
Date: 1966-05-02
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, 1st -- The Chinese Youth Delegation of seven people, headed by Hu Qili, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, returned to Beijing by plane today after paying friendly visits to Congo (Brazzaville), the United Arab Republic, Tanzania, Guinea, and Mauritania.



**Named Entities**


中国  China
北京  Beijing
刚果（布）  Congo (Brazzaville)
阿联  United Arab Republic

### 我青年代表团访非后回京

1966-05-02
第3版()
专栏：

    我青年代表团访非后回京
    新华社一日讯　以中华全国青年联合会副主席胡启立为首的中国青年代表团一行七人，在对刚果（布）、阿联、坦桑尼亚、几内亚和毛里塔尼亚等国进行友好访问之后，今天乘飞机回到北京。
