Title: Matsumura Kenzō Leaves Beijing for Visits to Other Places

Author:
Date: 1966-05-21
Edition: 6

    Matsumura Kenzō, advisor to the Japanese Liberal Democratic Party, along with Takeyama Yūtarō, Matsumoto Shun'ichi, and others, a group of six, left Beijing by plane this morning to visit southern China.
    Present at the airport to see them off were Liao Chengzhi and his wife, as well as Hu Yuzhi, Liu Xiwēn, Zhao Anbo, Yong Longgui, Wang Yinpu, Li Xinnong, Xiao Xiangqian, and other relevant personnel.
    Koichi Saiyonji, a Japanese peace activist in Beijing, also came to the airport to see them off.

### 松村谦三离北京去外地访问

1966-05-21
第6版()
专栏：

    松村谦三离北京去外地访问
    新华社二十日讯　日本自由民主党顾问松村谦三先生及竹山祐太郎、松本俊一等一行六人，今天早晨乘飞机离开北京，前往我国南方访问。
    到机场欢送的，有廖承志和夫人及其他有关方面人士胡愈之、刘希文、赵安博、勇龙桂、王荫圃、李新农、萧向前等。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也到机场欢送。
