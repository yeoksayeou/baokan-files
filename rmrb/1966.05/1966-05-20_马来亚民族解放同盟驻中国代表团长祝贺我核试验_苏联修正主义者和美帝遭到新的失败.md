Title: Leader of the Malayan National Liberation League Delegation to China Congratulates China on Nuclear Test: Soviet Revisionists and US Imperialism Suffer New Defeat
Author:
Date: 1966-05-20
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 19th. Sharma, head of the Malayan National Liberation League Delegation to China, today wrote to Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, warmly congratulating China on the success of its nuclear explosion.
    The congratulatory letter stated that China's possession and development of nuclear weapons is a great encouragement and impetus to all oppressed peoples. This new explosion will further encourage the Malayan people to smash the product of British and American neo-colonialism, "Malaysia," and strive for a truly independent, democratic, and unified Malaya.
    The congratulatory letter said that this nuclear explosion has brought a new defeat to the nuclear blackmail of US imperialism and its accomplices, and to the Soviet modern revisionists' conspiracy to collude with US imperialism to maintain their nuclear monopoly and undermine the revolutionary struggles of Asia, Africa, Latin America, and all oppressed nations and peoples.
    The Malayan people, together with all freedom-loving people in the world, are jubilant about this new contribution made by the Chinese people in defending world peace.

### 马来亚民族解放同盟驻中国代表团长祝贺我核试验  苏联修正主义者和美帝遭到新的失败

1966-05-20
第5版()
专栏：

    马来亚民族解放同盟驻中国代表团长祝贺我核试验
    苏联修正主义者和美帝遭到新的失败
    新华社十九日讯　马来亚民族解放同盟驻中国代表团团长沙尔玛，今天写信给中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志，热烈祝贺我核爆炸成功。
    贺信中说，中国拥有核武器和发展核武器，对所有被压迫人民是一种巨大的鼓舞和推动。这次新的爆炸，将进一步鼓舞马来亚人民粉碎英、美新殖民主义的产物——“马来西亚”，以及争取真正独立、民主和统一的马来亚的斗争。
    贺信说，这一次核爆炸，使美帝国主义及其帮凶的核讹诈，苏联现代修正主义者企图勾结美帝国主义来维持其核垄断和破坏亚、非、拉美和所有被压迫民族和人民的革命斗争的阴谋，遭到了新的失败。
    马来亚人民和世界所有爱好自由的人民一起，对中国人民在保卫世界和平方面所作的这一个新的贡献，感到欢欣鼓舞。
