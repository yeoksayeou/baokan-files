Title: Signing of the China-Afghanistan Cultural Cooperation Agreement Execution Plan
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-28
Edition: 5

    According to a dispatch from Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, May 24th: The 1966 execution plan for the China-Afghanistan Cultural Cooperation Agreement was signed here on the afternoon of the 24th.
    Representing their respective governments at the signing were Shi Huaibi, head of the Chinese Government Cultural Friendship Delegation and Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries of China; and Mohammad Nazim A’lia, Deputy Minister of Propaganda and Culture of Afghanistan.
    At the signing ceremony, Shi Huaibi and A’lia both expressed that the signing of this plan will further enhance mutual understanding and friendly relations between China and Afghanistan.

### 中阿文化合作协定执行计划签字

1966-05-28
第5版()
专栏：

    中阿文化合作协定执行计划签字
    据新华社喀布尔二十四日电　中国—阿富汗文化合作协定一九六六年执行计划二十四日下午在这里签字。
    代表双方政府签字的是：中国政府文化友好代表团团长、中国对外文化联络委员会副主任史怀璧和阿富汗宣传和文化部副大臣穆罕默德·纳季姆·阿里亚。
    史怀璧和阿里亚在签字仪式上一致表示，签订这个计划将进一步增进中国和阿富汗之间的互相了解和友好关系。
