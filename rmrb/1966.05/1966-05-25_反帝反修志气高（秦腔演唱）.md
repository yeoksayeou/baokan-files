Title: High Aspirations to Oppose Imperialism and Revisionism (Qinqiang Opera Excerpt)
Author: Wang Juzheng
Date: 1966-05-25
Edition: 4

    High Aspirations to Oppose Imperialism and Revisionism (Qinqiang Opera Excerpt)
    Shaanxi Weinan Commune Member Wang Juzheng
  Chairman Mao's works are truly excellent,
  Pointing out a path for us,
  The socialist sunny road,
  The capitalist single-plank bridge.
  It's like a lighthouse shining on the ocean,
  It's like a war drum beating in the heart,
  Every word and sentence is a treasure,
  It's like a guiding star for advancement.
  It tells us poor and lower-middle peasants to rely on each other closely,
  We will never forgive those who make trouble;
  It tells us to never forget class struggle,
  Grasp the gun firmly in our hands;
  It tells us not to be arrogant in the face of victory,
  Walk across the silver bridge onto the golden bridge;
  It tells us not to waver in the face of difficulties,
  With the bold spirit to change the world.
  The people of the whole country are learning it,
  With high aspirations to oppose imperialism and revisionism,
  The people of the whole world are learning it,
  The revolutionary red flag is flying everywhere.

### 反帝反修志气高（秦腔演唱）
王居正
1966-05-25
第4版()
专栏：工农兵诗画选

    反帝反修志气高（秦腔演唱）
    陕西渭南社员　王居正
  毛主席著作实在好，
  给咱指出路一条，
  社会主义阳关道，
  资本主义独木桥。
  它好比灯塔在海洋照，
  它好比战鼓心上敲，
  它一字一句都是宝，
  它好比前进的指路标。
  它叫咱贫下中农紧依靠，
  谁要捣乱决不饶；
  它叫咱把阶级斗争永不忘，
  手中的枪杆要攥牢；
  它叫咱胜利面前别骄傲，
  走了银桥上金桥；
  它叫咱困难面前别动摇，
  改天换地胆气豪。
  全国人民都学它，
  反帝反修志气高，
  全世界人民都学它，
  革命红旗处处飘。
