Title: Chairman Zhu Receives the Korean Trade Union Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-05-16
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, May 15th: Chairman Zhu De received the members of the Korean Trade Union Delegation, led by Kim Kuk-sam, Director of the Organization Department of the Central Committee of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea, this afternoon, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Ma Chun-ku, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others. Kim Soo-jong, Second Secretary of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, was also present.

### 朱委员长接见朝鲜工会代表团

1966-05-16
第1版()
专栏：

    朱委员长接见朝鲜工会代表团
    新华社十五日讯　朱德委员长今天下午接见了由朝鲜职业总同盟中央委员会组织部部长金国三率领的朝鲜工会代表团成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中华全国总工会副主席马纯古等。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使馆二等秘书金寿钟也在座。
