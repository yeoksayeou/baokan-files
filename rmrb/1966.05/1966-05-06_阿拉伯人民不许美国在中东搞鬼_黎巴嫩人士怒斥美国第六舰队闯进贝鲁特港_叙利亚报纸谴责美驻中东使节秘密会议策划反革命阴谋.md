Title: Arab People Do Not Allow the U.S. to Cause Trouble in the Middle East: Lebanese People Denounce the U.S. Sixth Fleet for Entering Beirut Port; Syrian Newspaper Condemns Secret Meeting of U.S. Envoys in the Middle East for Planning Counter-Revolutionary Conspiracies
Author:
Date: 1966-05-06
Edition: 6

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, May 3rd: Beirut News: The public in Lebanon and other Arab countries condemn the secret three-day meeting of U.S. diplomatic envoys to the Middle East region held in Beirut, the capital of Lebanon, and simultaneously condemn the U.S. Sixth Fleet for entering Beirut Port again.
    This meeting of U.S. envoys, which closed on April 29th, was presided over by U.S. Deputy Undersecretary of State for Middle Eastern Affairs Raymond Hare, who had just attended the CENTO Council meeting in Ankara, the capital of Turkey, where he received direct instructions from Secretary of State Rusk.
    The Middle East News Agency quoted well-informed sources in Beirut as saying that the meeting drafted a plan for U.S. policy in the region. The issues discussed included the "Islamic Treaty," the provision of weapons to Israel, U.S. relations with Arab countries, the Yemen problem, etc.
    The Middle East News Agency said that Lebanese sources pointed out that the holding of this meeting while the U.S. Sixth Fleet was docked in Beirut port proves that the U.S. is currently pursuing a policy of power diplomacy.
    The Lebanese public and political figures have condemned the Sixth Fleet's docking in Lebanese waters. Kamal Joumblatt, Minister of Public Works in the new Lebanese cabinet, said: "This practice violates the feelings of Lebanon and aims to create tension in the Middle East."
    Syrian newspaper *Al-Thawra* published an editorial on April 27th condemning the meeting of U.S. envoys to the Middle East. It pointed out that the meeting was held amidst the engine noise of U.S. Sixth Fleet warships and the reactionary clamor of the U.S. piecing together an "Islamic Treaty."
    Prior to this, *Al-Thawra* also condemned the meeting as merely plotting conspiracies against the people's revolution. It said, however, that the fate of this meeting will be the same as the series of meetings held by the U.S. in Saigon, Honolulu, and Latin America—doomed to failure.

### 阿拉伯人民不许美国在中东搞鬼  黎巴嫩人士怒斥美国第六舰队闯进贝鲁特港  叙利亚报纸谴责美驻中东使节秘密会议策划反革命阴谋

1966-05-06
第6版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯人民不许美国在中东搞鬼
    黎巴嫩人士怒斥美国第六舰队闯进贝鲁特港
    叙利亚报纸谴责美驻中东使节秘密会议策划反革命阴谋
    据新华社开罗三日电　贝鲁特消息：黎巴嫩和其他阿拉伯国家公众谴责美国驻中东地区外交使节在黎巴嫩首都贝鲁特举行三天秘密会议，同时谴责美国第六舰队又开进贝鲁特港。
    这次在四月二十九日闭幕的美国使节会议是由美国负责中东事务的副国务卿雷蒙德·黑尔主持的，他刚参加了在土耳其首都安卡拉举行的中央条约组织理事会会议，在那里他受到国务卿腊斯克的直接指示。
    中东通讯社援引贝鲁特的消息灵通人士的话说，这次会议为美国在这个地区的政策草拟了计划。它讨论的问题包括“伊斯兰条约”、向以色列提供武器、美国同阿拉伯国家的关系、也门问题等。
    中东通讯社说，黎巴嫩人士指出，在美国第六舰队在贝鲁特港口停泊期间举行这次会议，这证明美国目前正在执行强权外交政策。
    黎巴嫩的公众和政界人士纷纷谴责第六舰队在黎巴嫩水域停泊。黎巴嫩新内阁的公共工程部长卡迈勒·琼卜拉特说：“这种做法侵犯了黎巴嫩的感情，目的是要在中东地区制造紧张气氛。”
    叙利亚《革命报》四月二十七日发表社论谴责美国驻中东地区使节会议。它指出，这次会议是在美国第六舰队军舰的发动机声中和美国拼凑“伊斯兰条约”的反动叫嚣声中举行的。
    在这以前，《革命报》还谴责这次会议只是为了策划反对人民革命的阴谋。它说，但是，这次会议的命运也将象美国在西贡、檀香山和拉丁美洲召开的一系列的会议一样，遭到失败。
