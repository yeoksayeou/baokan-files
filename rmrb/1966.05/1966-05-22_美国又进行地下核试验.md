Title: U.S. Conducts Another Underground Nuclear Test
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-22
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 20th: The United States Atomic Energy Commission announced that the United States conducted another underground nuclear test at the Nevada Test Site on the 19th.
    This is the twentieth nuclear weapon test officially announced by the United States this year, and the seventy-eighth nuclear weapon test conducted by the United States since the signing of the Moscow Partial Nuclear Test Ban Treaty in August 1963.

### 美国又进行地下核试验

1966-05-22
第6版()
专栏：

    美国又进行地下核试验
    新华社二十日讯　华盛顿消息：据美国原子能委员会宣布，美国十九日又在内华达试验场进行了一次地下核试验。
    这是美国今年官方宣布的第二十次核武器试验，也是一九六三年八月莫斯科部分禁止核试验条约签订以来美国的第七十八次核武器试验。
