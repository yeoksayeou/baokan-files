Title: Ambassador Huang Hua Meets Chairman of the Palestine Liberation Organization

Author: 
Date: 1966-05-14
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, May 11th -- Chinese Ambassador to the United Arab Republic Huang Hua met here on the 11th with Said Shuqairi, Chairman of the Palestine Liberation Organization. They held cordial talks. Ambassador Huang Hua reiterated to Shuqairi that the Chinese people and government support the struggle of the Palestinian people to return to their homeland. The Chairman of the Palestine Liberation Organization briefed the Chinese Ambassador on the situation of the Palestinian people's struggle and recent developments, and expressed gratitude for the support of the Chinese people and government.



**Named Entities**


黄华 Huang Hua
赛义德·舒凯里 Said Shuqairi
巴勒斯坦解放组织 Palestine Liberation Organization
阿联 United Arab Republic
中国 China

### 黄华大使会见巴勒斯坦解放组织主席

1966-05-14
第6版()
专栏：

    黄华大使会见巴勒斯坦解放组织主席
    新华社开罗十一日电  中国驻阿联大使黄华十一日在这里会见了巴勒斯坦解放组织主席赛义德·舒凯里。他们进行了亲切的谈话。黄华大使向舒凯里重申中国人民和政府支持巴勒斯坦人民返回家园的斗争。巴勒斯坦解放组织主席向中国大使介绍了巴勒斯坦人民斗争的情况和最近的发展，并且表示感谢中国人民和政府的支持。
