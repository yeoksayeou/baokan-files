Title: Uruguayan Guest Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-05-06
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 5th—Carlos Maria Gutierrez, editor of the Uruguayan "Adelante Semanario" (Forward Weekly), who was invited by the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 4th.



**Named Entities**

中国-拉丁美洲友好协会	Zhongguo-Latin Meizhou Youhao Xiehui (Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association)
乌拉圭  Wūlāguī (Uruguay)
卡洛斯·马里亚·古铁雷斯  Kǎluòsī·Mǎlìyà·Gǔtiělěisī (Carlos Maria Gutierrez)
北京  Běijīng (Beijing)
新华社  Xīnhuá Shè (Xinhua News Agency)
前进周报  Qiánjìn Zhōubào (Adelante Semanario/Forward Weekly)

### 乌拉圭一客人到京

1966-05-06
第5版()
专栏：

    乌拉圭一客人到京
    新华社五日讯　应中拉友协邀请来我国访问的乌拉圭《前进周报》编辑卡洛斯·马里亚·古铁雷斯，已在四日乘飞机到达北京。
