Title: Newly Appointed Romanian Ambassador Luo Visits Vice Chairman Dong Biwu
Author:
Date: 1966-05-09
Edition: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, May 8th – The newly appointed Romanian Ambassador to China, Aurel Duma, paid a visit to the Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Dong Biwu, this morning. Vice Chairman Dong Biwu held friendly talks with him.

**Named Entities**

奥列尔·杜马  Àoliè'ěr Dūmǎ  Aurel Duma
董必武  Dǒng Bìwǔ  Dong Biwu

### 罗新任大使拜会董必武副主席

1966-05-09
第5版()
专栏：

    罗新任大使拜会董必武副主席
    新华社八日讯　罗马尼亚新任驻华大使奥列尔·杜马，今天上午拜会中华人民共和国副主席董必武。董必武副主席同他进行了友好的谈话。
