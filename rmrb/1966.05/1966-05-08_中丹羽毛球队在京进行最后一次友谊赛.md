Title: China-Denmark Badminton Teams Hold Final Friendly Match in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-05-08
Edition: 5

    China-Denmark Badminton Teams Hold Final Friendly Match in Beijing
    (Xinhua News Agency, 7th) China's men's and women's badminton team one defeated the Danish men's and women's badminton team by a score of 5-0 for the second time tonight at the Beijing Gymnasium. More than five thousand people watched the exciting performances of the two sides with different styles.
    This was the eighth and final friendly match played by the visiting team in our country.

### 中丹羽毛球队在京进行最后一次友谊赛

1966-05-08
第5版()
专栏：

    中丹羽毛球队在京进行最后一次友谊赛
    据新华社七日讯　中国男女羽毛球一队今晚在北京体育馆第二次迎战丹麦男女羽毛球队时，再一次以五比○的优势获胜。有五千多人观看了双方不同风格的精采表演。
    这是客队在我国进行的第八场也是最后一场友谊比赛。
