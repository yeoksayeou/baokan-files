Title: Chairman Shehu Receives Four Chinese Delegations
Author:
Date: 1966-05-23
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, May 21st - Albanian Council of Ministers Chairman Mehmet Shehu received four Chinese delegations visiting Albania on the 21st and had very cordial and friendly talks with the members of the delegations.
    The four delegations are: the Chinese Shipping Delegation led by Peng Deqing, Vice Minister of Communications; the Chinese Agricultural Delegation led by Zhu Rong, Vice Minister of Agriculture; the Chinese Academy of Sciences Delegation led by Pei Lisheng, Vice President of the Academy of Sciences; and the Chinese Photography Delegation led by Gao Fan, Editor-in-Chief of "China Photography" and Editor-in-Chief of "Liberation Army Pictorial". Present at the reception were Albanian Minister of Agriculture Piro Dodbiba, Minister of Foreign Affairs Naser, Minister of Transportation Kirko, Rector of the National University of Tirana Ili, Secretary-General of the Council of Ministers Rusa, and Director of the Albanian Telegraph Agency and Secretary-General of the Albanian Journalists Association Simitiu.
    Also present at the reception were Wang Jinchuan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, and Li Mingshan, Economic Counselor.

### 谢胡主席接见中国四个代表团

1966-05-23
第6版()
专栏：

    谢胡主席接见中国四个代表团
    新华社地拉那二十一日电　阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席穆罕默德·谢胡二十一日接见了正在阿尔巴尼亚访问的四个中国代表团并和代表团成员进行了十分亲切友好的谈话。
    这四个代表团是：由交通部副部长彭德清率领的中国航运代表团，由农业部副部长朱荣率领的中国农业代表团，由科学院副院长裴丽生率领的中国科学院代表团和由《中国摄影》主编、《解放军画报》总编辑高帆率领的中国摄影代表团。接见时在座的有阿尔巴尼亚农业部长多德比巴，外交部长纳赛，交通部长基尔科，国立地拉那大学校长伊利，部长会议秘书长鲁沙和阿尔巴尼亚通讯社社长、阿尔巴尼亚新闻工作者协会秘书长西米季乌。
    接见时，中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办王锦川和经济参赞李鸣山也在座。
