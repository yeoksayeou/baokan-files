Title: Dutch Chargé d'Affaires in China Holds Reception to Celebrate the Birthday of the Queen of the Netherlands
Author:
Date: 1966-05-01
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th – Dutch Chargé d'Affaires to China, Jan Vixseboxse, held a reception this afternoon to celebrate the birthday of Juliana, Queen of the Netherlands.
    Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Wang Bingnan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Fu Shenglin, and responsible persons from relevant departments, as well as diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries in China, were invited to attend the reception.

### 庆祝荷兰女王诞辰  荷兰驻华代办举行招待会

1966-05-01
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝荷兰女王诞辰
    荷兰驻华代办举行招待会
    新华社三十日讯　荷兰王国驻中国代办杨克仁今天下午举行招待会，庆祝荷兰女王朱丽安娜诞辰。
    外交部副部长王炳南、对外贸易部副部长傅生麟等有关方面负责人，各国驻华使节和外交官员等，应邀出席了招待会。
