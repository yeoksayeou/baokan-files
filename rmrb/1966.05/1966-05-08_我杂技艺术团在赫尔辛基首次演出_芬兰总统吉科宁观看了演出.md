Title: Chinese Acrobatic Troupe Gives First Performance in Helsinki; Finnish President Kekkonen Attends

Author:
Date: 1966-05-08
Edition: 5

    Our Acrobatic Arts Troupe Gives First Performance in Helsinki
    Finnish President Kekkonen Attends the Performance
    (Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki) The Chinese Acrobatic Arts Troupe gave its first performance to more than 1,500 spectators in the Finnish capital on the evening of the 3rd.
    The Chinese Acrobatic Arts Troupe arrived in Helsinki on the 1st after concluding its performance tour in Stockholm.
    Finnish President Urho Kekkonen attended the performance.
    Each program performed by the Chinese acrobatic artists was warmly welcomed by the audience.
    Also attending the performance were Finnish Speaker of Parliament Rafael Paasio, Finnish Prime Minister Johannes Virolainen, senior Finnish government officials, well-known figures from all walks of life, and foreign diplomatic envoys.
    Chinese Ambassador to Finland Yue Xin also attended the performance.
    During the intermission, the Finnish President received the head and members of the Chinese Acrobatic Arts Troupe and had a friendly conversation with them. The Finnish Prime Minister and Speaker of Parliament, as well as the Chinese Ambassador, were also present at the reception.

### 我杂技艺术团在赫尔辛基首次演出  芬兰总统吉科宁观看了演出

1966-05-08
第5版()
专栏：

    我杂技艺术团在赫尔辛基首次演出
    芬兰总统吉科宁观看了演出
    据新华社讯　赫尔辛基消息：中国杂技艺术团三日晚上在芬兰首都向一千五百多位观众作了首次演出。
    中国杂技艺术团是在结束了在斯德哥尔摩的访问演出之后于一日到达赫尔辛基的。
    芬兰总统乌尔霍·吉科宁观看了这次演出。
    中国杂技艺术家演出的每个节目都受到观众热烈欢迎。
    出席观看演出的还有芬兰议会议长拉斐尔·帕西奥，芬兰总理约翰内斯·维洛莱宁，芬兰政府的高级官员以及各界知名人士和各国外交使节。
    中国驻芬兰大使岳欣也出席观看了演出。
    在演出休息时，芬兰总统接见了中国杂技艺术团的团长和团员们，并且同他们进行了友好的谈话。接见时，芬兰总理和议会议长以及中国大使也在座。
