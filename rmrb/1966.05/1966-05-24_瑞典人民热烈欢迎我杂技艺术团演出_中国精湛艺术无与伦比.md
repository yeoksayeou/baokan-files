Title: Swedish People Warmly Welcome Performance by Chinese Acrobatic Arts Troupe, China's Exquisite Art is Unparalleled
Author:
Date: 1966-05-24
Edition: 5

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm: The Chinese Acrobatic Arts Troupe concluded its performances in Gothenburg, Sweden's second-largest city, and departed on the 17th for the Netherlands.
    During the troupe's stay in Gothenburg, from May 11th to 15th, it gave six performances, receiving warm praise from the audience. Many audience members wrote comments praising the troupe in the guestbook. One audience member wrote: "This is the most wonderful scene I have ever seen in my life." Another audience member said: "This is the most brilliant and wonderful performance in the world."
    *Göteborgs-Posten* wrote: The development level of Chinese acrobatics reflects the rapid development of the People's China in recent years. *Göteborgs Handels-och Sjöfartstidning* said: "These acrobatic performances all look like they are impossible for humans to do. Now, this acrobatic troupe is unparalleled."

### 瑞典人民热烈欢迎我杂技艺术团演出  中国精湛艺术无与伦比

1966-05-24
第5版()
专栏：

    瑞典人民热烈欢迎我杂技艺术团演出
    中国精湛艺术无与伦比
    据新华社斯德哥尔摩电  中国杂技艺术团结束在瑞典第二大城市哥德堡的演出，十七日离开那里前往荷兰。
    艺术团在哥德堡期间，从五月十一日到十五日演出了六场，获得观众的热烈赞扬。许多观众在留言簿上写下了他们赞扬艺术团的评语。一位观众写道：“这是我一生中见过的最奇妙的场面。”另一位观众说：“这是世界上最精采最美妙的表演”。
    《哥德堡邮报》写道：中国杂技艺术的发展水平体现了人民中国最近几年的迅速发展。《哥德堡晚报》说：“这些杂技艺术表演看起来都是人力所做不到的。现在，这个艺术团是无与伦比的”。
