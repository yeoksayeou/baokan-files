Title: The Standing Delegation of the South Vietnam National Front for Liberation in China Holds a Reception to Celebrate the Fifth Anniversary of the Founding of the South Vietnam Liberation Federation of Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1966-05-04
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 3rd – The Standing Delegation of the South Vietnam National Front for Liberation in China held a reception tonight to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the founding of the South Vietnam Liberation Federation of Trade Unions.
    Invited to the reception were Zhu Xuefan, Ma Chungu, Kang Yonghe, vice-chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Ding Xilin, deputy director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.
    The Albanian and Korean trade union delegations and delegations from some other countries were also invited to the reception.
    Nguyen Minh Phuong, acting head of the Standing Delegation of the South Vietnam National Front for Liberation in China, and the delegation of the South Vietnam Liberation Federation of Trade Unions attended as guests.
    After the reception, the films "Heroic Con Co Island" and "Vietnam Will Win, U.S. Imperialism is Bound to Fail" were shown.

### 庆祝南越解放工会联合会成立五周年  南越民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团举行招待会

1966-05-04
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝南越解放工会联合会成立五周年
    南越民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团举行招待会
    新华社三日讯　越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团今晚举行招待会，庆祝越南南方解放工会联合会成立五周年。
    应邀出席招待会的有中华全国总工会副主席朱学范、马纯古、康永和和对外文委副主任丁西林等。
    阿尔巴尼亚、朝鲜工会代表团和其他一些国家的代表团，也应邀出席了招待会。
    越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代理团长阮明芳和越南南方解放工会联合会代表团出席作陪。
    招待会后放映了影片《英雄的昏果岛》、《越南必胜、美帝国主义必败》。
