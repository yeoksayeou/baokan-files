Title: Chinese Cultural Delegation Arrives in Hanoi for Visit
Author:
Date: 1966-05-24
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 23rd -- A Chinese cultural delegation led by Li Qitao, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, arrived in Hanoi by plane on the afternoon of the 23rd for a friendly visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
    Welcoming the delegation at the airport were Pham Hong, Deputy Director of the Vietnam Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Nguyen Duc Qui, Deputy Minister of Culture; Hoang Tung, Secretary-General of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association; and others.
    Chen Liang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Vietnam, Lu Weizhao, Counselor, and Du Zhanchao, Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy and member of the Chinese cultural delegation, also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

### 我文化代表团到达河内访问

1966-05-24
第5版()
专栏：

    我文化代表团到达河内访问
    新华社河内二十三日电　由中国对外文化联络委员会副主任李琦涛率领的中国文化代表团，二十三日下午乘飞机到达河内，对越南民主共和国进行友好访问。
    到机场欢迎的有越南对外文化联络委员会副主任范鸿、文化部副部长阮德葵、越中友好协会秘书长黄俊等。
    中国驻越南大使馆临时代办陈亮、参赞陆维钊和中国文化代表团成员、大使馆文化参赞杜展潮也到机场迎接。
