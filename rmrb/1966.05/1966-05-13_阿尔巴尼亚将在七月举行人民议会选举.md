Title: Albania to Hold People's Assembly Elections in July
Author:
Date: 1966-05-13
Edition: 6

Albania to Hold People's Assembly Elections in July

	NEW DELHI, May 11 (Xinhua) - According to a report by the Albanian News Agency on May 11, the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania has issued an order stipulating that the People's Assembly elections will be held on July 10 this year.

	The People's Assembly elections in Albania are held every four years. The last election was held on June 6, 1962.

### 阿尔巴尼亚将在七月举行人民议会选举

1966-05-13
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚将在七月举行人民议会选举
    新华社地拉那十一日电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社十一日报道，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团颁布命令，规定在今年七月十日举行人民议会选举。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民议会选举每四年举行一次，上次选举是在一九六二年六月六日举行的。
