Title: Kashmir Liberation Front Leader Condemns Soviet Leaders for Aiding India and Opposing China and Pakistan; India Serves American Imperialism and Wins Support from the Soviet Government; Pakistani Newspaper Reveals that Soviet Leaders' "Tashkent Declaration" Was Intended to Interfere in Asian Affairs
Author:
Date: 1966-05-29
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi, 27th. According to a report in the "Dawn" newspaper on the 27th, Mukhtar Rana, convener of the Kashmir Liberation Front, pointed out in a letter to Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Kosygin that Soviet military aid to India has made India more aggressive towards Pakistan and China, and has encouraged India to intensify its mass killings and persecutions of the Kashmiri people under Indian occupation in Kashmir.
    He also pointed out: "The large-scale supply of arms by the Soviet Union to India means that India has won the support of the Soviet Union for serving the purposes of American imperialism."
    An letter to the editor published in the Rawalpindi newspaper "Jihad" on the 26th stated: "The efforts made by the Soviet leaders for the Tashkent Declaration were not out of sincerity, but in order to occupy an important position in Asian affairs."
    The letter, written by Mazur Ahmed of Dhaka, East Pakistan, said: "Like a businessman and a cunning politician, the Soviet Union has found a broader market for itself in India."
    The letter said: "For us, the Soviet Union and the United States stand on the same footing. They are both frantically pursuing their own goals." "When the Soviet Union has to choose between Pakistan and India, it always shows a tendency towards India."

### 克什米尔解放阵线领导人斥苏领导援印反对中巴  印度为美帝效劳赢得苏联政府支持  巴基斯坦报纸揭露苏联领导搞“塔什干宣言”是为干预亚洲事务

1966-05-29
第6版()
专栏：

    克什米尔解放阵线领导人斥苏领导援印反对中巴
    印度为美帝效劳赢得苏联政府支持
    巴基斯坦报纸揭露苏联领导搞“塔什干宣言”是为干预亚洲事务
    新华社拉瓦尔品第二十七日电　据《黎明报》二十七日报道，克什米尔解放阵线召集人穆克塔尔·拉纳在写给苏联部长会议主席柯西金的信中指出，苏联对印度的军事援助使印度对巴基斯坦和中国更富有侵略性，并已鼓励印度加紧对印度占领下的克什米尔的克什米尔人民进行大规模屠杀和迫害。
    他还指出：“苏联向印度大量供应军火这件事意味着，印度由于为美帝国主义的目的效劳而赢得了苏联的支持。”
    拉瓦尔品第的《战斗报》二十六日发表的一封读者来信说：“苏联领导人为塔什干宣言所作的努力，不是出于诚意，而是为了要在亚洲事务中占据重要地位。”
    这封由东巴基斯坦达卡的马祖尔·艾哈迈德写的信说：“象一个商人和狡猾的政客一样，苏联为自己在印度找到更加广阔的市场。”
    来信说：“对我们来说，苏联和美国是站在同一个立足点上的。它们都疯狂地追求自己的目标。”“当苏联不得不在巴基斯坦和印度之间作出选择时，它总是表示倾向于印度。”
