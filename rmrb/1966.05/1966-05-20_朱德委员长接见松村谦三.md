Title: Chairman Zhu De Receives Matsumura Kenzō
Author:
Date: 1966-05-20
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 19th. Chairman Zhu De received Matsumura Kenzō, advisor to the Japanese Liberal Democratic Party, and his entourage this afternoon, and had a friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the meeting were Lian Guan, Hu Yuzhi, and other responsible persons from relevant departments.

### 朱德委员长接见松村谦三

1966-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德委员长接见松村谦三
    新华社十九日讯　朱德委员长今天下午接见日本自由民主党顾问松村谦三和他的随行人员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有连贯、胡愈之等有关方面负责人。
