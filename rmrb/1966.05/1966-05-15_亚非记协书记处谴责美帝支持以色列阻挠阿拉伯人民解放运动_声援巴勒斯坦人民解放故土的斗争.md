Title: Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association Condemns US Imperialism for Supporting Israel and Obstructing the Arab People's Liberation Movement; Voices Support for the Palestinian People's Struggle to Liberate Their Homeland
Author:
Date: 1966-05-15
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, May 14th -- The Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association issued a statement today on the May 15th "Palestine Day," saying: "The Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association, representing its members from fifty-three countries and regions, expresses its full support for the heroic Palestinian people's fierce struggle to liberate their beloved homeland."
    The statement, signed by Djawoto, Secretary-General of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association, said that it "and all its members, together with the Palestinian people, strongly condemn the role played by Israel, the out-and-out tool of US imperialism, in obstructing the Arab people's liberation movement."
    The statement said: "On this Palestine Day, we are confident that, with the fullest support of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association and all progressive and fighting peoples worldwide, the Palestinian people, under the leadership of the Palestine Liberation Organization, will achieve even greater successes in the struggle to liberate their homeland."

### 亚非记协书记处谴责美帝支持以色列阻挠阿拉伯人民解放运动  声援巴勒斯坦人民解放故土的斗争

1966-05-15
第6版()
专栏：

    亚非记协书记处谴责美帝支持以色列阻挠阿拉伯人民解放运动
    声援巴勒斯坦人民解放故土的斗争
    新华社十四日讯　亚非新闻工作者协会书记处今天就五月十五日“巴勒斯坦日”发表声明说：“亚非新闻工作者协会书记处代表它的五十三个国家和地区的会员表示完全支持英勇的巴勒斯坦人民为解放自己亲爱的故土而进行的激烈斗争。”
    这份由亚非新闻工作者协会总书记查禾多签署的声明说，它“和它的全体会员同巴勒斯坦人民一道，强烈谴责美帝国主义的彻头彻尾的工具以色列所起的阻挠阿拉伯人民解放运动的作用。”
    声明说：“在这个巴勒斯坦日，我们确信，在亚非新闻工作者协会和全世界所有进步和战斗的人民的最充分的支持下，巴勒斯坦人民在巴勒斯坦解放组织的领导下，必将在解放自己故土的斗争中取得更大的成就。”
