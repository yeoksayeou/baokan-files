Title: Emerging Powerlifting Invitational Achieves World-Class Results; Chinese Athletes Xiao Mingxiang and Deng Guoyin Break Two World Records
Author:
Date: 1966-05-23
Edition: 6

    According to Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd—Nearly five thousand people in the capital gave a long and enthusiastic applause and cheers this afternoon at the Beijing Gymnasium, congratulating the 1966 Emerging Powerlifting Invitational Tournament for achieving world-class results in the featherweight category. Xiao Mingxiang, a 26-year-old featherweight athlete from China, successively jerked 155 kg and 157.5 kg barbells within thirteen minutes, breaking the world record for the jerk in this category twice. He also equaled the world record for the total score in this category (held by Japanese athlete Yoshinobu Miyake) with an excellent total score of 397.5 kg.

    His total score improved by 17.5 kg compared to two months ago and by 25 kg compared to the national record created by Ji Fayuan last year.

    Although Xiao Mingxiang achieved new results, he was not satisfied. Instead, he carried forward the spirit of continuous revolution and successfully jerked a 157.5 kg barbell in his final attempt – the fourth attempt – breaking the world record of 155 kg that he had just created thirteen minutes earlier.

    Xiao Mingxiang said excitedly that the achievements he made today are the result of the correct leadership of the Party.

    Ji Fayuan, another Chinese athlete who won second place, and Hiroya Fukuda, a Japanese athlete who won third place, also achieved good results that reached world-class standards – 382.5 kg and 380 kg, respectively.

    Today's competition results once again demonstrated the strong strength of emerging powerlifting athletes. The scores of the top three today all exceeded the scores of the top three in this category at the 1965 World Weightlifting Championships.

    The total scores of the other four athletes participating in this category were:
    Katsunobu Okada (Japan) – 332.5 kg; Mu. Azam (Pakistan) – 312.5 kg; A. Ra. Muzayyan (Syria) – 290 kg; Ishakan (Cambodia) – 260 kg.

    According to Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd—Deng Guoyin, a 23-year-old Chinese lightweight weightlifter, successfully pressed a 145.5 kg barbell tonight at the Beijing Gymnasium, breaking the world record of 145 kg for the lightweight press set by Soviet athlete Kachura in March this year.

    Abdul Karim Shawa, head of the Palestinian weightlifting delegation and referee for this competition, told reporters after the competition that the achievements of the Chinese athletes today not only make the Chinese people proud, but also make us proud. Now we can tell the imperialists: We have succeeded, and we will achieve even greater success. Today's achievements also prove that without international sports organizations manipulated by imperialism, we can still organize successful competitions.

    Deng Guoyin's total score today reached 425 kg, which not only greatly exceeded the record of 382.5 kg for the total score in this category at the First National Games, but also reduced the gap between this national record and the world record from the original 25 kg to 7.5 kg.

    Due to his success in the press, he became the tenth Chinese weightlifter to break a world record.

    Ten athletes participated in tonight's lightweight competition. Takahiro Yamaguchi of Japan won second place, and his total score and jerk score both broke the record of the First National Games. Kim Young-soo of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea won third place. Kim Young-soo's jerk score and the 117.5 kg he successfully snatched in his fourth attempt also broke the National Games record.

    Bo. Bodinaku, a 20-year-old Albanian athlete, broke Albanian records in total score and all three individual events today. His new achievements won enthusiastic applause.

    The total scores of the other six athletes participating in this category were:
    F. Khalid (Syria) – 312.5 kg;
    Di. Rafati (Palestine) – 300 kg;
    A. Jawaid (Pakistan) – 290 kg;
    Semponi (Cambodia) – 272.5 kg;
    M. N. Majid (Ceylon) – 270 kg;
    Houlun (Cambodia) – 1267.5 kg. (Photo Attached)

### 新兴力量举重邀请赛取得世界水平好成绩  我国选手萧明祥邓国银破两项世界纪录

1966-05-23
第6版()
专栏：

    新兴力量举重邀请赛取得世界水平好成绩
    我国选手萧明祥邓国银破两项世界纪录
    据新华社二十二日讯  首都近五千人今天下午在北京体育馆内长时间热烈鼓掌欢呼，祝贺一九六六年新兴力量举重邀请赛的次轻量级比赛取得了世界水平的好成绩。我国二十六岁的次轻量级选手萧明祥在十三分钟内，先后挺举起了一五五公斤和一五七·五公斤重的杠铃，两次刷新了这一级别挺举的世界纪录，并且以三九七·五公斤的优异总成绩，平了这一级别总成绩的世界纪录（由日本选手三宅义信保持）。
    他的总成绩比两个多月以前提高了十七·五公斤，比季发元去年创造的全国纪录提高了二十五公斤。
    萧明祥虽然取得了新的成绩，但是他并没有满足，而是发扬不断革命精神，又在挺举的最后一次试举——第四次试举时，成功地挺举起了一五七·五公斤重的杠铃，刷新了他在十三分钟前刚刚创造的一五五公斤的世界纪录。
    萧明祥激动地说，他今天取得的成绩是党的正确领导的结果。
    获得第二名的我国选手季发元和获得第三名的日本选手福田弘也分别创造了达到世界水平的好成绩——三八二·五公斤和三八○公斤。
    今天的比赛成绩，再一次显示了新兴力量举重运动员们的坚强实力，今天前三名的成绩全都超过了一九六五年世界举重锦标赛这一级别前三名的成绩。
    参加这一级别比赛的其他四名选手的总成绩是：
    冈田克信（日本）——三三二·五公斤；穆·阿扎姆（巴基斯坦）——三一二·五公斤；阿·拉·穆扎延（叙利亚）——二九○公斤；伊沙坎（柬埔寨）——二六○公斤。
    据新华社二十二日讯　我国二十三岁的轻量级举重选手邓国银今晚在北京体育馆成功地推举起了一四五·五公斤重的杠铃，打破了苏联运动员卡楚拉在今年三月间创造的轻量级推举一四五公斤的世界纪录。
    担任这场比赛主裁判的巴勒斯坦举重代表团团长兼裁判阿卜杜勒·卡里姆·沙瓦在比赛结束后对记者说，中国选手今天取得的成就，不仅使中国人民感到骄傲，也使我们感到骄傲。现在可以正告帝国主义：我们成功了，而且还要获得更大的成功。今天的成就还证明，没有帝国主义操纵的国际体育组织，我们同样可以组织成功的比赛。
    邓国银今天的总成绩达到了四二五公斤，不仅大大超过了第一届新运会这一级别总成绩三八二·五公斤的纪录，而且把这项全国纪录同世界纪录的差距由原来的二十五公斤缩小为七·五公斤。
    他由于推举的成功，从而成为我国第十个打破世界纪录的举重运动员。
    今晚的轻量级比赛有十名选手参加，获得第二名的是日本选手山口隆弘，他的总成绩和挺举成绩都打破了第一届新运会纪录。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国选手金永洙获得第三名。金永洙的挺举成绩和抓举第四次试举成功的一一七·五公斤的成绩，也打破了新运会纪录。
    二十岁的阿尔巴尼亚选手布·博迪纳库今天的总成绩和三个单项成绩都打破了阿尔巴尼亚纪录。他的新成就赢得了热烈的掌声。
    参加这一级别比赛的其他六名选手的总成绩是：
    法·哈利德（叙利亚）——三一二·五公斤；
    迪·拉法提（巴勒斯坦）——三○○公斤；
    阿·贾瓦伊德（巴基斯坦）——二九○公斤；
    森波尼（柬埔寨）——二七二·五公斤；
    姆·恩·马吉德（锡兰）——二七○公斤；
    侯伦（柬埔寨）——一二六七·五公斤。（附图片）
