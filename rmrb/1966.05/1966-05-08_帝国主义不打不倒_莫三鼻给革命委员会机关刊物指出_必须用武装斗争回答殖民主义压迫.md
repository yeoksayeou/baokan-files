Author:

Edition: 6

    Imperialism Cannot Be Overthrown Without a Fight
    Mozambique Revolutionary Committee Organ Publication Points Out
    Colonialist Oppression Must Be Answered with Armed Struggle
    (Xinhua News Agency, Cairo) The Mozambique Revolutionary Committee's organ publication, "Brave Hero," recently published in Cairo, stated in an editorial, "The only answer to Portuguese colonialism in Mozambique and elsewhere is armed struggle."

    The editorial said, "The contradiction between Portuguese colonialists and their allies and the oppressed Africans in the colonies is irreconcilable, thus causing a life-and-death conflict." The editorial condemned the United States and other members of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization for supporting Portuguese colonialists in their brutal oppression of the people of Mozambique and other Portuguese colonies. The editorial stated that the imperialist colonial policy leaves the oppressed people of Africa "with no other option but to carry out armed revolutionary struggle to regain national independence, defend their dignity, and protect their resources."

    (Xinhua News Agency, Dar es Salaam, 6th) The Mozambique Liberation Front headquarters issued a communique here on the 5th, stating that Mozambique freedom fighters killed another sixteen Portuguese colonial soldiers, wounded many more, and captured a large quantity of weapons and ammunition.

    These victories were achieved by freedom fighters in eight engagements in Cabo Delgado Province and Niassa Province from March 10th to April 5th.

    The communique said that on March 13th, freedom fighters ambushed Portuguese colonial troops traveling from Mueda to Luvia, killing eight Portuguese colonial soldiers and wounding many more. Freedom fighters also attacked a Portuguese military camp south of Luvia.

    The communique said that on April 5th, several Portuguese military vehicles were destroyed by landmines laid by freedom fighters on the road from Mueda to Chiúre. Portuguese aircraft later bombed the attacked area, but the freedom fighters had already withdrawn.

### 帝国主义不打不倒  莫三鼻给革命委员会机关刊物指出  必须用武装斗争回答殖民主义压迫

1966-05-08
第6版()
专栏：

    帝国主义不打不倒
    莫三鼻给革命委员会机关刊物指出
    必须用武装斗争回答殖民主义压迫
    据新华社开罗电　莫三鼻给革命委员会在开罗新出版的机关刊物《勇敢的英雄》最近发表社论说：“对在莫三鼻给和其他地方的葡萄牙殖民主义者的唯一回答是武装斗争。”
    社论说：“葡萄牙殖民主义者及其同盟者同殖民地的受压迫的非洲人之间的矛盾是不可调和的，因此引起了一场生死存亡的冲突。”社论谴责美国和北大西洋公约组织其他成员国支持葡萄牙殖民主义者残酷压迫莫三鼻给和其他葡属殖民地的人民。社论说，帝国主义的殖民政策使非洲被压迫人民“没有别的路好走，只好进行武装革命斗争来重新取得民族独立，维护他们的尊严和保护他们的资源”。
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆六日电　莫三鼻给解放阵线总部五日在这里发表公报说，莫三鼻给自由战士又打死葡萄牙殖民军士兵十六名，打伤多名，并且缴获了大批武器弹药。
    这些战果是自由战士从三月十日到四月五日在德尔加多角省和尼亚萨省的八次遭遇战中取得的。
    公报说，三月十三日，自由战士伏击了从马胡阿开往勒维亚的葡萄牙殖民军，打死八名葡萄牙殖民军，打伤多名。自由战士还袭击了勒维亚以南的一个葡萄牙军营。
    公报说，四月五日，几辆葡萄牙的军用车在从姆韦达到秋德的公路上被自由战士埋下的地雷所炸毁。葡萄牙飞机后来轰炸遭到袭击的地方，但是自由战士已经撤走。
