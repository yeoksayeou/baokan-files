Title: Delegation from the Vietnamese Ministry of State Farms Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-28
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 27th—The delegation from the Ministry of State Farms of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by Le Duy Trinh, Vice Minister of the Ministry of State Farms, arrived in Beijing by train today.
    Jiang Qixian, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Land Reclamation, and others were at the station to welcome them.
    Tran Tu Binh, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, was also at the station to greet them.

### 越南农场部代表团到京

1966-05-28
第5版()
专栏：

    越南农场部代表团到京
    新华社二十七日讯　由越南民主共和国农场部副部长黎维桢率领的越南农场部代表团一行七人，今天乘火车到达北京。
    到车站欢迎的，有农垦部副部长姜齐贤等。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使陈子平也到车站迎接。
