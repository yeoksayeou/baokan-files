Title: Latin America's Evil Neighbor
Author:
Date: 1966-05-06
Edition: 6

    Latin America's Evil Neighbor
    Mexican renowned economist and university professor José Luis Ceceña, in a recent issue of *Siempre!* magazine, published an article stating that the United States' century-long trampling, invasion, and exploitation of Latin American countries has aroused anti-American sentiment in Latin America. This has led the United States to constantly play tricks in Latin America: from the "big stick" to "good neighbor," then "good partner," and later the "Alliance for Progress." But no matter how it changes, the essence remains the same, and these practices are all aimed at "maintaining its (the United States') power in Latin America, while preventing revolutionary changes in our countries." (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 拉丁美洲的恶邻

1966-05-06
第6版()
专栏：

    拉丁美洲的恶邻
    墨西哥著名经济学家、大学教授何塞·路易斯·塞塞尼亚在最近一期《永久》周刊上发表文章说，美国一个世纪来对拉丁美洲国家进行的蹂躏、侵犯和剥削，在拉丁美洲激起了反美情绪。这使美国在拉丁美洲不断玩弄花样：从“大棒”到“睦邻”，又是“好伙伴”，后来又提出了“争取进步联盟”。可是万变不离其宗，这些做法都是为了“维持它（美国）在拉丁美洲的势力，同时在我们这些国家防止革命的变革”。（据新华社讯）
