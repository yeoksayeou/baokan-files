Title: Chinese Ambassador to Finland Holds Reception for Visiting Chinese Acrobatic Troupe
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-11
Edition: 6

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, May 9th: Chinese Ambassador to Finland Yue Xin held a reception on the 9th for the visiting Chinese Acrobatic Arts Troupe. Among the seventy-odd people attending the reception were Finnish Minister of Education Jussi Saukkonen, Deputy Minister of Social Affairs Department Kaarle Soikio, Governor of Uusimaa Province Reino Lehto, Vice Chairman of the Finland-China Association Onni Hiitunen, and other social figures and people from the cultural circles.

### 我驻芬大使为中国杂技团访问举行招待会

1966-05-11
第6版()
专栏：

    我驻芬大使为中国杂技团访问举行招待会
    据新华社九日讯　赫尔辛基消息：中国驻芬兰大使岳欣九日为中国杂技艺术团访问芬兰举行招待会。在出席招待会的七十多人中有芬兰教育部长尤西·绍科宁，社会事务部副部长卡尔莱·索基奥，乌西玛省省长雷诺·莱赫托，芬中协会副主席翁尼·希杜宁和其他社会人士及文化界人士。
