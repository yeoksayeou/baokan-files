Title: Anti-U.S. Imperialism and Support for the Struggle of the Vietnamese and Dominican Peoples Resound Through the Sky: People from All Countries Gather and March to Celebrate "May Day"

Author:

Date: 1966-05-04
Edition: 5

    This newspaper provides comprehensive reports. According to Xinhua News Agency: People from various countries held gatherings and parades on the 1st to celebrate International Labor Day, "May Day."
    The Communist Party of Ceylon, the Ceylon Federation of Trade Unions, the Ceylon Youth League Federation, and the United All-Ceylon Farmers' Congress jointly held a celebration meeting, which passed a resolution condemning the United States' aggression against the Vietnamese people and the Indonesian right-wing reactionary military forces' massacre of progressive forces in Indonesia. The resolution expressed firm belief that the progressive movement in Indonesia will achieve final victory.
    Approximately three thousand workers in Damascus, the capital of Syria, held a rally and demonstration. The banners they held read: "Recover Palestine!" "The Syrian working class supports the Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. imperialism!"
    In Conakry, the capital of Guinea, eighty thousand people held a mass rally at the "September 28th Stadium," and a contingent of thousands of people held a parade in front of the podium. At the end of the parade, President Touré and President Nkrumah both delivered speeches.
    The people of Tanzania held a parade and rally in Dar es Salaam. The marching crowds held up signs that read: "African workers resolutely oppose colonialist-orchestrated subversive activities." President Nyerere spoke at the conference.
    Zanzibar also held a military review, a mass parade, and a grand gathering. Tanzanian First Vice President Karume spoke to more than ten thousand people.
    Tens of thousands of people in Brazzaville, the capital of Congo (Brazzaville), held a mass rally and a grand parade. In his speech, Paul Bondou, the First Secretary of the Secretariat of the Congolese (Brazzaville) Trade Union Federation, severely condemned "the Johnson robber government's dirty war of aggression against the peace-loving Vietnamese people." Bondou also condemned U.S. and British imperialism for colluding to overthrow the Nkrumah government in Ghana.
    President Massamba-Débat spoke at the conference.
    Approximately ten thousand people in Mali held a parade in Independence Square in the capital, Bamako. They held up signs reading "Oppose imperialism," "Oppose colonialism," and "Long live international workers' solidarity," and marched in groups past the reviewing stand to the sound of the Internationale. Before the parade began, President Keita and the Secretary-General of the National Workers' Union of Mali, Sissoko, spoke.
    A grand parade with tens of thousands of participants was held in Algiers, the capital of Algeria. Demonstrations and parades were held separately throughout Algeria.
    In Cairo, the capital of the United Arab Republic, the masses held a mass rally in Mahalla al-Kubra, a textile city in the Nile Delta. President Nasser delivered a speech at the meeting.
    Approximately one thousand people held a demonstration in Santo Domingo, the capital of the Dominican Republic. Demonstrators chanted slogans such as "Workers unite!" and "Kill the invaders!" They strongly demanded that the U.S. invading troops immediately get out of the Dominican Republic.
    In Mexico City, the capital of Mexico, approximately five hundred thousand workers participated in the parade. The workers participating in the parade held up signs reading "U.S. invading troops immediately leave the Dominican Republic," "Stop the aggression against the Vietnamese people," and "The right to national self-determination and sovereignty of all countries must be respected."
    The working people of Montevideo, the capital of Uruguay, Santiago, the capital of Chile, San José, the capital of Costa Rica, and Caracas, the capital of Venezuela, all held demonstrations, demanding that the U.S. aggressors get out of South Vietnam and the Dominican Republic.
    The working people of Charleroi, an industrial city in Belgium, and Brussels, the capital, responded to the call of the local Communist Party organizations and held parades and gatherings. The number of participants in both cities was much larger than last year. The demonstrating masses were enthusiastic. In the parade in Charleroi, Central Committee Secretary Grippa of the Belgian Communist Party and several other leading comrades of the Central Committee of the Belgian Communist Party walked at the forefront of the parade. At the mass rally held after the demonstration, Grierna and Trifaux, two members of the Political Bureau of the Belgian Communist Party, and Schielmann, one of the main leaders of the "Women's Action Committee" of the National Arms Factory, spoke to cheers. In the parade in Brussels, the demonstrating masses pushed floats full of signs, expressing their determination to resolutely oppose U.S. imperialism and Khrushchev revisionists, resolutely support the struggle of the people of all countries for liberation, and resolutely fight for the basic rights of the working people. (Attached picture)
    On May 1st, nearly seven million workers and other working people in various parts of Japan held rallies to celebrate International Labor Day "May Day". The picture shows the gathering parade of workers, farmers and citizens in Tokyo. Facsimile photograph (issued by Xinhua News Agency)

### 反对美帝和支援越南多米尼加人民斗争呼声响彻云霄  世界各国人民集会游行庆祝“五一”节

1966-05-04
第5版()
专栏：

    反对美帝和支援越南多米尼加人民斗争呼声响彻云霄
    世界各国人民集会游行庆祝“五一”节
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：各国人民一日分别举行集会和游行，庆祝“五一”国际劳动节。
    锡兰共产党、锡兰工会联合会、锡兰青年联盟联合会和全锡兰农民大会联合举行的庆祝大会，通过决议谴责美国对越南人民的侵略和印度尼西亚右派反动军事势力对印度尼西亚进步力量的屠杀。决议表示深信印度尼西亚的进步运动必将取得最后胜利。
    叙利亚首都大马士革大约三千名工人举行集会和示威。他们举着的横幅上面写着：“收复巴勒斯坦！”“叙利亚工人阶级支持越南人民反对美帝国主义的斗争！”
    几内亚首都科纳克里八万人在“九月二十八日体育场”举行了群众大会，一支几千人的队伍在主席台前举行了游行。游行结束时，杜尔总统和恩克鲁玛总统都发表了讲话。
    坦桑尼亚人民在达累斯萨拉姆举行游行和集会。游行群众高举着标语牌，写着：“非洲工人坚决反对殖民主义者策划颠覆活动”。尼雷尔总统在大会上讲了话。
    桑给巴尔也举行了军事检阅、群众游行和盛大集会。坦桑尼亚第一副总统卡鲁姆向一万多群众讲了话。
    刚果（布）几万人在首都布拉柴维尔举行群众集会和盛大游行。刚果（布）工会联合会书记处第一书记保尔·邦杜在讲话中，严厉谴责“约翰逊强盗政府对爱好和平的越南人民发动的肮脏的侵略战争”。邦杜还谴责美英帝国主义合谋推翻加纳恩克鲁玛政府。
    马桑巴—代巴总统在大会上讲了话。
    马里人民约一万人在首都巴马科独立广场上举行游行。他们举着写有“反对帝国主义”，“反对殖民主义”，“国际工人团结万岁”的标语牌，在国际歌声中成群结队地通过了检阅台。在游行开始之前，凯塔总统和马里全国工人联合会总书记西索科讲了话。
    阿尔及利亚首都阿尔及尔举行了有几万人参加的盛大游行。阿尔及利亚全国各地都分别举行了示威和游行。
    阿联首都开罗群众在尼罗河三角洲的纺织城市马哈拉库布拉举行群众大会。纳赛尔总统在会上发表了讲话。
    多米尼加首都圣多明各约一千人举行了示威游行。示威者高呼“工人团结起来”、“打死侵略者”等口号。他们强烈要求美国侵略军立即滚出多米尼加。
    墨西哥首都墨西哥城，大约有五十万工人参加了游行。参加游行的工人高举着“美国侵略军立即离开多米尼加”，“停止对越南人民的侵略”，和“必须尊重各国人民自决权和主权”的标语牌。
    乌拉圭首都蒙得维的亚、智利首都圣地亚哥、哥斯达黎加首都圣约瑟、委内瑞拉首都加拉加斯的劳动人民都举行了示威游行，要求美国侵略者从南越和多米尼加滚出去。
    比利时工业城市沙勒罗瓦和首都布鲁塞尔的劳动人民，响应当地共产党组织的号召，分别举行了游行和集会。两个城市参加的人数都比去年多得多。示威群众情绪热烈。在沙勒罗瓦市的游行中，比共中央书记格里巴还有另外好几位比共中央领导同志走在游行队伍的最前列。在示威结束后举行的群众大会上，比共政治局的两名委员格里纳和特里福以及国家兵工厂“妇女行动委员会”的主要领导人之一歇尔曼在欢呼声中讲了话。在布鲁塞尔的游行中，示威群众推着挂满标语牌的彩车，表达了他们坚决反对美帝国主义和赫鲁晓夫修正主义者、坚决支持各国人民争取解放的斗争、坚决争取劳动人民基本权利的决心。（附图片）
    五月一日，日本各地将近七百万工人和其他劳动人民举行集会，庆祝“五一”国际劳动节。图为东京工人、农民和市民的集会游行队伍。　传真照片（新华社发）
