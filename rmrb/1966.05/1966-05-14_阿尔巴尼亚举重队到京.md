Title: Albanian Weightlifting Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-05-14
Edition: 6

    Albanian Weightlifting Team Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, 13th -- The Albanian weightlifting team, consisting of ten members led by Skender Tupe, editor-in-chief of the Albanian newspaper *Sporti Popullor* [People's Sport], arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon, invited to participate in the 1966 Emerging Forces Weightlifting Invitational Tournament in our country.
    At the airport, the guests were warmly welcomed by Zhu Debao, vice chairman of the organizing committee of this invitational tournament and deputy secretary-general of the All-China Sports Federation, and our country's weightlifters. Our weightlifters presented bouquets to all the guests.

### 阿尔巴尼亚举重队到京

1966-05-14
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚举重队到京
    新华社十三日讯  应邀来我国参加一九六六年新兴力量举重邀请赛的阿尔巴尼亚举重队一行十人，由阿尔巴尼亚《人民体育》报总编辑斯坎德·图皮亚率领，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    客人们在飞机场上，受到这次邀请赛组织委员会副主席、中华全国体育总会副秘书长朱德宝和我国举重运动员们的热烈欢迎。我国举重运动员们向全体客人献了鲜花。
