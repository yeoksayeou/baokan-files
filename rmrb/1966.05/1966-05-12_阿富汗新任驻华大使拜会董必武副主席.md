Title: New Afghan Ambassador to China Calls on Vice Chairman Dong Biwu
Author:
Date: 1966-05-12
Edition: 5

    New Afghan Ambassador to China Calls on Vice Chairman Dong Biwu
    Xinhua News Agency, 11th — The new Afghan Ambassador to China, Mohammad Asef Soheyl, paid a call on Vice Chairman Dong Biwu of the People's Republic of China this afternoon.
    Vice Chairman Dong Biwu had a friendly conversation with him.

### 阿富汗新任驻华大使拜会董必武副主席

1966-05-12
第5版()
专栏：

    阿富汗新任驻华大使拜会董必武副主席
    新华社十一日讯　阿富汗新任驻中国大使穆罕默德·阿沙夫·苏海尔，今天下午拜会中华人民共和国副主席董必武。
    董必武副主席同他进行了友好的谈话。
