Title: Pakistani Newspapers Praise China for Breaking Western Nuclear Monopoly; China Becoming a Nuclear Power Enhances the Prestige of Asian and African Countries
Author:
Date: 1966-05-16
Edition: 5

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi, Pakistani public opinion warmly welcomes the success of China's third nuclear explosion.
    The Lahore Daily "Eastern News" published an editorial on the 11th, saying: China is the "first Asian country" to master nuclear weapons, "which not only ends the Western countries' monopoly on nuclear power but also begins to rapidly change the world's balance of power. It is absolutely impossible for some world powers to isolate it, prevent it from enjoying its due position in world politics, and try to stop its progress."
    The editorial pointed out that China's nuclear test is an encouragement to Pakistan. "The path of hard work and self-reliance that China has pointed out to the emerging countries of the world is as remarkable as its atomic test."
    "Construction News" published an editorial on the 12th, saying: "China's growing strength and greater prosperity are a source of salvation and strength for the poor and oppressed countries in Asia, Africa, and the whole world."
    The editorial continued: "We are also proud of China's success, because it proves the wisdom and ability of the Asian people."
    "Achievement News" published an editorial on the 12th entitled "A Great Achievement," saying that we are happy for the success of the nuclear explosion of our sincere friend, the great neighbor China. "Among Asian countries, China was the first to shatter the superstition of so-called Western superiority. The Western countries' manufacturing of atomic bombs has no other purpose than to threaten small and weak countries in Asia and Africa. But our close friend China has enhanced the reputation of Asia in the eyes of Western countries and told them that science is not a monopoly of the United States and its good friends."
    The editorial also said that China's nuclear explosion "not only enhances the prestige of Asian and African countries but also provides the best guarantee for establishing world peace and defending small countries."
    "Mountain News" published an editorial on the 12th, saying that China's becoming an atomic power "makes the prospect of peace brighter."

### 巴基斯坦报纸赞扬我国打破了西方核垄断  中国成了核大国提高了亚非国家的威望

1966-05-16
第5版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦报纸赞扬我国打破了西方核垄断
    中国成了核大国提高了亚非国家的威望
    据新华社拉瓦尔品第电  巴基斯坦舆论热烈欢呼中国的第三次核爆炸成功。
    拉合尔日报《东方报》十一日发表社论说：中国是掌握核武器的“第一个亚洲国家，这不仅结束了西方国家在核力量方面的垄断地位，而且开始迅速地改变世界的力量对比。有些世界大国孤立它，不让它在世界政治中享有应有的地位，企图阻止它的前进，这是绝对办不到的。”
    社论指出，中国的核试验是对巴基斯坦的鼓舞。“中国向世界上的新兴国家指出的艰苦奋斗、自力更生的道路，和它的原子试验一样是了不起的。”
    《建设报》十二日发表社论说：“中国的实力增长和更加繁荣是亚非和全世界一切贫困的被压迫国家得到拯救和力量的源泉。”
    社论接着说：“我们也为中国的这一成功感到骄傲，因为这证实了亚洲人民的智慧和能力。”
    《成就报》十二日发表一篇题为《一个伟大的成就》的社论说，我们对真诚的朋友——伟大的邻邦中国进行核爆炸成功感到高兴。“在亚洲国家中，中国首先粉碎了所谓西方优势的迷信。西方国家制造原子弹除了威胁亚洲和非洲弱小国家外，没有其他目的。但是我们亲密的朋友中国提高了亚洲在西方国家心目中的声誉，并且告诉它们，科学不是美国和它的好朋友的垄断物。”
    社论还说，中国的核爆炸“不仅提高了亚非国家的威望，而且也为确立世界和平和保卫小国提供了最好的保证。”
    《山岳报》十二日发表社论说，中国成为原子大国，“它使和平的前景变得光明起来了。”
