Title: New Zealand Peace Council Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-05-27
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 26th—The New Zealand Peace Council Delegation, Alan Schierer and Mrs. Bennett, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace. Yang Ji, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and others were at the airport to welcome them.

### 新西兰和平理事会代表团到京

1966-05-27
第5版()
专栏：

    新西兰和平理事会代表团到京
    新华社二十六日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来我国访问的新西兰和平理事会代表团阿兰·希勒和本耐特夫人，今天乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的，有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席杨骥等。
