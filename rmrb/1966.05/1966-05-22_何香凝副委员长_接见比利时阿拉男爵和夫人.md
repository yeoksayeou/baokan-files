Title: Vice Chairman He Xiangning Receives Belgian Baron Alain and Wife
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-22
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 21st. He Xiangning, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received this afternoon Baron Alain, Chairman of the Belgian Action for Peace and the Independence of All Peoples and the Belgian Committee in Support of the South Vietnamese People, and his wife. They had a cordial and friendly conversation.
    Present at the reception were Jing Puchun, Liao Mengxing, Zhu Ziqi and other relevant personnel.

### 何香凝副委员长  接见比利时阿拉男爵和夫人

1966-05-22
第5版()
专栏：

    何香凝副委员长
    接见比利时阿拉男爵和夫人
    新华社二十一日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长何香凝，今天下午接见了比利时争取和平与各国人民独立行动和比利时声援越南南方人民委员会主席阿拉男爵和夫人，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有经普椿、廖梦醒、朱子奇等有关方面人士。
