Title: Latin American People Unite to Drive Out the Yankees
Author:
Date: 1966-05-06
Edition: 6

    The people of Uruguay and Chile demonstrate in solidarity with the people of Dominica
    Argentine and Colombian university students hold anti-American demonstrations and strikes
    (Xinhua News Agency, 4th dispatch) Uruguayan student and trade union organizations held a rally on the evening of April 29 in the auditorium of the University of Uruguay in Montevideo, the capital, to express solidarity with the Dominican people against American aggression.
    Student representative Ramon Martinez pointed out in his speech: "The aggression against the Dominican people is a provocation against the people of our entire continent." He said, "Faced with the brutal oppression of the United States, the people of all countries are increasingly strengthening their unity." Agustin Pedrosa, a member of the executive committee of the Uruguayan Workers' Central Union and leader of the construction union, demanded that the American aggressor troops withdraw from Dominica in his speech.
    After the meeting, the participants held a demonstration, shouting, "Yankees, get out of Dominica!"
    Students in Santiago, the capital of Chile, also held strikes and rallies on April 28 to protest against American aggression against Dominica and Vietnam.
    Juan Miquiel, leader of the Chilean University Student Union, pointed out at the meeting that the American intervention in the Dominican Republic clearly shows that the United States has destroyed all principles and is using force to promote its series of conspiracies on this continent. He called on people of all strata to unite to defeat American aggression.
    After the meeting, the students held a demonstration in the streets.
    The students also held a photo exhibition to expose the crimes committed by the American aggressors in Dominica and Vietnam.
    (Xinhua News Agency, 4th dispatch) Buenos Aires dispatch: Students at the University of Buenos Aires, the capital of Argentina, held a demonstration when Edwin M. Martin, the American ambassador to Argentina, visited the university on April 30, protesting against the American policy of aggression against Vietnam.
    It was reported that when this American diplomat and some other diplomats were visiting the new building of the Economics Department of the university, students gathered outside, shouting anti-American slogans and condemning American aggression against Vietnam. In the face of the aroused public anger, the American diplomatic representative hurriedly sneaked out of the university through the back door.
    (Xinhua News Agency dispatch) Bogota dispatch: University students in major cities of Colombia have launched an indefinite general strike starting from April 29 to oppose the penetration of American imperialism into Colombian universities, protest against the authorities' suppression of the student movement, and demand the improvement of educational conditions. This general strike marks a new climax in the student movement that has been going on for some time.
    Since late April, university students in various parts of Colombia have continuously held demonstrations, strikes, rallies, and marches, regardless of the ban on demonstrations issued by the authorities before the general election (May 5). They constantly clashed with the military police in the struggle, valiantly repelling the suppression by the authorities.

### 拉丁美洲人民团结起来赶走美国佬  乌拉圭智利群众示威游行声援多米尼加人民  阿根廷哥伦比亚大学生举行反美示威和罢课

1966-05-06
第6版()
专栏：

    拉丁美洲人民团结起来赶走美国佬
    乌拉圭智利群众示威游行声援多米尼加人民
    阿根廷哥伦比亚大学生举行反美示威和罢课
    据新华社四日讯　乌拉圭一些学生和工会的组织四月二十九日晚上在首都蒙得维的亚乌拉圭大学大厅中举行集会，声援多米尼加人民反对美国侵略。
    学生代表拉蒙·马丁内斯在讲话中指出：“对多米尼加人民的侵略就是对我们整个大陆人民的挑衅。”他说，“面对着美国残暴的压迫，各国人民正在越来越加强团结”。乌拉圭工人中央工会执行委员会委员、建筑工会的领导人阿古斯丁·佩多罗萨在讲话中要求美国侵略军撤出多米尼加。
    与会者在会后举行了示威，高呼“美国佬从多米尼加滚出去！”
    智利首都圣地亚哥学生也在四月二十八日举行了罢课和集会，抗议美国侵略多米尼加和越南。
    智利大学生联合会领导人胡安·米基埃尔在会上指出，美国对多米尼加共和国的干涉清楚地说明，美国已经破坏了一切原则，用武力在本大陆推行它的一系列阴谋。他号召全阶层人民团结起来打败美国的侵略。
    会后，学生们在大街上举行了示威游行。
    学生们还举办了图片展览会揭露美国侵略者在多米尼加和越南犯下的罪行。
    新华社四日讯　布宜诺斯艾利斯消息：阿根廷首都布宜诺斯艾利斯大学的学生，在美国驻阿根廷大使埃德温·麦·马丁四月三十日参观这所大学的时候，举行示威，抗议美国侵略越南的政策。
    据报道，当这个美国外交官和其他一些外交官在参观这所大学经济系的新大楼的时候，学生们聚在外面，高呼反美口号，谴责美国侵略越南。在群情激愤下，这个美国外交代表慌忙从后门溜出了这所大学。
    新华社讯　波哥大消息：哥伦比亚各主要城市的大学生四月二十九日起举行不定期总罢课，反对美帝国主义渗入哥伦比亚大学，抗议当局对学生运动的镇压，要求改善教育条件。这次总罢课是一个时期来的学生运动走向新的高潮的标志。
    四月下旬以来，哥伦比亚各地大学生们不顾当局发布的大选（五月五日）前夕不许游行的禁令，连续举行示威游行、罢课、集会和进军。他们在斗争中不断和军警发生冲突，英勇地还击当局的镇压。
