Title: Premier Zhou Meets Participants of the Emerging Forces Weightlifting Invitation Tournament
Author:
Date: 1966-05-26
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, May 25th—Premier Zhou Enlai met this afternoon with members of the weightlifting delegations, representatives, and observers from various countries and regions participating in the 1966 Emerging Forces Weightlifting Invitation Tournament, and engaged in cordial and friendly conversations with them.
    Those received were: the Albanian delegation led by head Skander Tupeja and deputy head Mihal Zipo; the Cambodian delegation led by head Sheng Ti and deputy head Kai Le; the Ceylonese delegation led by head Ji En· Lana Xin Ge; the Japanese delegation led by head Ega Kaoru Sheng; the delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea led by head Jin Renguo; the Pakistani delegation led by head Muhammad Naqi But and deputy head Muhammad Ayub Khan; the Palestinian delegation led by head Abdul Karim Shawa; the Syrian delegation led by head Nizar Nahid; as well as Azania (South Africa) representative Ahmed Gora Ibrahim, Guinean observers Diaso Mamadou and Bah Dirno, Malian observer Dalamane Sangare, and members of the Chinese weightlifting delegation.
    Present at the reception were Rong Gaotang, Chairman of the National Committee of the China Emerging Forces Games and Vice Minister of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission; Li Menghua, member of the National Committee of the China Emerging Forces Games and Vice Minister of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission; and Zhong Shitong, Chairman of the Organizing Committee of this Weightlifting Invitation Tournament and Chairman of the Chinese Weightlifting Association.

### 周总理接见参加新兴力量举重邀请赛人员

1966-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见参加新兴力量举重邀请赛人员
    新华社二十五日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了参加一九六六年新兴力量举重邀请赛的各国和地区举重代表团人员、代表和观察员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    被接见的有：由团长斯坎德·图皮亚和副团长米哈尔·齐波率领的阿尔巴尼亚代表团，由团长盛提和副团长凯莱率领的柬埔寨代表团，由团长季恩·拉纳辛格率领的锡兰代表团，由团长额贺熏生率领的日本代表团，由团长金仁国率领的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国代表团，由团长穆罕默德·纳基·布特和副团长穆罕默德·阿尤布汗率领的巴基斯坦代表团，由团长阿卜杜勒·卡里姆·沙瓦率领的巴勒斯坦代表团，由团长尼扎尔·纳希德率领的叙利亚代表团，以及阿扎尼亚（南非）代表艾哈迈德·戈拉·易卜拉欣，几内亚观察员迪亚索·马马杜、巴赫·迪尔诺，马里观察员达拉马内·桑加雷和中国举重代表团人员。
    接见时在场的，有中国新兴力量运动会全国委员会主席、国家体委副主任荣高棠，中国新兴力量运动会全国委员会委员、国家体委副主任李梦华，这次举重邀请赛组织委员会主席、中国举重协会主席钟师统。
