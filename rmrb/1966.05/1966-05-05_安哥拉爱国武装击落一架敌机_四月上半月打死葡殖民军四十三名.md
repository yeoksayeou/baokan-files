Title: Angolan Patriotic Armed Forces Shoot Down an Enemy Plane; Forty-Three Portuguese Colonial Soldiers Killed in the First Half of April
Author:
Date: 1966-05-05
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Algiers Dispatch: The Angolan National Liberation Army Headquarters announced in a communique on April 23 that the Angolan nationalist armed forces recently shot down a Portuguese colonial military reconnaissance plane. The pilot's credentials and various items found in the wreckage of the plane were recently exhibited at the base in Kinkuzu.
    The communique stated that in the first half of April 1966, the National Liberation Armed Forces active in various provinces of Angola killed forty-three Portuguese colonial soldiers and wounded dozens of enemy troops. (Photo attached)
    The picture shows the Angolan patriotic armed forces advancing, holding high the great banner. Xinhua News Agency Draft

### 安哥拉爱国武装击落一架敌机  四月上半月打死葡殖民军四十三名

1966-05-05
第6版()
专栏：

    安哥拉爱国武装击落一架敌机
    四月上半月打死葡殖民军四十三名
    新华社阿尔及尔电　安哥拉民族解放军参谋部四月二十三日发表公报宣布，安哥拉民族主义者武装力量最近击落葡萄牙殖民军的一架军事侦察机。在飞机残骸中发现的驾驶员的证件和各种物品，最近在金库祖根据地展览。
    公报说，一九六六年四月上半月，活动在安哥拉各省的民族解放武装力量打死四十三名葡萄牙殖民军，打伤几十名敌军。（附图片）
    图为安哥拉爱国武装力量队伍高举大旗前进　新华社稿
