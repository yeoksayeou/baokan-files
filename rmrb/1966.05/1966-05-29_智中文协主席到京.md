Title: Chairman of the Chile-China Cultural Association Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-05-29
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, May 28th – Mrs. Ana Ugarte, Chairman of the Chile-China Cultural Association and lawyer, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 27th at the invitation of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association.
    Those who welcomed her at the airport included Ren Yinglun, Vice President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, and related personnel such as Li Yannan, He Zili, and Wang Jiabin.

### 智中文协主席到京

1966-05-29
第6版()
专栏：

    智中文协主席到京
    新华社二十八日讯　应中拉友协邀请来我国访问的智利智中文协主席、律师安娜·乌加尔德夫人，已在二十七日乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有中拉友协副会长任映仑和有关方面人士李言年、何子立、王家宾等。
