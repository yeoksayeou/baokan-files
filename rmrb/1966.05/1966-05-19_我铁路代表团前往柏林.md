Title: Our Railway Delegation Leaves for Berlin
Author:
Date: 1966-05-19
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 18th -- A Chinese railway delegation, led by Guo Lu, Vice Minister of Railways, left by plane today for Berlin to attend the 11th Ministerial Meeting of the Organization for Cooperation of Railways of Socialist Countries. Among those seeing them off at the airport were Wu Jingtian and Su Jie, Vice Ministers of Railways, and responsible comrades from relevant departments.
    Li Boman, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China, also saw them off at the airport.

### 我铁路代表团前往柏林

1966-05-19
第5版()
专栏：

    我铁路代表团前往柏林
    新华社十八日讯 由铁道部副部长郭鲁率领的中国铁路代表团，今天乘飞机前往柏林，参加社会主义国家铁路合作组织第十一届部长会议。到机场送行的，有铁道部副部长武竞天、苏杰等有关方面负责人。
    德意志民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办李伯曼也到机场送行。
