Title: Fierce Fighting Erupts in Downtown Saigon; People's Armed Forces Attack U.S. Military Dormitory
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-11
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 10th dispatch from Saigon: The South Vietnamese Liberation Armed Forces once again attacked the Brink Hotel U.S. military dormitory in downtown Saigon on the morning of the 10th. According to preliminary reports, at least six U.S. soldiers were wounded in the bombing.
    According to Western news agencies, citing U.S. soldiers at the scene, a group of Liberation Armed Forces members, riding in a truck, threw grenades and opened fire with automatic weapons at the U.S. military dormitory as they passed the Brink Hotel on the morning of the 10th.
    The report stated that a large number of U.S. and puppet troops rushed to the scene and engaged in fierce fighting with the Liberation Armed Forces in the streets. After several tens of minutes of battle, the Liberation Armed Forces safely returned to their defenses.
    The Brink Hotel is a dormitory for high-ranking U.S. military officers in Saigon. On December 24, 1964, the Liberation Armed Forces bombed this dormitory, killing and wounding more than 150 U.S. soldiers.

### 西贡闹区发生激战  人民武装袭击美军宿舍

1966-05-11
第6版()
专栏：

    西贡闹区发生激战
    人民武装袭击美军宿舍
    新华社十日讯　西贡消息，越南南方解放武装力量十日晨再度袭击了在西贡市中心的布林克旅馆美军宿舍。据初步消息，至少有六名美军被炸伤。
    据西方通讯社援引在场的美国士兵的话说，十日清晨，一组解放武装力量乘坐一辆卡车，在经过布林克旅馆时，向美军宿舍投掷了手榴弹并用自动武器开火。
    报道说，大批美军和伪军仓惶开到现场，在街头同解放武装力量展开激战。经过几十分钟的战斗，解放武装力量安全返防。
    布林克旅馆是西贡美军高级军官的宿舍，一九六四年十二月二十四日，解放武装力量曾爆炸过这个宿舍，死伤美军一百五十多名。
