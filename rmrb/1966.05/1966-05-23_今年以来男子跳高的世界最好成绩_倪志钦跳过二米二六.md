Title: This Year's World's Best High Jump Result for Men: Ni Zhiqin Clears 2.26 Meters

Author:
Date: 1966-05-23
Edition: 4

    This Year's World's Best High Jump Result for Men
    Ni Zhiqin Clears 2.26 Meters
    Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd. Twenty-four-year-old Fujian outstanding high jumper Ni Zhiqin this afternoon in Beijing cleared the bar at a height of 2.26 meters, breaking his own national record of 2.25 meters set last November. This result is only two centimeters short of the world record for men's high jump and is the world's best result for men's high jump so far this year.
    Ni Zhiqin cleared this height during a performance at the 10th Beijing Municipal College Students' Track and Field Meet held at the Beijing College of Geology this afternoon.
    Before clearing the height of 2.26 meters this afternoon, Ni Zhiqin had successively cleared 1.90 meters, 1.95 meters, 2.00 meters, 2.05 meters, 2.10 meters, 2.14 meters, 2.18 meters, and 2.22 meters. He succeeded on his first attempt at the height of 2.26 meters.
    In another track and field competition held on the 20th, Inner Mongolia long-distance runner Ji Chengwen set a new national record in the 3000-meter steeplechase with a time of 8 minutes 57.7 seconds, breaking the previous national record of 8 minutes 59.8 seconds.

### 今年以来男子跳高的世界最好成绩  倪志钦跳过二米二六

1966-05-23
第4版()
专栏：

    今年以来男子跳高的世界最好成绩
    倪志钦跳过二米二六
    新华社二十二日讯　二十四岁的福建优秀跳高选手倪志钦今天下午在北京跳过了二米二六高度的横竿，打破了他本人在去年十一月间创造的二米二五的全国纪录。这个成绩距男子跳高世界纪录仅差二厘米，是今年以来男子跳高的世界最好成绩。
    倪志钦是在北京地质学院举行的北京市高等学校第十届学生田径运动会表演中，跳过这个高度的。
    今天下午，倪志钦在跳过二米二六高度以前，曾经先后跳过了一米九十、一米九五、二米、二米○五、二米一十、二米十四、二米十八、二米二二。他在跳二米二六的高度时，第一次试跳就获得了成功。
    在二十日举行的另一次田径比赛中，内蒙古长跑选手冀成文以八分五十七秒七的成绩，刷新了三千米障碍赛跑八分五十九秒八的全国纪录。
