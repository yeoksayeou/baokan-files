Title: American Imperialism's Guilt-Ridden and Embarrassed State: Rusk Tries Every Means to Cover Up the Crime of American Aircraft Invading Our Airspace; Openly Persists in Nuclear Blackmail Policy, Again Rejects the Chinese Government's Proposal

Author:

Date: 1966-05-19
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Eighteenth Dispatch from Washington: U.S. Secretary of State Dean Rusk, at a press conference on the seventeenth, dared not directly answer reporters' questions about China's protest against the intrusion of U.S. bandit aircraft into Chinese airspace, once again exposing the U.S. imperialism's guilt-ridden and embarrassed state.
    One reporter asked about the U.S. government's initial response to China's condemnation, "which was basically 'no comment.' After the investigation, is there any further disclosure?" In his reply, Rusk dared not directly answer the reporter's question, dared not repeat Washington's original claim that it would announce the situation after the investigation, but merely repeated the lies of the U.S. military in Saigon, saying that he "had nothing to add to what Saigon said a few days ago."
    In his speech, Rusk insisted on pursuing a policy of nuclear blackmail, and once again rejected the Chinese government's proposal to convene a summit conference of all countries in the world to achieve complete prohibition and thorough destruction of nuclear weapons, and for nuclear-weapon states to undertake obligations not to use nuclear weapons against each other and not to use nuclear weapons against non-nuclear-weapon states and regions.
    Rusk said that merely "issuing statements by these countries is not enough," and that "any measures related to the prohibition or control of nuclear weapons should involve verification and inspection."
    Rusk attacked China for refusing to sign the Moscow Three-Nation Treaty, baselessly claiming that China's proposal was intended to "dilute" the "impact" of China's non-participation in this fraud.
    Rusk also indicated that the United States still wants to limit China's development of nuclear weapons and maintain its nuclear hegemony through so-called "disarmament" discussions. He said that the United States is "ready to sit down" with China to "talk about issues like (preventing) the proliferation of nuclear weapons," "but we cannot discuss these major issues of war and peace solely on the basis of possibly meaningless and unverified declarations."

### 美帝国主义作贼心虚窘态百出  腊斯克百般掩饰美机侵犯我领空罪行  公然坚持核讹诈政策再次拒绝中国政府的建议

1966-05-19
第5版()
专栏：

    美帝国主义作贼心虚窘态百出
    腊斯克百般掩饰美机侵犯我领空罪行
    公然坚持核讹诈政策再次拒绝中国政府的建议
    新华社十八日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国务卿腊斯克十七日在记者招待会上不敢正面回答记者提出的关于中国抗议美国强盗飞机侵入中国领空的问题，再次暴露美帝国主义作贼心虚的窘态。
    有记者问，美国政府对于中国谴责的原先反应，“基本上等于是‘无可奉告’。在调查之后有何进一步的透露吗？”腊斯克在答复中不敢直截了当回答记者的问题，不敢重复华盛顿原先散布的所谓在调查之后再公布情况的论调，只是重复西贡美国军方人士的谎言，说什么他“对于前几天西贡所说的话，没有什么可以增添的了”。
    腊斯克在谈话中坚持奉行核讹诈政策，再次拒绝中国政府关于召开世界各国首脑会议，实现全面禁止和彻底销毁核武器，以及拥有核武器的国家承担义务，不互相使用核武器和不对无核武器国家和地区使用核武器的建议。
    腊斯克说，仅仅“这些国家发表声明是不够的”，“任何同禁止或控制核武器有关的措施应该涉及核实和视察的问题”。
    腊斯克攻击中国拒绝在莫斯科三国条约上签字，胡说中国的建议目的是要“冲淡”中国不参加这个骗局所造成的“影响”。
    腊斯克还表明，美国仍然想通过所谓“裁军”的讨论来限制中国发展核武器，保持它的核霸王地位。他说，美国“准备坐下来”同中国“谈象（防止）核武器扩散这样的问题”，“但是我们不能单在可能毫无意义的未经证实的宣言的基础上讨论这些战争和和平的重大问题”。
