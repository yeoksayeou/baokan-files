Title: Pakistan, Syria, and Japan Weightlifting Teams Arrive in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-19
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 18th – The weightlifting teams from Pakistan and Syria, invited to China to participate in the 1966 Games of the New Emerging Forces Weightlifting Invitational Tournament, arrived in Beijing by plane this evening.
    The Pakistani weightlifting team consists of seven people, led by Muhammad Naqi Butt, Secretary of the Pakistan Weightlifting Association. The Syrian weightlifting team consists of eleven people, led by Nizar Nahid, Vice President of the Syrian Weightlifting Association.
    The guests were warmly welcomed at the airport by Zhong Shitong, Chairman of the Organizing Committee of the Invitational Tournament and Vice President of the All-China Sports Federation, and outstanding Chinese weightlifting athletes.
    Xinhua News Agency, May 18th – The Japanese weightlifting team, defying all kinds of obstruction and difficulties to come to China to participate in the 1966 Games of the New Emerging Forces Weightlifting Invitational Tournament, arrived in Beijing by plane today.
    Zhu Debao, Vice Chairman of the Organizing Committee of the Invitational Tournament and Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Sports Federation, and outstanding Chinese weightlifting athletes warmly welcomed the Japanese friends at the airport today.

### 巴、叙、日举重队到京

1966-05-19
第5版()
专栏：

    巴、叙、日举重队到京
    新华社十八日讯  应邀来我国参加一九六六年新兴力量举重邀请赛的巴基斯坦和叙利亚举重队，今晚乘飞机到达北京。
    巴基斯坦举重队由七人组成，领队是巴基斯坦举重协会秘书穆罕默德·纳基·布特。叙利亚举重队由十一人组成，领队是叙利亚举重协会副主席尼扎尔·纳希德。
    客人们在机场受到这次邀请赛组织委员会主席、中华全国体育总会副主席钟师统和我国优秀举重运动员的热烈欢迎。
    据新华社十八日讯　冲破种种阻挠和困难来我国参加一九六六年新兴力量举重邀请赛的日本举重队一行九人，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    这次邀请赛组织委员会副主席、中华全国体育总会副秘书长朱德宝和我国优秀举重运动员们，今天到机场热烈欢迎日本朋友。
