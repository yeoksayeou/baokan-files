Title: Congolese (Brazzaville) Trade Union Delegation Leader Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-05-09
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 8th -- Maranda Florent, the head of the Congolese (Brazzaville) Trade Union Confederation delegation and Judicial Secretary of the Congolese (Brazzaville) Trade Union Confederation, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions for a visit to our country.
    Di Zicai, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others were at the airport to welcome them.

**Named Entities**

刚果（布） Congolese (Brazzaville)
中华全国总工会 All-China Federation of Trade Unions
刚果（布）工会联合会 Congolese (Brazzaville) Trade Union Confederation
马朗达·弗洛朗 Maranda Florent
狄子才 Di Zicai
北京 Beijing
新华社 Xinhua News Agency

### 刚果（布）工会代表团团长到京

1966-05-09
第5版()
专栏：

    刚果（布）工会代表团团长到京
    新华社八日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请来我国访问的刚果（布）工会联合会代表团团长、刚果（布）工会联合会司法书记马朗达·弗洛朗，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中华全国总工会书记处书记狄子才等。
