Title: Chinese Writers' Delegation Arrives in Somalia
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-23
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Mogadishu, May 21st. The Chinese Writers' Delegation, led by Du Xuan, arrived here from Sudan on the morning of the 21st. The delegation is here for a friendly visit in accordance with the 1966 implementation plan of the China-Somalia Cultural Agreement.
    Welcoming the delegation at the airport were Yasin Osmaf, Director of the Fourth Division of the Somali Ministry of Education, and Somali Coast Liberation Front leaders Mohamed Dahan and Ibrahim Dahir, among others. Yang Shouzheng, the Chinese Ambassador to Somalia, also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

### 我作家代表团到达索马里

1966-05-23
第6版()
专栏：

    我作家代表团到达索马里
    新华社摩加迪沙二十一日电　由杜宣率领的中国作家代表团二十一日上午从苏丹到达这里。代表团是根据中国—索马里文化协定一九六六年执行计划来这里进行友好访问的。
    在机场欢迎代表团的有索马里教育部第四司司长亚辛·奥斯马夫，索马里海岸解放阵线领导人穆罕默德·达汉和易卜拉欣·达希尔等。中国驻索马里大使杨守正也到机场欢迎。
