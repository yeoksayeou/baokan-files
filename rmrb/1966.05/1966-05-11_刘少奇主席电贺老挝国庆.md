Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Sends Congratulations on Laotian National Day

Author:
Date: 1966-05-11
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 10th May.  Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, sent a telegram to King Sisavang Vatthana of Laos on the 10th to congratulate him on the National Day of the Kingdom of Laos. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

    Luang Prabang
    His Majesty King Sisavang Vatthana of Laos:

    On the occasion of the National Day of the Kingdom of Laos, I extend warm congratulations to Your Majesty and the people of Laos. I wish your country prosperity and your people happiness. May the friendship between the people of China and Laos continue to develop.
          Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi
                    May 10, 1966

### 刘少奇主席电贺老挝国庆

1966-05-11
第6版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席电贺老挝国庆
    新华社十日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇于十日打电报给老挝国王西萨旺·瓦达纳，祝贺老挝王国国庆。电报全文如下：
    琅勃拉邦
    老挝西萨旺·瓦达纳国王陛下：
    在老挝王国国庆的时候，谨向陛下和老挝人民致以热烈的祝贺。祝贵国昌盛、人民幸福。祝中老两国人民的友谊不断发展。
          中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                    一九六六年五月十日
