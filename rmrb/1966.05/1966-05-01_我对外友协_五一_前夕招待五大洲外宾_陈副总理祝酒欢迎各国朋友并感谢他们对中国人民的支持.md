Title: All-China Friendship Association Fetes Foreign Guests from Five Continents on Eve of May Day; Vice Premier Chen Toasts, Welcoming Friends from All Countries and Thanking Them for Their Support of the Chinese People
Author:
Date: 1966-05-01
Edition: 4

    (Xinhua News Agency, 30th) At a grand reception held tonight by the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, more than 600 foreign guests from over 50 countries and regions on five continents gathered with people from all walks of life in the capital to warmly celebrate International Labor Day, May 1st.
    Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yi and Tan Zhenlin, and Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC Li Dequan attended the reception.
    Vice Premier Chen Yi proposed a toast at the reception. On behalf of the Chinese Communist Party, the Chinese government, and the Chinese people, and on behalf of Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Chairman Zhu De, he warmly welcomed friends from all countries of the world. He said that the Chinese people are a people armed with Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought. We are full of infinite confidence in our own revolutionary cause and the bright future of the people of the world. In building our own country and in the struggle against imperialism, the Chinese people have received the support of the vast majority of people in the world. Standing with us are the workers, peasants, revolutionary intellectuals, all progressive people, and all oppressed nations who account for more than 90 percent of the world's population. Compared with the broad masses of the world’s people, U.S. imperialism, modern revisionism, and reactionaries of all countries are only a tiny minority. They oppose the Chinese Communist Party, Comrade Mao Zedong, and fraternal Marxist-Leninist parties of various countries. This is not terrible; on the contrary, it is an honor for us. This is how we view the issue. We deeply and warmly thank friends from all countries for their support.
    Vice Premier Chen Yi said that the revolution and construction of the Chinese people have been supported by the people of all countries in the world. Without this support, the Chinese revolution would not have succeeded, and China’s construction would not be consolidated. I once again express my heartfelt gratitude to friends from all countries on behalf of our government and people. Vice Premier Chen Yi also pointed out that our work has achieved great success, but there are still many shortcomings. We welcome friends from all countries to criticize and advise us without reservation.
    At the beginning of the reception, Chu Tunan, president of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, delivered a speech. He extended a warm welcome and festive greetings to friends from all countries on behalf of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries and the Chinese people.
    In talking about the current excellent international situation, Chu Tunan pointed out that U.S. imperialism, modern revisionism, and reactionaries of all countries are colluding with each other to launch a frantic counterattack against the people of the world, but the power of the people cannot be suppressed, and the direction of historical development cannot be changed by any reactionary forces. Through tortuous and complex struggles, the people of the world will become more aware, more united, and more courageous and skilled in fighting. The days of U.S. imperialism and its running dogs are numbered, and final victory will surely belong to the people of the world.
    He said that the Chinese people have always clearly distinguished between friends and enemies. We have consistently taken a tit-for-tat approach and resolutely struggled against imperialism and its accomplices. We have consistently and unconditionally supported the cause of liberation of all oppressed peoples and oppressed nations. Our just stance has been attacked and cursed by imperialism and its accomplices, which precisely proves that we are doing the right thing. The Chinese people will always stand with the people of the world, develop and strengthen the international united front against U.S. imperialism and its running dogs to the greatest extent, and fight to the end for the great cause of overthrowing U.S. imperialism, defending world peace, and striving for the progress of mankind.
    Among those invited to attend the reception were the Albanian Handicraft Cooperative Delegation led by Kristo Semerko, Chairman of the Central Union of Handicraft Cooperatives of Albania; the Albanian Ministry of Interior Sports Delegation led by Niko Ceta; the Tanzanian Educational Delegation led by A. Makame; Erling Kristoffersen, Director of the Cultural Department of the Norwegian Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and K.S. Thapa, Assistant Private Secretary to the Crown Prince of Nepal.
    Also invited were the Cambodian-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation led by Lan Nget, President of the Cambodian-Chinese Friendship Association; the 12th Japanese-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation to China led by Norie Saito, Secretary-General of the Japanese-Chinese Friendship Association; Aladiyawanna, Chairman of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association, and his wife; the Canadian-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation led by Andrew Chow, initiator and organizer of the Canadian-Chinese Friendship Association; and the Finnish-Chinese Association Delegation led by Toivo Telenius, Chairman of the Finnish-Chinese Association.
    Also invited were the Vietnamese Labor Youth Union Delegation led by Vu Quang, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Youth Union; the Lao Patriotic Forces News Delegation led by Chaleun Vongsamsanh, Director of the "Voice of Laos" Radio Station; the Japanese Local Assembly Members' League for the Promotion of International Trade Delegation to China led by Hachiro Miura, Chairman of the Japanese Local Assembly Members' League for the Promotion of International Trade; the Japanese Local Government Friendship Delegation led by Daisuke Kawaguchi, Mayor of Akita City, Japan; the Japanese Chinese Language Researchers and Educators Delegation led by Akiho Todo, Professor of Tokyo University; the Lao Patriotic Front Art Troupe led by Ananh Broun; the Danish Badminton Team led by Jean-Louis Benin; and the Delegation of the French Student Union led by Jean-Louis Benin.
    Also invited to attend the reception were foreign guests permanently stationed in Beijing: Anna Louise Strong, the famous American female writer; Madame Quinim Pholsena, President of the Lao Women's Union; Nguyen Minh Phuong, Deputy Head of the Permanent Mission of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front in China; the wife of Rashid Said Jarbu, Deputy Director of the Palestine Liberation Organization Office in Beijing; Prahong Chulanond, Representative Abroad of the Patriotic Front of Thailand; and Sharma, Head of the Malayan National Liberation League Mission in China.
    Foreign friends from various fields including politics, economics, culture, youth, and women attended the reception. They came from over 50 countries and regions including Albania, Vietnam, Romania, Czechoslovakia, Burma, Cambodia, Ceylon, Indonesia, Iraq, Japan, Laos, Malaya, Nepal, Palestine, Syria, Thailand, Algeria, Basutoland, Congo (Brazzaville), Congo (Leopoldville), Guinea, Mali, Madagascar, Somalia, South Africa, Sudan, Tanzania, Uganda, the United Arab Republic, Argentina, Bolivia, Chile, Colombia, Dominica, Guatemala, Haiti, Mexico, Peru, Canada, the United States, Belgium, Denmark, Finland, France, Italy, Norway, Sweden, Switzerland, the United Kingdom, Australia, and New Zealand.
    Heads of relevant departments including Liao Chengzhi, Zhang Hanfu, Zhang Yan, Nan Hanchen, Zhang Zhiran, Lin Liru, Lian Guan, Zhang Kai, Ding Xilin, Song Yiping, Yang Chun, and Zhou Yiping attended the reception.

### 我对外友协“五一”前夕招待五大洲外宾  陈副总理祝酒欢迎各国朋友并感谢他们对中国人民的支持

1966-05-01
第4版()
专栏：

    我对外友协“五一”前夕招待五大洲外宾
    陈副总理祝酒欢迎各国朋友并感谢他们对中国人民的支持
    据新华社三十日讯　在中国人民对外文化友好协会今晚举行盛大的招待会上，来自五大洲的五十多个国家和地区的六百多位外宾同首都各界人士欢聚一起，热烈庆祝“五一”国际劳动节。
    国务院副总理陈毅、谭震林，政协全国委员会副主席李德全出席了招待会。
    陈毅副总理在招待会上祝酒。他代表中国共产党、中国政府和中国人民，代表毛泽东主席、刘少奇主席、周恩来总理、朱德委员长热烈地欢迎来自世界各国的朋友们。他说，中国人民是用马克思列宁主义、毛泽东思想武装起来的人民。我们对于自己的革命事业和全世界人民的光明前途，充满着无限的信心。中国人民在建设自己的国家和反对帝国主义的斗争中，得到了世界上绝大多数人民的支持。同我们站在一起的，是占全世界人口百分之九十以上的工人、农民、革命的知识分子、一切进步的人士和所有被压迫的民族。美帝国主义、现代修正主义和各国反动派与世界广大人民对比起来只是极少数。他们反对中国共产党，反对毛泽东同志和各国马克思列宁主义的兄弟党，这不可怕，反而是我们的一种光荣。我们就是这样看待问题的。我们深切、热烈地感谢各国朋友对我们的支持。
    陈毅副总理说，中国人民的革命和建设都得到了世界各国人民的支持，没有这种支持，中国的革命就不会成功，中国的建设也不会巩固。我再次代表我国政府和人民对各国朋友表示衷心的感谢。陈毅副总理还指出，我们的工作取得了很大成就，但是还有很多缺点，欢迎各国朋友不客气地对我们批评和指教。
    招待会开始的时候，中国人民对外文化友好协会会长楚图南致词，他代表中国人民对外文化友好协会和中国人民向各国朋友表示热烈的欢迎和节日的祝贺。
    楚图南在谈到目前大好的国际形势时指出，美帝国主义、现代修正主义和各国反动派正在串通一气，向世界人民进行疯狂的反扑，但是人民的力量是压不倒的，历史的发展方向是任何反动势力改变不了的。全世界人民通过曲折复杂的斗争，必将更加觉悟起来，团结起来，更加勇于战斗，善于战斗。美帝国主义及其走狗的寿命不会长了，最后胜利一定属于世界人民。
    他说，中国人民历来敌友分明。对于帝国主义及其帮凶，我们一贯针锋相对，坚决斗争。对于一切被压迫人民、被压迫民族争取解放的事业，我们一贯无条件地坚决支持。我们的这种正义立场，遭到帝国主义及其帮凶的攻击和咒骂，这恰恰证明我们做得对。中国人民将永远同全世界人民站在一起，最大限度地发展和加强反对美帝国主义及其走狗的国际统一战线，为打倒美帝国主义，为保卫世界和平和争取人类进步的伟大事业而奋斗到底。
    应邀出席招待会的，有由阿尔巴尼亚手工业合作社中央联合会主席克里斯托·赛梅尔科率领的阿尔巴尼亚手工业合作社代表团，由尼科·切塔率领的阿尔巴尼亚内务部体育代表团，由阿·马卡梅率领的坦桑尼亚教育代表团，挪威外交部文化司司长尔林·克里斯托费生，尼泊尔王太子的助理私人秘书克·斯·塔帕。
