Title: Cambodian Prime Minister Kang Tor Receives Ambassador Chen Shuliang
Author:
Date: 1966-05-25
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 21st – Cambodian Royal Government Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Prince Kang Tor received Chinese Ambassador Chen Shuliang on the morning of the 21st. They had a sincere and friendly conversation.

### 柬埔寨首相康托尔接见陈叔亮大使

1966-05-25
第5版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨首相康托尔接见陈叔亮大使
    新华社金边二十一日电　柬埔寨王国内阁首相兼外交大臣康托尔亲王，二十一日上午接见了中国大使陈叔亮。他们进行了诚挚友好的谈话。
