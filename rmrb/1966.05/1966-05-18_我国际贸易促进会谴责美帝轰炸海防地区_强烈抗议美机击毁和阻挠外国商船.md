Title: All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Condemns US Imperialism's Bombing of Haiphong Area, Strongly Protests US Aircraft's Destruction and Obstruction of Foreign Merchant Ships
Author:
Date: 1966-05-18
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, May 17th: A spokesperson for the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade issued a statement regarding the US imperialist aircraft bombing the Haiphong port area of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, destroying and obstructing foreign merchant ships trading with the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The statement strongly protested this new act of piracy by US imperialism and expressed firm support for the solemn position of the Chamber of Commerce of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

    The spokesperson for the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade emphasized that this is a serious step by US imperialism in attempting to blockade the ports of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and gradually "escalate" the war of aggression against Vietnam. It can only further expose the extremely weak and desperate predicament of the Johnson administration. The firm determination of the people of all countries to support the just struggle of the Vietnamese people against US aggression and for national salvation can never be threatened or intimidated. The Chinese people stand firmly with the heroic Vietnamese people and all peace-loving people, supporting the Vietnamese people's struggle against US aggression and for national salvation until final victory.

    On May 5th, the Chamber of Commerce of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam issued a statement regarding the US imperialist bombing of the Haiphong port area, the destruction of foreign merchant ships, and the obstruction of foreign merchant ships from navigating in the area. The statement strongly condemned US imperialism's violation of international law principles of free trade and free navigation for all countries and solemnly demanded an immediate cessation of all threats and provocative actions against merchant ships entering and leaving the ports of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

### 我国际贸易促进会谴责美帝轰炸海防地区  强烈抗议美机击毁和阻挠外国商船

1966-05-18
第6版()
专栏：

    我国际贸易促进会谴责美帝轰炸海防地区
    强烈抗议美机击毁和阻挠外国商船
    新华社十七日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会发言人就美帝国主义飞机轰炸越南民主共和国海防港口地区，击毁和阻挠同越南民主共和国进行贸易的外国商船一事，发表谈话，强烈抗议美帝国主义这一新的海盗行径，表示坚决支持越南民主共和国商会的严正立场。
    中国国际贸易促进委员会发言人强调指出，这是美帝国主义企图封锁越南民主共和国港口，逐步“升级”侵越战争的一个严重步骤，只能进一步暴露约翰逊政府十分虚弱和走投无路的绝望的窘境。各国人民支持越南人民抗美救国正义斗争的坚定决心，是永远威胁、恐吓不倒的。中国人民坚定地同英雄的越南人民和一切爱好和平人民站在一起，支援越南人民抗美救国的斗争直到取得最后的胜利。
    越南民主共和国商会五月五日曾就美帝国主义轰炸海防港口地区，击毁外国商船并阻挠外国商船在该地区行驶一事发表声明，强烈谴责美帝国主义违反世界各国的自由贸易和自由航行的国际法准则，并严正要求立即停止对出入越南民主共和国港口的商船的一切威胁和挑衅行为。
