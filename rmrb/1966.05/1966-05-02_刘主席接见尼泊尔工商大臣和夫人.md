Title: Chairman Liu Receives Nepalese Minister of Industry and Commerce and his Wife
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-02
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, 1st – Chairman Liu Shaoqi received the Nepalese Minister of Industry and Commerce, Mr. N.P. Rijal, and his wife this evening and held a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Lin Haiyun and Han Nianlong.
    The Nepalese Ambassador to China, Mr. Rendhir Subba, and his wife were also present. (Picture attached)
    May 1st, Chairman Liu Shaoqi receives the Nepalese Minister of Industry and Commerce, Mr. N.P. Rijal, and his wife. The reception photos are by Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian.

### 刘主席接见尼泊尔工商大臣和夫人

1966-05-02
第2版()
专栏：

    刘主席接见尼泊尔工商大臣和夫人
    新华社一日讯　刘少奇主席今晚接见了尼泊尔工商大臣纳·普·里贾尔和夫人，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有林海云、韩念龙。
    尼泊尔驻中国大使伦迪尔·苏巴和夫人也在座。（附图片）
    五月一日，刘少奇主席接见尼泊尔工商大臣纳·普·里贾尔和夫人。                   接见照片均为新华社记者　杜修贤摄
