Title: Liaison Delegation of the Vietnamese People's Army Condemns New Provocations by U.S. Imperialism; Protests Against U.S. Aircraft's Attack on Longzhu Island Lighthouse Attempting to Block Haiphong Port
Author:
Date: 1966-05-10
Edition: 6

    According to a May 9th Xinhua News Agency dispatch from Hanoi: The Liaison Delegation of the General Command of the Vietnamese People's Army wrote to the International Commission of Vietnam on May 5th, strongly protesting the aggressive crimes of U.S. aircraft attacking the Longzhu Island lighthouse in North Vietnam on April 28th.
    The letter stated that recently, in order to threaten foreign ships trading with the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, U.S. imperialism dispatched aircraft to bomb and strafe the outskirts of Haiphong City and the Cam Pha area, and continuously attacked ships of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and foreign countries operating in the Gulf of Tonkin. The U.S. aircraft's attack on the Longzhu Island lighthouse was an attempt to disrupt ships entering and leaving Haiphong Port, a blatant act of aggression by U.S. imperialism to gradually blockade North Vietnamese seaports and conspire to create difficulties for the Democratic Republic of Vietnam's shipping.

### 越南人民军联络代表团谴责美帝新挑衅  抗议美机袭击龙珠岛灯塔企图封锁海防港口

1966-05-10
第6版()
专栏：

    越南人民军联络代表团谴责美帝新挑衅
    抗议美机袭击龙珠岛灯塔企图封锁海防港口
    据新华社河内九日电　据越南通讯社报道：越南人民军总司令部联络代表团五日写信给越南国际委员会，强烈抗议美国飞机四月二十八日袭击越南北方龙珠岛灯塔的侵略罪行。
    信件说，最近，为了威胁同越南民主共和国贸易的外国船只，美帝国主义派遣飞机轰炸和扫射海防市外围和锦普地区，并接连袭击在北部湾海面活动的越南民主共和国和外国的船只。美国飞机袭击龙珠岛灯塔，是企图破坏船只出入海防港口，是美帝国主义逐步封锁越南北方海港、阴谋给越南民主共和国的航运制造困难的明目张胆的侵略行动。
