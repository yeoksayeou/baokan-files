Title: Romanian Party and Government Delegation Arrives in Hanoi
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-06
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 5th (Correspondent) -- The Romanian Party and Government Delegation, led by Emil Bodnăraș, Member of the Central Executive Committee of the Romanian Communist Party, Member of the Standing Presidium of the Central Committee, and First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, arrived in Hanoi by plane on the evening of the 5th for a visit. Vietnamese Party and government leaders Lê Đức Thọ, Nguyễn Duy Trinh, and others went to the airport to welcome them. Subsequently, a welcoming ceremony attended by hundreds of people was held in the Hanoi Grand Theater Square.
    Nguyễn Duy Trinh and Emil Bodnăraș spoke at the welcoming ceremony successively.
    Zhu Qiwén, the Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam, attended the welcoming ceremony.

### 罗马尼亚党政代表团到达河内

1966-05-06
第5版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚党政代表团到达河内
    新华社河内五日电　由罗马尼亚共产党中央执行委员会委员、中央常设主席团委员、政府部长会议第一副主席埃·波德纳拉希率领的罗马尼亚党政代表团，五日傍晚乘飞机到达河内进行访问。越南党政领导人黎德寿、阮维桢等前往机场欢迎。随后在河内大剧院广场上举行了有数百名群众参加的欢迎仪式。
    阮维桢和埃·波德纳拉希先后在欢迎仪式上讲了话。
    中国驻越南大使朱其文参加了欢迎仪式。
