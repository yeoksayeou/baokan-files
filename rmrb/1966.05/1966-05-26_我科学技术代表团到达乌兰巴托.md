Title: Our Science and Technology Delegation Arrives in Ulaanbaatar
Author:
Date: 1966-05-26
Edition: 3

    Our Science and Technology Delegation Arrives in Ulaanbaatar
    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, May 24th – The Chinese Science and Technology Delegation, which came to attend the Fourth Meeting of the Executive Body for Sino-Mongolian Scientific and Technological Cooperation, arrived in Ulaanbaatar by train on the morning of the 24th, led by Zhao Jichang, head of the delegation and Vice-Chairman of the Chinese Scientific and Technological Commission.

### 我科学技术代表团到达乌兰巴托

1966-05-26
第3版()
专栏：

    我科学技术代表团到达乌兰巴托
    新华社乌兰巴托二十四日电　前来参加中蒙科学技术合作执行机构第四届会议的中国科学技术代表团，在团长、中国科学技术委员会副主任赵继昌率领下，二十四日上午乘火车到达乌兰巴托。
