Title: China and Yemen Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement Implementation Plan
Author:
Date: 1966-05-25
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Sana, May 23rd - China and Yemen signed the 1966 and 1967 executive plan for the cultural cooperation agreement between the two countries here on the 23rd.
    Li Chang, head of the Chinese Government Cultural Friendship Delegation and Vice-Chairman of the Chinese Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, and Mohammed Halidi, the Yemeni Minister of Education, signed the plan on behalf of their respective governments.
    Wang Ruojie, the Chinese Ambassador to Yemen, Abdul Ghani Ahmed Ali, the Yemeni Minister of Information, and other senior government officials attended the signing ceremony.

### 中也签订文化合作协定执行计划

1966-05-25
第5版()
专栏：

    中也签订文化合作协定执行计划
    新华社萨那二十三日电　中国和也门二十三日在这里签订了两国文化合作协定一九六六年和一九六七年执行计划。
    中国政府文化友好代表团团长、中国对外文化联络委员会副主任李昌和也门教育部长穆罕默德·哈利迪分别代表两国政府在计划上签字。
    中国驻也门大使王若杰，也门新闻部长阿卜杜勒·加尼·艾哈迈德·阿里以及其他高级政府官员出席了签字仪式。
