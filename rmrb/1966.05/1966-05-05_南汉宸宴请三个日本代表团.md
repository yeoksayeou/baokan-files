Title: Nan Hanchen Hosts a Banquet for Three Japanese Delegations
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-05
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 4th May – Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, held a banquet this evening to welcome the Japanese Prefectural Assembly Members League for Promotion of International Trade Delegation headed by Miura Hachiro, the Japanese Women Prefectural Assembly Members Delegation headed by Gotō Mitsuru, and the Japanese Amagasaki City Economic Friendship Delegation headed by Ōmori Kiichi.  Attending the banquet were Xiao Fangzhou, Yong Longgui, Zhao Anbo, Yang Yunyu, Li Xinnong, and other responsible persons from relevant departments.
    Prior to the banquet, Nan Hanchen received the above three Japanese delegations and held friendly talks with them.

### 南汉宸宴请三个日本代表团

1966-05-05
第5版()
专栏：

    南汉宸宴请三个日本代表团
    新华社四日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸今晚举行宴会，欢迎以三浦八郎为首的日本地方议员促进国际贸易联盟访华代表团、以后藤满为首的日本妇女地方议员代表团和以大森基一为首的日本尼崎市经济友好代表团。出席宴会的，有萧方洲、勇龙桂、赵安博、杨蕴玉、李新农等有关方面负责人。
    宴会以前，南汉宸接见了以上三个日本代表团，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
