Title: Matsumura Kenzo Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-05-13
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 12th May: Mr. Matsumura Kenzo, advisor to the Japanese Liberal Democratic Party, and Mr. Takeyama Yutaro, Mr. Matsumoto Shunichi, and others, a group of six, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a friendly visit to our country at the invitation of Liao Chengzhi.
    Those who went to the airport to welcome them were Liao Chengzhi and his wife, as well as other figures Hu Yuzhi, Liu Xiwen, Zhao Anbo, Xiao Fangzhou, Wang Yinpu, Li Xinnong, and others. (Attached picture)
    May 12th, Mr. Matsumura Kenzo, advisor to the Japanese Liberal Democratic Party, and others arrive in Beijing by plane.

### 松村谦三等到京

1966-05-13
第6版()
专栏：

    松村谦三等到京
    新华社十二日讯　应廖承志邀请，前来我国进行友好访问的日本自由民主党顾问松村谦三先生及竹山祐太郎、松本俊一等一行六人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有，廖承志和夫人以及其他方面人士胡愈之、刘希文、赵安博、萧方洲、王荫圃、李新农等。（附图片）
    五月十二日，日本自由民主党顾问松村谦三等乘飞机到达北京。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　新华社记者　张桂玉摄
