Title: Ceylonese Weightlifting Team Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-18
Edition: 6

    According to Xinhua News Agency, May 17th, the Ceylonese weightlifting team, consisting of eight members led by the honorary secretary of the Ceylon Weightlifting Association, T.I. Jeon Lanashinge, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of our country to participate in the 1966 Emerging Forces Weightlifting Invitation Tournament.

**Named Entities**

锡兰    Xīlán (Ceylon)
北京   Běijīng (Beijing)

### 锡兰举重队到京

1966-05-18
第6版()
专栏：

    锡兰举重队到京
    据新华社十七日讯　应邀来我国参加一九六六年新兴力量举重邀请赛的锡兰举重队一行八人，由锡兰举重协会荣誉秘书季恩·拉纳辛格率领，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
