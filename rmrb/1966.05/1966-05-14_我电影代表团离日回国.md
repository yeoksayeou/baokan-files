Title: Our Film Delegation Departs Japan for Home

Author:

Date: 1966-05-14
Edition: 6

    Our Film Delegation Departs Japan for Home
    (Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, May 13) The Chinese film delegation, led by Yue Lin, director of Changchun Film Studio, left Tokyo on the 13th to return home after concluding its visit to Japan.
    On the evening of the 12th, the Chinese film delegation held a grand farewell reception to thank the Japanese friends for their warm hospitality towards the delegation.

### 我电影代表团离日回国

1966-05-14
第6版()
专栏：

    我电影代表团离日回国
    据新华社东京十三日电　由长春电影制片厂厂长岳林率领的中国电影代表团在结束了对日本的访问后，在十三日离开东京回国。
    十二日晚上，中国电影代表团举行盛大的告别酒会，感谢日本朋友对代表团的热情接待。
