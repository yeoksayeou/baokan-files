Title: Chinese and Albanian Leaders Cordially Celebrate May Day Together; Comrades Shehu and Kado Accompanied by Comrades Liu Shaoqi and Zhou Enlai Watch Ballet

Author:

Date: 1966-05-01
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th - On the eve of the "May 1st" International Labor Day, Comrades Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai and others held a get-together with Comrades Shehu, Kado, and all comrades of the Albanian Party and Government Delegation.
    At the get-together, Chinese artists performed songs and dances from both China and Albania, including "Eagle of the Guerrilla", "Beijing-Tirana", "Sailing the Seas Depends on the Helmsman", "Chairman Mao's Soldiers are Most Loyal to the Party", and "Commune Women Militia". The get-together was filled with a joyful atmosphere.
    Comrades Chen Yi, Bo Yibo, responsible persons from relevant departments of the CPC Central Committee and the government, the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and people's organizations, as well as people from all walks of life who had visited Albania, participated in the get-together. Invited to participate in the get-together were diplomatic officials from the Albanian Embassy in China, Albanian comrades currently visiting Beijing, Albanian experts, interns, and students.
    Xinhua News Agency, 30th - The Ministry of Culture and the China-Albania Friendship Association held an artistic soiree today to warmly welcome the Albanian Party and Government Delegation led by Comrade Shehu.
    Comrades Shehu, Kado, and all distinguished Albanian guests, accompanied by Comrades Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Chen Yi, Bo Yibo and others, watched the modern ballet "The Red Detachment of Women" performed by the Central Opera and Ballet Theater.
    After the performance, Comrades Shehu, Kado, Kelezi, Nasse, Natanailli and others, together with Comrades Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai and others, walked onto the stage amidst warm applause from the audience, shook hands cordially with the performers, and took photos together as souvenirs.
    Also accompanying the distinguished guests to watch the performance were Wu Xiuquan, Zhang Hanfu, Jiang Nanxiang, Kong Yuan, Wang Bingnan, Zhao Xinchu, and Xu Jianguo, our ambassador to Albania.

### 中阿两国领导人亲切联欢共庆“五一”  谢胡卡博同志由刘少奇周恩来同志陪同看芭蕾舞

1966-05-01
第4版()
专栏：

    中阿两国领导人亲切联欢共庆“五一”
    谢胡卡博同志由刘少奇周恩来同志陪同看芭蕾舞
    新华社三十日讯　“五一”国际劳动节的前夕，刘少奇、周恩来等同志，同谢胡、卡博同志和阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团的全体同志，举行了联欢。
    在联欢会上，中国文艺工作者演出了《游击队之鹰》、《北京—地拉那》、《大海航行靠舵手》、《毛主席的战士最听党的话》、《公社女民兵》等中阿两国的歌舞节目。联欢会上洋溢一片欢乐的气氛。
    陈毅、薄一波同志，中共中央和政府各有关部门、中国人民解放军和人民团体的负责人，曾经访问过阿尔巴尼亚的各界人士，参加了联欢。应邀参加联欢的，有阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使馆外交官员，正在北京访问的阿尔巴尼亚同志，阿尔巴尼亚专家、实习生和留学生。
    新华社三十日讯　文化部和中国阿尔巴尼亚友好协会今天举行文艺晚会，热烈欢迎由谢胡同志率领的阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团。
    谢胡同志、卡博同志和阿尔巴尼亚全体贵宾，由刘少奇、周恩来、陈毅、薄一波等同志陪同，观看了中央歌剧舞剧院芭蕾舞剧团演出的现代芭蕾舞剧《红色娘子军》。
    演出结束以后，谢胡、卡博、凯莱齐、纳赛、纳塔奈利等同志，和刘少奇、周恩来等同志，在全场热烈的掌声中一起走上舞台，同演员们亲切握手，并且合影留念。
    陪同贵宾们观看演出的，还有各方面的负责人伍修权、章汉夫、蒋南翔、孔原、王炳南、赵辛初，以及我国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使许建国。
