Title: Ramming Song

Author: Yu Shuheng

Date: 1966-05-09

Edition: 6

    Member of the Nijiacun Commune, Wendeng Gaocun Commune, Yu Shuheng, leads: Raise your eyebrows, open your eyes wide,
    Roll up your sleeves high!  All: Hai – yo –
    Leader: With a swing of the shoulder, shout "Work"!
    The mountains sway and the ground trembles! All: Hai – yo –
    Smash "poverty" and "blankness"!
    The sun and moon will take on a new look!
    Hai la – er yo – Leader: One ram makes the sun and moon round,
    Five rams falling like plum blossom petals! All: Hai – yo –
    Leader: See both the sunrise and sunset,
    Learn from the red plum blossom's laughter in the face of the cold! All: Hai – yo –
    Have the will to fight nature,
    Not afraid of the severe winter cold!
    Hai la – er yo – Leader: Transform the waterlogged land, control the barren mountains,
    Build reservoirs that amaze even the heavens! All: Hai – yo –
    Divert water to irrigate fertile fields,
    Produce more grain and cotton!
    Hai la – er yo – Leader: It's hard for one person to carry ten thousand catties,
    Everyone reaches out to hold up the sky! All: Hai – yo –
    Leader: Thousands of ramming ropes are pulled tight,
    Strength will never run out! All: Hai – yo –
    Good commune members,
    Every one is a tough guy,
    Hai la – er yo – All: Work harder!
    Work harder! Leader: The foundation of socialism, All: Ram it firm and strong!
      Hai – yo –



**Named Entities**


倪家村 Nijiacun
文登高村公社 Wendeng Gaocun Commune
于书恒 Yu Shuheng
穷和白  Poverty and Blankness

### 夯头号子
于书恒
1966-05-09
第6版()
专栏：

    夯头号子文登高村公社倪家村社员　于书恒领：竖起眉，瞪起眼，
    两只袖子高高挽呐！合：嗨——哟——领：肩膀一甩喊声“干”！
    高山摇摆地打颤呐！合：嗨——哟——
    砸烂“穷”和“白”！
    日月换新天呐！
    嗨啦?儿哟——领：一夯砸个日月圆，
    五夯落地梅花瓣呐！合：嗨——哟——领：日出月落两头见，
    要学红梅笑风寒呐！合：嗨——哟——
    有心战自然，
    不怕严冬寒呐！
    嗨啦?儿哟——领：改涝洼，治荒山，
    修起水库气老天呐！合：嗨——哟——
    引水灌良田，
    多产粮和棉呐，
    嗨啦?儿哟——领：一人难挑万斤担，
    众人伸手顶住天呐！合：嗨——哟——领：千股夯绳拉得紧，
    力气永远用不完呐！合：嗨——哟——
    公社好社员，
    个个是硬汉，
    嗨啦?儿哟——合：加油干呐！
    加油干呐！领：把社会主义的根基，合：夯得牢又坚！
      嗨——哟——
