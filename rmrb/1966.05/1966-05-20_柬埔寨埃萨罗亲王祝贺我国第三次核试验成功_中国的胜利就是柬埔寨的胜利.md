Title: Cambodian Prince Essaro Congratulates China on the Successful Third Nuclear Test: China's Victory is Cambodia's Victory
Author:
Date: 1966-05-20
Edition: 5

    According to a report from Xinhua News Agency in Phnom Penh on the 19th, Prince Essaro, representative of the Cambodian Royal Government to the Department of Sports, congratulated China on the successful third nuclear test in a speech on the evening of the 18th. He said: "This is a great contribution by the great Chinese people to world progress, honor, and peace, and at the same time gives great encouragement to Cambodia. Your victory is also our victory."

    Prince Essaro said: "These tremendous achievements have greatly shocked the imperialists and neo-colonialists and old colonialists because they can no longer use nuclear weapons to threaten the people of Asia." He concluded by shouting: "Long live the friendship between China and Cambodia!" "Long live Chairman Mao Zedong!" "Long live Prince Norodom Sihanouk!"

    Prince Essaro made this speech at a reception he held on the evening of the 18th for the Chinese table tennis team, which is currently visiting Cambodia.

    The Director of Administration of the Cambodian Sports Bureau, Ong Kim, the Chairman of the Reception Committee, Chiung Suo, Cambodian table tennis players, and other well-known figures in the Cambodian sports community attended the reception. Chen Shuliang, the Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia, also attended the reception.

### 柬埔寨埃萨罗亲王祝贺我国第三次核试验成功  中国的胜利就是柬埔寨的胜利

1966-05-20
第5版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨埃萨罗亲王祝贺我国第三次核试验成功
    中国的胜利就是柬埔寨的胜利
    据新华社金边十九日电 柬埔寨王国政府驻体育局代表埃萨罗亲王十八日晚在一次讲话中祝贺中国第三次核试验成功。他说：“这是伟大的中国人民对世界进步、荣誉和和平作出的伟大贡献，同时给柬埔寨以很大的鼓舞。你们的胜利也就是我们的胜利。”
    埃萨罗亲王说：“这些巨大的成就使帝国主义者和新老殖民主义者感到极大的震惊，因为他们不可能再用核武器威胁亚洲人了。”他最后高呼：“中柬友谊万岁！”“毛泽东主席万岁！”“诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王万岁！”
    埃萨罗亲王是在他十八日晚为正在柬埔寨访问的中国乒乓球队举行招待会上发表这一讲话的。
    柬埔寨体育局行政局局长翁金、接待委员会主席琼索、柬埔寨乒乓球运动员和柬埔寨体育界其他知名人士出席了招待会。中国驻柬埔寨大使陈叔亮也出席了招待会。
