Title: Breaking Imperialist Monopoly and Control over International Sports Again: Emerging Forces Weightlifting Invitational Tournament to Be Held in Beijing in May
Author:
Date: 1966-05-08
Edition: 6

    Once Again Breaking the Imperialist Monopoly and Control over International Sports
    Emerging Forces Weightlifting Invitational Tournament to Be Held in Beijing in May
    Xinhua News Agency, 7th – The 1966 Emerging Forces Weightlifting Invitational Tournament is confirmed to be held in Beijing from May 20th to 24th. This invitational tournament is sponsored by the National Committee of the Emerging Forces Games of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese Weightlifting Association.
    According to the organizing committee of this invitational tournament, more than sixty athletes or observers from twelve countries and regions have registered to participate in this invitational tournament, exceeding the scale of the weightlifting competition of the first Emerging Forces Games. At that time, forty-six athletes from eleven countries and regions participated. The twelve countries and regions that will participate in this invitational tournament are: Albania, Cambodia, Ceylon, China, Guinea, Japan, Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Mali, Mauritius, Pakistan, Palestine, and Syria.
    The chairman of the organizing committee of this invitational tournament is Zhong Shitong, Vice Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation and Chairman of the Chinese Weightlifting Association. The vice chairmen are Zhu Debao, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Sports Federation, and Lin Yizhong, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Branch of the All-China Sports Federation.
    The person in charge of the organizing committee told a Xinhua News Agency reporter that this invitational tournament is another new type of international competition independently organized by the people of the emerging forces, breaking through the monopoly and control of imperialism. He represented the organizing committee and Chinese weightlifting athletes in extending a warm welcome to the delegations or observers participating in the invitational tournament, and expressed his deep belief that this invitational tournament, in the spirit of enhancing friendship, mutual promotion, common development, and unity against imperialism, will be successful through the joint efforts of all delegations and observers.
    At present, various preparations for the invitational tournament are actively underway. The competition will be held at the Beijing Gymnasium. The organizing committee has provided sufficient training grounds and equipment for the delegations. The invitational tournament will feature competitions in eight weight categories: flyweight, bantamweight, featherweight, lightweight, middleweight, light heavyweight, middle heavyweight, and heavyweight.

### 再次打破帝国主义对国际体坛的垄断和控制  本年新兴力量举重邀请赛五月在北京举行

1966-05-08
第6版()
专栏：

    再次打破帝国主义对国际体坛的垄断和控制
    本年新兴力量举重邀请赛五月在北京举行
    新华社七日讯　一九六六年新兴力量举重邀请赛，已经确定五月二十日到二十四日在北京举行。这次邀请赛是由中华人民共和国新兴力量运动会全国委员会和中国举重协会主办的。
    据这次邀请赛组织委员会宣布，报名参加这次邀请赛的已经有十二个国家和地区的六十多名运动员或观察员，规模超过第一届新运会的举重比赛。那次比赛有十一个国家和地区的四十六名运动员参加。将参加这次邀请赛的十二个国家和地区是：阿尔巴尼亚、柬埔寨、锡兰、中国、几内亚、日本、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、马里、毛里求斯、巴基斯坦、巴勒斯坦和叙利亚。
    这次邀请赛组织委员会主席是中华全国体育总会副主席、中国举重协会主席钟师统，副主席是中华全国体育总会副秘书长朱德宝和中华全国体育总会北京分会副主席林毅忠。
    组织委员会的负责人对新华社记者说，这次邀请赛是新兴力量人民又一次冲破帝国主义的垄断和控制，独立自主举办的新型的国际比赛。他代表组织委员会和中国举重运动员，对参加邀请赛的各代表队或观察员表示热烈欢迎，并且表示深信，这次邀请赛本着增进友好、相互促进、共同发展、团结反帝的精神，在各代表队和观察员的共同努力下，将获得成功。
    目前，邀请赛的各项筹备工作正在积极进行。比赛将在北京体育馆举行。组织委员会已为各代表队提供了足够的练习场地和器材。邀请赛将进行次最轻量级、最轻量级、次轻量级、轻量级、中量级、轻重量级、次重量级和重量级共八个级别的比赛。
