Title: My Delegation to the Afro-Asian Economic Cooperation Organization Conference Arrives in Rabat
Author:
Date: 1966-05-14
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, 12th -- The Chinese delegation led by Hou Tong, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, arrived in Rabat, the capital of Morocco, on the evening of the 11th to attend the Fifth Conference of the Afro-Asian Economic Cooperation Organization.
    The Chinese delegation was greeted at the airport by representatives of the Moroccan Chamber of Commerce and Industry, the Vice-Chairman and the Secretary-General and Deputy Secretary-General of the Rabat Chamber of Commerce and Industry.
    Yang Qiliang, the Chinese Ambassador to Morocco, also welcomed the delegation at the airport.
    The Fifth Conference of the Afro-Asian Economic Cooperation Organization will be held in Casablanca, Morocco, from May 16 to 20.

### 我出席亚非经济合作组织会议代表团到拉巴特

1966-05-14
第6版()
专栏：

    我出席亚非经济合作组织会议代表团到拉巴特
    新华社十二日讯　拉巴特消息：由中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席侯桐率领的前往参加亚非经济合作组织第五届会议的中国代表团十一日晚上到达摩洛哥首都拉巴特。
    到机场迎接中国代表团的有：摩洛哥工商会主席的代表、拉巴特工商会副主席和正、副秘书长。
    中国驻摩洛哥大使杨琪良也到机场欢迎。
    亚非经济合作组织第五届会议将于五月十六到二十日在摩洛哥卡萨布兰卡举行。
