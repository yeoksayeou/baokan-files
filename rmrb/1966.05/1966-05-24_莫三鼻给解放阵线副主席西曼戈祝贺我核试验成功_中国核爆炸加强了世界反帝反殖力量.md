Title: FRELIMO Vice President Simango Congratulates on the Successful Nuclear Test: Chinese Nuclear Explosion Strengthens World Anti-Imperialist and Anti-Colonial Forces
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-24
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Dar es Salaam, May 22nd -- Uriah Simango, Vice President of the Mozambique Liberation Front (FRELIMO), told Xinhua reporters here on the 22nd: "We warmly congratulate China on the success of its third nuclear explosion. This strengthens the forces in the world that oppose imperialism and colonialism."
    Simango said: "China is a revolutionary and anti-imperialist country, a great supporter of national liberation movements. Its achievement of this recent nuclear explosion means strengthening all the revolutionary forces in the world and inspiring the liberation movements."
    He emphasized: "Imperialists will never give up aggression; they can only be defeated by the revolutionary people of the world." He also said: "Only when we become strong enough to defeat imperialism can we be sure of obtaining freedom, progress, and lasting peace. Therefore, we encourage all revolutionary and anti-imperialist countries in the world to strengthen their military forces to defend peace and oppose imperialist aggression."
    He emphatically pointed out: As long as the imperialists are still pursuing world control and carrying out aggression, we must strengthen our military forces.
    Taking the armed struggle of the Mozambican people as an example, he said that at the beginning of this month, the Portuguese colonialists sent 5,000 colonial troops as reinforcements to Mozambique. He said, "We must also strengthen our military forces to defeat the colonialists." He pointed out that to defeat the colonialists and achieve national independence, it is mainly necessary to rely on the nationalist forces of one's own country.

### 莫三鼻给解放阵线副主席西曼戈祝贺我核试验成功  中国核爆炸加强了世界反帝反殖力量

1966-05-24
第5版()
专栏：

    莫三鼻给解放阵线副主席西曼戈祝贺我核试验成功
    中国核爆炸加强了世界反帝反殖力量
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆二十二日电　莫三鼻给解放阵线副主席乌里亚·西曼戈二十二日在这里对新华社记者说：“我们热烈祝贺中国第三次核爆炸成功。这加强了世界上反对帝国主义和殖民主义的力量。”
    西曼戈说：“中国是一个革命的和反帝的国家，是民族解放运动的伟大支持者，它取得了最近这次核爆炸的成就，就意味着加强了世界上的一切革命力量，鼓舞了解放运动。”
    他强调说：“帝国主义者决不会放弃侵略，只有由世界革命人民来打败他们。”他还说：“只有在我们强大起来，能够打败帝国主义的时候，我们才能肯定得到自由、进步和持久和平。因此，我们鼓励世界上的一切革命和反帝的国家加强它们的军事力量，以保卫和平，反对帝国主义侵略。”
    他着重指出：只要帝国主义者还在追求控制世界，进行侵略，我们就必须加强我们的军事力量。
    他以莫三鼻给人民的武装斗争做例子说：就在本月初，葡萄牙殖民主义者把五千名殖民军增援部队派到莫三鼻给。他说：“我们也必须加强我们的军事力量来打败殖民主义者。”他指出，要打败殖民主义者，实现民族独立，主要要依靠本国的民族主义力量。
