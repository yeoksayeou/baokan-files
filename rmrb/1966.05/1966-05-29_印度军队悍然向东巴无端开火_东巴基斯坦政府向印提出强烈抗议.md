Title: Indian Troops Brazenly Open Fire on East Pakistan Unprovoked; East Pakistan Government Lodges Strong Protest with India
Author:
Date: 1966-05-29
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi, 27th. According to a report by the Pakistan United Press Agency on the 27th, Indian troops again opened fire unprovoked on Pakistani territory in Gobindapur, Sylhet, East Pakistan, on the 27th.
    On the 26th, Indian troops fired on Pakistani farmers cultivating land in Gobindapur with rifles and light automatic weapons. Despite Indian provocations, the East Pakistan border forces exercised extreme restraint.
    The Pakistan United Press Agency said that the East Pakistan government has lodged a strong protest with the Indian authorities in Assam regarding India's provocative actions.

    Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi dispatch. The "Pakistan Times" published an article on the 18th by Pakistani columnist Sulehri, condemning the United States for demanding that Pakistan reduce its defense forces and jointly run economic enterprises with India as preconditions for the United States to restore economic aid to Pakistan. The article said that this requirement is "completely unreasonable," "unimaginable and unrealistic."
    The article pointed out: These two preconditions show that the United States wants to act in accordance with its global strategy. The article said that Pakistan can only coordinate with foreign countries when it is in line with its own national interests. The article said, "If the purposes of the two conflict, we know what to do."
    Referring to the US demand that Pakistan reduce its defense forces, the article pointed out that this is "completely unreasonable." It pointed out that Pakistan had just suffered an armed invasion by India in September of last year. The article said: For its own security and survival, Pakistan has to maintain vigilance and prepare to a considerable level of defense.
    The article said: "The United States wants to coerce Pakistan into embracing India, which is completely impossible." The article also said: "When some countries face major issues of life and death, neither threats nor inducements can make them accept the goals prescribed by others."
    The article pointed out that the preconditions put forward by the United States are ultimately aimed at "containing" China, and in this regard, India has been used as an important tool.
    The article said: "How can Pakistan serve such a crazy strategy, and this also involves our country's sovereignty."
    The article said that if the United States prescribes an unreasonable path for us, we must not lose sight of our historical perspective. Indeed, we must resist it, even if we have to tighten our belts. It said that the Pakistani people are prepared to make all sacrifices for the security and sovereignty of the country, which is a definite fact.

### 印度军队悍然向东巴无端开火  东巴基斯坦政府向印提出强烈抗议

1966-05-29
第6版()
专栏：

    印度军队悍然向东巴无端开火
    东巴基斯坦政府向印提出强烈抗议
    新华社拉瓦尔品第二十七日电　据巴基斯坦联合通讯社二十七日报道，印度军队二十七日又对东巴基斯坦锡尔赫特的戈宾达普尔的巴基斯坦领土无端开火。
    二十六日印度军队曾用步枪和轻自动武器向戈宾达普尔正在耕地的巴基斯坦农民射击。尽管印度进行挑衅，东巴基斯坦的边防军还是竭力克制。
    巴基斯坦联合通讯社说，东巴基斯坦政府已经就印度的挑衅行为向阿萨姆的印度当局提出了强烈抗议。
    据新华社拉瓦尔品第电　《巴基斯坦时报》十八日发表巴基斯坦专栏作家苏勒里写的一篇文章，谴责美国要求巴基斯坦削减国防力量并且同印度合办经济企业作为美国恢复对巴基斯坦的经济援助的先决条件。文章说，这一要求是“完全没有道理的”，“不能设想和不切实际的”。
    文章指出：这两个先决条件表明，美国要按照它的全球战略来办事。文章说，巴基斯坦只有在符合自己国家的利益的情况下才能同外国协调。文章说，“要是这两者的目的有冲突，我们知道怎么办。”
    文章谈到美国要求巴基斯坦削减国防力量时指出，这“完全是没有道理的”。它指出，巴基斯坦在去年九月刚遭到印度的武装侵略。文章说：巴基斯坦为了本国的安全和生存，不得不保持警惕和准备到相当的国防水平。
    文章说：“美国要威逼巴基斯坦同印度拥抱，是完全办不到的。”文章还说：“当一些国家面临生死存亡的大问题时，无论威胁或利诱都不能使它们接受别人规定的目标。”
    文章指出，美国提出的先决条件归根到底是以“遏制”中国为目标的，在这方面，印度已被当作一个重要工具来使用。
    文章说：“怎么能使巴基斯坦为这样疯狂的战略服务呢，而且这还牵涉到我国的主权。”
    文章说，要是美国给我们规定一条不合理的道路，我们千万不能丧失我们的历史的眼光。的确，我们必须抵制它，即使我们得勒紧裤带。它说，巴基斯坦人民准备为国家的安全和主权作出一切牺牲，这是一个确定了的事实。
