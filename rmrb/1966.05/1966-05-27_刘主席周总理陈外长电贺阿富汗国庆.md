Title: Liu Chairman, Premier Zhou, Foreign Minister Chen Send Congratulatory Telegrams on Afghanistan's National Day
Author:
Date: 1966-05-27
Edition: 1

    Liu Chairman, Premier Zhou, Foreign Minister Chen Send Congratulatory Telegrams on Afghanistan's National Day
    Xinhua News Agency, May 26th – Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram to King Mohammed Zahir Shah of Afghanistan on May 26th, congratulating him on Afghanistan's National Day. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Majesty Mohammed Zahir Shah, King of Afghanistan, Kabul:
    On the occasion of Afghanistan's National Day, I extend to Your Majesty my heartfelt congratulations and best wishes. I wish Afghanistan prosperity and its people happiness. May the friendly relations between China and Afghanistan continue to strengthen and develop.
            Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                    May 26, 1966
    Xinhua News Agency, May 26th – Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram to Mohammed Hashim Maiwandwal, Prime Minister of Afghanistan, on May 26th, congratulating him on Afghanistan's National Day. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Excellency Mohammed Hashim Maiwandwal, Prime Minister of the Government of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Kabul:
    On the occasion of Afghanistan's National Day, I extend to Your Excellency and the Government of the Kingdom of Afghanistan my warm congratulations.
    China and Afghanistan are friendly neighbors. The friendly and cooperative relations between our two countries are based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. I firmly believe that, with the joint efforts of our two governments and peoples, this friendly and cooperative relationship will be increasingly consolidated and developed.
    I wish Afghanistan, under the leadership of His Majesty Zahir Shah, continuous achievements in the cause of safeguarding national independence and building its own country.
      Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                  May 26, 1966
    Xinhua News Agency, May 26th – Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram to Noor Ahmad Etemadi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of Afghanistan, on May 26th, congratulating him on Afghanistan's National Day. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Excellency Noor Ahmad Etemadi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Government of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Kabul:
    On the occasion of Afghanistan's National Day, I extend to Your Excellency my sincere congratulations. I wish the friendly relations between China and Afghanistan growing development.
        Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
                  May 26, 1966

### 刘主席周总理陈外长电贺阿富汗国庆

1966-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席周总理陈外长电贺阿富汗国庆
    新华社二十六日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇二十六日打电报给阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿，祝贺阿富汗国庆。电文如下：喀布尔阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿陛下：
    在阿富汗国庆的时候，我向陛下表示衷心的祝贺和良好的愿望。祝阿富汗国家昌盛，人民幸福。祝中阿两国的友好关系不断巩固和发展。
            中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                    一九六六年五月二十六日
    新华社二十六日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来二十六日打电报给阿富汗首相穆罕默德·哈希姆·迈万德瓦尔，祝贺阿富汗国庆。电文如下：喀布尔阿富汗王国政府首相穆罕默德·哈希姆·迈万
  德瓦尔阁下：
    在阿富汗国庆的时候，我向阁下和阿富汗王国政府致以热烈的祝贺。
    中阿两国是友好的邻邦。我们两国的友好合作关系是建立在和平共处的五项原则的基础上的。我深信，在我们两国政府和人民的共同努力下，这种友好合作关系必将日益巩固和发展。
    祝阿富汗在查希尔·沙阿陛下的领导下，在维护民族独立和建设自己国家的事业中不断取得成就。
      中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                  一九六六年五月二十六日
    新华社二十六日讯　中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅二十六日打电报给阿富汗外交大臣努尔·艾哈迈德·埃特马迪，祝贺阿富汗国庆。电文如下：喀布尔阿富汗王国政府外交大臣努尔·艾哈迈德·埃
  特马迪阁下：
    在阿富汗国庆的时候，我向阁下表示诚挚的祝贺。祝中阿两国的友好关系日益发展。
        中华人民共和国外交部长　陈　毅
                  一九六六年五月二十六日
