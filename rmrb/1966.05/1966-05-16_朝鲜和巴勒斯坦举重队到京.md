Title: North Korean and Palestinian Weightlifting Teams Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-05-16
Edition: 6

    According to Xinhua News Agency, May 15th, the weightlifting team of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, a group of eight people led by Kim In-guk, Chairman of the Korean Weightlifting Association, arrived in Beijing by train today. They are invited to China to participate in the 1966 Emerging Forces Weightlifting Invitational Tournament.

    According to Xinhua News Agency, May 14th, the Palestinian weightlifting team, a group of five people led by Abdul Karim Shawa, Chairman of the Palestinian Weightlifting Association, arrived in Beijing by plane tonight. They are invited to China to participate in the 1966 Emerging Forces Weightlifting Invitational Tournament.

### 朝鲜和巴勒斯坦举重队到京

1966-05-16
第6版()
专栏：

    朝鲜和巴勒斯坦举重队到京
    据新华社十五日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国举重队一行八人，由朝鲜举重协会委员长金仁国率领，今天乘火车到达北京。他们是应邀来我国参加一九六六年新兴力量举重邀请赛的。
    据新华社十四日讯　应邀来我国参加一九六六年新兴力量举重邀请赛的巴勒斯坦举重队一行五人，由巴勒斯坦举重协会主席阿卜杜勒·卡里姆·沙瓦率领，今晚乘飞机到达北京。
