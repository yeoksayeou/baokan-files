Title: Guinean Observers Arrive in Beijing to Attend the Emerging Forces Weightlifting Competition
Author:
Date: 1966-05-23
Edition: 6

    To Attend the Emerging Forces Weightlifting Competition
    Guinean Observers Arrive in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, 22nd - D'iazo Mamadou and Bah Dierno, Guinean observers invited to our country to participate in the 1966 Emerging Forces Weightlifting Invitation Tournament, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

**Named Entities**

几内亚	Guinea
北京	Beijing
新兴力量举重赛	Emerging Forces Weightlifting Competition
迪亚索·马马杜	D'iazo Mamadou
巴赫·迪尔诺	Bah Dierno
新华社	Xinhua News Agency
一九六六年新兴力量举重邀请赛	1966 Emerging Forces Weightlifting Invitation Tournament

### 参加新兴力量举重赛  几内亚观察员到京

1966-05-23
第6版()
专栏：

    参加新兴力量举重赛
    几内亚观察员到京
    新华社二十二日讯　应邀来我国参加一九六六年新兴力量举重邀请赛的几内亚观察员迪亚索·马马杜和巴赫·迪尔诺，今天乘飞机到达北京。
