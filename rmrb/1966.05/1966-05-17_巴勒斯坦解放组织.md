    The Palestine Liberation Organization
    In May 1964, more than 400 representatives from Palestine and various Arab countries held the Palestinian National Congress in Jerusalem (Jordanian sector). The congress adopted the Palestinian National Charter and the decision to establish the Palestine Liberation Organization. In June of the same year, the Palestine Liberation Organization was officially established. According to the Palestinian National Charter, the Palestine Liberation Organization is "responsible for leading the Palestinian people's struggle to liberate their homeland." The establishment of the Palestine Liberation Organization marked a more organized and planned stage in the Palestinian people's struggle against US imperialism and Israeli Zionism and to return to their homeland. The highest authority of this organization is the Provisional National Council of the Palestine Liberation Organization. Its Executive Committee is headquartered in Jerusalem, and the chairman is Ahmed Shukeiri.
    In August 1964, the Executive Committee of the Palestine Liberation Organization held its first meeting, formulating the general guidelines for the organization's activities, including the establishment of a Palestinian armed force, organizing the Palestinian people to raise funds for the liberation of Palestine, and explaining the Palestinian issue to the world, etc., and established offices in many Arab countries to carry out work.
    The struggle of the Palestinian people has received the active support of the people of Arab countries and all peace-loving people around the world. The Second Arab Summit Conference held in September 1964 welcomed the establishment of the Palestine Liberation Organization, considering it as "the vanguard of the joint Arab action to liberate Palestine."
    Last March, Shukeiri led a delegation from the Palestine Liberation Organization to visit China and received a warm welcome and reception from the Chinese government and people. In the joint statement issued by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs and the Palestine Liberation Organization, the Chinese people expressed their firm support for the Palestinian Arab people's struggle against imperialism and old and new colonialism for their legitimate rights and to return to their homeland.

### 巴勒斯坦解放组织

1966-05-17
第6版()
专栏：

    巴勒斯坦解放组织
    一九六四年五月，四百多名巴勒斯坦和各阿拉伯国家代表在耶路撒冷（约旦区）举行了巴勒斯坦人国民大会，会上通过了巴勒斯坦人国民宪章和成立巴勒斯坦解放组织的决定。同年六月，正式建立了巴勒斯坦解放组织。根据巴勒斯坦人国民宪章规定，巴勒斯坦解放组织“负责领导巴勒斯坦人民争取解放他们的家园的斗争”。巴勒斯坦解放组织的成立，标志着巴勒斯坦人民反对美帝国主义和以色列犹太复国主义、争取重返家园的斗争，进入了一个更加有组织、有计划的阶段。这个组织的最高权力机构是巴勒斯坦组织临时全国委员会，其执行委员会的总部设在耶路撒冷，主席是艾哈迈德·舒凯里。
    一九六四年八月，巴勒斯坦解放组织执行委员会举行第一次会议，制定了这个组织活动的总方针，其中包括建立一支巴勒斯坦武装力量，组织巴勒斯坦人民为解放巴勒斯坦筹款，以及向全世界说明巴勒斯坦问题等等，并在许多阿拉伯国家设立办事处开展工作。
    巴勒斯坦人民的斗争得到了阿拉伯各国人民和全世界一切爱好和平人民的积极支持。一九六四年九月召开的第二次阿拉伯国家首脑会议，表示欢迎巴勒斯坦解放组织的建立，认为这是“阿拉伯人为解放巴勒斯坦而采取的联合行动的先锋”。
    去年三月，舒凯里率领巴勒斯坦解放组织代表团访问中国，受到我国政府和人民的热烈欢迎和接待。中国人民外交学会在和巴勒斯坦解放组织共同发表的联合声明中，表示中国人民坚决支持巴勒斯坦阿拉伯人民反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义、争取合法权利和重返家园的斗争。
