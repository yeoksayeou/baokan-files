Title: China and Vietnam Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement Implementation Plan
Author:
Date: 1966-05-29
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 28th – The 1966 Implementation Plan of the China-Vietnam Cultural Cooperation Agreement was signed here today.
    Signing on behalf of the Chinese side was Li Qi涛, head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation and Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. Signing on behalf of the Vietnamese side was Phạm Ngọc Thuần, head of the Vietnamese Cultural Delegation and Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.
    Attending the signing ceremony were Nguyễn Cơ Thạch, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Vietnam; Cù Huy Cận, Deputy Minister of Culture; Hoàng Tuấn, Secretary-General of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, and others.
    Chen Liang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Vietnam, also attended the signing ceremony.

### 中越签订文化合作协定执行计划

1966-05-29
第6版()
专栏：

    中越签订文化合作协定执行计划
    新华社河内二十八日电  中国越南文化合作协定一九六六年执行计划，二十八日在这里签字。
    代表中国方面签字的是中国文化代表团团长、对外文化联络委员会副主任李琦涛，代表越南方面签字的是越南文化代表团团长、对外文化联络委员会主任范玉纯。
    参加签字仪式的有，越南外交部副部长阮基石，文化部副部长瞿辉近，越中友协秘书长黄俊等。
    中国驻越南大使馆临时代办陈亮也参加了签字仪式。
