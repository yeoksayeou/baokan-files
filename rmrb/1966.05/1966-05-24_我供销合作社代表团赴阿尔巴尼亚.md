Title: Our Supply and Marketing Cooperative Delegation Departs for Albania
Author:
Date: 1966-05-24
Edition: 5

    Our Supply and Marketing Cooperative Delegation Departs for Albania
    (Xinhua News Agency, May 23rd) At the invitation of the Central Federation of Consumer Cooperatives of Albania, a four-member delegation from the Chinese Supply and Marketing Cooperative, led by Cheng Hongyi, Vice Chairman of the Board of Directors of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives, departed by plane on the 23rd for a friendly visit to Albania. Among those seeing them off were Liang Yao, Vice Chairman of the Board of Directors of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives, and others.



**Named Entities**

阿尔巴尼亚    Ā'ěrbāníyǎ (Albania)
中国供销合作社代表团   Zhōngguó Gōngxiāo Hézuòshè Dàibiǎotuán (Chinese Supply and Marketing Cooperative Delegation)
程宏毅    Chéng Hóngyì
中华全国供销合作总社理事会   Zhōnghuá Quánguó Gōngxiāo Hézuò Zǒngshè Lǐshìhuì (All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives Board of Directors)
阿尔巴尼亚消费合作社中央联合会   Ā'ěrbāníyǎ Xiāofèi Hézuòshè Zhōngyāng Liánhéhuì (Central Federation of Consumer Cooperatives of Albania)
梁耀    Liáng Yào
中华全国供销合作总社    Zhōnghuá Quánguó Gōngxiāo Hézuò Zǒngshè (All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives)

### 我供销合作社代表团赴阿尔巴尼亚

1966-05-24
第5版()
专栏：

    我供销合作社代表团赴阿尔巴尼亚
    新华社二十三日讯　应阿尔巴尼亚消费合作社中央联合会的邀请，中国供销合作社代表团一行四人，在中华全国供销合作总社理事会副主任程宏毅率领下，二十三日乘飞机前往阿尔巴尼亚进行友好访问。前往送行的，有中华全国供销合作总社理事会副主任梁耀等。
