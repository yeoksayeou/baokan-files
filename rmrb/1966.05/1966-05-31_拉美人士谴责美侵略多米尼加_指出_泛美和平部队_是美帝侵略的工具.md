Title: Latin Americans Condemn U.S. Aggression Against Dominican Republic, Point Out "Pan-American Peace Force" is a Tool of U.S. Imperialist Aggression
Author:
Date: 1966-05-31
Edition: 6

    According to a dispatch from Xinhua News Agency, Mexico City, May 29th: According to a report in the Mexican newspaper *Excelsior*, Eduardo Sanchez Cabral, former Dominican Ambassador to the Organization of American States and professor at the University of Santo Domingo, recently condemned in Mexico City that the "Pan-American Peace Force," controlled by the United States, is a tool of U.S. aggression against Latin America and interferes in the internal affairs of the Dominican Republic. Sanchez Cabral also strongly demanded that the "Pan-American Peace Force" immediately withdraw from Dominican territory.

    Sanchez Cabral said that the "Pan-American Peace Force" is actually the "Pan-American War Force." As a result of its invasion, more than 3,000 Dominican patriots have been brutally murdered.

    Mexican Senator and former Chief Justice of the Supreme Court of Justice of the Nation, Juan Jose Gonzalez Bustamante, told the Mexican newspaper *El Dia* on the 26th that the Mexican people condemn U.S. imperialism's aggression against the Dominican Republic and oppose U.S. interference in the internal affairs of the Dominican people.

### 拉美人士谴责美侵略多米尼加  指出“泛美和平部队”是美帝侵略的工具

1966-05-31
第6版()
专栏：

    拉美人士谴责美侵略多米尼加
    指出“泛美和平部队”是美帝侵略的工具
    据新华社墨西哥城二十九日电　据墨西哥《至上报》报道，多米尼加前驻美洲国家组织大使、圣多明各大学教师爱德华多·桑切斯·卡夫拉尔最近在墨西哥城谴责说，美国控制的“泛美和平部队”是美国侵略拉丁美洲的工具，它干涉了多米尼加的内政。桑切斯·卡夫拉尔还强烈要求“泛美和平部队”立即从多米尼加的国土上撤出去。
    桑切斯·卡夫拉尔说，“泛美和平部队”实际上是“泛美战争部队”，由于它的入侵，已经有三千多名多米尼加爱国者惨遭杀害。
    墨西哥参议员、前国家最高法院院长胡安·何塞·冈萨雷斯·布斯塔门特二十六日向墨西哥《日报》发表谈话说，墨西哥人民谴责美帝国主义侵略多米尼加，反对美国干涉多米尼加人民的内政。
