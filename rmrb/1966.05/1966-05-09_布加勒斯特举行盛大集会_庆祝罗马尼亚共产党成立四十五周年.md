Title: Grand Rally Held in Bucharest to Celebrate the 45th Anniversary of the Founding of the Romanian Communist Party
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-09
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, May 7th (Telegraph) The Central Committee of the Romanian Communist Party held a rally on the evening of May 7th in the Palace of the Republic Hall in Bucharest with the participation of 3,000 people to celebrate the 45th anniversary of the founding of the Romanian Communist Party (May 8th).
    Romanian party and government leaders Ceaușescu, Chivu Stoica, and others attended the rally. Diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Romania were also invited to attend the rally.
    Nicolae Ceaușescu, General Secretary of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee, delivered a report at the meeting on "The Romanian Communist Party—The Successor to the Revolutionary Democratic Struggle of the Romanian People, the Successor to the Traditions of the Romanian Workers' and Socialist Movement." After the report, literary and artistic workers from Bucharest performed entertainment programs.

### 布加勒斯特举行盛大集会  庆祝罗马尼亚共产党成立四十五周年

1966-05-09
第5版()
专栏：

    布加勒斯特举行盛大集会
    庆祝罗马尼亚共产党成立四十五周年
    新华社布加勒斯特七日电　罗马尼亚共产党中央委员会七日晚上在布加勒斯特共和国宫大厅举行了有三千人参加的集会，庆祝罗马尼亚共产党成立四十五周年（五月八日）。
    罗马尼亚党政领导人齐奥塞斯库、基伏·斯托伊卡等出席了集会。各国驻罗马尼亚外交使节也应邀出席了集会。
    罗共中央总书记齐奥塞斯库在会上作了关于“罗马尼亚共产党——罗马尼亚人民革命民主斗争的继承者、罗马尼亚工人和社会主义运动传统的继承者”的报告。报告后，布加勒斯特的文艺工作者表演了文娱节目。
