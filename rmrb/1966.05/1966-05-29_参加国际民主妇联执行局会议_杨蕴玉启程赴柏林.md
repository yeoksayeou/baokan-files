Title: Yang Yiyun Departs for Berlin to Attend Executive Bureau Meeting of the Women's International Democratic Federation
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-29
Edition: 6

    Yang Yiyun Departs for Berlin to Attend Executive Bureau Meeting of the Women's International Democratic Federation
    Xinhua News Agency, 28th—Yang Yiyun, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation, left Beijing by plane today for Berlin to attend the Executive Bureau meeting of the Women's International Democratic Federation.

### 参加国际民主妇联执行局会议  杨蕴玉启程赴柏林

1966-05-29
第6版()
专栏：

    参加国际民主妇联执行局会议
    杨蕴玉启程赴柏林
    新华社二十八日讯　全国妇联书记处书记杨蕴玉今天乘飞机离开北京前往柏林，出席国际民主妇女联合会执行局会议。
