Title: Delegation from the National Technical University of Chile Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-05-24
Edition: 5

    A delegation of five members from the National Technical University of Chile, led by Professor Jorge Soto, Vice-Rector and Secretary-General of the National Technical University of Chile, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 16th at the invitation of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, according to Xinhua News Agency.


**Named Entities**

智利国立技术大学  National Technical University of Chile
豪尔赫·索托  Jorge Soto

北京  Beijing

### 智利国立技术大学代表团到京

1966-05-24
第5版()
专栏：

    智利国立技术大学代表团到京
    据新华社讯　应中拉友协邀请来我国访问的智利国立技术大学代表团一行五人，在团长、智利国立技术大学副校长兼秘书长豪尔赫·索托教授的率领下，十六日乘飞机到达北京。
