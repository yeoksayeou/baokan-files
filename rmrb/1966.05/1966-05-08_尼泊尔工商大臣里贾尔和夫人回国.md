Title: Nepalese Minister of Industry and Commerce, Rijal, and Wife Return Home
Author:
Date: 1966-05-08
Edition: 5

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 7th, Nepalese Minister of Industry and Commerce, N.P. Rijal, and his wife left Guangzhou by train this morning via Hong Kong to return to their country.
    N.P. Rijal and his wife were invited to visit our country for a friendly visit and to sign the agreement between the Chinese and Nepalese governments on trade, transportation, and other related issues between the Tibet Autonomous Region of China and Nepal.
    Before returning to their country, the Nepalese guests visited Shanghai and Hangzhou.

### 尼泊尔工商大臣里贾尔和夫人回国

1966-05-08
第5版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔工商大臣里贾尔和夫人回国
    据新华社广州七日电　尼泊尔工商大臣纳·普·里贾尔和夫人，今天上午乘火车离开广州取道香港回国。
    纳·普·里贾尔和夫人是应邀前来我国进行友好访问和签订中尼两国政府关于中国西藏自治区和尼泊尔之间的通商、交通和其他有关问题的协定的。
    尼泊尔客人回国前曾到上海和杭州参观访问。
