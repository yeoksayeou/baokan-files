Title: Romanian Trade Union Fifth Congress Concludes; Decides to Rename the Trade Union Central Council as the General Trade Union; Passes Motion Supporting the Vietnamese People's Anti-American Patriotic Struggle
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-21
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, May 19th - The Fifth Congress of Romanian Trade Unions concluded on the 19th after a four-day meeting.
    The congress opened on the 16th. Romanian party and government leaders Ceaușescu, Chivu Stoica, and others attended the opening ceremony.
    The congress decided to formally rename the Romanian Trade Union Central Council as the "General Trade Union of the Socialist Republic of Romania." Constantin Drăgan was re-elected as the chairman of the Romanian General Trade Union.
    The congress unanimously approved the report made by Drăgan, chairman of the Romanian Trade Union Central Council, at the meeting on the 16th, as well as the activities carried out by the Trade Union Central Council since the last congress. All delegates also unanimously approved the report of the Trade Union Central Supervisory Committee, the charter of the Romanian General Trade Union, the general resolution of the congress, and a motion supporting the Vietnamese people's anti-American patriotic struggle.
    Li Shubin, head of the Chinese trade union delegation and secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, who was invited to attend the congress, delivered a congratulatory speech at the congress on the 16th.
    At the closing meeting on the 19th, Drăgan, the newly elected chairman of the Romanian General Trade Union, delivered the closing speech.

### 罗马尼亚工会五大闭幕  决定把工会中央理事会改名为总工会  通过支持越南人民反美爱国斗争动议

1966-05-21
第6版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚工会五大闭幕
    决定把工会中央理事会改名为总工会  通过支持越南人民反美爱国斗争动议
    据新华社布加勒斯特十九日电　罗马尼亚工会第五次代表大会经过四天的会议后十九日闭幕。
    这次大会是在十六日开幕的。罗马尼亚党政领导人齐奥塞斯库、基伏·斯托伊卡等出席了大会开幕式。
    大会决定把罗马尼亚工会中央理事会正式改名为“罗马尼亚社会主义共和国总工会”。康斯坦丁·德拉冈再次当选为罗马尼亚总工会主席。
    大会一致批准了罗马尼亚工会中央理事会主席德拉冈在十六日会议上所作的报告，以及工会中央理事会自上届代表大会以来所进行的活动。全体代表还一致通过了工会中央监察委员会的报告、罗马尼亚总工会章程、大会总决议和一项支援越南人民反美爱国斗争的动议。
    应邀参加大会的中国工会代表团团长、中华全国总工会书记处书记栗树彬十六日在大会上致了贺词。
    在十九日的闭幕会议上，新选出的罗马尼亚总工会主席德拉冈致了闭幕词。
