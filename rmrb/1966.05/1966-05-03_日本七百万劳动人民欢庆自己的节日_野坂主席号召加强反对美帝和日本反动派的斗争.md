Title: Seven Million Japanese Workers Celebrate Their Holiday; Chairman Nosaka Calls for Strengthening the Struggle Against U.S. Imperialism and Japanese Reactionaries
Author:
Date: 1966-05-03
Edition: 4

    (Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, May 1st) Nearly seven million Japanese workers and other working people held grand rallies in 880 locations across the country on the 1st to celebrate International Labor Day, "May Day."
      The central "May Day" rally was held at Tokyo's spacious Yoyogi Park. It gathered 600,000 workers, farmers, and citizens, who held countless placards and red flags.
    Chairman Nosaka Sanzo of the Japanese Communist Party Central Committee delivered a speech at the meeting. He demanded that the U.S. imperialist troops be driven out of Vietnam, Japan, Asia, and all other countries, and that its military bases be removed from these places. He called on the working class to push forward the struggle against the U.S. imperialist aggression policy and against the revival and strengthening of Japanese militarism by the U.S.-Japanese monopoly capital groups.
    Chairman Sasaki Kozo of the Japanese Socialist Party said in his speech that any force trying to stop the rising storm of the struggle for national independence will inevitably be defeated. He condemned the Sato government for pursuing a policy hostile to China.
      The "May Day" rally passed resolutions on defending peace, improving workers' living conditions, and affirming workers' basic rights.
    After the meeting, a demonstration was held. Participants marched through the streets of Tokyo in five groups. They shouted slogans such as "Support the Vietnamese people!" "Yankees go home!" "Smash the U.S.-Japan Security Treaty!"

### 日本七百万劳动人民欢庆自己的节日  野坂主席号召加强反对美帝和日本反动派的斗争

1966-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

    日本七百万劳动人民欢庆自己的节日
    野坂主席号召加强反对美帝和日本反动派的斗争
    据新华社一日讯　东京消息：将近七百万日本工人和其他劳动人民，一日在全国八百八十个地方举行盛大的庆祝“五一”国际劳动节集会。
      “五一”节中央大会是在东京宽敞的代代木广场举行的。那里聚集了六十万工人、农民和市民，他们举着无数标语牌和红色旗帜。
    日共中央主席野坂参三在会上发表了讲话。他要求把美帝国主义的军队从越南、日本、亚洲和其他一切国家赶走，并从这些地方撤除它的军事基地。他号召工人阶级把反对美帝国主义的侵略政策和反对美日垄断资本集团复活和加强日本军国主义的斗争推向前进。
    日本社会党委员长佐佐木更三发表讲话说，任何势力要制止不断高涨的争取民族独立斗争的风暴，就不能不遭到失败。他谴责佐藤政府奉行敌视中国的政策。
      “五一”节大会通过了关于保卫和平、改善工人生活条件和确定工人基本权利的决议。
    会后举行了示威游行。与会群众分五批走过东京的大街。他们高呼：“支持越南人民！”“美国佬滚回去！”“粉碎日美安全体制！”等口号。
