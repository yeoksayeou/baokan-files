Title: New Emerging Forces Weightlifting Invitational Tournament Reports More Good News: Chinese Athlete Chen Manlin Breaks First New Games Record in Lightest Weight Class
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-22
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 21st. More good news from the 1966 New Emerging Forces Weightlifting Invitational Tournament. In the lightest weight class competition held tonight, our country's athlete Chen Manlin, who won the championship, achieved a total score of 342.5 kg (press 110, snatch 100, clean and jerk 132.5), breaking the record of 337.5 kg for the total score in this weight class at the First New Emerging Forces Games. The original record was set by our country's athlete Li Jiyuan.
    Li Jiyuan participated in a performance tonight, setting a new record of 105 kg for the press in this weight class at the First New Emerging Forces Games, surpassing the original record of 100 kg.
    Japanese athlete Tokio Taniai (谷合時雄) won second place in this weight class with a total score of 320 kg (press 90, snatch 100, clean and jerk 130). Another Japanese athlete, Iwao Izawa (伊泽杰), also achieved a total score of 320 kg (press 97.5, snatch 97.5, clean and jerk 125), but due to his weight (56 kg) being 0.15 kg heavier than Tokio Taniai (谷合時雄), he won third place.
    Tonight's competition lasted four hours, and each successful attempt by the eight athletes participating in the competition was met with loud applause from the more than 5,000 spectators in the audience.
    Syrian athlete Y. Koucho achieved a total score of 277.5 kg tonight (press 85, snatch 85, clean and jerk 107.5), breaking the Syrian record of 275 kg.
    Twenty-two-year-old Albanian athlete Yu. Pamper successfully pressed a 90 kg barbell, setting a new Albanian record, surpassing the original record of 87.5 kg. Pamper's snatch score was 72.5 kg, clean and jerk score was 100 kg, and total score was 262.5 kg.
    The scores of the other three athletes participating in this weight class competition are:
    Cambodian athlete K. Son achieved a total score of 225 kg (press 60, snatch 70, clean and jerk 95);
    Cambodian athlete Ch. Chun achieved a total score of 215 kg (press 70, snatch 65, clean and jerk 80);
    Ceylonese athlete V. Wickremasinghe achieved a total score of 182.5 kg (press 55, snatch 52.5, clean and jerk 75).
    The invitational tournament will hold the next lightest weight class and lightweight class competitions on the 22nd.

### 新兴力量举重邀请赛再传捷报  最轻量级比赛中国选手陈满林刷新第一届新运会纪录

1966-05-22
第6版()
专栏：

    新兴力量举重邀请赛再传捷报
    最轻量级比赛中国选手陈满林刷新第一届新运会纪录
    新华社二十一日讯　一九六六年新兴力量举重邀请赛再传捷报。在今晚举行的最轻量级比赛中，获得冠军的我国选手陈满林的总成绩达到了三四二·五公斤（推举一一○、抓举一○○、挺举一三二·五），打破第一届新兴力量运动会这一级别总成绩三三七·五公斤的纪录。原来的纪录是由我国选手黎纪源创造的。
    黎纪源今晚参加了表演，他以一○五公斤的成绩，刷新了第一届新运会这一级别推举一○○公斤的纪录。
    日本选手谷合时雄获得了这一级别的第二名，总成绩是三二○公斤（推九○、抓一○○、挺一三○）。另一名日本选手伊泽杰的总成绩也达到了三二○公斤（推九七·五、抓九七·五、挺一二五），但是由于他的体重（五十六公斤）比谷合时雄重○·一五公斤，因此获得了第三名。
    今晚的比赛历时四个小时，参加比赛的八名选手取得的每一次成功，都博得了全场五千多观众的响亮掌声。
    叙利亚选手约·库乔克今晚的总成绩达到了二七七·五公斤（推八五、抓八五、挺一○七·五），刷新了二七五公斤的叙利亚纪录。
    二十二岁的阿尔巴尼亚选手于·潘普里成功地推举起了九十公斤重的杠铃，创造了阿尔巴尼亚的新纪录，原来的纪录是八七·五公斤。潘普里的抓举成绩是七二·五公斤，挺举成绩是一○○公斤，总成绩是二六二·五公斤。
    参加这一级别比赛的另外三名选手的成绩是：
    柬埔寨的克森的总成绩是二二五公斤（推六○、抓七○、挺九五）；
    柬埔寨的纯冲的总成绩是二一五公斤（推七○、抓六五、挺八○）；
    锡兰的维杰维克雷姆的总成绩是一八二·五公斤（推五五、抓五二·五、挺七五）。
    邀请赛将在二十二日举行次最轻量级和轻量级比赛。
