Title: Cambodian Ministry of Information Refutes US Dissemination of Lies; US Authorities Create Pretexts for Aggression Against Cambodia; Washington Must Bear Full Responsibility for the Grave Consequences

Author:

Date: 1966-05-30
Edition: 6

    New China News Agency, Phnom Penh, May 29th (dispatch) – According to the Cambodian News Agency on May 29th, the Cambodian Royal Ministry of Information issued a communique, categorically refuting the lie spread by the United States that there are foreign troops on Cambodian territory, and pointed out that this is a pretext created by the United States in an attempt to take aggressive action against Cambodia.

    The communique stated that an American general named Stanley R. Larsen made the false statement at a press conference held in Washington on the 27th, claiming that six regiments of North Vietnamese troops, about 10,000 men, had gathered in an area of Ratanakiri Province in Cambodia bordering South Vietnam.

    The communique pointed out: "No foreign troops are stationed in the above-mentioned area or anywhere else in Cambodia."

    The communique said, "This kind of lie has happened many times. The US authorities have long been trying to piece together pretexts in order to justify the belligerent actions against Cambodia that it now seems to be preparing.” The communique said that this time Larsen’s slander against Cambodia’s neutrality is in fact part of a large-scale psychological offensive against Cambodia, which has been intensifying for weeks.

    The communique said, "Therefore, it seems very clear that the US military authorities, with the tacit or formal consent of the Johnson administration, have decided to use some necessary pretexts to take military action against Cambodia."

    The communique warned that "If Washington uses these pretexts to carry out aggression against Cambodian territory, then Washington must bear full responsibility for the most serious consequences arising therefrom."

### 柬埔寨新闻部驳斥美国散布谎言  美国当局制造借口是为了侵略柬埔寨  华盛顿必须对严重后果承担全部责任

1966-05-30
第6版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨新闻部驳斥美国散布谎言
    美国当局制造借口是为了侵略柬埔寨  华盛顿必须对严重后果承担全部责任
    新华社金边二十九日电　据柬埔寨新闻社二十九日报道，柬埔寨王国新闻部发表公报，断然驳斥美国散布的什么在柬埔寨领土上有外国军队的谎言，并且指出这是美国为了企图对柬埔寨采取侵略行动而制造的借口。
    公报说，一个名叫斯坦莱·拉逊的美国将军二十七日在华盛顿举行的一次记者招待会上造谣，说什么北越的六个团约一万人已在同南越接壤的柬埔寨腊塔纳基里省的一个地区集结。
    公报指出：“在上述地区或柬埔寨境内任何其他地方都没有驻扎任何外国军队。”
    公报说，“这种谎言已经多次发生过，美国当局早已在企图拼凑借口，以便为它目前看来正在准备的对柬埔寨的好战行动进行辩解。”公报说，这一次拉逊对柬埔寨中立的诬蔑，实际上是对柬埔寨的大规模心理攻势的一个组成部分，这种攻势几周来正在加剧。
    公报说，“因此，看来很清楚，美国军事当局在约翰逊政府的默许或者正式同意下，已经决定要利用一些必要的借口来对柬埔寨采取军事行动。”
    公报警告说，“如果华盛顿利用这些借口来对柬埔寨领土进行侵略的话，那么，华盛顿必须要对由此而产生的最严重的后果承担全部责任。”
