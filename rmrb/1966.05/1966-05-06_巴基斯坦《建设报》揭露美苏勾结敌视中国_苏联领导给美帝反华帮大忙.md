Title: Pakistani "Construction Daily" Exposes US-Soviet Collusion to Antagonize China; Soviet Leaders Greatly Help US Imperialism in Anti-China Efforts
Author:
Date: 1966-05-06
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi, May 1st - A Pakistani newspaper, the "Construction Daily," published an article on April 30th stating that the Soviet Union, along with the United States and other Western countries, has been jointly conducting a frenzied anti-China propaganda campaign.

    The article said that when the US imperialist propaganda slandering China failed to work, Soviet propaganda came out to help greatly, portraying China as warmongering.

    The article pointed out: Moscow and Washington cooperate with each other, publishing deceitful remarks about China in order to confuse people who are eager to understand the facts.

    The article said: "Surrounding China with military bases, occupying Taiwan through the Chiang Kai-shek clique, stationing the US Seventh Fleet in the Pacific, and establishing strong naval and air force bases in Okinawa, Japan, are facts sufficient to prove the intentions of US imperialism." The article also said that during the Sino-Indian border conflict, Soviet leaders colluded with Western countries to carry out anti-China propaganda. Even when India launched an armed attack on Pakistan, the British and American blocs, and even the Soviet Union, did not declare India to be the aggressor.

### 巴基斯坦《建设报》揭露美苏勾结敌视中国  苏联领导给美帝反华帮大忙

1966-05-06
第6版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦《建设报》揭露美苏勾结敌视中国
    苏联领导给美帝反华帮大忙
    新华社拉瓦尔品第一日电　巴基斯坦《建设报》四月三十日发表文章说，苏联同美国和其他西方国家一直在联合进行疯狂的反华宣传运动。
    文章说，美帝国主义诬蔑中国的宣传不能奏效时，苏联的宣传就出来大帮其忙，把中国说成是好战的。
    文章指出：莫斯科和华盛顿互相配合，发表关于中国的骗人的言论，以迷惑切望了解事实的人们。
    文章说：“用军事基地来包围中国、通过蒋介石集团霸占台湾，把美国第七舰队派驻在太平洋，在日本的冲绳群岛建立强大的海空军基地，这些就是足以证明美帝国主义意图的事实”。文章还说，在中印边界冲突中，苏联领导人同西方国家勾结起来进行敌视中国的宣传。甚至在印度武装进攻巴基斯坦时，英美集团，还有苏联也不宣布印度是侵略者。
