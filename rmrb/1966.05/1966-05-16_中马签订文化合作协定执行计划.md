Title: China and Mali Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement Execution Plan
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-16
Edition: 6

    China and Mali Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement Execution Plan
    Xinhua News Agency, Bamako, May 14th (by dispatch) – China and Mali signed the 1966 execution plan for the cultural cooperation agreement between the two countries here on the 13th.
    Li Qi, head of the Chinese government cultural delegation and Vice Minister of Culture, and Moussa Keita, High Commissioner for Youth and Sports of Mali, signed the above plan on behalf of their respective governments.
    At the signing ceremony, both Li Qi and Moussa Keita delivered speeches. Li Qi emphasized that in the common struggle against imperialism and new and old colonialism, the friendship and cooperation between China and Mali will continue to develop and be consolidated.
    In his speech, Moussa Keita praised China for setting an example in the development of culture.

### 中马签订文化合作协定执行计划

1966-05-16
第6版()
专栏：

    中马签订文化合作协定执行计划
    新华社巴马科十四日电　中国和马里十三日在这里签订了两国文化合作协定一九六六年执行计划。
    中国政府文化代表团团长、文化部副部长李琦和马里青年和体育高级专员穆萨·凯塔代表两国政府在上述计划上签字。
    在签字仪式上，李琦和穆萨·凯塔都讲了话。李琦强调指出，在反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义的共同斗争中，中马两国之间的友谊和合作将不断地得到发展和巩固。
    穆萨·凯塔在讲话中赞扬中国在发展文化方面树立了榜样。
