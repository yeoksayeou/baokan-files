Title: Zimbabwean People Intensify Armed Struggle, Frightening White Colonialists Who Flee Farms and Hide in Cities; Somali Newspaper Calls on African Countries to Support Zimbabwean People's Struggle
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-08
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Dar es Salaam, May 5th – The Zimbabwe African National Union issued a statement here on the 4th, announcing that Zimbabwean freedom fighters recently attacked a police station 25 miles from Sinoia, killing five policemen of the Smith colonial regime.

    The statement said that these policemen were protecting the main power lines from Kariba to Salisbury. The freedom fighters also shot down some small aircraft belonging to white colonialists.

    The statement said that in the face of the intensified struggle of the Zimbabwean freedom fighters, a large number of white colonialists have left their farms in the Sinoia, Fort Victoria, and Midlands areas and hid in large cities. The Smith regime has dispatched hundreds of colonial troops to various parts of the country and arrested many prominent African figures. The Smith regime has also blockaded news about the freedom fighters' attacks.

    The Zimbabwe African National Union expressed its firm belief in the statement that the Zimbabwean people will surely win the struggle for freedom and independence.

    Xinhua News Agency, Mogadishu, May 3rd – "The Voice of Somalia" published a commentary on May 2nd, supporting the armed struggle of the Zimbabwean people against the Smith colonial regime in Southern Rhodesia.

    The commentary said: "The flame of armed struggle by nationalists against racists has been ignited, and this news brings us the joy of victory on the Rhodesian issue." The commentary "calls on all African countries that oppose Britain and condemn the Smith regime to provide active support."

### 津巴布韦人民加强武装斗争  吓得白人殖民主义者慌慌张张逃离农场躲进城市  索马里报纸呼吁非洲国家支持津巴布韦人民斗争

1966-05-08
第6版()
专栏：

    津巴布韦人民加强武装斗争
    吓得白人殖民主义者慌慌张张逃离农场躲进城市
    索马里报纸呼吁非洲国家支持津巴布韦人民斗争
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆五日电　津巴布韦非洲民族联盟四日在这里发表声明宣布，津巴布韦自由战士最近袭击了离锡诺亚二十五英里的一个警察所，打死五名史密斯殖民政权的警察。
    声明说，这些警察是保护从卡里巴到索尔兹伯里的主要电线的。自由战士还打落白人殖民主义者的一些小型飞机。
    声明说，在津巴布韦自由战士加强斗争面前，大批白人殖民主义者离开他们在锡诺亚、维多利亚堡和米德兰兹地区的农场，躲到大城市里去。史密斯政权派出了数以百计的殖民军到全国各地，逮捕了许多非洲知名人士。史密斯政权还封锁了关于自由战士出击的新闻。
    津巴布韦非洲民族联盟在声明中表示深信，津巴布韦人民必将赢得争取自由和独立的斗争。
    据新华社摩加迪沙三日电《索马里之声报》五月二日发表评论，支持津巴布韦人民反对南罗得西亚史密斯殖民政权的武装斗争。
    评论说：“民族主义者反对种族主义者的武装斗争的火焰已燃烧起来了，这个消息在罗得西亚问题上给我们带来了胜利的喜悦。”评论“要求所有反对英国和谴责史密斯政权的非洲国家给予积极支持。”
