Title: Johnson Administration's Policy of Aggression Against Vietnam Continues to Arouse Public Indignation: People of Various Countries March and Demonstrate, Shouting "Yankees Get Out of Vietnam!"
Author:
Date: 1966-05-31
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 30th -- About 15,000 Japanese workers and citizens held a rally near the U.S. Yokota Air Base on the outskirts of Tokyo on the 29th, protesting the U.S. war of aggression in Vietnam and demanding the removal of U.S. military bases in Japan. They also marched in front of the air base.

    The rally unanimously passed letters of protest to President Johnson and Prime Minister Sato.

    Socialist Party Chairman Sasaki Kozo and others spoke at the rally. They all condemned the Sato government's policy of helping the U.S. government's aggression against Asia.

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 29th -- Five thousand citizens of Calcutta, India, demonstrated in front of the U.S. Information Service building on the 28th, protesting against U.S. imperialism's aggression against Vietnam.

    According to the Associated Press, the demonstrators shouted slogans such as "Down with U.S. Imperialism!" and "Americans Get Out of Vietnam!"

    The demonstrators retaliated against the police who came to suppress them with stones.

    Xinhua News Agency, Melbourne, 28th -- Approximately two thousand Australians held a grand demonstration in Melbourne again on the morning of the 28th, opposing conscription and Australia's involvement in the U.S. war of aggression against Vietnam.

    The demonstrators held a rally in Post Office Square in the city center. Prior to this, they held a demonstration march, starting from the State Parliament House and passing through the main streets of the city to reach the square. They held up hundreds of flags to demonstrate, demanding that the Australian government withdraw its troops from South Vietnam and pursue an independent foreign policy free from U.S. control.

    The demonstrators marched in columns of four, singing "We Shall Overcome" along the way.

    At a rally held after the demonstration march, a resolution was passed demanding that the Australian government withdraw its troops from South Vietnam.

    Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, 29th -- More than three hundred Swedish youths braved strong winds and rain on the afternoon of the 28th to hold rallies and demonstrations protesting the U.S. policy of aggression against Vietnam.

    Speakers at the meeting unanimously condemned the heinous crimes committed by the United States in Vietnam. The meeting passed a resolution opposing U.S. aggression against Vietnam and expressing support for the just struggle of the Vietnamese people.

    After the meeting, the participants held up placards opposing U.S. aggression against Vietnam and held a protest demonstration outside the U.S. Embassy. Ignoring police obstruction, they angrily burned many paper U.S. flags.

### 约翰逊政府侵越政策继续激起公愤  各国人民游行示威高呼美帝滚出越南

1966-05-31
第6版()
专栏：

    约翰逊政府侵越政策继续激起公愤
    各国人民游行示威高呼美帝滚出越南
    据新华社三十日讯　东京消息：大约一万五千名日本工人和市民二十九日在东京郊区美国横田空军基地附近举行大会，抗议美国在越南进行侵略战争，并要求撤除美国在日本的军事基地。他们还在空军基地前面举行游行。
    大会一致通过了致约翰逊总统和佐藤首相的抗议信。
    社会党委员长佐佐木更三等人在大会上发表了讲话。他们都谴责佐藤政府帮助美国政府侵略亚洲的政策。
    新华社二十九日讯　新德里消息：印度加尔各答五千市民二十八日在美国新闻处大楼前举行示威，抗议美帝国主义侵略越南。
    据美联社报道，示威者高呼“打倒美帝国主义！”“美国人撤出越南！”等口号。
    示威者用石块回击了前来镇压的警察。
    据新华社墨尔本二十八日电　大约有二千名澳大利亚人二十八日上午在墨尔本市再次举行盛大的示威游行，反对征兵和反对澳大利亚卷进美国的侵越战争。
    示威者们在市中心的邮政局广场举行集会。在这之前，他们举行了示威游行，从国家议会大厦出发穿过市内主要街道到达广场。他们高举几百面旗帜举行示威，要求澳大利亚政府撤出在南越的军队，并且奉行摆脱美国控制的独立外交政策。
    示威者四人一排列队行进，沿途高唱着“我们必胜”。
    在示威游行后举行的一次集会上，通过了一项决议，决议要求澳大利亚政府从南越撤回它的军队。
    新华社斯德哥尔摩二十九日电　三百多名瑞典青年二十八日下午冒着大风雨，举行集会和示威，抗议美国侵略越南的政策。
    在会上讲话的人一致谴责美国在越南犯下的滔天罪行。会议通过了一项决议，反对美国侵略越南，并且表示支持越南人民的正义斗争。
    会后，与会者高举反对美国侵略越南的标语牌，到美国使馆外面举行了抗议示威，他们不顾警察的阻挠，愤怒地烧毁了许多面纸做的美国国旗。
