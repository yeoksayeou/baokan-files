Title: Comrade Hoxha Receives Four Chinese Delegations
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-12
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, May 10th -- Comrade Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, received here on the 10th the Chinese shipping delegation led by Peng Deqing, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Communications of the People's Republic of China, the Chinese trade union delegation led by He Shumin, Vice Chairman of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Federation of Trade Unions, and the Chinese photography delegation led by Gao Fan, editor-in-chief of "China Photography" and editor-in-chief of "Liberation Army Pictorial".
    During the reception, Comrade Hoxha had a cordial and friendly conversation with the members of the three delegations.
    Present at the reception were Ramiz Alia, member of the Political Bureau and secretary of the Secretariat of the Albanian Labor Party, Kimo Koçani, secretary of the Central Council of Trade Unions, and Ajet Xhimitiku, Secretary-General of the Association of Journalists and Director of the Albanian Telegraph Agency.
    Also present at the reception were Wang Jinchuan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, and Li Mingshan, Economic Counselor.
    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, May 9th -- Comrade Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, received here on the 9th all the members of the Chinese Academy of Sciences delegation led by Pei Lisheng, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Alia, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, and Ili, President of the University of Tirana.
    Wang Jinchuan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, was also present.

### 霍查同志接见我四个代表团

1966-05-12
第5版()
专栏：

    霍查同志接见我四个代表团
    新华社地拉那十日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记恩维尔·霍查十日在这里接见了由中华人民共和国交通部副部长彭德清率领的中国航运代表团、由广西壮族自治区总工会副主席何庶民率领的中国工会代表团和由《中国摄影》主编、《解放军画报》总编辑高帆率领的中国摄影代表团。
    接见时，霍查同志同三个代表团的成员进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党政治局委员、书记处书记拉米兹·阿利雅，工会中央理事会书记基莫·科查尼，新闻工作者协会秘书长、阿尔巴尼亚通讯社社长阿耶特·西米季乌。
    接见时在座的还有中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办王锦川，经济参赞李鸣山。
    新华社地拉那九日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查九日在这里接见了由中国科学院副院长裴丽生率领的中国科学院代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时，在座的有阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、中央书记阿利雅，地拉那大学校长伊利。
    中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办王锦川也在座。
