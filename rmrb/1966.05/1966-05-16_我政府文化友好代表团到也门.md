Title: Our Government Cultural Friendship Delegation Arrives in Yemen
Author:
Date: 1966-05-16
Edition: 6

    Our Government Cultural Friendship Delegation Arrives in Yemen
    Xinhua News Agency, Taiz, May 13th. The Chinese Government Cultural Friendship Delegation, led by Li Chang, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, arrived in Taiz by plane on May 13th at the invitation of the Yemeni government.
    The Chinese delegation was welcomed at the airport by Yemeni Minister of Information Abdul Ghani Ali, as well as the Office Directors of the Yemeni Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Ministry of Information, and the Ministry of Education.
    Wang Ruojie, the Chinese Ambassador to Yemen, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 我政府文化友好代表团到也门

1966-05-16
第6版()
专栏：

    我政府文化友好代表团到也门
    新华社塔伊兹十三日电　由对外文化联络委员会副主任李昌率领的中国政府文化友好代表团应也门政府的邀请，十三日乘飞机到达塔伊兹。
    中国代表团在机场受到也门新闻部长阿卜杜勒·加尼·阿里以及也门外交部、新闻部和教育部的办公厅主任的欢迎。
    中国驻也门大使王若杰也到机场欢迎。
