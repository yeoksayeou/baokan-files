Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions and Beijing Federation of Trade Unions Hold Joint Soiree to Host Trade Union Delegations from Various Countries
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-03
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions held a joint soiree at the Beijing Hotel this evening to host friends from trade union delegations from various countries.
    At the soiree, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Performing Arts Troupe and other performing arts groups presented song, dance, and acrobatics programs.
    Attending the soiree were leading members of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, including Liu Ningyi, Zhu Xuefan, Chen Shaomin, Ma Chungu, Kang Yonghe, Gu Dachun, Peng Siming, and responsible persons from various industrial trade unions, as well as model workers. Workers from Hong Kong and Macau who came to Beijing to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations also attended the soiree.

### 全总和北京市总工会举行联欢晚会招待各国工会代表团

1966-05-03
第1版()
专栏：

    全总和北京市总工会举行联欢晚会招待各国工会代表团
    新华社二日讯　中华全国总工会和北京市总工会，今晚在北京饭店举行联欢晚会，招待各国工会代表团的朋友们。
    晚会上，全国总工会文工团等文艺团体演出了歌舞杂技节目。
    出席晚会的，有中华全国总工会和北京市总工会的负责人刘宁一、朱学范、陈少敏、马纯古、康永和、顾大椿、彭思明和各产业工会的负责人以及劳动模范等。出席晚会的，还有来北京参加“五一”国际劳动节的港澳工人。
