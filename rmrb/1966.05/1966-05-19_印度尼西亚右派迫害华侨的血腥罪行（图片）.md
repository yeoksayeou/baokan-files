Title: The Bloody Crimes of Indonesian Rightists Persecuting Overseas Chinese (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1966-05-19
Edition: 4

    Since October of last year, Indonesian right-wing reactionary forces, while launching a large-scale anti-communist and anti-people campaign, have stirred up a violent countercurrent of anti-China and anti-Chinese sentiment. They have organized, planned, and methodically carried out large-scale atrocities persecuting Overseas Chinese, using fascist terror methods of extermination to burn, kill, loot, kidnap, and rape Overseas Chinese. Colluding with Chiang Kai-shek's special agents, they further politically persecuted Overseas Chinese. The anti-Chinese atrocities have spread throughout the islands of Indonesia, causing countless Overseas Chinese families to be broken up and displaced.
    The thugs organized by the Indonesian right-wing reactionary forces robbed and burned the Chinese Overseas Chinese Association in Cimahi, West Java.
    The thugs organized by the Indonesian right-wing reactionary forces looted the residence of Overseas Chinese Yang Hongtang in Tanjung Priok Port, Jakarta. This is a desolate scene after the looting.
    The thugs organized by the Indonesian right-wing reactionary forces burned the Magelang Chinese School in Central Java to rubble.
    The thugs organized by the Indonesian right-wing reactionary forces smashed the Medan Chinese Overseas Chinese Association in North Sumatra, and the office was looted.
    Many Overseas Chinese shops and residences in Solo, Central Java, were burned to ruins by thugs organized by the Indonesian right-wing reactionary forces.
    The thugs organized by the Indonesian right-wing reactionary forces are frantically smashing the vehicles of Overseas Chinese in Makassar.
            (Xinhua News Agency Draft)

### 印度尼西亚右派迫害华侨的血腥罪行（图片）

1966-05-19
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚右派迫害华侨的血腥罪行
    自从去年十月以来，印度尼西亚右派反动势力在大举反共反人民的同时，掀起了一股反华排华的狂暴逆流。他们有组织、有计划、有步骤地制造了大规模迫害华侨的暴行，以灭绝人性的法西斯恐怖手段对华侨进行烧杀、抢掠、绑架、奸淫，并勾结蒋匪特务，从政治上进一步迫害华侨。排华暴行遍及印度尼西亚大小岛屿，使无数华侨家破人亡，流离失所。
    由印度尼西亚右派反动势力组织的暴徒，在西爪哇芝马圩抢劫和烧毁了中华侨团总会
    印度尼西亚右派反动势力组织的暴徒，洗劫了住在雅加达丹绒不碌港的华侨杨鸿堂的住宅。这是劫后的一片凄凉景象
    印度尼西亚右派反动势力组织暴徒将中爪哇的马吉冷马华学校烧成一片瓦砾
    印度尼西亚右派反动势力组织暴徒捣毁了北苏门答腊的棉兰中华侨团总会，办公室被抢劫一空
    在中爪哇梭罗的许多华侨商店和住宅被印度尼西亚右派反动势力组织的暴徒烧成废墟
    印度尼西亚右派反动势力组织的暴徒正在疯狂捣毁望加锡华侨的车辆
            （新华社稿）
