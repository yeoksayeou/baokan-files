Title: New East German Ambassador Calls on Premier Zhou Enlai

Author: 
Date: 1966-05-31
Edition: 6

    New East German Ambassador Calls on Premier Zhou Enlai
    Xinhua News Agency, May 30th. The new Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Martin Bierbach, paid a courtesy call on Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council at noon today. Premier Zhou Enlai had a friendly conversation with him.

### 民主德国新任大使拜会周恩来总理

1966-05-31
第6版()
专栏：

    民主德国新任大使拜会周恩来总理
    新华社三十日讯　德意志民主共和国新任驻华大使马丁·比尔巴赫，今天中午拜会国务院总理周恩来。周恩来总理同他进行了友好的谈话。
