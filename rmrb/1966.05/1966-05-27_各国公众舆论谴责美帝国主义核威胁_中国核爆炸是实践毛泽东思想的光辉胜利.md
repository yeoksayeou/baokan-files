Title: Public Opinion in Various Countries Condemns US Imperialist Nuclear Threats; China's Nuclear Explosion is a Glorious Victory for the Practice of Mao Zedong Thought
Author:
Date: 1966-05-27
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 25th – People and publications in Japan, Cambodia, and Pakistan have expressed their welcome for the successful third nuclear test conducted by China.
    Ichio Mishima, Vice Chairman of the Japan-China Friendship Association and a renowned historian, said that the success of China's third nuclear test is a glorious victory for the Chinese people in studying and practicing Mao Zedong Thought and achieving a great leap forward in socialist construction.
    Ichio Mishima stated that China's nuclear tests were entirely defensive measures forced upon them in opposition to the US imperialist nuclear war plans. He condemned US imperialism for openly making nuclear threats against China and Asia.
    Ichio Mishima expressed his full support for the Chinese government's consistent position on the complete prohibition and thorough destruction of nuclear weapons and the guarantee of no first use. He condemned the United States for stubbornly refusing to undertake the obligation of no first use of nuclear weapons. He said that as long as these two positions are compared, it can be known who the provocateur is.
    Kenzo Nakajima, Director-General of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, said that China's third nuclear test, like the previous two, was aimed at stopping US imperialism from waving nuclear weapons to engage in war adventures.
    He expressed his full support for the Chinese government's consistent position on the complete prohibition and thorough destruction of nuclear weapons.
    The Cambodian weekly *Le Nationaliste* recently pointed out: "The third nuclear test of the People's Republic of China is a response to US imperialism: Great China is not afraid of American weapons, and Great China is a barrier against the bellicose imperialists."
    The Lahore *Horizon* in Pakistan recently published an article on the successful third nuclear test in China, saying that the people of Pakistan are overjoyed by this achievement of their neighbor and friend, China.
    The article pointed out, "We believe that this event helps the people of Asia, Africa, and Latin America to get rid of the atomic war threats and blackmail of atomic powers, especially US imperialism."
    The article, in exposing the Soviet revisionist leading group's opposition to China's nuclear tests, said: Because the United States is constantly making nuclear threats against China, "China cannot disregard its own national defense simply because the United States, Russia, and Britain signed an agreement in Moscow—an agreement stipulating that they will not conduct nuclear tests in the air, but only conduct underground tests."
    The article said that China's vigilance towards its own national defense is not only good for itself but also a guarantee of the independence of all the oppressed people in the world.
    Xinhua News Agency, May 26th – Bogota News: Luis Villar Borda, leader of the Colombian Free Revolutionary Movement, recently commented on China's third nuclear explosion, saying that China's possession of nuclear weapons "curbs the claws of nuclear war provocateurs."
    He said that China's nuclear test is an encouragement to underdeveloped countries. This nuclear explosion proves the progress China "has made in the social, cultural, scientific, and economic fields in recent years."
    He pointed out, "China's possession of atomic weapons not only does not threaten world peace, but also safeguards world peace."

### 各国公众舆论谴责美帝国主义核威胁  中国核爆炸是实践毛泽东思想的光辉胜利

1966-05-27
第5版()
专栏：

    各国公众舆论谴责美帝国主义核威胁
    中国核爆炸是实践毛泽东思想的光辉胜利
    新华社二十五日讯　日本、柬埔寨和巴基斯坦人士和报刊表示欢迎中国第三次核试验成功。
    日中友好协会副会长、著名历史学家三岛一说，中国第三次核试验的成功，是中国人民学习和实践毛泽东思想，在社会主义建设的大跃进中取得的光辉胜利。
    三岛一说，中国进行核试验完全是为了反对美帝国主义的核战争计划而被迫采取的自卫措施。他谴责美帝国主义公然对中国和亚洲进行核威胁。
    三岛一表示完全支持中国政府关于全面禁止和彻底销毁核武器、保证不首先使用核武器的一贯立场。他谴责美国顽固地拒绝承担不首先使用核武器的义务。他说，只要把这两种立场对照起来看，就可知道究竟谁是挑衅者。
    日中文化交流协会理事长中岛健藏说，中国进行第三次核试验，同前两次一样，都是为了制止美帝国主义挥舞核武器进行战争冒险。
    他表示完全支持中国政府关于全面禁止和彻底销毁核武器的一贯立场。
    柬埔寨《民族主义者》周刊最近一期指出：“中华人民共和国的第三次核试验是对美帝国主义的回答：伟大的中国不怕美国的武器，伟大的中国是抗击好战的帝国主义者的屏障。”
    巴基斯坦拉合尔的《地平线报》最近就中国第三次核试验成功发表文章说，巴基斯坦人民为他们的邻邦和朋友——中国取得这一成就感到欢欣鼓舞。
    文章指出，“我们认为，这一事件有助于使亚、非、拉人民摆脱原子国家，特别是美帝国主义的原子战争的威胁和讹诈。”
    文章在揭露苏联修正主义领导集团反对中国核试验的立场时说：由于美国不断对中国进行核威胁，“中国不能仅仅因为美国、俄国和英国在莫斯科签订了一项协定——协定规定它们将不在空中进行核试验，而只限于进行地下试验——而不顾自己的国防。”
    文章说，中国对自己的国防保持警惕不仅对自己有好处，而且是世界上一切被压迫国家人民的独立的保证。
    新华社二十六日讯　波哥大消息：哥伦比亚自由革命运动领导人路易斯·比利亚尔·博尔达最近就中国第三次核爆炸发表谈话说，中国掌握核武器“制止着核战争挑拨者的魔爪”。
    他说，中国的核试验是对不发达国家的鼓舞。这次核爆炸证明了中国“最近几年在社会、文化、科学和经济方面取得的进展”。
    他指出，“中国拥有原子武器非但不威胁世界和平，而且是维护世界和平的。”
