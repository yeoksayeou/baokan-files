Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi and Others Receive Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-05-26
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, May 25th -- Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council this afternoon received all members of the Japanese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee delegation to China, headed by Minoru Ito. Vice Premier Chen Yi had a cordial and friendly conversation with them. Present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi, Wang Chuanbin, Wang Xiaoyun, Wang Zhifan, and others. Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji, who is currently in Beijing, was also present.
    Xinhua News Agency, May 25th -- Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee, today received the Japanese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee delegation headed by Minoru Ito and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them. Present were Yang Ji, Wang Chuanbin, Wang Xiaoyun, Wang Zhifan, and others. Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji, who is in Beijing, was also present. (Attached picture)
    May 25th, Vice Premier Chen Yi received all members of the Japanese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee delegation to China, headed by Minoru Ito (front row, third from right).
                                    Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian

### 陈毅副总理等接见日本客人

1966-05-26
第3版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理等接见日本客人
    新华社二十五日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今天下午接见了以伊藤实为首的日本亚非团结委员会访华代表团全体成员。陈毅副总理同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有廖承志、王传斌、王晓云、王植范等。在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也在座。
    新华社二十五日讯　中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志今天接见了以伊藤实为首的日本亚非团结委员会访华代表团，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。在座的有杨骥、王传斌、王晓云、王植范等。在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一在座。（附图片）
    五月二十五日，陈毅副总理接见以伊藤实（前右三）为首的日本亚非团结委员会访华代表团全体成员。
                                    新华社记者　杜修贤摄
