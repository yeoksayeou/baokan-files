Title: Indonesian Right-Wing Military Regime Hastens to Rely on American Imperialism, Begging for American "Aid"; Demands Japan Act as Intermediary to Join the "Asian Development Bank"
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-09
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 7th. According to a report in the Japanese newspaper Nihon Keizai Shimbun on the 7th, the Indonesian right-wing military regime, following its foreign policy of relying on the United States, has recently requested the Japanese government to act as an intermediary for it to join the "Asian Development Bank," which is controlled by the United States.
    The report stated that the Japanese Foreign Ministry expressed "welcome" to the Indonesian right-wing military regime's request to join the "Asian Development Bank" and indicated its willingness to "actively mediate" for it.
    The "Asian Development Bank" was established in December of last year based on President Johnson's "ten billion dollar Southeast Asia development plan." It is a tool for American imperialism to promote neocolonialism in the Asian region.

### 印尼右派军人政权加紧投靠美帝乞求美“援”  要求日本拉线参加“亚洲开发银行”

1966-05-09
第5版()
专栏：

    印尼右派军人政权加紧投靠美帝乞求美“援”
    要求日本拉线参加“亚洲开发银行”
    新华社东京七日电　据日本经济新闻七日报道，印度尼西亚右派军人政权按照它投靠美国的外交政策，最近已要求日本政府替它拉线，以便参加由美国控制的“亚洲开发银行”。
    报道说，日本外务省对印度尼西亚右派军人政权要求参加“亚洲开发银行”表示“欢迎”，并且表示愿意为它“积极斡旋”。
    “亚洲开发银行”是根据美国总统约翰逊提出的“用十亿美元开发东南亚计划”在去年十二月成立的，它是美帝国主义在亚洲地区推行新殖民主义的工具。
