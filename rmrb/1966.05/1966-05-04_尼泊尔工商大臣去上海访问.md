Title: Nepalese Minister of Industry and Commerce Visits Shanghai
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-04
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 3rd. Nepalese Minister of Industry and Commerce, Na·Pu· Rijar and his wife, accompanied by Acting Minister of Foreign Trade Lin Haiyun and his wife, left Beijing by plane this morning to visit Shanghai.
    Those seeing them off at the airport included Han Nianlong, Li Yingji, Zhou Shujia and other responsible persons from relevant departments. Nepalese Ambassador to China, Lundhir Subba and his wife also saw them off at the airport.

### 尼泊尔工商大臣去上海访问

1966-05-04
第5版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔工商大臣去上海访问
    新华社三日讯　尼泊尔工商大臣纳·普·里贾尔和夫人由对外贸易部代理部长林海云和夫人陪同，今天上午乘飞机离开北京去上海访问。
    到机场送行的，有韩念龙、李应吉、周叔迦等有关方面负责人。尼泊尔驻中国大使伦迪尔·苏巴和夫人也到机场送行。
