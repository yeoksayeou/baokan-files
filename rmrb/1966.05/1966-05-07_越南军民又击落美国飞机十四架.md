Title: Vietnamese Army and People Shoot Down Fourteen More American Aircraft

Author: 
Date: 1966-05-07
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 2nd (electric dispatch) According to announcements here, the army and people of Ha Tinh, Nghe An, and Thanh Hoa provinces in northern Vietnam each shot down one invading American bandit plane on May 2nd.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 3rd (electric dispatch) According to announcements here on the 3rd, the army and people of Me Island, Thanh Hoa province in northern Vietnam shot down an American jet fighter on the morning of May 1st.
    On the afternoon of the 2nd, the army and people of Thanh Hoa province shot down another American jet fighter.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 5th (electric dispatch) According to announcements here on the evening of the 5th, the army and people of northern Vietnam shot down a total of seven invading American bandit planes on the 5th. Among them, the army and people of Nghe An province shot down two enemy planes, and the army and people of Bac Thai, Ha Bac, Lang Son, Quang Ninh, and Thanh Hoa provinces each shot down one enemy plane.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 6th (electric dispatch) According to announcements here, the army and people of Yen Bai province in northern Vietnam shot down an invading American bandit plane on May 6th.
    According to supplementary reports, at the end of April, the army and people of Yen Bai province also shot down an American jet fighter.

### 越南军民又击落美国飞机十四架

1966-05-07
第5版()
专栏：

    越南军民又击落美国飞机十四架
    新华社河内二日电　据这里公布，越南北方河静、义安和清化三省军民五月二日各击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架。
    新华社河内三日电　据这里三日公布，越南北方清化省湄岛军民五月一日上午击落美国喷气式飞机一架。
    二日下午，清化省军民又击落美国喷气式飞机一架。
    新华社河内五日电　据这里五日晚上公布：越南北方军民五日共击落入侵的美国强盗飞机七架。其中，义安省军民击落敌机两架，北太、河北、谅山、广宁、清化等省军民各击落敌机一架。
    新华社河内六日电　据这里公布，越南北方安沛省军民五月六日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架。
    另据补充报道，在四月底，安沛省军民还击落美国喷气式飞机一架。
