Title: Lao Patriotic Forces News Delegation Leader Ong Sa-ang Cheers New Victories of Our Striving for Strength and Self-Reliance
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-12
Edition: 5

    Lao Patriotic Forces News Delegation Leader Ong Sa-ang
    Cheers New Victories of Our Striving for Strength and Self-Reliance
    Xinhua News Agency, 11th.—Zhalun·Ong Sa-ang, the leader of the Lao Patriotic Forces News Delegation, said that the delegation was extremely excited about China's successful nuclear explosion containing thermonuclear materials. He said that the delegation expressed its high respect, heartfelt praise, and warm cheers for the great achievements made by the Chinese people under the wise and correct leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong.
    Leader Zhalun·Ong Sa-ang, who is currently visiting Yan'an, said this in a telegram he sent to the All-China Journalists Association on the 10th. The All-China Journalists Association received this telegram.
    The telegram said that the delegation "warmly praised the spirit of the Chinese scientists, engineers, and workers for striving for strength and self-reliance, which made the nuclear test a brilliant success and contributed to strengthening national defense and safeguarding world peace."

### 老挝爱国力量新闻代表团长翁桑昂  欢呼我奋发图强自力更生的新胜利

1966-05-12
第5版()
专栏：

    老挝爱国力量新闻代表团长翁桑昂
    欢呼我奋发图强自力更生的新胜利
    新华社十一日讯　老挝爱国力量新闻代表团团长扎伦·翁桑昂说，代表团对中国成功地进行含有热核材料的核爆炸感到万分兴奋。他说，代表团对中国人民在中国共产党和毛泽东主席英明正确领导下所取得的这个伟大成就表示崇高的敬意、衷心的赞扬和热烈的欢呼。
    正在延安访问的扎伦·翁桑昂团长是在他十日致中华全国新闻工作者协会的一份电报中这样说的。中华全国新闻工作者协会收到了这份电报。
    这份电报说，代表团“热烈赞扬中国科学家、工程师、工人奋发图强、自力更生的精神，使得这次核试验取得辉煌的成功，为加强国防力量、保卫世界和平作出了贡献。”
