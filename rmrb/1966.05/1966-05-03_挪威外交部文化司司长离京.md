Title: Head of Cultural Affairs of the Norwegian Ministry of Foreign Affairs Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-05-03
Edition: 4

    The Head of Cultural Affairs of the Norwegian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Erling Kristoffersen, who came to China at the invitation of Song Yiping, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, to sign the Sino-Norwegian Cultural Exchange Program and to pay a visit, left Beijing by plane today to visit Southern China before returning to his country.

### 挪威外交部文化司司长离京

1966-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

    挪威外交部文化司司长离京
    新华社二日讯　应对外文委副主任宋一平邀请来我国签订中挪文化交流计划并进行访问的挪威外交部文化司司长尔林·克里斯托费生，今天乘飞机去我国南方访问，然后回国。
