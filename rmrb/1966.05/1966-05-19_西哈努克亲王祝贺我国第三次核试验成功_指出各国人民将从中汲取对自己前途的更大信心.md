Title: Prince Sihanouk Congratulates China on the Successful Third Nuclear Test, Points Out That People of All Countries Will Draw Greater Confidence in Their Future From It
Author:
Date: 1966-05-19
Edition: 5

    Prince Sihanouk Congratulates China on the Successful Third Nuclear Test
    Points Out That People of All Countries Will Draw Greater Confidence in Their Future From It
    Xinhua News Agency, May 18th -- Premier Zhou Enlai received a telegram on May 18th from Cambodian Head of State Prince Sihanouk, expressing his heartfelt thanks for China's reception of the delegation of the Cambodia-China Friendship Association and his most heartfelt congratulations on China's successful nuclear explosion containing thermonuclear materials. The full text of the telegram is as follows:
    To His Excellency Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:
    The warm welcome and multifaceted care that Your Excellency and the Chinese government have extended to the delegation of the Cambodia-China Friendship Association once again movingly demonstrate the fraternal friendship and unity between our two countries. On behalf of the Cambodian people and in my own name, I extend my heartfelt thanks to Your Excellency, the Chinese government, and the Chinese people. I would also like to take this opportunity to express to Your Excellency our most heartfelt congratulations on China's new nuclear test, which marks a glorious achievement of Chinese science. The people of all countries around the world who oppose imperialist aggressors, uphold justice, and love peace will draw greater confidence in their future from it and pay tribute to the wisdom displayed by China, the only atomic power to solemnly declare that it will never be the first to use such weapons of mass destruction.
    Please accept my highest and most friendly regards.
                            Norodom Sihanouk, Head of State of Cambodia
                                  Phnom Penh, May 18, 1966

### 西哈努克亲王祝贺我国第三次核试验成功  指出各国人民将从中汲取对自己前途的更大信心

1966-05-19
第5版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王祝贺我国第三次核试验成功
    指出各国人民将从中汲取对自己前途的更大信心
    新华社十八日讯　周恩来总理十八日收到了柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王的电报，他表示衷心感谢中国对柬中友协代表团的接待和最衷心地祝贺中国成功地进行了含有热核材料的核爆炸。电报全文如下：北京中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来先生阁下：
    阁下和中国政府盛情给予柬中友协代表团的热烈欢迎和多方关怀，再一次令人感动地表明了我们两国之间的兄弟般的友谊和团结。我代表柬埔寨人民，并以我本人的名义，向阁下、向中国政府和中国人民表示衷心的谢意。我还要借此机会高兴地就标志中国科学辉煌成就的中国的新的核试验，向阁下表示我们最衷心的祝贺。全世界反对帝国主义侵略者、主持正义和爱好和平的各国人民，从中汲取对他们前途的更大信心，并向唯一庄严宣告绝不首先使用这种大规模杀伤武器的原子强国——中国表现出来的明智表示敬意。
    顺致最崇高、最友好的敬意。
                            柬埔寨国家元首　诺罗敦·西哈努克
                                  一九六六年五月十八日于金边
