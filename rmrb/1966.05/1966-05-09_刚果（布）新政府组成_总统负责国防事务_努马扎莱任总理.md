Title: New Government Formed in Congo (Brazzaville): President Responsible for Defense Affairs, Noumazalaye Appointed Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1966-05-09
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Brazzaville, May 6th – Congolese (Brazzaville) President Massamba-Débat announced on the radio on the 6th the formation of a new government headed by Noumazalaye, the First Political Secretary of the Congolese National Revolution Movement, as Prime Minister.
    The new government is composed of ten members. In addition to serving as Prime Minister, Noumazalaye also holds the post of Minister of Planning. Other members of the new government include: Ganhao, Minister of Foreign Affairs, Cooperation, and responsible for tourism, civil aviation, and Equatorial African transportation links; Ebuka-Babaka, Minister of Finance, Budget, and Mining; Ombessa, Minister of Interior and Posts and Telecommunications; Dakosta, Minister of Construction, Agriculture, and Animal Husbandry; Makani, Minister of National Education; and Nfouvama, Minister of Information and responsible for youth, sports, civic education, culture, and arts affairs.
    President Massamba-Débat also announced that, at the request of the Political Bureau of the National Revolution Movement, he will continue to be responsible for defense affairs.
    Former Minister of Information Zoniaba, Minister of National Education Gabriel Betou, and Minister of Trade George Mantisa did not hold positions in the new government.
    In April, former Prime Minister Lissouba resigned. On April 15th, the Political Bureau of the Congolese National Revolutionary Committee held a meeting, instructing President Massamba-Débat to accept Lissouba's resignation and appoint another new Prime Minister and reorganize the government.

### 刚果（布）新政府组成  总统负责国防事务　努马扎莱任总理

1966-05-09
第5版()
专栏：

    刚果（布）新政府组成
    总统负责国防事务　努马扎莱任总理
    新华社布拉柴维尔六日电  刚果（布）总统马桑巴—代巴六日在电台宣布成立了以刚果全国革命运动第一政治书记努马扎莱为总理的新政府。
    新政府由十人组成，努马扎莱除担任总理外，兼任计划部长。新政府其他成员包括：外交、合作和负责旅行事业、民航和赤道非洲交通联系部长加纳奥，财政、预算和矿业部长埃布卡—巴巴卡，内政和邮电部长翁贝萨，建设、农业和畜牧业部长达科斯塔、国民教育部长马卡尼和新闻和负责青年、体育、公民教育、文化和艺术事务部长恩富瓦马。
    马桑巴—代巴总统还宣布，他应全国革命运动政治局的要求，继续负责国防事务。
    前政府中的新闻部长佐尼亚巴、国民教育部长加布里埃尔·贝图和贸易部长乔治·芒蒂萨没有在新政府中任职。
    四月间，前总理利苏巴提出辞职。四月十五日，刚果全国革命委员会政治局举行会议，指示总统马桑巴—代巴接受利苏巴的辞职，并任命另一位新总理和改组政府。
