Title: Sino-Latin American Friendship Association and China Council for the Promotion of International Trade Hold Reception to Welcome Chilean Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-05-28
Edition: 5

    The China-Latin America Friendship Association and the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade held a reception this evening to welcome the Chilean Parliamentary Delegation led by Carlos Sibo Li Arcereca, Second Vice President of the Chilean Chamber of Deputies.
    Chu Tunan, President of the China-Latin America Friendship Association, said at the reception that in recent years, the cultural exchanges and trade relations between the people of China and Chile have further developed, and a precious friendship has been established between the people of the two countries. Commander Sibo Li said in his speech that we express our respect for the great China and the hard-working Chinese people. We have personally witnessed the Chinese people constantly striving forward every day to build their own country.
    Attending the reception were Yong Longgui, Lian Guan, Wang Yinpu, Li Zewang, Zhao Zhengyi, Li Yiannian, Du Jinfang and other responsible persons from relevant departments and people who have visited Chile.

### 中拉友协和国际贸易促进会举行酒会  欢迎智利议员代表团

1966-05-28
第5版()
专栏：

    中拉友协和国际贸易促进会举行酒会
    欢迎智利议员代表团
    新华社二十七日讯　中国拉丁美洲友好协会和中国国际贸易促进委员会今晚举行酒会，欢迎由智利众议院第二副议长卡洛斯·席博里·阿尔塞雷卡率领的智利议员代表团。
    中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南在酒会上说，近年来，中国和智利两国人民间的文化交流和贸易关系有了进一步的发展，两国人民之间建立了珍贵的友谊。席博里团长在讲话中说，我们对伟大的中国和勤劳的中国人民表示敬意。我们亲眼看到中国人民为了建设自己的国家每天都在不断努力前进。
    出席酒会的，有勇龙桂、连贯、王荫圃、李则望、赵政一、李言年、杜近芳等有关方面负责人和访问过智利的人士。
