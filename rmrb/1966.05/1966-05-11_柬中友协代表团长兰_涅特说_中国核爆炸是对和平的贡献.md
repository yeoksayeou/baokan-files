Title: Cambodian-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Head Lan Niete Says: China's Nuclear Explosion is a Contribution to Peace
Author:
Date: 1966-05-11
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, May 10th - Lan Niete, head of the Cambodian-Chinese Friendship Association delegation and chairman of the Cambodian-Chinese Friendship Association, who is currently visiting here, said that we are very pleased with China's successful third nuclear explosion. China's nuclear weapons are for defending peace, and this nuclear explosion is another contribution to the cause of peace.
    The Cambodian guests arrived in Harbin from Beijing by plane on the 8th for a visit. That evening, Wang Yilun, Deputy Governor of Heilongjiang Province, hosted a welcome banquet for the guests. In the past few days, the guests have visited factories, schools, and the Harbin Children's Railway in Harbin, and have been warmly welcomed by the people.

### 柬中友协代表团长兰·涅特说  中国核爆炸是对和平的贡献

1966-05-11
第5版()
专栏：

    柬中友协代表团长兰·涅特说
    中国核爆炸是对和平的贡献
    新华社哈尔滨十日电　正在这里访问的柬中友协代表团团长、柬中友协主席兰·涅特说，中国成功地进行第三次核爆炸，使我们非常高兴。中国的核武器是保卫和平的，这次核爆炸是对和平事业的又一次贡献。
    柬埔寨贵宾是八日乘飞机从北京到哈尔滨访问的。当晚，黑龙江省副省长王一伦为贵宾们举行了欢迎宴会。这几天，贵宾们在哈尔滨参观了工厂、学校和哈尔滨儿童铁路，受到了人们的热烈欢迎。
