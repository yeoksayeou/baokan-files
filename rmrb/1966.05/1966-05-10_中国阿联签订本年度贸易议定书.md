Title: China and the United Arab Republic Sign This Year's Trade Protocol
Author:
Date: 1966-05-10
Edition: 5

    China and the United Arab Republic Sign This Year's Trade Protocol
    (Xinhua News Agency, Cairo) The trade protocol for 1966 under the trade agreement between China and the United Arab Republic was signed here on the 4th. According to the protocol, China will export tea, meat, hardware, chemical products, and other goods to the United Arab Republic, and import commodities such as cotton from the United Arab Republic. The trade volume between the two countries in 1966 will increase compared to 1965.
    Lei Renmin, head of the Chinese government trade delegation and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and Hussein Khaled Hamdy, head of the United Arab Republic government trade delegation and State Secretary of the Ministry of Economy and Foreign Trade, signed the protocol on behalf of their respective governments.

### 中国阿联签订本年度贸易议定书

1966-05-10
第5版()
专栏：

    中国阿联签订本年度贸易议定书
    据新华社开罗电　中国和阿联贸易协定一九六六年度的议定书四日在这里签订。议定书规定，中国将向阿联出口茶、肉类、五金、化工产品和其他货物，并从阿联进口棉花等商品。两国一九六六年的贸易额将比一九六五年有所增加。
    中国政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长雷任民和阿联政府贸易代表团团长、经济和对外贸易部国务秘书侯赛因·哈立德·哈姆迪分别代表两国政府在议定书上签了字。
