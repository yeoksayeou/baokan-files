Title: Chinese Trade Union Delegation Visits Cyprus
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-21
Edition: 6

    Our Trade Union Delegation Leaves for Cyprus
    (Xinhua News Agency, 18th) A Chinese trade union delegation led by Kang Yonghe, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, left Beijing by plane today for Cyprus to attend the 32nd Executive Committee meeting of the World Federation of Trade Unions to be held there.
    Those who saw the delegation off at the airport included Ma Chungu, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Wang Zhijie, Secretary of the Secretariat.

### 我工会代表团赴塞浦路斯

1966-05-21
第6版()
专栏：

    我工会代表团赴塞浦路斯
    新华社十八日讯　由中华全国总工会副主席康永和率领的中国工会代表团，今天乘飞机离开北京前往塞浦路斯，参加即将在那里举行的世界工会联合会第三十二次执行委员会会议。
    到机场送行的有中华全国总工会副主席马纯古，书记处书记王志杰等。
