Title: China and Norway Sign Cultural Exchange Plan
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-02
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th -- The 1966 and 1967 Cultural Exchange Plan between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Kingdom of Norway was signed in Beijing today. Song Yiping, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, signed on behalf of the Chinese government; Erling Kristoffersen, Director-General of the Cultural Department of the Norwegian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, signed on behalf of the Norwegian government.
    Attending the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were Chen Zhenji, Xue Gongchuo, Fu Bingyou, Wu Tang and other responsible persons from relevant departments; on the Norwegian side was Helge Akre, the Norwegian Ambassador to China.

### 中挪签订文化交流计划

1966-05-02
第3版()
专栏：

    中挪签订文化交流计划
    新华社三十日讯　中华人民共和国和挪威王国政府一九六六年和一九六七年文化交流计划今天在北京签字。代表中国政府签字的，是对外文委副主任宋一平；代表挪威政府签字的，是挪威外交部文化司司长尔林·克里斯托费生。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有陈振基、薛公绰、傅兵友、吴塘等有关方面负责人；挪威方面有挪威驻华大使黑尔格·阿克雷。
