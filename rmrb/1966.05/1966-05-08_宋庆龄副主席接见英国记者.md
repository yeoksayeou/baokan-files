Title: Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling Receives British Journalists
Author: Zheng Xiaozhen
Date: 1966-05-08
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling received the British freelance journalist Mr. and Mrs. Guilder this afternoon and had a friendly conversation with them.

    Gong Peng, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, was present at the meeting.

    Right picture: Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling receives British freelance journalist Guilder and his wife.

### 宋庆龄副主席接见英国记者
郑小箴
1966-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄副主席接见英国记者
    新华社七日讯　宋庆龄副主席今天下午接见了英国自由投稿记者吉尔德夫妇，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有外交部部长助理龚澎。
    右图：宋庆龄副主席接见英国自由投稿记者吉尔德和夫人。
    新华社记者　郑小箴摄
