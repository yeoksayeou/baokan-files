Title: Nordic Four Countries' Journalists Leave Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1966-05-19
Edition: 5

    Nordic Four Countries' Journalists Leave Beijing for Home
    (Xinhua News Agency, May 18th) A group of eight journalists from Denmark, Finland, Norway, and Sweden concluded their visit to our country and left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.


**Named Entities**


丹麦	Denmark
芬兰	Finland
挪威	Norway
瑞典	Sweden
北京	Beijing
新华社	Xinhua News Agency

### 北欧四国新闻工作者离京回国

1966-05-19
第5版()
专栏：

    北欧四国新闻工作者离京回国
    新华社十八日讯　丹麦、芬兰、挪威和瑞典新闻工作者一行八人，结束了对我国的访问，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
