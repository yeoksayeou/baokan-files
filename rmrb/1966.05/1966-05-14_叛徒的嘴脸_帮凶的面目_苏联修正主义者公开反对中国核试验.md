Title: The Ugly Face of a Traitor, the Visage of an Accomplice: Soviet Revisionists Publicly Oppose China's Nuclear Tests
Author:
Date: 1966-05-14
Edition: 5

    The Ugly Face of a Traitor, the Visage of an Accomplice
    Soviet Revisionists Publicly Oppose China's Nuclear Tests
    Xinhua News Agency, 13th – Moscow Dispatch: In order to collude with the United States in monopolizing nuclear weapons, the Soviet revisionists have openly expressed their opposition to socialist China's nuclear tests.
    According to a report from Moscow by the Yugoslav News Agency, someone asked the spokesperson of the Soviet Foreign Ministry to comment on the news of China's new nuclear explosion. The spokesperson said, "We oppose nuclear tests, and we have proven this by signing the Moscow Treaty." He also said, "The Soviet Union's attitude towards similar issues is well-known."
    Soviet newspapers reported the press communique of China's third nuclear test in only one sentence. While simply publishing the news of China's nuclear test on the 11th, the Red Star also maliciously reported that "The Japan Socialist Party's statement expressed regret about the explosion."
    On the 13th, Komsomolskaya Pravda, in its "What the World's Newspapers are Writing" column on the front page, mentioned that foreign newspapers continued to comment on China's nuclear tests, quoting some Indian newspapers as saying, "This nuclear explosion has encouraged representatives of political groups in India who are demanding that India also develop nuclear weapons."

### 叛徒的嘴脸　帮凶的面目  苏联修正主义者公开反对中国核试验

1966-05-14
第5版()
专栏：

    叛徒的嘴脸　帮凶的面目
    苏联修正主义者公开反对中国核试验
    新华社十三日讯　莫斯科消息：苏联修正主义者为了伙同美国垄断核武器，公然表示反对社会主义中国的核试验。
    据南斯拉夫通讯社从莫斯科报道说，有人要求苏联外交部发言人评论中国进行新的核爆炸的消息。发言人说，“我们反对核试验，我们通过在莫斯科条约上签字证明了这一点”。他还说，“苏联对类似问题的态度是众所周知的”。
    苏联报纸只用一句话报道了中国第三次核试验的新闻公报。《红星报》十一日在简单地刊登中国核试验的消息的同时，还恶意地报道说，“日本社会党的声明就爆炸一事表示遗憾”。
    《共青团真理报》十三日在第一版“世界报纸写些什么”栏中谈到外国报纸继续评论中国的核试验时，引印度的一些报纸的话说，“这次核爆炸鼓励了印度的那些要求印度同样也要制造核武器的政治集团的代表。”
