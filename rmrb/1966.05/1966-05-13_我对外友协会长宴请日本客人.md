Title: President of Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries Hosts Banquet for Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-05-13
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, May 12th. Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations and Friendship with Foreign Countries, held a banquet this evening to entertain the Japanese publishing delegation headed by Seiichi Noma.
    The hosts and guests spoke at the banquet, unanimously expressing their desire to make new contributions to further strengthening cultural exchanges between China and Japan.
    Present at the banquet were Liu Baiyu, Luo Jun, Lin Lin, Wang Yechiu, and other responsible persons from relevant departments.

### 我对外友协会长宴请日本客人

1966-05-13
第6版()
专栏：

    我对外友协会长宴请日本客人
    新华社十二日讯  对外文化友好协会会长楚图南今天晚上举行宴会，招待以野间省一为首的日本出版代表团。
    宾主在宴会上讲了话，一致表示为进一步加强中日两国文化交流而作出新的贡献。
    出席宴会的有刘白羽、罗俊、林林、王冶秋等有关方面负责人。
