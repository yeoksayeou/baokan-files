Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Friends from Four Latin American Countries
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-07
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Vice Premier Chen Yi received friends from four Latin American countries who are currently visiting here this evening. They are: Floreal Cavallo, a famous Argentine ophthalmologist; Alfonso Ospina Echavarría, a famous Colombian lawyer; Miguel Tovar Silva, a Colombian congressman; the delegation of the Mexican Association for Friendship with China, led by Juan Larios Tolentino; and Carlos María Gutiérrez, editor of the Uruguayan "Adelante Weekly."
    Vice Premier Chen Yi had cordial and friendly conversations with these friends. Present at the reception were Chu Tunan, Ren Yinglun, and others.

### 陈毅副总理接见拉丁美洲四个国家的朋友

1966-05-07
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见拉丁美洲四个国家的朋友
    新华社六日讯　陈毅副总理今晚接见了正在这里访问的拉丁美洲四个国家的朋友。他们是：阿根廷著名眼科医生弗洛雷阿尔·卡瓦略，哥伦比亚著名律师阿方索·奥斯皮纳·埃切维利奥，哥伦比亚国会议员米格尔·托瓦尔·席尔瓦，由胡安·拉里奥斯·托伦蒂诺率领的墨西哥中国友好协会代表团，乌拉圭《前进周报》编辑卡洛斯·马里亚·古铁雷斯。
    陈毅副总理同这些朋友进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的有楚图南、任映仑等。
