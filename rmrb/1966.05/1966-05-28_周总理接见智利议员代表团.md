Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Chilean Parliamentary Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-28
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, May 27th – Premier Zhou Enlai received Carlos Sibolet Arcelareca, Second Vice President of the Chilean Chamber of Deputies, and the members of the Chilean parliamentary delegation led by him this afternoon. They held friendly talks. Present at the reception were Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Lian Guan, Vice President, and others.

### 周总理接见智利议员代表团

1966-05-28
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见智利议员代表团
    新华社二十七日讯  周恩来总理今天下午接见了智利众议院第二副议长卡洛斯·席博里·阿尔塞雷卡，和由他率领的智利议员代表团的成员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有外交学会会长张奚若、副会长连贯等。
