Title: U.S. Warship Intrudes into Territorial Waters of Guangdong Area; I Issue the Four Hundred and Second Serious Warning

Author: 
Date: 1966-05-27
Edition: 6

    U.S. Warship Intrudes into Territorial Waters of Guangdong Area
    I Issue the Four Hundred and Second Serious Warning
    Xinhua News Agency, 24th. On May 24, from 9:40 to 13:37, a U.S. warship intruded into the territorial waters of the Yongxing Island and Dong Island area of the Xisha Islands in Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. warship, the spokesperson of our Foreign Ministry was instructed to issue the four hundred and second serious warning.



**Named Entities**


Places:
广东 Guangdong
西沙群岛 Xisha Qundao (Paracel Islands)
永兴岛 Yongxing Dao (Woody Island)
东岛 Dong Dao (East Island)

Organizations:

### 美舰侵入我广东地区领海  我提出第四百零二次严重警告

1966-05-27
第6版()
专栏：

    美舰侵入我广东地区领海
    我提出第四百零二次严重警告
    新华社二十四日讯　五月二十四日九时四十分至十三时三十七分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛、东岛地区领海。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百零二次严重警告。
