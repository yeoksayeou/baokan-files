Title: Liao Chengzhi Hosts a Banquet for Japanese Guests
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-28
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 27th – Liao Chengzhi, President of the China-Japan Friendship Association, hosted a banquet tonight to entertain the entire delegation of Japanese experts on Chinese Art History, headed by Hakushiba Bon.
    Among those attending the banquet were Wang Xiaoyun, Liu Kaiqu, Lin Lin, Hua Junwu, Wang Yuanfang, Zhang E, and others.
    Japanese peace activist in Beijing, Kinkazu Saionji, was also invited to attend.

### 廖承志会长宴请日本客人

1966-05-28
第5版()
专栏：

    廖承志会长宴请日本客人
    新华社二十七日讯　中日友协会长廖承志今晚设宴招待以白石凡为首的日本中国美术史研究家代表团全体成员。
    出席宴会的，有王晓云、刘开渠、林林、华君武、王元方、张谔等。
    在京日本和平人士西园寺公一也应邀出席。
