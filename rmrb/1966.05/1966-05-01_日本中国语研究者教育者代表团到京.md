Title: Japanese Delegation of Chinese Language Researchers and Educators Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-01
Edition: 6

    (Xinhua News Agency, 29th) A Japanese delegation of Chinese language researchers and educators, led by Dr. Akiharu Todo, professor at the University of Tokyo, consisting of five members, arrived in Beijing by plane today for an academic visit at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Department of Philosophy and Social Sciences of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

**Named Entities**

东京大学	Dongjing Daxue
藤堂明保	Todo Akiharu
中国人民对外文化友好协会	Zhongguo Renmin Duiwai Wenhua Youhao Xiehui
中国科学院哲学社会科学部	Zhongguo Kexueyuan Zhexue Shehui Kexue Bu
北京	Beijing
新华社	Xinhua News Agency

### 日本中国语研究者教育者代表团到京

1966-05-01
第6版()
专栏：

    日本中国语研究者教育者代表团到京
    据新华社二十九日讯　以东京大学教授藤堂明保博士为首的日本中国语研究者教育者代表团一行五人，应中国人民对外文化友好协会和中国科学院哲学社会科学部邀请来我国进行学术访问，今天乘飞机到达北京。
