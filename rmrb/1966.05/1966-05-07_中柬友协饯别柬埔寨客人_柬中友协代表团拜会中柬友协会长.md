Title: China-Cambodia Friendship Association Holds Farewell Banquet for Cambodian Guests; Delegation of the Cambodia-China Friendship Association Pays Courtesy Call to President of the China-Cambodia Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1966-05-07
Edition: 5

    According to Xinhua News Agency, May 6th, the China-Cambodia Friendship Association held a banquet this evening to bid farewell to the delegation of the Cambodia-China Friendship Association led by Lan Neth, President of the Cambodia-China Friendship Association.

    Zhang Hanfu, President of the China-Cambodia Friendship Association, and Lan Neth spoke at the banquet.

    Those present at the banquet this evening included Ding Xilin, Zhou Yiping, Wang Demao, Ren Yinglun, Shen Zijiu, Ju Zan, etc.

    Prince Naradipo, who is studying in Beijing, and Li Jun, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Cambodian Embassy in China, and his wife, were also invited to attend the banquet.

    According to Xinhua News Agency, May 6th, Lan Neth, President of the Cambodia-China Friendship Association and head of the delegation of the Cambodia-China Friendship Association, and other members of the delegation of the Cambodia-China Friendship Association, paid a courtesy call to Zhang Hanfu, President of the China-Cambodia Friendship Association, this afternoon. The two sides had a cordial and friendly conversation. Present at the meeting were Zhang Qian, Vice President of the China-Cambodia Friendship Association, Zhou Yiping, and Ding Xilin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, etc.

### 中柬友协饯别柬埔寨客人  柬中友协代表团拜会中柬友协会长

1966-05-07
第5版()
专栏：

    中柬友协饯别柬埔寨客人
    柬中友协代表团拜会中柬友协会长
    据新华社六日讯　中柬友好协会今晚举行宴会，欢送由柬中友好协会主席兰·涅特率领的柬中友好协会代表团。
    中柬友好协会会长章汉夫和兰·涅特在宴会上讲话。
    出席今晚宴会的有丁西林、周一萍、王德茂、任映仑、沈兹九、巨赞等。
    在北京学习的纳拉迪波王子，柬埔寨驻中国大使馆临时代办李军和夫人，也应邀出席了宴会。
    新华社六日讯　柬中友好协会主席、柬中友好协会代表团团长兰·涅特和柬中友好协会代表团其他成员，今天下午拜会了中柬友好协会会长章汉夫。双方进行了亲切友好的谈话。拜会时在座的有中柬友好协会副会长张茜、周一萍及中国人民对外文化友好协会副会长丁西林等。
