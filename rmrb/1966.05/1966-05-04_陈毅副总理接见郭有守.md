Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Guo Youshou
Author:
Date: 1966-05-04
Edition: 4

    Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Guo Youshou
    Vice Premier Chen Yi pointed out that members of the Chiang Kai-shek clique should follow Guo Youshou's actions, suddenly awaken to the truth, and return to the embrace of the motherland. This is their only way out.
    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd—Vice Premier Chen Yi received Guo Youshou, the former "Counselor" of the Chiang Kai-shek clique's "Embassy" in Belgium, and his wife this morning, and had a cordial conversation with them.
    Vice Premier Chen Yi praised Guo Youshou's patriotic action of resolutely breaking away from the Chiang gang and returning to the motherland. He also encouraged him to study Chairman Mao's works hard, stand with the broad masses of the people of the motherland, and contribute to the cause of socialist construction. Vice Premier Chen Yi pointed out that members of the Chiang Kai-shek clique should follow Guo Youshou's actions, suddenly awaken to the truth, and return to the embrace of the motherland. They will be welcomed by the people of the motherland, and this is their only way out. (Attached picture)

### 陈毅副总理接见郭有守

1966-05-04
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见郭有守
    陈毅副总理指出，蒋介石集团人员应效法郭有守的行动，幡然悔悟，回到祖国怀抱，这是他们唯一的出路。
    新华社三日讯　陈毅副总理今日上午接见了起义返国的前台湾蒋介石集团驻比利时“大使馆参事”郭有守和他的夫人，同他们进行了亲切的谈话。
    陈毅副总理对郭有守毅然脱离蒋帮起义返国的爱国行动表示嘉许，并勉励他努力学习毛主席著作，同祖国广大的人民站在一起，为社会主义建设事业贡献力量。陈毅副总理指出，蒋介石集团的人员应该效法郭有守的行动，幡然悔悟，回到祖国的怀抱，他们将受到祖国人民的欢迎，这是他们唯一的出路。（附图片）
