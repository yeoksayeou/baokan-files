Title: Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman, Hosts Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-05-12
Edition: 5

    Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Honorary President of the China-Japan Friendship Association, received and hosted a banquet this afternoon for the Japanese publishing delegation led by Noma Shoichi, Chairman of the Japan Book Publishers Association. Vice Chairman Guo Moruo had a cordial and friendly conversation with the Japanese friends. Present at the reception and banquet were Liao Chengzhi, Chu Tunan, Zhao Anbo, Lin Lin, and others.

### 郭沫若副委员长宴请日本客人

1966-05-12
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长宴请日本客人
    新华社十一日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、中日友协名誉会长郭沫若，今天下午接见并宴请日本书籍出版协会会长野间省一率领的日本出版代表团。郭沫若副委员长同日本朋友进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见和宴请时在座的，有廖承志、楚图南、赵安博、林林等。
