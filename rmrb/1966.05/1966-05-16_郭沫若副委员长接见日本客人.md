Title: Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman, Receives Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-05-16
Edition: 6

    Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Honorary President of the Sino-Japanese Friendship Association, received Kenzo Matsumura, advisor to the Japanese Liberal Democratic Party, and his entourage this morning, who are visiting our country for friendly exchanges. They had a friendly conversation.
    Wang Xiaoyun, Yu Liqun, and others were present at the reception.
    Xinhua News Agency, 14th - Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received all members of the Japanese Local Autonomy Friendship Delegation led by Daisuke Kawaguchi this evening, and had a friendly conversation with them.
    Wang Yinpu, Zhao Anbo, and others were present at the reception.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人

1966-05-16
第6版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人
    新华社十五日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、中日友协名誉会长郭沫若，今天上午接见了前来我国进行友好访问的日本自由民主党顾问松村谦三和他的随行人员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有王晓云、于立群等。
    新华社十四日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若今天晚上接见了由川口大助率领的日本地方自治友好代表团全体成员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有王荫圃、赵安博等。
