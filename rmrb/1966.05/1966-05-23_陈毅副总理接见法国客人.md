Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives French Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-05-23
Edition: 6

    Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives French Guests
    Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd: Vice Premier of the State Council, Chen Yi, received all members of the delegation from the French National Students' Union this afternoon and had friendly talks with them. Present at the reception were Wang Zhaohua, Xu Kui, and others. (Attached picture)
    May 22nd, Vice Premier Chen Yi received all members of the delegation from the French National Students' Union. From right: Michel Rostain, Marc Kravetz, Jean-Louis Peninou, Jean-Yves Hocquard, Alain Klondeyk.
                                    Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian

### 陈毅副总理接见法国客人

1966-05-23
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见法国客人
    新华社二十二日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今天下午接见了法国全国学生联合会代表团全体成员，和他们进行了友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有王照华、徐葵等。（附图片）
    五月二十二日，陈毅副总理接见法国全国学生联合会代表团全体成员。右起：米歇尔·罗斯坦、马尔克·克拉维茨、让·路易·佩尼努、让·约克·奥卡尔、阿兰·克隆佩克。
                                    新华社记者　杜修贤摄
