Title: Somali Radio Praises China's Timely Provision of Medical Aid; Chinese Medical Team Serves Somali People Sincerely
Author:
Date: 1966-05-09
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Mogadishu Electric Telegram: Somalia's capital, Mogadishu Radio, broadcast an editorial by the National Broadcasting Corporation in Somali on April 26, praising the Chinese medical team working in Somalia for sincerely serving the Somali people.

    The editorial said: "Today, whenever you visit our hospitals, you will find people rushing to the Chinese doctors' clinics. Why? The reason is simple: Somali people have great trust in the Chinese doctors' sincere attitude and medical skills."

    The editorial said: "The Somali government feels an urgent need to improve the health of the Somali people and calls on friendly countries to provide medical assistance. China quickly sent a medical team to Somalia. At that time, it was the dry season, the time when our country needed doctors most, and the medical team arrived very timely. China not only sent doctors but also transported medicines and medical equipment."

    The editorial praised the Chinese medical team's medical skills, which cured many Somali people. The editorial said that Somalia thanks the Chinese government and people for sending a medical team here, and we will never forget this medical team. Recently, many Somali people who have been cured by Chinese doctors have written letters to local newspapers expressing their gratitude to the Chinese medical team.

### 索马里电台赞扬我国及时提供医疗援助  中国医疗队真诚地为索马里人民服务

1966-05-09
第5版()
专栏：

    索马里电台赞扬我国及时提供医疗援助
    中国医疗队真诚地为索马里人民服务
    新华社摩加迪沙电　索马里首都摩加迪沙电台四月二十六日用索马里语广播了国家广播公司的一篇社论，赞扬在索马里工作的中国医疗队真诚地为索马里人民服务。
    社论说：今天你不论什么时候到我们的医院去参观，你都会发现群众往中国医生的诊疗室跑。为什么呢？原因很简单，索马里人对中国医生的真诚态度和医疗技术非常信任。
    社论说：索马里政府感到迫切需要改善索马里人民的健康情况，呼吁友好国家提供医疗援助。中国很快地就给索马里派来了一个医疗队。那时正是干旱的季节，是我国最需要医生的时候，医疗队来得非常及时。中国不仅派来了医生，还运来了药品和医疗器械。
    社论赞扬中国医疗队的医疗技术，给很多索马里人治好了病。社论说，索马里感谢中国政府和人民派一支医疗队来这里，我们永远忘不了这支医疗队。最近，有许多曾经被中国医生治好了病的索马里人给当地的报纸写信表示感谢中国医疗队。
