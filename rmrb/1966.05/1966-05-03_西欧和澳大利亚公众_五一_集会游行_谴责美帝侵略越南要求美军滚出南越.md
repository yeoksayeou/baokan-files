Title: Western European and Australian Public Gatherings and Marches on "May Day" Condemn American Imperialism's Aggression Against Vietnam and Demand American Troops Get Out of South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-05-03
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, London, May 1st, tens of thousands of people from all walks of life in London and Glasgow held marches and rallies on the 1st to celebrate "May Day" International Labor Day. The marching crowds protested against American aggression in Vietnam and opposed the Labor Party government's economic policies that cut into the interests of the working people. More than three thousand people in London held signs during the march that read: "American troops get out of Vietnam."
    In Glasgow, more than eight thousand people held a "May Day" march, demanding an end to American aggression in Vietnam and the withdrawal of American troops.
    According to Xinhua News Agency, Melbourne, May 2nd, two thousand people held a "May Day" march in Melbourne on the 1st, condemning American aggression against Vietnam, demanding the withdrawal of Australian troops from South Vietnam, and demanding that the Australian government no longer slavishly follow the United States.
    The marchers condemned American policy towards Vietnam. Many signs read: "We don't want to be American puppets!"
    After the march, a rally of three thousand people was held, where speakers unanimously condemned American aggression against Vietnam and demanded Australia's withdrawal from South Vietnam.
    In Sydney, two thousand people held a march on the 1st against the war of aggression against Vietnam, demanding the withdrawal of Australian troops from South Vietnam and opposing conscription.
    According to Xinhua News Agency dispatch on the 1st, three thousand people from all walks of life in Sweden held a march and rally in Stockholm on the 1st to celebrate "May Day" International Labor Day. The marching crowds strongly protested against American aggression against Vietnam and demanded that the United States get out of South Vietnam.
    The marching crowds held high the flag of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front and signs supporting the Vietnamese people's struggle against the United States. The signs read: "Support the South Vietnam National Liberation Front," "Oppose American aggression against Vietnam," "The United States must withdraw from Vietnam."
    It is reported that the marching crowds also demonstrated in front of the American Embassy, shouting "America—murderer." The angry crowd tore down and burned the American Stars and Stripes hanging in front of the embassy.
    It is also reported that the masses in Norway, Denmark, and Finland also held "May Day" celebrations.
    According to Xinhua News Agency, Paris, May 2nd, on "May Day," workers in many French cities participated in demonstrations, demanding better living and working conditions and expressing support for the Vietnamese people.
    Thousands of Parisians gathered in front of the Paris Labor Exchange on the 1st. They condemned the crimes committed by the Americans in Vietnam, chanting in unison: America is a murderer. In Lyon, thousands of people participated in rallies and marches. Demonstrators shouted: "Johnson is a murderer!" Mass rallies were also held in Clermont-Ferrand, Marseille, Nice and other cities.

### 西欧和澳大利亚公众“五一”集会游行  谴责美帝侵略越南要求美军滚出南越

1966-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

    西欧和澳大利亚公众“五一”集会游行
    谴责美帝侵略越南要求美军滚出南越
    据新华社伦敦一日电　伦敦和格拉斯哥各界公众上万人一日举行游行和集会庆祝“五一”国际劳动节。游行群众抗议美国侵略越南，同时反对工党政府向劳动人民开刀的经济政策。伦敦公众三千多人在游行时拿着标语牌，上面写着：“美国军队从越南滚出去”。
    在格拉斯哥，八千多人举行“五一”游行，要求制止美国侵略越南，撤走美国军队。
    据新华社墨尔本二日电墨尔本一日有两千人举行“五一”节游行，谴责美国侵略越南，要求把澳大利亚军队撤出南越，要求澳大利亚政府不再奴颜卑膝地追随美国。
    游行者谴责美国对越南的政策。许多标语牌上写着：“我们不愿做美国的傀儡！”
    游行后，举行了有三千人参加的大会，在会上讲话的人一致谴责美国侵略越南，要求澳大利亚撤出南越。
    在悉尼，一日有两千人举行了反对侵越战争，要求澳大利亚军队撤出南越和反征兵的游行。
    据新华社一日讯　瑞典各界三千人一日在斯德哥尔摩举行游行和集会，庆祝“五一”国际劳动节。游行群众强烈抗议美国侵略越南，要求美国从南越滚出去。
    游行群众高举越南南方民族解放阵线的旗帜和支持越南人民反美斗争的标语牌。标语牌上写着：“支援越南南方民族解放阵线”，“反对美国侵略越南”，“美国必须从越南撤走”。
    据报道，游行群众还到美国大使馆门前示威，他们高呼“美国——杀人犯”。愤怒的群众扯下并且烧毁了挂在大使馆前面的美国星条旗。
    另据报道，挪威、丹麦、芬兰的群众也举行了“五一”庆祝活动。
    据新华社巴黎二日电　在“五一”节，法国许多城市的工人参加了示威游行，要求改善生活和劳动条件，并且表示支持越南人民。
    数以千计的巴黎人一日在巴黎劳动介绍所门前举行集会。他们谴责美国人在越南犯下的罪行，齐声高喊：美国是杀人犯。在里昂，几千人参加了集会和游行。示威者大声高呼：“约翰逊是杀人犯！”在克勒蒙菲朗、马赛、尼斯和其他城市也举行了群众集会。
