Title: Albanian Newspapers Hail Sino-Albanian Joint Statement Strengthening Friendship Between the Two Countries. Mao Zedong Thought is the Source of China's Great Victories. The Albanian and Chinese Parties are Determined to Carry the Socialist Revolution Through to the End.
Author:
Date: 1966-05-22
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 18th - The Albanian newspaper *Bashkimi* published an editorial on the 17th stating, "The successful conclusion of the Albanian Party and Government delegation's visit to China and the signing of the Sino-Albanian Joint Statement constitute a new stage in the history of our two countries, making a very important contribution to further strengthening our eternal friendship, fraternal cooperation, and sincere relations."

    The editorial pointed out that the Sino-Albanian Joint Statement is a new milestone in our great and profound friendship and a very important Marxist-Leninist document embodying the complete agreement between the two sides on the views and positions on the issues discussed, reflecting the fighting cooperation between our two parties, the people of our two countries.

    The editorial said, "The Sino-Albanian Joint Statement inspires the people of our two countries to advance shoulder to shoulder under the glorious leadership of our two parties, headed by Comrade Mao Zedong and Comrade Enver Hoxha. In the current international situation, it arms the people of our two countries, enabling them to forever stand at the forefront of the revolution, and together with the revolutionary people of other countries, crush our most barbaric enemies: the imperialists, modern revisionists, and all other reactionaries."

    The editorial said that under the current conditions, when the Khrushchev revisionists have usurped the leadership of the Party and state in the Soviet Union and have gone far down the road of restoring capitalism, surrendering to imperialism, and promoting great-power chauvinism, the main task of the Marxist-Leninists is to carry the struggle against modern revisionism through to the end without hesitation, to draw a clear line with them on political, ideological, and organizational issues, to continue the polemic, and to oppose so-called "united action."

    The editorial pointed out, "Our Party emphasizes that throughout the entire historical period of socialist society, there is still a danger of revisionism and capitalist restoration in socialist countries. As the declaration strongly proposes, it is necessary to further strengthen the proletarian dictatorship and class struggle, adhere to the revolutionary line of Marxism-Leninism, the class education of revolutionary traditions, and highlight proletarian politics."

    The editorial concluded, "Our people unanimously approve and sincerely congratulate the Sino-Albanian Joint Statement. It further strengthens our great and unbreakable friendship, raises the revolutionary enthusiasm of the masses of working people in our country to a higher level, and enables the people of our country to hold high the banner of socialist victory. The statement is a powerful pillar of our common struggle to move forward shoulder to shoulder and build a new world without imperialism, without capitalism, and without revisionism."

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 20th - The Albanian newspaper *Zëri i Popullit* published an editorial on the 20th stating, "The Sino-Albanian Joint Statement is a powerful expression of the great socialist China and Albania's loyalty to Marxism-Leninism, proletarian internationalism, and the eternal friendship between the two countries."

    This editorial, titled "A Great Document of Loyalty to Marxism-Leninism and Eternal Friendship," said that the Albanian people have a special love for the great Chinese people and the Communist Party of China, headed by Chairman Mao Zedong. They congratulate the great victories of the fraternal Chinese people and regard these victories as their own. These victories are an example that inspires the revolutionary people of all countries. The source of these victories lies in the unwavering principled policy of the Communist Party of China, in the great thought of Chairman Mao Zedong, and in the great strength of the Chinese people. The editorial pointed out that "the Sino-Albanian Joint Statement is a new contribution from the people and parties of both countries towards defending the purity of Marxism-Leninism, defending the revolutionary cause of the international proletariat, and combining the truths of Marxism-Leninism with the practice of the revolutionary struggles of the international working class and the people of all countries".

    The editorial said, "The history of our two parties shows that arming the masses of working people with the revolutionary truth of Marxism-Leninism, raising their political consciousness, and enabling them to master the theory of Marxism-Leninism are necessary conditions for embarking on the road to socialism."

    The editorial emphasized that in order to prevent revisionism and avoid capitalist restoration, and in order to build socialism and communism, it is necessary to implement the revolutionary line of Marxism-Leninism, to implement the dictatorship of the proletariat, and to carry the socialist revolution through to the end on the economic, political, military, ideological, and cultural fronts.

    The editorial said, "As Comrade Mao Zedong said, the East Wind is prevailing over the West Wind. The struggle of the South Vietnamese people shows that U.S. imperialism is a paper tiger, a colossus with feet of clay. The U.S. imperialists and Soviet revisionists collude with each other to plot conspiracies and schemes, attempting to extinguish the revolutionary tide and to force the South Vietnamese people to submit. But all their efforts will be met with a shameful defeat."

    The editorial pointed out that at present, modern revisionists have become the "fire brigade" of imperialism. Therefore, the struggle against imperialism, headed by the United States, and its lackeys, and the struggle against modern revisionism, centered on the Soviet Communist Party leadership group, are inseparable tasks.

### 阿尔巴尼亚报纸欢呼阿中联合声明加强了两国友谊  毛泽东思想是中国取得伟大胜利的源泉  阿中两党坚决把社会主义革命进行到底

1966-05-22
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚报纸欢呼阿中联合声明加强了两国友谊
    毛泽东思想是中国取得伟大胜利的源泉  阿中两党坚决把社会主义革命进行到底
    新华社地拉那十八日电　阿尔巴尼亚《团结报》十七日发表社论说，“阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团访华圆满结束和阿中联合声明的签订，在我们两国的历史上构成了一个新的阶段，对进一步加强我们的永恒友谊、兄弟合作和真诚关系作出了十分重要的贡献。”
    社论指出，阿中联合声明是我们伟大和深厚友谊的新的里程碑，是体现我们两党、两国人民和两国战斗合作的十分重要的马克思列宁主义文件，表现了双方对所讨论的问题的观点和立场的完全一致。
    社论说，“阿中联合声明鼓舞着我们两国人民在以毛泽东同志和恩维尔·霍查同志为首的我们两党的光辉领导下并肩前进。在当前的国际形势下，它武装了我们两国人民，使他们永远站在革命的前线，同其他各国革命人民一起粉碎我们最野蛮的敌人：帝国主义者、现代修正主义者和一切其他反动派”。
    社论说，在目前条件下，当赫鲁晓夫修正主义者在苏联篡夺了党和国家的领导权，在恢复资本主义、投降帝国主义和推进大国沙文主义的道路上走得很远的时候，马克思列宁主义者的主要任务是把反对现代修正主义的斗争毫不犹豫地进行到底，在政治、思想和组织问题上与他们划清界线，继续论战，反对搞所谓“联合行动”。
    社论指出，“我们党强调指出，在社会主义社会的整个历史时期，在社会主义国家中还存在着产生修正主义和恢复资本主义的危险，就象声明所大力提出的那样，有必要进一步加强无产阶级专政和阶级斗争，坚持马克思列宁主义的革命路线、革命传统的阶级教育，突出无产阶级政治。”
    社论最后说，“我国人民一致赞同和衷心祝贺阿中联合声明。它进一步加强了我们的伟大和牢不可破的友谊，更高地提高了我国劳动人民群众的革命热潮，使我国人民更高地举起社会主义的胜利旗帜。声明是我们肩并肩地永往直前、建立一个没有帝国主义、没有资本主义、没有修正主义的新世界的共同斗争的强大支柱”。
    新华社地拉那二十日电　阿尔巴尼亚《劳动报》二十日发表社论说，“阿中联合声明是伟大的社会主义中国和阿尔巴尼亚忠实于马克思列宁主义、忠实于无产阶级国际主义和两国之间永恒友谊的强大表现。”
    这篇题为《忠实于马克思列宁主义和永恒友谊的伟大文件》的社论说，阿尔巴尼亚人民对伟大的中国人民、对以毛泽东主席为首的中国共产党怀有特别的热爱，他们祝贺兄弟中国人民的伟大胜利，并把这些胜利看作是自己的胜利。这些胜利是鼓舞世界各国革命人民的榜样。这些胜利的源泉在于中国共产党坚持不渝的原则性政策，在于毛泽东主席的伟大思想，在于伟大中国人民的巨大力量。社论指出，“阿中联合声明是我们两国人民和两党对保卫马克思列宁主义的纯洁性、保卫国际无产阶级的革命事业、把马克思列宁主义的真理同国际工人阶级和世界各国人民革命斗争的实践相结合的新的贡献”。
    社论说，“我们两党的历史表明，以马克思列宁主义的革命真理武装劳动人民群众，提高他们的政治觉悟，使他们掌握马克思列宁主义的理论，是走向社会主义道路的必要条件”。
    社论强调指出，为了防止修正主义和避免资本主义复辟，为了建设社会主义和共产主义，必须执行马克思列宁主义的革命路线，必须实行无产阶级专政，必须在经济、政治、军事、思想和文化战线上把社会主义革命进行到底。
    社论说，“正如毛泽东同志所说，是东风压倒西风。南越人民的斗争表明，美帝国主义是纸老虎，是泥足巨人。美帝国主义者和苏联修正主义者相互勾结策划阴谋和诡计，妄图扑灭革命浪潮，妄图使南越人民屈服。但是他们的一切努力必将遭到可耻的失败”。
    社论指出，目前，现代修正主义者成了帝国主义的“消防队”。因此，反对以美国为首的帝国主义及其仆从的斗争和反对以苏共领导集团为中心的现代修正主义的斗争是不可分割的任务。
