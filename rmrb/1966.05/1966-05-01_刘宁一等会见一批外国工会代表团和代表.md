Title: Liu Ningyi and Others Meet with a Group of Foreign Trade Union Delegations and Representatives
Author:
Date: 1966-05-01
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Vice-Chairmen Chen Shaomin, Ma Chungu, and Kang Yonghe, Secretariat Secretaries Di Zicai, Wang Zhijie, and Chen Yu, and Alternate Secretariat Secretary Shao Jingwa today met with a group of foreign trade union delegations and representatives who were invited to China to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations.
    These foreign trade union delegations and representatives are: the Albanian Trade Union Delegation headed by Petrit Ajkemejxhiu, the Bulgarian Trade Union Delegation headed by S. Petkov, the Hungarian Trade Union Delegation headed by Bartos István, the Free German Trade Union Federation Delegation headed by Werner Herrmann, the Korean Trade Union Delegation headed by Kim Kuk-sam, the Mongolian Trade Union Delegation headed by O. Shariv, Stefan Pastusiak, Member of the Central Council of Polish Trade Unions and Chairman of the Wroclaw Provincial Trade Union, the Soviet Trade Union Delegation headed by G. P. Sofonov, the Czechoslovak Trade Union Delegation headed by Jaroslav Gosner, the South Vietnam Liberation Trade Union Federation Delegation headed by Tran Hoai Nam, the Ceylon Federation of Trade Unions Delegation headed by T. N. Nadongge, the Japanese General Council of Trade Unions Delegation headed by Kitagawa Yoshiyuki, the Japanese Liaison Council of Neutral Trade Unions Delegation headed by Onihinata Michihiko, the Japan-China Friendship Association Workers Delegation headed by Uesugi Yoshio, the All-Japan Day Laborers' Union Delegation headed by Chayama Takeji, the Arab Trade Union Confederation Delegation headed by Abbas Mahmoud, the Trade Union Confederation of the Congo (Brazzaville) Delegation headed by Baisoko Joachim, the Moroccan Labour Union Delegation headed by Mellini Ali, and the Labour Union Federation of the United Arab Republic Delegation headed by Abdul Moneim Mansour.
    Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Vice-Chairmen Zhu Xuefan, Ma Chungu, and Kang Yonghe, and Secretariat Secretary Di Zicai, met on the 29th with the Ceylon Independent Trade Union Federation Delegation headed by Soesa, the Ceylon Independent Mercantile Union Delegation headed by K. M. Punchi Bandara, H. P. Liyanage, General Secretary of the Ceylon National Union of Machine and General Workers, the Syrian Workers' Trade Union Federation Delegation headed by Maruan Sala, the General Union of Algerian Workers Delegation headed by Mejaher Abdelkader, the National Confederation of Workers of Guinea Delegation headed by Louis Behanzin, the All-Tanganyika Federation of Labour Delegation headed by Joseph Clements Rwegasira, the Zanzibar Afro-Shirazi Party Workers' Department Delegation headed by Ali Mndahoma, Bernard Kinini Gumede, Deputy Secretary of the Zimbabwe African Congress of Trade Unions Textile Workers Union, and the United Workers' Center of Venezuela Delegation headed by Horacio Scott Power, who were invited to China to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations. On April 30th, the Ministry of Culture and the China-Albania Friendship Association held a literary and art soiree to welcome the Albanian Party and Government Delegation headed by Chairman Shehu.

### 刘宁一等会见一批外国工会代表团和代表

1966-05-01
第5版()
专栏：

    刘宁一等会见一批外国工会代表团和代表
    新华社三十日讯　中华全国总工会主席刘宁一，副主席陈少敏、马纯古、康永和，书记处书记狄子才、王志杰、陈宇，书记处候补书记邵井蛙，今天分别会见了应邀来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝活动的一批外国工会代表团和代表。
    这些外国工会代表团和代表是：以佩特利特·艾克梅克奇乌为首的阿尔巴尼亚工会代表团，以斯·彼得科夫为首的保加利亚工会代表团，以巴尔托斯·伊斯特万为首的匈牙利工会代表团，以维纳·海尔曼为首的自由德国工会联合会代表团，以金国三为首的朝鲜工会代表团，以奥·沙日夫为首的蒙古工会代表团，波兰工会中央理事会理事、弗罗茨瓦夫省工会主席斯特凡·帕斯图西亚克，以格·帕·索方洛夫为首的苏联工会代表团，以雅罗斯拉夫·高斯纳尔为首的捷克斯洛伐克工会代表团，以陈怀南为首的越南南方解放工会联合会代表团，以蒂·恩·纳东格为首的锡兰工会联合会代表团，以北川义行为首的日本工会总评议会代表团，以大日向道彦为首的日本中立工会联络会议代表团，以上杉芳夫为首的日中友好协会工人代表团，以茶山他家司为首的全日本散工工会代表团，以阿巴斯·马哈茂德为首的阿拉伯工会联合会代表团，以北索科·约阿基姆为首的刚果（布）工会联合会代表团，以梅利尼·阿里为首的摩洛哥劳工联合会代表团，以阿卜杜勒·莫内姆·曼苏尔为首的阿拉伯联合共和国劳工联合会代表团。
    新华社三十日讯　中华全国总工会主席刘宁一、副主席朱学范、马纯古、康永和，书记处书记狄子才，二十九日分别会见了应邀前来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝活动的以索埃沙为首的锡兰独立工会联合会代表团，以克·莫·庞奇·班达拉为首的锡兰独立工商工会代表团，锡兰全国机器和普通工人工会总书记赫·普·李亚纳格，以马鲁昂·萨拉为首的叙利亚工人工会总联合会代表团，以梅贾赫尔·阿卜杜勒卡达尔为首的阿尔及利亚工人总联合会代表团，以路易·贝昂赞为首的几内亚全国工人联合会代表团，以约瑟夫·克莱门斯·拉威格西拉为首的全坦噶尼喀工人联合会代表团，以阿里·姆达霍马为首的桑给巴尔非洲设拉子党工人部代表团，津巴布韦非洲人工会大会纺织工人工会副书记贝纳德·基尼尼·古麦迪，以奥拉西奥·斯科特·鲍维尔为首的委内瑞拉工人统一中央工会代表团。四月三十日，文化部和中国阿尔巴尼亚友好协会举行文艺晚会，欢迎由谢胡主席率领的阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团。
