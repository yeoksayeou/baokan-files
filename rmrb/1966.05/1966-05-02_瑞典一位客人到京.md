Title: A Swedish Guest Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-05-02
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Dr. Yu Bo, Curator of the Museum of Far Eastern Antiquities in Sweden, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 29th, having been invited to participate in the "May 1st" International Labor Day celebrations and to pay a friendly visit to our country.



**Named Entities**


瑞典    Ruìdiǎn (Sweden)
远东古物博物馆  Yuǎndōng Gǔwù Bówùguǎn (Museum of Far Eastern Antiquities)
俞博  Yú Bó (Yu Bo)
北京  Běijīng (Beijing)
五一国际劳动节  Wǔyī Guójì Láodòng Jié (May 1st International Labor Day)
