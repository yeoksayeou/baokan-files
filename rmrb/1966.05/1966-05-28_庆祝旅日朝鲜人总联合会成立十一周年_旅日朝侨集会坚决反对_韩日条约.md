Title: Celebrating the 11th Anniversary of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan: Korean Residents in Japan Rally to Resolutely Oppose the "Japan-ROK Treaty"
Author:
Date: 1966-05-28
Edition: 6

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, May 26th: Approximately 3,000 Korean residents in Japan held a rally in Tokyo on the 25th to warmly celebrate the 11th anniversary of the establishment of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan. Invited to attend the celebration were: Miyahara Akira, member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, Hatanaka Masaharu, Director-General of the Japan-Korea Association, Nakajima Kenzō, representative of Japanese cultural circles, as well as representatives from the Japanese Socialist Party, the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan (Sohyo), and women's circles.
    Gan Wenfang, representative of the Chinese residing in Japan, was also invited to attend the celebration.
    Han Tok-su, Chairman of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan, said at the meeting that for eleven years, the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan has firmly supported the policies of the Workers' Party of Korea and the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, striving to make the U.S. imperialism get out of South Korea, to smash the rule of the South Korean puppet group, to resolutely oppose the "Japan-ROK Treaty" aimed at establishing a Northeast Asian military alliance, and for the peaceful reunification of the motherland.
    Han Tok-su called on the Korean residents in Japan to further strengthen unity, strengthen friendly relations with the Japanese people, and oppose the aggressive policies of U.S. imperialism. The meeting passed a letter of thanks to Premier Kim Il-sung amidst warm applause. The meeting also read out congratulatory messages from various organizations of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and Chinese people's organizations.

### 庆祝旅日朝鲜人总联合会成立十一周年  旅日朝侨集会坚决反对“韩日条约”

1966-05-28
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝旅日朝鲜人总联合会成立十一周年
    旅日朝侨集会坚决反对“韩日条约”
    据新华社东京二十六日电  旅日朝鲜侨民约三千人二十五日在东京举行集会，热烈庆祝旅日朝鲜人总联合会成立十一周年。应邀出席庆祝会的有：日本共产党中央委员会政治局委员米原昶，日朝协会理事长畑中政春，日本文化界代表中岛健藏以及日本社会党、日本工会总评议会和妇女界的代表。
    旅日华侨代表甘文芳也应邀出席了庆祝会。
    旅日朝鲜人总联合会会长韩德铢在会上说，十一年来，旅日朝鲜人总联合会，坚决支持朝鲜劳动党和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府的方针，为使美帝国主义从南朝鲜滚出去，为粉碎南朝鲜傀儡集团的统治，为坚决反对旨在建立东北亚军事联盟的“韩日条约”，为了祖国的和平统一，进行了斗争。
    韩德铢号召旅日朝侨进一步加强团结，加强同日本人民的友好关系，反对美帝国主义的侵略政策。会上在热烈的掌声中通过了致金日成首相的感谢信。会上还宣读了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的各团体和中国各人民团体打来的贺电。
