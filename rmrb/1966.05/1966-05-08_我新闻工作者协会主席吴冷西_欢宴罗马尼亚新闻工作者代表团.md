Title: Chairman Wu Lengxi of the All-China Journalists Association Hosts a Banquet for the Romanian Journalists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-05-08
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Wu Lengxi, Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, held a banquet this evening to welcome the Romanian Journalists Delegation led by Nestor Ignat, Chairman of the Romanian Journalists Association.
    In his speech, Wu Lengxi pointed out that the frequent exchanges between Chinese and Romanian journalists reflect the further development of the friendly relations and friendship between the two countries. He said that this visit of the Romanian Journalists Delegation to China will surely contribute to strengthening the friendly cooperation between the people and journalists of China and Romania.
    Nestor Ignat said that the friendship and ties existing between Romania and China are developing. The Romanian Journalists Delegation will introduce to the Romanian people the achievements made by the Chinese people under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and will make its own contribution to strengthening the friendship and cooperation between the people of Romania and China.
    Attending the banquet were Fan Jin, Wang Yi, Deng Gang, Sun Xiaocun, Bi Jilong, Zhou Xinwu, Xing Fangqun, Mu Xin, Lü Liang, Yao Zhongwen, Du Jing and other responsible persons and journalists from the capital.
    Romanian Ambassador to China, Aurel Duma, was invited to attend the banquet.

### 我新闻工作者协会主席吴冷西  欢宴罗马尼亚新闻工作者代表团

1966-05-08
第5版()
专栏：

    我新闻工作者协会主席吴冷西
    欢宴罗马尼亚新闻工作者代表团
    新华社七日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会主席吴冷西今天晚上举行宴会，欢迎由罗马尼亚新闻工作者协会主席内斯托尔·伊格纳特率领的罗马尼亚新闻工作者代表团。
    吴冷西在致词中指出，中罗两国新闻工作者的频繁交往，反映了两国友好关系和人民之间的友谊进一步发展。他说，这次罗马尼亚新闻工作者代表团访问中国，必将对加强中罗两国人民和新闻工作者之间的友好合作作出贡献。
    内斯托尔·伊格纳特说，罗中两国存在着的友谊和联系正在发展。罗马尼亚新闻工作者代表团将把中国人民在中国共产党的领导下所取得的成就介绍给罗马尼亚人民，将为加强罗中两国人民的友谊和合作作出自己的贡献。
    出席宴会的有范瑾、王揖、邓岗、孙晓村、毕季龙、周新武、邢方群、穆欣、吕梁、姚仲文、杜敬等有关方面负责人和首都新闻界人士。
    罗马尼亚驻中国大使奥列尔·杜马应邀出席了宴会。
