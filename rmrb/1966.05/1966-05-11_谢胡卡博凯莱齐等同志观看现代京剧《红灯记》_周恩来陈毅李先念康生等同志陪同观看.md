Title: Comrade Shehu, Comrade Kapo, Comrade Kelezi, and Others Watch Modern Peking Opera "The Red Lantern" Comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, Kang Sheng, and Others Accompany Them

Author: 
Date: 1966-05-11
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 10th: Comrade Shehu, Comrade Kapo, Comrade Kelezi, and other comrades of the Albanian Party and Government Delegation, accompanied by Comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, Kang Sheng, and others, watched the modern Peking Opera "The Red Lantern" tonight. This opera was performed by the China Peking Opera Theater for the distinguished Albanian guests.
    Comrades Wu Xiuquan, Wang Bingnan, Xu Jianguo, and others also accompanied the distinguished guests.
    After watching the performance, Comrade Shehu, Comrade Kapo, and others, accompanied by Comrade Zhou Enlai and others, went on stage, warmly shook hands with the actors, and congratulated them on the success of this modern Peking Opera. (Picture attached)
    May 10th, Comrade Shehu and other distinguished Albanian guests, accompanied by Comrade Zhou Enlai and others, watched the modern Peking Opera "The Red Lantern" performed by the China Peking Opera Theater. After the performance, Comrade Shehu and other distinguished Albanian guests, accompanied by Comrade Zhou Enlai and others, went on stage and took a group photo with the actors.
　　　　　　　　　　　Xinhua

### 谢胡卡博凯莱齐等同志观看现代京剧《红灯记》  周恩来陈毅李先念康生等同志陪同观看

1966-05-11
第5版()
专栏：

    谢胡卡博凯莱齐等同志观看现代京剧《红灯记》
    周恩来陈毅李先念康生等同志陪同观看
    新华社十日讯　谢胡、卡博、凯莱齐等同志和阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团其他同志，今晚由周恩来、陈毅、李先念、康生等同志陪同，观看了现代京剧《红灯记》。这个戏是中国京剧院为阿尔巴尼亚贵宾演出的。
    陪同贵宾们观看演出的，还有伍修权、王炳南、许建国等同志。
    谢胡、卡博等同志看完演出以后，在周恩来等同志陪同下走上舞台，同演员们亲切握手，祝贺这个现代京剧演出的成功。（附图片）
    五月十日，谢胡同志等阿尔巴尼亚贵宾，在周恩来同志等陪同下，观看了中国京剧院演出的现代京剧《红灯记》。演出结束后，谢胡同志等阿尔巴尼亚贵宾，在周恩来同志等陪同下，走上舞台和演员合影。　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　新华社记者　刘庆瑞摄
    五月十日，阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团团长、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席穆罕默德·谢胡在北京举行盛大告别宴会。谢胡等同志陪同刘少奇等同志步入宴会大厅。
　　　　　　　　　　新华社记者　刘庆瑞摄
    谢胡同志在宴会上讲话。新华社记者　冀连波摄
