Title: Chinese Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Zambia
Author:
Date: 1966-05-16
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Lusaka, 13th -- A Chinese government trade delegation, led by Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, arrived in Lusaka on the 13th for a friendly visit to the Republic of Zambia.
    Welcoming the delegation at the airport were Zambia's Minister of Commerce and Industry, Chimba, Parliamentary Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Sikazwe, and other senior government officials. Qin Lizhen, the Chinese Ambassador to Zambia, and diplomatic envoys from some countries also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

### 我政府贸易代表团到赞比亚

1966-05-16
第6版()
专栏：

    我政府贸易代表团到赞比亚
    新华社十三日讯　卢萨卡消息：由对外贸易部副部长雷任民率领的中国政府贸易代表团十三日到达卢萨卡对赞比亚共和国进行友好访问。
    到机场欢迎代表团的有赞比亚工商部长钦巴、外交部议会秘书西卡塞拉，以及政府的其他高级官员。中国驻赞比亚大使秦力真和一些国家的外交使节也到机场欢迎。
