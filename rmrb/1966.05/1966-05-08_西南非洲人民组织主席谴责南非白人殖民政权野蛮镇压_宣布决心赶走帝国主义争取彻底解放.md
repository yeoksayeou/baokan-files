Title: SWAPO Chairman Condemns Savage Suppression by White South African Colonial Regime, Declares Determination to Drive Out Imperialism and Strive for Complete Liberation
Author:
Date: 1966-05-08
Edition: 6

    According to a New China News Agency dispatch from Dar es Salaam: Sam Nujoma, Chairman of the South West Africa People's Organization (SWAPO), announced at a press conference here on April 29 that the people of Namibia (South West Africa) are determined to completely liberate their motherland from the enslavement of foreign rule and drive colonialism and imperialism out of Africa.
    He strongly condemned the South African racist regime's barbaric suppression of Africans in South West Africa. He said that on March 20, when he arrived in Windhoek, the capital of South West Africa, he was unreasonably arrested by police and soldiers of the South African racist regime. Later, he was subjected to inhuman torture and abuse by the racist regime's police in the black prison. He revealed that Leo Shoopala, the organizational secretary of SWAPO in the northern region of South West Africa, was beaten to death by the South African racist authorities last month, and another member of the organization was also beaten and seriously injured.
    He emphasized that the suppression of the African people of South West Africa by the Verwoerd regime is receiving military support from the United States, Britain, and other imperialists. (Attached picture)
    The dying struggle and frenzied counterattack of imperialism cannot stop the torrent of the African people's anti-imperialist struggle. The picture shows youth in Congo (Brazzaville) holding placards with the slogan "Down with Imperialism" participating in a parade.
　　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency Reporter Gao Liang Photograph

### 西南非洲人民组织主席谴责南非白人殖民政权野蛮镇压  宣布决心赶走帝国主义争取彻底解放

1966-05-08
第6版()
专栏：

    西南非洲人民组织主席谴责南非白人殖民政权野蛮镇压
    宣布决心赶走帝国主义争取彻底解放
   据新华社达累斯萨拉姆电　西南非洲人民组织主席萨姆·努乔姆四月二十九日在这里举行的一次记者招待会上宣布，纳米布（西南非洲）人民决心使他们的祖国从外国统治的奴役下获得彻底解放，把殖民主义和帝国主义从非洲赶出去。
    他强烈谴责南非种族主义政权野蛮镇压西南非洲的非洲人。他说，三月二十日他到达西南非洲首府温得和克的时候被南非种族主义政权的警察和士兵无理逮捕。后来他在黑牢里遭到了种族主义政权警察的惨无人道的拷打和虐待。他透露，西南非洲人民组织在西南非洲北部地区的组织书记列奥·肖帕拉上个月被南非种族主义当局打死，这个组织的另外一个成员也被打，受了重伤。
    他强调指出，维沃尔德政权对西南非洲的非洲人民进行的镇压，是得到美、英和其他帝国主义的军事支持的。（附图片）
    帝国主义的垂死挣扎和疯狂反扑，阻挡不了非洲人民反帝斗争的洪流。图为刚果（布）青年举着打倒帝国主义的标语牌参加游行。　
　　　　　　　　　新华社记者　高　梁摄
