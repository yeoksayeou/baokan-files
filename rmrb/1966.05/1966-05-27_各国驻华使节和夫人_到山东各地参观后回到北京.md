Title: Foreign Envoys and Spouses Return to Beijing After Visiting Various Places in Shandong

Author:
Date: 1966-05-27
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 26th—Foreign envoys and their spouses, accompanied by Vice Foreign Minister Han Nianlong and his wife, Wang Zhen, and others, returned to Beijing today after traveling and visiting Jinan, Qufu, Tai'an, and Qingdao in Shandong Province.
    From May 14th to 26th, foreign envoys and their spouses visited factories, enterprises, people's communes, and schools, and toured Mount Tai and other scenic spots. Through this visit, they saw the situation of industrial and agricultural production, urban construction, culture and education, and people's lives in these places, and gained a further understanding of the achievements made by the Chinese people in socialist construction through self-reliance and hard work, especially the great spiritual and material power generated after emphasizing politics and studying Chairman Mao's works. They were warmly received by local governments and people in various places.

### 各国驻华使节和夫人  到山东各地参观后回到北京

1966-05-27
第5版()
专栏：

    各国驻华使节和夫人
    到山东各地参观后回到北京
    新华社二十六日讯　各国驻华使节和夫人，在外交部副部长韩念龙和夫人王珍等陪同下，到山东济南、曲阜、泰安、青岛旅行参观后，今天回到北京。
    从五月十四日到二十六日，各国驻华使节和夫人参观了工厂企业、人民公社、学校，并游览了泰山和其他名胜古迹。通过这次参观访问，他们看到了这些地方工农业生产、城市建设、文化教育、人民生活等方面的情况，对我国人民自力更生、奋发图强在社会主义建设中取得的成就，特别是在突出政治、大学毛主席著作以后所产生的伟大的精神和物质力量有了进一步的了解。他们在各地受到了当地政府和人民友好的接待。
