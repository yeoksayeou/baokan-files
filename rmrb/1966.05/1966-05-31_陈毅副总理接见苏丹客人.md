Title: Chen Yi, Vice Premier, Receives Sudanese Guests
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-31
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, May 30th – Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Sudanese Higher Education Institutions Teachers' Friendship Delegation headed by Muhammad Taum Tijani this morning. Vice Premier Chen Yi held a cordial and friendly conversation with the guests.
    Present at the reception were Gao Yi, Zhang Tiesheng, and others.

### 陈毅副总理接见苏丹客人

1966-05-31
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见苏丹客人
    新华社三十日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见以穆罕默德·陶姆·提加尼为首的苏丹高等院校教师友好代表团。陈毅副总理同客人们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有高沂、张铁生等。
