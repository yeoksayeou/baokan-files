Title: Malian Ambassador to China Leaves Post for Home

Author: 
Date: 1966-05-16
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 14th – Birama Traoré, Ambassador of the Republic of Mali to China, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home after completing his term.

### 马里驻华大使离任回国

1966-05-16
第6版()
专栏：

    马里驻华大使离任回国
    新华社十四日讯　马里共和国驻中国大使比拉马·特拉奥雷奉调离任，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
