Title: Evidence of American Bandit Planes Intruding into the Northeastern Region of Maguan, Yunnan, for War Provocations

Author:

Date: 1966-05-18
Edition: 1

    Evidence of American Bandit Planes Intruding into the Northeastern Region of Maguan, Yunnan, for War Provocations
    On May 12th, American bandit planes brazenly intruded into the airspace of the northeastern region of Maguan County, Yunnan Province, China, and stealthily attacked and shot down a Chinese training plane. This is another serious crime committed by U.S. imperialism in its deliberate war provocations against the Chinese people.
    American official spokesmen in Washington and the spokesmen for the U.S. invading forces in Saigon have repeatedly fabricated lies in an attempt to deny this crime. However, the missile debris and seven auxiliary fuel tanks dropped by American bandit planes on Chinese territory have completely shattered the lies of the American aggressors and exposed the heinous plot of U.S. imperialism to deliberately provoke war against the Chinese people and attempt to spread the flames of war to China. (Photos attached)
    Evidence of American bandit planes launching missiles in the airspace of the northeastern region of Maguan, Yunnan, to shoot down our training plane: The tail debris and fragments of "Sidewinder" air-to-air missiles.
                                      Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhu Yuhu
    Evidence of American bandit planes intruding into our country's airspace: Auxiliary fuel tanks dropped near Tangfang, 33 kilometers northeast of Maguan County, Yunnan. Yang Zheng (Xinhua News Agency Manuscript Fax Photo)

### 美国强盗飞机侵入我云南马关东北地区上空进行战争挑衅的罪证

1966-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

    美国强盗飞机侵入我云南马关东北地区上空进行战争挑衅的罪证
    五月十二日，美国强盗飞机公然侵入中国云南省马关东北地区上空，偷袭并击落中国训练飞机一架。这是美帝国主义蓄意向中国人民进行战争挑衅而犯下的又一严重罪行。
    华盛顿的美国官方发言人和西贡侵越美军发言人，事后一再编造谎言，妄图抵赖这一罪行。但是美国强盗飞机丢在中国国土上的导弹残骸和七个副油箱，彻底粉碎了美国侵略者的谎言，暴露了美帝国主义蓄意向中国人民进行战争挑衅，企图把战火烧到中国的罪恶阴谋。（附图片）
    美国强盗飞机在我国云南马关东北地区上空发射导弹，击落我训练飞机一架的罪证：“响尾蛇”空对空导弹尾部残骸和碎片。
                                      新华社记者　朱于湖摄
    美国强盗飞机侵入我国领空的罪证：丢落在我国云南马关县东北三十三公里唐房附近的副油箱。　杨　正摄（新华社稿　传真照片）
