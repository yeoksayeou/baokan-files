Title: A Guest from New Zealand Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-05-11
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Tom Newnham, editor-in-chief of New Zealand's "World Rural," arrived in Beijing on the 9th for a friendly visit to our country at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries. Chen Mo, a member of the council of the Friendship Association, and others were at the airport to welcome him.

### 新西兰一客人到京

1966-05-11
第6版()
专栏：

    新西兰一客人到京
    新华社十日讯　应中国人民对外文化友好协会邀请，前来我国进行友好访问的新西兰《世界农村》总编辑托姆·纽纳姆，九日到北京。前往机场欢迎的有对外友协理事陈沫等。
