Title: Wilcox Praises China's Successful Third Nuclear Explosion: China's Bomb is the Bomb of the World's Oppressed; China's New Victory is the Victory of Anti-Imperialist and Anti-Revisionist People
Author:
Date: 1966-05-18
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Wellington, May 17th (by telegram)  V.G. Wilcox, General Secretary of the Communist Party of New Zealand, issued a statement on May 17th on behalf of the National Secretariat of the Communist Party of New Zealand, hailing the success of China's third nuclear explosion.
    The statement said: The successful testing of the People's China's third nuclear bomb will undoubtedly be a tremendous encouragement to all anti-imperialist people.
    This victory is not just China's victory—it is a victory for the socialist camp, it is a victory for all opponents of revisionism. Nuclear weapons in the hands of Marxists-Leninists are weapons for defending peace and socialism, and at the same time they terrify the imperialist ranks, the enemies of mankind.
    The statement said that U.S. imperialism, relying on its large and ever-increasing nuclear arsenal, is engaging in nuclear blackmail, carrying out armed aggression in Vietnam and elsewhere, attempting to dominate the entire world and bury socialism. China's third nuclear test is another action to thwart these arrogant goals of the main enemy of mankind.
    U.S. imperialism and the modern revisionists of the Soviet Communist Party leadership are discovering that nuclear weapons, like all other new weapons in history, cannot be monopolized and cannot intimidate others.
    The statement said that anti-imperialist forces, progressive forces, are advancing towards victory everywhere. China's bomb makes the world's socialist regions and newly liberated regions safer, and is therefore beneficial. Some people, many of whom did not protest the United States' past continuous testing, are now saying that China should not conduct tests.
    They do not realize that the danger today is not China, but the continuous manufacture and storage of nuclear weapons by those countries that already possess them.
    The statement said that the appeal made by the Chinese government two years ago has not received much response, and this remains the policy of the Chinese government today. It should not be forgotten: China guarantees never to be the first to use nuclear weapons, while the United States has never made such a guarantee.
    The statement concluded by saying that China's bomb—the bomb of the world's poor and oppressed—guards socialism, national liberation, and peace.

### 威尔科克斯同志盛赞中国第三次核爆炸成功  中国的炸弹是世界上被压迫者的炸弹  中国的新胜利是反帝反修的人的胜利

1966-05-18
第5版()
专栏：

    威尔科克斯同志盛赞中国第三次核爆炸成功
    中国的炸弹是世界上被压迫者的炸弹
    中国的新胜利是反帝反修的人的胜利
    新华社惠灵顿十七日电  新西兰共产党总书记威尔科克斯十七日以新西兰共产党全国书记处的名义发表声明，欢呼中国第三次核爆炸成功。
    声明说：人民中国第三颗核弹试验成功，无疑将是对一切反帝的人民的一个巨大的鼓舞。
    这一胜利不只是中国的胜利——它是社会主义阵营的胜利，它是一切反对修正主义的人的胜利。掌握在马克思列宁主义者手中的核武器，是保卫和平和社会主义的武器，同时它们使人类的敌人帝国主义队伍惊慌失措。
    声明说，美帝国主义凭借自己大量的和不断增加的核武器储存进行核讹诈，对越南和其他地方进行武装侵略，企图统治整个世界和埋葬社会主义。中国的第三次核弹试验是挫败人类主要敌人的这些狂妄目的的又一个行动。
    美帝国主义以及苏共领导集团现代修正主义者正在发现，核武器，象历史上的所有其他的新武器一样，是垄断不了的，是吓不倒别人的。
    声明说，反帝力量，进步力量，正在到处向胜利迈进。中国的炸弹使世界上社会主义地区和新获得解放的地区更加安全，因此是有益的。有些人，其中许多人对美国过去不断进行试验不提出抗议，而现在却说，中国不应该进行试验。
    他们没有认识到，今天的危险不是中国，而是那些已经拥有核武器的国家不断制造和储存核武器。
    声明说，中国政府两年前提出的呼吁，没有得到什么响应，而这仍然是中国政府今天的政策。不应当忘记：中国保证决不首先使用核武器，而美国从没有作出这样的保证。
    声明最后说，中国的炸弹——世界上穷人和被压迫者的炸弹——守卫着社会主义、民族解放与和平。
