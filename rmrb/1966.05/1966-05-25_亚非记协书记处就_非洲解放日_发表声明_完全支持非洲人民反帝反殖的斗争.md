Title: Afro-Asian Journalists' Association Secretariat Issues Statement on "Africa Liberation Day" Fully Supports the African People's Struggle Against Imperialism and Colonialism
Author:
Date: 1966-05-25
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, May 24th. The Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association issued a statement on May 24th regarding "Africa Liberation Day" (May 25th), expressing its full support for the African people's struggle against imperialism and colonialism, for complete liberation and independence, and for the defense of sovereignty and African unity.
    The statement said, "The struggle of the African people is unfolding in close solidarity with the struggles of the Asian and Latin American peoples, because they face the same enemy - imperialism and neo-colonialism headed by the United States. 'Africa Liberation Day' marks a new upsurge and further strengthening on the African front of our worldwide struggle against US imperialism."
    The statement pointed out, "Over the past year, Africa has experienced many political changes, including certain subversive attempts and coups. However, this only proves how the imperialists and their lackeys are making a dying struggle to maintain or re-establish their colonial rule in Africa in the face of the great awakening of the African people and the high tide of their struggle. The true faces of the United Nations and the imperialists headed by the United States, as well as the false revolutionaries who claim to be friends of the African people, have been thoroughly exposed before the African people. The African people know who their true friends are and who their enemies are in their struggle for liberation. They know what they need to achieve their goals. The fact that the people of Zimbabwe, following the people of Congo (Kinshasa), "Portuguese" Guinea, Angola, Mozambique, Cameroon, etc., took up arms and rose up against the unilateral declaration of "independence" by the Smith fascist regime planned by imperialism, fully demonstrates the trend now sweeping among the African people that armed struggle is the only way to defeat imperialism and colonialism."
    The statement said, "This has been a year of great political upheaval and significant changes in Africa. The Afro-Asian journalists are deeply convinced that through this great upheaval, imperialism and its lackeys will be crushed, and the heroic African people will achieve victory and advance courageously towards final liberation, independence and unity."
    The statement said, "The Afro-Asian journalists, united under the banner of the Jakarta Declaration, will stand firmly on the side of the African people in the common struggle against imperialism and neo-colonialism headed by the United States."

### 亚非记协书记处就“非洲解放日”发表声明  完全支持非洲人民反帝反殖的斗争

1966-05-25
第6版()
专栏：

    亚非记协书记处就“非洲解放日”发表声明
    完全支持非洲人民反帝反殖的斗争
    新华社二十四日讯　亚非新闻工作者协会书记处二十四日就“非洲解放日”（二十五日）发表声明，表示完全支持非洲人民反对帝国主义和殖民主义，争取彻底解放和独立，维护主权和非洲统一的斗争。
    声明说，“非洲人民的斗争，是在同亚洲和拉丁美洲人民的斗争紧密团结的情况下展开的，因为他们面对着同一个的敌人——以美国为首的帝国主义和新老殖民主义。‘非洲解放日’标志着我们这个世界范围的反对美帝国主义的斗争在非洲战线上的新高涨和进一步加强。”
    声明指出，“一年来，非洲经历了许多政治变化，包括某些颠覆尝试和政变。但是，这只是证明了在非洲人民的巨大觉醒和他们的斗争高涨面前，帝国主义者及其走狗怎样作垂死的挣扎，企图保持或重新建立他们在非洲的殖民统治。联合国和以美国为首的帝国主义者，以及那些自称为非洲各国人民的朋友的假革命者的真面目，已经在非洲各国人民面前遭到彻底的揭露。非洲各国人民知道，在他们争取解放的斗争中，谁是他们的真正朋友，谁是他们的敌人。他们知道，为了实现他们的目标，他们需要什么。津巴布韦人民紧接刚果（利）、“葡属”几内亚、安哥拉、莫三鼻给、喀麦隆等人民之后，手执武器奋起反对帝国主义策划的史密斯法西斯政权的单方面宣布“独立”，这个事实充分表明了现在在非洲各国人民中间掀起的这样一个趋势——武装斗争是击败帝国主义和殖民主义的唯一道路。”
    声明说，“这是非洲发生政治大动荡和意义重大的变化的一年。亚非新闻工作者深信，通过这个大动荡，帝国主义及其走狗将被粉碎，英雄的非洲人民将取得胜利，朝着最后解放、独立和统一而奋勇前进。”
    声明说，“团结在雅加达宣言旗帜下的亚非新闻工作者，将在反对以美国为首的帝国主义和新老殖民主义的共同斗争中，坚定地站在非洲各国人民一边。”
