Title: Korean Leaders Warmly Congratulate Me on the Successful Nuclear Explosion Containing Thermonuclear Materials; China's New Achievement in Nuclear Testing is a Huge Blow to American Imperialism

Author:
Date: 1966-05-12
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 11th. Our Party and state leaders received on the 11th telegrams from Korean Party and state leaders warmly congratulating our country on the successful nuclear explosion containing thermonuclear materials. The full text of the telegram is as follows: To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Comrade Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:
    We, on behalf of the Korean people and ourselves, extend our warmest congratulations to you, and through you, to the fraternal Chinese people and Chinese scientists and technicians, on the successful nuclear test conducted by China containing thermonuclear materials.
    The success of this nuclear test by China is another victory for the fraternal Chinese people under the leadership of the Communist Party of China in their struggle to further strengthen national defense and safeguard national security; it is a huge blow to the American imperialists who are frantically pursuing a policy of nuclear blackmail and engaging in new war provocations.
    We wish the fraternal Chinese people new achievements in their struggle against the imperialist aggressors headed by American imperialism, for peace, for the socialist construction of the country and for the strengthening of national defense.

### 朝鲜领导人热烈祝贺我进行含有热核材料的核爆炸成功  中国核试验新成就是对美帝的巨大打击

1966-05-12
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜领导人热烈祝贺我进行含有热核材料的核爆炸成功
    中国核试验新成就是对美帝的巨大打击
    新华社十一日讯　我国党和国家领导人十一日收到了朝鲜党和国家领导人热烈祝贺我国成功地进行了含有热核材料的核爆炸的电报。电报全文如下：中国北京中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志，中华人民共和国主席刘少奇同志，中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德同志，中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志：
    中国成功地进行了含有热核材料的核试验，我们以朝鲜人民和我们个人的名义，向你们并通过你们向兄弟的中国人民和中国科学技术人员，表示热烈的祝贺。
    中国这次核试验的成功，是兄弟的中国人民在中国共产党的领导下，在进一步加强国防力量和保卫国家安全的斗争中取得的又一胜利；是对疯狂地奉行核讹诈政策和进行新战争挑衅活动的美帝国主义者的巨大打击。
    我们祝兄弟的中国人民在反对以美帝国主义为首的帝国主义侵略者、争取和平、进行国家的社会主义建设和加强国防的斗争中，取得新的成就。	朝鲜劳动党中央委员会委员长	朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相  金日成	朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会委员长					崔庸健				一九六六年五月十一日于平壤
