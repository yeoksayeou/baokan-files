Title: Lao Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-05-12
Edition: 5

    (Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 11th) The Lao Cultural Delegation, invited by the China-Laos Friendship Association to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The delegation was led by Outama Chulamani, Chairman of the Lao Patriotic Teachers Association.

### 老挝文化代表团到京

1966-05-12
第5版()
专栏：

    老挝文化代表团到京
    新华社十一日讯　应中国老挝友好协会邀请来我国访问的老挝文化代表团，在团长、老挝爱国教师协会副主席乌塔马·朱拉马尼率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
