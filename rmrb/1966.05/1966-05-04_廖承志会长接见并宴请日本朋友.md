Title: Liao Chengzhi Meets and Hosts Japanese Friends

Author: 
Date: 1966-05-04
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Liao Chengzhi, President of the China-Japan Friendship Association, received a group of Japanese friends who came to China to participate in the "May 1st" International Labor Day celebrations tonight. They are: the 12th Delegation of the Japan-China Friendship Association to China, led by Saito Norie, Secretary-General of the Japan-China Friendship Association; the Workers' Delegation of the Japan-China Friendship Association, led by Uesugi Yoshio; the Delegation of the Japan Workers' Music Council, led by Makino Hiroyuki; the Delegation of Japanese Activists for Expanding Readers of "People's China," led by Tanigawa Toshizo, Executive Director of the Japan-China Friendship Association; and the Delegation of the All-Japan Day Laborers' Union, led by Chayama Takaji, Central Executive Committee Member of the All-Japan Day Laborers' Union. Liao Chengzhi held cordial and friendly talks with the Japanese friends. After the reception, Liao Chengzhi hosted a banquet for the Japanese friends. At the banquet, the hosts and guests jointly wished for the further development of the friendship between the Chinese and Japanese people. Zhao Anbo, Gu Dachun, Yang Yunyu, Luo Jun, Lü Ji and others were present at the reception and banquet.

### 廖承志会长接见并宴请日本朋友

1966-05-04
第5版()
专栏：

    廖承志会长接见并宴请日本朋友
    新华社三日讯　中日友好协会会长廖承志今晚接见了来我国参加庆祝“五一”国际劳动节活动的一批日本朋友。他们是：由日中友协总务部长斋藤纪江率领的日中友协第十二次访华代表团，由上杉芳夫率领的日中友好协会工人代表团，由牧野弘之率领的日本勤劳者音乐协议会代表团，由日中友协常务理事长谷川敏三率领的日本扩大《人民中国》读者积极分子访华代表团，由全日本散工工会中央执行委员茶山他家司率领的全日本散工工会代表团。廖承志同日本朋友进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见后，廖承志设宴招待日本朋友。宾主在宴会上共祝中日两国人民的友谊进一步发展。陪同接见和参加宴会的，有赵安博、顾大椿、杨蕴玉、罗俊、吕骥等。
