Title: Liu Shaoqi and Other Comrades Continue Talks with Shehu and Other Comrades; Kaba and Other Comrades Visit the National Exhibition of Da寨-Style Agricultural Models
Author:
Date: 1966-05-03
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd May: Comrades Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Deng Xiaoping, Kang Sheng, and Wu Xiuquan continued talks with the Albanian Party and Government Delegation Comrades Mehmet Shehu, Hysni Kapo, Abdyl Këllezi, Nesti Nase, and Piro Bita on the afternoon of the 1st and 2nd.
    The talks were held in an extremely cordial and friendly atmosphere, and both sides were in complete agreement on all issues discussed.
    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd May: Comrade Hysni Kapo, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour, and Comrades Abdyl Këllezi and Nesti Nase, members of the Albanian Party and Government Delegation, visited the National Agricultural Exhibition Hall this morning to view the National Exhibition of Da寨-Style Agricultural Models.
    Tan Zhenlin, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, accompanied Comrade Kapo and other comrades on their visit and gave the Albanian comrades an introduction.
    Comrade Kapo considered the exhibition to be very good. He said, "During our visit to China this time, we visited a cooperative run with the spirit of 'poor sticks'. Comrade Shehu said that we had attended a university. Today, this exhibition of Da寨-style agricultural models that we saw is another great encouragement to us. We can now say that Albania has great potential for developing agricultural production." During the visit, Comrade Kapo accepted the National Exhibition of Da寨-Style Agricultural Models wall chart presented by the exhibition hall.
    Here, Comrade Kapo and other comrades also visited the Agricultural Exhibition Hall's Rural Subsidiary Industry Hall, Animal Husbandry Hall, Water Conservancy Hall, and Agricultural Mechanization Hall. Today, Cheng Zhaoxuan, Zhang Hanying, Xu Binzhou, Liang Yao, and other responsible persons from the Ministry of Agriculture, the Ministry of Water Resources and Electric Power, the Eighth Ministry of Machine Building, and the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives accompanied the Albanian comrades on their visit. The distinguished guests received a warm welcome from the responsible persons and staff of the various exhibition halls during their visit.

### 刘少奇等同志同谢胡等同志继续会谈  卡博等同志参观全国大寨式农业典型展览

1966-05-03
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇等同志同谢胡等同志继续会谈
    卡博等同志参观全国大寨式农业典型展览
    新华社二日讯　刘少奇同志、周恩来同志、邓小平同志、康生同志、伍修权同志，同阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团穆罕默德·谢胡同志、希斯尼·卡博同志、阿卜杜勒·凯莱齐同志、奈斯蒂·纳赛同志、皮罗·比塔同志，在一日和二日下午继续举行会谈。
    会谈是在极为亲切友好的气氛中进行的，双方在会谈中对所有谈到的问题意见完全一致。
    新华社二日讯　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员卡博同志，和阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团团员凯莱齐同志、纳赛同志，今天上午到全国农业展览馆，参观了全国大寨式农业典型展览。
    中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理谭震林陪同卡博等同志参观，并且向阿尔巴尼亚同志作了介绍。
    卡博同志认为这个展览很好。他说，我们这次访问中国，参观了用“穷棒子”精神办社，谢胡同志说我们上了一次大学。今天我们看的这个大寨式农业典型展览，对我们又是一个很大的鼓舞，我们现在可以说，阿尔巴尼亚发展农业生产的潜力是很大的。在参观的时候，卡博同志接受了展览馆赠送的全国大寨式农业典型展览挂图。
    在这里，卡博等同志还参观了农展馆的农村副业馆、畜牧馆、水利馆和农业机械化馆。今天陪同阿尔巴尼亚同志参观的，还有农业部、水利电力部、第八机械工业部、全国供销合作总社的负责人程照轩、张含英、徐斌洲、梁耀等。贵宾们在参观的时候，受到各展览馆负责人和工作人员的热烈欢迎。
