Title: Our Government Cultural Friendship Delegation Arrives in Iraq
Author:
Date: 1966-05-31
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, May 29th - The Chinese Government Cultural Friendship Delegation, led by Li Chang, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, arrived here on the afternoon of the 29th. The delegation is visiting Iraq for two weeks at the invitation of the Iraqi government.
    Greeting the delegation at the airport were Abdul Razzaq Jalili, Director of the Department of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Iraqi Ministry of Education, Hussein Musai, Director of the Administrative Department of the Ministry of Education, and Khalid Shawwaf, Director of the Cultural Department of the Ministry of Culture and National Guidance.
    Cao Chi, Chinese Ambassador to Iraq, also greeted them at the airport.
    Prior to this, the delegation had visited Syria, the United Arab Republic, and Yemen.

### 我政府文化友好代表团到达伊拉克

1966-05-31
第6版()
专栏：

    我政府文化友好代表团到达伊拉克
    新华社巴格达二十九日电  由对外文委副主任李昌率领的中国政府文化友好代表团在二十九日下午到达这里。代表团是应伊拉克政府的邀请，来伊拉克进行为期两个星期的友好访问。
    到机场欢迎代表团的有伊拉克教育部对外文化关系司司长阿卜杜勒·拉扎克·贾利利，教育部行政司司长侯赛因·穆塞以及文化和国家指导部文化司司长哈利德·沙瓦夫等。
    中国驻伊拉克大使曹痴也到机场迎接。
    在这之前，代表团曾经访问了叙利亚、阿联和也门。
