Title: China and Morocco Sign Additional Protocol to Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1966-05-22
Edition: 5

    China and Morocco Sign Additional Protocol to Trade Agreement
    Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, May 20th – An additional protocol to the China-Morocco Trade Agreement was signed here on the 20th. According to this protocol, China will export tea, textiles, machinery, etc. to Morocco, and import phosphate, cotton, and ore, etc. from Morocco.
    Yang Qiliang, the Chinese Ambassador to Morocco, and Abdel Hamid Krim, the Director of the Economic Department of the Moroccan Ministry of Foreign Affairs, signed the protocol.

### 中摩签订贸易协定附加议定书

1966-05-22
第5版()
专栏：

    中摩签订贸易协定附加议定书
    新华社拉巴特二十日电　中国—摩洛哥贸易协定附加议定书二十日在这里签字。根据这项议定书，中国将向摩洛哥出口茶叶、纺织品、机器等，并从摩洛哥进口磷酸盐、棉花和矿石等。
    在议定书上签字的是中国驻摩洛哥大使杨琪良和摩洛哥外交部经济司司长阿卜杜勒·哈米德·克里姆。
