Title: Nepalese Minister of Industry and Commerce, Mr. Rijal, and his Wife Visit Beijing

Author: 
Date: 1966-05-01
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 30th. The Nepalese Minister of Industry and Commerce, Mr. Ratna Prasad Rijal, and his wife, visited Beijing yesterday and today.

    Minister Rijal and his wife visited the National Exhibition of Agricultural Models of the Da寨 Style yesterday morning. After the visit, Mr. Rijal wrote in the guestbook that he was greatly encouraged by China's tremendous achievements in such a short period of time. He believes that the mutual exchange of achievements between Nepal and China will continuously develop and consolidate the friendship between the two countries.

    Today, the distinguished guests also visited a tannery, a shoe factory, an arts and crafts factory, and an obstetrics and gynecology hospital.

    Wherever the distinguished guests went, they were warmly welcomed by the masses. Responsible individuals from relevant departments, including Li Yingji and Cheng Zhaoxuan, accompanied the distinguished guests on their visits.

    Tonight, Mr. Rijal and his wife watched the modern Chinese Peking Opera "The Red Lantern" at the People's Theater.

### 尼泊尔工商大臣里贾尔和夫人在京参观

1966-05-01
第6版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔工商大臣里贾尔和夫人在京参观
    新华社三十日讯　尼泊尔工商大臣纳·普·里贾尔和夫人，今天和昨天在北京进行了参观访问。
    里贾尔大臣和夫人昨天上午参观了全国大寨式农业典型展览。参观后，里贾尔在留言簿上题词，他对中国在这么短的期间里取得这样巨大的成就，感到很大鼓舞，他认为，通过尼中两国之间成就的相互交流，将使两国的友谊不断发展和巩固。
    今天贵宾们还分别参观了制革厂、鞋厂、工艺美术工厂和妇产医院。
    贵宾们所到之处，都受到了群众热烈的欢迎。分别陪同贵宾参观的有李应吉、程照轩等有关方面负责人。
    今天晚上，里贾尔和夫人在人民剧场观看了中国现代京剧《红灯记》。
