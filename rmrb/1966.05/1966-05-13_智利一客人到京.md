Title: Chilean Guest Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-05-13
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 12th—Horacio Serrano Palma, former Chilean Minister of Agriculture, Secretary-General of the “Council of University Presidents,” and reporter for *El Mercurio*, arrived in Beijing on the 11th at the invitation of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association for a visit to China. Ren Yinglun, Vice-President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, and others welcomed him at the airport.

### 智利一客人到京

1966-05-13
第6版()
专栏：

    智利一客人到京
    新华社十二日讯　应中拉友协邀请来中国访问的智利前农业部长、“大学校长委员会”秘书长、《水星报》记者奥拉西奥·塞拉诺·帕尔马十一日到达北京。中拉友协副会长任映仑等前往欢迎。
