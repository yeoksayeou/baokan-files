Title: Sudanese Guest Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-06
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 5th: Mr. Mohammed Mahdi Moussa, Trade Manager of the Sudanese Ministry of Agriculture, who was invited to China to attend the 1966 Spring Chinese Export Commodities Fair held in Guangzhou, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon from Guangzhou.

### 苏丹一客人到京

1966-05-06
第5版()
专栏：

    苏丹一客人到京
    新华社五日讯　应邀来我国参加在广州举办的一九六六年春季中国出口商品交易会的苏丹农业部贸易经理穆罕默德·马赫迪·穆萨，今天下午由广州乘飞机到达北京。
