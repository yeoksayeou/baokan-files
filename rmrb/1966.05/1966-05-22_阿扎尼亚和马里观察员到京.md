Title: Azanian and Malian Observers Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-05-22
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, May 21st – At the invitation of our country to participate in the 1966 Emerging Forces Weightlifting Invitational Tournament, the Azanian (South Africa) observer, Ahmed Gora Ebrahim, and the Malian observer, Dalamane Sangare, arrived in Beijing by plane tonight.
    Representatives from the organizing committee of this invitational tournament and others warmly welcomed them at the airport.

### 阿扎尼亚和马里观察员到京

1966-05-22
第6版()
专栏：

    阿扎尼亚和马里观察员到京
    新华社二十一日讯　应邀来我国参加一九六六年新兴力量举重邀请赛的阿扎尼亚（南非）观察员艾哈迈德·戈拉·易卜拉欣和马里观察员达拉马内·桑加雷，今晚乘飞机到达北京。
    这次邀请赛组织委员会代表等前往机场热烈欢迎。
