Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Matsumura Kenzō
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-18
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 17th - Premier Zhou Enlai received Matsumura Kenzō, advisor to the Japanese Liberal Democratic Party, and his entourage this afternoon, and held friendly talks with them. Present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi, Qiao Guanhua, Liu Xiwu, and other responsible persons from relevant departments.

### 周恩来总理接见松村谦三

1966-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见松村谦三
    新华社十七日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见日本自由民主党顾问松村谦三和他的随行人员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有廖承志、乔冠华、刘希文等有关方面负责人。
