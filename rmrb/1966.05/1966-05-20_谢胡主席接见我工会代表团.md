Title: Chairman Shehu Receives Chinese Trade Union Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-20
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 19th - Albanian Council of Ministers Chairman Shehu received the Chinese Trade Union Delegation led by He Shumin, Vice Chairman of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Federation of Trade Unions, on the 18th and had a very cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Koçani, Secretary of the Central Council of Albanian Trade Unions, and Malile, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs.
    Wang Jinchuan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, was also present at the reception.

### 谢胡主席接见我工会代表团

1966-05-20
第5版()
专栏：

    谢胡主席接见我工会代表团
    新华社地拉那十九日电　阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席谢胡十八日接见了由广西壮族自治区总工会副主席何庶民率领的中国工会代表团，并同他们进行了极为亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有阿尔巴尼亚工会中央理事会书记科查尼和外交部副部长马利列。
    接见时，中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办王锦川也在座。
