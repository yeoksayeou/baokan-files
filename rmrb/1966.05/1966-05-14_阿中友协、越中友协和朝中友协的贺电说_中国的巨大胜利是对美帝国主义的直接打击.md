Title: Greetings from the China-Albania Friendship Association, Vietnam-China Friendship Association, and Korea-China Friendship Association Say China's Great Victory is a Direct Blow to U.S. Imperialism
Author:
Date: 1966-05-14
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 13th dispatch: The Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, the China-Vietnam Friendship Association, and the China-Korea Friendship Association have received telegrams from the Albania-China Friendship Association, the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, and the Korea-China Friendship Association, respectively, congratulating China on its third nuclear test.
    The telegram from Kellezi, Chairman of the Albania-China Friendship Association, stated: "With indescribable joy, we learned of the third nuclear explosion containing thermonuclear materials. We sincerely congratulate and wish the People's Republic of China and the fraternal Chinese people new and greater victories in the struggle against imperialism and modern revisionism, to the benefit of the cause of socialism and freedom for the people of all countries."
    The telegram from Hoang Quoc Viet, Chairman of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, stated: "This great victory will strengthen the defense capabilities of the People's Republic of China and, at the same time, deliver a heavy blow to the bellicose and aggressive U.S. imperialism." The telegram stated that this is a valuable contribution to the movement to defend world peace.
    The telegram stated that this victory of the fraternal Chinese people is a powerful encouragement to the Vietnamese people who are determined to defeat U.S. imperialism and its lackeys.
    The Central Committee of the Korea-China Friendship Association said in a telegram to the China-Korea Friendship Association: "This nuclear explosion test is another victory in the struggle to further strengthen the national defense capabilities of the People's Republic of China and safeguard national security, and is a direct blow to the U.S. imperialist policy of nuclear blackmail." The telegram stated: "We wish the Chinese people new achievements in the struggle against imperialism, to strengthen the socialist construction and national defense capabilities of their country."

### 阿中友协、越中友协和朝中友协的贺电说  中国的巨大胜利是对美帝国主义的直接打击

1966-05-14
第5版()
专栏：

    阿中友协、越中友协和朝中友协的贺电说
    中国的巨大胜利是对美帝国主义的直接打击
    新华社十三日讯　中阿友协、中越友协和中朝友协，分别收到了阿中友协、越中友协和朝中友协发来的祝贺我国第三次核试验的电报。
    阿中友协主席凯莱齐的电报说：“我们怀着难以形容的欢乐心情，得悉含有热核材料的第三次核爆炸。衷心地祝贺和祝愿中华人民共和国和兄弟的中国人民，在反对帝国主义和现代修正主义的斗争中取得新的更大的胜利，以利于世界各国人民的社会主义和自由的事业。”
    越中友协会长黄国越的电报说：“这一巨大的胜利将加强中华人民共和国的防御力量，同时是对好战和侵略成性的美帝国主义的一个沉重打击。”电报说，这是对保卫世界和平运动的一个宝贵贡献。
    电报说，兄弟的中国人民的这一胜利，对正在决心战胜美帝国主义及其走狗的越南人民是一个有力的鼓舞。
    朝中友协中央委员会在致中朝友协的电报中说：“这次核爆炸试验，是在进一步加强中华人民共和国的国防力量和保卫国家安全的斗争中取得的又一胜利，是对美帝国主义核讹诈政策的直接打击。”电报说：“祝中国人民在反对帝国主义、加强本国的社会主义建设和国防力量的斗争中，取得新的成就。”
