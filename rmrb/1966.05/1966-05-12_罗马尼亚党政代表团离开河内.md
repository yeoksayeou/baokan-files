Title: Romanian Party and Government Delegation Leaves Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1966-05-12
Edition: 5

    Hanoi, May 11 (Xinhua) The Romanian Party and Government Delegation, led by Emil Bodnăraș, member of the Central Executive Committee, member of the Standing Presidium of the Central Committee, and First Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian Communist Party, concluded its six-day visit to Vietnam and left Hanoi by plane this morning.
    This morning, a mass farewell ceremony was held in the Hanoi Grand Theater Square. At the farewell ceremony, Nguyen Duy Trinh, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, and delegation leader Emil Bodnăraș spoke successively. Zhu Qiwu, Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam, and Chen Hao, Military Attaché of the Embassy, attended the farewell ceremony.

### 罗马尼亚党政代表团离开河内

1966-05-12
第5版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚党政代表团离开河内
    新华社河内十一日电　由罗马尼亚共产党中央执行委员会委员、中央常设主席团委员、政府部长会议第一副主席埃·波德纳拉希率领的罗马尼亚党政代表团，结束了在越南为时六天的访问，十一日早晨乘飞机离开河内。
    十一日早晨，在河内市大戏院广场上，举行群众欢送仪式。在欢送仪式上，越南劳动党中央政治局委员、政府副总理兼外交部长阮维桢和埃·波德纳拉希团长先后讲了话。中国驻越南大使朱其文和大使馆武官陈皓参加了欢送仪式。
