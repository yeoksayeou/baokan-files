Title: Roman Pope Mediates US-Soviet Collusion, Lodge Meets with Pope After Gromyko
Author:
Date: 1966-05-04
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd dispatch: Vatican News: U.S. Ambassador to Saigon, Lodge, on his way back home to report on the situation in Vietnam, met with Pope Paul VI in the Vatican on the 2nd to discuss the Vietnam issue.
    Western news agencies pointed out that Lodge's special trip to the Vatican to meet with the Pope only five days after Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko met with the Pope, and the meeting time was "unexpectedly long", indicating that this "was an important political discussion." United Press International quoted Vatican sources in a news report as saying that the Pope would undoubtedly inform Lodge of "new prospects for resolving the Vietnam issue that emerged during his meeting with Gromyko."
    U.S. State Department spokesman McCloskey confirmed on the same day that Lodge's meeting with the Pope was arranged with the support of the U.S. State Department.
    During his two-day stay in Italy, Lodge also met with Italian Foreign Minister Fanfani.

### 罗马教皇为美苏勾结牵线  洛奇在葛罗米柯之后同教皇会谈

1966-05-04
第5版()
专栏：

    罗马教皇为美苏勾结牵线
    洛奇在葛罗米柯之后同教皇会谈
    新华社三日讯　梵蒂冈消息：美国驻西贡“大使”洛奇在回国报告越南局势途中，二日在梵蒂冈同教皇保罗六世进行了会谈，讨论了越南问题。
    西方通讯社指出，在苏联外长葛罗米柯同教皇会谈仅仅五天后，洛奇又专程前往梵蒂冈会见教皇，而且会谈时间“出乎意外的长”，表明了这“是一次重要的政治讨论”。合众国际社在一条消息中引梵蒂冈人士的话说，教皇无疑会把“他同葛罗米柯会谈时出现的解决越南问题的新前景”告知洛奇。
    美国国务院发言人麦克洛斯基同一天证实，洛奇同教皇的会谈是在美国国务院支持下安排的。
    洛奇在意大利的两天逗留中还会晤了意大利外交部长范范尼。
