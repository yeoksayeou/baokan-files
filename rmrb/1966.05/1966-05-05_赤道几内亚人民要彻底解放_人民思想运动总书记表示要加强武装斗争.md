Title: Equatorial Guinean People Must Be Thoroughly Liberated: General Secretary of the People's Ideological Movement Says Armed Struggle Must Be Strengthened
Author:
Date: 1966-05-05
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, Electric Dispatch: The "People's Ideological Movement of Equatorial Guinea" recently published a press communique in Conakry, signed by General Secretary Jesus Mba Ovono, stating that the people of Equatorial Guinea "are determined to eliminate all vestiges of colonialism in our country and continue to create the necessary conditions to increasingly strengthen the armed struggle" in order to strive for thorough liberation.
    The communique stated: "Spanish colonialists and imperialists can never undermine the firm resolve of our people to continue fighting with all the means at their disposal for the thorough liberation of Equatorial Guinea."
    The communique stated: "Although we do not have the same weapons and equipment as our oppressors, we have the most powerful weapon: the determination of our people to regain their independence and dignity."

### 赤道几内亚人民要彻底解放  人民思想运动总书记表示要加强武装斗争

1966-05-05
第6版()
专栏：

    赤道几内亚人民要彻底解放
    人民思想运动总书记表示要加强武装斗争
    新华社科纳克里电　“赤道几内亚人民思想运动”最近在科纳克里发表一项由总书记耶苏·姆巴·奥沃诺签署的新闻公报指出，赤道几内亚人民“决心在我国消灭一切殖民主义残余，继续创造必要的条件来日益加强武装斗争”，以争取彻底的解放。
    公报说：“西班牙殖民主义者和帝国主义者永远不能破坏我国人民用自己拥有的一切手段为争取赤道几内亚的彻底解放而继续进行斗争的坚强决心。”
    公报说：“虽然我们没有象我们的压迫者那样的武器装备，但是我们有最强大的武器：我们人民要求重新获得自己的独立和尊严的决心”。
