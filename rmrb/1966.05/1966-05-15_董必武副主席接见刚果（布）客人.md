Title: Vice Chairman Dong Biwu Receives Guests from Congo (Brazzaville)
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-15
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 14th – Vice Chairman Dong Biwu received Florent Maranda, the Judicial Secretary of the Congolese (Brazzaville) Trade Union Confederation, and all members of the Congolese (Brazzaville) Trade Union Confederation delegation led by him this afternoon. They had a cordial and friendly conversation.
    Also present at the meeting were Gu Dachun and others. (Attached picture)
    The picture shows Vice Chairman Dong Biwu meeting with Florent Maranda (third from left), the Judicial Secretary of the Congolese (Brazzaville) Trade Union Confederation, and a group photo of the Congolese (Brazzaville) Trade Union Confederation delegation led by him.
                                        Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Bin
