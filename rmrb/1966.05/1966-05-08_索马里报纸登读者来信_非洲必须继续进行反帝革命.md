Title: Somali Newspaper Publishes Reader's Letter: Africa Must Continue the Anti-Imperialist Revolution
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-08
Edition: 6

    According to a dispatch from Xinhua News Agency, Mogadishu: The *Somali News* published a reader's letter on April 22 stating, "We must continue the revolution against imperialist oppression."

    The reader's letter said, "We must make ourselves independent economically and industrially."

    Commenting on the recent coups on the African continent, the letter stated, "The pernicious influence coming to Africa from overseas has played a significant role. The same hateful policies are repeatedly attempting to engulf other free African nations in order to exploit the entire continent once again."

### 索马里报纸登读者来信  非洲必须继续进行反帝革命

1966-05-08
第6版()
专栏：

    索马里报纸登读者来信
    非洲必须继续进行反帝革命
    据新华社摩加迪沙电　《索马里新闻》四月二十二日登载一封读者来信说：“我们必须继续进行反对帝国主义压迫的革命。”
    这封读者来信说：“我们必须使我们自己在经济和工业上独立。”
    这封信在评论非洲大陆最近发生的政变时说，“从海外来到非洲的恶劣影响起了重要的作用。同样的可恨的政策一再地企图吞没其他的非洲自由国家，以便再一次剥削整个大陆。”
