Title: Cambodian Weightlifting Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-05-11
Edition: 6

    According to Xinhua News Agency, the Cambodian weightlifting team, invited to China to participate in the 1966 Games of the New Emerging Forces Weightlifting Invitation Tournament, arrived in Beijing by plane on the evening of the 6th.
    The weightlifting team is led by team leader Sheng Ti and deputy team leader Kai Lai. Upon their arrival, they were warmly welcomed by Zhu Debao, Vice Chairman of the Organizing Committee of the 1966 Games of the New Emerging Forces Weightlifting Invitation Tournament and Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Sports Federation, and Chinese weightlifters.

### 柬埔寨举重队到京

1966-05-11
第6版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨举重队到京
    据新华社七日讯　应邀来我国参加一九六六年新兴力量举重邀请赛的柬埔寨举重队，已在六日晚上乘飞机到达北京。
    举重队由领队盛提和副领队凯莱率领。他们到达时，受到一九六六年新兴力量举重邀请赛组织委员会副主席、中华全国体育总会副秘书长朱德宝和我国举重运动员们的热烈欢迎。
