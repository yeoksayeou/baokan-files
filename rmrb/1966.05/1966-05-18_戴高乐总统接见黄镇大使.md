Title: President De Gaulle Receives Ambassador Huang Zhen
Author:
Date: 1966-05-18
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 16th -- French President General de Gaulle received Huang Zhen, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to France, at the Élysée Palace this afternoon.
    They had a friendly conversation.

### 戴高乐总统接见黄镇大使

1966-05-18
第6版()
专栏：

    戴高乐总统接见黄镇大使
    新华社巴黎十六日电　法国总统戴高乐将军今天下午在爱丽舍宫接见了中华人民共和国驻法大使黄镇。
    他们进行了友好的交谈。
