Title: Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks Hold 130th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1966-05-27
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, May 25th: The 130th meeting of the Sino-US ambassadorial talks was held in Warsaw at 2:00 PM this afternoon. The meeting lasted for two hours and fifty minutes.
    After the meeting, both sides announced that the next meeting would be held at 2:00 PM on September 7th.

### 中美大使级会谈举行第一百三十次会议

1966-05-27
第6版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈举行第一百三十次会议
    新华社华沙二十五日电　中美大使级会谈第一百三十次会议，二十五日下午二时在华沙举行，这次会议历时两小时五十分。
    会后双方宣布，下次会议在九月七日下午二时举行。
