Title: Indian Prime Minister's Anti-China Rhetoric Reaches Absurd Levels; Fabricates Rumors that Severe Famine in India is Caused by China and Pakistan; Parliamentary Group of the Congress Party Cheers for the Indonesian Right-Wing Military Regime
Author:
Date: 1966-05-10
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, May 9th, New Delhi Dispatch: Indian Prime Minister Indira Gandhi, at a rally in Maharashtra on the 7th, once again issued anti-China clamor, slandering China as a "threat" to India and attacking China and Pakistan for "imposing war on India." She also viciously attributed the severe food crisis in India, caused by the Indian government's reactionary policies, to China and Pakistan. She said that China and Pakistan's "aggression" and "invasion" of India have "disrupted India's plans to improve agricultural production."

    She admitted that severe famines exist in some states, including Orissa, Gujarat, Maharashtra, and Mysore. She stated that the Indian government will continue to adopt "methods of domestic procurement and large-scale food imports" to deal with the famine and quell the anger of the Indian people.

    A report by the United News of India shows that the Parliamentary Group of the Indian National Congress recently also brazenly attacked China and Pakistan in its 1965-1966 annual report to the Executive Committee of the Congress Party, falsely accusing China of "occupying Indian territory" and threatening that "Sino-Indian relations will continue to deteriorate and become tense," and "may ignite a war of larger conflict." When discussing the Vietnam issue, this report shamelessly serves the "peace talks" swindle of U.S. imperialism. It said that efforts made "to resolve the conflict in order to bring peace to Vietnam" "will receive our full support."

    The report expressed joy at the reactionary forces in Indonesia seizing power and said that "the emergence of a new government in Indonesia will improve relations with our country."

### 印度总理反华叫嚣已到语无伦次地步  竟造谣说印度国内严重粮荒是中巴造成的  国大党议会党团为印尼右派军人政权叫好

1966-05-10
第6版()
专栏：

    印度总理反华叫嚣已到语无伦次地步
    竟造谣说印度国内严重粮荒是中巴造成的
    国大党议会党团为印尼右派军人政权叫好
    新华社九日讯　新德里消息：印度总理英迪拉·甘地夫人七日在马哈拉施特拉邦的一次集会上再一次发出反华叫嚣，诬蔑中国“威胁”印度，攻击中国和巴基斯坦“把战争强加于印度”。她还恶毒地把由于印度政府的反动政策而造成国内的严重粮食危机，说成是中国和巴基斯坦造成的。她说，由于中国和巴基斯坦对印度的“侵略”和“入侵”，“打乱了印度提高农业生产的计划”。
    她承认在包括奥里萨、古吉拉特、马哈拉施特拉和迈索尔的一些邦都存在严重的粮荒。她表示，印度政府将继续采取“在国内收购和大规模进口粮食的办法”来对付粮荒，以平息印度人民的愤怒。
    印度联合新闻社的一则报道表明，印度国大党议会党团最近在向国大党执行委员会提出的一九六五——一九六六年度报告中也放肆地攻击中国和巴基斯坦，诬蔑中国“占领印度领土”，并且威胁说，“印中关系将继续恶化和紧张”，而且“可能引起较大的冲突的战火”。这则报告在谈到越南问题时，毫不掩饰地为美帝国主义的“和谈”骗局效劳。它说，在“为了解决冲突以给越南带来和平”方面所作出的努力，“将得到我们的全力支持”。
    报告对印度尼西亚反动势力夺取政权表示高兴，并且说，“在印度尼西亚一个新政府的出现将改善同我国的关系”。
