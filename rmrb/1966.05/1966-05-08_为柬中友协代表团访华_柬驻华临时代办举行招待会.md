Title: Cambodian Chargé d'Affaires in China Holds Reception for Cambodian-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation's Visit to China
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-08
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Cambodian Chargé d'Affaires in China, Li Jun, and his wife, held a reception this evening for the Cambodian-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, led by the chairman of the Cambodian-Chinese Friendship Association, Leng Ngeth, on their visit to China.
    Vice Premier of the State Council, Tan Zhenlin, was invited to attend the reception.
    Leng Ngeth said in his speech at the reception that the delegation's visit to Beijing received a sincere and warm welcome from the Chinese government and the Chinese people. In the meetings and conversations between the delegation and Chinese leaders, it was agreed that the people of Cambodia and China should join hands to oppose the war provocations and acts of aggression carried out by our common enemy, US imperialism and its lackeys, in Asia, Africa, and Latin America. He also expressed his admiration for the achievements made by the Chinese people under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong in the struggle to liberate and build their own country.
    Zhang Hanfu, President of the China-Cambodia Friendship Association, praised the achievements made by the Cambodian people in opposing foreign interference and safeguarding national independence in his speech. He said, "The delegation will also visit other parts of our country. You will see the Chinese people's friendly feelings and firm support for the Cambodian people." He expressed that the China-Cambodia friendship associations of both countries should join hands to enhance the friendship between the two peoples and contribute to opposing the common enemy of both countries, US imperialism and its lackeys.
    Ding Xilin, Zhou Yiping, Lian Guan, Wang Demao, Song Yiping, Huang Shuze, Shen Zijiu and others were invited to attend the reception.
    Prince Norodom Naradipo, who is studying in Beijing, also attended the reception.

### 为柬中友协代表团访华  柬驻华临时代办举行招待会

1966-05-08
第5版()
专栏：

    为柬中友协代表团访华
    柬驻华临时代办举行招待会
    新华社七日讯　柬埔寨驻中国大使馆临时代办李军和夫人，今晚为柬中友好协会主席兰·涅特率领的柬中友好协会代表团访华举行招待会。
    国务院副总理谭震林应邀出席了招待会。
    兰·涅特在招待会上讲话，他说，代表团在北京的访问，受到了中国政府和中国人民真诚的热烈的欢迎。代表团同中国领导人会见和交谈中认为，柬中两国人民要携起手来，反对我们共同的敌人美帝国主义及其仆从在亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲进行的战争挑衅和侵略活动。他还对于中国人民在毛泽东主席领导下，在解放和建设自己祖国的斗争中所取得的成就表示赞赏。
    中柬友好协会会长章汉夫在讲话中赞扬了柬埔寨人民在反对外来干涉、维护民族独立方面作出的成就。他说，代表团还要到我国其他地方参观访问，你们可以看到中国人民对柬埔寨人民的友好感情和坚决支持。他表示，中柬两国友协应当携起手来，增强两国人民的友谊，为反对两国共同敌人美帝国主义及其仆从作出贡献。
    应邀出席招待会的，有丁西林、周一萍、连贯、王德茂、宋一平、黄树则、沈兹九等。
    在北京学习的纳拉迪波王子也出席了招待会。
