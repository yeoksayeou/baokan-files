Title: Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association Hosts Banquet for Four Chinese Delegations; Kellezi and Pei Lisheng Exchange Toasts to the Two Countries' Militant Friendship
Author:
Date: 1966-05-26
Edition: 3

    According to a dispatch from Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, May 24th, the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association held a grand banquet in Tirana on the evening of the 23rd to welcome the Chinese Academy of Sciences delegation, the shipping delegation, the agricultural delegation, and the photography delegation, who are currently on a friendly visit to Albania.
    Kellezi, Chairman of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association, warmly praised the militant friendship and fraternal cooperation between the Albanian and Chinese people at the banquet. He said, "Albanian-Chinese friendship is a great, unbreakable, and eternal friendship. It is a militant, revolutionary friendship because this friendship is based on and built on the principles of Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism; it was forged and tempered by our two glorious parties and their leaders, Comrade Enver Hoxha and Comrade Mao Zedong, in the common revolutionary struggle for the cause of socialism and communism in our two countries and the world, against imperialism headed by U.S. imperialism and modern revisionism centered on the Soviet Communist Party leadership group.”
    He warmly congratulated China on the successful third nuclear explosion with thermonuclear materials. He pointed out: "This is not only a brilliant victory for the Chinese people, but also for the Albanian people and all revolutionary and peace-loving people who are fighting for freedom, independence, peace, and socialism."
    Pei Lisheng, head of the Chinese Academy of Sciences delegation, spoke at the banquet on behalf of all members of the four Chinese delegations. He expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the Albanian people and the Party and government leaders at all levels in Albania for their warm reception to the delegation. He said, "The great friendship and militant unity between China and Albania, personally forged by our two great leaders, Mao Zedong and Enver Hoxha, will surely generate enormous power, emit more brilliant light, and will surely promote the world revolutionary cause to advance from victory to greater victory."
    The banquet was filled with a cordial and friendly atmosphere. The guests repeatedly toasted to Sino-Albanian friendship and to the good health of Comrade Mao Zedong and Comrade Hoxha.
    At the banquet, Albanian artists sang revolutionary songs from both countries, such as "A Rifle in One Hand, A Pickaxe in the Other" and "Sailing the Seas Depends on the Helmsman."

### 阿中友协欢宴我四个代表团  凯莱齐和裴丽生互颂两国战斗友谊

1966-05-26
第3版()
专栏：

    阿中友协欢宴我四个代表团
    凯莱齐和裴丽生互颂两国战斗友谊
    据新华社地拉那二十四日电　阿尔巴尼亚中国友好协会二十三日晚在地拉那举行盛大宴会，欢迎正在阿尔巴尼亚进行友好访问的中国科学院代表团、航运代表团、农业代表团和摄影代表团。
    阿尔巴尼亚中国友好协会主席凯莱齐在宴会上热烈赞扬阿中两国人民之间的战斗友谊和兄弟合作。他说，“阿中友谊是伟大的、牢不可破的、永恒的友谊。是战斗的革命友谊。因为这一友谊是根据和建立在马克思列宁主义和无产阶级国际主义原则基础之上的；是在为我们两国和世界的社会主义和共产主义事业、反对以美帝国主义为首的帝国主义和以苏共领导集团为中心的现代修正主义的共同的革命斗争中，由我们两个光荣的党及其领袖恩·霍查同志和毛泽东同志缔造和锤炼起来的。”
    他热烈祝贺中国带有热核材料的第三次核爆炸成功。他指出：“这不仅是中国人民的、而且是阿尔巴尼亚人民以及为自由、独立、和平与社会主义而斗争的所有革命和爱好和平人民的辉煌胜利。”
    中国科学院代表团团长裴丽生代表中国四个代表团的全体团员在宴会上讲话。他对阿尔巴尼亚人民和阿尔巴尼亚各级党政领导人对代表团的热情接待，表示衷心的感谢。他说，“由我们两国伟大的领袖毛泽东和恩·霍查亲手缔造的中阿两国的伟大友谊和战斗团结，一定会产生巨大的威力，放出更加灿烂的光辉，必将推动世界革命事业从胜利走向更大的胜利。”
    宴会充满了亲切友好的气氛。宾主一再为中阿友谊、为毛泽东同志和霍查同志的身体健康干杯。
    宴会上，阿尔巴尼亚艺术家演唱了《一手拿枪、一手拿镐》和《大海航行靠舵手》等两国的革命歌曲。
