Title: Sudanese Chief Justice Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-27
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 26th. Babiker Awadalla, the Chief Justice of Sudan, and his wife, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the China Political Science and Law Society for a visit to our country.
    Among those who welcomed them at the airport were Wu Defeng, Zhang Zhirang, Wang Jiren, Zhang Tiesheng, Shen Zijiu, Sha Mengbi and other responsible persons from relevant departments.

### 苏丹首席大法官到京

1966-05-27
第5版()
专栏：

    苏丹首席大法官到京
    新华社二十六日讯　应中国政治法律学会邀请来我国访问的苏丹首席大法官巴比克尔·阿瓦达拉和夫人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有吴德峰、张志让、王吉仁、张铁生、沈兹九、沙梦弼等有关方面负责人。
