Title: Romanian Party and Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing En Route Home; Comrades Zhou Enlai and Others Meet and Host Banquet for Romanian Comrades
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-12
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, May 11th: Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee and Premier of the State Council; Chen Yi and Li Xiannian, members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premiers of the State Council, this evening met with Emil Bodnăraș, member of the Executive Committee of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee, member of the Standing Presidium of the Central Committee, and First Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and all members of the Romanian Party and Government Delegation he led on a visit to Vietnam.

    The delegation members attending the meeting were: Paul Niculescu-Mizil, member of the Executive Committee of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee and Secretary of the Central Secretariat; Vasile Vlad, alternate member of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee and Director of the Central International Department; General Ion Ioniță, member of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee and Deputy Minister of the Armed Forces; Vasile Gliga, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, etc.

    Also present at the meeting were Zhao Yimin, alternate member of the CPC Central Committee; Qiao Guanhua, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Peng Shaohui, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, etc.

    Aurel Duma, Romanian Ambassador to China, was also present.

    Xinhua News Agency, May 11th: Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee and Premier of the State Council; Chen Yi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, hosted a banquet this evening to welcome Emil Bodnăraș, member of the Executive Committee of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee, member of the Standing Presidium of the Central Committee, and First Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and the Romanian Party and Government Delegation he led on a visit to Vietnam.

    Comrade Zhou Enlai and Comrade Bodnăraș toasted at the banquet, proposing a toast to the friendship between the Chinese and Romanian parties and the people of the two countries.

    Attending the banquet were Li Xiannian, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, as well as Zhao Yimin, Qiao Guanhua, Peng Shaohui, Cheng Zhaoxuan, Lu Ping, Cui Yueli, and other relevant responsible persons.

    Aurel Duma, Romanian Ambassador to China, and all diplomatic officials of the embassy were also invited to attend the banquet.

    Xinhua News Agency, May 11th: The Romanian Party and Government Delegation led by Emil Bodnăraș, member of the Executive Committee of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee, member of the Standing Presidium of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian Socialist Republic, and First Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers, arrived in Beijing by special plane at noon today en route home after visiting the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

    Chen Yi and Li Xiannian, members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premiers of the State Council; Liu Xiao, member of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Zhao Yimin, alternate member of the CPC Central Committee; Peng Shaohui, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Cui Yueli, Vice Mayor of Beijing; Lu Ping, President of the China-Romania Friendship Association, and others, greeted the distinguished Romanian guests at the airport. Young Pioneers of the capital presented flowers to the distinguished guests.

    Aurel Duma, Romanian Ambassador to China; Tran Tu Binh, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China; and Tran Van Thanh, head of the Permanent Delegation of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front to China, also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

    When the Romanian Party and Government Delegation passed through Nanning today, it was warmly welcomed and seen off at the airport by Qiao Xiaoguang, Second Secretary of the CPC Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Committee, Lu Shaowu, Vice Chairman of the Autonomous Region, and responsible persons of the Party, government, and army of the Autonomous Region and Nanning City. ### 罗马尼亚党政代表团归国途中到京  周恩来等同志会见宴请罗马尼亚同志

1966-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚党政代表团归国途中到京
    周恩来等同志会见宴请罗马尼亚同志
    新华社十一日讯　中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来，中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅、李先念，今天晚间会见了罗马尼亚共产党中央执行委员会委员、中央常设主席团委员、部长会议第一副主席埃米尔·波德纳拉希，和由他率领访问越南的罗马尼亚党政代表团全体团员。
    参加会见的代表团团员是：罗马尼亚共产党中央执行委员会委员、中央书记处书记保罗·尼古列斯库—米齐尔，罗马尼亚共产党中央候补委员、中央国际部部长瓦西里·弗拉德，罗马尼亚共产党中央委员、武装部队部副部长扬·约尼策将军，外交部副部长瓦西里·格利加等。
    会见时在场的，有中共中央候补委员赵毅敏，外交部副部长乔冠华，中国人民解放军副总参谋长彭绍辉等。
    罗马尼亚驻中国大使奥列尔·杜马也在场。
    新华社十一日讯　中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来，中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅今晚设宴，欢迎罗马尼亚共产党中央执行委员会委员、中央常设主席团委员、部长会议第一副主席埃米尔·波德纳拉希，和由他率领访问越南的罗马尼亚党政代表团。
    周恩来同志和波德纳拉希同志在宴会上祝酒，他们提议为中罗两党和两国人民的友谊而干杯。
    出席宴会作陪的，有中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理李先念，以及赵毅敏、乔冠华、彭绍辉、程照轩、陆平、崔月犁等有关方面的负责人。
    罗马尼亚驻中国大使奥列尔·杜马和使馆全体外交官员，也应邀出席了宴会。
    新华社十一日讯　由罗马尼亚共产党中央执行委员会委员、中央常设主席团委员、罗马尼亚社会主义共和国部长会议第一副主席埃米尔·波德纳拉希率领的罗马尼亚党政代表团，在越南民主共和国访问后归国途中，今天中午乘专机到达北京。
    中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅、李先念，中共中央委员、外交部副部长刘晓，中共中央候补委员赵毅敏，中国人民解放军副总参谋长彭绍辉，北京市副市长崔月犁，中国罗马尼亚友好协会会长陆平等，到机场欢迎罗马尼亚贵宾。首都少先队员向贵宾们献了花。
    罗马尼亚驻中国大使奥列尔·杜马，越南民主共和国驻中国大使陈子平，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长陈文成也到机场欢迎。
    罗马尼亚党政代表团今天途经南宁时，在机场上受到了中共广西壮族自治区委员会第二书记乔晓光，自治区副主席卢绍武及自治区和南宁市党、政府、军队负责人的热烈欢迎和欢送。（附图片）
    五月十一日，中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来，中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅、李先念会见罗马尼亚共产党中央执行委员会委员、中央常设主席团委员、部长会议第一副主席埃米尔·波德纳拉希（前左六）和由他率领的罗马尼亚党政代表团全体团员。　新华社记者　王敬德摄
    五月十一日，由罗马尼亚共产党中央执行委员会委员、中央常设主席团委员、罗马尼亚社会主义共和国部长会议第一副主席埃米尔·波德纳拉希（右六）率领的罗马尼亚党政代表团，在越南民主共和国访问后归国途中，乘专机到达北京。陈毅、李先念等同志到机场欢迎。图为在机场合影。　新华社记者　张　彬摄
