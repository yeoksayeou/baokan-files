Title: Japan-China Friendship Association Protests New Provocation of American Planes Intruding into Chinese Airspace
Author:
Date: 1966-05-14
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, May 13th – The Japan-China Friendship Association, on May 13th, submitted a letter of protest to U.S. President Johnson through the U.S. Embassy in Japan, condemning the provocative act of five American fighter planes intruding into Chinese airspace and shooting down a Chinese training plane.
    The protest letter expressed opposition to the U.S.'s policy of "containing" China and opposed the U.S.'s wild expansion of aggressive activities in Asia, completely disregarding the peaceful aspirations of the Japanese people and other Asian countries.
    The protest letter demanded that the U.S. military immediately withdraw from Japan and the whole of Asia.
    Representatives from the New Japan Women's Association, including Ayako Ishii, along with five others, also submitted a letter of protest to the U.S. Embassy in Tokyo regarding the intrusion of American planes into Chinese airspace, demanding that the U.S. immediately stop its aggression in Vietnam and swiftly cease its barbaric attacks on China, Cambodia, and Laos.

### 日中友协抗议美机侵入中国领空的新挑衅

1966-05-14
第6版()
专栏：

    日中友协抗议美机侵入中国领空的新挑衅
    新华社十三日讯　东京消息：日中友好协会十三日通过美国驻日本大使馆向美国总统约翰逊提出了一份抗议书，谴责五架美国战斗机侵入中国领空并且打下一架中国训练机的挑衅活动。
    抗议书表示反对美国的“遏制”中国政策，反对美国完全不顾日本和亚洲其它各国人民的和平愿望而在亚洲猖狂地扩大侵略活动。
    抗议书要求美军立即撤出日本和整个亚洲。
    新日本妇女会的代表石井绫子等六人也就美国飞机侵入中国领空向东京的美国大使馆提出抗议书，要求美国立即停止侵略越南和迅速停止对中国、柬埔寨和老挝的野蛮袭击。
