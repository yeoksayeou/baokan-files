Title: Chen Yi, Vice Premier, Receives Canadian Guests
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-06
Edition: 5

    Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Canadian Guests
    Xinhua News Agency, May 5th: Vice Premier Chen Yi received the delegation of the Canada-China Friendship Association led by Andrew Chou this morning and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Ren Yinglun, Ren Hong, Guo Kun, and responsible persons from relevant departments. (Picture attached)
    May 5th, Vice Premier Chen Yi receives the delegation of the Canada-China Friendship Association led by Andrew Chou (front, third from the left). Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Changzhong.

### 陈毅副总理接见加拿大客人

1966-05-06
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见加拿大客人
    新华社五日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见由安德鲁·周率领的加拿大—中国友好协会代表团，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有任映仑、任宏、郭坤和有关方面负责人。（附图片）
    五月五日，陈毅副总理接见由安德鲁·周（前左三）率领的加拿大—中国友好协会代表团。　新华社记者　刘长忠摄
