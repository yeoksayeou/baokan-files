Title: South Vietnamese Guerrillas Display Great Power, Heavily Strike American Aggressors; Guerrillas in Quang Ngai, Quang Nam, Quang Tri Provinces Maneuver Flexibly, Annihilating Large Numbers of American and Puppet Troops; People's Armed Forces of Binh Dinh Province Summarize Experiences in Guerrilla Warfare, Resolve to Continue Efforts to Strengthen Combat, Defend and Expand Liberated Areas
Author:
Date: 1966-05-18
Edition: 6

    According to a Hanoi dispatch from Xinhua News Agency on the 16th, guerrillas in Quang Ngai, Quang Nam, Quang Tri, and other provinces of South Vietnam continue to annihilate many enemy forces.
    The guerrillas of Binh Son District, Quang Ngai Province, near the Chu Lai U.S. military base, launched 32 attacks in the 20 days from March 28 to April 17, annihilating a total of 273 enemies, including 204 killed or wounded U.S. Marines, destroying two M-113 amphibious armored vehicles and two other military vehicles, and seizing a large number of weapons and ammunition.
    From April 26 to 28, the guerrillas of Que Tra Commune, Tam Ky District, Quang Nam Province, successively ambushed two battalions of U.S. Marines who came to "mop up," killing or wounding 38 enemies. On the night of April 29 and the early morning of April 30, the guerrillas of Tien Phuoc District suddenly attacked the Ba Hoa "strategic hamlet" and intercepted two enemy companies that came to rescue, killing 80 on the spot, seizing a batch of weapons, and destroying the Ba Hoa "strategic hamlet."
    On April 7, 13, and 25, the Liberation Armed Forces of Quang Tri Province and the local forces and guerrillas of Gio Linh and Huong Hoa districts annihilated 63 enemies.
    On April 30, the armed forces of Thua Thien Province intercepted a battalion of enemy troops launching a "sweep" of Phu Ninh Village, Phong Thai Commune, killing or wounding 74 enemies, including one puppet army captain and two puppet army lieutenants, and seizing a batch of weapons. On April 28 and 29, the guerrillas of Huong Tra District continuously attacked enemy troops in Huong Can Commune, wiping out one enemy platoon and seizing a batch of weapons.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 17th. According to a report from the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency, Binh Dinh Province in South Vietnam recently held a conference to summarize the experience of people's guerrilla warfare, and decided to carry forward the victories already achieved, more actively build guerrilla teams and combat villages, defend and expand the liberated areas, and thoroughly defeat the American aggressors and their running dogs.
    The conference pointed out that since 1965, especially in the first three months of this year, the people's guerrilla warfare in Binh Dinh Province has developed extensively and vigorously, consuming and annihilating a large number of enemy forces, especially the American aggressor troops. In the campaign to kill the enemy and perform meritorious service, many townships, villages, and guerrilla fighters have been awarded the honorary titles of "Heroic Annihilator of the Americans" and "Warrior to Annihilate the Americans" for their brilliant combat achievements.
    The conference pointed out that wherever the American aggressor troops carry out massacres, arson, and plunder, the people hate them even more, and the people's guerrilla warfare develops more vigorously.
    The conference commended a large number of guerrillas who had grown up in combat and made brilliant combat achievements. A Qing, a 19-year-old guerrilla from Hoai Nhon District, annihilated 17 American aggressor troops and shot down an American helicopter in one battle. A four-person guerrilla group led by A S of Ping An Township, Ping Khe District, fought bravely for a whole day in an anti-"sweep" battle, repelling many attacks by a large number of South Korean mercenary troops, and annihilating 41 enemies. A S himself killed 19 enemies. Another guerrilla fighter, A Nian, from Hoai Nhon District, together with two Liberation Army fighters, defended Height No. 10 in a battle, repelling ten enemy attacks in a row and killing 80 American aggressor troops. Finally, they threw the only three remaining grenades at the enemy, then stood tall on the position, singing the war song "Liberate the South" and heroically sacrificed themselves.
    The conference also commended many heroic female soldiers. Among them was A Tao, a female guerrilla from a township in Ping Khe District, who led a four-person team across the river at night in one battle, using grenades to destroy an enemy military vehicle, killing four enemies.

### 南越游击队大显神威沉重打击美国侵略者  广义广南广治等省游击队机动灵活歼灭美伪大批有生力量  平定省人民武装总结游击战争经验决定再接再厉加强战斗保卫和扩大解放区

1966-05-18
第6版()
专栏：

    南越游击队大显神威沉重打击美国侵略者
    广义广南广治等省游击队机动灵活歼灭美伪大批有生力量
    平定省人民武装总结游击战争经验决定再接再厉加强战斗保卫和扩大解放区
    据新华社河内十六日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方广义、广南、广治等省游击队继续歼灭敌人许多有生力量。
    广义省靠近朱莱美军基地的平山县游击队，从三月二十八日到四月十七日的二十天中，共出击三十二次，歼灭敌人二百七十三名，其中包括打死打伤美国海军陆战队二百零四名，炸毁M—113型两栖装甲车和其他军车各两辆，缴获大批武器和弹药。
    广南省三岐县岐茶乡游击队，从四月二十六日到二十八日接连狙击前来“扫荡”的美国海军陆战队两个营，共打死、打伤敌人三十八名。先福县游击队在四月二十九日夜间到三十日清晨突然袭击巴和“战略村”，同时截击前来救援的敌军两个连，当场打死八十名，缴获一批武器，还捣毁了巴和“战略村”。
    广治省解放武装力量和犹灵县、香化县地方部队和游击队，在四月七日、十三日和二十五日，歼敌六十三名。
    承天省武装力量四月三十日截击向丰泰乡扶宁村发动“扫荡”的一营敌军，打死、打伤敌人七十四名，其中打死一名伪军上尉和两名伪军中尉，缴获一批武器。香茶县游击队在二十八日和二十九日两天中，连续袭击香芹乡的敌军，全歼敌人一个排，缴获一批武器。
    新华社河内十七日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方平定省最近召开总结人民游击战争经验的会议，决定发扬已经取得的胜利，更加积极地建设游击队和战斗乡，保卫和扩大解放区，彻底打败美国侵略者和它的走狗。
    会议指出，从一九六五年以来，特别是在今年头三个月里，平定省的人民游击战争有了广泛和蓬勃的发展，消耗和歼灭了敌人、特别是美国侵略军的大批有生力量。在杀敌立功运动中，有许多乡、村和游击队员，由于创立了辉煌的战功，分别获得了“英勇歼美”单位和“歼美勇士”的光荣称号。
    会议指出，美国侵略军在哪里大肆烧杀抢掠，哪里的人民就更加仇恨他们，人民游击战争就更加蓬勃发展。
    会议表扬了大批在战斗中锻炼成长、并立下辉煌战功的游击队员。怀仁县十九岁的游击队员阿清，在一次战斗中，歼灭了美国侵略军十七名，打落美国直升飞机一架。平溪县平安乡阿S领导的一个四人游击小组，在一次反“扫荡”战斗中，连续英勇战斗了一整天，击退大批南朝鲜帮凶军的多次进攻，歼灭敌人四十一名。仅阿S自己一人就打死敌人十九名。怀仁县的另一名游击队员阿念，在一次战斗中，同两名解放军战士一起，坚守第十号高地，连续打退了敌人的十次进攻，打死美国侵略军八十名。最后，他们把仅剩下的三个手雷，一齐投向敌人，然后巍然挺立在阵地上，引吭高唱《解放南方》的战歌，壮烈牺牲。
    会议还表扬了许多英雄的女战士。其中有平溪县某乡的女游击队员阿桃，她在一次战斗中领导一个四人小组夜渡江河，用手榴弹炸毁敌人一辆军车，炸死敌人四名。
