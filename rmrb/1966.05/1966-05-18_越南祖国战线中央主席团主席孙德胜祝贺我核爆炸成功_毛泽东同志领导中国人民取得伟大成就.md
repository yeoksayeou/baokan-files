Title: Sun Desheng, Chairman of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front, Congratulates China on Successful Nuclear Explosion. Comrade Mao Zedong Leads the Chinese People to Achieve Great Success
Author:
Date: 1966-05-18
Edition: 5

    Vietnam Fatherland Front Central Committee Presidium Chairman Ton Duc Thang Congratulates China on its Successful Nuclear Explosion
    Comrade Mao Zedong Leads the Chinese People to Achieve Great Success
    Xinhua News Agency, May 17th - Zhou Enlai, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, received a letter from Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front, congratulating China on the success of its third nuclear test. The full text of the congratulatory letter is as follows:

    To Comrade Zhou Enlai, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Beijing:
    On the occasion of China's successful conduct of a nuclear explosion containing thermonuclear materials, I, on behalf of the Vietnamese people and the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front, extend to you, and through you to the fraternal Chinese people, the Communist Party of China, the government, and the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, our warmest congratulations.
    This great achievement is the result of the wise line of the Communist Party of China headed by Comrade Mao Zedong, combined with the tenacious revolutionary will, the spirit of self-reliance, and the boundless creativity of the Chinese people.
    China's possession of increasingly advanced and improved nuclear weapons is to strengthen the national defense of the People's Republic of China, to oppose the nuclear threat, aggression and provocative policies of U.S. imperialism, and is a great contribution by the Chinese people and government to strengthening the socialist camp and to the cause of national liberation struggles and the defense of world peace.
    The Vietnamese people are infinitely excited by the new great achievements of the fraternal Chinese people. This is a great encouragement to our people who are waging a struggle against U.S. aggression for national salvation to liberate the South, defend the North, and proceed to the peaceful reunification of the motherland.
    The Vietnamese people once again express their full support for the consistent fundamental position of the People's Republic of China on the issue of nuclear weapons and regard this position as the correct way to safeguard China's security and world peace.
    Please convey our warmest congratulations to the officers and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army, scientists, engineers, scientific and technical cadres, and Chinese workers.

    Ton Duc Thang
    Chairman of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front
    Hanoi, May 10, 1966

### 越南祖国战线中央主席团主席孙德胜祝贺我核爆炸成功  毛泽东同志领导中国人民取得伟大成就

1966-05-18
第5版()
专栏：

    越南祖国战线中央主席团主席孙德胜祝贺我核爆炸成功
    毛泽东同志领导中国人民取得伟大成就
    新华社十七日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会主席周恩来收到了越南祖国战线中央委员会主席团主席孙德胜发来的一封信，祝贺我国第三次核试验成功。贺信全文如下：北京中国人民政治协商会议全国委
  员会主席周恩来同志：
    在中国成功地进行了一次含有热核材料的核爆炸的时候，我谨代表越南人民和越南祖国战线中央委员会向你，并通过你向兄弟的中国人民、中国共产党、政府和中国人民政治协商会议致最热烈的祝贺。
    这一伟大的成就是以毛泽东同志为首的中国共产党的英明路线同中国人民的顽强革命意志、自力更生精神和无限的创造力的结果。
    中国拥有日益提高和完善的核武器这件事，是为了增强中华人民共和国的国防力量，反对美帝国主义的核威胁政策、侵略和挑衅政策，是中国人民和政府对加强社会主义阵营力量作出了巨大的贡献，对民族解放斗争和保卫世界和平作出了贡献。
    越南人民对兄弟的中国人民取得的新的伟大成就感到无限兴奋。这件事对正在进行抗美救国斗争，以解放南方、保卫北方、进而和平统一祖国的我国人民是一个巨大的鼓舞。
    越南人民再一次表示完全支持中华人民共和国对核武器问题始终如一的根本立场，并把这个立场看作是保卫中国的安全和世界和平的正确途径。
    请你向解放军指战员、科学家、工程师、科学技术干部和中国工人转达最亲切的祝贺。
  越南祖国战线中央委员会
        主席团主席　孙德胜
  一九六六年五月十日于河内
