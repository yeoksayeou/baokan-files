Title: GDR Technical Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-31
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, May 30th -- The German Democratic Republic (GDR) Technical Association Delegation, invited by the Science and Technology Association of the People’s Republic of China for a visit to our country, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 29th. The delegation consists of five people and is led by Mr. Slunzch, Chairman of the Chemical Fiber and Photographic Chemical Industry Branch of the German Technical Association.
    Wang Shuntong, Secretary of the Secretariat of the China Association for Science and Technology, and others greeted them at the airport.



**Named Entities**


新华社 Xinhua News Agency
中国科学技术协会 Science and Technology Association of the People’s Republic of China
德意志民主共和国 German Democratic Republic (GDR)

中国科协 China Association for Science and Technology
王顺桐 Wang Shuntong

### 德技术协会代表团到京

1966-05-31
第6版()
专栏：

    德技术协会代表团到京
    新华社三十日讯  应中华人民共和国科学技术协会邀请来我国访问的德意志民主共和国技术协会代表团一行五人，在团长、德国技术协会化学纤维及照象化学工业分会主席斯伦茨希率领下，已在二十九日乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场迎接的有中国科协书记处书记王顺桐等。
