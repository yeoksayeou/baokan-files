Title: Romanian Journalists Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-05-07
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 6th of May -- A five-member Romanian journalists delegation, at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association, arrived in Beijing today, led by Nestor Ignat, Chairman of the Romanian Journalists Association. The delegation members include: Vasili Rausel, Editor-in-Chief of the magazine *Economic Problems*, Jan Gherghe, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of *Free Romania Daily*, Jan Alexandru, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of Romanian Foreign Magazines Publishing House, and Ion Vodua, Head of the Economic Section of *Labor Daily*.
    Welcoming them at the airport were Wu Lengxi, Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, First Secretary Wang Yi, and Zhou Xinwu, Xing Fangqun, Mu Xin, Yao Zhongwen and other responsible persons from the capital's press circles.
    Also at the airport to welcome the Romanian guests was Bi Jilong, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
    Betrescu and Cosma, Second Secretaries of the Romanian Embassy in China, were also at the airport to greet them.

### 罗马尼亚新闻工作者代表团到京

1966-05-07
第5版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚新闻工作者代表团到京
    新华社六日讯　罗马尼亚新闻工作者代表团一行五人，应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请，在团长、罗马尼亚新闻工作者协会主席内斯托尔·伊格纳特率领下，今日到达北京。代表团成员有：《经济问题》杂志主编瓦西里·劳塞尔，《自由罗马尼亚报》副主编扬·格尔热，罗马尼亚对外杂志出版社副主编扬·亚历山德鲁，《劳动报》经济组组长伊昂·沃杜瓦。
    到机场欢迎的有：中华全国新闻工作者协会主席吴冷西、第一书记王揖以及首都新闻界负责人周新武、邢方群、穆欣、姚仲文等。
    到机场欢迎罗马尼亚客人的还有外交部新闻司副司长毕季龙。
    罗马尼亚驻华使馆二等秘书贝特勒斯库和考斯玛也到机场迎接。
