Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Sends Telegram of Congratulations on the 21st Anniversary of Czechoslovakia's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1966-05-09
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 8th. Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram to Czechoslovak President Novotny on the 8th, congratulating him on the 21st anniversary of Czechoslovakia's liberation. The full text of the congratulatory telegram is as follows:
To His Excellency Antonín Novotný, President of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic, Prague:
    On the occasion of the 21st anniversary of Czechoslovakia's liberation, I, on behalf of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people, extend warm congratulations to you and to the Czechoslovak people.
    I wish the Czechoslovak people achievements in the cause of building their country. May the friendship between the people of China and Czechoslovakia continue to develop.
          Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                    May 8, 1966

### 刘少奇主席电贺捷解放二十一周年

1966-05-09
第5版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席电贺捷解放二十一周年
    新华社八日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇八日打电报给捷克斯洛伐克总统诺沃提尼，祝贺捷克斯洛伐克解放二十一周年。贺电全文如下：布拉格捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国安托宁·诺沃提
  尼总统：
    在捷克斯洛伐克解放二十一周年的时候，我代表中华人民共和国和中国人民向你，向捷克斯洛伐克人民致以热烈的祝贺。
    祝捷克斯洛伐克人民在建设自己国家的事业中取得成就。祝中国和捷克斯洛伐克两国人民的友谊不断发展。
          中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                    一九六六年五月八日
