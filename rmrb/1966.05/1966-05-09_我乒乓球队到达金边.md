Title: Our Table Tennis Team Arrives in Phnom Penh
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-09
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 7th. The Chinese men's and women's table tennis team, composed of well-known players such as Zhuang Zedong, Zhang Xielin, Lin Huiqing, and Di Qianghua, led by Guo Lei, Deputy Director of the International Department of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Phnom Penh by plane at noon on the 7th for a friendly visit.
    Qeum Sophon, Chairman of the Cambodian Reception Committee, and other people from the sports circles went to the airport to welcome them. Cambodian athletes presented flowers to the Chinese table tennis players at the airport. Chinese Ambassador Chen Shuliang and Yang Huisheng, Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy, also went to welcome them.

### 我乒乓球队到达金边

1966-05-09
第5版()
专栏：

    我乒乓球队到达金边
    新华社金边七日电由庄则栋、张燮林、林慧卿和狄蔷华等著名选手组成的中国男女乒乓球队，在中华人民共和国体育运动委员会国际司副司长郭雷率领下，七日中午乘飞机到达金边进行友好访问。
    前往机场欢迎的有柬埔寨接待委员会主席琼索丰和体育界其他人士。柬埔寨运动员在机场上向中国乒乓球运动员献了花。中国大使陈叔亮和中国大使馆文化参赞杨惠生也前往迎接。
