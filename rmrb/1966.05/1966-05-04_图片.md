Title: Wang Jingde
Author: Wang Jingde
Date: 1966-05-04
Edition: 4

    The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held a reception on May 3rd to welcome more than 800 overseas Chinese, Hong Kong and Macao compatriots, and delegations from 15 ethnic minorities representing over 30 ethnic groups who came to Beijing to participate in the "May 1st" International Labor Day celebrations. Chen Yi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Vice Premier of the State Council, and Vice Chairman of the CPPCC National Committee, and Ye Jianying, Vice Chairman of the CPPCC National Committee, attended the reception. This is a photograph of Vice Premier Chen Yi speaking at the reception. Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Jingde.
