Title: Our Government Civil Aviation Delegation Goes to Paris
Author:
Date: 1966-05-05
Edition: 5

    A Chinese government civil aviation delegation of seven people, led by Shen Tu, Director of the Civil Aviation Administration of China, left Beijing by plane today for Paris at the invitation of the French government to hold discussions on Sino-French air traffic issues.
    Among those seeing them off at the airport were Liu Jinping, Li Ming, Xie Li, and other responsible persons from relevant departments.
    Mr. Chayet, Counselor of the French Embassy in China, and Mr. Richard, Commercial Counselor, also saw them off at the airport.

### 我政府民用航空代表团去巴黎

1966-05-05
第5版()
专栏：

    我政府民用航空代表团去巴黎
    新华社四日讯　由团长、中国民用航空总局副局长沈图率领的中国政府民用航空代表团一行七人，应法国政府邀请，今天乘飞机离开北京前往巴黎，就中法航空交通问题进行商谈。
    到机场送行的有刘锦平、黎明、谢黎等有关方面负责人。
    法国驻中国大使馆参赞沙耶和商务参赞里沙尔也到机场送行。
