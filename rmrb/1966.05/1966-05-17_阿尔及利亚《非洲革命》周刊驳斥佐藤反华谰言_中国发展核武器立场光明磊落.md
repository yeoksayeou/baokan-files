Title: Algerian Weekly "African Revolution" Refutes Sato's Anti-China Slanders; China's Stand on Developing Nuclear Weapons is Open and Aboveboard
Author:
Date: 1966-05-17
Edition: 5

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Algiers, May 14th. The Algerian National Liberation Front's central organ publication, "African Revolution" weekly, published a commentary on May 14th refuting the slanders made by the Japanese government and others against China's recent nuclear tests, and pointing out that China's position on nuclear weapons is open and aboveboard.
    The weekly said: "Throughout the world, people are watching with admiration the path taken by Chinese technicians in science. In politics, there are obviously hostile reactions. We only cite one of these reactions, because its meaning is very clear, and it is issued by a government that has reduced its territory to an atomic military base serving American imperialism – the Japanese government. When protesting against China's new tests, Prime Minister Sato shamelessly quoted the proverb: 'He who uses force will be destroyed by force.' Who is using force today? Is it China or the United States that is attacking Vietnam, Cambodia, and Laos, rearming Japan, and encircling the Asian continent with military bases for the clear purpose of launching wars in Asia?"
    The weekly said: "We will only use a few extremely simple words to answer those sanctimonious people who express anger at China's development of nuclear weapons. That is: if I am threatened by a robber with a weapon, who has decided to kill me and my whole family, and the robber has said this, and is prepared to do it, and has even started attacking my brothers' houses, what should I do? Should I stand idly by? Should I wait for this attack to come to my own head? Or should I, on the contrary, take up arms, rescue my brothers, and be able to effectively deal with him when the robber tries to break into my house?"

### 阿尔及利亚《非洲革命》周刊驳斥佐藤反华谰言  中国发展核武器立场光明磊落

1966-05-17
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚《非洲革命》周刊驳斥佐藤反华谰言
    中国发展核武器立场光明磊落
    据新华社阿尔及尔十四日电　阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线中央机关刊《非洲革命》周刊十四日发表一篇评论，驳斥日本政府等对中国最近进行的核试验的诬蔑，并且指出，中国在核武器问题上的立场是光明磊落的。
    这家周刊说：“在全世界，人们怀着钦佩的心情注视着中国技术人员在科学方面所走的道路。在政治方面，显然有着敌对的反应。我们只举其中的一个反应，因为它的含意是十分明显的，而且是由一个把自己的领土沦为替美帝国主义效劳的原子军事基地的政府——日本政府所发出的。佐藤首相在抗议中国进行新的试验时恬不知耻地引用了这样一句谚语：‘使用武力者将被武力所毁灭’。今天到底是谁在使用武力呢？进攻越南、柬埔寨和老挝，重新武装日本，为了在亚洲发动战争的明显目的而用军事基地对亚洲大陆进行包围的，究竟是中国还是美国呢？”
    这家周刊说：“我们只用极为简单的几句话来回答那些对中国发展核武器表示愤怒的道貌岸然的人。这就是：如果我遭到一个带有武器的强盗的威胁，他决定要把我和我全家的人杀死，这个强盗这样说了，并且准备这样做，甚至已开始袭击我的兄弟家里，那么我应该怎么办呢？是不是袖手旁观呢？是不是等待这种袭击轮到我的头上呢？还是相反地拿起武器，援救我的兄弟，并且当那个强盗企图闯入我的家里的时候，能够有效地对付他呢？”
