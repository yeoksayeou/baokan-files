Title: African Public Warmly Congratulates China on Successful Nuclear Explosion; People of All Countries Rejoice, Imperialists Tremble

Author:
Date: 1966-05-16
Edition: 5

    (Dispatch of this newspaper) According to Xinhua News Agency, Mohammed Mfomi Omar, Vice Chairman of the Workers' Department of the Zanzibar Afro-Shirazi Party, wrote to the relevant Chinese departments on the 13th to congratulate China on the success of its third nuclear explosion.
    The congratulatory letter stated that China's nuclear explosion has further strengthened the confidence and determination of the people of Asia, Africa, and Latin America in their struggle against imperialism and colonialism and in building their countries. At the same time, "the entire imperialist forces are trembling before the great victories achieved by your people under the banner of socialist revolution."
    Aristides Pereira, Deputy Secretary-General of the African Independence Party of Guinea and Cape Verde, and Almada Fidel, Secretary of the Secretariat, told Xinhua reporters in Conakry on the 14th that the success of China's third nuclear explosion is a significant event, and for the people of "Portuguese" Guinea, who are fighting for liberation and independence, it is an effective and tremendous support.
    Abdullahi Hirsi, a member of the Somali National Assembly, congratulated China on its nuclear explosion on the 10th. He said, "This explosion is beneficial to world peace."
    Mohammed Dahan, a leading member of the Central Committee of the Somali Coast Liberation Front, and Ibrahim Dahir, the secretary of the Zella branch of this organization, went to the Chinese Embassy to congratulate China on its successful nuclear explosion. They said that the success of China's nuclear test has frightened the imperialists.
    Seif-el-Aziz Miras, Chairman of the Mozambique African National Union, who is currently visiting Sudan, told Xinhua reporters on the 12th, "This new achievement of China is an encouragement to the people of all countries who are fighting against colonialism and imperialist aggression; it is a warning to U.S. imperialism and its allies: they can no longer use the threat of nuclear destruction to blackmail the people of the world who are struggling."

### 非洲公众热烈祝贺中国核爆炸成功  各国人民欢庆　帝国主义发抖

1966-05-16
第5版()
专栏：

    非洲公众热烈祝贺中国核爆炸成功
    各国人民欢庆　帝国主义发抖
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯，桑给巴尔非洲—设拉子党工人部副主席穆罕默德·姆福米·奥马尔十三日写信给中国有关部门，祝贺中国第三次核爆炸成功。
    贺信说，中国核爆炸，进一步增强了亚非拉各国人民反帝反殖斗争和建设国家的信心和决心。同时，“整个帝国主义势力正在贵国人民在社会主义革命的旗帜下取得的巨大胜利面前发抖”。
    几内亚和佛得角非洲独立党副总书记阿里斯蒂德·佩雷拉和总书记处书记阿尔马达·菲德尔十四日在科纳克里向新华社记者发表谈话说，中国第三次核爆炸成功是一个具有重大意义的事件，对我们正在为解放和独立而斗争的“葡属”几内亚人民来说，这是个有效的巨大的支持。
    索马里国民议会议员阿卜杜拉齐兹·赫尔西十日祝贺中国的核爆炸。他说，“这次爆炸是有利于世界和平的。”
    索马里海岸解放阵线中央委员会领导成员穆罕默德·达汉和这个组织泽拉支部的书记易卜拉欣·达希尔到中国大使馆对中国成功地进行核爆炸表示祝贺。他们说，中国核试验的成功使帝国主义者吓坏了胆。
    正在苏丹访问的莫三鼻给非洲民族联盟主席塞伊夫—阿齐兹·米拉斯十二日对新华社记者说，“中国这个新的成就对正在为反对殖民主义和帝国主义侵略而斗争的各国人民是一个鼓舞；对美帝国主义及其同盟者是一个警告：他们再也不能用核毁灭的威胁来讹诈全世界正在进行斗争的人民了。”
