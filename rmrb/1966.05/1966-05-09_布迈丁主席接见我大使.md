Title: Chairman Boumedienne Receives Our Ambassador
Author:
Date: 1966-05-09
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Algiers, 6th – Houari Boumedienne, Chairman of the National Revolutionary Council and Prime Minister of Algeria, received Zeng Tao, the Chinese Ambassador to Algeria, here on the 6th and had a friendly conversation with him.

### 布迈丁主席接见我大使

1966-05-09
第5版()
专栏：

    布迈丁主席接见我大使
    新华社阿尔及尔六日电　阿尔及利亚全国革命委员会主席兼总理胡阿里·布迈丁六日在这里接见了中国驻阿尔及利亚大使曾涛，并且同他进行了友好的谈话。
