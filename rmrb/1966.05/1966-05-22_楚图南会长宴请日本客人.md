Title: Chairman Chu Tunan Hosts Banquet for Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-05-22
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 21st. Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, held a banquet tonight to welcome all members of the Japanese delegation of researchers of Chinese art history, headed by Hakuho Bon. Among those present at the banquet were Yang Shuo, Liu Kaiqu, Lin Lin, Wang Yuanfang, Hua Junwu and others. Japanese peace activist Kōichi Saionji, who is in Beijing, was also invited to attend the banquet.
    The delegation arrived in Beijing on the 20th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Chinese Artists Association.

### 楚图南会长宴请日本客人

1966-05-22
第5版()
专栏：

    楚图南会长宴请日本客人
    新华社二十一日讯　中国人民对外文化友好协会会长楚图南今晚举行宴会，欢迎以白石凡为首的日本中国美术史研究家代表团全体成员。出席宴会的，有杨朔、刘开渠、林林、王元方、华君武等。在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也应邀出席宴会。
    代表团是应中国人民对外文化友好协会和中国美术家协会邀请于二十日到达北京的。
