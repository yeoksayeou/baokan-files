Title: Prince Sihanouk Thanks Afro-Asian Journalists Association for Supporting Cambodia; Afro-Asian Journalists Association Conference Resolution Contributes to Anti-Imperialist Front
Author:
Date: 1966-05-05
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, May 2nd (Electrically transmitted) – According to the Cambodian News Agency on May 2nd, Cambodian Head of State Prince Sihanouk wrote to the Afro-Asian journalists, saying: "The Cambodian people welcome the resolutions recently adopted by the Secretariat Meeting of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association on the Cambodian issue, and believe that these resolutions express valuable solidarity and support."
    The letter said: "For this, I would like to express my warmest thanks to you. The Afro-Asian Journalists Association's condemnation of imperialist aggression against Cambodia contributes to strengthening the front of struggle against the United States and its lackeys, who are the enemies of peace, independence, and the progress of people all over the world. We ardently hope that these resolutions can be implemented in coordinated and effective actions to help the people of all fraternal countries unite against the aggressors."

### 西哈努克亲王感谢亚非记协支持柬埔寨  亚非记协会议决议对反帝阵线作出贡献

1966-05-05
第6版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王感谢亚非记协支持柬埔寨
    亚非记协会议决议对反帝阵线作出贡献
    新华社金边二日电　据柬埔寨新闻社二日报道，柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王写信给亚非新闻工作者说：“柬埔寨人民欢迎这次亚非新闻工作者协会书记处会议最近通过的关于柬埔寨问题的一些决议，并且认为，这些决议表示了可贵的团结和支持。”
    信件说：“为此，我谨向你们表示最热烈的感谢。亚非新闻工作者协会谴责了帝国主义对柬埔寨的侵略，这种谴责对加强反对美国及其仆从的斗争阵线作出了贡献，而美国及其仆从是和平、独立和全世界人民进步的敌人。我们热切地希望这些决议能够贯彻在协同一致的有效行动中，以便帮助各兄弟国家的人民联合起来反对侵略者。”
