Title: Norwegian Ambassador to China Holds National Day Reception, Vice Premier Chen Yi and Others Attend by Invitation
Author:
Date: 1966-05-15
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, May 14th - Norwegian Ambassador to China, Helge Akre, held a reception this afternoon at the embassy to celebrate Norway's National Day.
    Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Chairman Yang Mingxuan, and other responsible persons from relevant departments including Wang Bingnan, Song Yiping, Xu Guangxiao, Fu Shenglin, and Yue Songsheng attended the reception by invitation.
    Ambassador Akre proposed a toast at the reception. He mentioned that the relationship between Norway and the People's Republic of China is friendly. He said that our friendship is long-standing and has been strengthened by the mutual visits of Chinese and Norwegian delegations in recent years. He proposed a toast to the progress and prosperity of the People's Republic of China.
    Vice Premier Chen Yi said in his toast that the relationship between China and Norway is friendly. The mutual visits of Chinese and Norwegian personnel, the continuous development of trade and cultural relations, illustrate this point. Recently, China and Norway signed a cultural exchange plan for 1966 and 1967. I believe that as long as both sides work hard, the relations between our two countries will be further developed.
    Diplomatic envoys from various countries to China were also invited to attend the reception.

### 挪威驻华大使举行国庆招待会  陈毅副总理等应邀出席

1966-05-15
第6版()
专栏：

    挪威驻华大使举行国庆招待会
    陈毅副总理等应邀出席
    新华社十四日讯　挪威驻中国大使黑尔格·阿克雷今天下午在大使馆举行招待会，庆祝挪威国庆。
    陈毅副总理，杨明轩副委员长，其他有关方面负责人王炳南、宋一平、徐光霄、傅生麟、乐松生等应邀出席了招待会。
    阿克雷大使在招待会上祝酒。他谈到，挪威和中华人民共和国之间的关系是友好的。他说，我们的友谊是悠久的，并且由于近几年来，中国和挪威代表团的相互访问而有所加强。他提议为中华人民共和国的进步和繁荣干杯。
    陈毅副总理在祝酒中说，中挪两国之间的关系是友好的，中挪两国人士的互访，贸易和文化关系的不断发展，就说明了这一点，最近中挪两国又签订了一九六六和一九六七年文化交流计划。我相信，只要双方努力，我们两国间的关系将会得到进一步发展。
    各国驻中国的外交使节也应邀出席了招待会。
