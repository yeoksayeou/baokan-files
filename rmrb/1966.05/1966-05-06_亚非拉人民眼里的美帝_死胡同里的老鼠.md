Title: The American Empire in the Eyes of the People of Asia, Africa, and Latin America: A Rat in a Dead End

Author: 
Date: 1966-05-06
Edition: 6

    The American Empire in the Eyes of the People of Asia, Africa, and Latin America
    A Rat in a Dead End
    The *Cambodge* newspaper published a commentary on April 18th entitled "A Rat in a Dead End," stating: "Everything shows that American imperialism is the number one enemy of the people of all countries."
    The commentary said that the appearance of B-52 strategic bombers over North Vietnam shows that the American warmongers, in order to satisfy their irrepressible desire to dominate the world, will not hesitate to adopt any cruel means. The commentary said that the use of force by American imperialism for suppression and aggression has never been able to prevent the peace-loving, freedom-loving, and progressive people of all countries from bravely fighting for freedom and justice.

### 亚非拉人民眼里的美帝  死胡同里的老鼠

1966-05-06
第6版()
专栏：

    亚非拉人民眼里的美帝
    死胡同里的老鼠
    《柬埔寨电讯报》四月十八日发表题为《死胡同里的老鼠》的评论说：“一切表明美帝国主义是世界各国人民的头号敌人”。
    评论说，B—52战略轰炸机出现在北越上空，这表明美国好战分子为了满足他们统治世界的不可压制的欲望，是不惜采取任何残酷手段的。评论说，美帝国主义使用武力进行镇压和侵略，从来都不能阻止爱好和平、自由和进步的各国人民为争取自由和正义而勇敢地进行斗争。
