Title: Chairman Liu Receives the Head of the Guinean Trade Union Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1966-05-02
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, 1st dispatch. Chairman Liu Shaoqi received Louis Behanzin, the head of the delegation from the National Confederation of Workers of Guinea, this evening and had a cordial and friendly conversation with him.

    During the meeting, Behanzin presented a letter from President Touré to Chairman Liu Shaoqi. Kang Yonghe was present at the meeting.

    Guinean Ambassador to China, Camara Sékou, was also present. (Attached picture)
    Chairman Liu Shaoqi receiving Louis Behanzin, head of the delegation from the National Confederation of Workers of Guinea, on May 1st.

### 刘主席接见几内亚工联代表团团长

1966-05-02
第2版()
专栏：

    刘主席接见几内亚工联代表团团长
    新华社一日讯　刘少奇主席今晚接见了几内亚全国工人联合会代表团团长路易·贝昂赞，同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见的时候，贝昂赞递交了杜尔总统写给刘少奇主席的一封信。接见时在座的有康永和。
    几内亚驻中国大使卡马拉·塞古也在座。（附图片）
    五月一日，刘少奇主席接见几内亚全国工人联合会代表团团长路易·贝昂赞。
