Title: Liao Chengzhi Receives Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-05-29
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, May 28th: Liao Chengzhi, President of the Sino-Japanese Friendship Association, received this afternoon all members of the Japanese Women Workers Delegation of the Asian-African Workers Conference Preparatory Committee, led by Tsuyako Tsuge, Secretary-General of the Federation of Trade Unions of the Japanese Red Cross Society, and held a friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Di Zicai, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others.
    Also present was Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji, who is currently in Beijing.

### 廖承志会长接见日本客人

1966-05-29
第6版()
专栏：

    廖承志会长接见日本客人
    新华社二十八日讯　中日友好协会会长廖承志，今天下午接见了由日本红十字会工会联合会总书记柘植津伊子率领的亚非工人会议日本筹备会访华女工代表团全体成员，同她们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有全国总工会书记处书记狄子才等。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也在座。
