Title: Japanese Socialist Party Activist Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-05-22
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 21st. The Japanese Socialist Party Activist Delegation led by Sakai Ikuzo, a group of sixteen people, arrived in Beijing by train today after visiting Guangzhou, Changsha, Wuhan and other places at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.
    Wang Yinpu, Zhao Anbo, Xiao Xiangqian, Wen Chi, Sun Yuliang and other responsible persons from relevant departments went to the station to greet them. Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji, who is in Beijing, also went to the station to greet them.
    The delegation arrived in Guangzhou on the 13th.

### 日社会党活动家访华团到京

1966-05-22
第5版()
专栏：

    日社会党活动家访华团到京
    新华社二十一日讯　由酒井郁造率领的日本社会党活动家访华团一行十六人，应中国人民外交学会邀请，在广州、长沙、武汉等地参观访问后，今天乘火车到达北京。
    前往车站迎接的有王荫圃、赵安博、萧向前、文迟、孙玉良等有关方面负责人。在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也前往车站迎接。
    代表团是在十三日到达广州的。
