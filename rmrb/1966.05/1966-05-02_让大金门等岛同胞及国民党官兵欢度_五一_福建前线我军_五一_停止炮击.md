Title: To Let Compatriots on Islands such as Greater Jinmen and Kuomintang Officers and Soldiers Celebrate "May Day": Our Army on the Fujian Front Ceases Shelling on "May Day"
Author:
Date: 1966-05-02
Edition: 2

    To Let Compatriots on Islands such as Greater Jinmen and Kuomintang Officers and Soldiers Celebrate "May Day":
    Fujian Front Our Army Ceases Shelling on "May Day"
    Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 1st—A spokesperson for the headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Fujian Front announced yesterday: May 1st is International Labor Day. In order to allow compatriots in the motherland and Kuomintang officers and soldiers on the islands of Greater Jinmen, Lesser Jinmen, Greater Dan, and Lesser Dan to celebrate this glorious festival of the working people of the world together with the people of the whole country, the artillery units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Fujian Front are ordered to cease shelling on May 1st to show our concern.

### 让大金门等岛同胞及国民党官兵欢度“五一”  福建前线我军“五一”停止炮击

1966-05-02
第2版()
专栏：

    让大金门等岛同胞及国民党官兵欢度“五一”
    福建前线我军“五一”停止炮击
    新华社福建前线一日电中国人民解放军福建前线司令部发言人昨天宣布：五月一日，是国际劳动节。为了让大金门、小金门、大担、二担等岛屿上的祖国同胞和国民党军官兵，同全国人民一道，欢度这个全世界劳动人民的光辉节日，中国人民解放军福建前线炮兵部队奉命在五月一日停止炮击，以示关怀。
