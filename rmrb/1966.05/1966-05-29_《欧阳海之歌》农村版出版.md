Title: "The Song of Ouyang Hai" Rural Edition Published
Author:
Date: 1966-05-29
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 28th. The "rural edition" of *The Song of Ouyang Hai* has been published and is being distributed to rural areas nationwide. Since its publication, *The Song of Ouyang Hai* has been warmly welcomed by the broad masses of rural readers. To meet the needs of rural readers across the country, the Rural Edition Book Selection Committee recently edited a "rural edition" based on the author's revised version, divided into two volumes, and published by the Rural Readers Publishing House.

### 《欧阳海之歌》农村版出版

1966-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

    《欧阳海之歌》农村版出版
    新华社二十八日讯　《欧阳海之歌》“农村版”已经出版，并向全国农村发行。《欧阳海之歌》发行以来，受到广大农村读者的热烈欢迎。为了满足全国农村读者的需要，最近农村版图书编选委员会根据作者修订的版本，编辑了“农村版”，分上下两册，由农村读物出版社出版。
