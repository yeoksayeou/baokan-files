Author:

    Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Leaders of Weightlifting Delegations from Various Countries and Regions
    Xinhua News Agency, 20th – Vice Premier of the State Council, Chen Yi, this evening received the heads and deputy heads of the weightlifting delegations from various countries and regions participating in the 1966 Emerging Forces Weightlifting Invitational Tournament, and held cordial and friendly talks with them.
    Those received included: Albania delegation head Skender Tupja, deputy head Mihal Cipo, Cambodia delegation head Sreng Ti, deputy head Ke Lé, Ceylon delegation head Tienne Ranasinghe, Japan delegation head Gakuo Haga, Democratic People's Republic of Korea delegation head Kim In-guk, Pakistan delegation head Muhammad Naqi Butt, deputy head Muhammad Ayub Khan, Palestine delegation head Abdul Karim Shawa, Syria delegation head Nizar Nahid, and China delegation head Qu Zhichuan.
    Present at the reception were Rong Gaotang, Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation of the Games of the New Emerging Forces and Vice Minister of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission; Li Menghua, Member of the All-China Sports Federation of the Games of the New Emerging Forces and Vice Minister of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission; and Zhong Shitong, Chairman of the Organizing Committee of this Weightlifting Invitational Tournament and Chairman of the Chinese Weightlifting Association. ### 陈毅副总理接见各国和地区举重代表团领导人

1966-05-21
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见各国和地区举重代表团领导人
    新华社二十日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今晚接见了参加一九六六年新兴力量举重邀请赛的各国和地区举重代表团团长、副团长，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    被接见的有：阿尔巴尼亚代表团团长斯坎德·图皮亚、副团长米哈尔·齐波，柬埔寨代表团团长盛提、副团长凯莱，锡兰代表团团长季恩·拉纳辛格，日本代表团团长额贺熏生，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国代表团团长金仁国，巴基斯坦代表团团长穆罕默德·纳基·布特、副团长穆罕默德·阿尤布汗，巴勒斯坦代表团团长阿卜杜勒·卡里姆·沙瓦，叙利亚代表团团长尼扎尔·纳希德和中国代表团团长曲治全。
    接见时在座的，有中国新兴力量运动会全国委员会主席、国家体委副主任荣高棠，中国新兴力量运动会全国委员会委员、国家体委副主任李梦华，这次举重邀请赛组织委员会主席、中国举重协会主席钟师统。（附图片）
    五月二十日，陈毅副总理接见参加一九六六年新兴力量举重邀请赛的各国和地区的举重代表团团长和副团长。
   　新华社记者　王敬德摄
