Title: Japanese Ono Saburo Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-02
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th – Japanese dyeing and printing expert Ono Saburo and his companion arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries for a tour and visit to our country.

### 日本小野三郎到京

1966-05-02
第3版()
专栏：

    日本小野三郎到京
    新华社三十日讯　应对外友协邀请来我国参观访问的日本印染专家小野三郎等二人，今天乘飞机到达北京。
