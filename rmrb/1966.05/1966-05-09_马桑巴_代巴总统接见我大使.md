Title: Massemba-Débat Receives Chinese Ambassador

Author: 
Date: 1966-05-09
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Brazzaville, 7th—Congolese (Brazzaville) President Massemba-Débat received the Chinese Ambassador to Congo (Brazzaville), Zhou Qiuye, here on the 6th, and held friendly talks with him.



**Named Entities**

马桑巴—代巴  Massemba-Débat
周秋野  Zhou Qiuye
刚果（布）  Congo (Brazzaville)
新华社  Xinhua News Agency
布拉柴维尔  Brazzaville

### 马桑巴—代巴总统接见我大使

1966-05-09
第5版()
专栏：

    马桑巴—代巴总统接见我大使
    新华社布拉柴维尔七日电　刚果（布）总统马桑巴—代巴六日在这里接见了中国驻刚果（布）大使周秋野，并且同他进行了友好的谈话。
