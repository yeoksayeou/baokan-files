Title: Vietnam and Albania Sign Agreement; Albania Provides Non-Reimbursable Material Assistance to Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-05-03
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 1st—Tran Huu Duyet, Minister of the Premier's Office of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Shefquet Hekali, Albanian Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to Vietnam, signed an aid agreement in Hanoi on April 29th on behalf of their respective countries.
    According to the agreement, Albania will provide a batch of materials to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in 1966 as non-reimbursable assistance.
    Present at the signing ceremony on the Vietnamese side were Deputy Prime Minister Le Thanh Nghi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Hoang Van Tien, and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Nghiem Ba Duc.
    On the Albanian side were officials from the Albanian Embassy in Hanoi.

### 越阿两国政府签订协定  阿尔巴尼亚向越南提供无偿物资援助

1966-05-03
第5版()
专栏：

    越阿两国政府签订协定
    阿尔巴尼亚向越南提供无偿物资援助
    新华社河内一日电　越南民主共和国总理府部长陈友翼和阿尔巴尼亚驻越南特命全权大使谢夫切特·赫卡利分别代表两国于四月二十九日在河内签订了一项援助协定。
    根据这项协定，阿尔巴尼亚在一九六六年向越南民主共和国提供一批物资，作为无偿援助。
    出席签字仪式的，越南方面有副总理黎清毅、外交部副部长黄文进和对外贸易部副部长严伯德。
    阿尔巴尼亚方面有阿尔巴尼亚驻河内大使馆的官员。
