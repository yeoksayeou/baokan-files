Title: Japanese-Chiang Gang Up Military Collusion: Sato Government Sends Large Number of Officers to Taiwan for Activities
Author:
Date: 1966-05-20
Edition: 6

    Japanese-Chiang Gang Up Military Collusion
    Sato Government Sends Large Number of Officers to Taiwan for Activities
    (Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 16th) According to the "Akahata" report on the 16th, sixty-eight officers from the Japanese "Self-Defense Forces" departed from the Iruma Air Base in Saitama Prefecture to Taiwan on the 15th on two Japanese military aircraft for activities.
    According to reports, this group of Japanese officers will "inspect" the "Kungkuan Air Base" used jointly by the US-Chiang troops, the Chiang gang's "National Defense Research Institute," and other military facilities in Taiwan.
    According to the Japanese "Self-Defense Forces" newspaper "Asagumo," the Chiang gang's military delegation was active in Japan from March 10 to April 3 this year.
    "Akahata" pointed out that as the United States expands its war of aggression in Vietnam, Taiwan's importance in the United States' global strategy is increasing. The activities of Japan and the Chiang Kai-shek clique to strengthen military collusion are aimed at serving the strategic plans of the United States.

### 日蒋加紧军事勾结  佐藤政府派大批军官去台湾活动

1966-05-20
第6版()
专栏：

    日蒋加紧军事勾结
    佐藤政府派大批军官去台湾活动
    据新华社东京十六日电　据《赤旗报》十六日揭露，日本“自卫队”军官六十八人，十五日乘两架日本军用飞机从埼玉县入间空军基地出发去台湾活动。
    据报道，这批日本军官将在台湾“考察”美蒋军队共同使用的“公馆空军基地”、蒋帮的“国防研究院”以及其它军事设施。
    据日本“自卫队”报纸《朝云》早些时候透露，蒋帮军事代表团在今年三月十日至四月三日到日本活动。
    《赤旗报》指出，随着美国扩大侵越战争，台湾在美国世界战略中的重要性日益增加。日本和蒋介石集团加强军事勾结的活动，其目的是在为美国的战略计划服务。
