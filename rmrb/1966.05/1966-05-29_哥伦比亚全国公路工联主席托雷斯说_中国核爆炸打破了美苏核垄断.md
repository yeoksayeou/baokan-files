Title: Colombian National Highway Workers Union Chairman Torres Says China's Nuclear Explosion Broke the US-Soviet Nuclear Monopoly
Author:
Date: 1966-05-29
Edition: 5

    Bogota, 27th (Xinhua News Agency) - Rafael Torres Flores, Chairman of the National Highway Workers Union of Colombia, recently made a statement on China's third nuclear explosion, saying, "The progress made by the People's Republic of China and its growing atomic power are a guarantee of world peace, because it has broken the atomic monopoly of the United States and the Soviet Union that existed not long ago."
    He pointed out, "The aggressive activities of the U.S. imperialists will encounter insurmountable obstacles in the face of China's nuclear power."
    He emphasized, "The irresistible force of the people of all countries seeking liberation is stronger than all atomic weapons."

### 哥伦比亚全国公路工联主席托雷斯说  中国核爆炸打破了美苏核垄断

1966-05-29
第5版()
专栏：

    哥伦比亚全国公路工联主席托雷斯说
    中国核爆炸打破了美苏核垄断
    新华社二十七日讯　波哥大消息：哥伦比亚全国公路工人联合会主席拉斐尔·托雷斯·弗洛雷斯最近就中国第三次核爆炸发表谈话说，“中华人民共和国所取得的进步及其日益增长的原子威力是世界和平的保证，因为它打破了不久前美国和苏联的原子垄断。”
    他指出，“美帝国主义者的侵略活动在中国核威力面前将遇到不可逾越的障碍。”
    他强调指出，“各国人民求解放的不可遏制的力量比所有原子武器都强大。”
