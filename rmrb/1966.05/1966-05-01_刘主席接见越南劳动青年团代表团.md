Title: Chairman Liu Receives Delegation from the Vietnam Labor Youth League
Author:
Date: 1966-05-01
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th – Chairman Liu Shaoqi today received all members of the delegation from the Vietnam Labor Youth League, led by Vu Quang, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Labor Youth League.
    Chairman Liu Shaoqi had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Hu Keshi and Yang Haibo, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and Qian Dawei, Member of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League and Director of the International Liaison Department, and others.
    Also present was Tran Tu Binh, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China.

### 刘主席接见越南劳动青年团代表团

1966-05-01
第4版()
专栏：

    刘主席接见越南劳动青年团代表团
    新华社三十日讯　刘少奇主席今天接见了由越南劳动青年团中央第一书记武光率领的越南劳动青年团代表团全体成员。
    刘少奇主席同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有共青团中央书记处书记胡克实、杨海波，共青团中央常委、国际联络部部长钱大卫等。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使陈子平也在座。
