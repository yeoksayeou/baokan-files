Title: Congolese (Brazzaville) Embassy Chargé d'Affaires Holds Reception for Congolese (Brazzaville) Delegations and Personnel Visiting China
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-04
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd – Congolese (Brazzaville) Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China, Bakoula Daniel, held a reception this afternoon for the Congolese (Brazzaville) Trade Union Confederation Delegation, the Congolese (Brazzaville) Science Delegation, Congolese (Brazzaville) students from the Asian-African Journalists Association training course, and Congolese (Brazzaville) journalists visiting China. Xu Guangxiao, Vice Minister of Culture, and responsible persons from relevant units Fan Jin, Xie Feng, Zhou Min, Fu Bingyou, Xu Xiaobing, and Shi Licheng from the Chinese side attended the reception. Ambassadors to China from Mali, Tanzania, and Guinea also attended the reception.
