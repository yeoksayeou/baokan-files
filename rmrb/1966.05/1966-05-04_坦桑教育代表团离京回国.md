Title: Tanzanian Education Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1966-05-04
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 3rd – The Tanzanian education delegation led by A. Makame left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.

    Zhang Guiru, Fang Nanjun, and others were at the airport to see them off.

    Tanzanian Ambassador to China, Wazir Juma, was also at the airport to see them off.

### 坦桑教育代表团离京回国

1966-05-04
第5版()
专栏：

    坦桑教育代表团离京回国
    新华社三日讯　由阿·马卡梅率领的坦桑尼亚教育代表团，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场送行的，有张桂如、方南君等。
    坦桑尼亚驻华大使瓦齐里·朱马也到机场送行。
