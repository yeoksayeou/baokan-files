Title: Press Communiqué Issued by the Delegation of the Malayan National Liberation League to China: The Indonesian Right-Wing Authorities Must Immediately Release Arrested Members of the Liberation League

Author: 
Date: 1966-05-07
Edition: 6

    According to Xinhua News Agency, May 6th. The Delegation of the Malayan National Liberation League to China issued a press communiqué today stating that people around the world are widely demanding the release of members of the Malayan National Liberation League delegation to Indonesia, who were arrested by the Indonesian right-wing military regime.
    The communiqué reiterated its appeal to international organizations, groups, and individuals to unite on the side of justice, isolate the Indonesian right-wing supported by U.S. imperialism, and demand an immediate end to all forms of illegal, fascist political persecution. It demanded the immediate release of the four members of the Malayan National Liberation League delegation to Indonesia and guarantees for their safety and freedom.
    The communiqué said that the demand by the Central Committee of the Malayan National Liberation League for the Indonesian right-wing military regime to immediately release the four members of the Malayan National Liberation League delegation to Indonesia has received widespread support from many groups, organizations, and individuals around the world.

### 马来亚民族解放同盟驻中国代表团发表新闻公报  印尼右派当局必须立即释放解放同盟被捕成员

1966-05-07
第6版()
专栏：

    马来亚民族解放同盟驻中国代表团发表新闻公报
    印尼右派当局必须立即释放解放同盟被捕成员
    据新华社六日讯　马来亚民族解放同盟驻中国代表团今天发表的一项新闻公报中说，世界各地广泛要求释放被印度尼西亚右派军人政权所逮捕的马来亚民族解放同盟驻印度尼西亚代表团团员。
    公报再次呼吁国际组织、团体和个人，团结在正义的一边，孤立受美帝国主义支持的印度尼西亚右派，并要求立即结束所有形式的非法的法西斯性质的政治迫害，要求立即释放马来亚民族解放同盟驻印度尼西亚代表团的四名成员，并保障他们的生命安全和自由。
    公报说，马来亚民族解放同盟中央委员会提出的要求印度尼西亚右派军人政权立即释放马来亚民族解放同盟驻印度尼西亚代表团四名成员的要求，已得到世界各地许多团体、组织和个人的广泛支持。
