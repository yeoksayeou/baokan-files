Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Tanzanian Delegations

Author: 
Date: 1966-05-03
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, May 2nd – Premier Zhou Enlai received the Tanzanian Cultural Delegation led by Joseph Kashiira Bantu and the Tanzanian Education Delegation led by A. Makame this evening.

    Premier Zhou Enlai had a cordial and friendly conversation with the Tanzanian friends.

    Present at the reception were Shi Huaibi, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Xu Guangxiao, Vice-Minister of Culture; Lin Liru, Vice-Minister of Education; and others.

    Tanzanian Ambassador to China, Wazir Juma, was also present.

### 周总理接见坦桑尼亚两代表团

1966-05-03
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见坦桑尼亚两代表团
    新华社二日讯　周恩来总理今晚接见了由约瑟夫·卡西拉·班图率领的坦桑尼亚文化代表团和由阿·马卡梅率领的坦桑尼亚教育代表团。
    周总理同坦桑尼亚朋友进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有对外文委副主任史怀璧、文化部副部长徐光霄、教育部副部长林砺儒等。
    坦桑尼亚驻华大使瓦齐里·朱马也在座。
