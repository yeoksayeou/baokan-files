Title: China-Nepal Friendship Association Holds Film Reception to Celebrate Sixth Anniversary of Signing of China-Nepal Treaty of Peace and Friendship
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-02
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: The China-Nepal Friendship Association held a film reception this afternoon to celebrate the sixth anniversary of the signing of the China-Nepal Treaty of Peace and Friendship.

    Narayan Prasad Rijal, the Nepalese Minister of Industry and Commerce, and his wife, who are currently on a friendly visit to Beijing, and K.S. Thapa, the Assistant Private Secretary to the Crown Prince of Nepal, were invited to attend the reception.

    Zhou Shujia, Vice President of the China-Nepal Friendship Association, and Minister Narayan Prasad Rijal spoke at the reception, warmly praising the growing friendly relations between the two countries since the signing of the China-Nepal Treaty of Peace and Friendship. They jointly wished the friendship between the peoples of the two countries would last forever.

    Among those attending the film reception were Lin Haiyun and his wife, Acting Minister of Foreign Trade, Han Nianlong, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Li Yingji, Vice Chairman of the Commission for Economic Relations with Foreign Countries, and Shi Huaibi, Vice Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

    The Nepalese Ambassador to China, Randhir Subba, and his wife, as well as Nepalese experts and students in Beijing, also attended the reception.

    The Chinese film "Spring in Shanghai" was shown at the reception.

### 中尼友协举行电影招待会  庆祝中尼和平友好条约签订六周年

1966-05-02
第3版()
专栏：

    中尼友协举行电影招待会
    庆祝中尼和平友好条约签订六周年
    新华社二十九日讯　中国尼泊尔友好协会今天下午举行电影招待会，庆祝中国尼泊尔和平友好条约签订六周年。
    正在北京进行友好访问的尼泊尔工商大臣纳·普·里贾尔和夫人，尼泊尔王太子的助理私人秘书克·斯·塔帕应邀出席了招待会。
    中国尼泊尔友好协会副会长周叔迦和纳·普·里贾尔大臣先后在招待会上讲话，热烈赞颂中国尼泊尔和平友好条约签订以来，两国友好关系日益发展。他们共祝两国人民的友谊万古长青。
    出席电影招待会的，有对外贸易部代部长林海云和夫人，外交部副部长韩念龙，对外经济联络委员会副主任李应吉，对外文化联络委员会副主任史怀璧等。
    尼泊尔驻中国大使伦迪尔·苏巴和夫人以及尼泊尔在北京的专家和留学生也出席了招待会。
    招待会上放映了中国影片《上海之春》。
