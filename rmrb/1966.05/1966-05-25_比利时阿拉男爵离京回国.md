Title: Belgian Baron Ara Departs Beijing for Home

Author:
Date: 1966-05-25
Edition: 5

    Belgian Baron Antoine Ara, Chairman of the Belgian Action for Peace and Independence of Peoples and the Belgian Committee for Supporting the People of South Vietnam, and his wife, who were invited by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace to visit our country, left Beijing by plane today to return home. Yang Ji, Yong Longgui and others were at the airport to see them off.

### 比利时阿拉男爵离京回国

1966-05-25
第5版()
专栏：

    比利时阿拉男爵离京回国
    新华社二十四日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来我国访问的比利时争取和平与各国人民独立行动和比利时声援越南南方人民委员会主席安东·阿拉男爵和夫人，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。到机场欢送的，有杨骥、勇龙桂等。
