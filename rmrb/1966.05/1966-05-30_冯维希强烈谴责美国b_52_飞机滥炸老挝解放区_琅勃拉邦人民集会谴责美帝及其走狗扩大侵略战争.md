Title: Phoumi Vongvichit Strongly Condemns US B-52 Bombing of Lao Liberated Areas; People of Luang Prabang Rally to Condemn US Imperialism and its Lackeys for Expanding the War of Aggression
Author:
Date: 1966-05-30
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Khang Khay, May 28th (via Pathet Lao Radio, May 28th): Phoumi Vongvichit, member of the Central Committee for the Implementation of the Geneva Accords on Laos, sent an urgent telegram on May 25th to the two chairmen of the Geneva Conference on Laos, strongly condemning the US imperialists for intensifying the bombing of Lao liberated areas with B-52 type bombers.

    Phoumi Vongvichit is the Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front and Minister of Information, Propaganda, and Tourism of the Lao National Union Government. His telegram stated that on May 23rd, more than ten US B-52 type bombers dropped hundreds of tons of bombs on a residential area in the northeastern part of Attopeu Province in southern Laos. This is the seventh large-scale bombing of Lao territory by US aggressors using B-52 type bombers since April 1966.

    The telegram said that in recent days, US aggressors have also continuously dispatched various types of jet aircraft from bases in Thailand and South Vietnam and the US Seventh Fleet to bomb the Lao liberated areas, especially Sam Neua Province, day and night.

    The telegram reiterated the request to the two chairmen of the Geneva Conference to condemn the aforementioned barbaric war actions of US imperialism and to take measures to urge US imperialism to immediately cease air raids on Lao territory and to respect and correctly implement the 1962 Geneva Accords on Laos.

    Xinhua News Agency, Khang Khay, May 29th (via Pathet Lao Radio): People from 107 villages in Oudomxay, Phong Thong, Pak Ou, Muang Ngoy, and Pak Seng areas of Luang Prabang Province, Laos, have held rallies since the end of April, strongly condemning the crimes of US imperialism and its lackeys in expanding the war of aggression in Laos.

    These rallies passed hundreds of letters to the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front. These letters strongly condemned the crimes of the US and its lackeys’ troops carrying out successive "sweeps" and US aircraft indiscriminately bombing the Lao liberated areas. These letters expressed their determination to unite closely and fight to defend the liberated areas and shatter the conspiracies of US imperialism and its lackeys.

### 冯维希强烈谴责美国B—52飞机滥炸老挝解放区  琅勃拉邦人民集会谴责美帝及其走狗扩大侵略战争

1966-05-30
第6版()
专栏：

    冯维希强烈谴责美国B—52飞机滥炸老挝解放区
    琅勃拉邦人民集会谴责美帝及其走狗扩大侵略战争
    新华社康开二十八日电  据巴特寮电台二十八日广播：老挝监察执行日内瓦协议中央委员会委员富米·冯维希二十五日打紧急电报给关于老挝问题的日内瓦会议两主席，强烈谴责美帝国主义加紧用B—52型轰炸机轰炸老挝解放区的罪行。
    冯维希是老挝爱国战线党中央委员会总书记、老挝民族团结政府新闻、宣传和游览大臣。他的电报说，五月二十三日，十多架美国B—52型轰炸机向老挝南部的阿速坡省东北部的一个居民区扔下了成百吨的炸弹。这是美国侵略者从一九六六年四月以来，第七次使用B—52型轰炸机对老挝领土进行的大规模轰炸。
    电报说，美国侵略者这几天还不断从泰国、南越的基地和美国第七舰队派出各种类型的喷气式飞机，对老挝解放区、特别是对桑怒省日夜进行轰炸。
    电报再一次要求日内瓦会议两主席谴责美帝国主义上述野蛮的战争行动，并且采取措施促使美帝国主义立即停止对老挝领土的空袭，尊重和正确执行一九六二年关于老挝问题的日内瓦协议。
    新华社康开二十九日电据巴特寮电台广播，老挝琅勃拉邦省乌东塞、丰通、巴乌、芒艾和巴森等地一百零七个村庄的人民，从四月底以来纷纷集会，强烈谴责美帝国主义及其走狗在老挝扩大侵略战争的罪行。
    这些集会通过了成百份给老挝爱国战线党中央委员会的信件。这些信件强烈谴责美国及其走狗的军队接连进行“扫荡”，美国飞机滥施轰炸老挝解放区的罪行。这些信件表示要紧密团结起来，为保卫解放区、粉碎美帝国主义及其走狗的阴谋而斗争。
