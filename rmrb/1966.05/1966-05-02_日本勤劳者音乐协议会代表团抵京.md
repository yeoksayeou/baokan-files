Title: Delegation of the Japan Federation of Workers' Music Councils Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-02
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, 29th.—The delegation of the Japan Federation of Workers' Music Councils, led by its head, Makino Hiroyuki, consisting of six members, arrived in Beijing by plane tonight for a visit to our country at the invitation of the Chinese Musicians Association. Wang Yuanfang, Lin Lin, and others were at the airport to welcome them.



**Named Entities**


日本勤劳者音乐协议会 Japan Federation of Workers' Music Councils
牧野弘之  Makino Hiroyuki
中国音乐家协会  Chinese Musicians Association
北京 Beijing
王元方 Wang Yuanfang
林林 Lin Lin
新华社  Xinhua News Agency

### 日本勤劳者音乐协议会代表团抵京

1966-05-02
第3版()
专栏：

    日本勤劳者音乐协议会代表团抵京
    新华社二十九日讯　由团长牧野弘之率领的日本勤劳者音乐协议会代表团一行六人，应中国音乐家协会邀请来我国访问，今晚乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有王元方、林林等。
