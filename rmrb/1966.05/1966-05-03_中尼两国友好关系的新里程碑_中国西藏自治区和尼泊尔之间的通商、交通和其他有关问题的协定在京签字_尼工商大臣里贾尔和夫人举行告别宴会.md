Title: A New Milestone in Friendly Relations Between China and Nepal: Agreement on Trade, Transportation, and Other Related Issues Between the Tibet Autonomous Region of China and Nepal Signed in Beijing; Nepalese Minister of Industry and Commerce, Rijāl, and His Wife Hold Farewell Banquet
Author:
Date: 1966-05-03
Edition: 1

    A New Milestone in Friendly Relations Between China and Nepal
    Agreement on Trade, Transportation, and Other Related Issues Between the Tibet Autonomous Region of China and Nepal Signed in Beijing
    Nepalese Minister of Industry and Commerce, Rijāl, and His Wife Hold Farewell Banquet
    Xinhua News Agency, May 2nd. The "Agreement Between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of His Majesty the King of Nepal on Trade, Transportation, and Other Related Issues Between the Tibet Autonomous Region of China and Nepal" was signed in Beijing this afternoon.
      The agreement states that the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of His Majesty the King of Nepal, in order to further develop friendly and good-neighborly relations between the two countries, reaffirm the Five Principles, namely: mutual respect for each other's territorial integrity and sovereignty, mutual non-aggression, mutual non-interference in internal affairs, equality and mutual benefit, and peaceful coexistence, as the basic principles guiding relations between the two countries, and on this basis, develop the traditional friendly relations between the people of the two countries, especially between the people of the Tibet Autonomous Region of China and the people of Nepal; unanimously agree to conclude this agreement on the basis of the "Agreement Between the People's Republic of China and the Kingdom of Nepal on Maintaining Friendly Relations and on Trade and Transportation Between the Tibet Region of China and Nepal" signed by the two governments on September 20, 1956.
      Vice Premier of the State Council, Chen Yi, and Nepalese Minister of Industry and Commerce, Nā. Pū. Rijāl, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective governments.
    Chinese participants in the signing ceremony included Lin Haiyun, Han Nianlong, Li Yingji, and other responsible persons from relevant departments.
    Nepalese participants in the signing ceremony included the Nepalese Ambassador to China, Rendhir Subba, and others.
    After the signing, the hosts and guests raised their glasses in warm congratulations.
    Xinhua News Agency, May 2nd. Nepalese Minister of Industry and Commerce, Nā. Pū. Rijāl, and his wife held a farewell banquet this evening.
    Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, Zhang Qian, were invited to attend the banquet.
    Nā. Pū. Rijāl spoke at the banquet. He congratulated the signing today of the agreement between the Chinese and Nepalese governments on trade, transportation, and other related issues between the Tibet Autonomous Region of China and Nepal. He said that the relationship between China and Nepal is solid and friendly. As time goes on, our friendship will continue to strengthen and become closer, which is beyond any doubt. I firmly believe that the peoples and governments of China and Nepal will make unremitting efforts on the road to further vigorously strengthen our friendship.
    He said that the Nepalese people are very grateful for the cooperation China has given in our country's development undertakings. The Nepalese people hope that the Chinese people will always achieve success in the great goal of building their own country.
    Vice Premier Chen Yi said in a toast that we have just signed the agreement between the Chinese and Nepalese governments on trade, transportation, and other related issues between the Tibet Autonomous Region of China and Nepal. This is a new milestone in the friendly relations between our two countries and a major event worth celebrating. I believe that with our joint efforts, this friendship will surely be further consolidated and developed. He asked the distinguished guests to convey to the Nepalese people the cordial greetings and high respect of all the Chinese people when they return home.
    Also invited to the banquet were Lin Haiyun, Han Nianlong, Li Yingji, Chu Tunan, Zhou Shujia, Le Songsheng, and other responsible persons from relevant departments.
    The Nepalese Ambassador to China, Rendhir Subba, and his wife also attended the banquet. (Attached photo)

### 中尼两国友好关系的新里程碑  中国西藏自治区和尼泊尔之间的通商、交通和其他有关问题的协定在京签字  尼工商大臣里贾尔和夫人举行告别宴会

1966-05-03
第1版()
专栏：

    中尼两国友好关系的新里程碑
    中国西藏自治区和尼泊尔之间的通商、交通和其他有关问题的协定在京签字
    尼工商大臣里贾尔和夫人举行告别宴会
    新华社二日讯　“中华人民共和国政府和尼泊尔国王陛下政府关于中国西藏自治区和尼泊尔之间的通商、交通和其他有关问题的协定”今天下午在北京签字。
      协定指出，中华人民共和国政府和尼泊尔国王陛下政府，为进一步发展两国友好睦邻关系，重申以五项原则，即：互相尊重彼此领土的完整和主权、互不侵犯、互不干涉内政、平等互利、和平共处，为指导两国关系的基本原则，并在这个基础上发展两国人民，特别是中国西藏自治区人民与尼泊尔人民之间的传统友好关系；一致同意在一九五六年九月二十日双方政府签订的“中华人民共和国和尼泊尔王国保持友好关系以及关于中国西藏地方和尼泊尔之间的通商和交通的协定”的基础上，缔结这一协定。
      国务院副总理陈毅和尼泊尔工商大臣纳·普·里贾尔，代表双方政府在协定上签了字。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的，有林海云、韩念龙、李应吉等有关方面负责人。
    尼泊尔方面参加签字仪式的，有尼泊尔驻中国大使伦迪尔·苏巴等。
    签字以后，宾主举杯热烈祝贺。
    新华社二日讯　尼泊尔工商大臣纳·普·里贾尔和夫人今晚举行告别宴会。
    陈毅副总理和夫人张茜应邀出席了宴会。
    纳·普·里贾尔在宴会上讲话。他祝贺今天签订了中尼两国政府关于中国西藏自治区和尼泊尔之间的通商、交通和其他有关问题的协定。他说，中国和尼泊尔的关系是巩固和友好的。随着时间的推移，我们的友谊将更加不断加强和密切起来，这是不容有任何怀疑的。我坚信，在进一步大力加强我们友谊的道路上，中尼两国人民和政府将会作出不懈的努力。
    他说，尼泊尔人民十分感谢中国在我国的发展事业中所给予的合作。尼泊尔人民希望中国人民在建设自己国家的伟大目标中永远获得成功。
    陈毅副总理在祝酒时说，我们刚才签订了中尼两国政府关于中国西藏自治区和尼泊尔之间的通商、交通和其他有关问题的协定。这是我们两国友好关系一个新的里程碑，是一件值得祝贺的大喜事。我相信，在我们双方的共同努力下，这种友谊一定能够得到进一步的巩固和发展。他请贵宾们在回国的时候，向尼泊尔人民转达全体中国人民的亲切问候和崇高敬意。
    应邀出席宴会的，还有林海云、韩念龙、李应吉、楚图南、周叔迦、乐松生等有关方面负责人。
    尼泊尔驻中国大使伦迪尔·苏巴和夫人也出席了宴会。（附图片）
