Date: 1966-05-24
Edition: 6

    Johnson's Distress
    The American economy is facing the dual dangers of inflation and declining production. The recent intense disputes among Washington's financial officials over economic policy reflect this.
    In order to maintain their economic "prosperity," the American ruling clique has, for several years, been increasing fiscal deficits and expanding bank credit at all costs, even using the continuous expansion of the war of aggression to stimulate domestic market demand. However, this method of quenching thirst with poison cannot solve the fundamental contradictions of the American capitalist economy, but instead severely damages American finance, leading to the current serious consequences of inflation.
    The Johnson administration cannot ignore the danger of American inflation. The latter is undermining the international position of the dollar and weakening the competitiveness of American foreign trade. At the same time, it is also devouring the purchasing power of the domestic people, stimulating monopoly capital to blindly expand production, and accelerating the trend towards a more serious economic crisis. The reason why some people within the American ruling group insist that the Johnson government must increase taxes and implement monetary tightening lies here.
    However, how easy is it to announce tax increases and implement monetary tightening? Doing so will inevitably compress market demand, slowing down all economic activities and production pace. At a time when America's so-called "prosperity" is disappearing, this measure is likely to trigger a very serious crisis. The Johnson administration is clearly afraid to act rashly.
    Unable to implement inflation, yet afraid to implement monetary tightening, truly a series of contradictions, a dilemma, this is Johnson's great distress.

### 约翰逊的苦闷

1966-05-24
第6版()
专栏：短评

    约翰逊的苦闷
    美国经济正在面临着通货膨胀和生产下降的双重危险。最近，华盛顿的财政官员们在经济政策问题上的激烈争吵，反映了这一点。
    美国统治集团为了维持他们的经济“繁荣”，几年来，不惜加大财政赤字，扩大银行信贷，甚至用不断扩大侵略战争来刺激国内市场的需求。可是，这种饮鸩止渴的办法，并不能解决美国资本主义经济的基本矛盾，反而严重破坏了美国的财政金融，导致了目前通货膨胀的严重恶果。
    约翰逊政府想无视美国通货膨胀的危险是不行的。后者正在破坏着美元的国际地位，削弱着美国对外贸易竞争能力。同时它也在吞噬着国内人民的购买力，刺激垄断资本去盲目扩大生产，加速走向更严重的经济危机。美国统治集团内一部分人所以坚持约翰逊政府必须增税，并实行通货紧缩，原因就在这里。
    但是，宣布增税和实行通货紧缩，这又谈何容易呢？这样做必然要压缩市场上的需求，使一切经济活动和生产步调放慢。在目前美国的所谓“繁荣”正在消失的时候，这个措施很可能触发一场十分严重的危机。约翰逊政府显然不敢贸然从事。
    既不能实行通货膨胀，又不敢实行通货紧缩，真是矛盾重重，左右为难，这就是约翰逊的极大苦闷。
