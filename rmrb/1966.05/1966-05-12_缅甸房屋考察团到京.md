Title: Burmese Housing Inspection Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-05-12
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 11th. A Burmese Housing Inspection Delegation of twelve people, invited by the Ministry of Construction Engineering to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The delegation was led by Lieutenant Colonel Dan Ting, Assistant Secretary of the Burmese Ministry of Public Works and Housing.

**Named Entities**

缅甸	Miǎndiàn (Burma/Myanmar)
北京	Běijīng (Beijing)
建筑工程部	Jiànzhú Gōngchéng Bù (Ministry of Construction Engineering)
丹丁	Dān Tīng (Dan Ting)
公共工程和住房部	Gōnggòng Gōngchéng hé Zhùfáng Bù (Ministry of Public Works and Housing)

### 缅甸房屋考察团到京

1966-05-12
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸房屋考察团到京
    新华社十一日讯　应建筑工程部邀请来我国访问的缅甸房屋考察团一行十二人，在团长、缅甸公共工程和住房部辅助秘书丹丁中校率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
