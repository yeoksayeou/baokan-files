Title: Basutoland Congress Party Deputy Secretary-General Mphanya and Others Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-05-01
Edition: 6

    According to Xinhua News Agency, 30th, Mr. Mphanya, Deputy Secretary-General of the Basutoland Congress Party, and Mr. Jiyani, representative of the Basutoland Congress Party in Cairo, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese People’s Institute of Foreign Affairs to participate in the “May Day” celebrations and visit our country.

开罗   Cairo
北京   Beijing
新华社   Xinhua News Agency
中国人民外交学会   Chinese People’s Institute of Foreign Affairs
五一节   May Day

### 巴苏陀兰大会党副总书记姆法尼亚等到京

1966-05-01
第6版()
专栏：

    巴苏陀兰大会党副总书记姆法尼亚等到京
    据新华社三十日讯　应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国参加“五一”节庆祝活动和访问的巴苏陀兰大会党副总书记姆法尼亚以及巴苏陀兰大会党驻开罗代表济亚尼等，今天乘飞机到达北京。
