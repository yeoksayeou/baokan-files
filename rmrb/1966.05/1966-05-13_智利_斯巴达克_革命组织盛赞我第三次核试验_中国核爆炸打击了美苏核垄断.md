Title: Chilean "Spartacus" Revolutionary Organization Praises China's Third Nuclear Test; China's Nuclear Explosion Strikes at US-Soviet Nuclear Monopoly
Author:
Date: 1966-05-13
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Santiago, May 11th: The National Leadership Committee of the Chilean revolutionary organization "Spartacus" issued a statement on the 11th, warmly congratulating the Chinese people and the Chinese government on the achievements made in the third nuclear test. It emphasized that "China's third nuclear test is a huge support for the revolutionary struggles of people all over the world."
    The statement pointed out that this nuclear test "is a powerful blow to the aggressive plans of US imperialism and the attempt by the US government and the Soviet government to implement nuclear monopoly in order to divide the world into their spheres of influence. This nuclear test is an important step in favor of world peace."
    The statement also said that it is particularly important to strengthen the national defense of the People's Republic of China, as US imperialism is determined to launch a frantic anti-China campaign and launch new aggressive adventures in Asia.

### 智利“斯巴达克”革命组织盛赞我第三次核试验  中国核爆炸打击了美苏核垄断

1966-05-13
第5版()
专栏：

    智利“斯巴达克”革命组织盛赞我第三次核试验
    中国核爆炸打击了美苏核垄断
    新华社圣地亚哥十一日电　智利革命组织“斯巴达克”全国领导委员会十一日发表声明，热烈祝贺中国人民和中国政府在第三次核试验中所取得的成就。它强调说，“中国的第三次核试验是对全世界人民革命斗争的巨大支持。”
    声明指出，这次核试验“是对美帝国主义的侵略计划以及美国政府和苏联政府为把世界分割成它们的势力范围而企图实行核垄断的一个有力的打击。这次核试验是有利于世界和平的一个重要步骤。”
    声明还说，美帝国主义决心展开疯狂的反华运动并在亚洲发动新的侵略冒险，因此，加强中华人民共和国的国防是特别重要的。
