Title: Comrade Bodnăraș and Others Watch the Ballet "The White-Haired Girl"
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-13
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, May 12th—Comrade Emil Bodnăraș and the entire Romanian Party and Government Delegation he is leading, accompanied by Comrade Li Xiannian, watched the revolutionary modern ballet "The White-Haired Girl" performed by the Shanghai Dance School tonight.
    After watching the performance, Comrade Bodnăraș and others, accompanied by Comrade Li Xiannian, went onto the stage, warmly shook hands with the actors, and congratulated them on the success of their performance.
    Responsible persons from relevant departments, including Zhao Yimin, Qiao Guanhua, Peng Shaohui, and Lu Ping, also accompanied the distinguished guests to watch the performance.
    Romanian Ambassador to China Aurel Duma and embassy staff also watched the performance. (Attached Photo)
    May 12th, Comrade Bodnăraș leading the Romanian Party and Government Delegation, accompanied by Comrade Li Xiannian, watched the revolutionary modern ballet "The White-Haired Girl" and then went on stage to take a group photo with the actors.

### 波德纳拉希同志等观看芭蕾舞剧《白毛女》

1966-05-13
第6版()
专栏：

    波德纳拉希同志等观看芭蕾舞剧《白毛女》
    新华社十二日讯　埃米尔·波德纳拉希同志和由他率领的罗马尼亚党政代表团全体成员，由李先念同志陪同，今天晚上观看了上海市舞蹈学校演出的革命现代芭蕾舞剧《白毛女》。
    波德纳拉希等同志看完演出以后，在李先念同志陪同下走上舞台，和演员们亲切握手，祝贺他们演出的成功。
    陪同贵宾们观看演出的，还有赵毅敏、乔冠华、彭绍辉、陆平等有关方面的负责人。
    罗马尼亚驻中国大使奥列尔·杜马和使馆人员也观看了演出。（附图片）
    五月十二日，波德纳拉希同志率领的罗马尼亚党政代表团，由李先念同志陪同观看革命现代芭蕾舞剧《白毛女》后，上台和演员合影。　　
　	　　　新华社记者　王敬德摄
