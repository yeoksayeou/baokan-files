Title: North Vietnamese Army and People Shoot Down Four More U.S. Planes

Author: 
Date: 1966-05-13
Edition: 6

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 12th: It was announced here on the 12th that the army and people of Quang Ninh and Quang Binh provinces in North Vietnam shot down two invading American bandit planes each on the afternoon of the 11th. One of the two American bandit planes shot down in Quang Binh Province was shot down by a militiaman from Bu Trach district.

### 越南北方军民又击落美机四架

1966-05-13
第6版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民又击落美机四架
    据新华社河内十二日电　据这里十二日公布：越南北方广宁、广平两省军民，十一日下午各击落入侵的美国强盗飞机两架。广平省击落的两架美国强盗飞机中，有一架是由布泽县的一个民兵击落的。
