Title: Chinese and Albanian Leaders Continue Talks
Author:
Date: 1966-05-01
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th April: Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee and Premier of the State Council, Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, and other party and state leaders, together with responsible comrades from relevant departments, continued talks this morning with Albanian Labor Party Central Committee Political Bureau member and Chairman of the Council of Ministers Shehu, Albanian Labor Party Central Committee Political Bureau member Kabo, and other comrades of the Albanian Party and Government Delegation, in a comradely and extremely cordial atmosphere.

### 中国和阿尔巴尼亚领导人继续会谈

1966-05-01
第4版()
专栏：

    中国和阿尔巴尼亚领导人继续会谈
    新华社三十日讯　中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来和中共中央总书记邓小平等党和国家领导人以及有关方面的负责同志，同阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、部长会议主席谢胡和阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员卡博以及阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团的其他同志，今天上午在同志式的极为亲切的气氛中，继续举行会谈。
