Title: The People and Armed Forces of North Vietnam Shot Down Thirteen More US Aircraft

Author:

Date: 1966-05-24
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 23rd (Electric dispatch) - According to an announcement here on the 23rd, the people and armed forces of Quang Ninh Province in North Vietnam shot down two invading US bandit aircraft on the 22nd.

    Another supplementary announcement stated that the people and armed forces of Quang Ninh and Ha Tinh provinces also shot down one US aircraft each on the 13th and 14th, respectively.

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi (Electric dispatch) - According to a supplementary announcement here on the evening of the 18th, in addition to the enemy aircraft already announced as shot down, the people and armed forces of North Vietnam shot down four more US aircraft and captured US pilots alive during the three days of May 6th, 10th, and 14th. Among them, the people and armed forces of Nghe An Province shot down one each on the 6th and 14th; the people and armed forces of Quang Binh Province shot down one each on the 10th and 14th.

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi (Electric dispatch) - According to an announcement here, the people and armed forces of Nghe An and Quang Binh provinces in North Vietnam shot down one invading US aircraft each on the 13th and 14th respectively.

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi (Electric dispatch) - According to an announcement here on the 14th, the people and armed forces of North Vietnam shot down a total of three invading US bandit aircraft on the 10th and 11th.

    The people and armed forces of Ha Tinh Province shot down one enemy aircraft on the 10th, and the militia of Quang Binh Province and the Vinh Linh area shot down one enemy aircraft each with light weapons on the 10th and 11th, respectively.

### 越南北方军民又击落美机十三架

1966-05-24
第6版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民又击落美机十三架
    新华社河内二十三日电　据这里二十三日公布：越南北方广宁省军民二十二日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机两架。
    另据补充公布，广宁和河静两省军民在十三日和十四日，还分别各击落美机一架。
    新华社河内电　据这里十八日晚补充公布：越南北方军民在五月六日、十日和十四日三天中除已公布打落的敌机以外，还打落美国飞机四架，并活捉了美国飞行员。其中，义安省军民在六日和十四日分别打落一架；广平省军民十日和十四日分别打落一架。
    新华社河内电　据这里公布：越南北方义安、广平两省军民分别在十三日和十四日两天击落入侵的美国飞机各一架。
    新华社河内电　据这里十四日公布，越南北方军民十日和十一日两天共击落入侵的美国强盗飞机三架。
    河静省军民十日击落敌机一架，广平省和永灵区的民兵分别在十日和十一日用轻武器各击落敌机一架。
