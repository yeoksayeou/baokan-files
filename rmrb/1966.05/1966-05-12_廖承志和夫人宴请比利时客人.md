Title: Liao Chengzhi and his wife host Belgian guests

Author:

Date: 1966-05-12
Edition: 5

    Liao Chengzhi, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and his wife hosted a luncheon today for Baron Alain, Chairman of the Belgian Action for Peace and Independence of Peoples and the Belgian Committee for Support of the South Vietnamese People, and his wife.  Also attending the banquet were Nan Hanchen, Tang Mingzhao, Song Yiping and others.

### 廖承志和夫人宴请比利时客人

1966-05-12
第5版()
专栏：

    廖承志和夫人宴请比利时客人
    新华社十一日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志和夫人今天中午设宴招待比利时争取和平与各国人民独立行动和比利时声援越南南方人民委员会主席阿拉男爵和夫人。参加宴会的有南汉宸、唐明照、宋一平等。
