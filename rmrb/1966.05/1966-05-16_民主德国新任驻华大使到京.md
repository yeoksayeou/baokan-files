Title: New Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic Arrives in Beijing

Author: 
Date: 1966-05-16
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, May 15th - Martin Bierbach, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the German Democratic Republic to the People's Republic of China, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.




**Named Entities**


德意志民主共和国   German Democratic Republic
中华人民共和国   People's Republic of China
马丁·比尔巴赫  Martin Bierbach
新华社 Xinhua News Agency
北京   Beijing

### 民主德国新任驻华大使到京

1966-05-16
第6版()
专栏：

    民主德国新任驻华大使到京
