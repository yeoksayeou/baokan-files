Title: Burmese "People's Daily" Says China Has Made New Contributions to Defending World Peace; China's Possession of Nuclear Weapons Encourages the Liberation Struggles of All Countries

Author: 
Date: 1966-05-16
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 13th. The Burmese "People's Daily" published an editorial on the 13th stating: "The Chinese people's possession of nuclear weapons is a great encouragement to the people of all countries who are bravely struggling for their own liberation, and is also a new contribution to defending world peace."

    The editorial said: "The United States was the first to manufacture atomic bombs. ... Having atomic weapons, the United States became arrogant and attempted to dominate the world."

    The editorial pointed out that current world affairs have become playthings in the hands of the two largest nuclear powers, the United States and the Soviet Union, who are striving to improve their understanding. Therefore, "China, for its own defense and to support the liberation struggles of other countries, had to work hard to manufacture nuclear weapons."

### 缅甸《人民报》说中国对保卫世界和平又作出新贡献  中国拥有核武器鼓舞各国人民解放斗争

1966-05-16
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸《人民报》说中国对保卫世界和平又作出新贡献
    中国拥有核武器鼓舞各国人民解放斗争
    新华社仰光十三日电　缅甸《人民报》十三日发表社论说：“中国人民拥有核武器是对正在为自己的解放英勇斗争的各国人民的巨大的鼓舞，也是对保卫世界和平的新贡献。”
    社论说：“首先制造原子弹的是美国。……美国有了原子武器，妄自尊大，并企图统治世界。”
    社论指出，目前的世界事务成了两个最大的核国家美国和苏联手中的玩物，它们正在努力增进它们之间的谅解。因此，“中国为了它自己的防务和支持其他国家的解放斗争，不得不努力制造核武器。”
