Title: Belgian Baron Alla Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-05-09
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 8th: Baron Alla, Chairman of the Belgian Action for Peace and Independence of Peoples and the Belgian Committee for Support of the South Vietnamese People, arrived in Beijing on the 6th for a visit to China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace.
    Greeting them at the airport were Zhu Ziqi, Yong Longgui, and others.

### 比利时阿拉男爵到京

1966-05-09
第5版()
专栏：

    比利时阿拉男爵到京
    新华社八日讯　比利时争取和平与各国人民独立行动和比利时声援越南南方人民委员会主席阿拉男爵和夫人，应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来我国访问，已在六日到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的，有朱子奇、勇龙桂等。
