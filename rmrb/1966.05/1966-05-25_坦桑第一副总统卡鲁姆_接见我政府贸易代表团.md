Title: Tanzanian First Vice President Karume Receives Trade Delegation from Our Government
Author:
Date: 1966-05-25
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Zanzibar, 22nd - Tanzanian First Vice President Karume received the Chinese government trade delegation led by Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, here on the morning of the 22nd, and had friendly talks with them.
    Present at the meeting were: Chambuso, Minister of State in the Office of the First Vice President; Moyo, Minister of Agriculture of Zanzibar; Khamis, Deputy Minister of Zanzibar in charge of Foreign Affairs; Mponda, Deputy Minister of Trade and Industry of Zanzibar; and Kuratan, Deputy Minister of Engineering, Transport and Power of Zanzibar. Liu Gan, Chinese Consul in Zanzibar, was also present.
    The Chinese delegation, which is currently visiting Tanzania, arrived in Zanzibar this morning.
    The delegation was welcomed at the airport by Mponda, Deputy Minister of Trade and Industry, and Nadif, Principal Secretary of the ministry.

### 坦桑第一副总统卡鲁姆  接见我政府贸易代表团

1966-05-25
第5版()
专栏：

    坦桑第一副总统卡鲁姆
    接见我政府贸易代表团
    新华社桑给巴尔二十二日电　坦桑尼亚第一副总统卡鲁姆二十二日上午在这里接见了由对外贸易部副部长雷任民率领的中国政府贸易代表团，并且同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有：第一副总统办公室国务部长琼布、桑给巴尔农业部长莫约、桑给巴尔主管外交事务的副部长哈米斯、桑给巴尔贸易和工业部副部长姆庞达以及桑给巴尔工程、交通和动力部副部长库拉坦。中国驻桑给巴尔领事刘干也在座。
    正在坦桑尼亚访问的中国代表团是在今天上午到达桑给巴尔的。
    代表团在机场受到贸易和工业部副部长姆庞达和这个部主任秘书纳迪夫的欢迎。
