Title: Chinese Government Delegation Arrives in Budapest to Attend Budapest International Fair
Author:
Date: 1966-05-19
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 17th -- The Chinese Government Delegation led by Fan Mu-han, Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building, arrived in Budapest on the evening of the 17th. The delegation is visiting at the invitation of the Hungarian government to attend the opening ceremony of the 1966 Budapest International Fair and to tour the fair.

    Greeting the Chinese delegation at the airport were Kish Janos, Deputy Minister of Metallurgy and Machine Industry of Hungary, and others.

    Also greeting the delegation at the airport were Han Ke-hua, Chinese Ambassador to Hungary, and Hou Fa-zeng, head of the Chinese exhibition group participating in the Budapest International Fair exhibition and members of the delegation.

### 应邀参加布达佩斯国际博览会  我政府代表团到达布达佩斯

1966-05-19
第5版()
专栏：

    应邀参加布达佩斯国际博览会
    我政府代表团到达布达佩斯
    新华社布达佩斯十七日电　由第一机械工业部副部长范慕韩率领的中国政府代表团十七日晚到达布达佩斯。代表团是应匈牙利政府的邀请前来参加一九六六年布达佩斯国际博览会开幕式并参观博览会的。
    到机场迎接中国代表团的有匈牙利冶金和机械工业部副部长基什·亚诺什等人。
    到机场迎接的还有中国驻匈牙利大使韩克华和代表团团员、参加布达佩斯国际博览会展出的中国展览团团长侯法曾等。
