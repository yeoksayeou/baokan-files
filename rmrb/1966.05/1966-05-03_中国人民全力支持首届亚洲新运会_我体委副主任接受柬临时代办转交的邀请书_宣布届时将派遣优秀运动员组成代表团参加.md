Title: The Chinese People Fully Support the First Asian New Emerging Forces Games; Deputy Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission Receives Invitation Letter from Cambodian Chargé d'Affaires Ad Interim; Announces Delegation of Outstanding Athletes Will Be Sent to Participate

Author:

Date: 1966-05-03
Edition: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Li Jun, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Kingdom of Cambodia in China, on April 29, on behalf of the Cambodian National Organizing Committee for the First Asian New Emerging Forces Games, presented an invitation letter from the Cambodian National Organizing Committee for the First Asian New Emerging Forces Games to Li Menghua, Deputy Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China.

　　Li Jun also handed over invitation letters to Ba Ga, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Mongolian People's Republic in China, and Colonel Zahid, Military Attaché of the Embassy of Pakistan in China.

　　Li Jun also held a reception at the embassy to present the invitation letters.

　　Li Jun spoke at the reception. He said that Cambodia is honored to be designated as the organizer of the First Asian New Emerging Forces Games. This Games will be held in Phnom Penh from November 25th to December 6th this year. This is a concrete result achieved after the great efforts of the countries concerned. He expressed his advance welcome on behalf of the Royal Government of Cambodia to the delegations of various countries participating in this Games.

　　Li Jun expressed his firm belief that, with the support of all participating countries, the First Asian New Emerging Forces Games will surely achieve the anticipated success. He said that the torch of the New Emerging Forces Games will emit greater light, more strongly illuminating the road forward for the people of the New Emerging Forces in their struggle to unite and oppose new and old colonialism and imperialism.

　　Li Menghua also spoke at the reception. On behalf of the National Committee of the People's Republic of China for the New Emerging Forces Games, Chinese athletes and sports workers, he expressed his gratitude for the hospitality of the host country in holding the First Asian New Emerging Forces Games, and announced: The Chinese people and athletes fully support the holding of the First Asian New Emerging Forces Games, and a delegation composed of outstanding athletes will be sent to participate at that time.

　　Li Menghua said that the Kingdom of Cambodia, regardless of the threats and sabotage of US imperialism and its lackeys, has generously assumed the responsibility of hosting the First Asian New Emerging Forces Games, and is making great efforts to ensure the success of the Games. This is a major contribution made by the Kingdom of Cambodia to the solidarity against imperialism and the development of sports in Asian countries. He said that we are deeply convinced that, in the spirit of increasing friendship, promoting each other, developing together, and uniting against imperialism, with the joint efforts of the Cambodian people and the people of Asian countries, the First Asian New Emerging Forces Games, which is of great historical significance in the history of Asian sports, will definitely be held well, be wonderful, and achieve complete success.

　　Also invited to attend the reception were Zhu Debao, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Sports Federation, diplomatic officials from the embassies of Mongolia and Pakistan in China, and others.

### 中国人民全力支持首届亚洲新运会  我体委副主任接受柬临时代办转交的邀请书  宣布届时将派遣优秀运动员组成代表团参加

1966-05-03
第5版()
专栏：

    中国人民全力支持首届亚洲新运会
    我体委副主任接受柬临时代办转交的邀请书
    宣布届时将派遣优秀运动员组成代表团参加
    新华社讯　柬埔寨王国驻中国大使馆临时代办李军，四月二十九日代表第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会柬埔寨全国组织委员会，向中华人民共和国体育运动委员会副主任李梦华，转交了第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会柬埔寨全国组织委员会的邀请书。
    李军同时还把邀请书转交给蒙古人民共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办巴嘎、巴基斯坦驻中国大使馆武官查希德上校。
    李军并为转交邀请书，在大使馆举行了招待会。
    李军在招待会上讲了话。他说，柬埔寨很荣幸地被指定为第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会的组织者。这届运动会将在今年十一月二十五日到十二月六日于金边举行。这是经过各有关国家的巨大努力之后获得的一个具体成果。他代表柬埔寨王国政府预先向参加这届运动会的各国代表团表示欢迎。
    李军表示深信，由于各参加国的支持，第一届亚洲新运会一定能够获得预计的成功。他说，新运会的火炬将发出更大的光芒，更强烈地照耀着各新兴力量人民争取自己的团结和反对新老殖民主义、帝国主义斗争的前进道路。
    李梦华也在招待会上讲了话。他代表中华人民共和国新兴力量运动会全国委员会、中国的运动员和体育工作者，对举办第一届亚洲新运会的东道国的盛情厚意表示感谢，并且宣布：中国人民和运动员全力支持第一届亚洲新运会的举行，届时将派出由优秀运动员组成的代表团参加。
    李梦华说，柬埔寨王国不顾美帝国主义及其走狗的威胁和破坏，慨然承担了举办第一届亚洲新运会的责任，并且为了确保运动会的成功而正在进行巨大的努力。这是柬埔寨王国对亚洲各国人民的团结反帝和体育事业的发展所作的重大贡献。他说，我们深信，本着增加友好、互相促进、共同发展和团结反帝的精神，在柬埔寨人民和亚洲各国人民的共同努力下，在亚洲体育史上具有重大历史意义的第一届亚洲新运会一定会开得好，开得精彩，取得圆满的成功。
    应邀出席招待会的，还有中华全国体育总会副秘书长朱德宝，蒙古和巴基斯坦驻中国大使馆的外交官员等。
