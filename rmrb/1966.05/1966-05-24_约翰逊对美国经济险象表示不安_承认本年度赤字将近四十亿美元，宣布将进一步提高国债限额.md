Title: Johnson Expresses Unease About the Perilous State of the U.S. Economy; Admits This Year's Deficit Will Approach $4 Billion, Announces Further Increase in National Debt Limit
Author:
Date: 1966-05-24
Edition: 6

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Washington, May 22nd: U.S. President Johnson, at a hastily arranged press conference at the White House on May 21st, expressed unease about the dangerous signs appearing in the U.S. economy.

    Speaking about the increasingly serious trend of inflation in the United States, Johnson said, "We recognize this problem, we are aware of this problem, and some of the best talent in America is addressing this problem."

    Johnson also said at the press conference that he estimates the United States will have a deficit of nearly $4 billion in the fiscal year ending June 30th. To make up for the financial shortfall, he announced that he would again ask Congress to further raise the national debt limit, from the current $328 billion to between $330 billion and $335 billion.

    According to a report from the U.S. Department of Commerce on May 21st, U.S. public and private debt increased by $93 billion in 1965, bringing the total public and private debt to $1,267.5 billion. Both figures are record highs.

### 约翰逊对美国经济险象表示不安  承认本年度赤字将近四十亿美元，宣布将进一步提高国债限额

1966-05-24
第6版()
专栏：

    约翰逊对美国经济险象表示不安
    承认本年度赤字将近四十亿美元，宣布将进一步提高国债限额
    据新华社二十二日讯　华盛顿消息：美国总统约翰逊二十一日在白宫举行的临时记者招待会上，对美国经济出现的危险信号表示不安。
    约翰逊在谈到美国越来越严重的通货膨胀趋势时说，“我们认识到这个问题，我们觉察到这个问题，美国的一些最优秀的人才正在处理这个问题。”
    约翰逊在记者招待会上还说，他估计到今年六月三十日结束的财政年度内，美国将有近四十亿美元的赤字。为了弥补财政亏空，他宣布将再次要求国会进一步提高国债限额，从目前的三千二百八十亿美元提高到三千三百亿到三千三百五十亿美元之间。
    据美国商务部二十一日报告，一九六五年美国公私债务增加了九百三十亿美元，使公私债务总额达到一万二千六百七十五亿美元。这两个数字都是创纪录的。
