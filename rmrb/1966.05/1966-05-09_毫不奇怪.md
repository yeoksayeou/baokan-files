Title: Not Surprising At All
Author:
Date: 1966-05-09
Edition: 5

    Not Surprising At All
    The famous American commentator Walter Lippmann recently said in an article: the United States “is now in a strange situation: it has become an isolated globalist power.”
    Lippmann's so-called "globalism" is actually a counter-revolutionary global strategy of seeking world hegemony. Because American imperialism wants to achieve this global strategic goal and carries out aggression everywhere, it cannot but be the enemy of the people of the world, and therefore cannot but put itself in an isolated situation surrounded by the people of the world. This is not surprising at all.

### 毫不奇怪

1966-05-09
第5版()
专栏：三言两语

    毫不奇怪
    美国著名评论员李普曼最近在一篇文章中说：美国“现在处在奇怪的境地：成了一个孤立的全球主义大国”。
    李普曼的所谓“全球主义”，实际上就是独霸世界的反革命全球战略。美帝国主义由于要实现这个全球战略目标而到处侵略，就不能不与全世界人民为敌，因而也就不能不使自己处于受全世界人民包围的孤立境地。这是一点也不奇怪的。
