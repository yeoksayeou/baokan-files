Title: Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Receives Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-05-28
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 27th - Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received all members of the Japanese China Art History Researchers Delegation led by Hitoshi Shiraishi this afternoon and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Liu Kaiqu, Lin Lin, and others.

**Named Entities**

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人

1966-05-28
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人
    新华社二十七日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若今天下午接见由白石凡率领的日本中国美术史研究家代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有刘开渠、林林等。
