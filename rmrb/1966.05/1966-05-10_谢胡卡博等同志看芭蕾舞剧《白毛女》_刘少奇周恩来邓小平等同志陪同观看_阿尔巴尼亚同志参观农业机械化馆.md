Title:  Comrades Shehu and Kambo Watch Ballet "The White Haired Girl"
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-10
Edition: 1

    Comrades Shehu and Kambo Watch Ballet "The White Haired Girl"
    Comrades Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Deng Xiaoping and others accompanied the viewing
    Albanian Comrades Visit the Agricultural Mechanization Hall
    Xinhua News Agency, 9th - Comrades Shehu, Kambo, Kelezi, and other comrades of the Albanian Party and Government Delegation, accompanied by Comrades Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Deng Xiaoping, and others, watched the revolutionary modern ballet "The White Haired Girl" tonight.
    The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed when they arrived at the theater.
    Also accompanying the performance were Comrades Chen Yi, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, Kang Sheng, Wu Xiuquan, Wang Bingnan, and Xu Jianguo.
    The ballet "The White Haired Girl" was performed for the distinguished Albanian guests by the teachers and students of the Shanghai Dance School.
    After the performance, Comrades Shehu, Kambo, etc., accompanied by Comrades Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Deng Xiaoping, etc., walked onto the stage amidst enthusiastic applause from the audience, shaking hands warmly with the performers and congratulating them on the successful performance of this revolutionary modern ballet.
    Xinhua News Agency, 9th - Comrade Shehu and other comrades of the Albanian Party and Government Delegation, accompanied by Comrade Tan Zhenlin, visited the Agricultural Mechanization Hall of the National Agricultural Exhibition Center this morning.
    During the visit, Comrade Shehu inquired in detail about the structure and function of various agricultural machines and watched the operating demonstrations of some agricultural machines by the staff of the exhibition center.
    Comrade Shehu and others were warmly welcomed by the leaders and all the staff of the National Agricultural Exhibition Center during the visit.
    Accompanying the distinguished guests today were Chen Zhengren, Minister of the Eighth Ministry of Machine Building, Wang Bingnan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Cheng Zhaoxuan, Jiang Yizhen, Xu Binzhou, and other responsible persons of relevant departments.

### 谢胡卡博等同志看芭蕾舞剧《白毛女》  刘少奇周恩来邓小平等同志陪同观看  阿尔巴尼亚同志参观农业机械化馆

1966-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

    谢胡卡博等同志看芭蕾舞剧《白毛女》
    刘少奇周恩来邓小平等同志陪同观看
    阿尔巴尼亚同志参观农业机械化馆
    新华社九日讯　谢胡、卡博、凯莱齐等同志和阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团其他同志，今晚由刘少奇、周恩来、邓小平等同志陪同，观看了革命现代芭蕾舞剧《白毛女》。
    贵宾们来到剧场的时候，受到人们的热烈欢迎。
    陪同观看演出的，还有陈毅、李富春、李先念、康生、伍修权、王炳南和许建国等同志。
    芭蕾舞剧《白毛女》是由上海市舞蹈学校师生，为阿尔巴尼亚贵宾演出的。
    演出结束以后，谢胡、卡博等同志，由刘少奇、周恩来、邓小平等同志陪同，在全场热烈的掌声中走上舞台，同演员们亲切握手，祝贺这个革命的现代芭蕾舞剧演出成功。
    新华社九日讯　谢胡同志和阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团的其他同志，今天上午在谭震林同志陪同下，参观了全国农业展览馆的农业机械化馆。
    谢胡同志在参观时，详细了解各种农业机械的构造和作用，并观看了展览馆工作人员对一些农业机械的操作表演。
    谢胡同志等在参观时，受到了全国农业展览馆的负责人和全体工作人员的热烈欢迎。
    今天陪同贵宾们参观的，还有第八机械工业部部长陈正人，外交部副部长王炳南和有关部门负责人程照轩、江一真、徐斌洲等。
