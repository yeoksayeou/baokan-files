Title: Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the NPC Standing Committee, Receives a Group of Japanese Friends
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-09
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 8th. Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, received today at noon the Japanese China Language Researchers and Educators Delegation led by Professor Tōdō Akiyasu of Tokyo University, as well as Mr. Shirato Gofu, Secretary-General of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, and historian Andō Hikotarō. Guo Moruo had a cordial and friendly conversation with the Japanese friends.
    After the reception, Guo Moruo hosted a banquet for the Japanese friends. At the banquet, the hosts and guests jointly wished for the further development of friendship between the cultural and academic circles of China and Japan.
    Present at the reception and banquet were Zhang Youyu, Deputy Director of the Department of Philosophy and Social Sciences of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Lin Lin, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, as well as Ye Laishi, Wang Yishan, and others.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见一批日本朋友

1966-05-09
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见一批日本朋友
    新华社八日讯　人大常委会副委员长、中国科学院院长郭沫若今天中午接见由东京大学教授藤堂明保率领的日本中国语研究者教育者代表团，以及日中文化交流协会事务局长白土吾夫、历史学家安藤彦太郎。郭沫若同日本朋友进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见后，郭沫若设宴招待日本朋友。宾主在宴会上共祝中日两国文化界、学术界的友谊进一步发展。
    陪同接见和参加宴会的，有中国科学院哲学社会科学部副主任张友渔、对外友协书记处书记林林，以及叶籁士、王亦山等。
