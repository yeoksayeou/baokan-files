Title: Chairman Liu Receives Cambodian-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-05
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 4th May – Chairman Liu Shaoqi received the Cambodian-Chinese Friendship Association delegation led by its chairman, Mr. Leng Nget, this afternoon. Chairman Liu had a cordial and friendly conversation with the Cambodian friends.
    Present at the reception were Zhang Hanfu, President of the Chinese-Cambodian Friendship Association, and leading members of the departments concerned, including Ding Xilin, Song Yiping, Ren Yinglun, Zhou Yiping, and Shen Zijiu.
    Also present were Cambodian Prince Norodom Naradipo, who is studying in Beijing, and Li Jun, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Cambodian Embassy in China, and his wife.

### 刘主席接见柬中友协代表团

1966-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席接见柬中友协代表团
    新华社四日讯　刘少奇主席今天下午接见了由柬中友好协会主席兰·涅特率领的柬中友协代表团。刘主席同柬埔寨朋友进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有中柬友好协会会长章汉夫，以及有关方面负责人丁西林、宋一平、任映仑、周一萍、沈兹九。
    在北京学习的柬埔寨王子诺罗敦·纳拉迪波、柬埔寨驻中国大使馆临时代办李军和夫人也在座。
