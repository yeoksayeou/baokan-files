Title: Lao Patriotic Forces News Delegation Holds Reception to Entertain Leaders of the Secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists Association and the All-China Journalists Association
Author: 
Date: 1966-05-04
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The Lao Patriotic Forces News Delegation, led by Chaleun Vongsamsone, Director of "The Voice of Laos" Radio Station, held a reception at the Beijing Hotel tonight to entertain the leaders of the Secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists Association and the All-China Journalists Association.
    Invited to the reception were Djawoto, Secretary-General of the Secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists Association, and the Secretaries of the Secretariat in Beijing from Syria and Ceylon; Fan Jin, An Gang, Li Wu, Zhou Xinwu, Hu Chi, Shi Mai, Bi Jilong, leaders of the All-China Journalists Association and figures from the capital's press circles, as well as journalists stationed in Beijing from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and South Vietnam.
    Mrs. Khampheng Boupha, Chairman of the Lao Women's Federation; the Lao Civil Servants' Federation Delegation headed by Keo Bounmaleithong; and Khamma Phomkong, head of the Lao Patriotic Front Art Troupe, and Chanthon Inthavong, deputy head, also attended the reception.
    Chaleun Vongsamsone delivered a speech at the reception, warmly congratulating the brilliant achievements of the Fourth Plenary (Enlarged) Meeting of the Secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists Association. He pointed out that the Lao people's struggle against U.S. imperialism is a component part of the national liberation movement in Asian and African countries, and the Lao people will fight resolutely until the final victory is won in the struggle against U.S. imperialism. He said that Lao patriotic forces journalists will contribute to striking at U.S. imperialism and new and old colonialism, and to defending world peace.
    In his toast, Djawoto said that the Lao and Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. imperialism has supported the Indonesian people's struggle for genuine liberation and the anti-imperialist cause of the Asian and African people. He proposed a toast to the unity and victory of the struggle against U.S. imperialism.
    In her toast, Fan Jin congratulated the Lao people on the victory of their anti-U.S. patriotic struggle and congratulated the Asian and African journalists on the continuous development of their militant solidarity in the anti-U.S. struggle.
    After the reception, the guests attended an evening party held by the Lao Patriotic Forces News Delegation and the Lao Patriotic Front Art Troupe. Lao artists performed artistic programs at the party.

### 老挝爱国力量新闻代表团举行招待会  招待亚非记协书记处和中国记协的负责人

1966-05-04
第5版()
专栏：

    老挝爱国力量新闻代表团举行招待会
    招待亚非记协书记处和中国记协的负责人
    新华社三日讯　“老挝之声”电台台长扎伦·翁桑昂率领的老挝爱国力量新闻代表团今天晚上在北京饭店举行招待会，招待亚非新闻工作者协会书记处和中华全国新闻工作者协会的负责人。
    应邀出席招待会的，有亚非新闻工作者协会书记处总书记查禾多和书记处在京的叙利亚、锡兰的书记，中华全国新闻工作者协会的负责人和首都新闻界人士范瑾、安岗、李伍、周新武、胡痴、史迈、毕季龙，以及越南民主共和国和越南南方的驻京记者。
    老挝妇女联合会主席贵宁·奔舍那夫人，以盖·班马莱通为首的老挝公务员联合会代表团，老挝爱国战线党文工团团长坎马·富贡、副团长占农·因塔冯也出席了招待会。
    扎伦·翁桑昂在招待会上致词，他热烈祝贺亚非新闻工作者协会书记处第四次全体会议（扩大）所取得的辉煌成果。他指出，老挝人民反对美帝国主义的斗争，是亚非各国民族解放运动的组成部分，老挝人民将坚决战斗，直到反对美帝国主义的斗争取得最后胜利。他说，老挝爱国力量新闻工作者将为打击美帝国主义和新老殖民主义、为保卫世界和平作出贡献。
    查禾多在祝酒时说，老挝人民和越南人民反对美帝国主义的斗争，支持了印度尼西亚人民争取真正解放的斗争，支持了亚非人民的反帝事业。他提议为反对美帝国主义斗争的团结和胜利干杯。
    范瑾在祝酒时祝贺老挝人民反美爱国斗争的胜利，祝贺亚非新闻工作者在反美斗争的战斗团结不断发展。
    招待会结束后，客人们参加了老挝爱国力量新闻代表团和老挝爱国战线党文工团举行的晚会。老挝艺术家们在晚会上演出了文艺节目。
