Title: Solar Eclipse Today
Author:
Date: 1966-05-20
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, May 19th: The Zijinshan Astronomical Observatory of the Chinese Academy of Sciences reports that a solar eclipse will occur tomorrow. Annular eclipses can be seen in parts of Xinjiang, Gansu, Inner Mongolia, Ningxia, Shaanxi, and Shanxi in our country. Partial eclipses can be seen throughout the country except for Taiwan Province.
    Beijing: Initial eclipse at 18:20, maximum eclipse at 19:19.
    Shenyang: Initial eclipse at 18:19.
    Xi'an: Initial eclipse at 18:24, maximum eclipse at 19:25.
    Shanghai: Initial eclipse at 18:26.
    Nanjing: Initial eclipse at 18:26.
    Wuhan: Initial eclipse at 18:28.
    Guangzhou: Initial eclipse at 18:36.
    Chengdu: Initial eclipse at 18:27, maximum eclipse at 19:29.
    Lhasa: Initial eclipse at 18:22, maximum eclipse at 19:28, end of eclipse at 20:26.
    Urumqi: Initial eclipse at 18:01, maximum eclipse at 19:14, end of eclipse at 20:19.
    Note: For the above areas, the times for maximum eclipse and end of eclipse are not listed because these times occur after sunset.

### 今天日食

1966-05-20
第4版()
专栏：

    今天日食
    新华社南京十九日电　中国科学院紫金山天文台消息，明天将发生日食。我国新疆、甘肃、内蒙古、宁夏、陕西、山西的一部分地方可以看到环食，除台湾省外，全国各地都能看到偏食。
    北京，十八时二十分初亏，十九时十九分食甚。
    沈阳，十八时十九分初亏。
    西安，十八时二十四分初亏，十九时二十五分食甚。
    上海，十八时二十六分初亏。
    南京，十八时二十六分初亏。
    武汉，十八时二十八分初亏。
    广州，十八时三十六分初亏。
    成都，十八时二十七分初亏，十九时二十九分食甚。
    拉萨，十八时二十二分初亏，十九时二十八分食甚，二十时二十六分复圆。
    乌鲁木齐，十八时零一分初亏，十九时十四分食甚，二十时十九分复圆。
    注：以上地区没有列出食甚、复圆时刻的，均因其时刻在日没以后。
