Title: Liu Ningyi and Others Meet with a Group of Foreign Trade Union Delegations
Author:
Date: 1966-05-03
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, May 2nd. Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Vice Chairmen Ma Chungu and Kang Yonghe, met on the 1st and 2nd respectively with the delegation of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor headed by Hoang Mau, the delegation of the All-Indonesia Central Organization of Trade Unions headed by Subriana, the delegation of the Lao Federation of Civil Servants headed by Guy Banmaliethong, the delegation of the Sudanese Workers Trade Unions Federation headed by Mohamed Numan Mohamed, and the General Secretary of the General Confederation of Labor of Guadeloupe, Nicolas Luchié, who were invited to China to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations.

### 刘宁一等会见一批外国工会代表团

1966-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

    刘宁一等会见一批外国工会代表团
    新华社二日讯　中华全国总工会主席刘宁一，副主席马纯古、康永和，一日和二日分别会见了应邀来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝活动的以黄茂为首的越南总工会代表团，以苏布利纳为首的全印度尼西亚中央职工会代表团，以盖·班马莱通为首的老挝公务员联合会代表团，以穆罕默德·努曼·穆罕默德为首的苏丹工人工会联合会代表团和瓜德罗普总工会总书记尼古拉·吕治埃。
