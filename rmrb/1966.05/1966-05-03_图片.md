    On May 2nd, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions held a grand evening party to entertain friends from various countries' trade union delegations who came to China to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day.
　Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Jingde took the picture.
