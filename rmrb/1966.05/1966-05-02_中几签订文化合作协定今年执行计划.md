Title: China and Guinea Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement for This Year's Implementation Plan
Author:
Date: 1966-05-02
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, April 30th – China and Guinea signed the 1966 Implementation Plan for the Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the two countries here on April 30th.
    Li Qi, head of the Chinese government cultural delegation and Vice Minister of Culture, and Seydou Conté, Minister of Education of Guinea, signed the plan on behalf of their respective governments.
    In their speeches, Head of Delegation Li Qi and Minister Seydou Conté expressed their hope for the further development of friendly and cooperative relations between China and Guinea.
    Present at the signing ceremony were Chai Zemin, Chinese Ambassador to Guinea, members of the Chinese government cultural delegation, and senior officials from the Guinean Ministry of Education and Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 中几签订文化合作协定今年执行计划

1966-05-02
第3版()
专栏：

    中几签订文化合作协定今年执行计划
    新华社科纳克里三十日电　中国和几内亚四月三十日在这里签订了两国文化合作协定一九六六年执行计划。
    中国政府文化代表团团长、文化部副部长李琦和几内亚教育部部长塞杜·贡特分别代表两国政府在计划上签字。
    李琦团长和塞杜·贡特部长在讲话中表示希望中国和几内亚之间友好合作关系进一步发展。
    签字时在场的有中国驻几内亚大使柴泽民、中国政府文化代表团团员，以及几内亚教育部和外交部的高级官员。
