Title: The U.S. Imperialists Cannot Deny Their Invasion of Chinese Airspace: American Broadcasting Commentator Admits U.S. Planes Intruded Into Our Airspace to Provoke

Author: 
Date: 1966-05-27
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, May 26th, Washington dispatch: CBS's diplomatic commentator, Kalb, said on a television broadcast on the 25th that U.S. jet aircraft had indeed intruded into Chinese airspace on May 12th and shot down a Chinese plane, just as Beijing had accused.
    He said his information came from "reliable sources."
    However, Kalb tried to mitigate the guilt of the United States for this serious act of war provocation, saying that these American aircraft had crossed the Sino-Vietnamese border "accidentally" due to "navigational errors."

### 美帝侵犯中国领空是抵赖不了的  美广播评论员供认美机曾侵入我领空挑衅

1966-05-27
第6版()
专栏：

    美帝侵犯中国领空是抵赖不了的
    美广播评论员供认美机曾侵入我领空挑衅
    新华社二十六日讯　华盛顿消息：美国哥伦比亚广播公司的外交评论员卡尔布二十五日在电视广播中说，美国喷气式飞机确实象北京指责的那样，曾在五月十二日侵入了中国领空并击落中国飞机一架。
    他说，他的情报是从“可靠人士”那里得来的。
    然而卡尔布力图替美国这一严重的战争挑衅行为减轻罪责，说什么这些美国飞机是由于“导航错误”“偶然”越过了越中边界。
