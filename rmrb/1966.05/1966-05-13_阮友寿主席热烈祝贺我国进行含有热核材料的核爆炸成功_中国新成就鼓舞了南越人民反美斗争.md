Title: Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho Warmly Congratulates Our Country on the Successful Nuclear Explosion Containing Thermonuclear Materials; China's New Achievements Inspire the South Vietnamese People's Anti-American Struggle
Author:
Date: 1966-05-13
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, May 12th: Our Party and state leaders received a telegram on the 12th from Nguyen Huu Tho, Chairman of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front, warmly congratulating China on the successful nuclear explosion containing thermonuclear materials. The full text of the telegram is as follows:
    To Comrade Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Comrade Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the People's Republic of China, Comrade Chairman Zhu De of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, Comrade Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:
    I am immensely excited about the successful nuclear explosion containing thermonuclear materials by the People's Republic of China. On behalf of the people of South Vietnam, the South Vietnam National Liberation Front, and in my own name, I extend my warmest congratulations to you, and through you to the Communist Party of China, the Chinese Government, the great Chinese people, the Chinese worker brothers and sisters, and scientists.
    This brilliant achievement proves that the fraternal Chinese people, under the wise leadership of the Communist Party of China headed by Chairman Mao Zedong, with their strong revolutionary will and by highly developing the spirit of self-reliance and creativity, are enabling China to reach the pinnacle of modern science and technology with unparalleled speed.
    This brilliant achievement of the People's Republic of China makes a significant contribution to strengthening the forces of the world against imperialism and colonialism, and is a great encouragement to the South Vietnamese people who are resisting the American imperialist aggressors and their running dogs, fighting for independence, democracy, peace, neutrality, and ultimately the reunification of the fatherland.
    I wish the Chinese people even more brilliant victories in strengthening the national defense of the People's Republic of China and making greater contributions to defending peace in Asia and the world.
              Nguyen Huu Tho, Chairman of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front
                            May 11, 1966, in South Vietnam

### 阮友寿主席热烈祝贺我国进行含有热核材料的核爆炸成功  中国新成就鼓舞了南越人民反美斗争

1966-05-13
第5版()
专栏：

    阮友寿主席热烈祝贺我国进行含有热核材料的核爆炸成功
    中国新成就鼓舞了南越人民反美斗争
    新华社十二日讯　我国党和国家领导人十二日收到了越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团主席阮友寿热烈祝贺中国成功地进行了含有热核材料的核爆炸的电报。电报全文如下：北京中国共产党中央委员会毛泽东主席阁下，中华人民共和国刘少奇主席阁下，中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会朱德委员长阁下，中华人民共和国国务院周恩来总理阁下：
    我为中华人民共和国成功地试验了含有热核材料的核爆炸而感到无比兴奋。我谨代表越南南方人民、越南南方民族解放阵线并以我个人的名义向您们，并通过您们向中国共产党、中国政府和伟大的中国人民，向中国工人兄弟姐妹们和科学家，致以最热烈的祝贺。
    这一光辉的成就，证明了兄弟的中国人民，在以毛泽东主席为首的中国共产党的英明领导下，以坚强的革命意志，高度发挥了自力更生的精神和创造性，使中国无比迅速地走向现代科学技术的顶峰。
    中华人民共和国这一光辉的成就，为增强全世界反对帝国主义、反对殖民主义的力量作出了重大贡献，对于正在反对美帝国主义侵略者及其走狗，为争取独立、民主、和平、中立进而统一祖国而进行抗战的越南南方人民是一个巨大的鼓舞。
    祝中国人民在增强中华人民共和国的国防力量方面取得更辉煌的胜利，为保卫亚洲和全世界的和平作出更大的贡献。
              越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团主席						阮友寿律师
                            一九六六年五月十一日于越南南方
