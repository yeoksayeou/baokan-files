Title: Signing of the Sino-German Scientific and Technological Cooperation Agreement; Chinese Scientific and Technological Delegation to Germany
Author:
Date: 1953-09-29
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 28th: The Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the German Democratic Republic, in order to develop cooperation between the two countries in the fields of science and technology, have decided to sign the Sino-German Scientific and Technological Cooperation Agreement. The delegation of the People's Republic of China, headed by Wang Daohan, Deputy Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building, consisting of nine people, departed from Beijing on the 28th.

### 签订中德科学与技术合作协定  我国科学技术代表团赴德

1953-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    签订中德科学与技术合作协定
    我国科学技术代表团赴德
    【新华社二十八日讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府和德意志民主共和国政府为了发展两国间在科学和技术方面的合作，决定签订中德科学与技术合作协定。以中央第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵为首的中华人民共和国科学技术代表团一行九人已于二十八日从北京出发。
