Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Visits the Mongolian Embassy to Offer Condolences

Author:

Date: 1953-09-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: After learning the unfortunate news of the passing of Comrade Gonchigiin Bumtsend, member of the Political Bureau of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural of the Mongolian People's Republic, Premier Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government's State Council, accompanied by Lin Boqu, Secretary-General of the Central People's Government; Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; and Ji Yatai, our country's ambassador to the Mongolian People's Republic who is currently back home for consultations, visited the Mongolian People's Republic's embassy in our country on the 24th to express deep condolences to Ambassador Ochirbat on the passing of Chairman Bumtsend. Peng Zhen, Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor, also visited the Mongolian Embassy to offer condolences.

The Mongolian Embassy flew its flag at half-mast all day, shrouded in an atmosphere of grief. In the center of the embassy's hall hung a portrait of Chairman Bumtsend, below which was a wreath presented by Chairman Mao Zedong of our Central People's Government. On either side were wreaths presented by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Premier Zhou Enlai, and various departments of the Central People's Government.

Diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China also visited the Mongolian Embassy to offer condolences.

The State Council of the Central People's Government has notified the central, North China, and Beijing municipal-level government organs to fly flags at half-mast on September 25th in mourning. (Attached Picture)

### 周恩来总理到蒙古大使馆吊唁

1953-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理到蒙古大使馆吊唁
    【新华社二十四日讯】我中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理，在获知蒙古人民革命党政治局委员、蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席冈奇金·布曼增迪不幸逝世的消息后，二十四日偕同中央人民政府秘书长林伯渠、中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长张宗逊、中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫和现在回国述职的我国驻蒙古人民共和国大使吉雅泰，到蒙古人民共和国驻我国大使馆向奥其尔巴特大使对布曼增迪主席的逝世致沉痛的吊唁。到蒙古大使馆吊唁的还有北京市人民政府市长彭真、副市长吴晗等。
    蒙古大使馆整日下半旗志哀，笼罩着哀痛的气氛。大使馆大厅中央悬挂着布曼增迪主席的遗像，遗像的下面摆着我中央人民政府毛泽东主席所献的花圈，两边还摆着中国共产党中央委员会、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会、周恩来总理和中央人民政府各部门所献的花圈。
    到蒙古大使馆吊唁的还有各国驻华使节和外交官员。
    中央人民政府政务院已通知中央、华北及北京市一级政府机关于九月二十五日一律下半旗志哀。（附图片）
