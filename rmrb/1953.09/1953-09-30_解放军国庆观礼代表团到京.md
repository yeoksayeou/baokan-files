Title: PLA National Day Observation Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-09-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: More than 500 representatives from the People's Liberation Army (PLA) National Day Observation Delegation have now all arrived in Beijing. The PLA National Day Observation Delegation is composed of representatives from the Northwest, Southwest, East China, Central South, North China, and Northeast Military Regions, as well as the Air Force, Navy, Armored Corps, Artillery Corps, Anti-Aircraft Force, Engineer Corps, and other branches of the military, and the PLA Military Academy, the General Advanced Infantry School, and other units. The PLA Tibet Military Region National Day Observation Delegation, after a long journey through mountains and rivers, also arrived in the capital a few days ago. Many of the representatives of the National Day Observation Delegations are senior cadres from various departments, and many are combat heroes and model workers from the front lines of defending the motherland's borders, coastal defenses, and various production fronts.

During their stay in Beijing, in addition to participating in the National Day observation ceremony on October 1st, the various delegations will also tour famous historical sites in the capital.

### 解放军国庆观礼代表团到京

1953-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

    解放军国庆观礼代表团到京
    【新华社二十九日讯】中国人民解放军各部国庆观礼代表团代表五百多人现已全部到达北京。解放军各国庆观礼代表团由西北、西南、华东、中南、华北、东北各大军区和空军、海军、装甲兵、炮兵、防空部队、工兵各兵种以及中国人民解放军军事学院、总高级步兵学校等部分别组成。中国人民解放军西藏军区国庆观礼代表团经过千山万水的长途跋涉，亦于日前到达首都。各国庆观礼代表团的代表有许多是各部的高级干部，有许多是来自守卫祖国边疆、海防和各生产战线上的战斗英雄和生产模范。
    各代表团在留京期间除参加十月一日的国庆观礼外，并将游览首都各名胜古迹。
