Title: Consumer Goods Supply to Residents Greatly Increased in the German Democratic Republic's Leipzig District Since Implementation of New Policies
Author:
Date: 1953-09-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: Since the German Democratic Republic adopted new policies on June 9th to further improve the people's living standards, the Leipzig district has achieved remarkable results. The supply of food and daily necessities to residents has greatly increased. Local industry has increased the production of consumer goods worth 58 million marks. Contracts worth 1 million marks for leather goods and other daily necessities have been signed with private businesses. This year, handicraftsmen will increase their normal output by producing household electrical appliances, sports equipment, and shoes worth 1.1 million marks. Leather goods merchants have received orders totaling 2.6 million marks.

### 民主德国来比锡区执行新方针以来  居民消费品供应大大增加

1953-09-22
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国来比锡区执行新方针以来
    居民消费品供应大大增加
    据新华社讯：自从德意志民主共和国在六月九日采取了进一步提高人民生活水平的新方针以来，来比锡区已获得显著成绩。对居民的食物和日用品供应已大大增加了。地方工业增加生产了价值五千八百万马克的消费品。与私营商业订立了价值一百万马克的皮革用品和其他日用品的定货合同，手工业者今年将比他们正常的产量增加生产价值一百一十万马克的家用电气器具、运动器械和鞋子。皮货商接到了总值两百六十万马克的定货合同。
