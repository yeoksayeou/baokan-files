Title: China People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Hosts Delegation from France-China Friendship Association
Author: 
Date: 1953-09-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 17th: The President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Xiruo, hosted a banquet in Beijing on the evening of the 17th to welcome all members of the delegation from the France-China Friendship Association, headed by Henri Dougnac.

Those attending the banquet as hosts included: Zhou Gengsheng, Chen Hansheng, Hu Yuzhi, and Qian Duansheng, Vice Presidents of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Wu Maosun, Secretary-General; Zhu Xuefan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Sha Kefu, Secretary of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles; Zou Dexin, Deputy Director of the International Working Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Wang Yi, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the People's Daily; Qu Jiwu, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the Worker's Daily; and many well-known figures from the literary, scientific, and educational circles, including Xiao San, Cao Yu, Hua Luogeng, Liang Sicheng, Pei Wenzhong, and Fang Shishan.

Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, delivered a speech warmly welcoming the friends who had come from afar. He said that the arrival of the delegation from the France-China Friendship Association signifies the increasing friendship between the Chinese and French peoples who love freedom and peace. He said that in the great cause of defending peace, the Chinese and French people have been, and will inevitably continue to be, very closely united. He said that through this visit of the delegation, the mutual understanding and friendly relations between the Chinese and French people will surely be further consolidated and developed.

The head of the delegation, Henri Dougnac, delivered a speech in reply. He said that the victory of the Chinese people's liberation struggle has shortened the distance between the Chinese and French people. He praised the democratic independence, freedom, and great achievements made by the Chinese people after liberation, as well as the happy life of the Chinese people. He said that all of this will further strengthen the delegates' confidence in their struggle for the liberation of all mankind. He expressed that the delegates will do everything possible to promote cultural and economic exchanges between the two peoples.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests repeatedly toasted to the friendship between the Chinese and French people and to world peace.

### 中国人民外交学会招待法中友协代表团

1953-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民外交学会招待法中友协代表团
    【新华社十七日讯】中国人民外交学会会长张奚若于十七日晚在北京设宴欢迎以亨利·窦尼为首的法中友好协会代表团全体团员。
    出席宴会作陪的有：中国人民外交学会副会长周鲠生、陈翰笙、胡愈之、钱端升、秘书长吴茂荪，中华全国总工会副主席朱学范，中华全国文学艺术界联合会秘书长沙可夫，中华全国民主妇女联合会国际工作部副部长邹得心，人民日报副总编辑王揖，工人日报副总编辑曲跻武，以及著名的文学界、科学界、教育界人士萧三、曹禺、华罗庚、梁思成、裴文中、方石珊等多人。
    中国人民外交学会会长张奚若致词向由远道而来的朋友们表示热烈欢迎。他说，法中友协代表团的来临，意味着热爱自由与和平的中法两国人民之间友好关系的日益增进。他说，在保卫和平的伟大事业中，中国和法国人民过去曾经是，今后也必然会极其紧密地团结在一起。他说，通过代表团这次访问，中法两国人民之间的相互了解和友谊关系，一定会得到更进一步的巩固和发展。
    代表团团长亨利·窦尼致答词。他说，中国人民解放斗争的胜利缩短了中法两国人民间的距离。他赞扬中国人民在解放后所取得的民主独立自由和巨大的成就以及中国人民的幸福生活。他说这一切将使代表们更增强他们为全人类的解放而斗争的信心。他表示代表们将尽一切努力来促进两国人民间文化和经济的交流。
    席间，宾主一再为中法两国人民的友谊和世界和平干杯。
