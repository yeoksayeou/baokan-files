Title: Korean Delegation Lays Wreath at Lenin and Stalin Mausoleum
Author:
Date: 1953-09-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 13th: The government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Cabinet Premier Kim Il-sung, laid a wreath at the Lenin and Stalin Mausoleum on September 12th. The inscription on the wreath read: "Respectfully dedicated to the great leader of the working people of the world, V.I. Lenin, and his great successor, Stalin, from the government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea."
Accompanying the Cabinet Premier were: Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, Park Jeong-ae; Vice-Premier of the Korean Cabinet, Jeong Il-ryong; Minister of Foreign Affairs, Nam Il; Chairman of the State Planning Commission, Jeong Jun-taek; Minister of Railways, Kim Hoe-il; Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the Soviet Union, Rim Hae; and other officials.

### 朝鲜代表团在列宁和斯大林陵墓前献花圈

1953-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜代表团在列宁和斯大林陵墓前献花圈
   【新华社十三日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：九月十二日，以内阁首相金日成为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团在列宁和斯大林陵墓前献花圈，花圈上面的题词是：“谨献给世界劳动人民的伟大领袖乌·伊·列宁和他的事业的光辉承继者伟大的斯大林，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团”。
  陪同内阁首相的有：朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱，朝鲜内阁副首相郑一龙，外务相南日，国家计划委员会委员长郑准泽、铁道相金会一、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使林海以及其他官员。
