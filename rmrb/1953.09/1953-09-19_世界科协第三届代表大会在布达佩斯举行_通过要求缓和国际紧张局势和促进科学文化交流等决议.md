Title: World Federation of Scientific Workers Third Congress Held in Budapest; Resolutions Passed Calling for Relaxation of International Tensions and Promotion of Scientific and Cultural Exchange

Author:

Date: 1953-09-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 18th: The Third Congress of the World Federation of Scientific Workers was held in Budapest from the 12th to the 15th. The conference heard reports from representatives of various countries and discussed the issue of international exchange of scientific information and the dispatch of scientific delegations. Tu Changwang, head of the Chinese delegation attending the meeting, spoke during the discussion.

The conference passed three resolutions. The first resolution called for easing international tensions, pointing out that science should serve the promotion of peace and human welfare, and called on scientists around the world to influence their countries to resolve international disputes peacefully. The second resolution stated that the World Federation of Scientific Workers would recommend that governments hold international consultations to eliminate weapons of mass destruction, such as atomic bombs, hydrogen bombs, chemical weapons, and bacteriological weapons. Another resolution called for promoting scientific and cultural exchange and opposing restrictions on academic freedom. The resolution called for exchanging information on scientific activities and scientists from various countries, exchanging scientific publications, and visits by scientific figures through the World Federation of Scientific Workers.

### 世界科协第三届代表大会在布达佩斯举行  通过要求缓和国际紧张局势和促进科学文化交流等决议

1953-09-19
第4版()
专栏：

    世界科协第三届代表大会在布达佩斯举行
    通过要求缓和国际紧张局势和促进科学文化交流等决议
    【新华社十八日讯】布达佩斯消息：世界科学工作者协会第三届代表大会已于十二日到十五日在布达佩斯举行。会议听取了各国代表的报告，并讨论了国际间互通科学消息和互派科学代表团的问题。出席会议的我国代表团团长涂长望曾在讨论中发言。
    会议通过了三个决议。第一个决议要求缓和国际紧张局势，指出科学应为促进和平和人类福利服务，并号召全世界科学工作者影响其国家以和平方式解决国际纠纷。第二个决议指出：世界科协将建议各国政府举行国际协商，以达到消除大规模屠杀人类武器，如原子弹、氢弹、化学武器和细菌武器等。另一个决议要求促进科学文化交流，反对学术自由受到限制。决议要求通过世界科协交换科学活动的消息和各国科学工作者的消息、交换科学刊物及科学界人物的访问。
