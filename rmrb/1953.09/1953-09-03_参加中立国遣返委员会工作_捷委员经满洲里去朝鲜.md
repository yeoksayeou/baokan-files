Title: Czechoslovakian Committee Member En Route to Korea via Manzhouli to Participate in Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission

Author:

Date: 1953-09-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Manzhouli, 1st -- At the invitation of both sides of the Korean armistice, Czechoslovakian member of the Korean Armistice Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary Ladislav Simovic, and political advisor, Minister Plenipotentiary Pavel Winkler, along with the first group of staff members from their country participating in the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission, have arrived in Manzhouli and have departed for Korea.

The welcoming party at the station included: Su Lin, the Mayor of Manzhouli City, and Wang Sihe, the Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, among others.

### 参加中立国遣返委员会工作  捷委员经满洲里去朝鲜

1953-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

    参加中立国遣返委员会工作
    捷委员经满洲里去朝鲜
    【新华社满洲里一日电】应朝鲜停战双方邀请，参加朝鲜停战中立国遣返委员会工作的捷克斯洛伐克委员、特命全权大使拉吉斯拉夫·西莫维茨和政治顾问、特命全权公使帕维尔·温克勒，偕同该国参加中立国遣返委员会的首批工作人员已经抵达满洲里，并已动身赴朝鲜。
    赴车站迎送的有：满洲里市市长苏林和内蒙古自治区人民政府外事处副处长王思和等。
