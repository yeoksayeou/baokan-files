Title: Embassy of the Mongolian People's Republic Thanks Various Circles in Our Country for Condolences on the Death of Bumtsend
Author:
Date: 1953-09-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency:
    Embassy of the Mongolian People's Republic
    Letter of Thanks to Various Circles in Our Country for Condolences on the Death of Bumtsend
    [Xinhua News Agency] The Embassy of the Mongolian People's Republic in China sent a letter to thank various organizations and people from all walks of life in our country for their condolences on the death of Chairman Bumtsend. The letter of thanks stated:
    When Gonchigin Bumtsend, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic and Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, passed away, we received condolences and mourning from various organizations and people from all walks of life. The Embassy of the Mongolian People's Republic in China hereby expresses its deep gratitude.

### 蒙古人民共和国驻我国大使馆  函谢我国各界对布曼增迪逝世的吊唁

1953-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民共和国驻我国大使馆
    函谢我国各界对布曼增迪逝世的吊唁
    【新华社讯】蒙古人民共和国驻华大使馆致函答谢我国各机关团体及各界人士对布曼增迪主席的吊唁。谢函称：
    当蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席、蒙古人民革命党中央委员会政治局委员冈奇金·布曼增迪逝世之时，承蒙各机关团体及各界人士吊慰和悼念，蒙古人民共和国驻华大使馆谨此表示深沉的谢意。
