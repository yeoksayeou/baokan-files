Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-09-15
Page: 1

Our great motherland's state-owned transportation departments are transporting products with guaranteed sales from regions rich in resources to all parts of the country. This is a ship docked at the Shanghai pier, which is loading a large number of winter clothing items, ready to be shipped to various places.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-09-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国国营交通运输部门正从货源丰富的区域将销路有保证的产品，运到各地去。这是停泊在上海码头的轮船，它正在装运大批冬季衣料，准备运到各地去。
