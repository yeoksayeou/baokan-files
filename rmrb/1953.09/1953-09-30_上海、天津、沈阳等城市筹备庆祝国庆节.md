Title: Shanghai, Tianjin, Shenyang and Other Cities Prepare to Celebrate National Day
Author:
Date: 1953-09-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency report: People in Shanghai, Tianjin, Shenyang, Chongqing, Nanjing, Harbin, Lüda, Changchun, Jilin and other cities are actively preparing to celebrate National Day.

    Shanghai is actively preparing for the celebration of the fourth National Day. The whole city is carrying out festive decorations. Workers, students, and office staff are using their spare time to practice new group dances to participate in the National Day evening parties. On the eve of National Day, Shanghai will hold a peace torch relay race with thousands of participants. The largest state-owned department store in China, the Shanghai No. 1 Department Store, has officially opened on Nanjing Road. It will provide ample goods to Shanghai people to celebrate National Day. Workers in Shanghai are launching a campaign to increase production and practice economy, striving to over fulfill production plans to welcome the first National Day of the first five-year construction plan. Employees of the XinHu Steel Plant are working hard to complete the annual production plan around National Day. The Ma Tie workshop of the factory has completed 170% of the September state plan on the 23rd. Female worker Wang Shuangfeng has created a new record of completing 182% of the production task. In the days leading up to National Day, people are even more reminiscent of the Chinese People's Volunteers standing guard on the front lines of peace. Workers in many factories are rushing to make various comfort items, striving to send them to the most beloved people as soon as possible.
    On the eve of National Day, the broad masses of workers in Tianjin are enthusiastically launching a production increase and economy practice campaign, striving to overfulfill the state plan and increase production and practice economy by two trillion yuan. While preparing to celebrate National Day, the people of the city especially miss the Chinese and Korean people's armies who are defending peace. Recently, the Tianjin branch of the Resist America and Aid Korea Association has received more than 12,000 letters of appreciation from people of all walks of life requesting to be forwarded to Korea.
    The Shenyang Municipal Celebration of National Day Preparatory Committee has decided to mobilize people from all walks of life to work hard to increase production and practice economy as the central theme of this year's National Day celebration propaganda. The cultural and educational departments and mass organizations have formulated propaganda plans and have widely launched propaganda.
    People from all walks of life in Chongqing are also actively preparing to celebrate National Day. The city is launching a propaganda upsurge, mobilizing workers, peasants, and other people from all walks of life to actively participate in the labor competition to increase production and practice economy.

### 上海、天津、沈阳等城市筹备庆祝国庆节

1953-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

    上海、天津、沈阳等城市筹备庆祝国庆节
    据新华社讯：上海、天津、沈阳、重庆、南京、哈尔滨、旅大、长春、吉林等城市人民积极筹备庆祝国庆节。
    上海市人民庆祝第四届国庆节的筹备工作正在积极进行。全市正热烈进行节日的装饰工作。工人、学生、机关工作者都在利用业余时间练习新的集体舞，准备参加国庆节晚上的联欢会。国庆节前夕，上海市将举行一次有数千人参加的和平火炬接力赛跑。在南京路上的一座我国规模最大的国营百货商店——国营上海市第一百货商店已正式开幕。它以充足的货物供应上海市人民欢度国庆。上海市的工人们都以开展增产节约竞赛争取超额完成生产计划来迎接祖国第一个五年建设计划第一年的国庆节。新沪钢铁厂职工正努力争取在国庆节前后完成全年的生产计划。这个厂的马铁车间已在二十三日完成了九月份国家计划的百分之一百七十。女工王双凤曾创造了完成生产任务百分之一百八十二的新纪录。在迎接国庆的日子里，人们更加怀念站在保卫和平前哨的中国人民志愿军。许多工厂的工人们正在赶制各种慰问品，争取早日送给最可爱的人。
    国庆节前夕，天津市广大职工正在热烈展开增产节约竞赛，为超额完成国家计划和增产节约两万亿元而努力。全市人民在筹备庆祝国庆的时候，特别怀念保卫和平的中、朝人民军队。最近，天津市抗美援朝分会已收到各界人民要求转寄到朝鲜的一万二千多封慰问信。
    沈阳市庆祝国庆节筹备委员会决定以动员各界人民努力增产节约为今年庆祝国庆节的宣传中心，各文教部门和群众团体已经制订了宣传计划，并已广泛展开宣传。
    重庆市各界人民也正在积极筹备庆祝国庆节。全市正在掀起一个宣传高潮，动员全市工人、农民及其他各界人民积极参加增产节约的劳动竞赛。
