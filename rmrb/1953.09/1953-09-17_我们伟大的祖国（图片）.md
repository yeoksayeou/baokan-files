Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author: 
Date: 1953-09-17
Page: 1

    Workers at the Xujiapeng Cotton Farm in Hubei Province, our great motherland, have worked hard to overcome natural disasters and achieved a bumper cotton harvest. These female cotton farm workers are sorting the cotton that has just been picked from the cotton fields.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-09-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国湖北省徐家棚棉场的工人们，努力战胜了自然灾害，获得棉花丰收。这些棉场女工正在整理着刚从棉田里摘回来的棉花。
