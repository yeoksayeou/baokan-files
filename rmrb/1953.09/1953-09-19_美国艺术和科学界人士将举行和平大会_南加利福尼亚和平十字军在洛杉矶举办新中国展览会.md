Title: American Art and Science Figures to Hold Peace Conference; Southern California Peace Crusaders to Hold New China Exhibition in Los Angeles
Author: 
Date: 1953-09-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 18th. The New York "Worker Daily" reported on the 15th that the peace conference initiated by the National Committee of American Arts, Sciences, and Professions will be held in New York City on September 27th. At the conference, renowned scholars and scientists will express their views on how to "move from the Korean armistice to world peace."

Speaking at the mass meeting will be black leader and historian Dr. W.E.B. Du Bois, atomic physicist Professor Philip Morrison, and archaeologist and Asian studies expert Professor Arthur Upham Pope.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 18th. According to the American "People's World" on the 14th, the New China Exhibition hosted by the Southern California Peace Crusaders will open in Los Angeles on the 18th. The exhibition will showcase Chinese paper-cuts, New Year pictures depicting the lives of the people in New China, and copies of the Dunhuang murals.

### 美国艺术和科学界人士将举行和平大会  南加利福尼亚和平十字军在洛杉矶举办新中国展览会

1953-09-19
第4版()
专栏：

    美国艺术和科学界人士将举行和平大会
    南加利福尼亚和平十字军在洛杉矶举办新中国展览会
    【新华社柏林十八日电】纽约“工人日报”十五日报道，美国艺术、科学及自由职业全国委员会所发起的和平大会，将于九月二十七日在纽约市举行，会上著名的学者和科学家将就如何“从朝鲜停战走向世界和平”的问题发表他们的见解。
    在群众大会上演讲的将有黑人领袖、历史学家杜波依斯博士，原子物理学家菲利普·摩里逊教授、考古学家与亚洲学专家阿瑟·厄巴姆·鲍普教授。
    【新华社柏林十八日电】据美国“人民世界报”十四日消息，南加利福尼亚和平十字军主办的新中国展览会将于十八日在洛杉矶开幕。展览会上将展出中国的窗花、描写新中国人民生活的年画以及敦煌壁画的摹本。
