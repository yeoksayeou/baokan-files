Title: Celebrating the Eighth Anniversary of the Founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Vietnamese Ambassador to China, Hoang Van Hoan, Holds Reception; Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Premier Zhou Enlai Invited to Attend
Author:
Date: 1953-09-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 2nd: Vietnamese Ambassador to China, Hoang Van Hoan, held a reception in Beijing on the evening of the 2nd to celebrate the eighth anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. Invited to attend were: Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government Liu Shaoqi, Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Huang Yanpei, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Central People's Revolutionary Military Commission Huang Kecheng, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government Zhang Hanfu, as well as heads of various departments of the Central People's Government, leaders of various democratic parties and people's organizations, and Deputy Mayor of Beijing Zhang Youyu, totaling nearly 400 people.

Also invited to attend were diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China.

Ambassador Hoang Van Hoan delivered a speech at the reception, saying that under the wise leadership of the Vietnam Workers' Party and President Ho Chi Minh, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam is growing stronger day by day. He pointed out that the achievements of the Vietnamese people are inseparable from the support of the fraternal countries of the peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union and the support of peace-loving people all over the world. He said that China is Vietnam's great neighbor, and in recent years, the inherent friendship between the people of Vietnam and China has become increasingly close and consolidated.

Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi then spoke. He said that the heroic Vietnamese people have already achieved significant victories in their struggle against foreign aggression and for national independence and liberation. The victory of the Vietnamese people has greatly weakened the forces of imperialist aggressors and has thereby made an important contribution to peace in Asia and the world. I am confident that the just struggle of the Vietnamese people, under the leadership of President Ho Chi Minh and with the sympathy and support of peace-loving people all over the world, will surely achieve even more brilliant victories.

Soviet Ambassador Kuznetsov also delivered a congratulatory speech at the reception.

The reception was filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere. The Vietnamese Embassy in China also held a film reception at the Capital Cinema on the evening of the 1st, showing the documentary "Great Victory in the Northwest" of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

In addition: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of our country and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai sent telegrams to President Ho Chi Minh and Foreign Minister Hoang Minh Giam of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on August 30 respectively, congratulating them on the eighth anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

### 庆祝越南民主共和国成立八周年  越南驻华大使黄文欢举行招待会  刘少奇副主席和周恩来总理等应邀出席

1953-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝越南民主共和国成立八周年
    越南驻华大使黄文欢举行招待会
    刘少奇副主席和周恩来总理等应邀出席
    【新华社二日讯】越南民主共和国驻华大使黄文欢于二日晚在北京举行招待会，庆祝越南民主共和国成立八周年纪念。应邀出席的有：中央人民政府副主席刘少奇，政务院总理周恩来、副总理黄炎培，中央人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长黄克诚，中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫，以及中央人民政府各部门首长、各民主党派、各人民团体负责人和北京市人民政府副市长张友渔等近四百人。
    应邀出席的还有各国驻华使节和外交官员。
    黄文欢大使在会上致词说，在越南劳动党和胡志明主席的英明领导下，越南民主共和国一天天地壮大起来。他指出：越南人民的成就，是和以苏联为首的和平民主阵营的各兄弟国家的支持及全世界爱好和平的人民的支持分不开的。他说：中国是越南的伟大邻邦，近几年来，越、中两国人民间固有的友谊，已日益密切而更趋巩固。
    刘少奇副主席接着讲话。他说：英勇的越南人民在反对外国侵略、争取祖国独立和解放的斗争中已经获得了重大的胜利。越南人民的胜利大大地削弱了帝国主义侵略者的力量，并从而对亚洲及世界的和平作了重要的贡献。我确信：越南人民的正义斗争在胡志明主席的领导下，在全世界爱好和平人民的同情与支持下，必将获得更加辉煌的胜利。
    苏联大使库兹涅佐夫也在招待会上致词祝贺。
    招待会充满热烈友好的气氛。越南驻华大使馆并于一日晚假首都电影院举行了电影招待会，放映越南民主共和国纪录片“西北大捷”。
    又讯：我国中央人民政府毛泽东主席和周恩来外交部长于八月三十日分别致电越南民主共和国主席胡志明和外交部长黄明鉴，祝贺越南民主共和国成立八周年纪念。
