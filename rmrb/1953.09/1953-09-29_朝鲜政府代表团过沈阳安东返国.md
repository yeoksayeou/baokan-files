Title: Korean Government Delegation Passes Through Shenyang and Andong on Return Trip Home
Author:
Date: 1953-09-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, September 28th: The government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Premier Kim Il-sung, arrived in Shenyang by special plane on the afternoon of the 28th, on its way home after visiting the Soviet Union.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included: Lin Feng, First Deputy Secretary of the Northeast Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Chairman of the Northeast Administrative Committee; Zhang Xiushan, Second Deputy Secretary of the Northeast Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Wang Jinxiang, Vice Chairman of the Northeast Administrative Committee; He Jinnian, Deputy Commander of the Northeast Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Huang Oudong, Secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China; Jiao Ruoyu, Mayor of the Shenyang Municipal People's Government; and Ning Wu, Zhang Weizhen, Xu Guang and other leaders of democratic parties and people's organizations in the Northeast.

Also present at the airport to welcome the delegation were Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, who was specially sent by our Central People's Government Ministry of Foreign Affairs to welcome the delegation, and Lu Xi, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Northeast Administrative Committee.

Wei Lixiajin, Consul General of the Soviet Consulate General in Shenyang, Deputy Consul Pralov, and Xu Yingde, Director of the Shenyang Liaison Office of the Korean Embassy in China, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

Premier Kim Il-sung and all members of the delegation shook hands with the welcomers after disembarking from the plane. The national flags of Korea and China were hung at the airport. The band played the national anthems of Korea and China. Premier Kim Il-sung and other members of the delegation reviewed the guard of honor.

After a short rest, the delegation left Shenyang by special train to return home.

Xinhua News Agency, Andong, September 29th: The government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Premier Kim Il-sung, arrived in Andong, our country's border city, by special train from Shenyang at 0:19 on the 29th. Accompanying the delegation to our country's border were Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of our Central People's Government, and Lu Xi, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Northeast Administrative Committee.

Greeting and seeing off the delegation at Andong Railway Station were: Gao Yang, Secretary of the Liaodong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Li Tao, Chairman of the Liaodong Provincial People's Government, Wang Ping, Deputy Political Commissar of the Liaodong Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Chen Beichen, Mayor of the Andong Municipal People's Government.

The national flags of Korea and China were hung at the station, and the band played the national anthems of Korea and China.

Before leaving our country's border, Premier Kim Il-sung and the members of the delegation reviewed the guard of honor.

### 朝鲜政府代表团过沈阳安东返国

1953-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜政府代表团过沈阳安东返国
    【新华社沈阳二十八日电】以金日成首相为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团，在访苏归国途中，于二十八日下午乘专机抵达沈阳。
    前往机场欢迎的，有：中共中央东北局第一副书记、东北行政委员会副主席林枫，中共中央东北局第二副书记张秀山，东北行政委员会副主席汪金祥，中国人民解放军东北军区副司令员贺晋年，中共沈阳市委书记黄欧东，沈阳市人民政府市长焦若愚和东北各民主党派、人民团体负责人宁武、张维桢、徐光等。
    到机场欢迎的，还有：我中央人民政府外交部特派前来欢迎代表团的外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航和东北行政委员会外事处处长陆曦。
    苏联驻沈阳总领事馆总领事魏立夏金、副领事普拉洛夫和朝鲜驻我国大使馆沈阳联络所所长徐应悳也到机场欢迎。
    金日成首相和代表团全体团员下机后与欢迎者一一握手。机场上悬有朝、中两国国旗。乐队高奏朝、中两国国歌。金日成首相和代表团其他人员检阅了仪仗队。
    代表团略事休息后，即乘专车离沈阳返国。
    【新华社安东二十九日电】以金日成首相为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团，在二十九日零时十九分乘专车自沈阳抵达我国国境安东。陪送代表团至我国国境者，有我中央人民政府外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航和东北行政委员会外事处处长陆曦。
    在安东车站上迎接和欢送代表团的，有：中共辽东省委书记高扬、辽东省人民政府主席李涛、中国人民解放军辽东军区副政治委员王屏和安东市人民政府市长陈北辰。
    车站上悬挂着朝中两国国旗，乐队奏起朝中两国国歌。
    金日成首相和代表团团员在离开我国国境前，曾检阅仪仗队。
