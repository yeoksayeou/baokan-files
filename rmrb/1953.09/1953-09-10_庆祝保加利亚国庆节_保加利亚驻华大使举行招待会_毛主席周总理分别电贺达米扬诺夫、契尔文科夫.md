Title: Celebrating Bulgarian National Day: Bulgarian Ambassador to China Holds Reception; Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Congratulatory Telegrams to Damyanov and Chervenkov
Author:
Date: 1953-09-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 9th—To celebrate the National Day of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to China, Petkov, held a reception in Beijing on the afternoon of the 9th.

Those invited to attend the reception included: Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government of China; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council; Huang Yanpei, Vice Premier; Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; heads of various departments of the Central People's Government; heads of various people's organizations; and Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, totaling more than 400 people. Also invited were diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China.

Ambassador Petkov and Vice Chairman Li Jishen delivered speeches at the reception, mutually wishing for the eternal friendship between the people of Bulgaria and China, the continuous new achievements of the people of both countries in national construction, and the ever-growing strength of the world's peace and democracy camp. Soviet Ambassador to China Kuznetsov also delivered a speech at the meeting to congratulate.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere. During the reception, the hosts and guests frequently toasted the friendship between the people of Bulgaria and China, the health of the leaders of the two peoples, and the health of the great Soviet Union and its leaders.

After the reception, a dance was held.

In addition: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government and Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council have respectively sent telegrams to Ge. Damyanov, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, and Vo. Chervenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, to congratulate them on the National Day of the People's Republic of Bulgaria.

### 庆祝保加利亚国庆节  保加利亚驻华大使举行招待会  毛主席周总理分别电贺达米扬诺夫、契尔文科夫

1953-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝保加利亚国庆节
    保加利亚驻华大使举行招待会
    毛主席周总理分别电贺达米扬诺夫、契尔文科夫
    【新华社九日讯】为庆祝保加利亚人民共和国国庆节，保加利亚人民共和国驻我国大使彼得科夫于九日下午在北京举行招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的有：我中央人民政府副主席李济深，政务院总理周恩来，副总理黄炎培，人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长张宗逊，外交部副部长伍修权及中央人民政府各部门首长，各人民团体负责人，和北京市人民政府副市长张友渔等四百余人，应邀到会的还有各国驻华使节和外交官员。
    彼得科夫大使和李济深副主席在招待会上相继致词，互祝保中两国人民的永久友谊，两国人民在国家建设中不断获得新的成就，并祝世界和平民主阵营的力量日益强大。苏联驻我国大使库兹湼佐夫也在会上致词祝贺。
    招待会在热烈友好气氛中进行。席间，宾主频频为保中两国人民的友谊、为两国人民领袖的健康而干杯，为伟大的苏联及其领袖们的健康而干杯。
    会后，并举行了舞会。
    又讯：我中央人民政府毛泽东主席和政务院周恩来总理曾分别致电保加利亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席格·达米扬诺夫和部长会议主席伏·契尔文科夫，祝贺保加利亚人民共和国国庆节。
