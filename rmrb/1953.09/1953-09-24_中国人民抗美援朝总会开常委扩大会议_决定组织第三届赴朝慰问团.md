Title: China People's Anti-American Aid Korea General Assembly Holds Standing Committee Enlarged Meeting Decides to Organize the Third Delegation to North Korea

Author:

Date: 1953-09-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd: The China People's Anti-American Aid Korea General Assembly held an enlarged meeting of the Standing Committee on the 23rd. In order to express sympathy to the victorious Chinese People's Volunteers and the heroic Korean military and civilians, and to continue to strengthen the work of resisting America and aiding Korea, it was discussed and decided to organize the Third Chinese People's Delegation to North Korea.

Participating in the meeting were Guo Moruo, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Situ Meitang, Xing Xiping, Shao Lizi, Ma Yinchu, Zhang Xiruo, Zhang Naiqi, Xu Deheng, Liang Xi, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zemin, Jiang Nanxiang, Liao Chengzhi, Xiao San, and Luo Longji, all standing committee members of the Anti-American Aid Korea General Assembly.

Also participating in the meeting were: Xu Zhizhen, representative of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Tian Demin, chairman of the All-China Student Federation, Li Dequan, vice chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Huang Jieran, representative of the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, Wu Yunfu, representative of the China People's Relief Association, as well as Nie Zhen, chairman of the North China Anti-American Aid Korea General Branch, and heads of various departments of the Anti-American Aid Korea General Assembly.

The meeting was presided over by Chairman Guo Moruo. First, Liao Chengzhi, a member of the Standing Committee of the association, reported on the mission of the delegation. He said: The mission of this delegation is to widely convey to the volunteers the respect and sympathy of the people of the motherland, to convey the great achievements of domestic construction and the firm will of the people of the whole country to resist America and aid Korea, to encourage them to continue to work hard and to heighten their vigilance in order to strive for the precise realization of the Korean armistice agreement and to fight for a peaceful solution to the Korean issue. At the same time, the delegation will express to the Korean military and civilians the cordial respect of the Chinese people, as well as the determination of our people to actively support them in healing the wounds of war and to support their great cause of striving for peace, unification and independence. He said that this delegation includes representatives from various democratic parties, people's organizations, and ethnic groups, as well as labor models from industry and agriculture and representatives of the People's Liberation Army, with a total of more than 4,000 people.

Finally, everyone unanimously adopted Liao Chengzhi's report and elected He Long as the head of the delegation. The delegation will begin preparatory work immediately.

### 中国人民抗美援朝总会开常委扩大会议  决定组织第三届赴朝慰问团

1953-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民抗美援朝总会开常委扩大会议
    决定组织第三届赴朝慰问团
    【新华社二十三日讯】中国人民抗美援朝总会在二十三日召开常委扩大会议，为慰问胜利的中国人民志愿军和英雄的朝鲜军民，继续加强抗美援朝工作，讨论决定组织中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团。
    参加会议的有抗美援朝总会常委郭沫若、彭真、陈叔通、司徒美堂、邢西萍、邵力子、马寅初、张奚若、章乃器、许德珩、梁希、黄炎培、彭泽民、蒋南翔、廖承志、萧三、罗隆基。
    参加会议的还有：中华全国总工会代表许之桢、中华全国学生联合会主席田德民、中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全、中华全国工商业联合会筹备委员会代表黄玠然、中国人民救济总会代表伍云甫，以及华北抗美援朝总分会主席聂真和抗美援朝总会各部门负责人等。
    会议由郭沫若主席主持。首由该会常委廖承志报告慰问团的任务，他说：本届慰问团的任务，是向志愿军广泛传达祖国人民对他们的敬意和慰问，传达国内各项建设的伟大成就及全国人民抗美援朝的坚强意志，鼓舞他们继续努力，提高警惕，以争取朝鲜停战协定的确切实现，为和平解决朝鲜问题而奋斗。慰问团同时向朝鲜军民表达中国人民的亲切敬意，以及我国人民积极支援他们医治战争创伤和支持他们争取和平、统一与独立的伟大事业的决心。他说：本届慰问团包括各民主党派、各人民团体、各民族的代表人物和工、农业劳动模范及解放军的代表，全团人数将达四千多人。
    最后，大家一致通过廖承志的报告，并推选贺龙为慰问团总团长。慰问团即日开始筹备工作。
