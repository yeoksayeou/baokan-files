Title: Sixty-Five Captured Chinese People's Volunteer Army Soldiers Scheduled to Return Today
Author:
Date: 1953-09-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, September 26th. The Korean-Chinese side received a notification from the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission stating that sixty-five captured Chinese People's Volunteer Army soldiers requesting repatriation will be handed over to the Korean-Chinese side tomorrow.

This morning, a total of sixty-eight captured Chinese People's Volunteer Army soldiers under the supervision of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission applied to the Indian Custodial Forces for repatriation. In the afternoon, the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission's Enforcement Committee interrogated the prisoners of war who applied for repatriation. The applications of sixty-five prisoners of war have been approved and will be handed over to the Korean-Chinese side tomorrow.

### 志愿军被俘人员六十五名定今日遣返归来

1953-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

    志愿军被俘人员六十五名定今日遣返归来
    【新华社开城二十六日电】朝中方面接到中立国遣返委员会的通知说，明天将有要求遣返的六十五名中国人民志愿军被俘人员遣交给朝中方面。
    今天上午，在中立国遣返委员会看管下的中国人民志愿军被俘人员共六十八人向印度看管部队提出申请，要求遣返。下午，中立国遣返委员会生效委员会对申请遣返的战俘进行了讯问，其中六十五名战俘的申请已决定生效，并将于明日遣交给朝中方面。
