Title: Literary and Press Circles in the Capital Gather to Commemorate Julius Fučík
Author:
Date: 1953-09-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 7th: The Foreign Cultural Affairs Administration of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government held a commemorative meeting on the 7th to mark the tenth anniversary of the sacrifice of Julius Fučík, the Czechoslovak revolutionary writer, on September 8th this year.

　　The commemorative meeting was presided over by Hong Shen, Director of the Foreign Cultural Affairs Administration. More than 200 people from cultural and artistic circles and the press attended the meeting, including Lao She, Cao Jinghua, Zhou Libo, Cao Ming, Hua Shan, Chen Ming, Wu Xue, and others.

　　Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Kousal, and many embassy staff members were also invited to attend the commemorative meeting.

　　Writer Lao She gave a report at the meeting on Fučík's thought and spirit. He pointed out that Fučík's name has become a symbol not only for the people of Czechoslovakia but also for all the heroes fighting for peace throughout the world. His spirit and works have educated progressive humanity throughout the world, as well as tens of millions of Chinese youth. His spirit is closely linked with us and participates in our struggle today. We can only commemorate Fučík by working hard to continue fighting for the happiness and prosperity of mankind.

　　Ambassador Kousal spoke at the meeting, recounting Fučík's glorious and heroic deeds in fighting for the proletarian cause and for the independence and freedom of his motherland. He said that Fučík's noble qualities of loving the people, loving the motherland, and heroically and unyieldingly adhering to the revolutionary struggle have today become the pride of the liberated Czechoslovak people and the power that inspires them to fight for the construction of socialism. The Czechoslovak people also thank the Chinese people for their love of Fučík's works. Fučík's heroic name and legacy will be eternally linked with the indestructible friendship between the Chinese and Czech peoples.

　　At the meeting, two young literary and artistic workers from the China Youth Art Theater recited a passage from Fučík's work "Report from the Gallows" and his other work "Salute to the Red Army".

　　The Czechoslovak State Film Studio's documentary film "Julius Fučík" was also screened at the meeting.

### 首都文艺界和新闻界人士集会纪念伏契克

1953-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

    首都文艺界和新闻界人士集会纪念伏契克
    【新华社七日讯】今年九月八日是捷克斯洛伐克革命作家尤利乌斯·伏契克牺牲十周年，中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局特在七日举行纪念会。
    纪念会由对外文化联络事务局局长洪深主持。参加纪念会的有文化艺术界和新闻界人士老舍、曹靖华、周立波、草明、华山、陈明、吴雪等二百多人。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使康萨拉和使馆人员多人也应邀出席了纪念会。
    作家老舍在会上作了关于伏契克的思想和精神的报告。他指出：伏契克的名字不仅成为捷克斯洛伐克人民、而且也是全世界为和平战斗的所有英雄们的一个象征。他的精神和作品教育了全世界进步的人类，也教育了千千万万的中国青年。他的精神和我们紧紧地联系在一起，也参加了我们的今天的斗争。我们只有努力继续为人类的幸福和繁荣而战斗，来纪念伏契克。
    康萨拉大使在会上讲话，叙述了伏契克的光辉的英勇的为无产阶级事业、为祖国的独立与自由而战斗的事迹。他说：伏契克的热爱人民、热爱祖国和英勇不屈地坚持革命斗争的高贵品质，今天已经成为解放了的捷克斯洛伐克人民的骄傲，和鼓舞他们为建设社会主义而斗争的力量。捷克斯洛伐克人民也感谢中国人民对于伏契克的著作的热爱。伏契克的英名和遗训将与中捷人民不可摧毁的友谊永世相连。
    会上，中国青年艺术剧院的两位青年文艺工作者朗诵了伏契克的作品“绞刑架下的报告”的一段和他的另一篇作品“向红军致敬”。
    会上还放映了捷克斯洛伐克国家制片厂出品的文献纪录影片“尤利乌斯·伏契克”。
