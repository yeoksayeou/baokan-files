Title: French-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing at Invitation to Visit China and Attend National Day Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1953-09-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 16th—At the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, the French-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, consisting of twelve members led by head of delegation Henri Douzon, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 16th for a friendly visit to China and to participate in our National Day celebrations.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included: Chen Hansheng and Qian Duansheng, Vice Presidents of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Wu Maosun, Secretary-General; Sha Kefu, Secretary of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles; Zhuang Yan, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Zou Dexin, Deputy Director of the International Work Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Wang Yi, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the People's Daily; Liang Sicheng and Shen Qiyi, representatives of the Beijing scientific community, and representatives of the religious community.

The list of the delegation is as follows: Head of Delegation: Henri Douzon (Professor of Political Economy at the Faculty of Law of the University of Rennes, President of the "Western Morning News" ) Members: Édouard Stoellès (Editor-in-Chief of "La Vie Ouvrière," member of the Steering Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat of the French-Chinese Friendship Association) Mrs. Germaine L'Hénaff (primary school teacher) Étienne Bathéas (Dean of the Faculty of Science at the University of Poitiers) Pierre Vellay (doctor, expert in painless childbirth) Max Stern (President of a construction cooperative, member of the editorial board of the Christian fortnightly magazine) Lucien Manteau (architect) Jacques Hédouin (metallurgical worker at the Renault factory) Léon Chausses (farmer) Cheffler Barberis (dockworker) Raymond Bertie (metallurgical worker at the Hispano-Suiza factory) Miss Noemi Marcel (textile worker)

### 应邀来我国访问并参加我国国庆典礼  法中友好协会代表团到达北京

1953-09-17
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国访问并参加我国国庆典礼
    法中友好协会代表团到达北京
    【新华社十六日讯】应中国人民外交学会邀请首次来我国作友谊访问并参加我国国庆典礼的法中友好协会代表团一行十二人，由团长亨利·窦尼率领，于十六日下午乘机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有：中国人民外交学会副会长陈翰笙、钱端升，秘书长吴茂荪，中华全国文学艺术界联合会秘书长沙可夫，中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长庄焰，中华全国民主妇女联合会国际工作部副部长邹得心，人民日报副总编辑王揖，北京科学界代表梁思成、沈其益及宗教界代表等多人。
    代表团名单如下：团长：亨利·窦尼（勒纳大学法学院政治经济学教
    授、“西方晨报”社社长）团员：爱德华·斯托拉斯（“工人生活报”总编辑、法中友好协会指导委员会委员兼秘书处秘书）歇曼娜·勒埃纳夫夫人（小学教员）艾蒂安娜·巴特（波亚叠大学理学院院长）皮埃尔·维莱（医生、无痛分娩法专家）马克斯·斯特恩（建筑合作社社长、基督教半月刊编辑委员）吕西恩·曼特里克（建筑师）雅克·埃敦（雷诺工厂冶金工人）利昂·邵斯（农民）歇弗勒·巴贝（码头工人）雷蒙·柏蒂（伊斯巴诺—瑞查工厂冶金工人）诺埃米·马赛尔小姐（纺织工人）
