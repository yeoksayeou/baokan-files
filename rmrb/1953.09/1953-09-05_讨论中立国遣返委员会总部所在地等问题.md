Title: Discussions on the Location of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission Headquarters and Other Issues
Author:
Date: 1953-09-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, September 4th: The secretaries of both sides of the Military Armistice Commission held a meeting on September 3rd with the Indian delegation participating in the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission to discuss issues such as the location of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission headquarters, and the accommodation and equipment for the Indian personnel of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission.

Brigadier General Singh, Deputy Commander of the Indian Custodial Forces, and Brigadier General White led the Indian delegation to the meeting. Brigadier General Singh put forward opinions on the accommodation and other facilities for the Indian personnel of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission and informed both secretaries that Lieutenant General Thimayya, Chairman of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission, and the Indian personnel of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission headquarters would arrive in two batches on September 9th and 15th. Lieutenant General Thimayya and his entourage will arrive first on the 9th.

After the meeting, the secretaries of both sides accompanied the Indian delegation to visit the headquarters area of the Military Armistice Commission and reached an agreement on the location of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission headquarters on the spot, deciding to begin the construction of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission headquarters and the accommodation facilities for the Indian personnel.

Colonel Zhu Ran, Secretary of the Chinese-North Korean side of the Military Armistice Commission, and Colonel Xu Ming, Assistant Secretary, invited the Indian delegation to lunch at Panmunjom, and then accompanied the Indian delegation to visit the barracks for the Indian Custodial Forces and the barracks for accommodating non-directly repatriated prisoners of war under our custody in Bausan-ri and Songgok-ri in the Demilitarized Zone.

Xinhua News Agency, September 3rd: Gajecki, Polish Commissioner and Minister Plenipotentiary, and Taykochinski, political advisor, who were invited by both sides of the Korean armistice to participate in the work of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission, have already left Beijing for Korea.

Those who went to the station to see them off included: Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Jiang Keding, Deputy Director of the Foreign Liaison Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, and Ma Zhenwu, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Beijing Municipal People's Government.

Kirilok, Ambassador of the Polish People's Republic to China, and Xu Zhe, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, also went to the station to see them off.

### 讨论中立国遣返委员会总部所在地等问题

1953-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜军事停战委员会双方秘书长和印度代表团举行会议
    讨论中立国遣返委员会总部所在地等问题
    波兰委员加耶夫斯基等去朝鲜参加中立国遣返委员会工作
    【新华社开城四日电】军事停战委员会双方秘书长在九月三日和参加中立国遣返委员会的印度代表团举行会议，讨论中立国遣返委员会总部所在地，和中立国遣返委员会印度人员的食宿地点与设备等问题。
    印度看管部队副司令官辛格准将和白佳准将率领印度代表团出席这次会议。辛格准将在会上提出关于中立国遣返委员会印度人员的住宿及其他设施的意见，并通知双方秘书长：中立国遣返委员会主席蒂迈雅中将和中立国遣返委员会总部印度人员将分两批在九月九日和十五日到达，蒂迈雅中将及其随行人员将在九日首先到达。
    双方秘书长在会后陪同印度代表团参观军事停战委员会总部地区，并在现场就中立国遣返委员会总部的所在地达成协议，决定开始进行中立国遣返委员会总部及印度人员住宿设备的修建工作。
    军事停战委员会朝中方面秘书长朱然上校和助理秘书长徐鸣上校邀请印度代表团在板门店共进午餐，随后并陪同印度代表团到非军事区内的钵山里和松谷里参观印度看管部队用的营舍和收容我方看管下的不直接遣返战俘的营舍。
    【新华社三日讯】应朝鲜停战双方邀请，参加朝鲜停战中立国遣返委员会工作的波兰委员、全权公使加耶夫斯基和政治顾问泰科琴斯基业已自北京动身前往朝鲜。
    赴车站送行的有：中央人民政府外交部交际处副处长沈平，人民革命军事委员会对外联络处副处长蒋克定和北京市人民政府外事处处长马振武等。
    波兰人民共和国驻华大使基里洛克和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办徐哲也到车站送行。
