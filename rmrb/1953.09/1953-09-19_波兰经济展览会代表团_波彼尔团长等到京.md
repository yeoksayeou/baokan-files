Title: Polish Economic Exhibition Delegation, Head Bo-bier and Others Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-09-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency Dispatch:

    The head of the Polish People's Republic Economic Exhibition Delegation, Minister of Shipping of Poland, Mi. Bo-bier, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 18th. Accompanying him were the Deputy Head of the Delegation, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of Poland, Gao. Bao-lie-si-la-fu, and delegation member Gu-ba-si-ji. A total of thirty-four delegation members have arrived in Beijing. Those who went to the airport to welcome them were: Ji Chao-ding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Wang Shou-dao, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Communications of the Central People's Government; Xu Xue-han, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and others. The Polish Ambassador to China, Kirilo, Commercial Counselor Goldberger, and embassy staff, as well as Kura and others, the head of the delegation who arrived earlier, also went to the airport to welcome them.
    The Polish Economic Exhibition is scheduled to open on September 26th at the Beijing Labor People's Cultural Palace. This exhibition is the largest exhibition held abroad by the Polish People's Republic. There are more than 2,600 exhibits in total, weighing more than 800 tons, including long-arm cranes, mining machinery, automobiles, machine tools, light industrial products, etc. During the exhibition, outstanding Polish workers will perform various operations and show movies.

### 波兰经济展览会代表团  波彼尔团长等到京

1953-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰经济展览会代表团
    波彼尔团长等到京
    据新华社讯：波兰人民共和国经济展览会代表团团长、波兰航运部部长米·波彼尔，于十八日乘飞机到达北京。同来的有代表团副团长、波兰对外贸易部副部长高·包列斯拉夫及团员古巴斯基。代表团先后到京的团员共计三十四人。前往机场欢迎的有：中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎，中央人民政府交通部副部长王首道，对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒等。波兰驻我国大使基里洛克，商务参赞高尔德格若伯和使馆人员，以及先期抵京的代表团负责人库拉等也到机场欢迎。
    波兰经济展览会定于九月二十六日在北京市劳动人民文化宫开幕，这次展览会是波兰人民共和国在国外举办的规模最大的一次展览会。全部展览品共有两千六百多件，共重八百多吨，其中包括长臂起重机、矿山机械、汽车、工作母机、轻工业产品等。展览期间，波兰优秀工人将进行各种操作表演，并放演电影。
