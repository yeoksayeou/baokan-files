Title: Kim Tu-bong Receives Representatives of Returning Korean People's Army Personnel
Author:
Date: 1953-09-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 1st: According to the Korean Central News Agency, Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, Park Jung-ai, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, and Choi Yong-kun, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of National Defense, received Kim Kuk-jin, Dong Rae-hong, and other representatives of the returning Korean People's Army personnel who had been captured on August 29th.

    At the reception, Kim Kuk-jin, on behalf of all returning personnel, expressed heartfelt gratitude to their beloved leader Marshal Kim Il-sung, the Workers' Party, and the Republic's government for rescuing them from the clutches of the enemy and bringing them a bright future again.

    Chairman Kim Tu-bong, on behalf of the Workers' Party and the Republic's government, extended warm welcome and regards to the representatives of the returning personnel, and inquired about the abuse and persecution they suffered in the US prisoner-of-war camps, their lives after returning to the embrace of the motherland, and their hopes and demands.

    Dong Rae-hong, a representative of the returning personnel, reported in detail on their resolute struggle in the US prisoner-of-war camps against forced detention and demanding repatriation, and angrily exposed the various atrocities committed by the US-Li gang in abusing and persecuting them. The representatives of the returning personnel asked Chairman Kim Tu-bong to convey their desire to participate in the great cause of building the motherland to their beloved leader Marshal Kim Il-sung. Chairman Kim Tu-bong accepted this request. He wished the returning personnel a speedy and complete recovery, diligent study, and participation in the glorious struggle to build the motherland.

### 金枓奉接见朝鲜人民军归来人员代表

1953-09-03
第4版()
专栏：

    金枓奉接见朝鲜人民军归来人员代表
    【新华社平壤一日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社报道：朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉、劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、内阁副首相兼民族保卫相崔庸健等，在八月二十九日接见了曾被俘的朝鲜人民军归来人员的代表金国镇、董来弘等十人。
    接见时，金国镇代表全体归来人员，向把他们从敌人的魔掌中拯救出来、并给他们重新带来光明前途的敬爱的领袖金日成元帅、劳动党及共和国政府表示衷心的感激。
    金枓奉委员长以劳动党与共和国政府的名义对归来人员的代表表示热烈欢迎和慰问，并询问了他们在美方战俘营中的被虐待和迫害的情况和返回祖国怀抱以后的生活情况，以及他们的希望和要求。
    归来人员代表董来弘详细地报告了他们在美方战俘营中为反对强迫扣留要求遣返而进行的坚决斗争，并愤怒地揭露了美李匪帮虐待和迫害他们的种种暴行。归来人员的代表们请金枓奉委员长把他们要求参加建设祖国的伟大事业的意愿转告敬爱的领袖金日成元帅。金枓奉委员长接受了这个请求。他祝归来人员们早日完全恢复健康，努力学习，参加建设祖国的光荣斗争。
