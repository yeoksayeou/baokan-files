Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-09-18
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The electrical engineering research section of the Changchun Comprehensive Research Institute of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, learning from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union, has created a 1 million-volt impulse test stand. This test stand can create artificial lightning, which is convenient for scientists to design various electrical appliances. This is a situation where the staff of the laboratory are conducting high-voltage impulse artificial lightning strike tests.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国科学院长春综合研究所电机研究室学习苏联先进经验，创造了一座一百万伏特的冲击试验台。这种试验台可以制造人工雷电，便利科学家设计各种电器设备。这是该室工作人员正在作高压冲击人工雷击试验的情况。
