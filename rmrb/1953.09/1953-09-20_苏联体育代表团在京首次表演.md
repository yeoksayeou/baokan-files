Title: Soviet Sports Delegation's First Performance in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1953-09-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th: The Soviet sports delegation held its first gymnastics performance in Beijing on the evening of the 19th. The performance was sponsored by the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission.

Among those attending the performance were Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government; Chen Yi, Wu Yuzhang, Rao Shushi, Zhang Zhizhong, Fu Zuoyi, Zhang Bojun, Zhang Xiruo, and other members of the Central People's Government; Li Dequan, Yang Xiufeng, Ding Xilin, Wu Xiuquan, Zhang Hanfu, Teng Daiyuan, Xiao Jingguang, Gan Siqi, Li Shucheng, and other heads of departments of the Central People's Government; and Hu Yaobang, Qian Junrui, and other heads of people's organizations, totaling more than 900 people.

Soviet Ambassador to China Kuznetsov and embassy staff were also invited to watch the performance.

At 8 o'clock, the opening ceremony was held. Cai Tingkai, Vice Chairman of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, delivered a speech. On behalf of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission and Chinese athletes, he expressed warm respect to the Soviet sports delegation and called on Chinese athletes to learn diligently from Soviet athletes in order to further develop China's sports.

In his speech, Gramadsky, head of the Soviet sports delegation, first paid tribute to the Chinese people. He said, "We hope that our gymnastics performance and the mutual exchange of sports experience between Chinese and Soviet athletes will help the development of sports in China and the Soviet Union, and will continue to consolidate the friendship between our two great peoples."

At 8:15, the performance began. As the silver curtain slowly rose, Muratov, the Soviet all-around champion, champion of the 4th World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival, and Merited Master of Sports, holding the Soviet flag, led twenty-five strong and robust outstanding male and female athletes onto the stage, the audience burst into thunderous applause. The Soviet athletes also shouted in Chinese, "Long live the great Chinese people!" The whole audience cheered at this moment.

Participating in the performance were all-around champions of the Soviet Union, Merited Masters of Sports, Master Athletes, champions of the 15th Olympic Games, and athletes of the 4th World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival. The program for the evening included: balance beam, uneven bars, vault (women's performance), horizontal bar, parallel bars, vault, pommel horse, rings (men's performance) and other apparatus gymnastics; they also performed a variety of free exercises. Each program aroused great interest and admiration from the audience.

Bochalova, who won the championship in the 15th Olympic Games, the all-around championship of the 4th World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival, and Merited Master of Sports, performed on the balance beam, and was warmly welcomed by the audience. With highly skilled techniques, she performed various beautiful rolls and dance postures on the balance beam. Zhukarin, who won the Soviet championship, the all-around championship of the 15th Olympic Games and the 4th World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival, and Merited Master of Sports, performed a variety of programs such as pommel horse, parallel bars, horizontal bar, and free exercises. His various movements were stable and skillful, and he was able to perform multiple somersaults on the parallel bars in succession. The audience repeatedly applauded enthusiastically for his wonderful performance.

Soviet champion, Merited Master of Sports, Urbanovich, and Soviet all-around champion, Master Athlete, Konovalova, and others' free exercises were also very successful. Muratov, the Soviet all-around champion, champion of the 4th World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival, and Merited Master of Sports, Azaryan, the Soviet champion and Master Athlete, and Korolkov, champion of the 4th World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival and Merited Master of Sports, all gave wonderful performances on the pommel horse, parallel bars, horizontal bar, and rings. Those who performed on the uneven bars included: Bochalova, Kalinchuk, who won the Soviet championship and the championship of the 15th Olympic Games, and Merited Master of Sports, Zhugli, the Soviet champion and Merited Master of Sports, and Zheleva, champion of the 4th World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival and Master Athlete, and many others. The free exercises of the Soviet male and female athletes won lasting applause from the audience. Their healthy bodies, combined with the pure skills of rolling, jumping, and circling, gave the audience a beautiful and pleasant feeling. The last item was the women's group exercise performance. Eight female athletes held rattan hoops and, to the melody of the piano accompaniment, lightly performed various beautiful poses with high skill. The audience repeatedly praised their performance.

The performance ended at 10:30. Representatives of Chinese athletes rushed to the stage to present flowers and thank the Soviet athletes. At this time, the entire audience stood up and applauded warmly. (Photo attached)

### 苏联体育代表团在京首次表演

1953-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联体育代表团在京首次表演
    【新华社十九日讯】十九日晚，苏联体育代表团在北京举行首次体操表演会。表演会由中央人民政府体育运动委员会主办。
    出席表演会的有中央人民政府副主席李济深，中央人民政府委员陈毅、吴玉章、饶漱石、张治中、傅作义、章伯钧、张奚若等及中央人民政府各部门负责人李德全、杨秀峰、丁西林、伍修权、章汉夫、滕代远、萧劲光、甘泗淇、李书城等以及各人民团体负责人胡耀邦、钱俊瑞等九百余人。
    苏联驻华大使库兹涅佐夫和使馆人员也应邀出席观看表演。
    八时，举行了开幕仪式。中央人民政府体育运动委员会副主任蔡廷锴致词。他代表中央人民政府体育运动委员会和中国运动员们向苏联体育代表团致热烈的敬意，并号召中国运动员努力向苏联运动员学习，以便进一步开展我国的体育运动。
    苏联体育代表团团长格拉玛德斯基在讲话中首先向中国人民致敬。他说，我们希望，我们的体操表演以及中苏两国运动员体育经验的互相交流，将会帮助中国与苏联体育的发展，将继续巩固我们两国伟大人民之间的友谊。
    八时一刻，表演会开始。当银色的帷幕徐徐上升，苏联绝对冠军、第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节冠军、功勋选手穆拉托夫举着苏联国旗率领二十五位身体健壮而魁梧的优秀男女运动员登上舞台时，全场掌声雷动，苏联运动员们也用中国话高呼：“伟大的中国人民万岁！”这时全场欢呼。
    参加表演的都是荣获苏联绝对冠军、功勋运动员、运动健将及第十五届奥林匹克运动会冠军和第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的运动员。今晚表演的节目有：平衡木、高低杠、木马（跳马）（以上女子表演）、单杠、双杠、木马（跳马）、木马（鞍马）、吊环（以上男子表演）等器械体操；他们还表演了多种多样的自由体操。表演的每一个节目，都引起了观众极大的兴趣和赞赏。
    曾荣获第十五届奥林匹克运动会冠军、第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节绝对冠军、功勋运动员柏查罗娃表演的平衡木，受到观众热烈欢迎，她以高度熟练技巧，在平衡木上做出各种优美的滚翻和舞蹈姿态。荣获苏联冠军、第十五届奥林匹克运动会和第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节绝对冠军、功勋运动员朱卡林表演了鞍马、双杠、单杠、自由体操等多种节目，他所表演的各种动作稳定熟练、操纵自如，并且能够在双杠上接连表演多次腾跃的动作。观众一再为他的精彩表演而热烈鼓掌。
    苏联冠军、功勋运动员乌尔芭诺维奇和苏联绝对冠军、运动健将科诺瓦洛娃等所表演的自由体操，都获得了很大的成功。苏联绝对冠军、第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节冠军、功勋运动员穆拉托夫，苏联冠军、运动健将阿札良，第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节冠军、功勋运动员科罗尔科夫等都作了木马（鞍马）、双杠、单杠、吊环等精彩表演。表演高低杠的有：柏查罗娃、有荣获苏联冠军和第十五届奥林匹克运动会冠军、功勋运动员卡琳楚克，苏联冠军、功勋运动员朱格丽和第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节冠军、运动健将热列耶娃等多人。苏联男女运动员们的自由体操博得观众经久不息的掌声。他们健美的身体，加上纯熟的技巧所表演的滚翻、跳跃、绕环等动作都给予观众一种优美的愉快的感觉。最后一个项目是女子团体操表演，八位女运动员手执藤圈，在钢琴伴奏的旋律下，轻盈地以高度的技巧表演了各种优美姿势，观众一再为她们的表演赞赏不绝。
    表演十时半结束，中国运动员代表涌向舞台向苏联运动员们献花致谢，这时，全场观众起立热烈鼓掌。（附图片）
