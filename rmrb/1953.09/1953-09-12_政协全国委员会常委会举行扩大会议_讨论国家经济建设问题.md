Title: Standing Committee of the CPPCC National Committee Holds Enlarged Meeting to Discuss National Economic Construction Issues
Author:
Date: 1953-09-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 11th. The 49th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference was held in an enlarged form from the 8th to the 11th. During the four-day meeting, Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government, Chen Yun, Member of the National Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, and Li Fuchun, Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the State Council, made reports on national economic construction issues and financial and economic work respectively. The meeting had a full discussion based on the above reports.
　　The following people attended the meeting: Zhou Enlai, Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Liu Shaoqi, Li Weihan, Jiang Guangnai, Zhang Bojun, Huang Yanpei, Zhang Naiqi, Ma Xulun, Zhang Xiruo, Yang Xiufeng, Liu Ningyi, Deng Yingchao, Shen Yanbing, Liang Xi, Chen Jiageng, Shao Lizi, Lin Boqu, Deng Xiaoping, Zhang Zhizhong, Ma Yinchu, Xu Guangping, Huang Qixiang, Xu Deheng, Jiang Nanxiang, Shi Liang, Qian Sanqiang, Sheng Pihua, Li Dequan, Liao Chengzhi and other thirty-two people.
　　The following people attended the meeting as observers: members of the National Committee in Beijing, members of the Central People's Government Committee, members of the State Council, heads of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, heads of the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, and heads of various people's organizations, totaling more than one hundred people.

### 政协全国委员会常委会举行扩大会议  讨论国家经济建设问题

1953-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会常委会举行扩大会议
    讨论国家经济建设问题
   【新华社十一日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会第四十九次会议，在八日至十一日扩大举行。在历时四天的会议中，中国人民政协全国委员会副主席、中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来，全国委员会委员、政务院副总理陈云，政务院财政经济委员会副主任李富春分别作了有关国家经济建设问题及财政、经济工作的报告。会议根据上述报告，作了充分讨论。
    出席此次会议的，有：中国人民政协全国委员会副主席周恩来、李济深、郭沫若、陈叔通，常务委员刘少奇、李维汉、蒋光鼐、章伯钧、黄炎培、章乃器、马叙伦、张奚若、杨秀峰、刘宁一、邓颖超、沈雁冰、梁希、陈嘉庚、邵力子、林伯渠、邓小平、张治中、马寅初、许广平、黄琪翔、许德珩、蒋南翔、史良、钱三强、盛丕华、李德全、廖承志等三十二人。
    列席这次会议的，有：全国委员会在京委员、中央人民政府委员会委员、政务院政务委员、中华全国总工会的负责人、中华全国工商业联合会筹备委员会负责人和各人民团体的负责人等共一百余人。
