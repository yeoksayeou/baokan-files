Title: "Labor News" of Korea Publishes Editorial Commenting on the Significance of the DPRK Government Delegation's Visit to the Soviet Union
Author: 
Date: 1953-09-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 2nd: The Korean "Labor News" published an editorial on September 2nd entitled "News that Aroused Enthusiastic Cheers" regarding the visit of the government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to Moscow. The editorial stated: At the invitation of the Soviet government, the government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Premier Kim Il-sung, departed for Moscow on September 1st. This news makes the Korean people express new gratitude to the great Soviet people, their benefactor in liberation and sincere supporter in building a democratic and independent country, and further strengthens their belief in the friendship and solidarity with the Soviet people. The delegation's visit to the Soviet Union is of great significance for consolidating and developing the friendship and solidarity between the Korean and Soviet peoples and will make a great contribution. It will lay the political and economic foundation for the peaceful reunification of the fatherland. The Korean people are making concerted efforts to victoriously complete this glorious task and build a peaceful life.

The editorial pointed out: The Soviet people welcomed and congratulated the Korean people's historical victory as if it were their own, and fervently wished the Korean people a happy life. The Soviet government has decided to provide the Korean people with 1 billion rubles in aid, and has recently decided on specific measures to implement this aid. The great Soviet Union's assistance will become a source of strength for the Korean people to achieve new results in the great cause of rebuilding their motherland, and will infinitely encourage the Korean people's firm confidence in peaceful construction.

The Soviet people, who wish the Korean people a peaceful life, not only assist the Korean people in restoring and developing the destroyed people's economy, but also make unremitting efforts to ensure the Korean people's peaceful life under the conditions that the American imperialists are manufacturing new aggression plans.

The editorial emphasized: The Korean people regard consolidating the fraternal friendship with the Soviet people as a guarantee of all their victories. The Korean people will further consolidate the friendship and solidarity with the great Soviet Union, the guarantee of all our victories, and rely on the consistent assistance of the Soviet Union to victoriously complete the glorious task of restoring and developing the post-war people's economy.

### 朝鲜“劳动新闻”发表社论  评朝政府代表团访苏的意义

1953-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜“劳动新闻”发表社论
    评朝政府代表团访苏的意义
    【新华社平壤二日电】朝鲜“劳动新闻”二日就朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团赴莫斯科访问发表题为“引起热烈欢呼的消息”的社论。社论说：应苏联政府的邀请以金日成首相为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团一行，已于九月一日启程赴莫斯科。这个消息，使朝鲜人民对自己的解放恩人、建设民主独立国家的诚挚援助者——伟大的苏联人民表示新的感谢，进一步加强了和苏联人民的友谊团结的信念。代表团的访问苏联，对于巩固和发展朝鲜人民和苏联人民的友谊团结，有着巨大的意义，并将有巨大贡献。奠定为和平统一祖国的政治与经济基础。朝鲜人民正在为胜利完成这一光荣任务与建设和平生活而一致努力。
    社论指出：苏联人民像自己的胜利似地欢迎和祝贺朝鲜人民所取得的历史性胜利，并热烈愿望朝鲜人民过幸福的生活。苏联政府已决定给予朝鲜人民以十亿卢布的援助，最近又决定了实现这个援助的具体措施。伟大的苏联如此的援助，将成为朝鲜人民在重建祖国的伟大事业中取得新的成果的力量源泉，无限地鼓舞了朝鲜人民和平建设的坚定信心。
    愿望朝鲜人民过和平生活的苏联人民不但援助朝鲜人民恢复和发展被破坏的人民经济，而且在美帝国主义者制造新的侵略计划的条件下，为保障朝鲜人民的和平生活而作着不懈的努力。
    社论强调指出：朝鲜人民把巩固与苏联人民的兄弟般的友谊关系，看作是自己的一切胜利的保证。朝鲜人民将进一步巩固与我们一切胜利的保证——伟大的苏联的友谊团结，依靠苏联始终如一的援助，胜利地完成恢复和发展战后人民经济的光荣任务。
