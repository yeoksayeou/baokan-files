Title: Buddhist Association President Venerable Yuan Ying Passes Away

Author: 
Date: 1953-09-22
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 21st: Venerable Yuan Ying, President of the Chinese Buddhist Association, passed away at Tiantong Temple in Ningbo, Zhejiang Province on the morning of September 20th due to esophageal cancer after a long period of unsuccessful treatment. He was 76 years old. In his "Will," he stated: "I wish all Buddhists throughout the country to be of one heart and one mind, actively participate in the movement to love and protect the motherland, and devote themselves to the cause of peace." The Chinese Buddhist Association has sent a telegram of condolence and entrusted Zhao Puchu, the Association's Vice President currently in Shanghai, to be responsible for handling the funeral arrangements.

### 佛教协会会长圆瑛法师病逝

1953-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

    佛教协会会长圆瑛法师病逝
    【新华社二十一日讯】中国佛教协会会长圆瑛法师，因患食道癌症经长期治疗无效，于九月二十日晨在浙江宁波天童寺逝世，享年七十六岁。他在“遗嘱”中表示：“愿我全国佛教徒，同心同德，积极参加爱护祖国运动，致力和平事业。”中国佛教协会已致电吊唁，并委托现在上海的该会副会长赵朴初负责料理治丧事宜。
