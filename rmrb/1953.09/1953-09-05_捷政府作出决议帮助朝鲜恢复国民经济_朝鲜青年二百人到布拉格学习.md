Title: Czechoslovak Government Resolves to Assist North Korea in Restoring the National Economy; Two Hundred Korean Youths Arrive in Prague to Study
Author:
Date: 1953-09-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 4th: According to a report by the Czechoslovak News Agency on the 2nd, the government of the Czechoslovak Republic has made a resolution to provide extensive assistance to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in its work of building and restoring the national economy, including material, scientific, and technological assistance.

The government of the Czechoslovak Republic announced that this resolution was made based on the universal concern of the Czechoslovak people for the peaceful and happy future of Korea. It expressed the sincere friendship between the people of Czechoslovakia and Korea.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague: According to a report in "Red Right News," on September 1st, approximately two hundred young Korean men and women arrived in Prague. They will study at technical colleges in Czechoslovakia so that they can better participate in the reconstruction of their motherland after returning home. Many of them participated in the Fatherland Liberation War and were rewarded for their merits and bravery. Upon their arrival in Prague, they were welcomed by the Deputy Minister of Higher Education of Czechoslovakia, K. Baiderna, and representatives of the Korean Embassy in Czechoslovakia.

These Korean youths will rest and recuperate for a month at Seibetov Castle in Moravia. This year, they will study Czech in order to begin their regular studies at Czechoslovak technical colleges next year.

### 捷政府作出决议帮助朝鲜恢复国民经济  朝鲜青年二百人到布拉格学习

1953-09-05
第4版()
专栏：

    捷政府作出决议帮助朝鲜恢复国民经济
    朝鲜青年二百人到布拉格学习
    【新华社布拉格四日电】据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社二日讯：捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府已作出决议，给朝鲜民主主义人民共和国建设和复兴国民经济的工作以广泛的帮助，其中包括物质、科学和技术各方面的帮助。
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府公告说，这个决议是根据捷克斯洛伐克人民对于朝鲜的和平与幸福前途的普遍关心而作出的。它表示了捷克斯洛伐克和朝鲜两国人民之间的真诚友谊。
    【新华社布拉格讯】据“红色权利报”报道，九月一日，约有二百个朝鲜男女青年抵达布拉格。他们将在捷克斯洛伐克的技术学院学习，以便将来回国后能更好地参加祖国的重建工作。他们中间有许多人曾经参加过祖国解放战争，并以他们的功绩和勇敢得到了奖励。他们到达布拉格时受到了捷克斯洛伐克高等教育部副部长卡·拜德尔纳和朝鲜驻捷克斯洛伐克公使馆代表的欢迎。
    这些朝鲜青年将在摩拉维亚塞拜托夫堡休息和休养一个月。今年，他们将学习捷文，以便明年能在捷克斯洛伐克的技术学院开始正规的学习。
