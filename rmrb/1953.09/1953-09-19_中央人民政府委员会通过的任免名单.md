Title: List of Appointments and Removals Approved by the Central People's Government Committee
Author: 
Date: 1953-09-19
Page: 1

List of Appointments Approved by the 28th Meeting of the Central People's Government Committee

I. Secretary-General of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government
	Secretary-General:
		Xi Zhongxun, formerly Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government.

II. Supplementary List of Deputy Directors of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government
	Deputy Directors:
		Deng Xiaoping (concurrent)
		Li Weihan, incumbent Chairman of the Ethnic Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government.

III. List of Minister and Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Finance of the Central People's Government
	Minister:
		Deng Xiaoping (concurrent)
	Vice Ministers:
		Jin Ming, formerly Secretary of the Hunan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China.
		Fang Yi, incumbent member of the East China Administrative Council and Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee.

IV. Supplementary List of Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Food of the Central People's Government
	Vice Minister:
		Chen Guodong, incumbent Vice Minister of the Ministry of Finance of the Central People's Government.

V. Supplementary List of Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Light Industry of the Central People's Government
	Vice Minister:
		Song Shaowen, incumbent Secretary-General of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government.

VI. Supplementary List of Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government
	Vice Minister:
		Zhong Fuxiang, formerly Director of the Central and Southern China Postal and Telecommunications Administration.

VII. Supplementary List of Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Forestry of the Central People's Government
	Vice Minister:
		Yong Wentao, formerly Minister of Forestry of the Northeast People's Government.

VIII. Supplementary List of Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Labor of the Central People's Government
	Vice Minister:
		Song Ping, incumbent Director of the Labor and Wage Planning Bureau of the State Planning Commission of the Central People's Government.

IX. Supplementary List of Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government
	Vice Minister:
		Liu Zhiming, incumbent Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Northeast Administrative Council.

X. Supplementary List of Vice Governors of the People's Bank of China
	Vice Governors:
		Cao Juru, incumbent Deputy Secretary-General of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government.
		Huang Yaguang, formerly Governor of the Northwest China Branch of the People's Bank of China.
		Chen Xiyu, formerly Governor of the Central and Southern China Branch of the People's Bank of China.

XI. Supplementary List of Deputy Procurators-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate of the Central People's Government
	Deputy Procurator-General:
		Gao Kelin, formerly Secretary of the Suiyuan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China.

XII. Supplementary List of Vice Chairmen of the State Planning Commission of the Central People's Government
	Vice Chairmen:
		Li Fuchun, incumbent Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government.
		Jia Tuofu, incumbent Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government.

XIII. Supplementary List of Staff Members of the State Planning Commission of the Central People's Government
	Deputy Director of the Comprehensive Planning Bureau:
		Fan Ruoyi, formerly Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Hebei Provincial People's Government.
	Deputy Director of the Local Industry Planning Bureau:
		He Youqi, formerly Vice Governor of the Central and Southern China Branch of the People's Bank of China.

List of Removals Approved by the 28th Meeting of the Central People's Government Committee

(I) Removed Li Weihan from the post of Secretary-General of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government.

(II) Removed Bo Yibo from the post of Minister of the Ministry of Finance of the Central People's Government and Chen Guodong from the post of Vice Minister.

(III) Removed Wang Zheng from the post of Vice Minister of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government.
