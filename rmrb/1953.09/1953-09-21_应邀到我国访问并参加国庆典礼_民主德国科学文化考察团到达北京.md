Title: Democratic German Scientific and Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing for Visit and National Day Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1953-09-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 20th: The nine-member scientific and cultural delegation from the German Democratic Republic, led by Dr. Gerhard Harig, Director of the Higher Education Department of the German Democratic Republic, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 20th, invited to visit China and participate in the National Day celebrations, according to the 1953 executive plan of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included: Zeng Zhaolun, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Higher Education of the Central People's Government; Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Government Administration Council's Committee of Culture and Education; Jiang Tianzuo, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Culture; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Tao Li, Vice President of the Central Institute of Health; and Dai Ze, Professor of the Painting Department of the Central Academy of Fine Arts, and others.

Also welcoming them at the airport were Mr. and Mrs. Grutner, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the diplomatic mission of the German Democratic Republic in China, and others.

The delegation list is as follows:

Head: Dr. Gerhard Harig (Physicist, mathematician, natural scientist, Director of the Higher Education Department of the German Democratic Republic)

Members: Dr. Robert Alt (Director of the Institute for Theoretical Education of the Education College of Berlin University, National Prize Winner)

Dr. Friedrich Moglich (Mathematician, physicist, Academician of the German Academy of Sciences)

Dr. Friedrich Jung (Pharmacologist, Professor, Director of the Institute of Medical Biology of the German Academy of Sciences)

Dr. Werner Rothmaler (Botanist, Professor)

Dr. Karl Blümel (Professor, Director of the Berlin National Museum)

Dr. Fritz Dähn (Painter and architectural designer, Leader of the Dresden Academy of Art)

Fritz Erpenbeck (Editor-in-chief of "Modern Theater" and "Theater Service" magazine)

Ernst Hoffmann (Working in the National Arts Committee, also serving as the delegation secretary)

### 应邀到我国访问并参加国庆典礼  民主德国科学文化考察团到达北京

1953-09-21
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀到我国访问并参加国庆典礼
    民主德国科学文化考察团到达北京
    【新华社二十日讯】根据中德文化合作协定一九五三年执行计划，应邀到我国访问并参加国庆典礼的德意志民主共和国科学文化考察团一行九人，由团长格哈德·哈里希博士率领，于二十日乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有：中央人民政府高等教育部副部长曾昭抡，中国科学院副院长吴有训，政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深，文化部办公厅副主任蒋天佐，外交部交际处处长王倬如，中央卫生研究院副院长陶励，和中央美术学院绘画系教授戴泽等。
    到机场欢迎的还有：德意志民主共和国驻我国外交使团临时代办葛鲁特纳夫妇等。
    考察团名单如下：
    团长：格哈德·哈里希博士（物理、数学、自然科学家、德意志民主共和国高等教育署署长）
    团员：罗伯特·阿尔特博士（柏林大学教育学院理论教育研究所所长、国家奖金得奖人）
    弗雷德里希·摩格利希博士（数学、物理学家、德国科学院院士）
    弗雷德里希·荣格博士（药物学家、教授、德国科学院医药生物研究所主任）
    威尔纳·洛特马勒博士（植物学家、教授）
    卡尔·布伦麦尔博士（教授、柏林国立博物院院长）
    弗里茨·戴恩博士（画家及建筑设计家、德累斯顿艺术专科学校领导人）
    弗里茨·艾尔彭白克（“现代戏剧”及“戏剧服务”杂志社总编辑）
    恩斯特·霍夫曼（在国家艺术委员会任职，兼考察团秘书）
