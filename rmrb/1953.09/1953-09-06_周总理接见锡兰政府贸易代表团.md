Title: Premier Zhou Receives Ceylon Government Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1953-09-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 5th—Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government received the head of the Ceylon Government Trade Delegation, Mr. Fang Xika, and members Mr. Alwabirai, Mr. Ginnadasa, and Mr. James, this afternoon.

    Also present were Mr. Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, and Mr. Lei Renmin, Deputy Minister, of the Central People's Government, and Mr. Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 周总理接见锡兰政府贸易代表团

1953-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见锡兰政府贸易代表团
    【新华社五日讯】我中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理在五日下午接见了锡兰政府贸易代表团团长方席卡，团员：阿尔瓦比拉伊、吉纳达萨、詹兹。
    在座的有我中央人民政府对外贸易部部长叶季壮、副部长雷任民，外交部亚洲司司长陈家康。
