Title: Chinese Delegation Arrives in Budapest to Attend the World Federation of Scientific Workers Congress
Author:
Date: 1953-09-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 10th. Budapest Dispatch: The delegation of the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies, headed by Tu Changwang, arrived in Budapest on the morning of the 9th to attend the Third Congress of the World Federation of Scientific Workers, which will be held from the 12th to the 14th.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included Kossuth Prize winner and President of the Hungarian People's Republic Academy of Sciences, István Rusznyák, Vice-President of the Academy of Sciences, Lajos Ligeti, representatives of the World Federation of Scientific Workers, and officials from the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in Hungary.

### 出席世界科学工作者协会代表大会  我国代表团到达布达佩斯

1953-09-12
第4版()
专栏：

    出席世界科学工作者协会代表大会
    我国代表团到达布达佩斯
    【新华社布拉格十日电】布达佩斯消息：以涂长望为首的中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会代表团，九日上午到达布达佩斯，出席将于十二日至十四日举行的世界科学工作者协会第三届代表大会。
    到机场欢迎的有科苏特奖金获得者、匈牙利人民共和国科学院院长伊斯特万·鲁斯尼雅克，科学院副院长拉约希·利格特，世界科学工作者协会代表及中华人民共和国驻匈大使馆官员。
