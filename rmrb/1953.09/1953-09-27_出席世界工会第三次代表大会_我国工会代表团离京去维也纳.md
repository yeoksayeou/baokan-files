Title: Chinese Trade Union Delegation Leaves Beijing for Vienna to Attend the Third World Federation of Trade Unions Congress
Author:
Date: 1953-09-27
Page: 1

    To Attend the Third World Federation of Trade Unions Congress
    Chinese Trade Union Delegation Leaves Beijing for Vienna
    [Xinhua News Agency, 26th] The delegation elected by the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions to attend the Third World Federation of Trade Unions Congress departed from Beijing by train to Vienna on the 26th.
    The Vietnamese, Japanese, and Indonesian delegations attending the Third World Federation of Trade Unions Congress passed through Beijing on the same day en route to Vienna.
    The full list of the Chinese delegation is as follows: Head of Delegation Liu Ningyi; Secretary-General Li Jiebo; Members Liu Changsheng, Kang Yonghe, Yang Chao, Zhao Guoqiang, Zhao Tiefu, Zhang Fanghai, Ma Chungu, Yang Zhihua (female), Chen Yu, Jin Ziming, Zhuang Yan, Liu Jiannong, Yu Zhiying (female), Xu Wanzhen (female), Ren Leiyuan, Song Chuan, Fan Xiaofeng (female), Liu Keming, Gao Shoushan, Yuan Baohua, Wu Dicang (female), Zhou Rong (female).

### 出席世界工会第三次代表大会  我国工会代表团离京去维也纳

1953-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    出席世界工会第三次代表大会
    我国工会代表团离京去维也纳
    【新华社二十六日讯】中国工会第七次全国代表大会选出的出席世界工会第三次代表大会代表团，在二十六日自北京乘车去维也纳。
    越南、日本和印尼出席世界工会第三次代表大会代表团，于同日经过北京前往维也纳。
    中国代表团全体代表名单如下：团长刘宁一；秘书长李颉伯；团员刘长胜、康永和、杨超、赵国强、赵铁夫、张方海、马纯古、杨之华（女）、陈宇、金子明、庄焰、刘健农、俞志英（女）、徐婉珍（女）、任雷远、宋川、范小凤（女）、刘克明、高首善、袁葆华、吴涤苍（女）、周镕（女）。
