Title: Japanese Public Leader Ikuo Oyama Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-09-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: Ikuo Oyama, Chairman of the Japanese Peace Supporters Committee, distinguished Japanese public leader, and winner of the Stalin International Prize for "Strengthening International Peace," along with his wife and secretary, Mr. Tan Tokusaburo, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 24th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace.

Those who welcomed him at the airport included: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman; Xiao San, Standing Committee Member; Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General; Wu Maosun, Deputy Secretary-General; Mao Dun and Hong Shen from the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles; and Lu Cui from the All-China Democratic Women's Federation.

Also at the airport to welcome them were Kameda Togo and Vanturelier, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, and Japanese personnel from the Asia-Australia Liaison Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions, Kaneko Kenta and Ogura Kinkichi.

Guo Moruo delivered a welcoming speech at the airport. On behalf of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, he extended a warm welcome to Ikuo Oyama's arrival. He said: Since the founding of New China, the friendship between the Chinese people and the peace-loving Japanese people has developed. We firmly believe that the visit of Mr. Ikuo Oyama and his wife to our country will further enhance the friendship between the two peoples and contribute to the cause of peace in Asia.

Ikuo Oyama then delivered a reply. He said: He hopes that the peace-loving people of China, Japan, and the world will work together to make world peace invincible. (Attached picture)

Ikuo Oyama delivering a speech at Beijing Airport. On the right is Guo Moruo. Xinhua News Agency reporter Niu Weiyu.

### 日本公众领袖大山郁夫到京

1953-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

    日本公众领袖大山郁夫到京
    【新华社二十四日讯】日本拥护和平委员会主席、杰出的日本公众领袖、“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金得奖人大山郁夫教授和他的夫人及秘书淡德三郎先生，应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的邀请，于二十四日乘飞机抵达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若、副主席陈叔通，常务委员萧三，秘书长刘贯一、副秘书长吴茂荪，中华全国文学艺术界联合会茅盾、洪深，中华全国民主妇女联合会陆璀等。
    到机场欢迎的还有亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长龟田东伍和万徒勒里，世界工会联合会亚澳联络局日籍人员金子健太、小仓金吉等。
    郭沫若在机场致欢迎辞。他代表中国人民保卫世界和平委员会对大山郁夫的到达表示热烈的欢迎。他说：新中国成立以来，中国人民和爱好和平的日本人民之间的友谊获得了发展，我们深信这次大山郁夫先生和夫人等来我国访问，必能进一步增进两国人民间的友谊，并有助于亚洲和平事业的发展。
    大山郁夫接着致答辞。他说：希望中国、日本及全世界爱好和平的人民协力合作，使世界和平成为不可战胜的。（附图片）
    大山郁夫在北京飞机场发表谈话。右为郭沫若。　新华社记者　牛畏予摄
