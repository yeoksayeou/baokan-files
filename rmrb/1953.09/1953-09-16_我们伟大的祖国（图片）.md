    Our Great Motherland
    Maijishan is located in Tianshui County, Gansu Province. During the Wei and Tang Dynasties, it was one of the most developed regions of Buddhist art in our country. The Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government organized a Maijishan Expedition Team to investigate and study the ancient grotto art of Maijishan. This is a scene of the team's art workers sketching statues.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    麦积山在甘肃省天水县，魏、唐时代它是我国佛教艺术最发达的地区之一。中央人民政府文化部组织了麦积山考察团，去考察和研究麦积山古代石窟艺术。这是该团的美术工作者正在临摹塑像的情形。
