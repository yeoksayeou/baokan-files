Title: Meeting of Activists of the Light Industry Workers of Moscow City and Moscow Oblast Guarantees Overfulfillment of This Year's Production Task of Light Industrial Products
Author:
Date: 1953-09-05
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 27th) According to TASS, a conference of activists of light industry workers in Moscow City and Moscow Oblast was recently held in Moscow. A.N. Kosygin, Minister of Light Industry and Food Industry of the Soviet Union, reported on the development of the textile, sewing, and footwear industries in the Moscow Oblast at the meeting.

In his report, Kosygin pointed out that the light industry of Moscow City and Moscow Oblast accounts for a large proportion of the production of people's daily necessities. Its output accounts for 30 percent of the total output of light industry in the Soviet Union. In 1954 alone, the woolen fabrics produced by enterprises in Moscow City and Moscow Oblast belonging to the All-Union will exceed the total output of woolen industrial enterprises belonging to the All-Union and the Union Republics in 1940. At present, Moscow's cotton textile enterprises are vigorously engaged in production. These enterprises will produce nearly 1.59 billion meters of textiles in 1953, more than 406 million meters more than in 1950. The Orekhovo United Factory, the Three Mountains Textile Factory, the Igorev United Factory, the Volodarsky Overcoat Factory, the First Calico Printing Factory, and other factories will be renovated recently. In 1953, the shoe factories in Moscow City and Moscow Oblast will produce a total of nearly 24 million pairs of shoes. In order to increase the production of high-quality shoes, the Paris Commune Shoe Factory, the Kapranov Shoe Factory, and the Saraisk Shoe Factory have adopted new assembly line methods to produce leather shoes. The Petrel Shoe Factory and other factories will increase the production of new-style leather shoes with varnished uppers or uppers made of steer hide and white sheepskin.

The conference adopted the following guarantees: to overproduce 33 million meters of woolen fabrics, 332,000 square meters of blankets, 600,000 knitted shirts, 50,000 knitted tops, leather clothing worth 100 million rubles, 10 million meters of cotton fabrics, 366,000 pairs of leather shoes, and sewing products worth 50 million rubles in 1953. The conference also guaranteed to save at least 150 million rubles more than planned by reducing production costs.

### 莫斯科市和莫斯科州轻工业工作人员活动分子开会  保证超额完成今年的轻工业品生产任务

1953-09-05
第4版()
专栏：

    莫斯科市和莫斯科州轻工业工作人员活动分子开会
    保证超额完成今年的轻工业品生产任务
    【新华社四日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科八月二十七日讯：莫斯科市和莫斯科州轻工业工作人员活动分子会议，最近在莫斯科市举行。苏联轻工业和食品工业部部长阿·尼·柯西金，在会上报告了莫斯科州纺织、缝纫和制鞋工业的发展情况。
    柯西金在报告中指出，莫斯科市和莫斯科州的轻工业在生产人民生活必需品方面，占很大的比重。它的生产量占苏联轻工业总产量的百分之三十。在一九五四年内，仅莫斯科市和莫斯科州内属于全联盟的各企业所生产的毛织品，就将超过一九四○年属于全联盟和各加盟共和国的毛织工业企业的产量总和。现在，莫斯科各棉纺织企业正在大力进行生产。这些企业在一九五三年要生产将近十五亿九千万公尺的纺织品，比一九五○年多四亿零六百多万公尺。奥列霍伏联合工厂、三山纺织厂、伊哥列夫联合工厂、沃洛达斯基外衣工厂、第一印花布工厂以及其他工厂，最近都要进行改建。一九五三年，莫斯科市和莫斯科州的各个制鞋工厂，总共要生产将近两千四百万双鞋。为了增加质量高的鞋子的产量，巴黎公社鞋厂、卡普拉诺夫鞋厂和萨拉伊斯克鞋厂，已按新的流水作业法来生产皮鞋。海燕鞋厂和其他一些工厂将增加光油鞋面或用香牛皮、白色软羊皮做鞋面的新式皮鞋的生产。
    大会通过了下列保证：一九五三年要超额生产三千三百万公尺毛织品，三十三万二千平方公尺的毯子，六十万件针织衬衣，五万件针织上衣，价值一亿卢布的皮衣，一千万公尺的棉织品，三十六万六千双皮鞋，价值五千万卢布的缝纫品。会议还保证从降低生产成本中最少超额节约一亿五千万卢布。
