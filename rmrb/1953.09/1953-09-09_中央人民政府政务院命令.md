Title: Order of the State Administration Council of the Central People's Government
Author: Zhou Enlai
Date: 1953-09-09
Page: 1

    Order of the State Administration Council of the Central People's Government

    The "Basic Summary of Experiences in Promoting National Regional Autonomy" from the Third (Enlarged) Meeting of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, and the "Basic Summary of Livestock Production in Certain Pastoral Areas of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and Suiyuan, Qinghai, and Xinjiang Provinces" have been discussed and approved by the 188th State Administration Council meeting on September 3, 1953. This order approves and promulgates them.

    Premier Zhou Enlai
    September 9, 1953

### 中央人民政府政务院命令
周恩来
1953-09-09

专栏：

    中央人民政府政务院命令
    中央人民政府民族事务委员会第三次（扩大）会议关于推行民族区域自治经验的基本总结和关于内蒙古自治区及绥远、青海、新疆等地若干牧业区畜牧业生产的基本总结两个文件，业经一九五三年九月三日政务院第一百八十八次政务会议讨论通过，特令批准并予公布。
    总理  周恩来
    一九五三年九月九日
