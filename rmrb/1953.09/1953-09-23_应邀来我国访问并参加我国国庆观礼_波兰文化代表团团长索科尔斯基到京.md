Title: Polish Cultural Delegation Head Sokorski Arrives in Beijing, Invited to Visit China and Attend National Day Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1953-09-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd: Sokorski, head of the Polish cultural delegation and Minister of Culture and Art of Poland, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 22nd, invited to visit China and attend the National Day ceremony according to the 1953 execution plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

Those welcoming him at the airport included: Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government; Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Government Administration Council's Committee of Cultural and Educational Affairs; Chen Ming, Director of the Exhibition Department of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Xie Xiaonai, Director of the Liaison Department; and Wang Zhuoru, Chief of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Also at the airport to welcome him were Kiryło, the Polish Ambassador to China, and Gao·Bolesław, Deputy Head of the Polish People's Republic Economic Exhibition Delegation.

### 应邀来我国访问并参加我国国庆观礼  波兰文化代表团团长索科尔斯基到京

1953-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国访问并参加我国国庆观礼
    波兰文化代表团团长索科尔斯基到京
    【新华社二十二日讯】根据中波文化合作协定一九五三年执行计划应邀前来我国访问并参加我国国庆观礼的波兰文化代表团团长、波兰文化艺术部部长索科尔斯基于二十二日下午乘机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有：中央人民政府文化部副部长丁西林、政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深、中国国际贸易促进委员会展览部部长陈明、联络部部长谢筱乃和外交部交际处处长王倬如等。
    赴机场欢迎的还有波兰驻我国大使基里洛克和波兰人民共和国经济展览会代表团副团长高·包列斯拉夫等。
