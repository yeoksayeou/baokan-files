Title: Nordic Foreign Ministers' Meeting Issues Communiqué Supporting the Korean Armistice to Strive for Lasting Peace
Author:
Date: 1953-09-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, 3rd: The meeting of the Foreign Ministers of Sweden, Denmark, Norway, and Iceland was held in Stockholm from August 31st to September 1st. The meeting mainly discussed some issues that will be discussed at the upcoming 8th UN General Assembly. After the meeting, a communiqué was issued, stating: "The four foreign ministers emphasized that they are willing to support efforts aimed at transforming the Korean armistice into a lasting peace. They hope that the result of the Korean armistice will be that the Beijing government can represent China in the United Nations in the near future."

### 北欧四国外长会议发表公报  支持朝鲜停战争取持久和平

1953-09-04
第4版()
专栏：

    北欧四国外长会议发表公报
    支持朝鲜停战争取持久和平
    【新华社三日讯】据塔斯社斯德哥尔摩二日讯：瑞典、丹麦、挪威和冰岛四国外交部长会议，于八月三十一日到九月一日在斯德哥尔摩举行。会议主要是讨论一些将在快要举行的联合国第八届大会上讨论的问题。会议闭幕后，发表了一个公报，公报中说：“四国外交部长强调指出，他们愿意支持旨在使朝鲜停战变为持久和平的努力。他们希望朝鲜停战的结果是：北京政府在不久的将来可以在联合国中代表中国。”
