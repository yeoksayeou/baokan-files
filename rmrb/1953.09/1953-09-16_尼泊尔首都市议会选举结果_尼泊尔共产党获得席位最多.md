Title: Results of Kathmandu Municipal Council Election in Nepal: Nepalese Communist Party Wins Most Seats
Author:
Date: 1953-09-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, September 11th: The Nepalese Communist Party won six seats in the first municipal council election held in Kathmandu, the capital of Nepal. The Kathmandu Municipal Council has a total of eighteen seats, and the Communist Party won the most seats. No other party won more than four seats.

### 尼泊尔首都市议会选举结果  尼泊尔共产党获得席位最多

1953-09-16
第4版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔首都市议会选举结果
    尼泊尔共产党获得席位最多
    【新华社十一日讯】新德里消息：尼泊尔共产党在尼泊尔首都加德满都举行的第一次市议会选举中获得六席。加德满都市议会共有十八席，共产党得的席位最多。其他政党所得席位没有一个超过四席。
