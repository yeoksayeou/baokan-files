Title: Cuban, Thai, and Other Youth Representatives Arrive in Beijing to Attend National Day Celebrations; Second Group of Seven Representatives from the Czech Cultural Delegation Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-09-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 26th:

    Invited by the Chinese New Democratic Youth League and the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth to attend the National Day celebrations in our country, nineteen representatives from Cuba, Thailand, and other countries who attended the Fourth World Youth and Students for Peace and Friendship Festival arrived in Beijing by train on the morning of September 26th.

    Among those who went to the station to welcome them were responsible persons from the Chinese New Democratic Youth League and the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, as well as more than 30 young people from the capital.

    Youth representatives from other countries who have already arrived in our country also went to the station to welcome them.

    Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: The second group of seven representatives from the Czechoslovak Cultural Delegation, who were invited to visit our country and participate in the National Day celebrations, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of September 26th.

    Among those who welcomed them at the airport were people from cultural and artistic circles in the capital and personnel from the Czechoslovak Embassy in our country.

### 应邀来我国参加国庆观礼  古巴、泰国等国青年代表到达北京  捷文化代表团第二批代表七人来京

1953-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国参加国庆观礼
    古巴、泰国等国青年代表到达北京
    捷文化代表团第二批代表七人来京
    【新华社二十六日讯】应中国新民主主义青年团和中华全国民主青年联合会邀请，前来我国参加国庆观礼的出席第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的古巴、泰国等国代表十九人，在二十六日上午乘火车到达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有：中国新民主主义青年团和中华全国民主青年联合会的负责人和首都青年三十多人。
    前往车站欢迎的还有已来我国的其他各国青年代表。
    【新华社二十六日讯】应邀来我国访问并参加国庆观礼的捷克斯洛伐克文化代表团第二批代表七人，于二十六日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有首都文化艺术界人士和捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使馆人员多人。
