Title: Breaking Through Special Agent Control During Transfer to Neutral Nation Custody; Nine of Our Captured Personnel Requesting Repatriation Scheduled for Repatriation Today
Author:
Date: 1953-09-13
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 12th—Nine North Korean People's Army prisoners of war who broke through special agent control and requested immediate repatriation during the transfer to neutral nations' custody in the demilitarized zone will be repatriated to the Korean-Chinese side at the Panmunjom exchange point on the morning of the 13th. The Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission approved the repatriation requests of these nine prisoners of war and notified the Military Armistice Commission that it was prepared to repatriate them on the morning of the 13th.
    According to a communiqué from the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission, the third batch of nine hundred and eighty-six Chinese People's Volunteer Army prisoners of war not directly repatriated were handed over to the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission for custody in the demilitarized zone on the 12th.

### 在移交中立国看管过程中突破特务控制  九名要求遣返的我方被俘人员定今日遣返

1953-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

    在移交中立国看管过程中突破特务控制
    九名要求遣返的我方被俘人员定今日遣返
     【新华社开城十二日电】在非军事区交由中立国看管过程中突破特务控制要求立即遣返的九名朝鲜人民军被俘人员，将于十三日早晨在板门店交接地点遣返给朝中方面。中立国遣返委员会批准了这九个战俘的遣返要求，并通知军事停战委员会，它准备在十三日早晨遣返他们。
    据中立国遣返委员会公报说，第三批九百八十六名未直接遣返的中国人民志愿军被俘人员已于十二日在非军事区交由中立国遣返委员会看管。
