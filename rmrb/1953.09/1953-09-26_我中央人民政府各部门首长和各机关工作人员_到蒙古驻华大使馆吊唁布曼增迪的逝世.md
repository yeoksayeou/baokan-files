Title: Leading Officials of the Central People's Government and Staff of Various Agencies Visit the Mongolian Embassy in China to Mourn the Death of Bumtsend
Author:
Date: 1953-09-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, September 25th: Leading officials of various departments of the Central People's Government and staff of various agencies visited the Mongolian People's Republic Embassy in our country on the 25th to mourn the death of Bumtsend, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic.

    Those who went to offer condolences included: Teng Daiyuan, Minister of the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government; Zhu Xuefan, Minister of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications; Xu Zirong, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Public Security; Li Zheren, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade; Shi Zhiren, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Railways; Ding Xilin, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture; Huang Songling, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Higher Education; Wei Que, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Education; Hu Jingyun, Deputy Governor of the People's Bank of China; and Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council.

    Wu Yuzhang, President of the Renmin University of China, also went to offer condolences.

    Also visiting to offer condolences were more than 500 staff members from the Ministry of Public Security of the Central People's Government, the Ministry of Foreign Trade, the Ministry of Railways, the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications, the Ministry of Culture, the Ministry of Higher Education, the People's Bank of China, the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Government Administration Council, the General Logistics Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, and the Foreign Affairs Office of the Beijing Municipal People's Government.

    Those who came to offer condolences stood in silent tribute before the portrait of Chairman Bumtsend.

### 我中央人民政府各部门首长和各机关工作人员  到蒙古驻华大使馆吊唁布曼增迪的逝世

1953-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我中央人民政府各部门首长和各机关工作人员
    到蒙古驻华大使馆吊唁布曼增迪的逝世
    【新华社二十五日讯】我中央人民政府各部门首长和各机关工作人员，在二十五日分别前往蒙古人民共和国驻我国大使馆，吊唁蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席布曼增迪的逝世。
    前往吊唁的有：中央人民政府铁道部部长滕代远、邮电部部长朱学范、公安部副部长徐子荣、对外贸易部副部长李哲人、铁道部副部长石志仁、文化部副部长丁西林、高等教育部副部长黄松龄、教育部副部长韦悫、中国人民银行副行长胡景澐、政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深。
    中国人民大学校长吴玉章也前往吊唁。
    前往吊唁的还有：中央人民政府公安部、对外贸易部、铁道部、邮电部、文化部、高等教育部、中国人民银行、政务院对外文化联络事务局、人民革命军事委员会总后方勤务部以及北京市人民政府外事处的工作人员共五百余人。
    前往吊唁者在布曼增迪主席遗像前静默志哀。
