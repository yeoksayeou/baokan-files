Title: To Meet the Needs of Further Development of the National Economy, The Soviet Government Establishes Five New Ministries
Author:
Date: 1953-09-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 15th: TASS reports: In order to ensure the further increase of food production and improve its quality, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, based on the request of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, has established the All-Union-Republican Ministry of Food Industry of the Soviet Union, based on the enterprises and institutions of the fish, meat, dairy and food industries of the Ministry of Light Industry and Food Industry of the Soviet Union.

According to the list approved by the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, the enterprises and institutions of the fish, meat, dairy, butter, cheese, sugar, bread, macaroni, confectionery, canned food, refined dry food, oil, liquor, wine, beer, tea, tobacco, spices, edible salt, and other industrial sectors will be transferred to the Ministry of Food Industry of the Soviet Union.

Vasily Petrovich Zotov is appointed as the Minister of the Food Industry of the Soviet Union.

In order to ensure the further increase of the production of daily necessities and improve their quality, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, based on the request of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, has established the All-Union-Republican Ministry of Daily Necessities Industry of the Soviet Union, based on the light industrial enterprises and institutions of the Ministry of Light Industry and Food Industry of the Soviet Union.

According to the list approved by the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, the enterprises and institutions of cotton, wool, silk, linen, leather, footwear, fur, knitwear, sewing, glass and other industrial sectors, as well as the man-made fiber industry, will be transferred to the Ministry of Daily Necessities Industry of the Soviet Union.

Alexei Nikolaevich Kosygin is appointed as the Minister of the Daily Necessities Industry of the Soviet Union.

In order to further develop commodity circulation and improve trade organizations to meet the growing needs of residents as much as possible, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, based on the request of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, has established the All-Union-Republican Ministry of Trade of the Soviet Union, based on the domestic trade institutions and enterprises of the Ministry of Domestic and Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union.

Anastas Ivanovich Mikoyan, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, is appointed as the Minister of Trade of the Soviet Union.

In order to further develop economic relations between the Soviet Union and the People's Democratic countries and other countries, and to improve trade with them, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, based on the request of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, has established the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union, based on the foreign trade institutions of the Ministry of Domestic and Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union.

Ivan Grigorievich Kabanov is appointed as the Minister of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union.

In view of the great significance of further increasing the production of high-quality agricultural products from state farms, and in order to fundamentally improve the work of state farms and turn state farms into model, highly productive socialist economies, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, based on the request of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, has established the All-Union-Republican Ministry of State Farms of the Soviet Union, and according to the list approved by the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, the state farms, horse farms and other related enterprises and institutions will be transferred to the Ministry of State Farms of the Soviet Union. It is also considered appropriate to establish Ministries of State Farms in the Russian Federation, Ukraine, Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan, Azerbaijan, Georgia, Belarus, Lithuania, Latvia, Estonia, and the Karelo-Finnish Republic.

Alexei Ivanovich Kozlov is appointed as the Minister of State Farms of the Soviet Union and is relieved of his duties as Minister of Agriculture and Agricultural Storage of the Soviet Union.

Ivan Alexandrovich Benediktov is appointed as the Minister of Agriculture and Agricultural Storage of the Soviet Union.

### 适应国民经济进一步发展的需要  苏联政府设立五个新的部

1953-09-17
第4版()
专栏：

    适应国民经济进一步发展的需要
    苏联政府设立五个新的部
    【新华社十六日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十五日讯：为了保证进一步增加食品生产并改进其质量，苏联最高苏维埃主席团根据苏联部长会议的呈请，在苏联轻工业和食品工业部的鱼类、肉类、乳类和食品工业的企业和机构的基础上，设立苏联（全联盟兼加盟共和国）食品工业部。
    根据苏联部长会议批准的清单，将鱼类、肉类、乳类、牛油、干酪、糖、面包、通心粉、糖果、罐头、精制干粮、油脂、烈性酒、葡萄酒、啤酒、茶叶、烟草、香料、食盐以及其他工业部门的企业和机构，拨交苏联食品工业部。
    任命瓦西里·彼得罗维奇·佐托夫为苏联食品工业部部长。
    为了保证进一步增加日用品的生产并改进其质量，苏联最高苏维埃主席团根据苏联部长会议的呈请，在苏联轻工业和食品工业部的轻工业企业和机构的基础上，设立苏联（全联盟兼加盟共和国）日用品工业部。
    根据苏联部长会议批准的清单，将棉织、毛织、丝织、麻织、皮革、鞋类、毛皮、针织、缝纫、玻璃等工业部门和人造纤维工业的企业和机构，拨交苏联日用品工业部。
    任命阿列克谢·尼古拉耶维奇·柯西金为苏联日用品工业部部长。
    为了进一步发展商品流通，并改善贸易机构以尽量满足居民不断增长的需求，苏联最高苏维埃主席团根据苏联部长会议的呈请，在苏联国内和对外贸易部的国内贸易机构和企业的基础上，设立苏联
    （全联盟兼加盟共和国）贸易部。
    任命苏联部长会议副主席阿纳塔斯·伊凡诺维奇·米高扬为苏联贸易部部长。
    为了进一步发展苏联和人民民主国家及其他国家的经济关系，并改善和它们的贸易，苏联最高苏维埃主席团根据苏联部长会议的呈请，在苏联国内和对外贸易部的对外贸易机构的基础上，设立苏联对外贸易部。
    任命伊凡·格里戈里耶维奇·卡巴诺夫为苏联对外贸易部部长。
    鉴于进一步增加国营农场优良的农产品的生产具有重大意义，同时为了根本改进国营农场的工作，并使国营农场变成示范的、生产力很高的社会主义经济，苏联最高苏维埃主席团根据苏联部长会议的呈请，设立苏联（全联盟兼加盟共和国）国营农场部，并根据苏联部长会议批准的清单，将国营农场、养马场及其他有关企业和机构，拨交苏联国营农场部，同时认为在俄罗斯联邦、乌克兰、哈萨克、乌兹别克、吉尔吉斯、阿塞拜疆、格鲁吉亚、白俄罗斯、立陶宛、拉脱维亚、爱沙尼亚和卡累利阿—芬兰等加盟共和国宜设立国营农场部。
    任命阿列克谢·伊凡诺维奇·科兹洛夫为苏联国营农场部部长，并解除他的苏联农业和农业储存部部长的职务。
    任命伊凡·亚历山德罗维奇·别涅吉克托夫为苏联农业和农业储存部部长。
