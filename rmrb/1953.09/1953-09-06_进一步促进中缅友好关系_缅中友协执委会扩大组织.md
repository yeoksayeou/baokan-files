Title: Further Promoting Friendly Relations Between China and Burma: Burma-China Friendship Association Executive Committee Expands Organization
Author:
Date: 1953-09-06
Page: 1

    Further Promoting Friendly Relations Between China and Burma
    Burma-China Friendship Association Executive Committee Expands Organization
    [Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 5th] To further promote friendly relations between Burma and China, the Burma-China Friendship Association has expanded its original Executive Committee into a Supreme Committee.
    On August 30th, the Supreme Committee held a plenary meeting and re-elected a new working Executive Committee, with Dr. Htin Aung remaining as Chairman, and U Tin Pe Myint, Lin Rongni, and U Maung as Vice-Chairmen. The meeting unanimously agreed to continue inviting former Burmese Minister of Culture U Tun Pe, and to additionally appoint current Burmese Minister of Culture U Than Wan as Honorary Chairmen of the association.
    At the meeting, Thakin Kodaw Hmaing, Chairman of the Burmese Committee for the Defense of World Peace, and Dr. Htin Aung, Chairman of the Burma-China Friendship Association, both delivered speeches emphasizing the significance of Sino-Burmese friendship. After the meeting, the documentary films "Burmese Cultural Delegation Visits China" and "We Must Harness the Huai River" were screened to entertain all attendees and guests.

### 进一步促进中缅友好关系  缅中友协执委会扩大组织

1953-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    进一步促进中缅友好关系
    缅中友协执委会扩大组织
    【新华社五日讯】仰光消息：为进一步促进缅甸和中国之间的友好关系，缅中友好协会已将原有的执行委员会扩大组成为最高委员会。
    八月三十日最高委员会举行了一次全体会议，改选出新的工作执行委员会，仍由信波博士任主席，吴登佩密、林荣尼、吴貌等任副主席。会议一致同意继续邀请前缅甸文化部长吴顿佩及加聘现任缅甸文化部长吴丹旺任该会名誉主席。
    会上，缅甸保卫世界和平委员会主席德钦哥都迈和缅中友好协会主席信波博士都致词强调中缅友好的意义。会后并放映“缅甸文化访华团”和“一定要把淮河修好”两纪录片，招待全体与会者和来宾。
