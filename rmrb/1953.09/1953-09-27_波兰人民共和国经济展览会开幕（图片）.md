Title: Opening of the Economic Exhibition of the People's Republic of Poland (Pictures)
Author: Zhu Bo
Date: 1953-09-27
Page: 4

    Opening of the Economic Exhibition of the People's Republic of Poland
    The Economic Exhibition of the People's Republic of Poland opened in Beijing on the 26th. The picture shows Mr. Popiel, head of the government delegation of the Polish Economic Exhibition and Minister of Shipping of Poland, delivering a speech.
    Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government, cuts the ribbon.
    Guests visiting various exhibits on display at the exhibition.
