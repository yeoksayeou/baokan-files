Title: Chinese Academy of Sciences Sends Representative to Poland to Attend Scientific Conference Commemorating Copernicus
Author:
Date: 1953-09-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The Polish Academy of Sciences will hold a scientific conference on September 14 to commemorate the 410th anniversary of Copernicus' death. The Chinese Academy of Sciences has specially sent Zhou Peiyuan, a representative of our country who went to Hungary to attend the Third Congress of the World Federation of Scientific Workers, to Poland to participate in the scientific conference after attending the conference. Zhou Peiyuan left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 7th.

### 中国科学院派代表赴波兰  参加纪念哥白尼的科学会议

1953-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中国科学院派代表赴波兰
    参加纪念哥白尼的科学会议
    【新华社七日讯】波兰科学院将于九月十四日举行科学会议，纪念哥白尼逝世四百一十周年，中国科学院特派去匈牙利参加世界科学工作者协会第三届代表大会的我国代表周培源在出席该会后到波兰参加科学会议。周培源已在七日上午乘飞机离京。
