Title: Northeast People Commemorate the Victory Day of the War of Resistance Against Japan: Sacrificial Ceremony Held at Soviet Martyrs' Cemetery and Monument
Author:
Date: 1953-09-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 4th: To commemorate the victory of the War of Resistance Against Japan and the eighth anniversary of the Soviet army's liberation of Northeast China, people from Shenyang, Lushun-Dalian, Songjiang Province and Harbin, Anshan, Hulunbuir League and Hailar City in Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region went to the local Soviet Martyrs' Cemetery and Monument on September 3rd to hold sacrificial ceremonies and lay wreaths to mourn these heroes who sacrificed themselves for the liberation of the Chinese people.

More than 3,800 people from all walks of life in Shenyang City held sacrificial ceremonies in front of the Soviet soldiers' cemetery and the Soviet Martyrs' Monument on the morning of the 3rd. He Jinnian, Deputy Commander of the Northeast Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Mo Wenhua, Director of the Political Department, Zhang Shude, Deputy Secretary-General of the Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Liu Baotian, Secretary-General of the Northeast Administrative Committee, Huang Oudong, Secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Jiao Ruoyu, Mayor of Shenyang and President of the Shenyang Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Ning Wu, Che Xiangchen, Zhang Weizhen and other leaders of democratic parties and people's organizations in the Northeast Region and Shenyang City also participated in the mourning ceremony. Wei Lixiajin, Consul General of the Soviet Consulate General in Shenyang, Xu Yingde, Director of the Shenyang Liaison Office of the Korean Embassy in China, as well as Soviet experts and Soviet citizens in Shenyang were also invited to participate in the sacrificial ceremony. Jiao Ruoyu spoke at the sacrificial ceremony. On behalf of the people of the city, he extended high respect and heartfelt thanks to the great Soviet Communist Party, the Soviet government, the Soviet army and the Soviet people, and expressed deep condolences to the Soviet soldiers who bravely sacrificed themselves for the liberation of Northeast China. Wei Lixiajin, Consul General of the Soviet Consulate General in Shenyang, also spoke at the sacrificial ceremony. Then, the leaders and representatives of all walks of life present laid wreaths in front of the tomb and monument.

More than 2,000 representatives from all walks of life in Lushun-Dalian City went to the Soviet Martyrs' Memorial Tower in Stalin Square on the morning of the 3rd to lay wreaths to the Soviet martyrs who liberated Northeast China and Lushun-Dalian. Guo Shushen, Deputy Secretary of the Lushun-Dalian Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Qiao Chuanjue, Deputy Mayor of the Lushun-Dalian Municipal People's Government, and heads of various people's organizations participated in the mourning. Morozov, Consul General of the Soviet Consulate General in Dalian, and Colonel Osachuk, representative of the Soviet troops stationed in Lushun, also came to participate. After laying the wreaths, Qiao Chuanjue, Deputy Mayor of the Lushun-Dalian Municipal People's Government, delivered a speech in front of the tower, saying: The Soviet army liberated Lushun-Dalian, allowing the people of Lushun-Dalian to get rid of more than 40 years of enslavement by Japanese imperialism and become masters of the new China. The great achievements of the Soviet army will last forever and be recorded in history forever. On the same morning, 700 representatives from all walks of life in Lushun City and representatives of the Soviet troops stationed in Lushunkou went to the Victory Tower in Lushun City to present flowers. Yang Xiping, Secretary of the Lushun Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Mayor of the Municipal People's Government, spoke at the meeting. He called on people from all walks of life to continue to work hard and cooperate with the Soviet army to consolidate the Lushunkou naval base in order to defend the Far East and world peace. Representatives of the Soviet troops stationed in Lushunkou also spoke at the meeting. After the flower presentation, the representatives went to the Soviet Martyrs' Cemetery to pay their respects. In the evening, representatives from all walks of life in Lushun-Dalian, representatives of the Soviet troops and Soviet experts held a celebration party. Guo Shushen, Deputy Secretary of the Lushun-Dalian Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, gave a report at the meeting.

More than 2,500 representatives from all walks of life in Songjiang Province and Harbin City presented flowers and wreaths to the Soviet Red Army Martyrs' Memorial Tower, the Soviet Red Army Martyrs' Cemetery, and the Soviet Red Army Liberation Northeast Memorial Tower in front of Harbin Railway Station on the afternoon of the 3rd. In addition to the leaders of the Party and government of Songjiang Province and Harbin City, representatives of various people's organizations, Doba Shen, Consul General of the Soviet Consulate General in Harbin, and Soviet experts in Harbin also presented flowers. After the flower presentation, Qiang Xiaochu, Chairman of the Songjiang Provincial People's Government, made a brief speech, stating that the Chinese people will never forget the Sino-Soviet friendship forged by the Soviet army's high internationalist spirit. After Doba Shen, the Consul General of the Soviet Consulate General in Harbin, spoke, the audience took off their hats in silence. In the evening, the Songjiang Province and Harbin Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held a Sino-Soviet celebration party at the Harbin International Travel Agency.

Anshan City held an evening party on September 3rd to celebrate the eighth anniversary of the victory of the War of Resistance Against Japan. The party was attended by leaders of the Party and government organs and various people's organizations in Anshan City, representatives of people from all walks of life, Soviet experts who are helping our country build in Anshan, and all members of the Inner Mongolia Art Troupe who came to Anshan to perform consolation performances.

People from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Hulunbuir League and Hailar City, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, solemnly held a meeting on the morning of the 3rd to commemorate the eighth anniversary of the victory of the War of Resistance Against Japan. More than 100 representatives from all ethnic groups, including Mongolian, Han, Hui, and Korean, attended the commemoration meeting. After the meeting, representatives from all walks of life went to the Red Army Martyrs' Memorial Tower to present wreaths. People from all ethnic groups and all walks of life unanimously expressed deep condolences and high respect to the Soviet Red Army soldiers who heroically sacrificed themselves for the liberation of the Greater Khingan Mountains and the Hulunbuir Grassland.

### 东北人民纪念抗日战争胜利日  在苏军烈士墓地与纪念碑前举行祭扫仪式

1953-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    东北人民纪念抗日战争胜利日
    在苏军烈士墓地与纪念碑前举行祭扫仪式
    【新华社四日讯】九月三日，东北沈阳市、旅大市、松江省及哈尔滨市、鞍山市、内蒙古自治区呼纳盟及海拉尔市等地人民，为了纪念抗日战争胜利和苏军解放东北八周年，分别前往当地苏军烈士墓地与纪念碑前举行祭扫和敬献花圈，悼念这些为解放中国人民而捐躯的英雄们。
    沈阳市各界人民三千八百多人三日上午分别在苏军将士墓和苏军阵亡将士纪念碑前举行祭扫仪式。中国人民解放军东北军区副司令员贺晋年、政治部主任莫文骅，中共中央东北局副秘书长张树德，东北行政委员会秘书长刘宝田，中共沈阳市委书记黄欧东，沈阳市人民政府市长、沈阳市中苏友好协会会长焦若愚和东北区、沈阳市各民主党派、人民团体的负责人宁武、车向忱、张维桢等都参加了祭奠的行列。苏联驻沈阳总领事馆总领事魏立夏金、朝鲜驻华大使馆沈阳联络所所长徐应德以及在沈阳的苏联专家和苏联侨民也应邀参加祭扫。焦若愚在祭扫仪式上讲话。他代表全市人民向伟大的苏联共产党、苏联政府、苏联军队和苏联人民致以崇高的敬意和衷心的感谢，并对为解放东北而英勇牺牲的苏军将士们致以深切的悼念。苏联驻沈阳总领事魏立夏金也在祭扫仪式上讲了话。接着，到会的首长和各界人民的代表相继在墓、碑前敬献花圈。
    旅大市各界代表二千余人，在三日上午齐赴斯大林广场苏军烈士纪念塔前，向解放东北及旅大的苏军烈士敬献花圈。参加悼念的有中共旅大市委副书记郭述申、旅大市人民政府副市长乔传珏以及各人民团体的负责人。苏联驻大连总领事莫罗佐夫、驻旅顺苏军部队代表奥萨邱克上校也前来参加。敬献花圈后，旅大市人民政府副市长乔传珏在塔前致词，他说：苏军解放了旅大，使旅大人民摆脱了日本帝国主义四十多年的奴役，成了新中国的主人。苏军这种丰功伟绩，将万古长存，永垂史册。同日上午，旅顺市各界人民代表七百人，和苏军驻旅顺口部队代表一同前往旅顺市胜利塔献花。中共旅顺市委书记、市人民政府市长杨西萍在会上讲话，他号召各界人民继续努力，协同苏军巩固旅顺口海军根据地，以保卫远东和世界和平。驻旅顺口苏军部队代表也在会上讲话。献花后，代表们同赴苏军烈士墓地祭扫。晚间，旅大市各界人民代表和苏军部队代表以及苏联专家举行了庆祝会。中共旅大市委副书记郭述申在会上作了报告。
    松江省及哈尔滨市各界人民代表两千五百多人，在三日中午向苏联红军烈士纪念塔、苏联红军烈士墓以及哈尔滨车站前的苏联红军解放东北纪念塔敬献鲜花和花圈。献花的除松江省、哈尔滨市党政负责人、各人民团体代表外，还有苏联驻哈尔滨市总领事馆总领事多巴申及在哈尔滨市的苏联专家等人。献花后，松江省人民政府主席强晓初作了简短的讲话，他表示中国人民将永不忘记苏军的高度国际主义精神所结成的中苏友谊。苏联驻哈尔滨市总领事多巴申讲话后，全场脱帽致哀。晚间，松江省及哈尔滨市中苏友好协会在哈尔滨国际旅行社举办了中苏联欢晚会。
    鞍山市在九月三日举行了庆祝抗日战争胜利八周年纪念晚会。参加晚会的有鞍山市党、政机关和各人民团体的负责人，各界人民代表，在鞍山帮助我国建设的苏联专家，以及前来鞍山作慰问演出的内蒙古文艺工作团全体人员。
    内蒙古自治区呼纳盟及海拉尔市各族各界人民，在三日上午隆重举行了抗日战争胜利八周年纪念会，参加纪念会的有蒙、汉、回、朝鲜等族各界代表一百多人。会后各界代表并赴红军烈士纪念塔前敬献花圈。各族各界人民一致对为解放大兴安岭和呼伦贝尔草原而英勇牺牲的苏联红军战士表示深切的悼念和崇高的敬意。
