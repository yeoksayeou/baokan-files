Title: The Chinese Committee for the Defense of World Peace and Four Other Organizations Host a Banquet for International Friends Attending the World Cultural Figures Commemoration Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-09-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: The Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace, the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the All-China Association of Literary Workers, the All-China Federation of Natural Scientific Societies, and the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization hosted a banquet on the evening of the 29th for Mr. Witold Jabłoński and Mr. Wojciech Sukrowsky, representatives of Poland, Mr. and Mrs. Vercors, representatives of France, and Mr. Nicolás Guillén, representative of Cuba, who are attending the World Commemoration Meeting for Four Cultural Figures.

    Those attending the banquet included: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace; Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman; Luo Longji, Xu Deheng, and Xiao San, Standing Committee members; Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General; Mao Dun, Zhou Yang, Hong Shen, and Zheng Zhenduo of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles; Lao She, Feng Xuefeng, Xia Yan, and Yang Hansheng of the All-China Association of Literary Workers; Li Siguang, Hou Debang, Wu Youxun, Qian Weichang, and Hua Luogeng of the All-China Federation of Natural Scientific Societies; Liang Xi, Zhu Kezhen, Ding Xilin, Mao Yisheng, and Chen Fengtong of the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization, and others.

    Also invited to the banquet were: Kameda Togo and Vantourout, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee; Committee member Louis Ayre; and former Australian Senator William Morrow, and others.

    At the banquet, Chairman Guo Moruo expressed a warm welcome to the representatives from Poland, France, and Cuba who attended the World Commemoration Meeting for Four Cultural Figures.

    During the banquet, the hosts and guests frequently toasted to the prosperity of culture in all countries and to lasting world peace.

### 我保卫世界和平委员会等五团体  欢宴参加世界文化名人纪念会的国际友人

1953-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我保卫世界和平委员会等五团体
    欢宴参加世界文化名人纪念会的国际友人
    【新华社二十九日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中华全国文学艺术界联合会、中华全国文学工作者协会、中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会、中华全国科学技术普及协会五个团体，于二十九日晚欢宴参加世界四位文化名人纪念大会的波兰代表维托尔德·雅布朗斯基先生、沃伊切赫·苏克罗夫斯基先生，法国代表维尔高尔先生夫妇，古巴代表尼古拉斯·纪廉先生。
    参加宴会的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，副主席陈叔通，常务委员罗隆基、许德珩、萧三，秘书长刘贯一；中华全国文学艺术界联合会茅盾、周扬、洪深、郑振铎；中华全国文学工作者协会老舍、冯雪峰、夏衍、阳翰笙；中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会李四光、侯德榜、吴有训、钱伟长、华罗庚；中华全国科学技术普及协会梁希、竺可桢、丁西林、茅以升、陈凤桐等。
    应邀参加宴会的还有；亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长龟田东伍、万徒勒里，委员路易·艾黎以及澳大利亚前参议员威廉·毛罗等。
    宴会上，郭沫若主席对出席世界四位文化名人纪念会的波兰、法国和古巴的代表表示热烈的欢迎。
    席间，宾主频频为各国文化的繁荣、为世界的持久和平而干杯。
