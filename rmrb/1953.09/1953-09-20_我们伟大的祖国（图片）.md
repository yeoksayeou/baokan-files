Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-09-20
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Jingdezhen, Jiangxi Province, a famous porcelain producing area in our country, has produced a new batch of porcelain called "National Construction Porcelain," which has a strong national style. After October this year, this new product will be available to meet the needs of the people of the whole country. This is a worker inspecting the finished product quality of the new porcelain.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国有名的瓷器产地——江西省景德镇，制出了一批新的瓷器——“建国瓷”，它具有浓厚的民族风格。今年十月以后，这种新产品就可供给全国人民的需要了。这是工人正在检查新瓷器的成品质量。
