Title: Supporting the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in Restoring and Developing the National Economy: The Central Committee of the Czechoslovak National Front Calls for a Donation Campaign to Aid Korea

Author:
Date: 1953-09-29
Page: 4

    Supporting the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in Restoring and Developing the National Economy
    The Central Committee of the Czechoslovak National Front Calls for a Donation Campaign to Aid Korea
    [Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 27th] The Central Action Committee of the Czechoslovak National Front and the Czechoslovak Committee for the Defense of Peace jointly issued a letter to the Czechoslovak people on the 24th, calling on the people to launch a mass donation campaign to aid Korea in support of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea's work in restoring and developing the national economy. The letter to the people stated: The Czechoslovak people will never forget the heroic Korean people and the Chinese People's Volunteers' historical contribution to the cause of world peace. The Czechoslovak people must help the Korean people heal the severe wounds inflicted on their homeland by the aggressors' atrocities. We call on all working people of Czechoslovakia to launch a broad donation campaign to aid Korea. Everyone participating in this movement will contribute to our country's extensive support for the Democratic People's Republic of Korea's work in restoring and developing the national economy, and can thereby express their firm will to resolutely maintain and consolidate peace in Korea, our country, and all other countries.
    The Chairman of the National Assembly of the Czechoslovak Republic, Fierlinger, delivered a radio address to the people of the country on the 24th to launch the donation campaign to aid Korea.

### 支援朝鲜民主主义人民共和国恢复和发展国民经济  捷民族阵线中央号召展开援朝捐献运动

1953-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    支援朝鲜民主主义人民共和国恢复和发展国民经济
    捷民族阵线中央号召展开援朝捐献运动
    【新华社布拉格二十七日电】捷克与斯洛伐克民族阵线中央行动委员会和捷克斯洛伐克保卫和平委员会，二十四日联合发表告捷克斯洛伐克人民书，号召人民展开群众性的援朝捐献运动，以支援朝鲜民主主义人民共和国恢复和发展国民经济的工作。告人民书说：捷克斯洛伐克人民永远不会忘记英雄的朝鲜人民和中国人民志愿军对世界和平事业的历史性的贡献，捷克斯洛伐克人民要帮助朝鲜人民治愈他们祖国因侵略者的暴行而遭受的严重创伤。我们号召捷克斯洛伐克全体劳动人民展开广泛的援朝捐献运动，参加这一运动的每一个人，都将有助于我国对朝鲜民主主义人民共和国恢复和发展国民经济工作的广泛支援，并可借此表示自己要坚决保持和巩固朝鲜、我国以及所有其他国家的和平的坚定意志。
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国国民议会主席费林格，在二十四日为展开援朝捐献运动向全国人民发表了广播演说。
