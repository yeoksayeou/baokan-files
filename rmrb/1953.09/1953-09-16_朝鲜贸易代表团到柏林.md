Title: Korean Trade Delegation Arrives in Berlin
Author:
Date: 1953-09-16
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 15th: A trade delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by Ko Hui-man, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Heavy Industry, and Kim Choi-sun, Director of the Import Bureau of the Ministry of Trade, arrived in Berlin on the 9th. They will sign a trade agreement with the government of the German Democratic Republic.

### 朝鲜贸易代表团到柏林

1953-09-16
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜贸易代表团到柏林
    【新华社柏林十五日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国贸易代表团，在重工业省副相高熙万和贸易省输入局局长金最善率领下，在九日到达柏林。他们将与德意志民主共和国政府签订贸易协定。
