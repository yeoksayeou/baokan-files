Title: Czechoslovak Communist Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session; Zápotocký Reports on Political Situation and Party Tasks
Author:
Date: 1953-09-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 16th. According to TASS, the "Red Right Newspaper" published on the 14th a communique on the plenary session of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party. The communique stated: The Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party recently held a plenary session in Prague. Antonín Zápotocký delivered a report on the political situation and the tasks of the party. The Central Committee authorized the Presidium of the Central Committee to formulate various tasks aimed at ensuring the coordinated development of the national economy and improving the material and cultural level of the working class and the working people, based on Zápotocký's report. The plenary session elected Antonín Novotný as the First Secretary of the Central Committee, and elected the following persons as Secretaries of the Central Committee: Bruno Köhler, Bedřich Biks-Vodusek, Václav Nosek, and Vratislav Krutina. The meeting elected alternate member of the Central Committee, Josef Teska, as a member of the Central Committee and elected him as a member of the Presidium of the Central Committee.

### 捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央举行全体会议  萨波托斯基报告政治形势和党的任务

1953-09-17
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央举行全体会议
    萨波托斯基报告政治形势和党的任务
    【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社布拉格十四日讯：“红色权利报”十四日刊载了关于捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会全体会议的公报。公报写道：捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会最近在布拉格举行了全体会议。安托宁·萨波托斯基在会上做了关于政治形势和党的任务的报告。党中央委员会授权中央委员会主席团根据萨波托斯基的报告，制定出旨在保证国民经济协调地发展和提高工人阶级和劳动人民物质和文化水平的各项任务。全体会议选出了安托宁·诺沃提尼为党中央委员会第一书记，并选出下列数人为中央委员会书记：布鲁诺·凯列尔、贝德尔热赫·比克斯—沃杜、瓦茨拉夫·诺西克和弗拉吉斯拉夫·克鲁蒂纳。会议选举中央委员会候补委员约瑟夫·捷斯拉为中央委员会委员，并把他选为中央委员会主席团委员。
