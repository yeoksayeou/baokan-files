Author:
Date: 1953-09-28

Our Great Motherland
A certain transformer factory under the Electrical Industry Administration Bureau of the First Ministry of Machine Building has successfully trial-produced large transformers according to Soviet standards. To welcome National Day, the factory workers are stepping up production. This is a technician and workers inspecting the assembly of a 10,000 kVA transformer.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    第一机械工业部电器工业管理局所属的某变压器厂，按照苏联标准，试制大型变压器业已成功。该厂职工为了迎接国庆，正加紧生产。这是技术员和工人正在检查一万仟伏安变压器装配的情况。
