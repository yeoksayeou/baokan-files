Title: Total Number of POWs Repatriated by Both Sides in Thirty-Three Days
Author: 
Date: 1953-09-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 10th. The repatriation of directly repatriated prisoners of war held by the Korean-Chinese side concluded on September 6th. During the thirty-three days from August 5th to September 6th, the Korean-Chinese side repatriated 4,912 non-Korean POWs and 7,848 Korean POWs to the other side, totaling 12,760. Among the non-Korean POWs were 3,597 Americans, 947 British, 228 Turks, 30 Canadians, 40 Filipinos, 22 Colombians, 12 French, 2 Greeks, 21 Australians, 1 Belgian, 7 South Africans, 3 Japanese, and 2 Dutch.
    During the same period the other side repatriated 70,159 captured personnel of the Korean People's Army and five thousand six hundred and forty captured personnel of the Chinese People's Volunteers to our side, totaling 75,799. Numerous materials prove that some of our captured personnel who should have been directly repatriated are still being detained by the US side and have not been repatriated.

### 三十三天中双方遣俘总数

1953-09-12
第4版()
专栏：

    三十三天中双方遣俘总数
     【新华社开城十日电】朝中方面收容下的直接遣返战俘的遣返工作已经在九月六日结束。从八月五日至九月六日的三十三天中，朝中方面遣送给对方非朝鲜籍战俘四千九百一十二名，朝鲜籍战俘七千八百四十八名，共一万二千七百六十名。在非朝鲜籍战俘中包括美国籍三千五百九十七名、英国籍九百四十七名、土耳其籍二百二十八名、加拿大籍三十名、菲律宾籍四十名、哥伦比亚籍二十二名、法国籍十二名、希腊籍二名、澳大利亚籍二十一名、比利时籍一名、南非联邦籍七名、日本籍三名、荷兰籍二名。
    同一期间，对方遣送给我方朝鲜人民军被俘人员七万零一百五十九名，中国人民志愿军被俘人员五千六百四十名，共七万五千七百九十九名。许多材料证明：还有一部分应该直接遣返的我方被俘人员仍在美方的拘禁下没有被遣返。
