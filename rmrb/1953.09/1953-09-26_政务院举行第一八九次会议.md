Title: The Government Administration Council Holds its 189th Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1953-09-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 24th. The 189th session of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 24th. The meeting listened to and approved the report by Li Siguang, Minister of the Central People's Government Ministry of Geology, on the geological work situation in the first half of 1953 and future work arrangements. The meeting approved various appointment and removal lists.

### 政务院举行第一八九次会议

1953-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一八九次会议
    【新华社二十四日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百八十九次政务会议在二十四日下午举行。会议听了和批准了中央人民政府地质部李四光部长所作的关于一九五三年上半年地质工作情况和今后的工作布置的报告。会议通过了各项任免名单。
