Title: State Council Holds 188th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-09-05
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd [September]: The 188th meeting of the Central People's Government's State Council was held on the afternoon of the 3rd. At the meeting, Liu Geping, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government's Nationalities Affairs Commission, reported on the "Basic Summary of Experiences in Promoting National Regional Autonomy" and the "Basic Summary of Animal Husbandry Production in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and Some Pastoral Areas in Suiyuan, Qinghai, and Xinjiang," which were adopted by the third (expanded) meeting of the Central Nationalities Affairs Commission in June. After listening to Vice Chairman Liu Geping's report, the State Council approved the aforementioned two basic summaries. The meeting also approved various appointment and removal proposals and proposals for submission to the Central People's Government Committee for approval of appointments and removals.

### 政务院举行第一八八次会议

1953-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一八八次会议
    【新华社三日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百八十八次政务会议在三日下午举行。会上，中央人民政府民族事务委员会刘格平副主任委员报告了中央民族事务委员会于六月间举行的第三次（扩大）会议所通过的“关于推行民族区域自治经验的基本总结”和“关于内蒙古自治区及绥远、青海、新疆等地若干牧业区畜牧业生产的基本总结”。政务会议在听取了刘格平副主任委员的报告后，批准了上述两个基本总结。会议并通过了各项任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案。
