Title: Khmer People's Liberation Army Eliminates 500 Enemy Troops
Author: 
Date: 1953-09-09
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, September 7th) According to Vietnam News Agency on September 5th: The General Headquarters of the Khmer People's Liberation Army recently issued a communique stating that in recent months, guerrilla warfare in southwestern Cambodia has been very intense. The Khmer People's Liberation Army has victoriously repelled many "sweeps" by the enemy and ambushed enemy vehicles on major transportation lines. Guerrilla warfare has been particularly intense in areas such as Kampong Chhnang and Kampot.

The communique stated that in the first four months of this year, the Khmer People's Liberation Army conducted ninety battles (not including battles in western Kampot), killing or wounding nearly five hundred enemy soldiers, including ten officers. They also shot down one plane, destroyed fifteen armored vehicles and trucks, captured two mortars, more than thirty machine guns, nearly sixty rifles, and a large amount of ammunition and equipment.

### 高棉人民解放军歼敌五百

1953-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

    高棉人民解放军歼敌五百
    【新华社七日讯】据越南通讯社五日讯：高棉人民解放军总司令部最近发表公报说，在最近几个月，高棉西南部的游击战争进行得很激烈。高棉人民解放军胜利地击退了敌军多次的“扫荡”，并在主要的交通线上伏击敌军的车辆。游击战在蓬乍叻、唝吥等地区进行得特别激烈。
    公报说，在今年头四个月中，高棉人民解放军进行了九十次战斗（还不包括在唝吥西部进行的战斗），共杀伤敌军近五百名，其中有十名军官。他们还击落飞机一架、击毁装甲车和卡车十五辆、缴获迫击炮两门、机枪三十多挺、步枪近六十支以及大量的弹药和装备。
