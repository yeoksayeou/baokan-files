Title: French and Polish Representatives Arrive in Beijing to Attend Commemorative Meeting for World Cultural Figures
Author:
Date: 1953-09-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd: At the invitation of the China Peace Committee, All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, All-China Association of Literary Workers, All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies, and All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization, the French representative Mr. Vercors and his wife, and the Polish representatives Professor Witold Jabłoński and Mr. Wojciech Sukrołowski, a total of four people, arrived in Beijing by plane on September 22nd to attend the commemorative meeting for world cultural figures in our country.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Xiao San, Liu Guan Yi, Tang Mingzhao, and Zhu Shi Lun from the China Peace Committee; Zheng Zhenduo and Hong Shen from the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and the All-China Association of Literary Workers; Wu Youxun and Qian Sanqiang from the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies; and Shen Qiyi from the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization.

Also present at the airport to welcome them were Deputy Secretary-General Kameda Togo and Wanthouret of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee.

### 参加世界文化名人纪念会的法、波代表到京

1953-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    参加世界文化名人纪念会的法、波代表到京
    【新华社二十二日讯】应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中华全国文学艺术界联合会、中华全国文学工作者协会、中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会、中华全国科学技术普及协会邀请，前来我国参加世界文化名人纪念会的法国代表维尔高尔先生夫妇和波兰代表维托尔德·雅布朗斯基教授、伏伊切赫·苏可罗夫斯基先生等四人于二十二日乘飞机抵达北京。
    前赴机场欢迎的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会萧三、刘贯一、唐明照、朱世纶，中华全国文学艺术界联合会和中华全国文学工作者协会郑振铎、洪深，中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会吴有训、钱三强，中华全国科学技术普及协会沈其益等。
    赴机场欢迎的还有亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长龟田东伍和万徒勒里等。
