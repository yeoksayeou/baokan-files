Title: Council and Technical Committee of the International Broadcasting Organisation Meet in Beijing; Representatives of Broadcasting Organizations from the Soviet Union and Other Countries Arrive in Beijing

Author: 
Date: 1953-09-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th: The 24th meeting of the Council of the International Broadcasting Organisation and the 9th meeting of the Technical Committee are scheduled to begin in Beijing on September 15th. The heads of broadcasting organizations from the Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, and Mongolia, as well as the Secretary-General of the International Broadcasting Organisation, Theodor Zeleznik, have all arrived in Beijing.

The representatives who arrived in Beijing by plane on the 14th included: Soviet representative I.I. Pozdnyak, Deputy Director of the Main Broadcasting Administration of the Soviet Ministry of Culture, and three others; Polish representative Romuald Gadomski, Chairman of the Polish Broadcasting Committee, and one other; Czechoslovak representative Jo. Vrabitz, First Deputy Chairman of the Czechoslovak Broadcasting Committee, and two others; Hungarian representative Szindro Sandor, First Deputy Director of the Hungarian Broadcasting Bureau, and one other; Romanian representative Mihai Rosianu, Chairman of the Romanian Broadcasting Committee, and one other; and Bulgarian representative M. Nikolov, Chairman of the Bulgarian Broadcasting Committee.

Mei Yi, Director of the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government, and Zuo Ying, Director of the International Liaison Department, and others, went to the airport to welcome them.

Also present at the airport to welcome them were Polish Ambassador to China, Kirylok, Romanian Ambassador to China, Guo Zuowen, and personnel from the Soviet, Czechoslovak, Mongolian, and Hungarian Embassies in China.

The Mongolian representative, Pulbi, and one other, as well as the Secretary-General of the International Broadcasting Organisation, Zeleznik, who are attending the meeting, arrived in Beijing on the 13th and 12th respectively.

### 国际广播组织理事会、技术委员会在京开会  苏联等国广播组织的代表先后到达北京

1953-09-15
第1版()
专栏：

    国际广播组织理事会、技术委员会在京开会
    苏联等国广播组织的代表先后到达北京
     【新华社十四日讯】国际广播组织理事会第二十四次会议和技术委员会第九次会议定于九月十五日起在北京举行。出席会议的苏联、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、蒙古的广播组织的负责人和国际广播组织秘书长狄奥多·热列兹尼，都已先后到达北京。
    十四日乘飞机到达北京的代表有：苏联代表、苏联文化部广播总管理局副局长伊·伊·波兹德略克等四人，波兰代表、波兰广播委员会主席罗·加多姆斯基等二人，捷克斯洛伐克代表、捷克斯洛伐克广播委员会第一副主席约·夫拉比茨等三人，匈牙利代表、匈牙利广播事业局第一副局长辛德罗·桑多等二人，罗马尼亚代表、罗马尼亚广播委员会主席米·罗山亚努等二人和保加利亚代表、保加利亚广播委员会主席米·尼科洛夫。
    前往机场欢迎的有中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会广播事业局局长梅益、国际联络部主任左荧等人。
    到机场欢迎的还有波兰驻我国大使基里洛克、罗马尼亚驻我国大使郭佐文和苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、蒙古、匈牙利驻我国大使馆人员。
    出席会议的蒙古代表浦尔比等二人和国际广播组织秘书长热列兹尼，已在十三日和十二日到达北京。
