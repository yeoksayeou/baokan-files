Title: Korean Ambassador to the Soviet Union Hosts Korean Delegation; Malenkov, Molotov, and Others Attend
Author:
Date: 1953-09-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 18th:林海, the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the Soviet Union, held a reception on the 18th to welcome the government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

    Those attending the reception included: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union G.M. Malenkov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union V.M. Molotov, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union N.S. Khrushchev, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union N.A. Bulganin, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union L.M. Kaganovich, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Minister of Trade of the Soviet Union A.I. Mikoyan, Chairman of the State Planning Committee of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union M.Z. Saburov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic M.P. Tarasov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic A.M. Puzanov, Ministers of the Soviet Union: M.G. Pervukhin, P.K. Ponomarenko, S.M. Tikhomirov, S.N. Kruglov, I.A. Benediktov, S.A. Akopov, P.Y. Yudin, N.A. Dygai, P.Y. Antropov, D.F. Ustinov, Z.A. Shashkov, and A.F. Tretyakov, Marshals of the Soviet Union: A.M. Vasilevsky, S.M. Budyonny, responsible personnel from various departments of the Soviet government, as well as representatives from public organizations and the press, scientists, and artists.

    From the Korean side, those attending the reception included: Premier of the Cabinet Kim Il-sung, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party Park Jung-ae, Vice-Premier of the Cabinet Chung Il-ryong, Minister of Foreign Affairs Nam Il, Chairman of the State Planning Committee Jung Joon-taek, Minister of Railways Kim Hoi-il, and personnel from the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in the Soviet Union.

    Also attending the reception were: Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Hungarian People's Republic A. Szikladan, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China Zhang Wentian, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Romanian People's Republic M. Dalea, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Polish People's Republic W. Lewikowski, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Czechoslovak Republic J. Vosahlik, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic D. Adilbish, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Albanian People's Republic M. Prifti, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Bulgarian People's Republic V. Vylkov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Nguyen Duc Qui, and Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the German Democratic Republic K. Seiz.

### 朝鲜驻苏大使招待朝鲜代表团  马林科夫、莫洛托夫等应邀出席

1953-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜驻苏大使招待朝鲜代表团
    马林科夫、莫洛托夫等应邀出席
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十八日讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使林海于十八日举行招待会，招待朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团。
    出席招待会的有：苏联部长会议主席格·马·马林科夫，苏联部长会议第一副主席兼苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫，苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫，苏联部长会议第一副主席兼苏联国防部部长尼·亚·布尔加宁，苏联部长会议第一副主席拉·莫·卡冈诺维奇，苏联部长会议副主席兼苏联贸易部部长阿·伊·米高扬，苏联部长会议国家计划委员会主席马·扎·萨布罗夫，俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席麦·普·塔拉索夫，俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国部长会议主席亚·麦·普扎诺夫，苏联各部部长：米·格·别尔乌辛，潘·康·波诺马连科，谢·米·季霍米罗夫，谢·涅·克鲁格洛夫，伊·亚·别涅吉克托夫，斯·阿·阿科波夫，帕·亚·尤金，尼·亚·德加伊，普·雅·安特罗波夫，德·费·乌斯季诺夫，佐·阿·夏希科夫和安·费·特雷季雅科夫，苏联元帅：阿·麦·华西列夫斯基，谢·米·布琼尼，苏联政府各部门负责人员，以及各公众团体和报界代表，科学家和艺术工作者。
    朝鲜方面出席招待会的有：内阁首相金日成，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱，内阁副首相郑一龙，外务相南日，国家计划委员会委员长郑准泽，铁道相金会一和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻苏大使馆人员。
    出席招待会的还有：匈牙利人民共和国特命全权大使阿·希克拉丹，中华人民共和国特命全权大使张闻天，罗马尼亚人民共和国特命全权大使麦·达列亚，波兰人民共和国特命全权大使瓦·列维科夫斯基，捷克斯洛伐克共和国特命全权大使雅·沃萨格里克，蒙古人民共和国特命全权大使德·阿迪尔毕希，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国特命全权大使米·普里夫蒂，保加利亚人民共和国临时代办维·维尔科夫，越南民主共和国临时代办阮德葵，德意志民主共和国临时代办克·赛兹。
