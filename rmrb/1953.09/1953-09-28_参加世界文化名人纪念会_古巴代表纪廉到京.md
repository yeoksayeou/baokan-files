Title: Cuban Representative Guillén Arrives in Beijing to Attend the Commemoration of World Cultural Figures
Author:
Date: 1953-09-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 27th: Mr. Nicolás Guillén, the Cuban representative invited by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and four other organizations to attend the Commemoration of World Cultural Figures in our country, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 27th.

Among those who went to the airport to welcome him were Liu Guan'yi, Yuan Shuipai, Zhu Shilun, Zhao Feng, and others.

Also at the airport to welcome him were Kameoka Togo and Wantuleli, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, as well as the French representatives, Mr. and Mrs. Vercors, and the Polish representatives, Professor Jablonski and Mr. Sukrowski, who had already arrived in Beijing to attend the Commemoration of World Cultural Figures.

### 参加世界文化名人纪念会  古巴代表纪廉到京

1953-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

    参加世界文化名人纪念会
    古巴代表纪廉到京
    【新华社二十七日讯】应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会等五团体邀请，前来我国参加世界文化名人纪念会的古巴代表尼古拉斯·纪廉先生于二十七日乘飞机抵达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有刘贯一、袁水拍、朱世纶、赵沨等多人。
    到机场欢迎的还有亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长龟田东伍和万徒勒里，以及前已抵京参加世界文化名人纪念会的法国代表维尔高尔夫妇和波兰代表雅布朗斯基教授和苏克罗夫斯基先生。
