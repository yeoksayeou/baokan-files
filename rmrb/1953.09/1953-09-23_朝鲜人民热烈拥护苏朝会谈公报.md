Title: The Korean People Warmly Support the Communiqué of the Soviet-Korean Talks
Author:
Date: 1953-09-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 22nd: The Korean people warmly support the communiqué of the talks between the Soviet Government and the Government Delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. On the 21st, Pyongyang newspapers published special editions, carrying the communiqué of the talks between the Soviet Government and the Government Delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the speeches by Chairman Malenkov and Premier Kim Il-sung at the banquet held in the Kremlin, and large portraits of Chairman Malenkov and Premier Kim Il-sung. The large-character headlines on the upper part of the first and second pages of the "Rodong Sinmun" special edition read: "Long Live the Eternal Friendship and Unity of the Korean and Soviet Peoples!" "The Great Soviet Union is the True Helper of the Korean People!"
	When the newspaper special edition was released, people could be seen everywhere on the main streets of Pyongyang, carefully and excitedly reading the Soviet-Korean talks communiqué in front of the display boards on both sides of the avenue, at the entrances of cinemas and state-owned stores. The news spread like a spring breeze to every construction site, government office, entertainment venue, and residential area. People warmly welcomed and supported the great achievements of the talks with joyous hearts. The staff and workers of the Municipal Facilities Undertaking Office, who are actively fighting to rebuild Pyongyang, held a mass meeting on the 21st to unanimously and warmly support the Soviet-Korean talks communiqué. When Kim Eun-taek, the person in charge of the Facilities Undertaking Office, read the full text of the communiqué and the speeches by Malenkov and Kim Il-sung at the meeting, thunderous applause and cheers rang out continuously; staff and workers took the stage one after another to express their gratitude for the Soviet Union's selfless assistance. Model worker Kim Ok-ja said: The great Soviet Union's assistance to us is the guarantee of our victory in completing the post-war recovery and construction work. We must intensify the great project of rebuilding Pyongyang and repay the great Soviet assistance with practical actions. Technician Kim Sang-ok is determined to complete his task of repairing the subgrade of Stalin Road by the end of September ahead of schedule.
	Representatives of various democratic parties, people's organizations, and people from all walks of life in Korea have successively published talks and articles in newspapers, unanimously expressing their warm support for the Soviet-Korean talks communiqué and expressing their sincere gratitude to the Soviet government and the Soviet people for their sincere assistance.

### 朝鲜人民热烈拥护苏朝会谈公报

1953-09-23
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民热烈拥护苏朝会谈公报
    【新华社平壤二十二日电】朝鲜人民热烈拥护苏联政府和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团会谈公报。平壤各报在二十一日都出版号外，刊载了苏联政府和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团会谈的公报、马林科夫主席和金日成首相在克里姆林宫举行的宴会上的谈话，并刊登了马林科夫主席和金日成首相的巨幅像片。“劳动新闻”号外一、二两版上方的大字标题是：“朝苏两国人民的永远友谊团结万岁！”“伟大的苏联是朝鲜人民的真正援助者！”
    当报纸号外发行后，在平壤市通衢大道上，在大道两旁、电影院和国营商店门口的揭示牌前，到处可以看到人们在仔细地兴奋地阅读苏朝会谈公报。消息像春风一样地立刻传遍了每一个建筑工地、机关、娱乐场所和居民区。人们都以欢乐的心情热烈欢迎和拥护会谈的巨大成就。正在为重建平壤市而积极斗争的市设施事业所的职工们，在二十一日举行群众大会，一致热烈拥护苏朝会谈公报。当设施事业所负责人金殷泽在会上朗读公报的全文和马林科夫、金日成的谈话时，场内响起了雷动的掌声和欢呼声，经久不息；职工们纷纷登台发言，感谢苏联大公无私的援助。模范工人金玉子说：伟大的苏联对我们的援助，是我们在完成战后恢复建设工作中取得胜利的保证。我们一定要加紧进行重建平壤的伟大工程，用实际的行动来报答苏联的伟大援助。技工金相玉决心提前在九月底完成自己在整修斯大林路路基中的任务。
    朝鲜各民主党派、人民团体代表和各界人民，纷纷在报上发表谈话和文章，一致表示热烈拥护苏朝会谈公报，并感谢苏联政府和苏联人民的真挚援助。
