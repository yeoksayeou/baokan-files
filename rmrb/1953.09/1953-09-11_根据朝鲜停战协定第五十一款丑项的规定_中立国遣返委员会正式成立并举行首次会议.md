Title: According to Item F of Article 51 of the Korean Armistice Agreement, the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission is Officially Established and Holds its First Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-09-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, September 10th: The Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission stipulated in Item F of Article 51 of the Korean Armistice Agreement was formally established on September 9th.

At 2:30 PM on September 9th, Lieutenant General K.S. Thimayya of India, Chairman of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission, arrived in Panmunjom with his alternate representative, Ambassador Chakravarty. Lieutenant General Lee Sang Cho, Chief Commissioner of the Korean-Chinese side of the Military Armistice Commission, and General Ting Kuo-yu welcomed them at Panmunjom and introduced Lieutenant General Thimayya and Ambassador Chakravarty to Colonel Šimovič, Czechoslovak Ambassador-rank Commissioner of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission, and Mr. Gajewski, Polish Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary-rank Commissioner.

At 6:00 PM, Lieutenant General Thimayya, Colonel Šimovič, and Mr. Gajewski, together with Mr. Stenström, the Swedish Commissioner of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission, and Mr. Däniker, the Swiss Commissioner, held the first meeting of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission.

### 根据朝鲜停战协定第五十一款丑项的规定  中立国遣返委员会正式成立并举行首次会议

1953-09-11
第4版()
专栏：

    根据朝鲜停战协定第五十一款丑项的规定
    中立国遣返委员会正式成立并举行首次会议
    【新华社开城十日电】朝鲜停战协定第五十一款丑项所规定的中立国遣返委员会于九月九日正式成立。
    九月九日下午二时半，中立国遣返委员会主席、印度蒂迈雅中将，偕其候补代表查克拉瓦蒂大使到达板门店。军事停战委员会朝中方面首席委员李相朝中将及委员丁国钰将军在板门店予以欢迎，并介绍蒂迈雅中将及查克拉瓦蒂大使，与中立国遣返委员会捷克斯洛伐克全权大使衔委员西莫维茨上校及波兰全权公使衔委员加耶夫斯基先生会晤。
    下午六时，蒂迈雅中将、西莫维茨上校、加耶夫斯基先生，与中立国遣返委员会的瑞典委员斯坦斯特鲁先生及瑞士委员邓尼克先生，举行了中立国遣返委员会第一次会议。
