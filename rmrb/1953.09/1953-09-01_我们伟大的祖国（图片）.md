    Our Great Motherland
    The Xiong Yue Agricultural School in Liaodong Province is a well-equipped secondary technical school. In the past four years, more than a thousand graduates have left this school to take up posts in the country's economic construction. This is a scene of first-year students at the school measuring rainfall.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-09-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    辽东省熊岳农业学校是一所设备完善的中等技术学校。四年以来，有一千多名毕业生从这个学校走上祖国经济建设的岗位。这是该校一年级学生正在测量雨量的情形。
