Our Great Motherland
Shanghai factories are providing a large amount of industrial equipment to support basic construction throughout the country. Workers at the state-owned Shanghai Wire Factory produced 600 li of operating cable and power cable for Anshan Iron and Steel. The photo shows workers loading the cable, ready to be shipped to various locations.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    上海各工厂正以大量的工业器材支援全国各地的基本建设。国营上海电线厂的工人为鞍钢生产了六百华里操作电缆和电力电缆。这是工人正在把电缆装起，准备运往各地。
