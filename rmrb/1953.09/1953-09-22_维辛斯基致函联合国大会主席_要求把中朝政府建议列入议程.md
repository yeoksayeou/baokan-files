Title: Vyshinsky Sends Letter to UN General Assembly President Requesting Inclusion of Sino-Korean Government Proposal on Agenda
Author:
Date: 1953-09-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New York, September 21st: TASS, New York, September 21st: Andrei Y. Vyshinsky, head of the Soviet delegation to the Eighth Session of the United Nations General Assembly, wrote a letter on September 18th to the President of the United Nations General Assembly.

The letter stated: "The Soviet delegation considers it necessary to include the following item on the agenda of the Eighth Session of the United Nations General Assembly: 'The Secretary-General's memorandum on the implementation of the third resolution adopted by the General Assembly on August 28, 1953.'

According to Rule 20 of the Rules of Procedure, I hereby submit an explanatory memorandum on this matter.

I would be very grateful if you could instruct that the explanatory memorandum attached to my letter be distributed as a General Assembly document."

The full text of the explanatory memorandum attached to Vyshinsky's letter is as follows:

"In accordance with the provisions of the third resolution of the General Assembly on August 28, 1953, the Secretary-General shall transmit the proposals on the Korean question adopted by the Third Session of the Seventh General Assembly of the United Nations, together with the records of the General Assembly's discussions, to the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and report in due course.

The Secretary-General has now distributed to the delegations attending the Eighth Session of the United Nations General Assembly a memorandum on the implementation of the third resolution of August 28, 1953.

The Soviet delegation considers that this memorandum and the replies of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea are of great importance, and hereby requests that the following item be included on the agenda of the Eighth Session of the United Nations General Assembly: The Secretary-General's memorandum on the implementation of the third resolution adopted by the General Assembly on August 28, 1953."

### 维辛斯基致函联合国大会主席  要求把中朝政府建议列入议程

1953-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

    维辛斯基致函联合国大会主席
    要求把中朝政府建议列入议程
    【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社纽约二十一日讯：出席联合国第八届大会的苏联代表团团长安·扬·维辛斯基，于九月十八日写了一封信给联合国大会主席。
    信上说：“苏联代表团认为有必要把下列项目列入联合国第八届大会议程：‘秘书长关于一九五三年八月二十八日大会通过的第三项决议案的执行情况的备忘录’。
    根据议事规则第二十条，我特就这个问题提出一个解释性的备忘录。
    如果你能指令把我信中所附的解释性备忘录作为大会文件分发，我将甚为感谢。”
    维辛斯基信中所附的解释性备忘录全文如下：
    “根据一九五三年八月二十八日大会第三项决议案的规定，秘书长应将联合国第七届大会第三期会议通过的关于朝鲜问题的各项提案，连同大会讨论的记录，送交中华人民共和国中央人民政府与朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府，并于适当时提出报告。
    秘书长现在已将一项关于一九五三年八月二十八日第三项决议案的执行情况的备忘录，分发给出席联合国第八届大会的各国代表团。
    苏联代表团认为这一备忘录以及中华人民共和国中央人民政府与朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府的答复都具有极大的重要性，兹要求把下列项目列入联合国第八届大会议程：秘书长关于一九五三年八月二十八日大会通过的第三项决议案的执行情况的备忘录。”
