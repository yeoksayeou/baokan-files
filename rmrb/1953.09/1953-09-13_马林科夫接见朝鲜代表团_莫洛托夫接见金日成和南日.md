Title: Malenkov Receives the Korean Delegation; Molotov Receives Kim Il-sung and Nam Il
Author:
Date: 1953-09-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 12th: TASS, Moscow: On September 11th, the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, G.M. Malenkov, received the government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea: Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Kim Il-sung, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, Park Jung-ae, Vice-Premier of the Cabinet, Jeong Il-ryong, Minister of Foreign Affairs, Nam Il, Chairman of the State Planning Commission, Jeong Jun-taek, Minister of Railways, Kim Hoe-il, and Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the Soviet Union, Rim Hae.

    The Soviet side present at the meeting included: V.M. Molotov, N.S. Khrushchev, N.A. Bulganin, A.I. Mikoyan, M.Z. Saburov, Soviet Ambassador to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, S.P. Suzdalev, and Soviet Trade Representative to Korea, P.I. Sachenko.

    Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, Zhang Wentian, also attended the meeting.

    The meeting was conducted in a friendly and warm atmosphere.
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 12th: TASS, Moscow: The First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, V.M. Molotov, received the Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Kim Il-sung, on September 11th.

    Present at the reception were: Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Nam Il, and Soviet Ambassador to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, S.P. Suzdalev.

### 马林科夫接见朝鲜代表团  莫洛托夫接见金日成和南日

1953-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

    马林科夫接见朝鲜代表团
    莫洛托夫接见金日成和南日
    【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：九月十一日，苏联部长会议主席格·马·马林科夫, 接见了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱，内阁副首相郑一龙，外务相南日，国家计划委员会委员长郑准泽，铁道相金会一，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻苏联大使林海。
    会谈时苏联方面在座的有：维·米·莫洛托夫，尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫，尼·亚·布尔加宁，阿·伊·米高扬，马·扎·萨布罗夫，苏联驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使斯·普·苏兹达列夫，苏联驻朝鲜商务代表普·伊·萨肯。
    中华人民共和国驻苏联大使张闻天也参加了会谈。
    会谈在友好和热诚的气氛中进行。
    【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议第一副主席兼苏联外交部长维·米·莫洛托夫，于九月十一日接见朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成。
    接见时在座的有：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务相南日，苏联驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使斯·普·苏兹达列夫。
