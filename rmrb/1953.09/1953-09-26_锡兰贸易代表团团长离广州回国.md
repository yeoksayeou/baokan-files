Title: Head of Ceylon Trade Delegation Leaves Guangzhou for Home

Author: 
Date: 1953-09-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 25th. Fang Xica, head of the Ceylon Government Trade Delegation, arrived in Guangzhou from Beijing on the evening of the 24th and left Guangzhou on the morning of the 25th via Hong Kong to return home. Representatives from the Guangzhou Municipal People's Government Foreign Affairs Office and the Reception Office, among other units, were at the station to see him off.

### 锡兰贸易代表团团长离广州回国

1953-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    锡兰贸易代表团团长离广州回国
    【新华社广州二十五日电】锡兰政府贸易代表团团长方席卡在二十四日晚从北京到达广州，二十五日晨离广州取道香港回国。到车站欢送的有广州市人民政府外事处、交际处等单位代表。
