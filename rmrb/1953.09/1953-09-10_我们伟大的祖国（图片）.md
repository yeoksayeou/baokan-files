    Our Great Motherland
    The Tianzhu Autonomous Region in Gansu Province has newly built sheep dip pools. This is an important facility for pastoral areas. After sheep are dipped, various ectoparasitic diseases can be prevented and controlled. This is a corner of the dipping pool. Herdsmen are driving the sheep into the dipping pool.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    甘肃省天祝自治区新建了绵羊药浴池。这是牧区的重要设备。绵羊经过药浴后，可以防治体外各种寄生虫病。这是药浴池的一角。牧民们正把羊群赶进药浴池里来。
