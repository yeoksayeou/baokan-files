Title: Results of the Leipzig Fair Show Expansion of East German Foreign Trade
Author:
Date: 1953-09-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 10th (TASS dispatch): The Ministry of Foreign and Domestic Trade of the German Democratic Republic issued a statement regarding the results of the traditional Leipzig International Fair, held from August 30th to September 9th.

The statement pointed out that the 1953 Leipzig Fair was a manifestation of the strength of the democratic world market; the fair demonstrated the economic achievements of the German Democratic Republic and illustrated the expansion of the German Democratic Republic's trade relations in the process of implementing the new policy aimed at raising the living standards of all strata of the people. The Leipzig Fair contributed to the development of trade between East and West.

This year, twenty-five countries participated in the fair, including fifteen Western countries. Six hundred and twenty-one West German companies participated in this fair, one hundred and thirty-five more than last year. Four hundred and seventy-eight companies from capitalist countries participated in this fair, one hundred and fifty more than last year.

At the 1953 Fair, the total turnover of foreign trade and domestic trade in Germany was 1,002,300,000 rubles, compared with 526,000,000 rubles last year. The total value of export contracts concluded by German Democratic Republic trading organizations at the fair was 797 million rubles, of which 468 million rubles of contracts were concluded with democratic countries and 207 million rubles with Western countries. The total value of import contracts concluded by the German Democratic Republic with other countries was 226 million rubles.

### 来比锡博览会的结果表明  民主德国对外贸易扩大

1953-09-14
第4版()
专栏：

    来比锡博览会的结果表明
    民主德国对外贸易扩大
   【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社柏林十日讯：德意志民主共和国对外与对内贸易部就八月三十日至九月九日举行的传统的来比锡国际博览会的结果发表声明。
    声明指出，一九五三年来比锡博览会是民主世界市场力量的表现；博览会说明了德意志民主共和国的经济成就，并说明了在执行目的在提高各阶层人民生活水平的新方针的过程中，德意志民主共和国贸易关系的扩展。来比锡博览会有助于东西方贸易的发展。
    今年参加博览会的有二十五个国家，其中有十五个西方国家。西德有六百二十一家公司参加这次博览会，比去年多一百三十五家。资本主义国家有四百七十八家公司参加这次博览会，比去年多了一百五十家。
    在一九五三年博览会上，对外贸易与德国内部贸易成交总数是十亿零二千三百万卢布，去年的成交数是五亿二千六百万卢布。德意志民主共和国贸易机构在博览会上缔结的出口合同总值是七亿九千七百万卢布，其中有四亿六千八百万卢布的合同是与民主国家缔结的，二亿零七百万卢布是与西方国家缔结的。德意志民主共和国与其它国家缔结的进口合同总值二亿二千六百万卢布。
