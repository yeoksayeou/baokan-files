Title: Pyongyang Newspapers Publish Congratulatory Telegrams from Chinese and Soviet Leaders on the Anniversary of the Victory in the War of Resistance Against Japan

Author:

Date: 1953-09-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 5th: Pyongyang newspapers on the 5th all prominently featured on the front page the congratulatory telegrams from Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government and Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government to Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union Malenkov and Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov on the occasion of the eighth anniversary of the victory in the War of Resistance Against Japan, as well as the congratulatory telegrams from Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union Malenkov and Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government and Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government.

### 平壤各报刊载中苏两国领袖  祝贺抗日战争胜利日的贺电

1953-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    平壤各报刊载中苏两国领袖
    祝贺抗日战争胜利日的贺电
    【新华社平壤五日电】平壤各报五日都以一版首要地位刊载了我中央人民政府毛泽东主席和中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来为祝贺抗日战争胜利八周年给苏联部长会议主席马林科夫和苏联外交部部长莫洛托夫的贺电，及苏联部长会议主席马林科夫和苏联外交部部长莫洛托夫给我中央人民政府毛泽东主席和中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来的贺电。
