Title: Zhou Enlai's Telegram to the UN Secretary-General Published in Full by the Soviet "Pravda"
Author:
Date: 1953-09-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 14th: The Pravda published in full on the 14th the telegram from Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, to UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld on September 13th. Pravda also published on the 14th news of the 24th session of the Central People's Government Committee and the report by Peng Dehuai, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, on the work of the Chinese People's Volunteers in resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea.
