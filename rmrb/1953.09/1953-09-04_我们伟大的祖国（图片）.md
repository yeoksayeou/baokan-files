Our Great Motherland
    Afforestation in the sandy wasteland in eastern Henan Province has achieved great results. The scourge of sandy wasteland in eastern Henan has now been basically eliminated. Farmers are expanding cultivated land area and concentrating on production under the protection of shelter forests. This is a forest farm near Lanfeng. Workers at the forest farm have cultivated some fruit trees and grapes in the sandy wasteland, and are preparing to popularize them among the peasant masses.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    河南省东部的沙荒地造林有很大的成绩。现在豫东一带的沙荒为害已基本消除，农民正在防护林的保护下扩大耕地面积，安心生产。这是兰封附近的一个林场。林场工人在沙荒地上培育了一些果树和葡萄，并准备推广到农民群众中去。
