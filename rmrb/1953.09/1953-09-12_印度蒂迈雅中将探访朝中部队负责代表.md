Title: Indian Lieutenant General Thimayya Visits Responsible Representatives of the Korean-Chinese Side

Author: 
Date: 1953-09-12
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, September 11th) Lieutenant General Thimayya of India, Chairman of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission, accompanied by his alternate representative Ambassador Chakravarti, Chief of Staff Brigadier General Gaul, and Secretary Mr. Hakser, visited the responsible representatives of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers in Kaesong at 7:30 PM on September 10th. Lieutenant General Li Sang-cho, Chief Commissioner of the Korean-Chinese side of the Military Armistice Commission, and General Ding Guoyu hosted a banquet, and the guests and hosts enjoyed themselves.

### 印度蒂迈雅中将探访朝中部队负责代表

1953-09-12
第4版()
专栏：

    印度蒂迈雅中将探访朝中部队负责代表
  【新华社开城十一日电】中立国遣返委员会主席印度蒂迈雅中将偕其候补代表查克拉瓦蒂大使、参谋长高尔准将及秘书哈克萨尔先生，于九月十日下午七时半前来开城探访朝鲜人民军及中国人民志愿军负责代表。军事停战委员会朝中方面首席委员李相朝中将及委员丁国钰将军设宴招待，宾主尽欢而散。
