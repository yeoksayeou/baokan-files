Title: The Third Congress of the International Association of Scientific Workers Held in Hungary; Delegation of the All-China Federation of Scientific Societies Departs for the Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-09-05
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, September 4th. The Third Congress of the International Association of Scientific Workers will be held on September 12th in Budapest, the capital of Hungary. The All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies (hereinafter referred to as "the Federation") is a member organization of the International Association of Scientific Workers, and has sent a delegation to attend the Congress. The delegation, led by Tu Changwang, the head of the delegation, departed by plane from Beijing on the morning of the 4th. (Zhou Peiyuan will depart on the 7th due to other commitments.) Those who came to the airport to see them off included Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and Vice Chairman of the Federation, Qian Sanqiang, Standing Committee Member of the Federation, Wu Huazhi, Secretary-General of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Government Administration Council's Committee on Cultural and Educational Affairs, and Ding Zhihui, Vice President of the Peking Union Medical College Hospital. The Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Hungarian Embassy, Csatorday, and staff members also came to the airport to see them off.
    The list of the delegation is as follows:
    Tu Changwang (Honorary Secretary of the International Association of Scientific Workers, Deputy Secretary-General of the Federation)
    Zhang Hanying (Member of the National Committee of the Federation, Head of the China Hydraulic Engineering Society)
    Zhou Peiyuan (Director of the Organization Department of the Federation, Head of the Chinese Physical Society)
    Yang Xiandong (Deputy Director of the Organization Department of the Federation, Head of the Chinese Agronomy Society)
    Wu Yingkai (Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the Federation, Director of the Chinese Medical Association)
    Cao Richang (Head of the Chinese Psychological Society)

### 国际科协三届代表大会在匈举行  全国科联代表团启程赴会

1953-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    国际科协三届代表大会在匈举行
    全国科联代表团启程赴会
    【新华社四日讯】国际科学工作者协会将于九月十二日在匈牙利首都布达佩斯举行第三届代表大会。中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会（以下简称科联）为国际科学工作者协会的会员组织，特派遣代表团出席大会。代表团已在四日上午由团长涂长望率领乘飞机离京（其中周培源代表因事将延至七日启程）。到机场欢送的有中国科学院副院长、科联副主席吴有训、科联常务委员钱三强、政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局秘书长吴化之及中国协和医院副院长丁志辉等。匈牙利大使馆临时代办察托尔道伊及随员也到机场欢送。
    代表团名单如下：涂长望（国际科学工作者协会名誉秘书、科联副秘书长）
    张含英（科联全国委员会委员、中国水利工程学会负责人）
    周培源（科联组织部部长、中国物理学会负责人）
    杨显东（科联组织部副部长、中国农学会负责人）
    吴英恺（科联国际联络部副部长、中华医学会理事）
    曹日昌（中国心理学会负责人）
