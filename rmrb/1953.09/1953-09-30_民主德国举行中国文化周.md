Title: German Democratic Republic Holds Chinese Culture Week
Author:
Date: 1953-09-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 28th: The German News Agency reports from Berlin: Around the National Day of the People's Republic of China (from September 30th to October 6th), the German Democratic Republic and the Democratic Sector of Berlin will hold a Chinese Culture Week. This Chinese Culture Week is being organized by the National Council of the National Front of Democratic Germany and the German Association for Foreign Cultural Relations. This cultural week will help the German people further understand the development and achievements of the Chinese people.

Among the various exhibitions held during the Chinese Culture Week, there will be an introduction to the control of the Huai River, as well as approximately thirty replicas of Dunhuang murals.

The Chinese Youth Art Troupe, consisting of one hundred and sixty people, will arrive in Berlin in the coming days. They will travel throughout the German Democratic Republic and will perform in Berlin on September 30th.

### 民主德国举行中国文化周

1953-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国举行中国文化周
    【新华社二十八日讯】据德意志通讯社柏林讯：在中华人民共和国国庆节前后（从九月三十日到十月六日），德意志民主共和国和柏林民主区将举行中国文化周。这个中国文化周是由民主德国全国阵线全国委员会和德国对外文化关系协会举办的。这个文化周将有助于德国人民进一步了解中国人民的发展和成就。
    在中国文化周举办的各种展览中，将有关于控制淮河的介绍，还有约三十件敦煌壁画的摹本。
    由一百六十个人组成的中国青年文工团日内即将抵柏林，他们将在民主德国各地旅行，并于九月三十日在柏林演出。
