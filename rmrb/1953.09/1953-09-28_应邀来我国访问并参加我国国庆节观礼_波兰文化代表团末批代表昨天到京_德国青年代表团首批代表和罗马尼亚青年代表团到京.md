Title: Polish Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing for Visit and National Day Celebrations; First Group of German Youth Delegation and Romanian Youth Delegation Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-09-28
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: The last group of the Polish Cultural Delegation, consisting of seven members, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 27th, invited to visit China and participate in the National Day celebrations according to the 1953 implementation plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement.
    Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Zhao Feng, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, and figures from the cultural, artistic, and scientific circles of the capital: Liu Kaiqu, Zou Difan, Yu Yixuan, Li Guoquan, Fan Jishen, Zhang Zhaoqian, and others.
    Also present at the airport to welcome them were the Polish Ambassador to China, Kirylok, and the head of the Polish Cultural Delegation, the Minister of Culture and Art of Poland, Sokolsky, who had arrived earlier. The full list of the delegation is as follows:
    Head: Włodzimierz Sokorski (Minister of Culture and Art, already arrived)
    Members: Witold Jabłoński (Professor at Warsaw University, already arrived)
    Wojciech Żukrowski (Professor, Writer, already arrived)
    Jadwiga Lechteńska (Professor, Biologist)
    Jadwiga Szelerska (Professor of Marxist Aesthetics at the Scientific Cadre Education Academy)
    Marian Szymański (Secretary of the Polish Teachers' Union)
    Andrzej Hiolski (Singer at the Bytom State Theatre)
    Edward Statkiewicz (Violinist, Orchestra Director of the Bydgoszcz State Concert Hall)
    Lidia Grychtołówna (Female Pianist)
    Aleksander Kobzdej (Painter, Professor at the Academy of Fine Arts)
    Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: The first group of the German Youth Delegation, consisting of nine members, and the Romanian People's Republic Youth Delegation, consisting of five members, led by their respective heads, Werner Erben (Germany) and Chizu Figoria (Romania), arrived in Beijing by plane on the 27th, invited by the Chinese New Democratic Youth League and the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth to visit China and participate in the National Day celebrations.
    Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Liu Daosheng, Qu Tangliang, and Zhang Ze, Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, Wu Xueqian, Minister of the International Liaison Department, Nie Yunhua, representative of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, and more than 30 youth representatives from Beijing.

### 应邀来我国访问并参加我国国庆节观礼  波兰文化代表团末批代表昨天到京  德国青年代表团首批代表和罗马尼亚青年代表团到京

1953-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国访问并参加我国国庆节观礼
    波兰文化代表团末批代表昨天到京
    德国青年代表团首批代表和罗马尼亚青年代表团到京
    【新华社二十七日讯】根据中波文化合作协定一九五三年执行计划，应邀到我国访问并参加国庆观礼的波兰文化代表团末批代表一行七人，于二十七日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有：中央人民政府文化部办公厅主任赵沨，和首都文化、艺术、科学界人士：刘开渠、邹荻帆、喻宜萱、黎国荃、范继森、张肇骞等。
    到机场欢迎的还有：波兰驻华大使基里洛克和先期抵京的波兰文化代表团团长、波兰文化艺术部部长索科尔斯基等。代表团全部名单如下：
    团长：沃吉米日·索科尔斯基（文化艺术部部长，已先期到京）
    团员：维托尔德·雅布朗斯基（华沙大学教授，已先期到京）
    沃伊切赫·苏克罗夫斯基（教授、作家，已先期到京）
    雅德维加·列克琴斯卡（教授、生物学家）
    雅德维加·谢克尔斯卡（科学干部教育学院马克思主义美学教授）
    马利扬·希曼斯基（波兰教师联合会书记）
    安杰伊·赫奥尔斯基（贝托姆城国家剧院歌唱家）
    艾德瓦尔德·斯达特凯维奇（提琴家，比得哥煦城国家音乐厅乐队指导）
    李季雅·格雷赫托尔（女钢琴家）
    亚历山大·科布兹德伊（画家，美术学院教授）
    【新华社二十七日讯】应中国新民主主义青年团及中华全国民主青年联合会邀请来我国访问并参加国庆观礼的德国青年代表团首批代表九人及罗马尼亚人民共和国青年代表团一行五人，分别在团长温纳·埃尔本（德国）及基祖·费戈里亚（罗马尼亚）率领下，于二十七日乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记刘导生、区棠亮、章泽，国际联络部部长吴学谦、中华全国民主青年联合会代表聂运华以及北京市青年代表三十多人。
