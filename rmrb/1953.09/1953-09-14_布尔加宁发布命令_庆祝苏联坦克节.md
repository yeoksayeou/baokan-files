Title: Bulganin Issues Order to Celebrate Soviet Tank Day
Author:
Date: 1953-09-14
Page: 4

    Bulganin Issues Order
    Celebrating Soviet Tank Day
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 13th] TASS, Moscow: The Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union, Marshal of the Soviet Union, N. A. Bulganin, issued the following order on the 13th to celebrate Soviet Tank Day:
    Comrades tankmen and tank manufacturers!
    Comrades soldiers and non-commissioned officers of the Army and Navy!
    Comrades officers and generals of the Army and Navy!
    Today, the Soviet Army, Navy, and all of our people are celebrating Tank Day together.
    On the occasion of celebrating Tank Day, I extend my greetings and congratulations to you.
    I wish tankmen and tank manufacturers new achievements in further strengthening the combat effectiveness of the armored and motorized forces of our Soviet state.
    To celebrate Tank Day, I order:
    Today, September 13, 1953, a twenty-gun salute will be fired in our country's capital, Moscow, and in the capitals of all the union republics.
    Long live Soviet tankmen and tank manufacturers!
    Long live the Soviet people and their glorious armed forces!
    Long live the Soviet government!
    Long live the Communist Party of the Soviet Union!

### 布尔加宁发布命令  庆祝苏联坦克节

1953-09-14
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁发布命令
    庆祝苏联坦克节
    【新华社十三日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联国防部部长、苏联元帅尼·亚·布尔加宁十三日发布庆祝苏联坦克节的命令如下：
    坦克手和坦克制造者同志们！
    陆海军战士和士官同志们！
    陆海军军官和将军同志们！
    今天，苏联陆军、海军和我们全体人民一道庆祝坦克节。
    在庆祝坦克节的时候，我谨向你们致敬和祝贺。
    我祝坦克手和坦克制造者在进一步加强我们苏维埃国家的装甲部队和摩托化部队的战斗力方面取得新的成就。
    为了庆祝坦克节，我命令：
    今天，一九五三年九月十三日，在我们祖国的首都莫斯科和各加盟共和国的首都各鸣礼炮二十响。
    苏联坦克手和坦克制造者万岁！
    苏联人民和他们光荣的武装部队万岁！
    苏联政府万岁！
    苏联共产党万岁！
