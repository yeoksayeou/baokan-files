Title: Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Other Units Commemorate Tolstoy's Birthday
Author:
Date: 1953-09-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 8th: The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Beijing Federation of Literary and Art Workers, and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries jointly held a commemorative meeting in Beijing on the evening of the 8th to mark the 125th anniversary of the birth of the great Russian writer Leo Tolstoy. Among those present at the meeting were Li Zhanwu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Zeng Ping, Secretary-General of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; and well-known Chinese literary artists and translators such as Cao Jinghua, Xu Beihong, Lü Ji, Ma Ke, and more than 500 people from the literary and art circles of the capital.

Also present were Sokolov, the representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in China, and many Soviet friends.

The commemorative meeting was presided over by Lao She, Vice President of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and a famous writer. At the meeting, Dong Qiusi, the translator of Tolstoy's masterpiece "War and Peace," gave a report entitled "The Great Russian Writer Leo Tolstoy." He described in detail Tolstoy's life, thoughts, and the historical circumstances and high artistic value of his great works. Dong Qiusi said: We commemorate Tolstoy today to inherit his literary legacy. What we want to inherit is not only Tolstoy's realistic art, but also his deep love for the people and his country.

A stage play film recording of Tolstoy's great work "Anna Karenina" was also screened at the meeting.

In addition: The Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries launched the "125th Anniversary of Leo Tolstoy's Birth Photo Exhibition" on the 8th, exhibiting a total of 110 photos related to Tolstoy's life, works, and the publication and performance of his works in the Soviet Union.

### 北京市中苏友协等单位  纪念托尔斯泰诞辰

1953-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市中苏友协等单位
    纪念托尔斯泰诞辰
    【新华社八日讯】北京市中苏友好协会、北京市文学艺术工作者联合会、苏联对外文化协会于八日晚在北京联合举行俄罗斯伟大作家列夫·托尔斯泰诞生一百二十五周年纪念会。出席纪念会的有中苏友好协会总会副总干事李沾吾，北京市中苏友好协会总干事曾平，我国著名文学艺术家、翻译家曹靖华，徐悲鸿，吕骥，马可和首都文学艺术界人士共五百多人。
    到会的还有苏联对外文化协会驻我国代表索高洛夫及苏联友人多人。
    纪念会由北京市中苏友好协会副会长、著名作家老舍主持。会上，托尔斯泰巨著“战争与和平”底译者董秋斯作了“伟大的俄罗斯作家列夫·托尔斯泰”的报告。他详细讲述了托尔斯泰的生平、思想及其伟大作品产生的历史情况与高度的艺术价值。董秋斯说：我们今天来纪念托尔斯泰是为了继承他的文学遗产，我们要继承的不仅是托尔斯泰的现实主义的艺术，还有他热爱人民热爱祖国的深厚感情。
    会上并放映了托尔斯泰伟大作品“安娜·卡列尼娜”的舞台剧电影记录片。
    又讯：苏联对外文化协会于八日起举办了“列夫·托尔斯泰诞辰一百二十五周年纪念图片展览”，展出有关托尔斯泰生平、著作和著作在苏联出版、演出等图片共一百一十幅。
