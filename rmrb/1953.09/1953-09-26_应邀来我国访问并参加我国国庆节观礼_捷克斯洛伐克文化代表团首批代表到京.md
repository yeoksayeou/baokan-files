Title: Czechoslovak Cultural Delegation's First Group of Representatives Arrives in Beijing to Visit China and Attend National Day Observances
Author:
Date: 1953-09-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th. According to the 1953 implementation plan of the Sino-Czechoslovak Cultural Cooperation Agreement, the first group of ten representatives of the Czechoslovak Cultural Delegation, invited to visit China and attend the National Day celebrations, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 25th, led by the head of the delegation, Marie Majerová, Vice-Chairman of the Czechoslovak Writers' Union and famous Czech female writer.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included: Ding Xilin, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government; Hong Shen, Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Government Administration Council's Cultural and Educational Committee; Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Ke Zhongping, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles; and figures from the capital's cultural and artistic circles: Zou Difan, Hua Junwu, Zhu Ming, Wu Xue, Guo Lanying, Lu Xi, and others.

Also at the airport to welcome them were Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. and Mrs. Kousal, and embassy staff.

The list of the delegation is as follows:

Head: Marie Majerová (female writer, National Prize winner, Vice-Chairman of the Czechoslovak Writers' Union)
Members: Marie Pujmanová (female writer, two-time National Prize winner)
Adolf Hoffmeister (painter and writer, professor at the Academy of Arts, Architecture and Design)
Dr. Vladimír Novotný (Director of the National Gallery, Director of the Art Theory and History Research Department of the Academy of Sciences)
Marie Medvecká (female painter, National Prize winner, not yet arrived in Beijing)
Dr. Jaroslav Jiránek (music theorist, pianist)
Vojtěch Mihálik (Slovak writer, National Prize winner)
Andrej Bagar (actor, National Prize winner)
Marie Blesková (actress of the Prague National Theatre, recipient of the Silver Shield Medal, not yet arrived in Beijing)
Dr. Dana Šťovíčková (female assistant teacher at the Oriental Institute of Charles University in Prague, Chinese translator)
Dr. Eva Karmínová (Chief of the China Section of the Czechoslovak Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations)
Dionýz Blaškovič (Academician of the Academy of Sciences, Director of the Institute of Virology of the Academy of Sciences)
Emil Sypták (reporter for "Red Right Newspaper", not yet arrived in Beijing)
Nina Házuchová (actress, female singer, not yet arrived in Beijing)
Karel Prasěk (Secretary of the "People's Defense Newspaper", not yet arrived in Beijing)
Jiří Felikl (Artistic Director of the Puppet Theatre, not yet arrived in Beijing)
Pavel Kohout (poet and writer, not yet arrived in Beijing)

### 应邀来我国访问并参加我国国庆节观礼  捷克斯洛伐克文化代表团首批代表到京

1953-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国访问并参加我国国庆节观礼
    捷克斯洛伐克文化代表团首批代表到京
    【新华社二十五日讯】根据中捷文化合作协定一九五三年执行计划，应邀来我国访问并参加国庆观礼的捷克斯洛伐克文化代表团首批代表十人，在代表团团长、捷克斯洛伐克作家联盟副主席、捷著名女作家玛丽·玛耶洛娃率领下，于二十五日乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有：中央人民政府文化部副部长丁西林，政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深，外交部交际处副处长王拓，中华全国文学艺术工作者协会副主席柯仲平，以及首都文化艺术界人士：邹荻帆、华君武、朱明、吴雪、郭兰英、路曦等。
    到机场欢迎的还有：捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使康萨拉夫妇及使馆人员。
    代表团名单如下：
    团长：玛丽·玛耶洛娃（女作家、国家奖金得奖人、捷克斯洛伐克作家联盟副主席）
    团员：玛丽·普伊玛诺娃（女作家、两次国家奖金得奖人）
    阿道夫·霍夫梅斯特（画家及作家、工艺学院教授）
    弗拉季米尔·诺沃特尼博士（国家美术馆馆长、科学院艺术理论及历史研究室主任）
    玛丽·麦德维茨卡（女画家、国家奖金得奖人，尚未到京）
    雅罗斯拉夫·伊拉聂克博士（音乐理论家、钢琴家）
    沃伊季赫·米哈利克（斯洛伐克作家、国家奖金得奖人）
    安德烈伊·巴加尔（演员、国家奖金得奖人）
    玛丽·布勒索娃（布拉格国家剧院女演员，曾获得银盾奖章，尚未到京）
    丹娜·斯却维茨科娃博士（布拉格卡罗瓦大学东方学院女助教、中文翻译家）
    埃娃·卡尔敏诺娃博士（捷对外文化联络委员会中国科科长）

    妮娜·哈祖霍娃（演员、女歌唱家，尚未到京）
    卡列尔·普拉赛克（“人民防线报”秘书，尚未到京）
