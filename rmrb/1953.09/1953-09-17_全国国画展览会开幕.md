Title: National Exhibition of Traditional Chinese Painting Opens
Author:
Date: 1953-09-17
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, September 15th: To promote the excellent traditions of national painting and advance traditional Chinese painting creation throughout the country, the National Exhibition of Traditional Chinese Painting, organized by the All-China Artists Association, opened on the 16th at the Yilan Hall in Beihai Park, Beijing. A preview of the exhibition was held on the afternoon of the 15th, and over 200 people from Beijing's literary and art circles visited.
    This exhibition of traditional Chinese painting is the first national exhibition since liberation, featuring 245 carefully selected works by more than 200 traditional Chinese painters from all over the country. These include works by famous traditional Chinese painters such as Qi Baishi, Chen Banding, Yu Feian, Xu Beihong, He Tianjian, Wu Hufan, Ye Qianyu, Guan Shanyue, Li Xiongcai, Huang Binhong, Jiang Zhaohe, and others, as well as works by many new young traditional Chinese painters such as Jiang Yan and others. During the exhibition, the All-China Artists Association will hold a symposium to discuss issues related to traditional Chinese painting creation and further clarify the direction of development for traditional Chinese painting creation.
    The works exhibited were collected by the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government from various provincial and municipal cultural departments in May. A total of 842 traditional Chinese paintings were received from 32 provinces and cities. After several rounds of selection by the National Artists Association, it was decided to exhibit 245 works. Beijing, Shanghai, Nanjing, Fuzhou, and other places held exhibitions of traditional Chinese painting this summer to widely solicit works for participation in this exhibition.

### 全国国画展览会开幕

1953-09-17
第1版()
专栏：

    全国国画展览会开幕
    【新华社十五日讯】为发扬民族绘画的优良传统，推动全国国画创作，中华全国美术工作者协会举办的全国国画展览会已于十六日在北京北海公园漪澜堂开幕。十五日下午举行展览会预展，北京文学艺术界人士二百多人前往参观。
    此次国画展览是解放后第一次全国性的展出，内容有全国各地国画家二百多人的精心作品二百四十五件。其中有著名国画家齐白石、陈半丁、于非闇、徐悲鸿、贺天健、吴湖帆、叶浅予、关山月、黎雄才、黄宾虹、蒋兆和等人的作品和不少新的青年国画家姜燕等的作品。中华全国美术工作者协会将在展览期间举行座谈会讨论国画创作问题，进一步明确国画创作的发展方向。
    这次展出的作品，是由中央人民政府文化部在五月间通知各省、市文化部门徵集的，共收到三十二个省、市的国画作品八百四十二件，经过全国美协数次评选，决定展出二百四十五件作品。北京、上海、南京、福州等地为广泛徵选作品，准备参加此次展出，曾在今年夏季分别举行了国画展览会。
