    Our Great Motherland
    The dance troupe of the Central Experimental Opera House, in order to establish national dance dramas, takes Chinese classical dance as its main course of study, and also studies Chinese folk dance and ethnic minority dance, as well as excerpts of opera performances. These students are learning the basic movements of Chinese classical dance.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中央实验歌剧院舞蹈演员队，为了建立民族舞剧，以中国古典舞为学习的主要课程，并学习中国民间舞和少数民族舞，以及歌剧中片断的表演。这些学员正在学习中国古典舞的基本动作。
