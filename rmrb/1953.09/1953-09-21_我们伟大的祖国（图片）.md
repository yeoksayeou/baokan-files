Our Great Motherland
　　Jingyi Road is a highway from Xizhimen in the capital to the Summer Palace. After the expansion of this highway, it will make transportation between the capital and the western suburbs more convenient. This is the expanded road surface. Workers are laying asphalt, striving to complete the entire road by National Day.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-09-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    京颐路是从首都西直门通往颐和园的一条公路。这条公路扩建以后，将使首都和西郊的交通更加方便。这是扩建后的路面。工人们正在铺沥青油，争取全路在国庆节完工。
