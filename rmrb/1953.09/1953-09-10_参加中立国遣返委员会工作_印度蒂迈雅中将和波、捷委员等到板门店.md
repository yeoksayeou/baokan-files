Title: Lieutenant General Thimayya of India and Polish and Czech Commissioners Arrive at Panmunjom to Participate in the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission

Author:
Date: 1953-09-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, September 9th: Lieutenant General K.S. Thimayya of India, Chairman of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission, arrived in Panmunjom at 2:30 PM on the 9th. Accompanying him was Mr. B.N. Chakravarti, the Vice Chairman.

Representing the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers to welcome them were General Li Sang-cho, Chief Commissioner of the Korean-Chinese side of the Military Armistice Commission, and General Ding Guoyu, Commissioner. General Li Sang-cho introduced to them Mr. Ladislav Simovic, Czechoslovak Ambassador-rank Commissioner of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission, and Mr. Stanisław Gajewski, Polish Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary-rank Commissioner of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission.

Upon arrival, General Thimayya reviewed an Indian honor guard.

In a written statement to the press, General Thimayya said, "We are ready to begin the work of the Commission as soon as possible." He said:

"Our delegation is not yet complete. We have come here in haste to investigate the situation on the ground so that we can better carry out the work of the Commission by September 25th."

Mr. Jan Stenström, the Swedish representative to the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission, and Mr. Armin Daeniker, the Swiss representative, also arrived in Panmunjom on the afternoon of the 9th.

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, September 9th: Colonel Simovic, Czechoslovak Ambassador Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary-rank Commissioner working for the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission, along with eight assistants, and Mr. Gajewski, Polish Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary-rank Commissioner, along with twelve assistants, have arrived in Kaesong by train one after another. They were warmly welcomed by General Li Sang-cho, Chief Commissioner of the Korean-Chinese side of the Military Armistice Commission, General Ding Guoyu and General Park Il-young, high-ranking officers of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers and Polish, Czech staff from the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission.

The remaining Polish and Czechoslovak personnel working in the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission will arrive here in the middle of this month.

### 参加中立国遣返委员会工作  印度蒂迈雅中将和波、捷委员等到板门店

1953-09-10
第4版()
专栏：

    参加中立国遣返委员会工作
    印度蒂迈雅中将和波、捷委员等到板门店
    【新华社开城九日电】担任中立国遣返委员会主席的印度的克·斯·蒂迈雅中将，于九日下午二时三十分到达板门店。随他前来的其他印度人员有副主席布·纳·查克拉瓦蒂先生。
    代表朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军前往欢迎的有军事停战委员会朝中方面首席委员李相朝将军和委员丁国钰将军。李相朝将军向他们介绍了中立国遣返委员会的捷克斯洛伐克全权大使衔委员拉吉斯拉夫·西莫维茨和中立国遣返委员会的波兰全权公使衔委员斯坦尼斯瓦夫·加耶夫斯基。
    蒂迈雅中将在到达时曾检阅了印度仪仗队。
    蒂迈雅中将在向报界发表的书面谈话中说：“我们准备尽速开始进行委员会的工作。”他说：
    “我们的代表团人员尚未到齐。我们匆忙来到这里是为了就地调查情况，以便到九月二十五日能更好地进行委员会的工作。”
    中立国遣返委员会的瑞典代表扬·斯坦斯特鲁先生和瑞士代表阿明·邓尼克先生也于九日下午到达板门店。
    【新华社开城九日电】参加中立国遣返委员会工作的捷克斯洛伐克特命全权大使衔委员西莫维茨上校及助理人员等一行八人，波兰特命全权公使衔委员加耶夫斯基及助理人员等一行十二人，已经先后乘火车到达开城。他们受到军事停战委员会朝中方面首席委员李相朝将军，委员丁国钰将军和朴一英将军，朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军的高级军官，以及中立国监察委员会的波兰和捷克斯洛伐克人员的热烈欢迎。
    波兰和捷克斯洛伐克在中立国遣返委员会担任工作的其余人员，将在本月月中到达这里。
