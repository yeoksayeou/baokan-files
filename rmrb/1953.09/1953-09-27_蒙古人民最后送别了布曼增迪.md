Title: The Mongolian People Give a Final Farewell to Bumtsend
Author:
Date: 1953-09-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar dispatch on the 26th: The Mongolian people are mourning the death of Gonchigiin Bumtsend, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural of the Mongolian People's Republic. Flags are flying at half-mast on government buildings. Memorial services are being held in factories, government offices, schools, state farms, and labor producers' cooperatives.

On September 25th, the Mongolian people gave Bumtsend a final farewell. In the morning, the meeting hall of the Great People's Khural, where Bumtsend's coffin was placed, was opened. Representatives of the Party, government, and social organizations took turns standing guard every five minutes. Then tens of thousands of people accompanied the funeral procession to the cemetery, where a memorial service was held. Damba, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, spoke at the memorial service.

### 蒙古人民最后送别了布曼增迪

1953-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民最后送别了布曼增迪
    【新华社二十六日讯】据塔斯社乌兰巴托讯：蒙古人民都在哀悼蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席冈·布曼增迪的逝世。各政府机关的大厦都下半旗。在各企业、机关、学校、国营农场和劳动者生产组合中都纷纷举行追悼会。
    九月二十五日，蒙古人民最后送别了布曼增迪。早晨，安放布曼增迪灵柩的大人民呼拉尔的会议大厅开放。党、政府和社会团体的代表每五分钟轮换一次守灵。然后好几万人送殡到公墓，在这里举行了追悼会。蒙古人民革命党中央委员会书记达姆巴在追悼会上讲了话。
