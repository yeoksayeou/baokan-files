    Our Great Motherland
    Farmers in the rice-producing areas of Zhejiang Province have overcome the impact of natural disasters such as wind and drought this year, enabling most areas to increase the average yield of mid-season rice by about 10% compared to last year. This is the drying ground of Liuxi Village, Xiaojing Township, Qingyun District, Fuyang County. Women are drying rice.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    浙江水稻产区的农民，今年战胜了风、旱等自然灾害的侵袭，使多数地区的中稻，平均比去年增产一成左右。这是富阳县青云区宵井乡柳溪村的晒谷场。妇女们正在晒稻子。
