Author:

    Our Great Motherland
    Mobile trade groups in the pastoral areas of Xinjiang go deep into the pastoral areas every spring and autumn to carry out purchasing and sales work. This is a large number of daily necessities brought by the mobile trade group. After the herdsmen sell various animal products to the trade group, they buy their daily necessities here.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-09-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新疆牧区的流动贸易小组，每逢春、秋两季深入牧区进行收购和推销工作。这是流动贸易小组带来的大量日用品。牧民们将各种畜产品卖给贸易小组以后，又在这里买进他们的日用品。
