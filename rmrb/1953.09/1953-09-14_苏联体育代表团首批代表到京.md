Title: First Group of Soviet Sports Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-09-14
Page: 1

The first ten representatives of the Soviet sports delegation, invited by the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the Central People's Government to visit China, led by the head of the delegation, G. A. Stepanovich, and the chief instructor, Soviet Honored Athlete N. Sheri, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 13th. Rong Gaotang, Secretary-General of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the Central People's Government, Huang Zhong, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Sports Federation, Dong Shouyi, Member of the International Olympic Committee, Wang Ke, representative of the Cultural Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government, representatives of the North China Sports Commission and the Beijing Sports Commission, members of the "August 1st" Sports Team of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, representatives of the Central Institute of Physical Education, and Wu Chuanyu, representative of the Chinese sports delegation to the 4th World Youth and Students Festival for Peace and Friendship, who had just returned from abroad, went to the airport to welcome them.

The Soviet sports delegation consists of thirty members, most of whom are famous athletes from the Soviet team who attended the 15th International Olympic Games and won championships, such as Soviet Honored Athlete Zhukarin, who won the absolute championship in gymnastics at the 15th International Olympic Games and the 4th World Youth and Students Festival for Peace and Friendship, Soviet champion and Honored Athlete Muratov, Soviet absolute champion Konovalova, and Soviet Honored Athlete and absolute champion of the 4th World Youth and Students Festival for Peace and Friendship, N. Bocharova.

After arriving in China, the Soviet sports delegation will visit Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Shenyang and other places, hold performances, and participate in our National Day celebrations and the 1953 National Athletics, Gymnastics, and Cycling Championships to be held in Beijing on October 2nd. (Attached picture)

### 苏联体育代表团首批代表到京

1953-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联体育代表团首批代表到京
   【本报讯】应中央人民政府体育运动委员会邀请来我国访问的苏联体育代表团首批代表十人，由团长格·爱·斯泰凡诺维奇和总指导、苏联功勋运动员尼·谢里率领，已于十三日下午乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有中央人民政府体育运动委员会秘书长荣高棠、中华全国体育总会副秘书长黄中、国际奥林匹克委员会委员董守义、中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会文化部代表王克，以及华北体育运动委员会和北京市体育运动委员会的代表、中国人民解放军总政治部“八一”体育工作队队员、中央体育学院的代表和刚由国外归来的我国出席第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节体育代表队的代表吴传玉等。
    苏联体育代表团由三十人组成，大多数团员都是曾经出席第十五届国际奥林匹克运动会并荣获冠军的苏联选手队的著名选手，如荣获第十五届国际奥林匹克运动会和第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节机巧运动绝对冠军称号的苏联功勋运动员朱卡林，苏联冠军、功勋运动员穆拉托夫，苏联绝对冠军科诺瓦洛娃，苏联功勋运动员、第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节绝对冠军尼·柏查罗娃等。
    苏联体育代表团来我国后，将访问北京、天津、上海、武汉、广州、沈阳等地，举行表演，并将参加我国国庆节典礼和十月二日在北京举行的我国一九五三年全国田径、体操、自行车运动大会。（附图片）
