Title: A Large Number of Industrial Workers Enter the Workers' and Peasants' Accelerated Secondary School Attached to Tsinghua University to Study
Author:
Date: 1953-09-10
Page: 3

    A Large Number of Industrial Workers Enter the Workers' and Peasants' Accelerated Secondary School Attached to Tsinghua University to Study
    In order to meet the needs of the country's planned economic construction and cultivate a large number of high-level technical personnel from worker and peasant backgrounds, the Workers' and Peasants' Accelerated Secondary School attached to Tsinghua University has expanded its enrollment this year to include seven classes of students, and the enrollment work has been successfully completed. Two hundred and eighty new students will enter the school for study on September 11. Among them are steelworkers, textile workers, and mechanical workers from Shijingshan, Tangshan, Taiyuan, Tianjin and other places; coal miners from the Kailuan, Jingxing, Yangquan, Datong and other mining areas; as well as construction workers from the capital and outstanding cadres from central government agencies.

### 大批产业工人进入清华大学附设的工农速成中学学习

1953-09-10
第3版()
专栏：

    大批产业工人进入清华大学附设的工农速成中学学习
    为了适应国家有计划的经济建设的需要，培养大批工农出身的高级技术人材，清华大学附设的工农速成中学，今年扩大招收了七个班的学生，招生工作已顺利完成。二百八十名新同学将于九月十一日进校学习。其中有来自石景山、唐山、太原、天津等地的炼钢工人、纺织工人、机械工人；有来自开滦、井陉、阳泉、大同等地矿区的煤矿工人；也有首都的建筑工人以及中央各机关的优秀干部。
