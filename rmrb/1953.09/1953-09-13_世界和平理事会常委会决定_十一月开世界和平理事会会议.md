Title: World Peace Council Standing Committee Decides to Hold World Peace Council Meeting in November
Author:
Date: 1953-09-13
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, 11th (TASS dispatch): The Standing Committee of the World Peace Council has decided to hold a World Peace Council meeting in November to discuss the issue of further developing the current movement to strive for a peaceful resolution of all international problems.
    The Standing Committee will submit the issue of possibly convening a World Peace Congress in 1954 for the World Peace Council to consider.

### 世界和平理事会常委会决定  十一月开世界和平理事会会议

1953-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会常委会决定
    十一月开世界和平理事会会议
    【新华社十二日电】塔斯社维也纳十一日讯：世界和平理事会常务委员会决定在十一月举行世界和平理事会会议，讨论进一步开展现正进行的争取和平解决一切国际问题的运动的问题。
    常务委员会将把在一九五四年可能召开世界和平大会的问题，提交世界和平理事会考虑。
