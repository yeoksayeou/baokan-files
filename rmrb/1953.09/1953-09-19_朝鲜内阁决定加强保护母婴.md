Title: Korean Cabinet Decides to Strengthen Protection for Mothers and Infants
Author:
Date: 1953-09-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 18th: The Korean Central News Agency reported on the 17th that the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued instructions on the 14th regarding expanding and strengthening the protection of mothers and young children.

The instructions stipulate: In the fourth quarter of this year, the pediatric departments of central hospitals in each province must be reorganized and strengthened to protect the health of infants; some wartime evacuated nurseries should be relocated to appropriate areas; all nurseries must be prepared for winter before the end of October; and the postpartum hospitalization period for mothers in nurseries will be extended. The instructions also stipulate improvements in the treatment of nursery staff and the establishment of women's health rooms and nurseries in departments with many female staff.

The instructions also stipulate that, starting in 1954, the Ministry of Public Health will provide technical guidance to nurseries and kindergartens, equip them with excellent caregivers, allocate dairy cows and goats, and promptly replenish necessary equipment and materials.

### 朝鲜内阁决定加强保护母婴

1953-09-19
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜内阁决定加强保护母婴
    【新华社平壤十八日电】朝鲜中央通讯社十七日讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁在十四日发布了关于扩大并加强对母亲和幼儿的保护工作的指示。
    指示中规定：在今年第四季度内要整理和加强各道的中央医院小儿科，以保护婴儿的健康；把在战时疏散的一些育儿院迁移到适当的地区；各育儿院要在十月末以前作好过冬准备工作；并将育儿院产妇住院期延长。指示并规定改善育儿院工作人员的待遇；在女工作人员多的部门设立妇女卫生室和托儿所。
    指示并规定：从一九五四年起，保健省将对育儿院和托儿所给以技术指导，配备优秀的保育员，拨给乳牛和乳羊，并及时补充所需的用具和资材。
