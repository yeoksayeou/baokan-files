Title: The Soviet Union and Iran Conclude an Agreement for the Mutual Sale of Goods
Author:
Date: 1953-09-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tehran, September 5th: According to TASS, as a result of negotiations between the Soviet trade representative in Iran and the Iranian Ministry of National Economy in July and August of this year, an agreement was reached on the mutual sale of goods. On September 3rd, the agreement was finalized through an exchange of letters between M.I. Kuznetsov, the Soviet trade representative in Iran, and Pour Homayoun, the Iranian Minister of National Economy. The Soviet and Iranian sides will increase the volume of export goods from the Soviet Union to Iran and from Iran to the Soviet Union, which had already been agreed upon, for the coming year.

The agreement on the mutual sale of goods will greatly increase the trade turnover between the Soviet Union and Iran compared to the past year, and will increase the variety of goods traded between the two countries in regular trade.

### 苏联和伊朗订立互售商品的协议

1953-09-08
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联和伊朗订立互售商品的协议
    【新华社七日讯】据塔斯社德黑兰五日讯：苏联在伊朗的贸易代表和伊朗国民经济部于今年七、八月间商谈的结果，成立一项互售商品的协议，并于九月三日由苏联在伊朗贸易代表麦·伊·库兹涅佐夫和伊朗国民经济大臣普尔·科马云举行换文。苏伊双方将于此后的一年，增加前已同意的从苏联运交伊朗和从伊朗运交苏联的出口商品量。
    互售商品的协议将使苏联和伊朗间的贸易周转比过去的一年大为增加，并且增加苏伊间往常贸易中成交的各种商品。
