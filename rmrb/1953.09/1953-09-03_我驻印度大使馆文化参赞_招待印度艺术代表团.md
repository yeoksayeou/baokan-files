Title: Cultural Counselor of Chinese Embassy in India Hosts Indian Art Delegation
Author:
Date: 1953-09-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 2nd -- Kang Maozhao, Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in India, held a reception at the Chinese Embassy on the evening of August 30 to host the Indian Art Delegation returning from a visit to China. Yuan Zhongxian, the Chinese Ambassador to India, also attended the reception.

Among those invited to the reception were Dr. Saifuddin Kitchlew, Chairman of the All-India Peace Council, Dr. Gyan Chand, convener of the preparatory committee of the All-India India-China Friendship Association, and T.N. Kaul, Joint Secretary of the Indian Government's Ministry of External Affairs. Also invited were many Indian members of parliament, cultural workers, educators, prominent social figures, and diplomatic personnel.

During the reception, members of the Indian Art Delegation vividly described their impressions of their visit to China. The reception took place in a friendly atmosphere.

### 我驻印度大使馆文化参赞  招待印度艺术代表团

1953-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我驻印度大使馆文化参赞
    招待印度艺术代表团
    【新华社新德里二日电】中国驻印度大使馆文化参赞康矛召于八月三十日晚在中国大使馆举行招待会，招待从中国访问归来的印度艺术代表团。中国驻印度大使袁仲贤也出席了招待会。
    应邀参加招待会的有全印和平理事会主席赛福丁·克其鲁博士、全印印中友好协会筹备委员会召集人吉安·昌德博士、印度政府外交部联合秘书特·纳·高尔等。应邀参加招待会的还有许多印度国会议员、文化工作者、教育工作者、著名的社会人士和外交人员等。
    在招待会中印度艺术代表团团员们生动地叙述了他们访问中国的观感，招待会在友好的气氛中进行。
