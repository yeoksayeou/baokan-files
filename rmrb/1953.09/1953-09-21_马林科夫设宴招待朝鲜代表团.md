Title: Malenkov Hosts Banquet for Korean Delegation
Author:
Date: 1953-09-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 20th: TASS Moscow, September 20th: On September 19th, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, G.M. Malenkov, hosted a banquet in the Kremlin for the government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

The Korean side attending the banquet included: Premier of the Cabinet Kim Il-sung, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea Park Jeong-ae, Vice-Premier of the Cabinet Jeong Il-ryong, Minister of Foreign Affairs Nam Il, Chairman of the State Planning Commission Jeong Jun-taek, Minister of Railways Kim Hoe-il, Minister of Culture and Propaganda Heo Jeong-suk, Vice-Minister of Heavy Industry Kim Hui-man, Vice-Minister of Posts and Telecommunications Shin Cheon-taek, and Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the Soviet Union, Rim Hae.

Also attending the banquet was Zhang Wentian, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China.

The Soviet side attending the banquet included: V.M. Molotov, N.S. Khrushchev, N.A. Bulganin, L.M. Kaganovich, A.I. Mikoyan, M.Z. Saburov, M.G. Pervukhin, M.A. Suslov, P.K. Ponomarenko, I.F. Tevosyan, S.N. Kruglov, A.N. Kosygin, V.A. Malyshev, S.M. Tikhomirov, I.A. Benediktov, S.A. Akopov, P.Y. Yudin, V.P. Zotov, M.A. Yasnov, Marshal of the Soviet Union A.M. Vasilevsky, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union A.A. Gromyko, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union S.A. Borisov, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea S.P. Suzdalev, Chairman of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee N.V. Popova, Marshal of Aviation P.F. Zhigarev, Marshal of Artillery M.I. Nedelin, Colonel General M.S. Malinin, Admiral N.E. Basisty, Commercial Representative of the Soviet Union to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea P.I. Saken, Director of TASS N.G. Palgunov, Head of the Capital Construction Department of the Soviet State Planning Committee P.S. Ivanov, Director of the Far East Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs Fedelin, Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs D.A. Zhukov, and Deputy Director of the Oriental Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Trade M.I. Slatkovsky.

The banquet took place in a warm and friendly atmosphere. G.M. Malenkov and Kim Il-sung both delivered speeches.

### 马林科夫设宴招待朝鲜代表团

1953-09-21
第1版()
专栏：

    马林科夫设宴招待朝鲜代表团
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十日讯：九月十九日，苏联部长会议主席格·马·马林科夫在克里姆林宫设宴招待朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团。
    朝鲜方面出席宴会的有：内阁首相金日成，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱，内阁副首相郑一龙，外务相南日，国家计划委员会委员长郑准泽，铁道相金会一，文化宣传相许贞淑，重工业省副相金熙万，递信省副相申天泽，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使林海。
    参加宴会的还有中华人民共和国特命全权大使张闻天。
    苏联方面出席宴会的有：维·米·莫洛托夫，尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫，尼·亚·布尔加宁，拉·莫·卡冈诺维奇，阿·伊·米高扬，马·扎·萨布罗夫，米·格·别尔乌辛，米·安·苏斯洛夫，潘·康·波诺马连科，伊·费·捷沃西安，谢·涅·克鲁格洛夫，阿·尼·柯西金，维·亚·马利歇夫，谢·米·季霍米罗夫，伊·阿·别涅吉克托夫，斯·阿·阿科波夫，帕·亚·尤金，瓦·彼·佐托夫，米·阿·雅斯诺夫，苏联元帅阿·麦·华西列夫斯基，苏联外交部副部长安·葛罗米柯，苏联对外贸易部副部长斯·阿·波里索夫，苏联驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国特命全权大使斯·普·苏兹达列夫，苏联妇女反法西斯委员会主席尼·瓦·波波娃，空军元帅普·佛·日加列夫，炮兵元帅米·伊·尼杰林，陆军上将麦·斯·马利宁，海军上将纳·叶·巴西斯蒂，苏联驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国商务代表普·伊·萨肯，塔斯社社长尼·格·帕尔古诺夫，苏联国家计划委员会基本建设处处长普·斯·伊凡诺夫，苏联外交部远东司司长费德林，苏联外交部交际司司长德·阿·茹科夫，苏联对外贸易部东方司副司长麦·伊·斯拉特科夫斯基。
    宴会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。格·马·马林科夫和金日成都发表了谈话。
