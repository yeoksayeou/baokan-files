Title: World Peace Council Secretary-General Lafferte Congratulates China on Holding Memorial Meeting for Four World Cultural Celebrities; Cuban Peace Committee Chairman and Cuban Peace Warriors Send Congratulatory Telegrams
Author:
Date: 1953-09-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: World Peace Council Secretary-General Lafferte sent a telegram to the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, which reads as follows: Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Beijing:

　　As China holds grand commemorations for the four great cultural figures, we would like to extend our warmest congratulations to the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and please convey our congratulations to all the major national organizations participating in the meeting. We believe that this great national activity will make a significant contribution to the development of cultural exchange and friendship between the people of all countries.

　　　　　　　　　　　　World Peace Council Secretary-General Lafferte
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　September 21, Prague

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: Cuban National Committee for the Defense of Peace Chairman Entralgo sent a telegram to the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, which reads as follows: Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Beijing:

　　We express our sincere gratitude and warm congratulations for the grand memorial meeting held by your committee for our national hero José Martí.

　　　　　　　　　　　　Cuban National Committee for the Defense of Peace Chairman Entralgo
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　September 10, Havana

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: Cuban peace warriors sent a telegram to the Beijing Commemoration of Four World Cultural Celebrities Conference, which reads as follows: Beijing Commemoration of Four World Cultural Celebrities Conference:

　　We have learned that a grand memorial meeting will be held for José Martí, and we express our heartfelt gratitude and wish that the ideals of this great man can be realized.

### 世界和平理事会总书记拉斐德  电贺我国举行的四位世界文化名人纪念会  古巴和平委员会主席及古巴和平战士来电祝贺

1953-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会总书记拉斐德
    电贺我国举行的四位世界文化名人纪念会
    古巴和平委员会主席及古巴和平战士来电祝贺
    【新华社二十七日讯】世界和平理事会总书记拉斐德致中国人民保卫世界和平委员会电，文如下：北京中国人民保卫世界和平委员会：
    当此中国举行盛大的四大文化名人纪念之际，我们谨向中国人民保卫世界和平委员会致以最热烈的祝贺，并请将我们的祝贺转达与会的全国各大组织。我们认为这种全国性的伟大活动，对发展各国人民间的文化交流和友谊，将有重大的贡献。
              世界和平理事会总书记拉斐德
                        九月二十一日于布拉格
    【新华社二十七日讯】古巴全国保卫和平委员会主席恩特拉尔哥致中国人民保卫世界和平委员会电，文如下：北京中国人民保卫世界和平委员会：
    对于贵会为我们的民族英雄何塞·马蒂所举行的盛大纪念会，我们表示真挚的感谢和热烈的祝贺。
    古巴全国保卫和平委员会主席　恩特拉尔哥
                          九月十日于哈瓦那
    【新华社二十七日讯】古巴和平战士致电北京纪念世界四位文化名人大会电，文如下：北京纪念世界四位文化名人大会：
    我们得悉将举行何塞·马蒂的隆重纪念大会，谨表示衷心感谢，并祝这位伟人的理想能够实现。
