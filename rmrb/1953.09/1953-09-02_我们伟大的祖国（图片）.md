    Our Great Motherland
    The sunflower fan is made of leaves from the Livistona chinensis tree, with those produced in Xinhui County, Guangdong Province being the most famous. The sunflower farmers cut the leaves and send them to factories for processing. This is a scene of workers in a processing plant drying the sunflower leaves.
