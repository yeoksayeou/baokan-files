Title: The Soviet Union Appoints Delegation to the Eighth Session of the United Nations General Assembly
Author:
Date: 1953-09-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 16th: TASS reported on the 15th that the Soviet Council of Ministers appointed the Soviet delegation to the Eighth Session of the United Nations General Assembly as follows:
    Head of Delegation: First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union and Permanent Representative of the Soviet Union to the United Nations, Andrei Yanuarevich Vyshinsky.
    Delegation Members: Soviet Ambassador to the United Kingdom, Yakov Alexandrovich Malik; Soviet Ambassador to the United States, Georgy Nikolaevich Zarubin; Deputy Permanent Representative of the Soviet Union to the United Nations, Semyon Tsarapkin; Minister of Culture of the Uzbek Soviet Socialist Republic, A. M. Kuchkarov.
    Alternate Members: K.V. Novikov, V.M. Khvostov, P.D. Morozov, G.P. Arkadyev.
    Delegation Advisors: G.F. Saksin, V.F. Grubiakov, N.P. Yemelyanov.
    The governments of the Byelorussian Soviet Socialist Republic and the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic have also appointed the Byelorussian and Ukrainian delegations to the Eighth Session of the United Nations General Assembly respectively. The head of the Byelorussian delegation is K.V. Kiselev, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of Byelorussia. The head of the Ukrainian delegation is A.M. Baranovsky, Minister of Foreign Affairs of Ukraine.

### 苏联任命出席联合国第八届大会的代表团

1953-09-17
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联任命出席联合国第八届大会的代表团
    【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社十五日讯：苏联部长会议任命出席联合国第八届大会的苏联代表团如下：
    代表团团长：苏联外交部第一副部长、苏联驻联合国常任代表安·扬·维辛斯基。
    代表团团员：苏联驻英国大使雅·亚·马立克，苏联驻美国大使格·纳·查鲁宾，苏联驻联合国常任副代表谢·查拉普金，乌兹别克苏维埃社会主义共和国文化部部长阿·麦·库奇卡罗夫。
    候补团员：基·维·诺维科夫，维·姆·赫沃斯托夫，普·德·莫罗佐夫，格·普·阿尔卡杰夫。
    代表团顾问：格·弗·萨克辛，维·弗·格鲁比亚科夫，恩·普·叶麦利亚诺夫。
    白俄罗斯苏维埃社会主义共和国政府和乌克兰苏维埃社会主义共和国政府，亦已分别任命了白俄罗斯和乌克兰出席联合国第八届大会的代表团。白俄罗斯代表团团长为白俄罗斯部长会议副主席兼外交部长库·维·基谢列夫。乌克兰代表团团长为乌克兰外交部长安·麦·巴拉诺夫斯基。
