Title: UN General Assembly General Committee Rejects Soviet Proposal to Include the Issue of a Political Conference on the Agenda. Vyshinsky Accuses the United States of Attempting to Delay the Convening of the Political Conference
Author:
Date: 1953-09-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New York, September 23rd: TASS, New York, September 23rd: The General Committee of the United Nations General Assembly held a meeting on September 22nd to review the Soviet proposal to include "The Secretary-General's Memorandum on the Implementation of the Third Resolution Adopted by the General Assembly on August 28, 1953" on the agenda of the General Assembly. According to the provisions of the third resolution adopted by the General Assembly, the Secretary-General should forward the proposals on the Korean issue adopted by the Seventh Session of the United Nations General Assembly, together with the records of the General Assembly's discussions, to the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

    A.Y. Vyshinsky, head of the Soviet delegation, spoke at the meeting to explain the Soviet proposal. U.S. representative Lodge expressed his opposition to the Soviet proposal. Representatives of Britain, France, and Belgium supported Lodge's opinion. Polish representative Katz-Suchy supported the Soviet proposal. After representatives of Mexico, Yugoslavia, El Salvador, Canada, Israel, Iraq, Venezuela and the Kuomintang clique spoke, A.Y. Vyshinsky spoke again, saying that rejecting the Soviet delegation's proposal and attempting to delay the Korean political conference was a "dangerous trick." Subsequently, a vote was held, and the Soviet proposal was rejected.

### 联大总务委员会否决苏联关于把政治会议问题列入议程的建议  维辛斯基斥美国企图拖延政治会议的召开

1953-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

    联大总务委员会否决苏联关于把政治会议问题列入议程的建议
    维辛斯基斥美国企图拖延政治会议的召开
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社纽约二十三日讯：联合国大会总务委员会于九月二十二日举行会议，审查苏联所提把“秘书长关于一九五三年八月二十八日大会通过的第三项决议案的执行情况的备忘录”列入大会议程的建议。根据大会通过的第三项决议案的规定，秘书长应将联合国第七届大会通过的关于朝鲜问题的各项提案，连同大会讨论的记录，送交中华人民共和国中央人民政府与朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府。
    苏联代表团团长安·扬·维辛斯基在会上发言，对苏联的建议加以说明。美国代表洛奇表示反对苏联的建议。英国、法国和比利时的代表都支持洛奇的意见。波兰代表卡茨—苏希支持苏联的建议。在墨西哥、南斯拉夫、萨尔瓦多、加拿大、以色列、伊拉克、委内瑞拉等国代表以及国民党集团代表发言以后，安·扬·维辛斯基再次发言说，拒绝苏联代表团的建议，而企图拖延朝鲜政治会议是一种“危险的把戏”。随后举行投票，苏联的建议遭到否决。
