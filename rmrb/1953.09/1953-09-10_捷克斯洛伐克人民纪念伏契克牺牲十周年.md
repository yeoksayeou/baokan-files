Title: Czechoslovak People Commemorate the Tenth Anniversary of Fucik's Sacrifice
Author:
Date: 1953-09-10
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 8th: The Czechoslovak people solemnly commemorated the tenth anniversary of the heroic sacrifice of Czechoslovak patriotic hero Julius Fucik. On the eve of the anniversary, September 7th, memorial meetings were held in Prague, the capital of Czechoslovakia, Bratislava, the capital of Slovakia, and other cities across the country. The President of the Czechoslovak Republic, Antonín Zápotocký, senior government officials, members of the Central Committee of the Communist Party, and leaders of various political parties and people's organizations participating in the National Front, all attended the memorial meeting held in Prague. The Vice-Premier of the Czechoslovak Republic, V. Kopecky, delivered a report at the meeting, talking about the infinite loyalty of this outstanding Communist Party member and fearless anti-fascist fighter to the people and the cause of socialism. Also speaking at the meeting were G.S. Pashchenko of the Soviet Embassy in Czechoslovakia, V. Faila, representative of the Italian National Association of Partisans, and A. Varela, representative of the World Peace Council and Argentine writer. Faila also presented a gold medal, posthumously awarded to Julius Fucik by the Italian National Association of Partisans, to Fucik's wife, Gusta Fucikova, as a sign of respect.

### 捷克斯洛伐克人民纪念伏契克牺牲十周年

1953-09-10
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克人民纪念伏契克牺牲十周年
    【新华社九日讯】据塔斯社布拉格八日讯：捷克斯洛伐克人民隆重纪念捷克斯洛伐克爱国英雄尤利乌斯·伏契克英勇牺牲的十周年。在纪念日的前夕——九月七日，捷克斯洛伐克首都布拉格、斯洛伐克首府布拉迪斯拉发和全国其他城市都举行了纪念会。捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统安·萨波托斯基和政府的高级官员、共产党中央委员会委员，以及参加民族阵线的各政党和人民团体的领导人，都参加了在布拉格举行的纪念会。捷克斯洛伐克共和国副总理瓦·柯别茨基在会上作了报告，谈到了这位优秀的共产党员和无畏的反法西斯战士对于人民和社会主义事业的无限忠诚。在会上发言的还有：苏联驻捷克斯洛伐克大使馆人员格·斯·帕什钦科，意大利全国游击队协会的代表维·法伊拉和世界和平理事会代表、阿根廷作家艾·瓦雷拉。法伊拉并将意大利全国游击队协会追赠给尤·伏契克的一枚金质奖章，交给伏契克的夫人古丝妲·伏契科娃，表示敬意。
