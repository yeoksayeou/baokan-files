Title: Vietnam Holds National Conference of Religious Organizations, Calls on Religious Followers to Unite and Thwart Enemy's Splitting Conspiracy
Author:
Date: 1953-09-13
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency dispatch:
    Vietnam Holds National Conference of Religious Organizations
    Calls on Religious Followers to Unite and Thwart Enemy's Splitting Conspiracy
    According to Xinhua News Agency: Upon the initiative of the Vietnam National United Front, the Vietnam National Conference of Religious Organizations was held in the Viet Bac Interzone, North Vietnam, in early August. 146 representatives from the Catholic, Christian, Buddhist, and Cao Dai churches participated in the conference.

    The conference unanimously passed a resolution to widely and thoroughly publicize the government's and the Viet Lien Front's specific policies on religion, and to strictly implement these policies, in order to strengthen the broad unity of the people within the national united front and thwart the enemy's splitting conspiracy; to thoroughly implement all government policies, especially the policy of mobilizing the peasant masses, in order to advance the war of resistance.

### 越南举行全国宗教团体会议  号召宗教徒团结挫败敌人分裂阴谋

1953-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

    越南举行全国宗教团体会议
    号召宗教徒团结挫败敌人分裂阴谋
    据新华社讯：在越南国民联合战线的倡议下，越南全国宗教团体会议八月初在北越越北联区举行。参加会议的有天主教、基督教、佛教和高台教教会的代表等一百四十六人。
    会议最后一致通过决议，决定广泛和深入地宣传政府与越联战线对宗教的具体政策，并严格执行这个政策，以便加强人民在民族统一战线中的广泛团结，挫败敌人的分裂阴谋；彻底执行政府的一切政策，特别是发动农民群众的政策，以便推进抗战。
