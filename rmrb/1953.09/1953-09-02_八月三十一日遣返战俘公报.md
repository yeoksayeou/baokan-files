Title: August 31st Communiqué on Repatriation of Prisoners of War
Author:
Date: 1953-09-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, August 31st: On August 31st, our side repatriated 150 non-Korean prisoners of war to the other side (all of whom were American), and 250 Korean prisoners of war. The total number of prisoners of war repatriated by our side to the other side was 400.
The other side repatriated 2,401 captured personnel of the Korean People's Army to our side.

### 八月三十一日遣返战俘公报

1953-09-02
第4版()
专栏：

    八月三十一日遣返战俘公报
    【新华社开城三十一日电】三十一日，我方遣送给对方非朝鲜籍战俘一百五十名（全系美国籍），朝鲜籍战俘二百五十名。我方遣送给对方的战俘总数是四百名。
    对方遣送给我方朝鲜人民军被俘人员两千四百零一名。
