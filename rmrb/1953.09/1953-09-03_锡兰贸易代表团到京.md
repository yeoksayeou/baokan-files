Title: Ceylon Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-09-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The Ceylon government trade delegation, consisting of four members led by Mr. Fonseka, arrived in Beijing on the evening of the 2nd via Guangzhou.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the station included: Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government; Ji Chao Ding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Xie Xiaonai, Director of the Liaison Department of the Council; Lu Xuzhang, Director of the Third Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government; Yang Mian, Director of the Protocol Department; Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Li Yingji, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation; and Liu Guanghan, Deputy Manager of the China National Cereals, Oils and Foodstuffs Export Corporation.

### 锡兰贸易代表团到京

1953-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

    锡兰贸易代表团到京
    【新华社二日讯】锡兰政府贸易代表团一行四人，在团长方席卡率领下，于二日晚经由广州到达北京。
    到车站欢迎的有：中央人民政府对外贸易部副部长雷任民，中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎和该会联络部部长谢筱乃，中央人民政府对外贸易部第三局局长卢绪章，交际处处长杨勉，中央人民政府外交部交际处副处长沈平，中国进出口公司副经理李应吉、中国粮谷油脂出口公司副经理刘光汉等。
