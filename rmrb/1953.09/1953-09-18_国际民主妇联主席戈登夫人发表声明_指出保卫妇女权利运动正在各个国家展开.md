Title: International Democratic Women's Federation President, Mrs. Gordon, Issues Statement: Movement to Defend Women's Rights Unfolding in Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1953-09-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 17th: The President of the International Democratic Women's Federation, Eugénie Cotton, issued a statement on the 16th saying: The International Democratic Women's Federation is now launching a large-scale movement to defend women's rights.

She said that the Declaration of Women's Rights, adopted at the World Women's Congress held in Copenhagen in June, has been welcomed everywhere. In many countries, the movement to defend women's rights has already made progress. In order to spread this movement around the world, the International Democratic Women's Federation calls for further strengthening of concerted action, so that the Declaration of Women's Rights can become a powerful weapon for women in their struggle. During the "March 8th" International Women's Day in 1954, all countries will summarize the initial achievements of the movement to defend women's rights, and women all over the world will once again express their will to fight for peace, for the happiness of their children, and to win their just demands as mothers, workers, and citizens. The statement concluded: Let us work together in this common struggle! Unity is the condition for victory.

### 国际民主妇联主席戈登夫人发表声明  指出保卫妇女权利运动正在各个国家展开

1953-09-18
第4版()
专栏：

    国际民主妇联主席戈登夫人发表声明
    指出保卫妇女权利运动正在各个国家展开
    【新华社柏林十七日电】国际民主妇女联合会主席欧仁妮·戈登于十六日发表声明说：国际民
    主妇联现在正在展开一个大规模的保卫妇女权利的运动。
    她说，六月间在哥本哈根举行的世界妇女大会上通过的妇女权利宣言，在到处都受到欢迎。在
    许多国家，保卫妇女权利的运动已经有了进展。为了使这个运动在全世界展开，国际民主妇联要求
    进一步加强一致行动，使妇女权利宣言成为妇女在斗争中的有力武器。在一九五四年的“三八”
    节，各国将要总结开展保卫妇女权利运动的初步成绩，全世界妇女将在明年“三八”节再度表示为
    和平、为她们孩子们的幸福、为赢得她们作为母亲、工人和公民的正义要求而斗争的意志。声明最
    后说：让我们在这个共同的斗争中共同努力吧！团结是取得胜利的条件。
