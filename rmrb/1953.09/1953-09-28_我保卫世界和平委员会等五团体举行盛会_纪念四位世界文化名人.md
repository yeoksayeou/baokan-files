Title: China Peace Committee and Four Other Organizations Hold Grand Meeting to Commemorate Four World Cultural Celebrities
Author:
Date: 1953-09-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: The China Peace Committee, the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the All-China Association of Literary Workers, The All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies, and the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization jointly held a grand meeting at Huairentang Hall on the evening of the 27th to commemorate four world cultural figures: the 2230th anniversary of the death of the Chinese patriotic poet Qu Yuan, the 410th anniversary of the death of the Polish astronomer Nicolaus Copernicus, the 400th anniversary of the death of the French writer François Rabelais, and the 100th anniversary of the birth of the Cuban writer and national independence movement leader José Martí.

On the solemn blue backdrop of the rostrum in Huairentang Hall hung giant portraits of the four world cultural celebrities.

At the beginning of the meeting, upon the invitation of Guo Moruo, the chairman of the meeting, Mao Dun, Zheng Zhenduo, Zhu Kezhen, Hong Shen, Wu Youxun, Liang Xi, Zhao Feng, and others accompanied the Polish representatives Mr. Witold Jabłoński and Mr. Sukrovsky, the French representative Mr. Vercors, and the Cuban representative Mr. Nicolás Guillén, who were invited to attend the commemorative meeting, to the rostrum.

Among those attending the commemorative meeting were: Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the China Peace Committee; Standing Committee members Situ Meitang, Shao Lizi, Luo Longji, and Xiao San; Secretary-General Liu Guanyi; Zhou Yang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles; Standing Committee members Xia Yan and Tian Han; Cao Yu and Shao Quanlin of the All-China Association of Literary Workers; Hou Debang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies; Standing Committee members Ye Qisun, Qian Sanqiang, Hua Luogeng, and Qian Weichang; Ding Xilin and Mao Yisheng, Vice Chairmen of the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization, and more than 1,200 people.

Foreign guests attending the commemorative meeting included: Professor Ikuo Oyama, Chairman of the Japanese Committee for the Defense of Peace and winner of the Stalin International Prize "For Strengthening Peace Between Nations," who was invited to China; representatives from the Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, and the Mongolian People's Republic who were attending the 24th meeting of the Council of the International Broadcasting Organization and the 5th meeting of the Technical Committee; the Science and Culture Delegation of the German Democratic Republic, which was invited to visit China and attend the National Day celebrations; personnel from the Polish and Czechoslovak cultural delegations and the Polish Economic Exhibition Government Delegation; and youth representatives from various countries who were invited to China to attend the National Day celebrations and had attended the 4th World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival.

Also attending the commemorative meeting were Kameoka Togo and Vantourlieri, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, and Committee member Rewi Alley.

Also attending the meeting were diplomatic envoys and embassy staff from various countries stationed in China.

After the opening was announced, Guo Moruo delivered a speech at the meeting entitled "Striving for the Victory of World Peace and the Prosperity of People's Culture" (the full text of the speech will be published separately), narrating the life stories of the four world cultural celebrities and their outstanding contributions to human culture. His speech won warm applause from the audience.

Following him, Mao Dun, the Polish representative Sukrovsky, Zhu Kezhen, the French representative Vercors, Zheng Zhenduo, the Cuban representative Guillén, and Hong Shen delivered speeches, introducing the growth, development, and struggle against the old forces of the four cultural celebrities respectively. Their speeches were praised by the audience.

During the meeting, telegrams from Raphael, Secretary-General of the World Peace Council, and Entralgo, Chairman of the Cuban National Committee for the Defense of Peace, to the China Peace Committee, and telegrams from twelve Cuban peace fighters to the commemorative meeting were read out. ### 我保卫世界和平委员会等五团体举行盛会  纪念四位世界文化名人

1953-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我保卫世界和平委员会等五团体举行盛会
    纪念四位世界文化名人
    【新华社二十七日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中华全国文学艺术界联合会、中华全国文学工作者协会、中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会、中华全国科学技术普及协会五个团体，二十七日晚在怀仁堂联合举行盛大的世界四位文化名人——中国爱国诗人屈原逝世二千二百三十周年、波兰天文学家尼古劳斯·哥白尼逝世四百一十周年、法国作家弗朗索瓦·拉伯雷逝世四百周年、古巴作家和民族独立运动领袖何塞·马蒂诞生一百周年纪念大会。
    在怀仁堂主席台的庄严的蓝色背幕上，挂着四位世界文化名人的巨像。
    大会开始时，经大会主席郭沫若的邀请，茅盾、郑振铎、竺可桢、洪深、吴有训、梁希、赵沨等人陪同应邀参加纪念大会的波兰代表维托尔德·雅布朗斯基先生和苏克罗夫斯基先生、法国代表维尔高尔先生、古巴代表尼古拉斯·纪廉先生走上主席台。
    出席纪念会的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通，常务委员司徒美堂、邵力子、罗隆基、萧三，秘书长刘贯一，中华全国文学艺术界联合会副主席周扬、常务委员夏衍、田汉，中华全国文学工作者协会曹禺、邵荃麟，中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会副主席侯德榜，常务委员叶企孙、钱三强、华罗庚、钱伟长，中华全国科学技术普及协会副主席丁西林、茅以升等一千二百余人。
    参加纪念大会的外宾有：应邀来中国的日本拥护和平委员会主席、“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金得奖人大山郁夫教授，出席国际广播组织理事会第二十四次会议和技术委员会第五次会议的苏联、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、蒙古人民共和国等国代表，应邀来我国访问并参加国庆观礼的德意志民主共和国科学文化考察团，波兰、捷克斯洛伐克两国文化代表团和波兰经济展览会政府代表团的人员，以及应邀来我国参加国庆观礼的出席第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的各国青年代表。
    参加纪念大会的还有亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长龟田东伍、万徒勒里，委员路易·艾黎。
    参加大会的还有各国驻我国的使节及使馆人员。
    宣布开会后，郭沫若在大会上作了题为“争取世界和平的胜利与人民文化的繁荣”的演说（演说全文另发），叙述了四位世界文化名人的生平事迹及他们对人类文化的卓越贡献。他的演说博得了全场热烈的掌声。
    接着发表演说的有：茅盾、波兰代表苏克罗夫斯基、竺可桢、法国代表维尔高尔、郑振铎、古巴代表纪廉、洪深，他们分别介绍了四位文化名人的生长、发展以及和旧势力斗争的经过，他们的演说，得到全场的称赞。
    会议进行中，并宣读了世界和平理事会总书记拉斐德和古巴全国保卫和平委员会主席恩特拉尔哥致中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的电文，及古巴十二名和平战士给纪念大会的来电。（附图片）
