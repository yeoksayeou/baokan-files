Title: The Bureau for Foreign Cultural Relations Held a Lecture Yesterday: Li Shijun Introduces Bulgaria's Achievements in Agricultural Science

Author:

Date: 1953-09-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 8th. The Bureau for Foreign Cultural Relations of the Government Administration Council's Committee of Cultural and Educational Affairs held a lecture at the Beijing International Club on the evening of the 8th to celebrate the ninth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of Bulgaria on September 9th. More than 200 representatives from cultural, educational, and other circles in the capital attended. Petkov, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to China, and embassy staff were also invited to attend.

The lecture was presided over by Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau for Foreign Cultural Relations. In his speech, he expressed warm congratulations on the brilliant achievements made by the Bulgarian people under the selfless assistance of the Soviet Union and the leadership of the Bulgarian Communist Party over the past nine years. Then, Li Shijun, Director of the General Bureau of Agricultural Administration of the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government, gave a lecture. He visited various parts of Bulgaria in June and July this year as the chief representative of the Chinese delegation of the International Plant Protection Observation Group. Based on his personal experience during the visit, he described the thriving and vigorous development of Bulgaria in political, economic, and cultural fields, and introduced in particular detail the remarkable achievements made by Bulgaria in agricultural science. His report was warmly welcomed by the audience.

After the lecture, the Bulgarian film "Alarm Clock" was shown.

### 我对外文化联络事务局昨天举行讲演会  李世俊介绍保加利亚农业科学方面的成就

1953-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我对外文化联络事务局昨天举行讲演会
    李世俊介绍保加利亚农业科学方面的成就
    【新华社八日讯】九月九日是保加利亚人民共和国国庆九周年，我中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局特于八日晚在北京国际俱乐部举行讲演会。到会的有首都文化、教育及其他各界代表二百多人。保加利亚人民共和国驻中国大使彼得科夫及大使馆人员也应邀到会。
    讲演会由对外文化联络事务局局长洪深主持，他在致词中对保加利亚人民九年来在苏联无私的帮助下和保加利亚共产党的领导下所获得的辉煌成就，表示了热烈的祝贺。接着，由中央人民政府农业部农政总局局长李世俊讲演。他在今年六七月间曾以国际植物保护参观团中国首席代表名义访问了保加利亚各地。他根据访问中的亲身见闻，叙述了保加利亚在政治、经济和文化各方面欣欣向荣蓬勃发展的情况，并特别详尽地介绍了保加利亚农业科学方面取得的显著成就。他的报告受到听众的热烈欢迎。
    讲演会后，并放映了保加利亚影片“警钟”。
