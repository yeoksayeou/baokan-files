Title: Polish Economic Exhibition Opens in Beijing; Polish Ambassador to China and Polish Government Delegation Hold Reception
Author:
Date: 1953-09-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 26th: The Polish People's Republic Economic Exhibition solemnly opened at the Beijing Working People's Cultural Palace on the afternoon of the 26th.

Among those attending the opening ceremony were: Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government; Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade; Zhang Bojun, Minister of Communications; Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture; Zhang Xiruo, Minister of Education; Zhang Naiqi, Minister of Food; Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Li Zheren, Lei Renmin, and Xu Xuehan, Vice Ministers of Foreign Trade; and Zhang Youyu and Wu Han, Deputy Mayors of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and more than two hundred others.

Also attending the ceremony were Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Ji Chao Ding, Secretary-General, and leaders of various people's organizations and figures from the cultural and scientific circles of the capital.

Also attending the ceremony were Polish Ambassador to China Kirilo Kruczkowski, Commercial Counselor Goldberg, and embassy staff, as well as members of the Polish People's Republic Economic Exhibition Government Delegation and the Polish People's Republic Cultural Delegation.

Diplomatic envoys and embassy staff from various countries stationed in China, as well as delegates from various countries who recently came to Beijing to attend the meetings of the Board of Directors and Technical Committee of the International Broadcasting Organization, also attended the opening ceremony.

At the meeting, Chairman Nan Hanchen first delivered the opening speech.

Then, Bobir, head of the Government Delegation of the Polish People's Republic Economic Exhibition and Minister of Shipping of Poland, delivered a speech. He pointed out that this exhibition demonstrates the achievements made by the Polish People's Republic in the past nine years, and it also marks the strengthening of the unity and friendship between the people of China and Poland. He expressed his belief that the holding of this exhibition will further consolidate and develop the friendship and economic cooperation between the two peoples.

Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government, delivered a speech on behalf of the Central People's Government. He first congratulated the successful opening of the exhibition, and then pointed out that in the past three years, the close cooperation between China and Poland in politics, economy, culture, and other aspects has been continuously developing, because the relationship between the two countries is a completely new type of international relationship built on the basis of internationalism. The Chinese people are implementing the first five-year plan, and the opening of the Polish People's Republic Economic Exhibition will help the Chinese people learn advanced construction experience.

Sokolsky, head of the Polish People's Republic Cultural Delegation and Minister of Culture and Art of Poland, also delivered a speech at the meeting. He talked about the cultural exchanges between China and Poland, and said: The exhibition of cultural and artistic works in this exhibition will further promote cultural exchanges between the two countries.

After the speeches, Vice Premier Deng Xiaoping cut the ribbon. Then, Head of Delegation Bobir led the guests to visit various parts of the exhibition.
   
Xinhua News Agency, 26th: After the Polish People's Republic Economic Exhibition opened in Beijing on the afternoon of the 26th, Polish Ambassador to China Kirilo Kruczkowski and Bobir, head of the Government Delegation of the Polish People's Republic Economic Exhibition and Minister of Shipping of Poland, jointly held a reception at the Beijing Peace Hotel on the same evening.

Invited to the meeting were Zhang Bojun, Minister of Communications of the Central People's Government, Zhang Naiqi, Minister of Food, Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Xu Xuehan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of Culture, Ji Chao Ding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, as well as heads of other departments of the Central People's Government, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, leaders of various people's organizations, and many figures from the cultural and scientific circles of the capital.

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 波兰经济展览会在京开幕  波兰驻华大使和波兰政府代表团举行招待会

1953-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰经济展览会在京开幕
    波兰驻华大使和波兰政府代表团举行招待会
    【新华社二十六日讯】波兰人民共和国经济展览会，于二十六日下午在北京劳动人民文化宫隆重开幕。
    参加开幕典礼的有：我中央人民政府政务院副总理邓小平，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，交通部部长章伯钧，文化部部长沈雁冰，教育部部长张奚若，粮食部部长章乃器，外交部副部长伍修权，对外贸易部副部长李哲人、雷任民和徐雪寒及北京市人民政府副市长张友渔、吴晗等二百余人。
    参加典礼的还有中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸、秘书长冀朝鼎及各人民团体负责人和首都文化、科学界人士。
    参加典礼的还有波兰驻华大使基里洛克、商务参赞高尔德格若伯及大使馆人员，和波兰人民共和国经济展览会政府代表团及波兰人民共和国文化代表团人员。
    各国驻华外交使节和使馆人员以及最近来北京参加国际广播组织理事会和技术委员会会议的各国代表也参加了开幕典礼。
    会上，首先由南汉宸主席致开幕词。
    接着，由波兰人民共和国经济展览会政府代表团团长、波兰航运部部长波彼尔致词。他指出，这个展览会表现着波兰人民共和国九年来取得的成就，它同时也标志着波中两国人民团结友好的加强。他表示相信这次展览会的举行，将使两国人民间的友谊和经济合作获得进一步的巩固和发展。
    中央人民政府政务院邓小平副总理代表中央人民政府致词。他首先祝贺展览会的胜利开幕，接着指出，三年多来，中波两国在政治、经济、文化等方面的亲密合作有着不断地发展，因为两国间的关系是完全新型的建立在国际主义基础上的国际关系。中国人民正在实行第一个五年计划，波兰人民共和国经济展览会的开幕，将有助于中国人民学习先进的建设经验。
    波兰人民共和国文化代表团团长、波兰文化艺术部部长索科尔斯基也在会上致词，他谈到了波中两国文化交流的情况，并且说：这次展览会中文化、艺术品的展出，将进一步促进两国间的文化交流。
    致词毕，由邓小平副总理剪采。接着，由波彼尔团长引导来宾们到展览会各部分参观。
   【新华社二十六日讯】波兰人民共和国经济展览会于二十六日下午在北京开幕后，波兰驻华大使基里洛克和波兰人民共和国经济展览会政府代表团团长、波兰航运部部长波彼尔于同日晚在北京和平宾馆联名举行招待会。
    应邀到会的有我中央人民政府交通部部长章伯钧、粮食部部长章乃器、外交部副部长伍修权、对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒、文化部副部长丁西林、中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎，以及中央人民政府其他部门首长，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，各人民团体负责人和首都文化科学界人士多人。
    宴会在热烈友好气氛中进行。
