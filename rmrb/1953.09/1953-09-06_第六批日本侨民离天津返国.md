Title: Sixth Group of Japanese Nationals Leaves Tianjin to Return Home

Author:
Date: 1953-09-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, September 2nd: The sixth group of 1,190 Japanese nationals who wished to return to their country left Tanggu New Port on the "Takasago Maru" on the 2nd to return to Japan. The Japanese nationals expressed their deep gratitude for the warm care of the Chinese people. Before their departure, they held a meeting to present a flag to Chairman Mao, the great leader of the Chinese people, as a sign of respect. Many Japanese nationals also wrote letters to thank the Chinese people.

### 第六批日本侨民离天津返国

1953-09-06
第4版()
专栏：

    第六批日本侨民离天津返国
    【新华社天津二日电】第六批愿意回国的日侨一千一百九十人，已于二日乘“高砂丸”离开塘沽新港回日本。日侨对中国人民热情的照顾表示十分感谢。临行前，他们曾举行了大会，向中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席献旗致敬，许多日侨并写信向中国人民致谢。
