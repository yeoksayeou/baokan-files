Title: Korean Government Delegation Passes Through Manchouli, China, to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1953-09-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Manchouli, 4th. The government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Premier Kim Il-sung of the DPRK Cabinet, arrived in Manchouli on the morning of the 4th, accompanied by Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Reception Office, and Lu Xi, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Northeast Administrative Committee. When the delegation's special train passed through Harbin, Qiang Xiaochu, Chairman of the Songjiang Provincial People's Government, Wang Yilun, Deputy Secretary of the CPC Songjiang Provincial Committee, and Lü Qi'en, Mayor of the Harbin Municipal People's Government, went to the station to welcome them. Premier Kim Il-sung and all members of the delegation got off the train and shook hands and talked with the welcomers.
    When the delegation's special train entered Manchouli Station, it was welcomed by Su Lin, Mayor of Manchouli City and Secretary of the CPC Manchouli Municipal Committee, Huang Hongrui, Commander of the Manchouli Garrison, and Zeng Yuanhui, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Government.
    Korean and Chinese national flags fluttered at the station. The band played the national anthems of Korea and China. Premier Kim Il-sung and all members of the delegation reviewed the guard of honor lined up at the station. After shaking hands with the welcoming personnel to bid farewell, the delegation boarded the special train sent by the Soviet government to welcome the delegation and left our country.

### 朝鲜政府代表团已过我国满洲里去苏联

1953-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜政府代表团已过我国满洲里去苏联
    【新华社满洲里四日电】以朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团，由我国中央人民政府外交部办公厅主任王炳南、交际处处长王倬如和东北行政委员会外事处处长陆曦陪同，在四日晨抵达满洲里。代表团专车在路过哈尔滨市时，松江省人民政府主席强晓初、中共松江省委副书记王一伦、哈尔滨市人民政府市长吕其恩等都到车站欢迎。金日成首相和全体团员曾下车与欢迎者握手交谈。
    代表团专车进入满洲里车站时，受到了满洲里市人民政府市长、中共满洲里市委员会书记苏林、满洲里市警备司令员黄鸿瑞和内蒙古自治区人民政府外事处处长曾远辉等人的欢迎。
    车站上飘扬着朝、中两国国旗。乐队奏起朝、中两国国歌。金日成首相和代表团全体团员检阅了排列在车站上的仪仗队。代表团全体与欢迎人员一一握手告别后，即乘苏联政府派来迎接代表团的专车离开我国。
