Title: Chinese Delegation Departs from Beijing for Romania to Attend the First Meeting of the Sino-Romanian Joint Committee on Scientific and Technological Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1953-09-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 5th: In accordance with the provisions of the Sino-Romanian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Agreement, the delegation of the People's Republic of China participating in the first meeting of the Sino-Romanian Joint Committee on Scientific and Technological Cooperation departed from Beijing for Romania on the 5th. Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Wang Lin, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Fuel Industry of the Central People's Government, Lin Qiying, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Textile Industry, and Shao Jingwa, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the China Textile Workers' Union, and many others. Also at the airport to see them off were Anding, Economic Counselor of the Romanian People's Republic Embassy in China, and Meng Tiannu, First Secretary.

The list of the delegation is as follows:
Head of Delegation:
    Li Renjun, Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Sino-Romanian Joint Committee on Scientific and Technological Cooperation, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Fuel Industry of the Central People's Government (already left for abroad on August 24th)
Members:
    Zhang Qinqiu (female), Member of the Chinese Group of the Sino-Romanian Joint Committee on Scientific and Technological Cooperation, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Textile Industry
    Zhang Zike, Member of the Chinese Group of the Sino-Romanian Joint Committee on Scientific and Technological Cooperation, Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in Romania (currently in Romania)
Secretary:
    Liu Fang, Secretary of the Chinese Group of the Sino-Romanian Joint Committee on Scientific and Technological Cooperation, Deputy Director of the Petroleum Administration Bureau of the Ministry of Fuel Industry of the Central People's Government

### 参加中罗科学与技术合作联合委员会第一届会议  我国代表团从北京启程前往罗马尼亚

1953-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    参加中罗科学与技术合作联合委员会第一届会议
    我国代表团从北京启程前往罗马尼亚
    【新华社五日讯】根据中罗科学与技术合作协定的规定，中华人民共和国参加中罗科学与技术合作联合委员会第一届会议代表团已在五日自北京启程前往罗马尼亚。前往机场欢送的有：中央人民政府燃料工业部办公厅主任王林，纺织工业部办公厅副主任林其英，中国纺织工会全国委员会副主席邵井蛙等多人。到机场欢送的还有罗马尼亚人民共和国驻华大使馆经济参赞安定和一等秘书孟天努。
    代表团名单如下：团长：
    李人俊  中罗科学与技术合作联合委员会中国组主席、中央人民政府燃料工业部副部长（已于八月二十四日先期出国）团员：
    张琴秋（女）  中罗科学与技术合作联合委员会中国组委员、中央人民政府纺织工业部副部长
    张子克  中罗科学与技术合作联合委员会中国组委员、中华人民共和国驻罗马尼亚大使馆商务参赞（现在罗马尼亚）秘书：
    刘放  中罗科学与技术合作联合委员会中国组秘书、中央人民政府燃料工业部石油管理总局副局长
