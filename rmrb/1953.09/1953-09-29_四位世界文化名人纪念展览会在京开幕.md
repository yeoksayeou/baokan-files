Title: Four World Cultural Figures Memorial Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-09-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th: The Memorial Exhibition of four world cultural figures, Qu Yuan, Copernicus, Rabelais, and Matisse, opened at the Beijing Library on the 28th. The exhibition displays nearly a hundred huge pictures, magazines, and their original works and translations in various languages. These pictures and books introduce the life stories of Qu Yuan, Copernicus, Rabelais, and Matisse, as well as their outstanding contributions to human culture.

The exhibition is jointly sponsored by five organizations: the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the All-China Association of Literary workers, the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies, and the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization. The day before the opening, Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Vice Chairman Chen Shutong, Standing Committee members Shao Lizi and Luo Longji, Secretary-General Liu Guanyi, and figures from the capital's cultural, artistic, and scientific circles, including Ding Xilin, Huang Songling, Wei Que, Zheng Zhenduo, Wu Yuzhang, Zhu Kezhen, and Qian Weichang, visited the preview one after another.

Mr. Witold Jabłoński and Mr. Wojciech Sukrowski, Polish representatives invited to China to attend the Beijing World Four Cultural Figures Commemoration Conference, Mr. and Mrs. Virgaux, French representatives, and Mr. Kameda Togo, Deputy Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, also visited the preview.

Professor Ikuo Oyama, Chairman of the Japan Peace Protection Committee and winner of the Stalin International Prize "For Strengthening International Peace," and his wife, who were invited to visit China, also attended the preview.

### 四位世界文化名人纪念展览会在京开幕

1953-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    四位世界文化名人纪念展览会在京开幕
    【新华社二十八日讯】世界四位文化名人屈原、哥白尼、拉伯雷、马蒂纪念展览会，二十八日在北京图书馆开幕。展览会陈列了近百幅巨幅图片、杂志和他们的原著和各国文字的译本。这些图片和书刊介绍了屈原、哥白尼、拉伯雷、马蒂的生平事迹以及他们对人类文化的卓越贡献。
    展览会是中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中华全国文学艺术界联合会、中华全国文学工作者协会、中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会和中华全国科学技术普及协会等五个团体联合主办的，开幕的前一天，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，副主席陈叔通，常务委员邵力子、罗隆基，秘书长刘贯一以及首都文化艺术科学界人士丁西林、黄松龄、韦悫、郑振铎、吴玉章、竺可桢、钱伟长等，都先后参观了预展。
    应邀来我国参加北京世界四位文化名人纪念大会的波兰代表维托尔德·雅布朗斯基先生、沃伊切赫·苏克罗夫斯基先生，法国代表维尔高尔先生夫妇，和亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长龟田东伍等也参观了预展。
    参观预展的还有应邀来我国访问的日本拥护和平委员会主席、“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金得奖人大山郁夫教授及其夫人。
