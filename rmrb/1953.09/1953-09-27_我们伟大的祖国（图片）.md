    Our Great Motherland
    The iron smelting workshop of Daye Steel Plant, Huazhong Steel Company, which once continuously set new national records for small blast furnace iron smelting, is launching a production increase and savings competition to strive to over fulfill the production plan as a gift for National Day. This is the scene of iron tapping from a small blast furnace.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    曾经连续创造小型高炉炼铁全国新纪录的华中钢铁公司大冶钢厂的炼铁车间，正展开增产节约竞赛，争取超额完成生产计划，作为国庆节的献礼。这是小型高炉出铁的情形。
