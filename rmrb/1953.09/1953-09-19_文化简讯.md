	Cultural Briefs

	"Signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement" Newsreel to be Shown Nationwide
	The news special "Signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement," produced by the Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio, has been completed and will be shown throughout the country. In the news special "Signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement," we can first see the solemn and respectful image of our side's representatives, led by General Nam Il of the Korean People's Army, signing the agreement inside the Panmunjom signing building. This film also includes the following content: Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army, Marshal Kim Il-sung, and Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, General Peng Dehuai, respectively signing the Korean Armistice Agreement and the Temporary Supplementary Agreement; after the order to cease fire in Korea was issued, the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers, in compliance with the armistice agreement, quickly withdrew from Shangganling and "Heartbreak Ridge," where our side had won major victories and which are now designated as demilitarized zones, and moved to new positions; the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers were warmly welcomed and慰问 by the Korean people on their way out of the demilitarized zone; a grand celebration held by the Korean people in Pyongyang to celebrate the signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement, etc.

	Central Film School Holds National Film Projection Instructor Training Class
	In order to meet the needs of the development of the national film projection network, the Film School of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government is holding a national film projection personnel instructor training class from this semester, with the training period tentatively set at one year. The students participating in this film projection personnel instructor training class are all selected by the film administrative departments of major administrative regions from the original film projection personnel in each province after passing the examination, with each province sending three people to study projection, power generation, and sound amplification respectively. In addition, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the armed forces have also sent personnel to study. After one year of study, these students will become instructors for the training of film projection personnel in each province.

	New China's First Customs School Opens
	New China's first formal customs school has been established in Shanghai and opened on September 14th. The Shanghai Customs School is under the dual leadership of the General Administration of Customs of the Central People's Government and the Ministry of Higher Education. It is a secondary technical school with a study period of two and a half years. In addition to general courses such as politics, Chinese, and political economy, students will mainly study the Customs Law of the People's Republic of China and its related laws and regulations, and learn from the advanced experience of Soviet customs, in order to train junior professional cadres who can practically carry out customs business operations such as supervising import and export cargo, collecting customs duties, investigating and prohibiting smuggling, and foreign trade administrative management, so as to meet the needs of the growing foreign trade and customs work.

### 文化简讯

1953-09-19
第3版()
专栏：文化简讯

    文化简讯
    “朝鲜停战协定签字”新闻影片将在各地放映
    中央新闻纪录电影制片厂摄制的“朝鲜停战协定签字”新闻特辑影片现已完成，将在全国各地放映。在“朝鲜停战协定签字”新闻特辑影片中，我们首先可以看到板门店签字大厦里面，以朝鲜人民军南日大将为首的我方代表在协定上签字的庄严、肃穆的形象。这部影片，还包括有下列内容：朝鲜人民军最高司令官金日成元帅和中国人民志愿军司令员彭德怀将军分别在朝鲜停战协定和临时补充协议上签字；朝鲜停战命令发布后，朝鲜人民军部队和中国人民志愿军部队遵守停战协定，迅速地撤离了我方曾获得重大胜利、现在划为非军事区的上甘岭、“伤心岭”等地，转移到了新的阵地；朝鲜人民军部队和中国人民志愿军部队在撤离非军事区途中，受到了朝鲜人民的热烈欢迎和慰问；朝鲜人民为庆祝朝鲜停战协议签字在平壤市举行的盛大的庆祝会等。
    中央电影学校举办全国电影放映师资训练班
    为适应全国电影放映网发展的需要，中央人民政府文化部电影局电影学校本期起举办全国电影放映人员师资训练班，训练期限暂定为一年。参加这个电影放映人员师资训练班的学员，都是由各大行政区电影行政主管部门从各省原有电影放映人员中经过考试合格后选送来的，每省各选派三人分别学习放映、发电、扩音。此外，中华全国总工会和部队也选派人员来学习。这些学员经过一年的学习后，将成为各省电影放映人员训练的师资。
    新中国第一所海关学校开学
    新中国第一所正规的海关学校已经在上海建立，九月十四日已开学。上海海关学校受中央人民政府海关总署和高等教育部的双重领导，是一所中等技术学校，学习年限为二年半。学生除学习政治、语文、政治经济学等一般课程外，将以中华人民共和国海关法及其有关法令为主，学习苏联海关先进经验，以培养能实际执行监管进出国境货运、征收关税、查禁走私和对外贸易行政管理等海关业务工作的初级专业干部，以适应日益发展的对外贸易事业和海关工作的需要。
