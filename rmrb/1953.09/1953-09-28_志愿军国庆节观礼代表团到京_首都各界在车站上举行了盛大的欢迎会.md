Title: Volunteer Army National Day Observation Delegation Arrives in Beijing; All Circles in the Capital Hold Grand Welcome Rally at the Train Station
Author:
Date: 1953-09-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: The Chinese People's Volunteer Army National Day Observation Delegation, a group of over one hundred people led by General Yang Dezhi, the commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and General Li Zhimin, the director of the Political Department of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, arrived in Beijing by train on the morning of September 27th.

Among those who welcomed them at the station were: Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea; Luo Longji, Standing Committee Member; Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General; Gan Siqi, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission; Xu Liqing, Deputy Minister of the General Cadre Department; Yang Lisan, Minister of the General Logistics Department; Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office; Liu Zhijian, Minister of the Propaganda Department; Chen Yi, Minister of the Ministry of Culture; Shao Lizi, Chen Shaoxian, Shi Liang, Hu Yuzhi, Zhou Shiguan, Sun Xiaocun, Yan Jingyao, Lei Jieqiong, Huang Qixiang, Lei Rongke, Wang Zhixiang, Zhu Xuefan, Hu Yaobang, Li Dequan, Xu Guangping, Wu Yunfu, Li Siguang, Zhu Kezhen, and Sha Qianli, representatives from various democratic parties and people's organizations; Liu Lantao, Secretary of the North China Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Zhang Su, Vice Chairman of the North China Administrative Council; Yang Chengwu, Chief of Staff of the North China Military Region Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Nie Zhen, Chairman of the North China Branch of the Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea; Zhang Xiruo, Chairman of the Beijing Branch of the Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea; Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, as well as over a thousand workers, soldiers, students, and Young Pioneers.

Also present at the station to welcome them were Xu Zhe, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, and embassy staff.

As the train slowly pulled into the station, military music played in unison, and thunderous applause erupted. After all the comrades of the delegation alighted from the train, they received flowers presented by members of the Capital Young Pioneers. A grand welcoming rally was then held at the station. Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea, delivered a welcoming speech at the rally. He first extended a warm welcome and congratulations on behalf of the people of the whole country to the victorious return of the Volunteer Army delegation and extended the highest respect and cordial greetings to all commanders and fighters of the Volunteer Army. After narrating the great victory of the Chinese and Korean people's forces fighting side by side, he said: The signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement is only the first step towards a peaceful resolution of the Korean issue. The enemy is still plotting to sabotage the Korean Armistice Agreement and hinder the smooth convening of the political conference. Therefore, we must heighten our vigilance and continue to deepen the movement to resist America and aid Korea to support the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean people in order to ensure the effective implementation of the Korean Armistice Agreement.

General Yang Dezhi, head of the delegation and Deputy Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, delivered a reply. He said: In the first year of the motherland's first great five-year construction plan, which is being carried out with redoubled efforts, and today, when glorious victory has been achieved in Korea, it is an immense honor and joy to return to the motherland to participate in the National Day celebrations and to greet our relatives and respected leaders! Please allow us to pay our respects on behalf of all the comrades of the Volunteer Army to the great people of the motherland! Pay our respects to the great leader Chairman Mao Zedong! After describing how the Volunteer Army has grown stronger in the past three years and expressing gratitude for the support of the motherland, he said: In the future, with the support and encouragement of the people of the motherland, we, together with the Korean People's Army, have full confidence in defending the victory of the armistice, defending the construction of the motherland, and striving for a further peaceful resolution of the Korean issue and for the defense of peace in the Far East and the world. ### 志愿军国庆节观礼代表团到京  首都各界在车站上举行了盛大的欢迎会

1953-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军国庆节观礼代表团到京
    首都各界在车站上举行了盛大的欢迎会
    【新华社二十七日讯】中国人民志愿军国庆节归国观礼代表团一行一百多人，在团长、中国人民志愿军副司令员杨得志将军和副团长、中国人民志愿军政治部主任李志民将军率领下，于九月二十七日上午乘火车到达北京。
    到车站欢迎的有：中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席陈叔通、常务委员罗隆基、秘书长刘贯一，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任甘泗淇、总干部部副部长徐立清、总后勤部部长杨立三、办公厅主任萧向荣、宣传部部长刘志坚、文化部部长陈沂，各民主党派和各人民团体代表邵力子、陈劭先、史良、胡愈之、周士观、孙晓村、严景耀、雷洁琼、黄琪翔、雷荣珂、王之相、朱学范、胡耀邦、李德全、许广平、伍云甫、李四光、竺可桢、沙千里，中国共产党中央华北局书记刘澜涛，华北行政委员会副主席张苏，中国人民解放军华北军区司令部参谋长杨成武，华北抗美援朝总分会主席聂真，北京市抗美援朝分会主席张奚若，北京市人民政府副市长张友渔，以及工人、部队、学生、少年先锋队员千余人。
    到车站欢迎的还有：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办徐哲及使馆人员。
    当火车徐徐开进车站时，军乐齐奏，掌声雷动。代表团全体同志下车后，接受了首都少年先锋队队员的献花。欢迎者并在车站上举行了盛大的欢迎会。中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席陈叔通在欢迎会上致欢迎词。他首先代表全国人民对志愿军代表团胜利的归来表示热烈的欢迎和祝贺，并向志愿军全体指挥员、战斗员致以最崇高的敬意和亲切的慰问。他在叙述了中朝人民部队并肩作战的伟大胜利后说：朝鲜停战协定的签订，还只是和平解决朝鲜问题的第一步。敌人还在阴谋破坏朝鲜停战协定，阻挠政治会议的顺利召开。因此，我们还要提高警惕，继续深入抗美援朝运动，来支持中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民，以保障朝鲜停战协定的确切实施。
      代表团团长、中国人民志愿军副司令员杨得志将军致答词。他说：在祖国第一个伟大的五年建设计划正在百倍紧张地进行的第一年，在朝鲜已经取得了光荣胜利的今天，回到祖国来参加国庆节观礼，来问候我们的亲人和敬爱的领袖，感到无比的光荣和高兴！请让我们代表志愿军全体同志，向伟大的祖国人民致敬！向伟大领袖毛泽东主席致敬！他在叙述志愿军三年来愈战愈强的情形和感谢祖国的支援后说：今后，在祖国人民支援和鼓舞下，我们和朝鲜人民军在一起，完全有信心保卫停战的胜利，保卫祖国的建设，为进一步和平解决朝鲜问题，为保卫远东和世界的和平而奋斗。（附图片）
    志愿军代表团下车后与欢迎者合影。右第一人为中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通，中间持花者为杨得志将军，左边持花者为李志民将军。　新华社记者牛畏予摄
