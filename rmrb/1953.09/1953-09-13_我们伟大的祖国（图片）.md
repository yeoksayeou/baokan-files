    Our Great Motherland
    Xiangtan City is located on the bank of the Xiang River and is an ancient city with a history of more than 1,200 years. It is not far from the famous Daye Iron Mine and Pingxiang Coal Mine, and it is also a distribution center for goods, so it has the conditions to develop into a new industrial city. This is the situation of the survey team carrying out measurements.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    湘潭市位于湘江之滨，是一座一千二百多年的古城。这里距著名的大冶铁矿和萍乡煤矿不远，又是货物的集散地，所以具备了发展成为一个新型工业城市的条件。这是勘测队正在进行测量的情形。
