Title: Central People's Government Commission for Physical Culture and Sports Holds Banquet to Welcome All Representatives of the Soviet Sports Delegation
Author:
Date: 1953-09-17
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, September 16th: The Central People's Government Commission for Physical Culture and Sports held a banquet in Beijing on the evening of the 16th to welcome all representatives of the Soviet Sports Delegation.
    Those attending the banquet included: Cai Tingkai, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government Commission for Physical Culture and Sports; Li Dequan, Minister of the Central People's Government Ministry of Health; Rong Gaotang, Secretary-General of the Central People's Government Commission for Physical Culture and Sports; Liu Zijiu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Li Zhanwu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Huang Zhong, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Sports Federation; Dong Shouyi, Member of the International Olympic Committee; representatives from units such as the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government People's Revolutionary Military Commission, the North China Sports Commission, the Beijing Municipal Sports Commission, and Wu Chuanyu and others, representatives of the sports delegation of our country attending the 4th World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival, totaling more than eighty people.
    Also invited to the banquet were Li Haiqiu, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, and other embassy staff.
    At the meeting, Vice Chairman Cai Tingkai delivered a welcoming speech, expressing warm welcome and respect to the Soviet Sports Delegation on behalf of Chinese sports workers. He said: The arrival of the Soviet Sports Delegation will not only greatly help the development and improvement of sports in our country, but will also further strengthen the close friendship between athletes and the people of China and the Soviet Union.
    Grāmatiņš [transliteration of Граматин, likely Grammatin], the head of the Soviet Sports Delegation, delivered a reply speech, expressing his heartfelt thanks on behalf of all representatives of the Soviet Sports Delegation.
    During the banquet, the guests frequently toasted to the unbreakable friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union.
    The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere throughout.
    After the meeting, the China Acrobatic Troupe gave a wonderful performance.
    Xinhua News Agency, September 15th: The second and third groups of representatives of the Soviet Sports Delegation, totaling twenty people, who were invited by the Central People's Government Commission for Physical Culture and Sports to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 14th and 15th respectively. The third group of thirteen representatives was led by E. Lagofer [transliteration of Лагофет, likely Lagofer], Deputy Head of the Soviet Sports Delegation.
    Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Huang Zhong, Deputy Secretary-General of the Central People's Government Commission for Physical Culture and Sports and the All-China Sports Federation, representatives of the Cultural Department of the Central People's Government People's Revolutionary Military Commission, as well as representatives from units such as the North China Sports Commission, the Beijing Municipal Sports Commission, and the Central Institute of Physical Education.
    Also at the airport to welcome them was A. Grāmatiņš [transliteration of Граматин, likely Grammatin], the head of the first group of the Soviet Sports Delegation to arrive in our country.

### 中央人民政府体育运动委员会  设宴欢迎苏联体育代表团全体代表

1953-09-17
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府体育运动委员会
    设宴欢迎苏联体育代表团全体代表
    【新华社十六日讯】中央人民政府体育运动委员会于十六日晚在北京设宴欢迎苏联体育代表团全体代表。
    出席宴会的有：中央人民政府体育运动委员会副主任蔡廷锴、中央人民政府卫生部部长李德全、中央人民政府体育运动委员会秘书长荣高棠、中华全国总工会书记处书记刘子久、中苏友好协会总会副总干事李沾吾、中华全国体育总会副秘书长黄中、国际奥林匹克委员会委员董守义和中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部文化部、华北体育运动委员会、北京市体育运动委员会等单位的代表以及我国出席第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的体育代表队的代表吴传玉等八十多人。
    应邀参加宴会的还有苏联驻我国大使馆参赞李海秋和其他使馆人员。
    会上蔡廷锴副主任致欢迎词，他代表中国体育工作者对苏联体育代表团表示热烈的欢迎和敬意。他说：苏联体育代表团的来到，不仅对我国体育运动的开展和提高有很大的帮助，而且将进一步加强中苏两国运动员和两国人民之间的亲密友谊。
    苏联体育代表团团长格拉玛德斯基致答词，他代表苏联体育代表团全体代表表示衷心的感谢。
    席间宾主频频为中苏两国人民牢不可破的友谊而干杯。
    宴会始终在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
    会后由中华杂技团作了精彩的表演。
    【新华社十五日讯】应中央人民政府体育运动委员会邀请来我国访问的苏联体育代表团第二、三批代表共二十人，已在十四、十五两日先后乘飞机到达北京。第三批代表十三人是由苏联体育代表团副团长恩·拉戈费特率领。
    前往机场欢迎的有中央人民政府体育运动委员会、中华全国体育总会副秘书长黄中、中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会文化部代表以及华北体育运动委员会、北京市体育运动委员会和中央体育学院等单位的代表。
    到机场欢迎的还有首批来到我国的苏联体育代表团团长爱·格拉玛德斯基。
