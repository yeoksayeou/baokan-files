Title: Volunteer Army Delegation Arrives in Shenyang for National Day Celebrations; Overseas Chinese Visiting Groups from Korea and Vietnam Arrive in Beijing

Author: 
Date: 1953-09-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, September 24th: The Chinese People's Volunteer Army National Day Observation Delegation, consisting of over 100 members, led by General Yang Dezhi, Deputy Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and General Li Zhimin, Director of the Political Department of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, arrived in Shenyang on September 23rd. The Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, the Northeast Administrative Committee, the Northeast Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Northeast Branch of the Chinese People's Resist-America Aid-Korea Association, the CPC Shenyang Municipal Committee, the Shenyang Municipal People's Government, and the Shenyang Branch of the Resist-America Aid-Korea Association jointly held a banquet at 6 p.m. on the 24th to welcome all the representatives of the delegation.

Zhang Mingyuan, Third Deputy Secretary of the Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Chairman of the Northeast Administrative Committee, and General Yang Dezhi, head of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army National Day Observation Delegation, spoke at the meeting.

The banquet proceeded in a warm atmosphere. An evening party was held afterwards.

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th: The Overseas Chinese Visiting Groups from Korea and Vietnam, participating in this year's National Day celebrations, arrived in Beijing by train on September 25th.

The Korean Overseas Chinese Visiting Group, led by Head Ma Yusheng, consisted of ten people. The Vietnamese Overseas Chinese Visiting Group, led by Head Yan Sheng, consisted of twelve people. Zhuang Xiquan, Deputy Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, Lu Xinyuan and Chen Manyun, Deputy Directors of the General Office, Zhang Shuming, Deputy Director of the Production Relief Department, Wang Jiyuan, Deputy Director of the Culture and Education Propaganda Department, Fei Zhendong, Deputy Secretary-General of the Beijing Overseas Chinese Friendship Association, representatives of the Indonesian Overseas Chinese Football Team, and representatives of overseas Chinese students studying in Beijing, totaling more than 50 people, welcomed them at the station.

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 25th: Ma Zhucai, Head of the Indian Overseas Chinese Visiting Group, and 19 others, Qiu Yijue, Head of the Burmese Overseas Chinese Visiting Group, and the second batch of eight members arrived in Guangzhou on the evening of the 24th. Wu Zhizhi, Director of the Guangdong Provincial Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, Yi Meihou, Director of the Guangzhou Overseas Chinese Friendship Association, and representatives from various organizations and groups in Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City welcomed them at the station. Su Zhijue and Yan Kongchang, Deputy Heads of the Burmese Overseas Chinese Visiting Group, who arrived in Guangzhou on the 22nd, also welcomed them at the station.

### 志愿军国庆节观礼代表团到沈阳  朝鲜、越南华侨回国观光团到京

1953-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军国庆节观礼代表团到沈阳
    朝鲜、越南华侨回国观光团到京
    【新华社沈阳二十四日电】中国人民志愿军国庆节归国观礼代表团一行一百多人，在团长、中国人民志愿军副司令员杨得志将军和副团长、中国人民志愿军政治部主任李志民将军率领下，在九月二十三日到达沈阳。中共中央东北局、东北行政委员会、中国人民解放军东北军区、东北抗美援朝总分会、中国共产党沈阳市委员会、沈阳市人民政府、沈阳市抗美援朝分会，在二十四日下午六时，联合举行宴会，欢宴代表团全体代表。
    中共中央东北局第三副书记、东北行政委员会副主席张明远和中国人民志愿军国庆节归国观礼代表团团长杨得志将军等都在会上讲了话。
    宴会在热烈的气氛中进行。会后举行了晚会。
    【新华社二十五日讯】参加今年国庆观礼的朝鲜华侨回国观光团、越南华侨回国观光团于九月二十五日先后乘火车到达北京。
    朝鲜华侨回国观光团由团长马玉声率领，共十人，越南华侨回国观光团由团长彦胜率领，共十二人。先后到车站欢迎他们的有：中央人民政府华侨事务委员会副主任委员庄希泉，办公厅副主任卢心远、陈曼云，生产救济司副司长张殊明，文教宣传司副司长王纪元，北京归国华侨联谊会副秘书长费振东，印尼华侨回国球队代表及在京归国华侨学生代表等五十余人。
    【新华社广州二十五日电】印度华侨回国观光团团长马铸材等二十人，缅甸华侨回国观光团团长邱诒厥和第二批团员共八人，在二十四日晚到达广州。到车站欢迎的有广东省华侨事务委员会主任委员伍治之，广州市归国华侨联谊会主任委员蚁美厚以及广东省和广州市各机关团体代表多人。二十二日到达广州的缅甸华侨回国观光团副团长苏知觉、严孔昌等也到车站欢迎。
