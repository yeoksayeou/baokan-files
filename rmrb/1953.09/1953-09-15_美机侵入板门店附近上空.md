Title: US Aircraft Intrudes Over Area Near Panmunjom
Author:
Date: 1953-09-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, September 14th: U.S. military aircraft blatantly violated the Armistice Agreement on September 13th, intruding over the area near Panmunjom, the headquarters of the Military Armistice Commission. At the secretaries' meeting between the two sides of the Military Armistice Commission on the 14th, our side's secretary, Colonel Zhu Ran, has requested the U.S. side to pay attention to this incident.

At around 9:05 AM on September 13th, two U.S. military jet aircraft, intruded over Panmunjom, the headquarters of the Military Armistice Commission, from the U.S.-controlled area directly east. Then, they intruded over our side's area west of the Demilitarized Zone, before flying southwest back to the U.S. area. This incident brings the number of U.S. aerial violations of the Armistice Agreement to 44, and the total number of U.S. violations of the Armistice Agreement in the air and on the ground to 59.

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, September 12th: At the secretaries' meeting between the two sides of the Military Armistice Commission on September 12th, our side's secretary, Colonel Zhu Ran, raised the issue of U.S. military aircraft violating the Armistice Agreement twice on September 10th and 11th by intruding over our side's area for reconnaissance activities, requesting the U.S. side to pay attention and take responsibility for preventing them. Including these two incidents, the total number of U.S. aerial and ground violations of the Armistice Agreement is 58.

At around 11:20 AM on the 10th, a U.S. military twin-engine aircraft flew across the Military Demarcation Line from the coastal highway in the south, and circled at low altitude over Mimao-ri, east of Goseong, before flying south. At 12:40 PM on the 11th, two U.S. military jet aircraft intruded over areas such as Runishan and Gunzangsan, south of Kaesong, from the southeast, and flew east back to the U.S. area.

### 美机侵入板门店附近上空

1953-09-15
第4版()
专栏：

    美机侵入板门店附近上空
   【新华社开城十四日电】美方军用飞机在九月十三日公然违反停战协定，侵入军事停战委员会总部所在地板门店附近上空。在军事停战委员会双方秘书长十四日会议上，我方秘书长朱然上校已要求美方注意此次事件。
    九月十三日上午九时零五分左右，美方军用喷气式飞机两架，由正东美方控制地区侵入军事停战委员会总部所在地板门店上空后，又侵入非军事区以西我方地区，然后向西南方飞回美方地区。这次事件的发生，使美方空中违反停战协定事件的次数增至四十四次，美方在空中和地面违反停战协定的事件的总次数增至五十九次。
   【新华社开城十二日电】九月十二日，军事停战委员会双方秘书长会议上，我方秘书长朱然上校提出美方军用飞机在九月十日和十一日两次违反停战协定侵入我方地区上空进行侦察活动的事件，要求美方注意并负责予以制止。连同这两次事件在内，美方全部空中和地面违反停战协定的事件共有五十八次。
    十日上午十一时二十分左右，美方军用双引擎飞机一架，由南方沿海岸公路飞越军事分界线，至高城以东的米茂里低空盘旋一周后向南方飞去。十一日十二时四十分，美方军用喷气式飞机两架，由东南方侵入开城以南的如尼山、军藏山等地上空向东飞回美方地区。
