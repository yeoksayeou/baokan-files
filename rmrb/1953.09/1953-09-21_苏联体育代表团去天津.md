Title: Soviet Sports Delegation Goes to Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1953-09-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 20th. The Soviet Sports Delegation, a group of thirty people led by the head of the delegation, Gramadsky, took a train to Tianjin on the afternoon of the 20th for a visit and performance.
    Those who went to the station to see them off included Rong Gaotang, Secretary-General of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, Huang Zhong, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Sports Federation, and representatives from the Beijing Municipal Sports Commission.

### 苏联体育代表团去天津

1953-09-21
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联体育代表团去天津
    【新华社二十日讯】苏联体育代表团一行三十人由团长格拉玛德斯基率领，在二十日下午乘火车去天津作访问表演。
    到车站欢送的有中央人民政府体育运动委员会秘书长荣高棠、中华全国体育总会副秘书长黄中和北京市体育运动委员会代表等。
