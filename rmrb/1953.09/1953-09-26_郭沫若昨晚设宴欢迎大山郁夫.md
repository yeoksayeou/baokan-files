Title: Guo Moruo hosted a banquet last night to welcome Oyama Ikuo
Author:
Date: 1953-09-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, hosted a banquet in Beijing on the evening of the 25th to welcome Professor Oyama Ikuo, Chairman of the Japan Peace Committee and winner of the "Strengthening International Peace" Stalin International Prize, and his wife.

Attending the banquet were: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman, Liao Chengzhi, Luo Longji, Xu Deheng, Xiao San, Standing Committee Members, Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General, Wu Maosun, Deputy Secretary-General, and figures from cultural and artistic circles including Hong Shen, Ke Zhongping, Zhao Feng, as well as Xu Guangping, Lu Cui, Qi Yun, and others from the All-China Democratic Women's Federation.

Also invited to the banquet were: Kameda Togo and Vantouriere, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, and Kaneko Kenta and Ogura Kinkichi from the World Federation of Trade Unions Asian-Australian Liaison Bureau.

During the banquet, Guo Moruo first stood up to express a warm welcome to the distinguished guests. Subsequently, Professor Oyama Ikuo delivered a reply speech. He expressed his gratitude for the warm hospitality of the China Peace Committee. He said: There is a long history of cultural exchange between the people of China and Japan. Although the imperialists have built a Great Wall between them, the strength of the people of China and Japan will surely break through this Great Wall to promote cultural and economic exchange and peaceful and friendly exchanges between the two peoples.

### 郭沫若昨晚设宴欢迎大山郁夫

1953-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若昨晚设宴欢迎大山郁夫
    【新华社二十五日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若于二十五日晚在北京设宴欢迎日本拥护和平委员会主席、“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金得奖人大山郁夫教授和他的夫人。
    出席宴会的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，副主席陈叔通，常务委员廖承志、罗隆基、许德珩、萧三，秘书长刘贯一、副秘书长吴茂荪等，文化艺术界洪深、柯仲平、赵沨以及中华全国民主妇女联合会许广平、陆璀、戚云等。
    应邀参加宴会的还有：亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长龟田东伍和万徒勒里，世界工会联合会亚澳联络局金子健太、小仓金吉等。
    席间，郭沫若首先起立向贵宾表示热烈的欢迎。接着，大山郁夫教授致答词。他对中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的热忱招待表示感谢。他说：中日两国人民之间的文化交流有悠久的历史。帝国主义者虽然在其间筑起了一道长城，但是中日两国人民的力量必能突破这道长城来促进两国人民之间的文化和经济交流与和平友好的往来。
