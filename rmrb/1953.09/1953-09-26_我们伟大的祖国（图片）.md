Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-09-26
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
  　In the Central-South District, the workers of the Huaxin Cement Plant are launching a 100-day safe operation competition for the kiln in order to welcome the National Day and successfully complete the annual production tasks. The workers are measuring the slurry in the thick slurry pond to ensure that the kiln is not cut off from the slurry.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
  　中南区华新水泥厂职工为了迎接国庆和胜利完成全年生产任务，正展开大窑安全运转一百天的竞赛。工人们正在厚浆池中量浆，保证大窑不致断浆。
