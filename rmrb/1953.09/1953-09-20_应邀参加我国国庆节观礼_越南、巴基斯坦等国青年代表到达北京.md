Title: Youth Representatives from Vietnam, Pakistan, and Other Countries Arrive in Beijing to Attend National Day Observances at Our Invitation
Author:
Date: 1953-09-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 19th—At the invitation of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League and the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, some youth representatives from Vietnam, Pakistan, Nepal, the Union of South Africa, Australia, Syria, Lebanon, Argentina, Bolivia, and Guatemala, who participated in the Fourth World Youth and Students for Peace and Friendship Festival, a total of twenty-eight people, arrived in Beijing by train on the morning of the 19th to attend the National Day observances in our country.

    Liu Daosheng and Qu Tangliang, Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, Wu Xueqian, Director of the International Liaison Department, Ding Cong and Sun Fuling, Deputy Secretaries-General of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, Guan Ruoluan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Students Federation, and more than 20 members of the Chinese Youth Art Troupe who participated in the Fourth World Youth and Students for Peace and Friendship Festival, went to the station to welcome them.

    More than ten youth representatives from Chile, Argentina, Cuba, Iraq, Indonesia, and other countries who were invited to participate in the National Day celebrations and had arrived in Beijing a few days ago also went to the station to welcome them.

### 应邀参加我国国庆节观礼  越南、巴基斯坦等国青年代表到达北京

1953-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加我国国庆节观礼
    越南、巴基斯坦等国青年代表到达北京
    【新华社十九日讯】应中国新民主主义青年团和中华全国民主青年联合会邀请前来我国参加国庆节观礼的第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的越南、巴基斯坦、尼泊尔、南非联邦、澳大利亚、叙利亚、黎巴嫩、阿根廷、玻利维亚、危地马拉等国部分青年代表共二十八人，已于十九日上午乘车到达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有：中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记刘导生、区棠亮，国际联络部部长吴学谦，中华全国民主青年联合会副秘书长丁聪、孙孚凌，中华全国学生联合会副主席关若鸾及参加第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的中国青年艺术团团员二十余人。
    前往车站欢迎的还有：应邀参加我国国庆观礼的已于日前来京的智利、阿根廷、古巴、伊拉克、印尼等国青年代表十余人。
