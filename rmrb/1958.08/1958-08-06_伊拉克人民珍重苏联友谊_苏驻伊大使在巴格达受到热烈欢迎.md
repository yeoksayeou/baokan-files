Title: Iraqi People Cherish Soviet Friendship; Soviet Ambassador to Iraq Warmly Welcomed in Baghdad
Author:
Date: 1958-08-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, August 4th: The new Soviet Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Republic of Iraq, Zaitsev, arrived in Baghdad on August 4th from Moscow.

    As Ambassador Zaitsev's car left the airport, enthusiastic crowds showered him with flowers and chanted slogans of friendship between the people of Iraq and the Soviet Union. The crowds also embraced each other to celebrate the arrival of the Soviet Ambassador.
    Zaitsev will meet with Iraqi Foreign Minister Jamal to arrange the presentation of his credentials.
    Upon arriving in Baghdad, Zaitsev expressed his respect for the Iraqi people and their successful revolution, and said that the Iraqi people have a friend in the Soviet people who are working for peace and friendship between all countries.
    He also said that the mission of the Soviet Embassy in Iraq is to enhance the friendship between the two countries. The Soviet people are deeply concerned about the Arab people's nationalist movement against imperialism and are pleased that the Republic of Iraq is pursuing an independent policy and the principles of the Bandung Conference.

### 伊拉克人民珍重苏联友谊  苏驻伊大使在巴格达受到热烈欢迎

1958-08-06
第4版()
专栏：

    伊拉克人民珍重苏联友谊
    苏驻伊大使在巴格达受到热烈欢迎
    新华社巴格达4日电　苏联新任驻伊拉克共和国特命全权大使扎伊采夫在4日从莫斯科到达巴格达。
    在扎伊采夫大使坐汽车离开飞机场的时候，热情的群众向他投掷鲜花，并且高呼伊拉克和苏联两国人民友好的口号。群众还互相拥抱，为苏联大使到达这件事表示庆贺。
    扎伊采夫即将同伊拉克外交部长贾迈尔会见，以便安排递交国书事宜。
    扎伊采夫在到达巴格达的时候，对伊拉克人民和他们的成功的革命表示敬意，并且说，伊拉克人民有着苏联人民这个为和平与各国之间的友好而工作的朋友。
    他还说，苏联驻伊拉克大使馆的使命是增进两国之间的友谊。苏联人民深切地关怀阿拉伯人民反对帝国主义的民族主义运动，并且因为伊拉克共和国奉行独立的政策和万隆会议原则而感到高兴。
