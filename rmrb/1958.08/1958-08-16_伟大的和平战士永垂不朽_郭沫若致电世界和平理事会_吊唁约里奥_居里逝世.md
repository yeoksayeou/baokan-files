Title: Great Peace Fighter Immortal - Guo Moruo Cables the World Peace Council to Mourn the Death of Joliot-Curie
Author:
Date: 1958-08-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 15th August

　　Great Peace Fighter Immortal
　　Guo Moruo Cables the World Peace Council
　  To Mourn the Death of Joliot-Curie
    Xinhua News Agency, August 15th. Guo Moruo today cabled the World Peace Council to mourn the death of Chairman Joliot-Curie. The cable is as follows:
    World Peace Council:
    I am shocked to learn of the death of Chairman Joliot-Curie, the great peace fighter and outstanding scientist beloved by the people of the world. On behalf of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the Chinese people, I extend my deepest condolences. Chairman Joliot-Curie always stood at the forefront of the times to serve the people, dedicating his glorious life to scientific research and the cause of human progress. The people of all countries thank him. In particular, in the last ten years, his wisdom and energy have been closely linked to the growing world peace movement. In the struggle to maintain world peace and oppose the forces of war, he demonstrated an indomitable spirit. He made the greatest efforts in the movement to ban nuclear weapons and other weapons of mass destruction. He often called on peace-loving people of all countries to unite, take control of their own destiny, and eliminate war forever. He set a shining example for all good people in the world.
    We join with the people of all countries in regretting the loss of such a great peace fighter and outstanding scientist. His death is a major loss to the peace movement and scientific endeavors. However, the great contributions made by Chairman Joliot-Curie throughout his life are immortal. His shining example will forever inspire mankind to move forward, and we must strive to undertake the obligations he left behind. We must learn and carry forward his noble qualities and fighting spirit, raise the bright banner even higher, and achieve new and greater achievements in the noble cause of defending world peace and promoting human happiness.
                    Guo Moruo
              August 15, 1958

### 伟大的和平战士永垂不朽　郭沫若致电世界和平理事会  吊唁约里奥—居里逝世

1958-08-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　伟大的和平战士永垂不朽
　　郭沫若致电世界和平理事会
　  吊唁约里奥—居里逝世
    新华社15日讯　郭沫若今天致电世界和平理事会，吊唁约里奥—居里主席的逝世。电文如下：
    世界和平理事会：
    惊悉世界人民所热爱的伟大和平战士和杰出的科学家约里奥—居里主席逝世，我谨代表中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和中国人民表示深切的吊唁。约里奥—居里主席始终站在时代的最前面为人民服务，把自己光辉一生献给了科学研究和人类的进步事业，世界各国人民都感谢他。特别是最近十年来他的智慧和精力是和日益壮大的世界和平运动紧紧联系在一起的。在维护世界和平和反对战争势力的斗争中，他表现了坚强不屈的精神。在禁止核武器和其他大量毁灭性武器的运动中，他尽了最大的努力。他经常号召爱好和平的世界各国人民团结起来，掌握自己的命运，永远消灭战争。他为世界上一切善良的人们树立了光辉的榜样。
    我们和各国人民一起痛惜失掉了这样一位伟大的和平战士和卓越的科学家。他的逝世是和平运动和科学事业的一项重大损失。但是约里奥—居里主席在他一生中所做出的各项伟大贡献，是永垂不朽的。他的光辉榜样将永远鼓舞着人类前进、他所遗留下来的义务我们一定要努力承担下来。我们要学习和发扬他的崇高品质和斗争精神，更高地举起鲜明的旗帜，使保卫世界和平和增进人类幸福的崇高事业，获得新的更大的成就。
                    郭沫若
              1958年8月15日
