Title: Iraqi Republic Government Refuses to Resume Diplomatic Relations with France
Author:
Date: 1958-08-05
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 3rd: The Middle East News Agency reported today from Baghdad that the Iraqi Republic government has rejected the French government's request to restore diplomatic relations between the two countries. Diplomatic relations between the two countries were severed in 1956 when Britain, France, and Israel invaded Egypt.

### 伊拉克共和国政府拒绝同法国复交

1958-08-05
第2版()
专栏：

    伊拉克共和国政府
    拒绝同法国复交
    据新华社开罗3日电　中东通讯社今天从巴格达发出的消息说，伊拉克共和国政府拒绝了法国政府提出的恢复两国外交关系的要求。两国间的外交关系是在1956年英、法、以色列三国侵略埃及的时候断绝的。
