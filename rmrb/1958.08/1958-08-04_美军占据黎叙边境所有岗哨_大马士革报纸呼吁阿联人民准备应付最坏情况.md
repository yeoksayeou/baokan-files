Title: U.S. Troops Occupy All Posts on Lebanese-Syrian Border; Damascus Newspaper Calls on UAR People to Prepare for the Worst

Author:

Date: 1958-08-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, August 3rd: TASS, Damascus, August 3rd: According to Lebanese sources, on the border line between Lebanon and the Syrian region of the United Arab Republic, the U.S. occupying forces have seized all forward posts and border posts. The Lebanese troops originally guarding these posts have withdrawn inland.

According to a report in today's "Guangming Daily" in Damascus, the people of Tripoli, a major city in Lebanon, held a large-scale demonstration on the 2nd, demanding the immediate withdrawal of U.S. troops and the resignation of the pro-Western President Chamoun.

Xinhua News Agency, August 3rd, Beirut News: More U.S. aggressor troops invaded Lebanon today.

A U.S. military transport ship brought 2,200 U.S. troops to Lebanon today. They have already landed in Beirut. Another U.S. military transport ship brought seventy-two tanks and other equipment.

On August 1st, the invading U.S. forces were reinforced by 1,800 men.

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, August 3rd: Damascus newspapers published editorials condemning the United States for sending more aggressor troops to land in Lebanon, and pointed out that the U.S.'s aggressive intentions are not only aimed at Lebanon, but also at the Republic of Iraq and other Middle Eastern countries.

"Al-Ayyam" called on the people of the United Arab Republic to prepare for the worst possible situation and to unite against imperialist aggression.

According to Damascus Radio's broadcast on the evening of August 2nd, the Vice-Premier of the United Arab Republic, Hourani, the Minister of Interior for the Syrian region, Sarraj, and prominent government figures continued to receive a steady stream of telegrams and petitions from residents of Damascus, Latakia, and other places. They all expressed protests against the US and British occupation of Lebanon and Jordan, condemned the imperialist armed intervention in the internal affairs of Lebanon and Jordan, and expressed their willingness to dedicate themselves to the cause of Arab national liberation.

### 美军占据黎叙边境所有岗哨  大马士革报纸呼吁阿联人民准备应付最坏情况

1958-08-04
第2版()
专栏：

    美军占据黎叙边境所有岗哨
    大马士革报纸呼吁阿联人民准备应付最坏情况
    据新华社3日讯　塔斯社大马士革3日讯：据黎巴嫩消息，在黎巴嫩和阿拉伯联合共和国的叙利亚地区接壤的国境线上，美国占领军占据了所有的前哨岗位和边防岗位。原来守卫这些岗哨的黎巴嫩部队已经撤往内地。
    据大马士革“光明报”今天报道，黎巴嫩大城市的黎波里的人民在2日举行大规模的示威游行，要求美军立即撤退和亲西方总统夏蒙下台。
    新华社3日讯　贝鲁特消息：今天有更多的美国侵略军队侵入黎巴嫩。
    一艘美军运输舰今天把美军两千二百人运到黎巴嫩，他们已经在贝鲁特登陆，另一艘美军运输舰载来了七十二辆坦克和其他装备。
    在1日，侵黎美军已经增援了一千八百人。
    据新华社大马士革3日电　大马士革各报发表社论谴责美国派遣更多的侵略军队在黎巴嫩登陆，并且指出，美国的侵略意图不仅是针对黎巴嫩的，而且是针对伊拉克共和国和其他中东国家的。
    “旷野报”呼吁阿拉伯联合共和国人民准备应付可能发生的最坏的情况，并且团结起来反对帝国主义侵略。
    据大马士革电台2日晚上广播，阿拉伯联合共和国副总理胡拉尼、负责叙利亚地区的内政部长萨拉杰和政府知名人物继续源源不断地收到大马士革、拉塔基亚和其它各地居民的电报和请愿书。他们都表示抗议美英占领黎巴嫩和约旦，谴责帝国主义武装干涉黎巴嫩和约旦的内政，并且表示将献身于阿拉伯民族解放的事业。
