Title: Stepping Up War Conspiracy Planning in the Taiwan Region: US Secretary of the Army Goes to Taiwan to Convene Military Conference
Author:
Date: 1958-08-31
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, August 30th, Taipei Dispatch: U.S. Secretary of the Army Brucker and U.S. Army Pacific Commander White arrived in Taipei today from Seoul and Pearl Harbor respectively to plot war provocations. They will convene a military conference with the U.S. military generals stationed in Taiwan, hold talks with the traitor Chiang Kai-shek, and inspect various military bases and facilities of the U.S. military and Chiang's army stationed in Taiwan.

According to a Washington report, in order to intensify the creation of tension in the Taiwan region, the United States continued to transfer the aircraft carrier "Midway" and the heavy cruiser "Los Angeles" to the Seventh Fleet on the 29th. The "Midway" left Pearl Harbor on the 28th, and the "Los Angeles" left Long Beach, California, on the 27th.

### 加紧在台湾地区策划战争阴谋  美陆军部长赴台召集军事会议

1958-08-31
第3版()
专栏：

　　加紧在台湾地区策划战争阴谋
    美陆军部长赴台召集军事会议
    新华社30日讯　台北消息：美国陆军部长布鲁克和美国太平洋陆军总司令怀特，今天分别从汉城和珍珠港到台北，策划战争挑衅的阴谋。他们将召集侵台美军将领举行军事会议，并且同卖国贼蒋介石举行会谈，以及视察侵台美军和蒋军的各个军事基地和设施。
    另据华盛顿消息，美国为了加紧在台湾地区制造紧张局势，29日继续抽调航空母舰“中途号”和重巡洋舰“洛杉矶号”加入第七舰队。“中途号”已经在28日离开珍珠港，“洛杉矶号”在27日离开加利福尼亚州的长滩。
