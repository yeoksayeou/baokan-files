Title: Britain Formally Requests a Special Meeting of the Security Council
Author:
Date: 1958-08-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New York, 2nd. British Acting Permanent Representative to the United Nations, Beeley, wrote to the President of the Security Council for August, George-Picot (France), on the 1st, formally requesting a special meeting of the Security Council with heads of government participating on August 12 to discuss "certain Middle Eastern issues." The letter suggests that permanent representatives of the Security Council member states, with the assistance of the United Nations Secretary-General, should "consult in advance" to "reach agreement on the items to be discussed by the Council and other relevant procedural matters."

### 英正式要求安理会召开特别会议

1958-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    英正式要求安理会召开特别会议
    新华社2日讯　纽约消息：英国驻联合国代理常驻代表比利1日写信给安全理事会8月份主席乔治—皮科（法国），正式要求在8月12日召开有政府首脑参加的安全理事会特别会议，来讨论“某些中东问题”。这封信建议安全理事会理事国常任代表在联合国秘书长协助下“事先进行磋商”，以便就“这次理事会将要讨论的项目和其他有关的程序问题达成协议”。
