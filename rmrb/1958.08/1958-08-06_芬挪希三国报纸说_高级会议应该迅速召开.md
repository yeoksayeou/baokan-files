Title: Finnish, Norwegian and Swedish Newspapers Say High-Level Meeting Should Be Convened Quickly
Author:
Date: 1958-08-06
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, August 5th Dispatch from Helsinki, TASS, August 4th: The meeting between Khrushchev and Mao Zedong in Beijing and the communiqué of the meeting have become the focus of attention for Finnish newspapers. The meeting between the leaders of the two major powers on pressing international issues is considered an important international event.
	All newspapers published the main content of the Khrushchev-Mao Zedong meeting communiqué with large headlines. "People's News" wrote: "The Soviet Union and China demand that heads of major powers should meet. Britain and the United States have an obligation to withdraw their troops from the Middle East." This newspaper, like other newspapers, pointed out that the two countries have the same views on current international issues and their solutions.
	Xinhua News Agency, August 5th Dispatch from Athens, TASS, August 4th: The Khrushchev-Mao Zedong Beijing meeting communiqué is the focus of attention for Athens newspapers. All newspapers reported this communiqué prominently with large headlines. "Independent News" pointed out that the communiqué aroused great interest from world public opinion, because the leaders of the two major powers demanded in the communiqué that the US and British troops invading the Middle East withdraw immediately, and that a high-level meeting be convened without delay.
	Many commentators believe that the communiqué will promote the rapid convening of a summit meeting.
	Xinhua News Agency, August 5th Dispatch from Oslo, TASS, August 5th: The Norwegian "Freedom Daily" pointed out in its commentary on the Sino-Soviet meeting communiqué that this meeting demonstrated the fraternal solidarity of the two major socialist countries, as well as their determination to prevent war, stop any imperialist aggression, and safeguard the right to self-determination of all peoples.
	The newspaper said: "The entire socialist world now stands on the front line against aggression. The strength and prestige of the socialist world are constantly growing. The communiqué signed in Beijing is proof of this factor of great significance. Today and in the future, the united socialist countries are the most decisive force for ensuring peace and security."

### 芬挪希三国报纸说  高级会议应该迅速召开

1958-08-06
第2版()
专栏：

    芬挪希三国报纸说
    高级会议应该迅速召开
    据新华社5日讯　据塔斯社赫尔辛基4日讯：赫鲁晓夫同毛泽东在北京的会谈和会谈的公报成了芬兰报刊注意的中心。两大国领导人就迫切的国际问题的会谈，被认为是重要的国际事件。
    各报都以大字标题刊登了赫鲁晓夫和毛泽东会谈公报的主要内容。“人民新闻”写道：“苏联和中国要求，大国首脑应该会晤。英国和美国有义务把军队撤出中东。”这家报纸和其他报纸一样，指出在两国对于当前国际问题及其解决办法的观点一致。
    新华社5日讯　塔斯社雅典4日讯：赫鲁晓夫和毛泽东的北京会谈公报是雅典各报注意的中心。各报用大字标题以显著地位报道这个公报。“独立新闻报”指出，公报引起了世界公众极大的兴趣，因为两个大国的领导人在公报中要求侵入中东的美英军队立即撤退，并且刻不容缓地召开最高级会议。
    许多评论员认为，公报将促使大国首脑会议迅速召开。
    新华社5日讯　据塔斯社奥斯陆5日讯：挪威“自由报”在评述中苏会谈公报时指出，这次会谈表明了两个社会主义大国的兄弟般的团结，以及它们防止战争、制止任何帝国主义侵略和维护各国人民自决权的决心。
    报纸说：“整个社会主义世界现在都站在反侵略的前哨。社会主义世界的力量和威信在不断增长。在北京签字的公报正是证明了这一具有巨大意义的因素。今天和将来，团结一致的社会主义国家都是保障和平与安全的最有决定意义的力量。”
