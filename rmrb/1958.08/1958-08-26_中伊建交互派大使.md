Title: China and Iraq Establish Diplomatic Relations and Exchange Ambassadors

Author:

Date: 1958-08-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 25th: The governments of the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Iraq have issued a joint communiqué regarding the two countries' decision to establish diplomatic relations and exchange diplomatic representatives at the ambassadorial level. The full text is as follows:

"The governments of the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Iraq, considering the mutual desires of both countries, have decided to establish diplomatic relations and exchange diplomatic representatives at the ambassadorial level."

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 24th: According to a broadcast by the Republic of Iraq Radio on the evening of August 24th: A spokesperson for the Iraqi Republic's Ministry of Foreign Affairs issued a statement announcing that, in view of the affirmation by both the Republic of Iraq and the People's Republic of China and their common desire to establish friendly and amicable relations between the two countries, it has been decided to establish political relations between the two countries and exchange diplomatic representatives at the ambassadorial level. "Dearest friends, we place our hands in your hands."

### 中伊建交互派大使

1958-08-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　中伊建交互派大使
    新华社25日讯　中华人民共和国政府和伊拉克共和国政府发表关于两国决定建立外交关系并互派大使级外交代表的联合公报，全文如下：
    “中华人民共和国政府和伊拉克共和国政府考虑到两国的相互愿望，已经决定建立外交关系，并且互派大使级的外交代表。”
    新华社开罗24日电　据伊拉克共和国电台24日晚上广播：伊拉克共和国外交部发言人发表声明宣布，鉴于伊拉克共和国和中华人民共和国双方表示了肯定和建立两国之间的友好和友谊关系的共同愿望，已经决定建立两国之间的政治关系和互派大使级外交代表。“最敬爱的朋友们，我们把我们的手放在你们的手里。”
