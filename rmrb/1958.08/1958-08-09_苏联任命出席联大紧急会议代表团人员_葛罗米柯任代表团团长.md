Title: Soviet Union Appoints Delegation to UN Emergency Meeting; Gromyko to Head Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-08-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 8th: TASS reports that the Soviet Council of Ministers has appointed a delegation to attend the United Nations General Assembly Emergency Special Session, which will open in New York. The head of the delegation is Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko.

The members of the delegation are Deputy Foreign Minister Zorin and the Soviet Union's Permanent Representative to the United Nations, Sobolev.

### 苏联任命出席联大紧急会议代表团人员  葛罗米柯任代表团团长

1958-08-09
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联任命出席联大紧急会议代表团人员
    葛罗米柯任代表团团长
    新华社8日讯  据塔斯社莫斯科8日讯：苏联部长会议任命出席将在纽约开幕的联合国大会紧急特别会议代表团，团长是苏联外交部长葛罗米柯。
    代表团的成员有外交部副部长佐林和苏联在联合国的常驻代表索波列夫。
