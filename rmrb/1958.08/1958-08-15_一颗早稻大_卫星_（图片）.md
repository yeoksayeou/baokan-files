Title: A Big Early Rice "Satellite" (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1958-08-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Reporter Yu Chengjian photographed:

　　A Big Early Rice "Satellite"
    Jian Guo First Agricultural Cooperative in Maxihe Township, Macheng County, Hubei Province, has a 1.016 mu high-yield early rice field, creating an astonishing record of 36,956 jin of dry grain per mu.
    1. The early rice in this high-yield field grows so densely that a child standing on it feels like they are on a sofa.
    2. Scientists are inspecting the high-yield field. From left to right: Zhang Ziming, Director of the Central China Agricultural Science Institute; Wang Xingwu, Deputy Director of the Hunan Agricultural Science Institute; Zhang Qishan, Deputy Director of the Jiangxi Agricultural Science Institute.
    3. People visit the high-yield field to harvest early rice with great interest.
    4. The provincial, prefectural, and county-level inspection teams are conducting acceptance inspections.

### 一颗早稻大“卫星”（图片）

1958-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　一颗早稻大“卫星”
    湖北省麻城县麻溪河乡建国第一农业社有块一点零一六亩早稻高产田，创造了亩产干谷三万六千九百五十六斤的惊人纪录。　　新华社记者　于澄建摄
    1、这块高产田里的早稻长的密密层层，孩子站在上面就像在沙发上似的。
    2、科学家正在丰产田里考察。自左至右：华中农业科学研究所所长张子明、湖南农业科学研究所副所长王星五、江西农业科学研究所副所长张岐山。
    3、人们怀着极大的兴趣参观高产田收早稻。
    4、省、专、县三级验收团在验收。
