Title: Another Batch of British Troops Lands in Amman
Author:
Date: 1958-08-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Amman, 7th: Another 500 British aggressor troops landed in the Jordanian capital, Amman, today.

	This battalion was airlifted from the port of Aqaba in southern Jordan. According to an Amman military spokesman, the airlift was to "determine the effective use of the new route from the port of Aqaba to Amman."

	Reports say that a British aircraft carrier arrived at the port of Aqaba this morning.

### 又一批英军在安曼降陆

1958-08-09
第2版()
专栏：

    又一批英军在安曼降陆
    据新华社7日讯  安曼消息：今天又有五百名英国侵略军在约旦首都安曼降陆。
    这一个营是从约旦南部的亚喀巴港空运来的。据安曼的一个军事发言人说，这次空运是为了“确定从亚喀巴港到安曼这条新航线的有效运用”。
    有消息说，今天上午有一艘英国航空母舰到达亚喀巴港。
