Title: Under the Banner of Anti-Revisionist Struggle, Uruguayan Communist Party Actively Prepares for "Seventeenth Congress"
Author:
Date: 1958-08-05
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Montevideo, August 2nd (TASS dispatch) – Preparations for the Uruguayan Communist Party's Seventeenth Congress, scheduled to open on August 15th, are actively underway.

    The Executive Committee of the Uruguayan Communist Party has issued two special communiques, which announced the draft Program Declaration, the Political Program, and the new Party Constitution. The ninth issue of the published magazine "Studies" also contains information related to this Congress.

    Party representative conferences and plenary meetings of party organizations are being held in various provinces and cities to discuss the draft Party Program, the draft Declaration, the draft Political Program, and the new draft Party Constitution, and to elect delegates to attend the Congress.

    Recently, a representative meeting of the capital's party organization was held in Montevideo. Perez, the provincial secretary of the Communist Party, delivered a report on the domestic political situation on the eve of the election and on the struggle of the Communist Party members in the capital to realize the Party's political program. The meeting discussed the international situation and adopted a series of resolutions on current policy issues.

    The preparations for the Congress are being carried out under the slogan of the struggle against modern revisionism.

### 在反修正主义斗争的口号下  乌共积极筹备“十七大”

1958-08-05
第3版()
专栏：

    在反修正主义斗争的口号下
    乌共积极筹备“十七大”
    新华社2日讯　据塔斯社蒙得维的亚2日讯：将在8月15日开幕的乌拉圭共产党第十七次代表大会的筹备工作正在积极进行。
    乌拉圭共产党执行委员会发表了两项特别公报，其中公布了纲领宣言、政治纲领和新党章草案。已出版的“研究”杂志第九期，还刊登了有关这次代表大会的各项资料。
    各省和各市正举行党的代表会议以及党组织的全体会议，讨论党的纲领、宣言草案和政治纲领草案以及新党章草案，并且选举参加代表大会的代表。
    最近在蒙得维的亚举行了首都党组织的代表会议。共产党省委书记佩雷斯在会上作了关于选举前夕的国内政治局势、关于首都共产党员为实现党的政治纲领而斗争的报告。会议讨论了国际局势，并且就目前政策问题通过了一系列决议。
    代表大会的筹备工作是在反对现代修正主义的斗争的口号下进行的。
