Title: The East Wind Will Always Prevail Over the West Wind (Opening Lyrics)
Author: Li Weiqing
Date: 1958-08-07
Page: 8

The East Wind Will Always Prevail Over the West Wind (Opening Lyrics)
　　National Folk Art Performance, Zhejiang Delegation, Li Weiqing

　　The sun rises in the East, a myriad miles red,
　　The East Wind will always prevail over the West Wind.
　　The West Wind, dying, struggles to resist,
　　With armed aggression in the Middle East.
　　It obstructs the summit conference,
　　It initiates tense situations,
　　Unwilling to coexist peacefully,
　　Its threats to peace are heavy with sin.
　　We resolutely oppose aggression,
　　Striving for peace without wavering,
　　Now we warn America and England,
　　You are not allowed to go crazy again.
　　War maniacs should take heed,
　　The power of peace is boundless!
　　China and the Soviet Union hold talks,
　　Khrushchev and Mao Zedong.
　　The joint communique is published,
　　Peace-loving people sing its praises,
　　The leaders of the two countries are of one mind,
　　The people of the two countries share the same view.
　　Protecting peace with hearts of iron,
　　Deeply rooted, unshakable.
　　The great unity of the people of the world,
　　A copper wall and iron bastion, difficult to breach.
　　Peace cannot be waited for,
　　Actively striving is most important.
　　Reasoning first, then force,
　　Warning America and England not to be fierce again.
　　If they play the lute to a cow,
　　The war maniacs will not understand,
　　Then we will give them a complete rout,
　　Their entire army wiped out in the Middle East.
　　If they: Dare to launch a new war,
　　Then we will: Make them vanish from the face of the earth forever!

### 东风永久压西风（开篇唱词）
李伟清
1958-08-07
第8版()
专栏：

    东风永久压西风（开篇唱词）
    全国曲艺会演浙江代表团　李伟清
    日出东方万里红，
    东风永久压西风。
    西风垂死想挣扎，
    武装侵略在中东。
    首脑会议它阻挠，
    紧张局势它发动，
    不愿和平来共处，
    威胁和平罪孽重。
    我们坚决反侵略，
    争取和平不放松，
    如今警告美英国，
    不准你们再发疯。
    战争狂人要注意，
    和平力量大无穷！
    中苏两国来会谈，
    赫鲁晓夫毛泽东。
    会谈公报一发表，
    和平人民齐歌颂，
    两国领袖一条心，
    两国人民意见同。
    保卫和平心如铁，
    树大根深摇不动。
    世界人民大团结，
    铜墙铁壁难打通。
    和平不能去等待，
    积极争取最着重。
    先讲理来后用兵，
    警告美英勿再凶。
    倘然对牛弹洋琴，
    战争狂人听不懂，
    叫它来个满盆倒，
    全军复没在中东。
    如果它：胆敢发动新战争，
    就叫它：地球上面永无踪！
