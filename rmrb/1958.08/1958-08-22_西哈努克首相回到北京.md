Title: Premier Sihanouk Returns to Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1958-08-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 21st: Cambodian Prime Minister Prince Norodom Sihanouk and the Cambodian Royal National Delegation, led by him, returned to Beijing by special train this afternoon, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, after visiting the Northeast and Tianjin. Vice Premier He Long and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo were at the station to welcome them. Some members of the Cambodian Royal National Delegation in Beijing and the head of the Cambodian Economic Delegation to China also greeted them at the station.

### 西哈努克首相回到北京

1958-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　西哈努克首相回到北京
    新华社21日讯  柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和由他率领的柬埔寨王国国家代表团，在访问了东北和天津等地后，由周恩来总理、陈毅副总理兼外交部长陪同，在今天下午乘专车回到北京。到车站欢迎的有贺龙副总理，外交部副部长罗贵波等。柬埔寨王国国家代表团在京的部分团员和柬埔寨驻中国经济代表团的负责人，也到车站迎接。
