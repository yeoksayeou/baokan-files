Title: My Sledgehammer
Author: Li Renzheng
Date: 1958-08-09
Page: 8

    My Sledgehammer
    Li Renzheng, Chengdu Railway Bureau Materials General Factory
    Fearing neither heaven nor earth,
    My sledgehammer has great strength.
    If the American and British robbers don't withdraw their troops,
    I'll strike them on the head!
