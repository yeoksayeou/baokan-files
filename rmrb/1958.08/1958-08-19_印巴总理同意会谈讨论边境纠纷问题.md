Title: Indian and Pakistani Prime Ministers Agree to Talks to Discuss Border Dispute Issues
Author:
Date: 1958-08-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 18th: Indian Prime Minister Nehru, speaking this morning at the opening of the Indian Council of States, stated that he is prepared to hold talks with Pakistani Prime Minister Noon to peacefully resolve the Indo-Pakistani border dispute. Prime Minister Nehru said that he received a letter from Noon on August 16th, in which Noon agreed to Nehru’s suggestion made last week in the People’s Assembly regarding holding talks.

Delhi reporters believe that the unanimous agreement between the Indian and Pakistani Prime Ministers to hold talks is helpful in resolving the border dispute.

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, August 18th: The Pakistani government announced on the 17th that Pakistani Prime Minister Noon has expressed his agreement to hold talks with Indian Prime Minister Nehru to discuss the India-East Pakistan border incidents.

### 印巴总理同意会谈讨论边境纠纷问题

1958-08-19
第5版()
专栏：

  　印巴总理同意会谈讨论边境纠纷问题
    据新华社新德里18日电　印度总理尼赫鲁今天上午在这里开幕的印度联邦院会议上发言时表示，他已经准备好同巴基斯坦总理努恩为和平解决印巴边境纠纷问题举行会谈。尼赫鲁总理说，他在8月16日接到努恩的来信，信中表示同意他上星期在人民院提出的关于举行会谈的建议。
    德里报界人士认为，印巴总理一致同意举行会谈，对解决边境纠纷问题是有帮助的。
    据新华社卡拉奇18日电　巴基斯坦政府在17日宣布，巴基斯坦总理努恩已经表示同意和印度总理尼赫鲁举行会谈，来讨论印度—东巴基斯坦的边境事件。
