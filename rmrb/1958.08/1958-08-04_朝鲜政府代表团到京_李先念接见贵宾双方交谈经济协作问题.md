Title: Korean Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing Li Xiannian Receives Distinguished Guests and Discusses Economic Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1958-08-04
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd August. A government delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by Vice Premier Li Joo-yeon of the Cabinet of Korea, arrived in Beijing by train at 11:35 this morning.
    The delegation members include Li Jong-ok, Chairman of the State Planning Commission, Kim Choi-seon, Deputy Minister of Internal and External Trade, and Li Yong-ho, the Korean Ambassador to China.
    The delegation will hold talks with the Chinese government on further developing economic cooperation between the two countries and will also visit various parts of our country.
    Welcoming the distinguished guests at the station today were Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Vice Minister Lin Haiyun, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission Liu Mingfu, Deputy Director of the State Technology Commission Zhang Youxuan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, Chinese Ambassador to Korea Qiao Xiaoguang, and Vice Mayor of Beijing Cheng Hongyi.
    Officials from the Korean Embassy in China also welcomed them at the station.
    Young women from the capital presented the distinguished guests with flowers.
    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd August. Vice Premier Li Xiannian received Li Joo-yeon, head of the government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and Vice Premier of the Korean Cabinet, this afternoon, along with delegation members: Li Jong-ok, Chairman of the State Planning Commission, Kim Choi-seon, Deputy Minister of Internal and External Trade, and Li Yong-ho, the Korean Ambassador to China.
    Also present at the reception were Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission Liu Mingfu, Deputy Director of the State Technology Commission Zhang Youxuan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Lin Haiyun, and our Ambassador to Korea Qiao Xiaoguang, among others.
    During the reception, the guests and hosts had a cordial conversation on further strengthening economic cooperation between China and Korea. (Attached picture)
    The Korean government delegation arrived in Beijing by train on August 3rd. ### 朝鲜政府代表团到京  李先念接见贵宾双方交谈经济协作问题

1958-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜政府代表团到京
    李先念接见贵宾双方交谈经济协作问题
    新华社3日讯　由朝鲜内阁副首相李周渊率领的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团，今天上午十一时三十五分乘火车到达北京。
    代表团团员有国家计划委员会委员长李锺玉、对内外商业省副相金最善和朝鲜驻中国大使李永镐。
    代表团将同中国政府就进一步发展两国的经济协作问题举行会谈，并且将到我国各地参观访问。
    今天到车站欢迎贵宾的，有国务院副总理李先念，对外贸易部部长叶季壮、副部长林海云，国家计划委员会副主任刘明夫，国家技术委员会副主任张有萱，外交部副部长曾涌泉，中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光，北京市副市长程宏毅。
    朝鲜驻中国大使馆官员也到车站欢迎。
    首都的女青年向贵宾们献了鲜花。
    新华社3日讯　李先念副总理今天下午接见了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团团长、朝鲜内阁副首相李周渊，团员：国家计划委员会委员长李锺玉、对内外商业省副相金最善和朝鲜驻中国大使李永镐。
    接见时在座的有，对外贸易部部长叶季壮、外交部副部长曾涌泉、国家计划委员会副主任刘明夫、国家技术委员会副主任张有萱、对外贸易部副部长林海云、我国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光等。
    接见时，宾主就进一步加强中、朝两国经济协作问题进行了亲切交谈。（附图片）
    朝鲜政府代表团8月3日乘火车到达北京。图为前来欢迎的李先念副总理（左第三人）、对外贸易部叶季壮部长（左第四人）和朝鲜副首相李周渊（左第二人）、朝鲜国家计划委员会委员长李锺玉（左第一人）等在车站上。　新华社记者刘东鳌摄
