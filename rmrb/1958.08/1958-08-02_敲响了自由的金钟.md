Title: Tolling the Golden Bell of Freedom
Author: Zang Kejia
Date: 1958-08-02
Page: 8

    Tolling the Golden Bell of Freedom
    Zang Kejia
    In the dark night,
    Tolling the golden bell of freedom,
    A new Iraq, a new Iraq,
    Rises with the morning sun.
    The people stretch out their iron fists,
    Faisal is struck dead,
    It's not just him who is struck dead,
    What's struck dead is the entire reactionary dynasty.
    The people use the raging fire of hatred,
    To burn the corpse of Said,
    What's burned is not just a corpse,
    But also the imperialist schemes of aggression burned to a crisp.
    The Baghdad Pact office is sealed,
    And diplomatic relations are restored with the great Soviet Union,
    The new Iraq,
    A good friend of China,
    Six hundred million people cheer and applaud your birth!
    A new Iraq,
    A new Iraq,
    Rises with the morning sun,
    The dark night has passed,
    The Iraqi people toll the golden bell of freedom.

### 敲响了自由的金钟
臧克家
1958-08-02
第8版()
专栏：

    敲响了自由的金钟
    臧克家
    在黑沉沉的夜里，
    敲响了自由的金钟，
    一个新的伊拉克，新的伊拉克，
    和早晨的太阳同时上升。
    人民伸出了铁拳，
    费萨尔被打死了，
    打死的不是他一个人，
    打死的是整个反动王朝。
    人民用仇恨的怒火，
    把赛义德的尸体焚烧，
    焚烧的不只是一具尸体，
    连帝国主义的侵略阴谋一齐烧焦。
    巴格达条约办事处贴上封条，
    和伟大的苏联恢复了邦交，
    新的伊拉克，
    中国的好朋友，
    六万万人为你的诞生欢呼，叫好！
    一个新的伊拉克，
    新的伊拉克，
    和早晨的太阳同时上升，
    沉沉的黑夜已经过去，
    伊拉克人民敲响了自由的金钟。
