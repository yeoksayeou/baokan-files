Title: Freedom and Democratic Rights Shall Not Be Infringed: Vietnam New Association Condemns Atrocities of British Authorities in Hong Kong
Author:
Date: 1958-08-31
Page: 2

　　Freedom and Democratic Rights Shall Not Be Infringed
    Vietnam New Association Condemns Atrocities of British Authorities in Hong Kong
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 30th. The Vietnamese newspaper "People's Daily" today published on its first page a telegram sent by the Vietnam Journalists Association on August 29th to the All-China Journalists Association. Vietnamese journalists expressed their strong condemnation in the telegram of the atrocities committed by the British authorities in Hong Kong against Chinese students, teachers, and journalists, and demanded that the British authorities immediately cease their acts of infringing upon the freedom and democratic rights of the Chinese people in Hong Kong.
    The telegram expressed full support for the serious protest lodged by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China with the British government regarding this atrocity by the British authorities. The telegram also expressed full support for the appeal made by the All-China Journalists Association to the International Organization of Journalists and all upright journalists around the world to oppose the persecution of Chinese journalists by the British authorities in Hong Kong.

### 自由民主权利不容侵犯  越南新协谴责香港英国当局暴行

1958-08-31
第2版()
专栏：

　　自由民主权利不容侵犯
    越南新协谴责香港英国当局暴行
    新华社河内30日电　越南“人民报”今天在第一版刊登了越南新闻工作者协会8月29日打给中华全国新闻工作者协会的一封电报。越南新闻工作者在电报中表示强烈谴责香港英国当局迫害中国学生、教师和新闻记者的暴行，并且要求英国当局立即停止侵犯在香港的中国人民的自由民主权利的行为。
    电报表示完全支持中华人民共和国政府外交部就英国当局这一暴行向英国政府提出的严重抗议。电报还表示完全支持中国新闻工作者协会为反对香港英国当局迫害中国记者而向国际新闻工作者协会和全世界正直新闻工作者发出的呼吁。
