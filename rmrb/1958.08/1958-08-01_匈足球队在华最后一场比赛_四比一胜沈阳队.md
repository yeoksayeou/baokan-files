Title: Hungarian Football Team's Last Game in China: 4-1 Victory over Shenyang Team
Author:
Date: 1958-08-01
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, July 30th. In their last friendly match in China, the Hungarian "Pénzügyőr" football team defeated the Shenyang football team today in Shenyang with a score of 4-1.

　　The match was held at the Shenyang People's Stadium. The stands, which can accommodate more than 14,000 spectators, were packed today.

　　Since July 18th, the Hungarian "Pénzügyőr" football team had previously drawn 2-2 with the "August 1st" team in Beijing; and lost 1-5 to the Beijing team. On the 27th, they drew 1-1 with the Lüda football team in Dalian.

### 匈足球队在华最后一场比赛  四比一胜沈阳队

1958-08-01
第7版()
专栏：体育运动

    匈足球队在华最后一场比赛
    四比一胜沈阳队
    据新华社沈阳30日电　匈牙利“潘久捷尔”足球队今天在沈阳举行访问我国的最后一场友谊比赛中，以四比一战胜沈阳市足球队。
    比赛是在沈阳人民体育场举行，在能容一万四千多名观众的看台上今天座无虚席。
    匈牙利“潘久捷尔”足球队从7月18日以来，曾在北京同八一队二比二打成平局；以一比五败给北京队。27日，在大连同旅大足球队的比赛中，以一比一踢成平局。
