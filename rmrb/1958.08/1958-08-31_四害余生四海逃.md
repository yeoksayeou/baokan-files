Title: The Remaining Evils Flee to the Four Seas
Author: Guo Moruo, Hua Junwu
Date: 1958-08-31
Page: 4

　　The Remaining Evils Flee to the Four Seas
　　Poem by Guo Moruo, Painting by Hua Junwu

　　1. Flies Flee to England

　　Fleeing far from China,
　　Is truly most pleasant.
　　Here we'll build a kingdom,
　　Passed down to sons and grandsons for generations.
　　Although in Chinese we're called "flies,"
　　In English, we're called "Fly". (Note)
　　It shows that English gentlemen,
　　Express loyal support.
　　England harmed China,
　　Owes the old debt of opium.
　　Flies help cholera,
　　And have been a great evil.
　　The Chinese people hate us,
　　Which is only right.
　　English gentlemen welcome us,
　　This is love between our kind.
　　(Note) Transliteration of "Fly"

　　2. Sparrows Flee to America

　　We are truly free,
　　Arrived in North America.
　　Mike, Ike, Jack, are all friends.
　　If we want to eat, there is grain to eat,
　　If we want to play, we can have a garden party.
　　Eating until we have big bellies,
　　Strolling on Wall Street.
　　People say super profits,
　　Are tainted with the stench of blood.
　　He who provides milk is my mother,
　　What does the stench of blood mean to me?
　　Thirty-nine billion in U.S. aid,
　　Some governments receive it.
　　Compared to a certain president,
　　Sparrows are not considered base.

　　3. Mosquitoes Flee to Japan

　　Mosquitoes may be small,
　　But our skills are not small.
　　No matter how heroic you are,
　　We can make you sick.
　　Malaria, encephalitis, yellow fever, thousands of villages are depopulated.
　　We are plague gods,
　　Banding together and causing chaos.
　　We are not allowed to stay in China anymore,
　　Now we flee to the Three Islands.
　　The Kishida government of the Three Islands,
　　Does not engage with China.
　　Suppresses the Japanese people,
　　And relies solely on American imperialism.
　　We share the same spirit,
　　We must suck our fill of blood.

　　4. Rats Flee to West Germany

　　Rats are also known as honored guests,
　　Accustomed to acting wildly on the beams.
　　Spreading plague bacteria,
　　As fierce as war.
　　Both World Wars,
　　Were started by Germany.
　　Now West Germany,
　　Still inherits the mantle.
　　Humans have war merchants,
　　Rats are the great gods of plague.
　　Naturally, we resonate with each other,
　　And don't hesitate to travel far to join them.
　　If West Germany doesn't accept us,
　　American imperialism will surely welcome us.
　　Heroes recognize heroes,
　　Like cherishes like. (Attached image)

### 四害余生四海逃
郭沫若  华君武
1958-08-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　四害余生四海逃
　　郭沫若诗　华君武画
　　1、苍蝇逃向英国
　　远从中国逃来，
　　真是十分愉快。
　　在此建立王国，
　　传之子孙万代。
　　中文虽叫苍蝇，
　　英文是叫“福来”。
    　　      （注）
　　可见英国绅士，
　　表示忠诚拥戴。
　　英国害过中国，
　　欠下鸦片旧债。
　　苍蝇帮助霍乱，
　　也曾为过大害。
　　中国人民憎恨，
　　那是理所应该。
　　英国绅士欢迎，
　　这是同类相爱。
　　（注）Fly的音译
　　2、麻雀逃向美国
　　我们真是自由，
　　到了北部美洲。
　　麦克、艾克、杰克，个个都是朋友。
　　要吃就有粮吃，
　　要游就可园游。
　　吃成便便大腹，
　　散步华尔街头。
　　人说超额利润，
　　带着血的腥臭。
　　有奶便是亲娘，
　　血腥于我何有？
　　三十九亿美援，
　　有些政府领受。
　　比起某位总统，
　　麻雀不算下流。
　　3、蚊子逃向日本
　　蚊子虽然微藐，
　　本领却是不小。
　　任你英雄好汉，
　　都可把他病倒。
　　疟疾、脑炎、黄热，千村万落人少。
　　我们就是瘟神，
　　结队成群乱搞。
　　中国不准再呆，
　　如今逃往三岛。
　　三岛岸姓政府，
　　不打中国交道。
　　镇压日本人民，
　　专向美帝投靠。
　　咱们同气相求，
　　吸血总要吸饱。
　　4、老鼠逃向西德
　　老鼠别号高客，
　　惯在梁上横行。
　　传播鼠疫细菌，
　　猛烈不亚战争。
　　两次世界大战，
　　都是德国兴兵。
　　如今西部德国，
　　衣钵犹然继承。
　　人有战争贩子，
　　鼠是瘟疫大神。
　　自然同声相应，
　　不辞远道投奔。
　　西德如果不收，
　　美帝一定欢迎。
　　英雄能识英雄，
　　惺惺惯惜惺惺。（附图片）
