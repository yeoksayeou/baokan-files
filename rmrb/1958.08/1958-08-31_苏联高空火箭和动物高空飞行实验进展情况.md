Title: Progress of Soviet High-Altitude Rocket and Animal High-Altitude Flight Experiments
Author:
Date: 1958-08-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 30th: Soviet authorities, in a discussion about the Soviet Union launching a geophysical rocket to an altitude of 450 kilometers, spoke about the progress of Soviet high-altitude rocket and animal high-altitude flight experiments.

He pointed out that the work of using rockets to investigate the upper atmosphere has been carried out in the Soviet Union for many years.

	In 1933, the Soviet Union launched its first liquid-fueled engine rocket to conduct this type of investigation.

	In May 1949, the first vertical rocket launch reached an altitude of 110 kilometers.

	In May 1957, a rocket carrying experimental instruments weighing a total of 2,200 kilograms ascended to an altitude of 212 kilometers. The instruments and experimental animals all safely returned to the ground from this height.

	On February 21, 1958, a Soviet single-stage geophysical rocket set a world record for similar rockets in terms of altitude, carrying instruments weighing 1,520 kilograms to an altitude of 473 kilometers.

	Since 1949, the Soviet Union has been conducting extensive biological research on the upper atmosphere.

	In the first stage, experimental animals (dogs) completed flights to heights of 100 to 210 kilometers aboard rockets. The experimental animals returned to the ground by detaching the sealed cabin from the rocket and then descending by parachute.

	In the second stage of research, ejection devices were used to land the animals and instruments. The system for ensuring flight safety worked very well.

	On August 27, 1958, two experimental puppies, "Belyanka" (Little White) and "Pustraya" (Little Mottled), became the first animals in the world to safely return to Earth after reaching an altitude of 450 kilometers.

	Previously, the maximum altitude reached by experimental dogs flying on rockets was 212 kilometers.

### 苏联高空火箭和动物高空飞行实验进展情况

1958-08-31
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联高空火箭和动物高空飞行实验进展情况
    新华社30日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联权威人士就苏联把地球物理火箭发射到四百五十公里的高空一事发表谈话，谈到了苏联高空火箭和动物高空飞行实验的进展情况。
    他指出，利用火箭考察大气高层的工作在苏联已进行了许多年。
    1933年苏联第一次发射了进行这种考察的液体燃料发动机火箭。
    1949年5月第一次垂直发射火箭时，火箭达到了一百一十公里的高空。
    1957年5月，一支带有总重二千二百公斤的试验仪器的火箭，上升到二百一十二公里的高空。仪器和试验动物都安全地从这个高度上回到了地面。
    1958年2月21日，苏联的一级地球物理火箭在升空高度上创造了同类火箭的世界纪录，这支火箭带着一千五百二十公斤重的仪器飞到了四百七十三公里的高空。
    从1949年起，苏联就开始广泛地进行大气高层飞行生物学研究。
    在第一阶段里，试验动物（狗）乘坐火箭完成了高一百到二百一十公里的飞行。试验动物回到地面，是通过使密封舱脱离火箭然后用降落伞下降的方法实现的。
    在研究的第二阶段，采用了弹射装置使动物和仪器着陆。保证飞行安全的系统，工作效能很好。
    1958年8月27日，两只试验小狗“别梁卡”（小白）和“彪斯特拉亚”（小花），成为世界上第一批到达四百五十公里的高空以后安全返回地面的动物。
    在这以前，试验狗乘火箭飞行所达到的最大高度为二百一十二公里。
