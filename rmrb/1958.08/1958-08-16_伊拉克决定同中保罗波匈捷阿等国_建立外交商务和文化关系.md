Title: Iraq Decides to Establish Diplomatic, Commercial, and Cultural Relations with China, Bulgaria, Romania, Poland, Hungary, Czechoslovakia, and Albania

Author: 
Date: 1958-08-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, August 14th: Baghdad Radio announced today that the Iraqi Republic’s Foreign Ministry spokesman stated that the Iraqi Republic’s Cabinet meeting decided to mutually establish diplomatic, commercial, and cultural relations with the People's Republic of China, Bulgaria, Romania, Poland, Hungary, Czechoslovakia, and Albania. The spokesman said that the Cabinet meeting decided to establish commercial and cultural relations with the German Democratic Republic at present.

It was announced that the Cabinet meeting also decided to establish diplomatic, commercial, and cultural relations with Yugoslavia.

### 伊拉克决定同中保罗波匈捷阿等国　建立外交商务和文化关系

1958-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　伊拉克决定同中保罗波匈捷阿等国
　　建立外交商务和文化关系
    新华社巴格达14日电　巴格达电台今天宣布，伊拉克共和国外交部发言人说，伊拉克共和国内阁会议决定同中华人民共和国、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、波兰、匈牙利、捷克斯洛伐克和阿尔巴尼亚相互建立外交、商务和文化关系。这位发言人说，内阁会议决定在目前同德意志民主共和国建立商务和文化关系。
    据宣布，内阁会议同时决定同南斯拉夫建立外交、商务和文化关系。
