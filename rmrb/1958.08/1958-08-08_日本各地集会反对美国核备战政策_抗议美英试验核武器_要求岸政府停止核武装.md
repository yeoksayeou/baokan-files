Title: Rallies Held Across Japan Against US Nuclear War Preparations; Protests Against US-British Nuclear Weapons Testing; Demands that the Kishi Government Halt Nuclear Armament
Author:
Date: 1958-08-08
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency dispatch: On August 6th, gatherings were held in Hiroshima, Tokyo, Toyama, Kanazawa, Kofu, and other places in Japan to mourn the victims of the US atomic bomb disaster thirteen years ago and to demand that the United States and Britain stop testing nuclear weapons.

### 日本各地集会反对美国核备战政策  抗议美英试验核武器  要求岸政府停止核武装

1958-08-08
第3版()
专栏：

    日本各地集会反对美国核备战政策
    抗议美英试验核武器  要求岸政府停止核武装
    本报综合报道  据新华社消息：8月6日，日本广岛、东京、富山、金泽、甲府等地纷纷举行集会，悼念十三年前遭受美国原子弹灾害的牺牲者，并且要求美英两国停止试验核武器。
