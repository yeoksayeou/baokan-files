Title: West River Moon

Author: 
Date: 1958-08-06
Page: 8

Rainbows fill the sky,
Red stars fly in the long sky.
The Sino-Soviet talks are in Beijing,
Hundreds of millions of people are happy.

Advocating peaceful coexistence,
Opposing wars of aggression,
The people of the world unite closely,
War mongers are alarmed.
(First National Folk Arts Performance, Liaoning Delegation)

### 西江月

1958-08-06
第8版()
专栏：

    西江月
    满天出现彩虹，
    长空飞来红星，
    中苏会谈在北京，
    亿万人民高兴。
    主张和平共处，
    反对侵略战争，
    世界人民团结紧，
    战争贩子吃惊。
    （第一届曲艺会演辽宁省代表团）
