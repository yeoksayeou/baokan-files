Title: Little Fountain Pen
Author:
Date: 1958-08-07
Page: 7
    Little Fountain Pen

    A little fountain pen, only three inches long,
    Always hanging on my clothes.
    During breaks I write a few words,
    And all the new characters will be wiped out.

### 小水笔

1958-08-07
第7版()
专栏：

    小水笔
    小小水笔三寸长，
    时时挂在衣服上。
    休息就来把字写，
    生字就能歼灭光。
