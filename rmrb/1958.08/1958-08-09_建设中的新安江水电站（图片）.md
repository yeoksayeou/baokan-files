Title: The Xinanjiang Hydropower Station Under Construction (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1958-08-09
Page: 6

    The Xinanjiang Hydropower Station Under Construction
    The construction of the Xinanjiang Hydropower Station is being accelerated. More than 13,000 builders are determined to complete this massive hydropower station one year ahead of schedule, and to provide new power to industry and agriculture as soon as possible.
    The above picture shows a panoramic view of the dam site of the Xinanjiang Hydropower Station. New China News Agency (Contributed by the Xinanjiang Engineering Bureau)

### 建设中的新安江水电站（图片）

1958-08-09
第6版()
专栏：

    建设中的新安江水电站
    新安江水电站工程正在加速建设。一万三千多名建设者决心要比计划提前一年建成这座规模巨大的水电站，及早向工业和农业提供新的电力。
    上图是新安江水电站坝址工地全景。新华社稿（新安江工程局供稿）
