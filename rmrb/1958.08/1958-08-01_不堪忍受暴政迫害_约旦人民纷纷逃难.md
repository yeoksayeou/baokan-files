Title: Unable to Endure Tyrannical Persecution, Jordanian People Flee in Droves

Author: 
Date: 1958-08-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 31st: According to the "Victory" newspaper, hundreds of refugees arrive in the Syrian region of the United Arab Republic from Jordan every day, fleeing the terror and persecution of the British and Jordanian authorities.

### 不堪忍受暴政迫害  约旦人民纷纷逃难

1958-08-01
第2版()
专栏：

    不堪忍受暴政迫害
    约旦人民纷纷逃难
    新华社31日讯　塔斯社大马士革7月31日讯：据“胜利报”报道，每天都有上百的逃亡者从约旦来到阿拉伯联合共和国叙利亚地区，他们是为躲避英国和约旦当局的恐怖和迫害而逃出来的。
