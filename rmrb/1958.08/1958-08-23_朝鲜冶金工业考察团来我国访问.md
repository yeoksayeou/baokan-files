Title: Korean Metallurgical Industry Delegation Visits China
Author:
Date: 1958-08-23
Page: 2

　　Korean Metallurgical Industry Delegation Visits China

　　(Our Newspaper News) The Metallurgical Industry Delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by Kang Yong-chang, Minister of the Metal Industry of Korea, arrived in Beijing at noon on August 20. Xia Yun, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Metallurgical Industry of China, welcomed them at the station. The main purpose of the delegation's visit to China is to inspect the ironmaking, steelmaking, and steel rolling industries in our country. After a short stay in Beijing, the delegation will visit Tianjin, Shenyang and other places.

### 朝鲜冶金工业考察团来我国访问

1958-08-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜冶金工业考察团来我国访问
    本报讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国冶金工业考察团，由朝鲜金属工业相姜永昌率领，于8月20日中午到达北京。我国冶金工业部副部长夏耘到车站欢迎。考察团这次来我国，主要是参观考察我国炼铁、炼钢、轧钢工业情况，考察团在北京作短期逗留后，将赴天津、沈阳等地参观。
