Title: Soviet Loan to Iceland
Author:
Date: 1958-08-21
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th. TASS reports: The Soviet Union and Iceland have signed an agreement under which the Soviet Union will lend Iceland a total of 50 million Icelandic Kronur for the purpose of purchasing fishing boats from the German Democratic Republic. The loan term is twelve years, with an annual interest rate of 2.5 percent. The loan will be repaid by Iceland providing commodities.

### 苏联贷款给冰岛

1958-08-21
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联贷款给冰岛
    新华社20日讯  据塔斯社莫斯科16日讯：苏联同冰岛签订了协定，贷给冰岛总数为五千万冰岛克朗的贷款，供它在德意志民主共和国购买捕鱼船之用。贷款期限为十二年，年利二厘五。这笔贷款将由冰岛提供商品来偿还。
