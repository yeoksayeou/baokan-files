Title: American Truck Drivers Stage Strike; Freight Traffic in Eleven Western States Paralyzed; Eight Thousand Canadian Steelworkers Strike Demanding Higher Wages
Author:
Date: 1958-08-14
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, August 13th Dispatch from San Francisco: Truck drivers in eleven western states in the United States went on strike on the 12th, demanding wage increases. Freight traffic in these areas has been paralyzed. An estimated 100,000 trucks have stopped running in Arizona, New Mexico, Nevada, Oregon, Washington, Idaho, Montana, Wyoming, and California.

Prior to this, truck workers in Oakland achieved victory last week after a ten-day strike. Workers and employers signed a new contract stipulating a wage increase of $1.60 per day, as well as new holiday and pension arrangements. The newly striking workers are demanding the same wages as the workers in Oakland.

Xinhua News Agency, August 13th Dispatch from Ontario: Eight thousand workers at "Canadian Steel Corporation" went on strike on the 12th, demanding higher wages.

The workers pointed out that because production, ex-factory prices, and company profits have increased, wages should also increase by 33%. However, the adjudication body dealing with disputes between workers and capitalists unreasonably rejected the workers' demands. (Attached picture)

Elderly retired workers in Detroit, the center of the American automobile industry, gather to demand higher retirement benefits. Xinhua News Agency manuscript

### 美国货车司机举行大罢工　西部十一个州的货运交通陷于瘫痪　加拿大钢铁公司八千工人罢工要求提高工资

1958-08-14
第7版()
专栏：

　　美国货车司机举行大罢工
　　西部十一个州的货运交通陷于瘫痪
　　加拿大钢铁公司八千工人罢工要求提高工资
    据新华社13日讯　旧金山消息：美国西部十一个州的货车司机在12日举行罢工，要求增加工资。这些地区的货运交通已陷于瘫痪。在亚利桑那、新墨西哥、内华达、俄勒冈、华盛顿、爱达荷、蒙大拿、怀俄明、加利福尼亚等州，估计有十万辆卡车停止行驶。
    在这以前，奥克兰的卡车工人在经过十天罢工后在上星期取得胜利。工人和雇主订了一个新合同，规定每天增加工资一元六角美元，并且规定了新的休假和领取养老金的办法。新罢工的工人要求取得同奥克兰的工人同样的工资。
    据新华社13日讯　安大略消息：“加拿大钢铁公司”的八千工人12日举行罢工，要求提高工资。
    工人们指出，由于生产、产品出厂价格和公司的利润都已经增加，工资也应该增加33％。但是处理工人和资本家之间的争端的裁决机构无理地拒绝了工人们的要求。（附图片）
    美国汽车工业中心底特律的老年退休工人集会，要求提高退休津贴。　新华社稿
