Title: Indonesia Announces Purchase of Weapons from the United States
Author:
Date: 1958-08-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 20th: The Indonesian Foreign Ministry Information Service announced on the 19th that the United States government had agreed to sell weapons to Indonesia a few days ago. The Indonesian government will use these weapons to equip its armed forces. The Information Service emphasized that "this is the same as the Indonesian government purchasing weapons from other countries several months ago." Indonesian Foreign Minister Subandrio told reporters that some of the American weapons have already been transported to Mayuran Airport in Jakarta. He declined to disclose the number of weapons purchased from the United States, saying that it is now fulfilling the request to purchase weapons from the United States that was made during the Ali Sastroamidjojo cabinet.
    Indonesian Minister of Information Sudibjo, in answering reporters' questions, said that the Indonesian government is purchasing weapons from the United States in accordance with its active and neutral policy. He also said: "We are also willing to accept weapons from other countries on a commercial basis."

### 印度尼西亚宣布  已向美国购买武器

1958-08-22
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚宣布
    已向美国购买武器
    新华社雅加达20日电  印度尼西亚外交部新闻司19日宣布，美国政府在几天前已经答应出售武器给印度尼西亚。印度尼西亚政府将用这些武器来装备它的武装部队。新闻司着重说：“这同印度尼西亚政府几个月前向其他国家购买武器是一样的。”印度尼西亚外交部长苏班德里约对新闻记者说，部分的美国武器已经运到雅加达的马腰兰机场。他拒绝透露向美国购买的武器的数目，他说，现在是在实现在阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约内阁期间提出的向美国购买武器的要求。
    印度尼西亚情报部长苏迪比约在回答新闻记者的问题时说，印度尼西亚政府是按照它的积极中立政策向美国购买武器的。他又说：“我们也愿意在商业的基础上接受其他国家的武器。”
