Title: Zhang Feigou, Deputy Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, Passes Away

Author:

Date: 1958-08-18
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, August 16th: Zhang Feigou, Deputy Director of the People's Republic of China's Physical Culture and Sports Commission, passed away at the Beijing Hospital on August 16th due to cirrhosis of the liver, after prolonged unsuccessful treatment. He was 41 years old.

Comrade Zhang Feigou joined the revolution in 1938 and joined the Chinese Communist Party in the same year. He successively served as the Propaganda Section Chief of the Political Department of the 120th Division of the Eighth Route Army, the Political Department of the Jin-Sui Military Region, the Political Department of the Northwest Military Region, Deputy Director and Director of the Propaganda Department, Secretary-General and Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the Southwest Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Secretary-General and Deputy Director of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, and other positions. He devoted his life to making many contributions to the propaganda work, sports work of the revolutionary army and the country.

The funeral committee for Comrade Zhang Feigou is composed of 27 people, including Vice Premier He Long.

### 国家体委副主任张非垢病逝

1958-08-18
第7版()
专栏：

　　国家体委副主任张非垢病逝
    新华社16日讯　中华人民共和国体育运动委员会副主任张非垢患肝硬变症，久治无效，于8月16日在北京医院逝世，享年四十一岁。
    张非垢同志于1938年参加革命，同年加入中国共产党。历任八路军一二○师政治部、晋西北军区政治部、晋绥军区政治部、西北军区政治部宣传科长、宣传部副部长、宣传部长，中共中央西南局宣传部秘书长、副部长，中华人民共和国体育运动委员会秘书长、副主任等职。毕生为革命军队和国家的宣传工作、体育工作作了许多贡献。
    张非垢同志治丧委员会由贺龙副总理等二十七人组成。
