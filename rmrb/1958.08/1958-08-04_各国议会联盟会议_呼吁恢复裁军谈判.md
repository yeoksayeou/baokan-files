Title: Inter-Parliamentary Union Conference Calls for Resumption of Disarmament Talks
Author:
Date: 1958-08-04
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Montevideo, August 2nd: The 47th Inter-Parliamentary Union Conference, which began on July 24th, concluded in Rio de Janeiro, the capital of Brazil. The participants unanimously adopted a resolution calling for the resumption of negotiations on disarmament and the prohibition of testing atomic and hydrogen weapons. The conference also unanimously adopted a resolution calling for the expansion of cultural exchanges between countries, as well as other resolutions.

### 各国议会联盟会议  呼吁恢复裁军谈判

1958-08-04
第3版()
专栏：

    各国议会联盟会议呼吁恢复裁军谈判
    新华社3日讯　据塔斯社蒙得维的亚2日讯：从7月24日开始举行的各国议会联盟第四十七次会议在巴西首都里约热内卢闭幕。会议参加者一致通过一项决议，呼吁恢复关于裁军问题和禁止试验原子武器和氢武器问题的谈判。会议还一致通过了一项要求扩大各国文化交流的决议以及通过其他一些决议。
