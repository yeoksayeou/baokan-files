Title: Dialogue
Author: Wei Wei
Date: 1958-08-01
Page: 8

    Dialogue
    Wei Wei
    American "Overseas Chinese"

    President, my dear,
    You care so much about overseas Chinese.
    But it's a great pity!
    You left us here,
    But never even ask.

    Eisenhower
    I'm sorry!
    We seem to have met,
    But I'm not sure I recognize you.
    Please allow me to ask:
    Who exactly are you?

    American "Overseas Chinese"
    Oh dear!
    It's only been a few years since we parted,
    How could you have forgotten us?
    We are genuine,
    Truly American overseas Chinese!

    Eisenhower
    Aha, old friends!
    May I ask where you reside,
    Is your life comfortable and stable?
    Is there any communist aggression,
    Threatening your existence?

    American "Overseas Chinese"
    Speaking of life here,
    It's both comfortable and stable,
    And there's no communism,
    Threatening our existence.
    It's just that the border here is heavily guarded,
    And we're no longer allowed to go back to see our families!

    Eisenhower
    Huh! What country is this,
    So shockingly unheard of?
    I will immediately lodge a protest,
    To make them,
    Issue you passports to return home!

    American "Overseas Chinese"
    Speaking of this country,
    It's utterly gloomy,
    The president is the famous King Yama.
    But he also says,
    It's part of the "free world."

    Eisenhower
    Oh dear!
    So you are ghosts, not people!
    Come! Quickly fight the ghosts!
    You unlucky ghosts,
    Why are you invading this old man!

    American "Overseas Chinese"
    No, no,
    We are Americans just like you.
    It's just that in the Korean War,
    We were relocated here as overseas Chinese.
    Dear one, quickly protect us,
    We are genuine American overseas Chinese,
    Dear one, quickly give us passports to return home,
    You are the one who cares most about us!

### 对话
魏巍
1958-08-01
第8版()
专栏：

    对话
    魏巍
    美国“侨民”
    总统先生，亲爱的，
    你对侨民多关心。
    可是遗憾得很！
    你把我们丢到这儿，
    却从来不问一问。
    艾森豪威尔
    对不起！
    我们仿佛见过面，
    可一时又不敢认。
    请允许我问一声：
    你们到底是什么人？
    美国“侨民”
    哎呀！
    刚刚分别了几年，
    您怎末就忘了我们？
    我们是地地道道，
    道道地地的美国侨民！
    艾森豪威尔
    呵哈，原来是老朋友！
    我能否问你们侨居在哪里，
    生活可还优裕安稳？
    是否有共产主义的侵略，
    威胁你们的生存？美国“侨民”
    说起这儿的生活，
    既优裕，又安稳，
    更没有什么共产主义，
    威胁我们的生存。
    只是这里的国境严森森，
    再不许我们回去见亲人！
    艾森豪威尔
    咦！这是什么国家，
    这样骇人听闻？
    我要马上提出抗议，
    要他们，
    把归国护照发给你们！
    美国“侨民”
    说起这个国家，
    一派阴森森，
    总统是大名鼎鼎的阎君。
    不过他也说，
    是“自由世界”的一部分。
    艾森豪威尔
    哎呀！
    原来你们是鬼，不是人！
    来！快打鬼！
    你们这些倒霉鬼，
    为什么要侵略我这老年人！
    美国“侨民”
    不，不，
    我们是和您一样的美国人。
    只是在侵朝战争里，
    我们才迁到这里当了侨民。
    亲爱的，快来保护我们吧，
    我们是地道的美国侨民，
    亲爱的，快给我们归国护照吧，
    你是最关心我们的人！
