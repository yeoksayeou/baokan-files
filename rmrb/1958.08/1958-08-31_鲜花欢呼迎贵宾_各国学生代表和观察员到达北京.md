Title: Flowers and Cheers Welcome Distinguished Guests: Student Representatives and Observers from Various Countries Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-08-31
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, August 30th: Today, student representatives of the capital, waving flowers and giving enthusiastic cheers, warmly welcomed fifty-one student representatives and observers from thirty-three countries and regions who came to participate in the Fifth Congress of the International Union of Students at the East Suburb Airport.

These countries and regions are: Belgium, Black Africa, Bolivia, Britain, Bulgaria, Cambodia, Cameroon, Ceylon, Chile, Colombia, Cyprus, Dhaka, Ecuador, Central Africa, Guadeloupe, Guatemala, Guyana, Hungary, Iran, Iraq, Italy, Japan, Jordan, Laos, Madagascar, Martinique, Palestine, Peru, Sierra Leone, Sudan, Togo, Uruguay, and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Two other representatives from Cyprus and Iraq arrived in Beijing by train this evening.

President of the International Union of Students, Bělík, and four vice-presidents and secretaries have already arrived in Beijing in previous days. Before today, a total of five representatives from Australia, Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, as well as some staff members of the International Union of Students, had arrived in Beijing.

Today, when the representatives and observers from thirty-three countries and regions arrived in Beijing, at the airport flying the flag of the International Union of Students, the representatives were warmly welcomed by Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, and other leaders, as well as more than 1,000 student representatives from the capital, and received bouquets of flowers. In his welcoming speech, Hu Qili said that Chinese students warmly welcome the Fifth Congress of the International Union of Students to be held in Beijing, the capital of China. We wish this congress great success with the support of peace-loving students around the world and the joint efforts of student representatives from all countries. Noël Ekoe Koutoucli, a student representative from Black Africa, said that through this congress, students from all over the world should further unite, work to strengthen friendship, and strive for peace. Let students all over the world live in peace and friendship. He ended his speech by shouting "Long live peace and friendship!" in Chinese. Manuel Cepeda, a student representative from Colombia, expressed his congratulations to Chinese students in his enthusiastic speech. He said that we have come from very, very far away to be with students all over the world and work for peace and friendship. For the sake of peace and students' studies, we oppose colonialism. He ended his speech by shouting "Long live peace!" in Chinese.

After the speeches by the guests and hosts, the student band once again played the majestic anthem of the International Union of Students and the song "Unity is Strength." At this time, the welcoming crowd waved flowers and rushed towards the student representatives from various countries, warmly applauding and singing, and rhythmically shouting the slogan "Long live peace" for a long time. ### 鲜花欢呼迎贵宾  各国学生代表和观察员到达北京

1958-08-31
第2版()
专栏：

　　鲜花欢呼迎贵宾
    各国学生代表和观察员到达北京
    新华社30日讯　今天首都学生代表挥舞着鲜花和用热情的欢呼，在东郊机场热烈欢迎前来参加国际学生联合会第五届代表大会的三十三个国家和地区的五十一名学生代表和列席的观察员。
    这些国家和地区是：比利时、黑非洲、玻利维亚、英国、保加利亚、柬埔寨、喀麦隆、锡兰、智利、哥伦比亚、塞浦路斯、达卡、厄瓜多尔、中非、瓜德罗浦、危地马拉、圭亚那、匈牙利、伊朗、伊拉克、意大利、日本、约旦、老挝、马达加斯加、马提尼克、巴勒斯坦、秘鲁、塞拉勒窝内、苏丹、多哥、乌拉圭和越南民主共和国。
    塞浦路斯和伊拉克的另外两位代表在今天晚间乘火车到达北京。
    国际学联主席贝利康和副主席、书记四人已经分别在日前到达北京。在今天以前到达北京的共有澳大利亚、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的代表五名以及部分国际学联工作人员。
    今天，当三十三个国家和地区的代表和观察员到达北京后，在飘扬着国际学生联合会会旗的飞机场上，代表们受到中华全国学生联合会主席胡启立等负责人以及首都一千多名学生代表的热烈欢迎，并接受了献花。胡启立在致欢迎词中说，我们中国学生热烈欢迎国际学生联合会第五届代表大会在中国首都北京召开。我们预祝这次大会在全世界爱好和平的学生的支持下，在各国学生代表们的共同努力下，获得巨大的成就。黑非洲学生代表诺埃尔·埃哥埃·库图克利讲话说，通过这次大会，让全世界的学生进一步团结起来，为加强友谊和为争取和平而努力。让全世界的学生生活在和平与友谊之中。他最后用中国话高呼“和平、友谊万岁！”哥伦比亚学生代表马努爱勒·塞伯达在他的热情讲话中向中国学生表示祝贺。他说，我们从很远很远的地方而来，是为同全世界学生在一起，为和平和友谊而努力。为了和平和学生的学习，我们反对殖民主义。他用中国话高呼“和平万岁”后结束了他的讲话。
    宾主讲话结束以后，学生乐队再一次奏起雄壮的国际学生联合会会歌和“团结就是力量”的歌子，这时欢迎群众挥舞鲜花，一齐向各国学生代表们涌去，热烈地鼓掌歌唱，并且长时间地有节奏地发出“和平万岁”的口号声。（附图片）
