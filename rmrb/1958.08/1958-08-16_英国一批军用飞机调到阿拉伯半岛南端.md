Title: A Batch of British Military Aircraft Transferred to the Southern End of the Arabian Peninsula
Author:
Date: 1958-08-16
Page: 5
　
　　British Military Aircraft
　　Transferred to the Southern End of the Arabian Peninsula
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 14th: TASS, Cairo, August 14th: The British military command is transferring a batch of military aircraft to the southern end of the Arabian Peninsula. According to the representative of the Imam of Oman in Cairo, a batch of British military aircraft are successively flying to the Shaya airport controlled by the British. He emphasized that since Britain began its invasion of Oman, there have never been so many military aircraft. This action proves that the British government is preparing to launch a new aggression in that region.

### 英国一批军用飞机调到阿拉伯半岛南端

1958-08-16
第5版()
专栏：

　　英国一批军用飞机
　　调到阿拉伯半岛南端
    新华社14日讯　塔斯社开罗14日讯：英军司令部正在把一批军用飞机调到阿拉伯半岛南端。据阿曼教长驻开罗代表说，有一批英国军用飞机正陆续飞抵英国控制下的沙亚机场。他着重说，从英国开始侵略阿曼以来，还从来没有出现那么多军用飞机。这种行动证明，英国政府准备在那个地区发动新的侵略。
