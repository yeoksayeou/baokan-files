Title: Sa'id Group's Conspiracy to Invade Syria: Revolutionary Court to Hold Public Trial
Author:
Date: 1958-08-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 8th: According to a Middle East News Agency dispatch from Baghdad on August 8th: The Iraqi Revolutionary Court will hold a public trial next week for the case of the Sa'id regime's conspiracy, in collaboration with Israel and Turkey, to invade the Syrian region of the United Arab Republic in early August. Some Iraqi politicians and military officers involved in this conspiracy will be tried.

It is reported that the trial will be held publicly in the House of Representatives building, and some important documents will be released during the trial.

### 赛义德集团侵叙阴谋案  伊革命法庭将予公审

1958-08-09
第2版()
专栏：

    赛义德集团侵叙阴谋案  伊革命法庭将予公审
    新华社开罗8日电  据中东通讯社巴格达8日消息：伊拉克革命法庭将在下周开庭公审赛义德政权阴谋协同以色列和土耳其在8月初侵略阿拉伯联合共和国叙利亚地区的案件。参与这一阴谋的一些伊拉克政客和军官将要受审。
    据报道，审讯将在众议院大厦公开举行，在审讯过程中将要公布一些重要文件。
