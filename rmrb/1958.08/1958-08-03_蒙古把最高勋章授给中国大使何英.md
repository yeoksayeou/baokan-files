Title: Mongolia Awards Highest Medal to Chinese Ambassador He Ying
Author:
Date: 1958-08-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, August 1st: The Presidium of the Mongolian Great People's Hural issued an order on July 31st to award the highest Mongolian medal, the Order of Sukhe-Bator, to He Ying, the Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, in recognition of his efforts in consolidating the fraternal friendship between the people of Mongolia and China and in effectively implementing China's aid to Mongolia's construction.

### 蒙古把最高勋章授给中国大使何英

1958-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古把最高勋章授给中国大使何英
    新华社乌兰巴托1日电　蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团7月31日发布命令，把蒙古最高的勋章苏赫—巴托尔勋章授给中国驻蒙古大使何英，以表彰他在巩固蒙中两国人民的兄弟友谊和成效卓著地贯彻执行中国对蒙古建设的援助工作中所作的努力。
