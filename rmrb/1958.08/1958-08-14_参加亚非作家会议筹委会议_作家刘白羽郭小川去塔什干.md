Title: Writers Liu Baiyu and Guo Xiaochuan Go to Tashkent to Attend Asian-African Writers' Conference Preparatory Meeting
Author:
Date: 1958-08-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 13th:

Writers Liu Baiyu and Guo Xiaochuan have left Beijing for Tashkent, the capital of the Uzbek Soviet Socialist Republic, to participate in the preparatory committee meeting of the Asian-African Writers' Conference, which will be held there on August 15th.

According to the agreement reached at the preparatory meeting held in Moscow in September this year by writers' representatives from the Soviet Union, China, India, Japan, and the United Arab Republic, the Asian-African Writers' Conference will be held in Tashkent, Soviet Union, from October 1st to 5th this year. This preparatory meeting also decided to invite writers from the Soviet Union, China, India, Indonesia, Burma, Iran, Turkey and other countries to form the preparatory committee for the conference.

### 参加亚非作家会议筹委会议　作家刘白羽郭小川去塔什干

1958-08-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　参加亚非作家会议筹委会议
　　作家刘白羽郭小川去塔什干
    新华社13日讯　中国作家刘白羽、郭小川已经离开北京前往苏联乌兹别克共和国首都塔什干，参加将于8月15日在那里举行的亚非作家会议筹备委员会会议。
    根据苏联、中国、印度、日本、阿拉伯联合共和国作家代表今年9月在莫斯科举行筹备会议的协议，亚非作家代表大会将于今年10月1日到5日在苏联的塔什干召开，这次筹备会还决定邀请苏联、中国、印度、印度尼西亚、缅甸、伊朗、土耳其等国的作家组成大会筹备委员会。
