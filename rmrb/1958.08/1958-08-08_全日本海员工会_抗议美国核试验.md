Title: All Japan Seamen's Union Protests US Nuclear Testing
Author:
Date: 1958-08-08
Page: 3

    All Japan Seamen's Union
    Protests US Nuclear Testing
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 6th — The All Japan Seamen's Union today submitted a letter of protest to Eisenhower, pointing out that the United States' designation of a "danger zone" in international waters for nuclear testing violates the principles of freedom of the high seas and international justice. The letter of protest also pointed out that these tests have already caused the Japanese ships "Takuyo" and "Satsuma" to suffer from radioactive fallout. Thirty-seven crew members have already contracted life-threatening leukopenia.

### 全日本海员工会  抗议美国核试验

1958-08-08
第3版()
专栏：

    全日本海员工会
    抗议美国核试验
    据新华社6日讯  东京消息：全日本海员工会今天向艾森豪威尔提出抗议书，指出美国在公海划定“危险区”进行核试验是违反公海自由和国际正义的原则的。抗议书还指出，这种试验已经使日本船“拓洋”号和“萨摩”号遭到含有放射性微尘雨的灾害。有三十七名船员已经患有威胁生命的白血球减少症。
