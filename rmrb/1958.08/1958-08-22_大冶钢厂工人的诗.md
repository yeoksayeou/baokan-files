Title: Poems by Workers of Daye Steel Plant
Author: Yuan Shaoqing
Date: 1958-08-22
Page: 2

Poems by Workers of Daye Steel Plant
　　Red steel is like a fire dragon turning,
　　Circling around the turntable on both sides,
　　Silver helmets and golden armor like lightning,
　　Surpassing old John of England.
　　Refine more steel and sweat more,
　　Exceed the quota and double it,
　　The rolling steel water burns in my heart,
　　Wholeheartedly want to overtake England.
              (Yuan Shaoqing)

### 大冶钢厂工人的诗
袁少卿
1958-08-22
第2版()
专栏：

　　大冶钢厂工人的诗
　　红钢好似火龙翻
　　围着围盘两边穿
　　银盔金甲如闪电
　　超过英国老约翰
　　多炼钢铁多流汗
　　超过定额翻两番
　　滚滚钢水心中燃
　　一心要把英国赶
              （袁少卿）
