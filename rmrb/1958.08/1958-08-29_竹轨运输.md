Title: Bamboo Rail Transportation
Author: Chun Hua
Date: 1958-08-29
Page: 2

Column: All Roads Lead

    Bamboo Rail Transportation
    A bamboo rail road has appeared in Baoshan Township, Wan'an County, Jiangxi Province. This bamboo rail road is six *li* long, and the twelve carts that run on it are also made of wood. This type of bamboo rail transportation is very efficient. Two people, using foot power, can travel twelve times a day, transporting a total of 144 tons of goods.
                  (Chun Hua)

### 竹轨运输
春花
1958-08-29
第2版()
专栏：四通八达

    竹轨运输
    江西省万安县宝山乡出现了一条竹轨路。这条竹轨路全长六华里，行驶在这里的十二辆土车皮也是用木头做的。这种竹轨运输效率很高。二个人用足踩，每天能走十二次，总共可拉货一百四十四吨。
                  （春花）
