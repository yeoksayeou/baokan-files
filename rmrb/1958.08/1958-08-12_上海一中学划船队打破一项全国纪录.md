Title: Shanghai Middle School Rowing Team Breaks National Record
Author:
Date: 1958-08-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 10th - In the 1958 Shanghai Rowing Championships held on the Huangpu River today, a middle school rowing team composed of five young students broke the national record for the men's four-man single sculls (with coxswain) over 2,000 meters against the current.

Their new record is 8 minutes 16.5 seconds, which is 4.4 seconds faster than the national record of 8 minutes 20.9 seconds held by the Shanghai Rowing Team.

### 上海一中学划船队打破一项全国纪录

1958-08-12
第5版()
专栏：体育运动

　　上海一中学划船队打破一项全国纪录
    据新华社上海10日电　今天在黄浦江上举行的1958年上海市划船锦标赛中，由五个青年学生组成的中学划船队，在逆水中打破了男子四人单桨（有舵手）二千公尺的全国纪录。
    他们的新成绩是八分十六秒五，比上海市划船队保持的全国纪录八分二十秒九快了四秒四。
