Title: Burmese Peace Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1958-08-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, August 1st. The Burmese Peace Delegation, led by Elder U Awgahta, arrived in Beijing by plane at noon today after attending the Stockholm Conference on Disarmament and International Cooperation, at the invitation of the Buddhist Association of China. The delegation will be visiting China for three weeks.
    Zhao Puchu, Vice President of the Buddhist Association of China, Master Juzan, and Yu Zheng, Secretary-General of the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council, and others welcomed the delegation at the airport.

### 缅甸和平代表团到京

1958-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸和平代表团到京
    新华社1日讯　由吴奥卡塔大长老率领的缅甸和平代表团一行十人，在参加斯德哥尔摩裁军和国际合作大会以后，应中国佛教协会邀请，今天中午乘飞机到达北京。代表团将在我国作三星期的访问。
    中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初、巨赞法师以及国务院宗教事务局秘书长余峥等人到机场欢迎。
