Title: Thai Government Requests US to Change "Aid" and Use Military "Aid" for Economic Construction
Author:
Date: 1958-08-16
Page: 5

　　Bangkok dispatch from Xinhua News Agency, August 15: According to newspaper reports, the Thai cabinet decided at a meeting on the 13th to request the US government to reallocate the 57 million baht originally intended for "aid" to Thailand in constructing ten large airports, to economic construction.

　　The report stated that the cabinet believes that Thailand is currently in urgent need of economic assistance to improve its backward economic situation. Therefore, the cabinet decided to authorize relevant Thai authorities to be responsible for contacting the United States on this matter.

　　According to newspaper reports, Thai political figures and public opinion have consistently expressed dissatisfaction with the US's focus on providing "aid" to Thailand in the military field, pointing out that US "aid" is not beneficial to Thailand.

### 泰国政府要求美国改变“援助”　把军“援”用于经济建设

1958-08-16
第5版()
专栏：

　　泰国政府要求美国改变“援助”
　　把军“援”用于经济建设
    新华社15日讯　曼谷消息：据报纸报道，泰国内阁在13日举行的会议上，决定要求美国政府将原定“援助”泰国修建十个大飞机场的五千七百万铢，改用于经济建设方面。
    消息说，内阁认为，泰国现在所急需的是经济上的援助，以便用来改善泰国落后的经济状况。因此，内阁决定授权泰国有关方面负责同美国接洽这件事。
    据报纸报道，泰国政界人士和公众舆论一直对美国只着重在军事方面“援助”泰国表示不满，他们指出美“援”对泰国并没有好处。
