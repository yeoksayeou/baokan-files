Author:

Date: 1958-08-20
Page: 8

Editor's Note

　　Starting today, we will successively publish Comrade Mao Dun's "Northeast in the Great Leap Forward," about five or six articles, with content focusing on the new atmosphere of the Great Leap Forward in Northeast industry and agriculture. In today's article, the author enthusiastically praises the soaring enthusiasm of the residents of Changchun streets in running industries. The second article will describe "Saiwai Jiangnan" - the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture.

　　We warmly welcome writers to contribute news reports and features to the supplement, promptly reflecting the new face of construction and new people and events in various parts of the motherland.

### 编后小语

1958-08-20
第8版()
专栏：编后小语

　　编后小语
    从今天起我们将陆续刊登茅盾同志所写的“跃进中的东北”，约五、六篇，内容是东北工农业大跃进的新气象。在今天这一篇里，作者热情地歌颂了长春街道居民办工业的冲天干劲。第二篇将是描写“塞外江南”——延边朝鲜族自治州的。
    我们十分欢迎作家们为副刊撰写通讯特写，及时地反映祖国各地建设的新面貌和新人新事。
