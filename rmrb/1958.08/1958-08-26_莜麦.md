Naked Oats
    Naked oats, also known as hulless oats, are an edible crop grown in some parts of China. Naked oats are cold-resistant and have a short growing season. They are cultivated in Shanxi, Shaanxi, Hebei (northern part), Inner Mongolia, Gansu, Qinghai and other places. Naked oat seeds can be ground into flour for human consumption, and green fodder can be used as feed for livestock. The straw after harvesting the seeds can also be used to feed livestock.

专栏：名词解释

　　莜麦
    莜麦即裸粒燕麦，是我国部分地区种植的一种食用作物。莜麦耐寒，生长期短，山西、陕西、河北（北部）、内蒙古、甘肃、青海等地都有栽培。莜麦种子可以磨面食用，青刈可作家畜等饲料，收获子实后的秸秆也能喂牲口。
