Title: Chiang Kai-shek's "Ambassador" to be Ousted from Iraq
Author:
Date: 1958-08-04
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, August 2nd – An official of the Iraqi Republic's Foreign Ministry told Xinhua News Agency reporters that Chen Chih-ping, the "ambassador" of the Chiang Kai-shek clique in Taiwan stationed in Iraq, will leave Iraq within two days.

### 蒋介石的“大使”将被撵出伊拉克

1958-08-04
第2版()
专栏：

    蒋介石的“大使” 将被撵出伊拉克
    新华社巴格达2日电　伊拉克共和国外交部的一位官员对新华社记者说，台湾蒋介石集团驻伊拉克的“大使”陈质平将在两天内离开伊拉克。
