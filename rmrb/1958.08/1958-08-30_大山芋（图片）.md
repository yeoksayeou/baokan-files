Author:
Date: 1958-08-30
Page: 8

Poetry and Painting

　　Large Yam
　　Pizhou County Guanhuzhen Poster
    Poem by Gao Mi
　　In previous years, tens of thousands of catties were transported, but today we encounter a giant immortal!
　　The wheels are sunk in the muddy swamp, and the old ox pants and sweats profusely.
