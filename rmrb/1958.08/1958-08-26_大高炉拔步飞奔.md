Title: The Great Blast Furnace Runs Forward by Leaps and Bounds
Author: Gu Zhengrong
Date: 1958-08-26
Page: 8

Using Both Indigenous and Foreign Methods

　　The Great Blast Furnace Runs Forward by Leaps and Bounds
　　Gu Zhengrong, Deputy Manager of Anshan Iron and Steel Company

　　It's not a fire in the heavenly palace,
　　It's not the sunset spitting out rosy clouds,
　　This is the magnificent blast furnace,
　　Spewing out a myriad of sparks.
　　It's not the roar of the thunder god,
　　It's not the rampage of mountain torrents,
　　These are the iron-smelting workers,
　　Performing the symphony of steel.
　　The sky is filled with billowing smoke,
　　The earth shakes in waves,
　　Nine steel giants,
　　Are racing on the sports field.
　　No. 3 is the strongest,
　　Always leading for a long time,
　　But the pace is getting slower and slower,
　　And soon overtaken by No. 8.
　　No. 6 and No. 7 are young athletes,
　　Agility and bravery are their strengths,
　　Sometimes following closely behind No. 3,
　　And suddenly running in front of No. 8.
　　Although No. 2 and No. 9 are lagging behind,
　　They are full of energy and strong will,
　　Constantly taking bigger and faster steps,
　　And are about to snatch ahead of No. 4.
　　In the thunderous cheers,
　　No. 8 speeds like lightning,
　　And from then on, runs at the very front for a long time,
　　"Go! I wish you to win the championship red flag."
　　To say that the speed is faster than a rocket,
　　May already be a bit cliché,
　　To run 10,000 meters at the speed of 100 meters,
　　Is also not an appropriate metaphor.
　　Poets don't have to bother thinking too hard,
　　The giants themselves say it best:
　　Faster than the fastest speed,
　　Whoever runs first, I will overtake.
　　One person does the work of ten thousand,
　　Half a day walks the road of ten years,
　　Many people say this is a miracle,
　　But it's actually a common thing.
　　Statistics on steel should use hundreds of millions and tens of thousands of tons,
　　Calculating time should use seconds and minutes,
　　We are steel giants,
　　We know how to do it to be considered fulfilling our responsibilities.
　　Look! A red flag is flying in the distance,
　　The red flag emits a huge attraction,
　　Nine giants are heading to the same destination,
　　The first to arrive wins the red flag!

### 大高炉拔步飞奔
谷正荣
1958-08-26
第8版()
专栏：土洋并举

　　大高炉拔步飞奔
　　鞍山钢铁公司副经理　谷正荣
　　不是天宫失火，
　　不是夕阳吐霞，
　　这是宏伟的高炉，
　　喷射出万丈火花。
　　不是雷公怒吼，
　　不是山洪猖狂，
　　这是炼铁的工人，
　　演奏着钢铁的交响。
　　漫天浓烟蒸腾，
　　大地阵阵摇晃，
　　九个钢铁巨人，
　　赛跑在运动场上。
　　3号长的最结实强壮，
　　在长时间内始终领先，
　　但是步子越来越慢，
　　很快就被8号赶上。
　　6号和7号都是青年健将，
　　敏捷勇敢是他们的特长，
　　有时紧跟在3号的后面，
　　忽而又跑到8号的前方。
　　2号和9号虽然落在后头，
　　却是精神充沛意志坚强，
　　步伐不断地迈大加快，
　　眼看就要抢在4号之前。
　　在雷鸣似的喝采声中，
　　8号像闪电一样的疾驰，
　　从此就长久地跑在最前，
    “加油！祝你夺取优胜红旗”。
　　把速度说成胜过火箭，
　　未免已经有些俗气，
　　以百米的时速跑完万米，
　　也是不够恰当的比喻。
　　诗人不必苦费心思，
　　巨人们自己说的最对：
　　比最快的速度还要快，
　　谁跑第一我就赶过谁。
　　一人作出万人的事业，
　　半天走完十年的路程，
　　很多人都说这是奇迹，
　　却实在是平常的事情。
　　统计钢铁要用亿和万吨，
　　计算时间要用秒和分，
　　咱们是钢铁的巨人，
　　知道怎样作才算尽了责任。
　　看！远方飘着一面红旗，
　　红旗发出巨大的吸引力，
　　九个巨人向着同一个目的地，
　　头名到达的夺取红旗！
