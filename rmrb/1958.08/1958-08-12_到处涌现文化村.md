Title: Cultural Villages Spring Up Everywhere
Author:
Date: 1958-08-12
Page: 5

Cultural Villages Spring Up Everywhere
The stars in the sky are bright and twinkling,
Literacy brightens the eyes and clears the mind,
Men and women, young and old, attend people's schools,
Cultural villages spring up everywhere,
The work of eliminating illiteracy takes on a new look.
                   ——Lu'an
