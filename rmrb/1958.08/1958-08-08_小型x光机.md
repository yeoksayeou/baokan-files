    Small X-ray Machine
    A head-mounted (i.e., modular) small X-ray machine has recently been successfully trial-produced at the Hubei Provincial Department of Commerce Precision Instrument Factory in Wuhan. This X-ray machine is small, lightweight, and suitable for medical needs in medium and small counties and the vast countryside.

### 小型X光机

1958-08-08
第6版()
专栏：创制更多更好的新产品

    小型X光机
    一种机头式（即组合式）的小型X光射线机，最近在武汉市的湖北省商业厅精密器械厂试制成功。这种X光射线机小巧轻便，适合于中、小县城和广大农村医疗需用。
