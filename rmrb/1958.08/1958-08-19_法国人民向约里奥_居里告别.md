Title: The French People Bid Farewell to Joliot-Curie
Author:
Date: 1958-08-19
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, August 17th: According to TASS, the French people bid farewell to the great scientist and outstanding social activist, the French patriot Frédéric Joliot-Curie. From early morning, people streamed to the University of Paris, where the coffin lay, to pay their respects to Joliot-Curie. Many carried bouquets and wreaths. An honor guard of university students, Joliot-Curie's colleagues, and the Republican Guard took turns guarding Frédéric Joliot-Curie's coffin every half hour.

Paris received many telegrams and letters from all over the world expressing deep condolences on the death of Joliot-Curie, including letters from the Italian National Peace Council, telegrams of condolence from Palmiro Togliatti, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party, and Pietro Nenni, Secretary of the Italian Socialist Party, as well as many social organizations and individuals.

French newspapers continue to publish many articles introducing Joliot-Curie's glorious life.

On the 18th, people will continue to bid farewell to Joliot-Curie's remains. The state funeral for Joliot-Curie will be held on the 19th.

### 法国人民向约里奥——居里告别

1958-08-19
第5版()
专栏：

　　法国人民向约里奥——居里告别
    新华社18日讯　据塔斯社巴黎17日讯：法国人民向伟大的科学家和杰出的社会活动家、法国爱国者弗雷德里克·约里奥—居里告别。从清晨开始人们就络绎不绝地前往停放灵柩的巴黎大学瞻仰约里奥—居里的遗容。许多人都拿着花束和花圈。大学生的仪仗队、约里奥—居里的同事以及共和国近卫军每半小时轮换一次在弗雷德里克·约里奥—居里的灵柩旁守灵。
    巴黎收到许多从世界各地发来的对约里奥—居里的逝世表示深切哀悼的函电，其中有意大利全国和平理事会的来信、意大利共产党中央委员会总书记陶里亚蒂和意大利社会党书记南尼以及许多社会组织和个人的唁电。
    法国各报继续刊载许多介绍约里奥—居里光辉的一生的文章。
    在18日，人们将继续向约里奥—居里的遗体告别。为约里奥—居里举行的国葬仪式将在19日举行。
