Title: Saudi Arabian Prime Minister Faisal Visits the United Arab Republic
Author:
Date: 1958-08-17
Page: 3

　  Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 15th: Saudi Arabian Crown Prince and Prime Minister Faisal arrived here by special plane this afternoon at the invitation of the government of the United Arab Republic.
　  It is expected that Faisal will hold talks with Nasser.
　  Upon Faisal's arrival, he was welcomed by the Vice President of the United Arab Republic, Boghdadi.
　  Observers here attach great importance to Faisal's immediate visit to the UAR after Marshal Amer's sudden visit to Saudi Arabia, and believe that this indicates a further improvement in relations between the two countries. Relations between the UAR and Saudi Arabia had been strained at one point before Faisal became Prime Minister.

### 沙特阿拉伯首相费萨尔到阿联访问

1958-08-17
第3版()
专栏：

　  沙特阿拉伯首相费萨尔到阿联访问
    新华社开罗15日电　沙特阿拉伯首相费萨尔王储应阿拉伯联合共和国政府的邀请，在今天下午乘专机到达这里。
    预料，费萨尔将同纳赛尔举行会谈。
    费萨尔到达时，受到阿拉伯联合共和国副总统波格达迪的欢迎。
    这里的观察家们十分重视在阿密尔元帅突然访问沙特阿拉伯之后费萨尔立刻到阿联来访问这件事，并且认为这表明两国之间的关系已经进一步改善。在费萨尔就任首相以前，阿联同沙特阿拉伯之间的关系曾经一度紧张。
