Title: Prime Minister Sihanouk Visits Lushun Naval Base
Author:
Date: 1958-08-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Luda, August 20th: Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, wished the Chinese People's Navy victorious development today during his visit to the Lushun Naval Base of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

At 8:30 AM, Prime Minister Sihanouk and the distinguished guests of the Cambodian Royal National Delegation, accompanied by Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, Mayor of Luda Song Li, and other responsible persons, arrived at the base and were warmly welcomed by Rear Admiral Liu Huaqing, head of the naval base, and all officers and soldiers.

At the base, Prime Minister Sihanouk and the distinguished guests of the delegation also boarded a speedboat for a sea voyage and visited naval vessels. During the visit, Prime Minister Sihanouk inscribed a message for the naval officers and soldiers in the cabin of a warship, saying: "Today we have received a noble honor," "Wishing the Chinese People's Navy victorious development."

### 西哈努克首相参观旅顺海军基地

1958-08-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　西哈努克首相参观旅顺海军基地
    据新华社旅大20日电  柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王今天在参观中国人民解放军旅顺海军基地的时候，祝愿中国人民海军胜利发展。
    八时三十分，西哈努克首相和由他率领的柬埔寨王国国家代表团的贵宾们，由陈毅副总理兼外交部长、旅大市市长宋黎等负责人陪同来到基地，受到了基地海军首长刘华清少将和全体官兵的热烈欢迎。
    在基地上，西哈努克首相和代表团的贵宾们还登上快艇出海行驶，并且参观了海军舰艇。在参观中，西哈努克首相在一艘军舰的舱中为海军官兵们题词，题词中说：“今天我们获得一个高贵的荣誉”，“祝愿中国人民海军胜利发展”。
