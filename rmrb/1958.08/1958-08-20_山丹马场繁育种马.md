Title: Shandan Horse Farm Breeds Stallions
Author:
Date: 1958-08-20
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency report: The state-owned Shandan Horse Farm, located on the northern slope of the Qilian Mountains, is being built into China's largest stallion breeding base. This year, the horse farm has produced more than 1,700 foals, achieving an unprecedented bumper harvest. Currently, all employees are exerting their utmost efforts, striving for advancement, and launching a "five-full" campaign of full mating, full accuracy, full birth, full survival, and full strength, with the goal of achieving complete improvement of breeds within three years. The first batch of three to forty new Shandan horse breeds can be bred this year.

### 山丹马场繁育种马

1958-08-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　山丹马场繁育种马
    据新华社讯  位于祁连山北麓的国营山丹马场，正在建设成为我国最大的种马基地。这个马场今年已产马驹一千七百多头，获得了空前丰收。目前，全场职工鼓足干劲，力争上游，展开全配、全准、全生、全活、全壮的“五全”运动，计划在三年内实现全部良种化。今年就可以育成第一批山丹马新品种三、四十匹。
