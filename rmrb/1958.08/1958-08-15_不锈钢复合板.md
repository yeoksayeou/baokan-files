　　Stainless Steel Clad Plate
    A substitute for stainless steel plate – "stainless steel clad plate" has been successfully trial-produced at the Wusan Factory. It has opened up a broad path for China to save nickel-chromium steel.
    This stainless steel clad plate has good mechanical properties and can be used in various departments such as the chemical industry, petroleum industry, automobile industry, shipbuilding industry, and national defense industry to replace stainless steel plate. For example, the equipment for a nitrogen fertilizer plant with an annual output of 50,000 tons of synthetic ammonia requires about 57 tons of stainless steel plate. If stainless steel clad plate is used instead, 40 tons of stainless steel can be saved.　　　(Li Baoyu)

### 不锈钢复合板
李宝玉
1958-08-15
第3版()
专栏：创制更多更好的新产品

　　不锈钢复合板
    一种不锈钢板的代用品——“不锈钢复合板”已在五三工厂试制成功。它为我国节约镍铬钢材开辟了一条广阔的道路。
    这种不锈钢复合板机械性能良好，可以用在化学工业、石油工业、汽车工业、造船及国防工业等各部门，代替不锈钢板。例如制造一座年产五万吨合成氨的氮肥厂设备，约需不锈钢板五十七吨，如采用不锈钢复合板代替即可节约不锈钢四十吨。　　　（李宝玉）
