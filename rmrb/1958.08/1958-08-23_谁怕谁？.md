Title: Who's Afraid of Who?
Author: Feihua
Date: 1958-08-23
Page: 8

Poetry and Painting Pairing

　　Who's Afraid of Who?
　　Feihua
　　Nadia is sentenced to death!
　　Is it the people who fear Hussein,
　　Or Hussein who fears the people?
　　Hussein hides in his palace,
　t daring to meet anyone,
　　Distrusting even his own guards.
　　Nadia is on trial in court,
　　She says she wants to blow up the palace,
　　If only she could get close.
　　Hussein immediately orders:
　　Search all the women's handbags,
　　Search them one by one for me!
　　Protests roar from around the world,
　　Don't kill Nadia!
　　Hussein hastily gives another order:
　　Confiscate all radios nationwide,
　　No broadcasts allowed,
　　Anyone who violates will pay with their life!
　　Global protests thunder like thunder,
　　Scaring Hussein so his teeth chatter,
　　Forcing him to revoke the death sentence.
　　Who is really afraid of whom?
　　Is it the people who fear the tyrant,
　　Or the little monkey who fears people? (Attached picture)
　　Hussein and Nadia　　　Ying Tao made

### 谁怕谁？
飞花
1958-08-23
第8版()
专栏：诗画配

　　谁怕谁？
　　飞花
　　纳迪亚被判死刑！
　　是人民怕侯赛因，
　　还是侯赛因怕人民？
　　侯赛因躲进深宫，
　　不敢接见任何人，
　　对自己的卫士也不信任。
　　纳迪亚在法庭受审，
　　她说她要炸毁王宫，
　　只要她能够走近。
　　侯赛因马上下令：
　　把所有妇女的手提包
　　都给我一个个地搜寻！
　　全世界传来抗议的吼声，
　　不许杀害纳迪亚！
　　侯赛因慌忙再下令：
　　没收全国收音机，
　　一切广播不许收听，
　　谁违法要谁的命！
　　全球的抗议响如雷鸣，
　　吓得侯赛因牙齿乱碰，
　　被迫撤销了死刑。
　　到底是谁怕谁？
　　是人民怕暴君，
　　还是猴崽子怕人？（附图片）
　　侯赛因和纳迪亚　　　英韬制
