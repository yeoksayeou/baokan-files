Title: French Economic Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-08-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 1st. At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, a six-member French economic delegation, led by Mr. Dumontier, Director of the French National Institute of Statistics and renowned economist, arrived in Beijing this afternoon.

Representatives from the Institute of Economics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries were at the airport to welcome them.

### 法国经济学家代表团到京

1958-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

    法国经济学家代表团到京
    新华社1日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的法国经济学家代表团一行六人，在团长法国全国统计局局长、著名经济学家杜蒙蒂埃率领下今天下午到达北京。
    中国科学院经济研究所和中国人民对外文化协会曾派代表到机场欢迎。
