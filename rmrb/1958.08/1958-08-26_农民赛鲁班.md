Title: Peasant Competes with Lu Ban
Author: Dai Sixiong
Date: 1958-08-26
Page: 3

　　Peasant Competes with Lu Ban
　　How can stars be as bright as the moon?
　　How can Zhao Qiao match Lu Ban's skill?
　　Now peasants compete with Lu Ban,
　　Creating farm tools that are clever and light.
　　　　(Dai Sixiong)

### 农民赛鲁班
戴思雄
1958-08-26
第3版()
专栏：

　　农民赛鲁班
　　星星哪有月亮明？
　　赵巧哪有鲁班行？
　　如今农民赛鲁班，
　　创造农具巧又轻。
          （戴思雄）
