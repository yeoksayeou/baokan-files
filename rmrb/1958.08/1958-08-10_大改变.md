Title: Great Change
Author: Li Hui
Date: 1958-08-10
Page: 4

    Great Change
    Li Hui
    Sister-in-law Li, Aunt Zhang,
    In the past, we chatted about everyday life,
    Now when we meet, there's a great change,
    We talk about the people-run small factories.
