Title: Yanqian Cooperative

Author:

Date: 1958-08-07
Page: 5

    Yanqian Cooperative
    The No. 1 Production Team of the Yanqian Cooperative in Tai'an Township, Leping County, Jiangxi Province, harvested 1.009 mu of early rice. After drying and winnowing, the actual yield of dry grain was more than 6,200 jin, with an average yield of 6,187 jin per mu, an increase of more than twelve times compared to last year. When this high-yield plot was harvested, the leader of the Leping Working Group of the Provincial Party Committee's Agricultural Inspection Team and the responsible comrades of the Leping County Party Committee participated in the acceptance.

### 岩前社

1958-08-07
第5版()
专栏：

    岩前社
    江西乐平县太安乡岩前社第一生产组收割的一点零零九亩早稻，晒干扬净后，实收干谷六千二百多斤，平均亩产六千一百八十七斤，比去年增产十二倍多。这块高额丰产田收割时，省委农业检查团乐平工作组组长和乐平县县委负责同志参加验收。
