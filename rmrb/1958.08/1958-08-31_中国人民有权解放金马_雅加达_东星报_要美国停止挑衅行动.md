Title: Chinese People Have the Right to Liberate Jinmen; Jakarta's "Dongxing Bao" Demands U.S. Stop Provocative Actions
Author:
Date: 1958-08-31
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 30th. "Dongxing Bao" published an editorial today pointing out that whether the crisis in the Taiwan Strait will be eliminated is not up to China to decide, but depends on whether the United States is willing to stop its military provocations and aggression in the Far East.

The editorial said that China's sovereignty and interests over Jinmen and Mazu, as well as China's right to defend its national sovereignty and the happy life of its people, are matters of vital importance to the Chinese people; therefore, removing those elements who undermine national sovereignty and independence from Jinmen and Mazu is China's right and responsibility. That is why China is bombing the Chiang Kai-shek troops on these islands.

The newspaper said that for China, whether a counterattack against the Chiang Kai-shek clique's provocative actions will develop into a major war is not a question of who should be responsible, but a question of whether it should defend national sovereignty and the happy life of its people. This is the fact that Western politicians and the U.S. Seventh Fleet cannot ignore.

### 中国人民有权解放金马  雅加达“东星报”要美国停止挑衅行动

1958-08-31
第3版()
专栏：

　　中国人民有权解放金马
    雅加达“东星报”要美国停止挑衅行动
    新华社雅加达30日电　“东星报”今天发表社论指出，台湾海峡的危机是否会消除，这不是由中国来决定的事，而是要看美国是否愿意停止它在远东的军事挑衅和侵略。
    社论说，中国对金门和马祖的主权和利益以及中国保卫自己的国家主权和人民幸福生活的权利，是中国人民的生死攸关的问题；因此，把那些破坏国家主权和独立的分子从金门和马祖清除出去，是中国的权利和责任。这就是为什么中国要炮轰这些岛屿上的蒋介石军队。
    这家报纸说，对中国来说，予蒋介石集团的挑衅行为以反击是否会发展成为大战，并不是它应否负责的问题，而是它是否要保卫国家的主权和人民的幸福生活的问题。这就是西方的政治家和美国的第七舰队所不能忽视的事实。
