Title: Argentine Ruling Party Senator Proposes Motion to Establish Diplomatic Relations with Our Country
Author:
Date: 1958-08-09
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Buenos Aires, August 8th: According to TASS, Senator Davila of the Argentine ruling party "Intransigent Radical Civic Union" has submitted a proposal to the Argentine Senate, suggesting that Argentina establish diplomatic, trade, and cultural relations with the People's Republic of China.

### 阿根廷执政党参议员提出同我国建交的议案

1958-08-09
第2版()
专栏：

    阿根廷执政党参议员
    提出同我国建交的议案
