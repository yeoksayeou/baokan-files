Title: Korean Embassy Holds Film Evening
Author:
Date: 1958-08-28
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 27th: The Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China held a film evening tonight to celebrate the 13th anniversary of the liberation of Korea and the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The attendees of the evening included people from all aspects of Chinese society, as well as foreign envoys and diplomatic officials stationed in China.
　　The Korean color feature film "The Tale of Shim Cheong" was screened at the evening.

### 朝鲜大使馆举行电影晚会

1958-08-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜大使馆举行电影晚会
    新华社27日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使馆今晚举行电影晚会，庆祝朝鲜解放十三周年和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国成立十周年。出席晚会的有中国各方面的人士、各国驻华使节和外交官员。
    晚会上放映了朝鲜彩色故事片“沈清传”。
