Title: Gromyko's Remarks at New York Airport: Current Priority is to Promote the Withdrawal of US and British Troops

Author:

Date: 1958-08-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 11th dispatch from New York: According to a TASS report from New York, the head of the Soviet delegation to the emergency session of the United Nations General Assembly, Soviet Foreign Minister Andrei Gromyko, arrived in New York on the 10th. He made the following statement to reporters at the airport:

　　The Soviet delegation has come to participate in the emergency session of the United Nations General Assembly in order to actively participate in the discussion of a very important issue. The Soviet government believed in the past and continues to believe that the issue of the withdrawal of American troops from Lebanon and British troops from Jordan is a very important issue. In order to maintain universal peace and maintain peace in the Near East, this issue must be resolved quickly. The Soviet government hopes that the General Assembly can find a positive solution to this issue, so as not to disappoint the expectations of the people of the world for this lofty institution of the United Nations. The Soviet delegation will do everything possible to help find such a positive solution.

### 葛罗米柯在纽约机场谈话　当前要务为促使美英撤军

1958-08-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　葛罗米柯在纽约机场谈话
　　当前要务为促使美英撤军
    新华社11日讯　据塔斯社纽约讯：出席联合国大会紧急会议的苏联代表团团长、苏联外交部长葛罗米柯10日抵达纽约，他在机场上对记者发表了下面的谈话：
    苏联代表团前来参加联合国大会紧急会议，是为了积极参加非常重要的问题的讨论。苏联政府过去认为并且继续认为：关于美国军队撤出黎巴嫩和英国军队撤出约旦的问题，是一个非常重要的问题。为了维护普遍和平和维护中近东和平，必须迅速解决这个问题。苏联政府希望，大会能够找出这个问题的积极解决办法，这样才不辜负世界人民对联合国这个崇高机构的期望。苏联代表团将尽一切努力来帮助找到这种积极的解决办法。
