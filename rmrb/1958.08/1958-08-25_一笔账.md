A Calculation
    During the period of the First Five-Year Plan, if the leftover slabs, wood, branches, and treetop wood from processing in the Greater Khingan Range forest area are processed and utilized, 360,000 tons of fiberboard can be produced, which can replace 1.4 million cubic meters of logs. If all the bark is peeled off to make baking adhesive, 50,000 tons of baking adhesive can be produced. If all the sawdust is chemically treated, more than 9,000 tons of industrial alcohol can be recovered.

### 一笔账

1958-08-25
第3版()
专栏：小统计

　　一笔账
    在第一个五年计划期间，大兴安岭林区如将加工剩下的板皮、木头、枝桠、梢头木加工利用，能生产出三十六万吨纤维板，可代替一百四十万立方公尺原木。如将树皮全剥下来制烤胶，可制出五万吨烤胶。如将全部锯末进行化学处理，可以收到九千吨以上的工业用酒精。
