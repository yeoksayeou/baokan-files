Title: Our People's Organizations and Cultural Figures Voice Support for the Innocent Defendants in the "Matsukawa Incident"
Author:
Date: 1958-08-17
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 16th: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the China Relief Association today sent telegrams to the Japan "Matsukawa Incident" Countermeasures Council, expressing condolences and respect to all innocent defendants in the "Matsukawa Incident" who have persisted in their struggle for nine years.
    The All-China Federation of Trade Unions said in its telegram that the "Matsukawa Incident" was a shameful conspiracy by the US-Japanese reactionaries to persecute Japan's democratic and progressive forces and suppress Japan's workers' movement. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions remitted RMB 8,000 to express its support and condolences to the Japanese worker brothers, all innocent defendants.
    The China Relief Association severely condemned in its telegram the crimes of US imperialism and the reactionary Japanese government in fabricating the "Matsukawa Incident" in order to persecute Japanese patriotic democrats. The China Relief Association also remitted RMB 6,000 to express condolences to the defendants in the "Matsukawa Incident" and their families.
    Xinhua News Agency, August 15th: China's famous writers Mao Dun, Lao She, Zhao Shuli, Zhou Libo, Xie Bingxin, as well as famous playwrights Tian Han, Ouyang Yuqian, Mei Lanfang, Yang Hansheng, and Cao Yu, today sent telegrams to the president of the Japan "Matsukawa Incident" Countermeasures Council, the famous Japanese writer Kazuo Hirotsu, and to Saburo Sugiura and others, the defendants in the "Matsukawa Incident," expressing their support for their just struggle against the illegal judgments of the Japanese court.

### 我人民团体和文化界人士　声援“松川事件”无辜被告

1958-08-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　我人民团体和文化界人士
　　声援“松川事件”无辜被告
    据新华社16日讯　中华全国总工会和中国人民救济总会今天分别致电日本“松川事件”对策协议会，向九年来坚持斗争的“松川事件”全体无辜被告表示慰问和敬意。
    中华全国总工会在电报中说，“松川事件”是美日反动派迫害日本民主进步势力、镇压日本工人运动的可耻阴谋。中华全国总工会汇去人民币八千元，表示对全体无辜被告的日本工人兄弟的支援和慰问。
    中国人民救济总会在电报中严厉谴责了美帝国主义和日本反动政府为了迫害日本爱国民主人士而捏造“松川事件”的罪行。中国人民救济总会并汇去人民币六千元，慰问“松川事件”的被告和他们的家属。
    据新华社15日讯　我国著名作家茅盾、老舍、赵树理、周立波、谢冰心以及著名戏剧家田汉、欧阳予倩、梅兰芳、阳翰笙、曹禺，今天分别致电日本“松川事件”对策协议会会长、日本著名作家广津和郎，以及“松川事件”的被告杉浦三郎等，表示支持他们为反对日本法院的非法裁判而进行的正义斗争。
