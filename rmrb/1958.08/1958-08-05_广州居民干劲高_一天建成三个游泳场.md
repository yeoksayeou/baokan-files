Title: Guangzhou Residents Show Great Enthusiasm: Three Swimming Pools Built in One Day
Author:
Date: 1958-08-05
Page: 7

Sports

    Guangzhou Residents Show Great Enthusiasm
    Three Swimming Pools Built in One Day
    (Our report) The residents of the southeast section of the Zhujiang District in Guangzhou launched a voluntary labor campaign and, after a day of hard work, built three swimming pools. Two of them are 25 meters long and 20 meters wide, and one is 50 meters long and 30 meters wide. All three swimming pools were built using the river embankment and local materials, without spending a single penny. They can accommodate four to five hundred people swimming at the same time. After the swimming pools were completed, the residents of the southeast section of the embankment also held completion and opening ceremonies.

### 广州居民干劲高  一天建成三个游泳场

1958-08-05
第7版()
专栏：体育运动

    广州居民干劲高
    一天建成三个游泳场
    本报讯　广州市珠江区东南堤段发动居民义务劳动，苦战一天，建成了三个游泳场。其中两个长二十五公尺，宽二十公尺，一个长五十公尺，宽三十公尺。三个游泳场都是利用河堤，就地取材，没有花一文钱修建起来的，可同时容纳四、五百人游泳。游泳场建成后，东南堤段居民还举行了落成和开幕典礼。
