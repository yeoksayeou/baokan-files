Title: Prime Minister Sihanouk Visits China's Atomic Reactor
Author:
Date: 1958-08-23
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 22nd. Prime Minister Sihanouk and other distinguished Cambodian guests, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premiers He Long and Chen Yi, visited China's first experimental atomic reactor and cyclotron this morning. The Prime Minister spent more than two hours touring the complete set of equipment. During the visit, China's famous physicist Qian Sanqiang gave the distinguished guests a detailed introduction.

### 西哈努克首相参观我国原子反应堆

1958-08-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　西哈努克首相参观我国原子反应堆
    新华社22日讯　西哈努克首相和柬埔寨的其他贵宾，今天上午由周恩来总理，贺龙、陈毅副总理陪同，参观了我国第一座实验性原子反应堆和回旋加速器。首相花了两个多小时的时间参观了全套设备。在参观的过程中，我国著名物理学家钱三强向贵宾们作了详细介绍。
