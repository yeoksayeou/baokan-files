Title: Liu Jiuxue Coal Mining Team Mines 2,050 Tons of Coal in One Day
Author:
Date: 1958-08-06
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, August 4th: The Liu Jiuxue Coal Mining Team, a national model worker in industry and a well-known model team for safe coal mining, created a high record of 2,050 tons of coal production per day on the 3031 working face of the Lifeng Coal Mine in the Jiaozuo Mining Area on July 31st.
    This is a new achievement they obtained after changing the original two-shift coal mining and one-shift roof caving to three-shift coal mining and a method of coal mining while roof caving, as well as promoting other advanced experiences.

### 刘九学采煤队一天采煤2050吨

1958-08-06
第6版()
专栏：

    刘九学采煤队一天采煤2050吨
    据新华社郑州4日电　全国工业劳动模范、著名的安全采煤模范队刘九学采煤队，7月31日在焦作矿区李封煤矿三○三一工作面创造日产煤二千零五十吨的高纪录。
    这是他们将原来两班采煤一班放顶，改为三班采煤；边采煤边放顶的方法，以及推广其他先进经验后得到的新成绩。
