Title: Giants Stand on the Forefront of Defending Peace
Author: Xiao San
Date: 1958-08-05
Page: 8

    Giants Stand on the Forefront of Defending Peace
    Xiao San
    What power surpasses hydrogen bombs and atomic bombs?
    Mao Zedong and Khrushchev's talks!
    What is a serious blow and warning to aggressors?
    It is the communiqué of the talks between the leaders of 800 million people!
    For whom do we cheer enthusiastically?
    For Mao Zedong and Khrushchev!
    What song shall we sing loudly?
    It is the ever closer and stronger unity between China and the Soviet Union!
    It is the firm desire of the socialist camp to defend peace!
    It is the unprecedented growth of the world's people's peace forces!
    This is our unambiguous stance:
    Prevent new wars;
    Ease international tension;
    Everyone coexist peacefully;
    Discuss matters through negotiation.
    Devils dare to drag humanity to the brink of war,
    Stop! Giants stand on the forefront of defending peace!
    There is no "vacuum" anywhere today,
    American imperialists get out! Get out!! Get out!!!
    Nowhere is your so called "backyard" anymore,
    British imperialists go back! Go back!! Go back!!!
    Warmongers! Listen to the golden voice of the giants:
    If you dare to impose war on people,
    The people will surely and completely eliminate you!
    The people will surely lay the foundation for lasting world peace!
