Title: Commemoration Gathering in Beijing's Cultural Circles on the 20th Anniversary of the Death of Albanian Revolutionary Poet Migjeni
Author:
Date: 1958-08-25
Page: 5
　　
Xinhua News Agency, August 24th: The Chinese Writers Association and the Beijing Library jointly held a commemoration meeting in the capital on the 24th to mark the 20th anniversary of the death of Albanian revolutionary poet Migjeni. Among those who attended the commemoration were well-known figures from the cultural circles of the capital, including Chu Tunan, Lao She, Xiao San, Ge Baoquan, and more than 500 literary enthusiasts. Albanian Ambassador to China, Bali Li, First Secretary Bino, and poet Çaci were also invited to attend the commemoration.
    Translator Ge Baoquan gave a detailed introduction at the meeting to the life and works of Migjeni, an outstanding revolutionary poet of Albania in the 1930s. Poet Xiao San said at the meeting: We are gathered here today to commemorate this outstanding poet to express our love and respect for him, and also to learn from him. We are proud to have such an outstanding pen pal.
    Albanian writer and poet Aleks Çaci said at the meeting: Today's gathering is a vivid proof of the tremendous changes that have taken place in the world. Commemorating an Albanian poet in China, thousands of miles away from Albania; commemorating Lu Xun and the great Chinese playwright Guan Hanqing of the thirteenth century in Albania. This fact shows that the brotherly unity and friendship between us is permanent and firm.

### 阿革命诗人米吉安尼逝世二十周年  北京文化界集会纪念

1958-08-25
第5版()
专栏：

　　阿革命诗人米吉安尼逝世二十周年
    北京文化界集会纪念
    据新华社24日讯　中国作家协会与北京图书馆24日在首都联合举行了阿尔巴尼亚革命诗人米吉安尼逝世二十周年纪念会。参加纪念会的有首都文化界著名人士楚图南、老舍、萧三、戈宝权等及文学爱好者共五百余人。阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使巴利里、一等秘书比诺和诗人恰奇都应邀出席了纪念会。
    翻译家戈宝权在会上详细介绍了阿尔巴尼亚二十世纪三十年代杰出的革命诗人米吉安尼的创作与生平。诗人萧三在会上说：我们今天集会纪念这位杰出的诗人是表示对他的敬爱，也是为了向他学习，我们以有这样一位卓越的笔友而自豪。
    阿尔巴尼亚作家和诗人阿列克斯·恰奇在会上讲话说：今天的集会是世界上已发生巨大变化的一次生动证明。在离阿尔巴尼亚有数万里之遥的中国纪念阿尔巴尼亚诗人；在阿尔巴尼亚纪念鲁迅和中国十三世纪伟大的剧作家关汉卿。这个事实说明我们之间兄弟般的团结和友谊是永久的和牢固的。
