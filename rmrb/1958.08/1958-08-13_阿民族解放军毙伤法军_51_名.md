Title: Algerian National Liberation Army Kills or Wounds 51 French Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1958-08-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Algiers, 12th: The Algerian National Liberation Army killed seven French colonial soldiers and wounded forty-four others in western Algeria on August 9th alone.

　　A vehicle traveling between Sidi Bel Abbès and Janto was shot at by insurgent forces, killing five French soldiers.

　　In a battle in Saida, two French soldiers were killed and twenty-four wounded. On the railway line between Bouktoub and Krallafa, the Algerian National Liberation Army caused a train to derail after it struck a mine, resulting in the death of three French soldiers and interrupting rail transport for twenty-four hours.

### 阿民族解放军毙伤法军51名

1958-08-13
第6版()
专栏：

　  阿民族解放军毙伤法军51名
    据新华社12日讯　阿尔及尔消息：仅在8月9日这一天，阿尔及利亚民族解放军在阿尔及利亚西部就击毙法国殖民军七人，击伤法军四十四人。
    在西迪倍尔阿卑斯和见多之间往来行驶的汽车遭到了起义部队的射击，法军五人被击毙。
    在赛伊达发生的战斗中，法军二人被击毙，伤二十四人。在布克图卜和克拉勒法拉之间的铁路线上，阿尔及利亚民族解放军使列车触雷出轨，结果有法军三人死亡，铁路运输因此中断了二十四小时。
