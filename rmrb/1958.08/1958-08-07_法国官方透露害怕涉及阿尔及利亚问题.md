Title: French Officials Reveal Fear of Involving the Algerian Question
Author:
Date: 1958-08-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 6th: French officials' initial reaction to the Soviet Union's request for an emergency United Nations General Assembly meeting has been ambiguous.

The French Minister of Information, Soustelle, said at a press conference on the 6th that the Soviet proposal "seems unrealistic to the French government."

Soustelle revealed that the French government is very afraid of convening a United Nations General Assembly to discuss the Middle East situation. He said, "In a United Nations General Assembly where many support Arab countries, any discussion of the Middle East issue would inevitably add the Algerian issue, which is unacceptable to France."

Soustelle made these remarks to reporters after a cabinet meeting at which the French Foreign Minister, Couve de Murville, reported on Khrushchev's letter to De Gaulle on the 5th.

### 法国官方透露害怕涉及阿尔及利亚问题

1958-08-07
第1版()
专栏：

    法国官方透露害怕涉及阿尔及利亚问题
    新华社6日讯　巴黎消息：法国官方对苏联要求举行联合国大会紧急会议的初步反应是态度暧昧。
    法国政府宣传部长苏斯戴尔6日在记者招待会上说，苏联这个建议在“法国政府看来是不现实的”。
    苏斯戴尔透露法国政府很害怕召开讨论中东局势的联合国大会。他说，“在很多人支持阿拉伯国家的联合国大会内，任何中东问题的讨论必然会附加上阿尔及利亚问题，而这是法国不能接受的。”
    苏斯戴尔是在一次内阁会议后对记者这样说的，在那次会上，法国外交部长德姆维尔报告了赫鲁晓夫5日给戴高乐的信件。
