Title: UAR Delegation Visits Democratic Germany
Author:
Date: 1958-08-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 24th: A ten-member delegation from the United Arab Republic, led by Minister of Industry Sidqi, left Cairo for Berlin on the 23rd at the invitation of the government of the German Democratic Republic to discuss the issue of loans from Democratic Germany to the United Arab Republic.

### 阿联代表团访民主德国

1958-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　阿联代表团访民主德国
    据新华社开罗24日电　阿拉伯联合共和国十人代表团由工业部长西德基率领，应德意志民主共和国政府的邀请在23日离开罗前往柏林，讨论民主德国给予阿拉伯联合共和国贷款的问题。
