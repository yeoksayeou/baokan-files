Title: Collective Living is Good

Author:

Date: 1958-08-28
Page: 8

　　Collective Living is Good
　　The east wind blows and a hundred flowers bloom,
　　Everyone loves the public canteen.
　　Collective living is good,
　　It brings us happiness.
　　Thanks to the Party's General Line,
　　Like a bright lamp shining.
　　Collectivism is a priceless treasure,
　　Pass it on to future generations. (Attached picture)

### 集体生活就是好

1958-08-28
第8版()
专栏：

　　集体生活就是好
　　东风吹透百花开，
　　公共食堂人人爱。
　　集体生活就是好，
　　它给咱带幸福来。
　　感谢党的总路线，
　　好比明灯放光彩。
　　集体主义无价宝，
　　传给子孙万万代。（附图片）
