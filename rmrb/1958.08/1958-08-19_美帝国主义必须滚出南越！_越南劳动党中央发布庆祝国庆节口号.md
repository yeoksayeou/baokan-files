Title: U.S. Imperialism Must Get Out of South Vietnam! The Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party Issues Slogans to Celebrate National Day
Author:
Date: 1958-08-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 18th: The Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party recently issued slogans to celebrate the 13th anniversary of the August Revolution in Vietnam (August 19th) and the proclamation of independence of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam (National Day) on September 2nd.

The slogans demand that U.S. imperialism get out of South Vietnam and warmly welcome the success of the Iraqi revolution.

The slogans express the determination of the Vietnamese people to strengthen unity with the people of socialist countries led by the Soviet Union and the people of the world. The slogans also express gratitude to the Soviet Union, China, and other brotherly countries for their assistance to the Vietnamese people.

The slogans emphasize that the entire Vietnamese people must unite as one and strive for the peace, unification, independence, democracy, and prosperity of Vietnam. The slogans point out that the people of North Vietnam must work hard to gradually bring North Vietnam into a socialist society in order to build a solid foundation for a unified country; they must strengthen the people's democratic dictatorship based on the worker-peasant alliance to ensure the victory of socialist construction in North Vietnam.

The slogans call on people of all strata in Vietnam to study politics and culture diligently; workers should strive to complete or overfulfill state plans in a faster, better, more economical way; peasants should actively consolidate and develop the mutual aid and cooperation movement; cadres and staff members of organizations must improve work efficiency, oppose bureaucracy and waste, corruption, and actively participate in socialist labor.

The slogans express warm support for the struggle of the South Vietnamese people against the oppression and exploitation by the United States and the Ngo Dinh Diem authorities, demanding improved living standards, demanding freedom and democracy, demanding the establishment of normal relations between North and South Vietnam, and demanding national unification through consultation.

### 美帝国主义必须滚出南越！　越南劳动党中央发布庆祝国庆节口号

1958-08-19
第5版()
专栏：

　　美帝国主义必须滚出南越！
　　越南劳动党中央发布庆祝国庆节口号
    新华社河内18日电　越南劳动党中央委员会最近发布庆祝8月19日越南八月革命和9月2日越南民主共和国宣告独立（国庆节）十三周年的口号。
    口号要求美帝国主义滚出南越、并且热烈欢迎伊拉克革命成功。
    口号表示了越南人民同以苏联为首的社会主义阵营各国人民和世界人民加强团结的决心。口号还表示感谢苏联、中国和其它兄弟国家给予越南人民的援助。
    口号强调越南全国人民必须团结一致，为争取越南的和平、统一、独立、民主和富强而努力奋斗。口号指出，越南北方人民必须努力促使越南北方逐步进入社会主义社会，以建立统一国家的稳固基础；必须加强以工农联盟为基础的人民民主专政的政权，以保证在越南北方建设社会主义的胜利。
    口号号召越南各阶层人民努力学习政治和文化；工人们要努力多、快、好、省地完成或超额完成国家计划；农民们要积极巩固和发展变工合作运动；机关干部和职工必须提高工作效率，反对官僚主义和浪费、贪污现象，并且积极参加社会主义劳动。
    口号表示热烈支持南越人民反抗美国和吴庭艳当局的压迫和剥削、要求改善生活、要求自由民主、要求越南北方和南方建立正常关系、要求通过协商以实现国家统一而进行的斗争。
