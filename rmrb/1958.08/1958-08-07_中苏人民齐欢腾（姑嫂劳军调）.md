Title: The Chinese and Soviet People Rejoice Together (Sister-in-law's Army Appreciation Tune)
Author: Gao Zhongxing
Date: 1958-08-07
Page: 8

    The sky is clear for ten thousand miles,
    Khrushchev arrives in Beijing,
    Meets with our Chairman Mao,
    Discussing major international events.
    The leaders of the two countries smile,
    Hundreds of millions of people rejoice together,
    The communiqué of the talks is powerful,
    World peace is guaranteed.
    The oil lamp flickers before it goes out,
    The dying duck flaps its wings,
    Imperialism is about to end,
    Aggression in the Middle East provokes world war.
    Peace doves fly all over the sky,
    People all over the world smile,
    War mongers, you do not know when to retreat,
    Like moths flying into a flame.

### 中苏人民齐欢腾（姑嫂劳军调）
高中兴
1958-08-07
第8版()
专栏：

    中苏人民齐欢腾（姑嫂劳军调）
    全国曲艺会演黑龙江省代表团　高中兴
    万里无云天响晴，
    赫鲁晓夫到北京，
    会见咱们毛主席，
    会谈国际大事情。
    两国领袖笑盈盈，
    亿万人民齐欢腾，
    会谈公报力量大，
    世界和平有保证。
    油灯将灭火一窜，
    鸭子临死把翅膀扇，
    帝国主义要完蛋，
    侵略中东挑拨世界战。
    和平鸽子满天飞，
    全世界的人民笑微微，
    战争贩子你不知进退，
    好似飞蛾扑火堆。
