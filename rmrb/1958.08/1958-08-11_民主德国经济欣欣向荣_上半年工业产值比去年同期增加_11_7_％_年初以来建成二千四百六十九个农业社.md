Title: The Economy of the German Democratic Republic is Thriving: Industrial Output in the First Half of the Year Increased by 11.7% Compared to the Same Period Last Year; 2,469 Agricultural Cooperatives Established Since the Beginning of the Year
Author:
Date: 1958-08-11
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin Dispatch: The total industrial output of the German Democratic Republic in the first half of 1958 increased by 11.7% compared to the same period in 1957 (exceeding the planned target by 4%), and labor productivity increased by 9.6%. This was announced in the communique on the implementation of the national economic plan for the first half of 1958, published today by the State Central Bureau of Statistics of the German Democratic Republic.

The communique shows the production growth of centrally-run state-owned enterprises as follows: coal 1%, mining and metallurgy departments 8%, chemical industry departments 10%, general machinery manufacturing 16%, electrical machinery 18%, textiles, clothing, and leather 9%, and the food industry 25%. The production growth of local state-owned enterprises averaged 12% in the first half of 1958.

The communique states: The production of consumer goods in the first half of 1958 increased compared to the same period last year: light motorcycles by 21.1%, motorcycles by 33.6%, refrigerators by 99.8%, portable radios by 145%, televisions by 82.9%, Perlon fiber by 63.7%, butter by 15.6%, milk by 18%, meat by 10.2%, eggs by 6%, and cocoa products by 28.3%.

According to a report by the German News Agency on the 9th, in the month of July alone, the total number of agricultural production cooperatives in the German Democratic Republic increased from 8,069 to 8,750, an increase of 681; the cultivated land area of agricultural cooperatives increased from 1,926,796 hectares (each hectare equals 15 mu) to 2,049,285 hectares. Since the beginning of this year, a total of 2,469 agricultural cooperatives have been added in the German Democratic Republic.

### 民主德国经济欣欣向荣　上半年工业产值比去年同期增加11.7％　年初以来建成二千四百六十九个农业社

1958-08-11
第7版()
专栏：

　　民主德国经济欣欣向荣
　　上半年工业产值比去年同期增加11.7％
　　年初以来建成二千四百六十九个农业社
    据新华社柏林电　德意志民主共和国1958年上半年的工业总产值比1957年同期增加了11.7％（超过计划指标4％），劳动生产率增长了9.6％。这是民主德国国家中央统计局今天发表的1958年上半年国民经济计划执行情况公报中宣布的。
    公报表明，中央国营企业的生产增长情况是：煤1％，采矿和冶金部门8％，化学工业部门10％，一般机器制造业16％，电机18％，纺织、服装和皮革9％，食品工业25％。地方国营企业在1958年上半年内的生产增长平均达到12％。
    公报说：1958年上半年消费品的生产比去年同期增长：轻便机器脚踏车21.1％，摩托车33.6％，冰箱99.8％，手提式收音机145％，电视机82.9％，派隆丝纤维63.7％，牛油15.6％，牛奶18％，肉10.2％，鸡蛋6％，可可制品28.3％。
    据德意志通讯社9日报道，在7月一个月中，德意志民主共和国的农业生产合作社总数从八千零六十九个增加到八千七百五十个，共增加了六百八十一个；农业合作社的耕地面积从一百九十二万六千七百九十六公顷（每公顷等于十五亩）增加到了二百零四万九千二百八十五公顷。今年年初以来，民主德国的农业合作社一共增加了二千四百六十九个。
