Title: Our Heavenly Eagles Shot Down a Chiang Kai-shek Plane into the Sea
Author:
Date: 1958-08-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Coastal Defense Front, August 26th: On August 25th, the People's Liberation Army Air Force fighter jet unit on the coastal defense front shot down an F86-type US-made Chiang Kai-shek plane. At 5:17 PM on August 25th, eight US-made Chiang Kai-shek F86 jet fighters intruded into the airspace over Weitou and Zhangzhou in Fujian Province to carry out provocative activities. Our People's Liberation Army Air Force fighter jets immediately took off and dealt them a head-on blow, shooting down one of them. The downed Chiang Kai-shek plane immediately crashed into the sea eight nautical miles southeast of Weitou.

### 我天鹰把蒋机一架打落大海

1958-08-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　我天鹰把蒋机一架打落大海
　　新华社海防前线26日电　海防前线中国人民解放军空军歼击机部队25日击落F86型美制蒋机一架。25日下午五时十七分，美制蒋机F86型喷气战斗机八架，窜入我福建省围头、漳州等地上空进行挑衅活动，我人民解放军空军歼击机立即起飞予以迎头痛击，击落其中一架。这架被击落的蒋机当即坠入围头东南八浬的大海中。
