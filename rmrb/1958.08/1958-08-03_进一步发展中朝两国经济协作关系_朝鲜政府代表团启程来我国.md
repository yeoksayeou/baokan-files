Title: To Further Develop Economic Cooperation Between China and North Korea: North Korean Government Delegation Departs for China
Author:
Date: 1958-08-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 2nd. A government delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea left Pyongyang by train this morning for Beijing. The delegation will hold talks with the Chinese government on developing and further strengthening economic cooperation between the two countries.

The head of the delegation is Vice Premier of the Korean Cabinet, Li Joo-yeon, and the members include Li Jong-ok, Chairman of the State Planning Commission, Kim Choi-seon, Deputy Minister of Foreign and Domestic Commerce, and Li Yong-ho, the North Korean Ambassador to China. Li Fanru, Commercial Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in North Korea, accompanied the delegation to Beijing.

Responsible cadres from the Korean Party and government were at the station to see them off today. Cao Keqiang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in North Korea, and embassy staff were also at the station to see them off.

### 进一步发展中朝两国经济协作关系  朝鲜政府代表团启程来我国

1958-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    进一步发展中朝两国经济协作关系
    朝鲜政府代表团启程来我国
    新华社平壤2日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团今天上午乘火车离平壤前往北京。代表团将同中国政府进行关于为了发展进一步加强两国的经济协作的会谈。
    代表团的团长是朝鲜内阁副首相李周渊，团员有国家计划委员会委员长李锺玉、对内外商业省副相金最善和朝鲜驻中国大使李永镐。中国驻朝鲜大使馆商务参赞李范如陪同代表团前往北京。
  今天到车站送行的有朝鲜党和政府的负责干部。中国驻朝鲜大使馆临时代办曹克强和使馆人员也到车站送行。
