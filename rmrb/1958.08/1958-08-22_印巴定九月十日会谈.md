Title: India and Pakistan to Hold Talks on September 10th
Author: 
Date: 1958-08-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: According to an official Indian announcement on the 20th, Indian Prime Minister Nehru and Pakistani Prime Minister Noon have decided to hold talks in New Delhi on September 10th to discuss border issues.

### 印巴定九月十日会谈

1958-08-22
第5版()
专栏：

    印巴定九月十日会谈
    据新华社讯  据印度官方20日宣布，印度总理尼赫鲁和巴基斯坦总理努恩已经决定9月10日在新德里就边境问题举行会谈。
