Title: Newly Discovered Iceland Spar
Author: Fang Dagen
Date: 1958-08-25
Page: 2

Column: Motherland Has Treasures Everywhere

    Newly Discovered Iceland Spar
    Iceland spar has been discovered in Kaihua County, Zhejiang Province. After repeated exploration, it has been proven that the county has large reserves of Iceland spar, and the quality is very good.
    Iceland spar is an important raw material for the optical instrument industry; it is a rare and precious mineral in the world.
                  (Fang Dagen)

### 新发现的冰洲石
方大根
1958-08-25
第2版()
专栏：祖国到处有宝藏

    新发现的冰洲石
    浙江省开化县发现冰洲石。经多次勘察证明，这个县的冰洲石蕴藏量大、质量很好。
    冰洲石是光学仪器工业的重要原料；是世界上稀少而又宝贵的矿石。
                  （方大根）
