Title: The People of the Motherland are with You: National and Guangzhou Educational Trade Union, Student Federation, Journalists Association and other organizations send telegrams to express condolences to teachers and students of Hong Kong Chung Wah Middle School and beaten journalists

Author:

Date: 1958-08-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 28th: The National Committee of the Educational Trade Union of China sent a telegram on the 27th to express condolences to all teachers and students of Hong Kong Chung Wah Middle School.
    The telegram stated, "In addition to lodging a serious protest with the British government, we extend our deep condolences to you and express to you that all educators and all people in the motherland firmly support your just struggle."
    Xinhua News Agency, August 28th: The All-China Students' Federation today sent a telegram to express condolences to all students of Hong Kong Chung Wah Middle School. The telegram stated: Loving the motherland, hanging the national flag of the motherland, singing the national anthem of the motherland, and using textbooks published by the motherland for learning are the sacred and inviolable basic rights of Chinese students in Hong Kong and Kowloon. Your struggle is not isolated. All the students in China are with you, and 600 million Chinese people are with you. The era of imperialists acting rampant and domineering towards the Chinese people is long gone; the great People's Republic of China is already a world power. Any imperialist elements who underestimate the strength of the Chinese people and dare to be enemies with the Chinese people are bound to be completely defeated!
    Xinhua News Agency, August 28th: The All-China Journalists Association sent telegrams on the 27th to the Hong Kong branch of Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong Ta Kung Pao, Hong Kong Wen Wei Po, and Huang Wenfang of the New Evening Post, expressing their cordial sympathy and condolences to them. The telegram of condolences stated: "The All-China Journalists Association expresses deep sympathy and condolences to the beaten journalists and firmly supports your protests to the British authorities in Hong Kong. We firmly support all your just struggles to carry out news gathering activities, safeguard personal safety and freedom rights, and oppose persecution."
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, August 29th: The Guangdong Provincial and Guangzhou Municipal Educational Trade Unions, the Guangzhou Municipal Student Federation, and the Guangzhou Branch of the All-China Journalists Association yesterday sent letters and telegrams to express condolences to the teachers and students of Chung Wah Middle School and Chinese journalists in Hong Kong who were beaten and humiliated by the British police.

### 祖国人民和你们在一起  全国和广州市教育工会学联记者协会等团体  分别致电慰问香港中华中学师生和被殴记者

1958-08-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　祖国人民和你们在一起
    全国和广州市教育工会学联记者协会等团体
    分别致电慰问香港中华中学师生和被殴记者
    新华社28日讯　中国教育工会全国委员会27日打电报慰问香港中华中学全体师生。
    电报说，“我们除了向英国政府提出严重抗议外，特向你们致以深切的慰问，并向你们表示：祖国的全体教育工作者和全体人民坚决支持你们正义的斗争。”
    据新华社28日讯　中华全国学生联合会今天打电报慰问香港中华中学全体同学。电报中说：热爱祖国，悬挂祖国的国旗，唱祖国国歌，用祖国出版的教科书进行学习是港九中国学生神圣不可侵犯的基本权利。你们的斗争不是孤立的，全中国的同学和你们在一起，六亿中国人民和你们在一起。帝国主义者对中国人民横行霸道的时代早已一去不返；伟大的中华人民共和国已经是世界强国。任何低估中国人民的力量，胆敢与中国人民为敌的帝国主义分子是一定要彻底失败的！
    新华社28日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会27日打电报给新华社香港分社、香港大公报、香港文汇报、新晚报黄文放等人，对他们表示亲切的同情和慰问。慰问电报表示：“中华全国新闻工作者协会对被殴打的记者表示深切的同情和慰问，坚决支持你们向香港英国当局提出的抗议。我们坚决支持你们为进行新闻采访活动、维护人身安全与自由权利、反对迫害而进行的一切正义斗争。”
    新华社广州29日电　广东省、广洲市教育工会、广州市学联和中华全国新闻工作者协会广州分会，昨天分别致函致电慰问香港受英国警察殴辱的中华中学师生和中国新闻记者。
