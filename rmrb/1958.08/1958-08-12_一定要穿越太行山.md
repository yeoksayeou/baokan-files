Title: We Must Cross the Taihang Mountains
Author: 
Date: 1958-08-12
Page: 3

The construction of the 385-kilometer-long Zhandong Railway, which runs west from Zhan Dian Station on the Beijing-Guangzhou Railway, passes through the Taihang Mountains, and connects to Dongguan Station on the Tongpu Railway line, has begun in full swing.

This railway must cross 30 kilometers of the Taihang Mountains and pass through dozens of tunnels, making the project extremely difficult.

Right picture: The 950-meter-long Dongtiecun No. 2 Tunnel is the longest tunnel crossing the Taihang Mountains.

### 一定要穿越太行山

1958-08-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　一定要穿越太行山
    从京广铁路上的詹店车站向西穿过太行山与同浦铁路线上的东观车站相接，全长三百八十五公里的詹东铁路开始全面施工。
    这条铁路，要穿过三十公里的太行山，打通数十个隧道，工程十分艰巨。
    右图：九百五十公尺长的东铁村二号隧道，是横穿太行山最长的一条隧道。
　　　　　　　　　　　  新华社记者  唐茂林摄
