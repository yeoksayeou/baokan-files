Title: Halt Hussein's Slaughter! World Opinion Condemns the Jordanian Authorities for Slaughtering Patriots
Author:
Date: 1958-08-21
Page: 4

　　Stop Hussein's Slaughter!
　　World Opinion Condemns the Jordanian Authorities for Slaughtering Patriots
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 19th – The Secretariat of the Women's International Democratic Federation sent a telegram today to UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld, angrily protesting the brutal act of the Jordanian reactionary government, under the instigation of imperialism, in sentencing Nadia and a group of young Jordanian patriots to death, and demanding that the United Nations intervene.
    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 18th – The World Federation of Democratic Youth sent telegrams to the United Nations General Assembly, the United Nations Human Rights Commission, and youth organizations around the world, calling on them to take joint action to stop the Jordanian authorities from sentencing young Jordanian patriots to death and life imprisonment.
    The World Federation of Democratic Youth also sent a telegram to the Prime Minister of Jordan, protesting the Jordanian authorities' crimes of persecuting patriotic youth. The telegram demanded that the Jordanian authorities immediately revoke the death and life imprisonment sentences against patriotic youth, and cease large-scale arrests and unjust verdicts. ### 制止侯赛因的屠刀！世界舆论谴责约旦当局屠杀爱国者

1958-08-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　制止侯赛因的屠刀！
　　世界舆论谴责约旦当局屠杀爱国者
    据新华社柏林19日电  国际民主妇女联合会书记处今天打电报给联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德，愤怒抗议约旦反动政府在帝国主义指使下判处纳迪亚等一批约旦青年爱国者死刑的残暴行为，并且要求联合国进行干预。
    据新华社布达佩斯18日电  世界民主青年联盟打电报给联合国大会、联合国人权委员会和全世界的青年组织，呼吁它们共同采取行动，来制止约旦当局对约旦爱国青年判处死刑和无期徒刑的行为。
    世界民主青年联盟同时还打电报给约旦首相，抗议约旦当局迫害爱国青年的罪行。电报要求约旦当局立即撤销对爱国青年的死刑和无期徒刑的判决，并且停止大规模的逮捕和不公正的判决。（附图片）
    约旦女爱国青年纳迪亚·萨勒提在押赴法庭途中。
                        （新华社稿）
