Title: Tongxu Cooperative

Author:

Date: 1958-08-12
Page: 2

    Tongxu Cooperative
    (Xinhua News Agency) The Tongxu Cooperative in Tongcheng County, Anhui Province, harvested a total of 4,953,000 jin of dry rice from its 1,500 mu of early rice, averaging 3,302 jin per mu, an increase of more than seven times the per-mu yield of early rice last year. Among them, 2.4 mu of early rice yielded an average of 8,950 jin per mu.

### 桐圩社

1958-08-12
第2版()
专栏：

    桐圩社
    据新华社讯　安徽省桐城县桐圩社全社一千五百亩早稻，共收干谷四百九十五万三千斤，平均亩产三千三百零二斤，比去年早稻的亩产量增加七倍多。其中有二点四亩早稻，平均亩产八千九百五十斤。
