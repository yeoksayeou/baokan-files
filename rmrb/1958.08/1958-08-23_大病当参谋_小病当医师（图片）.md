Title: Act as a Consultant for Serious Illnesses, Act as a Doctor for Minor Ailments (Picture)
Author: Sun Zhongjing
Date: 1958-08-23
Page: 3

Market Scene

　　Act as a Consultant for Serious Illnesses, Act as a Doctor for Minor Ailments
　　The clerks of the Chongqing Pharmacy Outpatient Department broke with convention and established a "Free Clinic Service Room," implementing "selling goods according to symptoms" and the service method of "acting as a consultant for serious illnesses and acting as a doctor for minor ailments." This is salesperson Sun Zhenshan giving a patient a stethoscope examination.　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Sun Zhongjing photo

### 大病当参谋　小病当医师（图片）
孙忠靖
1958-08-23
第3版()
专栏：市场新貌

　　大病当参谋　小病当医师
　　重庆市药房门市部的营业员打破常规，设立了“义务诊疗服务室”，实行了“对症售货”和“大病当参谋，小病当医师”的服务办法。这是营业员孙镇山在给病人听诊。　　　新华社记者　孙忠靖摄
