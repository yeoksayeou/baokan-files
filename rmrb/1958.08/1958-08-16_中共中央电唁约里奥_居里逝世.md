Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Condolences on the Death of Joliot-Curie
Author:
Date: 1958-08-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th August. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China today sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the French Communist Party, expressing condolences on the death of Comrade Joliot-Curie. The telegram reads as follows:
To the Central Committee of the French Communist Party:

We were deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate death of Comrade Joliot-Curie, a loyal son of the French working class, member of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party, a renowned contemporary scientist, and Chairman of the World Peace Council, on August 14th. We express our deepest condolences.

Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
August 15, 1958

### 中共中央电唁约里奥—居里逝世

1958-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央电唁约里奥—居里逝世
    新华社15日讯　中国共产党中央委员会今天致电法国共产党中央委员会，吊唁约里奥—居里同志的逝世，电文如下：法国共产党中央委员会：
    惊闻法国工人阶级忠实的儿子、法国共产党中央委员、当代著名的科学家、世界和平理事会主席约里奥—居里同志于8月14日不幸逝世，我们不胜悲悼之至，特电吊唁。
                      中国共产党中央委员会
                                1958年8月15日
