Title: When rapeseed flowers bloom, bees come near

Author: 

Date: 1958-08-11
Page: 5

When rapeseed flowers bloom, bees come near.
When rapeseed flowers bloom, bees come near,
New and old peasants are one family.
You teach me farm work,
I bring culture to your door.
　　——Collected by Zhang Jiaqi, Jiangsu

### 菜籽开花蜜蜂亲

1958-08-11
第5版()
