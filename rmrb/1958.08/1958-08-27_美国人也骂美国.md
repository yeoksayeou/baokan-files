Title: Americans Also Scold America
Author: Chun Cheng
Date: 1958-08-27
Page: 8

　　Poetry and Painting Accompaniment

　　Americans Also Scold America
　　Chun Cheng
    The old-line American bourgeois magazine "The New Yorker" recently published a cartoon titled "Seriously Ill Patient." Below the picture is a line of text, which is what the patient, symbolizing America, said to the doctor, translated as: "Doctor, everyone hates me."
    This is the truth. American imperialism is the public enemy of the people of the world, hated and spat upon by everyone. Even the American bourgeois press cannot conceal this fact, and even wants to use self-inflicted blows to ridicule itself. It is truly:
　　The whole world scolds America,
　　Americans also scold America.
　　American imperialism is like a rat crossing the street,
　　Everyone scolds it.
　　Bases are spread all over the southeast, northwest,
　　Missiles are shipped to Japan and West Germany.
　　Arson, subversion, robbery,
　　Threats, enticements, intimidation.
　　In the past, it fought for its own independence,
　　"Indirect aggression" had not been heard of.
　　Now that others are fighting for independence,
　　It arrogantly interferes.
　　American imperialism! American imperialism!
　　The source of all evils in the world,
　　The number one poisonous weed under heaven.
　　Poisonous weeds must be hoeed away as fertilizer,
　　Every farmer understands.

### 美国人也骂美国
春城
1958-08-27
第8版()
专栏：诗画配

　　美国人也骂美国
　　春城
    美国老牌资产阶级杂志“纽约客”，最近登了一幅漫画，题为“重病号”。图下有一行字，是那个象征美国的病人向医生说的话，译为：“医生，每一个人都恨我。”
    这是实话。美帝国主义是世界人民的公敌，人人痛恨，个个唾骂，即使美国资产阶级报刊也无法掩盖这一事实，甚至想用自打嘴巴的办法来自我解嘲了。真是：
　　全世界都骂美国，
　　美国人也骂美国。
　　美帝是只过街鼠，
　　人人骂得。
　　基地布满东南西北，
　　导弹运往日本西德。
　　放火颠复抢劫，
　　威胁利诱恫吓。
　　从前他自己闹独立，
　　“间接侵略”并没有听说。
　　现在人家闹独立，
　　他就蛮横干涉。
　　美帝！美帝！
　　人间万恶之源，
　　天下毒草第一。
　　毒草要锄去当肥料，
　　庄稼人个个懂得。
