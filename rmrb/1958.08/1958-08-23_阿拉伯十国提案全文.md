Title: Full Text of the Ten-Nation Arab Proposal
Author:
Date: 1958-08-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, August 22nd: The full text of the proposal regarding the Middle East situation submitted by Iraq, Jordan, Lebanon, Libya, Morocco, Saudi Arabia, Sudan, Tunisia, the United Arab Republic, and Yemen, which was adopted at the special emergency session of the United Nations General Assembly on the afternoon of August 21, 1958, is as follows:

    “The General Assembly,

    Having considered the item 'The question discussed by the Security Council at its 838th meeting on 7 August 1958';

    Noting the purpose revealed in the Charter that nations should 'practice tolerance and live together in peace with one another as good neighbours';

    Noting that the Arab States, in the Pact of the League of Arab States, 'have agreed to strengthen the close relations and numerous ties linking the Arab States, to support and stabilize these ties upon a basis of respect for the independence and sovereignty of these States, and to direct their efforts toward the common welfare of all the Arab countries, toward the improvement of their status, toward the security of their future, and toward the realization of their aspirations and hopes';

    Desiring to relieve international tension.

    A.

    1. Welcomes the renewed assurance by the Arab States of their continued adherence to the provisions of Article 8 of the Pact of the League of Arab States, which provides that 'Each member State shall respect the system of government established in the other member States and regard it as their exclusive concern,' and that 'Each member State pledges to abstain from any action calculated to change established systems of government.'

    2. Requests all Member States to act strictly in accordance with the principles of mutual respect for each other's territorial integrity and sovereignty, of non-aggression, of strict non-interference in each other's internal affairs, and of equal rights and mutual benefit, and to ensure that their conduct in the realm of international relations conforms to these principles.

    B.

    Requests the Secretary-General, in consultation with the Governments concerned, and in accordance with the Charter, and bearing in mind the considerations put forward in the preamble of this resolution, to make forthwith such practical arrangements as would adequately help in relation to Lebanon and Jordan in the present circumstances towards upholding the purposes and principles of the Charter, in order to facilitate the early withdrawal of foreign troops from the two countries.

    C.
    Invited the Secretary-General to continue his studies now under way and in this context to consult as appropriate with the Arab countries of the Near East concerning possible assistance regarding an Arab development institution designed to promote economic growth in these countries.

    D.

    1. Requests Member States to co-operate fully in carrying out this resolution.

    2. Requests the Secretary-General to report hereon as appropriate, the first such report to be made not later than 30 September 1958.”

### 阿拉伯十国提案全文

1958-08-23
第5版()
专栏：

　　阿拉伯十国提案全文
    新华社22日讯　纽约消息：联合国大会特别紧急会议1958年8月21日下午会议上通过的伊拉克、约旦、黎巴嫩、利比亚、摩洛哥、沙特阿拉伯、苏丹、突尼斯、阿拉伯联合共和国和也门关于中东局势的提案全文如下：
    “大会
    在考虑了“安全理事会在1958年8月7日第八三八次会议上讨论的问题”这一项目之后；
    注意到宪章所揭示的各国应当“力行容恕，彼此以善邻之道，和睦相处”的宗旨；
    注意到，阿拉伯国家在阿拉伯国家联盟公约中“一致同意加强联系着阿拉伯国家的密切关系和许多联系，并且在尊重这些国家独立和主权的基础上支持和稳定这些连系，致力于全体阿拉伯国家的共同福利、它们的状况的改善、它们的将来的安全和它们的意愿和希望的实现”；
    希望缓和国际紧张局势。
    甲
    一、欢迎阿拉伯国家重新保证遵守阿拉伯国家联盟公约第八条的规定：“每个成员国应尊重其他成员国建立的政府制度，并认为这种政府制度完全是这些国家自己的事情”，而且“每个成员国应保证不采取目的在于改变既定的政府制度的任何行动”。
    二、要求所有会员国严格按照互相尊重领土完整和主权、互不侵犯、严格地互不干涉内政和平等互利的原则行事，并且保证它们的行为在言论上和行动上符合于这些原则。
    乙
    责成秘书长在同有关政府磋商的情况下，并按照宪章以及在考虑到本决议甲项部分的情况下，立即作出实际安排以便在目前情况下在黎巴嫩和约旦的问题上，充分帮助维护宪章的宗旨和原则，从而便于外国军队早日撤出这两个国家。
    丙
    请秘书长继续进行他现在进行的研究并且在这种情况下在适当的时候同近东阿拉伯国家进行磋商，以便在一个目的在于促进这些国家的经济发展的阿拉伯发展机构方面提供可能的援助。
    丁
    一、要求会员国进行充分合作来执行本决议。
    二、请秘书长据此在适当的时候提出报告，第一个这样的报告不得迟于1958年9月30日。”
