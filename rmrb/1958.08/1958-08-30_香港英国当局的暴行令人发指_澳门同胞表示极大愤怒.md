Title: The outrageous atrocities of the British authorities in Hong Kong arouse great anger among Macao compatriots
Author:
Date: 1958-08-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, August 28th: Macao news: After learning that the British authorities in Hong Kong forcibly closed the Chung Wah Middle School with violence and beat the teachers, students, and Chinese journalists present at the scene, Macao compatriots from all walks of life expressed great anger. People unanimously believe that this is a blatant manifestation of the Hong Kong British authorities' hostility towards the Chinese people.

He Xian, Chairman of the Macao Chinese General Chamber of Commerce, said in a statement on the 27th: Forcibly closing the Chung Wah Middle School with violence is an increasingly barbaric act by the Hong Kong British authorities to undermine the patriotic education cause of the Chinese people. All Chinese people feel indignant about this barbaric act.

Leong Pui, Chairman of the Macao Federation of Trade Unions, expressed anger at the atrocities of the Hong Kong British authorities. He lodged a serious protest with the Hong Kong British authorities on behalf of the Macao workers. He said: The Hong Kong British authorities' acts of hostility towards the Chinese people have reached an intolerable level.

Cheung Ching-fai, Chairman of the Macao Women's Federation, said: The Hong Kong British authorities' barbaric behavior is outrageous. She lodged a firm protest against this matter on behalf of the Macao women and expressed firm support for the patriotic and just struggle of the teachers and students of Chung Wah Middle School.

Chan Lut-wo, Standing Director of the Macao Chinese Education Association and Principal of Kwong Tai Middle School, said: The Hong Kong British authorities forcibly closed the Chung Wah Middle School with violence! This is further proof that it intends to undermine the patriotic education cause in Hong Kong.

Others who protested to the Hong Kong British authorities in talks on the 27th included Choi Tak-kui, Vice Chairman of the Macao Chinese General Chamber of Commerce, Director Chan Chik-sang, Chairman of the Youth Association Lau Sin-peng, Chen Kwok-kit, head of the Theatre Business Association, and Yiu King-wai, Chairman of the Gold Industry Trade Association.

The Macao press also issued a written statement. The statement said: We, the press, express our utmost indignation and serious protest against the Hong Kong British police's barbaric interference with the freedom of Chinese journalists to conduct news interviews and their use of violence to beat news reporters. The statement pointed out that this behavior of the Hong Kong British authorities fully exposed the ugly face of the colonialists' hostility towards the patriotic education cause of the Chinese people and their deprivation of the freedom of Chinese journalists to conduct news interviews. The statement concluded by expressing firm support for the just struggle of the teachers, students, and staff of Chung Wah Middle School and firm support for the just struggle of Chinese journalists in Hong Kong to safeguard the freedom of news interviews. Those who signed this written statement included Wong Ka-ching, Editor-in-Chief of the Macao Daily News, and Tsoi Leng-seong, President of the Macao Ta Chung Pao, and more than eighty others.

### 香港英国当局的暴行令人发指  澳门同胞表示极大愤怒

1958-08-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　香港英国当局的暴行令人发指
    澳门同胞表示极大愤怒
    新华社广州28日电　澳门消息：澳门各界同胞在获悉香港英国当局用暴力强行封闭中华中学校舍，并殴打这个学校的教师、学生和在场的中国新闻记者的消息后，都表示极大的愤怒。人们一致认为，这是香港英国当局敌视中国人民的露骨表现。
    澳门中华总商会理事长何贤27日发表谈话说：用暴力强行封闭中华中学校舍，这是香港英国当局摧残中国人民爱国教育事业越来越蛮横的行动。对于这种蛮横行动，凡是中国人都感到愤慨。
    澳门工会联合总会理事长梁培对于香港英国当局的暴行表示愤怒，他代表澳门工人向香港英国当局提出严重的抗议。他说：香港英国当局这种敌视中国人民的行为已经达到了令人难以容忍的程度。
    澳门妇女联合会主席张晴晖说：香港英国当局这种蛮横的行为令人发指。她代表澳门妇女对此事提出坚决的抗议，并表示坚决支持中华中学师生的爱国正义斗争。
    澳门中华教育会常务理事、广大中学校长陈律和说：香港英国当局用暴力强行封闭中华中学校舍！这是它有意摧残香港爱国教育事业的又一明证。
    27日在谈话中向香港英国当局提出抗议的还有澳门中华总商会副理事长崔德祺、理事陈直生，青年联谊会主席刘羡冰，戏院业同业公会负责人陈国杰和金业同业公会主席姚景槐等。
    澳门新闻界还发表了一项书面声明。声明说：香港英国警察蛮横干涉中国记者新闻采访自由和施用暴力殴打新闻记者的行为，我们新闻同业对此表示无比愤慨和严重抗议。声明指出，香港英国当局的这种行为，充分暴露了殖民主义者敌视中国人民爱国教育事业和剥夺中国新闻记者新闻采访自由的丑恶面目。声明最后表示坚决支持中华中学师生员工的正义斗争，坚决支持香港中国新闻工作者为维护新闻采访自由而进行的正义斗争。在这项书面声明上签名的有“澳门日报”总编辑王家祯和“澳门大众报”社长蔡陵霜等八十多人。
