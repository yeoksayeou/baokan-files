Title: Defense Ministers of Fraternal Countries Send Greetings on the Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1958-08-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, August 1st. On the eve of the "August 1st" Army Day, Minister of National Defense Marshal Peng Dehuai received congratulatory telegrams from the following: Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, General Mikhailov; Minister of Armed Forces of the Romanian People's Republic, General L. Salajan; Minister of National Defense of the Hungarian People's Republic, General Revész Géza; Minister of National Defense of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, General Kim Kwang-hyop; Minister of National Defense of the Czechoslovak Republic, General Lomsky; Minister of National Defense of the Polish People's Republic, General Marian Spychalski; Minister of Military and Public Security of the Mongolian People's Republic, Lieutenant General Dorj; Minister of National Defense of the German Democratic Republic, General Stoph; Minister of People's Defense of the People's Republic of Albania, General Beqir Balluku; Minister of National Defense and Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnam People's Army of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, General Vo Nguyen Giap, each sending telegrams to congratulate the 31st anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

In their congratulatory telegrams, the defense ministers of the fraternal countries unanimously and warmly congratulated the Chinese People's Liberation Army for making increasing achievements under the strong leadership of the Chinese Communist Party in army building, consolidating national defense, and defending the socialist construction of its motherland, in strengthening the friendship and unity between the people of the socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union and the close fighting friendship between the armies, and in making more important contributions to the cause of world peace.

### 各兄弟国家国防部长  电贺我解放军建军节

1958-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

    各兄弟国家国防部长
    电贺我解放军建军节
    新华社1日讯　国防部长彭德怀元帅“八一”前夕接到保加利亚人民共和国国防部长米哈伊洛夫大将、罗马尼亚人民共和国武装部队部长利·萨拉扬上将、匈牙利人民共和国国防部长雷维斯·格佐上将、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国民族保卫相金光侠大将、捷克斯洛伐克共和国国防部长洛姆斯基上将、波兰人民共和国国防部长马里安·斯彼哈尔斯基上将、蒙古人民共和国军事和公安部长道尔吉中将、德意志民主共和国国防部长斯多夫上将、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民国防部长贝·巴卢库上将、越南民主共和国国防部长兼越南人民军总司令武元甲大将等，分别发来祝贺中国人民解放军建军三十一周年的电报。
    各兄弟国家国防部长在贺电中一致热烈祝贺中国人民解放军在中国共产党的坚强领导下，在军队建设、巩固国防和捍卫自己祖国社会主义建设事业中日益取得更大的成就，在加强以苏联为首的社会主义国家人民之间的友谊团结和军队之间的密切的战斗友谊和在维护世界和平事业中作出更重要的贡献。
