Title: Albanian Government Lodges Another Strong Protest, Accusing Yugoslavia of Shifting Responsibility for Murder
Author:
Date: 1958-08-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, August 14th: The Albanian government today lodged another strong protest with the Yugoslav government regarding the killing of Albanian citizen Spata, accusing the Yugoslav authorities of shirking responsibility.

The note stated that, under these circumstances, the Albanian government very naturally arrives at the following logical conclusion: not only is the life of Albanian expatriates residing in Yugoslavia or Albanian expatriates passing through Yugoslav territory not guaranteed, but even the minimum standards of international law concerning the protection of the lives of foreign expatriates are not respected.

The note said that the Albanian government awaits a prompt reply from the Yugoslav government and finds it necessary to once again remind the Yugoslav government of the serious responsibility it should bear for the murder of Albanian citizen Spata, and all the consequences that this crime will have on future relations between the two countries.

### 阿尔巴尼亚政府再次提出严正抗议　指斥南斯拉夫推卸杀人责任

1958-08-18
第5版()
专栏：

　　阿尔巴尼亚政府再次提出严正抗议
　　指斥南斯拉夫推卸杀人责任
    据新华社地拉那14日电　阿尔巴尼亚政府今天再一次向南斯拉夫政府就阿尔巴尼亚公民斯帕塔遭杀害一事提出严正抗议，指斥南斯拉夫当局推卸责任。
    照会说，在这种情况下，阿尔巴尼亚政府就很自然的得到这样一个合乎逻辑的结论：不仅住在南斯拉夫的阿尔巴尼亚侨民或者在南斯拉夫领土上经过的阿尔巴尼亚侨民的生命得不到保障，就是关于保障外国侨民生命安全的国际法准则也得不到最低限度的尊重。
    照会说，阿尔巴尼亚政府等待南斯拉夫政府方面迅速的答复，并有必要再一次提醒南斯拉夫政府对谋杀阿尔巴尼亚公民斯帕塔所应负的严重责任，和由此罪行对今后两国相互关系所产生的一切后果。
