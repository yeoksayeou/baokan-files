Title: U.S. Plane Intrudes into UAR Airspace
Author:
Date: 1958-08-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, August 11th—A military spokesman for the Syrian region said on the evening of the 10th that a U.S. jet aircraft from Lebanon intruded into the airspace of the Syrian region of the United Arab Republic on the evening of the 10th.

The spokesman said that the American plane followed a Lebanese plane into the skies over Damascus. Syrian anti-aircraft guns fired at the American plane, forcing it to flee towards Lebanon.

"Al-Nour" reported today that the plane belonged to the American occupation forces in Lebanon.

### 美机侵犯阿联领空

1958-08-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　美机侵犯阿联领空
    新华社大马士革11日电　叙利亚地区的军事发言人10日晚上说，来自黎巴嫩的美国喷气飞机10日晚上侵犯了阿拉伯联合共和国叙利亚地区的领空。
    这个发言人说，这架美国飞机尾随一架黎巴嫩飞机侵入大马士革上空，叙利亚的高射炮向美国飞机开火，迫使这架飞机向黎巴嫩方面逸去。
      “光明报”今天说，这架飞机是属于在黎巴嫩的美国占领军的。
