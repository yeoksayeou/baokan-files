My Heart Flies to Brazil
　　Liu Lanshan

　　Crossing the Pacific, leaping over the Andes, my heart joins your struggle; oh, my Brazilian sisters and brothers. Hot rain falls in Rio de Janeiro, a storm whips through the oak forests, the Brazilian people are eagles in the storm! You roar from the windows, counting on your fingers Dulles' ten major crimes, chasing him away like a rat crossing the street! You are drilling for oil at the oil wells. To prevent American rats from stealing oil, news of the Baghdad revolution is spreading from mouth to mouth… Crossing the Pacific, leaping over the Andes, my heart flies to the green Brazilian plateau, Brazilian friends, I feel like I'm standing before you…

### 我的心飞到了巴西
刘岚山
1958-08-14
第8版()
专栏：

　　我的心飞到了巴西
　　刘岚山渡过太平洋，翻过安第斯山脉，我的心参加了你们的斗争；哦，我的巴西姐妹和弟兄。里约热内卢落着热雨，橡树林中刮起了暴风，巴西人民是暴风雨中的鹰！你们从窗口发出雷鸣般的呼喊，指着鼻子数杜勒斯的十大罪状，像撵过街老鼠一样撵他滚蛋！你们正在石油井上打石油。为了不让美国老鼠偷油喝，巴格达革命的消息正在嘴上传流……渡过太平洋，翻过安第斯山脉，我的心飞到了绿色的巴西高原，巴西友人呵，我就像站在你面前……
