Title: Jakarta Garrison Commander Orders Ban on Chiang Kai-shek Clique Organizations and Activities
Author:
Date: 1958-08-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 25th: The Jakarta Garrison Commander, Dahyar, today announced a decree stipulating that from August 20th, Chiang Kai-shek's Kuomintang organizations and their activities are banned within the Jakarta area.

The decree also stipulates that groups and organizations with the same tendencies as the Chiang Kai-shek clique are also banned.

Members of the Chiang Kai-shek clique, or those who were once members of the Chiang Kai-shek clique, must register with the Jakarta military authorities.

Dahyar pointed out in the decree that the activities of the Kuomintang and those with the same tendencies as the Kuomintang are a threat to the security and order of the Republic of Indonesia, and therefore they must be banned.

### 雅加达卫戍司令下令  取缔蒋帮分子组织和活动

1958-08-26
第5版()
专栏：

    雅加达卫戍司令下令
    取缔蒋帮分子组织和活动
    新华社雅加达25日电　印度尼西亚首都雅加达的卫戍司令达赫亚尔今天公布的一项法令规定，从8月20日起，在雅加达地区内取缔蒋介石国民党的组织和它们的活动。
    法令还规定，同蒋介石集团有着相同倾向的集团和组织也被取缔。
    蒋介石集团分子或曾经一度是蒋介石集团分子的人必须在雅加达军事当局登记。
    达赫亚尔在法令中指出，国民党和那些与国民党有相同倾向的分子的活动是威胁印度尼西亚共和国的安全和秩序的，因此必须取缔他们。
