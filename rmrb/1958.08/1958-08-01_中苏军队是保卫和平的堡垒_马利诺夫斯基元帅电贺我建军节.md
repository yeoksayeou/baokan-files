Title: Sino-Soviet Armies are Bulwarks of Peace Marshal Malinovsky Sends Congratulations on Our Army Day
Author:
Date: 1958-08-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 31st - Defense Minister Marshal Peng Dehuai received a telegram from Soviet Defense Minister Marshal Malinovsky congratulating the 31st anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The full text of the congratulatory telegram is as follows: To Comrade Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, Marshal of the People's Republic of China:

　　On behalf of all personnel of the Soviet Armed Forces and myself, I extend to you, dear Comrade Peng Dehuai, and through you to all the soldiers of the friendly Chinese People's Liberation Army, my heartfelt congratulations on the 31st anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

　　The armies of the fraternal people of China and the Soviet Union were established and tempered in the arduous struggle to defend the interests and achievements of the working people, and they have become invincible and powerful forces for peace, socialism, democracy, and progress.

　　The armed forces of China and the Soviet Union are solid and unbreakable bulwarks for the security and welfare of the people of China and the Soviet Union, for peaceful and creative labor, and for the peace and security of all peoples of the world. Soviet soldiers sincerely wish all personnel of the fraternal armed forces of the People's Republic of China further achievements in strengthening the defense capabilities of the People's China and further developing and consolidating the friendship between the peoples and armed forces of China and the Soviet Union.

　　Marshal of the Soviet Union, Minister of National Defense of the Soviet Union, Malinovsky
　　July 29, 1958

### 中苏军队是保卫和平的堡垒  马利诺夫斯基元帅电贺我建军节

1958-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏军队是保卫和平的堡垒
    马利诺夫斯基元帅电贺我建军节
    新华社31日讯　国防部长彭德怀元帅接到苏联国防部长马利诺夫斯基元帅祝贺中国人民解放军建军三十一周年的电报。贺电全文如下：中华人民共和国国防部长、中华人民共和国元帅彭德怀同志：
    我谨代表苏联武装力量全体人员和我本人，向您，亲爱的彭德怀同志，并通过您向友好的中国人民解放军的全体军人，衷心地祝贺中国人民解放军建军三十一周年。
    中苏两国兄弟人民的军队是在保卫劳动人民的利益和成果的艰苦战斗中建立起来和久经锻炼的，它们已经变成了和平、社会主义、民主和进步的不可战胜的强大力量。
    中苏两国的武装力量是中苏两国人民的安全、福利及和平创造性劳动和全世界各国人民的和平和安全的巩固的、不可摧破的堡垒。苏联军人衷心地预祝兄弟般的中华人民共和国武装力量的全体人员在加强人民中国的国防威力，进一步发展和巩固中苏两国人民和武装力量的友谊方面获得进一步的成就。
  　苏联元帅、苏联国防部长　马利诺夫斯基
                                              1958年7月29日
