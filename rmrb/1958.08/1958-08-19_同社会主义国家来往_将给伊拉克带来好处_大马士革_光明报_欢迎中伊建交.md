Title: Interactions with Socialist Countries Will Benefit Iraq; Damascus "Al-Nour" Welcomes the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between China and Iraq
Author:
Date: 1958-08-19
Page: 4

Interactions with Socialist Countries
　Will Benefit Iraq
　　Damascus "Al-Nour" Welcomes the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between China and Iraq
　　Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, August 18th: "Al-Nour" published an editorial today welcoming the decision of the Republic of Iraq to establish diplomatic relations with China.
　　The editorial said that this is "a new and courageous step taken by the government of the Republic of Iraq in exercising its independence and sovereignty."
　　The editorial said, "All Arab patriots and democrats are watching with enthusiasm and joy the firm, courageous, and conscious national policy of the Republic of Iraq in striving for Arab liberation and independence, and commend the successful steps taken to realize this policy."
　　In praising the establishment of diplomatic relations between the Republic of Iraq and socialist countries, the editorial pointed out that if the Republic of Iraq establishes large-scale economic relations with socialist countries, it will benefit Iraq.

### 同社会主义国家来往  将给伊拉克带来好处　大马士革“光明报”欢迎中伊建交

1958-08-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　同社会主义国家来往
　  将给伊拉克带来好处
　　大马士革“光明报”欢迎中伊建交
    新华社大马士革18日电　“光明报”今天发表社论，欢迎伊拉克共和国关于同中国建立外交关系的决定。
    社论说，这是“伊拉克共和国政府在行使它的独立和主权方面所采取的一个新的勇敢的步骤”。
    社论说，“所有阿拉伯爱国者和民主人士都怀着热情和高兴的心情注视着伊拉克共和国坚决、勇敢和自觉地奉行的争取阿拉伯解放的和独立的民族政策，并且称赞为了实现这项政策而采取的成功的步骤。”
    社论在赞扬伊拉克共和国同社会主义国家建立外交关系的时候指出，如果伊拉克共和国同社会主义国家建立大规模的经济关系，那么，这将对伊拉克带来益处。
