Title: JCP Elects New Leadership

Author:
Date: 1958-08-05
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 4th: The Japanese Communist Party, at its first plenary session of the 7th Central Committee held on August 2nd, elected the new members of the Central Committee's Presidium (i.e., the Politburo) and the secretaries of the Secretariat. The list is as follows (in Japanese phonetic order):

    Nine members of the Presidium: Masami Kasuga, Katsumi Tokunaga, Koreto Kurahara, Yoshio Shiga, Ichizo Suzuki, Sanzo Nosaka, Risami Hakamada, Harushige Matsushima, Kenji Miyamoto.

    Seven secretaries of the Secretariat: Kuraji Anzai, Yashiro Ii, Masami Kasuga (concurrently), Hikoichi Nishikawa, Risami Hakamada (concurrently), Kenji Miyamoto (concurrently), Akira Yonehara.

### 日共选出新领导机构

1958-08-05
第3版()
专栏：

    日共选出新领导机构
    新华社4日讯　东京消息：日本共产党在2日召开第七次代表大会第一次中央委员会全体会议，选出新的中央委员会干部会（即政治局）委员和书记局书记。名单如下（按日文字音顺序）：
    干部会委员九名：春日正一、听涛克己、藏原惟人、志贺义雄、铃木市藏、野坂参三、袴田里见、松岛治重、宫本显治。
    书记局书记七名：安斋库治、伊井弥四郎、春日正一（兼任）、西川彦义、袴田里见（兼任）、宫本显治（兼任）、米原昶。
