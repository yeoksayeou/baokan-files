Title: Yoshida Shigeru Publicly Shouts that Japan Should Carry Out Nuclear Armament

Author:

Date: 1958-08-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 2nd. Yoshida Shigeru, the former Japanese Prime Minister, today publicly clamored at a press conference in Tokyo that Japan should carry out nuclear armament. He believes that Japan's armament with nuclear weapons for "self-defense" does "not violate the constitution."

This former Japanese Prime Minister, notorious for being pro-American, also made hostile remarks against China.

Currently, the Kishi Nobusuke government is violating the constitution, which stipulates that Japan must not possess war potential, and is stepping up Japan's nuclear armament.

### 吉田茂公然叫嚷  日本要进行核武装

1958-08-04
第3版()
专栏：

    吉田茂公然叫嚷
    日本要进行核武装
    新华社2日讯　东京消息：日本前首相吉田茂今天在东京举行的记者招待会上，公然叫嚷要使日本进行核武装。他认为，日本为了“自卫”而以核武器进行武装，并“不违反宪法”。
    这个以亲美而臭名远扬的日本前首相还发表了敌视中国的言论。
    目前，岸信介政府违反规定日本不得拥有战争力量的宪法，正在加紧使日本进行核武装。
