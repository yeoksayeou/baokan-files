Title: Eastern Siberia is a Good Place
Author:
Date: 1958-08-29
Page: 5

　　Eastern Siberia is a Good Place
　　Total area: 7.2 million square kilometers (only 600,000 square kilometers less than the territory of the United States)
　　Natural resources: Concentrates 50% of the Soviet Union's fuel, electricity, and forest resources. Coal resources are about three times those of the United States, greatly exceeding the total coal resources of all capitalist countries in the world. Diamond deposits are also very rich.

### 东西伯利亚好地方

1958-08-29
第5版()
专栏：

　　东西伯利亚好地方
    总面积：七百二十万平方公里（比美国领土只少六十多万平方公里）
    自然资源：集中了苏联50％的燃料、电力和森林资源。煤矿资源约等于美国的三倍，大大超过全世界所有资本主义国家煤矿资源的总和。金刚石矿藏也非常丰富。
