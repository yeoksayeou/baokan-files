Title: Voroshilov Departs Belgium to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1958-08-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, August 16th dispatch: According to TASS news: Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Voroshilov, and his entourage, Soviet Minister of Health Kovrigina, and First Deputy Minister of Culture of the USSR, Kaftanov, returned to Moscow from Brussels on the "Tu-104" aircraft on the 15th.

Voroshilov's visit to Belgium was to participate in the "Soviet Day" activities at the Brussels World Expo.

### 伏罗希洛夫离比返国

1958-08-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　伏罗希洛夫离比返国
    据新华社16日讯　据塔斯社消息：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫和他的随行人员苏联卫生部长科夫里金娜、苏联文化部第一副部长卡弗坦诺夫在15日乘“图—一○四”式飞机从布鲁塞尔回到莫斯科。
    伏罗希洛夫这次到比利时参加了布鲁塞尔世界博览会“苏联日”的活动。
