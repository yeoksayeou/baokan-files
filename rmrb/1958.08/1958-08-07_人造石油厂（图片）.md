Title: Artificial Oil Refinery (Picture)
Author: Yu Min
Date: 1958-08-07
Page: 6

Artificial Oil Refinery

Heilongjiang Province has unveiled a new page in the petroleum industry. The Mudanjiang Artificial Oil Refinery, with an annual output of 5,000 tons of crude oil, has recently been put into production. This oil refinery was built using a combination of indigenous and modern methods, and it is of great significance for supporting industrial and agricultural production in the province. This is an exterior view of the factory. (Photo by Yu Min, Xinhua News Agency)

### 人造石油厂（图片）
于敏
1958-08-07
第6版()
专栏：

    人造石油厂
    黑龙江省揭开了石油工业新的一页，一座年产五千吨原油的牡丹江人造石油厂已在最近投入生产。这座石油厂是用土洋结合的办法建起来的，它对支援本省工、农业生产有很大意义。这是工厂外景。　于敏摄（新华社稿）
