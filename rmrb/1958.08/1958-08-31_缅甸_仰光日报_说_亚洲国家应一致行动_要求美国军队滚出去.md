Title: Burmese "Rangoon Daily" says Asian countries should act in unison to demand American troops get out

Author:
Date: 1958-08-31
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, August 29th: Burmese newspapers continue to condemn the United States for interfering in China's internal affairs in the Taiwan Strait.

    Today, the "Mandalay Daily" said in an editorial that Americans should not station their Seventh Fleet in the Taiwan Strait. The newspaper pointed out that the situation on Jinmen Island is China's internal affair.

    The newspaper strongly demanded the restoration of the People's Republic of China's seat in the United Nations. It pointed out that the People's Republic of China is supported by Burma.

    The "Rangoon Daily" pointed out in a commentary that Asian countries should unanimously demand that the United States remove its military bases on land and sea in Asia. Only in this way can peace in Asia be maintained.

### 缅甸“仰光日报”说  亚洲国家应一致行动  要求美国军队滚出去

1958-08-31
第3版()
专栏：

　　缅甸“仰光日报”说
    亚洲国家应一致行动  要求美国军队滚出去
    新华社仰光29日电  缅甸报纸继续谴责美国在台湾海峡干涉中国内政。
    “曼岱日报”今天在社论中说，美国人不应该把他们的第七舰队驻在台湾海峡。报纸指出，金门岛的事态发展是中国的内政问题。
    报纸强烈要求恢复中华人民共和国在联合国中的席位。它指出，中华人民共和国受到缅甸的支持。
    “仰光日报”在评论中指出，亚洲国家应该一致要求美国撤除它在亚洲陆上和海上的军事基地。只有这样才能维护亚洲的和平。
