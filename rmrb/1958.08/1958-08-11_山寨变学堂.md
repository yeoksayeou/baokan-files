Title: Mountain Hamlet Transforms into School

Author:

Date: 1958-08-11
Page: 5

Mountain hamlet transforms into school,
Pine torches serve as lamplight,
Fingers become chalk,
The floor becomes paper,
We study characters with every meal,
Eradicating illiteracy in five months.
　　——Liancheng, Fujian
