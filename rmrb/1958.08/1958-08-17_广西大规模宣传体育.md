Guangxi Launches Large-Scale Sports Promotion

    (Our dispatch) The Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region will launch a region-wide sports promotion month in August. This was jointly announced in July by five units: the Autonomous Region Sports Commission, the National Defense Sports Association, the Education Department, the Federation of Trade Unions, and the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Committee of the Communist Youth League, to all relevant directly affiliated units.

    The promotion month will mainly publicize the purpose and significance of mass sports and national defense sports, as well as their significant role in realizing the general line for building socialism, promoting the great leap forward in various undertakings, and enhancing national defense reserve forces. At the same time, it will also publicize the principle of combining the popularization and improvement of sports work, as well as the situation of the great leap forward in sports throughout the country.

    This sports promotion month requires simultaneous promotion and mobilization, organizing staff, workers, and students in government offices, factories, schools, and mines to participate in physical exercise, with each person participating in at least one sports activity, learning the third set of broadcast exercises, and making work-break exercises and classroom-break exercises a regular occurrence. Street communities and rural areas should also popularize sports activities as much as possible.

### 广西大规模宣传体育

1958-08-17
第2版()
专栏：

　　广西大规模宣传体育
    本报讯　广西僮族自治区8月份开展一次全民性的体育运动宣传月。这是自治区体育运动委员会、国防体育协会、教育厅、工会联合会、共青团广西僮族自治区委员会等五个单位，在7月份联合通知各有关直属单位来进行的。
    宣传月主要是宣传群众体育和国防体育的目的、意义和对实现建设社会主义总路线，对促进各项事业的大跃进及增强国防后备力量的重大作用。同时，还要宣传体育工作普及与提高相结合的方针，以及全国各地体育运动大跃进的情形。
    这次体育运动宣传月要求边宣传，边发动，组织机关、工厂、学校、矿山的职员、工人和学生，人人参加体育锻炼，每人至少参加一项体育活动，学会做第三套广播体操，并使工间操、课间操经常化。街道、农村也尽可能普及体育活动。
