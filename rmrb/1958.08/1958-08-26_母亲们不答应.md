Title: Mothers Disapprove
Author: Chen Biqing
Date: 1958-08-26
Page: 8

    Mothers' hearts are most compassionate,
    They love their children and desire peace;
    Should anyone dare to be aggressive and interfere,
    Mothers will be the first to disapprove.
                                Chen Biqing

### 母亲们不答应
陈必清
1958-08-26
第8版()
专栏：

    母亲们不答应
　　仁慈不过母亲的心，
　　热爱孩子要和平；
　　谁敢逞凶来干涉，
　　首先母亲们不答应。
                陈必清
