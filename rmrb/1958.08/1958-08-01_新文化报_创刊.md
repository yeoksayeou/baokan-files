Title: "New Culture Daily" Launched
Author:
Date: 1958-08-01
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 31st. The "New Culture Daily," founded by the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, will be launched on August 1st. The newspaper will be published every five days. Its task is to: publicize and implement the central government's guidelines, policies, and instructions on cultural work; conduct ideological guidance for national cultural and artistic work; promote advanced experiences in cultural work in various regions; and encourage the vast number of cultural workers and the masses in their creation, invention, and technological innovation in culture and art.

### “新文化报”创刊

1958-08-01
第7版()
专栏：

    “新文化报”创刊
    新华社31日讯  中华人民共和国文化部创办的“新文化报”在8月1日创刊。该报每五天出刊一次。它的任务是：宣传和贯彻中央关于文化工作的方针政策和指示，进行对全国文化艺术工作的思想指导，推广各地文化工作的先进经验，鼓励广大文化工作者和群众在文化艺术方面的创造、发明和技术革新。
