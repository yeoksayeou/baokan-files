The Constitution of the Young Pioneers of China
Adopted by the Third Plenary Session of the Third Central Committee of the Communist Youth League on June 28, 1958

I. Our team name: Young Pioneers of China.

II. Our team's founder and leader: The Communist Party of China.

III. Our purpose: To unite children and young people, study diligently, strengthen our bodies, love labor, love the motherland, inherit the revolutionary traditions of the Chinese Communist Party, and aspire to be builders and defenders of communism.

IV. Our team flag: A red flag with a five-pointed star and a torch. The five-pointed star symbolizes the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, the torch symbolizes light, and the red flag symbolizes revolutionary victory.

V. Our symbol: The red scarf. It represents a corner of the red flag. Every member should wear and cherish it, and maintain its honor.

VI. Our team salute: The right hand, with five fingers together, raised above the head. It signifies that the interests of the people are above all else.

VII. Our call: "Be ready: Strive for the cause of communism!" Answer: "Always ready!"

VIII. Our style: Honesty, bravery, liveliness, unity.

IX. Our members: Any child or young person between the ages of nine and fifteen who is willing to join the Young Pioneers, willing to abide by the team constitution, can apply to the squadron committee and become a member after being approved by the squadron committee.

New members must plant a tree or do other public welfare work upon joining the team and must take an oath at the induction ceremony to accept and wear the red scarf.

Every member has the right to elect and be elected within the team and can make suggestions and requests regarding the team's work and activities.

Every member must abide by discipline, actively participate in team activities, obey team resolutions, perform the tasks assigned by the team well, and enthusiastically serve everyone.

When a member transfers from one brigade to another, they must bring their member registration form, report to the new brigade, and participate in team life.

Members who have reached the age of fifteen should leave the team. The brigade will hold a departure ceremony.

X. Our organization: Under the direct leadership of the Communist Youth League of China. Establish Young Pioneer headquarters in cities, counties, districts, and townships, and establish brigades or squadrons in schools, streets, and agricultural cooperatives (pastoral cooperatives). A squadron is divided into platoons. A platoon consists of seven to thirteen people. Each platoon has one platoon leader and two deputy platoon leaders.

A squadron consists of two to five platoons. A squadron committee is established for each squadron. The squadron committee consists of one squadron leader, two deputy squadron leaders, and two to four squadron members.

A brigade consists of two or more squadrons. A brigade committee is established for each brigade. The brigade committee consists of one brigade leader, two deputy brigade leaders, and four to ten brigade members.

Platoon leaders, squadron leaders, brigade leaders, and squadron and brigade committees are all elected by the members. Elections are held every six months or one year.

The brigade and squadron committees can establish committee members for flag bearers, labor, learning, culture, sports, organization, wall newspapers, etc., according to work needs.

XI. Our team's reward and punishment methods: Members and team organizations who perform actively and proactively in labor, study, and activities and can excellently complete tasks will be commended and rewarded by the team organization or the Communist Youth League.

If a member violates discipline or commits other mistakes, the team organization should patiently help them recognize and correct them. If they still do not correct after repeated criticism and help, they should be given a punishment. Punishments are divided into three types: warning, suspension of membership, and expulsion from membership. Warnings are discussed and passed by the squadron committee. Suspensions and expulsions must be discussed and passed by the brigade committee. Expulsions must also be approved by the Communist Youth League branch committee. Members who have been suspended or expelled from membership can have their membership restored or rejoin the team after correcting their mistakes and being discussed and approved by the brigade committee.

XII. Our instructors: Selected by the Communist Youth League to serve as excellent League members, they are close friends and guides to the Young Pioneer members, helping the squadron or brigade committee carry out work and organize activities.

Young Pioneer teams in junior high schools, upper grades of elementary schools, and agricultural cooperatives can also be established without instructors. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中国少年先锋队队章  一九五八年六月二十八日共青团三届三中全会通过

1958-08-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　中国少年先锋队队章
    一九五八年六月二十八日共青团三届三中全会通过
    一、我们的队名：中国少年先锋队。
    二、我们队的创立者和领导者：中国共产党。
    三、我们的目的：团结少年儿童，努力学习，锻炼身体，热爱劳动，热爱祖国，继承中国共产党的革命传统，立志做一个共产主义建设者和保卫者。
    四、我们的队旗：五角星加火炬的红旗。五角星代表中国共产党的领导，火炬象征着光明，红旗象征着革命胜利。
    五、我们的标志：红领巾。它代表红旗的一角。每个队员都应该佩带它和爱护它，保持它的荣誉。
    六、我们的队礼：右手五指并紧，高举头上。它表示人民的利益高于一切。
    七、我们的呼号：“准备着：为共产主义事业而奋斗！”回答：“时刻准备着！”
    八、我们的作风：诚实、勇敢、活泼、团结。
    九、我们的队员：凡是九周岁到十五周岁的少年儿童，愿意参加少年先锋队，愿意遵守队章，可以向中队委员会提出申请，经过中队委员会讨论批准后，就成为队员。
    新队员入队要种一棵树，或者做一件别的公益事情，要在入队仪式上举行宣誓，接受和佩带红领巾。
    每个队员在队里都有选举权和被选举权，可以对队的工作和队的活动提出意见和要求。
    每个队员都要遵守纪律，积极参加队的活动，服从队的决议，作好队交给的工作，热心为大家服务。
    队员由一个大队转到另一个大队，要带上队员登记表，到新的大队报到，过队的生活。
    满了十五周岁的队员应该离队。由大队举行离队仪式。
    十、我们的组织：受中国共产主义青年团的直接领导。在市、县、区、乡建立少先队队部，在学校、街道、农业社（牧业社）建立大队或中队，中队下设小队。小队由七到十三人组成。每个小队有小队长一人、副队长二人。
    中队由两个小队到五个小队组成。每个中队成立中队委员会。中队委员会由中队长一人、副中队长二人和中队委员二人到四人组成。
    大队由两个以上的中队组成。每个大队成立大队委员会。大队委员会由大队长一人、副大队长二人和大队委员四人至十人组成。
    小队长、中队长、大队长和中队、大队委员会都由队员选举产生。半年或一年选举一次。
    大队和中队委员会可以根据工作需要，设旗手、劳动、学习、文化、体育、组织、墙报等委员。
    十一、我们队的奖励和处分办法：队员和队的组织在劳动、学习、活动中，表现积极、主动，能出色地完成任务的，由队的组织或共青团给以表扬和奖励。
  队员不遵守纪律或犯了其它错误，队的组织要耐心帮助他认识和改正，如果经过多次批评和帮助仍不改正，应该给以处分。处分分警告、停止队籍、开除队籍三种。警告处分在中队委员会上讨论通过。停止队籍、开除队籍的处分都要在大队委员会上讨论通过。开除队籍还要经过共青团支部委员会批准。被停止或者开除队籍的队员改正错误以后，经过大队委员会的讨论通过，可以恢复队籍或者重新入队。
    十二、我们的辅导员：由共青团选派优秀团员来担任，他是少年先锋队员亲密的朋友和指导者，帮助中队或大队委员会进行工作，组织活动。
    初中、小学高年级和农业社的少先队也可以不设辅导员。　（新华社）
