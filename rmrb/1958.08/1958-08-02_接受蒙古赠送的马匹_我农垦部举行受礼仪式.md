Title: Accepting Horses Gifted by Mongolia: Our Ministry of Agricultural Reclamation Holds Acceptance Ceremony
Author: 
Date: 1958-08-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Our Ministry of Agricultural Reclamation held an acceptance ceremony at the South Suburb Farm in Beijing this afternoon to accept 15,000 draft horses gifted by the Mongolian People's Republic to support the great leap forward in our country's agricultural production. Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State Council, Jiang Qixian, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Agricultural Reclamation, Zeng Yongquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Feng Jiping, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and more than 1,500 employees of the South Suburb Farm attended the acceptance ceremony.

The representatives of the Mongolian People's Republic attending the ceremony were Lubusan, Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, Gongdebusulun, Deputy Minister of Agriculture and Animal Husbandry, and animal husbandry expert Sengge.

At the meeting, Feng Jiping, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, delivered a welcome speech first, followed by Lubusan, the representative of the Mongolian People's Republic. After expressing his gratitude to our country for helping the Mongolian People's Republic in its industrial and agricultural construction, he said: The horses the Mongolian people gifted to China are a symbol and manifestation of the friendship between the two peoples, and also a consolidation and development of the friendship between the Mongolian and Chinese peoples. The cooperation between the Mongolian and Chinese peoples of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union is a contribution to further consolidating the socialist camp.

Jiang Qixian, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Agricultural Reclamation, expressed his gratitude on behalf of the Chinese government to the Mongolian People's Republic for their assistance to us. He went on to say: The large number of draft horses gifted by Mongolia to China has provided favorable conditions for further promoting our country's agricultural production and fully expresses the concern and support of the Mongolian People's Republic for our country's socialist construction.

After the meeting, Vice Premier Li Xiannian accompanied the guests to view the first batch of draft horses that arrived in our country, and visited the dairy farm, pig farm and 10,000-jin rice fields of the South Suburb Farm.

In the evening, Jiang Qixian, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Agricultural Reclamation, held a banquet for the guests.

### 接受蒙古赠送的马匹  我农垦部举行受礼仪式

1958-08-02
第4版()
专栏：

    接受蒙古赠送的马匹
    我农垦部举行受礼仪式
    新华社1日讯　中华人民共和国农垦部今天下午在北京南郊农场举行受礼仪式，接受蒙古人民共和国为支援我国农业生产大跃进而赠送我国的一万五千匹耕马。参加受礼仪式的有我国国务院副总理李先念、农垦部副部长姜齐贤、外交部副部长曾涌泉、北京市副市长冯基平等，另外还有南郊农场的一千五百多名职工。
    出席仪式的蒙古人民共和国代表是蒙古人民共和国驻我国大使鲁布桑、农牧业部副部长宫德布苏伦和畜牧专家僧格。
    会上北京市副市长冯基平首先致欢迎词，接着蒙古人民共和国代表鲁布桑讲话。他对我国帮助蒙古人民共和国进行工农业建设表示感谢后说：蒙古人民赠送给中国的马匹是两国人民友谊的象征和表现，也是蒙、中两国人民的友谊的巩固和发展。以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的蒙、中两国人民的合作，是进一步巩固社会主义阵营的一项贡献。
    农垦部副部长姜齐贤代表我国政府向蒙古人民共和国给我们的帮助表示感谢。他接着说：蒙古赠给中国的大批耕马，对进一步促进我国农业生产提供了有利的条件，充分地表达了蒙古人民共和国对我国社会主义建设的关怀和支持。
    会后李先念副总理陪同客人们观看了首批到达我国的部分耕马，并参观了南郊农场的奶牛场、猪场和万斤稻田。
    晚上，农垦部姜齐贤副部长为客人们举行了宴会。
