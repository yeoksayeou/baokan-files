Title: Discovering Young and Outstanding Track and Field Athletes: University Track and Field Games to be Held in Xi'an
Author:
Date: 1958-08-22
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, August 21st: The National University Track and Field Games are scheduled to be held in Xi'an from the 24th to the 27th. All preparations for the games are actively underway.

This sports event will be the largest track and field games in terms of numbers since liberation. Participating are university teams from twenty-seven provinces, cities, and autonomous regions, totaling one thousand and fifty athletes, all of whom are ranked athletes. Through this competition, the achievements of physical training of university students across the country since liberation will be reviewed, and more young and outstanding track and field athletes will be discovered for the country.

So far, university track and field teams from twenty-five provinces, cities, and autonomous regions have arrived in Xi'an. The athletes are organizing study and training sessions. They have expressed that they will strive to be the best in this sports event, work hard to meet the standards of Master of Sports and first-class athletes, and strive to achieve more excellent results.

### 发掘年轻优秀的田径运动员  大学生田径运动会将在西安举行

1958-08-22
第7版()
专栏：

　　发掘年轻优秀的田径运动员
    大学生田径运动会将在西安举行
    据新华社西安21日电  全国大学生田径运动会定24日到27日在西安举行，大会各项准备工作正在积极进行。
    这次运动会是解放以来全国人数最多规模最大的一次田径运动会。参加的有二十七个省、市、自治区的大学生代表队，共一千零五十名运动员，他们全部是等级运动员。通过这次比赛，将检阅解放以来全国各地大学生体育锻炼的成果，进一步为国家发掘出年轻一代的优秀田径运动员。
    到目前为止，已有二十五个省、市、自治区的大学生田径队先后到达西安。运动员们正在组织学习和锻炼。他们表示，一定要在这次运动会上力争上游，努力向运动健将和一级运动员标准冲击，争取出现更多的优异成绩。
