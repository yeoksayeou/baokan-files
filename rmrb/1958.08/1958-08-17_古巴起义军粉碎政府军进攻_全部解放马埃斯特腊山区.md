Title: Cuban Rebel Army Crushes Government Offensive, Completely Liberates the Sierra Maestra Mountains
Author:
Date: 1958-08-17
Page: 3

　　Cuban Rebel Army Crushes Government Offensive
　　Completely Liberates the Sierra Maestra Mountains

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 16th. TASS reported from New York on the 14th: News from Cuba says that the Cuban rebel army has crushed the government army’s offensive against the Sierra Maestra Mountains (Editor's note: the rebel base area) in Oriente Province, forcing the government army to retreat, and has completely liberated the Sierra Maestra Mountains.

The "New York Times" wrote, "Military operations have begun in Pinar del Río Province in western Cuba, which means that the rebels have launched an open struggle against President Batista's regime in four of the country's six provinces." The rebel radio announced that Dr. Urrutia has been elected as the president of the "fighting Republic of Cuba," and the president's office is located in Venezuela.

### 古巴起义军粉碎政府军进攻　全部解放马埃斯特腊山区

1958-08-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　古巴起义军粉碎政府军进攻
　　全部解放马埃斯特腊山区
    新华社16日讯　据塔斯社纽约14日讯：来自古巴的消息说，古巴起义军已经粉碎了政府军向奥连特省马埃斯特腊山区（编者按：起义军根据地）发动的攻势，迫使政府军退却，全部解放了马埃斯特腊山区。
    “纽约时报”写道，“在古巴西部的比那尔德里奥省开始了军事行动，这意味着起义者已经在全国六个省中的四个省里展开了公开反对巴蒂斯塔总统的政权的斗争。”起义军电台宣布，乌鲁提阿博士被选为“战斗着的古巴共和国”的总统，总统的办公处设在委内瑞拉。
