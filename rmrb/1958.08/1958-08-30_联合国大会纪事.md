Author: He Jun, Mi Gu
Date: 1958-08-30
Page: 5

　　Chronicle of the United Nations General Assembly
　　Poem by He Jun, Illustration by Mi Gu
　　The United Nations,
　　Holds an emergency meeting,
　　Eisenhower rushes to give a speech,
　　Empty seats and sparse audience,
　　Aggression is spurned!
　　The two brothers,
　　Sit in the defendant's seat,
　　Lloyd covers his face with both hands,
　　Dulles, dispirited and lost,
　　The chief culprits are denounced by all!
　　Changing tactics,
　　Devious schemes behind the scenes,
　　Old goods labeled with new trademarks,
　　The American plan proposed by seven countries,
　　Still hits a wall!
　　Out of order,
　　The American voting machine,
　　"Backyard" partners drag their feet,
　t Letting Uncle Sam lead them by the nose,
　　Deserted by all, even family turns away!
　　Aggressor troops,
　　Get out of Lebanon and Jordan!
　　Ten-nation proposal put to a vote,
　　The U.S. and Britain are forced to raise their hands,
　　Bitter medicine must be swallowed!
　　Times are changing,
　　The East wind is prevailing,
　　The policy of aggression will not work,
　　Colonialism is nearing its end,
　　The old calendar is over! (Attached picture)

### 联合国大会纪事
禾君  米谷
1958-08-30
第5版()
专栏：

　　联合国大会纪事
　　禾君诗  米谷图
　　联合国，
　　紧急开会议，
　　艾克抢先来演说，
　　坐位空空听众稀，
　　侵略遭唾弃！
　　两兄弟，
　　坐上被告席，
　　双手掩面劳埃德，
　　丧魂失魄杜勒斯，
　　祸首千夫指！
　　手法换，
　　背后耍诡计，
　　旧货贴上新商标，
　　美国方案七国提，
　　依然碰了壁！
　　失灵了，
　　美国表决器，
　　“后院”伙伴扯后腿，
　　不让山姆牵鼻子，
　　众叛亲又离！
　　侵略军，
　　滚出黎、约去！
　　十国提案付表决，
　　美英被迫把手举，
　　药苦也得吃！
　　时代变，
　　东风正得意，
　　侵略政策行不通，
　　殖民主义临末日，
　　休矣老皇历！（附图片）
