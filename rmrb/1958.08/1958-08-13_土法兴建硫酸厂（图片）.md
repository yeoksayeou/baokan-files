Building a Sulfuric Acid Plant Using Indigenous Methods
In YuHang County, Zhejiang Province, a small sulfuric acid plant has been built using indigenous methods. The main equipment of this plant, the sulfuric acid tower, is built with locally fired ceramics. It can produce 80 tons of sulfuric acid per year, with a concentration of 78%. This is a view of representatives from various regions visiting the sulfuric acid tower. Photo by Wang Changhao (Xinhua News Agency manuscript)

### 土法兴建硫酸厂（图片）
王昌浩
1958-08-13
第3版()
专栏：

    土法兴建硫酸厂
    浙江省余杭县用土法建成一座小型硫酸厂。这个厂的主要设备硫酸塔是用当地烧制的陶瓷器砌起来的。它一年能产八十吨硫酸，浓度达78％。这是来自各地的代表正在参观硫酸塔。王昌浩摄（新华社稿）
