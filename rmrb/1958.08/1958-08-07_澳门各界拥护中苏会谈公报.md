Title: Macao Circles Support the Sino-Soviet Talks Communiqué
Author: 
Date: 1958-08-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, 6th: People from all walks of life in Macao support the communiqué of the talks between the leaders of China and the Soviet Union. They believe that this communiqué is a powerful guarantee of world peace.

    Ma Man-Kee, vice chairman of the Macao Chinese General Chamber of Commerce, said that Macao's industrial and commercial circles firmly support the propositions put forward in the communiqué of the talks between the leaders of China and the Soviet Union, and resolutely demand that the American and British aggressor troops immediately withdraw from the Middle East. Cheung Ching-fai, chairman of the Macao Women's Federation, said: At a time when imperialism is stepping up the creation of tension and plotting to launch a war, the measures decided upon at the meeting between the leaders of China and the Soviet Union will greatly help the struggle against aggression and in defense of peace. Li Shui-yee, principal of Macao Pui To Middle School, said: This communiqué demonstrates the great solidarity between China and the Soviet Union, and this solidarity is the most powerful guarantee of world peace.

    Well-known figures in Macao's industrial and commercial circles, Yao King-wai, Chan Chik-sang, Wong Ping-wo and Yuen Chun-cho, also made statements to the press, expressing their support for the communiqué, considering it an inestimable contribution to the defense of world peace.

### 澳门各界拥护中苏会谈公报

1958-08-07
第4版()
专栏：

    澳门各界拥护中苏会谈公报
    新华社香港6日电　澳门各界人士拥护中苏两国领导人的会谈公报。他们认为这一公报是世界和平的有力保证。
    澳门中华总商会副理事长马万祺说澳门工商界人士坚决拥护中苏两国领导人会谈公报提出的主张，坚决要求美英侵略军队立即撤出中东。澳门妇女联合会主席张晴晖说：在目前帝国主义加紧制造紧张局势、阴谋发动战争的时候，中苏两国领袖会谈决定的措施，将大大有助于反对侵略、维护和平的斗争。澳门培道中学校长李瑞仪说：这个会谈公报表明了中苏两国的伟大团结，这种团结是世界和平最有力的保证。
    澳门工商界知名人士姚景槐、陈直生、黄平和源晋初等也对报界发表谈话，表示拥护和支持这个会谈公报，认为它对保卫世界和平是无可估量的贡献。
