Title: United Nations Scientific Committee on the Effects of Atomic Radiation Believes That Stopping Nuclear Testing is Beneficial to Human Health
Author:
Date: 1958-08-12
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, New York, August 11th: The United Nations Scientific Committee on the Effects of Atomic Radiation released a report on the 10th, advocating for a cessation of nuclear testing.

The report by the committee, composed of scientists from fifteen countries, stated, "Radioactive contamination caused by the explosion of nuclear weapons is increasing the level of radioactivity worldwide. This situation will create new dangers, mostly unknown to people, for present and future residents." These dangers, "by their nature, are beyond the control of those exposed to them." Stopping nuclear weapons explosions will be "beneficial to human health."

### 联合国研究原子放射影响的科学委员会　认为停试核武器有利人类健康

1958-08-12
第7版()
专栏：

　　联合国研究原子放射影响的科学委员会
　　认为停试核武器有利人类健康
    新华社11日讯　纽约消息：联合国研究原子放射影响的科学委员会10日发表一个报告，主张停止进行核试验。
    这个由十五个国家的科学家组成的委员会的报告说，“由于核武器的爆炸而对周围发生的放射性污染使全世界的放射性程度日益增加。这种情况对于现在和未来的居民会产生新的和大部分为人们所不知道的危险。”这些危险“由于它们的性质，是遭受这种危险的人所不能控制的”。停止核武器爆炸将“有利于人类的健康”。
