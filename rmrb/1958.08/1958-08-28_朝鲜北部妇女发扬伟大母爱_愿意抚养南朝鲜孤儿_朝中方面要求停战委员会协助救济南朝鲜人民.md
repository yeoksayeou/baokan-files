Title: North Korean Women Display Great Maternal Love, Willing to Raise South Korean Orphans; Korean-Chinese Side Requests Armistice Commission to Assist in Relieving the South Korean People
Author:
Date: 1958-08-28
Page: 6

　　North Korean Women Display Great Maternal Love
    Willing to Raise South Korean Orphans
    Korean-Chinese Side Requests Armistice Commission to Assist in Relieving the South Korean People
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 26th: Women in northern Korea have been holding meetings in recent days, determined to support the Republic's Cabinet's decision to relieve unemployed people, homeless orphans, and needy young students in South Korea with practical actions.
    Mothers in Pyongyang passed a resolution at the meeting to take responsibility for raising 100,000 South Korean orphans. Mothers in Hamhung expressed their desire to take in all the homeless orphans in Busan, at the southern tip of Korea, to be raised by them.
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 26th: According to a report from the Korean Central News Agency in Kaesong: Major General Jiang Shanghao, Chief Commissioner of the Korean-Chinese side of the Korean Military Armistice Commission, suggested at a meeting of the Military Armistice Commission today that both sides of the Military Armistice Commission, in accordance with the Armistice Agreement, grant special permission to representatives from both North and South Korea, allowing them to pass through the demilitarized zone along routes designated by both sides, in order to discuss the issues of delivering and receiving relief supplies and South Korean homeless orphans, as well as awarding scholarships to South Korean students in accordance with the August 21st decision of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

### 朝鲜北部妇女发扬伟大母爱  愿意抚养南朝鲜孤儿  朝中方面要求停战委员会协助救济南朝鲜人民

1958-08-28
第6版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜北部妇女发扬伟大母爱
    愿意抚养南朝鲜孤儿
    朝中方面要求停战委员会协助救济南朝鲜人民
    据新华社平壤26日电　朝鲜北部妇女连日举行集会，决心以实际行动支持共和国内阁关于救济南朝鲜失业者、流浪孤儿和处境困难的青年学生的决定。
    平壤市的母亲们在集会时通过决议，要负责抚养十万名南朝鲜孤儿；咸兴市的母亲们表示要把朝鲜南端釜山市的全部流浪孤儿包下来，由她们抚养。
    据新华社平壤26日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社开城消息：朝鲜军事停战委员会朝中方面首席委员姜尚昊少将今天在军事停战委员会会议上建议军事停战委员会双方根据停战协定，给予南北朝鲜代表特别许可，让他们能够按照双方指定的路线经过非军事区，以便在一起讨论递交和接受救济物资以及南朝鲜流浪孤儿问题和按照朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁8月21日的决定颁发奖学金给南朝鲜学生的问题。
