Title: Korean Writer Yun Se-jung Visits Wuhan and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1958-08-05
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency: Korean writer Yun Se-jung, who came to China for a visit according to the 1958 executive plan of the Sino-Korean Cultural Cooperation Agreement, left Beijing on the afternoon of August 1st to visit Wuhan. Yun Se-jung arrived in Beijing on July 20th. After visiting Wuhan, he plans to visit Nanjing, Shanghai, Shenyang, Anshan and other places.

### 朝鲜作家尹世重去武汉等地访问

1958-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜作家尹世重去武汉等地访问
    新华社讯　根据中朝文化合作协定1958年执行计划来我国访问的朝鲜作家尹世重1日下午离北京去武汉访问。尹世重是7月20日到达北京的，他准备访问武汉后去南京、上海、沈阳、鞍山等地参观访问。
