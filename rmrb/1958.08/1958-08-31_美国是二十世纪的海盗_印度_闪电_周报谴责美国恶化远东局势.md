Title: The United States is the Pirate of the 20th Century: Indian "Blitz" Weekly Condemns the United States for Worsening the Situation in the Far East
Author:
Date: 1958-08-31
Page: 3

　　The United States is the Pirate of the 20th Century
    Indian "Blitz" Weekly Condemns the United States for Worsening the Situation in the Far East
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 29th - The "Blitz" weekly, published today, accused the United States of dispatching warships to Singapore and then to the Taiwan Strait for provocative activities. The weekly pointed out that "the United States' dispatch of ships to Singapore is a planned act of aggression."
    The "Blitz" weekly's editorial said: "The arrival of the U.S. fleet in the Indian Ocean region is a threat to India, as well as to China and Southeast Asian countries, especially Indonesia and Burma."
    The editorial said that in this matter, the United States is engaging in the activities of a 20th-century pirate, wantonly "provoking the spirit of the Bandung Conference."

### 美国是二十世纪的海盗  印度“闪电”周报谴责美国恶化远东局势

1958-08-31
第3版()
专栏：

　　美国是二十世纪的海盗
    印度“闪电”周报谴责美国恶化远东局势
    新华社新德里29日电　今天出版的“闪电”周报指责美国调派军舰到新加坡随后到台湾海峡进行挑衅活动。这家周报指出，“美国派遣舰只到新加坡去是有计划的侵略行动”。
    “闪电”周报的社论说：“美国舰队开到印度洋地区是对印度的威胁，也是对中国和东南亚国家特别是印度尼西亚和缅甸的威胁”。
    社论说，在这件事情上，美国干的就是二十世纪海盗的勾当，恣意对万隆会议的精神“进行无端的挑衅”。
