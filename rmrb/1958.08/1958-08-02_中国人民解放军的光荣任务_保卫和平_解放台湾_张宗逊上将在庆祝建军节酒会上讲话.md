Title: The Glorious Task of the Chinese People's Liberation Army—Defend Peace, Liberate Taiwan: Speech by General Zhang Zongxun at the Army Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1958-08-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 1st: At the reception held tonight to celebrate the 31st anniversary of "August 1st" Army Day, General Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, said that all officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army resolutely oppose the shameless acts of aggression and war conspiracies of American and British imperialism and warmly support the just struggle and national independence movements of the Arab people. At the same time, we have not forgotten that our territory of Taiwan is still occupied by American imperialism, and liberating Taiwan is the glorious task of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Military attaches and their wives from various countries stationed in China, as well as all personnel from the military attache offices, were invited to attend tonight's reception.

General Zhang Zongxun said in his speech at the beginning of the reception that with the rapid development of our country's socialist construction and the help of socialist countries led by the Soviet Union, our armed forces have also developed and grown stronger. Our armed forces are the defenders and builders of our country's socialism and also the defenders of world peace. We have no intention of aggression against any country, but we also resolutely oppose any aggressors.

General Zhang Zongxun emphasized: We believe that in the situation where the East Wind prevails over the West Wind, imperialist aggression is bound to fail.

Colonel Kocis, the Romanian military attache to China, expressed his gratitude to the host on behalf of the military attaches of various countries, and wished the Chinese People's Liberation Army continuous enhancement of its combat power in order to complete the glorious tasks of defending the motherland, liberating Taiwan, and safeguarding world peace.

Attending tonight's reception as hosts were: General Gan Siqi, General Yang Chengwu, Admiral Wang Hongkun, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General Cao Lihuai of the Air Force, Lieutenant General Chang Qiankun of the Air Force, Lieutenant General Liu Shaowen, and Major General Li Zhen (female).

During the reception, the guests and hosts toasted to the friendship between the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the armies of various friendly countries, to the friendship and happy life between the people of all countries, and to world peace!

### 中国人民解放军的光荣任务——保卫和平　解放台湾  张宗逊上将在庆祝建军节酒会上讲话

1958-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民解放军的光荣任务——
    保卫和平　解放台湾
    张宗逊上将在庆祝建军节酒会上讲话
    新华社1日讯　中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊上将在今晚举行的庆祝“八一”建军节三十一周年的酒会上说，中国人民解放军全体官兵，坚决反对美、英帝国主义无耻的侵略行为和战争阴谋，热烈地支持阿拉伯人民的正义斗争和民族独立运动。同时，我们也没有忘记，我国领土台湾仍然被美国帝国主义侵占着，解放台湾是中国人民解放军的光荣任务。
    各国驻华武官及夫人和武官处全体人员，应邀出席了今晚的酒会。
    张宗逊上将在酒会开始时讲话说，随着我国社会主义建设事业的飞跃发展，以及以苏联为首的社会主义各国对我们的帮助，我国武装力量也获得了发展和壮大。我国的武装力量是我国社会主义的保卫者和建设者，也是世界和平事业的保卫者。我们对任何国家都没有侵略的意图，但我们也坚决反对任何的侵略者。
    张宗逊上将强调指出：我们相信，在东风压倒西风的形势下，帝国主义的侵略是一定要失败的。
    罗马尼亚驻华武官科奇斯上校代表各国驻华武官向主人表示谢意，并祝中国人民解放军不断地增强战斗威力，以完成保卫祖国、解放台湾和保卫世界和平的光荣任务。
    出席今晚酒会作陪的有：甘泗淇上将、杨成武上将、王宏坤海军上将、萧向荣中将、曹里怀空军中将、常乾坤空军中将、刘少文中将和李贞少将（女）。
    席间，宾主为中国人民解放军同各友好国家军队之间的友谊，为各国人民之间的友谊和幸福生活，为世界和平干杯！
