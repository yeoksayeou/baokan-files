Title: Iraqi Government Announces Reduction in Housing Rents
Author:
Date: 1958-08-09
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, 7th. The Iraqi Republic government's decree, published in today's newspapers, announced that the rents for all rented houses and rooms would be reduced by 15% to 20%.

### 伊拉克政府宣布  减低房屋租金

1958-08-09
第2版()
专栏：

    伊拉克政府宣布
    减低房屋租金
    据新华社巴格达7日电  今天各报刊载的伊拉克共和国政府公布的法令宣布，一切出租的房屋和房间的租金减低15％—20％。
