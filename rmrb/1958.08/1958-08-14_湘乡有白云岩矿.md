Title: Xiangxiang Has Dolomite Mine
Author: Huang Jingyou
Date: 1958-08-14
Page: 3

　　Xiangxiang Has Dolomite Mine
    Geological personnel from the Hunan Provincial Geological Bureau have surveyed a dolomite deposit in Xiaowanluo Mountain, five miles northeast of Gushui Town, Xiangxiang. The reserves amount to 100 million tons, and the quality meets the requirements of second-grade products.
    This mining area is next to the Xiangqian Railway, with convenient transportation. The mine can also be open-pit mined, with very favorable conditions.
    Dolomite is a non-metallic mineral that can be used as a flux in the steel industry and as a refractory material.
          (Huang Jingyou)

### 湘乡有白云岩矿
黄镜友
1958-08-14
第3版()
专栏：祖国到处有宝藏

　　湘乡有白云岩矿
    湖南省地质局地质人员，在湘乡谷水镇东北五华里的小万罗山，踏勘了一个白云岩矿床，储量达一亿吨，质量达到二级品的要求。
    这个矿区在湘黔铁路旁边，交通便利；矿山也能露天开采，条件十分优越。
    白云岩是非金属矿产，可供钢铁工业上做熔剂以及做耐火材料之用。
          （黄镜友）
