Title: French Government Passes Resolution: Joliot-Curie to Receive State Funeral the Day After Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1958-08-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 15th (TASS dispatch): The French government has passed a resolution to express condolences for the death of Frédéric Joliot-Curie and has decided to hold a state funeral ceremony on August 19.

　　The representative of the President of France visited the Saint-Antoine Hospital, where Joliot-Curie's remains are lying in state, on the 15th to express condolences. French Prime Minister de Gaulle sent a telegram of condolence to Joliot-Curie's family, expressing his condolences.

### 法国政府通过决议?约里奥—居里后天国葬

1958-08-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　法国政府通过决议
　?约里奥—居里后天国葬
    新华社16日讯　据塔斯社巴黎15日讯：法国政府通过决议，为弗雷德里克·约里奥—居里的逝世致哀，并决定在8月19日举行国葬仪式。
    法国总统的代表15日访问了停放约里奥—居里灵柩的圣安托万医院表示悼念。法国总理戴高乐给约里奥—居里的家属发出唁电，表示哀悼。
