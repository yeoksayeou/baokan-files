Title: Wang Youping Appointed as China's Ambassador to Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1958-08-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 23rd:
　　Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, has appointed Wang Youping as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Cambodia.

### 王幼平任我国驻柬大使

1958-08-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　王幼平任我国驻柬大使
    新华社北京23日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东任命王幼平为中华人民共和国驻柬埔寨王国特命全权大使。
