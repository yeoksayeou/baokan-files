Title: Northeast Machine Manufacturing Plant Gains Good Experience; Central Leaders Value This Creation
Author:
Date: 1958-08-20
Page: 1

　　Northeast Machine Manufacturing Plant Gains Good Experience; Central Leaders Value This Creation
　　(Our report) Leading comrades of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, including Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Chen Yun, Deng Xiaoping, Lin Boqu, Peng Zhen, Li Xiannian, etc., received representatives from the Northeast Machine Manufacturing Plant in Beijing on August 4th to report their success. These representatives, along with the workers in the plant, recently created a set of "ants gnawing at bones" methods to produce large machines with small equipment.
　　The central leading comrades attached great importance to the creation of the workers of the Northeast Machine Manufacturing Plant. They carefully visited the model of the machine processed by the "ants gnawing at bones" method, as well as the model of the machine manufactured by this method.

### 东北机器制造厂获得好经验  中央领导同志重视这个创造

1958-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　东北机器制造厂获得好经验  中央领导同志重视这个创造
    本报讯  中共中央领导人刘少奇、周恩来、陈云、邓小平、林伯渠、彭真、李先念等，在8月4日接见了东北机器制造厂来京报捷的代表。这些代表同在厂的职工一起，在最近创造了一套“蚂蚁啃骨头”的办法，用小设备生产出了大机器。
    中央领导同志对东北机器制造厂职工的这一创造很重视，他们详细地参观了用“蚂蚁啃骨头”办法加工机器的模型，以及用这种办法制造出的机器的模型。
