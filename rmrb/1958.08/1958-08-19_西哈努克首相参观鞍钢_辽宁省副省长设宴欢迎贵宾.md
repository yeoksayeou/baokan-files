Title: Sihanouk Visits Anshan Iron and Steel; Liaoning Vice Governor Hosts Banquet for Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1958-08-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 18th -- Prince Norodom Sihanouk and the National Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia led by him, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi and Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, left Beijing by plane at 7:30 this morning to visit Anshan and other places.

Vice Premier He Long, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo, and Yang An, head of the Cambodian Economic Delegation to China, saw them off at the airport.

Xinhua News Agency, Anshan, August 18th -- Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, said to the steelworkers who welcomed him at Anshan Iron and Steel Company today, that the great progress you have achieved for the development and prosperity of your own country is worthy of praise, and we admire it very much.

Prince Sihanouk and the distinguished guests of the National Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia, accompanied by Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi, arrived in Anshan by special plane from Beijing at 9:30 this morning. They were warmly welcomed by Li Weimin, Mayor of Anshan, and others.

In Anshan, accompanied by Li Weimin, Mayor of Anshan, and Ma Bin, Deputy Manager of Anshan Iron and Steel Company, and others, the distinguished guests visited the No. 9 blast furnace and the tense and skilled operations of the workers in the large rolling mill and seamless steel tube workshop. When Li Wen, director of the Seamless Steel Tube Plant, introduced that the plant's employees are determined to achieve a great leap in production, and the annual output this year will increase by more than 160% compared with the original design capacity, Prime Minister Sihanouk praised repeatedly: "You are progressing very fast, progressing very fast."

After the distinguished guests left the seamless steel tube plant, five or six hundred steelworkers gathered in the square outside the workshop and applauded warmly to welcome the distinguished guests. At this time, Prince Sihanouk constantly clasped his hands together to thank the workers and made a speech. In his speech, he repeatedly emphasized that the friendship between China and Cambodia is a "brotherly friendship." He also expressed warm congratulations to Anshan Iron and Steel Company for its "good organization and the great efforts of the workers." The workers thanked the Prime Minister for his care and encouragement with prolonged applause and shouted in unison "Long live Sino-Cambodian friendship."

After the visit, Mayor Li Weimin accompanied Prince Sihanouk to lunch.

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, August 18th -- Qiu Youwen, Vice Governor of Liaoning Province, hosted a banquet this evening to welcome Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, and the distinguished guests of the National Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia led by him, who came to visit Liaoning.

Vice Governor Qiu Youwen and Prince Sihanouk toasted at the banquet, wishing the long-standing friendly relations between China and Cambodia will continue to develop and consolidate.

Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi attended the banquet. Also attending the banquet were Li Huang, Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Vice Governor Ning Wu, Lieutenant General Cheng Shicai, Commander of the Shenyang Garrison, Liu Baotian, Mayor of Shenyang, and heads of democratic parties and people's organizations in Liaoning Province and Shenyang City.

Prime Minister Sihanouk and the distinguished guests of the National Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia arrived in Shenyang by special plane from Anshan at 6:00 p.m. today. The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at Shenyang East Tower Airport by Vice Governor Qiu Youwen and the heads of the Party, government and military of Liaoning Province and Shenyang City. Young Pioneers of Shenyang City presented flowers to Prime Minister Sihanouk and other distinguished guests.

Kim Yong-bin, the Consul General of North Korea in Shenyang, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 西哈努克首相参观鞍钢　辽宁省副省长设宴欢迎贵宾

1958-08-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　西哈努克首相参观鞍钢
　　辽宁省副省长设宴欢迎贵宾
    新华社18日讯　诺罗敦·西哈努克首相和由他率领的柬埔寨王国国家代表团，由陈毅副总理和外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文陪同，今天早晨七点半乘飞机离开北京到鞍山等地参观访问。
    贺龙副总理、外交部副部长罗贵波和柬埔寨驻中国经济代表团团长杨安等到机场欢送。
    新华社鞍山18日电　柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王今天在参观鞍山钢铁公司时对欢迎他的钢铁工人们说，你们为了自己国家的发展和繁荣所实现的巨大进步，值得赞扬，我们十分钦佩。
    西哈努克亲王和柬埔寨王国国家代表团的贵宾们，由陈毅副总理兼外交部长陪同，今天上午九时半由北京乘专机到达鞍山。受到鞍山市市长李维民等人的热烈欢迎。
    在鞍山，贵宾们由鞍山市市长李维民和鞍山钢铁公司副经理马宾等陪同，参观了九号高炉炼铁的情况，以及工人们在大型轧钢车间、无缝钢管车间紧张而又熟练的操作情形。当无缝钢管厂厂长李文介绍了这个厂的职工决心实现生产大跃进，今年全年产量将要比原设计能力增加160％以上等情况时，西哈努克首相连声称赞说：“你们进步很快，进步很快”。
    贵宾们走出无缝钢管厂以后，五六百名钢铁工人聚集在车间外面的广场上热烈鼓掌欢迎贵宾们。这时，西哈努克亲王不断合十向工人们致谢，并且讲了话。他在讲话中一再强调指出中柬两国的友谊是“兄弟般的友谊关系”。他还对鞍山钢铁公司的“良好组织工作和工人们的巨大努力”，表示热烈的祝贺。工人们以长时间的鼓掌来感谢首相的关怀和鼓励，并且齐声高呼“中柬友好万岁”。
    参观以后，李维民市长陪同西哈努克亲王进午餐。
    新华社沈阳18日电　辽宁省副省长仇友文今天晚间设宴欢迎前来辽宁访问的柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和由他率领的柬埔寨王国国家代表团的贵宾们。
    仇友文副省长和西哈努克亲王在宴会上祝酒，祝中柬两国久已存在的友好关系不断发展和巩固。
    陈毅副总理兼外交部长出席了宴会。出席宴会作陪的，还有中共辽宁省委书记李荒，副省长宁武，沈阳驻军首长程世才中将，沈阳市市长刘宝田以及辽宁省、沈阳市各民主党派和人民团体的负责人。
    西哈努克首相和柬埔寨王国国家代表团的贵宾们，是在今天下午六时由鞍山乘专机到达沈阳的。贵宾们在沈阳东塔机场上受到副省长仇友文和辽宁省、沈阳市党政军负责人的热烈欢迎。沈阳市少先队员们向西哈努克首相和其他贵宾们献了鲜花。
    到机场欢迎的，还有朝鲜驻沈阳总领事金龙彬。
