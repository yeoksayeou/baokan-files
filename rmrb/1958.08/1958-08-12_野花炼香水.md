Title: Wildflowers Distilled into Perfume
Author:
Date: 1958-08-12
Page: 3

    Wildflowers Distilled into Perfume
    With the help of technical personnel from the Inner Mongolia Industrial Laboratory, the Xiguitu Banner in Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region has successfully extracted rose perfume from wild roses in the Greater Khingan Mountains.
    The Greater Khingan Mountains produce large quantities of wild roses, lily-of-the-valley and other aromatic flowers and plants that can be used to extract perfume. Xiguitu Banner is preparing to build a perfume factory.

### 野花炼香水

1958-08-12
第3版()
专栏：

    野花炼香水
    内蒙古自治区喜桂图旗在内蒙古工业试验室技术人员帮助下试用大兴安岭的野生玫瑰花提炼玫瑰香水成功。
    大兴安岭大量出产野玫瑰、伶兰香等可以提炼香水的芳香花草。喜桂图旗正筹备建筑香水工厂。
