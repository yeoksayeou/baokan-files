Title: Houyang Cooperative, Yinghu Cooperative Peanut Yield Exceeds 3,000 Jin Per Mu
Author:
Date: 1958-08-09
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency report: The Houyang Cooperative in Nan'an County, Fujian Province, and the Yinghu Cooperative in Jinjiang County have both set records for peanut yields exceeding 3,000 jin per mu. The 1.254 mu experimental plot of the Houyang Cooperative harvested a total of 4,221 jin of dry peanuts, with an average yield of 3,366 jin per mu. The fourth team of the tenth production brigade of the Yinghu Cooperative harvested a total of 3,495 jin of dry peanuts from a 1.116 mu plot, with an average yield of 3,013 jin per mu. Responsible comrades from the county committees and township party branches of these two counties participated in the acceptance inspection.
    The Houyang Cooperative not only set a high-yield record in the experimental plot but also achieved a large-area bumper harvest. The entire cooperative's 232 mu of peanuts averaged over 1,036 jin per mu, an increase of two and a half times compared to last year.

### 厚阳社、英湖社花生亩产三千多斤

1958-08-09
第5版()
专栏：

    厚阳社、英湖社
    花生亩产三千多斤
    据新华社讯  福建省南安县厚阳社和晋江县英湖社，都创造了花生亩产三千斤以上的纪录。厚阳社的一点二五四亩试验田，共收干花生四千二百二十一斤多，平均亩产三千三百六十六斤多。英湖社第十生产中队第四小队的一亩一分六厘田，共收干花生三千四百九十五斤多，平均亩产三千零十三斤。这两个县的县委负责同志和乡党支部的负责同志等都参加了验收工作。
    厚阳社不仅试验田创造了高产纪录，还获得了大面积丰产。全社二百三十二亩花生，平均亩产一千零三十六斤多，比去年增产两倍六。
