Title: Zhou Wenqing, Chen Tongbin, and Li Yuming Break National Marathon Record
Author:
Date: 1958-08-01
Page: 7
    Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, 29th - Prominent young long-distance runner Zhou Wenqing ran the full distance of 42 kilometers and 195 meters in two hours, forty-two minutes, and three and two-tenths seconds in a marathon test held in Jilin City on the 27th, breaking the national record of two hours, fifty-two minutes, and thirty-four and six-tenths seconds set by Zhang Liangyou last year. Two other athletes, Chen Tongbin and Li Yuming, also broke the national record with excellent results. Their scores have been recognized by national referee Wu Qingyuan. In this test, Zhou Wenqing and Chen Tongbin both exceeded the Master of Sports standard, and seven other athletes exceeded the First-Class Athlete standard.

### 周文庆、陈桐彬、李玉铭刷新马拉松全国纪录

1958-08-01
第7版()
专栏：

    周文庆、陈桐彬、李玉铭
    刷新马拉松全国纪录
    据新华社长春29日电  著名青年长跑运动员周文庆，27日在吉林市举行的马拉松测验中以二小时四十二分三秒二的速度跑完全程四十二公里一百九十五公尺，打破了去年张亮友创造的二小时五十二分三十四秒六的全国纪录。和他一起也以优异成绩打破全国纪录的，还有陈桐彬、李玉铭等二名运动员。他们的成绩已由国家裁判吴庆远承认。在这次测验中，周文庆、陈桐彬的成绩都超过了运动健将标准，还有七名运动员超过一级运动员标准。
