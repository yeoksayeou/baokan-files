Title: Soviet Red Moon Will Pass Over China Today and Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1958-08-04
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 3rd: TASS reports: As of 6:00 AM Moscow time on August 3rd, the Soviet Union's third artificial satellite has orbited the Earth one thousand and eighty-seven times.

    On August 4th, when the sky is clear and cloudless, areas between 42 and 63 degrees north latitude can see the satellite and its carrier rocket, areas between 38 and 64 degrees south latitude can see the satellite, and areas between 49 and 64 degrees can see the rocket.

    On August 5th, at 2:08 AM Beijing time, the satellite will fly from Novosibirsk in the Soviet Union, pass over Chengdu at 2:16 AM, and pass over Beijing at 3:51 PM.

### 苏联红月亮今明过我国

1958-08-04
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联红月亮今明过我国
    据新华社3日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：到8月3日莫斯科时间上午六点钟为止，苏联第三个人造卫星已经围绕地球运行了一千零八十七圈。
    8月4日天空晴朗无云时，北纬四十二度到六十三度之间地区可以看到卫星及其运载火箭，南纬三十八度到六十四度之间地区可以看到卫星，四十九度到六十四度之间地区可以看到火箭。
    8月5日北京时间清晨二点八分，卫星从苏联新西伯利亚飞来，二点十六分过成都，十五点五十一分过北京。
