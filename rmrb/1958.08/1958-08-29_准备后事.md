Title: Preparing for the Aftermath
Author: Yuan Shuipai
Date: 1958-08-29
Page: 5

　　Preparing for the Aftermath
    Yuan Shuipai
    According to reports: The U.S. Department of Defense has designated scientific institutions to specifically study the conditions under which to surrender. When the news came out, public opinion was in an uproar, causing Eisenhower great embarrassment.

　　American scandals are not few,
　　But preparing for surrender is even more wonderful,
　　Leaking the secret is too embarrassing,
　　They had to investigate and deny it.
　　Before the war has even started,
　　They are already studying surrender,
　　When to raise hands, how to beg for mercy,
　　Anxiously contemplating preparing for the aftermath.
　　From afar, they glare and bluster,
　　Up close, they are just paper and paste,
　　Truly: Exposing the Western trickery,
　　Just a paper tiger! (Attached picture)
　　Jiang Yousheng, Illustration

### 准备后事
袁水拍
1958-08-29
第5版()
专栏：

　　准备后事
    袁水拍
    报载：美国防部指定了科学机构专门研究在什么条件下投降的问题。消息传出，舆论哗然，弄得艾森豪威尔大为难堪。
　　美国丑闻本不少，
　　筹备投降事更妙，
　　秘密泄露太难听，
　　只得彻查又辟谣。
　　还没有打仗，
　　先研究投降，
　　啥时举手，怎么求饶，
　　准备后事苦思量。
　　远看瞪眼吹胡，
　　近看篾片浆糊，
　　正是：拆穿西洋镜，
　　一只纸老虎！（附图片）
　　江有生　插图
