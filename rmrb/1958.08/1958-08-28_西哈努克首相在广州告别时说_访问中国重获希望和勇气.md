Title: Prime Minister Sihanouk said at his farewell in Guangzhou that his visit to China renewed his hope and courage
Author:
Date: 1958-08-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, August 27th: Prime Minister Sihanouk, the National Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia, Princess Phanha Devi, Prince Chakrapong, Princess Sourya Rounsey, and others, left Guangzhou by special plane this morning via Nanning to return to their country. Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, accompanied the distinguished guests to Nanning.

Vice Premier Chen Yi saw the distinguished guests off at the airport. At the airport, Prime Minister Sihanouk once again praised China's construction achievements to the thousands of well-wishers, and once again cheered for Sino-Cambodian friendship. He said: "You have made your country achieve things that some people thought were impossible, setting an example for the people of Asia and Africa." "Our visit to China has brought us renewed hope and courage in mastering our destiny." "The future of the people will be decided by themselves, and no foreign influence will be able to stop their will to demand progress and development." The Prime Minister concluded: "Dearest friends, I am not saying goodbye to you, but see you again soon!"

Before boarding the plane, Vice Premier Chen Yi and Wei Jinfei, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, extended their best wishes to the distinguished guests.

This morning, thousands of people gathered at the airport to see off the distinguished guests. Facing the young men and women who were dancing the lion dance, waving flowers, and cheering for Sino-Cambodian friendship, the Prince repeatedly clasped his hands in return. At exactly eight o'clock, when the Prince's special plane took off, the well-wishers cheered enthusiastically, bidding farewell to the distinguished guests.

Yang An, head of the Cambodian Economic Delegation to China, went to the airport to see off the Prince. Also present at the airport to see off the guests today were Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou, and his wife, Vice Mayor Sun Leyi, Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Guangzhou, Lieutenant General Zhan Caifang, Major General Qin Guohan, Rear Admiral Zeng Sheng, Air Force Major General Zhu Yunqian, Zhang Boquan, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Wang De, Chairman of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the CPPCC, Vice Chairman Chen Qiu'an, and the heads of local organizations of various democratic parties in Guangdong and Guangzhou.

Komoček, Consul of Czechoslovakia in Guangzhou, Le Chung Thuy, Acting Consul General of Vietnam in Guangzhou, and Nowakzyk, representative of the Polish Consulate General in Guangzhou, also went to the airport to see off the distinguished guests.

Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, August 27th: Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, and the National Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia led by him, after ending their visit to our country, took a special plane from Guangzhou to Nanning this morning to return to their country.

He Ximing, Vice Chairman of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, and his wife, Ruan Hongchuan, Vice Mayor of Nanning, and his wife, went to the airport to see off the distinguished guests.

### 西哈努克首相在广州告别时说  访问中国重获希望和勇气

1958-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　西哈努克首相在广州告别时说
    访问中国重获希望和勇气
    新华社广州27日电　西哈努克首相和柬埔寨王国国家代表团以及帕花·黛维公主、夏卡朋王子和索丽亚·伦赛公主等人，今天上午乘专机离开广州取道南宁回国。我外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文陪同贵宾们到南宁。
    陈毅副总理到机场欢送贵宾。在机场上，西哈努克首相对数千欢送群众再一次称赞中国的建设成就，再一次为中柬友谊欢呼。他说：“你们使得你们的国家实现了某些人认为是不可能的事情，为亚洲和非洲人民树立了榜样。”“我们在中国的访问给我们在掌握我们的命运方面重新带来了希望和勇气。”“人民的前途将由他们自己决定，任何外国影响都将无法阻挡他们要求进步和发展的意志。”首相最后说：“最亲爱的朋友们，我不是向你们告别，而是向你们说不久再见！”
    贵宾们在登机前，陈毅副总理和广东省副省长魏今非向贵宾们作最好的祝愿。
    今天早晨，数千群众在机场上欢送贵宾。对着舞起醒狮和挥动鲜花、欢呼中柬友谊的男女青年，亲王连连合十还礼。八时整，当亲王所乘专机起飞时，欢送的群众热烈欢呼，向贵宾告别。
    柬埔寨驻中国经济代表团团长杨安到机场送别亲王。今天到机场欢送的，还有广州市市长朱光及夫人，副市长孙乐宜，中国人民解放军驻广州部队首长詹才芳中将、覃国翰少将、曾生海军少将、朱云谦空军少将，政协广东省委员会副主席张?村，政协广州市委员会主席王德、副主席陈秋安以及各民主党派广东省、广州市地方组织的负责人。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻广州领事考谟克、越南驻广州代理总领事黎终始、波兰驻广州总领事馆代表诺华奇克也到机场欢送贵宾。
    新华社南宁27日电　柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和由他率领的柬埔寨王国国家代表团，在结束了对我国的访问以后，于今天上午乘专机由广州途经南宁回国。
    前往机场欢送贵宾的有广西僮族自治区副主席贺希明及其夫人，南宁市副市长阮洪川及其夫人等。
