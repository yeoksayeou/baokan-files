Title: Cuban Rebel Army Repels Government Army Attack
Author:
Date: 1958-08-05
Page: 3

    Cuban Rebel Army
    Repels Government Army Attack
    Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 4th—Cuban rebel forces have defeated a large-scale offensive by government troops in the Sierra Maestra mountains. According to a rebel radio broadcast on the 2nd, the Cuban government troops, despite being supported by tanks, planes, and artillery, have been routed in the past three days.
    The report said that in this battle, the government troops suffered fifty-one casualties, including twenty-five killed.

### 古巴起义军  击退政府军进攻

1958-08-05
第3版()
专栏：

    古巴起义军
    击退政府军进攻
    新华社4日讯　哈瓦那消息：古巴起义军在马埃斯特腊山区击溃了政府军的一次大规模进攻。据起义军电台2日报道，古巴政府军尽管有坦克、飞机和大炮的支援，他们在过去三天中已经被击溃。
    消息说，在这次战斗中，政府军伤亡五十一人，其中有二十五人被打死。
