Title: Nasser-Faisal Talks

Author:

Date: 1958-08-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 17th: Following the third meeting between President Nasser of the United Arab Republic and Crown Prince Faisal, Prime Minister of Saudi Arabia, on the morning of the 17th, the talks between the two sides have concluded. Faisal arrived in Cairo on the 15th.

### 纳赛尔同费萨尔会谈

1958-08-19
第4版()
专栏：

　  纳赛尔同费萨尔会谈
    据新华社开罗17日电　阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔同沙特阿拉伯首相费萨尔王储在17日上午举行了第三次会谈后，双方会谈已经结束。费萨尔是在15日到达开罗的。
