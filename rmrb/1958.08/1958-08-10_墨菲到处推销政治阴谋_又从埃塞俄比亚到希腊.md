Title: Murphy Peddling Political Conspiracies Everywhere, Moves from Ethiopia to Greece
Author:
Date: 1958-08-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 9th dispatch: Addis Ababa dispatch: Murphy, the U.S. President's "special envoy" who has been engaged in conspiratorial activities in the Middle East, has arrived in Addis Ababa, the capital of Ethiopia, from Cairo.

The Ethiopian Ministry of Foreign Affairs issued a communique saying that Murphy had a two-hour meeting with the Ethiopian Emperor on the 8th, discussing "issues related to the Middle East situation and two issues of common concern." According to Western news agencies, the content of the talks was "American activities and plans in the Middle East."

Murphy has left Addis Ababa for Athens.

### 墨菲到处推销政治阴谋  又从埃塞俄比亚到希腊

1958-08-10
第2版()
专栏：

    墨菲到处推销政治阴谋
    又从埃塞俄比亚到希腊
    据新华社9日讯　亚的斯亚贝巴消息：在中东各地进行阴谋活动的美国总统“特使”墨菲从开罗到达埃塞俄比亚首都亚的斯亚贝巴。
    埃塞俄比亚外交部发表公报说，墨菲同埃塞俄比亚国王在8日会谈两小时，讨论了“有关中东局势和两个共同关心的问题”。据西方通讯社报道，会谈内容是“美国在中东的活动和计划”。
    墨菲已经离开亚的斯亚贝巴前往雅典。
