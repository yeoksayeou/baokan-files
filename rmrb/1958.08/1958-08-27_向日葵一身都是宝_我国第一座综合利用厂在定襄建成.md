Title: Sunflowers are Treasures from Head to Toe: China's First Comprehensive Utilization Factory Built in Dingxiang
Author:
Date: 1958-08-27
Page: 3

　　Sunflowers are Treasures from Head to Toe
    China's First Comprehensive Utilization Factory Built in Dingxiang
    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: China's first comprehensive sunflower utilization factory was recently basically completed in Dingxiang County, Shanxi Province. This factory includes seven workshops: oil extraction, furfural, soap, soy sauce, pig feed, monosodium glutamate, and activated carbon. By adopting the method of designing, building, experimenting, and producing simultaneously, more than twenty products have already been trial-produced. After the sunflower harvest, each workshop will be put into formal production successively.
    The factory has also built a set of experimental equipment to continuously conduct experimental research on the comprehensive utilization of sunflowers in many aspects.

### 向日葵一身都是宝  我国第一座综合利用厂在定襄建成

1958-08-27
第3版()
专栏：

　　向日葵一身都是宝
    我国第一座综合利用厂在定襄建成
    据新华社讯　我国第一座向日葵综合利用工厂最近在山西省定襄县基本建成。这个厂包括榨油、糖醛、肥皂、酱油、猪饲料、味精、活性炭七个车间。由于采取了边设计边建厂边试验边生产的方法，到现在已经试制出二十多种产品。向日葵收获后各个车间都将陆续投入正式生产。
    这个工厂还建设了一套试验设备，要不断地从多方面进行向日葵综合利用的试验研究工作。
