Title: My Song and Dance Troupe is Very Popular in Poland; Acting Chairman of the Polish Council of Ministers Receives Principal Actors
Author:
Date: 1958-08-28
Page: 6

　　My Song and Dance Troupe is Very Popular in Poland
    Acting Chairman of the Polish Council of Ministers Receives Principal Actors
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, August 27th: The Chinese Opera and Dance Troupe gave its farewell performance last night in the Great Hall of the Palace of Culture and Science here. More than four thousand spectators watched the performance.
    The Chinese actors performed ten programs, each of which won stormy and lasting applause. After the performance, the audience applauded for a long time, asking the actors to come out and take a bow.
    Afterwards, Indryhofski, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party and Chairman of the Polish Chinese Friendship Association, took the stage and spoke, expressing his heartfelt gratitude for the wonderful performance of the Chinese actors.
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, August 27th: Acting Chairman of the Polish Council of Ministers, Novak, received Qu Wu, head of the Chinese Opera and Dance Troupe, and famous actors Li Yuru, Yan Huizhu, Yu Zhenfei, Zhang Meijuan, and others in Warsaw today, and had a conversation with them that lasted one and a half hours.

### 我歌舞团在波兰大受欢迎  波部长会议代理主席接见主要演员

1958-08-28
第6版()
专栏：

　　我歌舞团在波兰大受欢迎
    波部长会议代理主席接见主要演员
    据新华社华沙27日电　中国戏曲歌舞团昨天晚上在这里的文化科学宫的大厅举行了临别演出。有四千多名观众观看了这场表演。
    中国演员们表演了十个节目，每个节目都博得了暴风雨般的、经久不息的掌声。演出以后，观众们长期地鼓掌，让演员们出来谢幕。
    接着，波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员、波中友好协会主席英德里霍夫斯基上台讲话，他对中国演员的精彩表演表示衷心的感谢。
    据新华社华沙27日电　波兰部长会议代理主席诺瓦克今天在华沙接见了中国戏曲歌舞团团长屈武和著名演员李玉茹、言慧珠、俞振飞、张美娟等人，并且同他们进行了历时一个半小时的谈话。
