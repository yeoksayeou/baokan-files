Title: Chairman Mao Sends Congratulations on Swiss National Day
Author: Mao Zedong
Date: 1958-08-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 31st: Chairman Mao Zedong today sent a telegram to Thomas Holenstein, President of the Swiss Confederation, congratulating him on the National Day of the Swiss Confederation. The telegram reads as follows:
To His Excellency Thomas Holenstein, President of the Swiss Confederation:

    On the occasion of the National Day of the Swiss Confederation, I would like to extend congratulations to Your Excellency and the Swiss people. I wish the Swiss Confederation prosperity and the people happiness.

                Chairman of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong
              　July 31, 1958

### 毛主席电贺瑞士国庆
毛泽东
1958-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席电贺瑞士国庆
    新华社31日讯　毛泽东主席今天致电瑞士联邦主席托马斯·霍伦斯太，祝贺瑞士联邦国庆。电文如下：瑞士联邦主席托马斯·霍伦斯太先生阁下：
    当瑞士联邦国庆纪念的时候，我谨向阁下和瑞士人民表示祝贺。祝瑞士联邦繁荣、人民幸福。
                中华人民共和国主席  毛泽东
              　1958年7月31日
