Title: Iraqi Republic Forces Receive Ammunition Depot at British Air Force Base
Author:
Date: 1958-08-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 3rd: The Cairo newspaper "Al-Ahram" reported on the 2nd that the Iraqi Republic forces have taken over the ammunition and explosives depot at the British Habbaniyah Air Force Base in Iraq.

The newspaper also stated that the depot contains heavy bombs, explosives, and rockets. The Iraqi army is heavily guarding these ammunition depots.

### 伊拉克共和国部队  接收英空军基地军火库

1958-08-04
第2版()
专栏：

    伊拉克共和国部队
    接收英空军基地军火库
    新华社开罗3日电　开罗“金字塔报”2日报道，伊拉克共和国部队已经接收了英国在伊拉克的哈巴尼雅空军基地的弹药和炸药库。
    这家报纸又说，库内有重型炸弹、炸药和火箭。伊拉克军队正在严密守卫这些弹药库。
