Title: Our Air Force Shoots Down Two Chiang Planes

Author: 
Date: 1958-08-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, August 13th: The Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force shot down two American-made Chiang planes today.

　　A batch of two American-made Chiang Kai-shek RF-84 aircraft intruded into the airspace over the Fuzhou area at 12:24 p.m. Our Air Force immediately took off to intercept them, and both Chiang planes were damaged.

### 我空军击伤蒋机两架

1958-08-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　我空军击伤蒋机两架
    新华社福建前线13日电　中国人民解放军空军今天击伤美制蒋机两架。
    美制蒋军飞机RF—84型一批两架，于十二时二十四分窜扰我福州地区上空。我空军当即起飞迎击，蒋机两架都被击伤。
