Title: Iraqi Students Show Backbone: Reject American Aggressors' Dirty Money; Seven Students Receive International Union of Students Scholarships to Study in Eastern Europe
Author:
Date: 1958-08-25
Page: 4

　　Iraqi Students Show Backbone
    Reject American Aggressors' Dirty Money
    Seven Students Receive International Union of Students Scholarships to Study in Eastern Europe
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 23rd: According to a report from Baghdad by the Middle East News Agency today, Iraqi students refused to accept the fees provided by the United States Technical Assistance Administration for studying abroad. They said that they refused to receive education by accepting money from a government that is aggressing against Arab countries.
    The Iraqi National Student Union received a notification from the International Union of Students stating that seven scholarship slots have been allocated to Iraqi students. These scholarships are for Iraqi students to study in Eastern European socialist countries.

### 伊拉克学生有骨气  拒绝接受美国侵略者的臭钱  七名学生获得国际学联到东欧留学奖金

1958-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　伊拉克学生有骨气
    拒绝接受美国侵略者的臭钱
    七名学生获得国际学联到东欧留学奖金
    新华社开罗23日电　据中东通讯社今天自巴格达报道，伊拉克学生拒绝接受美国技术援助署提供的去国外留学的费用。他们说，他们拒绝通过接受侵略阿拉伯国家的政府的钱去受教育。
    伊拉克全国学生联合会接到国际学生联合会的通知，通知说，已经分配给伊拉克学生七个奖学金名额。这批奖学金是供伊拉克学生到东欧社会主义国家去留学的。
