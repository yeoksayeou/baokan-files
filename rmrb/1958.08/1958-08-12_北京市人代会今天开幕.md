Title: Beijing Municipal People's Congress Opens Today
Author:
Date: 1958-08-12
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 11th: The first session of the Third Beijing Municipal People's Congress will begin on the morning of the 12th. This was decided by the Beijing Municipal People's Committee at its eleventh meeting on the 9th.
　　At this meeting, the delegates will review and approve the final accounts of Beijing's financial revenue and expenditure for 1957 and the budget for 1958; listen to and review the work report of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee; elect the members of the Third Beijing Municipal People's Committee, the presidents of the higher and intermediate people's courts, and the deputies to the Second National People's Congress to be elected from Beijing.
　　Prior to this, all districts in Beijing held district people's congresses on the basis of universal suffrage and elected six hundred and eighty-one deputies to the Third Beijing Municipal People's Congress.

### 北京市人代会今天开幕

1958-08-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京市人代会今天开幕
    新华社11日讯　北京市第三届人民代表大会第一次会议将在12日上午开始举行。这是北京市人民委员会在9日召开的第十一次会议上决定的。
    在这次会上，代表们将审查批准北京市1957年财政收支决算和1958年财政收支预算；听取和审查北京市人民委员会的工作报告；选举北京市第三届人民委员会组成人员，高、中级人民法院院长和北京市应选的第二届全国人民代表大会代表。
    在此以前，北京市各区都在普选的基础上举行了区人民代表大会，选出了北京市第三届人民代表大会代表六百八十一人。
