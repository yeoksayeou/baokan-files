Title: 1,000-Kilometer Peace March Reaches Fujisawa
Author:
Date: 1958-08-08
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 6th. The "Hiroshima-Tokyo 1,000 Kilometer Peace March," determined to oppose nuclear weapons, has completed 900 kilometers and reached Fujisawa City, southwest of Tokyo, today.
    This peace delegation, led by Abbot Atsushi Nishimoto, representative of the Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, was warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life when it arrived in Fujisawa City. The Fujisawa City Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs held a civic meeting with more than 500 people in front of the Chinese composer Nie Er Memorial Monument on Kugenuma Beach in Fujisawa City.
    The meeting passed a resolution expressing its determination to resolutely carry out the struggle to ban atomic and hydrogen bombs.
    Yasui Kaoru, Director of the Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, Nishimoto Atsushi, representative of the Peace March, and the Indian representative attending the Fourth World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for Disarmament in Japan, laid flowers at the Nie Er Memorial Monument.
    When the march passed through Hiratsuka City and Chigasaki City before arriving in Fujisawa, it was welcomed by local mayors, city council speakers, and representatives from all walks of life, including labor unions and women.
    The "Hiroshima-Tokyo 1,000 Kilometer Peace March" team of the Japanese people demanding a ban on atomic and hydrogen bombs is advancing.

### 一千公里和平大游行到达藤泽

1958-08-08
第3版()
专栏：

    一千公里和平大游行到达藤泽
    新华社6日讯  东京消息：坚决反对核武器的“广岛—东京一千公里和平大游行”的队伍，已经走完九百公里的路程，今天到达了东京西南的藤泽市。
    这支由日本禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会代表西本笃和尚率领的和平队伍，在到达藤泽市时，受到了当地各界的热烈欢迎。藤泽市禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会在藤泽市鹄沼海岸的中国作曲家聂耳纪念碑前举行了有五百多人参加的市民大会。
    大会通过决议，表示要坚决进行禁止原子弹和氢弹的斗争。
    日本禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会理事长安井郁、和平大游行的代表西本笃、到日本出席第四届禁止原子弹氢弹和争取裁军世界大会的印度代表等都向聂耳纪念碑献花。
    游行队伍在到达藤泽以前路过平冢市和茅崎市时，受到了当地市长、市议会议长和工会、妇女等各界代表的欢迎。
    日本人民要求禁止原子弹和氢弹的“广岛—东京一千公里和平大游行”队伍在前进中。
