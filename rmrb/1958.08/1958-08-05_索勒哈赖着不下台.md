Title: Solh clings to power and refuses to step down
Author:
Date: 1958-08-05
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, August 3rd (TASS): According to newspaper reports, the Lebanese cabinet decided at a meeting on August 2nd that the current government would not resign before the end of President Chamoun's term (September 23rd).

    "Al-Sharq" wrote that Speaker Osseiran, as well as some parliamentarians and members of the opposition, firmly demanded that the Solh government resign immediately.

### 索勒哈赖着不下台

1958-08-05
第2版()
专栏：

    索勒哈赖着不下台
    据新华社4日讯　据塔斯社贝鲁特3日讯：据报纸报道，黎巴嫩内阁在2日举行的会议上，决定现政府在夏蒙总统任期届满（9月23日）以前不提出辞职。
    “东方报”写道，议长奥塞兰以及一些议员和反对派坚决要求索勒哈政府立刻辞职。
