　　Galaxy Ballad
　　Poem by Tian Jian, Painting by Wang Shuhui
　　In the style of a Bai ethnic folk song. The Bai ethnic group has a folk song that goes like this:

　　Leap Forward mountain songs fly to the sky,
　　Mountain songs fly to the edge of the Milky Way,
　　Inviting the Cowherd to descend to earth,
　　To see iron oxen plowing the fields.

　　The Milky Way is like a silver flute,
　　Hanging by the Cowherd's side;
　　The Cowherd drives the iron ox,
　　The Weaver Girl plays the silver flute.

　　The Cowherd leads the iron ox,
　　And reunites with the Weaver Girl;
　　There is spring water in the Milky Way,
　　To cultivate the fields in the sky.

　　Machines hum in the air,
　　Satellites fly in the sky;
　　The red flag of the General Line,
　　Is planted in front of the Heavenly Palace gate!

### 银河曲田间诗  王叔晖画
田间　王叔晖
1958-08-21
第8版()
专栏：

　　银河曲
　　田间诗  王叔晖画
　　和白族的一首民歌。白族有一首民歌，这样唱道：
　　跃进山歌飞上天，
　　山歌飞到银河边，
　　请得牛郎下凡来，
　　看看铁牛在犁田。
　　银河好比银笛，
　　挂在牛郎身边；
　　牛郎赶着铁牛，
　　织女把银笛吹。
　　牛郎牵上铁牛，
　　和织女来团圆；
　　银河里有泉水，
　　在天上来种田。
　　机器在空中响，
　　卫星在天上飞；
　　总路线的红旗，
　　插到天宫门前！
