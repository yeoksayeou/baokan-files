Title: Culture Street and Tea Station
Author:
Date: 1958-08-14
Page: 5

　　Culture Street and Tea Station
　　Primary school students in Shanghai are actively participating in the anti-illiteracy work in their neighborhoods during the summer vacation. Yuying Road Primary School has taken on the responsibility of eradicating illiteracy on a street near the school, with the aim of turning it into a Culture Street.
　　Children have set up tea stations at bus and tram stops, providing boiled water to ticket sellers, drivers, and People's Police.

### 文化街和茶水站

1958-08-14
第5版()
专栏：暑期生活

　  文化街和茶水站
    上海市的小学生们在暑假里积极参加里弄扫盲工作。育婴路小学把学校附近一条街的文盲全部包下来，他们要把这条街变成文化街。
    孩子们在公共汽车和电车站上设立了茶水站，把开水送给汽车、电车的售票员、司机和人民警察喝。
