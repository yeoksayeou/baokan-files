Title: Khrushchev Receives US Democratic Leader Stevenson, Voroshilov to Visit World Expo in Belgium
Author:
Date: 1958-08-07
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, August 6th, Moscow Dispatch: According to TASS, Soviet Premier Khrushchev received US Democratic leader Stevenson, who is currently visiting the Soviet Union, on the 5th and had a long talk with him.

Xinhua News Agency, August 6th, Moscow Dispatch: TASS reports that the Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Voroshilov, will visit the 1958 Brussels World's Fair. The visit will take place on August 11th to 13th, during the "Soviet Day" at the fair. Accompanying Voroshilov will be Soviet Minister of Health Kovrigina, First Deputy Minister of Culture Kaftanov, and Soviet Ambassador to Belgium Avilov.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见美民主党领袖史蒂文森  伏罗希洛夫将赴比参观世界博览会

1958-08-07
第3版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见美民主党领袖史蒂文森
    伏罗希洛夫将赴比参观世界博览会
    新华社6日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫5日接见了正在苏联访问的美国民主党领袖史蒂文森，并且同他进行了长时间的谈话。
    新华社6日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫将前往参观1958年度布鲁塞尔世界博览会。这次访问将在8月11日到13日博览会举行“苏联日”的时候进行。随同伏罗希洛夫前往的将有：苏联卫生部长科夫里金娜、文化部第一副部长卡弗坦诺夫以及苏联驻比利时大使阿维洛夫。
