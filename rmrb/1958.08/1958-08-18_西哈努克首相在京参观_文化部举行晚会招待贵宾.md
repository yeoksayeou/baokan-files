Title: Sihanouk Visits Beijing; Ministry of Culture Holds Evening Party to Entertain Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1958-08-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 17th - Prime Minister Norodom Sihanouk and the entire delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia, led by him, today visited the Beijing Steel Plant and the Beijing Institute of Iron and Steel Technology, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai.
　　Princess Norodom Soriya Lon-sai, accompanied by Madam Chen Yi and He Xiaoming, daughter of Vice Premier He Long, visited the Beijing Children's Palace and watched an aviation parachuting performance.
　　Xinhua News Agency, 17th - Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, watched songs and dances performed by actors of all nationalities in the capital at an evening party specially held by the Ministry of Culture to welcome him this evening.
　　Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, and Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing accompanied the Prime Minister and all distinguished guests to watch the songs and dances.
　　Yang An, head of the Cambodian Economic Delegation to China, also attended the evening party.
　　At the evening party, actors from the Wuhan Experimental Opera House, who had visited and performed in Cambodia last year, sang "Flowers of Vientiane," composed by Prince Norodom Sihanouk, and performed Cambodian songs and dances such as "Fairy Dance in the Garden of Blooming Flowers" and "Rice Pounding Song." With applause from the audience, the actors who sang "Flowers of Vientiane" sang it again after finishing it once.
　　After the entire performance, Prince Sihanouk, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai and others, walked onto the stage to shake hands with the actors cordially and presented them with flower baskets.

### 西哈努克首相在京参观　文化部举行晚会招待贵宾

1958-08-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　西哈努克首相在京参观
　　文化部举行晚会招待贵宾
    新华社17日讯　诺罗敦·西哈努克首相和由他率领的柬埔寨王国国家代表团的全体贵宾，今天由周恩来总理陪同，参观了北京钢厂和北京钢铁工业学院。
    诺罗敦·索丽娅·伦赛公主由陈毅夫人和贺龙副总理的女儿贺小明陪同，参观了北京少年宫和航空跳伞表演。
    新华社17日讯　柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王今天晚间在文化部专门为欢迎他举行的晚会上，观看了首都各民族演员演出的歌舞。
    周恩来总理，贺龙副总理，陈毅副总理兼外交部长和文化部沈雁冰部长，陪同首相和全体贵宾观看歌舞。
    柬埔寨驻中国经济代表团团长杨安也参加了晚会。
    晚会上，去年曾经到柬埔寨访问演出过的武汉实验歌剧院演员演唱了诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王作曲的“万象之花”，演出了柬埔寨歌舞“百花盛开的花园中的仙女舞”、“舂米歌”等。在全场观众鼓掌欢迎下，演唱“万象之花”的演员唱完一遍以后又重唱了一遍。
    演出全部结束后，西哈努克亲王由周恩来总理等陪同走上舞台同演员们亲切握手，并向演员们赠送了花篮。
