Title: Elaboration on Basic Problems of Scientific Socialism Theory; Soviet Union Publishes Scientific Socialism Series
Author:
Date: 1958-08-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 13th: According to the recently published tenth issue of the "Communist" magazine, the Soviet State Political Books Publishing House has begun publishing a Scientific Socialism series in order to introduce the basic problems of the theory and history of scientific socialism to a wide audience.

This series includes all the excellent Marxist literature related to scientific socialism. The series elaborates on the following most important issues of the theory of scientific socialism: the Marxist-Leninist doctrine on the inevitability of capitalist collapse and socialist victory, socialist revolution, class and class struggle, the world historical mission of the proletariat, the dictatorship of the proletariat, socialist and communist construction in the Soviet Union and people's democratic countries, the communist and workers' movement, the strategy and tactics of communist and workers' parties in the struggle for socialism and communism, proletarian internationalism, the universal laws and characteristics of the transition from capitalism to socialism in various countries, socialist democracy, the gradual transition from socialism to communism, the characteristics of the liberation struggle at the present stage, modern pseudo-socialism, the criticism of revisionism and dogmatism, issues of communist morality, and other issues of scientific socialism.

The series consists of seventy volumes, some of which are complete works, and some are collections of articles compiled according to issues. Many works were published a long time ago, and some are being published in Russian for the first time.

The series will include the works of the founders of scientific communism, Marx, Engels, and Lenin, which elaborate on the problems of scientific socialism. The series includes the works of Stalin, Mao Zedong, Khrushchev, Thälmann, Gottwald, Dimitrov, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Pieck, Thorez, Duclos, Togliatti, Novotný, Ulbricht, Gheorghiu-Dej, Foster, Ibárruri, and other outstanding activists of the international communist movement and the workers' movement. Some volumes include the works of outstanding propagators and promoters of scientific socialism at the end of the 19th and the beginning of the 20th century, such as Plekhanov, Bebel, Lafargue, Luxemburg, Blagoev, Mehring, Wilhelm Liebknecht, Karl Liebknecht, Labriola, etc.

The entire Scientific Socialism series is scheduled to be published within three years. Twenty volumes will be published this year, and twenty-five volumes will be published in 1959 and 1960 respectively.

The target audience of this series is the vast number of Soviet readers - party, Soviet, trade union and Communist Youth League activists, those who are self-studying Marxist-Leninist theory, propagandists and agitators, and teachers and students of higher and secondary specialized schools.

### 阐述科学社会主义理论基本问题　苏联出版科学社会主义丛书

1958-08-15
第5版()
专栏：

　　阐述科学社会主义理论基本问题
　　苏联出版科学社会主义丛书
　　丛书中包括马克思、恩格斯、列宁和国际共产主义运动和工人运动杰出活动家的著作
    新华社莫斯科13日电　据最近出版的第十期“共产党人”杂志报道：苏联国家政治书籍出版局开始出版科学社会主义丛书，以便向广大读者介绍科学社会主义理论和历史的基本问题。
    这套丛书包括有关科学社会主义的全部优秀的马克思主义文献。丛书中阐述科学社会主义理论的下列最重要的问题：马克思列宁主义关于资本主义崩溃和社会主义胜利的必然性的学说、社会主义革命、阶级和阶级斗争、无产阶级的世界历史使命、无产阶级专政、苏联和人民民主国家的社会主义建设和共产主义建设、共产主义运动和工人运动、共产党和工人党在争取社会主义和共产主义的斗争中的战略和策略、无产阶级国际主义、各个国家从资本主义到社会主义的过渡时期的普遍规律和特点、社会主义民主、从社会主义到共产主义的逐步过渡、现阶段解放斗争的特点、现代假社会主义、修正主义和教条主义的批判、共产主义道德问题和科学社会主义的其他问题。
    丛书共有七十分册，有的是整部的著作，有的则是按问题编成的文集。有许多著作在很久以前出版过，有些是第一次用俄文出版。
    丛书中将有科学共产主义创始人马克思、恩格斯和列宁的阐述科学社会主义问题的著作。丛书中包括斯大林、毛泽东、赫鲁晓夫、台尔曼、哥特瓦尔德、季米特洛夫、刘少奇、周恩来、皮克、多列士、杜克洛、陶里亚蒂、诺沃提尼、乌布利希、乔治乌—德治、福斯特、伊巴露丽和国际共产主义运动和工人运动其他杰出活动家的著作。有一些分册收入了十九世纪末和二十世纪初杰出的科学社会主义传播者和宣传家的著作，他们是普列汉诺夫、倍倍尔、拉法格、卢森堡、布拉戈耶夫、梅林、威·李卜克内西、卡·李卜克内西、拉布里奥拉等。
    科学社会主义丛书计划在三年内全部出齐。今年出版二十分册，1959和1960年分别出版二十五分册。
    这套丛书的对象是广大的苏联读者——党的、苏维埃的、工会的和共青团的积极分子，马克思列宁主义理论自修者，宣传员和鼓动员，高等和中等专业学校的教员和学生。
