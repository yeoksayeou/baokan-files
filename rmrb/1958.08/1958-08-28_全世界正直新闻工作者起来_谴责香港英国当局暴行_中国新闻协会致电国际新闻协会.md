Title: Worldwide Upright Journalists Rise Up to Condemn the Atrocities of the British Authorities in Hong Kong; All-China Journalists Association Sends a Telegram to the International Organization of Journalists
Author:
Date: 1958-08-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 27th. The All-China Journalists Association today sent a telegram to the International Organization of Journalists, calling on the International Organization of Journalists and all upright journalists around the world to jointly condemn the atrocities of the British authorities in Hong Kong for beating Chinese journalists.

The telegram, after recounting the incident of the British authorities in Hong Kong beating Chinese journalists, said that this atrocity by the British authorities in Hong Kong, which infringes on the personal freedom of the Chinese people and uses force to interfere with the rights of Chinese journalists to report, has aroused great indignation among Chinese journalists and the Chinese people. In addition to strongly protesting to the British government against this barbaric act by the British authorities in Hong Kong, the All-China Journalists Association calls on the International Organization of Journalists and all upright journalists around the world to jointly condemn this unreasonable action by the British authorities in Hong Kong in order to safeguard the personal safety of journalists and the legitimate rights of news coverage.

### 全世界正直新闻工作者起来  谴责香港英国当局暴行  中国新闻协会致电国际新闻协会

1958-08-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　全世界正直新闻工作者起来
    谴责香港英国当局暴行
    中国新闻协会致电国际新闻协会
    新华社27日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会今天打电报给国际新闻工作者协会，呼吁国际新闻工作者协会和全世界正直新闻工作者共同谴责香港英国当局殴打中国新闻记者的暴行。
    电报在叙述了香港英国当局殴打中国记者的事件经过以后说，香港英国当局这种侵犯中国人民人身自由，武力干涉中国记者采访权利的暴行，已引起中国新闻工作者和中国人民的极大愤慨。中华全国新闻工作者协会除了向英国政府强烈抗议香港英国当局这种野蛮行为以外，并呼吁国际新闻工作者协会和全世界正直新闻工作者共同谴责香港英国当局的这种无理行动，以维护新闻工作者的人身安全和新闻采访的正当权利。
