Title: Production Needs Cultured People Even More
Author:
Date: 1958-08-09
Page: 7

    Plant seedlings early to enjoy shade sooner,
    Learn production techniques early for greater skill,
    When planting trees, make sure they're firmly set,
    Production needs cultured people even more.
     —Anhui, Lu'an

### 生产更要文化人

1958-08-09
第7版()
专栏：

    生产更要文化人
    早栽树苗早成荫，
    早学生产技术精，
    栽树要把树插稳，
    生产更要文化人。
     ——安徽六安
