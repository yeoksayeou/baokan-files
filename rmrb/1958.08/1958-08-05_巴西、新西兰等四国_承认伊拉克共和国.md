Title: Brazil, New Zealand and Two Other Countries Recognize the Republic of Iraq
Author:
Date: 1958-08-05
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Rio de Janeiro, August 2nd: The Brazilian Foreign Ministry announced on the evening of August 2nd that the Brazilian government has recognized the government of the Republic of Iraq.
    Xinhua News Agency, Wellington, August 4th: New Zealand Prime Minister Nash announced today the recognition of the government of the Republic of Iraq.
    Xinhua News Agency, Manila, August 4th: The Philippines announced today the recognition of the government of the Republic of Iraq.
    Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, August 4th: The Thai government announced today the recognition of the government of the Republic of Iraq.

### 巴西、新西兰等四国  承认伊拉克共和国

1958-08-05
第2版()
专栏：

    巴西、新西兰等四国
    承认伊拉克共和国
    新华社4日讯　里约热内卢消息：巴西外交部2日晚宣布，巴西政府已经承认伊拉克共和国政府。
    新华社4日讯　惠灵顿消息：新西兰内阁总理纳希今天宣布承认伊拉克共和国政府。
    新华社4日讯　马尼拉消息：菲律宾今天宣布承认伊拉克共和国政府。
    新华社4日讯　曼谷消息：泰国政府今天宣布承认伊拉克共和国政府。
