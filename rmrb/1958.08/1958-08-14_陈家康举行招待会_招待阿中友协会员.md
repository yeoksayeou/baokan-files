Title: Chen Jiakang Holds Reception for Members of the Arab-China Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1958-08-14
Page: 6
　　
　　Chen Jiakang Holds Reception
　　For Members of the Arab-China Friendship Association

　　Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 12th - Chen Jiakang, the Chinese Ambassador to the United Arab Republic, held a reception at the embassy tonight for members of the Arab-China Friendship Association.

　　Approximately two hundred guests attended the reception, including Bakuri, Minister of Religious Endowments of the United Arab Republic and President of the Arab-China Friendship Association, Minister of Industry Sidqi, as well as the Minister of Interior of the Egyptian Region Mohieddin, Minister of Economy and Trade Kaissouni, Minister of Communications Khalil, Minister of Municipal and Rural Affairs Nusseir, and Minister of Supply Stino.

　　Almost all the delegations from the United Arab Republic who have visited China since 1955 attended the reception.

　　Two films were shown at the reception. One was a documentary about the demonstrations in Beijing protesting the occupation of Lebanon and Jordan by the United States and Britain, and the other was the feature film "Shangganling."

　　The Arab-China Friendship Association was established in Cairo in June of this year.

　　The Director-General of the Presidential Palace, Ali Rashid, wrote a letter to Ambassador Chen Jiakang today, informing him that President Nasser hopes the association will be successful.

### 陈家康举行招待会　招待阿中友协会员

1958-08-14
第6版()
专栏：

　  陈家康举行招待会
　　招待阿中友协会员
    新华社开罗12日电　中国驻阿拉伯联合共和国大使陈家康今晚在大使馆为阿拉伯—中国友好协会的会员们举行了一次招待会。
    约有二百位客人出席了招待会，其中包括阿拉伯联合共和国宗教基金部长、阿拉伯—中国友好协会会长巴库里，工业部长西德基，以及埃及地区的内政部长毛希丁、经济与贸易部长凯舒尼、交通部长哈利勒、城乡事务部长努赛尔和供应部长斯提诺。
    几乎所有从1955年以来访问过中国的阿拉伯联合共和国的代表团都出席了这次招待会。
    会上放映了两部影片。一部是北京举行的抗议美英占领黎巴嫩和约旦的示威游行情况的纪录片，另一部是故事片“上甘岭”。
    阿拉伯—中国友好协会是在今年6月在开罗成立的。
    总统府总管阿里·拉希德今天写信给陈家康大使，告诉他纳赛尔总统希望这个协会获得成功。
