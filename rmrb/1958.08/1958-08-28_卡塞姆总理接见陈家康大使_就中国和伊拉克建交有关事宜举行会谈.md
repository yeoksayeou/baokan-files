Title: Prime Minister Qasim Receives Ambassador Chen Jiakang to Discuss Matters Related to the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between China and Iraq
Author:
Date: 1958-08-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, August 26th: Iraqi Republic Prime Minister Qasim received Chen Jiakang, the Chinese Ambassador to the United Arab Republic, at noon today and held talks with him. Ambassador Chen Jiakang arrived here on August 21st, following instructions from the Chinese government, to hold talks with the government of the Iraqi Republic on matters related to the establishment of diplomatic relations between the two countries.

During today's talks, Prime Minister Qasim and Ambassador Chen Jiakang exchanged views on strengthening the friendly relations between China and Iraq. Chen Jiakang conveyed the congratulations of Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people. Prime Minister Qasim requested Chen Jiakang to convey his best wishes to Chairman Mao Zedong, Premier Zhou Enlai, and the Chinese people. Earlier, Chen Jiakang met with Iraqi Minister of State Guidance and Acting Foreign Minister Shanshal, Minister of Economy Kubba, and Minister of Finance Hadid, and discussed with them the development of friendship and economic relations between the two countries.

### 卡塞姆总理接见陈家康大使  就中国和伊拉克建交有关事宜举行会谈

1958-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　卡塞姆总理接见陈家康大使
    就中国和伊拉克建交有关事宜举行会谈
　　新华社巴格达26日电　伊拉克共和国总理卡塞姆今天中午接见了中国驻阿拉伯联合共和国大使陈家康，并且同他进行了会谈。陈家康大使是根据中国政府的指示，为了同伊拉克共和国政府就两国之间建立外交关系的有关事宜进行会谈，在8月21日到达这里的。
    在今天的会谈中，卡塞姆总理和陈家康大使就加强中国和伊拉克之间的友好关系的问题交换了意见。陈家康转达了中华人民共和国毛泽东主席和中国人民的祝贺。卡塞姆总理要求陈家康向毛泽东主席、周恩来总理和中国人民转达他的最好的祝愿。早些时候，陈家康会见了伊拉克国家指导部长兼代理外交部长尚沙尔、经济部长库巴和财政部长哈迪德，同他们讨论了发展两国之间的友谊和经济关系的问题。
