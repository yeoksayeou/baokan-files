Title: Premier Zhou Watches Quyi Performance
Author:
Date: 1958-08-05
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, August 4th - Premier Zhou Enlai watched a performance of the First National Quyi Festival in Beijing tonight.

    At the end of the performance, Premier Zhou Enlai went on stage to meet the actors and had a cordial conversation with them.
    Also present tonight were Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Ministers Zheng Zhenduo, Xia Yan, Liu Zhiming, and others.

### 周总理观看曲艺演出

1958-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理观看曲艺演出
    新华社4日讯　周恩来总理今天晚上在北京观看了第一届全国曲艺会演的演出。
    在演出结束的时候，周恩来总理登上舞台同演员们见了面，并且进行了亲切的谈话。
    今天晚上前来观看演出的，还有文化部部长沈雁冰、副部长郑振铎、夏衍、刘芝明等。
