Title: The Correct Way to Overcome the Middle East Crisis: Indonesian Peace Committee Supports Summit Conference
Author:
Date: 1958-08-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 31st. The Indonesian Peace Committee today issued another statement expressing its support for the convening of a summit conference and condemning the aggressive acts of the United States and Britain.

The statement said that the quickest way to solve the Middle East issue is to quickly convene a summit conference with the participation of all major powers and relevant countries.

The statement expressed its support for the efforts made by the Indonesian government to resolve the Middle East issue. The statement said that the Indonesian government's prompt recognition of the Republic of Iraq strengthened the forces of peace against war-mongers.

The statement said that the dispatch of troops by the United States and Britain to Lebanon and Jordan was a brutal intervention in these countries and a threat to the Republic of Iraq, thereby exacerbating international tensions. The statement firmly demanded that the United States and Britain immediately withdraw their troops from Lebanon and Jordan.

The statement also condemned the Baghdad Pact as a source of international tension.

### 克服中东危机的正确办法  印度尼西亚和委会支持开首脑会议

1958-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    克服中东危机的正确办法
    印度尼西亚和委会支持开首脑会议
    据新华社雅加达31日电　印度尼西亚和平委员会今天再次发表声明，表示支持召开政府首脑会议，并且谴责美国和英国的侵略行为。
    声明说，迅速召开一个有所有大国和有关国家参加的政府首脑会议是解决中东问题最正确的办法。
    声明表示支持印度尼西亚政府为解决中东问题而作的努力。声明说，印度尼西亚政府迅速承认伊拉克共和国，加强了反对战争煽动者的和平力量。
    声明说，美国和英国派兵到黎巴嫩和约旦，是对这些国家的残暴干涉，并且是对伊拉克共和国的威胁，从而加剧了国际紧张局势。声明坚决要求美国和英国立即把军队撤出黎巴嫩和约旦。
    声明还谴责巴格达条约是一个造成国际紧张局势的根源。
