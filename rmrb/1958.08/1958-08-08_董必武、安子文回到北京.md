Title: Dong Biwu and An Ziwen Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-08-08
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, July 7th: Dong Biwu, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and An Ziwen, member of the Central Committee, returned to Beijing today after participating in the Seventh Congress of the Bulgarian Communist Party, the Eleventh Congress of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, and Fifth Congress of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany.

### 董必武、安子文回到北京

1958-08-08
第4版()
专栏：

    董必武、安子文回到北京
    新华社7日讯  中共中央政治局委员董必武、中央委员安子文，在参加保加利亚共产党第七次代表大会、捷克斯洛伐克共产党第十一次代表大会、德国统一社会党第五次代表大会后，已经在今天回到北京。
