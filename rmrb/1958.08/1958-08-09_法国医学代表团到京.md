Title: French Medical Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-08-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 8th. At the invitation of the Chinese Medical Association, the French medical delegation, consisting of six members and led by Professor René Émile Faville, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of August 7.
    The delegation is composed of well-known figures from the French medical community. During their visit to China, they will travel to various locations to give short lectures and conduct sightseeing visits.

### 法国医学代表团到京

1958-08-09
第4版()
专栏：

    法国医学代表团到京
    新华社8日讯  应中华医学会邀请前来我国访问的法国医学代表团一行六人，在团长雷纳·埃米尔·法维尔教授率领下，于8月7日下午乘机到达北京。
    代表团系由法国医学界著名人士组成，他们在我国访问期间，将赴各地作短期讲学和进行参观访问。
