Title: Argentine Railway Workers Strike
Author:
Date: 1958-08-20
Page: 5

　  Argentine Railway Workers Strike
    Xinhua News Agency, August 19th, Buenos Aires dispatch: The 250,000 railway employees in Argentina held a 24-hour general strike on the 17th. The strikers demanded increased wages and pensions.
    Due to the strike, Argentina's domestic railway transportation was paralyzed.

### 阿根廷铁路工人罢工

1958-08-20
第5版()
专栏：

　  阿根廷铁路工人罢工
    新华社19日讯  布宜诺斯艾利斯消息：阿根廷的二十五万铁路员工在17日举行了二十四小时总罢工。罢工者要求增加工资和养老金。
    由于罢工，阿根廷国内的铁路交通陷于瘫痪。
