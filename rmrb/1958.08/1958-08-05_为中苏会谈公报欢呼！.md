Title: Hail the Sino-Soviet Communiqué!
Author: Guo Moruo
Date: 1958-08-05
Page: 8

    Hail the Sino-Soviet Communiqué!
    Guo Moruo
    Comrade Khrushchev came to Beijing,
    He and Comrade Mao Zedong made a statement.
    This is a timely electric shock,
    That can cure the war maniacs of the US and Britain.
    China and the Soviet Union love peace,
    But we will never beg for peace.
    China and the Soviet Union oppose war,
    But we are never afraid of war.
    The will of the people is our weapon,
    International justice is our sturdy shield.
    Resisting aggression is our banner,
    Friendly cooperation is our Great Wall.
    We sympathize with the oppressed nations,
    We hate the rampant colonialists.
    We warn the war maniacs:
    Maniacs, you should wake up quickly!
    American troops get out of Lebanon quickly!
    Britain, withdraw your troops from Jordan quickly!
    Today, peaceful negotiation is still possible,
    Let all countries in the world be equal, large and small.
    We sympathize with our Arab brothers,
    We echo them in unison.
    Smash the shackles of colonialism,
    Yesterday's slaves will be masters.
    The people of Iraq have the right to revolution,
    The Arab people have the right to emancipate themselves.
    If you, the United States and Britain, want to interfere,
    May I ask: how do you deal with your ancestors?
    Outrageous, truly outrageous!
    Unreasonable things must not be tolerated!
    If anyone insists on starting a world war,
    Then let him be punished by the people!
    Mussolini's body was torn apart after being shot,
    Hitler committed suicide and was cremated.
    The end of war criminals is no different,
    The power of the people is far greater than the hydrogen bomb.
    Let's not talk about intercontinental ballistic rockets,
    Nor about artificial Earth satellites,
    As long as you come to talk about peaceful coexistence,
    Why must you dig your own grave?
    If a third world war breaks out,
    The imperialists will be wiped out.
    This is a truth clearer than the sun,
    No one can reverse the wheels of history!
    Comrade Khrushchev, we heartily congratulate you,
    The purity of Marxism-Leninism is even more firm and stable;
    The unbreakable brotherly friendship between China and the Soviet Union,
    Will form a just core for world peace.
    The fraternal camp of peace, democracy, and socialism
    The unity of nine hundred million people is related to the fate of mankind,
    We will certainly be able to safeguard world peace,
    We will certainly be able to eliminate wars of aggression!
        August 4, 1958

### 为中苏会谈公报欢呼！
郭沫若
1958-08-05
第8版()
专栏：

    为中苏会谈公报欢呼！
    郭沫若
    赫鲁晓夫同志来到北京，
    他和毛泽东同志作了声明。
    这是一种适时的电气休克，
    可以医疗美英的战争狂人。
    中苏两国是热爱和平，
    但我们决不乞讨和平。
    中苏两国是反对战争，
    但我们决不害怕战争。
    人民意志是我们的武器，
    国际道义是我们的干盾。
    反抗侵略是我们的旗帜，
    友好合作是我们的长城。
    我们同情被压迫的民族，
    我们憎恨殖民者的横行。
    我们向战争狂人们警告：
    狂人们啊，应该赶快清醒！
    美军赶快滚出黎巴嫩！
    英国赶快从约旦撤兵！
    今天也还能和平协商，
    让世界各国大小平等。
    我们同情阿拉伯弟兄，
    我们和他们同声相应。
    粉碎殖民主义的枷锁，
    昨天的奴隶要做主人。
    伊拉克人民有权革命，
    阿拉伯人民有权翻身。
    你美国英国要横加干涉，
    请问：怎样处理你们的先人？
    岂有此理，真是岂有此理！
    不合理的事断断不能容忍！
    谁个一定要发动世界大战，
    那就使他受到人民的处分！
    墨索里尼枪毙后尸体分崩，
    希特勒自杀后遭了火焚。
    战争罪人的末路没有两样，
    人民的威力超过氢弹远甚。
    且不谈洲际弹道火箭，
    也不谈人造地球卫星，
    只要你们来谈和平共处，
    为什么一定要自掘坟坑？
    第三次世界大战如果爆发，
    帝国主义者就会一扫而尽。
    这是比太阳还要明显的真实，
    谁也不能够逆转历史的车轮！
    赫鲁晓夫同志，我们衷心庆幸，
    马克思列宁主义的纯洁更加坚稳；
    中苏两国牢不可破的兄弟友谊，
    要为世界和平形成正义的核心。
    和平民主社会主义的兄弟阵营
    九亿人民的团结关系人类命运，
    我们一定能够维护世界和平，
    我们一定能够消灭侵略战争！
        1958年8月4日
