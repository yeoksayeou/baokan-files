Title: Collection of Cheers
Author: Zāng Kèjiā
Date: 1958-08-09
Page: 8

    I.
    A bright and brand-new political satellite,
    Rose into the sky from Beijing,
    People all over the world clapped and cheered,
    War maniacs were frightened at the sight.
    II.
    Aggressors clamor for war,
    Human history has reached a critical juncture!
    Two giants stand tall together,
    Extending their hands towards the avenue of peace.
    III.
    A new sun appears again in the sky,
    Radiating immense light,
    The war clouds cannot stand their ground,
    The people's fighting will is high.
    IV.
    Two big hands clasped together,
    The Sino-Soviet friendship is as solid as gold and stone!
    The peace fortress is unbreakable,
    The power of socialism is invincible!
    V.
    Gold is the most precious in the world,
    A word on the "Communiqué" is worth more than a thousand gold pieces,
    It concerns the fate of humankind now living,
    It concerns our future generations.
    VI.
    War vendors draw their swords and brandish their bows,
    The giant palm of peace presses them onto the table,
    "Withdraw the invading troops!"
    If you don't comply, there is no other way to think.
    VII.
    Words expressing the hopes of millions of people,
    The sound will forever remain on the historical record;
    Oh, embodiment of the great force of peace,
    Calling for the strength of the struggle for peace.
    VIII.
    This news is a great victory report,
    Spread everywhere on the wings of the east wind,
    Flying all over China, flying all over the Soviet Union,
    The world is swept up and runs with it.
    August 4th, Qingdao

### 欢呼集

1958-08-09
第8版()
专栏：

    欢呼集
    臧克家
    一
    一颗崭亮的政治卫星，
    从北京升上了天空，
    全世界人民拍手欢呼，
    战争狂人看见心惊。
    二
    侵略者叫嚣战争，
    人类历史到了紧要关头！
    两个巨人挺立在一起，
    向和平大道伸出了手。
    三
    天空里又出现一个新的太阳，
    放射着万丈光芒，
    战争乌云站不住脚，
    人民战斗的意志昂扬。
    四
    两只大手握在一起，
    金石一般的中苏友谊！
    和平堡垒牢不可破，
    社会主义的威力无敌！
    五
    世界上黄金最贵重，
    “公报”上一字胜过千金，
    它关系着脸前人类的命运，
    它关系着我们的后代子孙。
    六
    战争贩子剑拔弩张，
    和平巨掌把它按到桌上，
    “把侵略军队撤出去！”
    不照办，再也无法可想。
    七
    口里说出了千万人的希望，
    声音永远留在历史的片子上；
    伟大和平力量的化身呵，
    呼唤着和平斗争的力量。
    八
    这消息是一个大捷报，
    凭着东风到处传告，
    飞遍中国，飞遍苏联，
    世界被它卷起飞跑。
    8月4日于青岛
