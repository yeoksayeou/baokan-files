Title: World Parachuting Championships Conclude: Soviet Union Wins Men's and Women's Team and Individual Championships
Author:
Date: 1958-08-13
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 11th: The Fourth World Parachuting Championships concluded on the 10th in Bratislava. Soviet athletes won the world men's and women's team and individual championships.

    The Czechoslovakian men's team won the second place, followed by Bulgaria, Poland, Yugoslavia, and the United States. The Polish women's team won the second place, followed by Bulgaria, Romania, Czechoslovakia, and Hungary.

    Soviet athlete Ostrovsky, who has only been participating in parachuting for three years, won the world men's parachuting title with a total score of 1869.16 points. In the last event of the competition—the collective delayed opening accuracy landing competition with a free fall of 15 to 23 seconds from an altitude of 1,500 meters—he was only 2.86 meters away from the center target. Soviet athlete Priyachina won the world women's parachuting individual title with a total score of 1774.30 points.

    Nearly eighty athletes from fifteen countries participated in this World Championship.

### 世界跳伞锦标赛结束　苏联获得男女团体和个人冠军

1958-08-13
第7版()
专栏：

    世界跳伞锦标赛结束
　　苏联获得男女团体和个人冠军
    据新华社布拉格11日电　第四届世界跳伞锦标赛10日在布拉迪斯拉发结束。苏联运动员获得了世界男、女的团体冠军和个人冠军。
    男子队捷克斯洛伐克得亚军，后面是保加利亚、波兰、南斯拉夫、美国。女子队波兰得亚军，后面的是保加利亚、罗马尼亚、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利。
    参加跳伞运动只有三年历史的苏联运动员奥斯特罗夫斯基以一千八百六十九点一六分的总成绩获得了男子跳伞的世界冠军称号。这次比赛的最后一个项目——从一千五百公尺高空自由降落十五秒到二十三秒的集体延迟开伞准确着地比赛中，他距离中心目标只有二点八六公尺。获得世界女子跳伞个人冠军的是苏联的普里雅契娜，总成绩一千七百七十四点三○分。
    参加这次世界锦标赛一共有十五个国家的近八十名选手。
