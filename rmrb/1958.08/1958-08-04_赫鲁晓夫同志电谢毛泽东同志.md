Title: Comrade Khrushchev Sends Telegram of Thanks to Comrade Mao Zedong
Author:
Date: 1958-08-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 3rd—Nikita Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong upon leaving Chinese territory, expressing his gratitude for the warm and friendly reception he received. The telegram reads as follows:

To Comrade Mao Zedong, Beijing

Upon leaving the territory of fraternal China, please accept my heartfelt thanks for your warm and friendly hospitality, and express my complete satisfaction with the results of our joint work and our fruitful talks. These talks will further strengthen the unbreakable friendship between our two parties and the people of our two countries, will promote the consolidation of international peace, and the victory of our common cause.

With Communist greetings,

\t\t\t\tNikita Khrushchev
\t\t\t\tAugust 3, 1958

### 赫鲁晓夫同志电谢毛泽东同志

1958-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫同志电谢毛泽东同志
    新华社3日讯　苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记、苏联部长会议主席尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫在离开中国国境时致电毛泽东主席，对他受到的亲切友好的接待表示感谢，电文如下：北京毛泽东同志
    在离开兄弟中国的国境时，请接受我对你们亲切友好的招待的衷心感谢，并且对于我们的共同工作和我们的有益会谈的结果表示完全满意。这次会谈将进一步加强我们两党和两国人民之间的牢不可破的友谊，将促进国际和平的巩固和我们共同事业的胜利。
    致共产主义敬礼
                                   尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫
                                  　1958年8月3日（附图片）
