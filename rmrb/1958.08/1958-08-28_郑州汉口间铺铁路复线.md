Title: Zhengzhou-Hankou Railway Double-Tracking

Author:
Date: 1958-08-28
Page: 2

　　Zhengzhou-Hankou Railway Double-Tracking
    Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, August 27th. The double-tracking project of the Zhengzhou-Hankou section of the Beijing-Guangzhou Railway has recently started construction in sections. The double-track section is 514 kilometers long. The double-track project from Zhengzhou to Anyang on the Beijing-Guangzhou Railway has been put into operation. The double-track section from Anyang to Beijing is being laid at a rapid pace. A section of double-tracking from Wuchang to Puqi is also under construction.

### 郑州汉口间铺铁路复线

1958-08-28
第2版()
专栏：

　　郑州汉口间铺铁路复线
    新华社郑州27日电　京广铁路郑州汉口段复线工程在最近开始分段施工。这段复线工程全长五百一十四公里。京广铁路从郑州到安阳的复线工程已经交付使用；安阳到北京的复线正在紧张铺设；由武昌到蒲圻的一段复线也在修筑中。
