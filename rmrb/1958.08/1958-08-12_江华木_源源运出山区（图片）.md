Title: "Jianghua Wood" Continuously Transported Out of Mountainous Areas (Photo)
Author: Wang Ping
Date: 1958-08-12
Page: 2

The national famous "Jianghua Wood" timber production area - Jianghua Yao Autonomous County, Hunan Province, this year's timber production has been completed 170 days ahead of schedule with a surplus of 85 cubic meters of the state's production tasks.

The forestry workers here adopted methods such as early logging, accelerated transportation, and timber inspection to achieve this result. At present, the employees here have begun to prepare for the 1959 timber production. The picture below shows "Jianghua Wood" being transported to various places through waterways.

Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Ping

### “江华木”源源运出山区（图片）
王平
1958-08-12
第2版()
专栏：

　　“江华木”源源运出山区
    全国著名的“江华木”木材产区——湖南省江华瑶族自治县，今年木材生产已提前一百七十天以超额八十五立方公尺的成绩完成国家的生产任务。
    这里的林业工人是采用了提前采伐，加快运输和木材验收等工作，而取得这项成绩的。目前，这里的职工已经开始作1959年的木材生产准备工作。下图是“江华木”正通过河道运到各地去。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　新华社记者　王平摄
