Title: Iraqi Traitor Jamali Confesses: Imperialism Planned to Oppose the United Arab Republic
Author:
Date: 1958-08-05
Page: 2

    Iraqi Traitor Jamali Confesses
    Imperialism Planned to Oppose the United Arab Republic
    Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, August 4th: According to today's "Al-Rai" newspaper, the Iraqi government is continuing to interrogate the arrested traitors. It is said that former Iraqi Foreign Minister Jamali confessed to the imperialist plot to oppose the United Arab Republic.
    The newspaper said that Jamali also mentioned the names of the Syrian, Lebanese, and Jordanian imperialist agents who conspired with the Iraqi traitors.

### 伊拉克卖国贼贾马利供认  帝国主义策划反对阿联

1958-08-05
第2版()
专栏：

    伊拉克卖国贼贾马利供认
    帝国主义策划反对阿联
    新华社大马士革4日电　据“舆论报”今天报道，伊拉克政府正在继续对被捕的卖国贼进行审讯。据说伊拉克前外交大臣贾马利供认了帝国主义所策划的反对阿拉伯联合共和国的阴谋。
    这家报纸说，贾马利还提到了那些和伊拉克的卖国贼同谋的叙利亚、黎巴嫩和约旦的帝国主义代理人的名字。
