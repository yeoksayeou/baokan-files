　　Small Multi-Purpose Cultivator

　　Small multi-purpose cultivator,
　　Light, nimble and economical;
　　Can plow, can weed, can ridge,
　　Also good for harrowing and hoeing.
　　Pulling a trailer onto the road,
　　Can travel hundreds of miles a day.
　　　　  (Workers of Dalian Machinery Factory No. 5)
