Title: Kassem Thanks Socialist Countries for Recognizing Iraq
Author:
Date: 1958-08-08
Page: 1

    Kassem Thanks Socialist Countries for Recognizing Iraq
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 7th. Middle East News Agency today reported from Baghdad that Iraqi Prime Minister Kassem said that he thanked the socialist countries for recognizing the Republic of Iraq at a critical moment.
    Prime Minister Kassem, in a statement published in the Iraqi "National News," spoke of the relationship between Iraq and the socialist countries, saying: "Because we have gained freedom and are no longer oppressed by foreign forces, we hope to establish friendly relations with the East and West on an equal footing. We thank the socialist countries for recognizing us at a critical moment. We will not forget this."

### 卡塞姆感谢社会主义国家承认伊拉克

1958-08-08
第1版()
专栏：

    卡塞姆感谢社会主义国家承认伊拉克
    新华社开罗7日电  中东通讯社今天自巴格达报道，伊拉克总理卡塞姆说，他感谢社会主义国家在危急的时刻承认伊拉克共和国。
    卡塞姆总理在刊载在伊拉克“国家报”的声明中谈到伊拉克和社会主义国家之间的关系说：“由于我们获得自由和不再受外国势力的压迫，我们希望在平等的基础上同东西方建立友好关系。我们感谢社会主义国家在危急的时刻承认我们。我们将不会忘记这件事。”
