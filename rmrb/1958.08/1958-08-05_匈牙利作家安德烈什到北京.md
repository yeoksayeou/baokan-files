Title: Hungarian Writer Andras Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-08-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: Hungarian writer, Berggei Andras, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of August 1st for a visit to our country, according to the 1958 executive plan of the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 匈牙利作家安德烈什到北京

1958-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利作家安德烈什到北京
    新华社讯　根据中匈文化合作协定1958年执行计划前来我国访问的匈牙利作家贝尔盖西·安德烈什，1日下午到达北京。
