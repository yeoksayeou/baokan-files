Title: Poem on a Portrait of Lloyd
Author: Yi Heyuan
Date: 1958-08-22
Page: 8

　　Poem on a Portrait of Lloyd
　　Yi Heyuan
　　No matter how thick your skin is,
　　How dare demons face the sun?
　　Hands covering your face, head hard to raise,
　　A thousand sorrows and anxieties fill your thoughts.
　　Riding the tiger of Jordan, it's hard to dismount,
　　Rebuked at the assembly as if shot by a gun,
　　Desperately calling for God's protection in vain,
　　Nowhere to hide from the condemnation of all. (Attached image)
    The embarrassing appearance of British Foreign Secretary Lloyd at the United Nations General Assembly. [Cartoon photo]

### 题劳埃德像
易和元
1958-08-22
第8版()
专栏：诗画配

　　题劳埃德像
　　易和元
　　任你脸皮厚似墙，
　　妖魔岂敢见太阳？
　　双手蒙面头难举，
　　千愁万虑费思量。
　　骑虎约旦身难下，
　　受斥大会若挨枪，
　　惊呼上帝难保佑，
　　千夫所指无处藏。（附图片）
    英国外交部长劳埃德在联合国大会上的狼狈相。〔漫画照片〕
