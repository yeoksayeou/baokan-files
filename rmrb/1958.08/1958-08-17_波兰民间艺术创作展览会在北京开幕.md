Title: Polish Folk Art Creation Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-08-17
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, August 16th: A "Polish Folk Art Creation Exhibition," sponsored by the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries of the People's Republic of China, opened today at the Beijing Working People's Cultural Palace. The exhibition features more than 600 items with distinctive Polish national characteristics, including textile prints and dyes, weavings, pottery, paper cuttings, and toys.

　　At today's opening ceremony, Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, warmly congratulated the opening of the exhibition, saying: "The path of combining Polish artists and folk artists to make folk art serve craft production, thereby promoting and improving folk art, is worthy of our attention and study." Polish Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China, Mr. Furato, said in his speech: "The holding of this exhibition is a further confirmation of the increase and development of cultural exchanges between China and Poland and will further strengthen the friendship and fraternal relations between the two countries." Ms. Telakowska, Director of the Polish Institute of Arts and Crafts, introduced the development and experience of Polish arts and crafts work.

　　The "Polish Folk Art Creation Exhibition" is being held under the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement. These exhibits will be displayed in the capital from August 17 to September 7, after which they will be sent to Shanghai and Guangzhou for exhibition.

### 波兰民间艺术创作展览会在北京开幕

1958-08-17
第2版()
专栏：

　　波兰民间艺术创作展览会在北京开幕
    新华社16日讯　中华人民共和国对外文化联络委员会主办的“波兰民间艺术创作展览会”，今天在首都劳动人民文化宫开幕。这里展出了富有波兰民族特色的纺织印染品、编织品、陶器、剪纸、玩具等展品六百多件。
    在今天的开幕式上，对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥热烈祝贺展览会的开幕，他说：“波兰美术家和民间艺术家相结合，使民间艺术为工艺生产服务，从而发扬和提高民间艺术的道路，值得我们重视和学习。”波兰人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办傅拉托在讲话中说：“这个展览会的举行是波中两国文化交流增进和发展的进一步证实，将进一步密切两国之间的友谊和兄弟关系。”波兰工艺美术研究所所长泰拉科夫斯卡，向大家介绍了波兰工艺美术工作的发展情况和经验。
    “波兰民间艺术创作展览会”是根据中波签订的文化合作协定举办的。这些展品在8月17日到9月7日在首都展出后，还将送到上海、广州展出。
