Exhibition

　　Anti-American-British Intervention: Middle East Photo Exhibition
    Yesterday, the "Photo Exhibition Against Armed Intervention in Lebanon, Jordan, and Iraq's Internal Affairs by American and British Imperialism" began at the Beijing Working People's Cultural Palace. This exhibition focuses on introducing the anti-imperialist struggles of the people of Iraq, Lebanon, and Jordan, the piratical behavior of armed intervention by American and British imperialism, and the support of peace-loving and just people around the world for the struggles of the Arab people.

### 反对美英干涉  中东图片展览

1958-08-31
第4版()
专栏：展览会

　　反对美英干涉  中东图片展览
    昨天起，北京劳动人民文化宫开始展出“反对美国英国武装干涉黎巴嫩、约旦、伊拉克人民内政”的图片，这个展览重点介绍伊拉克、黎巴嫩、约旦人民的反帝斗争情况及美国英国帝国主义武装干涉的海盗行为，以及全世界爱好和平正义的人民支援阿拉伯人民斗争的情况。
