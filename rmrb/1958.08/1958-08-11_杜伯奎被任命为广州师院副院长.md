Title: Du Bokui Appointed Vice President of Guangzhou Normal College
Author:
Date: 1958-08-11
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, August 10th: Du Bokui, the principal of Hong Kong Pui Kiu Middle School who was unreasonably deported by the British authorities in Hong Kong, was appointed as the Vice President of Guangzhou Normal College by the Guangdong Provincial People's Committee on the 8th.
　　After accepting the appointment, Du Bokui told reporters that the appointment by the Provincial People's Committee was the Party and government's concern for him. He expressed his willingness to devote himself to the motherland's education and build socialism together with the people of the whole country.

### 杜伯奎被任命为广州师院副院长

1958-08-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　杜伯奎被任命为广州师院副院长
    新华社广州10日电　被香港英国当局无理递解出境的香港培侨中学校长杜伯奎，已在8日由广东省人民委员会任命为广州市师范学院副院长。
    杜伯奎在接受任命以后对记者说，省人民委员会的任命是党和政府对他的关怀。他表示愿意献身于祖国的教育事业，和全国人民一道建设社会主义。
