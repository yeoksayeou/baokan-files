Title: Indian Flagship Visits Haiphong
Author:
Date: 1958-08-19
Page: 5

　　Indian Flagship Visits Haiphong

　　Xinhua News Agency, Haiphong, 18th -- The Indian Navy flagship "Mysore," which came to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam for a friendly visit, arrived at Haiphong Port at 4:30 this afternoon and was welcomed by 2,000 people from all walks of life in Haiphong. At 5:30 in the afternoon, a welcome ceremony was held at the pier. Indian Fleet Commander Rear Admiral Chakravarti, accompanied by Vietnamese People's Army Deputy Chief of General Staff Major General Hoang Van Thai, reviewed the guard of honor.

　　In his welcoming speech, Major General Hoang Van Thai pointed out that the visit of the "Mysore" will further consolidate and develop the unity and friendship between the people and armies of Vietnam and India, built on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and the Bandung Spirit.

　　In his reply, Rear Admiral Chakravarti said that there has long been a close and friendly relationship between India and Vietnam in history. He hoped that this visit would further strengthen the friendship between the two countries.

### 印旗舰到海防访问

1958-08-19
第5版()
专栏：

　　印旗舰到海防访问
    据新华社海防18日电　前来越南民主共和国进行友好访问的印度海军旗舰“迈索尔号”在今天下午四点半抵达海防港，受到海防各界人民二千人的欢迎。下午五点半，码头上举行了欢迎仪式。印度舰队司令查克洛伐蒂海军少将在越南人民军副总参谋长黄文泰少将的陪同下，检阅了仪仗队。
    黄文泰少将在致欢迎词时指出，“迈索尔号”的访问将进一步巩固和发展越印两国人民和两国军队之间建立在和平共处五项原则和万隆精神基础上的团结和友谊。
    查克洛伐蒂海军少将致答词说，印越两国之间在历史上长久以来存在着亲密的友好关系。他希望这次访问将进一步密切两国之间的友谊。
