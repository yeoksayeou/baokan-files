Spring Wheat Harvest Under the Qilian Mountains
　　The spring wheat under the Qilian Mountains in the Hexi Corridor of Gansu is reaping a bumper harvest. Representatives from various provinces attending the National Spring Wheat On-Site Conference are visiting the high-yield wheat fields in Zhangye County. This year, the county's more than 300,000 mu of spring wheat has achieved a great harvest, with an average yield of 800 jin per mu. The Liangjiadun Township in the county planted more than 10,000 mu of spring wheat with an average yield of 1,064 jin per mu, an increase of 436 jin per mu compared to last year.

### 祁连山下春小麦丰收（图片）
傅振欣
1958-08-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　祁连山下春小麦丰收
    甘肃河西走廊祁连山下的春小麦丰收了。参加全国春小麦现场会议的各省代表们，正在张掖县参观丰产麦田。这个县今年的三十多万亩春小麦获得大丰收，平均每亩八百斤。该县梁家墩乡种植的一万多亩春小麦平均亩产一千零六十四斤，比去年每亩增加四百三十六斤。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　新华社记者　傅振欣摄
