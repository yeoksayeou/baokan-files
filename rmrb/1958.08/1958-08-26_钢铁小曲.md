Title: Steel Little Tunes
Author: Jiang Yongquan, Qian Li
Date: 1958-08-26
Page: 1

　　Steel Little Tunes
    Loading Machine
    Anshan Iron and Steel Worker Jiang Yongquan
    Loading machine, in front of the furnace,
    Non-stop, every second,
    Ten thousand tons of molten steel churn in the furnace,
    Steel like mountains is smelted overnight.
    Loading machine, in front of the furnace,
    Travels a thousand miles a day without fatigue,
    Charges into battle with the sound of drums,
    Scaring the Americans and British to scream incessantly.
    Miner's Song
    Qian Li
　　Steel workers are determined,
　　Labor enthusiasm breaks through the sky,
　　Mines and factories sing together,
　　Ten thousand miles of mountains laugh as iron waves surge.
　　The tiger's mouth is never idle,
　　The entire gold mountain cannot be satisfied,
　　Spitting out piles of little black gold,
　　Flowing into forty thousand silver rivers.
　　Thousands of silver rivers crisscross,
　　As high as the Yangtze River's waves,
　　Undulating and continuous, unafraid of danger,
　　Watering the bull until it rolls around and bellows.

### 钢铁小曲
姜永铨  千里
1958-08-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　钢铁小曲
    装料机
    鞍钢工人  姜永铨
    装料机，在炉前，
    一分一秒不停闲，
    万吨钢水炉内翻，
    连夜炼出钢如山。
    装料机，在炉前，
    日行千里不疲倦，
    冲锋陷阵擂鼓战，
    吓得美英叫连天。
    矿工之歌
    千里
　　钢铁工人意志坚，
　　劳动干劲冲破天，
　　矿山工厂大联唱，
　　万里山笑铁浪翻。
　　虎口天天不闲着，
　　整个金山吃不饱，
　　吐出堆堆小乌金，
　　流成银河四万条。
　　银河纵横千万条，
　　有如长江浪头高，
　　起伏连绵不畏险，
　　灌得牤牛打滚叫。
