Title: Yugoslav News Agency Denies Joint Naval Exercises by British and Italian Navies

Author:

Date: 1958-08-10
Page: 2

    Yugoslav News Agency
    Denies Joint Naval Exercises by British and Italian Navies
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 8th: The "Soviet Naval Gazette" of August 6th published a message from the Yugoslav News Agency on August 1st. The message stated: The Yugoslav News Agency issued a message on July 17th stating that a report published by an Italian newspaper, regarding the Italian, British, and Yugoslav naval fleets participating in joint exercises in the Adriatic Sea in August, was without any basis. However, recently a West German newspaper published a similar report. This report was reprinted in the "Soviet Naval Gazette" on July 31st.

### 南斯拉夫通讯社  否认英意南海军将联合演习

1958-08-10
第2版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫通讯社
    否认英意南海军将联合演习
    新华社莫斯科8日电　8月6日的“苏联海军报”刊载了南斯拉夫通讯社8月1日的一则消息。消息中说：南斯拉夫通讯社7月17日曾经发表一项消息说，意大利的一家报纸所刊载的关于意大利、英国和南斯拉夫海军舰队将于8月间参加在亚得里亚海的联合演习的消息，是缺乏任何根据的。但是，最近西德的一家报纸发表了一则类似的消息。这项消息曾转载于7月31日的“苏联海军报”上。
