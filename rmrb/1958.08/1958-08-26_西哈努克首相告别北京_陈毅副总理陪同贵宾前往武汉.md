Title: Prime Minister Sihanouk Bids Farewell to Beijing; Vice Premier Chen Yi Accompanies Distinguished Guests to Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1958-08-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 25th: As Prime Minister Sihanouk bid farewell to the people of the capital this morning, he once again enthusiastically shouted "Long live the brotherly friendship between Cambodia and China," and pointed out that Cambodian-Chinese friendship and solidarity have gained new strength and vitality from this visit.

This morning, the Prime Minister and all distinguished guests of the Cambodian Royal National Delegation, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi and others, took a special plane to Wuhan. After visiting Wuhan and Guangzhou, the distinguished guests will return home. Princess Norodom Bopha Devi, Prince Norodom Chakrapong, and Princess Norodom Soriya Roeungsey also left Beijing on the same plane.

Prime Minister Sihanouk, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, reviewed the guard of honor, and all the Cambodian distinguished guests accepted flowers presented to them by young women and Young Pioneers of the capital.

The Prime Minister delivered a farewell speech at the airport. He said in an excited tone: "Dearest friends, we place our hands in your hands." "We hope to have the honor and pleasure of receiving you in our country soon. On the unbreakable foundation we have jointly built, our relations and exchanges should develop smoothly."

Those who came to the airport this morning to see off the distinguished guests included Vice Premier He Long, Vice Chairmen Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Procurator-General Zhang Dingcheng, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Luo Guibo, Chinese Ambassador to the Kingdom of Cambodia Wang Youping, as well as people from all walks of life in China and diplomatic envoys from various countries to China.

Before the Prime Minister's speech, Premier Zhou Enlai delivered a farewell speech, warmly thanking Prime Minister Sihanouk for bringing the profound friendship of the Cambodian people to the Chinese people, and thanking him for making valuable contributions to further strengthening and developing the friendly and cooperative relations between our two countries.

At 8:30, as the special plane carrying the distinguished guests slowly took off, the crowd standing at the airport continued to wave to the sky, wishing the distinguished guests a safe journey. (Attached picture)

### 西哈努克首相告别北京  陈毅副总理陪同贵宾前往武汉

1958-08-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　西哈努克首相告别北京
    陈毅副总理陪同贵宾前往武汉
    据新华社25日讯　西哈努克首相今天早晨同首都人民告别的时候：再一次热情地高呼“柬埔寨和中国之间的兄弟般的友谊万岁”，并且指出柬中友谊和团结已经从他们这次访问中获得了新的力量和生气。
    首相和柬埔寨王国国家代表团全体贵宾今晨由陈毅副总理等陪同，乘专机前往武汉。贵宾们将在武汉和广州访问后回国。帕花·黛维公主，夏卡朋王子和索丽亚·伦赛公主也同机离京。
    西哈努克首相由周恩来总理陪同检阅了仪仗队，全体柬埔寨贵宾接受了首都女青年和男女少先队员们献给他们的鲜花。
    首相在机场发表了告别讲话。他用激动的语调表示：“最敬爱的朋友们，我们把我们的手放在你们的手里。”“我们希望不久能够在我国荣幸地和愉快地接待你们。在我们共同建立起来的牢不可破的基础上，我们的关系和往来应该顺利地发展。”
    今晨到机场上欢送贵宾的，还有贺龙副总理，李济深、沈钧儒、黄炎培、彭真副委员长，张鼎丞检察长，外交部章汉夫、罗贵波副部长，中国驻柬埔寨王国大使王幼平，以及中国各方面人士和各国驻华使节。
    在首相讲话以前，周恩来总理致欢送词，热情感谢西哈努克首相为中国人民带来了柬埔寨人民的深厚友情，感谢他对进一步加强和发展我们两国间的友好合作关系作出了宝贵的贡献。
    八时三十分，当贵宾们乘坐的专机慢慢起飞时，伫立在机场上的欢送人群还不断向空中挥手致意，祝贵宾们旅途平安。（附图片）
