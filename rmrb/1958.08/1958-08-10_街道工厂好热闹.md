Title: Street Factories are Bustling
Author: Cao Wenbin
Date: 1958-08-10
Page: 4

The General Line shines like the sun,
Street factories are bustling,
Aunties are factory directors,
Second aunts are workshop supervisors,
We girls and sisters-in-law,
Are new workers in the factory.

### 街道工厂好热闹
曹文彬
1958-08-10
第4版()
专栏：

    街道工厂好热闹
    湖南邵阳　曹文彬
    总路线像太阳照，
    街道工厂好热闹，
    大娘厂里当厂长，
    二婶车间做主任，
    我们姑娘嫂嫂呀，
    厂里当个新工人。
