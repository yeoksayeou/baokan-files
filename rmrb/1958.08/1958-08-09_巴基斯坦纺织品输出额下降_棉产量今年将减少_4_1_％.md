Title: Pakistan's Textile Exports Decline; Cotton Production to Decrease by 4.1% This Year
Author:
Date: 1958-08-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, August 8th: Affected by the economic crisis in the capitalist world, Pakistan's most important industry, the textile industry, has experienced very low product exports in the first half of this year. This situation not only exacerbates Pakistan's foreign exchange shortage but also makes it difficult for the textile industry to purchase sufficient new equipment and parts.

To encourage the export of cotton cloth and cotton yarn, the government has decided to include cotton textiles and cotton yarn in the export incentive plan. According to this plan, exporters are allowed to use 25% of their foreign exchange earnings to purchase new equipment and parts.

Xinhua News Agency report: The "Pakistan Times" reported on the 4th that Pakistan's cotton production for the 1957-1958 fiscal year is estimated at 1.666 million bales, a 4.1% decrease compared to the previous year's estimated cotton production of 1.737 million bales.

The newspaper said that the final estimated figure for Pakistan's cotton cultivated area in 1957-1958 was 3.563 million acres, a 0.8% decrease compared to the previous year's estimated figure of 3.591 million acres.

### 巴基斯坦纺织品输出额下降  棉产量今年将减少4.1％

1958-08-09
第3版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦纺织品输出额下降
    棉产量今年将减少4.1％
    据新华社卡拉奇8日电  巴基斯坦最主要的工业纺织工业由于受到资本主义世界经济危机的影响，今年上半年的产品输出额很低。这种情况不仅使巴基斯坦缺乏外汇的情况更加严重，并且使纺织工业在购买足够的新的设备和零件方面面临着困难。
    为了鼓励棉布和棉纱的输出，政府已经决定把棉织品和棉纱列入鼓励出口的计划中。根据这项计划，许可出口商把他们的外汇收入的25％用来购买新的设备和零件。
    据新华社讯  “巴基斯坦时报”4日报道，巴基斯坦1957—1958年度的棉花产量估计为一百六十六万六千包，比前一年的棉花估计产量一百七十三万七千包减少了4.1％。
    这家报纸说，巴基斯坦1957—1958年度的棉花耕地面积的最后估计数字是三百五十六万三千英亩，同前一年的棉花耕地面积估计数字三百五十九万一千英亩相比，减了0.8％。
