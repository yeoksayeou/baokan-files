Title: Nasser and Faisal Issue Joint Communiqué: Arab Lands Will Not Tolerate Foreign Troops; UAR and Saudi Arabia to Strengthen Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1958-08-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 18th: The United Arab Republic and Saudi Arabia issued a joint communiqué here on August 18th regarding the talks between President Nasser and Prime Minister Faisal, stating: "Both countries disagree with any foreign troops being stationed on the land of any Arab nation, and they also condemn any foreign nation or several foreign nations interfering in the internal affairs of other peoples, thereby endangering world peace and security."

The communiqué stated that after an exchange of views between Nasser and Faisal, "a completely unanimous agreement was reached, and the friendship and brotherly ties between these two sister countries were confirmed."

The communiqué stated that the talks held by the two statesmen from August 15th to 18th aimed to clarify the atmosphere, strengthen cooperation and fraternal relations between the two countries, and consolidate Arab nationalism on the basis of non-alignment and active neutrality. The communiqué also said that the international situation and the situation in the Middle East were also discussed during the talks.

Faisal had already left Cairo at noon on the 18th to return to his country.

### 纳赛尔和费萨尔发表会谈公报　阿拉伯土地不容外国驻军　阿联和沙特阿拉伯将加强合作

1958-08-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　纳赛尔和费萨尔发表会谈公报
　　阿拉伯土地不容外国驻军
　　阿联和沙特阿拉伯将加强合作
    新华社开罗18日电  阿拉伯联合共和国和沙特阿拉伯18日在这里就纳赛尔总统和费萨尔首相会谈发表的联合公报说：“两国都不同意任何外国军队驻在任何阿拉伯国家的土地上，它们还谴责任何一个外国或几个外国干涉其他人民的内政，从而使世界和平和安全遭到危险的行为。”
    公报说，纳赛尔和费萨尔交换了意见之后，“达成了完全一致的协议，并且证实了这两个姊妹国家之间的友谊和兄弟般的联系”。
    公报说，这两位政治家从8月15日到18日举行的会谈，目的在于澄清气氛，加强两国之间的合作和兄弟关系以及在不参加集团和积极中立的基础上巩固阿拉伯民族主义。公报还说，会谈中还讨论了国际局势和中东局势。
    费萨尔已经在18日中午离开罗回国。
