Title: Soviet Delegation to UN Emergency Meeting Arrives in New York; Ukrainian and Belorussian Delegations Arrive on Same Day
Author:
Date: 1958-08-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th - The Soviet delegation to the United Nations General Assembly emergency special session, led by Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko, arrived in New York by plane on the morning of the 9th, according to TASS.

The Ukrainian delegation, headed by Ukrainian Foreign Minister Palamarchuk, and the Belorussian delegation, headed by Belorussian Foreign Minister Kiselev, also arrived in New York by plane on the same day.

### 苏联出席联大紧急会议代表团抵纽约  乌克兰和白俄罗斯两代表团同天到达

1958-08-10
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联出席联大紧急会议代表团抵纽约
    乌克兰和白俄罗斯两代表团同天到达
    新华社9日讯　据塔斯社报道，出席联合国大会紧急特别会议的苏联代表团在苏联外交部部长葛罗米柯率领下于9日上午飞抵纽约。
    以乌克兰外交部部长帕拉马尔楚克为首的乌克兰代表团和以白俄罗斯外交部部长基谢廖夫为首的白俄罗斯代表团也在同一天飞抵纽约。
