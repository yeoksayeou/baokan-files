Title: Our Air Force Continuously Destroys and Damages Nine Chiang Planes; Ministry of National Defense Commends Air Combat Heroes
Author:
Date: 1958-08-16
Page: 1

　　Our Air Force Continuously Destroys and Damages Nine Chiang Planes
　　Ministry of National Defense Commends Air Combat Heroes
    Xinhua News Agency, August 15th. The Ministry of National Defense today issued a circular to commend the meritorious personnel of the Air Force units who recently shot down and damaged American-made Chiang planes intruding into our southeastern region in air combat.

    The circular stated that from July 29th to August 14th, our southeastern Air Force units shot down and damaged nine American-made Chiang planes in air combat, powerfully striking the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique's air force's intrusions into the mainland and safeguarding the security of the airspace in our southeastern coastal areas. Special commendation is hereby given to the meritorious personnel in the air combat.

    The circular called on all comrades in the Air Force units to continue strengthening combat readiness, give even greater blows to the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique's air force who dare to intrude again, and strive for greater victories in the battle to defend the motherland's airspace.

### 我空军连续毁伤蒋机九架　国防部嘉奖空战英雄

1958-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　我空军连续毁伤蒋机九架
　　国防部嘉奖空战英雄
    新华社15日讯　国防部今天通令嘉奖最近时期在空战中连续击落击伤窜扰我东南地区的美制蒋机的空军部队有功人员。
    通令说，从7月29日到8月14日，我东南空军部队在空战中击落击伤了美制蒋机九架，有力地打击了蒋介石卖国集团空军对大陆的窜扰，保卫了我东南沿海地区领空的安全。对于空战有功人员，特予通令嘉奖。
    通令号召空军部队全体同志，继续加强战斗准备，给予再来窜扰的蒋介石卖国集团空军以更大的打击，争取在保卫祖国领空的战斗中，取得更大的胜利。
