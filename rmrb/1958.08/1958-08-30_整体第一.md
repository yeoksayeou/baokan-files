　　Overall Perspective First
    The unconditional obedience of Baoding, Shijiazhuang and other regions of Hebei Province to the unified national plan, with the large-scale upward transfer of steel, is another example of communist style.
    Obeying the overall interests is our highest principle of action at all times. Can we say that the whole party and the entire people running industry and steel is encouraging decentralism? Of course not. On the basis of the whole people running industry, we must also realize this principle: national planning, centralized unity, shared responsibility, and full guarantee. Only with the efforts of the whole people on the one hand, and centralized unity on the other, can we have high speed and ensure the greatest interests of the whole people to the greatest extent.

### 整体第一

1958-08-30
第1版()
专栏：编辑的话

　　整体第一
    河北省保定、石家庄等地区无条件地服从国家统一计划，大批钢铁上调，这是共产主义风格的又一例证。
    服从整体利益，任何时候都是我们行动的最高准则。全党全民办工业、办钢铁能不能说这是鼓励分散主义呢？当然不是。在全民办工业的基础上，同时必须实现这样的原则：全国计划，集中统一，共同负责，全力保证。一方面有全民的努力，一方面又有集中统一，才能有高速度，才能使全民的最大利益得到最大的保证。
