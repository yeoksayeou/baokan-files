Title: Warriors' Poems
Author: Yiming, Zhang Laijin, Hong Tao, Yu Zong, Gong Chuixi
Date: 1958-08-04
Page: 8

    Warriors' Poems
    Song of the Pilot
    The Three Mountains and Five Peaks crane their necks to salute me,
    Rolling white clouds travel beneath my wings,
    Sound pants, chasing behind,
    I want to reach out and pluck the stars.
    (Anonymous)
    Busy Training
    Grasp the sun, don't let it set,
    Operating under the moon,
    Raining and windy, work doesn't stop,
    Determined to strive for full success.
    (Zhang Laijin)
    Ode to Flight
    A swift wind rises in the clear sky for ten thousand miles,
    Silver light flashes, dazzling and bright,
    Swift as a meteor, light as a swallow,
    Jade belt ten thousand feet, dancing in the long sky,
    Yesterday a shepherd boy in the countryside,
    Today showing extraordinary abilities in the air,
    Having developed skillful flying techniques,
    Defending the motherland, safeguarding peace.
    (Hong Tao)
    We Must Be Masters of Nature
    Make way, gods and immortals,
    Watch out, foreigners,
    Get out of here, demons and monsters,
    We want to create,
    We want to invent,
    We want to be masters of nature.
    Bow your heads, high mountains,
    Give way, rivers,
    Get out of the way, stumbling stones,
    We want to open the heavens,
    We want to cleave the earth,
    We want to stride towards Communism.
    (Yu Zong)
    Night Sky
    The searchlight's beam sweeps across the night sky,
    The snow-bright, snow-bright light, ah,
    How many miles of clouds and moon do you pierce through,
    How many pairs of artillerymen's eyes do you capture!
    From south to north, from west to east,
    Thousands upon thousands, crisscrossing,
    Like precious swords, like lightning,
    Building a solid aerial Great Wall.
    (Gong Chuixi)

### 战士的诗
佚名  张来金  洪涛  余棕  宫垂玺
1958-08-04
第8版()
专栏：

    战士的诗
    飞行员之歌
    三山五岳向我翘首致敬，
    滚滚白云在我翼下穿行，
    声音喘着气在后面追赶，
    我要举手摘下星星。
    （佚名）
    练兵忙
    抓住太阳不让落，
    月亮下面来操作，
    阴雨刮风不停工，
    决心争取满堂红。
    （张来金）
    咏飞行
    万里晴空起疾风，
    银光闪闪耀眼明，
    迅比流星轻似燕，
    玉带万丈舞长空，
    昨日村野牧羊童，
    今朝空中显奇能，
    练就娴熟飞行技，
    保卫祖国保和平。
    （洪涛）
    要作自然的主人
    让位吧，神仙，
    瞧着吧，洋人，
    滚开吧，牛鬼蛇神，
    我们要创造，
    我们要发明，
    我们要作自然的主人。
    低头吧，高山，
    让路吧，江河，
    滚开吧，绊脚的石头，
    我们要开天，
    我们要劈地，
    我们要迈向共产主义。
    （余棕）
    夜空
    探照灯的光柱掠过夜空，
    雪亮雪亮的灯光啊，
    你穿过多少里路云和月，
    你吸住多少双炮兵的眼睛！
    你从南到北，从西到东，
    千条万条，交叉纵横，
    像宝剑，像闪电，
    架起了坚固的空中长城。
    （宫垂玺）
