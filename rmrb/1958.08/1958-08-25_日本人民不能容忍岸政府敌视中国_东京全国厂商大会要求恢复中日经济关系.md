Title: The Japanese People Cannot Tolerate the Kishi Government's Hostility Towards China. National Manufacturers' Conference in Tokyo Demands Restoration of Sino-Japanese Economic Relations
Author:
Date: 1958-08-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 22nd: Approximately 700 representatives from Japanese manufacturers and trade circles held the "National Manufacturers' Conference to Open Up Sino-Japanese Economic Relations" in Tokyo today. The "Osaka-Tokyo Petition Motorcade to Promote the Restoration of Sino-Japanese Trade," composed of over 100 manufacturers' representatives from the Kinki, Chugoku, Shikoku, and Tokai regions, arrived in Tokyo from Osaka this morning in four buses and immediately participated in the conference.

The conference unanimously passed a statement demanding that the Kishi Nobusuke government change its so-called "wait-and-see" policy towards China and immediately take measures to promote the establishment of peaceful and friendly relations and the restoration of normal diplomatic relations between China and Japan. The statement said that only in this way could the current tense state of Sino-Japanese relations be resolved.

The conference passed petitions to Japanese Prime Minister Nobusuke Kishi, Foreign Minister Aiichiro Fujiyama, and Minister of International Trade and Industry Tatsunosuke Takasaki, demanding that the Kishi Nobusuke government take concrete measures to promote the normalization of Sino-Japanese diplomatic relations and the establishment of friendly relations, and demanding that the Kishi Nobusuke government restore Sino-Japanese trade.

The head of the "Osaka-Tokyo Petition Motorcade to Promote the Restoration of Sino-Japanese Trade," Shozo Morii, Chairman of the Kansai Small and Medium Enterprises Association for the Promotion of Sino-Japanese Trade, strongly criticized the Kishi Nobusuke government's "wait-and-see" attitude on Sino-Japanese relations at the meeting, and said that we have reached the point where we cannot tolerate it any longer. Morii demanded that the Kishi Nobusuke government change its hostile attitude towards China and accept the principles of friendship and mutual benefit.

### 日本人民不能容忍岸政府敌视中国  东京全国厂商大会要求恢复中日经济关系

1958-08-25
第5版()
专栏：

　　日本人民不能容忍岸政府敌视中国
    东京全国厂商大会要求恢复中日经济关系
    据新华社22日讯　东京消息：日本厂商和贸易界代表约七百人今天在东京举行了“打通日中经济关系全国厂商大会”。由近畿、中国、四国、东海地方的厂商代表一百多人组成的“大阪—东京促进恢复日中贸易汽车请愿团”，乘着四辆公共汽车在今天早晨从大阪到达东京之后立即参加了大会。
    大会一致通过一项声明，要求岸信介政府改变所谓“静观”的对华政策，并且立即采取措施促进在中日两国之间建立和平友好关系和恢复正常邦交。声明说，只有这样才能打开目前日中关系的紧张状态。
    大会通过了给日本首相岸信介、外务相藤山爱一郎和通商产业相高崎达之助等的请愿书，要求岸信介政府采取具体措施来促进日中邦交正常化和建立友好关系，并要求岸信介政府恢复日中贸易。
    “大阪——东京促进恢复日中贸易汽车请愿团”团长、关西中小企业促进日中贸易协议会委员长森井庄内在会上强烈指责了岸信介政府在日中关系上的“静观”态度，并且说，我们已经到了不能忍耐的地步。森井要求岸信介政府改变敌视中国的态度，接受友好、互利的原则。
