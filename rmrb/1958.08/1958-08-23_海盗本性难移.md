Title: A Pirate's Nature is Hard to Change
Author: 
Date: 1958-08-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, 21st. The pirate nature of the invading U.S. soldiers in Lebanon has surfaced. The U.S. invading forces' command in Lebanon admitted on the 20th that three U.S. sailors had committed armed robbery of a shop in Beirut.

It is confirmed that these sailors belonged to the U.S. beach forces stationed in the Beirut port area. They used pistols to coerce the shop owner and robbed him of a sum of money, the amount of which was not disclosed.

### 海盗本性难移

1958-08-23
第5版()
专栏：

　　海盗本性难移
    新华社21日讯　贝鲁特消息：侵黎美军士兵强盗本性发作。美国侵黎部队司令部20日承认，三名美国水兵曾经武装抢劫贝鲁特的一家商店。
    据证实，这些水兵属于驻扎在贝鲁特港口地区的美军海滩部队。他们曾经用手枪胁迫这家商店的店主，抢去他的一笔钱，钱的数字未经透露。
