Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister Sihanouk Joint Statement Signing Ceremony Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-08-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 24th: The signing ceremony of the Joint Statement between Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, was solemnly held in Beijing this afternoon.

The Chinese side attending the signing ceremony included: Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council; Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, and Peng Zhen, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; He Long and Chen Yi, Vice Premiers of the State Council; Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Xue Muqiao, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission; Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Wang Youping, Chinese Ambassador to the Kingdom of Cambodia; and Ye Jinghao, Head of the Chinese Economic Delegation to Cambodia.

The Cambodian side attending the signing ceremony included: Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia; Ran Niete, Advisor to the Royal High Council; Gu Lun, Advisor to the Royal High Council; Ting Yuan, Minister of National Defense, Ground Defense and National Security; Duojin, Minister of Economy and Finance; Qing Ha, Secretary-General of the Royal High Council; and Yang An, Head of the Cambodian Economic Delegation to China.

At 6:45 p.m., Premier Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister Sihanouk signed the Joint Statement.

After the signing, both sides raised their glasses in congratulation and shook hands warmly.

Prior to the signing, Premier Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister Sihanouk held another meeting. (Attached picture)

### 周恩来总理和西哈努克首相  联合声明签字仪式在京举行

1958-08-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　周恩来总理和西哈努克首相
    联合声明签字仪式在京举行
    新华社24日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来和柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王联合声明的签字仪式，于今天下午在北京隆重举行。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的有：中华人民共和国副主席朱德、国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、黄炎培、彭真，国务院副总理贺龙、陈毅，外交部副部长罗贵波，国家计划委员会副主任薛暮桥，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，中国驻柬埔寨王国大使王幼平，中国驻柬埔寨经济代表团团长叶景灏。
    柬埔寨方面参加签字仪式的有：柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王，皇廷最高会议顾问兰·涅特，皇廷最高会议顾问古伦，国防、地面防御和国家安全大臣丁源，经济和财政大臣笃金，皇廷最高会议秘书长清哈，柬埔寨驻中国经济代表团团长杨安。
    下午六时四十五分，周恩来总理和西哈努克首相分别在联合声明上签了字。
    签字后，双方举杯祝贺，亲切握手。
    在签字以前，周恩来总理同西哈努克首相举行了又一次会谈。（附图片）
