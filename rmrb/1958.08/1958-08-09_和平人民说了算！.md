Title: The People of Peace Decide!
Author: Wang Shihui
Date: 1958-08-09
Page: 8

    The people of peace decide!
    Now the East Wind prevails over the West Wind,
    The people of peace decide!
    American and British devils, get out!
    If you dare to invade and entangle again,
    We will smash you to pieces!
    Try it if you don't believe it!
    (Xinhua Printing Factory, Wang Shihui)
