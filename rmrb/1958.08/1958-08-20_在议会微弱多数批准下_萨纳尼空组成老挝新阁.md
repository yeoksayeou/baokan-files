Title: Under Weak Parliamentary Majority Approval, Sananikone Forms New Laotian Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1958-08-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 19th: According to Vientiane Radio, after three days of debate, the Laotian National Assembly voted on the afternoon of the 18th, resulting in a narrow majority of twenty-nine votes to twenty-one approving the Phoui Sananikone cabinet.

According to the cabinet list announced by Phoui Sananikone after being authorized to form a cabinet, the new cabinet excludes representatives of the Lao Patriotic Front, and ten of its eleven cabinet members belong to the Lao People's Rally.

### 在议会微弱多数批准下　萨纳尼空组成老挝新阁

1958-08-20
第5版()
专栏：

　  在议会微弱多数批准下
　　萨纳尼空组成老挝新阁
    据新华社河内19日电  据万象电台广播，老挝国民议会经过三天的辩论以后，在18日下午进行表决，结果以二十九票对二十一票的微弱多数批准了培·萨纳尼空内阁。
    根据培·萨纳尼空在受权组阁后宣布的内阁名单，新内阁排斥了老挝爱国党的代表，它的十一名阁员中有十名属于老挝人联合党。
