Title: Bumper Harvest of Flue-Cured Tobacco in Yuxi Prefecture, Yunnan
Author:
Date: 1958-08-24
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, August 23rd: Yuxi Prefecture, Yunnan, the famous main producing area of ​​Yunnan flue-cured tobacco, overcame the natural disasters of drought followed by floods, and achieved a bumper harvest of 300,000 mu of flue-cured tobacco, with a yield per unit area of ​​more than 300 jin, an increase of more than one and a half times compared with last year.

This year, the flue-cured tobacco in Yuxi Prefecture grew better than in any previous year. The seedlings are strong and neat, generally half a person tall, with about 2,000 plants per mu, and an average of fifteen to twenty leaves per plant. Due to early planting, the maturity period was also advanced. This year, the best harvest was in Jiangchuan County, where the average yield per mu of 67,500 mu of flue-cured tobacco can reach 450 jin.

### 云南玉溪专区烤烟大丰收

1958-08-24
第2版()
专栏：

　　云南玉溪专区烤烟大丰收
    新华社昆明23日电　著名的云南烤烟主要产地——玉溪专区，战胜了先旱后涝的自然灾害，三十万亩烤烟获得了大丰收，单位面积产量达到三百多斤，比去年增加一倍半以上。
    今年玉溪专区的烤烟长得比以往任何一年都好，苗棵粗壮整齐，一般有大半个人高，每亩约有两千棵，平均每棵有十五——二十个叶片。由于栽的早，成熟期也提前了。今年收获最好的是江川县，六万七千五百亩烤烟平均亩产量可达四百五十斤。
