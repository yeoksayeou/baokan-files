Title: Celebrating Army Day, People Across the Country Express Resolve: Ready to Support the Liberation of Taiwan at Any Time

Author:

Date: 1958-08-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. As people across the country warmly celebrated "August 1st" Army Day, they resolutely expressed their determination to learn from the People's Liberation Army (PLA), support the PLA, build the motherland with the PLA's spirit of courage and tenacity, actively participate in national defense construction with the PLA and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's patriotism and internationalism, stand ready to support the PLA in liberating Taiwan, and actively support the just struggle of the Arab people.
    Heilongjiang Province held a broadcast rally on the evening of July 31st to commemorate "August 1st" Army Day and oppose the Anglo-American aggression against the Middle East. People in towns, villages, and forest areas across the province gathered around radios and loudspeakers to listen to the broadcast. Li Fanwu, Secretary of the Heilongjiang Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, called on the people of the province to pay close attention to the development of the Middle East situation and fully support the struggle of the Middle Eastern people. Jiang Shidun, a representative from the Mishan and Hegang reclamation areas, representing more than 100,000 demobilized soldiers from the two reclamation areas, expressed their determination to contribute everything to support the Arab people's struggle and liberate our country's territory of Taiwan. At a celebration meeting held by young people from all walks of life in Shanghai, all the young people raised their arms and shouted: If the Anglo-American imperialists do not withdraw their claws of aggression, they will suffer the consequences. More than 20,000 demobilized soldiers in Shenyang assured the local Party, government, and military leaders at a celebration meeting: When the motherland needs us, we will immediately step forward, return to the front line, and fight to the end for the motherland and for peace. Four thousand five hundred representatives of demobilized soldiers and families of revolutionary martyrs in Chongqing held an "August 1st" gift-giving conference to the Party, government, and military leading organs. After presenting their gifts, the demobilized soldiers unanimously passed a pledge expressing their determination to raise revolutionary vigilance a hundredfold, be ready at any time to respond to the call of the motherland, and shoulder the sacred task of defending the motherland and safeguarding peace. More than 1,000 people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Hezuo City, Gansu Province, held a celebration meeting on August 1st. Huang Zhengqing (Tibetan), Governor of the Gannan Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture, recounted the deeds of the PLA troops stationed in Gannan in helping the Tibetan people with production and construction and consolidating public security. He extended his congratulations and gratitude to the PLA officers and soldiers present at the meeting.
    In recent days, youth leagues, trade unions, women's federations, and various factories and schools across the country have held many report meetings, forums, and get-togethers, inviting military leaders and combat heroes to give reports and participate in get-togethers. The cultural departments of major cities held a "August 1st" Army Day film week. A photo exhibition entitled "The Heroic Chinese People's Liberation Army" was held in the vast countryside. People from all walks of life in Guangzhou organized six comfort teams to visit military hospitals and sanatoriums to express their respect and concern to PLA wounded and sick soldiers. The Shanghai People's Art Theater and 13 other art troupes went to the army, navy, and air force units to perform comfort performances. The mother of the hero sailor Wu Cailiang also went to the fleet where Wu Cailiang served before his death to encourage the sailors to improve their skills and liberate Taiwan.

### 庆祝建军节全国人民表示决心  随时准备支援解放台湾

1958-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝建军节全国人民表示决心
    随时准备支援解放台湾
    新华社2日讯　全国各地人民在热烈庆祝“八一”建军节时，坚决表示要学习人民解放军，支援人民解放军，以解放军的英勇顽强精神建设祖国，以解放军和志愿军的爱国主义和国际主义精神积极参加国防建设，随时准备支援解放军解放台湾，积极支援阿拉伯人民的正义斗争。
    黑龙江省31日晚上举行了纪念“八一”建军节和反对美英侵略中东的广播大会，全省城镇、农村、林区的人民群众齐集收音机、扩音器旁收听广播。中共省委书记李范五号召全省人民密切注意中东局势的发展，全力支援中东人民的斗争。密山、合江垦区代表姜世敦代表两个垦区十多万转业军人表示，决心为声援阿拉伯人民斗争和解放我国领土台湾贡献自己的一切。上海市各界青年举行的庆祝集会上，全体青年振臂高呼：美英帝国主义如不缩回侵略魔手，必将自食其果。沈阳市两万多名复员转业军人在庆祝大会上向当地党、政、军首长保证：当祖国需要的时候，立即挺身而出，重返前线，为祖国为和平战斗到底。重庆市四千五百多名复员转业军人和烈军属代表，1日举行了“八一”向党政军领导机关献礼大会，复员转业军人献礼后一致通过誓词，表示要百倍提高革命警惕，时刻准备响应祖国的召唤，担负起保卫祖国、捍卫和平的神圣任务。甘肃省合作市各族各界人民一千多人1日举行庆祝大会，甘南藏族自治州州长黄正清（藏）叙述了驻甘南的解放军在草原上帮助藏族人民生产建设和巩固治安的事迹后，向到会的解放军官兵表示祝贺和感谢。
    连日来，全国各地共青团、工会、妇联等团体和各个工厂、学校，分别举行了许多报告会、座谈会、联欢会，邀请驻军首长、战斗英雄等作报告和联欢。各大城市的文化部门举办了庆祝“八一”建军节电影周。广大农村举办了“英勇的中国人民解放军”等图片展览。广州市各界人民组织六个慰问队，分别到驻军医院、休养所，向解放军伤、病员等表达敬意和关怀。上海市人民艺术剧团等十四个文艺团体分别到陆、海、空军部队进行慰问演出。英雄水兵吴才良烈士的妈妈，还到吴才良生前所在舰队，鼓舞水兵好好提高本领，解放台湾。
