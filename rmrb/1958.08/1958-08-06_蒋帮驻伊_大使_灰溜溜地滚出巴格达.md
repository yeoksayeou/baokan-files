Title: Chiang Gang's "Ambassador" to Iraq Slunk Out of Baghdad
Author:
Date: 1958-08-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, 5th: Chen Chih-ping, the "ambassador" to Iraq of the Taiwan Chiang Kai-shek clique, and the personnel of this "embassy" have already slunk out of Baghdad and left for Amman on the 5th.

### 蒋帮驻伊“大使”  灰溜溜地滚出巴格达

1958-08-06
第4版()
专栏：

    蒋帮驻伊“大使”
    灰溜溜地滚出巴格达
    新华社巴格达5日电　台湾蒋介石集团驻伊拉克“大使”陈质平和这个“大使馆”人员已经在5日灰溜溜地离开巴格达前往安曼。
