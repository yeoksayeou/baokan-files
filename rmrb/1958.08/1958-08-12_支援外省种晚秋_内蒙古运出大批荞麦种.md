Title: Supporting Other Provinces in Planting Late Autumn Crops: Inner Mongolia Ships Out Large Quantities of Buckwheat Seeds
Author:
Date: 1958-08-12
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, August 11th: The Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region has shipped out 2.6 million jin of late-autumn crop seeds—buckwheat—to support agricultural production in parts of Hebei and Shandong provinces.

### 支援外省种晚秋　内蒙古运出大批荞麦种

1958-08-12
第2版()
专栏：

　  支援外省种晚秋
　　内蒙古运出大批荞麦种
    据新华社呼和浩特11日电　内蒙古自治区已将二百六十万斤晚秋作物种籽——荞麦运出，支援河北、山东省部分地区农业生产。
