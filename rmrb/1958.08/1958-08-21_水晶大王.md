Title: Crystal King
Author:
Date: 1958-08-21
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 20th. A crystal stone weighing 3,700 kilograms has recently appeared in Donghai County, Jiangsu Province. It was mined by members of the Zhetang Agricultural Cooperative in Fangshan Township of this county.
    This Crystal King is six chi long, five chi wide, and more than three chi thick. After this crystal stone was discovered more than two zhang underground, the agricultural cooperative mobilized five hundred people, used fourteen jin of thick iron wire and dozens of thick ropes, and spent a whole afternoon to drag it to the surface.

### 水晶大王

1958-08-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　水晶大王
    新华社南京20日电  江苏省东海县最近出现了一块重三千七百公斤的水晶石。它是这个县房山乡柘塘农业社社员们开采出来的。
    这块水晶大王长六尺、宽五尺、厚三尺多。当这块水晶石在二丈多深的地下被发现之后，农业社里动员了五百个人，用十四斤粗铁丝，几十条粗绳子，足足花费了一个下午的时间，才把它拖上地面。
