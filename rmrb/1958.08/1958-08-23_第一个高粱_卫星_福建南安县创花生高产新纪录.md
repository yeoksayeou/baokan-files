Title: The First Sorghum "Satellite" - Nan'an County, Fujian Province, Sets New Peanut High-Yield Record
Author:
Date: 1958-08-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Fuyang, August 22nd: Sorghum, traditionally known as a low-yield crop, recently launched a high-yield "satellite" of 3,897.9 jin per mu in Fuquan Nineteenth Commune, Fuyang County, Anhui Province. With this, the label of sorghum as a low-yield crop has been removed.

　　The Fifteenth Production Team of this commune cultivated this experimental field. They planted the "Huangluosan" variety of sorghum on 1.45 mu of land, harvesting 5,651.5 jin of dry sorghum, averaging 3,897.9 jin per mu, while last year's yield was only 200 jin per mu.

　　When harvesting, Zhong Feng, Deputy County Head of the county, Xu Boyu, First Secretary of the Mazhai District Party Committee, Yao Rufeng, Deputy Secretary of the grassroots Party Committee of the township, Liu Ruilin, a technical guidance station employee, and others participated in the supervision of the harvest.

Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, August 22nd: The Wuxing Commune in Chenggong Township, Nan'an County, Fujian Province, harvested a total of 19,224.8 jin of dry peanuts from a 1.127 mu high-yield experimental field, averaging 17,058 jin per mu. This is 1,340 jin higher than the previous record of 15,717.13 jin per mu created by the Heping Commune in the same township not long ago.

　　This high-yield peanut "satellite" was jointly created by Wang Shuiwu, the captain of the Seventh Production Team of the commune, Vice-Captain Wang Shuitan, technician Wang Shuituan, and all 43 members of the team.

### 第一个高粱“卫星”  福建南安县创花生高产新纪录

1958-08-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　第一个高粱“卫星”
    福建南安县创花生高产新纪录
    据新华社阜阳22日电　历来被称为低产作物的高粱，最近在安徽省阜阳县阜泉十九社放出亩产三千八百九十七点九斤的高产“卫星”。至此，高粱低产作物的帽子被摘了。
    培养这块试验田的是该社第十五生产队。他们在一点四五亩土地上，栽培了“黄落伞”品种的高粱，实收干高粱五千六百五十一点五斤，平均亩产三千八百九十七点九斤，而去年亩产只有二百斤。
    在收割时，参加监收监打的有该县副县长锺峰，马砦区区委第一书记徐伯余，该乡基层党委副书记姚如风，技术指导站刘瑞林等。
    据新华社福州22日电　福建省南安县成功乡五星社在一亩一分二厘七毫的丰产试验田里，共收干花生一万九千二百二十四斤八两，平均亩产一万七千零五十八斤。比不久前这个乡和平社创造的亩产一万五千七百一十七斤十三两的最高纪录高出一千三百四十斤。
    这个花生丰产大“卫星”是该社第七生产队队长王水务、副队长王水潭、技术员王水团和全队四十三个社员共同创造的。
