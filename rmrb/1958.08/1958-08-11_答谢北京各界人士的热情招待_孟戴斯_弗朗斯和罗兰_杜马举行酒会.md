Title: Mendès-France and Roland Dumas Hold Reception to Thank People from All Circles in Beijing for Their Warm Hospitality
Author:
Date: 1958-08-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 10th: Former French Prime Minister Mendès-France, who came to China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Roland Dumas, a member of the French Democratic and Socialist Union of Resistance, held a reception at 6 p.m. today to thank people from all circles who warmly received them during their visit to Beijing.

Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, and President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Zhang Xiruo, among a total of thirty-one people, were invited to attend the reception.

### 答谢北京各界人士的热情招待　孟戴斯——弗朗斯和罗兰·杜马举行酒会

1958-08-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　答谢北京各界人士的热情招待
　　孟戴斯——弗朗斯和罗兰·杜马举行酒会
    新华社10日讯　应中国人民外交学会邀请来中国访问的法国前总理孟戴斯—弗朗斯，与法国民主社会抵抗联盟议员罗兰·杜马，今天下午六时举行酒会，答谢在他们访问北京期间热情招待过他们的各界人士。
    应邀出席酒会的有周恩来总理，陈毅副总理兼外交部长以及中国人民外交学会会长张奚若等共三十一人。
