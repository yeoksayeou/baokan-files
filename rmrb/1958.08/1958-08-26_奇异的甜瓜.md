Title: A Singular Melon
Author: Fan Baoren
Date: 1958-08-26
Page: 3

Unusual News

    A Singular Melon
    The Shenwan Production Team of the Niutun People's Commune in Yanjin County, Henan Province, has cultivated an extraordinary melon by hybridizing cantaloupe and pumpkin. It has already produced four large melons. Among these four large melons, the largest is 2.74 *shi chi* long and 7.4 *cun* in circumference, with a hollow core of 1.9 *cun*; the next largest weighs 13 *jin*; the two smallest each weigh 7 *jin*. These melons are still growing and it is estimated that each melon can grow an additional 7 *jin*. (Fan Baoren)

### 奇异的甜瓜
樊宝仁
1958-08-26
第3版()
专栏：珍闻

    奇异的甜瓜
    河南省延津县牛屯人民公社申湾生产队用甜瓜和南瓜杂交的方法培育的一棵奇异的甜瓜，已结了四个大甜瓜。这四个大甜瓜中一个最大的长二市尺七寸四分，粗七点四寸，心空一寸九分；次大的一个重十三斤；两个最小的各重七斤。这些瓜目前仍在生长中，估计每个瓜还可以增长七斤。　（樊宝仁）
