Title: Aggressors Still Stirring; British Troops Continue to Gather in the Persian Gulf
Author:
Date: 1958-08-19
Page: 4

　　Aggressors Still Stirring
　　British Troops Continue to Gather in the Persian Gulf

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 17th: TASS, Cairo, 17th: British troops continue to gather in the southern Arabian Peninsula. A statement issued by the Office of the Imam of Oman in Cairo stated that British military aircraft are constantly landing at the Adite Airport in Muscat. At the same time, large quantities of British arms and ammunition are being shipped to various ports in Muscat. British warships are concentrating in the Persian Gulf.

### 侵略者仍然蠢蠢欲动　英军继续向波斯湾集结

1958-08-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　侵略者仍然蠢蠢欲动
　　英军继续向波斯湾集结
    新华社17日讯　塔斯社开罗17日讯：英国军队继续在阿拉伯半岛南部集结。阿曼教长驻开罗办事处发表的声明说，在马斯喀特的阿迪特机场不断有英国军用飞机着陆。同时有大批英国军火运抵马斯喀特的各个港口。英国军舰正在向波斯湾集中。
