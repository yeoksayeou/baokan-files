Title: Jinxing Third Cooperative's Early Rice Yield Reaches 8657 Jin Per Mu
Author:
Date: 1958-08-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency: The Jinxing Third Cooperative in Macheng County, Hubei Province, harvested 20,258 jin of dried rice from 2.34 mu of early rice paddy, averaging 8,657 jin per mu.

### 金星三社早稻亩产8657斤

1958-08-04
第5版()
专栏：

    金星三社早稻亩产8657斤
    据新华社讯　湖北省麻城县金星三社有二点三四亩早稻田，共收干谷二万零二百五十八斤，平均亩产八千六百五十七斤。
