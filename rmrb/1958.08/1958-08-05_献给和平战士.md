Title: Dedicated to the Warriors of Peace
Author: Sà Yīdé
Date: 1958-08-05
Page: 8

    Dedicated to the Warriors of Peace
  　Lebanon　Sa'id
    Warrior, the path of glory is arduous,
    Don't let yourself stray,
    Before the darkness is dispelled,
    Be sure to persevere in the fight.
    The East is shining, the morning mist is rising.
    The people sing songs of peace,
    The people have cleared out
    The Western warmongers.
    There are still slaves in the world,
    And prisoners who cannot control their own destiny—
    Give them happiness and freedom!
    Warrior, your voice is the bugle call of battle.
    Down with the exploiters!
    Down with the thieving, plundering swindlers!
    Down with the colonialists, murderous masters,
    And the warmongers!
    May there be no agony of poverty
    In the days of peace.
    Peace is like an impregnable shield,
    Saving the lives of the next generation.
    Peace does not bring a hell of poverty,
    But rather the escape from suffering,
    It gives bread to the hungry,
    Medicine to the sick,
    Clear water to the thirsty.
    It brings us the joy of the future,
    And makes our nation prosperous and strong,
    It will tear down the moldy thatched huts,
    And let mansions shine on the original wasteland!
    May those who are beneficial to peace lead us,
    He will lead his people to goodness and joy.
    Warrior, you are fighting a life-and-death battle with the darkness,
    You must be as brave and alert as a vulture.
    Although the road is arduous,
    Those who seek light are not far from their goal.
              (Translated by Si Wei)

### 献给和平战士
萨依德
1958-08-05
第8版()
专栏：

    献给和平战士
  　黎巴嫩　萨依德
    战士啊，光荣的道路虽然艰难，
    不要让自己走入歧途，
    在黑暗还没有驱散之前，
    一定要坚持战斗。
    东方发出光芒，朝雾升起了。
    人民歌唱和平之歌，
    人民清除了
    西方的战争贩子。
    世界上还有奴隶，
    还有不能主宰自己命运的囚犯，——
    给他们幸福和自由！
    战士啊，你的声音就是战斗的号角。
    打倒剥削者！
    打倒抢劫掠夺的骗子！
    打倒殖民主义者、杀人的老爷
    还有战争贩子！
    但愿在和平的日子里，
    没有贫困的煎熬。
    和平好似不可攻破的盾牌，
    拯救了下一代的生命。
    和平带来的不是贫困的地狱，
    而是苦难的摆脱，
    它给饥饿的人——面包，
    害病的人——药物
    口渴的人——清水。
    它使我们获得未来的欢愉，
    使我们的民族兴隆富强，
    它要拆毁霉烂的茅屋，
    让大厦在原来的荒地上放光！
    但愿领导我们的是那对和平有利的人，
    他要把自己的人民引向善良和欢乐。
    战士啊，你跟黑暗作着殊死战，
    你要像兀鹰一样勇敢和机警。
    虽然道路艰难，
    追求光明的人已离目标不远。
              （斯微译）
