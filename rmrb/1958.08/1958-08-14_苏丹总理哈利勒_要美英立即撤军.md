Title: Sudanese Prime Minister Khalil Demands Immediate Withdrawal of US and British Troops
Author:
Date: 1958-08-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 11th – The Middle East News Agency reported from Khartoum on the 11th that Sudanese Prime Minister Khalil said that the instructions carried by the Sudanese Foreign Minister to the emergency session of the United Nations General Assembly recognized the necessity of immediately withdrawing aggressive troops from the Middle East and respecting the independence and active neutrality of the Arab people.

He said that the foreign troops stationed in Lebanon and Jordan threatened world peace, and therefore the whole world must resolutely force them to withdraw immediately.

He said that he instructed the Foreign Minister to cooperate with the delegations of Arab and Bandung Conference countries at the emergency session of the United Nations General Assembly.

### 苏丹总理哈利勒　要美英立即撤军

1958-08-14
第6版()
专栏：

　　苏丹总理哈利勒
　　要美英立即撤军
    新华社开罗11日电　中东通讯社11日自喀土穆报道，苏丹总理哈利勒说，苏丹外交部长带到联合国大会紧急会议的指示认识到立即从中东撤出侵略军队的必要性，尊重阿拉伯人民的独立和积极中立主义。
    他说，驻在黎巴嫩和约旦的外国军队威胁世界和平，因此全世界必须坚决迫使他们立即撤出。
    他说，他指示外交部长在联合国大会紧急会议中同阿拉伯和万隆会议国家的代表团合作。
