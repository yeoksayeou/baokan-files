Title: Defying the Opposition of People Worldwide, Britain to Test Nuclear Weapons Again

Author: 
Date: 1958-08-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, August 18th: London Dispatch: Defying the opposition of people worldwide, Britain is about to test nuclear weapons again.

    According to a statement by the British Ministry of Defence on the 17th, Britain is preparing to conduct a series of nuclear tests on Christmas Island in the Pacific soon.

Xinhua News Agency, August 18th: TASS, London Dispatch: "Reynolds News" in an editorial on the 17th called on the British government not to conduct nuclear tests. The "Spectator" weekly also issued a similar call.

### 不顾世界人民的反对　英国又要试验核武器

1958-08-19
第5版()
专栏：

　　不顾世界人民的反对
　　英国又要试验核武器
    新华社18日讯　伦敦消息：不顾世界人民的反对，英国又要试验核子武器。
    据英国国防部17日宣布，英国准备很快在太平洋上的圣诞岛进行一系列核试验。
    新华社18日讯　据塔斯社伦敦讯：“雷诺新闻”17日在一篇编辑部文章中呼吁英国政府不要进行核试验。“旁观者”周刊也发出过同样的呼吁。
