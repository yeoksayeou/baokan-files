Title: Our Artillery Fiercely Bombards Jinmen Chiang Ships
Author: 
Date: 1958-08-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, August 23rd: Today in the afternoon, a Chiang Kai-shek army transport ship was carrying Chiang soldiers to Jinmen. Our powerful artillery on the Fujian front immediately bombarded it fiercely, giving the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique's army a heavy blow.

### 我炮兵猛轰金门蒋舰

1958-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　我炮兵猛轰金门蒋舰
    新华社福建前线23日电　今天下午，一艘蒋军运输舰，载运蒋军至金门。我福建前线强大炮兵当即予以猛烈轰击，给蒋介石卖国集团的军队以沉重的打击。
