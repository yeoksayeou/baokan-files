Title: Hold High the Red Flag and Charge Towards the Cutting Edge of the World
Author:
Date: 1958-08-01
Page: 7

Hold high the red flag and charge towards the cutting edge of the world
Combat team members, charge forward and listen! The bugle call to charge has already sounded!
What is darkness?
    Electric light—is the sun!
What is sleep?
    Drawing board—is the steel bed!
Every drawing
    Is a fortress.
Every dimension
    Is layer upon layer of barbed wire.
Machining symbols,
Tolerance and fit,
    Must be resolutely, thoroughly, and completely swept away!
We—
    Have the Party to lead,
    Ideology—liberated,
    Enthusiasm—soaring to the sky,
    Fighting spirit—high!
Communist fighters, quickly plant the victory red flag on the summit!

(Editor's note: This is a poem written by a student in the graduating class of the Automobile Engineering program of the Power Engineering Department of Tsinghua University while working on the design of a free-piston gas turbine unit.)

### 高举红旗向世界尖端冲锋

1958-08-01
第7版()
专栏：

    高举红旗向世界尖端冲锋
    战斗队员冲向前听！冲锋号已经吹响什么黑夜？
    电灯——就是太阳什么睡眠？
    图桌——就是钢丝床
    每一张图纸
    就是一个堡垒
    每一个尺寸
    就是层层的铁丝网
    加工符号
    公差配合
    都坚决、彻底、全部一扫光
    我们　领导——有党
    思想——解放
    干劲——冲天
    斗志——昂扬
    共产主义战士们快把胜利的红旗向高峰插上！
    （编者注：这是清华大学动力系汽车专业毕业班一位学生在进行自由活塞——燃气轮机机组设计工作时写的诗。）
