Title: Eliminating Errors in Accounting Work
Author:
Date: 1958-08-28
Page: 2

Column: Criticism and Response

    Eliminating Errors in Accounting Work
    Regarding the letter published in your newspaper on August 13th from Comrades Li Derong and Li Jiaju of the Tianjin Municipal Building Materials Industrial Company criticizing our store for accumulating overdue payments for their company's purchases, the facts are completely correct. To prevent similar errors from recurring, starting August 1st, our store has established a dedicated mail-order transaction account at the Wangfujing Bank branch. Mail-order business personnel will directly interface with the bank, simplifying transfer procedures and eliminating errors in accounting work.
    Beijing Wangfujing Scientific Instruments Store

### 消除财会工作的差错

1958-08-28
第2版()
专栏：批评反应

    消除财会工作的差错
    8月13日你报刊登天津市建筑材料工业公司李德荣和李家驹两位同志批评我店积压他们公司购货款的来信，事实完全正确。为了防止今后再发生类似的错误，我店于8月1日起，在王府井银行办事处专设函售往来账户，函售业务人员直接和银行挂钩，简化传递手续，消除财会工作中的差错。
    北京市王府井科学仪器商店
