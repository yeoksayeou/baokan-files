　　Poetry and Painting

　　King Corn (Mural)
　　Painted by Grandma Liang
    Poem by Zuo Hai
　　Corn's height reaches the sky, its waist gently brushes the train smoke;
　　Mount Tai bows its head, airplanes avoid it, the seventy-year-old grandma's painting spreads the word.

　　玉米大王（壁画）
　　梁大娘作画
    左海配诗
　　玉米身高势接天，腰枝轻拂火车烟；
