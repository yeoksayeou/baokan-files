Title: Infighting within the Lebanese Ruling Clique Reveals the Truth: U.S. Troops Landed First, Chamoun "Invited" Later
Author:
Date: 1958-08-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 19th - "The People's Daily" today quoted Lebanese Prime Minister Solh as saying that the dispatch of American occupation troops to Lebanon was not notified to Chamoun in advance, nor was it at Chamoun's request as had been announced.

Solh, angered by Chamoun's attempt to dismiss him from his post, threatened to reveal the secrets about the landing of American occupation troops in Lebanon. He said that in order to make the landing of its troops appear legal, the United States had asked Chamoun and Malik, who was then in New York, to announce that the landing was carried out at the request of the Lebanese government.

Xinhua News Agency, 20th - Beirut News: The U.S. invading army is making preparations for a long-term occupation of Lebanon.

The U.S. invading army authorities in Beirut today categorically denied reports that another battalion of U.S. troops would be withdrawn from Lebanon. Yesterday in Washington, a U.S. State Department spokesman also publicly announced that "there are currently no plans to withdraw more U.S. troops from Lebanon."

Meanwhile, according to a report by a Deutsche Presse-Agentur reporter from Beirut today, U.S. troops are continuing to unload equipment along the Lebanese coast. The U.S. military is building a powerful radio station in Beirut, with tall antenna masts being erected.

### 黎巴嫩统治集团内哄露真情  美军先登陆  夏蒙后“邀请”

1958-08-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　黎巴嫩统治集团内哄露真情
    美军先登陆  夏蒙后“邀请”
    新华社开罗19日电  “人民报”今天援引黎巴嫩总理索勒哈的话说，美国占领军派到黎巴嫩来事先并未通知夏蒙，而且也不是像曾经宣布的那样是应夏蒙的请求而来的。
    索勒哈为夏蒙曾经准备解除他的职务所触怒，扬言要揭露关于美国占领军在黎巴嫩登陆的秘密。他说，美国为了使它的军队登陆看来是合法的，曾经要求夏蒙和当时在纽约的马利克宣布登陆是应黎巴嫩政府的请求而进行的。
    新华社20日讯  贝鲁特消息：美国侵略军正在进行长期侵占黎巴嫩的准备工作。
    在贝鲁特的侵黎美军当局今天断然否认将再有一营美军撤出黎巴嫩的消息。昨天在华盛顿，美国国务院发言人也公开宣布，“目前并没有从黎巴嫩撤出更多的美国军队的计划。”
    同时，据德意志新闻社记者今天自贝鲁特报道说，美军继续沿黎巴嫩海岸卸下装备。美军正在贝鲁特修建一座强力的电台，高大的天线杆正在树立中。（附图片）
