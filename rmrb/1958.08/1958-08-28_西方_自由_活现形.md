Title: The Living Embodiment of Western "Freedom"
Author: Yuan Shuipai
Date: 1958-08-28
Page: 8

The Living Embodiment of Western "Freedom"
　　袁水拍

　　What "freedom of the press"!
　　What "personal freedom"!
　　The British Empire has always boasted,
　　Now it's on full display in Hong Kong.
　　Throwing reporters out the door,
　　Driving teachers and students out of schools,
　　Sneakingly attacking the Chinese Middle School,
　　Even unleashing "specially trained fists"!
　　Viciously smashing cameras,
　　Afraid they'll expose your disgrace,
　　Even flashbulbs have become enemies,
　　All beaten up by the brave warriors.
　　How frightened you are!
　　Timid and vile.
　　How unfree you are!
　　Still boasting about what?
　　Hong Kong! Hong Kong!
　　Opium, secret agents, executioners,
　　Our beautiful land is being made
　　Filthy and stinking by the criminal colonialists!

### 西方“自由”活现形
袁水拍
1958-08-28
第8版()
专栏：

　　西方“自由”活现形
　　袁水拍
　　好一个“新闻自由”！
　　好一个“人身自由”！
　　大英帝国一向夸口，
　　如今在香港展览个够。
　　把新闻记者摔出大门，
　　把教师学生赶出校舍，
　　摸黑偷袭中华中学，
　　还施展出“特别训练过的拳头”！
　　把照相机狠狠地踩烂，
　　怕它使你们出乖露丑，
　　连闪光灯也成了大敌，
　　一起被勇士们打在里头。
　　害怕得很啊！
　　胆怯而又下流。
　　很不自由啊！
　　还吹什么牛！
　　香港！香港！
　　鸦片、特务、刽子手，
　　大好山河被罪恶的殖民者
　　搅得又脏又臭！
