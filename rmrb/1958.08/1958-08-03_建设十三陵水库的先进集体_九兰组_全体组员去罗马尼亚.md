Title: The Advanced Collective that Built the Ming Tombs Reservoir: All Members of the "Nine Lan Group" Go to Romania
Author:
Date: 1958-08-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The entire group of "Nine Lan Group," an advanced collective that won the title for building the Ming Tombs Reservoir, left Beijing at 6:30 this morning aboard a Tu-104 passenger plane for a friendly visit to the Romanian People's Republic at the invitation of Kiv Stoica, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic.

The Nine Lan Group visiting Romania includes the group leader, Hao Yulan, and members Yan Xiulan, Ji Shulan, Diao Zhenlan, Wang Guilan, Zhang Shulan, Sun Shulan, Liu Jilan, and Jin Shulan. They are all working women, the oldest being twenty-two years old and the youngest being seventeen years old. Wang Zhiming, Chairman of the Changping District Women's Federation of Beijing, is leading the Nine Lan Group abroad.

In April of this year, the Romanian government delegation headed by Chairman Kiv Stoica, who came to our country on a friendly visit, visited the Ming Tombs Reservoir. After meeting with the Nine Lan Group, Chairman Kiv Stoica extended an invitation to them to visit Romania after the reservoir was completed.

### 建设十三陵水库的先进集体  “九兰组”全体组员去罗马尼亚

1958-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

    建设十三陵水库的先进集体
    “九兰组”全体组员去罗马尼亚
    新华社2日讯　应罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席基伏·斯托伊卡邀请，在建设十三陵水库中获得先进集体称号的“九兰组”的全体组员，今晨六时半乘图—一○四型客机离开北京前往罗马尼亚人民共和国进行友好访问。
    访问罗马尼亚的九兰组，包括组长郝玉兰，组员阎秀兰、纪淑兰、刁振兰、王桂兰、张淑兰、孙淑兰、刘继兰和金淑兰。她们都是劳动妇女，最大的二十二岁，最小的十七岁。率领九兰组出国的是北京市昌平区妇女联合会主席王志明。
    今年4月间，来我国作友好访问的以基伏·斯托伊卡主席为首的罗马尼亚政府代表团到十三陵水库进行了参观。基伏·斯托伊卡主席在会晤了九兰组之后，向她们提出了在水库建成后访问罗马尼亚的邀请。
