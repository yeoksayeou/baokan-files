Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Holds Talks with Prime Minister Sihanouk
Author:
Date: 1958-08-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th. Premier Zhou Enlai, representing the Chinese government, began talks this afternoon with the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, Prince Norodom Sihanouk.

Participating in the talks on the Chinese side were: Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Lei Renmin, and Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission Xue Muqiao.

Participating in the talks on the Cambodian side were members of the Cambodian Royal Delegation: Royal High Council Advisor Nhiek Tioulong, Minister of National Defence, Territorial Defence and National Security Tea Kim, and Minister of Economy and Finance Touch Kim.

The talks proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

　　Premier Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister Sihanouk in talks. On the right is Nhiek Tioulong.
