Title: Police are as Ferocious as Jackals: Black Teenager Beaten to Death
Author:
Date: 1958-08-30
Page: 5

　　Police are as Ferocious as Jackals
    Black Teenager Beaten to Death
    Xinhua News Agency, August 29th: New York dispatch: An American black youth was inexplicably beaten to death by police in Montgomery, Alabama, on the night of the 27th.
    The black youth, named Foster, was only seventeen years old. He was beaten to death allegedly because he had "insulted" a white woman on the phone. (Attached picture)
　　(Originally published in Rome's "Report")

### 警察凶恶似豺狼  黑人少年被打死

1958-08-30
第5版()
专栏：

　　警察凶恶似豺狼
    黑人少年被打死
    新华社29日讯　纽约消息：一个美国黑人青年27日深夜在亚拉巴马州的蒙哥马利城平白被警察打死。
    这个黑人青年名叫福斯特，今年只有十七岁。他被打死的“理由”是据说他曾经在电话里“污辱”了一个白人妇女。（附图片）
　　（原载罗马“汇报”）
