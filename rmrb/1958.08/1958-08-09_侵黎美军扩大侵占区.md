Title: U.S. Troops Invading Lebanon Expand Occupied Areas
Author:
Date: 1958-08-09
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, August 8th: According to the Beirut newspaper "Al Kifah," U.S. troops invading Lebanon will occupy the Majayoun Pass in southern Lebanon, not far from the Syrian border, and are preparing to establish a submarine base in Jounieh Bay, north of Beirut.

### 侵黎美军扩大侵占区

1958-08-09
第2版()
专栏：

    侵黎美军扩大侵占区
    新华社8日讯  贝鲁特消息：据贝鲁特“斗争报”报道，侵黎美军将侵占黎巴嫩南部离叙利亚边境不远的迈尔杰乌荣隘口，并且准备在贝鲁特北面的朱尼亚湾建立一个潜水艇基地。
