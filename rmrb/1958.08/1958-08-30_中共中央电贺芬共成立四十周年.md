Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Message to Finnish Communist Party on its 40th Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1958-08-30
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 29th. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Finnish Communist Party on the 28th, congratulating the Finnish Communist Party on its 40th anniversary. The text of the telegram is as follows:
    To the Central Committee of the Finnish Communist Party:
    On the occasion of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Finnish Communist Party, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends its warm congratulations to you, and through you, to all members of the Finnish Communist Party and the Finnish people.
    We congratulate the Finnish Communist Party on the great achievements it has made in the struggle to safeguard the interests of the Finnish people over the past 40 years, and we congratulate the recent encouraging victory of the Finnish Communist Party and its allies in the parliamentary elections. This victory is the result of the Finnish Communist Party uniting all patriotic and democratic forces in an unwavering struggle to safeguard the interests of the Finnish nation and people. It vividly reflects the growing desire of the Finnish people to strive for peace, democracy, and social progress.
    We believe that the Finnish Communist Party, holding high the banner of Marxism-Leninism, and always vigilant against the conspiracies of imperialism and the dangers of revisionism, will surely be able to unite all forces that can be united more broadly, and achieve new successes in the struggle to safeguard the interests of the Finnish nation and people.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　August 28, 1958

### 中共中央电贺芬共成立四十周年

1958-08-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央电贺芬共成立四十周年
    新华社29日讯　中国共产党中央委员会28日致电芬兰共产党中央委员会，祝贺芬兰共产党成立四十周年。电文如下：芬兰共产党中央委员会：
    欣逢芬兰共产党成立四十周年纪念，中国共产党中央委员会谨向你们并通过你们向芬兰共产党全体党员和芬兰人民致以热烈的祝贺。
    我们祝贺芬兰共产党四十年来在为维护芬兰人民的利益的斗争中所取得的伟大成就，并且祝贺最近芬兰共产党及其盟友在议会选举中所取得的令人鼓舞的胜利，这个胜利是芬兰共产党团结一切爱国民主力量为维护芬兰民族和人民的利益而进行坚持不渝的斗争的结果，它生动地反映了芬兰人民对于争取和平、民主和社会进步的日益增长的愿望。
    我们相信，高举着马克思列宁主义旗帜的、时刻警惕着帝国主义阴谋和修正主义危害的芬兰共产党，一定能够更加广泛地团结一切可能团结的力量，在维护芬兰民族和人民的利益的斗争中取得新的成就。
                      中国共产党中央委员会　1958年8月28日
