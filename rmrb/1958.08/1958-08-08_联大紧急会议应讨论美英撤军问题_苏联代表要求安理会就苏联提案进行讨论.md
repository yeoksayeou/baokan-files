Title: UN General Assembly Emergency Meeting Should Discuss US-UK Troop Withdrawal; Soviet Representative Requests Security Council to Discuss Soviet Proposal
Author:
Date: 1958-08-08
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th dispatch from New York: The Soviet Union's representative to the United Nations submitted a proposal to the Security Council on the 6th, requesting that an emergency special session of the United Nations General Assembly be convened to discuss the issue of the withdrawal of US and British troops from Lebanon and Jordan, and requested that it be discussed at the Security Council meeting scheduled for the 7th. The full text of the proposal is as follows:
    "The Security Council,
    "Considering the situation in the Near and Middle East created by the entry of United States armed forces into Lebanon and the entry of United Kingdom armed forces into Jordan,
    "Noting that these actions by the United States and the United Kingdom constitute a threat to international peace and security,
    "Noting that the Security Council has proven unable to exercise its primary responsibility for the maintenance of international peace and security,
    "Decides to convene an emergency special session of the General Assembly to consider the question of the immediate withdrawal of United States troops from Lebanon and the immediate withdrawal of United Kingdom troops from Jordan."

### 联大紧急会议应讨论美英撤军问题  苏联代表要求安理会就苏联提案进行讨论

1958-08-08
第1版()
专栏：

    联大紧急会议应讨论美英撤军问题
    苏联代表要求安理会就苏联提案进行讨论
    新华社7日讯  纽约消息：苏联驻联合国代表6日向安全理事会提出一项要求召开联合国大会紧急特别会议来讨论美英军队撤出黎巴嫩和约旦的问题的提案，并且要求在订于7日举行的安全理事会会议上加以讨论。提案全文如下：
    “安全理事会，
    “考虑了由于美国武装部队进入黎巴嫩和联合王国武装部队进入约旦所造成的中近东局势，
    “注意到美国和联合王国的这些行动构成了对国际和平和安全的威胁，
    “注意到安全理事会已经证明不能够行使它的维护国际和平和安全的基本责任，
    “兹决定召开一次大会紧急特别会议来考虑美国军队立即撤出黎巴嫩和联合王国军队立即撤出约旦的问题。”
