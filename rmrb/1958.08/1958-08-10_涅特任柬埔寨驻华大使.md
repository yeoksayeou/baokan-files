Title: Nhek Tioulong Appointed Cambodian Ambassador to China
Author:
Date: 1958-08-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th: Former Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia and current Advisor to the Royal Council, His Excellency Nhek Tioulong, has been appointed as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Cambodia to the People's Republic of China, stationed in Beijing.

This appointment has been approved by the People's Republic of China.

### 涅特任柬埔寨驻华大使

1958-08-10
第1版()
专栏：

    涅特任柬埔寨驻华大使
    新华社9日讯　柬埔寨王国前首相、现任王庭最高会议顾问兰·涅特阁下已被任命为柬埔寨驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使，驻节北京。
    此项任命已获得中华人民共和国的同意。
