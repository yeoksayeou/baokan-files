Title: Khrushchev Returns to Moscow; Sends Greetings to Mongolian Party and Government Leaders En Route Home
Author:
Date: 1958-08-05
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 3rd: Nikita Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, along with his entourage, returned to Moscow by a special "Tu-104" plane at 7:25 PM Moscow time today.
    Greeting them at Vnukovo Airport were Soviet party and government leaders Kuusinen, Suslov, Shvernik, Kosygin, and others.
    Also present at the airport to greet them were Dong Biwu, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee who is currently in Moscow, Zhang Wentian, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, An Ziwen, member of the CPC Central Committee and Minister of the Organization Department of the CPC Central Committee, Liu Xiao, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Lieutenant General Han Zhenji, Military Attaché, and embassy staff.
    Xinhua News Agency, August 4th: According to TASS, Moscow: Nikita Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, sent a radio telegram on August 3rd to Damba and Tsedenbal, leaders of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the government, when his plane flew over the territory of the Mongolian People's Republic on his way back to Moscow from Beijing. In the telegram, he conveyed fraternal greetings to them and expressed regret for not being able to stay in Ulan Bator and meet with them. In the telegram, Khrushchev congratulated the Mongolian People's Republic on its achievements on the road to socialist development.

### 赫鲁晓夫回到莫斯科  归国途中电蒙古党政领导人致意

1958-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫回到莫斯科
    归国途中电蒙古党政领导人致意
    新华社莫斯科3日电　苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记、苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫及其随行人员一行在今天下午莫斯科时间七点二十五分乘“图一○四”专机回到莫斯科。
    到伏努科夫机场迎接的有苏联党和政府的领导人库西宁、苏斯洛夫、什维尔尼克、柯西金等。
    到机场迎接的还有正在莫斯科的中共中央政治局委员董必武，中共中央政治局候补委员、外交部副部长张闻天，中共中央委员、中共中央组织部部长安子文，中国驻苏联大使刘晓，武官韩振纪中将以及使馆人员。
    新华社4日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记、苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫3日离开北京飞返莫斯科途中经过蒙古人民共和国领土上空时，发了一封无线电报给蒙古人民革命党和政府领导人达姆巴和泽登巴尔。他在电报中向他们致兄弟的敬礼，并且为不能在乌兰巴托逗留和同他们会见表示遗憾。赫鲁晓夫在电报中祝贺蒙古人民共和国在社会主义的发展道路上获得成就。
