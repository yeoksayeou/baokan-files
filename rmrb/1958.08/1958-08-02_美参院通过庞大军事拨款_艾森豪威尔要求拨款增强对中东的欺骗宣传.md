Title: US Senate Passes Huge Military Appropriation; Eisenhower Requests Funds to Enhance Deceptive Propaganda in the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1958-08-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 31st dispatch from Washington: The US Senate passed the military appropriations bill for the 1959 fiscal year (July 1, 1958, to June 30, 1959) on the 30th, allocating $40,032 million for the US Department of Defense and the three armed services. This is $1,245 million more than US President Eisenhower originally requested and approximately $1.5 billion more than the House of Representatives passed. The increased funds are mainly for the development of new weapons. The bill also opposes reducing the number of US armed forces personnel.
    Since the appropriations figures passed by the Senate and the House of Representatives are different, the bill passed by the Senate is currently being returned to the House of Representatives for discussion.

Xinhua News Agency, 31st dispatch from Washington: US President Eisenhower requested Congress on the 30th for an additional appropriation of $22.3 million for the "Voice of America" to strengthen the deceptive broadcasts of this propaganda organ of US imperialism to the Middle East and Africa.
    White House Press Secretary Hagerty said that this money would be used to strengthen the broadcasting power equipment of the "Voice of America."
    This money will be used in the fiscal year beginning July 1.

### 美参院通过庞大军事拨款  艾森豪威尔要求拨款增强对中东的欺骗宣传

1958-08-02
第2版()
专栏：

    美参院通过庞大军事拨款
    艾森豪威尔要求拨款增强对中东的欺骗宣传
    新华社31日讯　华盛顿消息：美国参议院30日通过了1959财政年度（1958年7月1日到1959年6月30日）的军事拨款法案，规定拨出四百亿零三千二百多万美元作为美国国防部和三军的费用，这比美国总统艾森豪威尔原来要求的多十二亿四千五百多万美元，比众议院通过的多约十五亿美元，增加的款项主要用在发展新武器方面。法案同时还反对削减美国武装部队的人数。
    由于参议院和众议院通过的拨款数字不同，参议院通过的法案目前正拿回众议院讨论。
    新华社31日讯　华盛顿消息：美国总统艾森豪威尔30日要求国会给予“美国之音”二千二百三十万美元的额外拨款，加强美帝国主义的这个宣传机关对中东和非洲地区的欺世惑人的广播。
    白宫新闻秘书哈格蒂说，这笔钱将用来加强“美国之音”的广播电力设备。
    这笔钱将在7月1日开始的财政年度中使用。
