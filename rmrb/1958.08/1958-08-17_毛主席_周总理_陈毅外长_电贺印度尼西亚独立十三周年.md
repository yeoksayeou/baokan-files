Title: Chairman Mao, Premier Zhou, and Foreign Minister Chen Yi Send Congratulatory Telegrams on the 13th Anniversary of Indonesia's Independence
Author:
Date: 1958-08-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 16th -- Chairman Mao Zedong sent a telegram to President Sukarno of the Republic of Indonesia on the 15th, congratulating him on the 13th anniversary of the Republic of Indonesia's independence. The telegram reads as follows:
His Excellency President Sukarno of the Republic of Indonesia:

　　On the occasion of the 13th anniversary of the independence of the Republic of Indonesia, I extend warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the Indonesian people on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish the Indonesian people greater success in their just struggle to safeguard national unity and defend national independence and sovereignty. May the profound friendship between China and Indonesia continue to develop. I wish the Republic of Indonesia prosperity and strength.

　　Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, August 15, 1958, Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, August 16th -- Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to Premier Juanda of the Republic of Indonesia on the 14th, congratulating him on the 13th anniversary of the Republic of Indonesia's independence. The telegram reads as follows:
His Excellency Premier Juanda of the Republic of Indonesia:

　　On the occasion of the 13th anniversary of the independence of the Republic of Indonesia, I extend sincere congratulations to Your Excellency and the Indonesian government on behalf of the government of the People's Republic of China and in my own name.

　　In the past year, the Indonesian government and people have achieved significant victories in their struggle to safeguard national independence and sovereignty and oppose imperialist interference, and have made important contribution to safeguarding peace and security in Asia and the world. The Chinese government and people will unreservedly support your just struggle, and believe that your struggle will surely achieve complete victory.

　　May the friendly and cooperative relations between China and Indonesia be increasingly consolidated and developed. I wish the Indonesian government and people further achievements in the cause of building their own country.

　　Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, August 14, 1958, Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, August 16th -- Foreign Minister Chen Yi sent a telegram to Foreign Minister Subandrio of the Republic of Indonesia on the 14th, congratulating him on the 13th anniversary of the Republic of Indonesia's independence. The telegram reads as follows:
His Excellency Dr. Subandrio, Foreign Minister of the Republic of Indonesia:

　　On the joyous occasion of the 13th anniversary of the independence of the Republic of Indonesia, I extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency. May the friendship between China and Indonesia develop daily. May our two countries jointly make greater efforts to promote the friendly unity of Asian and African countries and to safeguard peace in Asia and the world.

　　Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of China, Chen Yi, August 14, 1958, Beijing

### 毛主席　周总理　陈毅外长　电贺印度尼西亚独立十三周年

1958-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席　周总理　陈毅外长
　　电贺印度尼西亚独立十三周年
    新华社16日讯　毛泽东主席15日致电印度尼西亚共和国苏加诺总统，祝贺印度尼西亚共和国独立十三周年。电文如下：印度尼西亚共和国苏加诺总统阁下：
    当印度尼西亚共和国独立十三周年的时候，我谨代表中国人民并且以我个人的名义，向阁下和印度尼西亚人民致热烈的祝贺。祝印度尼西亚人民在维护国家统一和捍卫国家独立和主权的正义斗争中取得更大的成就。祝中国和印度尼西亚两国间的深厚友谊不断发展。祝印度尼西亚共和国繁荣富强。
             中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东　1958年8月15日于北京
    新华社16日讯　周恩来总理14日致电印度尼西亚共和国朱安达总理，祝贺印度尼西亚共和国独立十三周年。电文如下：印度尼西亚共和国朱安达总理阁下：
    值此印度尼西亚共和国独立十三周年之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府并且以我个人的名义，向阁下和印度尼西亚政府致诚挚的祝贺。
    一年来，印度尼西亚政府和人民在维护国家的独立和主权、反对帝国主义干涉的斗争中获得了重大的胜利，对保卫亚洲和世界的和平和安全作出了重要的贡献。中国政府和人民将始终不渝地支持你们的正义斗争，并且相信你们的斗争一定能够获得完全的胜利。
    祝中国和印度尼西亚两国之间的友好合作关系日益巩固和发展。祝印度尼西亚政府和人民在建设自己国家的事业中获得进一步的成就。
      中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来　1958年8月14日于北京
    新华社16日讯　外交部长陈毅14日致电印度尼西亚共和国外交部长苏班德里约，祝贺印度尼西亚共和国独立十三周年。电文如下：印度尼西亚共和国外交部长苏班德里约博士阁下：
    欣逢印度尼西亚共和国独立十三周年，我谨向阁下表示热诚的祝贺。祝中国和印度尼西亚两国之间的友谊日益发展。祝贵我两国共同为促进亚非各国的友好团结和维护亚洲和世界的和平作出更大的努力。
        中华人民共和国外交部长　陈　毅　1958年8月14日于北京
