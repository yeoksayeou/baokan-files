Title: Yunnan Builds Heavy Machinery Plant
Author:
Date: 1958-08-11
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 9th: Construction of the Yunnan Heavy Machinery Plant began on the 8th.

After the plant is completed, it will be able to manufacture steel rolling and smelting equipment, machine tools, forging equipment, coal gas generators for synthetic ammonia plants, air compressors, and oil refining equipment. Yunnan Province will be able to supply its own general machinery needs.

### 云南兴建重型机械厂

1958-08-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　云南兴建重型机械厂
    据新华社昆明9日电　云南重型机械厂8日开土兴建。
    这座工厂建成以后，能制造轧钢和冶炼设备，机床，锻压设备，合成氨工厂的煤气发生炉，空气压缩机和炼油设备。云南全省所需要的一般的机械都能自给。
