Title: Chairman Mao Invited Prime Minister Sihanouk to Lunch

Author:
Date: 1958-08-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th - Chairman Mao Zedong invited Prime Minister Sihanouk to lunch on the 16th and had a cordial conversation. Chairman Mao's guests also included members of the Cambodian Royal National Delegation, Norodom Rannaridh, Kou Roun, and Ung Hong Sath. Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi were present.
