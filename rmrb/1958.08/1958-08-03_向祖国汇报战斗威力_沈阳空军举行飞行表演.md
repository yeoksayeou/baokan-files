Title: Reporting Combat Power to the Motherland: Shenyang Air Force Holds Flight Demonstration
Author:
Date: 1958-08-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, August 1st. A certain Air Force unit stationed in Shenyang of the Chinese People's Liberation Army held a flight demonstration this morning to report the powerful combat effectiveness of the Air Force to the people of the motherland. Party and government leaders of Liaoning Province and Shenyang City, along with more than 120,000 people from all walks of life, attended the demonstration.

Chen Dongshan, an "all-weather" pilot who has been awarded the honorary titles of First-Class Pilot and Second-Class Model, piloted the motherland's "War Eagle" and performed various stunts of intercepting enemy aircraft at high altitude.

After the dual-plane aerobatics and four-plane formation tracking air combat demonstrations, eight fighter jets performed an attack on simulated ground targets in the air. Their accurate shooting skills aroused applause and cheers from the audience.

### 向祖国汇报战斗威力  沈阳空军举行飞行表演

1958-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

    向祖国汇报战斗威力
    沈阳空军举行飞行表演
    据新华社沈阳1日电　中国人民解放军驻沈阳某空军部队，今天上午举行飞行表演，向祖国亲人汇报空军的强大战斗威力。辽宁省和沈阳市党政负责人与各界人民十二万多人参观了这场表演。
    荣获一级飞行员、二级模范光荣称号的“全天候”飞行员陈东山，驾驶着祖国的“战鹰”，在高空表演了追歼敌机的各种特技。
    在双机特技和四机编队跟踪空战表演以后，八架歼击机表演了在空中对地面假设目标的攻击。他们准确的射击技术，引起观众们的鼓掌和欢呼。
