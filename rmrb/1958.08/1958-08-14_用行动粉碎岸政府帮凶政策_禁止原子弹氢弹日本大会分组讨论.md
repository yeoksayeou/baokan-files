Title: Use Action to Crush the Kishida Government's Accomplice Policies: Japan Congress Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs Holds Group Discussions
Author:
Date: 1958-08-14
Page: 7
　　
　　Use Action to Crush the Kishida Government's Accomplice Policies
　　Japan Congress Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs Holds Group Discussions

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 13th: The Fourth World Congress Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for Disarmament, Japanese Congress, held discussions today in eighteen venues regarding the six topics reported by Kazami Akira and others at yesterday afternoon's conference.

    At the discussion meeting on the immediate and complete cessation of nuclear weapons testing, the prohibition of atomic and hydrogen bombs, and disarmament, participants unanimously condemned the United States and Britain for continuing to test atomic and hydrogen bombs. Representatives also pointed out that concrete actions must be taken to crush the accomplice policies of the Japanese government.

    At the discussion meeting on preventing Japan's nuclear armament, representatives unanimously emphasized that it is necessary to recognize that Japan's nuclear armament is aimed at China, and this policy should be crushed to promote the normalization of Sino-Japanese relations.

    At the discussion meeting on the movement to ban atomic and hydrogen bombs and the peace movement, participants unanimously advocated that mass movements must be launched to demand the enactment of laws prohibiting Japan's nuclear armament, and at the same time, to promote the adoption of resolutions by Japanese local assemblies to ban nuclear tests and prevent Japan's nuclear armament.

    Discussion meetings on the other three topics were also held simultaneously with lively discussions.

### 用行动粉碎岸政府帮凶政策　禁止原子弹氢弹日本大会分组讨论

1958-08-14
第7版()
专栏：

　　用行动粉碎岸政府帮凶政策
　　禁止原子弹氢弹日本大会分组讨论
    新华社13日讯　东京消息：第四届禁止原子弹氢弹与争取裁军世界大会日本大会今天就风见章等在昨天下午的大会上所报告的六项议题分别在十八个会场进行了讨论。
    在关于立即全面停止试验核武器、禁止原子弹氢弹和裁军问题的讨论会上，与会者一致谴责美英继续试验原子弹和氢弹的行为。代表们并且指出，必须以具体行动来粉碎日本政府的帮凶政策。
    在关于阻止核武装日本的问题的讨论会上，代表们一致强调指出，必须认清日本的核武装是对付中国的，应该粉碎这种政策，以推进日中关系的正常化。
    在关于禁止原子弹氢弹运动和和平运动的问题的讨论会上，与会者一致主张必须展开群众运动，要求制订禁止核武装日本的法律，同时还要推动日本各地方议会通过禁止核试验和阻止核武装日本的决议。
    关于其他三个议题的讨论会也都同时进行了热烈的讨论。
