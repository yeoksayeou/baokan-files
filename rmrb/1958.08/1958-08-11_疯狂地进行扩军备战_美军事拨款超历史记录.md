Title: Frantically Engaging in Military Expansion and War Preparations: US Military Appropriations Exceed Historical Records
Author:
Date: 1958-08-11
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 9th. The United States Congress passed a bill on the 7th stipulating that more than $39.6 billion be allocated from the expenditures for the 1959 fiscal year, beginning July 1 this year, as direct military expenses for the United States government. This amount is more than $800 million more than the government originally requested, with most of the increased funds earmarked for the manufacture of new weapons.

American newspapers commented that the military appropriation stipulated in the bill is the largest single appropriation in American history.

### 疯狂地进行扩军备战　美军事拨款超历史记录

1958-08-11
第7版()
专栏：

　　疯狂地进行扩军备战
　　美军事拨款超历史记录
    新华社9日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国会在7日通过了一项法案，规定在从今年7月1日开始的1959财政年度的支出中拨出三百九十六亿多美元作为美国政府的直接军事费用，这个数目比政府原来要求的数目还多八亿多美元，增拨的款项大部分都是规定用来制造新武器的。
    美国报纸评论这个法案时指出，法案所规定的军事拨款是美国历史上数目最大的一次拨款。
