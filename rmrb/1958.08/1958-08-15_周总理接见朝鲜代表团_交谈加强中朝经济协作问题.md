Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives North Korean Delegation to Discuss Strengthening Sino-Korean Economic Cooperation

Author:

Date: 1958-08-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 14th: Premier Zhou Enlai received the North Korean government delegation this afternoon, headed by Li Joo-yeon, Vice Premier of the North Korean Cabinet, along with all members of the delegation: Li Jong-ok, Chairman of the State Planning Commission; Kim Choi-seon, Vice Minister of Internal and External Commerce; and Li Yong-ho, North Korean Ambassador to China.

During the meeting, Premier Zhou engaged in cordial discussions with the North Korean guests on the issue of further strengthening economic cooperation between China and North Korea.

Present at the meeting were Vice Premier Li Xiannian, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Vice Minister of Water Resources and Electric Power Liu Lanbo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission Liu Mingfu, Deputy Director of the State Technology Commission Zhang Youxuan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Lin Haiyun, and Chinese Ambassador to North Korea Qiao Xiaoguang, among others.

### 周总理接见朝鲜代表团　交谈加强中朝经济协作问题

1958-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　周总理接见朝鲜代表团
　　交谈加强中朝经济协作问题
    新华社14日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了朝鲜政府代表团团长、朝鲜内阁副首相李周渊和代表团全体团员、国家计划委员会委员长李锺玉、对内外商业省副相金最善和朝鲜驻中国大使李永镐。
    接见时，周总理同朝鲜贵宾们就进一步加强中朝两国经济协作问题进行了亲切交谈。
    接见时在座的，有副总理李先念，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，水利电力部副部长刘澜波，外交部副部长曾涌泉，国家计划委员会副主任刘明夫，国家技术委员会副主任张有萱，对外贸易部副部长林海云，我国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光等人。
