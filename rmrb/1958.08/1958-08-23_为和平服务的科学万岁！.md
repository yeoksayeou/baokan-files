Title: Long Live Science Serving Peace!
Author: Yuan Shuipai
Date: 1958-08-23
Page: 8

　　Long Live Science Serving Peace!
    Yuan Shuipai
　　Like a thunderbolt, the news is spread:
　　A great invention of Soviet science!
　　Nuclear power is not for killing,
　　But for peaceful use of thermonuclear energy.
　　The brotherly friendship between China and the Soviet Union is profound,
　　Good news is announced in Beijing.
　　The jubilation of the masses shakes heaven and earth,
　　Even the enemy is alarmed!
　　The atomic madmen's dream has not ended,
　　Poisonous dust descends over the Pacific.
　　The warning from China and the Soviet Union carries immense weight:
　　A new war must be stopped!

### 为和平服务的科学万岁！
袁水拍
1958-08-23
第8版()
专栏：

　　为和平服务的科学万岁！
    袁水拍
　　霹雳一声传电讯：
　　苏联科学大发明！
　　核子不作杀人用，
　　和平利用热核能。
　　中苏兄弟情谊重，
　　喜事宣布在北京。
　　万众腾欢震天地，
　　哪怕敌人心不惊！
　　原子狂人梦未醒，
　　太平洋上降毒尘。
　　中苏警告千钧重：
　　一定制止新战争！
