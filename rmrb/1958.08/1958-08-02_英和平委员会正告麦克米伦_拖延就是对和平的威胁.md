Title: British Peace Committee Warns Macmillan: Delay is a Threat to Peace
Author:
Date: 1958-08-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, London, July 31st (TASS): The British Peace Committee sent a telegram to British Prime Minister Macmillan stating that "continued delays in determining the date and location of a top-level meeting to discuss the Near East problem will increase the threat to peace." The committee demanded "an immediate agreement on the date of the meeting and an extension of the parliamentary session."
    The committee also sent telegrams with the same content to the Labour Party and Liberal Party parliamentary leaders, Gaitskell and Grimond.
    An editorial in "World News" weekly, commenting on President Eisenhower's recent reply to Khrushchev regarding the convening of a summit conference, wrote: The British and American governments always evade every step that could lead to high-level talks. This delaying tactic must end. Imperialists are adept at taking steps that will lead to new wars at any time. Tensions in the Middle East cannot be eased until their armed forces are withdrawn from the region.

### 英和平委员会正告麦克米伦  拖延就是对和平的威胁

1958-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    英和平委员会正告麦克米伦
    拖延就是对和平的威胁
    据新华社1日讯　据塔斯社伦敦7月31日讯：英国和平委员会打电报给英国首相麦克米伦说，“在确定讨论中近东问题的最高级会议的日期和地点方面继续拖延将增加对和平的威胁”，委员会要求“立刻就会议的日期问题达成协议和延长议会会议”。
    委员会还把内容相同的电报发给工党和自由党议会党团领袖盖次克尔和格里蒙德。
    “世界新闻”周刊的社论在评论美国总统艾森豪威尔最近就召开政府首脑会议问题给赫鲁晓夫的复信时写道：英国和美国政府总是规避每一个能够导致高级会谈的步骤，这种拖延战术必须结束。帝国主义者在任何时机都善于采取将导致新战争的步骤。在他们的武装力量还没有撤出这个地区以前，中东的紧张局势不可能缓和。
