Title: Kuwaiti Education Minister Says Kuwait Supports Iraq
Author:
Date: 1958-08-01
Page: 3

    Kuwaiti Education Minister Says
    Kuwait Supports Iraq
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 30th. Sabah, the Kuwaiti Minister of Education, who is currently visiting the United Arab Republic, said on the 29th that Kuwait welcomes and supports the Republic of Iraq.
    The Minister said that conditions are now favorable for full cooperation between the two countries.
    He expressed the hope that the high-level meeting will realize the aspirations of the people of all countries for stability and peace.

### 科威特教育大臣说  科威特支持伊拉克

1958-08-01
第3版()
专栏：

    科威特教育大臣说
    科威特支持伊拉克
    新华社开罗30日电　正在阿拉伯联合共和国进行访问的科威特教育大臣萨巴哈29日说，科威特欢迎伊拉克共和国并且支持它。
    这位大臣说，现在，条件有利于两国进行充分合作。
    他表示，希望高级会议能够实现各国人民向往稳定和和平的愿望。
