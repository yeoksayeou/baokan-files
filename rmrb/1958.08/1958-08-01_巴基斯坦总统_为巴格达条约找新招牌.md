Title: Pakistani President Seeks New Signboard for Baghdad Pact
Author:
Date: 1958-08-01
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, July 31st. Pakistani President Mirza recently said while meeting with a reporter from the "Indian Express" stationed in Pakistan, that he advocates the formation of a new military alliance between the United States, Britain, Turkey, Iran, and Pakistan to replace the already bankrupt Baghdad Pact.

Mirza said that he does not feel ashamed or wrong about this proposition.

### 巴基斯坦总统  为巴格达条约找新招牌

1958-08-01
第2版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦总统
    为巴格达条约找新招牌
    新华社卡拉奇31日电　巴基斯坦总统米尔扎最近在接见“印度快报”驻巴基斯坦的记者时说，他主张美国、英国、土耳其、伊朗和巴基斯坦结成新的军事联盟，来代替已经破产的巴格达条约。
    米尔扎说，他对于这种主张并不感到羞愧和错了。
