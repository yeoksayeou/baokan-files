Title: Korean Government Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-08-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 13th: The Korean government delegation headed by Vice Premier Li Zhouyuan returned to Beijing by train tonight from Tangshan, accompanied by Lin Haiyun, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of our country.

The distinguished guests visited the Tangshan Steel Plant, the Tangjiazhuang Coal Mine which uses hydraulic coal mining, the Liutun Street People-Run Iron and Hardware Joint Factory, and the Tangshan Railway Institute in Tangshan.

Qiao Xiaoguang, our country's ambassador to Korea, and Wen Zaishu, Korean Chargé d'Affaires ad interim in China, and others greeted the distinguished guests at the Beijing Railway Station.

### 朝鲜政府代表团回北京

1958-08-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜政府代表团回北京
    新华社13日讯　以李周渊副首相为首的朝鲜政府代表团，今天晚间由我对外贸易部副部长林海云陪同从唐山乘火车回到北京。
    贵宾们在唐山先后参观了唐山钢厂、用水力采煤的唐家庄煤矿、刘屯街道民办机铁联合厂和唐山铁道学院等。
    我国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光，朝鲜驻中国临时代办文在洙等人到北京车站迎接贵宾。
