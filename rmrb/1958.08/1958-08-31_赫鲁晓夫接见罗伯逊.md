Title: Khrushchev Receives Robeson
Author:
Date: 1958-08-31
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Yalta, August 29th: TASS reports that while vacationing in southern Crimea, Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, received Paul Robeson, the famous American social activist and winner of the International Lenin Prize "For Strengthening Peace Among Peoples," at the "Lower Oreanda" sanatorium. Khrushchev had a cordial and warm conversation with Mr. and Mrs. Robeson.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见罗伯逊

1958-08-31
第3版()
专栏：

　　赫鲁晓夫接见罗伯逊
    据新华社30日讯　塔斯社雅尔塔29日讯：正在克里米亚南部休假的苏共中央第一书记、苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫在访问“下奥连安达”疗养院时，接见了在那里的美国著名社会活动家、“加强国际和平”列宁国际奖金的获得者罗伯逊。赫鲁晓夫同罗伯逊夫妇举行了亲切热诚的谈话。
