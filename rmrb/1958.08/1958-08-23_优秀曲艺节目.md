Title: Excellent Quyi Programs
Author:
Date: 1958-08-23
Page: 8

To further promote the prosperity and development of quyi art and better serve construction and workers, peasants, and soldiers, the First National Quyi Performance Conference has formed two troupes from the excellent programs in the conference, which will soon tour Shenyang, Tianjin, Jinan, Nanjing, Hangzhou, Nanchang, Guangzhou, Zhengzhou, Wuhan, Luoyang, Xi'an, Lanzhou, Chongqing, Chengdu and other places.

To meet the demands of the broad masses of quyi-loving audiences in the capital, these two troupes will hold several performances before leaving Beijing. These are all good programs worth listening to again and again. (Shu)

### 优秀曲艺节目

1958-08-23
第8版()
专栏：舞台和银幕

    优秀曲艺节目
    为了进一步推动曲艺艺术的繁荣发展，更好地为建设、为工农兵服务，第一届全国曲艺会演大会已将会演中的优秀节目，分别组成了两个团，即将到沈阳、天津、济南、南京、杭州、南昌、广州、郑州、武汉、洛阳、西安、兰州、重庆、成都等地巡回演出。
    这两个团为满足首都广大爱好曲艺观众的要求，离京前举行几场演出，这些都是值得一听再听的好节目。　（树）
