Title: Just Punishment
Author: Yuan Shuipai
Date: 1958-08-26
Page: 8

　　Just Punishment
　　Yuan Shuipai
　　I. You bomb our coastal villages and towns,
　　You kill our sailors and fishermen,
　　Chiang Kai-shek bandits! Your evil deeds are numerous,
　　Today you have received just punishment!
　　Every Chinese person,
　　Is of one heart with the People's Liberation Army!
　　Artillerymen, you shoot accurately!
　　Shoot well! Shoot hard!
　　Yes! All the cannons, all the guns,
　　Are aimed at the heads of the traitors.
　　Our territory is pure and sacred,
　　Allowing no dirty germs!
　　II. You steal our Taiwan,
　　You invade our territorial waters,
　　Dulles! You have done all kinds of bad things,
　　Today you still dare to interrupt!
　　The Chinese people overthrew the traitorous government,
　　We arrange our own destiny,
　　We have chosen our own path,
　　Determined to crush all obstacles!
　　Mountains cannot stop us, water cannot block us,
　　Six hundred million people have this heroic spirit,
　　Angry waves rise on the Pacific,
　　Seventh Fleet! Be careful of capsizing!

### 正义的严惩
袁水拍
1958-08-26
第8版()
专栏：

　　正义的严惩
　　袁水拍
　　一、你炸我们沿海村镇，
　　你杀我们海员渔民，
　　蒋贼军！你作恶多端，
　　今天受到了正义的严惩！
　　中国每一个老百姓，
　　都和人民解放军一个心！
　　神炮手，你们打得准！
　　打得好！打得狠！
　　对！所有的炮，所有的枪，
　　都瞄准卖国贼的脑门。
　　我们的国土纯洁神圣，
　　容不得肮脏细菌！
　　二、你窃据我们的台湾，
　　你侵犯我们的领海，
　　杜勒斯！你坏事作尽，
　　今天还敢插什么嘴！
　　中国人民把卖国政府推翻，
　　自己的命运自己安排，
　　我们选定了自己的道路，
　　决心要粉碎一切障碍！
　　山不能挡，水不能拦，
　　六亿人民有这个英雄气派，
　　太平洋上怒涛生，
　　第七舰队！你当心翻下海！
