Title: Philippine Columnist Says Chinese People Have the Right to Liberate Kinmen and Matsu
Author:
Date: 1958-08-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 29th: A columnist in the "Manila Chronicle" in the Philippines, Soliongco, said today that Kinmen and Matsu are part of China, just as Staten Island is part of the United States.

Soliongco said that after the Chinese people overthrew the rule of the Kuomintang and liberated the Chinese mainland, they have the legal right to obtain Kinmen and Matsu. He said that therefore, the "offensive launched by the Chinese people" against these islands "is a continuation of the Chinese civil war."

The columnist warned: "The real danger is that our officials may be fooled by the propaganda of the Kuomintang 'Ambassador' Chen Chih-mai and plunge the country into difficult and dangerous disputes."

### 菲律宾一专栏作家说  中国人民有权解放金马

1958-08-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　菲律宾一专栏作家说
    中国人民有权解放金马
    新华社29日讯　马尼拉消息：“马尼拉纪事报”专栏作家索力昂科今天说，金门和马祖是中国的一部分，正如斯塔腾岛是美国的一部分一样。
    索力昂科谈到中国人民推翻了国民党的统治，解放了中国大陆以后，在法律上有权得到金门和马祖。他说，因此，中国人民对这些岛屿“发动的进攻是中国内战的继续”。
    这位专栏作家警告说：“真正的危险是，我国官员可能上国民党‘大使’陈之迈的宣传的当，把国家投入困难的和危险的纠纷中。”
