Title: Italian Defense Ministry Budget Increases Sharply
Author:
Date: 1958-08-03
Page: 3
Brief News

    Italian Defense Ministry Budget Increases Sharply
    Rome dispatch: The Italian Senate passed the Defense Ministry budget draft on the 31st, stipulating expenditures of 595,194,400,000 lire for the 1958-59 fiscal year, which is 23,177,000,000 lire more than the previous fiscal year. Defense Minister Segni said that the government intends to establish a base for launching rockets in the country and requested continued increases in military appropriations.

### 意国防部预算激增

1958-08-03
第3版()
专栏：简讯

    意国防部预算激增
    罗马讯：意大利参议院31日通过了国防部预算草案，规定1958—59财政年度的开支为五千九百五十一亿九千四百万里拉，即比前一财政年度多两百三十一亿七千七百万里拉。国防部长塞尼说，政府打算在国内建立发射火箭的基地，要求继续增加军事拨款。
