Title: Culture Grows Daily
Author: Yongcheng
Date: 1958-08-13
Page: 5

　  Culture grows daily. Blackboards under the riverbank, textbooks on the meal-fields, asking people when you see them, explaining to people when you meet them, learning from each other, culture grows daily.
              --Yongcheng

### 文化日日长
永城
1958-08-13
第5版()
专栏：

　  文化日日长黑板下河塘，书本上饭场，见人就去问，逢人便去讲，互相来学习，文化日日长。
              ——永城
