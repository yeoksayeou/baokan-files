Title: Brazilian Radio and Television Artists Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-08-08
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, July 7th: A fourteen-member delegation of Brazilian radio and television artists arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.
    The delegation is composed of performers from Brazilian radio and television stations, the Farroupilha Five-Person Song and Dance Troupe, and well-known folk song soloists. Upon arrival in Beijing, the head of the delegation, Alberto Camo, told reporters that the program brought by the delegation this time consists of Brazilian folk songs, dances, and music, which reflect the current life and struggles of the Brazilian people.

### 巴西广播电视艺术家代表团到北京

1958-08-08
第4版()
专栏：

    巴西广播电视艺术家代表团到北京
    据新华社7日讯  巴西广播、电视艺术家代表团一行十四人今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    代表团是由巴西广播电台和电视台的表演家、法鲁比亚五人歌舞团和著名的民歌独唱家们组成的。代表团团长阿尔伯托·卡莫在到达北京后对记者说，这次代表团带来的节目都是巴西民间的歌曲、舞蹈和乐曲，它们反映了当前巴西人民的生活和斗争。
