　　Friendly Exchanges
　　Korean Government Delegation Returns to Beijing
　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 29th - The government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Vice Premier Li Joo-yeon, returned to Beijing by plane this morning from Shanghai. Cao Diqiu, Vice Mayor of Shanghai, Niu Shucai and others were at the airport to see them off.
　　The delegation arrived in Shanghai on the 24th. During their stay in Shanghai, they visited a large number of factories, including shipyards, printing and dyeing factories, paper mills, cement factories, vehicle steel ball factories, and so on. The representatives were particularly interested in small and medium-sized factories with outdated equipment but high production efficiency.
　　Head of the delegation Li Joo-yeon and some members also visited Hangzhou for two days.
　　Korean Light Industry Delegation Arrives in Beijing
　　Xinhua News Agency, 29th - The light industry delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, consisting of forty people, led by Minister of Light Industry of the Korean Cabinet, Moon Man-wook, arrived in Beijing by train at noon on the 28th, at the invitation of our country. Li Zhuchen, Minister of Light Industry, and others welcomed them at the station.
　　After a short stay in Beijing, the delegation will visit Shanghai and other places to see food, daily necessities, light industrial machinery equipment manufacturing, and handicrafts cooperatives.
　　British MP Crossman Arrives in Beijing
　　Xinhua News Agency, 29th - British Labour Party MP Crossman arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 28th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs. Representatives of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs welcomed him at the airport.
　　Dutch Writer Vries Arrives in Beijing
　　Xinhua News Agency, 29th - Dutch writer De Vries, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, arrived in Beijing on the 29th. Wang Yafan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Writers Association, and others went to the station to welcome him.

### 友好往来

1958-08-30
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

　　友好往来
    朝鲜政府代表团回京
    据新华社上海29日电　以李周渊副首相为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团今天早晨乘飞机离上海回北京。上海市副市长曹荻秋、牛树才等都到机场欢送。
    代表团是24日到达上海的。他们在上海期间曾参观了大批工厂，其中有造船厂、印染厂、造纸厂、水泥厂、车具钢珠厂等等。代表们对设备陈旧但生产效率很高的中小型工厂，特别感到兴趣。
    李周渊团长和部分团员还曾到杭州游览参观了两天。
　　朝鲜轻工业参观团到京
    新华社29日讯　应邀来我国访问的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国轻工业参观团一行四十人，由朝鲜内阁轻工业相文万郁率领，28日中午乘火车到达北京。到车站欢迎的有轻工业部部长李烛尘等。
    参观团在北京作短期逗留后，将到上海等地参观食品、日用品、轻工业机器设备制造及手工业合作社等部门。
　　英议员克罗斯曼到京
    新华社29日讯　英国工党议员克罗斯曼应中国人民外交学会邀请，在28日下午乘飞机抵达北京。中国人民外交学会的代表到机场欢迎。
　　荷兰作家弗里斯到京
    新华社29日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请的荷兰作家德·弗里斯已于29日抵达北京，前往车站欢迎的有中国作家协会副秘书长王亚凡等。
