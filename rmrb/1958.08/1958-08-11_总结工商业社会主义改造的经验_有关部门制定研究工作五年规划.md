Title: Summarizing the Experience of Socialist Transformation of Industry and Commerce: Relevant Departments Formulate a Five-Year Plan for Research Work
Author:
Date: 1958-08-11
Page: 5

Academic Trends:

　　Summarizing the Experience of Socialist Transformation of Industry and Commerce
　　Relevant Departments Formulate a Five-Year Plan for Research Work
    Our country has achieved a great victory in carrying out the socialist transformation of capitalist industry and commerce. Summarizing the experience of this transformation work and conducting theoretical research on it is an important task in scientific research. In order to carry out this scientific research work in a planned way, the Central Administration for Industry and Commerce and the Institute of Economics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and other relevant units have drafted a five-year plan (draft) for the research work on the socialist transformation of capitalist economy (1958-1962).
    The plan stipulates that the basic tasks of the research work are: to collect historical data on the emergence, development and demise of Chinese capitalism and the bourgeoisie, and to study its changing process; to summarize the experience of the Party and the state in the socialist transformation of capitalist industry and commerce and of national bourgeois elements; and to conduct theoretical research on the transition of the capitalist economy to a socialist economy. In order to complete this research task, the working principle of "combining theory with practice, learning while doing, collective collaboration, more, faster, better and more economical" will be adopted to make the data work and research work serve the reality of the socialist revolution.
    The main projects of the five-year plan are: (1) to compile five special subject materials on the socialist transformation of private industry, commerce, transportation industry, the national bourgeoisie, and the elimination of imperialist economic forces. (2) To compile historical and transformation data on twelve industrial sectors, seven commercial sectors, several typical enterprises, several capital groups, and representative figures of capitalists. (3) To compile three historical materials on national capital, bureaucratic capital, and imperialist capital in China. (4) To compile one hundred summaries of typical experiences in the socialist transformation of private industry and commerce.

### 总结工商业社会主义改造的经验　有关部门制定研究工作五年规划

1958-08-11
第5版()
专栏：学术动态

　　总结工商业社会主义改造的经验
　　有关部门制定研究工作五年规划
    我国对资本主义工商业进行社会主义改造，已经取得了伟大的胜利。总结这一改造工作的经验和对它进行理论研究，是科学研究工作中的重要任务。为了有计划地进行这一科学研究工作，中央工商行政管理局和科学院经济研究所等有关单位已经拟订了一个资本主义经济社会主义改造研究工作五年规划
  （草案）（1958—1962）。
    规划中规定了研究工作的基本任务是：整理中国资本主义和资产阶级发生、发展和灭亡的历史资料，并研究其变化过程；总结党和国家对资本主义工商业和对民族资产阶级分子社会主义改造的经验；进行资本主义经济过渡为社会主义经济的理论研究。为完成这一研究任务，将采取“结合实际、边干边学、集体协作、多快好省”的工作方针，使资料工作和研究工作为社会主义革命的现实服务。
    五年规划的主要项目是：（1）整理出私营工业、商业、运输业的社会主义改造和关于民族资产阶级以及肃清帝国主义经济势力等五部改造专题资料。（2）整理出十二个工业行业、七个商业行业、若干典型企业、若干资本集团和资本家代表人物的历史和改造资料。（3）整理出关于民族资本、官僚资本、帝国主义在华资本三部历史资料。
  （4）编辑私营工商业社会主义改造典型经验总结一百篇。
