Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Peace Fighters from Eleven Countries, Saying the Chinese Government and People Fully Support All Appeals and Declarations of the Disarmament and International Cooperation Conference
Author:
Date: 1958-08-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Vice Premier Chen Yi received forty-two peace fighters from eleven countries visiting China this evening. They are peace delegates attending the Stockholm "Disarmament and International Cooperation Conference" from: Argentina, Brazil, Burma, Ceylon, Chile, Colombia, Cuba, India, Indonesia, New Zealand, and Paraguay.

Vice Premier Chen Yi said during the reception that the Chinese government and people fully support all the appeals and declarations made by the Stockholm "Disarmament and International Cooperation Conference." He also expressed his admiration for the efforts made by the delegates at the conference. Vice Premier Chen Yi also hoped that the people of Asia, Africa, and Latin America would unite with peace-loving people all over the world to jointly oppose the crimes of US-British imperialism in invading the Middle East and defend world peace.

Also present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; and Zhao Puchu, Vice President of the Buddhist Association of China.

Peace activists from Japan, Korea, and New Zealand in Beijing were also present at the reception.

### 陈毅副总理接见十一个国家的和平战士时说  中国政府和人民完全支持裁军和国际合作大会的各项呼吁和宣言

1958-08-04
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见十一个国家的和平战士时说
    中国政府和人民完全支持裁军和
    国际合作大会的各项呼吁和宣言
    新华社3日讯　陈毅副总理今天晚间接见了十一个国家来我国访问的四十二位和平战士。他们是：阿根廷、巴西、缅甸、锡兰、智利、哥伦比亚、古巴、印度、印度尼西亚、新西兰、巴拉圭等国家出席斯德哥尔摩“裁军和国际合作大会”的和平代表。
    陈毅副总理在接见时说，中国政府和人民完全支持斯德哥尔摩“裁军和国际合作大会”所作出的各项呼吁和宣言。他并且对各位代表在大会上所作的努力表示钦佩。陈毅副总理还希望亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲各国人民同全世界爱好和平的人民团结起来，共同反对美英帝国主义侵略中东的罪行，保卫世界和平。
    接见时在座的，有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥，中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初等人。
    接见时，日本、朝鲜和新西兰在北京的和平人士也在座。
