Title: Afghan Deputy Prime Minister Naim to Visit China

Author: 
Date: 1958-08-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 20th: At the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China, Afghan Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Sardar Mohammed Naim will pay a friendly visit to China in mid-October.

### 阿富汗副首相纳伊姆将来我国访问

1958-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　阿富汗副首相纳伊姆将来我国访问
    新华社20日讯  阿富汗副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆应中华人民共和国周恩来总理的邀请，将于10月中旬来我国作友好访问。
