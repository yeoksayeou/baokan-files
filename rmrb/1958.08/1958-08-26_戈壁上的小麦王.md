Title: Wheat King of the Gobi Desert
Author:
Date: 1958-08-26
Page: 3
Rare News

    Wheat King of the Gobi Desert
    Production Team No. 1 of the Xiaye Village Third Farm in the Usu Reclamation Area of the Xinjiang Military Region Production and Construction Corps discovered a Wheat King on the edge of a winter wheat field planted in newly cultivated wasteland the previous year. A single grain of wheat produced 205 ears, each plump and strong, with full grains. On average, each ear contained 72 grains, totaling 14,760 grains. Based on a thousand-grain weight of 38 grams, this equates to 1.12 jin (approximately 1.12 pounds).

### 戈壁上的小麦王

1958-08-26
第3版()
专栏：珍闻

    戈壁上的小麦王
    新疆军区生产建设兵团乌苏垦区下野地第三农场生产一队，在头年开垦的荒地种植冬小麦的地边，发现一株小麦王。一粒小麦共发杈二百零五个穗，穗穗丰壮，粒粒饱满，平均每穗七十二粒，共有一万四千七百六十粒，按千粒重三十八克计算，折合一市斤零二两。
