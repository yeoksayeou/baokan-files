Title: Chairman Mao Meets with Prime Minister Sihanouk; Premier Zhou Hosts Informal Banquet to Welcome Distinguished Guests

Author:

Date: 1958-08-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. Chairman Mao met with Prime Minister Sihanouk, his daughter Princess Norodom Soriya Lounsay, and members of the Cambodian Royal National Delegation: Nhiek Tioulong, Kou Roun, Dy Touch, Touch Kim, Ching Hap, Phung Ma, and attendant Hong Kim Khoan at 10:30 tonight.

Present at the meeting were Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers He Long, Chen Yi, and Li Xiannian, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, and Peng Zhen.

Yang An, head of the Cambodian Royal Economic Delegation to China, and deputy head Nhay Valan Than were also present at the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. Premier Zhou Enlai received the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, Prince Norodom Sihanouk, and the members of the Cambodian Royal National Delegation led by him: Nhiek Tioulong, Kou Roun, Dy Touch, Touch Kim, Ching Hap, Phung Ma, attendant Hong Kim Khoan, and Prince Sihanouk's daughter, Princess Norodom Soriya Lounsay, this evening.

Accompanying the reception were Vice Premier He Long, Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wenjin, Director of the Protocol Department Yu Peiwen, and Head of the Chinese Economic Delegation to Cambodia Ye Jinghao.

Yang An, head of the Cambodian Economic Delegation to China, and deputy head Nhay Valan Than were also present at the reception.

After the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai hosted an informal banquet to welcome Prince Sihanouk, Nhiek Tioulong, Kou Roun, and Princess Norodom Soriya Lounsay. (Attached picture)

### 毛主席接见西哈努克首相　周总理设便宴为贵宾洗尘

1958-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席接见西哈努克首相
　　周总理设便宴为贵宾洗尘
    新华社15日讯　毛泽东主席今晚十时半接见了西哈努克首相和他的女儿诺罗敦·索丽娅·伦赛公主以及柬埔寨王国国家代表团团员：兰·涅特，古伦，丁源，笃金，清哈、冯马，随员洪金宽。
    接见时在座的有周恩来总理，贺龙、陈毅、李先念副总理，李济深、沈钧儒、黄炎培、彭真副委员长等。
    接见时，柬埔寨王国驻中国经济代表团团长杨安，副团长乃瓦朗丹也在座。
    新华社15日讯　周恩来总理今天晚间接见了柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和由他率领的柬埔寨王国国家代表团人员：兰·涅特、古伦、丁源、笃金·清哈、冯马，随员洪金宽以及西哈努克亲王的女儿诺罗敦·索丽娅·伦赛公主。
    陪同接见的有贺龙副总理，陈毅副总理和夫人，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部亚洲司司长章文晋，礼宾司司长俞沛文和中国驻柬埔寨经济代表团团长叶景灏。
    接见时，柬埔寨驻中国经济代表团团长杨安，副团长乃瓦朗丹也在座。
    接见后，周恩来总理设便宴为西哈努克亲王、兰·涅特、古伦以及诺罗敦·索丽娅·伦赛公主洗尘。（附图片）
