Title: Ammunition Depot Explosion in Brazilian Capital
Author:
Date: 1958-08-06
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Rio de Janeiro, 5th: An ammunition depot in Rio de Janeiro, the capital of Brazil, experienced a major explosion on the 2nd. The explosion began before dawn and continued for several hours.

Authorities estimate that the losses caused by the explosion may reach $135 million. Initial estimates suggest that approximately fifty people died in the explosion.

### 巴西首都军火库大爆炸

1958-08-06
第3版()
专栏：

    巴西首都军火库大爆炸
    新华社5日讯　里约热内卢消息：巴西首都里约热内卢的一个军火库在2日发生大爆炸。爆炸从黎明前开始一直延续了好几个钟头。
    据当局估计，这次爆炸造成的损失可能达到一亿三千五百万美元。初步估计有五十人左右在这次爆炸中死亡。
