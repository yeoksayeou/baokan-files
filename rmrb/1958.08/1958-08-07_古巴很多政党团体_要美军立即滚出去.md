Title: Many Cuban Political Parties and Groups Demand the Immediate Withdrawal of US Troops
Author:
Date: 1958-08-07
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 6th dispatch from New York, 5th dispatch from TASS: The "Americas Daily," published in Spanish in Miami, Florida, USA, carried a statement of protest from several major Cuban opposition organizations against the landing of American troops in Cuba.

    The statement "demands that the United States government immediately withdraw American troops from Cuban territory and stop taking interventionist actions to maintain the dictatorship." The statement expressed indignation at "the unprecedented, rude behavior of the United States in insulting the Cuban people."

    The statement calls on the people of Latin America and the United States, as well as all countries in the world, the United Nations General Assembly, and the Organization of American States to immediately discuss the situation in Cuba.

    The following organizations signed the statement: "July 26th Movement," "Revolutionary Party," "Revolutionary Directorate," "People's Party," "Democratic Party," "Federation of University Students," "National Workers' Organization," and "April 4th - 20th Military Organization," etc.

### 古巴很多政党团体  要美军立即滚出去

1958-08-07
第3版()
专栏：

    古巴很多政党团体
    要美军立即滚出去
    新华社6日讯　据塔斯社纽约5日讯：在美国佛罗里达州迈阿密地方用西班牙文出版的“美洲日报”刊载了古巴几个主要反对派组织抗议美国军队登陆古巴的声明。
    声明“要求美国政府立即从古巴领土上撤出美国军队，并且停止采取维护独裁的干涉行动”。声明对“美国采取前所未闻的侮辱古巴人民的粗暴行为”表示愤慨。
    声明呼吁拉丁美洲和美国的人民以及世界一切国家、联合国大会和美洲国家组织立刻讨论古巴的局势。
    在这个声明上签字的有：“七月二十六日运动”、“革命党”、“革命指导委员会”、“人民党”、“民主党”、“大学生联合会”、“全国工人组织”和“四月四日——二十日军事组织”等。
