Title: East-West Nuclear Experts Conference Unanimously Concludes: Nuclear Explosions Can Be Detected; US and UK's Excuse for Refusing to Stop Nuclear Testing is Removed
Author: Zhang Yang
Date: 1958-08-24
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 21st: Correspondent Zhang Yang reports: If violations of the agreement occur after the conclusion of an agreement to cease nuclear testing, they can be detected. This is the conclusion of the East-West nuclear experts conference which ended here today.

Four countries from the socialist camp and four countries from the imperialist camp attended the conference. They were the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Poland, and Romania, as well as the United States, the United Kingdom, France, and Canada.

On the final day of the conference, experts from the East and West adopted a "Final Report" for consideration by the governments concerned.

A communique issued after the meeting said that during the seven-week conference, experts exchanged views on various methods of detecting nuclear explosions. They concluded that: "Existing methods of detecting nuclear explosions, namely methods of collecting radioactive dust samples, recording seismic waves, sound waves and hydroacoustic waves, radio signal methods, and on-site investigations of suspicious unidentified events involving nuclear explosions, make it possible to detect and identify nuclear explosions within certain specific limits.

The communique went on to say that another conclusion of the meeting was: "It is technically possible to establish a supervisory system with certain capabilities and limitations that is feasible and effective for detecting violations of a possible worldwide agreement to cease nuclear weapons testing."

### 东西方核专家会议一致断定  核爆炸可以侦察出来  美英拒绝停止核试验借口被解除
张扬
1958-08-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　东西方核专家会议一致断定
    核爆炸可以侦察出来
    美英拒绝停止核试验借口被解除
    据新华社日内瓦21日电　记者张扬报道：在缔结停止核试验的协定之后，如果有违反协定的行为发生，那是可以被侦察出来的。这是今天在这里结束的东西方核专家会议的结论。
    社会主义阵营和帝国主义阵营各有四个国家出席了这次会议。它们是苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰和罗马尼亚以及美国、英国、法国和加拿大。
    在最后一天的会议上，来自东西方的专家们通过了一项“最后报告”，供有关各国政府考虑。
    会议结束后发表的公报说，在历时七星期的会议期间，专家们就侦察核爆炸的各种方法问题交换了意见。他们得出的结论是：“现有的各种侦察核爆炸的方法，即收集放射性微尘样品的方法，纪录地震波、声波和水声波的方法，无线电讯号方法以及对有核爆炸嫌疑的情况不明事件进行现场调查的方法，使得有可能在一定的具体范围内侦察和鉴定核爆炸。
    公报接着说，会议的另一个结论是：“建立一个具有一定的能力和限度而又可行和有效的监督体系，来侦察违反可能缔结的全世界停止核武器试验协定的行为，在技术上是可能办到的。”
