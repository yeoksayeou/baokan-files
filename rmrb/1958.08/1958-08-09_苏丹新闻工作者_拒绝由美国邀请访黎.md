Title: Sudanese Journalists Reject US Invitation to Visit Lebanon
Author: 
Date: 1958-08-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 8th: According to a report by the Middle East News Agency on the 7th, the Sudanese Journalists Union has stated its refusal to accept an invitation from the US Embassy in Sudan for Sudanese journalists to visit Beirut.

According to reports, the Union believes that the US issuing such an invitation is an infringement on the authority of the Lebanese government, as only the Lebanese government is qualified to issue such invitations.

### 苏丹新闻工作者  拒绝由美国邀请访黎

1958-08-09
第2版()
专栏：

    苏丹新闻工作者
    拒绝由美国邀请访黎
    新华社开罗8日电  据中东通讯社7日报道，苏丹新闻工作者联合会已经表示拒绝美国驻苏丹大使馆发出的要苏丹新闻工作者访问贝鲁特的邀请。
    据报道，联合会认为，美国发出这种邀请是侵犯了黎巴嫩政府的权力，因为只有黎巴嫩政府才有资格发出这种邀请。
