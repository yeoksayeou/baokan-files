Title: Cuban rebels attack in two provinces; dictator's troops flee in disarray

Author:

Date: 1958-08-11
Page: 6

　　Cuban rebels attack in two provinces
　　Dictator's troops flee in disarray
    Xinhua News Agency, New York, August 10th: United Press International's correspondent in Havana, Cuba, reported that Castro's rebel forces had launched a widespread offensive against government troops in Oriente Province and had "achieved great victories." The troops of Cuban dictator Batista were retreating in disarray.
    The rebel radio broadcast said that a government battalion of 600 men that had been surrounded in the Estrada Palma district a few days earlier "had ceased resistance and had begun to flee." The radio called on officers and soldiers of the government army to defect to the rebel side.
    Xinhua News Agency, Havana, August 10th: Cuban rebel forces are constantly attacking the troops of the Batista dictatorial government. According to a rebel radio report on the 7th, in a battle at Cuatro Caminos in Las Villas Province in central Cuba, the rebel forces killed forty government soldiers.
    On the Sancti Spíritus-Trinidad highway, the rebel forces wiped out a patrol of twenty-five government soldiers.

### 古巴起义军在两个省出击　独裁者的军队纷纷溃逃

1958-08-11
第6版()
专栏：

　　古巴起义军在两个省出击
　　独裁者的军队纷纷溃逃
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社纽约10日讯：合众国际社驻古巴哈瓦郡记者报道，卡斯特罗的起义部队开始在奥连特省向政府军展开广泛的进攻，并且“取得了很大的胜利”。古巴独裁者巴蒂斯塔的军队纷纷退却。
    起义部队的电台广播说，几天以前在埃斯特拉达帕尔马区受到包围的政府军一个营六百人“已经停止抵抗，并且开始逃窜”。这个电台号召政府军的官兵投到起义部队这边来。
    新华社10日讯　哈瓦那消息：古巴起义军正在不断地打击巴蒂斯塔独裁政府的军队。据起义军电台7日报道，在古巴中部拉斯维利亚斯省库瓦德马西耳的一次战斗中，起义军打死了四十名政府军。
    在圣克的斯彼利托—特立尼达公路上，起义军消灭了二十五名政府军的巡逻兵。
