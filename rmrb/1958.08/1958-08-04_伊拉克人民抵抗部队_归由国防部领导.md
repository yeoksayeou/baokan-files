Title: Iraqi People's Resistance Forces Placed Under Leadership of the Ministry of Defense
Author:
Date: 1958-08-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, August 2nd: According to a Damascus radio broadcast, a decree concerning the People's Resistance Forces was issued today in Baghdad, stipulating that the organization of the People's Resistance Forces in Iraq, composed of male and female volunteers from Iraq and other Arab countries, is under the jurisdiction of the Ministry of Defense.

The decree also stipulates that the responsibility of the People's Resistance Forces is to train citizens, upon the orders of the army's command, to assist the regular forces in civil defense and to maintain internal security in order to defend the country.

### 伊拉克人民抵抗部队  归由国防部领导

1958-08-04
第2版()
专栏：

    伊拉克人民抵抗部队
    归由国防部领导
    据新华社大马士革2日电　据大马士革电台广播，今天在巴格达颁布的一项关于人民抵抗部队的法令规定，伊拉克的和来自其他阿拉伯国家的男女志愿人员组成的人民抵抗部队组织都隶属于国防部。
    这项法令还规定，人民抵抗部队的责任是依据军队统帅部的命令训练国民协助正规部队进行民防和维护内部安全以保卫国家。
