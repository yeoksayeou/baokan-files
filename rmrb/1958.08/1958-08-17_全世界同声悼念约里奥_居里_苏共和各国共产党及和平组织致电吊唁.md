Title: Worldwide Condolences for Joliot-Curie; Soviet Communist Party, Various Communist Parties, and Peace Organizations Send Condolences

Author:
Date: 1958-08-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the French Communist Party, offering condolences on the death of Joliot-Curie. The telegram stated that Joliot-Curie's life was full of vibrant and multifaceted activity. His life set a shining example of a communist fighter who bravely and tirelessly struggled for the cause of the French working people, and a brilliant image of an outstanding scientist who dedicated his extensive knowledge and talent to the benefit of mankind and to the cause of peace and friendship among all peoples. The CPSU Central Committee said that the Soviet people will always deeply cherish the memory of Frederic Joliot-Curie, a loyal friend of the Soviet Union, who tirelessly fought to strengthen the friendship between the Soviet and French peoples.

Xinhua News Agency, August 16th Dispatch from Paris: Maurice Thorez, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party, wrote a letter on the 15th to Joliot-Curie's daughter, Hélène Langevin, and son, Pierre Joliot-Curie, to express his condolences on the death of their father. Thorez said in the letter: "Joliot-Curie was an outstanding scholar, a passionate patriot, a leader of the world peace movement, and at the same time, a loyal fighter for the cause of communism. He was a simple and kind man, the pride of our party and the glory of France."

The Central Committee of the French Communist Party has been continuously receiving a large number of condolence telegrams from fraternal parties around the world, as well as from people's organizations such as youth, women, and trade unions.

First Secretary of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, János Kádár, the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, and the Swiss Party of Labour, successively sent telegrams to the Central Committee of the French Communist Party to express their condolences on the death of Joliot-Curie.

Xinhua News Agency, August 16th Dispatch from Paris: Peace organizations and prominent figures from various countries deeply mourn the death of the famous French scientist and outstanding fighter for the world peace movement, Joliot-Curie. The Soviet Peace Committee, the British Peace Committee, the German Peace Committee, the West German Peace Committee, the Swiss Peace Committee, and the Indonesian Peace Committee respectively sent telegrams to the World Peace Council and the French National Peace Council to express their condolences on the death of Joliot-Curie. Condolence telegrams from people of various countries also continue to arrive from every corner of the world.

### 全世界同声悼念约里奥—居里　苏共和各国共产党及和平组织致电吊唁

1958-08-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　全世界同声悼念约里奥—居里
　　苏共和各国共产党及和平组织致电吊唁
    据新华社讯　苏联共产党中央委员会打电报给法国共产党中央委员会，吊唁约里奥—居里的逝世。电报说，约里奥—居里的一生充满了沸腾的、多方面的活动。他的一生树立了一个共产主义战士为了法国劳动人民的事业而英勇不息地奋斗的光辉榜样，树立了一个杰出的科学家为了造福人类和为和平和各国人民的友谊的事业贡献出自己广博的知识和才能的光辉形象。苏共中央说，苏联人民将永远深深怀念苏联的忠实朋友、为加强法苏两国人民的友谊不倦奋斗的弗雷德里克·约里奥—居里。
    据新华社16日讯　巴黎消息：法国共产党中央委员会总书记多列士15日写信给约里奥—居里的女儿海伦娜·郎之万和儿子皮埃尔·约里奥—居里，吊唁他们父亲的逝世。多列士在信里说：“约里奥—居里是卓越的学者、热情的爱国者、世界保卫和平运动的领导人，同时又是为共产主义事业奋斗的忠实战士。他是一个质朴而善良的人，是我们党的骄傲和法兰西的光荣。”
    法国共产党中央委员会不断收到各国兄弟党以及青年、妇女和工会等人民团体发来的大批唁电。
    匈牙利社会主义工人党第一书记卡达尔、德国统一社会党中央委员会和瑞士劳动党，先后打电报给法国共产党中央委员会，对约里奥—居里的逝世表示吊唁。
    据新华社16日讯　巴黎消息：各国和平组织和各国著名人士对法国著名科学家、世界和平运动的杰出战士约里奥—居里的逝世深表哀悼。苏联保卫和平委员会、英国和平委员会、德国和平委员会、西德和平委员会、瑞士和平委员会和印度尼西亚和平委员会分别打电报给世界和平理事会和法国全国和平理事会对约里奥—居里的逝世表示哀悼。各国人士的唁电也不断从世界各个角落飞来。
