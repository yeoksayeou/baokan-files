Title: Do Not Interfere in Chinese Affairs: Argentine Newspaper Condemns American Aggressors
Author:
Date: 1958-08-31
Page: 3

　　Do Not Interfere in Chinese Affairs
    Argentine Newspaper Condemns American Aggressors
    Xinhua News Agency, Buenos Aires, August 29th: "Our Word Weekly" severely criticized the provocative actions of the United States and its puppet Chiang Kai-shek against the People's Republic of China. The newspaper pointed out that the so-called exercises of the US Seventh Fleet off the coast of the People's Republic of China are nothing more than a threat and "an impudent interference in the affairs of the Chinese people."

### 不许干涉中国事务  阿根廷报纸斥美国侵略者

1958-08-31
第3版()
专栏：

　　不许干涉中国事务
    阿根廷报纸斥美国侵略者
    新华社30日讯　塔斯社布宜诺斯艾利斯29日讯：“我们的话周报”严厉抨击美国及其傀儡蒋介石对中华人民共和国的挑衅行为。报纸指出，所谓的美国第七舰队在中华人民共和国海岸的演习，不外是一种威胁和“对中国人民事务的无礼的干涉”。
