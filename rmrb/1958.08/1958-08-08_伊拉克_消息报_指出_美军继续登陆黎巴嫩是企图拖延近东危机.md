Title: Iraqi "Al-Akhbar" Points Out: Continued Landing of U.S. Troops in Lebanon is an Attempt to Delay the Near East Crisis
Author:
Date: 1958-08-08
Page: 2

    Iraqi "Al-Akhbar" Points Out
    Continued Landing of U.S. Troops in Lebanon
    Is an Attempt to Delay the Near East Crisis
    Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, July 6th: TASS Baghdad reported on the 6th that the Iraqi newspaper "Al-Akhbar" reported that U.S. troops are continuing to land in Lebanon. The newspaper emphasized that the U.S. action is to delay the Near East crisis and continue the occupation of Lebanon.
    The newspaper wrote that the U.S. aggression in the Near East confirms Khrushchev's accusation that the U.S. is obstructing the peaceful resolution of the Near East crisis through negotiations and deliberately delaying the start of negotiations to strengthen its military position in Lebanon.

### 伊拉克“消息报”指出  美军继续登陆黎巴嫩是企图拖延近东危机

1958-08-08
第2版()
专栏：

    伊拉克“消息报”指出
    美军继续登陆黎巴嫩
    是企图拖延近东危机
    据新华社7日讯  塔斯社巴格达6日讯：伊拉克“消息报”报道，美军继续在黎巴嫩登陆。报纸强调指出，美国的行动是要拖延近东危机和继续占领黎巴嫩。
    报纸写道，美国在近东的侵略活动证实了赫鲁晓夫提出的指责，就是美国阻挠通过谈判来和平解决近东危机，并且故意拖延谈判的开始来加强它在黎巴嫩的军事阵地。
