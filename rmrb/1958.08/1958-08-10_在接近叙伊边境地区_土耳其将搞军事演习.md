Title: Turkey to Conduct Military Exercises Near Syrian-Iraqi Border

Author:
Date: 1958-08-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 9th August: According to reports in the newspapers here today, Turkish troops massed on the Turkish border with Syria and Iraq will soon hold large-scale military exercises lasting a week.
    The Turkish army, navy and air force will all participate in these exercises.

### 在接近叙伊边境地区  土耳其将搞军事演习

1958-08-10
第2版()
专栏：

    在接近叙伊边境地区土耳其将搞军事演习
    新华社大马士革9日电　据这里的报纸今天报道，在土耳其接壤叙利亚和伊拉克的边境集结的土耳其军队将在最近举行为时一周的大规模军事演习。
    土耳其的海、陆、空三军都将参加这次演习。
