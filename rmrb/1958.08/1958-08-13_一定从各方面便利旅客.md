Title: Definitely Provide Convenience to Passengers in All Aspects
Author:
Date: 1958-08-13
Page: 3

　　On July 8th, the People's Daily published Comrade Wang Wenfan's letter "Why Not Open More Doors?", which contained completely correct criticism of us. Opening only one door after the train arrives at a station was a "three-close, one-open" work system we implemented a long time ago, unilaterally to ensure the safety of passengers getting on and off the train.

　　Later, we discovered that this system was very inconvenient for passengers getting on and off the train, and we announced the abolition of this system from April last year. However, some train attendants did not conscientiously implement it and still often opened only one door.

　　After the Party newspaper made this criticism, in addition to educating the relevant train attendants, helping them improve their understanding, and giving them severe punishments, we also demanded that all crew members better establish the viewpoint of serving the people and, in their work, provide convenience to passengers in all aspects.

### 一定从各方面便利旅客

1958-08-13
第3版()
专栏：

　  一定从各方面便利旅客
    7月8日人民日报发表了王文范同志的“为什么不多开车门”的来信，对我们的批评是完全正确的。火车到站后开一个车门，是我们很久以前片面地为照顾旅客上下车安全而实行的“三关一开”的工作制度。
    后来，我们发现这个制度对旅客上下车很不方便，已经从去年4月份起宣布废除这一制度。但是，有的列车员没有认真执行，还经常只开一个车门。
    这次党报提出批评以后，我们除对有关列车员进行教育，帮助他提高认识并给予严厉处分外，并且要求全体乘务员更好地树立为人民服务的观点，在工作中从各方面便利旅客。
                      大同车务段
