Title: No warmongers allowed to run wild; "Democratic Korea" calls for the US to immediately get out of Taiwan and South Korea
Author:
Date: 1958-08-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 29th: The "Democratic Korea" newspaper published a commentary today entitled "The Death Struggle of Warmongers," denouncing the US warmongers' provocative actions in the Taiwan Strait, which are intensifying the tense situation and seriously threatening China's security and peace in the Far East.

The commentary pointed out that for the sake of peace and security in the Far East, US imperialism must immediately cease its provocative actions in the Taiwan Strait, immediately get out of Taiwan and South Korea, and the legitimate status of the People's Republic of China in the United Nations should be restored immediately. This is the unanimous demand of peace-loving people all over the world.

The commentary said that everyone knows that the US armed aggression against China's territory of Taiwan is the main cause of the tense situation in the Taiwan Strait and even the Far East.

### 不许战争狂人胡作非为  “民主朝鲜报”要美国立即滚出台湾和南朝鲜

1958-08-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　不许战争狂人胡作非为
    “民主朝鲜报”要美国立即滚出台湾和南朝鲜
    新华社平壤29日电　“民主朝鲜报”今天发表了题为“战争狂人的垂死挣扎”的评论，痛斥美国战争狂人在台湾海峡加剧紧张局势，严重威胁中国的安全和远东和平的挑衅行为。
    评论指出，为了远东和平和安全，美帝国主义必须立即停止在台湾海峡的挑衅行为，立即从台湾和南朝鲜滚出去，中华人民共和国在联合国的合法地位应该立即恢复，这是全世界爱好和平人民的一致要求。
    评论说，任何人都知道，美国武装侵略中国领土台湾是造成台湾海峡以至远东地区紧张局势的主要原因。
