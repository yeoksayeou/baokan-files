Title: Sino-North Korean Talks on Economic Cooperation Issues; North Korean Delegation Visits Beijing Industrial Leap Forward Exhibition

Author: 
Date: 1958-08-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The head of the government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Li Joo-yeon, and all the distinguished guests of the delegation, accompanied by Qiao Xiaoguang and others, today visited the Beijing Industrial Leap Forward Exhibition held at the Working People's Cultural Palace. The distinguished guests showed great interest in the various exhibits, visiting from 3:00 PM to 8:00 PM.

Today, Li Jong-ok, member of the Korean government delegation and Chairman of the State Planning Commission, and Kim Choi-sun, Vice Minister of Internal and External Trade, also began talks with Ye Jizhuang, Liu Mingfu, Zhang Youxuan, Lin Haiyun, and Qiao Xiaoguang on further strengthening economic cooperation between China and North Korea.

### 中朝会谈经济协作问题  朝鲜代表团参观北京工业跃进展览会

1958-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中朝会谈经济协作问题
    朝鲜代表团参观北京工业跃进展览会
    新华社4日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团团长李周渊和代表团的全体贵宾，今天由乔晓光等陪同，参观了设在劳动人民文化宫内的北京市工业跃进展览会。贵宾们对展览会的各项展览品感到很大兴趣，从三时开始一直参观到八时。
    今天，朝鲜政府代表团团员、国家计划委员会委员长李锺玉、对内外商业省副相金最善，还同叶季壮、刘明夫、张有萱、林海云、乔晓光，就进一步加强中、朝两国经济协作问题开始进行了会谈。
