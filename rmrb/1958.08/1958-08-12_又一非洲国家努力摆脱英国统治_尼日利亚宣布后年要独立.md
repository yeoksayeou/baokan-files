Title: Another African Nation Striving to Break Free from British Rule: Nigeria Announces Independence in Two Years
Author: 
Date: 1958-08-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 11th, Lagos dispatch: The Nigerian government passed a resolution on August 5th, announcing that it would achieve independence on April 2, 1960.

    Nigerian Prime Minister Balewa called on political figures to treat the movement for independence as a "national issue."

    Nigeria, located in West Africa, is currently the largest remaining British colony, with an area of 966,700 square kilometers and a population of 33,368,000. It is rich in cocoa, cotton, and rubber, and has abundant mineral resources. Since Britain turned Nigeria into its colony in 1861, it has been carrying out cruel exploitation and plunder of the local residents and their natural wealth.

    Since Ghana declared its independence last spring, the Nigerian people's movement for independence has seen significant development. In this situation, British Colonial Secretary Lennox-Boyd pretended to "sympathetically consider" the Nigerian people's demands for independence, but he refused to set a specific date for the realization of Nigerian independence.

### 又一非洲国家努力摆脱英国统治　尼日利亚宣布后年要独立

1958-08-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　又一非洲国家努力摆脱英国统治
　　尼日利亚宣布后年要独立
    新华社11日讯　拉各斯消息：尼日利亚政府在8月5日通过一项决议，宣布要在1960年4月2日实现独立。
    尼日利亚总理巴勒瓦号召政界人士把争取独立的运动作为一个“全民问题”来进行。
    尼日利亚在非洲西部，现在是英国所剩下的殖民地中最大的一块，面积有九六六，七○○平方公里，人口有三三，三六八，○○○人，盛产可可、棉花和橡胶，矿产也很丰富。英国人从1861年把尼日利亚变为它的殖民地以后，一直在那里对当地居民和他们的天然财富进行着残酷的剥削和掠夺。
    在去年春天加纳宣布独立以后，尼日利亚人民争取独立的运动有了显著的发展。在这种情况下，英国殖民大臣伦诺克斯—波伊德曾佯言要
  “同情地考虑”尼日利亚人民争取独立的要求，但他拒绝规定实现尼日利亚独立的确定日期。
