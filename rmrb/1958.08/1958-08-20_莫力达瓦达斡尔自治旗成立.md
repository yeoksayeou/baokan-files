Title: Morin Dawa Daur Autonomous Banner Established
Author:
Date: 1958-08-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, August 19th: The third ethnic autonomous banner in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region – the Morin Dawa Daur Autonomous Banner – was formally established on August 15th. On the 17th, all the delegates to the first session of the first People's Congress of the Autonomous Region, guests, and people of all ethnic groups, held a celebration meeting in Neilji Town, the location of the banner's People's Committee, to celebrate the establishment of the Autonomous Banner.

A delegation jointly formed by the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the People's Committee of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, the Hulunbuir League Committee of the CCP, and the Hulunbuir League Commissioner's Office, as well as friendly neighboring areas such as the Meris Daur Autonomous Region, Nenjiang County, Nehe County, Fuyu County, and Gannan County of Qiqihar City, Heilongjiang Province, and delegations sent by the thirteen banners and counties of Hulunbuir League, all came to congratulate the meeting.

At the first session of the first People's Congress of the Autonomous Banner, the representatives elected the Autonomous Region People's Committee, consisting of nineteen people. Batu Bayar (Daur ethnicity) was elected as the Banner Head, Cui Xixian (Han ethnicity) and Tu Rong (Ewenki ethnicity) as Deputy Banner Heads, and Boyan (Daur ethnicity) as the President of the Banner People's Court.

### 莫力达瓦达斡尔自治旗成立

1958-08-20
第6版()
专栏：

　　莫力达瓦达斡尔自治旗成立
    据新华社呼和浩特19日电  内蒙古自治区第三个民族自治旗——莫力达瓦达斡尔自治旗在8月15日正式成立。17日，自治区第一届人民代表大会第一次会议全体代表、来宾和各族人民群众，在旗人民委员会所在地——尼尔基镇，举行了庆祝自治旗成立大会。
    中国共产党内蒙古自治区委员会、内蒙古自治区人民委员会、中共呼伦贝尔盟委员会、呼伦贝尔盟公署联合组成的代表团，黑龙江省齐齐哈尔市梅里斯达斡尔自治区、嫩江县、讷河县、富裕县、甘南县等友邻地区，和呼伦贝尔盟十三个旗、县派出的代表团，都前来向大会祝贺。
    在自治旗第一届人民代表大会第一次会议上，代表们选出了由十九人组成的自治区人民委员会，巴图巴雅尔（达斡尔族）当选为旗长，崔希贤（汉族）、涂荣（鄂温克族）为副旗长，博彦（达斡尔族）为旗人民法院院长。
