Title: Sihanouk Visits Rangoon, Held Talks with Nehru in New Delhi
Author:
Date: 1958-08-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 12th: Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, and his party left here this morning for a visit to Burma.

Those who saw him off at the airport included Indian Prime Minister Nehru, Defense Minister Menon and other cabinet ministers, as well as diplomatic envoys to India from various countries, including Chinese Ambassador Pan Zili.

Before his departure, Sihanouk received Ambassador Pan Zili.

This morning, Sihanouk announced at a press conference that the Kingdom of Cambodia had decided to recognize the Republic of Iraq. He revealed that the request for recognition was made to him last night by the Iraqi Ambassador to India.

Sihanouk pointed out that South Vietnamese troops had illegally occupied some villages on the Cambodian border, and the Cambodian government had requested the withdrawal of South Vietnamese troops. He is prepared to go to Saigon personally to negotiate this issue with Ngo Dinh Diem. He also said that there were border disputes with Thailand. He hopes to resolve this issue in a way that is satisfactory to both sides.

Sihanouk said that he had invited Nehru on behalf of King Suramarit of Cambodia to pay an official visit to Cambodia.

Sihanouk will stay in Calcutta for one day before leaving India for Rangoon.

Prime Minister Sihanouk held talks with Prime Minister Nehru on the evening of the 11th for one hour and forty-five minutes, discussing international issues facing Cambodia and other issues related to the development of the international situation.

Prime Minister Nehru hosted a banquet in honor of Prime Minister Sihanouk. The two sides continued their talks at and after the banquet.

### 西哈努克前往仰光访问　在新德里曾同尼赫鲁会谈

1958-08-13
第6版()
专栏：

　　西哈努克前往仰光访问
　　在新德里曾同尼赫鲁会谈
    新华社新德里12日电　柬埔寨王国首相西哈努克亲王及其一行今天上午离开这里，前往缅甸访问。
    到机场送行的有印度总理尼赫鲁、国防部长梅农和其他内阁部长们，以及包括中国大使潘自力在内的各国驻印度的外交使节。
    西哈努克在动身以前，接见了潘自力大使。
    今天上午，西哈努克在记者招待会上宣布，柬埔寨王国已经决定承认伊拉克共和国。他透露，希望承认的请求是伊拉克共和国驻印度大使昨晚向他提出的。
    西哈努克指出，南越军队非法占领了柬埔寨边境的一些村庄，柬埔寨政府已经要求南越军队撤退。他准备亲自到西贡去同吴庭艳谈判这个问题。他还说，同泰国也有边境纠纷。他希望在双方都感到满意的情况下来解决这一问题。
    西哈努克说，他已经代表柬埔寨国王苏拉玛里特邀请尼赫鲁正式访问柬埔寨。
    西哈努克将在加尔各答停留一天，然后离开印度前往仰光。
    西哈努克首相11日晚上同尼赫鲁总理举行了一小时四十五分钟的会谈，讨论了柬埔寨面临的国际问题和其它有关国际局势发展的问题。
    尼赫鲁总理设宴招待了西哈努克首相。双方在宴会上和宴会后继续进行了会谈。
