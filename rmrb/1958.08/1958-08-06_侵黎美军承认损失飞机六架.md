Title: U.S. Invasion Troops in Lebanon Admit to Losing Six Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1958-08-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, August 5th. The U.S. invasion troops in Lebanon headquarters issued a press release on the 4th admitting that since the U.S. invading forces entered Lebanon, the U.S. Sixth Fleet has lost six aircraft. It was announced that five of these aircraft, including jet fighters and helicopters, were lost during "normal operations at sea."
    
    It is also reported that a reconnaissance plane of the United Nations observer group crashed on the border between Lebanon and Syria.

### 侵黎美军承认损失飞机六架

1958-08-06
第4版()
专栏：

    侵黎美军承认损失飞机六架
    新华社5日讯　贝鲁特消息：侵黎美军司令部4日发表新闻公报承认，在美国侵略军侵入黎巴嫩以后，美国第六舰队已经损失了六架飞机。据宣布，其中有五架飞机，包括喷气式战斗机和直升飞机，是在“海上的正常活动中”损失的。
    另据报道，有一架联合国观察团的侦察机在黎巴嫩和叙利亚的边界上坠毁。
