Title: Yang Guocai Breaks 100-meter Sidestroke Record
Author:
Date: 1958-08-07
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, 5th -- Today morning, at the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Water Sports Meet, Yang Guocai, a member of the Wuzhou City team, broke the national record of one minute and thirteen seconds and seven tenths in the men's 100-meter sidestroke final with a time of one minute, twelve seconds and one tenth.

Yang Guocai just graduated from senior primary school this year. He won first place in the autonomous region's sidestroke competition last year.

### 杨国材破百公尺侧泳纪录

1958-08-07
第7版()
专栏：

    杨国材破百公尺侧泳纪录
    新华社南宁5日电　今天上午，在广西僮族自治区水上运动大会上，梧州市代表队队员杨国材在男子一百公尺侧泳决赛中，以一分十二秒一的成绩打破了一分十三秒七的全国纪录。
    杨国材今年刚从高小毕业，他在去年曾获得自治区侧泳比赛第一名。
