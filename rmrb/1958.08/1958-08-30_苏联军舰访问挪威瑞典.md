Title: Soviet Warships Visit Norway and Sweden
Author:
Date: 1958-08-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Murmansk, August 28th: According to TASS, the Soviet Northern Fleet's cruiser "October Revolution" and destroyer "Brave," under the command of Admiral Lobov, set sail on the morning of the 27th for friendly visits to Bergen, Norway, and Stockholm, the capital of Sweden.

### 苏联军舰访问挪威瑞典

1958-08-30
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏联军舰访问挪威瑞典
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社摩尔曼斯克讯：苏联北海舰队的“十月革命号”巡洋舰和“勇敢号”驱逐舰，27日早上在洛波夫海军上将率领下，启程前往挪威的卑尔根港和瑞典首都斯德哥尔摩作友好访问。
