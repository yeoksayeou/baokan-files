Title: British Occupation Forces in Jordan Commanded by American General Invading Lebanon
Author:
Date: 1958-08-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 4th: The "Rose al-Yusuf" weekly reported today from Damascus that the United States and Britain have now established a joint command for their occupation forces in the Middle East.

The report revealed that the British occupation forces in Jordan are receiving orders from the headquarters of the American occupation forces in Lebanon. The troops of both countries are commanded by American General Adams, who originally commanded the American occupation forces in Lebanon.

The report said that Adams is assisted in his headquarters by a committee composed of fifteen officers.

### 英在约旦占领军  由侵黎美国将军指挥

1958-08-06
第4版()
专栏：

    英在约旦占领军
    由侵黎美国将军指挥
    新华社开罗4日电　“鲁兹·优素福”周刊今天从大马士革报道，美国和英国现在为它们在中东的占领军设立了联合司令部。
    消息透露，在约旦的英国占领军接受在黎巴嫩的美国占领军总部的命令。这两国军队都由美国将军亚当斯指挥，他本来指挥在黎巴嫩的美国占领军。
    消息说，亚当斯在他的司令部里得到由十五名军官组成的一个委员会的协助。
