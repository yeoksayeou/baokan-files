Title: Water Music Boat and Television
Author:
Date: 1958-08-10
Page: 4

See, Play, and Listen

    Water Music Boat and Television
    Tonight in Beihai Park, there will be a water music boat in the center of the lake, with a youth folk art troupe performing a crosstalk show on the boat. Those who rent yachts can watch at will.
    At 7:30 p.m., television will be shown at Yong'an Temple in Beihai Park. Tickets are five fen each.

### 水上音乐船和电视

1958-08-10
第4版()
专栏：看看玩玩听听

    水上音乐船和电视
    北海公园今晚在海中心有水上音乐船，船上由青年曲艺队演出相声大会。租有游艇者可以随意去观看。
    晚七时半，在北海公园内永安寺放映电视，票价每张五分。
