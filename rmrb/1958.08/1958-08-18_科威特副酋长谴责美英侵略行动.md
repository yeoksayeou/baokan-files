Title: Kuwaiti Deputy Emir Condemns US-UK Aggression

Author: 
Date: 1958-08-18
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 17th: The Deputy Emir of Kuwait, Sabah, who is currently visiting Cairo, told a "People's Daily" reporter today that he strongly condemned the occupation of Lebanon and Jordan by US and British troops, which he considers a violation of the United Nations Charter.

　　He said: "Regardless of what excuses are used to justify the occupation of Lebanon and Jordan, the fact is that this action is condemned by Arab countries and all countries and peoples who hope to live in a world that does not rely on force as a political principle."

　　Kuwaiti Deputy Emir Abdullah Mubarak Al-Sabah said in Cairo on August 16 that Kuwait will "mobilize all its potential and strength" to serve the cause of Arab nationalism and the establishment of a more unified Arab world. He made this statement when referring to Eisenhower's six-point Middle East plan.

　　He also said: Eisenhower's "Near East Peace Plan," apart from offering economic assistance, is not substantially different from previous plans.

　　Al-Sabah arrived in Cairo on August 13.

### 科威特副酋长谴责美英侵略行动

1958-08-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　科威特副酋长谴责美英侵略行动
    新华社开罗17日电　正在访问开罗的科威特副酋长萨卜巴今天对“人民报”记者说，他强烈地谴责美英军队占领黎巴嫩和约旦，他认为这是违反联合国宪章的。
    他说：“不管用什么借口来为占领黎巴嫩和约旦辩解，事实是，这种行动受到阿拉伯国家以及希望在不依靠武力为政治准则的世界上生活的所有国家和人民的谴责。”
    科威特副酋长阿卜杜拉·穆巴拉克·萨卜巴16日在开罗说，科威特将“动员它一切的潜能和力量”来为阿拉伯民族主义的事业和建立一个更加团结的阿拉伯世界效劳。他是在谈到艾森豪威尔的中东六点计划时发表这一意见的。
    他还说：艾森豪威尔的“近东和平计划”除了提出经济援助之外，同以往的一些计划在实质上没有什么两样。
    萨卜巴是8月13日到开罗的。
