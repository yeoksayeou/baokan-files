Title: Storms Cause Disaster in Large Areas of the United States
Author:
Date: 1958-08-03
Page: 3

Brief News

    Storms Cause Disaster in Large Areas of the United States
    Kansas City Dispatch: Violent storms struck large areas of the United States, including the states of Kansas, Missouri, Indiana, and Ohio on July 31st, causing widespread flooding in many places.
    In Kansas, floods submerged thousands of acres of land near Kansas City, displacing hundreds of families. (Xinhua News Agency Dispatch)

### 美广大地区暴风雨成灾

1958-08-03
第3版()
专栏：简讯

    美广大地区暴风雨成灾
    堪萨斯城讯：7月31日猛烈的暴风雨袭击了美国的堪萨斯州、密苏里州、印第安纳州和俄亥俄州等广大地区，很多地方洪水成灾。
    在堪萨斯州，洪水吞没了堪萨斯城附近的数千英亩土地，成百的家庭流离失所。　（据新华社讯）
