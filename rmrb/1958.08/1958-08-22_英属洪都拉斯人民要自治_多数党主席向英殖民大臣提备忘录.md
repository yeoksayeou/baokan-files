Title: People of British Honduras Want Autonomy; Chairman of Majority Party Submits Memorandum to British Colonial Secretary
Author:
Date: 1958-08-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, August 21st, Paliz Dispatch: George Price, Chairman of the People's United Party, the majority party in British Honduras, has issued a memorandum to British Colonial Secretary Alan Lennox-Boyd, requesting that Britain grant the people of Honduras the rights to autonomy and self-determination, so that the people of Honduras can decide their own future and the future of the country. The memorandum stated, "We and only we are the legitimate masters of this country."

    The memorandum will be delivered to Vickers, the British Acting Governor of Honduras, on the evening of the 22nd. The People's United Party also plans to launch a mass demonstration demanding autonomy at that time.

### 英属洪都拉斯人民要自治  多数党主席向英殖民大臣提备忘录

1958-08-22
第5版()
专栏：

　　英属洪都拉斯人民要自治
    多数党主席向英殖民大臣提备忘录
　　新华社21日讯  帕利兹消息：英属洪都拉斯的多数党人民联合党的主席乔治·普赖斯发表了一份给英国殖民大臣艾伦·伦诺斯克—波伊德的备忘录，要求英国让洪都拉斯人民取得自治和自决的权利，使洪都拉斯人民可以决定自己的前途和国家的前途。备忘录说，“我们而且只有我们才是这个国家的合法的主人”。
    这个备忘录将于22日晚送给洪都拉斯的英国代理总督维克斯。人民联合党还计划在那个时候发动一个要求自治的群众示威。
