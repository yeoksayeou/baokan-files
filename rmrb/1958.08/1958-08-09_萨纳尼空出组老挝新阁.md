Title: Sananikone Asked to Form New Laotian Cabinet

Author: 
Date: 1958-08-09
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 8th. According to a Vientiane Radio broadcast: Phoui Sananikone was designated on August 8th to form a new Laotian cabinet.
    Phoui Sananikone is the Vice-Chairman of the Lao People's Rally and the Foreign Minister of the resigned Souvanna Phouma government. This time, after Prince Souvanna Phouma's attempt to form a cabinet failed, Prince Savang, representing the King of Laos, designated him to form the cabinet.
    Phoui Sananikone has indicated that he is prepared to formally decide whether to accept the task of forming a cabinet after seeking the opinion of the Lao People's Rally.

### 萨纳尼空出组老挝新阁

1958-08-09
第2版()
专栏：

    萨纳尼空出组老挝新阁
    新华社河内8日电  据万象电台广播：冯·萨纳尼空已经在8日被指定出来组织老挝新内阁。
    冯·萨纳尼空是老挝人联合党副主席、已辞职的富马政府的外交部长。这次是在富马亲王组织内阁宣告失败后由萨旺皇子代表老挝国王指定他出来组阁的。
    冯·萨纳尼空表示准备在征求老挝人联合党的意见之后再正式确定是否接受组阁任务。
