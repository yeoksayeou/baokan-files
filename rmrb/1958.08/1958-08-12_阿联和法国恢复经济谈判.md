Title: UAR and France Resume Economic Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1958-08-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 10th: An official from the Ministry of Finance revealed on the 9th that representatives from the United Arab Republic and France have resumed negotiations in Geneva regarding economic issues between the two countries.

### 阿联和法国恢复经济谈判

1958-08-12
第6版()
专栏：

    阿联和法国恢复经济谈判
    据新华社开罗10日电　财政部一位人士9日透露，阿拉伯联合共和国和法国的代表已经在日内瓦恢复讨论有关两国的经济问题的谈判。
