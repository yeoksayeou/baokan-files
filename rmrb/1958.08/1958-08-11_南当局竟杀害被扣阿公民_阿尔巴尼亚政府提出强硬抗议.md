Title: Southern Authorities Actually Murder Detained Albanian Citizen; Albanian Government Lodges Strong Protest
Author:
Date: 1958-08-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, August 9th: Just as the Albanian government lodged another serious protest on August 6th regarding the kidnapping of Albanian citizen Hasan Spata in the Yugoslav capital, the Yugoslav authorities secretly murdered Spata on August 7th. In response, the Albanian Ministry of Foreign Affairs issued a third note to the Yugoslav government on August 8th, strongly protesting this crime by the Yugoslav authorities. The note stated: Albanian citizen Spata was returning home through Yugoslavia with a formal Albanian government passport and a Yugoslav visa, but he disappeared in Belgrade on May 27th. Later, the Yugoslav government admitted that its public security organs had arrested Spata and detained him near Belgrade. However, the Albanian government's two protests received no response, and Spata was suddenly murdered on the 7th.

The note pointed out: This is a vile massacre, a malicious crime. It seriously violates the fundamental principles of international law. The Albanian government once again lodges a strong protest with the Yugoslav government for this reason, demanding that the Yugoslav authorities severely punish the culprits, immediately return the body, and demand compensation.

The note also emphasized: The Yugoslav government's claim that Spata was shot while attempting to escape from detention is precisely the excuse Yugoslavia habitually uses when murdering people.

### 南当局竟杀害被扣阿公民　阿尔巴尼亚政府提出强硬抗议

1958-08-11
第6版()
专栏：

　　南当局竟杀害被扣阿公民
　　阿尔巴尼亚政府提出强硬抗议
    新华社地拉那9日电　正当阿尔巴尼亚政府8月6日再度就阿尔巴尼亚公民哈桑·斯帕塔在南斯拉夫首都被绑架一事提出严重抗议的时候，南斯拉夫当局竟在8月7日阴谋杀害了斯帕塔。为此，阿尔巴尼亚外交部8日第三次向南斯拉夫政府发出了一份照会，强硬抗议南斯拉夫当局这一罪行。照会说：阿尔巴尼亚公民斯帕塔是持有阿尔巴尼亚政府正式护照和南斯拉夫签证经过南斯拉夫返国的，但是他却在5月27日在贝尔格莱德失踪了。后来南斯拉夫政府也承认它的公安机关逮捕了斯帕塔，并且把他拘留在贝尔格莱德附近。但是阿尔巴尼亚政府两次提出抗议，都没有得到答复，而斯帕塔却突然于7日被杀害了。
    照会指出：这是丑恶的屠杀，恶毒的罪行。它严重地破坏了国际法基本原则。阿尔巴尼亚政府为此再次向南斯拉夫政府提出强硬的抗议，要求南斯拉夫当局严惩祸首，立即交回尸体，并要求赔偿。
    照会中还强调指出：南斯拉夫政府诋赖斯帕塔是在企图从拘留所逃跑时而被枪杀的，这正是南斯拉夫在杀害人时所惯用的借口。
