Title:  Looting and Aggression, Full of Bloody Crimes: The United States is the Public Enemy of the People of Latin America
Author: 
Date: 1958-08-26
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 24th dispatch from Santiago: The Chilean Communist Party's newspaper "Siglo" recently published an article listing the bloody historical facts of the United States' plunder and aggression against Latin American countries, and pointed out that American imperialism is the common enemy of the people of all Latin American countries.

The newspaper said that the United States recently sent Marines to land in Cuba under the pretext of protecting the water supply system of its military base in Cuba, which is by no means an isolated event. U.S. imperialism has consistently participated in all reactionary coups and military riots in Latin America, controlled and dominated almost all of the natural wealth and import and export trade of Latin American countries, controlled and commanded the military machines of Latin American countries, and supported the oligarchy that has caused the backwardness of the people of Latin American countries.

The newspaper then listed the facts of the United States' aggression against Latin American countries under the title "History of Plunder and Blood." According to the materials published by the newspaper, since 1900, the United States has carried out no less than fifteen armed interventions in Latin American countries. These facts are:

	1900 - Forcibly formulated the Hay-Herrán Treaty and the Hay-Calvo Treaty on Nicaragua and Costa Rica in order to obtain access to the Pacific Ocean.
	1902 to 1934 - Forced Cuba to formulate the Platt Amendment.
	1903 - With the direct support and intervention of the United States, Panama seceded from Colombia and became independent.
	1903 - Forcibly formulated the Bunau-Varilla Treaty. According to this treaty, the United States enjoys sovereignty over the Panama Canal Zone forever.
	1906 to 1909 - The United States carried out military intervention in Cuba under the leadership of General Charles Magoon.
	1907 - Implemented financial intervention in the Dominican Republic, and the United States established a "Tax Bureau" in this country.
	1909 - Severed relations with Nicaragua and carried out armed intervention.
	1912 - "In order to protect the lives and interests of Americans," military intervention was again carried out in Cuba.
	1914 - Forcibly formulated the Bryan-Chamorro Treaty on Nicaragua.
	1915 to 1934 - Implemented armed attacks, aggression, and occupation of Haiti for a total of nineteen years.
	1916 - General Pershing carried out a "punitive" expedition and invaded Mexico in opposition to the Mexican Revolution.
	1916 to 1924 - Comprehensive armed intervention in the Dominican Republic, and the establishment of a "Dominican Government" composed of American officers.
	1926 to 1933 - The United States carried out its third armed intervention in Nicaragua and occupied the country. Finally, in 1934, Sandino was brutally murdered (the leader of the armed struggle against American aggression - editor's note).
	1954 - Diplomatic intervention and armed aggression against the legitimate government of Guatemala.
	1957 - Military intervention in Cuba, with the aim of protecting Batista.

### 掠夺和侵略血腥罪行累累  美国是拉丁美洲人民公敌

1958-08-26
第5版()
专栏：

　　掠夺和侵略血腥罪行累累
    美国是拉丁美洲人民公敌
    新华社24日讯　圣地亚哥消息：智利共产党机关报“世纪报”最近刊载一篇文章，历数美国对拉丁美洲各国进行掠夺和侵略的血腥史实，并且指出，美国帝国主义是拉丁美洲各国人民共同的敌人。
    这家报纸说，美国不久前以保护它在古巴的军事基地的供水系统为借口，派海军陆战队在古巴登陆，这决不是一件孤立的事件。美帝国主义一贯参与所有拉丁美洲反动的政变和军事暴动，控制和支配了拉丁美洲国家几乎全部的天然财富和进出口贸易，控制和指挥拉丁美洲国家的军事机器。支持造成拉丁美洲各国人民落后状况的寡头集团。
    报纸接着在“掠夺和血的历史”的标题下列举了美国对拉丁美洲各国进行侵略的事实。根据报纸刊登的材料，从1900年以来，美国对拉丁美洲各国进行的武装干涉主要的就有十五次之多。这些事实是：
    1900年——对尼加拉瓜和哥斯达黎加强行制定哈尔—科里亚和海伊—卡尔沃条约，以便得到太平洋的通路。
    1902至1934年——强迫古巴制定了普莱特补充条文。
    1903年——在美国的直接支持和干涉之下，巴拿马脱离哥伦比亚而独立。
    1903年——强行制订布瑙—瓦里亚条约。根据这项条约，美国永远对巴拿马运河区享有主权。
    1906至1909年——美国在查尔斯·梅根将军的领导下，对古巴进行了军事干涉。
    1907年——对多米尼加共和国实行财政干涉，美国在这个国家建立了“税务局”。
    1909年——断绝同尼加拉瓜的关系并且进行武装干涉。
    1912年——“为了保护美国人的生命和利益”，又对古巴进行军事干涉。
    1914年——对尼加拉瓜强行制订布赖—查莫罗条约。
    1915至1934年——对海地实行武装进攻、侵略和占领，共达十九年。
    1916年——帕欣将军进行“惩罚性”远征，侵入墨西哥，反对墨西哥革命。
    1916至1924年——全面地武装干涉多米尼加共和国，成立了一个由美国军官组成的“多米尼加政府”。
    1926至1933年——美国第三次武装干涉尼加拉瓜并且占领了这个国家。最后并且在1934年残杀了桑地诺（反对美国侵略的武装斗争的领袖——编者注）。
    1954年——对危地马拉合法政府进行外交干涉和武装侵略。
    1957年——对古巴进行军事干涉，目的是保护巴蒂斯塔。
