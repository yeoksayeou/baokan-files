Title: Thai Prime Minister Says Taiwan Situation is a Matter for the Chinese People
Author:
Date: 1958-08-31
Page: 3

　　Thai Prime Minister Says
    Taiwan Situation is a Matter for the Chinese People
    Xinhua News Agency, August 30th, Bangkok: Thai Prime Minister Thanom Kittikachorn told reporters on the 29th regarding the situation in the Taiwan Strait: "This is a matter for the Chinese people themselves. We can sit by and watch this matter."

### 泰国总理说  台湾局势是中国人的事

1958-08-31
第3版()
专栏：

　　泰国总理说
    台湾局势是中国人的事
    新华社30日讯　曼谷消息：泰国总理乃他侬·吉滴卡宗29日就台湾海峡局势对新闻记者发表谈话说：“这是中国人自己的事情。我们可以坐视这件事。”
