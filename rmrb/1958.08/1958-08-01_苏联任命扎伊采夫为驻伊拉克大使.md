Title: The Soviet Union Appoints Zaitsev as Ambassador to Iraq
Author:
Date: 1958-08-01
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 30th (TASS dispatch): The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union has appointed Grigory Zaitsev as the Plenipotentiary and Extraordinary Ambassador of the Soviet Union to the Republic of Iraq.

### 苏联任命扎伊采夫为驻伊拉克大使

1958-08-01
第2版()
专栏：

    苏联任命扎伊采夫为驻伊拉克大使
    新华社31日讯　塔斯社莫斯科30日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团任命格里戈里·扎伊采夫为苏联驻伊拉克共和国特命全权大使。
