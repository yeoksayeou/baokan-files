Title: Directly Attack the Four Pests' Base Camp
Author: 
Date: 1958-08-19
Page: 6

Directly Attack the Four Pests' Base Camp

A lively and innocent army,
With red scarves around their necks,
Directly attack the Four Pests' base camp,
Turning over nests and destroying dens to compete for merit.
The boy soldiers bravely annihilate the enemy,
Like Nezha stirring up the Dragon Palace.
The girl soldiers' sweeping is comparable to,
Mu Guiying on the Tianmen Formation.
Young heroism is vigorously flourishing,
Not withdrawing until victory is won.

　　　　　——Fujian

### 直捣四害根据地
福建
1958-08-19
第6版()
专栏：

　　直捣四害根据地天真活泼一支兵，项上挂着红领巾，直捣四害根据地，翻巢毁穴争功勋，男兵歼敌施勇猛，好像哪叱闹龙宫，女兵扫荡堪可比，天门阵上穆桂英，少年英气蓬勃勃，不获胜利不收兵。
　　　　　——福建
