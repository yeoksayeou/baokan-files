Title: Jubilant Throngs – Reading the Communiqué of the Meeting Between Chairman Mao and Khrushchev
Author: Sha Ou
Date: 1958-08-05
Page: 8

    Jubilant Throngs
    ——Reading the Communiqué of the Meeting Between Chairman Mao and Khrushchev
    Sha Ou
    I
    This is a rocket,
    Piercing through the hearts of a billion people,
    Leaving behind power,
    Leaving behind faith.
    II
    China and the Soviet Union shoulder to shoulder,
    Giant arms holding up the sky;
    Jubilant throngs,
    Rainbow clouds appear everywhere,
    Commanding the American and British aggressors to obey:
    No infringement on peace allowed!
    III
    A giant hand of support,
    Extends to the Middle East;
    A drumbeat of justice,
    Flies to the Middle East;
    The banner of peace,
    Is planted in the Middle East;
    The Middle East belongs to the people,
    Sweep the pirates into the sea!
    IV
    The tide surges forward,
    Chains are broken inch by inch,
    National liberation blazes like fire,
    Burning half the world red.
    V
    A mountain flies over,
    Heads of the war maniacs;
    Ten thousand arrows shoot,
    Into the hearts of the war maniacs;
    A natural moat is dug,
    Beside the feet of the war maniacs;
    Dare to act rashly,
    The day of death will surely be advanced!
    VI
    To save humanity from disaster,
    Two giants meet,
    Building fortresses and walls for peace,
    History will remember today.
    VII
    The East wind prevails over the West wind,
    The East wind blows stronger and stronger;
    How can withered branches and fallen leaves stop it,
    The Earth revolves around the Sun!

### 万众欢腾——读毛主席和赫鲁晓夫会谈公报
沙鸥
1958-08-05
第8版()
专栏：

    万众欢腾
    ——读毛主席和赫鲁晓夫会谈公报
    沙鸥
    一
    这是一支火箭，
    穿过亿万人民心间，
    留下威力，
    留下信念。
    二
    中苏肩并肩，
    巨臂顶住天；
    万众欢腾，
    彩云四现，
    叫美英侵略者听令：
    不准向和平侵犯！
    三
    支持的巨手，
    伸向中近东；
    正义的鼓声，
    飞向中近东；
    和平的大旗，
    插向中近东；
    中近东属于人民，
    把海盗扫下海中！
    四
    潮流滚滚，
    锁链寸断，
    民族解放烈如火，
    烧红世界半个天。
    五
    战争狂人头上，
    飞来一座山；
    战争狂人心中，
    射来万支箭；
    战争狂人脚边，
    挖了一条天堑；
    胆敢妄动，
    死期必定提前！
    六
    挽救人类灾难，
    两位巨人会见，
    为和平修堡筑墙，
    历史将记住今天。
    七
    东风压倒西风，
    东风越吹越猛；
    残枝败叶怎能拦阻，
    地球绕太阳转动！
