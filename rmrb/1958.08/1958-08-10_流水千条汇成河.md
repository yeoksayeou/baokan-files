Title: A Thousand Streams Converge into a River
Author: Cao Leng
Date: 1958-08-10
Page: 3

    A thousand threads are woven with one shuttle,
    A thousand streams converge into a river,
    Learning a few characters every night,
    Accumulation will lead to a lot.
        ——Cao Leng, Guizhou

### 流水千条汇成河

1958-08-10
第3版()
专栏：

    流水千条汇成河
    千纱织布靠一梭，
    流水千条汇成河，
    每晚学习几个字，
    日积月累就很多。
        ——贵州曹冷
