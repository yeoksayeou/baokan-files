Title: Our Embassies Abroad Hold Receptions Celebrating the Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1958-08-03
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency reports: The Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union held a reception on the evening of August 1st to celebrate the 31st anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The Soviet side attending the reception included Deputy Minister of National Defense, Marshal of the Soviet Union Biryuzov, marshals and generals of the Air Force, Navy, Artillery, and Armored Forces, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union Charubin, and military attachés of many countries' embassies and legations in the Soviet Union. Liu Xiao, China's ambassador to the Soviet Union, and Lieutenant General Han Zhenji, military attaché, attended the reception.

The military attachés of our embassies in Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria, Romania, the German Democratic Republic, Hungary, Poland, India, the United Arab Republic, Indonesia, Burma, Switzerland, Yugoslavia, Afghanistan and other countries also held receptions to celebrate the "August 1st" Army Day.

All Soviet military newspapers devoted special columns on July 31st to commemorate the 31st anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. "Red Star" published an editorial on this matter and also published an article by Marshal Luo Ronghuan entitled "From Difficulty to Victory."

The Romanian "Defend the Motherland" published an editorial congratulating the 31st anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, a report by its editor-in-chief Anghip visiting Chinese officers, poems by Chinese soldiers, and related pictures. "Spark" published pictures of artillery exercises of the Chinese People's Liberation Army on that day.

The Czechoslovak "People's Defense" also published editorials and articles to celebrate the 31st anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

### 我各驻外使馆举行招待会  庆祝中国人民解放军建军节

1958-08-03
第3版()
专栏：

    我各驻外使馆举行招待会
    庆祝中国人民解放军建军节
    本报综合报道　据新华社消息：中国驻苏联大使馆1日晚举行招待宴会，庆祝中国人民解放军建军三十一周年。苏联方面出席招待会的有国防部副部长、苏联元帅比留佐夫和空军、海军、炮兵、装甲兵等兵种的元帅和将军们，苏联外交部副部长查鲁宾，以及许多国家驻苏联大使馆和公使馆的武官。我国驻苏联大使刘晓和武官韩振纪中将出席了招待会。
    我国驻捷克斯洛伐克、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、德意志民主共和国、匈牙利、波兰、印度、阿拉伯联合共和国、印度尼西亚、缅甸、瑞士、南斯拉夫、阿富汗等国大使馆的武官也为庆祝“八一”建军节举行了招待会。
    苏联各军队报纸31日都辟了专栏，纪念中国人民解放军建军三十一周年。“红星报”就这件事发表了社论，并且刊登了罗荣桓元帅的题为“从困难到胜利”的专文。
    罗马尼亚“保卫祖国报”发表了祝贺中国人民解放军建军三十一周年的社论、它的总编辑安吉普访问中国军官的通讯、中国战士的诗和有关图片。“火花报”这一天刊登了中国人民解放军炮兵演习的图片。
    捷克斯洛伐克“人民防线报”也发表了社论和文章，庆祝中国人民解放军建军三十一周年。
