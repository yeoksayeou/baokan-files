Let's See Who Saves the Most Coal

In June, the national thermal power plants made great achievements in saving coal. Among the nineteen power plants with roughly the same equipment conditions, the coal consumption index of ten plants was much lower than in May. The most prominent were Datong Power Plant in Shanxi Province, Qingshan Power Plant in Hubei Province, and Zhuzhou Power Plant in Hunan Province, which burned less coal each month for three consecutive months.

Comparing the first half of the year by bureau, Liaoning Province had the lowest coal consumption index, averaging 0.461 kg of coal per kilowatt-hour, while the Shaanxi Provincial Electric Power Bureau used 0.471 kg. However, the four electric power bureau systems in Inner Mongolia, Guangdong, Shandong, and Hunan averaged 0.606 to 0.650 kg of coal per kilowatt-hour, nearly 0.2 kg more than Liaoning.

Two months ago, we calculated that if we could save 0.01 kg of coal per kilowatt-hour, the thermal power plants of the fifteen electric power bureaus across the country could save 6,000 tons of coal in one month and 72,000 tons of coal in one year. This is an amazing figure. As can be seen from the table below, the potential for coal saving in various power plants is still great. It is hoped that those lagging behind will work hard to catch up; the advanced ones should continue to make persistent efforts.

### 看谁用煤最节省

1958-08-13
第3版()
专栏：比一比

　　看谁用煤最节省
    6月份，全国火力发电厂节约煤炭的成绩很大。在十九个设备条件大致相同的发电厂中，有十个厂的煤耗指标比5月份又降低了许多，其中最突出的有山西省大同电厂、湖北省青山发电厂、湖南省株洲发电厂，三个月来烧煤一月比一月少。
    整个上半年以局为单位比较，辽宁省煤耗指标最低，平均每发一度电烧0.461公斤煤，陕西省电业局用0.471公斤。而内蒙古、广东、山东、湖南四个电业局系统，平均每发一度电要烧0.606到0.650公斤煤，比辽宁多用将近0.2公斤。
    两个月前，我们曾经算过一笔账：每发一度电，如果能节省0.01公斤煤，全国十五个电业局的火力发电厂一个月就可以节省六千吨煤，一年就可以节省七万二千吨煤，这是多么惊人的数字。从下面这个表可以看出，各地电厂节约煤的潜力还很大。希望那些落在后边的地区，努力赶上去；先进的再接再厉。
