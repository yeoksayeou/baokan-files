Title: Cairo Newspapers Point Out Significance of Sino-Soviet Talks: Making Arab People Very Happy, Making Imperialists Deeply Uneasy
Author:
Date: 1958-08-06
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 4th: Cairo's "Evening News" published an editorial today praising the Sino-Soviet leaders' joint communique as a "historical and peaceful document pointing out the path of maintaining peace and opposing aggression."

The newspaper said that this document embodies a true assessment of the world situation and proposes rapid and positive solutions.

The "Evening News" congratulated China and the Soviet Union, saying that this demonstrates the Soviet Union and China's desire to maintain world peace and their determination to eliminate aggression for the sake of peace. The newspaper said that this communique is indeed something that makes imperialists uneasy.

The newspaper expressed its welcome for the communique's support for the struggles of the Arab peoples. It said that the Arab peoples are very happy to see this great force supporting them.

### 开罗报纸指出中苏会谈意义  使阿拉伯人民很高兴  使帝国主义深感不安

1958-08-06
第2版()
专栏：

    开罗报纸指出中苏会谈意义
    使阿拉伯人民很高兴  使帝国主义深感不安
    新华社开罗4日电　开罗“晚报”今天发表社论，赞扬中苏两国领导人会谈公报是“指出维持和平和反对侵略的道路的历史性和平文件”。
    报纸说，这个文件体现了对世界局势的真实估计，并且提出了迅速的和积极的解决办法。
    “晚报”向中苏两国祝贺说，这说明苏联和中国渴望维持世界和平，并且决心为了和平消灭侵略。报纸说，这项公报的确是使帝国主义者感到不安的东西。
    这家报纸表示欢迎公报对阿拉伯各国人民斗争的支持。它说，阿拉伯各国人民很高兴看到这支伟大的力量在支持他们。
