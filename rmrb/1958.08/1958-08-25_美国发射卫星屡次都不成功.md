Title: The United States Fails Repeatedly in Launching Satellites
Author:
Date: 1958-08-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cape Canaveral dispatch, August 24th: The United States' attempt to launch an artificial Earth satellite weighing about seventeen kilograms using a "Jupiter-C" rocket failed again today.

According to a statement released by the U.S. Department of Defense six hours after the failed launch, the launch was unsuccessful. The satellite "did not enter orbit around the Earth." He said that "all reports and data" needed to be "studied" before determining why the satellite did not enter orbit.

### 美国发射卫星屡次都不成功

1958-08-25
第5版()
专栏：

　　美国发射卫星屡次都不成功
    新华社24日讯卡纳维拉尔角消息：美国今天利用“丘辟特—C”式火箭发射一颗重约十七公斤的人造地球卫星的尝试又遭到失败。
    据美国国防部在发射失败后六小时发表的公报说，这次发射没有成功。卫星“没有进入围绕地球的轨道”。他说，在确定为什么卫星没有进入轨道之前，还需要“研究所有的报告和资料”。
