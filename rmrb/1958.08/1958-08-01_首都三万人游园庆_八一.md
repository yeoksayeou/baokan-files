Title: 30,000 People in the Capital Celebrate "August 1st" in Parks
Author:
Date: 1958-08-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 31st – Today, senior generals and officers of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Beijing, staff members of state organs, and citizens, totaling more than 30,000 people, held a grand garden party and get-together to celebrate the "August 1st" Army Day. The Working People's Cultural Palace in Beijing was filled with red flags and bright lights. Huge slogans and cartoons opposing the aggression of the United States and Britain were posted all over the red palace walls.

　　At 7:30 p.m., the garden party began, with performances by seventeen professional art troupes, amateur soldier performance teams, and amateur art troupes from state organs. Many programs expressed people's condemnation and contempt for the aggressors and their support for the people of the Middle East.

　　Members of the extracurricular performance team from the Sichuan Provincial Revolutionary Disabled Soldiers Recuperation Center, who had just returned from a performance in Baoding at a little after 4:00 p.m. this afternoon, also rushed to the Cultural Palace to perform. Their performance stirred the hearts of the thousands of listeners packed in, and was constantly interrupted by thunderous applause from the audience.

　　In the Arts Hall, more than sixty famous painters from Beijing were painting heroic portraits of PLA heroes and meritorious individuals from all services and arms. This garden party continued until late at night.

### 首都三万人游园庆“八一”

1958-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    首都三万人游园庆“八一”
    据新华社31日讯　今天，中国人民解放军驻北京部队高级将领和官兵、国家机关工作人员、市民共三万多人，举行了盛大的庆祝“八一”建军节游园联欢晚会。北京劳动人民文化宫内红旗飘扬，灯火辉耀。红色的宫墙上贴遍了反对美英侵略的巨幅标语和漫画。
    七时三十分，游园晚会开始，十七个专业文艺团体、战士业余演出队和国家机关业余文艺团体分别举行了表演。许多节目都表达了人们对侵略者的谴责、鄙视和对中东人民的支持。
    四川省革命残废军人教养院课余演出队的队员们，今天下午四点多钟才从保定演出归来也赶到文化宫演出。他们的表演，激动了挤得满满的几千听众的心，不断为听众们暴风雨般的掌声所打断。
    在文艺大厅里，北京的六十多位著名画家给来自各军种兵种的解放军英雄、功臣们画着英武的肖像。这个游园会进行直到深夜。
