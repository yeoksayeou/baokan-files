Title: Uruguayan Communist Party Holds Seventeenth Congress, Adopts New Party Constitution and Elects New Central Leadership

Author:

Date: 1958-08-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Montevideo: According to TASS, the Seventeenth Congress of the Communist Party of Uruguay, which opened in Montevideo on the 15th, closed on the 17th. The congress heard and discussed the summary report of Rodney Arismendi, First Secretary of the Communist Party of Uruguay, the report of Executive Committee member Rodriguez on the party's tasks in the election campaign, and the report of party secretary Marcela on the program declaration and the party's action program for the recent period. It also heard and discussed Suarez's report on the revision of the party constitution.

The congress approved the above reports and adopted the new party constitution.

The congress called on the United Nations to demand the immediate withdrawal of the American and British invading forces from Lebanon and Jordan, and suggested that the Uruguayan Ministry of Foreign Affairs instruct the Uruguayan delegation to the United Nations to vote in favor of the proposal to withdraw these troops.

The congress passed letters of greetings to the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Communist Party of China, and other communist and workers' parties of various countries.

The congress elected a central committee composed of twenty-nine central committee members and eighteen alternate central committee members.

According to "El Popular," the central committee held a plenary session and elected the executive committee. The following were elected as members of the executive committee: Rodney Arismendi, Enrique Pastarino, Alberto Suarez, Jose Marcela, Rosario Pietraroia, Jaime Perez, Enrique Rodriguez, Julios Arevalo, Felix Diaz, Jose Blanco, Gregorio Zapin. The following were elected as alternate members of the executive committee: Cesar Reyes Daglio, Leopoldo Bruera, Alberto Altesor.

Rodney Arismendi was elected as First Secretary of the Central Committee.

### 乌共举行十七次代表大会  通过新党章并选出新的中央领导机构

1958-08-21
第5版()
专栏：

　　乌共举行十七次代表大会
    通过新党章并选出新的中央领导机构
    据新华社20日讯  据塔斯社蒙得维的亚讯：15日在蒙得维的亚开幕的乌拉圭共产党第十七次代表大会，已经在17日闭幕。大会听取和讨论了乌拉圭共产党第一书记阿里斯门迪的总结报告、执行委员会委员罗德里格斯关于党在竞选运动中的任务的报告以及党的书记马赛拉关于纲领宣言和最近时期党的行动纲领的报告，还听取和讨论了苏阿雷斯关于修改党章的报告。
    代表大会赞成上述报告，并且通过了新党章。
    代表大会呼吁联合国要美英侵略军立即撤出黎巴嫩和约旦，并且建议乌拉圭外交部指示乌拉圭在联合国的代表团投票赞成要这些军队撤走的提案。
    大会通过给苏联共产党、中国共产党和其他国家共产党和工人党致意的信件。
    代表大会选举了由二十九名中央委员和十八名候补中央委员组成的中央委员会。
    据“人民报”报道，中央委员会举行了全体会议，选出了执行委员会。当选为执行委员会委员的有：罗德尼·阿里斯门迪、恩里克·帕斯塔里诺、艾伯托·苏阿雷斯、何塞·马塞拉、罗萨利奥·彼特拉罗阿、哈伊麦·佩雷斯、恩里克·罗德里格斯、胡莉斯·阿雷瓦洛、费利克斯·迪亚斯、何塞·布兰科、格雷戈里奥·萨平。当选为执行委员会候补委员的有：塞萨尔·雷耶斯·达格里奥、莱奥波耳多·布鲁埃拉、阿尔贝托·阿尔泰索尔。
    罗德尼·阿里斯门迪当选为中央委员会第一书记。
