Title: Korean Government Statement Supports Soviet Proposal
Author:
Date: 1958-08-10
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 9th - The government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued a statement today, saying that the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea firmly believes that this UN General Assembly emergency meeting should, in accordance with its lofty mission of maintaining world peace and security, take effective measures to withdraw the US and British troops from Lebanon and Jordan, so as to ensure that the Arab people can justly resolve their internal affairs according to their own will and quickly restore peace in the region.

The statement condemned the US and British imperialists for rejecting the Soviet proposal for a summit conference of great powers and continuing to send troops to Lebanon and Jordan, as well as the US increasing its military forces and establishing missile bases in South Korea.

The statement said that the Korean people fully support the Soviet government's consistent efforts to maintain world peace and peacefully resolve the dangerous situation in the Middle East caused by the aggression of US and British imperialists, and resolutely demand that US and British imperialists immediately stop their armed aggression against small countries and interference in their internal affairs.

The statement said that the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Korean people actively support and stand in solidarity with the struggle of the Arab countries' people against imperialist aggression and for freedom and independence.

### 朝鲜政府声明  支持苏联建议

1958-08-10
第2版()
专栏：

    朝鲜政府声明  支持苏联建议
    新华社平壤9日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府今天发表声明说，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府坚决主张：这次联大紧急会议应该根据它的维护世界和平和安全的崇高使命，采取有效措施，使美英军队撤出黎巴嫩和约旦，以保障阿拉伯人民按照自己的意志正当地解决他们的内政问题，迅速恢复这个地区的和平。
    声明谴责了美英帝国主义者拒绝苏联关于召开大国首脑会议的建议和继续向黎巴嫩和约旦增兵，以及美国在南朝鲜增加军事力量建立导弹基地的行为。
    声明说，朝鲜人民完全支持苏联政府为维护世界和平和和平解决由于美英帝国主义者的侵略而造成的中近东危险局势的一贯的努力，并且坚决要求美英帝国主义者立即停止对小国的武装侵略和干涉它们的内政。
    声明说，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府和朝鲜人民积极支持和声援阿拉伯国家人民反对帝国主义侵略和争取自由独立的斗争。
