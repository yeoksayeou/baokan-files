Title: Bern Celebrates Swiss National Day with Reception

Author: 
Date: 1958-08-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st August: The Swiss Ambassador to China, Mr. Bern, held a reception this afternoon to celebrate the National Day of the Swiss Confederation.

    Vice Premier Bo Yibo and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo and others attended the reception. In his speech, Vice Premier Bo Yibo wished the Swiss Confederation prosperity and its people happiness, and wished the President of the Swiss Confederation, Thomas Holenstein, good health.

    Ambassador Bern raised his glass earlier in a speech, and proposed a toast to the good health of Chairman Mao Zedong, and wished the Chinese people prosperity and happiness.

    More than one hundred Chinese and foreign guests attended the reception.

### 贝努义举行招待会庆祝瑞士国庆日

1958-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    贝努义举行招待会庆祝瑞士国庆日
    新华社1日讯　瑞士驻中国大使贝努义今天下午举行招待会，庆祝瑞士联邦国庆日。
    薄一波副总理和外交部副部长罗贵波等出席了招待会。薄一波副总理在致词的时候，祝瑞士联邦繁荣和人民幸福，祝瑞士联邦主席托马斯·霍伦斯太的身体健康。
    贝努义大使在早一些时候举杯致词，他提议为毛泽东主席的身体健康干杯，并祝中国人民繁荣和幸福。
    有一百多位中外人士参加了这次招待会。
