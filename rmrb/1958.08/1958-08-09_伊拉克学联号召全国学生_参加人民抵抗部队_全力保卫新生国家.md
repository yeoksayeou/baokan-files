Title: Iraqi Student Union Calls on Students Nationwide to Join the People's Resistance Forces to Fully Defend the Newborn Nation

Author:

Date: 1958-08-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, 7th. The Supreme Committee of the Iraqi National Student Union issued a letter to students, calling on all students to immediately join the People's Resistance Forces to defend the Iraqi Republic.

The letter to students mentioned the heroic role of students in the revolution and called on students to do their best and muster the courage to defend the Republic.

The letter to students called on Iraqi students to become an organized force, a tool for defending the Iraqi Republic against aggression and maintaining its security.

### 伊拉克学联号召全国学生  参加人民抵抗部队  全力保卫新生国家

1958-08-09
第2版()
专栏：

    伊拉克学联号召全国学生
    参加人民抵抗部队全力保卫新生国家
    新华社巴格达7日电  伊拉克全国学生联合会最高委员会发表了告学生书，号召全体学生立即参加人民抵抗部队，保卫伊拉克共和国。
    告学生书提到学生们在革命中的英勇作用，号召学生们竭尽全力和鼓足勇气来保卫共和国。
    告学生书号召伊拉克学生成为有组织的力量，成为保卫伊拉克共和国抵抗侵略和维护它的安全的工具。
