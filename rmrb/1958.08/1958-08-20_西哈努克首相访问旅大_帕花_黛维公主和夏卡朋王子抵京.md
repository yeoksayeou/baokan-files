Title: Prime Minister Sihanouk Visits Lüda; Princess Phala Devi and Prince Chakrapong Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-08-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 19th August: Prime Minister Norodom Sihanouk and the National Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia led by him, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi and Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Yu Peiwen, left Shenyang by special plane at 9:40 this morning to visit Lüda.

Among those who saw the distinguished guests off at the airport were Qiu Youwen, Vice Governor of Liaoning Province; Li Huang, Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China; Lieutenant General Cheng Shicai, Commander of the Shenyang Garrison; Liu Baotian, Mayor of Shenyang; Xu Shaofu, Secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China; and responsible persons from various democratic parties and people's organizations in Liaoning Province and Shenyang City. Kim Yong-bin, Consul General of North Korea in Shenyang, also went to the airport to see them off.

During their stay in Shenyang, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Vice Governor Qiu Youwen and Mayor Liu Baotian, visited factories and toured the lively temple fair in Shenyang City.

Xinhua News Agency, Lüda, 19th August: Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, paid a friendly visit to the beautiful coastal city of Dalian today.

Today, Dalian was sunny and the sea breeze was gentle. Prime Minister Sihanouk and the distinguished guests of the National Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia arrived in Dalian from Shenyang by special plane at 11:00 a.m., accompanied by Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi and others. They were warmly welcomed at the airport by Song Li, Mayor of Lüda, and Zhang Xiuchuan, Major General of the local garrison, and others. A group of innocent and lively children ran forward to present bouquets of flowers to the distinguished guests.

In the afternoon, Prime Minister Sihanouk and the distinguished guests of the delegation, accompanied by Mayor Song Li and others, visited the Dalian Shipyard and were greeted by the workers with enthusiastic applause. The distinguished guests visited the huge engine workshop, the dock, the slipway and the workers' work. Sun Wenxue, Deputy Director of the Dalian Shipyard, introduced to the Prime Minister that in the past this was a small ship repair yard, but not long ago they had built a large ship with a deadweight of 5,000 tons and a displacement of 8,000 tons, and now they have begun to build large ships with a deadweight of more than 10,000 tons. Prime Minister Sihanouk praised, "Your efforts and achievements not only prosper your country, but also contribute to the cause of world peace." Then, the distinguished guests visited the Lüda Glass Products Factory to see the production process of glass products. Upon leaving, Prime Minister Sihanouk said to the workers who had gathered outside the workshop to welcome him: "We are very pleased to admire your skills and artistic style. Your cordial welcome shows the friendship between the people of Cambodia and China."

In the evening, Prime Minister Sihanouk enthusiastically climbed the eight-story harbor building near the Port of Dalian to view the panorama of the magnificent modern Port of Dalian. Then, Prime Minister Sihanouk and the distinguished guests of the delegation went to the Port of Dalian and took a boat with Vice Premier Chen Yi, Mayor Song Li, Deputy Director of the Dalian Port Authority Wang Wei, etc., to cruise in the bay. They leaned on the railing on the boat and talked cordially until the sun set.

In the evening, Prime Minister Sihanouk and the distinguished guests of the delegation attended a welcome banquet hosted by Mayor Song Li.

Xinhua News Agency, 19th August: Princess Phala Devi and Prince Chakrapong, the daughter and son of Prime Minister Sihanouk, arrived in Beijing by special plane on the afternoon of the 19th at the invitation of our country. Major General Nu Ho, Chief of Staff of the Cambodian Air Force, and a group of Cambodian artists arrived on the same plane.

Vice Premier He Long, Minister Li Dequan and Deputy Director Ding Xilin and others went to the airport to welcome the distinguished guests. Young women in the capital presented flowers to the distinguished guests.

Today, Ran Niete, Qing Ha, members of the National Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia, and Yang An, head of the Cambodian Economic Delegation to China, also went to the airport to welcome them.

On the evening of the 19th, Vice Premier He Long hosted a banquet at the Beijing Hotel to welcome the distinguished guests. (Attached Picture)

### 西哈努克首相访问旅大　帕花·黛维公主和夏卡朋王子抵京

1958-08-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　西哈努克首相访问旅大
　　帕花·黛维公主和夏卡朋王子抵京
    新华社沈阳19日电  诺罗敦·西哈努克首相和由他率领的柬埔寨王国国家代表团，由陈毅副总理和外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文陪同，今天上午九点四十分乘专机离开沈阳去旅大参观访问。
    到机场欢送贵宾的有，辽宁省副省长仇友文，中共辽宁省委书记李荒，沈阳驻军首长程世才中将，沈阳市市长刘宝田，中共沈阳市委书记徐少甫以及辽宁省和沈阳市各民主党派、各人民团体负责人。朝鲜驻沈阳总领事金龙彬也到机场欢送。
    贵宾们在沈阳期间，由仇友文副省长、刘宝田市长陪同，参观了工厂，并且游览了沈阳市区热闹的庙会。
    新华社旅大19日电  柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王今天到美丽的海滨城市大连进行了友好访问。
    今天，大连阳光灿烂，海风温柔。西哈努克亲王和由他率领的柬埔寨王国国家代表团的贵宾们在上午十一时由陈毅副总理兼外交部长等陪同，从沈阳乘专机到达大连。他们在机场上受到了旅大市市长宋黎和当地驻军首长张秀川少将等人的热烈欢迎。一群天真活泼的儿童跑上前去，把大束鲜花献给贵宾们。
    下午，西哈努克首相和代表团的贵宾们由宋黎市长等陪同来到大连造船厂，受到了工人们的夹道鼓掌欢迎。贵宾们在这里参观了巨大的轮机车间，船坞、造船台和工人们的工作情形。大连造船厂副厂长孙文学向首相介绍说，过去这里是一个不大的船只修理厂，不久前他们已造好载重量五千吨、排水量八千吨的大船，现在他们又开始建造载重量一万吨以上的大船。西哈努克首相称赞说，你们的努力和成就不仅繁荣了你们的国家，而且是对世界和平事业的贡献。接着，贵宾们又来到旅大玻璃制品厂参观了玻璃制品的生产过程。在离开的时候，西哈努克首相向聚集在车间外边欢迎他的工人们说：我们很高兴地欣赏了你们的技巧和艺术风格，你们对我们的亲切欢迎显示了柬中两国人民的友谊关系。
    傍晚，西哈努克首相兴致勃勃地登上大连港口附近八层高的海港大楼，观看了宏伟的现代化的大连港全景。接着，西哈努克首相和代表团的贵宾们走到大连港，同陈毅副总理、宋黎市长、大连港务局副局长王伟等乘轮船在海湾内游览，他们在船上凭栏眺望，亲切交谈，一直到夕阳西下。
    晚间，西哈努克首相和代表团的贵宾们出席了宋黎市长的欢迎宴会。
    新华社19日讯  西哈努克首相的女儿、儿子帕花·黛维公主和夏卡朋王子应邀访问我国，在19日下午乘专机到达北京。同机到达的有柬埔寨空军参谋长努·呼少将和一批柬埔寨艺术家。
    贺龙副总理、李德全部长和丁西林副主任等到机场欢迎贵宾，首都的女青年向贵宾们献了鲜花。
    今天，柬埔寨王国国家代表团团员兰·涅特、清哈和柬埔寨驻中国经济代表团团长杨安也到机场欢迎。
    19日晚间，贺龙副总理在北京饭店，设宴为贵宾们洗尘。（附图片）
