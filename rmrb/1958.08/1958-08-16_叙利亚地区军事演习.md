Title: Military Exercises in the Syrian Region
Author:
Date: 1958-08-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, August 15th: The Damascus radio station reported that Marshal Amir, Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces of the United Arab Republic, visited military exercises on the 14th of troops from the Syrian region equipped with new weapons.

After the exercises, Amir requested officers and soldiers to continue their training efforts in order to reach the highest technical level.

### 叙利亚地区军事演习

1958-08-16
第5版()
专栏：

　　叙利亚地区军事演习
    新华社大马士革15日电　据大马士革电台广播，阿拉伯联合共和国武装部队总司令阿密尔元帅14日参观了装备有新式武器的叙利亚地区部队的军事演习。
    阿密尔在演习后要求军官和士兵继续努力训练，以便达到最高的技术水平。
