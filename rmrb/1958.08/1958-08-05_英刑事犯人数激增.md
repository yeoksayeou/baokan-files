Title: Surge in Number of Criminals in Britain
Author:
Date: 1958-08-05
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency dispatch: British Home Secretary Butler said in a written reply to Parliament on July 31 that a total of 545,562 criminal cases occurred last year, an increase of 13.7% compared to 1956. The number of people committing prostitution offenses increased by 31%.

### 英刑事犯人数激增

1958-08-05
第3版()
专栏：

    英刑事犯人数激增
    据新华社讯　英国内政大臣巴特勒7月31日在给议会的书面答复中说，去年一共发生了五十四万五千五百六十二件刑事犯罪案，比1956年增加了13.7％。犯卖淫罪的人数增加了31％。
