　　Poetry and Painting

　　Sun Wukong Mistakenly Takes It for Flaming Mountains
　　Pixian County Poster
    Poem by Ding Shan
　　Local methods of fertilizer production have a trick, producing over two million catties at a time.
　　The flames soaring to the sky startled the Traveler; how could the banana leaf fan save the old Sun!
