Title: US Troops Landing in Singapore Threatens Peace; Navy Spokesman Makes Shameless Defense

Author:

Date: 1958-08-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 19th: Washington dispatch: After two thousand US Marines landed in Singapore, a US Navy Department spokesman publicly announced on the 18th that "the Marine Corps of the US Seventh Fleet stationed in the Pacific is always available to fulfill the obligations the United States has undertaken in the Indian Ocean or the Persian Gulf."

The spokesman said that these obligations of the United States are undertaken in accordance with the aggressive Baghdad Pact, the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization, and the Eisenhower Doctrine.

The spokesman also attempted to interpret the gathering of US warships in Singapore and the landing of US Marine Corps soldiers in Singapore as a "routine visit."

According to Reuters on the 19th, the US Marines landed in Singapore eleven days ago.

### 美军登陆新加坡威胁和平　海军发言人居然进行无耻辩解

1958-08-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　美军登陆新加坡威胁和平
　　海军发言人居然进行无耻辩解
    新华社19日讯  华盛顿消息：在两千名美国海军陆战队在新加坡登陆以后，美国海军部的一个发言人18日公开宣布说，“驻扎在太平洋的美国第七舰队的海军陆战队，随时可以用来履行美国在印度洋或波斯湾所承担的义务”。
    这位发言人说，美国的这些义务是根据侵略性的巴格达条约、东南亚条约组织和艾森豪威尔主义而承担的。
    这位发言人同时还企图把美国军舰在新加坡集结和美国海军陆战队的士兵在新加坡登陆的行动解释为“例行访问”。
    据路透社19日透露，美国陆战队是在十一天以前在新加坡登陆的。
