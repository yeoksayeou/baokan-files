　　Precision Instruments for Measuring Machinery
　　Wang Zhiping, a technician at the Xi'an State-owned Qing'an Machinery Factory, has successfully designed and manufactured China's first "electric contact micrometer." This instrument has a simple structure and high precision; the precision it reflects can reach a tolerance of "eleven ○·○○○ five." This product is mainly used to measure the inner diameter, outer diameter, length, width limits of various machinery. This product will soon be put into mass production.

### 测量机械的精密仪器

1958-08-15
第3版()
专栏：创制更多更好的新产品

　　测量机械的精密仪器
    西安国营庆安机器厂技术员王之平设计并制造成功我国第一部“电接触式测微仪”。这部仪器结构简单，精密度很高；它反映出的精密度可达到“十一○·○○○五”公差。这一产品主要用来测量各种机械的内径、外径、长、宽界限的呎吋。这种产品不久即可成批投入生产。
