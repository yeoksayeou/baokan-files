Title: Peace Cooperative Corn Yields 3621 Jin Per Mu
Author:
Date: 1958-08-12
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency report: The 1.604 mu of double-cropping cornfield cultivated by the high-yield crop team led by Liu Shuxian, the director of the Peace Agricultural Cooperative in Weinan County, Shaanxi Province, yielded 7,260 jin of corn from the first crop alone due to strengthened field management in the later stages. This is equivalent to 4,526 jin per mu, or 3,621 jin of dry corn per mu. When harvesting, personnel from the Shaanxi Provincial Department of Agriculture Technology Division, the Weinan County People's Committee, and the Shuangwang Township People's Committee participated in the verification and acceptance of the yield.

### 和平社玉米亩产3621斤

1958-08-12
第2版()
专栏：

    和平社玉米亩产3621斤
    据新华社讯　陕西省渭南县和平农业社社主任刘述贤领导的高额丰产作物小组种的一点六零四亩一年两熟玉米地，由于加强了田间后期管理，仅第一料（即第一熟，下同）就收了七千二百六十斤玉米，折合亩产四千五百二十六斤，折合干玉米亩产三千六百二十一斤。收割时，陕西农业厅技术处、渭南县人民委员会和双王乡人民委员会派人参加核产和验收。
