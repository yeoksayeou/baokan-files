Title: Changfeng Cooperative
Author:
Date: 1958-08-12
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 11th — The Changfeng Agricultural Cooperative in Xiaogan County, Hubei Province, has had its 1,050 mu of early rice inspected by a joint verification team from the county, district, and township levels. The average yield per mu reached 2,810 jin, an increase of more than three times compared to the average yield of 700 jin last year, and more than twice the original planned target of 1,200 jin per mu.

### 长风社

1958-08-12
第2版()
专栏：

    长风社
    据新华社武汉11日电　湖北省孝感县长风农业社，全社一千零五十亩早稻，经过县、区、乡三级联合验收组的验收，平均亩产达到二千八百一十斤，比去年平均亩产七百斤增产了三倍多，相当于原来规划亩产一千二百斤指标的两倍多。
