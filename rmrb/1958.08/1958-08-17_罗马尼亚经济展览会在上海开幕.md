Title: Romanian Economic Exhibition Opens in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1958-08-17
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 16th - The Romanian Economic Exhibition, long awaited by the people of Shanghai, grandly opened on the 15th at the Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Building.

　　The opening ceremony was presided over by the Romanian Ambassador to China, Rudenko. In his speech, he said: The exhibition in Beijing and Shanghai is a significant event for their country, presenting a part of the most important achievements of the national economy of the Romanian People's Republic to the Chinese people. Ambassador Rudenko then described the brilliant achievements of socialist construction in the Romanian People's Republic and the friendly and cooperative relations between China and Romania, and said: The exhibition of the Romanian Economic Exhibition will contribute to a deeper understanding between the two peoples, the further development of trade relations between the two countries, and the strengthening of the friendship between the two peoples.

　　Cao Diqiu, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, on behalf of the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee and the people of Shanghai, warmly congratulated the opening of the exhibition.

　　Attending the opening ceremony were Masa Abraham, head of the Romanian Economic Exhibition Delegation, all members of the delegation, Romanian Commercial Counselor to China, Wei Qiangnu, as well as Romanian experts and students in Shanghai.

　　Also attending the opening ceremony were Xu Jianguo, Niu Shucai, and Jin Zhonghua, Deputy Mayors of Shanghai, Li Zhuchen, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Sheng Pihua and Rong Yiren, Deputy Mayors of Shanghai, and Chairman and Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.

　　Consuls from various countries stationed in Shanghai also attended the opening ceremony.

　　The Romanian Economic Exhibition is the first large-scale exhibition held by Romania abroad. It has been exhibited in Beijing and has been warmly welcomed by the people of our country.

　　The exhibition will be held in Shanghai for one month.

### 罗马尼亚经济展览会在上海开幕

1958-08-17
第2版()
专栏：

　　罗马尼亚经济展览会在上海开幕
    据新华社上海16日电　上海人民渴望已久的罗马尼亚经济展览会，15日在上海中苏友好大厦隆重开幕。
    开幕典礼由罗马尼亚驻华大使鲁登科主持。他致词说：这个展览会在北京和上海的展出，对他们国家来说，是一项具有重大意义的事件，它把罗马尼亚人民共和国国民经济最重要成就的一部分介绍给中国人民。接着鲁登科大使叙述了罗马尼亚人民共和国社会主义建设的辉煌成就和中罗两国的友好合作关系，并说：这次罗马尼亚经济展览会的展出对两国人民之间更加深入了解、对两国的贸易关系的进一步发展和对两国人民间的友谊的加强，都将作出贡献。
    上海市副市长曹荻秋代表上海市人民委员会和上海人民，热烈祝贺展览会的开幕。
    参加开幕典礼的有罗马尼亚经济展览团团长玛萨·阿卜拉罕、展览团全体人员，罗马尼亚驻华大使馆商务参赞维强努以及在上海的罗马尼亚专家和留学生。
    参加开幕典礼的还有：上海市副市长许建国、牛树才、金仲华，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席李烛尘，上海市副市长、中国国际贸易促进委员会上海市委员会主任委员和副主任委员盛丕华、荣毅仁等。
    参加开幕典礼的还有各国驻上海领事。
    罗马尼亚经济展览会是罗马尼亚在国外举行的第一次大规模展览会，它曾在北京展出，受到我国人民的热烈欢迎。
    展览会将在上海展出一个月。
