    Electronic Heart Rate Meter
    The Tianjin Pharmaceutical Station recently successfully trial-produced an "Electronic Heart Rate Meter." This heart rate meter can accurately measure the patient's actual heart rate during surgery, allowing doctors to determine emergency treatment methods based on the reflected heart rate situation. ### 电子心速计

1958-08-15
第3版()
专栏：创制更多更好的新产品

    电子心速计
    天津医药站最近试制成功了一台“电子心速计”。这种心速计可以准确测定动手术时的病人的心动实况，使医生能根据反映出的心速情况，决定急救办法。（附图片）
