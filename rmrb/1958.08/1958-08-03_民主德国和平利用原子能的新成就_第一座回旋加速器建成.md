Title: New Achievements in Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy in the German Democratic Republic: First Cyclotron Built
Author:
Date: 1958-08-03
Page: 3

    New Achievements in Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy in the German Democratic Republic
    First Cyclotron Built
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 1st: The German Democratic Republic's first cyclotron has been completed and was officially handed over to the German Central Institute for Nuclear Physics today, and has begun operation. This cyclotron can be used to accelerate charged particles, allowing its energy to reach 10 million to 20 million electron volts, which can be used to produce radioactive isotopes for medical, biological and technical use.
    The completion of these two major pieces of equipment enables the German Democratic Republic’s Central Institute for Nuclear Physics (located in Rossendorf near Dresden) to begin scientific research on the peaceful use of atomic energy, allowing atomic energy to serve the socialist construction of the German Democratic Republic.

### 民主德国和平利用原子能的新成就  第一座回旋加速器建成

1958-08-03
第3版()
专栏：

    民主德国和平利用原子能的新成就
    第一座回旋加速器建成
    新华社柏林1日电　民主德国的第一座回旋加速器已经建成，今天正式移交给德国中央原子核物理研究所，并已开始运转。这个回旋加速器可以用来加速带电粒子，使它的能量达到一千万到二千万电子伏特，这样就能够制造出供医学、生物学和技术方面使用的放射性同位素。
    这两项重大设备的建成使民主德国中央原子核物理研究所（设在德累斯顿附近的罗森多尔夫）得以开始关于和平利用原子能的科学研究工作，让原子能为民主德国的社会主义建设服务。
