Title: Mom's Letter Praises the Canteen
Author: Li Bin
Date: 1958-08-27
Page: 8

　　Mom's Letter Praises the Canteen
　　Our commune has started a canteen,
　　My old mother wrote to me, explaining it again and again:
　　"Your wife also goes to the fields to labor,
　　No longer busy cooking at home;
　　Your mother, I, also went to the fields,
　　No longer sitting on the kang;
　　Your mother and wife labor for fifteen points a day,
　　The autumn harvest will surely be strong,
　　No need to send money home anymore,
　　Save the money in the People's Bank."
　　　　　　Zhangjiakou City 414 Mailbox Li Bin

### 俺妈来信夸食堂
李斌
1958-08-27
第8版()
专栏：

　　俺妈来信夸食堂
　　俺社办起了食堂，
　　老妈妈来信讲了又讲：
　　“你媳妇也下地去劳动，
　　不再在家里做饭忙；
　　妈妈我也下了地，
　　不再坐在炕头上；
　　我娘俩一天劳动十五分，
　　秋后收入一定强，
　　不用再往家寄钱，
　　把钱存到人民银行。”
　　　　　　张家口市414信箱  李斌
