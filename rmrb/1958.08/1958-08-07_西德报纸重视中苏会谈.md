Title: West German Newspapers Emphasize Sino-Soviet Talks
Author:
Date: 1958-08-07
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 5th: West German and West Berlin newspapers on August 5th universally published the news of the talks between Mao Zedong and Khrushchev, as well as abstracts of the communiqué, in prominent positions. Many newspapers also published photos of the leaders of the Chinese and Soviet governments and published editorials.

### 西德报纸重视中苏会谈

1958-08-07
第2版()
专栏：

    西德报纸重视中苏会谈
    据新华社柏林5日电　西德和西柏林各报5日都普遍以显著地位刊载了毛泽东和赫鲁晓夫会谈的消息以及会谈公报的摘要。不少报纸还刊登了中苏两国政府领导人的照片，并且发表了社论。
