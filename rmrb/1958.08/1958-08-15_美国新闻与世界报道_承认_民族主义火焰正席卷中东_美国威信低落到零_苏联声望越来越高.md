Title: "U.S. News & World Report" Admits Nationalist Flames Are Sweeping the Middle East, U.S. Prestige Plummets to Zero, Soviet Reputation Soars

Author:

Date: 1958-08-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, August 14th dispatch: New York dispatch: The August 15th issue of "U.S. News & World Report," published in advance, admits that the flames of nationalist revolution are sweeping across the entire Middle East, and nothing can stop it.

The magazine's reporter, in a dispatch from Baghdad, said that everyone he encountered in Baghdad, Cairo, and Damascus seemed to be "entangled in this flame of nationalism."

The reporter added, "When the policies of the United States and other Western countries are discussed, any Arab host immediately becomes angry. For them, the U.S. landing in Lebanon ominously reminds them of the years of occupation by Britain and France in most parts of this region."

He acknowledged that, in contrast to the predicament of Western countries, the Soviet Union's sympathy and support for the people of the Middle East is "obviously welcomed among Arabs of all classes." He quoted a Cairo shopkeeper as saying, "We like the Soviet government because the Soviets want peace, they want to help us develop and grow stronger."

The reporter believes that revolution is imminent throughout the vast Middle East. He said, "When you talk to Arabs from Jordan, from the distant Persian Gulf oil sheikdoms, and from almost anywhere in the Arab world, you can hear the same narrative: revolutions similar to the July 14th Iraqi coup will break out in their homelands sooner or later. They unanimously agree that nothing can stop it."

This reporter finally had to admit, "The prestige of the United States in these countries seems to be plummeting to zero—at the same time, the reputation of the Soviet Union is soaring."

### “美国新闻与世界报道”承认　民族主义火焰正席卷中东　美国威信低落到零　苏联声望越来越高

1958-08-15
第5版()
专栏：

　　“美国新闻与世界报道”承认
　　民族主义火焰正席卷中东
　　美国威信低落到零　苏联声望越来越高
    据新华社14日讯　纽约消息：提前出版的8月15日一期“美国新闻与世界报道”承认，民族主义革命的火焰正在席卷整个中东，没有任何东西能够制止它。
    这家刊物的记者在一篇发自巴格达的报道中说，他在巴格达、开罗、大马士革所接触过的每一个人看来都被这种“民族主义火焰缠住了”。
    记者又说，“当讨论到美国和其他西方国家的政策的时候，任何一个阿拉伯东道主都会马上变得愤怒起来。对他们来说，美国在黎巴嫩登陆不祥地使人想起英国和法国在这个地区的大部分地区的这么多年的占领。”
    他承认，与西方国家的这种处境相反，苏联对中东人民的同情和支持“显然在各阶层的阿拉伯人中间”受到欢迎。他引证了一个开罗店主的话：“我们喜欢苏联政府，因为苏联人希望和平，他们希望帮助我们发展和壮大。”
    记者认为，在幅员辽阔的整个中东，革命已经是山雨欲来风满楼了。他说，“当你同来自约旦、来自遥远的波斯湾石油酋长国和几乎是来自阿拉伯世界任何地方的阿拉伯人谈话的时候，你可以听到同样的叙述：类似7月14日伊拉克政变的革命迟早会在他们的祖国爆发。他们一致认为，没有任何东西能够制止它。”
    这个记者最后不得不承认，“美国在这些国家的威望看来正在低落到零——同时，苏联的声望越来越高。”
