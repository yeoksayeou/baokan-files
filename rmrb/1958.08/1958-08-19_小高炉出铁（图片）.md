Title: Small Blast Furnace Tapping (Picture)
Author: Xiao Guping
Date: 1958-08-19
Page: 3

Small Blast Furnace Tapping
This is an 8.5 cubic meter small blast furnace at the Suzhou Agricultural Machinery Factory. This small blast furnace can produce 1.5 tons of iron per tap and can tap ten times a day. Photo by Xiao Guping (New China Daily draft)

专栏：

　　小高炉出铁
