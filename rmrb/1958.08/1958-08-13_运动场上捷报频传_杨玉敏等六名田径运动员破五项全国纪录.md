Title: Good News from the Sports Field: Yang Yumin and Five Other Track and Field Athletes Break Five National Records

Author:

Date: 1958-08-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency Report: The sports field is filled with good news.
Yang Yumin and Five Other Track and Field Athletes Break Five National Records

    According to Xinhua News Agency, on the 10th and 11th, six track and field athletes broke five national records at the track and field performance competition of the National Middle School Student Sports Meeting held in Qingdao.

    On the 10th, famous female long jump athletes Yang Yumin and Liu Xingyu broke the national record of 5.54 meters with scores of 5.76 meters and 5.75 meters respectively. Sun Jiuyuan's discus throw reached 48.6 meters, 3.4 meters farther than the national record.

    On the 11th, outstanding track and field female athlete Wang Yi threw the javelin to a distance of 48 meters. This result is 66 centimeters higher than the national record of 47.34 meters she created last year. Zheng Renqiang's shot put reached 14.29 meters, breaking his own national record of 14.18 meters. Chen Zhengxiu's women's 800-meter run was 2 minutes 16.2 seconds, shortening the national record by one second.

### 运动场上捷报频传　杨玉敏等六名田径运动员破五项全国纪录

1958-08-13
第5版()
专栏：体育运动

　　运动场上捷报频传
　　杨玉敏等六名田径运动员破五项全国纪录
    据新华社讯　10日和11日，在青岛举行的全国中等学校学生田径运动大会表演赛中，有六个田径运动员打破了五项全国纪录。
    著名女子跳远运动员杨玉敏和刘兴玉在10日分别以五点七六公尺和五点七五公尺的成绩，打破了五点五四公尺的全国纪录。孙久远掷铁饼的成绩达到四十八点六公尺，比全国纪录远三点四公尺。
    11日，优秀田径女运动员王毅把标枪投掷到四十八公尺远的地方。这个成绩比她去年创造四十七公尺三十四公分的全国纪录，提高六十六公分。郑仁强推铅球的成绩达到十四点二九公尺，打破了他自己保持的十四点一八公尺的全国纪录。陈正绣的女子八百公尺跑成绩是二分十六秒二，比全国纪录缩短了一秒。
