Title: Indonesian Government Issues Statement Agreeing to Convene UN Emergency Meeting; Czechoslovak Government Demands Immediate Cessation of US-British Aggression
Author:
Date: 1958-08-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 7th August: Antara News Agency reports that the Indonesian government has reiterated its stance on the Middle East issue, which is that it believes the withdrawal of foreign troops from Lebanon and Jordan would help ease tensions there, and it agrees with the initial steps being taken to resolve the crisis through a UN emergency meeting or a summit.

A statement issued by the Indonesian Ministry of Information said that the Indonesian government is confident that the withdrawal of foreign troops from Lebanon and Jordan will contribute to efforts to ease tensions in that part of the world.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 7th August: A statement issued today by the Czechoslovak Ministry of Foreign Affairs stated that the Czechoslovak government agrees with the Soviet government's proposal to convene an emergency session of the United Nations General Assembly and has instructed the Czechoslovak Permanent Representative to the United Nations to visit the President of the Security Council and the Secretary-General of the United Nations to inform them of the Czechoslovak government's attitude towards the US-British aggression in the Near and Middle East.

The statement emphasized that Czechoslovakia will fully demand an immediate halt to the aggression and the withdrawal of all interventionist troops from Lebanon and Jordan to prevent further escalation of the aggression and to eliminate the risk of war in the Near and Middle East.

### 印度尼西亚政府发表声明  同意召开联大紧急会议  捷政府要求立即制止美英侵略

1958-08-08
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚政府发表声明
    同意召开联大紧急会议
    捷政府要求立即制止美英侵略
    据新华社雅加达7日电  据安塔拉通讯社报道，印度尼西亚政府重申它对中东问题的态度，这种态度就是：它认为外国军队撤出黎巴嫩和约旦将有助于缓和那里的紧张局势，它同意正在采取的为了通过联合国紧急会议或是最高级会议来消除这个危机的第一个步骤。
    由印度尼西亚情报部发表的声明说，印度尼西亚政府确信外国军队从黎巴嫩和约旦撤退将有助于缓和世界这个部分的紧张局势的努力。
    据新华社布拉格7日电  今天这里发表的捷克斯洛伐克外交部的一项声明说，捷克斯洛伐克政府同意苏联政府提出的召开联合国大会紧急会议的建议，并且已经指示捷克斯洛伐克驻联合国常驻代表拜访安全理事会主席和联合国秘书长，把捷克斯洛伐克政府对于美英侵略中近东的态度通知他们。
    声明强调指出，捷克斯洛伐克将全力要求立即制止侵略并且把一切干涉军撤出黎巴嫩和约旦，以防止侵略进一步扩大和消除中近东发生战争的危险。
