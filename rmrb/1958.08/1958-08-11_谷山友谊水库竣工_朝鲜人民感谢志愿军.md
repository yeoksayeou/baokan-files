Title: Gusan Friendship Reservoir Completed, Korean People Thank the Volunteer Army
Author:
Date: 1958-08-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 10th: The officers and soldiers of a certain unit of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army who participated in the construction of the Gusan Reservoir, along with over 5,000 people from all walks of life in Gusan County, solemnly held a completion ceremony at the reservoir construction site on the afternoon of the 9th.

Guo Rui, Chairman of the Huanghae North Province People's Committee, said in his speech at the completion ceremony that on behalf of the people of the entire province, he expressed his heartfelt gratitude and paid the highest tribute to the officers and soldiers of a certain unit of the Volunteer Army who built the reservoir. He announced that in order to forever commemorate your labor achievements and the friendship between the people of Korea and China, the Cabinet of the Republic has approved our proposal to name this project the "Gusan Friendship Reservoir."

Subsequently, Guo Rui presented certificates of appreciation to twenty-eight collective meritorious units and one hundred and forty-eight meritorious individuals from a certain unit of the Volunteer Army for their outstanding achievements in the construction of the Gusan Reservoir, and presented banners and reservoir commemorative medals to all officers and soldiers of the reservoir construction unit.

Zhang Xizhi, Major General, the commander of the Volunteer Army unit participating in the construction of the reservoir, said in his speech that on behalf of the officers and soldiers of the construction unit, he warmly thanked the Korean Workers' Party, the Republic Government, and the Korean people for the lofty honor and fraternal care they had given them.

After the completion ceremony, the people of eighteen villages in Gusan County brought wine, rice, and apples to the construction site to have a meal and drink with the Volunteer Army officers and soldiers who participated in the construction of the reservoir. Dancers from the Korean National Choi Seung-hee Dance Theater also performed wonderful programs for the construction unit at the construction site.

### 谷山友谊水库竣工  朝鲜人民感谢志愿军

1958-08-11
第4版()
专栏：

　  谷山友谊水库竣工
　  朝鲜人民感谢志愿军
    据新华社平壤10日电　中国人民志愿军参加修建谷山水库的某部官兵和谷山郡各界朝鲜人民共五千多人，9日下午在水库工地上隆重举行竣工典礼。
    黄海北道人民委员会委员长郭瑞在竣工典礼上讲话中，代表全道人民向建成水库的志愿军某部官兵表示衷心的感谢和致以崇高的荣誉。他宣布，我们为了永远纪念你们的劳动功勋和朝中两国人民的友谊，共和国内阁已经批准我们的建议，把这项工程命名为“谷山友谊水库”。
    接着，郭瑞分别向志愿军某部官兵在修建谷山水库中成绩卓越的二十八个集体立功的单位和一百四十八名功臣赠送感谢状，向水库施工部队全体官兵赠送了锦旗和水库纪念章。
    参加修建水库的志愿军某部首长张锡芝少将在讲话中，代表施工部队官兵热烈感谢朝鲜劳动党、共和国政府和朝鲜人民给予他们的崇高的荣誉和兄弟般的关怀。
    竣工典礼结束后，谷山郡十八个里的朝鲜人民，带了酒、饭、苹果，赶到工地上和参加修建水库的志愿军官兵一起聚餐畅饮。朝鲜国立崔承喜舞蹈剧场的舞蹈家们还在工地上为施工部队表演了精采节目。
