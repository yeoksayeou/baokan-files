Title: Soviet Ambassador to Iraq Arrives

Author:

Date: 1958-08-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, August 4th. TASS Baghdad: The Soviet Union's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Republic of Iraq, G. Ts. Zaitsev, and all the staff of the Soviet Embassy arrived in Baghdad on the 4th.

### 苏联驻伊拉克大使到任

1958-08-05
第2版()
专栏：

    苏联驻伊拉克大使到任
    据新华社4日讯　塔斯社巴格达讯：苏联驻伊拉克共和国特命全权大使格·齐·扎伊采夫和苏联大使馆的全体人员4日到达巴格达。
