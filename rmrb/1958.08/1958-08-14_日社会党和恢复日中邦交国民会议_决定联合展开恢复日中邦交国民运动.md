Title: Japanese Socialist Party and National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations Decide to Jointly Launch National Movement to Restore Japan-China Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1958-08-14
Page: 7

　　The Japanese Socialist Party and National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations
　　Decide to Jointly Launch National Movement to Restore Japan-China Diplomatic Relations
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 13th: The Japanese Socialist Party held a joint meeting today with the National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations to discuss how to break the current tense situation in Japan-China relations.
    The meeting decided to designate September and October as months for breaking the tense situation in Japan-China relations and restoring Japan-China diplomatic relations, and to actively launch a national movement.
    The meeting also decided to support the National Convention to Overcome the Crisis in Japan-China Relations to be held by the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan on October 4.
    In addition, the Japan Association of Manufacturers for Smooth Japan-China Economic Relations published a "White Paper on Japan-China Trade" on the 11th, demanding that the Kishi government liquidate its "Two Chinas" view and change its hostile attitude towards China.

### 日社会党和恢复日中邦交国民会议　决定联合展开恢复日中邦交国民运动

1958-08-14
第7版()
专栏：

　　日社会党和恢复日中邦交国民会议
　　决定联合展开恢复日中邦交国民运动
    据新华社13日讯　东京消息：日本社会党今天同恢复日中邦交国民会议举行联席会议，讨论了如何打开目前日中关系的紧张局面问题。
    会上决定：把9月和10月定为打开日中关系紧张局面和恢复日中邦交月，积极地展开国民运动。
    会上还决定支持日本工会总评议会将在10月4日举行的克服日中关系危机国民大会。
    此外，日本“打通日中经济关系厂商协议会”11日发表“日中贸易白皮书”，要求岸政府清算“两个中国”的看法，改变敌视中国的态度。
