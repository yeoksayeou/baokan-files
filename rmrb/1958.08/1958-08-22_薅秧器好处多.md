Title: The Weeding Device Has Many Advantages
Author:
Date: 1958-08-22
Page: 3

　　The weeding device has many advantages
　　The weeding device, has many advantages,
　　Replaces hand weeding and foot rubbing;
　　Light and nimble, quick to operate,
　　Yielding more than a thousand catties of grain per mu.
　　　　　　　　　　Guizhou Pan County

### 薅秧器好处多

1958-08-22
第3版()
专栏：

　　薅秧器好处多
    薅秧器，好处多，
    代替手薅和脚搓；
    轻巧灵便动作快，
    每亩产粮一千多。
                贵州盘县
