Title: Albania and Iraq Decide to Establish Diplomatic Relations; My Country's Embassy in Iraq Established in Baghdad
Author:
Date: 1958-08-31
Page: 3

Albania and Iraq Decide to Establish Diplomatic Relations
    My Country's Embassy in Iraq Established in Baghdad
    Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, August 29th: Guo Dakai, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Republic of Iraq, arrived in Baghdad this morning.
    The Chinese Embassy has been established in Baghdad today.
    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, August 29th: According to the Albanian Telegraph Agency, the government of the People's Republic of Albania and the government of the Republic of Iraq, in order to strengthen the friendly relations between the two countries, has decided to establish diplomatic ties and exchange diplomatic representatives at the ambassadorial level.

### 阿伊两国决定建交  我驻伊拉克使馆在巴格达设立

1958-08-31
第3版()
专栏：

　　阿伊两国决定建交
    我驻伊拉克使馆在巴格达设立
    新华社巴格达29日电　中华人民共和国驻伊拉克共和国大使馆临时代办郭达凯今天早上到达巴格达。
    中国大使馆今天已经在巴格达设立。
    新华社地拉那29日电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府和伊拉克共和国政府为了加强两国的友好关系，已经决定建立两国间的外交关系并且互派大使级的外交代表。
