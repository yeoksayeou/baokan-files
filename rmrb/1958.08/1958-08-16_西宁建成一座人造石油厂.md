Title: Xining Builds a Synthetic Petroleum Plant
Author: Cheng Guangyuan
Date: 1958-08-16
Page: 3

　　Xining Builds a Synthetic Petroleum Plant

　　The Xining Oil Shale Refinery, a synthetic petroleum plant built in eight days with an annual output of 300 tons, produced crude oil on August 2nd.

　　According to the design, the products of this factory include: kerosene, light diesel, heavy diesel, crude wax, ammonium sulfate, petroleum coke, shale ash, etc. The kerosene produced by this factory annually can supply lighting for one thousand agricultural cooperatives for seventeen months; the light diesel can supply twenty-one (standard) tractors for one year; the heavy diesel can supply thirty 20-horsepower ships for one year; and the ammonium sulfate produced can fertilize nine thousand mu of farmland. In addition, petroleum coke can be used for casting, and shale ash can be used to make cement, etc.

　　Synthetic petroleum plants like this can be built not only by counties, but also by townships and communes. Now, the entire Qinghai province is preparing to universally build such factories.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Cheng Guangyuan)

### 西宁建成一座人造石油厂
程光远
1958-08-16
第3版()
专栏：

　　西宁建成一座人造石油厂
    八天建成的一座年产三百吨人造石油工厂——西宁油页岩炼油厂，在8月2日炼出了原油。
    根据设计，这个厂生产的产品有：汽灯油、轻柴油、重柴油、粗腊、硫酸铵和石油焦、页岩灰等。这个厂每年生产的汽灯油可以供给一千户的农业社照明十七个月；轻柴油可供二十一台（标准台）拖拉机使用一年；重柴油可供二十马力的轮船三十艘用一年；生产的硫酸铵可以肥田九千亩。另外，石油焦可以作翻砂用，页岩灰可以制水泥等。
    像这样的人造石油厂，不仅县可以办，乡、社也可以办。现在青海全省准备普遍办这样的工厂。
                    （程光远）
