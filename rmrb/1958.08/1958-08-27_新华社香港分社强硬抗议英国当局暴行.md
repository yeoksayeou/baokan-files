Title: Xinhua News Agency Hong Kong Branch Strongly Protests Atrocities of British Authorities
Author:
Date: 1958-08-27
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency Hong Kong Branch Strongly Protests Atrocities of British Authorities
    Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, August 26th. This morning, Xinhua News Agency Hong Kong Branch reporters Huang Wenfang and Situ Qiang were unreasonably beaten and humiliated by British police while conducting interviews at the Chung Wah Middle School. The Hong Kong Branch immediately sent representatives to the British authorities in Hong Kong to lodge a strong protest.

### 新华社香港分社强硬抗议英国当局暴行

1958-08-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　新华社香港分社强硬抗议英国当局暴行
    新华社香港26日电　新华通讯社香港分社记者黄文放、司徒强今天早晨在中华中学采访时，被英国警察无理殴辱，本社香港分社当即派代表向香港英国当局提出强硬抗议。
