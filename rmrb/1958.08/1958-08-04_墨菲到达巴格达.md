Title: Murphy Arrives in Baghdad
Author:
Date: 1958-08-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, August 3rd: The American President's "Special Political Representative," Murphy, arrived here from Beirut on the afternoon of the 2nd.

	After his arrival, he held a two-hour meeting with Iraqi Minister of State Guidance, Shanashir.

	Murphy departed for Baghdad a few hours after the United States was forced to announce its recognition of the Republic of Iraq.

	According to United Press International, Murphy's mission is to probe the Iraqi government's attitude towards the "Baghdad Pact."

### 墨菲到达巴格达

1958-08-04
第2版()
专栏：

    墨菲到达巴格达
    新华社巴格达3日电　美国总统“特别政治代表”墨菲在2日下午从贝鲁特到达这里。
    他在到达以后，就同伊拉克国家指导部长尚沙尔进行了两小时的会谈。
    墨菲是在美国被迫宣布承认伊拉克共和国几小时以后动身前往巴格达的。
    据合众国际社说，墨菲的任务是要试探伊拉克政府对“巴格达条约”的态度。
