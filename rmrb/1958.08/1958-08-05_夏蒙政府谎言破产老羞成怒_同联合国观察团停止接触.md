Title: Shamun Government, Exposed as Liars, Flies into a Rage and Ceases Contact with UN Observer Group
Author:
Date: 1958-08-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, August 4th. Tass Beirut, August 4th: According to the "News" report, the Lebanese government has decided to cease contact with the United Nations Observer Group in Lebanon. The newspaper pointed out that the Lebanese government's decision is related to the second report of the United Nations Observer Group.

The second report of the United Nations Observer Group refuted the lies about the United Arab Republic's "interference" in Lebanon's internal affairs.

### 夏蒙政府谎言破产老羞成怒  同联合国观察团停止接触

1958-08-05
第2版()
专栏：

    夏蒙政府谎言破产老羞成怒
    同联合国观察团停止接触
    新华社4日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特4日讯：据“新闻报”报道，黎巴嫩政府决定停止同联合国在黎巴嫩的观察团接触。这家报纸指出，黎巴嫩政府作出这一决定同联合国观察团的第二次报告有关。
    联合国观察团的第二次报告驳斥了关于阿拉伯联合共和国“干涉”黎巴嫩内政的谎言。
