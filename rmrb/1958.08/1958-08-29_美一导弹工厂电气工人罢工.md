Title: US Missile Factory Electrical Workers Strike
Author:
Date: 1958-08-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, August 28th: 1,500 electrical workers at the "Redstone" Arsenal, a US missile manufacturing plant, went on strike on the 25th. The strike has forced the suspension of a construction project worth more than $32 million that the factory is undertaking.

The striking workers are demanding higher wages and improved working conditions.

### 美一导弹工厂电气工人罢工

1958-08-29
第5版()
专栏：

    美一导弹工厂电气工人罢工
    据新华社28日讯　纽约消息：美国制造导弹的“红石”兵工厂的一千五百名电气工人在25日举行罢工。罢工使得这个工厂正在进行的一项价值三千二百多万美元的修建工程被迫停止了。
    罢工的工人要求提高工资和改善工作条件。
