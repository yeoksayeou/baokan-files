Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Message to 17th Congress of Uruguayan Communist Party, Wishing Victory in the Struggle for Peace, Independence, and Democracy
Author:
Date: 1958-08-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 17th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram of congratulations to the 17th Congress of the Uruguayan Communist Party, which was held in Montevideo on August 15th. The full text of the congratulatory telegram is as follows: To the National Committee of the Uruguayan Communist Party and all comrades of the 17th Congress of the Uruguayan Communist Party:

    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm, fraternal congratulations to the 17th Congress of the Uruguayan Communist Party.

    For more than 30 years, the Uruguayan Communist Party has consistently struggled to safeguard Uruguay's national independence and to win democratic freedoms and improve the lives of the Uruguayan people, and has achieved remarkable successes. Today, as American imperialism faces an economic crisis, frantically engages in arms expansion and war preparations, carries out armed aggression in the Middle East, and intensifies its political intervention and economic plunder of Uruguay and other Latin American countries, the Uruguayan Communist Party unites the Uruguayan people to wage a struggle against the aggressive policies of American imperialism, which will surely make new contributions to safeguarding the national independence of Uruguay and other Latin American countries and to the cause of world peace and human progress.

    We congratulate you on your growing achievements in the struggle to defend world peace, safeguard Uruguay's national independence and democratic freedoms, and strive for the unity of the working class. We wish the Congress success!

                      The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, August 14, 1958

### 中共中央电贺乌共十七次代表大会　祝为和平独立民主的斗争得胜

1958-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央电贺乌共十七次代表大会
　　祝为和平独立民主的斗争得胜
    新华社17日讯　乌拉圭共产党第十七次代表大会8月15日在蒙得维的亚举行，中国共产党中央委员会特去电祝贺，贺电全文如下：乌拉圭共产党全国委员会并转乌拉圭共产党第十七次代表大会全体同志：
    中国共产党中央委员会向乌拉圭共产党第十七次代表大会致以热烈的、兄弟般的祝贺。
    乌拉圭共产党三十余年来一贯地为维护乌拉圭的民族独立、争取乌拉圭人民的民主自由和生活的改善而斗争，并且取得了卓越的成就。今天，当美帝国主义面临经济危机，疯狂进行备战，武装侵略中东，并且加紧对乌拉圭和拉丁美洲其他国家实行政治干预和经济掠夺的时候，乌拉圭共产党团结乌拉圭人民，进行反对美帝国主义侵略政策的斗争，对于维护乌拉圭和拉丁美洲各国的民族独立，争取世界和平和人类进步的事业必将作出新的贡献。
    祝贺你们在捍卫世界和平、保卫乌拉圭民族独立与民主自由、以及争取工人阶级团结的斗争中，获得与日俱增的成就。祝贺大会成功！
                      中国共产党中央委员会　1958年8月14日
