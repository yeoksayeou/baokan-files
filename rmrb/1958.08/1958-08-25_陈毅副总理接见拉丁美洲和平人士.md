Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Latin American Peace Advocates
Author:
Date: 1958-08-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 24th: Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi received Peruvian peace advocate Ernesto More and Venezuelan peace advocate Belén San Juan this morning and held friendly talks.

Those present at the meeting included Liao Chengzhi, Zhang Zhixiang, and Zhu Ziqi.

### 陈毅副总理接见拉丁美洲和平人士

1958-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见拉丁美洲和平人士
    新华社24日讯　陈毅副总理兼外交部长在今天上午接见了秘鲁和平人士埃尔内斯托·摩尔和委内瑞拉和平人士贝伦·圣胡安，进行了友好的谈话。
    陪同接见的，有廖承志、张致祥、朱子奇等人。
