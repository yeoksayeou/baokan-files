　　The People's Commune is Good
    After the establishment of the people's commune, all means of production and labor are unified and allocated and used by the commune, which can further develop industrial and agricultural production and realize the policy of taking both industry and agriculture as the mainstay.

### 人民公社好（图片）

1958-08-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　人民公社好
    人民公社建立后，一切生产资料和劳力归公社统一调配和使用，就能进一步发展工农业生产，实现工农业并举的方针。　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　（天津美术出版社供稿）
