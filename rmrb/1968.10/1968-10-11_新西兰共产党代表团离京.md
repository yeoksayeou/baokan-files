Title: Delegation of the New Zealand Communist Party Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-10-11
Edition: 4

The delegation of the New Zealand Communist Party, which came to China at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to participate in the National Day celebrations, departed from Beijing by plane today. They will visit and tour other places before returning to their country.

### 新西兰共产党代表团离京

1968-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    新西兰共产党代表团离京
    新华社十日讯　应中共中央邀请前来我国参加国庆活动的新西兰共产党代表团，今天乘飞机离开北京。他们将去外地参观访问后回国。
