Title: Forever Be an Ordinary Laborer
Author: Lin Wangmin
Date: 1968-10-09
Edition: 4

The latest directives shine with golden light,
Revolutionary cadres sing in unison,
Rushing to the forefront of labor,
Radiant sunlight warms the heart.

Forever be an ordinary laborer,
The three great styles are fully promoted,
Seriously carry out the struggle, criticism, and reform,
Be a pioneer in fighting the self and criticizing revisionism.

### 永做普通劳动者
林望民
1968-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    永做普通劳动者
    解放军某部　林望民
    最新指示闪金光，
    革命干部齐欢唱，
    奔赴劳动第一线，
    灿烂阳光暖心上。
    永做普通劳动者，
    三大作风大发扬，
    认真搞好斗、批、改，
    斗私批修当闯将。
