	Mao Zedong's Quotations
	It is a very good opportunity for cadres to go down to the countryside and engage in manual labor, except for the elderly, the weak, the sick, and the disabled. All cadres should do this. Cadres in office should also go down to the countryside in batches to engage in manual labor.

    毛主席语录
