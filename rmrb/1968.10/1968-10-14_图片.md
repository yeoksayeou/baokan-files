Title: Perpetuating the Revolutionary Spirit
Author: Shāo Tiānchéng
Date: 1968-10-14
Edition: 2

The Chairman of the Xiangtan Region Revolutionary Committee and Commander of the Xiangtan Military Sub-district, Comrade Wu Haiching, along with Deputy Chairman and revolutionary cadre Zhang Yaoming and Mao Shifeng, heeding the great leader Chairman Mao's instruction that "broad cadres going down to labor is a great opportunity for them to learn anew," have gone down to Shaoshan People's Commune to engage in productive labor and learn anew from the poor and lower-middle peasants.
