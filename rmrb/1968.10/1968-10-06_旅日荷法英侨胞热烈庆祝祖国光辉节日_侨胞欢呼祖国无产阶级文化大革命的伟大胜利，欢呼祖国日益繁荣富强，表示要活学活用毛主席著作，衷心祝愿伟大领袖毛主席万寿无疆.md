Title: Overseas Chinese in Japan, Holland, France, and England Celebrate National Day with Great Enthusiasm
Author:
Date: 1968-10-06
Edition: 6

Overseas Chinese in Japan, Holland, France, and England enthusiastically celebrate the glorious national day. Overseas Chinese cheer the great victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in their motherland, cheer the increasing prosperity and strength of their motherland, and express their determination to study and apply Chairman Mao's works. They sincerely wish for the long life of the great leader Chairman Mao.

According to Xinhua News Agency, the Tokyo Chinese Association held a grand meeting in the afternoon to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

More than 1,300 patriotic overseas Chinese from Tokyo and surrounding areas attended the celebration.

Leaders from various friendly organizations in Japan were also invited to attend the meeting.

Gan Wenfang, the president of the Tokyo Chinese Association, spoke at the meeting. He enthusiastically hailed the establishment of revolutionary committees in all provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions (excluding Taiwan Province).

The attendees repeatedly raised their arms and shouted, "Long live the complete victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!" "Long live the fighting friendship between the peoples of China and Japan!" "We must liberate Taiwan!" "Long live the invincible Mao Zedong Thought!" "Long live Chairman Mao for ten thousand years, ten thousand ten thousand years!"

One thousand five hundred overseas Chinese living in the Netherlands gathered in Amsterdam, the capital of the Netherlands, to enthusiastically celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The leader of the National Day Preparatory Committee of Overseas Chinese in the Netherlands first expressed their sincere wishes for the long life of the great leader Chairman Mao on behalf of the overseas Chinese in the Netherlands. He pointed out that the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, personally initiated and led by Chairman Mao, has achieved decisive victories. All provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions in China, except Taiwan Province, have established revolutionary committees. The motherland is red all over, which is a great victory of the invincible Mao Zedong Thought. He expressed that the overseas Chinese in the Netherlands will definitely unite more closely, and love the great leader Chairman Mao and the great socialist motherland more.

The acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese embassy in the Netherlands, Liao Hechun, was invited to speak at the meeting. Liao Hechun emphasized the good situation of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in the motherland and called on overseas Chinese in the Netherlands to study Mao Zedong Thought and Chairman Mao's works more diligently.

After the meeting, the Chinese color documentary film "The Red Lantern" with piano accompaniment was screened, which was warmly welcomed by the overseas Chinese.

On September 3, the Chinese Embassy in France held a reception in Paris for overseas Chinese to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

More than 600 patriotic overseas Chinese attended the reception. Some overseas Chinese came specially from Marseille, Lyon, Nantes, and Lille.

At the reception, overseas Chinese sincerely wished for the long life of the great leader Chairman Mao! They congratulated the great victories achieved by the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and wished for the prosperity and strength of the motherland. They expressed their determination to study and apply Chairman Mao's works and catch up with the pace of the people of the motherland.

On the afternoon of September 29, overseas Chinese in France held a celebration meeting in Paris. More than 700 people attended the meeting. The acting charge d'affaires of China in France, Yi Suzhi, and the leaders of the overseas Chinese community spoke at the meeting. Overseas Chinese enthusiastically hailed the great victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in the great motherland, hailed the great victory of Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line, firmly supported the motherland's determination to liberate Taiwan, and sincerely wished for the long life of the great leader Chairman Mao. The meeting also passed a telegram of respect to Chairman Mao. After the meeting, the color documentary film "The Red Lantern" with piano accompaniment was screened, which was warmly welcomed by the overseas Chinese in France.

On September 29, the acting charge d'affaires of China in the UK, Shen Ping, held a reception for patriotic overseas Chinese living in the UK. Nearly 800 patriotic overseas Chinese from various parts of the UK repeatedly shouted, "Long live the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!" "Long live the great leader Chairman Mao, ten thousand years, ten thousand ten thousand years!" In recent days, patriotic overseas Chinese from various parts of the UK have been rehearsing artistic performances praising Chairman Mao and the great victories of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, and actively holding activities to celebrate the great national day.

On October 2, the Chinese embassy in the Netherlands held a national day reception for overseas Chinese. Some patriotic leaders and young overseas Chinese spoke at the meeting, expressing their boundless love for the great socialist motherland and the great leader Chairman Mao.

### 旅日荷法英侨胞热烈庆祝祖国光辉节日  侨胞欢呼祖国无产阶级文化大革命的伟大胜利，欢呼祖国日益繁荣富强，表示要活学活用毛主席著作，衷心祝愿伟大领袖毛主席万寿无疆

1968-10-06
第6版()
专栏：

    旅日荷法英侨胞热烈庆祝祖国光辉节日
    侨胞欢呼祖国无产阶级文化大革命的伟大胜利，欢呼祖国日益繁荣富强，表示要活学活用毛主席著作，衷心祝愿伟大领袖毛主席万寿无疆
    据新华社讯　东京华侨总会一日下午举行盛大集会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。
    东京及附近地区的旅日爱国侨胞一千三百多人出席了庆祝大会。
    日本各友好团体的负责人也应邀出席了大会。
    东京华侨总会会长甘文芳在会上讲了话。他热烈欢呼全国各省、市、自治区（除台湾省外）全部成立革命委员会。
    到会群众不断振臂高呼：“无产阶级文化大革命的全面胜利万岁！”“中日两国人民的战斗友谊万岁！”“我们一定要解放台湾！”“战无不胜的毛泽东思想胜利万岁！”“敬祝毛主席万寿无疆！万寿无疆！”
    旅居荷兰的华侨一千五百人，一日在荷兰首都阿姆斯特丹集会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。旅荷华侨国庆筹备委员会负责人在大会上首先代表荷兰华侨敬祝伟大领袖毛主席万寿无疆。他指出，毛主席亲自发动和领导的无产阶级文化大革命已经赢得了决定性的胜利，全中国除台湾省外的各省、市、自治区全部成立了革命委员会，祖国山河一片红，这是战无不胜的毛泽东思想的伟大胜利。他表示旅荷华侨决心更加紧密地团结起来，更加热爱伟大领袖毛主席和伟大的社会主义祖国。
    中华人民共和国驻荷兰代办处临时代办廖和叔应邀在会上讲了话。廖和叔着重谈到祖国无产阶级文化大革命的大好形势，号召旅荷华侨更加努力地学习毛泽东思想，学习毛主席的著作。
    会后放映了中国彩色纪录影片钢琴伴唱《红灯记》，受到华侨热烈的欢迎。
    中国驻法国大使馆三日在巴黎为旅法侨胞举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。
    参加招待会的爱国华侨共六百多人。有些华侨是从马赛、里昂、南特、里耳等地专程赶来的。
    侨胞们在招待会上衷心祝愿伟大领袖毛主席万寿无疆！祝贺祖国无产阶级文化大革命所取得的伟大胜利，祝愿祖国繁荣富强。他们表示一定要努力活学活用毛主席著作，赶上祖国人民的前进步伐。
    九月二十九日下午，旅法侨胞在巴黎召开了庆祝大会。参加大会的有七百多人。中国驻法国临时代办易素之和侨领都在大会上讲了话。侨胞们热情欢呼伟大祖国无产阶级文化大革命的伟大胜利，欢呼毛主席无产阶级革命路线的伟大胜利，坚决拥护祖国一定要解放台湾，并热烈祝愿伟大领袖毛主席万寿无疆。大会还通过了给毛主席的致敬电。会后放映了彩色纪录影片钢琴伴唱《红灯记》，受到旅法侨胞的热烈欢迎。
    中国驻英国临时代办沈平在九月二十九日为旅居英国的爱国华侨举行了招待会。来自英国各地的爱国侨胞近八百人在招待会上一遍又一遍地高呼：“无产阶级文化大革命万岁！”“伟大领袖毛主席万岁，万岁，万万岁！”英国各地爱国华侨连日来还赶排歌颂毛主席和无产阶级文化大革命伟大胜利的文艺节目，纷纷举行庆祝祖国伟大节日的活动。
    二日，中国驻荷兰代办处为华侨举行国庆招待会。一些爱国侨领和华侨青年在会上讲话，表示他们对伟大社会主义祖国和对伟大领袖毛主席的无限热爱。
