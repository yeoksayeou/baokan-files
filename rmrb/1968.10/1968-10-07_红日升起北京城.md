Title: The Red Sun Rises Over Beijing
Author: Zhang Yulian
Date: 1968-10-07
Edition: 6

The red flag dances in the east wind,
The entire nation is red.
Billions of people celebrate National Day,
Thinking of Chairman Mao in their hearts.
A red sun shines over the vast sky,
The sky is red, the earth is red, and the people are even redder.
The red sun rises over Beijing,
Long live, long live Chairman Mao!

### 红日升起北京城
张玉良
1968-10-07
第6版()
专栏：

    红日升起北京城
    河北交河县寺门村公社社员　张玉良
    红旗舞东风，
    全国一片红。
    亿万人民庆国庆，
    心中想念毛泽东。
    一轮红日照长空，
    天红地红人更红。
    红日升起北京城，
    万岁万岁毛泽东！
