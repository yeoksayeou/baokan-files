Title: U.S. Warships and Military Aircraft Intrude into Our Territorial Waters and Airspace
Author:
Date: 1968-10-24
Edition: 5

U.S. warships and military aircraft intrude into our territorial waters and airspace

We issue the 464th serious warning

Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd

On October 22nd, from 5:04 AM to 11:44 AM, one U.S. warship intruded into our territorial waters east of the Matsu Islands in Fujian Province. On the same day, from 1:52 PM to 2:03 PM, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into our airspace over Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands, Guangdong Province. Regarding the U.S. military's provocative activities, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on instructions, issues the 464th serious warning.

### 美国军舰和军用飞机侵入我领海领空  我提出第四百六十四次严重警告

1968-10-24
第5版()
专栏：

    美国军舰和军用飞机侵入我领海领空
    我提出第四百六十四次严重警告
    新华社二十三日讯　十月二十二日五时零四分至十一时四十四分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省马祖列岛以东地区领海。同日十三时五十二分至十四时零三分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛地区领空。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅活动，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百六十四次严重警告。
