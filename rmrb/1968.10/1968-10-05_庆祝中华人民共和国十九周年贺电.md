Title: Celebrating the 19th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1968-10-05
Edition: 5

	Celebrating the 19th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China

	Telegram of Congratulations from the President of Zambia

	Xinhua News Agency, October 3rd: President Kenneth Kaunda of Zambia sent a telegram to congratulate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

	President Kaunda's telegram said: On the grand occasion of the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, I, on behalf of the government and people of the Republic of Zambia, extend our heartfelt congratulations to the Chinese people. We, the people of Zambia, wish the Chinese people and government prosperity and happiness. We wish that the deep friendship between the peoples of China and Zambia, forged in the common struggle against imperialism and old and new colonialism, will continue to strengthen and develop.

	Telegrams of Congratulations from Some National Leaders and Governments

	Xinhua News Agency, October 2nd: Our national leaders and government continue to receive telegrams of congratulations from leaders and governments of various countries on the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

	The following have sent telegrams: King Savang Vatthana of Laos, Emir Sabah Al-Salim Al-Sabah of Kuwait, Crown Prince and Prime Minister Jaber Al-Ahmad Al-Sabah of Kuwait, Speaker of the National Assembly of the Somali Republic Sheikh Mukhtar Muhammad Hussein, Prime Minister Mauno Koivisto of the Republic of Finland, Prime Minister Tage Erlander of the Kingdom of Sweden, Prime Minister Per Borten of the Kingdom of Norway, Prime Minister Hilmar Baunsgaard of the Kingdom of Denmark, President of the Federal Council Willy Spühler of Switzerland, the Government of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic, Chairman of the Federal Executive Council Mika Špiljak of the Socialist Federal Republic of Yugoslavia, the Presidium of the Great People's Khural and the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, the Revolutionary Government of Workers and Peasants of the Hungarian People's Republic, the State Council and the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic, the State Council and the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic, the Prime Minister of the Republic of India, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union.

### 庆祝中华人民共和国十九周年贺电

1968-10-05
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝中华人民共和国十九周年贺电
    赞比亚总统的贺电
    新华社三日讯　赞比亚总统卡翁达致电祝贺中华人民共和国成立十九周年。
    卡翁达总统的电报说：在中华人民共和国成立十九周年的盛大节日的时候，我代表赞比亚共和国政府和人民，向中国人民表示衷心的祝贺。我们赞比亚人民祝中国人民和政府繁荣和幸福。祝中国和赞比亚两国人民在反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义的共同斗争中结成的深厚友谊不断得到加强和发展。
    一些国家领导人和政府的贺电
    新华社二日讯　我国领导人和我国政府继续收到各国领导人和政府发来的祝贺中华人民共和国成立十九周年的电报。
    发来电报的有：老挝国王西萨旺·瓦达纳，科威特国埃米尔萨巴赫·萨利姆·萨巴赫，科威特国王储兼首相贾比尔·艾哈迈德·贾比尔，索马里共和国国民议会议长谢赫·穆克塔尔·穆罕默德·侯赛因，芬兰共和国总理毛诺·科伊维斯托，瑞典王国政府首相塔格·埃兰德，挪威王国政府首相佩尔·博顿，丹麦王国政府首相希尔马·包恩斯戈尔，瑞士联邦委员会主席维利·斯普勒，捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国政府，南斯拉夫社会主义联邦共和国联邦执行委员会主席米卡·什皮利亚克，蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团和部长会议，保加利亚人民共和国部长会议，匈牙利人民共和国工农革命政府，德意志民主共和国国务委员会和部长会议，波兰人民共和国国务委员会和部长会议，印度共和国总理，苏联最高苏维埃主席团和部长会议。
