Title: Chinese Charge d'Affaires and Military Attache Pay a Visit to the Great Leader of the Albanian People
Author:
Date: 1968-10-19
Edition: 5

Chinese Charge d'Affaires and Military Attache Pay a Visit to the Great Leader of the Albanian People

Most Sincere Greetings to Comrade Enver Hoxha on His 60th Birthday

Comrade Hoxha Had an Extremely Warm and Friendly Conversation with Comrade Li Tingquan and Others

	NEW YORK, Oct. 17 (Xinhua) -- The Chinese Charge d'Affaires in Albania, Li Tingquan, and Military Attache Li Zhongxuan, on the 16th, paid a visit to the great leader of the Albanian people, the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Party of Labor of Albania, Comrade Enver Hoxha, on behalf of the Chinese Embassy in Albania and Chinese comrades working and studying in Albania, to offer their most sincere greetings to Comrade Hoxha on his 60th birthday.

	Comrade Hoxha had an extremely warm and friendly conversation with Comrade Li Tingquan and others.

	Present at the greeting were: Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Party of Labor of Albania and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania, Comrade Shehu; Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat, Comrades Kaba and Alia; and Central Committee members, Comrades Ne Hoxha and Be Shutula.

### 我驻阿临时代办、武官等拜会阿人民伟大领袖  最衷心祝贺霍查同志六十寿辰  霍查同志同李庭荃等同志进行了极其亲切友好的谈话

1968-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

    我驻阿临时代办、武官等拜会阿人民伟大领袖
    最衷心祝贺霍查同志六十寿辰
    霍查同志同李庭荃等同志进行了极其亲切友好的谈话
    新华社地拉那十七日电　中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办李庭荃、武官李钟玄等，十六日拜会了阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记恩维尔·霍查同志，代表中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆，以及在阿尔巴尼亚工作和学习的中国同志，最衷心地祝贺霍查同志六十寿辰。
    霍查同志同李庭荃等同志进行了极其亲切友好的谈话。
    祝贺时，在座的有：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席谢胡，劳动党中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记卡博和阿利雅，劳动党中央委员涅·霍查和贝·什图拉。
