Title: Chinese Embassies in Czechoslovakia, Yugoslavia, Bulgaria, East Germany, Poland, and the Soviet Union Hold National Day Receptions
Author:
Date: 1968-10-05
Edition: 5

Chinese embassies in Czechoslovakia, Yugoslavia, Bulgaria, East Germany, Poland, and the Soviet Union held receptions to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

The Chinese Embassy in Czechoslovakia, headed by Acting Charge d'Affaires Yang Zhongchao, held a reception on the evening of September 30 to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Representatives from relevant sectors in Czechoslovakia attended the reception. Diplomatic envoys from several countries stationed in Czechoslovakia also attended.

The Chinese Embassy in Yugoslavia, headed by Acting Charge d'Affaires Yu Lizhen, held a reception on the evening of September 30 to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Representatives from relevant sectors in Yugoslavia attended the reception. Diplomatic envoys from several countries stationed in Yugoslavia, as well as some Asian, African, and Latin American students studying in Yugoslavia, also attended.

The Chinese Embassy in Bulgaria, headed by Acting Charge d'Affaires Wang Benzuo, held a reception on the evening of September 30 to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Attendees included representatives from relevant sectors in Bulgaria, people from various circles, and diplomatic envoys from several countries stationed in Bulgaria.

The Chinese Embassy in the German Democratic Republic, headed by Acting Charge d'Affaires Liu Pu, held a reception on October 1 to enthusiastically celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Representatives from relevant sectors in the German Democratic Republic, as well as envoys and diplomatic representatives from various countries stationed in the German Democratic Republic, attended the reception. A color documentary film of the piano-accompanied opera "The Red Lantern" was screened at the event.

The Chinese Embassy in Poland, headed by Acting Charge d'Affaires Chen Dong, held a reception on September 30 to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Representatives from relevant sectors in Poland attended the reception. Diplomatic envoys from several countries stationed in Poland, as well as some Asian, African, and Latin American students studying in Poland, also attended.

The Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, headed by Acting Charge d'Affaires An Zhiyuan, held a reception on the evening of October 1 to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Attendees included representatives from relevant sectors in the Soviet Union and diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in the Soviet Union.

At the reception, diplomatic envoys from friendly countries repeatedly raised their glasses to toast the great holiday of the Chinese people.

On the evening of September 28, the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union also held a film reception to entertain Chinese residents in Moscow and their family members.

### 我驻捷南保德等使馆举行国庆招待会

1968-10-05
第5版()
专栏：

    我驻捷南保德等使馆举行国庆招待会
    据新华社讯　中国驻捷、南、保、德、波、苏等国大使馆分别举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。
    中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使馆临时代办杨仲超，九月三十日晚举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。
    捷克斯洛伐克有关方面人士出席了招待会。一些国家驻捷克斯洛伐克的外交使节也出席了招待会。
    中国驻南斯拉夫大使馆临时代办于立暄一日晚举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。
    南斯拉夫有关方面负责人出席了招待会。
    一些国家驻南斯拉夫的外交使节和一些在南斯拉夫的亚、非、拉留学生也出席了招待会。
    中国驻保加利亚大使馆临时代办王本祚九月三十日晚举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。
    出席招待会的有，保加利亚有关方面负责人、各界人士和一些国家驻保加利亚的外交使节等。
    中国驻德意志民主共和国大使馆临时代办刘溥一日举行招待会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。
    德意志民主共和国有关方面负责人以及各国驻德意志民主共和国使节和外交代表出席了招待会。会上放映了钢琴伴唱《红灯记》的彩色纪录影片。
    中国驻波兰大使馆临时代办陈东九月三十日举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。
    波兰有关方面负责人出席了招待会。
    一些国家驻波兰的外交使节和一些在波兰的亚、非、拉留学生也出席了招待会。
    中国驻苏联大使馆临时代办安致远一日晚举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。
    出席招待会的有苏联有关方面负责人和各国驻苏联的外交使节等。
    在招待会上，各友好国家的外交使节一再举杯热烈祝贺中国人民的伟大节日。
    九月二十八日晚，中国驻苏联大使馆还举行电影招待会，招待中国在莫斯科的华侨及其家属。
