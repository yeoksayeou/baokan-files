Title: Chinese Government and Military Delegation Arrives in Guinea
Author:
Date: 1968-10-04
Edition: 6

A delegation from the People's Republic of China, led by Zhang Ting, arrived in Conakry on September 29th by airplane at the invitation of the Guinean government to participate in the celebrations of the 10th anniversary of the independence of the Republic of Guinea.

### 我政府和军事代表团到几内亚

1968-10-04
第6版()
专栏：

    我政府和军事代表团到几内亚
    据新华社科纳克里三十日电　由张挺率领的中华人民共和国政府和军事代表团，应几内亚政府的邀请前来参加几内亚共和国独立十周年庆祝活动，九月二十九日乘飞机到达科纳克里。
