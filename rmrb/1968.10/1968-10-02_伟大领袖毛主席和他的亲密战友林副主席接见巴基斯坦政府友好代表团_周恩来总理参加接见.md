Title: Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin Receive Pakistani Government Friendly Delegation
Author:
Date: 1968-10-02
Edition: 3

Our most beloved great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin today received on Tiananmen the advisor to Pakistani President Ayub Khan, Sayyid Fida Hasan, and the main members of the Pakistani government friendly delegation led by him: Sayyid Fida Hasan's wife, West Pakistan Minister of Communications Muhammad Khan Junejo, East Pakistan Minister of Law Abdul Haye Choudhury, and had a friendly conversation with them. Premier Zhou Enlai participated in the reception.

	Our most beloved great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin, on October 1st, received on Tiananmen Gate Tower the advisor to Pakistani President Ayub Khan, Sayyid Fida Hasan (third from the left), and the main members of the Pakistani government friendly delegation led by him. The photo shows a group photo taken during the reception.  [New China News Agency Photographer]

### 伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林副主席接见巴基斯坦政府友好代表团  周恩来总理参加接见

1968-10-02
第3版()
专栏：

    伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林副主席接见巴基斯坦政府友好代表团
    周恩来总理参加接见
    新华社一日讯　我们最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席，今天在天安门上接见巴基斯坦总统阿尤布·汗的顾问赛义德·菲达·哈桑和由他率领的巴基斯坦政府友好代表团主要成员：赛义德·菲达·哈桑夫人，西巴交通部长穆罕默德·汗·朱奈乔，东巴法律部长阿卜杜勒·海耶·乔杜里，同他们进行了友好的谈话。周恩来总理参加了接见。

    我们最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席，十月一日在天安门城楼上接见巴基斯坦总统阿尤布·汗的顾问赛义德·菲达·哈桑（左三）和由他率领的巴基斯坦政府友好代表团主要成员。图为接见时合影。                  新华社记者摄
