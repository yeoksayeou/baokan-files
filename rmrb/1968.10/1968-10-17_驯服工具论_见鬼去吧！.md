Title: To Hell with the "Tame Tool Theory"!
Author: Zhāoyáng
Date: 1968-10-17
Edition: 2

The great founder of the Communist Party of China, Chairman Mao, recently pointed out profoundly: "A person has arteries and veins, and through the heart, blood circulation is carried out. One must also breathe through the lungs, exhaling carbon dioxide and inhaling fresh oxygen. This is called expelling the old and taking in the new. A proletarian party must also expel the old and take in the new to remain vigorous. Without removing waste and absorbing fresh blood, the party will lose its vitality." Chairman Mao's latest directive creatively develops Marxism-Leninism, vividly and profoundly pointing out the fundamental direction for rectifying and building the party, and is a powerful criticism of the revisionist line of building the party advocated by the Chinese Khrushchev. Chairman Mao's latest directive expresses the sentiments of our hundreds of millions of revolutionary masses. We shout a thousand times, ten thousand times: Long live Chairman Mao! Long, long live Chairman Mao! We wish Chairman Mao eternal life! Eternal, eternal life!

"The Communist Party of China is the core leadership of all the people of China. Without such a core, the cause of socialism cannot be victorious." The party is the vanguard organization of the proletariat leading the people and striking at the enemy. Without a proletarian party that represents the interests of the people, the dictatorship of the proletariat cannot be consolidated, and the revolution will fail.

The Chinese Khrushchev, in order to achieve his goal of usurping the party and the state and restoring capitalism, attempted to turn our proletarian party into a bourgeois party. For a long time, he has been promoting a revisionist line of building the party within the party, opposing the line of building the party advocated by our great leader Chairman Mao. He shamelessly shouted, "What if you don't become a tool? Is it good to be a tool of the party? Is it good to be a tame tool? It's very good." What is this tame tool of the party? Its purpose is to stifle the revolutionary spirit of the broad masses of party members, to drive Communist Party members to become tame tools for him to promote his bourgeois reactionary line, to become loyal slaves for him to usurp the party and the state and restore capitalism. His intentions are indeed evil! Every class party serves its own class. Our proletarian party represents the proletariat and the interests of the broad masses of the people; while the bourgeois party represents the interests of the exploiting class. The Chinese Khrushchev, this representative of the bourgeoisie who has infiltrated the party, wants to make our Communist Party members subservient to his will, as his vehicles for restoring capitalism, which is a foolish dream!

We, the revolutionary warriors armed with Mao Zedong Thought, have the keenest eyes and the reddest hearts, and are the most loyal to Chairman Mao's revolutionary line. We hate the revisionist line of building the party the most. We will definitely, under the guidance of Chairman Mao's brilliant thoughts on building the party, take up the thousand-jin hammer of Mao Zedong Thought, smash the revisionist line of building the party advocated by the Chinese Khrushchev thoroughly, and thoroughly eliminate its poison, so that Chairman Mao's brilliant thoughts on building the party will shine forever.

### “驯服工具论”见鬼去吧！
朝阳
1968-10-17
第2版()
专栏：

    “驯服工具论”见鬼去吧！
    北京部队某部　朝阳
    中国共产党的伟大缔造者毛主席最近深刻地指出：“一个人有动脉，静脉，通过心脏进行血液循环，还要通过肺部进行呼吸，呼出二氧化碳，吸进新鲜氧气，这就是吐故纳新。一个无产阶级的党也要吐故纳新，才能朝气蓬勃。不清除废料，不吸收新鲜血液，党就没有朝气。”毛主席这一最新指示创造性地发展了马克思列宁主义，形象化地深刻地指明了整党建党的根本方向，也是给中国赫鲁晓夫修正主义建党路线的一个有力的批判。毛主席的最新指示说出了我们亿万革命群众的心里话，我们千遍万遍地高呼：毛主席万岁！毛主席万万岁！敬祝毛主席万寿无疆！万寿无疆！
      “中国共产党是全中国人民的领导核心。没有这样一个核心，社会主义事业就不能胜利。”党是领导人民，打击敌人的无产阶级的先锋队组织。没有一个代表人民群众利益的无产阶级政党，无产阶级专政就不能巩固，革命就要失败。
    中国赫鲁晓夫为了达到其篡党篡政、复辟资本主义的目的，企图把我们无产阶级政党变成资产阶级的政党。他长期以来在党内推行了一条修正主义建党路线，与我们伟大领袖毛主席的建党路线相对抗。他猖狂地叫嚣什么：“不做工具，做什么？做党的工具好不好？做驯服工具好不好？很好。”什么党的驯服工具，其目的就是扼杀广大党员的无产阶级革命造反精神，驱使共产党员变成他推行资产阶级反动路线的驯服工具，变成他篡党篡政、复辟资本主义的忠实奴才。其用心可谓毒矣！每个阶级的政党，都是为本阶级服务的。我们无产阶级的政党代表了无产阶级，代表了广大人民群众的利益；而资产阶级的政党，代表的是剥削阶级的利益。中国赫鲁晓夫这个钻进党内的资产阶级总代表，妄想让我们共产党员驯服于他的旨意之下，作为他复辟资本主义的车马，是痴心妄想！
    我们用毛泽东思想武装起来的革命战士眼最亮，心最红，对毛主席革命路线最忠诚。我们对修正主义建党路线最仇恨。我们一定要在毛主席光辉的建党思想的指引下，奋起毛泽东思想千钧棒，把中国赫鲁晓夫修正主义建党路线彻底砸烂，彻底肃清其流毒，让毛主席光辉的建党思想永放光芒。
