Title: Albanian Party and Government Delegation Visits Beijing New China Printing Factory
Author:
Date: 1968-10-07
Edition: 3

The Albanian Party and Government Delegation, led by Comrade Bekir Balluku, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Vice Premier and Minister of Defense of the People's Republic of Albania, visited the Beijing New China Printing Factory today and was warmly welcomed by all the workers of the factory.

Comrade Balluku and delegation members Qemal Charchani, Todi Theodhori, and Natanaili, accompanied by Comrade Li Xiannian, arrived at the New China Printing Factory. Workers lined up on both sides of the road, holding high portraits of our great leader Chairman Mao and the great leader of the Albanian people, Comrade Enver Hoxha, waving the national flags of both countries, and beating drums and gongs to enthusiastically welcome the Albanian comrades from the frontline of anti-imperialism and anti-revisionism. The workers waved the "Quotations from Chairman Mao," shouting slogans such as, "Long live the friendship between the peoples of China and Albania!" "Long live the Albanian Labor Party!" "Long live the Communist Party of China!" "Firmly support Albania's revolutionary action to withdraw from the Warsaw Pact!" "Down with U.S. imperialism! Down with Soviet revisionism! Down with all reactionaries!" "Long live the great leader of the Albanian people, Comrade Enver Hoxha!" "Long live our great leader Chairman Mao!"

Comrade Balluku shook hands warmly with the workers and together they shouted, "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Long live Enver!"

The workers of the New China Printing Factory held a grand welcome meeting in the workshop. Comrade Todi Theodhori, member of the Albanian Party and Government Delegation and candidate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party and Minister of Industry and Mines, spoke at the meeting. In his speech, he enthusiastically praised the workers of the New China Printing Factory for following Chairman Mao's great strategic deployment and achieving great victories in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. He thanked the workers of the New China Printing Factory for printing the Albanian version of the "Quotations from Chairman Mao" and portraits of the beloved leader of the Albanian people, Comrade Enver Hoxha.

In his speech, he also introduced to the workers of the New China Printing Factory the glorious achievements of the Albanian people under the wise leadership of Comrade Enver Hoxha in the revolutionary movement.

At the end of the meeting, a representative of the workers of the New China Printing Factory, amidst warm applause, presented portraits of Comrade Enver Hoxha and Chairman Mao printed by themselves to the Albanian comrades. The members of the Albanian Party and Government Delegation also had a friendly discussion with the leaders of the revolutionary committee, military representatives, and worker representatives of the New China Printing Factory. They also visited the typesetting, offset printing, gravure printing, rotary printing, and binding workshops.

### 阿党政代表团参观北京新华印刷厂

1968-10-07
第3版()
专栏：

    阿党政代表团参观北京新华印刷厂
    新华社六日讯　由阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议副主席兼国防部长贝基尔·巴卢库同志率领的阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团，今天参观北京新华印刷厂，受到了全厂工人的热烈欢迎。
    巴卢库同志和代表团团员查尔查尼、蒂奥多西和纳塔奈利等同志，由李先念同志陪同，来到新华印刷厂时，排列在道路两旁的工人们，高举着我们伟大领袖毛主席的画像和阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖恩维尔·霍查同志的画像，手持中阿两国国旗，敲锣打鼓，热烈欢迎来自反帝反修前线的阿尔巴尼亚战友。工人们挥动《毛主席语录》，不断高呼：“中国阿尔巴尼亚两国人民的友谊万岁！”“阿尔巴尼亚劳动党万岁！”“中国共产党万岁！”“坚决支持阿尔巴尼亚退出华沙条约的革命行动！”“打倒美帝！打倒苏修！打倒各国反动派！”“阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖恩维尔·霍查同志万岁！”“我们的伟大领袖毛主席万岁！”
    巴卢库同志同工人们亲切握手，并且和大家一起高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“恩维尔万岁！”
    新华印刷厂工人在车间里举行了隆重的欢迎大会。阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团团员、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局候补委员、工业和矿产部长蒂奥多西在大会上讲了话。他在讲话中热情地赞扬新华印刷厂工人紧跟毛主席的伟大战略部署，在无产阶级文化大革命中取得了伟大胜利。他感谢新华印刷厂工人给阿尔巴尼亚人民印刷了阿文版《毛主席语录》和阿尔巴尼亚人民的敬爱领袖恩维尔·霍查同志的画像。
    他在讲话中还向新华印刷厂工人介绍了阿尔巴尼亚人民在恩维尔·霍查同志的英明领导下，在革命化运动中取得的辉煌成就。
    大会结束时，新华印刷厂工人代表在热烈的掌声中，向阿尔巴尼亚同志赠送了自己印刷的恩维尔·霍查同志的画像和毛主席的画像。阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团的同志们在新华印刷厂，还同革命委员会的负责人、军代表和工人代表，进行了亲切的座谈，并且参观了制版、胶印、凹印、轮转、装订等车间。
