Title: Premier Zhou Enlai, General Huang Yongsheng, and Deputy Chief of Staff Wen Yucheng Hold Talks with the Albanian Party and Government Delegation Led by Comrade Baluku
Author:
Date: 1968-10-03
Edition: 2

The talks were held in an extremely cordial atmosphere, reflecting the eternal revolutionary friendship and great fighting unity between the two parties, the two peoples, and the two armies of China and Albania.

New China News Agency, October 2nd: Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Huang Yongsheng, and Deputy Chief of the General Staff Wen Yucheng held talks today with the Albanian Party and Government Delegation led by Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Vice Premier and Minister of Defense Bekir Baluku.

The Albanian side participating in the talks also included: Members of the Albanian Party and Government Delegation: Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Vice Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly, and First Secretary of the Party Committee of the District of Durres Rita Marko; Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Vice Premier Adil Carkcani; Candidate Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Minister of Industry and Mines Koco Theodhori; Albanian Ambassador to China Vasili Nataneli.

The Albanian military attaché to China, Hakani, also participated in the talks.

The talks were conducted in an extremely cordial atmosphere, reflecting the eternal revolutionary friendship and great fighting unity between the two parties, the two peoples, and the two armies of China and Albania.

		Zhou Enlai, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Premier of the State Council, Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Huang Yongsheng, and Deputy Chief of the General Staff Wen Yucheng held talks with the Albanian Party and Government Delegation led by Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Vice Premier and Minister of Defense Bekir Baluku in an extremely cordial atmosphere on October 2nd.

### 周恩来同志黄永胜总参谋长温玉成副总参谋长同以巴卢库同志为首的阿党政代表团举行会谈  会谈在极其亲切的气氛中进行，体现了中阿两党、两国人民和两国军队永恒的革命友谊和伟大的战斗团结

1968-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    周恩来同志黄永胜总参谋长温玉成副总参谋长同以巴卢库同志为首的阿党政代表团举行会谈
    会谈在极其亲切的气氛中进行，体现了中阿两党、两国人民和两国军队永恒的革命友谊和伟大的战斗团结
    新华社二日讯　中共中央政治局常委、国务院总理周恩来，中国人民解放军总参谋长黄永胜，副总参谋长温玉成，今天同以阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、部长会议副主席兼国防部长贝基尔·巴卢库为首的阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团，举行会谈。
    参加会谈的阿尔巴尼亚方面还有：阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团团员：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、人民议会主席团副主席、劳动党都拉斯区党委第一书记里塔·马尔科，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、部长会议副主席阿迪尔·查尔查尼，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局候补委员、工业和矿产部长科乔·蒂奥多西，阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使瓦西里·纳塔奈利。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使馆武官哈卡尼也参加了会谈。
    会谈是在极其亲切的气氛中进行的，体现了中阿两党、两国人民和两国军队永恒的革命友谊和伟大的战斗团结。
    （附图片）
    中共中央政治局常委、国务院总理周恩来，中国人民解放军总参谋长黄永胜，副总参谋长温玉成，十月二日同以阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、部长会议副主席兼国防部长贝基尔·巴卢库为首的阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团在极其亲切的气氛中进行会谈。
