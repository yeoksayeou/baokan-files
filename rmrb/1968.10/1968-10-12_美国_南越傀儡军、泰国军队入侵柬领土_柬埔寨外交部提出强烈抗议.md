Title: U.S.-South Vietnamese Puppet Army and Thai Army Invade Cambodian Territory
Author:
Date: 1968-10-12
Edition: 6

	U.S.-South Vietnamese Puppet Army and Thai Army Invade Cambodian Territory
	Cambodian Foreign Ministry Lodges Strong Protest

	New China News Agency, Phnom Penh, October 8 — According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on October 7, the Royal Government of Cambodia's Ministry of Foreign Affairs sent a note to the U.S. government and the Saigon puppet regime on September 30, strongly protesting the new crimes of the U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet army invading Cambodia's Takeo, Kampong Cham, and Kampong Speu provinces on September 5, 8, 9, 11, 13, and 22.

	The note pointed out that in these new invasions, the U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet army fired artillery and automatic weapons into Cambodian territory and invaded Cambodian territory, killing one Cambodian civilian and injuring five. The U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet regime also used aircraft to drop poison, causing severe damage to Cambodian civilians' livestock and crops.

	New China News Agency, Phnom Penh, October 7 — According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on October 6, the Royal Government of Cambodia's Ministry of Foreign Affairs sent a note to the Thai puppet authorities on October 3, strongly protesting the armed invasions by the Thai puppet army on September 13, 15, and 16 into Cambodia's Oddar Meanchey and Battambang provinces, killing one Cambodian and injuring eight.

	The note demanded that the Thai puppet authorities immediately stop these evil acts.

### 美国—南越傀儡军、泰国军队入侵柬领土  柬埔寨外交部提出强烈抗议

1968-10-12
第6版()
专栏：

    美国—南越傀儡军、泰国军队入侵柬领土
    柬埔寨外交部提出强烈抗议
    新华社金边八日电　据柬埔寨新闻社七日报道，柬埔寨王国政府外交部九月三十日照会美国政府和西贡傀儡集团，强烈抗议美国—南越傀儡军队在九月五日、八日、九日、十一日、十三日和二十二日侵犯柬埔寨柴桢省、磅针省和波萝勉省的新罪行。
    照会指出，在这几次新的侵犯事件中，美国—南越傀儡军队向柬埔寨境内开炮和用自动武器进行射击，并侵入柬埔寨境内，共打死柬埔寨居民一人、打伤五人。美国—南越傀儡集团还用飞机撒放毒药，使柬埔寨居民的牲畜和农作物遭受严重损害。
    新华社金边七日电　据柬埔寨新闻社六日报道，柬埔寨王国政府外交部十月三日向泰国傀儡当局发出照会，强烈抗议泰国傀儡军队九月十三日、十五日和十六日对柬埔寨奥多棉藏省和马德望省进行武装侵略，打死柬埔寨一人和打伤八人的罪行。
    照会要求泰国傀儡当局立即停止这种罪恶行径。
