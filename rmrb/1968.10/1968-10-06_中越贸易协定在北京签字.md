Title: Sino-Vietnam Trade Agreement Signed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-10-06
Edition: 6

The trade agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on mutual supply of goods and payments for 1969 was signed in Beijing on September 30.

The signatories were Li Qiang, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of China, and Li Ban, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Vice Premier Li Xiannian, Deputy Director of the State Economic Commission Xie Huai-de, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China Wu Mingluan, and Head of the Permanent Delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam to China Nguyen Van Quang attended the signing ceremony.

### 中越贸易协定在北京签字

1968-10-06
第6版()
专栏：

    中越贸易协定在北京签字
    新华社讯　中华人民共和国政府和越南民主共和国政府关于一九六九年相互供应货物和付款的协定，九月三十日在北京签字。
    在协定上签字的是，中国对外贸易部副部长李强和越南民主共和国对外贸易部副部长李班。
    国务院副总理李先念，对外经委副主任谢怀德，越南民主共和国驻中国大使吴明鸾，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长阮文广等，参加了签字仪式。
