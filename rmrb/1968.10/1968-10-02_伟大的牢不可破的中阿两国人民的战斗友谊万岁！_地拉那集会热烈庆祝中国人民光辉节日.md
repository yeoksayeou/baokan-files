Title: Long Live the Indestructible Combat Friendship Between the Peoples of China and Albania!
Author:
Date: 1968-10-02
Edition: 5

A grand rally was held at the Cultural Palace in Tirana on September 29 by the Albania-China Friendship Association and the Albania Foreign Cultural and Friendly Liaison Committee to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Present at the meeting were members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Secretary of the Secretariat Hysni Kapo, Political Bureau member and First Secretary of the Tirana City Committee Manush Muftiu, Political Bureau member and Chairman of the State Planning Commission Spiro Kolega, Candidate member of the Political Bureau and Chairman of the Albania-China Friendship Association Abdul Kelmendi, Candidate members of the Political Bureau Kadri Hazbi and Pilo Peristeri, Secretary of the Secretariat Qafez Spasiu, Central Committee members Behar Shytulla, Dashnor Mameqi, Fadil Pacrami, Hito Cako, Milo Kirca, Nestor Nas, Shefqet Pe√ßi, and Shyqyri Dajani, Candidate Central Committee members Haxhi Leshi, Jogi Premti, Karmen Ili, and Sefer Seferi, as well as Chairman of the Albania Foreign Cultural and Friendly Liaison Committee Misto Treska.

Workers of Tirana, representatives of the Albanian People's Army, and various mass organizations attended the celebration.

The Chinese Embassy in Albania, led by Charge d'Affaires Li Tingqian, and embassy staff and Chinese engineering and technical personnel working in Albania were invited to attend the celebration.

The stage was adorned with portraits of the great leader of the Albanian people, Comrade Enver Hoxha, and the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao. The national flags of both countries and the slogan "Long Live the People's Republic of China!" were prominently displayed.

The celebration was presided over by Chairman of the Albania-China Friendship Association Abdul Kelmendi.

Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party Qafez Spasiu addressed the meeting.

Spasiu said in his speech that the founding of the People's Republic of China on October 1, 1949, was one of the most significant events in human history, following the great October Socialist Revolution led by Lenin and Stalin. Today, the People's Republic of China has become an unshakable stronghold of world revolution and socialism, a fearless banner in the struggle against imperialism led by the United States and modern revisionism led by the Soviet revisionist clique, as well as against reactionaries in various countries.

Spasiu pointed out that the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, initiated and led by Comrade Mao Zedong, has mobilized hundreds of millions of workers, peasants, soldiers, students, intellectuals, and revolutionary cadres. Under the leadership of the Party, they have risen with tremendous force to defend Marxism-Leninism and the socialist system in China, and have dealt a fatal blow to the counter-revolutionary plots of the capitalist-roaders within the Party, headed by China's Khrushchev. The glorious victory of establishing revolutionary committees in all provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions of China, except Taiwan Province, marks the complete victory of Comrade Mao Zedong's Marxist-Leninist revolutionary line and strategy, and signifies that the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution has entered a new and higher stage, the stage of nationwide struggle, criticism, and transformation.

Spasiu said that the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China has blocked the road to the restoration of capitalism, ensuring that the giant China will forever remain red and advance unceasingly as the strongest shock brigade of international communism.

Spasiu pointed out that Comrade Mao Zedong's latest instructions on fully mobilizing the leading role of the working class in the Cultural Revolution and all work are of great significance for the further victory of this revolution. These instructions have aroused great revolutionary enthusiasm and optimism throughout China, further consolidating the position of Marxism-Leninism and the proletarian dictatorship of the People's Republic of China.

He pointed out that the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China not only has great national significance but also has great international significance for the people of the world, for all Marxist-Leninists and revolutionaries, and especially for the people of countries ruled by revisionist cliques. The great victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China has dealt a heavy blow to imperialism and modern revisionism.

Spasiu said that the great People's Republic of China has become an invincible stronghold of socialism, a powerful base for world revolution, a banner of Marxism-Leninism, and a resolute and steadfast defender of the freedom-loving peoples of all countries. The People's Republic of China, with its principled foreign policy, has resolutely and uncompromisingly fought against the aggressive policies of imperialism led by the United States and modern revisionism led by the Soviet revisionists, winning the admiration, respect, and support of the freedom-loving peoples of the world. The People's Republic of China has become a great example for them in their struggle against imperialism, modern revisionism, and reactionaries in various countries.

Spasiu said that the Albanian people and the Albanian Labor Party have, from the very beginning, strongly and unreservedly supported and endorsed the Cultural Revolution in China, enthusiastically hailing every victory of this revolution. They regard the victories of this revolution as victories of Marxism-Leninism, as victories of Comrade Mao Zedong's proletarian revolutionary line and thought, as victories of the Albanian people and the revolutionary peoples of the world, and are firmly convinced that this revolution will achieve complete victory.

Spasiu, in discussing the recent decision by the Albanian People's Assembly to withdraw from the Warsaw Pact, said that this is a revolutionary decision of great historical significance. He said that our Party and people have contemptuously condemned the Warsaw Pact, thrown it into the dustbin of history, smashed the revisionist "ace card," and completed another revolutionary action. This action is of great significance not only for our country but also for the cause of socialism, world peace, and the revolutionary peoples of the countries under revisionist rule.

Spasiu said that our Party and people's bold revolutionary decision has been warmly welcomed and highly praised by Marxist-Leninist parties and organizations and the revolutionary peoples of the world. This decision has received the firm support of the great Communist Party of China and the Chinese people.

Spasiu said that the telegram from the great leader of the Communist Party of China and the Chinese people, our most respected friend Chairman Mao Zedong, and his closest comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin Biao, as well as Premier Zhou Enlai, to our Party and people's leader Comrade Enver Hoxha and Chairman of the Council of Ministers Mehmet Shehu, is a brilliant document of the internationalist revolutionary unity between the great Chinese people and the Albanian people.

Spasiu said that this powerful telegram has inspired every Albanian, making our people even stronger, more invincible, and indestructible. This telegram is also a serious warning to the U.S. imperialists, Soviet revisionists, and their lackeys: if they dare to touch Albania, if they dare to play with fire, they will be completely and thoroughly burned in this fire.

Spasiu said, "Let the U.S. imperialists, Soviet revisionists, and their lackeys remember the solemn warning given by Comrade Enver Hoxha at the Fourth Congress of the Albanian Democratic Front a year ago: 'The heroes who can frighten the Albanians have not been born yet, neither in the past nor now.' The People's Republic of Albania will stand as firm as a rock on the Adriatic coast, making our enemies bleed."

Spasiu pointed out that currently, a new historical stage in the struggle against imperialism led by the United States and modern revisionism led by the Soviet revisionists has begun. The class contradictions in the capitalist world are deepening and sharpening. The positions of imperialism have been weakened by the heavy blows of the national liberation struggles and revolutionary movements of the peoples of the world, especially the heroic struggle of the Vietnamese people. For a long time, they have been playing the "peace talks" trick to deceive and conquer the Vietnamese people. These "peace talks" are a conspiracy jointly planned by the U.S. imperialists and Soviet revisionists to divide spheres of influence and rule the world with two imperialist powers.

Spasiu said that today, the Albanian people, with high revolutionary spirit, are steeling themselves in unity around the Party led by Comrade Enver Hoxha and the Central Committee, fighting to realize the grand program of the Party's historic Fifth Congress and the teachings of Comrade Enver Hoxha, and for the further revolutionization of all aspects of our country's life. They are "holding a hoe in one hand and a gun in the other," relying on their own strength, and advancing bravely to deepen the socialist revolution in all fields.

Spasiu said that the Sixth Plenary Session of the Party, which concluded yesterday, has set important tasks for our Party and people to further deepen the socialist revolution through class struggle and the mass line. Faithfully carrying out these tasks will make our revolution advance to new heights and become even more invincible.

Spasiu said that in the struggle to build socialism, the Albanian people have received and continue to receive the comprehensive, selfless, and internationalist aid of the brotherly Chinese people, the Communist Party of China, and its great leader Comrade Mao Zedong. We thank them for this generous aid and internationalist support.

He pointed out that the visit of the Party and government delegation led by Comrade Bekir Balluku will make a contribution to further strengthening the great, combat, and indestructible friendship between Albania and China and our brotherly all-round cooperation.

Charge d'Affaires Li Tingqian then spoke. He said that this year, the 700 million Chinese people are celebrating their great national day amidst the triumphant songs of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, initiated and led by our great leader Chairman Mao, achieving comprehensive victory.

Li Tingqian continued, saying that the heroic Albanian people, under the correct leadership of the Albanian Labor Party led by the great Marxist-Leninist Enver Hoxha, have hoisted the revolutionary red flag of Marxism-Leninism high, achieving brilliant victories in the socialist revolution and socialist construction. The series of revolutionary measures taken by the Albanian Labor Party to block the channels for the production of revisionism are of great value in developing the socialist revolution and preventing the restoration of capitalism. These revolutionary measures have further consolidated the proletarian dictatorship of Albania, making the great socialist beacon of Albania shine even more brilliantly.

Li Tingqian said that in the international class struggle, the Albanian Labor Party and the Albanian people have always hoisted the banner of anti-imperialism and anti-revisionism, resolutely opposing imperialism led by the United States and modern revisionism centered on the Soviet revisionist clique, deeply exposing and resolutely striking at the Soviet revisionists, the greatest traitors in history. The Albanian Labor Party and the Albanian people have always hoisted the banner of proletarian internationalism, resolutely supporting the revolutionary struggles of the oppressed peoples and nations of the world, resolutely supporting all Marxist-Leninist parties and organizations, and have established immortal merits in defending Marxism-Leninism and promoting the international proletarian revolutionary cause.

Recently, the People's Republic of Albania solemnly announced its withdrawal from the Warsaw Pact. This bold and revolutionary action is another heavy blow to the Soviet revisionist clique, greatly encouraging the struggle of the peoples of Eastern European countries against modern revisionism led by the Soviet revisionists, and has made a great contribution to the international communist movement. The Chinese people express their sincere admiration and firm support for this. The 700 million Chinese people, armed with Mao Zedong Thought and tempered in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, will, as always, stand firmly on the side of the brotherly Albanian people, following the teachings of our great leader Chairman Mao: "At any time and under any circumstances, we will always stand firmly on the side of the brotherly Albanian people."

Li Tingqian warmly praised the indestructible combat friendship between the two parties, two countries, and two peoples of China and Albania, forged by Comrade Mao Zedong and Comrade Enver Hoxha. He said that the 700 million Chinese people, tempered in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, will definitely hold high the great red banner of Marxism-Leninism-Mao Zedong Thought, follow the teachings of Chairman Mao, and forever stand together with the Albanian people, fight together, and win together.

The meeting was filled with the atmosphere of indestructible revolutionary friendship and combat unity between the peoples of Albania and China. The participants repeatedly shouted enthusiastically: "Enver-Ho! Mao Zedong!" "Long live Comrade Enver Hoxha!" "Long live Comrade Mao Zedong!" "Friendship is the guarantee!"
