Title: Praising the Great Leader of the People and the Socialist New Life
Author:
Date: 1968-10-17
Edition: 3

Albania held a grand folk song and dance evening in Tirana. The great leader of the Albanian people, Comrade Enver Hoxha, watched the performance.

Comrades Mehmet Shehu, Beqir Balluku, Spiro Koleka, Haxhi Lleshi, and other comrades also watched the performance.

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, October 16 - On the evening of October 15, the Ministry of Education and Culture of Albania held a grand folk song and dance evening at the Cultural Palace in Tirana. The beloved leader of the Albanian people, the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour, Comrade Enver Hoxha, watched the performance.

Those who watched the performance included: Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of Albania, Haxhi Lleshi; Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Mehmet Shehu; Politburo members of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour, Tuk Jakova, Kadri Hazbiu, Ramiz Alia, Manush Myftiu, Spiro Koleka, and Haki Toska; Alternate members of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour, Haki Toska, Spiro Koleka, and Kadri Hazbiu; Secretary of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour, Spiro Koleka; ministers of various government departments, representatives of the People's Army, various mass organizations, and industrial and mining enterprises.

During the evening, the performers enthusiastically sang and danced to a rich variety of folk songs and music, praising the glorious Albanian Party of Labour, praising the great leader of the Albanian people, Comrade Enver Hoxha, praising the Albanian revolutionary movement, and the new socialist life.

Performers from the Gjirokastra region also sang a folk song expressing the Albanian people's firm support for the revolutionary action of the Albanian government in withdrawing from the Warsaw Pact, and warmly welcoming the telegram sent by the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao, and his close comrade-in-arms, Vice Chairman Lin Biao, as well as Premier Zhou Enlai, to Comrade Enver Hoxha and Comrade Mehmet Shehu on September 17.

At the end of the evening, the entire audience stood up and applauded enthusiastically for a long time, cheering for Comrade Enver Hoxha. Comrade Enver Hoxha and other Party and government leaders went on stage and warmly shook hands with the performers, congratulating them on their successful performance.

The performances at this song and dance evening were all excellent programs selected from the national folk song and dance competition in Albania. The national folk song and dance competition in Albania was held from October 6 to 12 in the Gjirokastra Castle to celebrate the 60th birthday of Comrade Enver Hoxha.

### 歌颂人民的伟大领袖　歌颂革命化运动和社会主义新生活  地拉那举行盛大的民间歌舞晚会阿人民伟大领袖霍查同志观看演出

1968-10-17
第3版()
专栏：

    歌颂人民的伟大领袖　歌颂革命化运动和社会主义新生活
    地拉那举行盛大的民间歌舞晚会阿人民伟大领袖霍查同志观看演出
    同时观看演出的有列希、谢胡、托斯卡、卡博等同志
    新华社地拉那十六日电　阿尔巴尼亚教育和文化部十五日晚在地拉那文化宫举行盛大民间歌舞晚会。阿尔巴尼亚人民的敬爱领袖、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央第一书记恩维尔·霍查同志观看了演出。
    观看这次演出的还有：阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团主席列希，部长会议主席谢胡，劳动党中央政治局委员托斯卡、卡博、穆夫蒂乌、阿利雅、马尔科和科列加，劳动党中央政治局候补委员哈兹比乌、杜米和皮里斯特里，劳动党中央书记斯巴修，政府各部部长，人民军、各群众团体和工矿企业的代表等。
    晚会上，演员们以丰富多采的民间歌舞和音乐热情歌颂光荣的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党，歌颂阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖霍查同志，歌颂阿尔巴尼亚革命化运动和社会主义新生活。
    吉诺卡斯特区的演员还演唱了一首民歌，表示阿尔巴尼亚人民坚决支持阿尔巴尼亚政府退出华沙条约的革命行动，热烈欢呼中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席以及周恩来总理九月十七日给霍查同志和谢胡同志的电报。
    晚会结束时，全场起立，热烈鼓掌，长时间向着霍查同志欢呼。霍查同志同党和政府的其他领导人走上舞台，同演员们亲切握手，祝贺他们演出成功。
    这次歌舞晚会上演出的节目，都是阿尔巴尼亚全国民间歌舞会演中选拔出来的优秀节目。阿尔巴尼亚全国民间歌舞会演，是为庆祝霍查同志六十寿辰从十月六日到十二日在吉诺卡斯特城堡举行的。
