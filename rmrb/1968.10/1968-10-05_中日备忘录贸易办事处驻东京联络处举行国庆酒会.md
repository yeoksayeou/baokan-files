Title: The Liaison Office of the Sino-Japanese Trade Office in Tokyo Holds a National Day Banquet
Author:
Date: 1968-10-05
Edition: 5

The Liaison Office of the Sino-Japanese Trade Office in Tokyo held a grand banquet on the evening of September 30 to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

The banquet hall was adorned with Chairman Mao's important calligraphy and quotations presented to Japanese workers: "The Japanese people are a great people. They will never allow American imperialism to ride roughshod over them for long."

More than 700 friends from various circles in Japan and patriotic overseas Chinese residing in Japan attended the banquet. The hosts and guests raised their glasses repeatedly, enthusiastically celebrating the establishment of revolutionary committees in all provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions of China except Taiwan, celebrating the new victories of the great, invincible Mao Zedong Thought, and sincerely wishing Chairman Mao eternal life!

### 中日备忘录贸易办事处驻东京联络处举行国庆酒会

1968-10-05
第5版()
专栏：

    中日备忘录贸易办事处驻东京联络处举行国庆酒会
    据新华社一日讯　东京消息：中日备忘录贸易办事处驻东京联络处九月三十日晚上举行盛大酒会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。
    会场上悬挂着毛主席书赠日本工人的重要题词和语录：“日本民族是一个伟大的民族。它是绝不会让美帝国主义长期骑在自己头上的。”
    来自日本各地的各界朋友和旅日爱国侨胞七百多人参加了酒会。宾主频频举杯，热烈庆祝中国全国除台湾省外的各省、市、自治区全部成立革命委员会，庆祝伟大的战无不胜的毛泽东思想取得的新胜利，衷心敬祝毛主席万寿无疆！
