Title: Patriotic Military and Civilians of Xam Neua in Laos Hold Grand Rally to Celebrate Revolutionary Day

Author:

Date: 1968-10-17

Edition: 6

Thousands of patriotic military and civilians in Xam Neua, Laos, held a grand rally on October 13 to celebrate the 23rd anniversary of the Lao Revolution.

Prince Souphanouvong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front, Kaysone Phomvihane, Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front, members of the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front, representatives of the Supreme Command of the Lao People's Liberation Army, and representatives of the Lao Patriotic Neutralist Forces, including Kaysone Phomvihane, attended the rally.

Prince Souphanouvong delivered a speech at the rally. He analyzed the current domestic and international situation and called on the Lao patriotic military and civilians to press forward and defeat the American aggressors and their Vientiane lackeys.

Sisouk Na Champassak, a member of the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front and a member of the Lao Asia-Africa Solidarity Committee, also spoke at the rally. He expressed his firm belief that the struggle of the Lao people against American imperialism and its lackeys will certainly achieve final victory.

On October 12 and 13, patriotic military and civilians in various parts of Laos also held rallies to celebrate the occasion, expressing their determination to respond to the call of Prince Souphanouvong and the command of the Supreme Command of the Lao People's Liberation Army, and to advance the struggle against American aggression and for national salvation.

### 老挝桑怒爱国军民盛大集会庆祝革命节  苏发努冯出席大会，号召爱国军民乘胜前进打败美国侵略者及其走狗

1968-10-17
第6版()
专栏：

    老挝桑怒爱国军民盛大集会庆祝革命节
    苏发努冯出席大会，号召爱国军民乘胜前进打败美国侵略者及其走狗
    新华社康开十六日电　据巴特寮通讯社报道，老挝爱国军民一千多人十三日在桑怒举行盛大集会，庆祝老挝革命节二十三周年。
    老挝爱国战线党中央委员会主席苏发努冯亲王，老挝爱国战线党中央委员会总书记富米·冯维希，老挝爱国战线党中央委员会委员，老挝人民解放军最高指挥部的代表以及老挝爱国中立力量代表坎苏·高拉等出席了大会。
    苏发努冯亲王在会上发表了讲话。他分析了当前的国内外形势，号召老挝爱国军民乘胜前进，打败美国侵略者及其万象走狗。
    老挝爱国战线党中央委员会委员、老挝亚非团结委员会委员萨南·苏蒂责，也在会上讲了话。他表示坚信，老挝人民反对美帝国主义及其走狗的斗争，一定会取得最后胜利。
    十月十二日和十三日，老挝各地爱国军民也分别举行庆祝集会，表示决心响应苏发努冯亲王的号召和老挝人民解放军最高指挥部的命令，把抗美救国斗争推向前进。
