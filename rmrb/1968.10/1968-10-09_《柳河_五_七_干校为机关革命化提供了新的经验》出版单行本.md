Title: The Liuhe May 7th Cadres School Provides New Experience for the Revolutionization of Organs
Author:
Date: 1968-10-09
Edition: 2

The single-volume edition of "The Liuhe May 7th Cadres School Provides New Experience for the Revolutionization of Organs," which conveys the latest instructions of our great leader Chairman Mao, has been published by the People's Publishing House. It will begin to be distributed in the Beijing Xinhua Bookstore on October 9th and will be distributed in Xinhua Bookstores nationwide in succession.

### 《柳河“五·七”干校为机关革命化提供了新的经验》出版单行本

1968-10-09
第2版()
专栏：

    《柳河“五·七”干校为机关革命化提供了新的经验》出版单行本
    新华社八日讯　传达了我们伟大领袖毛主席最新指示的《人民日报》重要编者按和《人民日报》关于柳河“五·七”干校为机关革命化提供了新的经验的新闻报道，已由人民出版社出版单行本，十月九日开始在北京新华书店发行，并将在全国各地新华书店陆续发行。
