Title: Celebrating the 24th Anniversary of the Founding of the Romanian Armed Forces
Author:
Date: 1968-10-25
Edition: 5

The Ministry of National Defense held a report meeting today to celebrate the 24th anniversary of the founding of the armed forces of the Socialist Republic of Romania.

The Chargé d'Affaires of the Romanian Embassy to China, Mr. Dolobantzu, and Military Attaché Colonel Gheorgheu were invited to attend the meeting.

Vice Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Wang Xinting, Deputy Director of the General Logistics Department, Zhang Xianye, and responsible comrades from the Navy, Air Force, and Beijing Garrison, as well as 800 representatives from the PLA units stationed in Beijing, attended the meeting.

Zhang Zhongru, Political Commissar of the Foreign Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of National Defense, presided over the meeting. He, on behalf of the Ministry of National Defense and the PLA units stationed in Beijing, extended warm festive greetings to the Romanian armed forces. Colonel Gheorgheu was invited to give a report at the meeting.

### 庆祝罗马尼亚武装部队建军二十四周年  我国防部举行报告会

1968-10-25
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝罗马尼亚武装部队建军二十四周年
    我国防部举行报告会
    新华社二十四日讯　国防部今天举行报告会，庆祝罗马尼亚社会主义共和国武装部队建军二十四周年。
    罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆临时代办道洛班楚和武官乔治乌中校应邀出席了报告会。
    中国人民解放军副总参谋长王新亭、总后勤部副部长张贤约和海军、空军、北京卫戍区负责同志，以及中国人民解放军驻京部队代表八百人出席了报告会。
    国防部外事局政治委员张中如主持报告会，他代表国防部和人民解放军驻京部队指战员向罗马尼亚武装部队致以热烈的节日祝贺。乔治乌中校应邀在会上作了报告。
