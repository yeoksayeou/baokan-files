Title: Workers of Beijing Cotton Mill No. 3 Celebrate Chairman Mao's Latest Directive
Author:
Date: 1968-10-19
Edition: 2

The revolutionary workers of Beijing Cotton Mill No. 3 were deeply moved upon hearing Chairman Mao's latest directive on rectification. Cheers of "Long live Chairman Mao!" echoed throughout the offices and workshops. This is a scene of workers from the spinning and drawing department and members of the revolutionary committee celebrating the issuance of Chairman Mao's latest directive.
