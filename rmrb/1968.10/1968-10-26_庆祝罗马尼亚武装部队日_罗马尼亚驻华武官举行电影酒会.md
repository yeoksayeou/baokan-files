Title: Celebrating the Romanian Armed Forces Day
Author:
Date: 1968-10-26
Edition: 6

Romanian Military Attaché Holds Film and Wine Reception

New China News Agency, October 25th: The Romanian People's Republic's military attaché to China, Colonel Gheorghe Georgiu, held a film and wine reception today to celebrate the Romanian People's Republic's Armed Forces Day.

Vice Chief of Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Li Tianyou, Deputy Director of the General Logistics Department, Zhang Xianye, and heads of various military branches and corps, as well as representatives of model soldiers, were invited to attend the reception. Also in attendance were relevant Chinese officials such as Qiao Qianhua, Ding Xilin, and Xiang Qian.

The Romanian People's Republic's chargé d'affaires to China, Dolobanțu, attended the reception.

Military attachés and ambassadors from various countries in China were also invited to attend the reception.

A Romanian film was screened during the reception.

### 庆祝罗马尼亚武装部队日  罗马尼亚驻华武官举行电影酒会

1968-10-26
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝罗马尼亚武装部队日
    罗马尼亚驻华武官举行电影酒会
    新华社二十五日讯　罗马尼亚社会主义共和国驻中国大使馆武官乔治乌中校，为庆祝罗马尼亚社会主义共和国武装部队日，今天举行电影酒会。
    中国人民解放军副总参谋长李天佑、总后勤部副部长张贤约和各军种、兵种负责人，以及五好战士代表应邀出席了酒会。应邀出席酒会的还有我有关方面负责人乔冠华、丁西林、向前等。
    罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆临时代办道洛班楚出席了酒会。
    各国驻华使节和大使馆武官也应邀出席了酒会。
    酒会上放映了罗马尼亚电影。
