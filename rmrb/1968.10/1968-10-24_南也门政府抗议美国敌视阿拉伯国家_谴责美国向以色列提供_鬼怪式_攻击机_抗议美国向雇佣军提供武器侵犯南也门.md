Title: South Yemen Government Protests U.S. Hostility Towards Arab Countries
Author:
Date: 1968-10-24
Edition: 5

The South Yemen government on October 20 sent two protests to the United States, condemning U.S. hostility towards Arab countries and the decision to supply Israel with "Phantom" fighter-bombers. The government also protested U.S. arms supplies to mercenaries invading South Yemen.

According to a report from the Aden radio station, South Yemen's Foreign Minister Qadhi handed two protest notes to the U.S. Chargé d'Affaires, Eglington, on October 20. In the protest note against the U.S. decision to supply Israel with "Phantom" fighter-bombers, the South Yemen government pointed out that this step by the U.S. government is a hostile act against Arab countries. This step is part of the U.S.-Israel conspiracy to carry out an expansionist and hegemonic foreign policy, encouraging Israel's continued occupation of Arab lands and supporting Israel's aggressive policies.

The note emphasized that the People's Republic of South Yemen will not tolerate or ignore this hostile step and will take a clear stance against the U.S. together with other Arab countries.

In another protest note to the U.S. Chargé d'Affaires, the South Yemen government protested against the U.S. supplying weapons, ammunition, and materials to Saudi reactionaries and mercenaries to oppose South Yemen. The note pointed out that South Yemen's armed forces seized these weapons during battles with mercenaries in the Behan region on October 14 and 15. The boxes of these weapons were marked with the U.S. "friendship" insignia. The note said that these weapons seized from mercenaries coming from Saudi Arabia clearly show that the U.S. is interfering with and supporting the Saudi reactionaries' invasion of the People's Republic of South Yemen's territory, undermining its sovereignty, security, and independence. Therefore, the People's Republic of South Yemen will take all necessary measures to defend its own security and independence.

The note also pointed out that South Yemen's armed forces had seized a large number of U.S. weapons in the battle of Sayyid two months ago, and these weapons were displayed to the public during the military parade on October 14 to celebrate the anniversary of the People's Republic of South Yemen.

### 南也门政府抗议美国敌视阿拉伯国家  谴责美国向以色列提供“鬼怪式”攻击机  抗议美国向雇佣军提供武器侵犯南也门

1968-10-24
第5版()
专栏：

    南也门政府抗议美国敌视阿拉伯国家
    谴责美国向以色列提供“鬼怪式”攻击机
    抗议美国向雇佣军提供武器侵犯南也门
    新华社亚丁二十二日电　南也门人民共和国政府二十日在给美国的两项照会中，抗议美国对阿拉伯国家采取敌视态度，决定向以色列提供“鬼怪式”攻击机；抗议美国通过向反对南也门人民共和国的雇佣军提供美国武器，干涉和侵犯南也门人民共和国的主权、安全和独立。
    据亚丁电台报道，南也门外交部长扎莱二十日交给美国驻亚丁代办伊格尔顿两项抗议照会。南也门政府在抗议美国决定向以色列提供“鬼怪式”攻击机的照会中指出，美国政府采取的这个步骤是敌视阿拉伯国家的行动。这个步骤是美国和以色列合谋对阿拉伯国家实行扩张主义和强权外交政策，助长了以色列对阿拉伯土地的继续占领，助长和支持了以色列的侵略政策。
    照会强调说，南也门人民共和国不会容忍和忽视这个敌视步骤，并将同其他阿拉伯国家一起对美国采取明确的立场。
    南也门政府在给美国代办的另一项照会中抗议美国向沙特阿拉伯反动派和雇佣军提供武器、弹药和物资来反对南也门。照会指出，南也门武装部队十月十四日和十五日在贝汉地区与雇佣军作战中缴获了这些武器，武器的箱子上都刻有美国“友谊”的标记。照会说，从来自沙特阿拉伯的雇佣军手中缴获的这些武器清楚地表明，美国插手和支持沙特阿拉伯反动派侵略南也门人民共和国的领土，破坏它的主权、安全和独立。因此，南也门人民共和国肯定要采取一切必要的措施来保卫它自己的安全和独立。
    照会还指出，南也门武装部队两个月前在赛义德的战斗中曾缴获大量的美国武器，在十月十四日庆祝南也门人民共和国国庆时，已在军事检阅中向公众展出了这些武器。
