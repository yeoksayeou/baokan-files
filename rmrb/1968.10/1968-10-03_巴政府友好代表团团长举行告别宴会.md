Title: The Head of the Pakistani Government Friendship Delegation Holds a Farewell Banquet
Author:
Date: 1968-10-03
Edition: 2

The head of the Pakistani Government Friendship Delegation, Advisor to the President of Pakistan Ayub Khan, Sayyid Fida Hassan, held a farewell banquet this evening.

Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and President of the China-Pakistan Friendship Association Saifuddin, as well as Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong and responsible officials of the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee Ding Guoyi, were invited to attend the banquet.

The banquet was held in a friendly atmosphere. Sayyid Fida Hassan and Vice Premier Chen Yi both spoke at the banquet.

Sayyid Fida Hassan said in his speech, "We are honored to have been received by the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao. We are grateful for his friendship. Chairman Mao's reception left us with the most precious memories. He praised the achievements of the Chinese people under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong. He said that the great progress made by the Chinese people in industrial development and accelerating production has left us with a very deep impression."

When talking about the further development of relations between the two countries, he pointed out that anyone who attempts to sabotage the development of friendship between Pakistan and China will not succeed. The friendship between Pakistan and China will contribute to the cause of justice, peace, and progress for humanity.

Vice Premier Chen Yi said in his speech, "The visit of Sayyid Fida Hassan and the Pakistani guests to our country has deepened our mutual understanding and made an important contribution to further consolidating and developing Sino-Pakistani friendship."

Vice Premier Chen Yi thanked the Pakistani government for publicly refuting the slander of certain malicious people who accused China of persecuting Muslims. He said, "The Pakistani government and people pursue an independent foreign policy, safeguard national independence and autonomy, oppose foreign aggression and interference, and adhere to the principle of Sino-Pakistani friendship. The Chinese government and people are greatly encouraged by this. We are firmly convinced that the hardworking and brave Pakistani people, by strengthening unity and vigilance, will be able to defeat any foreign aggression and interference. Pakistani friends can rest assured that the Chinese government and people will firmly support your just struggle. The Chinese people will forever be reliable friends of the Pakistani people."

Pakistani Ambassador to China Sultan M. Khan also attended the banquet.

### 巴政府友好代表团团长举行告别宴会

1968-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    巴政府友好代表团团长举行告别宴会
    周总理陈毅副总理郭沫若赛福鼎副委员长等出席
    赛义德·菲达·哈桑顾问在讲话中赞扬中国人民在毛主席领导下所取得的成就
    陈毅副总理说，巴基斯坦贵宾的访问为进一步巩固发展中巴友好作出重要贡献
    新华社二日讯　巴基斯坦政府友好代表团团长、巴基斯坦总统阿尤布·汗的顾问赛义德·菲达·哈桑，今天晚上举行告别宴会。
    国务院总理周恩来、副总理陈毅，人大常委会副委员长郭沫若，人大常委会副委员长、中巴友好协会会长赛福鼎，以及外交部副部长韩念龙和北京市革命委员会负责人丁国钰，应邀出席了宴会。
    宴会在友好的气氛中进行。赛义德·菲达·哈桑顾问和陈毅副总理在宴会上先后讲话。
    赛义德·菲达·哈桑在讲话中说，我和我的同事们荣幸地受到了中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席的接见，我们感激他对我们的友情。毛主席的接见给我们留下了最珍贵的记忆。他赞扬中国人民在毛泽东主席领导下所取得的成就。他说，中国人民在发展工业和加速生产方面所取得的巨大进展，给我们留下了极其深刻的印象。
    他在谈到巴中两国关系的进一步发展时指出，任何人企图破坏巴中两国友谊的发展，都是决不能得逞的。巴中两国的友谊将对人类的正义、和平和进步事业作出贡献。
    陈毅副总理在讲话中说，赛义德·菲达·哈桑和巴基斯坦贵宾们这次在我国访问，加深了我们彼此间的了解，为进一步巩固和发展中巴友好作出了重要贡献。
    陈毅副总理感谢巴基斯坦政府公开驳斥某些别有用心的人诬蔑中国迫害穆斯林的谰言。他说，巴基斯坦政府和人民奉行独立自主的外交政策，维护民族独立和自主，反对外来的侵略和干涉，坚持中巴友好的原则。中国政府和中国人民为此感到十分鼓舞。我们深信，勤劳勇敢的巴基斯坦人民，加强团结，提高警觉，就能够击败任何外来侵略和干涉的阴谋。巴基斯坦朋友们可以相信，中国政府和中国人民将坚决支持你们的正义斗争。中国人民将永远是巴基斯坦人民可靠的朋友。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆·汗也出席了宴会。
