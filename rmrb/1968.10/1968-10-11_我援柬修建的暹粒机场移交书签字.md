Title: The Handing Over Document for the Siem Reap Airport, Built with Chinese Aid, is Signed
Author:
Date: 1968-10-11
Edition: 4

The handing over document for the Siem Reap Airport, built with Chinese aid, was signed on the 5th in Phnom Penh.

The Chinese Embassy in Cambodia's Chargé d'Affaires ad interim, Zheng Sixiong, and Cambodia's Minister of Public Works and Telecommunications, Nuan Caiji, signed the document on behalf of their respective governments. At the signing ceremony, Nuan Caiji, on behalf of Cambodian Head of State Prince Sihanouk and the Cambodian people, once again expressed gratitude to the Chinese government and people for their aid to Cambodia.

The construction of the Siem Reap Airport was completed in April this year, and Prince Sihanouk personally presided over the completion ceremony on June 22nd.

### 我援柬修建的暹粒机场移交书签字

1968-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    我援柬修建的暹粒机场移交书签字
    新华社金边电　中华人民共和国政府和柬埔寨王国政府关于中国援助柬埔寨修建的暹粒机场的移交书，五日在这里签字。
    中国驻柬埔寨大使馆临时代办郑斯雄和柬埔寨公共工程和邮电大臣阮财基，分别代表两国政府在移交书上签字。在签字仪式上，阮财基代表柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王和柬埔寨人民，再一次感谢中国政府和人民对柬埔寨的援助。
    暹粒机场于今年四月全部建成，西哈努克亲王在六月二十二日亲自主持了落成仪式。
