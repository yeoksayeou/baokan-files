Author:
Date: 1968-10-30
Edition: 6

Pakistani President's Advisor Speaks at Chinese Embassy Reception: Sino-Pakistani Friendship Further Strengthened

Any Attempt by External Forces to Damage Sino-Pakistani Friendship Will Not Succeed

Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi: On the 24th, Advisor to Pakistani President Ayub Khan, Fida Hassan, stated here: "Any attempt by external forces to damage the friendship between Pakistan and China will not succeed."

Fida Hassan made these remarks at a reception held by the acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Pakistan, Xu Ying, on the evening of the 24th for the Pakistani government's friendly delegation, which had recently participated in the National Day activities of the People's Republic of China and was led by him.

Fida Hassan said that there is a good cooperative and friendly relationship between the Pakistani people and the Chinese people. This cooperation and friendship will be further strengthened in the future.

When talking about his impressions from his visit to China, he said that the tremendous progress China has made in all aspects, including industry, agriculture, and self-reliance in producing various advanced products, left a deep impression on him.

When discussing the Pakistani government's friendly delegation's visit to the Nanjing Yangtze River Bridge, he said that the completion of this bridge is a miracle in construction and typifies the self-reliance and creative spirit demonstrated by the Chinese people under the leadership of their great leader, Chairman Mao Zedong.

He said that an unlimited bright future lies ahead for China.

At the end of his speech, Fida Hassan wished Chairman Mao a long life.

Those present at the reception included Pakistani Foreign Minister Asad Hussian, Deputy Chairman of the Planning Commission Ahmad, and other high-ranking officials and prominent figures from various sectors of the Pakistani government.

The acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Pakistan, Xu Ying, also spoke at the reception.

After the reception, the Chinese color documentary film "Piano Accompaniment to 'The Red Lantern'" was screened and was warmly received by the audience.

### 巴基斯坦总统顾问在我驻巴使馆举行的招待会上说  巴中人民的合作和友谊进一步加强  外来机构想要损害巴中友谊的任何企图都是不能得逞的

1968-10-30
第6版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦总统顾问在我驻巴使馆举行的招待会上说
    巴中人民的合作和友谊进一步加强
    外来机构想要损害巴中友谊的任何企图都是不能得逞的
    新华社拉瓦尔品第电  巴基斯坦总统阿尤布·汗的顾问菲达·哈桑二十四日在这里说：“外来机构想要损害巴基斯坦和中国的友谊的任何企图，都是不能得逞的。”
    菲达·哈桑是在中国驻巴基斯坦大使馆临时代办徐英二十四日晚为最近参加中华人民共和国国庆活动的由他率领的巴基斯坦政府友好代表团举行的招待会上讲这番话的。
    菲达·哈桑说，巴基斯坦人民和中国人民之间存在着良好的合作和友谊关系。这种合作和友谊今后将会得到进一步的加强。
    他在谈到访华观感时说，中国在各方面，包括在工业、农业和通过自力更生生产各种高级产品方面取得的巨大进步，给了他深刻的印象。
    他在谈到巴基斯坦政府友好代表团参观南京长江大桥时说，这座大桥的建成是建筑上的一个奇迹，它典型地体现了中国人民在他们的伟大领袖毛泽东主席领导下所表现出来的自力更生和创造精神。
    他说，呈现在中国面前的是无限光明的前景。
    菲达·哈桑在讲话结束时祝愿毛主席万寿无疆。
    出席招待会的有：巴基斯坦外交部长阿沙德·侯赛因，计划委员会副主席艾哈迈德，以及巴基斯坦政府其他高级官员和各界著名人士。
    中国驻巴基斯坦大使馆临时代办徐英也在招待会上讲了话。
    招待会后，放映了中国彩色纪录影片《钢琴伴唱〈红灯记〉》，受到了观众的热烈欢迎。
