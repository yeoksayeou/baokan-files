Title: Cambodian Foreign Ministry Protests Thai Puppet Army Invasion
Author:
Date: 1968-10-17
Edition: 6

The Cambodian Foreign Ministry has lodged a strong protest against the Thai puppet army's armed invasion and banditry in Cambodia's Oddar Meanchey Province.

According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on October 14, the Royal Government of Cambodia's Foreign Ministry sent a note to the Thai puppet regime on October 8, strongly protesting the armed invasion and banditry by the Thai puppet army in Cambodia's Oddar Meanchey Province on September 12 and 16.

The Cambodian News Agency also reported that when the Thai puppet army attacked Cambodian positions on September 16, they were counterattacked by the Cambodian side, resulting in four deaths and five injuries among the Thai forces.

### 柬外交部抗议泰国傀儡军入侵

1968-10-17
第6版()
专栏：

    柬外交部抗议泰国傀儡军入侵
    新华社金边十四日电　据柬埔寨新闻社十四日报道，柬埔寨王国政府外交部十月八日照会泰国傀儡当局，强烈抗议泰国傀儡军队九月十二日和十六日对柬埔寨奥多棉藏省进行的武装侵略和强盗行径。
    柬埔寨新闻社还报道，泰国傀儡军队在九月十六日袭击柬埔寨阵地时，遭到柬埔寨方面的反击，被打死四人、打伤五人。
