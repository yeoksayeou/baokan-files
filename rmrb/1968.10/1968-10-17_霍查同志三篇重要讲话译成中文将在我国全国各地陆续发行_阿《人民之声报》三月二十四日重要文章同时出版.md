Title: Three Important Speeches by Comrade Hoxha to be Published Nationwide in Chinese Translation
Author:
Date: 1968-10-17
Edition: 3

The three important speeches by Comrade Enver Hoxha, the great leader of the Albanian people, have been translated and published as separate volumes by the People's Publishing House. The titles are "Further Revolutionizing the Party and the State," "Further Revolutionizing Our Schools," and "Speech at the Reception Held by the Chinese Embassy in Albania to Celebrate the 19th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China."

Additionally, a separate volume has been published containing the important article "The Working Class in Revisionist Countries Must Fight and Reestablish the Dictatorship of the Proletariat," which was published in the Albanian newspaper *People's Voice* on March 24, 1968.

These books will be available for purchase starting October 16 at the Xinhua Bookstore in Beijing and will gradually be available at Xinhua Bookstores across the country.

### 霍查同志三篇重要讲话译成中文将在我国全国各地陆续发行  阿《人民之声报》三月二十四日重要文章同时出版

1968-10-17
第3版()
专栏：

    霍查同志三篇重要讲话译成中文将在我国全国各地陆续发行
    阿《人民之声报》三月二十四日重要文章同时出版
    新华社十六日讯　阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖恩维尔·霍查同志的三篇重要讲话，已由人民出版社翻译出版单行本，书名是《党和政权的进一步革命化》、《关于我国学校的进一步革命化》、《在中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆举行的庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年的招待会上的讲话》。
    同时出版的单行本，还有阿尔巴尼亚《人民之声报》一九六八年三月二十四日发表的重要文章《修正主义国家的工人阶级必须投入战斗和重建无产阶级专政》。
    这几本书从十月十六日起在北京新华书店发行，并将在全国各地新华书店陆续发行。
