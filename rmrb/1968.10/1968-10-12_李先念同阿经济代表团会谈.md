Title: Li Xiannian Meets with Albanian Economic Delegation
Author:
Date: 1968-10-12
Edition: 5

China's Communist Party Central Political Bureau member and Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian held talks with the Albanian Government Economic Delegation led by Albanian Labor Party Central Political Bureau member and Vice Chairman of the Albanian Council of Ministers Adil Charcani on October 9.

The Albanian People's Republic Government Economic Delegation was invited by the Government of the People's Republic of China to visit China for friendly talks.

The Albanian side participating in the talks included members of the Albanian People's Republic Government Economic Delegation: Albanian Labor Party Central Political Bureau alternate member and Minister of Industry and Mining Koco Theodosi, Deputy Chairman of the Albanian State Planning Commission Pupo Shyti, Deputy Minister of Construction Rahman Hanku, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Vasili Kati, Deputy Minister of Agriculture Sari Kubati, and Commercial Counselor of the Albanian Embassy in China Kouzma.

The Chinese side participating in the talks included relevant officials Luo Guibo, Li Qiang, Xie Huai-de, and Yuan Hua-bing.

The talks were conducted in an extremely friendly and cordial atmosphere, fully reflecting the fraternal friendship and fighting unity between the two parties, the two governments, and the peoples of China and Albania.

### 李先念同阿经济代表团会谈

1968-10-12
第5版()
专栏：

    李先念同阿经济代表团会谈
    新华社十一日讯　中国共产党中央政治局委员、国务院副总理李先念，九日同阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、阿尔巴尼亚部长会议副主席阿迪尔·查尔查尼率领的阿尔巴尼亚政府经济代表团进行会谈。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府经济代表团是应中华人民共和国政府邀请，前来我国进行友好访问的。
    阿尔巴尼亚方面参加会谈的，还有阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府经济代表团团员：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局候补委员、工业和矿产部部长科乔·蒂奥多西，阿尔巴尼亚国家计划委员会副主席普波·希提，阿尔巴尼亚建工部副部长拉赫曼·汉库，阿尔巴尼亚贸易部副部长瓦西里·卡蒂，阿尔巴尼亚农业部副部长萨里·库巴蒂，阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使馆商务参赞考兹玛。
    中国方面参加会谈的，还有有关方面负责人罗贵波、李强、谢怀德、苑化冰等。
    会谈是在极为亲切友好的气氛中进行的，充分体现了中阿两党、两国政府和两国人民的兄弟情谊和战斗团结。
