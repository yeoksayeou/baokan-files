Title: Guo Moruo Hosts Banquet for Delegation from University of Tirana
Author:
Date: 1968-10-12
Edition: 5

Guo Moruo, president of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, hosted a reception and banquet tonight to warmly welcome the delegation from the University of Tirana, led by Vice-President Vehmi Shehu of the University of Tirana, People's Socialist Republic of Albania.

The reception and banquet were held in an atmosphere of great warmth and friendliness.

Present at the reception and banquet were officials from relevant departments.

Also present were Babaani, Counselor of the Albanian Embassy in China, and others.

### 郭沫若宴请地拉那大学代表团

1968-10-12
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若宴请地拉那大学代表团
    新华社十一日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若今晚接见并设宴热烈欢迎由阿尔巴尼亚国立地拉那大学副校长弗赫米·谢胡率领的阿尔巴尼亚国立地拉那大学代表团。
    接见和宴会是在极为亲切友好的气氛中进行的。
    接见和宴会时，在座的有我有关方面的负责人。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使馆参赞巴巴尼等也在座。
