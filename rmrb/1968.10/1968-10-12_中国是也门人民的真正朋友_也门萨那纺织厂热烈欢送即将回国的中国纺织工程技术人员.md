Title: China is a True Friend of the Yemeni People
Author:
Date: 1968-10-12
Edition: 5

The management committee chairman of the Sana'a Textile Factory in Yemen, Amil, hosted a banquet on October 1 to bid farewell to a group of Chinese textile engineering and technical personnel who are about to leave Yemen and return to their homeland.

Yemen's Minister of Economy, Abdul Aziz Abdulgani, attended the banquet and delivered a speech. He said that the Chinese engineering and technical personnel have made tremendous contributions to the Yemeni people, and on behalf of the government of the Yemen Arab Republic, he expressed deep gratitude for this. He said that China's aid is selfless, while the "aid" from imperialist countries is only for their own interests. In his speech, he also praised the great achievements of the Chinese people in building their own country under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong. Chairman Amil of the Sana'a Textile Factory Management Committee said in his speech that the Chinese engineering and technical personnel have been with us, sharing our hardships and persevering in their work during the most difficult and arduous times of our struggle. This shows that China is a true friend of Yemen. He requested that the Chinese friends convey the greetings and respect of the Yemeni people to Chairman Mao Zedong upon their return to their homeland.

The head of the Chinese textile technical team, Xie Wuyuan, also spoke at the banquet. He said that the Chinese engineering and technical personnel are following the teachings of the great leader Chairman Mao, working in cooperation with the Yemeni people to serve the Yemeni people. He praised the hardworking spirit of the Yemeni workers and technical personnel and expressed firm support for the just struggle of the Yemeni government and people against imperialist interference, subversion, and aggression.

### 中国是也门人民的真正朋友  也门萨那纺织厂热烈欢送即将回国的中国纺织工程技术人员

1968-10-12
第5版()
专栏：

    中国是也门人民的真正朋友
    也门萨那纺织厂热烈欢送即将回国的中国纺织工程技术人员
    新华社萨那电　也门萨那纺织厂管理委员会主席阿密尔一日晚上举行宴会，热烈欢送一批即将离开也门回国的中国纺织工程技术人员。
    也门经济部长阿卜杜勒·阿齐兹·阿卜杜勒加尼出席了宴会，并讲了话。他说，中国工程技术人员为也门人民作出了巨大贡献，他代表阿拉伯也门共和国政府对此表示深切的感谢。他说，中国的援助是无私的，而帝国主义国家的“援助”只是为了它们自己的私利。他在讲话中还赞扬中国人民在毛泽东主席的英明领导下，依靠自己的力量在建设自己的国家方面所取得的巨大成就。萨那纺织厂管理委员会主席阿密尔在讲话时说，中国工程技术人员在我们斗争最困难最艰苦的时刻和我们一起同甘共苦坚持工作，这说明中国是也门的真正朋友。他要求中国朋友回国后转达也门人民对毛泽东主席的问候和敬意。
    中国纺织技术工作组负责人谢武元也在宴会上讲了话。他说，我们中国工程技术人员是遵照伟大领袖毛主席的教导，在也门人民的协作下为也门人民服务的。他赞扬也门工人和技术人员的艰苦奋斗的工作精神，并表示坚决支持也门政府和人民所进行的反对帝国主义干涉、颠覆和侵略的正义斗争。
