Title: Great Leader Chairman Mao Meets with Revolutionary Comrades from Across the Country
Author:
Date: 1968-10-02
Edition: 4

The great leader Chairman Mao, along with his close comrade-in-arms Comrade Lin Biao and Premier Zhou Enlai, warmly met with revolutionary comrades from all over the country. The representatives enthusiastically shouted, "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Wishing Chairman Mao eternal life!"

The photographs on this page were taken by the Capital National Day Photography Reporting Team.
