Title: Seven Rivets (and One More)
Author: Zheng Chengyi
Date: 1968-10-09
Edition: 4

	Seven Rivets (and One More)
	Zheng Chengyi

	Eaglets spread their wings and soar,
	The apprentice tries the rivet gun for the first time,
	A rivet, a red flower,
	The youth's face glows with red light.
	After work, a storm chases after them,
	The master calmly climbs the steel beam,
	Each rivet is checked,
	At the post-shift meeting, the master speaks—
	“Today, the last seven rivets,
	Loose in thought,
	‘Our responsibility is to the people,’
	Riveters must understand the weight of rivets!”
	From then on, whenever the youth picks up the rivet gun,
	He hears the train rumbling,
	Motherland, test us!
	Each rivet stands guard for you.
	The Yangtze River Bridge is completed,
	Each rivet rings clearly;
	The youth forever holds high the Little Red Book,
	Tightly riveted to the post of building the motherland!

### 七颗铆钉（外一首）
郑成义
1968-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    七颗铆钉（外一首）
    郑成义
    雏鹰展翅飞翔，
    艺徒初试铆枪，
    一颗铆钉，一朵红花，
    青年满脸红光。
    下班，暴风雨追上来了，
    师傅从容不迫攀上钢梁，
    颗颗铆钉检查完了，
    班后会上师傅把话讲——
    “今天，最后七颗铆钉，
    松就松在思想上，
    ‘我们的责任，是向人民负责’，
    铆工应该懂得铆钉的份量！”
    此后，青年每当端起铆枪，
    就听见列车在隆隆轰响，
    祖国，您检验吧！
    颗颗铆钉在为您站岗。
    长江大桥架成了，
    颗颗铆钉当当响；
    青年永远高举红宝书，
    紧紧铆在建设祖国的岗位上！
