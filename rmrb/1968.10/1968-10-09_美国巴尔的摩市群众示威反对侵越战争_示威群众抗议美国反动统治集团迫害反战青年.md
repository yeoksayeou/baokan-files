Title: Baltimore Residents Protest U.S. Invasion of Vietnam and Persecution of Anti-War Youth
Author:
Date: 1968-10-09
Edition: 6

	Washington, October 8 (Xinhua) -- More than 2,000 people in Baltimore, Maryland, held a large-scale demonstration and march on October 7 to oppose U.S. imperialism's war of aggression against Vietnam and to strongly protest the persecution of American youth who refuse to be cannon fodder for the U.S. reactionary ruling clique.

	The federal court in Baltimore was scheduled to "trial" nine American youths on October 7 for publicly burning their draft cards in protest against the war in Vietnam. The U.S. reactionary ruling clique's suppression of American youth opposing the war in Vietnam has sparked strong resistance from the broad masses of the American people.

	To support these nine youths and protest the persecution of the U.S. reactionary ruling clique, more than 2,000 people in Baltimore carried placards demanding the withdrawal of U.S. troops from Vietnam and marched through the streets of Baltimore for more than two hours on October 7. This forced the U.S. reactionary ruling clique to announce the postponement of the "trial."

### 美国巴尔的摩市群众示威反对侵越战争  示威群众抗议美国反动统治集团迫害反战青年

1968-10-09
第6版()
专栏：

    美国巴尔的摩市群众示威反对侵越战争
    示威群众抗议美国反动统治集团迫害反战青年
    新华社八日讯　华盛顿消息：美国巴尔的摩市二千多人七日举行大规模示威游行，反对美帝国主义侵略越南的战争，并强烈抗议美国反动统治集团迫害拒绝为他们当炮灰的美国青年。
    美国巴尔的摩市的联邦法院准备在七日开庭“审判”不久前为了反对侵越战争而公开焚毁征兵证的九名美国青年。美国反动统治集团镇压美国青年反对侵越战争的行径，引起美国广大群众的强烈反抗。
    为了声援这九名青年，抗议美国反动统治集团对他们的迫害，巴尔的摩市的二千余名群众七日举着要求美国从越南撤退侵略军等标语牌，在巴尔的摩市区内举行了两个多小时的示威游行，迫使美国反动统治集团不得不宣布推迟进行“审判”。
