Title: Facing the Sun
Author: Qún Xiàngyáng
Date: 1968-10-15
Edition: 4

More than forty educated youth who have returned to their hometowns in Dongcheng Brigade, Dongcheng Commune, Yangyuan County, Hebei Province, are resolutely following the path of integrating with workers, peasants, and soldiers as guided by Chairman Mao. They are receiving re-education from workers, peasants, and soldiers. This is a scene of them studying Mao Zedong Thought during their rest time from labor.
