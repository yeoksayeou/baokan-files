Title: Long Live Chairman Mao
Author: Hong Bing
Date: 1968-10-07
Edition: 6

	Your works are so precious,
	Shining with golden light,
	Containing boundless wisdom.
	Your calls are like thunder in spring,
	Scaring the enemies' hearts to pieces,
	Giving us unshakable confidence.
	You are the red sun,
	We are sunflowers,
	The sunlight makes the sunflowers more beautiful.
	We raise the red flag high,
	Passing it down from generation to generation,
	The bright red color will never fade.
	The thought of Mao Zedong will forever shine,
	We shout, Long live Chairman Mao,
	Long live! Long live! Ten thousand years!

### 毛主席万岁
红兵
1968-10-07
第6版()
专栏：

    毛主席万岁
    红兵
    您的著作多宝贵，
    闪烁金色的光辉，
    蕴藏无穷的智慧。
    您的号召如春雷，
    吓得敌人心胆碎，
    使我们信心百倍。
    您是红太阳，
    我们是向日葵，
    阳光照得葵花更美。
    我们高举红旗，
    一辈传一辈，
    鲜红颜色永不褪。
    毛泽东思想永放光辉，
    我们高呼毛主席万岁，
    万岁！万岁！万万岁！
