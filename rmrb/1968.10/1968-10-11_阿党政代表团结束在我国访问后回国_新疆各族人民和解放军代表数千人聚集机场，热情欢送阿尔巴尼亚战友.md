Title: Albanian Party and Government Delegation Concludes Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1968-10-11
Edition: 1

By the Albanian Labor Party Politburo member, Vice Premier and Minister of Defense Bekir Balluku, an Albanian Party and Government delegation concluded its friendly visit to China and returned home by plane on the 9th.

Chinese People's Liberation Army Vice Chief of Staff Wang Xinting, Vice Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Wang Enmao and Pei Zhouyu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qiao Qianhua and other comrades went to the airport to see them off.

Also present at the airport to see them off were Vice Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Yang Liye, Hu Liangcai, Ziya, Wu Julu, Chief of Staff of Xinjiang Military Region Tan Kaiyun, Cao Danuo, Kong Ruiyun, Li Jingshan, Wang Xiangxiong, and Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of Urumqi City Huang Yu and other comrades. Representatives of workers of various ethnic groups, poor and lower-middle peasants, Five Good Soldiers of the People's Liberation Army, military farmers, and Red Guards were also present.

Albanian Ambassador to China Nataneli also came to the airport to see them off.

The airport was adorned with the national flags of China and Albania, and portraits of the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao, and the great leader of the Albanian people, Comrade Enver Hoxha. Thousands of representatives from various ethnic groups and the People's Liberation Army gathered at the airport to enthusiastically see off the Albanian comrades.

Comrade Balluku and others, accompanied by Comrades Wang Xinting, Wang Enmao, Pei Zhouyu, and Qiao Qianhua, walked around the airport to bid farewell to the welcoming crowd. At this moment, the airport was filled with the sound of drums and cheers. The welcoming crowd waved the national flags of China and Albania, shouting slogans: "Workers of the world, unite!" "Workers and oppressed peoples and nations of the world, unite!" "Down with U.S. imperialism! Down with Soviet revisionism! Down with all reactionaries!" "Long live China-Albania friendship!" "Long live Comrade Enver Hoxha!" "Long live Chairman Mao!"

Before boarding the plane, Comrade Balluku and others shook hands and warmly embraced Comrades Wang Xinting, Wang Enmao, and others.

As the plane took off, the welcoming crowd waved red books of "Quotations from Chairman Mao," wishing the Albanian comrades a safe journey.

### 阿党政代表团结束在我国访问后回国  新疆各族人民和解放军代表数千人聚集机场，热情欢送阿尔巴尼亚战友

1968-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

    阿党政代表团结束在我国访问后回国
    新疆各族人民和解放军代表数千人聚集机场，热情欢送阿尔巴尼亚战友
    新华社乌鲁木齐十日电  由阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、部长会议副主席兼国防部长贝基尔·巴卢库同志率领的阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团，结束了在我国的友好访问，九日乘飞机回国。
    中国人民解放军副总参谋长王新亭，新疆维吾尔自治区革命委员会副主任王恩茂、裴周玉，外交部副部长乔冠华等同志，前往机场欢送。
    到机场欢送的，还有新疆维吾尔自治区革命委员会副主任杨立业、胡良才、孜牙、吴巨轮，新疆军区、新疆军区生产建设兵团、七三三五部队的负责人谭开云、曹达诺夫、孔瑞云、李荆山、王香雄，以及乌鲁木齐市革命委员会主任黄瑜等同志。到机场欢送的还有各族工人的代表和贫下中农、解放军五好战士、军垦战士、红卫兵小将的代表。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使纳塔奈利同志也到机场送行。
    机场上空飘扬着中国和阿尔巴尼亚两国国旗，矗立着中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席的画像和阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖恩维尔·霍查同志的画像。各族人民和解放军代表数千人聚集在机场上，热情欢送阿尔巴尼亚战友。
    巴卢库同志等由王新亭、王恩茂、裴周玉、乔冠华等同志陪同，绕场一周，同欢送群众告别。这时，机场上锣鼓齐鸣，欢呼声四起。欢送的群众挥动中阿两国国旗，不断高呼口号：“全世界无产者联合起来！”“全世界无产者同被压迫人民、被压迫民族联合起来！”“打倒美帝！打倒苏修！打倒各国反动派！”“中国阿尔巴尼亚友谊万岁！”“恩维尔·霍查同志万岁！”“毛主席万岁！”
    巴卢库同志等在走上飞机前，同王新亭、王恩茂等同志亲切握手，热烈拥抱。
    飞机起飞了，欢送的人们挥动红色宝书《毛主席语录》，祝阿尔巴尼亚战友一路平安。
