Title: Workers' Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Teams Enter Changchun Geological Institute
Author:
Date: 1968-10-11
Edition: 1

After the Workers' Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Teams entered Changchun Geological Institute, they vigorously grasped class struggle and the struggle between the two lines, and seriously carried out the struggle, criticism, and reform. The picture shows members of the propaganda team from Liaoyuan Mining Bureau using the miserable experiences of miners in the old society being exploited and oppressed by capitalists to link with the reality of class struggle, and re-educating revolutionary teachers and students.
