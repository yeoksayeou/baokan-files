Title: Vice Chairman Lin Biao Meets Comrade Baluku
Author:
Date: 1968-10-03
Edition: 1

Vice Chairman Lin Biao met Comrade Baluku.

Vice Chairman Lin Biao warmly shook hands with Comrade Baluku and had a very cordial conversation.

Those present at the meeting included General Chief of Staff Huang Yongsheng, Deputy Chief of Staff Wu Faxian, and Deputy Chief of Staff Wen Yucheng.

	BEIJING, Oct. 2 (Xinhua) -- Lin Biao, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Minister of National Defense, met today with Bekir Baluku, Head of the Albanian Party and Government Delegation, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and Minister of National Defense.

	During the meeting, Vice Chairman Lin Biao warmly shook hands with Comrade Baluku and had a very cordial conversation.

	General Chief of Staff Huang Yongsheng, Deputy Chief of Staff Wu Faxian, and Deputy Chief of Staff Wen Yucheng of the Chinese People's Liberation Army were also present at the meeting.

### 林彪副主席会见巴卢库同志  林副主席同巴卢库同志热烈握手，进行了极为亲切的谈话  会见时在座的有总参谋长黄永胜同志，副总参谋长吴法宪、温玉成同志

1968-10-03
第1版()
专栏：

    林彪副主席会见巴卢库同志
    林副主席同巴卢库同志热烈握手，进行了极为亲切的谈话
    会见时在座的有总参谋长黄永胜同志，副总参谋长吴法宪、温玉成同志
    新华社二日讯　中国共产党中央委员会副主席、国防部长林彪同志，今天下午会见了阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团团长、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、部长会议副主席兼国防部长贝基尔·巴卢库同志。
    会见的时候，林彪副主席同巴卢库同志热烈握手，并且进行了极为亲切的谈话。
    中国人民解放军总参谋长黄永胜同志，副总参谋长吴法宪、温玉成同志，会见时也在座。
