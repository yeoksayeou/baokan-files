Title: Celebrating Zambia's Fourth Anniversary of Independence
Author:
Date: 1968-10-24
Edition: 5

For the celebration of the fourth anniversary of the independence of the Republic of Zambia, the China-Africa Friendship Association held a reception today.

The Chargé d'Affaires of the Zambian Embassy in China, Mr. Lembi, and other diplomatic staff from the embassy were invited to attend the reception.

Vice President of the China-Africa Friendship Association, Ding Xilin, and Mr. Lembi, the Chargé d'Affaires, both spoke at the reception, expressing their wishes for the continuous strengthening and development of the friendship between the peoples of China and Zambia.

Relevant officials such as Qiao Guanhua, Xie Huai-de, and Zhou Huamin attended the reception.

### 庆祝赞比亚独立四周年  中非友协举行招待会

1968-10-24
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝赞比亚独立四周年
    中非友协举行招待会
    新华社二十三日讯  为庆祝赞比亚共和国独立四周年，中非人民友好协会今天举行招待会。
    赞比亚驻中国大使馆临时代办伦比和使馆外交官员应邀出席了招待会。
    中非友协副会长丁西林和伦比临时代办在招待会上讲话，共祝中赞两国人民的友谊不断巩固和发展。
    各有关方面负责人乔冠华、谢怀德、周化民等出席了招待会。
