Title: Albanian Government Economic Delegation Visits Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1968-10-20
Edition: 6

An economic delegation from the Albanian government, led by Deputy Premier Adil Charchani of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania, arrived in Tianjin on the morning of the 18th for a friendly visit.

On the evening of the 18th, the Tianjin Revolutionary Committee held a banquet to welcome the Albanian comrades. At the banquet, Yang Yinsheng, a responsible member of the Tianjin Revolutionary Committee, and Comrade Charchani spoke. They jointly toasted the unbreakable fighting friendship between the peoples of China and Albania, and wished the great leader of the Albanian people, Comrade Enver Hoxha, and the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao, good health and longevity.

Accompanying the Albanian government economic delegation to Tianjin for the visit were Xie Huai-de, Deputy Director of the State Economic Relations Commission of China, Wang Yingxian, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Water Resources and Electric Power, and responsible members of the Tianjin Revolutionary Committee, the People's Liberation Army stationed in Tianjin, Liu Zheng, Wang Yi, and representatives of the Tianjin Workers' Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Team. They attended the banquet. After the banquet, the Albanian comrades attended a gala evening hosted by the Tianjin Revolutionary Committee and watched performances by the Tianjin Workers' Amateur Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Team and the Red Guards' Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Team, which sang and danced in praise of the great friendship between the two parties, two countries, and two peoples of China and Albania.

The Albanian comrades visited Tianjin for two days on the 18th and 19th, receiving extremely warm welcomes and send-offs. During the two days, the Albanian comrades visited the Tianjin Textile Machinery Factory, Tianjin Watch Factory, and Tianjin New Port. At Tianjin New Port, the Albanian comrades visited the "Jianshe" ship docked in the harbor.

### 阿尔巴尼亚政府经济代表团到天津访问

1968-10-20
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚政府经济代表团到天津访问
    新华社天津十九日电　由阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议副主席阿迪尔·查尔查尼同志率领的阿尔巴尼亚政府经济代表团，十八日上午到天津进行友好访问。
    十八日晚上，天津市革命委员会举行宴会，欢迎阿尔巴尼亚战友。宴会上，天津市革命委员会负责成员杨银声和查尔查尼同志讲了话。他们共祝中、阿两国人民牢不可破的战斗友谊不断发展和巩固，共祝阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖恩维尔·霍查同志和中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席健康长寿。
    陪同阿尔巴尼亚政府经济代表团到天津访问的我国对外经济联络委员会副主任谢怀德，水利电力部副部长王英先，以及天津市革命委员会、中国人民解放军驻天津部队负责成员刘政、王一等和天津市工人毛泽东思想宣传队代表，出席了宴会。宴会后，阿尔巴尼亚战友出席了天津市革命委员会举行的联欢晚会，观看了天津市工人业余毛泽东思想宣传队和红卫兵毛泽东思想宣传队演出的歌颂中、阿两党、两国和两国人民的伟大友谊的革命歌舞。
    阿尔巴尼亚战友十八、十九日在天津参观访问了两天，受到了极其热烈的迎送。两天中，阿尔巴尼亚战友参观了天津市纺织机械厂、天津市手表厂和天津新港。在天津新港，阿尔巴尼亚战友参观了停泊在港内的我国“建设”号轮船。
