Title: Brimming with the Brotherly Affection of 700 Million Chinese People, the Albanian Party and Government Delegation Returns to Tirana
Author:
Date: 1968-10-14
Edition: 5

A delegation from the Albanian Party and Government, led by Bekir Balluku, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania, and Minister of Defense, returned to Tirana by plane on October 13. The delegation had been invited by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Government of the People's Republic of China to visit China and participate in the celebrations of the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

At the airport to warmly welcome the delegation were: Mehmed Shehu, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania; Hysni Kapo and Ramiz Alia, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and Central Secretaries; Haki Toska, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers; Manush Myltyri, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee; Spiro Koleka, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and Chairman of the State Planning Commission; Petrit Dumo and Pilo Peristeri, alternate members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee; and Albanian government ministers Nestor Nas, Kico Ngjela, and Xhevdet Peçi. Also present were Central Committee members Behar Shytura, Alen Pashko, and Kristaq Dhamo; Secretary-General of the Council of Ministers Kico Kasapi; Head of the International Department of the Central Committee Piro Bita; and Deputy Minister of Defense Tanush Shyti, among others.

The Chinese Charge d'Affaires in Albania, Li Tingqian, military attache Li Zhongxuan, other embassy staff, as well as Chinese engineering and technical personnel and student representatives in Albania, were also at the airport to warmly welcome the Albanian Party and Government delegation.

### 满载七亿中国人民的兄弟情谊  阿党政代表团回到地拉那  谢胡卡博阿利雅等同志到机场热烈欢迎

1968-10-14
第5版()
专栏：

    满载七亿中国人民的兄弟情谊
    阿党政代表团回到地拉那
    谢胡卡博阿利雅等同志到机场热烈欢迎
    新华社地拉那十三日电　由阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议副主席兼国防部长贝基尔·巴卢库同志率领的阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团，应中国共产党中央委员会和中华人民共和国政府的邀请，在对中国进行了友好访问，并参加了中华人民共和国成立十九周年的庆祝活动以后，十三日乘飞机回到地拉那。
    到机场热烈欢迎代表团的有：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席穆罕默德·谢胡，劳动党中央政治局委员、中央书记希斯尼·卡博和拉米兹·阿利雅，劳动党中央政治局委员、部长会议副主席哈基·托斯卡，劳动党中央政治局委员马努什·穆夫蒂乌，劳动党中央政治局委员、国家计划委员会主席斯皮罗·科列加，劳动党中央政治局候补委员彼得里特·杜米和皮洛·皮里斯特里，阿尔巴尼亚政府部长奈斯蒂·纳赛、基乔·恩杰拉、谢夫切特·佩奇，劳动党中央委员贝哈尔·什图拉、艾莱妮·帕什科、克里斯塔契·多拉库，部长会议秘书长基乔·卡萨皮，劳动党中央国际部部长皮罗·比塔，阿尔巴尼亚国防部副部长塔努什·舒蒂等。中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办李庭荃，武官李钟玄，大使馆其他工作人员，以及在阿尔巴尼亚的中国工程技术人员和留学生代表等也到机场热烈欢迎阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团。
