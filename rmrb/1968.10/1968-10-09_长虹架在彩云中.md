Title: An Arch of Light in the Clouds
Author: Hu Zhiliang
Date: 1968-10-09
Edition: 4

	Long rainbow arching in the clouds,
	Four directions, Hu Zhiliang

	Under the blue sky, courageous and bold,
	Shouldering the sun, moon, and stars,
	Bridge builders with the spirit of Yu Gong,
	Move mountains and seas to build a great bridge.

	Welding women ride the clouds,
	Skillfully cutting steel to frame a rainbow,
	Silver light flashes, golden flowers fall,
	Long rainbow arching in the clouds.

	In the harsh winter, snow flies and ice seals the path,
	The river's cold wind roars like a tiger,
	Riveters step onto the bridge,
	Guns in hand, laughing towards the sky.

	Bridge builders are heroes,
	With colorful brushes, painting a long rainbow,
	Underneath the vast waves,
	A loyal heart faces the red sun.

### 长虹架在彩云中
胡志良
1968-10-09
第4版()
专栏：南京长江大桥工地诗选

    长虹架在彩云中
    四处　胡志良
    头顶蓝天胆气豪，
    日月星辰肩上挑，
    桥工立下愚公志，
    移山倒海修大桥。
    电焊女工驾彩云，
    巧剪钢铁架长虹，
    银光闪闪金花落，
    长虹架在彩云中。
    严冬飞雪冰封道，
    江面寒风如虎啸，
    铆工迈步上桥梁，
    双手持枪向天笑。
    桥梁工人是英雄，
    手持彩笔画长虹，
    波涛万顷脚下踩，
    一颗忠心向阳红。
