Title: Vietnamese Ministry of Culture Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1968-10-08
Edition: 5

The delegation of the Ministry of Culture of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by Vice Minister Qu Hoi Kim, concluded its visit to our country and departed Beijing by train this evening to return home.

During their visit to our country, the delegation toured several factories and people's communes in Beijing and Shanghai and was warmly welcomed by revolutionary masses.

When the delegation left Beijing, Vice Minister Luo Guibo went to the station to see them off. The acting charge d'affaires of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, Leh Zhongshi, also went to the station to bid farewell.

### 越南文化部代表团离京回国

1968-10-08
第5版()
专栏：

    越南文化部代表团离京回国
    新华社七日讯　由越南民主共和国文化部副部长瞿辉瑾率领的越南民主共和国文化部代表团，结束了在我国的访问，今晚乘火车离开北京回国。
    代表团在我国访问期间，曾在北京、上海参观了一些工厂和人民公社等单位，受到革命群众的热烈欢迎。
    代表团离开北京时，罗贵波副部长到车站欢送。越南驻中国大使馆临时代办黎终始也到车站送行。
