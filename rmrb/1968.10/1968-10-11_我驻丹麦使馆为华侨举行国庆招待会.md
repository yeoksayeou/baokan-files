Title: The Chinese Embassy in Denmark Holds a Reception for Overseas Chinese to Celebrate National Day
Author:
Date: 1968-10-11
Edition: 4

The Chinese Embassy in Denmark held receptions for overseas Chinese on the 6th and 9th to enthusiastically celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Many overseas Chinese at the reception wished Chairman Mao a long life. They also enthusiastically celebrated the great victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in the motherland. The reception also screened the color documentary film "Piano Accompaniment to 'The Red Lantern.'" Many overseas Chinese were delighted and inspired by the great achievements of socialist construction in their motherland.

Additionally, the Denmark-China Cultural Association recently held a meeting to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

### 我驻丹麦使馆为华侨举行国庆招待会

1968-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    我驻丹麦使馆为华侨举行国庆招待会
    新华社讯　哥本哈根消息：中国驻丹麦大使馆六日和九日为旅丹华侨举行招待会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。
    许多华侨在招待会上敬祝毛主席万寿无疆。他们还热情祝贺祖国无产阶级文化大革命的伟大胜利。在招待会上还放映了彩色纪录影片《钢琴伴唱〈红灯记〉》。许多华侨对祖国社会主义建设的伟大成就感到欢欣鼓舞。
    此外，丹麦中国文化协会最近也举行集会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。
