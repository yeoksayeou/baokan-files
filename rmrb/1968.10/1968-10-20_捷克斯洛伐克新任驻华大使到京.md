Title: New Ambassador of Czechoslovakia Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-10-20
Edition: 6

The new Ambassador of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic to China, Stanislav Kohoutsek, arrived in Beijing today.

New China News Agency, October 19th: The new Ambassador of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic to China, Stanislav Kohoutsek, arrived in Beijing today.

### 捷克斯洛伐克新任驻华大使到京

1968-10-20
