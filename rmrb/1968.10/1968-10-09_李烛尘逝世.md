Title: Li Zhu Chen Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1968-10-09
Edition: 2

Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association, and Minister of the First Light Industry Department Li Zhu Chen passed away at Peking University Hospital at 9:40 AM on October 7, 1968, at the age of 87.

### 李烛尘逝世

1968-10-09
第2版()
专栏：

    李烛尘逝世
    本报讯　全国政协副主席、全国民建会副主任委员、第一轻工业部部长李烛尘，于一九六八年十月七日上午九时四十分，病故于北大医院，享年八十七岁。
