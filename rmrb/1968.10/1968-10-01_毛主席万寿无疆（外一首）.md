Title: Long Live Chairman Mao Forever (Plus One More Poem)
Author: Yan Kehe
Date: 1968-10-01
Edition: 4

	Long Live Chairman Mao Forever (Plus One More Poem)
	Shanghai Soap Factory	Yan Kehe

	The pine and cypress trees are evergreen in all four seasons,
	The Yangtze River flows eastward forever,
	The red sun shines brightly for a thousand autumns,
	Long live Chairman Mao forever!
