Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with the Vietnamese Ministry of Culture Delegation
Author:
Date: 1968-10-06
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council held a reception for Vice Minister Qu Huy Chinh of the Vietnamese Ministry of Culture and the delegation from the Vietnamese Ministry of Culture led by him this evening. They had a friendly conversation.

Present at the reception were Vice Minister Luo Guibo and others.

After the reception, Vice Premier Chen Yi hosted a banquet in honor of the Vietnamese guests.

### 陈毅副总理接见越文化部代表团

1968-10-06
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见越文化部代表团
    新华社五日讯　国务院副总理陈毅，今天晚上接见越南民主共和国文化部副部长瞿辉瑾和由他率领的越南民主共和国文化部代表团，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有，罗贵波副部长等。
    接见后，陈毅副总理设宴招待越南客人。
