Title: Chinese Embassies in France, Denmark, Netherlands, and Norway Hold National Day Receptions
Author:
Date: 1968-10-04
Edition: 5

The chargé d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in France, Yi Suyizhi, held a reception on the evening of October 1 to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Over a thousand people from various circles attended the reception. French government officials and ambassadors from several countries also attended. Many progressive friends at the reception warmly congratulated the great victory of China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and the great achievements of China's socialist construction. They also warmly wished the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao, eternal life!

The chargé d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Denmark, Ding Hao, held a reception on the evening of September 30 to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. About 300 people, including Danish government and military officials, representatives from various circles, and diplomats from other countries, attended the reception.

The reception was held in a friendly atmosphere.

The chargé d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in the Netherlands, Liao Hexu, held a reception in The Hague on September 30 to warmly celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Hundreds of Dutch friends from various circles attended the reception, along with officials from the Dutch Foreign Ministry and diplomats from several countries. The reception was filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere throughout. Dutch friends at the reception warmly wished the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao, eternal life, and congratulated the great victory of China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

The chargé d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Norway, Liu Minghua, held a reception in Oslo on October 1 to warmly celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Attendees included Norwegian Minister of Labor and Municipal Affairs Sepp, Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs Jakobson, Secretary-General of the Foreign Ministry Boi, and other high-ranking military and political figures. Also present were Norwegian friends from various circles and diplomats from several countries.

At the reception, many friends warmly congratulated the great victory of China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and wished the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao, eternal life!

### 我驻法丹荷挪使节举行国庆招待会  进步朋友热情祝愿毛主席万寿无疆

1968-10-04
第5版()
专栏：

    我驻法丹荷挪使节举行国庆招待会
    进步朋友热情祝愿毛主席万寿无疆
    据新华社讯　我驻法国大使馆临时代办易素之一日晚举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。
    出席招待会的有各界人士一千多人。法国政府官员和一些国家的驻法使节出席了招待会。出席招待会的许多进步朋友热情地祝贺中国无产阶级文化大革命的伟大胜利和中国社会主义建设的伟大成就，并且热情地祝愿中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席万寿无疆！
    我驻丹麦大使馆临时代办丁浩在九月三十日晚上举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。出席招待会的有丹麦政府和军队的官员、各界人士以及各国外交使节等约三百人。
    招待会在友好气氛中进行。
    我驻荷兰代办处临时代办廖和舒九月三十日在海牙举行招待会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。
    数百名荷兰各界友好人士出席了招待会，荷兰外交部官员以及一些国家驻荷兰的使节也参加了招待会。招待会上自始至终洋溢着热烈友好的气氛。荷兰友好人士在招待会上，热情祝愿中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席万寿无疆！祝贺中国无产阶级文化大革命的伟大胜利。
    我驻挪威大使馆临时代办刘明华一日在奥斯陆举行招待会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。
    出席招待会的有：挪威劳工和市政大臣塞普，外交部国务秘书雅柯勃逊，外交部秘书长鲍依，一些高级将领和其他军政人员。出席招待会的还有挪威各界友好人士以及一些国家驻挪威的外交使节。
    在招待会上，许多朋友热情祝贺中国无产阶级文化大革命的伟大胜利，祝愿中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席万寿无疆！
