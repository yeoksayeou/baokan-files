Title: Workers at Beijing First Machine Tool Plant Express Love for Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1968-10-06
Edition: 5

Revolutionary workers at the Beijing First Machine Tool Plant, filled with boundless love for Chairman Mao, stand in front of the newly completed B-89 template milling machine. They hold the first template-processed portrait of Chairman Mao and raise high the red book of revolution, sincerely wishing Chairman Mao eternal life! Eternal life!
