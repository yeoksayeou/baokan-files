Title: East German Ambassador to China Completes His Term and Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1968-10-13
Edition: 5

East German Ambassador to China Completes His Term and Returns Home

New China News Agency, October 12th: The Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Comrade Birlbach, completed his term and returned to his home country today.
