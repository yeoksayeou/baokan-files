Title: Uganda Celebrates Its Sixth Anniversary of Independence
Author:
Date: 1968-10-12
Edition: 5

In Kampala, the capital of Uganda, a military parade and mass rally were held on the 9th to celebrate Uganda's sixth anniversary of independence.

Uganda's President Obote reviewed the troops and delivered a speech.

Since the 5th, the people of Uganda have been holding various activities to celebrate the sixth anniversary of their independence. Large-scale mass parades were held in Kampala and another Ugandan city, Jinja, on the 5th and 6th respectively.

### 乌干达庆祝独立六周年

1968-10-12
第5版()
专栏：

    乌干达庆祝独立六周年
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆十日电　坎帕拉消息：在乌干达首都坎帕拉，九日举行了阅兵式和群众大会，庆祝乌干达独立六周年。
    乌干达总统奥博特检阅了部队，并发表了讲话。
    从五日起，乌干达人民就开始举行各种活动，欢庆独立六周年。在坎帕拉和乌干达另一城市金贾，曾先后在五日和六日举行了大规模群众游行。
