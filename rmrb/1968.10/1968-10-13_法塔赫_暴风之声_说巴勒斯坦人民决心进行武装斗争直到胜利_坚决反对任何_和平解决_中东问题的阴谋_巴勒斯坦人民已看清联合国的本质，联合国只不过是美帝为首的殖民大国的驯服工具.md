Title: Fatah's "Voice of the Storm" Declares Palestinian People's Determination to Fight Until Victory
Author:
Date: 1968-10-13
Edition: 5

Fatah's "Voice of the Storm" radio station recently issued a commentary stating: "The Palestinian Arab people do not endorse any talk of peaceful solutions, peace plans, or agreements regarding the so-called Middle East crisis. Because our people are determined to continue the armed struggle until victory. They do not recognize any resolutions published by the United Nations or any other organization, whether by major or minor powers."

This radio station, in response to the Soviet revisionist leadership's collusion with American imperialism to impose a so-called peaceful solution to the Middle East crisis on the Arab people, aiming to further control the Arab people, said: "No matter how great the force, it cannot stop our people's resolve and revolution. We have embarked on the correct path to liberate our land, the path of people's revolution. We are revolutionary people and will not allow the schemes of certain major powers to succeed. We will not deviate from the path we have chosen."

The radio station said these international merchants treat Palestinian Arabs as some commodity in their hands. They are now desperately defending Zionism in Israel in the lounges of the United Nations. However, the Palestinian Arabs are very clear about what the United Nations is. The radio station pointed out, "Since its establishment after World War II, the United Nations has been a tool for colonialist countries and international Zionism to realize their schemes and ambitions and to justify their aggression. Sometimes, the United Nations itself is actively engaged in aggressive activities. In short, the United Nations is merely a tame tool and a puppet subjected to the pressure of the colonial powers led by the United States."

The radio station said the Palestinian people have recognized the true nature of the United Nations and have no hope for it. Therefore, "The Palestinian people firmly believe that armed revolution is the only path to liberation. The main battlefield is in Palestine, not anywhere else. The Palestinian people can defend their rights with weapons. The only way to achieve the Palestinian cause is now in the hands of its people, which is the weapons in the hands of the guerrillas, and with them, victory can be achieved."

The radio station also reminded the people to be wary of the conspiracy to send so-called United Nations forces to the Middle East region. It emphasized, "The Palestinian revolution will not be responsible for the safety of so-called international observers or United Nations forces. They will be considered as elements hostile to the Palestinian revolution and will be regarded as enemy soldiers."

### 法塔赫“暴风之声”说巴勒斯坦人民决心进行武装斗争直到胜利　坚决反对任何“和平解决”中东问题的阴谋　巴勒斯坦人民已看清联合国的本质，联合国只不过是美帝为首的殖民大国的驯服工具

1968-10-13
第5版()
专栏：

    法塔赫“暴风之声”说巴勒斯坦人民决心进行武装斗争直到胜利
    坚决反对任何“和平解决”中东问题的阴谋
    巴勒斯坦人民已看清联合国的本质，联合国只不过是美帝为首的殖民大国的驯服工具
    新华社开罗电　“法塔赫”（巴勒斯坦民族解放运动）的“暴风之声”电台最近发表评论说：“巴勒斯坦阿拉伯人民决不赞成任何关于解决所谓中东危机的和平解决、和平计划或协议的论调。因为我们人民决心继续进行武装斗争直到胜利。他们决不承认无论是联合国或任何其他组织，无论是大国或小国所公布的任何决议。”
    这家电台针对苏修领导集团勾结美帝国主义，企图把所谓和平解决中东危机的方案强加给阿拉伯人民，以便进一步控制阿拉伯人民的阴谋说：“无论多么大的力量都不能挡住我们人民的决心和革命。我们已经走上了解放我们土地的正确道路，即人民革命的道路。我们是革命人民，决不让某些大国的阴谋得逞，我们将寸步不离开我们已经选择的道路。”
    这家电台说，这些国际商贩把巴勒斯坦阿拉伯人当作他们手中的某种商品。他们现在正在联合国的休息室中拚命为以色列的犹太复国主义辩护。但是巴勒斯坦阿拉伯人十分清楚联合国是什么东西。电台指出，“从第二次世界大战后成立以来，联合国一直是殖民主义国家和世界犹太复国主义实现它们的阴谋和野心以及为它们的侵略进行辩解的工具。有时候，联合国自己就在为侵略活动卖力。总之，联合国只不过是以美国为首的殖民大国手中的驯服工具和屈服于它们的压力的一个傀儡而已。”
    这家电台说，巴勒斯坦人民已经认识到联合国的本质，对它不抱任何希望。因此，“巴勒斯坦人民坚信，武装革命是取得解放的唯一道路。主要的战场是在巴勒斯坦，而不是在其他任何地方。巴勒斯坦人民能够用武器维护他们的权利。实现巴勒斯坦事业的唯一办法现正掌握在它的人民手中，这就是游击队手中的武器，依靠它们就能够取得胜利。”
    这家电台还提醒人民注意把所谓联合国部队开到中东地区的阴谋。它强调说，“巴勒斯坦革命绝不对所谓国际观察员或联合国部队的安全负责。他们将被认为是敌视巴勒斯坦革命的分子，并且将被看成是敌人的士兵。”
