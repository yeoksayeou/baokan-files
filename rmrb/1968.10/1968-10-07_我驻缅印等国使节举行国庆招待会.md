Title: Chinese Ambassadors in Burma, India, and Other Countries Hold National Day Receptions
Author:
Date: 1968-10-07
Edition: 5

Chinese Ambassador to Burma, Chiao Ming, held a reception on the evening of October 6 in Rangoon to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Some Burmese military and government officials attended the reception.

Also present were some Burmese social figures and youths.

Ambassadors from several other countries stationed in Burma also attended the reception.

Chinese Ambassador to India, Chen Chao-yuan, held a reception on the evening of October 6 in New Delhi to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Indian Foreign Ministry officials, people from various circles in India, and ambassadors from several other countries stationed in India attended the reception.

Chinese Ambassador to Hungary, Liu Chun-chang, held a reception on the evening of October 6 to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Representatives from relevant sectors in Hungary attended the reception.

Ambassadors from several other countries stationed in Hungary also attended the reception.

### 我驻缅印等国使节举行国庆招待会

1968-10-07
第5版()
专栏：

    我驻缅印等国使节举行国庆招待会
    我驻缅甸大使馆临时代办肖明，一日晚在仰光举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。
    一些缅甸军政官员出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的还有一些缅甸社会人士和青年。
    驻缅甸的一些国家的外交使节也出席了招待会。
    我驻印度大使馆临时代办陈肇源，一日晚在新德里举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。
    印度外交部官员、印度各界人士和驻印度的一些国家的外交使节出席了招待会。
    我驻匈牙利大使馆临时代办刘俊章一日晚举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。
    匈牙利有关方面人士出席了招待会。
    一些国家驻匈牙利的外交使节也出席了招待会。
                                        　（新华社讯）
