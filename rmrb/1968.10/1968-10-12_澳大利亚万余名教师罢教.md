Title: Over Ten Thousand Teachers in Australia Go on Strike
Author:
Date: 1968-10-12
Edition: 5

Over ten thousand teachers in New South Wales, Australia, went on a twenty-four-hour strike on October 11th, demanding an increase in educational funds and higher salaries.

Thousands of teachers also held a large demonstration outside the state legislature, demanding the resignation of officials in the education department. They stated that if their various demands are not immediately met, they will go on strike again.

### 澳大利亚万余名教师罢教

1968-10-12
第5版()
专栏：

    澳大利亚万余名教师罢教
    新华社墨尔本电  澳大利亚新南威尔士州一万多名教师一日举行二十四小时罢教，要求增加教育经费和提高工资。
    数千名教师还在州议会外面举行了大示威，要求教育部门的负责人辞职。他们表示，如果不立即满足他们的各项要求，他们将再次罢教。
