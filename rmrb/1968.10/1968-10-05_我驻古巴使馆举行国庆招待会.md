Title: The Chinese Embassy in Cuba Holds a National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1968-10-05
Edition: 5

The Chinese Embassy in Cuba held a reception on October 1 to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

According to Xinhua News Agency in Havana, the Chinese Chargé d'Affaires in Cuba, Li Shanyi, held a reception on October 1 to warmly celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Guests at the reception included Cuban Foreign Minister Raul Roa, Minister of Foreign Trade Marcelo Fernandez, Minister of Basic Industries Joel Domenech, and Chairman of the Cuba-China Friendship Association Baldomero Alvarez. Also present were representatives from various sectors of Cuban society, diplomats from various countries in Cuba, friends from various Latin American countries in Cuba, and overseas Chinese patriots in Cuba.

At the reception, many foreign friends warmly celebrated the glorious national day of the Chinese people, and enthusiastically congratulated the great victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China and the great victory of Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line.

### 我驻古巴使馆举行国庆招待会

1968-10-05
第5版()
专栏：

    我驻古巴使馆举行国庆招待会
    据新华社哈瓦那二日电　中国驻古巴大使馆临时代办李善一在一日举行招待会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。
    古巴外交部长劳尔·罗亚、外贸部长马尔塞洛·费尔南德斯、基本工业部长霍埃尔·多梅内奇和古中友协主席巴尔多梅罗·阿尔瓦雷斯等出席了招待会。出席招待会的还有：古巴各界人士，各国驻古巴的外交使节，在古巴的拉丁美洲各国朋友，以及旅古爱国华侨。
    在招待会上，许多外国朋友热烈庆贺中国人民的这一光辉节日，热烈祝贺中国无产阶级文化大革命的伟大胜利和毛主席的无产阶级革命路线的伟大胜利。
