Title: Workers' Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Team in Tonghua City's Second Middle School Helps Revolutionaries in Education Fight, Criticize, and Transform
Author:
Date: 1968-10-04
Edition: 4

The Workers' Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Team stationed at the Second Middle School in Tonghua City, Jilin Province, is helping revolutionary teachers and students to thoroughly carry out the struggle, criticism, and transformation. This is a scene of them jointly criticizing the revisionist educational line with the revolutionary teachers and students.
