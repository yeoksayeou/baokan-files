Title: Days by Chairman Mao's Side
Author:
Date: 1968-10-13
Edition: 3

Under the great commander Chairman Mao's directive, the working class of our country has boldly ascended to the political stage of struggle, criticism, and transformation in all domains of the superstructure. At this great historical moment, Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms, Vice Chairman Lin Biao, approved the invitation for workers' representatives from all parts of the country to come to Beijing to participate in the National Day celebrations. The workers' representatives from various regions, by Chairman Mao's side, expressed their loyalty, shared experiences from the struggle, criticism, and transformation, and resolved to better shoulder the great historical mission of the working class leading everything. This fully expressed the working class of our country's boundless love and loyalty to the great leader Chairman Mao.

### 在毛主席身边的日子里

1968-10-13
第3版()
专栏：

    在毛主席身边的日子里
    据新华社十二日讯　在伟大统帅毛主席的号令下，我国工人阶级豪迈地登上了上层建筑各个领域斗、批、改的政治舞台。在这伟大的历史时刻，毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席，批准邀请全国各地工人代表来北京参加了国庆观礼。各地工人代表在毛主席身边，向毛主席敬献忠心，交流斗、批、改的经验，决心更好地肩负起工人阶级必须领导一切的伟大历史使命，充分表达了我国工人阶级对伟大领袖毛主席的无限热爱和无限忠诚。
