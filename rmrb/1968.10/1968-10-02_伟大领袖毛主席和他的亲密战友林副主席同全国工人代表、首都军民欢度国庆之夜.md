Title: Great Leader Chairman Mao and His Close Comrade-in-Arms Vice Chairman Lin Celebrate National Day Evening with Workers' Representatives and Beijing Military and Civilians
Author:
Date: 1968-10-02
Edition: 3

Our great teacher, great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin Biao, tonight ascended the Tiananmen Gate Tower to celebrate National Day evening with outstanding representatives of the Chinese working class and the broad masses of military and civilians in Beijing, watching a grand fireworks display.

At 8 PM, Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin, amidst shouts of "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years!," energetically ascended the Tiananmen Gate Tower. Chairman Mao, on the Tiananmen Gate Tower, warmly received outstanding representatives of the Chinese working class and leaders of revolutionary committees from various provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions, as well as representatives from other sectors. The Tiananmen Gate Tower was filled with joy, presenting a touching scene of revolutionary soldiers' boundless love for the great leader Chairman Mao.

Accompanying Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin on the Tiananmen Gate Tower to participate in the evening celebration were Comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Jiang Qing, Zhang Chunqiao, Yao Wenyuan, Xie Fuzhi, Huang Yongsheng, Wu Faxian, Ye Qun, Wang Dongxing, and Wen Yucheng.

Revolutionary comrades and friends from various countries participating in the evening celebration on the Tiananmen Gate Tower included: Comrade Bekir Balluku and members of the Albanian Party and Government Delegation led by him: Rita Marko, Adil Charcani, Koco Tiodosi, Vasili Nataneli, and the wife of Ambassador Nataneli; Pakistani Presidential Advisor Sayyid Fida Hassan and members of the Pakistani Government Friendship Delegation led by him: Sayyid Fida Hassan's wife, Muhammad Khan Junajjo, and Abdul Haye Choudhry; Political Bureau member of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party and leader of the Indonesian Communist Party Central Delegation Yusuf Ajitorop; leader of the New Zealand Communist Party Delegation Joe Fells, as well as comrades from the anti-American struggle frontlines in Vietnam and Laos, and friends from Japan and other countries.

On National Day evening, the Tiananmen Square was filled with revolutionary songs and brilliant fireworks, presenting a magnificent and grand revolutionary scene. The colorful national day fireworks soared into the sky, with "Ten Thousand Red Flowers," "Red Waves Surge," "Red Light Radiates," and 26 other all-red fireworks especially attracting attention when they burst open, filling the sky with red light.

Workers, soldiers, and Red Guards from Beijing participating in the celebration performed rich and colorful revolutionary cultural programs.

Tonight, fireworks celebrations were also held simultaneously at Workers' Stadium, Zhongguancun, and Shijingshan.

	Mao and Comrade Balluku had a friendly chat during the intermission of the celebration.

### 伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林副主席同全国工人代表、首都军民欢度国庆之夜

1968-10-02
第3版()
专栏：

    伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林副主席同全国工人代表、首都军民欢度国庆之夜
    周恩来陈伯达康生江青张春桥姚文元谢富治黄永胜吴法宪叶群
    汪东兴温玉成等同志同来自各国的战友和朋友参加了节日晚会
    天安门城楼上一片欢腾，呈现出革命战士无限热爱伟大领袖毛主席的动人场面
    新华社一日讯　我们的伟大导师、伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席，今晚登上天安门城楼，同全国工人阶级的优秀代表和首都广大军民一起欢度国庆之夜，观看盛大的节日焰火。
    晚八时，毛主席和林副主席在“毛主席万岁！万万岁！”的欢呼声中健步登上天安门城楼。毛主席在天安门城楼上，亲切接见了我国工人阶级的优秀代表和各省、市、自治区革命委员会的负责人以及其他方面的代表。接见时，天安门城楼上一片欢腾，呈现出革命战士无限热爱伟大领袖毛主席的动人场面。
    同毛主席、林副主席一起在天安门城楼参加节日晚会的，有周恩来、陈伯达、康生、江青、张春桥、姚文元、谢富治、黄永胜、吴法宪、叶群、汪东兴、温玉成等同志。
    在天安门城楼参加晚会的来自各国的革命战友和朋友有：贝基尔·巴卢库同志和由他率领的阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团的成员：里塔·马尔科，阿迪尔·查尔查尼，科乔·蒂奥多西，瓦西里·纳塔奈利，以及纳塔奈利大使的夫人；巴基斯坦总统顾问赛义德·菲达·哈桑和由他率领的巴基斯坦政府友好代表团的成员：赛义德·菲达·哈桑夫人，穆罕默德·汗·朱奈乔，阿卜杜勒·海耶·乔杜里；印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会政治局委员、印度尼西亚共产党中央代表团团长优素福·阿吉托罗普；新西兰共产党代表团团长乔埃·福尔兹，以及来自反美斗争前线的越南、老挝的战友，和日本等国的朋友。
    国庆之夜，天安门广场凯歌飞扬，礼花灿烂，呈现一片宏伟壮丽的革命景象。五彩缤纷的节日焰火，一阵阵腾空而起，其中，“红花万朵”、“红浪翻腾”、“红光四射”等二十九种全红礼花，散开时漫天红光，特别引人注目。
    参加晚会的首都工人、解放军战士和红卫兵小将表演了丰富多彩的革命文艺节目。
    今晚，在工人体育场、中关村、石景山也同时举行了焰火晚会。
