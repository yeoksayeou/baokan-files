Title: Military and Civilians in the Western Highlands of South Vietnam Destroy Nearly 4,000 Enemy Troops in September
Author:
Date: 1968-10-12
Edition: 6

	Military and civilians in the Western Highlands of South Vietnam destroyed nearly 4,000 enemy troops in September. Various provincial guerrilla forces and local troops in the southern region achieved new victories.

	According to the New China News Agency in Hanoi on the 7th, the Vietnam South Liberation News Agency reported: The military and civilian forces of various ethnic groups in the Kontum, Gia Lai, and Dac Lac provinces of the Western Highlands of South Vietnam frequently struck the enemy on the battlefield in September, annihilating over 3,900 enemy troops, including more than 1,900 American invaders.

	During this period, the military and civilians in the Western Highlands also destroyed 207 enemy tanks, armored vehicles, and other military vehicles, destroyed 27 cannons of various types, shot down and destroyed 82 enemy aircraft, demolished 103 fortifications, blew up 7 bridges, burned 8 ammunition depots, 1 fuel storage, and many military supplies.

	In this month, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in Dac Lac Province closely coordinated their actions, successively surrounded and attacked the enemy in the De Li area, and inflicted heavy losses on the American invaders who came to reinforce them. The People's Liberation Armed Forces also successively attacked the enemy's airfields, several important strongholds, and the 14th and 21st highways, causing the enemy to suffer heavy losses and creating many difficulties for the enemy's transportation and logistics. Statistics show that the military and civilians in Dac Lac Province annihilated more than 1,800 enemy troops in one month, including nearly 1,000 American invaders. The military and civilians in Gia Lai Province killed, wounded, and captured more than 1,500 enemy troops in this month, destroyed 136 military vehicles, and shot down and destroyed 22 enemy aircraft.

	According to the New China News Agency in Hanoi on the 9th, the Vietnam South Liberation News Agency reported: The guerrilla forces and local troops in various provinces of the southern region of South Vietnam continuously attacked the enemy and counterattacked the enemy's "sweeping operations" around various cities and towns in September, annihilating many American and puppet troops.

	The guerrilla forces in Chau Thanh County, Binh Duong Province, from September 10th to 20th, continuously struck the enemy north of Binh Duong City and on the 6th Highway, killing and wounding 150 American and puppet troops, shooting down and destroying 6 aircraft, and burning 1 ammunition depot. The guerrilla forces in Long An Province continuously annihilated more than 350 American and puppet troops in Chau Thanh, Tan An, and Le Vang counties in September, including completely annihilating a puppet security company, and destroying 2 armored vehicles. The local troops and guerrilla forces in My Tho, Di An, Can Tho, Soc Trang, and Binh Thuy provinces continuously counterattacked the enemy's "sweeping operations" in September, killing and wounding more than 300 enemy troops.

	The military and civilians in Chau Thanh, Tan An, and Tan Bien counties in My Tho Province destroyed many sections of the 4th Highway in September, blowing up 2 bridges. From September 14th to 19th, the guerrilla forces in this province killed and wounded nearly 150 American and puppet troops on the 4th Highway, destroying 5 military vehicles and 1 cannon. The guerrilla forces and local troops in An Giang Province launched a fierce attack on the enemy attempting to open the 24th Highway on September 19th, repelling the enemy's multiple counterattacks, and killing and wounding 350 enemy troops. The guerrilla forces in Cu Chi County, Gia Dinh Province, continuously attacked enemy convoys and enemy bases on the 1st Highway in mid and late September, annihilating more than 250 American and puppet troops, destroying and damaging 14 tanks, and shooting down 1 aircraft.

### 南越西原地区军民九月份歼敌近四千名  南部地区各省游击队和地方部队取得新战绩

1968-10-12
第6版()
专栏：

    南越西原地区军民九月份歼敌近四千名
    南部地区各省游击队和地方部队取得新战绩
    据新华社河内七日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方西原地区昆嵩、嘉莱和多乐省各族军民，九月份在战场上频频打击敌人，消灭敌军三千九百多名，其中包括美国侵略军一千九百多名。
    在这期间，西原地区军民还击毁敌军坦克、装甲车和其他军车二百零七辆，击毁各种炮二十七门，击落击毁敌机八十二架，摧毁碉堡一百零三座，炸毁桥梁七座，烧毁弹药库八座、汽油库一座和许多军用物资。
    在这个月里，多乐省人民解放武装力量紧密配合作战，接连包围和袭击德立地区的敌人，并且使前往增援的美国侵略军遭到沉重打击。人民解放武装力量还接连袭击敌人的机场、一些重要据点以及第十四号、第二十一号公路，使敌人遭到重大损失，并给敌人的交通运输造成了许多困难。据统计，多乐省军民在一个月里共消灭敌人一千八百多名，其中包括美国侵略军近一千名。嘉莱省军民在这个月里打死打伤和俘虏敌人一千五百多名，击毁军车一百三十六辆，击落击毁敌机二十二架。
    据新华社河内九日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方南部地区各省游击队和地方部队，九月份在各个城镇周围连续袭击敌人和反击敌人的“扫荡”，消灭许多美伪军。
    槟椥省周城县游击队九月十日到二十日接连地在槟椥市北面和第六号公路上打击敌人，打死打伤美伪军一百五十名，击落击毁飞机六架，烧毁弹药库一座。永隆省游击队九月份接连在周城、丐绒和勒倭县消灭美伪军三百五十多名，其中包括全歼伪军一个保安连，并击毁装甲车两辆。美萩、迪石、芹苴、朔庄、薄辽等省地方部队和游击队九月份接连反击敌人的“扫荡”，打死打伤敌人三百多名。
    美萩省周城、丐?和丐礼三县军民九月份破坏了第四号公路的许多路段，炸毁两座桥梁。从九月十四日到十九日，这个省的游击队在第四号公路上打死打伤美伪军近一百五十名，击毁军车五辆和大炮一门。鹅贡省游击队和地方部队九月十九日猛烈打击企图打通第二十四号公路的敌人，击退敌人的多次反扑，共打死打伤敌人三百五十名。嘉定省古芝县游击队，在九月中旬和下旬接连在一号公路上袭击敌军车队和敌军驻地，消灭美伪军二百五十多名，击毁击伤坦克十四辆，击落飞机一架。
