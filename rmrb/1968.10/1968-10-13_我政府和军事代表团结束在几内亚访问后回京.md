Title: Chinese Government and Military Delegation Returns to Beijing After Completing Visit to Guinea
Author:
Date: 1968-10-13
Edition: 5

A delegation led by Zhang Ting, comprising government and military representatives of the People's Republic of China, concluded its friendly visit to Guinea and returned to Beijing by airplane today. The delegation was invited by the Guinean government to participate in the celebrations marking the 10th anniversary of the independence of the Republic of Guinea.

Relevant officials from China, including Luo Guibo, Xie Huai-de, Zhang Zhong-ru, and Ding Xilin, were at the airport to welcome the delegation. The Guinean Ambassador to China, Segou Kamara, and other diplomatic staff from the embassy were also present at the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 我政府和军事代表团结束在几内亚访问后回京

1968-10-13
第5版()
专栏：

    我政府和军事代表团结束在几内亚访问后回京
    新华社十二日讯　由张挺率领的中华人民共和国政府和军事代表团，结束了在几内亚的友好访问，今天乘飞机回到北京。代表团是应几内亚政府邀请，前往参加几内亚共和国独立十周年庆祝活动的。
    我有关方面负责人罗贵波、谢怀德、张中如、丁西林等到机场迎接。
    几内亚驻中国大使塞古·卡马拉和大使馆其他外交官员也到机场迎接。
