Title: The U.S. Imperialists Turn Malaya into an Invasion Base
Author:
Date: 1968-10-31
Edition: 6

The U.S. imperialists are colluding with the Rahman-Lee Kuan Yew puppet regime to turn Malaya (including Singapore) into a base for the U.S. war of aggression against Vietnam.

A student publication at the University of Malaya revealed on the 25th that some U.S. military personnel are currently using the university's affiliated hospital and biology department to research various bacteria and other toxic germs, intending to use them in the U.S. imperialists' planned bacteriological warfare in Vietnam.

According to newspaper reports, the U.S. imperialists have already used a jungle warfare school set up by British imperialists in Johor State to train thousands of U.S. officers and South Vietnamese puppet officers.

According to a report from a Singapore newspaper on the 26th, the U.S. Navy has intensified the use of Singapore's docks over the past year to repair U.S. Navy transport ships. These ships are specifically used to transport military supplies, fuel, food, and other military necessities to support the U.S. invasion forces in Vietnam.

### 美帝把马来亚变成侵略基地

1968-10-31
第6版()
专栏：

    美帝把马来亚变成侵略基地
    美帝国主义正在勾结拉赫曼—李光耀傀儡集团，使马来亚（包括新加坡）变成美国侵越战争的基地。
    马来亚大学一个学生会刊物的编辑二十五日揭露，一些美国军事人员目前正在利用马来亚大学的附属医院和生物系研究各种病菌和其它有毒的细菌，以便用于美帝国主义在越南策划的细菌战争。
    据报纸透露，美帝国主义已经利用英帝国主义设在柔佛州的一所丛林战学校训练了数以千计的美国军官和南越傀儡军官。
    据新加坡报纸二十六日透露，美国海军在最近一年以来加紧利用新加坡的船坞来修理美国海军的运输船。这些船是专门运输军火、汽油、粮食和其它军需品供应侵越美军的。
