Title: Chairman of the Azad Jammu and Kashmir Government Says China Consistently Supports the Struggle of the Kashmiri People
Author:
Date: 1968-10-24
Edition: 5

According to a report by Xinhua News Agency in Rawalpindi, the _Pakistan Times_ reported that on October 2, Chairman Abdul Hamid Khan of the Azad Jammu and Kashmir Government delivered a speech at a public gathering near the capital of Azad Jammu and Kashmir, Muzaffarabad. He said that China has consistently supported the Kashmiri people's struggle for the right to self-determination. He said that the Kashmiri people are proud to have such a sincere friend as the Chinese people.

According to a report by the Pakistan Associated Press of Pakistan, Pakistan's Defense and Interior Minister Aftab Khan in a public gathering in Gilgit said that the Pakistani government will always strive for the Kashmiri people to gain the right to self-determination.

### 查谟和克什米尔自由政府主席说  中国一贯支持克什米尔人民的斗争

1968-10-24
第5版()
专栏：

    查谟和克什米尔自由政府主席说
    中国一贯支持克什米尔人民的斗争
    据新华社拉瓦尔品第电　据《巴基斯坦时报》报道，查谟和克什米尔自由政府主席阿卜杜勒·哈米德·汗十月二日在自由克什米尔首府穆扎法拉巴德附近举行的一次公众集会上发表讲话说，中国一贯支持克什米尔人民争取自决权利的斗争。他说，克什米尔人民为有象中国人民这样的真诚的朋友而感到自豪。
    据巴基斯坦联合通讯社报道，巴基斯坦国防兼内政部长阿夫扎尔·拉赫曼·汗在吉尔吉特举行的一次公众集会上说，巴基斯坦政府将始终为克什米尔人民获得自决权利而努力。
