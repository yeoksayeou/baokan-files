Title: Luxembourg-China Friendship Association Holds "A Red Banner on China's Agricultural Front -- Dazhai" Photo Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1968-10-11
Edition: 4

Luxembourg-China Friendship Association Holds
"A Red Banner on China's Agricultural Front -- Dazhai" Photo Exhibition

New China News Agency, October 10th: A photo exhibition titled "A Red Banner on China's Agricultural Front -- Dazhai" opened in Luxembourg on the 1st, receiving high praise from the audience.

People from various circles in Luxembourg, as well as many workers and students, visited the exhibition.

The photos on display reflect the situation in Dazhai Brigade, Xiyang County, Shanxi Province, since the establishment of the Dazhai People's Commune in 1958. They show how the people of Dazhai, with a spirit of self-reliance and hard struggle, have transformed a poor mountain valley into a prosperous socialist new rural community.

This photo exhibition was organized by the Luxembourg-China Friendship Association.

### 卢森堡中国友协举办  “中国农业战线的一面红旗——大寨”图片展览

1968-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    卢森堡中国友协举办
    “中国农业战线的一面红旗——大寨”图片展览
    新华社十日讯　卢森堡消息：“中国农业战线的一面红旗——大寨”图片展览，一日在卢森堡开幕，博得了观众的好评。
    卢森堡各界人士和许多工人、学生参观了展览。
    展览的图片反映了中国山西省昔阳县大寨人民公社大寨大队自一九五八年成立人民公社以来，发扬自力更生、艰苦奋斗的革命精神，把一个贫困的山沟建设成繁荣的社会主义新农村的情况。
    这次图片展览是卢中友协举办的。
