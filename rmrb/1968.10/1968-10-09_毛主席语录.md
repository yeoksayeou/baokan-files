Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1968-10-09
Edition: 1

	Broad cadres should be sent down to labor, which provides an excellent opportunity for them to learn anew. Except for the elderly, the weak, the sick, and the disabled, all should do so. Cadres in office should also be sent down to labor in batches.

### 毛主席语录

1968-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    广大干部下放劳动，这对干部是一种重新学习的极好机会，除老弱病残者外都应这样做。在职干部也应分批下放劳动。
