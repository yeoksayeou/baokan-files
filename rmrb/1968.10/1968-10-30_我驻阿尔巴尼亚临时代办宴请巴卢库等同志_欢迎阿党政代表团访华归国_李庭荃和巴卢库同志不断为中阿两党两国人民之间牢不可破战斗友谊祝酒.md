Date: 1968-10-30
Edition: 6

Chinese Chargé d'Affaires Hosts Banquet for Baluku and Comrades

Welcome the Albanian Party and Government Delegation Returning from China

Li Tingcan and Baluku Comrade Repeatedly Toast to the Indestructible Fighting Friendship Between the Two Parties and Peoples of China and Albania

NEW CHINA NEWS AGENCY, TIRANA, October 29 — On October 28, Li Tingcan, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, hosted a luncheon at the embassy to welcome the Albanian Party and Government delegation, led by Comrade Baluku, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania, and Minister of Foreign Affairs, who had just returned from a friendly visit to China.

The Albanian Party and Government delegation was invited by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Government of the People's Republic of China for a friendly visit to China.

Comrade Marko, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Vice Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania, and First Secretary of the District Party Committee of Durres, also attended the luncheon.

Also present were Comrade Dum and Foreign Minister Nasi, alternate members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party.

Li Zhongxuan, military attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, also attended the luncheon.

During the banquet, Comrade Li Tingcan and Comrade Baluku, in a very friendly and cordial atmosphere, repeatedly toasted to the great and indestructible fighting friendship between the two parties and peoples of China and Albania. They also repeatedly raised their glasses to wish long life to the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao Zedong, and the great leader of the Albanian people, Comrade Enver Hoxha.

### 我驻阿尔巴尼亚临时代办宴请巴卢库等同志  欢迎阿党政代表团访华归国  李庭荃和巴卢库同志不断为中阿两党两国人民之间牢不可破战斗友谊祝酒

1968-10-30
第6版()
专栏：

    我驻阿尔巴尼亚临时代办宴请巴卢库等同志
    欢迎阿党政代表团访华归国
    李庭荃和巴卢库同志不断为中阿两党两国人民之间牢不可破战斗友谊祝酒
    新华社地拉那二十九日电　中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办李庭荃二十八日在使馆举行午宴，欢迎不久前访问中国归来的，由阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议副主席兼国访部长巴卢库同志率领的阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团。
    阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团是应中国共产党中央委员会和中华人民共和国政府的邀请对中国进行友好访问的。
    阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团成员、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团副主席、劳动党都拉斯区党委第一书记马尔科同志出席了午宴。
    出席午宴的还有阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局候补委员杜米和外交部长纳赛等同志。
    中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆武官李钟玄也出席了午宴。
    宴会上，李庭荃同志和巴卢库同志在极其亲切和友好的气氛中，不断为中阿两党和两国人民之间的伟大的、牢不可破的战斗友谊祝酒，并不断举杯祝愿中国人民的伟大领袖毛泽东主席和阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖恩维尔·霍查同志万寿无疆。
