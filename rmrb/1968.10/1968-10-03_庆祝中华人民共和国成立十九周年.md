Title: Celebrating the 19th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1968-10-03
Edition: 6

Celebrating the 19th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China

	Guinea President's Telegram
	To Chairman Mao Zedong, Beijing:

	On the occasion of your national day, I have the honor, on behalf of the people of Guinea, the Guinean Democratic Party, and in my personal capacity, to extend to you my warmest congratulations. We view with admiration and respect the brilliant achievements made by the Chinese people under your wise leadership in the course of their cultural revolution. We wish you happiness and good health, and we wish the Chinese people ever greater prosperity. We once again express to you our fighting solidarity and our sincere hope that, on the basis of our common struggle against imperialism and old and new colonialism, we may strengthen our friendly cooperation.

	With the highest respect,
	President of the Republic of Guinea, Ahmed Sekou Toure
	Conakry, October 1, 1968

	President of Mali's Telegram
	To Chairman Mao Zedong, Beijing:

	On the occasion of your national day, I am very pleased, on behalf of the people of Mali, the Sudanese Union Party, the Government of Mali, and in my personal capacity, to extend to you my most sincere and warmest congratulations. I wish the heroic brotherly Chinese people happiness and prosperity, and I wish you excellent health. I once again assure you of our determination to further strengthen the friendly relations between our two countries. We most warmly wish that our two countries, through our joint efforts, may achieve harmonious cooperation.

	With the highest and fraternal respect,
	President of the Republic of Mali, Modibo Keita
	Koulouba, October 1, 1968

	Head of State of the Congo (Brazzaville)'s Telegram
	To Chairman Mao Zedong, Beijing:

	At a time when the peoples of Africa, Asia, and Latin America are undergoing severe tests in their national liberation struggles, the people of Congo, the Government of Congo, and I myself, with fraternal feelings, share in the joy of the heroic Chinese people as they celebrate their national day. On behalf of the Government of Congo and in my personal capacity, I extend to you my warmest congratulations and wish you good health, and I wish the heroic Chinese people happiness.

	With the highest respect,
	Head of State and Head of Government of the Congo (Brazzaville), Alfred Raoul
	Brazzaville, October 1, 1968

	President of Uganda's Telegram
	To Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Beijing:

	On the occasion of the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, I, on behalf of the Government and people of the Republic of Uganda and in my personal capacity, extend to you our warmest congratulations and respect, and we congratulate you on your achievements. Your achievements have contributed to humanity and to the maintenance of peace and harmony among nations. We wish that the friendly relations existing between the Republic of Uganda and the People's Republic of China may be further strengthened.

	President of Uganda, A. M. Obote
	Kampala, October 1, 1968

	President of South Yemen's Telegram
	To Chairman Mao Zedong, Beijing:

	On the occasion of the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, I am extremely pleased to extend to you, to the people and Government of the People's Republic of China, my congratulations. I take this opportunity to express, on behalf of the people of South Yemen and myself, our deep admiration for your courageous struggle against colonialism, imperialism, and their lackeys, and for the great achievements made by the Chinese people under your resolute and wise leadership. Please accept my best wishes for your personal happiness and for the continuous progress and prosperity of the friendly Chinese people.

	President of the People's Republic of South Yemen, Qahtan Muhammad al-Shaabi
	Aden, October 1, 1968

	President of Tanzania's Telegram
	To Premier Zhou Enlai, Beijing:

	On the occasion of the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, I, on behalf of the Government and people of Tanzania and in my personal capacity, extend to you and to the Government and people of China our warmest congratulations. In the Republic of Tanzania, we who have had the opportunity to visit the People's Republic of China cherish very precious and vivid memories of the great progress made by the Chinese people. I particularly recall the great victories of the Cultural Revolution that we witnessed during our second visit to your country not long ago. I am convinced that no enemy can destroy the strong friendship and unity that exist between our two countries and our peoples. It is with this spirit that the people of Tanzania join with the Chinese people in celebrating this day. We wish you, the Government and people of China greater achievements in your just cause.

	Julius K. Nyerere
	Dar es Salaam, October 1, 1968

	Head of State of Cambodia's Telegram
	To Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

	On the occasion of the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, I am happy to extend to you my warmest congratulations and, together with the people of Cambodia, to express our most sincere wishes to friendly China and to the great Chinese people. We warmly wish the Chinese people and their outstanding leaders, under the guidance of the thought of Chairman Mao Zedong, new and great achievements in their historic cause. I wish to reaffirm that the friendly relations between Cambodia and China are unbreakable. I once again assure you that, in the struggle against imperialism in all its forms, particularly against U.S. neo-colonialism, we stand fraternally united with you. China's consistent and resolute support for our policy of complete independence and self-reliance and for our national construction is of particular importance for Cambodia's present and future, whose survival is threatened. While expressing our deepest gratitude to you and your Government for your understanding of China, we extend to you our most sincere thanks.

	We warmly wish that the cooperation between Cambodia and China may be continuously strengthened in an atmosphere of mutual trust and mutual benefit.

	With the highest and most friendly respect,
	Head of State of Cambodia, Norodom Sihanouk
	Phnom Penh, October 2, 1968

### 庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年

1968-10-03
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年
    几内亚总统的贺电北京毛泽东先生阁下：
    在庆祝贵国国庆的时候，我荣幸地代表几内亚人民、几内亚民主党并以我个人的名义，向你致以热烈的祝贺。我们以钦佩和崇敬的心情看到，在你的英明领导下，中国人民在文化革命的道路上取得了辉煌的成就。我们祝你幸福和身体健康，祝中国人民日益昌盛。我们再次向你表示我们的战斗团结的心情，我们真诚地希望在共同反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义的基础上加强我们的友好合作关系。
    顺致最崇高的敬意
                  几内亚共和国总统　艾哈迈德·塞古·杜尔
                              一九六八年十月一日于科纳克里
    马里总统的贺电北京毛泽东主席先生阁下：
    在贵国国庆的时候，我很高兴地代表马里人民、苏丹联盟党和马里政府，并以我个人的名义，向你致以最诚挚和最热烈的祝贺，祝英勇的兄弟中国人民幸福、昌盛，祝阁下身体非常健康。我再次向你保证：我们决心进一步加强我们两国之间的友好关系。我们最热烈地祝愿我们两国共同努力，实现我们两国之间融洽的合作。
    顺致最崇高和兄弟般的敬意。
                            马里共和国总统　莫迪博·凯塔
                                一九六八年十月一日于库鲁巴
    刚果（布）国家元首的贺电北京毛泽东阁下：
    正当非洲、亚洲和拉丁美洲各国人民经受民族解放斗争严峻考验的时候，刚果人民、刚果政府和我本人，以兄弟般的心情分享英勇的中国人民庆祝她国庆时的欢乐。我代表刚果政府、并以我个人的名义，最热烈地祝愿阁下身体健康，祝愿英勇的中国人民幸福。
    顺致最崇高的敬意。
    刚果（布拉柴维尔）国家元首、政府首脑
                                  阿尔弗雷德·拉乌尔少校
                                      十月一日于布拉柴维尔
    乌干达总统的贺电北京中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东先生阁下阁下：
    在中华人民共和国成立十九周年之际，我以我个人的名义，并代表乌干达共和国政府和人民表示热烈的祝贺和敬意，并对你们取得的成就向你表示祝贺。你们的成就对人类、对维护各国之间的和平与和睦都作出了贡献。祝乌干达共和国和中华人民共和国之间存在着的友好关系日益增强。
                              乌干达总统　阿·密·奥博特
                                一九六八年十月一日于坎帕拉
     南也门总统的贺电北京毛泽东主席阁下：
    欣逢中华人民共和国成立十九周年之际，我以极其愉快的心情，向阁下、向中华人民共和国人民和政府表示祝贺。我借此机会以南也门人民和我个人的名义，向阁下表示我们深深的钦佩，钦佩你们反对殖民主义、帝国主义和他们的走狗的英勇斗争和在您的果敢和英明的领导下中国人民所取得的伟大的成就。请接受我的最良好的祝愿，祝阁下个人幸福，祝友好的中国人民不断进步和繁荣。
              南也门人民共和国总统  卡坦·穆罕默德·沙比
                                  一九六八年十月一日于亚丁
    坦桑尼亚总统的贺电北京周恩来总理阁下：
    在中华人民共和国成立十九周年之际，我谨代表坦桑尼亚政府和人民，并以我个人的名义，向你和中国政府和人民，致以最热烈的祝贺。在坦桑尼亚共和国，我们这些曾经有机会访问过中华人民共和国的人们，对于中国人民所取得的巨大进步，怀有非常宝贵的、生动的记忆。我特别回想起，不久前我和我率领的代表团第二次到贵国访问时目睹的文化大革命所取得的巨大胜利。我相信，任何敌人都无法破坏存在于我们两国和两国人民之间的坚强友谊关系和团结。坦桑尼亚人民就是本着这样的精神和中国人民一道庆祝这个纪念日的。祝你、祝中国政府和人民在你们的正义事业中，取得更大成就。
                                    朱利叶斯·克·尼雷尔
                          一九六八年十月一日于达累斯萨拉姆
    柬埔寨国家元首的贺电北京中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来先生阁下：
    在中华人民共和国成立十九周年的时候，我高兴地向阁下表示最热烈的祝贺，并同柬埔寨人民一道，向友好的中国和伟大的中国人民致以最诚挚的祝愿。我们热烈祝愿中国人民及其卓越的领导人在毛泽东主席的思想的指引下，在他们正在进行的历史性事业中，取得新的巨大的成就。
    我愿意重申，柬中两国的友好关系是牢不可破的。我再次向阁下保证，在反对各种形式的帝国主义，特别在反对美国新殖民主义的斗争中，我们同你们兄弟般地团结在一起。中国一贯坚决支持我国完全独立自主的政策和我国的国家建设，这对于生存受到威胁的柬埔寨的今天和前途来说，都具有格外重要的意义。在对中国的谅解表示最诚挚敬意的同时，我向阁下及其政府表示我们最深切的感激之意。
    我们热烈祝愿柬中两国的合作在相互信任和互利的气氛中，不断得到加强。
    顺致最崇高和最友好的敬意。
                        柬埔寨国家元首　诺罗敦·西哈努克
                                  一九六八年十月二日于金边
