Title: U.S. Ships and Aircraft Intrude into Our Territorial Waters and Airspace
Author:
Date: 1968-10-31
Edition: 6

U.S. ships and aircraft intruded into our territorial waters and airspace. We issue the 465th serious warning.

	New China News Agency, October 30th

	From 15:34 on October 29th to 22:02 on the same day, and from 00:48 on October 30th to 7:51 on the same day, a U.S. warship intruded into our territorial waters in the area east of Minjiang Mouth in Fujian Province twice. On October 29th, from 17:58 to 19:06, another U.S. warship intruded into our territorial waters in the area south of Danjiao Island in Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 16:50 to 16:58 and from 17:00 to 17:13, two U.S. military aircraft intruded into our airspace in the area around Yongxing Island in Guangdong Province twice.

	Regarding the military provocation by the U.S. warships and military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the Ministry, issues the 465th serious warning.

### 美舰和美机侵入我领海领空  我提出第四百六十五次严重警告

1968-10-31
第6版()
专栏：

    美舰和美机侵入我领海领空
    我提出第四百六十五次严重警告
    新华社三十日讯　十月二十九日十五时三十四分至二十二时零二分，和十月三十日零时四十八分至七时五十一分，美国军舰一艘，两次侵入我福建省闽江口以东地区领海。十月二十九日十七时五十八分至十九时零六分，又有美国军舰一艘，侵入我广东省担杆岛以南地区领海。同日十六时五十分至五十八分，和十七时零分至十三分，美国军用飞机两架，两次侵入我广东省永兴岛地区领空。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百六十五次严重警告。
