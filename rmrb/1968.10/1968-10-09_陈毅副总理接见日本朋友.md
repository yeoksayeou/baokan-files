Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Japanese Friends
Author:
Date: 1968-10-09
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council received the honorary head of the Japan-China Friendship Association (Genuine) and the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, Saionji Kinmochi, and the head of the delegation, Kuroda Jitsuo, along with all members of the delegation today. He also received Tanaka Shujiro, a standing director of the Japan Association for the Promotion of International Trade.

Vice Premier Chen Yi had a cordial and friendly conversation with the Japanese friends.

Present at the reception were relevant responsible persons, including Ding Xilin.

### 陈毅副总理接见日本朋友

1968-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见日本朋友
    新华社八日讯　国务院副总理陈毅，今天接见了日中友协（正统）、日中文化交流协会联合代表团名誉团长西园寺公一、团长黑田寿男和代表团全体成员。同时，还接见了日本国际贸易促进协会常务理事田中修二郎。
    陈毅副总理同日本朋友进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时，有关方面负责人丁西林在座。
