Title: Pakistani Government Friendship Delegation Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-10-04
Edition: 6

A delegation from the Pakistani government, led by Advisor to the President of Pakistan, Sayyid Fida Hassan, left Beijing today on a special plane to visit the southern part of our country. They were accompanied by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong and others.

Vice Premier Chen Yi and over 2,000 revolutionary masses and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army of China were at the airport to give a warm send-off to the Pakistani guests.

The welcoming crowd held up portraits of our great leader Chairman Mao and President Ayub Khan, as well as banners reading, "Firmly support the Pakistani people in their struggle to maintain national independence and oppose foreign aggression!" and "Firmly support the Kashmiri people in their struggle for national self-determination!"

When Advisor Sayyid Fida Hassan and others bid farewell to the welcoming crowd accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi, people waved the national flags of China and Pakistan and repeatedly chanted slogans: "Long live the friendship between the peoples of China and Pakistan!" "Down with U.S.-led imperialism!" "Down with modern revisionism centered on the Soviet revisionist renegade clique!" "Long live the unity of the Asian and African peoples!" "Long live the great unity of the peoples of the world!"

Pakistani Ambassador to China Sultan M. Khan also accompanied Advisor Sayyid Fida Hassan and others on their visit to the southern part of our country.

Those who went to the airport to see them off also included relevant officials from the relevant departments of the Chinese government, the People's Liberation Army, the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee, and representatives of the Beijing Workers' Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Team, representatives of the leaders of various revolutionary mass organizations in Beijing, and representatives of the Five Good Soldiers stationed in Beijing.

During their stay in Beijing, the Pakistani guests, accompanied by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong, visited factories, people's communes, and toured the Great Wall and other places.

	Pakistani President Ayub Khan's Advisor Sayyid Fida Hassan and the Pakistani government friendship delegation he led departed from Beijing by plane on October 3. Vice Premier Chen Yi and the revolutionary masses of the capital went to the airport to give them a warm send-off.

### 巴政府友好代表团离京

1968-10-04
第6版()
专栏：

    巴政府友好代表团离京
    新华社三日讯　由巴基斯坦总统阿尤布·汗的顾问赛义德·菲达·哈桑率领的巴基斯坦政府友好代表团，在我外交部副部长韩念龙等陪同下，今天乘专机离开北京，前往我国南方参观访问。
    国务院副总理陈毅和首都革命群众、中国人民解放军指战员两千多人到机场热烈欢送巴基斯坦贵宾。
    欢送群众高举着我们伟大领袖毛主席的画像和阿尤布·汗总统的画像，举着“坚决支持巴基斯坦人民维护民族独立、反对外来侵略的斗争！”“坚决支持克什米尔人民争取民族自决权利的斗争！”的横幅标语。当赛义德·菲达·哈桑顾问等在陈毅副总理陪同下，和欢送群众告别时，人们挥动中巴两国国旗，不断高呼口号：“中巴两国人民的友谊万岁！”“打倒以美国为首的帝国主义！”“打倒以苏修叛徒集团为中心的现代修正主义！”“亚非人民团结万岁！”“全世界人民大团结万岁！”
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆·汗也陪同赛义德·菲达·哈桑顾问等赴我国南方参观访问。
    今天到机场欢送的，还有我国政府有关部门、中国人民解放军、北京市革命委员会等有关方面负责人赛福鼎、罗贵波、林海云、谢怀德、吴忠、张忠如以及首都工人毛泽东思想宣传队代表，北京市各革命群众组织负责成员和驻京部队五好战士代表。
    巴基斯坦贵宾在京期间，由我外交部副部长韩念龙陪同，参观了工厂、人民公社，游览了长城等地。
