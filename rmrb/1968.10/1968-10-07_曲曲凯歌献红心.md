Title: Triumphant Songs and Red Hearts
Author: Zhang Dongfang
Date: 1968-10-07
Edition: 6

	In the midst of struggle, criticism, and reform,
	Millions rejoice and express their fervor;
	Revolution and production sing triumphant songs,
	Good news spreads across the vast land.

	Song after song of triumph, hearts aflame,
	Every red heart turns towards Beijing;
	Endless loyalty to Chairman Mao,
	Forever march forward in revolution.
