Title: Members of the Pakistani Government Friendship Delegation Share Their Impressions of Visiting China After Returning Home
Author:
Date: 1968-10-15
Edition: 5

    Members of the Pakistani Government Friendship Delegation, after returning home, spoke about their impressions of visiting China, praising the great achievements of the Chinese people in self-reliance.

    Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi, October 10th: Advisor to Pakistani President Ayub Khan, Fida Hassan, said on the 7th in Rawalpindi that during his recent visit to China, the spirit of the Chinese people working hard to build their own country left a very deep impression on him.

    As the leader of the Pakistani Government Friendship Delegation participating in China's National Day activities, he said to the news reporters upon his return to Rawalpindi Airport after visiting China, "This visit, touring factories, people's communes, and other places, was a very good education for all members of the delegation."

    He said when talking about his meeting with the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao, "Chairman Mao Zedong is in very good health. Chairman Mao and other Chinese leaders asked me to convey their warm greetings to the President and people of Pakistan."

    When talking about the friendly relations between China and Pakistan, he said, "The relations between the two countries remain as before, close and friendly."

    Earlier, Fida Hassan, during his stopover at Dhaka Airport on his way back to Rawalpindi, said to the news reporters, "During the visit to China, wherever we went, the Chinese people showed great concern and friendship towards the Pakistani people."

    The Karachi Morning News published an editorial on October 5th, enthusiastically welcoming the visit of the Pakistani Government Friendship Delegation during China's National Day, which strengthened Sino-Pakistani friendship.

    The editorial said, "China has clearly reiterated its strong support for the struggle of the Kashmiri people for the right to self-determination, which will greatly encourage the just struggle of the Kashmiri people."

    Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi, October 14th: Members of the Pakistani Government Friendship Delegation, who recently returned home after participating in the celebrations of the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, enthusiastically praised the great achievements of the Chinese people under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong and warmly hailed the growing Sino-Pakistani friendship.

    Member of Parliament Shah Azizur Rahman said in an interview with the Pakistan Times reporter in Dhaka on the 13th, praising the 700 million Chinese people under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao, who have achieved great achievements through self-reliance and hard work. He said, "China will play an increasingly important role in world affairs."

    When talking about Sino-Pakistani relations, he said, "The Chinese government and people are friendly towards the Pakistani people. The Chinese people unreservedly support the struggle of the Kashmiri people for the right to self-determination and sincerely and wholeheartedly support the just cause of the Pakistani people." He hopes that Sino-Pakistani friendship will further develop.

    Muhammad Khan Minajjo, Minister of Transportation of West Pakistan, said in an interview with news reporters in Lahore, "The delegation visited some people's communes in China, and the amazing progress in agriculture left a very deep impression on me."

    The Chairman of the All-Pakistan Women's Association, Sayyid Fida Hassan, said at a women's gathering in Lahore, "Chinese women actively engage in beneficial work. Women often study together the works of Chairman Mao Zedong and use Mao Zedong Thought to solve various problems." She said, "In China, women and men enjoy equal status, they are respected, which left a deep impression on me."

### 巴基斯坦政府友好代表团成员回国后谈访华观感  赞扬中国人民自力更生的巨大成就

1968-10-15
第5版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦政府友好代表团成员回国后谈访华观感
    赞扬中国人民自力更生的巨大成就
    新华社拉瓦尔品第十日电　巴基斯坦总统阿尤布·汗的顾问菲达·哈桑七日在这里说，他在最近访问中国期间，中国人民为建设自己的国家而努力工作的精神，使他得到非常深刻的印象。
    他作为参加中国国庆活动的巴基斯坦政府友好代表团团长在访问中国后回到拉瓦尔品第机场时对新闻记者说，这次访问，参观了工厂、人民公社和其他地方，对代表团全体团员是一个很好的教育。
    他在谈到他受到中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席的接见时说，毛泽东主席身体非常健康。毛主席和其他中国领导人请他向巴基斯坦总统和人民转达他们的热情问候。
    他在谈到中国和巴基斯坦的友好关系时说，两国的关系，一如既往，是亲切的、友好的。
    早些时候，菲达·哈桑在前往拉瓦尔品第途中在达卡机场作短时间的停留时对新闻记者说，在访问中国期间，所到之处，中国人民都对巴基斯坦人民表示极大的关怀和友好。
    卡拉奇《晨报》十月五日发表社论，热烈欢呼在中国国庆节期间巴基斯坦政府友好代表团的访问加强了巴中友谊。
    社论说，中国明确重申大力支持克什米尔人民争取自决权的斗争，这将大大鼓舞克什米尔人民的正义斗争。
    新华社拉瓦尔品第十四日电　曾经参加中华人民共和国成立十九周年庆祝活动、最近回国的巴基斯坦政府友好代表团团员，热烈赞扬中国人民在毛泽东主席领导下取得的巨大成就，热情欢呼日益增长的中巴友谊。
    议员沙阿·阿齐祖尔·拉赫曼十三日在达卡对《巴基斯坦时报》记者发表谈话，赞扬七亿中国人民在毛主席英明领导下，自力更生，艰苦奋斗，取得了巨大成就。他说，中国在世界事务中将发挥日益重要的作用。
    他在谈到中巴关系时说，中国政府和人民对巴基斯坦人民是友好的。中国人民毫无保留地支持克什米尔人民争取自决权的斗争，并且热情和诚恳地支持巴基斯坦人民的正义事业。他希望中巴友谊进一步发展。
    西巴基斯坦省交通部长穆罕默德·汗·米奈乔在拉合尔对新闻记者谈话说，代表团在中国访问了一些人民公社，中国在农业方面的惊人进步给了他极为深刻以的印象。
    全巴基斯坦妇女协会主席赛义德·菲达·哈桑夫人在拉合尔的一次妇女集会上说，中国妇女积极从事有益的工作。妇女们经常在一起学习毛泽东主席的著作，并用毛泽东思想来解决各种问题。她说，在中国，妇女和男子享有平等地位，她们受到尊重，这给她留下了深刻的印象。
