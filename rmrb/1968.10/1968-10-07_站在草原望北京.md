Title: Standing on the Grassland Looking Towards Beijing
Author: Yú Wéndá
Date: 1968-10-07
Edition: 6

    Standing on the grassland looking towards Beijing,
    The pines and cypresses on the plateau are exceptionally green;
    Rivers flow for a thousand miles, mountains rise in countless layers,
    Chairman Mao and we are heart to heart.

### 站在草原望北京
俞文达
1968-10-07
第6版()
专栏：

    站在草原望北京
    内蒙古　俞文达
    高原松柏分外青，
    站在草原望北京；
    千里江河万重山，
    毛主席和咱心连心。
