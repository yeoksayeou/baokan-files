Title: Publication of the Single-Volume Edition of "Absorbing the Fresh Blood of the Proletariat"
Author:
Date: 1968-10-18
Edition: 2

The single-volume edition of "Absorbing the Fresh Blood of the Proletariat," an important editorial from the *Red Flag* magazine that conveys the latest instructions from the great leader Chairman Mao on party rectification and construction work and on relying on the revolutionary masses, has been published by the People's Publishing House. It will begin to be distributed at the New China Bookstore in Beijing on October 18 and will subsequently be available at New China Bookstores across the country.

### 《吸收无产阶级的新鲜血液》单行本出版

1968-10-18
第2版()
专栏：

    《吸收无产阶级的新鲜血液》单行本出版
    新华社十七日讯　传达了伟大领袖毛主席关于整党建党工作和依靠革命人民群众的最新指示的《红旗》杂志重要社论《吸收无产阶级的新鲜血液》，已由人民出版社出版单行本，十月十八日开始在北京新华书店发行，并将在全国各地新华书店陆续发行。
