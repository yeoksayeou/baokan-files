Title: Members of the Revolutionary Committee of Beijing Chemical Factory No. 4 and Revolutionary Cadres and Intellectuals Warmly Welcome the Latest Directive of Chairman Mao
Author: Yang Changzhong
Date: 1968-10-08
Edition: 2

Members of the Revolutionary Committee of Beijing Chemical Factory No. 4, revolutionary cadres, and intellectuals have enthusiastically hailed the publication of Chairman Mao's latest directive. They unanimously expressed their determination to carry out Chairman Mao's great teachings on cadres going down to the countryside to labor, to seriously engage in struggle, criticism, and transformation, and to achieve a comprehensive victory in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.
