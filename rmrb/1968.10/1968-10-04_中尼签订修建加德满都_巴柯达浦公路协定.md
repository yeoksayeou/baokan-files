Title: China and Nepal Sign Agreement to Build Kathmandu-Bakhodapur Road
Author:
Date: 1968-10-04
Edition: 6

The Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Kingdom of Nepal signed an agreement on September 27 in Kathmandu to build the Kathmandu-Bakhodapur Road. The agreement was signed by Tuo Guowei, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Nepal, and Surendra Raj Sharma, Acting Secretary of the Nepalese Ministry of Finance, on behalf of their respective governments.

Surendra Raj Sharma, in his speech at the signing ceremony, said that the friendship and economic cooperation between Nepal and China are growing stronger every day. He expressed gratitude to the Chinese government for its assistance in Nepal's economic development.

Tuo Guowei, in his remarks, praised the friendship between China and Nepal.

The Nepalese Ministry of Finance issued a press release stating that Nepal sincerely appreciates the economic cooperation provided by China in Nepal's development efforts.

The Kathmandu-Bakhodapur Road starts from the urban area of Kathmandu and ends at a point west of Bakhodapur City, located on the Kathmandu-Kodari Road.

### 中尼签订修建加德满都—巴柯达浦公路协定

1968-10-04
第6版()
专栏：

    中尼签订修建加德满都—巴柯达浦公路协定
    新华社加德满都电  中华人民共和国政府和尼泊尔王国政府九月二十七日在加德满都签订了关于修建加德满都—巴柯达浦公路的协定。协定是由中国驻尼泊尔大使馆临时代办屠国维和尼泊尔财政部代理秘书苏伦德拉·拉杰·夏尔玛分别代表两国政府签字的。
    苏伦德拉·拉杰·夏尔玛在签字仪式上发表讲话说，尼中友谊和经济合作正在日益增强。他感谢中国政府帮助尼泊尔的经济发展工作。
    中国驻尼泊尔大使馆临时代办屠国维在讲话中赞扬了中尼友谊。
    尼泊尔财政部发表新闻公报说，尼泊尔衷心感谢中国在尼泊尔的发展工作中所给予的经济合作。
    加德满都—巴柯达浦公路是以加德满都市市区为起点，以巴柯达浦市以西、位于加德满都—科达里公路上的一个地点为终点。
