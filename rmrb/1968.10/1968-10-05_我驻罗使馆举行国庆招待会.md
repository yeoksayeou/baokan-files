Title: The Chinese Embassy in Romania Holds a Reception for National Day
Author:
Date: 1968-10-05
Edition: 5

The Chinese Embassy in Romania held a reception to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

The Chinese chargé d'affaires in Romania, Ma Xusheng, hosted the reception on the evening of October 2. Romanian Communist Party Central Committee Standing Committee member and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Maurer, RCP Central Committee Standing Committee member and trade union chairman Apostol, RCP Central Committee Executive Committee alternate member and Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Bănescu, and Romanian State Council Vice Chairman Cherecheanu were invited to attend the reception. Also present were RCP Central Committee Department of International Liaison head Vlad, Romanian Minister of National Defense Ionita, Minister of Roads, Waterways and Air Transport Baicu, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications Brănescu, Minister of Electricity Groza, and First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Macovescu, among others.

Representatives from several countries' embassies in Romania and some students from Asia, Africa, and Latin America also attended the reception.

The reception was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 我驻罗使馆举行国庆招待会

1968-10-05
第5版()
专栏：

    我驻罗使馆举行国庆招待会
    新华社布加勒斯特二日电　中国驻罗马尼亚大使馆临时代办马叙生一日晚举行招待会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。
    罗马尼亚共产党中央常设主席团委员、部长会议主席毛雷尔，罗共中央常设主席团委员、工会主席阿波斯托尔，罗共中央执行委员会候补委员、部长会议副主席邦克，罗马尼亚国务委员会副主席克勒琼恩应邀出席了招待会。出席招待会的还有，罗共中央对外联络部部长弗拉德，罗马尼亚武装部队部部长约尼查，公路、水路和航空运输部部长巴伊库，邮电部部长勃勒内斯库，电力部部长格罗查，外交部第一副部长马科维斯库等。
    一些国家驻罗马尼亚的使节和一些亚非拉留学生也出席了招待会。
    招待会是在热烈、友好的气氛中进行的。
