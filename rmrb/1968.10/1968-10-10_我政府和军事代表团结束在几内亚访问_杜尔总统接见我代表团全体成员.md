Title: Chinese Government and Military Delegation Concludes Visit to Guinea
Author:
Date: 1968-10-10
Edition: 6

A Chinese government and military delegation led by Zhang Ting concluded their visit to Guinea after attending the 10th anniversary celebrations of the Republic of Guinea's independence. They departed from Conakry by airplane on the 8th.

On the evening of the 7th, Guinea's President Sekou Toure received the entire Chinese delegation at the presidential palace and engaged in friendly conversations with them. Present during the reception were Guinea's Minister of Foreign Affairs Lansana Beavogui, Minister of Economic Development Ismail Toure, Minister of Trade Keta Enfamara, Minister of the People's Army and Defense of the Revolution Dianne Lansana, Minister of Social Affairs Toure Mamouna, and Speaker of the National Assembly Leon Maka.

China's Chargé d'Affaires in Guinea, Lin Bingnan, was also present.

During their visit, the Chinese delegation met with Guinea's Minister of the People's Army and Defense of the Revolution Dianne Lansana, Minister of Foreign Affairs Lansana Beavogui, and Minister of Economic Development Ismail Toure, engaging in friendly discussions. Zhang Ting, the head of the Chinese delegation, presented a banner on behalf of the Chinese government to Dianne Lansana, which read, "Long live the fighting friendship between the Chinese and Guinean peoples!" Dianne Lansana, on behalf of the Guinean government and people, expressed heartfelt thanks to the Chinese government and people.

The Chinese delegation also visited the cities of Labe, Pita, Dalaba, and Mamou in Guinea, as well as the Kankan hydroelectric power station, which was built with Chinese assistance. They were warmly welcomed by local officials and civilians.

On the 8th, when the Chinese delegation departed from Conakry, several Guinean officials, including the State Secretary for Transport, Mori Keta, and the Deputy Chief of Staff of the Guinean People's Army, Kamara Diaby, were present at the airport to see them off.

China's Chargé d'Affaires in Guinea, Lin Bingnan, and other embassy staff were also at the airport to bid farewell to the delegation.

### 我政府和军事代表团结束在几内亚访问  杜尔总统接见我代表团全体成员

1968-10-10
第6版()
专栏：

    我政府和军事代表团结束在几内亚访问
    杜尔总统接见我代表团全体成员
    新华社科纳克里八日电　由张挺率领的中华人民共和国政府和军事代表团，应几内亚政府的邀请参加几内亚共和国独立十周年庆祝活动后，八日乘飞机离开科纳克里回国。
    七日晚，几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔在总统府接见了中国代表团的全体成员，并且同他们进行了友好的谈话。接见时在座的有几内亚外交部长兰萨纳·贝阿沃吉，经济发展部长伊斯梅尔·杜尔，贸易部长凯塔·恩法马拉，人民军和保卫革命部部长迪亚奈·兰萨纳，社会事务部长杜尔·马穆纳和国民议会议长莱昂·马卡。
    中国驻几内亚大使馆临时代办林秉南也在座。
    在访问期间，中国代表团先后拜访了几内亚人民军和保卫革命部部长迪亚奈·兰萨纳、外交部长兰萨纳·贝阿沃吉和经济发展部长伊斯梅尔·杜尔，并同他们进行了友好的交谈。中国代表团团长张挺代表中国政府，向迪亚奈·兰萨纳赠送了上面绣有“中国人民和几内亚人民之间的战斗友谊万岁！”的锦旗。迪亚奈·兰萨纳代表几内亚政府和人民，向中国政府和人民表示衷心的感谢。
    中国代表团还访问了几内亚的拉贝、皮塔、达拉巴、马穆等城市，以及由中国帮助建成的金康水电站，受到当地官员和军民的热烈欢迎。
    在中国代表团八日离开科纳克里时，到机场送行的有几内亚负责运输的国务秘书莫里·凯塔和几内亚人民军副总参谋长卡曼·迪亚比。
    中国驻几内亚大使馆临时代办林秉南和中国大使馆人员也到机场送行。
