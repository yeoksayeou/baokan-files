Title: Chinese Ambassador to Ceylon Hosts National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1968-10-08
Edition: 5

The Chinese Ambassador to Ceylon, Charge d'Affaires Xi Yingsheng, hosted a reception on October 7 to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. Over 600 people from various sectors in Ceylon attended the reception. Guests included Ceylon Senate President A. Ranasinghe, Minister of Agriculture and Food Banada, Minister of Home Affairs Dahanayake, Minister of Commerce and Trade Hugh Fernando, Minister of Broadcasting and Information Amaratunga, and Minister of Science and Housing Jayawadena. Diplomats from several countries stationed in Ceylon also attended the reception.

### 我驻锡兰使节举行国庆招待会

1968-10-08
第5版()
专栏：

    我驻锡兰使节举行国庆招待会
    据新华社讯　我驻锡兰大使馆临时代办奚业胜一日晚上举行招待会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。锡兰各界人士六百多人出席了招待会。来宾中有锡兰参议院议长阿·拉纳雅克、农业和粮食部长班达、内政部长达哈纳亚克、商业和贸易部长休·费尔南多、广播和新闻部长阿马拉顿吉、科学研究和住房部长贾雅瓦德尼等高级官员。驻锡兰的一些国家的外交使节也出席了招待会。
