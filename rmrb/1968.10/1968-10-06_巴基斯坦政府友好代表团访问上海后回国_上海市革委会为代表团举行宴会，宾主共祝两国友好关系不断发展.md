Title: Pakistani Government Friendship Delegation Visits Shanghai and Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1968-10-06
Edition: 6

By a delegation led by Advisor Sayyid Fida Hasan to President Ayub Khan of Pakistan, the Pakistani government friendship delegation concluded its friendly visit to our country and departed Shanghai by plane this afternoon to return home.

The Pakistani government friendship delegation arrived in Shanghai on the morning of the 4th, accompanied by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong. When the Pakistani guests arrived in and departed from Shanghai, leaders of the Shanghai Municipal Revolutionary Committee and responsible officials of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Shanghai, Wang Shaoyong and Duan Dezhang, representatives of the Shanghai Workers' Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Team, responsible members of various revolutionary mass organizations in Shanghai, representatives of poor and lower-middle peasants, and representatives of five-good soldiers stationed in Shanghai, as well as revolutionary masses, went to the airport to warmly welcome and see them off.

On the evening of the 4th, the Shanghai Municipal Revolutionary Committee held a banquet for the delegation. At the banquet filled with friendly atmosphere, Wang Shaoyong and Advisor Hasan both spoke, expressing their wishes for the continuous development of friendly relations between China and Pakistan and the deepening of friendship between the peoples of the two countries.

During their stay in Shanghai, the Pakistani guests visited the Shanghai Watch Factory and the Shanghai Diesel Engine Factory, among others, and were warmly welcomed by revolutionary workers.

Today, when the delegation left Shanghai, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong went to the airport to see them off. Sultan Muhammad Khan, the Pakistani Ambassador to China, and his wife, who accompanied the delegation to Shanghai, as well as Pakistani friends in Shanghai, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 巴基斯坦政府友好代表团访问上海后回国  上海市革委会为代表团举行宴会，宾主共祝两国友好关系不断发展

1968-10-06
第6版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦政府友好代表团访问上海后回国
    上海市革委会为代表团举行宴会，宾主共祝两国友好关系不断发展
    新华社上海五日电　由巴基斯坦总统阿尤布·汗的顾问赛义德·菲达·哈桑率领的巴基斯坦政府友好代表团，结束了在我国的友好访问以后，今天下午乘飞机离开上海回国。
    巴基斯坦政府友好代表团是四日上午在我国外交部副部长韩念龙陪同下到达上海的。巴基斯坦贵宾们在到达和离开上海时，上海市革命委员会领导成员和人民解放军驻上海部队负责人王少庸、段德彰，上海工人毛泽东思想宣传队代表，上海各革命群众组织负责成员、贫下中农代表和驻上海部队五好战士代表，以及革命群众等，前往机场热烈迎送。
    四日晚上，上海市革命委员会为代表团举行了宴会。在洋溢着友好气氛的宴会上，王少庸同志和哈桑顾问先后讲话，他们共祝中巴两国的友好关系不断发展，两国人民之间的深厚友谊日益增进。
    巴基斯坦贵宾们在上海期间，参观了上海手表厂和上海柴油机厂等，受到革命职工的热烈欢迎。
    代表团今天离开上海时，外交部副部长韩念龙到机场欢送。随同代表团到上海的巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆·汗和夫人，以及在上海的巴基斯坦朋友也到机场送行。
