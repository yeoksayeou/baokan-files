Title: The Book "Advancing Victoriously on the Road to Victory" Published
Author:
Date: 1968-10-07
Edition: 2

The important speech made by Comrade Lin Biao, Vice Chairman of the Communist Party of China and Chairman Mao's close comrade-in-arms, at the celebration of the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the speech made by Premier Zhou Enlai at the reception celebrating the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, and the editorial "Advancing Victoriously on the Road to Victory" published in the People's Daily, Red Flag Magazine, and Liberation Army Daily, have been compiled and published by the People's Publishing House under the title "Advancing Victoriously on the Road to Victory."

The book will be available for purchase at the Xinhua Bookstore in Beijing starting from October 6 and will be available in Xinhua Bookstores across the country in the coming days.

### 《在胜利的大道上奋勇前进》一书出版

1968-10-07
第2版()
专栏：

    《在胜利的大道上奋勇前进》一书出版
    新华社五日讯　伟大领袖毛主席的亲密战友林彪副主席在中华人民共和国成立十九周年庆祝大会上的重要讲话，周恩来总理在庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年招待会上的讲话，《人民日报》、《红旗》杂志、《解放军报》社论《在胜利的大道上奋勇前进》，已由人民出版社汇编出版，书名为《在胜利的大道上奋勇前进》。
    这本书从十月六日开始在北京新华书店发行，并将在全国各地新华书店陆续发行。
