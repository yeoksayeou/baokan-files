Title: Delegation from the Japan-China Friendship Association (Orthodox) and the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-10-01
Edition: 7

At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries and the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Japan, a delegation from the Japan-China Friendship Association (Orthodox) and the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, consisting of 10 people led by Chairman Kuroda Juman, arrived in Beijing by plane this evening. The delegation is in China to participate in the celebrations of the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and to conduct a friendly visit.

Relevant officials from China, including Ding Xilin, welcomed the delegation at the airport.

The honorary chairman of the delegation, Nishisonji Koichi, who is in Beijing, along with some Japanese friends also present in Beijing, went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 日中友好协会（正统）  日中文化交流协会联合代表团到京

1968-10-01
第7版()
专栏：

    日中友好协会（正统）  日中文化交流协会联合代表团到京
    新华社二十九日讯　应中国人民对外文化友好协会和中国日本友好协会邀请，前来参加中华人民共和国成立十九周年庆祝活动，并进行友好访问的日本中国友好协会（正统）、日本中国文化交流协会联合代表团，一行十人，在团长黑田寿男率领下，今晚乘飞机到达北京。
    我有关方面负责人丁西林等前往机场欢迎。
    在北京的这个代表团的名誉团长西园寺公一和在北京的一些日本朋友也到机场欢迎。
