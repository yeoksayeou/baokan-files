Title: Vietnamese Party and State Leaders Send Congratulatory Message on China's National Day
Author:
Date: 1968-10-01
Edition: 5

	Vietnamese Party and State Leaders Send Congratulatory Message on China's National Day
	Wish for Everlasting Brotherly Friendship Between Vietnamese and Chinese Peoples

	New China News Agency, September 30th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Vice Chairman Lin Biao of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received a congratulatory telegram from Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, Chairman of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Chairman Long Zheng of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Premier Pham Van Dong of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on the occasion of the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The full text is as follows:

	To Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Vice Chairman Lin Biao of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Dear Comrades:

	On the occasion of celebrating the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, we, on behalf of the Vietnamese people, the Vietnam Workers' Party, the National Assembly, and the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and in our personal capacity, extend our warmest congratulations to you, to the 700 million Chinese people, the Communist Party of China, the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, and the Government of the People's Republic of China.

	The victory of the Chinese revolution and the establishment of the People's Republic of China are the most significant events in the history of world revolution since the great October Socialist Revolution. This victory has brought about a completely favorable change in the balance of world forces for socialism, greatly encouraging the national liberation movements in Asia, Africa, and Latin America.

	Over the past 19 years, the fraternal Chinese people, under the leadership of the Communist Party of China headed by the respected Chairman Mao Zedong, have gone through arduous struggles and achieved one great victory after another in the cause of socialist revolution and socialist construction. China's possession of nuclear weapons is a great achievement in the field of national defense for a powerful socialist country; at the same time, it is a significant contribution to the world people's struggle for peace, national independence, democracy, and socialist revolution. The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution has achieved brilliant victories, and revolutionary committees have been established throughout the country (except Taiwan Province). The Chinese people are striving to build a new China into a powerful socialist country with modern industry, agriculture, national defense, and science and technology as soon as possible.

	The Communist Party of China, the Government of the People's Republic of China, and the Chinese people have consistently held high the banner of opposing imperialism led by the United States, strongly supporting the national liberation movements, and safeguarding world peace.

	The Vietnamese people are immensely excited by the brilliant victories achieved by the fraternal Chinese people and regard them as a great encouragement to their own revolutionary cause.

	The Vietnamese people sincerely wish that the Chinese people, armed with Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought, under the leadership of the Communist Party of China, will achieve even greater and more comprehensive victories in the cause of socialist construction.

	Our two countries have traditional friendly relations. The revolutionary struggles of our two peoples against imperialism for the construction of socialism have further consolidated and developed this fraternal relationship on the basis of Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism. In the past anti-French colonial war, in the current socialist construction in the North, and in the anti-American national salvation war, the Vietnamese people have consistently received sincere support and great assistance from the Chinese people in various aspects. On this occasion, we express our deep gratitude for the great support and precious assistance given by the Communist Party of China, the Government of the People's Republic of China, and the fraternal Chinese people.

	For the interests of our two peoples and for the interests of the world people's revolutionary cause, the Vietnamese people, the Vietnam Workers' Party, the National Assembly, and the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam are determined to do their utmost to further consolidate and strengthen the fraternal friendship and fighting unity between Vietnam and China.

	May the fraternal friendship and fighting unity between the peoples of Vietnam and China last forever.

	Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, Chairman of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
	Chairman Long Zheng of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
	Premier Pham Van Dong of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam

	September 29, 1968, Hanoi

### 越南党和国家领导人电贺我国庆  祝越中两国人民的兄弟友谊万古长青

1968-10-01
第5版()
专栏：

    越南党和国家领导人电贺我国庆
    祝越中两国人民的兄弟友谊万古长青
    新华社三十日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志、中国共产党中央委员会副主席林彪同志、中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志，收到了越南劳动党中央委员会主席、越南民主共和国主席胡志明同志、越南民主共和国国会常务委员会主席长征同志、越南民主共和国政府总理范文同同志发来的祝贺中华人民共和国成立十九周年的电报，全文如下：北京中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志，中国共产党中央委员会副主席林彪同志，中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志，亲爱的同志们：
    值此庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年之际，我们谨代表越南人民、越南劳动党、越南民主共和国国会和政府，并以我们个人的名义，向你们，向七亿中国人民、中国共产党、中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会和中华人民共和国政府致以最热烈的祝贺。
    中国革命的胜利和中华人民共和国的成立是世界革命史上继伟大的十月社会主义革命之后的最重大的事件。这一胜利使世界力量对比发生了完全有利于社会主义的变化，极大地鼓舞了亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲的民族解放运动。
    十九年来，兄弟的中国人民在以敬爱的毛泽东主席为首的中国共产党领导下，经过了坚强的斗争，在社会主义革命和社会主义建设事业中，取得了一个又一个的巨大胜利。中国拥有核武器，这是一个强大的社会主义国家在国防方面所取得的一个伟大成就；同时，对世界人民争取和平、民族独立、民主和社会主义的革命斗争，也是一个重大贡献。无产阶级文化大革命已经取得辉煌的胜利，革命委员会在全国（除台湾省外）各省、市、自治区已经全部成立。中国人民正在为把新中国迅速建成一个具有现代工业、现代农业、现代国防和现代科学技术的社会主义强国而不断奋斗。
    中国共产党、中华人民共和国政府和中国人民一贯高举反对以美国为首的帝国主义的旗帜，大力支持民族解放运动，保卫世界和平。
    越南人民为兄弟的中国人民所取得的光辉胜利，感到无比兴奋，并把它看做是对自己革命事业的巨大鼓舞。
    越南人民真诚地祝愿用马克思列宁主义和毛泽东思想武装起来的中国人民，在中国共产党的领导之下，在社会主义建设事业中取得更加巨大的全面的胜利。
    我们两国人民有着传统的友好关系。我们两国人民为建设社会主义而进行的反对帝国主义的革命斗争，使得这种兄弟关系在马克思列宁主义和无产阶级国际主义基础上不断地更加巩固和发展。在过去的反对法国殖民主义的抗战中，在现在的北方社会主义建设事业中和抗美救国战争中，越南人民一贯得到中国人民多方面的真诚支持和巨大援助。趁此机会，我们对中国共产党、中华人民共和国政府和兄弟的中国人民所给予的这种巨大支持和宝贵的援助表示深切的感谢。
    为了我们两国人民的利益，为了全世界人民革命事业的利益，越南人民、越南劳动党、越南民主共和国国会和政府，决心竭尽全力来进一步巩固和加强越中两国之间的兄弟友谊和战斗团结。
    祝越中两国人民之间的兄弟友谊和战斗团结万古长青。
       越南劳动党中央委员会主席、越南民主共和国主席　胡志明
       越南民主共和国国会常务委员会主席  长征
       越南民主共和国政府总理　范文同
                                一九六八年九月二十九日于河内
