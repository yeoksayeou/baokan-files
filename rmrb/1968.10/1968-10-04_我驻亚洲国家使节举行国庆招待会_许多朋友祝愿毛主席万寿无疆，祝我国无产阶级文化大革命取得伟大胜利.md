Title: Chinese Envoys in Asian Countries Hold National Day Receptions
Author:
Date: 1968-10-04
Edition: 5

Chinese embassies in some Asian countries held receptions on October 1 to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

The Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Pakistan, Xu Ying, held a reception on October 1 to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

More than 300 guests attended the reception, including Pakistan's Defense and Interior Minister Aftab Khan, Commerce Minister Abdul Ghaffar Khan, Deputy Chairman of the Planning Commission Ahmed, Commander-in-Chief of the Pakistan Army General Yahya Khan, Deputy Speaker of the National Assembly Chaudhry Fazal Elahi, and other high-ranking military and political officials, as well as prominent figures from various sectors.

The reception was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere. The guests congratulated the Chinese people on their great achievements under the leadership of Chairman Mao in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and socialist construction. Many guests expressed their wishes for the long life of Chairman Mao, the great leader of the Chinese people, and for the everlasting friendship between China and Pakistan.

Diplomatic envoys from some other countries in Pakistan also attended the reception.

On the same evening, the Chinese Consulates General in Karachi and Dhaka also held receptions to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

The Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Afghanistan, Yao Jiyuan, held a reception on October 1 in Kabul to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Attendees included Afghanistan's Prince Shah Wali Khan Ghazi, Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Nur Ahmad Etemadi, Speaker of the People's Assembly Abdul Zahir, Minister of Culture and Propaganda Mohammad Anas, Minister of Commerce Nur Ali, Minister of Public Works Mohammad Hussein Mas, Minister of Agriculture Mir Mohammad Akbar Reza, Minister without Portfolio Abdul Wahab Sarlabi, Chief of Staff Gulam Faruq General, Air Defense Commander Abdul Razak General, Governor of Kabul Province Mohammad Akram, and more than 260 other military and political officials and friends from various sectors.

Diplomatic envoys from some other countries in Afghanistan also attended the reception.

The reception was filled with the friendly atmosphere of China and Afghanistan. Afghan friends highly praised the great achievements of the Chinese people and wished the People's Republic of China to be stronger and stronger. The hosts and guests toasted to the continuous development of the traditional friendship and good-neighborly relations between China and Afghanistan.

The Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Nepal, Tu Guowei, held a reception on the evening of October 1 to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Attendees included Nepal's Queen Ratna, the second son of the King Jigme Dorji, the second brother of the King Himala, and other members of the royal family.

Also in attendance were Nepal's Prime Minister Surendra Bahadur Thapa, Defense Minister Giri Prasad Budhathoki, Minister of Finance and Industry Suralendra Bahadur Basnet, Minister of Foreign Affairs and Education Jyendra Bahadur Rajbhandari, Minister of Land Reform, Food, and Agriculture Rudra Prasad Giri, several ministers of state, Commander-in-Chief of the Royal Army Suralendra Bahadur Shah, and other high-ranking military and political officials.

Attendees also included: Vice Chairman of the National Assembly Praja Raj Lakshmi Singh, President of the Nepal-China Friendship Association Purna Bahadur, Chairman of the Nepal-China Cultural Association Buban Lal Pradhan, and other friendly individuals. Diplomatic envoys from some other countries in Nepal also attended the reception. More than 500 guests attended the reception, which was held in a friendly atmosphere.

Many Nepalese friends expressed their wishes for the long life of Chairman Mao, for the great achievements of the Chinese people under the leadership of Chairman Mao in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and in all aspects, and for the everlasting friendship between the peoples of Nepal and China.

The Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in the Kingdom of Cambodia, Zheng Sixiong, held a reception on October 1 to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Attendees included: Princess Rasmey Sobean, Chairman of the Cambodian Cabinet and Chairman of the Cambodia-China Friendship National Committee Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Speaker of the National Assembly Chou Sengkhosal, Vice Chairman of the Supreme Council of the Throne Prince Kantol, Prince Norodom Sihanouk's son Prince Norodom Narindrapong, Director of the Office of the Head of State Ron Sopheap, Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs Oun Narith, and military and political officials and representatives from various sectors. Diplomatic officials from some other countries in Cambodia and other friendly individuals also attended the reception.

The reception was held in a warm and cordial atmosphere from beginning to end.

The Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Laos, Li Lianping, held a reception on the evening of October 1 to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Lao government officials and prominent figures attended the reception.

The representative of the Lao Patriotic Front in Vientiane also attended the reception.

After the reception, the Chinese color documentary film "The Red Lantern" with piano accompaniment and "The Great Victory of Mao Zedong Thought" were shown.

### 我驻亚洲国家使节举行国庆招待会  许多朋友祝愿毛主席万寿无疆，祝我国无产阶级文化大革命取得伟大胜利

1968-10-04
第5版()
专栏：

    我驻亚洲国家使节举行国庆招待会
    许多朋友祝愿毛主席万寿无疆，祝我国无产阶级文化大革命取得伟大胜利
    据新华社讯　中国驻亚洲一些国家使馆在十月一日分别举行招待会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。
    我驻巴基斯坦大使馆临时代办徐英一日举行招待会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。
    三百多名客人出席了招待会，其中有巴基斯坦国防兼内政部长阿夫扎尔·拉赫曼·汗、商业部长阿卜杜勒·加福尔·汗、计划委员会副主席艾哈迈德、巴基斯坦陆军总司令叶海亚汗将军、国民议会首席副议长乔德里·法查尔·埃拉希和其他政府高级军政官员以及各界著名人士。
    招待会是在热烈友好的气氛中进行的。出席招待会的客人表示祝贺中国人民在毛主席领导下，在无产阶级文化大革命和社会主义建设中取得的伟大胜利。许多客人表示祝愿中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席万寿无疆，祝愿中巴友谊万古长青。
    驻巴基斯坦的一些国家的外交使节也出席了招待会。
    同一天晚上，中国驻卡拉奇总领事馆和驻达卡总领事馆也分别举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。
    我驻阿富汗大使馆临时代办姚吉年一日在喀布尔举行招待会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。
    出席招待会的有阿富汗王叔沙阿·瓦利·汗·加齐元帅、首相兼外交大臣努尔·艾哈迈德·埃特马迪、人民院议长阿卜杜勒·查希尔、文化和宣传大臣穆罕默德·阿纳什、商业大臣努尔·阿里、公共工程大臣穆罕默德·侯赛因·马萨、农业大臣米尔·穆罕默德·阿克巴尔·雷扎、不管部大臣阿卜杜勒·瓦希卜·萨拉比、总参谋长古拉姆·法鲁克将军、空防司令阿卜杜勒·拉扎克将军、喀布尔省省长穆罕默德·阿克拉姆等军政高级官员和各界朋友二百六十多人。
    驻阿富汗的一些外交使节也出席了招待会。
    招待会充满了中国和阿富汗两国友好的气氛。阿富汗朋友们热烈赞扬中国人民所取得的伟大成就，祝愿中华人民共和国日益强大。宾主共祝中国和阿富汗的传统友谊和友好睦邻关系不断发展。
    我驻尼泊尔大使馆临时代办屠国维一日晚上举行招待会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。
    出席招待会的有尼泊尔王后拉特纳、国王的次子贾伦德拉、国王的二弟喜马拉雅和王室的其他一些成员。
    出席招待会的还有尼泊尔首相苏里雅·巴哈杜尔·塔帕，国防大臣吉里·普拉萨德·布达托基，财政和工商大臣苏伦德拉·巴哈杜尔·巴斯奈特，外交和教育大臣杰亨德拉·巴哈杜尔·拉杰班达里，土地改革、粮食和农业大臣鲁德拉·普拉萨德·吉里，一些国务大臣，皇家陆军总司令苏伦德拉·巴哈杜尔·沙阿，以及其他高级军政官员。
    出席招待会的还有：全国评议会副议长普拉贾·拉贾·拉克斯米·辛格夫人，尼中友好协会会长普尔纳·巴哈杜尔，尼泊尔—中国文化协会主席布班·拉尔·普拉丹以及其他友好人士。一些国家驻尼泊尔的外交使节也出席了招待会。到会客人共五百多人。招待会在友好的气氛中进行。
    许多尼泊尔朋友表示祝愿毛主席万寿无疆，祝愿中国人民在伟大领袖毛主席的领导下，在无产阶级文化大革命中和各个方面取得更大的胜利和成就，祝愿尼中两国人民的友谊万古长青。
    我驻柬埔寨王国大使馆临时代办郑斯雄一日举行招待会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。
    出席招待会的有：拉斯美·索巴娜王姑，柬埔寨内阁首相、柬埔寨—中国友好全国委员会主席宾努亲王，国民议会议长周森古沙，王位最高委员会副主席康托尔亲王，柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王的儿子诺林达拉邦王子，国家元首办公厅主任罗恩索方，代理外交大臣阮财基，以及军政官员和各界代表等。一些国家驻柬埔寨的外交官员和其他友好人士也出席了招待会。
    招待会自始至终在热烈和亲切的气氛中进行。
    我驻老挝大使馆临时代办李联平一日晚举行招待会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。
    老挝政府官员和知名人士出席了招待会。
    老挝爱国战线党驻万象代表也出席了招待会。
    会后放映了中国彩色纪录影片钢琴伴唱《红灯记》和《毛泽东思想的伟大胜利》。
