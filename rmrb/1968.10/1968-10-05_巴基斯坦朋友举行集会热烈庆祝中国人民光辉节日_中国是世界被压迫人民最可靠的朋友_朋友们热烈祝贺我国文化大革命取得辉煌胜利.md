Title: Pakistani Friends Hold Rally to Warmly Celebrate the Glorious Festival of the Chinese People
Author:
Date: 1968-10-05
Edition: 6

Pakistani friends held public rallies to warmly celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

On September 30, in the Pakistani capital of Rawalpindi, Central Transport Minister Sabur Khan, speaking at a rally organized by the Pakistan-China Friendship Association, highly praised the great progress made by the Chinese people under the leadership of Chairman Mao in all fields.

In discussing Sino-Pakistani relations, he said that the friendship between the two countries, which serves the common interests of the two peoples, has withstood the test of time. With the passage of time, this friendship will grow stronger.

In concluding his speech, he wished long life to the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao!

Ayub Mirza, Secretary of the Pakistan-China Friendship Association in Rawalpindi, also spoke at the rally. He extended warm congratulations to the Chinese people on the brilliant victories achieved in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution personally initiated and led by Chairman Mao. He said: The Cultural Revolution is another epoch-making great achievement made by the Chinese people since the founding of the People's Republic of China in consolidating and defending the socialist system.

In Lahore, the capital of West Pakistan, Montaz Ahmad Khan, Chairman of the Lahore Pakistan-China Friendship Association, speaking at a public rally, said that the Pakistani people are proud to have the friendship of China, because under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong, China has now become the strongest bastion and most reliable friend of all oppressed peoples in the world. A powerful People's Republic of China stands tall in Asia, which is a guarantee of the independence and freedom of all newly emerging countries in their struggle against imperialist aggression.

The friends attending the rally repeatedly shouted slogans: "Long live the great leader Chairman Mao!" "Long live the People's Republic of China!" "Long live Sino-Pakistani friendship!" "Down with American imperialism!"

In Dhaka, the capital of East Pakistan, former Chief Justice of the East Pakistan High Court, S.M. Mushtaque, presided over the opening ceremony of a three-day celebration of China's National Day organized by the East Pakistan Pakistan-China Friendship Association. In his opening address at the public rally, he said: The Pakistani people, with grateful hearts, thank the Chinese people for the valuable support and assistance they provided in 1965 when Pakistan was subjected to Indian armed aggression.

Sino-Pakistani friendship is a lasting friendship, and it will continue to grow stronger.

Farmers and townspeople from the town of Taxila near Rawalpindi held a rally on September 29 to celebrate China's National Day. Speakers at the rally said: The great Chinese revolution has shown the workers and peasants and other oppressed peoples of the world a new hope for life and has dealt a crushing blow to world imperialism led by the United States. In this era when imperialism is heading towards total collapse, the Chinese revolution is a beacon of light for all oppressed and exploited peoples.

A Punjabi poet recited a poem at the rally, enthusiastically praising the invincible Mao Zedong Thought.

Diplomats from the Chinese Embassy in Pakistan attended these rallies and spoke at the meetings.

### 巴基斯坦朋友举行集会热烈庆祝中国人民光辉节日  中国是世界被压迫人民最可靠的朋友  朋友们热烈祝贺我国文化大革命取得辉煌胜利

1968-10-05
第6版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦朋友举行集会热烈庆祝中国人民光辉节日
    中国是世界被压迫人民最可靠的朋友
    朋友们热烈祝贺我国文化大革命取得辉煌胜利
    新华社拉瓦尔品第三日电　巴基斯坦朋友举行公众集会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立十九周年。
    一日，在巴基斯坦首都拉瓦尔品第，中央交通部长萨布尔汗，在拉瓦尔品第巴中友协主办的集会上发表讲话，高度赞扬中国人民在毛主席的领导下，在各方面所取得的巨大进步。
    他在谈到中巴关系时说，符合两国人民共同利益的两国之间的友谊，经受住了时间的考验。随着时间的推移，这种友谊将日益增强。
    他在讲话结束时，祝愿中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席万寿无疆！
    拉瓦尔品第巴中友协秘书阿尤布·米尔扎也在会上讲了话。他对中国人民在毛主席亲自发动和领导的无产阶级文化大革命中所取得的辉煌胜利表示热烈的祝贺。他说：文化大革命是中国人民自从中华人民共和国成立以来，在巩固和捍卫社会主义制度方面所取得的又一个划时代的伟大成就。
    在西巴基斯坦首府拉合尔，拉合尔巴中友协主席蒙塔兹·艾哈迈德汗，在一次公众集会上发表讲话说，巴基斯坦人民为有中国的友谊而感到自豪，因为在毛泽东主席的领导下，中国现在已成为全世界所有被压迫人民的最坚强的堡垒和最可靠的朋友。一个强大的中华人民共和国巍然屹立在亚洲，这是抗击帝国主义侵略的所有新兴国家的独立和自由的一个保证。
    参加集会的朋友们一再高呼：“伟大领袖毛主席万岁！”“中华人民共和国万岁！”“巴中友谊万岁！”“打倒美帝国主义！”
    在东巴基斯坦首府达卡，前东巴高等法院首席法官赛·马·穆希德，主持了由东巴巴中友协举办的历时三天的祝贺中国国庆的活动的开幕仪式。他在公众集会上致开幕词说：巴基斯坦人民怀着感激的心情，感谢中国人民一九六五年在巴基斯坦遭受印度武装侵略的时刻，所给予的宝贵支持和援助。
    中巴友谊是持久的友谊，它将继续不断增长。
    拉瓦尔品第附近的城镇塔克西拉的农民和城镇居民，九月二十九日举行集会，祝贺中国国庆。在会上发言的人说：伟大的中国革命向全世界的工人和农民以及其他被压迫的人民展示了生活的新希望，并给予以美国为首的世界帝国主义以粉碎性的打击。在帝国主义走向全面崩溃的这个时代，对于所有被压迫和被剥削人民来说，中国革命是光芒四射的灯塔。
    一位旁遮普诗人在会上热情地朗诵了歌颂战无不胜的毛泽东思想的诗歌。
    中国驻巴基斯坦大使馆的外交官员出席了这些集会，并在会上讲了话。
