	Chairman Mao's Quotations

	It is a very good opportunity for cadres to go down to the countryside to labor and learn. Except for the old, weak, sick, and disabled, all cadres should do this. Cadres in office should also go down to the countryside in batches to labor.

### 毛主席语录

    毛主席语录
