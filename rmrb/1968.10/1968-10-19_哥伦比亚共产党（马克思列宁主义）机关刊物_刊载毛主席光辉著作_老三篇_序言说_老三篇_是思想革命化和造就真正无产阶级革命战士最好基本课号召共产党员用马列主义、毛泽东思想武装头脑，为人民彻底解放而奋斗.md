Title: The Official Publication of the Colombian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist) Publishes Chairman Mao's "Three Articles"
Author:
Date: 1968-10-19
Edition: 6

The official publication of the Colombian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist), "Marxist-Leninist Thought," recently published Chairman Mao's three brilliant works: "In Memory of Norman Bethune," "Serve the People," and "On the Rectification of Work Style." In the preface to these works, the publication states that the "Three Articles" are the best basic course for ideological revolutionization and for fostering genuine proletarian revolutionaries. The preface calls on Communist Party members to arm themselves with Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought and to strive for the complete liberation of the people.

	Xinhua News Agency, October 18th: Bogota, Colombia -- The official publication of the Colombian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist), "Marxist-Leninist Thought," recently published Chairman Mao's three brilliant works: "In Memory of Norman Bethune," "Serve the People," and "On the Rectification of Work Style." In the preface to these works, the publication states that the "Three Articles" are the best basic course for ideological revolutionization and for fostering genuine proletarian revolutionaries.

	The preface states that the Colombian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist) is the shining guide for the Colombian working class, peasants, and other strata of people in their struggle against American imperialism and its lackeys, the big bourgeoisie and landlords in Colombia. It is fully committed to the cause of the proletarian revolution and tirelessly struggles for the complete liberation of the Colombian people.

	The preface points out: "To fully achieve these revolutionary goals, the best guarantee is to have the excellent guidance of Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought in our minds and to enable the masses to grasp this powerful, invincible weapon to carry out the revolution."

	The preface states: "We are on the path of learning to correctly combine the universal truths of Marxism-Leninism with the practice of our Colombian revolution." The preface points out that it is necessary to learn and creatively apply Mao Zedong Thought. The preface continues: The prerequisite for achieving revolutionary victory is to use Mao Zedong Thought as the guiding principle in all our actions in life.

	The preface states: "Comrade Mao Zedong is the greatest Marxist-Leninist of our time. He has brilliantly and comprehensively developed Marxism to a new stage. One of Comrade Mao Zedong's greatest contributions to Marxism-Leninism is the development of the theory of the communist worldview contained in the 'Three Articles.'"

	The preface points out: "The 'Three Articles,' this great work of Marxism-Leninism, is the best guide for our ideological revolutionization, the best basic course for communist education, and for fostering genuine proletarian warriors. We should study Comrade Mao Zedong's works, taking the study of the 'Three Articles' as the foundation and starting point. If we do this, there is no doubt that our work will greatly improve, and the revolution will have a bright future."

	The preface concludes: "Let us advance resolutely, mobilize the masses, and bravely march forward to seize power under the guidance of Comrade Mao Zedong's thought!"

1968-10-19
第6版()
专栏：

    哥伦比亚共产党（马克思列宁主义）机关刊物
    刊载毛主席光辉著作“老三篇”
    序言说“老三篇”是思想革命化和造就真正无产阶级革命战士最好基本课号召共产党员用马列主义、毛泽东思想武装头脑，为人民彻底解放而奋斗
    新华社十八日讯　波哥大消息：哥伦比亚共产党（马克思列宁主义）机关刊物《马列主义思想》最近刊载了毛主席的《为人民服务》、《纪念白求恩》和《愚公移山》三篇光辉著作。这家刊物在为发表毛主席这三篇著作所写的序言中指出，“老三篇”是思想革命化和造就真正的无产阶级革命战士的最好的基本课。
    序言说，哥伦比亚共产党（马列）是哥伦比亚工人阶级、农民和其他各阶层人民在反对美帝国主义及其在哥伦比亚的奴仆大资产阶级、大地主的斗争中的光辉向导。它完全致力于无产阶级的革命事业，不倦地为取得哥伦比亚人民的彻底解放而斗争。
    序言指出：“我们要完全实现这些革命目标，最好的保证是在我们的头脑里有马克思列宁主义、毛泽东思想的卓越指导和使群众掌握这个强大的、战无不胜的武器，进行革命。”
    序言说：“我们正处在学习把马克思列宁主义的普遍真理同我们哥伦比亚革命的实践正确地结合起来的道路上。”序言指出，必须学习和创造性地运用毛泽东思想。序言接着说：要取得革命胜利的前提，是在我们生活的一切行动中用毛泽东思想挂帅。
    序言说：“毛泽东同志是当代最伟大的马克思列宁主义者。他天才地、全面地把马克思主义发展到一个崭新的阶段。毛泽东同志对马克思列宁主义的最伟大的贡献之一就是发展了包含在‘老三篇’中的共产主义世界观的理论。”
    序言指出：“老三篇”这一马克思列宁主义的伟大著作，是使我们思想革命化的最好指导，是进行共产主义教育和用来造就真正的无产阶级战士的最好基本课。我们学习毛泽东同志的著作，应该把学习“老三篇”作为基础和起点。如果我们做到这一点，无疑我们的工作将大大改进，革命将有一个光辉的前途。
    序言最后说：“让我们坚定地前进，动员群众，在毛泽东同志的思想的指引下勇敢地向夺取政权挺进！”
