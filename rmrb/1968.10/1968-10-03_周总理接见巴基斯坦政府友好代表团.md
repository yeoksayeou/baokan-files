Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Pakistani Government Friendship Delegation
Author:
Date: 1968-10-03
Edition: 2

Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council met today with Advisor to the President of Pakistan Ayub Khan, Said Fida Hasan, and the entire delegation of the Pakistani Government Friendship Delegation led by him. They had a warm and friendly conversation.

Vice Premier Chen Yi and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qian Hanlun were present at the meeting.

Pakistani Ambassador to China Sultan Muhammad Khan was also present.

### 周总理接见巴基斯坦政府友好代表团

1968-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理接见巴基斯坦政府友好代表团
    新华社二日讯　国务院总理周恩来，今天接见了巴基斯坦总统阿尤布·汗的顾问赛义德·菲达·哈桑和由他率领的巴基斯坦政府友好代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时，国务院副总理陈毅，外交部副部长韩念龙在座。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆·汗也在座。
