Title: End of Visit to the Soviet Union: Delegation of Demobilized Chinese Soldiers Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-29
Page: 5

    Ending the Visit to the Soviet Union
    Delegation of Demobilized Chinese Soldiers Returns to Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, 28th - The Chinese delegation of demobilized soldiers, led by Wang Weizhou, member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and member of the Standing Committee of National People's Congress, returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon after concluding its visit to the Soviet Union.
    The delegation visited the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Soviet Committee of Old Revolutionary Soldiers. During their visit to the Soviet Union, the delegation visited old Soviet revolutionary soldiers in cities such as Moscow, Leningrad, and Stalingrad, and brought back the deep friendship of the old Soviet revolutionary soldiers towards demobilized Chinese soldiers.
    Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport today included Qian Ying, member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Minister of Internal Affairs, and Vice Ministers of Internal Affairs Wang Ziyi, Chen Qiyuan, Wang Yifu, and Zhang Yibai, as well as Major General Wang Xinggang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense, and others.

### 结束在苏联的参观访问  我复员军人访苏代表团回京

1959-10-29
第5版()
专栏：

    结束在苏联的参观访问
    我复员军人访苏代表团回京
    新华社28日讯　由中共中央委员、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员王维舟率领的中国复员军人访苏代表团，今天下午乘飞机回到北京。
    代表团是应苏联老革命军人委员会的邀请去苏联访问的。代表团在苏联访问了莫斯科、列宁格勒、斯大林格勒等城市的苏联老革命军人，带回了苏联老革命军人对中国复员军人的深厚友情。
    今天到机场迎接代表团的有中共中央委员、内务部部长钱瑛、内务部副部长王子宜、陈其瑗、王一夫、章夷白，国防部办公厅副主任王兴纲少将等。
