Title: Albanian Party and Government Delegation Leaves Hangzhou for Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1959-10-08
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 7th October. After their tour and visit in Hangzhou, the Albanian Party and Government Delegation led by Mehmet Shehu, member of the Political Bureau, Central Committee of the Albanian Labour Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Albanian People's Republic, left Hangzhou by special plane this morning for a visit to Wuhan. Zeng Shan, member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Minister of Transportation of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Kang Maozhao, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, accompanied them.
    Those who went to the airport to welcome the distinguished guests included Jiang Hua, alternate member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and First Secretary of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Zhou Jianren, Governor of Zhejiang Province; Huo Shilian, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province; Xian Yi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Hangzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Vice Mayor of Hangzhou; and Lieutenant General Zhou Guanwu, head of the Zhejiang Garrison.
    Yesterday, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Zeng Shan, Huo Shilian, Xian Yi and others, toured the scenic spots and historical sites of West Lake in Hangzhou and visited the city of Hangzhou. Yesterday evening, the distinguished guests attended a reception and enjoyed Zhejiang local opera.

### 阿党政代表团离杭州去武汉

1959-10-08
第2版()
专栏：

    阿党政代表团离杭州去武汉
    新华社杭州7日电  阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会政治局委员、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席穆·谢胡和他率领的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国党政代表团，在杭州参观访问后，今天上午乘专机离开杭州前往武汉访问。中共中央委员、中共中央交通工作部部长曾山和外交部新闻司副司长康茅召陪同前往。
    到机场欢送贵宾的有中共中央候补委员、中共浙江省委第一书记江华、浙江省省长周建人，中共浙江省委书记处书记、浙江省副省长霍士廉，中共杭州市委书记处书记、杭州市副市长冼依，浙江驻军首长周贯五中将等人。
    昨天，贵宾们由曾山、霍士廉和冼依等人陪同，在杭州游览了西湖名胜古迹，并参观了杭州市容。昨天晚上，贵宾们出席了招待晚会，欣赏了浙江地方戏。
