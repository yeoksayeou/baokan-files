Title: Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia Holds Reception for Three Chinese Delegations Visiting Mongolia; Tsedenbal Receives Two Chinese Delegations
Author:
Date: 1959-10-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 8th - Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, Xie Fusheng, held a reception tonight for the Chinese-Mongolian Friendship Association delegation, the Chinese Women's Delegation, and the Chinese Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Song and Dance Troupe visiting Mongolia. Ambassador Xie Fusheng spoke at the reception. He said that the three delegations, including the Chinese-Mongolian Friendship Association, have come to Mongolia with the sincere intentions of the Chinese people and will soon return to China laden with the deep friendship of the Mongolian people. He firmly believes that the activities of these three delegations in Mongolia will help to further consolidate and develop the friendly cooperation between the two countries.

Mongolian Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Chairman of the Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Association, Luvsan, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Ramsuren, and the head of the Chinese-Mongolian Friendship Association delegation, Mao Qihua, successively raised their glasses at the meeting, wishing the unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Mongolian people would continue to develop. After the reception, the Chinese color film "Young People in Our Village" was shown.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 9th - The First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Chairman of the Mongolian Council of Ministers, Tsedenbal, today separately received the Chinese-Mongolian Friendship Association delegation headed by Mao Qihua and the Chinese Women's Delegation headed by Guo Mingqiu.

### 为我国三代表团访蒙  我国驻蒙大使举行酒会  泽登巴尔接见我国两代表团

1959-10-10
第5版()
专栏：

    为我国三代表团访蒙
    我国驻蒙大使举行酒会
    泽登巴尔接见我国两代表团
    新华社乌兰巴托8日电　中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生今晚为中蒙友好协会代表团、中国妇女代表团和中国内蒙古自治区歌舞团访问蒙古举行了酒会。谢甫生大使在酒会上讲了话。他说，中蒙友好协会等三个代表团怀着中国人民正直的心意来到了蒙古、即将满载蒙古人民的深情厚谊回到中国。他深信，这三个代表团在蒙古的活动有助于两国友好合作的进一步巩固和发展。
    蒙古部长会议副主席、蒙中友好协会主席鲁布桑、部长会议副主席拉姆苏伦和中蒙友好协会代表团长毛齐华先后在会上举杯，祝中蒙两国人民的牢不可破的友谊不断发展。酒会结束后放映了中国彩色影片“我们村里的年轻人”。
    新华社乌兰巴托9日电　蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记、蒙古部长会议主席泽登巴尔今天分别接见了以毛齐华为首的中蒙友好协会代表团和以郭明秋为首的中国妇女代表团。
