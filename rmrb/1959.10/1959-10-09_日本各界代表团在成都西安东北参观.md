Title: Japanese Delegations Visit Chengdu, Xi'an, and Northeast China
Author:
Date: 1959-10-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, October 8th: The head of the Japanese delegation from all walks of life celebrating the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, former Japanese Prime Minister Tetsu Katayama, deputy heads Tadaharu Kobata, Hitoshi Shiraishi, and thirteen other members arrived in Chengdu on the afternoon of the 7th. On the 8th, accompanied by Liao Jiamin, the vice mayor of Chengdu, they visited the Chengdu Measuring & Cutting Tool Factory and the Shiyang People's Commune in Huayang County.

Katayama Tetsu and other Japanese guests arrived in Chengdu after visiting Xi'an. Another part of the delegation, led by Deputy Head Masato Honda, visited Harbin and Changchun, and left Changchun for Shenyang today. They visited the Changchun First Automobile Manufacturing Plant, the Changchun Rolling Stock Plant, and the Changchun Geological Institute in Changchun. In Harbin, they visited the Harbin Turbine, Boiler, and Electric Motor three major power plants, etc.

### 日本各界代表团在成都西安东北参观

1959-10-09
第6版()
专栏：

    日本各界代表团在成都西安东北参观
    据新华社8日讯　日本各界庆祝中国建国十周年代表团团长、前日本首相片山哲，副团长小畑忠良，白石凡和部分团员共十三人，7日下午到达成都。8日，他们由成都市副市长廖家岷陪同，参观了成都量具刃具厂和华阳县石羊人民公社。
    片山哲和其他日本客人们是在西安进行参观访问后到达成都的。代表团的另外一部分团员由副团长本多正登率领，在哈尔滨和长春进行参观访问后，今天离开长春去沈阳。他们在长春参观了长春第一汽车制造厂、长春车辆厂和长春地质学院，在哈尔滨参观了哈尔滨汽轮机、锅炉、电机三大动力厂等。
