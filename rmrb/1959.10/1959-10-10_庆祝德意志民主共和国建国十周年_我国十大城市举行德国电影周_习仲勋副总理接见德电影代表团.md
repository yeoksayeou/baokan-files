Title: Celebrating the 10th Anniversary of the German Democratic Republic, Ten Major Cities in China Hold German Film Week; Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun Receives German Film Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-10-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 9th: To celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic (GDR), the GDR Film Week is being held simultaneously in ten major cities across the country from the 9th to the 15th. This afternoon, the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries of the People's Republic of China, and the Sino-German Democratic Republic Friendship Association held an opening ceremony for the Film Week in the capital.

Among those attending today's opening ceremony were Vice Premier of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Chairmen of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhang Zhixiang and Luo Jun, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, Vice President of the Sino-German Democratic Republic Friendship Association Liu Aifeng, Deputy Director of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture Situ Huimin, Director of the Beijing Film Bureau Gao Ge, and over 1,200 people from all walks of life in the capital. GDR Ambassador to China Wang Daier, representative of the GDR film delegation who arrived in Beijing on the 8th, screenwriter and National Prize winner Wolfgang Kohlhaase, and film actor, Dresden National Theatre actor and director Otto Starke attended the opening ceremony. Diplomatic personnel from various countries' embassies in China also attended the opening ceremony.

Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing delivered the opening speech. He expressed his belief that through this Film Week, mutual understanding between China and the German Democratic Republic would be further strengthened, and the friendly unity between the people of the two countries would be further enhanced.

GDR Ambassador to China Wang Daier also spoke. After congratulating the People's Republic of China on its tremendous achievements over the past ten years, he said: China and the German Democratic Republic are both holding Film Weeks with the participation of film delegations from both countries, which will make a great contribution to the friendship between the two countries.

Wolfgang Kohlhaase, representative of the GDR film delegation, also spoke at the opening ceremony. He extended heartfelt greetings from GDR film workers to Chinese film workers.

At the opening ceremony, Young Pioneers presented flowers to the German guests. Finally, the GDR film "Sailor's Song" was screened.

Xinhua News Agency, 9th: This afternoon, Vice Premier of the State Council Xi Zhongxun received members of the German Democratic Republic film delegation.

The members of the delegation are: Wolfgang Kohlhaase, film screenwriter and National Prize winner, and Otto Starke, film actor, Dresden National Theatre actor and director.

### 庆祝德意志民主共和国建国十周年  我国十大城市举行德国电影周  习仲勋副总理接见德电影代表团

1959-10-10
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝德意志民主共和国建国十周年
    我国十大城市举行德国电影周
    习仲勋副总理接见德电影代表团
    据新华社9日讯　为庆祝德意志民主共和国成立十周年而举办的德意志民主共和国电影周，9日至15日在全国十大城市同时举行。今天下午，中华人民共和国文化部、中华人民共和国对外文化联络委员会、中国德意志民主共和国友好协会在首都为电影周举行了开幕式。
    参加今天开幕式的有国务院副总理习仲勋，文化部部长沈雁冰，对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥、罗俊，外交部副部长曾涌泉，中国德意志民主共和国友好协会副会长刘皑风，文化部电影事业管理局副局长司徒慧敏，北京市电影事业管理局局长高戈和首都各界人士等一千二百余人。德意志民主共和国驻华大使汪戴尔，在8日到达北京的德意志民主共和国电影代表团代表、电影编剧、国家奖金获得者渥尔夫岗·柯尔哈泽和电影演员、德累斯顿国家剧院演员兼导演奥托·斯塔克参加了开幕式。参加开幕式的还有各国驻华使馆外交人员。
    文化部部长沈雁冰致开幕词。他表示相信，通过这一次电影周的举行，将进一步加强中国同德意志民主共和国之间的互相了解，进一步增强两国人民的友好团结。
    德意志民主共和国驻华大使汪戴尔也讲了话。他在祝贺中华人民共和国成立十年来的巨大成就之后说：中国同德意志民主共和国都同时在举行有两国电影代表团参加的电影周，它将为两国之间的友谊作出巨大的贡献。
    德意志民主共和国电影代表团代表渥尔夫岗·柯尔哈泽也在开幕式上讲了话。他代表德意志民主共和国电影工作者向中国电影工作者致以衷心的问候。
    开幕式上，少先队员们向德国贵宾们献了花，最后放映了德意志民主共和国影片“水兵之歌”。
    据新华社9日讯　今天下午，国务院副总理习仲勋接见了德意志民主共和国电影代表团成员。
    代表团的成员是：电影编剧、国家奖金获得者渥尔夫岗·柯尔哈泽，电影演员、德累斯顿国家剧院演员兼导演奥托·斯塔克。
