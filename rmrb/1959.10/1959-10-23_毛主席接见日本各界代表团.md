Title: Chairman Mao Receives Japanese Delegation

Author:
Date: 1959-10-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd: Chairman Mao Zedong received the Japanese delegation celebrating the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China this evening, headed by delegation leader Tetsu Katayama and deputy leaders Tadayoshi Obata and Hitoshi Shiraishi. Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council, was present at the reception. Also present were Liu Ningyi, Liao Chengzhi, Zhang Xiruo, and others. Kinkazu Saionji, a Japanese peace activist in Beijing, was also present.

### 毛主席接见日本各界代表团

1959-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见日本各界代表团
    新华社22日讯　毛泽东主席今天晚间接见了以团长片山哲，副团长小畑忠良、白石凡为首的日本各界庆祝中华人民共和国建国十周年代表团。接见时在场的有国务院副总理陈毅。在场的还有刘宁一、廖承志、张奚若等。在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也在场。
