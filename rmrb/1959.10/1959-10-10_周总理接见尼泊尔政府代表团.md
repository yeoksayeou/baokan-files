Title: Premier Zhou Receives Nepalese Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-10-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, October 9th: Premier Zhou Enlai received the Minister of Village Development of the Kingdom of Nepal, the head of the Nepalese Government Delegation, Dr. Tulsi Giri, and delegation member, the Foreign Affairs Secretary of the Nepalese Foreign Ministry, Tapa, this afternoon and held a cordial conversation.

Present at the reception were Zhang Hanfu and He Ying. (Attached picture)

On October 9th, Premier Zhou Enlai received Dr. Tulsi Giri (center), Minister of Village Development of the Kingdom of Nepal and head of the Nepalese Government Delegation, and Tapa (right), delegation member and Foreign Affairs Secretary of the Nepalese Foreign Ministry. The picture shows the scene of the reception. Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Ruifeng.

### 周总理接见尼泊尔政府代表团

1959-10-10
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理接见尼泊尔政府代表团
    新华社9日讯　周恩来总理在今天下午接见了尼泊尔王国乡村发展大臣、尼泊尔王国政府代表团团长图尔西·吉里博士和团员尼泊尔王国外交部外事秘书塔帕，并进行了亲切的交谈。
    接见时，在座的有章汉夫、何英。（附图片）
