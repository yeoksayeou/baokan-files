Title: Medium-Sized Blast Furnaces Built in Fujian and Zhejiang

Author:

Date: 1959-10-22
Page: 3

News from All Directions: Construction News

    Xinhua News Agency reports: The No. 1 blast furnace of the Sanming Iron and Steel Plant, the first medium-sized iron and steel complex in Fujian Province, has been completed and produced iron. The volume of this blast furnace is 255 cubic meters, with an annual production capacity of 120,000 tons of pig iron.

    The Sanming Iron and Steel Plant is a key industrial construction project in Fujian Province this year. During its construction period, it received strong support from 21 provinces and cities, and the People's Liberation Army guarding the coastal defense front also sent troops to participate in the construction.

    Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 19th: Zhejiang Province's first medium-sized ironmaking blast furnace—the No. 3 blast furnace of the Banshan Iron and Steel Plant, with a volume of 255 cubic meters—smoothly produced its first heat of molten iron on the afternoon of the 17th.

    Wu Xian, Secretary of the Zhejiang Provincial Party Committee Secretariat, and Liu Jian, Minister of Industry and Transportation, went to the factory to congratulate the No. 3 blast furnace on producing iron, and to celebrate the victory of high speed.

    After the No. 3 blast furnace of the Banshan Iron and Steel Plant is put into production, it will create favorable conditions for the leap-forward development of the iron and steel industry in Zhejiang Province in the future, and for completing this year's iron and steel production tasks ahead of schedule and exceeding the quota. According to its design capacity, this blast furnace can produce 120,000 tons of iron per year. (Attached picture)

### 福建浙江有了中型高炉

1959-10-22
第3版()
专栏：建设喜讯四方来

    福建浙江有了中型高炉
    据新华社讯　福建省第一个中型钢铁联合企业——三明钢铁厂的一号高炉，已经建成出铁。这座高炉的容积是二百五十五立方米，能年产生铁十二万吨。
    三明钢铁厂是福建省今年工业建设的重点工程。它在建设期中，曾得到了二十一个省市的大力支援，守卫在海防前线的人民解放军也派出部队参加建设。
    新华社杭州19日电　浙江省第一座中型炼铁高炉——半山钢铁厂二百五十五立方米的三号高炉，17日下午顺利地流出了第一炉铁水。
    中共浙江省委书记处书记吴宪和工业交通工作部部长刘剑都到厂祝贺三号高炉出铁，祝贺高速度的胜利。
    半山钢铁厂三号高炉投入生产以后，为浙江省今后钢铁工业生产的飞跃发展，为提前和超额完成今年全省钢铁生产任务，创造了良好条件。这座高炉按照设计能力，一年能产生铁十二万吨。（附图片）
