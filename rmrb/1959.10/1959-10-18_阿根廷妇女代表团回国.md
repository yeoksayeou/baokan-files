Title: Argentine Women's Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 17th: The Argentine Women's Delegation of five, led by Rosa Ingaillenelia, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home after completing their visit to our country.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Shen Zijiou, member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation, and Li Lilien, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's National Committee for the Protection of Children.

### 阿根廷妇女代表团回国

1959-10-18
第3版()
专栏：

    阿根廷妇女代表团回国
    新华社17日讯　由罗莎·因加利内利亚率领的阿根廷妇女代表团一行五人，结束了在我国的参观访问后，今天早晨乘飞机离开北京回国。
    前往机场送行的，有全国妇女联合会主席团委员沈兹九和中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会副秘书长李丽莲等。
