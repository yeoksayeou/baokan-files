Title: Chairman Liu and Chairman Zhu Meet with Chairman Zawadzki
Author:
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 30th - Chairman of the People's Republic of China and Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, Liu Shaoqi, and Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, Zhu De, met this evening with Chairman of the Council of State of the Polish People's Republic and Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Aleksander Zawadzki, and the members of the Polish People's Republic Party and Government Delegation led by him: Deputy Prime Minister of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic and Chairman of the Supreme Committee of Polish United Peasants' Party, Stefan Ignar; Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, First Secretary of the Katowice Provincial Committee, Edward Gierek; Member of Parliament of the Polish People's Republic and Vice Chairman of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Polish Democratic Party, Jan Karol Wende; Secretary of the Council of State of the Polish People's Republic and Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Committee of the Polish United Peasants' Party, Julian Horodecki; Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Polish People's Republic, Franciszek Modrzewski; Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Polish People's Republic, P. Ogrodziński; Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish People's Republic Embassy in China, Stanisław Flato.
    The leaders of the two parties and states held cordial and friendly talks during the meeting. (Picture attached)
    The scene when Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, met with the head of the Polish Party and Government Delegation, Chairman of the Council of State of the Polish People's Republic, Aleksander Zawadzki (left).
  　Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dong'ao

### 刘主席朱委员长会见萨瓦茨基主席

1959-10-01
第6版()
专栏：

    刘主席朱委员长会见萨瓦茨基主席
    新华社30日讯　中华人民共和国主席、中共中央副主席刘少奇和全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长、中共中央副主席朱德，今天晚间会见了波兰人民共和国国务委员会主席、波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员亚历山大·萨瓦茨基和由他率领的波兰人民共和国党政代表团团员：波兰人民共和国部长会议副主席、波兰统一农民党最高委员会主席斯特瓦·伊格纳尔，波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记、卡托维兹省委员会第一书记爱德华·格雷克，波兰人民共和国议会议员、波兰民主党中央委员会主席团副主席杨·卡罗尔·温德，波兰人民共和国国务委员会秘书、波兰统一农民党最高委员会主席团委员尤利安·霍罗德茨基，波兰人民共和国对外贸易部副部长弗朗齐歇克·莫德日夫斯基，波兰人民共和国外交部部长助理普·奥格罗任斯基，波兰人民共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办斯坦尼斯拉夫·傅拉托。
    两国的党和国家领导人在会见中进行了亲切友好的交谈。（附图片）
