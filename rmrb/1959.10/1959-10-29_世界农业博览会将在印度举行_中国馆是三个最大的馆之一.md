Title: World Agricultural Exposition to be Held in India; Chinese Pavilion is One of the Three Largest
Author:
Date: 1959-10-29
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, October 27th: According to newspaper reports, a World Agricultural Exposition will be held in New Delhi from December 11th of this year to February 14th of next year.
    This exposition, organized by the Indian Farmers' Forum, will be inaugurated by Indian President Prasad.
    So far, in addition to India, fourteen countries, including the Soviet Union, China, and the United States, have decided to participate in this exposition. According to newspaper reports, the secretariat of the exposition announced on the 26th that the Chinese pavilion would be one of the three largest exhibition halls.
    The various states of India will also participate in the exposition and are currently stepping up the construction of exhibition halls that reflect the characteristics of each state.

### 世界农业博览会将在印度举行  中国馆是三个最大的馆之一

1959-10-29
第6版()
专栏：

    世界农业博览会将在印度举行
    中国馆是三个最大的馆之一
    新华社新德里27日电　据报纸报道：一个世界农业博览会将从今年12月11日到明年2月14日在新德里举行。
    这个由印度农民论坛举办的博览会将由印度总统普拉沙德主持开幕礼。
    到现在为止，除印度以外，还有苏联、中国和美国等十四个国家决定参加这个博览会。据报纸报道，博览会的秘书处在26日宣布，中国馆将是三个最大的展览馆之一。
    印度各邦也将参加博览会，目前正在加紧修建具有各邦特色的展览馆。
