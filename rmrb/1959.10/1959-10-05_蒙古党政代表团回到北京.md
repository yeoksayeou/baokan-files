Title: Mongolian Party and Government Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-05
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, October 4th—The Mongolian Party and Government Delegation, led by Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, returned to Beijing this evening after visiting Dalian and Tianjin.

    Today, the delegation visited the Xinli Village People's Commune in Tianjin and held a discussion with the commune's cadres for an hour and a half. Chairman Tsedenbal and members of the delegation learned in detail about the commune's organization, cadre allocation, distribution principles, and the living welfare of the members, etc. The deputy secretary of the commune's Party committee, Bian Mingdao, gave a detailed introduction to the distinguished guests.

    The distinguished guests visited the commune's high-yield rice fields and were warmly welcomed by the members who were harvesting rice. Chairman Tsedenbal and other distinguished guests personally harvested rice with sickles. The delegation also visited the commune's home for the elderly and nursery. Chairman Tsedenbal presented Mongolian-Chinese friendship badges to the nursery workers and children. Before leaving the commune, Chairman Tsedenbal presented the commune with a banner embroidered with the words "Long Live the Friendship between the Chinese and Mongolian People" and other gifts.

    The delegation flew to Tianjin from Dalian this morning. Lin Tie, First Secretary of the Hebei Provincial Party Committee, Liu Zihou, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Hebei Provincial Party Committee and Governor of Hebei Province, and Li Gengtao, Secretary of the Tianjin Municipal Party Committee and Mayor of Tianjin, hosted a banquet to entertain the distinguished guests.

    While in Dalian, the distinguished guests visited the naval units in the Lushun area, the Dalian Shipyard, the Dalian Seaport, and the local exhibition celebrating the tenth anniversary of the founding of the country.

    When the distinguished guests visited the naval units, they were given a grand reception. Ships full of flags lined up to welcome them, and Chairman Tsedenbal, accompanied by Rear Admiral Peng Lin, political commissar of the local naval unit, reviewed the naval honor guard. The distinguished guests also boarded the ships for a visit. After the visit, Chairman Tsedenbal cordially addressed the naval officers and soldiers.

    Last night, the distinguished guests visited the "Yuejin" ocean-going freighter with a deadweight of 13,400 tons, which is under construction at the Dalian Shipyard, with great interest.

    Last night, Guo Shushen, First Secretary of the Dalian Municipal Party Committee, and Hu Ming, Secretary of the Dalian Municipal Party Committee and Mayor of Dalian, also hosted a banquet to entertain the distinguished guests.

### 蒙古党政代表团回到北京

1959-10-05
第2版()
专栏：

    蒙古党政代表团回到北京
    据新华社4日讯　以蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记、蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席尤穆佳·泽登巴尔为首的蒙古人民共和国党政代表团参观访问了旅大市和天津后，已在今天傍晚回到北京。
    今天，代表团在天津参观了天津新立村人民公社，并且和公社干部进行了一个半小时的座谈。泽登巴尔主席和代表团团员详细地了解有关公社组织、干部配备、分配原则、社员的生活福利等各方面的情况，公社的党委副书记卞明道向贵宾们一一作了介绍。
    贵宾们参观了公社的丰产稻田，受到了正在收割稻谷的社员们的热烈欢迎。泽登巴尔主席和其他贵宾们亲自开镰割了稻子。代表团还参观了公社的敬老院和托儿所。泽登巴尔主席把蒙中友谊章送给了保育员和孩子们。临离开公社前，泽登巴尔主席送给公社一面绣有“中蒙两国人民友谊万岁”字样的锦旗和其他礼物。
    代表团是在今天上午自旅大市飞抵天津的。中共河北省委第一书记林铁，中共河北省委书记处书记、河北省省长刘子厚，天津市市委书记、天津市市长李耕涛设宴招待了贵宾们。
    贵宾们在旅大时参观了旅顺地区海军部队、大连造船厂、大连海港以及该地的庆祝建国十周年展览会。
    当贵宾们参观海军部队时，受到隆重的接待，挂满旗帜的舰艇列队欢迎，泽登巴尔主席在当地海军部队政委彭林海军中将的陪同下检阅了海军仪仗队；贵宾们还登上舰艇参观。参观结束后，泽登巴尔主席亲切地向海军官兵们讲了话。
    贵宾们在昨夜怀着浓厚的兴趣在大连造船厂参观了正在建造中的载重一万三千四百吨的远洋货轮“跃进”号。
    昨晚，中共旅大市委第一书记郭述申、中共旅大市委书记、旅大市市长胡明还设宴招待了贵宾。
