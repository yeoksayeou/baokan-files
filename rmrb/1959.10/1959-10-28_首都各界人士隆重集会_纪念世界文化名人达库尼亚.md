Title: People from All Walks of Life in the Capital Grandly Gather to Commemorate World Cultural Figure Euclides da Cunha
Author:
Date: 1959-10-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th: People from all walks of life in the capital grandly gathered today to commemorate the 50th anniversary of the death of the world cultural figure and Brazilian writer Euclides da Cunha. Da Cunha is one of the world cultural figures recommended for commemoration by the World Peace Council this year. The commemoration meeting was jointly sponsored by four units, namely, the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace, the Chinese People's Association of Foreign Cultural Relations, the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and the Chinese Writers Association. More than 1,300 prominent figures from the capital's cultural circles, including Guo Moruo, Chu Tunan, Mao Dun, Yang Hansheng, and Shao Quanlin, attended the commemoration meeting.

Those attending the commemoration meeting included: Enrique Fajardo, Vice President of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers; Blanca Fajardo, Vice President of the International Democratic Women's Federation and President of the Brazilian Women's Federation; Jose Geraldo Vieira, Academician of the Sao Paulo Academy of Letters of Brazil and writer; Maria de Lourdes Teixeira, Brazilian writer; James Gareth Endicott, member of the Presidium of the World Peace Coucil, president of the Canadian Peace Congress, and his wife; cultural delegations from Brazil, Cuba, Chile, Argentina, Uruguay, Peru, and other countries; and cultural representatives from Haiti, Paraguay, Nicaragua, and Colombia. Also attending the commemoration meeting were diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China.

The commemoration meeting was presided over by Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. Zhou Erfu, member of the Council of the Chinese Writers Association and Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, delivered a report on "The Genius of Latin America" at the meeting.

Jose Geraldo Vieira, Academician of the Sao Paulo Academy of Letters of Brazil, spoke at the meeting. He praised the achievements of the Chinese people in ten years of construction and the Great Leap Forward. He thanked Chinese cultural workers for holding a grand commemoration meeting for the 50th anniversary of the death of Euclides da Cunha in Beijing, the capital of more than 600 million people. He said that after returning to his country, he would tell the Brazilian people about all the good things he saw in China and convey the Chinese people's warm friendship and deep concern for the Brazilian people.

James Gareth Endicott, member of the Presidium of the World Peace Council and president of the National Council of the Canadian Peace Congress, also spoke at the meeting.

### 首都各界人士隆重集会  纪念世界文化名人达库尼亚

1959-10-28
第5版()
专栏：

    首都各界人士隆重集会
    纪念世界文化名人达库尼亚
    据新华社27日讯　首都各界人士今天隆重集会纪念世界文化名人、巴西作家欧克利德斯·达库尼亚逝世五十周年。达库尼亚是今年世界和平理事会建议纪念的世界文化名人之一。纪念会是由中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国人民对外文化协会、中国文学艺术界联合会、中国作家协会等四个单位联合主办的。首都文化界著名人士郭沫若、楚图南、茅盾、阳翰笙、邵荃麟等一千三百余人出席了纪念会。
    出席纪念会的有：国际民主法律工作者协会副主席恩里克·费雅利奥，国际民主妇女联合会副主席、巴西妇女联合会主席布朗卡·费雅利奥，巴西圣保罗文学院院士、作家约赛·谢劳多·维埃伊拉，巴西作家玛丽亚·德洛德斯·特克西拉，世界和平理事会主席团委员、加拿大和平大会主席文幼章和他的夫人，巴西、古巴、智利、阿根廷、乌拉圭、秘鲁等国文化代表团和海地、巴拉圭、尼加拉瓜、哥伦比亚等国文化代表。出席纪念会的还有各国驻华外交官员。
    纪念会由中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南主持。中国作家协会理事、中国人民对外文化协会秘书长周而复在会上作了“拉丁美洲的天才”的报告。
    巴西圣保罗文学院院士约赛·谢劳多·维埃伊拉在会上讲了话。他盛赞中国人民十年建设和大跃进的成就。他感谢中国文化工作者在六亿多人民的首都——北京，为欧克利德斯·达库尼亚逝世五十周年举行隆重的纪念会。他说，他回国后，将向巴西人民叙述他在中国所看到的美好的一切，并将转达中国人民对巴西人民的热情的友谊和深切的关怀。
    世界和平理事会主席团委员、加拿大和平大会全国理事会主席文幼章也在会上讲了话。
