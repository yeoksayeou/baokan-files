Title: President Sukarno Invites Khrushchev and Mikoyan to Visit

Author:
Date: 1959-10-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, October 13th: According to TASS, Indonesian President Sukarno has invited Khrushchev and Mikoyan to visit Indonesia. President Sukarno extended this invitation during a meeting with the delegation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union at the State Palace in Jakarta.

President Sukarno said to the Soviet guests, "I have been to the Soviet Union twice, and both times I received particularly friendly treatment from the Soviet people. As you can see, we also warmly welcome guests. We also very much hope that the leaders of the Soviet Union can visit our country. We would be very happy to welcome Khrushchev and Mikoyan to our land, and I have invited them to come to our country."

The head of the Supreme Soviet delegation, Mukhitdinov, Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the Nationalities Council of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, expressed his gratitude to President Sukarno for inviting Khrushchev and Mikoyan to visit the Republic of Indonesia. He said that he would certainly convey the President's kindness to the two leaders of the Soviet Union.

### 苏加诺总统邀请赫鲁晓夫米高扬访问

1959-10-16
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏加诺总统
    邀请赫鲁晓夫米高扬访问
    新华社13日讯　据塔斯社雅加达13日讯：印度尼西亚总统苏加诺已经邀请赫鲁晓夫和米高扬访问印度尼西亚。苏加诺总统是在雅加达国家宫会见苏联最高苏维埃代表团时提出这一邀请的。
    苏加诺总统对苏联客人说，“我曾经两次到过苏联，两次都受到苏联人民的特别友好的款待。你们看到了，我们也是热烈欢迎客人的。我们还非常希望苏联的领导人能访问我们的国家。我们将非常高兴在我们的土地上欢迎赫鲁晓夫和米高扬，我已经邀请他们到我国来。”
    苏联最高苏维埃代表团团长、苏联最高苏维埃民族院外交委员会主席穆希金诺夫对苏加诺总统邀请赫鲁晓夫和米高扬访问印度尼西亚共和国一事表示感谢。他说，他一定向苏联的这两位领导人转达总统的厚意。
