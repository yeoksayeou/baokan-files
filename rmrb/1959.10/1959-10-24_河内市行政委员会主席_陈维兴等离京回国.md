Title: Chairman of the Hanoi Municipal Administrative Committee, Tran Duy Hung, and Others Depart Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1959-10-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd: Tran Duy Hung, Chairman of the Hanoi Municipal Administrative Committee of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Nguyen Minh Can, Standing Committee Member of the Hanoi Municipal Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party and Director of the "Capital Hanoi Newspaper," who were invited to visit Beijing, concluded their visit to Beijing and returned home by plane this morning.

### 河内市行政委员会主席  陈维兴等离京回国

1959-10-24
第6版()
专栏：

    河内市行政委员会主席
    陈维兴等离京回国
    据新华社23日讯　应邀来北京市访问的越南民主共和国河内市行政委员会主席陈维兴和越南劳动党河内市委常务委员、“首都河内报”主任阮明勤，结束了在北京市的参观访问，已于今晨乘飞机回国。
