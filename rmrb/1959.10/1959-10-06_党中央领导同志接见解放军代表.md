Title: Central Party Leaders Receive Representatives of the People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1959-10-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th.
    Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairmen Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Lin Biao, Political Bureau member Peng Zhen, Marshals of the People's Republic of China Liu Bocheng, He Long, Chen Yi, Luo Ronghuan, Ye Jianying and other comrades received this morning representatives from various units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army who were attending the National Day Tenth Anniversary celebrations, representatives of the troops who participated in the National Day Tenth Anniversary parade, the Chinese People's Liberation Army Art Troupe who gave performances as a gift for the National Day Tenth Anniversary, and the Chinese People's Liberation Army Sports Delegation who participated in the National Games. When the generals, officers, soldiers, actors and athletes of the Chinese People's Liberation Army met Chairman Mao, they burst into prolonged cheers and applause. Chairman Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Lin Biao and other comrades went around the field for a week to meet with everyone. The heads of the various departments, services and arms of the Chinese People's Liberation Army headquarters were present at the reception.
    Prior to the reception, Marshals Lin Biao, Liu Bocheng, He Long, Chen Yi, Luo Ronghuan, Ye Jianying and the heads of the General Staff Department and the General Political Department held cordial talks with some of the representatives separately. (Attached picture)
    Chairman Mao and others receive representatives of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The picture shows a group photo at the reception. Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Qingrui

### 党中央领导同志接见解放军代表

1959-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

    党中央领导同志接见解放军代表
    新华社5日讯　中共中央主席毛泽东，副主席刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、林彪，政治局委员彭真，中华人民共和国元帅刘伯承、贺龙、陈毅、罗荣桓、叶剑英等，今天上午接见了中国人民解放军各部队参加建国十周年大典观礼代表团、参加建国十周年大典接受检阅的部队代表、向建国十周年献礼演出的中国人民解放军文艺代表队和参加全国运动会的中国人民解放军体育代表团。中国人民解放军的将军、军官、士兵、演员和运动员们在会见毛主席的时候，发出了长久的欢呼声和掌声，毛泽东主席和刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、林彪等同志绕场一周，同大家会见。接见的时候，中国人民解放军总部各部、各军种、兵种的负责人都在场。
    接见以前，林彪元帅、刘伯承元帅、贺龙元帅、陈毅元帅、罗荣桓元帅、叶剑英元帅和总参谋部、总政治部的负责人等，分别同一部分代表进行了亲切的交谈。（附图片）
