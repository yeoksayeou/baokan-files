Title: Prime Minister Pham Van Dong Arrives in Beijing; Vice Premier Chen Yi and Others Welcome Him at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1959-10-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 16th: Pham Van Dong, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party and Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, leading the Vietnamese Party and Government Delegation, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon on their way back home, after attending the celebrations of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included: Chen Yi, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Premier of the State Council; Wang Shusheng, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Minister of National Defense; Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Wang Gengling, member of the State Planning Commission, and others. Tran Tu Binh, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, and Wang Guender, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, also welcomed them at the airport.

### 范文同总理到京  陈毅副总理等到机场欢迎

1959-10-17
第5版()
专栏：

　　范文同总理到京
    陈毅副总理等到机场欢迎
    新华社16日讯　越南劳动党中央政治局委员、越南民主共和国总理范文同率领的越南党政代表团，在参加了德意志民主共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼后，在回国途中，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场迎接的有：中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅，中共中央委员、国防部副部长王树声大将、外交部副部长姬鹏飞、国家计划委员会委员王耕令等。越南民主共和国驻华大使陈子平、德意志民主共和国驻华大使汪戴尔也到机场迎接。
