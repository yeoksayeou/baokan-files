Title: Argentine Congressman Bayol and Others Return Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-30
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, October 29th: Augusto Bayol, Chairman of the Argentine Senate's Industrial and Commercial Committee, Bé. Fe. Deferrari, member of the House of Representatives' Foreign Affairs Committee, and A. Te. Gómez, a member of the Mendoza Provincial Assembly, left Beijing by plane today to return home.
    Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Council member Shen Jian, and Secretary-General Wu Maosun saw them off at the airport.

### 阿根廷议员巴约耳等回国

1959-10-30
第5版()
专栏：

    阿根廷议员巴约耳等回国
    新华社29日讯　阿根廷参议院工商委员会主席奥古斯托·巴约耳和众议院外交委员会委员贝·费·德费拉里、门多萨省议员阿·特·戈麦斯等三人今天乘飞机离开北京返国。
    中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之、理事申健、秘书长吴茂荪等到机场送行。
