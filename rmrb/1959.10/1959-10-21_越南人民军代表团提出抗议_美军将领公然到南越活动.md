Title: Delegation of the Vietnam People's Army Lodges Protest: U.S. Military Generals Openly Conduct Activities in South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1959-10-21
Page: 6

    Hanoi, October 16th (Xinhua News Agency) - Newspapers here reported today that the Liaison Delegation of the General Command of the Vietnam People's Army wrote to the International Commission for Vietnam on October 12th, protesting that U.S. military general Felt led the military delegation of the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization to illegally enter South Vietnam, demanding that the International Commission for Vietnam draw appropriate conclusions on the above-mentioned incident and take measures to prevent similar incidents from recurring in South Vietnam.

### 越南人民军代表团提出抗议  美军将领公然到南越活动

1959-10-21
第6版()
专栏：

    越南人民军代表团提出抗议
    美军将领公然到南越活动
    据新华社河内16日电　此间报纸今天报道：越南人民军总司令部联络代表团10月12日写信给越南国际委员会，抗议美军将领费耳特率领东南亚条约组织军事代表团非法进入南越，要求越南国际委员会就上述事件作出应有的结论，并且采取措施以制止类似的事件在南越重演。
