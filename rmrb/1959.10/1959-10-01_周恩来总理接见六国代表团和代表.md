Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Delegations and Representatives from Six Countries
Author:
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Premier Zhou Enlai received the delegation from the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, headed by Ben Youssef Ben Khedda, Minister of Social Welfare of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria; the government delegation of the Republic of Iraq, headed by Brigadier General Ahmed Mohammed Yahya, Minister of Interior of the Republic of Iraq; U Chit Thoung, Minister of Culture of the Government of the Union of Burma and representative of the Government of the Union of Burma; and the government delegation of the Kingdom of Nepal, headed by Tulsi Giri, Minister of Village Development of the Kingdom of Nepal, on the 29th.

　　Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, was present at the reception.

　　Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Premier Zhou Enlai received Barry Diawandou, Minister of Education of the Republic of Guinea and representative of the Government of the Republic of Guinea, who came to our country to attend the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the country, this evening.

　　Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was present at the reception.

　　Xinhua News Agency, 30th. This afternoon, Premier Zhou Enlai received A.I.M. Gubara, Registrar of the University of Khartoum of the Republic of Sudan, head of the cultural delegation representing the government of the Republic of Sudan to China to attend the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the country, and members of the delegation. Vice Premier He Long, Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, and Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, were present at the reception.

### 周恩来总理接见六国代表团和代表

1959-10-01
第6版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见六国代表团和代表
    新华社30日讯　周恩来总理在29日接见了以阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府社会福利部长本·优素福·本·赫达为首的阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团，以伊拉克共和国内政部部长艾哈迈德·穆罕默德·叶海亚准将为首的伊拉克共和国政府代表团，缅甸联邦政府文化部部长、缅甸联邦政府代表吴漆东，以尼泊尔王国乡村发展大臣图尔西·吉里为首的尼泊尔王国政府代表团。
    接见时在座的，有外交部副部长罗贵波。
    新华社30日讯　周恩来总理今天晚间接见了前来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的几内亚共和国政府代表、几内亚教育部部长巴里·迪亚万杜。
    接见时在座的有我国外交部礼宾司副司长马振武。
    新华社30日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了代表苏丹共和国政府来中国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的文化代表团团长、苏丹共和国喀土穆大学注册长阿·依·墨·古巴拉和代表团团员。接见时在座的有副总理贺龙，对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长罗贵波。
