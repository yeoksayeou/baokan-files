Title: Joint Notice from the Ministry of Metallurgical Industry and the Heavy Industry Trade Union: Promoting the Experience of Technical Performance Competitions
Author:
Date: 1959-10-12
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 11th: The Ministry of Metallurgical Industry and the National Committee of the Heavy Industry Trade Union jointly issued a notice on the 10th, calling on all affiliated factories and mining enterprises to actively promote the experience of technical performance competitions created by the workers of the Anshan Iron and Steel Company's No. 3 Steelmaking Plant.

The notice stated: Technical performance competitions are aimed at key production aspects. Through on-site observation and performance, they are competitions of enthusiasm and dedication, competitions of operational skills, competitions of learning and mastering technology, improving technology, enhancing technology, and collaborating and helping each other, to achieve the goal of comprehensively completing the national plan. Through this competition, Anshan Iron and Steel Company's No. 3 Steelmaking Plant truly formed a surge of enthusiasm where everyone performs, everything is performed, every moment is performed, and every shift performs. They spoke, did, taught, learned, experimented, appraised, summarized, and promoted simultaneously, achieving significant results in production.

The notice stated: We believe that the experience of technical performance competitions is good. This is a good method for industrial enterprises to implement the mass line, raise mass awareness, rapidly improve production technology, strengthen the technical force, and improve enterprise management. The notice called on all affiliated factories and mining enterprises to promote this experience under the leadership of the Party Committee, in conjunction with their own situations, and to combine the promotion of technical performance competitions with "opponent races," "one-dragon" collaboration races, model worker races, etc., to give play to the role of various forms of competition, consolidate and continuously develop the new upsurge of the increase production and practice economy movement that has already formed on the metallurgical industry front, and ensure the early and over-fulfillment of this year's increase production and practice economy plan.

### 冶金部和重工业工会联合通知  推广技术表演竞赛的经验

1959-10-12
第2版()
专栏：

　　冶金部和重工业工会联合通知
    推广技术表演竞赛的经验
    新华社11日讯　冶金工业部和重工业工会全国委员会10日联合发出通知，号召所属各厂矿企业积极推广鞍钢第三炼钢厂职工创造的技术表演竞赛的经验。
    通知说：技术表演竞赛是针对生产关键，通过现场观摩表演等形式，比赛干劲和钻劲，比赛操作技术，比赛学习和掌握技术、改进技术、提高技术、协作互助等，以达到全面完成国家计划的目的。鞍钢第三炼钢厂通过这一竞赛，全厂各个工序和各个岗位真正形成了人人表演、事事表演、时时表演、班班表演的热潮。他们边说、边做、边教、边学、边试验、边鉴定、边总结、边推广，在生产上取得了显著的成绩。
    通知说：我们认为技术表演竞赛的经验是好的。这是工业企业中，贯彻群众路线，提高群众觉悟，迅速提高生产技术水平，壮大技术队伍，改进企业管理的一个好方法。通知号召所属各厂矿企业在党委领导下，结合自己的情况推广这个经验，并且把推行技术表演竞赛，同对手赛、“一条龙”协作赛、标兵赛等结合起来，发挥各种竞赛形式的作用，把目前冶金工业战线上已经形成的增产节约运动的新高潮，巩固地持续地发展下去，保证提前和超额完成今年的增产节约计划。
