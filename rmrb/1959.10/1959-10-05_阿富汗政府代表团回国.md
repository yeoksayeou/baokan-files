Title: Afghan Government Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-05
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 4th: The Afghan Royal Government Delegation, led by the Afghan Minister of Commerce and head of the delegation, Sherzad, who were invited to attend the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, left Guangzhou by train this morning and returned home via Hong Kong.

Those who went to the station to see them off included Wei Jinfei, Deputy Governor of Guangdong Province, Zhang Ruiquan, Deputy Mayor of Guangzhou, Yang Yi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Provincial People's Committee, Ruan Keming, Deputy Director of the Foreign Trade Bureau, and heads of various democratic parties and people's organizations.

### 阿富汗政府代表团回国

1959-10-05
第2版()
专栏：

    阿富汗政府代表团回国
    新华社广州4日电　应邀参加中华人民共和国建国十周年庆祝典礼的阿富汗王国政府代表团，在阿富汗商业大臣、代表团团长舍尔扎德率领下，于今晨乘火车离广州，取道香港回国。
    前往车站欢送的有，广东省副省长魏今非、广州市副市长张瑞权、省人民委员会副秘书长杨毅、外贸局副局长阮克明以及各民主党派、人民团体的负责人等。
