Title: President Moumié of the Union of the Peoples of Cameroon Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 24th: Félix-Roland Moumié, President of the Union of the Peoples of Cameroon, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning for a visit to our country at the invitation of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity.

Those who welcomed him at the airport included Bao Erhan, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Zhu Ziqi and Yang Shuo, members of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity.

### 喀麦隆人民联盟主席穆米埃到京

1959-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

    喀麦隆人民联盟主席穆米埃到京
    新华社24日讯　应中国亚非团结委员会邀请来我国访问的喀麦隆人民联盟主席费利克斯·罗兰·穆米埃，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有中国亚非团结委员会副主席包尔汉，中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之以及中国亚非团结委员会委员朱子奇、杨朔等。
