Title: Representatives from All Sectors of Kaesong and Citizens Lay Wreaths at the Cemetery of Martyrs of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers

Author:
Date: 1959-10-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 25th: According to the Korean Central News Agency, today—the ninth anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers' participation in the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea—representatives from the Kaesong City Party and government organs, social organizations, and the Korean and Chinese sides of the Military Armistice Commission, along with more than 5,000 citizens, laid wreaths at the cemetery of martyrs of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers in Kaesong.

### 开城市各界代表和市民  到朝中人民部队烈士陵园献花圈

1959-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    开城市各界代表和市民
    到朝中人民部队烈士陵园献花圈
    新华社平壤25日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社报道，今天——中国人民志愿军抗美援朝参战九周年，开城市党政机关、社会团体和军事停战委员会朝中方面的代表以及五千多市民一起到开城市朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军烈士陵园内献了花圈。
