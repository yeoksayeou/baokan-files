Title: Bolivian Communist Party Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to visit our country and participate in the celebrations of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the head of the Bolivian Communist Party delegation, member of the Central Committee of the Bolivian Communist Party, Jesús Lara, and delegation member, member of the Central Committee of the Bolivian Communist Party, Gerson Justiniano Gitlitz, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

Welcoming them at the airport were Li Xiannian, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, and An Ziwen and Wu Xiuquan, members of the Central Committee. (Attached picture)

Li Xiannian (right), member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, welcomes Jesús Lara, head of the Bolivian Communist Party delegation and member of the Central Committee of the Bolivian Communist Party, at the airport.
Xinhua News Agency Reporter Yang Zhenhe

### 玻利维亚共产党代表团到京

1959-10-01
第6版()
专栏：

    玻利维亚共产党代表团到京
    新华社30日讯　应中国共产党中央委员会邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的玻利维亚共产党代表团团长、玻利维亚共产党中央委员会委员赫苏斯·拉腊，代表团团员、玻利维亚共产党中央委员赫尔森·胡斯蒂尼亚诺·吉特拉斯，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    在机场欢迎的，有中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记李先念，中央委员安子文和伍修权。（附图片）
