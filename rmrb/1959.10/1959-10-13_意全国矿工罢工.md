Title: Italian National Miners' Strike
Author:
Date: 1959-10-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rome, 12th: Italian miners across the country began a seventy-two hour strike today to protest the employers' refusal to sign a collective contract on wage increases.

Members of trade unions of various political persuasions participated in the strike. The strike affected all coal, iron, and mercury mines in Italy.

### 意全国矿工罢工

1959-10-13
第6版()
专栏：

    意全国矿工罢工
    新华社12日讯　罗马消息：意大利全国各地的矿工今天开始举行为时七十二小时的罢工，抗议资方拒绝签订关于增加工资的集体合同。
    参加这次罢工的有各种政治倾向的工会的会员。这次罢工波及到意大利所有煤矿、铁矿和汞矿。
