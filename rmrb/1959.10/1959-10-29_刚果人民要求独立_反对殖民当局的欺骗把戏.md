Title: Congolese People Demand Independence, Oppose Colonial Authorities' Deceptive Tricks
Author:
Date: 1959-10-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: According to reports, the Belgian Minister for Congolese and Ruanda-Urundi Affairs, De Schrijver, announced on the 16th a so-called plan for a phased "independence" for the Congo. According to De Schrijver's plan, Belgium would only allow the Congo to have a government of "internal autonomy" in the next four years, and the head of this government must be a Governor-General appointed by the Belgian King. Even after four years, the Congo would not be able to achieve true independence through its own choices. This deceptive plan has been opposed by the vast majority of the Congolese people.

The Congolese nationalist parties ABAKO and PSA issued a joint statement on the 23rd, categorically rejecting the deceptive plan on the recent promise of "independence" for the Congo proposed by the Belgian government.

The statement said, "The De Schrijver plan proposes a false independence. The system proposed in the plan, and the techniques used to deprive direct universal suffrage of any democratic character, are not found anywhere in Africa."

The two parties' statement said that independence should be "the right of a country's people to manage themselves, the right to choose their own system according to their own customs, the right to formulate just laws for equality for all, and at the same time the right to express the will of the people."

Patrice Lumumba, chairman of the main Congolese party, the MNC, said in Léopoldville on the 21st that the MNC demanded immediate independence for the Congo and could not agree to the Belgian government's plan.

ABAKO Chairman Kasavubu also pointed out in an interview with Agence France-Presse that what the Congo needs is true sovereignty.

He reiterated that ABAKO would not change its earlier decision to boycott the "elections" arranged by the Belgian authorities, scheduled for December, because these "elections" would only hand over power to the Belgian colonial authorities.

### 刚果人民要求独立  反对殖民当局的欺骗把戏

1959-10-29
第6版()
专栏：

    刚果人民要求独立
    反对殖民当局的欺骗把戏
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：比利时刚果和卢安达—乌隆迪事务大臣德舒里弗16日宣布了所谓让刚果分阶段“独立”的计划。按照德舒里弗的计划，比利时在今后四年中只让刚果有一个“内部自治”的政府，而且这个政府的首脑必须由比利时国王指派的总督担任。四年以后，刚果也不可能通过自己的选择取得真正的独立。这个欺骗性计划，遭到了刚果广大人民的反对。
    刚果民族主义政党阿巴科党和非洲团结党23日发表联合声明，断然拒绝比利时政府最近提出的关于允诺刚果“独立”的欺骗性计划。
    声明说，“德舒里弗计划提出的是假的独立。计划中所提出的体制，以及用来使直接普选失去任何民主性质的那些手法，是非洲任何地方所没有的”。
    两党的声明说，独立应当“是一国人民管理自己的权利，依据自己的风俗习惯选择自己的制度的权利，制订人人平等的公正法律的权利，同时是表达人民的意志的权利”。
    刚果主要政党民族运动党的主席卢蒙巴21日在利奥波德维尔说，刚果民族运动党要求让刚果立即独立，不能同意比利时政府的计划。
    阿巴科党主席卡萨武布在接见法新社记者的一次谈话中也指出，刚果所需要的是真正的主权。
    他再一次表示，阿巴科党不改变它早先的决定，将抵制比利时当局布置的预定在12月举行的“选举”，因为这种“选举”只会将权力交给比利时殖民当局。
