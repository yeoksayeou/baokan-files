Title: Telegram of Greetings from Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko
Author:
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 8

To Comrade Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China,

Dear Comrade Chen Yi,

Please allow me to extend my heartfelt congratulations to you on the occasion of the Chinese people's National Day – the tenth anniversary of the People's Republic of China.

Under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, the great Chinese people have achieved historical victories in socialist construction. Together with all socialist countries, they steadfastly pursue a foreign policy of peace. I sincerely wish you new achievements in your fruitful work for the happiness of the Chinese people, for the further strengthening of the unity of the world socialist camp, and for world peace and the security of all peoples.

May the unbreakable Soviet-Chinese friendship last forever!

Soviet Foreign Minister A. Gromyko
Moscow, September 29, 1959

### 苏联外交部长葛罗米柯的贺电

1959-10-01
第8版()
专栏：

    苏联外交部长葛罗米柯的贺电
    中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅同志
    亲爱的陈毅同志，
    值此中国人民国庆日——中华人民共和国十周年之际，请允许我向你表示衷心的祝贺。
    伟大的中国人民在中国共产党的领导下在社会主义的建设中取得了历史性的胜利，他们同所有的社会主义国家一起坚定不渝地奉行和平的外交政策。真诚地祝你在为中国人民的幸福、为进一步加强世界社会主义阵营的团结、为世界和平和各国人民的安全而进行的富有成效的工作中取得新的成就。
    愿牢不可破的苏中友谊万古长青！
                            苏联外交部长安·葛罗米柯
                                1959年9月29日于莫斯科
