Title: Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Council Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th — The Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Council Delegation, headed by Youssef el-Sebai, and including members Mazmout Diop and technical secretary Moussi Saad Eddine, who were invited to China to attend the 10th anniversary celebration of the founding of the People's Republic of China, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.

### 亚非人民团结理事会代表团回国

1959-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    亚非人民团结理事会代表团回国
    新华社6日讯　应邀来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的亚非人民团结理事会代表团团长优素夫·西巴伊，团员马兹穆特·迪奥普和技术秘书穆西·萨得丁，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
