Title: Premier Zhou Enlai's "Great Decade"
Author:
Date: 1959-10-24
Page: 3

    "Under the conditions of centralized leadership, overall planning, division of labor, and coordination, both central and local industries should be developed simultaneously, large and small and medium-sized industries should be developed simultaneously, and modern and indigenous methods of production should be developed simultaneously. Implementing such a 'walking on two legs' policy is not only to maintain the necessary balance between various economic sectors, but also, and more importantly, to fully mobilize the positive factors of all parties, open up the broadest possible front, enable the construction teams of the broadest masses of people to deploy, and enable their strength to be used most fully in the cause of socialist construction, thereby ensuring the realization of the requirements of more, faster, better, and more economical results. ... Under China's current conditions, while developing large-scale, modern-method production industries, paying attention to the development of small and medium-sized, indigenous or semi-indigenous industries is not a waste of manpower, but a rational utilization of manpower; it is not delaying the speed of industrialization, but greatly accelerating the speed of industrialization."
  　——Excerpt from Premier Zhou Enlai's "Great Decade"

### 摘自周恩来总理“伟大的十年”

1959-10-24
第3版()
专栏：

    “在集中领导、全面规划、分工协作的条件下，中央工业和地方工业同时并举，大型工业和中小型工业同时并举，洋法生产和土法生产同时并举。实行这样的‘两条腿走路’方针，不但是为了保持各个经济部门间的必要的平衡，而且首先是为了最充分地动员各个方面的积极因素，开辟最广大的阵地，使最广大的人民群众的建设队伍能够展开，使他们的力量能够最充分地使用到社会主义建设的事业中来，从而使多快好省要求的实现得到保证。……在我国目前条件下，在发展大型的、洋法生产的工业的同时，注意发展中小型的、土法的或者半土半洋的工业，并不是浪费了人力，而正是合理地利用了人力；并不是延缓了工业化的速度，而正是大大加速了工业化的速度。”
  　——摘自周恩来总理“伟大的十年”
