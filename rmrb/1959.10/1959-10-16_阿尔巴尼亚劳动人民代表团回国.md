Title: Albanian Labor People's Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-16
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, 15th. The Albanian Labor People's Delegation, led by Koço Tashko, Chairman of the Central Inspection Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, consisting of twelve members, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home after concluding their visit to our country. Those who saw them off at the airport included Jiang Nanxiang, Zhang Xiuzhu, Qu Tangliang, Liu Xiyuan, Guo Mingqiu, and Lin Shixiao, heads of various inviting organizations.
    Also seeing them off at the airport was the Albanian Ambassador to China, Mihal Prifti, and others.
    Delegation members Mihal Lista and Ferit Xhunga will be visiting North Korea in the coming days. Another member, Vasil Dhallani, remained in Shanghai due to business and will return home soon.

### 阿尔巴尼亚劳动人民代表团回国

1959-10-16
第5版()
专栏：

　　阿尔巴尼亚劳动人民代表团回国
    新华社15日讯　由阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央检查委员会主席科乔·塔什科率领的阿尔巴尼亚劳动人民代表团一行十二人，结束在我国的参观访问后，在今天早晨乘飞机离开北京回国。到机场送行的，有各邀请团体的负责人蒋南翔、张修竹、区棠亮、刘西元、郭明秋和林士笑等人。
    到机场送行的还有阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使米哈尔·帕里夫蒂等。
    代表团团员米哈尔·里斯塔、菲里特·江斯卡将在近日去朝鲜访问。另一位团员瓦西里·达拉尼，因事留在上海，将在最近回国。
