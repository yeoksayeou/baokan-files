Title: Ambassador Li Yong-ho and his wife hold film reception to commemorate the 9th anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers entering Korea and the 1st anniversary of their withdrawal from Korea
Author:
Date: 1959-10-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th. Korean Ambassador to China, Li Yong-ho, and his wife held a film reception this evening to commemorate the 9th anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers entering Korea and the 1st anniversary of their withdrawal from Korea. At the reception, the Korean narrative poem color film "Our Glorious Motherland" was screened.

General Tan Zheng, Deputy Minister of National Defense and Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, along with Generals Wang Shusheng, Yang Chengwu, and Gan Siqi, and other senior generals and officers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, were invited to attend the reception.

Former Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, General Yang Yong, was also invited to attend the reception this evening. Also invited to attend the reception this evening were Bao Erhan, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Hu Yuzhi, Vice Minister of Culture; Li Dequan, President of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association, and Vice President Lao She; Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; and Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 纪念志愿军入朝九周年和撤出朝鲜一周年  李永镐大使夫妇举行电影招待会

1959-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    纪念志愿军入朝九周年和撤出朝鲜一周年
    李永镐大使夫妇举行电影招待会
    新华社25日讯　朝鲜驻华大使李永镐及夫人今晚举行电影招待会，纪念中国人民志愿军入朝参战九周年和撤出朝鲜一周年。会上，放映了朝鲜叙事诗彩色片“我们光荣的祖国”。
    国防部副部长、中国人民解放军总政治部主任谭政大将和王树声大将、杨成武上将、甘泗淇上将等中国人民解放军高级将领和军官，应邀出席了招待会。
    前中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇上将等也应邀出席了今晚的招待会。应邀出席今晚招待会的还有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席包尔汉，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，文化部副部长胡愈之，中朝友好协会会长李德全、副会长老舍，对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若，对外文化协会会长楚图南等。
