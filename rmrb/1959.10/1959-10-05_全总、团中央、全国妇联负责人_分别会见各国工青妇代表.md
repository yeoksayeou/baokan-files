Title: Leaders of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and the All-China Women's Federation Meet with Representatives of Trade Unions, Youth Leagues, and Women's Organizations from Various Countries
Author: 
Date: 1959-10-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The responsible persons of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, and the All-China Women's Federation, on October 2nd, met separately with trade union workers, model workers, youth league workers, and women representatives from various socialist countries and other countries who came to China to participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Hu Yaobang, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and Li Dequan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, extended warm welcomes to the guests who had come from afar during the meetings. The guests and hosts had warm and friendly conversations for several hours, congratulating each other on their common friendship, the achievements of socialist construction, and the development of the cause of world peace.

Those who participated in the meetings separately included trade union workers, model workers, youth league workers, and women representatives from the delegation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the labor people's delegations from Albania, Bulgaria, Hungary, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the German Democratic Republic, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Mongolia, Poland, Romania, and Czechoslovakia.

Trade union workers from Argentina, Japan, Nigeria, and Uruguay, as well as Anita Vittorio, the wife of former World Federation of Trade Unions Chairman Vittorio, also participated in the meeting held by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

During the meetings, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and the All-China Women's Federation respectively held receptions, banquets, and tea parties to entertain the guests. The responsible persons of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and the All-China Women's Federation were all present.

During the meetings, youth league workers and women representatives from various socialist countries also presented gifts to the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League and the All-China Women's Federation, respectively.

### 全总、团中央、全国妇联负责人  分别会见各国工青妇代表

1959-10-05
第2版()
专栏：

    全总、团中央、全国妇联负责人
    分别会见各国工青妇代表
    新华社4日讯　中华全国总工会、中国共产主义青年团中央委员会和中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会的负责人，在10月2日分别会见了来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的各社会主义国家和其他国家的工会工作者、劳动模范、青年团工作者和妇女代表。
    全国总工会主席刘宁一，共青团中央第一书记胡耀邦，全国妇联副主席李德全，在会见时分别对远道而来的客人表示热烈欢迎。宾主们一起进行了几个小时热情友好的交谈，相互为共同的友谊、为社会主义建设事业的成就和为世界和平事业的发展祝贺。
    分别参加会见的，有苏中友好协会代表团和阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、匈牙利、越南民主共和国、德意志民主共和国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、蒙古、波兰、罗马尼亚、捷克斯洛伐克劳动人民代表团中的工会工作者、劳动模范、青年团工作者和妇女代表。
    阿根廷、日本、尼日利亚、乌拉圭的工会工作者和世界工联前主席维多里奥的夫人阿尼塔·维多里奥也参加了全国总工会举行的会见。
    会见时，全国总工会、共青团中央和全国妇联分别举行了酒会、宴会、茶会招待客人。全国总工会、共青团中央和全国妇联的负责人都在座。
    各社会主义国家的青年团工作者和妇女代表在会见时还分别向共青团中央、全国妇联赠送了礼物。
