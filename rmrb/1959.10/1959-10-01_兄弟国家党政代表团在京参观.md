Title: Party and Government Delegations from Fraternal Countries Visit Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, 30th September – Party and government delegations from eleven fraternal countries, namely the Soviet Union, Vietnam, Czechoslovakia, North Korea, Mongolia, Poland, Hungary, Bulgaria, Albania, the German Democratic Republic, and Romania, continued their visits in Beijing yesterday and today.

    Yesterday morning, all members of the Soviet Party and Government Delegation, some members of the Vietnamese Party and Government Delegation, and the head and all members of the Czechoslovak Party and Government Delegation visited scenic spots and historical sites and large buildings in Beijing. They toured the Forbidden City, the Temple of Heaven Park, the Dingling Museum, the Ming Tombs Reservoir, and the Summer Palace. The National Agricultural Exhibition, which introduces the movement of people's communes in our country and the development of agricultural production, received the Party and Government Delegations of six fraternal countries: North Korea, Mongolia, Poland, Bulgaria, Albania, and the German Democratic Republic, yesterday and today. Some members of these six delegations also visited some factories and large buildings in Beijing.

    The head and all members of the Hungarian Party and Government Delegation visited the National Industrial and Transportation Exhibition yesterday, viewing various exhibits showing the continuous leaps forward in China's industrial and transportation sectors. All members of the Romanian Party and Government Delegation toured the Beijing Railway Station and the Beijing Department Store yesterday.

### 兄弟国家党政代表团在京参观

1959-10-01
第7版()
专栏：

    兄弟国家党政代表团在京参观
    新华社30日讯　苏联、越南、捷克斯洛伐克、朝鲜、蒙古、波兰、匈牙利、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚、德意志民主共和国、罗马尼亚等十一个兄弟国家党政代表团昨天和今天继续在北京进行参观访问。
    昨天上午，苏联党政代表团全体团员、越南党政代表团部分团员和捷克斯洛伐克党政代表团团长和全体团员曾分别参观了北京的名胜古迹和大型建筑物。他们游览过的地方有故宫、天坛公园、定陵博物馆、十三陵水库，以及颐和园等处。介绍我国人民公社运动和农业生产发展情况的全国农业展览会，昨天和今天先后接待了朝鲜、蒙古、波兰、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚、德意志民主共和国六个兄弟国家的党政代表团。这六个代表团的部分团员还分别参观了北京的一些工厂和大型建筑物。
    匈牙利党政代表团团长和全体团员昨天到全国工业交通展览会，观看了表示我国工业和交通事业不断跃进的各种展品。罗马尼亚党政代表团全体团员昨天曾游览了北京车站和百货大楼。
