Title: Dögei Returns to Beijing After Visiting Various Parts of China

Author: 
Date: 1959-10-19
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. Dögei István, Chairman of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, returned to Beijing by special plane this morning after visiting Shanghai, Wuhan, Chengdu, Kunming, and Xi'an, accompanied by Wang Zhen, Minister of Agricultural Reclamation, and Linping, Deputy Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Welcoming the distinguished guest at the airport were Tan Zhenlin, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, Chen Zhengren, Minister of Agricultural Machinery, Zeng Yongquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Liu Ruilong, Vice Minister of Agriculture, Jiang Qixian and Liu Xing, Vice Ministers of Agricultural Reclamation, Qu Wu, President of the Sino-Hungarian Friendship Association, and Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.

Sándor Nográdi, Ambassador of the Hungarian People's Republic to China, also went to the airport to welcome him.

When Chairman Dögei left Xi'an today, those who saw him off at the airport included Zhao Boping, Secretary of the Shaanxi Provincial Committee of the CPC and Governor of Shaanxi Province, Fang Zhongru, Secretary of the Provincial Committee, Zhao Shouyi and Yan Kelun, Alternate Secretaries of the Provincial Committee, Shi Yizhi and Li Qiming, Deputy Governors, Zhang Ce, First Secretary of the Xi'an Municipal Committee of the CPC, Liu Geng, Secretary of the Municipal Committee and Mayor, and Yang Jiarui, Major General, Commander of the PLA stationed in Xi'an, and others.

### 道比主席在我国各地访问后回到北京

1959-10-19
第2版()
专栏：

　　道比主席在我国各地访问后回到北京
    据新华社18日讯　匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比·伊斯特万，参观访问了上海、武汉、成都、昆明和西安等城市后，今天上午在农垦部部长王震和外交部美澳司副司长林平陪同下由西安乘专机返回北京。
    到机场欢迎贵宾的，有中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理谭震林，农业机械部部长陈正人，外交部副部长曾涌泉，农业部副部长刘瑞龙，农垦部副部长姜齐贤、刘型，中匈友好协会会长屈武，外交部苏欧司副司长陈伯清等人。
    匈牙利人民共和国驻华大使诺格拉第·山道尔也到机场欢迎。
    道比主席今天离开西安时，到机场欢送的有中共陕西省委书记、陕西省省长赵伯平、省委书记方仲如，省委候补书记赵守一、严克伦、副省长时逸之、李启明，中共西安市委第一书记张策，市委书记、市长刘庚和人民解放军驻西安部队首长杨嘉瑞少将等。
