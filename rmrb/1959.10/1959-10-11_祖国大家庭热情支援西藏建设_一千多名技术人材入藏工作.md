Title: The Motherland's Big Family Enthusiastically Supports Tibet's Construction: Over a Thousand Technical Personnel Arrive in Tibet to Work
Author:
Date: 1959-10-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, October 10th. More than a thousand technical personnel from all over the country who have come to support Tibet's construction arrived in Lhasa at the beginning of this month and have been dispatched to Shannan, Northern Tibet, Rikaze, Jiangzi and other places to work. Among these personnel are architectural engineers, workers, medical workers, and professional technicians in various fields such as agriculture, animal husbandry, water conservancy, geology, and transportation. Most of them come from provinces and cities such as Beijing, Shanghai, Sichuan, Henan, Zhejiang, and Guizhou. Some have just graduated from universities and secondary specialized schools, while others have been transferred from hospitals, construction projects, and other units in various provinces and cities to support Tibet's construction.

### 祖国大家庭热情支援西藏建设  一千多名技术人材入藏工作

1959-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　祖国大家庭热情支援西藏建设
    一千多名技术人材入藏工作
    新华社拉萨10日电　从祖国各地来支援西藏建设的各种技术人材一千多人，本月初陆续到达拉萨，并已分赴山南、藏北、日喀则、江孜等地进行工作。这批人员中，有建筑工程师、工人、医务工作者和农牧水利、地质、交通等各方面的专业技术人员。他们大部分来自北京、上海、四川、河南、浙江、贵州等省市，有的刚从大学和中等专业学校毕业，有的是从各省市医院、建设工程等单位中调来支援西藏建设的。
