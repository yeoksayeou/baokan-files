Title: Suwirjo Talks About Impressions of Visiting China: The Chinese People are United Like One Person
Author:
Date: 1959-10-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 15th: According to Antara News Agency today, Suwirjo, Chairman of the Indonesian Nationalist Party, who is currently visiting China, told the agency's Beijing correspondent that his deepest impression of visiting China was the various construction projects completed in the year since the establishment of the Chinese People's Communes.

After participating in the celebrations for the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China in Beijing, Suwirjo visited other cities in China. He said that in the past ten years, the Chinese people have achieved amazing results in construction. Under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong, people from all walks of life in China are united like one person, and the national leaders and the people are closely united.

When talking about the Chinese People's Communes, Suwirjo pointed out that his impressions after visiting the People's Communes were completely different from some of the reports outside of China. The People's Communes are good for China.

### 苏维约谈访华观感  中国人民团结得像一个人

1959-10-17
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏维约谈访华观感
    中国人民团结得像一个人
    新华社雅加达15日电　据安塔拉通讯社今天报道，正在中国访问的印度尼西亚国民党总主席苏维约对这家通讯社驻北京记者说，他访问中国印象最深的是，在中国人民公社成立以后一年期间所完成的各项建设工程。
    苏维约在北京参加了中华人民共和国建国十周年的庆祝典礼以后，又前往中国其他城市访问。他说，过去十年中，中国人民在建设方面获得了惊人的成就。在毛泽东主席的领导下，中国各阶层人民团结得像一个人一样，国家领导人和人民紧密地团结一致。
    苏维约在谈到中国人民公社时指出，他参观过人民公社以后所得到的印象同中国国外所报道的一些情况完全不同。人民公社对中国来说是好的。
