Title: Chairman Mao Receives Comrade Zawadzki
Author:
Date: 1959-10-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, received Aleksander Zawadzki, Chairman of the State Council of the Polish People's Republic and member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, this morning, and had a cordial and friendly conversation.

Present from the Polish side at the reception were:  Stefan Ignar, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Repubic and Chairman of the Supreme Committee of the Polish United Peasant Party; Edward Gierek, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee, and First Secretary of the Katowice Provincial Committee, of the Polish United Workers' Party; Jan Karol Wende, member of the Polish People's Republic's Parliament and Vice-Chairman of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Polish Democratic Party; Julian Horodecki, Secretary of the State Council of the Polish People's Republic and member of the Presidium of the Supreme Committee of the Polish United Peasant Party; Franciszek Modrzewski, Vice-Minister of Foreign Trade of the Polish People's Republic; P. Ogrodziński, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Polish People's Republic; and Stanisław Flato, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Polish People's Republic in China.

Present from the Chinese side at the reception were: Bo Yibo, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council; Wang Shoudao, Member of the CPC Central Committee and Minister of Communications; Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Yu Zhan, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 毛主席接见萨瓦茨基同志

1959-10-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席接见萨瓦茨基同志
    新华社14日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志，在今天早晨接见了波兰人民共和国国务委员会主席、波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局委员亚历山大·萨瓦茨基同志，进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时波兰方面在座的，有波兰人民共和国部长会议副主席、波兰统一农民党最高委员会主席斯特瓦·伊格纳尔，波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局委员、书记处书记、卡托维兹省委员会第一书记爱德华·格雷克，波兰人民共和国议会议员、波兰民主党中央委员会主席团副主席杨·卡罗尔·温德，波兰人民共和国国务委员会秘书、波兰统一农民党最高委员会主席团委员尤利安·霍罗德茨基，波兰人民共和国对外贸易部副部长弗朗齐歇克·莫德日夫斯基，波兰人民共和国外交部部长助理普·奥格罗任斯基，波兰人民共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办斯坦尼斯拉夫·傅拉托。
    接见时中国方面在座的，有中共中央政治局候补委员、国务院副总理薄一波，中共中央委员、交通部部长王首道，外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文，外交部苏联东欧司副司长余湛。
