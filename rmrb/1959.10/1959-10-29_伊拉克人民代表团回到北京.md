Title: Iraqi People's Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-10-29
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, October 28th: The Iraqi People's Delegation, led by the Deputy Head of the Delegation and Prosecutor General of the Iraqi Ministry of Justice, Abdul Amir Okli, returned to Beijing by plane from Shanghai today.
    After leaving Beijing on October 10th, the Iraqi People's Delegation visited Shenyang, Anshan, Changchun, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other cities.

### 伊拉克人民代表团回到北京

1959-10-29
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克人民代表团回到北京
    新华社28日讯　伊拉克人民代表团在副团长、伊拉克司法部检察长阿卜杜勒·阿密尔·奥克里率领下，今天乘飞机从上海回到北京。
    伊拉克人民代表团在10月10日离开北京以后，曾经参观访问了沈阳、鞍山、长春、南京、上海、杭州等城市。
