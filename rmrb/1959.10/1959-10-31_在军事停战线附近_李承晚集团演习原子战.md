Title: Near the Military Demarcation Line, Syngman Rhee Clique Conducts Atomic War Exercises
Author:
Date: 1959-10-31
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 29th. According to news from Seoul on the 28th, the Syngman Rhee clique of South Korea, under the instigation of US imperialism, conducted provocative atomic war exercises near the military demarcation line north of Seoul from October 20th to 23rd.

More than 50,000 soldiers of the Syngman Rhee army participated in the exercises. The Syngman Rhee clique's air force also participated. Troops from the US Fourth Missile Command supported the exercises with "Honest John" rockets and atomic cannons.

### 在军事停战线附近  李承晚集团演习原子战

1959-10-31
第6版()
专栏：

    在军事停战线附近
    李承晚集团演习原子战
    据新华社平壤29日电　据汉城28日消息，南朝鲜李承晚集团在美帝国主义指使下，从10月20日到23日在汉城以北军事停战线附近举行了挑衅性的原子战演习。
    参加演习的李承晚陆军达五万人以上。李承晚集团的空军也参加了演习。美国第四导弹指挥部的部队以“诚实的约翰”火箭和原子弹大炮支援了这次演习。
