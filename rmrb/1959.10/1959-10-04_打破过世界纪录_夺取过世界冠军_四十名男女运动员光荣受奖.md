Title: Forty Male and Female Athletes Awarded for Breaking World Records and Winning World Championships
Author:
Date: 1959-10-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, October 3rd. Forty athletes (including twenty-six women) from our country who have broken world records and won world championships over the years were awarded sports honor medals by the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission at the closing ceremony of the First National Games on the 3rd. The achievements, times, and locations of these athletes are as follows:

        Weightlifting
    Chen Jingkai (Guangdong), Snatch in the Bantamweight category: 133 kg, June 7, 1956, Shanghai; 135 kg, November 11, 1956, Guangzhou; 135.5 kg, November 29, 1956, Shanghai; 139.5 kg, August 6, 1957, Moscow; 140.5 kg, September 26, 1958, Leipzig. Snatch in the Featherweight category: 148 kg, March 14, 1959, Moscow.
    Huang Qianghui (Heilongjiang), Snatch in the Lightweight category: 155 kg, April 7, 1958, Chongqing; 158 kg, November 29, 1958, Beijing; 158.5 kg, April 19, 1959, Taiyuan.
    Zhao Qingkui (Hebei), Snatch in the Light Heavyweight category: 176.5 kg, September 30, 1958, Beijing; 177.5 kg, November 30, 1958, Beijing.
      Swimming
    Men's 100m Breaststroke:
    Qi Lieyun (Guangdong), 1 minute 11.6 seconds, May 1, 1957, Guangzhou.
    Mu Xiangxiong (Hebei), 1 minute 11.4 seconds, December 20, 1958, Beijing; 1 minute 11.3 seconds, August 30, 1959, Beijing; 1 minute 11.1 seconds, September 17, 1959, Beijing.
        Track and Field
    Zheng Fengrong (Shandong), Women's High Jump: 1.77 meters, November 17, 1957, Beijing.
      Parachuting
    Women's 1000m Daytime Group Accuracy Landing:
    He Jianhua (PLA), Geng Guifang (PLA), Cui Xiuying (PLA), 9.81 meters, September 21, 1958, Beijing;
    He Jianhua (PLA), Li Guizhen (PLA), Li Shuhui (PLA), 2.21 meters, July 30, 1959, Xuzhou;
    Mei Yan (Shaanxi), Guo Xin'e (Shaanxi), Zhang Jingwen (Shaanxi), 5.11 meters, September 18, 1959, Beijing.
    Women's 600m Daytime Group Combined Jump:
    Li Shuhua (PLA), Cui Xiuying (PLA), Li Shuhui (PLA), 12.39 meters, November 5, 1958, Beijing.
    Women's 1500m Daytime Group Combined Jump:
    He Jianhua (PLA), Geng Guifang (PLA), Cui Xiuying (PLA), 2.69 meters, January 8, 1959, Kaifeng.
    Men's 1000m Daytime Group Accuracy Landing:
    Wang Jianye (Sichuan), Guo Ronglian (Shandong), Liu Jialin (Shandong), 2.99 meters, June 27, 1959, Chengdu.
    Women's 600m Daytime Group Accuracy Landing:
    Gao Ming (Shandong), Wang Suzhen (Sichuan), Zhao Chengying (Sichuan), 7 meters, June 29, 1959, Chengdu.
    Women's 1000m Daytime Group Combined Jump:
    Lü Xuehui (Beijing), Hua Shaolin (Beijing), Wei Xiuling (Beijing), 6.80 meters, July 3, 1959, Zhuoxian.
        Table Tennis
    Rong Guotuan (Guangdong), won the Men's Singles Championship at the 25th World Table Tennis Championships on April 5, 1959.
        Shooting
    Zhang Hong (PLA), Men's Free Pistol 50m 60 Shots Slow Fire: 567 rings, April 24, 1959, Beijing;
    Chen Rong (PLA), Women's Free Small-Bore Rifle 50m and 100m 30 Shots Prone: 589 rings, September 24, 1959, Beijing.
      Aviation Model
    Liu Litian (PLA), Wang? (PLA), Piston Engine Model Helicopter Straight-Line Flight Distance: 18.083 kilometers, April 26, 1959, Beijing;
    Wang Yongxi (PLA), Zhao Jiazhen (PLA), Radio-Controlled Piston Engine Model Airplane Flight Altitude: 1260 meters, September 27, 1959, Beijing.
        Mountaineering
    Qimi (Tibet), Xirao (Tibet), Chamujin (Tibet), Pando (Tibet), Wang Yiqin (Tibet), Wang Guihua (Beijing), Zhou Yuying (Shanxi), Cong Zhen (Beijing) and other eight female athletes collectively and safely reached the summit of Muztagh Ata (located in Xinjiang), which is 7,546 meters above sea level, on July 7, 1959.
    Yuan Yang (Beijing), reached a height of 7,500 meters above sea level on Muztagh Ata on July 7, 1959. (Attached photo)
    Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long presented gold "Sports Honor Medals" to outstanding athletes who have broken world records and won world championships in China at the closing ceremony of the First National Games held on October 3. This is a group photo after the award ceremony.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Hesong photo

### 打破过世界纪录　夺取过世界冠军  四十名男女运动员光荣受奖

1959-10-04
第2版()
专栏：

    打破过世界纪录　夺取过世界冠军
    四十名男女运动员光荣受奖
    新华社3日讯　我国历年来打破过世界纪录和取得过世界冠军的四十名运动员（其中女子二十六人），3日在第一届全国运动会的闭幕式上得到了国家体育运动委员会颁发的体育运动荣誉奖章。这些选手取得的成绩和时间、地点如下：
        举　重
    陈镜开（广东），最轻量级挺举：一百三十三公斤，1956年6月7日，上海；一百三十五公斤，1956年11月11日，广州；一百三十五点五公斤，1956年11月29日，上海；一百三十九点五公斤，1957年8月6日，莫斯科；一百四十点五公斤，1958年9月26日，来比锡。次轻量级挺举：一百四十八公斤，1959年3月14日，莫斯科。
    黄强辉（黑龙江），轻量级挺举：一百五十五公斤，1958年4月7日，重庆；一百五十八公斤，1958年11月29日，北京；一百五十八点五公斤，1959年4月19日，太原。
    赵庆奎（河北），轻重量级挺举：一百七十六点五公斤，1958年9月30日，北京；一百七十七点五公斤，1958年11月30日，北京。
      游　泳
    男子一百米蛙泳：
    戚烈云（广东），一分十一秒六，1957年5月1日，广州。
    穆祥雄（河北），一分十一秒四，1958年12月20日，北京；一分十一秒三，1959年8月30日，北京；一分十一秒一，1959年9月17日，北京。
        田　径

      飞机跳伞
