Title: Chairman Liu and Chairman Zhu Meet with Delegations from Seven Brotherly Countries
Author:
Date: 1959-10-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, October 1st: Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China and Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, and Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, met tonight with Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Chairman and General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, and members of the Party and Government Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam led by him: Vo Nguyen Giap, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee, Secretary of the Secretariat, Director of the General Military Commission, Deputy Premier, Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnam People's Army, and Minister of National Defense; Nghien Xuan Yem, Minister of Agriculture and Forestry, General Secretary of the Vietnam Democratic Party; Nguyen Xien, Minister without Portfolio, General Secretary of the Vietnam Socialist Party; Ung Van Khiem, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; and Tran Tu Binh, Vietnamese Ambassador to China.
　　The leaders of the two parties and countries held cordial talks during the meeting.
　　Luo Ruiqing, Vice Premier of the State Council and member of the CPC Central Committee, was present at the meeting.
　　Xinhua News Agency, October 1st: Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China and Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, and Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, met tonight with Kim Il Sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, and all members of the Korean Party and Government Delegation led by him: Kim Chang-man, member of the Standing Committee and Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea; Kim Kwang-hyup, member of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and Minister of National Defense of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; Han Sang-du, alternate member of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and Minister of Metal Industry of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; Li Yong-ho, member of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and Korean Ambassador to China; and Hyun Moo-kwang, Chairman of the South Hamgyong Provincial Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea.
　　During the meeting, the hosts and guests engaged in cordial talks.
　　He Long, Vice Premier of the State Council and member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, was present at the meeting.
　　Xinhua News Agency, October 1st: Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China and Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, and Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, met tonight with Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, and all members of the Party and Government Delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic led by him: Batyn Lhamsuren, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic; Byadalyn Choijil, First Deputy Minister of People's Army Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic; Jambalyn Banzar, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic; and Gotovyn Sharav, Mongolian Ambassador to China.
　　The leaders of the two parties and countries engaged in cordial and friendly talks during the meeting.
　　Xi Zhongxun, Vice Premier of the State Council and member of the CPC Central Committee, was present at the meeting.
　　Xinhua News Agency, October 1st: Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China and Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, and his wife, and Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, met this evening with Dimitar Ganev, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, and his wife, and members of the Party and Government Delegation of the People's Republic of Bulgaria: Boyan Balgaranov, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, and his wife; Petar Kolarov, member of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and Minister of Health of the People's Republic of Bulgaria; General Ivan Kinov, member of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and First Deputy Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of Bulgaria; and Petar Panchevski, member of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and Bulgarian Ambassador to China, and his wife, etc.
　　The party and state leaders of the two countries held cordial talks.
　　Ulanhu, Vice Premier of the State Council and alternate member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, was present at the meeting.
　　Xinhua News Agency, October 1st: Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China and Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, and Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, met tonight with Mehmet Shehu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania and member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor, and members of the Party and Government Delegation of the People's Republic of Albania led by him: Haki Toska, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor, and secretary to the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor; Adil Çarçani, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor, and Minister for Industry and Mines of the People's Republic of Albania; and Mihal Prifti, Alternate Member of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor and Ambassador of the People's Republic of Albania to China.
　　The leaders of the two parties and countries held cordial talks during the meeting.
　　Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State Council and member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, was present at the meeting.
　　Xinhua News Agency, October 1st: Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China and Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, and Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, met tonight with Hermann Matern, First Deputy of the Presidium of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, and all members of the Party and Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic led by him: Max Sefrin, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Health of the German Democratic Republic; Bernhard Quandt, Member, Central Committee, Socialist Unity Party of Germany, and First Secretary, Bezirk Schwerin Party Committee; Alexander Abusch, Member, Central Committee, Socialist Unity Party of Germany, and Minister of Culture; Helmut Speer, Deputy Chairman of the Dresden District Committee and Member of the Central Committee of the Liberal Democratic Party of Germany; Alfred Hennecke, Deputy Chairman of the Karl-Marx-Stadt District Committee and Member of the Standing Committee of the Executive Committee of the National Democratic Party of Germany; Kurt Nawack, First Secretary of the Bitterfeld County Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany; Heinz Barwich, Member of the Scientific Council for Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy of the German Democratic Republic, Director of the Central Institute for Nuclear Physics, Member of the Physics Division of the German Academy of Sciences, and winner of the Lenin Prize; Professor Heinz Kress, Director of the Institute for Plant Breeding and Agricultural Biology of the Academy of Sciences; and Paul Wandel, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the German Democratic Republic to China.
　　The hosts and guests engaged in cordial talks during the meeting.
　　Nie Rongzhen, Vice Premier of the State Council and member of the CPC Central Committee, was present at the meeting.
　　Xinhua News Agency, October 1st: Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China and Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, and Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, met tonight with Emil Bodnăraș, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic and member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, and all members of the Romanian Party and Government Delegation led by him: Dumitru Coliu, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, and Chairman of the State Control Commission of the Romanian People's Republic; Paul Niculescu-Mizil, Member of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, and Minister of Propaganda and Agitation of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party; Ludovic Supor, Member of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, and First Secretary of the Hungarian Autonomous Region Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party; and Babu Zaharescu, Alternate Member of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party and Ambassador of the Romanian People's Republic to China.
　　The leaders of the two parties and countries held cordial talks during the meeting.
　　Lu Dingyi, Vice Premier of the State Council and alternate member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, was present at the meeting.

### 刘主席朱委员长会见七个兄弟国家代表团

1959-10-02
第4版()
专栏：

    刘主席朱委员长会见七个兄弟国家代表团
    新华社1日讯　中华人民共和国主席、中共中央副主席刘少奇和全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长、中共中央副主席朱德，今晚会见了越南民主共和国主席、越南劳动党中央委员会主席兼总书记胡志明和他所率领的越南民主共和国党政代表团团员：越南劳动党中央政治局委员、书记处书记、总军事委员会主任、副总理、越南人民军总司令、国防部长武元甲，农林部部长、越南民主党总书记严春庵，不管部部长、越南社会党总书记阮阐，外交部副部长雍文谦，越南驻中国大使陈子平。
    两国党和国家的领导人在会见时进行了亲切的交谈。
    会见时在座的，有国务院副总理、中共中央委员罗瑞卿。
