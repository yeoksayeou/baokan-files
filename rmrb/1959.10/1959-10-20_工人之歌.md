Title: Workers' Songs
Author: Jiang Liechao Zhang Tingxun Xi Ren
Date: 1959-10-20
Page: 3

　　Workers' Songs
    To Overtake England
　　As long as more steel ingots are produced every day,
　　Even if sweat flows like a river,
　　The hotter the furnace, the hotter the heart,
　　To overtake England in ten years.
        　　      Jiang Liechao
    Song of the Fitter
　　A pair of pliers,
　　A cold chisel,
　　A piece of steel is given life,
　　These hands, ah,
　　What can't they make!
    　　          Zhang Tingxun
    Rafting
　　Rapid currents, shallow stony beach,
　　The wooden raft rushes straight down, the mountain spins.
　　The bamboo pole strikes the cliff rocks with a loud sound,
　　The sound of the oars draws out the red sun.
    Lumbering
　　Wearing a bamboo hat, wearing straw sandals,
　　The lumberjacks are in high spirits,
　　Stars overhead, clouds underfoot,
　　One axe chop, ten thousand pieces of timber.
    　　          Xi Ren

### 工人之歌
江列潮  章廷勋  习仁
1959-10-20
第3版()
专栏：工人之歌

　　工人之歌
    为了超英国
　　只要钢锭日日多，
　　那怕汗水流成河，
　　炉火越热心越热，
　　为了十年超英国。
        　　      江列潮
    钳工的歌
　　一把钳刀，
　　一把堑子，
　　一块钢就有了生命，
　　这双手啊，
　　有什么不能做成！
    　　          章廷勋
    流放
　　急流水，浅石滩，
　　木筏直泻山旋转。
　　竹篙敲得崖石响，
　　浆声引出红日来。
    伐木
　　戴笠帽，穿草鞋，
　　伐木工人好神采，
　　头顶星星脚踩云，
　　一斧劈下万根材。
    　　          习仁
