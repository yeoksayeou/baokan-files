Title: Argentine, Danish, and Norwegian Communist Party Delegations and Head of Chilean Communist Party Delegation Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-19
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, October 18th: The Argentine Communist Party delegation headed by Comrade Victorio Codovilla, the head of the Chilean Communist Party delegation, Comrade José González, the Danish Communist Party delegation headed by Comrade Robert Salltaurt, and the Norwegian Communist Party delegation headed by Comrade Jørgen Vogt, have returned to Beijing today after visiting other locations.
    The distinguished guests visited factories, people's communes, schools, various exhibitions and memorial halls in Shanghai, Wuhan, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Nanjing, Zhengzhou and other cities, and toured scenic spots and historical sites. The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed everywhere.
    Members of the Chilean Communist Party delegation and one member of the Argentine Communist Party delegation are currently continuing their visits in other locations.

### 阿根廷、丹麦、挪威共产党代表团和智利共产党代表团团长返京

1959-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

    阿根廷、丹麦、挪威共产党代表团和智利共产党代表团团长返京
    新华社18日讯　以维克托里奥·柯都维亚同志为首的阿根廷共产党代表团，智利共产党代表团团长何塞·冈萨雷斯同志，以罗伯特·萨尔陶里同志为首的丹麦共产党代表团，以约尔根·福克特同志为首的挪威共产党代表团，在外地参观访问之后，今天已经回到北京。
    贵宾们曾分别在上海、武汉、杭州、广州、南京、郑州等城市，参观访问了工厂、人民公社、学校、各种展览会和纪念馆，并游览了名胜古迹。贵宾们到处受到热烈的欢迎。
    智利共产党代表团团员和阿根廷共产党代表团的一位团员目前继续在外地参观访问。
