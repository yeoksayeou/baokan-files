Title: Madame Andrée leaves Beijing

Author: 
Date: 1959-10-09
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, October 8th. Madame Andrée Andreen, Vice-President of the Women's International Democratic Federation, member of the World Peace Council, and Swedish medical doctor, who was invited by the All-China Women's Federation to visit China, left Beijing by plane this afternoon for Prague. Her daughter, Hillevi Svedberg, took the same plane back to her country via Moscow.

　　Madame Moudidio and Helen Keissian, members of the delegation of the Women's International Democratic Federation, also left Beijing for Prague on the same plane.

　　The delegation of the Tunisia-China Association, headed by Rachid Ben Ammar, also left Beijing for home today.

### 安德琳夫人等离京

1959-10-09
第6版()
专栏：

    安德琳夫人等离京
    新华社8日讯　应全国妇联邀请来我国访问的国际民主妇女联合会副主席、世界和平理事会理事、瑞典医学博士安德蕾亚·安德琳夫人，今天下午乘飞机离开北京去布拉格。她的女儿希勒维·斯维德贝格乘同一架飞机经莫斯科回国。
    国际民主妇女联合会代表团团员穆迪迪约夫人和海伦·凯西安，也乘同一架飞机离开北京去布拉格。
    今天离开北京回国的，还有以拉希德·贝勒·阿米扎为首的突尼斯—中国协会代表团。
