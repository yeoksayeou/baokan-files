Title: Chen Yi, Vice Premier, Receives Algerian Government Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1959-10-05
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi received the delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria this afternoon. Those received included the head of the delegation, Benyoucef Ben Khedda, Minister of Social Welfare of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria; the deputy head of the delegation, Ahmed Tewfik El Madani, Minister of Cultural Affairs of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria; delegation members: Government Counselor Ahmed Abdel Hafid, Haji Hilshali, Director of the Office of the Minister of Social Welfare, and Riza Malik, editor-in-chief of the Battle Magazine and special envoy. The two sides had a friendly conversation during the meeting.
    Those present at the reception included Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, He Gongkai and Gong Dafei, Deputy Directors of the Department of West Asia and Africa of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department.

### 陈毅副总理接见阿尔及利亚政府代表团

1959-10-05
第2版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见阿尔及利亚政府代表团
　　新华社4日讯　国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅，今天下午接见了阿及利亚共和国临时政府代表团。被接见的有代表团团长、阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府社会福利部部长本·优素福·本·赫达，代表团副团长、阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府文化事务部部长艾哈迈德·陶菲克·迈达尼，团员：政府参事艾哈迈德·阿卜杜勒·哈菲德，社会福利部部长办公室主任哈吉·希尔沙利，战斗杂志主编、特派员里扎·马利克。接见时双方进行了友好的交谈。
    接见时在座的有外交部副部长章汉夫，外交部西亚非洲司副司长何功楷、宫达非，礼宾司副司长马振武。
