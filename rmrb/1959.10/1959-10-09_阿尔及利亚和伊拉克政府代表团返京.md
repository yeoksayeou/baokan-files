Title: Algerian and Iraqi Government Delegations Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-09
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 8th: The delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, headed by Ben Yousef Ben Khedda, Minister of Social Welfare of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, and the Iraqi government delegation, headed by Brigadier General Ahmed Muhammad Yahya, Minister of Interior of the Republic of Iraq, returned to Beijing by plane this morning from Shenyang.

Gong Dafei, Deputy Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, accompanied the delegation back to Beijing.

The Algerian and Iraqi government delegations visited Shenyang for two and a half days. They visited the Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Plant, the Cable Factory, and the Heavy Machinery Plant. They also visited the Daqing People's Commune in Shenyang County and went to Anshan to visit the steelmaking and ironmaking plants of Anshan Iron and Steel, where they were warmly welcomed by workers and commune members everywhere.

### 阿尔及利亚和伊拉克政府代表团返京

1959-10-09
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚和伊拉克政府代表团返京
    据新华社8日讯　以阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府社会福利部部长本·优素福·本·赫达为首的阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团，和以伊拉克共和国内政部长艾哈迈德·穆罕默德·叶海亚准将为首的伊拉克政府代表团，今天上午乘飞机从沈阳回到北京。
    外交部西亚非洲司副司长宫达非陪同代表团回到北京。
    阿尔及利亚和伊拉克两国政府代表团在沈阳进行了两天半的访问，他们参观了沈阳第一机床厂、电缆厂、重型机器厂等工厂，访问了沈阳县大青人民公社，还去鞍山参观了鞍钢的炼钢、炼铁等工厂，到处受到工人、公社社员的热烈欢迎。
