Title: Warm-Loving Crops in the Plateau
Author:
Date: 1959-10-20
Page: 4

　　Warm-Loving Crops in the Plateau
    In the Bapao Farm in Qilian Mountain, Qinghai Province, at an altitude of 2,776 meters, the warm-loving vegetable—tomato—trial planting this year achieved a high yield. One of the plants was as tall as 1.5 meters and yielded 15 kilograms.

### 喜温作物在高原

1959-10-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　喜温作物在高原
    在海拔二千七百七十六米的青海祁连山八宝农场，今年试种的喜温蔬菜——西红柿获得了高产。其中有一株高达一点五米，结实十五斤。
