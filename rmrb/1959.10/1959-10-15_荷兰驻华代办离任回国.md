Title: Dutch Chargé d'Affaires to China Leaves Post and Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. The Dutch Chargé d'Affaires to China, Mr. S.H. Spalingius, has left his post and returned home on the 13th.
