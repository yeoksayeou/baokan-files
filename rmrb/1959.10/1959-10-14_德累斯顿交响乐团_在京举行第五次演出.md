Title: Dresden Philharmonic Orchestra Holds Fifth Performance in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-14
Page: 5

　　Dresden Philharmonic Orchestra
    Holds Fifth Performance in Beijing

    Xinhua News Agency, 13th — The Dresden Philharmonic Orchestra of the German Democratic Republic held its fifth concert tonight, receiving enthusiastic appreciation from more than 1,400 audience members, giving music lovers in the capital another unforgettable artistic experience.
    The Dresden Philharmonic Orchestra's performances in Beijing have come to an end for now. They will continue their tour to Xi'an, Chongqing, Nanjing, Shanghai and other places, before returning to Beijing.

### 德累斯顿交响乐团  在京举行第五次演出

1959-10-14
第5版()
专栏：

　　德累斯顿交响乐团
    在京举行第五次演出
    据新华社13日讯　德意志民主共和国德累斯顿交响乐团今晚举行的第五次音乐会，获得了一千四百多观众的热烈赞赏，使首都许多音乐爱好者又一次得到了难忘的艺术享受。
    德累斯顿交响乐团在北京的演出至此告一段落。他们将去西安、重庆、南京、上海等地继续访问演出，然后再回北京。
