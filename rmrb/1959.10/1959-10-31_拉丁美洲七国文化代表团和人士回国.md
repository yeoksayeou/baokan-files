Title: Latin American Seven-Nation Cultural Delegations and Personnel Return Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 30th: The Cuban Cultural Friendship Delegation, the Brazilian Cultural Friendship Delegation, the Chilean Cultural Friendship Delegation, the Argentine Cultural Friendship Delegation, the Peruvian Cultural Friendship Delegation, and the members of the Colombian Cultural Friendship Delegation, Manuel Manotas, and the Haitian writer Frank Fouchier, who were invited to China to participate in the celebrations of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, have concluded their visits in China and left Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 29th to return to their countries.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, Ding Xilin, Vice President, Zhou Erfu, Secretary-General, Zhu Boshen, Deputy Secretary-General, and Xiao San, Executive Director.

After participating in the celebrations of the 10th anniversary of the founding of our country in Beijing, the guests visited Chengdu, Chongqing, Wuhan, Changsha, Zhengzhou, Taiyuan, Hefei, Wuxi, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Shenyang, Anshan, Changchun and other places.

### 拉丁美洲七国文化代表团和人士回国

1959-10-31
第5版()
专栏：

    拉丁美洲七国文化代表团和人士回国
    新华社30日讯　应邀来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的古巴文化友好代表团、巴西文化友好代表团、智利文化友好代表团、阿根廷文化友好代表团、秘鲁文化友好代表团以及哥伦比亚文化友好代表团团员曼努埃尔·马诺塔斯和海地作家佛兰克·富希等，结束了在我国的参观访问，已经在29日下午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的有中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南、副会长丁西林、秘书长周而复、副秘书长朱伯深、常务理事萧三等。
    客人们在北京参加我国建国十周年庆祝典礼后，曾到成都、重庆、武汉、长沙、郑州、太原、合肥、无锡、南京、上海、杭州、广州、沈阳、鞍山、长春等地进行了参观访问。
