Title: Delegation of the Cuban Revolutionary Directorate Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-30
Page: 5

    Cuban Revolutionary Directorate Delegation Returns Home
    Xinhua News Agency, October 29th: The delegation of the Cuban Revolutionary Directorate, led by Faure Chomón Mediavilla, Secretary-General of the Cuban Revolutionary Directorate, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.
    Those seeing them off at the airport included Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Director Shen Jian, and Secretary-General Wu Maosun, among others.

### 古巴革命指导委员会代表团回国

1959-10-30
第5版()
专栏：

    古巴革命指导委员会代表团回国
    新华社29日讯　由古巴革命指导委员会总书记福雷·乔蒙·梅迪亚维利亚率领的古巴革命指导委员会代表团一行五人，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    在机场送行的有：中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之、理事申健、秘书长吴茂荪等。
