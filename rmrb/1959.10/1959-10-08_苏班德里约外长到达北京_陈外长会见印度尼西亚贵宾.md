Title: Foreign Minister Subandrio Arrives in Beijing; Foreign Minister Chen Meets with Indonesian Guests of Honor
Author:
Date: 1959-10-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 7th: Indonesian Foreign Minister Subandrio and his entourage arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a friendly visit to our country at the invitation of Vice Premier of the State Council and Foreign Minister Chen Yi.

    Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi welcomed the guests at the airport. Beijing children presented the guests with flowers.

    Also at the airport to welcome them were: Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Lei Renmin, Director of the First Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wenjin, responsible persons of various democratic parties, Wang Renshu, Director of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, Da Pangsheng, Vice Chairman of the China Islamic Association, NPC representative and Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese Yu Yangzu, Situ Zan, Chairman of the Jakarta Chinese Association, and others.

    Indonesian Ambassador to China Wijopranoto and Indian Ambassador to China Parthasarathi were also at the airport to welcome them.

    Also at the airport to welcome them were Heng Antong, Deputy Head of the Indonesian-China Friendship Association Delegation, and some members of the delegation, who are currently visiting our country.

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th: Vice Premier of the State Council and Foreign Minister Chen Yi met this afternoon with Indonesian Foreign Minister Subandrio and his entourage, who arrived today for a friendly visit to our country.

    Present at the meeting were: Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu and Zhang Wenjin, Director of the First Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

    Also present was Indonesian Ambassador to China Wijopranoto.

### 苏班德里约外长到达北京  陈外长会见印度尼西亚贵宾

1959-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    苏班德里约外长到达北京
    陈外长会见印度尼西亚贵宾
    新华社7日讯　应国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅邀请来我国进行友好访问的印度尼西亚外交部长苏班德里约和他的随行人员，在今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    陈毅副总理兼外交部长到机场欢迎客人。北京少年儿童向客人们献了鲜花。
    在机场欢迎的还有：外交部副部长章汉夫，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，外交部第一亚洲司司长章文晋，各民主党派负责人，中国印度尼西亚友好协会理事王任叔，中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生，全国人民代表大会代表、中华全国归国华侨联合会副主席尤扬祖，雅加达中华侨团总会主席司徒赞等。
    印度尼西亚驻华大使维约普拉诺托和印度驻华大使帕塔萨拉蒂也在机场欢迎。
    在机场欢迎的还有现在我国访问的印度尼西亚中国友好协会代表团副团长亨·安东和部分团员。
    新华社7日讯　国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅在今天下午会见了今天到达的、来我国进行友好访问的印度尼西亚外交部长苏班德里约和他的随行人员。
    会见时在座的有：外交部副部长章汉夫、外交部第一亚洲司司长章文晋。
    在座的还有印度尼西亚驻华大使维约普拉诺托。
