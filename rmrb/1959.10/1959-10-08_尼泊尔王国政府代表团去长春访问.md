Title: Delegation from the Government of the Kingdom of Nepal Visits Changchun
Author:
Date: 1959-10-08
Page: 2
    Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, October 6th: The delegation from the Government of the Kingdom of Nepal, led by Dr. Tulsi Giri, Minister of Rural Development of the Kingdom of Nepal, concluded its visit to Shenyang and Anshan and took a train from Shenyang to Changchun this afternoon for a visit.
    During their stay in Shenyang and Anshan, the delegation visited the Shenyang Cable Factory, the No. 1 Machine Tool Plant, the Heavy Machinery Plant, and Anshan Iron and Steel's steelmaking, ironmaking, and seamless steel pipe factories. The distinguished guests also visited small blast furnace groups in Anshan and believed that China's approach of "walking on two legs" in construction was very successful. (Photo attached)

### 尼泊尔王国政府代表团去长春访问

1959-10-08
第2版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔王国政府代表团去长春访问
    据新华社沈阳6日电　以尼泊尔王国乡村发展大臣图尔西·吉里博士为首的尼泊尔王国政府代表团，结束了在沈阳、鞍山的参观访问，今天下午由沈阳乘火车去长春访问。
    代表团在沈阳、鞍山期间，先后参观了沈阳电缆厂、第一机床厂、重型机器厂和鞍钢的炼钢、炼铁、无缝钢管等工厂。贵宾们在鞍山还参观了小高炉群，认为我国建设采取两条腿走路的办法很成功。（附图片）
