Title: Attending the Congress of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea, Trade Union Delegations from China and Vietnam Arrive in Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1959-10-31
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 30th. The Chinese trade union delegation, led by Liu Changsheng, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, arrived in Pyongyang today at the invitation to attend the Third National Congress of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea.
    The Vietnamese trade union delegation, led by Bui Khuy, a member of the Central Executive Committee of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor, arrived in Pyongyang in the same train.
    Greeting them at the station were Li Hyo-sun, Chairman of the Central Committee of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea, and other leading cadres of the General Federation of Trade Unions, as well as Qiao Xiaoguang, Chinese Ambassador to Korea, and Phan The Huu, Third Secretary of the Vietnamese Embassy in Korea.

### 参加朝鲜职业总同盟代表大会  中越两国工会代表团抵平壤

1959-10-31
第5版()
专栏：

    参加朝鲜职业总同盟代表大会
    中越两国工会代表团抵平壤
    新华社平壤30日电　应邀来参加朝鲜职业总同盟第三次全国代表大会的中国工会代表团在中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜率领下今天到达平壤。
    由越南总工会中央执行委员会委员裴葵率领的越南工会代表团同车到达平壤。
    到车站欢迎的有朝鲜职业总同盟中央委员会委员长李孝淳等职业总同盟领导干部以及中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光、越南驻朝鲜大使馆三等秘书潘世友等。
