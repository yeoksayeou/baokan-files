Title: Colombian Communist Party Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-30
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, 29th. The Colombian Communist Party delegation, headed by Comrade Victor H. Merchan, member of the Central Executive Committee of the Colombian Communist Party and National Secretary for Agricultural Work, left Beijing by plane today to return home after attending the celebrations of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and touring various parts of our country.

　　Li Xiannian, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee, Tan Zheng, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee, and Wu Xiuquan, member of the Central Committee, were at the airport to see them off.

### 哥伦比亚共产党代表团回国

1959-10-30
第5版()
专栏：

    哥伦比亚共产党代表团回国
    新华社29日讯　以哥伦比亚共产党中央执行委员会委员、全国农业工作书记维克托·赫·梅尔昌同志为首的哥伦比亚共产党代表团，在参加了中华人民共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼和在我国各地参观访问之后，于今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的，有中共中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记李先念，中央书记处书记谭政，中央委员伍修权。
