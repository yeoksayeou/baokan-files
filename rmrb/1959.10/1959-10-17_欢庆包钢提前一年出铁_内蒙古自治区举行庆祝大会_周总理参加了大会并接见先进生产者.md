Title: Celebrating Baogang's Iron Production One Year Ahead of Schedule; Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Holds Celebration Meeting; Premier Zhou Attends the Meeting and Receives Advanced Producers
Author:
Date: 1959-10-17
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Baotou, October 16th: The Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the People's Committee of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region held a celebration meeting for the first blast furnace of Baogang producing iron at 9:00 this morning in Baotou.

Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the CPC and Premier of the State Council, attended today's celebration meeting and delivered a speech.

Some responsible persons from various departments of the Central Government and various people's organizations who came to participate in the celebration of Baogang's iron production also attended the meeting.

Chairman Ulanhu presided over the celebration meeting. In his speech, he said: As we celebrate the first blast furnace of Baogang producing iron, we look forward to the future with even greater excitement. It is hoped that all staff and workers and people of all nationalities in Baotou City, under the leadership of the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao, will hold high the red flags of the General Line, the Great Leap Forward, and the People's Commune, unite as one, work together, ride the waves, give full play to favorable conditions, overcome various difficulties on the road forward, and leap forward even greater, better, and more comprehensively. We will unswervingly strive to build the Baotou industrial base centered on Baogang ahead of schedule, for the greater development of economic and cultural construction in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, and for the accelerated construction of a great socialist motherland with modern industry, modern agriculture, and modern science and culture.

After the celebration meeting, Premier Zhou Enlai received more than 1,000 advanced producers from Baogang, the Second Engineering Bureau of the Ministry of Construction Engineering, the Hohhot-Baotou Power Grid Bureau, the Baotou Mining Bureau, the Inner Mongolia Thermal Power Installation Company, the Baotou Office of the Hohhot Railway Bureau, and local industrial, financial, trade, and transportation units in Baotou City, and took photos with them. (Attached picture)

On October 15th, Baogang held a ribbon-cutting ceremony for the first blast furnace producing iron, and Premier Zhou Enlai personally attended the ribbon-cutting. Xinhua News Agency reporters Lu Ke and Li Jilu.

### 欢庆包钢提前一年出铁  内蒙古自治区举行庆祝大会  周总理参加了大会并接见先进生产者

1959-10-17
第2版()
专栏：

　　欢庆包钢提前一年出铁
    内蒙古自治区举行庆祝大会
    周总理参加了大会并接见先进生产者
    新华社包头16日电　中共内蒙古自治区委员会、内蒙古自治区人民委员会，今天上午九时在包头举行包钢一号高炉出铁庆祝大会。
    中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来参加了今天的庆祝大会，并且讲了话。
    前来参加庆祝包钢出铁的中央各部门、各人民团体的部分负责人，也参加了大会。
    庆祝大会由乌兰夫主席主持。他在讲话中说：当我们欢庆包钢一号高炉出铁的时候，瞻望将来，更加无比兴奋。希望我们包头市全体职工和各民族人民，在党中央和毛主席的领导下，更高地举起总路线、大跃进和人民公社的红旗，同心同德，团结一致，乘风破浪，充分发挥有利条件，克服前进道路上的各种困难，更大更好更全面地跃进再跃进，为提前建成以包钢为中心的包头工业基地，为内蒙古自治区经济、文化建设的更大发展，为加快建成具有现代工业、现代农业和现代科学文化的伟大的社会主义祖国而坚持不懈地奋斗。
    庆祝大会结束后，周恩来总理接见了包钢、建筑工程部第二工程局、呼和浩特——包头电网局、包头矿务局、内蒙古火电安装公司、呼和浩特铁路局包头办事处和包头市的地方工业、财贸、交通等单位的一千多名先进生产者，并同他们一起照了像。（附图片）
    10月15百，包钢举行了第一号高炉出铁剪彩典礼大会，周恩来总理亲临剪彩。　新华社记者　陆轲　李基禄摄
