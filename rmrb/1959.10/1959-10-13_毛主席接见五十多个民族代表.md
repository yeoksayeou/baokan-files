Title: Chairman Mao Receives Representatives of Over Fifty Nationalities
Author:
Date: 1959-10-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairmen Liu Shaoqi, Zhu De, Lin Biao of the CPC Central Committee, members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee Peng Zhen, He Long, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, Bainqen Erdini Qoigyi Gyaincain, Vice Premier of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the CPPCC Li Siguang, Bao Erhan, Pagbalha Geleg Namgyal, and Ngabo Ngawang Jigme, received the visiting delegations of various nationalities and youth study groups who came to Beijing to participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China this afternoon.

During the reception, representatives of more than fifty nationalities crowded around Chairman Mao, enthusiastically cheering and applauding for a long time. Chairman Mao cordially shook hands with the representatives.

### 毛主席接见五十多个民族代表

1959-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席接见五十多个民族代表
    新华社12日讯　中共中央主席毛泽东，副主席刘少奇、朱德、林彪，中共中央政治局委员彭真、贺龙，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长沈钧儒、黄炎培、李维汉、陈叔通、班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞，国务院副总理习仲勋，政协全国委员会副主席李四光、包尔汉、帕巴拉·卓列朗杰、阿沛·阿旺晋美，在今天下午接见了来北京参加庆祝建国十周年典礼的各民族观礼团和青年学习团。
    接见时，五十多个民族的代表拥到毛主席的周围，长时间热烈地欢呼和鼓掌，毛主席和代表们亲切地握手。
    接见的时候，全国人民代表大会民族委员会和中华人民共和国民族事务委员会的负责人都在场。
