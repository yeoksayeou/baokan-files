Title: Kanzo Uchiyama's Ashes Interred at Shanghai International Cemetery; Representatives from the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries and Other Groups Attend the Burial Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1959-10-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 27th. The ashes of Mr. Kanzo Uchiyama, Vice-Chairman of the Japan-China Friendship Association, were interred yesterday at the Shanghai International Cemetery, in accordance with his wishes during his lifetime.

Mr. Kanzo Uchiyama was invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of our country, and unfortunately passed away in Beijing on September 20th.

Those who attended the burial ceremony of Mr. Kanzo Uchiyama yesterday included Zhang Chunqiao, President of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Vice-President Su Buqing, and representatives from Shanghai people's organizations and cultural circles Shen Cuizhen, Wei Jinzhi, Du Xuan, Chen Qiucao, Chen Yusun, and Feng Zikai, a friend of Mr. Kanzo Uchiyama during his lifetime.

Mrs. Uchiyama, Vice-Chairman of the Japan-China Friendship Association, Natsu Kawasaki, and Toshizo Hasegawa, Acting Secretary-General of the Japan-China Friendship Association, also attended the burial ceremony.

The tomb was filled with wreaths from the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Shanghai people's organizations, and friends of Mr. Uchiyama during his lifetime. Jiichiro Matsumoto, President of the Japan-China Friendship Association, and Kenzo Matsumura, Advisor to the Liberal Democratic Party of Japan, also sent wreaths.

Zhang Chunqiao delivered a eulogy at the burial ceremony on behalf of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. In his eulogy, he spoke of Mr. Kanzo Uchiyama's valuable contributions to promoting friendship between the people of China and Japan and cultural exchange between the two countries.

### 内山完造骨灰安葬上海万国公墓  我对外文协等团体代表参加安葬仪式

1959-10-28
第5版()
专栏：

    内山完造骨灰安葬上海万国公墓
    我对外文协等团体代表参加安葬仪式
    据新华社上海27日电　日中友好协会副会长内山完造先生的骨灰，按照他生前的意愿，昨天安葬在上海的万国公墓。
    内山完造先生是应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼，不幸于9月20日在北京病逝的。
    昨天参加内山完造先生骨灰安葬仪式的，有中国人民对外文化协会上海市分会会长张春桥、副会长苏步青，上海各人民团体和文化界代表沈粹缜、魏金枝、杜宣、陈秋草、陈虞荪，以及内山完造先生生前友好丰子恺等。
    内山完造夫人，日中友协副会长河崎夏，日中友协代理事务局长长谷川敏三等也参加了安葬仪式。
    墓前放满了中国人民对外文化协会、上海各人民团体和内山先生生前友好送来的花圈。日中友好协会会长松本治一郎、日本自由民主党顾问松村谦三也送了花圈。
    张春桥代表中国人民对外文化协会在安葬仪式上致悼词。他在悼词中谈到内山完造先生在促进中日两国人民的友好和两国文化交流等方面所作的可贵贡献。
