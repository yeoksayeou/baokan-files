Title: Chairman Mao Receives Foreign Minister Subandrio, Foreign Minister Subandrio Holds Banquet Last Night
Author:
Date: 1959-10-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 11th: Chairman Mao Zedong received Indonesian Foreign Minister Subandrio at 1:00 PM on the 11th.

Present at the meeting were Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs.

Also present at the meeting was Indonesian Ambassador to China, Wirjopranoto.

Xinhua News Agency, October 10th: Indonesian Foreign Minister Dr. Subandrio, held a banquet this evening.

Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi and his wife attended the banquet.

Also present at today's banquet were: Liao Chengzhi, Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and Bao Erhan, President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association.

Indonesian Ambassador to China Wirjopranoto and his wife also attended the banquet. (Attached picture)

### 毛主席接见苏班德里约外长  苏班德里约外长昨晚举行宴会

1959-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席接见苏班德里约外长
    苏班德里约外长昨晚举行宴会
    新华社11日讯　毛泽东主席在11日一时接见了印度尼西亚外交部部长苏班德里约。
    接见时在座的，有国务院副总理兼外交部部长陈毅、外交部副部长章汉夫。
    接见时，印度尼西亚驻中国大使维约普拉诺托也在座。
    新华社10日讯　印度尼西亚外交部长苏班德里约博士，今天晚上举行宴会。
    陈毅副总理兼外交部长和夫人出席了宴会。
    出席今天宴会的还有：华侨事务委员会主任廖承志，外交部副部长章汉夫，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，中国印度尼西亚友好协会会长包尔汉。
    印度尼西亚驻华大使维约普拉诺托和夫人也出席了宴会。（附图片）
    毛泽东主席接见印度尼西亚外交部长苏班德里约。图为接见时情形。
