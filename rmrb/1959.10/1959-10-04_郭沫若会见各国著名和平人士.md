Title: Guo Moruo Meets with Prominent Peace Advocates from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1959-10-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 3rd: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, met with the World Peace Council delegation led by Professor Bernal, Executive Chairman of the World Peace Council Presidium, and other peace advocates from various countries this morning. He also held a reception to welcome them.

Among the international peace advocates attending the meeting and reception were all members of the World Peace Council delegation, as well as sixty-seven heads of peace organizations and prominent peace advocates from thirty countries in socialist countries, Asia, Africa, Europe, the Americas, Latin America, and Australia.

At the reception, Guo Moruo extended a warm welcome to the international peace advocates who had traveled from afar to China to attend the 10th anniversary celebration of the founding of the People’s Republic of China. Bernal said at the reception, "We should continue to unite and fight until the forces of war are completely eliminated from the world."

At the reception, peace fighters from various countries spoke freely about their good wishes for maintaining peace. The guests frequently raised their glasses to toast the friendship of the people of all countries and world peace.

Among those attending the reception were Liao Chengzhi, Chen Shutong, and Bao Erhan, Vice-Chairmen of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, as well as heads of various people's organizations and prominent figures from the capital's science, culture, art, and political and legal circles.

### 郭沫若会见各国著名和平人士

1959-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若会见各国著名和平人士
    据新华社3日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若今天上午同以世界和平理事会主席团执行主席贝尔纳教授为首的世界和平理事会代表团以及各国和平人士会见，并举行酒会欢迎他们。
    参加会见和出席酒会的各国和平人士有，世界和平理事会代表团全体成员以及来自社会主义国家和亚洲、非洲、欧洲、美洲、拉丁美洲、澳洲的三十个国家和平组织负责人和著名和平人士共六十七人。
    郭沫若在酒会上向不远千里而来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的各国和平人士表示热烈欢迎。贝尔纳在酒会上说，我们应当继续团结一致斗争下去，直到战争势力从世界上彻底消灭掉。
    酒会上，各国和平战士畅谈维护和平的美好心愿。宾主频频起立为各国人民的友谊和世界和平干杯。
    出席酒会的有，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志、陈叔通、包尔汉以及各人民团体的负责人和首都的科学、文化、艺术、政法等各界著名人士。
