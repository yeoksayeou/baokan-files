Title: Head of the Moroccan National Union of People's Forces Delegation, Mehdi Ben Barka, Leaves Guangzhou for Home

Author:

Date: 1959-10-19
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 18th October: Mehdi Ben Barka, Head of the Moroccan National Union of People's Forces Delegation and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Moroccan National Union of People's Forces, who was invited to China to participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the country and to visit, and his wife, left Guangzhou by train this morning via Hong Kong to return home.

　　The guests arrived in Guangzhou by plane from Shanghai on the afternoon of the 16th. In Guangzhou, they visited the site of the "Guangzhou Peasant Movement Training Institute" formerly run by Chairman Mao Zedong, the Guangdong Revolutionary History Museum, the China Export Commodities Exhibition Hall, visited Zhongshan Fourth Road Primary School, and toured Yuexiu Mountain, Huanghuagang and other places. Zhu Guang, the Mayor of Guangzhou, and his wife hosted a banquet on the evening of the 16th to welcome the guests.

### 摩洛哥人民力量全国联盟代表团长  迈赫迪·巴尔卡离广州回国

1959-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

　　摩洛哥人民力量全国联盟代表团长
    迈赫迪·巴尔卡离广州回国
    新华社广州18日电　应邀前来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼和访问的摩洛哥人民力量全国联盟代表团团长、摩洛哥人民力量全国联盟书记处书记迈赫迪·本·巴尔卡和他的夫人，今天上午乘火车离开广州取道香港回国。
    客人们是16日下午乘飞机从上海到达广州的。他们在广州参观了毛泽东主席在过去主办的“广州农民运动讲习所”旧址、广东革命历史博物馆、中国出口商品陈列馆，访问了中山四路小学，并游览了越秀山、黄花岗等地。广州市市长朱光夫妇于16日晚上设宴欢迎客人。
