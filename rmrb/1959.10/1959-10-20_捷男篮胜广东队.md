Title: Czechoslovakian Men's Basketball Team Defeats Guangdong Team
Author:
Date: 1959-10-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 19th. The Czechoslovakian National Men's Basketball Team defeated China's Guangdong Men's Basketball Team tonight in Beijing by a score of 88 to 73. In the first half, the visiting team led 50 to 35. In the second half, both sides were evenly matched, each scoring 38 points.

The Czechoslovakian National Men's Basketball Team has already played seven friendly matches in our country, resulting in five wins and two losses. On the evenings of the 21st and 22nd, they will also play their final two friendly matches in China in Beijing against the "August 1st" team and the Beijing team, respectively.

### 捷男篮胜广东队

1959-10-20
第5版()
专栏：

　　捷男篮胜广东队
    新华社19日讯　捷克斯洛伐克国家男子篮球队今晚在北京以八十八比七十三战胜了我国广东男子篮球队。上半场客队以五十比三十五领先；下半场双方打得不分胜负，都得了三十八分。
    捷克斯洛伐克国家男子篮球队已经在我国进行了七场友谊比赛，结果五胜两负。21日和22日晚，他们还将在北京先后同“八一”队和北京队进行最后的两场访华友谊比赛。
