Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, Foreign Minister Chen Yi Send Congratulatory Messages on the National Day of the Republic of Guinea
Author:
Date: 1959-10-02
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, October 1st – Chairman of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi sent a telegram to President Sékou Touré of the Republic of Guinea on September 28th, congratulating him on the National Day of the Republic of Guinea. The full text of the telegram is as follows:
To His Excellency Sékou Touré, President of the Republic of Guinea, Conakry:

    On the occasion of the National Day of the Republic of Guinea, I extend my heartfelt congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of Guinea on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish your country prosperity and your people happiness.

        Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, September 28, 1959

Xinhua News Agency, October 1st – Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to President Sékou Touré of the Republic of Guinea on September 28th, congratulating him on the National Day of the Republic of Guinea. The full text of the telegram is as follows:
To His Excellency Sékou Touré, President of the Republic of Guinea, Conakry:

    On the occasion of the first anniversary of the founding of the Republic of Guinea, I extend my warm congratulations to your government and Your Excellency on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China, and in my own name.

    The Chinese people are pleased to see that the Guinean people have continuously achieved new victories in the struggle to safeguard national independence over the past year. I sincerely hope that the friendly relations between China and Guinea will continue to develop under the guidance of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and the Bandung Spirit. I wish Your Excellency and the Guinean people new achievements in building your beautiful country and promoting the cause of friendship and solidarity among Asian and African countries.

        Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, September 28, 1959

Xinhua News Agency, October 1st – Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of China Chen Yi sent a telegram to President and Foreign Minister Sékou Touré of the Republic of Guinea on September 28th, congratulating him on the National Day of the Republic of Guinea. The full text of the telegram is as follows:
To His Excellency Sékou Touré, President and Foreign Minister of the Republic of Guinea, Conakry:

    Please accept my sincere congratulations on the occasion of the first anniversary of the founding of the Republic of Guinea.

    I wish the friendship between the people of China and Guinea will develop day by day.

        Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, September 28, 1959

### 刘少奇主席周恩来总理陈毅外长  电贺几内亚共和国国庆

1959-10-02
第2版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席周恩来总理陈毅外长
    电贺几内亚共和国国庆
    新华社1日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇在9月28日打电报给几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔，祝贺几内亚共和国国庆。电报全文如下：科纳克里几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔先生阁下：
    欣逢几内亚共和国国庆，我谨代表中国人民，并且以我个人的名义，向阁下和几内亚人民表示衷心的祝贺。祝贵国国家繁荣，人民幸福。
            中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇　1959年9月28日
    新华社1日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在9月28日打电报给几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔，祝贺几内亚共和国国庆。电报全文如下：科纳克里几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔先生阁下：
    值此几内亚共和国成立一周年之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民，并且以我个人的名义，向贵国政府和阁下致以热烈的祝贺。
    中国人民高兴地看到，一年来，几内亚人民在维护民族独立的斗争中不断取得新的胜利。我衷心地希望，中国和几内亚之间的友好关系将在和平共处五项原则和万隆精神的指导下日益发展。祝阁下和几内亚人民在建设自己美好的国家和促进亚非各国友好团结的事业中，获得新的成就。
        中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来　1959年9月28日
    新华社1日讯　中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅在9月28日打电报给几内亚共和国总统兼外交部长塞古·杜尔，祝贺几内亚共和国国庆。电报全文如下：科纳克里几内亚共和国总统兼外交部长塞古·杜尔先生阁下：
    值此几内亚共和国成立一周年之际，请接受我热诚的祝贺。
    祝中国和几内亚两国人民的友谊日益发展。
            中华人民共和国外交部长　陈毅　1959年9月28日
