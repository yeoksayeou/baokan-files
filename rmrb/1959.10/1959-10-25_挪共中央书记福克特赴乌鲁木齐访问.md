Title: Norwegian Communist Party Central Secretary Fokk Visits Urumqi

Author:
Date: 1959-10-25
Page: 4

    Norwegian Communist Party Central Secretary Fokk Visits Urumqi
    Xinhua News Agency, 24th. Comrade Jørgen Fokk, head of the Norwegian Communist Party delegation and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Norwegian Communist Party Central Committee, left Beijing by plane today for a visit to Urumqi.

### 挪共中央书记福克特赴乌鲁木齐访问

1959-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

    挪共中央书记福克特赴乌鲁木齐访问
    新华社24日讯　挪威共产党代表团团长、挪威共产党中央书记处书记约尔根·福克特同志，今日乘飞机离开北京，前往乌鲁木齐参观访问。
