Title: Dresden Symphony Orchestra to Visit Xi'an
Author:
Date: 1959-10-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. The entire Dresden Symphony Orchestra of the German Democratic Republic left Beijing this morning to visit and perform in Xi'an and other places. Responsible persons from the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and other relevant departments, as well as people from the music circles in the capital, went to see them off. The symphony orchestra is scheduled to visit and perform in Xi'an, Chongqing, Nanjing, and Shanghai, and will return to Beijing in mid-November.

### 德累斯顿交响乐团去西安

1959-10-16
第5版()
专栏：

　　德累斯顿交响乐团去西安
    新华社15日讯　德意志民主共和国德累斯顿交响乐团全体音乐家，今天上午离开北京去西安等地访问演出。对外文化联络委员会等有关部门负责人和首都音乐界人士前往送行。交响乐团预定在西安、重庆、南京和上海访问演出后，将于11月中旬再回到北京。
