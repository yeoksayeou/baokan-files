Title: Chairman Liu, Chairman Zhu, and Premier Zhou Receive the Yemen Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-10-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 1st October: Chairman of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Zhu De, and Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai received the Yemen Kingdom Government Delegation headed by Yemen Kingdom Minister of State Sayyid Ali al-Moayyad tonight.

### 刘主席、朱委员长、周总理接见也门政府代表团

1959-10-02
第3版()
专栏：

    刘主席、朱委员长、周总理接见也门政府代表团
    新华社1日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德，国务院总理周恩来今天晚上接见了以也门王国国务大臣赛义德·阿里·穆安亚德为首的也门王国政府代表团。
