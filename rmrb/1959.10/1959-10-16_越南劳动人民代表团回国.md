Title: Vietnamese Labor People's Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 15th. The Vietnamese Labor People's Delegation, led by Bui Ky, member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Fatherland Front and President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, returned home by train this afternoon from Wuhan after visiting Shanghai, Zhengzhou, Wuhan and other places. Li Dongqing, Vice Chairman of the Hubei Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Sun Lianqing, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the Communist Youth League, and Chen Yuxi, Vice Chairman of the Wuhan Federation of Trade Unions, and others went to the station to see the guests off.

### 越南劳动人民代表团回国

1959-10-16
第5版()
专栏：

　　越南劳动人民代表团回国
    据新华社武汉15日电　由越南祖国战线中央委员会主席团委员、越中友好协会会长裴?率领的越南劳动人民代表团，在上海、郑州、武汉等地参观访问后，今天下午由武汉乘火车回国。中国人民保卫世界和平委员会湖北省分会副主席李冬青、共青团湖北省委书记处书记孙连清、武汉市工会联合会副主席陈玉玺等到车站欢送客人。
