Title: Mexican Exhibition of Works by Mexican Artists Visiting China
Author:
Date: 1959-10-22
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Mexico City, 20th (TASS) – The exhibition hall of the Mexican National Institute of Fine Arts today exhibited artistic works by Mexican artists reflecting the situation in the People's Republic of China. On display here are sketches, drawings, prints, and sculptures by Mexican artists Calderón, Belles, and Herero, who have successively visited the People's Republic of China. The exhibition is very popular with the audience and will continue until October 31.

### 墨西哥展出墨艺术家访华作品

1959-10-22
第5版()
专栏：

    墨西哥展出墨艺术家访华作品
    新华社21日讯　塔斯社墨西哥20日讯：墨西哥国立美术研究所展览厅今天展出了墨西哥艺术家反映中华人民共和国情况的艺术作品。在这里展出了先后访问过中华人民共和国的墨西哥艺术家卡尔德隆、贝尔特和埃雷罗等人的写生画、素描、版画和雕塑。展览会很受观众欢迎，它将一直展出到10月31日。
