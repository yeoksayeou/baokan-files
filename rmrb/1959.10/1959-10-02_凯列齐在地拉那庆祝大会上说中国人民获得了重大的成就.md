Title: Kellezi Says Chinese People Have Achieved Significant Achievements at Tirana Celebration

Author:
Date: 1959-10-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, September 30th. A grand rally was held by people from all walks of life in the Albanian capital tonight at the People's Theater to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of our country, and the establishment of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association was announced at the celebration rally.

Participating in this grand rally were: First Secretary of the Albanian Labor Party, Hoxha; members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Labor Party, Nushi, Kabo, Belishova, Muftiu, Koleka, and member of the Central Committee of the Labor Party, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association, Kellezi, and other party and government leaders, representatives of workers, peasants, students and troops, as well as people from cultural and artistic circles.

The celebration rally was presided over by Kabo, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party.

Kellezi gave a report on the tenth anniversary of the founding of China at the meeting. He said that with the generous assistance of the Soviet Union and other people's democratic countries, the Chinese people have achieved tremendous achievements.

He said that the general line, the Great Leap Forward, and the People's Communes of the Chinese Communist Party specifically embody the determination and great wisdom of the 650 million industrious and brave Chinese people, and embody the creative combination of China's reality and the truth of Marxism-Leninism by the Chinese Communist Party headed by Mao Zedong.

Our country's ambassador to Albania, Luo Shigao, spoke at the celebration rally.

### 凯列齐在地拉那庆祝大会上说中国人民获得了重大的成就

1959-10-02
第6版()
专栏：

    凯列齐在地拉那庆祝大会上说中国人民获得了重大的成就
    据新华社地拉那9月30日电　阿尔巴尼亚首都各界人民今晚在人民剧院举行盛大集会，庆祝我国建国十周年，在庆祝大会上并宣告成立阿中友好协会。
    参加这个盛大集会的有：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第一书记霍查，劳动党中央政治局委员努什、卡博、别利绍娃、穆夫蒂乌、科列加和劳动党中央委员、部长会议副主席、阿中友好协会筹备委员会主任凯列齐等党政领导人，工人、农民、学生和部队的代表，以及文化、艺术界人士。
    庆祝大会由阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央书记处书记卡博主持。
    凯列齐在会上做了关于中国建国十周年的报告。他说，中国人民在苏联和各人民民主国家慷慨的援助下，获得了巨大的成就。
    他说，中国共产党的总路线、大跃进和人民公社具体地体现了六亿五千万勤劳勇敢的中国人民的决心和伟大的智慧，体现了以毛泽东为首的中国共产党创造性地使中国的实际和马克思列宁主义的真理相结合。
  我国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使罗士高在庆祝大会上讲了话。
