Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Continues Talks with Subandrio
Author: 
Date: 1959-10-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 9th - Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi continued talks with Indonesian Foreign Minister Subandrio this morning. The Chinese side participating in the talks included: Liao Chengzhi, Zhang Hanfu, and Zhang Wenjin. The Indonesian side participating in the talks included: Indonesian Ambassador to China Wijono, Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs Director Suska, and Bureau Director Ilyas Hamsah.

### 陈毅副总理同苏班德里约继续会谈

1959-10-10
第2版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理同苏班德里约继续会谈
    新华社9日讯　国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅今天上午同印度尼西亚外交部长苏班德里约继续进行会谈。中国方面参加会谈的有：廖承志、章汉夫和章文晋。印度尼西亚方面参加会谈的有：印度尼西亚驻华大使维约普拉诺托，印度尼西亚外交部司长苏斯卡，局长伊里亚斯·汉姆沙。
