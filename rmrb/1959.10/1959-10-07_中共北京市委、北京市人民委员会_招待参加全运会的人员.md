Title: CPC Beijing Municipal Committee and Beijing Municipal People's Committee Host Personnel Participating in the National Games
Author:
Date: 1959-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th - The CPC Beijing Municipal Committee and the Beijing Municipal People's Committee held a grand reception today to host over 10,000 athletes, referees, and staff participating in the First National Games.

The reception was held in the banquet hall of the Great Hall of the People. Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, First Secretary of the CPC Beijing Municipal Committee, and Mayor of Beijing, and He Long, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Vice Premier of the State Council, and Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, both spoke at the reception, encouraging all athletes to exert their utmost effort, strive for excellence, study diligently, improve their political and technical levels, and be good sons and daughters of the Party and good athletes.

During the reception, Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Hu Yaobang, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, Zhang Yun, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, and others raised their glasses to congratulate the achievements of this National Games and expressed their hope that the athletes would create more new records.

At the reception, representatives of athletes, referees, and staff from various units also raised their glasses to thank the Party and the government for their care and expressed their determination to work hard to improve their political and technical levels and win glory for the motherland in the future.

The banquet hall was filled with melodious music and a joyous atmosphere. Before the reception, Peng Zhen and He Long also met with the heads of the sports delegations participating in the First National Games, as well as Rong Guotuan, world champion in table tennis singles, and Chen Jingkai, Mu Xiangxiong, Zheng Fengrong, Wang Suzhen, Zhang Hong, Zhao Jiazhen, Chamujin, and more than 30 other world record creators in weightlifting, swimming, track and field, parachuting, shooting, aviation modeling, and mountaineering, and had cordial talks with them.

Present at the meeting were Liu Ren, Second Secretary of the CPC Beijing Municipal Committee, Secretaries of the Secretariat Chen Peng, Zheng Tianxiang, Deng Tuo, Chen Kehan, Fan Rusheng, and Vice Mayors of Beijing Wan Li, Wu Han, Wang Kunlun, Cheng Hongyi, Le Songsheng, as well as Ma John, Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation, and Li Da, Lu Han, Rong Gaotang, Huang Zhong, and others, Deputy Directors of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission.

### 中共北京市委、北京市人民委员会  招待参加全运会的人员

1959-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    中共北京市委、北京市人民委员会
    招待参加全运会的人员
    新华社6日讯　中共北京市委员会和北京市人民委员会今天举行盛大招待会，招待一万多名参加第一届全国运动会的运动员、裁判员和工作人员等。
    招待会在人民大会堂的宴会厅里举行。中共中央政治局委员、中共北京市委第一书记、北京市市长彭真，中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理、体育运动委员会主任贺龙都在招待会上讲了话，勉励全体运动员要鼓足干劲，力争上游，勤学苦练，提高政治、技术水平，作一个党的好儿女和好运动员。
    在招待会进行中，中华全国总工会主席刘宁一、共青团中央委员会第一书记胡耀邦、全国妇女联合会副主席章蕴等人举杯祝贺这次全运会的成就，并希望运动员们创造出更多的新纪录。
    在招待会上，各单位的运动员代表、裁判员代表和工作人员代表也举杯感谢党和政府的关怀，并且表示今后努力提高政治、技术水平，为祖国争光。
    宴会大厅乐曲悠扬，充满了欢乐的气氛。在招待会前，彭真、贺龙还接见了参加第一届全国运动会的各体育代表团的负责人，以及世界乒乓球单打冠军容国团和陈镜开、穆祥雄、郑凤荣、王素珍、张鈜、赵嘉桢、查姆金等三十多名举重、游泳、田径、跳伞、射击、航空模型和登山等项运动的世界纪录创造者们，并且同他们进行了亲切的谈话。
    接见时，在场的有中共北京市委第二书记刘仁、书记处书记陈鹏、郑天翔、邓拓、陈克寒、范儒生和北京市副市长万里、吴晗、王昆仑、程宏毅、乐松生，以及中华全国体育总会主席马约翰，国家体委副主任李达、卢汉、荣高棠、黄中等人。
