Title: Chairman Mao and Chairman Liu Receive Latin American Guests; Receive Indonesian Guests on the Same Day
Author:
Date: 1959-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th October: Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and other Party and state leaders Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Peng Zhen, and Wang Jiaxiang, received this afternoon guests from various Latin American countries who came to our country to participate in the 10th anniversary celebrations and for visits. The reception took place in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

These guests are:

All members of the Argentine Parliamentary Delegation: Augusto Bayol, Luis Agustin Leon, Berta Fekin de Ferrari, Armando Tejada Gomez; All members of the Cuban Revolutionary Guidance Committee Delegation: Faure Chomón Mediavilla, Gustavo Machín Hoed de Beche, Guillermo Jiménez Soler, Osvaldo Antonino Castells Valdés, José Antonino Lavassat; Brazilian Congressman De Vasconcelos and his wife;

All members of the Argentine Cultural Friendship Delegation: Miguel Carlos Lombardi, Mario Feldmann, Jorge Rolelo, Bernardo Moreira; All members of the Brazilian Cultural Friendship Delegation: Henrique Fiallo and his wife, Edmundo Vasconcelos and his wife, Wosvaldo Cavalcanti and his wife, Guilherme Figueiredo and his wife, Jorge Giraldo Vieira and his wife, Ron Dantas; All members of the Chilean Cultural Friendship Delegation: Francisco Cuevas Mackenna and his wife, Luis Armando Sepúlveda Morales, Jorge Errázuriz, Pedro Foncea, Mario Sais Lagos; All members of the Colombian Cultural Friendship Delegation: Enrique Gómez Hurtado and his wife, Jaime Venegas, Alfonso López Michelsen and his wife, Enrique Liévano, Manuel Manotas; All members of the Cuban Cultural Friendship Delegation: Nicolás Guillén, Enrique Labrador Ruiz, Mariano Rodríguez, Vicente Revuelta, Isabel Morel; All members of the Mexican Cultural Friendship Delegation: Guillermo Aro, Pedro Rojas, Ignacio Romano Vargas Iturbe, Guillermo Montaño, Raquel Laviera; All members of the Peruvian Cultural Friendship Delegation: Asunción Caballero Méndez, Carlos Núñez Anavitarte, César Guardia Mayorga; All members of the Uruguayan Cultural Friendship Delegation: Sarandí Cabrera and his wife, Angel Hernandez, Camilo Bosduriño, Guillermo Aristi, Genaro Zavala; All members of the Venezuelan Cultural Friendship Delegation: Humberto Quenca, Rafael Viyelzana, Enrique Betancourt, Eleazar Díaz Rangel, Carlos Díaz; Ricardo Anaya, Chairman of the Bolivian Left-Wing Revolutionary Party and President of the San Simón School of Economics; Haitian poet Frank Fouché; One representative from Nicaragua; Paraguayan poet Eviyo Romero and his wife;

Silva, President of the Brazilian International Trade Promotion Association, his wife and daughter;

Roberto Gabaldón, President of the Venezuelan National School of Agriculture, and his wife.

Xinhua News Agency, 7th October: Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Vice Premier Chen Yi, received this evening the Indonesian guests who came to our country to participate in the 10th anniversary celebrations and for visits.

They are: Rasuna Said, head of the Indonesian-China Friendship Association Delegation, Henk Anton, deputy head, and all members of the delegation: Ghani Binadiburata, Mrs. Soeska, Li Quanxin, Manan El Yanu, Zhou Qingfu, Gasmin, Naim Abdul Razak, Soezono, Oscar Brandão, Soebardo, I Gusti Gura Jeren, Goesoemo Soedibjo;

Suwirjo, Chairman of the Indonesian Nationalist Party, and his wife;

Hadi, former First Deputy Prime Minister of Indonesia, and his wife;

Muali Nasution, Secretary to Indonesian President Sukarno, and his wife.

During the reception, Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and others held cordial talks with the guests.

### 毛主席刘主席接见拉丁美洲来宾  同日接见印度尼西亚客人

1959-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席刘主席接见拉丁美洲来宾
    同日接见印度尼西亚客人
    新华社7日讯　毛泽东主席、刘少奇主席以及党和国家的其他领导人周恩来、朱德、彭真、王稼祥，今天下午接见了前来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼和参观访问的拉丁美洲各国客人。接见是在热烈的友好气氛中进行的。
    这些客人是：
    阿根廷议员团全体成员：奥古斯托·巴约耳，路易斯·奥古斯丁·莱昂，贝尔塔·费金·德费拉里，阿曼多·特哈达·戈麦斯；古巴革命指导委员会代表团全体成员：福雷·萧蒙·梅迪亚维利亚，古斯塔沃·马钦·沃艾德，吉列尔英·希梅内斯·索莱尔，奥斯瓦尔多·安东尼诺·卡斯特尔·瓦尔德斯，何塞·安东尼诺·拉瓦萨；巴西众议员德瓦拉斯科和夫人；

    印度尼西亚国民党总主席苏维约和他的夫人；
