Title: Celebrating the 10th Anniversary of the Founding of the German Democratic Republic: "Berlin Festival" Arts Festival Opens with Unprecedented Scale, Powerful Lineup, and Rich Content
Author:
Date: 1959-10-05
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 3rd – The "Berlin Festival" Arts Festival, held in celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic, began tonight at the German State Opera in Berlin. The opening ceremony was presided over by Waldemar Schmidt, Deputy Mayor of Greater Berlin. Beethoven's Ninth Symphony was performed at the ceremony.

    Ulbricht and other party and state leaders, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to the German Democratic Republic, attended the opening ceremony.

    This year's "Berlin Festival" art performance is unprecedented in scale and rich in content. There are more than 380 programs, with over 6,000 performers. Tickets for many of the programs are already sold out. Foreign art troupes invited to participate in the festival have arrived in Berlin one after another, including the Soviet Ukrainian National Folk Song and Dance Ensemble and the Chinese Sichuan Opera Troupe.

    Some art exhibitions will also be held during this "Berlin Festival." The Exhibition of Fine Arts Celebrating the 10th Anniversary of the Founding of the German Democratic Republic opened this afternoon. Prime Minister Grotewohl attended the opening ceremony. The Second International Photography Exhibition also opened on the same day.

### 庆祝德意志民主共和国建国十周年  “柏林节”文艺大会演开幕  规模空前　阵容强大　内容丰富

1959-10-05
第7版()
专栏：

    庆祝德意志民主共和国建国十周年
    “柏林节”文艺大会演开幕
    规模空前　阵容强大　内容丰富
    新华社柏林3日电　为庆祝德意志民主共和国建国十周年而举行的“柏林节”文艺大会演，今晚在柏林德国国家歌剧院开始。开幕式由大柏林市副市长华尔德马·施密特主持。会上演出了贝多芬的第九交响乐。
  　乌布利希等党和国家领导人，以及各国驻德意志民主共和国的外交使节都出席了开幕式。
    今年的“柏林节”文艺演出，规模空前，内容丰富多彩。节目有三百八十多项，演员达六千余人。许多节目的票现已售光。应邀参加大会演的外国艺术团已经先后到达柏林，其中有苏联乌克兰国立民间歌舞团和中国川剧团等。
    这次“柏林节”中还将举行一些艺术展览会。庆祝德意志民主共和国成立十周年造型艺术展览会今天下午开幕。格罗提渥总理出席了开幕式。第二届国际摄影展览会也在同日开幕。
