Title: First Group of Swimming and Water Polo Team from the German Democratic Republic Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-10-21
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, 20th — The first group of twenty members of the swimming and water polo team from the German Democratic Republic, invited to visit our country and participate in friendly matches, arrived in Beijing by plane today, led by Oskar Nonner, Vice Chairman of the German Democratic Republic Swimming Association. They were warmly welcomed at the airport by Li Menghua, Chairman of the Chinese Swimming Association, and Chinese swimmers.

### 德意志民主共和国游泳、水球队首批人员到京

1959-10-21
第5版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国游泳、水球队首批人员到京
    据新华社20日讯　应邀来我国访问和进行友谊比赛的德意志民主共和国游泳、水球队第一批人员二十人，今天由德意志民主共和国游泳协会副主席奥斯卡·农泽率领乘飞机到达北京。他们在飞机场上受到中国游泳协会主席李梦华和我国游泳运动员的热烈欢迎。
