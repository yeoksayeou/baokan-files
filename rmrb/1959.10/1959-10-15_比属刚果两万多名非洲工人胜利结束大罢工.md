Title: More Than 20,000 African Workers in the Belgian Congo Successfully End Strike
Author:
Date: 1959-10-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Leopoldville, October 14th: The great strike of 24,000 African transportation workers in the Belgian Congo, which lasted for eight days, ended successfully on the 13th.

This strike began on the 5th in Leopoldville and Stanleyville, and quickly spread to areas such as Matadi. The striking workers demanded wage increases from the Belgian capitalists. Due to the workers' persistent struggle, the Belgian capitalists were finally forced to agree to increase wages.

### 比属刚果两万多名非洲工人胜利结束大罢工

1959-10-15
第6版()
专栏：

    比属刚果两万多名非洲工人
    胜利结束大罢工
    新华社14日讯　利奥波德维尔消息：坚持了八天的比属刚果两万四千名非洲交通运输工人的大罢工，已经在13日胜利结束。
    这次大罢工从5日在利奥波德维尔和斯坦利维尔开始后，很快就扩大到马哈迪港等地区。罢工的工人要求比利时资方增加工资。由于工人的坚持斗争，比利时资方最后已被迫同意增加工资。
