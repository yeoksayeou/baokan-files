Workers' Words

    Slicing Difficulties in Half

　　"Whatever work is difficult,
　　we will do;
　　Whatever work is heavy, we will undertake.
　　In our hearts,
　　there is only socialism.
　　In our hands,
　　we hold the demon-slaying sword specifically for slicing difficulties in half."
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Da Tong Coal Mine Miner

    Driving Nails

    The wall always looks seamless, but if you want to drive a nail into it, you can always drive it in. Solutions are found by people. Encountering difficulties is precisely a good opportunity to find a knack.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　    Chongqing Innovation Expert Li Bingzhong

    Our Responsibility

    Guaranteeing the completion of the tasks assigned by the state is the responsibility of our working class. The greater the difficulty, the more people are tempered; the more urgent the task, the more solutions there are, and the faster the technology improves.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　    Guangdong Tractor Factory He Kang

    Potential is Dug Deeper and Deeper

    The potential in production is both limited and unlimited. When each of our members acts as a "Zhuge Liang," constantly carrying out technical revolution, the potential is like digging a well, the deeper it is dug, the bigger it gets.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　    Shanghai Worker Ying Zhongfa

### 工人的话

1959-10-19
第3版()
专栏：工人的话

　　工人的话
    腰斩困难
　　“什么活难，
　　我们去做；
　　什么活重，我们去干。
　　我们心里，
　　只有社会主义。
　　我们手上，
　　拿着专门腰斩困难的斩魔剑。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　大同煤矿矿工
　　钉钉子
    壁上看起来总是无缝的，但是你如果要往上钉钉子，总能钉进去。办法是人想出来的，遇到了困难，正是有了找窍门的好机会。
　　　　　　　　　　　　    重庆革新能手　李秉中
    我们的责任
    保证完成国家下达的任务，这是我们工人阶级的责任。困难愈大，就愈能锻炼人；任务愈急，办法就愈多，技术就提高得愈快。
　　　　　　　　　　　　    广东拖拉机厂　何康
    潜力越挖越深
    生产中的潜力是有限的又是无限的。当我们每个成员都来当“诸葛亮”，经常不断地开展技术革命的时候，潜力就像掘井一样，越掘越深，越掘越大。
　　　　　　　　　　　　    上海工人　应忠发
