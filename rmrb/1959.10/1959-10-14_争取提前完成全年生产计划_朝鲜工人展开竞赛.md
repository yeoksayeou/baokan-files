Title: Korean Workers Launch Competition to Strive for Early Fulfillment of the Annual Production Plan
Author:
Date: 1959-10-14
Page: 5

　　Striving to Fulfill the Annual Production Plan Ahead of Schedule
    Korean Workers Launch Competition
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang dispatch: Korean workers are engaged in a fervent labor competition to strive for the early and over-quota fulfillment of this year's production plan. Recently, good news has been frequently reported, and various industrial sectors have achieved significant accomplishments.
    Workers in various steel plants are vigorously shortening steelmaking time and increasing equipment utilization rates. The steelmaking workshop of the Hwanghae Iron Works shortened steelmaking time by 41 minutes compared to the plan in September, overfulfilled the September production plan, and produced more than 2,500 tons of steel than in August. The large rolling mill workshop of this factory increased its steel output in September by 30% compared to August.
    In the light industrial sector, workers at the Pyongyang Rubber Factory improved the mechanical equipment for rolling flat belts, changing from rolling one layer at a time to rolling two layers. They also built two machines for rolling belts themselves, which doubled production. By the end of September, they had completed 96% of this year's production plan. The workers are further developing the labor competition to complete the annual production task by October 20. Workers at the Pyongyang Silk Weaving Factory have adopted advanced production technologies and expanded production equipment, producing 2.4 times more silk fabrics in the first nine months of this year than in the same period last year. Workers of the Chollima Work Team of the No. 2 Silk Weaving Workshop of this factory, promoted the virtue of mutual assistance and cooperation, and rapidly improved production technology. The current production level is 80% higher than at the beginning of this year.

### 争取提前完成全年生产计划  朝鲜工人展开竞赛

1959-10-14
第5版()
专栏：

　　争取提前完成全年生产计划
    朝鲜工人展开竞赛
    据新华社平壤电　朝鲜工人为争取提前超额完成今年生产计划，展开了热火朝天的劳动竞赛。最近捷音频传，各个工业部门都取得了显著的成就。
    各钢铁厂的工人，大力缩短炼钢时间、提高设备利用率。黄海制铁所的炼钢车间9月份的炼钢时间比计划缩短了四十一分钟，超额完成了9月份的生产计划，比8月份多生产了二千五百多吨钢。这个厂的大型轧钢车间，9月份的钢材产量比8月份增加了30％。
    在轻工业部门，平壤橡胶工厂的工人改进了轧制平皮带的机械设备，由原来一次轧一层改为轧二层，另外还自造了两台轧皮带的机器，使产量猛增了一倍。他们到9月底已完成了今年生产计划的96％，工人们正进一步展开劳动竞赛，要在10月20日前完成全年的生产任务。平壤丝织厂的工人由于采用了先进生产技术，扩大了生产设备，今年头九个月生产了等于去年同期的二点四倍的丝织品。这个厂的第二织绸车间的车应子千里马作业班的工人们，发扬了互助合作的美德，迅速提高了生产技术，现在的生产水平比今年年初提高了80％。
