Title: Vice Premier Lu Dingyi Receives Uruguayan Cultural Friendship Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-10-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th: Vice Premier of the State Council Lu Dingyi received the head of the Uruguayan Cultural Friendship Delegation, President of the Uruguay-China Cultural Friendship Association Gabriela, and all members of the delegation this afternoon.

Present at the meeting was Zhou Erfu, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, who had just returned from a visit to Uruguay.

### 陆定一副总理  接见乌拉圭文化友好代表团

1959-10-28
第5版()
专栏：

    陆定一副总理
    接见乌拉圭文化友好代表团
    新华社27日讯　国务院副总理陆定一今天下午接见了乌拉圭文化友好代表团团长、乌中文化友好协会主席加勃莱拉和代表团的全体团员。
    接见时在座的有刚从乌拉圭访问回国的中国人民对外文化协会秘书长周而复。
