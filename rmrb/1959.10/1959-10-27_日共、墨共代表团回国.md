Title: JCP, Mexican CP Delegations Return Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-27
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 26th - The Japanese Communist Party delegation, headed by Comrade Sanzo Nosaka, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, left Guangzhou by special train on the evening of the 25th to return home via Hong Kong, after attending the celebrations of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and concluding its visit to our country.
    Those who saw them off at the station included Tao Zhu, member of the CPC Central Committee and First Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the CPC, Chen Yu, member of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the CPC, Wang De, First Secretary of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the CPC, Zhang Gensheng, Secretary-General of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the CPC, Zeng Zhi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the CPC, Wang Yanguang, Secretary-General of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the CPC, and others. Guan Xiangsheng, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the CPC, accompanied the delegation to Shenzhen.
    The JCP delegation arrived in Guangzhou by special plane from Beijing on the 23rd. During their stay in Guangzhou, accompanied by Chen Yu, member of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the CPC, and Wang De, First Secretary of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the CPC, the delegation visited the site of the Peasant Movement Training Institute, which was sponsored by Comrade Mao Zedong in his early years, and the Guangdong Folk Arts and Crafts Exhibition Hall, and took a boat trip on the Pearl River. Some members of the delegation also visited the Guangzhou Cultural Park.

### 日共、墨共代表团回国

1959-10-27
第5版()
专栏：

    日共、墨共代表团回国
    新华社广州26日电　以日本共产党中央委员会主席野坂参三同志为首的日本共产党代表团，在参加了中华人民共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼和结束了在我国的访问以后，于25日晚乘专车离广州取道香港回国。
    到车站欢送的有中共中央委员、中共广东省委第一书记陶铸，中共中央委员、中共广东省委书记处书记陈郁，中共广州市委第一书记王德，中共广东省委秘书长张根生，中共广州市委书记处书记曾志，中共广州市委秘书长王炎光等。中共广东省委派办公厅副主任关相生陪同代表团前往深圳。
    日共代表团是在23日由北京乘专机到达广州的。代表团在广州期间，由中共中央委员、中共广东省委书记处书记陈郁和中共广州市委第一书记王德陪同，参观了毛泽东同志早年主办的农民运动讲习所旧址和广东民间工艺展览馆，乘船游览了珠江。部分团员还游览了广州文化公园。
    新华社26日讯　墨西哥共产党代表团在参加了中华人民共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼和在我国各地参观访问之后，于今日乘飞机离开我国。
