Title: Head of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association Delegation Yang Xiufeng Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-22
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, October 21st: Yang Xiufeng, head of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association delegation and president of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association, returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon after visiting the Democratic Republic of Vietnam at the invitation of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association to participate in activities celebrating the tenth anniversary of the founding of our country. Other members of the delegation had returned to Beijing earlier.

### 中越友好协会代表团团长杨秀峰回京

1959-10-22
第5版()
专栏：

    中越友好协会代表团团长杨秀峰回京
    新华社21日讯　应越中友好协会邀请去越南民主共和国参加庆祝我国建国十周年活动的中越友好协会代表团团长、中越友好协会会长杨秀峰，今天下午乘飞机回到北京。代表团其他成员已先期返京。
