Title: Chairman Zhu De Receives the Uruguayan Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-10-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, October 29th: Chairman Zhu De received this morning the Speaker of the House of Representatives of Uruguay, Francisco Rodriguez Camusso, and all members of the Uruguayan Parliamentary Delegation he led: Dr. Angel Maria Gianola, Cemara Micelia, Juan Rodriguez Correa, Martinez Moreno, Francisco Mario Ubillos.

The guests and hosts engaged in friendly talks during the meeting.

Also present at the reception were Zhang Su and Lian Guan, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Wu Xinyu, member of the Standing Committee, Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Hu Yuzhi, Vice President, Wu Maosun, Secretary-General, and Shen Jian, Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. (Attached picture)
Chairman Zhu De and Speaker of the House of Representatives of Uruguay, Francisco Rodriguez Camusso, in cordial conversation.
\tXinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dongao

### 朱委员长接见乌拉圭议会代表团

1959-10-30
第2版()
专栏：

    朱委员长接见乌拉圭议会代表团
    新华社29日讯　朱德委员长今天上午接见了乌拉圭众议院议长弗朗西斯科·罗德里格斯·卡穆索和由他率领的乌拉圭议会代表团全体团员：安赫耳·玛丽亚·希亚诺拉博士，塞马拉·米切利亚，胡安·罗德里格斯·科雷亚，马丁内斯·莫雷诺，弗朗西科斯·马里奥·乌比略斯。
    接见时，宾主进行了友好的交谈。
    接见时在座的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏、连贯，委员武新宇，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、副会长胡愈之、秘书长吴茂荪，外交部美洲澳洲司司长申健等。（附图片）
