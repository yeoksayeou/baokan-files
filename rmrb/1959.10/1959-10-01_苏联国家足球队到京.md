Title: The Soviet National Football Team Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, 30th - The Soviet National Football team, a delegation of twenty-two people invited to visit China and attend the 10th Anniversary National Day celebrations, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.
    The seventeen members of the Soviet National Football team, led by the Secretary of the Soviet Football Association, A. Starostin, are all Master Athletes and Honored Athletes. This includes the well-known goalkeeper Yasin, forward Ivanov, and defender Kuznetsov, familiar to Chinese audiences.
    They will participate in the friendly football tournament between the Soviet Union, Hungary, and China held in Beijing after National Day.

### 苏联国家足球队到京

1959-10-01
第7版()
专栏：

    苏联国家足球队到京
    新华社30日讯　应邀来中国访问并参加国庆十周年观礼的苏联国家足球队一行二十二人，于今天下午乘机抵达北京。
    由苏联足球协会秘书长安·斯塔罗斯京率领的苏联国家足球队的十七名队员全部是运动健将和功勋运动员。其中包括中国观众所熟悉的著名守门员雅辛，前锋伊凡诺夫和后卫库兹?佐夫等。
    他们将参加国庆节后在北京举行的苏联、匈牙利和中国三国的足球友谊赛。
