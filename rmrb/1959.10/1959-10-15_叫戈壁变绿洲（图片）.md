Title: Turning the Gobi into an Oasis (Picture)
Author: Tian Ping
Date: 1959-10-15
Page: 4

The heroic people of Zhangye Special District, Gansu Province, in order to fundamentally control sandstorm disasters and build the Hexi Corridor into a grain and cotton base, are building a 5,000-kilometer-long windbreak forest belt along the three-thousand-mile sandy line. The forest belt stretches from Yiwanquan in the east to Hala Lake in Dunhuang in the west. In addition to the main forest belt, there are branch forest belts, riverbank protection forests on both sides of rivers, and field protection forests around farmland, which are interwoven to form a green barrier to block wind and sand attacks and protect farmland.

After the hard struggle of the Great Leap Forward in 1958 and this year, the Great Green Wall has taken initial shape. People sing heroically: "The Great Green Wall is truly ten thousand miles long, the insignificant Qin Emperor bows his head low, three thousand willows are like a drop in the ocean, today the spring breeze blows all over Hexi."

### 叫戈壁变绿洲（图片）
田萍
1959-10-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　叫戈壁变绿洲
    英雄的甘肃省张掖专区人民，为了根治风沙灾害，把河西走廊建成为粮棉基地，正在三千里风沙线上营造总长五千多公里的防沙林带。林带东起一碗泉，西至敦煌哈拉湖，除基干林带外，还有支干林带、河流两旁的护岸林和农田四周的护田林，交错组成绿色屏障，阻挡风沙的袭击，护育农田。
    经过大跃进的1958年和今年的艰苦奋战，绿色长城已具雏形。人们豪迈地歌颂：“绿色长城真万里，渺小秦皇把头低，三千杨柳沧海粟，今日春风遍河西。”
　　　　                田萍摄（新华社稿）
    上：为营造“万里绿色长城”所建起的林场和苗圃
    中：浩浩荡荡的造林大军向沙漠开战
    下：绿色长城已具雏形，它阻挡住沙浪的移动，捍卫着庄稼的生长
    上：勘测队在腾格里沙漠中进行林带的勘测规划
    下：中国科学院派来了治沙队在河西工作。这是工作人员观测在不同风级下含沙测定器的含沙量，为防治风沙提供资料
