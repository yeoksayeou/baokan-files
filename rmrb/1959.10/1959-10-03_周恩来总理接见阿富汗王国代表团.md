Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Delegation from the Kingdom of Afghanistan
Author:
Date: 1959-10-03
Page: 2

    Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Delegation from the Kingdom of Afghanistan
    Xinhua News Agency, October 2nd. Premier Zhou Enlai received Ghulam Muhammad Sherzad, Minister of Commerce of the Kingdom of Afghanistan and head of the delegation from the Kingdom of Afghanistan, this evening and had a cordial conversation.
    Present at the reception was Abdul Samad, the Afghan ambassador to China.
    Ye Ji-zhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, and Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, were also present. (Attached picture)
    (Right picture) Premier Zhou Enlai received Ghulam Muhammad Sherzad, Minister of Commerce of the Kingdom of Afghanistan and head of the government delegation from the Kingdom of Afghanistan, on October 2nd. The picture shows Premier Zhou shaking hands with Ghulam Muhammad Sherzad.
    Xinhua News Agency Reporter Du Xiuxian

### 周恩来总理接见阿富汗王国代表团

1959-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见阿富汗王国代表团
    新华社2日讯　周恩来总理今晚接见了阿富汗王国代表团团长阿富汗王国商业大臣吴拉姆·穆罕默德·舍尔扎德，进行了亲切的交谈。
    接见时在座的，有阿富汗驻华大使阿卜杜勒·萨马德。
    对外贸易部部长叶季壮、外交部副部长罗贵波也在座。（附图片）
    （右图）周恩来总理10月2日接见了阿富汗王国政府代表团团长、阿富汗王国商业大臣吴拉姆·穆罕默德·舍尔扎德。图为周总理和吴拉姆·穆罕默德·舍尔扎德握手。
    新华社记者　杜修贤摄
