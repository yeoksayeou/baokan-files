Title: Ceylon Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th -- The Ceylon government trade delegation, consisting of three members, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The head of the delegation is the Ceylon Ambassador to China, Mr. Gooneratne (already in Beijing), and the members are the Ceylon Food Commissioner, Mr. Jayaneetti, the Commissioner of Commodity Purchases, Mr. Ratnawear, and the Deputy Superintendent of Treasury Finance of the Ministry of Finance, Mr. Weeratunge.

Among those who went to the airport to greet them were the relevant responsible persons from the Ministry of Foreign Trade and diplomatic officials from the Ceylon Embassy in China.

### 锡兰政府贸易代表团到京

1959-10-29
第5版()
专栏：

    锡兰政府贸易代表团到京
    新华社27日讯　锡兰政府贸易代表团一行三人今天乘飞机到达北京。代表团团长为锡兰驻华大使高伯拉瓦（已在北京），团员为锡兰粮食专员贾亚内蒂、商品采购专员拉特纳瓦耳和财政部国库财政副监督维拉通格。
  前往机场迎接的，有对外贸易部的有关负责人和锡兰驻华大使馆的外交官员。
