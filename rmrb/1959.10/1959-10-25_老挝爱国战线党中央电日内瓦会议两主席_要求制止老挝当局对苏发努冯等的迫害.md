Title: Lao Patriotic Front Central Committee Cables Geneva Conference Co-Chairmen, Demanding Cessation of Lao Authorities' Persecution of Souphanouvong, et al.
Author:
Date: 1959-10-25
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, October 24th: The Vietnam News Agency reports that Phay Dang, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front, on behalf of the Party's Central Committee, sent a telegram on the 21st to the two chairmen of the 1954 Geneva Conference—Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko and British Foreign Secretary Lloyd—regarding the illegal act of the Phoui Sananikone government preparing to "try" Prince Souphanouvong and seven other leaders of the Lao Patriotic Front.

The telegram stated, "We solemnly and urgently inform you that, according to numerous reports from abroad, the Phoui Sananikone government will submit Prince Souphanouvong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front, Member of the National Assembly, former Minister of the Coalition Government, and seven other leaders of the Lao Patriotic Front to a court "trial" on October 26th. This event, like the Phoui Sananikone-led delegation of the Kingdom of Laos to New York requesting the United Nations to dispatch a United Nations standing committee to Laos, is a decisive new step in the entire conspiracy of US imperialism to eliminate the leadership of the Lao Patriotic Front, abolish the Lao Patriotic Front's legal status, strike at the fighting spirit of the Lao people, and at the same time obstruct all measures for a peaceful resolution of the Lao issue. The planners of this case hope that by doing so, they can unscrupulously further expand the civil war, use force to eliminate the patriotic and progressive forces in Laos, and create tension in Indochina and Southeast Asia. This illegal 'trial' is an extremely serious violation of the Geneva Accords and the Vientiane Agreements, which affirmed the great contributions of the Lao fighting forces in the struggle for independence and recognized the legal rights and status of the Lao Patriotic Front and former resistance personnel."

The telegram said, "We, on behalf of the Lao Patriotic Front—the successor organization to the Lao fighting forces, as a signatory to the Geneva Accords and the Vientiane Agreements on the Laotian issue—strongly oppose the aforementioned 'trial.' We request the two chairmen of the Geneva Conference on Indochina to take urgent measures to compel the Phoui Sananikone government to immediately cease this illegal 'trial' and restore the freedom of Prince Souphanouvong and other leaders of the Lao Patriotic Front; the Laotian issue must be resolved on the basis and within the scope of the Geneva Accords and the Vientiane Agreements; all acts of interference by US imperialism in Laos must cease."

On the same day, Phay Dang also wrote a letter with similar content to Indian Prime Minister Nehru.

### 老挝爱国战线党中央电日内瓦会议两主席  要求制止老挝当局对苏发努冯等的迫害

1959-10-25
第3版()
专栏：

    老挝爱国战线党中央电日内瓦会议两主席
    要求制止老挝当局对苏发努冯等的迫害
    新华社河内24日电　据越南通讯社报道，老挝爱国战线党中央委员会副主席费当代表党的中央委员会，在21日就培·萨纳尼空政府准备“审讯”苏发努冯亲王和老挝爱国战线党其他七位领导人的非法行为，打电报给1954年日内瓦会议两位主席——苏联外交部长葛罗米柯和英国外交大臣劳埃德。
    电报说，我们谨郑重地、紧急地通知你们，据来自外国的许多消息，培·萨纳尼空政府将在10月26日把老挝爱国战线党中央委员会主席、国民议会议员、前联合政府部长苏发努冯亲王和老挝爱国战线党其他七位领导人提交法庭“审讯”。这事件同培·萨纳尼空率领老挝王国代表团前往纽约要求联合国派出一个联合国常驻老挝的小组委员会的事件一样，是美帝国主义妄图消灭老挝爱国战线党的领导机关、取消老挝爱国战线党的合法地位、打击老挝人民的斗争精神、同时阻挠和平解决老挝问题的一切措施的全部阴谋中的一个决定性的新步骤。这一案件的策划者妄想这样做就可以肆无忌惮地进一步扩大内战，用武力消灭老挝的爱国和进步力量，制造印度支那和东南亚紧张局势。这一非法的“审讯”是对日内瓦协议和万象协议的极其严重的破坏行为；而这些协议肯定了寮国战斗部队在争取独立的斗争中的巨大功劳，承认老挝爱国战线党和前抗战人员在法律上的权利和合法地位。
    电报说，我们代表老挝爱国战线党——寮国战斗部队的继承组织，作为关于老挝问题的日内瓦协议、万象各项协议签字的一方，强烈反对上述的“审讯”。我们要求关于印度支那问题的日内瓦会议两位主席采取紧急措施，迫使培·萨纳尼空政府立即停止这一非法的“审讯”，并且恢复苏发努冯亲王和老挝爱国战线党其他领导人的自由；老挝问题必须在日内瓦协议、万象各项协议的基础上和范围内加以解决；美帝国主义对老挝的一切干涉行为必须停止。
    费当在这一天并且写了一封内容类似的信给印度总理尼赫鲁。
