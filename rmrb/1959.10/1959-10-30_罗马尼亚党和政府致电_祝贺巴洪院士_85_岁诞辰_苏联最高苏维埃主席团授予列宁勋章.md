Title: Romanian Party and Government Send Telegrams to Congratulate Academician Parhon on His 85th Birthday; USSR Supreme Soviet Presidium Awards Order of Lenin
Author:
Date: 1959-10-30
Page: 5

    Bucharest dispatch of this newspaper, October 29th -- October 28th was the eighty-fifth birthday of the famous Romanian scholar, honorary president of the Romanian Academy of Sciences, chairman of the Romanian-Soviet Friendship Association, and outstanding social activist, Academician C.I. Parhon.
    The Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, the Council of Ministers, and the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly jointly sent a telegram of congratulations.
    To recognize his long-term and fruitful activities in strengthening Romanian-Soviet friendship and cultural cooperation, the Presidium of the USSR Supreme Soviet awarded him the Order of Lenin.

### 罗马尼亚党和政府致电  祝贺巴洪院士85岁诞辰  苏联最高苏维埃主席团授予列宁勋章

1959-10-30
第5版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚党和政府致电
    祝贺巴洪院士85岁诞辰
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团授予列宁勋章
    本报布加勒斯特29日电　10月28日是罗马尼亚著名学者、罗马尼亚科学院名誉院长、罗苏友协主席、杰出的社会活动家切·依·巴洪院士八十五岁的生日。
    罗马尼亚工人党中央、部长会议、大国民议会主席团联名致电祝贺。
    为表彰他在加强罗苏友谊和文化合作方面长期的卓有成效的活动，苏联最高苏维埃主席团授给他列宁勋章。
