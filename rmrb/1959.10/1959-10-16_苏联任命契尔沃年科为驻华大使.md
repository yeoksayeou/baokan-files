Title: USSR Appoints Chervonenko as Ambassador to China
Author:
Date: 1959-10-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 15th: Stepan Vasilyevich Chervonenko has been appointed as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to the People's Republic of China. The former ambassador, P. Yudin, has been transferred to another post.

### 苏联任命契尔沃年科为驻华大使

1959-10-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联任命契尔沃年科为驻华大使
    新华社莫斯科15日电　斯捷潘·瓦西里耶维奇·契尔沃年科被任命为苏联驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使。原任大使帕·尤金已另调新职。
