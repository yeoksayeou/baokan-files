Title: Romanian Rowing Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-25
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, October 24th – The Romanian men's and women's rowing team, a group of twenty-seven people led by team leader Haim Rabapo, arrived in Beijing today for a visit and friendly matches in our country. This is the first foreign rowing team invited to visit our country. The guest team will go to Wuhan on the 25th to prepare for friendly matches with our rowing athletes there on October 31st and November 1st.

### 罗划船队到京

1959-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

    罗划船队到京
    据新华社24日讯　应邀来我国访问和进行友谊比赛的罗马尼亚男、女划船队一行二十七人，今天由领队哈伊姆·腊巴波率领来到北京。这是应邀来我国访问的第一个外国的划船队。客队将在25日去武汉，准备10月31日和11月1日在那里同我国的划船运动员举行友谊比赛。
