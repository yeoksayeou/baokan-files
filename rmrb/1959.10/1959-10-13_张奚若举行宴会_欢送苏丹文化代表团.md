Title: Zhang Xiruo Hosts Banquet to Bid Farewell to Sudanese Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-10-13
Page: 5

　　Zhang Xiruo Hosts Banquet
    To Bid Farewell to Sudanese Cultural Delegation

    Xinhua News Agency, 12th - Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, hosted a banquet this evening to bid farewell to the Sudanese Cultural Delegation, which had visited our country and participated in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the nation. The delegation will leave Beijing on the 13th to return to their country.

    In his speech at the banquet, Zhang Xiruo warmly wished for the continuous strengthening of the friendship and cultural relations between the people of China and Sudan, and congratulated the unity of the Asian and African people. The head of the Sudanese Cultural Delegation, A.I.M. Gubara, who came to our country on behalf of the Government of the Republic of Sudan to participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the nation, repeatedly expressed his gratitude for the warm reception from the hosts. He stated that he would bring the sincere friendship of the Chinese people back to the Sudanese people.

    Among those present at the banquet were Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ding Xilin, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Lin Liru, Vice Minister of Education, and heads of relevant departments.

### 张奚若举行宴会  欢送苏丹文化代表团

1959-10-13
第5版()
专栏：

　　张奚若举行宴会
    欢送苏丹文化代表团
    新华社12日讯　对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若今天晚间举行宴会欢送前来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的苏丹文化代表团。代表团将在13日离开北京回国。
    张奚若在宴会上致词，热情地祝中国苏丹两国人民间的友谊和文化关系日益增强，祝贺亚非人民的团结。代表苏丹共和国政府来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的苏丹文化代表团团长阿·依·墨·古巴拉在宴会上一再感谢主人们的热诚接待，他表示要把中国人民的真挚友情带回给苏丹人民。
    出席宴会的，有外交部副部长章汉夫、对外文化联络委员会副主任丁西林、教育部副部长林砺儒以及各有关部门的负责人。
