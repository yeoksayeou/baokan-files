Title: Soviet Primorsky Krai Labor Delegation Leaves Changchun for Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, October 3rd. The Soviet Primorsky Krai Labor Delegation, invited by the Jilin Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Jilin Provincial People's Committee to attend the Jilin Province and Changchun City people's celebrations of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, left Changchun by train on the evening of the 2nd to return home.

Leading figures from the Jilin Province and City Party, government, military, and people's organizations went to the station to see them off.

### 苏联沿海边区劳动人民代表团离长春回国

1959-10-04
第2版()
专栏：

    苏联沿海边区劳动人民代表团离长春回国
    新华社长春3日电　应中共吉林省委、吉林省人民委员会邀请前来参加吉林省和长春市人民庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年大会的苏联沿海边区劳动人民代表团，于2日晚乘火车离开长春回国。
    前往车站欢送的，有吉林省、市的党、政、军和人民团体负责人。
