Title: The Night of the Commune
Author: Yan Ming
Date: 1959-10-25
Page: 8

    The Night of the Commune
    Yan Ming
    In front of the radio
    ——Listening to the General Chorus's Choral

  In the club,
  The generals' chorus,
  Resounds like an overwhelming force.
  The generals,
  With one hand, push away suffering,
  And with the other, hold up happiness. The banner of Jinggang Mountain, the flute of Taihang Mountain, fly high in the commune...

    In front of the television
    ——Watching Soviet ballet like the morning glow, shining here; like a sea of stars, sparkling here. They are transparent clouds, drifting over piece by piece; they are white snow lotuses, blooming cluster after cluster. Ah! The fairies, spread their wings of love, and descend gently in the joyous commune.

    The evening party begins. The loudspeaker shakes down the sky full of stars. The spotlight is a bright moon, hanging at the village entrance. The laughter lifts up the village, which is brighter than daytime here. The evening party begins, the girls' small chorus, already floats in the boys' hearts...

      October 1959, Beijing suburbs.

### 公社的夜
晏明
1959-10-25
第8版()
专栏：

    公社的夜
    晏明
    收音机前
    ——听将军合唱团大合唱
  俱乐部里，
  将军的合唱，
  排山倒海般响起。
  将军们，
  一手推开苦难，
  一手把幸福捧起。井冈山的大旗，太行山的短笛，在公社里高高飘起……
    电视机前
    ——看苏联芭蕾舞像朝霞，在这里闪光；像星海，在这里发亮。是透明的云，朵朵飘来；是白色的雪莲，丛丛盛开。呵！仙女们，张开爱情的翅膀，在欢乐的公社，温柔地下降。
    晚会开始了扩音器，震落了，满天星斗。聚光灯，是一轮明月，挂在村头。欢笑声，抬起了村庄，这里比白昼还明亮。晚会开始了，姑娘们的小合唱，早在小伙子心里飘荡……
      1959年10月京郊。
