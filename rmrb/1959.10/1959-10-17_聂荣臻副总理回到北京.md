Title: Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-17
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. The Chinese Party and Government Delegation, led by Nie Rongzhen, member of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon after attending the celebrations for the 10th anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic.

Chen Yi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, Han Guang, Deputy Director of the Science and Technology Commission, Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Luo Shuzhang, Vice Minister of Light Industry, were at the airport to greet them.

Wang Guozhen, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, was also at the airport to greet them.

### 聂荣臻副总理回到北京

1959-10-17
第2版()
专栏：

　　聂荣臻副总理回到北京
    新华社16日讯　由中共中央委员、国务院副总理聂荣臻率领的我国党政代表团，在参加德意志民主共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼后，今天下午乘飞机回到北京。
    中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅，科学技术委员会副主任韩光，外交部副部长姬鹏飞、轻工业部副部长罗叔章等到机场迎接。
    德意志民主共和国驻华大使汪戴尔也到机场迎接。
