Title: CPPCC Standing Committee Holds Enlarged Meeting; Listens to Liao Luyan's Report on Agricultural Issues
Author:
Date: 1959-10-25
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, October 24th. The Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference held an enlarged meeting this afternoon.
    Minister of Agriculture Liao Luyan delivered a report on agricultural issues at the enlarged meeting. This report was made to coordinate with the study of the documents of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the CPC by the democratic parties and other units. In the future, the CPPCC National Committee will organize a series of reports on economic construction issues.
    Bao Erhan, Vice Chairman of the CPPCC National Committee, presided over today's meeting. Members of the Standing Committee of the CPPCC National Committee attended the meeting.
    Members of the CPPCC National Committee in Beijing, members of the Central Committees of various democratic parties, and members of the Beijing Municipal CPPCC also attended the meeting to listen to the report.

### 政协常委会举行扩大会议  听取廖鲁言关于农业问题报告

1959-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    政协常委会举行扩大会议
    听取廖鲁言关于农业问题报告
    新华社24日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会今天下午举行扩大会议。
    农业部部长廖鲁言在扩大会议上作了关于农业问题的报告。这个报告是为了配合各民主党派等单位对中共八届八中全会文件的学习而进行的。今后政协全国委员会还将组织一系列有关经济建设问题的报告。
    政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉主持今天的会议。出席会议的，有政协全国委员会常务委员会委员。
    在北京的政协全国委员会委员、各民主党派的中央委员、北京市政协的委员等也到会听报告。
