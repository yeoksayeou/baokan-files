Title: US Launches Small Satellite Weighing Less Than Fifty Kilograms
Author:
Date: 1959-10-15
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, October 14th dispatch from Cape Canaveral: The United States launched a small artificial Earth satellite, "Explorer VII," at Cape Canaveral on the 13th.

This artificial satellite weighs only 41.7 kilograms, less than half the weight of the first artificial satellite launched by the Soviet Union. Its orbit around the Earth has an apogee of 1,070 kilometers above the ground and a perigee of 557 kilometers. It takes 101 minutes to orbit the Earth once. It is said that this satellite was launched using a four-stage rocket.

### 美国放出小卫星  体重不到五十公斤

1959-10-15
第6版()
专栏：

    美国放出小卫星
    体重不到五十公斤
    新华社14日讯　卡纳维拉尔角消息：美国13日在卡纳维拉尔角发射了一个小人造地球卫星“探险者七号”。
    这个人造卫星只有四十一点七公斤重，还不及苏联发射的第一个人造卫星的一半。它围绕地球运行的轨道的远地点距地面一千零七十公里，近地点五百五十七公里。绕地球一周的时间是一百零一分钟。据说，这个卫星是用一支四级火箭发射上去的。
