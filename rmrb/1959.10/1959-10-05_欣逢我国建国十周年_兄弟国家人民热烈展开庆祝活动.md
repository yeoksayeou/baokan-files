Title: To Celebrate the Tenth Anniversary of Our Country's Founding, Brotherly Countries' People Enthusiastically Launch Celebration Activities
Author: 
Date: 1959-10-05
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, October 3rd: Around the time of our country's tenth anniversary, socialist brotherly countries have launched multifaceted activities to celebrate this festival.

    **Soviet Union**
    On October 1st, the capital of Azerbaijan, Baku, Tallinn (Estonia), Yerevan (Armenia), Novosibirsk, Leningrad, Tbilisi (Georgia), and many other cities and villages in the Soviet Union held grand rallies and Soviet-Chinese friendship evenings. According to the resolution of the Tbilisi City Workers' Deputies Soviet, a new central avenue in the city was renamed Beijing Avenue. Many republic newspapers prominently published materials on October 1st regarding the unbreakable friendship and fraternal cooperation between the Chinese and Soviet people.
    To celebrate the tenth anniversary of our country's founding, the large-scale choral work "Forever Together," composed by Soviet composer Muradeli, premiered at a concert held at the Kremlin Theater on the 1st.

    **Vietnam**
    In recent days, rallies have been held in various locations to celebrate the tenth anniversary of our country's founding. More than 7,000 employees of the Vietnam Railways General Administration held a celebration conference in Hanoi on the 1st. On September 29th and 30th, more than 8,000 people's representatives in Haiphong, nearly 10,000 in Nam Dinh, 15,000 in Thanh Hoa, and more than 3,000 in the Thai-Meo Autonomous Region also held conferences to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

    **German Democratic Republic**
    The "Socialist Construction Exhibition of the People's Republic of China," organized by the German Democratic Republic's Association for Foreign Cultural Relations to celebrate the tenth anniversary of our country's founding, opened in Berlin on the afternoon of the 1st.
    Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Bolz, Chairman of the Central Committee's Cultural Committee of the Socialist Unity Party, Kurera, and others attended the opening ceremony.
    People throughout the German Democratic Republic have been enthusiastically celebrating the tenth anniversary of our country's founding in recent days. Many cities have held celebrations. Newspapers in various regions of the country have recently published articles or printed pictures introducing China's achievements over the past decade and the friendship between the Chinese and German people.

    **Poland**
    In Warsaw, the capital of Poland, the premiere of the Chinese film "Frontier Beacon Fire" was held on the evening of the 2nd. Speaker of the Polish Parliament, Wicek, as well as prominent figures from all walks of life and foreign envoys to Poland, attended the opening ceremony. Cinemas in Lodz, Gdansk, and other places are showing Chinese films. In recent days, provinces, cities, counties, major enterprises, and institutions have held celebrations to celebrate the tenth anniversary of our country's founding. Many places have also designated October as the Polish-Chinese Friendship Month.

    **Czechoslovakia**
    The working people in various locations continue to hold rallies to warmly celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. As of the 1st, 18 out of the 19 states in the country had held celebration conferences.
    At the celebration conferences held in various locations, the heads of many states spoke at the meetings. Many factories also put forward new production guarantees to celebrate the tenth anniversary of our country's founding. In particular, those factories that manufacture orders for China expressed their intention to deliver products ahead of schedule to support China's socialist construction and to celebrate China's festival with this practical action.

    **Romania**
    The "Exhibition of Achievements in China's Economic Construction" and the "Exhibition of Chinese Books," held to celebrate the tenth anniversary of our country's founding, opened in Bucharest on September 30th. In addition, in Bucharest's "Stalin" Park, the Mao Zedong Machine Manufacturing Factory, and the "August 23rd" Heavy Machinery Manufacturing Factory, exhibitions of pictures of China's construction achievements have recently been organized. The "Oriental Red" agricultural production cooperative in Elin Pelin village near Sofia, the capital of Bulgaria, which has close friendly ties with the Red Star People's Commune in the suburbs of Beijing, held a Chinese-Bulgarian friendship evening on the evening of September 29th to warmly celebrate the tenth anniversary of our country's founding.
    Bogdanov, the Party Committee Secretary of the "Oriental Red" Agricultural Cooperative, said in his speech that the general line for building socialism formulated by the Chinese Communist Party and Comrade Mao Zedong is a concentrated expression of the revolutionary will and determination of the entire Chinese people, and it links objective possibilities with the revolutionary enthusiasm of hundreds of millions of people. Speaking of China's completion of the main production targets of the Second Five-Year Plan this year, he said: "This is a real great leap forward in China's economic development."

    **Albania**
    The "Exhibition of the People's Republic of China," organized by the Albanian Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations to celebrate the tenth anniversary of our country's founding, opened in Tirana on the afternoon of the 1st. Albanian Deputy Minister of Education and Culture, Mitroyorgi, presided over the opening ceremony. Member of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Secretary of the Secretariat, Kabo, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Chairman of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association, Kereci, and others attended the opening ceremony.

### 欣逢我国建国十周年  兄弟国家人民热烈展开庆祝活动

1959-10-05
第6版()
专栏：

    欣逢我国建国十周年
    兄弟国家人民热烈展开庆祝活动
    据新华社3日讯　在我建国十周年的前后，社会主义兄弟国家中开展了多方面的庆祝这个节日的活动。
    苏联
    阿塞拜疆首都巴库、塔林（爱沙尼亚）、埃里温（亚美尼亚）、新西伯利亚城、列宁格勒、第比利斯（格鲁吉亚），以及苏联的许多其他城市和乡村，10月1日都举行了隆重的集会和苏中友谊晚会。根据第比利斯市劳动者代表苏维埃的决议，把市的一条新的中心大街改名为北京大街。许多共和国报纸10月1日都在显著地位登载了有关中国和苏联人民的牢不可破的友谊和兄弟般的合作的材料。
    为庆祝我建国十周年，苏联作曲家穆拉杰利创作的大型合唱曲“永远在一起”，1日在克里姆林剧院举行的音乐会上首次演出。
    越南
    近几天来各地举行了庆祝我建国十周年的集会。越南铁路总局职工七千多人1日在河内举行了庆祝大会。9月29日和30日，海防人民代表八千多人、南定近一万人、清化一万五千人、傣族—苗族自治区三千多人也分别举行大会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年。
    德意志民主共和国
    德意志民主共和国对外文化联络协会为庆祝我建国十周年而举办的“中华人民共和国社会主义建设展览会”，1日下午在柏林开幕。
    德意志民主共和国副总理兼外交部长博尔茨、统一社会党中央委员会文化委员会主席库雷拉等出席了开幕式。
    德意志民主共和国各地人民连日来热情庆祝我建国十周年。许多城市都举行了庆祝会。全国各个专区的报纸最近都分别发表文章或刊登图片，介绍十年来中国的成就和中德两国人民的友谊。
    波兰
    在波兰首都华沙，2日晚举行了中国影片“边寨烽火”首映开幕式。波议会议长维策赫、及各界著名人士和各国驻波使节出席了开幕式。罗兹、革但斯克等地的影院都在上映中国影片。各省、市以至各县、各大企业、各机关，近日来都纷纷举行庆祝我建国十周年的集会。许多地方还把10月份定为波中友好月。
    捷克斯洛伐克
    各地劳动人民继续举行集会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年。到1日为止，在全国十九个州中已有十八个州举行了庆祝大会。
    在各地举行的庆祝大会上，许多州的负责人都在会上讲了话。不少工厂还为庆祝我建国十周年提出了新的生产保证，特别是那些为中国制造订货的工厂都表示要提前交出产品，以支援中国的社会主义建设，并以这种实际行动来庆祝中国的节日。
    罗马尼亚
    为庆祝我建国十周年而举办的“中国经济建设成就图片展览会”和“中国书籍展览会”，9月30日在布加勒斯特开幕。此外，在布加勒斯特“斯大林”公园、毛泽东机器制造厂、“8月23日”重型机器制造厂最近也组织了中国建设成就图片展览会。保加利亚与北京市郊区的红星人民公社有着密切友谊联系的保加利亚首都索非亚附近的埃林彼林村“东方红”农业生产合作社，9月29日晚举行了中保友谊晚会，热烈庆祝我建国十周年。
    “东方红”农业社党委书记波格达诺夫在讲话中说，中国共产党和毛泽东同志制订的建设社会主义的总路线是全中国人民的革命意志和决心的集中表现，它把客观可能性与亿万人民群众的革命积极性联结了起来。他在谈到中国将在今年完成第二个五年计划的主要生产指标时说：“这是中国经济发展的真正大跃进。”
    阿尔巴尼亚
  　阿尔巴尼亚对外文化联络委员会为庆祝我建国十周年而举办的“中华人民共和国展览会”，1日下午在地拉那开幕。阿尔巴尼亚教育和文化部副部长米特罗约尔基主持了开幕式。阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员、书记处书记卡博、部长会议副主席、阿中友好协会主席凯列齐等出席了开幕式。
