International Briefs

    Moroccan King Receives Algerian Prime Minister
    Rabat Dispatch: King Mohammed V of Morocco received Ferhat Abbas, Prime Minister of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, on the afternoon of the 14th. Also present at the meeting were Moroccan Crown Prince Moulay Hassan and Algerian Government Minister for North African Affairs, Mehri.
    According to a report from the United Nations by a reporter from the Moroccan newspaper "Liberation," Moroccan Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Ibrahim met with United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld and informed him that the King and Government of Morocco were willing to do their best to facilitate direct contact between the Algerian Government and the French Government.

    Singapore Government Releases a Group of Political Prisoners
    Singapore Dispatch: The Singapore Ministry of Home Affairs announced on the 13th the release of twenty-two political prisoners. Most of them are students, the rest are workers, and one is the secretary of a local organization of the People's Action Party.

    国际简讯
    摩国王接见阿尔及利亚总理
    拉巴特讯　摩洛哥国王穆罕默德五世14日下午接见了阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府总理费尔哈特·阿巴斯。接见时在场的有摩洛哥王储穆莱·哈桑和阿尔及利亚政府北非事务部长迈赫里。
    据摩洛哥“解放报”记者从联合国发来的消息说，摩洛哥首相兼外交大臣易卜拉欣会见了联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德，并通知他，摩洛哥国王和政府愿意尽力促使阿尔及利亚政府和法国政府进行直接接触。
    新加坡政府释放一批政治犯
    新加坡讯　新加坡内政部13日宣布释放二十二名政治犯。其中大部分是学生，其余是工人，有一名是人民行动党地方组织的秘书。
