Title: Telegram from the Popular Socialist Party of Cuba
Author: Blas Roca
Date: 1959-10-03
Page: 3

    Telegram from the Popular Socialist Party of Cuba to Mao Zedong
    The Popular Socialist Party warmly celebrates the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and respectfully extends congratulations to the Chinese people and their leaders, headed by Mao Zedong. Congratulations on ending the humiliation of the nation, ending feudalism and backwardness, and on the great leaps forward achieved in the socialist system, industrial development, people's communes, and cultural development. We salute China as a great bastion of peace and anti-imperialism, whose existence and friendship are a great aid to our Cuban revolution.
    Central Committee of the Popular Socialist Party Juan Marinello
                                                      Blas Roca

### 古巴人民社会党的贺电
布拉斯·罗加
1959-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    古巴人民社会党的贺电毛泽东
    人民社会党热情庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年，并谨向中国人民及其以毛泽东为首的领导人致贺。祝贺你们结束了国家被凌辱的局面，结束了封建主义和落后，并在社会主义制度、工业发展、人民公社和文化发展方面取得的巨大跃进。谨向作为和平、反帝的伟大堡垒的中国致敬，它的存在和友谊对我们古巴革命是巨大的援助。
    人民社会党中央委员会  胡安·马里内略
                            布拉斯·罗加
