Title: Hungarian National Fencing Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-31
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, October 30th: The Hungarian National Men's and Women's Fencing Team, a delegation of twenty-one people led by team leader Geresh Tibor, arrived in Beijing today. They are the first foreign fencing team invited to China to perform and compete, and will hold a friendly match with Chinese fencers in Beijing on November 4th. The Hungarian fencing team includes fifteen male and female athletes, four of whom are female athletes who won the women's team foil championship at this year's World Fencing Championships.

### 匈国家击剑队到京

1959-10-31
第5版()
专栏：

    匈国家击剑队到京
    据新华社30日讯　具有世界水平的匈牙利国家男女击剑队一行二十一人，今天由领队格雷什·蒂博尔率领到达北京。他们是第一个应邀来我国进行表演比赛的外国击剑队，将于11月4日在北京同我国击剑选手举行友谊赛。匈牙利击剑队中包括十五名男女运动员。其中四名女运动员是荣获今年世界击剑锦标赛女子轻剑团体冠军的选手。
