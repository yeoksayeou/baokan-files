Title: Precision Coordinate Boring Machine: Machining Parts with Precision Up to One-Seventh the Thickness of a Hair

Author:

Date: 1959-10-10
Page: 3

1959 New Products

    Precision Coordinate Boring Machine
    Machining Parts with Precision Up to One-Seventh the Thickness of a Hair
    (Our dispatch) The Kunming Machine Tool Plant has recently successfully trial-produced a precision coordinate boring machine controlled by optics. It is a specialized machine tool with extremely high precision, used for machining holes and flat surfaces of aircraft and automobile parts. The bore distance precision of parts machined by this machine tool can reach 0.01 millimeters (i.e., one-seventh the thickness of a human hair). Workers must install it in a dust-free, temperature-controlled room for operation.
    What makes this modern boring machine superior to ordinary boring machines is that it has a coordinate system composed of optical instruments that can magnify the processed parts eighty times, thus ensuring extremely accurate machining movement. In order to flexibly process various parts of the components, it is equipped with a circular worktable that can be converted 360 degrees and an inclined worktable that can be tilted 90 degrees. In addition, this new boring machine is also equipped with a comprehensive variable speed device of DC motor and gears, unlike ordinary boring machines that simply use gear speed, allowing for a more rational selection of cutting parameters.
    Currently, this boring machine is on display at the National Industrial and Transportation Exhibition in Beijing. (Text) (With picture)

### 精密座标镗床  加工部件的精密度达到头发丝的七分之一

1959-10-10
第3版()
专栏：1959年的新产品

    精密座标镗床
    加工部件的精密度达到头发丝的七分之一
    本报讯　昆明机床厂最近试制成功一种利用光学控制的精密座标镗床。它是一种精密度极高、加工飞机、汽车零件孔平面的专用机床。这种机床加工部件的孔矩精密度可以达到零点零一毫米（即一根头发丝的七分之一）。工人们必须把它安装到一尘不染的恒温室里进行操作。
    这种现代化的镗床优异于普通镗床的是：它有一套光学仪器组成的座标，可将加工的部件放大八十倍，因而能保证加工移动量十分准确。为了灵活地加工部件的各个部分，它备有一个可转换三百六十度的圆形工作台和一个能倾斜九十度的斜形工作台。另外，这台新式镗床还安装有直流电动机和齿轮的综合变速装置，不像普通镗床那样单纯用齿轮转速，可以更加合理地选择切削用量。
    目前，北京全国工业交通展览会上正展出这种镗床。　（文）（附图片）
