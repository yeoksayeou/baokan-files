Title: My Ambassador to Iraq Visits Qassem
Author:
Date: 1959-10-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, 11th – Chen Zhifang, the Chinese Ambassador to Iraq, visited Prime Minister Qassem at Peace Hospital this afternoon to express his condolences.

Prime Minister Qassem expressed his gratitude for Premier Zhou Enlai's message of condolence and thanked the Chinese government and people for their concern. He said, "The conspiracies of imperialism and reactionaries have promoted the friendship and cooperation between the people of (Iraq and China)." He pointed out that the various conspiracies of imperialism and reactionaries will not succeed.

### 我驻伊大使探望卡塞姆

1959-10-13
第6版()
专栏：

　　我驻伊大使探望卡塞姆
    新华社巴格达11日电　中国驻伊拉克大使陈志方今天下午到和平医院去探望卡塞姆总理，向他表示慰问。
    卡塞姆总理对周恩来总理致电慰问表示感谢，并感谢中国政府和人民的关怀。他说，“帝国主义和反动派的阴谋促进了（伊、中）两国人民的友好和合作。”他指出，帝国主义和反动派的各种阴谋是不会得逞的。
