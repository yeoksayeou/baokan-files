Title: Pakistan Begins Moving Capital

Author: 
Date: 1959-10-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, October 20th: The work of Pakistan moving its capital from Karachi to Rawalpindi began today. The special train carrying the first group of officials of the central government left here this afternoon for Rawalpindi. Pakistani President Ayub Khan will accompany his headquarters to Rawalpindi on October 24.

### 巴基斯坦开始迁都

1959-10-22
第6版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦开始迁都
    据新华社卡拉奇20日电　巴基斯坦把首都从卡拉奇迁到拉瓦尔品第的工作在今天开始，中央政府的第一批官员所乘的专车已在这天下午离开这里去拉瓦尔品第。巴基斯坦总统阿尤布汗将在10月24日随同他的总部前往拉瓦尔品第。
