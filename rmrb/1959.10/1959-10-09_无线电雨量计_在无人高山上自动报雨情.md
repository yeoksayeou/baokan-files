Title: Radio Rain Gauge: Automatically Reports Rainfall Conditions on Unmanned High Mountains
Author: Zhou Yiqi
Date: 1959-10-09
Page: 2

Radio Rain Gauge
Automatically Reports Rainfall Conditions on Unmanned High Mountains

The Nanjing Water Conservancy and Hydroelectric Instrument Factory recently trial-produced a radio automatic reporting rain gauge, which is a great joy for China's hydrological and meteorological undertakings. It has a complete set of automated equipment that can automatically and accurately report rainfall conditions in remote areas. With this radio rain gauge, "unmanned rain gauge stations" can be established in China, and rainfall conditions can be automatically reported in high mountains where no one can live.

In designing the automatic control system for this product, the factory's employees absorbed opinions from relevant parties across the country, searched for information separately, and everyone put forward new plans. After more than ten technical discussions, they finally integrated the advantages of various plans and designed a complete set of equipment with a "memory" device, a "sunny-rain switch" device, and an automatic recording process.
(Zhou Yiqi)

### 无线电雨量计  在无人高山上自动报雨情
周益祺
1959-10-09
第2版()
专栏：1959年的新产品

    无线电雨量计
    在无人高山上自动报雨情
    南京水利电力仪表厂最近试制成一种无线电自动发报雨量计，这是我国水文气象事业上的一件大喜事。它有一套完全自动化的设备，可以在辽远的地区自动地准确地报告雨情，有了这种无线电雨量计即可在我国建立“无人雨量站”，可以在高山无人可住的地方自动报告雨情。
    这个厂的职工在设计这个产品的自动控制系统中，曾吸收了全国各地有关方面的意见，分头寻找资料，人人提出新方案，经过十多次技术讨论，最后才综合了各种方案的优点，设计出一套有“记忆”装置、“晴雨开关”装置以及自动记录过程的完整设备。
                              （周益祺）
