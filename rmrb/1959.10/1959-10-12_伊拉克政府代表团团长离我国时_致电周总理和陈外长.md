Title: Iraqi Government Delegation Head Sends Telegram to Premier Zhou and Foreign Minister Chen Upon Leaving Our Country
Author:
Date: 1959-10-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 11th — The head of the Iraqi government delegation, Ahmed Mohammed Yehia, sent telegrams to Premier Zhou Enlai and Foreign Minister Chen Yi respectively upon leaving our country, thanking them and the delegation for the reception they received in China. The telegram stated: "Upon leaving your beautiful and glorious capital, I, on behalf of the Iraqi government delegation and in my own name, express sincere and deep gratitude for the hospitality and friendly reception you have extended to us, and wish Your Excellency, the great Chinese people, and the government further achievements, happiness, and tranquility."

### 伊拉克政府代表团团长离我国时  致电周总理和陈外长

1959-10-12
第5版()
专栏：

　　伊拉克政府代表团团长离我国时
    致电周总理和陈外长
    新华社11日讯　伊拉克政府代表团团长艾哈迈德·穆罕默德·叶海亚在离开我国的时候，分别致电周恩来总理和陈毅外交部长，感谢他和代表团在中国受到的接待，电文说：“在离开你们美丽和光荣的首都的时候，我代表伊拉克政府代表团并以我个人的名义，对你们给予我们的款待和友好的接待表示诚挚和深切的谢意，并祝阁下、伟大的中国人民和政府获得进一步的成就、幸福和安宁。”
