Title: A Spanish Writer Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 24th: At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Spanish writer Luis Landínez arrived in Beijing today for a visit to China. Representatives from the Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Writers Association were at the airport to welcome him.

### 西班牙一位作家到京

1959-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

    西班牙一位作家到京
    新华社24日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请，来中国访问的西班牙作家路易斯·兰迪内斯，于今天到达北京。到机场欢迎的，有对外文化协会和作家协会的代表。
