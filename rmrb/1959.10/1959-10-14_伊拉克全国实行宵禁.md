Title: Iraq Imposes Nationwide Curfew
Author:
Date: 1959-10-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, 12th - Iraqi military governor Abdi announced in a notice that a nationwide curfew would be imposed in Iraq from 8 p.m. tonight until 5 a.m. the following morning.

Since the assassination attempt on Prime Minister Qassem on October 7, the Iraqi authorities have declared a curfew in the capital city of Baghdad and its suburbs. The military governor had previously announced on the 11th that the Baghdad curfew would be shortened to run from 10 p.m. to 5 a.m. the following morning.

### 伊拉克全国实行宵禁

1959-10-14
第6版()
专栏：

　　伊拉克全国实行宵禁
    新华社巴格达12日电　伊拉克军事长官阿卜迪在一项通告中宣布，伊拉克全国今天开始从晚上八时到次晨五时实行宵禁。
    自从10月7日发生谋杀卡塞姆总理的事件以后，伊拉克当局已经宣布在首都巴格达市和郊区实行宵禁。军事长官曾经在11日宣布将巴格达宵禁时间缩短到从晚上十时至次晨五时。
