Title: Polish Science and Technology Cooperation Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-10-20
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, 19th: The Polish Science and Technology Cooperation Delegation, led by Koiy, Deputy Minister of the Polish Ministry of Heavy Industry, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon to attend the Sixth Session of the Sino-Polish Joint Standing Committee for Technical and Technical Science Cooperation. Zhong Ziyun, Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Committee and Vice Minister of the Ministry of Coal Industry, and others went to the airport to welcome them. Szymanski, Commercial Counselor of the Polish Embassy in China and member of the Polish Group of the Committee, also went to the airport to greet them.

### 波兰科技合作代表团抵京

1959-10-20
第5版()
专栏：

　　波兰科技合作代表团抵京
    新华社19日讯　前来我国出席中波技术和技术科学合作联合常任委员会第六届会议的波兰科技合作代表团在波兰重工业部副部长科依率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的，有委员会中国组主席、煤炭工业部副部长钟子云等。波兰驻华大使馆商务参赞、委员会波兰组委员席曼斯基也前往机场迎接。
