Title: Münnich Says at Budapest Celebration Rally that October 1st is a Festival for All Progressive Humanity
Author:
Date: 1959-10-02
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, September 30th: The National Council of the Hungarian Patriotic People's Front and the Hungarian-Chinese Friendship Association held a grand rally here tonight with thousands of people participating to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

    Hungarian party and government leaders such as Münnich, Marosán, Rónai, and Nemes attended the celebration. Hao Deqing, the Chinese Ambassador to Hungary, and all the diplomats from the embassy were also invited to attend.

    Münich, member of the Political Bureau of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and Prime Minister of the Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government, delivered a report at the meeting. Münnich said that October 1st is not only a festival for the Chinese people, but also a festival for the entire socialist camp and all progressive humanity. The founding of the People's Republic of China is a great event of world historical significance. Progressive people all over the world cheered for it. China is forging ahead vigorously on the path it has opened up.

    Münnich then gave a detailed introduction to the brilliant achievements China has made in socialist revolution and socialist construction over the past ten years, especially the great initiatives of the Chinese people under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Comrade Mao Zedong since the Great Leap Forward.

    Ambassador Hao Deqing also spoke at the meeting.

### 明尼赫在布达佩斯庆祝大会上说10月1日是全体进步人类的节日

1959-10-02
第6版()
专栏：

    明尼赫在布达佩斯庆祝大会上说
    10月1日是全体进步人类的节日
    据新华社布达佩斯9月30日电　匈牙利爱国人民阵线全国委员会和匈中友好协会今晚在这里举行有数千人参加的盛大集会，隆重庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年。
    明尼赫、马罗山、罗奈伊、内梅什等匈牙利党政领导人出席了庆祝会。中国驻匈大使郝德青和使馆全体外交官也应邀出席了庆祝会。
    匈牙利社会主义工人党政治局委员、工农革命政府总理明尼赫在会上作了报告。明尼赫说，10月1日不仅是中国人民的节日，同时也是整个社会主义阵营和全体进步人类的节日。中华人民共和国的成立是一件具有世界历史意义的伟大事件。全世界进步人类都为之欢呼。中国正势如破竹地在它所开辟的道路上勇往直前。
    明尼赫接着详细介绍了十年来中国在社会主义革命和社会主义建设方面所取得的辉煌成就，特别是大跃进以来在中国共产党和毛泽东同志的领导下中国人民的伟大创举。
    郝德青大使在会上也讲了话。
