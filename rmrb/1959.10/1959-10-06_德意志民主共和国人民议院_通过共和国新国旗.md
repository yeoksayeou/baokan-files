Title: People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic Adopts New National Flag

Author:
Date: 1959-10-06
Page: 6

    People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic
    Adopts New National Flag
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 5th - The People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic recently passed an amendment to the law concerning the national emblem and flag of the German Democratic Republic, which came into effect on October 1st.
    According to this amendment, the new national flag of the German Democratic Republic is black, red, and gold, with the national emblem of the German Democratic Republic in the center. The national emblem is located in the center of the new national flag, that is, the center of the circular national emblem is located at the intersection of the diagonals connecting the corners of the national flag. The width of the flag is three-fifths of its length, and the ratio of the diameter of the national emblem to the length of the flag is one to three. Therefore, if the flag is three meters long, the diameter of the national emblem is one meter; if the flag is two meters long, the diameter of the national emblem should be sixty-seven centimeters, and so on. ### 德意志民主共和国人民议院  通过共和国新国旗

1959-10-06
第6版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国人民议院
    通过共和国新国旗
    新华社5日讯　柏林消息：德意志民主共和国人民议院最近通过了关于德意志民主共和国国徽和国旗的法律的修正案，并且于10月1日起开始生效。
    根据这项修正案，德意志民主共和国的新国旗为黑、红、黄三色，中间带有德意志民主共和国的国徽。国徽位于新国旗的中央，即，圆形国徽的中心位于联结国旗对角的对角线的交叉点。国旗的宽度为长度的五分之三，国徽的直径和国旗长度的比例为一比三。因此，如果国旗为三米长，则国徽的直径为一米，如果旗子为二米长，则国徽的直径应为六十七公分，以此类推。（附图片）
