Title: Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Mr. Li Jishen, Passed Away Yesterday; Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Others Paid Their Respects

Author:
Date: 1959-10-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Mr. Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China,  the Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and Chairman of  the  Chinese Kuomintang Revolutionary Committee, passed away in Beijing at 8:27 AM on October 9, 1959, due to stomach cancer and cerebral arterial thrombosis, despite extensive medical treatment. Mr. Li Jishen was a native of Cangwu County, Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, and was 75 years old.

Before Vice Chairman Li Jishen's death, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Chairman Peng Zhen, among others, visited him in the hospital.

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The body of Vice Chairman Li Jishen was placed in a coffin at 5:00 PM on the 9th. According to the deceased's will, the body was cremated in the evening.

Today, those who came to pay their respects to the remains and bid farewell included: Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Lin Boqu, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, Lin Feng, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and some members; He Xiangning, Cheng Qian, Cai Tingkai, Zhang Zhizhong, Xiong Kewu, Vice Chairmen of the Chinese Kuomintang Revolutionary Committee, as well as some standing committee members, central committee members, alternate central committee members, heads of various departments, and friends of Vice Chairman Li Jishen.

During the placing of the body in the coffin, Mr. Li Jishen's wife, children, and relatives were present.

The Funeral Committee for Vice Chairman Li Jishen Established

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The funeral committee for Vice Chairman Li Jishen was established on the 9th. Funeral Committee for Vice Chairman Li Jishen Announcement

The mourning hall for Vice Chairman Li Jishen will be located in the Zhongshan Hall of Zhongshan Park. Organizations or individuals wishing to pay their respects should go on October 11th (Sunday) from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

The funeral committee office is located in the Zhongshan Hall of Zhongshan Park. Phone numbers: 3rd Bureau 7128, 3rd Bureau 6528

Obituary

Mr. Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and Chairman of the Chinese Kuomintang Revolutionary Committee, passed away due to illness in Beijing at 8:27 AM on October 9, 1959. A public memorial service will be held on a selected date. This announcement is hereby made.

### 全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长  李济深先生昨日逝世  朱德委员长周恩来总理等前往吊唁

1959-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长
    李济深先生昨日逝世
    朱德委员长周恩来总理等前往吊唁
    新华社9日讯　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席、中国国民党革命委员会主席李济深先生，患胃癌及脑动脉血栓形成，经医生多方诊治无效，于1959年10月9日上午8时27分在北京逝世。李济深先生广西僮族自治区苍梧县人，享年七十五岁。
    李济深副委员长逝世前，周恩来总理和彭真副委员长等，曾前往医院看望他。
    新华社9日讯　李济深副委员长的遗体在9日下午五时入殓。根据死者生前的遗嘱，遗体并已于晚间火葬。
  今天前往瞻仰遗容和向遗体告别的有：全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德，国务院总理、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会主席周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林伯渠、沈钧儒、黄炎培、李维汉、陈叔通、林枫和部分委员，中国国民党革命委员会副主席何香凝、程潜、蔡廷锴、张治中、熊克武以及部分常务委员、中央委员、候补中央委员和各部门负责人、李济深副委员长生前友好等。
    入殓时，李济深先生的夫人、子女及其亲属在旁守灵。
    李济深副委员长治丧委员会成立
