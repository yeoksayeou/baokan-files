Title: IUS Secretary Alvarez Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - Alvarez, the Secretary of the International Union of Students (IUS), a member of the IUS delegation invited by the All-China Students' Federation to participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the country, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. Prior to this, Kolesnikov, Deputy Minister of the University Work Department of the Soviet Union Central Committee of the Komsomol, and Mansouri Mohammed, Secretary of the National Union of Students of Morocco, members of the IUS delegation, had arrived in Beijing by plane. Also, Shiren Das Gupta, General Secretary of the All India Students' Federation and a member of the delegation, is on his way to Beijing.

### 国际学联书记阿耳瓦雷斯到京

1959-10-01
第7版()
专栏：

    国际学联书记阿耳瓦雷斯到京
    新华社30日讯　应中华全国学生联合会邀请来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的国际学生联合会代表团团员、国际学生联合会书记阿耳瓦雷斯，今天下午乘飞机到京。在此以前，已有国际学联代表团团员、苏联团中央大学工作部副部长科列斯尼科夫、摩洛哥全国学生联合会书记曼苏里·穆罕默德，先后乘飞机到达北京。还有代表团团员、全印度学生联合会总书记希伦·达斯·古普塔，正在来京途中。
