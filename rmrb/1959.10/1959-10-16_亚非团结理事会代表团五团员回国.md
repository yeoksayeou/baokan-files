Title: Five Members of the Afro-Asian Solidarity Council Delegation Return Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. Five members of the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Council delegation, namely Abdurashidov, Moussa Camara Sagna, Abubakar Hamoud Socolo, Hash Deo Malaviya, and Masao Kitazawa, departed from Beijing by plane this morning to return to their countries after concluding their visit to China. They were seen off at the airport by Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee, Secretary-General Zhu Ziqi, Deputy Secretary-General Yang Shuo, and committee member Wang Mingyuan, among others.
	Sudanese peace activist Ahmed Mohammed Kheir also came to the airport to see them off.
	The head of the delegation, Youssef el-Sebai, and other members returned to their countries on the 6th.

### 亚非团结理事会代表团五团员回国

1959-10-16
第5版()
专栏：

　　亚非团结理事会代表团五团员回国
    新华社15日讯　亚非人民团结理事会代表团团员阿布杜拉西多夫、莫沙·卡马拉·桑几亚那、阿布巴喀·哈穆得·索科洛、哈什·迪奥·马拉维亚、北泽正雄等五人，结束在我国的参观访问后，在今天早晨乘飞机离开北京回国。到机场送行的，有中国亚非团结委员会副主席刘宁一，秘书长朱子奇，副秘书长杨朔和委员王明远等人。
    苏丹和平人士阿赫迈德·穆罕默德·凯尔也到机场送行。
    代表团团长优素夫·西巴伊及其他团员已经在6日回国。
