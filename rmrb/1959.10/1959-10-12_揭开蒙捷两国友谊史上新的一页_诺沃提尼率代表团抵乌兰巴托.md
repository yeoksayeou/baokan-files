Title: Opening a New Chapter in the History of Friendship Between Mongolia and Czechoslovakia: Novotny Leads Delegation to Ulaanbaatar
Author:
Date: 1959-10-12
Page: 5

　　Opening a New Chapter in the History of Friendship Between Mongolia and Czechoslovakia
    Novotny Leads Delegation to Ulaanbaatar

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, October 11th: The delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic, led by Antonín Novotný, President of Czechoslovakia and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, arrived in Ulaanbaatar this afternoon. The delegation is visiting here for a three-day friendly visit at the invitation of the Mongolian government.
    The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at the airport by Mongolian Party and government leaders, including Tsedenbal, Sambuu, Tsende, and Molomjamts, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Mongolia.
    Novotny, accompanied by Tsedenbal and Sambuu, reviewed the guard of honor.
    A grand welcoming ceremony was held at the airport. Tsedenbal delivered a welcoming speech, saying that close friendly relations have been established between Mongolia and Czechoslovakia. The friendly cooperation between the two peoples in political, economic, cultural and other fields is being consolidated day by day. The friendly visit of the delegation opens a new chapter in the history of friendship between Mongolia and Czechoslovakia.
    Novotny delivered a speech thanking the Mongolian people for their warm welcome. He expressed his belief that this friendly visit will further consolidate the fraternal friendship between the two peoples.
    In the afternoon, Novotny and members of the delegation called on Mongolian Party and government leaders, including Tsedenbal and Sambuu.
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 11th: After a four-day visit to Korea at the invitation of the Korean government, Antonín Novotný, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia and President of the Czechoslovak Republic, and his party left Pyongyang by special plane this morning.
    Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and Premier of the Cabinet, Choi Yong-kun, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, as well as Park Jung-ae, Kim Chang-man, Chung Il-yong, Nam Il and other leaders of the Workers' Party of Korea and the government, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Korea saw them off at the airport.

### 揭开蒙捷两国友谊史上新的一页  诺沃提尼率代表团抵乌兰巴托

1959-10-12
第5版()
专栏：

　　揭开蒙捷两国友谊史上新的一页
    诺沃提尼率代表团抵乌兰巴托
    新华社乌兰巴托11日电　由捷克斯洛伐克总统、捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会第一书记诺沃提尼率领的捷克斯洛伐克共和国代表团今天下午到达乌兰巴托。代表团应邀将在这里作三天的友好访问。
    贵宾们在机场受到泽登巴尔、桑布、曾德、莫洛姆扎木茨等蒙古党政领导人和各国驻蒙古的外交使节的热烈欢迎。
    诺沃提尼由泽登巴尔和桑布陪同检阅了仪仗队。
    在飞机场举行了隆重的欢迎仪式。泽登巴尔致欢迎词。他说，蒙捷两国建立了亲密的友好关系。两国人民在政治、经济和文化等各方面的友好合作正在日益巩固。代表团的友好访问揭开了蒙捷两国友谊史上的新的一页。
    诺沃提尼致词感谢蒙古人民的盛情欢迎。他表示相信通过这次友好访问必将进一步巩固两国人民的兄弟友谊。
    当天下午，诺沃提尼和代表团团员拜会了泽登巴尔和桑布等蒙古党政领导人。
    据新华社平壤11日电　应邀来朝鲜访问的捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会第一书记、捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统安·诺沃提尼一行，在朝鲜访问四天以后，今天上午乘专机离开平壤。
    到机场送行的有朝鲜劳动党中央委员会委员长、内阁首相金日成，朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健，以及朴正爱、金昌满、郑一龙、南日等朝鲜劳动党和政府的领导人和各国驻朝使节。
