Title: Mikoyan Leaves Finland for Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-29
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, October 28th: According to TASS, Soviet First Deputy Premier Mikoyan left Helsinki today to return home after a week-long visit to Finland.
    Mikoyan arrived in Helsinki on October 22nd at the invitation of the Finnish government. During his stay in Finland, he held talks with Finnish government officials, presided over the opening ceremony of the Soviet Industrial Exhibition, attended the signing ceremony of the Soviet-Finnish trade agreement, and visited some factories and schools.

### 米高扬离芬兰回国

1959-10-29
第5版()
专栏：

    米高扬离芬兰回国
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社赫尔辛基讯：苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬在芬兰进行了一周的访问之后在今天离开赫尔辛基回国。
    米高扬是应芬兰政府的邀请在10月22日到赫尔辛基的。他在芬兰期间同芬兰政府负责人进行了会谈，主持了苏联工业展览会的开幕典礼、参加了苏芬贸易协定的签字仪式，并且参观了一些工厂学校。
