Title: Sudanese Cultural Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 13th: The Sudanese Cultural Delegation, headed by A. I. M. Gubara, which represented the government of the Republic of Sudan to attend the celebrations of the tenth anniversary of the founding of our country, and all its members, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Ding Xilin, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and responsible persons of relevant departments.

### 苏丹文化代表团回国

1959-10-14
第5版()
专栏：

    苏丹文化代表团回国
    新华社13日讯　代表苏丹共和国政府来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的苏丹文化代表团团长阿·依·墨·古巴拉和全体团员，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场送行的，有对外文化联络委员会副主任丁西林和有关部门的负责人。
