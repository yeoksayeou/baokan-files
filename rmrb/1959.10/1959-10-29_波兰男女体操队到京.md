Title: Polish Men's and Women's Gymnastics Teams Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 28th. The Polish men's and women's gymnastics team, a group of twenty-four people led by the Secretary-General of the Polish Gymnastics Association, Grochowski, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of our country.

The Polish men's and women's gymnastics teams ranked fifth and eighth in the team events at last year's World Gymnastics Championships, respectively. They will hold a friendly competition with our country's men's and women's gymnastics teams in Beijing on November 1st.

### 波兰男女体操队到京

1959-10-29
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰男女体操队到京
    新华社28日讯　应邀来我国访问的波兰男女体操队一行二十四人，今天由波兰体操协会秘书长格罗霍夫斯基率领乘飞机到达北京。
    波兰男女体操队分别是去年世界体操锦标赛团体第五名和第八名，他们11月1日将在北京同我国男女体操队举行友谊比赛。
