Title: World Federation of Scientific Workers Vice-President Nørregaard Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - Dr. Nørregaard, Vice-President of the World Federation of Scientific Workers, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Science and Technology Association of the People's Republic of China to attend the celebrations for the 10th anniversary of the founding of the country. Accompanying him were Indonesian scientists Dr. Hubenchy, Dr. Asa Munanda, Mr. Yan Afui, and Ceylonese scientist Dr. Weidankun, who had attended the Sixth Congress of the World Federation of Scientific Workers in Warsaw.
    The Chinese scientific workers delegation of thirteen members who attended the Sixth Congress of the World Federation of Scientific Workers, led by head of delegation Zhu Kezhen, also returned to Beijing on the same plane.
    Those who welcomed them at the airport today included Li Siguang, Chairman of the China Association for Science and Technology, Vice-Chairman Fan Changjiang, Secretariat Secretaries Tu Changwang, Zhang Zhenqiu, and heads of various specialized societies Yang Xiandong, Chen Shixiang, Wu Ruyang, and others, totaling more than 30 people.

### 世界科协副主席诺瑞加德到京

1959-10-01
第7版()
专栏：

    世界科协副主席诺瑞加德到京
    新华社30日讯　应中华人民共和国科学技术协会邀请前来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的世界科学工作者协会副主席诺瑞加德博士，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。同来的有曾在华沙出席世界科学工作者协会第六届代表大会的印度尼西亚科学家胡本智博士、阿萨·慕南达博士、燕·阿夫伊硕士和锡兰科学家魏湍昆博士。
    出席世界科协第六届代表大会的中国科学工作者代表团一行十三人，在竺可桢团长率领下，也同机回到北京。
    今天到机场欢迎的有中国科协主席李四光、副主席范长江，书记处书记涂长望、张震球及各专门学会负责人杨显东、陈世骧、武汝扬等共三十多人。
