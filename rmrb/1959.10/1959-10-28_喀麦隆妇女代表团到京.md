Title: Cameroon Women's Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th: The Cameroon Women's Delegation, invited by the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Beijing by plane today under the leadership of the head of the delegation, Madame Carmen Monique. Shen Zijiang, member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation, and others welcomed the guests at the airport.

Also welcoming them at the airport was Félix-Roland Moumié, Chairman of the Cameroon People's Union, who is currently visiting China.

### 喀麦隆妇女代表团到京

1959-10-28
第5版()
专栏：

    喀麦隆妇女代表团到京
    新华社27日讯　应中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会邀请来我国访问的喀麦隆妇女代表团一行三人，在团长卡门·莫尼克夫人率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。全国妇联主席团委员沈兹九等人到机场欢迎客人。
    到机场欢迎的还有正在我国访问的喀麦隆人民联盟主席费利克斯·罗兰·穆米埃。
