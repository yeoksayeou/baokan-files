Title: Danish Communist Youth League Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th: At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China, a four-member delegation of the Danish Communist Youth League, led by Per Stroe, Second Secretary of the Danish Communist Youth League Central Committee, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

### 丹麦共青团代表团到京

1959-10-28
第5版()
专栏：

    丹麦共青团代表团到京
    新华社27日讯　应中国共产主义青年团中央委员会邀请来我国访问的丹麦共产主义青年团代表团一行四人，在丹麦共青团中央第二书记佩耳·斯特洛率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
