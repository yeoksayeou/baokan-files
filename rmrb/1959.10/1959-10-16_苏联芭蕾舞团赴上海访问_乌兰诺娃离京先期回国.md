Title: Soviet Ballet Troupe Visits Shanghai; Ulanova Leaves Beijing and Returns Home Ahead of Schedule
Author:
Date: 1959-10-16
Page: 5
　　
Xinhua News Agency, 15th: The Soviet Bolshoi Theatre Ballet troupe left Beijing by train this morning for a visit and performance in Shanghai and other places. Soviet People's Artist Ulanova left Beijing by plane this morning to return home ahead of schedule.

Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture, and Antonov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, were at the station to see off the ballet troupe. Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Ge Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and dancer Dai Ailian went to the airport to see off Ulanova. The ballet troupe is scheduled to visit and perform in Shanghai, Guangzhou, and Wuhan, and will return to Beijing in early November.

### 苏联芭蕾舞团赴上海访问  乌兰诺娃离京先期回国

1959-10-16
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏联芭蕾舞团赴上海访问
    乌兰诺娃离京先期回国
    新华社15日讯　苏联国家大剧院芭蕾舞团今天上午乘火车离开北京前往上海等地访问演出。苏联人民演员乌兰诺娃在今晨乘飞机离开北京先期回国。
    到车站为芭蕾舞团送行的，有文化部副部长夏衍，苏联驻中国大使馆临时代办安东诺夫等人。前往机场欢送乌兰诺娃的，有对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥，中苏友好协会总会副秘书长戈宝权，舞蹈家戴爱莲等。芭蕾舞团预定在上海、广州和武汉访问演出后，将于11月上旬再回北京。
