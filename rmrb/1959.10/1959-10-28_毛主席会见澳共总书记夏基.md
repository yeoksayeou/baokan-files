Title: Chairman Mao Meets with Australian Communist Party General Secretary Sharkey
Author:
Date: 1959-10-28
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 27th. Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, met with Comrade Lance Louis Sharkey, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Australia, in Jinan, Shandong Province, on the morning of October 26, and had a cordial conversation.

    Present at the meeting were Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, and Shu Tong, member of the CPC Central Committee and First Secretary of the Shandong Provincial Committee of the CPC.

    After the conversation, Comrade Mao Zedong also had lunch with Comrade Sharkey. Comrades Wang Jiaxiang and Shu Tong were also present.

### 毛主席会见澳共总书记夏基

1959-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席会见澳共总书记夏基
    新华社济南27日电　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志，在10月26日上午于山东济南，会见了澳大利亚共产党中央委员会总书记兰斯·路易斯·夏基同志，并且进行了亲切的谈话。
    会见时在座的，有中共中央书记处书记王稼祥，中共中央委员、中共山东省委第一书记舒同。
    谈话结束以后，毛泽东同志还同夏基同志共进午餐。王稼祥和舒同同志也在座。
