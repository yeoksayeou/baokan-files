Title: IUS President Balikó Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-14
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 13th. Yuri Balikó, head of the International Union of Students (IUS) delegation and IUS President, and Klisnikov, a member of the delegation and Deputy Director of the University Work Department of the Central Committee of the Soviet Komsomol, left Beijing by plane today to return home after completing their visit to China.

Liu Xiyuan, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, and Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, were at the airport to see them off.

### 国际学联主席贝利康回国

1959-10-14
第5版()
专栏：

    国际学联主席贝利康回国
    新华社13日讯　国际学生联合会代表团团长、国际学生联合会主席尤里·贝利康和代表团团员、苏联共青团中央大学工作部副部长克利斯尼柯夫，结束在我国的参观访问后，已经在今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场送行的，有共青团中央书记处书记、全国青联主席刘西元，全国学联主席胡启立等人。
