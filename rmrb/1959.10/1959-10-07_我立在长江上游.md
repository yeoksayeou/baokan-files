Title: I Stand on the Upper Reaches of the Yangtze River
Author: Ge Bizhou
Date: 1959-10-07
Page: 8

I stand on the upper reaches of the Yangtze River,
　　Let my song fly away,
　　Spread its wings to meet the morning sun in the air,
　　Accompanied by the great river flowing eastward.
　　Wings beat the Yellow River's thousand-foot waves,
　　Soaring over the Kunlun Mountains,
　　Ten thousand miles east, west, north, and south,
　　Everywhere is a great leap forward, a bumper harvest.
　　Cities are stars,
　　Turning night into day,
　　The countryside is a blue sky,
　　Clouds and birds are free,
　　A new paradise on earth,
　　Knowing no hunger, cold, or sorrow.
　　Who is waving the mountain-driving whip?
　　Teaching high mountains to walk in line!
　　Who is using the river-turning magic?
　　Teaching great rivers to climb mountains!
　　Who is using the shrinking-earth method?
　　Traveling a thousand miles in a day!
　　Who is borrowing heavenly fire?
　　Igniting the Yangtze and Yellow Rivers,
　　Everywhere a torrent of steel!
　　Who has harnessed the east wind?
　　Blowing in new life,
　　Blowing away the old and decaying.
　　Blowing some people dizzy,
　　Opening their eyes and spouting nonsense;
　　The east wind is blowing strongly,
　　Making the enemies tremble!
　　My song is singing:
　　The pair of hands of the Pangu of today!

### 我立在长江上游
戈壁舟
1959-10-07
第8版()
专栏：

    我立在长江上游
    戈壁舟
    我立在长江上游，
    让我的歌儿飞走，
    展翅临空迎朝阳，
    伴着大江东流。
    翅拍黄河千尺浪，
    翱翔昆仑山头，
    东西南北万里，
    处处跃进、丰收。
    城市是星星，
    黑夜变白昼，
    农村是青天，
    云彩飞鸟任自由，
    人间新乐园，
    不知饥寒和忧愁。
    谁摇赶山鞭？
    教高山排队走！
    谁用翻江术？
    教大河爬山头！
    谁使缩地法？
    迢迢千里一日游！
    谁借天火来？
    点燃了长江黄河，
    处处钢铁洪流！
    谁驾起了东风？
    吹来了新生，
    吹掉了老朽。
    吹得有人头昏脑胀，
    睁着眼睛瞎胡诌；
    东风阵阵紧，
    吹得敌人们发抖！
    我的歌儿在歌唱：
    当今盘古的一双手！
