Title: Telegram of Greetings from the Tunisian Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1959-10-02
Page: 6

    Telegram of Greetings from the Tunisian Communist Party
    On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of the People's Republic of China, the Tunisian Communist Party extends its warm congratulations to you. The great achievements of the People's Republic of China have inspired our people and all progressive and freedom-loving people around the world. We wish the heroic Chinese people even greater achievements in socialist construction.
          Mohammed Ennafaa, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Tunisian Communist Party
                                          September 29, 1959

### 突尼斯共产党的贺电

1959-10-02
第6版()
专栏：

    突尼斯共产党的贺电
    值此中华人民共和国十周年之际，突尼斯共产党谨向你们表示热烈的祝贺。中华人民共和国巨大的成就鼓舞了我们的人民和全世界进步和爱好自由的人们。我们祝英勇的中国人民在社会主义建设中取得更大的成就。
          突尼斯共产党中央委员会总书记穆罕默德·恩纳法
                                          1959年9月29日
