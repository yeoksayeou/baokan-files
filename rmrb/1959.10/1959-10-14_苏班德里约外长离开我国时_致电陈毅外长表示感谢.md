Title: Foreign Minister Subandrio Calls Foreign Minister Chen Yi to Express Gratitude Upon Leaving Our Country
Author:
Date: 1959-10-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 13th: Indonesian Republic Foreign Minister Subandrio sent a telegram to our country's Foreign Minister Chen Yi to express gratitude upon leaving our country.

The telegram stated: "Before leaving Chinese territory, I would like to express my gratitude to Your Excellency for accepting my visit to China. Although my visit was very short, I had the opportunity to see some of the construction scenes in China's development. In particular, my conversations with the Marshal, as well as Chinese leaders Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai, increased understanding between China and Indonesia."

### 苏班德里约外长离开我国时  致电陈毅外长表示感谢

1959-10-14
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏班德里约外长离开我国时
    致电陈毅外长表示感谢
    新华社13日讯　印度尼西亚共和国外交部长苏班德里约在离开我国的时候，致电我国外交部长陈毅表示感谢。
    电文说：“在离开中国国土以前，谨对阁下接受我到中国来访问表示感谢。虽然我访问的时间非常短促，但我已得到机会看到了中国建设发展中的若干建设的情景。尤其是我和元帅以及中国的领导人毛泽东主席和周恩来总理的交谈，增加了中国和印度尼西亚之间的了解。”
