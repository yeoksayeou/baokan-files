Title: Korean Party and Government Delegation Returns to Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1959-10-04
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 3rd: The Party and Government Delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and Premier of the Cabinet, returned to Pyongyang by special train this afternoon after attending the celebrations for the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Welcoming them at the station were party and government leaders including: Choi Yong-kun, Kim Il, Hong Myung-hee, Jung Il-ryong, and Nam Il.

Qiao Xiaoguang, the Chinese Ambassador to North Korea, embassy staff, and the delegation of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association headed by Wei Chuantong, who are currently visiting North Korea, also greeted the delegation at the station.

### 朝鲜党政代表团回到平壤

1959-10-04
第2版()
专栏：

    朝鲜党政代表团回到平壤
    新华社平壤3日电　应邀参加了中华人民共和国建国十周年庆祝典礼的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国党政代表团，在团长、朝鲜劳动党中央委员会委员长、内阁首相金日成的率领下，今天下午乘专车回到平壤。
    到车站上欢迎的有：崔庸健、金一、洪命熹、郑一龙、南日等党和政府的领导人。
    中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光和使馆人员，正在朝鲜访问的以魏传统为首的中朝友好协会代表团也都到车站上迎接代表团。
