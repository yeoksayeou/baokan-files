Title: Spanish, Algerian, and Icelandic Fraternal Party Delegations Leave China
Author:
Date: 1959-10-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 20th: The Spanish Communist Party delegation headed by Comrade Dolores Ibárruri, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Spanish Communist Party; the Algerian Communist Party delegation headed by Comrade Rabi Bouhali, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Algerian Communist Party; and the Icelandic United Socialist Party delegation headed by Comrade Tsao Bo-yang-na-xun, member of the Central Committee of the Icelandic United Socialist Party, left our country by the same plane this morning after participating in the celebrations of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and visiting various parts of our country. Lin Boqu, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and Cai Chang and Wu Xiuquan, members of the Central Committee, were at the airport to see them off.
  The four members of the Spanish Communist Party delegation arrived in Nanjing on the 19th for a visit and will continue to visit various parts of our country before returning home.

### 西班牙、阿尔及利亚和冰岛  三国兄弟党代表团离我国

1959-10-21
第5版()
专栏：

    西班牙、阿尔及利亚和冰岛
    三国兄弟党代表团离我国
    新华社20日讯　以西班牙共产党中央委员会总书记多洛雷斯·伊巴露丽同志为首的西班牙共产党代表团，以阿尔及利亚共产党中央委员会第一书记拉比·布哈利同志为首的阿尔及利亚共产党代表团，以冰岛统一社会党中央委员曹勃扬纳逊同志为首的冰岛统一社会党代表团，在参加了中华人民共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼和在我国各地参观访问之后，于今天上午同乘一架飞机离开我国。到飞机场欢送的，有中共中央政治局委员林伯渠，中央委员蔡畅、伍修权。
  西班牙共产党代表团的四位团员已在19日到达南京参观访问，他们将在我国各地继续参观访问以后回国。
