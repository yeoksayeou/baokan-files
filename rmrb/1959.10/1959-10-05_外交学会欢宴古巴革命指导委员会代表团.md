Title: Foreign Affairs Institute Hosts Banquet for Delegation of Cuban Revolutionary Directorate

Author:

Date: 1959-10-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 4th October. Hu Yuzhi, Vice-President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, held a banquet tonight to welcome the delegation of the Cuban Revolutionary Directorate, led by Faure Chomón Mediavilla, Secretary-General of the Cuban Revolutionary Directorate.

Among those present at the banquet were Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhou Erfu, Secretary-General, Ji Chaoding, Vice-Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and heads of relevant departments such as the All-China Youth Federation.

### 外交学会欢宴古巴革命指导委员会代表团

1959-10-05
第2版()
专栏：

    外交学会欢宴古巴革命指导委员会代表团
    新华社4日讯　中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之，今晚举行宴会，欢迎由古巴革命指导委员会总书记福·乔·梅迪亚维利亚率领的古巴革命指导委员会代表团。
    出席宴会作陪的有中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南、秘书长周而复，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎和全国青年联合会等有关部门的负责人。
