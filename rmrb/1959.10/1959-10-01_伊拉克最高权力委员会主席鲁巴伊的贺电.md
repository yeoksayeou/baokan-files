Title: Congratulatory Telegram from Chairman Rubai of the Supreme Authority Committee of Iraq
Author:
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 8

To His Excellency Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:

    On the anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, I am very pleased to extend to Your Excellency my sincere congratulations and best wishes, wishing you happiness and health, and the distinguished Chinese people sublime honor and glory.

                    Najib Rubai, Chairman of the Supreme Authority Committee
                                  Baghdad, September 26, 1959

### 伊拉克最高权力委员会主席鲁巴伊的贺电

1959-10-01
第8版()
专栏：

    伊拉克最高权力委员会主席鲁巴伊的贺电
    中华人民共和国主席刘少奇先生阁下：
    在中华人民共和国国庆周年纪念日的时候，我十分愉快地向阁下致以热诚的祝贺和最良好的愿望，祝你幸福和健康，祝尊贵的中国人民取得崇高的荣誉和光荣。
                    最高权力委员会主席纳吉布·鲁巴伊
                                  1959年9月26日于巴格达
