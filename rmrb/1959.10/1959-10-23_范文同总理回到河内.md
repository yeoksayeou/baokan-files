Title: Premier Pham Van Dong Returns to Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1959-10-23
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, October 22nd: After attending the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic in Berlin and visiting China on his way back home, the Party and Government Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, headed by Premier Pham Van Dong, returned to Hanoi by special train tonight.
    Those who went to the station to welcome the delegation included: Deputy Prime Ministers Truong Chinh, Vo Nguyen Giap, Phan Ke Toai, Pham Hung, and ministers of various ministries of the Vietnamese government.
    The ambassadors of China, the Soviet Union, and the German Democratic Republic to Vietnam also went to the station to welcome them.
    Pham Van Dong, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party and Premier of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and the Party and Government Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam he led, passed through Nanning by train this morning.

### 范文同总理回到河内

1959-10-23
第5版()
专栏：

    范文同总理回到河内
    新华社河内22日电　以范文同总理为首的越南民主共和国党政代表团在前往柏林参加德意志民主共和国建国十周年庆祝典礼、并在归国途中访问中国之后，在今天晚上乘专车返抵河内。
    前往车站欢迎代表团的有：越南政府副总理长征、武元甲、潘继遂、范雄和各部部长等。
    中国、苏联、德意志民主共和国驻越南大使也到车站欢迎。
    越南劳动党中央政治局委员、越南民主共和国总理范文同和他率领的越南民主共和国党政代表团，是今天上午乘火车路过南宁的。
