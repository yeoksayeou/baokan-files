Title: Guo Moruo Meets with Delegation from the Ceylon-China Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1959-10-08
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 7th – Guo Moruo, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, met this afternoon with Martin Wickramasinghe, Chairman of the Ceylon Writers' Association and head of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association delegation, and some members of the delegation, and had a friendly conversation.

Present at the meeting were Ding Xilin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Yang Shuo, Director of the Chinese Writers Association.

### 郭沫若会见锡中友协代表团

1959-10-08
第2版()
专栏：

    郭沫若会见锡中友协代表团
    新华社7日讯　中华全国文学艺术界联合会主席郭沫若今天下午会见了锡兰作家协会主席、锡中友协代表团团长马丁·魏克拉马沁和代表团的部分团员，进行了友好的交谈。
    会见时在座的有中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林，中国作家协会理事杨朔。
