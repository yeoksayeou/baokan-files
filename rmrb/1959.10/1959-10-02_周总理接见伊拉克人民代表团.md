Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Iraqi People's Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-10-02
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Premier Zhou Enlai received the head of the Iraqi People's Delegation, Colonel Fadhil Abbas Mahdawi, President of the Iraqi Supreme Special Military Tribunal, the deputy head of the delegation, Attorney General A.A. Al-Ubaydi, and Colonel Majid Muhammad Amin, Attorney General of the Iraqi Supreme Special Military Tribunal, this evening.
    Vice Premier Chen Yi was present at the reception.

### 周总理接见伊拉克人民代表团

1959-10-02
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理接见伊拉克人民代表团
    新华社1日讯　周恩来总理今天晚间接见了伊拉克人民代表团团长、伊拉克最高特别军事法庭庭长法齐勒·阿巴斯·马赫达维上校，代表团副团长、总检察长阿·阿·奥克里，伊拉克最高特别军事法庭总检察长马吉德·穆罕默德·阿明上校。
    接见时，陈毅副总理在座。
