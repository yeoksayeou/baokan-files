Title: Novotný Visits North Korea: 100,000 Citizens of Pyongyang Warmly Welcome the Distinguished Guest
Author:
Date: 1959-10-08
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 7th October: At the invitation of Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, and Choe Yong-gon, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the president of the Czechoslovak Republic, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, Antonín Novotný, arrived in Pyongyang today by special plane for a visit. The distinguished guest was warmly welcomed by Kim Il-sung, Choe Yong-gon, and 100,000 citizens of Pyongyang.

Accompanying President Novotný on his visit to Korea were also the Deputy Prime Minister of Czechoslovakia and Minister of the Interior, Rudolf Barák, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, Jiří Hendrych, and the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Czechoslovakia, Václav David. Also present at the airport today to welcome the distinguished guests were Kim Il, Pak Jeong-ae, Kim Chang-man, Jeong Il-ryong, Nam Il and other leaders of the Workers' Party of Korea and the government, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to Korea.

Novotný, accompanied by Kim Il-sung, travelled by car from the airport to the city of Pyongyang.

Pyongyang was jubilant today for the friendship between Korea and Czechoslovakia. Along the streets from the airport to the city center, Korean and Czechoslovak national flags fluttered everywhere, and slogans welcoming President Novotný were hung. Tens of thousands of Pyongyang citizens, holding flowers and colorful flags and dressed in festive attire, lined the streets to welcome the distinguished guest, and warm cheers resounded throughout the city.

Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and Premier of the Cabinet, and Choe Yong-gon, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, met with Antonín Novotný , First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and President of the Czechoslovak Republic in afternoon of the 7th. During the meeting, the two sides held cordial talks.

### 诺沃提尼访问朝鲜  平壤十万市民热烈欢迎贵宾

1959-10-08
第5版()
专栏：

    诺沃提尼访问朝鲜
    平壤十万市民热烈欢迎贵宾
    据新华社平壤7日电　捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统、捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会第一书记安·诺沃提尼应朝鲜劳动党中央委员会委员长金日成和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健的邀请，今天乘专机到达平壤访问。贵宾受到了金日成、崔庸健和十万平壤市民的热烈欢迎。
    同诺沃提尼总统一同来朝访问的还有，捷克斯洛伐克副总理、内务部长鲁·巴拉克、捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会书记伊里·亨德里赫和捷克斯洛伐克外交部长瓦·戴维。今天到机场欢迎贵宾的还有：金一、朴正爱、金昌满、郑一龙、南日等朝鲜劳动党和政府的领导人以及各国驻朝使节。
    诺沃提尼在金日成陪同下乘汽车由机场前往平壤市区。
    平壤市今天为朝捷友谊而欢腾起来。从机场到市内的沿途街道上，到处飘扬着朝捷两国的国旗，悬挂着欢迎诺沃提尼总统的标语；拿着鲜花彩旗、穿着节日服装的成千上万的平壤市民，夹道欢迎贵宾，热烈的欢呼声响彻全城。
    朝鲜劳动党中央委员会委员长、内阁首相金日成和朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健7日下午会见了捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会第一书记、捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统诺沃提尼。会见时双方进行了亲切的交谈。
