Title: Delegations and Representatives of Fraternal Parties from Various Countries Visit Different Locations
Author:
Date: 1959-10-21
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 20th – Delegations and representatives of communist and workers' parties from various countries who attended the celebrations of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, with the exception of those who have already returned home, are currently continuing their visits to various parts of our country. The Canadian Communist Party delegation and the head of the Ecuadorian Communist Party delegation have returned to Beijing today after visiting other places.

The Indonesian Communist Party delegation, headed by Comrade Njoto, visited Shanghai and Hangzhou after completing its visit to Wuhan on the 16th. In Wuhan, the guests visited the Wuhan Iron and Steel Company, the Wuhan Heavy Machine Tool Plant, and the Yangtze River Bridge.

Delegations and representatives of fraternal parties from fourteen Latin American countries, including Brazil, Argentina, and Chile, have visited factories, people's communes, and other places in Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Wuhan, and other cities since the 15th. In Guangzhou, the guests also visited the Guangdong Revolutionary History Museum and the former site of the Guangzhou Peasant Movement Institute, which was founded by Comrade Mao Zedong in his early years.

Comrade Lance Louis Sharkey, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Australia, and Mrs. Sharkey, a member of the Australian Communist Party delegation, arrived in Zhengzhou on the 19th after visiting Shanghai and Wuhan. In Shanghai, the guests visited the site of the First National Congress of the Communist Party of China—the Shanghai Revolutionary History Memorial Hall—as well as the Shanghai Industrial Exhibition and the Electrical Machinery Factory, Cotton Textile Factory, Toy Factory, Wharf, Workers' Cultural Palace, and Children's Palace.

The Canadian Communist Party delegation, headed by Comrade Nelson Clarke, has visited Shanghai, Wuhan, Zhengzhou, Kaifeng, Shijiazhuang, and other cities.

The guests have received warm welcomes everywhere during their visits.

### 各国兄弟党代表团和代表在各地参观访问

1959-10-21
第5版()
专栏：

    各国兄弟党代表团和代表在各地参观访问
    新华社20日讯　参加中华人民共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼的各国共产党和工人党的代表团和代表，除已经回国的以外，其余的目前继续在我国各地参观访问。加拿大共产党代表团和厄瓜多尔共产党代表团团长，在外地访问后，已于今天回到北京。
    以约多同志为首的印度尼西亚共产党代表团16日结束了在武汉的访问以后，又先后参观访问了上海和杭州两城市。贵宾们在武汉曾参观了武汉的钢铁公司、武汉重型机床厂和长江大桥。
    巴西、阿根廷、智利等十四个拉丁美洲国家的兄弟党的代表团和代表，从15日以来，先后在杭州、广州、武汉等城市参观访问了工厂、人民公社等，贵宾们在广州还分别参观了广东革命历史博物馆和毛泽东同志早年主办的广州农民运动讲习所旧址。
    澳大利亚共产党中央委员会总书记兰斯·路易斯·夏基同志和澳大利亚共产党代表团团员夏基夫人在参观访问了上海、武汉以后，于19日到达郑州。贵宾们在上海曾参观访问了中国共产党第一次全国代表大会会址——上海革命历史纪念馆，以及上海工业展览会和电机厂、棉纺织厂、玩具厂、码头、工人文化宫以及少年宫等处。
    以纳尔逊·克拉克同志为首的加拿大共产党代表团先后参观访问了上海、武汉、郑州、开封、石家庄等城市。
    贵宾们在各地参观访问中到处受到热烈的欢迎。
