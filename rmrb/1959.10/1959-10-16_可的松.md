　　Little Knowledge

　　"Cortisone"
　　Some chemical substances are secreted from one part of the human body, transported through the blood to another part to stimulate or inhibit its function, thereby promoting the body's activity or growth. These chemical substances are called hormones. There are many types of hormones, and adrenal cortical hormones are one of the main categories. "Cortisone" is one of the most potent adrenal cortical hormones, and it is produced by chemical synthesis. Hydrocortisone is a white crystalline powder, and hydrocortisone injection is suitable for acute asthma, allergies, Addison's disease (also known as bronze disease), etc. Hydrocortisone acetate tablets are suitable for diseases of renal insufficiency and can treat rheumatoid arthritis, bronchial asthma, and other conditions.

### “可的松”

1959-10-16
第3版()
专栏：小常识

　　“可的松”
    有一些化学物质，从人体内一部分分泌出来，通过血液输送到另外的部分发生激发或约制的作用，从而促进机体的活动或成长，这种化学物质便叫激素。激素的种类很多，肾上腺皮质激素是其中主要的一类，而“可的松”便是肾上腺皮质激素中功效最强的一种，它是用化学合成方法生产出来的。氢化可的松，呈白色的结晶性粉状，制剂氢化可的松注射液，适用于急性气喘、过敏症、阿狄森氏病（又称青铜色病）等。醋酸氢化可的松片，适用于肾上机能不全的疾病，可治疗风湿性关节炎、支气管气喘等症。
