Title: Vice Premier Tan Zhenlin Receives Korean Agricultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-10-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 29th — Vice Premier Tan Zhenlin received Kim Man-keum, Minister of Agriculture of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, along with all members of the Korean agricultural delegation led by him, this evening.

Present at the reception were Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture, Cai Ziwei, Vice Minister, and others.

### 谭震林副总理  接见朝鲜农业代表团

1959-10-30
第5版()
专栏：

    谭震林副总理
    接见朝鲜农业代表团
    新华社29日讯　谭震林副总理今天晚上接见了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国农业相金万金和由他率领的朝鲜农业代表团全体人员。
    接见时在座的，有农业部部长廖鲁言、副部长蔡子伟等。
