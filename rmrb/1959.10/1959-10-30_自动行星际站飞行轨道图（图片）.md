Automatic Interplanetary Station Flight Path Diagram

    I. The corresponding positions of the automatic interplanetary station and the moon's orbit. The automatic interplanetary station goes from 1 (including the stage before separating from the rocket) around 2 to 3. At the same time, the moon goes sequentially from I through II to III.
    II. The orbital planes of the automatic interplanetary station and the moon. Plane A is the moon's orbital plane. Plane B is the orbital plane of the automatic interplanetary station before orbiting the moon. Plane C is the orbital plane of the automatic interplanetary station after orbiting the moon.
    Due to the influence of the Earth's and Moon's gravity, the orbital plane of the automatic interplanetary station gradually changes, from plane B to plane C.　TASS facsimile photo (Xinhua News Agency)

### 自动行星际站飞行轨道图（图片）

1959-10-30
第5版()
专栏：

    自动行星际站飞行轨道图
    一、自动行星际站和月球运行轨道的对应位置。自动行星际站从1（包括未脱离火箭阶段）经2绕到3。同时月球顺序从Ⅰ经Ⅱ到Ⅲ。
    二、自动行星际站和月球的运行轨道平面。甲平面是月球轨道平面。乙平面是自动行星际站环绕月球前的轨道平面。丙平面是自动行星际站环绕月球后的轨道平面。
    由于受到地球和月球引力的影响，自动行星际站运行的轨道平面逐渐改变，从乙平面改变到丙平面。　塔斯社传真照片（新华社发）
