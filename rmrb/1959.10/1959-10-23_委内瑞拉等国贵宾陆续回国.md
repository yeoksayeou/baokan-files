Title: Distinguished Guests from Venezuela and Other Countries Return Home One After Another
Author:
Date: 1959-10-23
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd: The Venezuelan Cultural Friendship Delegation, two members of the Brazilian Cultural Friendship Delegation, two members of the Chilean Cultural Friendship Delegation, Bolivian cultural representative Dr. Ricardo Anaya, Paraguayan poet Romero and his wife, the Finnish Cultural Delegation, the Danish Cultural Delegation, the Chairman of the Luxembourg-China Friendship Association, Mr. and Mrs. Bloom, the Swiss Cultural Delegation, the Belgian Cultural Delegation, Israeli printmaker Charlotte Bleiweiss, and Jordanian social figure Dr. Abdul Rahman Sukair, who were invited to China to attend the 10th anniversary celebration of the founding of the People's Republic of China, have concluded their visits in China and have left Beijing by plane between the 14th and 22nd to return to their countries.
After attending the 10th anniversary celebration of the founding of the People's Republic of China in Beijing, the guests visited Chengdu, Chongqing, Wuhan, Wuxi, Suzhou, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Taiyuan, Hefei, Shenyang, Anshan, Changchun, Tianjin and other places.

### 委内瑞拉等国贵宾陆续回国

1959-10-23
第5版()
专栏：

    委内瑞拉等国贵宾陆续回国
    新华社22日讯　应邀来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的委内瑞拉文化友好代表团，巴西文化友好代表团的二位团员，智利文化友好代表团的二位团员，玻利维亚文化代表利卡尔多·阿纳亚博士，巴拉圭诗人罗梅洛夫妇，芬兰文化代表团，丹麦文化代表团，卢森堡中国友好协会主席布鲁姆夫妇，瑞士文化代表团，比利时文化代表团，以色列版画家夏洛蒂·勃莱丝，约旦社会名流阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼·苏凯尔博士等，结束了在我国的参观访问，已经在14日到22日分别离开北京乘飞机回国。
    客人们在北京参加我建国十周年庆祝典礼后，曾到成都、重庆、武汉、无锡、苏州、上海、杭州、广州、太原、合肥、沈阳、鞍山、长春、天津等地参观访问。
