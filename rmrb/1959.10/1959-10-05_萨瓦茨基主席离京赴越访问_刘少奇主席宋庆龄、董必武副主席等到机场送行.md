Title: Chairman Zawadzki Departs Beijing for Visit to Vietnam; Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Vice-Chairpersons Soong Ching Ling, Dong Biwu See Him Off at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1959-10-05
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, 4th October. The Party and Government Delegation of the Polish People’s Republic, headed by Aleksander Zawadzki, Chairman of the Council of State of the Polish People's Republic, and member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, left Beijing this morning by special plane for a visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam via Guangzhou. After visiting Vietnam, the delegation will also tour various parts of our country.
    Liu Shaoqi, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the CPC and Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Soong Ching Ling and Dong Biwu, Vice-Chairpersons of the People's Republic of China, and other Party and state leaders were all at the airport today to bid farewell to the distinguished guests.
    After the band played the national anthems of Poland and China, Chairman Zawadzki, accompanied by Chairman Liu Shaoqi, reviewed the guard of honor of the three services of the Army, Navy, and Air Force. The distinguished guests accepted flowers presented by children of the capital and warmly shook hands with the well-wishers.
    Also present at the airport to see off the distinguished guests today were Chen Yi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Vice-Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Bo Yibo, Alternate member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice-Premier of the State Council, Tan Zheng and Li Xuefeng, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, Huang Yanpei and Li Weihan, Vice-Chairpersons of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Cheng Qian, Vice-Chairman of the National Defence Council, Burhan, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC, Xie Juezai, President of the Supreme People's Court, Luo Guibo, Ji Pengfei, and Zeng Yongquan, Vice-Ministers of Foreign Affairs, responsible persons of central departments, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, responsible persons of various democratic parties and people's organizations, and others.
    Personnel from the Polish Embassy in China and diplomatic envoys from various countries to China also went to the airport to see them off.

### 萨瓦茨基主席离京赴越访问  刘少奇主席宋庆龄、董必武副主席等到机场送行

1959-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    萨瓦茨基主席离京赴越访问
    刘少奇主席宋庆龄、董必武副主席等到机场送行
    新华社4日讯　由波兰人民共和国国务委员会主席、波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局委员亚历山大·萨瓦茨基率领的波兰人民共和国党政代表团，今天早晨乘专机离　开北京经广州去越南民主共和国访问。访问越南后，代表团还将在我国各地参观。
    中共中央副主席、中华人民共和国主席刘少奇，中华人民共和国副主席宋庆龄、董必武，以及党和国家其他领导人今天都到机场欢送贵宾。
    乐队奏波中两国国歌后，萨瓦茨基主席由刘少奇主席陪同检阅了陆、海、空三军仪仗队。贵宾们接受首都少年儿童献花，同欢送的人们热情地握手告别。
    今天到机场欢送贵宾的，还有中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅，中共中央政治局候补委员、国务院副总理薄一波，中共中央书记处书记谭政、李雪峰，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长黄炎培、李维汉，国防委员会副主席程潜，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，最高人民法院院长谢觉哉，外交部副部长罗贵波、姬鹏飞、曾涌泉，中央各部门负责人，中国人民解放军高级将领，各民主党派和人民团体负责人等。
    波兰驻华大使馆人员和各国驻华使节也到机场欢送。
