Title: Comrade Liu Shaoqi Meets with French Communist Party Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-10-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th - Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, met this afternoon with the French Communist Party delegation that was invited to China to attend the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The delegation was headed by Comrade Waldeck Rochet, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party. Members of the delegation included: Comrade Marcel Servin, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party; Comrade Bernard Jodoin, member of the Central Committee and Secretary of the Paris Provincial Committee; Comrade Juliette Dubois, member of the Central Committee; and Comrade Georges Marane, Chairman of the Central Financial Supervisory Committee.

The host and guests had a cordial conversation during the meeting.

Present at the meeting were Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, and Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee.

### 刘少奇同志会见法共代表团

1959-10-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　刘少奇同志会见法共代表团
    新华社15日讯　中共中央副主席刘少奇同志今天下午会见了应邀来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的法国共产党代表团团长、法国共产党中央政治局委员、中央委员会书记瓦德克·罗歇同志，代表团团员：法国共产党中央政治局委员、中央委员会书记马赛尔·赛尔文，中央委员、巴黎省委书记贝纳尔·儒杜伊，中央委员朱丽埃特·杜布瓦，中央财政监察委员会主席乔治·马兰等同志。
    宾主在会见中进行了亲切的交谈。
    会见时在座的，有中共中央书记处书记王稼祥，中央委员刘宁一。
