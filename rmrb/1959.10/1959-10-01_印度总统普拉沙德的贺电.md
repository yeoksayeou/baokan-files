Title: President Prasad of India Sends Congratulatory Telegram

Author: 
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 8

    President Prasad of India's Congratulatory Telegram
    To His Excellency Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:
    On the occasion of the anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, I extend to Your Excellency the most sincere congratulations from the government and people of India, and myself, as well as the best wishes for Your Excellency's personal happiness and the continued progress and prosperity of the people of your country.
                                Rajendra Prasad

### 印度总统普拉沙德的贺电

1959-10-01
第8版()
专栏：

    印度总统普拉沙德的贺电
    中华人民共和国主席刘少奇主席阁下：
    欣逢中华人民共和国成立周年纪念日，我向阁下致以印度政府、人民和我本人的最诚挚的祝贺以及对阁下的个人幸福和贵国人民的不断进步和昌盛的最良好的祝愿。
                                拉金德拉·普拉沙德
