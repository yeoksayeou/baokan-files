Title: Telegram of Congratulations from Yugoslav President Tito
Author:
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 8

To Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the People's Republic of China:

On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, I extend my best congratulations to you and the Chinese people on behalf of the people of all nationalities of Yugoslavia and in my own name, and sincerely wish the Chinese people prosperity.

                                      Josip Broz Tito
                                      President of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia
                                      September 30, 1959

### 南斯拉夫总统铁托的贺电

1959-10-01
第8版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫总统铁托的贺电
    中华人民共和国刘少奇主席：
    在中华人民共和国建国十周年的时候，我代表南斯拉夫各族人民并以我个人的名义向你和中国人民致以最良好的祝贺，并诚挚地希望中国人民昌盛。
              南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国总统约·布·铁托
                                      1959年9月30日
