Title: Chairman Liu Accepts Invitation to Visit Poland
Author:
Date: 1959-10-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 4th: During his visit to Beijing, Comrade Aleksander Zawadzki, Chairman of the Council of State of the Polish People's Republic and member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, the Council of State of the Polish People's Republic, and the Government of the Polish People's Republic, invited the Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, to visit the Polish People's Republic at a suitable time. Chairman Liu Shaoqi expressed his acceptance of this invitation.

### 刘主席接受访问波兰的邀请

1959-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席接受访问波兰的邀请
    新华社4日讯　波兰人民共和国国务委员会主席、波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局委员亚历山大·萨瓦茨基同志在北京访问期间，代表波兰统一工人党中央委员会、波兰人民共和国国务委员会和波兰人民共和国政府，邀请中华人民共和国主席刘少奇在适当时机访问波兰人民共和国。刘少奇主席表示接受这个邀请。
