Title: Subandrio Departs Guangzhou for Hong Kong
Author:
Date: 1959-10-13
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 12th— Indonesian Foreign Minister Dr. Subandrio and his entourage left Guangzhou for Hong Kong by train via Shenzhen this morning.
    Those who saw them off at the station included Sun Leyi, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou; Bi Xingyu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Guangzhou Municipal People's Committee; Yang Kezhong, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Guangdong Provincial People's Committee; Ren Bosheng, Secretary-General of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association; Sha Mengbi, Chairman of the Guangzhou Islamic Association, and responsible persons from relevant units of Guangzhou in culture, education, foreign trade, etc.

### 苏班德里约由广州去香港

1959-10-13
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏班德里约由广州去香港
    新华社广州12日电　印度尼西亚外交部长苏班德里约博士和他的随行人员，今天上午由广州乘火车经深圳去香港。
    到车站欢送的，有广州市副市长孙乐宜、广州市人民委员会副秘书长毕醒愚、广东省人民委员会外事处副处长杨可忠、中国印度尼西亚友好协会总干事任泊生、广州市伊斯兰教协会主席沙梦弼，以及广州市文化、教育、对外贸易等有关单位的负责人。
