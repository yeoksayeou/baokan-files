Title: Soviet Scientists Are Manufacturing Special Equipment to Safely Fly People to High Altitudes
Author:
Date: 1959-10-29
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 27th: According to a report in "Moscow Evening News" on the 27th, Soviet scientists are manufacturing special high-altitude equipment to enable people to fly into the upper atmosphere.

    The newspaper wrote that manned flights to high altitudes can now reach approximately 30 kilometers. Furthermore, scientists are studying the issue of enabling people to reach even higher altitudes in the near future. The newspaper pointed out that Soviet scientists will ensure the absolute safety of such flights and a smooth return to Earth.

    The newspaper also published photos of people undergoing training in a pressure chamber along with the article.

### 苏联科学家正制造专门装置  使人安全飞上高空

1959-10-29
第7版()
专栏：

    苏联科学家正制造专门装置
    使人安全飞上高空
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科27日讯：据“莫斯科晚报”在27日报道，为了使人飞入大气高层，苏联科学家正在制造专门的高空装置。
    报纸写道，现在，载人飞入高空已经可以达到约三十公里的高度。而且，科学家们正在研究，在最近时间内可以使人达到更高高度的问题。报纸指出，苏联科学家将保证这种飞行的绝对安全和顺利返回地面。
    报纸在发表文章时还刊登了人在气压试验室里进行训练的照片。
