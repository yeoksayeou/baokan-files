Title: Sino-Korean Friendship Association Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 29th. The delegation of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association, composed of four members, led by Wei Chuantong, Vice President of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association, returned to Beijing by train today after concluding its visit to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to participate in the Sino-Korean Friendship Month activities at the invitation of the Korea-China Friendship Association.

Li Dequan, President of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association, and Chu Tunan, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, were among those who welcomed the delegation at the station. The Korean Ambassador to China, Li Yonghao, also welcomed them at the station.

### 中朝友好协会代表团返京

1959-10-30
第5版()
专栏：

    中朝友好协会代表团返京
    新华社29日讯　应朝中友好协会邀请，到朝鲜民主主义人民共和国参加朝中友好月活动的中朝友好协会代表团一行四人，结束了在朝鲜的访问，在团长、中朝友好协会副会长魏传统的率领下，今天乘火车返抵北京。
    前往车站欢迎的，有中朝友好协会会长李德全，对外文化联络委员会副主任楚图南等。朝鲜驻华大使李永镐也到车站欢迎。
