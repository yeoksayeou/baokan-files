Title: Four Fraternal Party Delegations and Representatives Depart China
Author:
Date: 1959-10-28
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, October 27th: The delegation of the Communist Party of Venezuela, headed by Comrade Alberto Lovera, the head of the delegation of the Communist Party of Paraguay, Comrade Rogelio Espínola, the representative of the Guatemalan Labor Party, and the delegation of the People's Vanguard Party of Costa Rica, headed by Comrade Arnoldo Ferreto, left China by the same plane today after attending the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and visiting various parts of our country.

　　Present at the airport to see them off were Kang Sheng, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Yang Shangkun, alternate secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, and Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee.

　　The two members of the delegation of the Communist Party of Paraguay will continue their visits in our country before returning home.

### 四个兄弟党代表团和代表离开我国

1959-10-28
第5版()
专栏：

    四个兄弟党代表团和代表离开我国
    新华社27日讯　以阿耳贝托·洛维拉同志为首的委内瑞拉共产党代表团，巴拉圭共产党代表团团长罗赫略·埃斯平多拉同志，危地马拉劳动党代表，以阿诺耳多·弗雷托同志为首的哥斯达黎加人民先锋党代表团，在参加了中华人民共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼和在我国各地参观访问之后，于今天同乘一架飞机离开我国。
    到飞机场欢送的，有中共中央政治局候补委员康生，中央书记处候补书记杨尚昆，中央委员刘宁一。
    巴拉圭共产党代表团的两位团员将在我国继续参观访问后回国。
