Title: Hu Yuzhi Hosts Banquet for Moroccan Guests

Author: 
Date: 1959-10-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 13th: Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, hosted a banquet tonight to bid farewell to Mehdi Ben Barka, head of the Moroccan National Union of Popular Forces delegation, and his wife, as well as Abdurahman Zniber, the deputy head of the delegation, and all members of the delegation.

Hu Yuzhi spoke at the banquet. He said that the delegation's visit to China would play a significant role in enhancing mutual understanding and friendly cooperation between the two peoples. He expressed his hope that the delegation would bring back to Morocco the Chinese people's friendship for the Moroccan people, and wished for greater development of the friendship between the Chinese and Moroccan peoples.

Head of the delegation, Ben Barka, said at the banquet that the struggle against imperialism and the remnants of feudalism is bringing the peoples of our two countries closer together.

He said that during the delegation's visit to China, they witnessed the brilliant achievements made by the Chinese people through arduous labor over the past decade. He expressed great respect for these achievements.

Ben Barka expressed his gratitude for the warm reception and the cordial meetings with leaders in China, and expressed his willingness to bring this warm friendship back to Morocco.

Among those present were Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Ke Hua, Director of the West African Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Da Punsheng, Vice Chairman of the China Islamic Association, as well as Zhang Tiesheng, head of the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe, who had visited Morocco.

### 胡愈之欢宴摩洛哥贵宾

1959-10-14
第5版()
专栏：

　　胡愈之欢宴摩洛哥贵宾
    新华社13日讯　中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之今晚设宴欢送摩洛哥人民力量全国联盟代表团团长迈赫迪·本·巴尔卡和他的夫人及代表团副团长阿卜杜拉赫曼·兹尼巴尔和全体团员。
    胡愈之在宴会上讲话。他说，代表团在中国的访问对两国人民之间的相互了解和友好合作将起重大的作用。他表示希望代表团能将中国人民对摩洛哥人民的友情带回去，并祝中摩两国人民的友谊更加发展。
    本·巴尔卡团长在宴会上讲话说，反对帝国主义和反对封建主义残余的斗争使我们两国人民日益接近。
    他说，代表团在中国访问期间看到了中国人民十年来经过艰苦劳动所获得的辉煌成就。他对这些成就表示极大的敬意。
    本·巴尔卡团长对在中国所受到的热情接待和领导人的亲切会见表示感谢，并表示愿意把这些热情的友谊带回到摩洛哥去。
    出席作陪的有：全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，外交部西非洲司司长柯华和中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生以及曾赴摩洛哥访问的中国杂技艺术团团长张铁生等。
