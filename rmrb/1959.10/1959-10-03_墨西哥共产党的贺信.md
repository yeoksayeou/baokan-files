Title: Message of Greetings from the Communist Party of Mexico
Author:
Date: 1959-10-03
Page: 3

To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Great Chinese People,

Dear Comrades:

On the occasion of celebrating the tenth anniversary of the victory of the People's Revolution, the delegation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Mexico extends warm congratulations to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Chinese people. The Chinese Revolution is of great historical significance to the people of the world who are resisting imperialist oppression.

Under the leadership of the Party, its Central Committee, and Comrade Mao Zedong, with the help of socialist countries, especially the Soviet Union, and with the support of the great masses of the people, the Chinese Revolution has rapidly developed its productive forces in a short period of time, making China a first-class power in the world.

The victory of Chinese socialism and the victory of Soviet communist construction are guarantees for maintaining world peace and liberating mankind from capitalist enslavement.

Long live the People's Republic of China!
Long live peace!
Long live the victory of communism throughout the world!

October 1, 1959, Beijing
Delegation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Mexico

### 墨西哥共产党的贺信

1959-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    墨西哥共产党的贺信致中国共产党中央委员会和伟大的中国人民敬爱的同志们：
    值此庆祝人民革命胜利十周年之际，墨西哥共产党中央委员会代表团向中国共产党中央委员会和中国人民致以热烈的祝贺。中国革命对正在反对帝国主义压迫的全世界人民具有伟大的历史意义。
    在党，和他的中央委员会以及毛泽东同志的领导下，在社会主义阵营国家尤其是苏联的帮助下，在伟大的人民群众的支持下，中国革命在短时期内迅速发展了生产力，使中国已成为世界上第一流的强国。
    中国社会主义的胜利和苏联共产主义建设的胜利，是维护世界和平，使人类从资本主义奴役中解脱出来的保证。
    中华人民共和国万岁！
    和平万岁！
    全世界共产主义的胜利万岁！
                      1959年10月1日北京
          墨西哥共产党中央委员会代表团
