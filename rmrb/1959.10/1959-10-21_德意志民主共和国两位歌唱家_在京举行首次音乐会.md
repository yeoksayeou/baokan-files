Title: Two Singers from the German Democratic Republic Hold First Concert in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-21
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 20th -- Young soprano Christa Maria Ziese and tenor Wilfried Krug from the German Democratic Republic, held their first concert tonight at the Cultural Palace of Nationalities Theater in the capital. Professor Heinz Bongartz, conductor and artistic director of the Dresden Symphony Orchestra, specially flew back to Beijing from Xi'an to accompany them on the piano.

	Tonight's program included eighteen songs by music masters such as Beethoven, Schubert, Schumann, Brahms, and Strauss. Their performance was warmly welcomed by the audience. After the concert, the two singers and Professor Heinz Bongartz received flowers from young Chinese musicians at the meeting. The two singers arrived in Beijing on the 18th and will visit and perform in our country with the Dresden Symphony Orchestra.

### 德意志民主共和国两位歌唱家  在京举行首次音乐会

1959-10-21
第5版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国两位歌唱家
    在京举行首次音乐会
    据新华社20日讯　德意志民主共和国青年女高音歌唱家克莱斯特·玛丽娅·齐塞和男高音歌唱家维尔弗莱德·克路克，今晚在首都民族文化宫剧场举行首次音乐会。德累斯顿交响乐团团长、艺术指导兼指挥海恩茨·波恩格茨教授专门从西安乘飞机回北京为他们担任钢琴伴奏。
    今晚的节目包括贝多芬、舒伯特、舒曼、勃拉姆斯、斯特劳司等音乐大师的十八首歌曲。他们的演唱受到观众热烈欢迎。音乐会结束后，两位歌唱家和海恩茨·波恩格茨教授在会上接受了中国青年音乐家的献花。两位歌唱家是在18日到北京的，他们将和德累斯顿交响乐团一起在我国进行访问演出。
