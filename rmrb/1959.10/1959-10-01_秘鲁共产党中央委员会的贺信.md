Title: Letter of Greetings from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Peru
Author:
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 6

    To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China,
    Respected Comrades:
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of Peru extends its respects and congratulations to the glorious Communist Party of China, the brilliant Comrade Mao Zedong, the Government of the People's Republic of China, and the Chinese people, congratulating you on the great victories and achievements you have made in socialist construction during the ten years since liberation from the rule of imperialism and its allies within China and the existence of the people's regime.
    Filled with confidence in the talent, unity, and patriotism of the Chinese people, we warmly wish the Chinese people, led by the Communist Party of China, even greater achievements in socialist construction, the completion of the Second Five-Year Plan, catching up with and surpassing the United Kingdom in terms of average per capita output, and liberating Taiwan, Penghu, Jinmen, and Mazu from the hands of the American imperialists and the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique who are attempting to use these islands to carry out provocations that threaten world peace.
    The socialist camp, headed by the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Communist Party of China, is consolidated by its great creations and victories. It is an example of friendship, an impregnable fortress of peace, inspiring the people and communist parties of all countries, strengthening their struggle for national independence, democracy, and socio-economic welfare, and bringing them closer to the ultimate goal of socialism and communism.
    We joyfully congratulate the People's Republic of China on its tenth anniversary. As we warmly express our respects to you, we assure you that we will faithfully implement the universal principles of Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism, and redouble our efforts in our anti-imperialist and anti-feudal revolutionary struggle, as a contribution to the common cause of defending peace and striving for national liberation for the people of all countries.
    Long live the glorious Communist Party of China!
    Long live the vast People's China!
    Long live peace and friendship among the people of all countries!

                          维·拉乌尔·阿哥斯达·斯 (Signed)
                        维·拉乌尔·阿哥斯达·斯 (Víctor Raúl Acosta Salas)
                                1959年9月11日，秘鲁，利马 (September 11, 1959, Lima, Peru)

### 秘鲁共产党中央委员会的贺信

1959-10-01
第6版()
专栏：

    秘鲁共产党中央委员会的贺信致中国共产党中央委员会敬爱的同志们：
    秘鲁共产党中央委员会向光荣的中国共产党、天才的毛泽东同志，向中华人民共和国政府和中国人民致敬并表示祝贺，祝贺你们从帝国主义及其中国国内的同盟者的统治下获得解放和人民政权存在的十年间，在社会主义建设中所取得的伟大胜利和成就。
    我们满怀着对中国人民的才能、团结和爱国主义的信心，热烈地祝愿以中国共产党为首的中国人民在社会主义建设中取得更多的成就，完成第二个五年计划，在按人口平均产量方面赶上并超过英国，把台湾、澎湖、金门和马祖从那些企图利用这些岛屿来进行威胁世界和平的挑衅活动的美国帝国主义和蒋介石卖国集团手中解放出来。
    以苏联共产党和中国共产党为首的社会主义阵营以它们的伟大的创造和胜利而得到巩固，它们是友谊的榜样，是不可攻克的和平堡垒，鼓舞着世界各国人民和共产党，加强他们为争取民族独立、民主和社会经济福利的斗争，并使他们接近社会主义和共产主义的最终目标。
    我们欢欣鼓舞地祝贺中华人民共和国成立十周年。当我们向你们热烈地表示敬意时，我们保证我们忠实地贯彻马克思列宁主义和无产阶级国际主义的普遍原则，在我们反帝、反封建的革命斗争中加倍努力，作为向各国人民保卫和平和争取民族解放的共同事业的贡献。
    光荣的中国共产党万岁！
    幅员广大的人民中国万岁！
    各国人民的和平与友谊万岁！
                          秘鲁共产党中央委员会总书记
                        维·拉乌尔·阿哥斯达·斯（签名）
                                1959年9月11日，秘鲁，利马
