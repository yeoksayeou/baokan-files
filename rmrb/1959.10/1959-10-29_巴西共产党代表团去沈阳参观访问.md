Title: Brazilian Communist Party Delegation Visits Shenyang

Author: 
Date: 1959-10-29
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, October 28th: The Brazilian Communist Party delegation, led by Comrade Luís Carlos Prestes, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Brazilian Communist Party, left Beijing by plane this morning to visit Shenyang and other places.

### 巴西共产党代表团去沈阳参观访问

1959-10-29
第5版()
专栏：

    巴西共产党代表团去沈阳参观访问
    新华社28日讯　以巴西共产党中央委员会总书记路易斯·卡洛斯·普列斯特斯同志为首的巴西共产党代表团，今天上午乘飞机离开北京，前往沈阳等地参观访问。
