Title: Comrade Zhou Enlai Meets with Comrade Matern
Author:
Date: 1959-10-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th: Comrade Zhou Enlai, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Premier of the State Council, held a meeting and dinner this evening with Comrade Hermann Matern, head of the German Democratic Republic Party and Government Delegation, member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, and First Deputy Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic.

    Present from the German Democratic Republic were all members of the German Democratic Republic Party and Government Delegation: Max Sefrin, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Health of the German Democratic Republic; Bernhard Quand, member of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and First Secretary of the Bezirk Schwerin Committee; Alexander Abusch, member of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of German and Minister of Culture; Helmut Speer, Deputy Chairman of the Dresden Bezirk Committee and member of the Central Committee of the Liberal Democratic Party of Germany; Alfred Hennig, Deputy Chairman of the Karl-Marx-Stadt Bezirk Committee and Standing Committee member of the Executive Committee of the National Democratic Party of Germany; Kurt Nawack, First Secretary of the Bitterfeld County Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany; Heinz Barwich, member of the Scientific Committee for Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy of the German Democratic Republic, Director of the Central Institute for Nuclear Physics, member of the Physics Division of the German Academy of Sciences, and Lenin Prize winner; Professor Heinz Kress, Director of the Institute for Plant Cultivation and Agricultural Biology of the Academy of Sciences; and Paul Wandel, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the German Democratic Republic to China.

    Present from the Chinese side were: Tan Zhenlin, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Secretary of the Secretariat, and Vice Premier of the State Council; Li Weihan, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Huang Yanpei, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Chairman of the China Democratic National Construction Association; and Shi Liang, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Vice Chairman of the China Democratic League.

### 周恩来同志和马特恩同志会谈

1959-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来同志和马特恩同志会谈
    新华社6日讯　中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来同志今天晚上同德意志民主共和国党政代表团团长、德国统一社会党中央委员会政治局委员、德意志民主共和国人民议院主席团第一副主席赫尔曼·马特恩同志举行了会谈，并共进晚餐。
    德意志民主共和国方面在座的有德意志民主共和国党政代表团全体团员：德意志民主共和国副总理兼卫生部部长马克斯·塞弗林，德国统一社会党中央委员会委员、什米林专区党委第一书记贝恩哈德·克旺特，德国统一社会党中央委员会委员、文化部长亚历山大·阿布施，德累斯顿专区委员会副主席、德国自由民主党中央委员赫耳穆特·施佩尔，卡尔·马克思城专区委员会副主席、德国国家民主党执行委员会常务委员艾尔弗雷德·亨尼希，德国统一社会党比特弗耳特县委第一书记库尔特·纳瓦克，德意志民主共和国和平利用原子能科学委员会委员、中央核子物理研究所所长、德意志科学院物理学部委员、列宁奖金获得者汉因茨·巴尔维希，科学院植物栽培和农业生物研究所所长汉因茨·克雷斯教授，德意志民主共和国驻中国特命全权大使保罗·汪戴尔。
    中国方面在座的有：中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记、国务院副总理谭震林，中共中央委员、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李维汉，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、中国民主建国会主任委员黄炎培，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员、中国民主同盟副主席史良。
