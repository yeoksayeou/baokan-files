Title: Motherland
Author: Fang Jing
Date: 1959-10-19
Page: 8

　　Motherland
　　Fang Jing
　　We love the full moon of the fifteenth,
　　We love even more the rising sun of the east,
　　The stars in the sky also fill us with joy,
　　Motherland, you surpass the sun, moon, and starlight.
　　Motherland, in your warm embrace,
　　We gaze upon your sweet smile,
　　Embracing you tightly with our arms,
　　But how can we sing your praises to the fullest?
　　Delicate hearts, delicate needles,
　　Strands of colorful emotion,
　　Red thread is love, golden thread is happiness,
　　Embroidering a beautiful scene of the motherland.
　　The steelmaking workshop is a beautiful garden,
　　Here it is always bright spring,
　　Crimson peonies in the steelmaking furnace,
　　Bloom on the chest of the motherland.
　　Vast fields of golden hues reflect emerald green,
　　Such a beautiful idea of the People's Commune,
　　How many diligent and wise hands are competing
　　To dress the motherland in new festive clothes.
　　Hydroelectric and thermal power, with golden fingers,
　　Are for the motherland's victorious crown,
　　Exquisitely carving pearls and gems,
　　Each gem inlaid on the crown, sparkling and brilliant.
　　Everywhere, arms of light are waving,
　　Using light to build the most magnificent hall,
　　Silver beams, golden pillars,
　　The giant statue of the motherland shines above.
　　Whitewashed walls, painted red doors and windows,
　　Wiping the glass cabinets extra clean,
　　Motherland, the city has been dressed anew,
　　Displaying to you colorful gifts.
　　What a wonderful garden this is,
　　A good course to learn ideals and virtues,
　　To express our warm respect for the motherland,
　　We take off the red scarf from our necks.
　　Climbing the peaks of culture and science,
　　The red and expert banner flutters in the sky,
　　Fearing no obstacles, to pluck for the motherland
　　The fruits of culture and science.
　　Trains on land, fleets of ships on the water,
　　Iron birds in the sky are rushing back and forth,
　　Transporting for the motherland loads of joy,
　　Loads of blessings, like lightning.
　　Polished gun barrels, polished cannon bores,
　　Even brighter is the heart that loves the motherland,
　　Guarding the border means guarding
　　The beautiful life, the beloved peace.
　　Motherland, in your warm embrace,
　　We gaze upon your sweet smile,
　　Embracing you tightly with our arms,
　　But how can we sing your praises to the fullest?
　　We love the full moon of the fifteenth,
　　We love even more the rising sun of the east,
　　The stars in the sky also fill us with joy,
　　Motherland, you surpass the sun, moon, and starlight.

### 祖国
方敬
1959-10-19
第8版()
专栏：

　　祖国
　　方敬
　　我们爱十五圆圆的月亮，
　　我们更爱那东升的朝阳，
　　满天的星星也使我们喜爱，
　　祖国，你却胜过日月和星光。
　　祖国，在你热烈的怀抱里，
　　我们望着你甜美的笑容，
　　用双臂紧紧地拥抱着你，
　　但怎样才能把你尽情歌颂？
　　精细的心，精细的针，
　　一缕缕多采的感情，
　　红线是爱，金线是幸福，
　　绣出一幅祖国的美景。
　　炼钢车间是美丽的花园，
　　这里终年是明媚的春光，
　　炼钢炉里大红的牡丹，
　　朵朵开在祖国的胸膛上。
　　辽阔的田野金黄映着碧绿
　　好一片人民公社的美意，
　　多少勤劳智慧的手在争着
　　为祖国披上节日的新衣。
　　水电和火电，用金的手指，
　　在为祖国胜利的王冠，
　　精琢细磨珍珠和宝石，
　　颗颗嵌上王冠晶莹灿烂。
　　到处交挥着光的手膀，
　　用光明建造最宏丽的厅堂，
　　银的横梁，金的圆柱，
　　祖国的巨像闪耀在上方。
　　粉白了墙壁、漆红了门窗，
　　把玻橱擦得分外明净，
　　祖国，市容已打扮一新，
　　向你陈列着五采的礼品。
　　这是多么美好的园地，
　　好学习理想和美德的课程，
　　为了要向祖国热烈致敬，
　　从脖颈上取下了红领巾。
　　在攀登文化科学的高峰，
　　又红又专的旗帜凌空飘起，
　　不畏任何险阻，去为祖国
　　摘下文化科学的果实。
　　陆上的列车，水上的船队，
　　空中的铁鸟在来回奔忙，
　　为祖国运送满载的欢乐，
　　满载的祝福，像闪电一样。
　　擦亮了枪筒，擦亮了炮膛，
　　更亮的是爱祖国的心，
　　守卫在边境，就是守卫着
　　美好的生活，心爱的和平。
　　祖国，在你热烈的怀抱里，
　　我们望着你甜美的笑容，
　　用双臂紧紧地拥抱着你，
　　但怎样才能把你尽情歌颂？
　　我们爱十五圆圆的月亮，
　　我们更爱东升的朝阳，
　　满天的星星也使我们喜爱，
　　祖国，你却胜过日月和星光。
