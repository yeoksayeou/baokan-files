Title: On the Eve of the 10th Anniversary of the Founding of the German Democratic Republic, Chairman Zhu Receives German Journalists, Expressing Festive Greetings to German Brothers on Behalf of the Chinese People
Author:
Date: 1959-10-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th: Chairman Zhu De received Hansik, a reporter from the German Democratic Republic News Agency, this morning. Xu Huang, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was present.

　　In the conversation, Chairman Zhu De extended brotherly and warm congratulations on behalf of the Chinese people to the people of the German Democratic Republic on the upcoming glorious 10th anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic.

### 德意志民主共和国成立十周年前夕  朱委员长接见德记者  代表中国人民向德国兄弟致节日祝贺

1959-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国成立十周年前夕
    朱委员长接见德记者
    代表中国人民向德国兄弟致节日祝贺
    新华社5日讯　朱德委员长今天上午接见了德意志民主共和国通讯社记者汉希克。陪同接见的有外交部新闻司副司长徐晃。
    朱德委员长在谈话中，就即将到来的德意志民主共和国成立十周年的光辉节日，代表中国人民向德意志民主共和国人民，表示兄弟般的热烈的祝贺。
