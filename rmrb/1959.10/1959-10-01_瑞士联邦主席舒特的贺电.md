Title: Swiss Federal President Schutter's Congratulatory Message
Author:
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 8

To His Excellency Mr. Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:

    As the People's Republic of China celebrates its tenth anniversary this year, I would like to extend to Your Excellency the Federal Council's warmest congratulations, as well as my personal best wishes, for happiness to you and the Chinese people.

                              Paul Chaudet
                              President of the Swiss Confederation
                              Berne, September 29, 1959

### 瑞士联邦主席舒特的贺电

1959-10-01
第8版()
专栏：

    瑞士联邦主席舒特的贺电
    中华人民共和国主席刘少奇先生阁下：
    在中华人民共和国今年庆祝十周年国庆的时候，我谨向阁下致以联邦委员会的最热烈的祝贺，以及我个人的最良好的祝愿，祝你本人和中国人民幸福。
                              瑞士联邦主席保尔·舒特
                              1959年9月29日于伯尔尼
