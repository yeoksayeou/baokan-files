Title: World Peace Council Delegation Leader Bernal Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. John Desmond Bernal, head of the World Peace Council delegation, Executive President of the Presidential Committee of the World Peace Council, and British physicist, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home after completing his visit to our country. He was seen off at the airport by Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Wu Youxun, Vice-President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Qu Tangliang, Secretary-General, and Zheng Senyu and Wang Mingyuan, Deputy Secretaries-General.

### 世界和平理事会代表团长贝尔纳回国

1959-10-16
第5版()
专栏：

　　世界和平理事会代表团长贝尔纳回国
    新华社15日讯　世界和平理事会代表团团长、世界和平理事会主席团执行主席、英国物理学家约翰·德斯豪德·贝尔纳，结束在我国的参观访问后，在今天早晨乘飞机离开北京回国。到机场送行的，有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席刘宁一，中国科学院副院长吴有训，和大秘书长区棠亮，副秘书长郑森禹、王明远等。
