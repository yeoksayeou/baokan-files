Title: Receptions Held by Our Ambassadors to Asian, African, and Western European Countries to Celebrate the Tenth Anniversary of the Founding of Our Country
Author:
Date: 1959-10-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, October 1st: Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia, Huang Zhen, held a grand reception in Jakarta tonight to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

　　A total of 800 people attended the reception, including Indonesian First Minister of the Cabinet Djuanda, Speaker of the Parliament Sartono and three Deputy Speakers, Chairman of the National Planning Council Yamin, Deputy Chairman of the Provisional Supreme Advisory Council Abdoelgani, Foreign Minister Subandrio and other ministers, Air Force Chief of Staff Air Vice-Marshal Suryadarma, Acting Naval Chief of Staff Colonel Matadinata, Assistant Army Chief of Staff Colonel Jani, and other senior officers of the Indonesian Armed Forces.

　　Also attending the reception were: Chairman of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party, Aidit, and Vice-Chairman Lukman, as well as leaders or representatives of the Indonesian Nationalist Party, the Islamic Teachers Association, the Perti Islamic Party, the Common People's Party, and the Indonesian Islamic Union Party. In addition, there were well-known figures from the cultural, educational, and artistic circles, people from all walks of life, and representatives of overseas Chinese.

　　Diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union and some other countries to Indonesia also attended the reception.

　　At the reception, hosts and guests repeatedly toasted the achievements China has made in the past decade and their friendship with each other. The reception proceeded in a warm atmosphere.

　　Deputy Chairman of the Indonesian Supreme Advisory Council, Abdoelgani, on behalf of all members of the Advisory Council, wrote a letter to Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia, Huang Zhen, yesterday, celebrating the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The letter stated, "We hope that the People's Republic of China and its people will prosper, and we hope that the friendly relations between Indonesia and China will develop smoothly."

    Our Ambassador to Burma Holds Reception

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, October 1st: Chinese Ambassador to Burma, Li Yimang, held a reception here this evening to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. More than 700 guests attended the reception.

　　Among the guests attending the reception were U Myint Thein, Chief Justice of the Burmese Supreme Court, Sao Hkun Hkio, Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities, Deputy Prime Minister U Lun Baw, ministers and secretaries of various ministries, Deputy Chief of Staff (Air Force) of the Burmese Defense Forces, Clift, and other senior military officers.

　　Also attending the reception were Thakin Kodaw Hmaing, Chairman of the Burmese Committee for the Defense of World Peace, Dr. Htin Aung, Chairman of the Burma-China Friendship Association, key members of various political parties, members of parliament, famous writers, artists, social workers, journalists, and women and youth leaders.

　　Also attending the reception were diplomatic envoys from some countries to Burma and representatives of overseas Chinese.

　　At the reception, Burmese Deputy Prime Minister U Lun Baw and Chinese Ambassador Li Yimang proposed toasts to the health of Chairman Liu Shaoqi and President U Win Maung, and to the prosperity and happiness of the people of both countries.

　　The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere for three hours before it ended.

    Our Ambassador to Cambodia Holds Reception

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, October 1st: Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia, Wang Youping, held a grand reception on the evening of the 1st to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

　　Cambodian Prime Minister Prince Sihanouk attended the reception. Prince Sihanouk said at the reception, "Cambodia, as a friend of China, is very pleased to see the great achievements that the Chinese people have made in all aspects under the leadership of their government."

The reception was held in the Ambassador's residence, which was decorated very solemnly and beautifully. Colored electric lights formed the numbers 1949-1959. Also attending the reception were Princess Rasmi, Princess Yu Kanthor, the Speakers and members of the National Assembly and the Council of the Kingdom, members of the Royal High Council, and members of the government, etc. Diplomatic envoys from some countries to Cambodia also attended the reception.

　　Ambassador Wang Youping proposed a toast to the friendship between China and Cambodia and to the health of the King and Queen of Cambodia. Prince Sihanouk, on behalf of the King, the government, and in his own name, proposed a toast to the happiness and prosperity of the Chinese people and to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong and Chairman Liu Shaoqi.

    Our Ambassador to Afghanistan Holds Reception

Xinhua News Agency, October 2nd: Kabul dispatch: Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan, Hao Ting, held a reception on the 1st to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Attending the reception were the Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Foreign Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Ali Mohammed Khan, the uncle of the King of Afghanistan, Marshal Shah Wali and Marshal Shah Mohammed, cabinet ministers, the Chief of Staff of the Armed Forces, senior officials, and some generals, as well as prominent figures from all walks of life in Afghanistan, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Afghanistan, etc.

    Our Ambassador to the United Arab Republic Holds Reception

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, October 1st: To celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, Chinese Ambassador to the United Arab Republic, Chen Jiakang, held a grand reception here tonight.

### 庆祝我国建国十周年  我驻亚非和西欧各国使节举行招待会

1959-10-04
第3版()
专栏：

    庆祝我国建国十周年
    我驻亚非和西欧各国使节举行招待会
    我驻印度尼西亚大使举行招待会
    新华社雅加达1日电　中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇今晚在雅加达举行盛大招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国建国十周年。
  　出席招待会的共有八百人，其中包括印度尼西亚内阁第一部长朱安达，国会议长沙多诺和三位副议长，国家计划委员会主席亚明，临时最高谘询委员会副主席阿卜杜加尼，外交部长苏班德里约和其他部长，空军参谋长苏里亚达马空军中将，海军代理参谋长马塔迪纳塔上校，陆军参谋长助理扬尼上校以及印度尼西亚武装部队三军的其他高级官员。
  出席招待会的还有：印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会主席艾地和副主席鲁克曼，印度尼西亚国民党、伊斯兰教师联合会、白尔蒂伊斯兰党、平民党和印度尼西亚伊斯兰联盟党的领导人或代表。此外还有文化、教育和艺术界的知名人士，社会人士以及华侨代表。
    苏联和其他一些国家驻印度尼西亚的外交使节也出席了招待会。
  在招待会上，宾主不断为中国在过去十年中所取得的成就和相互之间的友谊干杯。招待会在热烈的气氛中进行。
    印度尼西亚最高谘询委员会副主席阿卜杜加尼代表谘询委员会的全体委员，在昨天写信给中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年。信中写道，“我们希望中华人民共和国和它的民族繁荣，并且希望印度尼西亚和中国之间的友好关系顺利地获得发展。”
    我驻缅甸大使举行招待会
  新华社仰光1日电　中国驻缅甸大使李一氓今天晚间在这里举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年。出席招待会的共有七百多位客人。
    在出席招待会的客人中，有缅甸最高法院首席法官吴敏登、民族院议长萧恢塔、副总理吴伦博、各部的部长和秘书、缅甸国防军副总参谋长（空军）克利夫特以及其他高级军官。
  　出席招待会的还有缅甸保卫世界和平委员会主席德钦哥都迈、缅中友好协会主席信波博士、各个政党的主要成员、国会议员、著名作家、艺术家、社会工作者、新闻工作者以及妇女和青年领袖。
    出席招待会的还有一些国家驻缅甸的外交使节和华侨代表。
    在招待会上，缅甸副总理吴伦博和中国大使李一氓提议为刘少奇主席和吴温貌总统的健康以及两国人民的繁荣和幸福干杯。
    招待会在热烈和友好的气氛中进行了三小时才结束。
    我驻柬埔寨大使举行招待会
    新华社金边1日电　中国驻柬埔寨大使王幼平在1日晚上举行盛大招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年。
    柬埔寨首相西哈努克亲王出席了招待会。　　西哈努克亲王在招待会上讲话说，“柬埔寨作为中国的朋友，看到中国人民在他们的政府的领导下在各方面取得了巨大的成就而感到非常高兴。”
  招待会是在装饰得非常庄严、美丽的大使官邸中举行的。彩色电灯组成了1949—1959的字样。出席招待会的还有：拉斯美王姑、余冈多尔王姑、国民议会和王国会议的议长、议员、皇廷最高会议的成员和政府成员等。一些国家驻柬埔寨的外交使节也出席了招待会。
    王幼平大使建议为中柬两国间的友谊和柬埔寨国王和王后的健康干杯。西哈努克亲王代表国王、政府，并且以他个人的名义建议为中国人民的幸福和繁荣以及毛泽东主席和刘少奇主席的健康干杯。
    我驻阿富汗大使举行招待会
