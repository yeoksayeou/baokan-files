Title: Cuban and Ecuadorian fraternal party honored guests return home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 24th. Comrade Aníbal Escalante, Executive Secretary of the National Committee of the Cuban People's Socialist Party, member of the Executive Bureau of the National Committee, and head of the Cuban People's Socialist Party delegation, and the Ecuadorian Communist Party delegation headed by Comrade Alejandro Idrovo, member of the Central Committee of the Ecuadorian Communist Party, left Beijing by plane today to return to their countries after participating in the celebrations of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and visiting various parts of our country.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Tan Zhenlin, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary of the Central Secretariat, Tan Zheng, Secretary of the Central Secretariat, and Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee.

One member of the Cuban People's Socialist Party delegation has already returned home, and the other two members will continue to visit our country before returning home.

### 古巴和厄瓜多尔兄弟党贵宾回国

1959-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

    古巴和厄瓜多尔兄弟党贵宾回国
    新华社24日讯　古巴人民社会党全国委员会执行书记、全国委员会执行局委员、古巴人民社会党代表团团长阿尼巴尔·埃斯卡兰特同志，以厄瓜多尔共产党中央委员亚历杭德罗·伊德罗沃同志为首的厄瓜多尔共产党代表团，在参加了中华人民共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼和在我国各地参观访问之后，于今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
  到飞机场欢送的，有中共中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记谭震林，中央书记处书记谭政，中央委员刘宁一。
    古巴人民社会党代表团的一位团员已经回国，另外两位团员将在我国继续参观访问后回国。
