Title: Chairman Brandweiner of the Austrian Peace Council Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 30th—At the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Brandweiner, Chairman of the Austrian Peace Council, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon to participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of our country.

### 奥和平理事会主席布兰德魏纳到京

1959-10-01
第7版()
专栏：

    奥和平理事会主席布兰德魏纳到京
    新华社30日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请，来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的奥地利和平理事会主席布兰德魏纳，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
