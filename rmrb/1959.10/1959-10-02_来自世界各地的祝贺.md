Telegrams of Congratulations from Romanian Party and State Leaders
To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Comrade Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:
On the occasion of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, dear comrades, we extend our brotherly respect and warm congratulations to you and the entire Chinese people.
The hardworking and talented Chinese people, who have shaken off the chains of generations of slavery and the poverty and extreme backwardness of the past, have engaged in tremendous activities in the decade since liberation on the road to establishing a socialist system. The working people of the People's Republic of China, with unwavering faith, follow the general line of socialist construction formulated by the tested Communist Party of China, and have made great achievements in developing industry and agriculture, improving the living conditions of the working people, and developing culture and education. The face of the entire country is changing year by year. The People's Republic of China has become a great socialist power, making significant contributions to further strengthen the unity of the powerful socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union and to the struggle for lasting world peace..
The Romanian and Chinese people have established friendly, fraternal, and mutually supportive relations in the common struggle to build socialism and strive for progress and peace. The Romanian people are sincerely happy for the historically significant achievements made by the Chinese people and share the joy of the great National Day with the Chinese working people.
We wish the fraternal Chinese people new achievements in realizing the great tasks set by the Communist Party of China and the Government of the People's Republic of China, and in the struggle to achieve the victory of socialism in their motherland.
May the great People's Republic of China prosper and flourish forever!
Long live the Communist Party of China and its Central Committee!
Long live the Government of the People's Republic of China!
Long live the fraternal friendship between the Romanian and Chinese people!
    Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej
    First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party
    Chivu Stoica
    Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic
    Janghe Maurer
    Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic
        Bucharest, September 30, 1959

Telegram of Congratulations from World Federation of Trade Unions General Secretary Saillant to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions
As the Chinese people celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China with due pride, the World Federation of Trade Unions extends its warmest congratulations to the Chinese working people, the Chinese trade unions, and the leaders of the Chinese people. The World Federation of Trade Unions fully recognizes that under the experienced and outstanding leadership of the glorious Communist Party of China, the Chinese trade unions closely participated in the magnificent work of economic and social transformation of your country. Ten years ago, the working people all over the world celebrated the founding of the Chinese workers' and peasants' state with joy, regarding it as a decisive factor in strengthening world peace and promoting the collapse of the colonialist system, and as a historically significant event. Since then, they have watched the exemplary victories achieved by the creative labor of the Chinese brothers with admiration and deep sympathy. They have expressed unbreakable solidarity with the Chinese workers, which has been increasingly strengthened with the growing influence of the Chinese trade unions in international trade union organizations. The World Federation of Trade Unions condemns the stubborn attitude of the imperialist groups in refusing to recognize the government freely elected by the 600 million Chinese people, and considers it unreasonable and contrary to the interests of world peace. The World Federation of Trade Unions, together with the workers of the world, once again rejoices in the strength of the forces of peace and the international prestige that the People's Republic of China has gained in ten years due to the close unity of the people of the whole country, the achievements in the economic field, and the socialist transformation, as well as the policy of peace and friendship with the people of all countries. The World Federation of Trade Unions, on behalf of the workers of the world, congratulates the Chinese workers and trade unions for their significant achievements in the cause of strengthening international workers' friendship and unity, and wishes them new victories in building a socialist motherland!
Long live the People's Republic of China!
Long live the Chinese workers and the All-China Federation of Trade Unions!
Long live international workers' solidarity and peace!
    Louis Saillant
    General Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions
        Prague, September 24, 1959
