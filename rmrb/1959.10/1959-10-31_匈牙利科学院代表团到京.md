Title: Hungarian Academy of Sciences Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 30th: The delegation of the Hungarian Academy of Sciences, led by Corresponding Academician Major Máté, arrived in Beijing by plane today to sign the 1960 Executive Plan for the Sino-Hungarian Academies of Sciences Scientific Cooperation Association. Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and head of the Chinese Academy of Sciences delegation, was at the airport to welcome them.

### 匈牙利科学院代表团到京

1959-10-31
第5版()
专栏：

    匈牙利科学院代表团到京
    新华社30日讯　来我国签订中、匈两国科学院科学合作协会1960年执行计划的匈牙利科学院代表团，在马约尔·马泰通讯院士率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中国科学院副院长、中国科学院代表团团长吴有训。
