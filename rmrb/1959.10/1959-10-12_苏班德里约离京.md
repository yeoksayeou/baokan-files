Title: Subandrio Departs Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th: Indonesian Foreign Minister Dr. Subandrio and his entourage left Beijing by plane this morning.

Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi went to the airport to see the guests off. Beijing children presented the guests with flowers.

Also at the airport to see them off were: Liao Chengzhi, Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; heads of various democratic parties; Bao Erhan, President of the Chinese-Indonesian Friendship Association; Da Punsheng, Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association; Wu Yixiu, Deputy to the National People's Congress and Standing Committee Member of the All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese; Situ Zan, Chairman of the Jakarta Chinese Association, and others.

Indonesian Ambassador to China, Wijopranoto, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 苏班德里约离京

1959-10-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏班德里约离京
    新华社11日讯　印度尼西亚外交部长苏班德里约博士和他的随行人员，今天上午乘飞机离开北京。
    陈毅副总理兼外交部长到飞机场欢送客人。北京少年儿童向客人们献了鲜花。
    到机场欢送的还有：华侨事务委员会主任廖承志，外交部副部长章汉夫，各民主党派负责人，中国印度尼西亚友好协会会长包尔汉，中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生，全国人民代表大会代表、中华全国归国华侨联合会常务委员吴益修，雅加达中华侨团总会主席司徒赞等。
    印度尼西亚驻华大使维约普拉诺托也到机场欢送。
