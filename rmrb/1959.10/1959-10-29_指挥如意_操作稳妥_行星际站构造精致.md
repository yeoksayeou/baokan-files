Title: Skillful Command, Steady Operation, Exquisite Construction of Interplanetary Station
Author:
Date: 1959-10-29
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th -- According to TASS: The Soviet newspaper "Pravda" today published a detailed introduction of the Soviet automatic interplanetary station device.

    The automatic interplanetary station is a spacecraft equipped with complex scientific equipment: radio technology equipment, phototelegraphy equipment, scientific exploration instruments, specialized orientation equipment, instrument working program control equipment, automatic temperature adjustment system, and energy sources.

    A specialized radio technology system ensures the measurement of the interplanetary station's orbital elements, transmits television and scientific telemetry information back to Earth, and broadcasts commands from Earth to control the operation of the interplanetary station's instruments. The orientation system ensures the interplanetary station's position in space relative to the sun and moon, which is a necessary condition for taking pictures of the far side of the moon.

    The operation of the interplanetary station's instruments is entirely controlled by the ground station through radio links and the interplanetary station's instrument working program control equipment. This combined device makes it possible to most conveniently control scientific experiments and to obtain information from any part of the interplanetary station's orbit within the radio visibility range of the ground observation station.

    The automatic temperature control system continuously operates to maintain a predetermined temperature inside the station. The energy system includes automatic chemical battery packs and shock-absorbing chemical battery packs, as well as solar cell arrays. The energy consumed by the chemical battery packs is compensated by solar energy.

    The interplanetary station is a thin-walled sealed structure, cylindrical in shape with a spherical bottom. Its maximum diameter is 1200 millimeters and its length is 1300 millimeters (excluding antennas).

    Various instruments and chemical energy sources are installed on the frame inside the cylinder. Some scientific exploration instruments, antennas, and solar cell arrays are installed on the outside. The upper end is embedded with a circular transparent window with a cover that can automatically open during photography. Transparent windows are provided at the upper and lower ends for the solar sensors in the orientation system. The lower end is equipped with the control engine of the orientation system.

    The orientation system includes optical sensors, gyroscopic sensors, logic electronic devices, and control engines. (Attached picture)

### 指挥如意　操作稳妥  行星际站构造精致

1959-10-29
第7版()
专栏：

    指挥如意　操作稳妥
    行星际站构造精致
    新华社27日讯　据塔斯社消息：苏联“真理报”今天公布了苏联自动行星际站装置的详细介绍。
    自动行星际站是一个宇宙飞行装置，上面带有复杂的科学设备：无线电技术设备、照片传真设备、科学考察仪器、专门定向设备、仪器工作程序控制设备、自动调整温度系统和能源。
    专门无线电技术系统保证测量行星际站的轨道要素，向地球发回电视和科学遥测情报，以及传播地球上发出的控制行星际站仪器工作的号令。定向系统保证了行星际站在宇宙空间中距太阳和月球的方位，这是拍摄月球背面照片所必需的条件。
    行星际站仪器的工作完全由地面站通过无线电线路以及行星际站仪器工作程序控制设备来操纵。这种联合装置使得有可能最便利地控制科学实验，有可能在地面观测站无线电可见度的范围内从行星际站轨道的任何部分获得情报。
    自动调节温度系统不断工作，以保持站内的预定温度。能源系统包括自动化学电池组和减震化学电池组，以及太阳能电池组。化学电池组消耗的能量由太阳能源来补偿。
    行星际站是一个薄壁密封结构，圆柱形、底部呈球形。其最大直径为一千二百毫米，长为一千三百毫米（不算天线）。
    圆柱体内部的架上安装着各种仪器和化学能源。外部安装有一部分科学考察仪器、天线和太阳能电池组。上底嵌有一个带盖的圆形透明窗，在摄影时窗盖能自动打开。在上底和下底均设有透明窗，供定向系统中的太阳能传感器用。在下底设有定向系统的操纵发动机。
    定向系统包括光学传感器、回转传感器、逻辑电子装置和操纵发动机。（附图片）
