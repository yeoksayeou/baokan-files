Title: East German Men's Handball Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-22
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, October 21st. The East German national men's handball team, a group of twenty-one people led by Hermann Milius, Chairman of the East German Handball Association, arrived in Beijing today at our invitation for a visit and friendly matches in our country.

    The East German national handball team has a very high level of skill. Among the fifteen athletes who came to our country this time, seven are members of the all-German team that won the World Handball Championship this year. They are scheduled to hold friendly matches with the Beijing, People's Liberation Army, and Heilongjiang men's handball teams in Beijing on the 23rd, 25th, and 27th respectively.

### 德国家男子手球队到京

1959-10-22
第5版()
专栏：

    德国家男子手球队到京
    新华社21日讯　应邀来我国访问和进行友谊比赛的德意志民主共和国国家男子手球队一行二十一人，今天由德意志民主共和国手球协会主席赫尔曼·米留斯率领到达北京。
    德意志民主共和国国家手球队有着很高的水平，这次来我国的十五名运动员中，有七人是获得今年世界手球锦标赛冠军的全德手球队的成员。他们预定在23日、25日、27日在北京先后同北京、解放军和黑龙江男子手球队举行友谊比赛。
