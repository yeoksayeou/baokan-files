Title: General Secretary of the Communist Youth Union of Chile, Prado, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 7

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th - Manuel Cantero Prado, General Secretary of the Communist Youth Union of Chile, a member of the World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation invited by the All-China Youth Federation to participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, has arrived in Beijing by plane.

### 智利共产主义青年联盟总书记普拉多到京

1959-10-01
第7版()
专栏：

    智利共产主义青年联盟总书记普拉多到京
    新华社30日讯　应中华全国青年联合会邀请来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的世界民主青年联盟代表团团员、智利共产主义青年联盟总书记曼努埃尔·坎特罗·普拉多，已经乘飞机到达北京。
