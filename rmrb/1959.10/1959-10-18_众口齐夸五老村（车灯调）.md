Title: Everyone Praises Five Old Men Village (Cart Lamp Tune)
Author: Zeng Tianjia
Date: 1959-10-18
Page: 8

Everyone Praises Five Old Men Village (Cart Lamp Tune)
　　Zeng Tianjia

　　Nanjing City, Five Old Men Village,
　　This street is very famous,
　　The sanitation work has great achievements,
　　A first-class model, known far and wide.
　　Stroll along the wide roads,
　　The road surface is clean and free of dust.
　　The factory motors are humming,
　　The schools are filled with the sound of reading,
　　In the Home of Happiness, the old people are playing chess,
　　The children in kindergarten are babbling innocently.
　　Everyone, men and women, is happy!
　　Strong and energetic!
　　Five Old Men Village is a happy village,
　　Men, women, old and young, are all smiling,
　　Looking back on the past,
　　Tears streaming down my face.
　　A folk song once described,
　　Every word is vivid:
　　In the old days, Five Old Men Village,
　　Was a village of suffering,
　　Maggots in piles, flies in swarms,
　　Dung balls rolling with the water,
　　Disease always present,
　　Mosquitoes breaking skin,
　　Garbage and sewage suffocating people!
　　Like a thunderclap in spring,
　　Poor people have turned themselves!
　　Every household cleans,
　　Every household stresses hygiene.
　　Filling in the stinky ponds,
　　Renovating the crude latrines,
　　Moving away the garbage heaps,
　　The roads are all paved.
　　Everyone is working hard to eliminate the four pests,
　　Completely free of mosquitoes and flies!
　　A brand new scene,
　　Everyone praises Five Old Men Village!

### 众口齐夸五老村（车灯调）
曾田家
1959-10-18
第8版()
专栏：

　　众口齐夸五老村（车灯调）
　　曾田家
　　南京市，五老村，
　　这条街道很有名，
　　卫生工作成绩大，
　　甲等模范远近闻。
　　宽宽马路随游逛，
　　路面整洁无灰尘。
　　工厂马达轰轰响，
　　学校朗朗读书声，
　　幸福院里老人把棋下，
　　幼儿园孩子咦呀学语好天真。
　　男女个个心舒畅！
　　身强力壮有精神！
　　五老村是幸福村，
　　男女老少笑盈盈，
　　低头回想当年事，
　　不觉两眼泪淋淋。
　　一首民谣曾描述，
    一字一句活生生：
　　当年五老村，
　　是个苦恼村，
　　蛆成堆，蝇成群，
　　屎球随水滚，
　　疫病不离身，
　　蚊子碰破脸，
　　垃圾污水熏死人！
　　霹雳一声春雷响，
　　穷苦百姓大翻身！
　　家家搞清洁，
　　户户讲卫生。
　　填平臭水塘，
　　翻改土毛坑，
　　搬走垃圾堆，
　　道路都修平。
　　除四害人人都展劲，
　　彻彻底底无蚊蝇！
　　面目一新好光景，
　　众口齐夸五老村！
