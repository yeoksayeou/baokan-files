Title: Our Party and Government Delegation Depart for Berlin to Attend the 10th Anniversary Celebration of the German Democratic Republic
Author: 
Date: 1959-10-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. In response to invitations from the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and the Government of the German Democratic Republic to attend the 10th Anniversary Celebration of the German Democratic Republic, our Party and Government Delegation, led by Nie Rongzhen, member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Vice Premier of the State Council, departed for Berlin by special plane this morning. The delegation members traveling on the same plane included Zhao Shoushan, Governor of Shaanxi Province, and Li Zhuchen, Minister of Light Industry and Vice Chairman of the China Democratic National Construction Association. Kingdom Rights, our country's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the German Democratic Republic and Ding Ying, President of the Chinese Academy of Agricultural Sciences, both members of the delegation, are already in the German Democratic Republic.

Those who saw the delegation off at the Capital Airport today included Chen Yi, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Vice Premier of the State Council; Luo Ruiqing, member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Vice Premier of the State Council; Wu Xiuquan, member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Zeng Yongquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Luo Shuzhang and Zhang Daowu, Vice Ministers of Light Industry, and others.

Wandel, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, and embassy staff members also saw the delegation off at the airport.

### 前往参加德意志民主共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼  我党政代表团启程去柏林

1959-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

    前往参加德意志民主共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼
    我党政代表团启程去柏林
    新华社3日讯　应德国统一社会党和德意志民主共和国政府的邀请参加德意志民主共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼的我国党政代表团，今天早晨由中共中央委员、国务院副总理聂荣臻率领，乘专机前往柏林。同机前往的代表团成员有陕西省省长赵寿山，轻工业部部长、中国民主建国会副主任委员李烛尘。代表团成员、我国驻德意志民主共和国特命全权大使王国权和中国农业科学院院长丁颖已在德意志民主共和国。
    今天到首都机场送行的有中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅，中共中央委员、国务院副总理罗瑞卿，中共中央委员伍修权，外交部副部长曾涌泉和轻工业部副部长罗叔章、张道吾等。
    德意志民主共和国驻华大使汪戴尔和大使馆人员也到机场送行。
