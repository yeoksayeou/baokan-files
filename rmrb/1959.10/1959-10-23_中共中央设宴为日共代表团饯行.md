Title: CCP Central Committee Holds Banquet to Bid Farewell to JCP Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-10-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd: The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party held a banquet tonight to bid farewell to all comrades of the Japanese Communist Party delegation. The banquet was filled with an extremely warm and friendly atmosphere.

The JCP comrades attending tonight's banquet included Sanzo Nosaka, Riichi Hakamada, Kura Harakazu, Kazuyoshi Suna, Masao Iwama, and Chikara Hoshino. The CCP participants included Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Deng Xiaoping, Peng Zhen, Wang Jiaxiang, Yang Shangkun, Liao Chengzhi, Liu Ningyi, Wu Xiuquan, and others.

### 中共中央设宴为日共代表团饯行

1959-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央设宴为日共代表团饯行
    新华社22日讯　中共中央今晚设宴为日本共产党代表团全体同志饯行。宴会上充满了极其热烈的友好的气氛。
    出席今晚宴会的日共同志，有野坂参三、袴田里见、藏原惟人、砂间一良、岩间正男、星野力。中共方面参加宴会的，有刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、邓小平、彭真、王稼祥、杨尚昆、廖承志、刘宁一、伍修权等。
