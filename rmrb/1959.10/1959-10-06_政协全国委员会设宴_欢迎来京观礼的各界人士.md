Title: CPPCC National Committee Hosts Banquet to Welcome People from All Walks of Life Attending the National Day Observation Ceremony in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-06
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held a banquet at noon on the 4th to welcome people from democratic parties, cultural and educational circles, business circles, and religious circles from various provinces, cities, and autonomous regions who came to Beijing to attend the National Day observation ceremony. More than 140 people attended the banquet. At the banquet, Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the CPPCC National Committee, delivered a welcome speech. The hosts and guests raised their glasses to celebrate the great victory of the general line for socialist construction and to wish Chairman Mao a long life.
    Among those attending the banquet were Li Weihan and Burhan, Vice Chairmen of the CPPCC National Committee, Xu Bing, Secretary-General, as well as the Director and Deputy Directors of the Liaison Committee, some Standing Committee members in Beijing, and Deputy Secretaries-General.

### 政协全国委员会设宴  欢迎来京观礼的各界人士

1959-10-06
第4版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会设宴
    欢迎来京观礼的各界人士
    新华社4日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会于4日中午举行宴会，欢迎各省、市、自治区来京观礼的民主党派、文教界、工商界以及宗教界人士，参加宴会的有一百四十余人。在宴会上，政协全国委员会副主席陈叔通致欢迎词，宾主之间举杯祝社会主义建设总路线的伟大胜利，祝毛主席万寿无疆。
    参加宴会的有中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席李维汉、包尔汉，秘书长徐冰，还有联络委员会主任、副主任，部分在京常务委员和副秘书长等人。
