Title: Chairman Mao Meets Comrade Khrushchev and Separately Meets Comrades Novotny and Kim Il-sung
Author:
Date: 1959-10-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 1st: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, before the grand celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China this morning, met with N.S. Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and held cordial talks.

    Present from the Soviet side at the meeting was M.A. Suslov, member of the Presidium and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.
    Present from the Chinese side at the meeting were Party and state leaders Liu Shaoqi, Soong Ching Ling, Dong Biwu, Zhu De, Zhou Enlai, and Deng Xiaoping.
    Xinhua News Agency, 1st: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China met with Antonín Novotný, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia and President of the Czechoslovak Republic, and his wife on the evening of the 1st, and held cordial talks.
    Present from the Czechoslovak side at the meeting were Rudolf Barák, member of the Politburo of the CPCz Central Committee, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Interior of the Czechoslovak Republic, and his wife; Jiří Hendrych, member of the Politburo and Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPCz, and his wife; Václav David, member of the Central Committee of the CPCz and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Czechoslovak Republic, and his wife; and Jan Bušniak, Ambassador of the Czechoslovak Republic to China, and his wife.
    Present from the Chinese side at the meeting were Li Fuchun, member of the Politburo of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, and Cai Chang, member of the CPC Central Committee and Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation.
    Xinhua News Agency, 1st: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China met with Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, on September 28th and held cordial talks.
    Present from the Korean side were Kim Chang-man, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, and Kim Kwang-hyup, member of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee.
    Present from the Chinese side were Liu Shaoqi and Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairmen of the CPC Central Committee, and Peng Zhen, member of the Politburo.

### 毛主席会见赫鲁晓夫同志  并分别会见诺沃提尼同志金日成同志

1959-10-02
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席会见赫鲁晓夫同志
    并分别会见诺沃提尼同志金日成同志
    新华社1日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东，今天上午在庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年大典以前，会见了苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记、苏联部长会议主席尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫，进行了亲切的交谈。
    会见时，苏联方面在座的有苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员、书记处书记苏斯洛夫。
    会见时中国方面在座的有党和国家的领导人刘少奇、宋庆龄、董必武、朱德、周恩来和邓小平。
    新华社1日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东在1日晚会见了捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会第一书记、捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统诺沃提尼和他的夫人，进行了亲切的交谈。
    会见时捷克斯洛伐克方面在座的，有捷共中央政治局委员、捷克斯洛伐克共和国副总理兼内务部长鲁道夫·巴拉克和他的夫人，捷共中央政治局委员、中央委员会书记依里·亨德里赫和他的夫人，捷共中央委员、捷克斯洛伐克共和国外交部部长瓦茨拉夫·戴维和他的夫人，捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻中国大使扬·布希尼亚克和他的夫人。
    会见时中国方面在座的，有中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理李富春，中共中央委员、全国妇女联合会主席蔡畅。
    新华社1日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东在9月28日会见了朝鲜劳动党中央委员会委员长金日成，进行了亲切的谈话。
    朝鲜方面在座的，有朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长金昌满，中央常务委员金光侠。
    中国方面在座的，有中共中央副主席刘少奇、周恩来，政治局委员彭真。
