Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Blum
Author:
Date: 1959-10-18
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, 17th: Vice Premier Chen Yi received Reiner Blum, Chairman of the Luxembourg-China Friendship Association, and his wife this afternoon.
　　Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, was present at the reception.

### 陈毅副总理接见布鲁姆

1959-10-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　陈毅副总理接见布鲁姆
    新华社17日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见了卢森堡中国友好协会主席雷纳·布鲁姆和他的夫人。
    接见时中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南在座。
