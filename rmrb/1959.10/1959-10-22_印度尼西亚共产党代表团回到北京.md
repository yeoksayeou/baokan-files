Title: Indonesian Communist Party Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-22
Page: 5

    Indonesian Communist Party Delegation Returns to Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, October 21st: The Indonesian Communist Party delegation, headed by Comrade Djoto, returned to Beijing by train this evening. Prior to this, the Indonesian Communist Party delegation had visited and toured the cities of Xi'an, Yan'an, Chengdu, Chongqing, Wuhan, Shanghai, and Hangzhou.

### 印度尼西亚共产党代表团回到北京

1959-10-22
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚共产党代表团回到北京
    新华社21日讯　以约多同志为首的印度尼西亚共产党代表团，于今天晚上乘火车回到北京。印度尼西亚共产党代表团在这以前曾参观访问了西安、延安、成都、重庆、武汉、上海、杭州等城市。
