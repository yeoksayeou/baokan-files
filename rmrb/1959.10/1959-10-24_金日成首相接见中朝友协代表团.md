Title: Premier Kim Il-sung Receives Delegation from the Sino-Korean Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1959-10-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 23rd: Premier Kim Il-sung of the Korean Cabinet received this morning the delegation from the Sino-Korean Friendship Association, headed by Wei Chuantong, Vice President of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association, and all members of the delegation. Qiao Xiaoguang, the Chinese Ambassador to Korea, accompanied the delegation.

Present at the reception were Park Chung-ae, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party; Ha Ang-cheon, Chairman of the Korea-China Friendship Association; and Park Sung-chul, Director of the International Department of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party.

Premier Kim Il-sung had a friendly and cordial conversation with the members of the delegation.

### 金日成首相接见中朝友协代表团

1959-10-24
第6版()
专栏：

    金日成首相接见中朝友协代表团
    新华社平壤23日电　朝鲜内阁首相金日成今天上午接见了中朝友好协会代表团团长、中朝友好协会副会长魏传统和代表团全体团员。中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光陪同代表团前往。
    接见时在座的有朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱，朝中友好协会委员长河仰天，劳动党中央国际部长朴成哲。
    金日成首相同代表团团员们作了友好和亲切的谈话。
