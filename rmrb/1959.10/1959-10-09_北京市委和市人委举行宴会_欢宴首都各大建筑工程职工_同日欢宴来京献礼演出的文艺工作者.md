Title: Beijing Municipal Party Committee and Municipal People's Committee Hold Banquet to Celebrate Capital's Major Construction Project Workers; Simultaneously Host Banquet for Art Workers Performing in Beijing for National Day Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1959-10-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 8th -- The Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Beijing Municipal People's Committee held a banquet tonight in the Great Hall of the People to celebrate the successful completion of construction projects such as the Great Hall of the People and the victory of the Miyun Reservoir flood control project.

Premier Zhou Enlai attended the banquet.

Tonight, over 300 people, including administrative heads, designers, engineers, advanced workers of the Great Hall of the People, the Revolutionary Museum, the Museum of History, the Military Museum, the Agricultural Exhibition Hall, the National Cultural Palace, the Workers' Stadium, the Beijing Railway Station, the Minzu Hotel, the Overseas Chinese Mansion, and other construction projects, as well as the Miyun Reservoir, and representatives from relevant units in the capital, gathered together in joy. The venue was filled with a warm and joyful atmosphere.

Peng Zhen, First Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Mayor of Beijing, spoke at the banquet. On behalf of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Municipal People's Committee, he expressed his gratitude to the builders of the major projects in the capital. He emphasized that the successful completion of the major construction projects in the capital should first and foremost be attributed to the wise leadership of the Party. It is the result of the Great Leap Forward and a great victory for the Party's General Line.

Peng Zhen said that under the direct leadership and concern of the Party Central Committee and the State Council, and with the strong support of the central ministries and the vigorous assistance of various provinces (autonomous regions) and municipalities, our capital's construction has achieved great success. However, we must not be complacent. In order to build the capital even better, we need to continue to exert our utmost efforts, strive for the top, and continue the Great Leap Forward.

Also attending the banquet tonight were Minister of Metallurgical Industry Wang Heshou, Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building Zhao Erlu, Minister of Construction Engineering Liu Xiufeng, as well as other Vice Ministers of relevant ministries such as Lü Zhengcao, Li Baohua, and Qian Junrui, Deputy Directors of relevant committees, and senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Xiao Hua, Yang Yong, and Liu Zhijian.

Liu Ren, Second Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Wan Li, Secretary of the Secretariat and Vice Mayor of Beijing; Chen Peng and Chen Kehan, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; and Wu Han, Wang Kunlun, Cheng Hongyi, Jia Tingsan, and Le Songsheng, Vice Mayors of Beijing, as well as heads of various departments, also attended the banquet.

After the banquet, the Fujian Provincial Opera Troupe gave a wonderful performance.

Xinhua News Agency, 8th -- The Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Beijing Municipal People's Committee held a banquet at noon today in the banquet hall of the Great Hall of the People, joyfully hosting more than 2,300 cultural and art workers of various ethnic groups from more than 30 art troupes from various regions and the Chinese People's Liberation Army who participated in the National Day celebrations.

Peng Zhen, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, First Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Mayor of Beijing, raised his glass at the beginning of the banquet to congratulate the Great Leap Forward in literary and artistic work, and encouraged everyone to make persistent efforts and strive for a greater and better leap forward. During the banquet, representatives of actors of all ethnic groups who came to Beijing to participate in the National Day celebrations offered toasts, and many famous actors took the stage to sing impromptu, filling the entire banquet hall with an atmosphere of unity and joy.

Also attending today's banquet were Lu Dingyi, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Vice Premier of the State Council; Zhou Yang, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Qi Yanming, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council; Qian Junrui and Xu Maijin, Deputy Directors of the Cultural and Educational Office of the State Council; Xia Yan and Lin Mohan, Vice Ministers of Culture; Jiang Siyi, Director of the Propaganda Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Zhang Xiuzhu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; and famous figures in the literary and art circles of the capital Yang Hansheng, Tian Han, Ouyang Yuqian, Lao She, Zhou Weizhi, Mei Lanfang, Wu Zuoren, Cai Ruohong, Tao Dun, Sheng Jie, and heads of cultural and artistic circles in the capital, famous writers, directors, actors, conductors, photographers, musicians, and artists.

Before the banquet, Comrade Peng Zhen, together with Liu Ren, Second Secretary of the Municipal Party Committee, Vice Mayor Wan Li, General Yang Yong of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and other responsible comrades of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Municipal People's Committee, also met with the heads and leading actors of the theater troupes from various places who came to Beijing to participate in the National Day celebrations: Yu Zhenfei, Yan Huizhu, Chen Bohua, Chang Xiangyu, Xu Yulan, Ma Jianling, Su Yumin, Han Junqing, Peng Linong, Gao Xizhong, Qi Linyu, Sun Tai, Li Mingyu, Lin Dongzhi, Cai Dan, Jin Renshu, Bao Yindeligeer, Zhang Changcheng, Gu Baozhang, Li Yin, Wang Shan, and others, and held cordial talks.

### 北京市委和市人委举行宴会  欢宴首都各大建筑工程职工  同日欢宴来京献礼演出的文艺工作者

1959-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    北京市委和市人委举行宴会
    欢宴首都各大建筑工程职工
    同日欢宴来京献礼演出的文艺工作者
    新华社8日讯　中共北京市委员会和北京市人民委员会今晚在人民大会堂举行宴会，庆祝人民大会堂等建筑工程的胜利完成和密云水库拦洪工程的胜利。
    周恩来总理出席了宴会。
    今晚，人民大会堂、革命博物馆、历史博物馆、军事博物馆、农业展览馆、民族文化宫、工人体育场、北京车站、民族饭店和华侨大厦等建筑工程和密云水库的行政负责人、设计师、工程师、先进工作者以及首都各有关单位的代表，共三百多人欢聚一堂。会场上充满了热烈欢乐的气氛。
    中共北京市委会第一书记、北京市市长彭真在宴会上讲话。他代表中共北京市委和市人委向首都各大工程的建设者表示感谢。他强调指出，首都各大建筑工程的胜利完成，首先应归功于党的英明领导。它是大跃进的成果，是党的总路线的伟大胜利。
    彭真说，在党中央和国务院的直接领导和关怀下，在中央各部的大力支持与各省（区）、市大力支援之下，我们首都的建设取得了伟大的成绩。但是，我们还不要自满。为了把首都建设得更美好，我们还需要继续鼓足干劲，力争上游，继续大跃进。
    出席今晚宴会的还有冶金工业部部长王鹤寿，第一机械工业部部长赵尔陆，建筑工程部部长刘秀峰以及其他各有关部副部长吕正操、李葆华、钱俊瑞等，各有关委员会副主任，中国人民解放军高级将领萧华、杨勇、刘志坚等。
    中共北京市委第二书记刘仁，书记处书记、北京市副市长万里，中共北京市委书记处书记陈鹏、陈克寒，北京市副市长吴晗、王昆仑、程宏毅、贾庭三、乐松生以及各部门负责人也出席了宴会。
    宴会后，由福建省戏曲巡回演出团演出了精采节目。
    新华社8日讯　中共北京市委员会和北京市人民委员会今天中午在人民大会堂宴会大厅，欢宴各地区和中国人民解放军参加庆祝建国十周年献礼演出的三十多个文艺团体的二千三百多名各族文化艺术工作者。
    中共中央政治局委员、中共北京市委第一书记、北京市市长彭真在宴会开始时举杯祝贺文艺工作大跃进，并勉励大家再接再厉，争取更大更好的跃进。在宴会进行中，各地来京参加献礼演出的各族演员代表纷纷祝酒，许多著名演员即兴登台演唱，整个宴会大厅里充满了团结欢乐的气氛。
    出席今天宴会的，还有中共中央政治局候补委员、国务院副总理陆定一，中共中央宣传部副部长周扬，国务院副秘书长齐燕铭，国务院文教办公室副主任钱俊瑞、徐迈进，文化部副部长夏衍、林默涵，中国人民解放军总政治部宣传部部长姜思毅，中华全国总工会书记处书记张修竹，以及首都文艺界著名人士阳翰笙、田汉、欧阳予倩、老舍、周巍峙、梅兰芳、吴作人、蔡若虹、陶钝、盛婕，和首都文化艺术界的负责人、著名作家、导演、演员、指挥、摄影师、音乐工作者和美术工作者等。
    在宴会前，彭真同志和市委第二书记刘仁、副市长万里、中国人民解放军杨勇上将，以及中共北京市委、市人民委员会其他负责同志还会见了各地来京参加庆祝建国十周年献礼演出的剧团负责人和主要演员俞振飞、言慧珠、陈伯华、常香玉、徐玉兰、马健翎、苏育民、韩俊卿、彭俐侬、高希中，祁林玉、孙泰、李铭玉、林栋治、才丹、金仁淑、宝音德力格尔、张长城、顾宝璋、李茵、王珊等，并且进行了亲切的谈话。
