Title: Foreign Dignitaries Visit and Tour Various Locations; Bulgarian Party and Government Delegation Leaves Hangzhou for Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1959-10-08
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, October 7th. The Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee Political Bureau member, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, Georgi Traykov, and the Bulgarian People's Republic Party and Government Delegation he led, departed Hangzhou this morning by special plane for a visit to Shanghai after their tour in Hangzhou. An Ziwen, member of the CPC Central Committee and Minister of the Organization Department of the CPC Central Committee, accompanied them.

Jiang Hua, alternate member of the CPC Central Committee and First Secretary of the CPC Zhejiang Provincial Committee, Zhou Jianren, Governor of Zhejiang Province, Wu Xian, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Zhejiang Provincial Committee, Deputy Governor of Zhejiang Province, First Secretary of the CPC Hangzhou Municipal Committee, and Mayor of Hangzhou, and Lieutenant General Zhou Guanwu, leading officer of the Zhejiang Garrison, were at the airport to see off the distinguished guests.

Yesterday, accompanied by An Ziwen and Wu Xian, the distinguished guests visited the Du Jinsheng Silk Weaving Factory in Hangzhou. The guests carefully learned about the production process in each workshop of the factory, as well as the training of technicians and the production situation. Chairman Traykov encouraged the workers to maintain the glorious title of advanced unit and wished them greater achievements in production. Afterwards, the distinguished guests visited the Qiantang River Bridge, toured the scenic spots and historical sites of West Lake, and visited the city of Hangzhou. Yesterday evening, Jiang Hua, First Secretary of the CPC Zhejiang Provincial Committee, and Zhou Jianren, Governor of Zhejiang Province, hosted a banquet to welcome the distinguished guests.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, October 7th. The Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee Political Bureau member, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, Georgi Traykov, and the Bulgarian People's Republic Party and Government Delegation he led, arrived in Shanghai this morning by special plane from Hangzhou for a visit. An Ziwen, member of the CPC Central Committee, accompanied them.

Welcoming the distinguished guests at the airport were Ke Qingshi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, First Secretary of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee, and Mayor of Shanghai, Chen Pixian, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee, Cao Diqiu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee and Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, Kang Zhiqiang and Jiang Tengjiao, leading officers of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Shanghai, as well as responsible persons from the Shanghai local organizations of various democratic parties, people's organizations, and representatives from all walks of life.

Consuls from various countries stationed in Shanghai also went to the airport to welcome them.

After arriving in Shanghai, accompanied by An Ziwen, Chen Pixian, and Shanghai Deputy Mayor Niu Shucai, the distinguished guests visited the site of the First National Congress of the CPC—the Shanghai Revolutionary History Memorial Hall, the Shanghai Electric Machinery Factory, and the Shanghai Turbine Factory, and toured the urban area of Shanghai by car.

### 各国贵宾在各地参观访问  保加利亚党政代表团离杭州到上海

1959-10-08
第2版()
专栏：

    各国贵宾在各地参观访问
    保加利亚党政代表团离杭州到上海
    新华社杭州7日电　保加利亚共产党中央委员会政治局委员、保加利亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席吉·加?夫和他率领的保加利亚人民共和国党政代表团，在杭州参观访问后，今天上午乘专机离开杭州去上海访问。中共中央委员、中共中央组织部部长安子文陪同前往。
    中共中央候补委员、中共浙江省委第一书记江华，浙江省省长周建人，中共浙江省委书记处书记、浙江省副省长、中共杭州市委第一书记、杭州市市长吴宪，浙江驻军首长周贯五中将等到机场欢送贵宾。
    昨天，贵宾们由安子文、吴宪陪同，在杭州访问了都锦生丝织厂。贵宾们仔细了解了这个厂各个车间的生产过程，以及培养技术工人和生产方面的情况。加?夫主席勉励工人们保持先进单位的光荣称号，并预祝工人们在生产上取得更大的成就。随后，贵宾们参观了钱塘江大桥，游览了西湖名胜古迹，并参观了杭州市容。昨天晚上，中共浙江省委第一书记江华和浙江省省长周建人设宴欢迎贵宾。
    据新华社上海7日电　保加利亚共产党中央委员会政治局委员、保加利亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席吉·加?夫和他率领的保加利亚人民共和国党政代表团，今天上午由杭州乘专机到达上海访问。中共中央委员安子文陪同前来。
    在机场上欢迎贵宾的有中共中央政治局委员、中共上海市委第一书记、上海市市长柯庆施，中共上海市委书记处书记陈丕显，中共上海市委书记处书记、上海市副市长曹荻秋，人民解放军驻上海部队首长康志强、江腾蛟以及各民主党派上海地方组织、各人民团体负责人和各界代表人士。
    各国驻上海领事也到机场欢迎。
    贵宾们到上海后，在安子文、陈丕显和上海市副市长牛树才等陪同下，参观访问了中共第一次全国代表大会会址——上海革命历史纪念馆和上海电机厂、上海汽轮机厂，并驱车游览了上海市区。
