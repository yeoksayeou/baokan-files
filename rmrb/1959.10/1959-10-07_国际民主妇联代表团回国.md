Title: Delegation of the Women's International Democratic Federation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 6th — The delegation of the Women's International Democratic Federation, invited to our country to participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, headed by Mrs. Marie-Claude Vaillant-Couturier, and members Adela Betineli and Zeinab Nabukh, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.

Prior to this, members Karen Snastrup and Elde Laszloni had left Beijing to return home on the 2nd and 3rd respectively.

### 国际民主妇联代表团回国

1959-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    国际民主妇联代表团回国
    新华社6日讯　应邀来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的国际民主妇女联合会代表团团长玛丽—克洛德·瓦扬——古久里夫人，团员阿德拉·贝蒂内利和宰伊纳卜·纳布赫，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    在这以前，团员卡伦·斯纳斯特鲁普和埃尔德·拉斯洛妮已分别于2日、3日离北京回国。
