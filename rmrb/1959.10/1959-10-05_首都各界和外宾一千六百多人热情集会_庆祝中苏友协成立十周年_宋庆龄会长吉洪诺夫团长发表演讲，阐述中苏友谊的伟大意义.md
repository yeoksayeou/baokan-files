Title: Over 1,600 People from All Circles in the Capital and Foreign Guests Enthusiastically Gather to Celebrate the Tenth Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Chairman Soong Ching Ling and Head of Delegation Tikhonov Deliver Speeches, Elaborating on the Great Significance of Sino-Soviet Friendship
Author: 
Date: 1959-10-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The grand rally celebrating the tenth anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association was solemnly held at Huairen Hall this afternoon.

Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Premier of the State Council, Soong Ching Ling, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic and President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Dong Biwu, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and others attended the celebration rally.

Tikhonov, head of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, Deputy Head Danilov, and all members of the delegation also attended the celebration rally.

Huairen Hall was filled with a friendly and warm atmosphere today. At the entrance to the venue, a deep red velvet curtain was adorned with Chairman Mao's inscription: "The unity of the Chinese and Soviet people is extremely important. When the people of these two countries are united, the unity of the people of the world will not be difficult." The front of the rostrum was filled with flowers, and in the center hung the emblem of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the golden characters "1949-1959," marking the increasing consolidation and strengthening of the friendship and unity between the Chinese and Soviet people over the past decade.

At 3:10 PM, Wu Yuzhang, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, announced the opening of the celebration rally. The band played the national anthems of China and the Soviet Union.

Vice President Wu Yuzhang delivered an opening speech, saying that we are holding this rally to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association with the most joyful mood. On behalf of all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, he expressed the warmest welcome to everyone for attending today's celebration rally.

Soong Ching Ling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, delivered a speech entitled "Great Friendship, Great Unity" amidst warm applause (full text on page 3). Then, the entire venue welcomed Tikhonov, head of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, with thunderous applause (full text on page 3). In their speeches, they elaborated on the great significance of the friendship and unity between the Chinese and Soviet people.

Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, then read out the news at the meeting that the Soviet Union successfully launched its third cosmic rocket on October 4, 1959. At this moment, warm applause rang out once again throughout the venue.

Wu Yuzhang, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, delivered the closing remarks. He said that common ideals and common interests firmly unite the people of China and the Soviet Union. No force in the world can hinder the development and consolidation of the great friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people. In the future, under the correct leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and with the enthusiastic support of the Soviet people, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association will redouble its efforts to further strengthen Sino-Soviet friendship, to achieve new victories in China's socialist construction, and to strive to defend world peace.

Also on the rostrum today were Lin Boqu, Peng Zhen, and Chen Yi, members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat, Panchen Erdeni, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Lu Dingyi, Vice Premier of the State Council, Guo Moruo, Li Jishen, Wu Yuzhang, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Cheng Qian, Ulanhu, Liu Ningyi, Liao Chengzhi, Li Dequan, Li Siguang, Shen Yanbing, and Shao Lizi, Vice Presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Qian Junrui, Secretary-General, and Deng Tuo, President of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

Also on the rostrum were Gur, Deputy Head of the Mongolian Labor People's Delegation, Antonov, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, and Counselor Sudarikov, and Fumin, Chief of the Soviet Experts.

Participating in today's conference were responsible persons from various democratic parties and people's organizations, national industrial and agricultural labor models, senior generals and combat heroes of the People's Liberation Army, representatives of various ethnic minorities and returned overseas Chinese, as well as responsible persons and representatives from various agencies, organizations, factories, mines, and enterprises of the Central Government and Beijing, members of people's communes, and representatives from various schools.

Diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various brother countries stationed in China, various Soviet delegations and representatives who came to participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of China's founding and to visit, Soviet experts who are helping China in its construction, government delegations and representatives from Asian and African countries, as well as other foreign guests, participated in today's conference.

A total of more than 1,600 people participated in today's conference. The celebration rally concluded in the song "Moscow-Beijing" and warm applause. After the celebration rally, famous Chinese singers Guo Lanying and Guo Shuzhen, and national song and dance troupes performed wonderful programs.

### 首都各界和外宾一千六百多人热情集会  庆祝中苏友协成立十周年  宋庆龄会长吉洪诺夫团长发表演讲，阐述中苏友谊的伟大意义

1959-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    首都各界和外宾一千六百多人热情集会
    庆祝中苏友协成立十周年
    宋庆龄会长吉洪诺夫团长发表演讲，阐述中苏友谊的伟大意义
    据新华社4日讯　庆祝中苏友好协会成立十周年大会今天下午在怀仁堂隆重举行。
    中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来、中华人民共和国副主席、中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄、中华人民共和国副主席董必武等出席了庆祝大会。
    苏中友好协会代表团团长吉洪诺夫、副团长达尼洛夫和全体团员，也出席了庆祝大会。
    怀仁堂今天充满着友好、热烈的气氛。大会会场入口处，迎面一幅深红色的丝绒帷幕上面，缀着毛主席的亲笔题词：“中苏两国人民的团结极为重要，这两国人民团结一致了，世界人民的团结就不困难了。”主席台的前面摆满了鲜花，正中高悬中苏友好协会的会徽和金色的1949—1959的字样，标志着十年来中苏两国人民友谊和团结的日益巩固与加强。
    三时十分，中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章，宣布庆祝大会开始。乐队奏中、苏两国国歌。
    吴玉章副会长致开幕词说，我们怀着最愉快的心情来举行庆祝中苏友好协会成立十周年的大会。他代表中苏友好协会全体会员对大家光临今天的庆祝大会，表示最热烈的欢迎。
    中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄，在热烈的掌声中发表了题为“伟大的友谊，伟大的团结”的讲话（全文见第三版）。接着，全场以春雷般的掌声，欢迎苏中友好协会代表团团长吉洪诺夫讲话（全文见第三版）。他们在讲话中阐述了中苏人民的友谊和团结的伟大意义。
    中苏友好协会总会秘书长钱俊瑞，接着在会上朗读了苏联在1959年10月4日成功地发射了第三个宇宙火箭的新闻。这时全场再一次响起了热烈的掌声。
    中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章最后致结束语。他说，共同的理想和共同的利益把中苏两国人民牢牢地团结在一起。世界上没有任何力量能够阻碍中苏两国人民伟大友谊的发展和巩固。今后，中苏友好协会在中国共产党的正确领导下，在苏联人民的热情支持下，将加倍努力，为进一步加强中苏友谊、为我国社会主义建设事业的新的胜利，为保卫世界和平而努力奋斗。
    今天在主席台上的，还有中共中央政治局委员林伯渠、彭真、陈毅，书记处书记王稼祥，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长班禅额尔德尼，国务院副总理陆定一，中苏友好协会总会副会长郭沫若、李济深、吴玉章、沈钧儒、黄炎培、程潜、乌兰夫、刘宁一、廖承志、李德全、李四光、沈雁冰、邵力子，秘书长钱俊瑞，北京市中苏友好协会会长邓拓。
    在主席台上的，还有蒙古劳动人民代表团副团长古尔，苏联驻华大使馆临时代办安东诺夫和参赞苏达利柯夫，苏联专家总负责人符明。
    参加今天大会的有各民主党派和各人民团体的负责人，全国工业和农业劳动模范，人民解放军高级将领和战斗英雄，各少数民族和归国华侨代表，以及中央和北京市各机关、各团体、各厂矿企业负责人和代表，人民公社社员和各学校代表等。
    各兄弟国家驻华使节和外交官员，前来参加我国建国十周年庆祝典礼和访问的苏联各个代表团和代表，帮助我国建设的苏联专家，亚非各国政府代表团和代表，以及其他各国来宾，参加了今天的大会。
    参加今天大会的共一千六百多人。庆祝大会在“莫斯科—北京”歌声和热烈的掌声中闭幕。庆祝大会以后，中国著名歌唱家郭兰英、郭淑贞和民族歌舞团体表演了精彩的节目。
