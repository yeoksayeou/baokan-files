Title: Picture
Author: Liu Dong'ao
Date: 1959-10-21
Page: 2

    Mr. Matsumura Kenzo, a Japanese guest invited by Premier Zhou Enlai to visit our country, and his entourage arrived in Beijing by plane on October 20th. The picture shows Liao Chengzhi accompanying Mr. Matsumura Kenzo and others as they walk out of the airport. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dong'ao)
