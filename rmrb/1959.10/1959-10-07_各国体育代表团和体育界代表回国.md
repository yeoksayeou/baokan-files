Title: Sports Delegations and Sports Representatives from Various Countries Return Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th - Sports delegations from the Soviet Union, Bulgaria, Romania, Hungary, North Korea, Czechoslovakia, Poland, the Mongolian People's Republic, the German Democratic Republic, and Vietnam, the sports delegation of the French Labor Sports Federation, sports representatives from Iraq and Sudan, and Mr. Roy Evans, Honorary Secretary of the International Table Tennis Federation, who were invited to visit the First National Games of our country and participate in the celebrations of the tenth anniversary of the founding of our country, have already left Beijing for home between the 2nd and 6th.

### 各国体育代表团和体育界代表回国

1959-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    各国体育代表团和体育界代表回国
    新华社6日讯　应邀来我国参观第一届全国运动会和参加我国建国十周年庆祝典礼的苏联、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、朝鲜、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、蒙古人民共和国、德意志民主共和国和越南的体育代表团，法国劳动体育联合会体育代表团和伊拉克、苏丹的体育界代表，以及国际乒乓球联合会名誉秘书长罗依·伊凡斯等，已经分别在2日到6日离北京回国。
