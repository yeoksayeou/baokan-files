Title: Algerian, Iraqi, and Nepalese Government Delegations Visit Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, 30th - Algerian, Iraqi, and Nepalese government delegations, who came to our country to participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, have been visiting Beijing in the past few days.

    The Algerian Republic Provisional Government Delegation, headed by Ben Youcef Ben Khedda, Minister of Social Welfare of the Provisional Government of the Algerian Republic, and the Iraqi Republic Government Delegation, headed by Brigadier General Ahmed Muhammad Yehia, Minister of Interior of the Republic of Iraq, visited the Museum of History and the Industrial Transportation Exhibition. The Iraqi Republic Government Delegation also toured Beijing's cityscape and the Summer Palace, visited the Great Wall and the Ming Tombs, and visited Tsinghua University.

    The Nepalese Royal Government Delegation, headed by Dr. Tulsi Giri, Minister of Rural Development of the Kingdom of Nepal, visited the Forbidden City, the newly built Beijing Railway Station and the Zoo, and also toured the Summer Palace and visited the Beijing Electronic Tube Factory.

    The distinguished guests also watched our dance drama "Lotus Lantern" and the ballet "Le Corsaire."

### 阿尔及利亚　伊拉克　尼泊尔  三国政府代表团在京参观

1959-10-01
第7版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚　伊拉克　尼泊尔
    三国政府代表团在京参观
    新华社30日讯　来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团、伊拉克共和国政府代表团和尼泊尔王国政府代表团，这几天在北京进行了参观访问。
    以阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府社会福利部长本·优素福·本·赫达为首的阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团和以伊拉克共和国内政部长艾哈迈德·穆罕默德·叶海亚准将为首的伊拉克共和国政府代表团参观了历史博物馆和工业交通展览会，伊拉克共和国政府代表团还游览了北京的市容和颐和园，参观了长城和十三陵，并访问了清华大学。
    以尼泊尔王国乡村发展大臣图尔西·吉里博士为首的尼泊尔王国政府代表团参观了故宫、新建的北京车站和动物园，还游览了颐和园和访问了北京电子管厂。
    贵宾们还观看了我国的舞剧“宝莲灯”和芭蕾舞剧“海侠”。
