Title: Delegations and Representatives of Four Fraternal Parties Visit Xi'an and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1959-10-08
Page: 2
    Xinhua News Agency, 7th dispatch: The delegations and representatives of four fraternal parties who attended the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China left Beijing this morning to visit various parts of our country.
    The Japanese Communist Party delegation, headed by Comrade Nosaka Sanzō, went to Xi'an; the Belgian Communist Party delegation, headed by Comrade Frans Van den Branden, went to Shenyang; the Nepalese Communist Party delegation, headed by Comrade Keshar Jung Rayamajhi, went to Guangzhou; and Comrade Azar Ojetlu, a member of the Iranian People's Party delegation, went to Shanghai.

### 四个兄弟党的代表团和代表到西安等地访问

1959-10-08
第2版()
专栏：

    四个兄弟党的代表团和代表到西安等地访问
    新华社7日讯　参加了中华人民共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼的四个兄弟党的代表团和代表，今天上午离开北京到我国各地参观访问。
    以野坂参三同志为首的日本共产党代表团去西安，以弗朗斯·范登布兰敦同志为首的比利时共产党代表团去沈阳，以克沙尔·忠格·腊伊马吉同志为首的尼泊尔共产党代表团去广州，伊朗人民党代表团团员阿札尔·欧捷鲁同志去上海。
