Title: Japanese Communist Party Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1959-10-24
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd: The Japanese Communist Party delegation, headed by Comrade Sanzo Nosaka, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, left Beijing by special plane this morning to return home, after attending the celebrations of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and concluding its visit to our country.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee, and Wu Xiuquan, member of the Central Committee.

Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji, who is in Beijing, also went to the airport to see them off. (Picture attached)

### 日本共产党代表团离京回国

1959-10-24
第2版()
专栏：

    日本共产党代表团离京回国
    新华社23日讯　以日本共产党中央委员会主席野坂参三同志为首的日本共产党代表团，在参加了中华人民共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼和结束了在我国的访问之后，于今日上午乘专机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的，有中共中央副主席朱德，中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记彭真，中央书记处书记王稼祥，中央委员伍修权。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也到机场欢送。（附图片）
    朱德同志在机场上和野坂参三同志道别。　新华社记者　牛畏予摄
