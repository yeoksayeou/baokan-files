Title: Foreign Cultural Association Holds Symposium for Latin American Guests
Author:
Date: 1959-10-05
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries held a symposium on the 3rd for seventy guests from fourteen Latin American countries to discuss the situation in China's economy, politics, law, culture, and other fields. The symposium was presided over by Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries.
The fourteen Latin American countries that participated in the symposium were: Argentina, Brazil, Chile, Colombia, Cuba, Guatemala, Haiti, Mexico, Nicaragua, Paraguay, Peru, Bolivia, Uruguay, and Venezuela. The attendees were well-known figures and representatives from various sectors, including culture, art, education, political science, law, and economics.
Chinese figures from various fields, including economics, culture, and political science and law, participated in the symposiums related to these areas.

### 对外文协为拉丁美洲贵宾举行座谈会

1959-10-05
第2版()
专栏：

    对外文协为拉丁美洲贵宾举行座谈会
    新华社4日讯　中国人民对外文化协会于3日上午为拉丁美洲十四个国家的七十位客人举行了有关我国经济、政法、文化等方面情况的座谈会。座谈会由中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南主持。
    分别参加座谈会的十四个拉丁美洲国家是：阿根廷、巴西、智利、哥伦比亚、古巴、危地马拉、海地、墨西哥、尼加拉瓜、巴拉圭、秘鲁、玻利维亚、乌拉圭、委内瑞拉等国的文化、艺术、教育、政法、经济等各界著名人士和代表。
    参加经济、文化、政法方面座谈的，有我国经济、文化、政法等各方面人士。
