Title: Two Poems
Author: Chen Yi
Date: 1959-10-21
Page: 8

    Two Poems
    Chen Yi
    Ten years, ten years, joy ascends Tiananmen,
    The magnificent square is filled with auspicious air.
    The world faces great changes,
    The Sino-Soviet unity is flawless jade.
    Asia and Africa celebrate newly established nations,
    Who in Europe and America summons the soul of unemployment?
    Red spreads across the East, truth resides,
    Do not forget Lenin's early detailed arguments.

  Chang'an, Chang'an, the grand avenue shines brightly,
    East is the morning sun, west the evening glow.
    Giant buildings scrape the sky in startling prominence,
    Crafts and arts bloom everywhere, vying for splendor.
    China's leap forward with newborn strength,
    A friendship flower of nations.
    The second step of the revolutionary Long March,
    Will ride a raft to the universe.

### 诗二首

1959-10-21
第8版()
专栏：

    诗二首
    陈毅
    十年十年喜上天安门，雄伟广场瑞气存。世界面临大改革，中苏团结玉无痕。亚非常庆新开国，欧美谁招失业魂。红遍东方真理在，莫忘列宁早细论。
  长安长安大道放光华，东是朝暾西月霞。巨厦摩天惊突兀，工艺遍地斗奇葩。神州跃进新生力，万国衣冠友谊花。革命长征第二步，会向宇宙泛仙槎。
