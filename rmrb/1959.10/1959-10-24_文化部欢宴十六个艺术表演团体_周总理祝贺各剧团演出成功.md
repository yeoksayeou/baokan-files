Title: Ministry of Culture Hosts Banquet for Sixteen Performing Arts Troupes; Premier Zhou Congratulates Troupes on Successful Performances
Author:
Date: 1959-10-24
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd: The Ministry of Culture held a grand banquet today at noon to entertain the sixteen performing arts troupes from various provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions in Beijing.

Premier Zhou Enlai attended the banquet, along with other Party and government leaders including Peng Zhen, Chen Yi, He Long, Li Xiannian, Lu Dingyi, Kang Sheng, Luo Ruiqing, Nie Rongzhen, Zhou Yang, and others.

Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, Vice Ministers Qian Junrui and Xia Yan, and other government officials also attended the banquet.

Premier Zhou, while raising his glass to congratulate the troupes on their successful performances, pointed out with great enthusiasm: "Over the past ten years, the literary and art front has achieved great victories under the guidance of Chairman Mao's literary and art policies."

He congratulated the literary and art circles on their achievements in letting a hundred flowers bloom and weeding through the old to bring forth the new. He said: "We place more hope on the younger generation. At the same time, veteran artists have rich artistic experience that is worth learning from for young people. New and old literary and art workers should learn from each other, help each other, and jointly develop our country's drama cause."

Premier Zhou encouraged literary and art workers to adhere to the revolutionary spirit, constantly advance, and continuously innovate to facilitate weeding through the old to bring forth the new and letting a hundred flowers bloom; to study diligently, strengthen practice, and improve their ideological and artistic levels.

The performing troupes from various provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions participating in today's banquet included: the Yunnan Peking Opera Troupe, the Guangdong Chao Opera Institute, the Sichuan Youth Sichuan Opera Troupe, the Guangxi Gui Opera Troupe, the Jiangxi Tea-Picking Opera Troupe, the Hebei Province Leap Forward Bangzi Opera Troupe, the Tianjin Little Hundred Flowers Bangzi Opera Troupe, the Hebi City Henan Opera Troupe, the Xingtai Henan Opera Troupe No. 1, the Hebei Handan East Wind Henan Opera Troupe, the Xinjiang Production and Construction Corps Peking Opera Theatre, the Benxi Drama Troupe, the Ningxia Autonomous Region Song and Dance Troupe, the Tibet Autonomous Region Song and Dance Troupe, the Xinjiang Military Region Song and Dance Troupe, and the Lanzhou Military Region Combat Song and Dance Troupe. Most of the actors in these troupes are young.

Mei Lanfang, Li Shaochun, Du Jinfang, Li Yilan, Li Guiyun, and other famous actors of various Beijing opera genres, as well as well-known figures in the capital's literary and art circles such as Tian Han and Lao She, were invited to attend the banquet. A total of more than 1,300 people attended the banquet. During the banquet, new and old actors had cordial conversations, and actors from various troupes also performed to add to the festive atmosphere.

### 文化部欢宴十六个艺术表演团体  周总理祝贺各剧团演出成功

1959-10-24
第2版()
专栏：

    文化部欢宴十六个艺术表演团体
    周总理祝贺各剧团演出成功
    新华社23日讯　文化部今天中午举行盛大宴会，招待各省、市、自治区来京的十六个艺术表演团体。
    周恩来总理出席了宴会，出席宴会的党政负责同志还有：彭真、陈毅、贺龙、李先念、陆定一、康生、罗瑞卿、聂荣臻、周扬等。
    文化部部长沈雁冰，副部长钱俊瑞、夏衍以及政府其他负责人，也出席了宴会。
    周总理在举杯祝贺各剧团演出成功时，十分兴奋地指出：“十年来，文艺战线在毛主席的文艺方针指导下获得了伟大的胜利”。
    他祝贺文艺界在百花齐放、推陈出新方面所取得的成就。他说：“我们寄更多的希望于年青的一代，同时，老艺人有丰富的艺术经验，值得青年人学习，新老文艺工作者要互相学习，互相帮助，共同发展我国的戏剧事业。”
    周总理勉励文艺工作者要坚持革命精神，不断前进，不断革新，以利推陈出新，百花齐放；要努力学习，加强实践，以提高思想水平和艺术水平。
    今天参加宴会的各省、市、自治区来京演出团体有：云南京剧团、广东潮剧院、四川青年川剧团、广西桂剧团、江西采茶剧团、河北省跃进梆子剧团、天津市小百花梆子剧团、鹤壁市豫剧团、邢台豫剧一团、河北邯郸东风豫剧团、新疆生产建设兵团京剧院、本溪话剧团、宁夏自治区歌舞团、西藏自治区歌舞团、新疆军区歌舞团、兰州军区战斗歌舞团等，这些剧团中大部分是青年演员。
    梅兰芳、李少春、杜近芳、李忆兰、李桂云等北京各剧种著名演员和首都文艺界著名人士田汉、老舍等应邀出席了宴会。出席宴会的共有一千三百多人。席间，新老演员亲切交谈，各地剧团演员还表演助兴。
