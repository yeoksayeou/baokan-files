Title: Chen Yi Receives the Ghana Trade Friendship Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-10-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Ghana Trade Friendship Delegation headed by Mr. Kojo, Minister of Trade and Industry of Ghana, this afternoon.

Present at the reception were Lu Xuzhang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; He Gongkai, Deputy Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Liu Xiwu, Deputy Director of the Fourth Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade.

### 陈毅接见加纳贸易友好代表团

1959-10-06
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅接见加纳贸易友好代表团
    新华社5日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见了以加纳贸易和工业部部长库埃多先生为首的加纳贸易友好代表团。
    接见时在座的有对外贸易部副部长卢绪章，外交部西亚非洲司副司长何功楷，对外贸易部第四局副局长刘希文等人。
