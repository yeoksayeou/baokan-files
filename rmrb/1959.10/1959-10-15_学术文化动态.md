Academic and Cultural Trends

    New Measures to Ensure Increased Rice Production
    Japanese Scientists Invent Water Temperature Raising Agent

    Their research began this way. Most of Japan's rice-producing areas are in the north, where temperatures are low. Rice is a crop that requires high temperatures, so preventing cold damage is one of the main ways to ensure a good harvest. After long-term research, the water temperature raising agent "OED" was synthesized not long ago. It is said to have a great effect on increasing rice production.

    "OED" is the abbreviation of Hydroxyethyl docosanol, with the chemical formula C22H45O(CH2CH2O)nH. This substance is a white powder that is insoluble in water. Its molecule is long, about 0.5 nanometers wide (1 nanometer = one billionth of a meter) and about 3 nanometers long. One end of this long molecule is hydrophilic and the other end is lipophilic, so when sprinkled on the water surface, they will stand upright one by one to form a monolayer, as if a thin film has been laid on the water surface. This film prevents the evaporation of water, thus allowing the sunlight absorbed by the water to accumulate inside, raising the water temperature. To help OED form a monolayer, another agent, carboxymethyl cellulose, is also added. According to tests, sprinkling 5 grams of OED on every 1,000 square meters of water surface can reduce water evaporation to 20-30% and increase water temperature significantly. According to the test results of the model paddy field (5 square meters), the average water temperature can be increased by 4.5°C during a day and night after applying OED, the average water temperature can be increased by 1.5°C within 19 days, and the water temperature can be increased by 8.2°C at noon.

    According to the records of the Aomori Prefectural Agricultural Experiment Station in Japan, if the average day and night water temperature is below 20°C during the rice growing season, there will be no harvest at all. However, if the water temperature is 1-2°C higher than 20°C, the yield will increase significantly.

    In rice cultivation, the role of OED can be summarized as follows:

    1. In seedbeds, it can not only promote the growth and development of seedlings, but also inhibit the harm of green algae.

    2. When raising seedlings early and transplanting early, the water temperature in the field is often not high enough, and the seedlings are difficult to root. Applying OED can promote seedling rooting.

    3. In areas with low temperatures or years with cold damage, applying OED is not only beneficial to the growth of seedlings in the early stage of rice growth, but also plays a protective role in the later stage, avoiding cold damage and ensuring yield.

    4. When dry-raising seedlings, applying OED can not only save irrigation water, but also increase soil temperature, thereby promoting germination and growth.

    5. In areas with wind damage (such as typhoons), even if the temperature is high, if OED is applied to raise the water temperature, it can advance the maturity period and avoid wind damage.

    The Soviet Union Attaches Importance to the Study of the History of Science and Technology

    In the Soviet Union, the study of the history of science and technology occupies an important position in the field of historical research. The Institute of Natural Science History and Technology History of the Soviet Academy of Sciences has published nearly 180 books in the past ten years.

    The institute's seven-year work plan, recently formulated, stipulates that the three-volume "History of Natural Science in the Soviet Union" will be completed by the end of 1961. This is a history of the development of natural science in the Soviet Union from ancient times to the mid-twentieth century. The first volume was published as early as 1957 and was well received by the scientific community at home and abroad. The three-volume "A Brief History of Technology in the Soviet Union" will be completed by the end of 1964, and collective writing has begun this year. The three-volume "A General History of Natural Science" and "A General History of Technology" will be started in 1962. The book "Great Scientific Inventions of the Twentieth Century" will be published at the end of 1961. The book "History of Great Creative Inventions in the Soviet Union" will be published in 1963. The second and third volumes of "History of the Soviet Academy of Sciences" will be published successively by the end of 1961 (the first volume was published in 1958).

    In recent years, the staff of this institute will also complete special papers such as "A Brief History of Modern Mathematics", "A Brief History of Modern Mechanics", "The Road of Development of Soviet Physics", "History of Physical Chemistry", "History of the Electronic Theory of Organic Chemistry", "History of the Russian Electrochemical Industry", "History of Russian Darwinism", "History of Pre-Evolutionary Thought in the Mid-Twentieth Century", "History of Botany Before the Mid-Twentieth Century", "History of Animal Morphology", and "History of Soviet Physiology".

    In the "Classical Scientists" collection, from 1959 to 1965, the institute will continue to edit and publish selected works or special collections of Lomonosov, Mendeleev, Chernov, Zhukovsky, Tsiolkovsky, Pavlov, Vernadsky, Aristotle, Strabo, Biruni, Copernicus, Faraday, Darwin, Maxwell, Curie, Rutherford, Planck, Smoluchowski, and Einstein. Starting in 1960, a series of "Biographies of Scientists" will be published successively every year.

专栏：学术文化动态

　　学术文化动态
    保证水稻增产的新措施
    日科学家发明水温上升剂
    他们的研究是这样开始的。日本主要产稻区多在北部，气温较低。水稻是需要高温的作物，因此在那里防止冷害就成为保证丰产的主要途径之一。经过长期的研究，不久以前合成了水温上升剂“OED”。据说对于提高水稻的产量有很大作用。
    “OED”是羟乙基二十二碳烷醇（Oxyethylene　docosanol）的简称，化学式为C22H45O（CH2CH2O）nH。这种物质是一种白色粉末，不溶于水。它的分子是长形的，宽约0.5毫微米（1毫微米＝十亿分之一米），长约3毫微米。这种长形分子的一端是亲水的，另一端是亲油的，所以撒在水面上，它们就会一个个地直立起来成一个单分子层，就好像在水面上铺了一层薄膜一样。这层薄膜防止水分的蒸发，因而使水分所吸收的阳光蓄积在水内，使水温升高。为了帮助OED形成单分子层，还要加另一种药剂胩氧甲基纤维素。据试验，在每一千平方米的水面上撒5克OED，就能使水分的蒸发减少到20—30％，提高水温很多。根据模型水田（5平方米）的试验结果，施用OED后一昼夜间平均可使水温升高4.5℃，十九天内平均使水温升高1.5℃，在中午时可使水温升高8.2℃。
    根据日本青森县农业试验场的记录，在水稻生长期内，如果平均昼夜水温在20℃以下，就一点也没有收成，可是如果水温比20℃高1—2°，产量就有显著的增加。
    在水稻栽培中，OED的作用可归纳为以下几点：
    1，在秧田中不仅能促进秧苗的生长和发育，还能抑制绿藻的为害。
    2，在提早育秧、提早插秧时，田中水温常不够高，秧苗很难发根，而施用OED就能促进秧苗发根。
    3，在气温较低的地区或有寒害的年份，施用OED不仅在水稻生育初期对秧苗的生长有利，就是在后期也起保护作用，免除冷害，保证产量。
    4，在旱育苗时，施用OED不但能节省灌水，而且还能提高地温，从而促进发芽及生长。
    5，在有风害（如台风）的地区，即便气温较高，如果施用OED使水温上升，可提早成熟期，躲避风害。
　　苏联重视科学技术史的研究
    在苏联，科学技术发展史的研究工作在历史科学的研究领域中占着重要的地位。苏联科学院自然科学史及技术史研究所最近十年来，已出版了将近一百八十种书籍。
    这个研究所在不久前制定的今后七年工作规划中规定：在1961年末将完成三卷本的“苏联自然科学史”，这是一部苏联自远古时代至二十世纪中叶的自然科学的发展史。其第一卷早在1957年已出版，获得国内外科学界的好评。在1964年末将完成三卷本的“苏联技术简史”，今年已着手集体编写。1962年将开始编写三卷本的“自然科学通史”和“技术通史”。1961年末将出版“二十世纪伟大的科学发明”一书。1963年将出版“苏联伟大的创造发明史”一书。至1961年末将陆续出版“苏联科学院院史”的第二卷和第三卷（第一卷已在1958年出版）。
    在最近几年内，这个研究所的工作人员还将完成“近代数学简史”、“近代力学简史”、“苏联物理学的发展道路”、“物理化学史”、“有机化学电子理论史”、“俄国电化学工业史”、“俄国达尔文主义史”、“二十世纪中叶前进化论思想史”、“二十世纪中叶前的植物学史”、“动物形态学史”、“苏联生理学史”等专题论文。
    在“古典科学家”集丛方面，从1959年至1965年这个研究所将继续编辑出版罗蒙诺索夫、门德列耶夫、切尔诺夫、茹科夫斯基、齐奥尔科夫斯基、巴甫洛夫、魏尔纳德斯基、亚里斯多德、斯特累波、比鲁尼、哥白尼、法拉第、达尔文、麦克斯韦、居里、卢瑟福、普朗克、斯莫路霍夫斯基和爱因斯坦等著作的选集或专集。从1960年起，每年还将陆续出版“科学家传记”丛书。
