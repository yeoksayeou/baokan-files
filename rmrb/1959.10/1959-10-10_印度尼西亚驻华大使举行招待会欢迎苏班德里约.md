Title: Indonesian Ambassador to China Holds Reception to Welcome Subandrio
Author:
Date: 1959-10-10
Page: 2
    Xinhua News Agency, 9th - Indonesian Ambassador to China, Wijopranoto, held a reception tonight to welcome Indonesian Foreign Minister Dr. Subandrio, who is on a friendly visit to our country.
    Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi attended the reception.
    Also invited to attend the reception were: Liao Chengzhi, Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission; Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; heads of various democratic parties; Burhan, President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association; deputies to the National People's Congress; You Yangzu, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese; Situ Zan, Chairman of the Jakarta Chinese Association, and others.
    Heads of diplomatic missions in China were also invited to attend tonight's reception.

### 印度尼西亚驻华大使举行招待会欢迎苏班德里约

1959-10-10
第2版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚驻华大使举行招待会欢迎苏班德里约
    新华社9日讯　印度尼西亚驻华大使维约普拉诺托今晚举行招待会，欢迎来我国进行友好访问的印度尼西亚外交部长苏班德里约博士。
    周恩来总理，陈毅副总理出席了招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的还有：华侨事务委员会主任廖承志，对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若，外交部副部长章汉夫，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，各民主党派负责人，中国印度尼西亚友好协会会长包尔汉，全国人民代表大会代表，中华全国归国华侨联合会副主席尤扬祖，雅加达中华侨团总会主席司徒赞等。
    各国驻华使节也应邀出席了今晚的招待会。
