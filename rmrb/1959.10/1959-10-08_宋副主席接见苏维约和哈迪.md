Title: Vice Chairman Song Qingling Receives Suwirjo and Hardi
Author:
Date: 1959-10-08
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 7th October: Vice Chairman Song Qingling received Suwirjo, the Chairman of the Indonesian Nationalist Party, and his wife, and Hardi, the Central Standing Committee member of the Indonesian Nationalist Party and former First Deputy Prime Minister, and his wife this afternoon, and held cordial talks.

Present at the reception were Qiao Guanhua, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Wu Maosun, Secretary-General, and He Ying, Deputy Director of the First Asian Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. (Attached picture)

The scene of Vice Chairman Song Qingling receiving Suwirjo and his wife, and Hardi and his wife. From right to left: Suwirjo, Suwirjo's wife, Song Qingling, Hardi's wife, Hardi. Xinhua News Agency reporter Niu Weiyu

### 宋副主席接见苏维约和哈迪

1959-10-08
第2版()
专栏：

    宋副主席接见苏维约和哈迪
    新华社7日讯　宋庆龄副主席今天下午接见了印度尼西亚国民党总主席苏维约和夫人、印度尼西亚国民党中央常务委员、前第一副总理哈迪和夫人，并进行了亲切的谈话。
    接见时，在座的有中国人民外交学会副会长乔冠华、秘书长吴茂荪和外交部第一亚洲司副司长何英等。（附图片）
