Title: 60,000 kVA 220,000 Volt High-Voltage Giant Transformer
Author:
Date: 1959-10-29
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang Dispatch: China's first 60,000 kVA, 220,000 volt high-voltage giant transformer has recently been successfully trial-produced at the Shenyang Transformer Factory. It is designed for power transmission from large power stations to areas over 300 kilometers away. Structurally, this giant high-voltage transformer uses a method of interleaving two layers of high-voltage coils with low-voltage coils. This reduces the weight of the transformer "body" (excluding the oil tank and oil) by more than 20% compared to using a typical structure, saving more than ten tons of silicon steel sheets. The oil tank uses the latest "bell jar" design, eliminating the need to lift the "body" out of the oil tank during maintenance; simply lift the bell jar on the oil tank. This eliminates the need to install a large crane, reducing construction investment.
    By fully utilizing the potential of existing equipment, the Shenyang Transformer Factory completed the trial production task more than three months ahead of schedule.

### 容量六万千伏安电压二十二万伏  高压巨型变压器

1959-10-29
第3版()
专栏：

    容量六万千伏安电压二十二万伏
    高压巨型变压器
    据新华社沈阳电　我国第一台容量为六万千伏安电压为二十二万伏的高压巨型变压器，最近在沈阳变压器厂试制成功。它是大型电站向三百公里以外地区输电用的。这台巨型高压变压器在结构上采用了两层高压线圈与低压线圈交错放置的方法，因此使变压器“器身”的重量（不包括油箱和油）比采用一般的结构减轻了20％以上，节省了十几吨矽钢片。在油箱方面采用了最新式的“钟罩式”，检修时不必把“器身”从油箱里吊出来，只要吊开油箱上的钟罩即可。因此不需要安装大型吊车，可以减少建设投资。
    沈阳变压器厂由于充分地利用了现有设备的潜力，比原计划提前三个多月完成了试制任务。
