Title: Image
Author: Niu Weiyu
Date: 1959-10-03
Page: 3

    The above four photos (from left to right) show: Premier Zhou Enlai on September 29th respectively meeting the delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria headed by Ben Youssef Ben Khedda, the Burmese Federal Government representative U Chit Thoung, the Government delegation of the Republic of Iraq headed by Brigadier General Ahmed Mohammed Yehia, and the Government delegation of the Kingdom of Nepal headed by Tulsi Giri. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Niu Weiyu photographed)
