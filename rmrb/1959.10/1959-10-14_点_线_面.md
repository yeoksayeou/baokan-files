Title: Point, Line, Surface
Author: Zhu Xiangyuan
Date: 1959-10-14
Page: 8

　　Point, Line, Surface
　　Zhu Xiangyuan

　　A point, moving forward, becomes only a line;
　　A line, moving forward, becomes a surface.
　　A single plow passes, turning up a ridge of soil;
　　A row of plows passes, turning up a large field.
　　Single-crop farming is a ridge of soil, a line;
　　Agriculture, forestry, animal husbandry, sideline occupations, and fishing, multiple operations moving forward together,
　　Become a surface, a large field, the commune's income is countless thousands.

### 点·线·面
朱相远
1959-10-14
第8版()
专栏：

　　点·线·面
　　朱相远
　　一个点，向前移动只成一条线；
　　一根线，向前移动就成一个面。
　　一部犁走过，翻起一垅土；
　　一排犁走过，翻起一大片。
　　单种经营是一垅土，一条线；
　　农、林、牧、副、渔，多种经营齐向前，
　　成了面，一大片，公社收入万万千。
