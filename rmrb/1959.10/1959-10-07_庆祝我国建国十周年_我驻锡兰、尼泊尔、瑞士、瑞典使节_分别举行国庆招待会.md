Title: Celebrating the 10th Anniversary of the Founding of Our Country: Our Envoys to Ceylon, Nepal, Switzerland, and Sweden Hold National Day Receptions

Author:
Date: 1959-10-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, October 5th: Chinese Ambassador to Ceylon, Zhang Canming, held a grand reception here tonight to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Approximately 600 people attended the reception, including senior Ceylonese government officials, members of parliament, political leaders, representatives from various social organizations, prominent figures from all walks of life, and foreign diplomatic envoys to Ceylon.

Minister of Health Jayasuriya attended the reception on behalf of the Ceylonese government. He raised a glass to wish the People's Republic of China greater prosperity and strength, and to the continuous strengthening of friendship between Ceylon and China.

Ambassador Zhang Canming congratulated the people of Ceylon on their prosperity and happiness, and wished the friendship between the two countries would last forever.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, October 5th: Kathmandu dispatch: Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, Pan Zili, held a grand reception tonight at the "Sital Niwas Palace" here to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

King Mahendra of Nepal and the Queen, the Second Prince, the Third Prince, and other members of the royal family attended today's reception.

Also attending today's reception were Prime Minister Koirala, all cabinet ministers and deputy ministers, the two speakers of the Upper and Lower Houses, the Commander-in-Chief, marshals, as well as senior Nepalese government officials and senior military officers.

The Soviet, Indian, and British ambassadors to Nepal also attended the reception.

The two-and-a-half-hour reception was filled with a friendly atmosphere. The hosts and guests had cordial conversations on further strengthening the friendly relations between the two countries and raised glasses to wish for the prosperity and strength of both nations.

Xinhua News Agency, October 5th: Bern dispatch: Chinese Ambassador to Switzerland, Li Qingquan, held a reception on the 1st to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. More than 300 people attended the reception, including Swiss Political Minister Petitpierre, the Speaker of the National Council, the Governor of Bern, the General Secretary of the Labour Party, Voka, other Swiss government officials, people from all political parties and all walks of life, and foreign diplomatic envoys to Switzerland.

Xinhua News Agency, October 5th: Stockholm dispatch: Chinese Ambassador to Sweden, Dong Yueqian, held a reception on the 1st to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Approximately 350 people attended the reception, including the Swedish Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs, Kling, and other government officials, people from Swedish political, business, and cultural circles, foreign diplomatic envoys to Sweden, and overseas Chinese in Sweden.

### 庆祝我国建国十周年  我驻锡兰、尼泊尔、瑞士、瑞典使节  分别举行国庆招待会

1959-10-07
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝我国建国十周年
    我驻锡兰、尼泊尔、瑞士、瑞典使节
    分别举行国庆招待会
    据新华社科伦坡5日电　中国驻锡兰大使张灿明今天晚上在这里举行盛大招待会庆祝中华人民共和国建国十周年。
    锡兰高级政府官员、议会议员、政界领袖、各社会团体代表、各界著名人士以及各国驻锡兰外交使节等约六百人出席了招待会。
    卫生部长贾亚苏里亚代表锡兰政府参加招待会。他举杯祝贺中华人民共和国更加繁荣昌盛，锡中两国友谊日益加强。
    张灿明大使祝贺锡兰人民昌盛幸福，两国友谊万年常青。
    据新华社新德里5日电　加德满都消息：中国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力今晚在这里的“西图阿安尼瓦斯宫”举行盛大招待会庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年。
    尼泊尔国王马亨德拉和王后、二太子、三太子和其他王族成员出席了今天的招待会。
    出席今天招待会的还有柯伊拉腊首相、内阁各位大臣和副大臣、上院和下院的两位议长、总司令、元帅们以及尼泊尔政府高级官员和高级军官。
    苏联、印度和英国的驻尼泊尔大使也出席了招待会。
    历时两小时半的招待会充满了友好的气氛。宾主对于进一步加强两国之间的友好关系作了亲切的交谈，并且举杯祝两国的繁荣昌盛。
    新华社5日讯　伯尔尼消息：中国驻瑞士大使李清泉1日举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国建国十周年。出席招待会的有瑞士政治部长彼蒂彼爱，国民议会议长，伯尔尼州长，劳动党总书记沃克，瑞士政府其他官员，各政党和各界人士和各国驻瑞士外交使节等三百多人。
    新华社5日讯　斯德哥尔摩消息：中国驻瑞典大使董越千1日举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国建国十周年。
    出席招待会的有瑞典代理外交大臣克林和其他政府官员、瑞典政界、工商界、文化界人士，各国驻瑞典外交使节和在瑞典的华侨等约三百五十人。
