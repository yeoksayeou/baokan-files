Title: Polish Party and Government Delegation Returns to Beijing After Concluding Visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
Author: 
Date: 1959-10-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 12th - The Polish Party and Government delegation, headed by Aleksander Zawadzki, Chairman of the State Council of the Polish People's Republic and member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon after concluding its visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The delegation flew from Hanoi via Wuhan, accompanied by Wang Shoudao, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Minister of Communications.

Welcoming them at the airport were Bo Yibo, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Premier of the State Council; Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Yu Zhan, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs.

Fularato, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China, and embassy staff also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

When the Polish Party and Government delegation passed through Wuhan, Wang Renzhong, First Secretary of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China; Zhao Xinchu, Secretary of the Secretariat and Vice Governor of Hubei Province; Li Minghao, Chen Jingshao, and Nie Guoqing, Vice Governors of Hubei Province; Song Kanfu, First Secretary of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China; Liu Huinong, Secretary of the Secretariat and Mayor of Wuhan; Zhang Guangcai, Major General of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Wuhan; and responsible persons of local organizations of various democratic parties and people's organizations, went to the airport today to welcome and see off the distinguished guests.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 12th - The delegation of the Polish People's Republic, led by Aleksander Zawadzki, Chairman of the State Council of the Polish People's Republic, concluded its visit to Vietnam and left Hanoi by special plane this morning.

President Ho Chi Minh, Acting Premier Truong Chinh, Le Duan, member of the Political Bureau of the Labor Party Central Committee, Chairman Ton Duc Thang, Vice Premiers Vo Nguyen Giap, Phan Ke Toai, Pham Hung, ministers of various government departments, diplomatic envoys of various countries to Vietnam, and representatives of people from all walks of life went to the airport to see them off.

President Ho Chi Minh delivered a farewell speech and warmly said to Chairman Zawadzki: "I give you a parting word: 'Friendship lasts a thousand years.'"

Chairman Zawadzki expressed his heartfelt thanks to President Ho Chi Minh, the Central Committee of the Labor Party, the government and people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in his speech. (Attached photo)

### 结束在越南民主共和国的访问  波兰党政代表团回到北京

1959-10-13
第5版()
专栏：

　　结束在越南民主共和国的访问
    波兰党政代表团回到北京
    据新华社12日讯　以波兰人民共和国国务委员会主席、波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员亚历山大·萨瓦茨基为首的波兰人民共和国党政代表团全体贵宾结束了在越南民主共和国的访问后，今天下午乘飞机由河内路经武汉，由中共中央委员、交通部部长王首道陪同来北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有中共中央政治局候补委员、国务院副总理薄一波，外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文、苏联东欧司副司长余湛。
    波兰驻中国大使馆临时代办傅拉托和大使馆人员也前往机场欢迎。
    波兰人民共和国党政代表团路过武汉时，中共湖北省委第一书记王任重，书记处书记、湖北省副省长赵辛初，湖北省副省长李明灏、陈经畬、聂国青，中共武汉市委第一书记宋侃夫，书记处书记、武汉市市长刘惠农，人民解放军驻武汉部队首长张广才少将及各民主党派地方组织、各人民团体的负责人，今天到机场欢迎和欢送贵宾。
    据新华社河内12日电　由波兰人民共和国国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基率领的波兰人民共和国代表团结束了在越南的访问，今天上午乘专机离开河内。
    胡志明主席、代理总理长征，劳动党中央政治局委员黎笋，孙德胜委员长，副总理武元甲、潘继遂、范雄和政府各部部长以及各国驻越南外交使节和各界人民代表等到飞机场送行。
    胡志明主席致欢送词，并热情地对萨瓦茨基主席说：“惜别时赠你一句：‘友谊之情垂千秋’”。
    萨瓦茨基主席在讲话中对胡志明主席、劳动党中央委员会、越南民主共和国政府和人民表示衷心的谢意。（附图片）
