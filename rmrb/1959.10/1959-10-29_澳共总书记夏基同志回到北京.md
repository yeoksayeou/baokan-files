Title: Australian Communist Party General Secretary Sharkey Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 28th. Comrade Lance Louis Sharkey, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Australian Communist Party, accompanied by Comrade Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, returned to Beijing by special plane on the 27th after visiting Jinan.

Comrade Sharkey arrived in Jinan on the 25th. During his stay in Jinan, he was accompanied by Bai Rubing, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Shandong Provincial Committee of the CPC, and visited the Provincial Party Committee and the Provincial People's Committee Iron and Steel Plant, the Yangzhuang Siphon Irrigation Project drawing water from the Yellow River in the Jinan suburbs, and toured famous scenic spots such as Daming Lake, Pearl Spring, and Baotu Spring.

The leading comrades of the province and city who welcomed and saw Comrade Sharkey off at the airport upon his arrival and departure from Jinan included: Shu Tong, First Secretary of the Shandong Provincial Committee of the CPC, Bai Rubing, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Provincial Party Committee and Deputy Governor of Shandong Province, Liu Jiping, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Provincial Party Committee, Liu Binglin, member of the Standing Committee of the Provincial Party Committee, Yang Xuanwu, First Secretary of the Jinan Municipal Committee of the CPC, Liu Naidian, Mayor of Jinan, and other responsible comrades of the province and city.

### 澳共总书记夏基同志回到北京

1959-10-29
第5版()
专栏：

    澳共总书记夏基同志回到北京
    新华社28日讯　澳大利亚共产党中央委员会总书记兰斯·路易斯·夏基同志，由中共中央书记处书记王稼祥同志陪同，到济南参观访问后，已于27日乘专机回到北京。
    夏基同志是在25日到达济南的，在济南期间，由中共山东省委书记处书记白如冰陪同，参观了省委和省人委炼铁厂，济南郊区杨庄虹吸管引黄灌溉工程，游览了大明湖、珍珠泉、趵突泉等名胜。
    夏基同志到达和离开济南时到机场迎送的有：中共山东省委第一书记舒同，省委书记处书记、山东省副省长白如冰，省委书记处书记刘季平，省委常委刘秉琳，中共济南市委第一书记杨宣武，济南市市长刘乃殿等省、市负责人。
