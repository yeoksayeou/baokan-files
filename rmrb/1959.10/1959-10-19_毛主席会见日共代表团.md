Title: Chairman Mao Meets with Japanese Communist Party Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-10-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 18th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China met with the Japanese Communist Party delegation headed by Nozaka Sanzō, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, this evening.

    During the meeting, Comrade Mao Zedong had a warm and cordial conversation with Comrade Nozaka Sanzō and the comrades of the Japanese Communist Party delegation.

    Present at the meeting on the Japanese Communist Party delegation side were: Nozaka Sanzō, member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, Kūta Satomi, Kurahara Koreto, members of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, Suna Kazuichisa, and Iwama Masao.

    Present on the Chinese Communist Party side were: Liu Shaoqi and Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairmen of the CPC Central Committee; Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee; Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee; and Liao Chengzhi, member of the CPC Central Committee. ### 毛主席会见日共代表团

1959-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席会见日共代表团
    新华社18日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东，在今天晚间会见了以日本共产党中央委员会主席野坂参三为首的日本共产党代表团。
    会见中，毛泽东同志同野坂参三同志和日本共产党代表团的同志们进行了热烈的亲切的谈话。
    会见时日本共产党代表团方面在座的，有日共中央干部会委员裤田里见、藏原惟人，日共中央委员砂间一良、岩间正男。
    中国共产党方面在座的，有中共中央副主席刘少奇、周恩来，中共中央政治局委员彭真，中共中央书记处书记王稼祥，中共中央委员廖承志。（附图片）
    毛主席会见以野坂参三为首的日本共产党代表团。图为会见时情形。右起第二人是野坂参三同志。　新华社记者　侯波摄
