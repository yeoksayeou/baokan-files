Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Meets Chairman Dobi

Author:

Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China and Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, met this afternoon with István Dobi, Chairman of the Presidential Council of the Hungarian People's Republic and head of the Hungarian People's Republic Party and Government Delegation, along with delegation members Béla Biszku, member of the Political Bureau of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party Central Committee and Minister of Internal Affairs of the Hungarian People's Republic, and István Antos, alternate member of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party Central Committee and Minister of Finance of the Hungarian People's Republic.

The leaders of the two parties and countries held cordial and friendly talks during the meeting. (Picture attached)

### 刘少奇主席会见道比主席

1959-10-01
第6版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席会见道比主席
    新华社30日讯　中华人民共和国主席、中共中央副主席刘少奇在今天下午会见了匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席、匈牙利人民共和国党政代表团团长道比·伊斯特万，代表团团员：匈牙利社会主义工人党中央政治局委员、匈牙利人民共和国内务部部长比斯库·贝洛和匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会候补委员、匈牙利人民共和国财政部部长安托什·伊斯特万。
    两国党和国家领导人在会见中进行了亲切友好的交谈。（附图片）
    中华人民共和国主席、中共中央副主席刘少奇会见匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席、党政代表团团长道比·伊斯特万（左第二人）时情形。　新华社记者　吕厚民摄
