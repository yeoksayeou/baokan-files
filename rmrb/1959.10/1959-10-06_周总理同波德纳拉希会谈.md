Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Holds Talks with Bodnaras
Author:
Date: 1959-10-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. Comrade Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee and Premier of the State Council, held talks and had dinner this evening with Comrade Emil Bodnaras, head of the Romanian Party and Government Delegation, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, and Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic.

Present on the Romanian side were all members of the Romanian Party and Government Delegation: Dumitru Coliu, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of Romanian Workers' Party and Chairman of the State Control Commission of the Romanian People's Republic; Paul Niculescu-Mizil, member of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party and Minister of Propaganda and Agitation of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party; Ludovic Szabo, member of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party and First Secretary of the Hungarian Autonomous Region Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party; and Barbu Zaharescu, alternate member of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party and Ambassador of the Romanian People's Republic to China.

Present on the Chinese side were Lu Dingyi, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, and Liu Ruilong, Vice Minister of Agriculture.

### 周总理同波德纳拉希会谈

1959-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理同波德纳拉希会谈
    新华社5日讯　中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来同志今天晚间同罗马尼亚党政代表团团长、罗马尼亚工人党中央政治局委员、罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议副主席埃米尔·波德纳拉希同志进行了会谈，并进晚餐。
    在座的，罗马尼亚方面有罗马尼亚党政代表团全体团员：罗马尼亚工人党中央政治局候补委员、罗马尼亚人民共和国国家监察委员会主席杜米特鲁·柯柳，罗马尼亚工人党中央委员、罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会宣传鼓动部长保罗·尼库列斯库—米齐耳，罗马尼亚工人党中央委员、罗马尼亚工人党匈牙利自治州委员会第一书记卢多维克·苏波，罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会候补委员、罗马尼亚人民共和国驻华大使巴尔布·查哈勒斯库。
    在座的，我国方面有中共中央政治局候补委员、国务院副总理陆定一，农业部副部长刘瑞龙。
