Title: Norwegian Communist Party Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 30th -- At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Norwegian Communist Party delegation, led by Jørgen Vogt, Secretary of the Central Secretariat of the Norwegian Communist Party, and including delegation members Kirsten Bærnseth, Secretary of the Central Secretariat of the Norwegian Communist Party and Women's Secretary, and Reidar Bjåstad, Chairman of the Vest-Agder Region of the Norwegian Communist Party, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon to visit our country and participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Welcoming them at the airport were Li Xiannian, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, An Ziwen, member of the Central Committee, and Wu Xiuquan. (Attached picture)

Li Xiannian (right), member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, shakes hands with Jørgen Vogt, head of the Norwegian Communist Party delegation and Secretary of the Central Secretariat of the Norwegian Communist Party, at the airport. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Yang Zhenhe)

### 挪威共产党代表团到京

1959-10-01
第6版()
专栏：

    挪威共产党代表团到京
    新华社30日讯　应中国共产党中央委员会邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的挪威共产党代表团团长、挪威共产党中央书记处书记约尔根·福克特，代表团团员：挪威共产党中央书记处书记兼妇女书记基尔斯登·贝恩曾，挪威共产党西阿格特地区主席雷达尔·比亚斯塔，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    在机场欢迎的，有中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记李先念，中央委员安子文和伍修权。（附图片）
