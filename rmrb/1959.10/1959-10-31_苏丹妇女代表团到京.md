Title: Sudanese Women's Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 30th. At the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China, the Sudanese Women's Delegation, led by Mrs. Salma Muhammad Suleiman, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. Shen Ziji, member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation, and others welcomed them at the airport.

### 苏丹妇女代表团到京

1959-10-31
第5版()
专栏：

    苏丹妇女代表团到京
    新华社30日讯　应中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会邀请来我国访问的苏丹妇女代表团一行三人，在团长萨勒玛·穆罕默德·苏莱曼夫人率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。全国妇联主席团委员沈兹九等人到机场欢迎。
