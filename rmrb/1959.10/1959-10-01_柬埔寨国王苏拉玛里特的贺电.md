Title: Telegram of Greetings from King Suramarit of Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 8

To His Excellency Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:

	On the occasion of the National Day of the People's Republic of China, I am delighted to extend, on behalf of the Khmer nation and myself, my warmest congratulations to Your Excellency, wishing you complete success in your noble mission, personal happiness, and the prosperity of the Chinese people.

	Norodom Suramarit
	King of Cambodia
	Phnom Penh, September 30, 1959

### 柬埔寨国王苏拉玛里特的贺电

1959-10-01
第8版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨国王苏拉玛里特的贺电
    中华人民共和国主席刘少奇先生阁下：
    在中华人民共和国国庆的时候，我高兴地以高棉民族和我个人的名义向阁下致以最热烈的祝贺，祝你的崇高使命取得圆满成功，祝你个人幸福并祝中国人民昌盛。
                        柬埔寨国王诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特
                                      1959年9月30日金边
