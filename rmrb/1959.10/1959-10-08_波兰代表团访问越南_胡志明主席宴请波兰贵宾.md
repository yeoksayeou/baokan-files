Title: Polish Delegation Visits Vietnam, Chairman Ho Chi Minh Hosts Banquet for Polish Guests
Author:
Date: 1959-10-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi: A delegation from the Polish People's Republic, led by Zawadzki, Chairman of the Council of State of the Polish People's Republic and member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, arrived in Hanoi on the morning of the 6th by special plane for a five-day visit to Vietnam.

At 10:00 a.m., the plane carrying the delegation landed at Gia Lam Airport, which was bathed in sunshine and decorated with flags. When Chairman Zawadzki stepped off the plane, he was warmly embraced by Chairman Ho Chi Minh, who had come to greet him. At this moment, a salute was fired.

Among those who came to the airport to welcome the distinguished guests were Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Vietnamese National Assembly, Le Duan, member of the Political Bureau of the Vietnam Workers' Party, Deputy Prime Ministers Truong Chinh, Vo Nguyen Giap, Phan Ke Toai, Pham Hung, ministers of various ministries, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Vietnam.

Chairman Zawadzki, accompanied by Chairman Ho Chi Minh, reviewed the guard of honor. Afterwards, Chairman Ho Chi Minh delivered a speech, extending a warm welcome to Chairman Zawadzki and the delegation he led.

Zawadzki and Ho Chi Minh rode in an open-top car to the Presidential Palace, where they were greeted by more than 150,000 people lining the streets.

Zawadzki and the delegation of the Polish People's Republic he led paid a courtesy call on Chairman Ho Chi Minh on the morning of the 6th.

Chairman Ho Chi Minh returned the visit to Chairman Zawadzki and the delegation of the Polish People's Republic at noon on the 6th. Subsequently, delegations from the Vietnam Workers' Party, the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the National Assembly, and the Fatherland Front paid a courtesy call on Chairman Zawadzki and the delegation of the Polish People's Republic.

On the evening of the 6th, Chairman Ho Chi Minh hosted a banquet in honor of the delegation of the Polish People's Republic led by Chairman Zawadzki.

At 10:00 a.m. on the 7th, the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, headed by Chairman Ho Chi Minh, and the delegation of the Polish People's Republic, headed by Chairman Zawadzki, held talks at the Presidential Palace.

The two delegations exchanged views on the relations between Vietnam and Poland and major international issues of current concern. The talks were conducted in an atmosphere of fraternal intimacy.

### 波兰代表团访问越南  胡志明主席宴请波兰贵宾

1959-10-08
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰代表团访问越南
    胡志明主席宴请波兰贵宾
    据新华社河内电　由波兰人民共和国国务委员会主席、波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员萨瓦茨基率领的波兰人民共和国代表团，6日上午乘专机到达河内，在越南进行为期五天的访问。
    十时，代表团乘坐的飞机降落在阳光灿烂、旗帜招展的嘉林机场。萨瓦茨基主席走下飞机时，前来迎接的胡志明主席同他热烈拥抱。这时，礼炮齐鸣。
    到机场欢迎贵宾的还有越南国会常务委员会委员长孙德胜，越南劳动党政治局委员黎笋，政府副总理长征、武元甲、潘继遂、范雄和各部部长以及各国驻越南外交使节。
    萨瓦茨基主席由胡志明主席陪同检阅了仪仗队后，胡志明主席致词，对萨瓦茨基主席和他率领的代表团表示热烈的欢迎。
    萨瓦茨基和胡志明主席同乘敞篷汽车前往主席府，沿途受到十五万多群众的夹道欢迎。
    萨瓦茨基和他率领的波兰人民共和国代表团6日上午拜会了胡志明主席。
    胡志明主席在6日中午回拜了萨瓦茨基主席和波兰人民共和国代表团。接着，越南劳动党、越南民主共和国政府、国会和祖国战线的代表团拜会了萨瓦茨基主席和波兰人民共和国代表团。
    6日晚上，胡志明主席设宴招待由萨瓦茨基主席率领的波兰人民共和国代表团。
    7日上午十点钟，以胡志明主席为首的越南民主共和国代表团和以萨瓦茨基主席为首的波兰人民共和国代表团在主席府举行了会谈。
    双方代表团就有关越波两国的关系和目前重大国际问题交换了意见。会谈在兄弟般的亲密气氛中进行。
