Title: British Canterbury Dean Dr. Johnson Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-10-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 20th. Dr. Hewlett Johnson, the renowned British peace advocate and Dean of Canterbury, and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included: Burhan, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Qu Tangliang, Secretary-General; Wang Mingyuan and Zheng Senyu, Deputy Secretaries-General; Wu Maosun, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Shen Yuanhui, Executive Committee Member of the All-China Women's Federation; and Jiang Yizhen and Zhao Fusan, Vice-Chairmen of the Beijing Three-Self Patriotic Movement Committee of Christianity.

### 英国坎特伯雷教长约翰逊博士到京

1959-10-21
第5版()
专栏：

    英国坎特伯雷教长约翰逊博士到京
    新华社20日讯　英国著名和平人士、英国坎特伯雷教长约翰逊博士和他的夫人，应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的邀请，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席包尔汉、秘书长区棠亮、副秘书长王明远、郑森禹，中国人民外交学会秘书长吴茂荪，全国妇联执行委员沈元晖，北京市基督教三自爱国运动委员会副主席蒋翼振、赵复三等。
