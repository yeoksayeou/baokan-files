Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Cambodian Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 6th: Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi received the Cambodian cultural delegation led by Cambodian Deputy Secretary of State for Education, Phleuk Trach, this evening.

Present at the reception were Ding Xilin, Vice Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Lin Liru, Vice Minister of Education, and Chen Shuliang, Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Also present at the reception was Cambodian Ambassador to China, Leng Ngeth.

### 陈毅副总理接见柬埔寨文化代表团

    陈毅副总理接见柬埔寨文化代表团
