Title: Polish and Czechoslovak Labor People's Delegations Return Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 20th – The Polish and Czechoslovak Labor People's Delegations, who were invited to visit our country and participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, concluded their visits in our country and left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.

Those who saw them off at the airport were the heads of the inviting organizations: Shen Yanbing, Liu Changsheng, Xia Yan, Shi Liang, Ding Xilin, Zheng Senyu, Hu Qili, and others.

Acting Chargé d'Affaires of the Polish Embassy in China, Fularato, and Counselor of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, Lehman, also saw them off at the airport.

### 波捷劳动人民代表团回国

1959-10-21
第5版()
专栏：

    波捷劳动人民代表团回国
    新华社20日讯　应邀来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的波兰劳动人民代表团和捷克斯洛伐克劳动人民代表团，结束了在我国的参观访问后，已经在今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场送行的有各邀请团体的负责人：沈雁冰、刘长胜、夏衍、史良、丁西林、郑森禹、胡启立等。
    波兰驻华大使馆代办傅拉托和捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆参赞雷曼等也到机场送行。
