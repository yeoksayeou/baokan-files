Title: General Secretary of the Communist Party of Venezuela Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-05
Page: 2
Column:
    Xinhua News Agency, 4th - Comrade Jesús Faría, head of the delegation of the Communist Party of Venezuela and General Secretary of the Communist Party of Venezuela, left Beijing by plane yesterday morning to return to his country after attending our National Day's 10th Anniversary Celebration.
    Chen Yi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and Wu Xiuquan, member of the Central Committee, were at the airport to see him off.
    The three members of the delegation will continue their visits in various parts of our country.

### 委内瑞拉共产党总书记回国

1959-10-05
第2版()
专栏：

    委内瑞拉共产党总书记回国
    新华社4日讯　委内瑞拉共产党代表团团长、委内瑞拉共产党总书记赫苏斯·法利亚同志在参加过我国建国十周年庆祝典礼之后，昨天上午乘飞机离开北京返国。
    到机场欢送的，有中共中央政治局委员陈毅，中央委员伍修权。
    代表团的三位团员将在我国各地继续参观访问。
