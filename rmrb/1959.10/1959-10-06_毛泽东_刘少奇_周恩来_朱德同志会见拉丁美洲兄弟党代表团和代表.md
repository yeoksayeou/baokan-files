Title: Comrades Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, and Zhu De Meet with Latin American Fraternal Party Delegations and Representatives
Author:
Date: 1959-10-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th: Comrades Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Vice-Chairmen Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, and Zhu De, met this afternoon with the delegations and representatives of fourteen Latin American fraternal parties who came to China to visit and participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

They are: Luís Carlos Prestes, Head of the Delegation of the Brazilian Communist Party and General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Brazilian Communist Party; members of the delegation: Ramiro Lucchesi and Denilli, members of the Central Committee of the Brazilian Communist Party; Victorio Codovilla, Head of the Delegation of the Argentine Communist Party and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Argentine Communist Party; members of the delegation: Rodolfo Ghioldi, member of the Executive Committee of the Central Committee of the Argentine Communist Party, Miguel Contraras, member of the Central Committee of the Argentine Communist Party; Aníbal Escalante, Head of the Delegation of the Cuban People's Socialist Party, Executive Secretary of the National Committee of the Cuban People's Socialist Party, and member of the Executive Bureau of the National Committee; members of the delegation: Antero Regalado, member of the National Farmers' Committee, Alberto Luis Rodriguez, member of the Havana Municipal Committee Executive Committee; Alberto Lovera, member of the Central Committee of the Venezuelan Communist Party and alternate member of the Political Bureau, Carlos Pardo, Secretary of the Aragua State Committee, Radamés Larrazábal, Secretary of the Suria State Committee; José González, Head of the Delegation of the Chilean Communist Party, member of the Central Political Committee, and Secretary of the Secretariat; members of the delegation: Desiderio Millanao, member of the Central Committee of the Chilean Communist Party, José Cademartori, alternate member of the Central Committee of the Chilean Communist Party; members of the delegation of the Mexican Communist Party; Víctor J. Merchán, Head of the Delegation of the Colombian Communist Party, member of the Central Executive Committee of the Colombian Communist Party, and Secretary of National Agricultural Work; members of the delegation: Gustavo Castro, member of the Central Committee of the Colombian Communist Party, Carlos J. Gómez, alternate member of the Central Committee of the Colombian Communist Party; Enrique Rodríguez, Head of the Delegation of the Uruguayan Communist Party, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Uruguayan Communist Party; Raúl Tellardi, member of the Central Committee of the Uruguayan Communist Party; Rogelio Espínola, Head of the Delegation of the Paraguayan Communist Party, member of the Central Committee of the Paraguayan Communist Party; members of the delegation: José Asunción Flores, Head of Cultural Work of the Paraguayan Communist Party, Ángel Rodríguez, member of the Central Committee of the Paraguayan Communist Party; Alejandro Idrobo, Head of the Delegation of the Ecuadorian Communist Party, member of the Central Committee of the Ecuadorian Communist Party; members of the delegation: Pascual Palomino and Hernán Acevedo, members of the Central Committee of the Ecuadorian Communist Party; Jesús Lara, Head of the Delegation of the Bolivian Communist Party, member of the Central Committee of the Bolivian Communist Party; members of the delegation: Gelson Justiniano Gittras, member of the Central Committee of the Bolivian Communist Party; representative of the Guatemalan Labor Party; members of the delegation of the Nicaraguan Socialist Party; Arnoldo Ferreto, Head of the Delegation of the Costa Rican People's Vanguard Party, member of the Central Committee of the Costa Rican People's Vanguard Party; members of the delegation: Antonino Acosta, member of the Central Committee of the Costa Rican People's Vanguard Party, and women leader Gladis Saez, etc.

Present at the meeting were Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, Chen Yi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, and Wu Xiuquan, member of the Central Committee. ### 毛泽东  刘少奇  周恩来  朱德同志会见拉丁美洲兄弟党代表团和代表

1959-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东  刘少奇  周恩来  朱德同志会见拉丁美洲兄弟党代表团和代表
    新华社5日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东、副主席刘少奇、周恩来、朱德，今天下午在一起会见了来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的拉丁美洲十四个兄弟党的代表团和代表。
    他们是：巴西共产党代表团团长、巴西共产党中央总书记路易斯·卡洛斯·普列斯特斯，代表团团员：巴西共产党中央委员拉米罗·卢切西和丹尼利；阿根廷共产党代表团团长、阿根廷共产党中央书记处书记维克托里奥·柯都维亚，代表团团员：阿根廷共产党中央执行委员会委员罗道夫·基奥迪，阿根廷共产党中央委员米格尔·孔特拉尔斯；古巴人民社会党代表团团长、古巴人民社会党全国委员会执行书记、全国委员会执行局委员阿尼巴耳·埃斯卡兰特，代表团团员：全国农民委员会委员安特罗·雷加拉多，哈瓦那市委执行委员阿耳贝托·路易斯·罗德里格斯；委内瑞拉共产党代表团团员：委内瑞拉共产党中央委员、政治局候补委员阿尔贝托·洛维拉，阿拉瓜省委书记卡洛斯·帕尔多，苏里亚省委书记拉达梅斯·拉腊萨瓦耳；智利共产党代表团团长、中央政治委员会委员、书记处书记何塞·冈萨雷斯，代表团团员：智利共产党中央委员德西德里奥·米利亚瑙，智利共产党中央候补委员何塞·卡德马托里；墨西哥共产党代表团的成员；哥伦比亚共产党代表团团长、哥伦比亚共产党中央执行委员会委员、全国农业工作书记维克托·赫·梅尔昌，代表团团员：哥伦比亚共产党中央委员古斯塔沃·卡斯特罗，哥伦比亚共产党中央候补委员卡洛斯·赫·戈麦斯；乌拉圭共产党代表团团长、乌拉圭共产党中央书记处书记恩里克·罗德里格斯，代表团团员、乌拉圭共产党中央委员劳尔·特亚耳迪；巴拉圭共产党代表团团长、巴拉圭共产党中央委员罗赫略·埃斯平多拉，代表团团员：巴拉圭共产党文化工作负责人何塞·阿桑逊·弗洛雷斯，巴拉圭共产党中央委员安赫耳·罗德里格斯；厄瓜多尔共产党代表团团长、厄瓜多尔共产党中央委员亚历杭德罗·伊德罗沃，代表团团员：厄瓜多尔共产党中央委员帕斯库亚耳·帕洛米诺和埃尔南·阿塞维多；玻利维亚共产党代表团团长、玻利维亚共产党中央委员赫苏斯·拉腊，代表团团员、玻利维亚共产党中央委员赫尔森·胡斯蒂尼亚诺·吉特拉斯；危地马拉劳动党代表；尼加拉瓜社会主义党代表团的成员；哥斯达黎加人民先锋党代表团团长、哥斯达黎加人民先锋党中央委员阿诺耳多·弗雷托，代表团团员：哥斯达黎加人民先锋党中央委员安东尼诺·阿科斯塔和妇女领袖格拉蒂斯·赛斯等。
    会见时在座的，有中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记彭真，中央政治局委员陈毅，中央书记处书记王稼祥，中央委员伍修权。（附图片）
