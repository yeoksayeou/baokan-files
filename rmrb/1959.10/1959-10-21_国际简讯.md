International Briefs

    Israeli President Visits Burma
    Rangoon dispatch: Israeli President Yitzhak Ben-Zvi arrived here by plane on the 19th for a visit. Ben-Zvi is scheduled to leave Rangoon on October 28 to return home.

    Britain Invites Adenauer to Visit London
    London dispatch: The British Foreign Office announced that the British Ambassador to Bonn has formally invited Adenauer to visit London. The visit is expected to take place in the second half of November.

    French Farmers Protest Low Purchase Prices for Agricultural Products
    Paris dispatch: On the 19th, 30,000 farmers in the French province of Finistère blocked roads in protest against the low purchase prices for agricultural products and the large price scissors between agricultural and industrial products.

    UAR and Australia Agree to Restore Diplomatic Relations
    Cairo dispatch: The Director of the UAR News Agency, Afra, announced on the 19th that the governments of the UAR and Australia have agreed to restore diplomatic relations and reopen the Australian Legation in Cairo.

### 国际简讯

1959-10-21
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    以色列总统访问缅甸
    仰光讯　以色列总统伊萨克·本—兹维19日乘飞机到达这里访问。本—兹维预定在10月28日离仰光回国。
    英国邀请阿登纳访问伦敦
    伦敦讯　英国外交部宣布，驻波恩英国大使已经正式邀请阿登纳访问伦敦。访问日期预计在11月下半月。
    法农民抗议低价收购农产品
    巴黎讯　19日法国菲尼斯台尔省三万个农民堵塞了这一省的道路，表示抗议农产品收购价格太低，抗议农业品价格同工业品价格剪刀差很大。
    阿澳同意恢复两国外交关系
    开罗讯　阿联新闻局局长阿弗拉19日宣布，阿联政府和澳大利亚政府已经同意恢复外交关系，并且重开澳大利亚驻开罗公使馆。
