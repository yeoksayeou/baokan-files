Title: Yunnan Diqing Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture: 60,000 Mu of Highland Barley Increases Production by 50%
Author:
Date: 1959-10-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming News: Diqing Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture in Yunnan Province has achieved an unprecedented bumper harvest of highland barley. The average yield per mu for the entire prefecture's 60,000 mu of highland barley reached 300 jin, an increase of approximately 50% compared to last year. The total output more than doubled compared to last year.

After the completion of democratic reforms in Diqing Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture in 1957, cooperativization was achieved in 1958. 97% of the prefecture's farming households participated in agricultural cooperatives or mutual aid teams. Tibetan farmers' enthusiasm for production soared to unprecedented heights. This spring, a total of 60,000 mu of highland barley were planted, an increase of 35% compared to last year. The land was generally ploughed more than two cun deeper than last year, and 50 to 100 dan of fertilizer were applied per mu. The crops were weeded four times and battles were waged against drought and flooding.

### 云南迪庆藏族自治州  六万亩青稞增产五成

1959-10-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　云南迪庆藏族自治州
    六万亩青稞增产五成
    新华社昆明讯　云南省迪庆藏族自治州青稞空前大丰收。全州六万亩青稞平均亩产三百斤，比去年增产50％左右；总产量比去年增加了一倍多。
    迪庆藏族自治州1957年完成民主改革后，在1958年实现了合作化，全州97％的农户都参加了农业合作社或互助组。藏族农民生产积极性空前高涨，今春共播种了六万亩青稞，播种面积比去年扩大了35％。土地比去年普遍深耕了两寸多，每亩施了五十担至一百担肥料，先后薅了四道草，并且和旱涝进行了斗争。
