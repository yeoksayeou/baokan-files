Title: Chairman Suwirjo of the Indonesian Nationalist Party Leaves Guangzhou for Home

Author: 
Date: 1959-10-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 18th. Chairman Suwirjo of the Indonesian Nationalist Party and his wife, and Hadi, member of the Central Standing Committee of the Indonesian Nationalist Party and former First Deputy Prime Minister, and his wife, who were invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to visit our country, left Guangzhou by train this morning via Hong Kong to return to their country after visiting Beijing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Nanjing, Wuhan, Guangzhou and other places. The guests arrived in Nanjing by plane from Shanghai on the 13th. On the same day, Suwirjo and his wife, and Hadi and his wife, laid wreaths at the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum, paid their respects to the Cemetery of Revolutionary Martyrs of Yuhuatai, visited the Tomb of the King of Boni of Indonesia, and toured the scenic spots. On the 14th, the distinguished guests flew to Wuhan and visited the Wuhan Iron and Steel Company, the Wuhan Heavy Machine Tool Plant, the Wuhan Tianyi Printing and Dyeing Factory, the Yangtze River Bridge, and the Exhibition of Achievements in Economic and Cultural Construction in Hubei Province over the Past Ten Years. On the 17th, the distinguished guests flew to Guangzhou. Zhu Guang, the Mayor of Guangzhou, hosted a banquet in the evening to welcome the distinguished guests.

### 印度尼西亚国民党总主席  苏维约离广州回国

1959-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚国民党总主席
    苏维约离广州回国
    新华社广州18日电　应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的印度尼西亚国民党总主席苏维约和他的夫人，印度尼西亚国民党中央常务委员会委员、前第一副总理哈迪和他的夫人，在北京、上海、杭州、南京、武汉、广州等地参观访问后，今天上午乘火车离开广州取道香港回国。客人们在13日由上海乘飞机到达南京。当天，苏维约和夫人、哈迪和夫人到中山陵献了花圈，并凭吊了雨花台人民革命烈士墓，瞻仰了印度尼西亚浡泥国士墓，游览了风景区。14日，贵宾们乘飞机到达武汉，在武汉参观了武汉钢铁公司、武汉重型机床厂、武汉天一印染厂、长江大桥和湖北省十年经济文化建设成就展览会。17日，贵宾乘飞机到达广州。广州市市长朱光当晚曾设宴欢迎贵宾。
