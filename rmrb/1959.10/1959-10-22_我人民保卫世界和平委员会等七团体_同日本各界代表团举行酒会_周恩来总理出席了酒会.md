Title: Our People's Committee for World Peace and Seven Other Organizations Hold Reception for Japanese Delegation; Premier Zhou Enlai Attends

Author:
Date: 1959-10-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 21st. The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China, the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and the All-China Youth Federation held a reception after the signing ceremony of a joint statement with the Japanese delegation from all walks of life celebrating the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

　　Premier Zhou Enlai attended the reception.

　　Those attending the reception included Guo Moruo, Liu Ningyi, Liao Chengzhi, Cai Chang, Zhang Xiruo, Chu Tunan, Liu Xiyuan, Wang Zhen, Liu Changsheng, Li Jiebo, Xu Guangping, Yang Hansheng, Zhao Puchu, Yang Yu, Cai Tingkai, Hu Yuzhi, Li Zhuchen, Wu Maosun, Wu Kejian, Lian Guan, Xie Bingxin, Mei Lanfang, Zhou Peiyuan, Wang Yunsheng, Ou Tangliang, Xiao Fangzhou, and others.

　　Those attending the reception included Katayama Tetsu, head of the Japanese delegation from all walks of life celebrating the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, deputy heads Obata Tadashi, Shiraishi Hitoshi, Honda Masato, and all members of the delegation.

　　Japanese peace activist in Beijing, Saionji Kinkazu, attended the reception.

　　In a reception filled with a friendly atmosphere, the people of China and Japan continuously raised their glasses, engaging in warm conversation and mutual congratulations. (Attached pictures)

　　The picture shows Premier Zhou Enlai with members of the Japanese delegation from all walks of life celebrating the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and the heads of our seven organizations. The sixth person from the left in the front row is delegation leader Katayama Tetsu.

### 我人民保卫世界和平委员会等七团体  同日本各界代表团举行酒会  周恩来总理出席了酒会

1959-10-22
第5版()
专栏：

    我人民保卫世界和平委员会等七团体
    同日本各界代表团举行酒会
    周恩来总理出席了酒会
    新华社21日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中华全国总工会、中国亚非团结委员会、中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会、中国人民外交学会、中国人民对外文化协会、中华全国青年联合会同日本各界庆祝中华人民共和国建国十周年代表团在举行共同声明签字仪式以后，举行了酒会。
    周恩来总理出席了酒会。
    参加酒会的，有郭沫若、刘宁一、廖承志、蔡畅、张奚若、楚图南、刘西元、王震、刘长胜、李颉伯、许广平、阳翰笙、赵朴初、杨煜、蔡廷锴、胡愈之、李烛尘、吴茂荪、吴克坚、连贯、谢冰心、梅兰芳、周培源、王芸生、区棠亮、萧方洲等。
    参加酒会的，有日本各界庆祝中华人民共和国建国十周年代表团团长片山哲，副团长小畑忠良、白石凡、本多正登和代表团的全体成员。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一参加了酒会。
    在充满一片友好气氛的酒会上，中、日两国人民不断举杯，热烈交谈，互相祝贺。（附图片）

                                新华社记者　塔吉古勒摄
