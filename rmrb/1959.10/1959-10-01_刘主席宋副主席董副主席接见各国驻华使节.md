Title: Chairman Liu, Vice-Chairwoman Soong, and Vice-Chairman Dong Receive Ambassadors to China from Various Countries
Author: 
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 30th. Chairman of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi, Vice-Chairwoman Soong Ching Ling, and Vice-Chairman Dong Biwu, today received ambassadors to China from various countries. During the reception, the acting head of the diplomatic corps, Afghan Ambassador to China Abdul Samad, delivered a speech on behalf of the ambassadors, warmly congratulating the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. He also presented a letter of congratulations from the diplomatic corps to Chairman Liu Shaoqi. Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Vice-Chairwoman Soong Ching Ling and Vice-Chairman Dong Biwu toasted the ambassadors and thanked them for their congratulations.

Present at the reception was Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu.

The letter of congratulations reads: Your Excellency,

On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the diplomatic corps in Beijing extends warm congratulations to the people and government of the People's Republic of China.

The members of the diplomatic corps have witnessed, in varying lengths of time, the enormous progress made by the Chinese people in all aspects. The diplomatic corps has been watching with admiration the amazing achievements that the Chinese people have already made and the tireless and sincere efforts they are making under the leadership of the government to lay the foundation for the rapid development of the country's economic and cultural life.

The diplomatic corps is confident that the People's Republic of China will continue to achieve success in its great task of promoting the well-being of its people and further improving their lives.

The diplomatic corps wishes to extend its warmest wishes to Your Excellency, wishing the Chinese people and their leaders prosperity, progress and happiness.

The diplomatic corps extends to Your Excellency its highest respects.

        Acting Head of the Diplomatic Corps in Beijing
                    Abdul Samad

### 刘主席宋副主席董副主席接见各国驻华使节

1959-10-01
第6版()
专栏：

    刘主席宋副主席董副主席接见各国驻华使节
    新华社30日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇，副主席宋庆龄、董必武，今天接见了各国驻中国的使节。在接见的时候，外交团代理团长、阿富汗驻中国大使阿卜杜勒·萨马德代表各国使节致词，热烈祝贺中华人民共和国成立十周年的伟大节日，他并且把外交团的一封贺信递交给刘少奇主席。刘少奇主席和宋庆龄、董必武副主席同各国使节干杯，感谢他们的祝贺。
    接见时在座的，有外交部副部长章汉夫。
    贺信写道：阁下：
    值此中华人民共和国成立十周年之际，在北京的外交团敬向中华人民共和国的人民和政府致以热烈的祝贺。
    外交团的成员们在或长或短的时间里亲眼看到中国人民在各方面取得的巨大的进步。外交团一直怀着钦佩的心情注视中国人民已经取得的惊人成就以及他们在政府领导下为了替这个国家的经济和文化生活的迅速发展奠定基础而作的不倦的竭诚的努力。
    外交团深信中华人民共和国在促进它的人民的幸福、进一步提高人民生活这一伟大任务方面继续取得成就。
    外交团愿向阁下致以最热烈的祝愿，祝中国人民和他们的领袖们繁荣、进步和幸福。
    外交团顺向阁下致以最崇高的敬意。
                驻北京的外交团代理团长
                              阿卜杜勒·萨马德
