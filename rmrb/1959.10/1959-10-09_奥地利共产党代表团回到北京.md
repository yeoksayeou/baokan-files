Title: Austrian Communist Party Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-09
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, October 8th — The Austrian Communist Party delegation, headed by Comrade Koplenig, returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon after visiting Shanghai and Wuhan.
    In Shanghai, the distinguished guests visited the site of the First National Congress of the Communist Party of China – the Shanghai Revolutionary History Memorial Hall, the Shanghai Industrial Exhibition, Shanghai Steel Plant No. 3, and the Minhang People’s Commune. During their stay in Wuhan, the distinguished guests visited Wuhan Iron and Steel Corporation, Wuhan Heavy Machine Tool Plant, the Yangtze River Bridge, Wuhan University, and the Exhibition of Achievements in Economic and Cultural Construction in Hubei Province over the Past Ten Years.
    The distinguished guests received a warm welcome everywhere during their visits.

### 奥地利共产党代表团回到北京

1959-10-09
第6版()
专栏：

    奥地利共产党代表团回到北京
    新华社8日讯　结束了在上海、武汉访问的以柯普勒尼希同志为首的奥地利共产党代表团，今天下午乘飞机回到北京。
    贵宾们曾在上海参观了中国共产党第一次代表大会会址——上海革命历史纪念馆、上海工业展览会，上钢三厂、闵行人民公社。贵宾们在武汉期间参观了武汉钢铁公司、武汉重型机床厂、长江大桥、武汉大学和湖北省十年经济、文化建设成就展览会。
    贵宾们在参观中到处受到热烈的欢迎。
