Title: Grand Banquet Held by the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Party Committee and Other Units to Celebrate the Early Tapping of Iron from Baogang's No. 1 Blast Furnace
Author:
Date: 1959-10-16
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Baotou, 15th: To celebrate the early tapping of iron from Baogang's No. 1 Blast Furnace, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Committee of the Communist Party of China, the People's Committee of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, and the Ministry of Metallurgical Industry held a grand banquet in Baotou City this afternoon. Premier Zhou Enlai and the heads of various central departments and mass organizations who came to participate in the celebration ceremony, as well as the heads of various provinces, cities, and autonomous regions, and representatives of factories and mines from all over the country who supported the construction of Baogang, attended the banquet.
    The Soviet delegation headed by Fu Ming, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, and Soviet experts who helped our country with construction, also attended today's banquet.
    Chairman Ulanhu said at the banquet: The early tapping of iron from Baogang's No. 1 Blast Furnace is inseparable from the care of the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao, and the strong support of fraternal provinces, cities, and regions. It is a great joyous event for the whole country and the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. It will play a significant role in accelerating national industrialization and changing the economic outlook of the autonomous region. In order to thank the Soviet Union and Soviet experts for their selfless assistance to Baogang, and to thank the people of all ethnic groups in fraternal provinces, cities, and regions for their strong support, tens of thousands of employees of all ethnic groups in Baogang are determined to double their efforts to complete the national plan ahead of schedule.
    Premier Zhou Enlai raised his glass at the banquet, wishing for the realization of all Baogang's construction plans, for striving to realize the industrialization of Baotou, for the prosperity of both humans and livestock and the economic prosperity of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, and for the health of the Soviet experts and their wives who are helping us with construction.
    Fu Ming, head of the Soviet delegation, and others also spoke at the banquet.

### 庆祝包钢一号高炉提前出铁  内蒙古自治区党委等单位举行盛大宴会

1959-10-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　庆祝包钢一号高炉提前出铁
    内蒙古自治区党委等单位举行盛大宴会
    新华社包头15日电　为庆祝包钢一号高炉提前出铁，中共内蒙古自治区委员会、内蒙古自治区人民委员会、冶金工业部今天下午在包头市举行盛大宴会。周恩来总理和前来参加庆祝典礼的中央各部门、各群众团体的负责人，各省市自治区负责人，支援包钢建设的全国各地厂矿代表出席了宴会。
    前来参加庆祝典礼的以苏联驻华大使馆参赞符明为首的苏联代表团和帮助我国建设的苏联专家，也出席了今天的宴会。
    乌兰夫主席在宴会上说：包钢一号高炉提前出铁，是与党中央和毛主席的关怀，兄弟省、市、区的大力支援分不开的，是全国和内蒙古自治区的一件大喜事，它对加速国家工业化和改变自治区的经济面貌都起很大作用。为了感谢苏联和苏联专家对包钢的无私援助，为了感谢全国各兄弟省、市、区各族人民的大力支援，包钢数万名各民族职工坚决为提前完成国家计划而加倍努力。
    周恩来总理在宴会上举杯，为争取包钢建设计划的全部实现，为努力实现包头的工业化，为内蒙古自治区人畜两旺、经济繁荣，为帮助我们建设的苏联专家和他们的夫人健康祝酒。
    苏联代表团团长符明等也在宴会上讲了话。
