Title: Uruguayan Parliamentary Delegation Arrives in Beijing for Friendly Visit to China at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1959-10-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 19th — The Uruguayan Parliamentary Delegation, led by the Speaker of the House of Representatives of Uruguay, Francisco Rodriguez Camusso, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs for a friendly visit to our country.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them were: Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Wu Maosun, Secretary-General; Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General; Shen Jian, Council Member; Zhou Erfu, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Chen Ming, head of the Chinese People's Bank delegation that visited Latin America, and Yao Zhen, head of the Chinese Journalists Delegation, and others.

The list of the Uruguayan Parliamentary Delegation is as follows:

Head of Delegation: Francisco Rodriguez Camusso, Speaker of the House of Representatives

Members:

Dr. Angel Maria Gianola, Chairman of the Labor Legislation Committee

Semara Michellini, Member of the Labor Legislation Committee

Juan Rodriguez Correa, Former Minister of National Defense, Vice Chairman of the Internal Affairs Committee

Martinez Moreno, Member of the Finance Committee

Francisco Mario Ubillos, Member of the Finance Committee. (Photo attached)

The picture shows members of the Uruguayan Parliamentary Delegation and welcomers at the airport. The third person from the left in the front row is the head of the delegation, Francisco Rodriguez Camusso, and the fourth person from the left in the front row is Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Hesong)

### 应邀来我国进行友好访问  乌拉圭议会代表团到京

1959-10-20
第5版()
专栏：

　　应邀来我国进行友好访问
    乌拉圭议会代表团到京
    新华社19日讯　应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的乌拉圭议会代表团，在团长、乌拉圭众议院议长弗朗西斯科·罗德里格斯·卡穆索率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有：中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之、秘书长吴茂荪、副秘书长吴晓达、理事申健，中国人民对外文化协会秘书长周而复；曾经访问过拉丁美洲的中国人民银行代表团团长陈明和中国新闻工作者代表团团长姚溱等。
    乌拉圭议会代表团名单如下：
    团长　弗朗西斯科·罗德里格斯·卡穆索　众议院议长
    团员
    安赫耳·玛丽亚·希亚诺拉博士　劳动立法委员会主席
    塞马拉·米切利亚　劳动立法委员会委员
    胡安·罗德里格斯·科雷亚前国防部长、内务委员会副主席
    马丁内斯·莫雷诺　财政委员会委员
    弗朗西科斯·马里奥·乌比略斯　财政委员会委员。（附图片）
