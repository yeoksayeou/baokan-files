Title: Cuban Revolutionary Directorate Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The Cuban Revolutionary Directorate Delegation, a group of five led by the Secretary-General of the Cuban Revolutionary Directorate, Faure Chomón Mediavilla, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning.

The delegation is here to attend the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and was delayed due to the long distance.

Greeting them at the airport were Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Wu Maosun, Secretary-General, Shen Jian, Director, and Yao Zhen, Head of the Chinese Journalists' Delegation to Latin America.

### 古巴革命指导委员会代表团到京

1959-10-04
第2版()
专栏：

    古巴革命指导委员会代表团到京
    新华社3日讯　古巴革命指导委员会代表团一行五人由古巴革命指导委员会总书记福·乔·梅迪亚维利亚率领，今天上午飞抵北京。
    代表团是来参加中华人民共和国建国十周年庆祝典礼的，因路远来迟。
    到机场欢迎的有中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之、秘书长吴茂荪、理事申健和中国新闻工作者访问拉丁美洲代表团团长姚溱。
