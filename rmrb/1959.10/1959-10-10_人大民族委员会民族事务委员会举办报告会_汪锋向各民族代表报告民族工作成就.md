Title: NPC Nationalities Committee and Nationalities Affairs Commission Hold Report Meeting; Wang Feng Reports to Representatives of All Nationalities on Achievements in Nationalities Work
Author:
Date: 1959-10-10
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 9th: The National People's Congress (NPC) Nationalities Committee and the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China jointly hosted a report meeting today in the auditorium of the National Culture Palace.

The report meeting was presided over by Xie Fumin, Vice Chairman of the NPC Nationalities Committee. Wang Feng, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee and Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, gave a report on "Achievements in Nationalities Work over the Past Ten Years" at the meeting.

More than 1,100 representatives of all nationalities from the visiting delegations and youth study groups of all nationalities who came to the capital to participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China attended the report meeting.

Panchen Erdeni Qoigyijabu, Acting Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, and Vice Chairmen Pagbalha Geleg Namgyal and Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, also Secretary-General, attended the report meeting.

Some religious figures also attended the report meeting.

### 人大民族委员会民族事务委员会举办报告会  汪锋向各民族代表报告民族工作成就

1959-10-10
第2版()
专栏：

    人大民族委员会民族事务委员会举办报告会
    汪锋向各民族代表报告民族工作成就
    新华社9日讯　全国人民代表大会民族委员会、中华人民共和国民族事务委员会今天在民族文化宫礼堂联合主办报告会。
    报告会由全国人民代表大会民族委员会副主任委员谢扶民主持。中共中央统战部副部长、民族事务委员会副主任汪锋在会上作了“关于十年来民族工作成就”的报告。
    来首都参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的各民族观礼团、各民族青年学习团的一千一百多各民族代表出席了报告会。
    西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞，副主任委员帕巴拉·卓列朗杰、副主任委员兼秘书长阿沛·阿旺晋美出席了报告会。
    出席报告会的还有部分宗教界人士。
