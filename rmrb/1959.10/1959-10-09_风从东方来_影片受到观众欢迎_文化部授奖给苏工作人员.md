Title: "The Wind from the East" Film Welcomed by Audiences, Ministry of Culture Awards Soviet Staff

Author:
Date: 1959-10-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 8th: The Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China awarded the Soviet staff members of the "The Wind from the East" film crew, a joint production of the Moscow Film Studio and the Changchun Film Studio, the "Excellent Worker" certificate and medal. The award ceremony was held in Beijing yesterday afternoon.

Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan, on behalf of Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, presented the certificates and medals to Vladimir Nikolaevich Surin, General Manager of the Moscow Film Studio, and Yefim Rivovich Dzigan, the Soviet Chief Director of "The Wind from the East," who are currently in Beijing. He also presented them each with two beautifully bound "China" pictorial albums and ivory commemorative badges marking the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Those present at the ceremony also included Luo Jun, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Cai Chusheng, Chairman of the China Film Workers Association, Chen Huangmei, Acting Director of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture, Yu Xuewei, the Chinese Chief Director of "The Wind from the East," and actors Cui Wei and Tian Fang. Antonov, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, and Cultural Counselor Sudarikov also attended the ceremony.

Before the ceremony, Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan hosted a luncheon to bid farewell to the Soviet film workers delegation who were about to leave Beijing to return to their country.

### “风从东方来”影片受到观众欢迎  文化部授奖给苏工作人员

1959-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    “风从东方来”影片受到观众欢迎
    文化部授奖给苏工作人员
    据新华社8日讯　中华人民共和国文化部授给莫斯科电影制片厂和长春电影制片厂合拍的“风从东方来”影片摄制组的苏联工作人员以“优秀工作者”的奖状和奖章。昨天下午在北京举行了授奖仪式。
    文化部副部长夏衍代表文化部部长沈雁冰把奖状和奖章交给了正在北京的莫斯科电影制片厂总厂长符拉季米尔·尼可拉耶维奇·苏林和“风从东方来”影片的苏方总导演叶菲姆·里沃维奇·吉甘。他同时送给他们两人两本精装的“中国”画册和纪念新中国十周年的象牙纪念章。
    出席仪式的，还有对外文化联络委员会副主任罗俊、中国电影工作者联谊会主席蔡楚生、文化部电影局代局长陈荒煤、“风从东方来”影片的中方总导演于学伟、演员崔嵬、田方等。苏联驻华大使馆临时代办安东诺夫和文化参赞苏达利柯夫也出席了仪式。
    仪式以前，文化部副部长夏衍为送别即将离北京返国的苏联电影工作者代表团举行了便宴。
