Title: Chairman Dobi Visits Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1959-10-09
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, October 8th: István Dobi, Chairman of the Presidential Council of the Hungarian People's Republic, arrived in Wuhan by plane this afternoon from Shanghai at the invitation of Chairman Liu Shaoqi for a visit to our country. Chen Zhengren, Minister of Agricultural Machinery, and Lin Ping, Deputy Director of the American and Australian Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, accompanied the distinguished guests to Wuhan.

The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at the airport by Wang Renzhong, First Secretary of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Zhang Tixue, Governor of Hubei Province, Song Kanfu, First Secretary of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and others.

Dr. Molnár Erik, head of the Hungarian Labor People's Delegation and President of the Hungarian-Chinese Friendship Association, who came to Wuhan for a visit on the 7th, also went to the airport to welcome them.

In the afternoon, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Chen Zhengren and Zhang Tixue, visited the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge. The person in charge of the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge Engineering Office gave Chairman Dobi a detailed introduction to the construction of the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge and presented the distinguished guests with picture albums and commemorative badges of the bridge. In the evening, Wang Renzhong, First Secretary of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Zhang Tixue, Governor of Hubei Province, hosted a banquet in honor of the distinguished guests.

When the distinguished guests left Shanghai, Ke Qingshi, member of the Political局 of the Central Committee, First Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Mayor of Shanghai, went to the airport to see them off.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, October 7th: István Dobi, Chairman of the Presidential Council of the Hungarian People's Republic and head of the Hungarian People's Republic Party and Government Delegation, accompanied by Chen Zhengren, alternate member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Minister of Agricultural Machinery, and Ma Tianshui, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, visited the Malu People's Commune in the suburbs of Shanghai today.

The person in charge of the commune introduced Chairman István Dobi to the organization, production, income distribution, and welfare of the members of the commune, and cited specific facts about the development of the commune to illustrate the superiority of the people's commune. Chairman István Dobi visited the commune's own agricultural machinery factory, winery, fiber factory, and cotton and rice fields with promising harvests, and visited the home of a commune member.

In the afternoon, Chairman István Dobi took a boat trip on the Huangpu River.

### 道比主席到武汉访问

1959-10-09
第6版()
专栏：

    道比主席到武汉访问
    新华社武汉8日电　应刘少奇主席的邀请在我国进行访问的匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比·伊斯特万，今天下午乘飞机从上海到达武汉。农业机械部部长陈正人、外交部美澳司副司长林平陪同贵宾到达武汉。
    贵宾在机场上受到了中共湖北省委第一书记王任重、湖北省省长张体学、中共武汉市委第一书记宋侃夫等人的热烈欢迎。
    在7日来武汉参观访问的匈牙利劳动人民代表团团长、匈牙利—中国友好协会主席莫尔纳尔·埃里克博士也到机场欢迎。
    下午，贵宾由陈正人、张体学陪同，参观了武汉长江大桥，长江大桥桥工处负责人向道比主席详细地介绍了武汉长江大桥的建设情况，并向贵宾赠送了大桥的画册和纪念章。晚上，中共湖北省委第一书记王任重、湖北省省长张体学设宴招待贵宾。
    贵宾离开上海的时候，中共中央政治局委员、中共上海市委第一书记、上海市市长柯庆施等曾到机场送行。
    新华社上海7日电　匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席、匈牙利人民共和国党政代表团团长道比·伊斯特万，今天在中共中央候补委员、农业机械部部长陈正人，中共上海市委书记处书记马天水等人陪同下，访问了上海市郊马陆人民公社。
    公社负责人向道比·伊斯特万主席介绍了公社的组织、生产、收入分配和社员生活福利等方面的情况，并列举公社发展的具体事实，说明人民公社的优越性。道比·伊斯特万主席参观公社自办的农具机械厂、酿酒厂、纤维厂和丰收在望的棉田和稻田，并访问了一个社员的家庭。
    下午，道比·伊斯特万主席乘船游览了黄浦江。
