Title: Ten Springs – Watching the Color Feature Documentary “The Tenth Spring”
Author: Bai Yuan
Date: 1959-10-05
Page: 8

    Ten Springs
    --Watching the Color Feature Documentary “The Tenth Spring”
    Bai Yuan
    Ten springs, ten autumns,
    Fleeting time,
    Ten years have passed in the blink of an eye.
    Ten years ago, our new motherland,
    Was born in the eastern dawn;
    The road traveled in ten years is like a surging river,
    In an instant, it stirred up a splendid landscape.
    The children born ten years ago have become teenagers,
    Now they are sitting full in the cinema,
    Watching our motherland's "The Tenth Spring."
    The movie hasn't started yet,
    The children are noisy, boisterous, shouting and yelling,
    Until the screen lights up,
    A melodious music sounds,
    They suddenly cheered and applauded.
    Flowers sway on the screen, doves fly,
    Spring is everywhere in the motherland,
    Leaving behind the arduous footprints of the pioneers...
    This is the first spring of our motherland.
    I don't know when,
    The children became so quiet,
    At this time, Chairman Mao is standing on Tiananmen,
    Announcing the birth of the People's Republic of China.
    Red flags like a sea in front of Tiananmen,
    Surging with joyous crowds,
    The parade resounding with booming drums...
    Since then, ten springs have passed,
    Ten springs remain in our hearts,
    Time has flowed away in ten years in an instant,
    Our dear motherland,
    Grows in one moving scene after another.
    One unforgettable scene after another,
    Appears before my eyes,
    One unforgettable scene after another,
    On the road we walked together,
    Huai River, Yangtze River, Yellow River...
    Anshan Iron and Steel, Wuhan Iron and Steel, Baotou Iron and Steel...
    The river surges,
    The crowd rolls,
    Lights like a sea all over the mountains and plains,
    The tumbling earth shook down a sky full of stars...
    Great Leap Forward, People's Commune,
    Another Great Leap Forward,
    This is the tenth spring of our motherland.
    We will always remember those sleepless nights,
    In the east of the world,
    The raging fire of socialism was ignited...
    There are thousands of roads leading to communism,
    The road of the People's Commune is broad and glorious;
    Who says there is no endless spring in the world?
    We move from one spring into another.
    The motherland's youthful flowers,
    Bloom one by one on the brilliant screen;
    The seedlings of children's lives,
    Grow in the garden of eternal youth;
    The Great Leap Forward march sings loudly,
    The children step to the beat of the song,
    Stamping their feet so hard, applauding,
    We step to the beat together,
    Let's go, children,
    No matter what others say,
    We take our ten springs,
    To finish the spring of socialism,
    And then enter the spring of communism.

### 十个春天——看彩色长纪录片“第十个春天”
白原
1959-10-05
第8版()
专栏：

    十个春天
    ——看彩色长纪录片“第十个春天”
    白原
    十个春天，十个秋天，
    飞逝的时间，
    转眼又过了十年。
    十年前，我们新的祖国，
    在东方的朝霞里诞生；
    十年走过的道路像奔腾的江水，
    转眼间泛起了一片锦绣的河山。
    十年前诞生的孩子已经成少年，
    如今他们正坐满在电影院里，
    看的是我们祖国的“第十个春天”。
    电影还没有放映，
    孩子们吵呵，闹呵，又叫又嚷，
    等到银幕一亮，
    响起了悠扬的乐音，
    他们一下子欢叫又鼓掌。
    银幕上花枝摇曳，白鸽飞翔，
    祖国的大地上春光处处，
    留下了开拓者们艰辛的脚印……
    这是我们祖国的第一个春天。
    不知从什么时候起，
    孩子们变得那么安静，
    这时候毛主席正站在天安门上，
    宣告中华人民共和国的诞生。
    天安门前红旗似海，
    汹涌着狂欢的人群，
    游行的队伍响着隆隆的鼓声……
    从此十个春天过去了，
    十个春天留驻在我们心间，
    时间一下子流过了十年，
    我们亲爱的祖国，
    在一幕一幕动人的景象里成长。
    一幕一幕难忘的景象，
    在我的眼前出现，
    一幕一幕难忘的景象，
    在我们一起走过的道路上，
    淮河，长江，黄河……
    鞍钢，武钢，包钢……
    江水滔滔，
    人群滚滚，
    漫山遍野灯如海，
    翻腾的大地摇下了满天星……
    大跃进，人民公社，
    又是一个大跃进，
    这是我们祖国的第十个春天。
    我们将永远记得那些不眠的夜晚，
    在世界的东方，
    燃起了社会主义的熊熊的火光……
    通往共产主义的道路千万条，
    人民公社的道路宽广又辉煌；
    谁说世上没有不尽的春光？
    我们从一个春天走进另一个春天。
    祖国的青春的花朵，
    一朵朵在绚烂的银幕上开放；
    孩子们的生命的幼苗，
    在永恒的青春的花园里成长；
    大跃进进行曲歌声嘹亮，
    孩子们踏着歌曲的节拍，
    那么使劲地踏着脚，鼓着掌，
    我们一起踏着节拍，
    我们走吧，孩子们，
    不管别人说些什么，
    我们带着我们的十个春天，
    走完社会主义的春天，
    再走进共产主义的春天。
