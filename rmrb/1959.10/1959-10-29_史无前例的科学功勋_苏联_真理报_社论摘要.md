Title: Unprecedented Scientific Achievement – Excerpts from a "Pravda" Editorial
Author:
Date: 1959-10-29
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 27th: The "Pravda" published today an editorial entitled "Unprecedented Scientific Achievement."

    The editorial pointed out that for the first time in human history, photographic images of the far side of the moon, invisible from Earth, have been obtained. This is the result of the heroic efforts of Soviet scientists, designers, engineers, technicians, and workers who built the automatic interplanetary station.

    The editorial said that the automatic interplanetary station flew past the moon and the earth! This cosmic laboratory photographed the far side of the moon from a distance of 65,000 kilometers! It transmitted images of the invisible part of the moon from the depths of space back to Earth! Every achievement is an unprecedented scientific feat! This feat was achieved by the Soviet people – this is the most natural phenomenon. This feat confirms the unprecedented victory of mathematics, mechanics, physics, radio electronics, and chemistry, and the achievements of Soviet metallurgists, astronomers, and other scientific workers, our engineers, technicians, and workers; they have embodied theoretical calculations into concrete technical designs, which in our era are the pinnacle of human genius.

    This feat is a manifestation of our country's high industrial power, enabling us to manufacture the equipment, instruments, and materials necessary to realize these grand ideals. It marks the indisputable superiority of the Soviet socialist system and demonstrates to the world what miracles can be created by the creative labor of people who have been freed from capitalist oppression.

    The editorial emphasized that the epoch-making achievements of Soviet scientists, experts, and workers are a great victory for the entire Soviet people building communism under the leadership of the Leninist Party. This is an outstanding contribution to the world's scientific development. Progressive people all over the world are applauding the Soviet space conquerors, congratulating them on their remarkable achievements.

    The editorial pointed out that it is of great significance that the new and unparalleled victory of Soviet science and technology was accomplished on the eve of the Great October Revolution anniversary. These new victories once again clearly demonstrate that the Great October Socialist Revolution, which ended the exploitation of man by man, opened up the only correct path of development for the people of all countries – the path of building socialism and communism, which leads to unprecedented prosperity in the economy, culture, and people's material welfare.

    The editorial said that the multi-stage rocket made by the Soviet people sent the interplanetary station into its predetermined orbit, allowing it to fly past the moon and send back images of the lunar surface. What more proof is needed of what modern Soviet science and technology can do, its achievements, and its leading position in important fields of human knowledge!

    The editorial pointed out that three hundred and fifty years have passed since the first use of telescopes to observe the moon, during which time selenography has made great achievements. Today, these achievements have been developed by Soviet space researchers into an unprecedented scientific victory. A new era of celestial research has begun. After preliminary study of the first images of the far side of the moon, Soviet scientists were able to draw important conclusions about the characteristics of the lunar surface. Further study of the details of the far side of the moon will expand people's understanding of planetary evolution. But even now, the new names determined by the Soviet Academy of Sciences Committee have enriched the content of selenography. These names commemorate the victory of Soviet science, which has made invaluable contributions to world science.

    The editorial exclaimed: Long live the Soviet scientists, designers, engineers, technicians, and workers who, for the first time in history, realized a cosmic flight to the moon, thus opening a new era of human conquest of outer space and winning glory for the motherland!

### 史无前例的科学功勋——苏联“真理报”社论摘要

1959-10-29
第7版()
专栏：

    史无前例的科学功勋
    ——苏联“真理报”社论摘要
    新华社27日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科27日讯：“真理报”今天发表了题为“史无前例的科学功勋”的社论。
    社论指出，在人类历史上第一次获得从地球上看不到的月球背面的照片影像，这是制造自动行星际站的苏联科学家、设计师、工程师、技术员和工人们英勇努力的结果。
    社论说，自动行星际站绕过月球和地球飞行！这个宇宙试验室在距离月球六万五千公里的地方对月球的背面拍照！把月球不可见部分的影像从宇宙的深处传送到地球！每一个成就都是史无前例的科学功勋！这个功勋是苏联人树立的——这是最符合规律的现象。这个功勋证实了数学、机械学、物理学、无线电电子学、化学的空前的胜利，苏联冶金学家、天文学家和其他科学工作者，我们的工程师、技术员和工人们的成就；他们把理论计算具体体现为技术设计，在我们的时代里，这种设计是人类天才成就的顶峰。
    这一功勋是我国高度的工业威力的表现，我国已能制造为实现这些宏伟的理想所必需的设备、仪器和材料。它标志着苏维埃社会主义制度的无可争辩的优越性，并向全世界表明，摆脱了资本主义压迫的人民的创造性劳动能够创造出什么样的奇迹来。
    社论强调说，苏联的科学家、专家和工人所获得的划时代的成绩，是在列宁党领导下建设共产主义的全体苏联人民的伟大胜利。这是对世界科学发展的卓越贡献。全世界进步的人们都对苏联宇宙征服者拍手欢庆，庆贺他们获得卓越的成绩。
    社论指出，具有重大意义的是，苏联科学技术新的无可伦比的胜利，是在伟大的十月革命节前夕完成的。这些新的胜利再一次明显地表明，结束了人剥削人的伟大的十月社会主义革命给各国人民开辟了唯一正确的发展道路——建设社会主义和共产主义的道路，这条道路导致经济、文化和人民物质福利的空前繁荣。
    社论说，苏联人民制造的多级火箭把行星际站送到了预定的轨道，使它绕过了月球，把获得的月球表面的影像发送回来。这就是现代苏联科学和技术所能做的事，它的成就，它在人类知识重要领域中的首位，还需要什么证明呢！
    社论指出，自从初次使用天体望远镜观察月球以来，已经过去了三百五十年，在这期间，月面学取得了巨大的成就。今天，这些成就被苏联宇宙研究者们发展成为史无前例的科学胜利。研究天体的新纪元开始了。初步研究了月球背面的第一批照片之后，就使苏联科学家能够对月球表面的特征作出重要的结论。对月球背面的表面详细情况的进一步研究，将扩大人们对行星演化的认识。但是，即使是在现在，苏联科学院委员会所确定的新的名称也已经丰富了月面学的内容。这些名称铭记了为世界科学作出了难以估价的贡献的苏联科学的胜利。
    社论高呼：在历史上第一次实现向月球作宇宙飞行，从而开辟了人类征服宇宙空间的新纪元、并以此为祖国争光的苏联科学家、设计工作者、工程师、技师和工人万岁！
