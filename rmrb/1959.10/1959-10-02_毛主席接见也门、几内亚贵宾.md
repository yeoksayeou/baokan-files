Title: Chairman Mao Meets Distinguished Guests from Yemen and Guinea
Author:
Date: 1959-10-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 1st October - Chairman Mao Zedong received the Yemeni Kingdom Government Delegation headed by Yemeni Kingdom Minister of State Sayyid Ali al-Mu'ayyad this evening. Vice Premier Chen Yi was present at the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, 1st October - Chairman Mao Zedong received the Guinean Republic Government Representative and Guinean Minister of Education, Barry Diawandou, this evening. Vice Premier Chen Yi was present at the meeting.

### 毛主席接见也门、几内亚贵宾

1959-10-02
第3版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见也门、几内亚贵宾
    新华社1日讯　毛泽东主席今天晚上接见了以也门王国国务大臣赛义德·阿里·穆安亚德为首的也门王国政府代表团。接见时在座的有陈毅副总理。
    新华社1日讯　毛泽东主席今晚接见了几内亚共和国政府代表、几内亚教育部部长巴里·迪亚万杜。接见时在座的有副总理陈毅。
