Title: Korean Agricultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-26
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, October 25th. The Korean agricultural delegation of twenty people, headed by Kim Man-geum, the Minister of Agriculture of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, arrived in Beijing today by train at the invitation of Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture. Liao Luyan, Deputy Director of the Rural Work Department of the CPC Central Committee and Minister of Agriculture, Cai Ziwei, Deputy Minister of Agriculture, Li Qiangfen, Deputy Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others went to the station to welcome them.
    Li Yong-ho, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, also welcomed them at the station.

### 朝鲜农业代表团抵京

1959-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜农业代表团抵京
    新华社25日讯　以朝鲜民主主义人民共和国农业相金万金为首的朝鲜农业代表团一行二十人，应农业部部长廖鲁言邀请，今天乘火车到达北京。前往车站欢迎的，有中共中央农村工作部副部长、农业部部长廖鲁言，农业部副部长蔡子伟，外交部第二亚洲司副司长李强奋等人。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使李永镐也到车站欢迎。
