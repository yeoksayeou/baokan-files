Title: President Novotný Leads Delegation Away from Beijing; Liu Shaoqi, Soong Ching Ling, Dong Biwu, and Zhu De See Them Off at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1959-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: The Czechoslovak Communist Party Central Committee First Secretary and President of the Czechoslovak Republic, Antonín Novotný, leading the Czechoslovak Republic Party and Government Delegation, left Beijing by special plane this morning to visit the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee and Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Soong Ching Ling and Dong Biwu, Vice Chairmen of the People's Republic of China, and Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee and Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People’s Congress, along with other Party and state leaders, were at the airport to see off the distinguished guests.

After the band played the national anthems of Czechoslovakia and China, President Novotný, accompanied by Chairman Liu Shaoqi, reviewed the honor guard of the Army, Navy, and Air Force. All the distinguished Czechoslovak guests received flowers presented by the young pioneers of the capital.

Also present at the airport to see them off today were Lin Boqu, Chen Yi, and Li Fuchun, members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee; Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat; Xi Zhongxun, Vice Premier of the State Council; Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, and Bainqen Erdini Qoigyi Gyaincain, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Burhan Shahidi, Pagbalha Geleg Namgyal, and Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the CPPCC; Zhang Zhizhong, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council; Xie Juezai, President of the Supreme People's Court; Ji Pengfei and Zeng Yongquan, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs; Xia Yan, President of the Sino-Czechoslovak Friendship Association; responsible persons from various departments of central state organs, various democratic parties, and various people's organizations, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and representatives from all walks of life in the capital.

The Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Josef Bushniak, and diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries to China, were also at the airport to see them off.

Pak Gwang-seon, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and Li Ik-hwan, the Korean Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, who came specifically to welcome the distinguished guests, accompanied the delegation to Pyongyang on the same plane today.

Before leaving, President Novotný warmly shook hands with Chairman Liu Shaoqi and other leaders of our Party and state to say goodbye. At about nine o'clock, as the special plane carrying the distinguished guests slowly took off, the well-wishers waved to the distinguished guests and wished them a pleasant journey.

### 诺沃提尼总统率代表团离京  刘少奇、宋庆龄、董必武、朱德等到机场欢送

1959-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    诺沃提尼总统率代表团离京
    刘少奇、宋庆龄、董必武、朱德等到机场欢送
    新华社7日讯　捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会第一书记、捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统安·诺沃提尼率领的捷克斯洛伐克共和国党政代表团，今天上午乘专机离开北京，前往朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访问。
    中共中央副主席、中华人民共和国主席刘少奇，中华人民共和国副主席宋庆龄、董必武，中共中央副主席、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德，以及党和国家的其他领导人，都到机场欢送贵宾。
    乐队高奏捷中两国国歌后，诺沃提尼总统由刘少奇主席陪同，检阅了陆海空三军仪仗队。全体捷克斯洛伐克贵宾接受了首都男女少先队员献给他们的鲜花。
    今天到机场送行的，还有中共中央政治局委员林伯渠、陈毅、李富春，中共中央书记处书记王稼祥、候补书记杨尚昆，国务院副总理习仲勋，人大常委会副委员长郭沫若、黄炎培、班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉、帕巴拉·卓列朗杰、阿沛·阿旺晋美，国防委员会副主席张治中，最高人民法院院长谢觉哉，外交部副部长姬鹏飞、曾涌泉，中捷友好协会会长夏衍，中央国家机关各部门、各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人，中国人民解放军高级将领和首都各界代表人士。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使布希尼亚克和各国驻华使节与外交官员，也到机场送行。
    专程前来迎接贵宾的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务省副相朴光善和朝鲜驻捷克斯洛伐克大使李益焕，今天陪同代表团同机前往平壤。
    临行前，诺沃提尼总统同刘少奇主席和我党和国家的其他领导人热烈握手道别。九时许，当贵宾们乘坐的专机徐徐起飞时，欢送的人群不断地向贵宾们挥手致意，祝他们一路顺风。
