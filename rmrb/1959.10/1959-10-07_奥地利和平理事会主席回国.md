Title: Chairman of the Austrian Peace Council Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Mr. Brandweiner, Chairman of the Austrian Peace Council, who was invited to China to attend the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.

### 奥地利和平理事会主席回国

1959-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    奥地利和平理事会主席回国
    新华社6日讯　应邀来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的奥地利和平理事会主席布兰德魏纳，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
