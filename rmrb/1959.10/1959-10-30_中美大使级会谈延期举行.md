Title: Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks Postponed
Author:
Date: 1959-10-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 29th: It was announced here that the ninety-third meeting of the Sino-US ambassadorial talks, originally scheduled for November 3rd, has been postponed to November 6th by mutual agreement for administrative reasons.

### 中美大使级会谈延期举行

1959-10-30
第5版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈延期举行
    新华社华沙29日电　此间宣布，原订于11月3日举行的中美大使级会谈第九十三次会议，因行政性理由经双方协议，推迟至11月6日举行。
