Title: Vice Premier Lu Dingyi Receives Iraqi Journalists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-10-29
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, October 28th: Vice Premier Lu Dingyi received the members of the Iraqi Journalists Delegation this morning: Muhammad Hilmi Ali Sharif, Muhammad Hassan Suri, Farid Yusuf Ufi, and Mufid Jazari.

Present at the reception was Iraqi Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China, Jalal Jaf.

Also present at the reception were Mei Yi, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association, Kang Maozhao, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Gong Dafei, Deputy Director of the West Asia and Africa Department. The head of the Iraqi Journalists Delegation, Abdullah Abbas Ali, had already returned to his country after visiting China.

### 陆定一副总理  接见伊拉克新闻工作者代表团

1959-10-29
第5版()
专栏：

    陆定一副总理
    接见伊拉克新闻工作者代表团
    新华社28日讯　陆定一副总理今天上午接见了伊拉克新闻工作者代表团成员：穆罕默德·赫勒米·阿里·谢里夫、穆罕默德·哈桑·苏里、法里德·尤素福·乌菲、穆费德·贾扎里。
    接见时在座的有伊拉克驻中国临时代办贾拉尔·贾夫。
    接见时在座的还有中华全国新闻工作者协会副会长梅益，外交部新闻司副司长康茅召，西亚非洲司副司长宫达非等。伊拉克新闻工作者代表团团长阿卜杜拉·阿巴斯·阿里在访问我国后，已先期回国。
