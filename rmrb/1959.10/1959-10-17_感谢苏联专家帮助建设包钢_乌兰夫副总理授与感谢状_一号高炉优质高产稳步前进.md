Title: Thanking Soviet Experts for Aiding in the Construction of Baotou Steel, Vice Premier Ulanhu Presents Certificates of Appreciation; No. 1 Blast Furnace Steadily Advances with High Quality and High Yield

Author:

Date: 1959-10-17
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Baotou, 16th. Ulanhu, Vice Premier of the State Council, First Secretary of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, received on the afternoon of the 14th the entire delegation of the Soviet Union, headed by Fu Ming, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, who were invited to attend the ribbon-cutting ceremony for the first blast furnace at Baotou Steel. He also received nine Soviet experts, including Stebanski, who were assisting in the construction in Baotou.

   Present at the meeting were Xia Yun, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Metallurgical Industry, Su Qianyi, Secretary of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and First Secretary of the Baotou Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Kui Bi, Secretary of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Vice Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Wang Yilun, Vice Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, as well as the party and government leaders of Baotou City and Baotou Steel.

   During the meeting, Vice Premier Ulanhu thanked the Soviet government and Soviet experts for their selfless assistance in the construction of Baotou Steel. On behalf of Premier Zhou Enlai, he presented a certificate of appreciation to Stebanski, the leader of the Soviet expert group, and Xia Yun, on behalf of Wang Heshou, Minister of the Ministry of Metallurgical Industry, presented a certificate of appreciation to Levin, the leader of the Soviet pig iron expert group. Ulanhu also presented "Sino-Soviet Friendship" commemorative badges to the Soviet experts who helped build Baotou Steel.

   Stebanski, the leader of the Soviet expert group, expressed his gratitude on behalf of all Soviet experts working at Baotou Steel for the high honor given by the Chinese government. Fu Ming, the head of the Soviet delegation, on behalf of the Soviet Embassy in China, expressed his gratitude and congratulations to Vice Premier Ulanhu and the Chinese government, congratulating them on the successful construction of the first blast furnace at Baotou Steel.

   Xinhua News Agency, Baotou, 16th. The daily pig iron production level of Baotou Steel's No. 1 automated large blast furnace on the 15th has increased by 1.7 times compared to the first day of operation, and all the pig iron produced is of qualified steelmaking quality.

   The 15th was the day when Baotou Steel workers gathered to celebrate the early production of iron from the No. 1 blast furnace. Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai personally attended the ribbon-cutting ceremony, and many party and government leaders from the Central Government, Inner Mongolia, and Baotou City came to the site to congratulate, greatly boosting the morale of the workers.

   Since the No. 1 blast furnace at Baotou Steel began producing iron on September 26, it has been steadily advancing along the path of high quality and high yield. The average daily output in early October increased by 53.3% compared to the first five days of October; the average daily output from the 11th to the 14th increased by 20.91% compared to the average daily output in early October.

### 感谢苏联专家帮助建设包钢  乌兰夫副总理授与感谢状  一号高炉优质高产稳步前进

1959-10-17
第2版()
专栏：

　　感谢苏联专家帮助建设包钢
    乌兰夫副总理授与感谢状
    一号高炉优质高产稳步前进
    新华社包头16日电　国务院副总理、中共内蒙古自治区委员会第一书记、内蒙古自治区主席乌兰夫，14日下午接见了应邀参加包钢一号高炉出铁剪彩典礼大会的以苏联驻华大使馆参赞符明为首的苏联代表团全体团员，同时接见了在包头帮助建设的苏联专家斯捷班斯基等九位专家。
    接见时，在座的有冶金工业部副部长夏耘，中共内蒙古自治区委员会书记、中共包头市委第一书记苏谦益，中共内蒙古自治区委员会书记、内蒙古自治区副主席奎璧，内蒙古自治区副主席王逸伦，以及包头市和包钢的党政负责人。
   乌兰夫副总理在接见时，感谢苏联政府和苏联专家对包钢建设的无私援助。他代表周恩来总理向苏联专家组组长斯捷班斯基授予感谢状，夏耘代表冶金工业部部长王鹤寿向苏联炼铁专家组组长列文授予感谢状。乌兰夫还给帮助建设包钢的苏联专家佩戴了“中苏友谊”纪念章。
    苏联专家组组长斯捷班斯基代表在包钢工作的全体苏联专家，对中国政府给以的崇高荣誉表示感谢。苏联代表团团长符明代表苏联驻华大使馆，向乌兰夫副总理和中国政府表示感谢和祝贺，祝贺包钢一号高炉的胜利建成。
    据新华社包头16日电　包钢一号自动化大型高炉15日这天的日产生铁水平，已较开炉生产的第一天提高了一点七倍，所产生铁全部是合格的炼钢铁。
    15日是包钢职工集会庆祝一号高炉提前出铁的日子。中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来亲临剪彩和中央、内蒙古、包头市的许多党政领导同志到现场祝贺，大大地鼓舞了职工们的干劲。
    包钢一号高炉自从9月26日开始出铁以来，一直沿着优质高产的大道稳步前进。10月上旬的平均日产量，比10月头五天提高了53.3％；11日到14日的平均日产量，又比10月上旬的平均日产量提高了20.91％。
