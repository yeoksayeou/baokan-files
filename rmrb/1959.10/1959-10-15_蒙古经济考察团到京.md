Title: Mongolian Economic Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. The economic delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, led by Tumenbayar Alagacha, First Vice Chairman of the State Planning Commission of the Mongolian People's Republic, arrived in Beijing by train tonight.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the station included Gu Zhuoxin and Wang Guangwei, vice chairmen of the State Planning Commission; Zhang Daowu, vice minister of the Ministry of Light Industry; Wang Chun, director of the Beijing Municipal Planning Commission; Li Qiangfen, deputy director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and heads of relevant units. Dorji, Commercial Counselor of the Mongolian People's Republic's Embassy in China, also welcomed them at the station.

### 蒙古经济考察团到京

1959-10-15
第5版()
专栏：

　　蒙古经济考察团到京
    新华社14日讯　蒙古人民共和国经济考察团在蒙古人民共和国国家计划委员会第一副主席图门巴雅尔·阿拉噶查率领下，今晚乘火车到达北京。
    到车站欢迎的有国家计划委员会副主任顾卓新、王光伟，轻工业部副部长张道吾，北京市计划委员会主任王纯，外交部第二亚洲司副司长李强奋，以及各有关单位负责人。蒙古人民共和国驻华大使馆商务参赞道尔吉等也到车站欢迎。
