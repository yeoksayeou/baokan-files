Title: Soviet National Football Team Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-07
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 6th: The Soviet National Football Team, which was invited to visit and compete in our country and participate in the National Day celebrations for the tenth anniversary of the founding of our country, returned home by plane from Beijing today.
    During their stay in Beijing, the football team played friendly matches with the Chinese National Football Team and the second team of the Hungarian National Football Team, winning 1-0 and drawing 1-1 respectively. Their superb skills left a deep impression on more than 100,000 Chinese spectators.

### 苏联国家足球队回国

1959-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联国家足球队回国
    新华社6日讯　应邀来我国访问比赛和参加我国建国十周年观礼的苏联国家足球队，今天乘飞机离北京回国。
    足球队在北京期间，先后同我国国家足球队和匈牙利国家足球队第二队进行了友谊比赛，分别以一比○获胜和以一比一踢成平局。他们的高超技术给十多万中国观众留下了深刻的印象。
