Title: Korean Federation of Trade Unions Condemns Franco's Persecution of Patriots
Author:
Date: 1959-10-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 17th: The Central Committee of the Korean Federation of Trade Unions today wrote to Franco, strongly condemning the Spanish government's recent criminal acts of arresting and persecuting Spanish patriots such as Montero. The letter stated that persecuting the patriot Montero, as well as the working people and democrats, on the charge of "rebellion" is a blatant violation of human rights.

The letter strongly demanded that Franco immediately cease such repressive measures and release Montero and the many other arrested Spanish working people.

### 朝鲜职业总同盟  谴责佛朗哥迫害爱国者

1959-10-19
第6版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜职业总同盟
    谴责佛朗哥迫害爱国者
    据新华社平壤17日电　朝鲜职业总同盟中央委员会今天写信给佛朗哥，强烈谴责西班牙政府最近逮捕和迫害西班牙爱国者蒙特罗等人的犯罪行为。信中说，对爱国者蒙特罗以及劳动人民和民主主义者加上“叛乱”的罪名而进行迫害，是粗暴地侵犯人权的行为。
    信中强烈要求佛朗哥立即停止这种镇压手段，并释放蒙特罗等许多被捕的西班牙劳动人民。
