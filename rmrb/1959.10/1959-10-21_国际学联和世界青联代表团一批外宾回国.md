Title: Batch of Foreign Guests from International Student Union and World Federation of Youth Representatives Return Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 20th: A group of members of the International Student Union delegation who were invited to visit China and participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the country have completed their visit to China and are returning to their countries by plane from Beijing this morning. The members include: Mamadou Barry, Vice President of the International Student Union; Conrado, President of the National Student Union of Brazil; Ahmadi, former President of the Student Union of Khartoum University, Sudan; Thiané, representative of the West African Student Union; Mansouri Mohamed, Secretary of the National Student Union of Morocco; and Alvarez, representative of the National Student Union of Venezuela and Secretary of the International Student Union.

Mehdi Abdul Karim, Chairman of the General Federation of Students of the Republic of Iraq, a member of the delegation, returned to his country on the 18th. Nuri Abdul Razzaq Hussein, Secretary General of the Iraqi Democratic Youth Federation, a member of the World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation, also returned with Karim. Another member of the World Federation of Youth, Mazhika Aimee, returned to her country on the 17th.

### 国际学联和世界青联代表团一批外宾回国

1959-10-21
第5版()
专栏：

    国际学联和世界青联代表团一批外宾回国
    新华社20日讯　应邀来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的国际学生联合会代表团团员：国际学联副主席马马杜·巴里，巴西全国学生联合会主席康拉多，苏丹喀土穆大学学生会前主席艾哈迈迪，西非学生总联合会代表第亚尼，摩洛哥全国学生联合会书记曼苏里·穆罕默德，委内瑞拉全国学联代表、国际学联书记阿耳瓦雷斯，结束了在我国的参观访问后，在今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    代表团团员、伊拉克共和国学生总联合会主席麦赫迪·阿卜杜勒·卡里姆，已经在18日回国。和卡里姆一起回国的，还有世界民主青年联盟代表团团员、伊拉克民主青年联合会总书记努里·阿卜杜勒·拉扎克·侯赛因。世界青联的另一名团员玛齐卡·艾梅，已经在17日回国。
