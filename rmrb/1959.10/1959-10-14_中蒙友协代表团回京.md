Title: Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 12th — The Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association delegation of four members, led by Mao Qihua, Vice President of the Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association, returned to Beijing by train this afternoon after visiting the Mongolian People's Republic to participate in Sino-Mongolian friendship activities at the invitation of the Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Association.

### 中蒙友协代表团回京

1959-10-14
第5版()
专栏：

    中蒙友协代表团回京
    新华社12日讯　应蒙中友好协会邀请到蒙古人民共和国参加蒙中友好活动的中蒙友好协会代表团一行四人，在团长、中蒙友好协会副会长毛齐华率领下，今天下午乘火车回到北京。
