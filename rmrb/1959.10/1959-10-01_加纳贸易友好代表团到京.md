Title: Ghana Trade Friendship Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 7

    Ghana Trade Friendship Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, October 30th: An eight-member Ghana Trade Friendship Delegation, led by Ghana's Minister of Trade and Industry, Kojo Botsio, arrived in Beijing by plane today.
    Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Lu Xuzhang and others were at the airport to welcome them.

### 加纳贸易友好代表团到京

1959-10-01
第7版()
专栏：

    加纳贸易友好代表团到京
    新华社30日讯　加纳贸易友好代表团一行八人，在加纳贸易和工业部长库埃多率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有对外贸易部副部长卢绪章等人。
