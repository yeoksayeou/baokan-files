Title: Japanese National Relief Association Holds Congress; Our Relief Federation and Other Organizations Send Congratulatory Telegrams
Author:
Date: 1959-10-31
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, October 30th. The China People's Relief Association, the China Peace Committee, and the All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent telegrams to the Japanese National Relief Association, congratulating the Relief Association on the convocation of its Fourteenth Congress.
    The telegrams said: Over the years, your association has done a great deal of work in the humanitarian field. We believe that with your association's continued efforts in the future, your association will surely achieve greater success, and your association's cause will surely develop day by day, making greater contributions to safeguarding justice and human rights, and to striving for Japan's democracy, freedom, independence, and peace. We wish the congress a success.

### 日本国民救援会召开大会  我救济总会等团体致电祝贺

1959-10-31
第5版()
专栏：

    日本国民救援会召开大会
    我救济总会等团体致电祝贺
    新华社30日讯　中国人民救济总会、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和中华全国总工会打电报给日本国民救援会，祝贺救援会召开第十四次大会。
    电文说：多年来，贵会在人道主义方面作了不少工作。相信今后在贵会继续努力下，贵会必将取得更大成就，贵会事业必将日益发展，为维护正义和人权，为争取日本的民主、自由、独立与和平做出更大的贡献。预祝大会成功。
