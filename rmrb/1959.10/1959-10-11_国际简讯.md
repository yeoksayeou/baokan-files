	International Briefs

	Kassem in Good Health
	Baghdad dispatch: Prime Minister Kassem appeared twice on television here on the evening of the 9th. His health is good.
	It is reported that Prime Minister Kassem holds cabinet meetings in the hospital every day.
	A health bulletin on Kassem broadcast by Baghdad Radio on the evening of the 9th stated that he has been able to get up and walk back and forth in his ward.

	Editor of Cambodian "People's Daily" Assassinated
	Phnom Penh dispatch: According to a report in the "People's Daily" on the 10th, the editor of the Cambodian People's Party organ, "People's Daily," Norodom Phurissara, was assassinated on the evening of the 9th.
	The newspaper said that Norodom Phurissara was shot near the "Wat Langka" temple shortly after leaving the office at 22:00. The newspaper also said that personnel from the National Security immediately sent the seriously injured Norodom Phurissara to the hospital, and the murderer escaped.

	Pakistani President to Visit Iran
	Karachi dispatch: According to a report in the "Dawn" newspaper on the 9th, Pakistani President Ayub Khan has accepted an invitation from the Shah of Iran, Reza Pahlavi, to visit Iran.

### 国际简讯

1959-10-11
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

　　国际简讯
    卡塞姆健康情况良好
    巴格达讯　卡塞姆总理在9日晚间两次在这里的电视中出现。他的健康情况良好。
    据悉，卡塞姆总理每天在医院里召开内阁会议。
    巴格达电台9日晚上广播的卡塞姆健康情况公报说，他已经能够起来在病房中来回走动。
    柬“人民报”社长遇刺
    金边讯　据“人民报”10日报道，柬埔寨人民派机关报“人民报”的社长诺波芬在9日晚上遇刺。
    这家报纸说，诺波芬在二十二时刚离开办公室之后在“瓦特·拉因卡”寺院附近遭到射击。这家报纸又说，国家保安处的人员立即把受了重伤的诺波芬送进了医院，凶手逃跑了。
    巴总统将访伊朗
    卡拉奇讯　据“黎明报”9日报道，巴基斯坦总统阿尤布汗已经接受伊朗国王巴列维的邀请，打算前往伊朗访问。
