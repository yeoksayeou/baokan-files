Title: Comrade Mao Zedong and Comrade Khrushchev Hold Talks; Comrade Mao Zedong and Others Host Banquet for All Members of the Soviet Party and Government Delegation Headed by Comrade Khrushchev
Author:
Date: 1959-10-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd — Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, this afternoon, held talks with First Secretary N.S. Khrushchev of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

On the Soviet side, those who participated in the talks were Suslov, Member of the Presidium and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Gromyko, Member the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Antonov, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China.

On the Chinese side, those who participated in the talks were Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, and Lin Biao, Vice-Chairmen of the CPC Central Committee, Peng Zhen and Chen Yi, Members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee.

The talks were conducted in a cordial and friendly atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd — Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and other leaders of the Party and the state hosted a banquet this evening to welcome all members of the Soviet Party and Government delegation headed by N.S. Khrushchev , First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union.

On the Soviet side, the attendees included all members of the Soviet Party and Government Delegation: Suslov, Member of the Presidium and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Gromyko , Member the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Tatyщев , Alternate Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Deputy Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Shelepin , Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Soviet Union and Chairman of the State Security Committee of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Nikolayeva, Secretary of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, Chervonenko, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Ukraine, Arkhipov, Deputy Chairman of the State Committee for Foreign Economic Relations of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Tupolev, Chief Aircraft Designer of the Soviet Union, Andropov, Minister of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and Antonov, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China.

On the Chinese side, those present included Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, and Lin Biao, Vice-Chairmen of the CPC Central Committee, Song Qingling and Dong Biwu, Vice-Chairmen of the People's Republic of China, Peng Zhen and Chen Yi, Members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretary. ### 毛泽东同志和赫鲁晓夫同志举行会谈  毛泽东同志等欢宴以赫鲁晓夫同志为首的苏联党政代表团全体成员

1959-10-03
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东同志和赫鲁晓夫同志举行会谈
    毛泽东同志等欢宴以赫鲁晓夫同志为首的苏联党政代表团全体成员
    新华社2日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志今天下午同苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫同志，举行了会谈。
    苏联方面参加会谈的，有苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员、书记处书记苏斯洛夫，苏联共产党中央委员会委员、苏联外交部部长葛罗米柯，苏联驻中国大使馆临时代办安东诺夫。
    中国方面参加会谈的，有中共中央副主席刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、林彪，中共中央政治局委员彭真、陈毅，中共中央书记处书记王稼祥。
    会谈是在亲切友好的气氛中进行的。
    新华社2日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志和党和国家的其他领导人在今天晚间设宴欢迎以苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记、苏联部长会议主席尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫同志为首的苏联党政代表团全体成员。
    苏联方面出席的，有苏联党政代表团全体成员：苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员、书记处书记苏斯洛夫，苏联共产党中央委员会委员、苏联外交部部长葛罗米柯，苏联共产党中央委员会候补委员、苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席塔谢涅夫，苏联共产党中央委员会委员、苏联部长会议国家安全委员会主席谢列平，全苏工会中央理事会书记尼古拉耶娃，乌克兰共产党中央委员会书记契尔沃年科，苏联部长会议国家对外经济关系委员会副主席阿希波夫，苏联飞机总设计师图波列夫，苏联共产党中央委员会部长安德罗波夫，以及苏联驻中国大使馆临时代办安东诺夫。
    中国方面出席的，有中共中央副主席刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、林彪，中华人民共和国副主席宋庆龄、董必武，中共中央政治局委员彭真、陈毅，中共中央书记处书记王稼祥、候补书记杨尚昆。（附图片）
