Title: Indonesian Circles Prepare to Hold Large-Scale Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1959-10-13
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, October 12th:
　　An unprecedented large-scale peace conference in Indonesia—the All-Indonesia Peace Consultative Conference—is scheduled to be held in December this year. The preparatory committee for the conference was established here yesterday.
　　In addition to discussing the Indonesian peace movement, the conference will also discuss some issues related to world peace.
　　The initiators of the conference include Maradi, Deputy Minister of Information of the Indonesian government, Sudiro, Mayor of Jakarta, the wife of Foreign Minister Subandrio, Sumantri, President of Padjadjaran University, and other social figures. Representatives from all levels of Indonesian society will participate in the conference.
　　Among the thirteen chairmen of the preparatory committee are Indonesian Nationalist Party leader Mansjur, Indonesian Communist Party leader Yono, Islamic leaders Anwar Tjokroaminoto and Abbas, and Christian leader Rumambi.

### 印度尼西亚各界人士筹备举行大规模和平会议

1959-10-13
第6版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚各界人士
    筹备举行大规模和平会议
    新华社雅加达12日电　印度尼西亚空前大规模的和平会议——全印度尼西亚和平协商会议预定在今年12月召开。会议的筹备委员会昨天在这里成立。
    这次会议，除了将讨论印度尼西亚和平运动的问题，还将讨论有关世界和平的一些问题。
    这次会议的倡议者有印度尼西亚政府情报部副部长马拉迪、雅加达市长苏迪罗、外交部长苏班德里约的夫人、国立巴查查兰大学校长苏曼特里和其它社会名流。印度尼西亚全国各阶层人民的代表将参加这次会议。
    在筹备委员会的十三位主席中，有印度尼西亚国民党领袖曼休尔、印度尼西亚共产党领袖约诺、伊斯兰教领袖安瓦尔·佐克罗阿米诺托和阿巴斯、基督教领袖鲁曼比等。
