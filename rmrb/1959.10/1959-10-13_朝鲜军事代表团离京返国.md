Title: Korean Military Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1959-10-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 12th - The Korean Military Delegation, headed by Vice-Chief of Delegation General Choi Yong-jin, left Beijing by train this morning to return home.

During its stay in China, the Korean Military Delegation, accompanied by Vice Minister of National Defense General Wang Shusheng, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau Colonel Zhang Bingyu, and our military attaché to Korea Colonel Zhang Leting, visited Shanghai, Guangzhou, Wuhan, and other places.

Among those who came to the station to see the guests off today were senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army including Vice Ministers of National Defense General Tan Zheng, General Wang Shusheng, General Yang Chengwu, General Xiao Hua, General Gan Siqi, General Yang Yong, Air Force General Liu Zhen, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, and Naval Lieutenant General Luo Shunchu.

Korean Ambassador to China Li Yong-ho and military attaches from various countries to China also came to the station to see them off. (Attached Picture)

The Korean Military Delegation, headed by Vice-Chief of Delegation General Choi Yong-jin (second from the right), left Beijing by train on October 12 to return home. General Tan Zheng (third from the right), Vice Minister of National Defense, went to the station to see them off. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zou Jiandong.

### 朝鲜军事代表团离京返国

1959-10-13
第5版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜军事代表团离京返国
    新华社12日讯　以副团长崔勇进上将为首的朝鲜军事代表团，今天上午乘火车离开北京回国。
    朝鲜军事代表团在中国期间，由我国防部副部长王树声大将、外事处副处长张秉玉上校和我国驻朝鲜武官张乐亭上校陪同，曾在上海、广州、武汉、等地参观访问。
    今天到车站欢送客人的，有国防部副部长谭政大将、王树声大将，杨成武上将、萧华上将、甘泗淇上将、杨勇上将、刘震空军上将，萧向荣中将、罗舜初海军中将等中国人民解放军高级将领。
    朝鲜驻华大使李永镐和各国驻华武官也到车站欢送。（附图片）
    以副团长崔勇进上将（右第二人）为首的朝鲜军事代表团，10月12日乘火车离开北京回国，国防部副部长谭政大将（右第三人）前往车站欢送。　　　新华社记者　邹健东摄
