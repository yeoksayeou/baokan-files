Title: China's Foreign Cultural Association Holds Reception for Cultural Delegations from Thirteen Countries
Author:
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - The President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, Chu Tunan, and Vice-President Yang Hansheng, held a reception at noon today to entertain cultural friendship delegations from thirteen countries in Northwest Europe, Asia, and Africa. They were all invited by the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries to come to our country to participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Friends from Finland, Sweden, Greece, Denmark, Norway, New Zealand, Ceylon, Japan, Israel, France, Italy, Belgium, the Netherlands and other countries gathered with cultural and artistic figures from the capital, engaging in cordial conversations and toasting to the further development of common friendship and the cause of world peace.

Chu Tunan spoke at the reception, extending a warm welcome and heartfelt gratitude to the friends who had come from afar. After describing the great achievements made in various fields and the improvement of people's lives in China under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party over the past ten years since the founding of the People's Republic of China, he said that the Chinese people have always actively advocated peaceful coexistence and friendly exchanges between people of all countries, supported the efforts of people of all countries to strive for national independence and democratic freedom, and opposed colonial oppression, acts of aggression, and war conspiracies. He said, I believe that through your visit to China this time, you will definitely make beneficial contributions to the friendship, mutual understanding, and cultural ties between the Chinese people and the people of all countries in the world.

The reception proceeded in a joyful and friendly atmosphere from beginning to end.

### 我对外文协举行酒会  招待十三国文化代表团

1959-10-01
第7版()
专栏：

    我对外文协举行酒会
    招待十三国文化代表团
    新华社30日讯　中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南和副会长阳翰笙今天中午举行酒会，招待来自西北欧和亚非地区十三个国家的文化友好代表团。他们都是应中国人民对外文化协会的邀请前来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的。
    来自芬兰、瑞典、希腊、丹麦、挪威、新西兰、锡兰、日本、以色列、法国、意大利、比利时、荷兰等国的朋友们，同首都的文化艺术界人士欢聚一堂，亲切交谈，互相为共同的友谊和世界和平事业的进一步发展干杯。
    楚图南在酒会上讲话，向远道来中国的朋友们表示热烈的欢迎和衷心的感谢。他在叙述了新中国建国十年来在中国共产党领导下各方面所取得的巨大成就和人民生活的改善情况以后说，中国人民一贯积极主张各国人民之间的和平共处和友好往来，支持各国人民争取民族独立和民主自由的努力，反对殖民压迫、侵略行为和战争阴谋。他说，我相信，通过你们这次到中国的访问，一定会对中国人民和世界各国人民的友谊、相互了解和文化联系作出有益的贡献。
    酒会自始至终在欢乐、友好的气氛中进行。
