Title: World Peace Council Delegation Leader Bernal Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. John Desmond Bernal, head of the World Peace Council delegation, Lenin International Peace Prize laureate, Executive Chairman of the Presidium of the World Peace Council, and British physicist, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. He was invited to China to participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Those who went to the airport to welcome him were Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Li Siguang and Wu Youxun, Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Qu Tangliang, Secretary-General of the China Peace Committee, and Zheng Senyu, Secretary of the Chinese Delegation to the World Peace Council.

### 世界和理会代表团团长贝尔纳到京

1959-10-01
第7版()
专栏：

    世界和理会代表团团长贝尔纳到京
    新华社30日讯　世界和平理事会代表团团长、列宁国际和平奖金获得者、世界和平理事会主席团执行主席、英国物理学家约翰·德斯豪德·贝尔纳，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。他是应邀来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的。
    前往机场欢迎的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席、中国科学院院长郭沫若，中国科学院副院长李四光、吴有训，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长区棠亮，中国驻世界和平理事会书记处书记郑森禹等。
