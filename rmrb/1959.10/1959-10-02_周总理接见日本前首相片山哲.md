Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Former Japanese Prime Minister Tetsu Katayama
Author:
Date: 1959-10-02
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, October 1st: Premier Zhou Enlai received tonight the head of the Japanese delegation from all walks of life celebrating the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, former Japanese Prime Minister, President of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, and Member of the House of Representatives, Tetsu Katayama, and deputy heads: Tadaharu Obata, Chairman of the Kansai Branch of the National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations, Bon Shiraishi, Advisor to the Editorial Committee of Asahi Shimbun, and Masato Honda, Vice Chairman of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan.

### 周总理接见日本前首相片山哲

1959-10-02
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理接见日本前首相片山哲
    新华社1日讯　周恩来总理今晚接见了日本各界庆祝中华人民共和国建国十周年代表团团长、日本前首相、日中文化交流协会会长、众议院议员片山哲和副团长：恢复日中邦交国民会议关西分会会长小畑忠良、朝日新闻社论委员会顾问白石凡、日本工会总评议会副议长本多正登。
