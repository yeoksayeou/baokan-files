Title: Czech and Bulgarian Basketball Teams Depart for Visits to Shanghai and Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1959-10-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The national men's basketball teams of Czechoslovakia and Bulgaria left Beijing on the 7th and 8th respectively for visits and friendly matches in Shanghai and Tianjin.

The two teams will also play fifteen friendly matches with ten Chinese basketball teams in our country.

### 捷、保篮球队分赴上海天津访问

1959-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    捷、保篮球队分赴上海天津访问
    据新华社8日讯　捷克斯洛伐克和保加利亚的国家男子篮球队已经先后在7日和8日离开北京，分别到上海和天津访问比赛。
    两个队今后还要在我国同我国的十个篮球队进行十五场友谊比赛。
