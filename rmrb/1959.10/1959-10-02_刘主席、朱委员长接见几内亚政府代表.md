Title: Chairman Liu and Chairman Zhu Receive Guinean Government Representatives
Author: 
Date: 1959-10-02
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Chairman Zhu De received Bari Diawandou, the representative of the Government of the Republic of Guinea and the Minister of Education of Guinea, tonight.

### 刘主席、朱委员长接见几内亚政府代表

1959-10-02
第3版()
专栏：

    刘主席、朱委员长接见几内亚政府代表
    新华社1日讯　刘少奇主席、朱德委员长今晚接见了几内亚共和国政府代表、几内亚教育部部长巴里·迪亚万杜。
