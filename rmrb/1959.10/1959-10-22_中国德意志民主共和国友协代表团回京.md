Title: GDR-China Friendship Association Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-22
Page: 5

    GDR-China Friendship Association Delegation Returns to Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, October 21st. The five-member delegation of the China-German Democratic Republic Friendship Association, led by Gao Kelin, Vice President of the Friendship Association, returned to Beijing by plane today after being invited by the German Democratic Republic to participate in the celebrations of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and the 10th anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic.

### 中国德意志民主共和国友协代表团回京

1959-10-22
第5版()
专栏：

    中国德意志民主共和国友协代表团回京
    新华社21日讯　应德意志民主共和国邀请，赴德参加中华人民共和国成立十周年和德意志民主共和国成立十周年庆祝活动的中华人民共和国德意志民主共和国友好协会代表团一行五人，在友协副会长高克林率领下，今天乘飞机回到北京。
