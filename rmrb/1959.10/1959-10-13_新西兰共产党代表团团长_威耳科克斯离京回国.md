Title: New Zealand Communist Party Delegation Leader Wilcox Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:
Date: 1959-10-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 12th -- Comrade Vic Wilcox, head of the New Zealand Communist Party delegation and General Secretary of the National Committee of the New Zealand Communist Party, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home after attending the celebrations for the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and visiting various parts of our country.

Those who went to the airport to see him off included Tan Zhenlin, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and Wu Xiuquan, member of the Central Committee.

Two members of the delegation left Beijing today for Wuhan to visit. They will continue to visit our country before returning home.

### 新西兰共产党代表团团长  威耳科克斯离京回国

1959-10-13
第5版()
专栏：

    新西兰共产党代表团团长
    威耳科克斯离京回国
    新华社12日讯　新西兰共产党代表团团长、新西兰共产党全国委员会总书记维克·威耳科克斯同志，在参加了中华人民共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼和在我国各地参观访问之后，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的，有中共中央政治局委员谭震林，中央委员伍修权。
    代表团的两位团员，今天离开北京去武汉访问，他们将在我国继续参观访问之后回国。
