Title: President of the Chilean Journalists Association Writes Highly Praising the Great Achievements of the Chinese People
Author:
Date: 1959-10-21
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Santiago, October 19th. Juan Emilio Pacull, President of the Chilean Journalists Association, who returned after attending the celebrations for the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, published an article in the Chilean "Horizon" newspaper today, praising the great achievements of the Chinese people. Pacull's article about his impressions of his visit to China occupied two pages in the "Horizon" newspaper.
    
    Speaking of the People's Communes, Pacull said that China's People's Commune movement is "to eliminate poverty and to build a great, respectable socialist country." He emphasized that "the entire China is going all out in heroic labor." He believes that China will surpass Britain economically in a short period of time.

### 智利新闻工作者协会主席撰文  盛赞中国人民的伟大成就

1959-10-21
第5版()
专栏：

    智利新闻工作者协会主席撰文
    盛赞中国人民的伟大成就
    新华社圣地亚哥19日电　参加中华人民共和国建国十周年庆祝典礼后回国的智利新闻工作者协会主席胡安·埃米略·帕库耳今天在智利“视野报”上发表文章，盛赞中国人民的伟大成就。帕库耳的这篇谈他访华观感的文章在“视野报”上占了两版的篇幅。
    帕库耳在谈到人民公社时说，中国的人民公社运动是“为了消灭贫困，为了建立一个伟大的受人尊敬的社会主义国家”。他强调指出，“整个中国都在全力以赴地进行着英雄的劳动”。他认为中国将在不长的时间内在经济上超过英国。
