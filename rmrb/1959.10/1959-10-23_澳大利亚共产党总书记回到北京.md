Title: General Secretary of the Communist Party of Australia Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd: Comrade Lance Louis Sharkey, head of the delegation of the Communist Party of Australia and General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Australia, and Mrs. Sharkey, a member of the delegation, returned to Beijing this afternoon after visiting other places. The distinguished guests visited Nanjing, Shanghai, Wuhan, Zhengzhou and other cities.

### 澳大利亚共产党总书记回到北京

1959-10-23
第5版()
专栏：

    澳大利亚共产党总书记回到北京
    新华社22日讯　澳大利亚共产党代表团团长、澳大利亚共产党中央委员会总书记兰斯·路易斯·夏基同志，代表团团员夏基夫人，在外地参观访问之后，于今天下午回到北京。贵宾们曾参观访问了南京、上海、武汉、郑州等城市。
