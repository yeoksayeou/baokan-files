Title: Beautiful Melodies Strike a Chord: Dresden Symphony Orchestra Performs Again
Author:
Date: 1959-10-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 9th: The musicians of the Dresden Symphony Orchestra of the German Democratic Republic held their second concert in Beijing tonight. The beautiful melodies and moving sounds once again captivated the hearts of more than a thousand Beijing audience members.

The first piece played by the musicians from the German Democratic Republic tonight was Ma Sicong's "Song of the Forest." After this beautiful piece was finished, Ma Sicong stepped onto the stage amidst the thunderous applause that resounded throughout the hall, to thank the musicians from the German Democratic Republic for their outstanding performance. The German musicians presented Ma Sicong with a large bouquet of flowers. Next, they performed Mozart's Sinfonia Concertante. Chinese and German musicians also collaborated on the "Piano Concerto No. 3 in C Minor." The last program of the concert was Beethoven's "Symphony No. 6 in F Major, Pastoral." After this successful concert ended, Li Delun, conductor of the Central Philharmonic Orchestra, presented a bouquet of flowers to Siegfried Geißler, conductor of the Dresden Symphony Orchestra. Literary and art workers from the capital presented flower baskets to the artists from the German Democratic Republic, thanking them for their performance.

### 美妙旋律扣人心弦  德累斯顿交响乐团再次演奏

1959-10-10
第5版()
专栏：

    美妙旋律扣人心弦
    德累斯顿交响乐团再次演奏
    据新华社9日讯　德意志民主共和国德累斯顿交响乐团的音乐家们，今晚在北京举行了第二次演奏会，那美妙的旋律和动人的音响，再一次扣住了一千多名北京观众的心弦。
    今晚德意志民主共和国音乐家们演奏的第一支乐曲是马思聪的“山林之歌”。当这个动听的乐曲演奏完毕以后，马思聪在响彻着全场的掌声中登上舞台，感谢德意志民主共和国音乐家们卓越的演奏，德国的音乐家们把一大束鲜花送给了马思聪。接着，演奏了莫扎特的交响协奏曲。中德两国音乐家并合作演奏了“C小调第三钢琴协奏曲”。演奏会的最后一个节目是贝多芬的“F大调第六田园交响乐”。当这次成功的演奏会结束以后，中央乐团的指挥李德伦把一束鲜花赠给德累斯顿交响音乐团的指挥西夫里特·盖斯勒，首都的文艺工作者向德意志民主共和国的艺术家们送了花篮，感谢他们的演奏。
