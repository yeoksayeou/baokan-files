Title: Mexican Communist Party Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 30th—The delegation of the Mexican Communist Party, invited by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to visit our country and participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

### 墨西哥共产党代表团到京

1959-10-01
第6版()
专栏：

    墨西哥共产党代表团到京
    新华社30日讯　应中国共产党中央委员会邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的墨西哥共产党代表团，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
