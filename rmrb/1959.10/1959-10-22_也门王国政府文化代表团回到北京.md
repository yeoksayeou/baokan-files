Title: Yemen Kingdom Government Cultural Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 21st: The Yemen Kingdom Government Cultural Delegation, headed by Yemen Kingdom Minister of State Sayyid Ali Mu'ayyad, ended its visit to Shenyang and returned to Beijing by plane today.

The Yemen Kingdom Government Cultural Delegation went to Shenyang from Beijing on the 17th. Qiu Youwen, Deputy Governor of Liaoning Province, held a banquet that evening to welcome the guests. During their stay in Shenyang, the delegation visited and toured the Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Plant, the Cable Factory, the Workers' Village, and the Liaoning Exhibition Hall. They also visited Anshan and Fushun, including the steelmaking plant, iron smelting plant, and large rolling mill of Anshan Iron and Steel, as well as small blast furnaces. In Fushun, they visited the open-pit coal mine, the No. 1 Petroleum Plant, and the Dahuofang Reservoir.

### 也门王国政府文化代表团回到北京

1959-10-22
第5版()
专栏：

    也门王国政府文化代表团回到北京
    新华社21日讯　以也门王国国务大臣赛义德·阿里·穆安亚德为首的也门王国政府文化代表团结束了在沈阳的参观访问，今天乘飞机回到北京。
    也门王国政府文化代表团是17日从北京去沈阳的。辽宁省副省长仇友文曾在当晚举行宴会欢迎客人。代表团在沈阳期间，参观访问了沈阳第一机床厂、电缆厂、工人村和辽宁展览馆，还去鞍山、抚顺参观了鞍钢的炼钢厂、炼铁厂、大型轧钢厂等和小高炉，在抚顺参观了露天煤矿、石油一厂和大伙房水库。
