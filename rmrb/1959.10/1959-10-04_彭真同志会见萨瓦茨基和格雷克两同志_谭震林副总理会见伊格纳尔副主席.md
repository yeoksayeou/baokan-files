Title: Comrade Peng Zhen Meets Comrades Zawadzki and Gierek, Vice Premier Tan Zhenlin Meets Vice Chairman Ignar
Author:
Date: 1959-10-04
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd October: Comrade Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, met and hosted a banquet this evening to welcome Comrades Zawadzki and Gierek, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party.

    Present were Bo Yibo, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and Liu Lantao and Yang Shangkun, alternate secretaries of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee.
    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd October: Tan Zhenlin, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, met today with Ignar, Chairman of the Supreme Committee of the United Peasant Party of Poland and Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic.

### 彭真同志会见萨瓦茨基和格雷克两同志  谭震林副总理会见伊格纳尔副主席

1959-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

    彭真同志会见萨瓦茨基和格雷克两同志
    谭震林副总理会见伊格纳尔副主席
    新华社3日讯　中共中央政治局委员彭真同志在今天晚间会见并设宴欢迎波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局委员萨瓦茨基和格雷克两位同志。
    在座的，有中共中央政治局候补委员薄一波，中共中央书记处候补书记刘澜涛、杨尚昆。
    新华社3日讯　中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理谭震林，今天会见了波兰统一农民党最高委员会主席、波兰人民共和国部长会议副主席伊格纳尔。
