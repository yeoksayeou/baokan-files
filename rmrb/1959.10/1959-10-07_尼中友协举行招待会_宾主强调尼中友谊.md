Title: Nepal-China Friendship Association Holds Reception, Hosts Emphasize Nepal-China Friendship
Author:
Date: 1959-10-07
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, October 5th: The Nepal-China Friendship Association held a reception here in Kathmandu on the evening of the 4th to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

    Prem Bahadur Kansakar, Acting President of the Nepal-China Friendship Association, said at the meeting that the long-standing friendship between Nepal and China is being further strengthened. He emphasized, "Some people believe that the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence are dead, but we believe that these five principles are immortal and will be permanently embodied in the relations between our two countries." He said that the Nepalese delegation's visit to China this time is a symbol of the increasing unity between the people of our two countries. He believes that the friendship between Nepal and China will last forever and become increasingly stronger.

    Ambassador Pan Zili thanked the Nepal-China Friendship Association for its contribution to promoting Nepal-China friendship, and said that this friendly relationship has broad prospects because in many respects, the two countries will certainly be able to learn from each other and help each other.

### 尼中友协举行招待会  宾主强调尼中友谊

1959-10-07
第6版()
专栏：

    尼中友协举行招待会
    宾主强调尼中友谊
    据新华社新德里5日电　加德满都消息：尼中友好协会4日晚上在这里举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国建国十周年。
    尼中友好协会代理会长普雷姆·巴哈杜尔·卡乌沙卡尔在会上说，悠久的尼中友谊正在进一步加强。他强调说，“有些人认为，和平共处的五项原则已经死亡，但是我们认为，这五项原则是不朽的，并且将永久体现在我们两国的关系中。”他说，尼泊尔代表团这次去中国的访问是我们两国人民团结日益增强的象征。他认为，尼泊尔和中国的友谊将永远存在、日益加强。
    潘自力大使感谢尼中友好协会在促进尼中友谊方面所作的贡献，并且说，这种友好关系有着广阔的前途，因为在许多方面，两国一定能够相互学习和相互帮助。
