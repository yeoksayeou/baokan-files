Title: Chairman Mao, Chairman Liu and Other Leaders Pay Their Respects to Vice Chairman Li Jishen
Author:
Date: 1959-10-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th dispatch:

　　Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairmen Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, and Lin Biao of the CPC Central Committee, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China Soong Ching Ling, and member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee Peng Zhen, paid their respects to Vice Chairman Li Jishen at the Sun Yat-sen Hall in Zhongshan Park this evening.

    The mourning hall for Vice Chairman Li Jishen—the Sun Yat-sen Hall—was solemn and respectful today. The hall was filled with wreaths sent by Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling, Vice Chairman Dong Biwu, Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou, and other Party and state leaders, the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the National Political Consultative Conference, the State Council and other state leading organs, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, and other democratic parties and people's organizations. As the Party and state leaders slowly walked into the mourning hall and paid their respects to the spirit tablet of Vice Chairman Li Jishen, the orchestra played funeral music.

    Also paying respects along with the Party and state leaders were Cheng Qian, Cai Tingkai, Zhang Zhizhong, Xiong Kewu, and others, all Vice Chairmen of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang.

    Premier Chen Yi of the State Council, Vice Chairman Lin Feng of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Vice Chairmen Li Siguang and Kang Sheng of the National Political Consultative Conference, and President Xie Juezai of the Supreme People's Court, among others, paid their respects at the Sun Yat-sen Hall this morning and afternoon.

    Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Bainqen Erdini Qoigyi Gyaincain, and Vice Chairmen of the National Political Consultative Conference Pagbalha Geleg Namgyai and Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme also paid their respects at the Sun Yat-sen Hall yesterday afternoon.

    From nine o'clock in the morning to four o'clock in the afternoon today, people from all walks of life in the capital also went to the Sun Yat-sen Hall to pay their respects. Among those who paid their respects were representatives from various democratic parties, people's organizations, central and Beijing municipal organs, and various schools, as well as leaders of the Central and Beijing organizations of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang and members of the KMT in Beijing, and close friends of Mr. Li Jishen during his lifetime.

    Heads of diplomatic missions and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China also went to the Sun Yat-sen Hall this morning to pay their respects and presented wreaths. (Attached pictures)

### 毛主席刘主席等领导人吊唁李济深副委员长

1959-10-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席刘主席等领导人
    吊唁李济深副委员长
    新华社11日讯　中共中央主席毛泽东、副主席刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、林彪，中华人民共和国副主席宋庆龄，中共中央政治局委员彭真，今晚前往中山公园中山堂吊唁李济深副委员长。
    李济深副委员长的灵堂——中山堂今天庄严、肃穆。灵堂里摆着毛泽东主席、刘少奇主席、宋庆龄副主席、董必武副主席、朱德委员长、周恩来总理以及党和国家其他领导人，全国人民代表大会常务委员会、政协全国委员会、国务院以及国家其他领导机关，中国共产党中央委员会、中国国民党革命委员会以及其他各民主党派、各人民团体送的花圈。当党和国家领导人徐步走进灵堂并向李济深副委员长灵位致哀时，乐队奏起了哀乐。
    同党和国家领导人一起吊唁的，还有中国国民党革命委员会副主席程潜、蔡廷锴、张治中、熊克武等。
    今天上午和下午前往中山堂吊唁的，有国务院副总理陈毅，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林枫，政协全国委员会副主席李四光、康生，最高人民法院院长谢觉哉等。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞，政协全国委员会副主席帕巴拉·卓列朗杰，阿沛·阿旺晋美昨天下午也曾到中山堂吊唁。
    今天从上午九时起，到下午四时止，首都各界人士也都纷纷前往中山堂吊唁。吊唁的有各民主党派、各人民团体、中央和北京市各机关、各学校的代表，中国国民党革命委员会中央和北京市组织的负责人和民革在京成员，李济深先生生前挚友等。
    各国驻华使节和外交官员今天上午也前往中山堂吊唁，并赠送花圈。（附图片）
