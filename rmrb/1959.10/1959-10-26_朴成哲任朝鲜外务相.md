Title: Park Sung-chul Appointed as Foreign Minister of North Korea
Author:
Date: 1959-10-26
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 25th: The Pyongyang newspapers reported today that the Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea issued a decree on the 23rd, removing Nam Il from his concurrent post as Foreign Minister, while he remains Deputy Prime Minister of the Cabinet, and appointed Park Sung-chul as Foreign Minister.

### 朴成哲任朝鲜外务相

1959-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    朴成哲任朝鲜外务相
    新华社平壤25日电　平壤报纸今天报道，朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会23日发表政令，免去朝鲜内阁副首相兼外务相南日兼任的外务相的职务，任命朴成哲为外务相。
