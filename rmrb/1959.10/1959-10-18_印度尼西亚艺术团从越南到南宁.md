Title: Indonesian Art Troupe Arrives in Nanning from Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1959-10-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, 17th -- The Indonesian Art Troupe concluded its visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and returned to Nanning from Vietnam by special train this morning to continue its performance tour in our country.

　　Guo Ming, Chairman of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Federation of Literary and Art Circles, Man Qianzi, Chairman of the Guangxi Branch of the Chinese Musicians Association, and others welcomed them at the station.

　　Nguyen Mai, Acting Consul of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Nanning, also welcomed them at the station.

### 印度尼西亚艺术团从越南到南宁

1959-10-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚艺术团从越南到南宁
    新华社南宁17日电　印度尼西亚艺术团结束了在越南民主共和国的访问，今天上午从越南乘专车回到南宁，继续在我国访问演出。
    到车站欢迎的有广西僮族自治区文学艺术界联合会主席郭铭、中国音乐家协会广西分会主席满谦子等。
    越南民主共和国驻南宁代领事阮梅也到车站欢迎。
