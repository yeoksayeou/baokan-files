Title: International Association of Democratic Lawyers President Pritt Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-30
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, October 29th — The President of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, D.N. Pritt, and his wife left Beijing by plane this morning to return home. Those who saw them off at the airport included Wu Defeng, Vice President of the China Political Science and Law Association, and Yao Zhongming, Secretary of the Secretariat.

### 国际民主法协主席普里特回国

1959-10-30
第5版()
专栏：

    国际民主法协主席普里特回国
    新华社29日讯　国际民主法律工作者协会主席丹·诺·普里特和他的夫人今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。到机场送行的有中国政治法律学会副会长吴德峰、书记处书记姚仲明等。
