Title: Afghan Government Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, October 3rd. The government delegation of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, headed by Ghulam Mohammad Sherzad, Minister of Commerce of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.

Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs, went to the airport to see the distinguished guests off.

After the band played the national anthems of Afghanistan and China, the head of the delegation, Ghulam Mohammad Sherzad, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi, reviewed the guard of honor. Children from the capital presented flowers to the distinguished guests.

Also present at the airport to see the distinguished guests off today were Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade; Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; General Yang Chengwu, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; heads of various government ministries; senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; and heads of various democratic parties and people's organizations.

Diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China also went to the airport to see the distinguished guests off.

On the 2nd, Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, met with the government delegation of the Kingdom of Afghanistan headed by Ghulam Mohammad Sherzad, and hosted a luncheon for the distinguished guests.

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 3rd. The government delegation of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, headed by Ghulam Mohammad Sherzad, Minister of Commerce of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, arrived in Guangzhou by plane this afternoon from Beijing after attending the celebrations for the 10th anniversary of the founding of our country in Beijing.

Wei Jinfei, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, Yang Yi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Provincial People's Committee, and Zhang Ruiquan, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou, went to the airport to welcome them.

### 阿富汗政府代表团回国

1959-10-04
第2版()
专栏：

    阿富汗政府代表团回国
    新华社3日讯　以阿富汗王国商业大臣吴拉姆·穆罕默德·舍尔扎德为首的阿富汗王国政府代表团，今天上午离开北京，乘飞机回国。
    国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅到机场欢送贵宾。
    乐队奏阿富汗和中国两国国歌后，代表团团长吴拉姆·穆罕默德·舍尔扎德由陈毅副总理陪同检阅了仪仗队。首都儿童向贵宾献了鲜花。
    今天到机场欢送贵宾的还有对外贸易部部长叶季壮、文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长章汉夫。中国人民解放军副总参谋长杨成武上将以及政府各部负责人，中国人民解放军高级将领，各民主党派和人民团体的负责人。
    各国驻华使节和外交官员也到机场欢送贵宾。
    2日，对外贸易部部长叶季壮会见了吴拉姆·穆罕默德·舍尔扎德为首的阿富汗王国政府代表团，并设午宴招待贵宾。
    新华社广州3日电　阿富汗王国商业大臣吴拉姆·穆罕默德·舍尔扎德为首的阿富汗王国政府代表团，在北京参加我国建国十周年庆祝典礼后，今天下午乘飞机由北京到达广州。
    前往机场欢迎的有广东省副省长魏今非、省人民委员会副秘书长杨毅，广州市副市长张瑞权等。
