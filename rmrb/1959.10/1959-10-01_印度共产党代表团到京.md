Title: Indian Communist Party Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - The Indian Communist Party delegation, invited by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to visit China and participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the country, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning. The delegation included: Ajoy Kumar Ghosh, General Secretary of the National Committee of the Communist Party of India and head of the delegation; delegation members: Bhupesh Gupta, Member of the Central Executive Committee and Secretary of the Central Secretariat of the Communist Party of India; Parasada Rao and Romesh Chandra, Members of the Central Executive Committee; and S.V. Parulekar, Member of the Maharashtra Regional Committee of the Communist Party of India.

Welcoming them at the airport were Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee; Peng Zhen, Member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee; Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee; and Liu Lantao, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee. ### 印度共产党代表团到京

1959-10-01
第6版()
专栏：

    印度共产党代表团到京
    新华社30日讯　应中国共产党中央委员会邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的印度共产党代表团团长、印度共产党全国委员会总书记阿约艾·库马尔·高士，代表团团员：印度共产党中央执行委员会委员、中央书记处书记布佩希·古普塔，中央执行委员会委员帕拉萨达·拉奥、罗米希·浅德拉，印度共产党马哈拉施特拉地区委员会委员斯·符·帕鲁勒卡，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
    在机场欢迎的，有中共中央副主席朱德，中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记彭真，中央书记处书记王稼祥、候补书记刘澜涛。（附图片）
