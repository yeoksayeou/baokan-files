Author: Yan Ming
Date: 1959-10-29
Page: 8

    A Poem Dedicated to the Gathering of Heroes
    Yan Ming
  Ten thousand clumps of peonies, the heroes have come,
  Blooming together in Beijing,
  Ten thousand phoenixes,
  Spreading their wings in Beijing,
  Ah, heroes from all directions,
  Heroes gather in the Great Hall of the People!
  The dome of the Great Hall is wider than the universe,
  The lamps of the Great Hall are an ocean of stars,
  Ah, the brilliance of the heroes is brighter than the stars,
  Heroes gather in the Great Hall of the People!

  Sing for the heroes, O sea,
  Your colorful brush,
  Kunlun, O my paper,
  Write ten thousand poems for the heroes,
  Sun, moon, and stars all sing together!
  Heroes, marching out on horseback,
  Like the roaring waves of the sea,
  Heroes, their aura overwhelming,
  How can the towering Kunlun compare!

### 向群英会献诗
晏明
1959-10-29
第8版()
专栏：

    向群英会献诗
    晏明
  英雄们来了一万丛牡丹，在北京一齐开放，一万支凤凰，在北京展开翅膀，呵，英雄们来自四面八方，英雄们欢聚在万人会堂！大会堂的圆顶，比宇宙更宽广，大会堂的灯盏，是星星的海洋，呵，英雄的光辉比星星更明亮，英雄们欢聚在万人会堂！
  为英雄们欢唱大海呵，你的彩笔，昆仑呵，我的纸张，为英雄们写万卷诗，日月星辰一齐欢唱！英雄呵，跨马出征，像大海波涛万丈腾起，英雄呵，气势夺天，高高的昆仑怎能相比！
