Title: Chairman Liu Appoints Qiu Huizuo as Director of the General Logistics Department

Author:

Date: 1959-10-15
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, on the 14th, according to the decision of the Tenth Session of the Standing Committee of the Second National People's Congress, appointed Qiu Huizuo as Director of the General Logistics Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

### 刘主席任命丘会作为总后方勤务部长

1959-10-15
第2版()
专栏：

　　刘主席任命丘会作为总后方勤务部长
    新华社14日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇14日根据第二届全国人民代表大会常务委员会第十次会议的决定，任命丘会作为中国人民解放军总后方勤务部部长。
