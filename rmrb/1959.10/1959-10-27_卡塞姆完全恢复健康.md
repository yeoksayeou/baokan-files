Title: Kassem Fully Recovered
Author:
Date: 1959-10-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 26th -- The Iraqi Republic's Embassy in China announced today regarding Prime Minister Kassem's health that Prime Minister Kassem has fully recovered and even gained seven kilograms during his convalescence in the hospital. Currently, Prime Minister Kassem has abundant energy to handle official duties and will be discharged from the hospital within days.

The Iraqi Embassy pointed out that the reports in some foreign newspapers and foreign news agencies regarding Prime Minister Kassem's health are inaccurate.

### 卡塞姆完全恢复健康

1959-10-27
第6版()
专栏：

    卡塞姆完全恢复健康
    新华社26日讯　伊拉克共和国驻华大使馆今天发表关于卡塞姆总理的健康情况说，卡塞姆总理业已完全恢复健康，甚至在医院疗养中体重增加了七公斤。目前卡塞姆总理处理公务精力十分充沛，日内即可出院。
    伊拉克大使馆指出，近来某些外国报纸和外国新闻机构对卡塞姆总理的健康情况的报道是失实的。
