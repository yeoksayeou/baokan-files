Title: Voitec Says at Bucharest Celebration Rally that Romania Rejoices in China's Victory
Author:
Date: 1959-10-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, September 30th. Representatives from all walks of life in the Romanian capital held a grand rally here tonight to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Romanian Workers' Party Central Political Bureau member Gheorghe Apostol, Workers' Party Central Secretariat Secretary Vladimir Gheorghiu, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Gherasim Popa, and other party and government leaders, ministers, heads of various people's organizations, senior generals of the People's Army, and representatives from all walks of life, a total of more than 800 people, attended the celebration.

Romanian Workers' Party Political Bureau alternate member and Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Ștefan Voitec, spoke at the celebration.

Voitec spoke enthusiastically about the brilliant achievements of the Chinese people in socialist construction. He said that under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, the Chinese people, through heroic labor, have basically completed the socialist transformation in all aspects of economic and social life. At present, there has been a great upsurge in the economic and cultural construction of People's China. Voitec cited detailed statistics to illustrate the great achievements made by the Chinese people in the past ten years.

Voitec concluded by saying that the Romanian people rejoice in every victory achieved by the Chinese people on the socialist road and regard it as their own victory and the victory of the entire socialist camp.

Ambassador Xu Jianguo also spoke at the meeting.

### 沃伊捷克在布加勒斯特庆祝大会上说罗马尼亚为中国的胜利欢欣鼓舞

1959-10-02
第6版()
专栏：

    沃伊捷克在布加勒斯特庆祝大会上说罗马尼亚为中国的胜利欢欣鼓舞
    据新华社布加勒斯特9月30日电　罗马尼亚首都各界代表今晚在这里举行集会，隆重庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年。
    罗马尼亚工人党中央政治局委员格奥尔基·阿波斯托尔、工人党中央书记处书记弗拉吉米尔·乔治乌、部长会议副主席格拉希姆·波帕等党政领导人、各部部长、各人民团体负责人、人民军高级将领、各界代表共八百多人参加了庆祝会。
    罗马尼亚工人党政治局候补委员、部长会议副主席斯捷凡·沃伊捷克在庆祝会上讲了话。
    沃伊捷克热情地谈到了中国人民在社会主义建设中所取得的辉煌成就。他说，中国人民在中国共产党的领导下，经过英勇的劳动，在经济和社会生活等各方面已基本上完成了社会主义的改造。目前，在人民中国的经济文化建设等方面出现了巨大的高潮。沃伊捷克引用详细的统计数字说明了中国人民十年来取得的伟大成就。
    沃伊捷克最后说，罗马尼亚人民为中国人民在社会主义道路上所取得的每个胜利而欢欣鼓舞，并且把它看成是自己的胜利和整个社会主义阵营的胜利。
    许建国大使也在会上讲了话。
