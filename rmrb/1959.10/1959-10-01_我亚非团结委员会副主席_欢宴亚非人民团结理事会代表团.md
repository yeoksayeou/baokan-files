Title: Vice-Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity Holds Reception for Delegation of the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Council
Author:
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 7
    Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, gave a luncheon today to welcome all members of the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Council delegation headed by Youssef El-Sebai, Secretary-General of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Council.
    Among those attending the banquet were Mao Dun, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity; Ji Chao-ding, Vice-Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Zhang Jie, Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association; Zhang Tiesheng, Secretary-General of the Chinese Afro-Asian Society; Zhu Ziqi, Secretary-General of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity; and Yang Shuo, Deputy Secretary-General.

### 我亚非团结委员会副主席  欢宴亚非人民团结理事会代表团

1959-10-01
第7版()
专栏：

    我亚非团结委员会副主席
    欢宴亚非人民团结理事会代表团
    新华社30日讯　中国亚非团结委员会副主席刘宁一今天中午设宴欢迎以亚非人民团结理事会书记处总书记优素福·西巴伊为首的亚非人民团结理事会代表团全体成员。
    参加宴会的有中国亚非团结委员会副主席茅盾、中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎、中国伊斯兰教协会副主任张杰、中国亚非学会秘书长张铁生、中国亚非团结委员会秘书长朱子奇、副秘书长杨朔等。
