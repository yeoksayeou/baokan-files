Title: Central Treaty Organization Creates Tension, To Hold Joint Military Exercises
Author:
Date: 1959-10-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 12th -- Newspapers here reported today that the Central Treaty Organization has decided to conduct a joint maritime military exercise in the Arabian Sea off Karachi from November 16 to December 4. The United States, Britain, Iran, Pakistan, and Turkey will participate in this exercise. It is claimed that the main purpose of this exercise is to "conduct tactical exercises for the navies of the participating countries in artillery battles, underwater battles, telecommunications, and especially in escort defense."

### 中央条约组织搞紧张  将举行联合军事演习

1959-10-14
第6版()
专栏：

　　中央条约组织搞紧张
    将举行联合军事演习
    新华社卡拉奇12日电　据这里报纸今天报道，中央条约组织决定从11月16日至12月4日在卡拉奇附近的阿拉伯海海面进行联合海上军事演习。参加这次演习的有美国、英国、伊朗、巴基斯坦和土耳其。据称，这次演习的主要目的是“进行参加国的海军在炮战、水底战斗、电讯以及特别着重在护航防御方面的战术演习”。
