Title: Two Colombian Guests Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 30th — Mrs. Enrique Gomez Hurtado and Mrs. Agoso Lopez Michelsen, members of the Colombian cultural and friendship delegation, who were invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China and participate in the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China celebration, arrived in Beijing by plane on September 30.

### 哥伦比亚两位客人到京

1959-10-01
第7版()
专栏：

    哥伦比亚两位客人到京
    新华社30日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的哥伦比亚文化友好代表团团员恩里克·戈麦斯·乌尔塔多夫人和阿高索·洛佩斯·未切尔森夫人于9月30日乘飞机到达北京。
