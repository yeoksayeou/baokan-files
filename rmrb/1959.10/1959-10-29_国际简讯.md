    International Briefs
    Guinean President Touré Arrives in Washington
    Washington dispatch: Guinean President Touré arrived in Washington on the 26th for a sixteen-day state visit. Touré met with U.S. President Eisenhower that day and held talks with Eisenhower and U.S. Secretary of State Herter on the 27th.
    UAR and American Entrepreneurs Exchange Visits
    Cairo dispatch: It was announced here on the evening of the 25th that the UAR government has selected a delegation representing UAR entrepreneurs and trade union workers to visit the United States for two months. According to newspaper reports, Brand, Director of the U.S. Development Loan Fund, will visit in November to "discuss the possibility of financing some UAR projects."

### 国际简讯

1959-10-29
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    几内亚总统杜尔抵华盛顿
    华盛顿讯　几内亚总统杜尔26日抵达华盛顿作为期十六天的国事访问。杜尔当天会见了美国总统艾森豪威尔。并在27日同艾森豪威尔和美国国务卿赫脱会谈。
    阿联和美国企业家相互访问
    开罗讯　25日晚此间宣布，阿联政府选出一个代表阿联的企业家和工会工作者的代表团去美国作为期两个月的访问。据报纸报道，美国开发贷款基金会主任布兰德将在11月间前来访问，以便“讨论资助阿联的一些工程的可能性”。
