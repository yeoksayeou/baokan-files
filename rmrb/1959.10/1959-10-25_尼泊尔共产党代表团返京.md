Title: Nepalese Communist Party Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-10-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 24th: The Nepalese Communist Party delegation, headed by Comrade Keshar Jung Rayamajhi, returned to Beijing by train today after visiting other places. The distinguished guests left Shanghai by train on the 23rd. While in Shanghai, they visited the Shanghai Revolutionary History Memorial Hall, the Shanghai Industrial Exhibition, the No. 2 State Cotton Factory, the Jiangnan Paper Mill, the Guangci Hospital, and workers' residences.

### 尼泊尔共产党代表团返京

1959-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔共产党代表团返京
    新华社24日讯　以克沙尔·忠格·腊伊马吉同志为首的尼泊尔共产党代表团，在外地参观访问之后，于今日乘火车回到北京。贵宾们于23日乘火车离开上海，在上海曾参观访问了上海革命历史纪念馆、上海工业展览会、国棉二厂、江南造纸厂、广慈医院、工人住宅等。
