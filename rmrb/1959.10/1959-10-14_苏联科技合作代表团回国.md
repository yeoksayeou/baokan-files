Title: Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-14
Page: 5

　　Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Delegation Returns Home
    Xinhua News Agency, 13th — The Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Delegation, headed by Siluyanov, participated in the tenth meeting of the Sino-Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee in our country, and left Beijing by plane today to return home.

### 苏联科技合作代表团回国

1959-10-14
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏联科技合作代表团回国
    据新华社13日讯　以西鲁扬诺夫为首的苏联科学技术合作代表团在我国参加了中苏科学技术合作委员会第十届会议以后，在今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
