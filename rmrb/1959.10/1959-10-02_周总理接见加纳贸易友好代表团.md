Title: Premier Zhou Receives Ghana Trade Friendship Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1959-10-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 1st October. Premier Zhou Enlai received the Ghana Trade Friendship Delegation led by Ghana's Minister of Trade and Industry, Mr. Kojo Botsio, this evening.

Present at the reception were Ye Jiuzhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, and Lu Xuzhang, Vice Minister.

### 周总理接见加纳贸易友好代表团

1959-10-02
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理接见加纳贸易友好代表团
    新华社1日讯　周恩来总理今天晚上接见了由加纳贸易和工业部长库埃多率领的加纳贸易友好代表团。
    接见时在座的，有我国对外贸易部部长叶季壮、副部长卢绪章。
