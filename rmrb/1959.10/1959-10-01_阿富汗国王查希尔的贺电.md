Title: Telegram of Greetings from King Zahir of Afghanistan
Author:
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 8

    Telegram of Greetings from King Zahir of Afghanistan
    To His Excellency Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:
    On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, I am very pleased to extend to Your Excellency and the Chinese people my warmest congratulations and most sincere wishes, wishing Your Excellency personal happiness and the great Chinese people prosperity.
                                    Mohammed Zahir
                                Kabul, September 29, 1959

### 阿富汗国王查希尔的贺电

1959-10-01
第8版()
专栏：

    阿富汗国王查希尔的贺电
    中华人民共和国主席刘少奇阁下：
    值此中华人民共和国建国十周年之际，我十分愉快地向阁下和中国人民致以最热烈的祝贺和最诚挚的祝愿，祝阁下个人幸福、祝伟大的中国人民昌盛。
                                    穆罕默德·查希尔
                                1959年9月29日喀布尔
