Title: Chilean Doctor Acuña Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-28
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, October 27th: Justo Ulloa Acuña, a Chilean surgeon invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by train today.

### 智利医生阿库纳到京

1959-10-28
第5版()
专栏：

    智利医生阿库纳到京
    新华社27日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的智利外科医生胡斯多·乌利奥阿·阿库纳，今天乘火车到达北京。
