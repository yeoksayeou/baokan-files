Title: Buddhist Community in Beijing Mourns Master Xu Yun
Author:
Date: 1959-10-20
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, 17th: The Buddhist community in Beijing held a memorial service today at Guangji Temple to mourn Master Xu Yun, Honorary President of the Buddhist Association of China. He suffered from achalasia and chronic indigestion for several months, and after unsuccessful medical treatment, passed away on October 13th at Zhenru Temple on Yunju Mountain in Yongxiu County, Jiangxi Province.

　　Among those who attended the memorial service were Zhao Puchu and Zhou Shujia, Vice Presidents of the Buddhist Association of China, and responsible individuals from the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council.

　　Ju Zan, Vice President of the Buddhist Association of China, has gone to Jiangxi Province to offer condolences.

### 首都佛教界追悼虚云法师

1959-10-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　首都佛教界追悼虚云法师
    新华社17日讯首都佛教界今天在广济寺举行追悼会，追悼中国佛教协会名誉会长虚云法师。他患噎膈慢性消化不良症，历时数月，医治无效，于10月13日在江西省永修县云居山真如寺逝世。
    参加追悼会的有中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初、周叔迦及国务院宗教事务局的负责人。
    中国佛教协会副会长巨赞已去江西省吊唁。
