Title: Telegram of Congratulations from Ceylon Prime Minister Bandaranaike
Author:
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 8

To His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:

    On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, I am very pleased to extend to Your Excellency, the Chinese government, and the Chinese people the warm congratulations of the Ceylon government, the Ceylon people, and myself. Please accept the best wishes for the happiness and prosperity of the Chinese people.
                                        Bandaranaike
    (Note: Prime Minister Bandaranaike's telegram of congratulations was sent the day before he was assassinated.)

### 锡兰总理班达拉奈克的贺电

1959-10-01
第8版()
专栏：

    锡兰总理班达拉奈克的贺电
    中华人民共和国国务院总理
    周恩来先生阁下：
    欣逢中华人民共和国建国十周年，我十分高兴地向阁下、中国政府和人民表达锡兰政府、锡兰人民和我本人的热烈的祝贺。请接受对中国人民的幸福和昌盛的最良好的祝愿。
                                        班达拉奈克
    （按：班达拉奈克总理的贺电是在他遇害前一天发出的）
