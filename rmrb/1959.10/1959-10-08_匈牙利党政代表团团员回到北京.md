Title: Members of the Hungarian Party and Government Delegation Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-08
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 7th October: Members of the Hungarian People's Republic Party and Government Delegation: Béla Biszku, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and Minister of Internal Affairs of the Hungarian People's Republic; István Antos, Alternate Member of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and Minister of Finance of the Hungarian People's Republic; Sándor Nógradit, Member of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and Ambassador of Hungarian People's Republic to China, returned to Beijing today by special plane after visiting Shanghai.

Welcoming the distinguished guests at Beijing Airport were Li Xiannian, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Premier of the State Council; Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Yu Zhan, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department, and others. When the distinguished guests left Shanghai, those who saw them off at the airport included Ma Tianshui, Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee Secretariat of the Communist Party of China; Song Richang, Vice Mayor of Shanghai; Lieutenant General Wang Bicheng, head of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Shanghai, and representatives from all walks of life. Chen Zhengren, Alternate Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Minister of Agricultural Machinery, and Lin Ping, Deputy Director of the American and Australian Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, who accompanied the distinguished guests to Shanghai, were also at the airport to see them off.

### 匈牙利党政代表团团员回到北京

1959-10-08
第2版()
专栏：

    匈牙利党政代表团团员回到北京
    新华社7日讯　匈牙利人民共和国党政代表团团员：匈牙利社会主义工人党中央政治局委员、匈牙利人民共和国内务部部长比斯库·贝洛，匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会候补委员、匈牙利人民共和国财政部部长安托什·伊斯特万，匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员、匈牙利人民共和国驻华大使诺格拉第·山道尔在上海参观访问后，今天乘专机返回北京。
    到北京机场欢迎贵宾的，有中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理李先念，外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文、苏联东欧司副司长余湛等人。贵宾们离开上海时，到机场欢送的有中共上海市委书记处书记马天水、上海市副市长宋日昌、人民解放军驻上海部队首长王必成中将和各界代表人士。陪同贵宾们来上海的中共中央候补委员、农业机械部部长陈正人，外交部美澳司副司长林平也到机场送行。
