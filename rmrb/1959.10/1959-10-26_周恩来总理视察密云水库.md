Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Inspects Miyun Reservoir
Author:
Date: 1959-10-26
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th: Premier Zhou Enlai inspected the Miyun Reservoir today. On the way to the Miyun Reservoir, he also visited the Huairou Reservoir.

Accompanying Premier Zhou Enlai to the Miyun Reservoir construction site were Wang Zhen, Liao Chengzhi, Kong Yuan, Li Baohua, Zhang Hanying, Qian Zhengying, Duan Junyi, Bai Jian, Wang Guangwei, Wang Xin San, and Yang Zuocai, heads of various government departments. Also present were Zhang Tixue, Governor of Hubei Province, Yan Dakai and Yang Yingjie, Vice Governors of Hebei Province, Zhao Fan, Director of the Rural Work Department of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, as well as representatives attending the National Water Conservancy Conference in Beijing.

Premier Zhou Enlai also invited Mr. Matsumura Kenzō of Japan, who is currently visiting Beijing, and his entourage, as well as Dr. Hewlett Johnson, the Dean of Canterbury, England, and his wife, to travel with him to visit the Miyun Reservoir.

Premier Zhou Enlai met with Yūtarō Takeyama, Ichitaro Ide, Yoshimi Furui, and Masakichi Tabayashi, members of Matsumura Kenzō's entourage, as well as Japanese journalists who accompanied them.

### 周恩来总理视察密云水库

1959-10-26
第2版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理视察密云水库
    新华社25日讯　周恩来总理今天视察了密云水库。在前往密云水库的途中，看了怀柔水库。
    随同周总理一起到密云水库工地去的，有政府各部门负责人王震、廖承志、孔原、李葆华、张含英、钱正英、段君毅、白坚、王光伟、王新三、杨作材。一起去的还有湖北省省长张体学，河北省副省长阎达开、杨英杰，中共北京市委农村工作部部长赵凡，以及正在北京出席全国水利会议的代表。
    周恩来总理今天还邀请了正在北京访问的日本松村谦三先生和他的随行人员，英国坎特伯雷教长休勒特·约翰逊博士和他的夫人，与他同车前往密云水库参观。
    周恩来总理在途中接见了松村谦三的随行人员竹山佑太郎、井出一太郎、古井喜实、田林政吉等以及随同前来的日本记者。
