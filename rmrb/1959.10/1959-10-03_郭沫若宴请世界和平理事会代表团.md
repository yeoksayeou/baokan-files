Title: Guo Moruo Hosts Banquet for World Peace Council Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-10-03
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd October. Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, hosted a banquet this evening to welcome the World Peace Council delegation, who are in China to participate in the 10th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China. The delegation is led by John Desmond Bernal, a British physicist, winner of the Lenin International Peace Prize, and Executive Chairman of the Presidium of the World Peace Council, and consists of world-renowned peace advocates.
    In his speech at the banquet, Guo Moruo warmly welcomed the peace advocates from afar. He said that today also marks the 10th anniversary of the founding of the China Peace Committee. The China Peace Committee was established the day after the founding of the People's Republic of China, which clearly demonstrates that the Chinese people have always loved peace and regarded the cause of safeguarding peace as a major task. He raised his glass to wish the cause of world peace further progress in the future.
    In his speech, Bernal praised the great achievements of the Chinese people, saying that we believe the Chinese people are one of the strongest supporters of the cause of world peace. He wished the Chinese people more achievements in maintaining world peace and developing cultural and scientific undertakings.
    Liao Chengzhi, Bao Erhan, Liu Changsheng, Chu Tunan, Li Dequan, Wu Youxun, Lao She, Ji Chaoding and others attended the banquet.

### 郭沫若宴请世界和平理事会代表团

1959-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    郭沫若宴请世界和平理事会代表团
    新华社2日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若今天晚间设宴招待前来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的世界和平理事会代表团。这个代表团是以列宁国际和平奖金获得者、世界和平理事会主席团执行主席、英国物理学家约翰·德斯豪德·贝尔纳为首的世界著名和平人士组成的。
    郭沫若在宴会上致词，热情地欢迎远道而来的各国和平人士，他说，今天也是中国人民保卫世界和平委员会成立十周年纪念。十年前，在中华人民共和国建立后的第二天就成立了中国人民保卫世界和平委员会，这清楚地说明了中国人民一直是热爱和平、把维护和平的事业看作一项重大任务。他举杯祝世界和平事业今后进一步向前发展。
    贝尔纳在致词中盛赞中国人民所取得的伟大成就，他说，我们认为中国人民是世界和平事业的最强大的支持者之一。他祝中国人民在维护世界和平和发展文化科学事业上取得更多的成就。
    出席宴会的有廖承志、包尔汉、刘长胜、楚图南、李德全、吴有训、老舍、冀朝鼎等。
