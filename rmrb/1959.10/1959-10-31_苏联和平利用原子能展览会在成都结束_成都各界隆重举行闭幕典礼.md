Title: Soviet Exhibition on Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy Concludes in Chengdu; Closing Ceremony Held Solemnly by All Circles in Chengdu
Author:
Date: 1959-10-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, October 30th: The Soviet Exhibition on Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy Science and Technology concluded its exhibition in the Chengdu region of our country on the afternoon of the 29th. The exhibition has now completely concluded its tour in our country.

On the afternoon of the 29th, over 1,800 representatives from all circles in Sichuan Province and Chengdu City solemnly held the closing ceremony of the exhibition.

Vice Premier Chen Yi attended the closing ceremony and delivered a speech. He said: The exhibition of the Soviet Union's peaceful use of atomic energy science and technology in China has undoubtedly played a significant role in promoting the development of science and technology for the peaceful use of atomic energy in our country. On behalf of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, I express my heartfelt gratitude to the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Soviet government, and the Soviet people for this selfless assistance. The Soviet Party, government, and people have consistently provided fraternal and selfless assistance to our country in all aspects. The great achievements of our country's socialist construction over the past decade are inseparable from the strong support of the Soviet Union. The exhibition of the Soviet Union's peaceful use of atomic energy science and technology in China is another clear example. This fully demonstrates that the fraternal friendship between the two great socialist countries, China and the Soviet Union, is eternal and unbreakable.

Those who attended the closing ceremony included: Li Dazhang, Secretary of the Sichuan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Governor of Sichuan Province; Wang Shuntong, representative of the State Council's Working Committee for the Soviet Exhibition on Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy Science and Technology and the State Science and Technology Commission; and Li Zonglin, Mayor of Chengdu City.

Fu Ming, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, Soviet experts, and experts from other fraternal countries attended the closing ceremony.

Li Dazhang, Secretary of the Sichuan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Governor of Sichuan Province, first delivered a closing speech at the meeting. He congratulated the success of the exhibition on behalf of the Sichuan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Sichuan Provincial People's Committee and expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the Soviet Party, government, and people.

Subsequently, Fu Ming, Economic Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, spoke at the meeting. He elaborated in detail on the consistent efforts made by the Soviet Union to prohibit the use of atomic weapons and to peacefully utilize atomic energy, as well as the great achievements that the Soviet Union has already made in the peaceful use of atomic energy.

Also speaking at the meeting were Wang Shuntong, representative of the State Council's Working Committee for the Soviet Exhibition on Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy Science and Technology and the State Science and Technology Commission; Li Zonglin, Mayor of Chengdu City; and Xie Lihui, Vice Chairman of the Sichuan Provincial Science and Technology Association, and others.

On the evening of the 29th, the Sichuan Provincial and Chengdu Municipal People's Committees jointly held a banquet and a soiree.

Since its opening in Shanghai last December, the Soviet Exhibition on Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy Science and Technology has been exhibited in Shanghai, Guangzhou, and Chengdu. During the six-month exhibition period, it received a total of more than 1.34 million visitors, and all provinces, cities, and autonomous regions across the country sent visiting groups and study groups to visit and learn. During the exhibition, various professional training courses were also held, with more than 2,800 students; more than 190 report meetings and forums were held, with 133,000 listeners; more than 4,700 film screenings were held, with an audience of 2.8 million. In addition, extensive publicity was carried out through books, periodicals, radio, and newspapers.

### 苏联和平利用原子能展览会在成都结束  成都各界隆重举行闭幕典礼

1959-10-31
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联和平利用原子能展览会在成都结束
    成都各界隆重举行闭幕典礼
    新华社成都30日电　苏联和平利用原子能科学技术展览会在我国成都地区的展出，已在29日下午闭幕。这个展览会在我国的展出，至此全部结束。
    29日下午，四川省、成都市各界代表一千八百多人隆重地举行了展览会的闭幕典礼。
    陈毅副总理出席了闭幕典礼，并讲了话。他说：这次苏联和平利用原子能科学技术展览会在中国的展出，对于我国和平利用原子能的科学技术的发展，无疑地起了很大的推动作用。我代表中华人民共和国国务院，对于苏联共产党、苏联政府和苏联人民这种无私援助表示衷心的感谢。苏联党、政府和人民对我国历来就是从各方面进行兄弟般的和无私援助的。我国十年来社会主义建设的伟大成绩，就是和苏联的大力支援分不开的。这次苏联和平利用原子能科学技术展览会在中国的展出，又是一个显明的例证。这充分说明了中苏两个伟大的社会主义国家的兄弟般的友谊是永恒的牢不可破的。
    出席闭幕典礼的有：中共四川省委书记、四川省省长李大章，国务院苏联和平利用原子能科学技术展览会工作委员会和国家科学技术委员会代表王顺桐，成都市市长李宗林。
    苏联驻华大使馆参赞符明、苏联和其他兄弟国家的专家等出席了闭幕典礼。
    中共四川省委书记、四川省省长李大章曾首先在会上致闭幕词。他代表中共四川省委、四川省人民委员会祝贺展览会的成功，并向苏联党和政府以及苏联人民表示衷心的感谢。
    接着，苏联驻华大使馆经济参赞符明在会上讲话。他详细地论述了苏联为了禁止使用原子武器与和平利用原子能所作的一贯的努力，以及苏联在和平利用原子能方面所已经取得的伟大成就。
    在会上讲话的，还有国务院苏联和平利用原子能科学技术展览会工作委员会和国家科学技术委员会代表王顺桐、成都市市长李宗林和四川省科学技术协会副主席谢立惠等。
    29日夜，四川省和成都市人民委员会还联合举行了宴会和晚会。
    苏联和平利用原子能科学技术展览会从去年12月在上海开幕以来，先后在上海、广州、成都三地展出。在六个多月展出期中，共接待观众一百三十四万多人次，全国各省市自治区都派了参观团、学习组前去参观学习。展出期间，还举办了各种专业训练班，学员有二千八百多人；举行报告会、座谈会一百九十多次，听众一十三万三千人；演出电影四千七百多场，观众达二百八十万人次。此外，还通过书刊、广播、报纸进行了广泛的宣传。
