Title: Sino-German Musicians Hold Cordial Meeting; Dresden Symphony Orchestra Holds Fourth Performance
Author:
Date: 1959-10-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 12th: The Dresden Symphony Orchestra of the German Democratic Republic held its fourth concert at the Beijing Exhibition Center Theater tonight, performing a comprehensive program of four classical works by three great German musicians - Beethoven, Schubert, and Mendelssohn. It was warmly welcomed by more than two thousand audience members.

This morning, the German musicians also held a special concert for more than two thousand people from Chinese music circles.

After the concert, famous musicians from China and Germany had a cordial conversation. Chinese musicians Lü Ji and Ma Sicong both told the German musicians that they were very interested in modern German music.

Prior to this, the German musicians had also held five small concerts in Beijing on the 7th, 8th, 9th, and 11th to entertain the Central Conservatory of Music, the Military Band, the Central Philharmonic Orchestra, famous figures in the capital's music scene, as well as the staff and drivers of the hotel where they were staying, all of which were very well received.

### 中德音乐家亲切会晤  德累斯顿交响乐团举行第四次演奏

1959-10-13
第5版()
专栏：

　　中德音乐家亲切会晤
    德累斯顿交响乐团举行第四次演奏
    据新华社12日讯　德意志民主共和国德累斯顿交响乐团今晚在北京展览馆剧场举行第四次演奏会，综合演出了德国三个伟大音乐家——贝多芬、舒伯特和门德尔松四个古典作品，受到了两千多观众最热烈的欢迎。
    今天上午，德国音乐家们还曾为中国二千多位音乐界人士举行了一个特别音乐会。
    音乐会后，中德两国著名音乐家曾进行亲切交谈，中国音乐家吕骥和马思聪都对德国音乐家们表示，他们对德国现代乐曲非常感到兴趣。
    在此以前，德国音乐家们还曾先后在7日、8日、9日和11日在京举行了五次小型音乐会，招待中央音乐学院、军乐团、中央乐团、首都音乐界著名人士以及他们所在旅馆职工和司机等，都受到了极大欢迎。
