Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Indian Member of Parliament Joachim Alva
Author:
Date: 1959-10-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Premier Zhou Enlai received Indian Member of Parliament Joachim Alva this morning. Present were He Ying, Deputy Director of the First Asian Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

### 周总理接见印度国会议员乔奇姆·阿尔瓦

1959-10-10
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理接见印度国会议员乔奇姆·阿尔瓦
    新华社9日讯　周恩来总理在今天上午接见了印度国会议员乔奇姆·阿尔瓦。在座的，有外交部第一亚洲司副司长何英，中国人民外交学会副秘书长吴晓达。
