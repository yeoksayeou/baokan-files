Title: UN General Assembly, Manipulated by the US, Places the So-Called "Tibetan Issue" on the Agenda; Soviet and Romanian Representatives Denounce US Provocative Conspiracy; Indonesian Representative Points Out that Raising the "Tibetan Issue" Aims to Intensify the Cold War
Author:
Date: 1959-10-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 13th: The United Nations General Assembly held a plenary session on the 12th to discuss the General Committee's recommendation to include the so-called "Tibetan issue," which is purely China's internal affair, on the agenda of the General Assembly. Under the control of the mechanical majority of the United States, the General Assembly brazenly disregarded the opposition of the Soviet Union and many other countries and decided to include the so-called "Tibetan issue" on the agenda. This once again exposed the international reactionaries headed by US imperialism who openly undermined the UN Charter, used the UN as a tool for imperialists to interfere in China's internal affairs, and used the UN as a place to create a Cold War and hinder the relaxation of international tensions.
    Soviet representative Kuznetzov's speech at the meeting powerfully exposed the shameful purpose of the international reactionaries, headed by US imperialism, who insisted that the UN General Assembly discuss the "Tibetan issue."
    Kuznetzov said that the Soviet delegation firmly opposed the General Committee's decision to include the so-called "Tibetan issue" on the agenda of the General Assembly (full text to be published separately).
    Romanian representative, Mr. Bucan, also spoke against including the so-called "Tibetan issue" on the agenda. He pointed out that raising the "Tibetan issue" was actually to continue the "Cold War," and that discussing this "issue" was absurd.
    Indonesian representative, Mr. Sastroamidjojo, emphasized that those who raised the "Tibetan issue" had only one purpose: to intensify the "Cold War" and worsen relations between major powers. He said that Tibet is an inseparable part of the territory of the People's Republic of China, and that the UN General Assembly's inclusion of the "Tibetan issue" on the agenda is an interference in the internal affairs of a sovereign country.
    US representative, Mr. Lodge, in his speech, categorically denied the shameful purpose of the US's forcing the UN General Assembly to discuss the "Tibetan issue" in order to intensify the "Cold War." In his speech, he confused right and wrong, attempting to portray Tibet as a "country." He once again slandered the Chinese government for "committing crimes" in Tibet and clamored for the UN to interfere in China's internal affairs.
    Lodge was enraged by the Soviet representative's exposure of the US State Department. Unable to refute it, he had to insist that the Soviet representative's accusations were so-called "dangerous misunderstandings" and "groundless."
    Representatives from New Zealand, El Salvador, and Thailand also demanded that the "Tibetan issue" be included on the agenda of the General Assembly. New Zealand representative, Mr. Shanahan, slandered China for "large-scale violations of human rights." El Salvador's representative, Mr. Urrutia, also fabricated claims that ignoring the "suppression of the Tibetan regime" would "amount to abandoning respect for the law." Thai representative, Mr. Itthyathtithamrong, described the Chinese government's suppression of the armed rebellion of the upper-class reactionary elements in Tibet and the Tibetan people's liberation from the shackles of serfdom as a "tragic event" and said that the UN could not turn a blind eye to it.
    During the vote on whether to include the "Tibetan issue" on the agenda, the following delegations voted in favor: Argentina, Australia, Austria, Belgium, Bolivia, Brazil, Canada, Chile, Kuomintang Group, Colombia, Costa Rica, Cuba, Denmark, Ecuador, El Savador, Federation of Malaya, Greece, Guatemala, Haiti, Honduras, Iceland, Tran, Ireland, Italy, Japan, Laos, Luxembourg, Mexico, Netherlands, New Zealand, Nicaraqua, Norway, Pakistan, Panama, Paraguay, Philippines, Sweden, Thailand, Turkey, United Kingdom, United States, Uruguay, Venezuela; the following delegations voted against: Albania, Bulgaria, Byelorussia, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Indonesia, Poland, Romania, Ukraine, Soviet Union, Yugoslavia; the following abstained: Afghanistan, Myanmar, Cambodia, Ceylon, Dominican Republic, Finland, France, Ghana, Guinea, Iraq, Jordan, Lebannon, Liberia, Libya, Morocco, Nepal, Peru, Portugal, Saudi Arabia, Spain, Sudan, Tunisia, South Africa, United Arab Republic, Yemen; the following did not vote: India; the following were absent: Ethiopia and Israel.
    Agence France-Presse commented on the voting results: "A considerable number of delegations abstained because some delegations hesitated on the legal basis of discussing the 'Tibetan issue', and many believe that it is an internal affair of China."

### 联大在美国操纵下把所谓“西藏问题”列入议程  苏罗代表痛斥美国挑衅阴谋  印度尼西亚代表指出提出“西藏问题”旨在加剧冷战

1959-10-14
第6版()
专栏：

　　联大在美国操纵下把所谓“西藏问题”列入议程
    苏罗代表痛斥美国挑衅阴谋
    印度尼西亚代表指出提出“西藏问题”旨在加剧冷战
    据新华社13日讯　纽约消息：联合国大会12日举行全体会议，讨论总务委员会关于把纯属中国内政的所谓“西藏问题”列入大会议程的建议。大会在美国的机械多数的控制下，悍然不顾苏联及其他许多国家的反对，决定把所谓“西藏问题”列入议程。这样就再一次暴露了以美帝国主义为首的国际反动派公然破坏联合国宪章，把联合国作为帝国主义者干涉中国内政的工具，并且把联合国作为制造冷战的场所，阻挠国际紧张局势的缓和。
    苏联代表库兹?佐夫在会上的发言中有力地揭露了以美帝国主义为首国际反动派硬要联合国大会讨论“西藏问题”的可耻目的。
    库兹?佐夫说，苏联代表团坚决反对总务委员会决定把所谓“西藏问题”列入大会议程（全文另发）。
    罗马尼亚代表布鲁坎也发言反对把所谓“西藏问题”列入议程。他指出，提出“西藏问题”实际上是要继续进行“冷战”，讨论这个“问题”是荒唐的。
    印度尼西亚代表沙斯特罗阿米佐约强调说，提出“西藏问题”的人只有一个目的，就是加剧“冷战”和恶化大国之间的关系。他说，西藏是中华人民共和国领土不可分割的一部分，联合国大会把“西藏问题”列入议程是对一个主权国家的内政的干涉。
    美国代表洛奇在发言中矢口抵赖美国强使联合国大会讨论“西藏问题”是为了加剧“冷战”这一可耻目的。他在发言中混淆是非，企图把西藏说成为一个“国家”。他再一次诬蔑中国政府在西藏“犯下了罪行”，并叫嚷要联合国干涉中国内政。
    洛奇对苏联代表对美国国务院的揭发恼羞成怒，他无法进行反驳，只好硬说苏联代表的指责是所谓“危险的误解”和“毫无根据”。
    新西兰、萨尔瓦多和泰国代表也要求把“西藏问题”列入大会议程。新西兰代表申哈汗诽谤中国“大规模侵犯人权”。萨尔瓦多代表乌基亚也胡诌什么忽视“对西藏政权的镇压行为”将“等于放弃对法律的尊重”。泰国代表抑他育蒂把中国政府平息西藏上层反动分子的武装叛乱和西藏人民正在摆脱农奴制度的桎梏和获得解放说成是“悲惨事件”，并且说联合国对此不能不闻不问。
    大会在就是否把“西藏问题”列入议程进行表决时，投赞成票的有：阿根廷、澳大利亚、奥地利、比利时、玻利维亚、巴西、加拿大、智利、国民党集团、哥伦比亚、哥斯达黎加、古巴、丹麦、厄瓜多尔、萨尔瓦多、马来亚联合邦、希腊、危地马拉、海地、洪都拉斯、冰岛、伊朗、爱尔兰、意大利、日本、老挝、卢森堡、墨西哥、荷兰、新西兰、尼加拉瓜、挪威、巴基斯坦、巴拿马、巴拉圭、菲律宾、瑞典、泰国、土耳其、联合王国、美国、乌拉圭、委内瑞拉等四十三个代表团；投反对票的有：阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、白俄罗斯、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、印度尼西亚、波兰、罗马尼亚、乌克兰、苏联、南斯拉夫等十一个代表团；弃权的有：阿富汗、缅甸、柬埔寨、锡兰、多米尼加共和国、芬兰、法国、加纳、几内亚、伊拉克、约旦、黎巴嫩、利比里亚、利比亚、摩洛哥、尼泊尔、秘鲁、葡萄牙、沙特阿拉伯、西班牙、苏丹、突尼斯、南非、阿联、也门等二十五个代表团；没有投票的有：印度代表团；缺席的有：埃塞俄比亚和以色列代表团。
    法新社在评论这个表决结果时说，这次表决“有相当多的代表团弃权，这是因为一些代表团对于讨论‘西藏问题’的法律基础感到犹疑，许多人认为，这是一个中国的内政问题。”（附图片）
