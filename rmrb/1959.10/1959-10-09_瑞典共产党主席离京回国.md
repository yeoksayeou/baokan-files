Title: Swedish Communist Party Chairman Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 8th: Comrade Hilding Hagberg, head of the Swedish Communist Party delegation and Chairman of the Central Committee of the Swedish Communist Party, and his wife, left Beijing by plane this afternoon to return home after participating in the celebrations of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and visiting various parts of our country. They were seen off at the airport by Tan Zhenlin, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee.

Two members of the delegation will continue to visit China before returning home.

### 瑞典共产党主席离京回国

1959-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

    瑞典共产党主席离京回国
    新华社8日讯　瑞典共产党代表团团长、瑞典共产党中央委员会主席希尔丁·哈格堡同志和他的夫人，在参加了中华人民共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼和在我国各地参观访问之后，今天下午乘飞机离开北京回国。到机场欢送的，有中共中央政治局委员谭震林，中央委员刘宁一。
    代表团的两位团员，将在我国继续参观访问以后回国。
