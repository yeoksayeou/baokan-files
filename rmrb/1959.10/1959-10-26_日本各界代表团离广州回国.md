Title: Japanese Delegations from Various Circles Leave Guangzhou for Home

Author:
Date: 1959-10-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 25th: The Japanese delegation from various circles celebrating the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, headed by former Japanese Prime Minister, President of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, and Member of the House of Representatives, Tetsu Katayama, and accompanied by Yang Jin, Director of the Chinese Committee for Asian-African Solidarity, arrived in Guangzhou by plane from Beijing on the afternoon of the 24th. This morning, they traveled by car to Shenzhen and will return home via Hong Kong.

Those who went to the station to see off the guests included: Zhu Guang, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province and Mayor of Guangzhou; Sun Leyi, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou; Feng Naichao, Chairman of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; and responsible persons from relevant provincial and municipal units and people's organizations.

On the evening of the 24th, Zhu Guang hosted a banquet to bid farewell to all the guests.

Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, October 25th: The Japanese delegation from various circles celebrating the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China arrived in Hong Kong from Guangzhou today. The head of the delegation, Tetsu Katayama, told reporters in Hong Kong: On the issue of defending world peace and restoring normal relations between Japan and China, the goals of the Japanese people and the Chinese people are the same, and we will work together for this.

Katayama said: The Japanese people are launching a massive movement against the Kishi Nobusuke government's revision of the Japan-U.S. Security Treaty. He said: "The Japanese people not only oppose the revision of the Japan-U.S. Security Treaty, but also demand the abolition of this treaty, which is beneficial to peace in Asia and the Far East."

Katayama reiterated the principle that the political and economic relations between Japan and China cannot be separated. He also stated that the joint statement issued by the Japanese delegation and seven Chinese organizations this time will promote the friendly relations between the two peoples.

### 日本各界代表团离广州回国

1959-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    日本各界代表团离广州回国
    据新华社广州25日电  以日本前首相、日中文化交流协会会长、众议院议员片山哲为首的日本各界庆祝中华人民共和国建国十周年代表团，由中国亚非团结委员会理事杨进陪同，于24日下午由北京乘飞机到达广州，今天上午乘车前往深圳，取道香港回国。
    前往车站欢送客人的有：广东省副省长、广州市市长朱光，广州市副市长孙乐宜，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广州市分会主席冯乃超以及省、市有关单位、人民团体的负责人。
    24日晚上，朱光曾设宴欢送全体客人。
    据新华社香港25日电　日本各界庆祝中华人民共和国建国十周年代表团今天从广州到达香港。团长片山哲在香港对新闻记者说：在保卫世界和平恢复日中两国的正常关系上，日本人民和中国人民的目标是一致的，我们将为此而共同努力。
    片山哲说：日本人民正在展开一个声势浩大的反对岸信介政府修改日美安全保障条约的运动。他说：“日本人民不但反对修改日美安全保障条约，而且还要求废除这一条约，这样对亚洲和远东地区的和平都有好处。”
    片山哲重申日中两国间的政治关系和经济关系不能分开的原则，他还表示，这次日本各界代表团和中国七个团体发表的共同声明将促进两国人民的友好关系。
