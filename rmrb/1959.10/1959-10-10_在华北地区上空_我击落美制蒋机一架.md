Title: I Shot Down a U.S.-Made Chiang Kai-shek Plane Over North China
Author:
Date: 1959-10-10
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 9th. A U.S.-made Chiang Kai-shek Air Force RB-57D high-altitude reconnaissance plane intruded into the airspace over North China on the morning of the 7th and was shot down by the Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

### 在华北地区上空  我击落美制蒋机一架

1959-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

    在华北地区上空
    我击落美制蒋机一架
    新华社9日讯　美制蒋空军RB—57D型高空侦察机一架，于7日上午窜扰我华北地区上空，被我中国人民解放军空军部队击落。
