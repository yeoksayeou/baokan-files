Title: Chaohu Produces Slag Cement
Author:
Date: 1959-10-22
Page: 3

A dispatch of good news from all directions concerning construction:

    Chaohu Produces Slag Cement
    (Xinhua News Agency dispatch) The first rotary kiln at the Chaohu Cement Plant in Anhui Province was put into production on October 8th.
    The entire production process of this factory is mechanized. It produces slag Portland cement of grade 400 and above, for use in the construction of the iron and steel industry and the power industry.
    The Chaohu Cement Plant adopted the method of one-time construction and phased installation. According to the design, three rotary kilns will be built, each with an annual output of 150,000 tons.
    From the official start of construction in November of last year to the basic completion by the end of September this year, it only took more than ten months, shortening the construction time by two years compared to the usual situation. (Attached picture)
    The Guizhou Cement Plant, which is under accelerated construction, is carrying out the final installation work on its first rotary kiln with an annual output of 90,000 tons, and will soon be ready for trial operation. Xinhua News Agency reporter Tian Jianzhi Photo

### 巢湖生产矿渣水泥

1959-10-22
第3版()
专栏：建设喜讯四方来

    巢湖生产矿渣水泥
    据新华社讯　安徽省巢湖水泥厂第一座旋窑在10月8日投入生产。
    这个工厂生产过程全部机械化；生产的全是四百号以上矿渣矽酸盐水泥，供钢铁工业和电力工业等部门建设使用。
    巢湖水泥厂是采取一次建设分次安装的办法进行的。按照设计，要建立三座旋窑，每座年产十五万吨。
    这个厂从去年11月正式开始建设到今年9月底基本建成，前后只有十个多月时间，比通常情况下建设时间缩短了两年。（附图片）
    加速兴建中的贵州水泥厂，一座年产九万吨的第一号旋窑正进行最后的安装工程，不久就能试运转。　新华社记者　田建之摄
