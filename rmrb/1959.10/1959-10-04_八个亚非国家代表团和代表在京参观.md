Title: Delegations and Representatives from Eight Asian and African Countries Visit Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd October. Government delegations and representatives from eight friendly Asian and African countries visited Beijing yesterday and today.

    The government delegation of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, headed by Ghulam Muhammad Sherzad, visited the newly built Beijing Railway Station yesterday and went to the Great Wall to appreciate the scenery.

    The delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, headed by Ben Youssef Ben Khedda, and the delegation of the Government of the Republic of Iraq, headed by Ahmed Muhammad Yehia, visited the Palace of National Culture today. Yesterday, both delegations visited the Agricultural Exhibition Hall.

    U Chit Thoung, representative of the Government of the Union of Burma; the Sudanese cultural delegation headed by A.I.M. Gubara, representing the Government of the Republic of Sudan to attend the 10th anniversary celebration of the founding of the People's Republic of China; and the Yemeni government delegation headed by Sayyid Ali Mu'ayyad, visited the Agricultural Exhibition Hall this morning. Yesterday, they also visited the Industrial and Transportation Exhibition Hall and the Historical Museum, and went to the Summer Palace to admire the autumn scenery.

    Bari Diawandou, representative of the Government of the Republic of Guinea, visited the Industrial and Transportation Exhibition Hall and the Agricultural Exhibition Hall yesterday and today. He also visited the Forbidden City yesterday.

    The government delegation of the Kingdom of Nepal, headed by Tulsi Giri, visited the Industrial and Transportation Exhibition Hall today. Yesterday, they first visited the Ming Tombs Reservoir and the Great Wall. The distinguished guests also visited the zoo yesterday.

### 八个亚非国家代表团和代表在京参观

1959-10-04
第2版()
专栏：

    八个亚非国家代表团和代表在京参观
    新华社3日讯　八个亚非友好国家政府代表团和政府代表昨天和今天分别在京参观访问。
    以吴拉姆·穆罕默德·舍尔扎德为首的阿富汗王国政府代表团昨天参观了新近建成的北京车站，并前往长城欣赏了塞上景色。
    以本·优素福·本·赫达为首的阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团和以艾哈迈德·穆罕默德·叶海亚为首的伊拉克共和国政府代表团今天参观了民族文化宫。昨天，这两个代表团参观了农业展览馆。
    缅甸联邦政府代表吴漆东，以阿·依·墨·古巴拉为首的代表苏丹共和国政府来中国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的苏丹文化代表团，以赛义德·阿里·穆安亚德为首的也门政府代表团，今天上午参观了农业展览馆。昨天他们还参观了工业交通展览馆和历史博物馆，并到颐和园观赏秋天景色。
    几内亚共和国政府代表巴里·迪亚万杜昨天和今天先后参观了工业交通展览馆和农业展览馆。他在昨天还游览了故宫。
    以图尔西·吉里为首的尼泊尔王国政府代表团今天参观了工业交通展览馆。昨天，他们首到十三陵水库和长城参观。贵宾们昨天还到动物园游览。
