Title: Hungarian Biochemist Straub, Bruno Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-28
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, October 27th. Academician Straub, Bruno, a biochemist from the Hungarian Academy of Sciences, and his wife, arrived in Beijing by plane today, according to the 1959 implementation plan of the scientific cooperation agreement between the Chinese and Hungarian Academies of Sciences.

### 匈牙利生物化学家斯特  劳布·布鲁诺到京

1959-10-28
第5版()
专栏：

    匈牙利生物化学家斯特
    劳布·布鲁诺到京
    新华社27日讯　根据中、匈两国科学院科学合作协定1959年执行计划来我国访问的匈牙利科学院生物化学家斯特劳布·布鲁诺院士和他的夫人，已经在今天乘飞机到达北京。
