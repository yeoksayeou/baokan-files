Title: Dutch Communist Party Delegation and Swiss Labor Party Delegation Leave Beijing for Home

Author:
Date: 1959-10-19
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, 18th -- The Dutch Communist Party delegation headed by Comrade Bosuwen, member of the Central Committee of the Dutch Communist Party, and the Swiss Labor Party delegation headed by Comrade Guido Cavagnia, member of the Central Committee of the Swiss Labor Party, left Beijing by the same plane today to return to their countries after attending the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and visiting various parts of our country.
　　Those who went to the airport to see them off were Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, and Lin Feng and Wu Xiuquan, members of the Central Committee.
　　Lydia Walker, a member of the Swiss Labor Party delegation, will continue her visit in China before returning to her country.

### 荷兰共产党代表团和瑞士劳动党代表团离京回国

1959-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

　　荷兰共产党代表团和瑞士劳动党代表团离京回国
    新华社18日讯　以荷兰共产党中央委员波素文同志为首的荷兰共产党代表团，以瑞士劳动党中央委员古伊多·卡瓦尼亚同志为首的瑞士劳动党代表团，在参加中华人民共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼和在我国各地参观访问之后，于今天同乘一架飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的，有中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记彭真，中央委员林枫、伍修权。
    瑞士劳动党代表团团员莉迪娅·沃克将在我国继续参观访问以后回国。
