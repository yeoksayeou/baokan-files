Title: After Participating in "Korea-China Friendship Month" Activities, China-Korea Friendship Association Delegation Leaves Pyongyang for Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-29
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 28th: The delegation of the China-Korea Friendship Association, headed by Wei Chuantong, Vice President of the China-Korea Friendship Association, concluded its visit to Korea today and left Pyongyang by international train for home.

Those who saw them off at the station included: Ha Ang-cheon, Chairman of the Korea-China Friendship Association, Seo Cheol, Vice Chairman, Song Young, Chairman of the Korean Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations, Park Gwang-seon, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Qiao Xiaoguang, Chinese Ambassador to Korea.

During their visit to Korea for more than a month at the invitation of the Korean people, the delegation participated in the "Korea-China Friendship Month" activities held by the Korean people to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. They visited factories, agricultural cooperatives, and schools in Pyongyang, Kaesong, Wonsan, and other places, and met with members of the Korea-China Friendship Agricultural Cooperative in Gimcheon County, North Hwanghae Province, Zhao Yuli, the "Mother of the Volunteers," and the Young Pioneers of the Mao Zedong Branch of the Pyongyang Moranbong Middle School. The delegation was welcomed everywhere.

On the evening of the 26th, Ha Ang-cheon, Chairman of the Korea-China Friendship Association, held a banquet to bid farewell to the delegation.

### 参加“朝中友好月”活动后  中朝友协代表团离平壤回国

1959-10-29
第5版()
专栏：

    参加“朝中友好月”活动后
    中朝友协代表团离平壤回国
    新华社平壤28日电　以中朝友好协会副会长魏传统为首的中朝友好协会代表团，今天结束了在朝鲜的访问，乘国际列车离平壤回国。
    到车站送行的有：朝中友好协会委员长河仰天、副委员长徐哲，朝鲜对外文化联络委员会委员长宋影，外务省副相朴光鲜和中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光等。
  代表团在应邀来朝鲜访问的一个多月中，参加了朝鲜人民为庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年而举行的“朝中友好月”活动，访问了平壤、开城、元山等地的工厂、农业社和学校，会见了黄海北道金川郡朝中友谊农业社的社员、志愿军妈妈赵玉礼以及平壤牡丹中学朝鲜少年团毛泽东分团的少年团员。代表团到处受到欢迎。
    26日晚上，朝中友好协会委员长河仰天曾经举行宴会欢送代表团。
