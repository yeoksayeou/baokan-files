Title: Congratulations on the Successful Launch of the Third Soviet Cosmic Rocket. Song Qingling and Li Siguang Send Telegrams to Popova, Andreyev, Nesmeyanov, Siruyanov, and Mitin
Author:
Date: 1959-10-06
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, October 4th. Song Qingling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, sent a telegram today to Comrade Popova, Chairman of the Presidium of the Soviet Society for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Comrade Andreyev, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, and Comrade Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, congratulating the Soviet Union on the successful launch of the third cosmic rocket. The telegram stated: "As the Chinese people celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, and as the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the establishment of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association is in full swing, we are extremely pleased to learn of the successful launch of the third cosmic rocket by the Soviet Union. On behalf of all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, we extend our warm congratulations to you, and through you, to the great Soviet people and the outstanding Soviet scientists."
    The telegram stated: "The continuous successful launches of cosmic rockets by the Soviet Union demonstrate the unparalleled superiority of the socialist system. It not only heralds the increasingly imminent moment of mankind flying to the moon, but also plays a huge role in further strengthening world peace."
    The telegram stated: "The Chinese people sincerely feel honored and proud of the victory of their best friend – the great Soviet people, because every victory achieved by the Soviet people is also a victory for the Chinese people, a victory for the entire socialist camp."
    The telegram concluded: "We wish the Soviet people ever-increasing achievements in the glorious cause of conquering the universe! Long live the great and unbreakable Sino-Soviet friendship!"
    Xinhua News Agency, October 4th. Li Siguang, Chairman of the China Association for Science and Technology, sent a telegram today to Comrade Siruyanov, Chairman of the All-Soviet Scientific and Technological Association, and Comrade Mitin, Chairman of the All-Soviet Association for the Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge, congratulating the Soviet Union on the successful launch of the third cosmic rocket. The telegram stated: "We are extremely excited about the successful launch on October 4th by the Soviet Union of the third cosmic rocket equipped with an automatic interplanetary station. I would like to extend my warm congratulations to you and, through you, to the Soviet scientific and technological workers and the entire Soviet people."

### 祝贺苏联发射第三个宇宙火箭成功  宋庆龄、李四光分电波波娃、安德烈耶夫、涅斯米扬诺夫、西鲁扬诺夫、米丁

1959-10-06
第4版()
专栏：

    祝贺苏联发射第三个宇宙火箭成功
    宋庆龄、李四光分电波波娃、安德烈耶夫、涅斯米扬诺夫、西鲁扬诺夫、米丁
    新华社4日讯　中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄今天打电报给苏联对外友好文化联系协会主席团主席波波娃同志、苏中友好协会理事会主席安德烈耶夫同志、苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫同志，祝贺苏联成功地发射了第三个宇宙火箭。电报说：“在中国人民欢庆中华人民共和国建国十周年的节日里，当庆祝中苏友好协会成立十周年大会正在热烈进行的时候，我们万分高兴地获悉，苏联成功地发射了第三个宇宙火箭。谨代表中苏友好协会全体会员，向你们，并通过你们，向伟大的苏联人民和卓越的苏联科学家们，表示热烈的祝贺。”
    电报说：“苏联连续成功地发射宇宙火箭，显示着社会主义制度的无比优越性，它不仅预示着人类飞向月球的时刻日益临近，而且对于世界和平的进一步加强起着巨大的作用。”
    电报说：“中国人民真诚地为自己最好的朋友——伟大的苏联人民的胜利，感到光荣和骄傲，因为苏联人民所获得的每一个胜利，也就是中国人民的胜利，是整个社会主义阵营的胜利。”
    电报最后说：“祝苏联人民在征服宇宙的光荣事业中，获得日新月异的成就！伟大的牢不可破的中苏友谊万岁！”
    新华社4日讯　中国科学技术协会主席李四光今天打电报给全苏科学技术协会主席西鲁扬诺夫同志、全苏政治与科学知识普及协会主席米丁同志，祝贺苏联成功地发射了第三个宇宙火箭。电报中说：“苏联10月4日成功地发射了装有自动行星际站的第三个宇宙火箭，我们感到无比兴奋。我谨向您们并通过您们向苏联科学技术工作者和全体苏联人民致以热烈的祝贺。”
