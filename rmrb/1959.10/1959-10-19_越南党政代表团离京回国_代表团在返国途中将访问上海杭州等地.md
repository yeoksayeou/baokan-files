Title: Vietnamese Party and Government Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home; Delegation to Visit Shanghai, Hangzhou, and Other Places on Return Trip

Author:

Date: 1959-10-19
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The Vietnamese Party and Government Delegation, led by Pham Van Dong, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party and Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, left Beijing by special train this morning to return home. After attending the celebrations for the 10th anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic, the delegation arrived in Beijing on the 16th of this month on its way home.

Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Premier of the State Council, went to the station to see off the distinguished guests. Those who saw them off at the station included: Lu Zhengcao, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Minister of Railways, Wang Shusheng, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and General and Vice Minister of National Defense, Wang Guangwei, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission, and Luo Guibo and Ji Pengfei, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs.

Wandel, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, also went to the station to see them off.

On their way home, Prime Minister Pham Van Dong and all the distinguished guests will also pass through Shanghai, Hangzhou and other places for visits. Chen Shuliang, Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of our country, and Tran Tu Binh, Vietnamese Ambassador to China, will accompany them. During their stay in Beijing, the distinguished guests visited the newly built Great Hall of the People, the Chinese History Museum, and the Beijing Railway Station. (Attached picture)

Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister Pham Van Dong shake hands warmly at the station. Xinhua News Agency reporter Yuan Kezhong

### 越南党政代表团离京回国  代表团在返国途中将访问上海杭州等地

1959-10-19
第2版()
专栏：

　　越南党政代表团离京回国
    代表团在返国途中将访问上海杭州等地
    新华社18日讯　越南劳动党中央政治局委员、越南民主共和国总理范文同率领的越南党政代表团，今天上午乘专车离开北京回国。代表团是在参加德意志民主共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼以后，在回国途中于本月16日到达北京的。
    中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来到车站欢送贵宾。到车站送行的还有：中共中央委员、铁道部副部长吕正操，中共中央委员、国防部副部长王树声大将，国家计划委员会副主任王光伟，外交部副部长罗贵波、姬鹏飞等。
    德意志民主共和国驻华大使汪戴尔也到车站送行。
    在回国途中，范文同总理和全体贵宾还将路过上海、杭州等地参观访问。我国外交部第二亚洲司司长陈叔亮，越南驻中国大使陈子平等，陪同他们前往。在北京期间，贵宾们曾经参观了新建的人民大会堂、中国历史博物馆和北京火车站。（附图片）
　　周总理和范文同总理在车站亲切握手。　新华社记者　袁克忠摄
