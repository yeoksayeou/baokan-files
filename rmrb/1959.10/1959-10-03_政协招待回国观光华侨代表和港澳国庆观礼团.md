Title: CPPCC Hosts Reception for Overseas Chinese Visiting Group and Hong Kong and Macao National Day Observation Group
Author:
Date: 1959-10-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd -- The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held a reception at the Beijing Hotel this afternoon to welcome the Overseas Chinese Visiting Group, the Hong Kong and Macao National Day Observation Group, and the tourism group of employees from foreign and Hong Kong and Macao banks and insurance companies.

Guo Moruo, Shen Junru, Chen Shutong, Bao Erhan, Pabala Gelielangjie, and Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, vice chairmen of the CPPCC National Committee, attended the reception.

He Xiangning, vice chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Fang Fang, deputy director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, also attended the reception.

Guo Moruo, vice chairman of the CPPCC National Committee, spoke at the meeting, expressing, on behalf of the CPPCC, a warm welcome to the Overseas Chinese Visiting Group, the Hong Kong and Macao National Day Observation Group, and the tourism group of employees from foreign and Hong Kong and Macao banks and insurance companies. Guo Moruo called on the representatives of overseas Chinese and compatriots in Hong Kong and Macao to convey the situation of the motherland to all overseas Chinese and compatriots in Hong Kong and Macao after they return.

Overseas Chinese visiting representative Situ Zan and Hong Kong and Macao compatriots representative Gao Zhuoxiong also spoke at the meeting. (Image attached)

### 政协招待回国观光华侨代表和港澳国庆观礼团

1959-10-03
第4版()
专栏：

    政协招待回国观光华侨代表和港澳国庆观礼团
    新华社2日讯  中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会今天下午在北京饭店举行酒会，欢迎回国观光华侨、港澳国庆观礼团和国外及港澳银行、保险公司职员观光团。
    政协全国委员会副主席郭沫若、沈钧儒、陈叔通、包尔汉、帕巴拉·卓列朗杰、阿沛·阿旺晋美出席了酒会。
    人大常委副委员长何香凝和华侨事务委员会副主任方方也出席了酒会。
    政协全国委员会副主席郭沫若在会上讲话，他代表政协对回国观光华侨，港澳国庆观礼团和国外及港澳银行、保险公司职员观光团表示热烈欢迎。郭沫若号召侨胞、港澳同胞代表回去后把祖国的情况传达给所有的侨胞和港澳同胞。
    回国观光华侨代表司徒赞和港澳同胞代表高卓雄也在会上讲了话。（附图片）
