Title: Adding Splendor to the Land of Rivers and Lakes North of the Great Wall (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1959-10-27
Page: 7

    The Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region has abundant coal reserves with a complete range of varieties, offering broad prospects for development. The region's raw coal production will increase from over 1.2 million tons in 1958 to 2.45 million tons this year. The picture shows the Shizuishan Coal Mine construction site under intense construction. (Photo by Wang Xinzhu)
    The Great Leap Forward of 1958 unveiled a new chapter in the history of the metallurgical industry of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region. On the basis of the mass steelmaking campaign in 1958, a joint iron and steel enterprise with an annual output of 50,000 tons of steel was established in Shizuishan. The picture shows a corner of the small blast furnace cluster at the Huinong Iron Smelting Plant. (Photo by Wang Xinzhu)
    The picture shows the Yellow River (West Bank) diversion station, one of the main projects of the Qingtongxia Water Conservancy Hub, currently under construction. (Photo by Wang Wenyu)
    In the first year after the establishment of people's communes, the autonomous region achieved an unprecedented bumper harvest in agricultural production. This year, the average grain yield per mu in the Yellow River irrigation area reached 401 jin. The picture shows members of the Bei Quan Brigade of the Yanghe People's Commune in Yongning County harvesting rice. (Photo by Ha Zhiping)
    Since the establishment of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region a year ago, the ranks of Hui and Han technical workers have continued to grow under the care and cultivation of the Party. This is worker Wang Wenzhou (right) and female worker Wang Yanli (Hui) inspecting the "softener" in the softening room. (Photos by Ha Zhiping and Wang Xinzhu)
    During the days of the Great Leap Forward, the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region established three institutions of higher learning: an agricultural college, a medical college, and a normal college. The picture shows first-year students at the Ningxia Medical College conducting general physics experiments under the guidance of a teacher. (Photo by Ha Zhiping)
    The world-renowned Tan sheep of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region have been increasing year after year. Flocks of sheep, resembling white clouds, can be seen everywhere in the autonomous region, roaming on the fertile natural pastures. (Photo by Wang Xinzhu)

### 塞上江南添锦绣（图片）

1959-10-27
第7版()
专栏：

    塞上江南添锦绣
    ?宁夏回族自治区煤炭储藏量丰富、品种齐全，有广阔的发展前途。自治区的原煤产量今年将由1958年的120多万吨提高到245万吨。图为正在紧张施工中的石嘴山煤矿工地。　　　　　（王新著摄）
    ?大跃进的1958年，揭开了宁夏回族自治区冶金工业历史上新的一页。在1958年全民大炼钢铁的基础上，石嘴山建立起了一座年产5万吨钢的钢铁联合企业。图为：惠农炼铁厂的小高炉群的一角。　　（王新著摄）
    ?图为正在施工中的青铜峡水利枢纽主体工程之一的河西（黄河西岸）渠首电站。
          （王文玉摄）
    ?人民公社化后的第一年，自治区农业生产获得了空前的大丰收。今年黄河灌区的粮食亩产量平均达到四百零一斤。图为永宁县养和人民公社北全大队的社员们正在收割水稻。　　（哈治平摄）
    ?宁夏回族自治区成立一年来，在党的关怀培养下，回、汉族技术工人队伍不断发展壮大。这是工人王文舟（右）和女工王燕丽（回族）在软化室检查“软化器”。（哈治平　王新著摄）
  ?宁夏回族自治区在大跃进的日子里，建立了农学院、医学院、师范学院等三所高等院校。图为：宁夏医学院一年级的同学们在老师的指导下进行普通物理实验。　（哈治平摄）
    ?宁夏回族自治区驰名中外的滩羊，连年不断地增加，在自治区到处都可看到犹如白云似的羊群，在肥沃的天然牧场上游动着。
    （王新著摄）
