Title: Ho Chi Minh and Zawadzki Sign Joint Communiqué; Grand Rally in Hanoi Welcomes Polish Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-10-12
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 11th: According to an announcement here tonight: The joint communiqué regarding the visit of the Chairman of the Council of State of the Polish People's Republic, Zawadzki, and the delegation of the Polish People's Republic to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam was signed at the Presidential Palace at 7:00 PM tonight. President Ho Chi Minh and Chairman Zawadzki signed the communiqué.

On the Vietnamese side, those participating in the signing ceremony included: Acting Premier of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Truong Chinh; Vice Premiers Vo Nguyen Giap and Pham Hung; Minister of Culture Hoang Minh Giam; Minister of Agriculture and Forestry Nghiem Xuan Yem; Minister of Education Nguyen Van Huyen; Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Nguyen Duc Duong; and Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to Poland, Hoang Van Tien.

On the Polish side, those participating in the signing ceremony included: Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic and Chairman of the Supreme Committee of the United Peasant Party of Poland, Stefan Ignar; Member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Edward Gierek; Vice Chairman of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Democratic Party of Poland and Member of Parliament, Jan Karol Wende; Secretary of the Council of State of the Polish People's Republic, Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Committee of the United Peasant Party of Poland, and Member of Parliament, Julian Horodecki; Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Polish People's Republic, P. Ogrodzinski; and Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Polish People's Republic in Vietnam, J. Gracok.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 11th: Over 120,000 people from all walks of life in Hanoi held a grand rally today in Ba Dinh Square to warmly welcome the delegation of the Polish People's Republic headed by Chairman Zawadzki.

When President Ho Chi Minh accompanied Chairman Zawadzki and other members of the Polish delegation onto the rostrum, thunderous applause erupted throughout the square. The masses waved colorful flags and flowers, paying tribute to the distinguished Polish guests.

In his speech at the rally, Chairman Zawadzki sternly condemned the imperialist forces for their acts of sabotage against the Geneva Accords. He stated that the Polish people fully support the peaceful policy of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the right of the Vietnamese people to peaceful reunification of their homeland as stipulated in the Geneva Accords.

Before Chairman Zawadzki's speech, Acting Chairman of the Hanoi Administrative Committee, Tran Danh Tuyen, delivered a speech. On behalf of the people of Hanoi, Tran Danh Tuyen presented a banner to Chairman Zawadzki. The banner read: "May the friendship between the Vietnamese and Polish peoples last forever."

President Ho Chi Minh also delivered a brief speech at the rally and led everyone in singing the song "Solidarity is Strength" at the end.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 11th: The Chairman of the Council of State of the Polish People's Republic, Zawadzki, held a grand banquet at the Hanoi International Club tonight to mark the end of the visit of the delegation of the Polish People's Republic to Vietnam.

President Ho Chi Minh attended the banquet.

Also attending the banquet were Acting Premier of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Truong Chinh, Vice Premiers Vo Nguyen Giap, Phan Ke Toai, and Pham Hung, ministers of various government departments, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Vietnam.

Chairman Zawadzki delivered a speech at the beginning of the banquet. He once again expressed his gratitude for the warm reception from the Vietnamese people. He said that regarding the achievements of the visit and the talks held with President Ho Chi Minh and the leaders of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, a joint communiqué has been signed. In this communiqué, we expressed our unanimous views on international issues related to our two countries. We also pointed out the importance and benefits of the exchange of experiences and the development of future cooperation in various aspects between our two countries.

Subsequently, President Ho Chi Minh delivered a speech, in which he expressed, on behalf of the Vietnamese people, the Vietnamese Labor Party, and the government, his heartfelt gratitude to Chairman Zawadzki for his enthusiastic speech to the Vietnamese people.

After recounting the disasters experienced by Vietnam and Poland in the past and the achievements of today's construction, President Ho Chi Minh said that the Vietnamese people are loyal friends of the Polish people and will always be grateful to the Polish people and government for their assistance to Vietnam.

### 胡志明和萨瓦茨基签署联合公报  河内盛大集会欢迎波兰代表团

1959-10-12
第5版()
专栏：

　　胡志明和萨瓦茨基签署联合公报
    河内盛大集会欢迎波兰代表团
    新华社河内11日电　据这里今晚公布：关于波兰人民共和国国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基和波兰人民共和国代表团访问越南民主共和国的联合公报今天晚上七时在主席府举行签字仪式。胡志明主席和萨瓦茨基主席在公报上签了字。
    越南方面参加签字仪式的有：越南民主共和国政府代理总理长征，副总理武元甲、范雄，文化部长黄明鉴，农林部长严春庵，教育部长阮文煊，外交部副部长阮德扬，越南民主共和国驻波兰大使黄文进。
    波兰方面参加签字仪式的有：波兰人民共和国部长会议副主席、波兰统一农民党最高委员会主席斯特凡·伊格纳尔，波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局委员、中央书记处书记爱德华·格雷克，波兰民主党中央委员会主席团副主席、议会议员杨·卡罗尔·温德，波兰人民共和国国务委员会秘书、波兰统一农民党最高委员会主席团委员、议会议员尤利安·霍罗德茨基，波兰人民共和国外交部部长助理普·奥格罗任斯基，波兰人民共和国驻越南大使馆临时代办约·格拉柯。
    据新华社河内11日电　河内各界十二万多人今天在巴亭广场举行盛大集会，热烈欢迎以萨瓦茨基主席为首的波兰人民共和国代表团。
    当胡志明主席陪同萨瓦茨基主席和波兰代表团其他成员走上主席台时，全场响起了雷鸣般的掌声，到会群众挥舞着各色彩旗和花朵，向波兰贵宾们致敬。
    萨瓦茨基主席在会上发表讲话时，严厉谴责帝国主义势力破坏日内瓦协议的行为。他表示波兰人民完全支持越南民主共和国政府的和平政策和日内瓦协议所规定的越南人民和平统一祖国的权利。
    在萨瓦茨基主席讲话前，河内行政委员会代理主席陈名宣致词。陈名宣代表河内市人民把一面锦旗赠给萨瓦茨基主席。锦旗上写着：“越波人民之间的情谊万古长春”。
    胡志明主席也在会上发表了简短的讲话，并且在最后领着大家唱“团结就是力量”的歌。
    新华社河内11日电　波兰人民共和国国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基今晚在河内国际俱乐部为他率领的波兰人民共和国代表团结束在越南的访问举行盛大宴会。
    胡志明主席出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的还有越南民主共和国代理总理长征，副总理武元甲、潘继遂、范雄和政府各部部长以及各国驻越南外交使节等。
    萨瓦茨基主席在宴会开始时发表讲话。他再一次对越南人民的热情接待表示感谢。他说，我们就访问的成就以及同胡志明主席和越南民主共和国政府的领导人进行的会谈签订了一项联合公报。在这项公报中，我们表示了对同我们两国有关的国际问题的一致观点。我们也指出了我们两国之间的经验交流和今后在各方面的合作的发展的重要性和好处。
    接着，胡志明主席讲话，他代表越南人民、越南劳动党和政府衷心感谢萨瓦茨基主席对越南人民所发表的充满热情的讲话。
    胡志明主席在叙述了越南和波兰两国过去所经历的灾难和今天的建设成就之后说，越南人民是波兰人民的忠实朋友，永远感谢波兰人民和政府对越南的帮助。
