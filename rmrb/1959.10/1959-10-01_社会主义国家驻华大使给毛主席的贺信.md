Author:
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 8

Congratulatory Messages to Chairman Mao from Ambassadors of Socialist Countries to China

Dear Comrade Mao Zedong:

    On this glorious and historically significant day—the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China—please allow us, in our own names and on behalf of the staff of the embassies of the socialist countries, to extend to you, and through you, to the great and hardworking Chinese people and their leadership—the Communist Party of China—our heartfelt congratulations on the great National Day.

    The people of the socialist countries of Europe and Asia, together with the freedom-loving people of your country, celebrate this festival, because the people of our countries share the same goal—striving for the complete victory of communism.

    The people of our countries watch with pride and infinite joy the significant achievements of the Chinese people in the cause of building socialism and in building a strong, industrialized socialist country in China. They give high praise to the firm policy full of proletarian internationalism spirit pursued by the Communist Party of China and the Government of the People's Republic of China which are fraternal, friendly and united with all the countries in the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union.

    The Communist Party of China and its Central Committee, as well as you personally, have made valuable contributions to the theory and practice of socialist construction by creatively applying Marxist-Leninist theory under Chinese conditions. As representatives of the governments of socialist countries stationed in your beautiful country, we have personally witnessed the smooth implementation of the general line of the Communist Party of China for building socialism, and the great achievements made by the Chinese people in economy, science, and culture in the process of realizing the Great Leap Forward. The people's communes, which were only established a year ago, have already demonstrated their vitality in achieving great achievements in agriculture and water conservancy construction.

    The People's Republic of China has become a strong pillar of the unbreakable unity of socialist countries by firmly pursuing a peaceful foreign policy and striving for the ideal of peace. Its prestige on the international stage is growing day by day. The attempts by the imperialist reactionaries and their lackeys to isolate China and slander China in front of their own people are futile. The policies of these dark forces are doomed to shameful failure.

    Dear Comrade Mao Zedong, please allow us to wish you and all the Chinese people even more fruitful work in achieving the great goal—the victory of socialism in your great country.

    Ambassador of the Hungarian People's Republic, Nográdi

    Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Li Yonghao

    Ambassador of the Czechoslovak Republic, Bušniak

    Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic, Wandel

    Ambassador of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, Panchevsky

    Ambassador of the Romanian People's Republic, Zaharescu

    Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic, Sharav

    Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Trần Tử Bình

    Ambassador of the People's Republic of Albania, Pàlluçi

    Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, Antonov

    Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish People's Republic, Flato

                        September 30, 1959, in Beijing

### 社会主义国家驻华大使给毛主席的贺信

1959-10-01
第8版()
专栏：

    社会主义国家驻华大使给毛主席的贺信亲爱的毛泽东同志：
    在此光辉的具有历史意义的日子——中华人民共和国建国十周年之际，请允许我们以自己的名义，并代表社会主义国家大使馆工作人员向你，并通过你，向伟大勤劳的中国人民及其领导力量——中国共产党衷心地祝贺伟大的国庆节。
  欧洲和亚洲社会主义各国人民同爱好自由的贵国人民一起庆祝这个节日，因为我们各国人民的目标相同——争取共产主义的完全胜利。
    我们各国人民怀着骄傲和无限欢欣的心情注视着中国人民在建设社会主义事业中、在中国建成强大的、工业化的社会主义国家的事业中所取得的重大成就。他们对中国共产党和中华人民共和国政府所奉行的坚定的、充满无产阶级国际主义精神的、同以苏联为首的社会主义阵营各国兄弟友好和团结的政策给予崇高的评价。
    中国共产党及其中央委员会以及你个人在中国条件下创造性地运用马克思列宁主义理论时对社会主义建设的理论和实践作出了宝贵的贡献。我们作为社会主义阵营各国政府派驻在你的美好国家的代表，亲眼看到中国共产党关于建设社会主义的总路线的顺利实现，看到中国人民在实现大跃进过程中在经济、科学文化等方面所取得的巨大成就。仅仅一年以前才成立的人民公社，已经在取得农业和水利建设方面的巨大成就中显示出了它的生命力。
    中华人民共和国在坚定地奉行爱好和平的外交政策和为实现和平的理想而努力奋斗时，成为社会主义各国牢不可破的团结的强大支柱。它在国际舞台上的威望与日俱增。帝国主义反动集团及其走狗想孤立中国和在本国人民面前诬蔑中国的图谋是徒劳无益的。这些黑暗势力的政策注定要遭到可耻的失败。
    亲爱的毛泽东同志，请允许我们祝你和全体中国人民为实现伟大目标——社会主义在你们伟大国家的胜利——而进行的工作更加富有成效。
    匈牙利人民共和国大使诺格拉第
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使李永镐
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国大使布希尼亚克
    德意志民主共和国大使汪戴尔
    保加利亚人民共和国大使潘切夫斯基
    罗马尼亚人民共和国大使查哈勒斯库
    蒙古人民共和国大使沙拉布
    越南民主共和国大使陈子平
    阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国大使帕里夫蒂
    苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟临时代办安东诺夫
