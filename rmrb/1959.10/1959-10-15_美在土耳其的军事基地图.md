Title: Map of US Military Bases in Turkey
Author: Luo Feng
Date: 1959-10-15
Page: 6

　　Map of US Military Bases in Turkey

　　Turkey occupies a very important position in the US aggression plan. Its territory is full of a network of American military bases, which seriously threatens the security of the Middle East. The map published here only shows the main US naval and air force bases in Turkey.

　　In recent years, about fifty new and rebuilt airfields have been constructed in Turkey, bringing the total to over one hundred, including places where aircraft can land. According to Turkish press reports, twenty-four large airfields will be completed by the end of 1960. Adana, Diyarbakir, Izmir, Ankara, and Istanbul are important existing air force bases for strategic air force use, all controlled by the US Air Force.

　　The largest US air force base in the Middle East is Adana. Bombers taking off from US air force bases in Morocco can use Adana as a relay station to transfer to the US air force base in Saudi Arabia. During the US invasion of Lebanon, the Adana air force base and several other airfields were frequently used by the United States to transfer personnel, equipment, and ammunition to Lebanon.

　　It is especially noteworthy that US imperialism is turning Turkey into a major atomic rocket base in the Middle East. Turkey has begun preparing to deploy US missiles. Shortly after the conclusion of a bilateral military agreement between the United States and Turkey, the United States immediately sent a military delegation to Turkey to discuss the issue of equipping the Turkish army with missiles. According to foreign press reports, the United States plans to establish fifteen medium-range missile "Thor" and "Jupiter" launch sites in the Anatolian region of Turkey. US imperialism is doing its utmost to turn Turkey into an atomic rocket base, which will further threaten the security of the Middle East region and neighboring countries of Turkey, and will aggravate the tense situation in that region. (Luo Feng)

### 美在土耳其的军事基地图
罗峰
1959-10-15
第6版()
专栏：

　　美在土耳其的军事基地图
    土耳其在美国侵略计划中占居极重要的地位。它的境内布满了美国军事基地网，使中近东地区的安全受到了严重威胁。这里刊登的一幅地图只画出了美国在土耳其的主要海空军基地。
    近年来，在土耳其境内新建和改建的飞机场约有五十个，连可供飞机着陆的场所在内，总计已超过一百个。据土耳其报刊报道，到1960年末还要建成二十四个大型飞机场。亚达那、狄雅倍基尔、伊斯密尔、安卡拉和伊斯坦布尔，是现有的可供战略空军使用的重要空军基地，都由美国空军控制。
    美国在中近东最大的空军基地是亚达那。美国在摩洛哥的空军基地起飞的轰炸机可以利用亚达那作为中继站转飞到沙特阿拉伯的美空军基地。在美国侵略黎巴嫩期间，亚达那空军基地及若干其它飞机场，频繁地被美国利用来向黎巴嫩转运兵员、装备和弹药。
    尤其值得注意的是美帝国主义正在把土耳其变成它在中近东的一个主要的原子火箭基地。土耳其已经开始准备配置美国导弹。不久前，在美土缔结双边军事协定后，美国立即派军事使团赴土耳其商讨以导弹装配土耳其军队问题。据外国报刊透露，美国准备在土耳其的安那托利亚地方建立十五个中程导弹“雷神式”和“丘辟特式”的发射场。美帝国主义极力要把土耳其变成原子火箭基地，这将使中近东地区和土耳其邻近国家的安全进一步受到威胁，并将加剧那个地区的紧张局势。　（罗峰）
