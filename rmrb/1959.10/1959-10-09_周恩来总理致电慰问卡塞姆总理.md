Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Sends Condolences to Premier Kassem
Author: 
Date: 1959-10-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 8th October. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China today sent a telegram to the Prime Minister of the Republic of Iraq, Kassem, expressing his condolences for his injury in the assassination attempt on October 7th. The telegram reads as follows: To His Excellency Abdul Karim Kassem, Prime Minister of the Republic of Iraq:

    I was shocked to learn that Your Excellency was injured in an assassination attempt on the afternoon of October 7th. On behalf of the Chinese government and people, and in my own name, I extend my sincere condolences to Your Excellency and wish you a speedy recovery. The Chinese people firmly condemn the subversion and assassination plots of the imperialists, and firmly believe that the united strength of the Iraqi people will surely shatter all the conspiracies of imperialism.

          Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                              October 8, 1959

### 周恩来总理致电慰问卡塞姆总理

1959-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理致电慰问卡塞姆总理
    新华社8日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天致电伊拉克共和国总理卡塞姆，对他在10月7日遇刺受伤表示慰问。电文如下：伊拉克共和国阿卜杜勒·卡里姆·卡塞姆总理阁下：
    惊悉阁下10月7日下午遇刺受伤，我谨代表中国政府和人民并以我个人的名义向阁下致以诚挚的慰问，并且祝阁下早日康复。中国人民坚决谴责帝国主义者的颠复和暗杀阴谋，并且深信，伊拉克人民团结的力量，一定能够粉碎帝国主义的一切阴谋。
          中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                              1959年10月8日
