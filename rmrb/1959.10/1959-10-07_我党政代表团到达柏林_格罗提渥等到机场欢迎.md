Title: Our Party and Government Delegation Arrives in Berlin; Grotewohl and Others Welcome at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1959-10-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, October 5th. At the invitation of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and the Government of the German Democratic Republic, the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, led by Nie Rongzhen, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Premier of the State Council, arrived in Berlin on the afternoon of the 5th to attend the celebrations for the tenth anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic.
    Welcoming the delegation at the airport were Otto Grotewohl, member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic; Mickenberg, member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party; Lothar Bolz, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic; Hermann Matern, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Germany; Marshal Zaharov, Commander-in-Chief of the Soviet Forces stationed in Germany, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to the German Democratic Republic.
    Wang Guoquan, Chinese Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, a member of the delegation, and personnel from the embassy also welcomed the delegation at the airport.
    Head of the delegation Nie Rongzhen, accompanied by Prime Minister Grotewohl, reviewed the guard of honor at the airport.
    According to reports here, Party and government delegations from eleven socialist countries and delegations from more than twenty fraternal parties in Europe, Asia, and Latin America have been invited to participate in the celebrations for the tenth anniversary of the German Democratic Republic.

### 我党政代表团到达柏林  格罗提渥等到机场欢迎

1959-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我党政代表团到达柏林
    格罗提渥等到机场欢迎
    新华社柏林5日电　应德国统一社会党和德意志民主共和国政府的邀请，前来参加德意志民主共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼的中国党政代表团，在中国共产党中央委员会委员、国务院副总理聂荣臻率领下，于5日下午到达柏林。
    到机场欢迎代表团的有德国统一社会党中央政治局委员、德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥，统一社会党中央政治局委员米肯伯格，德意志民主共和国副总理兼外交部长博尔茨，德国共产党中央委员会第一书记雷曼，驻德苏军部队总司令扎哈罗夫元帅和各国驻德意志民主共和国的外交使节。
    中国驻德意志民主共和国大使、代表团团员王国权和大使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
    代表团团长聂荣臻在格罗提渥总理陪同下在机场上检阅了仪仗队。
    据这里发表的消息，应邀参加德意志民主共和国十周年庆祝典礼的将有十一个社会主义国家的党政代表团和欧洲、亚洲和拉丁美洲二十多个兄弟党代表团。
