Title: Premier Pham Van Dong Arrives in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1959-10-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 19th October: The Vietnamese Party and Government Delegation, led by Pham Van Dong, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Labour Party and Premier of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, arrived in Shanghai this afternoon by special train from Beijing on their way home after attending the celebrations for the tenth anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic. They were accompanied by Chen Shuliang, Director of the Second Asian Department of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Tran Tu Binh, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China.

Welcoming the distinguished guests at the station were Cao Diqiu, Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Vice Mayor of Shanghai; Lieutenant General Wang Bicheng, Commander of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Zhang Yaohui, Deputy Secretary-General of the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee, and others.

After arriving in Shanghai, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Cao Diqiu and others, toured the city and the Shanghai No. 1 Department Store, visited the Children's Palace and the Shanghai Industrial Exhibition. Some of the guests also visited the site of the First National Congress of the Chinese Communist Party – the Shanghai Revolutionary History Memorial Hall.

In the evening, Ke Qingshi, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, First Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party,  and Mayor of Shanghai, hosted a banquet to welcome the distinguished guests.

### 范文同总理抵上海

1959-10-20
第5版()
专栏：

　　范文同总理抵上海
    新华社上海19日电　越南劳动党中央政治局委员、越南民主共和国总理范文同率领的越南党政代表团，在参加德意志民主共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼后，回国途中路经我国，今天下午由我国外交部第二亚洲司司长陈叔亮、越南民主共和国驻华大使陈子平陪同，从北京乘专车到达上海。
    到车站欢迎贵宾的有中共上海市委书记处书记、上海市副市长曹荻秋，中国人民解放军上海警备区司令员王必成中将、上海市人民委员会副秘书长张耀辉等。
    贵宾们到上海后，由曹荻秋等人陪同游览了市区和上海市第一百货商店，参观了少年宫和上海工业展览会。一部分客人还参观了中共第一次全国代表大会会址——上海革命历史纪念馆。
    晚间，中共中央政治局委员、中共上海市委第一书记、上海市市长柯庆施设宴欢迎贵宾。
