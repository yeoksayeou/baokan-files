Title: Cambodian Cultural Delegation Leaves Guangzhou for Home

Author:

Date: 1959-10-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 12th October: The Cambodian Cultural Delegation concluded its visit to our country and left Guangzhou by train this morning to return home. Responsible persons from relevant units in Guangzhou Municipality were at the station to see them off.

### 柬文化代表团离广州回国

1959-10-13
第5版()
专栏：

　　柬文化代表团离广州回国
    新华社广州12日电　柬埔寨文化代表团结束了在我国的参观访问，今天上午乘火车离开广州回国。到车站欢送的有广州市有关单位的负责人。
