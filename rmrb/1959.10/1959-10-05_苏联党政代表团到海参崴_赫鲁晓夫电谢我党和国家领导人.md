Title: Soviet Party and Government Delegation Arrives in Vladivostok; Khrushchev Sends Telegram Thanking Chinese Party and State Leaders
Author:
Date: 1959-10-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Vladivostok, 4th: According to TASS, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, arrived in Vladivostok on the 4th from Beijing.

Khrushchev received a warm welcome from the leaders of the Party, Soviet, and social organizations, as well as the working people, of the Soviet Far East.

When Khrushchev's "Il-18" aircraft arrived at the airport, those welcoming him extended warm greetings. Khrushchev got off the plane, approached those welcoming him, and greeted them.

Khrushchev rode in an open-top car from the airport to the city center. The roadsides were lined with welcoming crowds, and the route was decorated with many huge banners expressing welcome.

Xinhua News Agency, 4th: Comrade Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPSU, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and head of the Soviet Party and Government Delegation, sent a telegram to our Party and state leaders on his way back to the country, thanking them for their warm reception and cordial friendship. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To Comrade Mao Zedong, Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Comrade Zhu De, Comrade Zhou Enlai:

Dear friends, as I leave the borders of the fraternal People's Republic of China, I once again express my sincere and deep gratitude to you for the warm reception and cordial friendship extended to us – the loyal friends and brothers of the great people of China, the representatives of the Soviet Union. Dear friends, I warmly wish you, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Government of the People's Republic of China, and the great Chinese people, further achievements in socialist construction and in the struggle for the happiness of the people and world peace.

Head of the Soviet Party and Government Delegation Khrushchev
Sent on October 4, 1959, on board the Il-18, No. 75749.

### 苏联党政代表团到海参崴  赫鲁晓夫电谢我党和国家领导人

1959-10-05
第2版()
专栏：

    苏联党政代表团到海参崴
    赫鲁晓夫电谢我党和国家领导人
    新华社4日讯　据塔斯社海参崴4日讯：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记、苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫4日从北京抵达海参崴。
    赫鲁晓夫受到了苏联远东地区的党、苏维埃和社会组织的负责人以及劳动人民的热烈欢迎。
    当赫鲁晓夫乘“伊尔—一八”飞机到达机场的时候，欢迎者向他热烈致意。赫鲁晓夫下飞机后走近欢迎者，并向他们问好。
    赫鲁晓夫乘坐敞篷汽车从机场驶向市区。道路两旁站满了欢迎人群，沿途装饰着许多表示欢迎的巨幅标语。
    新华社4日讯　苏共中央第一书记、苏联部长会议主席、苏联党政代表团团长赫鲁晓夫同志乘飞机回国途中，打电报给我国党和国家领导人员，感谢对他们的热情接待和亲切友情，电文如下：毛泽东同志，刘少奇同志，朱德同志，周恩来同志：
    亲爱的朋友们，在离开兄弟的中华人民共和国国境的时候，我再一次向你们表示诚挚的、深切的谢意，感谢对我们——伟大的人民中国忠实的朋友和兄弟苏联的代表——所给予的热情的接待和亲切的友情。亲爱的朋友们，热烈地祝你们，中国共产党中央委员会、中华人民共和国政府和伟大的全中国人民在社会主义建设和争取人民的幸福和世界和平的斗争中获得进一步的成就。
    苏联党政代表团团长　赫鲁晓夫
    1959年10月4日在伊尔一八型
    第七五七四九号飞机上发。
