Title: Subandrio Says in Tokyo That China Hopes for Relaxation of International Situation
Author:
Date: 1959-10-15
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, October 13th: Indonesian Foreign Minister Subandrio said in Tokyo today that he felt he had reached a basic understanding with China regarding the necessity of easing international tensions.
　　Having just visited China, Subandrio also said that he felt China supported holding top-level talks to ease world tensions. He said that China understands the world's desire to ease tensions and not engage in armed conflict, so that neither large nor small countries need to rely on force.
　　Subandrio will be in Japan for a five-day visit. It is reported that he will hold talks with Japanese government leaders on economic relations between the two countries.

### 苏班德里约外长在东京说  中国希望国际局势缓和

1959-10-15
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏班德里约外长在东京说
    中国希望国际局势缓和
    新华社13日讯　东京消息：印度尼西亚外交部长苏班德里约今天在东京说：他感觉到在关于缓和国际紧张局势的必要性方面他已经同中国取得了基本的了解。
    刚访问过中国的苏班德里约还说：他感到中国支持举行最高级会谈来缓和世界的紧张局势。他说：中国了解世界上这种愿望：缓和紧张局势和不进行武装，这样，不论大国或小国都不需要依靠武力。
    苏班德里约将在日本作为时五天的访问。据报道，他将同日本政府首脑会谈两国间的经济关系问题。
