Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions, All-China Women's Federation, Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China Send Congratulatory Telegrams on the National Day of the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1959-10-07
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 6th - The All-China Federation of Trade Unions Chairman Liu Ningyi, the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China, and the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China, respectively sent telegrams to the Chairman of the Free German Trade Union Federation, the German Democratic Women's Federation, and the Free German Youth League, warmly congratulating the 10th anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic.

    The congratulatory telegrams pointed out that in the past ten years, under the leadership of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and the government, the German people have achieved brilliant achievements in economic and cultural construction and the struggle to defend peace. The congratulatory telegrams wished the people of the German Democratic Republic new achievements in socialist construction, the struggle to conclude a peace treaty with Germany and reunify Germany, and defend peace. The congratulatory telegrams also wished the friendship between the working people of the two countries to be increasingly consolidated and developed.

### 我国总工会、妇联、共青团中央  电贺德意志民主共和国国庆

1959-10-07
第2版()
专栏：

    我国总工会、妇联、共青团中央
    电贺德意志民主共和国国庆
    新华社6日讯　中华全国总工会主席刘宁一、中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会、中国共产主义青年团中央委员会，分别打电报给自由德国工会联合会主席、德国民主妇女联合会、自由德国青年联盟，热烈祝贺德意志民主共和国成立十周年。
    这些贺电中指出，十年来，德国人民在德国统一社会党和政府的领导下，在经济、文化建设和保卫和平的斗争中取得了辉煌的成就。贺电祝德意志民主共和国人民在社会主义建设、争取缔结对德和约和重新统一德国以及保卫和平的斗争中取得新的成就。贺电并祝两国劳动人民的友谊日益巩固和发展。
