Title: International Democratic Women's Federation Council Passes Resolution to Organize Broad Movement for Complete Disarmament
Author:
Date: 1959-10-17
Page: 6
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 16th: The Council of the International Democratic Women's Federation held a meeting in Prague from the 12th to the 15th. The Council decided to actively celebrate the 50th anniversary of International Women's Day "March 8th" (1960) and decided to organize a broad movement to strive for complete and thorough disarmament.

　　The resolution of the International Democratic Women's Federation Council on member organizations in various countries calls on member organizations in various countries to launch broad movements to strive for complete and thorough disarmament, the prohibition of nuclear testing, the end of the Cold War, and the resolution of all unresolved issues through negotiations. The Council decided to convene an international women's conference for disarmament in December of this year.

　　The resolution also called on member organizations of the International Democratic Women's Federation in various countries to begin preparing for the celebration of the 50th anniversary of International Women's Day and suggested that member organizations in various countries deploy national, regional, and international conferences and women's conferences to discuss important issues related to all aspects of peace.

　　Mrs. Kalman Szántó, Secretary-General of the International Democratic Women's Federation, delivered a report on the International Democratic Women's Federation's participation in the 50th anniversary celebrations of International Women's Day "March 8th" on the 12th.

　　The conference held general speeches on the 13th and 14th.

　　Li Dequan, head of the Chinese delegation, said in a speech at the conference on the 14th that women's actions should be linked to the cause of defending peace of the people all over the world. She talked about the warm support and endorsement of Chinese women for the comprehensive and thorough disarmament proposal put forward by Khrushchev, and she expressed her active support for the World Women's Conference for Disarmament proposed by the International Democratic Women's Federation. In her speech, she also talked about the contributions made by liberated Chinese women in the socialist construction of the motherland.

### 国际民主妇联理事会通过决定  组织广泛的争取全面裁军运动

1959-10-17
第6版()
专栏：

　　国际民主妇联理事会通过决定
    组织广泛的争取全面裁军运动
    据新华社布拉格16日电　国际民主妇女联合会理事会12日至15日在布拉格举行了会议。理事会决定积极庆祝国际“三八”妇女节五十周年（1960年），决定组织广泛的运动以争取全面彻底裁军。
    国际民主妇联理事会关于所属各国会员组织的决议，号召各国会员组织发动广泛的运动，来争取全面彻底裁军、禁止核试验、结束冷战、通过谈判的途径解决一切没有解决的问题。理事会决定于本年12月召开一次争取裁军的国际妇女会议。
    决议同时号召国际民主妇联所属各国会员组织开始为庆祝国际妇女节五十周年进行准备工作，并建议各国会员组织部署全国规模的、区域性的和国际规模的会议和妇女会议，来讨论有关和平的各方面的重大问题。
    国际民主妇女联合会总书记卡尔曼·桑蒂夫人12日作了关于国际民主妇女联合会参加国际“三八”妇女节五十周年纪念活动的报告。
    会议在13日和14日进行大会发言。
    中国代表团团长李德全在14日的大会上发言说，要把妇女的行动和当前全世界人民保卫和平的事业联系起来。她谈到中国妇女对赫鲁晓夫所提出的全面彻底裁军的建议的热烈支持和拥护，她表示积极支持国际妇联所提出的召开世界妇女争取裁军的会议。她在发言中还谈到解放了的中国妇女在祖国的社会主义建设中所作的贡献。
