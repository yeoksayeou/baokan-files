Title: Li Xiannian Receives Advanced Workers in Finance and Trade
Author:
Date: 1959-10-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: Vice Premier of the State Council, Li Xiannian, received more than 200 advanced workers in finance and trade from various regions on October 3rd, who came to Beijing to participate in the National Day celebrations. Vice Premier Li Xiannian encouraged them to connect with the masses, rely on the masses, better serve production, serve consumers, and do a good job in the current procurement and supply work. Present at the reception were Ma Mingfang, Minister of the Central Finance and Trade Department of the CPC Central Committee, and responsible comrades from the Fifth Office of the State Council, various departments of the finance and trade system, and the People's Bank of China.

The advanced finance and trade workers from various regions who came to Beijing have attended various celebration activities for the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People’s Republic of China in recent days.

### 李先念接见财贸先进工作者

1959-10-06
第4版()
专栏：

    李先念接见财贸先进工作者
    新华社讯　国务院副总理李先念于10月3日接见了从各地来京参加国庆观礼的二百多位财贸先进工作者。李先念副总理鼓励他们要联系群众，依靠群众，更好地为生产服务，为消费者服务，做好当前的收购和供应工作。接见时在座的有中共中央财贸部部长马明方，以及国务院第五办公室、财贸系统各部和中国人民银行的负责同志。
    各地来京的财贸先进工作者，连日出席了建国十周年各种庆祝活动。
