Title: Mrs. Vittorio Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 30th — Mrs. Anita Vittorio, wife of the former Chairman of the World Federation of Trade Unions, Vittorio, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to visit our country and participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

### 维多里奥夫人到京

1959-10-01
第7版()
专栏：

    维多里奥夫人到京
    新华社30日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请，来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的世界工联前主席维多里奥的夫人阿尼塔·维多里奥今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
