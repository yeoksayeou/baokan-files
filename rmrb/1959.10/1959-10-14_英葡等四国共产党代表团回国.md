Title: Communist Party Delegations from Britain, Portugal and Other Countries Return Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 13th: The British Communist Party delegation headed by Comrade Harry Pollitt, the Portuguese Communist Party delegation, and Comrade Zvi Brestain of the Israeli Communist Party, after participating in the celebrations of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and visiting various parts of our country, left Beijing by the same plane this morning to return home.

Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, Comrade Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, Comrade Chen Yi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and Wu Xiuquan, member of the Central Committee, were at the airport to see them off.

Xinhua News Agency, October 13th: Comrade K.P. de Silva, head of the Ceylon Communist Party delegation, and Comrade P. Kumarasiri, a member of the delegation, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home after participating in the celebrations of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and visiting Beijing.

Comrade Chen Yi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Comrade Li Fuchun, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, and Wu Xiuquan, member of the Central Committee, were at the airport to see them off.

One member of the delegation also left Beijing by plane today to visit Shenyang. He will continue to visit our country before returning home.

### 英葡等四国共产党代表团回国

1959-10-14
第5版()
专栏：

　　英葡等四国共产党代表团回国
    新华社13日讯　以哈里·波立特同志为首的英国共产党代表团，葡萄牙共产党代表团，以色列共产党代表兹维·布莱斯坦同志，在参加了中华人民共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼和在我国各地访问之后，今天上午同乘一架飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的，有中共中央副主席刘少奇，中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记彭真，中共中央政治局委员陈毅、中央委员伍修权。
    新华社13日讯　锡兰共产党代表团团长克·普·德·席尔瓦同志和代表团团员普·库玛拉西里同志，在参加了中华人民共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼和在北京访问之后，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的，有中共中央政治局委员陈毅，中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记李富春，中央委员伍修权。
    代表团的一位团员，也在今天乘飞机离开北京去沈阳访问，他将在我国继续参观访问后回国。
