Title: Our Youth Musician Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-16
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 14th: The Chinese Youth Musician Delegation, invited by the Albanian Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations to participate in Albania's celebrations of the 10th anniversary of the founding of China, left here today to return home after a half-month of performance tours in Albania.

### 我青年音乐家代表团回国

1959-10-16
第5版()
专栏：

　　我青年音乐家代表团回国
    新华社地拉那14日电　应阿尔巴尼亚对外文化联络委员会的邀请，前来参加阿尔巴尼亚庆祝中国建国十周年活动的中国青年音乐家代表团在阿尔巴尼亚进行了为期半个月的访问演出之后，今天离开这里回国。
