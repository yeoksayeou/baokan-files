International Briefs

	British and French Foreign Ministers to Meet in Mid-November

	London Dispatch: The British and French Foreign Ministries announced on the 17th that British Foreign Secretary Lloyd will meet with French Foreign Minister Couve de Murville in Paris on November 11th and 12th.

	It is reported that the two foreign ministers will discuss the following issues: attitudes towards an East-West summit conference, issues concerning the Atlantic group, and European political and economic cooperation.

	Subandrio Visits the Philippines

	Manila Dispatch: Indonesian Foreign Minister Subandrio arrived in Manila by plane on the afternoon of the 17th after concluding a two-day visit to Japan, and will visit the Philippines for two days.

### 国际简讯

1959-10-19
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    英法外长定11月中会谈
    伦敦讯　英、法两国外交部17日宣布，英国外交大臣劳埃德将在11月11日和12日在巴黎同法国外交部长德姆维尔举行会谈。
    据报道，两国外交部长将讨论下面这些问题：对东西方最高级会议的态度，大西洋集团问题和欧洲政治与经济合作问题。
    苏班德里约访问菲律宾
    马尼拉讯　印度尼西亚外交部长苏班德里约17日下午结束了在日本的两天访问后，乘飞机到达马尼拉，将在菲律宾访问两天。
