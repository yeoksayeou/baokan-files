Title: Ministry of National Defense Hosts Banquet for PLA Representatives
Author:
Date: 1959-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th October: The Ministry of National Defense of the People's Republic of China held a banquet today to entertain all members of the delegation of the Chinese People's Liberation Army from various units who came to Beijing to attend the National Day 10th Anniversary celebrations, representatives of various colleges and units who participated in the National Day 10th Anniversary parade, the People's Liberation Army art troupe which presented performances for the National Day 10th Anniversary celebrations, and representatives of the military sports delegation participating in the National Games.

The banquet was presided over by Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense, Marshal Lin Biao. The atmosphere at the meeting was filled with joy and enthusiasm. The officers and soldiers frequently raised their glasses to congratulate the victory of the Party's general line for socialist construction, the great leap forward in socialist construction, the victory of the people's communes, and the health of our great leader Chairman Mao.

Marshal Lin Biao spoke at the banquet. He said that over the past decade, our motherland's socialist construction, especially since the great leap forward in 1958, has achieved extremely great achievements in industry, agriculture, science, culture, and education. These brilliant achievements, such high-speed development, are the result of the wise leadership of our Party and Comrade Mao Zedong, the result of the firm implementation by the people of the whole country of the Party's general line of going all out, aiming high, and achieving greater, faster, better, and more economical results in building socialism, and the result of the assistance from fraternal socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union. He then called on all officers and soldiers to strengthen political study, strengthen military training, strive to improve tactical and technical levels, and build our army into a modern revolutionary army, faithfully fulfilling the glorious tasks of defending the socialist construction of the motherland and participating in socialist construction.

Present at today's banquet were Marshal Luo Ronghuan, Marshal Ye Jianying, General Tan Zheng, Naval General Xiao Jingguang, General Xu Guangda, and the heads of the General Staff Department, the General Political Department, and various services and arms.

### 国防部欢宴解放军代表

1959-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    国防部欢宴解放军代表
    新华社6日讯　中华人民共和国国防部今天举行宴会，招待来京的中国人民解放军各部队参加建国十周年大典观礼代表团的全体人员、参加建国十周年大典接受检阅的各院校和部队代表、向建国十周年献礼演出的人民解放军文艺代表队和参加全国运动会的部队体育代表团的代表。
    宴会由副总理兼国防部部长林彪元帅主持。会上充满了欢乐热烈的气氛，到会官兵们频频为党的社会主义建设总路线的胜利、社会主义建设的大进跃、人民公社的胜利和我们伟大领袖毛主席健康举杯祝贺。
    林彪元帅在宴会上讲了话。他说，十年以来，我们祖国的社会主义建设，特别是1958年大进跃以来，我国在工业、农业、科学文化教育事业各方面取得了极其伟大的成就。这些辉煌成就，这样高速度的发展，是我们的党和毛泽东同志英明领导的结果，是全国人民坚决执行党的鼓足干劲，力争上游，多快好省地建设社会主义总路线的结果，是以苏联为首的各兄弟社会主义国家对我们援助的结果。接着，他号召全体官兵加强政治学习，加强军事训练，努力提高战术和技术水平，把我军建设成为一支现代化的革命军队，忠实地执行保卫祖国社会主义建设和参加社会主义建设的光荣任务。
    出席今天宴会的有罗荣桓元帅、叶剑英元帅、谭政大将、萧劲光海军大将、许光达大将和总参谋部、总政治部、各军种各兵种的负责人等。
