Title: Belgian Communist Party Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 26th. The Belgian Communist Party delegation headed by Comrade Frans Van den Brande returned to Beijing by plane today from Wuhan after visiting other places. The distinguished guests successively visited Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Wuhan and other cities.

### 比利时共产党代表团回到北京

1959-10-27
第5版()
专栏：

    比利时共产党代表团回到北京
    新华社26日讯　以弗朗斯·范登布兰敦同志为首的比利时共产党代表团在外地访问之后，今天由武汉乘飞机回到北京。贵宾们曾先后参观访问了上海、杭州、广州、武汉等城市。
