Title: Turkish Communist Party Delegation Leaves China
Author:
Date: 1959-10-09
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The Turkish Communist Party Delegation, after participating in the celebrations for the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and visiting various parts of our country, left China by plane this afternoon.

### 土耳其共产党代表团离开我国

1959-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

    土耳其共产党代表团离开我国
    新华社8日讯　土耳其共产党代表团在参加了中华人民共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼和在我国各地参观访问之后，今天下午乘飞机离开中国。
