Title: French Communist Party Central Office Attacked by Thugs; "l'Humanité" Calls on Republicans to Be Vigilant
Author:
Date: 1959-10-30
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, October 28th, Paris Dispatch: On the 27th, the central committee office of the French Communist Party was attacked by a thug who threw a bottle containing an incendiary agent, causing a fire. The flames were quickly extinguished, and no significant damage was sustained.

    "l'Humanité" emphasized today that "this fascist act committed in the center of Paris should alert Republicans to be vigilant."

### 法共中央办公处被暴徒袭击  “人道报”号召共和人士提高警惕

1959-10-30
第6版()
专栏：

    法共中央办公处被暴徒袭击
    “人道报”号召共和人士提高警惕
    新华社28日讯　巴黎消息：27日，法国共产党中央委员会办公处被一名暴徒投掷装有燃烧剂的瓶子，以致起火。火焰很快被扑灭，未受重大损失。
    “人道报”今天强调指出，“在巴黎市中心干出的这种法西斯勾当应当引起共和人士提高警惕”。
