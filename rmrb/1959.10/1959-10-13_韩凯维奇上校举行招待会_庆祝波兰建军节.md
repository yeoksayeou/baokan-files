Title: Colonel Hankiewicz Holds Reception to Celebrate Polish Army Day
Author:
Date: 1959-10-13
Page: 5

　　Colonel Hankiewicz Holds Reception
    Celebrating Polish Army Day
    Xinhua News Agency, 12th - Colonel Hankiewicz, Military Attaché of the Polish People's Republic to China, held a reception tonight to celebrate the 16th anniversary of the founding of the Polish People's Army.

　　General Tan Zheng, Deputy Minister of National Defense and Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, was invited to attend the reception. He extended warm congratulations to the fraternal Polish People's Army on behalf of all officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

　　General Tan Zheng said that the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Polish People's Army are both people's armies led by Marxist-Leninist parties. The thought of Marxism-Leninism closely links the hearts of the people and armies of our two countries. Our task is to defend the peaceful labor of our own people, to defend the integrity of our national territorial sovereignty, and to defend world peace. We neither threaten any country in the world, nor will we ever allow others to invade us. Therefore, the friendship between the Chinese and Polish armies, which have common principles of army building and goals of struggle, is eternal.

　　Colonel Hankiewicz spoke first at the reception. He said that now, the Polish People's Army, equipped with new weapons and receiving regular training, is stronger than ever before. It firmly defends the rights of the working people and vigilantly safeguards the achievements of socialist construction. In his speech, Colonel Hankiewicz praised the ever-strengthening friendship between the armies of Poland and China.

　　The following senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army were invited to attend the reception: General Xu Guangda, General Zhang Zongxun, General Xiao Hua, General Song Shilun, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General of the Air Force Wu Faxian, and others, as well as Zeng Yongquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs.

　　Mr. Furato, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China, attended the reception.

　　Diplomatic envoys and military attachés of various countries to China were also invited to attend the reception.

### 韩凯维奇上校举行招待会  庆祝波兰建军节

1959-10-13
第5版()
专栏：

　　韩凯维奇上校举行招待会
    庆祝波兰建军节
    据新华社12日讯　波兰人民共和国驻华武官韩凯维奇上校今晚举行招待会，庆祝波兰人民军建军十六周年。
    国防部副部长、中国人民解放军总政治部主任谭政大将应邀出席招待会。他代表中国人民解放军全体官兵向兄弟般的波兰人民军致以热烈的祝贺。
    谭政大将说，中国人民解放军和波兰人民军，都是马克思列宁主义的政党所领导的人民军队，马克思列宁主义的思想把我们两国人民和军队的心紧紧地连结在一起，我们的任务都是保卫自己祖国人民的和平劳动、保卫祖国领土主权的完整和世界和平。我们既不威胁世界上任何国家，也绝不允许别人侵略我们。因此，具有共同建军原则和奋斗目标的中波两国军队之间的友谊，是永恒的。
    韩凯维奇上校在招待会上首先讲话。他说，现在，装备有新式武器和受到正规训练的波兰人民军比过去任何时候更为强大，它坚定地保卫劳动人民的权利，时刻警惕地捍卫着社会主义建设的成果。韩凯维奇上校在讲话中盛赞了波中两国军队之间日益加强的友谊。
    应邀出席招待会的有：许光达大将、张宗逊上将、萧华上将、宋时轮上将、萧向荣中将、吴法宪空军中将等中国人民解放军高级将领，外交部副部长曾涌泉。
    波兰驻我国大使馆临时代办傅拉托出席了招待会。
    各国驻华使节和武官也应邀出席招待会。
