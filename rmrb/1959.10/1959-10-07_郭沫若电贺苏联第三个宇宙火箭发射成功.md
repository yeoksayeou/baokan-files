Title: Guo Moruo Sends Congratulatory Telegram on the Successful Launch of the Soviet Union's Third Cosmic Rocket
Author: Guo Moruo
Date: 1959-10-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, sent a telegram to A.N. Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, congratulating him on the successful launch of the Soviet Union's third cosmic rocket. The text of the telegram is as follows:

Respected President A.N. Nesmeyanov:

The successful launch of the Soviet Union's third cosmic rocket, establishing an automatic interplanetary station orbiting the moon, marks another great step forward in the conquest of outer space. Soviet scientists and technicians have once again made outstanding contributions, fully demonstrating the superiority of the socialist system. On behalf of the Chinese scientific workers, I extend to you, and through you to all Soviet scientific workers, my warmest congratulations. I wish you and the Soviet scientific workers even greater achievements in future scientific endeavors.

President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Guo Moruo

### 郭沫若电贺苏联第三个宇宙火箭发射成功
郭沫若
1959-10-07
第6版()
专栏：

    郭沫若电贺苏联第三个宇宙火箭发射成功
    新华社6日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若打电报给苏联科学院院长?斯米扬诺夫，祝贺苏联第三个宇宙火箭发射成功。电文如下：敬爱的?斯米扬诺夫院长：
    苏联的第三个宇宙火箭的发射成功，建立了围绕月球飞行的自动行星际站，在征服宇宙空间方面又迈进了一大步。苏联的科学技术工作者再一次作出了卓越的贡献，充分地证明了社会主义制度的优越性。谨代表中国科学工作者向您，并通过您向全体苏联科学工作者，致以最热烈的祝贺。并祝您和苏联科学工作者，在今后的科学事业上取得更辉煌的成就。
  　中国科学院院长　郭沫若
