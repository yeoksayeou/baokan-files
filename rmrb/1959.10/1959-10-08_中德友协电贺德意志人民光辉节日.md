Title: Sino-German Friendship Association Sends Congratulatory Telegram for the Glorious Festival of the German People
Author:
Date: 1959-10-08
Page: 2
    
Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The Sino-German Friendship Association of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram to the National Council of the National Front of Democratic Germany on the 6th, warmly congratulating the tenth anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic. The telegram said: "On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic, we extend to you our warm and fraternal congratulations. Over the past ten years, under the leadership of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, the German Democratic Republic has made brilliant achievements in the cause of socialist construction. We are greatly encouraged and delighted by your achievements. We sincerely wish you new and more splendid victories in the cause of socialist construction."

### 中德友协电贺德意志人民光辉节日

1959-10-08
第2版()
专栏：

    中德友协电贺德意志人民光辉节日
    新华社7日讯　中华人民共和国德意志民主共和国友好协会6日致电民主德国全国阵线全国委员会，热烈祝贺德意志民主共和国成立十周年。电文说：值此德意志民主共和国成立十周年之际，谨向你们致以热烈的、兄弟般的祝贺。十年来，德意志民主共和国在德国统一社会党的领导下，在社会主义建设事业中取得了辉煌的成就。我们对于你们的成就感到莫大的欢欣鼓舞。衷心祝贺你们在社会主义建设事业中获得新的、更辉煌的胜利。
