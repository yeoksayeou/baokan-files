Title: Šimůnek Says at Prague Celebration Meeting that China's Achievements Transcend Conventional Measures
Author:
Date: 1959-10-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 30th: The Central Committee of the Czechoslovak National Front held a grand meeting here tonight to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

    Czechoslovak party and government leaders Köpeczki, Jankovcová, Krutina, Kautsky, and diplomatic envoys from socialist countries to Prague attended the celebration.

    Šimůnek, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and Deputy Prime Minister of Czechoslovakia, spoke at the celebration. He said that October 1, 1949, was one of the fundamental milestones in the development of the entire world socialist system, and also one of the fundamental milestones in the entire modern history. He emphasized that the achievements of the Chinese people have surpassed all conventional measures. China has become a peaceful and powerful country building socialism on a broad front.

    In his report, Šimůnek warmly praised the great achievements of the Chinese people in socialist construction. Finally, Šimůnek also gave high praise to the Chinese Communist Party for its contribution to safeguarding the purity of Marxism-Leninism and strengthening the unity of the world communist movement.

    Ambassador Cao Ying also spoke at the meeting.

### 西穆涅克在布拉格庆祝大会上说中国的成就超越了常规的尺度

1959-10-02
第6版()
专栏：

    西穆涅克在布拉格庆祝大会上说
    中国的成就超越了常规的尺度
    据新华社布拉格9月30日电　捷克斯洛伐克民族阵线中央委员会今晚在这里举行了庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年的盛大集会。
    捷克斯洛伐克党政领导人柯别茨基、扬科夫卓娃、克鲁蒂纳、考茨基等和社会主义国家驻布拉格外交使节出席了庆祝会。
    捷共中央政治局委员、捷克斯洛伐克副总理西穆涅克在庆祝会上讲了话。他说，1949年10月1日是整个世界社会主义体系的发展中的根本性的里程碑之一，也是整个现代史上的根本性的里程碑之一。他强调指出，中国人民所取得的成就超越了一切常规的尺度。中国已变成在广阔的战线上建设社会主义的和平强国。
    西穆涅克在报告中热烈赞扬中国人民在社会主义建设中的伟大成就。西穆涅克最后还对中国共产党为维护马克思列宁主义的纯洁性、为加强世界共产主义运动的团结作出的贡献，给予了崇高的评价。
    曹瑛大使也在会上讲了话。
