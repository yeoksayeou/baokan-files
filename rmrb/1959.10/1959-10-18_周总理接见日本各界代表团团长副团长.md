Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Heads and Deputy Heads of Japanese Delegations from All Walks of Life
Author:
Date: 1959-10-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 17th: Premier Zhou Enlai received Tetsuji Katayama, head of the Japanese delegation from all walks of life celebrating the 10th anniversary of the founding of China, and deputy heads Tadashi Obata, Hitoshi Shiraishi, and Masato Honda this afternoon. (Attached picture)
    The scene of Premier Zhou Enlai receiving the heads and deputy heads of the Japanese delegation from all walks of life. From left: Zhou Enlai, Tetsuji Katayama, Tadashi Obata, Hitoshi Shiraishi, Masato Honda.
                        Xinhua News Agency reporter Tajikule photographed

### 周总理接见日本各界代表团团长副团长

1959-10-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　周总理接见日本各界代表团团长副团长
　　新华社17日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了日本各界庆祝中国建国十周年代表团团长片山哲，副团长小畑忠良、白石凡、本多正登。（附图片）
    周恩来总理接见日本各界代表团团长和副团长时的情形。左起：周恩来、片山哲、小畑忠良、白石凡、本多正登。
                        新华社记者  塔吉古勒摄
