    (Left Two) The Ministry of Culture held a Peking Opera evening on October 2nd, with performances of "Mu Guiying Takes Command" by the famous performing artist Mei Lanfang and others, to entertain distinguished guests from various countries who came to China to participate in the 10th anniversary celebration of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The Vietnamese Party and government delegation headed by President Ho Chi Minh, the Mongolian Party and government delegation headed by Chairman Tsedenbal, the Hungarian Party and government delegation headed by Chairman Dobi, and others attended the performance and took photos with the actors on stage after the performance.
              Xinhua News Agency Reporter Qi Guanshan

专栏：

    （左二）文化部10月2日举行京剧晚会，由著名表演艺术家梅兰芳等演出“穆桂英挂帅”，招待来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的各国贵宾。以胡志明主席为首的越南党政代表团，以泽登巴尔主席为首的蒙古党政代表团，以道比主席为首的匈牙利党政代表团等出席观看了演出，并在演出结束后上台和演员们合影
              新华社记者　齐观山摄
