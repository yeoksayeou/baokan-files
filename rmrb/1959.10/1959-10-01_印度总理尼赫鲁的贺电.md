Title: Telegram from Indian Prime Minister Nehru

Author:

Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 8

To His Excellency Mr. Zhou Enlai, Premier of the People's Republic of China:

    On this day of your national celebration, I would like to extend my congratulations to Your Excellency, and through you, to the Government and people of the People's Republic of China, on behalf of my colleagues in the Indian Government and in my own name, and sincerely hope that the people of your country will progress and prosper.

    I take this opportunity to express my hope that the friendly relations between our two countries will be further strengthened in the years to come.

                                  Jawaharlal Nehru

### 印度总理尼赫鲁的贺电

1959-10-01
第8版()
专栏：

    印度总理尼赫鲁的贺电
    中华人民共和国总理周恩来先生阁下：
    在你们举国庆祝的日子里，我谨代表印度政府中的同事并以我个人的名义向阁下，并通过你向中华人民共和国政府和人民致贺，并诚挚地希望贵国人民进步和昌盛。
    我借此机会表示希望我们两国之间的友好关系在今后的年代里将日益加强。
                                  贾瓦哈拉尔·尼赫鲁
