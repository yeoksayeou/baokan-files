Title: Our Inner Mongolia Song and Dance Troupe Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, October 17th: The Chinese Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Song and Dance Troupe concluded its performance tour in Mongolia and returned home by train this morning.
	Mongolian Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Ramsuren, held a reception last night to bid farewell to the delegation.

### 我内蒙古歌舞团回国

1959-10-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　我内蒙古歌舞团回国
    新华社乌兰巴托17日电　中国内蒙古自治区歌舞团结束了在蒙古的访问演出后，今天上午从这里乘火车回国。
    蒙古部长会议副主席拉姆苏伦昨天晚上曾举行招待会，欢送代表团。
