Title: Two Brazilian Guests Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, September 30th. At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, members of the Brazilian Cultural Delegation, Guilherme de Oliveira Figueiredo and his wife, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of September 30 to visit China and participate in the celebrations for the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

### 巴西两位客人到京

1959-10-01
第7版()
专栏：

    巴西两位客人到京
    新华社30日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国参观访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的巴西文化代表团团员吉叶耶尔美·德·奥里凡伊拉·菲古艾雷多和他的夫人，于9月30日下午乘飞机到达北京。
