Title: Push Mass Sports Activities to a New Climax: National Games Successfully Concluded. Chairman Ho Chi Minh and Distinguished Guests from Various Countries Attended the Closing Ceremony. Premier Zhou and Vice Premier He Awarded Honorary Medals to Forty Athletes.
Author:
Date: 1959-10-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd: The grand First National Games of the People's Republic of China has successfully concluded. The closing ceremony, attended by over 80,000 people, was held at the Beijing Workers' Stadium this afternoon at a little after 2 o'clock. This National Games, with its unprecedented scale, rich content, and excellent results, will be forever recorded in the history of China's sports movement. It reflects the thriving and vigorous scene of China's mass sports movement under the guidance of the policy of serving production and national defense construction, and will push China's mass sports activities to a new climax.

Present on the rostrum at today's closing ceremony were Party and state leaders Zhou Enlai, Soong Ching Ling, Dong Biwu, Deng Xiaoping, and others.

Chairman Ho Chi Minh, members of the Soviet Party and government delegation, heads of various fraternal party delegations, and government delegations and government representatives from seven Asian-African friendly countries were also invited to attend the closing ceremony on the rostrum.

Also attending today's meeting were national model workers, combat heroes, representatives of minority nationalities, and members of overseas Chinese returning for sightseeing, who came to Beijing to attend the National Day celebrations.

Also attending the meeting were diplomatic envoys of various countries to China, foreign experts in Beijing, foreign sports delegations, and delegations and representatives from various countries participating in the celebrations of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

At today's meeting, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long of the State Council awarded sports honorary medals to forty athletes (four of whom were absent) who had broken world records and won world championships in the past decade. Premier Zhou Enlai also presented the Beijing Municipal Commemorative Award for Group Exercises to the representatives of the "Nationwide Celebration" group exercises.

Today, the Beijing Workers' Stadium was adorned with colorful flags. Referees and athletes of all nationalities participating in the meeting, wearing various colors of clothing, stood in formation in the center of the stadium. At the front were the flags of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Beijing Municipality, Shanghai Municipality, Tibet, and the twenty-five provinces and autonomous regions. The athletes were all in high spirits and full of energy. During the Games, they broke national records in batches. Six hundred and sixty-four people broke a total of eight hundred and forty-four national records in one hundred and six individual events including track and field, swimming, weightlifting, rowing, archery, parachuting, aviation modeling, radio, shooting, and naval modeling. Seven people broke world records in swimming, parachuting, shooting, and aviation modeling. The competitive level in ball games and other competitions has also generally improved. New forces have emerged and talents have sprung up, achieving excellent results. Today, the athletes, holding many trophies awarded to their delegations by the Games and wearing shining medals, participated in the closing ceremony.

He Long, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Vice Premier of the State Council, and Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, delivered the closing speech. He said: This National Games has been a great success. It is a victorious and united meeting!

Vice Premier He Long described the unprecedented harvest of the Games in terms of competitive achievements. The number of national records broken in this National Games exceeded the total number of records broken in the entire year of 1958, concentratingly demonstrating the leaping improvement of China's sports movement on the basis of the great leap forward in 1958. Throughout the entire competition process, the athletes of all nationalities demonstrated the communist spirit of exerting great effort, striving for the top, uniting and loving each other, cooperating with each other, and not being arrogant in victory or discouraged in defeat, as well as excellent moral qualities.

Vice Premier He Long called on all sports workers and athletes to not be arrogant in victory or discouraged in defeat, to earnestly summarize the experiences of this National Games, to continue to carry forward the communist spirit, to strive to improve their ideological awareness and technical level, and to strive to become outstanding people's athletes and referees.

Vice Premier He Long's speech was punctuated by applause from time to time.

At today's closing ceremony, some primary schools, middle schools, and universities in Beijing also performed the "Nationwide Celebration" group exercises. The Chinese national football team and the Soviet national football team played a wonderful friendly match. As a result of the match, the Soviet team won one to zero. (Attached picture)

### 把群众性的体育活动推向新的高潮  全国运动会胜利闭幕  胡志明主席和各国贵宾们应邀参加了闭幕式  周总理贺副总理向四十名运动员颁发荣誉奖章

1959-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

    把群众性的体育活动推向新的高潮
    全国运动会胜利闭幕
    胡志明主席和各国贵宾们应邀参加了闭幕式
    周总理贺副总理向四十名运动员颁发荣誉奖章
    新华社3日讯　规模盛大的中华人民共和国第一届运动会胜利闭幕。由八万多人参加的闭幕式今天下午两点多钟在北京工人体育场举行。这次运动会以它空前的规模，丰富的内容和优良的成绩永远载入我国体育运动的史册，反映出了我国群众性体育运动在为生产为国防建设服务的方针指导下，出现了一片欣欣向荣的蓬勃景象，并且将把我国群众性的体育活动推向新的高潮。
    今天在主席台上参加闭幕式的，有党和国家领导人周恩来、宋庆龄、董必武、邓小平等。
    胡志明主席和苏联党政代表团团员，各兄弟党代表团团长，七个亚非友好国家政府代表团和政府代表也应邀在主席台上参加闭幕式。
    今天参加大会的还有来京参加国庆节观礼的全国劳动模范、战斗英雄、少数民族代表、华侨归国观光团的人员。
    参加大会的还有各国驻华使节、在京外国专家、外国体育代表团和各国参加中华人民共和国建国十周年庆祝典礼代表团和代表。
    在今天的大会上，国务院周恩来总理、贺龙副总理向十年来打破过世界纪录和得过世界冠军的四十名运动员（内四名未到）颁发了体育运动荣誉奖章。周恩来总理还向“全民同庆”团体操的代表颁发了北京市团体操纪念奖。
    北京工人体育场今天彩旗招展，参加大会的裁判员和各民族运动员们穿着各种颜色的服装，列队站在体育场的中心广场。前面是中国人民解放军、北京市、上海市、西藏地方和全国二十五个省、自治区代表队的队旗。运动员们个个兴高采烈、精神焕发。运动会期间，他们成批地刷新全国纪录，有六百六十四人，八百四十四次分别打破田径、游泳、举重、赛艇、射箭、飞机跳伞、航空模型、无线电、射击、航海模型等一百零六个单项全国纪录；有七人分别打破了游泳、跳伞、射击、航空模型等四项世界纪录，在球类赛和其他各项竞赛中，竞技水平也普遍提高，新军突起、人材辈出，获得了优良的成绩。今天，运动员们抱着大会奖给他们代表队的许多奖杯，佩戴着金光闪闪的奖章，参加闭幕式。
    中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理、国家体育运动委员会主任贺龙致闭幕词。他说：这次运动会开得很好，是一次胜利的、团结的大会！
    贺龙副总理讲述了这次运动会在各项竞技成绩方面的空前大丰收。这次运动会破全国纪录的次数超过1958年全年破纪录次数的总和，集中地表现了我国体育运动在1958年的大跃进的基础上飞跃的提高。在整个竞赛过程中，各民族运动员们表现出了鼓足干劲、力争上游、团结友爱、互相协作、胜不骄败不馁的共产主义风格和优良的道德品质。
    贺龙副总理号召全体体育工作者和运动员们胜不骄、败不馁，认真总结这次运动会的经验，继续发扬共产主义精神，努力提高自己的思想觉悟和技术水平，争取成为优秀的人民运动员和裁判员。
    贺龙副总理的讲话不时为掌声打断。
    在今天的闭幕式上，北京市一些小学、中学和高等学校还表演了“全民同庆”团体操。我国国家足球队和苏联国家足球队进行了一场精彩的友谊比赛。比赛结果，苏联队以一比零获胜。（附图片）
