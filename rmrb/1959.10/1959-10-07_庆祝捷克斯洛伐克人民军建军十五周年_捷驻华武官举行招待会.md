Title: Czechoslovak Military Attaché in China Holds Reception to Celebrate the 15th Anniversary of the Founding of the Czechoslovak People's Army
Author:
Date: 1959-10-07
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, October 6th. Colonel Rak, the Czechoslovak Military Attaché in China, held a reception tonight to celebrate the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Czechoslovak People's Army.

Vice Premier Marshal He Long was invited to attend the reception. On behalf of all officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, he extended warm and festive greetings to the fraternal Czechoslovak People's Army. Marshal He Long said that the Czechoslovak People's Army has made tremendous achievements in the modernization of its military and in military-political training. The Czechoslovak People's Army has become a strong armed force defending the socialist construction of the motherland and safeguarding peace in Europe and the world. The Chinese People's Liberation Army is overjoyed and encouraged by the brilliant achievements of the fraternal Czechoslovak People's Army.

Marshal He Long said that the momentum of the East Wind prevailing over the West Wind is growing stronger, and the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union is holding high the banner of Marxism-Leninism and is advancing by leaps and bounds along the bright road of socialism and communism. Our two peoples and armies, in the common struggle to defend the socialist cause, safeguard world peace, and promote the cause of human progress, are closely united in the socialist family headed by the Soviet Union, and our fraternal friendship is unbreakable. We believe that this friendship will continue to develop in the future.

Colonel Rak spoke first at the reception. After outlining the glorious history of struggle of the Czechoslovak People's Army, he said that the leading role of the Czechoslovak Communist Party guarantees that our army is a socialist army. He said that our army has now become a modern and powerful fighting force. It is always ready to carry out the most arduous tasks of defending the motherland.

Colonel Rak said that our People's Army has a solid internationalist alliance with the armies of various socialist countries. The most sincere friendship connects the Czechoslovak people and their army with the Chinese people and their army. This sincere friendship is formed by the common goal of building socialism and the common task of defending the socialist camp. He finally expressed his warm congratulations on the great achievements made by the Chinese people and army in the past decade.

General Tan Zheng, General Su Yu, General Xiao Hua, General Yang Chengwu, General Peng Shaohui, General Li Zhimin, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Vice Admiral Liu Daosheng, Lieutenant General Cao Lihuai and other senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan were invited to attend the reception.

The Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Jan Bušniak, and members of the Czechoslovak military sports delegation who came to Beijing to attend the Third Plenary Meeting of the International Military Sports Council were present at the reception.

Also invited to the reception were diplomatic envoys and military attachés of various countries in China, representatives of the China-Czechoslovakia Friendship People's Commune in the suburbs of Beijing, and representatives of the capital militia division.

### 庆祝捷克斯洛伐克人民军建军十五周年  捷驻华武官举行招待会

1959-10-07
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝捷克斯洛伐克人民军建军十五周年
    捷驻华武官举行招待会
    新华社6日讯　捷克斯洛伐克驻华武官拉克上校今晚举行招待会，庆祝捷克斯洛伐克人民军建军十五周年。
    副总理贺龙元帅应邀出席招待会。他代表中国人民解放军全体官兵，向兄弟般的捷克斯洛伐克人民军致以热烈的、节日的祝贺。贺龙元帅说，捷克斯洛伐克人民军，在现代化的建军和军事政治训练中取得了巨大的成就。捷克斯洛伐克人民军已经成为一支保卫祖国社会主义建设，维护欧洲和世界和平的坚强的武装力量。中国人民解放军，为兄弟般的捷克斯洛伐克人民军取得的光辉成就感到欢欣鼓舞。
    贺龙元帅说，现在，东风压倒西风的声势越来越强大，以苏联为首的社会主义阵营，高举着马克思列宁主义的旗帜，正沿着社会主义、共产主义的光明大道突飞猛进。我们两国人民和军队，在保卫社会主义事业，保卫世界和平和促进人类进步事业的共同斗争中，紧密地团结在以苏联为首的社会主义大家庭里，我们之间的兄弟友谊是牢不可破的。我们相信：这一友谊今后必将不断地向前发展。
    拉克上校在招待会上首先讲话，他概述了捷克斯洛伐克人民军光荣的斗争历史后说，捷克斯洛伐克共产党的领导作用保证了我们军队是社会主义性质的军队。他说，现在我国军队已成为一支现代化的、具有强大战斗力的力量。它随时准备着执行保卫祖国国防的最艰巨的任务。
    拉克上校说，我们人民军与各社会主义国家的军队有牢固的国际主义同盟。最真诚的友谊把捷克斯洛伐克人民及其军队与中国人民及其军队联系在一起。这种真诚的友谊是由建设社会主义的共同目的和保卫社会主义阵营的共同任务中结成的。他最后对十年来中国人民和军队所取得的巨大成就表示热烈的祝贺。
  谭政大将、粟裕大将、萧华上将、杨成武上将、彭绍辉上将、李志民上将、萧向荣中将、刘道生海军中将、曹里怀空军中将等中国人民解放军高级将领和外交部副部长曾涌泉应邀出席了招待会。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使扬·布希尼亚克和来北京参加国际友军体育运动委员会第三次全体会议的捷克斯洛伐克军队体育代表团人员出席了招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的，还有各国驻华使节、武官和北京郊区中捷友好人民公社代表以及首都民兵师代表。
