Title: Vietnamese Delegation from Three Provinces and Regions Leaves Kunming for Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, October 17th. The Party and government people's delegation from the Thai-Miao Autonomous Region, Ha Giang Province, and Lao Cai Province of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by delegation leader Hoang Xuan Thai, who were invited by the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee to Kunming to participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of our country, left Kunming for home today. Before their departure, Sun Yuting, Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Liu Piyun, Vice Governor, hosted a farewell banquet.

During their stay in Kunming, the delegation, in addition to participating in the celebrations, also visited factories, people's communes, ethnic minority institutes, comprehensive exhibition halls, and museums, and toured scenic spots in the outskirts of Kunming. On their way back home, the delegation will also visit the Honghe Hani and Yi Autonomous Prefecture.

### 越南三省区代表团离昆明回国

1959-10-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　越南三省区代表团离昆明回国
    新华社昆明17日电应中共云南省委和云南省人民委员会邀请到昆明参加我国建国十周年庆祝活动的越南民主共和国泰族—苗族自治区、河江省、老街省党政人民代表团，在团长黄春泰率领下，今天离开昆明回国。行前，中共云南省委书记孙雨亭、副省长刘披云曾设宴欢送。
    代表团在昆明期间，除参加了庆祝活动外，并参观了工厂、人民公社、民族学院、综合展览馆和博物馆，游览了昆明近郊风景区。代表团在回国途中，还将顺路访问红河哈尼族彝族自治州。
