Title: Eighty-Two Chiang Kai-shek Soldiers Who Fled to Burma Return and Surrender

Author:

Date: 1959-10-20
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 19th: In the past month, eighty-two Chiang Kai-shek soldiers who had fled to Burma have returned and surrendered.

　　Among those who returned to surrender, Yang Guangfa, the captain of the Chiang army's "Guerrilla Armed Detachment," and soldiers Zhao Lao Er, Ding Chunxing, etc., returned to the embrace of the motherland as a result of the joint anti-enemy propaganda work carried out by the military and civilians on the Yunnan border.

　　Their return was welcomed by the People's Liberation Army and the local people. For those who returned with their weapons, the local border troops of the People's Liberation Army also issued them bonuses in accordance with the regulations of the Ministry of National Defense.

### 八十二名逃缅蒋军回国投诚

1959-10-20
第2版()
专栏：

    八十二名逃缅蒋军回国投诚
    新华社昆明19日电　最近一个月，有八十二名逃缅蒋军回国投诚。
    在回国投诚的人员中，蒋军“游击武装大队”大队长杨光发、士兵赵老二、丁春兴等，是由于云南边防线上军民共同开展对敌宣传工作以后，回到祖国怀抱的。
    他们的归来，受到解放军和驻地人民的欢迎。对于携枪归来的人员，解放军驻地边防部队还根据国防部的规定，发给他们奖金。
