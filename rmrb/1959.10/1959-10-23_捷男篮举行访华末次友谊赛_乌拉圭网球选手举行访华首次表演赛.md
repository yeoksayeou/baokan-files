Title: Czechoslovak Men's Basketball Team Holds Final Friendly Match in China; Uruguayan Tennis Player Holds First Performance Match in China

Author: 
Date: 1959-10-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd: The Czechoslovakian National Men's Basketball Team lost to the Beijing Men's Basketball Team by a score of 77 to 87 in their final friendly match in China tonight at the Beijing Gymnasium.

Since arriving in China on September 28th, the Czechoslovakian National Men's Basketball Team has played eight friendly matches against eight Chinese men's basketball teams in Beijing, Shanghai, and Tianjin over the course of more than three weeks, resulting in five wins and three losses. They will leave Beijing on the 24th to return to their country.

Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd: Uruguay's famous tennis player Eduardo Argon held his first performance match in China today at the Beijing Gymnasium tennis court with Zhu Zhenhua, this year's Chinese men's singles champion, and Mei Fuji, the runner-up. The Chinese players won with a score of 2-0.

The friendly match adopted a best-of-three sets format.

### 捷男篮举行访华末次友谊赛  乌拉圭网球选手举行访华首次表演赛

1959-10-23
第5版()
专栏：

    捷男篮举行访华末次友谊赛
    乌拉圭网球选手举行访华首次表演赛
    据新华社22日讯　捷克斯洛伐克国家男子篮球队今晚在北京体育馆举行访华最后一场友谊比赛时，以七十七比八十七败给了北京男子篮球队。
    捷克斯洛伐克国家男子篮球队自9月28日来我国以后，在三个多星期内共在北京、上海、天津同我国的八个男子篮球队举行了八场友谊比赛，结果五胜三负。他们将在24日离北京回国。
    据新华社22日讯　乌拉圭著名的网球选手爱德华·阿贡，今天在北京体育馆网球场同我国今年的男子网球单打冠军朱振华、亚军梅福基举行访华第一次表演赛，结果中国选手都以二比零获胜。
    友谊比赛采取三盘两胜制。
