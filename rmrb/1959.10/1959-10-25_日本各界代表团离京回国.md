Title: Japanese Delegation Departs Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1959-10-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 24th: The Japanese delegation from various circles celebrating the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, headed by former Japanese Prime Minister, President of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, and member of the House of Representatives, Tetsu Katayama, left Beijing by plane this morning via Guangzhou to return home.

Before his departure, Mr. Katayama delivered a speech, on behalf of all members of the delegation, once again expressing his gratitude for the warm reception received during his visit to China.

He also said that the situation of New China's construction that he saw during his visit to China deeply moved them. He stated that after the delegation returns to Japan, they will convey this feeling of emotion to the Japanese people.

Finally, under the leadership of Tetsu Katayama, the entire delegation raised their arms and shouted "Long live the friendship between the people of Japan and China," "Early restoration of diplomatic relations between Japan and China," and "Long live the friendly relations between Japan and China."

Among those who saw them off at the airport were Liao Chengzhi, Zhang Xiruo, Chu Tunan, Liu Xiyuan, Liu Changsheng, Cai Tingkai, Xu Guangping, Cao Mengjun, Wu Maosun, Yang Hansheng, Zhao Puchu, Zhao Anbo, Xie Nanguang, Yang Chunsong, Ding Tuo, and other responsible persons of people's organizations and people from all walks of life.

### 日本各界代表团离京回国

1959-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

    日本各界代表团离京回国
    新华社24日讯　以日本前首相、日中文化交流协会会长、众议院议员片山哲为首的日本各界庆祝中华人民共和国建国十周年代表团，今天上午乘飞机离开北京经广州回国。
    片山哲先生在临行前发表谈话，代表全体团员再一次对在中国访问期间所受到的盛情接待表示感谢。
    他还说，在中国访问期间所看到的新中国建设情况，使他们深为感动。他表示代表团回国后，将对日本人民传达这种感动的心情。
    最后，代表团全体在片山哲领导下，振臂高呼“日中两国人民友好万岁”，“早日恢复日中邦交”和“日中两国友好关系万岁”。
    在机场送行的有各人民团体负责人和各界人士廖承志、张奚若、楚图南、刘西元、刘长胜、蔡廷锴、许广平、曹孟君、吴茂荪、阳翰笙、赵朴初、赵安博、谢南光、杨春松、丁拓等。
