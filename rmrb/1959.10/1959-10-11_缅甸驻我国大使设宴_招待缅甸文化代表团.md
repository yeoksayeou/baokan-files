Title: Burmese Ambassador to China Hosts Banquet for Burmese Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-10-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 10th - U Win, the Ambassador of the Union of Burma to China, hosted a banquet this afternoon in honor of the Burmese government representatives, led by U Chit Thoung, Minister of Culture of Burma, and the Burmese cultural delegation he leads, who are invited to China to participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and to visit.

    Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs, was invited to attend the banquet. He said at the banquet that China and Burma have a long history of friendship, and that the governments and people of our two countries now wish to maintain and strengthen this friendship. We are willing to make unremitting efforts for this friendship and wish it everlasting.

    Burmese Ambassador U Win said in his speech at the banquet that cultural exchanges and interactions will promote the development of friendship between the two countries. He expressed that this visit of the cultural delegation will help the development of friendship between the two countries.

    Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture of China, Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Ding Xilin, Deputy Director, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Liu Zizai, Vice Minister of Education, Sa Kongliao, Vice Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, and Jian Bozan, a famous historian and director of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, were also invited to attend today's banquet.

    This morning, Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture, briefed the delegation on the development of China's cultural undertakings. In the afternoon, the delegation participated in a symposium on language issues.

### 缅甸驻我国大使设宴  招待缅甸文化代表团

1959-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　缅甸驻我国大使设宴
    招待缅甸文化代表团
    新华社10日讯　缅甸联邦驻我国大使叫温今天下午举行宴会，招待应邀来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼和参观访问的缅甸政府代表、缅甸文化部部长吴漆东和他所率领的缅甸文化代表团。
    国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅应邀出席了宴会。他在宴会上说，中国和缅甸在历史上有着悠久的友谊，我们两国政府和人民现在都愿望保持并加强这种友谊。我们愿意为这种友谊作不懈的努力，并愿它万古长青。
    缅甸大使叫温在宴会上致词说：文化的交流和往来会促使两国友谊的发展。他表示这次文化代表团的访问将有助于两国友谊发展。
    我国文化部部长沈雁冰，对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若、副主任丁西林，外交部副部长章汉夫，教育部副部长刘子载，民族事务委员会副主任萨空了，著名历史学家、中缅友协理事翦伯赞等人也应邀出席了今天的宴会。
    今天上午，文化部副部长夏衍向代表团介绍了我国文化事业的发展情况。下午，代表团参加了关于语言问题的座谈会。
