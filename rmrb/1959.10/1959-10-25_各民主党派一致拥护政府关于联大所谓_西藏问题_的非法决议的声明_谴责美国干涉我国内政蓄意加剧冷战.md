Title: Statements from Various Democratic Parties Unanimously Supporting the Government's Statement on the Illegal Resolution of the UN Regarding the So-Called "Tibetan Issue" Condemning the United States for Interfering in China's Internal Affairs and Deliberately Intensifying the Cold War
Author:
Date: 1959-10-25
Page: 1

    This newspaper reports: Responsible persons from the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, Shen Junru (Chairman of the China Democratic League), Huang Yanpei (Chairman of the Central Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association), Chen Shutong (Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce), Wang Shao'ao (Vice Chairman of the China Association for Promoting Democracy), Ji Fang (Chairman of the Presidium of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party), Chen Qiyou (Chairman of the China Zhi Gong Party), Xu Deheng (Chairman of the Jiusan Society), and responsible persons from the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League, issued statements regarding the UN's adoption of the illegal resolution on the so-called "Tibetan issue." They expressed extreme anger and strong protest against the United Nations, under the planning and manipulation of the United States, for discussing and adopting the illegal resolution on the so-called "Tibetan issue" at its General Assembly meeting, considering it a crude interference in China's internal affairs, a serious violation of the United Nations Charter, and an action endangering international peace and security. They unanimously expressed their firm support for the Chinese government's statement regarding the illegal resolution on the so-called "Tibetan issue" passed by the UN General Assembly, and resolved to work together with the people of the whole country to use all their strength to smash the aggressive conspiracies of American imperialism. (The full text of the statements can be found on page 3 today.)

### 各民主党派一致拥护政府关于联大所谓“西藏问题”的非法决议的声明  谴责美国干涉我国内政蓄意加剧冷战

1959-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    各民主党派一致拥护政府关于联大所谓“西藏问题”的非法决议的声明
    谴责美国干涉我国内政蓄意加剧冷战
    本报讯　中国国民党革命委员会负责人、中国民主同盟主席沈钧儒、中国民主建国会中央委员会主任委员黄炎培、中华全国工商业联合会主任委员陈叔通、中国民主促进会副主席王绍鏊、中国农工民主党中央委员会主席团主席季方、中国致公党主席陈其尤、九三学社主席许德珩和台湾民主自治同盟负责人，就联大通过所谓“西藏问题”的非法决议发表谈话。他们对联合国在美国策划和操纵下，竟然在大会会议上讨论并通过了所谓“西藏问题”的非法决议，表示极大愤怒和强烈抗议，认为这是粗暴的干涉我国内政，严重的违反联合国宪章、危害国际和平与安全的行动。他们一致表示坚决拥护我国政府关于联大通过的所谓“西藏问题”的非法决议的声明，并决心和全国人民一道，用一切力量粉碎美帝国主义的侵略阴谋活动。（谈话的全文见今日第三版）
