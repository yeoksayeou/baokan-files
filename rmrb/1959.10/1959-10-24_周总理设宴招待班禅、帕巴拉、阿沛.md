Title: Premier Zhou Hosts Banquet for Panchen, Pagbalha, and Ngapoi
Author:
Date: 1959-10-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd: Premier Zhou Enlai hosted a banquet at the Minzu Hotel tonight in honor of Panchen Erdeni Qoigyijabu, Acting Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, and Vice Chairmen Pagbalha Geleg Namgyal and Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme.

Before the banquet began, Premier Zhou Enlai met with these three leaders of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region and Panchen Erdeni's entourage, and held cordial talks with them.

Also invited to the banquet tonight were Changdong Jigme, Laming Yexi Chuchen, Sampo Tenzin Rinchen, Ngawang Jinba, and Tangqiao Yapei, all Standing Committee members of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region; Ngapoi Cedan Zhoigar and Yao Xi Gonbo Cedan, members of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region; Enqiu Lobsang Qunpei, Vice President of the Tibetan Branch of the Buddhist Association of China; as well as other members of Panchen Erdeni's entourage.

Dorje Phagmo, the female Living Buddha who was recently brought back from India after being coerced by rebels, was also invited to tonight's banquet.

Vice Premiers of the State Council He Long, Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, and Xi Zhongxun, and heads of relevant central departments attended the banquet.

Also attending the banquet were Baorhan, Deputy Director of the National People's Congress Nationalities Committee; Wang Feng, Yang Jingren, and Xie Hechou, Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission; Ping Jiesan and Xue Zizheng, Deputy Ministers of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee; and Zhou Renshan, Deputy Secretary of the CPC Tibet Working Committee.

Deng Yingchao, Vice Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, Xirao Gyatso, President of the Buddhist Association of China, and Da Pahsheng, Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association, also attended as guests.

After the banquet, Premier Zhou Enlai, along with Panchen Erdeni and others, watched performances by the Tianjin Xiaobaihua Opera Troupe and the Hebei Youth Leap Forward Opera Troupe at the auditorium of the Cultural Palace of Nationalities.

### 周总理设宴招待班禅、帕巴拉、阿沛

1959-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理设宴招待班禅、帕巴拉、阿沛
    新华社23日讯　周恩来总理今晚在民族饭店举行宴会，招待西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞和副主任委员帕巴拉·卓列朗杰、阿沛·阿旺晋美。
    宴会开始前，周恩来总理接见了这三位西藏自治区筹委会的负责人和班禅额尔德尼的随行人员，并且同他们进行了亲切的谈话。
    今晚应邀出席宴会的，还有西藏自治区筹备委员会常务委员詹东·计晋美、拉敏·益喜楚臣、桑颇·才旺仁增、纳旺金巴、唐曲雅培，西藏自治区筹备委员会委员阿沛·才旦卓噶、尧西·贡保才旦，中国佛教协会西藏分会副会长恩久·洛桑群培，以及班禅额尔德尼的其他随行人员。
    被叛乱分子裹胁到印度最近回国的多吉帕姆女活佛也应邀出席了今晚的宴会。
    国务院副总理贺龙、陈毅、李先念、习仲勋，中央有关部门的负责人都出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的，还有全国人民代表大会民族委员会副主任委员包尔汉，民族事务委员会副主任汪锋、杨静仁、谢鹤筹，中共中央统战部副部长平杰三、薛子正，中共西藏工委副书记周仁山等。
    全国妇联副主席邓颖超，中国佛教协会会长喜饶嘉错，中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生也出席作陪。
    宴会结束以后，周恩来总理同班禅额尔德尼等在民族文化宫礼堂观看了天津市小百花剧团和河北省青年跃进剧团的演出。
