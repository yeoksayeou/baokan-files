Title: General Secretary of the Communist Party of Jordan Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:
Date: 1959-10-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 5th - Comrade Fadil Nasser, head of the delegation of the Communist Party of Jordan and General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Jordan, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home after participating in China's 10th Anniversary National Day celebrations and visiting Beijing.

Li Fuchun, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, and Wu Xiuquan, member of the Central Committee, were at the airport to see him off.

One member of the delegation will continue to visit China.

### 约旦共产党总书记离京回国

1959-10-06
第4版()
专栏：

    约旦共产党总书记离京回国
    新华社5日讯　约旦共产党代表团团长、约旦共产党中央委员会总书记法德·纳赛尔同志在参加过我国建国十周年庆祝典礼和在北京参观访问之后，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的，有中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记李富春，中央委员伍修权。
    代表团的一位团员将在我国继续参观访问。
