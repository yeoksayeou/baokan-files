Title: Chairman Mao Zedong and Comrade Liu Shaoqi Meet with Comrade Ghosh
Author:
Date: 1959-10-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice-Chairman Liu Shaoqi met this afternoon with Ajoy Kumar Ghosh, head of the Indian Communist Party delegation and General Secretary of the National Committee of the Indian Communist Party, who was invited to attend the celebrations of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, and members of the delegation: Bhupesh Gupta, member of the Central Executive Committee and Secretary of the Central Secretariat of the Indian Communist Party, Parasaada Rao, member of the Central Executive Committee, Romesh Chandra, and S.V. Parulekar, member of the Maharashtra Regional Committee of the Indian Communist Party.

Present at the meeting were Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and Wang Jiaxiang, secretary of the Central Secretariat.

### 毛泽东刘少奇同志会见高士同志

1959-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东刘少奇同志会见高士同志
    新华社6日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志、副主席刘少奇同志，今天下午会见了应邀前来参加中华人民共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼的印度共产党代表团团长、印度共产党全国委员会总书记阿约艾·库马尔·高士同志，代表团团员：印度共产党中央执行委员会委员、中央书记处书记布佩希·古普塔同志，中央执行委员会委员帕拉萨达·拉奥同志和罗米希·钱德拉同志，以及印度共产党马哈拉施特拉地区委员会委员斯·符·帕鲁勒卡同志。
    会见时在座的，有中共中央政治局委员彭真，中央书记处书记王稼祥。
