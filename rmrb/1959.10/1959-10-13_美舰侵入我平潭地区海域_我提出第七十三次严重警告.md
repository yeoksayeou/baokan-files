Title: U.S. Warship Intrudes into Waters off Pingtan Area; I Issue 73rd Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1959-10-13
Page: 2

　　U.S. Warship Intrudes into Waters off Pingtan Area
    I Issue 73rd Serious Warning
    Xinhua News Agency, 12th – On October 12th, from 08:40 to 11:18, a U.S. warship intruded into the waters off the Pingtan area of Fujian Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. warship, a spokesman for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 73rd serious warning.

### 美舰侵入我平潭地区海域  我提出第七十三次严重警告

1959-10-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　美舰侵入我平潭地区海域
    我提出第七十三次严重警告
    新华社12日讯　10月12日八时四十分至十一时十八分，美国军舰一艘侵入我福建省平潭地区海域。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第七十三次严重警告。
