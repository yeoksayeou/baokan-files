Title: Khrushchev Returns Home After Visiting Romania
Author:
Date: 1959-10-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, Oct. 25th—According to TASS: At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic, and the Presidium of the Romanian Grand National Assembly, the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, paid an unofficial visit to Romania from October 19 to 25, where he also took a short rest.

During his stay in Romania, Khrushchev met with the leaders of the Party and government of the Republic and held talks with them. These meetings and talks took place in a very warm and sincere atmosphere. Khrushchev visited many places of scenic and historical interest in Romania.

Khrushchev left Bucharest on the 25th and returned to Moscow on the same day.

### 赫鲁晓夫访罗后返国

1959-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫访罗后返国
    据新华社25日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：应罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会、罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议、罗马尼亚大国民议会主席团的邀请，苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记、苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫自10月19日至25日在罗马尼亚作了非正式的访问，并在那里作了短期的休息。
    赫鲁晓夫在罗马尼亚逗留期间，会见了共和国的党和政府领导人，并同他们进行了交谈，这些会见和交谈都是在十分热烈和诚挚的气氛中进行的。赫鲁晓夫参观了罗马尼亚的许多名胜古迹。
    赫鲁晓夫在25日离开布加勒斯特，并于当天回到莫斯科。
