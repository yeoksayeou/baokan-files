Title: Premier Kim Il-sung Leads Korean Party and Government Delegation Back Home; Party and State Leaders Such as Zhou Enlai and Lin Biao See Them Off at the Train Station
Author:
Date: 1959-10-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 2nd. The Party and Government Delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, left Beijing by train this afternoon to return home.

General Kim Kwang-hyup, head of the Military Delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, also left Beijing on the same train to return home.

Party and state leaders such as Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee and Premier of the State Council, and Marshal Lin Biao, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, were at the station to see off the distinguished guests.

After the band played the national anthems of Korea and China, Premier Kim Il-sung, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, reviewed the honor guard of the army, navy, and air force.

Premier Kim Il-sung, General Kim Kwang-hyup, and all the distinguished guests received flowers from the capital's children and warmly shook hands with the people who came to see them off. The train slowly left the station amidst the farewell music and enthusiastic applause.

Also at the station today to see off the distinguished guests were Chen Yi and He Long, members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee; Liu Lantao and Yang Shangkun, alternate secretaries of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee; Nie Rongzhen, Vice Premier of the State Council; Li Weihan, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Guo Moruo and Burhan, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Zhang Zhizhong, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council; Xie Juezai, President of the Supreme People's Court; Zhang Dingcheng, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate; Luo Guibo and Ji Pengfei, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs; Li Dequan, President of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association; senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; and heads of government departments, democratic parties, and people's organizations.

Nosaka Sanzo, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party and head of the Japanese Communist Party Delegation, and delegation members Hakamada Satomi, Kurahara Koreto, Sunaama Ichiro, and Iwama Masao, who came to our country to participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China; General Vo Nguyen Giap, head of the Military Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; personnel from the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China; and diplomatic envoys and military attaches of various countries in China also came to the station to see them off.

Lieutenant General Choi Yong-jin, Deputy Head of the Military Delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and the delegation members remained in Beijing to continue their tour, and they also came to the station to see them off today.

On the evening of the 1st, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long visited Premier Kim Il-sung at the guesthouse and had dinner together. (Attached photo)

### 金日成首相率朝鲜党政代表团回国  周恩来林彪等党和国家领导人到车站欢送

1959-10-03
第1版()
专栏：

    金日成首相率朝鲜党政代表团回国
    周恩来林彪等党和国家领导人到车站欢送
    新华社2日讯　由朝鲜劳动党中央委员会委员长、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成率领的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国党政代表团，今天下午乘车离开北京回国。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国军事代表团团长金光侠大将也同车离北京回国。
    中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来，中共中央副主席、国务院副总理兼国防部部长林彪元帅等党和国家的领导人到车站欢送贵宾。
    乐队奏朝中两国国歌后，金日成首相由周恩来总理陪同检阅了陆海空三军仪仗队。
    金日成首相、金光侠大将和全体贵宾接受了首都少年儿童献花后，同欢送的人们热情地握手告别。列车在欢送的乐曲和热烈的掌声中徐徐离开车站。
    今天到车站欢送贵宾的，还有中共中央政治局委员陈毅、贺龙，中共中央书记处候补书记刘澜涛、杨尚昆，国务院副总理聂荣臻，人大常委会副委员长李维汉，政协全国委员会副主席郭沫若、包尔汉，国防委员会副主席张治中，最高人民法院院长谢觉哉，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，外交部副部长罗贵波、姬鹏飞，中朝友好协会会长李德全，中国人民解放军高级将领，政府各部门、各民主党派和人民团体的负责人。
    前来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的日本共产党中央委员会主席、日本共产党代表团团长野坂参三和代表团团员袴田里见、藏原惟人、砂间一良、岩间正男，越南民主共和国军事代表团团长武元甲大将，朝鲜驻中国大使馆人员以及各国驻华使节和武官也到车站欢送。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国军事代表团副团长崔勇进上将和团员们仍留在北京继续参观访问，今天他们也到车站欢送。
    1日晚上，周恩来总理、贺龙副总理到宾馆拜访了金日成首相，并共进晚餐。（附图片）
