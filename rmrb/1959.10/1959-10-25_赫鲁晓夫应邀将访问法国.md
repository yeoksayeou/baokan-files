Title: Khrushchev Accepts Invitation to Visit France
Author:
Date: 1959-10-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 23rd (TASS): Chairman of the USSR Council of Ministers, Khrushchev, has informed French President de Gaulle that he accepts the invitation to visit France and expressed his gratitude for the invitation.

Further discussions are pending regarding the timing and itinerary of Khrushchev's visit to France.

### 赫鲁晓夫应邀将访问法国

1959-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫应邀将访问法国
    新华社24日讯　塔斯社莫斯科23日讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫通知法国总统戴高乐说，他接受访问法国的邀请，并对这一邀请表示感谢。
    关于赫鲁晓夫访问法国的时间和日程问题，还有待进一步商定。
