Title: China and Morocco Sign Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1959-10-03
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd October. The governments of the People's Republic of China and the Kingdom of Morocco signed a trade agreement for 1959-1960 in Beijing on September 30th.

    Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Lei Renmin signed the agreement on behalf of the Chinese side. Mr. Larak, head of the Moroccan government trade delegation, signed on behalf of the Moroccan side.

    Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of China, He Gongkai, Deputy Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Liu Xiweng, Deputy Director of the Fourth Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and Zhang Tingyu, Commercial Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Morocco, attended the signing ceremony.

    Barka, former Chairman of the National Consultative Assembly of the Kingdom of Morocco, also attended the signing ceremony.

### 中摩签订贸易协定

1959-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    中摩签订贸易协定
    新华社2日讯　中华人民共和国政府同摩洛哥王国政府于9月30日在北京签订了1959年至1960年的贸易协定。
    代表中国方面在协定上签字的是中国对外贸易部副部长雷任民；代表摩洛哥方面签字的是摩洛哥王国政府贸易代表团团长拉腊克先生。
    参加签字仪式的有中国对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部西亚非洲司副司长何功楷，对外贸易部第四局副局长刘希文以及中国驻摩洛哥大使馆商务参赞张廷禹。
    摩洛哥王国前国民谘询议会主席巴尔卡也参加了签字仪式。
