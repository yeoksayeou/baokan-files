Title: Danish Communist Party Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 22nd -- The Danish Communist Party delegation, headed by Comrade Robert Sorltaug, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Danish Communist Party, left Beijing by plane today to return home after participating in the celebrations of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and visiting various parts of our country.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Li Fuchun, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the CPC and Secretary of the Central Secretariat, Yang Shangkun, alternate secretary of the Central Secretariat, and Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee.

### 丹麦共产党代表团回国

1959-10-23
第5版()
专栏：

    丹麦共产党代表团回国
    新华社22日讯　以丹麦共产党中央政治局委员罗伯特·萨尔陶里同志为首的丹麦共产党代表团，在参加了中华人民共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼和在我国各地参观访问之后，于今日乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的，有中共中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记李富春，中央书记处候补书记杨尚昆、中央委员刘宁一。
