Title: Nepalese Government Delegation Visits Changchun
Author:
Date: 1959-10-09
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, 8th - The Nepalese Government Delegation, led by Dr. Tulsi Giri, Minister of Village Development of the Kingdom of Nepal, who was invited to our country to participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, left Changchun by train this afternoon for Beijing after visiting Changchun.
    Xu Yuanquan, Vice Governor of Jilin Province, Chen Zhong, Mayor of Changchun, and others went to the station to see them off.
    During their stay in Changchun, the delegation visited the Changchun No. 1 Automobile Manufacturing Plant, the Changchun Film Studio, and the Jingyue People's Commune in the suburbs of Changchun.
    The delegation arrived in Changchun from Shenyang on the evening of the 6th.

### 尼泊尔政府代表团在长春访问

1959-10-09
第6版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔政府代表团在长春访问
    新华社长春8日电　应邀来我国参加庆祝建国十周年典礼的尼泊尔王国政府代表团一行四人，在团长、尼泊尔王国乡村发展大臣图尔西·吉里博士率领下，在长春参观访问后，今天下午乘火车离开长春回北京。
    前往车站送行的有吉林省副省长徐元泉、长春市市长陈钟等。
    代表团在长春期间，参观访问了长春第一汽车制造厂、长春电影制片厂和长春市郊净月人民公社。
    代表团是在6日晚上从沈阳到达长春的。
