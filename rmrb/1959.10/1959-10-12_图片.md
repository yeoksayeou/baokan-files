    Based on the experience of rational close planting of wheat over the years, Ganggou Commune, Licheng County, Jinan City, Shandong Province, adopted different close planting methods according to the quality of the soil this year during sowing. This is the Sixth Production Team of the Ganggou Production Management Area using a "three-legged drill" for sowing. Xinhua News Agency reporter Xu Qingfu photographed.
