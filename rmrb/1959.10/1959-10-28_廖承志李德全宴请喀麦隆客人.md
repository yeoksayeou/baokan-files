Title: Liao Chengzhi and Li Dequan Host Banquet for Cameroonian Guests
Author:
Date: 1959-10-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th: Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Asian-African Solidarity, and Li Dequan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome Mr. Félix-Roland Moumié, President of the Union of the Peoples of Cameroon, and the Cameroonian women's delegation headed by Mrs. Carmen Monique.

Those present at the banquet tonight included: Bao Erhan, Shen Zijiu, Yang Yunyu, Zhang Zhixiang, Zhu Ziqi, Zhang Tiesheng, and others.

### 廖承志李德全宴请喀麦隆客人

1959-10-28
第5版()
专栏：

    廖承志李德全宴请喀麦隆客人
    新华社27日讯　中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志、中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会副主席李德全今晚设宴欢迎喀麦隆人民联盟主席费利克斯·罗兰·穆米埃先生和以卡门·莫尼克夫人为首的喀麦隆妇女代表团。
    出席今晚宴会的有：包尔汉、沈兹九、杨蕴玉、张致祥、朱子奇、张铁生等。
