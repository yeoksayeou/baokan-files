Title: Distinguished Guests from Various Countries Visit and Tour Various Parts of Our Country
Author:
Date: 1959-10-14
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, October 13th: Chairman Dobi István of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic arrived in Kunming today morning from Chengdu by special plane for a visit. Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China Dong Biwu, Minister of State Farms and Land Reclamation Wang Zhen, and Deputy Director of the American and Australian Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Lin Ping accompanied the distinguished guests.

Those who welcomed the distinguished guests at the airport included Yan Hongyan, First Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Liu Zhuofu, Secretary of the Provincial Committee and Vice Governor; Zhao Zengyi, First Secretary of the Kunming Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; and Chen Kang, Lieutenant General, Commander of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Kunming.

Huang Xuantai, head of the Vietnamese Party and Government Delegation from three provinces who came to Kunming to participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of our country's founding, Wu Wen Huan, Vietnamese Consul in Kunming, and Wu La Dun, Burmese Deputy Consul in Kunming, also welcomed them at the airport.

After arriving in Kunming, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Wang Zhen, Yan Hongyan, Liu Zhuofu, and Zhao Zengyi, visited the Kunming Institute of Botany of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

Tonight, Yan Hongyan, First Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, hosted a banquet for the distinguished guests. After the banquet, the distinguished guests attended a national song and dance evening.

When Chairman Dobi left Chengdu today, Li Dazhang, Secretary of the Sichuan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Governor of Sichuan Province, Zhao Cangbi, Secretary of the Provincial Committee and Vice Governor, Li Zonglin, Mayor of Chengdu, and He Bingyan, General, Commander of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Chengdu, and others saw them off at the airport.

Belgian Communist Party Delegation Returns to Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, October 13th: The delegation of the Belgian Communist Party, headed by Comrade Vanden Branden, returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon after visiting Shenyang and other places.

In Shenyang, Fushun, Anshan, and Changchun, the distinguished guests visited various modern large-scale factories, as well as kindergartens, nursing homes, and workers' residential areas. The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed everywhere.

Suwirjo and Others Leave Shanghai for Nanjing

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, October 13th: Suwirjo, Chairman of the Indonesian Nationalist Party, and his wife, Hadi, member of the Central Standing Committee of the Indonesian Nationalist Party and former First Deputy Prime Minister, and his wife, who were invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to visit our country, left for Nanjing by plane this morning after visiting Shanghai for three days.

Jin Zhonghua, Vice Mayor of Shanghai and Director of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Liang Yufan, Director of the Institute, and others saw them off at the airport.

During their stay in Shanghai, the distinguished guests visited the former residence of Sun Yat-sen, the Children's Palace, textile factories, toy factories, and suburban people's communes, and toured the city and the famous amusement park "Great World." The distinguished guests also visited Hangzhou for a day.

Cultural Delegations and Friendship Association Delegations from Various Countries Visit Various Places

Xinhua News Agency, October 13th: Cultural delegations, friendship association delegations, cultural figures, and painters from Cuba, Brazil, Argentina, Chile, Venezuela, Colombia, Mexico, Peru, Paraguay, Bolivia, Uruguay, Haiti, Nicaragua, Belgium, Denmark, France, Finland, Greece, Italy, the Netherlands, New Zealand, Sweden, Switzerland, Luxembourg, Iraq, Burma, Sudan, Cambodia, Indonesia, Ceylon, Nepal, Pakistan, Israel, and other countries, who were invited to participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of our country, visited Beijing, Shanghai, Nanjing, Hangzhou, Wuxi, Suzhou, Hefei, Shenyang, Changchun, Anshan, Fushun, Tianjin, Taiyuan, Zhengzhou, Luoyang, Xi'an, Chengdu, Chongqing, Kunming, Wuhan, Changsha, Guangzhou, Urumqi and other places from the 4th to the 13th.

World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation Returns to Beijing

According to Xinhua News Agency, October 13th: The head of the delegation of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, Eshar, Secretary-General of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, and members of the delegation Miroslav Vacek, Nouri Abdul Razzaq Hussein, Mario Balliona, Manuel Cantero Prado, Ratzer Jose, and Mazika Aimee, have returned to Beijing from Shanghai by plane today after completing their visits in Wuhan, Nanjing, Shanghai and other places.

The member of the delegation, Beyes Alion Badara, Chairman of the African Democratic Youth Conference, had already returned to his country on October 6. The member of the delegation, Ratnavira, Chairman of the Ceylon Communist Youth and Progressive Youth League Federation, has flown from Shanghai to Guangzhou and will return to his country from there.

### 各国贵宾在我国各地参观访问

1959-10-14
第5版()
专栏：

　　各国贵宾在我国各地参观访问
    道比主席到昆明访问
    新华社昆明13日电　匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比·伊斯特万今天上午由成都乘专机到达昆明访问。中华人民共和国副主席董必武和农垦部部长王震、外交部美澳司副司长林平陪同贵宾前来。
    到机场欢迎贵宾的，有中共云南省委第一书记阎红彦，省委书记、副省长刘卓甫，中共昆明市委第一书记赵增益和人民解放军驻昆明部队首长陈康中将等。
    来昆明参加我国建国十周年庆祝典礼的越南三省党政代表团团长黄春泰、越南驻昆明领事武文缓和缅甸驻昆明副领事吴拉吞也到机场欢迎。
    贵宾到昆明后，由王震、阎红彦、刘卓甫和赵增益等人陪同，参观了中国科学院昆明植物研究所。
    今晚，中共云南省委第一书记阎红彦设宴招待贵宾。宴会后，贵宾出席了民族歌舞晚会。
    道比主席今天离开成都时，中共四川省委书记、四川省省长李大章，省委书记、副省长赵苍璧，成都市市长李宗林，人民解放军驻成都部队首长贺炳炎上将等人到机场欢送。
    比利时共产党代表团回到北京
    新华社13日讯　以范登布兰敦同志为首的比利时共产党代表团，在沈阳等地参观访问之后，今日下午乘飞机回到北京。
    贵宾们在沈阳、抚顺、鞍山和长春等城市，参观访问了各种现代化的大型工厂，以及幼儿园、养老院和职工住宅区等。贵宾们到处都受到热烈的欢迎。
    苏维约等离上海去南京访问
    新华社上海13日电　应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的印度尼西亚国民党总主席苏维约和夫人、印度尼西亚国民党中央常务委员会委员、前第一副总理哈迪和夫人，在上海访问了三天后，今天早晨乘飞机前往南京。
    到机场欢送的有上海市副市长、中国人民外交学会理事金仲华，外交学会理事梁于藩等。
    贵宾们在上海期间，参观访问了中山故居、少年宫、纺织厂、玩具厂和郊区人民公社，并游览了市容和著名游乐场“大世界”。贵宾们还到杭州游览了一天。
    各国文化代表团和友协代表团分别到各地参观访问
    新华社13日讯　应邀来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的古巴、巴西、阿根廷、智利、委内瑞拉、哥伦比亚、墨西哥、秘鲁、巴拉圭、玻利维亚、乌拉圭、海地、尼加拉瓜、比利时、丹麦、法国、芬兰、希腊、意大利、荷兰、新西兰、瑞典、瑞士、卢森堡、伊拉克、缅甸、苏丹、柬埔寨、印度尼西亚、锡兰、尼泊尔、巴基斯坦、以色列等国的文化代表团、友好协会代表团、文化人士和画家等，4日到13日分别在北京、上海、南京、杭州、无锡、苏州、合肥、沈阳、长春、鞍山、抚顺、天津、太原、郑州、洛阳、西安、成都、重庆、昆明、武汉、长沙、广州、乌鲁木齐等地参观访问。
    世界民主青联代表团回到北京
    据新华社13日讯　世界民主青年联盟代表团团长、世界民主青年联盟总书记埃夏尔和代表团团员米罗斯拉夫·维茨克尔、努里·阿卜杜勒·拉扎克·侯赛因、马里奥·巴里昂纳、曼努埃尔·坎特罗·普拉多、拉特泽尔·何塞、玛齐卡·艾梅，结束在武汉、南京、上海等地的参观访问以后，已经在今天由上海乘飞机回到北京。
    代表团团员、非洲民主青年大会主席贝耶斯·阿利翁·巴达拉，已经在10月6日先行回国。代表团团员、锡兰共产主义青年和进步青年联盟联合会主席拉特纳维拉，已由上海乘飞机到广州，将从那里回国。
