Title: Iraqi and Algerian Government Delegations Return Home; Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi and Others Greeted Them at the Airport

Author: 
Date: 1959-10-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 10th: The Iraqi Republic government delegation, led by Brigadier General Ahmed Mohammed Yehia, Minister of Interior of the Iraqi Republic, left Beijing by plane this afternoon to return home. Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi greeted the distinguished guests at the airport.

At the airport, the band played the Iraqi and Chinese national anthems. Delegation leader Brigadier General Yehia, accompanied by Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, reviewed the guard of honor. The distinguished guests accepted flowers from the capital's children and warmly shook hands with the people who came to see them off.

Also present at Beijing Airport to see them off today were Bao Erhan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Zhang Hanfu and Zeng Yongquan, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs; Senior Generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Su Yu, Zhang Zongxun, Xiao Hua, and Gan Siqi; Wang Yifu, Vice Minister of the Interior; as well as heads of government departments, democratic parties, and people's organizations.

Jalal Jaaf, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Iraqi Republic's Embassy in China, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to China also saw them off at the airport today.

Xinhua News Agency, October 10th: The Provisional Government delegation of the Republic of Algeria, which was invited to China to participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, left Beijing by plane this afternoon to return home.

Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi, as well as heads of various government departments, saw the distinguished guests off at the airport.

After the band played the Algerian and Chinese national anthems, Vice Premier Chen Yi accompanied the head of the delegation, Ben Youssef Ben Khedda, Minister of Social Welfare of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, to review the guard of honor. Before boarding the plane, the capital's children presented flowers to the distinguished guests. The distinguished guests warmly shook hands with the people who came to see them off.

Also present at the airport to see off the distinguished guests today were Bao Erhan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Zhang Hanfu and Zeng Yongquan, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs; as well as ministers and vice ministers of various government departments, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and heads of democratic parties and people's organizations.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries to China also saw them off at the airport.

### 伊拉克、阿尔及利亚政府代表团回国  陈毅副总理兼外长等到机场欢送

1959-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　伊拉克、阿尔及利亚政府代表团回国
    陈毅副总理兼外长等到机场欢送
    新华社10日讯　由伊拉克共和国内政部长艾哈迈德·穆罕默德·叶海亚准将率领的伊拉克共和国政府代表团，今天下午乘飞机离开北京回国。陈毅副总理兼外交部长到机场欢送贵宾。
    机场上，乐队奏伊拉克、中国国歌，代表团团长叶海亚准将在陈毅副总理兼外交部长陪同下检阅了仪仗队。贵宾们接受了首都少年儿童的献花，同欢送的人们热情地握手告别。
    今天到北京机场欢送的，还有政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，外交部副部长章汉夫、曾涌泉，中国人民解放军高级将领粟裕大将、张宗逊上将、萧华上将、甘泗淇上将，内务部副部长王一夫，以及政府各部门、各民主党派和人民团体的负责人等。
    伊拉克共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办贾拉尔·贾夫和各国驻华使节今天也到机场欢送。
    新华社10日讯　应邀来中国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团，今日下午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅以及政府各部门负责人到机场欢送贵宾。
    乐队奏阿尔及利亚和中国国歌之后，陈毅副总理陪同代表团团长、阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府社会福利部部长本·优素福·本·赫达检阅了仪仗队。登上飞机以前，首都少年儿童向贵宾献花。贵宾们同欢送的人热情地握手告别。
    今天到机场欢送贵宾的，还有政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，外交部副部长章汉夫、曾涌泉，以及政府各部部长、副部长，中国人民解放军高级将领，各民主党派和人民团体的负责人。
    各国驻华使节也到机场欢送。（附图片）
