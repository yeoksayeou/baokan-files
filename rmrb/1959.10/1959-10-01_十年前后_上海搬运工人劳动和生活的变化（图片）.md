Title: Ten Years Before and After—Changes in the Labor and Life of Shanghai Stevedores (Photos)
Author: Chen Juanmei, Ji Lianbo
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 10

Ten Years Before and After
    ——Changes in the Labor and Life of Shanghai Stevedores
    Before liberation, Shanghai's stevedores worked tirelessly all day long, but still could not make ends meet. Once they stopped, they would gamble to relieve their worries. Xinhua News Agency
    Now, stevedores have good places to rest. This is a corner of the night shift workers' rest room in the Third Stevedoring District on the Huangpu River in Shanghai. Xinhua News Agency reporter Chen Juanmei.
    This is a photograph from 1948. In the cold winter, dockworkers in Shanghai harbor were barefoot, unloading cargo on a twelve-foot-high gangplank. One misstep could cost them their lives. Xinhua News Agency.
    Now, Shanghai Port is a well-equipped port with mechanical equipment, which greatly reduces the physical labor of dockworkers. Xinhua News Agency reporters Chen Juanmei, Ji Lianbo.

### 十年前后——上海搬运工人劳动和生活的变化（图片）
陈娟美  冀连波
1959-10-01
第10版()
专栏：

    十年前后
    ——上海搬运工人劳动和生活的变化
    解放前，上海市的搬运工人整天不停地干活，但仍不得温饱，一旦停下来就赌博解愁。　新华社稿
    现在，搬运工人有了很好的休息地方。这是上海黄浦江畔第三装卸区夜班工人休息室的一角。　新华社记者　陈娟美摄
    这是一张一九四八年的照片。寒冷的冬天，上海港码头工人赤着脚，在一丈二尺高的长跳板上卸货，一失足就会断送性命。
    新华社稿
    现在的上海港已是机械设备很好的港口了，大大减轻了码头工人的体力劳累。　新华社记者　陈娟美、冀连波摄
