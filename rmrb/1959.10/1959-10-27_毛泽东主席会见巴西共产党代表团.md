Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Meets with Delegation of the Communist Party of Brazil
Author:
Date: 1959-10-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 26th - Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China met in Jinan, Shandong on the morning of October 25th with the head of the delegation of the Communist Party of Brazil, Luiz Carlos Prestes, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Brazil, and members of the delegation of the Communist Party of Brazil, Ramiro Lucchesi and Danieliri, members of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Brazil, and held cordial talks with them.
    Present at the meeting were Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, and Shu Tong, member of the CPC Central Committee and First Secretary of the CPC Shandong Provincial Committee.
    After the talks, Comrade Mao Zedong also had lunch with all the comrades of the delegation of the Communist Party of Brazil.

### 毛泽东主席会见巴西共产党代表团

1959-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席会见巴西共产党代表团
    新华社济南26日电　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东，在10月25日上午，于山东济南会见了巴西共产党代表团团长、巴西共产党中央委员会总书记路易斯·卡洛斯·普列斯特斯，巴西共产党代表团团员、巴西共产党中央委员拉米罗·卢切西和丹尼利里，并且同他们进行亲切的谈话。
    会见时在座的，有中共中央书记处书记王稼祥，中共中央委员、中共山东省委第一书记舒同。
    谈话完了以后，毛泽东同志还同巴西共产党代表团全体同志共进午餐。
