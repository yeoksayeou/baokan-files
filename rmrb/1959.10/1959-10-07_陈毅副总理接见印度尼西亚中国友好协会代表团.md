Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Delegation from the Indonesia-China Friendship Association
Author: 
Date: 1959-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th - Vice Premier of the State Council, Chen Yi, received Henk Anton, Central Councilor of the Indonesia-China Friendship Association and Deputy Head of the Indonesia-China Friendship Association Delegation, and all members of the delegation this evening. At the same time, Vice Premier Chen Yi also received Mr. and Mrs. Mualiff Nasution.

Present at the reception were Burhan Shahidi, President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, Hu Yuzhi, Vice President, and others.

### 陈毅副总理接见印度尼西亚中国友好协会代表团

1959-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见印度尼西亚中国友好协会代表团
    新华社6日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今天晚上接见了印度尼西亚中国友好协会中央理事、印度尼西亚中国友好协会代表团副团长亨·安东和代表团全体团员。同时，陈毅副总理还接见了穆阿利夫·纳苏蒂安夫妇。
    接见时在座的有中国印度尼西亚友好协会会长包尔汉、副会长胡愈之等。
