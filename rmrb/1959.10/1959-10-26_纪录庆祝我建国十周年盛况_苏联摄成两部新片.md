Title: USSR Films Two New Movies Recording the Grand Celebration of the 10th Anniversary of the Founding of Our Nation
Author:
Date: 1959-10-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 25th: The Soviet Central Documentary Film Studio has produced a new documentary film entitled "In the Festival of Our Brothers."

　　This film uses vivid shots to record in detail the grand occasion of the capital Beijing during the 10th anniversary of the founding of our nation, including the People's Congress celebration, Comrade Khrushchev's arrival in Beijing, and the military parade and mass parade in Tiananmen Square on October 1st.

　　The studio has also filmed another documentary, "Friendship," about the people of various parts of the Soviet Union celebrating the 10th anniversary of the founding of our nation.

### 纪录庆祝我建国十周年盛况  苏联摄成两部新片

1959-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    纪录庆祝我建国十周年盛况
    苏联摄成两部新片
    据新华社莫斯科25日电　苏联中央文献纪录电影制片厂摄成了一部名为“在我们兄弟的节日里”的新纪录影片。
    这部影片用鲜明生动的镜头，详细记载了在我建国十周年节日里的首都北京、人大庆祝大会、赫鲁晓夫同志到达北京、以及10月1日天安门广场阅兵、群众游行等盛况。
    这个制片厂同时还拍成了另一部苏联各地人民庆祝我建国十周年的纪录片“友谊”。
