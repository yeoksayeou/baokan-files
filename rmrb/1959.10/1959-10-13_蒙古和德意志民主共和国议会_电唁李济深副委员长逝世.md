Title: Mongolian and German Democratic Republic Parliaments Send Condolences on the Death of Vice Chairman Li Jishen
Author:
Date: 1959-10-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 12th - The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Chairman Zhu De received telegrams from the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic and Dr. Johannes Dieckmann, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, expressing condolences on the death of Vice Chairman Li Jishen.

The telegrams expressed deep sorrow over the death of Vice Chairman Li Jishen.

### 蒙古和德意志民主共和国议会  电唁李济深副委员长逝世

1959-10-13
第5版()
专栏：

　　蒙古和德意志民主共和国议会
    电唁李济深副委员长逝世
    新华社12日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会和朱德委员长，收到了蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团和德意志民主共和国人民议院主席团主席约·狄克曼博士发来的吊唁李济深副委员长逝世的电报。
    唁电中对李济深副委员长的逝世，表示深切的哀悼。
