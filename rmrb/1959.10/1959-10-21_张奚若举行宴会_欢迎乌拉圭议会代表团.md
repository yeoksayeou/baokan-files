Title: Zhang Xiruo Holds Banquet to Welcome Uruguayan Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-10-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 20th: Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, held a banquet tonight to welcome the Uruguayan Parliamentary Delegation visiting China.

The head of the Uruguayan Parliamentary Delegation, Speaker of the Uruguayan House of Representatives Francisco Rodriguez Camusso, and all members of the delegation attended the banquet.

President Zhang Xiruo spoke at the beginning of the banquet. On behalf of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, he extended a warm welcome to the Uruguayan Parliamentary Delegation for their visit to China.

He said: This visit to our country by the Uruguayan Parliamentary Delegation will greatly help to enhance the friendship and mutual understanding between the Chinese and Uruguayan peoples.

Head of the delegation Francisco Rodriguez Camusso said at the banquet that China is one of the oldest countries in the world, and the great achievements of the Chinese people in industry, agriculture, and all aspects will play a significant role in the development of human history. He expressed deep respect for the tenacious perseverance shown by the Chinese people in the anti-aggression struggle, and proposed a toast to the permanent friendship and long-term coexistence between the Uruguayan and Chinese peoples.

Uruguayan journalists Carlos Borche and Hugo Rodriguez Bella were also invited to attend the banquet.

Also present at tonight's banquet were Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Hu Yuzhi and Qiao Guanhua, Vice Presidents of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Li Zhuchen, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Ding Xilin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries; Secretary-General Zhou Erfu; Le Songsheng, Deputy Mayor of Beijing; and Chen Ming and Yao Zhen, who have visited Latin America, and others.

### 张奚若举行宴会  欢迎乌拉圭议会代表团

1959-10-21
第5版()
专栏：

    张奚若举行宴会
    欢迎乌拉圭议会代表团
    新华社20日讯　中国人民外交学会会长张奚若今晚举行宴会，欢迎来我国访问的乌拉圭议会代表团。
    乌拉圭议会代表团团长、乌拉圭众议院议长弗朗西斯科·罗德里格斯·卡穆索和代表团全体团员应邀出席了宴会。
    张奚若会长在宴会开始时讲了话。他代表中国人民外交学会对乌拉圭议会代表团来中国访问表示热烈的欢迎。
    他说：乌拉圭议会代表团这次来我国访问，这对于增进中国人民和乌拉圭人民之间的友谊和相互了解将有很大的帮助。
    弗朗西斯科·罗德里格斯·卡穆索团长在宴会上讲话说，中国是世界上最古老的国家之一，中国人民在工业、农业以及各方面所取得的巨大成就，对整个人类历史的发展将起很大作用。他对中国人民在反侵略斗争中所表现的顽强毅力表示深深的敬意，并提议为乌拉圭和中国人民之间的永久友谊和长远相处而干杯。
    乌拉圭新闻工作者卡洛斯·博尔切和乌果·罗德里格塞·贝拉也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席今晚宴会的还有：全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏，中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之、乔冠华，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席李烛尘，中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林，秘书长周而复，北京市副市长乐松生和曾经访问过拉丁美洲的人士陈明、姚溱等。
