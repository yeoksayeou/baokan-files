Title: Dresden Orchestra Performs Beethoven's Masterpieces; Premier Zhou, Vice Premier Chen Yi, and Others Attend Concert
Author:
Date: 1959-10-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 11th: The Dresden Symphony Orchestra of the German Democratic Republic held its third concert in Beijing tonight, performing Beethoven's Ninth Symphony. A choir of one hundred and eighty-five people from the Central Philharmonic Orchestra, the Central Broadcasting Orchestra, and the Central Conservatory of Music participated in the grand chorus.

Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhang Xiruo, and others, as well as the Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Wang Dael, and some foreign guests in Beijing, attended the concert. When the extremely wonderful concert ended, the audience applauded and cheered for five or six minutes. Li Delun and Yan Liangkun, conductors of the Central Philharmonic Orchestra, presented flowers to Professor Bongartz, who conducted the performance. Literary and art workers from the capital presented flower baskets to the symphony orchestra. Li Ling, head of the Central Philharmonic Orchestra, said that the performance by the musicians from the German Democratic Republic was very powerful and fully expressed the spirit of Beethoven. Musician Ma Sicong said that the tone of the concert was very beautiful and the performance was very emotional. Professor Bongartz, head, artistic director and conductor of the symphony orchestra, said excitedly that the Chinese and German musicians and singers cooperated quite well.

### 德累斯顿乐团演奏贝多芬名曲  周总理、陈毅副总理等出席音乐会

1959-10-12
第5版()
专栏：

　　德累斯顿乐团演奏贝多芬名曲
    周总理、陈毅副总理等出席音乐会
    新华社11日讯　德意志民主共和国德累斯顿交响乐团今晚在北京举行第三次音乐会，演奏贝多芬的第九交响乐。中央乐团、中央广播乐团和中央音乐学院一百八十五人组成的合唱队参加了大合唱。
    周恩来总理、陈毅副总理、对外文化联络委员会张奚若主任等，德意志民主共和国驻中国大使汪戴尔，以及在北京的一部分外宾，出席了音乐会。当这场十分精采的音乐会结束的时候，观众鼓掌喝采达五六分钟之久。中央乐团的指挥李德伦和严良坤向指挥这场演奏的波恩格茨教授赠送了鲜花，首都的文艺工作者向交响乐团赠送了花篮。中央乐团团长李凌说，德意志民主共和国音乐家的演奏很有力量，充分表达了贝多芬的精神。音乐家马思聪说，这次音乐会的音色非常美，演奏得很有感情。交响乐团团长、艺术指导兼指挥波恩格茨教授兴奋地说，德中两国音乐家和歌唱家合作得相当好。
