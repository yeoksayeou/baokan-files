Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters and Other Units Hold Report Meeting to Commemorate the 100th Anniversary of Kosta Khetagurov's Birth
Author:
Date: 1959-10-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 17th: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and the Beijing Library jointly held a report meeting today to commemorate the 100th anniversary of the birth of Kosta Khetagurov, the founder of Soviet Ossetian literature and a great poet.

　　Go Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters and Director of the Chinese Writers Association, gave a report on Khetagurov's works and life. He said that Khetagurov was a versatile writer; he was a poet, novelist, political commentator, and also a painter. He dedicated his life to the struggle for the freedom and liberation of the Ossetian people. His works reflected the painful life of the Ossetian people under Tsarist rule and called on the people to rise up and fight.

　　After the meeting, the Georgian Film Studio's color film "Fatima," based on the author's long poem, was shown.

### 中苏友协总会等单位举行报告会  纪念赫塔古罗夫诞生一百周年

1959-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

　　中苏友协总会等单位举行报告会
    纪念赫塔古罗夫诞生一百周年
    据新华社17日讯　中苏友好协会总会、北京市中苏友好协会和北京图书馆今天联合举行报告会，纪念苏联奥谢蒂亚文学奠基者、大诗人科斯塔·赫塔古罗夫诞生一百周年。
    中苏友好协会总会副秘书长、中国作家协会理事戈宝权在会上作了关于赫塔古罗夫的作品和生平的报告。他说，赫塔古罗夫是个多才多艺的作家，他是诗人、小说家、政论家，同时也是画家。他的一生都献给了争取奥谢蒂亚人民的自由和解放的斗争。在他的作品中，反映了奥谢蒂亚人民在沙皇统治下的痛苦生活，号召人民起来进行斗争。
    会后放映了格鲁吉亚电影制片厂根据作者的长诗拍摄的彩色影片“法蒂玛”。
