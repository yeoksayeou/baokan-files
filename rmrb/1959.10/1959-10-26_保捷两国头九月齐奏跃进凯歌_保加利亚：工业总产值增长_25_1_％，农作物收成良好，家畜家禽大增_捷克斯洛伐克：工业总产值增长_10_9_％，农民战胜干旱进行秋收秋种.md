Title: Bulgaria and Czechoslovakia Achieve Great Leap Forward in the First Nine Months: Bulgaria: Industrial output value increases by 25.1%, good harvests, large increase in livestock and poultry. Czechoslovakia: Industrial output value increases by 10.9%, farmers overcome drought to carry out autumn harvest and planting.
Author:
Date: 1959-10-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 24th -- The Central Statistical Bureau of the Bulgarian Council of Ministers published its production communique for the first nine months of this year on the 24th. The communique announced that in the first nine months of this year, Bulgaria completed 100.4% of its total industrial output value plan, and the total industrial output value increased by 25.1% compared with the same period last year. The communique listed the output of 42 important industrial products, all of which increased by more than 10% compared with the same period last year.

The communique pointed out that in the first nine months of this year, Bulgaria produced a total of 2.7655 billion kilowatt-hours of electricity, an increase of 26% compared with the same period last year; 11,227,700 tons of coal, an increase of 19% compared with the same period last year; 170,510 tons of steel, an increase of 10% compared with the same period last year; 25,402 tons of lead, an increase of 35% compared with the same period last year. The output of other products and the growth rate compared with the same period last year are as follows: Nitrogen fertilizer - 42,076 tons, 22%; Phosphate fertilizer - 20,216 tons, doubled; Cement - 1.07 million tons, 57%; Cotton fabrics - 153.008 million meters, 23%; Woolen fabrics - 13.841 million meters, 33%.

Among other industrial products, compared with the same period last year, vegetable canned goods increased by 35%, fruit canned goods increased by 64%, cream increased by 33%, vegetable oil increased by 20%, and sugar products increased by 19% in the first nine months of this year.

The communique said that great achievements had also been made in agriculture. Although the climate in some areas was unfavorable to certain crops, thanks to the efforts of members of agricultural producers' cooperatives, the harvests of almost all crops were very good.

In the first nine months of this year, the number of tractors serving agricultural producers' cooperatives increased by 5,250 compared with the same period last year (each calculated at 15 horsepower). During this period, the total amount of work completed by national machine tractor stations was 38% more than in the same period last year.

Since the beginning of this year, Bulgaria's animal husbandry has also made great progress. As of October 1, the number of cattle, sheep, pigs, and chickens in state farms and agricultural producers' cooperatives across the country increased by 32%, 15%, 54%, and two and a half times respectively compared with the same period last year. Among them, the number of pigs in agricultural producers' cooperatives across the country more than doubled.

The communique said that in the first nine months of this year, the number of employees in Bulgaria's industrial, construction, domestic trade, and transportation departments increased by 100,2485 compared with the same period last year. Compared with the same period last year, labor productivity in industry and construction increased by 6.9% and 5.7% respectively.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 24th -- According to announcements here: In the first three quarters of this year, Czechoslovakia's industrial production plan was completed by 101.7%, and the total industrial output value increased by 10.9% compared with the same period last year.

In the first nine months of this year, Czechoslovakia's electricity production increased to 15.751 billion kilowatt-hours, an increase of 11.4% compared with the same period last year; anthracite production reached 21.135 million tons, an increase of 4.4%; lignite production reached 38.1008 million tons. Pig iron production reached 3.149 million tons, an increase of 12.8% compared with the same period last year; steel production reached 4.528 million tons, an increase of 10.8%; steel (excluding steel pipes) production reached 2.932 million tons, an increase of 5.9%; cement production was 3.555 million tons, an increase of 18.9%; the number of passenger cars produced was 36,865, an increase of 18.5% compared with the first three quarters of last year; tractor production reached 21,285 units, an increase of more than 18%.

Compared with the same period last year, the output of household refrigerators in Czechoslovakia increased by more than 25%, cotton fabrics increased by 5.5%, woolen fabrics increased by 6.7%, and leather and rubber shoes increased by 8% in the first three quarters of this year.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 23rd -- This reporter reports: In this year's autumn harvest and planting, Czechoslovak farmers waged a fierce battle against a drought unseen in many years. With the strong support of the people across the country, they worked day and night and finally achieved victory.

Currently, although autumn field work is still in full swing, both the autumn harvest and planting have achieved good results.

This year, Czechoslovakia's grain, hops, feed, and other crops were sown in a timely manner, so the harvest was very good and has already been completed. The crops that farmers have been rushing to harvest recently are mainly sugar beets and potatoes. Now, the potato harvest has been completed, and the sugar beet harvest will be completed within days. With the exception of winter wheat, the sowing of other crops has also been basically completed. According to Czechoslovak newspapers, the potato harvest this year is good, and the sugar beet harvest is also expected to complete the plan, with yields in some states even increasing.

### 保捷两国头九月齐奏跃进凯歌  保加利亚：工业总产值增长25.1％，农作物收成良好，家畜家禽大增  捷克斯洛伐克：工业总产值增长10.9％，农民战胜干旱进行秋收秋种

1959-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    保捷两国头九月齐奏跃进凯歌
    保加利亚：工业总产值增长25.1％，农作物收成良好，家畜家禽大增
    捷克斯洛伐克：工业总产值增长10.9％，农民战胜干旱进行秋收秋种
    新华社索非亚24日电　保加利亚部长会议中央统计局24日发表了今年头九个月的生产公报。公报宣布，今年头九个月，保加利亚完成了工业总产值计划的100.4％，工业总产值比去年同期增长了25.1％。公报中列举的四十二种重要的工业品的产量都比去年同期增加10％以上。
    公报指出，在今年头九个月内，保加利亚共生产了二十七亿六千五百万度电，比去年同期增长26％；一千一百二十二万七千吨煤，比去年同期增长19％；十七万零五百一十吨钢，比去年同期增长10％；二万五千四百零二吨铅，比去年同期增长35％，其他产品的产量和与去年同期相比的增长率如下：氮肥——四万二千零七十六吨，22％；磷肥——二万零二百一十六吨，一倍；水泥——一百零七万吨，57％；棉织品——一亿五千三百万零八千米，23％；毛织品——一千三百八十四万一千米，33％。
    在其他工业制品中，与去年同期比较，今年头九个月蔬菜罐头增长了35％，水果罐头增长了64％，奶油增长了33％，植物油增长了20％，糖制品增长了19％。
    公报说，在农业方面也取得了很大成绩。虽然一些地区的气候对某些农作物不利，但是，由于农业生产合作社社员们的努力，几乎所有农作物的收成都很好。
    在今年头九个月内，为农业生产合作社服务的拖拉机比去年同期增加了五千二百五十台（每台按十五匹马力计算）。在这期间，全国机器拖拉机站完成的工作总量比去年同期多38％。
    今年以来，保加利亚的畜牧业也取得了很大发展。到10月1日为止，全国国营农场和农业生产合作社的牛、羊、猪和鸡的头数，分别比去年同期增长了32％、15％、54％和两倍半。其中，全国农业生产合作社猪的头数增加了一倍多。
  公报说，今年头九个月内，在保加利亚工业、建筑业、国内贸易和运输等部门，职工人数比去年同期增加了十万零二千四百八十五人。与去年同期比较，工业和建筑业中的劳动生产率分别增加了6.9％和5.7％。
    新华社布拉格24日电　据此间宣布：今年前三个季度内，捷克斯洛伐克的工业生产计划完成了101.7％，工业总产值比去年同一时期增加了10.9％。
    在今年头9个月中，捷克斯洛伐克的电力产量增加为一百五十七亿五千一百万度，比去年同期增加了11.4％；无烟煤产量达到二千一百十三万五千吨，增加了4.4％；褐煤产量达到三千八百一十万零八千吨。生铁产量达到三百一十四万九千吨，比去年同期增加了12.8％；钢产量达到四百五十二万八千吨，增加了10.8％；钢材（钢管不包括在内）产量达到二百九十三万二千吨，增加了5.9％；水泥产量为三百五十五万五千吨，增加了18.9％；客车的生产数量为三万六千八百六十五辆，比去年前三个季度增加18.5％；拖拉机产量达到二万一千二百八十五台，增加了18％以上。
    与去年同期比较，今年头三个季度内，捷克斯洛伐克的家用冰箱产量增加了25％以上，棉织品增加了5.5％，毛织品增加了6.7％，皮鞋和胶鞋增加了8％。
    据新华社布拉格23日电　本社记者报道：在今年的秋收秋种中，捷克斯洛伐克农民同多年未有的干旱进行了一场激烈的战斗。他们在全国人民的大力支援下，夜以继日地进行劳动，终于取得了胜利。
    目前，虽然秋季田间工作还在紧张进行，但秋收秋种都已取得了良好成果。
    今年，捷克斯洛伐克的粮食、啤酒花、饲料和其它作物播种得及时，因此，收成很好，而且早已收割完毕。农民们最近抢收的主要是甜菜和马铃薯。现在，马铃薯的收获已经完毕，甜菜的收获日内即将完成。除冬小麦外，其它农作物的播种工作也都已基本完成。据捷克斯洛伐克报纸报道，今年马铃薯的收成是良好的，预计甜菜的收成也将完成计划，某些州的产量还将有所提高。
