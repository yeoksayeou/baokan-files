Title: Yemen Kingdom Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing to Attend Our 10th Anniversary Celebration

Author:

Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. The government delegation of the Kingdom of Yemen, led by the Minister of State of the Kingdom of Yemen, Sayyid Ali Al-Moayyad, arrived in Beijing by plane today to attend the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.
The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at the Capital Airport by Ding Xilin, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, and responsible persons from relevant departments of the Chinese government.

### 应邀参加我建国十周年盛典  也门王国政府代表团到京

1959-10-01
第7版()
专栏：

    应邀参加我建国十周年盛典
    也门王国政府代表团到京
    新华社30日讯　由也门王国国务大臣赛义德·阿里·穆安亚德率领的也门王国政府代表团，今天乘飞机到达北京，前来参加中华人民共和国建国十周年庆祝典礼。
    贵宾们在首都机场受到对外文化联络委员会副主任丁西林及我国政府有关部门负责人的热烈欢迎。
