Title: Grand Rally in Pyongyang Welcomes President Novotný; Kim Il-sung and Novotný Emphasize Friendship Between Korean and Czechoslovak Peoples
Author:
Date: 1959-10-09
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 8th: A grand rally of 200,000 people was held this afternoon in Kim Il-sung Square, Pyongyang, to welcome the President of the Czechoslovak Republic and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, Novotný.

	Today, Kim Il-sung Square was a sea of flowers and a forest of flags. Workers, students, and citizens dressed in festive attire held up portraits of Kim Il-sung and Novotný. Huge Czechoslovak and Korean national flags and slogans were hung on the buildings on both sides of the square. The slogans read: "Long Live the Eternal Friendship and Unity Between the Peoples of Korea and Czechoslovakia," "Long Live the Indestructible Solidarity of the Great Socialist Camp."

	At 4:30 PM, Novotny, accompanied by Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party and Prime Minister of the Cabinet, and Choi Yong-kun, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, ascended the rostrum. At this moment, the crowd in the square raised the flowers and colored flags in their hands and gave thunderous cheers, and countless colored balloons flew into the sky.

	After the start of the meeting, Kim Il-sung spoke first. He expressed a warm welcome to the distinguished guests. He expressed his gratitude to the Czechoslovak people for the material and spiritual assistance given to the Korean people during the Korean War and after the war. He praised the great achievements of the Czechoslovak people in socialist construction. He said: We have learned many experiences from the Czechoslovak people, and at the same time, we have achieved rapid development in socialist construction.

	Novotný then spoke at the meeting. He expressed his gratitude for the warm welcome from the Korean people. He said: Through my visits to China and Korea, I have further seen the socialist camp becoming increasingly stronger. He finally wished the Korean people greater achievements in the future and the realization of the peaceful reunification of the motherland.

### 平壤盛会欢迎诺沃提尼总统  金日成和诺沃提尼强调朝捷人民友谊

1959-10-09
第6版()
专栏：

    平壤盛会欢迎诺沃提尼总统
    金日成和诺沃提尼强调朝捷人民友谊
    据新华社平壤8日电　平壤市二十万群众今天下午在金日成广场举行大会，欢迎捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统、捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会第一书记诺沃提尼。
    今天的金日成广场上，鲜花似海，旗帜如林。穿着节日服装的工人、学生、市民高举着金日成和诺沃提尼的画像。广场两旁的大厦上悬挂着巨幅的朝捷两国国旗和标语，标语上写着“朝捷两国人民间的永恒的友谊团结万岁”、“伟大的社会主义阵营的牢不可破的团结万岁”。
    四时半，诺沃提尼由朝鲜劳动党中央委员会委员长、内阁首相金日成和朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健陪同登上主席台。这时，广场上的群众高举起手中的鲜花和彩旗，发出雷鸣般的欢呼，无数的彩色气球飞上天空。
    大会开始后，金日成首先讲话。他对贵宾们表示热烈的欢迎。他对捷克斯洛伐克人民在朝鲜战争时期和战后给朝鲜人民的物质和精神援助表示感谢。他赞扬了捷克斯洛伐克人民在社会主义建设中取得的巨大成就。他说：我们向捷克斯洛伐克人民学习了许多经验，同时我们也在社会主义建设中取得了飞跃的发展。
    诺沃提尼接着在大会上讲话。他对朝鲜人民的热烈欢迎表示感谢。他说：我通过对中国和朝鲜的访问，进一步看到社会主义阵营日益加强。他最后祝朝鲜人民今后取得更大的成就，实现祖国的和平统一。
