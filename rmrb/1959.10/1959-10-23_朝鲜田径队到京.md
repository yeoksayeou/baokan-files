Title: Korean Track and Field Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-23
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd: The first group of eleven members of the track and field team from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by team leader Li Soon-gil, arrived in Beijing by train today at the invitation of our country to participate in the China, Mongolia, and North Korea trilateral track and field friendship competition. The second group of six track and field team members is scheduled to arrive on the 23rd.

### 朝鲜田径队到京

1959-10-23
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜田径队到京
    据新华社22日讯　应邀来我国参加朝、蒙、中三国田径友谊赛的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国田径队第一批人员十一人，今天由领队李顺吉率领乘火车到达北京。田径队第二批人员六人预定23日到达。
