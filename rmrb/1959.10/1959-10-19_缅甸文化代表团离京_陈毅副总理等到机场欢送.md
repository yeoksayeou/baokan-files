Title: Burmese Cultural Delegation Leaves Beijing; Vice Premier Chen Yi and Others See Them Off at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1959-10-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The Burmese government representative, Minister of Culture of Burma U Chit Thoung, and the Burmese cultural friendship delegation he led, left Beijing by plane this morning.

Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi went to the airport to see them off. Also present at the airport were Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Ding Xilin, Deputy Director, Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and others.

The Burmese Ambassador to China, Kyaw Winn, and embassy officials, and Pham Binh, Counselor of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, also saw them off at the airport.

Before boarding the plane, the distinguished guests received flowers presented by young women from Beijing.

### 缅甸文化代表团离京  陈毅副总理等到机场欢送

1959-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

　　缅甸文化代表团离京
    陈毅副总理等到机场欢送
    新华社18日讯　缅甸政府代表、缅甸文化部部长吴漆东和他率领的缅甸文化友好代表团，在今天上午乘飞机离开北京。
    陈毅副总理兼外交部长到机场欢送。到机场欢送的，还有对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若、副主任丁西林，文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长章汉夫等人。
    缅甸驻中国大使叫温和大使馆官员，越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆参赞范平也到机场欢送。
    在登上飞机以前，贵宾们接受了北京女青年的献花。
