Title: China and Guinea Establish Diplomatic Relations; Vice Premier Chen Yi Holds Talks with Guinean Government Representatives and Agrees to Issue a Joint Communiqué on the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1959-10-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Government of the People's Republic of China, and The Government of the Republic of Guinea, decide to establish diplomatic relations, and send diplomatic representatives to each other at the ambassadorial level.

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi, this afternoon received the representative of the Government of the Republic of Guinea, Minister of Education of Guinea, Barry Diawandou, and held talks on the issue of developing relations between the two countries. Both sides unanimously agreed to issue a joint communiqué on the establishment of diplomatic relations between the two countries.

Diawandou also presented valuable gifts to Chairman Mao Zedong on behalf of the President of the Republic of Guinea.

Present at the reception were Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jiuzhuang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhang Zhixiang. He Gongkai, Deputy Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department, were also present.

### 我国和几内亚建立外交关系  陈毅副总理和几内亚政府代表会谈，同意发表建交联合公报

1959-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我国和几内亚建立外交关系
    陈毅副总理和几内亚政府代表会谈，同意发表建交联合公报
    新华社4日讯　中华人民共和国和几内亚共和国建立外交关系的联合公报
    中华人民共和国政府和几内亚共和国政府决定建立外交关系，并且互派大使级的外交代表。
    新华社4日讯　国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅，今天下午接见了几内亚共和国政府代表、几内亚教育部部长巴里·迪亚万杜就发展两国关系问题进行了会谈，双方一致同意发表两国建交的联合公报。
    迪亚万杜并代表几内亚共和国总统向毛泽东主席赠送了珍贵的礼物。
    接见时在座的，有对外贸易部部长叶季壮、外交部副部长章汉夫、中国人民对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥。外交部西亚非洲司副司长何功楷、礼宾司副司长马振武也在座。
