Title: Korean People's Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-20
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, 19th. The Korean Labor People's Delegation, led by Choi Ki-chul, Vice Chairman of the Korean Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations and Vice Chairman of the Korean National Committee for the Defense of Peace, left Beijing by train this morning to return home after completing its visit to our country.
　　Liu Changsheng, Chu Tunan, Lao She, Xi Zhanyuan, Shen Zijiu, Zhu Yujin, Qi Muguan and others were at the station to see them off.
　　Korean Ambassador to China, Li Yongho, and Korean peace activist, Huang Feng-jiu, and others also saw them off at the station.

### 朝鲜人民代表团回国

1959-10-20
第5版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民代表团回国
    新华社19日讯　由朝鲜对外文化联络委员会副委员长、朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会副委员长崔基哲率领的朝鲜劳动人民代表团，结束了在我国的参观访问后，在今天上午乘火车离开北京回国。
    到车站送行的有刘长胜、楚图南、老舍、郗占元、沈兹九、朱语今、戚慕光等人。
    朝鲜驻华大使李永镐和朝鲜和平人士黄凤九等也到车站送行。
