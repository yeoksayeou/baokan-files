Title: Premier Zhou Receives Subandrio
Author:
Date: 1959-10-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 9th October. Premier Zhou Enlai received the Indonesian Foreign Minister Subandrio at noon today.

    Present at the reception were Chen Yi, Liao Chengzhi, Zhang Hanfu and Zhang Wenjin.

    Also present at the reception were the Indonesian Ambassador to China, Wirjopranoto, the Director of the Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Suska, the Director of the Bureau, Ilias Hamsah, and the Secretary, Kasno.

    After the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi had lunch with Subandrio and others. (Attached picture)
    Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council received Indonesian Foreign Minister Subandrio (center) on October 9. The picture shows the scene of the reception. Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Ruifeng.

### 周总理接见苏班德里约

1959-10-10
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理接见苏班德里约
    新华社9日讯　周恩来总理今天中午接见了印度尼西亚外交部长苏班德里约。
    接见时，在座的有陈毅、廖承志、章汉夫和章文晋。
    接见时，在座的还有印度尼西亚驻华大使维约普拉诺托、印度尼西亚外交部司长苏斯卡、局长伊里亚斯·汉姆沙、秘书卡斯诺。
    接见后，周恩来总理、陈毅副总理同苏班德里约等共进午餐。（附图片）
