Title: Sino-German Swimming and Water Polo Tournament Concludes
Author:
Date: 1959-10-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th. The two-day Sino-German swimming and water polo friendly matches concluded this evening. In the final eight swimming events held this evening, swimmers from the Free German Youth League of the German Democratic Republic won six championships. Chinese youth swimming team athletes won the men's 200-meter freestyle and the men's 400-meter medley relay, and broke three national records. The visiting water polo team defeated the Chinese People's Liberation Army water polo team tonight by a score of 10 to 5. The visiting team led 5 to 4 in the first half.

After the friendly matches, Vice Premier of the State Council Bo Yibo met with the athletes from both sides.

The visiting team will soon leave Beijing to visit and compete in Shanghai, Guangzhou and other places.

### 中德游泳、水球赛结束

1959-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    中德游泳、水球赛结束
    新华社25日讯　为时两天的中德游泳、水球友谊赛，今晚全部结束。在今晚举行的最后八项游泳比赛中，德意志民主共和国自由德国青年联盟游泳选手获得了六项冠军；中国青年游泳队选手获得了男子二百米自由泳、男子四百米混合式接力两项冠军，并且打破了三项全国纪录。客队水球队今晚以十比五战胜了中国人民解放军水球队。上半场客队以五比四领先。
    友谊比赛结束以后，国务院副总理薄一波接见了双方的运动员。
    客队即将离北京去上海、广州等地访问比赛。
