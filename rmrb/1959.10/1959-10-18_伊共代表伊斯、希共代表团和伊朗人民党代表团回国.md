Title: Iraqi Communist Party Representative Is, Greek Communist Party Delegation and Iranian People's Party Delegation Return Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-18
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 17th. The Iraqi Communist Party delegation representative, Comrade Mohammed Hussein Abu Is; the Greek Communist Party delegation, headed by the Chairman of the Central Committee of the Greek Communist Party, Comrade Apostolos Grozos; and the Iranian People's Party delegation, headed by Comrade Tabari, member of the Central Executive Committee of the Iranian People's Party, left Beijing by the same plane today to return home after attending the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and visiting various parts of our country.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Tan Zhenlin, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat; Liu Lantao, alternate secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee; and Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee.

Azar Ojeru, a member of the Iranian People's Party delegation, will continue to visit our country before returning home.

### 伊共代表伊斯、希共代表团和伊朗人民党代表团回国

1959-10-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　伊共代表伊斯、希共代表团和伊朗人民党代表团回国
    新华社17日讯　伊拉克共产党代表团代表穆罕默德·侯赛因·阿布·伊斯同志，以希腊共产党中央委员会主席阿波斯托洛斯·格罗佐斯同志为首的希腊共产党代表团，以伊朗人民党中央执行委员会委员塔巴里同志为首的伊朗人民党代表团，在参加中华人民共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼和在我国各地参观访问之后，于今天同乘一架飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的，有中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记谭震林，中央书记处候补书记刘澜涛，中央委员刘宁一。
    伊朗人民党代表团团员阿札尔·欧捷鲁将在我国继续参观访问以后回国。
