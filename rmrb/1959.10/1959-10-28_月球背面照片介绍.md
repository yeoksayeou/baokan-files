Title: Introduction to Photographs of the Far Side of the Moon
Author: 
Date: 1959-10-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th -- According to TASS: The Soviet "Pravda" today published photographs of the far side of the moon taken by an automatic interplanetary station and provided a detailed introduction.

    The photographs capture most of the far side of the moon, as well as a small portion of the visible side. The presence of the visible side in the photographs allows for the determination of the coordinates of objects on the far side.

    The photographs show that the far side of the moon has many mountainous regions, but few of the maria (seas) found on the visible side. There are some very prominent ringed maria in the southern hemisphere and near the equator of the far side.

    South-southeast of Humboldt Sea is a mountain range, more than 2,000 kilometers in length, extending through the equator into the southern hemisphere. Behind the mountains is a vast continent with strong reflectivity.

    East of the aforementioned mountain range is a cluster of four medium-sized ringed mountains. The largest of these ringed mountains is approximately 70 kilometers in diameter.

    Between 20 and 30 degrees north latitude and 140 and 160 degrees west longitude on the far side, there is a ringed sea approximately 300 kilometers in diameter. The southern end of this sea has a bay.

    In the southern hemisphere of the far side, there is a large ringed mountain, more than 100 kilometers in diameter, with a dark bottom and a bright central hill.

    The photographs show that a large part of the Sea of ​​the South, which is visible on the moon, is also on the far side of the moon.

### 月球背面照片介绍

1959-10-28
第5版()
专栏：

    月球背面照片介绍
    新华社27日讯　据塔斯社消息：苏联“真理报”今天公布了自动行星际站拍摄的月球背面的照片，并对它作了详细的介绍。
    照片摄入了月球背面的大部分，同时也摄入了月球可见面的一小部分。由于照片上有可见面的一部分，从而就能够确定月球背面对象的座标。
    照片表明，月球背面有许多山区，而像可见面中的那种海却很少。在月球背面南半球和赤道附近有一些非常突出的寰形海。
    在洪保德海南南东方向有一条山系，全长二千公里以上，通过赤道向南半球延伸。山的后面有一块辽阔的大陆，它的反射力很强。
    上述山系以东，有四个中等寰形山组成的山群。其中最大的寰形山直径约七十公里。
    在月球背面北纬二十度到三十度之间、西经一百四十度到一百六十度之间，有一个直径约三百公里的寰形海。这个海的南端有一个海湾。
    在背面南半球有一座大寰形山，直径一百多公里，它的底部深暗，有一个明亮的中央丘。
    照片表明，在月球可见面上的南海还有很大一部分是在月球的背面。

    罗荣诺索夫（1711—1765年），伟大的俄罗斯科学家，他的著作奠定了物理、化学和地理学的基础。
