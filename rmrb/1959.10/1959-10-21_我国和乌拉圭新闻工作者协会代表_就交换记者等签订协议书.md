Title: Representatives of Chinese and Uruguayan Journalists' Associations Sign Agreement on Exchange of Journalists, etc.
Author:
Date: 1959-10-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 20th — Representatives of the All-China Journalists Association and the Uruguayan Journalists' Association, after friendly talks, reached an agreement on the regular exchange of journalists and exchange of professional experience between the two journalists' associations in the future. The signing ceremony of the agreement was held in Beijing at 6:30 pm today.

Wang Yi, representative of the All-China Journalists Association, and Carlos Borche and Hugo Rodriguez Vela, representatives of the Uruguayan Journalists' Association, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective parties.

On the Chinese side, Wu Lengxi, Acting President of the All-China Journalists Association, Vice President Wang Yunsheng, as well as Yao Zhen, Chang Zhiqing, Li Bingquan, and Gao Ji attended the signing ceremony.

On the Uruguayan side, Francisco Rodriguez Camusso, head of the Uruguayan Parliamentary Delegation to China, members Dr. Angel Maria Gianola, Semala Michellia, and Franciscos Mario Ubillos attended the signing ceremony. (Attached picture)

### 我国和乌拉圭新闻工作者协会代表  就交换记者等签订协议书

1959-10-21
第5版()
专栏：

    我国和乌拉圭新闻工作者协会代表
    就交换记者等签订协议书
    新华社20日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会的代表和乌拉圭新闻工作者协会的代表在进行了亲切的会谈后，就两国新闻工作者协会今后经常交换记者和交流业务经验等问题达成了协议。协议书的签字仪式今天下午六时半在北京举行。
    中华全国新闻工作者协会代表王揖，乌拉圭新闻工作者协会代表卡洛斯·博尔切和乌果·罗德里格塞·贝拉，分别代表双方在协议书上签字。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的，有中华全国新闻工作者协会代会长吴冷西、副会长王芸生以及姚溱、常芝青、李炳泉、高集。
    乌拉圭方面参加签字仪式的，有乌拉圭议会访华代表团团长弗朗西斯科·罗德里格斯·卡穆索，团员安赫耳·玛丽亚·希亚诺拉博士，塞马拉·米切利亚，弗朗西科斯·马里奥·乌比略斯。（附图片）
