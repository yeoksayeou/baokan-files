Title: Hoang Van Hoan Says at Hanoi Celebration Banquet That the People's China's Ten Years Are Full of Victory and Glory
Author:
Date: 1959-10-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 30th: The Central Preparatory Committee of Vietnam for Celebrating the Tenth Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China held a grand banquet last night at the Ba Dinh Club in Hanoi to entertain Chinese experts and technicians who are helping Vietnam in its construction.

Among those present at the banquet were: Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Ministers Truong Chinh, Pham Hung, Phan Ke Toai, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of Central Preparatory Committee of Vietnam for Celebrating the Tenth Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China Hoang Van Hoan, Vice President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association Nguyen Xuan Thuy, and ministers of various government departments.

Hoang Van Hoan said in his speech at the banquet that the People's Republic of China has been established for ten years, which is a decade full of victory and glory, a decade in which the Chinese people have built their magnificent country, and a decade in which China has made great contributions to the cause of human progress around the world.

Hoang Van Hoan praised the great achievements made by China in the ten years since its founding. He said that the Chinese people's leap forward of "one day equals twenty years" made the imperialists tremble, while the people of socialist countries felt extremely excited and strengthened their confidence.

Ambassador He Wei also spoke at the meeting.

### 黄文欢在河内庆祝宴会上说人民中国的十年充满了胜利和光辉

1959-10-02
第6版()
专栏：

    黄文欢在河内庆祝宴会上说
    人民中国的十年充满了胜利和光辉
    据新华社河内9月30日电　越南庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年中央筹备委员会昨天晚上在河内巴亭俱乐部举行盛大宴会，招待帮助越南建设的中国专家和技术人员。
    出席宴会的有：越南民主共和国总理范文同、副总理长征、范雄、潘继遂，国会常务委员会委员长孙德胜，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年中央筹备委员会主席黄文欢，越中友好协会副会长阮春水和政府各部部长。
    黄文欢在宴会上致词说，中华人民共和国成立已经十年了，这是充满着胜利和光辉的十年，是中国人民建设自己雄伟的国家的十年，也是中国为全世界人类进步事业作出巨大贡献的十年。
    黄文欢赞扬了中国建国十年来所取得的伟大成就。他说，中国人民的“一天等于二十年”的跃进，使帝国主义发抖，而社会主义各国人民却感到无比兴奋，并且增强了信心。
    何伟大使在会上也讲了话。
