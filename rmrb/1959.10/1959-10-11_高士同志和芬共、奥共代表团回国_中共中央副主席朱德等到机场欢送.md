Title: Comrade Ghosh and the Delegations of the Finnish Communist Party and Austrian Communist Party Return Home; Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, and Others Welcomed Them at the Airport

Author:
Date: 1959-10-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 10th. Comrade Ajoy Kumar Ghosh, head of the delegation of the Communist Party of India and General Secretary of the National Committee of the Communist Party of India; the delegation of the Finnish Communist Party headed by Comrade Aimo Aaltonen, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Finnish Communist Party; and the delegation of the Austrian Communist Party headed by Comrade Koplenig, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Austrian Communist Party, after participating in the celebrations of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and visiting various parts of our country, left Beijing by the same plane this afternoon to return home.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee; Lin Boqu, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee; Tan Zheng, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee; and Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee.

### 高士同志和芬共、奥共代表团回国  中共中央副主席朱德等到机场欢送

1959-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　高士同志和芬共、奥共代表团回国
    中共中央副主席朱德等到机场欢送
    新华社10日讯　印度共产党代表团团长、印度共产党全国委员会总书记阿约艾·库马尔·高士同志，以芬兰共产党中央委员会主席艾摩·阿尔托宁同志为首的芬兰共产党代表团，以奥地利共产党中央委员会主席柯普勒尼希同志为首的奥地利共产党代表团，在参加了中华人民共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼和在我国各地参观访问之后，今天下午同乘一架飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的，有中共中央副主席朱德，中央政治局委员林伯渠，中央书记处书记谭政，中央委员刘宁一。
