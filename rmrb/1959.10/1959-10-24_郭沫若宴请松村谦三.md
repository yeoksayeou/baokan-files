Title: Guo Moruo Hosts Banquet for Matsumura Kenzō
Author:
Date: 1959-10-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd — Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, hosted a dinner at his residence this evening for Mr. Matsumura Kenzō and his daughter and sons: Kobori Haruko and Hirayama Hisao.

Those present included: Takeyama Yūtarō, Ide Ichitarō, Furui Yoshimi, and Wada Rinseiji.

Shen Zijiu was present as an escort.

### 郭沫若宴请松村谦三

1959-10-24
第6版()
专栏：

    郭沫若宴请松村谦三
    新华社23日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若今日晚间在住宅同松村谦三先生和他的小姐和公子：小堀治子和平山久雄共进晚餐。
    出席的有：竹山祐太郎、井出一太郎、古井喜实、和田林政吉。
    出席作陪的有沈兹九。
