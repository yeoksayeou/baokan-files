Title: Czechoslovak Production Innovators Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-22
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, October 21st: The Czechoslovak Production Innovators Delegation, consisting of six members led by Antonín Malík, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Machine Manufacturing Trade Union, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the National Committee of the First Machine Building Trade Union of China for a friendly visit to our country to disseminate and exchange advanced experiences. Shao Ziyan, Chairman of the First Machine Building Trade Union of China, and others went to the airport to welcome them.

### 捷生产革新者代表团到京

1959-10-22
第5版()
专栏：

    捷生产革新者代表团到京
    新华社21日讯　应中国第一机械工会全国委员会邀请前来我国进行友好访问并传播和交流先进经验的捷克斯洛伐克生产革新者代表团一行六人，由团长、捷克斯洛伐克机器制造工会中央委员会主席安托宁·马立克率领，今天乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的，有中国第一机械工会主席邵子言等人。
