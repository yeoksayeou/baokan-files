Title: The Soviet Union Exceeds Industrial Output Plan for First Three Quarters; Production of Steel, Coal, Oil, Electricity, and Other Products Greatly Increased Compared to the Same Period Last Year; Hungarian State-Owned Industry Exceeds Third Quarter Plan; Agricultural Production is Excellent
Author:
Date: 1959-10-15
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 14th: According to an official communiqué issued by the Central Statistical Bureau of the Soviet Council of Ministers, the Soviet Union exceeded its industrial production plan for the first nine months of this year by 4%. Compared with the same period last year, the total industrial output value increased by 12% in the first nine months of this year. Labor productivity in industry increased by 8%.

The communiqué stated that the Soviet Union produced the following in the first nine months of this year: pig iron - 31.8 million tons, steel - 44.4 million tons, rolled steel - 34.7 million tons, coke - 39.7 million tons, iron ore - 70.5 million tons, coal - 380 million tons, oil - 95.3 million tons, gas - 26.1 billion cubic meters, and electricity - 166 billion kilowatt-hours.

Compared with the same period last year, the increase in the output of various products is as follows: pig iron - 2.7 million tons, steel - 3.6 million tons, rolled steel - 2.6 million tons, coal - 8.6 million tons, oil - 11.9 million tons, gas - 5 billion cubic meters, electricity - 18 billion kilowatt-hours, and the production of chemical industry products greatly increased.

In the first nine months of this year, the Soviet Union produced 3.4 billion square meters of cotton fabrics, 308 million square meters of woolen fabrics, 358 million square meters of linen fabrics, 509 million square meters of silk fabrics, and 287 million pairs of leather shoes.

State purchases of meat, eggs, and wool have greatly increased.

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 14th: Newspapers here today published a communiqué from the Hungarian Central Statistical Office stating that Hungarian state-owned industry exceeded its production plan for the third quarter of this year by 6%. Production in the first nine months of this year increased by 10% compared to the same period last year.

The average daily production of state-owned industry in the third quarter of this year increased by 12% compared to the average daily production in the same period last year.

The communiqué stated that coal production in the third quarter was 6,321,000 tons, an increase of 5% over the same period last year, oil - 286,000 tons, an increase of 30%, electricity - 1.759 billion kilowatt-hours, an increase of 9%, steel - 436,000 tons, an increase of 11%, aluminum - 12,000 tons, an increase of 14%, and tractor production increased by 32%. Nitrogen fertilizer increased by 37%, and television sets by 131%.

The average daily production per worker in the third quarter increased by 4% compared to the same period last year.

The communiqué stated that agricultural production this year has been completed very well, and 30% more bread grain and feed grain have been harvested than last year. There has also been a corresponding increase in state grain purchases.

The foreign trade plan for the third quarter was also overfulfilled. Exports increased by 8% compared to the same period last year, and imports increased by 33%.

Residents' cash income increased by 8% compared to the same period last year.

The retail sales of goods in the third quarter increased by 13% compared to the same period last year.

### 苏联前三季工业总产值计划超额完成  钢铁煤石油电力等产品产量比去年同期大大增加  匈国营工业超额完成第三季度计划农业生产很好

1959-10-15
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏联前三季工业总产值计划超额完成
    钢铁煤石油电力等产品产量比去年同期大大增加
    匈国营工业超额完成第三季度计划农业生产很好
    新华社14日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科14日讯：苏联部长会议中央统计局发表的正式公报说，苏联今年头九个月的工业生产超额4％完成了总产值计划。同去年同一时期相比，今年头九个月的工业总产值增加了12％。工业中的劳动生产率提高了8％。
    公报说，苏联在今年头九个月中生产生铁——三千一百八十万吨、钢——四千四百四十万吨、钢材——三千四百七十万吨、焦炭——三千九百七十万吨、铁矿石——七千零五百万吨、煤——三亿八千万吨、石油——九千五百三十万吨、煤气——二百六十一亿立方米、电力——一千六百六十亿度。
    同去年同一时期相比，各种产品产量的增加情况如下：生铁——二百七十万吨、钢——三百六十万吨、钢材——二百六十万吨、煤——八百六十万吨、石油——一千一百九十万吨、煤气——五十亿立方米、电力——一百八十亿度，化学工业产品的生产大大增加了。
    今年头九个月苏联生产棉织品三十四亿平方米、毛织品——三亿零八百万平方米、亚麻织品——三亿五千八百万平方米、丝织品——五亿零九百万平方米、皮鞋——两亿八千七百万双。
    国家对肉类、蛋类和羊毛的收购量大大增加了。
    新华社布达佩斯14日电　此间报纸今天刊登的匈牙利中央统计局的公报指出，匈牙利国营工业超额6％完成了今年第三季度的生产计划。今年头九个月的生产比去年同时期增长了10％。
    国营工业今年第三季度每天的平均生产比去年同时期每天的生产增长了12％。
    公报说，第三季度煤产量为六百三十二万一千吨，比去年同期增长5％，石油——二十八万六千吨，增长30％，电力——十七亿五千九百万度，增长9％，钢——四十三万六千吨，增长11％，铝——一万二千吨，增长14％，拖拉机产量增长32％。氮肥增长37％，电视机131％。
    第三季度每个工人每天的平均生产比去年同期增长4％。
    公报说，今年的农业生产完成得很好，面包谷物和饲料谷物都比去年多收了30％。国家收购的粮食也有相应的增长。
    第三季度的对外贸易计划也超额完成。出口比去年同期增加8％，进口增加33％。
    居民的现金收入比去年同期增长8％。
    商品零售额在第三季度比去年同期增长13％。
