Title: Panchen, Pagbalha, and Others Visit Various Places; Ngapoi, Jigmey, and Others Leave Beijing by Plane to Return to Tibet
Author:
Date: 1959-10-27
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 26th - Panchen Erdeni Qoigyijabu, Acting Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, and Pagbalha Geleg Namgyal, Vice Chairman of the Preparatory Committee, accompanied by Wang Feng, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee and Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, left Beijing by special train this afternoon to visit various places.

Xi Zhongxun, Deputy Premier of the State Council, Burhan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC, Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Yang Jingren, and Xie Hechou, Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, and others saw them off at the station.

Accompanying Panchen Erdeni on his visits to various places were Lhalu Tsewang Dorje, Tangkyab Rabten, both Standing Committee members of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, Yao Xigongbo Tsetan, a member of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, Ngachu Losang Chonpel, Vice President of the Tibetan Branch of the Buddhist Association of China, Dorje Pagmo, a female Living Buddha who recently returned from India, as well as other entourage members of Panchen Erdeni.

Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, Vice Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, and Changdong Jigmey, Sampo Tsewang Rigzin, and Ngapoi Tsetan Drolkar, members of the Preparatory Committee, returned to Tibet by plane this morning.

Deputy Premier Xi Zhongxun and heads of relevant departments saw off Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme and others at the airport.

During their stay in Beijing, Panchen Erdeni and others, in addition to participating in various celebrations for the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, also visited the National Culture Palace, the History Museum, the National Industrial and Transportation Exhibition, the National Agricultural Exhibition, the Miyun Reservoir, and toured the Summer Palace, among other places.

### 班禅、帕巴拉等到各地参观  阿沛、计晋美等离京飞返西藏

1959-10-27
第3版()
专栏：

    班禅、帕巴拉等到各地参观
    阿沛、计晋美等离京飞返西藏
    新华社26日讯　西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞和副主任委员帕巴拉·卓列朗杰在中共中央统战部副部长、民族事务委员会副主任汪锋陪同下，今天下午乘专车离京前往各地参观访问。
    国务院副总理习仲勋，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏，民族事务委员会副主任杨静仁、谢鹤筹等到车站送行。
    同班禅额尔德尼一起前往各地参观访问的，还有西藏自治区筹备委员会常务委员拉敏·益喜楚臣、唐曲雅培，西藏自治区筹备委员会委员尧西·贡保才旦，中国佛协西藏分会副会长恩久·洛桑群培，最近从印度回国的多吉帕姆女活佛，以及班禅额尔德尼的其他随行人员。
    西藏自治区筹备委员会副主任委员阿沛·阿旺晋美和常务委员詹东·计晋美，桑颇·才旺仁增，委员阿沛·才旦卓噶今天上午乘飞机返西藏。
    习仲勋副总理和有关部门负责人到机场欢送阿沛·阿旺晋美等。
    班禅额尔德尼等在北京这一期间，除了参加建国十周年各项庆祝活动外，还参观了民族文化宫、历史博物馆、全国工业交通展览会、全国农业展览会、密云水库和游览了颐和园等地。
