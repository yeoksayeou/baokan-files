Title: Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-14
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 13th - The Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, headed by the Vice-Chairman of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, G.S. Tikhomirov, who were invited to visit our country and participate in the celebrations of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home. The deputy head of the delegation, Lavrentiev, returned home earlier on the 4th of this month.
    Those who went to the airport to see the guests off today included Liu Ningyi and Shao Lizi, Vice-Presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Liu Changsheng, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Qu Tangliang, Secretary-General, Liu Xiyuan, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League and Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, Liu Qingyang, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, and members of the Presidium, Shen Zijiu, Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Wu Han, Vice-President of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Ge Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and other leaders of various people's organizations.

### 苏中友协代表团回国

1959-10-14
第5版()
专栏：

    苏中友协代表团回国
    新华社13日讯　应邀来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的以苏中友好协会副主席吉洪诺夫为首的苏中友好协会代表团，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。代表团副团长拉甫连捷夫已在本月4日先行回国。
    今天到机场欢送客人们的，有中苏友好协会总会副会长刘宁一、邵力子、全国总工会副主席刘长胜，和大秘书长区棠亮，共青团中央书记处书记、全国青联主席刘西元，全国妇联副主席刘清扬、主席团委员沈兹九，全国学联主席胡启立，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，北京市中苏友好协会副会长吴晗，中苏友好协会总会副秘书长戈宝权等各人民团体的负责人。
