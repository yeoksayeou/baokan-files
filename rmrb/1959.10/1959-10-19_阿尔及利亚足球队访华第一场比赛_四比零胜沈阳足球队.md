Title: Algerian Football Team's First Visit to China: Four to Zero Victory Over Shenyang Football Team
Author:
Date: 1959-10-19
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: The Algerian national football team played its first friendly match in China against the Shenyang football team (i.e., Liaoning team) at the Beijing Xian Nong Tan Stadium today, winning with a score of four to zero. The score was one to zero at halftime.

Tens of thousands of spectators in the capital watched the visiting team's superb skills with a unique style for the first time, frequently applauding their varied passing techniques, highly threatening shots on goal, and good sportsmanship.

Before the start of today's match, a welcoming ceremony was held for the visiting team, with the band playing the national anthems of Algeria and China. After the match, Vice Premier of the State Council He Long and others met the athletes of both sides on the field.

The Algerian national football team will also play a friendly match against the Beijing football team in Beijing on the 21st.

### 阿尔及利亚足球队访华第一场比赛  四比零胜沈阳足球队

1959-10-19
第2版()
专栏：

　　阿尔及利亚足球队访华第一场比赛
    四比零胜沈阳足球队
    据新华社18日讯　阿尔及利亚国家足球队今天在北京先农坛体育场同沈阳足球队（即辽宁队）举行访华的第一场友谊比赛，以四比零获得胜利。上半场是一比零。
    上万名首都观众第一次观赏了有独特风格的客队的精湛球艺，不时为他们那变化多端的传球技巧、极有威胁力的临门劲射和良好的道德作风，报以热烈的掌声。
    在今天的比赛开始前，曾经举行了客队访华的欢迎仪式，乐队高奏阿、中两国国歌。比赛结束以后，国务院副总理贺龙等人到球场上接见了双方的运动员。
    阿尔及利亚国家足球队21日还将在北京同北京足球队进行友谊比赛。
