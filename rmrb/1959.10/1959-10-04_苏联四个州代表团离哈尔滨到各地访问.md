Title: Soviet Delegations from Four States Depart Harbin for Visits to Various Locations
Author:
Date: 1959-10-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 3rd. The Soviet delegations from the Buryat Autonomous Soviet Socialist Republic, Primorsky Krai, Amur Oblast, and Sakhalin Oblast, who were invited to Heilongjiang Province to participate in the celebrations of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, participated in the National Day parade viewing ceremony in Harbin on the 1st and conducted visits. This morning, they departed Harbin by car to visit various locations. The Amur Oblast delegation will visit Qiqihar City for one day and then return home via Heihe; the remaining three delegations will return home via Suifenhe through Mudanjiang.

Leading officials from the Heilongjiang Provincial Party, government, and military went to the station to bid farewell to the guests.

### 苏联四个州代表团离哈尔滨到各地访问

1959-10-04
第2版()
专栏：

    苏联四个州代表团离哈尔滨到各地访问
    新华社哈尔滨3日电　应邀前来黑龙江省参加中华人民共和国建国十周年庆祝典礼的苏联伯力边区、沿海边区、阿穆尔州、萨哈林州四个代表团，1日在哈尔滨参加庆祝国庆节游行的观礼和进行参观、访问后，于今天上午乘车离开哈尔滨到各地访问。阿穆尔州代表团将在齐齐哈尔市参观一天后经黑河归国；其余三个代表团将取道牡丹江经绥芬河归国。
    黑龙江省党、政府和军队的负责人到车站欢送客人。
