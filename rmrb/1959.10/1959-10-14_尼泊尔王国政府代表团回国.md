Title: Nepalese Royal Government Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-10-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 13th—The head of the Nepalese Royal Government Delegation, Dr. Tulsi Giri, Minister of Village Development of the Kingdom of Nepal, and delegation member, Nara Pratap Thapa, Foreign Affairs Secretary of the Nepalese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home. Lieutenant General Daman Shamsher Jung Bahadur Rana, the Nepalese Ambassador to China, also returned home on the same plane today.

Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi saw them off at the airport. The band played the national anthems of Nepal and China. Head of Delegation Tulsi Giri, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi, reviewed the honor guard. Children of the capital presented flowers to all the distinguished Nepalese guests.

Those who saw them off at the airport today included Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Agriculture Yang Xiandong, Vice President of the China-Nepal Friendship Association Zhou Shujia, heads of government departments, democratic parties, and people's organizations, and senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Diplomatic envoys from various countries to China also went to the airport to see them off today. Before leaving Beijing today, Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi received all the distinguished Nepalese guests and had cordial and friendly talks with them. Also present at the meeting were Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Director of the First Asian Department He Ying, and Deputy Director of the Protocol Department Ma Zhenwu.

### 尼泊尔王国政府代表团回国

1959-10-14
第5版()
专栏：

　　尼泊尔王国政府代表团回国
    据新华社13日讯　尼泊尔王国政府代表团团长、尼泊尔王国乡村发展大臣图尔西·吉里博士，代表团团员、尼泊尔外交部外事秘书纳腊·普腊塔普·塔帕今天上午乘飞机离北京回国。尼泊尔驻中国大使达曼·沙姆谢尔·忠格·巴哈杜尔·拉纳中将今天也同机回国。
    陈毅副总理兼外交部长到机场送行。乐队奏尼中两国国歌，图尔西·吉里团长由陈毅副总理陪同，检阅了仪仗队。首都少年儿童向尼泊尔全体贵宾献花。
    今天到机场欢送的，还有外交部副部长章汉夫、农业部副部长杨显东、中国尼泊尔友好协会副会长周叔迦，政府各部门、各民主党派、各人民团体负责人，中国人民解放军高级将领等。各国驻华使节今天也到机场送行。尼泊尔全体贵宾在今天离开北京前，陈毅副总理兼外交部长接见了他们，并进行了亲切友好的交谈。接见时在座的，有外交部副部长章汉夫、第一亚洲司副司长何英和礼宾司副司长马振武。
