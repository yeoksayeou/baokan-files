Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Matsumura Kenzō
Author:
Date: 1959-10-24
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd: Vice Premier of the State Council, Chen Yi, received Mr. Matsumura Kenzō this morning and had a cordial conversation with him in a friendly atmosphere. Liao Chengzhi was present at the reception. (Attached photo)

### 陈毅副总理接见松村谦三

1959-10-24
第2版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见松村谦三
    新华社23日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今天上午接见了松村谦三先生，在友好的气氛中进行了亲切的会谈。接见时廖承志在座。（附图片）
    陈毅副总理和松村谦三谈话
            新华社记者　刘东鳌摄
