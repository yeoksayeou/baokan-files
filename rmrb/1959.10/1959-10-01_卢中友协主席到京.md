Title: President of the Luxembourg-China Friendship Association Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 30th -- At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the President of the Luxembourg-China Friendship Association, René Blum, and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon to visit China and attend the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the country.

### 卢中友协主席到京

1959-10-01
第7版()
专栏：

    卢中友协主席到京
    新华社30日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请，来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的卢中友好协会主席雷纳·布鲁姆和他的夫人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
