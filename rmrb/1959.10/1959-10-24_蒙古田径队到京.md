Title: Mongolian Track and Field Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-10-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd: The Mongolian People's Republic Track and Field Team, a delegation of seventeen people led by team leader O. Guanmubausuleng, arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon. They were invited to China to participate in the China-Mongolia-North Korea Track and Field Friendship Games to be held in Beijing starting on October 25th.

    The Mongolian People's Republic Track and Field Team consists of nine male athletes and six female athletes, who began their training activities this afternoon after arriving.

    The second group of seven people from the Track and Field Team of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, who are also participating in this Friendship Games, also arrived in Beijing today.

### 蒙古田径队到京

1959-10-24
第6版()
专栏：

    蒙古田径队到京
    新华社23日讯　蒙古人民共和国田径队一行十七人，今天下午由领队敖·关木包苏楞率领乘火车到达北京。他们是应邀来我国参加10月25日开始在北京举行的朝、蒙、中三国田径友谊赛的。
    蒙古人民共和国田径队中共有九名男运动员和六名女运动员，他们下午到达以后就开始了练习活动。
    参加这次友谊赛的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国田径队的第二批人员七人，今天也已到达北京。
