Title: Typhoon, Frost, and Gale Forecast

Author: 
Date: 1959-10-14
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 13th - The Central Meteorological Science Research Institute issued a typhoon warning at 7:00 PM on the 13th:

    The center of the typhoon over the southwestern Pacific was located at 2:00 PM today approximately 350 kilometers east of northern Philippines, around 18.3 degrees north latitude and 125.6 degrees east longitude. The maximum wind force near the center is above force 12. The typhoon center is currently moving northwest at a speed of 6 to 9 knots per hour. It is expected that the wind force in the Taiwan Strait will increase to force 9 to 11 within the next 48 hours, the wind force in the eastern part of the South China Sea and the southern part of the East China Sea will increase to force 7 to 9, the wind force along the central and southern coasts of Fujian will increase to force 8 to 10, and the wind force along the northern coast of Fujian, the coast of Zhejiang, and the eastern coast of Guangdong will increase to force 6 to 8.

    In addition, affected by cold air, there will be frost in northern Shaanxi, northern and central Shanxi, northern and central Hebei, and the non-coastal areas of Liaoning on the mornings of the 15th and 16th. On the 15th and 16th, there will be northerly winds of force 6-7 in the Bohai Sea and the northern Yellow Sea.

### 台风、霜冻、大风预告

1959-10-14
第2版()
专栏：气象台

　　台风、霜冻、大风预告
    据新华社13日讯　中央气象科学研究所13日下午七时发布台风警报：
    西南太平洋上的台风，今天下午二时中心位置在菲律宾北部以东三百五十公里左右的洋面上，就是北纬十八点三度，东经一二五点六度附近。中心附近最大风力在十二级以上。目前台风中心正以每小时六浬至九浬的速度向西北方向移动，预计未来四十八小时内，台湾海峡风力将加大到九级至十一级，南海东部、东海南部风力将加大到七级至九级，福建中部、南部沿海风力将加大到八级至十级，福建北部沿海、浙江沿海、广东东部沿海风力将加大到六级至八级。
    另外，受冷空气影响，15日到16日早晨，陕西北部，山西北部、中部，河北北部、中部，辽宁除沿海以外的其余地区将有霜冻。15日到16日，渤海、黄海北部将有六——七级的偏北风。
