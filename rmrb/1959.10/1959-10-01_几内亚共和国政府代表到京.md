Title: Government Representative of the Republic of Guinea Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-10-01
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 30th.
Government Representative of the Republic of Guinea, Minister of Education of Guinea, Barry Diawandou, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. He was invited by our government to Beijing to attend the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of our country.
	Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs, welcomed the distinguished guest at the airport. Among those who went to welcome him were Liu Aifeng, Vice Minister of Education, Zeng Yongquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, heads of various government departments, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and heads of various democratic parties and people's organizations. Diplomatic envoys to China were also present to welcome him.
	After the band played the national anthems of Guinea and China, Minister Barry Diawandou, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi, reviewed the guard of honor, and the distinguished guest received flowers from the children of the capital. (Attached picture)
	The picture shows Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi (right) welcoming Barry Diawandou, Government Representative of the Republic of Guinea, at the airport.
			Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Ping.

### 几内亚共和国政府代表到京

1959-10-01
第7版()
专栏：

    几内亚共和国政府代表到京
    新华社30日讯　几内亚共和国政府代表、几内亚教育部部长巴里·迪亚万杜于今天下午乘飞机抵达北京。他是应我国政府邀请来北京参加我国建国十周年庆祝典礼的。
  国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅到机场欢迎贵宾。前往欢迎的还有教育部副部长刘皑风、外交部副部长曾涌泉和政府各部门负责人、中国人民解放军高级将领和各民主党派、人民团体负责人。欢迎的还有驻华外交官员。
    在乐队奏几内亚和中国国歌后，巴里·迪亚万杜部长在陈毅副总理陪同下检阅了仪仗队，贵宾接受了首都儿童献花。（附图片）
