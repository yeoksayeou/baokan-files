Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Guests from Cuba and Uruguay
Author:
Date: 1959-10-19
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, October 18th: Vice Premier Chen Yi received this morning the delegation of the Cuban Revolutionary Directorate, led by the Secretary-General of the Cuban Revolutionary Directorate, Faure Chomón Mediavilla, and all members of the delegation: Gustavo Machín Hoed, José Antonio Lavassa, Guillermo Jiménez Soler, and Osvaldo Antonio Castell Valdés. During the meeting, the hosts and guests engaged in a cordial and friendly conversation.

Present at the reception were Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Shen Jian, Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General of the Institute of Foreign Affairs.

Xinhua News Agency, October 18th: Vice Premier Chen Yi received this afternoon two journalists from Uruguay, Carlos Borche and Hugo Rodríguez Bela, held friendly talks, and answered some questions they raised.

Present at the reception were Wang Yi, Standing Director of the All-China Journalists Association, Li Bingquan, Deputy Secretary-General, and Xu Huang, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 陈毅副总理接见古巴和乌拉圭客人

1959-10-19
第2版()
专栏：

　　陈毅副总理接见古巴和乌拉圭客人
    新华社18日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了古巴革命指导委员会代表团团长、古巴革命指导委员会总书记福雷·乔蒙·梅迪亚维利亚和代表团全体团员：古斯塔沃·马钦·沃艾德，何塞·安东尼奥·拉瓦萨，吉利尔莫·希梅内斯·索莱尔，奥斯瓦尔多·安东尼奥·卡斯特耳·瓦尔德斯。接见时，宾主进行了亲切的友好谈话。
    接见时在座的有：中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之，外交部美澳司司长申健，外交学会副秘书长吴晓达。
    新华社18日讯　陈毅副总理在今天下午接见了乌拉圭的两位新闻工作者卡洛斯·博尔切和乌果·罗德里格塞·贝拉，进行了友好的谈话，并且回答了他们提出的一些问题。
    接见时在座的，有中华全国新闻工作者协会常务理事王揖、副秘书长李炳泉，外交部新闻司副司长徐晃。
