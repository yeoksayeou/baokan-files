Title: There is a Fusuijing in Beijing
Author: Bai Yuan, Miao Di
Date: 1959-10-18
Page: 8

　　Beijing has a Fusuijing
　　Poem by Bai Yuan, Painting by Miao Di
　　On the western edge of Beijing,
　　There is an old street called Fusuijing,
　　Old temples remain on the street,
　　Dark prayer halls accompany the dead gods.
　　The street trees newly planted last year have already provided shade,
　　No more old dust can be seen on the streets,
　　Whispers and laughter are heard under every roof,
　　Joyful life reflects the bright and clean doorways.
　　People come and go on the street,
　　Ten years have changed the world,
　　Women have established street factories,
　　Flowers bloom in the kindergartens within the lacquered gates.
　　In Fusuijing, there is a scene of happiness and tranquility,
　　How much the beautiful life makes people linger,
　　Beijing's streets are connected one after another,
　　There is no end to the joy in the world.

### 北京有条福绥境
白原  苗地
1959-10-18
第8版()
专栏：

　　北京有条福绥境
　　白原诗  苗地画
　　在北京的西城边，
　　有条古老的街道叫福绥境，
　　街道上留存着古老的寺庙，
　　幽暗的经堂伴着死去的神明。
　　去年新种的街树已经成荫，
　　街路上再也看不见旧日的风尘，
　　笑语呢喃在家家屋檐下，
　　欢腾的生活辉映着光洁的门庭。
　　街道上人来人往，
　　十年岁月换了人间，
　　妇女们成立街道小工厂，
　　漆门里鲜花开满幼儿园。
　　福绥境里一片福绥景，
　　美好的生活多么使人留恋，
　　北京的街道一条连着一条，
　　说不尽多少欢乐在人间。
