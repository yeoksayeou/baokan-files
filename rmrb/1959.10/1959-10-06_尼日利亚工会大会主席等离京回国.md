Title: Nigerian Trade Union Congress Chairman and Others Leave Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1959-10-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 5th. Michael Imoudu, Chairman of the Nigerian Trade Union Congress, and one secretary, who were invited to China to attend the National Day 10th Anniversary celebrations, left Beijing by plane on the 3rd to return home.

Foreign guests who participated in China's National Day 10th Anniversary celebrations and have already left Beijing to return home also include Professor Svennson of the University of Iceland and his wife.

### 尼日利亚工会大会主席等离京回国

1959-10-06
第4版()
专栏：

    尼日利亚工会大会主席等离京回国
    新华社5日讯　应邀来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的尼日利亚工会大会主席迈克尔·伊穆杜和秘书一人，已经在3日乘飞机离京回国。
    参加我国建国十周年庆祝典礼后已经离开北京回国的外宾还有冰岛大学教授斯汶逊夫妇。
