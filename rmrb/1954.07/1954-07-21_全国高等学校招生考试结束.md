Title: National Higher Education Entrance Examination Concluded
Author:
Date: 1954-07-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

    The national higher education entrance examination was held from July 15th to 18th. Now, with the exception of the professional examinations still being conducted for students applying to the Drama Department's acting major at the Drama Academy, the entrance examinations have concluded. During the examination period, the applicants consciously abided by the examination discipline and maintained good order.
    Currently, the higher education enrollment committees in various regions are organizing teachers from higher education institutions in their respective regions to diligently grade the candidates' examination papers in order to carry out the admission and allocation work according to the original schedule.

### 全国高等学校招生考试结束

1954-07-21
第3版()
专栏：

    全国高等学校招生考试结束
    全国高等学校招生考试已在七月十五日到十八日举行，现在除报考戏剧学院话剧表演系学生正在继续进行专业考试外，招生考试已经结束。在考试期间，报考学生自觉地遵守考试纪律，秩序良好。
    现在，各区高等学校招生工作委员会，正组织各区高等学校教师，紧张地评阅考生试卷，以便按照原定日程，进行录取分配工作。
                                  （新华社）
