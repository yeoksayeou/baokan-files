Title: Premier Zhou's Visits to India and Burma (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1954-07-03
Page: 4

Premier Zhou's Visits to India and Burma

    Top Left: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, arrived in New Delhi, the capital of India, by plane on June 25th. The picture shows Premier Zhou Enlai (front row, right) and Prime Minister Nehru (front row, left), who went to the airport to welcome him, walking out of the airport together.

    Top Right: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Rangoon, the capital of Burma, by plane on June 28th. The picture shows Premier Zhou Enlai inspecting the guard of honor after disembarking the plane, followed by Burmese Prime Minister U Nu (first person on the left) introducing the ministers of the Burmese government to Premier Zhou.

    Middle Left: Dr. Rajendra Prasad, President of the Republic of India, held a reception on June 25th to welcome Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. The picture shows Premier Zhou with Indian President Rajendra Prasad (first from the right), Vice President Radhakrishnan (second from the left), and Prime Minister Nehru (first from the left).

    Middle Right: Ba U, President of the Union of Burma, held a banquet on June 28th to welcome Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. The picture shows Premier Zhou Enlai, Burmese President Ba U (second from the right), and Prime Minister U Nu (first from the right) talking before the banquet.

    Bottom Left: Premier Zhou Enlai visited the suburbs and urban areas of Delhi on June 26th. The picture shows Premier Zhou Enlai receiving flowers from the children of the Kasturba Women and Children's Welfare Home.

    Bottom Right: People from all walks of life in Delhi, India, held a grand welcome rally on June 27th to welcome Premier Zhou Enlai. The picture shows Premier Zhou, accompanied by Prime Minister Nehru, on his way to the meeting, receiving warm cheers and salutes from the citizens of Delhi. (Xinhua News Agency draft)

### 周总理在印度和缅甸的访问（图片）

1954-07-03
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理在印度和缅甸的访问
    上左  中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来在六月二十五日乘飞机到达印度首都新德里。图为周恩来总理（前排右）和前往机场欢迎的尼赫鲁总理（前排左）一同步出机场。
    上右  中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来于六月二十八日乘飞机抵达缅甸首都仰光。图为周恩来总理下机检阅仪仗队后，由缅甸总理吴努（左第一人）介绍缅甸政府各部部长和周总理见面。
    中左  印度共和国总统普拉沙德博士在六月二十五日举行招待会，招待中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来。图为周总理和印度总统普拉沙德（右起第一人），副总统拉德哈克里希南（左起第二人）、总理尼赫鲁（左起第一人）合影。
    中右  缅甸联邦总统巴宇在六月二十八日举行宴会，招待中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来。图为周恩来总理、缅甸联邦总统巴宇（右第二人）、总理吴努（右第一人）在宴会前谈话。
    下左  周恩来总理在六月二十六日参观了德里的郊区和市区。图为周恩来总理接受卡斯图巴妇女儿童教养院的儿童们献花的情形。
    下右  印度德里各界人民在六月二十七日举行盛大的欢迎会欢迎周恩来总理。图为周总理在尼赫鲁总理陪同赴会途中，受到德里市民热烈欢呼致敬情形。（新华社稿）
