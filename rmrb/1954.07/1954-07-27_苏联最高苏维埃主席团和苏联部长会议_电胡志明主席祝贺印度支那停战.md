Title: Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR and Council of Ministers of the USSR Send Telegram to Chairman Ho Chi Minh Congratulating the Indochina Armistice
Author: 
Date: 1954-07-27
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 26th dispatch from Moscow: TASS, Moscow: Moscow newspapers on the 25th published a congratulatory telegram from the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR and the Council of Ministers of the USSR to Ho Chi Minh, Chairman and Prime Minister of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The text of the telegram is as follows:

    To Comrade Ho Chi Minh, Chairman and Prime Minister of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam:

    On the occasion of the Geneva Conference reaching agreements on the Indochina armistice and the restoration of peace, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR and the Council of Ministers of the USSR extend friendly congratulations to the Vietnamese people, the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and to you personally, Comrade Ho Chi Minh. The Geneva Accords mark a great victory for the forces of peace, and also mark the recognition of the Vietnamese people's selfless struggle for freedom and national independence. The Soviet people and the Soviet government extend congratulations to the heroic Vietnamese people and their government, and wish them rapid completion of the country's reconstruction work and victory in the cause of developing the economy and culture under peaceful conditions.

    Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR
    Council of Ministers of the USSR (Signed)

### 苏联最高苏维埃主席团和苏联部长会议  电胡志明主席祝贺印度支那停战

1954-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃主席团和苏联部长会议
    电胡志明主席祝贺印度支那停战
    【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：莫斯科各报在二十五日刊载了苏联最高苏维埃主席团和苏联部长会议给越南民主共和国政府主席兼总理胡志明的贺电。电文如下：越南民主共和国政府主席兼总理胡志明同志：值此日内瓦会议已就印度支那停战和恢复和平达成协议之际，苏联最高苏维埃主席团和苏联部长会议谨向越南人民、越南民主共和国政府和您本人，胡志明同志，致以友谊的祝贺。日内瓦的协定标志着和平力量的巨大胜利，同时也标志着越南人民争取自由和民族独立的忘我斗争得到了承认。苏联人民和苏联政府向英勇的越南人民及其政府致贺，并祝他们迅速完成国家的复兴工作，并在和平条件下发展经济和文化的事业中取得胜利。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团  苏联部长会议（签署）
