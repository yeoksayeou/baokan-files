Title: Chinese Trade Delegation Invited to Visit Belgium

Author: 
Date: 1954-07-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 16th. The Belgian Federation of Industries wrote to invite the Chinese Trade Delegation to visit Belgium after their visit to Britain. The Chinese Trade Delegation has now accepted the invitation of the Belgian Federation of Industries and has decided that Shi Zhi'ang, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, will lead a delegation of six trade experts and staff members to Brussels on the 16th for a visit and will discuss the development of direct trade between China and Belgium with Belgian business circles.

### 我国贸易访问团应邀到比利时参观访问

1954-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我国贸易访问团应邀到比利时参观访问
      【新华社日内瓦十六日电】比利时工业联合会在中国贸易访问团访问英国期间曾写信邀请中国贸易访问团在访问英国以后，到比利时参观访问。中国贸易访问团现已接受比利时工业联合会的邀请，并决定由中国进出口公司副经理石志昂为团长，率领贸易专家和工作人员等六人在十六日前往布鲁塞尔进行参观访问，并将和比利时工商界就发展中比两国之间的直接贸易问题进行商谈。
