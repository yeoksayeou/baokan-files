Title: Entire Yinghe County, Hà Đông Province, Vietnam, Liberated; Vietnamese People's Army Annihilates Over Two Hundred Fleeing Enemy Troops from Phủ Lý

Author:
Date: 1954-07-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 9th: Vietnam News Agency reported on the 8th: After being forced to withdraw from several cities and regions in the southern Red River Delta, French forces were again forced to retreat from the military zone on Highway 22, including the Vân Đình stronghold south of Hà Đông Province's capital. From then on, the entire Yinghe County of Hà Đông Province was liberated.

Xinhua News Agency, July 7th: According to Vietnam News Agency on the 6th: On July 3rd, the Vietnamese People's Army annihilated more than two hundred enemy troops in a battle intercepting retreating enemy forces from Phủ Lý. In addition, the People's Army shot down one enemy B-26 bomber, damaged three enemy fighter planes, and destroyed five enemy tanks. After the enemy was forced to withdraw from Phủ Lý, the capital of Hà Nam Province, the entire Hà Nam Province was liberated. Hà Nam Province has an area of nearly 1,570 square kilometers and a population of more than 540,000.

### 越南河东省应和县全部解放  越南人民军歼府里逃敌二百余

1954-07-12
第4版()
专栏：

    越南河东省应和县全部解放
    越南人民军歼府里逃敌二百余
    【新华社九日讯】越南通讯社八日讯：法军在被迫撤离红河三角洲南部的若干城市和地区以后，又被迫从包括河东省省会以南云亭据点在内的第二十二号公路上的军分区撤退。从此河东省应和县便全部解放了。
    【新华社七日讯】据越南通讯社六日讯：越南人民军七月三日在截击从府里撤退的敌军的战斗中，歼敌二百多名。此外，人民军击落敌军B——二十六型轰炸机一架，击伤敌军战斗机三架，并击毁敌军坦克五辆。这次敌军被迫撤出河南省省会府里后，河南省便全部解放了。河南省面积近一千五百七十平方公里，人口有五十四万多。
