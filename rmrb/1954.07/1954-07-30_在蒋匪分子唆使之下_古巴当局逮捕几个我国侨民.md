Title: Under the Instigation of Chiang Kai-shek Bandits, Cuban Authorities Arrest Several of Our Overseas Chinese
Author:
Date: 1954-07-30
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, New York, July 23rd (TASS): The "Illustrated Weekly" published by the Cuban Socialist People's Party stated that under the instigation of Kuomintang representatives, Cuban authorities arrested several Chinese侨民 (overseas Chinese). The weekly pointed out that Cuban police also searched the homes of Chinese who were suspected of sympathizing with the People's Republic of China.

### 在蒋匪分子唆使之下  古巴当局逮捕几个我国侨民

1954-07-30
第4版()
专栏：

    在蒋匪分子唆使之下
    古巴当局逮捕几个我国侨民
    【新华社讯】塔斯社纽约二十三日讯：古巴社会主义人民党发行的“图画周报”说，在国民党代表的唆使之下，古巴当局逮捕了几个中国侨民。周报指出，古巴警察还搜查了有同情中华人民共和国嫌疑的中国人的住宅。
