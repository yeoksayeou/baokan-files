Title: Mendes France Arrives in Geneva
Author:
Date: 1954-07-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 11th: French Premier and Foreign Minister Pierre Mendès France arrived in Geneva on the 10th. Upon arriving at the airport, Mendès France issued the following statement to Radio Geneva:

You all know the purpose of my coming to Geneva. If, with the assistance of other delegations, I am able to secure peace in the Far East, it will give me more reason to love this beautiful city which evokes so many memories for me.

### 孟戴斯—弗朗斯到达日内瓦

1954-07-12
第1版()
专栏：

    孟戴斯—弗朗斯到达日内瓦
    【新华社日内瓦十一日电】法国总理兼外交部部长孟戴斯—弗朗斯在十日到达日内瓦。孟戴斯—弗朗斯到飞机场后向日内瓦电台发表了下列声明：
    我来日内瓦的目的你们是都知道的。如果在其他代表团的协助下，我能够求得远东的和平，这将使我有理由更加喜爱你们这个引起我许多回忆的美丽城市。
