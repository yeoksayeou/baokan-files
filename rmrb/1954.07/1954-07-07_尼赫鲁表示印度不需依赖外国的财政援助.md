Title: Nehru States India Does Not Need to Rely on Foreign Financial Aid
Author:
Date: 1954-07-07
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, 3rd: According to the "Indian Express", Prime Minister Nehru gave a speech at a mass meeting in the border area, calling on the Indian people to work together to carry out national construction. Nehru said, "We are not beggars, and we do not need to rely on foreign financial assistance."

### 尼赫鲁表示印度不需依赖外国的财政援助

1954-07-07
第4版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁表示印度不需依赖外国的财政援助
    【新华社讯】塔斯社德里三日讯：据“印度快报”报道，尼赫鲁总理在边境地区的群众大会上发表演说，号召印度人民同心协力进行国家的建设工作。尼赫鲁说，“我们不是叫化子，用不着倚赖外国的财政援助。”
