Title: Resolution on Developing Agriculture Adopted by the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party
Author:
Date: 1954-07-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 4th dispatch: According to the Polish News Agency, the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party held its second plenary session in Warsaw on the 2nd and 3rd of July.

The plenary session first observed a moment of silence for Central Committee member Modelewski, who recently passed away. Then, Political Bureau member Nowak delivered a report at the meeting on "Current tasks in raising the level of agriculture, especially in eliminating fallow land and improving grassland and pastures." The plenary session discussed Nowak's report, and First Secretary of the Central Committee, Beirut, summarized the discussion. The plenary session adopted the resolution "On Eliminating Fallow Land, Improving and Better Utilizing Grassland and Pastures."

### 波兰统一工人党中央通过发展农业的决议

1954-07-06
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰统一工人党中央通过发展农业的决议
    【新华社四日讯】据波兰通讯社华沙讯：波兰统一工人党中央委员会二日和三日在华沙举行了第二次全体会议。
    全体会议首先为最近逝世的中央委员莫杰莱夫斯基默哀。接着，政治局委员诺瓦克在会上作了“目前在提高农业水平特别是在消灭熟荒地和改善草地和牧场方面的任务”的报告。全体会议讨论了诺瓦克的报告，中央委员会第一书记贝鲁特对讨论作了总结。全体会议通过了“关于消灭熟荒地、改善和更好地利用草地和牧场” 的决议。
