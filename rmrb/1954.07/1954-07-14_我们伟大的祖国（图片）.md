    Our Great Motherland
    The "Tea Picking and Butterfly Catching" dance is an adaptation of the classical "Tea Picking Lantern" dance from Longyan, western Fujian. This dance expresses the tea-picking girls' love for labor and their yearning for and pursuit of a happy life.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-07-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    “采茶扑蝶”舞是根据福建西部龙岩一带古典的“采茶灯”舞加工改编的。这个舞蹈表现了采茶少女对于劳动的热爱，对于幸福生活的向往和追求。
