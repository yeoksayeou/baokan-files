Title: Sino-Vietnamese Military Conference: Chief Representatives of Both Sides Discuss Agenda Issues; Both Sides Exchange 200 Sick and Wounded Prisoners of War at Hai Village and Viet Tri
Author:
Date: 1954-07-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 16th: Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, July 16th: On the morning of July 15th, the chief representatives of the delegation of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army and the delegation of the General Headquarters of the French Union Forces in Indochina attending the Sino-Vietnamese local military conference in North Vietnam held talks to discuss the agenda issues for future meetings.

On the morning of July 16th, the chief representatives of the two delegations held talks again. At the same time, the sub-committee responsible for studying the implementation of the ceasefire continued its work.

Xinhua News Agency, July 16th: Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, July 16th: According to the agreement reached at the Sino-Vietnamese local military conference by the delegations of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army and the General Headquarters of the French Union Forces in Indochina, liaison officers from both sides met at two locations for the transfer of sick and wounded prisoners of war, namely Viet Tri and Hai Village, two kilometers north of Censem.

On July 14th, the Vietnam People's Army transferred one hundred prisoners of war to the French side at Hai Village. The French side also transferred one hundred prisoners of war to the Vietnam People's Army, including four civilians previously captured by the French army.

The transfer of sick and wounded prisoners of war in Viet Tri took place at 10:00 a.m. on July 15th.

A total of 200 sick and wounded prisoners of war were transferred by each side at the above two locations.

According to the agreement reached at the Sino-Vietnamese Military Conference, liaison officers from both sides will meet at Phuong Thanh, eight kilometers northwest of Qui Nhon, at 2:00 p.m. on July 16th to specifically discuss matters related to the transfer of sick and wounded prisoners of war.

### 中亦军事会议双方首席代表讨论议程问题  双方在海村和越池交换病伤战俘二百名

1954-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

    中亦军事会议双方首席代表讨论议程问题
    双方在海村和越池交换病伤战俘二百名
    【新华社十六日讯】越南通讯社北越十六日讯：七月十五日上午，出席北越中亦当地军事会议的越南人民军总司令部代表团和印度支那法兰西联邦部队总司令部代表团的首席代表举行会谈，讨论了今后会议的议程问题。
    七月十六日上午，两代表团的首席代表再度举行会谈。同时，负责研究执行停火问题的小组委员会继续进行工作。
    【新华社十六日讯】越南通讯社北越十六日讯：根据越南人民军总司令部和印度支那法兰西联邦部队总司令部双方代表团在中亦当地军事会议上达成的协议，双方联络的军官在移交病伤战俘的两个地点会晤，这两个地点就是越池和岑山以北两公里的海村。
    七月十四日，越南人民军方面在海村移交给法军方面一百名战俘。法军方面也移交给越南人民军方面一百名战俘，其中包括法军过去俘掳的四名平民。
    在越池的病伤战俘移交工作在七月十五日上午十时进行。
    每方在上述两个地点移交的病伤战俘共二百名。
    根据中亦军事会议达成的协议，双方联络军官将在七月十六日下午二时在归仁西北八公里的丰清会晤，以便具体讨论病伤战俘移交事宜。
