Title: Pakistani Prime Minister Advocates for the Restoration of Our Country's Status in the United Nations
Author:
Date: 1954-07-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, July 16th (Tass dispatch): Pakistani Prime Minister Muhammad Ali stated at a press conference in Karachi on the 15th that Pakistan will support the People's Republic of China's entry into the United Nations. He added, "We have always advocated for this."

### 巴基斯坦总理主张恢复我国在联合国地位

1954-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦总理主张恢复我国在联合国地位
    【新华社十七日讯】塔斯社喀喇蚩十六日讯：巴基斯坦总理穆罕默德·阿里十五日在喀喇蚩举行记者招待会时说，巴基斯坦将支持中华人民共和国进入联合国组织。他又说：“我们过去一直是这样主张的。”
