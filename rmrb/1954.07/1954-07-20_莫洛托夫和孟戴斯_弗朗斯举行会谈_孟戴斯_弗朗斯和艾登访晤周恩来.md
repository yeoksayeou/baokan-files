Title: Molotov and Mendès-France Hold Talks; Mendès-France and Eden Meet with Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1954-07-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 18th: On July 18th, before the start of the restricted meeting of the nine-nation delegations in Geneva, the chief representative of the Soviet delegation, Molotov, held talks with the chief representative of the French delegation, Mendès-France.
    
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 19th: French Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Mendès-France and British Foreign Secretary Eden visited Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, at 1:00 PM on the 19th, for an hour of talks.

### 莫洛托夫和孟戴斯—弗朗斯举行会谈  孟戴斯—弗朗斯和艾登访晤周恩来

1954-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫和孟戴斯—弗朗斯举行会谈
    孟戴斯—弗朗斯和艾登访晤周恩来
    【新华社日内瓦十八日电】七月十八日，苏联出席日内瓦会议代表团的首席代表莫洛托夫在九国代表团的限制性会议开始之前，同法国代表团的首席代表孟戴斯—弗朗斯举行了一次会谈。
   【新华社日内瓦十九日电】法国总理兼外交部部长孟戴斯—弗朗斯和英国外交大臣艾登在十九日下午一时访晤中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，会谈一小时。
