Title: Korean Cooperative Delegation and Head of German Cooperative Delegation Arrive in Beijing to Attend the First National Congress of Chinese Cooperatives

Author:

Date: 1954-07-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th. The Korean Cooperative Delegation, invited by the All-China Federation of Cooperatives to attend the First National Congress of Chinese Cooperatives, arrived in Beijing by train on the 16th.

Those who went to the station to welcome them included Deng Jie, Vice Chairman of the Provisional Council of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives, and Yu Shude, Vice Chairman of the Provisional Supervisory Committee.

Xu Zhe, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, also welcomed them at the station.

The list of the Korean Cooperative Delegation is as follows:

	Head: Kim Jung-tae, Vice Chairman of the Central Alliance Committee of Korean Production Cooperatives.
	Members: Kim Young-joo, Director of the Economic Planning Department of the Central Committee of the Korean Fisheries Cooperative.
		 Hwang Joo-cheol, Director of the Organization and Culture Department of the Central Alliance Committee of Korean Production Cooperatives.
		 Kim Sung-rok, Standing Committee Member of the Central Committee of the Korean Fisheries Cooperative.
		 Kim Eui-gyeong, Chairman of the Pyongyang City Committee of the Korean Production Cooperative.
		 Kim Wang-seok, Chairman of the South Pyongan Province Committee of the Korean Consumer Cooperative.
		 Choi Yong-gil, Director of the Import Commodity Department of the Central Committee of the Korean Consumer Cooperative.

Xinhua News Agency, 19th. Loechter, Head of the Cooperative Delegation of the German Democratic Republic, who was invited by the All-China Federation of Cooperatives to attend the First National Congress of Chinese Cooperatives, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 16th. Meng Yongqian, Vice Chairman of the Provisional Council of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives, and Wang Jinshan, Director of the Hebei Provincial Cooperative Headquarters, who previously attended the Fourth Congress of the Soviet Consumer Cooperatives, returned to Beijing on the same flight.

Cheng Zihua, Acting Chairman of the Provisional Council of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives, and Liang Yao, Vice Chairman, welcomed them at the airport.

### 应邀参加中华全国合作社第一次代表大会  朝合作社代表团和德合作社代表团长到京

1954-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加中华全国合作社第一次代表大会
    朝合作社代表团和德合作社代表团长到京
   【新华社十九日讯】应中华全国合作社联合总社的邀请前来我国参加中华全国合作社第一次代表大会的朝鲜合作社代表团已在十六日乘车到达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有：中华全国合作社联合总社临时理事会副主任邓洁、临时监事会副主任于树德等。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办徐哲也到车站欢迎。
    朝鲜合作社代表团名单如下：
    团长：金重泰  朝鲜生产合作社中央联盟委员会副委员长。
    团员：金荣渚  朝鲜水产合作社中央委员会经济计划部部长。
          黄周哲  朝鲜生产合作社中央联盟委员会组织文化部部长。
          金成录  朝鲜水产合作社中央委员会常务委员。
          金义炅  朝鲜生产合作社平壤市委员会委员长。
          金旺锡  朝鲜消费合作社平安南道委员会委员长。
          崔龙吉  朝鲜消费合作社中央委员会输入商品部部长。
    【新华社十九日讯】应中华全国合作社联合总社的邀请前来我国参加中华全国合作社第一次代表大会的德意志民主共和国合作社代表团团长卢赫特已在十六日下午乘飞机到达北京。前参加苏联消费合作社第四次代表大会的中华全国合作社联合总社临时理事会副主任孟用潜和河北省合作总社主任王金山同机回京。
    到机场欢迎的有中华全国合作社联合总社临时理事会代主任程子华、副主任梁耀等。
