Title: Celebrating the Tenth Anniversary of Poland's National Rebirth Day: Grand Parade and Procession Held in Lublin; Joint Celebration Meeting Held in Warsaw by the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party and the Council of Ministers
Author:
Date: 1954-07-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 23rd: A grand military parade and procession were held in Lublin, the location of the first people's power organ in Polish history—the Polish Committee of National Liberation—on the 22nd, to celebrate the tenth anniversary of Poland's National Rebirth Day, according to the Polish Press Agency.

On the honorary platform were Bolesław Bierut, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party; Nikolai Bulganin, Member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and Marshal of the Soviet Union; Aleksander Zawadzki, Chairman of the Polish Council of State; Józef Cyrankiewicz, Chairman of the Council of Ministers; Konstantin Rokossovsky, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Marshal of Poland, other party and government leaders, as well as outstanding representatives from the cultural and scientific circles and advanced workers in industry and agriculture.

On the reviewing stand were diplomatic envoys and military attachés from various countries stationed in Poland, as well as foreign delegations who came to Poland to participate in the celebrations of the tenth anniversary of Poland's National Rebirth Day.

Bolesław Bierut, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, delivered a speech to those participating in the review and procession. In his speech, he pointed out that the Polish people had overcome various difficulties and achieved tremendous successes in all aspects over the past decade. He said that these achievements were due to the tireless efforts of the Polish people and the vigorous assistance of the Soviet people, and because Poland belongs to the powerful peace and socialist camp of 900 million people. He finally called on the Polish people to strive for the further development of Poland and to improve the people's living standards more rapidly.

Following this, a grand military parade and a procession of 25,000 young men and women were held.

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 23rd: The Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party and the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic jointly held a celebration meeting in Warsaw on the 21st to celebrate the tenth anniversary of Poland's National Rebirth Day, according to the Polish Press Agency.

Present at the celebration meeting were Bolesław Beirut, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Aleksander Zawadzki, Chairman of State Council, Józef Cyrankiewicz, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Konstantin Rokossovsky, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Marshal of Poland, other party and government leaders, as well as representatives of advanced workers and science and culture circles.

Nikolai Bulganin, Member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, attended the celebration meeting.

Diplomatic representatives from various countries stationed in Poland and foreign delegations visiting Poland also attended the celebration meeting.

The meeting was opened by Zawadzki, who congratulated those attending the meeting. Subsequently, Bierut and Bulganin delivered speeches at the meeting, which won warm applause from the audience.

The Warsaw Song and Dance Troupe and the Soviet Small Theatre Troupe, which is visiting Poland, performed brilliant programs at the meeting.

On the evening of the same day, Józef Cyrankiewicz, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Poland, held a reception at the Council of Ministers Building to entertain the participants of the Poland National Rebirth Day.

### 庆祝波兰国家复兴节十周年  卢布林举行盛大的阅兵和游行  波兰统一工人党中央和部长会议在华沙联合举行庆祝大会

1954-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝波兰国家复兴节十周年
    卢布林举行盛大的阅兵和游行
    波兰统一工人党中央和部长会议在华沙联合举行庆祝大会
    【新华社二十三日讯】据波兰通讯社华沙讯：二十二日，在波兰民族历史上第一个人民政权机关——波兰民族解放委员会的所在地卢布林举行了盛大的阅兵和游行，庆祝波兰国家复兴节十周年。
    在荣誉台上的有波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记贝鲁特，苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员、苏联部长会议第一副主席、苏联元帅布尔加宁，波兰国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基，部长会议主席西伦凯维兹，部长会议副主席、波兰元帅罗科索夫斯基和其他党政领导人，以及文化科学界的杰出代表和工农业中的先进工作者。
    在观礼台上的有各国驻波兰外交使节和武官以及到波兰参加波兰国家复兴节十周年庆祝典礼的外国代表团。
    波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记贝鲁特向参加检阅和游行的人发表了演说。他在演说中指出：波兰人民在过去十年中克服了种种困难，在各方面取得了巨大的成就。他说，这些成就的取得是由于波兰人民的不倦的努力和苏联人民的大力帮助，是由于波兰是属于强大的九亿人口的和平和社会主义阵营。他最后号召波兰人民为争取波兰的进一步发展和更迅速地提高人民生活水平而努力。
    接着，举行了盛大的阅兵和两万五千男女青年的游行。
    【新华社二十三日讯】据波兰通讯社华沙讯：波兰统一工人党中央委员会和波兰人民共和国部长会议，二十一日在华沙联合举行波兰国家复兴节十周年庆祝大会。
    出席庆祝大会的有波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记贝鲁特，国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基，部长会议主席西伦凯维兹，部长会议副主席、波兰元帅罗科索夫斯基和其他党政领导人，以及先进工人和科学、文化界的代表。
    参加庆祝大会的有苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员、苏联部长会议第一副主席、苏联元帅布尔加宁。
    各国驻波兰外交代表和正在波兰访问的外国代表团也参加了庆祝大会。
    大会由萨瓦茨基致开幕词，他向参加大会的人祝贺。接着，贝鲁特和布尔加宁先后在会上发表了演说。他们的演说博得了全场热烈的掌声。
    华沙歌舞团和正在波兰访问的苏联小剧院剧团在会上表演了精采的节目。
    同日晚间，波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹在部长会议大厦举行招待会，招待波兰国家复兴节的参加者。
