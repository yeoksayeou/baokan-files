Title: Vietnamese and French Delegations Issue Joint Communiqué on Military Conference: Agreement Reached on Principles and Methods for Transferring Sick and Wounded Prisoners of War; First Group of Sick and Wounded Prisoners of War Agreed to Be Transferred on July 14th
Author:
Date: 1954-07-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, July 6th: The Trung Gian military conference held its third meeting from 3:00 PM to 4:30 PM on the afternoon of the 5th. At this meeting, delegations from both General Headquarters discussed the issue of transferring sick and wounded prisoners of war.

    The delegation from the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army put forward specific suggestions regarding the principles and methods for transferring sick and wounded prisoners of war. These suggestions were agreed upon by the delegation from the General Headquarters of the French Union Forces in Indochina, and the above issues were resolved in a positive atmosphere.

    The two delegations also agreed to organize the transfer of the first group of prisoners of war on July 14, 1954.

    The fourth meeting will be held on the morning of July 6th to discuss the issue of improving the living conditions of prisoners of war.
    
    Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, July 6th: The full text of the joint communiqué issued by the delegation from the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army and the delegation from the General Headquarters of the French Union Forces in Indochina regarding the July 5th meeting of the local military conference is as follows:

    On the afternoon of July 5, 1954, the local military conference held in Trung Gian discussed the issue of transferring sick and wounded prisoners of war in order to fulfill the decisions made at the military conference in Geneva.

    The two delegations have reached an agreement on the principles and methods for transferring prisoners of war. The first group of prisoners of war will be transferred on July 14, 1954.

### 越法双方代表团发表军事会议联合公报  双方就移交病伤战俘原则和方式取得协议  并同意第一批病伤战俘在七月十四日移交

1954-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

    越法双方代表团发表军事会议联合公报
    双方就移交病伤战俘原则和方式取得协议
    并同意第一批病伤战俘在七月十四日移交
     【新华社六日讯】越南通讯社北越六日讯：中亦军事会议在五日下午三时到四时三十分举行第三次会议。双方总司令部的代表团在这次会议上讨论了移交病伤战俘问题。
    越南人民军总司令部代表团提出了关于移交病伤战俘的原则和方式的具体建议。这些建议得到了印度支那法兰西联邦部队总司令部代表团的同意，上述问题在良好的气氛中获得了解决。
    双方代表团并同意在一九五四年七月十四日组织第一批战俘的移交。
    第四次会议将在七月六日上午举行，讨论改善战俘的生活条件问题。
    【新华社六日讯】越南通讯社北越六日讯：越南人民军总司令部代表团和印度支那法兰西联邦部队总司令部代表团发表的关于当地军事会议七月五日会议的联合公报全文如下：
    一九五四年七月五日下午，在中亦举行的当地军事会议讨论了移交病伤战俘以履行在日内瓦举行的军事会议所作出的决定的问题。
    双方代表团已经就移交战俘的原则和方式取得协议。第一批战俘将在一九五四年七月十四日移交。
