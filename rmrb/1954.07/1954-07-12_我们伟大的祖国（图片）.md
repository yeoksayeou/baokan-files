    Our Great Motherland
    The Dalian Locomotive and Rolling Stock Manufacturing Factory began producing new 50-ton tank cars in June. The underframe of this type of tank car is manufactured using electric welding, which is low in cost, strong and durable. It will meet the transportation needs of our country's rapidly developing petroleum industry.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-07-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    大连机车车辆制造工厂六月份开始生产五十吨油槽车的新产品。这种油槽车的底架是采用电焊焊接方法制造的，成本低廉，坚固耐用。它将满足我国日益发展的石油工业在运输上的需要。
