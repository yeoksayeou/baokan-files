Title: China Peace Committee for World Protection and Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Host Banquet for Japanese Peace Delegation and Japanese Diet Members Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-07-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 27th—The China Peace Committee for World Protection and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries jointly hosted a banquet on the evening of the 27th to welcome the Japanese Peace Delegation and the Japanese Diet Members Delegation visiting China.

    All members of the Japanese Peace Delegation, led by Kenjiro Yanagida, and the Japanese Diet Members Delegation, led by Shotaro Matsuura and Shigeyoshi Matsumae, were invited to attend the banquet. Also invited were: Dongwu Kameda, Deputy Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, Japanese peace activist Uemon Nakamura, and Kentaro Kaneko, Japanese representative of the Asian-Australian Liaison Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions.

    The hosts present included: Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the China Peace Committee for World Protection, Standing Committee members Shen Yanbing, Liu Ningyi, Xu Baoju, Secretary-General Liu Guanyi, Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China and member of the World Peace Council, Cai Tingkai and Zhang Bojun, members of the World Peace Council, Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Vice Presidents Ding Xilin, Standing Committee members Chen Hansheng, Ji Chaoding, Zhao Feng, council members Xie Bingxin, and Secretary-General Chen Zhongjing, among others.

    The banquet took place in a friendly atmosphere.

### 我国保卫世界和平委员会和对外文化协会  欢宴日本和平代表团和日本国会议员代表团

1954-07-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我国保卫世界和平委员会和对外文化协会
    欢宴日本和平代表团和日本国会议员代表团
    【新华社二十七日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和中国人民对外文化协会二十七日晚联合设宴，欢迎日本和平代表团和日本国会议员代表团来我国访问。
    以柳田谦十郎为首的日本和平代表团和以松浦周太郎、松前重义为首的日本国会议员代表团全体人员都应邀出席了宴会。　应邀出席宴会的还有：亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长龟田东伍，日本和平人士中村玩右卫门，世界工会联合会亚澳联络局日本代表金子健太。
    主人方面出席的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志、常务委员沈雁冰、刘宁一、许宝驹、秘书长刘贯一，中国红十字会总会会长、世界和平理事会理事李德全，世界和平理事会理事蔡廷锴、章伯钧，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南、副会长丁西林、常务理事陈翰笙、冀朝鼎、赵沨、理事谢冰心、秘书长陈忠经等。
    宴会在友好的空气中进行。
