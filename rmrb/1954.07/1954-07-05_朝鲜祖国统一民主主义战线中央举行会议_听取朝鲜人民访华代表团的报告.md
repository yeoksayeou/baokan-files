Title: Meeting Held by the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Front for the Unification of the Fatherland, Hearing Report from the Korean People's Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1954-07-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 4th: According to the Korean Central News Agency, the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Front for the Unification of the Fatherland held a meeting on the 3rd to hear the return report of the Korean People's Delegation to China.

Attending the meeting were Kim Dal-hyun, Lee Young, Kim Chun-hae, Lee Keuk-ro, Yoo Young-joon, and Jung Ro-shik, members of the Presidium of the Korean Democratic Front for the Unification of the Fatherland, as well as members of the Central Committee of the Fatherland Front and representatives from various democratic parties and social organizations.

Kim Eung-ki, head of the Korean People's Delegation to China, delivered the return report at the meeting. The report stated that with the deep care and active assistance of the Chinese Communist Party and the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, the Korean People's Delegation visited various parts of China, had extensive contact with people from all walks of life, including Chinese workers, farmers, youth, women, scientists, cultural workers, and various ethnic minorities, and received a warm welcome from the fraternal Chinese people.

During their visits to various parts of China, the delegation, through numerous report meetings and forums, conveyed to the Chinese people the Korean people's deep gratitude and respect for the Chinese people, who aided the Korean people with blood during the war and continue to provide material and technical assistance to the Korean people in the post-war reconstruction. The delegation also introduced the heroic deeds of the Korean and Chinese people's forces in the war against aggression, the achievements of the Korean people in post-war reconstruction with the assistance of various fraternal countries, and the strong determination of the Korean people to achieve peaceful reunification of the fatherland. During their visit to China, the art troupe accompanying the delegation performed 151 artistic and consolation performances, with a total audience of 901,000 people.

The report detailed the leaping developments of the new China in politics, economy, culture, and other aspects, and the ever-improving material and cultural living standards of the people; it introduced the deeds of the Chinese people in their struggle for socialist construction. The report also introduced the ongoing movement to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea, which the Chinese people are continuing to carry out in depth.

Through this visit to China, the delegation more deeply experienced how profound and noble the friendship between the Korean and Chinese people, forged with blood, is. The fraternal friendship and unity between the two peoples is a great force for realizing the just cause of the two peoples and striving for peace in Asia and the world.

The report concluded by saying that the Korean people must overcome all difficulties, complete the task of rebuilding the motherland after the war, and achieve the peaceful reunification of the fatherland in order to repay the enormous assistance given to the Korean people by various fraternal countries.

Attendees warmly welcomed the report. The meeting also unanimously passed a letter from the Central Committee of the Korean Father Front to the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the Chinese People's Committee for Resistance Against American Aggression and Aid Korea.

### 朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央举行会议  听取朝鲜人民访华代表团的报告

1954-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央举行会议
    听取朝鲜人民访华代表团的报告
    【新华社平壤四日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会在三日举行会议，听取了朝鲜人民访华代表团的归还报告。
    出席这次会议的，有朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线议长团议长金达铉、李英、金天海、李克鲁、刘英俊、郑鲁湜，祖国战线中央委员和各民主党派、社会团体的代表。
    朝鲜人民访华代表团团长金应基在会上作了归还报告。报告中说，朝鲜人民访华代表团在中国共产党和中华人民共和国中央人民政府的深厚关怀和积极帮助下，在中国各地进行了访问，和中国工人、农民、青年、妇女、科学家、文化工作者、各少数民族等各界人民进行了广泛的接触，受到了兄弟般的中国人民的热烈的欢迎。
    代表团在中国各地访问时，通过多次的报告会、座谈会，向中国人民传达了朝鲜人民对于在战争期间以鲜血援助了朝鲜人民，并在战后的恢复建设中继续给予朝鲜人民以物质和技术援助的中国人民的深切的感谢和敬意。并介绍了在反抗侵略的战争中朝、中人民部队的英雄事迹，介绍了朝鲜人民在各兄弟国家的援助下在战后恢复建设中所取得的成就，和朝鲜人民为争取祖国和平统一的坚强的决心。在中国访问期间，随代表团的艺术团共进行了一百五十一次艺术演出和慰问演出，观众共有九十万一千多人。
    报告详细地介绍了新中国人民在政治、经济、文化等各方面的飞跃发展，和人民物质、文化生活水平日益提高的情况；报告介绍了中国人民在进行社会主义建设中的斗争事迹。报告并介绍了目前中国人民正在继续深入开展着的抗美援朝运动。
    代表团通过这次访问中国，更深刻地体验到朝、中两国人民以鲜血结成的友谊是多么深厚，多么高贵。两国人民的兄弟般的友谊团结，是实现两国人民的正义事业和争取亚洲和世界和平的伟大的力量。
    报告最后说，朝鲜人民要克服一切困难，完成战后重建祖国的事业，实现祖国的和平统一，以回答各兄弟国家给予朝鲜人民的巨大的援助。
    与会者一致热烈地欢迎这个报告。会议并一致通过了朝鲜祖国战线中央委员会写给中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会和中国人民抗美援朝总会的信。
