Title: Vice Minister Li Kenong and Others Return to China from Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1954-07-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 29th: Li Kenong, representative of the Chinese delegation to the Geneva Conference and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, along with delegation advisor Lei Yingfu and accompanying personnel, departed from Prague by plane this morning to return to China via Moscow. They were seen off at the airport by Czechoslovak Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Sekaninová-Čakrtová, Director of the Protocol Department Jecny, Director of the Asian Department Klička, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Czechoslovakia Li Qingquan, Commercial Counselor Li Zhuozhi, and the head of the Chinese delegation to the second session of the Sino-Czechoslovak Joint Committee on Scientific and Technological Cooperation, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Fuel Industry Liu Lanbo, and Vice Minister of the Second Ministry of Machine Building Zhang Linzhi, among others. The Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to Czechoslovakia, Yang Yongsun, also saw them off at the airport.

### 李克农副部长等离捷回国

1954-07-30
第1版()
专栏：

    李克农副部长等离捷回国
    【新华社布拉格二十九日电】我国出席日内瓦会议代表团代表、外交部副部长李克农和代表团顾问雷英夫及随员等，在二十九日晨乘飞机离布拉格经莫斯科返国。到机场欢送的有捷克斯洛伐克外交部副部长塞卡尼诺娃—查克尔托娃、交际司司长耶茨尼、亚洲司司长克利奇卡，我国驻捷大使馆临时代办李清泉、商务参赞李琢之，以及在布拉格参加中捷科学与技术合作联合委员会第二届会议的我国代表团团长、燃料工业部副部长刘澜波和第二机械工业部副部长张霖之等。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻捷大使杨永笋也到机场欢送。
