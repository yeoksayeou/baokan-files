Title: Italian Political and Cultural Leaders Hold Meeting to Discuss Establishing Cultural and Economic Relations with Our Country
Author:
Date: 1954-07-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Rome, July 9th: "Il Popolo di Roma" reports that public figures belonging to different political groups held a meeting in Rome to discuss the issue of establishing cultural and economic relations with the People's Republic of China.

Those participating in the meeting included: Communist Party members of parliament, Terracini and Banfi; Socialist Party member Lombardi; Royalist Party member and former Foreign Minister Guariglia; Milan University President Francesco; Vice President of the Senate, Molè (left-wing non-party member); former Prime Minister Parri and many other political and cultural leaders.

After extensive discussions, the meeting decided to establish an organizing committee. This committee intends to convene a national conference to discuss the issue of relations with the People's Republic of China.

### 意大利政界和文化界领袖举行会议  讨论和我国建立文化和经济关系问题

1954-07-11
第4版()
专栏：

    意大利政界和文化界领袖举行会议
    讨论和我国建立文化和经济关系问题
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社罗马九日讯：“罗马人民报”报道，属于不同政治集团的公众领袖在罗马举行会议，讨论和中华人民共和国建立文化和经济关系的问题。
    参加这次会议的有：议会的共产党议员特拉齐尼和班菲，社会党党员龙巴迪，保皇党党员前外交部长古阿里利亚和米兰大学校长弗朗切斯科，上院副议长摩勒（左翼无党无派人士），前总理帕里和其他许多政界和文化界领袖。
    会议在进行了广泛的讨论之后，决定成立一个组织委员会，这个委员会打算召集一次全国会议，讨论和中华人民共和国的关系问题。
