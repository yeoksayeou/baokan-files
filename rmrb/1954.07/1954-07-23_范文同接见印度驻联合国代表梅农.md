Title: Pham Van Dong Receives Indian Representative to the United Nations Menon
Author:
Date: 1954-07-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 21st: Pham Van Dong, Vice Premier and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, received Menon, the Indian representative to the United Nations, on the morning of July 21st.

### 范文同接见印度驻联合国代表梅农

1954-07-23
第3版()
专栏：

    范文同接见印度驻联合国代表梅农
    【新华社日内瓦二十一日电】越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外交部部长范文同二十一日上午接见了印度驻联合国代表梅农。
