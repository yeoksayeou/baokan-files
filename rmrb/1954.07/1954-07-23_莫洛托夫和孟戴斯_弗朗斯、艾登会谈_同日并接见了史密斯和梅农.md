Title: Molotov Meets with Mendès-France and Eden, Also Received Smith and Menon on the Same Day
Author:
Date: 1954-07-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 21st: Vyacheslav Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, held lengthy discussions with French Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Pierre Mendès-France, at the Soviet delegation's residence on July 21st.

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 21st: Soviet Foreign Minister V.M. Molotov received U.S. Under Secretary of State Walter Bedell Smith on July 21st.

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 22nd: On July 21st, Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov received Krishna Menon, Indian representative to the United Nations.

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 22nd: On July 21st, after the close of the Geneva Conference, Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov and British Foreign Secretary Eden held a meeting.

V.V. Kuznetsov, Deputy Foreign Minister of the Soviet Union, and H. Caccia, Deputy Under-Secretary of the British Foreign Office, were also present at the meeting.

### 莫洛托夫和孟戴斯—弗朗斯、艾登会谈  同日并接见了史密斯和梅农

1954-07-23
第3版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫和孟戴斯—弗朗斯、艾登会谈
    同日并接见了史密斯和梅农
    【新华社日内瓦二十一日电】苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部部长莫洛托夫七月二十一日在苏联代表团住所和法国总理兼外交部部长孟戴斯—弗朗斯进行了长时间的商谈。
    【新华社日内瓦二十一日电】苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫七月二十一日接见了美国副国务卿华·贝·史密斯。
      【新华社日内瓦二十二日电】二十一日，苏联外交部部长莫洛托夫接见了印度驻联合国代表克里希纳·梅农。
    【新华社日内瓦二十二日电】二十一日，日内瓦会议闭幕后，苏联外交部部长莫洛托夫和英国外交大臣艾登举行了一次会谈。
    会谈时在座的还有苏联外交部副部长瓦·瓦·库兹涅佐夫和英国外交部副次官哈·卡西亚。
