Title: Indonesian Art Workers Delegation Departs for Visit and Performance in Our Country
Author:
Date: 1954-07-22
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, July 21st: The Indonesian Art Workers Delegation, headed by Mengkatao Nasution, Deputy Director of the Cultural Department of the Indonesian Ministry of Education, will depart for China on the 21st at the invitation of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. Zhong Qingfa, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Indonesia, held a reception on the evening of the 19th to bid farewell to the Indonesian Art Workers Delegation. The Indonesian Art Workers Delegation includes sixty members from all over Indonesia, most of whom are students from the Solo Conservatory of Music, Gadjah Mada University, and other institutions of higher learning. The delegation will perform in China for one month and will bring eighty paintings and some sculptures for exhibition.

Before their departure, the delegation gave a performance at the Indonesian Presidential Palace on the 13th. President Sukarno, Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo, and other prominent figures from the political and cultural circles of Jakarta attended the performance. Among the foreign guests present were the Chinese Trade Delegation currently in Jakarta and personnel from the Chinese Embassy in Indonesia.

### 印尼艺术工作者代表团启程来我国访问和演出

1954-07-22
第3版()
专栏：

    印尼艺术工作者代表团
    启程来我国访问和演出
    【新华社雅加达二十一日电】以印尼教育部文化司副司长孟加塔奥·纳苏申为首的印尼艺术工作者代表团，应中华人民共和国中央人民政府的邀请，将于二十一日动身前往中国。我国驻印尼大使馆临时代办钟庆发在十九日晚上举行了招待会，欢送印尼艺术工作者代表团。印尼艺术工作者代表团包括来自印尼各地的六十位团员，其中大多数是梭罗音乐学院、加查马达大学和其他高等学校的学生。代表团将在中国演出一月，并将带去八十幅图画和一些雕刻品以举行展览。
    临行以前，代表团十三日在印尼总统府举行表演，出席观看表演的有苏加诺总统、阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理，以及雅加达政界和文化界其他著名人士。出席的外国宾客中有现在在雅加达的中国贸易代表团和中国驻印尼大使馆人员。
