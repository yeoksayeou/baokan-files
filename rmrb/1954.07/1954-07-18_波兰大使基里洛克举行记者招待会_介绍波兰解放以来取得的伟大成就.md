Title: Polish Ambassador Kirilołek Holds Press Conference to Introduce the Great Achievements of Poland Since Liberation
Author:
Date: 1954-07-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 17th—On July 22nd, the tenth anniversary of Poland's National Rebirth Day, the Polish Ambassador to China, Kirilołek, specially held a press conference on the morning of the 17th.

More than 20 people attended the press conference, including representatives from newspapers and magazines in the capital, as well as representatives from newspapers in Shanghai and Tianjin stationed in Beijing.

At the press conference, Polish Ambassador to China Kirilołek introduced the great achievements made by the People's Republic of Poland in the ten years since liberation.

### 波兰大使基里洛克举行记者招待会  介绍波兰解放以来取得的伟大成就

1954-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰大使基里洛克举行记者招待会
    介绍波兰解放以来取得的伟大成就
    【新华社十七日讯】七月二十二日是波兰人民共和国国家复兴节十周年，波兰人民共和国驻我国大使基里洛克特在十七日上午举行记者招待会。
    参加招待会的有：首都各报刊杂志代表和上海、天津各报驻京代表等二十多人。
    招待会上，波兰人民共和国驻我国大使基里洛克介绍了波兰人民共和国在解放后十年中所取得的伟大成就。
