Title: Vice Minister Li Kenong Arrives in Prague from Geneva
Author: 
Date: 1954-07-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 26th: Li Kenong, representative of the Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Geneva Conference and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, and Lei Yingfu, advisor to the delegation, arrived in Prague by plane from Geneva on the morning of the 25th.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Karbisik, representative of the Czechoslovak Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Li Qingquan, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Czechoslovakia; Li Zhuozhi, Commercial Counselor; and Chen Yi, head of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe and Minister of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, who recently came to Czechoslovakia at the invitation to perform.

Vice Minister Li will visit the Czechoslovak Foreign Minister on the morning of the 26th.

### 李克农副部长由日内瓦到达布拉格

1954-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

    李克农副部长由日内瓦到达布拉格
    【新华社布拉格二十六日电】出席日内瓦会议的中华人民共和国代表团代表、中央人民政府外交部副部长李克农和代表团顾问雷英夫等一行在二十五日上午自日内瓦飞抵布拉格。
    到机场欢迎的有捷克斯洛伐克外交部代表卡尔比西克，我驻捷大使馆临时代办李清泉、商务参赞李琢之和最近应邀来捷作访问演出的中国人民解放军歌舞团团长、人民革命军事委员会总政治部文化部部长陈沂等。
    李副部长将在二十六日上午访问捷外交部长。
