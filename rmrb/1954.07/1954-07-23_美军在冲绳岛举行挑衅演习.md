Title: US Military Holds Provocative Exercises on Okinawa Island
Author:
Date: 1954-07-23
Page: 3

    US Military Holds Provocative Exercises on Okinawa Island

    (Xinhua News Agency, 22nd) The United States held provocative amphibious landing operation exercises on Okinawa Island in the Western Pacific on the 20th and 21st. Participating in these exercises were more than ten US warships, many combat aircraft, and combat troops from the Marine Corps and the Army. The United States is holding these provocative military exercises at the same time that the Geneva Conference has reached an agreement to restore peace in Indochina, with the aim of maintaining tensions in Asia and hindering the establishment of peace and security in Asia.

### 美军在冲绳岛举行挑衅演习

1954-07-23
第3版()
专栏：

    美军在冲绳岛举行挑衅演习
    【新华社二十二日电】美国又在二十日和二十一日在西太平洋的冲绳岛举行挑衅性的两栖登陆作战演习。参加这次演习的有美国军舰十余艘，许多作战飞机，和海军陆战队和陆军的作战部队。美国在日内瓦会议达成恢复印度支那和平的协议的同时举行这种挑衅性的军事演习的目的，是企图保持亚洲的紧张局势，阻挠亚洲和平和安全的建立。
