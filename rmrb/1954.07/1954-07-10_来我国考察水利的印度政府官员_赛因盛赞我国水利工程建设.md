Title: Indian Government Officials Visiting China to Inspect Water Conservancy, Sain Praises China's Water Conservancy Project Construction
Author:
Date: 1954-07-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 8th. Sain, Chairman of the Central Water and Power Commission of the Indian Ministry of Water and Power, who was sent by the Indian government to China to inspect water conservancy, returned to India on the 5th after conducting inspections in various parts of China for more than a month.

Sain was deeply impressed by the speed with which China completed huge water conservancy projects. In an interview with a reporter from the "Indian Express," he said that his visit to China would greatly help the progress of India's Kosi Project.

### 来我国考察水利的印度政府官员  赛因盛赞我国水利工程建设

1954-07-10
第4版()
专栏：

    来我国考察水利的印度政府官员
    赛因盛赞我国水利工程建设
    【新华社新德里八日电】印度政府派到我国考察水利的印度水利和电力部中央水利和电力委员会主席赛因在我国各地进行了一个多月的考察后，于五日回到印度。
    赛因对我国完成巨大的水利工程的速度有很深的印象。他在接见“印度快报”的记者时说，他的访问中国将大大有助于印度柯西工程的进行。
