Title: The Vietnamese People's Army Liberates Phu Ly and Intercepts Fleeing Enemy Troops
Author:
Date: 1954-07-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 4th – According to the Vietnam News Agency on July 4th: Under the pressure of the Vietnamese People's Army, the enemy troops were forced to abandon Phu Ly, the capital of Ha Nam province, on July 3rd and flee towards Ha Dong and Hanoi. The fleeing enemy troops were intercepted by the People's Army and suffered heavy casualties.

### 越南人民军解放府里并截击逃窜的敌军

1954-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

    越南人民军解放府里并截击逃窜的敌军
    新华社四日讯】据越南通讯社四日讯：在越南人民军的压力下，敌军被迫在七月三日放弃河南省省会府里，向河东和河内逃窜。逃窜的敌军遭到人民军的截击，伤亡重大。
