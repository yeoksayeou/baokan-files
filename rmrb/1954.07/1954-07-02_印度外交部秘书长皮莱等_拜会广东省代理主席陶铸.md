Title: Indian Foreign Ministry Secretary Pillai and Others Call on Tao Zhu, Acting Chairman of Guangdong Province
Author:
Date: 1954-07-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, June 30th: N.R. Pillai, Secretary-General of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and his wife, along with B.K. Acharya, Deputy Secretary of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, who accompanied Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, from New Delhi to Guangzhou, paid a visit to Tao Zhu, Acting Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, at 6:00 PM on the 30th, accompanied by Cao Ruoming, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Guangzhou Municipal People's Government. Also present were Yi Xiuxiang, Feng Baiju, and He Ximing, Vice Chairmen of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, and Lin Keze, Secretary-General.

The guests and hosts had a cordial conversation for half an hour in a warm and friendly atmosphere and took photos as a memento.

At 7:20 PM, Tao Zhu, Acting Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, hosted a banquet for Pillai and his wife, and Acharya. After the banquet, the guests and hosts watched a performance by the South China Song and Dance Troupe.

### 印度外交部秘书长皮莱等  拜会广东省代理主席陶铸

1954-07-02
第1版()
专栏：

    印度外交部秘书长皮莱等
    拜会广东省代理主席陶铸
    【新华社广州六月三十日电】自新德里陪送中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来到达广州的印度外交部秘书长纳·鲁·皮莱和他的夫人和印度外交部副秘书长布·克·阿护迦，由广州市人民政府外事处处长曹若茗陪同，在三十日下午六时拜会了广东省人民政府代理主席陶铸，在座的有广东省人民政府副主席易秀湘、冯白驹、贺希明，秘书长林克泽等。
    宾主在热烈友好气氛中畅谈了半小时，并摄影留念。
    七时二十分，广东省人民政府代理主席陶铸设宴招待皮莱和他的夫人和阿护迦。会后，宾主观看了华南歌舞团的演出。
