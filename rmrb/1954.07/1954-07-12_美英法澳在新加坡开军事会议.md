Title: US, UK, France, and Australia Hold Military Conference in Singapore
Author:
Date: 1954-07-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 9th: Military officials from the United States, Britain, France, and Australia began a conference in Singapore on the 6th to discuss military issues in Southeast Asia. The conference is chaired by the British High Commissioner for Southeast Asia, MacDonald. According to Reuters, the conference will cover "detailed information on the military situation in Southeast Asia" and the issue of taking "collective action" in Southeast Asia. The recommendations and plans made at the meeting will serve as the basis for the next discussion among the United States, Britain, France, Australia, New Zealand, and other countries on the establishment of a Southeast Asian aggressive bloc.

### 美英法澳在新加坡开军事会议

1954-07-12
第4版()
专栏：

    美英法澳在新加坡开军事会议
    【新华社九日讯】美、英、法和澳大利亚四国的陆、海、空军官员六日起在新加坡举行会议，讨论东南亚军事问题。会议由英国驻东南亚最高专员麦克唐纳担任主席。据路透社报道，会议内容涉及“东南亚军事形势的详细情况”和在东南亚采取“集体行动”的问题。会议作出的建议和计划将作为美、英、法、澳大利亚、新西兰等五国下一步讨论建立东南亚侵略集团问题的根据。
