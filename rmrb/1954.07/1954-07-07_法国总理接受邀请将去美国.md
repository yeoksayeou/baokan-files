Title: French Prime Minister Accepts Invitation to Visit the United States
Author:
Date: 1954-07-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, June 5th (Tass): According to a report from the "New York Times" correspondent in Geneva, Callender, French Prime Minister Mendès France has accepted an invitation to meet with U.S. President Eisenhower in Washington.

### 法国总理接受邀请将去美国

1954-07-07
第4版()
专栏：

    法国总理接受邀请将去美国
    【新华社六日讯】塔斯社纽约五日讯：据“纽约时报”驻日内瓦记者卡伦德报道，法国总理孟戴斯—弗朗斯已接受邀请，将在华盛顿会晤美国总统艾森豪威尔。
