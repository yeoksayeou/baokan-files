Title: Pyongyang Holds Meeting to Commemorate First Anniversary of Victory in the Great Patriotic War
Author:
Date: 1954-07-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch:
A report meeting with over 1,000 participants was held in Pyongyang City at 8 PM on the 26th to commemorate the first anniversary of the victory in the Great Patriotic War. Kim Tu-bong, Park Chung-ai, Choi Yong-kun, Hong Myong-hui, Jung Il-ryong, Park Ui-wan and others attended the meeting. The delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteers, headed by General Tan Shanhe, was invited to attend the meeting and received a warm welcome. Also invited to attend the meeting were diplomatic envoys and officials from the Soviet Union, China, and various People's Democratic countries stationed in Korea. The Chinese youth delegation, headed by Hu Keshi, was also invited to attend the meeting. Lieutenant General Li Quanwu of the Korean People's Army delivered a commemorative report at the meeting. He said that the Korean people and the Korean People's Army, through their heroic struggle, achieved victory in the armistice, defended the freedom and independence of their homeland, made a significant contribution to the cause of peace in Asia and the world, and inspired the national liberation struggles of the oppressed peoples of the East. He expressed gratitude for the contributions of the Chinese People's Volunteers. Li Quanwu then stated that the people of both North and South Korea yearn for the reunification of their country. Although the United States and the Syngman Rhee clique disrupted the discussions on the Korean issue at the Geneva Conference, the constructive proposals for the reunification of Korea put forward by our Republic's delegation will continue to be the basis for a peaceful resolution of the Korean issue. Li Quanwu congratulated the reaching of the Indochina armistice agreement. General Tan Shanhe, the chief representative of the Chinese People's Volunteers delegation, spoke amidst warm applause. He stated that the Korean and Chinese peoples had the strength in the past to achieve victory in the Korean armistice, and in the future, they will have even greater strength to crush any adventurous attacks by the enemy and continue to strive for a peaceful resolution of the Korean issue.

### 平壤举行大会纪念卫国战争胜利一周年

1954-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

    平壤举行大会纪念卫国战争胜利一周年
    据新华社讯：二十六日晚八时在平壤市举行了有一千多人参加的报告大会，纪念卫国战争胜利一周年。金枓奉、朴正爱、崔庸健、洪命熹、郑一龙、朴义琓等参加了大会。以谭善和将军为首的中国人民志愿军代表团应邀参加大会，受到热烈欢迎。应邀参加大会的还有苏联、中国和各人民民主国家驻朝鲜的外交使节和外交官员。以胡克实为首的中国青年代表团也应邀参加大会。朝鲜人民军李权武中将在会上作纪念报告。他说，朝鲜人民和朝鲜人民军以英雄的斗争，取得了停战的胜利，捍卫了祖国的自由独立，对亚洲和世界和平事业作出了巨大的贡献，鼓舞了东方被压迫人民的民族解放斗争。他对中国人民志愿军的贡献表示感谢。李权武接着说：南北朝鲜人民都渴望着祖国的统一。尽管美国和李承晚匪帮破坏了日内瓦会议关于朝鲜问题的讨论，但是我共和国代表团提出的统一朝鲜的建设性的方案今后仍将是和平解决朝鲜问题的基础。李权武对达成印度支那停战协议表示祝贺。中国人民志愿军代表团首席代表谭善和将军在热烈的掌声中讲话。他表示朝、中人民过去既有力量取得朝鲜停战的胜利，今后更有力量粉碎敌人的任何冒险进攻，并继续争取朝鲜问题的和平解决。
