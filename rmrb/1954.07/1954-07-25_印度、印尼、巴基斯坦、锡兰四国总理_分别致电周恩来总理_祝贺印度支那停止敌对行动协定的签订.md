Title: Prime Ministers of India, Indonesia, Pakistan, and Ceylon Send Cables to Premier Zhou Enlai Congratulating on the Signing of the Agreement on the Cessation of Hostilities in Indochina
Author:
Date: 1954-07-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 24th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government received a cable from Indian Prime Minister Nehru congratulating on the agreement reached at the Geneva Conference on the cessation of hostilities in Indochina. The full text of the congratulatory message is as follows:

    I extend my sincerest congratulations to Your Excellency and the Government of Your Country on the agreements reached in Geneva on the question of Indochina – in the achievement of which you played a very great and significant part. I earnestly hope that this will be followed by further settlements so as to ensure peaceful progress for the countries of Asia, free from the fear of war, and will help in the easing of world tensions.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, July 24th: Indonesian Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo sent a cable to Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China on July 22nd, congratulating on the signing of the Indochina armistice agreement in Geneva. The full text of the congratulatory message is as follows:
    To His Excellency Premier Zhou Enlai of the People’s Republic of China:
    I extend to Your Excellency my congratulations on the occasion when an agreement has been reached in Geneva bringing mankind nearer to international peace.

    On behalf of the Government and the people of Indonesia I convey to Your Excellency the congratulations and feelings of satisfaction and relief of the Government and people of Indonesia with the auspicious development towards international peace we all are striving for, being realized on the soil of the long suffering people of Asia.
    May this auspicious course of development be followed for the happiness and prosperity of mankind.
                                        Ali Sastroamidjojo
                                        Prime Minister of the Republic of Indonesia

Xinhua News Agency, July 24th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government received a cable from Pakistani Prime Minister Muhammad Ali on July 21st, congratulating on the agreement reached at the Geneva Conference on the cessation of hostilities in Indochina. The full text of the congratulatory message is as follows:

    Please allow me to extend to Your Excellency my congratulations on the ceasefire agreement finally reached as a result of the successful Geneva negotiations, and hope that it will lead to a lasting peaceful settlement in Indochina.

Xinhua News Agency, July 24th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government received a cable from Ceylon Prime Minister Kotelawala congratulating on the agreement reached at the Geneva Conference on the cessation of hostilities in Indochina. The text of the cable is as follows:

    Please accept my warmest congratulations for your invaluable contribution towards achieving a ceasefire in Indo-China.

    The termination of hostilities resulting from the Geneva Agreements is to us a matter of extreme satisfaction.

    It is particularly gratifying as it provides the hope of a just and lasting settlement arrived at by the peoples of Indo-China themselves free from all outside influences. We feel confident that China will be willing to assist in guaranteeing the right of the peoples of Indo-China to decide their own future, and in doing so to assist the cause of peace. Kindest regards.

### 印度、印尼、巴基斯坦、锡兰四国总理  分别致电周恩来总理  祝贺印度支那停止敌对行动协定的签订

1954-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

    印度、印尼、巴基斯坦、锡兰四国总理
    分别致电周恩来总理
    祝贺印度支那停止敌对行动协定的签订
   【新华社二十四日讯】我中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来收到印度总理尼赫鲁的来电，对日内瓦会议达成的在印度支那停止敌对行动的协定表示祝贺。贺电全文如下：
    我谨向阁下和贵国政府对于在日内瓦达成的关于印度支那问题的协议——您在达成协议方面起了极其伟大和显著的作用——致以最诚挚的祝贺。我热诚地希望，此后将继之以进一步的解决，以便保证亚洲各国的和平进展，而免于战争恐惧，同时有助于消除世界的紧张局势。
   【新华社雅加达二十四日电】印度尼西亚总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约二十二日致电中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来祝贺印度支那停战协定在日内瓦签字。贺电全文如下：中华人民共和国总理周恩来阁下：
    值此在日内瓦取得了使人类更接近国际和平的协定之际，我特向阁下致贺。
    我代表印度尼西亚政府和人民向阁下转致贺忱，印度尼西亚政府和人民由于我们都在努力争取的转向国际和平的有利发展已在长期遭受苦难的亚洲人民的土地上实现而感到愉快，同时也感到宽慰。
    为了人类的幸福和繁荣，愿这种有利的事态发展方向得到遵循。
                                        印度尼西亚共和国总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约
   【新华社二十四日讯】我中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来收到巴基斯坦总理穆罕默德·阿里二十一日来电，对日内瓦会议达成的在印度支那停止敌对行动的协定表示祝贺。贺电全文如下：
    请允许我对日内瓦协商成功的结果终于达成的停火协议向阁下表示祝贺，并希望它将导向印度支那持久的和平解决。
   【新华社二十四日讯】我中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来收到锡兰总理科特拉瓦拉的来电，对日内瓦会议达成在印度支那停止敌对行动的协定表示祝贺。电文如下：
    为了您在取得印度支那停战方面的宝贵贡献，请接受我的最热烈的祝贺。
    日内瓦达成的协定导致了敌对行动的终止，这对我们来说是一件极其令人满意的事情。
    它提供了由印度支那人民不受一切外界影响自己作出一个公正和持久的解决的希望，因此，它尤其令人满意。我们确信，中国一定会愿意协助保证印度支那人民的决定他们自己前途的权利，并且愿意这样做来协助和平事业。谨致问候。
