Title: Joint Celebration by the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association and the Yangon Business Community to Celebrate the Conclusion of the Sino-Burmese Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1954-07-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, 29th: During the Second Sino-Burmese Friendship Week, the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association and the Yangon business community jointly held a celebration meeting on the 23rd to celebrate the conclusion of the Sino-Burmese trade agreement.

The celebration was presided over by prominent Burmese businessman U Ba Chit. Among those attending the celebration were Dr. Htin Aung, chairman of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, Vice-Chairman U Thein Pe Myint, and people from Burmese business and cultural circles. U Chan Maung Kyi, Director of the Burmese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was invited to attend. The Chinese Embassy in Burma was also invited to send representatives.

U Ba Chit said in his speech that this was an exciting celebration meeting because the signing of the Sino-Burmese trade agreement and the further growth of friendship between the two peoples were both worthy of joy.

Then Professor Thala of Yangon University spoke. He said that the conclusion of the Sino-Burmese trade agreement had opened up a broad path for promoting trade between the two countries, and the development of Sino-Burmese trade would still help promote cultural exchange between the two countries.

In his speech, U Thein Pe Myint made a clear comparison between old and new China. He said that the progress of new China was rapid. U Thein Pe Myint finally emphasized that the conclusion of the Sino-Burmese trade agreement would be of great benefit to Burma, and hoped that business people would work for the further development of Sino-Burmese trade.

Chairman of the Burmese Writers Association, Shawa Na, and businessman U Chan Maung, etc., also spoke at the meeting. They all agreed that China and Burma have been friendly and amicable neighbors since ancient times, and hoped that in the future, through the development of trade, the friendly relations between the two peoples would become even closer.

The celebration took place in a warm atmosphere. The commemorative special issue "Culture and Friendship" celebrating Sino-Burmese Friendship Week was also distributed at the meeting.

### 庆祝中缅贸易协定的缔结  缅中友协和仰光工商界联合举行庆祝会

1954-07-01
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝中缅贸易协定的缔结
    缅中友协和仰光工商界联合举行庆祝会
    【新华社二十九日讯】仰光消息：在第二届缅中友好周期间，缅中友好协会和仰光工商界曾在二十三日联合举行庆祝会，庆祝中缅贸易协定的缔结。
    庆祝会由缅甸工商界著名人士吴巴漆阵主持。参加庆祝会的有缅中友好协会主席信波博士、副主席吴登佩密，缅甸工商界和文化界人士。缅甸外交部司长吴阵貌季应邀到会。我国驻缅甸大使馆也应邀派人参加。
    吴巴漆阵致词说，这是一个令人兴奋的庆祝会，因为中缅贸易协定的签订和两国人民的友谊进一步增长都值得高兴。
    接着由仰光大学教授塔拉博士讲话。他说，中缅贸易协定的缔结为促进两国之间的贸易开辟了一条宽阔的道路，而中缅贸易的开展仍然有助于两国的文化交流。
    吴登佩密在讲话中把旧中国和新中国作了鲜明的对比。他说，新中国的进步一日千里。吴登佩密最后着重指出，中缅贸易协定的缔结将对缅甸有很大的好处，希望商界人士为中缅贸易更进一步的发展而努力。
    缅甸作家协会主席沙瓦那、工商界人士吴阵貌等都在会上讲了话。他们一致认为，中缅两国自古以来就是友好亲善的邻邦，希望今后经由贸易的发展，使两国人民的友谊关系更加亲密。
    庆祝会在热烈的空气中进行。会上并分发了庆祝缅中友好周的纪念专刊“文化与友好”。
