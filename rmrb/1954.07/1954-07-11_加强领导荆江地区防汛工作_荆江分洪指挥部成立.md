Title: Strengthen Leadership of Flood Control Work in the Jingjiang Area: Jingjiang Flood Diversion Headquarters Established
Author:
Date: 1954-07-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, July 10th: The Yangtze River has entered its high-water season. To strengthen the specific leadership of flood control work in the Jingjiang area of the middle reaches of the Yangtze River, under the instructions of the Central-South China Flood Control Headquarters, the Jingjiang Flood Diversion Headquarters has been established and began operations in Shashi, Hubei Province, on July 5th. The Jingjiang Flood Diversion Headquarters is composed of the heads of the Jingzhou Special District of Hubei Province, the Changde Special District of Hunan Province, and the Middle Reach Engineering Bureau of the Yangtze River Water Conservancy Commission, who will serve as commanders and deputy commanders. At the same time, the South Bank Flood Control Headquarters has been established at Ouchikou on the south bank of the Jingjiang Flood Diversion Area to strengthen the flood control work of the South Dam and ensure its safety.

### 加强领导荆江地区防汛工作  荆江分洪指挥部成立

1954-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

    加强领导荆江地区防汛工作
    荆江分洪指挥部成立
    【新华社武汉十日电】长江已届大汛期，为加强长江中游荆江地区防汛工作的具体领导，在中南区防汛总指挥部的指示下，荆江分洪指挥部已宣告成立，并于七月五日起在湖北省沙市开始办公。荆江分洪指挥部由湖北省荆州专区、湖南省常德专区和长江水利委员会中游工程局的负责人担任指挥和副指挥。同时在荆江分洪区南堤的藕池口建立了南堤防汛指挥部，以便加强南大堤的防汛工作，保障南大堤的安全。
