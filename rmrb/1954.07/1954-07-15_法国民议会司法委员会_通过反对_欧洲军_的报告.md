Title: French National Assembly Justice Committee Passes Report Opposing "European Army"
Author:
Date: 1954-07-15
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 13th - The French National Assembly's Justice Committee recently passed a report submitted by Social Republican (formerly Gaullist) member of parliament, Palewski, opposing the ratification of the "European Army" treaty, by a vote of 23 to 15. Prior to this, the French National Assembly's Foreign Affairs Committee and Defense Committee had each passed reports opposing the ratification of the "European Army" treaty in June.
    When the National Assembly's Defense Committee re-elected its chairman on the 7th (the former chairman, General Koenig, had been appointed French Minister of National Defense and Armed Forces), another opponent of the "European Army" treaty, Socialist member of parliament, Le Ronnand, was elected chairman.

### 法国民议会司法委员会  通过反对“欧洲军”的报告

1954-07-15
第4版()
专栏：

    法国民议会司法委员会
    通过反对“欧洲军”的报告
    【新华社十三日讯】巴黎消息：法国国民议会司法委员会最近以二十三票对十五票通过了社会共和党（前戴高乐派）议员巴列夫斯基提出的反对批准“欧洲军”条约的报告。在这以前，法国国民议会外交委员会和国防委员会在六月间已各通过了反对批准“欧洲军”条约的报告。
    国民议会国防委员会七日改选主席时（原任主席柯尼格将军已被任命为法国国防和武装部队部长），另一个反对“欧洲军”条约的分子、社会党议员勒荣纳当选为主席。
