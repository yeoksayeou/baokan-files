Title: Premier Zhou Visits Soviet Red Army Martyrs' Cemetery and Lays Wreath
Author:
Date: 1954-07-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 24th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, accompanied by Zhang Wentian, Wang Bingnan, and Qiao Guanhua, and in the company of Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, Bolz, and our Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, Ji Pengfei, visited the Soviet Red Army Martyrs' Cemetery at 9:00 a.m. on the 24th and laid a wreath.

Also present at the wreath-laying ceremony were Deputy Prime Ministers of the German Democratic Republic, Loch and Schulz, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Matern and Hildemann, Chairman of the German Communist Party, Lehmann, Mayor of Greater Berlin, Ebert, and heads of the government and various people's organizations of the German Democratic Republic.

Also present at the wreath-laying ceremony were Deputy Senior Commissioner of the Soviet Union in Germany and Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in the German Democratic Republic, Miroshnichenko, and Soviet military representative Major General P.T. Gibrova, among others.

### 周总理谒苏联红军烈士墓并献花圈

1954-07-26
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理谒苏联红军烈士墓并献花圈
        【新华社柏林二十四日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来偕张闻天、王炳南、乔冠华在德意志民主共和国副总理兼外交部部长博尔茨和我驻德意志民主共和国大使姬鹏飞陪同下，在二十四日上午九时谒苏联红军烈士墓并献花圈。
    献花圈时在场的有德意志民主共和国副总理洛赫、舒尔茨，德国统一社会党中央委员会政治局委员马特恩、希德万，德国共产党主席雷曼，大柏林市长艾柏特，以及德意志民主共和国政府和各人民团体的负责人。
    献花圈时在场的还有苏驻德副高级专员兼苏联驻德意志民主共和国大使馆临时代办米罗什尼钦科，苏军代表帕·特·吉布罗瓦少将等。
