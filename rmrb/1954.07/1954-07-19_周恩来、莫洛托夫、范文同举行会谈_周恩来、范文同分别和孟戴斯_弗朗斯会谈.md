Title: Zhou Enlai, Molotov, and Pham Van Dong Hold Talks; Zhou Enlai and Pham Van Dong Hold Separate Talks with Mendès-France
Author:
Date: 1954-07-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 17th: Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Vyacheslav Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, and Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, held talks at Molotov's residence from 1:00 PM to 4:30 PM on the 17th.

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 17th: Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, met with French Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Mendès-France at 4:50 PM on the 17th for a 50-minute discussion.

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 17th: It is reported that Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, held talks with French Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Mendès-France on the evening of the 17th.

### 周恩来、莫洛托夫、范文同举行会谈  周恩来、范文同分别和孟戴斯—弗朗斯会谈

1954-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来、莫洛托夫、范文同举行会谈
    周恩来、范文同分别和孟戴斯—弗朗斯会谈
    【新华社日内瓦十七日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来、苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部部长莫洛托夫、越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外交部部长范文同，在十七日下午一时至四时三十分在莫洛托夫寓所举行会谈。
    【新华社日内瓦十七日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来在十七日下午四时五十分访晤法国总理兼外交部部长孟戴斯—弗朗斯，会谈五十分钟。
    【新华社日内瓦十七日电】据悉，越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外交部部长范文同十七日晚间和法国总理兼外交部部长孟戴斯—弗朗斯举行了会谈。
