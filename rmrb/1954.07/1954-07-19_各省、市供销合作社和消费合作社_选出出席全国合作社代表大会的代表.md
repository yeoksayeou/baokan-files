Title: Supply and Marketing Cooperatives and Consumer Cooperatives in Various Provinces and Cities Elect Delegates to the National Cooperative Congress
Author:
Date: 1954-07-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 18th -- The elections in the supply and marketing cooperatives and consumer cooperatives of various provinces and cities across the country have successfully concluded, with a total of more than 570 official delegates elected to attend the first Congress of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives. This election began in early June, and according to incomplete statistics up to July 17th, 39 provinces and cities' supply and marketing cooperatives and consumer cooperatives have convened member representative assemblies or member representative meetings. Hebei, Heilongjiang, Qinghai, Guizhou, and ten other provinces, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, and Beijing, Harbin, and five other cities convened member representative assemblies from the bottom up. At these meetings, the councils and supervisory committees made work reports and elected new councils and supervisory committees. Shandong, Jiangsu, Sichuan, Henan, Guangxi, and sixteen other provinces, and Shanghai, Tianjin, and seven other cities convened representative meetings of supply and marketing cooperatives and consumer cooperatives.

### 各省、市供销合作社和消费合作社  选出出席全国合作社代表大会的代表

1954-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

    各省、市供销合作社和消费合作社
    选出出席全国合作社代表大会的代表
    【新华社十八日讯】全国各省供销合作社和各市消费合作社的选举已经胜利结束，共选出了出席中华全国合作社第一次代表大会的正式代表五百七十多名。这次选举从六月上旬开始，据到七月十七日的不完全统计，全国已有三十九个省、市的供销合作社和消费合作社召开了社员代表大会或社员代表会议。河北、黑龙江、青海、贵州等十个省、内蒙古自治区和北京、哈尔滨等五市从下而上召开了社员代表大会，在这些会上，理、监事会作了工作报告，选出了新的理事会和监事会。山东、江苏、四川、河南、广西等十六个省和上海、天津等七市召开了供销合作社、消费合作社的代表会议。
