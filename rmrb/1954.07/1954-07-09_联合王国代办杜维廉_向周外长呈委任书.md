Title: United Kingdom Chargé d'Affaires D'Arcy William　Presents Letter of Credence to Foreign Minister Zhou
Author: 
Date: 1954-07-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 8th: Mr. D'Arcy William, Chargé d'Affaires of the United Kingdom to the People's Republic of China, called on Mr. Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, on the afternoon of the 8th to present his letter of credence. Also present were: Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Zhang Yue, Deputy Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs.

### 联合王国代办杜维廉  向周外长呈委任书

1954-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

    联合王国代办杜维廉
    向周外长呈委任书
    【新华社八日讯】联合王国驻中华人民共和国代办杜维廉先生在八日下午拜会我中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来呈交其委任书。在座的还有：外交部副部长章汉夫和西欧非洲司副司长张越。
