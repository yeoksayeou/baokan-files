Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Hosts Mendès France; British Foreign Secretary Eden Bids Farewell to Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1954-07-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 21st: Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China hosted a banquet for French Premier Mendès France at 1:30 PM on the 21st.
    The French side invited to the banquet included Mr. Lauchambre, Minister for Relations with the Member States of the Indochinese Federation; Mr. Chauvel, the French Ambassador to Switzerland; Mr. Roux, Director of the Asian Department of the French Foreign Ministry; and Mr. Guemas, advisor to the French delegation.
    The Chinese side attending as guests included Vice Foreign Ministers Zhang Wentian and Li Kenong, and Wang Bingnan, Secretary-General of the Chinese delegation.
    The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.
    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 21st: British Foreign Secretary Eden visited Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China at 11:45 AM on the 21st to bid farewell to Zhou Enlai.

### 周恩来总理设宴招待孟戴斯—弗朗斯  英国外交大臣艾登向周恩来外长告别

1954-07-22
第4版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理设宴招待孟戴斯—弗朗斯
    英国外交大臣艾登向周恩来外长告别
    【新华社日内瓦二十一日电】中华人民共和国总理周恩来在二十一日下午一时三十分设宴招待法国总理孟戴斯—弗朗斯。
    法国方面应邀出席宴会的还有印度支那联邦成员国关系部部长拉香波、法国驻瑞士大使萧维尔、法国外交部亚洲司司长鲁克斯、法国代表团顾问吉埃马斯。
    中国方面出席作陪的有外交部副部长张闻天、李克农和中国代表团秘书长王炳南。
    宴会在友好空气中进行。
    【新华社日内瓦二十一日电】英国外交大臣艾登在二十一日上午十一时四十五分访晤中华人民共和国外交部部长周恩来，向周恩来告别。
