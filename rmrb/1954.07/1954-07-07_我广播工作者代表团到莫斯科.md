Title: Chinese Broadcasting Workers Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1954-07-07
Page: 1
    
    (Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 6th) According to a TASS dispatch from Moscow: The Chinese broadcasting workers delegation to the Soviet Union arrived in Moscow by plane on the 5th. Representatives of Soviet broadcasting workers welcomed the delegation at the airport.

### 我广播工作者代表团到莫斯科

1954-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我广播工作者代表团到莫斯科
    【新华社六日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：中国广播工作者访苏代表团在五日乘飞机抵达莫斯科。到机场欢迎的有苏联广播工作者的代表们。
