Title: Chinese Trade Delegation Begins Discussions with Belgian Industrial Circles
Author:
Date: 1954-07-20
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Brussels, 19th: The Chinese trade delegation to Belgium is conducting tours and visits. On the 17th, the delegation visited the Châtel Electric Factory and attended a luncheon hosted by the Federation of Belgian Industries. Among those present at the luncheon were the Chairman and Director-General of the Federation of Belgian Industries, the Vice Chairman of the Belgian Bank, and representatives from the steel, non-ferrous metals, metal machinery, chemical, textile, asbestos, and other associations. The Chinese trade delegation to Belgium also held discussions on trade issues with representatives from various sectors of Belgian industry.

### 我国贸易访问团开始和比利时工业界商谈

1954-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我国贸易访问团开始和比利时工业界商谈
    【新华社十九日讯】布鲁塞尔消息：中国赴比利时贸易访问团进行参观和访问。访问团十七日参观了沙城电器工厂，并出席了比利时工业联合会举行的午宴。参加午宴的有比利时工业联合会主席，总干事，比利时银行副董事长以及钢铁、有色金属、金属机器、化工、纺织、石棉等联合会的代表多人。中国赴比利时贸易访问团并和比利时工业界各行业的代表就贸易问题进行了商谈。
