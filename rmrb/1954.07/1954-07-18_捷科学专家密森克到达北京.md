Title: Czechoslovak Science Expert Míšek Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-07-18
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 17th. According to the Sino-Czechoslovak Cultural Cooperation Agreement, Dr. Karel Míšek, a scientific expert from the Technical Physics Institute of the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 17th to conduct scientific research in China. Cao Richang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and representatives from the Bureau for External Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government Administration Council, were at the airport to welcome him.

### 捷科学专家密森克到达北京

1954-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    捷科学专家密森克到达北京
    【新华社十七日讯】根据中捷文化合作协定来中国进行科学研究工作的捷克斯洛伐克科学院技术物理研究所科学专家卡列尔·密森克博士在十七日乘飞机到达北京。中国科学院办公厅副主任曹日昌和中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局代表等前往机场欢迎。
