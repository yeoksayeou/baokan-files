Title: Zhou Enlai Pays Respects at the Berlin Socialist Martyrs Cemetery and Lays a Wreath
Author:
Date: 1954-07-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 23rd: Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China, accompanied by Zhang Wentian, Wang Bingnan, Lei Renmin, Qiao Guanhua, Chen Jiakang, and others, paid respects at the Friedrich Socialist Martyrs Cemetery at 5:30 PM on the 23rd and laid a wreath.

From the German Democratic Republic, those accompanying included: Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Bolz, Deputy Prime Ministers Loch and Schultz, Mayor of Greater Berlin Ebert, Alternate Member of the Politburo of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Honecker, and others.

Ji Pengfei, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the German Democratic Republic, also accompanied the delegation.

When Premier Zhou Enlai laid the wreath, the band played "Eternal Glory to the Martyrs."

After laying the wreath, Premier Zhou Enlai and others paid their respects at the tombs of martyrs such as Liebknecht, Rosa Luxemburg, and Thälmann.

### 周总理谒柏林社会主义烈士公墓并献花圈

1954-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理谒柏林社会主义烈士公墓并献花圈
    【新华社柏林二十三日电】中华人民共和国总理周恩来偕张闻天、王炳南、雷任民、乔冠华、陈家康等在二十三日下午五时三十分谒腓特烈社会主义烈士公墓，并献花圈。
    德意志民主共和国方面陪同前往的有：副总理兼外交部部长博尔茨、副总理洛赫、舒尔茨，大柏林市长艾柏特，德国统一社会党中央政治局候补委员昂纳克等。
    中华人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国大使姬鹏飞也陪同前往。
    周恩来总理献花圈时，乐队奏“烈士永垂不朽”曲。
    献花圈后，周恩来总理等瞻仰了李卜克内西、罗萨·卢森堡、台尔曼等烈士墓。
