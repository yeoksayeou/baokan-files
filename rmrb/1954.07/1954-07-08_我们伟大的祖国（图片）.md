Author:
Date: 1954-07-08

    Our Great Motherland
    Twelve national middle schools have been established in the minority areas of Guizhou Province. This is Ban Yunxiu, a Buyi student and Communist Youth League member from Huishui National Middle School in the Buyi and Miao Autonomous Region of Huishui County. She is speaking at a weekly meeting, reviewing the week's study progress.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    贵州省各少数民族地区设有十二所民族中学。这是惠水县布依族苗族自治区惠水民族中学的布依族学生、青年团员班运秀。她在周会上发言，检查一周的学习情况。
