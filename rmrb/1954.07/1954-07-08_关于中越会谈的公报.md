    Communiqué on Sino-Vietnamese Talks

    Premier of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, and President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Ho Chi Minh, held talks on the Sino-Vietnamese border from July 3 to 5, 1954. Premier Zhou Enlai and President Ho Chi Minh fully exchanged views on the Geneva Conference regarding the restoration of peace in Indochina and other related issues. Also participating in the talks were the Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the People's Republic of China, Hoang Van Hoan, and Qiao Guanhua, Advisor to the Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Geneva Conference.

### 关于中越会谈的公报

1954-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

    关于中越会谈的公报
    中华人民共和国总理周恩来和越南民主共和国主席胡志明，于一九五四年七月三日至五日在中越边境举行会谈。周恩来总理和胡志明主席就日内瓦会议关于恢复印度支那和平问题及其他有关问题，充分地交换了意见。参加会谈的还有越南民主共和国驻中华人民共和国大使黄文欢和出席日内瓦会议的中华人民共和国代表团顾问乔冠华。
