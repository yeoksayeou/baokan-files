Title: Greetings to the All-India Peace Council Meeting in Calcutta - Telegram from the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee
Author:
Date: 1954-07-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 29th: The Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee sent a telegram to congratulate the All-India Peace Council on its upcoming meeting in Calcutta. The telegram reads as follows:

To the All-India Peace Council:

At a time when people around the world are enthusiastically celebrating the Geneva Conference's agreement on the Indochina issue, your council is holding a meeting to discuss how to strengthen Asian security and unity and oppose weapons of mass destruction. This is of great significance. We believe that the convening of this meeting will not only benefit the peace cause in your country, but will also make an important contribution to peace in Asia and the world.

Please accept our heartfelt congratulations.

Liu Ningyi, Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, July 29, 1954

### 祝贺全印和平理事会在加尔各答召开的会议  亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会的贺电

1954-07-30
第4版()
专栏：

    祝贺全印和平理事会在加尔各答召开的会议
    亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会的贺电
    【新华社二十九日讯】亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会致电祝贺全印和平理事会即将在加尔各答召开的会议。电文如下：全印和平理事会：
    正当全世界人民热烈庆祝日内瓦会议对印度支那问题达成协议之际，贵会召开理事会会议，讨论如何加强亚洲安全与团结以及反对大规模毁灭性武器等问题，这是具有重大意义的。我们相信，这次会议的召开，不仅有益于贵国和平事业，而且对亚洲及世界和平也一定会有重要贡献。
    谨致以衷心的祝贺
    亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会秘书长  刘宁一　一九五四年七月二十九日
