Title: Mendès-France Delivers Weekly Radio Address to the French Nation, Claims Timely Achievement of Indochina Ceasefire Agreement is Possible
Author:
Date: 1954-07-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, July 19th: According to TASS, Mendès-France, head of the French delegation to the Geneva Conference, delivered his weekly radio address to the French nation from Geneva on the afternoon of July 17th. His entire speech was about the progress of the Geneva Conference and the possibility of reaching an agreement before July 20th.

	Mendès-France expressed his deep conviction that reaching an agreement on an Indochina ceasefire within the stipulated timeframe was possible. He said, "From the beginning, I was deeply convinced that there was no reason to prevent us from reaching an early agreement with our enemies in accordance with a glorious solution. Today, after so many talks and meetings discussing substantive issues, I am even more certain of this. The differences between us are neither terrible nor difficult to overcome."

### 孟戴斯—弗朗斯向法国国内发表每周广播演说  声称如期达成印度支那停火协议是可能的

1954-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

    孟戴斯—弗朗斯向法国国内发表每周广播演说
    声称如期达成印度支那停火协议是可能的
   【新华社十九日讯】据塔斯社巴黎十九日讯：法国出席日内瓦会议代表团团长孟戴斯—弗朗斯在七月十七日下午从日内瓦向法国国内发表了他的每周广播演说。他的整个演说是讲日内瓦会议工作的经过以及在七月二十日以前达成协议的可能性。
    孟戴斯—弗朗斯表示他深信在规定的期限之前达成印度支那停火的协议是可能的。他说，“最初我就深信没有理由可以阻挠我们遵照光荣的解决办法来和我们的敌人早日达成协议。在经过了那么多次的会谈和讨论实质问题的会议之后的今天，我更加确信这一点。目前我们之间的分歧既不是可怕的也不是难于克服的。”
