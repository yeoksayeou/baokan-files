Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Leaves Moscow for Ulaanbaatar
Author:
Date: 1954-07-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 30th. Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, after visiting Moscow, flew to Ulaanbaatar from Moscow at 7:00 AM on the 30th. Accompanying Premier Zhou Enlai were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary-General of the Chinese Delegation to the Geneva Conference Wang Bingnan, and advisors Shi Zhe, Qiao Guanhua, and Chen Jiakang.

Those at the airport to see him off were: First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union Molotov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union Zorin, Soviet Ambassador to the People's Republic of China Yudin, Chairman of the Moscow Soviet Yasnov, Acting Director of the First Far Eastern Department of the Soviet Foreign Ministry Gudev (yesterday translated as Kuljukov), Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Foreign Ministry Kapustin, and Acting Commander of the Moscow City Defense Command Kovalev.

Also at the airport to see him off were: Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Pham Van Dong, and representatives of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam delegation to the Geneva Conference Phan Anh and Hoang Van Hoan.

The heads of diplomatic missions of various countries to the Soviet Union who saw him off at the airport included: Hungarian Ambassador Sikladan, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Nguyen Luong Bang, Czechoslovak Ambassador Vosahlik, Albanian Ambassador Prifti, Romanian Ambassador Rab, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the German Democratic Republic Seitz, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Mongolian People's Republic Tobo, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Park De-hwan, Polish Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Maletski, and other personnel.

Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Zhang Wentian and personnel from the embassy also saw him off at the airport.

### 周恩来总理离莫斯科去乌兰巴托

1954-07-31
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理离莫斯科去乌兰巴托
    【新华社莫斯科三十日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来访问莫斯科后在三十日晨七时由莫斯科飞赴乌兰巴托。随同周恩来总理前往的有我外交部副部长王稼祥，我国出席日内瓦会议代表团秘书长王炳南，顾问师哲、乔冠华、陈家康等。
    到机场欢送的有：苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部部长莫洛托夫，苏联外交部副部长佐林，苏联驻中华人民共和国大使尤金，莫斯科市苏维埃主席雅斯诺夫，苏联外交部第一远东司代理司长顾德夫（昨译库尔纠科夫），苏联外交部交际司副司长卡普斯金，莫斯科市城防司令部代理司令科瓦列夫。
    到机场欢送的还有：越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外交部部长范文同，越南民主共和国出席日内瓦会议代表团代表潘英和黄文欢。
    到机场欢送的各国驻苏使节有：匈牙利大使希克拉丹，越南民主共和国大使阮良朋，捷克斯洛伐克大使沃萨格里克，阿尔巴尼亚大使普里夫蒂，罗马尼亚大使拉布，德意志民主共和国临时代办赛兹，蒙古人民共和国临时代办托博，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国临时代办朴德焕，波兰临时代办马勒茨基及其他人员。
    我国驻苏联大使张闻天和使馆人员也到机场欢送。
