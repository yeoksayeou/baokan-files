Title: Head of US Military Advisory Group Arrives in Karachi
Author:
Date: 1954-07-31
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency report:

    The United States is stepping up its efforts to turn Pakistan into an American military base under the guise of "aid."

    Brigadier General Saxton, head of the US Military Assistance Advisory Group, which is implementing the aggressive US-Pakistan "Mutual Defense Assistance Agreement," and formerly Chief of Staff of the US Sixth Army, has arrived in Karachi, the capital of Pakistan. He will set up a headquarters there. More than thirty American "military experts" who are members of the Military Assistance Advisory Group will also arrive in Pakistan soon. According to foreign news agencies, the first batch of "military assistance" equipment given by the United States to Pakistan will be shipped to Pakistan in October.

### 美军事顾问团团长到达喀喇蚩

1954-07-31
第4版()
专栏：

    美军事顾问团团长到达喀喇蚩
    据新华社讯：美国正加紧在“援助”的幌子下把巴基斯坦变为美国的军事基地。
    执行侵略性的美巴“共同防御援助协定”的美国军事援助顾问团团长、原任美国第六军参谋长的萨克斯顿准将已经到达巴基斯坦首都喀喇蚩。他将在那里设立一个总部。作为军事援助顾问团团员的三十多个美国“军事专家”不久也将到达巴基斯坦。据外国通讯社报道，美国给予巴基斯坦的第一批“军事援助”装备将在十月间运到巴基斯坦。
