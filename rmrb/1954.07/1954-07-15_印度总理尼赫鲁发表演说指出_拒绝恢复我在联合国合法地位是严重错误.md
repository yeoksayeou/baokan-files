Title: Indian Prime Minister Nehru Delivers Speech Pointing Out that Refusal to Restore My Country's Legitimate Status in the United Nations is a Serious Mistake
Author:
Date: 1954-07-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, July 14th: According to Indian news sources: Indian Prime Minister Nehru delivered a speech at a mass meeting in Mirzapur, Uttar Pradesh, on the 12th, stating that refusing to restore the legitimate status of the People's Republic of China in the United Nations is a serious mistake. Nehru said: Not recognizing People's China is like closing one's eyes to the reality of Asia. This is a matter of concern for India because it affects not only the entire Asian continent but also the entire world.
    Nehru mentioned that some people say that if the People's Republic of China enters the United Nations, they will withdraw from the United Nations. He said that he could not understand their attitude if some major powers handle world political issues in this way.

### 印度总理尼赫鲁发表演说指出  拒绝恢复我在联合国合法地位是严重错误

1954-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    印度总理尼赫鲁发表演说指出
    拒绝恢复我在联合国合法地位是严重错误
    【新华社十四日讯】据印度新闻处消息：印度总理尼赫鲁十二日在北方省米尔查普尔的群众大会上发表演说，他说，拒绝恢复中华人民共和国在联合国的合法地位是严重的错误。尼赫鲁说：不承认人民中国就像是对亚洲的现实闭起眼睛来一样。这是令印度感到忧虑的事，因为这不仅对整个亚洲大陆发生影响，而且对整个世界都发生影响。
    尼赫鲁提到某些人谈到如果中华人民共和国进入联合国他们就要退出联合国时说，假如一些大国这样来处理世界的政治问题，他是不能理解它们的态度的。
