Title: Vietnamese and French Military Conference Begins Discussing Ceasefire Issues
Author:
Date: 1954-07-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, 9th: Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, 9th dispatch: The subcommittee of the Central and Northern Vietnam local military conference, responsible for studying the issue of improving the living conditions of prisoners of war, began its work on the morning of July 7th. Discussions on this issue were completed on the afternoon of July 8th. Representatives from both sides reached agreements on many fundamental points related to this issue in a good atmosphere. On July 9th, when the plenary session of the Central and Northern Vietnam local military conference entered into discussions on the issue of achieving a ceasefire, the aforementioned subcommittee discussed the issue of sending letters and medicines to prisoners of war.

### 越法双方军事会议开始讨论实现停火问题

1954-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

    越法双方军事会议开始讨论实现停火问题
    【新华社九日讯】越南通讯社北越九日讯：北越中亦当地军事会议的负责研究改善战俘生活条件问题的小组委员会是从七月七日上午开始工作的。关于这个问题的讨论已在七月八日下午完成。双方代表在良好的气氛中，就有关这个问题的许多基本之点达成了协议。七月九日，在中亦当地军事会议的全体会议进入实现停火问题的讨论的时候，上述小组委员会讨论了运送信件和药品给战俘的问题。
