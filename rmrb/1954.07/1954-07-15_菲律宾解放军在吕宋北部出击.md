Title: Philippine Liberation Army Attacks in Northern Luzon
Author:
Date: 1954-07-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Manila, July 14th: The Philippine People's Liberation Army launched an attack on the 12th in the Alagao Mountain area near Muñoz City in northern Luzon, more than 600 kilometers from Manila, inflicting losses on a team of Philippine government troops.

At the same time, fierce fighting broke out between the Philippine People's Liberation Army and Philippine government troops on the western slopes of Mount Arayat, 80 kilometers north of Manila.

### 菲律宾解放军在吕宋北部出击

1954-07-15
第4版()
专栏：

    菲律宾解放军在吕宋北部出击
    【新华社十四日讯】马尼拉消息：菲律宾人民解放军十二日在吕宋北部离马尼拉六百多公里的务倪城附近阿拉戈山地区出击，使一队菲律宾政府军受到损失。
    同时，在马尼拉以北八十公里的亚拉悦山的西面山坡，菲律宾人民解放军和菲律宾政府军展开了激烈的战斗。
