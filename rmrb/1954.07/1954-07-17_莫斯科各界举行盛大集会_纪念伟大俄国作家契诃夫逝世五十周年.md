Title: Grand Gathering Held in Moscow by All Circles to Commemorate the 50th Anniversary of the Death of the Great Russian Writer Chekhov
Author:
Date: 1954-07-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th: According to TASS, Moscow: A grand gathering commemorating the 50th anniversary of the death of the great Russian writer Anton Chekhov was held on the 15th in the Hall of Columns of the Trade Union Building in Moscow. Present at the meeting were Soviet writers, actors, scientists, artists, students, and representatives of the Moscow public, foreign guests invited to Moscow to participate in the commemorative activities, and heads of various countries' embassies and legations in the Soviet Union.

The meeting was presided over by the famous Soviet writer Konstantin Fedin. The famous literary figure Vladimir Yermilov delivered a report on Chekhov's life and work. He said that Chekhov was not only a great satirist, but also a great lyrical poet; not only the most faithful and fearless explorer of the most arduous and darkest aspects of life in his time, but also a singer of beauty and happiness. At the same time, Chekhov was also a true innovator, raising humorous short stories to the height of great literature. He understood how to describe entire lives, portray characters, and deeply depict the relationships between people and the contradictions of life in short stories. In world literature, no writer has such a diverse range of colors, tones, and creative aspects, resulting in an infinitely rich style as Chekhov. In Chekhov's works, humor, lyricism, drama, and tragedy all stem from his implicit, passionate, and faithful love for the common people. Yermilov emphasized: We can say with a happy heart and love for Chekhov that Chekhov lives, and today, lives in the life of our motherland and the life of all mankind. Chekhov's friends are friends of peace in this beautiful world!

Soviet People's Artist Mikhail Kedrov spoke at the meeting, discussing the important significance of Chekhov's dramatic creation method to the development of Russian drama.

Belgian public figure and Stalin International Peace Prize winner, Madame Isabelle Blume, spoke on behalf of the World Peace Council. She emphasized that Chekhov's works are intimate to people of all countries, and they unite readers of all countries with a common love for humanity.

Professor Yoshitaro Hirano, Secretary-General of the Japan Peace Committee, Chinese writer Ba Jin, as well as writers from Romania, Bulgaria, and the German Democratic Republic, also spoke at the meeting. They talked about Chekhov's influence on the development of world literature and the widespread popularity of his works among people of all countries.

After the meeting, a performance was held, including excerpts from Chekhov's plays.

### 莫斯科各界举行盛大集会  纪念伟大俄国作家契诃夫逝世五十周年

1954-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

    莫斯科各界举行盛大集会
    纪念伟大俄国作家契诃夫逝世五十周年
    【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：伟大俄国作家安东·契诃夫逝世五十周年纪念大会十五日在莫斯科的工会大厦圆柱大厅举行。到会的有苏联作家、演员、科学家、艺术家、学生和莫斯科公众代表，应邀来莫斯科参加纪念活动的外国来宾，以及各国驻苏联大使馆和公使馆的负责人。
    大会由苏联著名作家康·费定主持。著名的文学家弗·叶尔米洛夫在会上作了关于契诃夫生平和工作的报告。他说，契诃夫不但是一个伟大的讽刺家，而且是一个伟大的抒情诗人；不但是他那个时代的生活的最艰苦、最黑暗面的最忠实无畏的探究者，而且是一个美丽和幸福的歌手。同时，契诃夫又是一个真正的革新者，他把幽默的小故事提高到伟大的文学的高度。他懂得在小故事中描写整个人生、描写人物和深入刻画人与人之间的关系及生活的矛盾。在世界文学中，没有一个作家像契诃夫那样，有着具有这样多种多样的色彩、情调和创作面的无限丰富的风格。在契诃夫的著作中，幽默、抒情、戏剧和悲剧都是来自他对普通人民的含蓄的、热情的、忠实的爱。叶尔米洛夫强调说：我们可以怀着愉快的心情和对契诃夫的热爱说，契诃夫活着，而且今天，就活在我们祖国的生活和全人类的生活中。契诃夫的朋友就是这个美丽的世界上的和平的朋友！
    苏联人民艺术家米·凯德罗夫在会上发言，他谈到了契诃夫的戏剧创作法对俄罗斯戏剧发展的重要意义。
    比利时公众领袖、“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金得奖人伊·布伦姆夫人代表世界和平理事会发言。她着重指出，契诃夫的著作对各国人民都是亲切的，它们以对人类的共同的爱团结各国读者。
    日本拥护和平委员会总书记平野义太郎教授、中国作家巴金以及罗马尼亚、保加利亚、德意志民主共和国的作家都在会上讲了话。他们谈到了契诃夫对世界文学发展的影响和他的著作在各国人民中普遍受到欢迎的情形。
    会后，举行了演出会，演出节目包括契诃夫剧作中的一些片断。
