Title: Bulganin Arrives in Warsaw to Attend Celebrations of the Polish National Rebirth Day
Author:
Date: 1954-07-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 21st: According to TASS, Nikolai Bulganin, Member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and Marshal of the Soviet Union, arrived in Warsaw by plane on the 20th to attend the celebrations for the 10th anniversary of the Polish National Rebirth Day.

Welcoming him at the airport were Bolesław Bierut, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Aleksander Zawadzki, Chairman of the Polish Council of State, Józef Cyrankiewicz, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Marshal of Poland, Konstantin Rokossovsky, and other party and government leaders.

Soviet Ambassador to Poland, Mikhailov, and embassy staff also welcomed him at the airport.

At the airport, the bands played the national anthems of the Soviet Union and Poland. Bulganin reviewed the guard of honor.

### 参加波兰国家复兴节庆祝典礼  布尔加宁到达华沙

1954-07-22
第3版()
专栏：

    参加波兰国家复兴节庆祝典礼
    布尔加宁到达华沙
    【新华社二十一日讯】据塔斯社华沙讯：苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员、苏联部长会议第一副主席、苏联元帅布尔加宁，二十日乘飞机抵达华沙，参加波兰国家复兴节十周年庆祝典礼。
    到机场欢迎的有波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记贝鲁特、波兰国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基、部长会议主席西伦凯维兹、波兰元帅罗科索夫斯基和其他党政领袖。
    苏联驻波兰大使米哈伊洛夫和大使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
    机场上乐队奏着苏波两国的国歌，布尔加宁检阅了仪仗队。
