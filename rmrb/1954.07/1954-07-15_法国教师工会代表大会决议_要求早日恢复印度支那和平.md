Title: French Teachers' Union Congress Resolution Demands Early Restoration of Peace in Indochina
Author:
Date: 1954-07-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, July 10th: TASS reports that the French National Teachers' Union held a congress in Paris. The congress, representing 155,000 French teachers, passed several resolutions, including resolutions opposing colonial oppression and striving for peace in Indochina.

    The congress unanimously opposed sending regular troops to Indochina, pointing out that such a measure would be a serious threat to world peace. The congress demanded that the participants of the Geneva Conference—first and foremost the French delegation—do everything possible to achieve an early ceasefire and restoration of peace in Indochina.

### 法国教师工会代表大会决议  要求早日恢复印度支那和平

1954-07-15
第4版()
专栏：

    法国教师工会代表大会决议
    要求早日恢复印度支那和平
    【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社巴黎十日讯：法国全国教师工会在巴黎举行代表大会。代表大会代表十五万五千名法国教师通过了若干决议，其中包括反对殖民压迫和争取印度支那和平的决议。
    代表大会一致反对派正规军到印度支那去，并指出这种措施将是对世界和平的严重威胁。代表大会要求日内瓦会议的参加者——首先是法国代表团，尽一切可能使得印度支那早日停火和恢复和平。
