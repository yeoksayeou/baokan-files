Author:
Date: 1954-07-30

    Our Great Motherland
    The second phase of the Lianwei project in the first, second, and sixth districts of Shunde County, Guangdong Province, one of the eight major water conservancy projects in Guangdong Province, has been completed. After the completion of the entire project, the enclosed fields will be protected from floods and water transportation will be facilitated. This is the newly built Guipan Sea Sluice in the second phase of the Shunde Lianwei project.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-07-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    广东省八大水利工程之一的顺德县第一、二、六区联围第二期工程已经完成。这个工程全部完成后，可使围内田亩免受水灾，并便利水上的交通。这是顺德联围第二期工程中新建的桂畔海水闸。
