Title: Zhou Enlai Meets with Phoui Sananikone and Receives Menon
Author:
Date: 1954-07-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 14th: Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, met with Phoui Sananikone, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Laos, at 3:47 p.m. on the 14th.
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 14th: Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, received Mr. Menon, Indian Representative to the United Nations, at 2:00 p.m. on the 14th.

### 周恩来外长访晤萨纳尼空并接见梅农

1954-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来外长访晤萨纳尼空并接见梅农
    【新华社日内瓦十四日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来在十四日下午三时四十七分访晤老挝王国外交大臣冯·萨纳尼空。
    【新华社日内瓦十四日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来在十四日下午二时接见印度驻联合国代表梅农。
