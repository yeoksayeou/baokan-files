Title: Czechoslovakia and Canada Decide to Expand the Volume of Barter Trade Between the Two Countries
Author:
Date: 1954-07-11
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 10th) According to a report by the Czechoslovak News Agency on the 8th: Czechoslovakia and Canada recently held trade negotiations in Ottawa and decided to expand the volume of barter trade between the two countries. Canada's exports to Czechoslovakia of food, agricultural products, and other raw materials, as well as Czechoslovakia's exports to Canada of industrial products, will increase compared to the past.

During the negotiations, both parties also reached agreements on tariffs and payment issues.

### 捷克斯洛伐克和加拿大  决定扩大两国的换货额

1954-07-11
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克和加拿大
    决定扩大两国的换货额
    【新华社布拉格十日电】据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社八日讯：捷克斯洛伐克和加拿大最近在渥太华举行了贸易谈判，决定扩大两国的换货额。加拿大输往捷克斯洛伐克的食品、农产品和其他原料以及捷克斯洛伐克输往加拿大的工业品都要比过去增加。
    在谈判的过程中，双方对关税和支付的问题也达成了协议。
