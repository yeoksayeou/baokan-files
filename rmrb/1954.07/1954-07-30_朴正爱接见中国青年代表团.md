Title: Park Jung-ae Receives Chinese Youth Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1954-07-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 29th: Park Jung-ae, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, received the Chinese Youth Delegation on July 28th, who were invited to attend the Korean National Congress of Youth Activists.

Present at the reception were: Park Kim-chul, member of the Political Committee of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party; Choi Chang-ik, Vice-Premier of the Cabinet; Ha Yang-cheon, Minister of the Propaganda and Agitation Department of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party; Nam Il, Minister of Foreign Affairs; Kim Ki-soo and Kim Young-sook, Vice-Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Youth League, and others.

Also present was Li Shi, First Secretary of the Chinese Embassy in Korea.

Hu Keshi, head of the Chinese Youth Delegation, presented Vice-Chairman Park Jung-ae with gifts from the Chinese youth to the Korean people's leader, Marshal Kim Il-sung.

### 朴正爱接见中国青年代表团

1954-07-30
第4版()
专栏：

    朴正爱接见中国青年代表团
    【新华社平壤二十九日电】朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱二十八日接见了应邀参加朝鲜全国青年积极分子大会的中国青年代表团。
    接见时在座的有：朝鲜劳动党中央政治委员会委员朴金喆，内阁副首相崔昌益，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会宣传鼓动部部长河仰天，外务相南日，朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会副委员长金基洙、金英淑等。
    在座的还有我国驻朝鲜大使馆一等秘书李石。
    中国青年代表团团长胡克实向朴正爱副委员长献赠中国青年送给朝鲜人民领袖金日成元帅的礼物。
