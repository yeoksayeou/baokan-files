Title: France Continues to Send Troops to North Africa, Uses Terror Tactics Against Residents of Tunisia and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1954-07-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, July 3rd: News reports indicate that the situation in French North Africa remains very tense.

    "Liberation" reports that French colonialists' assault troops are using terror tactics against local residents in Tunisia. Colonial terror actions have also occurred in Morocco.

    According to newspaper reports, French authorities continue to send reinforcements to North Africa and are mobilizing reserve personnel for service. Newspapers report that 4,000 French troops will be sent to Casablanca (Morocco) within a few days. "l'Humanité" reports that the Ministry of Defense has issued an order mobilizing certain categories of reserve personnel and sending them to Tunisia for service. "l'Humanité" also reported news about two teams of gendarmerie forces about to arrive in Morocco.

### 法国继续增派军队到北非  以恐怖手段对付突尼斯等地居民

1954-07-06
第4版()
专栏：

    法国继续增派军队到北非
    以恐怖手段对付突尼斯等地居民
    【新华社四日讯】据塔斯社巴黎三日讯：据报纸消息：法属北非的局势仍然非常紧张。
    “解放报”说，法国殖民者的突击部队在以恐怖手段对付突尼斯的当地居民。在摩洛哥也发生了殖民恐怖的行动。
    据报纸消息说，法国当局继续派遣增援部队去北非，并且动员后备军人员服役。报纸报道：有四千法国军队将在几天之内被派往卡萨布兰卡（摩洛哥）。“义勇军报”报道，国防部发布命令，动员某几类的后备军人员，派他们往突尼斯服役。“人道报”还报道了关于两队宪兵部队即将抵达摩洛哥的消息。
