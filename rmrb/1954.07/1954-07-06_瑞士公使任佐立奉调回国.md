Title: Swiss Minister Plenipotentiary René Zoellig Recalled Home

Author:
Date: 1954-07-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 5th: René Zoellig, the first Swiss Federal Minister Plenipotentiary to China, has been recalled and left Beijing for home on the 4th. Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, was at the station to see him off. Diplomatic envoys and staff members of various countries' embassies in China, as well as Mr. Cuelli, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Swiss Legation in China, also went to see him off.

### 瑞士公使任佐立奉调回国

1954-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

    瑞士公使任佐立奉调回国
    【新华社五日讯】瑞士联邦驻我国首任特命全权公使任佐立已奉调在四日离京返国。前往车站送行的有中央人民政府外交部交际处副处长沈平。各国驻华使节、使馆人员及瑞士驻华公使馆临时代办崔礼等也前往送行。
