Title: Government Administration Council Holds 219th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-07-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th.

    Government Administration Council Holds 219th Meeting
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 30th] The 219th session of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 24th. The meeting heard and approved the report by Liu Jingfan, Deputy Director of the People's Supervisory Commission of the Government Administration Council, on the Third National Supervisory Work Conference. After hearing the explanation by Teng Daiyuan, Minister of the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government, the meeting also adopted the decision on establishing a People's Supervisory Bureau in the Ministry of Railways and strengthening supervisory work, and approved the work regulations of the People's Supervisory Bureau of the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government. In addition, the meeting approved various appointment and removal cases.

### 政务院举行第二一九次会议

1954-07-01
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第二一九次会议
    【新华社三十日讯】中央人民政府政务院第二百一十九次政务会议在二十四日下午举行。会议听了和批准了政务院人民监察委员会副主任刘景范所作的关于第三次全国监察工作会议的报告。会议并在听了中央人民政府铁道部部长滕代远所作的说明后，通过了关于在铁道部建立人民监察局和加强监察工作的决定，批准了中央人民政府铁道部人民监察局工作条例。此外，会议通过了各项任免案。
