Title: Bolivian Jurists Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-07-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 7th: Bolivian jurist Assad Simon, physicians Aranade Guillermo and Prada Javier, after attending the World Peace Council's special meeting in Berlin, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the seventh at the invitation of the China Peace Committee and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations With Foreign Countries for a visit to our country.

Zhou Junliang, section chief of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations With Foreign Countries, and representatives of the China People's Committee for World Peace went to the airport to greet them.

Wan Toli, Deputy Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, and his wife also went to greet them.

### 玻利维亚法学家等到京

1954-07-09
第4版()
专栏：

    玻利维亚法学家等到京
    【新华社七日讯】玻利维亚法学家阿萨德·西蒙，医学家阿兰纳德·吉勒摩、普拉达·哈维尔在参加世界和平理事会柏林特别会议之后，应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和中国人民对外文化协会的邀请来我国访问，已在七日下午乘飞机抵达北京。
    前往机场迎接的有中国人民对外文化协会处长周君亮及中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的代表。
    前往迎接的还有亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长万徒勒里及其夫人。
