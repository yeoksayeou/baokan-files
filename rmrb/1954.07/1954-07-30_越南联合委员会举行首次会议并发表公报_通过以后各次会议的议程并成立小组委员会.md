Title: Vietnam Joint Committee Holds First Meeting and Issues Communiqué; Agenda for Subsequent Meetings Adopted and Subcommittees Established
Author:
Date: 1954-07-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, July 29th dispatch from Vietnam News Agency: The Central Joint Committee responsible for implementing the Vietnam Armistice Agreement held its first meeting at Trung Gia, North Vietnam, from 9:00 AM to 11:00 AM on the 29th. The Central Joint Committee discussed the agenda and established subcommittees at this meeting.

The delegation of the Vietnam People's Army High Command was headed by General Văn Tiến Dũng and included the following personnel: Colonel Song Hao, Colonel Lê Quang Đạo, Lieutenant Colonel Lê Minh, Lieutenant Colonel Đồng Văn Cống, and Major Hồ Xuân Anh.

The six-member delegation of the High Command of the French Union Forces in Indochina was led by General Delteil.

The first communiqué of the Central Joint Committee is as follows:

On July 29, 1954, in Trung Gia, the Central Joint Committee responsible for implementing the Vietnam Armistice Agreement held its first expanded meeting in a good atmosphere. The committee has adopted the agenda for subsequent meetings.

### 越南联合委员会举行首次会议并发表公报  通过以后各次会议的议程并成立小组委员会

1954-07-30
第1版()
专栏：

    越南联合委员会举行首次会议并发表公报
    通过以后各次会议的议程并成立小组委员会
    【新华社二十九日讯】越南通讯社北越二十九日讯：负责执行越南停战协定的中央联合委员会二十九日上午九时到十一时在北越中稼举行了第一次会议。中央联合委员会在这次会议中讨论了议程并成立了小组委员会。
    越南人民军总司令部代表团以文进勇将军为首并由下列人员组成：双豪上校，黎光道上校，黎明中校，同文贡中校和胡春英少校。
    印度支那法兰西联邦部队总司令部的六人代表团是由戴尔特尔将军率领的。
    中央联合委员会的首次公报如下：
    一九五四年七月二十九日在中稼，负责执行越南停战协定的中央联合委员会在良好的气氛中举行了第一次扩大会议。委员会已通过以后各次会议的议程。
