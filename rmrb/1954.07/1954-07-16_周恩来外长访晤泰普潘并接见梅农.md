Title: Zhou Enlai Meets with Tep Phan and Receives Menon

Author: 
Date: 1954-07-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 14th: Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, visited Tep Phan, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Cambodia, at 5:00 PM on the 14th.
   
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 15th: Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, received Menon, the Indian Representative to the United Nations, at 11:30 AM on the 15th. Qiao Guanhua, Advisor to the Chinese Delegation to the Geneva Conference, was present at the reception.

### 周恩来外长访晤泰普潘并接见梅农

1954-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来外长访晤泰普潘并接见梅农
    【新华社日内瓦十四日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来在十四日下午五时访晤柬埔寨王国外交大臣泰普潘。
   【新华社日内瓦十五日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来在十五日上午十一时三十分接见印度驻联合国代表梅农。接见时在座的有中国出席日内瓦会议代表团顾问乔冠华。
