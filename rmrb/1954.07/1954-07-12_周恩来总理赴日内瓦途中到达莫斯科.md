Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Arrives in Moscow En Route to Geneva

Author:

Date: 1954-07-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th — Zhou Enlai, Chief Representative of the Delegation of the People’s Republic of China to the Geneva Conference, and Premier and Foreign Minister of the State Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Moscow on the afternoon of the 10th while en route by plane from Beijing to Geneva.

Hoang Van Hoan, Representative of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Geneva Conference and Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the People’s Republic of China, arrived on the same plane.

Also arriving on the same plane were Qiao Guanhua, Wang Zhuoru, and others, advisors to the delegation of the People’s Republic of China to the Geneva Conference.

Mikhoyan, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Vyshinsky, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Yasnov, Chairman of the Moscow Soviet, Kovalev, Acting Commander of the Moscow City Defense Command, Fedlin, Director of the Far East Department of the Soviet Foreign Ministry, Kabushkin, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department, and others welcomed them at the airport.

Lam Hai, Ambassador of the Democratic People’s Republic of Korea to the Soviet Union, and Nguyen Luong Bang, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Soviet Union, also welcomed them at the airport.

Wen Ning, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of our Embassy in the Soviet Union, and embassy staff also welcomed them at the airport.

### 周恩来总理赴日内瓦途中到达莫斯科

1954-07-12
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理赴日内瓦途中到达莫斯科
    【新华社莫斯科十日电】中华人民共和国出席日内瓦会议代表团首席代表、中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来在从北京乘飞机赴日内瓦途中在十日下午到达莫斯科。
    越南民主共和国出席日内瓦会议代表、驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使黄文欢同机到达。
    同机到达的还有我出席日内瓦会议代表团顾问乔冠华、王倬如等。
    苏联部长会议副主席米高扬，苏联外交部第一副部长维辛斯基，莫斯科市苏维埃主席雅斯诺夫，莫斯科市城防司令部代理司令科瓦列夫，苏联外交部远东司司长费德林、交际司副司长卡布什金等人到机场欢迎。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻苏联大使林海，越南民主共和国驻苏联大使阮良朋也到机场欢迎。
    我驻苏联大使馆临时代办温宁和使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
