    Our Great Motherland
    In order to better meet the needs of urban residents, this year, a large portion of the approximately 50,000 mu of vegetable farmland in the suburbs of Tianjin has adopted advanced agricultural technologies, which has accelerated the maturation period and increased vegetable production. Members of the Gao Jinzhang Agricultural Production Cooperative in the western suburbs of Tianjin are harvesting bumper crops of cucumbers.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为了更好地满足城市居民的需要，今年天津郊区约五万余亩的蔬菜耕地，大部采用了先进的农业技术，提早了成熟期，并增加了蔬菜的产量。天津西郊高金章农业生产合作社的社员正在采摘丰产的黄瓜。
