Title: Molotov and Heads of British and French Delegations Hold Talks for Several Days; Pham Van Dong Meets with Mendès-France and Receives Menon
Author:
Date: 1954-07-18
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 17th: The two chairmen of the Geneva Conference, Molotov and Eden, held an informal meeting on the 16th with the head of the French delegation, Mendès-France, to discuss the work of the Geneva Conference.
    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 16th: The First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Molotov, held a lengthy meeting on the afternoon of the 15th with the Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of France, Mendès-France, to discuss the issues currently being debated at the Geneva Conference.
    The meeting was held at the headquarters of the French delegation. Mendès-France hosted a banquet for Molotov there. Representatives and advisors from the Soviet and French delegations attending the Geneva Conference also attended the banquet.
    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 16th: The Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Molotov, held a meeting with the British Foreign Secretary, Eden, on the 15th to discuss matters concerning the Geneva Conference.
    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 16th: The Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Molotov, held a meeting at the headquarters of the Soviet delegation on the 16th with the Indian public figure Krishna Menon.
    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 16th: The Vice Premier and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Pham Van Dong, held a meeting with the Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of France, Mendès-France, on the afternoon of the 16th. Pham Van Dong also received the Indian representative to the United Nations, Menon.

### 莫洛托夫和英法代表团长连日举行会谈  范文同和孟戴斯—弗朗斯会谈并接见梅农

1954-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫和英法代表团长连日举行会谈
    范文同和孟戴斯—弗朗斯会谈并接见梅农
    【新华社日内瓦十七日电】日内瓦会议的两位主席莫洛托夫和艾登十六日同法国代表团团长孟戴斯—弗朗斯就日内瓦会议的工作问题举行了一次非正式的会谈。
    【新华社日内瓦十六日电】苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部部长莫洛托夫十五日下午和法国总理兼外交部部长孟戴斯—弗朗斯就日内瓦会议上正在讨论的问题举行了一次为时较长的会谈。
    会谈是在法国代表团总部举行的。孟戴斯—弗朗斯在那里设宴招待了莫洛托夫。参加宴会的还有苏联和法国出席日内瓦会议的代表团代表和顾问。
    【新华社日内瓦十六日电】苏联外交部部长莫洛托夫十五日和英国外交大臣艾登举行一次会谈，讨论了关于日内瓦会议的问题。
    【新华社日内瓦十六日电】苏联外交部部长莫洛托夫十六日在苏联代表团总部和印度公众领袖克里希纳·梅农举行了一次会谈。
    【新华社日内瓦十六日电】越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外交部长范文同在十六日下午和法国总理兼外交部长孟戴斯—弗朗斯举行了会谈。范文同也接见了印度驻联合国代表梅农。
