Title: The Second Class of Students from the Central School of Political Science and Law Graduates

Author:

Date: 1954-07-09
Page: 3

    The Second Class of Students from the Central School of Political Science and Law Graduates
    The Central School of Political Science and Law held a graduation ceremony for the second class of students on the morning of July 4th. Dong Biwu, Vice Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government, Peng Zhen, Principal of the Central School of Political Science and Law, Xie Juezai, Shi Liang, Vice Principals, and Wu Yuzhang, Luo Ruiqing, members of the School Affairs Committee, and many heads of political and legal departments attended the ceremony.
    Principal Peng Zhen delivered a report at the meeting. He called on the students to overcome arrogance and complacency, demand more from themselves with higher standards, and truly become wholehearted servants of the people.
    The second class of graduates from the Central School of Political Science and Law numbered nearly a thousand and were all responsible cadres from county-level political and legal departments. The entry of these graduates into political and legal work will play a certain role in further strengthening political and legal work.
                                  (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中央政法干部学校第二期学员结业

1954-07-09
第3版()
专栏：

    中央政法干部学校第二期学员结业
    中央政法干部学校在七月四日上午举行第二期学员结业典礼。中央人民政府政务院副总理董必武，中央政法干部学校校长彭真，副校长谢觉哉、史良，校务委员吴玉章、罗瑞卿和政法部门首长多人参加了这一典礼。
    校长彭真在会上作了报告。他号召学生们要力戒骄傲自满，以更高的标准来要求自己，使自己真正成为人民群众全心全意的勤务员。
    中央政法干部学校第二期结业学员近千人，都是县级政法部门的负责干部。这批学员投入政法工作，对于政法工作的进一步加强，将起一定作用。
                                  （新华社）
