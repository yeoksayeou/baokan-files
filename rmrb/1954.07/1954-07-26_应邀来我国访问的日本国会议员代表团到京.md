Title: Japanese Diet Member Delegation Arrives in Beijing on Invitation

Author:
Date: 1954-07-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 25th: The Japanese Diet Member Delegation, led by Matsuura Shutaro, a Diet member of the Reform Party, and Matsumae Shigeyoshi, a Diet member of the Right-wing Socialist Party, consisting of thirteen members, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 25th, at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included: Liu Guan-yi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Tang Mingzhao, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department, Chen Zhongjing, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Ji Chao-ding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and others.

Kameta Togo, Deputy Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, Nakamura Kanzaburo, a Japanese peace activist, and Kaneko Kenta, the Japanese representative of the Asian-Australasian Liaison Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions, also went to the airport to welcome them.

The list of the delegation is as follows:

　　Head of the delegation: Matsuura Shutaro (Diet member of the Reform Party)
　　Deputy Head of the delegation: Matsumae Shigeyoshi (Diet member of the Right-wing Socialist Party)
　　Members: Sonoda Sunao (Diet member of the Reform Party)
　　　　　Nishimura Naoami (Diet member of the Liberal Party)
　　　　　Imazumi Isamu (Diet member of the Right-wing Socialist Party)
　　　　　Suzuki Hajime (Independent Diet member)
　　　　　Nagashima Ginzō (Diet member of the Liberal Party)
　　　　　Sakurauchi Yoshio (Diet member of the Reform Party)
　　　　　Nakasone Yasuhiro (Diet member of the Reform Party)
　　　　　Domori Yoshio (Diet member of the Right-wing Socialist Party)
　　　　　Sudō Gorō (Diet member of the Communist Party)
　　　　　Kobayashi Shin'ichi (Independent Diet member)
　　Secretary: Kamimura Yukio

### 应邀来我国访问的日本国会议员代表团到京

1954-07-26
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国访问的日本国会议员代表团到京
    【新华社二十五日讯】应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国访问的、以改进党议员松浦周太郎和右派社会党议员松前重义为首的日本国会议员代表团一行十三人在二十五日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一、联络部副部长唐明照、中国人民对外文化协会秘书长陈忠经、中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎等多人。
    亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长龟田东伍、日本和平人士中村翫右卫门、世界工会联合会亚澳联络局日本代表金子健太也到机场欢迎。
