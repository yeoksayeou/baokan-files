Title: Singapore Ten Thousand Municipal Workers Go on Strike
Author:
Date: 1954-07-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Singapore: Approximately ten thousand municipal workers in Singapore went on strike on the 19th for wage increases. Participants included workers from the electricity, water, gas, sewage, sanitation, road, hygiene, and construction engineering departments.

### 新加坡一万市政工人大罢工

1954-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

    新加坡一万市政工人大罢工
    【新华社讯】新加坡消息：新加坡约一万市政工人为追加工资在十九日举行大罢工，参加的有电力、自来水、煤气、下水道、清道、公路、卫生和建筑工程等部门的工人。
