Title: Pham Van Dong and Mendès France Hold Third Meeting; Pham Van Dong Meets with New Foreign Minister of the Bảo Đại Government, Trần Văn Độ
Author:
Date: 1954-07-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 13th: Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Foreign Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and French Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Mendès France, held their third meeting here from 12:15 to 13:20 today. In order to bring the opinions of both sides closer, they continued to exchange views on various issues related to the restoration of peace in Indochina, which were discussed in the previous two meetings. The meeting was conducted in a harmonious atmosphere.

Prior to this, Pham Van Dong held his first meeting with Trần Văn Độ, the new Foreign Minister of the Bảo Đại government, between 11:30 and 12:20.

In the evening of the 13th, Pham Van Dong also received Trần Văn Hữu, the former Prime Minister of the Bảo Đại government.

### 范文同和孟戴斯—弗朗斯举行第三次会谈  范文同和保大政府新任外长陈文度会谈

1954-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    范文同和孟戴斯—弗朗斯举行第三次会谈
    范文同和保大政府新任外长陈文度会谈
    【新华社日内瓦十三日电】越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外长范文同和法国总理兼外长孟戴斯—弗朗斯十三日十二时三刻到十三时二十分在此间举行了第三次会谈。为了使双方意见趋于接近，他们继续就前两次会谈中所涉及的有关恢复印度支那和平的各种问题交换了意见。会谈在和谐的气氛中进行。
    在这以前，范文同在十一时半到十二时二十分之间第一次和保大政府新任外交部长陈文度举行了会谈。
    十三日傍晚，范文同又接见了保大政府前总理陈文友。
