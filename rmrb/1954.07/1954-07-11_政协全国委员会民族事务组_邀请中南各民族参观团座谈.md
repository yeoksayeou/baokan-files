Title: The CPPCC National Committee's Ethnic Affairs Group Invites the Central South Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation to a Discussion

Author:
Date: 1954-07-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 8th—The Ethnic Affairs Group of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference held a reception on the 8th, inviting the Central South Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation to a discussion. Present at the discussion were Xiong Shouqi, head of the Central South Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation, deputy heads Han Liren and Zhang Shengzhen, and all 87 members of the delegation. The discussion was presided over by Zhu Zaoguan, head of the Ethnic Affairs Group, and Gan Chunlei, deputy head. At the meeting, Zhu Zaoguan first welcomed the Central South Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation to Beijing. Then, he explained the provisions on ethnic issues in the draft constitution, pointing out that the draft constitution guarantees the friendship, mutual assistance, and cooperation of all ethnic groups on an equal basis, and fully protects the rights of all ethnic autonomous regions to develop their political, economic, and cultural construction. He hoped that representatives of all ethnic groups would carefully discuss the draft constitution and publicize it to the local masses after returning.

Zhang Shengzhen, deputy head of the visiting delegation, spoke, introducing the achievements in various aspects of construction in the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Region of Guangxi Province since its establishment more than a year ago. Following him, representatives of the Yao ethnic group from Guangxi Province, Li Wenfu, the Li ethnic group from Guangdong Province, Ma Kangning, the Dong ethnic group from Guangxi Province, Chen Yi, the Yao ethnic group from Guangdong Province, Long Heyangba, the Miao ethnic group from Hunan Province, Zhang Yuzhen, the Dong ethnic group, Yao Yaodong, the Yao ethnic group Zheng Changbin, and the Hui ethnic group from Henan Province, Zhao Yunsan, also spoke. They respectively described the development of agricultural production and various construction undertakings, as well as the improvement of people's lives, in the Hainan Li and Miao Autonomous Region of Guangdong Province, the Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Region of Hunan Province, and the Liannan Yao Autonomous Region of Guangdong Province in recent years. In their speeches, they unanimously expressed their warm support for the draft constitution. Finally, Xiong Shouqi, head of the visiting delegation, spoke, saying: The draft constitution reflects the long-standing wishes of the people of all ethnic groups, and we wholeheartedly support the draft constitution.

### 政协全国委员会民族事务组  邀请中南各民族参观团座谈

1954-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会民族事务组
    邀请中南各民族参观团座谈
    【新华社八日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会民族事务组在八日举行招待会，邀请中南各民族参观团座谈。出席座谈会的有中南各民族参观团团长熊寿祺，副团长韩立人、张声震和全体团员共八十七人。座谈会由民族事务组组长朱早观、副组长甘春雷主持。会上，朱早观首先对中南各民族参观团来北京参观表示欢迎。接着，他就宪法草案中关于民族问题的规定进行讲解，他指出：宪法草案保证了各民族在平等基础上的友好、互助、合作，并充分保障了各民族自治区发展其政治、经济和文化建设事业的权利。他希望各族代表认真讨论宪法草案，回去后向本地群众进行宣传。
    参观团副团长张声震发言，他介绍了广西省桂西僮族自治区成立一年多来各方面建设的成就。接着发言的有广西省瑶族代表黎文富，广东省黎族代表马康宁，广西省侗族代表陈裔，广东省瑶族代表龙河养八，湖南省苗族代表张玉珍、侗族代表姚耀东、瑶族代表郑昌斌，河南省回族代表赵云三等。他们分别讲述了广东省海南黎族苗族自治区、湖南省湘西苗族自治区、广东省连南瑶族自治区等地几年来农业生产和各项建设事业的发展，以及人民生活改善的情况，他们在发言中并一致表示热烈拥护宪法草案。最后，参观团团长熊寿祺发言，他说：宪法草案反映了各民族人民多年来的愿望，我们衷心拥护宪法草案。
