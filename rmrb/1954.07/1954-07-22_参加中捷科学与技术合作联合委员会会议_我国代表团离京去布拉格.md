Title: China Delegation Leaves Beijing for Prague to Attend Sino-Czech Joint Committee on Scientific and Technological Cooperation Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-07-22
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency:

    China Delegation Leaves Beijing for Prague to Attend Sino-Czech Joint Committee on Scientific and Technological Cooperation Meeting

    [Xinhua News Agency] The second meeting of the Sino-Czech Joint Committee on Scientific and Technological Cooperation will be held in Prague soon. The delegation of the People's Republic of China, consisting of nineteen people and led by Liu Lanbo, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Fuel Industry of the Central People's Government, left Beijing for Prague by plane on the 16th.

    Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Li Renjun, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Fuel Industry of the Central People's Government, Wang Lin, Director of the General Office, Guan Hantao, Deputy Director of the Technical Cooperation Bureau of the State Planning Commission, and more than twenty other people.

    Also present at the airport to see them off were: Šmíd, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Czechoslovak Republic Embassy in China, Šal, Commercial Counselor, and Molis, Deputy Commercial Counselor, and others.

### 参加中捷科学与技术合作联合委员会会议  我国代表团离京去布拉格

1954-07-22
第3版()
专栏：

    参加中捷科学与技术合作联合委员会会议
    我国代表团离京去布拉格
    【新华社讯】中捷科学与技术合作联合委员会第二届会议即将在布拉格举行。参加会议的中华人民共和国代表团一行十九人，由中央人民政府燃料工业部副部长刘澜波率领，在十六日乘飞机自北京去布拉格。
    前往机场欢送的有：中央人民政府燃料工业部副部长李人俊、办公厅主任王林，国家计划委员会技术合作局副局长管寒涛等二十多人。
    前往机场欢送的还有：捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻华大使馆临时代办石密茨、商务参赞沙尔、商务副参赞莫利斯等人。
