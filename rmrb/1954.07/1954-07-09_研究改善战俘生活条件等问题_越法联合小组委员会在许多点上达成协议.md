Title: Researching the Improvement of Prisoner of War Living Conditions and Other Issues: The Vietnam-France Joint Subcommittee Reaches Agreement on Many Points
Author:
Date: 1954-07-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, July 8th: According to the Vietnam News Agency, in order to implement the decision made at the local Trung Giap military conference on July 6th, the joint subcommittee responsible for studying the issue of improving prisoner of war living conditions, transporting medicine to prisoners of war, and exchanging prisoner of war letters, began its work on the morning of the 7th. According to direct sources, the representatives of the General Command of the Vietnamese People's Army and the General Command of the French Union Forces in the joint subcommittee reached an agreement on many points.

### 研究改善战俘生活条件等问题  越法联合小组委员会在许多点上达成协议

1954-07-09
第4版()
专栏：

    研究改善战俘生活条件等问题
    越法联合小组委员会在许多点上达成协议
    【新华社八日讯】越南通讯社北越八日讯：为履行中亦当地军事会议七月六日作出的决定，负责研究改善战俘生活条件问题、运送药品给战俘问题和交换战俘信件问题的联合小组委员会，在七日上午开始工作。据直接得到的消息说，联合小组委员会中的越南人民军总司令部代表和法兰西联邦部队总司令部代表在许多点上达成了协议。
