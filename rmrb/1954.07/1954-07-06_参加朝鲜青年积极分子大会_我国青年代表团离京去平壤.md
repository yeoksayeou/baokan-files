Title: Chinese Youth Delegation Leaves Beijing for Pyongyang to Attend Korean Youth Activists Conference
Author:
Date: 1954-07-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 5th: At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Youth League, the Chinese youth delegation, led by head of delegation Hu Keshi, departed Beijing for Pyongyang by train on July 5th to attend the upcoming Korean National Youth Activists Conference. Liu Daosheng, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, ministers of various departments of the Central Committee of the Youth League, and Tian Demin, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, were at the station to see them off. Xu Zhe, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Korean Embassy in China, also went to the station to see them off.

The list of the Chinese youth delegation:

    Head: Hu Keshi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League
    Deputy Head: Yang Haibo, First Deputy Secretary of the Northeast Working Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League
    Members: Zhong Peizhang, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of "China Youth Daily"
    Gao Wenhui, Chief of the Youth Work Section of the Youth Department of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government People's Revolutionary Military Commission
    Zhang Jiming, Minister of the Youth Workers Department of the Northwest Working Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League
    Lin Qingyang (female), Minister of the Youth Department of the Political Department of the Guangzhou Railway Administration
    Yi Shijuan (female), textile worker, National Model Worker of the Textile Industry
    Qiao Liguang, Deputy Director of Geng Changsuo Agricultural Production Cooperative
    Hu Qili, Secretary of the Peking University Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League
    Staff: Jin Dahong

### 参加朝鲜青年积极分子大会  我国青年代表团离京去平壤

1954-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

    参加朝鲜青年积极分子大会
    我国青年代表团离京去平壤
    【新华社五日讯】应朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会的邀请前往参加即将举行的朝鲜全国青年积极分子大会的中国青年代表团，由团长胡克实率领，已在七月五日乘火车离北京去平壤。前往车站欢送的有中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记刘导生、青年团中央委员会各部部长和中华全国学生联合会主席田德民等。朝鲜驻华大使馆临时代办徐哲也到车站欢送。
    中国青年代表团名单：
    团长  胡克实  中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记
