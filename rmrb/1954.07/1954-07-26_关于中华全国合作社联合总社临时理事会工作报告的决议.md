Title: Resolution on the Work Report of the Provisional Council of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives
Author:
Date: 1954-07-26
Page: 2

    Resolution of the First Congress of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives on the Work Report of the Provisional Council of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives, the full text is as follows:

    The First Congress of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives unanimously approved the report made by Comrade Cheng Zihua on behalf of the Provisional Council of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives, "Striving to further develop urban-rural material exchange, promote the agricultural production increase movement centered on mutual aid and cooperation, and support national industrialization," and called on all members and staff of supply and marketing cooperatives and consumer cooperatives across the country to conscientiously study this report and strengthen unity, closely relying on the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and the People's Government, to strive for the victorious realization of the basic tasks of supply and marketing cooperatives and consumer cooperatives during the transitional period of the nation. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 关于中华全国合作社联合总社临时理事会工作报告的决议

1954-07-26
第2版()
专栏：

    关于中华全国合作社联合总社临时理事会工作报告的决议
    中华全国合作社第一次代表大会关于中华全国合作社联合总社临时理事会工作报告的决议，全文如下：
    中华全国合作社第一次代表大会一致同意程子华同志代表中华全国合作社联合总社临时理事会所作的“为进一步开展城乡物资交流，促进以互助合作为中心的农业增产运动，支援国家工业化而奋斗”的报告，并号召全国供销合作社和消费合作社的全体社员和职工认真学习这个报告，并加强团结，紧紧依靠中国共产党和人民政府的领导，为胜利地实现供销合作社和消费合作社在国家过渡时期的基本任务而奋斗。　（新华社）
