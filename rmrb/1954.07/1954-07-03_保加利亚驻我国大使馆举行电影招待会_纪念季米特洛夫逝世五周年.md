Title: Bulgarian Embassy in China Holds Film Reception to Commemorate the Fifth Anniversary of Dimitrov's Death
Author:
Date: 1954-07-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 2nd – To commemorate the fifth anniversary of the death of Georgi Dimitrov, the outstanding activist of the international workers' movement and mentor of the Bulgarian people's revolution, the Embassy of the People's Republic of Bulgaria in China held a film reception on the evening of the 2nd, showing the Bulgarian feature film "Song of Man" to entertain guests from all walks of life.
    Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Liu Aifeng, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Higher Education, and Xu Xuehan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, were invited to attend.
    Famous figures from the capital's cultural, educational, artistic, and journalistic circles also attended the reception.
    Diplomatic envoys and embassy staff from various countries to China also attended the reception.
    A total of more than 600 people attended the reception.
    The embassy also held a "Photo Exhibition Commemorating the Fifth Anniversary of the Death of Georgi Dimitrov," introducing the great life of Georgi Dimitrov. All the guests mourned this great and ardent fighter for the victory of communism with deep respect and sorrow.

### 保加利亚驻我国大使馆举行电影招待会  纪念季米特洛夫逝世五周年

1954-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

    保加利亚驻我国大使馆举行电影招待会
    纪念季米特洛夫逝世五周年
   【新华社二日讯】为纪念国际工人运动的杰出活动家、保加利亚人民革命导师格奥尔基·季米特洛夫逝世五周年，保加利亚人民共和国驻华大使馆特在二日晚举行电影招待会，放映保加利亚故事片“人之歌”招待各界。
    我中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权，高等教育部副部长刘皑风，对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒等都应邀出席。
    出席招待会的还有首都文化、教育、艺术、新闻界著名人士。
    各国驻华使节和使馆人员也出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的共有六百多人。
    大使馆并同时举办了“纪念格奥尔基·季米特洛夫逝世五周年图片展览”，介绍格奥尔基·季米特洛夫的伟大生平。来宾们都怀着深切的敬意和沉痛的心情，悼念着这位伟大的争取共产主义胜利的热情斗士。
