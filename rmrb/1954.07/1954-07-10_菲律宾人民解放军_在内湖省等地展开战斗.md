Title: Philippine People's Liberation Army Engages in Combat in Laguna Province and Other Areas
Author:
Date: 1954-07-10
Page: 4

    Philippine People's Liberation Army
    Engages in Combat in Laguna Province and Other Areas
    [Xinhua News Agency, Manila, 8th] According to disclosures from Philippine government military authorities, the Philippine People's Liberation Army is engaged in combat with government forces in Laguna Province and Pampanga Province. Government forces announced on the 7th that they had suffered casualties in Laguna Province.

### 菲律宾人民解放军  在内湖省等地展开战斗

1954-07-10
第4版()
专栏：

    菲律宾人民解放军
    在内湖省等地展开战斗
    【新华社八日讯】马尼拉消息：据菲律宾政府军事当局披露，菲律宾人民解放军正在内湖省和邦邦牙省和政府军展开战斗。政府军方面七日公布，政府军在内湖省有伤亡。
