Title: Joint Communiqué Issued by Vietnamese and French Military Conference: Both Sides Reach Agreement on the Conference's Tasks, Authority, and Agenda

Author:

Date: 1954-07-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, July 5th: The Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, July 5th, reports: The full text of the joint communiqué of the opening meeting of the local military conference between the delegations of the General Command of the Vietnam People's Army and the General Command of the French Federation Forces in Indochina is as follows:

The local military conference between the delegation of the General Command of the Vietnam People's Army and the delegation of the General Command of the French Federation Forces in Indochina opened at Trung Giac on Route 3 at 8:00 AM Vietnam Democratic Republic standard time, 9:00 AM Saigon standard time, on July 4, 1954.

The opening meeting was conducted in a good atmosphere. The chief representatives of both delegations, in their opening remarks, expressed the desire of the people of both countries for peace and their earnest hope that the military conference would achieve good results.

On July 4th, both delegations reached an agreement on the conference's tasks, authority, agenda, working principles, and working methods.

Subsequent meetings will discuss specific issues.

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, July 5th: The Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, July 5th, reports: The local military conference between the delegation of the General Command of the Vietnam People's Army and the delegation of the General Command of the French Federation Forces in Indochina held its second meeting at 2:00 PM on July 4th to discuss the conference's tasks, content, and agenda.

The two delegations reached the following agreement on the tasks and content of the conference:

1. The local military conference will discuss and make recommendations on issues related to all military matters raised by the Geneva Conference.

2. The conference will discuss and decide on measures to implement the agreements reached at the Geneva Conference on military matters.

3. The conference will also discuss military issues arising from specific local circumstances.

Regarding the agenda, both delegations agreed to discuss the following issues:

1. The issue of prisoners of war within the scope of the provisions unanimously agreed upon at the Geneva Conference.

2. The issue of implementing the ceasefire.

3. The issue of adjusting re-groupment zones.

4. The issue of joint committees.

5. Issues raised by the Geneva Military Conference that the local military conference deems necessary to raise.

The second meeting ended at 5:00 PM on the 4th. The third meeting will be held in the afternoon of the 5th to discuss the issue of prisoners of war.

### 越法双方军事会议发表联合公报  双方就会议的任务、权限和议程达成协议

1954-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

    越法双方军事会议发表联合公报
    双方就会议的任务、权限和议程达成协议
    【新华社五日讯】越南通讯社北越五日讯：越南人民军和印度支那法兰西联邦部队双方总司令部代表团当地军事会议的开幕会议联合公报全文如下：
    越南人民军总司令部代表团和印度支那法兰西联邦部队总司令部代表团之间的当地军事会议，在一九五四年七月四日越南民主共和国标准时间上午八时、西贡标准时间上午九时在第三号公路上的中亦开幕。
    开幕会议在良好的气氛中进行。双方代表团的首席代表在他们的开幕发言中表明了两国人民谋求和平的愿望以及他们要求军事会议取得良好结果的殷切期望。
    在七月四日，双方代表团就会议的任务、权限、议程、工作原则和工作方法达成了协议。
    以后的会议将讨论具体的问题。
    【新华社五日讯】越南通讯社北越五日讯：越南人民军总司令部代表团和印度支那法兰西联邦部队总司令部代表团的当地军事会议在七月四日下午二时举行第二次会议，讨论会议的任务、内容和议程。
    双方代表团就会议的任务和内容达成了下列协议：
    一、当地军事会议将就和日内瓦会议提出的一切军事问题有关的问题进行讨论和提出建议。
    二、会议将讨论并决定实现日内瓦会议就军事问题达成的协议的措施。
    三、会议也将讨论当地具体情况所引起的军事问题。
    在议程方面，双方代表团同意讨论下列问题：
    一、在日内瓦会议一致协议的条款范围内的战俘问题。
    二、实现停火的问题。
    三、调整重新集结的地区的问题。
    四、联合委员会的问题。
    五、日内瓦军事会议所提出而当地军事会议认为有必要提出的问题。
    第二次会议在四日下午五时结束。第三次会议将在五日下午举行，讨论战俘问题。
