Title: Australian "Guardian" Publishes Editorial Praising China's Draft Constitution
Author:
Date: 1954-07-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 7th:

    The Australian "Guardian" published an editorial on the 1st, entitled "China's New Constitution: All Power to the People," commenting on the promulgation of the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China.

    The editorial said: "Six hundred and one million people united in a democratic country, organized under the unified goal of serving the material and cultural interests of the people - this is how this new and great country has risen in the world. The interests of this country are built on peace and friendship." The editorial also stated that establishing friendly relations between Australia and this amiable great country is "all benefit and no harm."

### 澳大利亚“卫报”发表社论赞扬我国宪法草案

1954-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

    澳大利亚“卫报”发表社论赞扬我国宪法草案
    【新华社七日讯】澳大利亚“卫报”一日以“中国的新宪法：一切权力归于人民”为题发表社论，评论中华人民共和国宪法草案的公布。
    社论说：“六亿零一百多万人民团结在一个民主的国家里，在为人民的物质和文化的利益而服务的统一的目标下组织起来——这就是这个新的、巨大的国家在世界上兴起的情况。这一个国家的利益建立在和平与友谊之上。”社论并说，澳大利亚和这个可亲的大国建立友好关系，是“有百利而无一弊的”。
