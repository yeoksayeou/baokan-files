Title: The First Session of the First People's Congresses Held in Leagues, Administrative Regions, and Autonomous Region-Administered Cities of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region
Author:
Date: 1954-07-28
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency

    The seven leagues, two administrative regions, and two autonomous region-administered cities of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region held their first sessions of the first People's Congresses successively from early April to the end of July. A total of 376 representatives to the First People's Congress of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region were elected. The representatives included workers, farmers, herdsmen, and representatives from other strata of ten ethnic groups, including Mongolian, Han, Hui, Manchu, Tibetan, Korean, and Oroqen.
    The People's Congresses of the leagues, administrative regions, and cities enthusiastically discussed the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China. The representatives unanimously agreed that the Draft Constitution embodies the spirit of equality, unity, friendship, mutual assistance among all ethnic groups in the country, and it will further consolidate the unity of the motherland and the solidarity of all ethnic groups. Representatives of all ethnic groups adopted resolutions to thoroughly publicize and organize the broad masses to discuss the Draft Constitution.
    The meetings also carefully reviewed the work of the People's Governments of the leagues, administrative regions, and cities, and in accordance with the proposals of the people of all ethnic groups and in combination with the spirit of the general task of the state during the transition period, formulated work plans for 1954. The People's Congresses of Hulunbuir, Xilin Gol, and Ulanqab leagues focused on discussing proposals for developing livestock production, and determined that in the future, the various policies and guidelines of the Central People's Government in pastoral areas will be further implemented, and the mutual aid and cooperation movement for livestock production will be gradually carried out to increase the number of fine breeds of livestock and animal products for the country. The Ulanqab League People's Congress called on farmers and herdsmen throughout the league to increase the net number of various livestock by 270,000 head this year. The Hetao Administrative Region is an agricultural area, and the Administrative Region People's Congress adopted a plan to increase grain production by 100 million jin this year. The People's Congresses of Hohhot and Baotou determined that supporting basic construction, increasing production and practicing economy, and strengthening the unity of all ethnic groups will be the central tasks for the future. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 内蒙古自治区各盟、行政区和自治区直辖市  举行第一届第一次人民代表大会会议

1954-07-28
第3版()
专栏：

    内蒙古自治区各盟、行政区和自治区直辖市
    举行第一届第一次人民代表大会会议
    内蒙古自治区七个盟、两个行政区和两个自治区直辖市，已在四月初到七月底，先后举行了第一届第一次人民代表大会会议，共选出内蒙古自治区第一届人民代表大会代表三百七十六名。代表中包括蒙、汉、回、满、藏、朝鲜、鄂伦春等十个民族的工人、农民、牧民和其他阶层的代表。
    各盟、行政区、市的人民代表大会会议，都热烈地讨论了中华人民共和国宪法草案。代表们一致认为宪法草案贯彻了国内各民族平等、团结、友爱、互助的精神，它将进一步巩固祖国的统一和各族的团结。各族代表通过了深入宣传并组织广大群众讨论宪法草案的决议。
    会议还认真地审查了各盟、行政区、市人民政府的工作，根据各族人民的提案结合国家过渡时期总任务的精神，制订了一九五四年的工作计划。呼伦贝尔、锡林郭勒、乌兰察布等盟的人民代表大会会议，都着重讨论了关于发展畜牧业生产的提案，确定今后将进一步贯彻中央人民政府在牧区的各项政策方针，和逐步开展畜牧业生产互助合作运动，为国家增殖更多的优良牲畜和畜产品。乌兰察布盟人民代表大会会议号召全盟农、牧民今年纯增殖各种牲畜二十七万头。河套行政区是农业地区，行政区人民代表大会会议通过了今年增产粮食一亿斤的计划。呼和浩特、包头二市人民代表大会会议都确定以支援基本建设、增产节约和加强各民族团结等，为今后的中心工作。（新华社）
