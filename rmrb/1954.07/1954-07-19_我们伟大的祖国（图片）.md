    Our Great Motherland
    This is a Ding ware baby pillow from the Song Dynasty (960-1279 AD). This porcelain piece has precise modeling and a lovely image, fully demonstrating the high level of achievement of Ding ware in white porcelain with engraving and carving during the Song Dynasty.
