Title: Ceylon Federation of Trade Unions Delegation Head, A. Vaidialingam, Departs Guangzhou for Home

Author:
Date: 1954-07-05
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, July 4th. A. Vaidialingam, the head of the Ceylon Federation of Trade Unions delegation, who was invited to visit China, arrived in Guangzhou from Beijing on the 3rd and departed Guangzhou for Hong Kong on the 4th to return home. Among those who saw him off at the station were Rao Weihua, Director of the Labor Insurance Department of the Guangzhou Federation of Trade Unions, and others.

### 锡兰工会联合会代表团团长  瓦伊德林干离广州回国

1954-07-05
第4版()
专栏：

    锡兰工会联合会代表团团长
    瓦伊德林干离广州回国
    【新华社广州四日电】应邀来我国参观、访问的锡兰工会联合会代表团团长阿·瓦伊德林干，在三日由北京到达广州，四日离广州经香港回国。到车站欢送的有广州市工会联合会劳保部部长饶卫华等人。
