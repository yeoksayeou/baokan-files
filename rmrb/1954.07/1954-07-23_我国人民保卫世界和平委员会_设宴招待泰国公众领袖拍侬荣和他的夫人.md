Title: China Peace Committee Hosts Banquet for Thai Public Leader Phibun Songkhram and His Wife
Author:
Date: 1954-07-23
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd. The China People's Committee for World Peace held a banquet on the 22nd to entertain the well-known Thai public leader, Mr. Phibun Songkhram, and his wife. The banquet was hosted by Liao Chengzhi, Vice-Chairman of the China People's Committee for World Peace. Mr. Sawasdi Daolarueang of Thailand, Liu Ningyi, Standing Committee Member of the China People's Committee for World Peace, and Secretary-General Liu Guanyi attended as guests.
Mr. Phibun Songkhram formerly served as the Prime Minister of the Thai government and a member of the Thai Parliament.
