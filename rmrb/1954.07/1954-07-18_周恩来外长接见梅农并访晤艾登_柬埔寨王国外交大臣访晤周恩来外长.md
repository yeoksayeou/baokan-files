Title: Zhou Enlai Meets with Menon and Visits Eden; Cambodian Foreign Minister Visits Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1954-07-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 16th. Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, received Menon, the Indian Representative to the United Nations, at 10:00 PM on the 16th. Qiao Guanhua, advisor to the Chinese delegation to the Geneva Conference, was present at the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 17th. Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, visited British Foreign Secretary Eden at 11:40 AM on the 17th, for a 50-minute meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 17th. Cambodian Foreign Minister Tep Phan visited Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, at 10:00 AM on the 17th, for a one hour and twenty minute meeting.

### 周恩来外长接见梅农并访晤艾登  柬埔寨王国外交大臣访晤周恩来外长

1954-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来外长接见梅农并访晤艾登
    柬埔寨王国外交大臣访晤周恩来外长
    【新华社日内瓦十六日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来十六日晚十时接见印度驻联合国代表梅农。接见时在座的有中国出席日内瓦会议代表团顾问乔冠华。
    【新华社日内瓦十七日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来十七日上午十一时四十分访晤英国外交大臣艾登，会谈五十分钟。
   【新华社日内瓦十七日电】柬埔寨王国外交大臣泰普潘十七日上午十时访晤中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来，会谈一小时二十分钟。
