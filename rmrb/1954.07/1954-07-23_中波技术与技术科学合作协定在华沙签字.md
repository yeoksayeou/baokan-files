Title: Sino-Polish Agreement on Technical and Technical Science Cooperation Signed in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1954-07-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd -- The agreement between the People's Republic of China and the People's Republic of Poland on technical and technical science cooperation was signed in Warsaw on the 20th after friendly consultations between the two parties. Wang Shoudao, head of the Chinese technical and technical science delegation and Vice Minister of the Ministry of Communications of the Central People's Government, signed on behalf of China; Lesh, Vice Chairman of the State Economic Planning Commission, signed on behalf of Poland. Zeng Yongquan, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Poland, and members of the Chinese and Polish committees who participated in the consultations attended the signing ceremony.

The Sino-Polish technical and technical science cooperation will not only promote the extensive development of technology and technical science in the two countries, but also be of great significance for strengthening the socialist construction of the two countries and enhancing the friendship between the two peoples.

### 中波技术与技术科学合作协定在华沙签字

1954-07-23
第3版()
专栏：

    中波技术与技术科学合作协定在华沙签字
     【新华社二十二日讯】中华人民共和国和波兰人民共和国技术与技术科学合作协定，经过双方在友好的气氛下商谈后，于二十日在华沙签字。代表中国方面签字的是中国技术与技术科学代表团团长、中央人民政府交通部副部长王首道；代表波兰方面签字的是国家经济计划委员会副主席莱什。参加协定签字仪式的有中华人民共和国驻波兰大使曾涌泉以及参加商谈的中波双方委员。
    中波技术与技术科学合作不仅促进两国技术与技术科学的广泛发展，而且对加强两国的社会主义建设与增进两国人民间的友谊也有重大意义。
