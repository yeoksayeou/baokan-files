Title: Telegram of Congratulations from the Central Council of the Polish "Peasant Mutual Aid" Agricultural Cooperatives

Author: 
Date: 1954-07-26
Page: 2

Telegram of Congratulations from the Central Council of the Polish "Peasant Mutual Aid" Agricultural Cooperatives
The Central Council of the Polish "Peasant Mutual Aid" Agricultural Cooperatives, in a telegram to the First Congress of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives, said: On behalf of the three million members and cooperative workers of our cooperative, we warmly congratulate your cooperative's achievements. The growth of your cooperative is a strong fortress promoting the worker-peasant alliance of the people's regime.
We warmly congratulate you on the further consolidation and development achieved in your country's cooperative work. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 波兰“农民互助”农业合作社中央理事会贺电

1954-07-26
第2版()
专栏：

    波兰“农民互助”农业合作社中央理事会贺电
    波兰“农民互助”农业合作社中央理事会致中华全国合作社第一次代表大会的贺电说，谨代表我们合作社的三百万社员和合作社工作者，热烈的祝贺你们合作社的成就，你们合作社的成长乃是促成工农联盟人民政权的坚强堡垒。
    热烈的祝贺你们在你们祖国的合作社工作中获得进一步的巩固和发展。（新华社）
