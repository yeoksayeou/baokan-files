Title: Sino-Vietnamese Military Conference Subcommittee Studies Ceasefire Issues
Author:
Date: 1954-07-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, 13th: The North Vietnamese Sino-Vietnamese local military conference subcommittee studied the issue of implementing a ceasefire on the morning of July 12th. The discussions took place in a good atmosphere. No meeting was held on the afternoon of July 12th.
    The subcommittee continued its discussions on the morning of July 13th.

### 中亦军事会议小组委员会研究停火问题

1954-07-14
第1版()
专栏：

    中亦军事会议小组委员会研究停火问题
    【新华社十三日讯】越南通讯社北越十三日讯：北越中亦当地军事会议的一个小组委员会在七月十二日上午研究了实行停火的问题，讨论是在良好的气氛中进行的，七月十二日下午没有举行会议。
    小组委员会在七月十三日上午继续进行讨论。
