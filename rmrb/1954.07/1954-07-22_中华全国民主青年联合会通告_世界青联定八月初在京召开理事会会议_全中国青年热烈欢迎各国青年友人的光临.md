Title: All-China Youth Federation Notice: World Federation of Democratic Youth to Hold Executive Committee Meeting in Beijing in Early August; All Chinese Youth Warmly Welcome the Presence of Young Friends from All Countries
Author:
Date: 1954-07-22
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, July 21st: The All-China Youth Federation issued a notice on the 21st regarding the World Federation of Democratic Youth holding an Executive Committee meeting in Beijing, the full text of which is as follows:

    The World Federation of Democratic Youth has decided to hold a meeting of its Executive Committee in our capital, Beijing, from August 9th to 15th this year. We wholeheartedly support this decision and warmly welcome young friends from all countries to come.

    The World Federation of Democratic Youth is a united organization of peace-loving and democratic youth from all countries. It unites youth from all countries around the world, regardless of race, color, political views, or religious beliefs, to fight for peace, democracy, national independence, and a bright future for youth, and has achieved brilliant accomplishments in these struggles. Therefore, more and more young people are joining the ranks of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, which now has member organizations in ninety-three countries, including more than eighty million members. In addition to the members of the Executive Committee of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, representatives of other youth organizations with different views will also participate in this Executive Committee Meeting, coming from all over the world, holding the common aspiration of defending peace, to attend the meeting in Beijing. This is another grand unity meeting of youth around the world and is one of the important events in the current world youth movement.

    The agenda of this meeting includes issues concerning the colonial youth movement, preparing for the International Rural Youth Conference, and holding the Fifth World Youth and Student Festival. In the current situation where the struggles of people and youth in various countries to defend peace and strive for national independence are rising, holding such a broad international youth conference in our country to discuss the main issues related to the world youth movement will strengthen the struggles of youth in various countries for peace and national independence, enhance the broad unity of youth in various countries, and promote the friendship between youth in various countries and youth in our country, which is of great importance and significance. It will also make an important contribution to the world peace movement and will surely receive the enthusiastic attention and importance of youth across the country.

    Now, the meeting of the Executive Committee of the World Federation of Democratic Youth is about to open. Chinese youth, with their tradition of internationalism, will always be loyal to the common cause of the world's democratic youth. The All-China Youth Federation calls on the youth of the whole country to promote among the youth the activities and achievements of the World Federation of Democratic Youth in recent years, to promote the important significance of this meeting of the Executive Committee of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, to strengthen the friendship and unity between Chinese youth and youth of all countries in the world, and to prepare to warmly receive our international youth friends. At the same time, we must carry forward the spirit of patriotism and internationalism in our own positions, and take practical actions of hard work in production, work, and study to better build our great socialist country, to warmly welcome the arrival of international youth friends, and to greet the convening of the meeting of the Executive Committee of the World Federation of Democratic Youth.

### 中华全国民主青年联合会通告  世界青联定八月初在京召开理事会会议  全中国青年热烈欢迎各国青年友人的光临

1954-07-22
第3版()
专栏：

    中华全国民主青年联合会通告
    世界青联定八月初在京召开理事会会议
    全中国青年热烈欢迎各国青年友人的光临
    【新华社二十一日讯】中华全国民主青年联合会二十一日发出关于世界民主青年联盟在北京召开理事会会议的通告，全文如下：
    世界民主青年联盟决定于今年八月九日至十五日在我国首都北京召开世界民主青年联盟理事会会议。我们衷心拥护这一个决定，热烈欢迎各国青年友人光临。
    世界民主青年联盟是世界各国爱好和平民主青年的联合组织。它在团结着全世界各国的青年，不分种族肤色、政治见解和宗教信仰，为争取和平、民主、民族独立与青年美好的将来而斗争，并在这些斗争中获得了光辉的成就。因此，愈来愈多的青年汇集到世界民主青年联盟的行列中来，它现在拥有九十三个国家的会员团体，包括八千多万会员。参加这次理事会会议的，除了世界民主青年联盟理事会理事外，还有其他不同见解的青年组织的代表，从世界各地，怀抱着保卫和平的共同愿望，前来北京列席会议，这是全世界青年又一次大团结的会议，是当前世界青年运动的重要事件之一。
    这次会议议程是关于殖民地青年运动、筹备国际农村青年大会、举行第五届世界青年与学生联欢节等问题。在目前各国人民和青年为保卫和平和争取民族独立的斗争更加高涨的形势下，在我国举行这样一个广泛的国际青年会议，来讨论有关世界青年运动的主要问题，这对加强各国青年争取和平与民族独立的斗争，增进各国青年的广泛团结，促进各国青年同我国青年的友谊，是具有重要的作用和意义的。它对世界保卫和平的运动亦将会有重要的贡献，它必将得到全国青年的热情关怀和重视。
    现在，世界民主青年联盟理事会会议快要开幕了。有着国际主义传统的中国青年，是永远忠实于世界民主青年的共同事业的。中华全国民主青年联合会向全国青年发出这样号召：要在青年中宣传世界民主青年联盟几年来的活动和成就，宣传这次世界民主青年联盟理事会会议的重要意义，加强中国青年和世界各国青年的友谊团结，准备热烈地接待我们的国际青年友人。同时，要在自己的岗位上发扬爱国主义与国际主义精神，以努力生产、工作和学习的实际行动，更好地建设我们伟大的社会主义国家，来热烈欢迎国际青年友人的光临，和迎接世界民主青年联盟理事会会议的召开。
