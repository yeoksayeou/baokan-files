Title: Youth League Organizations in Yining City, Xinjiang Host First Children's Summer Camp
Author:
Date: 1954-07-12
Page: 3

    Youth League Organizations in Yining City, Xinjiang
    Host First Children's Summer Camp
    The first children's summer camp in Yining City, sponsored by the Yining City Working Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League of Xinjiang Province, was held at Tuohulasu Ranch, 65 kilometers from the city. Tuohulasu Ranch is a summer pasture for Kazakh herders in the Tianshan Mountains. The scenery is beautiful and the climate is cool, making it a famous summer resort. One hundred and thirty Young Pioneer members of Uyghur, Kazakh, Hui, Han, Mongolian, Tatar, Uzbek, Xibe, and Kirgiz ethnicities participated in the summer camp. During the summer camp, they will carry out various study and recreational activities with the theme of loving the great motherland. The Yining City Working Committee of the Youth League also hired seven middle and primary school teachers and nine counselors to provide guidance. The summer camp will last for twenty-five days and is scheduled to end in early August. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 新疆伊宁市青年团组织  举办首届少年夏令营

1954-07-12
第3版()
专栏：

    新疆伊宁市青年团组织
    举办首届少年夏令营
    中国新民主主义青年团新疆省伊宁市工作委员会主办的伊宁市首届少年夏令营，在距城六十五公里的脱乎拉苏牧场举行。脱乎拉苏牧场是天山支脉中哈萨克族牧民夏季放牧的地方，风景优美，气候凉爽，是有名的避暑地带。参加夏令营的有维吾尔、哈萨克、回、汉、蒙古、塔塔尔、乌兹别克、锡伯、柯尔克兹等族的一百三十名少年先锋队队员。在夏令营中，他们将以热爱伟大祖国为主要课题展开各种学习和文娱活动。青年团伊宁市工作委员会还聘请了七名中、小学教员和九名辅导员进行指导。夏令营营地活动日期二十五天，计划在八月初结束。　　　　　　（新华社）
