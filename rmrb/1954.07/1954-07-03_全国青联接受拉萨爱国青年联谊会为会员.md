Title: All-China Youth Federation Accepts Lhasa Patriotic Youth Association as Member
Author: 
Date: 1954-07-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 2nd—The Standing Committee of the All-China Democratic Youth Federation accepted the application of the Lhasa Patriotic Youth Cultural Association on May 4, 1954, and approved the Lhasa Patriotic Youth Cultural Association as a member of the All-China Democratic Youth Federation.
    Attached: Full text of the telegram from the Lhasa Patriotic Youth Cultural Association to the All-China Democratic Youth Federation applying for membership:
    To the All-China Democratic Youth Federation:
    Since its official establishment on January 31, 1953, the Lhasa Patriotic Youth Cultural Association now has more than 440 male and female members and has achieved certain successes in carrying out patriotic studies and cultural, recreational, and sports activities. After listening to the report by Xuekang Tudeng Nima, a member of the All-China Democratic Youth Federation, on the spirit of the resolutions of the Second National Congress of the All-China Youth, we realized that the All-China Democratic Youth Federation is the organization of the youth of all nationalities in our motherland. We are determined, under the leadership of the great leader of all nationalities, Chairman Mao, and the Central People's Government, to raise the banner of patriotism and contribute our youthful strength to the great cause of building the great family of all nationalities in the motherland. On the occasion of the 35th anniversary of the "May Fourth" Movement, all members of the Lhasa Patriotic Youth Cultural Association unanimously approved joining the All-China Democratic Youth Federation and are willing to carry out all work and study under your leadership in the future.
        All Members of the Lhasa Patriotic Youth Cultural Association
                          May 4, 1954

### 全国青联接受拉萨爱国青年联谊会为会员

1954-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

    全国青联接受拉萨爱国青年联谊会为会员
   【新华社二日讯】中华全国民主青年联合会常务委员会接受了拉萨市爱国青年文化联谊会一九五四年五月四日的申请，通过拉萨市爱国青年文化联谊会为中华全国民主青年联合会会员。
    附：拉萨市爱国青年文化联谊会向中华全国民主青年联合会申请加入为会员的电报全文：中华全国民主青年联合会：
    拉萨市爱国青年文化联谊会自一九五三年一月三十一日正式成立以来，现已有男女会员四百四十余人，在进行爱国主义的学习和文化娱乐体育活动等方面，都取得了一定成绩。最近听取了中华全国民主青年联合会委员雪康·土登尼玛关于中华全国青年第二次代表大会决议精神的传达报告后，我们认识到中华全国民主青年联合会就是我们祖国各民族青年自己的组织。我们决心在各族人民伟大的领袖毛主席和中央人民政府的领导下，举起爱国主义的旗帜，将自己青春的力量贡献给建设祖国各民族大家庭的伟大事业中。当此“五四”三十五周年纪念之际，拉萨市爱国青年文化联谊会全体会员一致通过了加入中华全国民主青年联合会，今后愿在你们的领导下进行全部工作与学习。
        拉萨市爱国青年文化联谊会全体会员
                          一九五四年五月四日
