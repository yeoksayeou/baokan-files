Title: Molotov Hosts Banquet for Zhou Enlai and Pham Van Dong and Meets with Eden to Discuss the Continuation of the Geneva Conference
Author:
Date: 1954-07-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 14th: Soviet First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Molotov, hosted a banquet at 8 p.m. on the 14th to entertain Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People’s Republic of China, and Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

    The Chinese side attending the banquet included: Zhang Wentian and Li Kenong, representatives of the People’s Republic of China delegation to the Geneva Conference, Wang Bingnan, Secretary-General of the delegation, and advisors Shi Zhe, Qiao Guanhua, Chen Jiakang, Huang Hua, and Wang Zhuoru.

    The Vietnamese side attending the banquet included: Phan Anh, Hoang Van Hoan, and Ta Quang Buu, representatives of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam delegation to the Geneva Conference.

    The Soviet side attending the banquet included: Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Kuznetsov, member of the Collegium of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Ilyichev, Soviet Ambassador to France Vinogradov, Director of the Southeast Asia Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs Novikov, and Director of the First European Department Lavrishev.

    The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.
   Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 14th: Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov held a meeting with British Foreign Secretary Eden, who returned from Paris on the same day, at the residence of the Soviet delegation on the 14th.

    The meeting concerned the continuation of the Geneva Conference.

### 莫洛托夫设宴招待周恩来和范文同  并和艾登会谈日内瓦会议继续进行的问题

1954-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫设宴招待周恩来和范文同
    并和艾登会谈日内瓦会议继续进行的问题
    【新华社日内瓦十四日电】苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部部长莫洛托夫在十四日晚八时设宴招待中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来和越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外交部部长范文同。
    中国方面出席宴会的有：出席日内瓦会议的中华人民共和国代表团代表张闻天、李克农和代表团秘书长王炳南，顾问师哲、乔冠华、陈家康、黄华、王倬如。
    越南方面出席宴会的有：出席日内瓦会议的越南民主共和国代表团代表潘英、黄文欢、谢光宝。
    苏联方面出席宴会的有：苏联外交部副部长库兹涅佐夫，外交部部务委员会委员伊利切夫，苏联驻法大使维诺格拉多夫，苏联外交部东南亚司司长诺维科夫、第一欧洲司司长拉夫里谢夫。
    宴会在热烈友好空气中进行。
   【新华社日内瓦十四日电】苏联外交部部长莫洛托夫十四日在苏联代表团住所同当天从巴黎回来的英国外交大臣艾登举行了一次会谈。
    会谈的是有关日内瓦会议继续进行的问题。
