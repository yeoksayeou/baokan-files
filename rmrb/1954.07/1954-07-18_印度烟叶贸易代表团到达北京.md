Title: Indian Tobacco Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-07-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 17th:

    The Indian tobacco trade delegation, headed by K. Raghumiah, and including members M. Subba Rao, M. V. S. Rao, G. Narahari, and I. Gubbay, arrived in Beijing on the evening of the 15th. They were greeted at the station by Ni Weiting, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, and others.

    On the evening of the 16th, Deputy Manager Ni Weiting hosted a banquet for the entire delegation, which was attended by Wei Mengling, Deputy Director of the Liaison Office of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government, and others.

### 印度烟叶贸易代表团到达北京

1954-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    印度烟叶贸易代表团到达北京
    【新华社十七日讯】印度烟叶贸易代表团团长克·拉古迈雅，团员麦·斯巴特尔、麦·维·斯·拉奥、格·纳拉哈里、伊·顾拜等一行五人，已在十五日晚到达北京。前往车站迎接的，有中国进出口公司副经理倪蔚庭等多人。
    十六日晚，倪蔚庭副经理曾设宴招待代表团全体人员，中央人民政府对外贸易部交际处副处长魏梦龄等人出席了这个宴会。
