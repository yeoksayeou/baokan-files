Title: Shanghai Opens New Building Hardware Store
Author:
Date: 1954-07-08
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency

    Shanghai Opens New Building Hardware Store
    A state-owned building hardware store serving basic construction was newly opened in Shanghai on July 5th. On the opening day, purchasing personnel stationed in Shanghai from units such as Anshan Iron and Steel Company, Central China Iron and Steel Company, the First Automobile Manufacturing Plant, and the Soviet Exhibition Hall in Beijing came to visit or purchase goods.
    This state-owned building hardware store is the third specialized store newly established by the Shanghai Branch of the China National Hardware and Machinery Corporation, following the tool store and the steel store, in order to meet the needs of national economic construction. The hardware and equipment sold in this store comprises twenty-four categories totaling more than three thousand varieties. The vast majority of these products are domestically produced, and mainly manufactured by factories in Shanghai.

### 上海新设建筑五金商店开幕

1954-07-08
第2版()
专栏：

    上海新设建筑五金商店开幕
    上海新设的一座为基本建设服务的国营建筑五金商店在七月五日开幕。开幕的这一天，鞍山钢铁公司、华中钢铁公司、第一汽车制造厂和北京苏联展览馆等单位驻上海的采购人员，都前来参观或采购商品。
    这个国营建筑五金商店是中国五金机械公司上海分公司为适应国家经济建设的需要，继工具商店、钢铁商店之后新设的第三个专业商店。这个商店经营的五金器材共有二十四类计三千多种。这些商品绝大部分是国产品，而且主要是上海的工厂生产的。　　（新华社）
