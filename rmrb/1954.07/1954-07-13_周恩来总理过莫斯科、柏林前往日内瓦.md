Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Passes Through Moscow and Berlin En Route to Geneva
Author:
Date: 1954-07-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 12th: Zhou Enlai, Chief Representative of the People's Republic of China Delegation to the Geneva Conference, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, and his entourage, departed from Moscow by plane to Geneva on the morning of the 12th.

Those who went to the airport to see him off included: Mikoyan, Vice-Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers and Minister of Trade; Vyshinsky, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union; Yasnov, Chairman of the Moscow City Soviet; Kovalev, Acting Commander of the Moscow City Defense Command; Fedlin, Director of the Far East Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Kabushkin, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department.

Also present at the airport to see him off were Lin Hai, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the Soviet Union, and Nguyen Luong Bang, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Soviet Union.

Wen Ning, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of our Embassy in the Soviet Union, and embassy staff also went to the airport to see him off.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 12th: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, passed through Berlin at 10:00 AM on the 12th en route to Geneva.  Those who welcomed him at the airport included: Bolz, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic; Deputy Prime Ministers Schulz and Loch; Thun, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others. Miroshnichenko, Deputy Senior Commissioner of the Soviet Union to Germany and Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in Germany, Ji Pengfei, our Ambassador to Germany, and diplomatic personnel from our embassy also welcomed him at the airport.

### 周恩来总理过莫斯科、柏林前往日内瓦

1954-07-13
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理过莫斯科、柏林前往日内瓦
    【新华社莫斯科十二日电】出席日内瓦会议的中华人民共和国代表团首席代表、中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来和随行人员在十二日上午乘飞机自莫斯科前往日内瓦。
    前往飞机场欢送的有：苏联部长会议副主席兼贸易部部长米高扬、苏联外交部第一副部长维辛斯基、莫斯科市苏维埃主席雅斯诺夫、莫斯科市城防司令部代理司令科瓦列夫、苏联外交部远东司司长费德林、交际司副司长卡布什金等人。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻苏联大使林海、越南民主共和国驻苏联大使阮良朋也到飞机场欢送。
    到飞机场欢送的还有我国驻苏联大使馆临时代办温宁和使馆人员。
    【新华社柏林十二日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来在十二日上午十时路过柏林去日内瓦。到机场欢迎的有：德意志民主共和国副总理兼外交部部长博尔茨，副总理舒尔茨、洛赫，外交部交际司司长图恩等人。苏联驻德副高级专员兼苏联驻德使馆代办米罗什尼钦科，我驻德大使姬鹏飞及我使馆外交人员等也到机场欢迎。
