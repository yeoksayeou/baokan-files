Title: Malenkov Receives Saifuddin Kitchlew, Chairman of the All-India Peace Council
Author:
Date: 1954-07-15
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 14th: TASS Moscow reported: Georgy Maximilianovich Malenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, received Saifuddin Kitchlew, Chairman of the All-India Peace Council, in Moscow on the 13th, who came at the invitation of the Soviet Peace Committee and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 马林科夫接见全印和平理事会主席克其鲁

1954-07-15
第4版()
专栏：

    马林科夫接见全印和平理事会主席克其鲁
    【新华社十四日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席格·马·马林科夫十三日接见了应苏联拥护和平委员会和苏联对外文化协会的邀请前来莫斯科的全印和平理事会主席赛福丁·克其鲁。
