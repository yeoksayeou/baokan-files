Title: People from All Circles in the Capital Hold Grand Rally to Warmly Celebrate the Tenth Anniversary of Poland's National Revival Day
Author:
Date: 1954-07-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Report: People from all circles in the capital held a grand rally on the evening of the 21st to warmly celebrate the tenth anniversary of the Polish People's Republic's National Revival Day. The celebration was organized by the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government.

Those attending the meeting included: Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government; Lin Boqu, Secretary-General; Chen Yun and Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premiers of the Government Administration Council; Shen Junru, President of the Supreme People's Court; Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the Government Administration Council; Lu Dingyi, Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council; Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture; Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Peng Zhen, Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; as well as heads of various ministries, commissions, committees, academies, departments, and banks under the Government Administration Council, heads of various people's organizations, and people from the capital's science, education, culture, art, and press circles, totaling more than 500 people.

Foreign guests attending the meeting included: Kiryluk, Ambassador of the Polish People's Republic to China, and embassy officials.

Other foreign guests attending the meeting included: Romanian Ambassador Cotzoveanu, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic König, Soviet Chargé d'affaires ad interim Vassikov, Pakistani Chargé d'affaires ad interim Sultan Muhammad Khan, Vietnamese Democratic Republic Chargé d'affaires ad interim Zhou Liang, Czechoslovak Chargé d'affaires ad interim Šmíd, Hungarian Chargé d'affaires ad interim Czétényi, Bulgarian Chargé d'affaires ad interim Grigorov, Finnish Chargé d'affaires ad interim Seppälä, Swiss Chargé d'affaires ad interim Cuttat, Korean Democratic People's Republic Chargé d'affaires ad interim Seo Cheol, Dutch negotiation representative Smant, and diplomatic officials from various countries' embassies in China.

At the beginning of the meeting, the orchestra played the national anthems of Poland, China, and the Soviet Union. The entire audience stood up and applauded thunderously.

Amidst warm applause, Lu Dingyi, Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government, delivered a speech (full text to be published separately, see page 3).

The entire audience welcomed Kiryluk, Ambassador of the Polish People's Republic to China, with sustained applause as he delivered a speech (full text to be published separately, see page 3).

At the celebration, Chinese literary and artistic workers performed wonderful programs, which were warmly welcomed.

### 首都各界人民举行盛大集会  热烈庆祝波兰国家复兴节十周年

1954-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

    首都各界人民举行盛大集会
    热烈庆祝波兰国家复兴节十周年
    据新华社讯：首都各界人民二十一日晚举行盛大集会，热烈庆祝波兰人民共和国国家复兴节十周年。庆祝会由中央人民政府文化部举办。
    出席大会的有：中央人民政府副主席朱德，秘书长林伯渠，政务院副总理陈云、邓小平，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，政务院秘书长习仲勋，政务院文化教育委员会副主任陆定一，文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长伍修权，北京市人民政府市长彭真，以及政务院所属各部、委、会、院、署、行首长，各人民团体负责人，首都科学、教育、文化、艺术、新闻界人士等五百余人。
    出席会议的外宾有：波兰人民共和国驻我国大使基里洛克和使馆官员。
    出席会议的外宾还有：罗马尼亚大使郭佐文，德意志民主共和国大使柯尼希，苏联临时代办华司考，巴基斯坦临时代办苏尔唐·穆罕默德·汗，越南民主共和国临时代办周亮，捷克斯洛伐克临时代办石密茨，匈牙利临时代办察托尔道伊，保加利亚临时代办格利郭洛夫，芬兰临时代办谢列克，瑞士临时代办崔礼，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国临时代办徐哲，荷兰谈判代表司曼，以及各国驻我国使馆的外交官员。
    会议开始，乐队奏波、中、苏三国国歌，全场起立，掌声雷动。
    在热烈的掌声中，中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会副主任陆定一讲话（全文另发，见第三版）。
    全场以经久不息的掌声欢迎波兰人民共和国驻我国大使基里洛克讲话（全文另发，见第三版）。
    庆祝会上，中国的文艺工作者演出了精采的节目，受到热烈欢迎。
