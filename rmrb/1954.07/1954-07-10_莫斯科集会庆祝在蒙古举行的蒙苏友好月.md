Title: Moscow Rally Celebrates Month of Mongolian-Soviet Friendship Held in Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1954-07-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 9th: TASS Moscow reported: The Soviet Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Soviet Peace Committee held a conference in Moscow on the 8th to celebrate the Month of Mongolian-Soviet Friendship held in the Mongolian People's Republic. The conference was attended by scientific and cultural workers, representatives of well-known industrial workers in Moscow, and mass organizations.

Also attending the conference were B. Shirendyb, First Deputy Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, and D. Adilbish, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to the Soviet Union.

Soviet People's Artist Sergei Gerasimov spoke in his opening remarks about the trip of Soviet cultural workers to Mongolia, the warm reception they received, Mongolia's economic development, and the progress of its national culture.

Ambassador D. Adilbish was warmly welcomed by the participants, and he expressed his gratitude to the Soviet people.

The conference concluded with a concert, featuring performances by Soviet artists and Mongolian young singer Kh. Gangor.

### 莫斯科集会庆祝在蒙古举行的蒙苏友好月

1954-07-10
第4版()
专栏：

    莫斯科集会庆祝在蒙古举行的蒙苏友好月
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联对外文化协会和苏联拥护和平委员会八日在莫斯科举行大会，庆祝在蒙古人民共和国举行的蒙苏友好月。参加大会的有科学和文化工作者、莫斯科著名产业工人和群众团体的代表。
    参加大会的还有蒙古人民共和国第一副总理巴札伦·锡林迪布和蒙古人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使达兴·阿吉尔毕希。
    苏联人民艺术家谢尔盖·格拉西莫夫在开会词中谈到了苏联文化工作者到蒙古去的旅行，他们所受到的热诚的招待，蒙古的经济发展和民族文化的进步。
    达兴·阿吉尔毕希大使受到与会者的热烈欢迎，他向苏联人民表示感谢。
    大会最后举行了音乐会，参加演出的有苏联艺术家们和蒙古青年歌唱家哈伊达夫·冈戈尔。
