Title: Two Romanian Agricultural Scientists Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-07-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, July 5th. Two Romanian agricultural scientists, Maier Ion and Ilca C. Florin, arrived in Beijing by plane on July 5th to inspect agriculture in China in accordance with the 1954 implementation plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement. Ion is currently the Deputy Dean and Director of the Teaching and Research Office of the Bucharest Agricultural Institute, and Florin is currently the Director of the General Bureau of Collective Farms and a lecturer at the Bucharest Agricultural Institute. They will be visiting China for two months.

### 罗马尼亚两农业科学家到京

1954-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚两农业科学家到京
    【新华社五日讯】根据中罗文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划来我国考察农业的两位罗马尼亚农业科学家马叶尔·伊昂和伊尔卡·克·弗洛林，在七月五日乘飞机到达北京。伊昂现任布加勒斯特农学院副院长兼教研室主任，弗洛林现任集体农庄总局局长兼布加勒斯特农学院讲师，他们在中国将作两个月的访问。
