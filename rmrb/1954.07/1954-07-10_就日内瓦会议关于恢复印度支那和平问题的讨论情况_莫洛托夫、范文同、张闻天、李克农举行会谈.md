Title: Discussion on the Geneva Conference on the Restoration of Peace in Indochina: Molotov, Pham Van Dong, Zhang Wentian, and Li Kenong Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1954-07-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 9th: Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov held talks on July 8th with Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Zhang Wentian and Li Kenong, Deputy Foreign Ministers of the People's Republic of China.

	During the talks, they exchanged views on the discussions at the Geneva Conference regarding the restoration of peace in Indochina.
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 9th: Zhang Wentian and Li Kenong, representatives of the People's Republic of China delegation attending the Geneva Conference, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 8th in honor of Chauvel, the acting head of the French delegation.

	The French side was also attended by: Tournay, Director of Political Affairs of the French Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Raymond Offroy, Ambassador to Thailand; Guillemart, Advisor; Seger, Acting Secretary-General of the delegation; and Ligalov, Interpreter.

	The Chinese side was also attended by Wang Bingnan, Secretary-General of the delegation; Chen Jiakang, Huan Xiang, Advisors; and Dong Ningchuan, Interpreter.

	The banquet proceeded in a harmonious and friendly atmosphere throughout.

### 就日内瓦会议关于恢复印度支那和平问题的讨论情况  莫洛托夫、范文同、张闻天、李克农举行会谈

1954-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

    就日内瓦会议关于恢复印度支那和平问题的讨论情况
    莫洛托夫、范文同、张闻天、李克农举行会谈
    张闻天、李克农宴请法代表团代理团长萧维尔
   【新华社日内瓦九日电】苏联外交部部长莫洛托夫在七月八日同越南民主共和国副总理范文同和中华人民共和国外交部副部长张闻天、李克农进行了会谈。
    他们在会谈中就日内瓦会议关于恢复印度支那和平问题的讨论情况交换了意见。
   【新华社日内瓦九日电】出席日内瓦会议的中华人民共和国代表团代表张闻天和李克农八日晚宴请法国代表团代理团长萧维尔。
    法国方面参加的还有：法国外交部政务司司长杜尔奈、驻泰国大使雷蒙·奥弗罗瓦、顾问吉勒马兹、代表团代理秘书长塞格和译员利加洛夫。
    我方参加的还有代表团秘书长王炳南，顾问陈家康、宦乡，译员董宁川。
    宴会始终在和谐友好的空气中进行。
