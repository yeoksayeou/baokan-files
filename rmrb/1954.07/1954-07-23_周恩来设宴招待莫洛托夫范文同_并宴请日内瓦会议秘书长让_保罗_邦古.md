Title: Zhou Enlai Hosts a Banquet for Molotov and Phạm Văn Đồng and Hosts a Luncheon for Geneva Conference Secretary-General Jean Paul-Boncour
Author:
Date: 1954-07-23
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 21st: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, hosted a banquet at 8:00 PM on the 21st to entertain Vyacheslav Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, and Phạm Văn Đồng, Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
    The Soviet side attending the banquet included Kuznetsov, Vinogradov, Molochkov, Novikov, Fegin, Lavrishev, Chichikin, Ledovsky, and Kazansky.
    The Democratic Republic of Vietnam was represented by Phan Anh, Trần Công Tường, Tạ Quang Bửu, Đặng Thai Mai, and Hà Văn Lâu.
    The Chinese side accompanying them included Zhang Wentian, Li Kenong, Wang Bingnan, Lei Renmin, Shi Zhe, Feng Xuan, Qiao Guanhua, Chen Jiakang, Huan Xiang, Wang Zhuoru, and Lei Yingfu.
    The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 22nd: Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, hosted a luncheon at 1:00 PM on the 22nd for Jean Paul-Boncour, Secretary-General of the Geneva Conference (stage on the Indochina question), and his wife.

### 周恩来设宴招待莫洛托夫范文同  并宴请日内瓦会议秘书长让·保罗—邦古

1954-07-23
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来设宴招待莫洛托夫范文同
    并宴请日内瓦会议秘书长让·保罗—邦古
    【新华社日内瓦二十一日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，在二十一日晚间八时设宴招待苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部部长莫洛托夫和越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外交部部长范文同。
    苏联方面出席宴会的有库兹涅佐夫、维诺格拉多夫、莫洛奇科夫、诺维科夫、费金科、拉夫里谢夫、奇基奇科、列多夫斯基、卡赞斯基。
    越南民主共和国方面出席宴会的有潘英、陈公祥、谢光宝、邓性、何文楼。
    中国方面出席作陪的有张闻天、李克农、王炳南、雷任民、师哲、冯铉、乔冠华、陈家康、宦乡、王倬如、雷英夫。
    宴会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
    【新华社日内瓦二十二日电】中华人民共和国外交部部长周恩来在二十二日下午一时宴请日内瓦会议（讨论印度支那问题阶段）秘书长让·保罗—邦古及其夫人。
