Title: Grand Rally Held by People from All Walks of Life in Pyongyang: Head of the DPRK Delegation to China, Kim Ung-gi, Reports on Visit to China

Author:
Date: 1954-07-07
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 6th: A grand rally was held in Pyongyang on the 5th by people from all walks of life. Kim Ung-gi, head of the DPRK People's Delegation to China, reported on their visit to China at the meeting.

    The meeting was attended by: DPRK Deputy Prime Minister Park Ui-wan, Chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly Lee Young, Director of the Propaganda and Agitation Department of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea Ha Ang-cheon, Minister of Culture and Propaganda Heo Jeong-suk, Minister of Justice Hong Ki-ju, and other party and government leaders, representatives of various people's organizations, cultural and artistic figures, officers and soldiers of the Korean People's Army, and working people of Pyongyang.

    Also attending the meeting were personnel from the Chinese Embassy in North Korea and diplomatic officials from other People's Democratic countries stationed in North Korea.

    Kim Ung-gi began his report amidst warm applause. He described how the DPRK People's Delegation to China was warmly welcomed by the Chinese people, and introduced in detail the brilliant achievements the delegation saw in China's great socialist construction, and how the Chinese people continued to deepen the movement to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea to support the DPRK people's post-war reconstruction.

    Kim Ung-gi concluded by saying: The tremendous assistance given to the DPRK people by the Chinese people, together with the assistance from the Soviet Union and the people of other fraternal countries, infinitely inspires us in our struggle to rebuild our motherland after the war. We must overcome all difficulties, successfully complete the restoration and construction of the people's economy after the war, and strive for the peaceful reunification of our motherland in order to repay the assistance given to the DPRK people by the Soviet Union, China, and the people of other fraternal countries.

    After the meeting, the art troupe of the DPRK People's Delegation to China performed programs staged during their visit to China.

    The DPRK People's Delegation to China will soon depart to report on their visit to China to the people of the DPRK in various provinces.

### 平壤市各界人民举行盛大集会  朝鲜访华团团长金应基报告访问中国情形

1954-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

    平壤市各界人民举行盛大集会
    朝鲜访华团团长金应基报告访问中国情形
    【新华社平壤六日电】平壤市各界人民在五日举行盛大集会，朝鲜人民访华代表团团长金应基在会上报告了访问中国的情形。
    参加会议的有：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相朴义琓、朝鲜最高人民会议议长李英、朝鲜劳动党中央委员会宣传鼓动部部长河仰天、文化宣传相许贞琡、司法相洪箕畴等党、政首长和各人民团体代表、文化艺术界人士、朝鲜人民军将士和平壤市劳动人民。
    参加会议的还有我国驻朝鲜大使馆人员和其他人民民主国家驻朝鲜外交官员。
    金应基在热烈的掌声中开始报告，他叙述了朝鲜人民访华代表团受到中国人民热烈欢迎的情形，并详细地介绍了代表团在中国看到中国人民在伟大的社会主义建设中取得的辉煌成就，中国人民继续深入开展抗美援朝运动支援朝鲜人民战后恢复建设的情形。
    金应基最后说：中国人民给予朝鲜人民的巨大援助，和苏联及各兄弟国家人民的援助一起，无限地鼓舞着我们进行战后重建祖国的斗争。我们要克服一切困难，胜利完成战后人民经济的恢复建设，争取祖国的和平统一，以回答苏联、中国和各兄弟国家人民给予朝鲜人民的援助。
    会后，朝鲜人民访华代表团艺术团演出访问中国时上演的节目。
    朝鲜人民访华代表团即将出发到朝鲜各道向朝鲜人民报告访问中国的情形。
