Title: USSR Minister of Defense Issues Order Celebrating Soviet Navy Day
Author:
Date: 1954-07-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 25th: TASS reports: The Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union, Marshal of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, issued an order on the 25th celebrating Soviet Navy Day. The order stated: Comrades, naval soldiers, sergeants, junior officers, senior officers, and generals! Today, the Soviet Armed Forces, together with all the people of the Soviet Union, are celebrating Soviet Navy Day. On this Navy Day, I extend my congratulations to you and wish all naval personnel and all workers, engineers, staff, and designers in the shipbuilding industry greater success in developing and improving the new operational technology of the Soviet Navy and improving their combat readiness.

### 苏联国防部部长发布命令庆祝苏联海军节

1954-07-26
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联国防部部长发布命令庆祝苏联海军节
    【新华社二十五日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联国防部部长、苏联元帅布尔加宁，二十五日发布了庆祝苏联海军节的命令。命令说：海军战士、士官、尉官、校官和将军同志们！今天，苏联的武装部队和苏联全体人民一道庆祝苏联海军节。在这海军节的时候，我谨向你们祝贺，并祝海军的全体人员和造船工业的全体工人、工程师、职员和设计者在发展和改进苏联海军的新的作战技术及提高他们的战斗准备方面，获得更大的成就。
