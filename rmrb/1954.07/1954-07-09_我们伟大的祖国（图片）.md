    Our Great Motherland
    Xilinhot is a newly emerging town on the Inner Mongolian grasslands, and it has become the political, economic, and cultural center of the Xilin Gol League. This is a newly built club that can accommodate more than a thousand people. Herdsmen from nearby areas often come here to watch performances by the Xilin Gol League Cultural Troupe and amateur mass theater groups.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    锡林浩特是内蒙草原上的一个新兴的市镇，这里已经成为锡林郭勒盟的政治、经济和文化的中心。这是不久以前新建的一所能容一千多人的俱乐部，附近各地的牧民经常来这里看锡林郭勒盟文工团和群众业余剧团的表演。
