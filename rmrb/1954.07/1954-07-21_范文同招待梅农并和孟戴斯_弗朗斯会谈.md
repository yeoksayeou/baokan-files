Title: Pham Van Dong Hosts Menon and Holds Talks with Mendès France
Author:
Date: 1954-07-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 19th: Pham Van Dong, Vice Premier and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, hosted a luncheon for Indian Representative to the United Nations, Menon, on the 19th.

The banquet took place in a harmonious atmosphere.

At 10:30 PM on the same day, Pham Van Dong and French Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Mendès France, held talks.

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 19th: Pham Van Dong, Vice Premier and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, met with approximately thirty French journalists at 5:30 PM on the 19th. It is reported that Pham Van Dong's talk to the journalists concerned issues related to the Geneva Conference, especially the issue of Vietnamese unification and the relationship between Vietnam and France; Pham Van Dong also answered questions raised by the journalists.

### 范文同招待梅农并和孟戴斯—弗朗斯会谈

1954-07-21
第1版()
专栏：

    范文同招待梅农并和孟戴斯—弗朗斯会谈
      同日接见法国记者谈有关日内瓦会议的问题
    【新华社日内瓦十九日电】越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外交部部长范文同在十九日中午设宴招待印度驻联合国代表梅农。
    宴会在和谐的气氛中进行。
    同日晚十时半，范文同和法国总理兼外交部部长孟戴斯—弗朗斯会谈。
    【新华社日内瓦十九日电】越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外交部部长范文同在十九日下午五时半接见约三十名法国记者。据悉，范文同对记者们的谈话内容是有关日内瓦会议的问题，特别是有关越南统一的问题和越南与法国的关系问题；范文同并回答了记者们提出的问题。
