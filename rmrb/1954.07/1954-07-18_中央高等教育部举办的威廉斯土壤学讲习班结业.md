Title: The Williams Soil Science Workshop Held by the Central Higher Education Department Concludes

Author:
Date: 1954-07-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

    The Williams Soil Science Workshop held by the Central Higher Education Department concluded on the 15th. The workshop began on March 15th of this year, with more than 200 students from agricultural colleges across the country, including professors, lecturers, teaching assistants, soil science workers from agricultural organizations, and soil science experts. Under the direct guidance of Soviet experts, after four months of study, they clarified the boundaries between the old and new soil science, recognized the importance of applying dialectical materialism in soil science, and studied and discussed the teaching plans and syllabuses for soil science in universities.
    At the concluding summary meeting of the workshop, Zeng Zhaolun, Vice Minister of the Central People's Government's Ministry of Higher Education, and Sun Xiaocun, President of Beijing Agricultural University, spoke successively, urging the students to gradually implement the learned theories into teaching work, scientific research, and agricultural production, so as to lay a good foundation for the new development of soil science in our country. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中央高等教育部举办的威廉斯土壤学讲习班结业

1954-07-18
第3版()
专栏：

    中央高等教育部举办的威廉斯土壤学讲习班结业
    中央人民政府高等教育部举办的威廉斯土壤学讲习班在十五日结业了。讲习班是今年三月十五日开办的，全班二百多学员，都是来自全国各地农学院的教授、讲师、助教、农业机关的土壤科学工作者和土壤学专家，他们在苏联专家直接的指导下，经过四个月的学习，划清了新旧土壤学的界限，认识到在土壤学中运用唯物辩证法的重要意义，并且研究和讨论了高等学校土壤学的教学计划、教学大纲。
    在讲习班的结业总结大会上，中央人民政府高等教育部副部长曾昭抡、北京农业大学校长孙晓村相继发言，要求学员们把学得的理论逐步贯彻到教学工作、科学研究和农业生产中去，为我国土壤科学新的发展打下良好的基础。（新华社）
