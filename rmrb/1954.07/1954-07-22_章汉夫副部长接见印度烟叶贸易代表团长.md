Title: Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu Receives Head of Indian Tobacco Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-07-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 21st. Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu of the Central People's Government received K. Raghuramaiah, Head of the Indian Tobacco Trade Delegation, on the afternoon of July 21st. He was accompanied by the Indian Ambassador to China, N. Raghavan. Present at the reception was He Ying, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 章汉夫副部长接见印度烟叶贸易代表团长

1954-07-22
第3版()
专栏：

    章汉夫副部长接见印度烟叶贸易代表团长
    【新华社二十一日讯】我中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫在七月二十一日下午接见印度烟叶贸易代表团团长克·拉古拉迈雅。陪同前往的是印度驻我国大使赖嘉文。接见时在座的有外交部亚洲司副司长何英。
