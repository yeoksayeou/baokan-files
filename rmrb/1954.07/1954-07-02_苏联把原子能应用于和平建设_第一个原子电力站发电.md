Title: The Soviet Union Applies Atomic Energy to Peaceful Construction: First Atomic Power Station Generates Electricity
Author:
Date: 1954-07-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 30th, TASS dispatch: The Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union issued the following communique regarding the start of electricity generation by the Soviet Union's first industrial atomic power station:

    Soviet scientists and engineers have victoriously completed the design and construction of the Soviet Union's first industrial atomic power station, which has a usable generating capacity of five thousand kilowatts.

    On June 27, 1954, this atomic power station began generating electricity, transmitting the first stream of current to industry and agriculture in nearby areas.

    This is the first time that atomic energy—the fission of uranium nuclei—has been used instead of burning coal or other fuels to operate industrial turbines.

    The start of electricity generation by the atomic power station represents a major step forward in the application of atomic energy for peaceful purposes.

    Soviet scientists and engineers are designing industrial atomic power stations with generating capacities of 50,000 to 100,000 kilowatts.

### 苏联把原子能应用于和平建设  第一个原子电力站发电

1954-07-02
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联把原子能应用于和平建设
    第一个原子电力站发电
    【新华社一日讯】塔斯社莫斯科六月三十日讯：苏联部长会议发表关于苏联第一个原子能工业电力站开始发电的公报如下：
    苏联科学家和工程师已胜利地完成了苏联第一个原子能工业电力站的设计和建筑工作，这个电力站可使用的发电能力为五千瓩。
    一九五四年六月二十七日这个原子电力站开始发电，把第一股电流输送给附近地区的工业和农业。
    不烧煤或其他燃料而用原子能——铀原子核的分裂——来开动工业用的涡轮机，这还是第一次。
    由于原子电力站的开始发电，在把原子能应用于和平目的方面就前进了一大步。
    苏联科学家和工程师正在设计发电能力为五万到十万瓩的原子能工业电力站的工程。
