Title: Our Embassy in Indonesia Holds Reception Welcoming Our Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-07-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 1st. The Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Republic of Indonesia held a reception on the evening of June 29 to welcome the Chinese Trade Delegation.

Invited to attend the reception were: Prime Minister of the Republic of Indonesia Ali Sastroamidjojo, Minister of Agriculture Sadjarwo, Minister of Social Affairs Soeroso, Minister of Finance Ong Eng Die, members of the Indonesian delegation participating in the trade negotiations, and prominent figures from Indonesian society.

Also invited to attend the reception were personnel from various countries' diplomatic missions in Indonesia.

### 我驻印尼大使馆举行招待会  欢迎我国贸易代表团

1954-07-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我驻印尼大使馆举行招待会
    欢迎我国贸易代表团
    【新华社雅加达一日电】中华人民共和国驻印尼共和国大使馆在六月二十九日晚举行招待会，欢迎中国贸易代表团。
    应邀出席招待会的有：印尼共和国总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约、农业部部长萨茄乌、社会事务部部长苏罗梭、财政部部长王永利、参加贸易谈判的印尼代表团团员，以及印尼社会名流。
    应邀出席招待会的还有各国驻印尼外交使团人员。
