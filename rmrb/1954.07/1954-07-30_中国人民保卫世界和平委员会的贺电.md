Title: Congratulatory Telegram from the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace
Author:
Date: 1954-07-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 29th: The All-India Peace Council is scheduled to hold a meeting in Calcutta from July 30th to August 1st to discuss strengthening Asian security and unity, as well as opposing weapons of mass destruction. The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace sent a telegram of congratulations. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To the All-India Peace Council: The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace extends warm congratulations to your meeting scheduled to be held in Calcutta from July 30th to August 1st.

We believe that the convening of your conference and the issues to be discussed are very timely. In recent years, the positive development of the Indian peace movement and the various peace appeals issued by your council have greatly contributed to the peace movement in Asia and the world. We believe that this meeting will further enhance its role. We, the Chinese people, are willing to work with the Indian people to strive together for the current peace movement, especially for the maintenance of lasting peace in Asia and the world.

We wish the conference a successful outcome.

The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, July 29, 1954

### 中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的贺电

1954-07-30
第4版()
专栏：

    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的贺电
    【新华社二十九日讯】全印和平理事会定在七月三十日到八月一日在加尔各答举行会议，讨论加强亚洲安全与团结以及反对大规模毁灭性武器等问题。中国人民保卫世界和平委员会特去电祝贺。电文如下：
    全印和平理事会：贵会定于七月三十日至八月一日在加尔各答举行会议，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会谨向贵会会议致以热烈的祝贺。
    我们认为贵会此次会议的召开和所要讨论的问题，非常切合时宜，年来印度和平运动的良好开展和贵会所发布的各项和平号召，对亚洲和全世界和平运动贡献甚大。我们相信贵会此次会议必能更进一步地发挥其作用。我们中国人民愿与印度人民一起，为当今的和平运动特别为维护亚洲以及全世界的持久和平而共同奋斗。
    谨祝会议胜利成功。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会  一九五四年七月二十九日
