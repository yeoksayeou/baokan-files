Title: Lao Foreign Minister Sananikone Meets with Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai
Author: 
Date: 1954-07-19
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 18th. Lao Kingdom Foreign Minister Phoui Sananikone met with People's Republic of China Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai at 11:30 AM this morning, holding talks for 1 hour and 15 minutes.

### 老挝外交大臣萨纳尼空访晤周恩来外长

1954-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

    老挝外交大臣萨纳尼空访晤周恩来外长
    【新华社日内瓦十八日电】老挝王国外交大臣冯·萨纳尼空十八日上午十一时三十分访晤中华人民共和国外交部部长周恩来，会谈一小时十五分。
