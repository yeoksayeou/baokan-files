Title: PLA Troops Stationed in Xiamen Shoot Down a Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Fighter Plane
Author:
Date: 1954-07-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th -- PLA troops stationed in Xiamen shot down an American-made Chiang Kai-shek F-47 fighter plane that intruded into the airspace over Xiamen on the morning of the 14th. The downed enemy plane crashed into the sea channel between Greater and Lesser Kinmen Islands.

### 解放军驻厦门部队  击落蒋匪战斗机一架

1954-07-01
第1版()
专栏：

    解放军驻厦门部队
    击落蒋匪战斗机一架
    【新华社三十日讯】中国人民解放军驻厦门的部队在十四日早晨击落窜入我厦门上空的美造蒋匪F—四七型战斗机一架。被击落的敌机坠入大、小金门岛中间的海峡中。
