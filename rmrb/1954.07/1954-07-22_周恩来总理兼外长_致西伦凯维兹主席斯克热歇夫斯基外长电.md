Title: Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's Telegram to Chairman Cyrankiewicz and Foreign Minister Skrzeszewski
Author:
Date: 1954-07-22
Page: 1

Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai
Telegram to Chairman Cyrankiewicz and Foreign Minister Skrzeszewski

To Comrade Józef Cyrankiewicz, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic:
To Comrade Stanisław Skrzeszewski, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Polish People's Republic:

On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the Polish People's Republic, please accept my heartfelt congratulations. I wish the Polish people even greater achievements in the cause of socialist construction, and I wish the unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Polish people will be further consolidated and developed in the cause of defending world peace.

                                  Zhou Enlai, July 20, 1954

### 周恩来总理兼外长  致西伦凯维兹主席斯克热歇夫斯基外长电

1954-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理兼外长
    致西伦凯维兹主席斯克热歇夫斯基外长电波兰人民共和国部长会议主席约瑟夫·西伦凯维兹同志：波兰人民共和国外交部部长斯坦尼斯瓦夫·斯克热歇夫斯基同志：
    值此波兰人民共和国成立十周年之际，请接受我衷心的祝贺。祝波兰人民在社会主义建设事业上获得更大的成就，并祝中波两国人民之间牢不可破的友谊在保卫世界和平事业中日益巩固与发展。
                                  周恩来一九五四年七月二十日
