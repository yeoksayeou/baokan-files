Title: British Youth Delegation Publishes Observations on China Visit, Praising Our Country's Peaceful Construction
Author:
Date: 1954-07-20
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, London, July 15th) TASS London, July 15th: The British Youth Delegation, composed of representatives from various British religious groups, political groups, and trade unions, held a press conference in London on the 14th after returning from the People's Republic of China. The delegation stated that its members "received warm hospitality everywhere" in China, and added, "What impressed us most was the kindness, generosity, and sincere friendship of the Chinese people. In Beijing, Nanjing, Shanghai, and other cities, we saw examples of large-scale industrial, agricultural, and cultural development nationwide under the leadership of the People's Government."

The statement expressed the hope that this visit by the delegation would "open the way for establishing closer relations between Britain and China."

Members of the delegation described the scale of China's industrial and cultural construction and answered some questions raised by reporters.

### 英国青年代表团发表访华观感  赞扬我国的和平建设

1954-07-20
第4版()
专栏：

    英国青年代表团发表访华观感
    赞扬我国的和平建设
   【新华社十六日讯】塔斯社伦敦十五日讯：由英国各宗教团体、政治团体和工会团体的代表组成的英国青年代表团从中华人民共和国返国后，十四日在伦敦举行了记者招待会。代表团发表的声明说，代表团的团员在中国“到处都受到热烈的招待”，并说，“给我们印象最深的是中国人民的善良、大度和真挚的友情。在北京、南京、上海和其他各城市，我们看到了在人民政府领导下全国工业、农业和文化的大规模发展的事例。”
    声明表示，希望代表团的这一次访问“为确立英国和中国的更密切的关系打开道路”。
    代表团团员们叙述了中国工业建设和文化建设的规模，并答复了记者提出的一些问题。
