Title: "Pravda" Publishes Article by Borovsky Exposing the Crimes of American Monopoly Capital in Enslaving Guatemala

Author:

Date: 1954-07-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 3rd. TASS, Moscow, 1st: "Pravda" published an article by V. Borovsky, entitled: "American Monopoly Capital Enslaves Guatemala." In this article, the author describes in detail how the American rulers prepared the coup in Guatemala. Borovsky believes that this coup, carried out on the orders of American diplomats and agents, was an insidious plot against the Guatemalan people.

Borovsky said that the fact that American imperialists crudely interfered in Guatemala's internal affairs once again exposed them as the deadly enemies of the people's freedom and independence. This interference proves that the methods used by American rulers in Latin America are the same piratical methods they used in brutally and insidiously persecuting the Philippine people who defended their independence, shamelessly separating Panama from Colombia, and carrying out countless naval landings in Latin American countries. In the last fifty years, the United States has organized more than a hundred armed interventions and "rebellions" in Latin America.

Borovsky wrote that while American rulers cruelly crack down on Latin American regimes and governments they dislike, they also issue hypocritical statements of so-called "respect" for the independence of other countries, and even try to put on the cloak of defenders of independence. However, no matter what political tricks the American agents in Guatemala play, no matter what false statements their American masters make, world public opinion clearly knows that the American ruling clique is sacrificing Guatemala to the interests of monopoly capital, and preventing its people from arranging their own lives according to their wishes. The United States has once again exposed itself before the world as the strangler of the people's freedom and independence, the enemy of equal and peaceful international cooperation, and the enslaver of nations.

### “真理报”刊载波罗夫斯基的文章  揭露美国垄断资本奴役危地马拉的罪行

1954-07-04
第4版()
专栏：

    “真理报”刊载波罗夫斯基的文章
    揭露美国垄断资本奴役危地马拉的罪行
    【新华社三日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科一日讯：“真理报”刊载了维·波罗夫斯基的一篇文章，题目是：“美国垄断资本奴役危地马拉”。作者在这篇文章中详细地叙述了美国统治者怎样准备危地马拉的政变。波罗夫斯基认为这次根据美国外交官和特务的命令进行的政变是对危地马拉人民的阴险的暗算。
    波罗夫斯基说，美帝国主义者粗暴地干涉危地马拉的内政的事实又一次揭露了他们是人民的自由和独立的死对头。这次干涉证明，美国统治者在拉丁美洲使用的方法，就是他们在野蛮地和阴险地迫害保卫自己的独立的菲律宾人民、无耻地使巴拿马脱离哥伦比亚并在拉丁美洲国家实行无数次海军登陆中所使用的海盗式方法。在最近五十年中，美国在拉丁美洲组织了一百多次武装干涉和“叛变”。
    波罗夫斯基写道，美国统治者一方面残酷地打击他们所不喜欢的拉丁美洲政权和政府，另一方面又发表所谓“尊重”其他国家的独立的伪善声明，甚至想披上独立的维护者的外衣。但是，不管美国在危地马拉的代理人耍什么政治花样，不管他们的美国主子发表什么假声明，世界舆论清楚地知道，美国统治集团是为了垄断资本的利益而使危地马拉浸沉在血泊之中，使它的人民不能按照他们的愿望来安排自己的生活。美国又一次在全世界面前暴露了它是人民的自由和独立的扼杀者，是平等和和平的国际合作的敌人和民族奴役者。
