Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-07-15
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Single-edition copies of the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China, printed in various ethnic minority languages, have been distributed in various ethnic minority areas. The picture shows Enhe (left), a cadre of the Eastern Inner Mongolia Regional Committee of the Communist Party of China, explaining the Mongolian version of the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China to Hu Helete (right), the chairman of the Hu Helete Livestock Production Cooperative, and Sidebu (center), the vice-chairman.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    用各种民族文字排印的中华人民共和国宪法草案单行本，已经在各少数民族地区发行。图为中国共产党内蒙古东部区党委干部恩和（左）向胡和勒泰畜牧业生产合作社社长胡和勒泰（右）和副社长斯德布（中）讲解蒙文中华人民共和国宪法草案。
