Title: American-Chiang Kai-shek Gangsters Again Hijack British Merchant Ship
Author:
Date: 1954-07-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, July 18th—Under the instigation of the American authorities, the remnant bandits of the Chiang Kai-shek clique in Taiwan are stepping up their piratical activities of intercepting merchant ships on the high seas, endangering the international navigation safety and the interests of international trade of various countries. According to an Associated Press report, the British merchant ship "Hubei" was illegally intercepted by two American-Chiang Kai-shek bandit warships on the Taiwan Strait. The two bandit warships chased the "Hubei" for as long as three hours. One of the bandit ships checked the manifest and the cargo being carried. The other bandit ship damaged the bow of the "Hubei" during the chase.

### 美蒋匪帮又拦劫英国商船

1954-07-19
第4版()
专栏：

    美蒋匪帮又拦劫英国商船
    【新华社十八日讯】在美国当局指使下，台湾国民党残余匪帮正加紧在公海上进行拦劫商船的海盗活动，危害各国的国际航行安全和国际贸易的利益。据美联社消息透露，英国商船“湖北”号在台湾海峡海面上遭受美蒋匪帮两艘军舰的非法拦劫。两艘匪舰追逐“湖北”号达三小时之久。其中一艘匪舰盘查了商船上的载货清单和运载的货物。另一艘匪舰在追逐时并把“湖北”号的船头撞伤。
