Title: Malayan National Liberation Army Averaged Nearly 100 Attacks Per Month Last Year
Author:
Date: 1954-07-18
Page: 4

    Malayan National Liberation Army
    Averaged Nearly 100 Attacks Per Month Last Year
    [Xinhua News Agency, 16th dispatch] Singapore message: According to the 1953 annual report released by the British government of the Federation of Malaya on the 10th, in 1953, the Malayan National Liberation Army averaged 99 attacks per month. The Malayan National Liberation Army and government troops and police forces encountered each other an average of 117 times per month. The report admitted that in these battles, government troops and police were killed, wounded, or captured in large numbers.

### 马来亚民族解放军  去年平均每月出击近百次

1954-07-18
第4版()
专栏：

    马来亚民族解放军
    去年平均每月出击近百次
    【新华社十六日讯】新加坡消息：据马来亚联合邦英国政府十日发表的一九五三年常年报告书透露，在一九五三年内，马来亚民族解放军平均每月出击九十九次。马来亚民族解放军和政府的军队及警察接触的次数每月平均达一百一十七次。报告书承认，在这些战斗中，政府军队和警察被打死、打伤或俘掳多人。
