Title: At a Mass Rally of 100,000 People in Ajmer, Prime Minister Nehru Praised the Indochina Armistice Agreement
Author:
Date: 1954-07-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Delhi: According to the Indian News Bureau, Indian Prime Minister Nehru spoke at a mass rally of over 100,000 people in Ajmer on the 25th. He said that the Indochina Agreement recently signed in Geneva was a great victory for the world, saving mankind from the scourge of a possible atomic war and hydrogen weapon war. Nehru also pointed out that the Indochina Agreement placed a major responsibility on India. Nehru said: "It is an honor that the great powers of the world have shown trust in us and asked us to shoulder this major responsibility, which must be carried out with caution." At this time, the venue resounded with a storm of applause.

### 在亚日米尔十万人群众大会上  尼赫鲁总理赞扬印度支那停战协定

1954-07-28
第1版()
专栏：

    在亚日米尔十万人群众大会上
    尼赫鲁总理赞扬印度支那停战协定
   【新华社二十七日讯】塔斯社德里讯：印度新闻局消息，印度总理尼赫鲁二十五日在亚日米尔的十万余人的群众大会上演说。他说，最近在日内瓦签订的印度支那协定是全世界的巨大胜利，它挽救了人类免于遭到可能的原子战争和氢武器战争的祸害。尼赫鲁还指出，印度支那协定使印度承担了一项重大的责任。尼赫鲁说：“世界大国对我们表示信任，并要我们负担这一必须谨慎从事的、重大的责任，这是很荣幸的。”这时会场中响起了暴风雨般的掌声。
