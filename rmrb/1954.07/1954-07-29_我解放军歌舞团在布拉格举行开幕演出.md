Title: PLA Song and Dance Troupe Holds Opening Performance in Prague
Author:
Date: 1954-07-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe, invited to perform in Czechoslovakia, held its opening performance at the Smetana Theatre in Prague on the 26th.

Attending the opening ceremony were President of the Czechoslovak Republic, Zápotocký, First Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of National Defense Čepička, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Culture Kopecký, Chairman of the National Assembly Fierlinger, Foreign Minister David, members of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and ministers of various government departments, prominent Czechoslovak artists, and people from all walks of life in Prague.

Also attending the ceremony were Soviet Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Filyubin, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Czechoslovakia Li Qingquan and Deputy Military Attaché Hu Wei, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to Czechoslovakia Yang Yong-seop, and diplomatic envoys of other countries to Czechoslovakia.

Li Kenong, representative of the Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Geneva Conference and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, currently visiting Czechoslovakia, also attended the opening ceremony.

At the ceremony, the Director of the Czechoslovak Army Theatre, People's Artist Brian, spoke first. He expressed the warmest welcome to the song and dance troupe on behalf of the Czechoslovak people, army, and literary and art workers. Brian also extended a warm welcome to Li Kenong for attending the opening ceremony.

Next, Chen Yi, head of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe and Minister of Culture of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government, delivered a speech. He paid tribute to the glorious Czechoslovak Communist Party, government, people, and President Zápotocký on behalf of the Chinese people and the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and congratulated the Czechoslovak people and their army on their achievements in socialist construction and the defense of world peace. He said that the friendship between the Chinese and Czech peoples has a long history, and especially in recent years, the friendship between the two peoples has further developed. The performance of the song and dance troupe in Czechoslovakia will further develop and consolidate the friendship between the two peoples.

Subsequently, the song and dance troupe began to perform. They performed many programs with national characteristics and reflecting the life of the people and troops in the new China, such as the Army Waist Drum, Tibetan Cavalry, Female Chorus, Marching Rest Dance, and Havoc in the Dragon Palace, which were warmly welcomed by the audience. The Czech songs sung in Czech and the Soviet songs sung in Russian by the song and dance troupe were particularly popular, and the performers repeatedly bowed to the audience. Finally, the performance ended in applause and cheers.

During and after the performance, representatives of the Czechoslovak army, representatives of literary and art workers, and representatives of the city of Prague presented flowers to the song and dance troupe. President Zápotocký presented the song and dance troupe with a laurel wreath, and Čepička, Kopecký, David, and others presented flower baskets.

The song and dance troupe will perform in Prague and other major cities in Czechoslovakia.

### 我解放军歌舞团在布拉格举行开幕演出

1954-07-29
第4版()
专栏：

    我解放军歌舞团在布拉格举行开幕演出
    据新华社讯：应邀到捷克斯洛伐克作访问演出的中国人民解放军歌舞团，已于二十六日在布拉格斯麦塔那剧院举行开幕演出。
    参加开幕典礼的有捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统萨波托斯基，第一副总理兼国防部长契皮茨卡，副总理兼文化部长柯别茨基，国民议会主席费林格，外交部长戴维，捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会政治局委员和政府各部部长，捷克斯洛伐克著名艺术工作者和布拉格各界人士。
    参加典礼的还有苏联驻捷大使费留宾，中国驻捷大使馆临时代办李清泉和副武官胡畏，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻捷大使杨永笋，以及其他国家驻捷外交使节。
      现在捷克斯洛伐克访问的中华人民共和国出席日内瓦会议代表团代表、中央人民政府外交部副部长李克农也参加了开幕典礼。
    典礼上捷克斯洛伐克的军队剧院院长、人民艺术家布里安首先讲话。他代表捷克斯洛伐克人民、军队和文艺工作者向歌舞团表示最热烈的欢迎。布里安并特别对李克农的参加开幕典礼表示热烈的欢迎。
    接着，中国人民解放军歌舞团团长、中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部文化部部长陈沂致词。他代表中国人民和中国人民解放军向光荣的捷克斯洛伐克共产党、政府、人民和萨波托斯基总统致敬，并对捷克斯洛伐克人民及其军队在社会主义建设和保卫世界和平中获得的成就表示祝贺。他说，中捷两国人民的友谊有悠久的历史，尤其近几年来两国人民的友谊有了进一步的发展，歌舞团在捷克斯洛伐克的演出将使两国人民的友谊更加发展和巩固。
    随后，歌舞团开始表演。演出了许多具有民族风格和体现新中国人民和部队生活特点的节目，如陆军腰鼓、藏民骑兵队、女声合唱、行军休息舞、闹龙宫等，受到观众的热烈欢迎。歌舞团用捷克语唱的捷克歌曲和用俄语唱的苏联歌曲尤受欢迎，演唱者一再向观众谢幕。最后，表演在掌声和欢呼声中结束。
    表演当中和表演结束后，捷克斯洛伐克部队代表、文艺工作者代表和布拉格市代表纷纷向歌舞团献花。萨波托斯基总统并赠送给歌舞团以桂冠，契皮茨卡、柯别茨基、戴维等赠送了花篮。
    歌舞团将在布拉格和捷克斯洛伐克其他各大城市演出。
