Title: PLA Shoots Down and Damages Five Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Planes in Southeast Coastal Area

Author:

Date: 1954-07-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 16th: The People's Liberation Army's air force and ground troops stationed in the southeast coastal area recently shot down and damaged five American-made Chiang Kai-shek planes.

On the morning of the 6th, an air force unit shot down and damaged two American-made Chiang Kai-shek F-47 fighter planes that intruded into the airspace over eastern Zhejiang. The enemy plane that was shot down crashed into the sea east of Xiangshan County.

On the morning of the 3rd, troops stationed on Xiamen Island damaged an American-made Chiang Kai-shek F-38 reconnaissance plane that intruded into the airspace over Xiamen.

### 解放军在东南沿海击落击伤蒋匪飞机五架

1954-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

    解放军在东南沿海击落击伤蒋匪飞机五架
    【新华社十六日讯】中国人民解放军驻东南沿海的空军和地面部队最近击落、击伤了五架美造蒋机。
    六日早晨，空军某部击落和击伤了窜入我浙东上空的美造蒋机F—四七型战斗机各两架。被我击落的敌机坠入象山县东面海中。
    三日上午，驻厦门岛的部队击伤了窜入厦门上空的美造蒋机F—三八型侦察机一架。
