Title: Sino-Vietnamese Military Conference Continues to Discuss POW Issues; Vietnamese and French Sides Agree to Mutually Transfer a Batch of Sick and Wounded POWs on the 14th

Author:

Date: 1954-07-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, July 7th: The local Sino-Vietnamese military conference in Trung Gia (spelled as "中亦" in original text) held its fourth meeting on the morning of July 6th to discuss the issue of improving the living conditions of prisoners of war.

The delegations of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army and the General Headquarters of the French Union Forces in Indochina both acknowledged the need to raise this issue. After discussing the specific issues of how to conduct discussions on improving the living conditions of prisoners of war, the two delegations agreed to entrust a joint sub-committee to study this issue, and then the joint sub-committee will report to the conference at a meeting to be scheduled separately.

This joint sub-committee began its work on July 7th.

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, July 7th: In order to implement the resolution made by the local Sino-Vietnamese military conference in Trung Gia on July 5 regarding the transfer of sick and wounded prisoners of war, the chief representative of the delegation of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army notified the delegation of the General Headquarters of the French Union Forces in Indochina on July 6 that the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army had decided to transfer two hundred sick and wounded French prisoners of war on July 14. The transfer will take place in two locations: Viet Tri in Phu Tho province and Nga Son in Thanh Hoa province.

The delegation of the General Headquarters of the French Union Forces in Indochina also announced that the General Headquarters of the French Union Forces in Indochina would also transfer a number of sick and wounded prisoners of war from the Vietnam People's Army at the above two locations. Liaison officers from both sides will go to Viet Tri and Nga Son to inspect the local conditions in order to determine the method of transferring prisoners of war.

### 中亦军事会议继续讨论战俘问题  越法双方定十四日相互移交一批病伤战俘

1954-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

    中亦军事会议继续讨论战俘问题
    越法双方定十四日相互移交一批病伤战俘
    【新华社七日讯】越南通讯社北越七日讯：越南中亦当地军事会议在七月六日上午举行第四次会议，讨论改善战俘的生活条件问题。
    越南人民军总司令部代表团和印度支那法兰西联邦部队总司令部代表团都承认有必要提出这个问题。双方代表团在讨论了如何进行关于改善战俘生活条件问题的讨论的具体问题以后，同意委托一个联合小组委员会负责研究这一问题，然后再由联合小组委员会在一次会议上向会议提出报告，这次会议的时间另行规定。
    这个联合小组委员会从七月七日起开始工作。
    【新华社七日讯】越南通讯社北越七日讯：为履行中亦当地军事会议七月五日作出的关于移交病伤战俘的决议，越南人民军总司令部代表团首席代表在七月六日通知印度支那法兰西联邦部队总司令部代表团说，越南人民军总司令部决定在七月十四日移交二百名法方病伤战俘。移交将在两个地点进行：富寿省的越池和清化省的峨山。
    印度支那法兰西联邦部队总司令部代表团也宣布，印度支那法兰西联邦部队总司令部也将在上述两个地点移交若干名越南人民军病伤战俘。双方联络官将到越池和峨山去视察当地的情况，以便决定移交战俘的方式。
