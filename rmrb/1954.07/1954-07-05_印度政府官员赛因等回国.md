Title: Indian Government Officials Sain, etc., Return Home
Author: 
Date: 1954-07-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, July 4th: Sain, Chairman of the Central Water and Power Commission of the Indian Ministry of Water Resources and Power, and Rawo, Director of Design of the Commission, who were sent by the Indian government to China to inspect water conservancy, arrived in Guangzhou from Beijing on the 3rd, and left Guangzhou for home via Hong Kong on the 4th.

### 印度政府官员赛因等回国

1954-07-05
第4版()
专栏：

    印度政府官员赛因等回国
    【新华社广州四日电】印度政府派遣来我国考察水利的印度水利和电力部中央水利和电力委员会主席赛因和委员会设计主任劳氏，在三日由北京到达广州，四日离广州经香港回国。
