Title: China and Vietnam sign this year's goods exchange protocol
Author:
Date: 1954-07-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 8th

    China and Vietnam sign this year's goods exchange protocol
    [Xinhua News Agency, 8th] After friendly and mutually beneficial negotiations, the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Trade Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam signed the Protocol on Goods Exchange between China and Vietnam for 1954 and the Protocol on Small Border Trade between the two countries on July 7th in Beijing.

    According to the above-mentioned goods exchange protocol, the goods to be supplied to each other in 1954 will be greatly increased compared to 1953. The People's Republic of China will supply the Democratic Republic of Vietnam with goods such as cotton cloth, cotton yarn, machinery, transportation vehicles and equipment, telecommunications equipment, medicines, medical equipment, and paper. The Democratic Republic of Vietnam will supply the People's Republic of China with goods such as non-ferrous metals, tea, Amomum villosum, coffee beans, pepper, livestock, and leather.

    Xu Xuehan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government, signed the protocol on the Chinese side, and Hoang Van Hoan, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the People's Republic of China, signed on the Vietnamese side.

    Attending the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were: Ye Jizhuang, Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Central People's Government and Minister of Foreign Trade, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Huang Yaguang, Vice President of the People's Bank of China, Ding Guitang, Deputy Director of the General Administration of Customs of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, He Ying, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Bai Xiangyin, Deputy Director of the First Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, Zhan Wu, Director of the Foreign Business Bureau of the People's Bank of China, specialist Chen Dabang, and Yang Mian, Director of the Reception Department of the Ministry of Foreign Trade. On the Vietnamese side were: Nguyen Duy Li, Deputy Head of the Trade Delegation, Zhou Liang, First Secretary, Pham Binh, Second Secretary of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, and all members of the Trade Delegation.

    In order to meet the needs of small trade between the border people of China and Vietnam, the protocol between the People's Bank of China and the State Bank of Vietnam on border currency exchange was also signed on the same day.

### 中越签订今年货物交换议定书

1954-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

    中越签订今年货物交换议定书
    【新华社八日讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府对外贸易部和越南民主共和国贸易代表团在友好互助气氛中进行谈判后，七月七日在北京签订了中越关于一九五四年货物交换的议定书和关于中越两国边境小额贸易的议定书。
    根据上述货物交换议定书规定，在一九五四年相互供应的货物将比一九五三年大大增加。中华人民共和国将以棉布、棉纱、机器、交通工具和器材、电讯器材、医药、医疗器械、纸张等货物供给越南民主共和国，越南民主共和国将以有色金属、茶叶、砂仁、咖啡豆、胡椒、牲畜、皮革等货物供给中华人民共和国。
    在议定书上签字的，中国方面是中央人民政府对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒，越南方面是越南民主共和国驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使黄文欢。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有：中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会副主任兼对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国人民银行副行长黄亚光，对外贸易部海关总署副署长丁贵堂，外交部亚洲司副司长何英，对外贸易部第一局副局长白向银，中国人民银行国外业务局局长詹武、专员陈达邦，对外贸易部交际处处长杨勉。越南方面有：贸易代表团副团长阮维利，越南民主共和国驻华大使馆一等秘书周亮、二等秘书范平以及贸易代表团全体人员。
    为了适应中国和越南两国边境人民进行小额贸易的需要，中国人民银行和越南国家银行间关于边境货币兑换的议定书也在同日签字。
