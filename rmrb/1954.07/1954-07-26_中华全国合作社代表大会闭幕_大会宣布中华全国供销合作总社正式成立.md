Title: The National Congress of Cooperatives of the People's Republic of China Concludes; The Congress Announces the Formal Establishment of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives

Author:
Date: 1954-07-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The First National Congress of Cooperatives of the People's Republic of China victoriously concluded on the 25th. The congress announced the formal establishment of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives.

　　A total of 554 delegates attended today's meeting.

　　After the congress approved the list of candidates for members and alternate members of the National Committee of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives, directors of the Board of Directors, and supervisors of the Board of Supervisors, elections were held. The congress elected 97 members of the National Committee of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives (including one vacancy for a director of the Board of Directors as a matter of course), 31 alternate members, 17 directors of the Board of Directors (including one vacancy), and 7 supervisors of the Board of Supervisors. Among the members and alternate members of the National Committee are outstanding workers and model workers from cooperative organizations in various regions of the country, representatives of grassroots cooperatives, women representatives, and representatives of ethnic minorities such as Uyghur, Mongolian, Zhuang, Miao, and Korean. The Executive Chairman announced at the meeting that the leading and supervisory bodies of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives had been formally established, and the entire hall erupted in warm cheers and thunderous applause, with all the representatives standing to express their congratulations.

　　Cheng Zihua, Acting Director of the Provisional Council of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives, delivered a comprehensive speech at the congress in the morning regarding the discussion of the work report of the Provisional Council of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives. In a very warm and excited atmosphere, the congress unanimously adopted the resolution on supporting the draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China. In the morning, the congress unanimously passed the following five resolutions without any dissent or abstention: the resolution on the work report of the Provisional Council of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives, the resolution on the work report of the Provisional Supervisory Committee of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives, the resolution on approving the report on the balance sheet as of December 31, 1953, of the Provisional Council of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives, the resolution on adopting the charter of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives, and the resolution on the 1954 budget of the Supervisory Committee of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives.

　　In the afternoon, the closing ceremony of the congress was solemnly held.

　　The congress unanimously adopted the congratulatory telegram from the First National Congress of Cooperatives of the People's Republic of China to Chairman Mao Zedong, the congratulatory telegram to the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and the congratulatory telegram to the Chinese People's Volunteers.

　　The congress presented gifts and flowers to the cooperative delegations from the Soviet Union and various People's Democratic countries, thanking them for their warm congratulations and sharing of valuable experiences. Timofeev, the head of the Soviet cooperative delegation, delivered a speech of thanks on behalf of the cooperative delegations from various countries. Zhang Qilong then delivered the closing speech on behalf of the presidium.

　　After the solemn and majestic national anthem and The Internationale were played, the congress solemnly announced its closing.

### 中华全国合作社代表大会闭幕  大会宣布中华全国供销合作总社正式成立

1954-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

     中华全国合作社代表大会闭幕
    大会宣布中华全国供销合作总社正式成立
    据新华社讯：中华全国合作社第一次代表大会在二十五日胜利闭幕。大会宣布中华全国供销合作总社正式成立。
    今天到会的代表共五百五十四人。
    大会在通过了中华全国供销合作总社全国委员会委员、候补委员，理事会理事，监事会监事候选人名单后进行选举。大会共选出了中华全国供销合作总社全国委员会委员九十七名（内有理事会理事当然委员空额一名）、候补委员三十一名，理事会理事十七名（内空额一名），监事会监事七名。在全国委员会委员和候补委员中有：全国各地区合作社组织中的优秀工作者和模范工作者，基层社的代表，妇女代表及维吾尔族、蒙族、僮族、苗族、朝鲜族等少数民族代表。执行主席在大会上宣布了中华全国供销合作总社的领导机构和监察机构正式成立，全场响起了热烈的欢呼声和雷动的掌声，全体代表起立，表示庆贺。
    中华全国合作社联合总社临时理事会代主任程子华上午在大会上作了关于讨论全国合作总社临时理事会的工作报告的综合发言。大会在十分热烈的兴奋的气氛中，一致通过了关于拥护中华人民共和国宪法草案的决议。上午，大会无一异议、无一弃权地一致通过了下列五个决议：关于中华全国合作社联合总社临时理事会工作报告的决议，关于中华全国合作社联合总社临时监事会工作报告的决议，关于批准中华全国合作社联合总社临时理事会关于一九五三年十二月三十一日资产负债表报告的决议，关于通过中华全国供销合作总社章程的决议，关于中华全国供销合作总社监事会一九五四年度概算的决议等。
    下午，大会隆重地举行了闭幕式。
    大会一致通过了中华全国合作社第一次代表大会向毛泽东主席致敬电，向中国人民解放军致敬电和向中国人民志愿军致敬电。
    大会向苏联和各人民民主国家的合作社代表团赠礼献花，感谢他们的热诚祝贺和介绍宝贵经验。苏联合作社代表团团长吉莫费耶夫代表各国合作社代表团致谢词。继由张启龙代表主席团致闭幕词。
    在高奏庄严、雄壮的国歌和国际歌后，大会隆重宣布闭幕。
