Title: China Peace Committee Cables Vietnam Peace Committee
Author:
Date: 1954-07-28
Page: 2

China Peace Committee Cables Vietnam Peace Committee
To the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace:

The Geneva Conference has reached an agreement on the issue of the cessation of hostilities and restoration of peace in Indochina, which has delighted the people of the world. The China Peace Committee, on behalf of all the Chinese people, extends warm congratulations to your committee and, through your committee, to the people of Indochina who have fought heroically for eight years.

The restoration of peace in Indochina is the result of eight years of arduous struggle by the people of Indochina and the common efforts of the people of the world. This great victory not only brings peace to the people of Indochina but will also contribute to further easing the tense situation in Asia and the world. We, the Chinese people, will work tirelessly with the people of Indochina and the people of the world to strive for the thorough implementation of the agreement and to safeguard peace in Asia and the world.

China Peace Committee
July 26, 1954

### 我保卫和平委员会电越南保卫和平委员会

1954-07-28
第2版()
专栏：

    我保卫和平委员会电越南保卫和平委员会越南保卫世界和平委员会：
    日内瓦会议关于印度支那停战和恢复和平问题达成了协议，全世界人民为之欢欣鼓舞。中国人民保卫世界和平委员会谨代表全中国人民向贵会并通过贵会向八年来英勇斗争的印度支那人民致以热烈的庆贺。
    印度支那和平的恢复是印度支那人民八年艰苦奋斗得来的，也是全世界人民共同争取得来的。这一伟大的胜利，不仅给印度支那人民带来了和平，而且也将促成亚洲以及全世界紧张局势的进一步缓和。我们中国人民将与印度支那人民以及全世界人民一起，为争取协议的彻底实现并为维护亚洲与世界的和平而努力不懈。中国人民保卫世界和平委员会      一九五四年七月二十六日
