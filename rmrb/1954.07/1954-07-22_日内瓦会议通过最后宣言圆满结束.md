Title: Geneva Conference Successfully Concludes with the Adoption of Final Declaration
Author:
Date: 1954-07-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 21st: The Geneva Conference held its final plenary session from 3:00 PM to 5:20 PM on July 21st.

The conference adopted the final declaration and agreed that the two chairmen of the conference should invite India, Poland, and Canada to form an International Control Commission to oversee the implementation of the agreements on the cessation of hostilities in Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia.

The session on July 21st was chaired by British Foreign Secretary Anthony Eden. The Chairman first announced that the three agreements on the cessation of hostilities in Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia had been signed, including provisions for international supervision of the ceasefire. Before adopting the final declaration, the Chairman consulted the opinions of the delegations of the participating countries one by one. The delegations of France, the Kingdom of Laos, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the People's Republic of China, the United Kingdom, the Soviet Union, and the Kingdom of Cambodia all announced their full agreement with the final declaration. U.S. representative Smith stated that the United States was not prepared to participate in the final declaration, but he announced that the U.S. government, in accordance with Article 2, Paragraph 4 of the United Nations Charter, would not use force to undermine this declaration and the agreements on the cessation of hostilities in Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia, and would regard any violation of these agreements and the declaration as a threat to international security. Bao Dai Vietnam representative Tran Van Do announced that the Bao Dai government would not use force to undermine the ceasefire agreement and the final declaration, but groundlessly reserved its opinion on these documents.

Before the end of the conference, British Foreign Secretary Eden, Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov, Acting Foreign Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Pham Van Dong, Chinese Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai, Lao Foreign Minister Sananikone, Cambodian Foreign Minister Tep Phan, and French Foreign Minister Mendès France spoke successively, unanimously pointing out that the Geneva Conference had achieved significant success and expressed their willingness to work towards the implementation of the agreements reached. The foreign ministers emphasized the spirit of consultation demonstrated by the delegations at the Geneva Conference and expressed the hope that this spirit would continue to be carried forward.

The conference finally concluded successfully in an atmosphere of harmony.

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 21st: The agreements on the cessation of hostilities in Vietnam and Laos were signed at 3:30 AM on July 21st at the former League of Nations building. Xie Guangbao, representative of the General Command of the Vietnam People's Army, and De Lattre, representative of the General Command of the French Union Forces, signed the agreement on the cessation of hostilities in Vietnam on behalf of their respective commands. Subsequently, Xie Guangbao, representative of the Vietnamese volunteer personnel and the Laotian combat units, and De Lattre, representative of the Lao Royal Forces, signed the agreement on the cessation of hostilities in Laos on behalf of the belligerent parties.

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 21st: The agreement on the cessation of hostilities in Cambodia was signed at 12:30 PM on July 21st at the former League of Nations building. Xie Guangbao, representative of the Vietnamese volunteer personnel and the Khmer combat units, and Diao Long, representative of the Cambodian Royal Forces, signed the agreement.

Xinhua News Agency, July 21st: Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, July 21st: The subcommittee responsible for studying the implementation of the ceasefire held a meeting on the morning of July 20th. The subcommittee discussed the ways to implement the ceasefire. The work was carried out in a good atmosphere.

The liaison officer of the General Command of the Vietnam People's Army also held a meeting with the liaison officer of the French side to notify the French side's liaison officer of the transfer of sick and wounded prisoners of war of the French Union Forces in Viet Tri. The liaison officers of both sides discussed the issue of how to transfer the next batch of sick and wounded prisoners of war.

### 日内瓦会议通过最后宣言圆满结束

1954-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

    印度支那停战和恢复和平问题达成协议
    日内瓦会议通过最后宣言圆满结束
    越南、老挝、柬埔寨三国交战双方签订停止敌对行动协定
    越南当地双方军事会议小组委员会举行会议讨论执行停火的方式
    【新华社日内瓦二十一日电】日内瓦会议二十一日下午三时至五时二十分举行了最后一次全体会议。
    会议通过了最后宣言，并同意由会议的两位主席邀请印度、波兰、加拿大三国组成监察越南、老挝、柬埔寨的停止敌对行动的协定实施的国际监察委员会。
    二十一日会议主席由英国外交大臣艾登担任。主席首先宣布已经签订了越南、老挝、柬埔寨的三个停止敌对行动的协定，这些协定中并包括了停战的国际监察的规定。主席在通过最后宣言前，逐一征询了与会各国代表团的意见。法国、老挝王国、越南民主共和国、中华人民共和国、联合王国、苏联、柬埔寨王国的代表团都宣布完全同意最后宣言。美国代表史密斯声称美国不准备参加最后宣言，但他宣布美国政府将按照联合国宪章第二条第四款不使用武力破坏这一宣言和越南、老挝、柬埔寨的停止敌对行动的协定，同时对任何违反这些协定和宣言的行为认为是危害国际安全。保大越南代表陈文杜宣布保大政府不以武力破坏停战协定和最后宣言，但又毫无理由地保留它对这些文件的意见。
    在会议结束前，联合王国外交大臣艾登、苏联外长莫洛托夫、越南民主共和国代理外长范文同、中华人民共和国外长周恩来、老挝王国外交大臣萨纳尼空、柬埔寨王国外交大臣泰普潘以及法国外长孟戴斯—弗朗斯先后发言，一致指出日内瓦会议已获得重大成就，并愿为已达成的协议的实现而努力。外长们强调日内瓦会议各代表团所表现的协商的精神，并希望这种精神继续发扬下去。
    会议最后在和谐的空气中圆满结束。
    【新华社日内瓦二十一日电】越南和老挝停止敌对行动协定已于七月二十一日晨三时三十分在前国联大厦签字。越南人民军总司令部代表谢光宝和法兰西联邦部队总司令部代表戴尔特尔分别代表双方司令部，在越南停止敌对行动协定上签字。接着，越南志愿人员和寮国战斗单位代表谢光宝，老挝王国部队代表戴尔特尔分别代表交战双方，在老挝停止敌对行动协定上签字。
    【新华社日内瓦二十一日电】关于柬埔寨停止敌对行动协定已在七月二十一日十二时三十分在前国联大厦签字。在协定上签字的是越南志愿人员和高棉战斗单位代表谢光宝和柬埔寨王国部队代表刁隆。
    【新华社二十一日讯】越南通讯社北越二十一日讯：负责研究执行停火问题的当地军事会议的小组委员会，在七月二十日上午举行了会议。小组委员会讨论了执行停火的方式。工作是在良好的气氛中进行的。
    越南人民军总司令部的联络官也和法国方面的联络官举行了会议，通知法国方面联络官关于在越池移交法兰西联邦部队病、伤战俘事宜。双方联络官讨论了关于移交下一批病、伤战俘的方式问题。
