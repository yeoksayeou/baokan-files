Title: Argentine Medical Scientist Braunstein Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-07-20
Page: 4

    Argentine Medical Scientist Braunstein Arrives in Beijing
    (Xinhua News Agency, July 19th) Argentine medical scientist T. Braunstein and his wife, Ester Manasse, arrived in China for a visit on July 17th by plane, at the invitation of the China Peace Committee and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations With Foreign Countries, after attending the World Peace Council Special Session in Berlin. Tang Mingzhao, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department of the China Peace Committee, and Zhou Junliang, Section Chief of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations With Foreign Countries, were among those who welcomed them at the airport.

### 阿根廷医学家布朗斯坦抵京

1954-07-20
第4版()
专栏：

    阿根廷医学家布朗斯坦抵京
    【新华社十九日讯】阿根廷医学家特·布朗斯坦和他的夫人埃斯特·马纳赛在参加世界和平理事会柏林特别会议之后，应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和中国人民对外文化协会的邀请来我国访问。他们在十七日下午乘飞机抵达北京。前往机场欢迎的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会联络部副部长唐明照和中国人民对外文化协会处长周君亮等。
