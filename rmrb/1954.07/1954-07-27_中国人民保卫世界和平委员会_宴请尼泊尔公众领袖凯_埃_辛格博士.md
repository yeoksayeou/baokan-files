Title: Chinese People's Committee for World Peace Hosts Banquet for Nepalese Public Leader Dr. K. I. Singh
Author:
Date: 1954-07-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, July 26th: The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace hosted a banquet on the 25th for Nepalese public leader Dr. K. I. Singh. The banquet was presided over by Liao Chengzhi, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace. Attending as guests were Mr. Indra Kanwal and Mr. Mongkey from Nepal, Liu Ningyi, Standing Committee Member of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General, Chen Hansheng, Vice-President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Liu Zunqi, Director of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 中国人民保卫世界和平委员会  宴请尼泊尔公众领袖凯·埃·辛格博士

1954-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会
    宴请尼泊尔公众领袖凯·埃·辛格博士
      【新华社二十六日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会在二十五日宴请尼泊尔公众领袖凯·埃·辛格博士。宴会由中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志主持。出席作陪的有尼泊尔英得拉·康瓦及蒙琦先生，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员刘宁一，秘书长刘贯一，中国人民外交学会副会长陈翰笙，中国人民对外文化协会理事刘尊棋等。
