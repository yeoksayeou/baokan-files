Title: "Anshan Iron and Steel Technological Innovation Exhibition" Concludes in Beijing; All Exhibits to be Transported to Shanghai for Display

Author:

Date: 1954-07-17
Page: 2

The "Anshan Iron and Steel Technological Innovation Exhibition" in Beijing concluded on July 15th. The exhibition, which opened on April 15th, lasted for three months and was visited by more than 364,000 people from the capital and other regions. Visitors included workers, cadres, soldiers, and students from factories, mines, government agencies, military units, and schools in Beijing, as well as residents of Beijing. Renowned Chinese scientists, Soviet experts, and representatives of trade union delegations from various countries who were invited to China to attend the "May 1st" International Labor Day celebrations also visited the exhibition.

The "Anshan Iron and Steel Technological Innovation Exhibition" in Beijing received great attention from various central government departments. Leading comrades from many departments of the Central People's Government also visited the exhibition.

During the exhibition period, eight regional delegations from North China, Northwest China, Northeast China, Southwest China, Central South China, Inner Mongolia, and the cities of Beijing and Tianjin came to visit and study in twenty-one groups. A total of 5,745 representatives from various regions visited, including Party, government, trade union, and Communist Youth League cadres, model workers, advanced producers, skilled workers, and engineering technicians from industrial systems such as ferrous metallurgy, machinery, coal mining, electricity, chemicals, light industry, textiles, construction, railway, and highway transportation. During their visits and studies, the representatives carefully visited the exhibition, earnestly studied various experiences of technological innovation at Anshan Iron and Steel, and conducted in-depth discussions in light of the specific conditions of their regions and units, gaining a great deal. Many Party, government, and trade union cadres, after visiting and studying, further realized that enterprises must rely on the creativity and enthusiasm of the masses of workers to be well-run. They expressed that after returning to their posts, they would definitely support the rationalization proposals of the masses of workers. Many workers and engineering technicians, after visiting and studying, further realized that labor experience must be closely combined with scientific and technological knowledge in order to carry out inventions and creations more smoothly. Some model workers and advanced producers, after visiting, expressed that they would learn from the spirit of Anshan Iron and Steel's advanced producers who are not afraid of difficulties and study diligently, and actively innovate technology after returning. After the visits and studies, the delegations from various regions all made serious summaries; some delegations also formulated plans for conveying and studying the experiences of technological innovation at Anshan Iron and Steel.

Many government cadres, soldiers, and citizens who visited the "Anshan Iron and Steel Technological Innovation Exhibition" also received great education and encouragement. After visiting, many people wrote down their impressions in the opinion book, expressing their intention to learn from the creative spirit of Anshan Iron and Steel's technological innovators and do their jobs well.

Xinhua News Agency: After the "Anshan Iron and Steel Technological Innovation Exhibition" in Beijing concludes, all exhibits will be transported to Shanghai for display.

### “鞍钢技术革新展览会”在北京的展出结束  全部展品就要由北京运到上海展出

1954-07-17
第2版()
专栏：

    “鞍钢技术革新展览会”在北京的展出结束
    全部展品就要由北京运到上海展出
    “鞍钢技术革新展览会”在北京的展出，七月十五日已经结束。这个展览会自四月十五日开幕以来，共展出了三个月，首都和各地前来参观的有三十六万四千多人。参观的包括北京各厂矿、机关、部队、学校等单位的职工、干部、战士、学生和北京市的市民群众。参观展览会的，还有我国著名的科学家、苏联专家和应邀来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节观礼的各国工会代表团的代表。
    “鞍钢技术革新展览会”在北京展出，受到了中央各部门的重视。中央人民政府许多部门的负责同志也参观了展览会。
    展出期间，华北、西北、东北、西南、中南、内蒙古等地区和北京、天津二市共派出了八个地区代表团分二十一批前来参观和学习。各地前来参观的代表共有五千七百四十五人，其中包括五金冶炼、机械、煤矿、电业、化工、轻工业、纺织、建筑、铁路、公路运输等产业系统的党、行政、工会、青年团的干部，劳动模范，先进生产者，技术工人和工程技术人员。在参观和学习期间，代表们一面仔细地参观了展览会，认真地学习了鞍钢技术革新的各种经验；一面结合本地区和本单位的具体情况，深入进行讨论，得到了很大收获。许多党、行政和工会干部经过参观学习，进一步明确了要依靠职工群众的创造性和积极性来办好企业。他们表示回到工作岗位后，一定要很好地支持职工群众的合理化建议。许多工人和工程技术人员经过参观学习，进一步明确了劳动经验必须和科学技术知识紧密地结合起来，才能更顺利地进行发明和创造。有些劳动模范和先进生产者参观后，表示要学习鞍钢先进生产者的不怕困难、刻苦钻研的精神，回去后要积极革新技术。各地代表团在参观和学习结束以后，都认真地做了总结；有的代表团还作出了传达计划和学习鞍钢技术革新经验的计划。
    许多参观过“鞍钢技术革新展览会”的机关干部、战士和市民群众，也受到了很大的教育和鼓舞。许多人参观后，在意见簿上写下了自己的观感，表示要学习鞍钢技术革新者的创造精神，把自己担负的工作做好。
    “鞍钢技术革新展览会”在北京的展出结束后，全部展品就要运到上海展出。（新华社）
