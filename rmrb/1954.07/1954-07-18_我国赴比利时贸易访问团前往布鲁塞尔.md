Title: Chinese Trade Delegation to Belgium Travels to Brussels
Author:
Date: 1954-07-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 16th. The Chinese trade delegation to Belgium, consisting of seven members led by delegation head Shi Zhiang, departed for Brussels by plane at 6:30 PM on the 16th. Those who came to the airport to see them off included Lei Renmin, advisor to the Chinese delegation to the Geneva Conference and Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government, Cao Zhongshu, head of the Chinese trade delegation to the UK, and other Chinese trade personnel.

### 我国赴比利时贸易访问团前往布鲁塞尔

1954-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我国赴比利时贸易访问团前往布鲁塞尔
    【新华社日内瓦十六日电】中国赴比利时贸易访问团一行七人在团长石志昂率领下，于十六日下午六时半乘飞机赴布鲁塞尔。赴机场欢送的有：中国出席日内瓦会议代表团顾问、中央人民政府对外贸易部副部长雷任民，中国赴英贸易访问团团长曹中枢及其他中国贸易方面人员。
