Title: Chinese Trade Delegation Returns to Geneva from Britain
Author:
Date: 1954-07-16
Page: 1

(Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 14th) The Chinese trade delegation headed by Cao Zhongshu returned to Geneva by plane at 1:00 PM on the 14th after a seventeen-day visit to Britain. Lei Renmin, Huan Xiang, and Wang Zhuoru, advisors to the Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Geneva Conference, and Wen Pengjiu, Consul General of the People's Republic of China in Geneva, were at the airport to welcome them.

During its visit to Britain, the delegation had extensive contact with British industrialists and businessmen and members of parliament, visited sixteen factories, and exchanged views on Sino-British trade issues with twenty-five industrial and commercial organizations and fifty-six manufacturers and trading companies. More than 1,200 British industrialists and businessmen met with the delegation.

Cao Zhongshu, head of the delegation, held a press conference on the morning of the 13th, delivered a speech, and answered questions from more than 40 reporters on various issues related to Sino-British trade.

The delegation held a farewell reception on the eve of its departure, attended by more than 460 people. Among the guests were Members of Parliament Bevan, Wilson, Robson-Brown, Davies, and Silverman, officials from the British Board of Trade, the Ministry of Food, and the Ministry of Supply, as well as Flint, Vice Chairman of the British China Trade Committee, Kepping, Director-General of the Federation of British Industries, Sir Boyd-Orr, Chairman of the British International Trade Promotion Committee, Mrs. Robinson, Vice Chairman, and important figures from British industrial, commercial, financial, and journalistic circles. They generally expressed a strong desire to develop Sino-British trade. British public opinion believes that the delegation's visit to Britain has greatly benefited the promotion of Sino-British trade.

### 我国贸易访问团由英国回到日内瓦

1954-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我国贸易访问团由英国回到日内瓦
    【新华社日内瓦十四日电】以曹中枢为首的中国贸易访问团在英国作了十七天的访问之后已于十四日下午一时乘飞机返抵日内瓦。到机场欢迎的有，出席日内瓦会议的中华人民共和国代表团顾问雷任民、宦乡、王倬如以及中华人民共和国驻日内瓦总领事温朋久等。
    访问团在访问英国期间曾和英国工商界人士及议会议员广泛接触，参观了十六个工厂并分别和二十五个工商团体及五十六个制造业者与商行就中英贸易问题交换了意见。英国工商界人士和访问团会见者达一千二百余人。
    访问团团长曹中枢在十三日上午举行了记者招待会，发表谈话并向与会记者四十余人解答了有关中英贸易的各项问题。
    访问团在临别前夕举行了酒会，到会者达四百六十余人。来宾中有比万、威尔逊、罗布逊—布朗、戴维斯、锡尔弗曼等议会议员，英国贸易部、粮食部与供应部官员以及英国中英贸易委员会副主席弗林特、英国工业联合会总干事基平、英国国际贸易促进委员会主席波伊德—奥尔爵士、副主席罗宾逊夫人以及英国工商界、金融界、新闻界重要人士。他们普遍表示对开展中英贸易的热烈愿望。英国舆论认为访问团此次访英对促进中英贸易有很大裨益。
