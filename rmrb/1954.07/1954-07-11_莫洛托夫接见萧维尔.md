Title: Molotov Receives Chauvel
Author:
Date: 1954-07-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 9th: Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov received Chauvel, a representative of the French delegation to the Geneva Conference, on July 9th and held talks with him regarding the conference.

### 莫洛托夫接见萧维尔

1954-07-11
第4版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫接见萧维尔
    【新华社日内瓦九日电】苏联外交部部长莫洛托夫在七月九日接见了出席日内瓦会议的法国代表团代表萧维尔，和他进行了关于会议的谈话。
