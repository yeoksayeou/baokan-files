Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-07-03
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, July 2nd: The delegation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association headquarters, which went to the Soviet Union to attend the May Day celebrations, returned to Beijing by train on the afternoon of July 2nd, led by the head of the delegation, Qian Junrui, after completing the summary of their visit to the Soviet Union in Harbin.
    Welcoming them at the station were: Liao Jingtian and Li Zhanwu, Deputy Secretary-Generals of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association headquarters; Gao Zhendong, Deputy Secretary-General of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Fan Changjiang, Deputy Secretary-General of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government's State Council; Chen Zhongjing, Deputy Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Liaison; and figures from the cultural, artistic, and scientific fields, including Hua Junwu, Sun Shen, Wu Xue, Li Chao, and Cao Richang.
    Also present at the station to welcome them were Soviet Embassy Counselor S.G. Skachkov, Head of the Consular Section of the Soviet Embassy in China, Romanin, and representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Sokolov.
    Some members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association headquarters delegation to the Soviet Union, including Yang Hansheng, Ke Zhongping, Lü Ji, Wang Chaowen, Zhou Xiaoyan, and Zhang Guangnian, joined China's cultural delegation to Poland in early June and are still abroad. Delegation members Zhu Qiwen and Wang Yuechen attended the International Association of Democratic Lawyers Council meeting held in Leipzig, German Democratic Republic, from June 5th to 7th and returned to Beijing earlier at the end of June.

### 中苏友协代表团回到北京

1954-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友协代表团回到北京
    【新华社二日讯】中苏友好协会总会赴苏联参加“五一”节观礼代表团在哈尔滨完成了赴苏访问的总结工作以后，七月二日下午在团长钱俊瑞率领下乘火车回到北京。
    在车站欢迎的有：中苏友好协会总会副总干事廖经天、李沾吾，北京市中苏友好协会副总干事高振东，中央人民政府政务院文教委员会副秘书长范长江，对外文化联络事务局副局长陈忠经以及文化艺术和科学界人士华君武、孙慎、吴雪、李超、曹日昌等。
    苏联驻我国大使馆参赞司高磋、苏联驻我国大使馆领事处处长罗满宁和苏联对外文化协会代表索柯洛夫等也到车站欢迎。
    中苏友好协会总会赴苏代表团中阳翰笙、柯仲平、吕骥、王朝闻、周小燕、张光年等部分团员在六月上旬参加了我国赴波兰的文化代表团，现尚在国外。代表团团员朱其文、王悦尘参加了六月五日至七日在德意志民主共和国在来比锡召开的国际民主法律工作者协会理事会会议后，已在六月底先期回到北京。
