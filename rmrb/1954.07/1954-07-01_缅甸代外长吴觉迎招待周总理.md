Title: Burmese Acting Foreign Minister U Kyaw Nyein Hosts Premier Zhou

Author: 
Date: 1954-07-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, June 30th: Burmese Acting Foreign Minister U Kyaw Nyein held a reception at the Rangoon City Hall at 6:00 PM on June 29th to welcome Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China.

Among those invited to the reception were: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu, Chief Justice of the Supreme Court U Thein Maung, Judge U Min Thein, Minister of Land Nationalization Tekin Tin, Minister of Commerce Tekin Tha Kin, and members of parliament and parliamentary secretaries of various government ministries.

Yao Zhongming, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Burma, was also invited to attend the reception.

Also invited to attend the reception were diplomatic envoys from various countries to Burma: Pakistani Ambassador Sultanuddin Ahmed, Indian Ambassador K.K. Chettur, British Ambassador Paul Henry Gore-Booth, Ceylonese Minister Vilupillai Coomaraswamy, Israeli Minister David Hacohen, Soviet Chargé d'affaires Semyon P. Dzioukarev, Indonesian Chargé d'affaires T.M. Habsjah, Dutch Chargé d'affaires Joseph Verশিং and the Czechoslovak Consul General, among others.

A total of more than 300 people attended the reception.

The reception took place in a friendly atmosphere.

When Premier Zhou Enlai arrived at the reception and stepped out of the car to enter the City Hall, and when he left the City Hall after the reception, he was warmly applauded and cheered by nearly a thousand people standing outside the gate.

### 缅甸代外长吴觉迎招待周总理

1954-07-01
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸代外长吴觉迎招待周总理
    【新华社仰光三十日电】缅甸外交部代理部长吴觉迎六月二十九日下午六时在仰光市政厅举行招待会，欢迎中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来。
    应邀参加招待会的有：缅甸总理吴努，最高法院首席法官吴登貌，法官吴敏登，土地国有化部部长德钦阵，商业部部长德钦达庆以及国会议员和政府各部的国会秘书等。
    中华人民共和国驻缅甸大使姚仲明也应邀出席招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的还有各国驻缅甸外交使节：巴基斯坦大使苏丹努丁·艾哈迈德，印度大使克·克·契图尔，英国大使保罗·亨利·戈尔—布斯，锡兰公使维卢皮莱·科马拉斯瓦米，以色列公使戴维·哈科汉，苏联代办谢米昂·普·久卡列夫，印尼代办特·麦·哈布斯雅，荷兰代办约瑟夫·韦尔辛和捷克斯洛伐克总领事等。
    出席招待会者共计三百多人。
    招待会在友好空气中进行。
    当周恩来总理出席招待会下车步入市政厅时和在招待会完毕走出市政厅门口时，受到站立门外近千群众的热烈鼓掌和欢呼。
