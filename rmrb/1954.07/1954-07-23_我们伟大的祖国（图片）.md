    Our Great Motherland
    The Dalian Locomotive and Rolling Stock Technical School has eleven specialty classes dedicated to training technical workers for manufacturing locomotives and rolling stock. In addition to the eight on-campus practice factories, the workshops of the Dalian Locomotive and Rolling Stock Manufacturing Factory are also available for student internships. This is a newly made locomotive model. The teacher is explaining its performance and manufacturing process to the students.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-07-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    大连机车车辆技工学校设有十一个专业班，专门培养制造机车车辆的技术工人。除校内设有八个实习厂外，大连机车车辆制造工厂各车间亦可供学生实习。这是新制的机车模型。教师正在为学生讲解它的性能和制造过程。
