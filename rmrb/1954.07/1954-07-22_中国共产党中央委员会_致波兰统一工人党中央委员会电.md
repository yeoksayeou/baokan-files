Title: Telegram from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party
Author:
Date: 1954-07-22
Page: 1

To the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party,
To Comrade Bolesław Bierut,

    On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of Poland, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm fraternal congratulations to the Polish United Workers' Party, the Polish people, and to you.

    In the past ten years, under the leadership of the Polish United Workers' Party, the Polish people have victoriously carried out democratic reforms and socialist transformations, making Poland an advanced socialist industrial country. The Chinese people are delighted by the brilliant achievements of the Polish people.

    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China wishes the Polish United Workers' Party further victories in leading the Polish people in the cause of socialist construction, and in developing agriculture and light industry to continuously improve the people's livelihood.

              The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, July 20, 1954, in Beijing

### 中国共产党中央委员会  致波兰统一工人党中央委员会电

1954-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

    中国共产党中央委员会
    致波兰统一工人党中央委员会电波兰统一工人党中央委员会波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志：
    值此波兰人民共和国成立十周年之际，中国共产党中央委员会谨向波兰统一工人党、波兰人民和您致以热烈的兄弟的祝贺。
    过去十年，波兰人民在波兰统一工人党的领导下，胜利地进行了民主改革和社会主义改造，使波兰成为先进的社会主义工业国。中国人民为波兰人民辉煌的成就而欢欣鼓舞。
    中国共产党中央委员会祝波兰统一工人党在领导波兰人民进行社会主义建设的事业中、在发展农业和轻工业以不断改善人民生活方面取得进一步的胜利。
              中国共产党中央委员会一九五四年七月二十日于北京
