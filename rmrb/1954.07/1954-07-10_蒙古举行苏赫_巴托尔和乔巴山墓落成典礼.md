Title: Tomb Dedication Ceremony for Sukhe-Bator and Choibalsan Held in Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1954-07-10
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator: A dedication ceremony for the tombs of Sukhe-Bator and Choibalsan was held in Ulan Bator on the 8th.

　　A grand mass meeting was held at Sukhe-Bator Square at 4 p.m. to commemorate the completion of the mausoleum. Damba, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, delivered the opening speech at the meeting. Prime Minister Tsedenbal, old guerilla fighter and herdsman Isodnomdarza, worker Sansaladorje, and representatives of the army and Mongolian intellectuals spoke at the meeting.

　　After the mass meeting, representatives of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the People's Republic of Mongolia, the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Mongolia, the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, and various mass organizations laid wreaths at the tombs of Sukhe-Bator and Choibalsan amidst the sound of salutes. The Soviet Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the People's Republic of Mongolia, Pisarev, and the diplomatic envoys of the various People's Democratic countries also laid wreaths at the tombs.

### 蒙古举行苏赫—巴托尔和乔巴山墓落成典礼

1954-07-10
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古举行苏赫—巴托尔和乔巴山墓落成典礼
    【新华社九日讯】据塔斯社乌兰巴托讯：八日在乌兰巴托举行了苏赫—巴托尔和乔巴山墓落成典礼。
    下午四时在苏赫—巴托尔广场举行盛大的群众大会，纪念陵墓的落成。群众大会由蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴致开幕词。总理泽登巴尔，老游击队员、牧民伊索德诺姆达尔扎，工人桑萨拉多日以及军队和蒙古知识分子的代表们都在会上讲了话。
    开过群众大会后，在礼炮声中，蒙古人民共和国大呼拉尔主席团、蒙古人民共和国部长会议、蒙古人民革命党中央委员会和各群众团体的代表到苏赫—巴托尔和乔巴山墓前献花圈。苏联驻蒙古人民共和国特命全权大使皮萨列夫和各人民民主国家的外交使节也在墓前献了花圈。
