Title: Marshal Kim Il-sung Sends Telegram to Chairman Ho Chi Minh; Foreign Minister Nam Il Sends Telegram to Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Foreign Minister Pham Van Dong
Author:
Date: 1954-07-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 24th: Marshal Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and Foreign Minister Nam Il, on the 23rd, sent telegrams to President Ho Chi Minh of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs Pham Van Dong, respectively, congratulating them on the signing of the Vietnam armistice agreement.

Marshal Kim Il-sung's congratulatory telegram to Chairman Ho Chi Minh:
To Comrade Ho Chi Minh, President of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam:
On the occasion of the signing of the armistice agreement, which marks the glorious victory of the Vietnamese people, I extend my heartfelt brotherly congratulations to you, and through you, to the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the heroic Vietnamese people, on behalf of the entire Korean people, the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and myself.

The end of the eight years of bloody war imposed on the Vietnamese people, who love their country's freedom, independence, and peace, is a great victory for the Vietnamese people and a glorious victory for the world's peace-loving democratic camp headed by the Soviet Union; it is a huge contribution to easing international tensions.

The Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea wishes the fraternal Vietnamese people new achievements in their struggle to heal the wounds of the long war as soon as possible and in their struggle for the peaceful reunification of their motherland.

Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea
                          Marshal Kim Il-sung
                July 23, 1954, Pyongyang

Nam Il's congratulatory telegram to Pham Van Dong:
To Comrade Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam:
On the occasion of the signing of the Vietnam armistice agreement, which is a great victory for the heroic struggle of the fraternal Vietnamese people and their consistent efforts to restore peace, I extend my warm congratulations to you.

I wish you even greater achievements in the noble cause of consolidating peace in Vietnam and peacefully reunifying your motherland.

Foreign Minister of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Nam Il
                July 23, 1954, Pyongyang

### 金日成元帅电贺胡志明主席  南日外务相电贺范文同副总理兼代外长

1954-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

    金日成元帅电贺胡志明主席
    南日外务相电贺范文同副总理兼代外长
    【新华社平壤二十四日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成元帅和外务相南日在二十三日分别致电越南民主共和国政府胡志明主席和副总理兼代理外交部部长范文同，祝贺越南停战协定的签订。
    金日成元帅给胡志明主席的贺电越南民主共和国政府主席胡志明同志：
    值此标志着越南人民的辉煌胜利的停战协定签订之际，我谨以全体朝鲜人民、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府和我个人的名义，向您并通过您向越南民主共和国政府和英勇的越南人民致以衷心的兄弟般的祝贺。
    强加于热爱自己祖国自由、独立和和平的越南人民身上的八年流血战争的结束，是越南人民的巨大胜利，也是以苏联为首的世界爱好和平民主阵营的光辉胜利；对缓和国际紧张局势，是巨大的贡献。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府谨祝兄弟般的越南人民在为早日治愈长期战争的创伤的斗争和为和平统一祖国的斗争中，取得新的成就。
  朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相
                          元帅　金日成
                一九五四年七月二十三日　平壤
    南日给范文同的贺电越南民主共和国政府副总理兼代理外交部部长范文同同志：
    值此成为兄弟般的越南人民的英雄斗争和为恢复和平始终如一的努力的巨大胜利的越南停战协定签订之际，我向您致以热烈的祝贺。
    祝您在为巩固越南和平和和平统一自己祖国的崇高事业中，取得更大的成就。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务相　南日
                一九五四年七月二十三日　平壤
