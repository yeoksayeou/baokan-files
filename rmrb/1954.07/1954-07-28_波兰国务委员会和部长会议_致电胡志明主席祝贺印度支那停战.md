Title: Polish State Council and Council of Ministers Send Telegram to President Ho Chi Minh to Congratulate on the Indochina Armistice
Author:
Date: 1954-07-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 26th: The State Council and the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic sent a telegram to President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to congratulate him on the signing of the agreement on the cessation of hostilities in Indochina. The telegram said: "The Polish people warmly congratulate the glorious Vietnamese people on their victory in the struggle for freedom and wish the Vietnamese people continuous success in the labor of peaceful construction. All honest people believe that the Geneva Conference is a major victory for the defenders of peace throughout the world."

### 波兰国务委员会和部长会议  致电胡志明主席祝贺印度支那停战

1954-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰国务委员会和部长会议
    致电胡志明主席祝贺印度支那停战
    【新华社二十七日讯】据塔斯社华沙二十六日讯：波兰人民共和国国务委员会和部长会议就停止印度支那敌对行动协定的签订电贺越南民主共和国主席胡志明。贺电说：“波兰人民热烈祝贺在争取自由的斗争中获得光荣的越南人民，并祝越南人民在和平建设的劳动中不断获得成功。一切正直的人们都认为，日内瓦会议是全世界的和平捍卫者的一个重大胜利。”
