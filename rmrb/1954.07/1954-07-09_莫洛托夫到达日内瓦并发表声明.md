Title: Molotov Arrives in Geneva and Issues Statement
Author:
Date: 1954-07-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 8th: Vyacheslav Molotov, head of the Soviet delegation to the Geneva Conference and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, arrived in Geneva by plane at 6:30 AM on the 8th.

Those at the airport to welcome him included members of the Soviet delegation headed by Vasily Kuznetsov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, and the Soviet Minister to Switzerland.

Also at the airport to welcome him were: Li Kenong, representative of the Chinese delegation to the Geneva Conference and Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China; Wang Bingnan, Secretary-General of the delegation; Lei Renmin and Shi Zhe, advisors; as well as Feng Xuan, the People's Republic of China's Minister to Switzerland, and Wen Pengjiu, Consul General in Geneva.

Also at the airport to welcome him were members of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam delegation, headed by Phạm Văn Đồng, head of the delegation.

Molotov issued a statement at the airport. The full text of the statement is as follows:

"In the near future, the Geneva Conference on the restoration of peace in Indochina will continue its work with the participation of ministers.

The work done in the past two weeks or more by the representatives who remained in Geneva in place of the ministers is of considerable significance for achieving a positive outcome of the Geneva Conference.

Before the meeting of the foreign ministers resumes, I hope to have a few days of rest and to be able to understand more specifically the work done here recently."

### 莫洛托夫到达日内瓦并发表声明

1954-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫到达日内瓦并发表声明
   【新华社日内瓦八日电】出席日内瓦会议的苏联代表团首席代表、苏联外交部部长莫洛托夫于八日上午六时半乘飞机到达日内瓦。
    到机场欢迎的有以苏联代表团代表、苏联外交部副部长库兹涅佐夫为首的苏联代表团人员和苏联驻瑞士公使。
    到机场欢迎的还有：中华人民共和国代表团代表、中华人民共和国外交部副部长李克农，代表团秘书长王炳南，顾问雷任民、师哲等，以及中华人民共和国驻瑞士公使冯铉，驻日内瓦总领事温朋久。
    到机场欢迎的还有以越南民主共和国代表团首席代表范文同为首的越南民主共和国代表团人员。
    莫洛托夫在机场发表了声明。声明全文如下：
    “在最近期间，关于恢复印度支那和平问题的日内瓦会议将在部长们的参加下继续工作。
    在过去两个多星期中，代替部长们留在日内瓦的代表所做的工作，对于达成日内瓦会议的肯定结果具有相当重大的意义。
    在外交部长们的会议恢复之前，我希望有几天的休息，并能够更具体地了解最近期间在这里所做的工作。”
