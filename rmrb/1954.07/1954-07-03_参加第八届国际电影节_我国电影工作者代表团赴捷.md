Title: Chinese Film Workers Delegation to Czechoslovakia to Participate in the 8th International Film Festival
Author:
Date: 1954-07-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 2nd:
    The Chinese film workers delegation, invited to participate in the 8th International Film Festival, led by head of delegation Tian Fang and deputy head Zhai Qiang, flew from Beijing to the Czechoslovak Republic on the morning of the 2nd.
    This time, the Chinese film workers delegation brought five films to participate in the competition at the 8th International Film Festival: the color feature film "The Butterfly Lovers," the feature films "People on the Prairie" and "Taking Tiger Mountain by Strategy," the color documentary "Folk Sports Performances," and the large-scale documentary "Anshan Iron and Steel Works Under Construction."

### 参加第八届国际电影节  我国电影工作者代表团赴捷

1954-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

    参加第八届国际电影节
    我国电影工作者代表团赴捷
    据新华社二日讯：应邀请参加第八届国际电影节的我国电影工作者代表团在团长田方、副团长翟强率领下，已在二日晨由北京乘飞机飞往捷克斯洛伐克共和国。
    这次我国电影工作者代表团携带了彩色故事影片“梁山伯与祝英台”，故事影片“草原上的人们”、“智取华山”，彩色纪录影片“民间体育表演”和大型纪录影片“鞍钢在建设中”等五部影片去参加第八届国际电影节的竞赛。
