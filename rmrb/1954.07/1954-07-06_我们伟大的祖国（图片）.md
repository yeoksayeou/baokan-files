    Our Great Motherland
    Anshan Iron and Steel powerfully supported the steel rail production of Chongqing 101 Iron and Steel Plant. The steel rails rolled from the steel billets transported from Anshan Iron and Steel in 1953 alone were enough to lay two Chengdu-Chongqing Railways. This is a batch of steel billets newly transported from Anshan Iron and Steel.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    鞍钢有力地支援了重庆一○一钢铁厂的钢轨生产。仅在一九五三年用鞍钢运来的钢胚轧制的钢轨，就可铺两条成渝铁路。这是新近由鞍钢运来的一批钢胚。
