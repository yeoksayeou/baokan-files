Title: Government Administration Council Holds 220th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-07-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 8th—The 220th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 1st. After listening to explanations by Teng Daiyuan, Minister of the Ministry of Railways, Li Renjun, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Fuel Industry, and Li Gengxin, Deputy Director of the Materials Allocation Bureau of the State Planning Commission, the meeting adopted the "Decision on Gradually Promoting the Regional Production and Marketing Balance and Rational Transportation System for Coal." The meeting also approved various appointment and removal proposals.

### 政务院举行第二二零次会议

1954-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第二二零次会议
    【新华社八日讯】中央人民政府政务院第二百二十次政务会议在一日下午举行。会议在分别听了中央人民政府铁道部部长滕代远、燃料工业部副部长李人俊、国家计划委员会物资分配局副局长李耕辛所作的说明后，通过了关于逐步推行煤炭分区产销平衡合理运输制度的决定。会议并通过了各项任免案。
