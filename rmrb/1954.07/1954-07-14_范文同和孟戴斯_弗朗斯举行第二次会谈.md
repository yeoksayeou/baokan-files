Title: Pham Van Dong and Mendès France Hold Second Meeting

Author:

Date: 1954-07-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 12th. Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Foreign Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and French Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Mendès France held their second meeting here from 5:30 PM to 7:15 PM this afternoon. They exchanged views on various issues related to the restoration of peace in Indochina.

The meeting took place in a harmonious atmosphere.

### 范文同和孟戴斯—弗朗斯举行第二次会谈

1954-07-14
第1版()
专栏：

    范文同和孟戴斯—弗朗斯举行第二次会谈
    【新华社日内瓦十二日电】越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外长范文同和法国总理兼外长孟戴斯—弗朗斯十二日下午五时半到七时一刻在此间作了第二次会谈，他们就有关恢复印度支那和平的各种问题交换了意见。
    会谈在和谐的气氛中进行。
