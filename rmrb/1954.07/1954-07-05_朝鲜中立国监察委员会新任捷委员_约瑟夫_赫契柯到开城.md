Title: The New Czech Member of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission in Korea, Josef Hečko, Arrives in Kaesong
Author:
Date: 1954-07-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 2nd - The new Czechoslovakian member of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission in Korea, General Josef Hečko (formerly translated as Josef Heaoko), has arrived in Kaesong to replace General František Bláha, the former Czechoslovakian member. General Hečko attended the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission meeting on June 30th.

### 朝鲜中立国监察委员会新任捷委员  约瑟夫·赫契柯到开城

1954-07-05
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜中立国监察委员会新任捷委员
    约瑟夫·赫契柯到开城
    【新华社开城二日电】朝鲜中立国监察委员会新任捷克斯洛伐克委员约瑟夫·赫契柯（前译约瑟夫·赫奥科）将军已经到达开城，接替前任捷克斯洛伐克委员弗朗齐歇克·布莱希将军的工作。赫契柯将军已在六月三十日出席了中立国监察委员会的会议。
