Title: Soviet Party and Government Leaders Visit "German Democratic Republic" Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1954-07-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 4th: According to TASS, Soviet Party and Government leaders Malenkov, Voroshilov, Khrushchev, Molotov, and Mikoyan visited the "German Democratic Republic" exhibition currently being held in Moscow on the 3rd.

The Soviet Party and Government leaders were welcomed by the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the German Democratic Republic, R. Appelt, the Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic, W. Banaschek, and representatives of the exhibition management. They visited the exhibition for almost an hour and a half.

Note: The "German Democratic Republic" exhibition opened in Moscow on June 5th, displaying more than 2,500 exhibits. The exhibition has been warmly welcomed by the Soviet people, with tens of thousands of visitors daily.

### 苏联党和政府领袖参观“民主德国”展览会

1954-07-05
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联党和政府领袖参观“民主德国”展览会
    【新华社四日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党和政府的领袖马林科夫、伏罗希洛夫、赫鲁晓夫、莫洛托夫和米高扬三日参观了目前正在莫斯科举行的“民主德国”展览会。
    苏联党和政府的领袖受到德意志民主共和国特命全权大使鲁·阿佩尔特、德意志民主共和国大使馆商务参赞维·巴纳谢克和展览会管理处代表的欢迎。他们在展览会中参观了差不多一个半小时。
    按：“民主德国”展览会六月五日在莫斯科开幕，陈列的展览品有二千五百件以上。展览会受到苏联人民的热烈欢迎，每日参观的人数以万计。
