Title: People Across the Country Warmly Welcome the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1954-07-04
Page: 5

    Pastoralists from the Hulunbuir, Xilin Gol, Ulanqab, Zhaowuda, and Jerim leagues, who are temporarily residing in the suburbs of Hohhot due to business, are reading the "Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China" together with joy and excitement. (Inner Mongolia Daily)

    Members of minority nationality visiting groups who had just arrived in Changsha from various minority areas of Hunan Province felt extremely joyous and encouraged upon seeing the news of the publication of the "Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China." (New Hunan Daily, Photo by Zhang Yunlong)

    The democratic party branches of the Beijing Municipal Constitution Draft Discussion Committee held a study report meeting on the "Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China" at the Tianqiao Theater on July 1st. This is a corner of the venue. (Our reporter Gao Liang)

    Lamaseries in Hohhot's Da Zhao (Lama Temple) are discussing the "Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China." (Inner Mongolia Daily)

    After seeing the "Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China," students from Beijing No. 25 Middle School are enthusiastically engaging in discussions during their free time and expressing their thoughts.
                      (Our reporter Wang Zhiyuan)

    The news of the publication of the "Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China" reached the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Region in Jilin Province. Members of the Cui Zhusong Agricultural Production Cooperative, who were working in the fields, rushed to read the newspaper carrying the draft constitution. (Xinhua News Agency, Photo by Jin Zhenhao)

    Workers across the country are welcoming the "Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China" with great enthusiasm and excitement. The amateur troupe organized by the staff and workers of the Anshan Iron and Steel Company Power Plant is publicizing the "Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China" among the staff and workers through literary and artistic forms. This is a scene of the amateur troupe performing. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Tan Zhiqiang)

    The members of the 502 shallow drilling team of the Jiuquan Geological Team working in the Jiuquan Basin, Gansu Province, immediately started a discussion after seeing the "Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China." They expressed that they would welcome the "Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China" with practical actions such as carrying out technological innovations and exceeding the June plan.
                               (Xinhua News Agency reporter Yao Zongyi)

    Cadres and soldiers of various units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army warmly welcome the "Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China." The picture shows the soldiers of the Special Merit Third Company of a unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army reading and chatting about the "Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China" in groups. (Liberation Army Pictorial)

    Dairy workers at the state-owned Wulidian Farm in the suburbs of Beijing are reading the "Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China."
                                    (Our reporter Zhang Li)

    When talking about the "Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China" with female workers from the State-owned No. 6 Cotton Factory, Shi Xiaomei, an old worker in Shanghai, said: I have participated in the revolutionary struggle for more than 30 years and have seen how much blood the people shed for the revolution. Our victory did not come easily. The publication of the draft constitution is really a great event for the people of the whole country. (Labor Daily, Photo by Xu Wenjun)

    Members of the First Agricultural Production Cooperative in Xipu Township, Pixian County, Sichuan Province, are reading the news of the publication of the "Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China." (Sichuan Daily)

    The Beijing Municipal Constitution Draft Discussion Committee has begun training reporters for the entire city. The picture shows reporters from Dongdan District participating in a group study and discussion meeting. (Our reporter Zhang Li)

    All the representatives of the first session of the first People's Congress of Dongdan District, Beijing, discussed and unanimously passed a resolution supporting the "Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China" with infinite excitement. The picture shows the scene of the conference. (Our reporter Wang Zhiyuan)

### 全国各地人民热烈欢迎中华人民共国宪法草案（图片）

1954-07-04
第5版()
专栏：

    全国各地人民热烈欢迎中华人民共国宪法草案
    因事到呼和浩特暂住在市郊的呼伦贝尔、锡林郭勒、乌兰察布、昭乌达、哲里木等五个盟的牧民们，以欢欣鼓舞的心情在一起阅读“中华人民共和国宪法草案”。内蒙古日报稿
    从湖南各少数民族地区刚到达长沙的少数民族参观团的团员，看到“中华人民共和国宪法草案”公布的消息，都感到无比的欢欣和鼓舞。新湖南报稿　张云龙摄
    北京市宪法草案讨论委员会各民主党派分会，于七月一日在天桥剧场举行了“中华人民共和国宪法草案”学习报告会。这是会场的一角。本报记者　高粮摄
    呼和浩特大召（喇嘛庙）的喇嘛们在讨论“中华人民共和国宪法草案”。内蒙古日报稿
    北京市第二十五中学的同学们看到“中华人民共和国宪法草案”以后，就在课外热烈地展开讨论，并纷纷发表自己的感想。
                      本报　王志渊摄
    “中华人民共和国宪法草案”公布的消息传到了吉林省延边朝鲜族自治区，正在田间劳动的崔竹松农业生产合作社社员们争先赶来阅读登载宪法草案的报纸。新华社稿　金振镐摄
    全国各地工人以万分热烈兴奋的心情欢迎“中华人民共和国宪法草案”。鞍山钢铁公司发电厂职工组织的业余剧团，通过文艺形式在职工群众中宣传“中华人民共和国宪法草案”。这是业余剧团在演唱的情形。新华社记者　谭志强摄
    在甘肃省酒泉盆地上工作的酒泉地质大队五零二浅钻队员们看到了“中华人民共和国宪法草案”后立即展开了讨论。他们表示要以开展技术革新、超额完成六月份计划的实际行动来欢迎“中华人民共和国宪法草案”。
                               新华社记者　姚宗仪摄
    中国人民解放军各部队的干部和战士热烈欢迎“中华人民共和国宪法草案”。图为中国人民解放军某部，特功第三连的战士们分组阅读与漫谈“中华人民共和国宪法草案”的情形。解放军画报稿
    北京市郊国营五里店农场的挤奶工人在阅读“中华人民共和国宪法草案”。
                                    本报  张力摄
    上海老工人史小妹和国棉六厂女工谈论“中华人民共和国宪法草案”时说：我参加革命斗争三十多年，看到了人民为革命流了多少鲜血，我们的胜利是不容易得来的。宪法草案的公布实在是全国人民的大喜事。　劳动报　徐文钧摄
    四川省郫县犀浦乡第一农业生产合作社的社员们，在阅读“中华人民共和国宪法草案”公布的消息。四川日报稿
    北京市宪法草案讨论委员会训练全市报告员的工作已经开始了。图为参加受训的东单区的报告员在开小组学习讨论会。本报　张力摄
    北京市东单区第一届第一次人民代表大会的全体代表，怀着无限兴奋的心情，讨论并一致通过拥护“中华人民共和国宪法草案”的决议。图为大会会场的场景。　本报　王志渊摄
