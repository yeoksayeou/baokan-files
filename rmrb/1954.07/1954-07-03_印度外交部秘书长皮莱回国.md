Title: Indian Foreign Ministry Secretary General Pillai Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1954-07-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 2nd. N.R. Pillai, Secretary-General of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, who accompanied Zhou Enlai, Premier and Foreign Minister of the State Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, from New Delhi to Guangzhou, along with his wife and B.K. Ahluwalia, Deputy Secretary of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, left Guangzhou at 9:15 a.m. on the 1st by the same plane to return to their country.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Zhu Guang, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou, Cao Ruoming, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of Guangzhou, Zheng Tianbao, Director of the Reception Office of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, Wang Lan, Deputy Director of the Reception Office of Guangzhou, and more than 20 others.

Wang Zhuoru, an advisor who accompanied Premier Zhou Enlai on his visit to India, also went to the airport to see them off.

During their stay in Guangzhou, Pillai and the other two paid a visit to Tao Zhu, Acting Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, and were invited to attend a banquet hosted by Tao Zhu and watch a performance by the South China Song and Dance Troupe. They also visited Yuexiu Mountain, the Guangzhou People's Museum, the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall, the Beixiu Lake Swimming Pool, and the Lingnan Cultural Relics Palace.

### 印度外交部秘书长皮莱回国

1954-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

    印度外交部秘书长皮莱回国
   【新华社广州二日电】自新德里陪送中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来到广州的印度外交部秘书长纳·鲁·皮莱和他的夫人和印度外交部副秘书长布·克·阿护迦等三人，已于一日上午九时十五分乘原机离广州返国。
    前往机场欢送的有：广州市人民政府副市长朱光、广州市人民政府外事处处长曹若茗、广东省人民政府交际处处长郑天保、广州市人民政府交际处副处长王岚等二十余人。
    随同周恩来总理访问印度的顾问王倬如也到机场欢送。
    皮莱等三人在广州逗留期间，曾拜会了广东省人民政府代理主席陶铸，并应邀出席了陶铸的宴会和观看了华南歌舞团的演出，游览了越秀山、广州市人民博物馆、中山纪念堂、北秀湖游泳池、岭南文物宫等。
