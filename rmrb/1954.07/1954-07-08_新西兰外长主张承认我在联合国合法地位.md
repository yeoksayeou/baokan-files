Title: New Zealand Foreign Minister Advocates for Recognition of China's Legitimate Status in the United Nations
Author:
Date: 1954-07-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Wellington, July 7th: According to a Reuters dispatch from Wellington, New Zealand Foreign Minister Webb stated in the House of Representatives on the 6th that he firmly believes the time has come to recognize the legitimate status of the People's Republic of China in the United Nations.

Webb believes that denying the People's Republic of China its legitimate status in the United Nations "hinders the easing of international tensions" and "endangers world peace."

### 新西兰外长主张承认我在联合国合法地位

1954-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

    新西兰外长主张承认我在联合国合法地位
    【新华社七日讯】据路透社惠灵顿消息，新西兰外长韦伯六日在众议院说，他坚信，承认中华人民共和国在联合国的合法地位的时机已经来到了。
    韦伯认为：否认中华人民共和国在联合国的合法地位“妨碍了国际紧张局势的缓和”，并“危害了世界和平”。
