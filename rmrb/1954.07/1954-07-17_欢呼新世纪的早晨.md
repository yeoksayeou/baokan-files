Title: Cheer for the Morning of the New Century
Author: Zhu Ziqi
Date: 1954-07-17
Page: 3

    Cheer for the Morning of the New Century
    Zhu Ziqi
    When the morning breeze blows on the awakening streets of Moscow,
    When I ride in a car past Pushkin Square,
    It's as if the roar of salutes suddenly resounds,
    It's as if the golden bell of good news suddenly rings,
    The radio in the car transmits an astonishing piece of news,
    The world inside and outside the car is instantly filled with joy!
    —The Soviet Union has built the first atomic power station,
    —It started generating electricity on June 27th.
    —Power output of five thousand kilowatts,
    —Transmitted to factories and collective farms!
    Oh, announce to the world that the new century of science begins today!
    Announce to the world the new victory of socialist peaceful industry!
    It's as if the high tower of the atomic power station is shining before my eyes,
    It's as if the motor under the tower is singing happily in my heart.
    I am so happy that I really want to jump out of the speeding car,
    To embrace the kind Muscovites walking on the road!
    "Glory to the Soviet people!" contemporaries say,
    "Thank you, benefactor!" future children say.
    Have you heard? The first Soviet atomic power station,
    Started generating electricity on this day—June 27th.
    On this day, countless chimneys of factories stretch out their heads to cheer!
    On this day, large tracts of land in collective farms open their chests to cheer!
    From now on, atomic energy—a happy life become one word,
    From now on, the ideal of atomic energy serving peace becomes reality.
    Yes, across the sea, some people desperately clamor for atomic weapons,
    Across the sea, some people frantically produce hydrogen bombs to incite war.
    What they are calculating is how many mothers and infants will die,
    What they are hoping for is how to destroy towns and villages.
    Oh! How can mankind allow science to be desecrated and commit crimes?
    Mankind must rise up and fight to save life and civilization!
    Under the light of atomic radio waves emitted from Soviet soil,
    How shameful and dim is the atomic smog over the Marshall Islands!
    In the roar of the atomic motor on Soviet soil,
    How small and pitiful is the hydropower and thermal power of the Mississippi River!
    Announce to the world the new victory of socialist peaceful industry!
    Oh, announce to the world that the new century of science has begun!
    The whole earth cheers, the first Soviet atomic power station,
    Started generating electricity on the memorable day—June 27th.
    This day is the morning of our new century of science!
    This day has written a glorious page in world history!
    "Glory to the Soviet people!" contemporaries say,
    "Thank you, benefactor!" future children say.

### 欢呼新世纪的早晨
朱子奇
1954-07-17
第3版()
专栏：

    欢呼新世纪的早晨
    朱子奇
    当晨风吹着苏醒的莫斯科大街的时候，
    当我坐车驰过普希金广场的时候，
    像是隆隆的礼炮突然响起来，
    像是报喜的金钟突然敲起来，
    车内的收音机传出一个惊人的消息，
    车内车外的世界顿时充满了一片欢喜！
    ——苏联建立了第一个原子电力站，
    ——六月二十七日开始发电。
    ——电力五千个瓩特，
    ——输送给工厂农庄！
    呵，向世界宣布科学的新世纪从今天开始！
    向世界宣布社会主义和平工业的新胜利！
    仿佛原子站的高塔在我眼前闪亮，
    仿佛高塔下的马达在我心中欢唱。
    我高兴得真想从飞奔的车中跳出去呀，
    去拥抱那行走在道上的可亲的莫斯科人！
    “光荣属于苏维埃人！”同时代人这样说，
    “感谢你啊，造福者！”未来的孩子这样说。
    听见了吗？苏联第一个原子电力站，
    在这一天——六月二十七日开始发电。
    这一天，工厂的无数烟囱伸出头来欢呼！
    这一天，农庄的大块土地敞开胸来欢呼！
    从此，原子能—幸福生活连成了一个字，
    从此，原子能服务和平的理想变为现实。
    是的，海那边有人死命地叫嚣原子武器，
    海那边有人为煽动战火而疯狂地生产氢弹。
    他们盘算的是多少母亲和婴儿的死亡，
    他们盼望的是如何使城镇和村庄毁光。
    呵！人类怎能允许科学被亵渎、去犯罪呢？
    人类要为拯救生命和文明而奋起抗争！
    在苏维埃土地上射出的原子电波的光芒下，
    马绍尔群岛上空的原子烟雾是多么可耻和暗淡！
    在苏维埃土地上轰鸣的原子马达声中，
    密士失必河的水电火电动力又何等渺小可怜！
    向世界宣布社会主义和平工业的新胜利！
    呵，向世界宣布科学的新世纪已经开始！
    整个地球欢呼呀，苏联第一个原子电力站，
    在可纪念的日子——六月二十七日开始发电。
    这一天，是我们科学的新世纪的早晨！
    这一天，写下了世界史辉煌的一页！
    “光荣属于苏维埃人！”同时代人说，
    “感谢你啊，造福者！”未来的孩子说。
