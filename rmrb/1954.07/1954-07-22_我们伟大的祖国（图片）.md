Author:
Date: 1954-07-22

    Our Great Motherland
　　The working people of our country have the right to rest. The state gradually expands the material conditions for working people's rest and recuperation to ensure that working people enjoy this right. These are textile workers from various places recuperating at the Qingdao Textile Workers' Sanatorium.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
　　我国劳动者有休息的权利，国家逐步扩充劳动者休息和休养的物质条件，以保证劳动者享受这种权利。这是在青岛纺织工人休养所休养的各地纺织工人。
