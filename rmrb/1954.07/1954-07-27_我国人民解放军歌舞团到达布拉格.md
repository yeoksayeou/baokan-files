Title: Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe Arrives in Prague
Author:
Date: 1954-07-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch:
The Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe, invited by the Czechoslovak Ministry of National Defense to visit and perform in Czechoslovakia, arrived in Prague by train on the morning of the 24th, led by Chen Yi, head of the troupe and Minister of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government of China.

Those who welcomed the troupe at the station included Kožela, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Czechoslovak Ministry of National Defense, Brival, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Republic, Sekaninová-Čakrtová, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, A. Svoboda, Mayor of Prague, as well as army representatives and several thousand citizens. Li Qingquan, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Czechoslovakia, and Hu Wei, Deputy Military Attaché, as well as Yang Yongshun, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to Czechoslovakia, and others, also welcomed the troupe at the station.

A mass rally was held outside the train station to welcome the troupe. At the rally, Kožela and Brival delivered welcoming speeches respectively. Chen Yi delivered a reply. On behalf of the Chinese people, the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Commander-in-Chief Zhu De, he extended fraternal and warm greetings to the Czechoslovak people and army, and wished them even greater achievements in socialist construction and the cause of defending world peace.

### 我国人民解放军歌舞团到达布拉格

1954-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

    我国人民解放军歌舞团到达布拉格
    据新华社讯：应捷克斯洛伐克国防部邀请前来捷克斯洛伐克作访问演出的中国人民解放军歌舞团，在歌舞团团长、我国中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部文化部部长陈沂的率领下，二十四日晨乘火车抵达布拉格。
    到车站欢迎的有捷克斯洛伐克国防部总政治部副主任科泽拉、共和国文化部副部长布里瓦尔、外交部副部长塞卡尼诺娃—查克尔托娃、布拉格市市长阿·斯沃波达以及军队代表和市民数千人。到车站欢迎的还有我国驻捷大使馆临时代办李清泉和副武官胡畏，以及朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻捷大使杨永旬等。
    火车站外举行了群众欢迎大会。会上，科泽拉和布里瓦尔分别致欢迎词。陈沂致答词。他代表中国人民、中国人民解放军和朱德总司令向捷克斯洛伐克人民和军队致以兄弟般的热情的问候，并祝他们在社会主义建设和保卫世界和平事业中获得更大的成就。
