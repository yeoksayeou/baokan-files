Title: International Women's Federation and World Federation of Youth Welcome the Indochina Armistice
Author:
Date: 1954-07-30
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 28th: The Secretariat of the Women's International Democratic Federation warmly welcomes the historically significant event of the signing of the Indochina armistice agreement, as it ends the war that has caused immense sacrifices for the people of Indochina for seven and a half years. The Secretariat also extends its congratulations to women around the world, as women in various countries have demonstrated their dedication and contributed to making negotiation triumph over force. The Secretariat's telegram to the Vietnam Women's Union reads as follows: We welcome the signing of the armistice agreement with boundless joy. Hundreds of millions of women around the world know the heroic struggle and immense sacrifices your people have made for national independence. They express admiration and boundless respect for your cause of defending peace, and we assure you of our warm and vigilant support. We firmly believe that the new victory of peace will enable you to build a happy and unified Vietnam.
      Xinhua News Agency, July 25th: TASS, Budapest, July 24th: The Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth issued a statement. The statement said:
    Peace-loving youth around the world are overjoyed to learn of the end to the eight-year war that ravaged Vietnam.
    The Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, on behalf of its 83 million members and all peace-loving young friends around the world, congratulates this major event. It is a great victory for the forces of peace, and a new step forward in easing international tensions and consolidating world peace.
    The Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth warmly congratulates the heroic Vietnamese youth. The Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth also congratulates the youth of France and other countries for their efforts in promoting the armistice in Vietnam, and calls on young people to continue their efforts to achieve new achievements in the sacred cause of defending peace.

### 国际妇联和世界青联欢迎印度支那停战

1954-07-30
第4版()
专栏：

    国际妇联和世界青联欢迎印度支那停战
    【新华社柏林二十八日电】国际民主妇女联合会书记处热烈欢迎签订印度支那停战协定的具有历史意义的事件，因为它结束了七年半以来使印度支那人民遭受了巨大牺牲的战争。书记处还向全世界的妇女表示祝贺，因为各国的妇女在使协商战胜武力方面表现了她们的忠诚，并作出了贡献。书记处致越南妇女联合会的贺电如下：我们怀着无限的欢欣，欢迎停战协定的签订。全世界千百万妇女都知道你们人民为争取民族独立所进行的英勇斗争和遭受的巨大牺牲。她们对于你们保卫和平的事业表示爱慕和无限的敬仰，我们保证给予你们热烈的和保持警惕的支持，我们深信新的和平的胜利将使你们能够建设一个幸福和统一的越南。
      【新华社二十五日讯】塔斯社布达佩斯二十四日讯：世界民主青年联盟书记处发表了声明。声明说：
    全世界爱好和平的青年得知结束蹂躏越南的八年战争这个消息时，非常高兴。
    世界民主青年联盟书记处代表八千三百万世界民主青年联盟盟员和全世界所有爱好和平的青年朋友祝贺这一重大的事件。它是和平力量的伟大胜利，它是在缓和国际紧张局势和巩固世界和平方面迈进的新的一步。
    世界民主青年联盟书记处热烈地祝贺英勇的越南青年。世界民主青年联盟书记处也同样地向法国青年和其他国家的青年祝贺，因为由于他们的努力促成了越南的停战，并号召青年们继续努力以求在为保卫和平的神圣事业中获得新的成就。
