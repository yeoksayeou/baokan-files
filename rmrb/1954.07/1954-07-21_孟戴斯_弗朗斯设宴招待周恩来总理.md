Title: Mendes France Hosts a Banquet in Honor of Premier Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1954-07-21
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 20th: French Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Mendes France, hosted a banquet at 2:00 PM on the 20th in honor of Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People’s Republic of China, Zhou Enlai.

    Also attending the banquet from the Chinese side were Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, Li Kenong, and Secretary-General of the Chinese Delegation, Wang Bingnan.

    Attending as hosts from the French side were Lachambre, Minister for Relations with Member States of the Indochina Federation; Chauvel, French Ambassador to Switzerland; Joxe, French Ambassador to the Soviet Union; Boris, Secretary-General of the Premier's Office; and Guermas, Advisor to the French Delegation.

    The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 孟戴斯—弗朗斯设宴招待周恩来总理

1954-07-21
第1版()
专栏：

    孟戴斯—弗朗斯设宴招待周恩来总理
    【新华社日内瓦二十日电】法国总理兼外交部部长孟戴斯—弗朗斯在二十日下午二时设宴招待中华人民共和国总理兼外交部部长周恩来。
    中国方面出席宴会的还有外交部副部长张闻天、李克农和中国代表团秘书长王炳南。
    法国方面出席作陪的有印度支那联邦成员国关系部部长拉香波、法国驻瑞士大使萧维尔、法国驻苏联大使约克斯、总理办公厅秘书长波里斯、法国代表团顾问吉埃马斯。
    宴会在友好气氛中进行。
