Title: Bulgarian Cooperative Delegation and Soviet, German Representatives Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-07-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd: The Bulgarian Cooperative Delegation, led by Georgiev (Vice Chairman of the Central Cooperative Union), and members Khorozov (Chairman of the Pleven District Cooperative Branch), and the German Democratic Republic Cooperative Delegation, members Pister (Secretary of the Karl-Marx-Stadt Supply and Marketing Cooperative District Committee), Weiss (Director of the Zwickau District Consumer Cooperative), who were invited by the All-China Federation of Cooperatives to attend the First Congress of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 22nd. Deng Jie, Vice Chairman of the Interim Council of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives, and others welcomed them at the airport. Also present at the airport to welcome them were Grigorov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Bulgarian Embassy, and Konstantin, Commercial Counselor.

In addition, Galakhova (Head of the Labor and Wages Department of the Central Union of Consumer Cooperatives of the Soviet Union), a member of the Soviet Cooperative Delegation invited to our country, also arrived in Beijing by plane on the 21st.

### 保加利亚合作社代表团和苏、德代表到京

1954-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

    保加利亚合作社代表团和苏、德代表到京
    【新华社二十二日讯】应中华全国合作社联合总社的邀请前来参加中华全国合作社第一次代表大会的保加利亚合作社代表团团长格奥尔吉耶夫（合作社中央联合会副主席）、团员霍罗佐夫（普勒文市区合作分社主席），德意志民主共和国合作社代表团团员彼斯特（卡尔·马克思斯塔特供销合作社专区委员会秘书）、魏斯（苏尔专区消费合作社主任）等在二十二日乘飞机到达北京。中华全国合作社联合总社临时理事会副主任邓洁等到机场欢迎。到机场欢迎的还有保加利亚大使馆临时代办格利郭洛夫和商务参赞康士坦丁诺夫。
    此外，应邀来我国的苏联合作社代表团团员加拉霍娃（全苏消费合作社中央联合会劳动工资处处长）也在二十一日乘飞机到达北京。
