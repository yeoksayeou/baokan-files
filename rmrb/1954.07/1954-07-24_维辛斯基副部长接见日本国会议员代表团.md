Title: Vyshinsky Receives Delegation of Japanese Diet Members

Author: 
Date: 1954-07-24
Page: 4

    Vyshinsky Receives Delegation of Japanese Diet Members
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 22nd. TASS Moscow, July 21st: The First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Andrei Yanuaryevich Vyshinsky, received on the 21st the delegation of Japanese Diet members headed by Liberal Party member Naoji Nishimura, which is visiting the Soviet Union.

### 维辛斯基副部长接见日本国会议员代表团

1954-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

    维辛斯基副部长接见日本国会议员代表团
    【新华社二十二日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十一日讯：苏联外交部第一副部长安·扬·维辛斯基在二十一日接见了在苏联访问的以自由党议员西村直已为首的日本国会议员代表团。
