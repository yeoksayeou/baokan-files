Title: CPPCC National Committee Standing Committee Holds Enlarged Meeting; Premier Zhou Enlai Attends and Delivers Report; Participating Members Unanimously Praise the Achievements of Sino-Indian, Sino-Burmese, and Sino-Vietnamese Talks and Support the Work of the Chinese Delegation to the Geneva Conference

Author: 
Date: 1954-07-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 8th: The 57th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference was held in an enlarged session on the afternoon of the 8th. Zhou Enlai, Chief Representative of the People's Republic of China to the Geneva Conference, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, delivered a report at the meeting on his attendance at the Geneva Conference, his visits to India and Burma, and the Sino-Vietnamese talks.

After Premier Zhou Enlai's report, Li Jishen, Zhang Bojun, Huang Yanpei, Chen Qiyou, Xu Deheng, Guo Moruo, Chen Shutong, Wu Yaozong, Xu Guangping, Zhang Xiruo, Wang Yunsheng, Hou Debang and others spoke successively at the meeting. They unanimously agreed with Premier Zhou Enlai's report and expressed unanimous praise for the achievements of Premier Zhou's visits to India and Burma and the Sino-Vietnamese talks. They expressed support and endorsement for the work of the Chinese delegation to the Geneva Conference.

Among those attending the meeting were Mao Zedong, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and Vice-Chairmen Zhou Enlai, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, and Chen Shutong, Standing Committee members Liu Shaoqi, Li Weihan, Wang Kunlun, Jiang Guangnai, Zhang Lan, Zhang Bojun, Huang Yanpei, Zhang Naiqi, Ma Xulun, Zhang Xiruo, Zhu De, Liu Ningyi, Deng Yingchao, Shen Yanbing, Liang Xi, Shao Lizi, Lin Boqu, Deng Xiaoping, Luo Longji, Shi Fuliang, Xu Guangping, Huang Qixiang, Xu Deheng, Chen Qiyou, Jiang Nanxiang, Shi Liang, Li Siguang, Qian Sanqiang, Sheng Pihua, Liao Chengzhi, Wu Yaozong, and Xiong Kewu, totaling thirty-eight people. Also present at the meeting were members of the Central People's Government Committee Li Lisan, He Xiangning, Wu Yuzhang, Peng Zhen, Dong Biwu, Xu Teli, Chen Yun, Gao Chongmin, Situ Meitang, Cai Tingkai, Xi Zhongxun, Peng Zemin, Li Zhuchen, Zhang Nanxian, and Long Yun, as well as members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in Beijing, members of the State Council, heads of various departments of the Central People's Government, and heads of various people's organizations, totaling more than 300 people.

### 政协全国委员会常委会举行扩大会议  周恩来总理出席作报告  到会委员一致赞扬中印、中缅、中越会谈成就并支持我出席日内瓦会议代表团工作

1954-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会常委会举行扩大会议
    周恩来总理出席作报告
    到会委员一致赞扬中印、中缅、中越会谈成就并支持我出席日内瓦会议代表团工作
   【新华社八日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会第五十七次会议在八日下午扩大举行。中华人民共和国出席日内瓦会议首席代表、中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来在会上就出席日内瓦会议以及访问印度、缅甸和举行中越会谈等项问题作了报告。
    周恩来总理报告后，李济深、章伯钧、黄炎培、陈其尤、许德珩、郭沫若、陈叔通、吴耀宗、许广平、张奚若、王芸生、侯德榜等在会上相继发言，一致同意周恩来总理的报告，对于周总理访问印度、缅甸和中越会谈的成就一致表示赞扬，对于我国出席日内瓦会议代表团的工作，表示支持和拥护。
    出席这次会议的有中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会主席毛泽东，副主席周恩来、李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、陈叔通，常务委员刘少奇、李维汉、王昆仑、蒋光鼐、张澜、章伯钧、黄炎培、章乃器、马叙伦、张奚若、朱德、刘宁一、邓颖超、沈雁冰、梁希、邵力子、林伯渠、邓小平、罗隆基、施复亮、许广平、黄琪翔、许德珩、陈其尤、蒋南翔、史良、李四光、钱三强、盛丕华、廖承志、吴耀宗、熊克武等三十八人。列席会议的有中央人民政府委员会委员李立三、何香凝、吴玉章、彭真、董必武、徐特立、陈云、高崇民、司徒美堂、蔡廷锴、习仲勋、彭泽民、李烛尘、张难先、龙云，以及中国人民政协全国委员会在京委员、政务院政务委员、中央人民政府各部门和各人民团体负责人等共三百多人。
