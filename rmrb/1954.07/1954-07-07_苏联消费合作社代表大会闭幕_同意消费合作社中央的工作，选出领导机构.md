Title: Soviet Consumer Cooperative Congress Concludes; Approves Work of Central Union of Consumer Societies, Elects Leadership

Author:
Date: 1954-07-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 4th: The Fourth Congress of the Soviet Consumer Cooperative continued its session in Moscow on the 2nd and 3rd.

Leaders of the Soviet Communist Party and Government, Voroshilov, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Molotov, and Khrushchev, attended the meeting on the 2nd. The entire hall burst into prolonged cheers as they took their seats on the stage.

The meeting on the 2nd continued the discussion of the report of the Board of the Central Union of Consumer Societies of the USSR. The representatives who spoke addressed the development of cooperative trade in the Soviet countryside, the achievements and shortcomings in the work of consumer cooperatives, and plans for future work. The Deputy Chairman of the Board of the Central Union of Consumer Societies of the USSR, Lyubimov, pointed out in his speech that although prices had been reduced, the sales volume of the All-Union Consumer Cooperative in the first five and a half months of this year still increased by 10 billion rubles compared to the same period last year. The Chairman of the Board of the Consumer Cooperative Union of the Georgian Republic, Narsia, said at the meeting when talking about the development of rural trade in Georgia that due to the increase in farmers' income, the republic's sales volume last year had doubled compared to 1948. Among them, the sales volume of high-grade industrial products increased particularly rapidly.

At the end of the discussion, the Chairman of the Board of the Central Union of Consumer Societies of the USSR, Khokhlov, made a concluding speech.

At the meeting on the 3rd, the congress accepted the proposal of some representatives and decided to establish the Russian Federation Consumer Cooperative Union, and to convene the Russian Federation Consumer Cooperative Congress in the near future.

The congress approved the business accounts of the All-Union Consumer Cooperative Central Union as of January 1 this year, with a total business volume of 2,934,000,000 rubles, of which the profit was 213,000,000 rubles. The congress pointed out that in recent years, the financial situation of the All-Union Consumer Cooperative has been greatly consolidated.

The Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Mikoyan, spoke at the meeting on the 3rd. His speech was warmly welcomed by the representatives.

At the meetings on the 2nd and 3rd, congratulatory messages were delivered to the congress by representatives of cooperative organizations from the German Democratic Republic, Romania, Albania, the Mongolian People's Republic, as well as Italy, Sweden, Norway, Argentina, and Israel.

The congress continued its session on the 5th.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 6th: The Fourth Congress of the Soviet Consumer Cooperative concluded in Moscow on the 5th. The congress basically approved the work of the Board of the Central Union of Consumer Societies of the USSR and approved the report of the inspection committee.

At the closing meeting, the Council, Executive Committee, and Inspection Committee of the Central Union of Consumer Societies of the USSR were elected. Klimov was elected Chairman of the Executive Committee, and Timofeyev was elected Chairman of the Inspection Committee.

### 苏联消费合作社代表大会闭幕  同意消费合作社中央的工作，选出领导机构

1954-07-07
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联消费合作社代表大会闭幕
    同意消费合作社中央的工作，选出领导机构
   【新华社四日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联消费合作社第四次代表大会，二日和三日在莫斯科继续开会。
    苏联共产党和政府的领袖伏罗希洛夫、马林科夫、米高扬、莫洛托夫和赫鲁晓夫出席了二日的会议。当他们在主席台上就座时，全场报以经久不息的欢呼。
    二日会议继续讨论全苏消费合作社中央联合会委员会的报告。发言的代表谈到了苏联农村合作社贸易的发展、消费合作社工作中的成绩和缺点以及今后的工作计划。全苏消费合作社中央联合会委员会副主席柳比莫夫在发言中指出，虽然物价降低了，全苏消费合作社在今年五个半月中的销售额仍比去年同时期增加了一百亿卢布。格鲁吉亚共和国消费合作社联合委员会主席纳尔西雅在会上谈到格鲁吉亚农村贸易的发展情况时说，由于农民的收入增加了，共和国去年的商品销售量已比一九四八年增加了一倍。其中，高级品种的工业品的销售量增加得特别快。
    讨论结束时，全苏消费合作社中央联合会委员会主席霍赫洛夫作了总结发言。
    在三日会议上，代表大会接受一些代表的建议决定成立俄罗斯联邦消费合作社联合会。并于最近召开俄罗斯联邦消费合作社代表大会。
    大会批准了全苏消费合作社中央联合会截至今年一月一日为止的营业帐目，营业总额为二十九亿三千四百万卢布，其中利润为二亿一千三百万卢布。大会指出：近几年来，全苏消费合作社的财政情况已经大大巩固了。
    苏联部长会议副主席米高扬在三日的会议上讲了话。他的发言受到代表们的热烈欢迎。
    在二日和三日的会议上，向代表大会致贺词的有：德意志民主共和国、罗马尼亚、阿尔巴尼亚、蒙古人民共和国以及意大利、瑞典、挪威、阿根廷和以色列等国合作社组织的代表。
    代表大会在五日继续开会。
   【新华社六日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联消费合作社第四次代表大会五日在莫斯科闭幕。代表大会基本同意全苏消费合作社中央联合会委员会的工作，并批准了检查委员会的报告。
    在闭幕会议上选出了全苏消费合作社中央联合会理事会，执行委员会和检查委员会。克利莫夫当选为执行委员会主席、季莫费耶夫当选为检查委员会主席。
