Title: Indian Trade Union Representative Joshi Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1954-07-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 14th. V.V. Joshi, the representative of the Indian Trade Union and the secretary of the All-India Trade Union Congress Rajasthan Provincial Trade Union, who was invited to China to attend the "May Day" celebrations, arrived in Guangzhou from Beijing on the 12th and left Guangzhou for home via Hong Kong on the 13th.

### 印度工会代表乔希回国

1954-07-15
第4版()
专栏：

    印度工会代表乔希回国
    【新华社广州十四日电】应邀来我国参加“五一”节观礼的印度工会代表、全印总工会拉贾斯坦省工会书记维·维·乔希，在十二日由北京到达广州，十三日离广州经香港回国。
