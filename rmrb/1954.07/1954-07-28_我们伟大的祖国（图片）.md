Author:
Date: 1954-07-28

    Our Great Motherland
    The western Liaoning protective forest belt has already played a role in preventing wind, sand and floods, and the grain harvest in this area has increased. The picture shows farmers in Laowobao Village, Zhangwu County, western Liaoning Province, weeding in the fields under the protection of the windbreak forest.

    我们伟大的祖国
