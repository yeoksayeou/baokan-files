Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Council and Secretariat Hold Joint Meeting; Qian Junrui Reports on Delegation's Visit to Soviet Union and Work Summary
Author:
Date: 1954-07-10
Page: 1

    Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Council and Secretariat Hold Joint Meeting
    Qian Junrui Reports on Delegation's Visit to Soviet Union and Work Summary
    [Xinhua News Agency, July 9th] The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Council and Secretariat held a joint meeting on the morning of the 9th to hear Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Head of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Delegation to the Soviet Union, report on the delegation's visit to the Soviet Union and work summary.
    Wu Yuzhang, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, spoke at the meeting, expressing satisfaction with the great achievements of the delegation's visit to the Soviet Union, and wishing further consolidation and development of Sino-Soviet friendship.
    The meeting unanimously approved Qian Junrui's report "The Great Soviet People Marching Towards Communism" and the work summary of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Delegation to the Soviet Union, and adopted the delegation's proposal that after returning to their respective regions, members of the delegation should make serious and systematic reports to the local Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations. The local Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations should, in conjunction with current work, organize communication in a planned manner to further promote the continued consolidation and development of the friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union, and advance the great cause of socialist construction in our country.
    The following attended the meeting: Wu Yuzhang, Shen Junru, and Huang Yanpei, Vice Presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; council members Ding Xilin, Wang Zhixiang, Ge Baoquan, Wu Juenong, Zhou Jianren, Shao Lizi, Hou Wailu, Gao Chongmin, Liang Xi, Cao Jinghua, Cao Mengjun, Tao Menghe, Huang Jing, Liao Mengxing, Liu Qingyang, Jiang Guangnai, Yan Baohang, Sa Kongliao, Tan Tiwu; Deputy Secretary-Generals Luo Shuzhang, Liao Jingtian, Li Zhanwu; and directors in Beijing, among others.
    Members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Delegation to the Soviet Union currently in Beijing, as well as heads of various departments of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, also attended the meeting.
    Romanin and Sokolov, representatives of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in China, were also invited to attend the meeting.

### 中苏友协理事会和干事会举行联席会议  钱俊瑞报告代表团访苏经过和工作总结

1954-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友协理事会和干事会举行联席会议
    钱俊瑞报告代表团访苏经过和工作总结
    【新华社九日讯】中苏友好协会理事会和干事会在九日上午举行联席会议，听取中苏友好协会总干事、中苏友好协会访苏代表团团长钱俊瑞报告代表团访苏经过和工作总结。
    中苏友好协会副会长吴玉章在会上讲话，对代表团的访苏工作获得很大成绩表示满意，并祝中苏友谊获得进一步巩固和发展。
    会议一致批准了钱俊瑞作的“向着共产主义迈进的伟大的苏联人民”的报告和中苏友好协会访苏代表团的工作总结，并通过了代表团的建议，即代表团团员回到各地后，即应向各地中苏友好协会作认真的系统的汇报，并由各地中苏友好协会结合当前工作，有计划地组织传达，以进一步促进中苏两国人民的友谊的继续巩固和发展，推进我国社会主义建设的伟大事业。
    出席会议的有：中苏友好协会副会长吴玉章、沈钧儒、黄炎培，理事丁西林、王之相、戈宝权、吴觉农、周建人、邵力子、侯外庐、高崇民、梁希、曹靖华、曹孟君、陶孟和、黄敬、廖梦醒、刘清扬、蒋光鼐、阎宝航、萨空了、谭惕吾、副总干事罗叔章、廖经天、李沾吾和在京干事等。
    中苏友好协会访苏代表团现在北京的团员以及中苏友好协会总会各部门负责人，也参加了会议。
    苏联对外文化协会驻华代表罗满宁、索柯洛夫也应邀出席了会议。
