Title: Chinese Youth Delegation Arrives in Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1954-07-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 7th: The Chinese Youth Delegation, consisting of ten members led by Hu Keshi, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, arrived in Pyongyang on the evening of the 6th at the invitation of the Korean National Youth Activists Congress.

Those who went to the station to welcome the delegation included Kim Ki-soo, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Youth League, representatives from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Ministry of Culture and Propaganda of the Cabinet, and more than 2,000 representatives of youths and children from all walks of life in Pyongyang. Li Shi, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Korea, also went to the station to welcome the delegation. The welcomers held a grand welcoming ceremony on the platform.

### 中国青年代表团到达平壤

1954-07-09
第4版()
专栏：

    中国青年代表团到达平壤
    据新华社平壤七日电：应邀参加朝鲜全国青年积极分子大会的中国青年代表团一行十人，在团长、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡克实率领下，在六日晚到达平壤。
    前往车站欢迎的有朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会副委员长金基洙和内阁外务省、文化宣传省等机关代表，平壤市各界青年和少年儿童代表等共两千多人。我国驻朝鲜大使馆代办李石也到车站欢迎。欢迎者在月台上举行了盛大的欢迎会。
