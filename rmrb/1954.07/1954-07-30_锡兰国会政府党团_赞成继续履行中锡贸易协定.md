Title: Ceylon Parliamentary Government Faction Favors Continuing to Implement the China-Ceylon Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1954-07-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, 26th: According to a Reuters report from Colombo, the Ceylon Parliamentary Government faction, after discussion, supports the continuation of the China-Ceylon trade agreement.

Former Ceylon Prime Minister Dudley Senanayake pointed out that the China-Ceylon trade agreement is beneficial to Ceylon. He said that last year Ceylon obtained a net profit of 90 million rupees from this agreement, and so far this year has obtained a profit of 41 million rupees.

### 锡兰国会政府党团  赞成继续履行中锡贸易协定

1954-07-30
第1版()
专栏：

    锡兰国会政府党团
    赞成继续履行中锡贸易协定
    【新华社二十六日讯】据路透社科伦坡消息：锡兰国会政府党团经过讨论，赞成把中锡贸易协定继续下去。
    锡兰前总理杜德莱·沈纳那亚克指出，中锡贸易协定对锡兰是有利的。他说，去年锡兰从这个协定中得到纯利九千万卢比，今年到目前为止已得到利润四千一百万卢比。
