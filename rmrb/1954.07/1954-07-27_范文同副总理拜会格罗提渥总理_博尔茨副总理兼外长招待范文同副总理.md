Title: Deputy Prime Minister Pham Van Dong Visits Prime Minister Grotewohl; Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Bolz Hosts Deputy Prime Minister Pham Van Dong
Author:
Date: 1954-07-27
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 26th: According to a TASS report from Berlin: The German News Agency reported that the Deputy Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Pham Van Dong, paid a visit to the Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Grotewohl, on the 24th. The Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, Bolz, participated in the meeting.
    Grotewohl congratulated Pham Van Dong on the signing of the Indochina armistice agreement in Geneva.
    They discussed economic issues during the meeting. Grotewohl emphasized that the Vietnamese people can receive assistance from the German Democratic Republic in restoring their country.
    On the same day, the Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, Bolz, hosted a banquet in honor of the Deputy Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Pham Van Dong, and the members of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam delegation attending the Geneva Conference.
    The banquet took place in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 范文同副总理拜会格罗提渥总理  博尔茨副总理兼外长招待范文同副总理

1954-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

    范文同副总理拜会格罗提渥总理
    博尔茨副总理兼外长招待范文同副总理
   【新华社二十六日讯】据塔斯社柏林讯：德意志通讯社报道，越南民主共和国副总理范文同在二十四日拜会德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥。德意志民主共和国副总理兼外交部部长博尔茨参加了会谈。
    格罗提渥向范文同祝贺在日内瓦签订了印度支那的停战协定。
    他们在会谈中讨论了经济问题。格罗提渥强调指出，越南人民在恢复他们的国家中可以得到德意志民主共和国的援助。
    同一天，德意志民主共和国副总理兼外交部部长博尔茨设宴招待越南民主共和国副总理范文同和参加日内瓦会议的越南民主共和国代表团人员。
    宴会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
