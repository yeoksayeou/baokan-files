Title: Molotov and Mendès-France Meet
Author:
Date: 1954-07-12
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 11th: On July 10th, the First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, V.M. Molotov, and the French Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Mendès-France, who arrived here that afternoon to attend the Geneva Conference, held a meeting.
    The meeting was held at the headquarters of the Soviet delegation, where Molotov hosted a banquet in honor of Mendès-France.
    The French side attending the banquet included: French Ambassador to Switzerland, Jean Chauvel; Director of Political Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Guy de La Tournelle; and Chief of Staff of the Prime Minister's Office, Georges Boris.
    The Soviet side attending the banquet included: V.V. Kuznetsov, S.A. Vinogradov, F.F. Molochkov, K.V. Novikov, and A.A. Lavrishchev.

### 莫洛托夫和孟戴斯—弗朗斯会谈

1954-07-12
第1版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫和孟戴斯—弗朗斯会谈
    【新华社日内瓦十一日电】七月十日，苏联部长会议第一副主席兼苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫和当天下午到达这里出席日内瓦会议的法国总理兼外交部部长孟戴斯—弗朗斯举行了一次会谈。
    会谈在苏联代表团总部举行，莫洛托夫在那里设宴招待孟戴斯—弗朗斯。
    法国方面出席宴会的还有：法国驻瑞士大使让·萧维尔、外交部政治司司长居伊·德拉杜纳尔和总理办公厅主任乔治·波里斯。
    苏联方面出席宴会的还有：瓦·瓦·库兹湼佐夫、斯·阿·维诺格拉多夫、弗·弗·莫洛奇科夫、基·维·诺维科夫和阿·阿·拉夫里谢夫。
