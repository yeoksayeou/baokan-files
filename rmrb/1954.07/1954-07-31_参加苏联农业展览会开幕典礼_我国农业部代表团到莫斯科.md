Title: Chinese Agricultural Delegation Arrives in Moscow to Attend Opening Ceremony of Soviet Agricultural Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1954-07-31
Page: 1

    Participating in the Opening Ceremony of the Soviet Agricultural Exhibition
    Chinese Agricultural Delegation Arrives in Moscow
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 29th] The first group of members of the Chinese Ministry of Agriculture delegation, invited by the Soviet Ministry of Agriculture to participate in the opening ceremony of the Soviet Agricultural Exhibition, arrived in Moscow by plane on the afternoon of the 28th, led by Li Shucheng, Minister of Agriculture of the Central People's Government. They were welcomed at the airport by Matskevich, Deputy Minister of the Soviet Ministry of Agriculture, and Li Tao, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, and others.

### 参加苏联农业展览会开幕典礼  我国农业部代表团到莫斯科

1954-07-31
第1版()
专栏：

    参加苏联农业展览会开幕典礼
    我国农业部代表团到莫斯科
    【新华社莫斯科二十九日电】应苏联农业部的邀请参加苏联农业展览会开幕典礼的我国农业部代表团的第一批人员，在中央人民政府农业部部长李书城率领下，于二十八日下午乘飞机抵达莫斯科。到机场欢迎的有苏联农业部副部长马茨凯维奇及我国驻苏联大使馆参赞李滔等人。
