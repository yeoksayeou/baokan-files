Title: Vietnam News Agency Reports on the Progress of Military Meetings between Vietnam and France
Author:
Date: 1954-07-06
Page: 1
Vietnam News Agency

Reports on the Progress of Military Meetings between Vietnam and France

[Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, 5th dispatch] According to the Vietnam News Agency, the local military conference of representatives from the General Command of the Vietnam People's Army and the General Command of the French Union Forces in Indochina began on the 4th in Trung Gia, located on Highway 3, south of Thai Nguyen city. The conference site is located within a demilitarized zone, and all military activities have ceased within a radius of seven kilometers around the site.

The delegation from the General Command of the Vietnam People's Army, led by Major General Van Tien Dung, included Colonel Song Hao, Colonel Le Quang Dao, Lieutenant Colonel Nguyen Van Long, and Lieutenant Colonel Le Minh. The delegation from the General Command of the French Union Forces in Indochina consisted of eight officers, with Colonel Lannoy serving as the chief representative.

Many Vietnamese journalists, film reporters, and foreign journalists and photographers were present at the opening session of the military conference.

The military conference opened at 8:00 AM. After exchanging credentials, the chief representatives of both delegations introduced their members. Major General Van Tien Dung spoke first. He pointed out the important tasks of the local military conference and stated that the delegation from the General Command of the Vietnam People's Army would make its best efforts not only to enable the conference to fulfill the agreements reached by both sides in Geneva but also to put forward useful suggestions to the Geneva Conference and to resolve locally those issues that both sides deem necessary to raise in order to quickly implement a ceasefire and armistice and restore peace in Indochina.

Colonel Lannoy, the chief representative of the delegation from the General Command of the French Union Forces in Indochina, then spoke. In his speech, he also welcomed the hope for peace through consultation.

The opening session concluded in a good atmosphere at 9:00 AM. The chief representatives of both delegations then held an hour-long meeting to discuss the tasks, powers, and content of the local military conference. The meeting continued at 2:00 PM on the 4th to discuss the agenda.

### 越南通讯社  报道越法双方军事会议情况

1954-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

    越南通讯社
    报道越法双方军事会议情况
    【新华社五日讯】据越南通讯社北越五日讯：越南人民军和印度支那法兰西联邦部队双方总司令部代表团的当地军事会议四日在中亦开始举行。中亦位于第三号公路上，在太原城以南。会议场所在一个非军事区内，在会议场所周围半径七公里的范围内，一切军事活动都停止了。
    以文进勇少将为首的越南人民军总司令部代表团包括了双豪上校、黎光道上校、阮文龙中校和黎明中校。印度支那法兰西联邦部队总司令部代表团由八名军官组成。首席代表是兰努约上校。
    在军事会议的开幕会议上有许多越南的记者和电影记者以及外国的记者或摄影记者。
    上午八时，军事会议开幕。在交换资格证书以后，双方代表团首席代表介绍了他们的团员。第一个发言的是文进勇少将。他指出当地军事会议的重要任务，并表示越南人民军总司令部代表团将尽最大努力来不仅使会议能够履行双方在日内瓦达成的协议，而且能向日内瓦会议提出有益的建议，并在当地解决那些双方认为要迅速实行停火和停战并恢复印度支那和平就必须提出的问题。
    印度支那法兰西联邦部队总司令部代表团首席代表兰努约上校接着发言。他在发言中也对通过协商实现和平的希望表示欢迎。
    开幕会议在上午九时在良好的气氛中结束。双方代表团首席代表然后举行了一小时的会议，来讨论当地军事会议的任务、权限和内容。四日下午二时继续举行会议讨论议程。
