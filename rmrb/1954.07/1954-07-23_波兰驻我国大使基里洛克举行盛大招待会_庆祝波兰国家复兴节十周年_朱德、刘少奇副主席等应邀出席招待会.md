Title: Polish Ambassador to China, Kiriloek, Holds Grand Reception Celebrating the Tenth Anniversary of the Polish National Day of Revival; Vice Chairmen Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, and Others Attend Reception Upon Invitation
Author:
Date: 1954-07-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd: Polish Ambassador to China, Kiriloek, held a grand reception on the evening of July 22nd to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the Polish National Day of Revival.

Invited to attend the reception were: Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De and Liu Shaoqi, Vice Premiers of the Government Administration Council Chen Yun and Deng Xiaoping, President of the Supreme People's Court Shen Junru, Members of the Central People's Government Committee Shen Yanbing, Cai Tingkai, Xi Zhongxun, Peng Zemin, Members of the Government Administration Council Xie Juezai, Zeng Shan, Chen Shaoxian, Shao Lizi, Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council Lu Dingyi, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Director of the General Political Department Gan Siqi, Deputy Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Wu Xiuquan, Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, as well as responsible persons from various commissions, ministries, committees, institutes, administrations, and banks under the Central People's Government Administration Council, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, responsible persons from various people's organizations, responsible persons from various organs and people's organizations in Beijing, and figures from the capital's science, education, literature, art, and news circles.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in China were also invited to attend the reception, including: Burmese Ambassador U Law Maung, Indian Ambassador N. Raghavan, Swedish Ambassador Wistrand, Romanian Ambassador Teodor Rudenco, Mongolian Ambassador Ochirbat, Indonesian Ambassador Mononutu, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic Koenig, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Choi Il, Danish Minister গ্রেভে, Soviet Chargé d'affaires ad interim Hua Sike, Pakistani Chargé d'affaires ad interim Sultan Muhammad Khan, Vietnamese Chargé d'affaires ad interim Zhou Liang, Czechoslovak Chargé d'affaires ad interim Smíd, Hungarian Chargé d'affaires ad interim Czitor, Bulgarian Chargé d'affaires ad interim Grigorov, Finnish Chargé d'affaires ad interim Seppälä, Swiss Chargé d'affaires ad interim Cuttat, British Chargé d'affaires Tuvilliam, Dutch Negotiating Representative Smith, Norwegian Negotiating Representative Gimode, as well as diplomatic officials from various countries' embassies in China.

A total of more than eight hundred people attended the reception.

At the reception, Polish Ambassador to China Kiriloek and Vice Chairman of our Central People's Government Zhu De spoke successively, mutually wishing the great friendship between the people of China and Poland would develop and consolidate daily, wishing the people of both countries greater achievements in their construction undertakings, and wishing the forces of the world peace, democracy, and socialism camp headed by the Soviet Union would grow stronger daily.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere. During the event, guests and hosts continuously toasted to the friendship between the people of Poland and China, to the health of the leaders of the two countries, and to the growing strength of the peaceful, democratic, and socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union.

At the reception, Chinese artists performed songs and dances, and a dance party was held at the end.

### 波兰驻我国大使基里洛克举行盛大招待会  庆祝波兰国家复兴节十周年  朱德、刘少奇副主席等应邀出席招待会

1954-07-23
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰驻我国大使基里洛克举行盛大招待会
    庆祝波兰国家复兴节十周年
    朱德、刘少奇副主席等应邀出席招待会
    【新华社二十二日讯】波兰人民共和国驻我国大使基里洛克二十二日晚举行盛大招待会，庆祝波兰人民共和国国家复兴节十周年。
    应邀出席招待会的有：中央人民政府副主席朱德、刘少奇，政务院副总理陈云、邓小平，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，中央人民政府委员会委员沈雁冰、蔡廷锴、习仲勋、彭泽民，政务院政务委员谢觉哉、曾山、陈劭先、邵力子，政务院文化教育委员会副主任陆定一，人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长张宗逊，总政治部副主任甘泗淇，外交部副部长章汉夫、伍修权，北京市人民政府市长彭真，以及中央人民政府政务院所属各委、部、会、院、署、行的负责人，中国人民解放军高级将领，各人民团体的负责人，北京市所属各机关、各人民团体的负责人，首都科学、教育、文学、艺术、新闻界人士等。
    应邀出席招待会的各国驻我国外交使节有：缅甸大使吴拉茂，印度大使赖嘉文，瑞典大使魏斯特朗，罗马尼亚大使郭佐文，蒙古大使奥其尔巴特，印尼大使莫诺努图，德意志民主共和国大使柯尼希，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使崔一，丹麦公使格瑞杰生，苏联临时代办华司考，巴基斯坦临时代办苏尔唐·穆罕默德·汗，越南民主共和国临时代办周亮，捷克斯洛伐克临时代办石密茨，匈牙利临时代办察托尔道伊，保加利亚临时代办格利郭洛夫，芬兰临时代办谢列克，瑞士临时代办崔礼，英国代办杜维廉，荷兰谈判代表司曼，挪威谈判代表季茂登，以及各国驻我国使馆的外交官员。
    出席招待会的共有八百多人。
    招待会上，波兰人民共和国驻我国大使基里洛克和我中央人民政府副主席朱德相继讲话，互祝波中两国人民的伟大友谊日益发展和巩固，祝两国人民在建设事业中取得更大的成就，并祝以苏联为首的世界和平民主社会主义阵营的力量日益强大。
    招待会在热烈和友好的空气中进行。席间，宾主不断为波、中两国人民的友谊、为两国人民的领袖的健康、为以苏联为首的和平民主社会主义阵营的日益强大而干杯。
    招待会上，中国的文艺工作者表演了歌舞，最后并举行了舞会。
