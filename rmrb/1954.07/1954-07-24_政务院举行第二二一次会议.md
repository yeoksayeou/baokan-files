Title: Government Administration Council Holds 221st Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1954-07-24
Page: 2

The 221st meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 9th. The meeting heard and approved the report by Zeng Zhaolun, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Higher Education of the Central People's Government, on the National Conference on Secondary Specialized Education Administration, adopted the decision on improving secondary specialized education, and approved the articles of association for secondary specialized schools. The meeting also heard and approved the reports by Huang Songling, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Higher Education, on the National Comprehensive University Conference, the National Higher Finance and Economics Education Conference, the Teaching Experience Discussion Conference of the Renmin University of China, and the National Political and Legal Education Conference. In addition, the meeting approved various appointment and removal proposals. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 政务院举行第二二一次会议

1954-07-24
第2版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第二二一次会议
    中央人民政府政务院第二百二十一次政务会议在九日下午举行。会议听了和批准了中央人民政府高等教育部副部长曾昭抡所作的关于全国中等专业教育行政会议的报告，通过了关于改进中等专业教育的决定，批准了中等专业学校章程。会议并听了和批准了高等教育部副部长黄松龄所作的关于全国综合大学会议、全国高等财经教育会议、中国人民大学教学经验讨论会、全国政法教育会议的报告。此外，会议通过了各项任免案。（新华社）
