Title: Chinese and U.S. Delegations Continue Contact on the Issue of Nationals

Author:

Date: 1954-07-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 21st: The delegations of the People's Republic of China and the United States of America held another meeting at 10:00 AM today to discuss the issue of nationals of both countries residing in each other's territories.

    Pu Shan, representing the Chinese delegation, submitted to the U.S. side a list of six American nationals in China and pointed out that the Chinese government had approved their applications to leave the country. He then requested the U.S. side to provide information on Chinese nationals and students in the United States and inquired about the U.S. side's opinion on the Chinese side's previous proposal to have the interests of nationals of both sides represented by the embassies of third countries.

    Jenkins, representing the U.S. delegation, expressed gratitude for the Chinese side's action in approving the exit applications of the six American nationals but did not provide any new information regarding Chinese nationals and students in the United States. Jenkins continued to refuse to consider the Chinese side's proposal.

### 我国代表团和美国代表团  继续就侨民问题进行接触

1954-07-23
第3版()
专栏：

    我国代表团和美国代表团
    继续就侨民问题进行接触
    【新华社日内瓦二十一日电】中华人民共和国代表团方面和美国代表团方面，在二十一日上午十时就两国在对方境内的侨民问题进行了又一次的接触。
    中国代表团出席人员浦山向美方提交了六名在华美侨的名单，并指出这六名美侨所提出的出境申请，已获得中国政府的批准。他接着要求美方提供在美国的中国侨民和留学生的情况，并询问美方对于中国方面过去所提出的请第三国使节代管双方侨民的利益的建议的意见。
    美国代表团出席人员詹金斯对中国方面批准六名美侨出境申请的行动表示感谢，但是没有提供什么关于在美国的中国侨民和留学生的新情况。詹金斯对中国方面的建议依然拒绝加以考虑。
