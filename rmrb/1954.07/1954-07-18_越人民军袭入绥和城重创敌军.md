Title: Viet People's Army Raids Tuy Hoa City, Inflicting Heavy Losses on Enemy
Author:
Date: 1954-07-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 17th: Vietnam News Agency reported on the 16th that the Vietnamese People's Army, on the night of the 6th and the early morning of the 7th, briefly raided the city of Tuy Hoa in central Vietnam, attacking the enemy's 11th Mobile Regiment, inflicting heavy casualties on the enemy and destroying three of the enemy's ammunition depots.

### 越人民军袭入绥和城重创敌军

1954-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    越人民军袭入绥和城重创敌军
    【新华社十七日讯】据越南通讯社十六日讯：越南人民军在六日晚和七日清晨，一度袭入中越的绥和城，袭击敌人第十一机动团，使敌军遭到重大伤亡，并摧毁敌军军火库三处。
