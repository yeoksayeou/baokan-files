Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Arrives in Geneva and Issues Statement, Visits Molotov and Pham Van Dong, and Meets with Menon
Author:
Date: 1954-07-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 12th -- Zhou Enlai, Chief Representative of the Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Geneva Conference, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs, returned to Geneva at 3:40 pm on the 12th. He was accompanied by advisors to the Chinese delegation, Shi Zhe, Qiao Guanhua, and Wang Zhuoru. Hoang Van Hoan, representative of the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, arrived on the same plane.

Welcoming him at the airport were: Molotov, Chief Representative of the Soviet delegation, Minister of Foreign Affairs; Kuznetsov, Representative, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Lavrishchev, and Novikov.

Also welcoming him at the airport were: Pham Van Dong, Chief Representative of the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs, and representative Phan Anh.

Also welcoming him at the airport was Menon, Indian Representative to the United Nations.

Zhang Wentian, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs and representative of the Chinese delegation, Li Kenong, Secretary-General of the delegation Wang Bingnan, advisors Lei Renmin, Chen Jiakang, Huang Hua, Huan Xiang, Wu Lengxi, and Lei Yingfu; Feng Xuan, Chinese Envoy to Switzerland, Wen Pengjiu, Chinese Consul General in Geneva, and other personnel from the Embassy and Consulate General, all welcomed him at the airport.

Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai issued the following statement at the airport:

	I am pleased to return to this beautiful, peaceful city, which has special significance for easing world tensions.

	The Geneva Conference on the restoration of peace in Indochina is about to enter an important stage with the renewed participation of the Foreign Ministers. I believe that with the spirit of seeking peace and reconciliation on the part of all parties concerned, the Geneva Conference can quickly complete the important task of restoring peace in Indochina. I hope everyone will work towards this goal.
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 13th -- Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, visited Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov at 7:00 pm on the 12th.

Accompanying Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai were Deputy Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, Li Kenong, and Shi Zhe, advisor to the Chinese delegation to the Geneva Conference. Present on the Soviet side was Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Kuznetsov.
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 13th -- Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, visited Pham Van Dong, Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, at 9:00 pm on the 12th.

Accompanying Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai were Deputy Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian and Li Kenong. Present on the Vietnamese side were Phan Anh, Tran Cong Tuong, and Hoang Van Hoan, representatives of the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Geneva Conference.
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 13th -- Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, met with Menon, Indian Representative to the United Nations, at 12:30 pm on the 13th.

### 周恩来总理到日内瓦并发表声明  分别访晤莫洛托夫和范文同并接见梅农

1954-07-14
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理到日内瓦并发表声明
    分别访晤莫洛托夫和范文同并接见梅农
    【新华社日内瓦十二日电】中华人民共和国出席日内瓦会议代表团首席代表、总理兼外交部部长周恩来，在十二日下午三时四十分回到日内瓦。随机前来的有中国代表团顾问师哲、乔冠华、王倬如。同机前来的有越南民主共和国代表团代表黄文欢。
    到机场欢迎的有：苏联代表团首席代表、外交部部长莫洛托夫，代表、外交部副部长库兹涅佐夫，拉夫里谢夫，诺维科夫。
    到机场欢迎的还有：越南民主共和国代表团首席代表、副总理兼代外长范文同，及代表潘英。
    到机场欢迎的还有：印度驻联合国代表梅农。
    中国代表团代表、外交部副部长张闻天、李克农，代表团秘书长王炳南，顾问雷任民、陈家康、黄华、宦乡、吴冷西、雷英夫；中国驻瑞士公使冯铉，中国驻日内瓦总领事温朋久，以及公使馆、总领事馆其他人员，都到机场欢迎。
    周恩来总理兼外长在机场发表声明如下：
    当我重新回到这个美丽的、和平的、并对缓和世界紧张局势具有特别意义的城市的时候，我感到高兴。
    关于恢复印度支那和平问题的日内瓦会议即将在外交部长们的重新参加下进入重要阶段。我相信，在有关各方具有谋求和平的和解精神之下，日内瓦会议是可以迅速完成恢复印度支那和平的重大任务的。我希望大家能为此努力。
   【新华社日内瓦十三日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来在十二日下午七时访晤苏联外交部部长莫洛托夫。
    随同周恩来外长前往的有外交部副部长张闻天、李克农和出席日内瓦会议的中国代表团顾问师哲。苏联方面在座的有苏联外交部副部长库兹涅佐夫。
  【新华社日内瓦十三日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来在十二日下午九时访晤越南民主共和国代理外交部部长范文同。
    随同周恩来外长前往的有外交部副部长张闻天、李克农。越南方面在座的有越南民主共和国出席日内瓦会议的代表团代表潘英、陈公祥、黄文欢。
   【新华社日内瓦十三日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来在十三日午间十二时三十分接见印度驻联合国代表梅农。
