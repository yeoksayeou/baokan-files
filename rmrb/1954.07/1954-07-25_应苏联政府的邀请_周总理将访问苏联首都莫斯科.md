Title: At the Invitation of the Soviet Government, Premier Zhou Will Visit Moscow, the Capital of the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1954-07-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 24th. The Premier of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, at the invitation of the Soviet government, has decided to visit Moscow, the capital of the Soviet Union, for a two-day stay after visiting Berlin, the capital of the German Democratic Republic, and Warsaw, the capital of the Polish People's Republic.

### 应苏联政府的邀请  周总理将访问苏联首都莫斯科

1954-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

    应苏联政府的邀请
    周总理将访问苏联首都莫斯科
   【新华社柏林二十四日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府总理周恩来，受到苏联政府的邀请，决定在访问德意志民主共和国首都柏林和波兰人民共和国首都华沙以后，访问苏联首都莫斯科，作两日逗留。
