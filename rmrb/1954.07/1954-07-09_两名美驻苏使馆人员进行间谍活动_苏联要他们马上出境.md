Title: Two US Embassy Personnel Engage in Espionage; Soviet Union Orders Immediate Departure

Author:

Date: 1954-07-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 6th: Soviet authorities have confirmed that Lieutenant Colonel Fetscher, Deputy Military Attaché of the US Embassy in the Soviet Union, and Major McKinney, Deputy Air Attaché, have been engaging in espionage activities during their stay in the Soviet Union, conducting activities inconsistent with their diplomatic status. Therefore, Lieutenant Colonel Fetscher and Major McKinney have been declared personae non gratae. The Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs has requested the US Embassy in the Soviet Union to take measures to ensure their immediate departure from the Soviet Union.

### 两名美驻苏使馆人员进行间谍活动  苏联要他们马上出境

1954-07-09
第4版()
专栏：

    两名美驻苏使馆人员进行间谍活动
    苏联要他们马上出境
   【新华社七日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科六日讯：苏联有关机关已经证实，美国驻苏联大使馆陆军副武官费契林中校和空军副武官麦金尼少校利用他们居留在苏联的机会从事间谍工作，进行了和他们的外交身份不相称的活动。因此，费契林中校和麦金尼少校已经被宣布为不受欢迎的人。苏联外交部已经要求美国驻苏联大使馆采取措施，要他们立即离开苏联。
