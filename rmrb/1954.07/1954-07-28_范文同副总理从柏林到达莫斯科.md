Title: Vice Premier Pham Van Dong Arrives in Moscow from Berlin
Author:
Date: 1954-07-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 27th: Pham Van Dong, Chief Representative of the Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Geneva Conference and Vice Premier and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, left Berlin on the morning of the 26th to return home.

Those who saw him off at the airport included: Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Grotewohl; Deputy Prime Ministers Nuschke, Loch, and Schulz; Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Bolz; members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Matern, Hildmann, and Oesner; candidate member of the Political Bureau, Honecker; Chairman of the National Council of the National Front of Democratic Germany, Correns; Mayor of Greater Berlin, Ebert; and heads of other government departments.

Also at the airport to see him off were: Deputy High Commissioner of the Soviet Union in Germany and Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in the German Democratic Republic, Miroshnichenko, and other personnel from the High Commissioner's Office.

Also at the airport to see him off were: Chinese Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, Ji Pengfei, and other foreign envoys stationed in Germany.

Xinhua News Agency, July 27th: According to TASS, Moscow, July 26th: The delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Geneva Conference, led by Vice Premier Pham Van Dong, arrived in Moscow on July 26th.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included: First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Molotov; Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Zorin; Director of the Southeast Asia Department of the Soviet Foreign Ministry, Novikov; Director of the First European Department of the Soviet Foreign Ministry, Lavrishev; Chairman of the Moscow Soviet, Yasnov; Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Foreign Ministry, Kapustin; and Acting Commander of the Moscow City Defense Command, Colonel Kovalev, among others.

Also at the airport to welcome the delegation were personnel from the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, headed by Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary Nguyen Luong Bang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the People's Republic of China, Wen Ning, and Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Mongolian People's Republic, Tobo.

### 范文同副总理从柏林到达莫斯科

1954-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

    范文同副总理从柏林到达莫斯科
    【新华社柏林二十七日电】出席日内瓦会议的越南民主共和国代表团首席代表、越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外交部部长范文同一行于二十六日上午九时离柏林返国。
    到机场欢送的有：德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥，副总理努舍克、洛赫、舒尔茨，副总理兼外交部部长博尔茨，德国统一社会党中央政治局委员马特恩、希德万、厄斯纳，政治局候补委员昂纳克，民主德国全国阵线全国委员会主席柯伦斯，大柏林市长艾柏特以及政府其他各部门的负责人。
    到机场欢送的还有：苏联驻德副高级专员兼苏联驻德意志民主共和国大使馆临时代办米罗什尼钦科和高级专员公署其他人员。
    到机场欢送的还有：中国驻德意志民主共和国大使姬鹏飞及其他外国驻德使节。
   【新华社二十七日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科二十六日讯：出席日内瓦会议的越南民主共和国代表团在副总理范文同率领下，在七月二十六日到达莫斯科。
    到机场欢迎的有苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部长莫洛托夫、苏联外交部副部长佐林、苏联外交部东南亚司司长诺维科夫、苏联外交部第一欧洲司司长拉夫里谢夫、莫斯科市苏维埃主席雅斯诺夫、苏联外交部交际司副司长卡普斯金、莫斯科市城防司令部代理司令科瓦列夫上校等人。
    到机场欢迎代表团的还有以特命全权大使阮良朋为首的越南民主共和国大使馆人员、中华人民共和国临时代办温宁和蒙古人民共和国临时代办托博。
