Title: All-China Youth Federation Sends Telegram to Vietnam Youth Federation
Author:
Date: 1954-07-28
Page: 2

    All-China Youth Federation Sends Telegram to Vietnam Youth Federation
    To the Vietnam Youth Federation:
    The Chinese youth warmly welcome the agreement reached at the Geneva Conference on the issues of ceasefire and political matters in Indochina. We extend our heartfelt and warm congratulations to the fraternal Vietnamese people and youth.
    The agreement on restoring peace in Indochina is the result of the long-term and arduous struggle of the heroic Vietnamese people and youth. We believe that the Vietnamese people and youth, who have achieved a great victory, will continue to achieve greater victories in the cause of realizing the peace, unification, independence, and democracy of their motherland under the leadership of the Vietnam Workers' Party and President Ho Chi Minh.
    The Chinese youth have always been concerned with and supported the just struggle of the Vietnamese people and youth. We will certainly continue to work together with the Vietnamese youth and the youth of the world to strive for the thorough implementation of the Geneva Conference agreements and to safeguard peace in Asia and the world!
    All-China Youth Federation
            July 26th

### 中华全国民主青年联合会电越南青年联盟

1954-07-28
第2版()
专栏：

    中华全国民主青年联合会电越南青年联盟越南青年联盟：
    中国青年热烈欢迎日内瓦会议就印度支那停战问题和政治问题达成协议，我们向兄弟般的越南人民和青年致以衷心的热烈的祝贺。
    恢复印度支那和平问题的达成协议，是英勇的越南人民和青年长期艰苦奋斗的结果。我们相信：取得伟大胜利的越南人民和青年，一定会在越南劳动党和胡志明主席的领导下，继续在实现祖国的和平、统一、独立和民主的事业中，获得更大的胜利。
    中国青年一向关心与支持越南人民和青年的正义斗争，我们一定继续和越南青年以及全世界青年一起，为争取日内瓦会议协议的彻底实现，为保卫亚洲与世界和平而努力！中华全国民主青年联合会
            七月二十六日
