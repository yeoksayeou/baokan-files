Title: People's Vote in the German Democratic Republic Concluded Successfully; The Vast Majority of Voters Approved the Conclusion of a Peace Treaty and the Withdrawal of Foreign Troops
Author:
Date: 1954-07-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 2nd: The people's vote held in the German Democratic Republic from June 27th to 29th has concluded successfully.

More than eight hundred polling stations in the democratic sector of Berlin and polling stations across the country simultaneously counted ballots at 8 PM on June 29th. The voting results show that the vast majority of the people of the German Democratic Republic support the conclusion of a peace treaty and the withdrawal of foreign occupying forces. According to a report by the German News Agency, preliminary statistics of the voting results are as follows: 93.5% of all voters voted in favor of a peace treaty and the withdrawal of foreign occupying forces, while 6.5% voted in favor of the "European Defense Community" treaty and allowing the occupying forces to continue to be stationed for fifty years. Of the 13,588,397 people in the country eligible to vote, 13,397,640 participated in the vote.

### 德意志民主共和国人民投票已经胜利结束  绝大多数投票人赞成缔结和约和撤退外国军队

1954-07-04
第4版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国人民投票已经胜利结束
    绝大多数投票人赞成缔结和约和撤退外国军队
    【新华社柏林二日电】德意志民主共和国从六月二十七日到二十九日举行的人民投票已经胜利结束。
    柏林民主区八百多个投票站以及全国各地的投票站都在六月二十九日晚八时同时开票。投票结果表明：德意志民主共和国的人民绝大多数赞成缔结和约和撤退外国占领军。据德意志通讯社消息，投票结果初步统计如下：投票赞成和约和撤退外国占领军的占全体投票人的百分之九十三点五，赞成“欧洲防务集团”条约以及让占领军继续留驻五十年的占百分之六点五。全国有投票资格的人有一千三百五十八万八千三百九十七人，参加投票的有一千三百三十九万七千六百四十人。
