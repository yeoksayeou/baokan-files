Title: Nam Il, Foreign Minister, Reports on Geneva Conference Situation in Chongjin City
Author:
Date: 1954-07-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 17th: Nam Il, Foreign Minister and Chief Delegate of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea delegation to the Geneva Conference, reported on the work of the Korean delegation at the Geneva Conference on the 11th in Chongjin City, North Hamgyong Province, to more than 1,000 representatives of workers from various factories and enterprises, as well as heads of Party and government organs and social organizations. On the 12th and 13th, delegates of the delegation, including Paek Nam-un and advisor Chang Chun-san, also gave reports in various districts of Pyongyang and in factories, government offices, and schools.

In his report, Foreign Minister Nam Il described in detail the process of the Geneva Conference's discussion on the Korean issue and the consistent efforts made by the delegations of Korea, the Soviet Union, and China to peacefully resolve the Korean issue. He vehemently denounced the United States and the Syngman Rhee clique for trampling on the common aspirations of peace-loving people around the world, unreasonably rejecting the proposals and suggestions of the delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and sabotaging the discussion on the Korean issue. He pointed out that the United States and the Syngman Rhee clique must bear full responsibility for this.

Foreign Minister Nam Il emphasized that in order to achieve the peaceful reunification of our motherland as soon as possible, all the Korean people must unite more closely around the Korean Workers' Party, the Republic's government, and Marshal Kim Il-sung, and strive for the victorious completion of the Three-Year Plan for the post-war rehabilitation and development of the people's economy.

The people who attended the lectures expressed great anger at the criminal acts of the United States and the Syngman Rhee clique in sabotaging the discussion on the Korean issue at the Geneva Conference. They expressed their determination to actively participate in the work of post-war reconstruction and to fight to the end for the great cause of the peaceful reunification of the motherland.

### 南日外务相在清津市报告日内瓦会议情况

1954-07-18
第4版()
专栏：

    南日外务相在清津市报告日内瓦会议情况
    【新华社平壤十七日电】参加日内瓦会议的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国代表团首席代表南日外务相，十一日在咸镜北道清津市向一千多名各工厂企业的工人代表和党政机关、社会团体的负责人报告了朝鲜代表团在日内瓦会议上进行的工作。十二日和十三日，代表团代表白南云、顾问张春山等在平壤市各区和工厂、机关、学校里也分别作了报告。
    南日外务相在报告中详细地叙述了日内瓦会议关于朝鲜问题讨论的过程和朝、苏、中三国代表团为和平解决朝鲜问题而作的始终如一的努力。他痛斥美国和李承晚集团蹂躏全世界爱好和平人民的共同愿望，毫无道理地拒绝朝鲜民主主义人民共和国代表团的方案和建议，破坏了关于朝鲜问题的讨论。他指出：美国和李承晚集团对此必须承担全部责任。
    南日外务相强调说：为了早日实现我们祖国的和平统一，全体朝鲜人民要更加紧密地团结在朝鲜劳动党、共和国政府和金日成元帅的周围，争取战后人民经济恢复发展三年计划的胜利完成。
    参加讲演会的人们，都对美国和李承晚集团破坏日内瓦会议关于朝鲜问题讨论的罪恶行为，表示极大愤怒。他们纷纷表示要积极参加战后重建的工作，为和平统一祖国的伟大事业而奋斗到底。
