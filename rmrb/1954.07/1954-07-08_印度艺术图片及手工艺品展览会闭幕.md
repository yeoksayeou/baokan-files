Title: Indian Art Pictures and Handicraft Exhibition Closes

Author: 
Date: 1954-07-08
Page: 3

    Indian Art Pictures and Handicraft Exhibition Closes
    The "Indian Art Pictures and Handicraft Exhibition" held in Beijing by the Sino-Indian Friendship Association closed on the 6th.
    The exhibition lasted for a total of twenty-two days. The many exquisite artworks on display attracted great attention from the audience, with more than 42,400 people visiting the exhibition. Many people even watched it several times in a row.
    Soong Ching Ling, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government, visited the exhibition. Prominent figures from all walks of life in China also visited the exhibition, including representatives of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association delegation who had visited India, as well as famous figures in the capital's art circles.
    The audience showed great appreciation for the artistic works of the Indian people. One audience member wrote in the opinion book: "From the huge and magnificent palace architecture (pictures) to the tiny and exquisite ivory carvings, one can see the wisdom, perseverance, and high artistic skills of the Indian people."
    Many viewers congratulated in the opinion book: The people of China and India join hands to work together to safeguard the culture of the people, defend the fruits of their labor, and defend peace. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 印度艺术图片及手工艺品展览会闭幕

1954-07-08
第3版()
专栏：

    印度艺术图片及手工艺品展览会闭幕
    中印友好协会在北京举办的“印度艺术图片及手工艺品展览会”已于六日闭幕。
    展览会共计展出二十二天。展出的许多精美的艺术品，吸引了观众的很大的注意，有四万二千四百多人参观了展览会。有很多人还连续看了几次。
    中央人民政府副主席宋庆龄曾参观了展览会。参观展览会的还有我国社会各界著名人士，其中包括曾经访问印度的中印友好协会代表团代表们以及首都艺术界的著名人士。
    观众对印度人民的艺术作品都非常喜爱。一个观众在意见簿上写道：“从巨大的华美的宫殿建筑（图片）直到微小的精细的象牙雕刻，都可以看出印度人民的智慧、毅力和高度的艺术技巧。”
    很多观众都在意见簿上祝贺：中印两国人民携起手来，共同为维护人民的文化而努力，保卫自己劳动的果实，保卫和平。（新华社）
