Title: All-China Women's Federation Sends a Telegram to the Vietnam Women's Union
Author: 
Date: 1954-07-30
Page: 2

    All-China Women's Federation Sends a Telegram to the Vietnam Women's Union
    The All-China Democratic Women's Federation sent a telegram to the Vietnam Women's Union, congratulating them on the agreement reached on the issue of restoring peace in Indochina. The telegram said:
    The Geneva Conference has reached an agreement on the ceasefire and restoration of peace in Indochina. On behalf of Chinese women, I would like to extend our heartfelt and warm congratulations to you. This is the result of the long and heroic struggle of the people and women of Indochina for national independence, and a great victory for the people who love peace all over the world in defending world peace. This victory greatly contributes to ensuring the peaceful life of the people, women and children of Indochina and all of Asia and the world. Chinese women will work with Vietnamese sisters to strive for the thorough implementation of this agreement and to safeguard and consolidate peace in Asia and the world. We wish you new victories in your just struggle to consolidate peace, achieve unity, and achieve national independence and democracy.
                                  (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中华全国民主妇联致电越南妇女联合会

1954-07-30
第2版()
专栏：

    中华全国民主妇联致电越南妇女联合会
    中华全国民主妇女联合会致电越南妇女联合会，祝贺印度支那恢复和平问题达成协议。贺电说：
    日内瓦会议已就印度支那停战和恢复和平问题达成协议，谨代表中国妇女向您们致以衷心的热烈的祝贺。这是印度支那人民和妇女为民族独立进行长期英勇斗争的结果，是全世界爱好和平的人民保卫世界和平事业中的伟大胜利。这一胜利大大有助于保障印度支那和全亚洲、全世界人民和妇女、儿童的和平生活。中国妇女将与越南姐妹一道，为争取这一协议的彻底实现，维护和巩固亚洲及世界和平而奋斗。祝您们为巩固和平、实现统一、在全国取得独立和民主的正义斗争中获得新的胜利。
                                  （新华社）
