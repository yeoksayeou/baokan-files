Title: Soviet Union Holds Open Competition for Design of Pantheon for Soviet Greats
Author:
Date: 1954-07-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 5th: According to TASS, Moscow, July 4th: The State Construction Committee of the Soviet Union's Council of Ministers and the Executive Committee of the Moscow City Soviet of Working People's Deputies, acting on instructions from the Soviet Union's Council of Ministers, have announced an open competition to solicit the best designs for a Pantheon for Soviet Greats. This Pantheon will be built in Moscow to forever commemorate the great leaders V.I. Lenin and J.V. Stalin, and to entomb the prominent figures of the Communist Party and the Soviet state interred in Red Square, at the Kremlin Wall.

The Pantheon will be built in the southwestern district of Moscow, on Lenin Hills, three and a half kilometers south of the new Moscow University buildings. According to the competition plan and conditions, it will be a simple, magnificent, and grand building with a volume of 80,000 cubic meters. The Pantheon will be constructed and decorated with high-quality and durable materials.

A memorial park will be developed around the Pantheon, utilizing the natural conditions of the area.

The Pantheon's design should include:

    A large hall housing a crystal sarcophagus containing Lenin's remains and a crystal sarcophagus containing Stalin's remains.

    One or more halls housing the urns and remains of prominent figures of the Communist Party and the Soviet state moved from beneath the Kremlin Wall in Red Square.

The competition will conclude on November 1st. Substantial prizes will be awarded to the best Pantheon designs submitted in the competition.

### 苏联举行设计苏联伟人纪念堂的公开竞赛

1954-07-06
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联举行设计苏联伟人纪念堂的公开竞赛
    【新华社五日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科四日讯：苏联部长会议国家建设委员会和莫斯科市劳动人民代表苏维埃执行委员会，奉苏联部长会议的指令，宣布举行征求苏联伟人纪念堂的最好设计的公开竞赛。这座纪念堂将建筑在莫斯科以永远纪念伟大的领袖——乌·伊·列宁和约·维·斯大林——以及安葬在红场上克里姆林宫墙下的共产党和苏维埃国家的杰出活动家。
    纪念堂将建筑在莫斯科西南区的列宁山上，在新的莫斯科大学校舍以南三公里半的地方。根据竞赛计划和条件，这将是一座体积达八万立方公尺的朴素、壮丽而宏伟的建筑物。纪念堂将用质量高而能耐久的材料来建筑和装饰。
    在纪念堂周围将利用当地的自然条件来开辟一个纪念公园。
    纪念堂的设计应包括：
    安放装着列宁遗体的水晶棺和装着斯大林遗体的水晶棺的一个大厅。
    安放从红场上克里姆林宫墙下迁出来的共产党和苏维埃国家的杰出活动家的骨灰瓶和遗体的一个或几个大厅。
    竞赛将在十一月一日结束。在竞赛中提出的纪念堂的最好设计将得到巨额奖金。
