Title: People in Da Nang, French-Occupied Central Vietnam, Demonstrate, Demanding Peace and Opposing Forced Conscription
Author:
Date: 1954-07-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 17th: According to Vietnam News Agency on the 15th, on June 30th, approximately two thousand people in Da Nang, French-occupied Central Vietnam, held a demonstration against forced conscription and demanding peace.

The demonstrating masses chanted slogans against the war and demanded the restoration of peace in Indochina. During the march, they encountered some trucks carrying puppet troops. When the puppet troops on the trucks learned that the people were fighting against forced conscription, many of them also joined the march.

### 中越法占区岘港人民示威  要求和平并反对强迫征兵

1954-07-18
第4版()
专栏：

    中越法占区岘港人民示威
    要求和平并反对强迫征兵
    【新华社十七日讯】据越南通讯社十五日讯：六月三十日，在中越法占区岘港约有两千人举行游行示威，反对强迫征兵，并要求和平。
    示威的群众高呼反对战争的口号，并要求恢复印度支那的和平。在游行途中，他们遇到了一些载有伪军的卡车。卡车上的伪军在知道人民是在为反对强迫征兵而进行斗争时，有许多人也参加了游行。
