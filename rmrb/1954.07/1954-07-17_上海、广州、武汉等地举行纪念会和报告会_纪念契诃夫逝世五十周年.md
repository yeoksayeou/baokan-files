Title: Shanghai, Guangzhou, Wuhan and other places hold memorial meetings and report meetings to commemorate the 50th anniversary of Chekhov's death

Author:
Date: 1954-07-17
Page: 3

Shanghai, Guangzhou, Wuhan and other places hold memorial meetings and report meetings
Commemorating the 50th Anniversary of Chekhov's Death
Shanghai, Guangzhou, Wuhan, Shenyang, Chongqing and other cities held memorial meetings and report meetings to commemorate the 50th anniversary of the death of the world cultural celebrity and outstanding Russian writer Chekhov.

Shanghai held a meeting on the evening of the 15th to commemorate the 50th anniversary of Chekhov's death. More than 800 people attended the memorial meeting. At the meeting, Xia Yan, Vice President of the Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Chairman of the East China Writers Association, gave a report on Chekhov's life, and Chekhov's plays were performed and films adapted from Chekhov's works were shown. The Shanghai Municipal People's Library held a Chekhov works review meeting on the evening of the 11th. The Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Museum and the Shanghai Municipal People's Library have held Chekhov picture and book exhibitions in their respective halls since the 15th.

Guangzhou held a grand meeting on the evening of the 15th with 1,000 people to commemorate the 50th anniversary of Chekhov's death. At the meeting, writer Sima Wensen gave a report on Chekhov's life and works, and recited Chekhov's short stories.

Wuhan held a meeting on the 14th to commemorate the 50th anniversary of Chekhov's death. More than 300 writers, literary workers, workers, students and literary enthusiasts from the PLA in the Central South District, Hubei Province and Wuhan City participated. Li Rui, Vice Chairman of the Central South Federation of Literary and Art Circles, spoke at the meeting. Lini, Deputy Director of the Central South People's Literature and Art Publishing House, who translated many of Chekhov's works, gave a report on Chekhov's life and his literary life. In the evening, Chekhov's famous one-act plays "The Proposal" and "The Bear" were performed at the Central South Theater in Hankou.

The Northeast Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Northeast Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and the Northeast Writers Association jointly held a report meeting on the evening of the 15th, with more than 800 people attending. Kabralov, representative of the Soviet Union's External Cultural Association in Shenyang, and Chinese writer Shi Tianshou introduced reports on Chekhov's life. An exhibition of photos and works related to Chekhov's life was also held at the meeting. After the meeting, the film "The Man in a Case," based on Chekhov's work, was shown.

The Chongqing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Chongqing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and the Chongqing Federation of Literary and Art Workers jointly held a report meeting on the 14th. A total of more than 400 people attended the meeting. Ren Baige, Chairman of the Chongqing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, gave a report on Chekhov's life, works and creative attitude at the meeting. The Chongqing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has held the "Exhibition of Pictures Commemorating the 50th Anniversary of the Death of the Great Russian Writer Chekhov" at the Chongqing Literary and Art Center since the 15th. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 上海、广州、武汉等地举行纪念会和报告会  纪念契诃夫逝世五十周年

1954-07-17
第3版()
专栏：

    上海、广州、武汉等地举行纪念会和报告会
    纪念契诃夫逝世五十周年
    上海、广州、武汉、沈阳、重庆等市分别举办纪念会和报告会，纪念世界文化名人、俄罗斯杰出作家契诃夫逝世五十周年。
    上海十五日晚举行了纪念契诃夫逝世五十周年大会，参加纪念会的有八百多人。会上，由上海市中苏友好协会副会长、华东作家协会主席夏衍作了有关契诃夫生平的报告，并演出了契诃夫的剧作和放映了根据契诃夫作品改编的影片。上海市人民图书馆曾在十一日晚举行了契诃夫作品评介会。上海市中苏友谊馆和上海市人民图书馆从十五日起，分别在各馆举办契诃夫图片和图书展览。
    广州市有一千人在十五日晚举行大会，隆重纪念契诃夫逝世五十周年。会上，作家司马文森作了关于契诃夫生平和创作的报告，并朗诵了契诃夫的短篇小说。
    武汉市十四日举行了契诃夫逝世五十周年纪念会，有中南区、湖北省、武汉市的作家、文学工作者、工人、学生和部队中的文学爱好者三百多人参加。中南文学艺术界联合会副主席李蕤在会上讲话。翻译了不少契诃夫著作的中南人民文学艺术出版社副社长丽尼作了有关契诃夫生平及其文学生活的报告。晚上，在汉口中南剧场演出了契诃夫名著独幕话剧“求婚”和“蠢货”。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会东北总分会、东北中苏友好协会和东北作家协会十五日晚联合举办了报告会，到会八百多人。苏联对外文化协会驻沈阳代表卡布拉洛夫和我国作家师田手介绍有关契诃夫生平的报告。会上并展出有关契诃夫生平的照片和作品。会后放映了契诃夫作品“套中人”影片。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会重庆市分会、重庆市中苏友好协会、重庆市文学艺术工作者联合会在十四日共同举办了报告会。到会的共四百多人。中国人民保卫世界和平委员会重庆市分会主席任白戈在会上作了关于契诃夫生平、著作及创作态度的报告。重庆市中苏友好协会自十五日起在重庆市文艺馆举办“俄罗斯伟大作家契诃夫逝世五十周年纪念图片展览”。（据新华社讯）
