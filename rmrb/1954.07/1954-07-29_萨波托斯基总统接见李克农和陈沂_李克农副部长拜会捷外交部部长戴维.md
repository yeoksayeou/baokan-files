Title: President Zápotocký Receives Li Kenong and Chen Yi
Author:
Date: 1954-07-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 28th. The President of the Czechoslovak Republic, Zápotocký, received Li Kenong, representative of the Chinese delegation to the Geneva Conference and Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, and Chen Yi, head of the Song and Dance Troupe of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, on the 26th. Present at the reception were: Czechoslovak First Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of National Defense Čepička, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Culture Kopecký, Chairman of the National Assembly Fierlinger, and Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of our Embassy in Czechoslovakia, Li Qingquan.
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 27th. Li Kenong, representative of the Chinese delegation to the Geneva Conference and Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, paid a visit to the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Czechoslovak government, David, on the 26th. He was accompanied by Li Qingquan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of our Embassy in Czechoslovakia, and Lei Yingfu, advisor to the delegation.

### 萨波托斯基总统接见李克农和陈沂  李克农副部长拜会捷外交部部长戴维

1954-07-29
第4版()
专栏：

    萨波托斯基总统接见李克农和陈沂
    李克农副部长拜会捷外交部部长戴维
    【新华社布拉格二十八日电】捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统萨波托斯基，二十六日接见了出席日内瓦会议的我国代表团代表、中央人民政府外交部副部长李克农和中国人民解放军歌舞团团长、人民革命军事委员会总政治部文化部部长陈沂。接见时在座的有：捷克斯洛伐克第一副总理兼国防部部长契皮茨卡，副总理兼文化部部长柯别茨基，国民议会主席费林格，和我国驻捷大使馆临时代办李清泉。【新华社布拉格二十七日电】我国出席日内瓦会议代表团代表、中央人民政府外交部副部长李克农二十六日拜会了捷克斯洛伐克政府外交部部长戴维。陪同前往的有我国驻捷大使馆临时代办李清泉和代表团顾问雷英夫。
