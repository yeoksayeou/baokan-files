Title: Chinese Trade Delegation Concludes Visit to Britain
Author:
Date: 1954-07-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, London, July 11th: The Chinese Trade Delegation, after continuing to discuss relevant trade matters with British steel, hardware, vehicle and other industry manufacturers on July 9th, held its final meeting with the Sino-British Trade Council. During the meeting, the head of the delegation made a concluding speech on the contribution of this visit to the development of future trade relations between China and Britain. The Sino-British Trade Council also hosted a banquet to bid farewell to the delegation.

### 中国贸易访问团在英国访问结束

1954-07-12
第1版()
专栏：

    中国贸易访问团在英国访问结束
    【新华社十一日讯】伦敦消息：中国贸易访问团七月九日在继续和英国钢铁、五金、车辆等业厂商洽谈有关的贸易事务后，和中英贸易委员会举行了最后一次会谈。会谈中，访问团负责人就此次访问对发展中英两国间今后的贸易关系的贡献作了总结发言。中英贸易委员会并设宴为访问团送行。
