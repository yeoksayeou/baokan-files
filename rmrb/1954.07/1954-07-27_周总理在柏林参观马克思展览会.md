Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Visits Marx Exhibition in Berlin
Author:
Date: 1954-07-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 26th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China, accompanied by Zhang Wentian, Wang Bingnan, Lei Renmin, Qiao Guanhua, Chen Jiakang, and others, visited the Marx Exhibition at the German History Museum in Berlin at 4:00 PM on July 25th, accompanied by Comrades Matern and Oelsner, members of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, and Comrade Lehmann, Chairman of the Communist Party of Germany.

### 周总理在柏林参观马克思展览会

1954-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理在柏林参观马克思展览会
    【新华社柏林二十六日电】中华人民共和国总理周恩来偕张闻天、王炳南、雷任民、乔冠华、陈家康等，于七月二十五日下午四时，在德国统一社会党中央委员会政治局委员马特恩和厄斯纳、德国共产党主席雷曼的陪同下，参观了柏林德国历史博物馆的马克思展览会。
