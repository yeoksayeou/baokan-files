Title: Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Other Units Jointly Hold Report Meeting Commemorating the Fiftieth Anniversary of the Death of World Cultural Figure Chekhov
Author:
Date: 1954-07-15
Page: 1

    Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Other Units Jointly Hold Report Meeting
    Commemorating the Fiftieth Anniversary of the Death of World Cultural Figure Chekhov
    [Xinhua News Agency, July 14th] To commemorate the fiftieth anniversary of the death of Anton Pavlovich Chekhov, a world cultural figure and outstanding Russian writer, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Beijing Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and the Beijing Soviet Foreign Cultural Association jointly held a report meeting on the evening of July 14th.
    More than a hundred precious pictures introducing Chekhov's childhood and youth and his dramatic works were displayed around the venue.
    Those attending the report meeting included Shu Sheyu, Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Literary and Art Circles and Vice President of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Cao Jinghua, Vice President of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, as well as dramatists Wu Xue, Sun Weishi, Li Chao, and more than 600 cultural and artistic and drama workers.
    Romanin, representative of the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association in China, also attended the meeting.
    After Shu Sheyu delivered the opening speech, Romanin gave a report on "Anton Pavlovich Chekhov's Creative Path." Romanin narrated Chekhov's life and Chekhov's arduous creative process.
    After the meeting, the color film "Anna on the Neck," produced by the Soviet Gorky Film Studio based on Chekhov's novel, was screened.

### 北京市中苏友协等单位联合举行报告会  纪念世界文化名人契诃夫逝世五十周年

1954-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市中苏友协等单位联合举行报告会
    纪念世界文化名人契诃夫逝世五十周年
    【新华社十四日讯】为了纪念世界文化名人、俄罗斯杰出作家安东·巴夫洛维奇·契诃夫逝世五十周年，北京市中苏友好协会、北京市文学艺术工作者联合会和北京苏联对外文化协会在七月十四日晚联合举行了报告会。
    会场的四周展出了介绍契诃夫的童年和少年时代和他的戏剧作品的珍贵图片一百多幅。
    出席报告会的有北京市文学艺术工作者联合会主席、北京市中苏友好协会副会长舒舍予，北京市中苏友好协会副会长曹靖华以及戏剧家吴雪、孙维世、李超等和文化艺术、戏剧工作者六百多人。
    出席会议的还有苏联对外文化协会驻华代表罗满宁。
    报告会由舒舍予致开幕词后，罗满宁作了关于“安东·巴夫洛维奇·契诃夫的创作道路”的报告。罗满宁叙述了契诃夫的生平以及契诃夫艰苦奋斗的创作过程。
    会后放映了由苏联高尔基电影制片厂根据契诃夫小说摄制成的五彩电影“依人为生的安娜”。
