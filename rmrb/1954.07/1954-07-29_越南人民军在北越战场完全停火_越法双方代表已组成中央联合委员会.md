Title: The Vietnam People's Army Completely Ceases Fire on the Northern Vietnam Battlefield; Representatives from Vietnam and France Form Central Joint Commission
Author:
Date: 1954-07-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, 28th—According to the Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, on July 28th: At a press conference held on the 27th, the spokesman for the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army spoke about the Vietnam People's Army's implementation of the ceasefire order on the Northern Vietnam battlefield. The spokesman said that the entire People's Army of North Vietnam had strictly implemented the ceasefire order issued by the Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnam People's Army, and had completely ceased fire from 7:00 AM on the 27th.

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, 28th—According to the Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, on July 28th: In order to implement the armistice agreement signed at the Geneva Conference and the terms reached at the local military conference in Vietnam, a Central Joint Commission has been formed by representatives of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army and the General Headquarters of the French Union Forces in Indochina. This Joint Commission is responsible for formulating a plan for the implementation of the terms of the armistice agreement, and for supervising and promoting the implementation of this plan.

The Central Joint Commission for the implementation of the armistice agreement will begin work in Zhongjia, North Vietnam, starting on the morning of July 29th.

### 越南人民军在北越战场完全停火  越法双方代表已组成中央联合委员会

1954-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

    越南人民军在北越战场完全停火
    越法双方代表已组成中央联合委员会
    【新华社二十八日讯】据越南通讯社北越二十八日讯：越南人民军总司令部发言人在二十七日举行的记者招待会上谈到越南人民军在北越战场上执行停火命令的情形。发言人说，北越的全体人民军队已严格执行了越南人民军总司令所发布的停火命令，而且从二十七日上午七时起已完全停火。
    【新华社二十八日讯】越南通讯社北越二十八日讯：为了执行在日内瓦会议上签订的停战协定和在越南当地军事会议上达成的条款，已由越南人民军总司令部和印度支那法兰西联邦部队总司令部代表组成中央联合委员会。这个联合委员会负责拟定执行停战协定条款的计划，并监督和促进这个计划的实施。
    执行停战协定的中央联合委员会将从七月二十九日早晨起在北越中稼开始工作。
