Title: President of the Chinese Department of the University of Indonesia Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1954-07-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 7th:
Zeng Zusen, the President of the Chinese Department of the University of Indonesia, who was sent by the Ministry of Education and Culture of the Republic of Indonesia to visit our country, arrived in Beijing on the 6th. Ren Bosheng, Section Chief of the Foreign Culture Liaison Bureau of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government Administrative Council, Professor Xiang Da of Peking University, Wang Chongwu of the Institute of History of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Amon Palaja, First Secretary of the Embassy of the Republic of Indonesia in China, were at the station to welcome him.

### 印尼大学汉学院院长到京

1954-07-09
第4版()
专栏：

    印尼大学汉学院院长到京
    【新华社七日讯】印度尼西亚共和国文教部派来我国访问的印尼大学汉学院院长曾祖森已在六日抵京。到车站欢迎的有中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局处长任泊生，北京大学教授向达，中国科学院历史研究所王崇武等，印度尼西亚共和国驻华大使馆一等秘书阿蒙拍拉加也到站欢迎。
