Title: Zhou Enlai Invites Menon to Dinner and Meets with Tep Phan
Author:
Date: 1954-07-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 19th: Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, invited V. K. Krishna Menon, the Indian representative to the United Nations, to dinner at 8 p.m. on the 19th.

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 20th: Tep Phan, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Cambodia, met with Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, at 11 a.m. on the 20th and held talks for two hours.

### 周外长邀梅农共进晚餐并和泰普潘会谈

1954-07-21
第1版()
专栏：

    周外长邀梅农共进晚餐并和泰普潘会谈
    【新华社日内瓦十九日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来十九日下午八时邀印度驻联合国代表梅农共进晚餐。
    【新华社日内瓦二十日电】柬埔寨王国外交大臣泰普潘在二十日上午十一时访晤中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来，会谈两小时。
