Title: Ho Chi Minh and Vo Nguyen Giap Send Condolences to Wounded Soldiers, Sick Soldiers and Families of Martyrs
Author: 
Date: 1954-07-29
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, July 28th: According to the Vietnam News Agency, July 27th was Vietnam's War Invalids and Martyrs Day. President Ho Chi Minh wrote a letter to the War Invalids Department saying: On behalf of the government, I sincerely express my condolences to the wounded and sick soldiers of the Vietnam People's Army, my regards to the families of the martyrs, and my gratitude to the compatriots who receive and care for the wounded and sick soldiers. At the same time, President Ho Chi Minh donated his one month's salary and more than 30,000 Vietnamese Dong sent by overseas Vietnamese to the wounded and sick soldiers of the People's Army. General Vo Nguyen Giap, Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnam People's Army, also issued a message on this festival, expressing his condolences and respect to the wounded and sick soldiers and families of martyrs, and congratulating the honorary soldiers on their great achievements in the movement to increase production and in implementing the agricultural policies of the Vietnamese Labor Party and the government in various places.

### 胡志明和武元甲慰问伤病员和烈士家属

1954-07-29
第4版()
专栏：

    胡志明和武元甲慰问伤病员和烈士家属
    【新华社二十八日讯】据越南通讯社讯：七月二十七日是越南荣军和烈士节。胡志明主席写信给荣军部说：我谨代表政府诚恳地表示对越南人民军伤病员的慰问，对烈士家属的问候，以及对接待和照顾伤病员的同胞们的感谢。同时，胡志明主席把他的一个月的薪水和海外的越侨寄来的三万多越南币赠送给人民军的伤病员。越南人民军总司令武元甲将军也在这个节日发表文告，对人民军伤病员和烈士家属表示慰问和敬意，并祝贺荣誉军人在增加生产运动中以及在各地执行越南劳动党和政府的农业政策中所取得的很大成就。
