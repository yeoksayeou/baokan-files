Title: Marshall Islands Residents Appeal to UN to Stop US Hydrogen Bomb Tests, Soviet Union Suggests Trusteeship Council Stop This Atrocity by the US, British Scientists Demand Ban on Weapons of Mass Destruction
Author:
Date: 1954-07-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch from New York, July 7th, via TASS: The United Nations Trusteeship Council has received an appeal to the United Nations from the Standing Committee of the Congress of Representatives of the Marshall Islands – a territory under U.S. trusteeship.

The appeal states: The numerous atomic and hydrogen bomb tests conducted by the United States on the territory of the Marshall Islands have caused immense suffering to the residents of several islands, particularly the Rongelap and Utirik atolls. Many residents have, in effect, been permanently disabled.

The appeal states: To conduct atomic and hydrogen weapons tests, the U.S. authorities are driving increasing numbers of Marshall Islands residents off their land. Eniwetok and Kwajalein islands have been specifically designated as sites for atomic and hydrogen bomb tests, and the residents of Bikini and Eniwetok islands have been evicted from their land by the U.S. authorities. The signatories to the appeal express the will of all Marshall Islands residents, who demand an immediate halt to all lethal weapons testing in the region. The appeal states: Given the ever-increasing danger to our lives… and the threat of being deprived of our land rights, we submit this appeal to the United Nations, hoping it will heed our plea and take the necessary measures.

The United Nations has also received an appeal from a U.S. citizen, who demands an immediate halt to atomic and hydrogen bomb tests in trust territories. He asks: “What measures is the United Nations taking to prevent the administering authority from taking such destructive actions in the future?”

The Soviet representative submitted a proposal on July 6th to the Standing Committee on Petitions of the Trusteeship Council, stating that conducting hydrogen and atomic bomb tests in trust territories is inconsistent with the purposes and principles of the trusteeship system stipulated in Chapter XII of the United Nations Charter.

The proposal says: In the spirit of Article 83, paragraph 2, of the United Nations Charter, instruct the United States government, as the administering authority, to: (a) Cease conducting hydrogen and atomic bomb tests in the trust territory. (b) Compensate the local residents of the trust territory for material losses. (c) Restore to the local residents all rights to possess and use the land they originally possessed and used, and guarantee these residents unimpeded return to these lands.

The Soviet proposal will be studied at a subsequent meeting of the committee.

Xinhua News Agency dispatch from July 14th, via TASS: The British "Times" published a letter signed by numerous scientists – including the renowned economist Professor G. D. H. Cole – demanding a ban on the use of thermonuclear weapons. The letter reads as follows:

"We believe that the British government should automatically declare that Britain will not use any kind of thermonuclear weapons, including hydrogen bombs, or allow other countries to use thermonuclear weapons from bases on British territory, unless some other country first uses thermonuclear weapons to attack any part of the Commonwealth or any country with which Britain has a military alliance."

At the German Christian Lutheran Church Congress, attended by delegations from the German Democratic Republic and West Germany, as well as many other countries, many speakers unanimously called for a ban on the use of atomic bombs, hydrogen bombs, and other weapons of mass destruction.

### 马绍尔群岛居民向联合国呼吁制止美国试验氢弹  苏联建议托管理事会制止美国这种暴行  英国科学家要求禁止使用大规模毁灭性武器

1954-07-15
第4版()
专栏：

    马绍尔群岛居民向联合国呼吁制止美国试验氢弹
    苏联建议托管理事会制止美国这种暴行
    英国科学家要求禁止使用大规模毁灭性武器
    【新华社讯】据塔斯社纽约七日讯：联合国托管理事会接到马绍尔群岛——美国的托管领地——代表大会驻会委员会致联合国的呼吁书。
    呼吁书说：美国在马绍尔群岛领土上进行的许多次原子弹和氢弹试验给一些岛上的居民，特别是朗格拉普和尤蒂里克珊瑚岛的居民带来了巨大的痛苦。许多居民事实上已终生成为残废。
    呼吁书说：美国当局为了要进行原子武器和氢武器的试验，正在把越来越多的马绍尔群岛居民赶出他们的土地。恩纳威托克岛和瓜加林岛被特别指定作为原子弹和氢弹试验的地方，比基尼岛和恩纳威托克岛的居民被美国当局赶出他们的土地。在呼吁书上签名的人表示了马绍尔群岛全体居民的意志，他们要求立刻停止在这个地区进行一切致命武器的试验。呼吁书说：鉴于我们的生命遭受的危险日益增加……以及占有土地的权利有被剥夺的危险，我们向联合国提出这个呼吁书，希望它会倾听我们的恳求，并采取必要的措施。
    联合国也接到一位美国公民寄来的一份呼吁书，这位公民要求立刻停止在托管领地上进行原子弹和氢弹试验。他问道：“联合国正在采取什么措施来防止管理当局将来采取这一类的破坏行动？”
    苏联代表在七月六日向托管理事会的常设请愿书审查委员会提出了一项提案，提案指出，在托管地区进行氢弹和原子弹试验是和联合国宪章第十二章所规定的托管制度的目的和原则不相符合的。
    提案说：本着联合国宪章第八十三条第二款的精神，指令作为管理当局的美国政府：（甲）终止在托管地区进行氢弹和原子弹试验。（乙）赔偿该托管地区的当地居民的物质损失。（丙）恢复当地居民占有和使用他们原来所占有和使用的土地的一切权利，并保证这些居民们不受阻碍地回到这些土地上去。
    苏联的这个提案将在委员会随后举行的一次会议上加以研究。
    【新华社十四日讯】据塔斯社讯：英国“泰晤士报”刊载了一封由许多科学家——其中包括著名的经济学家格·德·赫·柯尔教授——署名的信，要求禁止使用热核子武器。信件内容如下：
      “我们相信，英国政府应该自动地宣布，英国将不使用包括氢弹在内的任何种热核子武器或允许其他国家从英国领土上的基地出发使用热核子武器，除非某个其他国家首先使用热核子武器来攻击英联邦的任何一部分或任何和英国结成了军事同盟的国家。”
    在有德意志民主共和国和西德的基督徒以及其他许多国家的代表团参加的德国基督教信义福音会代表大会上，许多发言人都一致要求禁止使用原子弹、氢弹及其他大规模毁灭性武器。
