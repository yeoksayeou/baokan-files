Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Calls on Comrade Bierut; Comrade Bierut Hosts Premier Zhou Enlai

Author:

Date: 1954-07-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 27th: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, called on Comrade Bierut, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, at 11:45 a.m. on the 26th.

Accompanying Premier Zhou Enlai were Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, and Wang Bingnan, Secretary-General of our delegation to the Geneva Conference. Zeng Yongquan, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the People's Republic of Poland, also accompanied them.

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 27th: Comrade Bierut, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, hosted a luncheon on the 26th at 2:00 p.m. in honor of Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian. Present at the luncheon were members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, including Zawadzki, Cyrankiewicz, Minc, Nowak, Rokossowski, Ochab, Berman, Mazur, Jóźwiak-Witold, Radkiewicz, Dworakowski, Samborski, and Zeng Yongquan, the Ambassador of our country to Poland.

### 周恩来总理拜会贝鲁特同志  贝鲁特同志招待周恩来总理

1954-07-28
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理拜会贝鲁特同志
    贝鲁特同志招待周恩来总理
    【新华社华沙二十七日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，在二十六日上午十一时四十五分拜会了波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记贝鲁特。
    随同周恩来总理前往的有我中央人民政府外交部副部长张闻天和我参加日内瓦会议代表团秘书长王炳南。中华人民共和国驻波兰人民共和国大使曾涌泉也陪同前往。
   【新华社华沙二十七日电】波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记贝鲁特在二十六日下午二时设宴招待中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来和外交部副部长张闻天。午宴时在座的有波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局委员萨瓦茨基、西伦凯维兹、明兹、诺瓦克、罗科索夫斯基、奥哈布、贝尔曼、马茹尔、约兹维亚克—韦托德、拉德凯维奇、德沃拉科夫斯基、萨姆布罗夫斯基，以及我国驻波兰大使曾涌泉。
