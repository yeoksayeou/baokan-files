Title: Sino-Vietnamese Military Conference: Chief Representatives of Both Sides Hold Meeting, Approve Joint Report on Previous Meetings and on Improving Living Conditions for Prisoners of War
Author:
Date: 1954-07-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, July 17th: The Vietnam News Agency reported from North Vietnam on July 17th that at the meeting on July 16th, the chief representatives of the Vietnam People's Army General Headquarters delegation and the French Union Forces General Headquarters delegation in Indochina, attending the Sino-Vietnamese local military conference in North Vietnam, approved the joint report on previous meetings and on improving the living conditions of prisoners of war. This joint report will be published to the prisoners of war in various prison camps and to the people.

On the morning of July 17th, the subcommittee responsible for studying the implementation of the ceasefire continued its work.

### 中亦军事会议双方首席代表举行会议  批准关于前几次会议和关于改善战俘生活条件联合报告

1954-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    中亦军事会议双方首席代表举行会议
    批准关于前几次会议和关于改善战俘生活条件联合报告
    【新华社十七日讯】越南通讯社北越十七日讯：在七月十六日的会议上，出席北越中亦当地军事会议的越南人民军总司令部代表团和印度支那法兰西联邦部队总司令部代表团的首席代表批准了关于前几次会议和关于改善战俘生活条件的联合报告。这个联合报告将向各个战俘营中的战俘公布和向人民公布。
    七月十七日上午，负责研究执行停火问题的小组委员会继续进行工作。
