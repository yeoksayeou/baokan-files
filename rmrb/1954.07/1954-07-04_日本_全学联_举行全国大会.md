Title: Japanese "Zengakuren" Holds National Congress
Author:
Date: 1954-07-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 2nd: The 7th National Congress of the All-Japan Student Self-Government Association Federation (Zengakuren) was held in Tokyo from June 13th to 17th. More than 900 student representatives from schools across the country attended the conference.

    The conference discussed the central executive department's general policy report on "The Current Tasks of Japanese Students," and then adopted the "Zengakuren's" general work policy. The policy first raised the issues of maintaining education, culture, and student life, and pointed out that the basic task of "Zengakuren" lies in strengthening student unity and striving to maintain Japan's education and culture, and to defend student life. Therefore, it is necessary to oppose the revival of militarism and to oppose war and atomic weapons.

### 日本“全学联”举行全国大会

1954-07-04
第4版()
专栏：

    日本“全学联”举行全国大会
    【新华社二日讯】东京消息：全日本学生自治会总联合会（简称“全学联”）第七届全国大会已从六月十三日到十七日在东京举行。出席大会的有全国许多学校学生代表九百多人。
    大会根据“全学联”中央执行部关于“日本学生的当前任务”的总方针报告进行了讨论，然后通过了“全学联”的一般工作方针。方针首先提出了维护教育、文化和学生生活的问题，并指明“全学联”的基本任务在于加强学生的团结，为维护日本的教育和文化，为保卫学生生活而奋斗。因此，就需要反对军国主义复活，反对战争和原子武器。
