Title: Philippine People's Liberation Army in Combat in Pampanga Province
Author:
Date: 1954-07-01
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Manila) According to disclosures from the Magsaysay government's military authorities in the Philippines, the Philippine People's Liberation Army has been engaged in new battles with 4,000 government troops in the Mount Arayat area of Pampanga Province on Luzon Island since June 17. Government military sources acknowledged that government troops suffered losses in the operations.

### 菲律宾人民解放军  在邦邦牙省进行战斗

1954-07-01
第4版()
专栏：

    菲律宾人民解放军
    在邦邦牙省进行战斗
    【新华社讯】马尼拉消息：菲律宾麦格赛赛政府军事当局披露，菲律宾人民解放军自六月十七日起在吕宋岛邦邦牙省亚拉悦山地区和四千名政府军展开新的战斗。政府军方面的消息承认，政府军在作战中受到损失。
