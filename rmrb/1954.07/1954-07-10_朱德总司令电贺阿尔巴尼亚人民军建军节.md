Title: Commander-in-Chief Zhu De Sends Telegram of Congratulations on the Anniversary of the Founding of the Albanian People's Army
Author:
Date: 1954-07-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 9th: On July 10th, the anniversary of the founding of the Albanian People's Army, Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army sent a telegram to General Hoxha, Commander-in-Chief of the Albanian People's Army, expressing congratulations. The telegram reads as follows:

To General Hoxha, Commander-in-Chief of the Albanian People's Army,

Respected Comrade General:

On the occasion of the 11th anniversary of the founding of the Albanian People's Army, I, on behalf of all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, extend to you our heartfelt congratulations, and sincerely wish the Albanian People's Army great achievements in the cause of strengthening its combat power.

Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Zhu De, July 9, 1954

### 朱德总司令电贺阿尔巴尼亚人民军建军节

1954-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德总司令电贺阿尔巴尼亚人民军建军节
    【新华社九日讯】七月十日是阿尔巴尼亚人民军建军节，中国人民解放军总司令朱德致电阿尔巴尼亚人民军总司令霍查大将，表示祝贺。电文如下：
    阿尔巴尼亚人民军总司令霍查大将
    敬爱的将军同志：
    值此阿尔巴尼亚人民军建军十一周年之际，我谨代表中国人民解放军全体指挥员、战斗员向您致以衷心的祝贺，谨祝阿尔巴尼亚人民军在加强战斗威力的事业中获得巨大的成就。
    中国人民解放军总司令  朱德  一九五四年七月九日
