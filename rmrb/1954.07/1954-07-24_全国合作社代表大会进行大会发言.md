Title: National Cooperative Congress Conducts Plenary Speeches
Author: 
Date: 1954-07-24
Page: 2

    The First National Congress of Cooperatives of the People's Republic of China held plenary speeches on the 23rd. Representatives from thirteen provinces delivered speeches at the meeting.
    Cooperative delegations from Mongolia, the German Democratic Republic, Bulgaria, and Vietnam delivered congratulatory messages at the conference today. They also presented gifts and banners to the congress.
    The presidium of the congress read out congratulatory telegrams from the Central Council of the Polish "Farmers' Mutual Aid" Agricultural Cooperative and the Chairman of the Council of the Hungarian Cooperative Federation.
                                  (Xinhua News Agency)

### 全国合作社代表大会进行大会发言

1954-07-24
第2版()
专栏：

    全国合作社代表大会进行大会发言
    中华全国合作社第一次代表大会二十三日进行大会发言，在会上发言的有十三个省的合作社的代表。
    蒙古、德意志民主共和国、保加利亚、越南的合作社代表团今天在大会上致祝词。他们并赠给大会礼物和锦旗。
    大会主席团宣读了波兰“农民互助”农业合作社中央理事会和匈牙利合作社联合会理事会主席拍来的贺电。
                                  （新华社）
