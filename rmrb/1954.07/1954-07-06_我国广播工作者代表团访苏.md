Title: Chinese Broadcasting Workers Delegation Visits the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1954-07-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th—At the invitation of the Soviet Union's General Administration of Radio Information, the Chinese broadcasting workers delegation left Beijing by plane this morning for Moscow.

The delegation is composed of fifteen Chinese broadcasting workers. The head of the delegation, Wen Jize, deputy director of the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council, is already in Moscow.

The delegation will visit and tour Moscow Radio Station and comprehensively learn from the advanced experience of Soviet radio propaganda work.

### 我国广播工作者代表团访苏

1954-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我国广播工作者代表团访苏
    【新华社四日讯】应苏联广播新闻总局的邀请，中国广播工作者访苏代表团在四日早晨乘飞机离开北京去莫斯科。
    代表团由我国广播工作者十五人组成，代表团团长、中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会广播事业局副局长温济泽已经在莫斯科。
    代表团将要访问和参观莫斯科广播电台，全面的学习苏联广播宣传工作的先进经验。
