Title: In Order to Carry Out Various Provocations Against Soviet Ships Sailing on the High Seas, U.S. Military Aircraft Continuously Reconnoiter Soviet Merchant Ships
Author:
Date: 1954-07-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vladivostok, 8th: In connection with the seizure of the Soviet oil tanker "Tuapse" by the U.S. Navy in the Taiwan area, attention has been drawn here to the fact that for a long time, U.S. military aircraft have been continuously reconnoitering Soviet merchant ships sailing in various seas of the Far East and the Pacific Ocean (including the Taiwan area).

Regarding these intolerable actions taken by U.S. aircraft against Soviet ships, the following recent incidents in the Taiwan area can be cited as examples.

	On May 19, at 14:10, in the sea south of Taiwan, a U.S. military aircraft circled at low altitude above the Soviet "Kamenetz-Podolsk" ship sailing from Singapore to Dalian. On May 20, at 22 degrees 06 minutes north latitude and 123 degrees 21 minutes east longitude, another U.S. aircraft circled above the aforementioned ship.

	On June 14, in the sea 70 nautical miles southeast of Taiwan, two U.S. military aircraft circled at an altitude of 60 to 400 meters above the Soviet ship "Kutuzov" sailing from Odessa to Nakhodka for one hour.

	On June 16, at 18:57, in the sea 90 nautical miles east of Taiwan, a U.S. military aircraft circled at low altitude above the Soviet ship "Kserksipp" sailing from Singapore to Xingang.

	On June 24, at 10:50, in the sea area near Taiwan, a U.S. military aircraft circled at low altitude above the Soviet ship "Apsheron."

These actions by U.S. military aircraft are a serious violation of freedom of navigation on the high seas and prove that the U.S. military authorities completely disregard recognized norms of international law. At the same time, they are clearly intended to obtain information about Soviet ships sailing in the Taiwan area in order to carry out various provocations against Soviet ships on the high seas, as they did to the aforementioned Soviet oil tanker "Tuapse".

### 为了对航行在公海上的苏联船只实行各种挑衅  美国军用飞机接连对苏联商船进行侦察

1954-07-09
第4版()
专栏：

    为了对航行在公海上的苏联船只实行各种挑衅
    美国军用飞机接连对苏联商船进行侦察
   【新华社八日讯】据塔斯社海参崴讯：由于美国海军在台湾岛地区劫走了苏联油船“图阿普斯号”，此间注意到长期来美国的军用飞机连续侦察航行在远东各海和太平洋海面（其中包括台湾岛地区）的苏联商船的事实。
    关于美国飞机对苏联船只采取的这种不可容忍的行动，可举出最近在台湾岛地区所发生的下列一些事件作为例子。
    五月十九日十四时十分，在台湾岛以南的海面上，美国军用飞机一架在从新加坡开往大连港的苏联“卡麦奈兹—波多尔斯克号”海船上空低空盘旋了几圈。五月二十日，在北纬二十二度零六分、东经一百二十三度二十一分的海面上，又有美国飞机一架在上述海船的上空盘旋了几圈。
    六月十四日，在台湾岛东南七十海里的海面上，美国军用飞机两架在自敖德萨开往纳霍德卡港的苏联海船“库图佐夫号”六十到四百公尺的上空作了一小时的盘旋。
    六月十六日十八时五十七分，在台湾岛以东九十海里的海面上，美国军用飞机一架在自新加坡开往新港的苏联海船“克塞尔皮普号”的上空作了低空盘旋。
    六月二十四日十时五十分，在台湾岛地区的海面上，美国军用飞机一架在苏联海船“阿普塞伦号”的上空低空盘旋了几圈。
    美国军用飞机的这类行动是对在公海上的航行自由的严重破坏，并且证明美国军事当局全然不把公认的国际法准则放在眼中。同时，他们显然是为了获得关于航行于台湾岛地区的苏联海船的情报，以便对公海上的苏联海船实行各种挑衅，像他们对上述的苏联油船“图阿普斯号”所做的那样。
