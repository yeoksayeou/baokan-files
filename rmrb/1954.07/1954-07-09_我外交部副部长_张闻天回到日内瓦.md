Title: My Deputy Foreign Minister Zhang Wentian Returns to Geneva
Author:
Date: 1954-07-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 8th. Zhang Wentian, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, returned to Geneva on the afternoon of the 8th.
