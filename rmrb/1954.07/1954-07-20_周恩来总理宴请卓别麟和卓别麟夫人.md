Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Hosts a Banquet for Charlie Chaplin and Mrs. Chaplin
Author:
Date: 1954-07-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 18th. Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, hosted a banquet at 7:00 p.m. on the 18th for Charlie Chaplin, the renowned British film artist and winner of the 1953 International Peace Prize, and Mrs. Chaplin.

Those attending as guests included representatives of the Chinese film workers delegation attending the 8th International Film Festival currently being held in Czechoslovakia: film director Zhai Qiang, actors Fan Ruijuan, Yao Xiangli, U Lana, and Yang Shaoren, deputy manager of the China Film Distribution Company, as well as members of the delegation of the People's Republic of China attending the Geneva Conference.

### 周恩来总理宴请卓别麟和卓别麟夫人

1954-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理宴请卓别麟和卓别麟夫人
   【新华社日内瓦十八日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来在十八日晚七时宴请一九五三年度国际和平奖金得奖人、著名的英国电影艺术家卓别麟和卓别麟夫人。
    出席作陪的有参加现在捷克斯洛伐克举行的第八届国际电影节的中国电影工作者代表团代表、电影导演翟强，演员范瑞娟、姚向黎、乌日娜和中国电影发行公司副经理杨少任及中华人民共和国出席日内瓦会议代表团人员。
