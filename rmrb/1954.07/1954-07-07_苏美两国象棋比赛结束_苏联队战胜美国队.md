Title: Soviet-U.S. Chess Match Ends: Soviet Team Defeats U.S. Team
Author: 
Date: 1954-07-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, June 6th: The chess match between the Soviet and American teams, which began in mid-June, ended on June 25th. After four rounds of intense competition, the Soviet chess team defeated the American team by a score of 20 to 12.

The Soviet chess team has recently traveled in two groups to England and Canada to hold matches with the British and Canadian chess players.

### 苏美两国象棋比赛结束  苏联队战胜美国队

1954-07-07
第4版()
专栏：

    苏美两国象棋比赛结束
    苏联队战胜美国队
   【新华社六日讯】据塔斯社纽约消息：六月中旬开始的苏、美两国象棋选手队比赛，已经在六月二十五日结束，经过四轮紧张比赛后，苏联象棋选手队以二十分对十二分战胜了美国队。
    苏联象棋选手队最近已分两批前往英国和加拿大和英、加两国的象棋选手举行比赛。
