Title: Mendès France and Eden Meet with Premier Zhou Separately
Author:
Date: 1954-07-14
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 13th: French Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Mendès France, met with Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, at 10:30 a.m. on the 13th, for a discussion lasting one hour and fifteen minutes.
    Present at the meeting on the Chinese side were Vice Foreign Ministers Zhang Wentian and Li Kenong, and Wang Bingnan, Secretary-General of the Chinese Delegation to the Geneva Conference; on the French side were the Ambassador to Switzerland, Chauvel, and Guémas, advisor to the French Delegation to the Geneva Conference.
    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 13th: British Foreign Secretary Eden met with Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, at 11:45 a.m. on the 13th, for a discussion lasting thirty minutes.
    Present at the meeting on the Chinese side were Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Wentian, Wang Bingnan, Secretary-General of the Chinese Delegation to the Geneva Conference, and advisor Huan Xiang; on the British side were Caccia, Deputy Under-Secretary of the British Foreign Office, and Allen, Assistant Under-Secretary.

### 孟戴斯—弗朗斯和艾登分别访晤周总理

1954-07-14
第1版()
专栏：

    孟戴斯—弗朗斯和艾登分别访晤周总理
    【新华社日内瓦十三日电】法国总理兼外交部部长孟戴斯—弗朗斯在十三日上午十时三十分访晤中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，会谈一小时又十五分钟。
    会谈时在座的，中国方面有外交部副部长张闻天、李克农，出席日内瓦会议的中国代表团秘书长王炳南；法国方面有驻瑞士大使萧维尔和出席日内瓦会议的法国代表团顾问吉埃马斯。
   【新华社日内瓦十三日电】英国外交大臣艾登在十三日上午十一时四十五分访晤中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来，会谈三十分钟。
    会谈时在座的，中国方面有外交部副部长张闻天，出席日内瓦会议的中国代表团秘书长王炳南，顾问宦乡；英国方面有英国外交部副次官卡西亚、助理次官艾伦。
