Title: Excerpt of Congratulations from Pei Baoyun, Head of the Vietnamese Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-07-26
Page: 2

Excerpt of Congratulations from Pei Baoyun, Head of the Vietnamese Delegation
    Pei Baoyun, Head of the Vietnamese Delegation, delivered a congratulatory speech to the First National Congress of Cooperatives of China, saying: Our Vietnamese people are always concerned about the achievements of the Chinese people in transforming their own country.
    He said that our Vietnamese delegation is participating in this congress with a learning attitude.
    He said, you are ahead of us, and every step you take can gain new experience. We believe that learning from the experiences of our comrades will surely enable us to achieve many successes in our future work. He particularly pointed out that all of us Vietnamese people have realized that by studying Chinese experience and the great Mao Zedong Thought, our work will definitely achieve good results.
    Finally, he said that the help of the comrades and the Vietnamese people's trust in their great friends symbolize that the unbreakable friendship between the Vietnamese and Chinese people will be increasingly consolidated.

### 越南代表团团长裴宝云祝词（摘要）

1954-07-26
第2版()
专栏：

    越南代表团团长裴宝云祝词（摘要）
    越南代表团团长裴宝云向中华全国合作社第一次代表大会致祝词，他说：我们越南人民经常关心中国人民在改造自己的国家中所获得的成就。
    他说，我们越南代表团抱着学习态度来参加这次大会。
    他说，你们走在我们前面，你们每步都可以取得一个新的经验。我们相信学习了同志们的经验，一定会使我们今后的工作获得许多成就。他特别指出，我们全体越南人民已认识到，学习了中国经验，学习了伟大的毛泽东思想，工作就一定能获得良好成绩。
    最后他说，同志们的帮助和越南人民对伟大的朋友们的帮助的信任，这象征着越、中两国人民牢不可破的友谊将日益巩固。
