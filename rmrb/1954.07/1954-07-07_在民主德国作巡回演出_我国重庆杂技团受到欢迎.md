Title: The Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe of China Welcomed During a Tour in the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1954-07-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 6th - According to the 1954 implementation plan of the Sino-German cultural cooperation agreement, the Chinese Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe, on tour in the German Democratic Republic, gave its premiere performance on the evening of July 3rd at the Stalin Avenue Sports Hall in Berlin. Present at the performance were the Minister of Culture of the German Democratic Republic, Johannes R. Becher; Han Seigewasser, member of the Presidium of the National Council of the National Front of the German Democratic Republich; representatives from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic and the German Association for Foreign Cultural Relations; and more than 6,000 spectators. Ji Pengfei, the Ambassador of China to the German Democratic Republic, and other members of the Embassy also attended the performance. All the programs performed by the acrobatic troupe were warmly welcomed and praised by the audience.

After the performance, Seigewasser and Zhu Ming, head of the acrobatic troupe, delivered speeches respectively. They unanimously pointed out that the acrobatic troupe's visit to Germany for performances will strengthen the ever-increasing cultural exchanges and friendly relations between China and Germany.

The acrobatic troupe performed for the citizens of Potsdam on the evening of the 4th and began its tour of the German Democratic Republic on the 5th.

### 在民主德国作巡回演出  我国重庆杂技团受到欢迎

1954-07-07
第4版()
专栏：

    在民主德国作巡回演出
    我国重庆杂技团受到欢迎
    【新华社柏林六日电】根据中德文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划赴德意志民主共和国作巡回演出的中国重庆杂技艺术团，于七月三日晚在柏林斯大林大街体育馆作首次公演。出席观看演出的有德意志民主共和国文化部部长约·贝希尔、民主德国全国阵线全国委员会主席团委员汉·赛格瓦塞、德意志民主共和国外交部和德国对外文化联络协会代表以及观众六千多人。我国驻德意志民主共和国大使姬鹏飞和大使馆其他人员也都出席了演出会。杂技艺术团所演出的各项节目都受到观众的热烈欢迎和赞扬。
    演出后，赛格瓦塞和杂技艺术团团长朱明分别发表演说。他们一致指出，这次杂技艺术团来德演出将加强中德两国间日益增进的文化交流和友好关系。
    杂技艺术团四日晚为波茨坦市民表演，从五日起开始前往德意志民主共和国各地作巡回演出。
