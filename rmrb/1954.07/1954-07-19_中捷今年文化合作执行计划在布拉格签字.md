Title: Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan for This Year Signed in Prague
Author:
Date: 1954-07-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 18th—The 1954 Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan was signed in Prague on the 17th. The Chinese side participating in the signing ceremony included Li Qingquan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Czechoslovakia, and embassy officials. The Czechoslovak side included Jiří Taufer, First Deputy Minister of Culture, Ka. Kurka, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and representatives from the Ministry of Education and the Academy of Sciences.
    Li Qingquan signed on behalf of the Chinese side, and Taufer signed on behalf of the Czechoslovak side.

### 中捷今年文化合作执行计划在布拉格签字

1954-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

    中捷今年文化合作执行计划在布拉格签字
    【新华社布拉格十八日电】中捷一九五四年文化合作执行计划十七日在布拉格签字。参加签字仪式的，中国方面是中国驻捷大使馆临时代办李清泉和使馆官员；捷克斯洛伐克方面是文化部第一副部长伊里·陶菲尔、外交部副部长卡·库尔卡和教育部、科学院的代表等。
    李清泉代表中国方面签了字，陶菲尔代表捷克斯洛伐克方面签了字。
