Title: Molotov and Eden Agree to Hold Nine-Power Foreign Ministers Conference to Discuss Basic Issues of Restoring Peace in Indochina; Subcommittee of Local Military Conference in Vietnam Continues to Study the Implementation of the Ceasefire

Author: 
Date: 1954-07-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 17th: It is reported that Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov and British Foreign Secretary Eden, in their talks, determined that the next Nine-Power Foreign Ministers Conference is scheduled to be held on July 18th.

Molotov and Eden agreed that the meeting would be held at 4 p.m. on Sunday. At this meeting, the basic issues of restoring peace in Indochina will be discussed. In the recent period, many informal talks have been held on these issues.

Xinhua News Agency, July 18th: Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, July 18th: The subcommittee of the local military conference responsible for studying the implementation of the ceasefire continued its work in a good atmosphere from 7:30 a.m. to 10:30 a.m. on July 17th.

There will be no meeting on Sunday, July 18th.

### 莫洛托夫和艾登商定举行九国外长会议  讨论恢复印度支那和平基本问题  越南当地军事会议小组委员会继续研究执行停火的问题

1954-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫和艾登商定举行九国外长会议
    讨论恢复印度支那和平基本问题
    越南当地军事会议小组委员会继续研究执行停火的问题
    【新华社日内瓦十七日电】据悉，苏联外交部部长莫洛托夫在和英国外交大臣艾登会谈中确定，下次九国外长会议订于七月十八日举行。
    莫洛托夫和艾登同意会议在星期天下午四时举行，在这次会议中，将讨论恢复印度支那和平的基本问题。在最近期间，关于这些问题曾进行了许多非正式的会谈。
    【新华社十八日讯】越南通讯社北越十八日讯：负责研究执行停火问题的当地军事会议小组委员会在七月十七日上午七时三十分到十时三十分继续在良好空气中进行工作。
    七月十八日星期日，无会。
