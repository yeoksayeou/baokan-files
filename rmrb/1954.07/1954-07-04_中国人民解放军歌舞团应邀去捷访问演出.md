Title: Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe Invited to Visit and Perform in Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1954-07-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 3rd:

    The Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe, consisting of 270 members, departed for Czechoslovakia on the invitation of the Czechoslovak Ministry of National Defense for a performance tour. They will also visit and perform in the Soviet Union, Poland, and Romania in return for previous performances. The Song and Dance Troupe, led by Chen Yi, the head of the troupe and Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government, left Beijing on the afternoon of the 3rd.

    Those who came to the station to see them off included Xiao Hua and Gan Siqi, Deputy Directors of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government, Liu Qiren, Deputy Secretary-General, Liu Zhijian, Director of the Propaganda Department, Li Zhaobing, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture, Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government, Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Zhao Feng, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, as well as more than 600 literary and art workers from the armed forces and the capital.

    Also present at the station to see them off were Šmíd, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, and Colonel Procházka, Military Attaché.

    Also present at the station to see them off were Guo Zuowen, Romanian Ambassador to China, Lieutenant Colonel Dublin, Military Attaché, Colonel Fomin, Chief Deputy Military Attaché of the Soviet Embassy in China, and Deng Bosi, Attaché, and Captain Daskiewicz, Deputy Military Attaché of the Polish Embassy in China.

    Prior to their departure, the Czechoslovak Embassy in China hosted a banquet in honor of the leaders and some performers of the Song and Dance Troupe.

    Before going abroad, the Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe prepared many programs reflecting the combat life of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and our country's excellent folk songs and dances.

### 中国人民解放军歌舞团应邀去捷访问演出

1954-07-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民解放军歌舞团应邀去捷访问演出
    【新华社三日讯】中国人民解放军歌舞团一行二百七十人，应捷克斯洛伐克国防部的邀请，前往捷克斯洛伐克作访问演出，并将到苏联、波兰、罗马尼亚进行访问和答谢演出。歌舞团在团长、中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部文化部部长陈沂率领下，在三日下午离京。
    到车站欢送的，有中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华、甘泗淇，副秘书长刘其人，宣传部部长刘志坚，文化部副部长李兆炳，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会办公厅主任萧向荣，中央人民政府外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航，中央人民政府文化部办公厅主任赵沨，以及部队和首都的文艺工作者等六百余人。
    到车站欢送的还有捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆临时代办石密茨，武官普罗查上校。
    到车站欢送的还有罗马尼亚驻华大使郭佐文，武官杜布林中校，苏联驻华首席副武官富敏科上校，波兰驻华随员邓博思、副武官达斯凯维奇大尉等。
    启程前，捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使馆曾设宴为歌舞团负责人和部分演员饯行。
    中国人民解放军歌舞团在出国前，准备了许多反映中国人民解放军战斗生活和我国优秀的民间歌舞的节目。
