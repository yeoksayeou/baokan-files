Our Great Motherland
The high-quality cotton produced in Xinjiang is being supplied in large quantities to various cotton textile factories in Shanghai. This cotton has uniform fiber length, making it suitable for spinning the most currently needed 20-count yarn, and can also be used to spin 32-count yarn. The picture shows workers at the Shanghai Purchasing and Supply Station of the China National Cotton Yarn and Cloth Corporation handling and counting Xinjiang cotton.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新疆出产的优良品种棉花正大量供应上海各棉纺织厂的需要。这种棉花的纤维长度整齐，适合纺目前需要量最大的二十支纱，也可以纺三十二支纱。图为中国花纱布公司上海采购供应站的工人搬运和清点新疆棉花。
