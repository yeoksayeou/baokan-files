Title: Molotov Returns to Moscow

Author:
Date: 1954-07-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd: TASS Moscow, July 22nd: V.M. Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, returned to Moscow from Geneva on July 22nd after the Geneva Foreign Ministers Conference.

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 22nd: V.M. Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, left Geneva for Moscow on July 22nd.

Seeing him off at the airport were Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, representatives of the Soviet delegation, the delegation of the People's Republic of China, and the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam attending the Geneva Conference, experts and advisors of each delegation, and V.V. Molochkov, Soviet Minister to Switzerland.

Also seeing him off at the airport was Charles Duboule, President of the Geneva State Government.

### 莫洛托夫外长回到莫斯科

1954-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫外长回到莫斯科
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十二日讯：苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部部长莫洛托夫，在日内瓦外长会议结束后，二十二日从日内瓦回到莫斯科。
    【新华社日内瓦二十二日电】七月二十二日，苏联部长会议第一副主席、苏联外交部部长莫洛托夫离日内瓦回莫斯科。
    在机场送行的有中华人民共和国政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来、越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外交部部长范文同，出席日内瓦会议的苏联代表团、中华人民共和国代表团和越南民主共和国代表团的代表，各代表团的专家和顾问，苏联驻瑞士公使弗·弗·莫洛奇科夫。
    到机场送行的还有日内瓦州政府主席查·杜博莱。
