Title: Heads of Press Departments of Soviet, Vietnamese, and Chinese Delegations Jointly Host Cocktail Reception for Journalists from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1954-07-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 21st: The heads of the press departments of the delegations attending the Geneva Conference – Ilyichev of the Soviet delegation, Hoang Van Hoan of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam delegation, and Huang Hua of the People's Republic of China delegation – jointly hosted a cocktail reception at the "Hotel Metropole" here on the evening of the 21st to entertain journalists from various countries covering the conference. More than 240 journalists attended the reception. The reception proceeded in a warm and joyful atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 21st: Huang Hua, spokesman for the Chinese delegation to the Geneva Conference, held a press conference on the afternoon of the 21st, after the successful conclusion of the final plenary session on the Indochina issue. Huang Hua briefed the journalists on the situation of the meeting and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's speech at the meeting, and answered questions raised by the journalists.

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 21st: Hoang Van Hoan, spokesman for the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Geneva Conference, held a press conference at the "Palace of Nations" here on the afternoon of the 21st. Hoang Van Hoan briefed the journalists on the situation of the meeting on the 21st and answered questions raised by the journalists.

### 苏、越、中三国代表团新闻处负责人  联合举行鸡尾酒会招待各国记者

1954-07-23
第3版()
专栏：

    苏、越、中三国代表团新闻处负责人
    联合举行鸡尾酒会招待各国记者
    【新华社日内瓦二十一日电】出席日内瓦会议的苏联代表团新闻处负责人伊利切夫、越南民主共和国代表团新闻处负责人黄文欢和中华人民共和国代表团新闻处负责人黄华，二十一日晚在这里的“大都会饭店”联合举行鸡尾酒会，招待采访会议的各国记者。出席酒会的记者有二百四十余人。酒会在热烈、欢腾的气氛中进行。
    【新华社日内瓦二十一日电】出席日内瓦会议的中国代表团发言人黄华二十一日下午在关于印度支那问题的最后一次全体会议圆满结束后举行了记者招待会。黄华向记者们介绍了会议的情况和周恩来外长在会上的发言，并回答了记者们提出的问题。
    【新华社日内瓦二十一日电】越南民主共和国出席日内瓦会议代表团发言人黄文欢二十一日下午在此间“国联大厦”举行了记者招待会。黄文欢向记者们介绍了二十一日会议的情况，并回答了记者们提出的问题。
