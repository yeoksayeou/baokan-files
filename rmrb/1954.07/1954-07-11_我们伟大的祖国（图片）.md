Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-07-11
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
　　This year, the mutual aid and cooperation movement in our country's rural areas has greatly developed. In Minji Village, Suihua County, Heilongjiang Province, all 180 households in the village, except for two non-agricultural households, have joined agricultural production cooperatives. This is a scene of the members of the first production team of the "July 1st" agricultural production cooperative in Minji Village, which has been awarded the title of Red Flag Winning Team, weeding in the wheat field.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
　　今年我国农村的互助合作运动有了很大的发展。黑龙江省绥化县民吉村全村一百八十户，除两户非农业户外，已全部加入农业生产合作社。这是民吉村“七一”农业生产合作社得到红旗优胜队称号的第一生产队的队员们，正在麦地里铲地的情形。
