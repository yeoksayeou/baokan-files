Title: Vietnamese Cooperative Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-07-22
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, July 21st: The Vietnamese delegation, led by Bui Bao Van, and members Pham Ngoc Tan and Nguyen An, invited by the All-China Federation of Cooperatives to attend the First Representative Congress of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives, arrived in Beijing by train on the 21st. Meng Yongqian, Deputy Director of the Provisional Council of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives, and others welcomed them at the station.

### 越南合作社代表团昨日到达北京

1954-07-22
第3版()
专栏：

    越南合作社代表团昨日到达北京
    【新华社二十一日讯】应中华全国合作社联合总社的邀请前来参加中华全国合作社第一次代表大会的越南代表团团长裴宝云，团员范玉新、阮安，在二十一日乘火车到达北京。中华全国合作社联合总社临时理事会副主任孟用潜等曾到车站欢迎。
