Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-07-26
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Thanks to the peasants' active efforts to overcome natural disasters and strengthen field management, most areas of Fujian Province have increased production this year. The 980,000 mu of early rice in the Minhou Special District have generally achieved a bumper harvest. The picture shows members of an agricultural cooperative in Pandun Township, Minhou Special District, selecting seeds in the abundant rice fields.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    由于农民积极战胜自然灾害，加强田间管理，今年福建大部地区增产，闽侯专区九十八万亩早稻普遍获得丰收。图为闽侯专区潘墩乡的一个农业合作社的社员们在丰产的稻田里进行选种。
