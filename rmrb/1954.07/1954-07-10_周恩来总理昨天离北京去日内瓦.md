Title: Premier Zhou Enlai left Beijing for Geneva yesterday
Author: 
Date: 1954-07-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 9th: Zhou Enlai, Chief Representative of the Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Geneva Conference, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 9th for Geneva to continue efforts to achieve the restoration of peace in Indochina. Hoang Van Hoan, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the People's Republic of China, flew from Beijing on the same plane.

Those who went to the airport to see him off included: Lin Boqu, Dong Biwu, Chen Yun, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Deng Xiaoping, Shen Junru, Chen Shutong, He Long, Xi Zhongxun, Peng Zhen, Zhang Xiruo, Peng Zemin, Luo Ruiqing, Li Xiannian, Shen Yanbing, Lu Dingyi, Huang Kecheng, Xiao Hua, Zhang Bojun, Chen Shaoxian, Luo Longji, Zhang Naiqi, Shao Lizi, Gao Chongmin, Yang Shangkun, Cai Tingkai, Xu Deheng, Liu Ningyi, Hu Yaobang, Deng Yingchao, Liao Chengzhi, Zhang Hanfu, Wu Xiuquan, and more than a hundred others.

### 周恩来总理昨天离北京去日内瓦

1954-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理昨天离北京去日内瓦
    【新华社九日讯】出席日内瓦会议的中华人民共和国代表团首席代表、中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，在九日上午乘飞机离开北京，前往日内瓦，为争取恢复印度支那和平的实现而继续努力。同机飞离北京的，有越南民主共和国驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使黄文欢。
    前往机场欢送的，有：林伯渠、董必武、陈云、郭沫若、黄炎培、邓小平、沈钧儒、陈叔通、贺龙、习仲勋、彭真、张奚若、彭泽民、罗瑞卿、李先念、沈雁冰、陆定一、黄克诚、萧华、章伯钧、陈劭先、罗隆基、章乃器、邵力子、高崇民、杨尚昆、蔡廷锴、许德珩、刘宁一、胡耀邦、邓颖超、廖承志、章汉夫、伍修权等百余人。
