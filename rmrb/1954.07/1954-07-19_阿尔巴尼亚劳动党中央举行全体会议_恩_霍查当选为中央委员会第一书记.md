Title: Albanian Labor Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session; Enver Hoxha Elected First Secretary of the Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1954-07-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, July 17th: The Albanian Telegraph Agency reported that the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party held a plenary session on the 12th. The meeting listened to and discussed the report made by Enver Hoxha on behalf of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee, "On the tasks of consolidating the Party and further improving the work of Party and state organs."

The plenary session adopted Hoxha's report as an instruction to the entire Party.

The meeting decided to abolish the post of General Secretary of the Party and elected Hoxha as the First Secretary of the Central Committee.

Due to the transfer of M. Mufti and Y. Pashko to leading positions in the government, the plenary session relieved them of their positions as secretaries of the Central Committee. The meeting also elected G. Nushi and L. Belišova as secretaries of the Central Committee.

### 阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央举行全体会议  恩·霍查当选为中央委员会第一书记

1954-07-19
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央举行全体会议
    恩·霍查当选为中央委员会第一书记
    【新华社十八日讯】据塔斯社地拉那十七日讯：阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会十二日举行了全体会议。会议听取并讨论了恩·霍查代表中央委员会政治局所作的“关于巩固党和进一步改进党和国家机关的工作的任务”的报告。
    全体会议通过霍查的报告作为对全党的指示。
    会议决定不再设置党总书记的职务，并选举霍查为中央委员会第一书记。
    由于马·穆夫蒂乌和约·帕什科转任政府的领导职务，全体会议免除了他们所任中央委员会书记的职务。会议并选出果·努什和利·别利绍娃为中央委员会书记。
