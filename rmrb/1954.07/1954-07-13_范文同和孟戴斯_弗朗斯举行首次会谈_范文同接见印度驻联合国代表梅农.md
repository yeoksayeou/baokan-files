Title: Pham Van Dong and Mendès France Hold First Meeting; Pham Van Dong Receives Indian Representative to the UN, Menon
Author: 
Date: 1954-07-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 11th: Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Foreign Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and French Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Mendès France held their first meeting on the evening of the 11th. The meeting took place in a harmonious atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 11th: Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Foreign Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, received Menon, the Indian representative to the United Nations, on the afternoon of the 11th and exchanged views on issues related to the Geneva Conference.

### 范文同和孟戴斯—弗朗斯举行首次会谈  范文同接见印度驻联合国代表梅农

1954-07-13
第1版()
专栏：

    范文同和孟戴斯—弗朗斯举行首次会谈
    范文同接见印度驻联合国代表梅农
    【新华社日内瓦十一日电】越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外长范文同和法国总理兼外长孟戴斯—弗朗斯在十一日晚举行首次会谈，会谈在和谐的空气中进行。
    【新华社日内瓦十一日电】越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外长范文同在十一日下午接见印度驻联合国代表梅农，就有关日内瓦会议问题交换意见。
