Title: Molotov Receives Deputy Prime Minister Pham Van Dong; Deputy Prime Minister Pham Van Dong and Others Visit Lenin and Stalin's Mausoleum and Lay Wreaths
Author:
Date: 1954-07-30
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 29th: TASS Moscow, 28th: Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs Molotov, on the 28th, received Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

    Xinhua News Agency, 29th: According to TASS Moscow: The delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam attending the Geneva Conference, currently visiting Moscow, led by Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs Pham Van Dong, visited the Lenin and Stalin Mausoleum on the 28th and laid wreaths. Accompanying the visit were Nguyen Luong Bang, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Soviet Union, and Mukhin, First Secretary of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 莫洛托夫外长接见范文同副总理  范文同副总理等谒列宁斯大林墓并献花圈

1954-07-30
第1版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫外长接见范文同副总理
    范文同副总理等谒列宁斯大林墓并献花圈
    【新华社二十九日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十八日讯：苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部部长莫洛托夫，在二十八日接见了越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外交部部长范文同。
    【新华社二十九日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：目前正在莫斯科访问的越南民主共和国出席日内瓦会议的代表团，在副总理兼代理外交部部长范文同的率领下，二十八日拜谒了列宁斯大林陵墓，并献花圈。陪同拜谒的有越南民主共和国驻苏联大使阮良朋和苏联外交部交际司一等参事穆欣。
