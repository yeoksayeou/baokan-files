Title: Fourth National Convention of the Japan-China Friendship Association Opens in Osaka
Author:
Date: 1954-07-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, July 10th: The Fourth National Convention of the Japan-China Friendship Association opened in Osaka on the 9th and will last for three days. Among those attending the convention were Matsumoto Jiichiro, President of the Japan-China Friendship Association, Uchiyama Kanzo, Chairman of the Board of Directors, Saiyuanji Kōichi, Director, Yamamoto Kumaichi, Chairman of the Japan-China Trade Promotion Conference, and approximately 300 delegates from across the country.

The convention will discuss issues such as trade between Japan and China, cultural exchange, and the restoration of normal relations between the two countries, and will determine future work guidelines. At the plenary session on the 9th, general reports and local reports on the Japan-China friendship movement were heard.

### 日中友协第四届全国大会在大阪开幕

1954-07-12
第4版()
专栏：

    日中友协第四届全国大会在大阪开幕
    【新华社十日讯】东京消息：日中友好协会第四届全国大会九日在大阪开幕，大会将举行三天。出席大会的有日中友好协会会长松本治一郎、理事长内山完造、理事西园寺公一，日中贸易促进会议主席山本熊一和来自全国各地的代表共约三百人。
    大会将讨论日本和中国之间的贸易问题，文化交流问题和恢复两国的正常关系等问题，并确定今后的工作方针。九日的全体大会听取了关于日中友好运动的总报告和地方报告。
