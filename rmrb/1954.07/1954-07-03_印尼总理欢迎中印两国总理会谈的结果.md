Title: Indonesian Prime Minister Welcomes the Results of the Meeting Between the Prime Ministers of China and India
Author:
Date: 1954-07-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta: According to TASS, Indonesian Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo said in Parliament on the 1st that he welcomes the meeting between Premier Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister Nehru and the results of the meeting.

### 印尼总理欢迎中印两国总理会谈的结果

1954-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

    印尼总理欢迎中印两国总理会谈的结果
   【新华社二日讯】据塔斯社雅加达讯：印度尼西亚总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约一日在国会中说，他欢迎周恩来总理和尼赫鲁总理的会谈和会谈的结果。
