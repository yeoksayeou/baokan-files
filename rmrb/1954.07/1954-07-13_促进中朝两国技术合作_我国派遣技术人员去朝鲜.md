Title: Promote Technological Cooperation Between China and North Korea: China Sends Technical Personnel to North Korea
Author:
Date: 1954-07-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th: To promote technological cooperation between China and North Korea, the third group of Chinese technical personnel sent to North Korea to assist in production departed for North Korea on the direct Beijing-Pyongyang train on the morning of the 12th. Xu Xuehan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government, and representatives from relevant departments were at the station to see them off.

Li Zhongyi and Jin Guofu, counselors of the North Korean Embassy in China, were also at the station to see them off.

Prior to this, two groups of Chinese technical personnel had arrived in North Korea in May and June, respectively.

### 促进中朝两国技术合作  我国派遣技术人员去朝鲜

1954-07-13
第1版()
专栏：

    促进中朝两国技术合作
    我国派遣技术人员去朝鲜
    【新华社十二日讯】为了促进中朝两国技术合作，由中国派往朝鲜生产部门协助工作的第三批技术人员，已在十二日晨搭乘北京—平壤直达车前往朝鲜。到车站欢送的有我中央人民政府对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒及有关部门代表。
    朝鲜驻我国大使馆参赞李忠翼、金国辅也到车站欢送。
    在此以前，中国已有两批技术人员分别在五、六月间到达朝鲜。
