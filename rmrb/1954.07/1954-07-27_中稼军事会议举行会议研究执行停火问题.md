Title: Sino-Vietnamese Military Conference Holds Meeting to Study Implementation of Ceasefire

Author: 
Date: 1954-07-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 26th: The Sino-Vietnamese local military conference continued to study the ceasefire issue on July 25th, after a two-day break, and held two emergency meetings.

    In these two meetings, the delegation of the General Command of the Vietnam People's Army and the delegation of the General Command of the French Federation Forces in Indochina worked hard to complete the discussion on the ceasefire and prepared to sign documents on the implementation of the ceasefire.
    The morning meeting continued on July 26th. According to reliable sources, the Sino-Vietnamese local military conference will end on the 27th.

### 中稼军事会议举行会议研究执行停火问题

1954-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

    中稼军事会议举行会议研究执行停火问题
   【新华社二十六日讯】越南通讯社北越二十六日讯：中稼当地军事会议在两天没有举行会议以后，七月二十五日继续研究停火问题并举行了两次紧急会议。
    在这两次会议中，越南人民军总司令部代表团和印度支那法兰西联邦部队总司令部代表团努力完成关于停火的讨论，并为签订关于执行停火的文件进行准备。
    七月二十六日上午会议继续举行。据可靠人士称，中稼当地军事会议将于二十七日结束。
