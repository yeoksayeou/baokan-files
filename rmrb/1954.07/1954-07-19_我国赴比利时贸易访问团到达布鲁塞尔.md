Title: Chinese Trade Delegation Arrives in Brussels

Author: 
Date: 1954-07-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 17th: The Chinese trade delegation to Belgium arrived in Brussels at 8:00 PM on the 16th. Those who welcomed them at the airport included Mr. Ro. de Staercke, Chairman of the Belgian Industrial Federation, Mr. Herve, Chairman of the Foreign Trade Committee of the Belgian Industrial Federation, and Mr. Dalman, Chairman of the Economic Department of the Belgian Industrial Federation, Chairman of the Chemical Industry Federation, Chairman of the Iron and Steel Federation, Secretary of the Far East Committee, and Far East Manager of the S.A. des Ateliers de Constructions Electriques de Charleroi (ACEC), as well as reporters from the Belgian News Agency and "Red Flag Newspaper."

    Shi Zhiyong, head of the Chinese trade delegation to Belgium, issued the following statement at the airport:
    "The Chinese trade delegation is grateful for the opportunity to visit the Kingdom of Belgium at the invitation of the Belgian Industrial Federation. I would like to express my gratitude on behalf of the Chinese trade delegation.
    "There is a good record of trade relations between the People's Republic of China and the Kingdom of Belgium in the past. In order to further develop trade relations between China and Belgium, the Chinese trade delegation hopes to enhance mutual understanding through direct contact with the Belgian industrial and commercial sectors, through visits and discussions, and to jointly seek further possibilities for the development of trade between China and Belgium with Belgian friends.
    "Let us wish the development and continued expansion of Sino-Belgian trade, and I would like to take this opportunity to extend greetings from Chinese industrialists and businessmen to the Belgian people."
    The Chairman of the Belgian Industrial Federation issued a brief statement, expressing a warm welcome.
    The Chinese trade delegation to Belgium is scheduled to stay in Belgium for four or five days, visiting the ACEC, locomotive manufacturing plants, refrigeration equipment manufacturing plants, steel mills, textile machinery manufacturing plants, fertilizer plants, and rayon factories. It is also scheduled to visit the port of Antwerp on the 21st. During this period, discussions will be held with the five major industries of the Belgian Industrial Federation: machinery manufacturing, hardware industry, electrical and chemical engineering, textile industry, and import and export trade, as well as the Belgian International Trade Promotion Committee.

### 我国赴比利时贸易访问团到达布鲁塞尔

1954-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我国赴比利时贸易访问团到达布鲁塞尔
    【新华社日内瓦十七日电】中国赴比利时贸易访问团在十六日下午八时到达布鲁塞尔。到机场欢迎的有比利时工业联合会主席罗·德·斯达尔克先生，比利时工业联合会对外贸易委员会主席赫维尔和比利时工业联合会经济部主席、化工联合会主席、钢铁联合会主席、远东委员会秘书、沙城电器公司远东经理达尔曼以及比利时通讯社、“红旗报社”记者等。
    中国赴比利时贸易访问团团长石志昂在机场发表声明如下：
    “中国贸易访问团此次应比利时工业联合会的邀请，得有机会访问比利时王国。我谨代表中国贸易访问团表示感谢。
    中华人民共和国和比利时王国之间的贸易关系，过去已有很好的记录。为了进一步发展中、比间的贸易关系，中国贸易访问团希望能通过此次和比利时工商业界的直接接触，通过参观和交谈，增进彼此的了解，并和比利时朋友一道共同寻求进一步发展中、比贸易的可能性。
    让我们预祝中、比贸易的发展和继续扩大，我并愿乘此机会代表中国工商业者向比利时人民致意。”
    比利时工业联合会主席发表了简单的声明，表示热烈欢迎。
    中国赴比利时贸易访问团预定在比利时逗留四、五日，参观沙城电器公司、机车制造厂、冷藏设备制造厂、钢铁厂、纺织机器制造厂、肥田粉厂和人造丝工厂等。并订于二十一日去安特卫普参观港口。在此期间，并将和比利时工业联合会的机器制造业、五金工业、电工化工、纺织和进出口业五大行业以及比利时国际贸易促进委员会进行商谈。
