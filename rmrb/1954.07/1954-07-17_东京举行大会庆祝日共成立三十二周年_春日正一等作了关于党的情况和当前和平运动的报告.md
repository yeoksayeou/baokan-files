Title: Tokyo Holds Rally to Celebrate the 32nd Anniversary of the Founding of the Japanese Communist Party; Kasuga Shoichi and Others Report on the Party's Situation and the Current Peace Movement
Author:
Date: 1954-07-17
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 16th: Tokyo News: July 15th marked the 32nd anniversary of the founding of the Japanese Communist Party. The JCP Central Guidance Department, the Kanto Regional Committee, and the Tokyo Metropolitan Committee jointly held a celebration rally in Tokyo on the evening of the 14th. More than a thousand people attended the rally.
    At the meeting, Kasuga Shoichi, chairman of the JCP Central Guidance Department, and other party leaders gave reports on the party's situation and the current peace movement.

### 东京举行大会庆祝日共成立三十二周年  春日正一等作了关于党的情况和当前和平运动的报告

1954-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

    东京举行大会庆祝日共成立三十二周年
    春日正一等作了关于党的情况和当前和平运动的报告
    【新华社十六日讯】东京消息：七月十五日是日本共产党成立三十二周年。日共中央指导部、关东地方委员会和东京都委员会十四日晚在东京联合举行了庆祝大会。参加大会的有一千多人。
    会上，日共中央指导部主席春日正一和党的其他领导人作了关于党的情况和当前和平运动的报告。
