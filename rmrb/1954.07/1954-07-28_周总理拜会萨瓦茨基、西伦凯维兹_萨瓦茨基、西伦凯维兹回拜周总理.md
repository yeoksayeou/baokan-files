Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Visits Zawadzki and Cyrankiewicz; Zawadzki and Cyrankiewicz Return the Visit to Premier Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1954-07-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 27th: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, visited Aleksander Zawadzki, Chairman of the Council of State of the People's Republic of Poland, at 12:30 noon on the 26th.
	Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, and Wang Bingnan, Secretary-General of the delegation to the Geneva Conference, accompanied Premier Zhou Enlai. Zeng Yongquan, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the People's Republic of Poland, also accompanied the visit.
	Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 27th: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, visited Józef Cyrankiewicz, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Poland, at 5:45 p.m. on the 26th.
	Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, and Wang Bingnan, Secretary-General of the delegation to the Geneva Conference, accompanied Premier Zhou Enlai. Zeng Yongquan, Chinese Ambassador to Poland, also accompanied the visit.
	Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 27th: Aleksander Zawadzki, Chairman of the Council of State of the People's Republic of Poland, returned the visit to Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China at 5:00 p.m. on the 26th. Present during the return visit were Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Wang Bingnan, Secretary-General of the Chinese delegation to the Geneva Conference, and Zeng Yongquan, Chinese Ambassador to Poland.
	Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 27th: Józef Cyrankiewicz, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Poland, returned the visit to Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, at 6:30 p.m. on the 27th. Present were Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, and Zeng Yongquan, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Poland.

### 周总理拜会萨瓦茨基、西伦凯维兹  萨瓦茨基、西伦凯维兹回拜周总理

1954-07-28
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理拜会萨瓦茨基、西伦凯维兹
    萨瓦茨基、西伦凯维兹回拜周总理
    【新华社华沙二十七日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，在二十六日中午十二时三十分拜会了波兰人民共和国国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基。
    随同周恩来总理前往的有我中央人民政府外交部副部长张闻天和我参加日内瓦会议代表团秘书长王炳南。中华人民共和国驻波兰人民共和国大使曾涌泉也陪同前往。
   【新华社华沙二十七日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，在二十六日下午五时四十五分拜会了波兰人民共和国部长会议主席西伦凯维兹。
    随同周恩来总理前往的有我中央人民政府外交部副部长张闻天和我出席日内瓦会议代表团秘书长王炳南。我国驻波兰大使曾涌泉也陪同前往。
   【新华社华沙二十七日电】波兰人民共和国国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基，在二十六日下午五时回拜中华人民共和国总理周恩来。回拜时在座的有我中央人民政府外交部副部长张闻天、我国参加日内瓦会议代表团秘书长王炳南和我国驻波兰大使曾涌泉。
   【新华社华沙二十七日电】波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹在二十七日下午六时三十分回拜中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来。在座的有中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部副部长张闻天和中华人民共和国驻波兰大使曾涌泉。
