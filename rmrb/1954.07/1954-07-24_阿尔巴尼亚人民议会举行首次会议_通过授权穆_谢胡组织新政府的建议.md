Title: Albanian People's Assembly Holds First Session; Approves Recommendation to Authorize Mehmet Shehu to Organize New Government
Author:
Date: 1954-07-24
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 22nd -- According to TASS: The first session of the third People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania, elected in May this year, opened in Tirana on the 19th.
    Gogo Nushi was elected Chairman of the People's Assembly, and Fiqret Shehu was elected Vice-Chairman.
    The meeting adopted the following agenda: Approving the rules of procedure of the People's Assembly; electing various committees; ratifying the decrees promulgated by the Presidium of the People's Assembly; amending certain articles of the Constitution; electing the Presidium of the People's Assembly; ratifying the bill on reorganizing certain ministries of the government; organizing the Council of Ministers; replacing the members of the Supreme Court of the People's Republic of Albania.
    The meeting elected members of the Budget Committee, the Legislative Committee, and the Foreign Affairs Committee.
    Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, made a statement resigning from the government. Hoxha emphasized that the current government had implemented the Albanian Party of Labour's plan to build the foundation of socialism in the country. He thanked the Labour Party and the People's Assembly for the boundless honor and trust they had given him in the position of Chairman of the Council of Ministers for ten years. On behalf of the Central Committee of the Labour Party, he proposed authorizing Mehmet Shehu, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Labour Party, to organize the new government of the Republic.
    After discussion, the meeting unanimously approved Hoxha's recommendation to authorize Mehmet Shehu to organize the new government.
    The meeting is continuing.

### 阿尔巴尼亚人民议会举行首次会议  通过授权穆·谢胡组织新政府的建议

1954-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚人民议会举行首次会议
    通过授权穆·谢胡组织新政府的建议
    【新华社二十二日讯】据塔斯社地拉那讯：今年五月间选出的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国第三届人民议会第一次会议十九日在地拉那开幕。
    果·努什当选为人民议会主席、费·谢胡当选为副主席。
    会议通过议程如下：批准人民议会议事规则：选举各委员会；批准人民议会主席团所颁布的法令；修改宪法的某些条款；选举人民议会主席团；批准关于改组政府某些部的法案；组织部长会议；更换阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国最高法院的成员。
    会议选出了预算、立法和外交三个委员会的委员。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记、部长会议主席恩·霍查提出政府辞职的声明。霍查强调指出，本届政府已经执行了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党关于在国内建设社会主义基础的计划。他感谢劳动党和人民议会十年来给予他所担任的部长会议主席这一职务的无限荣誉和信任。他代表劳动党中央委员会建议授权劳动党中央委员会政治局委员穆·谢胡组织共和国的新政府。
    会议经过讨论后，一致通过霍查所提出的授权穆·谢胡组织新政府的建议。
    会议在继续进行。
