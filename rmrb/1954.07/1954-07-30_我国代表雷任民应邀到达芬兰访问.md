Title: Chinese Representative Lei Renmin Arrives in Finland for Visit at Invitation
Author: 
Date: 1954-07-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, July 29th: Lei Renmin, representative of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, arrived in Helsinki, the capital of Finland, by plane from Moscow on July 28th, at the invitation of the Government of the Republic of Finland. Accompanying him were Chen Ming, Deputy Director of the Third Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and Zou Siyi, Commissioner of the Third Bureau.

Those who went to the airport to greet him included: Finnish Minister of Foreign Affairs Kekkonen, Finnish Envoy to China Sundström, Tarmairos, Director of the Commercial Department of the Finnish Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Mattila, Commercial Counselor of the Finnish Embassy in China.

Also present at the airport to welcome him were Soviet Envoy to Finland Lebedev and envoys and commercial representatives from various People's Democratic countries stationed in Finland.

Yao Dengshan, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Finland, Zhang Yunxiao, Commercial Commissioner, and all the staff of the embassy also greeted him at the airport.

Also at the airport were Ye Wenpei, Chairman of the Finnish-Chinese Association, Vice Chairman Tovi, Secretary-General Mrs. Laurikainen, Committee Member Aaltonen, and Luma, Deputy Head of the Finnish Cultural Delegation who visited China in 1953.

After getting off the plane, Lei Renmin accepted flowers from the welcomers and made a statement at the airport. He said: "I am very pleased and honored to be invited by the Finnish government to visit Finland shortly after the signing of the 1954 Trade Agreement between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Finland. I hope that this visit will help develop trade and economic relations between China and Finland, thereby enhancing the friendship between the peoples of China and Finland. Let me take this opportunity to express my sincere gratitude to the Finnish government for their hospitality and to extend my greetings to the Finnish people."

Lei Renmin will stay in Finland for several days. During his visit, he will talk with Finnish government officials and people from the business community, and visit major cities in Finland.

### 我国代表雷任民应邀到达芬兰访问

1954-07-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我国代表雷任民应邀到达芬兰访问
      【新华社赫尔辛基二十九日电】应芬兰共和国政府邀请到芬兰访问的中华人民共和国中央人民政府代表、对外贸易部副部长雷任民七月二十八日由莫斯科乘飞机抵达芬兰首都赫尔辛基。陪同前来的有对外贸易部第三局副局长陈明和第三局专员邹斯颐。
    前往机场迎接的有：芬兰共和国外交部部长吉科宁、芬兰驻我国公使孙士敦、芬兰外交部商务司司长塔尔麦罗斯、芬兰驻我国公使馆商务参赞马狄拉。
    前往机场迎接的还有苏联驻芬兰公使列别杰夫和各人民民主国家驻芬使节和商务代表。
    我国驻芬兰公使馆临时代办姚登山、商务专员张云啸和使馆全体人员也在机场迎接。
    在机场迎接的还有芬中协会主席叶文佩、副主席托维、秘书长劳里凯南夫人、委员阿尔托宁和曾于一九五三年来我国访问的芬兰文化代表团副团长卢玛等。
    下飞机后，雷任民接受了欢迎者的献花并在机场发表谈话。他说：“我能在中华人民共和国和芬兰共和国一九五四年贸易协定签订之后不久承芬兰政府的邀请来芬兰访问感到非常愉快和荣幸。我希望这次访问有助于中芬两国之间贸易经济关系的发展，从而增强中芬两国人民间的友谊。让我乘此机会向芬兰政府的盛意表示衷心的感谢并向芬兰人民致意。”
      雷任民将在芬兰逗留数日，在访问期间将与芬兰政府官员和工商界人士交谈，并赴芬兰各大城市参观。
