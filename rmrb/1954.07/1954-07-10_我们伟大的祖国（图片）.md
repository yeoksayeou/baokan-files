Author: 
Date: 1954-07-10
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The electromechanical installation project of the newly built potassium chlorate factory in Lushun-Dalian is nearing completion. Currently, workers are installing various equipment such as electrolysis chambers, the automated control panel in the basement, and underground pipeline projects, striving to start production on National Day. Workers are installing electrolytic cells in the electrolysis chamber here.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    旅大新建的氯酸钾工厂的机电安装工程已接近完工。现在工人们正在安装电解室、地下室的自动化操纵台和地下管道工程的各项设备，争取在国庆节开工生产。工人们正在这里安装电解室中的电解槽。
