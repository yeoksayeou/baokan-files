Title: Mass Rally Held in Warsaw to Welcome Premier Zhou; Polish State Council Awards Premier Zhou Enlai with Medal
Author:
Date: 1954-07-29
Page: 1
	
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 28th: A mass rally was held in Warsaw on the 27th by the Polish National Front Committee to welcome Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China.

	Accompanying Premier Zhou Enlai to the rally were: Zhang Wentian, representative of the Chinese delegation to the Geneva Conference and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Wang Bingnan, Secretary-General; and Qiao Guanhua and Chen Jiakang, advisors.

	Also attending the rally were all members of the Chinese Technical and Technological Science Delegation headed by Wang Shoudao and the Chinese Cultural Delegation headed by Yang Hansheng, who are currently visiting Poland. Zeng Yongquan, Chinese Ambassador to Poland, also attended the rally.

	The Polish side attending the rally included: Bolesław Bierut, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party (PZPR) and Chairman of the Polish National Front Committee; Aleksander Zawadzki, Chairman of the Polish State Council; Józef Cyrankiewicz, Chairman of the Polish Council of Ministers; Marian Nowak, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers; Konstantin Rokossovsky, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Marshal of Poland; Jakub Berman, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers (all six of whom are members of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the PZPR); Edward Ochab, Roman Zambrowski, and Franciszek Jóźwiak, members of the Politburo and Secretaries of the Central Committee of the PZPR; Józef Jóźwiak-Witold and Julian Radkiewicz, members of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the PZPR; Hilary Minc and Eugeniusz Chełchowski, alternate members of the Politburo; Stanisław Baczyński, Chairman of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Democratic Party and Vice Chairman of the State Council; Ignacy Matuszewski, Deputy Chairman of the Supreme Executive Committee of the United Peasant Party and Vice Chairman of the State Council; Foreign Minister Stanisław Skrzeszewski; Wiktor Kłosiewicz, Chairman of the Central Council of Polish Trade Unions and member of the State Council; State Council Secretary Rybicki; Władysław Pawlak, First Secretary of the Warsaw Committee of the PZPR; Albrecht, Chairman of the Presidium of the Warsaw People's Council; Wende, Secretary-General of the Polish Committee for Cultural Cooperation with Foreign Countries; Eugenia Musiałowa, Chairman of the Central Council of the Polish Women's League and member of the State Council; Vignar, Secretary of the Central Council of the Polish Youth League; Parliamentarian Hołodynski, as well as over 6,000 advanced workers from industrial enterprises in Warsaw and people from all walks of life.

	Soviet Ambassador to Poland Mikhailov also attended the rally.

	The rally began with an opening speech by Bolesław Bierut, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party and Chairman of the Polish National Front Committee (full text to be released separately).

	Then, amid warm applause, Premier Zhou Enlai stepped onto the podium and delivered a speech to the welcomers (full text to be released separately).

	Chairman of the Polish Council of Ministers Józef Cyrankiewicz also spoke at the rally (full text to be released separately).

	Next, Aleksander Zawadzki, Chairman of the State Council of the Polish People's Republic, read out the State Council's resolution, passed on the recommendation of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party and the Polish government, regarding the awarding of a medal to Premier Zhou Enlai: "The State Council of the Polish People's Republic hereby awards Mr. Zhou Enlai, outstanding son of the Chinese people, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, the Order of Polonia Restituta, First Class, to express the fraternal friendship between the Polish People's Republic and the People's Republic of China, to commend his contribution to the maintenance of world peace, and to reward his merits in promoting comprehensive cooperation and sincere friendship between the Polish and Chinese peoples."

	After reading the resolution, State Council Chairman Zawadzki pinned the Order of Polonia Restituta, First Class, on Premier Zhou Enlai's chest.

	After receiving the medal, Premier Zhou Enlai gave a speech of thanks for the honor bestowed upon him. His speech was warmly applauded by the masses.

	At the end of the meeting, Bolesław Bierut, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party and Chairman of the Polish National Front Committee, said: "I would like to once again ask Premier Zhou Enlai to convey our people's warm respect and most sincere brotherly feelings to the great 600 million Chinese people, the People's Government, the fraternal Chinese Communist Party, and its great leader Comrade Mao Zedong. The Polish people sincerely hope that the Chinese people will achieve further success in the work of building a new social system, in developing their economy, and in strengthening their country—one of the most powerful factors for peace in Asia, one of the great and invincible fortresses in the world peace camp headed by our common friend, the great and invincible Soviet Union."

### 华沙举行群众大会欢迎周总理  波兰国务委员会授予周恩来总理以勋章

1954-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

    华沙举行群众大会欢迎周总理
    波兰国务委员会授予周恩来总理以勋章
    【新华社华沙二十八日电】波兰全国阵线委员会二十七日在华沙举行群众大会，欢迎中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来。
    随同周恩来总理出席大会的有：出席日内瓦会议的我国代表团代表、外交部副部长张闻天，秘书长王炳南，顾问乔冠华、陈家康等。
    出席大会的还有：目前正在波兰访问的以王首道为首的我国技术与技术科学代表团和以阳翰笙为首的我国文化代表团全体团员。中国驻波兰大使曾涌泉也出席了大会。
    波兰方面出席大会的有：波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记、波兰全国阵线委员会主席贝鲁特，波兰国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基，波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹，部长会议第一副主席诺瓦克，部长会议副主席、波兰元帅罗科索夫斯基，部长会议副主席贝尔曼（以上六人均为统一工人党中央委员会政治局委员），统一工人党中央委员会政治局委员、中央委员会书记处书记奥哈布、马茹尔和德沃拉科夫斯基，统一工人党中央委员会政治局委员约兹维亚克—韦托德和拉德凯维奇、候补委员拉帕茨基和海乌霍夫斯基，民主党中央委员会主席团主席、国务委员会副主席巴齐科夫斯基，统一农民党最高执行委员会副主席、国务委员会副主席伊格纳尔，外交部部长斯克热歇夫斯基，波兰工会中央理事会主席、国务委员会委员克沃谢维奇，国务委员会秘书雷比茨基，统一工人党华沙委员会第一书记帕夫拉克，华沙人民会议主席团主席阿尔布列赫特，波兰对外文化合作委员会秘书长温德，波兰妇女联盟中央理事会主席、国务委员会委员穆霞沃娃，波兰青年联盟中央理事会书记维格纳，议会议员荷罗丁斯基，以及华沙的工业企业中的先进工作者和各界人民共六千多人。
    苏联驻波兰大使米哈伊洛夫也参加了大会。
    大会首先由波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记、波兰全国阵线委员会主席贝鲁特致开会词（全文另发）。
    接着，周恩来总理在热烈的掌声中走上了讲台，向欢迎者发表了讲话（全文另发）。
    波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹也在大会上讲了话（全文另发）。
    接着，波兰人民共和国国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基宣读了国务委员会根据波兰统一工人党中央委员会和波兰政府的建议通过的关于以勋章授予周恩来总理的决议：“波兰人民共和国国务委员会特授予中国人民的优秀儿子、中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来先生以一级波兰复兴勋章，以表示波兰人民共和国和中华人民共和国之间的兄弟友谊，表扬他对维护世界和平的贡献，并酬答他促进波兰人民和中国人民之间的全面合作和真挚友谊的功劳。”
    国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基在宣读决议完毕后，以一级波兰复兴勋章佩在周恩来总理的胸前。
    周恩来总理在接受勋章后致了答词，对给予他的荣誉表示感谢。他的答词获得了群众的热烈鼓掌欢迎。
    在会议结束时，波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记、波兰全国阵线委员会主席贝鲁特说：“我愿再一次要求周恩来总理把我国人民的热烈的敬意和最诚挚的兄弟感情转达给伟大的六亿中国人民、人民政府、兄弟般的中国共产党和它的伟大领袖毛泽东同志。波兰人民热烈希望中国人民在建设新社会制度的工作中，在发展他们的经济、加强他们的国家——亚洲和平的最有力的因素之一，以我们的共同朋友、伟大和无敌的苏联为首的世界和平阵营中的伟大和不可战胜的堡垒之一——的力量的事业中，获得进一步的成就。”
