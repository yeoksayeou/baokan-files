Title: Saudi Arabian Government Notifies the United States Government of the Abolition of the Two Countries' "Point Four Program" Agreement
Author: 
Date: 1954-07-14
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 11th: The Egyptian newspaper "Al-Akhbar" reported that the Saudi Arabian government notified the U.S. government on the 10th that it had abolished all agreements signed with the United States under Truman's Point Four technical "aid" program.

### 沙特阿拉伯政府通知美国  政府废除两国“第四点计划”协定

1954-07-14
第4版()
专栏：

    沙特阿拉伯政府通知美国
    政府废除两国“第四点计划”协定
    【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社开罗十一日讯：埃及“消息报”报道，沙特阿拉伯政府十日通知美国政府说，它已经把根据杜鲁门第四点技术“援助”计划和美国签订的所有协定废除了。
