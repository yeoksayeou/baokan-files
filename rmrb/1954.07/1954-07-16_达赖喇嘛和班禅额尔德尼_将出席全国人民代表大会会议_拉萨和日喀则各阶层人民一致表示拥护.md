Title: The Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni Will Attend the National People's Congress Meeting; People of All Walks of Life in Lhasa and Xigaze Unanimously Express Support
Author:
Date: 1954-07-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, July 15th. People from all walks of life, both monks and laypeople, in Lhasa and Xigaze unanimously expressed their support for the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni representing the people of Tibet at the National People's Congress. On June 14th, the Tibetan local government announced to all monks and laypeople in Tibet that the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni would go to Beijing. The announcement stated: The Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni will attend this year's National People's Congress meeting to pass the constitution and elect the Chairman of the People's Republic of China. This is a concrete manifestation of our Tibetan people returning to the big family of the motherland and enjoying equal rights among nationalities, and it will lay a good foundation for further strengthening the unity of all nationalities in the motherland and building a new Tibet.

The people of Lhasa from all walks of life feel honored that their ethnic group has representatives participating in the great event of the motherland. Kalon Sokong Wangqing Gelei of the Tibetan local government said in a statement: It is unprecedented for the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni to attend the National People's Congress meeting. In the past, the Tibetan people lived a dark life under the policy of national oppression. Today, we can have such rights, and we should thank the great leader Chairman Mao. Zasa Liuxia Tudeng Taba of the Tibetan local government said that the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni going to Beijing to attend the National People's Congress and meet Chairman Mao marks the great unity of all nationalities in the country and the unification of the motherland. This fact will be a heavy blow to any rumors and provocations by imperialism. Gongdelin Jinmeijicun, Yaoxi Zeren Zhuoma, and Qaba Gesang Wangdui, a member of the Lhasa Patriotic Youth Cultural Association, who have visited various parts of the motherland, also made statements, unanimously believing that after the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni visit the mainland this time to see the great achievements of the motherland's economic construction, it will enhance their confidence in building a new Tibet.

The people of all walks of life in Xigaze warmly congratulate the Panchen Erdeni on attending the National People's Congress meeting, and entrusted him and the Dalai Lama to pay their respects to Chairman Mao on behalf of the Tibetan people. The Panchen Kanpo Conference Hall Committee also held a celebration banquet to send off the Panchen Erdeni.

### 达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼  将出席全国人民代表大会会议  拉萨和日喀则各阶层人民一致表示拥护

1954-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

    达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼
    将出席全国人民代表大会会议
    拉萨和日喀则各阶层人民一致表示拥护
   【新华社拉萨十五日电】拉萨、日喀则两市各阶层僧俗人民对达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼将代表西藏人民出席全国人民代表大会会议，一致表示拥护。六月十四日西藏地方政府向西藏全体僧俗人民宣布达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼将去北京的文告。文告中说：达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼将要出席今年全国人民代表大会会议，通过宪法，选举中华人民共和国主席。这是我们藏族人民回到祖国大家庭享受民族平等权利的具体表现，对进一步加强祖国各民族的团结和为建设新西藏都将打下良好的基础。
    拉萨市各阶层人民都为本民族有代表参加祖国的大喜事感到光荣。西藏地方政府噶伦索康·旺清格来发表谈话说：达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼将出席全国人民代表大会会议，这是我们西藏人民从来没有过的事情。过去西藏人民在民族压迫的政策下过着黑暗的生活，今天能有这样的权利，我们应该感谢伟大的领袖毛主席。西藏地方政府扎萨柳霞·土登塔巴说，达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼将去北京出席全国人民代表大会会议并谒见毛主席，标志着全国各民族的大团结和祖国的统一。这一事实对帝国主义的任何造谣和挑拨将是一个沉重的打击。曾到祖国各地参观过的公德林·晋美吉村、尧西·泽仁卓玛、拉萨市爱国青年文化联谊会委员恰巴·格桑旺堆等人都发表了谈话，一致认为达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼这次去内地参观祖国经济建设的伟大成就后，将会增强他们建设新西藏的信心。
    日喀则市各阶层人民热烈庆贺班禅额尔德尼将出席全国人民代表大会会议，并拜托他和达赖喇嘛代表西藏人民向毛主席致敬。班禅堪布会议厅委员会并举行庆祝宴会，为班禅额尔德尼送行。
