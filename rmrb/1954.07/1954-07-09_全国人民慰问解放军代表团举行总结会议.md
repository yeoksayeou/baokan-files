Title: National People's Delegation to Console the People's Liberation Army Holds Summary Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-07-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 8th: The general delegation of the National People's Delegation to Console the People's Liberation Army held its final summary meeting on the 8th. Those present at the meeting included the head of the general delegation, Dong Biwu, deputy heads Chen Shutong, Teng Daiyuan, Liao Chengzhi, Liang Xi, Deng Yingchao, Li Zhuchen, Shi Liang, Wang Congwu, He Changgong, Xu Guangping, Yuan Renyuan, and Li Zaiwen, as well as the Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission, Gan Siqi, and the Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea, Liu Guanyi, and Deputy Secretary-General Cui Yueli. The general delegation decided to write a written summary report of the work of consoling the People's Liberation Army, which will be reported to the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the Standing Committee of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea, and the people of the whole country.

### 全国人民慰问解放军代表团举行总结会议

1954-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民慰问解放军代表团举行总结会议
    【新华社八日讯】全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团总团在八日举行最后的总结会议。出席会议的有总团长董必武，副总团长陈叔通、滕代远、廖承志、梁希、邓颖超、李烛尘、史良、王从吾、何长工、许广平、袁任远、栗再温，还有中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任甘泗淇，中国人民抗美援朝总会秘书长刘贯一、副秘书长崔月犁等。慰问团总团决定把慰问人民解放军的工作写成书面总结报告，向中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会、中国人民抗美援朝总会常务委员会和全国人民汇报。
