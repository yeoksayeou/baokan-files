Title: The Polish Government Accepts Molotov and Eden's Invitation to Participate in the International Supervisory and Monitoring Commission for the Indochina Armistice
Author:
Date: 1954-07-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 25th: TASS, Warsaw, July 24th: Polish newspapers report that Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov and British Foreign Secretary Eden, in their capacity as chairmen of the Geneva Conference, invited the Polish government on July 22nd on behalf of the conference to participate in the International Commission to supervise and monitor the implementation of the cessation of hostilities agreements in Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia.

The Polish government replied to Foreign Minister Molotov and Foreign Secretary Eden on July 23rd, stating that the Polish government welcomes the signing of the Indochina cessation of hostilities agreements, and declares that: In order to make its contribution to the cause of consolidating peace, the Polish government expresses its agreement to the participation of Polish representatives in the International Supervisory and Monitoring Commission.

### 波兰政府接受莫洛托夫和艾登的邀请  参加印度支那停战国际监督和监察委员会

1954-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰政府接受莫洛托夫和艾登的邀请
    参加印度支那停战国际监督和监察委员会
    【新华社二十五日讯】塔斯社华沙二十四日讯：波兰报纸报道：苏联外交部部长莫洛托夫和英国外交大臣艾登七月二十二日以日内瓦会议主席的身份代表会议邀请波兰政府参加监督和监察越南、老挝、柬埔寨停止敌对行动协定条款的履行的国际委员会。
    波兰政府七月二十三日答复莫洛托夫外长和艾登外交大臣说，波兰政府欢迎印度支那停止敌对行动协定的签订，并声明：波兰政府为了在巩固和平的事业中作出自己的贡献，表示同意波兰代表参加国际监督和监察委员会。
