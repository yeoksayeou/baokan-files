Title: Sino-Vietnamese Military Conference Subcommittee Continues to Study Ceasefire Issues
Author:
Date: 1954-07-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, North Vietnam, July 14th: The subcommittee of the Sino-Vietnamese Military Conference responsible for studying the implementation of the ceasefire held meetings from 8:00 to 11:00 AM and from 2:00 to 4:00 PM on July 13th. No meeting will be held on July 14th. The subcommittee will continue its work on the morning of the 15th.

### 中亦军事会议小组委员会续研究停火问题

1954-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中亦军事会议小组委员会续研究停火问题
    【新华社十四日讯】越南通讯社北越十四日讯：负责研究执行停火的问题的北越中亦军事会议的小组委员会，在七月十三日上午八时到十一时和下午二时到四时举行了会议。七月十四日将不举行会议。这个小组委员会将在十五日上午继续工作。
