    Our Great Motherland
    After adopting electric milking machines, the state-owned Wulidian Farm in the suburbs of Beijing has reduced the physical labor of workers and increased milk production. At the same time, because there is no contact with the air, the sanitary conditions of the milk are further guaranteed.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    北京市郊区国营五里店农场采用电器挤奶器挤奶后，减轻了工人的体力劳动和增加了牛奶产量，同时由于与空气没有接触，又进一步保证了牛奶的卫生条件。
