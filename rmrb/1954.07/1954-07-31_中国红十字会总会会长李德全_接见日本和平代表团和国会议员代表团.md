Title: Li Dequan, President of the All-China Women's Federation, Receives Japanese Peace Delegation and Diet Members Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-07-31
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, July 30th—Li Dequan, President of the All-China Women's Federation, received the visiting Japanese Peace Delegation and the Japanese Diet Members Delegation on the afternoon of the 29th. Wu Yunfu, Standing Director, and Zhao Anbo, Advisor of the Federation, were also present. Members of both the Japanese Peace Delegation and the Diet Members Delegation expressed their gratitude for the assistance of the Chinese Red Cross in helping Japanese nationals in China return to their country. President Li Dequan pointed out that since the large-scale repatriation of Japanese nationals had ended, the Chinese Red Cross had continued to provide assistance in accordance with the communique to individual Japanese nationals who applied to return. President Li Dequan then stated that, to her knowledge, a group of former Japanese soldiers who had committed various crimes would be pardoned in accordance with the lenient policy of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The Chinese Red Cross is prepared, after being entrusted by the Chinese government, to contact the Japanese Red Cross, the Japan Peace Liaison Committee, and the Secretariat of the Japan-China Friendship Association to coordinate the repatriation of these former Japanese soldiers. Finally, President Li Dequan expressed regret that the invitation from Mr. Tadatsugu Shimazu, President of the Japanese Red Cross, for a delegation from the Chinese Red Cross to visit Japan had not yet been realized. Members of both Japanese delegations unanimously stated that they would continue to work to promote the realization of a visit by a Chinese Red Cross delegation to Japan after their return home.
      【Further News】Hirano Yoshitaro, a member of the Japanese Peace Delegation, and Tanaka Toshio (Left-wing Socialist Party), Hori Makoto (Labor-Farmer Party), and Kuroda Hisao (Labor-Farmer Party), members of the Japanese Diet Members Delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 30th.

### 中国红十字会总会会长李德全  接见日本和平代表团和国会议员代表团

1954-07-31
第1版()
专栏：

    中国红十字会总会会长李德全
    接见日本和平代表团和国会议员代表团
    【新华社三十日讯】中国红十字会总会会长李德全二十九日下午分别接见了应邀来我国访问的日本和平代表团和日本国会议员代表团。陪同接见的还有该会常务理事伍云甫、顾问赵安博等人。日本和平代表团与国会议员代表团各团员对于中国红十字会协助在中国的日本侨民回国一事，一致表示感谢。李德全会长指出：自从大批日侨回国截止后，个别日侨有申请归国的，中国红十字会都按照公报，继续予以协助。李德全会长接着声称：据她所知，有一批犯有各种罪行的前日本军人将根据中国人民解放军的宽大政策，获得宽赦。中国红十字会准备在得到中国政府的委托之后，和日本红十字会、日本和平联络会、日中友好协会三团体联络事务局，就协助这批前日本军人的回国问题进行联系。最后，李德全会长对日本红十字会会长岛津忠承先生邀请中国红十字会代表团访问日本一事直到今天未能实现表示遗憾。日本两代表团团员一致认为他们回国后将继续努力促成中国红十字会代表团访问日本的实现。
      【又讯】应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的日本和平代表团团员平野义太郎，日本国会议员代表团团员田中稔男（左派社会党）、堀真琴（劳农党）和黑田寿男（劳农党）一共四人，在三十日下午乘飞机到达北京。
