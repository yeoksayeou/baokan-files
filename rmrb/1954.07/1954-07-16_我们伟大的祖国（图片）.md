Our Great Motherland
Students from many universities and colleges across the country carried out production internships during the summer vacation. This is a group of graduates from the Geophysical Prospecting Department of the Beijing College of Geology conducting internship work in the seismic team of a geological brigade. They are burying geophones here to detect changes in various underground rock formations.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    各地许多大、专学校的学生在暑期进行了生产实习。这是北京地质学院地球物理探矿系专修科本届毕业生在某地质大队地震队进行实习工作，他们正在这里埋设探明地下各种岩层变化的检波机。
