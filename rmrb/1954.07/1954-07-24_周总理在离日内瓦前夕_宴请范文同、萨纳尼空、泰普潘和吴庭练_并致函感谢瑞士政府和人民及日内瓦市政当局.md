Title: Premier Zhou Hosts a Banquet for Pham Van Dong, Phoui Sananikone, Tep Phan, and Ngo Dinh Luyen on the Eve of His Departure from Geneva, and Sends Letters of Gratitude to the Swiss Government and People, and the Geneva Municipal Authorities
Author:
Date: 1954-07-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 22nd: Zhou Enlai, Chief Representative of the Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Geneva Conference, hosted a banquet at 7:00 PM on the 22nd for Pham Van Dong, Chief Representative of the Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Phoui Sananikone, Head of Delegation of the Kingdom of Laos; Tep Phan, Head of Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia; and Ngo Dinh Luyen, Representative of the State of Vietnam.

Also attending the banquet from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam were Ta Quang Buu, Ha Van Lau, Dang Sinh, Tran Lam, and Hoang Van Hoan.

Also attending the banquet from the Kingdom of Laos were Kou Voravong, Phao Luang, and Phoumi.

Also attending the banquet from the Kingdom of Cambodia were Sang Sary, Tiaolong, Dap Chhuon, Pou Chai, and Truong Daransay.

Accompanying the Chinese delegation were Zhang Wentian, Li Kenong, Wang Bingnan, Qiao Guanhua, Chen Jiakang, Lei Yingfu, and Wang Zhuoru.

The banquet was held in a friendly atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 23rd: Prior to his departure from Geneva, Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, sent letters to Petitpierre, Minister of the Political Department (i.e., the Ministry of Foreign Affairs) of the Swiss Federal Government, and to Duboule, President of the Government of the Canton of Geneva, respectively, expressing his deep gratitude to the Swiss government and people, and to the Geneva municipal authorities and people, for their care and assistance to the conference and to the delegation of the People's Republic of China during the Geneva Conference.

### 周总理在离日内瓦前夕  宴请范文同、萨纳尼空、泰普潘和吴庭练  并致函感谢瑞士政府和人民及日内瓦市政当局

1954-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理在离日内瓦前夕
    宴请范文同、萨纳尼空、泰普潘和吴庭练
    并致函感谢瑞士政府和人民及日内瓦市政当局
    【新华社日内瓦二十二日电】中华人民共和国出席日内瓦会议代表团首席代表周恩来在二十二日晚七时宴请越南民主共和国代表团首席代表范文同、老挝王国代表团团长冯·萨纳尼空、柬埔寨王国代表团团长泰普潘、越南国代表吴庭练。
    越南民主共和国方面出席宴会的还有谢光宝、何文楼、邓性、陈林、黄元。
    老挝王国方面出席宴会的还有谷·高拉冯、泡銮、富尼。
    柬埔寨王国方面出席宴会的还有桑·萨里、刁隆、达普春、蒲柴、张达朗赛。
    中国代表团方面出席作陪的有张闻天、李克农、王炳南、乔冠华、陈家康、雷英夫、王倬如。
    宴会在友好的气氛中进行。
    【新华社日内瓦二十三日电】中华人民共和国总理兼外交部部长周恩来在离开日内瓦前分别致函瑞士联邦政府政治部（即外交部）部长彼蒂彼爱和日内瓦州政府主席杜博莱，对瑞士政府和人民及日内瓦市政当局和人民在日内瓦会议期间对会议和中华人民共和国代表团的照顾和帮助，表示深切的感谢。
