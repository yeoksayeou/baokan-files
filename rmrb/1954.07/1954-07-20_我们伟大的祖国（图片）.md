Author:
Date: 1954-07-20

Our Great Motherland
    The flood prevention work in Wuhan has received support from people all over the country. Large numbers of pumps have been transported from Beijing, Shenyang, Henan, and other places. Workers are working around the clock to install them and discharge the floodwater from Wuhan outside the dikes.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    武汉的防汛工作得到了全国各地人民的支援。北京、沈阳和河南等地都运去了大批的抽水机，工人们正夜以继日地进行安装，把武汉的渍水排出堤外。
