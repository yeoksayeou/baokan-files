Title: Soviet Union and Finland Sign a Five-Year Commodity Exchange Agreement
Author:
Date: 1954-07-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 17th: Following a series of negotiations, the Soviet Union and Finland signed an agreement in Moscow on July 17, 1954, concerning the exchange of goods for the period 1956-1960.

    The agreement stipulates that Soviet imports from Finland (the quantity stipulated in the 1954 protocol on the delivery of goods is approximately 580 million rubles) will increase to 590 million rubles in 1956, 595 million rubles in 1957, 630 million rubles in 1958, 640 million rubles in 1959, and 655 million rubles in 1960.

    On the other hand, the agreement stipulates that Soviet exports to Finland (which will be no less than 425 million rubles in 1954) will be 430 million rubles in 1956, 435 million rubles in 1957, 470 million rubles in 1958, 480 million rubles in 1959, and 495 million rubles in 1960.

    During the period from 1956 to 1960, the difference between Finland and Soviet deliveries will reach 160 million rubles annually, of which 120 million rubles will be compensated by agreements with other countries. Another option is for the Soviet Union to provide Finland with 40 million rubles in convertible currency each year.

    The agreement stipulates that the items imported by the Soviet Union from Finland from 1956 to 1960 will continue to be: products of the shipbuilding industry, machinery industry, cable industry and other metal processing industries, prefabricated houses, sawn timber, pulpwood, cellulose, paper, cardboard, and various paper products.

    The Soviet Union will mainly export: grain, sugar, feed, artificial fertilizers, liquid and solid fuels, metals, rolled metals, cotton, chemicals, and furs. In addition, the Soviet Union will export industrial equipment and automobiles to Finland.

### 苏联和芬兰签订为期五年的交换商品协定

1954-07-19
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联和芬兰签订为期五年的交换商品协定
    【新华社十八日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科十七日讯：苏联和芬兰在进行一系列的谈判之后，已于一九五四年七月十七日在莫斯科签订了一项关于在一九五六年—一九六零年交换商品的协定。
    协定规定：苏联从芬兰的输入（一九五四年交付商品议定书规定的数量约为五亿八千万卢布）在一九五六年增至五亿九千万卢布，一九五七年增至五亿九千五百万卢布，一九五八年增至六亿三千万卢布，一九五九年增至六亿四千万卢布，一九六零年增至六亿五千五百万卢布。
        另一方面，协定规定：苏联对芬兰的输出（一九五四年将不低于四亿二千五百万卢布）一九五六年为四亿三千万卢布，一九五七年为四亿三千五百万卢布，一九五八年为四亿七千万卢布，一九五九年为四亿八千万卢布，一九六零年为四亿九千五百万卢布。
    在一九五六年—一九六零年期间，芬兰和苏联交付的差额每年达一亿六千万卢布，其中将有一亿二千万卢布通过和其他国家订立协定来弥补，另一种办法是由苏联每年交给芬兰四千万卢布的可兑换的货币。
    协定规定，一九五六年—一九六零年苏联从芬兰输入的物品将像先前一样是：造船业、机器业、电缆工业以及其他金属加工工业的产品，预装配房屋，锯成的木材，制纸浆用的木材，纤维素，纸，纸板和各种纸产品。
    苏联输出的主要是：谷物、糖、饲料、人造肥料、液体和固体燃料、金属、压延金属、棉花、化学品和毛皮。此外苏联将向芬兰输出工业装备和汽车等。
