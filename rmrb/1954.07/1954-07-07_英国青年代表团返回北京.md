Title: British Youth Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-07-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: The British Youth Delegation, which visited China at the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation, returned to Beijing on the afternoon of the 3rd after visiting Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Tianjin, Shenyang, Fushun, and other places. The delegation visited factories, rural areas, hospitals, schools, and churches in various places, and also had contact and discussions with people from trade unions, religious circles, and other sectors.

### 英国青年代表团返回北京

1954-07-07
第4版()
专栏：

    英国青年代表团返回北京
    【新华社讯】应中华全国民主青年联合会邀请来我国访问的英国青年代表团，已在南京、上海、杭州、天津、沈阳、抚顺等地访问完毕，在三日下午返回北京。代表团在各地参观了工厂、农村、医院、学校和教堂，并曾和工会、宗教界等人士进行了接触和座谈。
