Title: U.S. House Foreign Affairs Committee Actually Passes Resolution Opposing the Restoration of China's Legitimate Rights in the United Nations
Author:
Date: 1954-07-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 14th. TASS, Washington dispatch: It is reported from Washington that the U.S. House Foreign Affairs Committee passed a resolution opposing the restoration of the legitimate rights of the People's Republic of China in the United Nations.

### 美众议院外交委员会竟通过决议  反对恢复我国在联合国合法权利

1954-07-18
第4版()
专栏：

    美众议院外交委员会竟通过决议
    反对恢复我国在联合国合法权利
    【新华社十五日讯】塔斯社纽约十四日讯：据华盛顿消息，美国众议院外交委员会通过一项决议，反对恢复中华人民共和国在联合国的合法权利。
