Title: Nehru States India Accepts Geneva Conference Mandate to Participate in International Supervisory and Control Commission for Indochina Ceasefire
Author:
Date: 1954-07-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, 26th - According to TASS, Indian Prime Minister Nehru said in a speech to the Indian National Congress National Committee in Ajmer on the 25th that the Geneva Conference had entrusted India with a major responsibility, and we accept this responsibility.

Nehru expressed confidence that, with the assistance and sincerity of all the people, India would be able to successfully complete its assigned mission. Nehru said that Indian representatives will serve as chairmen of the three International Supervisory and Control Commissions that will oversee the ceasefires in Laos, Cambodia, and Vietnam.

### 尼赫鲁表示印度接受日内瓦会议的委托  参加印度支那停战国际监督和监察委员会

1954-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁表示印度接受日内瓦会议的委托
    参加印度支那停战国际监督和监察委员会
   【新华社二十六日讯】据塔斯社德里讯：印度总理尼赫鲁二十五日在亚日米尔举行的印度国大党全国委员会上演说时说，日内瓦会议委托给印度一项重大的责任，我们接受这种责任。
    尼赫鲁表示信心说，由于全体人民的协助和诚意，印度能胜利地完成自己所担当的使命。尼赫鲁说，印度代表将在监察老挝、柬埔寨和越南停战的三个国际监督和监察委员会中担任主席。
