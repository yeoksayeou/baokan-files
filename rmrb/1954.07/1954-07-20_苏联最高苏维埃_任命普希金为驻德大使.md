Title: The Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Appoints Pushkin as Ambassador to Germany
Author:
Date: 1954-07-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 17th, TASS: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union has appointed G.M. Pushkin as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to the German Democratic Republic.

    The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union relieved V.S. Semyonov of his post as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to the German Democratic Republic, and he will be assigned to another position.

    The Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union appointed Pushkin as the Soviet High Commissioner to Germany. The Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union relieved Semyonov of his post as Soviet High Commissioner to Germany.

### 苏联最高苏维埃  任命普希金为驻德大使

1954-07-20
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃
    任命普希金为驻德大使
   【新华社十八日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十七日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团任命格·马·普希金为苏联驻德意志民主共和国特命全权大使。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团免除弗·斯·谢麦诺夫所担任的苏联驻德意志民主共和国特命全权大使的职务，另有任用。
    苏联部长会议任命普希金为苏联驻德国高级专员。苏联部长会议免除谢麦诺夫所担任的苏联驻德国高级专员的职务。
