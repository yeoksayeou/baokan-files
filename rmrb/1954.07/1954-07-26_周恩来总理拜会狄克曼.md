Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Visits Dieckmann
Author:
Date: 1954-07-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 25th: Premier of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, visited the acting President of the German Democratic Republic and Chairman of the People's Chamber, Dieckmann, at 1:30 p.m. on the 24th.

Accompanying Premier Zhou Enlai were Zhang Wentian, Wang Bingnan, Lei Renmin, Qiao Guanhua, and others.

Present at the meeting were the four Vice-Chairmen of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, Matern (Member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party), Goldenbaum (Chairman of the Democratic Farmers' Party), Gotting (General Secretary of the Christian Democratic Union), Homann (Vice-Chairman of the National Democratic Party), and members of the Presidium of the People's Chamber, Mrs. Hilmer-Plescher, Groh-Kummerlo, Erich Geisske, Albert, and Dr. Robedanz, Chairman of the Senate of the German Democratic Republic.

### 周恩来总理拜会狄克曼

1954-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理拜会狄克曼
    【新华社柏林二十五日电】中华人民共和国总理周恩来于二十四日下午一时三十分拜会了德意志民主共和国代理总统、人民议院主席狄克曼。
    随同周恩来总理进见的还有张闻天、王炳南、雷任民、乔冠华等。
    拜会时在座的有德意志民主共和国人民议院的四位副主席马特恩（统一社会党中央政治局委员）、戈登堡姆（民主农民党主席）、戈丁（基督教民主党总书记）、霍曼（国家民主党副主席）及人民议院主席团委员希尔默—普莱什尔夫人、格罗—库末尔洛、埃里希·盖斯克、艾柏特以及德意志民主共和国参议院主席罗贝丹兹博士。
