Our Great Motherland
The Yellow River Water Conservancy Commission is collecting hydrological data for the Yellow River Basin planning. An automatic cable car has been erected at the Balihutong Hydrological Station in the middle reaches to overcome the difficulties in measuring flood flow and sediment content during the flood season.
