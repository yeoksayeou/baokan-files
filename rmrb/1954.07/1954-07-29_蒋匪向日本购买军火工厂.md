Title: Chiang Kai-shek Bandits Purchasing Munitions Factory from Japan
Author:
Date: 1954-07-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, July 27th: According to Kyodo News Agency, the Chiang Kai-shek remnant bandits in Taiwan are currently in contact with the Osaka Machinery Works of the Japanese Mitsubishi Zaibatsu to order a complete set of equipment for a munitions factory worth 100 million yen, which will be used to manufacture 81mm and 60mm mortar shells. This is part of the Taiwan Chiang bandits' plan to establish a series of munitions industries using the US "Mutual Security Act Assistance."

### 蒋匪向日本购买军火工厂

1954-07-29
第4版()
专栏：

    蒋匪向日本购买军火工厂
    【新华社二十七日讯】东京消息：据共同社报道，台湾蒋介石残匪最近正在和日本三菱财阀的大阪机械制作所接洽订购一个共值一亿日元的制造八十一公厘和六十公厘迫击炮弹的军火工厂全套装备。这是台湾蒋匪用美国“共同安全法援助”建立一系列军火工业的计划的一部分。
