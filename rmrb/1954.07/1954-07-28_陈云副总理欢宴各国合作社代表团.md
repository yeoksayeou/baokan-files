Title: Vice Premier Chen Yun Hosts Banquet for Cooperative Delegations from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1954-07-28
Page: 2

    Vice Premier Chen Yun Hosts Banquet for Cooperative Delegations from Various Countries
    On the evening of the 26th, Chen Yun, Vice Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government, held a grand banquet to warmly welcome the cooperative delegations from the Soviet Union and various People's Democratic countries.
    The attendees of the banquet included: the Soviet cooperative delegation headed by Comrade Timofeev, the Mongolian cooperative delegation headed by Comrade Ganruerzhifu, the German Democratic Republic cooperative delegation headed by Comrade Lucht, the Korean cooperative delegation headed by Comrade Kim Jung-tae, the Czechoslovak cooperative delegation headed by Comrade Smrčka, the Romanian cooperative delegation headed by Comrade Dumitrescu, the Bulgarian cooperative delegation headed by Comrade Georgiev, and the Vietnamese delegation headed by Comrade Pei Baoyun.
    The ambassadors and embassy staff of the above-mentioned fraternal countries to China were also invited to attend the banquet.
    Soviet experts Seviakov and Fedotov also attended the banquet.
    Among those present to accompany the guests were: Zeng Shan, Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the State Council of the Central People's Government, Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Yao Yilin, Deputy Minister of Commerce, Li Fanwu, Deputy Minister of Forestry, and Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
    Also present were: Cheng Zihua, Zhang Qilong, Meng Yongqian, members and alternate members of the National Committee of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives, members of the Council, and Yu Shude, supervisor of the Supervisory Committee.
      Approximately 180 people attended the banquet.
    At the beginning of the banquet, Vice Premier Chen Yun, on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China, extended the warmest welcome and heartfelt thanks to the cooperative representatives from various countries. He said: We are deeply honored to have you travel from afar to participate in the First National Congress of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives, which is a great encouragement to the Chinese people, the members of Chinese cooperatives, and cooperative workers. He also said that he firmly believed that through this congress, the fraternal friendship between Chinese cooperatives and the cooperatives of the Soviet Union and various People's Democratic countries would be further strengthened; and that this fraternal friendship would have a significant promoting effect on the further development of China's cooperative cause. Finally, Vice Premier Chen Yun raised his glass to celebrate the growing development of the cooperative cause in the Soviet Union and various People's Democratic countries, to celebrate the growing strength of the peace, democracy, and socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and to celebrate the growing consolidation of peace in the Far East and the world.
    At the banquet, the head of the Soviet cooperative delegation and the heads of other national cooperative delegations spoke successively.
    The atmosphere was filled with warmth, friendship, and joy. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 陈云副总理欢宴各国合作社代表团

1954-07-28
第2版()
专栏：

    陈云副总理欢宴各国合作社代表团
    中央人民政府政务院陈云副总理在二十六日晚举行盛大宴会，热烈欢迎苏联和各人民民主国家合作社代表团。
    出席宴会的有：以吉莫费耶夫团长为首的苏联合作社代表团，以甘茹尔日夫团长为首的蒙古合作社代表团，以卢赫特团长为首的德意志民主共和国合作社代表团，以金重泰团长为首的朝鲜合作社代表团，以斯穆鲁卡团长为首的捷克斯洛伐克合作社代表团，以杜米特列斯库团长为首的罗马尼亚合作社代表团，以格奥尔吉耶夫团长为首的保加利亚合作社代表团和以裴宝云团长为首的越南代表团。
    以上各兄弟国家驻华使节和使馆人员也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的还有苏联专家谢维雅科夫和费多托夫等。
    出席作陪的有：中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会副主任曾山，外交部副部长伍修权，商业部副部长姚依林，林业部副部长李范五，外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航等。
    作陪的还有：中华全国供销合作总社全国委员会委员、候补委员，理事会理事程子华、张启龙、孟用潜及监事会监事于树德等。
      参加宴会的共约一百八十人。
    宴会开始时，陈云副总理代表中华人民共和国政府向各国合作社代表表示最热诚的欢迎和衷心的感谢。他说：中华全国合作社第一次代表大会承蒙你们远道前来参加，对于中国人民、中国合作社社员和合作社工作人员是一个极大的鼓舞。他又说，我深信通过这次代表大会，中国合作社与苏联和各人民民主国家合作社之间的兄弟的友谊将更加密切地巩固起来；而这种兄弟的友谊对于中国合作事业进一步的发展将发生重大的促进作用。最后，陈云副总理举杯庆祝苏联和各人民民主国家的合作事业的日益发展，庆祝以苏联为首的和平、民主、社会主义阵营日益强盛，远东和世界和平日益巩固。
    宴会上，苏联合作社代表团团长和其他各国合作社代表团团长都相继讲话。
    席间充满了热烈、友好和欢乐的气氛。（新华社）
