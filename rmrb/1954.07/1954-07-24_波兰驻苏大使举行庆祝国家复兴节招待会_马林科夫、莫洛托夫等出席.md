Title: Polish Ambassador to the Soviet Union Holds Reception Celebrating National Day of Rebirth; Malenkov, Molotov, and Others Attend
Author:
Date: 1954-07-24
Page: 4

    Polish Ambassador to the Soviet Union Holds Reception Celebrating National Day of Rebirth
    Malenkov, Molotov, and Others Attend

    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 23rd] According to TASS, Moscow: Polish Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Mr. Lewikowski, held a reception in Moscow on the 22nd to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the Polish National Day of Rebirth.
    Those attending the reception included Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Molotov, Saburov, Khrushchev, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation Tarasov, Soviet Ministers G.F. Alexandrov, Antropov, Yelyutin, Zotov, P.A. Yudin, Raizer and Tikhomirov, Marshals of the Soviet Union Zhukov, Admiral of the Fleet N.G. Kuznetsov, General of the Army Kurasov, Chairman of the Moscow City Soviet Yasnov, leading members and representatives of various social organizations, science and culture workers, production innovators from Moscow enterprises, and Soviet and foreign journalists.
    Diplomatic envoys from various countries to the Soviet Union also attended the reception.
    Ambassador Lewikowski and Chairman Malenkov spoke at the reception successively. The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 波兰驻苏大使举行庆祝国家复兴节招待会  马林科夫、莫洛托夫等出席

1954-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰驻苏大使举行庆祝国家复兴节招待会
    马林科夫、莫洛托夫等出席
    【新华社二十三日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：波兰驻苏联大使列维科夫斯基二十二日在莫斯科举行招待会，庆祝波兰国家复兴节十周年。
    出席招待会的有卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、莫洛托夫、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫，俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃主席团主席塔拉索夫，苏联各部部长格·费·亚历山德罗夫、安特罗波夫、叶留金、佐托夫、帕·亚·尤金、拉伊泽和季霍米罗夫，苏联元帅朱可夫、海军元帅纳·格·库兹涅佐夫、陆军大将库拉索夫，莫斯科市苏维埃主席雅斯诺夫，各社会团体的负责人和代表、科学和文化工作者、莫斯科各企业的生产革新者，以及苏联和其他国家的新闻记者。
    各国驻苏联的外交使节也出席了招待会。
    列维科夫斯基大使和马林科夫主席在会上相继讲话。招待会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
