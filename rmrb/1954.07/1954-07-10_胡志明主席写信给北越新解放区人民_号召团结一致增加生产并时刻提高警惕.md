Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Writes to the People of Newly Liberated Areas in North Vietnam, Calling for Unity, Increased Production, and Constant Vigilance
Author:
Date: 1954-07-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 9th: According to the Vietnam News Agency on July 7th, Chairman Ho Chi Minh wrote a letter on July 2nd to the people, soldiers, and cadres of the newly liberated areas of the North Vietnamese plains, expressing his cordial greetings and calling on everyone to unite.

The letter said: It has been eight years since the beginning of the war of resistance. During these eight years, although you have suffered much under the enemy's shackles, your patriotism and fighting will have continued to grow. Today, you have been liberated and have returned to the embrace of our beloved motherland.

In his letter, Chairman Ho Chi Minh called on everyone to unite, love each other, help each other, actively increase production to improve their lives, and constantly be vigilant. In the letter, he pointed out to the Catholic compatriots in the newly liberated areas that they should trust the government's policy of freedom of belief and not listen to or be exploited by enemy rumors.

In his letter, Chairman Ho Chi Minh instructed cadres and soldiers to be kind to the people, approach and help the people, implement the government's lines and policies, and maintain a clean and honest style.

The letter also stated: Former French colonial officials, officers, and soldiers who abandon evil and return to good will be treated leniently by the government and people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

### 胡志明主席写信给北越新解放区人民  号召团结一致增加生产并时刻提高警惕

1954-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席写信给北越新解放区人民
    号召团结一致增加生产并时刻提高警惕
    【新华社九日讯】据越南通讯社七日讯：胡志明主席在七月二日写给北越平原新解放区人民、战士和干部一封信，对他们表示亲切的慰问，并号召大家团结一致。
    信上说：从开始抗战到现在已经八年了。在这八年当中，你们在敌人的枷锁下，虽然受到了许多痛苦，但是你们的爱国主义精神和斗争意志却不断地高涨。今天，你们已经获得解放，回到可爱的祖国的怀抱里来了。
    胡志明主席在信中号召大家团结一致，互相爱护，互相帮助，积极增加生产以改善生活，时刻提高警惕。信中对新解放区的天主教同胞们指出要信任政府关于信仰自由的政策，不要听信敌人的谣言和受敌人的利用。
    胡志明主席在信中指示干部和战士应该对人民和蔼，接近和帮助人民，要执行政府的路线和政策，并保持廉洁的作风。
    信中又说：法国殖民者的旧职员、军官和士兵，如果弃邪归正，都会得到越南民主共和国政府和人民的宽大。
