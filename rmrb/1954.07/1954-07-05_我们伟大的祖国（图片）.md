Author:
Date: 1954-07-05
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    With the enthusiastic support of Tibetan people from both the north and south sides of the Gangdise Mountains and both banks of the Yarlung Tsangpo River, the construction project of the Lhasa-Tatzhao section of the Kangzang Highway fully commenced in mid-March of this year. This is a model female migrant worker who proposed a rationalization suggestion for alternating operations to open up steep slopes, which increased work efficiency by nearly two times. She is accepting prizes and merit certificates at the celebration meeting.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    康藏公路拉萨至太昭段修筑工程，在来自冈底斯山南北、雅鲁藏布江两岸的藏民的热烈支援下，已于今年三月中旬全面开工。这是提出轮流操作开辟陡坡合理化建议，使工作效率提高近两倍的模范女民工，她在庆功大会上接受奖品和立功证。
