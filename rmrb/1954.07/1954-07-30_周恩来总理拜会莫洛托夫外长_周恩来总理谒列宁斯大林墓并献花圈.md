Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Calls on Foreign Minister Molotov. Premier Zhou Enlai Visits the Lenin-Stalin Mausoleum and Lays a Wreath
Author:
Date: 1954-07-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 29th: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, called on Vyacheslav Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, at 5:30 p.m. on the 28th. He was accompanied by Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs and Ambassador to the Soviet Union, and Wang Jiaxiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs. The meeting lasted approximately one hour.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 29th: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, visited the Lenin-Stalin Mausoleum at 10:00 a.m. on the 29th and laid a wreath in tribute. Accompanying him were Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs and Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Wang Jiaxiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Wang Bingnan, Secretary-General of the Chinese Delegation to the Geneva Conference, advisors Shi Zhe, Qiao Guanhua, Chen Jiakang, Huang Hua, as well as Kapustin, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Mukhin, First Secretary of the Protocol Department.

### 周恩来总理拜会莫洛托夫外长  周恩来总理谒列宁斯大林墓并献花圈

1954-07-30
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理拜会莫洛托夫外长
    周恩来总理谒列宁斯大林墓并献花圈
    【新华社莫斯科二十九日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，在二十八日下午五时半拜会了苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部部长莫洛托夫。陪同前往的有我外交部副部长兼驻苏联大使张闻天和我外交部副部长王稼祥。会谈约经过一小时。
    【新华社莫斯科二十九日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，在二十九日上午十时前往拜谒列宁斯大林陵墓，并献花圈致敬。陪同拜谒的有我国外交部副部长兼驻苏联大使张闻天，外交部副部长王稼祥，我国出席日内瓦会议代表团秘书长王炳南，顾问师哲、乔冠华、陈家康、黄华，以及苏联外交部交际司副司长卡普斯金和交际司一等参事穆欣等人。
