Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-04-27
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Workers across our country are enthusiastically engaging in production competitions to welcome the great "May Day" International Labor Day. Workers at the trolleybus factory in Shenyang, Northeast China, are rushing to build a trolleybus. They want to present this vehicle as a gift to celebrate "May Day" International Labor Day.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国各地工人正在以热烈的生产竞赛来迎接伟大的“五一”国际劳动节。东北沈阳电车厂的工人们正在赶制一辆无轨电车。他们要把这辆车作为对“五一”国际劳动节的献礼。
