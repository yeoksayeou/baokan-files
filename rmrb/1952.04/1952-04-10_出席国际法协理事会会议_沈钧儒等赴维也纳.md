Title: Shen Junru and Others Go to Vienna to Attend the International Association of Democratic Lawyers Council Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-04-10
Page: 4

    Shen Junru, Vice President of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, and Councilor Ke Bonian, along with their party, left Beijing by plane at 6:00 AM on the 9th to attend the International Association of Democratic Lawyers Council Meeting in Vienna. Xie Juezai, Shi Liang, Zhang Zhizhiang, Qian Duansheng, Wu Gaizhi, Zhang Xiruo, Tao Xijin, Luo Shuzhang, Yan Baohang, Min Ganghou, Liao Mengxing, Wu Maosun, Yuan Baohua and many other legal workers and representatives from various people's organizations in the capital saw them off at the airport.
    The International Association of Democratic Lawyers Council Meeting is scheduled to be held from April 16th to 18th. The meeting will hear the report of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers Investigation Team on the bacteriological warfare conducted by the American imperialists in Korea and Northeast China. It will draw conclusions based on the report and pass resolutions to publish the report and take action based on the Investigation Team's conclusions.

### 出席国际法协理事会会议  沈钧儒等赴维也纳

1952-04-10
第4版()
专栏：

    出席国际法协理事会会议
    沈钧儒等赴维也纳
    【新华社九日讯】国际民主法律工作者协会副主席沈钧儒、理事柯柏年等一行，在九日上午六时搭飞机离开北京，前往维也纳出席国际民主法律工作者协会理事会会议。到机场送行的有首都法律工作者和各人民团体代表谢觉哉、史良、张志让、钱端升、吴溉之、张奚若、陶希晋、罗叔章、阎宝航、闵刚侯、廖梦醒、吴茂荪、袁葆华等多人。
    国际民主法律工作者协会理事会会议定于四月十六日到十八日举行。会议将听取国际民主法律工作者协会调查团关于美帝国主义者在朝鲜和中国东北进行细菌战的报告，并将根据报告作出结论，和通过发表这个报告以及根据调查团的结论采取行动的决议。
