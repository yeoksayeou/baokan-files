Title: Wen Youzhang Arrives in Shenyang to Investigate American Crimes of Germ Warfare
Author:
Date: 1952-04-11
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 10th -- Wen Youzhang, Chairman of the Canadian Peace Congress, arrived in Shenyang from Beijing on the 9th to investigate the crimes of bacteriological warfare committed by American imperialism in Northeast China. Liu Zhiming, Vice-Chairman of the Northeast Anti-American Aid Korea General Branch, Wang Bin, Minister of Health of the Northeast People's Government, and Jiao Ruoyu, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, were among those who went to the station to welcome him. After staying in Shenyang for two days, Wen Youzhang will personally visit the sites where American aircraft dropped bacteria-laden insects and interview local residents.

### 文幼章抵达沈阳  调查美国细菌战罪行

1952-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

    文幼章抵达沈阳
    调查美国细菌战罪行
   【新华社沈阳十日电】加拿大和平大会主席文幼章九日由北京抵达沈阳，调查美帝国主义在东北进行细菌战的罪行。前往车站欢迎的有：东北抗美援朝总分会副主席刘芝明、东北人民政府卫生部部长王斌、沈阳市副市长焦若愚等。文幼章在沈阳逗留两日后，将亲赴发现美机投掷细菌毒虫的现场访问当地居民。
