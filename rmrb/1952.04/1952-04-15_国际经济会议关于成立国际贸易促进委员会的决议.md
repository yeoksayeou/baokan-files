Title: Resolution of the International Economic Conference on the Establishment of the International Trade Promotion Committee
Author:
Date: 1952-04-15
Page: 1
(Xinhua News Agency according to TASS Moscow dispatch)
    The International Economic Conference has decided to continue its initiated work, namely, to strive to promote trade between countries on the basis of equality and mutual benefit for the economic development of all countries and the improvement of the welfare of their people. To this end, the Conference specifically decided to establish the International Trade Promotion Committee.

    The task of this committee is to assist in disseminating information about the International Economic Conference and to assist in expanding trade between countries on the basis of equality and appropriately considering the special needs of industrialization in backward countries.

    The International Economic Conference entrusted the committee to submit the letter to the United Nations General Assembly, which was adopted by the conference on April 12, 1952, to the United Nations General Assembly.

    At the same time, the committee will be responsible for determining the time and place for the Second International Economic Conference. The expenses of the committee will be voluntarily donated by individuals, groups, and national committees concerned with promoting international trade.

    The International Trade Promotion Committee elected by the International Economic Conference is composed of the following personnel:

    20. Peili (United Kingdom) – Manager of a outerwear and textiles domestic sales and export company.

### 国际经济会议关于成立国际贸易促进委员会的决议

1952-04-15
第1版()
专栏：

    国际经济会议关于成立国际贸易促进委员会的决议
    国际经济会议决定继续它所发起的工作，即为各国经济的发展及其人民福利的增进，在平等与互利的基础上，努力促进各国之间的贸易。为此，会议特决定成立国际贸易促进委员会。
    这个委员会的任务是协助传播关于国际经济会议的消息，和协助在平等与适当照顾落后国家工业化的特别需要的基础上扩大各国之间的贸易。
    国际经济会议责成委员会将会议在一九五二年四月十二日通过的致联合国大会书送交联合国大会。
    委员会同时将负责决定举行第二届国际经济会议的时间和地点。委员会的费用将由关心促进国际贸易的个人、团体和各国委员会自动捐助。
    国际经济会议所选出的国际贸易促进委员会由下列人员组成：
