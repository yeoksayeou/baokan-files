Title: Indian, Australian, and Ceylonese Trade Union Delegations Invited to Attend May Day Celebrations to Arrive in Beijing Soon; Korean Trade Union Delegation Has Departed from Pyongyang for Our Country
Author:
Date: 1952-04-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, April 26th: The first group of Indian trade union delegations and the Australian trade union delegation, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the May Day celebrations in our country, arrived in Guangzhou by train on the 25th. The chief representative of the first group of the Indian trade union delegation is Mr. Shah, Chairman of the All-India Trade Union Congress. The chief representative of the Australian trade union delegation is Mr. Wright, Chairman of the Australian Sheet Metal Workers' Union. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Lin Qiangyun, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangdong Provincial Federation of Trade Unions; Huang Minwei, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangzhou Municipal Federation of Trade Unions; Tan Qike, Vice Chairman of the South China Regional Committee of the Chinese Seamen's Union; Chen Nengxing, Secretary-General of the Guangdong Provincial Federation of Trade Unions; Liao Limin, Secretary-General of the Guangzhou Municipal Federation of Trade Unions; and Wu Jiemin, Director of the Department of Culture and Education. On the evening of the 25th, the two trade union delegations traveled to Beijing by train, accompanied by Xuan Rong, Vice Chairman of the South China Regional Committee of the Chinese Seamen's Union.

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, April 26th: Saranankara (First Chairman of the Ceylon Federation of Trade Unions and editor of the "Progressive Monthly" of New Sinhala) of the Ceylon trade union delegation, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the May Day celebrations in our country, arrived in Wuhan by train from Guangzhou on the 26th and transferred to a train to Beijing that evening. Lu Liang, Director of the Organization Department of the Wuhan Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, and Zhou Jianyi, Director of the Production Department, and others went to the station to see him off.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, April 26th: The Korean trade union delegation, led by团长 Park Hyo-bin, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the May Day celebrations in our country, departed from Pyongyang for Beijing on the 25th.

### 应邀前来我国参加“五一”节观礼  印度澳大利亚锡兰工会代表团即将来京  朝鲜工会代表团已自平壤启程来我国

1952-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀前来我国参加“五一”节观礼
    印度澳大利亚锡兰工会代表团即将来京
    朝鲜工会代表团已自平壤启程来我国
    【新华社广州二十六日电】应中华全国总工会的邀请前来我国参加“五一”节观礼的首批印度工会代表团和澳大利亚工会代表团已在二十五日乘车抵达广州。首批印度工会代表团首席代表是印度统一工会大会主席沙氏，澳大利亚工会代表团首席代表是澳大利亚钢板工会主席莱特。前往车站欢迎的有：广东省总工会筹备会主任林锵云、广州市总工会筹备会主任黄民伟、中国海员工会华南区委员会副主席谭其科、广东省总工会秘书长陈能兴、广州市总工会秘书长廖立民和文教部长吴介民等。二十五日晚上，两工会代表团乘车赴北京，陪行者有中国海员工会华南区委员会副主席禤荣。
    【新华社武汉二十六日电】应中华全国总工会的邀请前来参加我国“五一”节观礼的锡兰工会代表撒拉南卡拉（锡兰工会联合会第一届主席、新辛震里斯“进步月刊”编辑），已在二十六日自广州乘车到达武汉，当天晚上转车前往北京。赴车站欢送的有武汉市总工会组织部长吕梁、生产部长周建宜等。
    【新华社平壤二十六日电】应中华全国总工会的邀请前往我国参加“五一”节观礼的朝鲜工会代表团，由团长朴孝斌率领，已在二十五日由平壤启程赴北京。
