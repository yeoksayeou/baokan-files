Title: Trade Union Delegations from Romania, Czechoslovakia and Other Countries Continue Their Visit in Beijing; Vietnamese Trade Union Delegation Has Passed Wuhan and is En Route to Beijing by Train
Author:
Date: 1952-04-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 25th: Trade union delegations from Romania, Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, Sweden, and Denmark, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the May Day celebrations in our country, have been visiting Beijing in recent days. From April 22nd to 25th, the delegates visited Peking University, Tsinghua University, the Affiliated Girls' Middle School of Beijing Normal University, Beijing Municipal No. 8 Middle School, and the Beihai Beijing Experimental Nursery School, and were warmly welcomed by teachers, students, and children. At Peking University, they visited the place where Chairman Mao used to work at the university and the Li Dazhao Memorial Room, and toured the school history exhibition hall and Democracy Square. The delegates also visited student internships in the Department of Electrical Engineering and the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Tsinghua University, and met with students from working-class backgrounds at Municipal No. 8 Middle School. They were very excited to see the happy lives of children in our country at the nursery school of the Affiliated Girls' Middle School of Beijing Normal University and the Beihai Beijing Experimental Nursery School. Delegates from various countries also visited famous scenic spots such as Beihai Park, Western Suburbs Park, and the Temple of Heaven, praising the ancient Chinese architecture and the great creativity of the Chinese working people. In addition, the delegates also enjoyed films such as "Steel Fighter," "The White Haired Girl," and "The Great Unity of the Chinese Nation."
    Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, April 25th: The Vietnamese trade union delegation of five, led by the head of the delegation, Yang Bai Lian, who were invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to come to our country to participate in the May Day celebrations, arrived in Wuhan by train from Nanning on the morning of the 25th. Greeting them at Wuchang Railway Station were Zhao Min, Vice Chairman of the Central South Federation of Trade Unions and Chairman of the Wuhan Federation of Trade Unions, Zhuang Yuming, Deputy Secretary-General of the Central South Federation of Trade Unions, Cao Zhizhen, Minister of Production and Wages, and others. The delegation left Hankou by train for Beijing on the evening of the 25th.

### 罗捷等国工会代表团连日在京参观访问  越南工会代表团已过武汉乘车前来北京

1952-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    罗捷等国工会代表团连日在京参观访问
    越南工会代表团已过武汉乘车前来北京
    【新华社二十五日讯】应中华全国总工会的邀请前来我国参加五一节观礼的罗马尼亚、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、瑞典和丹麦工会代表团，连日在北京参观访问。代表们在二十二日到二十五日，分别参观了北京大学、清华大学、北京师范大学附属女子中学校、北京市立第八中学和北海北京实验托儿所，受到了教师、学生和儿童们的热烈欢迎。他们在北京大学访问了毛主席过去在校工作处和李大钊先生纪念室，参观了校史陈列室和民主广场。代表们并在清华大学参观电机工程系和机械工程系的学生实习，并在市立第八中学和劳动人民出身的学生晤谈。他们在师大女附中托儿所和北海北京实验托儿所，看到我国儿童的幸福快乐生活，感到非常兴奋。各国代表也游览了北海公园、西郊公园、天坛等名胜，对于中国古代建筑物和中国劳动人民的伟大创造力称赞不已。此外，代表们还欣赏了“钢铁战士”、“白毛女”和“中华民族大团结”等影片。
    【新华社汉口二十五日电】应中华全国总工会的邀请前来我国参加五一节观礼的越南工会代表团一行五人，在团长杨白莲率领下，在二十五日早晨由南宁乘车到达武汉。到武昌车站欢迎的有：中南总工会副主席、武汉市总工会主席赵敏、中南总工会副秘书长庄玉铭、生产工资部长曹志贞等多人。代表团已在二十五日晚由汉口乘车前往北京。
