Title: Pakistan-China Friendship Association Holds Grand Tea Party to See Off Chinese Delegation Participating in Industrial Exhibition

Author:

Date: 1952-04-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi: The Pakistan-China Friendship Association held a grand tea party in Karachi, the capital of Pakistan, on the 20th to see off the Chinese delegation participating in the Pakistan International Industrial Exhibition. More than 250 representatives from Pakistani cultural, industrial, commercial, and journalistic circles attended the meeting. Chinese Ambassador Han Nianlong and representatives from the diplomatic missions of Poland, Czechoslovakia, and Hungary were also invited.

    The Vice-Chairman of the Pakistan-China Friendship Association, Mr. Shakur, said in his speech: "Today, New China is the most powerful bastion against imperialism and colonialism in Asia. The powerful progressive forces of New China have changed the political landscape of vast areas of Asia. The friendship between Pakistan and China is very necessary, not only in terms of the political situation in Asia but also in terms of the world political situation." After listing the various achievements made by China under the leadership of Chairman Mao after liberation, he said: The China Pavilion has been an eye-opener for those visiting this International Industrial Exhibition. The Chinese delegation is a friendly envoy from great China.

    Chen Ming, the head of the Chinese delegation, said in his reply: The Chinese people firmly believe that the friendly cooperation between the Chinese and Pakistani people will not only contribute to the happiness of the two peoples but also to the maintenance of peace in Asia and the world.

    The tea party ended with slogans of "Long live the friendship between the Pakistani and Chinese people!"

### 巴中友协举行盛大茶会  欢送我国参加工展代表团

1952-04-28
第4版()
专栏：

    巴中友协举行盛大茶会
    欢送我国参加工展代表团
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：喀喇蚩消息：巴中友好协会于二十日在巴基斯坦首都喀喇蚩举行盛大茶会，欢送我国参加巴基斯坦国际工业展览会代表团。参加大会的有巴基斯坦文化界、工商界、新闻界代表二百五十多人，我国韩念龙大使和波兰、捷克斯洛伐克和匈牙利的外交使团代表也应邀参加。
    巴中友好协会副主席霞库尔致词说：“今天新中国是亚洲反帝国主义、反殖民主义最强大的堡垒，新中国强大的进步力量已经改变了亚洲广大地区的政治面貌。巴、中两国的友好，不仅就亚洲政治形势而且就世界政治形势而言，都是很必要的。”他在列举了中国解放后在毛主席领导下所得到的种种成就后说：中国馆使参观这次国际工业展览会的人们大开眼界。中国代表团是来自伟大中国的亲善使节。
    我国代表团团长陈明致答词说：中国人民深信中、巴两国人民的友好合作不仅有助于两国人民的幸福，而且有助于维护亚洲和世界的和平事业。
    茶会在“巴中人民友好万岁”的口号声中结束。
