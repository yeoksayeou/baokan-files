Title: Hungarian Performing Arts Troupe Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-04-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 29th:

    The Hungarian National People's Performing Arts Troupe, after touring and performing in Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and other locations, returned to Beijing by train on the morning of the 29th, accompanied by Zhang Guangnian, Deputy Director of the Arts Administration Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government. The Hungarian Ambassador to China, Sándor Sziklai, and his wife, who accompanied the troupe on their visits to various locations, also arrived in Beijing on the same train. Those who welcomed them at the station included Sha Ke夫, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government and Secretary-General of the National Committee of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Government Administration Council's Committee of Cultural and Educational Affairs, Li Bozhao, Vice President of the Central Academy of Drama, as well as many literary and art workers from the capital. Counselor Szibka of the Hungarian Embassy in China and embassy staff also welcomed them at the station.
    The Hungarian National People's Performing Arts Troupe's performances were warmly welcomed by the Chinese people in various locations. They performed a total of nine shows in Nanjing, Shanghai, and Hangzhou, with an audience of over 65,000 people. The troupe will put on a larger-scale performance in the capital in early May.

### 匈牙利文工团返京

1952-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    匈牙利文工团返京
    【新华社二十九日讯】匈牙利国家人民文工团在南京、上海、杭州等地作巡回演出后，已于二十九日上午由中央人民政府文化部艺术事业管理局副局长张光年陪同，乘火车返抵北京。陪同文工团到各地参观的匈牙利驻华大使夏法朗柯和夫人也同车抵京。到车站欢迎的有：中央人民政府文化部办公厅主任、中华全国文学艺术界联合会全国委员会秘书长沙可夫、政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深、中央戏剧学院副院长李伯钊，以及首都文艺工作者多人。匈牙利驻华大使馆参赞席布卡和使馆人员也到车站欢迎。
    匈牙利国家人民文工团在各地的表演受到了中国人民的热烈欢迎。总计在南京、上海、杭州演出了九场，观众达六万五千多人。文工团将于五月初在首都作较大规模的演出。
