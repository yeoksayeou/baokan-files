    Our Great Motherland
    The victory of the anti-corruption struggle has brought a new look to the production sector. In the anti-corruption movement, workers at the Shanghai State-owned Asia Steel Plant improved the furnace lining method and created a new record of continuous smelting of twenty-four furnaces. This is the scene of steel being tapped from the steelmaking furnace.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    反贪污斗争的胜利，使得生产部门出现了一片新气象。上海国营亚细亚钢铁厂的工人，在反贪污运动中，改进了搪炉法，创造了连续冶炼二十四炉的新纪录。这是炼钢炉正在出钢的情形。
