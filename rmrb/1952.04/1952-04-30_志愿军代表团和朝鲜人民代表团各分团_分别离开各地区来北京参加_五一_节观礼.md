Title: The sub-delegations of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Delegation and the Korean People's Delegation Depart from Various Regions to Beijing to Attend the "May 1st" Celebration

Author:
Date: 1952-04-30
Page: 1

(Xinhua News Agency, April 29th) The East China, Central South, Southwest, and Northwest sub-delegations of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation and the Korean People's Visit China Delegation have departed from their respective regions to Beijing to attend the "May 1st" celebration. The North China sub-delegations of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation and the Korean People's Visit China Delegation have arrived in Beijing.

The delegates visited wounded and sick Volunteer soldiers and families of Volunteer soldiers in various locations, delivered numerous reports, and visited vast urban and rural areas, as well as areas inhabited by ethnic minorities. The East China sub-delegations of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation and the Korean People's Visit China Delegation visited 120 counties and cities, 52 villages and towns, the Huai River construction site, and the Huainan coal mine in East China. Their reports and broadcasts, as well as the anti-American and aid-Korea physical object and picture exhibition they organized, educated approximately five million people in East China on the subject of resisting America and aiding Korea. The Central South sub-delegations of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation and the Korean People's Visit China Delegation visited 265 counties, cities, and small towns, as well as 83 townships in six provinces and two administrative regions in the Central South region. They delivered a total of 2,835 reports and broadcasts in the Central South region, reaching an audience of approximately 12 million people. The Northwest sub-delegations of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation and the Korean People's Visit China Delegation visited 106 cities, 19 villages, and ethnic minority areas such as Zhuoni and Xiahe in Gansu Province, Ta'er Temple in Qinghai Province, and Yili and Kashgar in Xinjiang Province; and also visited Chairman Mao's former residence in Yan'an. They delivered a total of 275 reports to people of all ethnic groups in the Northwest, meeting with 3.5 million people. The North China sub-delegations of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation and the Korean People's Visit China Delegation visited 14 municipalities directly under the provincial government, 261 counties, 4 leagues, 13 banners, 35 villages and towns, and 3 directly administered districts (belonging to Suiyuan Province, equivalent to banners) in the five provinces of North China. They delivered numerous reports, reaching an audience of 4,143,160 people.

When the sub-delegations left their respective regions, the local people held grand farewell gatherings.

### 志愿军代表团和朝鲜人民代表团各分团  分别离开各地区来北京参加“五一”节观礼

1952-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军代表团和朝鲜人民代表团各分团
    分别离开各地区来北京参加“五一”节观礼
    【新华社二十九日讯】中国人民志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团的华东、中南、西南、西北各分团已离开各地区，来北京参加“五一”节观礼。中国人民志愿军归国代表团华北分团和朝鲜人民访华代表团华北分团已到达北京。
    代表们在各地慰问了志愿军伤病员和志愿军家属，作了许多次报告，访问了广大城市、乡村和少数民族地区。中国人民志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团的华东分团在华东访问了一百二十个县、市和五十二个村镇，以及淮河工地、淮南煤矿。他们的报告和广播，以及他们举办的抗美援朝实物图片展览会，使华东约有五百万人受到了抗美援朝的教育。中国人民志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团的中南分团在中南区曾访问了六个省和两个行署区的二百六十五个县，市和小市镇以及八十三个乡。他们在中南区共作了二千八百三十五次报告和广播，听众约达一千二百多万人。中国人民志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团的西北分团曾访问了一百零六个城市、十九个村庄，和甘肃省卓尼、夏河、青海省塔尔寺、新疆省伊犁、喀什等少数民族地区；并到延安参观了毛主席的故居。他们向西北各族人民共作了二百七十五次报告，和三百五十万群众见了面。中国人民志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团的华北分团访问了华北五省的十四个省直属市、二百六十一个县、四个盟、十三个旗、三十五个村镇和三个直属区（属绥远省，相当于旗）。他们作了许多次报告，听众达四百一十四万三千一百六十人。
    各分团离开各地区时，当地人民都举行了盛大的欢送会。
