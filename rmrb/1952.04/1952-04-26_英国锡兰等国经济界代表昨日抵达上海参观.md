Title: Economic Representatives from Britain, Ceylon, and Other Countries Arrived in Shanghai Yesterday for a Visit
Author:
Date: 1952-04-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, April 25th: Eleven representatives from Britain, Ceylon, Mexico, Australia, and Brazil, who attended the International Economic Conference and are now visiting China, arrived in Shanghai from Beijing on the morning of the 25th for a visit. Welcoming them at the station were Wu Xuezhi and Rong Yiren, Vice Chairmen of the Shanghai Federation of Industry and Commerce, Secretary-General Hu Ziying, Guo Dihuo, Chairman of the Shanghai Cotton Textile Industry Association, Jin Zhonghua, chief writer of the Xinwen Daily, and Chen Yisheng and Hu Juewen, representatives of the Shanghai local organization of the China Democratic National Construction Association.

### 英国锡兰等国经济界代表昨日抵达上海参观

1952-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    英国锡兰等国经济界代表昨日抵达上海参观
    【新华社上海二十五日电】参加国际经济会议后前来我国访问的英国、锡兰、墨西哥、澳大利亚和巴西的代表十一人，二十五日上午，从北京来到上海参观。到车站欢迎的有：上海市工商业联合会副主任委员吴雪之、荣毅仁，秘书长胡子婴，上海棉纺工业同业公会主任委员郭棣活，新闻日报总主笔金仲华，民主建国会上海地方组织代表陈巳生、胡厥文等。
