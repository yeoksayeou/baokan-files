    Our Great Motherland
    In order to cultivate agricultural technical personnel for our country, Northeast China has successively established many new types of agricultural schools above the specialized level, implementing a teaching method that combines theory and practice. This is a scene of students at the Northeast Agricultural College learning Michurin's breeding methods and identifying the overwintering performance of winter wheat.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-04-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为了培养我国农业技术人材，东北已先后成立了许多新型的专科以上的农业学校，实行理论与实际相结合的教学方法。这是东北农学院的学生们正在学习米丘林的育种方法，鉴别冬小麦越冬性能的情形。
