Title: Preparatory Office of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Preparatory Committee and the All-China Federation of Democratic National Construction Invite Economic Representatives of Various Countries to China for a Symposium

Author:
Date: 1952-04-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd: The Preparatory Office of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Preparatory Committee, together with the All-China Federation of Democratic National Construction, held a symposium on the afternoon of the 23rd to meet with representatives from various countries attending the International Economic Conference who came to China for a visit. Those present included: Chen Shutong, Director of the Preparatory Office of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Preparatory Committee, members Ye Jizhuang, Zhang Naiqi, Xue Muqiao, Xiang Shuxiang, Huang Yanpei, convener of the All-China Federation of Democratic National Construction, standing committee members Wang Xinyuan, Sun Xiaocun, Yang Weiyu, Sun Qimeng, Ji Chaoding, member of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and Sheng Pihua and Wu Yunfu, representatives of China attending the International Economic Conference, Fu Huating, Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce, and many others.

The symposium was opened and presided over by Chen Shutong, followed by Huang Yanpei, who talked about the economic development of the People's Republic of China in the past two years. At the meeting, Ye Jizhuang, Xue Muqiao, and Sun Qimeng answered questions raised by representatives from various countries regarding China's economy and foreign trade. In the evening, Chen Shutong, Director of the Preparatory Office of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Preparatory Committee, Huang Yanpei, convener of the All-China Federation of Democratic National Construction, Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, jointly hosted a banquet for all the representatives, and people from Beijing's industrial and commercial circles were invited to attend.

### 全国工商联筹委会筹备处和民建总会邀来我国的各国经济界代表座谈

1952-04-24
第1版()
专栏：

    全国工商联筹委会筹备处和民建总会邀来我国的各国经济界代表座谈
    【新华社二十三日讯】中华全国工商业联合会筹备委员会筹备处会同民主建国会总会，于二十三日下午举行座谈会，与前来我国参观的各国出席国际经济会议的代表们晤面。到会的有：中华全国工商业联合会筹备委员会筹备处主任陈叔通、委员叶季壮、章乃器、薛暮桥、项叔翔，民主建国会总会召集人黄炎培、常务委员王新元、孙晓村、杨卫玉、孙起孟，国际贸易促进委员会委员冀朝鼎，我国出席国际经济会议代表盛丕华、吴蕴初，北京市工商业联合会主任委员傅华亭等多人。
    座谈会由陈叔通主持开幕，接着由黄炎培谈述了中华人民共和国两年来经济发展情况。会上并由叶季壮、薛暮桥、孙起孟答复了各国代表们所提出的有关我国经济和对外贸易问题。晚间，中华全国工商业联合会筹备委员会筹备处主任陈叔通、民主建国会总会召集人黄炎培、中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一和中国科学院院长郭沫若联合设宴招待全体代表，北京市工商界人士应邀作陪。
