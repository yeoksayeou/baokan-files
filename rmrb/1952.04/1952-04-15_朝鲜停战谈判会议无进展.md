Title: Korean Armistice Talks Show No Progress
Author:
Date: 1952-04-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, April 14th: The Third Agenda Subcommittee of the Korean Armistice Talks met for only half a minute today. The other side indicated they had no new opinions, and the meeting made no progress. Regarding our suggestion for the swift resumption of the Fourth Agenda staff meeting, the other side claimed they were still not fully prepared.

### 朝鲜停战谈判会议无进展

1952-04-15
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判会议无进展
    【新华社开城十四日电】十四日朝鲜停战谈判第三项议程小组委员会开会仅半分钟，对方表示无新意见，会议无进展。对于我方关于第四项议程参谋会议迅速复会的建议，对方声称仍未准备完绪。
