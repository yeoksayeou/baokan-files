Title: Faggi, Banfi, and Others Arrive in Beijing to Attend Commemorative Meeting for Four World Cultural Celebrities
Author:
Date: 1952-04-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th: At the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, French and Italian prominent figures, Faggi, Banfi, and others arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 28th to attend the "Commemorative Meeting for Four World Cultural Celebrities (Hugo, Gogol, Vinci, and Avicenna)." Those who welcomed them at the airport included: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression; Vice-Chairman Chen Shutong; Secretary-General Liu Ningyi; Liu Guanyi; Mao Dun, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles; Secretary-General Sha Kefu; Deputy Secretary-General Shu Qun; Ding Ling, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Association of Literary Workers; Li Siguang, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies; Zhou Peiyuan, Chairman of the Chinese Physical Society; and Wu Han, Vice-Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government.

The list of foreign guests is as follows:
    France Faggi, Chairman of the French Peace Council, Member of the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council
    Mrs. Faggi
    Roa, Writer, Poet, Journalist
    Mrs. Roa
    Italy Banfi, Senator, Professor of Philosophy at the University of Milan
    Panzini, Professor of Physics at the University of Rome

### 应邀来我国参加世界四大文化名人纪念会  法奇、班菲等一行昨天到达北京

1952-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国参加世界四大文化名人纪念会
    法奇、班菲等一行昨天到达北京
    【新华社二十八日讯】应中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会邀请，前来我国参加“世界四大文化名人（雨果、果戈理、芬奇、阿维森纳）纪念大会”的法国、意大利著名人士法奇、班菲等一行，于二十八日下午乘飞机抵达北京。到机场欢迎的有：中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会主席郭沫若，副主席陈叔通，秘书长刘宁一，刘贯一；中华全国文学艺术界联合会全国委员会副主席茅盾，秘书长沙可夫，副秘书长舒群；中华全国文学工作者协会副主席丁玲；中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会主席李四光，中国物理学会理事长周培源和北京市人民政府副市长吴晗。
    外宾名单如下：
    法国法奇法国和平理事会主席、世界和平理事会执行局委员
    法奇夫人
    罗阿作家、诗人、新闻记者
    罗阿夫人
    意大利班菲上议员、米兰大学哲学教授
    潘齐尼罗马大学物理学教授
