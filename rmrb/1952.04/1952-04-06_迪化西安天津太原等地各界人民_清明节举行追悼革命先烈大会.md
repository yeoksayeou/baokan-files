Title: People from All Walks of Life in Dihua, Xi'an, Tianjin, Taiyuan and Other Places Held Memorial Meetings for Revolutionary Martyrs on Qingming Festival
Author: 
Date: 1952-04-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 5th: On Qingming Festival, the 5th, people from all walks of life in Dihua, Xi'an, Tianjin, Taiyuan, Zhangjiakou, Qingdao and other places solemnly held memorial meetings for revolutionary martyrs and swept the tombs of the martyrs.

In Dihua City, party, government, and military leaders Wang Zhen, Bao Erhan, and representatives from various ethnic groups, including Ma Liangjun, the chief instructor of the Hui people, and He Jia Juyazi, the chief instructor of the Uyghur people, nearly a thousand people in total, lined up on the 5th to pay their respects and mourn at the tombs of Chen Tanqiu, one of the founders of the CCP and a member of the CCP Central Committee, Mao Zemin, Qiao Guozhen, Lin Jilu and other martyrs who were outstanding members of the CCP, and patriotic democratic personage Du Chongyuan. The entire delegation of the Northwest Branch of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation and the Northwest Branch of the Korean People's Visit to China Delegation also went to sweep the tombs. Wang Zhen, the chief celebrant, delivered a speech in front of the tomb, saying: Under the leadership of Comrade Mao Zedong, the Chinese revolution, after decades of heroic struggle and the continuous sacrifice of countless outstanding revolutionary martyrs, has achieved today's victory. We should learn from the spirit of the martyrs who served the people wholeheartedly, heighten our class vigilance, resolutely fight against all open and hidden enemies, strengthen the close unity of all ethnic groups, launch a great production competition, build the frontier of the motherland, continue to strengthen the work of resisting US aggression and aiding Korea, and consolidate the people's democratic dictatorship.

People from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Xi'an held a public memorial meeting for revolutionary martyrs. More than 5,000 people attended the meeting, including family members of the martyrs, representatives of local organizations of various democratic parties in Xi'an, and representatives of government agencies, troops, workers, and students. Li Xuesan, the head of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation and Hong Chunzhe, the head of the Korean People's Visit to China Delegation, who arrived in Xi'an a few days ago, also participated in the ceremony and presented wreaths to the revolutionary martyrs to pay their respects. Li Xuesan, on behalf of Commander Peng Dehuai of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and all commanders and combatants, expressed deep condolences to the revolutionary martyrs, especially those who gloriously sacrificed in the war to resist US aggression and aid Korea, and extended sincere condolences to the martyrs and the families of the Volunteer Army martyrs. Hong Chunzhe, the head of the Korean People's Visit to China Delegation, recited a poem entitled "People Who Live Forever" and presented it to the Chinese revolutionary martyrs.

People from all walks of life in Tianjin held a memorial meeting for revolutionary martyrs. More than 500 people attended the meeting, including family members of the martyrs and representatives from all walks of life. Zhou Shutao, the deputy mayor of Tianjin, expressed his condolences and respect to the revolutionary martyrs on behalf of the people of the city and extended cordial greetings to the glorious families of the martyrs. On the morning of the same day, more than a hundred representatives from the literary and art circles in Tianjin went to the tombs of patriotic artists Chang Baokun and Cheng Shutang, who sacrificed on the front lines in Korea, to pay their respects.

One thousand six hundred representatives from all walks of life in Taiyuan solemnly held a memorial meeting for revolutionary martyrs in front of the Martyrs' Monument in People's Park. Tian Bo, the head of the North China Branch of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation, and Shen Guozhen, the head of the North China Branch of the Korean People's Visit to China Delegation, also participated in the ceremony.

More than 18,000 representatives from various government agencies, organizations, and troops stationed in Zhangjiakou, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, and representatives from all walks of life in Zhangjiakou City, Chahar Province, jointly held a public memorial meeting for revolutionary martyrs in Victory Square, Zhangjiakou City. Ha Feng'a, the vice chairman of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Yang Gengtian, the chairman of the People's Government of Chahar Province, and Lu Fu, the vice chairman, presided over the ceremony. When Ha Feng'a paid tribute to the martyrs who gloriously sacrificed for the cause of people's liberation on behalf of the people of all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Inner Mongolia, he called on everyone to learn from the spirit of patriotism and revolutionary heroism of the revolutionary martyrs, mobilize all the people to form a strong epidemic prevention line, and resolutely defeat the vicious conspiracy of the US imperialism's germ warfare.

On the 5th, more than 2,900 representatives from government agencies, organizations, schools, and citizens in Qingdao went to the martyrs' cemetery to sweep the tombs. The representatives were extremely indignant at the heinous crimes of the American aggressors in spreading germs and poisonous insects in Korea and our country's Northeast and Qingdao. They unanimously expressed their determination to actively respond to the call of the people's government to strengthen the work of resisting US aggression and aiding Korea, do a good job in preferential treatment, and implement the "Three-Anti" and "Five-Anti" movements and the increase production and practice economy movement with practical actions, and strive for the final victory in resisting US aggression and aiding Korea.

### 迪化西安天津太原等地各界人民  清明节举行追悼革命先烈大会

1952-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    迪化西安天津太原等地各界人民
    清明节举行追悼革命先烈大会
    【新华社五日讯】五日清明节，迪化、西安、天津、太原、张家口、青岛各地各界人民都隆重举行追悼革命先烈大会，到烈士墓地扫墓。
    迪化市党、政、军机关首长王震、鲍尔汉等和各族各界代表回族总教长马良骏、维吾尔族总教长和加局牙孜等近千人，五日列队前往中共创始人之一，故中共中央委员陈潭秋烈士、中共优秀党员毛泽民、乔国桢、林基路等烈士及爱国民主人士杜重远烈士墓前奠祭悼念。中国人民志愿军归国代表团西北分团、朝鲜人民访华代表团西北分团全体代表也前往扫墓。主祭人王震在墓前发表演说，他说：中国革命在毛泽东同志领导下，经过数十年的英勇奋斗，无数优秀的革命烈士前仆后继流血牺牲，才换取到今天的胜利。我们应当学习先烈们全心全意为人民服务的精神，提高阶级警惕性，和一切公开的和隐蔽的敌人作坚决斗争，加强各民族的亲密团结，展开生产大竞赛，建设祖国边疆，继续加强抗美援朝工作，巩固人民民主专政。
    西安市各民族各界人民举行了公祭革命先烈大会，到会的有烈士家属、各民主党派西安市地方组织的代表和机关、部队、工人、学生代表五千余人。日前到达西安的中国人民志愿军归国代表团团长李雪三和朝鲜人民访华代表团团长洪淳哲也参加祭典，并在革命先烈灵前献花圈致敬。李雪三代表中国人民志愿军彭德怀司令员和全体指挥员、战斗员向革命先烈，尤其是在抗美援朝战争中光荣牺牲的烈士们致沉痛的悼念，并向烈士和志愿军烈士家属致诚挚的慰问。朝鲜人民访华代表团团长洪淳哲朗诵了以“永远活着的人们”为题的诗篇，献呈中国革命先烈。
    天津市各界人民举行了纪念革命先烈追悼大会，到会的有烈士家属和各界代表五百多人。天津市副市长周叔弢在会上代表全市人民向革命先烈致以哀悼和崇敬，并向光荣的烈士家属致以亲切的慰问。同日上午天津市文艺界代表百余人前往在朝鲜前线牺牲的爱国艺人常宝堃、程树棠二烈士墓前祭奠。
    太原市各界代表一千六百人在人民公园烈士纪念碑前，隆重举行了追悼革命先烈大会。中国人民志愿军归国代表团华北分团团长田波、朝鲜人民访华代表团华北分团团长沈国镇也参加祭奠。
    内蒙古自治区驻张家口各机关团体部队代表及察哈尔省张家口市各界代表等一万八千多人在张家口市胜利广场联合举行公祭革命烈士纪念大会。由内蒙古自治区人民政府副主席哈丰阿、察哈尔省人民政府主席杨耕田和副主席吕复等主祭。哈丰阿代表内蒙古各族各界人民向为人民解放事业光荣牺牲的烈士们致祭时，号召大家学习革命烈士们的爱国主义和革命英雄主义的精神，发动全体人民组成强大的防疫战线，坚决打败美帝国主义细菌战的毒辣阴谋。
    青岛市各机关、团体、学校及市民代表共两千九百余人五日前往烈士墓地祭扫陵墓。代表们对美国侵略者在朝鲜及我国东北、青岛等地撒布细菌毒虫的滔天罪行感到万分愤慨。他们一致表示要积极响应人民政府号召加强抗美援朝工作，做好优抚工作，并以实际行动贯彻“三反”“五反”及增产节约运动，争取抗美援朝的最后胜利。
