Title: China Peace Committee and All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Send Telegram to Commemorate the 500th Anniversary of the Birth of Italian Artist da Vinci
Author:
Date: 1952-04-14
Page: 1

The China Peace Committee and the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles
    Send Telegram to Commemorate the 500th Anniversary of the Birth of Italian Artist da Vinci

[Report from this newspaper] April 15th is the 500th anniversary of the birth of the great Italian artist da Vinci. The China People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression and the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles sent a telegram on April 12th to the Italian Commemorative Committee for the 500th Anniversary of the Birth of da Vinci to express congratulations. The telegram said: "On the occasion of the 500th anniversary of the birth of the great Italian artist and pioneer of modern invention, da Vinci, we, on behalf of the Chinese people, extend our heartfelt congratulations. Da Vinci's artistic masterpieces and creative ideas reflect the struggle of the broad masses of people at that time who demanded progress, opposed darkness, and adhered to the truth. The Chinese people deeply cherish da Vinci's brilliant achievements in art and science, and consider them a common treasure of the people of the world. In today's era, when peace-loving people all over the world are engaged in a great struggle to defend world peace and strengthen cultural exchanges, commemorating da Vinci has even greater significance. The Chinese people are willing to join hands with the Italian people to work together to defend world peace and strengthen the exchange of people's cultures."

### 我保卫和平反美侵略委员会和全国文联  致电纪念意大利艺术家芬奇诞生五百周年

1952-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我保卫和平反美侵略委员会和全国文联
    致电纪念意大利艺术家芬奇诞生五百周年
    【本报讯】四月十五日是意大利伟大艺术家芬奇诞生五百周年纪念日，中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会和中华全国文学艺术界联合会，于四月十二日致电芬奇诞生五百周年意大利纪念委员会祝贺。电文说：兹值意大利伟大艺术家、现代发明的先驱者——芬奇诞生五百周年，我们代表中国人民谨致衷心的祝贺。芬奇的艺术杰作和创造性思想，反映当时广大人民要求进步、反对黑暗、坚持真理的斗争。中国人民非常珍视芬奇在艺术上及科学上的辉煌功绩，认为这是世界人民共同的宝藏。在今天全世界爱好和平的人民正在为保卫世界和平、加强文化交流而进行伟大斗争的时代，我们纪念芬奇，具有更重大的意义。中国人民愿与意大利人民携手，为保卫世界和平、加强人民文化的交流而共同努力。
