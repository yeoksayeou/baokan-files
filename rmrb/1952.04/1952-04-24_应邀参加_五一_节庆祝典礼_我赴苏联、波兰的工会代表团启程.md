Title: In Response to Invitation to Attend May Day Celebration Ceremonies, Our Trade Union Delegations Depart for the Soviet Union and Poland

Author:
Date: 1952-04-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd:

In response to the invitation from the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, the delegation from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, which is going to the Soviet Union to participate in the May Day celebration ceremonies, has already departed from Beijing by plane on the morning of the 23rd.  Li Zaiwen, Director of the General Office of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department, and others were at the airport to see them off.

The list of the delegation is as follows:

Head of Delegation: Liu Ziju, Minister of Culture and Education Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions

Members: Wang Xiaoci, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the China Railway Trade Union
	Song Xinhuai, Chairman of the Hunan Provincial Federation of Trade Unions
	Yu Zhou, Chairman of the Shanghai District Committee of the China Railway Trade Union
	Peng Yangqin, Director of a Power Plant, Model Worker
	Ma Liuhaizi, Coal Miner, Model Worker
	Hao Jianxiu (female), Textile Worker, Model Worker
	Zhang Shaoru, Ordnance Worker, Model Worker
	Liu Dezhen, Electricity Worker, Model Worker
	Zhang Mingqi, Translator
	Yu Yehai, Translator

Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd: In response to the invitation from the Central Council of Trade Unions of Poland, the delegation from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, which is going to Poland to participate in the May Day celebration ceremonies, departed from Beijing by plane on the morning of the 23rd. Li Zaiwen, Director of the General Office of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department, and others were at the airport to see them off.

Also at the airport to see them off were: Gula, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Republic of Poland in China, Gushiduo, Commercial Attaché, Shi Shike, Military Attaché, and Luo Buman, Press Attaché.

The list of the delegation is as follows:

Head of Delegation: An Lifu, Chairman of the National Committee of the China Transport Workers' Union

Members: Su Changyou, Construction Worker Model Worker
	Feng Xuran, Coal Miner Model Worker
	Zhao Lanying (female), Textile Worker, Model Worker
	Yuan Baohua, Translator
	Liu Xiangtai, Translator

### 应邀参加“五一”节庆祝典礼  我赴苏联、波兰的工会代表团启程

1952-04-24
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加“五一”节庆祝典礼
    我赴苏联、波兰的工会代表团启程
    【新华社二十三日讯】中华全国总工会应全苏工会中央理事会的邀请，派往苏联参加五一节庆祝典礼的代表团，已在二十三日晨全部乘飞机离京。到机场欢送的有中华全国总工会办公厅主任栗再温、国际联络部副部长金子明等人。

    团长：刘子久  中华全国总工会文教部部长

    宋新怀  湖南省总工会主席
