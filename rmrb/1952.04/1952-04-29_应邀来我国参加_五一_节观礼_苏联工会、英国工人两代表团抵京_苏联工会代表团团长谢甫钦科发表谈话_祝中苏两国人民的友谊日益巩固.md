Title: In Response to Invitation to Attend "May 1st" Celebrations in China, Soviet Union Trade Union and British Worker Delegations Arrive in Beijing; Head of Soviet Union Trade Union Delegation Shevchenko Delivers Speech; Wishes that the Friendship Between the Chinese and Soviet People will be Increasingly Consolidated.
Author:
Date: 1952-04-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th: The Soviet Union Trade Union delegation, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the May 1st celebrations in China, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 28th. The British worker delegation, also invited to attend the celebrations, arrived at the same time. Those who welcomed them at the airport included: Li Lisan, Zhu Xuefan, and Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Xu Zhizhen, Minister of the Organization Department; Dong Xin, First Deputy Director of the Policy Research Office; Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department; Wu Ping, Deputy Director of the Women Workers Department; Zhao Guoqiang, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the China Metal and Smelting Workers' Union; Jin Zhifu, Chairman of the National Committee of the China Coal Miners' Union; Fang Ming, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the China Educational Workers' Union, and many others. S. Gaoqiao, Counselor of the Soviet Union Embassy in China, also went to the airport to welcome the Soviet Union Trade Union delegation.

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th: Shevchenko, head of the Soviet Union Trade Union delegation invited to attend the "May 1st" celebrations in China, made the following statement to a reporter from this agency after arriving in Beijing today:

    Please allow me, on behalf of the people of all nationalities of the Soviet Union, to extend warm congratulations and best wishes to the great people of the People's Republic of China through your agency.

    Dear friends! We—representatives of the Soviet people, representatives of the socialist country—have come to China at your invitation.

    We have come to you to celebrate our common holiday, the holiday of the working people of all countries of the world—"May 1st" Labor Day—together with you, the great Chinese people.

    This day is a day for the working people of all countries of the world to sum up their struggles for the cause and rights of the working people, and a day for the working people to show their unity and determination to fight to the end for their own interests.

    Let this meeting further consolidate the permanent and indestructible friendship between the working people of China and the Soviet Union.

    Let our friendship be a stern warning to those who violate the rights of the working people, to those who attempt to launch a new world war.

    We sincerely thank you for your invitation, and at the same time declare: The Soviet people are engaged in peaceful and creative labor and are unswervingly implementing a policy of world peace. It will forever advance along this path together with the working people of the whole world.

    May the indestructible friendship between the great People's Republic of China and the Soviet Union be consolidated and last forever.

    We extend our heartfelt respect to the Chinese people and their great leader Mao Zedong.

### 应邀来我国参加“五一”节观礼  苏联工会、英国工人两代表团抵京  苏联工会代表团团长谢甫钦科发表谈话  祝中苏两国人民的友谊日益巩固

专栏：

    应邀来我国参加“五一”节观礼
    苏联工会、英国工人两代表团抵京
    苏联工会代表团团长谢甫钦科发表谈话
    祝中苏两国人民的友谊日益巩固
    【新华社二十八日讯】应中华全国总工会的邀请前来我国参加五一节观礼的苏联工会代表团已于二十八日下午乘飞机到达北京。应邀前来观礼的英国工人代表团也同时到达。到机场欢迎的有：中华全国总工会副主席李立三、朱学范、刘宁一，组织部部长许之桢，政策研究室第一副主任董昕，国际联络部副部长金子明，女工部副部长吴平，中国五金冶炼工会筹备委员会主任赵国强，中国煤矿工会全国委员会主席金直夫，中国教育工会全国委员会副主席方明等多人。赴机场欢迎苏联工会代表团的还有苏联驻华大使馆参赞司高磋等。

    【新华社二十八日讯】应邀前来参加我国“五一”节观礼的苏联工会代表团团长谢甫钦科，今日到达北京后对本社记者发表谈话如下：
    请允许我代表苏联各族人民通过贵社向伟大的中华人民共和国人民致热烈的祝贺和最好的祝愿。
    亲爱的朋友们！我们——苏联人民的代表，社会主义国家的代表——应你们的邀请来到了中国。
    我们来到你们这里，是为了和你们——伟大的中国人民一道迎接我们共同的节日，世界各国劳动人民的节日——“五一”劳动节。
    这一天是全世界各国劳动人民总结他们为劳动人民的事业和权利而进行的斗争的日子，是劳动人民显示他们为切身利益而斗争到底的团结和决心的日子。
    让我们这一次会面进一步地巩固中苏两国劳动人民永久的不可摧毁的友谊。
    让我们的友谊成为对那些侵犯劳动人民权利的人们，对那些企图发动新的世界战争的人们的严峻警告。
    我们衷心感谢你们的邀请，并同时表明：苏联人民进行着和平的创造性的劳动，而且始终不渝地执行着世界和平政策。它将永远地和全世界劳动人民一道沿着这条道路前进。
    祝伟大的中华人民共和国和苏联两国人民的不可摧毁的友谊巩固和永存。
    向中国人民及其伟大的领袖毛泽东致以衷心的敬意。
