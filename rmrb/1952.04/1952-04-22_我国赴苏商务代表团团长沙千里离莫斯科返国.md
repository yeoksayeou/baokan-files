Title: Head of Chinese Trade Delegation to the Soviet Union, Sha Qianli, Departs Moscow for Home

Author:
Date: 1952-04-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 21st - Sha Qianli, head of the Chinese Trade Delegation to the Soviet Union to participate in the Sino-Soviet trade negotiations, departed Moscow by plane on the 20th to return home. Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Pavlov and Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Zhang Wentian, among others, were at the airport to see him off.

### 我国赴苏商务代表团团长沙千里离莫斯科返国

1952-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    我国赴苏商务代表团团长沙千里离莫斯科返国
    【新华社莫斯科二十一日电】前来苏联参加中苏贸易谈判的我国商务代表团团长沙千里已在二十日乘飞机离莫斯科返国，苏联对外贸易部副部长巴夫洛夫、我国驻苏大使张闻天等都曾到机场欢送。
