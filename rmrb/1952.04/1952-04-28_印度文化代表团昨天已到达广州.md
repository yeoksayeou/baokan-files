Title: Indian Cultural Delegation Arrived in Guangzhou Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-04-28
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, April 27th. The Indian cultural delegation, invited to visit China, led by the head of the delegation Mrs. Pandit, a group of twenty-six people, arrived in Guangzhou at 3 p.m. on the 27th. Zhu Guang, Deputy Mayor of Guangzhou, Yun Guangying, Secretary-General of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, Du Guoxiang, Director of the Provincial Department of Culture and Education, Lu Xiangcang, Deputy Director of the Municipal Bureau of Culture and Education, Cao Ruoming, Director of the Guangzhou Foreign Affairs Office, and others went to welcome them. Chen Shuliang, Special Commissioner of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, and Zou Difan, Director of the Office of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council, came from Beijing to greet them.
    [This newspaper's report] According to Xinhua News Agency: Before the Indian cultural delegation departed from India, Shen Jian, Charge d'Affaires of our embassy in India, gave a farewell banquet for the delegation on the evening of the 24th. Indian Prime Minister Nehru, important officials from the Indian government's Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Ministry of Education, as well as many members of the Indian Goodwill Delegation to China who visited our country last year, also attended the reception.

### 印度文化代表团昨天已到达广州

1952-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    印度文化代表团昨天已到达广州
    【新华社广州二十七日电】应邀前来访问我国的印度文化代表团，由团长潘迪特夫人率领一行二十六人，已于二十七日下午三时抵达广州。广州市人民政府副市长朱光、广东省人民政府秘书长云广英、省文教厅长杜国庠、市文教局副局长陆向苍、广州外事处处长曹若茗等均前往欢迎。中央人民政府外交部特派亚洲司专员陈叔亮、政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局特派办公室主任邹荻帆由京前来迎接。
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：印度文化代表团启程离印度前，我国驻印度大使馆代办申健曾于二十四日晚为代表团饯行。印度总理尼赫鲁、印度政府外交部和教育部的重要官员以及曾在去年访问我国的印度亲善访华团团员多人，也出席了招待会。
