Title: International Conference for the Defence of Children Opens in Vienna
Author:
Date: 1952-04-14
Page: 1

    International Conference for the Defence of Children Opens in Vienna
    [Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, 13th] TASS, Vienna: The International Conference for the Defence of Children opened on the 12th in Vienna, the capital of Austria. Five hundred delegates from sixty countries are participating in the conference. These delegates are people with different political and religious beliefs, including children's parents, prominent educators, doctors, scientists, writers, actors, civil servants, and trade union leaders. The World Federation of Trade Unions, the Women's International Democratic Federation, the World Democratic Youth League, and other international organizations have also sent representatives to the conference.
    The conference hall is decorated with slogans in various languages: "Defend the lives, health, and education of children by maintaining peace," and is decorated with peace emblems and the national flags of the countries represented at the conference.
    The conference was declared open by Kotz-Bethel, Chairman of the Austrian Association for the Protection of Children. On behalf of the Austrian working people, she warmly welcomed the attendees, and emphasized that this conference will play a major role in strengthening international peace and friendship and protecting children from the threats of war and hunger.
    The conference will hear and discuss the following four reports: "What can be done for children under peaceful conditions," "Children's health issues," "Children's education issues," and "The impact of literature, newspapers, radio, film, and art on the spiritual and cultural development of children."
    At the morning session, Professor Monod, the delegate from France, delivered the first report.

### 国际保卫儿童大会在维也纳开幕

1952-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    国际保卫儿童大会在维也纳开幕
    【新华社十三日讯】据塔斯社维也纳讯：国际保卫儿童大会于十二日在奥地利首都维也纳开幕。参加大会的有六十个国家的五百名代表。这些代表都是具有不同的政治和宗教信仰的人，他们当中有儿童的父母、著名的教育工作者、医生、科学家、作家、演员、公务员和工会领袖。世界工会联合会、国际民主妇女联合会、世界民主青年联盟及其他国际组织，也派代表参加大会。
    会议大厅装饰着以各种文字书写的标语：“通过维护和平来保卫儿童的生命、健康和教育”，并装饰着和平徽和有代表参加大会的各国国旗。
    大会由奥地利保护儿童协会主席柯兹——贝特尔宣布开幕。她代表奥地利劳动人民热烈欢迎到会者，并强调说：这次大会将在加强国际和平与友好、保护儿童使之免受战争和饥饿的威胁方面，起重大的作用。
    大会将听取并讨论下列四个报告：“在和平条件下可以为儿童做些什么”、“儿童健康问题”、“儿童教育问题”、“文学、报纸、无线电、电影和艺术对于儿童的精神和文化发展的影响”。
    在上午会议上，法国代表莫诺教授作了第一个报告。
