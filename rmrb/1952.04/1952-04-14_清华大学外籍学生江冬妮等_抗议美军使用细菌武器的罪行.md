Author: 
Date: 1952-04-14
Page: 2

Reader's Letters

    Tsinghua University Foreign Students Jiang Dongni and Others
    Protest the U.S. Military's Crimes of Using Bacteriological Weapons
    We are students from Eastern European New Democratic countries studying at Tsinghua University. Like peace-loving people all over the world, we indignantly protest the U.S. aggressors' crime of using bacteriological weapons in Korea and China, weapons of mass slaughter of peaceful people and forbidden by international law.
    This insane action by U.S. imperialism is a desperate act after its plot to enslave the peace-loving Korean people was shattered. These so-called "civilized" barbarians, after leisurely burning, destroying Korean cities, villages, schools, libraries, monuments, and cultural palaces, etc., have launched a bacteriological war,妄想杀尽全朝鲜人民和屠杀世界上更多的不愿意受它们奴役的人民. But we are sure that U.S. imperialists will be defeated by peace-loving people all over the world. Peace-loving people all over the world will surely chop off the infinitely sinful poisonous hands of the American bacteriological war criminals.
    The just court of peace-loving people all over the world will severely punish the bacteriological war criminals!
    Eastern European Students at Tsinghua University: Jiang Dongni, Jia Xuelie, Fa Si, Janos, Li Ximeng

### 清华大学外籍学生江冬妮等  抗议美军使用细菌武器的罪行

1952-04-14
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    清华大学外籍学生江冬妮等
    抗议美军使用细菌武器的罪行
    我们是在清华大学学习的东欧新民主主义国家的学生。我们和全世界和平人民一样，愤慨地抗议美国侵略者在朝鲜和中国使用大规模屠杀和平人民的、为国际公法所禁止的细菌武器的罪行。
    美帝国主义这一疯狂行动，是它企图奴役爱好和平的朝鲜人民的阴谋被粉碎之后的狗急跳墙的表现。这些所谓“文明”的野蛮人，在怡然自得地焚毁、破坏朝鲜的城市、乡村、学校、图书馆、纪念碑和文化宫等等之后，又发动了细菌战争，妄想杀尽全朝鲜人民和屠杀世界上更多的不愿意受它们奴役的人民。但是，我们确信，美帝国主义者一定要被全世界和平人民所击败。全世界和平人民必将斩断美国细菌战犯的无比罪恶的毒手。
