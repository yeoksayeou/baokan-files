Title: International Association of Democratic Lawyers Investigation Team Members Arrive in Austria to Attend IADL Council Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-04-15
Page: 4

    International Association of Democratic Lawyers Investigation Team Members Arrive in Austria
    To Attend IADL Council Meeting
    [Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, April 14th] TASS Vienna, April 12th: Professor Brandweiner (Austria), Mrs. Morens (Belgium), Cavallari (Italy), Gastel (Great Britain), and Mrs. Wasilkowska (Poland), members of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers investigation team, have arrived in Vienna.
    The investigation team members will submit a report to the International Association of Democratic Lawyers Council meeting to be held in Vienna next week regarding the crimes of the American invading forces using bacteriological weapons in Korea and China.

### 国际民主法协调查团团员抵奥  将出席国际法协理事会议

1952-04-15
第4版()
专栏：

    国际民主法协调查团团员抵奥
    将出席国际法协理事会议
    【新华社十四日讯】据塔斯社维也纳十二日讯：国际民主法律工作者协会调查团的布兰德魏纳教授（奥地利）、莫伦斯夫人（比利时）、卡瓦莱里（意大利）、加斯特尔（英国）、华西尔考夫斯卡夫人（波兰），已经抵达维也纳。
    调查团团员们将向下星期在维也纳举行的国际民主法律工作者协会理事会会议，提出关于美国侵略军在朝鲜和中国使用细菌武器的罪行的报告。
