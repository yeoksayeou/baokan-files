Title: Railway Workers Across the Country Write Letters Resolving to Extinguish the Bacteriological Warfare Launched by the American Aggressors - Excerpts from Readers' Letters
Author:
Date: 1952-04-07
Page: 2

Readers' Letters

    Railway Workers Across the Country Write Letters
    Resolving to Extinguish the Bacteriological Warfare Launched by the American Aggressors
    -- Excerpts from Readers' Letters
    Railway workers across the country have written to this newspaper, resolving to fulfill transportation tasks, vigorously support the Volunteer Army, and shatter the bacteriological warfare launched by the American aggressors.
    The entire membership of the Trade Union of the Railway Research Institute of the Ministry of Railways wrote, "We cannot tolerate the heinous crime of the American aggressors in spreading bacteria. We are determined to improve railway technology and support the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. We are ready at any time to respond to the call of the motherland, go to the front line of the anti-bacteriological warfare, and deal a fatal blow to the bacteriological warfare criminals!" All the railway workers at Shanhaiguan Station wrote, "We are determined to work hard to increase production and practice economy to support the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and let the American imperialists and bacteria perish together." All 292 employees of the Qingdao Railway Branch Train Section wrote, "In our work, we resolutely eliminate accidents and ensure the safety of transportation, so that the materials from the people of the motherland to support the Volunteer Army can be smoothly transported to the Korean front line to strike at the American aggressors who launched bacteriological warfare." Wan Jing of the Zhengzhou Railway Administration wrote, "More than 8,000 employees and some family members in the Zhengzhou area of ​​the Zhengzhou Railway Administration unanimously protested against the heinous crimes of the American aggressors in conducting bacteriological warfare. All employees guarantee to do a good job in production and transportation tasks, complete the 1952 production and financial plan, accumulate more wealth for the motherland, support the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and give the bacteriological warfare criminals a devastating blow." All the railway workers of Transportation Team C of Hengyang Station wrote, "We are determined to eliminate all man-made accidents in our work, step up the study of advanced shunting methods, overfulfill transportation tasks, support the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and strike at the American aggressors who are conducting bacteriological warfare." Xu Changfeng and 112 other family members of the second dormitory of the Xuzhou Railway Branch wrote, "We must help our husbands explain their respective problems clearly in the 'Three-Anti' movement, mobilize our husbands to actively produce, and we ourselves must also work hard to produce; we must use these practical actions to protest against the crimes of American imperialism in using bacteria to kill the Chinese and Korean people."

### 全国各地铁路职工纷纷来信  决心扑灭美国侵略者发动的细菌战——读者来信摘要

1952-04-07
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    全国各地铁路职工纷纷来信
    决心扑灭美国侵略者发动的细菌战
    ——读者来信摘要
    全国各地铁路职工纷纷写信给本报，决心完成交通运输任务，努力支持志愿军，粉碎美国侵略者发动的细菌战。
    铁道部铁道研究所工会全体会员来信说：“我们不能容忍美国侵略者撒布细菌的滔天罪行。我们决心改进铁道技术，支援中国人民志愿军。我们随时准备响应祖国的号召，走上反细菌战的前线，给细菌战犯以致命的打击！”山海关站全体铁路职工来信说：“我们决心努力增产节约，以支援中国人民志愿军，让美帝国主义者和细菌同归于尽。”青岛铁路分局列车段全体二百九十二名职工来信说：“我们在工作中坚决地消灭事故，保证运输的安全，使祖国人民支援志愿军的物资顺畅地运到朝鲜前线，以打击发动细菌战的美国侵略者。”郑州铁路管理局万晶来信说：“郑州铁路管理局郑州地区职工和一部分家属共八千多人，一致抗议美国侵略者进行细菌战的滔天罪行。全体职工保证搞好生产运输任务，完成一九五二年度生产财务计划，为祖国积累更多财富，支援中国人民志愿军，给细菌战犯以毁灭性的打击。”衡阳站运输丙班全体铁路职工来信说：“我们决心在工作中消灭一切人为的事故，加紧学习先进调车法，超额完成运输任务，支援中国人民志愿军，以打击进行细菌战的美国侵略者。”徐州铁路分局职工第二宿舍家属徐长凤等一百十二人来信说：“我们要在‘三反’运动中帮助丈夫交代清楚各自的问题，动员丈夫积极生产，我们自己也努力生产；我们要用这些实际行动，抗议美帝国主义使用细菌杀害中朝人民的罪行。”
