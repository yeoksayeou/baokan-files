Title: Reports on Investigations into American Bacterial Warfare Crimes Prominently Published in Moscow Newspapers
Author: 
Date: 1952-04-30
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 27th -- "Pravda," "Izvestia," "Trud," and "Red Star" prominently published on the 27th the investigation report by the Chinese "Investigation Group on American Imperialist Bacterial Warfare Crimes" regarding the crimes of American imperialism spreading bacteria in Korea.

### 关于美国细菌战罪行调查报告  莫斯科各报都以巨幅刊载

1952-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    关于美国细菌战罪行调查报告
    莫斯科各报都以巨幅刊载
