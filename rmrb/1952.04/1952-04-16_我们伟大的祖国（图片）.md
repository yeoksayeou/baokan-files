    Our Great Motherland
    Pottery is one of China's famous special products. After liberation, due to smooth transportation and the active purchasing by native product companies, the sales of pottery have been increasing day by day. This is a scene of a large number of daily-use pottery produced in Yixing County, Sunan Administrative Office, being transported by water to various places.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    陶器是我国的著名特产之一。解放以后因为运输畅通和土产公司积极收购，陶器的销路日益扩大。这是苏南行署区宜兴县出产的大批日用陶器正在从水路向各地转运的情形。
