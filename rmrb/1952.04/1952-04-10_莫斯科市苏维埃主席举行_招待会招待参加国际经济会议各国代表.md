Title: Moscow City Soviet Chairman Holds Reception to Welcome Delegates from Various Countries Attending the International Economic Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-04-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 8th: According to TASS, the Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Moscow City Soviet, Yasnov, held a reception on the 6th to welcome delegates from various countries attending the International Economic Conference.

Those attending the reception included: International Economic Conference Secretary-General Chamberlin, Nan Hanchen (China), Professor Taublaetzberg (Austria), Lord Boyd-Orr (UK), Rivera (Argentina), Oliveira (Brazil), Zheng Wenbing (Vietnam), De Gey (Hungary), Kuchkov (German Democratic Republic), De Vries (Netherlands), Taijia Sukmana (Indonesia), Professor Chand (India), Dr. Daftari (Iran), Franco (Italy), Iftikharuddin (Pakistan), Professor Lange (Poland), Basti (France), Wickley (USA), Bauer (Czechoslovakia), and World Federation of Trade Unions leaders Vittorio and Saillant, as well as entrepreneurs, engineers, economists, trade union and cooperative workers from other countries participating in the conference.

Also attending the reception were: All-Soviet Chamber of Commerce Chairman Nesterov and members of the Soviet delegation to the International Economic Conference, Soviet Ministers Zverev, Kumykin, Tikhomirov, Stoletov, Moscow City Soviet Executive Committee Vice-Chairmen Bobrovnikov, Buduzov, Gorodov, Kolotygin, Kononenko, Lozmanov, Mironova, Lykov, Filyubin, Moscow City Soviet Executive Committee Secretary Leonov, as well as representatives of the people of Moscow, representatives of the scientific, literary, artistic, and economic circles. Representatives of Soviet and foreign press circles also attended the reception. The reception proceeded in a harmonious and friendly atmosphere. (Picture attached)

　　The International Economic Conference opened in Moscow on April 3. The photograph shows Moscow City Soviet Chairman Yasnov delivering a welcoming speech at the opening ceremony.

### 莫斯科市苏维埃主席举行  招待会招待参加国际经济会议各国代表

1952-04-10
第4版()
专栏：

    莫斯科市苏维埃主席举行
    招待会招待参加国际经济会议各国代表
    【新华社八日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：莫斯科市苏维埃执行委员会主席雅斯诺夫在六日举行招待会，招待参加国际经济会议的各国代表。
    出席招待会的有：国际经济会议秘书长香贝朗，南汉宸（中），陶布莱茨伯格教授（奥地利），波以德—奥尔勋爵（英），里维拉（阿根廷），奥立维拉（巴西），郑文炳（越南），德盖伊（匈），库赫科夫（德意志民主共和国），德·甫里斯（荷），台佳苏克曼那（印尼），昌德教授（印度），达夫塔里博士（伊朗），佛兰科（意），伊夫提卡鲁丁（巴基斯坦），兰基教授（波），巴斯迪（法），维克利（美），鲍尔（捷）和世界工会联合会领袖维多里奥、赛扬，以及参加会议的其他国家的企业家、工程师、经济学家、工会和合作社工作者。
    出席招待会的还有：全苏商会主席奈斯特洛夫和参加国际经济会议的苏联代表团人员，苏联部长兹维列夫、库米金、提贺米尔罗夫、斯托列托夫，莫斯科市苏维埃执行委员会副主席波布罗尼科夫、布杜佐夫、果洛多夫、科洛提金、科诺年科、洛兹曼诺夫、密罗诺娃、李若科夫、费留宾，莫斯科市苏维埃执行委员会书记里昂诺夫，以及莫斯科人民的代表，科学界、文学界、艺术界和经济团体的代表。苏联和外国报界的代表也出席了招待会。招待会在和睦友好的气氛中进行。（附图片）
    四月三日，在莫斯科举行的国际经济会议开幕。照片上是莫斯科市苏维埃主席雅斯诺夫在开幕会上致欢迎词时的情形。
