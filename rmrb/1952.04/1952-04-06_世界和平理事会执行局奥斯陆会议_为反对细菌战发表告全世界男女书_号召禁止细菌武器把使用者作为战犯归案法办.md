Title: World Peace Council Executive Bureau Oslo Meeting Issues Appeal to Men and Women Worldwide Against Bacteriological Warfare; Calls for Ban on Bacteriological Weapons and for Bringing Users to Justice as War Criminals
Author:
Date: 1952-04-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 4th: TASS, Oslo, April 2nd: The World Peace Council Executive Bureau Oslo meeting passed the following appeal to men and women worldwide entitled "Against Bacteriological Warfare": We have carefully studied the documents concerning the current bacteriological warfare being waged in China and Korea, and we are deeply concerned and shocked. Bacteriological warfare is not only an abhorrent crime that should be stopped, but also a threat to all mankind. We appeal to the conscience of men and women around the world, calling on them to demand the cessation of bacteriological warfare and the banning of bacteriological weapons. We first appeal to the men and women of America, because each of them is faced with a question of integrity and character. The fact that the Korean War is being conducted in the name of the United Nations is a particularly serious situation; we believe that the governments that approved the United Nations' intervention and are therefore morally and politically responsible for the methods used in the war, should be held accountable for this situation. As for us, we have a dual obligation: first, to report the situation to public opinion and to tell the truth to everyone, so that everyone can judge the ugly facts we already know. We have decided to publish the documents we currently have, so that the crimes must be brought to light. In order to bring these crimes to light, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression recommends the establishment of an international committee capable of collecting all the facts. The authority and impartiality of this committee should be indisputable. To establish this committee, we invite the most prestigious representatives of the scientific, legal, and religious communities to participate. We are confident that world public opinion will support the efforts of this committee. Our second obligation is to protect all people from the devastation of bacteriological warfare. We believe that only the United States, the only major country, has not ratified the International Agreement of June 17, 1925, banning the use of poison gas and bacteriological weapons. We call on the people of the world to put pressure on their governments to ensure that all countries sign, ratify, and abide by this agreement without exception. We also demand strong action to bring those criminals who use the most despicable, filthy, and appalling weapons to justice as war criminals. We are confident that our demand to protect the innocent and punish the guilty represents the opinion of the vast majority. We represent them in defending the noblest cause—the right of all children to survive and respect for humanity. If the people of all countries do not take immediate action to stop bacteriological warfare, then the cruel brutality of the forces of destruction will be limitless. We call on humanity to rise up in self-defense.
    World Peace Council Chairman: Joliot-Curie.
    World Peace Council Vice-Chairmen: D'Arboussier, Fadeyev, Guo Moruo, Lady Gorden, Professor Bernal, Professor Infeld, Lundkvist.
    World Peace Council Executive Bureau Members: Mrs. Streit, Gottwald, Ehrenburg, Professor Hromadka, Lombardo, Seillant, Casanova, Mao Dun, Faggi, Korneichuk, Mukarovsky.
    World Peace Council Secretary-General: Lafferd.
    World Peace Council Secretaries: Mrs. Blumm, Deschamps, Finolart, Guryayev, Dahl, Gore, Xiao San, Montagu.
    Special Guests at the Meeting: Wellmann (Representative of the German Peace Committee), Theunissen (World Federation of Democratic Youth), Li Kiyong (Korea), Mrs. Hansson (Norway), Schaffer (UK).

### 世界和平理事会执行局奥斯陆会议  为反对细菌战发表告全世界男女书  号召禁止细菌武器把使用者作为战犯归案法办

1952-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会执行局奥斯陆会议
    为反对细菌战发表告全世界男女书
    号召禁止细菌武器把使用者作为战犯归案法办
    【新华社四日讯】塔斯社奥斯陆二日讯：世界和平理事会执行局奥斯陆会议通过的题为“反对细菌战”的告全世界男女书如下：我们仔细地研讨了有关目前在中国和朝鲜进行的细菌战的文件以后，深感焦虑和震惊。细菌战不仅是应该予以制止的丑恶罪行，而且是对全人类的威胁。我们向全世界男女的良心呼吁，号召他们要求制止细菌战和禁止细菌武器。我们首先向美国的男女呼吁，因为他们每个人的面前都出现了一个正直与否和品格如何的问题。朝鲜战争之用联合国名义进行，是一种特别严重的情况；我们认为：那些赞成联合国进行干涉因而对进行战争所使用的方法负有道义和政治责任的政府，应对这种情况负责。至于我们，则负有双重义务：首先便是向舆论界报告情况，并把真相告诉每一个人，使人人都能判断我们已经晓得的丑恶事实。我们决定公布我们现有的文件，必须使罪行大白于天下。为了使这些罪行大白于天下，中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会建议成立一个能够收集一切事实的国际委员会。这个委员会的权威性和公正性应该是无可争辩的。为了建立这个委员会，我们请科学界、法律界和宗教界最有威望的代表参加。我们确信世界舆论必将支持这个委员会的努力。我们的第二个义务就是保卫所有的人民，使免于细菌战的浩劫。我们认为：只有美国，也就是大国中唯一的国家，才没有批准一九二五年六月十七日禁止使用毒气和细菌武器的国际公约。我们要求全世界人民对本国的政府施加压力，以使所有国家都毫无例外地签署、批准并遵守这个公约。我们同时要求采取强有力的行动，把那些使用最卑鄙龌龊、最骇人听闻的武器的罪犯们，作为战犯归案法办。我们确信，我们提出保护无辜者而惩办罪犯的要求，是代表着绝大多数人的意见的。我们代表他们捍卫最崇高的事业——一切儿童生存的权利以及对人性的尊重。如果各国人民不立即行动起来制止细菌战，那么，破坏力量的残酷狂暴是没有止境的。我们号召人类起来自卫。世界和平理事会主席：约里奥—居里。
    世界和平理事会副主席：达波赛、法捷耶夫、郭沫若、戈登夫人、贝尔纳教授、英费尔德教授、伦德奎斯特。    世界和平理事会执行局委员：斯崔特夫人、戈特、爱伦堡、赫鲁马特卡教授、龙巴迪、赛扬、卡桑诺伐、茅盾、法奇、考涅楚克、穆卡罗夫斯基。
    世界和平理事会秘书长：拉斐德。
    世界和平理事会秘书：布伦姆夫人、德香布兰、芬诺尔塔、顾里亚耶夫、达尔、戈尔、萧三、蒙塔古。
    会议特邀人士：卫尔曼（德国和平委员会代表）、特尼（世界民主青年联盟）、李箕永（朝鲜）、汉斯坦夫人（挪威）、夏菲尔（英国）。
