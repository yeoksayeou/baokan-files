Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Hosts Banquet for Tibetan Representatives from Xikang and Qinghai, Calling for Increased Vigilance to Prevent Imperialist Sabotage Schemes and Further Consolidate National Unity
Author:
Date: 1952-04-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th - Premier Zhou Enlai hosted a banquet at 7:00 PM on the 10th to entertain the Xie Wa La Living Buddha of the Changdu Lama Temple, the Kang Gu Living Buddha of Chaya Zong, the Xia Ri Cang Living Buddha of the Longwu Temple in Tongren County, Gongbu, the Acting Chief Officer of the Ninth Generation of the uprising Tibetan Army, and all members of the Xikang Changdu Tibetan Delegation and the Qinghai Tongren Tibetan Delegation. Among those present were Huang Yanpei, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council; Zhang Xiruo, Deputy Director of the Political and Legal Affairs Committee of the Government Administration Council; Li Weihan, Secretary-General of the Government Administration Council and Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission; Shao Lizi, Member of the Government Administration Council; Ye Jizhuang, Minister of the Central Ministry of Trade; Li Shucheng, Minister of the Central Ministry of Agriculture; Tao Xijin, Secretary-General of the Political and Legal Affairs Committee of the Government Administration Council; Su Jinguan, Deputy Minister of the Central Ministry of Health; Wei Que, Deputy Minister of the Central Ministry of Education; Yu Xinqing, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Central People's Government Committee; Zhu Zaoguan, Deputy Director of the General Office of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Luo Qiong, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Zheng Zhenduo, Director of the Bureau of Social and Cultural Undertakings of the Central Ministry of Culture; and members of the Central Nationalities Affairs Commission in Beijing.

At the meeting, Premier Zhou Enlai delivered a welcome speech. In his speech, Premier Zhou pointed out that the peaceful liberation of Tibet has enabled the Tibetan people to forever break away from the shackles of imperialism and return to the great family of the motherland. This is a great victory in the cause of unity of all nationalities in our country and a great defeat for imperialism. However, imperialism will not be reconciled to failure. We must heighten our vigilance, strictly guard against all sabotage schemes of imperialism, and strive to further consolidate national unity.

Following this, Xie Wa La Living Buddha and Xia Ri Cang Living Buddha spoke successively. In his speech, Xie Wa La Living Buddha expressed his firm will to always follow Chairman Mao and strive for the construction of the Changdu area and the consolidation of national defense. Xia Ri Cang Living Buddha said that all nationalities should unite closely under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Communist Party and strive to build our new China to the end.

The banquet was filled with an atmosphere of unity and friendship.

### 周总理欢宴西康和青海藏族代表  号召提高警惕严防帝国主义的破坏阴谋进一步巩固民族团结

1952-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理欢宴西康和青海藏族代表
    号召提高警惕严防帝国主义的破坏阴谋进一步巩固民族团结
   【新华社十日讯】周恩来总理在十日下午七时设宴招待昌都喇嘛寺谢娃拉活佛、察雅宗康古活佛、同仁县隆务寺夏日仓活佛、起义藏军第九代本代理主官贡布及西康昌都藏族参观团和青海同仁藏族参观团全体人员。出席作陪的有政务院副总理黄炎培，政务院政治法律委员会副主任张奚若，政务院秘书长兼民族事务委员会主任委员李维汉，政务委员邵力子，中央贸易部部长叶季壮，中央农业部部长李书城，政务院政治法律委员会秘书长陶希晋，中央卫生部副部长苏井观，中央教育部副部长韦悫，中央人民政府委员会办公厅副主任余心清，人民革命军事委员会办公厅副主任朱早观，中华全国民主妇女联合会副秘书长罗琼，中央文化部社会文化事业管理局局长郑振铎及中央民族事务委员会在京委员等。
    会上周恩来总理致词欢迎。周总理在讲话中指出，西藏的和平解放，使得西藏人民永远地脱离了帝国主义的羁绊，回到祖国大家庭中来了，这是我国各民族团结事业中的伟大胜利，是帝国主义的巨大失败。但是帝国主义是不会甘心失败的，我们必须提高警惕，严防帝国主义的一切破坏阴谋，为进一步巩固民族团结而奋斗。
    接着，谢娃拉活佛和夏日仓活佛先后讲了话。谢娃拉活佛在讲话中表示了永远跟着毛主席走，愿为昌都地区的建设和巩固国防而奋斗的坚决意志。夏日仓活佛说，各民族要在毛主席和共产党的领导下亲密团结起来，为建设我们的新中国而奋斗到底。
    宴会中充满了团结友爱的空气。
