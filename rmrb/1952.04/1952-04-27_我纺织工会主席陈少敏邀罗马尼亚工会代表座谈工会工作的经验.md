Title: Chen Shaomin, Chairman of the Chinese Textile Workers' Union, Invited Romanian Trade Union Representatives to Discuss Experiences in Trade Union Work
Author: 
Date: 1952-04-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 26th: Chen Shaomin, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Textile Workers' Union, convened a symposium on the afternoon of the 26th to exchange work experiences with members of the Romanian trade union delegation who were invited to our country to attend the "May Day" celebrations. After a brief speech by Chairman Chen Shaomin at the meeting, Stoia Elina, head of the Romanian trade union delegation and Chairman of the Romanian Textile and Leather Workers' Union, introduced the country's enterprise management, trade union organization, and worker welfare. Then, Chairman Chen Shaomin answered questions raised by Stoia Elina regarding China's enterprises and trade union organizations. The symposium was filled with an atmosphere of unity and friendship.

### 我纺织工会主席陈少敏邀罗马尼亚工会代表座谈工会工作的经验

1952-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我纺织工会主席陈少敏邀罗马尼亚工会代表座谈工会工作的经验
    【新华社二十六日讯】中国纺织工会全国委员会主席陈少敏，在二十六日下午召开座谈会，和应邀前来我国参加“五一”节观礼的罗马尼亚工会代表团人员交流工作经验。陈少敏主席在会上简短致辞后，由罗马尼亚工会代表团团长、罗马尼亚纺织皮革工会主席斯托雅·爱丽娜介绍了本国的企业管理、工会组织和工人福利等情况。接着陈少敏主席答复了斯托雅·爱丽娜所提出的有关我国企业和工会组织等问题。座谈会上充满团结友好的气氛。
