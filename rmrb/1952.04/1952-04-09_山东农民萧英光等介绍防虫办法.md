Title: Shandong Farmers Xiao Yingguang and Others Introduce Pest Control Methods
Author: Xiao Yingguang Yang Binghui
Date: 1952-04-09
Page: 6

Letter Summary:

    Shandong Farmers Xiao Yingguang and Others Introduce Pest Control Methods
    In many areas of Shandong, the corn stalks that farmers store as fuel harbor many insects that harm crops. These insects become active when the weather warms up in spring. To prevent and control this pest infestation early and strive for a bumper harvest this year, we have begun to burn the remaining corn stalks. This is a relatively easy pest control method, and we would like to introduce it to farmers everywhere for joint adoption.
            Xiao Yingguang, Yang Binghui, Dongtahou Village, Tahou Township, Rongcheng, Shandong

### 山东农民萧英光等介绍防虫办法
萧英光  杨炳会
1952-04-09
第6版()
专栏：来信摘要

    山东农民萧英光等介绍防虫办法
    山东很多地区农民储作燃料的玉米秸中，藏有许多为害庄稼的虫子。这些虫子在春暖后就要活动起来。我们为了提早防治这种虫害，争取今年丰产，已开始赶烧残存的玉米秸。这是比较容易进行的一种防虫方法，我们愿意介绍给各地农民弟兄一齐采用。
            山东荣成塔后乡东塔后村　萧英光、杨炳会
