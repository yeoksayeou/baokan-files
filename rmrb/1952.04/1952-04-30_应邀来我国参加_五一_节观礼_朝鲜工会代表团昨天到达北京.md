Title: Korean Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing Yesterday to Attend May Day Celebrations at Our Invitation
Author:
Date: 1952-04-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: The Korean Trade Union Delegation, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the "May Day" celebrations in our country, arrived in Beijing by train at noon on the 29th. Those who went to the station to welcome them included: Zhu Xuefan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Xu Zhizhen, Minister of the Organization Department; Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department; Wu Ping, Deputy Director of the Women Workers Department; and Zhao Guoqiang, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the China Metal and Smelting Workers Union. Choi Young, Counselor of the Korean Embassy in China, also went to the station to welcome them.

List of the Korean Trade Union Delegation

Head of Delegation: Park Hyo-bin, Chairman of the Pyongan Bukto Committee of the Korean Professional Federation of Trade Unions.

Member: Zhu Baoyu, Model Worker of the Pyongyang Textile Factory.

Kim Won-il, Labor Hero of the Hamgyong Namdo Heungdok Mine.

### 应邀来我国参加“五一”节观礼  朝鲜工会代表团昨天到达北京

1952-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国参加“五一”节观礼
    朝鲜工会代表团昨天到达北京
    【新华社二十九日讯】应中华全国总工会的邀请前来我国参加“五一”节观礼的朝鲜工会代表团已于二十九日中午乘车到达北京。前往车站欢迎的有：中华全国总工会副主席朱学范、组织部部长许之桢、国际联络部副部长金子明、女工部副部长吴平和中国五金冶炼工会筹备委员会主任赵国强等人。朝鲜驻华大使馆参赞崔英也到车站欢迎。
    朝鲜工会代表团名单
    团长：朴孝斌  朝鲜职业总同盟平安北道委员会委员长
    团员：朱宝玉  平壤市纺织工厂劳动模范
    金元曰  咸镜南道险德矿山劳动英雄
