Title: International Association of Democratic Lawyers Investigation Team Leaves Beijing by Plane Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-04-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th: The International Association of Democratic Lawyers Investigation Team, consisting of Chief Brandweiner, Deputy Chief Cavaglieri, and members Gaster, Morens, and Wasilkovska, successfully completed their mission of investigating American military crimes in Korea and Northeast China. They departed Beijing by plane at 6:00 AM on April 4th and will return to their countries via Moscow. Shen Junru and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the China Peace Committee, and representatives of legal professionals and people's organizations from the capital, including Zhang Zhizhiang, Qian Duansheng, Shi Liang, Yan Baohang, Liu Guanyi, and Ke Bonian, were present at the airport to see them off. Also present were Choi Gyu-heung, Deputy Prosecutor General of the Supreme Prosecutor's Office of Korea, and Ro Bu-man, Press Officer of the Polish Embassy in China.

The International Association of Democratic Lawyers Investigation Team members Di Brito and Jasgay left Beijing on April 2nd to return to their countries.

### 国际民主法协调查团  昨天乘飞机离北京

1952-04-05
第1版()
专栏：

    国际民主法协调查团
    昨天乘飞机离北京
    【新华社四日讯】国际民主法律工作者协会调查团团长布兰德魏纳、副团长卡瓦莱里、团员加斯特尔、莫伦斯、华西尔考夫斯卡五人，已胜利完成在朝鲜和我国东北调查美军罪行的任务，在四月四日早晨六时乘机离京，将经莫斯科返国。中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席沈钧儒、陈叔通，中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会副主席彭真，以及首都法律工作者、人民团体代表张志让、钱端升、史良、阎宝航、刘贯一、柯柏年等亲至机场欢送，参加欢送的还有朝鲜最高检察所副检察总长蔡圭亨、波兰驻华大使馆新闻专员罗步曼等人。
    国际民主法律工作者协会调查团团员狄布里托和贾斯盖已在四月二日离京返国。
