Our Great Motherland

Spring plowing has begun in the upper reaches of the Yellow River. Peach blossoms are in full bloom near Yantan, Lanzhou, Gansu. Waterwheels are turning, and farmers are using light water transportation—sheepskin rafts—to transport fertilizer to the fields.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-04-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    黄河上游地区的春耕开始了。甘肃兰州雁滩附近的桃花都已盛开，水车正在转动，农民们利用着轻便的水上交通工具——羊皮筏，正把肥料运到地里去。
