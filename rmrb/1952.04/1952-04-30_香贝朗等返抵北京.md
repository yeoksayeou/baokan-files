Title: Chamberlin and Others Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-04-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: Chamberlin, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and his wife, along with eighteen representatives from India, Burma, Indonesia, the United Kingdom, Argentina, Canada, Chile, Venezuela, and France who attended the International Economic Conference, returned to Beijing by train from Tianjin on the 29th. Eleven representatives from Australia, Brazil, the United Kingdom, Ceylon, and Mexico, who had been in Beijing and traveled to Shanghai for visits, also returned to Beijing by train on the same morning.

### 香贝朗等返抵北京

1952-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    香贝朗等返抵北京
    【新华社二十九日讯】由京赴津参观的国际贸易促进委员会秘书长香贝朗和夫人，以及出席国际经济会议的印度、缅甸、印尼、英国、阿根廷、加拿大、智利、委内瑞拉、法国的代表一行十八人，二十九日由天津乘火车返抵北京。由京赴上海参观的澳大利亚、巴西、英国、锡兰和墨西哥的代表十一人，也在同日上午乘火车返抵北京。
