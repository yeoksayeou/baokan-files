    Our Great Motherland
    The shark liver oil factory affiliated to the state-owned Shanghai Aquatic Products Company is large in scale and well-equipped. It can process 2,400 catties of shark liver into fish liver oil every day. This is a scene of workers in the fish liver oil factory dissecting shark livers.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营上海水产公司所属的鱼肝油厂，规模较大，设备也比较完善，每天可以将二千四百斤鲨鱼肝制成鱼肝油。这是鱼肝油厂的工人正在剖取鲨鱼肝的情形。
