Title: Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council Continues Meeting to Discuss the Issue of Bacteriological Warfare by American Aggressors
Author:
Date: 1952-04-01
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Oslo, March 31st: The Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council continued its meeting on the 30th to discuss Vice-Chairman Guo Moruo's report on bacteriological warfare and the statement by Li Qiyong, representative of the Korean National Committee for the Defense of Peace, on bacteriological warfare. Speakers at the meeting included Ehrenfeld (Poland), Pajetta (Italy), Deschamps (France), Mukarovsky (Czechoslovakia), Blum (Belgium), Dahl (USA), D’Arboussier (Africa), and Ehrenburg (USSR). The discussion on the above issues was then concluded.
    Executive Bureau member Fajon (France) delivered a report on the remilitarization of Germany. Blum and Weelmann, representative of the German Peace Committee who had just arrived to attend the meeting, spoke on this issue.
    Executive Bureau member Mao Dun (China) then delivered a report on the remilitarization of Japan. Joliot-Curie and Guo Moruo spoke on this issue.
    Executive Bureau member Lombardo (Italy) delivered a report on national sovereignty.
    The committees of the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council will begin their work on the 31st to discuss various proposals.

### 世界和平理事会执行局继续举行会议  讨论关于美国侵略者进行细菌战的问题

1952-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会执行局继续举行会议
    讨论关于美国侵略者进行细菌战的问题
    【新华社三十一日讯】据塔斯社奥斯陆讯：世界和平理事会执行局在三十日继续举行会议，讨论执行局副主席郭沫若关于细菌战的报告和朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会代表李箕永关于细菌战的声明。在会上发言的有英费尔德（波）、巴也塔（意）、德香布兰（法）、穆卡罗夫斯基（捷）、布伦姆（比）、达尔（美）、达波赛（非洲）、爱伦堡（苏）等人。这些代表发言以后，关于上述问题的讨论就宣告结束。
    执行局委员法奇（法）在会上作了关于德国重新军国主义化的报告。布伦姆和刚刚赶到参加会议的德国和平委员会代表卫尔曼就这个问题发了言。
    执行局委员茅盾（中）接着作了关于日本重新军国主义化的报告。约里奥—居里和郭沫若就这个问题发了言。
    执行局委员龙巴迪（意）作了关于民族主权的报告。
    世界和平理事会执行局各委员会将在三十一日开始工作，讨论各项提案。
