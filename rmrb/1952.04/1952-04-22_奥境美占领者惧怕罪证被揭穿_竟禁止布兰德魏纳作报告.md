Title: U.S. Occupation Authorities in Austria Fear Exposure of Crimes, Prohibit Brandweiner from Giving Report
Author:
Date: 1952-04-22
Page: 4

    U.S. Occupation Authorities in Austria Fear Exposure of Crimes
    Prohibit Brandweiner from Giving Report
    [Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, 21st dispatch] TASS, Vienna, 21st dispatch: The Austrian Peace Council planned to hold a gathering on April 19th at the Messepalast, one of the largest buildings in the U.S. occupied zone of Vienna. Professor Brandweiner was originally scheduled to report on the results of his investigation into the facts of the U.S. military's bacteriological warfare in Korea at the meeting. However, at the last moment, at the request of the American occupation authorities, the Vienna police department prohibited the gathering.

### 奥境美占领者惧怕罪证被揭穿  竟禁止布兰德魏纳作报告

1952-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    奥境美占领者惧怕罪证被揭穿
    竟禁止布兰德魏纳作报告
    【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社维也纳二十一日讯：奥地利和平理事会准备于四月十九日在维也纳美占区最大的建筑物之一博览宫举行集会，布兰德魏纳教授原定在会上报告调查美军在朝鲜进行细菌战事实的结果。可是到了最后一刻，由于美国占领当局的要求，维也纳警察局禁止了这次集会。
