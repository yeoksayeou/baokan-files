Author:

    Our Great Motherland
    The Marriage Law has opened up a path to freedom and happiness for the vast number of young men and women in our country. Liu Ali and Lin Xiling, a young couple in Huian County, Fujian Province, overcame feudal forces and finally married freely according to their own wishes. After their marriage, they are living a happy life of working together.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    婚姻法为我国广大的青年男女开辟了自由幸福的道路。福建惠安县的一对青年男女——刘阿礼和林西玲，战胜了封建势力，终于按照自己的心意自由结婚了。他们结婚后正过着共同劳动的愉快生活。
