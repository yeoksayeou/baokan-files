Title: To Celebrate May Day, Beijing's Parks Will Be Open for Free for One Day
Author:
Date: 1952-04-29
Page: 1

    To Celebrate May Day
    Beijing Parks to Open Free for One Day
    [This newspaper's dispatch] To celebrate International Labor Day on May 1st, the Beijing Municipal People's Government Park Administration Committee has decided to open Beihai Park, the Western Suburban Park, the Summer Palace, and the Temple of Heaven free of charge for one day on May 1st. Zhongshan Park will be temporarily closed from 12:00 on April 30th, and will reopen for free visits after the May 1st rally of people from all walks of life in the capital celebrating International Labor Day.
    Furthermore, Zhongshan Park is scheduled to open as a youth recreation area on Youth Day, May 4th. On that day, only those holding admission tickets issued by the May Fourth Preparatory Committee will be admitted; general visits will be suspended.

### 为庆祝“五一”劳动节  北京市各公园免费开放一天

1952-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    为庆祝“五一”劳动节
    北京市各公园免费开放一天
    【本报讯】为庆祝五一劳动节，北京市人民政府公园管理委员会，决定在五月一日将北海公园、西郊公园、颐和园和天坛免费开放一天。中山公园从四月三十日十二时起暂停开放，到五月一日首都各界人民庆祝五一劳动节大会结束后，仍将免费开放游览。
    又中山公园已定在五月四日青年节开放为青年游戏场所，那一天，除持有五四筹备会所发之入门券者外，一般游览停止。
