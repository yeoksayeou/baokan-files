Title: Southwest, Qinghai, and Qamdo Ethnic Minority Visiting Groups Present Flags to Chairman Mao in Beijing to Pay Respects Yesterday

Author: 
Date: 1952-04-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th. The Southwest Ethnic Minority Visiting Group, the Qinghai Tibetan Visiting Group, and the Qamdo Tibetan Visiting Group presented flags to Chairman Mao at 8:00 PM on the 19th, and Chairman Mao personally accepted the flags. Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Li Jishen, Zhang Lan, Secretary-General Lin Boqu, and many others attended the ceremony. A song and dance soiree was held after the flag presentation.

### 西南、青海、昌都三个民族参观团  昨天在京向毛主席献旗致敬

1952-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    西南、青海、昌都三个民族参观团
    昨天在京向毛主席献旗致敬
    【新华社十九日讯】西南各民族参观团、青海藏族参观团和昌都藏族参观团，在十九日下午八时向毛主席献旗，毛主席亲自受旗。参加典礼的还有中央人民政府副主席朱德、刘少奇、李济深、张澜、秘书长林伯渠等多人，献旗后举行歌舞晚会。
