Title: Burmese Overseas Chinese Sightseeing Group Arrives in Guangzhou and Transfers to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-04-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, April 24th: The "Burmese Overseas Chinese Sightseeing Group," consisting of 33 people from various sectors including industry and commerce, education and culture, and women from Burmese Overseas Chinese communities, arrived in Guangzhou on the 22nd by train under the leadership of the head of the delegation Xu Simin, and deputy heads Chen Zhanmei, Huang Tiejin, and Li Yanxing. They transferred to Beijing on the evening of the 23rd.

### 缅甸华侨回国观光团抵广州后转车来京

1952-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸华侨回国观光团抵广州后转车来京
    【新华社广州二十四日电】由缅甸华侨的工商界、文教界和妇女等各界人士组成的“缅甸华侨回国观光团”一行共三十三人，在团长徐四民和副团长陈占梅、黄铁金、李雁行率领下，于二十二日乘车抵达广州，二十三日晚转车前往北京。
