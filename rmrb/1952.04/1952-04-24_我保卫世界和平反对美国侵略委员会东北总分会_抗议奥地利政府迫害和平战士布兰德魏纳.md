Title: Northeast Sub-branch of the China Peace Committee Protesting U.S. Aggression Condemns the Austrian Government's Persecution of Peace Warrior Brandweiner
Author:
Date: 1952-04-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 23rd. The Northeast Sub-branch of the China Peace Committee Protesting U.S. Aggression, in response to the Austrian government's persecution of peace warrior Brandweiner, issued a statement saying: The Austrian government, at the instigation of the U.S. government, is persecuting Professor Brandweiner, head of the investigation team of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers. The people of Northeast China express their utmost indignation at this. The heinous crimes of the American aggressors in using bacteriological weapons in Korea and Northeast China have been confirmed by on-the-spot investigations by international jurists and Chinese scientists, and the truth has been revealed to the world. All peace-loving and humanitarian people around the world are filled with righteous indignation and are condemning the American germ warfare criminals, enemies of humanity. The U.S. government, attempting to cover the eyes and ears of the world, has blatantly resorted to gangster tactics after its conspiracy was exposed, instigating the Austrian government to persecute the just peace warrior Professor Brandweiner, which only proves its guilty conscience and despicable shamelessness. The people of Northeast China, standing at the forefront of the fight against germ warfare, unanimously support Professor Brandweiner's just struggle and strongly protest the U.S. government's crime of instructing the Austrian government to persecute peace warriors.

### 我保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会东北总分会  抗议奥地利政府迫害和平战士布兰德魏纳

1952-04-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会东北总分会
    抗议奥地利政府迫害和平战士布兰德魏纳
    【新华社沈阳二十三日电】中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会东北总分会，为抗议奥地利政府迫害和平战士布兰德魏纳一事，发表声明称：奥地利政府在美国政府的怂恿下，迫害国际民主法律工作者协会调查团团长布兰德魏纳教授，东北人民对此表示无比愤慨。美国侵略者在朝鲜和我国东北使用细菌武器的灭绝人性的罪行，经国际法学家和我国科学家实地调查加以证实后，真相业已大白于天下。全世界爱好和平、主张人道的人士无不义愤万分，群起声讨人类公敌的美国细菌战犯。妄图一手掩盖天下人耳目的美国政府，在其阴谋败露之余，竟公然采取流氓手段，怂恿奥地利政府迫害公正的和平战士布兰德魏纳教授，适足以证明其做贼心虚和下流无耻。站在反细菌战最前线的东北人民，一致声援布兰德魏纳教授的正义斗争，严重抗议美国政府指使奥地利政府迫害和平战士的罪行。
