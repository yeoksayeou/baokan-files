    Our Great Motherland
    The Lufengosaurus was a complete fossil of the earliest dinosaur that lived in China about 150 million years ago. It was discovered in Lufeng County, Yunnan Province in 1938. Its discovery is a major contribution of Chinese geologists to paleontology. This is the fossil of Lufengosaurus, 2.5 meters high and 6 meters long, now stored in the Institute of Geology, Chinese Academy of Sciences in Nanjing.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-04-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    禄丰龙是一九三八年在云南禄丰县被发掘出来的距今约一亿五千万年前在我国生存的最早的恐龙的完整化石。它的发现是中国地质学家对古生物学的一个重大贡献。这是禄丰龙的化石，高二公尺半，长六公尺，现存南京中国科学院地质研究所。
