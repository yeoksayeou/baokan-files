Author:
Date: 1952-04-07
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The Northeast Institute of Scientific Research successfully trial-produced a vertical magnetic prospecting instrument in November of last year. After field testing, the instrument's performance was excellent, capable of surveying the location and reserves of magnetic minerals, petroleum, and copper ore. Mass production has begun this year. This is a scene of the staff of the Northeast Institute of Scientific Research preparing the vertical magnetic prospecting instrument.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    东北科学研究所在去年十一月试制垂直磁力探矿仪，已经获得成功。经实地试验，这个仪器的性能良好，能勘测出带磁性的矿物、石油和铜矿的地位和藏量。今年已开始大量生产。这是东北科学研究所的工作人员正在配制垂直磁力探矿仪的情形。
