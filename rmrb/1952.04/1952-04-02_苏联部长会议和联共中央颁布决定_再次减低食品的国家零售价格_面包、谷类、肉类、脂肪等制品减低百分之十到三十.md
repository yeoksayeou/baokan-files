Title: The Soviet Council of Ministers and the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks) Issue Decision to Further Reduce State Retail Prices of Food - Prices of Bread, Cereals, Meat, Fats, and Other Products Reduced by Ten to Thirty Percent
Author: 
Date: 1952-04-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 31st (TASS): The Soviet Council of Ministers and the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks) have recently issued a decision on further reducing state retail prices of food (note that this price reduction is the fifth since 1947). The decision states: In view of the achievements of industrial and agricultural production in 1951, the increase in labor productivity, and the reduction of production costs, the Soviet government and the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks) deem it possible to reduce the state retail prices of general food products from April 1st.

The decision then details the price reductions for various foods. Among them, bread, flour, and macaroni products are reduced by twelve to twenty percent; cereals, rice, legumes, and concentrated foods are reduced by ten to twenty percent; rye, wheat, barley, oats, and other grains and feed are reduced by twelve to fifteen percent; meat and meat products are reduced by fifteen to twenty percent; fats, cheeses and dairy products, sweets, tea, coffee, and other food sundries are reduced by ten to thirty percent; fruits are reduced by twenty percent; and canned vegetables and canned fruits are reduced by ten to twenty percent.

In addition, prices in restaurants, canteens, and other public catering establishments will be reduced accordingly; retail prices of textbooks and other books will be reduced by an average of eighteen percent; and hotel rents will be reduced by an average of fifteen percent.

### 苏联部长会议和联共中央颁布决定  再次减低食品的国家零售价格  面包、谷类、肉类、脂肪等制品减低百分之十到三十

1952-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联部长会议和联共中央颁布决定
    再次减低食品的国家零售价格
    面包、谷类、肉类、脂肪等制品减低百分之十到三十
    【新华社一日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科三月三十一日讯：苏联部长会议和苏联共产党（布）中央委员会，顷颁布关于再次减低食品国家零售价格的决定（按这次减价是一九四七年以来的第五次减价）。决定说：鉴于一九五一年工农业生产的成就，劳动生产率的提高和生产成本的降低，苏联政府和联共中央认为有可能自四月一日起减低一般食品的国家零售价格。
    决定接着详细开列了各种食品的减价情形。其中面包、面粉和通心粉制品减低百分之十二到二十；谷类、大米、荚豆和浓缩食品减低百分之十到二十；裸麦、小麦、大麦、燕麦及其他粮食和饲料减低百分之十二到十五；肉类和肉制品减低百分之十五到二十；脂肪、乾酪和乳制品、糖果、茶叶、咖啡及其他食品什货减低百分之十到三十；水果减低百分之二十；罐头蔬菜和罐头水果减低百分之十到二十。
    此外，各饭店、食堂及其他公共饮食店的价目也相应减低；教科书及其他书籍的零售价格平均减低百分之十八；旅馆租金平均减低百分之十五。
