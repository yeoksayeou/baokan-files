Title: Premier Zhou Receives the Hungarian National People's Art Troupe; Hungarian Art Troupe Holds First Musical and Dance Performance in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-04-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 3rd: Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Zhou Enlai, held a reception on the evening of the 3rd to host all members of the Hungarian People's Republic National People's Art Troupe, who were invited to perform in our country.

Invited to attend were Chen Shutong, Huang Yanpei, Li Fuchun, Ma Yinchu, Liu Lantao, Lu Dingyi, Zhang Hanfu, Wu Xiuquan, and many well-known figures from the fields of literature, film, fine arts, music, drama, dance, and opera, as well as Korean artists working in Beijing. Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, Hungarian Ambassador Szaffrankó, and embassy staff from both countries were also invited.

At the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai delivered a welcoming speech, saying:

Comrades of the Hungarian People's Republic National People's Art Troupe, your troupe's invitation to perform in our country will help promote mutual understanding and cultural exchange between the people of China and Hungary. On behalf of the people and government of the People's Republic of China, I extend a warm welcome.

Let us raise a glass to strengthening the friendly relations between the people of China and Hungary, and to strengthening the common struggle of the people of China and Hungary against imperialist aggression in the cause of world peace!

Let us raise a glass to the health of Comrade Rákosi!

Let us raise a glass to the health of Comrade Mao Zedong!

Let us raise a glass to the health of Comrade Stalin!

The head of the Hungarian National People's Art Troupe, Szellessy György, gave a reply. He expressed his gratitude for the warm welcome the art troupe received in the People's Republic of China.

Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, in his speech, wished the Hungarian people's artists great success in their performances on the land of the great Chinese people.

The reception was filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere. Everyone present toasted and cheered for the health of Stalin, Mao Zedong, and Rákosi.

Xinhua News Agency, April 3rd: The Hungarian People's Republic National People's Art Troupe held a musical and dance performance at Huairen Hall in Beijing on the evening of the 3rd. The performance was hosted by the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government.

Attending the performance were Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, and Li Jishen; Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhou Enlai; Vice Premiers Chen Yun and Huang Yanpei; Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Chen Shutong; President of the Supreme People's Court Shen Junru; Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Luo Ronghuan; Acting Chief of General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Nie Rongzhen; Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government Zhang Hanfu and Wu Xiuquan; members of the Central People's Government Committee and the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in Beijing; heads of various departments of the Central People's Government; senior officers of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; heads of various democratic parties and people's organizations; and more than 500 well-known writers, musicians, dancers, artists, and actors.

Foreign guests attending the performance included: Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic Koenig, Mongolian Ambassador Jalkhasaihan, Hungarian Ambassador Szaffrankó, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Korean Ambassador Kwon O-jik, Czechoslovak Ambassador Konsal, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy Isha Mahdi, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy Dodin, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Delegation Zhou Liang, as well as personnel from the Romanian, Indian, and Pakistani embassies in China.

Korean dancer Choi Seung-hee and Chilean painter José Venturelli and his wife also attended the performance.

The performance began at 7:30 p.m. Sha Ke Fu, representative of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, delivered a welcoming speech, saying: This performance by the Hungarian National People's Art Troupe in our country, like the performance of our Youth Art Troupe in Hungary last year, will further consolidate and develop the friendship between the people of the two countries through the excellent artistic performances and mutual learning and influence. Each of our mutual achievements and influences is a contribution to the cause of safeguarding world peace. Our mutual learning and encouragement are a blow to the warmongers. Our goal is one, our cause is common, let us join hands more closely and move forward together.

The head of the Hungarian National People's Art Troupe, Szellessy György, then gave a reply, saying: Although our country is small and our people are few, we are strong because we have the leadership of the Working People's Party and Rákosi, and we have such friends as the great Stalin and the great Mao Zedong. Our art troupe is proud and happy to be guests of the heroic Chinese people and to have the opportunity to learn about the labor creations and excellent culture of the Chinese people. We use today's first performance in China to express the highest respect of the Hungarian working people and Hungarian artists to the Chinese people.

The art troupe performed a total of twelve programs today. These programs fully demonstrated the excellent traditions of Hungarian folk art. Each program won enthusiastic applause from the audience.

### 周总理招待匈牙利国家人民文工团  匈文工团昨在京举行首次音乐舞蹈表演会

1952-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理招待匈牙利国家人民文工团
    匈文工团昨在京举行首次音乐舞蹈表演会
    【新华社三日讯】中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来，在三日夜举行招待会，招待应邀来我国表演的匈牙利人民共和国国家人民文工团全体人员。
    应邀作陪的有陈叔通、黄炎培、李富春、马寅初、刘澜涛、陆定一、章汉夫、伍修权，以及文学、电影、美术、音乐、戏剧、舞蹈、戏曲界著名人士和朝鲜在北京的艺术工作者多人。苏联大使罗申、匈牙利大使夏法朗柯和两国使馆人员也应邀作陪。
    在招待会上，周恩来总理致欢迎辞说：
    匈牙利人民共和国国家人民文工团的同志们，贵团应邀来我国表演，是有助于促进中、匈两国人民间相互了解和文化交流的，我谨代表中华人民共和国的人民和政府表示热烈的欢迎。
    让我们为加强中匈两国人民间的友好关系，为加强中匈两国人民在世界和平事业中共同反对帝国主义侵略的斗争而干杯！
    让我们为拉科西同志的健康而干杯！
    让我们为毛泽东同志的健康而干杯！
    让我们为斯大林同志的健康而干杯！
    匈牙利国家人民文工团团长歇勒西·纠腊作了答辞。他对文工团在中华人民共和国受到热诚欢迎表示感谢。
    苏联大使罗申在致辞中祝匈牙利人民艺术家在伟大的中国人民的土地上的表演获得伟大的成就。
    招待会充满热烈、友好的气氛。每个到会者，都为斯大林、毛泽东、拉科西的健康而干杯，而欢呼。
    【新华社三日讯】匈牙利人民共和国国家人民文工团于三日晚在北京怀仁堂举行了音乐舞蹈表演会。主持这次演出的是中央人民政府文化部。
    出席这次表演会的，有中央人民政府副主席朱德、刘少奇、李济深，政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来，副总理陈云、黄炎培，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈叔通，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，最高人民检察署检察长罗荣桓，人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻，中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫、伍修权，以及中央人民政府委员会和中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会在京委员，中央人民政府各部门首长，人民革命军事委员会高级军官，各民主党派、人民团体负责人，和著名的作家、音乐家、舞蹈家、美术家、演员等五百余人。
    出席这次表演会的外宾有：苏联大使罗申、德意志民主共和国外交使团团长柯尼希、蒙古大使贾尔卡赛汗、匈牙利大使夏法朗柯、保加利亚大使彼得科夫、朝鲜大使权五稷、捷克斯洛伐克大使康萨拉、印度尼西亚大使馆临时代办伊沙·玛蒂、波兰大使馆临时代办道定、越南代表团临时代办周亮，以及罗马尼亚、印度和巴基斯坦驻华使馆的人员等。
    出席这次表演会的还有朝鲜舞蹈家崔承喜和智利画家何塞·万徒勒里夫妇。
    下午七时半，表演会开始。中央人民政府文化部代表沙可夫首先致欢迎词。他说：这次匈牙利国家人民文工团来我国表演，正如去年我国青年文工团去匈牙利表演一样，通过两国人民的优秀艺术表演，和相互的学习和影响，将使两国人民的友谊更加巩固和发展。我们相互的每一种成就和影响，都是对于保卫世界和平事业的贡献。我们相互间的学习和鼓励，就是给予战争贩子们的打击。我们的目标是一个，我们的事业是共同的，让我们更加紧密地携起手来，齐步前进。
    匈牙利国家人民文工团团长歇勒西·纠腊接着致答词说：我们的国家虽小，我国的人民为数虽少，但我们是有力量的，因为我们有劳动人民党和拉科西的领导，我们有伟大的斯大林和伟大的毛泽东这样的朋友。我们文工团以能够作英勇的中国人民的客人，能够有机会认识到中国人民的劳动创造和优秀文化而感到骄傲和幸福。我们用今天的在中国的第一次演出，来表示匈牙利劳动人民、匈牙利艺术家对中国人民的最崇高的敬意。
    文工团今天共演出了十二个节目。这些节目充分表现了匈牙利民间艺术的优良传统。每一个节目，都博得了到会者的热烈掌声。
