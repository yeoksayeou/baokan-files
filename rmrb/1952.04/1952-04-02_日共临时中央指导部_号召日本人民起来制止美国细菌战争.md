Title: Japanese Communist Party Provisional Central Guidance Department Calls on Japanese People to Rise Up and Stop American Bacteriological Warfare
Author:
Date: 1952-04-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 1st. News: The Provisional Central Guidance Department of the Japanese Communist Party issued a solemn statement protesting the use of bacteriological weapons by the American aggressors in Korea, and calling on the Japanese people to unite and demand an end to this inhuman bacteriological warfare.

The statement pointed out: The use of bacteriological weapons is the most despicable and inhuman act, whether from a humanitarian or moral point of view. The statement called on the Japanese people to take action to ensure the immediate withdrawal of American interventionist troops from Korea and the rapid success of the Korean armistice negotiations. The statement demanded an immediate end to bacteriological warfare; the abolition of the separate peace treaty and the "Security Treaty"; and demanded the immediate resignation of the Yoshida traitorous government, which is an accomplice to the American war of aggression against Korea.

### 日共临时中央指导部  号召日本人民起来制止美国细菌战争

1952-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

    日共临时中央指导部
    号召日本人民起来制止美国细菌战争
    【新华社一日讯】东京消息：日本共产党临时中央指导部发表严正声明，抗议美国侵略者在朝鲜使用细菌武器，并号召日本人民一致起来，要求制止这种不人道的细菌战。
    声明指出：使用细菌武器，不论从人道上或道德上来说，都是最卑鄙和最不人道的行为。声明号召日本人民为使美国干涉军立即撤出朝鲜和朝鲜停战谈判迅速成功而行动起来。声明要求立即停止细菌战；废除单独和约和“安全条约”；并要求美国进行侵朝战争的帮凶吉田卖国政府立即辞职。
