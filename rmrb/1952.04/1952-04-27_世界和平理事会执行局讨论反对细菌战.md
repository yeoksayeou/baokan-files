Title: World Peace Council Executive Bureau Discusses Opposition to Germ Warfare
Author:
Date: 1952-04-27
Page: 4

    World Peace Council Executive Bureau Discusses Opposition to Germ Warfare
    The Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council held a meeting in Oslo, the capital of Norway, from March 29 to April 1 this year, discussing the issue of opposing US imperialism's germ warfare in Korea and China. The meeting adopted a letter to men and women all over the world entitled "Against Germ Warfare," calling on people all over the world to rise up and stop the crimes of the American aggressors in carrying out germ warfare. (Attached picture)
    The above picture shows Guo Moruo, the representative of China and Vice-Chairman of the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council, giving a report on the US's germ warfare at the meeting on March 29. Sitting beside him is Qian Sanqiang, the representative of China and member of the China Peace Committee.
    The picture below shows a part of the venue during the discussion of the agenda against germ warfare. From left to right in the picture are Guo Moruo, the Chinese representative and Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council, Lafitte, the French representative and Secretary-General of the World Peace Council, Professor Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the World Peace Council, and Mrs. Gordon, Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council, who attended the meeting of the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council.

### 世界和平理事会执行局讨论反对细菌战

1952-04-27
第4版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会执行局讨论反对细菌战
    世界和平理事会执行局今年三月二十九日至四月一日在挪威首都奥斯陆举行的会议，讨论了反对美帝国主义在朝鲜和中国进行细菌战的问题。会议通过了以“反细菌战”为题的告全世界男女书，号召全世界人民起来制止美国侵略者进行细菌战的罪行。（附图片）
    上图为我国代表、世界和平理事会执行局副主席郭沫若三月二十九日在会上作关于美国进行细菌战的报告。旁坐者为我国代表、中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会委员钱三强。
    下图为讨论反对细菌战议程时会场的一部分。图中自左至右，为参加世界和平理事会执行局会议的中国代表、世界和平理事会副主席郭沫若，法国代表、世界和平理事会秘书长拉斐德，世界和平理事会主席约里奥—居里教授，世界和平理事会副主席戈登夫人。
    （叶华摄影）
