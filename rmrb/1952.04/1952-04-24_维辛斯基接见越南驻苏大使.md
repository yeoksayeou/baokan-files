Title: Vyshinsky Receives Vietnamese Ambassador to the Soviet Union
Author: 
Date: 1952-04-24
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 22nd: TASS Moscow, April 22nd - Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky received Nguyen Luong Bang, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Soviet Union, on the 22nd to discuss matters relating to the presentation of his credentials to the Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.

### 维辛斯基接见越南驻苏大使

1952-04-24
第4版()
专栏：

    维辛斯基接见越南驻苏大使
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十二日讯：苏联外交部长维辛斯基在二十二日接见越南民主共和国驻苏联特命全权大使阮良朋，商谈向苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席呈递国书事宜。
