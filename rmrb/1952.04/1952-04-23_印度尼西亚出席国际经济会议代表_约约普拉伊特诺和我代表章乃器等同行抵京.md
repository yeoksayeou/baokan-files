Title: Indonesian Representatives Attending International Economic Conference, Jojopranoto, and Our Representatives Zhang Naiqi and Others Arrive in Beijing Together
Author:
Date: 1952-04-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd - Jojopranoto (Member of Parliament, trade union worker), the Indonesian representative attending the Moscow International Economic Conference, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 22nd for a visit. Accompanying him were members of our country's delegation to the International Economic Conference, including Zhang Naiqi, Chen Weiji, Li Zhuchen, Sheng Pihua, Wu Yunshu, Fan Hong, and Di Chaobai. Ji Chao Ding, a member of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, welcomed them at the airport.

### 印度尼西亚出席国际经济会议代表  约约普拉伊特诺和我代表章乃器等同行抵京

1952-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚出席国际经济会议代表
    约约普拉伊特诺和我代表章乃器等同行抵京
    【新华社二十二日讯】出席莫斯科国际经济会议的印度尼西亚代表约约普拉伊特诺（国会议员、工会工作者），在二十二日下午搭乘飞机到达北京参观。同行的有我国出席国际经济会议的代表团团员章乃器、陈维稷、李烛尘、盛丕华、吴蕴初、樊弘、狄超白等。到机场欢迎的有国际贸易促进委员会委员冀朝鼎。
