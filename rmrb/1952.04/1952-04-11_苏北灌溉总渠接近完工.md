Title: The Northern Jiangsu General Irrigation Canal Nears Completion
Author:
Date: 1952-04-11
Page: 2
	
	The Northern Jiangsu General Irrigation Canal project is now nearing completion. In the four months since construction began, over 60 million cubic meters of earthwork has been completed, accounting for 88% of the total earthwork.
	According to the decision of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government, the earthwork for the Northern Jiangsu General Irrigation Canal and the Gaoliangjian and Canal East sluices must be basically completed this year. To this end, the Northern Jiangsu Engineering Headquarters of the Huai River Harnessing Committee has expanded construction this spring and accelerated the work. The Yancheng Special District, with its 320,000 workers, has made rapid progress and has now completed all of its construction tasks. This Northern Jiangsu General Irrigation Canal, which stretches 170 kilometers from Gaoliangjian on the east bank of Hongze Lake to Biandang Port on the coast of the Yellow Sea, and the two sluices are expected to be completed by the end of April this year.
	(Xinhua News Agency)

### 苏北灌溉总渠接近完工

1952-04-11
第2版()
专栏：

    苏北灌溉总渠接近完工
    苏北灌溉总渠工程现已接近完成。开工四个月来，全部工程的土工已完成了六千多万公方，占土工总数字的百分之八十八。
    根据中央人民政府政务院的决定，苏北灌溉总干渠土方工程和高良涧，运（河）东两水闸都必须保证在本年基本上完工。为此，治淮委员会苏北工程指挥部今春特扩大施工，加紧赶修。工程进展较快的盐城专区三十二万民工，现在已经全部完成了他们的施工任务。这条西起洪泽湖东岸高良涧，东到黄海滨扁担港，全长一百七十公里的苏北灌溉总渠和两个水闸，预计今年四月底就可全部修成。
    （新华社）
