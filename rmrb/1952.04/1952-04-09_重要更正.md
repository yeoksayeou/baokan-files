    Important Correction
    The "Songs" editorial department of the Arts Administration Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government wrote: "Regarding the song 'Eliminate Germ Warfare' (lyrics by Guo Moruo, music by Lü Ji), published on page three of the People's Daily on April 5th, we hereby correct the 35th measure, which was an error when we sent out the manuscript. We sincerely apologize to the author and readers!"
