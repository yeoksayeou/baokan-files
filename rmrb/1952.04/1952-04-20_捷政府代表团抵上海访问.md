Title: Czechoslovakian Government Delegation Arrives in Shanghai for Visit
Author:
Date: 1952-04-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, April 19th: The Czechoslovakian government delegation, consisting of eight members led by the head of the delegation, Mr. Kopecki, the Minister of Propaganda and Culture of Czechoslovakia, arrived in Shanghai on the morning of the 18th at 9:00 AM by train from Beijing to negotiate and sign agreements on cultural cooperation, scientific and technological cooperation, and postal and telecommunications between China and Czechoslovakia. Those who welcomed them at the station included: Wu Kejian, Secretary-General of the East China Military and Administrative Committee, Chen Wangdao, Minister of the Department of Culture, Pan Hannian, Deputy Mayor of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government, and many others. Mr. and Mrs. Sliemak, the Czechoslovakian Consul-General in Shanghai, also welcomed them at the station.

### 捷政府代表团抵上海访问

1952-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    捷政府代表团抵上海访问
    【新华社上海十九日电】来我国谈判并签订中捷文化合作、科学与技术合作、邮电等协定的捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团一行八人，由团长、捷克斯洛伐克宣传与文化部部长柯别茨基率领，在十八日上午九时自北京乘车抵达上海访问。到车站欢迎的有：华东军政委员会秘书长吴克坚、文化部部长陈望道，上海市人民政府副市长潘汉年等多人。捷克斯洛伐克驻上海总领事斯立马克夫妇也到车站欢迎。
