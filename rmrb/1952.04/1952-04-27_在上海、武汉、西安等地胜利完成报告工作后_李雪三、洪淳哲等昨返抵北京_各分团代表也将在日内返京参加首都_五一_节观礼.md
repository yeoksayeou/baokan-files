Title: After Successfully Completing Report Work in Shanghai, Wuhan, Xi'an and Other Places, Li Xuesan, Hong Chunzhe and Others Returned to Beijing Yesterday; Representatives of Various Sub-Delegations Will Also Return to Beijing in the Coming Days to Participate in the "May Day" Observation Ceremony in the Capital
Author:
Date: 1952-04-27
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 26th: After successfully completing report work in Shanghai, Nanjing, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Changsha, Wuhan, Xi'an, Qingdao, Jinan and other places, Li Xuesan, head of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Homecoming Delegation, and Hong Chunzhe, head of the Korean People's Delegation to China, and a party of seven, returned to Beijing on the afternoon of the 26th. Those who went to the station to welcome them included: Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Resist America, Aid Korea General Association, Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General, Wu Maosun, Deputy Secretary-General, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, representatives of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission and the People's Liberation Army Land, Sea, Air, Armored, and Artillery Forces, and more than 70 other people.
    Representatives of various sub-delegations of the Volunteer Army Homecoming Delegation and the Korean People's Delegation to China will also return to Beijing one after another in the coming days to participate in the "May Day" observation ceremony in the capital.

### 在上海、武汉、西安等地胜利完成报告工作后  李雪三、洪淳哲等昨返抵北京  各分团代表也将在日内返京参加首都“五一”节观礼

1952-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    在上海、武汉、西安等地胜利完成报告工作后
    李雪三、洪淳哲等昨返抵北京
    各分团代表也将在日内返京参加首都“五一”节观礼
    【新华社二十六日讯】中国人民志愿军归国代表团李雪三团长和朝鲜人民访华代表团洪淳哲团长等一行七人，在上海、南京、杭州、广州、长沙、武汉、西安、青岛、济南等地胜利完成报告工作后，于二十六日下午返抵北京。前往车站欢迎的有：中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席陈叔通、秘书长刘贯一、副秘书长吴茂荪、北京市人民政府副市长吴晗、人民革命军事委员会及人民解放军陆、海、空、装甲、炮兵部队代表等七十余人。
    志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团各分团代表也将在日内陆续返京，参加首都“五一”节观礼。
