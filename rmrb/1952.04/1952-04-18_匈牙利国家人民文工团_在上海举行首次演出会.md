Title: Hungarian State Folk Ensemble Holds First Performance in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1952-04-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 17th—The Hungarian State Folk Ensemble held its first performance at the Shanghai Municipal People's Government Auditorium on the evening of the 16th. Among those attending the performance were Zeng Shan, Vice Chairman of the East China Military and Political Committee, Pan Hannian, Deputy Mayor of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government, heads of various government agencies in East China and Shanghai, heads of people's organizations in East China and Shanghai, and more than 2,000 representatives from all walks of life. Diplomatic officials from the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, India and other countries stationed in Shanghai, as well as the Hungarian Ambassador to China, Xia Falan Ke, and his wife, who accompanied the ensemble to Shanghai, also attended the performance. Before the performance, a grand welcoming ceremony was held under the auspices of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government's Bureau of Culture. During the performance, each program received warm applause and cheers. After the performance, representatives of the Shanghai Federation of Literary and Art Circles presented a flag to the Hungarian State Folk Ensemble as a sign of respect.

### 匈牙利国家人民文工团  在上海举行首次演出会

1952-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

    匈牙利国家人民文工团
    在上海举行首次演出会
    【新华社上海十七日电】匈牙利国家人民文工团十六日晚在上海市人民政府大礼堂举行首次演出会。出席演出会的有华东军政委员会副主席曾山、上海市人民政府副市长潘汉年和华东及上海各政府机关首长，华东及上海各人民团体负责人，以及各界人民代表等二千多人。苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、印度等国驻上海外交官员及陪同文工团来上海的匈牙利驻华大使夏法朗柯及其夫人也出席了演出会。节目上演以前，在上海市人民政府文化局主持下举行了一个隆重的欢迎仪式。在演出过程中，每一个节目都得到了热烈的掌声和欢呼。表演结束后，上海市文学艺术界联合会的代表曾向匈牙利国家人民文工团献旗致敬。
