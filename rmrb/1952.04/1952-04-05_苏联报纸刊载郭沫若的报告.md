Title: Soviet Newspapers Publish Guo Moruo's Report
Author:
Date: 1952-04-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 3rd: The Soviet newspapers *Pravda* and *Izvestia* published in full on the 1st the report by Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council and head of the Chinese delegation, to the Executive Bureau meeting of the World Peace Council, regarding the crimes of bacteriological warfare by American imperialism.
