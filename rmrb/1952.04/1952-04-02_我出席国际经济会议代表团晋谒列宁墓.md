Title: Delegation of Chinese Attendees to International Economic Conference Pays Respects at Lenin Mausoleum
Author:
Date: 1952-04-02
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 1st. The delegation from China attending the International Economic Conference, led by head of delegation Nan Hanchen, paid respects at the Lenin Mausoleum on March 30th and laid a wreath to pay their respects.

### 我出席国际经济会议代表团晋谒列宁墓

1952-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

    我出席国际经济会议代表团晋谒列宁墓
    【新华社莫斯科一日电】我国参加国际经济会议代表团在三月三十日由团长南汉宸率领晋谒列宁墓，并在墓前献花圈致敬。
