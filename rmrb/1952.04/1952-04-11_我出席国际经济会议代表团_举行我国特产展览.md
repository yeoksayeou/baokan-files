Title: Our Delegation to the International Economic Conference Holds Exhibition of Chinese Products
Author: 
Date: 1952-04-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th. On the eve of the opening of the International Economic Conference, the Chinese delegation arranged an exhibition hall in the Moscow Soviet Hotel to display more than 600 kinds of Chinese products. The exhibits include: 1. Tea; 2. Native products: industrial raw materials, medicinal materials, food, handicrafts; 3. Animal products: pig bristles, silk, feathers, leather, carpets; 4. General merchandise: cotton fabrics, knitwear, embroidery, enamelware, stationery; 5. Special handicrafts: ivory carvings, cloisonné, carved lacquerware, jewelry, ironwork, etc. In the past few days, delegates from various countries attending the International Economic Conference have been visiting in an endless stream and have expressed great interest.

### 我出席国际经济会议代表团  举行我国特产展览

1952-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我出席国际经济会议代表团  
    举行我国特产展览
   【新华社莫斯科十日电】在国际经济会议开幕前夕，我国代表团在莫斯科苏维埃旅馆布置了一个展览厅，陈列我国特产六百多件。陈列品包括一、茶叶；二、土产：工业原料、药材、食品、手工业品；三、畜产：猪鬃、丝、羽毛、皮、地毯；四、百货：棉织品、针织品、刺绣、搪瓷、文具；五、特种手工艺品：象牙雕刻、景泰蓝、雕漆、首饰、铁花等等。几天来参加国际经济会议的各国代表们络绎不绝地前往参观，并表示了很大的兴趣。
