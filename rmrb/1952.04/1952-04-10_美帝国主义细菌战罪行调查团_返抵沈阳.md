Title: "Investigation Team on the Crimes of Bacteriological Warfare by U.S. Imperialism" Returns to Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1952-04-10
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 9th. The "Investigation Team on the Crimes of Bacteriological Warfare by U.S. Imperialism" returning from Korea, led by head of the team Li Dequan and deputy head Liao Chengzhi, arrived in Shenyang on the morning of the 9th. Wang Bin, Minister of Health of the Northeast People's Government, Vice Minister Bai Xiqing, Li Guisen, Director of the Reception Office of the Northeast People's Government, and others welcomed them at the station.
    All members of the investigation team will return to Beijing by train on the 10th.

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 9th. The "Investigation Team on the Crimes of Bacteriological Warfare by U.S. Imperialism," led by head of the team Li Dequan and deputy head Liao Chengzhi, conducted on-the-spot investigations in the Korean front and rear for more than half a month, and obtained full and conclusive evidence to prove that U.S. imperialism was conducting large-scale bacteriological warfare. After successfully completing its mission, the team left Pyongyang on the 7th to return to China.
    When the team left Pyongyang, Park Jung-ai, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party; Kim Chun-hae, Minister of Social Affairs; Li Byung-nam, Minister of Public Health of the Republic; Baek Nam-un, Minister of Education; Li Dong-geon, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Lu Jin-han, Deputy Minister of Public Health; Ryu Ki-chun; Ki Seok-bok, Deputy Minister of Culture and Propaganda, and other party and government leaders; Hyun Heon, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Vocational Federation; Kim Ki-soo, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Democratic Youth League, and other heads of mass organizations; Gan Yetao, Chargé d'affaires of our embassy in Korea, and representatives from all walks of life in Pyongyang went to see them off. Lu Jin-han, Deputy Minister of Public Health of the Republic, personally escorted the team to the Sino-Korean border.

### “美帝国主义细菌战罪行调查团”返抵沈阳

1952-04-10
第4版()
专栏：

    “美帝国主义细菌战罪行调查团”返抵沈阳
    【新华社沈阳九日电】由朝鲜返国的“美帝国主义细菌战罪行调查团”，在团长李德全、副团长廖承志率领下，已在九日上午到达沈阳。东北人民政府卫生部部长王斌、副部长白希清，东北人民政府交际处处长李桂森等前往车站欢迎。
    调查团全体人员将在十日乘车返回北京。
    【新华社平壤九日电】“美帝国主义细菌战罪行调查团”在团长李德全、副团长廖承志率领下，在朝鲜前线和后方进行了半个多月的实地调查，获得了证实美帝国主义大规模进行细菌战的充分的确凿的证据。该团在胜利完成任务之后，已在七日离平壤回国。
    该团离平壤时，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会书记朴正爱；社会部长金千海；共和国政府保健相李炳南、教育相白南云、外务省副相李东建、保健省副相卢镇汉、柳基春、文化宣传省副相奇石福等党政首长；朝鲜职业总同盟中央委员会委员长玄勋、民主青年同盟中央委员会副委员长金基洙等群众团体负责人，和我国驻朝大使馆代办甘野陶，以及平壤军民各界代表都曾前往欢送。共和国保健省副相卢镇汉并亲自送该团到中朝国境。
