Title: British Communist Party's 22nd Congress Passes Emergency Resolution Protesting U.S. Germ Warfare
Author: 
Date: 1952-04-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 19th -- The 22nd Congress of the British Communist Party passed an emergency resolution protesting the U.S. aggressors' germ warfare in Korea and China. The full text of the resolution is as follows:
    "This Congress protests against the act of American aircraft dropping insects carrying bacteria in Korea and China.
    This Congress hails the vigilance and heroism displayed by the people of Korea and China in fighting this despicable and inhumane war.
    The Congress demands that the news blockade and official denials be smashed, because these prevent the British people from knowing the truth, which they would immediately oppose if they knew it.
    The Congress believes that the invitation by the Chinese People's Committee for Defending World Peace and Opposing American Aggression to conduct an authoritative and impartial investigation is welcome.
    The Congress further protests against the use of chemical poisons to destroy crops in Malaya. It is a national disgrace that the British army is the first to openly wage this kind of chemical warfare.
    The use of bacteriological weapons, chemical weapons, and other biological weapons is a crime against the people and a complete abandonment of the standards and principles of civilization.
    Such behavior is an extreme corruption of medicine and science.
    The Congress calls for the launch of the broadest protest movement to stop this barbaric method of warfare."

### 英共第二十二届代表大会  通过紧急决议抗议美国进行细菌战

1952-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    英共第二十二届代表大会
    通过紧急决议抗议美国进行细菌战
    【新华社布拉格十九日电】英国共产党第二十二届代表大会通过了抗议美国侵略者在朝鲜和中国进行细菌战的紧急决议，决议全文如下：
    “本代表大会对于美国飞机在朝鲜和中国投掷带有细菌的昆虫的行为表示抗议。
    本代表大会对于朝、中两国人民跟这种卑鄙不人道的战争作斗争时所表现的警觉性和英雄主义，表示欢呼。
    大会要求粉碎新闻封锁，粉碎官方的否认，因为这使英国人民不能知道事实的真相，否则他们是会马上表示反对的。
    大会认为，中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会邀请进行权威和公正的调查，是值得欢迎的。
    大会更抗议在马来亚使用化学毒品破坏农作物。首先公然进行这种化学战的，竟是英国军队，这是我们的民族耻辱。
    使用细菌武器、化学武器及其他生物武器，是对人民的罪行，是完全摒弃了文明的标准和原则的。
    这种行为是极端败坏了医学和科学的。
    大会号召发起最广泛的抗议运动，以制止这种野蛮的战争方法。”
