Title: The Chinese People's Struggle Against Germ Warfare Will Surely Be Victorious

Author:
Date: 1952-04-13

Editor's Note: The Northeast Branch of the "Investigative Group on the Crimes of American Imperialist Germ Warfare" has successfully completed its on-site investigation and published the "Investigation Report on the Crimes of American Imperialists Spreading Germs in Northeast China." Here are articles written for this publication by Chen Qiyuan (Director of the Supervisory Committee of the China People's Relief Administration), Shen Qiyi (Plant Pathology Expert, Standing Director of the All-China Science and Technology Popularization Association), Wu Maosun (Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs), and group members Peng Zemin (Vice President of the Red Cross Society of China), Mei Ru'ao (International Law Expert), Zhou Jianren (Biology Expert), and Cheng Shaojiong (Veterinary Medicine Expert).

### 中国人民反对细菌战的斗争一定胜利

1952-04-13
第4版()
专栏：

    中国人民反对细菌战的斗争一定胜利
    编者按：“美帝国主义细菌战罪行调查团”东北分团已经胜利完成了实地调查的工作，并发表了“关于美帝国主义在中国东北地区撒布细菌罪行调查报告书”。这里发表的是该团团长陈其瑗（中国人民救济总会监察委员会主任）、副秘书长沈其益（植物病理学专家、中华全国科学技术普及协会常务理事）、副秘书长吴茂荪（中国人民外交学会秘书长）和团员彭泽民（中国红十字总会副会长）、梅汝璈（国际法学专家）、周建人（生物学专家）、程绍迥（兽医学专家）等为本刊写的文章。
