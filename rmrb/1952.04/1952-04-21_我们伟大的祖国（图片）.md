Author:
Date: 1952-04-21

    Our Great Motherland
    Since the promulgation of the "Labor Insurance Regulations of the People's Republic of China," the welfare of workers in various regions has seen universal development. This is the Dalian Workers' Sanatorium, standing on the most beautiful "Tiger Beach" seaside in Dalian. Backed by mountains and facing the sea, with a beautiful environment, it welcomes many workers for recuperation throughout the year.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    “中华人民共和国劳动保险条例”公布以来，各地工人的福利事业有了普遍的发展。这是矗立在大连市最美丽的“老虎滩”海滨的大连工人休养所。这里背山面海，环境幽美，一年四季不断地迎接着许多工人来休养。
