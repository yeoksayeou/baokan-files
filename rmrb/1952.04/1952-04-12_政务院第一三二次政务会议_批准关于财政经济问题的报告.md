Title: The 132nd Government Administration Council Meeting of the Government Administration Council Approves Report on Financial and Economic Issues
Author:
Date: 1952-04-12
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 11th: The 132nd Government Administration Council meeting of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 11th. The meeting listened to and approved the report on financial and economic issues made by Chen Yun, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council and Director of the Financial and Economic Committee. The meeting also passed several appointment cases and appointment cases to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院第一三二次政务会议  批准关于财政经济问题的报告

1952-04-12
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院第一三二次政务会议
    批准关于财政经济问题的报告
    【新华社十一日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百三十二次政务会议在十一日下午举行。会议听取和批准了政务院副总理兼财政经济委员会主任陈云所作的关于财政经济问题的报告。会议并通过任命案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。
