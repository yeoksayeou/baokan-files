Title: Preparatory Committee for Celebrating "May 1st" Labor Day Established by People from All Walks of Life in the Capital
Author:
Date: 1952-04-25
Page: 1
    People from All Walks of Life in the Capital Form Preparatory Committee to Celebrate "May 1st" Labor Day

    [This newspaper reports] The Preparatory Committee for Celebrating May 1st Labor Day by People from All Walks of Life in the Capital has been established. The office is located in Zhongshan Park. The telephone numbers are (5)○554 or (3)-1321.

### 首都各界人民组成庆祝“五一”节筹委会

1952-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    首都各界人民组成庆祝“五一”节筹委会
    【本报讯】首都各界人民庆祝五一劳动节筹备委员会已成立。该会在中山公园内办公。电话为（五）○五五四或（三）一三二一。
