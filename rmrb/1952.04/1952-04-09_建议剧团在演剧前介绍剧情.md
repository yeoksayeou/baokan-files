Title: Suggesting Theater Troupes Introduce the Plot Before Performances
Author: Fan Xiayun
Date: 1952-04-09
Page: 6

    Letter to the Editor Excerpts:

    Suggesting Theater Troupes Introduce the Plot Before Performances
    In order to enable drama to play a greater role in propaganda and education, I suggest that both new and old theater troupes briefly introduce the content of the drama and the significance of the performance to the audience before performing. This introduction is especially necessary for performances in rural areas. I hope my suggestion will attract the attention of theater troupes.
                                        Pingyuan Weihui Normal School　Fan Xiayun

### 建议剧团在演剧前介绍剧情
范霞云
1952-04-09
第6版()
专栏：来信摘要

    建议剧团在演剧前介绍剧情
    为了使戏剧能起更大的宣传教育作用，我建议新旧剧团在演剧以前，把戏剧的内容和演出的意义，向观众简单地介绍一下。尤其是在农村演出，这种介绍更为必要，希望我的建议能引起剧团的注意。
                                        平原卫辉师范　范霞云
