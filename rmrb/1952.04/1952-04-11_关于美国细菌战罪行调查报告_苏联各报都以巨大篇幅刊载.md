Title: Soviet Newspapers Carry Extensive Coverage of Investigation Report on American Bacteriological Warfare Crimes
Author:
Date: 1952-04-11
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 9th: The "Truth," "News," and "Red Star" newspapers on the 8th carried extensive coverage of the report by the Northeast Branch of the "Investigation Team on American Imperialist Bacteriological Warfare Crimes." The "Truth" newspaper on the 7th had published the statement issued by the International Association of Democratic Lawyers' investigation team at a press conference in Beijing.
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 10th: The "Truth," "News," "Red Star," and "Labor News" newspapers on the 9th carried the report by the International Association of Democratic Lawyers' investigation team on the use of bacteriological weapons by American troops in Northeast China.

### 关于美国细菌战罪行调查报告  苏联各报都以巨大篇幅刊载

1952-04-11
第4版()
专栏：

    关于美国细菌战罪行调查报告
    苏联各报都以巨大篇幅刊载
    【新华社莫斯科九日电】“真理报”、“消息报”、“红星报”八日以巨大篇幅刊载“美帝国主义细菌战罪行调查团”东北分团的报告。“真理报”七日曾刊载国际民主法律工作者协会调查团在北京记者招待会上发表的声明。
    【新华社莫斯科十日电】“真理报”、“消息报”、“红星报”、“劳动报”在九日刊载了国际民主法律工作者调查团关于美国军队在中国东北地区使用细菌武器的报告。
