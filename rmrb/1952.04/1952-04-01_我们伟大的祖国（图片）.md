Our Great Motherland
The staff of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Railway Locomotive Group are working day and night to support the Chinese and Korean People's forces. They overcame the American aerial bandits and gloriously completed the arduous transportation work. This is a heroic portrait of driver Li Yuchun and his comrades, driving the locomotive forward courageously.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国人民志愿援朝铁路机车组的工作人员，日夜奔驰，支援着中朝人民部队。他们战胜了美国空中强盗，光荣地完成了艰巨的运输工作。这是司机长李遇春和他的同伴，驾驰着机车奋勇前进的雄姿。
