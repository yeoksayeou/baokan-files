Title: Korean Armistice Talks Show No Progress
Author:
Date: 1952-04-17
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 16th. On the 16th, during the meeting of the Third Agenda Subcommittee of the Korean Armistice Negotiations, the other side once again stated that they had no new opinions, and the meeting made no progress.

### 朝鲜停战谈判会议无进展

1952-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判会议无进展
    【新华社开城十六日电】十六日朝鲜停战谈判第三项议程小组委员会上，对方再次表示无新意见，会议无进展。
