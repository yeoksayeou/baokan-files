Title: Changdu Area Tibetan Representative Visiting Delegation Arrives in Beijing Yesterday; Xia Ricang Living Buddha of Longwu Temple, Tongren County, Qinghai Province, and his party also arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-04-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 8th: The Changdu Area Tibetan Representative Visiting Delegation, composed of more than 30 people, including Xie Wa La Living Buddha of Changdu Lama Temple in Xikang Province, Kanggu Living Buddha of Chaya Zong (Zong is equivalent to a county), Gongbu, the acting chief officer of the Ninth Generation of the insurgent Tibetan army, and lamas and headmen from various Zongs in Changdu, arrived in Beijing on the 8th. Among those who went to the station to welcome them were members of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government in Beijing and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government.

Xia Ricang Living Buddha of Longwu Temple in Tongren County, Qinghai Province, and his party of seven people also arrived in Beijing on March 24th. All members of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government in Beijing went to the station to welcome them. The visiting delegation and Xia Ricang Living Buddha and others will pay their respects to Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government and visit the great construction projects of the motherland.

### 昌都地区藏族代表参观队昨天到达北京  青海省同仁县隆务寺夏日仓活佛等一行亦已抵京

1952-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

    昌都地区藏族代表参观队昨天到达北京
    青海省同仁县隆务寺夏日仓活佛等一行亦已抵京
    【新华社八日讯】西康省昌都喇嘛寺谢娃拉活佛、察雅宗（宗相当于县）康古活佛、起义藏军第九代本代理主官贡布及昌都各宗的喇嘛、头人等三十多人所组成昌都地区藏族代表赴京参观队，已在八日到达北京。前往车站欢迎的有中央人民政府民族事务委员会在京委员及北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等多人。
    青海省同仁县隆务寺夏日仓活佛等一行七人，也在三月二十四日来到北京。中央人民政府民族事务委员会全体在京委员曾到车站欢迎。参观队和夏日仓活佛等，将向毛主席和中央人民政府致敬，并参观伟大祖国的各项建设。
