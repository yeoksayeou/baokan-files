Title: Nan Hanchen Departs Moscow for Return to China
Author:
Date: 1952-04-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 24th: Nan Hanchen, head of the Chinese delegation to the International Economic Conference, left Moscow by plane this morning, April 24th, to return to Beijing. Nesterov, Chairman of the All-Soviet Chamber of Commerce, was present at the airport to see him off.

### 南汉宸离莫斯科返国

1952-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    南汉宸离莫斯科返国
    【新华社莫斯科二十四日电】我国出席国际经济会议代表团团长南汉宸在二十四日晨乘飞机离莫斯科返北京。全苏商会主席奈斯特洛夫曾到机场送行。
