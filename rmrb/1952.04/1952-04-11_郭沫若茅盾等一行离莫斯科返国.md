Title: Guo Moruo, Mao Dun, and Entourage Leave Moscow for Return to China
Author:
Date: 1952-04-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th—Guo Moruo, Mao Dun, and their entourage, the representatives of our country attending the World Peace Council Executive Bureau meeting, left Moscow by plane on the morning of the 10th to return to China.

### 郭沫若茅盾等一行离莫斯科返国

1952-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若茅盾等一行离莫斯科返国
   【新华社莫斯科十日电】我国出席世界和平理事会执行局会议的代表郭沫若、茅盾等一行，在十日晨乘飞机离莫斯科返国。
