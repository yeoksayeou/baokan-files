Title: Mr. and Mrs. James Endicott Leave Beijing to Return Home

Author:

Date: 1952-04-17
Page: 1

    Mr. and Mrs. James Endicott Leave Beijing to Return Home
    [Xinhua News Agency, 16th] After visiting and touring China for more than two months, James Endicott, Chairman of the Canadian Peace Congress, and his wife, left Beijing by plane on the 16th to return home. Those who saw them off at the airport included Liu Guan-yi, Secretary-General of the China Peace Committee, Meng Yu and Tang Mingzhao, Deputy Directors of the Liaison Department, and Yu Zhiying of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.
    Furthermore, Guo Moruo, Chairman, and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman, of the China Peace Committee, hosted a banquet on April 15th to bid farewell to James Endicott and his wife, and to welcome the famous Chilean painter José Venturelli and his wife. Those present included Mao Dun, Li Dequan, Liao Chengzhi, Wu Maosun, Ding Zan, Gong Peng, Meng Yu, Tang Mingzhao, Lao She, Cao Yu, Jiang Feng, Wu Zuoren, and Li Keran.

### 文幼章夫妇离京返国

1952-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

    文幼章夫妇离京返国
    【新华社十六日讯】加拿大和平大会主席文幼章及其夫人在我国访问参观二月余后，于十六日乘飞机离京返国。到机场欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会秘书长刘贯一、联络部副部长孟雨、唐明照及中华全国总工会俞志英等。
    又中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会郭沫若主席、陈叔通副主席于四月十五日设宴欢送文幼章及其夫人，同时欢迎智利名画家何塞·万徒勒里及其夫人。作陪者有茅盾、李德全、廖承志、吴茂荪、丁瓒、龚澎、孟雨、唐明照、老舍、曹禺、江丰、吴作人、李可染等。
