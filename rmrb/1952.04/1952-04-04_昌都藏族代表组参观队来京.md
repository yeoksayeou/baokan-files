Title: Changdu Tibetan Delegation Arrives in Beijing for Visit
Author:
Date: 1952-04-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 2nd: The Changdu Tibetan delegation from the Xikang Province, consisting of over thirty members (including nineteen official delegates), arrived in Chongqing on March 30th and boarded a ship eastward on April 1st. Leaders of the Southwest Military and Administrative Committee, the Southwest Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and the Southwest Military Region met with the entire delegation on the 31st and accepted their gifts. Deng Xiaoping, Vice Chairman of the Southwest Military and Administrative Committee, and He Long, Commander of the Military Region, expressed their gratitude to the Tibetan people of Changdu for their support of the troops entering Tibet during the peaceful liberation of Tibet when meeting with the delegates, and encouraged the delegates to actively participate in the construction of the Changdu area after visiting Beijing.
The Changdu Tibetan delegation is composed of the Second Living Buddha of Changdu Lamasery, Sewa Lha Hutuktu, Kanggu Living Buddha of Chaya Dzong (i.e., Chaya County), Gongbu, the acting chief officer of the Ninth Generation of the uprising Tibetan army, as well as lamas, chieftains, and people's representatives from various dzongs in Changdu. They will go to Beijing to pay respects to Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government and visit the capital's great construction projects.

### 昌都藏族代表组参观队来京

1952-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    昌都藏族代表组参观队来京
    【新华社重庆二日电】西康省昌都地区藏族代表赴京参观队一行三十多人（其中正式代表十九人）在三月三十日到达重庆，四月一日搭轮东下。西南军政委员会，中共中央西南局及西南军区诸首长曾在三十一日接见该队全体代表，并接受他们的献礼。西南军政委员会副主席邓小平和军区司令员贺龙在接见代表时都表示感谢昌都地区藏族人民在和平解放西藏中对进藏部队的支援，并勉励代表们在赴京参观后积极参加昌都地区的建设。
    昌都地区藏族代表赴京参观队由昌都喇嘛寺第二活佛谢娃拉呼图克图、察雅宗（即察雅县）康古活佛、起义藏军第九代本代理主官贡布及昌都各宗的喇嘛、头人和人民代表所组成。他们到北京向毛主席和中央人民政府致敬，并参观首都的各项伟大建设。
