Title: Japanese "Sanbetsu" and National Relief Society Protest US Germ Warfare
Author:
Date: 1952-04-16
Page: 4

    Japanese "Sanbetsu" and National Relief Society
    Protest US Germ Warfare
    [This newspaper's dispatch] The Chairman of the Japanese Congress of Industrial Unions (abbreviated as "Sanbetsu"), Yoshida Shiji, sent a telegram to the World Peace Council on March 31 regarding the issue of US imperialism conducting germ warfare. The telegram reads as follows:
    "Japanese workers strongly protest the germ warfare being conducted in Korea and demand the punishment of the germ warfare criminals. At the same time, please pay attention to the criminal activities of the war criminal Lieutenant General Ishii, a medical officer during World War II, who is secretly active under the protection of reactionary forces, and demand that strict measures be taken against them."
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 15th] TASS Tokyo, 15th: "The People's Rights Newspaper" reported that the Japan National Relief Society sent letters to Truman and Lie in late March, protesting the US military's use of bacteriological weapons.

### 日本“产别”和国民救援会  抗议美国进行细菌战

1952-04-16
第4版()
专栏：

    日本“产别”和国民救援会
    抗议美国进行细菌战
    【本报讯】日本产业别劳动组合会议（简称“产别”）主席吉田资治于三月三十一日就美帝国主义进行细菌战问题致电世界和平理事会，电文如下：
    “日本工人严重抗议在朝鲜进行的细菌战，要求惩办细菌战犯。同时请注意第二次世界大战时期的战犯石井军医中将等在反动势力庇护下正在暗中活动的罪恶行为，并要求对他们采取严厉的措施。”
    【新华社十五日讯】塔斯社东京十五日讯：“人权民报”消息说：日本国民救援会于三月下旬致函杜鲁门和赖伊，抗议美军使用细菌武器。
