Title: Peking University Hospital Establishes Newspaper Reading System
Author:
Date: 1952-04-12
Page: 2

Response to People's Daily Readers' Criticism and Construction

    Peking University Hospital Establishes Newspaper Reading System

    Editor: The "Reader's Letters" column in the March 22nd People's Daily exposed the fact that staff at Peking University Hospital were neglecting newspaper reading and study. The leadership of Peking University Hospital immediately conducted a deep self-examination and formulated improvement measures. They also mobilized staff to hold discussions and launched criticism and self-criticism regarding the erroneous view of neglecting newspaper reading and study.

    The current improvements are as follows: 1. The number of newspapers subscribed to by the hospital has increased from 25 to 83. 2. Each unit has formed a newspaper reading group, reading newspapers for at least half an hour each day and conducting focused discussions. 3. Trial implementation of periodic current affairs quizzes to supervise and help everyone read newspapers. 4. A designated person is responsible for highlighting the key points of newspaper reading each day and posting newspapers at the entrance and dining hall, etc.

    The hospital has also paid attention to the problem of patients reading and studying newspapers.

    Peking University Medical College Correspondent Group

### 北大医院建立读报制度

1952-04-12
第2版()
专栏：对人民日报读者批评建设的反应

    北大医院建立读报制度
    编辑同志：三月二十二日人民日报“读者来信”栏揭发了北大医院工作人员忽视读报学习的事实，北大医院的领导方面立即作了深刻的检讨，订出了改进办法；并发动工作人员进行讨论，对忽视读报学习的错误观点，展开了批评和自我批评。
    目前的改进情况是：一、院方订阅的报纸已由二十五份增到八十三份；二、各单位都组成了读报小组，每天至少读报半小时，并进行重点讨论；三、试行定期的时事测验，以督促和帮助大家读报；四、由专人负责，每天划出读报重点，并在大门口和饭厅等地张贴报纸。
    院方还注意了病人的读报学习问题。
    北大医学院通讯组
