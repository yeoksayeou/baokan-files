    Economic Briefs
    ▲The Northeast region has achieved another great success in adjusting the power load of the ultra-high-voltage grid. The region has adjusted another 8,000 kilowatts of power, bringing the total to more than 33,000 kilowatts. This has saved the country the equivalent of 40,000 kilowatts of power generation equipment.
    ▲In northern Jiangsu, agricultural implement production cooperatives and agricultural implement production groups attached to supply and marketing cooperatives have been widely organized in various places to manufacture a large number of agricultural implements to supply the vast number of farmers, so as to facilitate the development of the patriotic production increase movement. As of the end of March, the region had organized 77 agricultural implement production cooperatives and 309 agricultural implement production groups, with a total of 3,439 iron and wood handicraft workers and independent producers. They have produced more than 605,000 pieces of 62 kinds of agricultural implements, most of which have been sold to farmers in a timely manner. These agricultural implement production cooperatives and groups have also repaired many old and broken agricultural implements for farmers, powerfully supporting spring ploughing production.

### 经济简讯

1952-04-29
第2版()
专栏：经济简讯

    经济简讯
    ▲东北区超高压网调整电力负荷又获得巨大成绩。全区又调整出八千余瓩的电力，连前共调整出三万三千余瓩的电力，给国家节省了相当于四万瓩电力的发电设备。
    ▲苏北各地普遍组织农具生产合作社和附属于供销合作社的农具生产小组，制造大量农具供应广大农民，以利开展爱国丰产运动。到三月底为止，全区已组织七十七个农具生产合作社和三百零九个农具生产小组，共拥有铁、木手工业工人和独立生产者三千四百三十九人，已生产出大小农具六十二种、六十万五千多件，大多已及时销售给农民。这些农具生产合作社和小组还给农民修理了不少破旧的农具，有力地支援了春耕生产。
