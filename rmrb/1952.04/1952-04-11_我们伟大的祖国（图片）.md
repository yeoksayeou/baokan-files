    Our Great Motherland
    The Shanghai State-owned Yuanshun Steel Plant has achieved great victories in both the anti-corruption struggle and production. The plant's production of ribbed steel bars increased by 24 percent in February compared to January. This is a scene of a large quantity of ribbed steel bars leaving the factory.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    上海国营源顺钢厂在反贪污斗争和生产这两方面同时获得了很大的胜利。该厂竹节钢的生产量，在二月份比一月份增加了百分之二十四。这是竹节钢大量出厂的情形。
