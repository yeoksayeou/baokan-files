Title: Soviet Union Announces List of Delegates to International Economic Conference; Delegations from Poland, the United States, Britain, and Other Countries Have Arrived in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1952-04-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow Dispatch from TASS: Soviet trade, industrial, cooperative, and trade union organizations, as well as scientific and economic institutions, have nominated representatives to form a delegation to attend the International Economic Conference. The representatives include: Nesterov, Chairman of the All-Union Chamber of Commerce; Kuznetsov, Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions; Hogolov, Chairman of the Board of the Central Union of Consumer Cooperatives of the USSR; Polisenko, Vice Chairman of the All-Union Chamber of Commerce; Nichkov, Chairman of the Soviet "Exporter" Organization; Yeremin, Chairman of the Soviet Machine Import Association; Kamensky, Chairman of the Soviet Various Goods Import Association; Osipov, Chairman of the Soviet Grain Export Association; Dashkevich, Chairman of the Soviet Technical Equipment Import Association; Rasin, Chairman of the Soviet Transport Machine Import Association; Alikhanov, Director of the Azerbaijan Coastal Oil Administration; Nishanov, Manager of the Uzbek Republic Cotton Trust; Vlasov, Director of the Moscow Stalin Automobile Factory; Tsvizdina, Director of the "Great Ivanovo" Textile Factory; Chuyev, Director of the Moscow Oblast Office of the Central Automobile and Tractor Sales Administration; Chakis, Director of the Riga Popov Radio Equipment Factory; Orlov, Director of the Scientific Research Institute attached to the Ministry of Foreign Trade; Ostrovityanov, Corresponding Academician of the Soviet Academy of Sciences; Academician Strumilin of the Soviet Academy of Sciences; Academician Varga of the Soviet Academy of Sciences; Academician Gulisashvili of the Georgian Republic Academy of Sciences; Beisenov, Chairman of the Kazakh Republic Trade Union Council; Muravyeva, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Soviet Textile Workers' Union; Malikov, Chairman of the Ukrainian Consumer Cooperative Union; Vaino, Chairman of the Estonian Republic Consumer Cooperative Union.

Also, delegations from various countries attending the International Economic Conference are arriving in Moscow to participate in the conference. On the 1st, representatives from Poland, the United States, Britain, West Germany, France, Italy, Denmark, Norway, Iceland, Mexico, Argentina, Paraguay, Iran, Egypt, Israel, Ceylon, Indonesia, and other countries arrived.

Xinhua News Agency, April 3rd: The seven-member Burmese delegation to the International Economic Conference, led by its head Hanthawaddy U Mya, arrived in Beijing by train from Guangzhou on March 31st. The delegation stayed in Beijing for two days and visited scenic spots such as the Summer Palace, the Temple of Heaven, Beihai Park, and Yonghe Temple. They left Beijing by plane on the 2nd for Moscow. Among those who saw them off at the airport were Liu Guan-yi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression; Meng Yu, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department; and Yu Zhi-ying and Yuan Bao-hua, representatives of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

### 苏联公布出席国际经济会议代表团名单  波、美、英等国代表团已陆续到达莫斯科

1952-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联公布出席国际经济会议代表团名单
    波、美、英等国代表团已陆续到达莫斯科
   【新华社三日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联各贸易、工业、合作社和工会组织以及科学和经济学机关已经推定代表，组成代表团出席国际经济会议。代表包括：全苏商会主席奈斯特洛夫、全苏工会中央理事会主席库兹涅佐夫、全苏消费合作社中央联合会委员会主席贺何洛夫、全苏商会副主席波里森科、苏联“出口商”组织主席尼契阔夫、苏联机器进口协会主席叶列明、苏联各种货物进口协会主席卡缅斯基、苏联粮食出口协会主席奥希波夫、苏联技术设备进口协会主席达希凯维奇、苏联运输机器进口协会主席拉辛、阿塞拜疆海滨石油管理局局长阿利哈诺夫、乌兹别克共和国棉业托辣斯经理尼夏诺夫、莫斯科斯大林汽车工厂厂长伏拉索夫、“大伊凡诺夫”纺织厂厂长茨维兹迪娜、中央汽车与拖拉机销售管理处莫斯科州办事处主任楚耶夫、里加波波夫无线电器材厂厂长查基斯、对外贸易部附设科学研究所所长奥尔洛夫、苏联科学院通讯院士奥斯特罗维捷扬诺夫、苏联科学院院士斯特鲁米林、苏联科学院院士瓦尔加、格鲁吉亚共和国科学院院士古利萨希维利、哈萨克共和国工会理事会主席贝依塞诺夫、苏联纺织工人工会中央委员会主席穆拉维耶娃、乌克兰消费合作社联合会主席马利科夫、爱沙尼亚共和国消费合作社联合会主席瓦伊诺。
    又讯：各国出席国际经济会议的代表正陆续抵达莫斯科参加会议。一日抵达的有波兰、美国、英国、西德、法国、意大利、丹麦、挪威、冰岛、墨西哥、阿根廷、巴拉圭、伊朗、埃及、以色列、锡兰、印度尼西亚及其他国家的代表。
    【新华社三日讯】出席国际经济会议缅甸代表团一行七人，在团长汉沙达宇妙率领下于三月三十一日由广州乘火车抵京。代表团在京逗留两天，并参观颐和园、天坛、北海公园、雍和宫等名胜后，已于二日乘机离京赴莫斯科。到机场欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会秘书长刘贯一、联络部副部长孟雨及中华全国总工会国际联络部代表俞志英、袁葆华等十余人。
