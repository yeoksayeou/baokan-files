Title: Panchen Erdene Enters Tibetan Territory
Author: 
Date: 1952-04-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 15th—Panchen Erdene and his entire entourage, returning to Tibet from Qinghai, have crossed the Tanggula Snow Mountain on the border of Qinghai and Tibet and entered Tibetan territory, receiving a warm welcome from all circles in Tibet. Representatives sent by the Dalai Lama and the Tibetan local government to welcome Panchen, as well as representatives from Tashilhunpo Monastery in Rear Tibet, met with Panchen in the Banzha Nianzha area north of Tanggula Mountain on February 26th. They also carried a letter from the Dalai Lama to Panchen. In response, Panchen telegraphed the Dalai Lama, Jikang Loben, the representative of Rear Tibet stationed in Lhasa, and officials and religious figures of Tashilhunpo Monastery, expressing his gratitude.

The representatives sent by the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army to welcome Panchen met with Panchen Erdene and his entire entourage on the summit of Tanggula Mountain on March 9th. Panchen Erdene was very grateful for this and specifically telegraphed General Zhang Jingwu, representative of the Central People's Government in Tibet, General Zhang Guohua, commander of the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Deputy Political Commissar General Fan Ming, on March 12th to express his thanks. The telegram stated: We are deeply grateful for your concern and love, sending personnel from afar to welcome us and bringing cattle, horses, and supplies. Panchen and the entire entourage are extremely grateful.

### 班禅额尔德尼已进入西藏境内

1952-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

    班禅额尔德尼已进入西藏境内
    【新华社拉萨十五日电】从青海返西藏的班禅额尔德尼及其行辕的全体人员，已翻越青海、西藏交界的唐古拉大雪山，进入西藏境内，备受西藏各界的热烈欢迎。达赖喇嘛和西藏地方政府派往欢迎班禅的代表及后藏扎什伦布寺代表在二月二十六日在唐古剌山北板扎念扎地区与班禅会合。他们并带有达赖给班禅的信札。为此，班禅曾致电达赖和后藏驻拉萨代表吉康洛本，扎什伦布寺政教僧俗官员表示感谢。
    中国人民解放军西藏军区派往欢迎的代表，在三月九日在唐古拉山顶和班禅额尔德尼及其行辕的全体人员会合。班禅额尔德尼对此十分感激，特在三月十二日致电中央人民政府驻西藏代表张经武将军、中国人民解放军西藏军区司令员张国华将军、副政治委员范明将军等致谢。电报中说：承蒙你们的关心和热爱，不远千里派员前来欢迎并带来牛马、补给，班禅及行辕全体人员甚为感激。
