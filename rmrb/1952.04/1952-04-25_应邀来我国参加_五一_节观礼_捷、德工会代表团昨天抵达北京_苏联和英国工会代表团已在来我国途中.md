Title: Czechoslovak and German Trade Union Delegations Arrive in Beijing Yesterday to Attend May Day Celebrations; Soviet and British Trade Union Delegations En Route to China
Author:
Date: 1952-04-25
Page: 1

    Invited to Attend May Day Celebrations in China
    Czechoslovak and German Trade Union Delegations Arrive in Beijing Yesterday
    Soviet and British Trade Union Delegations En Route to China
    [Xinhua News Agency, April 24th] The Czechoslovak and German Democratic Republic trade union delegations, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend May Day celebrations in China, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 24th.
    Those who welcomed them at the airport included: Zhu Xuefan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Xu Zhizhen, Minister of the Organization Department; Dong Xin, First Deputy Director of the Policy Research Office; Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department; Wang Rong, Deputy Minister of the Wage Department; Zhao Guoqiang, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Hardware and Smelting Workers' Union; Fang Ming, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Education Union; Jiang Tao, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Chemical Workers' Union, and others. Jabroski, First Secretary of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, and others also went to the airport to welcome them, as did the wife of Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Konsala.
    The list of the Czechoslovak Trade Union Delegation is as follows:
    Head of Delegation: Juleš Suntěk, Vice-Chairman and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Council of Czechoslovak Trade Unions
    Member: Vaclav Beran, General Secretary of the Czechoslovak General Workers' Union
    Květá Gácelová (female), Textile Worker, Model Worker
    František Socholík, Steel Worker, Model Worker
    Vaclav Holub, Secretary of the International Department of the Central Council of Czechoslovak Trade Unions, Secretary of the Delegation
    The list of the German Democratic Republic Trade Union Delegation is as follows:
    Head of Delegation: Gunter Herbig, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Free German Trade Union Federation
    Member: Herbert Harz, Member of the Presidium of the German Metalworkers' Union, Railway Factory Worker, Model Worker
    Paul Rutzmann, Steel Worker, Model Worker
    Rosemarie Polster (female), Textile Worker
    Janna Stern (female), Screenwriter, Secretary of the Delegation
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 24th] The Soviet trade union delegation, led by Shevchenko, Secretary of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the May Day celebrations in China, left Moscow by plane on the morning of the 24th for Beijing. Ge Baoquan, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, went to the airport to see them off.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Prague, April 24th] The British trade union delegation, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the May Day celebrations in China, arrived in Prague from Britain on the 20th and left Prague for Beijing on the 22nd.

### 应邀来我国参加“五一”节观礼  捷、德工会代表团昨天抵达北京  苏联和英国工会代表团已在来我国途中

1952-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国参加“五一”节观礼
    捷、德工会代表团昨天抵达北京
    苏联和英国工会代表团已在来我国途中
    【新华社二十四日讯】应中华全国总工会的邀请前来我国参加“五一”节观礼的捷克斯洛伐克和德意志民主共和国工会代表团，已于二十四日下午乘飞机抵达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有：中华全国总工会副主席朱学范、组织部部长许之桢、政策研究室第一副主任董昕、国际联络部副部长金子明、工资部副部长王榕、中国五金冶炼工会筹备委员会主任赵国强、中国教育工会全国委员会副主席方明、中国化学工会筹备委员会主任江涛等。捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆一等秘书雅布罗斯基等均往机场欢迎，捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使康萨拉的夫人也到机场欢迎。
    捷克斯洛伐克工会代表团名单如下：
    团长尤莱·逊捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会副主席兼书记处书记
    团员  伐次拉夫·贝朗捷克斯洛伐克各业工人工会总书记
    格维也塔·加采洛娃（女）纺织工人、劳动模范
    弗朗悌歇克·索霍力克  钢铁工人、劳动模范
    伐次拉夫·霍鲁勃捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会国际部秘书、代表团秘书
    德意志民主共和国工会代表团名单如下：
    团长  古特·海尔比希自由德国工会联合会书记处书记
    团员  赫伯特·哈兹德国五金工会主席团委员、铁路工厂工人、劳动模范
    保尔·鲁兹曼钢铁工人、劳动模范
    罗雪娜·波尔斯特（女）纺织工人
    让纳·斯德恩（女）电影剧本作家、代表团秘书
    【新华社莫斯科二十四日电】应中华全国总工会的邀请，前来我国参加五一节观礼的苏联工会代表团，由全苏工会中央理事会书记谢甫钦科率领，已在二十四日晨乘飞机离莫斯科赴北京。我国驻苏大使馆参赞戈宝权曾到机场欢送。
    【新华社布拉格二十四日电】应中华全国总工会的邀请前往中国参加“五一”节观礼的英国工会代表团，已于二十日由英国抵达布拉格，并于二十二日由布拉格转赴北京。
