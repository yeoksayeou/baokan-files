Title: American Federation of Scientists Publishes Statement Confirming Long-Term US Government Preparation for Germ Warfare
Author:
Date: 1952-04-09
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Eighth Dispatch from Moscow: New York dispatch: A statement published by the American Federation of Scientists in a recent issue of its journal, the "Bulletin of the Atomic Scientists," confirms that the United States has long been preparing to use bacteriological weapons.

    The statement says: "During World War II and in the period since, we have spent millions of dollars to investigate the possibilities of bacteriological warfare. According to official reports, methods have been devised for using bacteria, viruses, germs, and organically produced poisons, with the aim of slaughtering humans and maiming animals and plants or causing diseases among them."

    The statement also cited a speech made by former US Secretary of Defense Forrestal on March 12, 1949, regarding extensive plans for preparing for germ warfare, the purpose of which was to "use such weapons should it become necessary in the future."

    The statement said: "Therefore, the means to conduct germ warfare have been adopted in our military arsenal."

### 美国科学家联合会在会刊上发表声明  证实美政府长期以来准备进行细菌战

1952-04-09
第4版()
专栏：

    美国科学家联合会在会刊上发表声明
    证实美政府长期以来准备进行细菌战
    【新华社八日讯】莫斯科广播：纽约消息：美国科学家联合会在该会机关刊“美国科学家联合会通报”最近一期上发表的声明，证实美国长期以来即在准备使用细菌武器。
    声明说：“在第二次世界大战期间及其以后的时期，我们耗费了千百万美元来研究进行细菌战的可能性。据官方报道，已经发明了使用细菌、病毒、病菌及用有机物制成的毒品的方法，目的是屠杀人类和残害动植物或在他们中间引起疾病。”
    声明并引证了美国前国防部长福莱斯特尔在一九四九年三月十二日发表的关于准备进行细菌战的广泛计划的谈话，这个计划的目的是“在将来有必要时使用这种武器”。
    声明说：“因此，在我国的军备中已采用了进行细菌战的手段。”
