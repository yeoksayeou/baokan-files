Title: French Peace Council Cables China Peace Committee, Expressing French People's Indignation and Condemnation of American Aggressors' Germ Warfare

Author:
Date: 1952-04-18
Page: 1

    French Peace Council Cables China Peace Committee, Expressing French People's Indignation and Condemnation of American Aggressors' Germ Warfare
    [Xinhua News Agency, April 17] The French National Peace Council sent a cable to the China Peace Committee, condemning the barbaric crime of the American aggressors in conducting germ warfare. The text of the cable is as follows:
    To the China Peace Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression:
    The Standing Committee of the French National Peace Council has read the documents submitted by the Chinese delegation to the Executive Bureau meeting of the World Peace Council in Oslo regarding the American germ warfare, as well as the report issued by the investigation team of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers after conducting on-the-spot investigations in Korea and Northeast China.
    We feel boundless indignation at this heinous crime that violates human rights and undermines international law.
    The Standing Committee of the French National Peace Council, on behalf of all loyal, peace-loving French people, expresses solidarity and friendship to the people of China and Korea. We will immediately conduct extensive publicity and launch a campaign regarding these exposed shameful facts in order to arouse a high tide of mass collective protest, forcing all governments to absolutely prohibit the use of weapons of mass destruction.
    The French people and the people of China and Korea have a long and profound friendship. The French people are proud that Pasteur made contributions to mankind. We condemn this barbaric crime and the despicable criminals and their accomplices.
    Long live the struggle of the people of the world for peace!
          The French National Peace Council
                Paris, April 10

### 法国和平理事会电我保卫和平委员会表示  法国人民愤怒谴责美国侵略者进行细菌战

1952-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

    法国和平理事会电我保卫和平委员会表示
    法国人民愤怒谴责美国侵略者进行细菌战
    【新华社十七日讯】法国全国和平理事会致电中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会，谴责美国侵略者进行细菌战的野蛮罪行。电文如下：中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会：
    法国全国和平理事会常设委员会阅读了中国代表提交奥斯陆世界和平理事会执行局会议关于美国进行细菌战争的文件，以及国际民主法律工作者协会调查团在朝鲜和中国东北实地调查后所发表的报告。
    我们对这种违反人权和破坏国际公法的万恶罪行，感到无比的愤怒。
    法国全国和平理事会常设委员会代表所有忠实的、爱好和平的法国人民，向中朝两国人民表示团结友爱。我们对这已经揭露的可耻事实，将立即进行广泛宣传和展开运动，以激起群众集体抗议的高潮，迫使各国政府绝对禁止使用大规模毁灭的武器。
    法国人民和中朝两国人民有着悠久和深厚的友谊关系，法国人民为曾以巴斯德贡献给人类而感到骄傲。我们谴责这种野蛮的罪行和卑鄙可耻的罪犯们及其同谋者。
    全世界人民为和平的斗争万岁！
          法国全国和平理事会
                四月十日于巴黎
