    Our Great Motherland
    The economies of agricultural and pastoral areas in our Xinjiang are thriving day by day. Now, the snow on the Tianshan Mountains has melted and is irrigating the fields; flocks of sheep are grazing on the pastures. This is a beautiful spring scene on the northwestern grasslands.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国新疆农业区和牧畜区的经济都在日益繁荣。现在，天山的积雪已经融化，正灌溉着田地；羊群在牧地上吃草。这是西北草原上春天的美景。
