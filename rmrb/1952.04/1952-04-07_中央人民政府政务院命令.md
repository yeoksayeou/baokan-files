Date: 1952-04-07
Page: 1

    Order of the State Council of the Central People's Government

    He Hengfu, a member of the Central and Southern Military and Political Committee, member of the Central and Southern Financial and Economic Committee, and member of the Wuhan Municipal People's Government, organized and led a large-scale theft ring, engaged in concealing large amounts of enemy property; dispatched spies to infiltrate government agencies, colluded with and bribed government staff, stole economic intelligence, stole a large amount of state property; trafficked in large quantities of narcotics; smuggled huge amounts of gold for export; and refused to confess during the anti-corruption and anti-theft movement, and also prevented other criminals in the theft ring from confessing. For this reason, He Hengfu should be immediately removed from all his government positions and arrested and brought to justice. Except as already reported to and approved by Chairman Mao, this is hereby ordered.

    Premier Zhou Enlai
    April 4, 1952

### 中央人民政府政务院命令

1952-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府政务院命令
    中南军政委员会委员、中南财政经济委员会委员兼武汉市人民政府委员贺衡夫，组织并领导大规模的盗窃集团，进行隐匿大宗敌产；派遣坐探钻入政府机关，勾结和贿赂政府工作人员，盗窃经济情报，盗窃大量国家财产；贩卖大宗毒品；盗运巨额黄金出口；并在反贪污、反盗窃运动中，拒不坦白，又阻止该盗窃集团中其他犯罪分子坦白。为此，应即撤销贺衡夫在政府中的一切职务，并予逮捕法办。除已报经毛主席批准外，特此令知。
    总理  周恩来
    一九五二年四月四日
