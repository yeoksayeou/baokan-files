Title: Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Soviet Foreign Cultural Association Jointly Hold Commemorative Meeting for Lenin's Birthday
Author:
Date: 1952-04-22
Page: 4

    Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Soviet Foreign Cultural Association
    Jointly Hold Commemorative Meeting for Lenin's Birthday

    [Report from this newspaper] April 22nd is the 82nd anniversary of the birth of Lenin, the leader and teacher of the working people of the world. The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association will jointly hold a commemorative meeting at 7:00 p.m. on the 22nd at No. 19 Nanheyan, the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association. Representatives from all branches may attend with admission tickets.

### 北京中苏友协和苏联对外文协  联合举办列宁诞辰纪念会

1952-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    北京中苏友协和苏联对外文协
    联合举办列宁诞辰纪念会
    【本报讯】四月二十二日为全世界劳动人民的领袖和导师列宁诞生八十二周年的纪念日，北京市中苏友好协会和苏联对外文化协会定于二十二日下午七时在南河沿十九号苏联对外文化协会联合举办纪念会，届时各支会代表可持入场券前往参加。
