Title: Australian, Brazilian and British Representatives Arrive in Beijing for Visit After Attending International Economic Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-04-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 20th—A delegation of twelve representatives from Australia, Brazil, and the United Kingdom, who attended the International Economic Conference in Moscow, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 20th for a visit. They were welcomed at the airport by Liu Ningyi, a member of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Xiang Shuxiang, a member of the preparatory office of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Preparatory Committee; Sun Xiaocun, a standing committee member of the China Democratic National Construction Association; Liu Zunqi, a representative of the preparatory committee of the China National News and Publication Workers' Union; Fu Huating, chairman of the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce; Chen Dingmin, a professor at Tsinghua University, and many others. Jiang Ming, Deputy Director of the Foreign Trade Department of the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government, also welcomed them at the airport.

The list of representatives from each country is as follows:

    Hoffman, Australian businessman;
    Oliveira, Brazilian engineer and economist;
    Quimares, Brazilian journalist;
    Martin, Brazilian builder and journalist;
    Sequino, Brazilian textile industrialist;
    Wayne, General Secretary of the Derbyshire Miners' Union, UK;
    Slater, British women's clothing manufacturer;
    Horsley, British dairy manufacturer;
    McClelland, British grocery retailer;
    Boulton, British importer and exporter;
    Silver, British cotton merchant;
    Kins, British hardware and chemical exporter.

### 出席国际经济会议后来我国参观  澳大利亚巴西和英国代表昨天抵达北京

1952-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    出席国际经济会议后来我国参观
    澳大利亚巴西和英国代表昨天抵达北京
    【新华社二十日讯】出席在莫斯科举行的国际经济会议的澳大利亚、巴西和英国代表一行十二人，在二十日下午搭飞机抵达北京参观。到机场欢迎的有：国际贸易促进委员会委员刘宁一、中华全国工商业联合会筹备委员会筹备处委员项叔翔、民主建国会总会常务委员孙晓村、中国新闻出版印刷工会筹备委员会代表刘尊棋、北京市工商业联合会主任委员傅华亭、清华大学教授陈定民等多人。中央人民政府贸易部国外贸易司副司长江明也到机场欢迎。
    各国代表名单如下：
    霍夫曼澳大利亚商业家；
    奥立维巴西工程师、经济学家；
    奎马瑞巴西新闻工作者；
    马丁巴西建筑商、新闻工作者；
    塞基诺巴西纺织工业家；
    韦恩英国多贝夏州矿工工会总书记；
    斯莱特尔英国女子服装制造商；
    霍斯莱英国牛乳制造商；
    麦克利兰英国杂货零售商；
    鲍尔顿英国进出口商；
    锡尔佛英国棉商；
    金氏英国五金化学品出口商。
