Title: Central Ministry of Culture and All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Host Banquet for Hungarian Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1952-04-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency report: The Central People's Government Ministry of Culture and the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles jointly held a banquet on the evening of the 6th to welcome all members of the Hungarian National People's Art Troupe.

    Present at the banquet were all members of the Hungarian National People's Art Troupe. Invited guests included prominent literary and art workers in Beijing such as Ouyang Yuqian, Hong Shen, and Lao She. Hungarian Ambassador to China, Szikla Sándor, and some members of the embassy staff were also invited to attend.

    At the banquet, Sha Kefu, representative of the Central Ministry of Culture and the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, delivered a welcoming speech. The head of the Hungarian National People's Art Troupe, Seresi Gyula, and Hungarian Ambassador to China, Szikla Sándor, also delivered speeches successively. All participants at the banquet repeatedly toasted the everlasting friendship and cultural exchange between China and Hungary, toasted Chairman Mao and Rákosi, and toasted world peace.

    Chinese and Hungarian artists gathered together in a hall filled with fraternal and joyful friendship. All members of the Hungarian National People's Art Troupe sang the song "The East is Red" impromptu, which aroused cheers and applause from the audience. After the banquet, Chinese and Hungarian artists performed artistic programs for each other.

### 中央文化部和全国文联  欢宴匈牙利文工团

1952-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

    中央文化部和全国文联
    欢宴匈牙利文工团
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：中央人民政府文化部、中华全国文学艺术界联合会在六日晚联合举行宴会，欢宴匈牙利国家人民文工团全体团员。
    出席宴会的有匈牙利国家人民文工团全体团员。应邀作陪的有在京的著名的文学艺术工作者欧阳予倩、洪深、老舍等。匈牙利驻华大使夏法朗柯和大使馆一部分工作人员也应邀出席作陪。
    席上，中央文化部和全国文联代表沙可夫首先致欢迎词。匈牙利国家人民文工团团长歇勒西·纠腊，匈牙利驻华大使夏法朗柯也先后致词。全体参加宴会人员不断为中匈永久的友谊和文化交流干杯，为毛主席、拉科西干杯，为世界和平干杯。
    中匈两国艺术家欢聚一堂，席间充满了兄弟般愉快的友情。匈牙利国家人民文工团全体团员并曾即席齐唱“东方红”歌曲，引起了全场欢呼和掌声。宴会后，中匈两国艺术家曾互相表演艺术节目。
