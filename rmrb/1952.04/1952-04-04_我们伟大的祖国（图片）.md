    Our Great Motherland
    The agricultural producers' cooperative is a superior organizational form for agricultural labor mutual aid. The achievements of the agricultural producers' cooperative led by Geng Changsuo, a model agricultural laborer in Wugong Village, Raoyang County, Hebei Province, over the past eight years are remarkable. This year, the Geng Changsuo agricultural producers' cooperative started spring plowing ahead of schedule. This is a scene of cooperative members plowing the land with new-style walking plows.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    农业生产合作社是农业劳动互助的优越的组织形式。河北省饶阳县五公村农业劳动模范耿长锁领导的农业生产合作社八年来所获得的成绩是显著的。今年，耿长锁农业生产合作社提前开始春耕。这是社员们用新式步犁耕地的情形。
