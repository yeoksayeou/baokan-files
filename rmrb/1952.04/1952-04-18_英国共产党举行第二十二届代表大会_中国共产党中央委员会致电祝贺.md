Title: Communist Party of Great Britain Holds 22nd National Congress; Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Sends Congratulatory Telegram

Author:
Date: 1952-04-18
Page: 1

    Communist Party of Great Britain Holds 22nd National Congress
    Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Sends Congratulatory Telegram
    [This newspaper dispatch] The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram congratulating the opening of the 22nd National Congress of the Communist Party of Great Britain. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To Comrade Harry Pollitt, Executive Committee of the Communist Party of Great Britain, forwarded to all the representatives of the 22nd Congress of the Communist Party of Great Britain:

    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, representing all its members, extends warm, fraternal congratulations to the 22nd National Congress of the Communist Party of Great Britain! We wish the conference complete success and that you achieve further victories in the struggle to defend the interests of the British people and world peace.
              The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
                   April 16, 1952, Beijing

### 英国共产党举行第二十二届代表大会  中国共产党中央委员会致电祝贺

1952-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

    英国共产党举行第二十二届代表大会
    中国共产党中央委员会致电祝贺
    【本报讯】中国共产党中央委员会致电祝贺英国共产党第二十二届代表大会开幕。电文如下：伦敦英国共产党执行委员会亲爱的波立特同志转英国共产党第二十二届代表大会的各位代表同志：
    中国共产党中央委员会代表全体党员特向英国共产党第二十二届代表大会致热烈的兄弟的祝贺！敬祝大会的圆满成功，和你们在保卫英国人民利益和世界和平的斗争中获得进一步的胜利。
              中国共产党中央委员会
                   一九五二年四月十六日，北京
