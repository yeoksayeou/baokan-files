Title: Chinese Writers and Artists Delegation Arrives in Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1952-04-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 2nd. The All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles' Writing Delegation, led by Ba Jin, arrived in Pyongyang on March 31st from the Korean front. The delegation was warmly welcomed by the Korean government, people from all walks of life in Korea, and the Chinese Embassy in Korea.
    The delegation will stay in Pyongyang for a short period, visit the leadership organs of the Korean People's Army, and hold discussions with Korean literary and art circles to exchange creative experiences. Then, they will be divided into groups to experience life in various arms of the Chinese People's Volunteers at the front.

### 我国文联创作组到达平壤

1952-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    我国文联创作组到达平壤
    【新华社平壤二日电】中华全国文学艺术界联合会赴朝创作组在巴金率领下，在三月三十一日从朝鲜前线抵达平壤。创作组受到朝鲜政府、朝鲜各界人民和我国驻朝大使馆的热烈欢迎。
    创作组将在平壤作短期逗留，访问朝鲜人民军领导机关，并和朝鲜文艺界举行座谈，交流创作经验，然后分组到前线志愿军各兵种部队里去体验生活。
