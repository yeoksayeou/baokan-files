Title: Chinese Trade Union Delegation Leaves Beijing for Various Countries to Attend May Day Celebrations in Poland, Romania, Hungary, Czechoslovakia, and Germany
Author:
Date: 1952-04-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, April 21st—At the invitation of the trade unions of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, the Romanian People's Republic, the Hungarian People's Republic, the Czechoslovak Republic, and the German Democratic Republic, five delegations from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, dispatched to each country to participate in the May Day celebrations, departed from Beijing by plane on the morning of the 21st, bound for their respective destinations. Li Zaiwen, Director of the General Office of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Zhang Lizhi, Deputy Director of the Organization Department, Li Xian, Deputy Director of the Labor Protection Department, and Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department, were all at the airport to see them off.

Also present at the airport to see them off were Rudenko, Ambassador of the Romanian People's Republic to China, König, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic to China with the title of Ambassador, Tsarvoranov, Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to China, Szibułka, Counselor of the Embassy of the Hungarian People's Republic to China, and Jabroski, First Secretary of the Embassy of the Czechoslovak Republic to China, and others.

The lists of the delegations are as follows:

Delegation to the People's Republic of Bulgaria:
	Head: Shao Jingwa, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the China Textile Workers' Union
	Member: Liu Hansheng, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions
	Yu Yimin (female), Translator

Delegation to the Romanian People's Republic:
	Head: Yuan Zhihe, Chairman of the National Committee of the China Food Workers' Union
	Member: Gu Xiaobo, Vice-Chairman of the Tianjin Federation of Trade Unions
	Guo Menghe, Petroleum worker, Labor Model
	Wang Fuqing, Textile worker, Labor Model
	Shen Yinzhi, Translator

Delegation to the Hungarian People's Republic:
	Head: Chang Jinglin, Chairman of the National Committee of the China Store Employees' Union
	Member: Xiao Jing, Chairman of the Jilin Provincial Federation of Trade Unions
	Guo Maoan, Translator

Delegation to the Czechoslovak Republic:
	Head: He Yingcai, Vice-Chairman of the Central-South Federation of Trade Unions
	Member: Zhu Zitong, Vice-Chairman of the Xi'an Federation of Trade Unions
	Qin Bingsheng, Automobile repair worker, Labor Model
	Shi Junqing, Electricity worker, Labor Model
	Shen Peirong (female), Translator
	Chen Fen (female), Translator

Delegation to the German Democratic Republic:
	Head: Wang Weigang, Deputy Director of the Southwest Office of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions
	Member: Liu Laifu, Vice-Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions
	Liu Dezhen, Steel worker, Labor Model
	Liu Jinliang, Electricity worker, Labor Model
	Xu Pingyuan, Farm implement worker, Labor Model

### 应邀参加保罗匈捷德五国“五一”节庆典  我国工会代表团离京分赴各国

1952-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀参加保罗匈捷德五国“五一”节庆典
    我国工会代表团离京分赴各国
    【新华社二十一日讯】中华全国总工会应保加利亚人民共和国、罗马尼亚人民共和国、匈牙利人民共和国、捷克斯洛伐克共和国与德意志民主共和国总工会邀请，派往各该国参加“五一”节庆祝典礼的五个代表团，已在二十一日晨乘飞机离京，分赴各国。全国总工会办公厅主任栗再温、组织部副部长张立之，劳保部副部长李仙、国际联络部副部长金子明等都到机场欢送。
