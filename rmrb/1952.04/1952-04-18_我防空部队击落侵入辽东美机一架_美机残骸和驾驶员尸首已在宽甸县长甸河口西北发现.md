Title: Our Air Defense Forces Shot Down an Intruding American Plane Over Liaodong; Wreckage and Pilot's Body Found Northwest of Changdianhekou, Kuandian County
Author:
Date: 1952-04-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 17th—On the 13th at 9:16 AM, four American aggressor military planes intruded into the airspace over Changdianhekou, Kuandian County, Liaodong Province. One of them was immediately shot down by the anti-aircraft fire of our air defense forces, while the other three fled in panic. The wreckage of the downed American plane has been found near Huanghuagou, nine kilometers northwest of Changdianhekou. The plane was an F-86 jet fighter, numbered [0636]. The pilot fell to the ground and died; his body was found in Xiaoxigou, ten kilometers northwest of Changdianhekou.

### 我防空部队击落侵入辽东美机一架  美机残骸和驾驶员尸首已在宽甸县长甸河口西北发现

1952-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我防空部队击落侵入辽东美机一架
    美机残骸和驾驶员尸首已在宽甸县长甸河口西北发现
    【新华社沈阳十七日电】美国侵略者的军用飞机四架，在十三日上午九时十六分侵入我辽东省宽甸县长甸河口上空，其中一架当即被我防空部队的高射炮火击落，其余三架仓慌逃遁。被击落的美机残骸已在长甸河口西北九公里的黄花沟附近发现，该机为F——86型喷气式战斗机，番号为〔0636〕号。驾驶员坠地身死，其尸首也在长甸河口西北十公里的小西沟发现。
