Title: US Germ Warfare in Korea Has Caused Malignant Dysentery in Japan
Author:
Date: 1952-04-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 23rd: The nefarious germ warfare waged by US imperialism in Korea has already produced dire consequences in Japan. According to TASS Tokyo, April 23rd: News has it that a type of malignant dysentery, which is uncommon during this season, has broken out in Kobe. According to the "Zen Tsushin News" of April 17th, doctors and scientists in Osaka pointed out that Japan has never had this dysentery bacteria before, and the newspaper said that the "Central Japan Heavy Industries" company is using scrap iron shipped from South Korea, and the infectious disease occurred among the company's workers. The newspaper emphasized that this infectious disease is undoubtedly the result of germ warfare within Korea.

### 美国在朝鲜进行细菌战已使日本发生恶性痢疾

1952-04-25
第4版()
专栏：

    美国在朝鲜进行细菌战已使日本发生恶性痢疾
    【新华社二十四日讯】美帝国主义在朝鲜进行的罪恶的细菌战，已经在日本产生了恶果。据塔斯社东京二十三日讯：有消息说，这个季节不常发生的一种恶性痢疾已在神户发生。据四月十七日的“全递新闻”说，大阪的医生和科学家指出，日本以前从未有过这种痢疾细菌，报纸并说，“中日本重工业”公司在使用由南朝鲜运来的废铁，传染病就是在这个公司的工人中发生的。该报强调指出，这种传染病毫无疑问是朝鲜境内细菌战的结果。
