Title: Korean Armistice Talks Make No Progress
Author: 
Date: 1952-04-06
Page: 1
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 5th -- The meeting of the Third Agenda Subcommittee of the Korean Armistice Talks on the 5th made no progress, as the other side insisted on its so-called demand to restrict airport equipment in North Korea, which interferes with our internal affairs.

### 朝鲜停战谈判会议无进展

1952-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判会议无进展
    【新华社开城五日电】五日朝鲜停战谈判第三项议程小组委员会会议上，对方坚持其所谓限制朝鲜境内机场设备的干涉我方内政的要求，会议无进展。
