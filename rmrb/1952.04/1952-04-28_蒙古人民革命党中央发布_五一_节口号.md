Title: Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party Central Committee Issues May Day Slogans
Author:
Date: 1952-04-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar: TASS reports: The Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party issued May Day slogans on April 25, calling on the people of the republic to launch labor competitions more widely, complete the national plan for developing the national economy and culture, and strengthen the country's strength with new labor achievements.

The slogans expressed the republic's people's gratitude to the Soviet people and Mongolia's best friend, Joseph Stalin, for their constant concern and assistance in the development of the Mongolian People's Republic.

The Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party sends fraternal greetings to all the people of all countries struggling for peace, democracy, and socialism.

### 蒙古人民革命党中央发布“五一”节口号

1952-04-28
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民革命党中央发布“五一”节口号
    【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社乌兰巴托讯：蒙古人民革命党中央委员会四月二十五日发布五一节口号，号召共和国人民更广泛地展开劳动竞赛，完成发展国民经济和文化的国家计划，以新的劳动成绩加强国家的力量。
    口号表示共和国人民感谢苏联人民和蒙古人民的最好的朋友——约·维·斯大林在发展蒙古人民共和国方面的经常的关注和协助。
    蒙古人民革命党中央委员会向为和平、民主和社会主义而斗争的所有各国人民致兄弟的敬礼。
