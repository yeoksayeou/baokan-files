Title: Commemorating the Great Leader of the Working People, Lenin's Birthday: Moscow Residents Organize Various Gatherings to Introduce Lenin's Life; A Constant Stream of People Visit Lenin's Mausoleum in Red Square

Author:

Date: 1952-04-22
Page: 4

    Commemorating the Great Leader of the Working People, Lenin's Birthday
    Moscow Residents Organize Various Gatherings to Introduce Lenin's Life
    A Constant Stream of People Visit Lenin's Mausoleum in Red Square
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 21st dispatch from TASS] On the 22nd, it is the 82nd anniversary of the birth of Lenin, the great leader of the world's working people and the founder of the Bolshevik Party and the world's first socialist country. In the past few days, factories, institutions, educational institutions, and military units in Moscow have been organizing symposiums, lectures, and reports to discuss Lenin's life and revolutionary activities, the great friendship between Lenin and Stalin, and the various achievements made by the Soviet Union under the leadership of the Bolshevik Party.
    Millions of working men and women are visiting Moscow and its suburbs in groups, places associated with Lenin's name. There has been a particularly large number of visitors to the Lenin Central Museum in recent days, including residents of Moscow, representatives of various republics, border regions, and states, as well as foreign guests. On the 20th alone, nearly four thousand people visited the museum. At the same time, a constant stream of people is visiting Lenin's Mausoleum in Red Square. Sixteen thousand people visited the mausoleum on the 20th. With reverence, they slowly entered Lenin's final resting place to pay their respects to the kind face of the great leader.

### 纪念劳动人民伟大领袖列宁诞辰  莫斯科人民组织各种集会介绍列宁生平  前往红场拜谒列宁陵墓的人们川流不息

1952-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    纪念劳动人民伟大领袖列宁诞辰
    莫斯科人民组织各种集会介绍列宁生平
    前往红场拜谒列宁陵墓的人们川流不息
    【新华社二十一日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：二十二日，是世界劳动人民的伟大领袖、布尔什维克党和世界上第一个社会主义国家的缔造者列宁诞生的八十二周年。几天来莫斯科各工厂、机关、教育机关及部队都在组织座谈会、讲演会和报告会，讨论列宁的生平和革命活动、列宁和斯大林的伟大友谊以及苏联在布尔什维克党的领导下所获得的种种成就。
    千千万万的劳动男女都在成群结队地访谒莫斯科及其近郊与列宁名字相连的地方。最近几天前往列宁中央博物馆参观的人特别多，其中有莫斯科居民、各共和国、各边疆区和各州的代表以及外国来宾。单是二十日一天，到博物馆去参观的就差不多有四千人。同时，前往红场拜谒列宁陵墓的人也川流不息。二十日前往谒陵的有一万六千人。他们怀着崇敬的心情缓缓地步入列宁最后安息的地方，瞻仰这位伟大领袖可亲的遗容。
