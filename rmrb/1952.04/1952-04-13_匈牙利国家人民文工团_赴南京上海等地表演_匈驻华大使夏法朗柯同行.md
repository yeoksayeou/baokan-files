Title: Hungarian National People's Performing Arts Troupe Performs in Nanjing, Shanghai and Other Places; Hungarian Ambassador to China, Halász Ferenc, Accompanies

Author:
Date: 1952-04-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 12th: The Hungarian National People's Performing Arts Troupe, accompanied by Zhang Guangnian, Deputy Director of the Art Affairs Administration Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, left here on the evening of the 12th to perform in Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other places. Hungarian Ambassador to China, Halász Ferenc, and his wife also went to Nanjing and other places with the troupe. Many people from the capital's literary and art circles went to the station to see them off.
Since arriving in Beijing on the 2nd, the Hungarian National People's Performing Arts Troupe has performed musical and dance programs many times, and has been warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life in Beijing. During their stay in Beijing, they visited the capital's scenic spots such as Beihai, the Temple of Heaven, and the Summer Palace; and also visited the joint song and dance performances of various literary and art groups in the capital and the dance performances of Korean dancer Choi Seung-hee.
The Hungarian National People's Performing Arts Troupe will return to Beijing at the end of this month, and will hold a larger-scale performance in Beijing again in early May.

### 匈牙利国家人民文工团  赴南京上海等地表演  匈驻华大使夏法朗柯同行

1952-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    匈牙利国家人民文工团
    赴南京上海等地表演
    匈驻华大使夏法朗柯同行
    【新华社十二日讯】匈牙利国家人民文工团在十二日晚由中央人民政府文化部艺术事业管理局副局长张光年陪同，离此赴南京、上海、杭州等地表演。匈牙利驻华大使夏法朗柯及其夫人也和该文工团同赴南京等地。首都文艺界人士多人曾到车站欢送。
    匈牙利国家人民文工团全体人员自二日抵京后，曾演出音乐、舞蹈节目多次，受到北京各界人士的热烈欢迎。他们留京期间，曾游览了首都的名胜北海、天坛、颐和园等地；并曾参观首都各文艺团体的联合歌舞演出和朝鲜舞蹈家崔承喜的舞蹈演出。
    匈牙利国家人民文工团将在本月底返京，并将在五月初再度在京举行较大规模的演出。
