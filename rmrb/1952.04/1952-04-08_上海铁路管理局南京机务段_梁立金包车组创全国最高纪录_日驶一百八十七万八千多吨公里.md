Title: Shanghai Railway Administration, Nanjing Locomotive Depot, Liang Lijin Chartered Group Creates New National Record: Daily Run of Over 1,878,000 Ton-Kilometers
Author: Yang Juzhang
Date: 1952-04-08
Page: 2

    Shanghai Railway Administration, Nanjing Locomotive Depot
    Liang Lijin Chartered Group Creates New National Record
    Daily Run of Over 1,878,000 Ton-Kilometers
    The No. 7559 locomotive chartered group of Liang Lijin of the Nanjing Locomotive Depot of the Shanghai Railway Administration, created a new national record of running over 1,878,000 ton-kilometers per day. The group leader Liang Lijin and all his employees, in the great "Three-Anti" movement, clearly recognized the enemy and us, actively participated in the "tiger hunting" struggle, and strived to learn advanced operating techniques and locomotive maintenance methods. On March 18th, with the close cooperation of the locomotive dispatcher and other departments, they brought into full play the potential of the locomotive. They hauled an overloaded train for 1,032 kilometers in one day, completing a workload of 1,878,480 ton-kilometers. The creation of this new record has given a powerful blow to the conservative thinking of "more hauling cannot run fast" and has given endless encouragement to chartered groups across the country. (Yang Juzhang)

### 上海铁路管理局南京机务段  梁立金包车组创全国最高纪录  日驶一百八十七万八千多吨公里
杨居漳
1952-04-08
第2版()
专栏：

    上海铁路管理局南京机务段
    梁立金包车组创全国最高纪录
    日驶一百八十七万八千多吨公里
    上海铁路管理局南京机务段厶?7五五九号机车梁立金包车组，创日驶一百八十七万八千多吨公里的全国最高纪录。包车组司机长梁立金和他全组的职工，在伟大的“三反”运动中，认清了敌我，便积极参加了“打虎”斗争，并努力学习先进的操作技术和保养机车方法。三月十八日，他们在机车调度员及其他部门周密配合下，发挥了机车的潜在力，一天内牵引超重列车走行了一千零三十二公里，完成一百八十七万八千四百八十吨公里的工作量。这个新纪录创造出来以后，给“多拉不能快跑”的保守思想以有力的打击，给全国各包车组以无限的鼓舞。（杨居漳）
