Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-04-29
Page: 1

    Our great motherland
    The scope of material exchange between urban and rural areas in our country is constantly expanding. The transportation on the Jialing River in Sichuan is also busy. These are the ships traveling on the Jialing River. They are transporting large quantities of fertilizers and industrial products to the countryside, and transporting grain and raw materials from the countryside to the cities.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国城乡物资交流的范围正在不断扩大。四川嘉陵江的运输也繁忙了。这是来往在嘉陵江上的船只。它们正把大批的肥料和工业品运到农村去，把农村中的粮食和原料运到城市来。
