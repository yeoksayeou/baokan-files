Author:
Date: 1952-04-08
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The anti-corruption struggle has further enhanced the political awareness and production enthusiasm of the working class in our country. The steelmaking department of Tianjin Steel Plant completed the first quarter's plan for increasing production and practicing economy six days and eleven minutes ahead of schedule on March 25th; and created a record of shortening the smelting time by 16% compared with the past. This is a picture of workers in the steelmaking department of Tianjin Steel Plant actively repairing the furnace, preparing to continue production.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-04-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    反贪污斗争进一步提高了我国工人阶级群众的政治觉悟和生产热情。天津钢厂炼钢部于三月二十五日提前六天零十一分完成了今年第一季增产节约的计划；并创造了冶炼时间比过去缩短百分之十六的成绩。这是天津钢厂炼钢部工人正在积极补炉，准备继续生产的情形。
