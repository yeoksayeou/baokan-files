Title: Six National People's Organizations in China Issue Joint Statement Severely Protesting the Greek Government's Crime of Killing Beloyannis
Author:
Date: 1952-04-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 19th: Six national people's organizations, including the China Peace Committee, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others, issued a joint statement on the 19th, severely protesting the Greek government's heinous crime of killing Nikos Beloyannis, a member of the Central Committee of the Greek Communist Party, and his comrades, and demanding that the Greek government immediately release all illegally arrested democrats. The statement is as follows:
To the Government of Athens, Greece:
We have read with unparalleled anger the news that your government, disregarding the opposition of people around the world, has outrageously murdered Beloyannis and his comrades. On behalf of the entire Chinese people, we lodge a serious protest against this bloody crime committed by your government and demand that your government immediately release all illegally arrested democrats.
    The China Peace Committee
    All-China Federation of Trade Unions
    All-China Democratic Women's Federation.
    All- China Youth Federation
    All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles
    All-China Students' Federation

### 我国六个全国性人民团体发表联合声明  严重抗议希腊政府杀害贝劳扬尼斯的罪行

1952-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我国六个全国性人民团体发表联合声明
    严重抗议希腊政府杀害贝劳扬尼斯的罪行
    【新华社十九日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会、中华全国总工会等人民团体十九日发表联合声明，严重抗议希腊政府悍然杀害希腊共产党中央委员会委员贝劳扬尼斯和他的同志们的罪行，并要求希腊政府立即释放所有被非法逮捕的民主人士。声明如下：雅典希腊政府：
    我们以无比的愤怒阅读了你政府不顾全世界人民的反对，悍然杀害了贝劳扬尼斯和他的同志们的消息。我们代表全中国人民向你政府制造的这种血腥罪行提出严重的抗议，同时要求你政府立即释放所有被非法逮捕的民主人士。
    中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会
    中华全国总工会
    中华全国民主妇女联合会
    中华全国民主青年联合总会
    中华全国文学艺术界联合会
    中华全国学生联合会
