Title: "New China Exhibition" Opens in Romanian Capital
Author:
Date: 1952-04-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, April 25th: The "New China Exhibition," organized by the Romanian Foreign Trade Association, opened on the 22nd at the exhibition building on Moghaiyou Street in Bucharest. Those attending the opening ceremony included: Parhon, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly; Groza, Chairman of the Council of Ministers; Pauk, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs; Luca, Secretary of the Romanian Workers' Party; Rădăceanu, Minister of Labor and Social Insurance; Constantinescu, Chairman of the State Planning Committee; Constantinescu-Iași, Vice Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly; Bunașiu, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Bârlădeanu, Minister of Foreign Trade; Marinescu, Minister of Domestic Commerce; Apostol, Chairman of the General Trade Union, and 400 representatives from various people's organizations and diplomatic representatives from various countries stationed in Bucharest.

At the opening ceremony, Novotny, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of Romania, said: Through this exhibition, the Romanian people will be able to more clearly understand the great achievements made by the Chinese people in the political, economic, and cultural fields under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong and the Chinese Communist Party with the assistance of the Soviet Union in the past two and a half years. To better understand the improvement of the living standards of the Chinese people. Liu Jingyu, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of our embassy in Bucharest, also spoke at the opening ceremony.

The Romanian people are very interested in this exhibition. On the 23rd alone, 18,653 people visited. Trade unions and other people's organizations across the country are organizing visiting groups in preparation for visiting on weekends and holidays.

### “新中国博览会”在罗马尼亚首都揭幕

1952-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    “新中国博览会”在罗马尼亚首都揭幕
    【新华社布拉格二十五日电】罗马尼亚对外贸易协会所主办的“新中国博览会”，二十二日在布加勒斯特的摩格亥犹街展览大楼开幕。出席开幕典礼的有：国民议会主席团主席巴洪、部长会议主席格罗查、部长会议副主席兼外交部长波克、罗马尼亚工人党书记卢加、劳动与社会保险部长拉达齐亚努、国家计划委员会主席康斯坦丁尼斯库、国民议会主席团副主席康斯坦丁尼斯库—阿希、外交部第一副部长布纳修、对外贸易部长巴尔拉蒂亚努、国内商业部长马林斯基、总工会主席阿波斯托尔等，以及各人民团体的四百个代表和各国驻布加勒斯特的外交代表。
    罗马尼亚对外贸易部副部长诺渥茨在开幕典礼上说：借着这个博览会，罗马尼亚人民就能够更清楚地了解中国人民两年半以来在英明的领袖毛泽东主席和中国共产党的领导下，在苏联的援助下，在政治、经济和文化各方面所取得的伟大成就。更清楚地了解中国人民生活水准提高的情况。我国驻布加勒斯特大使馆临时代办刘靖宇也曾在开幕典礼上讲话。
    罗马尼亚人民对于这个博览会很感兴趣。仅在二十三日一天，参观的就有一万八千六百五十三人。全国各地的工会和其他人民团体都正在组织参观团，准备在周末和假日前来参观。
