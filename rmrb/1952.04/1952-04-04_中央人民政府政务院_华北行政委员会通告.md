Title: Announcement from the State Administrative Council of the Central People's Government and the North China Administrative Committee
Author: 
Date: 1952-04-04
Page: 1

    Announcement from the State Administrative Council of the Central People's Government and the North China Administrative Committee
    Announcement: The North China Branch of the Supreme People's Court of the Central People's Government will officially open for business at No. 3 Wangfuma Hutong, Dongcheng, Beijing, on April 5th. Hereafter, all civil and criminal appeal cases from the five provinces of North China and the two municipalities of Beijing and Tianjin will be handled by the North China Branch of the Supreme People's Court. This is hereby announced.
                                        April 4, 1952

### 中央人民政府政务院  华北行政委员会通告

1952-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府政务院
    华北行政委员会通告
    中央人民政府最高人民法院华北分院，订于四月五日在北京东城王驸马胡同三号正式办公，嗣后所有华北五省及北京、天津二市民事、刑事上诉案件，统归最高人民法院华北分院处理。特此通告。
                                        一九五二年四月四日
