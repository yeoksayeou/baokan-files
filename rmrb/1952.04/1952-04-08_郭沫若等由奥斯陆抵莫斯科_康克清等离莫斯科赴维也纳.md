Title: Guo Moruo and Others Arrive in Moscow from Oslo; Kang Keqing and Others Leave Moscow for Vienna
Author:
Date: 1952-04-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 6th: Guo Moruo, Mao Dun, and other members of the Chinese delegation attending the World Peace Council Executive Bureau meeting arrived in Moscow from Oslo on the 4th. Representatives from the Soviet Peace Committee and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, as well as Yudin, Secretary-General of the Stalin International Peace Prize Committee for "Strengthening International Peace," and others, were present at the airport to welcome them.

According to Xinhua News Agency: The Chinese delegation attending the International Conference for the Defence of Children, led by Kang Keqing, left Moscow for Vienna by train on the evening of the 5th.

### 郭沫若等由奥斯陆抵莫斯科  康克清等离莫斯科赴维也纳

1952-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若等由奥斯陆抵莫斯科
    康克清等离莫斯科赴维也纳
    【新华社莫斯科六日电】出席世界和平理事会执行局会议的我国代表郭沫若、茅盾等一行，在四日从奥斯陆飞抵莫斯科。前往机场欢迎的有苏联拥护和平委员会和苏联对外文化协会的代表及“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金委员会秘书主任尤金等多人。
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：由康克清率领的我国出席国际保卫儿童大会的代表团，已在五日晚乘火车离莫斯科赴维也纳。
