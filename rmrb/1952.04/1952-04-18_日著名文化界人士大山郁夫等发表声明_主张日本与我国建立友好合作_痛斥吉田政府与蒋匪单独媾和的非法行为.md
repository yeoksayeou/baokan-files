Title: Prominent Japanese Cultural Figures Oyama Ikuo and Others Issue Statement Advocating the Establishment of Friendly Cooperation Between Japan and China, Condemning the Illegal Act of the Yoshida Government's Separate Peace Treaty with the Chiang Kai-shek Bandits
Author:
Date: 1952-04-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 17th dispatch: Oyama Ikuo, Wataru Kaji, Sumio Takakuwa, Tokuichiro Tan, Toitsu Nawa, Giichiro Hirano, and many other prominent Japanese cultural figures recently signed and issued a "Joint Statement by Intellectuals Regarding Friendly Cooperation with China," denouncing the illegal act of the Yoshida government in making a separate peace treaty with the Kuomintang bandit gang in Taiwan, violating the will of the Japanese people, and pointing out the importance of friendly cooperation between Japan and China.

The statement pointed out: If Japan does not befriend the Chinese people and does not engage in economic cooperation with China, Japan will have no independence or Asia any peace. The statement said: The Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China has repeatedly stated in its statement on the peace treaty with Japan that it opposes attempts to restrict trade between Japan and China. If trade between China and Japan is artificially blockaded, it will be like cutting off the economic throat of Japan, blocking the path of Japan's peaceful development.

The statement calls on the Japanese people to correctly understand China, abandon the prejudices of the past militarist era, and attach importance to and develop the prospects for reasonable Sino-Japanese cultural cooperation.

The statement concludes: The clause in the Japanese Constitution renouncing war must first and foremost be implemented in relations with China.

### 日著名文化界人士大山郁夫等发表声明  主张日本与我国建立友好合作  痛斥吉田政府与蒋匪单独媾和的非法行为

1952-04-18
第4版()
专栏：

    日著名文化界人士大山郁夫等发表声明
    主张日本与我国建立友好合作
    痛斥吉田政府与蒋匪单独媾和的非法行为
    【新华社十七日讯】东京消息：大山郁夫、鹿地亘、高桑纯夫、淡德三郎、名和统一、平野义太郎等许多日本著名的文化界人士，最近签名发表了“文化人关于与中国友好合作的联合声明”，斥责吉田政府违背日本人民意志与台湾国民党匪帮单独媾和的非法行为，并指出日本与中国友好合作的重要性。
    声明指出：日本如果不与中国人民友好亲善，不与中国实行经济合作，就没有日本的自立与亚洲的和平。声明说：中华人民共和国中央人民政府关于对日媾和的声明，曾再三表示反对限制日本与中国进行贸易的企图。中、日两国的贸易如果遭到人为的封锁，正如割断日本经济的咽喉一样，杜绝了日本和平发展的道路。
    声明要求日本人民正确认识中国，抛弃过去军国主义时代的偏见，重视和开辟合理的中日文化合作的前途。
    声明最后说：日本宪法所规定的放弃战争的条款，首先必须在与中国的关系中贯彻实行。
