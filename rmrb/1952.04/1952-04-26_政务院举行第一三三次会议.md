Title: Government Administration Council Holds 133rd Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-04-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 25th—The 133rd Government Administration Council meeting of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 25th. After hearing the explanation made by Xie Juezai, Minister of the Interior of the Central People's Government, the meeting approved the Ministry's key work points for 1952.

### 政务院举行第一三三次会议

1952-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一三三次会议
    【新华社二十五日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百三十三次政务会议在二十五日下午举行。会议在听取中央人民政府内务部谢觉哉部长所作的说明后，批准了该部一九五二年工作要点。
