Title: China's delegation to the International Economic Conference concluded trade agreements with French businesses worth 8 million pounds, and also concluded trade agreements with countries such as Switzerland and Ceylon

Author:
Date: 1952-04-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 14th: The Chinese delegation to the International Economic Conference concluded a trade agreement with French businessmen during the conference, with a transaction value of 8 million pounds. China will import metals, machinery, pharmaceuticals, pharmaceutical raw materials, etc. from France, and will export silk, tea, animal products, oils, plant seeds, handicrafts, manganese ore and other products to France. The Chinese delegation also concluded trade agreements with Switzerland, Ceylon and other countries.

### 我国出席国际经济会议代表团  与法商成交货物价值八百万英镑  还和瑞士、锡兰等国缔结了贸易协定

1952-04-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我国出席国际经济会议代表团
    与法商成交货物价值八百万英镑
    还和瑞士、锡兰等国缔结了贸易协定
    【新华社莫斯科十四日电】我国出席国际经济会议的代表团，在会议期间和法国商人缔结了一项贸易协定，成交的货物价值达八百万英镑。我国将由法国输入金属、机器、药品、制药原料等，并将对法国输出丝绸、茶叶、畜产、油类、植物子实、手工艺品、锰砂及其他产品。我国代表团还和瑞士、锡兰等国缔结了贸易协定。
