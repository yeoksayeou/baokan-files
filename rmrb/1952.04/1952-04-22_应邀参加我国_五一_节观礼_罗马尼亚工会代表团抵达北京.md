Title: Romanian Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing to Attend China's "May Day" Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1952-04-22
Page: 4

    Invited to Attend China's "May Day" Celebrations
    Romanian Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, April 21st] The Romanian trade union delegation, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the May Day celebrations in our country, arrived in Beijing by train at noon on the 21st. The delegation is composed of Stoia Elina, Chairman of the Textile and Leather Workers' Union and member of the Romanian General Trade Union Council, as the head of the delegation, and members Gania Peter and Aleksink Shemin. Among those who went to the station to welcome them were Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Xu Zhizhen, Minister of the Organization Department, Jin Ziming, Deputy Minister of the International Liaison Department, Zhang Xiuzhu, Deputy Minister of the Culture and Education Department, Wang Rong, Deputy Minister of the Wage Department, and representatives from the National Committee of the Chinese Textile Workers' Union. Romanian Ambassador to China Rudenko and Romanian Embassy Counselor Sirad also welcomed them at the station.

### 应邀参加我国“五一”节观礼  罗马尼亚工会代表团抵达北京

1952-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀参加我国“五一”节观礼
    罗马尼亚工会代表团抵达北京
    【新华社二十一日讯】应中华全国总工会的邀请前来我国参加五一节观礼的罗马尼亚工会代表团，已于二十一日中午乘车抵达北京。代表团由团长罗马尼亚总工会理事、纺织皮革工会主席斯托雅·爱丽娜和团员加尼亚·彼得及阿列克辛克·谢明组成。前往车站欢迎的有：中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一、组织部部长许之桢、国际联络部副部长金子明、文教部副部长张修竹、工资部副部长王榕及中国纺织工会全国委员会代表等多人。罗马尼亚驻华大使鲁登科、罗马尼亚大使馆参赞席拉德也到站欢迎。
