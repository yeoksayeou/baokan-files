Title: After Attending the International Economic Conference, Vietnamese and Argentine Representatives Arrive in Beijing for a Visit / Head of Chinese Delegation Nan Hanchen and Others Return from the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1952-04-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 27th: Vietnamese and Argentine representatives who attended the International Economic Conference in Moscow arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 27th for a visit. Those who welcomed them at the airport included: Ji Chao Ding and Liu Ningyi, members of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Xiang Shuxiang, member of the Preparatory Office of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Preparatory Committee; Sun Xiaocun, Standing Committee member of the Democratic National Construction Association; Fu Huating, Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce; and Chen Dingmin, professor at Tsinghua University, among others. Jiang Ming, Deputy Director of the Foreign Trade Department of the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government, and Zhou Liang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, also welcomed them at the airport.
The list of representatives from each country is as follows:
	Trinh Van Binh, Economist of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Deputy Minister of Finance
	Nguyen Son Ha, Vietnamese Industrialist
	Nguyen Thanh Ha, Representative of the Vietnamese Farmers' National Salvation Association
	Dang Van Vinh, Director of the Office of the Finance and Economics Committee of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
	Olivari, Argentine Industrialist
	Mrs. Olivari
Xinhua News Agency, April 27th: Nan Hanchen, head of the Chinese delegation to the Moscow International Economic Conference, along with six members of the delegation, returned to Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 27th. Those who welcomed them at the airport included: Ji Chao Ding and Liu Ningyi, members of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Yao Yilin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government; Hu Jingyun, Deputy Governor of the People's Bank; Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; Ji Fang, Central Committee member of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party; Xiang Shuxiang, member of the Preparatory Office of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Preparatory Committee; Sun Xiaocun, Standing Committee member of the Democratic National Construction Association; and Fu Huating, Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce, among others.

### 出席国际经济会议后来我国参观  越南和阿根廷代表昨到达北京  我国代表团团长南汉宸等自苏联返国

1952-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    出席国际经济会议后来我国参观
    越南和阿根廷代表昨到达北京
    我国代表团团长南汉宸等自苏联返国
    【新华社二十七日讯】出席在莫斯科举行的国际经济会议的越南和阿根廷代表，于二十七日下午乘飞机抵达北京参观。到机场欢迎的有：国际贸易促进委员会委员冀朝鼎、刘宁一，中华全国工商业联合会筹备委员会筹备处委员项叔翔，民主建国会总会常务委员孙晓村，北京市工商业联合会主任委员傅华亭，清华大学教授陈定民等多人。中央人民政府贸易部国外贸易司副司长江明、越南民主共和国驻华代表团临时代办周亮也到机场欢迎。
    各国代表名单如下：
    郑文炳  越南民主共和国经济学家、财政部副部长
    阮山河  越南工业家
    阮清河  越南农民救国会代表
    邓文荣  越南民主共和国财经委员会办公厅主任
    奥利瓦里  阿根廷实业家
    奥利瓦里夫人
    【新华社二十七日讯】我国出席莫斯科国际经济会议的代表团团长南汉宸偕团员六人，于二十七日下午搭乘飞机返抵北京。到机场欢迎的有：国际贸易促进委员会委员冀朝鼎、刘宁一，中央人民政府贸易部副部长姚依林，人民银行副行长胡景澐，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，农工民主党中央委员季方，中华全国工商业联合会筹备委员会筹备处委员项叔翔，民主建国会总会常务委员孙晓村，北京市工商业联合会主任委员傅华亭等多人。
