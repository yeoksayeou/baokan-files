Readers' Letters

    Representatives of People from All Walks of Life in North China Express Determination to Crush American Bacteriological Warfare
    -- Summary of Readers' Letters
    The newspaper continues to receive letters from representatives of people from all walks of life in various regions. In these letters, they expressed their firm determination to extinguish the poisonous flames of American bacteriological warfare.
    The entire body of representatives to the 11th People's Representative Conference of Changzhi County, Shanxi Province, wrote: "We unanimously protest the heinous crimes of the American bandits in spreading bacteria. The people of our entire county are determined to increase grain production by 1.5 dou per mu this year, do a good job in supporting the army and cultivating land on their behalf, and guarantee that each person will prepare one mu of soil to retain moisture. We will add 1,200 waterwheels and dig 2,000 water wells throughout the county, expand hemp fields by 3,000 mu, and promote 260 new-style single-blade plows. We will use these practical actions to support the Chinese People's Volunteers." The entire body of representatives to the First Session, Seventh Meeting of the People's Representative Conference of Yongji County, Shanxi Province, wrote: "The 150,000 people of our county unanimously hate the crimes of the American aggressors with utmost resentment. We are determined to do a good job in drought prevention and resistance, developing water sources, and repairing and digging wells, using the practical actions of expanding irrigated land by 50,000 mu, retaining moisture on 100,000 mu, producing 77,280,000 jin of wheat, and producing 7,430,000 jin of cotton to support the Chinese People's Volunteers." The entire body of representatives to the Third Session, Second Meeting of the People's Representative Conference of Wuyi County, Hebei Province, wrote: "We absolutely cannot tolerate the atrocities of the American aggressors. We guarantee to thoroughly implement our county's 1952 patriotic production increase plan and do a good job in drought prevention and resistance to support the Volunteers in combat." The entire body of representatives to the Third Session, First Meeting of the People's Representative Conference of Jiaohe County, Hebei Province, wrote: "We represent the 300,000 people of the entire county and resolutely support the solemn statement of Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai protesting the American aggressors' spreading of bacteria. We call on the people of the entire county to do a good job in drought prevention, resistance and spring plowing, launch a patriotic production increase campaign, increase the material basis for resisting America and aiding Korea, and thoroughly smash the American aggressors' conspiracy to launch bacteriological warfare."

### 华北各地各界人民代表会议代表  表示粉碎美国细菌战的决心——读者来信综述

1952-04-02
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    华北各地各界人民代表会议代表
    表示粉碎美国细菌战的决心
    ——读者来信综述
    本报继续收到各地各界人民代表会议代表的来信。他们在这些信中表示了坚决扑灭美国细菌战毒焰的决心。
    山西省长治县第十一次各界人民代表会议全体代表来信说：“我们一致抗议美国强盗撒布细菌的滔天罪行。我们全县人民，今年决心努力使每亩土地增产粮食一斗五升，做好拥军代耕工作，每人保证做好一亩保墒地。我们要在全县增加水车一千二百部、打水井两千眼，扩大麻田三千亩，推广新式农具单把犁二百六十张。我们要以这些实际行动，支援中国人民志愿军。”山西省永济县第一届第七次各界人民代表会议全体代表来信说：“我县十五万人民，一致切齿痛恨美国侵略者的罪行。我们决心做好防旱抗旱、开发水源、修井开井等工作，用扩大水地面积五万亩、保墒十万亩、生产小麦七千七百二十八万斤、生产棉花七百四十三万斤的实际行动，支援中国人民志愿军。”河北省武邑县第三届第二次各界人民代表会议全体代表来信说：“我们绝对不能容忍美国侵略者的暴行。我们保证彻底实现我县一九五二年爱国丰产计划，作好防旱抗旱工作，以支持志愿军作战。”河北省交河县第三届第一次各界人民代表会议全体代表来信说：“我们代表全县三十万人民，坚决拥护外交部周恩来部长抗议美国侵略者撒布细菌的严正声明。我们号召全县人民作好防旱抗旱和春耕工作，开展爱国丰产运动，增加抗美援朝的物质基础，以彻底粉碎美国侵略者发动细菌战的阴谋。”
