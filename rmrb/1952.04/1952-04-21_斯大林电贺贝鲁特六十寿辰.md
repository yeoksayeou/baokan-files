Title: Stalin Sends Congratulatory Message to Bierut on His Sixtieth Birthday
Author:
Date: 1952-04-21
Page: 4

    Stalin Sends Congratulatory Message to Bierut on His Sixtieth Birthday
    [Xinhua News Agency, 20th] TASS, Moscow, 19th: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, J.V. Stalin, sent a telegram to President Bierut of the Polish Republic, congratulating him on his sixtieth birthday. The telegram reads as follows:
    To President Bierut of the Polish Republic:
    On the occasion of your sixtieth birthday, Comrade President, please allow me to pay my respects to you, the great builder and leader of a unified, independent, and people's democratic new Poland.
    I sincerely wish you good health and success in your activities to benefit the fraternal Polish people and further consolidate the friendship between the Polish Republic and the Soviet Union, in order to promote peace throughout the world.
    J. Stalin
    [Xinhua News Agency, 20th] TASS, Moscow, 19th: Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Shvernik, sent a telegram to President Bierut of the Polish Republic, congratulating him on his sixtieth birthday. The telegram reads as follows:
    To Comrade President Bierut of the Polish Republic:
    On the occasion of your sixtieth birthday, I request you, Comrade President, to accept my heartfelt congratulations. I wish you good health and more success in your activities to benefit the Polish people and strengthen the unbreakable friendship between our two peoples.
    N. Shvernik

### 斯大林电贺贝鲁特六十寿辰

1952-04-21
第4版()
专栏：

    斯大林电贺贝鲁特六十寿辰
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十九日讯：苏联部长会议主席约·维·斯大林致电祝贺波兰共和国总统贝鲁特六十寿辰。电文如下：
    波兰共和国总统贝鲁特：
    值您六十寿辰之际，总统同志，请允许我向您，统一独立的人民民主的新波兰的伟大建设者与领袖，致敬。
    我衷心地祝您身体健康，在造福波兰兄弟人民和进一步巩固波兰共和国与苏联之间的友谊以促进全世界和平的活动中，取得胜利。
    约·斯大林
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十九日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席什维尔尼克致电祝贺波兰共和国总统贝鲁特六十寿辰。电文如下：
    波兰共和国总统贝鲁特同志：
    值您六十寿辰之际，我请求您，总统同志，接受我衷心的祝贺。我祝您身体健康，在造福波兰人民和巩固我们两国人民之间的牢不可破的友谊的活动中，取得更多的胜利。
    尼·什维尔尼克
