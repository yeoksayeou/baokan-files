Title: Korean Armistice Talks Still Make No Progress
Author: 
Date: 1952-04-16
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, April 15th: On April 15th, during the third agenda item subcommittee meeting of the Korean armistice talks, the other side still expressed no new opinions, and the meeting made no progress. The other side also did not give any further response to our proposal for the swift resumption of the fourth agenda item's staff meeting.

### 朝鲜停战谈判仍然无进展

1952-04-16
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判仍然无进展
    【新华社开城十五日电】十五日朝鲜停战谈判第三项议程小组委员会上，对方仍然表示无任何新意见，会议无进展。对于第四项议程参谋会议迅速复会的我方建议，对方也未作进一步的答复。
