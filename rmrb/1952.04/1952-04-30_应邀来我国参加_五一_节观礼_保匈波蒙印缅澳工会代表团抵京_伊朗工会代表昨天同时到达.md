Title: Union Delegations from Bulgaria, Hungary, Poland, Mongolia, India, Burma, and Australia Arrive in Beijing to Attend "May Day" Celebrations; Iranian Union Representatives Arrive Yesterday Simultaneously
Author:
Date: 1952-04-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: Bulgarian, Hungarian, Polish, Mongolian, Indian (second group), and Burmese union delegations, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the "May Day" celebrations in our country, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of April 29th. Soviet Union trade union delegation member He Jieyev (Chairman of the Trade Union Council of the Uzbek Republic), Romanian trade union delegation members Inger Maria (advanced textile worker) and Geodore Sgu Nicolae (advanced train driver), and Iranian trade union representatives also arrived at the same time.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included: Zhu Xuefan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Chen Shaomin, Chairman of the National Committee of the China Textile Workers' Union; Xu Zhizhen, Minister of the Organization Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Dong Xin, First Deputy Director of the Policy Research Office; Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department; Zhang Lizhi, Deputy Minister of the Organization Department; Wang Rong, Deputy Minister of the Wage Department; Wu Ping, Deputy Minister of the Women Workers' Department, and others.

Also at the airport to welcome them were Mongolian Ambassador to China Jalkhasaikhan, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Polish Embassy Chargé d'affaires Gula, Romanian Embassy Counselor Sirad, Hungarian Embassy Counselor Sibka, Soviet Embassy Attache Guo Huali, and others. Also welcoming them were Elina, head of the Romanian trade union delegation, and Jiujin, secretary of the Soviet trade union delegation, who arrived in Beijing a few days ago.

### 应邀来我国参加“五一”节观礼  保匈波蒙印缅澳工会代表团抵京  伊朗工会代表昨天同时到达

1952-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国参加“五一”节观礼
    保匈波蒙印缅澳工会代表团抵京
    伊朗工会代表昨天同时到达
    【新华社二十九日讯】应中华全国总工会的邀请前来我国参加“五一”节观礼的保加利亚、匈牙利、波兰、蒙古、印度（第二批）、缅甸工会代表团已于二十九日下午先后乘飞机到达北京。苏联工会代表团团员贺捷耶夫（乌兹别克共和国工会理事会主席）、罗马尼亚工会代表团团员英格·玛利亚（先进纺织女工）和杰奥多列斯古·尼古拉耶（先进火车司机）及伊朗工会代表也同时到达。
    前往机场欢迎的有：中华全国总工会副主席朱学范、中国纺织工会全国委员会主席陈少敏、中华全国总工会组织部部长许之桢、政策研究室第一副主任董昕、国际联络部副部长金子明、组织部副部长张立之、工资部副部长王榕、女工部副部长吴平等人。
    蒙古人民共和国驻华大使贾尔卡赛汗、保加利亚大使彼得科夫、波兰大使馆临时代办古拉、罗马尼亚大使馆参赞席拉德、匈牙利大使馆参赞席布卡、苏联大使馆随员郭华立等也到机场欢迎。欢迎的还有日前抵京的罗马尼亚工会代表团团长爱丽娜、苏联工会代表团秘书玖金。
