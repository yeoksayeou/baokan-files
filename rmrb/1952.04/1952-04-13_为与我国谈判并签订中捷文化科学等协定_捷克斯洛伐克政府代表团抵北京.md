Title: Czechoslovak Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing to Negotiate and Sign Sino-Czechoslovak Cultural, Scientific, and Other Agreements
Author:
Date: 1952-04-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 12th—The Government of the Czechoslovak Republic, in order to negotiate and sign agreements with the Central People's Government of China on culture, science, technology, post and telecommunications, etc., has dispatched a government delegation to China headed by Mr. Kopecky, Minister of Propaganda and Culture of the Czechoslovak Republic. The delegation of seven arrived in Beijing by train at 6:25 PM on the 12th. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Zhu Xuefan, Minister of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government, Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Song Shaowen, Director of the Planning Bureau of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council, Sha Kefu, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Culture, Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council, Chen Zhongjing, Deputy Director, Xu Yixin, Deputy Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wei Baoshan, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Also present at the station to welcome the delegation were Soviet Ambassador to China, Roshchin; Koenig, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic to China, with the title of Ambassador; Mongolian Ambassador to China, Jalkhasaikhan; Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Petkov; Korean Ambassador to China, Kwon O-jik; Indonesian Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China, Isah Marti; Polish Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China, Dowding; Zhou Liang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China; Szibka, Counselor of the Hungarian Embassy in China; Ahmed Ali, Counselor of the Pakistani Embassy in China; Saifani, First Secretary of the Indian Embassy in China; and Charlie Pirlou, Third Secretary of the Burmese Embassy in China.

Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Kanzala, and all members of the embassy also went to the station to welcome the delegation.

### 为与我国谈判并签订中捷文化科学等协定  捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团抵北京

1952-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    为与我国谈判并签订中捷文化科学等协定
    捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团抵北京
    【新华社十二日讯】捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府为与我中央人民政府谈判并签订中捷文化、科学、技术、邮电等协定，特派遣以捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府宣传与文化部部长柯别茨基为首的政府代表团前来我国。该团一行七人已于十二日下午六时二十五分乘车抵京。赴站欢迎者有我中央人民政府邮电部朱学范部长，外交部伍修权副部长，政务院财经委员会计划局宋劭文局长，文化部办公厅沙可夫主任，外交部办公厅阎宝航副主任，政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局洪深局长、陈忠经副局长，外交部苏联东欧司徐以新副司长、外交部交际处魏宝善副处长。
    前往车站欢迎者尚有苏联驻华大使罗申、德意志民主共和国驻华外交使团大使衔团长柯尼希、蒙古驻华大使贾尔卡赛汗、保加利亚驻华大使彼得科夫、朝鲜驻华大使权五稷、印度尼西亚驻华大使馆临时代办伊沙·玛蒂、波兰驻华大使馆临时代办道定、越南民主共和国驻华代表团临时代办周亮、匈牙利驻华大使馆参赞席布卡、巴基斯坦驻华大使馆参赞阿默德·艾礼、印度驻华大使馆一等秘书赛发尼、缅甸驻华大使馆三等秘书查理辟鲁等。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使康萨拉及全体使馆人员亦均前往车站欢迎。
