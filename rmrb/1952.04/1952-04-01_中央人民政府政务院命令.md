    Order of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government

    The "Regulations on Recovering赃款赃物 from Corrupt Elements" of the Central Anti-Corruption Inspection Committee have been approved by the 130th Government Administration Council Meeting of this Council. It is hereby promulgated, and it is hoped that all regions will comply with it.

    Premier Zhou Enlai
    March 31, 1952
