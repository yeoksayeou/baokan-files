Title: Chinese Delegation to International Children's Protection Conference in Vienna Arrives in Moscow Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-04-04
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 3rd. The Chinese delegation to the International Children's Protection Conference in Vienna, led by head of delegation Kang Keqing, arrived in Moscow on April 3rd. Those who welcomed them at the station included: Popova, Chairman of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee; Petrova, Secretary-General of the Committee; Vaskov, Deputy Director of the First Far East Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Bastoev, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Yerofeyev, Minister of the Far East Department of the Soviet Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. Ho Quei, head of the delegation from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the International Children's Protection Conference, who had arrived in Moscow earlier, and Liu Ying, wife of Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Zhang Wentian, also welcomed them at the station.

### 赴维也纳出席国际保卫儿童大会  我国代表团昨天抵莫斯科

1952-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    赴维也纳出席国际保卫儿童大会
    我国代表团昨天抵莫斯科
     【新华社莫斯科三日电】我国出席在维也纳举行的国际保卫儿童大会的代表团，由团长康克清率领，在四月三日抵达莫斯科。到车站欢迎的有：苏联妇女反法西斯委员会主席波波娃、该会秘书长彼得罗娃、苏联外交部第一远东司副司长瓦斯科夫、外交部交际司副司长巴斯托也夫、苏联对外文化协会远东部部长叶罗非也夫等人。先期抵达莫斯科的越南民主共和国出席国际保卫儿童大会代表团团长荷桂、及我国驻苏大使张闻天夫人刘英等也到站欢迎。
