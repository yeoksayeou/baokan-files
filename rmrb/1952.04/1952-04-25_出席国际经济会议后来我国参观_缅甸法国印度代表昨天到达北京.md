Title: Representatives from Burma, France, and India Arrive in Beijing Yesterday After Attending International Economic Conference for Visits to China
Author:
Date: 1952-04-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 24th—Ten representatives from Burma, France, and India, who attended the International Economic Conference in Moscow, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 24th for visits to China. Chen Hansheng and Lu Xuzhang, members of the Chinese delegation to the International Economic Conference, accompanied them. Those who welcomed them at the airport included Ma Yinchu, Vice Chairman of the China Economic Society; Ji Chaoding, member of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Xiang Shuxiang, member of the Preparatory Office of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce; Sun Xiaocun, Standing Committee Member of the Democratic National Construction Association; Fu Huating, Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce; and Chen Dingmin, professor at Tsinghua University, among others. Jiang Ming, Deputy Director of the Foreign Trade Department of the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government, and U Thaw, Third Secretary of the Burmese Embassy in China, also welcomed them at the airport.

The list of representatives from each country is as follows:

    U Mya: Governor of the State Bank of Burma, Member of Parliament
    U Ba Che: Burmese Industrialist
    U Khin Han: Burmese Businessman
    U Hla: Burmese Publisher
    U Aye Lwin: Lecturer in Economics at Rangoon University
    U Cho Min: Governor of the Central Commercial Bank of Burma
    Lebrun: Secretary of the French General Confederation of Labour, Engineer
    Hakutiamba: Lebrun's Private Secretary
    Janik: Indian Peasant Leader
    De Zula: Janik's Private Secretary

### 出席国际经济会议后来我国参观  缅甸法国印度代表昨天到达北京

1952-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    出席国际经济会议后来我国参观
    缅甸法国印度代表昨天到达北京
    【新华社二十四日讯】出席在莫斯科举行的国际经济会议的缅甸、法国、印度代表共十人，于二十四日下午乘飞机到达北京参观，同行的有我国出席国际经济会议的代表团团员陈翰笙、卢绪章。到机场欢迎的有：中国经济学会副主任委员马寅初、国际贸易促进委员会委员冀朝鼎、中华全国工商业联合会筹备委员会筹备处委员项叔翔、民主建国会总会常务委员孙晓村、北京市工商业联合会主任委员傅华亭、清华大学教授陈定民等多人。中央人民政府贸易部国外贸易司副司长江明，缅甸驻华大使馆三等秘书吴陶也到机场欢迎。
