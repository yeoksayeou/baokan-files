Title: At the Korean Armistice Talks Delegates' Meeting, US Side Requests to Enter Administrative Session
Author:
Date: 1952-04-29
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, April 28th: At the Korean Armistice Talks delegates' meeting on the 28th, the other side requested to enter an administrative session to discuss a comprehensive solution to the remaining issues on the agenda. Our side's representative indicated: "Based on our consistent position of rapidly reaching a ceasefire, our side is willing to resolve issues in any meeting format. The resolution of issues does not lie in changes to the meeting format, but in the sincerity of both sides in resolving the issues. Since your side believes that the administrative session format is conducive to a comprehensive resolution of the issues, our side agrees to adopt the administrative session format to seek a reasonable resolution of all outstanding issues. When either side wishes to terminate the administrative session format, the administrative session should be terminated." The April 28th administrative session of the delegates' meeting concluded at 2:36 PM. The date and time for the resumption of the meeting will be notified by the liaison officers.

### 朝鲜停战谈判双方代表团大会上  美方要求进入行政性会议

1952-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判双方代表团大会上
    美方要求进入行政性会议
    【新华社开城二十八日电】二十八日朝鲜停战谈判双方代表团大会上，对方要求进入行政性会议，讨论各项议程中剩余问题的全盘解决方案。我方代表指示：“我方本着迅速达成停战的一贯立场，愿在任何会议形式中解决问题，问题的解决不在于会议形式的变化，而在于双方解决问题的诚意。既然你方认为行政性会议的形式有助于问题的全面解决，我方同意采取行政性会议的形式，寻求所有未决问题的合理解决。当双方中任何一方希望终止行政性会议的形式时，行政性会议即应终止。”二十八日双方代表团大会行政性会议在下午二时三十六分结束。大会复会的日期与时间，将由联络官通知。
