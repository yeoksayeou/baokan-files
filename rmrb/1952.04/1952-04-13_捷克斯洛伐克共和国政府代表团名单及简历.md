Title: List of Members and Biographies of the Government Delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic
Author:
Date: 1952-04-13
Page: 1

    List of Members and Biographies of the Government Delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic
    Head of Delegation:
    Kopeczki: Minister of Propaganda and Culture of the Czechoslovak Republic, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party. He is one of President Gottwald's closest comrades-in-arms, and has been an active organizer of the Czechoslovak Communist Party since 1920. He was previously engaged in journalism and served as editor of various newspapers of the Czechoslovak Communist Party. He assisted President Gottwald in exposing and crushing the conspiracies of the traitor Slánský and others at several meetings of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party from 1950 to 1952.
    Members:
    Dr. Norman: Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of the Czechoslovak Republic.
    Konsala: Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Czechoslovak Republic to China.
    Möller: Deputy Minister of Light Machine Industry of the Czechoslovak Republic.
    Dr. Šimovič: Director of the Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Czechoslovak Republic. He served as Ambassador of the Czechoslovak Republic to the People's Republic of Bulgaria from 1948 to 1951.

### 捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团名单及简历

1952-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府
    代表团名单及简历
    团长柯别茨基捷克斯洛伐克共和国宣传与文化部部长，捷共中央政治局委员。他是哥特瓦尔德总统最亲密的战友之一，一九二○年起即成为捷共的积极组织者。他曾经从事新闻工作，担任过捷共各种报纸的编辑。他曾在一九五○年至一九五二年捷共中央多次会议中协助哥特瓦尔德总统竭力揭发和粉碎了叛徒斯兰斯基等的阴谋活动。团员诺曼博士捷克斯洛伐克共和国邮电部部长。康萨拉捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻华特命全权大使。摩勒捷克斯洛伐克共和国轻机器工业部副部长。石莫维契博士捷克斯洛伐克共和国外交部司长。一九四八年至一九五一年曾任捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻保加利亚人民共和国大使。
