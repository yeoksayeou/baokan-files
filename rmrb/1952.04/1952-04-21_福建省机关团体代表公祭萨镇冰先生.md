Title: Representatives of Organizations and Groups in Fujian Province Hold Public Memorial for Mr. Sa Zhenbing
Author:
Date: 1952-04-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, 20th—More than 300 representatives from the Fujian Provincial People's Government, the Fujian Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Fujian Provincial Committee of the People's Representative Conference, various democratic parties in Fujian Province, and various people's organizations held a public memorial service at 8:00 a.m. on the 19th for the late Mr. Sa Zhenbing, member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, member of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission, member of the Fujian Provincial People's Government, and senior figure in the navy. After the public memorial, all the representatives escorted the coffin to the Meiting Mountain Cemetery in the western suburbs of Fuzhou for burial.

After Mr. Sa Zhenbing passed away in Fuzhou on April 10th, a funeral committee composed of Chen Yi and 16 others was immediately formed to oversee the funeral arrangements. Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Li Jishen of the Central People's Government, Premier Zhou Enlai of the Government Administration Council, and Rao Shushi, Chen Yi, Su Yu, Jiang Guangnai, Cai Tingkai, Chen Mingshu, Chen Jiageng, and other figures, as well as various overseas Chinese groups from Fujian Province in Nanyang, sent telegrams of condolence to Mr. Sa Zhenbing's family.

### 福建省机关团体代表公祭萨镇冰先生

1952-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    福建省机关团体代表公祭萨镇冰先生
    【新华社福州二十日电】福建省人民政府、中国人民解放军福建军区、福建省各界人民代表会议协商委员会、福建省各民主党派和各人民团体代表共三百多人，在十九日上午八时公祭已故中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会委员、中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会委员、福建省人民政府委员、海军前辈萨镇冰先生。公祭完毕后，全体代表送灵柩到福州市西郊梅亭山墓地安葬。
    萨镇冰先生于四月十日在福州市病逝后，当即由陈毅等十七人组成治丧委员会，主持丧葬事宜。中央人民政府毛泽东主席、李济深副主席、政务院周恩来总理和饶漱石、陈毅、粟裕、蒋光鼐、蔡廷锴、陈铭枢、陈嘉庚等，以及远在南洋的福建省各华侨团体，都曾电萨镇冰先生家属致唁。
