Title: Council of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers Continues Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-04-19
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, April 18th: TASS reports: The Council of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers continued its meeting on April 17th.
    The morning session discussed the draft resolution on Professor Brandweiner's report on the results of the investigation into war crimes committed by the American armed forces in Korea and the use of bacteriological weapons by the Americans in Korea and China.

### 国际民主法律工作者协会理事会继续开会

1952-04-19
第1版()
专栏：

    国际民主法律工作者协会理事会继续开会
    【新华社十八日讯】塔斯社维也纳讯：国际民主法律工作者协会理事会四月十七日继续开会。
    上午会议对于布兰德魏纳教授提出的调查美国武装部队在朝鲜的战争罪行和美国人在朝鲜及中国使用细菌武器一事的结果的报告，讨论了决议草案。
