Title: Guo Moruo, Mao Dun, and Others Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-04-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 13th: Guo Moruo, Mao Dun, and the other five members of the Chinese delegation who attended the World Peace Council Executive Bureau meeting in Oslo, returned to Beijing by plane on the 12th. Those who greeted them at the airport included Peng Zhen, Lu Dingyi, Zhang Hanfu, Cai Tingkai, Mei Ru'ao, Li Siguang, Tao Menghe, Liu Guan'yi, Dong Yueqian, Wu Maosun, Shao Quanlin, Hong Shen, Liao Mengxing, Wu Xueqian, Qu Tangliang, Yuan Baohua, and dozens of others.

### 郭沫若茅盾等返京

1952-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若茅盾等返京
    【新华社十三日讯】出席世界和平理事会执行局奥斯陆会议的我国代表郭沫若、茅盾等一行五人，于十二日乘飞机返抵北京。到机场欢迎的有：彭真、陆定一、章汉夫、蔡廷锴、梅汝璈、李四光、陶孟和、刘贯一、董越千、吴茂荪、邵荃麟、洪深、廖梦醒、吴学谦、区棠亮、袁葆华等数十人。
