Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Invitations to Trade Union Representatives from Various Countries to Attend the "May Day" Festival in Beijing; Trade Union Organizations from the Soviet Union and Other Countries Invite Chinese Trade Union Delegations to Participate in Celebration Ceremonies

Author: 
Date: 1952-04-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 7th—The All-China Federation of Trade Unions has sent invitations to trade union delegations from the following countries to come to China and participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebration ceremony held in Beijing this year. The worker organizations to which the invitations were sent include: the trade union federations of the Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, Albania, the German Democratic Republic, North Korea, Mongolia, and Vietnam; the Indian Federation of Labour, the All-India Trade Union Congress, and the Indian National Trade Union Congress in India; the All-Indonesia Central Trade Union Organization, the Indonesian Trade Union Federation, the Central Organization of Indonesian Workers, and the Indonesian Islamic Trade Union in Indonesia; the Pakistan Trade Union Federation and the Pakistan Labour Federation in Pakistan; the Trade Union Congress of Burma, the Trade Union Congress (Burma), the All-Burma Government Employees' Union, the All-Burma Inland Water Transport Workers' Union, and the All-Burma Railway Workers' Union in Burma; the Ceylon Federation of Trade Unions and other trade union organizations in Ceylon; the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, the Japan Joint Struggle Council for Wage Increases, and the All-Japan Federation of Industrial Labor Unions in Japan; the Australian Council of Trade Unions and the Waterside Workers' Federation of Australia in Australia. In addition, trade union organizations from the Middle East, Near East, and North Africa, trade union organizations from New Zealand, and representatives of workers from the Sino-British Friendship Association and Denmark and Sweden have also been invited.

After sending out the invitations, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions has successively received enthusiastic replies from trade union organizations of various countries, stating that they are actively selecting representatives to prepare for their journey to China.

Xinhua News Agency, April 7th—The All-China Federation of Trade Unions has received telegrams from trade union organizations of the Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, and the German Democratic Republic inviting the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to send delegations to participate in the "May Day" celebration ceremonies in their respective countries this year. The Czechoslovak Trade Union Federation also invited our trade union delegation to participate in the 7th anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia after participating in the "May Day" Labor Day celebration. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions is actively preparing to send delegations.

### 中华全国总工会发出请帖  邀各国工会代表来京参加“五一”节  苏联等国工会组织邀请我工会代表团参加庆祝典礼

1952-04-08
第1版()
专栏：

    中华全国总工会发出请帖
    邀各国工会代表来京参加“五一”节
    苏联等国工会组织邀请我工会代表团参加庆祝典礼
    【新华社七日讯】中华全国总工会已经发出请帖，邀请下列各国的工会代表团前来中国，参加今年在北京举行的“五一”国际劳动节庆祝典礼。发给请帖的各国工人团体，计有：苏联、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚、德意志民主共和国、朝鲜、蒙古、越南各国的总工会，印度的印度劳工联合会、全印工会大会、印度全国工会大会；印尼的印尼总工会、印尼工会联合会、印尼工人中央组织、印尼回教工会；巴基斯坦的巴基斯坦工会联合会、巴基斯坦劳工联合会；缅甸的缅甸工会大会、工会大会（缅甸）、全缅公务员工会、全缅内河航运工会、全缅铁路工会；锡兰的锡兰工会联合会及其他工会组织；日本的日本工会总评议会、日本工资联合斗争联络会、全日本产业别劳动组合；澳大利亚的澳大利亚工会理事会、码头工人工会。此外，还邀请了中东、近东、北非各国的工会组织，新西兰的工会组织，以及英中友好协会和丹麦、瑞典的工人代表。
    中华全国总工会在发出请帖之后，已经陆续接获各国工会组织热诚应邀的复电，说明他们正在积极选派代表准备首途前来中国。
    【新华社七日讯】中华全国总工会已收到苏联、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、罗马尼亚和德意志民主共和国等国工会组织邀请中华全国总工会派遣代表团，前往参加各该国今年“五一”节庆祝典礼的电文。捷克斯洛伐克总工会并邀请我工会代表团在参加“五一”劳动节之后，参加捷克斯洛伐克解放七周年纪念。中华全国总工会正在积极准备派遣代表团中。
