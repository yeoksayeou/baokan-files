Title: "Investigation Team on the Crimes of Bacteriological Warfare by American Imperialism" Northeast Branch Returns to Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-04-03
Page: 1

    "Investigation Team on the Crimes of Bacteriological Warfare by American Imperialism"
    Northeast Branch Returns to Beijing Yesterday
    [Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd] The Northeast Branch of the "Investigation Team on the Crimes of Bacteriological Warfare by American Imperialism," led by Deputy Head Chen Qiyuan, returned to Beijing on the afternoon of April 2nd after successfully completing its investigation mission. Representatives from various democratic parties and people's organizations in the capital were at the station to welcome them, including Chen Shutong, Shen Junru, He Cheng, Wu Yunfu, Fu Lianzhang, Su Jingguan, Hu Yuzhi, Liu Guanyi, Mei Gongbin, Wang Kunlun, Qu Wu, and Shen Zijiu, totaling more than fifty people.

### “美帝国主义细菌战罪行调查团”  东北分团昨天返京

1952-04-03
第1版()
专栏：

    “美帝国主义细菌战罪行调查团”
    东北分团昨天返京
    【新华社二日讯】由陈其瑗副团长率领的“美帝国主义细菌战罪行调查团”东北分团在胜利完成调查任务后，已在四月二日下午返抵北京。首都各民主党派、各人民团体代表到车站欢迎的有陈叔通、沈钧儒、贺诚、伍云甫、傅连暲、苏井观、胡愈之、刘贯一、梅龚彬、王崑崙、屈武、沈兹九等五十多人。
