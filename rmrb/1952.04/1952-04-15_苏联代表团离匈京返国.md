Title: Soviet Delegation Leaves Hungarian Capital for Home

Author:

Date: 1952-04-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th TASS: The Soviet government delegation led by Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Marshal of the Soviet Union, Voroshilov, returned to Moscow from Budapest on April 9th. The delegation participated in the Hungarian celebrations for the 7th anniversary of the Soviet Army's liberation.

### 苏联代表团离匈京返国

1952-04-15
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联代表团离匈京返国
    【新华社讯】塔斯社莫斯科十日电：苏联部长会议副主席、苏联元帅伏罗希洛夫率领的苏联政府代表团，已于四月九日从布达佩斯返抵莫斯科。代表团曾参加匈牙利为苏军解放七周年而举行的庆典。
