    Our Great Motherland
    Wuxi City, located in the Sunan Administrative Region, was liberated three years ago today. Wuxi is not only an industrial city with 110,000 workers, but also one of the most famous scenic spots in the country. This is the beautiful scenery of Taihu Lake on the western outskirts of Wuxi City.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-04-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    苏南行署区无锡市是在三年前的今天解放的。无锡不仅是一个拥有十一万工人的工业城市，而且也是全国有名的风景区之一。这是无锡市西郊太湖的美丽景色。
