Title: Beirut Cables the CPC Central Committee to Express Gratitude
Author:
Date: 1952-04-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 25th: On April 18th, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a congratulatory telegram to Bolesław Bierut, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, on his sixtieth birthday. Bierut sent a return telegram on the 24th to express his gratitude. The telegram is as follows:

To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China:

Please accept my warm gratitude for your heartfelt congratulations on my sixtieth birthday, dear comrades.

Bierut
Warsaw, April 24, 1952

### 贝鲁特复电中共中央委员会答谢

1952-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    贝鲁特复电中共中央委员会答谢
    【新华社二十五日讯】四月十八日为波兰统一工人党中央委员会主席贝鲁特六十寿辰，中国共产党中央委员会曾致电祝贺，贝鲁特在二十四日特复电答谢。电文如下：中国共产党中央委员会：
    我六十岁生日时，承蒙致衷心的祝贺。亲爱的同志们，请接受我热烈的谢忱。
    贝鲁特
    一九五二年四月二十四日于华沙
