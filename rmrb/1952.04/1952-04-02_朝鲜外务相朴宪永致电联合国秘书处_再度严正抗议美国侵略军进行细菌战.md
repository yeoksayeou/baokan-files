Title: DPRK Foreign Minister Pak Hon-yong Sends Telegram to UN Secretariat, Reiterating Stern Protest Against US Aggressor Troops' Germ Warfare

Author: 
Date: 1952-04-02
Page: 4

    DPRK Foreign Minister Pak Hon-yong Sends Telegram to UN Secretariat
    Reiterating Stern Protest Against US Aggressor Troops' Germ Warfare
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 1st] DPRK Foreign Minister Pak Hon-yong sent a telegram to the UN Secretariat on March 29, notifying the UN of the DPRK government's statement on February 22 protesting the US aggressor troops' use of bacteriological weapons against the Korean people, and sternly protesting to the UN the heinous crimes of the US aggressor troops in continuing to wage germ warfare, demanding severe punishment for the organizers of germ warfare. The telegram reads as follows: Lake Success, New York, UN Secretariat.
    I am now notifying the United Nations of the statement issued by the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea on February 22 of this year regarding the use of bacteriological weapons by the American interventionists against the Korean people.
    This atrocity of the interventionists has been investigated and confirmed by the investigation team of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers. Because the US military continues to use bacteriological weapons, the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea once again lodges a firm protest with the United Nations against this barbaric atrocity and demands severe punishment for the organizers of germ warfare.
                        Pak Hon-yong, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea
                          March 29, 1952, Pyongyang, Korea

### 朝鲜外务相朴宪永致电联合国秘书处  再度严正抗议美国侵略军进行细菌战

1952-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜外务相朴宪永致电联合国秘书处
    再度严正抗议美国侵略军进行细菌战
    【新华社平壤一日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务相朴宪永在三月二十九日致电联合国秘书处，把朝鲜政府在二月二十二日抗议美国侵略军使用细菌武器的声明通知联合国，并向联合国严正抗议美国侵略军继续进行细菌战的滔天罪行，要求严惩细菌战的组织者。电文如下：纽约成功湖联合国秘书处
    我现在把朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府在今年二月二十二日就美国干涉者对朝鲜人民使用细菌武器一事所发表的声明通知给联合国。
    干涉者的这一暴行，已经为国际民主法律工作者协会调查团所调查和证实。因为美国军队继续使用细菌武器，所以朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府再度向联合国提出反对这种野蛮暴行的坚决抗议，并要求严惩细菌战的组织者。
                        朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务相　朴宪永  
                          一九五二年三月二十九日　朝鲜平壤
