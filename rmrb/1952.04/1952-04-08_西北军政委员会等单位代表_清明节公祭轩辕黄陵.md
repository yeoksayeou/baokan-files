Title: Representatives of the Northwest Military and Political Committee and Other Units Publicly Commemorate the Yellow Emperor's Mausoleum on Qingming Festival
Author:
Date: 1952-04-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 6th. On Qingming Festival the 5th, the Northwest Military and Political Committee, the Shaanxi Provincial People's Government, and the Yan'an Prefectural Commissioner's Office all sent representatives to publicly commemorate the Yellow Emperor's Mausoleum. People from all walks of life in Huangling County also flocked to pay their respects. After the representatives offering sacrifices to the mausoleum presented wreaths to the Yellow Emperor's Mausoleum, Zhang Jichun, Deputy Minister of the Cultural Department of the Northwest Military and Political Committee, the chief officiant, delivered a brief speech in front of the mausoleum, calling on everyone to inherit the excellent traditions of bravery and diligence of our great ancestors, work hard to increase production, practice strict economy, strengthen national defense, and strengthen resistance to America and aid Korea, and smash the aggressive conspiracies of US imperialism. The day before Qingming Festival, Lu Guangchuan, a representative of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army returning to China, and Kim Jang-seok and six others, representatives of the Korean people visiting China, paid their respects to the Yellow Emperor's Mausoleum.

### 西北军政委员会等单位代表  清明节公祭轩辕黄陵

1952-04-08
第1版()
专栏：

    西北军政委员会等单位代表
    清明节公祭轩辕黄陵
    【新华社西安六日电】五日清明节，西北军政委员会、陕西省人民政府、延安专员公署都派了代表，公祭轩辕黄陵。黄陵县各界人民也纷纷前往晋谒。祭陵代表向黄帝陵献花圈致敬后，主祭人西北军政委员会文化部副部长张季纯在陵前发表了简短演说，号召大家继承伟大祖先勇敢勤劳的优秀传统，努力增加生产，厉行节约，充实国防力量，加强抗美援朝，粉碎美帝国主义的侵略阴谋。清明节前一日，中国人民志愿军归国代表卢广川和朝鲜人民访华代表金长石等七人曾晋谒了黄帝陵。
