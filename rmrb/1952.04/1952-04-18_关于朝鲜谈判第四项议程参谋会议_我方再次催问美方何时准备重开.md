Title: Regarding the Staff Meeting on the Fourth Item of the Korean Negotiations: Our Side Repeatedly Asks the U.S. Side When It Is Ready to Reopen

Author: 
Date: 1952-04-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 17th—At the third item subcommittee meeting of the Korean armistice negotiations on the 17th, the other party expressed no new opinions regarding the remaining nominations for neutral nations and the so-called issue of restricting airport facilities in Korea. Our side repeatedly asked the other party when it was prepared to reconvene the staff meeting on the fourth agenda item. The other party did not give a specific answer but agreed to inform its person in charge of the fourth agenda item.

### 关于朝鲜谈判第四项议程参谋会议  我方再次催问美方何时准备重开

1952-04-18
第4版()
专栏：

    关于朝鲜谈判第四项议程参谋会议
    我方再次催问美方何时准备重开
    【新华社开城十七日电】十七日朝鲜停战谈判第三项议程小组委员会上，对于剩余的中立国提名问题与所谓限制朝鲜境内机场设备的问题，对方表示无新意见。我方再次催问对方何时准备重行召开第四项议程参谋会议，对方未予具体答复，但允转告其第四项议程负责人。
