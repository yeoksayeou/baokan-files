    Our Great Motherland
  　The agricultural implements station is an institution in our country's rural areas for promoting new-style agricultural implements. It began to be established in the spring of 1950, and in the past two years has developed to 88 stations nationwide. This is the staff of the new-style agricultural implements promotion station in Jixian County, Songjiang Province. They are patiently teaching farmers how to use the Soviet-made horse-drawn ten-row seeder.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-04-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
  　农具站是我国农村中推广新式农具的机构。它从一九五○年春天开始建立，两年来在全国已发展至八十八个站。这是松江省集贤县新式农具推广站的工作人员。他们正在耐心地给农民讲授苏联马拉十行播种机的使用法。
