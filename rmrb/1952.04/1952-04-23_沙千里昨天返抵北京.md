Title: Sha Qianli Returns to Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-04-23
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd—Sha Qianli, head of the Chinese commercial delegation that went to Moscow to participate in the Sino-Soviet 1952 trade negotiations, returned to Beijing on the afternoon of the 22nd. Those who welcomed him at the airport included Yao Yilin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government, Hu Renkui, Director of the General Office, Jiang Ming and Jia Shi, Deputy Directors of the Foreign Trade Department, and dozens of heads of various foreign trade companies.

### 沙千里昨天返抵北京

1952-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

    沙千里昨天返抵北京
    【新华社二十二日讯】前往莫斯科参加中苏一九五二年贸易谈判的我国商务代表团团长沙千里，在二十二日下午返抵北京。到机场欢迎的有中央人民政府贸易部副部长姚依林，办公厅主任胡仁奎，国外贸易司副司长江明、贾石，以及各对外贸易公司负责人等数十人。
