Title: Vietnamese Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing to Attend “May Day” Celebrations in China
Author:
Date: 1952-04-28
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 27th: The Vietnamese Trade Union Delegation, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the May Day celebrations in China, arrived in Beijing by train this morning. Greeting them at the station were: Zhu Xuefan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Li Zaiwen, Director of the General Office; Dong Xin, First Deputy Director of the Policy Research Office; Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department; Jiang Tao, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Chemical Industry Trade Union, and others. Huang Yuan, Second Secretary of the Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Tran Tien Quan, Liaison Officer of the Vietnam General Federation of Trade Unions in China, also greeted them at the station.
    The list of the Vietnamese Trade Union Delegation is as follows:
    Head of Delegation: Duong Bach Lien, Member of the Central Executive Committee of the Vietnam General Federation of Trade Unions
    Member: Ho Dac Di, Doctor of Medicine, National Labor Model of Vietnam
    Tran Nhat Soai, Worker at an Ordnance Factory, National Labor Model of Vietnam

### 应邀来我国参加“五一”节观礼  越南工会代表团昨天到达北京

1952-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国参加“五一”节观礼
    越南工会代表团昨天到达北京
    【新华社二十七日讯】应中华全国总工会邀请前来我国参加五一节观礼的越南工会代表团，已于今晨乘车到达北京。到车站欢迎的有：中华全国总工会副主席朱学范、办公厅主任栗再温、政策研究室第一副主任董昕、国际联络部副部长金子明，中国化学工会筹备委员会主任江涛等人。越南民主共和国代表团二等秘书黄元和越南总工会驻华联络员陈进军也到车站欢迎。
    越南工会代表团名单如下：
    团长  杨白莲  越南总工会中央执行委员
    团员  胡得遗  医学博士、越南全国劳动模范
    陈曰帅  兵工厂工人、越南全国劳动模范
