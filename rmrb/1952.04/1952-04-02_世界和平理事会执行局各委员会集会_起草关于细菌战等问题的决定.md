Title: Meeting of Committees of the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council; Drafting Decisions on Issues such as Bacteriological Warfare
Author:
Date: 1952-04-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Oslo, 1st: TASS reports: At the meeting of the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council on March 31, it discussed the report made by Longobardi (Italy) on the issue of national sovereignty. Speakers included: D'Arboussier (Africa), Montague (UK), Xiao San (China), Gurjiyev (USSR), and Deschamps (France).

The Soviet representative, Konenchuk, expressed his views on the report by World Peace Council Secretary-General Lafferre (France) regarding the achievements of the Council's activities.

The meeting also discussed measures for the reduction of armaments. Speakers included: Professor Bernal (UK), Fajon (France), Casanova (France), Fadeyev (USSR), Joliot-Curie (France), Pajetta (Italy), Street (Australia), Deschamps (France), and Blume (Belgium).

The three committees established to draft the decisions of the Executive Bureau meeting held meetings in the evening. These three committees are: the committee on bacteriological warfare issues chaired by Goth (France), the committee chaired by Fajon (France) on the issue of the signature campaign for demanding the conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact, the issue of eliminating German and Japanese militarism, and measures for the reduction of armaments; and the committee chaired by Xiao San (China) on the development of international cultural relations.

### 世界和平理事会执行局各委员会集会  起草关于细菌战等问题的决定

1952-04-02
第1版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会执行局各委员会集会
    起草关于细菌战等问题的决定
    【新华社一日讯】塔斯社奥斯陆讯：世界和平理事会执行局在三月三十一日会议上讨论了龙巴迪（意）所作的关于民族主权的报告。发言者有：达波赛（非洲）、蒙塔古（英）、萧三（中）、顾里亚耶夫（苏）、德香布兰（法）。
    苏联代表考湼楚克在会上就世界和平理事会秘书长拉斐德（法）关于理事会活动成绩的报告发表意见。
    会议并讨论了关于裁减军备的措施问题。发言者有：贝尔纳教授（英）、法奇（法）、卡桑诺伐（法）、法捷耶夫（苏）、约里奥—居里（法）、巴也塔（意）、斯崔特（澳）、德香布兰
    （法）、布伦姆（比）。
    为起草执行局会议的决定而设立的三个委员会在晚上开了会。这三个委员会是戈特（法）主持的关于细菌战争问题的委员会，法奇（法）主持的关于要求缔结五大国和平公约的签名运动问题、肃清德国与日本军国主义问题、裁减军备的措施问题的委员会，以及由萧三（中）主持的关于发展国际文化关系的委员会。
