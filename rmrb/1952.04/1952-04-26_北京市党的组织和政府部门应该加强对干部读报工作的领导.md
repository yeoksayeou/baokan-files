Title: Party Organizations and Government Departments in Beijing Municipality Should Strengthen Leadership over Cadres' Newspaper Reading Work
Author:
Date: 1952-04-26
Page: 2

Readers' Letters

    北京市党的组织和政府部门应该加强对干部读报工作的领导
    编者按：本报今天发表了批评北京市郊十一区、十五区和十六区的干部忽视读报的来信。在这以前，本报也曾发表过批评北京市公共卫生局所属医院医务工作人员及北京市百货公司、合作社等经济机关工作人员不重视读报的来信。由此可以看出：北京市许多机关的干部有忽视读报工作的现象。为加强干部的时事教育，北京市党的组织和政府部门应该深入检查各机关干部读报的情况，并且订出有效的改进办法来。同时，我们也希望各地各级的组织和政府部门，特别是负责宣传教育工作的部门，都来注意这个问题，立即认真检查干部读报的情况，并把检查结果、改进办法和加强读报工作以后的情况告诉我们。

Editorial Note: Today, this newspaper published letters criticizing cadres in the 11th, 15th, and 16th districts of suburban Beijing for neglecting newspaper reading. Prior to this, this newspaper also published letters criticizing medical staff in hospitals under the Beijing Public Health Bureau and staff in economic organizations such as the Beijing Department Store and cooperatives for not valuing newspaper reading. From this, it can be seen that many cadres in Beijing's organizations have a phenomenon of neglecting newspaper reading. In order to strengthen cadres' current affairs education, Party organizations and government departments in Beijing Municipality should thoroughly examine the newspaper reading situation of cadres in various organizations and formulate effective improvement measures. At the same time, we also hope that organizations and government departments at all levels in all regions, especially those responsible for propaganda and education work, will pay attention to this issue, immediately conduct a serious inspection of cadres' newspaper reading, and inform us of the inspection results, improvement measures, and the situation after strengthening newspaper reading work.
