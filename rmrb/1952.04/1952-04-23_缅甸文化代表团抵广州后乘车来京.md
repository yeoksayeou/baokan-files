Title: Burmese Cultural Delegation Arrives in Guangzhou and Travels to Beijing by Train
Author:
Date: 1952-04-23
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, April 22nd: The Burmese Cultural Delegation, consisting of fifteen members and led by U Tun Pe, arrived in Guangzhou on a visit to New China at the invitation of the Chinese side and to reciprocate the visit to Burma by the Chinese cultural delegation to India and Burma last year. The delegation arrived in Guangzhou on the 21st by special train. Chen Ying, Special Commissioner of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, and Zou Difan, Director of the Special Office of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council, went to greet them from Beijing. Those who welcomed them at the station included Zhu Guang, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou Municipality, Yun Guangying, Secretary-General of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, Du Guoxiang, Director of the Guangdong Provincial Department of Culture and Education, Lu Xiangcang, Director of the Guangzhou Municipal People's Government Bureau of Culture and Education, and Cao Ruoming, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Guangzhou Municipal People's Government. The delegation, accompanied by Chen Ying and Zou Difan, took a train to Beijing on the evening of the 21st.

### 缅甸文化代表团抵广州后乘车来京

1952-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸文化代表团抵广州后乘车来京
    【新华社广州二十二日电】缅甸文化代表团一行十五人，由团长宇吞帕率领，应邀前来访问新中国，并答聘我国赴印缅文化代表团去年访问缅甸之行。代表团已在二十一日乘专车到达广州。我中央人民政府外交部特派亚洲司专员陈应、政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局特派办公室主任邹荻帆由京前往迎接。到车站欢迎的有：广州市人民政府副市长朱光、广东省人民政府秘书长云广英、广东省文教厅长杜国庠、广州市人民政府文教局长陆向苍和广州市人民政府外事处长曹若茗等。代表团已在二十一日晚由陈应、邹荻帆陪同乘车前往北京。
