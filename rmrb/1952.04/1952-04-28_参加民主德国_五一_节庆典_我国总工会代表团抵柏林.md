Title: Chinese Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Berlin to Participate in Democratic Germany's "May Day" Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1952-04-28
Page: 4

    To Participate in Democratic Germany's "May Day" Celebrations
    Chinese Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Berlin
    [Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 27th] A delegation from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, headed by Wang Weigang, arrived in Berlin on the 26th to participate in the May Day celebrations of the German Democratic Republic. Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Dieter, the Fourth Secretary of the Free German Trade Union Federation, other trade union workers, and Li Xin, the counselor of the Chinese diplomatic mission in Germany.

### 参加民主德国“五一”节庆典  我国总工会代表团抵柏林

1952-04-28
第4版()
专栏：

    参加民主德国“五一”节庆典
    我国总工会代表团抵柏林
    【新华社柏林二十七日电】以王维纲为首的中华全国总工会代表团于二十六日到达柏林，参加德意志民主共和国五一节庆祝典礼。前赴机场欢迎的有自由德国工会联合会第四书记狄特和其他工会工作人员，以及中国驻德外交使团参赞李欣。
