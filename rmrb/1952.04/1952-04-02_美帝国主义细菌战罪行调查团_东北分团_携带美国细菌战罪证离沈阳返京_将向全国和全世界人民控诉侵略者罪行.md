Title: “Investigation Group on American Imperialist Bacteriological Warfare Crimes” Northeast Branch Departed Shenyang with Evidence of American Bacteriological Warfare to Return to Beijing, Will Accuse the Aggressors' Crimes to the People of the Whole Country and the World

Author:

Date: 1952-04-02
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 1st – The Northeast Branch of the "Investigation Group on American Imperialist Bacteriological Warfare Crimes" completed its investigation and departed Shenyang by train at 6:28 PM on April 1st to return to Beijing. Wang Bin, Minister of Health of the Northeast People's Government, Vice Minister Bai Xiqing, and Jiao Ruoyu, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, and others went to the station to see them off.

    After arriving in Shenyang on March 16th, the group went to Andong, Kuandian, Fushun, and the suburbs of Shenyang for on-site investigations, lasting 17 days. During the investigation, the group photographed and filmed a large amount of testimonial and material evidence of the American aggressors spreading poisonous insects and substances. They also brought back to Beijing specimens of bacterial bomb fragments, poisonous insects, and poisonous substances thrown by American military aircraft found in various parts of Northeast China, as well as the results of research and testing by local epidemic prevention agencies, and will make a solemn accusation to the people of the whole country and the world.

    Before leaving Shenyang, the group held a symposium with responsible personnel of the Northeast People's Government on the evening of March 30th, attended by Lin Feng, Vice Chairman of the Northeast People's Government, Wang Bin, Minister of Health, Bai Xiqing, Vice Minister, Liu Baotian, Deputy Minister of Civil Affairs, and others. During the symposium, the members of the investigation group unanimously praised the great achievements of the people of Northeast China on the anti-bacterial warfare front, and provided many valuable suggestions for future epidemic prevention work.

### “美帝国主义细菌战罪行调查团”东北分团  携带美国细菌战罪证离沈阳返京  将向全国和全世界人民控诉侵略者罪行

1952-04-02
第1版()
专栏：

    “美帝国主义细菌战罪行调查团”东北分团
    携带美国细菌战罪证离沈阳返京
    将向全国和全世界人民控诉侵略者罪行
    【新华社沈阳一日电】“美帝国主义细菌战罪行调查团”东北分团在完成了调查任务后，已于四月一日午后六时二十八分乘车离开沈阳返北京。东北人民政府卫生部部长王斌、副部长白希清、沈阳市人民政府副市长焦若愚等前往车站欢送。
    该团自三月十六日抵达沈阳后，即前往安东、宽甸、抚顺、沈阳市郊等地进行实地调查，历时十七天。在调查中，该团已将美国侵略者撒布毒虫、毒物的大量人证、物证拍摄、制成照片和电影，并将东北各地所发现的美国军用飞机所投掷的细菌弹弹片、毒虫、毒物的标本，和各地防疫机关研究、化验的结果带回北京，将向全国、全世界人民提出严正的控诉。
    该团离沈阳前，曾于三月三十日晚与东北人民政府负责人员举行座谈会，东北人民政府副主席林枫、卫生部部长王斌、副部长白希清、民政部副部长刘宝田等参加。在座谈中，调查团团员一致称赞东北人民在反细菌战线上的巨大成就，并对今后防疫工作提供了许多宝贵的意见。
