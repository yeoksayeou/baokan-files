Title: In Commemoration of "May Day" Holiday, State-Owned Retail Goods Reduced in Price for Four Days
Author:
Date: 1952-04-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: To commemorate the "May Day" International Labor Day and celebrate the great victory of the "Three-Anti" and "Five-Anti" struggles, the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government has notified state-owned department stores and retail companies across the country to reduce the price of all retail goods by 5% of the original price for four days. In order to facilitate laboring people to purchase on their days off, it is specifically stipulated that this will be advanced to start from April 27th (Sunday) to April 30th.

### 为纪念“五一”节  国营零售商品减价四天

1952-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

    为纪念“五一”节
    国营零售商品减价四天
    【新华社二十二日讯】为纪念“五一”国际劳动节日和庆祝“三反”“五反”斗争的伟大胜利，中央人民政府贸易部已通知全国各地国营百货公司和零售公司，将全部零售商品一律按照原价九五折减价四天。并为照顾劳动人民便于在休息日购买起见，特规定提前自四月二十七日（星期日）起至三十日止。
