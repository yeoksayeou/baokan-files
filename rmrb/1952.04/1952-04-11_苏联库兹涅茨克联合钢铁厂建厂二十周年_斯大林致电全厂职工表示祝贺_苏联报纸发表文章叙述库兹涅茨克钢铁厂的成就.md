Title: 20th Anniversary of the Founding of the Soviet Kuznetsk Integrated Iron and Steel Works: Stalin Sends Congratulatory Telegram to All Workers; Soviet Newspapers Publish Articles Describing the Achievements of the Kuznetsk Steel Plant
Author:
Date: 1952-04-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 3rd (TASS): On the occasion of the 20th anniversary of the founding of the Kuznetsk Integrated Iron and Steel Works, the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, J.V. Stalin, sent a telegram to the workers, engineers, technicians and staff of the plant and the Stalinsk Industrial Construction Trust, congratulating them. The telegram reads as follows:

    Stalinsk
    Kuznetsk Integrated Iron and Steel Works
    Comrade Biran, Director:
    Comrade Yermolayev, Chief Engineer:
    Comrade Kapov, Organizer of the Party Committee of the CPSU (B) at the Kuznetsk Integrated Iron and Steel Works:
    Comrade Pisgishin, Chairman of the Trade Union Committee of the Kuznetsk Integrated Iron and Steel Works:
    Comrade Leznikov, Organizer of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League at the Kuznetsk Integrated Iron and Steel Works:

    On the occasion of the 20th anniversary of the completion and opening of the Kuznetsk Integrated Iron and Steel Works—our country's powerful and technologically advanced metallurgical enterprise—I extend my respects and congratulations to the workers, engineers, technicians, and staff of the Kuznetsk Integrated Iron and Steel Works and the Stalinsk Industrial Construction Trust.

    The Kuznetsk metallurgical workers are at the forefront of the heroic working class of the Soviet Union, and they have gloriously completed and are continuing to complete the tasks assigned by the Party and the government regarding the supply of hardware to the national economic sectors.

    I sincerely congratulate you on your new achievements in your work.

    J.V. Stalin

[Report from this Newspaper] According to Xinhua News Agency: Soviet newspapers have published many articles commemorating the festival, describing the achievements of the Kuznetsk Integrated Iron and Steel Works in various aspects. Biran, director of the Integrated Iron and Steel Works, wrote in Pravda, pointing out that the Integrated Iron and Steel Works played an extremely important role in the country's industrialization and the Soviet people's victory over the enemy during the Great Patriotic War. During the post-war Five-Year Plan, the plant completed the five-year plan for the production of steel and rolled metal in three years, and the five-year plan for the production of milled iron in three and a half years. The selfless labor of the plant's workers has made valuable contributions to the restoration and further development of the Soviet national economy.

### 苏联库兹涅茨克联合钢铁厂建厂二十周年  斯大林致电全厂职工表示祝贺  苏联报纸发表文章叙述库兹涅茨克钢铁厂的成就

1952-04-11
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联库兹涅茨克联合钢铁厂建厂二十周年
    斯大林致电全厂职工表示祝贺
    苏联报纸发表文章叙述库兹涅茨克钢铁厂的成就
    【新华社讯】塔斯社莫斯科三日电：苏联部长会议主席约·维·斯大林在库兹涅茨克联合钢铁厂成立二十周年之际，致电祝贺该厂及斯大林斯克工业建设托辣斯的男女工人、工程师、技师和职员。电文如下：
    斯大林斯克
    库兹涅茨克联合钢铁厂
    厂长比兰同志：
    总工程师叶莫莱耶夫同志：
    苏联共产党（布）中央委员会库兹涅茨克联合钢铁厂党组织者卡波夫同志：
    库兹涅茨克联合钢铁厂工会委员会主席皮斯吉辛同志：
    苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会库兹涅茨克联合钢铁厂团组织者莱兹尼可夫同志：
    值此库兹涅茨克联合钢铁厂——我国的强大的和具有先进技术的冶金企业落成开工二十周年纪念之际，我特向库兹涅茨克联合钢铁厂和斯大林斯克工业建设托辣斯的男女工人、工程师、技师和职员致敬和祝贺。
    库兹涅茨克冶金工作者走在苏联英勇的工人阶级的前列，他们已经光荣地完成了而且正在继续完成党和政府指定的关于以五金供应国民经济部门的任务。
    我衷心祝贺你们在工作中获得新的成就。
    约·维·斯大林
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：苏联报纸发表了许多纪念节日的文章，叙述库兹涅茨克联合钢铁厂各方面的成就。联合钢铁厂厂长比兰在真理报上撰文指出：联合钢铁厂在国家的工业化和苏联人民在伟大卫国战争期间战胜敌人的事业中，起了极为重要的作用。在战后五年计划期间，全厂以三年的时间完成了生产钢和压延金属的五年计划，以三年半的时间完成了铣铁生产的五年计划。全厂工人的忘我劳动已对苏联国民经济的恢复和进一步发展，作出了宝贵的贡献。
