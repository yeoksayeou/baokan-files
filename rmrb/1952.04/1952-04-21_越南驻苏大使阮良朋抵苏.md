Title: Vietnamese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Nguyen Luong Bang Arrives in the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1952-04-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 19th. TASS Moscow: Nguyen Luong Bang, the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Soviet Union, and his embassy staff, arrived in Moscow on April 17th.
Those who went to Yaroslavsky Station to welcome Nguyen Luong Bang included Kulazhenkov, Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and the heads of embassies and legations of various countries in Moscow.

### 越南驻苏大使阮良朋抵苏

1952-04-21
第4版()
专栏：

    越南驻苏大使阮良朋抵苏
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：越南民主共和国驻苏联特命全权大使阮良朋与大使馆人员一行，在四月十七日抵达莫斯科。
    前往雅罗斯拉夫车站欢迎阮良朋的，有苏联外交部交际司司长库拉仁科夫及各国驻莫斯科大使馆和公使馆的首长。
