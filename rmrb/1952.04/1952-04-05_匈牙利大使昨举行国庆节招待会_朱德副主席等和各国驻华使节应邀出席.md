Title: Hungarian Ambassador Hosts National Day Reception Yesterday; Vice Chairman Zhu De and Other Foreign Envoys to China Attend
Author:
Date: 1952-04-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th: The Hungarian Ambassador to China, Sándor Szirmai, held a reception at the Hungarian Embassy at 7:00 PM on April 4th to celebrate the National Day of the Hungarian People's Republic.

Those invited to attend included: Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Li Jishen, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Government Administration Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Chen Yun, Huang Yanpei, President of the Supreme People's Court Shen Junru, Acting Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Nie Rongzhen, Deputy Chief of the General Staff Su Yu, Vice Chairman of the China Peace Committee Chen Shutong, heads of various departments of the Central People's Government, and leaders of various democratic parties and people's organizations Situ Meitang, Zhang Xiruo, Peng Zemin, Ma Yinchu, Lu Dingyi, Zhu Xuefan, Li Shucheng, Liang Xi, Fu Zuoyi, Shi Liang, Xu Deheng, Xu Teli, Wu Yuzhang, Zhang Hanfu, Wu Xiuquan, Yao Yilin, Shao Lizi, Yu Xinqing, Feng Wenbin, and Vice Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, totaling more than 500 people.

Foreign envoys to China who were invited to attend included: Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Indian Ambassador Panikkar, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic König, Mongolian Ambassador Jalkhasaikhan, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Burmese Ambassador U Hla Maung, Pakistani Ambassador Raza, Korean Ambassador Kwon O-jik, Czechoslovak Ambassador Kousal, Swiss Minister Renzo Lardelli, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy Isa Matari, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy Doeding, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Delegation Zhou Liang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Danish Legation Meysto, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Swedish Embassy Béve, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Finnish Legation Brongstedt, as well as embassy staff from various countries.

The entire staff of the Hungarian People's Republic National Folk Art Ensemble, who were invited to perform in our country, also attended the reception.

The reception was filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere. The guests frequently toasted the health of Stalin, Mao Zedong, and Rákosi. After the reception, the Hungarian National Folk Art Ensemble performed songs and dances to add to the festivities.

Chairman Mao Zedong and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China sent telegrams to Chairman Rónai and Foreign Minister Kisz of the Hungarian People's Republic on the 2nd, congratulating them on Hungarian National Day. On the evening of the 2nd, the Hungarian Embassy screened the Hungarian feature film "Western Region" to entertain people from all walks of life in the capital.

### 匈牙利大使昨举行国庆节招待会  朱德副主席等和各国驻华使节应邀出席

1952-04-05
第1版()
专栏：

    匈牙利大使昨举行国庆节招待会
    朱德副主席等和各国驻华使节应邀出席
    【新华社四日讯】四月四日是匈牙利人民共和国国庆日，匈牙利驻我国大使夏法朗柯于四日下午七时在匈牙利大使馆举行招待会。
    应邀出席的有：中央人民政府副主席朱德、李济深，政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来、副总理陈云、黄炎培，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻、副总参谋长粟裕，中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会副主席陈叔通，以及中央人民政府各部门首长、各民主党派和人民团体负责人司徒美堂、张奚若、彭泽民、马寅初、陆定一、朱学范、李书城、梁希、傅作义、史良、许德珩、徐特立、吴玉章、章汉夫、伍修权、姚依林、邵力子、余心清、冯文彬、和北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等五百多人。
    应邀到会的各国驻华使节有：苏联大使罗申、罗马尼亚大使鲁登科、印度大使潘尼迦、德意志民主共和国外交使团团长柯尼希、蒙古大使贾尔卡赛汗、保加利亚大使彼得科夫、缅甸大使吴拉茂、巴基斯坦大使罗查、朝鲜大使权五稷、捷克斯洛伐克大使康萨拉、瑞士公使任佐立、印度尼西亚大使馆临时代办伊沙·玛蒂、波兰大使馆临时代办道定、越南代表团临时代办周亮、丹麦公使馆临时代办迈思妥、瑞典大使馆临时代办贝凡、芬兰公使馆临时代办勃朗斯坦特，以及各国使馆人员。
    应邀前来我国表演的匈牙利人民共和国国家人民文工团全体人员也参加了招待会。
    招待会充满热烈、友好的气氛。会上宾主频频为斯大林、毛泽东、拉科西的健康而干杯。会后由匈牙利国家人民文工团表演歌舞节目助兴。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东主席和周恩来外交部长，曾在二日分别致电匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席罗奈伊和外交部长基斯，祝贺匈牙利国庆日。 二日晚，匈牙利大使馆曾放映匈牙利故事片“西部地区”，招待首都各界人士。
