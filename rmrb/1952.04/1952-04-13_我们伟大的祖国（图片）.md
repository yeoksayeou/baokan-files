Our Great Motherland

Nodular cast iron is a new invention in China's industry, and it can replace some cast steel. The Institute of Engineering Experiment of the Chinese Academy of Sciences began research in July 1950 and successfully trial-produced it on October 9 of the same year. It is currently being promoted throughout the country. This is a scene of the Daxin Machine Factory of the East China Industrial Department in Shanghai, which is carrying out mass production based on the successful experience of nodular cast iron.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    球墨铸铁是我国工业上的一种新发明，它可以代替一部分铸钢。中国科学院工学实验馆于一九五○年七月开始研究，同年十月九日试制成功，目前已向全国各地推广。这是上海华东工业部大鑫机器厂，根据球墨铸铁的成功经验，正进行大量生产的情形。
