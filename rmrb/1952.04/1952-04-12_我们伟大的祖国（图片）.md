Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-04-12
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    In the anti-corruption and anti-theft struggle, a large number of activists have emerged from factories and enterprises across the country. They are gradually becoming cadres in various aspects of work. Yang Shaochang, a technician in the foundry workshop of the Shanghai joint state-private Hua Feng Steel Factory, has firmly stood on the position of the working class and closely contacted the masses during the struggle. He has been selected as the acting deputy director of the factory. Here, Yang Shaochang (right) is working with the factory's engineering staff to study a new plan.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-04-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    各地工厂企业在反贪污反盗窃斗争中涌现了大批积极分子，他们正逐渐成为各方面工作的干部。上海公私合营的华丰钢铁厂翻砂间的技工杨绍昌，在斗争中站稳了工人阶级的立场，密切联系群众，已被选拔为该厂代理副厂长。在这里，杨绍昌（右）正和该厂的工务人员共同研究一项新的计划。
