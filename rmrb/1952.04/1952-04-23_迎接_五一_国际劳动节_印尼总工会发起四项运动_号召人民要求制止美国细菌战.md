Title: Welcoming "May Day" International Labor Day: Indonesian General Labor Union Launches Four Movements, Calling on People to Demand a Halt to American Bacteriological Warfare
Author:
Date: 1952-04-23
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 22nd: To commemorate this year's "May Day" International Labor Day, the Central Committee of the Indonesian General Labor Union issued a statement calling on the people of Indonesia to carry out four movements: First, a signature campaign to demand the purchase of cheap rice, and a movement to donate a cup of rice from each household to help the poor; Second, sign the declaration demanding that the five major powers conclude a peace pact, and demand a halt to the bacteriological warfare waged by American imperialists in Korea; Third, hold mass rallies and demonstrations in various places; Fourth, launch a movement to promote people's culture.

### 迎接“五一”国际劳动节  印尼总工会发起四项运动  号召人民要求制止美国细菌战

1952-04-23
第4版()
专栏：

    迎接“五一”国际劳动节
    印尼总工会发起四项运动
    号召人民要求制止美国细菌战
    【新华社二十二日讯】雅加达消息：为纪念今年的“五一”国际劳动节，印尼总工会中央委员会发表声明，号召印尼全国人民进行四项运动：一、要求购买廉价米的签名运动，以及每家捐助一杯大米以救济贫民的运动；二、在要求五大国缔结和平公约宣言上签名，并要求制止美帝国主义者在朝鲜进行细菌战；三、在各地举行群众大会和示威游行；四、发动促进人民文化的运动。
