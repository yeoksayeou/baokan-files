Title: Japan Publishes Marxist-Leninist Works and Begins Printing Selected Works of Mao Zedong
Author:
Date: 1952-04-17
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 15th dispatch from TASS: The Japanese working people's interest in Marxist-Leninist works is growing daily. Otsuki Shoten has begun publishing Stalin's works and is about to complete the publication of the 23-volume collection of Marx and Engels. Otsuki Shoten also plans to publish collections of works by Marx, Engels, and Lenin, as well as a two-volume collection of selected works by Marx and Engels.
    At the same time, Misuzu Shobo has begun publishing Selected Works of Mao Zedong.

### 日本出版马列主义著作并开始印行毛泽东选集

1952-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

    日本出版马列主义著作并开始印行毛泽东选集
    【新华社十六日讯】塔斯社东京十五日讯：日本劳动人民对马克思—列宁主义著作的兴趣与日俱增。大月书店已开始出版斯大林的著作，并且即将完成马克思和恩格斯二十三卷著作的出版工作。大月书店还计划出版马克思、恩格斯和列宁文集以及两卷本的马克思和恩格斯选集。
    同时，三一书房已开始出版毛泽东选集。
