Title: To Commemorate International Labor Day, Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications Issues Commemorative Stamps
Author:
Date: 1952-04-27
Page: 1

    To Commemorate International Labor Day
    Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications Issues Commemorative Stamps
    [Xinhua News Agency, 26th] To commemorate International Labor Day, the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government is issuing three types of "International Labor Day Commemorative Stamps": Hammer and Sickle design (red), Worker-Peasant Giant Hands design (emerald green), and Giant Hammer and Chimney design (orange-red). All are vertical rectangles with a face value of 800 yuan. They will be sold at post offices nationwide from May 1st to October 31st of this year. Throughout the month of May, the "International Labor Day Commemorative Date Stamp" can be used to cancel stamps for stamp collectors.

### 为纪念“五一”国际劳动节  中央邮电部发行纪念邮票

1952-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    为纪念“五一”国际劳动节
    中央邮电部发行纪念邮票
    【新华社二十六日讯】中央人民政府邮电部为纪念“五一”国际劳动节，特发行“国际劳动节纪念邮票”三种：锤和镰图（红色）、工农巨掌图（翠绿色）、巨锤和烟突图（橘红色），都是立长方形，面值八百元。今年五月一日起至十月三十一日止，在全国各地邮局发售。五月份全月内，可以用“国际劳动节纪念日戳”盖销集邮邮票。
