Title: Sino-British Delegations Officially Sign Trade Agreement Stipulating the Value of Traded Goods Will Reach 20 Million Pounds
Author:
Date: 1952-04-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 12th: Nan Hanchen, head of the Chinese delegation participating in the International Economic Conference, and Boyd-Orr, chief representative of the British delegation, officially signed a trade agreement in Moscow on the 12th, based on the trade agreement exchanged on the 8th by Lu Xuzhang, a member of the Chinese delegation, and Laurie麦, a member of the British delegation. It stipulates that the value of traded goods will reach 20 million pounds.

Xinhua News Agency, 12th: According to TASS Moscow: During the recess on the 10th and 11th, representatives of business circles from various countries participating in the International Economic Conference continued the trade negotiations and contract signings they had begun during the conference at the Trade Union Building. According to representatives, many industrialists, especially the British industrialists attending the conference, have been entrusted by some domestic manufacturers to sign trade contracts on behalf of these manufacturers.

On the evening of the 10th, representatives were invited by the Soviet Preparatory Committee to visit an art performance at the Grand Theatre. Performers included artists from the Grand Theatre Ballet, the Moscow "Little Birch" Ballet Troupe, the Pyatnitsky Russian Folk Choir, and the Soviet National Folk Dance Troupe. The program included Russian and Western classical pieces, Soviet ballet, and dances from various Soviet ethnic groups.

### 中英代表团正式签订贸易协定  规定成交货物的价值将达二千万英镑

1952-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    中英代表团正式签订贸易协定
    规定成交货物的价值将达二千万英镑
    【新华社莫斯科十二日电】参加国际经济会议的我国代表团团长南汉宸，和英国代表团首席代表波以德—奥尔，根据我国代表团团员卢绪章和英国代表团团员劳芮麦在八日交换的贸易协议书，于十二日在莫斯科正式签订了一项贸易协定。其中规定成交货物的价值将达二千万英镑。
    【新华社十二日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：参加国际经济会议的各国实业界代表，于十日、十一日休会期间，在工会大厦继续进行他们在会议过程中开始的贸易谈判和签订合同等活动。据代表们宣布，许多实业家，特别是出席会议的英国实业界代表，已受到本国若干厂商的委托，代表这些厂商签订贸易合同。
    十日晚，代表们曾应苏联筹备委员会的邀请，前往大戏院参观艺术表演。参加演出的有大戏院巴莱舞团、莫斯科“小白桦”巴莱歌舞团、皮亚特尼茨基俄罗斯民间合唱团和苏联国立民间舞蹈团的艺术工作者们。演出节目包括俄罗斯和西方古典名曲、苏联巴莱舞以及苏联各民族舞蹈。
