Title: Hungary Holds Celebration Ceremony for the Seventh Anniversary of Soviet Army Liberation; Marshal Voroshilov Leads Soviet Delegation to Participate
Author:
Date: 1952-04-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, April 2nd: TASS reports: The Soviet government delegation, led by Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Soviet Marshal Voroshilov, arrived in Budapest on the afternoon of the 2nd to participate in the Hungarian celebration ceremony for the seventh anniversary of the Soviet Army's liberation. The Soviet delegation includes Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Bogomolov and Soviet Ambassador to Hungary Kiselev. Chairman Gro of the National Economic Committee of the Hungarian People's Republic, Foreign Minister Kis of the Hungarian People's Republic, and others went to the Hungarian-Soviet border to welcome the Soviet government delegation.

The following people welcomed the Soviet government delegation at the East Railway Station in Budapest: Rakosi, General Secretary of the Hungarian Working People's Party, Hungarian government officials led by Chairman Dobi of the Council of Ministers, leaders of the Hungarian Working People's Party and representatives from all walks of life, as well as senior staff of the Soviet Embassy in Hungary, and members of diplomatic missions from friendly countries.

Thousands upon thousands of Budapest workers gathered in the square in front of the station to welcome the Soviet delegation. An honor guard was lined up in the square.

When the special train of the Soviet delegation approached the station, the band played the Soviet and Hungarian national anthems. Chairman Dobi of the Council of Ministers of the Hungarian People's Republic delivered a welcome speech to the Soviet delegation on behalf of the Hungarian government and the Hungarian people. Soviet Marshal Voroshilov delivered a reply on behalf of the Soviet government delegation.

When the Soviet government delegation arrived in the square, the thousands upon thousands of workers who had gathered in the square expressed a warm welcome with long and continuous applause and cheers.

### 匈牙利举行为苏军解放七周年庆祝典礼  伏罗希洛夫元帅率领苏联代表团参加

1952-04-11
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利举行为苏军解放七周年庆祝典礼
    伏罗希洛夫元帅率领苏联代表团参加
    【新华社讯】塔斯社布达佩斯二日电：苏联部长会议副主席、苏联元帅伏罗希洛夫率领的苏联政府代表团，已于二日下午到达布达佩斯，准备参加匈牙利为苏军解放七周年的庆祝典礼。苏联代表团中包括苏联外交部副部长鲍格莫洛夫和苏联驻匈大使基塞列夫。匈牙利人民共和国国民经济委员会主席格罗、匈牙利人民共和国外交部长基斯等，特往匈苏边界迎接苏联政府代表团。
    在布达佩斯东车站欢迎苏联政府代表团的有：匈牙利劳动人民党总书记拉科西，部长会议主席道比率领的匈牙利政府要员们，匈牙利劳动人民党领袖们和各界代表，以及苏联驻匈大使馆高级人员，各友邦外交使馆人员们。
    千千万万布达佩斯劳动人民聚集在车站前面的广场上迎候苏联代表团。仪节队整列在广场上。
    当苏联代表团专车驶近车站时，乐队奏苏联国歌和匈牙利国歌。匈牙利人民共和国部长会议主席道比，代表匈牙利政府和匈牙利人民向苏联代表团致欢迎辞。苏联元帅伏罗希洛夫代表苏联政府代表团致答辞。
    苏联政府代表团到达广场上时，已聚集在广场上的千千万万劳动人民以历久不绝的掌声与欢呼声表示热烈欢迎。
