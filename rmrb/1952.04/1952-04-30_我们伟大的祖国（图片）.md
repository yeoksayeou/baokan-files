Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-04-30
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The People's Government cares about the welfare of workers at all times and in all places. The Tianjin Municipal People's Government's Construction Management Committee has decided to build new villages outside Zhongshanmen in the urban area to provide housing for workers in Tianjin's factories and their families. The first batch of workers' dormitories officially started construction on April 19. This is a picture of construction engineers studying construction blueprints at the standard model site for the new workers' dormitories.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    人民政府随时随地都关心工人的福利。天津市人民政府建筑管理委员会，决定在市区中山门外等地建筑新村，以供天津各工厂工人和他们的家属居住。第一批工人宿舍已于四月十九日正式动工。这是建筑工程师在工人新宿舍标准示型场地研究工程图样的情形。
