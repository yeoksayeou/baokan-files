Title: Chambellan and Others Arrive in Tianjin for Visit
Author:
Date: 1952-04-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, April 26th:

    Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Chambellan, along with his wife and son; and a delegation of eighteen representatives from India, Burma, Indonesia, the United Kingdom, France, Argentina, Canada, Chile, and Venezuela, who attended the International Economic Conference in Moscow, arrived in Tianjin at 10:30 a.m. on the 26th for a visit. Those who welcomed them at the station included: Li Zhuchen, Chairman of the Tianjin Federation of Industry and Commerce and Chairman of the Tianjin Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association; Du Xinbo, Vice Chairman of the Tianjin Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association; Zhu Jisheng, Vice Chairman of the Tianjin Federation of Industry and Commerce; Meng Qiujiang, representative of the Preparatory Committee of the Tianjin Branch of the All-China Journalists Association; and Chen Shunli, Vice Dean of Academic Affairs of Nankai University.

### 香贝朗等抵天津参观

1952-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    香贝朗等抵天津参观
    【新华社天津二十六日电】国际贸易促进委员会秘书长香贝朗偕其夫人及公子；出席莫斯科举行的国际经济会议的印度、缅甸、印尼、英国、法国、阿根廷、加拿大、智利、委内瑞拉的代表一行十八人，在二十六日上午十时半由北京到达天津参观。到车站欢迎的有：天津市工商业联合会主任委员、民主建国会天津分会主任委员李烛尘、民主建国会天津分会副主任委员杜新波、天津市工商业联合会副主任委员朱继圣、中华全国新闻工作者协会天津分会筹备委员会代表孟秋江、南开大学副教务长陈舜礼等。
