Title: Agreed Issues on the Third Agenda Item of the Korean Negotiations: Both Sides Agree to Submit to the Subcommittee
Author:
Date: 1952-04-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 2nd. At the staff meeting on the third agenda item of the Korean armistice negotiations on the 2nd, both sides agreed to submit the issues that had already been agreed upon to the subcommittee. The subcommittee will begin meeting on April 3.

At the staff meeting on the fourth agenda item, the two sides continued to exchange views on the principle of prisoner of war repatriation.

### 朝鲜谈判第三项议程已协议问题  双方同意提交小组委员会

1952-04-03
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜谈判第三项议程已协议问题
    双方同意提交小组委员会
    【新华社开城二日电】二日朝鲜停战谈判第三项议程参谋会议上，双方同意将已经协议的问题一并提交小组委员会。小组委员会在四月三日开始举行。
    第四项议程参谋会议上，双方就遣俘原则问题继续交换意见。
