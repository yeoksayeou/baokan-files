Title: Stalin Receives Indian Ambassador to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1952-04-08
Page: 4

    Stalin Receives Indian Ambassador to the Soviet Union
    (Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th) TASS, Moscow, 6th: The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, J.V. Stalin, received the Indian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Radhakrishnan, on April 5th. The Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, A.Y. Vyshinsky, was also present.

### 斯大林接见印度驻苏大使

1952-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    斯大林接见印度驻苏大使
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科六日讯：苏联部长会议主席约·维·斯大林在四月五日接见了印度驻苏联大使拉德加克里斯南。苏联外交部长安·扬·维辛斯基也在座。
