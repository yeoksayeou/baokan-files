Title: Naval Pioneer Mr. Sa Zhenbing Passes Away in Fuzhou; Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Condolence Telegrams Respectively; Issue Orders to the Fujian Provincial People's Government to Organize a Funeral Committee to Oversee Funeral Arrangements

Author:

Date: 1952-04-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 12th: Mr. Sa Zhenbing, member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, member of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission, member of the Fujian Provincial People's Government, and a naval pioneer, passed away in Fuzhou at 10:10 PM on April 10th due to a recurrence of old illnesses including heart disease and pneumonia, at the age of 94. Upon hearing the news, Chairman Mao of the Central People's Government and Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council telegraphed the Fujian Provincial People's Government, ordering them to organize a funeral committee to oversee funeral arrangements, and sent condolence telegrams to Mr. Sa's family. The telegrams are as follows:

(I) Chairman Mao's Condolence Telegram:

To the Family of Mr. Sa Zhenbing:

Deeply saddened by the passing of Mr. Sa Zhenbing. Sending this telegram to express my condolences.

Mao Zedong
April 12, 1952

(II) Premier Zhou Enlai's Condolence Telegram:

To the Family of Mr. Sa Zhenbing:

Deeply grieved to learn of the passing of Mr. Sa Zhenbing. Please accept my sincere condolences.

Zhou Enlai
April 12, 1952

### 我国海军前辈萨镇冰先生在福州病逝  毛主席和周总理分别致电悼唁  并电令福建省人民政府组织治丧委员会主持丧葬事宜

1952-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我国海军前辈萨镇冰先生在福州病逝
    毛主席和周总理分别致电悼唁
    并电令福建省人民政府组织治丧委员会主持丧葬事宜
    【新华社十二日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会委员、中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会委员、福建省人民政府委员、海军前辈萨镇冰先生因心脏病、肺炎症旧病复发，在四月十日下午十时十分在福州病逝，享年九十四岁。中央人民政府毛主席和政务院周恩来总理闻讯后已电令福建省人民政府组织治丧委员会主持丧葬事宜，并电萨先生家属致唁。电文如下：
    （一）毛主席唁电
    萨镇冰先生家属礼鉴：
    萨镇冰先生因病逝世，无任悼念，特电致唁。
    毛泽东
    一九五二年四月十二日
    （二）周恩来总理唁电
    萨镇冰先生家属礼鉴：
    惊悉萨镇冰先生逝世，谨致深切的哀悼。
    周恩来
    一九五二年四月十二日
