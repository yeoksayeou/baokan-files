Title: Evidence of the U.S. Military Using Bacterial Bombs on the Korean Front (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1952-04-18
Page: 4

    Evidence of the U.S. Military Using Bacterial Bombs on the Korean Front
    This is a bacterial bomb fired by the invading U.S. military at position No. 600 of a certain unit of the Chinese People's Volunteers on the Korean front at 21:45 on March 7th. There are many flies and mosquitoes on the snow around the shell.
    Staff members of our epidemic prevention team picked up the flies and mosquitoes around the bacterial bomb and placed them in bottles. (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 美军在朝鲜前线使用细菌炮弹的罪证（图片）

1952-04-18
第4版()
专栏：

    美军在朝鲜前线使用细菌炮弹的罪证
    这是三月七日二十一时四十五分侵朝美军向我国人民志愿军某部阵地第六○○号高地打来的细菌炮弹。在炮弹周围的雪地上，有许多苍蝇和蚊子。
    我防疫队工作人员将细菌炮弹周围的苍蝇和蚊子拣入瓶中。（新华社稿）
