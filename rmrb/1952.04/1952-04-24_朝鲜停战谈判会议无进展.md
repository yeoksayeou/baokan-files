Title: Korean Armistice Talks Make No Progress
Author:
Date: 1952-04-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, April 23rd: At the Staff Officers' Meeting on the third agenda item of the Korean armistice talks on the 23rd, our side reiterated: Our side has already pointed out reasonable solutions to the remaining issues of the third agenda item; if the other side continues to adopt an attitude of unwillingness to solve the problem and insists on its unreasonable demands, the negotiations will not be able to make any progress.

At the Staff Officers' Meeting on the fourth agenda item, the staff officers of both sides continued to exchange views on the issue of prisoner repatriation. The meeting made no progress.

### 朝鲜停战谈判会议无进展

1952-04-24
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判会议无进展
    【新华社开城二十三日电】二十三日朝鲜停战谈判第三项议程参谋会议上，我方再次指出：我方对于第三项议程各项剩余问题，早已指出了合理的解决方案；如果对方继续采取不愿解决问题的态度，坚持其无理要求，谈判将不可能有任何进展。
    第四项议程参谋会议上，双方参谋人员继续就遣俘问题交换意见，会议无进展。
