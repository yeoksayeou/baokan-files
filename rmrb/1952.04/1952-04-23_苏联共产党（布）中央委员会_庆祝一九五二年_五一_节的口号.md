Title: Slogans of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) for the Celebration of the May 1st International Labor Day in 1952
Author: 
Date: 1952-04-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 21st (TASS dispatch)

    Slogans of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) for the Celebration of the May 1st International Labor Day in 1952

    I. Long live May 1st – the festival of solidarity of the working people of the world, the festival of fraternal friendship of the workers of all countries!

    II. Fraternal greetings to the people of all countries who are fighting against aggressors and instigators of a new war, for peace, democracy, and socialism!

    III. Fraternal greetings to the working people of the People's Democracies who are successfully building socialism!

    IV. Fraternal greetings to the great Chinese people who have achieved new successes in the construction of People's Democratic China!
    Long live the sacred and inviolable friendship between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union – a powerful force in the struggle for world peace!

    V. Fraternal greetings to the staunch Korean people who are bravely defending their freedom and independence in the fight against foreign aggressors!

    VI. Greetings to the democratic forces of Germany who are fighting against the criminal plan to turn West Germany into an aggressive base for imperialism in Europe! For the establishment of a unified, independent, democratic, and peace-loving Germany!

    VII. Greetings to the glorious Yugoslav patriots who are waging a liberation struggle against the fascist rule of the Tito-Ranković clique, to free themselves from imperialist enslavement and win the independence of their motherland!

    VIII. Greetings to the Japanese people who are fighting heroically against foreign occupation, for the revival and independence of their motherland, and for the defense of peace!

    IX. Long live the friendship of the peoples of Britain, the United States, and the Soviet Union in the struggle to prevent war and ensure lasting world peace!

    X. Working people of all countries! Peace can be maintained and consolidated if the people of all countries take on the cause of maintaining peace and persevere in it to the end! Consolidate the unity of the people in the struggle for peace, expand and unite the ranks of peace supporters!

    XI. Peace supporters of the world! Expose and smash the sinister plans of the imperialist aggressors, prevent the warmongers from deceiving the masses with lies and dragging the people into a new world war!

    XII. Long live the foreign policy of the Soviet Union – a policy of peace and security, equality and friendship among all peoples!

    XIII. Glory to the Soviet Army and Navy, which guard the peace and security of our motherland!

    XIV. Soldiers of the Soviet Union! Persistently improve your military and political knowledge, perfect your combat skills! Consolidate the power of the Soviet armed forces!

    XV. Long live the Soviet border guards – the vigilant sentinels of the sacred borders of our motherland!

    XVI. Workers, peasants, and intellectuals of the Soviet Union! Fight for a new, powerful upsurge in the culture and economy of our great socialist motherland, for the further growth of the power of the Soviet state! Expand socialist competition more widely, and strive to complete the 1952 national economic plan ahead of schedule!

    XVII. Working people of the Soviet Union! In all sectors of the national economy, adopt the achievements of science and technology and advanced experience more boldly! Increase labor productivity, improve quality, and reduce production costs! Save raw materials, fuel, materials, and electricity!

    XVIII. Working people of the Soviet Union! Victoriously complete the great construction projects on the Volga and Dnieper Rivers, and the Don and Amu Rivers, and make new contributions to the cause of communist construction!

    XIX. Workers, engineers, and technical experts in the coal mining industry! Expand and improve the mechanization of coal mining! Accelerate the construction of new mines! Produce more coal for the national economy!

    XX. Workers, engineers, and technical experts in the petroleum industry! Increase the speed of drilling oil wells and develop new oil fields more quickly! Build new oil fields and refineries at a greater speed! Produce more high-quality petroleum products for the socialist economy!

    XXI. Workers, engineers, and technical experts in the iron and steel industry and non-ferrous metal industry! Improve the utilization of production capacity and machinery, perfect production technology, and adopt advanced labor methods! Continuously increase the production of pig iron, steel, rolled metal, and non-ferrous metals!

    XXII. Workers, engineers, and technical experts in power stations! Make new generating capacity available for our use more quickly! Adopt new technical equipment more widely! Supply the national economy with a continuous flow of electricity!

    XXIII. Workers, engineers, and technical experts in machine-building enterprises! Accelerate the mastery and increase the production of new and improved machines, tools, and equipment! Supply the national economy with advanced technical equipment!

    XXIV. Workers, engineers, and technical experts in the automobile and tractor industry! Adopt new technical equipment more widely in production, fight to save metal, and improve the quality of automobile and tractor products! Supply the motherland with more automobiles and tractors!

    XXV. Workers, engineers, and technical experts in the chemical industry! Perfect production methods and reduce production costs! Increase output, increase product variety, and improve the quality of chemical products!

    XXVI. Workers, engineers, and technical experts in the shipbuilding industry! Accelerate the construction of new ships and improve the quality of shipbuilding! Build a powerful fleet for the Soviet state!

    XXVII. Workers, engineers, and technical experts in the construction industry! Adopt industrial construction methods more widely! Reduce costs and improve the quality of construction work! Complete enterprises, housing, and cultural welfare facilities more quickly!

    XXVIII. Workers, engineers, and technical experts in the building materials industry! Use all means to increase the production of building materials for communist construction projects, industry, and housing construction! Master the mass production of new types of building materials, and make full use of technical equipment!

    XXIX. Workers, engineers, and technical experts in the timber, paper, and woodworking industries! Improve the use of machine parts and improve the labor organization of timber harvesting and rafting! Continuously improve labor productivity! Produce more timber, furniture, and paper for the state!

    XXX. Workers, engineers, and technical experts in the light industry! Increase the variety of consumer goods! Save raw materials, improve product quality, and reduce production costs! Produce more textiles, shoes, clothing, knitted goods, and other items for the people!

    XXXI. Staff members in the food and meat and dairy industries! Increase the production of high-quality products! Produce more sugar, fats, meat, milk, and other products for the people!

    XXXII. Fishery workers! Increase the amount of fish caught, improve product quality, and increase product variety! Make better use of fishing boats and fishing gear!

    XXXIII. Staff members in local industries and production cooperatives! Use local raw materials to produce more consumer goods! Reduce product costs and improve product quality! Better meet the daily needs of the working people!

    XXXIV. Soviet geologists! Use scientific achievements and advanced experience more boldly in geological surveys! Discover our motherland's infinite mineral resources at a greater speed!

    XXXV. Staff members in the railway transportation industry! Increase the volume of transportation and reduce transportation costs! Accurately execute the train timetable, increase the average daily mileage of locomotives, accelerate the turnover rate of wagons, and improve the quality of vehicle and road repairs! Increase the speed of railway construction! Improve service to passengers!

    XXXVI. Staff members of the maritime fleet and inland river fleet! Accurately organize the work of ports and docks and ensure that ships operate according to schedule! Accelerate the turnover rate of ships, increase the volume of transportation, and reduce transportation costs!

    XXXVII. Communications workers! Perfect communication technology and improve the work of postal, telegraph, telephone, and radio services! Improve the technology of serving the people!

    XXXVIII. Agricultural workers! Strive to further increase the yield per unit area of various crops, and develop public livestock breeding with all means! Produce abundant food for the people and abundant raw materials for industry!

    XXXIX. Male and female collective farmers, male and female workers of machine tractor stations and state farms, and agricultural experts! Carry out spring sowing and other agricultural work in an exemplary manner! Strive for a bumper harvest on all sown areas of collective farms and state farms!

    XL. Male and female collective farmers! Strive for the further consolidation and all-round development of the collective economy of collective farms! Strengthen labor discipline and strictly abide by the articles of association of agricultural cooperatives – the fundamental law of collective farm life!

    XLI. Tractor drivers, combine drivers, and workers of machine tractor stations and state farms! Strive to make full use of technical equipment! Increase the working rate of tractors and combines! Improve the quality of farmland work and strive for bumper harvests every year!

    XLII. Male and female collective farmers, male and female workers of state farms, livestock breeding experts, and veterinary workers! Strive to further increase the number of public livestock and improve the productivity of livestock products! Create a strong feed base for livestock breeding!

    XLIII. Agricultural, forestry, and water conservancy workers! Use all means to expand the area of protective forest belts and improve the maintenance of protective forests! Implement a correct crop rotation system, better master new irrigated land, and dig ponds and reservoirs. Switch to new irrigation systems more quickly! Guarantee bumper harvests every year!

    XLIV. Male and female collective farmers, male and female workers of cotton-growing collective farms, state farms, and machine tractor stations, and cotton-growing experts! Strive to increase cotton production and improve cotton cultivation! Provide our socialist motherland with more cotton!

    XLV. Workers in the Soviet trade industry, cooperatives, and public food supply organs! Use all means to develop Soviet urban and rural trade! Improve service to Soviet consumers!

    XLVI. Workers in state organs! Improve the work of Soviet organs, strengthen state discipline, and pay attention to the demands of the working people!

    XLVII. Workers in scientific research institutions and institutions of higher learning! Enrich science and technology with new research, discoveries, and inventions! Carry out creative criticism and self-criticism in scientific work more boldly! Expand and strengthen cooperation with production workers! Improve the quality of training experts in the national economy!

    XLVIII. Workers in literature, art, and film! Continuously improve your skills! Create new works of art with a high ideological level worthy of the Soviet people!

    XLIX. Male and female teachers! Workers in national education! Equip students with basic scientific knowledge! Educate our youth in the spirit of love for the Soviet motherland and cultivate active builders of communism!

    L. Medical workers! Use all means to improve medical services to the people! Use the advanced knowledge of Soviet science more widely! Apply the achievements of Soviet medicine to practice!

    LI. Various trade unions of the Soviet Union! Carry out socialist competition more widely in order to complete the state plans of each enterprise ahead of schedule! Promote the experience of production innovators! Always be concerned with further improving the material and cultural level of workers and staff! Long live the trade unions of the Soviet Union – schools of communism!

    LII. Women of the Soviet Union! Strive for new progress in the economy and culture, and consolidate the power of our great socialist motherland! Long live the women of the Soviet Union – active builders of communism!

    LIII. Long live the advanced team of young communist builders, the Communist Party's reliable helpers and reserves – the Leninist-Stalinist Communist Youth League!

    LIV. Young men and women of the Soviet Union! Master science, technology, and culture! Be strong and brave, and always be prepared to overcome new difficulties! Increase the achievements of the Soviet people in communist construction with your labor! Long live our glorious Soviet youth!

    LV. Young Pioneers and elementary school students! Master knowledge diligently and persistently! Prepare to become active fighters for the cause of Lenin and Stalin!

    LVI. Communist Party members and Communist Youth League members! March at the forefront of the battle and fight for the further enhancement of the power of our motherland and for the construction of communism in our country!

    LVII. Long live the bulwark of friendship and glory among the nationalities of our motherland, the indestructible bulwark of world peace – the great Union of Soviet Socialist Republics!

    LVIII. Long live the vanguard of the Soviet people steeled in battle, the inspirer and organizer of our victories – the great Communist Party, the party of Lenin and Stalin!

    LIX. Under the banner of Lenin, under the leadership of Stalin, advance towards the victory of communism!

    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks)

### 苏联共产党（布）中央委员会  庆祝一九五二年“五一”节的口号

1952-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联共产党（布）中央委员会
    庆祝一九五二年“五一”节的口号
    一、五一节——全世界劳动人民团结的节日，一切国家的工人兄弟友爱的节日——万岁！
    二、向一切为反对侵略者和新战争的挑拨者，为和平、民主与社会主义而斗争的各国人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    三、向胜利地建设着社会主义的人民民主国家的劳动人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    四、向在人民民主中国的建设中获得了新的成就的、伟大的中国人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    中苏两国人民神圣不可侵犯的友谊——为世界和平而斗争的强大力量万岁！
    五、向在反对外国侵略者的斗争中英勇地卫护自己的自由和独立的、刚毅的朝鲜人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    六、向对要把西德变成帝国主义在欧洲的侵略基地的罪恶计划作斗争的德国民主力量致敬！建立统一、独立、民主、爱好和平的德国！
    七、向为反对铁托—兰科维奇集团的法西斯统治、为摆脱帝国主义的奴役、争取自己祖国的独立而进行解放斗争的光荣的南斯拉夫爱国者致敬！
    八、向为反对外国占领、为自己祖国的复兴和独立、为保卫和平而英勇斗争的日本人民致敬！
    九、英、美、苏三国人民在为制止战争、保证世界持久和平的斗争中的友谊万岁！
    十、一切国家的劳动人民！如果各国人民将维护和平的事业担当起来，并且把这一事业坚持到底，和平就能够保持和巩固！巩固人民为和平而斗争的团结，扩大并紧密和平拥护者的队伍！
    十一、全世界的和平拥护者们！揭露并粉碎帝国主义侵略者的罪恶计划，防止战争贩子用谎言欺骗人民大众而把人民拖入新的世界大战！
    十二、苏联的外交政策——和平与安全、各族人民平等与友好的政策万岁！
    十三、光荣归于警卫着和平与我们祖国安全的苏联陆军和海军！
    十四、苏联的军人们！坚持不渝地提高自己的军事和政治知识，使自己的战斗造诣臻于完善！巩固苏联的武装力量的威力！
    十五、苏联边防军——我们祖国神圣边疆的警觉的哨兵万岁！
    十六、苏联的工人、农民和知识分子们！为我们伟大的社会主义祖国的文化和经济的新的强有力的高涨，为苏维埃国家威力进一步的增长而斗争！更广泛地展开社会主义竞赛，争取提前完成一九五二年国民经济计划！

    十九、煤矿工业的男女工人、工程师和技术专家们！扩大并改善采煤的机械化！加速更好地建造新矿场！为国民经济生产更多的煤！
