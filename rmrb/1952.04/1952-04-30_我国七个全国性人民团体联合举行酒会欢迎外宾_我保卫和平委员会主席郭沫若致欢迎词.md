Title: Seven National People's Organizations in China Jointly Host Reception for Foreign Guests; Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, Delivers Welcome Speech
Author:
Date: 1952-04-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: The China Peace Committee, All-China Federation of Trade Unions, All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and All-China Federation of Scientific Societies jointly held a reception tonight to welcome foreign guests who have come to China to attend the "May Day" celebrations and to visit. More than 500 people attended the reception.

Invited guests included: Frédéric Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the French Council for Peace, and his wife, and French writer Vercors and his wife, who came to Beijing to attend the memorial meetings for the four great cultural figures; Italian Senator and Professor of Philosophy Antonio Banfi; and Italian Professor of Physics Bruno Pontecorvo.

Foreign trade union delegations and representatives attending the reception included: Kalibanov and Sandom, members of the Asian-Australian Liaison Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions; the Soviet trade union delegation headed by Shevchenko; the Bulgarian trade union delegation headed by Mitov; the Romanian trade union delegation headed by Elena; the Hungarian trade union delegation headed by Podgornyik; the Korean trade union delegation headed by Park Hyo-bin; the Czechoslovak trade union delegation headed by Juraček; the Polish trade union delegation headed by Drozynski; the Mongolian trade union delegation headed by Ganbaatar; the German Democratic Republic trade union delegation headed by Herbig; the Vietnamese trade union delegation headed by Yang Bai Lian; the first Indian trade union delegation headed by Shah; the second Indian trade union delegation headed by Bora; the Swedish trade union delegation headed by Sandegård; the Danish "May Day" observation delegation headed by Abel; the Burmese trade union delegation headed by Thein Pe Myint; Ceylonese trade union representative Sarvanamuttu, the Australian trade union delegation headed by Light; the British workers' delegation headed by Haywood; and the Iranian trade union representative, etc.

Also invited were representatives from various countries who came to China for a visit after attending the International Economic Conference, including representatives from Vietnam, India, Burma, Indonesia, Australia, Brazil, Britain, Argentina, Canada, Ceylon, Chile, France, Mexico, and Venezuela.

The Hungarian National People's Art Ensemble, which came to China to perform, was also invited.

Present as hosts were: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee; Vice-Chairmen Chen Shutong; Standing Committee Members Shen Junru, Shao Lizi, Ma Yinchu, Zhang Xiruo, Xu Deheng, Xu Baoju, Huang Yanpei, and Luo Longji; World Peace Council Chinese members Zhang Bojun, Cai Tingkai, and Mei Ru'ao; Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Li Lisan, Zhu Xuefan, and Liu Ningyi; Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth Liao Chengzhi, Vice-Chairman Sha Qianli; Honorary Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation He Xiangning, Vice-Chairwomen Deng Yingchao, Li Dequan, and Xu Guangping; Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Qian Junrui; Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Mao Dun; Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Scientific Societies Wu Youxun; and other leaders of people's organizations Xie Juezai, Shi Liang, Wang Kunlun, Zhang Zhiran, Liang Xi, Zhu Kezhen, Mao Yisheng, Yu Xinqing, Fu Lianzhang, Chen Qiyuan, and Guan Ruoluan.

Nan Hanchen, head of the Chinese delegation to the Moscow International Economic Conference, Secretary-General Ji Chao Ding, and members Zhang Naiqi, Li Zhuchen, Sheng Pihua, and Wu Yun Chu also attended the reception.

More than 40 labor model representatives from major administrative regions across the country who came to participate in the "May Day" celebrations were also invited.

Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, delivered a welcome speech, saying: "Dear friends, on behalf of the Chinese people, we extend our sincere welcome to you. You are all envoys of peace, and we thank you for bringing us such beautiful friendship. We hope that this wonderful friendship will continue forever. We hope to continuously conduct cultural and economic exchanges. We hope that all peace-loving people around the world can live together peacefully and happily like we are tonight."

The reception took place in a warm and friendly atmosphere. Guests and hosts repeatedly raised their glasses to congratulate the friendship and solidarity of the people of Asia and the people of the world. After the reception, a film evening was held, showing the famous film "The Great Solidarity of the Chinese Nation."

### 我国七个全国性人民团体联合举行酒会欢迎外宾  我保卫和平委员会主席郭沫若致欢迎词

1952-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我国七个全国性人民团体联合举行酒会欢迎外宾
    我保卫和平委员会主席郭沫若致欢迎词
    【新华社二十九日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中华全国总工会、中华全国民主青年联合总会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中苏友好协会总会、中华全国文学艺术界联合会、中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会今晚联合举行酒会，欢迎来中国参加“五一”节观礼和参观的外宾。出席酒会的有五百多人。
    应邀出席的有来京出席四大文化名人纪念会的法国和平理事会主席法奇及夫人，法国作家罗阿及夫人，意大利上议员、哲学教授班菲，意大利物理学教授潘齐尼。
    出席酒会的外国工会代表团和工会代表有：世界工会联合会亚澳联络局委员考里班诺夫和桑顿，以谢甫钦科为首的苏联工会代表团，以米笃夫为首的保加利亚工会代表团，以爱丽娜为首的罗马尼亚工会代表团，以波德哥尔尼克为首的匈牙利工会代表团，以朴孝斌为首的朝鲜工会代表团，以尤莱·逊为首的捷克斯洛伐克工会代表团，以德洛日支为首的波兰工会代表团，以冈波苏仑为首的蒙古人民共和国工会代表团，以海尔比希为首的德意志民主共和国工会代表团，以杨白莲为首的越南工会代表团，以沙氏为首的第一批印度工会代表团、以波拉为首的第二批印度工会代表团，以赛南德为首的瑞典工会代表团，以阿伯尔为首的丹麦“五一”节观礼代表团，以德钦钦素为首的缅甸工会代表团，锡兰工会代表撒拉南卡拉，以莱特为首的澳大利亚工会代表团，以海渥德为首的英国工人代表团和伊朗工会代表等。
    应邀出席的还有出席国际经济会议之后来中国参观的各国代表，他们是：越南、印度、缅甸、印尼、澳大利亚、巴西、英国、阿根廷、加拿大、锡兰、智利、法国、墨西哥、委内瑞拉等国的代表。
    来中国表演的匈牙利国家人民文工团也应邀出席。
    出席作陪的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若、副主席陈叔通、常务委员沈钧儒、邵力子、马寅初、张奚若、许德珩、许宝驹、黄炎培、罗隆基，世界和平理事会中国理事章伯钧、蔡廷锴、梅汝璈，中华全国总工会副主席李立三、朱学范、刘宁一，中华全国民主青年联合总会主席廖承志、副主席沙千里，中华全国民主妇女联合会名誉主席何香凝、副主席邓颖超、李德全、许广平，中苏友好协会总会总干事钱俊瑞，中华全国文学艺术界联合会副主席茅盾，中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会副主席吴有训，以及其他各人民团体的负责人谢觉哉、史良、王昆仑、张志让、梁希、竺可桢、茅以升、余心清、傅连暲、陈其瑗、关若鸾等。
    我国出席莫斯科国际经济会议的代表团团长南汉宸、秘书长冀朝鼎、团员章乃器、李烛尘、盛丕华、吴蕴初等也出席了酒会。
    由全国各大行政区来参加“五一”节观礼的劳动模范代表四十多人也应邀作陪。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若致欢迎词说：“各位亲爱的朋友们：我们今天代表中国人民对你们表示诚恳的欢迎。你们都是和平的使者，谢谢你们给我们带来了很美丽的友谊。我们希望这美好的友谊能永远继续下去。我们希望经常不断地作文化上的交流和经济上的交流。我们希望全世界爱好和平的人们都像我们今天晚上一样愉快地和平共处。”
    酒会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。宾主一再举杯，祝贺亚洲人民和世界人民的友好团结。会后举行电影晚会，放映名片“中华民族大团结”。
