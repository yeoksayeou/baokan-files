Title: List of Appointments Approved by the 15th Meeting of the Central People's Government Committee
Author:
Date: 1952-04-27
Page: 1

The following lists of appointments were approved by the 15th Meeting of the Central People's Government Committee:

    I. Supplementary List of Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Public Security of the Central People's Government
    Vice Minister Xu Zirong Currently Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Public Security of the Central People's Government.
    Chen Long Currently Director of the First Bureau of the Ministry of Public Security of the Central People's Government.

    II. Supplementary List of Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Transportation of the Central People's Government
    Vice Minister Wang Shoudao Currently Chairman of the People's Government of Hunan Province.

    III. Supplementary List of Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government
    Vice Minister Fu Lianzhang Currently Vice Minister of the Health Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government.

    IV. Supplementary List of Deputy Directors of the Central People's Government Publishing Administration
    Deputy Director Chen Kehan Currently President of the Xinhua News Agency.

    V. Supplementary List of Deputy Chiefs of General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government
    Deputy Chief of General Staff Su Yu (concurrently)

    VI. List of President and Vice Presidents of the North China Branch of the Supreme People's Court of the Central People's Government
    President Zhang Su (concurrently)
    Vice President He Lanjie Currently Deputy Presiding Judge of the Criminal Tribunal of the Supreme People's Court of the Central People's Government.
    Han Youtong (female) Currently Deputy Director of the Department of Secondary Education of the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government.

    VII. List of President of the East China Branch of the Supreme People's Court of the Central People's Government
    President Wei Ming Currently Director of the Wannan People's Administrative Office.
