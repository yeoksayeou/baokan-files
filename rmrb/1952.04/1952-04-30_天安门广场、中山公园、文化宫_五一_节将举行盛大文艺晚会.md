Title: Tiananmen Square, Zhongshan Park, and Cultural Palace to Hold Grand Literary and Art Evening Parties for "May Day"

Author: 
Date: 1952-04-30
Page: 1

Tiananmen Square, Zhongshan Park, and Cultural Palace
“May Day” to Hold Grand Literary and Art Evening Parties

[This newspaper reported] To celebrate "May Day" Labor Day and allow the capital's working people to joyfully spend their holiday, grand evening parties will be held on the evening of May 1st at Tiananmen Square, the Working People's Cultural Palace, Zhongshan Park, and other locations.

The mass dance party held at Tiananmen Square is scheduled to begin at 7:30 PM on the 1st, with tens of thousands of workers, soldiers, cadres, students, and citizens participating. Fireworks will also be set off during the dance party.

The Working People's Cultural Palace and Zhongshan Park will be open to the public free of charge at the same time. The Municipal Bureau of Culture and the Municipal Sino-Soviet Friendship Association film team will screen movies outdoors in the park. Acrobatics, Quyi performances, and Peking Opera will be performed at the Cultural Palace's Labor Theater, presented by the Chinese Opera Research Institute's Peking Opera Experimental Troupe and Quyi Experimental Troupe. Quyi and Ping Opera will be performed at the Zhongshan Park Concert Hall. The above three locations will begin performances at 7:30 PM. To entertain people from all walks of life, the Preparatory Committee of the Capital's People Celebrating "May Day" has issued admission tickets.

### 天安门广场、中山公园、文化宫  “五一”节将举行盛大文艺晚会

1952-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    天安门广场、中山公园、文化宫
    “五一”节将举行盛大文艺晚会
    【本报讯】为了庆祝“五一”劳动节，使首都劳动人民欢乐地度过自己的节日，在五月一日晚，天安门广场、劳动人民文化宫、中山公园等处都将举行盛大晚会。
    天安门广场举行的群众舞会，定一日晚七时半开始，将有数万工人、战士、干部、学生及市民参加。在舞会进行时，并将放焰火。
    劳动人民文化宫、中山公园同时免费开放，园内由市文艺处、市中苏友好协会电影队露天放映电影。在文化宫劳动剧场将演出杂技、曲艺及京剧，由中国戏曲研究院京剧实验工作团、曲艺实验工作团演出。在中山公园音乐堂将演出曲艺及评戏。以上三地的演出，从下午七时半开始。为招待各界劳动人民，首都各界人民庆祝“五一”节筹委会已发出入场券。
