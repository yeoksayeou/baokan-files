Title: Hungarian Art Troupe Arrives in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1952-04-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, April 15th: The entire Hungarian National People's Art Troupe arrived in Shanghai from Nanjing on the morning of the 15th. Welcoming them at the station were Xia Yan, Director of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government Culture Bureau and Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Federation of Literary and Art Circles, Shen Zhiyuan, Vice Chairman of the East China General Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, and representatives from Shanghai's cultural and artistic workers, workers, youth, students, and troops, totaling over a thousand people. A warm welcome meeting was held at the station.

### 匈牙利文工团抵上海

1952-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

    匈牙利文工团抵上海
    【新华社上海十五日电】匈牙利国家人民文工团全体人员在十五日晨由南京到达上海。到车站欢迎的有上海市人民政府文化局局长、上海市文学艺术界联合会主席夏衍，中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会华东总分会副主席沈志远和上海市文化艺术工作者、工人、青年、学生、部队的代表共一千多人。车站上曾举行了热烈的欢迎会。
