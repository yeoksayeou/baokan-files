Title: Central People's Government Administrative Council Held Meeting Yesterday: Passed Order to Dismiss Arch-Thief He Hengfu from All Government Positions and Arrest Him for Prosecution
Author:
Date: 1952-04-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th—The 131st Administrative Meeting of the Central People's Government Administrative Council was held in the afternoon of the 4th. After listening to the explanation given by Cai Chusheng, Director of the Art Committee of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, the meeting approved the 1952 film production plan. The meeting also passed an order to dismiss arch-thief He Hengfu (currently a member of the Central-South Military and Political Committee, a member of the Central-South Finance and Economics Committee, and a member of the Wuhan Municipal People's Government) from all government positions and arrest him for prosecution.

### 中央人民政府政务院昨天举行会议  通过命令撤消大盗窃犯贺衡夫在政府中一切职务，并予逮捕法办

1952-04-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府政务院昨天举行会议
    通过命令撤消大盗窃犯贺衡夫在政府中一切职务，并予逮捕法办
    【新华社四日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百三十一次政务会议在四日下午举行。会议在听取中央人民政府文化部电影局艺术委员会主任委员蔡楚生所作的说明后，批准了一九五二年电影制片工作计划。会议并通过命令，撤消大盗窃犯贺衡夫（现任中南军政委员会委员、中南财政经济委员会委员兼武汉市人民政府委员）在政府中的一切职务，并予逮捕法办。
