Title: Zhou Enlai Hosts Foreign Minister Molotov
Author:
Date: 1954-05-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 29th. Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, hosted a banquet on the 29th in honor of V.M. Molotov, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics.

Those attending the banquet included: A.A. Gromyko and V.V. Kuznetsov, Deputy Ministers of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union; L.F. Ilyichev and Fedorin, members of the Collegium of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union; P.F. Yudin, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to the People's Republic of China; A.A. Soldatov, Secretary-General of the Soviet Delegation to the Geneva Conference; D. A, Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Zhukov, and A.M. Ledovsky, Deputy Director of the First Far Eastern Department.

On the Chinese side, those attending the banquet included: Zhang Wentian, Wang Jiaxiang, Li Kenong, Wang Bingnan, Shi Zhe, Qiao Guanhua, Chen Jiakang, Huang Hua, and Wang Zhuoru.

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 周恩来外长招待莫洛托夫外长

1954-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来外长招待莫洛托夫外长
   【新华社日内瓦二十九日电】中华人民共和国外交部部长周恩来二十九日设宴招待苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫。
    参加宴会的还有：苏联外交部副部长安·阿·葛罗米柯、瓦·瓦·库兹涅佐夫，苏联外交部部务委员会委员勒·弗·伊利切夫、费德林，苏联驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使帕·费·尤金，苏联出席日内瓦会议代表团秘书长阿·阿·索尔达托夫，苏联外交部交际司司长德·阿·茹科夫和第一远东司副司长阿·麦·列多夫斯基。
    中国方面出席宴会的有：张闻天，王稼祥，李克农，王炳南，师哲，乔冠华，陈家康，黄华，王倬如。
    宴会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
