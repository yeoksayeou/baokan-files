Title: The American People Generally Oppose Participating in the Indochina War
Author:
Date: 1954-05-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, May 5th (TASS dispatch): An article in "U.S. News & World Report" states: If the U.S. Congress were now forced to decide on whether American troops should go to fight in Indochina, then Congress would negate this suggestion, because the members of Congress are influenced by the American people's strong attitude of opposition.

The magazine quoted some members of Congress; according to the article, these words reflect the main trend of public reaction throughout the United States. For example, the chairman of an important Senate committee said: "Ninety-nine percent of the letters I have received request: Do not let Americans participate in Asian wars like the Korean War or the Indochina War again." A Republican congressman from a Midwestern state said: "If we send troops to Indochina, the Republicans can even forget about preparing for the November elections." A Republican congressman from Wisconsin said: "If war occurs during the current administration's tenure, it could mean the end of the Republican Party."

The magazine wrote: Gallup poll results show that only eight percent of Americans favor sending troops to Indochina, eighty-five percent oppose it, and seven percent have no opinion.

### 美国人民普遍反对参加印度支那战争

1954-05-07
第4版()
专栏：

    美国人民普遍反对参加印度支那战争
    【新华社五日讯】塔斯社纽约五日讯：“美国新闻与世界报道”杂志刊载的一篇文章写道：如果美国国会现在迫不得已要就美国军队是否应前往印度支那作战问题作出决定，那末国会是会否定这项建议的，这是因为国会的议员们受到美国人民强烈反对的态度的影响的缘故。
    该杂志引述了一些国会议员的话，据这篇文章说，这些话反映了美国全国公众反应的主要趋向。例如参议院某一重要委员会的主席说：“我所收到的百分之九十九的信件中都要求：不要再让美国人去参加像朝鲜战争或是印度支那战争的亚洲战争。”美国中西部某一州的某一共和党议员说：“如果我们派遣军队去印度支那的话，共和党人甚至就可以不用准备参加十一月的竞选了。”威斯康星州的某一共和党议员说：“如果在现政府执政期间发生战争的话，可能就意味着共和党的完蛋。”
    该杂志写道：盖洛普的民意测验结果表明，只有百分之八的美国人赞成派遣军队去印度支那，百分之八十五表示反对，百分之七没有表示意见。
