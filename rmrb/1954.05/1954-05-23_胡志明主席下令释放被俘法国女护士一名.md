Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Orders Release of Captured French Nurse

Author:

Date: 1954-05-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd dispatch: Vietnam News Agency reported on May 21st that Chairman Ho Chi Minh ordered the release of French nurse Geneviève de Galard, who was captured by the Vietnamese People's Army at Dien Bien Phu. On May 19th, Chairman Ho Chi Minh's birthday, Geneviève de Galard wrote a letter to Chairman Ho Chi Minh, thanking the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam for its magnanimous policy of releasing wounded and captured soldiers at Dien Bien Phu.

In her letter, de Galard also extended her wishes for peace to Chairman Ho Chi Minh. She said: "We all hope that our two countries can establish friendly relations as soon as possible in a world where war is prohibited.

"If I am fortunate enough to return to my homeland, I will definitely strive to create an atmosphere among the young people around me that will enable our two peoples to fully understand each other, so as to help maintain and rebuild the cause of peace that we all desire."

Chairman Ho Chi Minh replied to Geneviève de Galard, thanking her for her wishes.

### 胡志明主席下令释放被俘法国女护士一名

1954-05-23
第4版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席下令释放被俘法国女护士一名
    【新华社二十二日讯】据越南通讯社二十一日讯：胡志明主席命令释放在奠边府被越南人民军俘掳的法国女护士杰尼维夫·德格拉尔。杰尼维夫·德格拉尔，在五月十九日胡志明主席生日时，向胡志明主席写信，感谢越南民主共和国政府释放奠边府伤俘的宽大政策。
    德格拉尔在信中还向胡志明主席提出和平的祝愿。她说：“我们全都希望我们两国能够尽早地在一个禁止战争的世界上建立友谊的关系。
    “假若我能幸运地回到我的祖国，我一定要努力在我周围的青年中建立一种能使我们两国人民互相充分了解的气氛，以有助于维护与重建我们全都希望的和平事业。”
    胡志明主席复信杰尼维夫·德格拉尔，感谢她提出的祝愿。
