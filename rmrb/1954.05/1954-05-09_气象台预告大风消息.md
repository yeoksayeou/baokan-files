Title: Weather Bureau Forecasts Gale Warning
Author:
Date: 1954-05-09
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a gale warning at 8:00 PM on May 8th: Tonight, northern and western parts of North China will still have northerly winds of level 5 or 6. It is expected that the middle and lower reaches of the Han River, the Dongting Lake, and the areas near the Poyang Lake will have northerly winds of about level 6 on the evening of the 8th and the 9th. The southern Yellow Sea and the northern Jiangsu coast will have northerly winds of level 5 or 6. The East China Sea and the East China Sea coast, the mouth of the Yangtze River, and the mouth of the Qiantang River will have easterly winds of about level 6.

### 气象台预告大风消息

1954-05-09
第1版()
专栏：

    气象台预告大风消息
    【新华社八日讯】五月八日晚上八时中央气象台发布大风消息：今晚华北北部和西部仍有五、六级的偏北风。预计八日晚和九日汉水下游、洞庭湖和鄱阳湖附近地区有六级左右的偏北风。黄海南部海面和江苏北部沿岸有五、六级的偏北风，东海海面和东海沿岸、长江口和钱塘江口有六级左右的偏东风。
