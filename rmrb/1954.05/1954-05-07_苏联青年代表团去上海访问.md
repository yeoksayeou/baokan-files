Title: Soviet Youth Delegation Visits Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1954-05-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 5th: The Soviet Youth Delegation, invited to visit our country, left Beijing by train on the night of the 5th, accompanied by Qu Tangliang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, to visit Shanghai and other places.

Those who went to the station to see them off included Hu Yaobang, Liao Chengzhi, Liu Daosheng, Luo Yi, Rong Gaotang, and Hu Keshi, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Youth League; Ge Buhai, Secretary of the North China Working Committee of the Youth League; Zhang Dazhong, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Youth League; and more than 100 cadres from the central organs of the Youth League.

### 苏联青年代表团去上海访问

1954-05-07
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联青年代表团去上海访问
    【新华社五日讯】应邀来我国访问的苏联青年代表团五日夜在中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记区棠亮陪同下乘火车离开北京到上海等地访问。
    前往车站欢送的有青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡耀邦、廖承志、刘导生、罗毅、荣高棠、胡克实，青年团华北工作委员会书记葛步海，青年团北京市委员会书记张大中及青年团中央机关干部共一百多人。
