Title: Molotov Hosts a Banquet in Honor of British Foreign Secretary Eden
Author: 
Date: 1954-05-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 21st: TASS, Geneva—Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov hosted a banquet on the 20th in honor of British Foreign Secretary Eden.

### 莫洛托夫外长设宴招待英国外交大臣艾登

1954-05-22
第4版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫外长设宴招待英国外交大臣艾登
    【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社日内瓦讯：苏联外交部部长莫洛托夫二十日设宴招待英国外交大臣艾登。
