Title: Hungarian Working People's Party Congress Concludes Discussion of First Item on Agenda
Author:
Date: 1954-05-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, May 28th: According to TASS, the Third Congress of the Hungarian Working People's Party concluded the discussion of the first item on the agenda at the afternoon session on the 27th.

    Speakers at the session included L. Kovács, First Secretary of the Vas County Party Committee; B. Pál, Kossuth Prize-winning painter; F. Szabó, Kossuth Prize-winning composer; B. Fekete, Secretary of the Party Branch of the "Red Star" Agricultural Producers Cooperative in Sóstóhegy; and L. Köber, Secretary of the Party Committee of the Rakosi United Factory. In his speech, L. Kovács pointed out that the recent resolutions of the Hungarian Working People's Party and the government have sparked a political and labor upsurge among the farmers of Vas County, and the farmers' interest in agricultural production has increased. However, much work still needs to be done to enhance the prestige of agricultural producers' cooperatives and to combat the conspiracies of class enemies. He criticized the bureaucratic leadership methods of agricultural organs.

    Over the past three days, a total of forty people have spoken in the discussion of the first item on the agenda. At the afternoon session on the 27th, the Congress unanimously adopted a resolution approving the general line and practical work of the Central Committee, and decided to take the tasks put forward in the report of Comrade Rákosi, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, as the Party's political line.

    At the afternoon session on the 27th, representatives of the Albanian Labor Party, the Communist Party of Great Britain, the Finnish Communist Party, and the Spanish Communist Party delivered congratulatory messages to the Congress, and they were warmly welcomed by the delegates to the Congress.

    When the Congress read out the congratulatory telegram from the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party to the Congress, the entire venue erupted in sustained applause.

### 匈劳动人民党代表大会首项议程讨论结束

1954-05-30
第4版()
专栏：

    匈劳动人民党代表大会首项议程讨论结束
    【新华社二十九日讯】据塔斯社布达佩斯二十八日讯：匈牙利劳动人民党第三次代表大会二十七日下午会议结束了第一项议程的讨论。
    在这次会议上发言的有瓦斯州党委会第一书记鲁·科瓦奇，科苏特奖金得奖人、画家贝·保尔，科苏特奖金得奖人、作曲家弗·萨波，绍什托赫地方的“红星”农业生产合作社党组织书记贝·菲凯特，拉科西联合工厂党委书记拉·科贝尔等。鲁·科瓦奇在发言中指出，匈牙利劳动人民党和政府最近的决议，掀起了瓦斯州农民的政治和劳动的热潮，农民对农业生产的兴趣提高了。但是，还必须进行许多工作来提高农业生产合作社的威信和打击阶级敌人的阴谋。他对农业机关官僚主义的领导方法提出了批评。
    三天来，在讨论第一项议程中发言的共有四十人。大会在二十七日下午一致通过了一项决议，批准中央委员会的总路线和实际工作，并决定以匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会第一书记拉科西同志的报告中所提出的任务作为党的政治方针。
    在二十七日下午会议上向代表大会致贺词的有阿尔巴尼亚劳动党、英国共产党、芬兰共产党和西班牙共产党的代表，他们受到大会代表热烈的欢迎。
    当大会宣读朝鲜劳动党中央委员会给大会的贺电时，全场响起经久不息的掌声。
