Title: Czech Art History Expert Hašěk Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1954-05-08
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, May 2nd: Lubor Hašěk, Director of the Oriental Department of the Czechoslovak National Gallery and expert in Far Eastern art history, arrived in Beijing by plane on May 2nd for a visit to China in accordance with the Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 捷美术史专家哈谢克到北京

1954-05-08
第4版()
专栏：

    捷美术史专家哈谢克到北京
    【新华社二日讯】根据中捷文化合作协定来我国访问的捷克斯洛伐克国家美术馆东方司司长、远东美术史专家露博尔·哈谢克博士在五月二日乘飞机到达北京。
