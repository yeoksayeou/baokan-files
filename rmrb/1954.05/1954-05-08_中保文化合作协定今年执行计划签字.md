Title: Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement this Year's Implementation Plan Signed
Author:
Date: 1954-05-08
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 4th. The 1954 Implementation Plan of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement was signed in Sofia on May 4th. The signatories were Cao Xiangren, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Bulgaria, and Ra. Todorova, Chairman of the Bulgarian Committee for Foreign Friendship and Cultural Relations.
    In addition to the negotiating representatives of both parties, the signing ceremony was attended by Bulgarian Minister of Culture Ru. Levi, Minister of Education Jie. Yaniev, Director of the First Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Tsaerfulanov, Director of the Department of Communication Alekseev, editors-in-chief of Sofia newspapers, and more than 30 people from the cultural and educational circles, including Ma. Kirilova and Ge. Karaslavov, who had visited China.

### 中保文化合作协定今年执行计划签字

1954-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

    中保文化合作协定今年执行计划签字
    【新华社索非亚四日电】中保文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划，五月四日在索非亚签字。在执行计划上签字的中国方面为中华人民共和国驻保加利亚大使曹祥仁，保加利亚方面为保加利亚对外友好与文化联络委员会主席拉·托多罗娃。
    参加签字仪式的除双方谈判代表外，尚有保加利亚文化部长鲁·列维、教育部长杰·雅涅夫、外交部第一司长察尔伏拉诺夫、交际司长亚历克谢夫、索非亚各报社社长以及访问过中国的玛·基里洛娃和格·卡拉斯拉沃夫等文化教育界人士共三十余人。
