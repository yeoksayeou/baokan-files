Title: Panchen Lama Presides Over Prayer Ceremony to Commemorate the Third Anniversary of the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet
Author:
Date: 1954-05-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, May 26th: On the eve of the third anniversary of the peaceful liberation of Tibet, the Panchen Erdeni presided over a prayer ceremony for good fortune at the Deqing Pozhang (a temple belonging to the Zhaxilhunbu Monastery) in the western suburbs of Xigaze. More than 40,000 monks and laypeople from various places came to attend the ceremony. During the assembly, in addition to reciting famous Tibetan scriptures, the Panchen Erdeni used many vivid examples to tell the monks and laypeople attending the meeting about the greatness of Chairman Mao's ethnic policies and the achievements made by the People's Liberation Army in Tibet and the Tibetan people in implementing the agreement on the peaceful liberation of Tibet over the past three years. He called on the Tibetan monks and laypeople to further unite closely, actively participate in various constructions under the leadership of the Central People's Government and with the help of all fraternal ethnic groups in the motherland, consolidate national defense, and strive for the complete realization of the agreement.
During the assembly, the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Xigaze screened movies and staged Tibetan-language dramas for the monks and laypeople attending the meeting.

### 纪念西藏和平解放三周年  班禅主持祈祷法会

1954-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

    纪念西藏和平解放三周年
    班禅主持祈祷法会
    【新华社拉萨二十六日电】西藏和平解放三周年纪念日前夕，班禅额尔德尼在日喀则西郊的德庆颇章（札什伦布寺所属的一个殿堂）主持举行祈祷吉祥法会。从各地赶来赴会的僧俗人民有四万多人。会期中，班禅额尔德尼除诵读西藏有名的经文外，并用许多生动事例，向赴会僧俗人民讲述毛主席民族政策的伟大和三年来进藏人民解放军和西藏人民执行和平解放西藏办法的协议所获得的成就。他号召西藏僧俗人民进一步亲密团结，在中央人民政府的领导和祖国各兄弟民族的帮助下，积极参加各项建设，巩固祖国国防，为彻底实现协议而努力。
    会期中，驻日喀则的中国人民解放军曾给赴会的僧俗人民放映电影和上演藏语话剧。
