Title: Mongolian Cycling Team Visits Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1954-05-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 5th:

    The Mongolian People's Republic Ulaanbaatar-Beijing Cycling Expedition, which came to China for a visit, left Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 5th to visit Tianjin, Shanghai, and other places. During their stay in Beijing, in addition to participating in the May Day parade, the cycling expedition visited Beijing's scenic spots - the Summer Palace and the Forbidden City, and visited the Central Institute of Physical Education and the sports performances held at the Labor People's Cultural Palace during the "May Fourth" Youth Day. The head of the cycling team, Danbi, held a forum with responsible comrades from the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the Central People's Government to exchange information and experiences on the development of sports in Mongolia and China.

### 蒙古自行车队去天津参观

1954-05-07
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古自行车队去天津参观
    【新华社五日讯】来我国访问的蒙古人民共和国乌兰巴托—北京自行车远足队在五日下午乘火车离京到天津、上海等地游览。在北京期间，自行车远足队除参加五一节游行外，并游览了北京的名胜——颐和园和故宫，参观了中央体育学院以及“五四”青年节在劳动人民文化宫举行的体育表演。自行车队负责人丹毕曾和我中央人民政府体育运动委员会的负责同志举行座谈会，交换蒙中两国人民体育运动开展的情况与经验。
