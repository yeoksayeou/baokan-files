Our Great Motherland
    Since its establishment a year ago, the Shanghai China Welfare Institute Children's Palace has seen approximately 200,000 children participate in its various collective activities. Currently, there are nine groups established, including science and engineering, chemical industry, biology, aviation model, fine arts, music, dance, literature, and embroidery. This is the dance group, whose members are practicing the "Knight's Dance."

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    上海中国福利会少年宫成立一年来，参加少年宫各项集体活动的少年儿童前后约有二十万人。目前已成立了理工、化工、生物、航空模型、美术、音乐、舞蹈、文学、刺绣等九个小组。这是舞蹈小组、的组员，正在练习“骑士舞”。
