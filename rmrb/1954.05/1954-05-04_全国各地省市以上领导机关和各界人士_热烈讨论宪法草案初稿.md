Title: Leaders of Provincial and Municipal Organs and People from All Walks of Life Across the Country Enthusiastically Discuss the Draft Constitution

Author:
Date: 1954-05-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: Leaders of provincial and municipal organs and people from all walks of life across the country are enthusiastically discussing the initial draft of the Constitution of the People's Republic of China. As of April 25th, with the exception of the Northeast Region and the provinces and cities of Hebei, Gansu, Xinjiang, Sichuan, Chengdu, Jiangxi, and Anshan, which are preparing to begin discussions at the end of April or the beginning of May, five major administrative regions, one autonomous region, twenty-three provinces, twenty-one centrally administered municipalities, and provincial cities with populations of over 500,000 have entered the stage of in-depth discussion. More than 6,000 representatives from various sectors, including leading cadres, democratic parties, people's organizations, industry and commerce, cultural circles, education circles, science circles, ethnic minorities, returned overseas Chinese, and religious circles, are participating in the discussions.

All regions attach great importance to the discussion of the initial draft of the Constitution. Major administrative regions and provinces/municipalities have generally established committees for the discussion of the initial draft of the Constitution or central groups for the discussion of the initial draft of the Constitution, and have set up offices. Groups that have started discussions have generally held two to four discussion meetings. This discussion will end in late May.

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 3rd Telegram: Responsible persons of various organs, democratic parties, and people's organizations in the Northeast Administrative Region, as well as responsible persons of organs and organizations in Shenyang, Fushun, Luda, Songjiang and other provinces and cities, have begun discussing the initial draft of the Constitution of the People's Republic of China.

The Northeast Administrative Committee convened a meeting on the 27th of responsible persons from Party, government, and military organs at the Northeast level, as well as from democratic parties and people's organizations, and established the Committee for the Discussion of the Initial Draft of the Constitution. Lin Feng, First Deputy Secretary of the Northeast Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Chairman of the Northeast Administrative Committee, was appointed as the Chairman, with Gao Chongmin, Li Zhuoran, Wang Jinxiang, Gu Zhuoxin, Zhou Huan, and Che Xiangchen as Vice Chairmen. The committee has an office responsible for the specific organization and liaison work related to the discussion of the initial draft of the Constitution. A total of 365 people are participating in the discussions, which began on April 30th and will end at the end of May. The Shenyang Municipal People's Government Committee and the Shenyang Municipal People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee held a joint meeting on the 20th and decided that the Municipal Consultative Committee would preside over and lead the discussion of the initial draft of the Constitution by responsible persons from various organs, democratic parties, and people's organizations in Shenyang. More than 140 people are participating in the discussion, which began on the 20th with two to three discussions per week, and is scheduled to end on May 24th.

### 全国各地省市以上领导机关和各界人士  热烈讨论宪法草案初稿

1954-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

    全国各地省市以上领导机关和各界人士
    热烈讨论宪法草案初稿
    【新华社讯】全国各地省、市以上领导机关和各界人士，正在热烈地讨论中华人民共和国宪法草案初稿。到四月二十五日为止，除东北区一级和河北、甘肃、新疆、四川、成都、江西、鞍山等省、市准备在四月底或五月初开始讨论外，全国已有五个大行政区、一个自治区、二十三个省、二十一个中央直辖市和人口在五十万以上的省辖市，进入了深入讨论的阶段。参加讨论的有各地领导机关负责人，各民主党派、各人民团体、工商界、文化界、教育界、科学界及少数民族、归国华侨、宗教界等各方面的代表人士共六千多人。
    各地对宪法草案初稿的讨论都很重视。大区和省、市普遍成立了宪法草案初稿讨论委员会或宪法草案初稿讨论中心组，并设立了办公机构。已开始讨论的小组一般都举行过二次到四次讨论会。这一讨论将在五月下旬结束。
    【新华社沈阳三日电】东北行政区各机关、各民主党派和各人民团体负责人，以及沈阳、抚顺、旅大、松江等省市机关、团体负责人，已开始讨论中华人民共和国宪法草案初稿。
    东北行政委员会在二十七日召集东北一级党、政、军机关和各民主党派、各人民团体负责人开会，成立了宪法草案初稿讨论委员会，由中共中央东北局第一副书记、东北行政委员会副主席林枫任主任委员，高崇民、李卓然、汪金祥、顾卓新、周桓、车向忱等任副主任委员。委员会下设办公室，负责有关宪法草案初稿讨论的具体组织和联络等工作。参加讨论的有三百六十五人，从四月三十日开始讨论，到五月底结束。沈阳市人民政府委员会和沈阳市各界人民代表会议协商委员会在二十日举行联席会议，决定由市协商委员会主持和领导沈阳市各机关、各民主党派、各人民团体负责人的宪法草案初稿的讨论工作。参加讨论的有一百四十多人，从二十日已开始每周讨论两次到三次，准备到五月二十四日结束。
