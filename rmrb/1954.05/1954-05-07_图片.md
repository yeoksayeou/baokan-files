    On May 1, 1954, a grand parade and labor people's festival procession were held on Red Square in Moscow. On the reviewing stand, from left to right: S.M. Budyonny, N.G. Kuznetsov, G.K. Zhukov, A.M. Vasilevsky, K.E. Voroshilov, N.A. Bulganin, G.M. Malenkov, N.S. Khrushchev, L.M. Kaganovich, A.I. Mikoyan, M.Z. Saburov, N.M. Shvernik, M.A. Suslov, P.N. Pospelov, N.N. Shatalin, V.A. Malyshev, I.F. Tevosyan, A.N. Kosygin. With the Soviet party and government leaders are children offering them flowers. (TASS dispatch)
