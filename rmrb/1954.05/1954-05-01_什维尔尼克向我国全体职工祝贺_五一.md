Title: Shvernik Congratulates All Chinese Workers on "May Day"
Author: Ni·Shweiernike
Date: 1954-05-01
Page: 4

Comrade Lai Ruoyu of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions:

    On behalf of the Soviet working people, the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions extends congratulations to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and all workers of the People's Republic of China on the festival of solidarity of working people around the world and the festival of fraternal friendship of workers of all countries – "May Day."

    The All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions warmly congratulates the Chinese working people and the Chinese trade unions on their new achievements in the cause of national socialist industrialization, in raising the national economy and culture, and in further developing and consolidating their people's democratic system.

    Long live the unbreakable, brotherly friendship and cooperation between the people of the Soviet Union and China, a powerful factor in maintaining peace and safeguarding the security of all nations! May this friendship and cooperation develop and flourish!

      Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, N. Shvernik

### 什维尔尼克向我国全体职工祝贺“五一”
尼·什维尔尼克
1954-05-01
第4版()
专栏：

     什维尔尼克向我国全体职工祝贺“五一”中华全国总工会赖若愚同志：
    全苏工会中央理事会代表苏联劳动人民，向中华全国总工会和中华人民共和国全体职工祝贺全世界劳动人民团结的节日和一切国家工人兄弟友爱的节日——“五一”节。
    全苏工会中央理事会热烈地祝贺中国劳动人民和中国工会在国家社会主义工业化、在提高国民经济和文化、在进一步发展和巩固自己的人民民主制度的事业中获得新的成就。
    维护和平与保卫各国人民安全的强有力的因素——苏中两国人民牢不可破的、兄弟般的友谊和合作万岁！祝这种友谊和合作发展昌盛！
      全苏工会中央理事会主席  尼·什维尔尼克
