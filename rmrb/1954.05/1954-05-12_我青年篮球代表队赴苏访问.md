Title: Our Youth Basketball Delegation Visits the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1954-05-12
Page: 4

    [This News] Our Chinese Youth Basketball Delegation (including men's and women's teams), a group of thirty-four people, led by team leader Chai Jisen, left Beijing by train on the afternoon of May 9th to visit the Soviet Union in Moscow. Those who came to the station to see them off included Huang Zhong, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, representatives from the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, the North China and Beijing Municipal Sports Commissions, and Zhong Shitong, President of the Central Institute of Physical Education, and Xu Yingchao, Vice President, and others.

### 我青年篮球代表队赴苏访问

1954-05-12
第4版()
专栏：

    我青年篮球代表队赴苏访问
    【本报讯】中国青年篮球代表队（包括男子队和女子队）一行三十四人，由领队柴吉森率领下，已于五月九日下午乘火车离京前往莫斯科访问苏联。到车站欢送的有中央人民政府体育运动委员会副秘书长黄中，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部文化部、华北和北京市体育运动委员会等单位的代表，以及中央体育学院院长锺师统、副院长徐英超等。
