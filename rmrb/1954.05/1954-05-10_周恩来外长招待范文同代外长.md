Title: Zhou Enlai Hosts a Reception for Acting Foreign Minister Pham Van Dong

Author:
Date: 1954-05-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 8th. Zhou Enlai, Chief Representative of the Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Geneva Conference, and Minister of Foreign Affairs, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 8th to welcome Pham Van Dong, Chief Representative of the Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs.

The Vietnamese side invited to the banquet included: Phan Anh, Minister of Industry and Commerce, Tran Cong Tuong, Deputy Minister of Justice, Hoang Van Hoan, Ambassador to China, and Ta Quang Buu, Deputy Minister of National Defense, among others from the Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

The Chinese side attending the banquet included: Zhang Wentian, Wang Jiaxiang, Li Kenong, Wang Bingnan, Qiao Guanhua, Chen Jiakang, Huang Hua, and Wang Zhuoru.

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 周恩来外长招待范文同代外长

1954-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来外长招待范文同代外长
    【新华社日内瓦八日电】出席日内瓦会议的中华人民共和国代表团首席代表、外交部部长周恩来八日晚设宴招待越南民主共和国代表团首席代表、代理外交部部长范文同。
    越南方面应邀出席宴会的还有：越南民主共和国代表团代表工商部部长潘英、司法部副部长陈公祥、驻华大使黄文欢、国防部副部长谢光宝等。
    中国方面出席宴会的有：张闻天、王稼祥、李克农、王炳南、乔冠华、陈家康、黄华、王倬如。
    宴会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
