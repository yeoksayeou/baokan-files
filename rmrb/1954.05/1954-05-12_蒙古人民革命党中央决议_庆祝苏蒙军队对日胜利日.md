Title: Resolution of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party on Celebrating the Anniversary of the Victory of Soviet-Mongolian Troops Against Japan
Author:
Date: 1954-05-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, May 10th: The Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party has passed a resolution to celebrate the fifteenth anniversary of the glorious victory of the Soviet and Mongolian troops over the Japanese aggressors in the Khalkhin Gol region, to commemorate the heroic Soviet soldiers who sacrificed their lives for the freedom and independence of the Mongolian People's Republic, and to approve the plan for celebrating this historical festival.

    The resolution of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party decided to erect a monument in Ulan Bator to express the Mongolian people's deep gratitude and boundless thanks to the heroic Soviet army.

### 蒙古人民革命党中央决议  庆祝苏蒙军队对日胜利日

1954-05-12
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民革命党中央决议
    庆祝苏蒙军队对日胜利日
    【新华社十一日讯】据塔斯社乌兰巴托五月十日讯：蒙古人民革命党中央委员会通过了一项决议，决定庆祝苏联和蒙古军队在哈尔新河地区战胜日本侵略者的光荣胜利的十五周年纪念，追悼为蒙古人民共和国的自由和独立而捐躯的英勇的苏军战士，并批准了庆祝这一历史节日的计划。
    蒙古人民革命党中央委员会的决议决定在乌兰巴托建立纪念碑，以表示蒙古人民对英勇的苏联军队的深切的感戴和无限的谢意。
