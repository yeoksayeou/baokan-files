Title: Khmer People's Liberation Army Annihilates Enemy Troops at An Phong
Author:
Date: 1954-05-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 5th: According to the Vietnam News Agency, the Government of the Khmer Resistance announced on the 4th that the Khmer People's Liberation Army launched an attack on An Phong, a relatively large enemy stronghold located 40 kilometers north of Chai Zhen, the capital of Chai Zhen Province in southeastern Cambodia. The entire enemy force defending the stronghold was annihilated, and eighty machine guns and rifles were seized.

### 高棉人民解放军歼安封敌军

1954-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

    高棉人民解放军歼安封敌军
    【新华社五日讯】据越南通讯社讯：高棉抗战政府四日发表的公报说，高棉人民解放军对位于高棉东南部柴桢省省会柴桢以北四十公里的一个比较大的敌军据点安封发动攻击，歼灭了防守该据点的全部敌军，并缴获敌军机枪和步枪八十挺（支）。
