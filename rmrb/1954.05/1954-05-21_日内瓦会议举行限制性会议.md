Title: Geneva Conference Holds Restricted Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-05-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 19th—The delegations of the nine countries participating in the Geneva Conference on the Indochina issue held a restricted meeting on the afternoon of the 19th and issued the following communique:

    “The nine delegations continued to discuss the issue of restoring peace in Indochina in a restricted meeting.
    Another restricted meeting will be held on May 21st.”

### 日内瓦会议举行限制性会议

1954-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

    日内瓦会议举行限制性会议
    【新华社日内瓦十九日电】参加日内瓦会议关于印度支那问题讨论的九个国家的代表团十九日下午举行了限制性的会议后发表公报如下：
    “九国代表团在限制性会议中继续讨论恢复印度支那和平问题。
    五月二十一日将再举行限制性会议。”
