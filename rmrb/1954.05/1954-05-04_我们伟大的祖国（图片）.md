Author:

    Our Great Motherland
    The rapid development of the telecommunications industry not only strengthens the mutual connections between various parts of our country, but also plays a significant role in building the motherland and consolidating national defense. This is a picture of Li Peilin, a model worker at the Beijing Telecommunications Bureau and a long-distance operator, connecting calls.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    电信事业的迅速发展，不仅加强了我国各地的相互联系，而且对建设祖国和巩固国防也都有重大的作用。这是北京电信局劳动模范、长途台话务员李佩琳接线的情形。
