Title: Geneva Conference Holds Restricted Meeting to Continue Discussion on Restoring Peace in Indochina

Author:
Date: 1954-05-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 18th: The delegations of the nine countries participating in the Geneva Conference's discussions on the Indochina issue held a restricted meeting on the afternoon of the 18th. A communique was issued afterwards stating: "The nine delegations, at their restricted meeting on May 18th, continued to discuss the issue of restoring peace in Indochina."

### 日内瓦会议举行限制性会议  继续讨论恢复印度支那和平问题

1954-05-20
第4版()
专栏：

    日内瓦会议举行限制性会议
    继续讨论恢复印度支那和平问题
    【新华社日内瓦十八日电】参加日内瓦会议关于印度支那问题讨论的九个国家的代表团十八日下午举行了限制性的会议后发表公报如下：“九国代表团在五月十八日的限制性会议中，继续商讨了恢复印度支那和平的问题。”
