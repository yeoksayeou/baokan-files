Title: French Comedy
Author: Yuan Shuibai
Date: 1954-05-27
Page: 3

    French Comedy
    Yuan Shuibai
    News report: The French government has banned the Soviet Ballet Company from performing in Paris.
    "What? Russian ballet?
    Good heavens, is there anything more terrifying than this!
    Stop! Quickly, draw the curtain!
    "What? Ulanova's pointe work?
    Good heavens, is there anything more dangerous than this!
    Stop! Absolutely cannot be staged!"
    A patient with consumption
    Is startled by the sound of a pin dropping;
    A Lanier government like today's
    Cannot withstand a classical dance performance!
    The tenderness of the prince by "Swan Lake"
    May provoke the jealousy of Wall Street bosses;
    The beauty of the maiden in "The Bronze Horseman"
    Might cause the Great French cabinet to collapse.
    Pushkin's rhymes, Tchaikovsky's melodies,
    Constitute a "communist threat";
    The people do not want hunger, the people demand culture,
    So you have to fabricate the myth of "Soviet aggression."
    The Soviet Ballet Company is banned from performing in Paris,
    But the French Comédie-Française is performing in Leningrad.
    Noble gentlemen, where is the Soviet "iron curtain"?
    Where is the freedom of the "free world" you boast of?

### 法兰西喜剧
袁水拍
1954-05-27
第3版()
专栏：

    法兰西喜剧
    袁水拍
    报载：法国政府禁止苏联巴蕾舞蹈团在巴黎演出。
    “什么？俄罗斯的巴蕾舞？
    上帝啊，还有什么比这更恐怖！
    停止！快快给我拉上幕！
    “什么？乌兰诺娃的足尖？
    上帝啊，还有什么比这更危险！
    停止！万万不能上演！”
    一个五痨七伤的病人
    听见针掉下地都会吃惊；
    一个像今天这样的拉尼埃政府
    就经不起一场古典舞的演出！
    “天鹅湖”畔王子的柔情
    也许会惹起华尔街老板的嫉恨；
    “青铜骑士”里少女的美貌
    说不定会使大法兰西内阁倾倒。
    普希金的韵脚，柴可夫斯基的旋律，
    构成了“共产主义的威胁”；
    人民不要饥饿，人民要求文化，
    所以你们要制造“苏联侵略”的神话。
    苏联巴蕾舞蹈团在巴黎被禁演，
    法兰西喜剧团却在列宁格勒表演。
    高贵的绅士们，苏维埃的“铁幕”呢？
    你们所吹嘘的“自由世界”的自由呢？
