Title: DPRK Foreign Minister Nam Il Hosts Foreign Minister Molotov

Author: 
Date: 1954-05-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 6th—According to a Korean Central News Agency reporter in Geneva: Nam Il, chief representative of the delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the Geneva Conference and Minister of Foreign Affairs, hosted a banquet on the 4th in honor of V.M. Molotov, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union.

The Soviet side attending the banquet included: A.A. Gromyko, V.V. Kuznetsov, G.N. Zarubin, S.P. Suzdalev, P.F. Yudin, F.F. Molochkov, L.F. Ilyichev, Fedelin, A.A. Soldatov.

The Korean side attending the banquet included: Bai Nan-un, Ki Sok-bok, Jang Chun-san, Tian Dong-he, Kim Taek-yong, Kim Myong-gui, Bang Seung-jik, and Moon Jae-soo.

The banquet was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 南日外务相招待莫洛托夫外长

1954-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

    南日外务相招待莫洛托夫外长
    【新华社平壤六日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社驻日内瓦记者报道：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国出席日内瓦会议代表团首席代表、外务相南日在四日设宴招待苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫。
    苏联方面出席宴会的有：安·阿·葛罗米柯、瓦·瓦·库兹涅佐夫、格·纳·查鲁宾、斯·普·苏兹达列夫、帕·费·尤金、费·弗·莫洛奇科夫、勒·弗·伊利切夫、费德林、阿·阿·索尔达托夫。
    朝鲜方面出席宴会的有：白南云、奇石福、张春山、田东赫、金泽泳、金明龟、方承直、文在洙。
    宴会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
