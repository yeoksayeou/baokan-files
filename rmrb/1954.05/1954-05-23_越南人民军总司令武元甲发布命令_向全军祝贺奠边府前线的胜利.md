Title: General Vo Nguyen Giap, Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnamese People's Army, Issues Order, Congratulating the Entire Army on the Victory at the Dien Bien Phu Front
Author:
Date: 1954-05-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd: According to the Vietnam News Agency on May 21st, General Vo Nguyen Giap, Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnamese People's Army, issued an order to the cadres and soldiers of the meritorious units of the Vietnamese People's Army on the Dien Bien Phu front, as well as to the cadres and soldiers of the regular army, local forces, militia, and guerrillas throughout the country, stating that he was acting on the orders of President Ho Chi Minh and the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to congratulate the entire army on the glorious victory at the Dien Bien Phu front.

General Vo Nguyen Giap, after emphasizing that the victory at Dien Bien Phu was the greatest victory in the history of the Vietnamese People's Army's resistance war, continued by saying that together with the regular army, local forces, militia, and guerrillas of the Vietnamese People's Army, and together with the brotherly forces of the Khmer and Laotian People's Liberation Armies, we have fundamentally thwarted the "Navarre Plan" and dealt a heavy blow to the conspiracy of the American interventionists and French colonial war-mongers to expand the war.

Analyzing the reasons for achieving this victory, General Vo Nguyen Giap pointed out the wise leadership of the Vietnam Workers' Party and the government, the heroic fighting and overcoming of difficulties by the cadres and soldiers, and the comprehensive coordination of the laborers, the people in the rear, the local forces, and the guerrillas. General Vo Nguyen Giap, on behalf of the Vietnamese People's Army, expressed gratitude to all the laborers and the entire people, and extended warm congratulations to all the cadres and soldiers of the People's Army on all fronts throughout the country.

General Vo Nguyen Giap emphasized the close coordination on the battlefields of Vietnam, Cambodia, and Laos. He said: The glorious victory of the Vietnamese People's Army at Dien Bien Phu is due to the close coordination of the People's Liberation Armies of the two brotherly countries of Cambodia and Laos. I would like to express my gratitude to the People's Liberation Armies of Cambodia and Laos on behalf of the Vietnamese People's Army.

General Vo Nguyen Giap emphasized that the Vietnamese People's Army should not be complacent because of this and should not underestimate the enemy. He also called on the People's Army to resolutely smash every new conspiracy of the enemy.

### 越南人民军总司令武元甲发布命令  向全军祝贺奠边府前线的胜利

1954-05-23
第4版()
专栏：

    越南人民军总司令武元甲发布命令
    向全军祝贺奠边府前线的胜利
    【新华社二十二日讯】据越南通讯社二十一日讯：越南人民军总司令武元甲将军向奠边府战线上的越南人民军作战有功部队的干部和战士以及全国的正规军、人民军地方部队、民兵和游击队的干部和战士发布命令说：他奉胡志明主席和越南民主共和国政府的命令，向全军祝贺奠边府前线的光辉胜利。
    武元甲将军在着重指出这次奠边府的胜利是越南人民军抗战史上最大的一次胜利以后，接着说，我们和全国的越南人民军的正规军、地方部队、民兵和游击队一起，和高棉及寮国人民解放军兄弟部队一起，我们已经基本上挫败了“纳瓦尔计划”，并给美国干涉者和法国殖民主义战争贩子扩大战争的阴谋以沉重的打击。
    在分析取得这次胜利的原因时，武元甲将军指出了越南劳动党和政府的英明领导，干部和战士的英勇战斗和克服困难的精神以及民工们、后方人民、地方部队及游击队的全面配合。武元甲将军代表越南人民军向所有的民工和全体人民表示感谢，并向全国各战线上的人民军全体干部和战士致热烈祝贺。
    武元甲将军着重指出了越南、高棉和寮国三国战线上的密切配合。他说：越南人民军在奠边府取得辉煌的胜利是由于高棉和寮国两个兄弟国家的人民解放军的密切配合。我谨代表越南人民军向高棉和寮国的人民解放军表示感谢。
    武元甲将军强调说，越南人民军不要因此而自满，不要对敌人估计不足。他并号召人民军坚决粉碎敌人的每一个新的阴谋。
