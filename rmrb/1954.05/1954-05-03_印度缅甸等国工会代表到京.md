Title: Trade Union Representatives from India, Burma, and Other Countries Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-05-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: Indian trade union representatives and a Burmese trade union delegation, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the May Day celebrations in China, arrived in Beijing by train.

The list of Indian trade union representatives is as follows:

	Shyambehari Sharan, Acting Representative of the All-India United Trade Union Congress, Secretary of the All-India United Trade Union Congress, Bihar Province
	Nandaraju Ragavendra Rao, Executive Committee Member of the All India Railwaymen’s Federation
	Binoy Roy, President of the All India Bank Employees Association
	V.V. Joshi, Secretary of the All India Trade Union Congress, Rajasthan Province
	H. Saxena, President of the All India Sugar Workers' Federation
	Sisir Roy, Council Member of the All-India United Trade Union Congress

The list of the Burmese trade union delegation is as follows:

	Head of Delegation: Tekin Lun, Chairman of the All-Burma Trade Union Congress
	Secretary: Ko Dong Sheng, Secretary of the Labor Protection Department of the All-Burma Trade Union Congress, Secretary of the All-Rangoon Cooperative Workers' Union
	Member: Guo Juemao, Secretary of the Wanxinglong Lumber Mill Trade Union

In addition: Israeli trade union representatives, Shmuel Schemolo, Hoiberger Yitzhak, Tunnier Yoel and two Greek trade union representatives arrived in Beijing on April 27th and 28th.
