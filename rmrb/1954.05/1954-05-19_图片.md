Title: The Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Attending the Geneva Conference Arrives in Geneva
Author:
Date: 1954-05-19
Page: 4

    The delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam attending the Geneva Conference arrived in Geneva on May 4th. The picture shows the chief delegate of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam delegation, Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Pham Van Dong (first person from the left in the front row), and Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of our country (first person from the right), and Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Gromyko (first person to the left of Pham Van Dong wearing a black coat) walking out of the airport, who came to the airport to welcome them. (Xinhua News Agency Draft)
