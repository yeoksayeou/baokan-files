Our Great Motherland
The expansion project of Daye Steel Plant is undergoing construction preparation. After the expansion, Daye Steel Plant will become a well-equipped steel plant in southern China, capable of producing a variety of high-quality steel. This is a crane unloading mechanical equipment continuously transported from various places.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-05-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    大冶钢厂的扩建工程正在进行施工准备。扩建后的大冶钢厂将成为我国南部的一个设备完善的、能够生产多种优质钢的钢厂。这是起重机正在起卸从各地源源运来的机械设备。
