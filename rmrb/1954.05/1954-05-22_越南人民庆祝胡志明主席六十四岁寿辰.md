Title: Vietnamese People Celebrate Chairman Ho Chi Minh's Sixty-Fourth Birthday
Author:
Date: 1954-05-22
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 21st: According to Vietnam News Agency, people in various parts of Vietnam held mass rallies on May 19th to warmly celebrate Chairman Ho Chi Minh's sixty-fourth birthday.
    On this day, Chairman Ho Chi Minh received guests who came to offer congratulations at the Presidential Palace. These guests included the Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Vietnamese National Assembly, ministers and deputy ministers of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, as well as representatives from the Vietnamese National United Front, the Vietnam Workers' Party, and various mass organizations, along with combat heroes, labor models, elders, and children.
    Ton Duc Thang, representing the Vietnamese National Assembly and the Vietnamese National United Front, expressed the trust and gratitude of all the people towards Chairman Ho Chi Minh and wished him good health to lead the nation in the patriotic struggle for peace, independence, unity, and democracy.
    Other representatives also recounted the Vietnamese people's love and gratitude towards Chairman Ho Chi Minh and pledged to further strengthen the unity of the people.
    Chairman Ho Chi Minh expressed his gratitude to the representatives and asked them to convey his thanks to all his compatriots.

### 越南人民庆祝胡志明主席六十四岁寿辰

1954-05-22
第4版()
专栏：

    越南人民庆祝胡志明主席六十四岁寿辰
    【新华社二十一日讯】据越南通讯社讯：越南各地人民在五月十九日举行了群众大会，热烈庆祝胡志明主席的六十四岁寿辰。
    胡志明主席这一天在主席府接见了前来祝贺的来宾。这些来宾有越南国民大会常务委员会主席、越南民主共和国政府的部长和副部长，还有越南国民联合战线，越南劳动党和各群众团体的代表，以及战斗英雄、劳动模范、老年人和儿童。
    孙德胜代表越南国民大会和越南国民联合战线表示了全体人民对胡志明主席的信任和感谢，并祝贺他身体健康，以领导全国人民进行争取和平、独立、统一和民主的爱国斗争。
    其他代表也都叙述了越南人民对胡志明主席的热爱和感谢，并保证进一步加强人民的团结。
    胡志明主席向代表们表示感谢，并请他们把他的谢意转达给全体同胞。
