Title: PLA Units' Leading Organs Discuss Draft Constitution

Author:
Date: 1954-05-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th — The leading organs of the People's Liberation Army units are enthusiastically discussing the draft constitution of the People's Republic of China. The departments and command headquarters of various military branches under the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission formed discussion groups on the draft constitution in early April, with the leading heads of each department serving as group leaders. The General Political Department of the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission also established an office for the discussion of the draft constitution, responsible for collecting opinions from various departments' discussions. The leading organs of the major military regions also established discussion groups in April, stipulating the time and steps for discussion, and have already conducted earnest discussions.

### 解放军各部队领导机关讨论宪法草案初稿

1954-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    解放军各部队领导机关讨论宪法草案初稿
    【新华社十二日讯】人民解放军各部队领导机关正热烈讨论中华人民共和国宪法草案初稿。中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会所属各部、各兵种司令部在四月初就已组成宪法草案初稿的讨论小组，由各部门负责首长担任组长。中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部并成立了宪法草案初稿讨论的办公室，负责收集各部门讨论的意见。各大军区领导机关也已在四月间先后成立了讨论小组，规定了讨论的时间和步骤，并已进行了认真的讨论。
