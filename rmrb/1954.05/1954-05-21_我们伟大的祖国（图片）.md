    Our Great Motherland
    Fujian Province has a vast area of natural forest land, distributed in more than forty counties, and is one of the four major forest areas in China. Fir trees on both banks of the Futun River are being transported out in a steady stream to meet the needs of construction and railway sleepers in various places.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国福建省天然林地面积广阔，分布达四十余县，是我国四大林区之一。富屯溪两岸的杉木正源源外运，供各地建设和铁路枕木的需要。
