Title: Acting Foreign Minister Pham Van Dong Hosts a Banquet in Honor of Foreign Minister Molotov
Author:
Date: 1954-05-26
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 24th: Pham Van Dong, Chief Representative and Acting Foreign Minister of the Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Geneva Conference, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 24th in honor of Soviet Foreign Minister V.M. Molotov.
    On the Soviet side, Deputy Foreign Ministers A.A. Gromyko, V. Kuznetsov, and member of the Collegium of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, K.V. Novikov, also attended the banquet.
    Chinese Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai and Vice Foreign Minister Wang Jiaxiang were invited to attend the banquet.
    Foreign Minister Nam Il of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea was also invited to attend the banquet.
    On the Vietnamese side, Minister of Industry and Commerce Phan Anh, Deputy Minister of Justice Tran Cong Tuong, Ambassador to the People’s Republic of China Hoang Van Hoan, and Deputy Minister of National Defense Ta Quang Buu attended as guests.
    The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 范文同代理外长设宴招待莫洛托夫外长

1954-05-26
第4版()
专栏：

    范文同代理外长设宴招待莫洛托夫外长
　　【新华社日内瓦二十四日电】出席日内瓦会议的越南民主共和国代表团首席代表、代理外交部部长范文同二十四日晚设宴招待苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫。
    苏联方面出席宴会的还有外交部副部长安·阿·葛罗米柯、瓦·库兹涅佐夫和外交部部务委员会委员基·维·诺维科夫。
    应邀出席宴会的还有中华人民共和国外交部部长周恩来、副部长王稼祥。
    应邀出席宴会的还有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务相南日。
    越南方面出席作陪的有工商部部长潘英、司法部副部长陈公祥、驻中华人民共和国大使黄文欢和国防部副部长谢光宝。
    宴会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
