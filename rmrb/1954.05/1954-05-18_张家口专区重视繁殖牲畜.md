Title: Zhangjiakou Special District Emphasizes Livestock Breeding
Author:
Date: 1954-05-18
Page: 2
The farmers of Zhangjiakou Special District, Hebei Province, are actively breeding livestock. From February to the end of April this year, the entire special district organized 613 breeding animals to travel to various places for mating. According to incomplete statistics from twelve counties, 9,520 livestock have already been mated. The state-owned central breeding station used excellent livestock breeds to mate 979 high-quality livestock in grassland areas. The entire special district can breed 10,499 livestock this year.

### 张家口专区重视繁殖牲畜

1954-05-18
第2版()
专栏：

    张家口专区重视繁殖牲畜
    河北省张家口专区农民积极繁殖牲畜，从今年二月到四月底，全专区共组织了六百一十三头种畜巡回到各地配种。据十二个县的不完全统计，已配种了九千五百二十头牲畜。国营中心配种站，以优良牲畜品种到草原地区配种了九百七十九头优良牲畜。全专区今年可繁殖一万零四百九十九头牲畜。
