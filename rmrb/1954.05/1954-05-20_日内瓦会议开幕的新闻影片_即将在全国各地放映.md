Title: News Film of the Opening of the Geneva Conference to be Screened Nationwide
Author: 
Date: 1954-05-20
Page: 3

    News Film of the Opening of the Geneva Conference
    To be Screened Nationwide
    The Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio recently completed "International News" No. 10, which mainly reports on the arrival of the Chinese and Soviet delegations to the Geneva Conference in Geneva, and the written statement made by Zhou Enlai, Chief Representative of the Chinese Delegation to the Geneva Conference and Minister of Foreign Affairs, at the airport. The film also introduces the scene on April 26, the opening day of the Geneva Conference, with Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of China, V.M. Molotov, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, and Nam Il, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, at the venue. The film will be screened nationwide soon. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 日内瓦会议开幕的新闻影片  即将在全国各地放映

1954-05-20
第3版()
专栏：

    日内瓦会议开幕的新闻影片
    即将在全国各地放映
    中央新闻纪录电影制片厂最近完成了“国际新闻”第十号，主要内容是报道我国和苏联出席日内瓦会议的代表团到达日内瓦的情况，以及我国出席日内瓦会议代表团首席代表、外交部部长周恩来在机场发表书面声明的情况。影片同时也介绍了四月二十六日日内瓦会议开幕的一天，我国外交部部长周恩来、苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务相南日在会场的情况。该片即将在全国各地放映。　（新华社）
