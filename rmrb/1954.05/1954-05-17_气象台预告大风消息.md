Title: Weather Bureau Forecasts Strong Winds
Author: 
Date: 1954-05-17
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 16th. The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a strong wind advisory at 8:00 PM on the 16th: It is expected that on the 17th, central and southern Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region will experience westerly winds of force six or seven. On the afternoon of the 17th, eastern Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, the Northeast Plain, the lower reaches of the Yellow River, and northwestern North China will experience southwesterly winds of force five or six.

### 气象台预告大风消息

1954-05-17
第1版()
专栏：

    气象台预告大风消息
    【新华社十六日讯】中央气象台十六日晚八时发布大风消息：预计十七日内蒙古自治区中部和南部将有六七级的偏西风，十七日下午内蒙古自治区东部、东北平原、黄河下流、华北的西北部将有五六级的西南风。
