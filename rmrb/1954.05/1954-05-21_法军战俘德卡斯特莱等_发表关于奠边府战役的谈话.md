Title: French POWs de Castries and Others Speak About the Battle of Dien Bien Phu
Author:
Date: 1954-05-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 19th (via Vietnam News Agency, 18th): Captured French garrison commander of Dien Bien Phu, Brigadier General de Castries, and some French officers made statements about the Battle of Dien Bien Phu.

Brigadier General de Castries, speaking about the situation in Dien Bien Phu during the final days, said: Previously, we placed our hopes on the small airstrip still controlled by our troops. However, since the Vietnamese People's Army took control of the airport, our hope turned into disappointment... The French High Command ordered us to break out and escape to Laos, abandoning the thousands of wounded soldiers in Dien Bien Phu. The Dien Bien Phu headquarters solicited opinions from the French officers of various units, but almost everyone answered: the retreat route is too long, and we are already exhausted. If we run away, there is only a dead end; staying is the best option, and we have a better chance of survival.

Captain Capéran said: Now that we have become prisoners, our only hope is for the success of the Geneva Conference so that we can go home. If it weren't for this damn airborne operation, I would have returned to France and reunited with my wife and children long ago (Captain Capéran's service period expired a month ago - Vietnam News Agency note).

Colonel Trancart also said: We firmly believe that if the French government acts in accordance with the wishes of the French people and the United States does not undermine the Geneva Conference, then the conference will be successful.

### 法军战俘德卡斯特莱等  发表关于奠边府战役的谈话

1954-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

    法军战俘德卡斯特莱等
    发表关于奠边府战役的谈话
    【新华社十九日讯】据越南通讯社十八日讯：被越南人民军俘掳的奠边府法国守军司令德卡斯特莱准将和一些法军军官发表了关于奠边府战役的谈话。
    德卡斯特莱准将谈到奠边府最后几天的情况说：以前，我们把希望寄托在仍然由我们的军队控制着的一小块飞机场上。但是，自从越南人民军控制了飞机场以后，我们的希望就变成了失望……。法军总司令部命令我们突围逃往寮国，把奠边府几千名伤兵丢下不管。奠边府司令部征求各部队的法国军官们的意见，但是差不多所有的人的答复都是：退却的道路太长，并且我们已经筋疲力尽了。假使我们逃跑的话，只有死路一条；留下来还是上策，我们生还的机会比较多。
    卡培龙上尉说：现在我们已经当了俘虏，我们唯一的希望是日内瓦会议成功，以使我们能够回家。假使没有这次该死的空降的话，我早已回到法国和我的妻子、孩子团聚了（卡培龙上尉的服役期限已于一个月以前届满——越南通讯社注）。
    特兰卡上校也说：我们深信，假使法国政府按照法国人民的愿望行事，同时美国不破坏日内瓦会议的话，这次会议是会成功的。
