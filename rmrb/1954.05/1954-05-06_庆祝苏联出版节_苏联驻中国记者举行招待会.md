Title: Celebrating Soviet Publishing Festival: Soviet Journalists in China Hold Reception
Author:
Date: 1954-05-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 5th

To celebrate the Soviet Publishing Festival, Soviet journalists from TASS, Pravda, and Literary Gazette stationed in China held a reception in Beijing on May 5th.

Among those invited to attend were: Lu Dingyi, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Zhou Yang, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government; Hu Yuzhi, Director of the Publishing Administration; Zhou Jianren and Sa Kongliao, Deputy Directors; Liao Jingtian and Li Zhanwu, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Cheng Zhiping, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of External Cultural Relations Affairs of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council; Mei Yi, Director of the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau; Hu Jiwei, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the People's Daily; Zhu Muzhi, Deputy Director of Xinhua News Agency; writers Lao She, Xiao San, Liu Baiyu, and more than one hundred representatives from the news, publishing, and literary and art circles.

Also invited were journalists from various countries stationed in Beijing.

To celebrate this festival, the People's Daily, Xinhua News Agency, and Wenyi Bao (Literary Gazette) also held banquets on the same morning to entertain the Soviet journalists stationed in China.

### 庆祝苏联出版节  苏联驻中国记者举行招待会

1954-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝苏联出版节
    苏联驻中国记者举行招待会
    【新华社五日讯】苏联塔斯社、真理报、文学报驻中国记者五月五日在北京举行招待会，庆祝苏联出版节。
    应邀出席的有：中共中央宣传部副部长陆定一，中央人民政府文化部副部长周扬，出版总署署长胡愈之、副署长周建人、萨空了，中苏友好协会总会副总干事廖经天、李沾吾，中央人民政府外交部情报司副司长程之平，政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深，广播事业局局长梅益，人民日报社副总编辑胡绩伟，新华通讯社副社长朱穆之，作家老舍、萧三、刘白羽，以及新闻、出版、文艺界代表一百多人。
    应邀出席的还有各国驻京记者。
    为了庆祝这个节日，人民日报、新华通讯社、文艺报也在同日上午举行宴会，招待苏联驻中国记者。
