Title: China Peace Committee Selects Liao Chengzhi as Vice Chairman and Secretary-General
Author: 
Date: 1954-05-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 14th:

    China People's Committee for World Peace
    Selects Liao Chengzhi as Vice Chairman and Secretary-General

    The China People's Committee for World Peace held its 18th Standing Committee meeting on the morning of the 14th. Those attending the meeting included Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China People's Committee for World Peace, Vice Chairmen Peng Zhen and Chen Shutong, Standing Committee members Situ Meitang, Li Siguang, Shen Yanbing, Shao Lizi, Ma Yinchu, Zhang Naiqi, Xu Deheng, Liang Xi, Huang Yanpei, Liao Chengzhi, Xiao San, Luo Longji, Secretary-General Liu Guanyi, and heads of various departments, a total of twenty-four people.

    The meeting discussed personnel matters, approved the request of Liu Ningyi, a Standing Committee member of the China People's Committee for World Peace, to resign from his concurrent post as Secretary-General due to his heavy workload, and unanimously approved the supplementary election of Liao Chengzhi as Vice Chairman and Secretary-General of the China People's Committee for World Peace.

    Finally, Liao Chengzhi reported at the meeting on the preparations for the Chinese delegation to attend the World Peace Council's special meeting in Berlin.

### 中国人民保卫世界和平委员会  选廖承志为副主席兼秘书长

1954-05-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会
    选廖承志为副主席兼秘书长
    【新华社十四日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会十四日上午举行第十八次常务委员会议。出席会议的，有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，副主席彭真、陈叔通，常务委员司徒美堂、李四光、沈雁冰、邵力子、马寅初、章乃器、许德珩、梁希、黄炎培、廖承志、萧三、罗隆基，秘书长刘贯一及各部门负责人共二十四人。
    会议讨论了人事问题，批准中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员刘宁一因工作繁忙辞去兼任秘书长职务的请求，并一致通过增选廖承志为中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席兼秘书长。
    最后，廖承志在会上报告了出席世界和平理事会柏林特别会议的中国代表团筹组经过。
