Title: Signing of This Year's Implementation Plan for the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement
Author: 
Date: 1954-05-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 28th: The 1954 implementation plan for the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement was signed in Beijing on the 28th after friendly negotiations between the two sides. Fan Changjiang, Deputy Secretary-General of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government of China, signed the implementation plan on behalf of the Chinese side, and Guo Zuowen, Ambassador of the Romanian People's Republic to China, signed on behalf of the Romanian side.

The Chinese side participating in the signing ceremony included: Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Wang Youping, Chinese Ambassador to Romania; Wei Que, Deputy Minister of Education; Liu Aifeng, Deputy Minister of Higher Education; and Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations Affairs of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council. The Romanian side included Meng Tiannu, First Secretary of the Embassy in China, and others.

### 中罗文化合作协定今年执行计划签字

1954-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中罗文化合作协定今年执行计划签字
　　【新华社二十八日讯】中罗文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划，经过双方友好的谈判后，已于二十八日在北京签字。在执行计划上签字的中国方面为我中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会副秘书长范长江，罗马尼亚方面为罗马尼亚人民共和国驻我国大使郭佐文。
    参加签字仪式的中国方面有：外交部副部长伍修权、我国驻罗马尼亚大使王幼平、教育部副部长韦悫、高等教育部副部长刘皑风、政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深等。罗马尼亚方面有驻华大使馆一等秘书孟天努等。
