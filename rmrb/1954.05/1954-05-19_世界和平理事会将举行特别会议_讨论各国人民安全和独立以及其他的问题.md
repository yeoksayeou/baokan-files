Title: World Peace Council to Hold Special Meeting to Discuss the Security and Independence of All Peoples and Other Issues
Author: 
Date: 1954-05-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, May 17th, TASS: The Secretariat of the World Peace Council issued the following statement:

    The World Peace Council will hold a special meeting in Berlin from May 23rd to 28th.

    The issues to be discussed by the World Peace Council include the security and independence of all peoples, as well as the necessity of eliminating the threat of the use of atomic and hydrogen weapons.

    The World Peace Council will express the will of international public opinion and strive to help resolve major international issues in the spirit of easing tensions and in the spirit of peace.

### 世界和平理事会将举行特别会议  讨论各国人民安全和独立以及其他的问题

1954-05-19
第4版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会将举行特别会议
    讨论各国人民安全和独立以及其他的问题
    【新华社十八日讯】塔斯社维也纳十七日讯：世界和平理事会书记处发表声明如下：
    世界和平理事会将于五月二十三日到二十八日在柏林举行特别会议。
    世界和平理事会所将要讨论的问题包括各国人民的安全和独立的问题，以及有必要消除使用原子武器和氢武器的威胁的问题。
    世界和平理事会将表达国际舆论的意志，竭力本着缓和紧张局势的精神与和平的精神，帮助解决重大的国际问题。
