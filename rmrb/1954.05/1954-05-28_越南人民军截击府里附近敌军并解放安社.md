Title: Vietnamese People's Army Intercepts Enemy Troops Near Phu Ly and Liberates An Xa
Author:
Date: 1954-05-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 27th: According to the Vietnam News Agency on the 26th, the Vietnamese People's Army intercepted enemy troops near Phu Ly in North Vietnam, captured the enemy's stronghold of An Xa, and destroyed two enemy trains carrying American weapons.

The Vietnamese People's Army intercepted an enemy battalion thirteen kilometers south of Phu Ly, the capital of Ha Nam province; according to preliminary reports, more than 300 enemy troops were annihilated, including one major, three captains, and two lieutenants.

On May 21st, the People's Army captured An Xa, a major enemy stronghold located twenty kilometers northwest of Thai Binh, the capital of Thai Binh province, killing and capturing ninety enemy troops and seizing 107 enemy weapons. On the 20th, the People's Army annihilated an enemy company in an ambush in Vinh Bao district, Kien An province.

In addition, on May 14th and 16th, people's armed forces destroyed two trains carrying weapons supplied by the American imperialists to the French colonialists on the railway connecting Hanoi and Haiphong.

### 越南人民军截击府里附近敌军并解放安社

1954-05-28
第1版()
专栏：

    越南人民军截击府里附近敌军并解放安社
    【新华社二十七日讯】据越南通讯社二十六日讯：越南人民军在北越截击府里附近敌军，拔除敌军据点安社，并炸毁敌军装运美国武器的火车两列。
    越南人民军在河南省省会府里以南十三公里的地方截击敌军一个营；据初步消息，歼灭敌军三百多名，其中有少校一名、上尉三名和中尉两名。
    五月二十一日，人民军拔除位于太平省省会西北二十公里的敌军较大据点安社，毙、俘敌军九十名，缴获敌军武器一百零七件。二十日，人民军在建安省永保县进行的一次伏击战中，歼灭敌军一个连。
    此外，人民武装五月十四和十六两日在连接河内和海防的铁路上，炸毁载着美帝国主义者供给法国殖民者的武器的火车两列。
