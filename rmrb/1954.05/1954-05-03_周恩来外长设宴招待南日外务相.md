Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Hosts Banquet for Foreign Minister Nam Il
Author:
Date: 1954-05-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 1st: Zhou Enlai, Chief Representative of the Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Geneva Conference and Minister of Foreign Affairs, hosted a banquet tonight for Nam Il, Chief Representative of the Delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and Minister of Foreign Affairs.

    Also attending the banquet from the Korean side were: Delegation representatives, Minister of Education Baek Nam-woon and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ki Seok-bok, as well as Jang Chun-san, Tian Dong-he, Kim Taek-yong, and Kim Myong-gui.

    Attending the banquet from the Chinese side were: Zhang Wentian, Wang Jiaxiang, Li Kenong, Wang Bingnan, Qiao Guanhua, Chen Jiakang, Huang Hua, and Wang Zhuoru.

    The banquet proceeded in a friendly and warm atmosphere.

### 周恩来外长设宴招待南日外务相

1954-05-03
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来外长设宴招待南日外务相
   【新华社日内瓦一日电】出席日内瓦会议的中华人民共和国代表团首席代表、外交部部长周恩来，于今晚设宴招待朝鲜民主主义人民共和国代表团首席代表、外务相南日。
    朝鲜方面出席宴会的还有：代表团代表、教育相白南云和外务省副相奇石福，以及张春山、田东赫、金泽泳、金明龟等。
    中国方面出席宴会的有：张闻天、王稼祥、李克农、王炳南、乔冠华、陈家康、黄华、王倬如。
    宴会在友好、热烈的空气中进行。
