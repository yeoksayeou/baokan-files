Title: Opening of the 16th Congress of the Communist Party of Austria; Chairman Koplenig Gives Report on the Political Situation
Author:
Date: 1954-05-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, May 13th: The 16th Congress of the Communist Party of Austria opened on May 13th in the Vienna Music Hall, according to TASS. Participating in the congress were representatives from party organizations across Austria, numerous guests, and representatives of workers, farmers, and intellectuals.

Franz Honner, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Austria, delivered the opening address. Honner suggested that the congress observe a moment of silence in memory of J.V. Stalin and Klement Gottwald. After electing the presidium and various committees and determining the working procedures of the congress, the congress adopted the following agenda:

	1. Central Committee report on the political situation – the road to achieving and guaranteeing Austria's independence;
	2. Central Committee financial summary report;
	3. Central Auditing Commission summary report;
	4. Election of the Central Committee and the Central Auditing Commission.

The chairman of the congress announced that Johann Koplenig, chairman of the Communist Party of Austria, would deliver a report on the first item on the agenda. In his report, Koplenig elaborated in detail on the domestic and international situation of Austria, summarized the work of the party between the 15th and 16th Congresses, and put forward the task of striving for the independence and peace of the Austrian people to Austrian communists and all progressive forces in Austria.

Next, Fredl Fühnber, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Austria, read out the congratulatory telegram from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union to the congress. The telegram wished the Communist Party of Austria success in the struggle for the country's independent and democratic development, for uniting all democratic and patriotic forces against militarism and the threat of a new Austro-German merger, for safeguarding the vital interests of the working people, for consolidating its own ranks, and for peace and friendship among all peoples.

The congratulatory telegram from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union was met with stormy and sustained applause from the delegates and guests. Everyone stood and sang "The Internationale."

Subsequently, the congress heard the Central Committee's financial summary report and the Central Auditing Commission's summary report and approved both reports.

Riccardo Lavanian, representing the Italian Communist Party, and Károly Kiss, representing the Hungarian Working People's Party, extended congratulations to the 16th Congress of the Communist Party of Austria. The delegates and guests listened with great attention to the congratulatory telegram from the Workers' Party of Korea.

The congress is continuing.

### 奥地利共产党第十六次代表大会开幕  奥共主席柯普勒尼希作关于政治局势的报告

1954-05-16
第4版()
专栏：

    奥地利共产党第十六次代表大会开幕
    奥共主席柯普勒尼希作关于政治局势的报告
      【新华社十四日讯】据塔斯社维也纳十三日讯：奥地利共产党第十六次代表大会于五月十三日在维也纳音乐大厅开幕。参加大会的有奥地利各地党组织的代表和许多来宾以及工人、农民和知识分子的代表。
    奥地利共产党中央委员会政治局委员弗朗茨·杭纳尔致开幕词。杭纳尔建议大会为纪念约·维·斯大林和克利门特·哥特瓦尔德肃立静默。大会在选出了主席团和各委员会并确定了代表大会工作程序之后，通过了下列议程：
    一、中央委员会关于政治局势的报告——取得和保证奥地利独立的道路；
    二、中央委员会财政总结报告；
    三、中央检查委员会总结报告；
    四、选举中央委员会和中央检查委员会。
    大会主席宣布由奥地利共产党主席约翰·柯普勒尼希就第一项议程做报告。柯普勒尼希在报告中详细阐述了奥地利的国内与国外的局势，总结了党在第十五次代表大会与第十六次代表大会期间的工作，向奥地利共产党员和奥地利的全体进步力量提出了为保障奥地利人民的独立与和平而奋斗的任务。
    接着，奥地利共产党中央委员会总书记弗雷德尔·费恩柏宣读苏联共产党中央委员会致大会的贺电。贺电祝奥地利共产党在为国家的独立和民主发展、为联合所有反对军国主义和新的德奥合并的威胁的民主和爱国力量、为维护劳动人民的切身利益、为巩固自己的队伍、为和平与各国人民的友谊的斗争中获得成就。
    代表和来宾对苏联共产党中央委员会的贺电报以暴风雨般的、经久不息的掌声。全体起立唱“国际歌”。
    随后，代表大会听取了中央委员会财政总结报告和中央检查委员会总结报告，并批准了这两个报告。
    里卡尔多·拉万扬代表意大利共产党、卡罗伊·基什代表匈牙利劳动人民党向奥地利共产党第十六次代表大会致贺。代表和来宾极其注意地听取了朝鲜劳动党的贺电。
    代表大会继续进行中。
