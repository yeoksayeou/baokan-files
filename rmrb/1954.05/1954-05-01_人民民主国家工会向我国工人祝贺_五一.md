Title: People's Democratic Countries' Trade Unions Congratulate Chinese Workers on "May Day"
Author:
Date: 1954-05-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: On the eve of the "May Day" International Labor Day, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions received telegrams from the Korean Federation of Trade Unions, the Central Council of Polish Trade Unions, the Central Council of Hungarian Trade Unions, the Central Council of Czechoslovak Trade Unions, the Central Council of the General Confederation of Labor of Romania, and the Central Council of the General Confederation of Labor of Bulgaria, congratulating on "May Day" International Labor Day. The congratulatory telegrams warmly congratulated the Chinese working class on the great victories achieved on the economic construction front and wished the fraternal friendship between the Chinese and working classes of all countries would be strengthened and consolidated day by day.

### 人民民主国家工会向我国工人祝贺“五一”

1954-05-01
第4版()
专栏：

    人民民主国家工会向我国工人祝贺“五一”
    据新华社讯：中华全国总工会于“五一”国际劳动节前夕，收到朝鲜职业总同盟、波兰工会中央理事会、匈牙利工会中央理事会、捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会、罗马尼亚总工会中央理事会、保加利亚总工会中央理事会的来电，祝贺“五一”国际劳动节。贺电中热烈地祝贺中国工人阶级在经济建设战线上取得的伟大的胜利，并祝中国与各国工人阶级的兄弟般的友谊日益加强与巩固。
