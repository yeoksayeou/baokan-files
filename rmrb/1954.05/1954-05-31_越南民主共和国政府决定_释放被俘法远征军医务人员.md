Title: The Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Decides to Release Captured French Expeditionary Force Medical Personnel
Author:
Date: 1954-05-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 30th: Vietnam News Agency reported on the 30th that on the 29th, Cao Van Khanh, Deputy Division Commander and representative of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army, formally announced to the twenty-seven medical personnel of the French Expeditionary Force captured by the Vietnam People's Army at Dien Bien Phu that the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam had recently decided to release them after they made a request. The twenty-seven French medical personnel included Major Doctor Growin, Captain Doctor Radamani, Lieutenant Pons, thirteen sergeants, eight corporals, and three privates.

The General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army has decided to allow French helicopters to come to Dien Bien Phu on May 30th and 31st to evacuate the released French medical personnel to Hanoi.

At the same time, it is worth pointing out that on May 23rd, French aircraft bombed for the second time the French Expeditionary Force prisoners of war moving near Son La on Highway 41, killing and wounding dozens of prisoners of war.

### 越南民主共和国政府决定  释放被俘法远征军医务人员

1954-05-31
第4版()
专栏：

    越南民主共和国政府决定
    释放被俘法远征军医务人员
   【新华社三十日讯】据越南通讯社三十日讯：二十九日，越南人民军总司令部代表高文庆副师长向在奠边府被越南人民军俘掳的法国远征军的二十七个医务工作人员正式宣布：越南民主共和国政府在他们提出要求后，最近已经决定释放他们。这二十七个法军医务工作人员包括少校军医格罗温、上尉军医拉达马尼、中尉彭斯、十三个中士、八个下士和三个兵士。
    越南人民军总司令部已经决定让法军直升飞机在五月三十日和三十一日到奠边府来撤运被释放的法军医务工作人员到河内去。
    同时，值得指出的是，法军飞机在五月二十三日第二次轰炸了在第四十一号公路上山萝附近移动的法国远征军战俘，毙、伤战俘数十名。
