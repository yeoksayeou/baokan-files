Title: Guangdong Province Selects Excellent Cadres to Support National Industrial Construction
Author:
Date: 1954-05-30
Page: 1

    Guangdong Province Selects Excellent Cadres
    to Support National Industrial Construction
     [This Newspaper News] In order to support national industrial construction, Guangdong Province will select more than 900 excellent cadres this year to work in industrial construction positions. At present, the first batch of about 300 people has set off one after another, including two provincial-level cadres, 26 cadres above the prefectural committee level, and 86 cadres above the county level. They will be assigned to the Central China Iron and Steel Company and factories in Central and Southern China under the Central First Ministry of Machine Building. They all feel extremely honored and excited to be able to directly participate in the country's industrial construction and dedicate their efforts to realizing the country's industrialization.

### 广东省抽调优秀干部  支援国家工业建设

1954-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

    广东省抽调优秀干部
    支援国家工业建设
     【本报讯】为了支援国家工业建设，广东省今年将抽调九百多名优秀干部到工业建设岗位。目前第一批三百人左右已陆续启程，其中有省级干部二人，地委以上的干部二十六人，县级以上干部八十六人。他们将被分配到华中钢铁公司，和中央第一机械工业部所属的在中南的工厂。他们都以能够直接参加祖国的工业建设，为实现国家的工业化而献出自己的一份力量，感到极大的光荣和兴奋。
