Title: Molotov Hosts Zhou Enlai and Eden
Author:
Date: 1954-05-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 30th — Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov hosted a banquet on the 30th in honor of Zhou Enlai, Foreign Minister of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, and British Foreign Secretary Eden.

### 莫洛托夫招待周恩来和艾登

1954-05-03
第1版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫招待周恩来和艾登
   【新华社日内瓦三十日电】苏联外交部部长莫洛托夫在三十日设宴招待中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来和英国外交大臣艾登。
