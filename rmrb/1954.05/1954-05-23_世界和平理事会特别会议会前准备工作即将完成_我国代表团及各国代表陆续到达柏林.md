Title: Preparations Nearing Completion for the World Peace Council Special Session; Chinese Delegation and Delegates from Various Countries Arrive in Berlin One After Another
Author:
Date: 1954-05-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, May 21st: Preparations for the upcoming special session of the World Peace Council in Berlin are nearing completion. Delegates from various countries have begun arriving in Berlin one after another. The head of the Chinese delegation, Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council, Guo Moruo, and council members Mao Dun, Ma Yinchu, Zhang Bojun, Cai Tingkai, Liao Chengzhi, Xiao San, Liu Guanyi, and others arrived in Berlin on the afternoon of the 21st. Representatives of the German Peace Committee, as well as Li Yimang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the World Peace Council, who had arrived in Berlin earlier, and Ji Pengfei, the Chinese Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, went to welcome them.

On the 20th, representatives of the German Peace Committee welcomed Heino Yoshitaro, Secretary-General of the Japanese Committee for the Defense of Peace, and members of the Indian delegation who came to attend the meeting. Le Dinh Tham, Chairman of the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace, Saxena, a member of the All-India Peace Council, Tekin Lun, Vice-Chairman of the Burma Committee for the Defense of World Peace, and Silva, Vice-Chairman of the Ceylon Coconut Wine Workers' Union, also arrived on the 21st. Newspaper reports: Delegations of peace fighters from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Burma, Australia, Ceylon, and other countries are on their way to Berlin.

However, it is reported that many delegates and guests will not be able to arrive before the originally scheduled opening date for various reasons. Therefore, Heinz Willmann, Secretary-General of the German Peace Committee, announced that the special session of the World Peace Council has been rescheduled to open in Berlin on May 24th.

### 世界和平理事会特别会议会前准备工作即将完成  我国代表团及各国代表陆续到达柏林

1954-05-23
第4版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会特别会议会前准备工作即将完成
    我国代表团及各国代表陆续到达柏林
    据新华社讯：塔斯社柏林二十一日讯：即将举行的世界和平理事会特别会议的会前准备工作即将在柏林完成。各国代表已开始陆续前来柏林。我国代表团团长、世界和平理事会副主席郭沫若和理事茅盾、马寅初、章伯钧、蔡廷锴、廖承志、萧三、刘贯一等人，在二十一日下午到达柏林。德国和平委员会代表和已先期到达柏林的世界和平理事会书记处书记李一氓和我国驻德意志民主共和国大使姬鹏飞等人都前往欢迎。
    二十日德国和平委员会的代表迎接了前来参加会议的日本拥护和平委员会总书记平野义太郎和印度代表团的人员。越南保卫世界和平委员会主席黎廷探、全印和平理事会理事萨克森纳、缅甸保卫世界和平委员会副主席德钦伦、锡兰椰酒工人工会副主席席尔瓦也在二十一日到达柏林。报刊消息：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、缅甸、澳大利亚、锡兰及其他一些国家的和平战士代表团正在来柏林的途中。
    然而，据悉还有许多代表与客人由于各种原因，不能在前预定的开会期限以前到达，因此德国和平委员会总书记海恩茨·卫尔曼通知，世界和平理事会特别会议改于五月二十四日在柏林开幕。
