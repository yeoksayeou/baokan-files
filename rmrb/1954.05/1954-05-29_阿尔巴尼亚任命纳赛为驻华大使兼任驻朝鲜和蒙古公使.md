Title: Albania Appoints Nase as Ambassador to China Concurrently Serving as Minister to North Korea and Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1954-05-29
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 26th: According to the Albanian Telegraph Agency, Tirana: The Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania has appointed Nesti Nase, the Albanian Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the People's Republic of China, to concurrently serve as the Albanian Minister Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Mongolian People's Republic.

### 阿尔巴尼亚任命纳赛为驻华大使兼任驻朝鲜和蒙古公使

1954-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚任命纳赛为驻华大使兼任驻朝鲜和蒙古公使
   【新华社布拉格讯】据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社地拉那二十六日讯：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团任命阿尔巴尼亚驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使奈斯蒂·纳赛，兼任阿尔巴尼亚驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和蒙古人民共和国特命全权公使。
