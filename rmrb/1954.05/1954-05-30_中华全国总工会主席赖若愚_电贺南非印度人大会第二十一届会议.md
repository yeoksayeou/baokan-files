Title: Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Sends a Congratulatory Message to the 21st Session of the South African Indian Congress
Author:
Date: 1954-05-30
Page: 1

Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions,
Sends a Congratulatory Message to the 21st Session of the South African Indian Congress

To Mr. Katchalia Mistry, Joint Honorary Secretary of the South African Indian Congress:

    I am delighted to learn of the convening of the 21st session of the South African Indian Congress. On behalf of the entire Chinese working people, I extend warm congratulations.

    I wish the conference success, and I wish you to closely unite with all the people of South Africa, regardless of race or color, and achieve greater success in the struggle against national discrimination and oppression, for democratic freedom, and in defense of world peace.

       Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, May 29th

### 中华全国总工会主席赖若愚  电贺南非印度人大会第二十一届会议

1954-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

    中华全国总工会主席赖若愚
    电贺南非印度人大会第二十一届会议南非印度人大会联合名誉书记卡查利亚米斯特里先生：
    欣悉南非印度人大会举行第二十一届会议，谨代表中国全体劳动人民致以热烈的祝贺。
    祝大会成功，并祝你们不分种族肤色地与南非全体人民紧密团结，在为反抗民族歧视与压迫、为争取民主自由和保卫世界和平的斗争中获得更多的成就。
       中华全国总工会主席  赖若愚  五月二十九日
