Title: Egyptian Ambassador to Pakistan Answers Questions: Egypt Is Unwilling to Participate in Any Aggression Plan
Author:
Date: 1954-05-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 12th TASS: Egyptian Ambassador to Pakistan, Abdul Wahab Assam, told the "Pakistan Times" correspondent in Lahore that Egypt and other Arab countries are unwilling to participate in any country's aggression plans. He said, "We are willing to coexist peacefully with all those who respect our sovereignty and national aspirations."

In answering reporters' questions, Assam emphasized that Arab countries "do not believe that communist countries will launch any aggression or infringe upon their sovereignty."

### 埃及驻巴基斯坦大使答记者问  埃及不愿参加任何侵略计划

1954-05-18
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及驻巴基斯坦大使答记者问
    埃及不愿参加任何侵略计划
    【新华社讯】塔斯社喀喇蚩十二日讯：埃及驻巴基斯坦大使阿卜杜勒·瓦哈布·阿萨姆对“巴基斯坦时报”驻拉合尔记者说，埃及和其他阿拉伯国家不愿参加任何国家的侵略计划。他说：“我们愿意和所有尊重我们的主权和民族愿望的人友好共处。”
  阿萨姆在答记者问时着重指出，阿拉伯国家“并不认为共产党国家会发动任何侵略或侵犯它们的主权”。
