Title: Government Administration Council Holds 215th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-05-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 11th

    The 215th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 6th. At the meeting, Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, on behalf of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, submitted two suggestions to the Central People's Government Administration Council regarding the promulgation of the "Draft of Internal Labor Regulations for State-Owned Enterprises" and the "Provisional Regulations on Rewards for Inventions, Technical Improvements, and Rationalization Proposals Related to Production". After discussion, the meeting approved the "Draft of Internal Labor Regulations for State-Owned Enterprises" and the "Provisional Regulations on Rewards for Inventions, Technical Improvements, and Rationalization Proposals Related to Production," and also approved the decision on the promulgation of the "Draft of Internal Labor Regulations for State-Owned Enterprises." Following a statement by Li Zheren, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government, the meeting approved the decision to establish a Foreign Trade Arbitration Commission within the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade. In addition, the meeting approved various appointment and removal proposals and proposals to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval of appointments and removals.

### 政务院举行第二一五次会议

1954-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第二一五次会议
    【新华社十一日讯】中央人民政府政务院第二百一十五次政务会议在六日下午举行。会上，中华全国总工会主席赖若愚代表中华全国总工会提出了关于颁布“国营企业内部劳动规则纲要”和“有关生产的发明、技术改进及合理化建议的奖励暂行条例”向中央人民政府政务院的两个建议。会议经过讨论后，通过了“国营企业内部劳动规则纲要”和“有关生产的发明、技术改进及合理化建议的奖励暂行条例”，并通过了关于颁布“国营企业内部劳动规则纲要”的决定。会议继在听取中央人民政府对外贸易部副部长李哲人的说明后，通过了关于在中国国际贸易促进委员会内设立对外贸易仲裁委员会的决定。此外，会议并通过了各项任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案。
