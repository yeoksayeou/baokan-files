Title: Weather Forecast Announces Gale Warning
Author:
Date: 1954-05-04
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, May 3rd: The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a gale warning at 8:00 PM on May 3rd: It is expected that from noon on the 4th, the central and southern parts of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, the northwestern part of North China, and the lower reaches of the Yellow River will experience southwesterly winds of around force six.

### 气象台预告大风消息

1954-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

    气象台预告大风消息
    【新华社三日讯】中央气象台五月三日下午八时发布大风消息：预计四日中午起，内蒙古自治区的中部和南部、华北的西北部、黄河下游将有六级左右的西南风。
