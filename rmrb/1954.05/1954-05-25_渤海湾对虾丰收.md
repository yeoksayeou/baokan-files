Bumper Harvest of Prawns in Bohai Bay

The Bohai Bay has had a bumper harvest of prawns. In the first half of last year, the six areas of Qinhuangdao, Linyu, Funing, Changli, Leting, and Fengnan produced a total of approximately 500,000 jin of prawns. Currently, in only some coastal areas of Qinhuangdao, Changli, and Luannan, the daily output of prawns is between 350,000 jin and 550,000 jin. Qinhuangdao alone has caught more than 2.4 million jin of prawns since April 26th. On April 25th, fishermen in Nanbu Township, Luannan County, caught 200,000 jin of prawns in a single day. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 渤海湾对虾丰收

1954-05-25
第2版()
专栏：

    渤海湾对虾丰收
    渤海湾对虾丰收。去年上半年，秦皇岛、临榆、抚宁、昌黎、乐亭、丰南六地共约产对虾五十万斤，目前仅秦皇岛、昌黎、滦南三地的部分沿海区，每天就产对虾三十五万斤到五十五万斤。秦皇岛一地从四月二十六日以来已捕对虾二百四十多万斤。滦南县南堡乡渔民在四月二十五日一天即捕对虾二十万斤。　（据新华社讯）
