Title: Moscow Newspapers Publish Pham Van Dong's Statement
Author:
Date: 1954-05-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 11th: Moscow's major newspapers on the 11th all published the full text of the statement made by Pham Van Dong, Chief Delegate of the Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Geneva Conference, on the 10th at the Geneva Conference. The newspapers also published news of the banquet hosted by Zhou Enlai, Chief Delegate of our country's delegation, on the 8th in honor of Pham Van Dong.

### 莫斯科各报刊载范文同声明

1954-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科各报刊载范文同声明
    【新华社莫斯科十一日电】莫斯科各主要报纸十一日都刊载了越南民主共和国出席日内瓦会议代表团首席代表范文同十日在日内瓦会议上发表的声明全文。各报并刊登了我国代表团首席代表周恩来在八日设宴招待范文同的消息。
