Title: Employees of Shenyang Pneumatic Tool Factory Successfully Trial-Produce Soviet-Style Pneumatic Drill
Author: Liu Jianbang
Date: 1954-05-30
Page: 2

    Employees of the Shenyang Pneumatic Tool Factory, under the direct guidance of Soviet experts, successfully trial-produced China's first Soviet-style pneumatic drill on May 17th. The quality and specifications fully meet Soviet standards. This product is a tool used for coal mining and mining, and each processed part is very precise. Workers overcame many difficulties in the trial production process. The new pneumatic drill will be produced in large quantities in the third quarter to meet the needs of various mining areas and basic construction units across the country.

### 沈阳风动工具厂职工  试制苏式风镐成功
刘建邦
1954-05-30
第2版()
专栏：

    沈阳风动工具厂职工  试制苏式风镐成功
    沈阳风动工具厂职工在苏联专家直接指导下，于五月十七日试制我国第一把苏式风镐成功，质量、规格完全达到苏联标准。这种产品是采煤、采矿用的一种工具，每个加工部分都很精密，工人们在试制过程中克服了许多困难。新风镐将在第三季度大量生产，供应全国各矿区和基本建设单位的需要。　　　　（刘建邦）
