Title: Chinese Delegation Holds Press Conference
Author: 
Date: 1954-05-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 11th: Huang Hua, spokesperson for the People's Republic of China delegation attending the Geneva Conference, held a press conference after the meeting on the 11th to brief Chinese and foreign journalists on the situation of the 11th meeting. Huang Hua also answered questions raised by the journalists.

### 我国代表团举行记者招待会

1954-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我国代表团举行记者招待会
   【新华社日内瓦十一日电】出席日内瓦会议的中华人民共和国代表团发言人黄华在十一日会后举行记者招待会，向中国和外国的记者们介绍了十一日会议的情况。黄华并回答了记者们提出的问题。
