Title: Groundbreaking Ceremony Held Yesterday for Beijing Department Store Construction Project
Author:
Date: 1954-05-29
Page: 2

Beijing Department Store Construction Project Breaks Ground Yesterday

The construction project for the Beijing Department Store officially commenced yesterday (May 28th) at 4:00 PM. Attending the groundbreaking ceremony were Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, Peng Cheng, Director of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Commercial Bureau, representatives from the Ministry of Commerce of the Central People's Government, all construction workers involved in the building's construction, and over 700 employees from the Beijing branch of the China National Department Store Corporation. In his address, Deputy Mayor Wu Han pointed out that the construction of the Beijing Department Store is a major event in the lives of the capital's people. He called on all employees to learn from advanced Soviet experiences, strengthen planned management, establish a sound responsibility system, launch labor competitions, and improve engineering quality, so that the project achieves "good, fast, economical, and safe" results. Deputy Mayor Wu Han broke ground at the conclusion of the ceremony.

The total area of the department store was originally planned to be more than 18,400 square meters, but it has now been increased to more than 19,100 square meters.

### 北京市百货大楼建筑工程昨日破土

1954-05-29
第2版()
专栏：

    北京市百货大楼建筑工程昨日破土
    北京市百货大楼的建筑工程，已在昨日（二十八日）下午四时动工。参加开工典礼的，有北京市人民政府副市长吴晗、北京市人民政府商业局局长彭城、中央人民政府商业部代表、参加大楼建筑的全体建筑工人和中国百货公司北京市公司职工等七百多人。吴晗副市长在致词中指出：北京市百货大楼的兴建，是首都人民生活中的一件大事。他号召全体职工学习苏联先进经验，加强计划管理，建立健全的责任制，开展劳动竞赛，提高工程质量，使工程做到“好、快、省、安全”。典礼结束时由吴晗副市长破土。
    按百货大楼的总面积原计划为一万八千四百多平方公尺，现增加为一万九千一百多平方公尺。
