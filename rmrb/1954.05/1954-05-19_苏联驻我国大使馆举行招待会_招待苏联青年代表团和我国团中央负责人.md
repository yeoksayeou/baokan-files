Title: The Soviet Embassy in China Holds Reception for Soviet Youth Delegation and Responsible Persons from the Chinese Communist Youth League Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1954-05-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 18th: The Soviet Embassy in China held a reception on the evening of the 18th to entertain all members of the Soviet Youth Delegation and responsible persons from the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League.

    All members of the Soviet Youth Delegation, headed by delegation leader Shelepin, attended the reception.

    Those invited to attend included: Hu Yaobang, Liu Daosheng, Luo Yi, Qu Tangliang, and Hu Keshi, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League; Wu Han, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth; Tian Demin, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, and Guan Ruoluan, Vice Chairman; responsible persons from various departments of the Central Committee of the Youth League, the North China Working Committee, and the Beijing Municipal Committee; as well as people from educational, cultural, and artistic circles in the capital.

    Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, was also invited to attend the reception.

    At the reception, Shelepin and Hu Yaobang both spoke.

    Members of the Soviet Youth Delegation and young artists from the capital performed wonderful programs at the meeting.

    Finally, a Soviet film was shown.

### 苏联驻我国大使馆举行招待会  招待苏联青年代表团和我国团中央负责人

1954-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联驻我国大使馆举行招待会
    招待苏联青年代表团和我国团中央负责人
    【新华社十八日讯】苏联驻我国大使馆在十八日晚举行招待会，招待苏联青年代表团全体人员和中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会负责人。
    以谢列平团长为首的苏联青年代表团全体团员都出席了招待会。
    应邀出席的还有：中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡耀邦、刘导生、罗毅、区棠亮、胡克实，中华全国民主青年联合会副主席吴晗，中华全国学生联合会主席田德民、副主席关若鸾，青年团中央委员会所属各部门、华北工作委员会和北京市委员会的负责人以及首都教育、文化、艺术界人士等。
    我中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权也应邀出席了招待会。
    在招待会上，谢列平和胡耀邦都讲了话。
    苏联青年代表团团员和首都青年艺术工作者都在会上表演了精采节目。
    最后，并放映了苏联影片。
