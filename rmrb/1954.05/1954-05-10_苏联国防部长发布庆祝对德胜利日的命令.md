Title: Soviet Minister of Defense Issues Order Celebrating Victory Over Germany
Author:
Date: 1954-05-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 9th. TASS: Soviet Minister of Defense, Marshal of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, issued the following order on May 9th, commemorating the ninth anniversary of the victory over Fascist Germany:

    Comrade soldiers and sailors of the Army and Navy! Comrade non-commissioned officers and junior officers!
    Comrade field officers! Comrade generals!
    Today marks the ninth anniversary of the victorious end of the Soviet Union's Great Patriotic War against Hitlerite Germany.
    Nine years ago, the Soviet Armed Forces, together with the armed forces of our allies, achieved a victory of world-historic significance in the struggle against Hitlerite Germany.
    During the Great Patriotic War, the personnel of the Soviet Army and Navy displayed unparalleled heroism and outstanding combat qualities, demonstrating faithfulness to their military duty and boundless loyalty to their motherland.
    On this Victory Day, I extend my congratulations to the personnel of the Soviet Armed Forces.
    To commemorate Victory Day, I order: today, May 9th, a thirty-gun salute shall be fired in our motherland's capital, Moscow, in the capitals of each of the Union Republics, and in the hero cities of Leningrad, Stalingrad, Sevastopol, and Odessa, in celebration.
    Long live our socialist motherland!
    Long live the powerful Soviet people and their heroic armed forces!
    Long live the Soviet government!
    Long Live the Communist party of Soviet Union, the inspirer and organizer of all our victories!
    Eternal glory to the heroes who sacrificed themselves in the struggle to defend the freedom and independence of our motherland!

### 苏联国防部长发布庆祝对德胜利日的命令

1954-05-10
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联国防部长发布庆祝对德胜利日的命令
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联国防部部长、苏联元帅布尔加宁于五月九日发布庆祝战胜法西斯德国九周年纪念日的命令如下：
    陆海军战士同志们！士官和尉官同志们！
    校官同志们！将军同志们！
  今天是苏联抵抗希特勒德国的伟大卫国战争胜利结束九周年纪念日。
    九年以前，苏联武装部队和我们的盟国的武装部队一道，在抵抗希特勒德国的斗争中取得了具有世界历史意义的胜利。
    在伟大的卫国战争中，苏联的陆军和海军人员表现了无比的英雄主义和卓越的战斗素质，表现了对于他们的军事职责的忠实以及对于他们的祖国的无限忠诚。
    值此胜利日，我谨向苏联武装部队人员祝贺。
    为了纪念胜利日，我命令：今天，五月九日，在我们祖国的首都莫斯科，在各加盟共和国的首都，在列宁格勒、斯大林格勒、塞瓦斯托波尔和敖德萨等英雄城市，一律鸣放礼炮三十响，以示庆祝。
    我们的社会主义祖国万岁！
    强大的苏联人民和他们英勇的武装部队万岁！
    苏维埃政府万岁！
    我们一切胜利的鼓舞者和组织者苏联共产党万岁！
    在保卫我们祖国的自由和独立的战斗中牺牲的英雄们永垂不朽！
