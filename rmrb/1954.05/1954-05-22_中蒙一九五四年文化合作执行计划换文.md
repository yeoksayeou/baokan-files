Title: Exchange of Notes on the 1954 Sino-Mongolian Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan
Author:
Date: 1954-05-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 21st. The 1954 Sino-Mongolian Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan, formulated according to the Sino-Mongolian Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement, was exchanged on the 19th by Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China, and Ochirbat, Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, representing the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Mongolian People's Republic respectively, after consultation and agreement between the two parties.

### 中蒙一九五四年文化合作执行计划换文

1954-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

    中蒙一九五四年文化合作执行计划换文
    【新华社二十一日讯】根据中蒙经济及文化合作协定制订的中蒙一九五四年文化合作执行计划，经双方商谈协议后，已在十九日由我中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫和蒙古人民共和国驻我国大使奥其尔巴特，分别代表中华人民共和国中央人民政府和蒙古人民共和国换文。
