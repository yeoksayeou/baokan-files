Title: Zhou Enlai Meets with Menon
Author:
Date: 1954-05-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 27th: Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, met with Indian Representative to the United Nations, Menon, at 9:00 PM on the 27th, for an hour-long meeting.

### 周恩来外长接见梅农

1954-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

    周恩来外长接见梅农
    【新华社日内瓦二十七日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来二十七日下午九时接见印度驻联合国代表梅农，会谈一小时。
