Title: China and Hungary Sign 1954 Goods Exchange and Payment Agreement
Author: 
Date: 1954-05-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 2nd: After friendly and amicable negotiations, the government delegations of the People's Republic of China and the Hungarian People's Republic signed the China-Hungary 1954 Goods Exchange and Payment Agreement in Budapest on April 30th. Huang Zhen, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Hungarian People's Republic, signed the agreement on behalf of China, and József Bognár, Minister of Domestic and Foreign Trade of the Hungarian People's Republic, signed on behalf of Hungary.

According to the agreement, the range of goods exchanged between the two countries in 1954 will be wider than last year. China will export various minerals, non-ferrous metals, hides, wool, feathers, casings, textile raw materials, flue-cured tobacco, and other local products and industrial raw materials to Hungary. Hungary will export trucks, buses, refrigerators, power station equipment, instruments, medicines, medical equipment, and other industrial products to China.

After the signing ceremony, József Bognár, Minister of Domestic and Foreign Trade of Hungary, held a banquet to celebrate the signing of the agreement.

### 中匈签订一九五四年交换货物及付款协定

1954-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

    中匈签订一九五四年交换货物及付款协定
    【新华社二日讯】中华人民共和国和匈牙利人民共和国的政府代表团经过友好融洽的谈判后，已于四月三十日在布达佩斯签订了中匈一九五四年交换货物及付款协定。在协定上签字的，中国方面是中华人民共和国驻匈牙利人民共和国特命全权大使黄镇，匈牙利方面是匈牙利人民共和国国内和对外贸易部部长约·波格纳尔。
    根据协定规定，一九五四年两国所交换的商品的范围将比去年更为广泛。中国将向匈牙利输出各种矿产、有色金属、皮张、羊毛、羽毛、肠衣、纺织原料、烤烟及其他土产和工业原料。匈牙利将向中国输出载重汽车、公共汽车、冷冻机、发电站设备、仪器、药品、医疗设备及其他工业品。
    签字仪式举行后，匈牙利国内和对外贸易部部长约·波格纳尔举行宴会，庆祝协定的签订。
