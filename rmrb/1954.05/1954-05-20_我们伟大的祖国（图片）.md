Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-05-20
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The Harbin Electrical Machinery Manufacturing Plant is one of the key projects aided by the Soviet Union in our country's construction. It is expected to begin production in the third quarter of this year. This is a scene of workers installing a thirty-ton crane to be used for installing various machines and equipment inside the factory building.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    哈尔滨电机制造厂是苏联帮助我国建设的重点工程之一，预计在今年第三季度即可开始生产。这是工人正在安装三十吨重的大吊车，以便用它来安装厂房内的各种机器设备。
