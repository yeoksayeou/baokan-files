Title: Indonesian Trade Union Representatives Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-05-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the delegation of the Indonesian Central Organization of Trade Unions, the representative of the Indonesian Postal and Telegraph Workers Union, the representative of the Indonesian Pawnshop Workers Union, and the representative of the Indonesian Health Workers Union arrived in Beijing by train on the evening of April 30 to attend the "May Day" celebrations and observe the ceremonies.
        The list of the delegation of the Indonesian Central Organization of Trade Unions is as follows:
    Leader: Zhuwari Sidin Atma, Director of the Administrative Office of the Central Bureau of the Indonesian Central Organization of Trade Unions
    Members: Si. Mardiono, First General Secretary of the West Java Regional Executive Committee of the Indonesian Central Organization of Trade Unions
          Tati (female), Second Secretary of the Organization Department of the West Java Regional Executive Committee of the Indonesian Plantation Workers Union
          Zainal Abidin, First General Secretary of the Tanjung Enim Branch of the Indonesian Mining Workers Union
          Aslam, Staff Member of the Organization Department of the Central Bureau of the Indonesian Central Organization of Trade Unions
        The list of the representative of the Indonesian Postal and Telegraph Workers Union is as follows:
          Suratno, First General Secretary of the Central Executive Committee of the Postal and Telegraph Workers Union
        The list of the representative of the Indonesian Pawnshop Workers Union is as follows:
          Mo. Afandi, Chairman of the Central Executive Committee of the Pawnshop Workers Union
        The list of the representative of the Indonesian Health Workers Union is as follows:
          Atmosugito, Vice Chairman of the Central Executive Committee of the Health Workers Union

    Also, according to another dispatch: At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the delegation of the Federation of Indonesian Trade Unions arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of April 30 to attend the "May Day" celebrations and observe the ceremonies.
        The list of the delegation of the Federation of Indonesian Trade Unions is as follows:
    Leader: Muhammad Hidayat, General Secretary of the Indonesian Teachers Union
    Members: Muhammad Sanusi, General Secretary of the Indonesian People's Bank Employees Union, Central Committee Member of the Federation of Indonesian Trade Unions
          Basuki Somiyadileja, Chairman of the Judges Association, Central Committee Member of the Federation of Indonesian Trade Unions
          Simanjuntak Sahara, Chairman of the Jakarta Branch of the Indonesian Radio Workers Union
          Karamoy Jonas, Secretary-General of the Greater Jakarta Region of the Indonesian Teachers Union.
