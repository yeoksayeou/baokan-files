    Our Great Motherland
    Workers Liu Zhiguang (right), Guo Guangwei (middle), and Wang Bensheng (left) of the Sino-Soviet Shipbuilding Company's shipyard proposed a method for saving welding rods, which allows for the full use of previously unusable welding rod ends. Implementing this method will save the shipyard seventeen tons and seven hundred kilograms of Soviet five-millimeter welding rods per year.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-05-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中苏造船公司造船厂工人刘志广（右）郭光玮（中）王本盛（左）等提出一项节约电焊条的方法，可以全部使用过去不能用的电焊条根。实行这个方法，造船厂一年可节省十七吨又七百公斤苏联五粍电焊条。
