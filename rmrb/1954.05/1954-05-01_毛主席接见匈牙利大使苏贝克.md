Title: Chairman Mao Receives Hungarian Ambassador Szobek
Author:
Date: 1954-05-01
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government received Szobek, the Ambassador of the Hungarian People's Republic to China, this afternoon. Chairman Mao inquired in detail about the situation in Hungary and expressed his pleasure at the achievements made by the Hungarian people in various fields. Also present were Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government, and Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs.

### 毛主席接见匈牙利大使苏贝克

1954-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见匈牙利大使苏贝克
    【新华社三十日讯】中央人民政府毛泽东主席今日下午接见了匈牙利人民共和国驻华大使苏贝克。毛主席对于匈牙利的情况询问颇详，并对匈牙利人民在各方面所获得的成就表示高兴。在座的还有中央人民政府政务院副总理邓小平及外交部副部长伍修权。
