Title: The Vietnamese People's Army Endeavors to Facilitate the French Army's Evacuation of Wounded and Prisoners from Dien Bien Phu
Author:
Date: 1954-05-17
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 16th: According to Vietnam News Agency, at noon on May 13th, a French helicopter marked with a red cross, carrying Professor Hure, a representative of the French Indochina Command, and a medical team of French medical officers and staff, was permitted to land in Dien Bien Phu.

    The General Command of the Vietnamese People's Army dispatched Colonel Cao Van Khanh, Deputy Division Commander of the Vietnamese People's Army, and Nguyen Thuc Mau, a military doctor from the medical team of the Vietnamese People's Army, to meet with Professor Hure.

    Colonel Cao Van Khanh told Professor Hure that, based on the humane policy of President Ho Chi Minh and the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam towards enemy wounded and prisoners, the Vietnamese People's Army has given the enemy wounded all the care possible on the front lines and is prepared to release these wounded. Professor Hure expressed his gratitude to the representatives of the General Command of the Vietnamese People's Army for the full care given by the Vietnamese People's Army to the French army wounded in Dien Bien Phu.

    The representatives of both sides reached the following agreement:

    (1) The work of evacuating French wounded and prisoners will be carried out in three batches, with the first two batches totaling 753 wounded, and the third batch to be decided by the General Command of the Vietnamese People's Army later.

    (2) The Vietnamese People's Army will release 80 to 100 enemy wounded and prisoners of war each day.

    (3) During the entire period of evacuation of the wounded, French aircraft will cease all activities in the designated area.

    In order to accelerate the evacuation, the representatives of the General Command of the Vietnamese People's Army allowed the other party to immediately evacuate more than ten French army wounded before the agreement was signed; these released enemy wounded expressed their gratitude for the full care of the Vietnamese People's Army before leaving Dien Bien Phu.

### 越南人民军努力促成法军撤运奠边府伤俘

1954-05-17
第4版()
专栏：

    越南人民军努力促成法军撤运奠边府伤俘
    【新华社十六日讯】据越南通讯社讯：五月十三日中午，一架载着印度支那法军总司令部代表胡尔教授和法军医疗队的若干医官和工作人员的涂有红十字标记的法国直升飞机被允许在奠边府降落。
    越南人民军总司令部派遣越南人民军副师长高文庆上校和越南人民军医疗队军医阮叔茂前去会晤胡尔教授。
    高文庆上校告诉胡尔教授说，越南人民军本着胡志明主席和越南民主共和国政府的对待敌军伤俘的人道政策，已经给予敌军伤员以在战线上可能给予的一切照顾，并且准备释放这些伤员。胡尔教授向越南人民军总司令部代表表示感谢越南人民军给予奠边府法军伤员的充分照顾。
    双方代表达成如下协议：
    （一）运走法军伤俘的工作将分三批进行，头两批共七百五十三名伤员，第三批将由越南人民军总司令部以后决定。
    （二）越南人民军每天将释放八十到一百名敌军伤俘。
    （三）在撤退伤员的整个时期内，法国飞机将停止在指定地区内的一切活动。
    为了加速撤运工作，越南人民军总司令部代表在协议签字以前即允许对方立即运走十多名法军伤俘；这些被释放的敌军伤俘在离开奠边府前都对越南人民军的充分照顾表示感谢。
