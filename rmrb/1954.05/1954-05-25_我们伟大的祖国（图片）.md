Our Great Motherland
    The Northwest Institute of Animal Husbandry and Veterinary Medicine is the first higher education institution of animal husbandry and veterinary medicine in our country, specializing in training senior animal husbandry technical cadres for state-owned farms, pastures, and pastoral areas, as well as senior technical cadres with theoretical knowledge and technology for the prevention and treatment of livestock diseases. This is a veterinary anatomy teacher explaining the structure of a camel skeleton in the laboratory.

    我们伟大的祖国
    西北畜牧兽医学院是我国第一所高等畜牧兽医学校，专门培养国营农场、牧场、牧区的高级畜牧技术干部和具有家畜疾病预防、治疗理论知识与技术的高级技术干部。这是兽医系解剖科的教员在实验室里讲解骆驼骨骼的构造。
