Explanation of the National Children's Literature and Art Creation Awards for the Past Four Years

I. This time, the children's literature and art creations recommended by various regions include nine categories of works: novels, fairy tales, poems, plays, picture stories, comic strips, propaganda posters, songs, and science stories, totaling 442 pieces, including works by 289 writers. The sources are as follows:

　　(1) The central Ministry of Education notified relevant departments in various regions (including 15 provinces and 11 cities) and recommended a total of 551 works.

　　(2) The Chinese Writers Association forwarded 19 works recommended by the literary and art circles federations and writers associations of two major administrative regions.

　　(3) The China Youth Publishing House, China Young Pioneers Newspaper, People's Fine Arts Publishing House, Shandong People's Publishing House, Children's Era Publishing House, and others recommended a total of 57 works.

　　(4) 110 literary and art writers who frequently write for children were invited to recommend their own or others' outstanding creations, totaling 103 works.

　　(The above totals 730 pieces. If duplicates are not counted, the actual number is 442 pieces.)

II. According to the regulations on the scope of selection, works not published within the prescribed period will not be considered for selection.

III. A total of 46 works (songs) were awarded prizes. Among them, 9 works (songs) won first prize, each awarded a bonus of 4 million yuan; 12 works (songs) won second prize, each awarded a bonus of 3 million yuan; and 25 works (songs) won third prize, each awarded a bonus of 2 million yuan.

IV. Although the selection work this time was recommended from the bottom up, and some supplements were made from various aspects, omissions may still be unavoidable due to time constraints, and the recommended works may not include all the outstanding works. Therefore, the selected works are not necessarily complete, and the ranking of grades has not been able to determine a clearly defined different standard. The above results are only obtained after repeated evaluation and comparison based on various opinions from the existing works.

### 关于四年来全国儿童文艺创作评奖的说明

1954-05-31
第3版()
专栏：

    关于四年来全国儿童文艺创作评奖的说明
    一、这次各地推荐的儿童文艺创作有小说、童话、诗歌、剧本、图画故事、连环画、宣传画、歌曲    及科学故事等九类作品共计四百四十二篇，包括作家二百八十九人的作品，其来源如下：
    （1）由中央教育部通知各地所属部门（包括十五个省、十一个市）共推荐了作品五百五十一篇。
    （2）中国作家协会转来有两个大行政区文学艺术界联合会、作家协会推荐的作品十九篇。
    （3）中国青年出版社、中国少年报社、人民美术出版社、山东人民出版社、儿童时代社等共推荐了作品五十七篇。
    （4）函请一百一十位经常为儿童写作的文艺作家推荐自己的或别人的优秀创作计一百零三篇。
    （以上共计七百三十篇，如不计算重复者，实际为四百四十二篇。）二、根据评选范围的规定，凡不是在规定期限内发表的作品一律不予评选。三、得奖作品共四十六篇（首），计获得一等奖作品共九篇（首），每篇授与奖金四百万元；二等奖作品共十二篇（首），每篇授与奖金三百万元；三等奖作品共二十五篇（首），每篇授与奖金二百万元。四、此次评选工作虽系从下到上层层推荐，并从各方面作了一些补充，但因限于时间，遗漏仍恐难免，并且所推荐的作品不一定包括了所有的优秀作品，从而所选出的作品也不尽是完整无缺的，而等级的排列也未能定出显然划分的不同标准，只是从现有的作品中根据各方面意见反复评比而后得出以上的结果。
