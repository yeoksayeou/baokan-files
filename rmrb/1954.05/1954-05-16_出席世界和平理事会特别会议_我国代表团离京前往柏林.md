Title: Chinese Delegation Departs Beijing for Berlin to Attend the World Peace Council Special Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-05-16
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, May 15th: A special meeting of the World Peace Council will be held in Berlin at the end of May. The Chinese delegation attending the meeting, led by its head Guo Moruo, departed by plane from Beijing this morning, May 15th, for Moscow, where they will transfer to Berlin.

    The Vietnamese delegation, represented by Le Dinh Tham and Nguyen Xuan Thuy, and the Japanese delegation, represented by Togo Kameda, who are also attending the World Peace Council's special meeting in Berlin, also departed from Beijing for Moscow by plane on the 15th, where they will transfer to Berlin.

    More than 40 people, including Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, were at the airport to see them off.

    The list of the Chinese delegation attending the World Peace Council's special meeting in Berlin is as follows:

    Head of Delegation: Guo Moruo.

    Council Members: Mao Dun, Ma Yinchu, Zhang Bojun, Cai Tingkai, Liao Chengzhi, Xiao San, Liu Guanyi, Lu Cui, Jin Zhonghua, Li Yimang (currently abroad).

    Secretary-General: Liao Chengzhi (concurrent), Liu Guanyi (concurrent).

    Deputy Secretary-General: Tang Mingzhao.

### 出席世界和平理事会特别会议  我国代表团离京前往柏林

1954-05-16
第1版()
专栏：

    出席世界和平理事会特别会议
    我国代表团离京前往柏林
    【新华社十五日讯】世界和平理事会特别会议将于五月底在柏林举行，出席会议的我国代表团在团长郭沫若率领下，已于十五日晨乘飞机赴莫斯科转往柏林。
    出席世界和平理事会柏林特别会议的越南代表团代表黎廷探、阮春水及日本代表团代表龟田东伍也于十五日同时由北京乘飞机赴莫斯科转往柏林。
    到机场欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通等四十多人。
    出席世界和平理事会柏林特别会议中国代表团名单如下：
    团长：郭沫若。
    理事：茅盾、马寅初、章伯钧、蔡廷锴、廖承志、萧三、刘贯一、陆璀、金仲华、李一氓（现在国外）。
    秘书长：廖承志（兼）、刘贯一（兼）。
    副秘书长：唐明照。
