Title: Vice Premier Pham Van Dong Issues Statement at Geneva Conference: Allowing French Troops to Transport Severely Wounded Prisoners from Dien Bien Phu
Author:
Date: 1954-05-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 10th: Pham Van Dong, Vice Premier and Acting Foreign Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, who is attending the Geneva Conference, issued a statement at today's meeting. The statement said:

"The delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, acting on instructions from its government, hereby makes the following statement:

The Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam adheres to a humanitarian policy in this war, especially towards prisoners of war and the wounded. Based on this humanitarian policy, my government is prepared to allow the transportation of severely wounded personnel of the French Expeditionary Force captured at Dien Bien Phu.

If the French government wishes to transport these severely wounded personnel, representatives of both commands will take practical measures on the spot to address this matter."

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 10th: The delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam participating in the Geneva Conference issued the following communique:

The statement issued by the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam at the Geneva Conference on May 10, 1954, stated:

"...my government is prepared to allow the transportation of severely wounded personnel of the French Expeditionary Force captured at Dien Bien Phu."

Regarding this matter, we make the following clarification:

Our so-called "French Expeditionary Force" refers to all the troops under the command of General de Castries at Dien Bien Phu, regardless of their nationality.

### 范文同副总理在日内瓦会议上发表声明  允许法军运走在奠边府被俘的重伤人员

1954-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

    范文同副总理在日内瓦会议上发表声明
    允许法军运走在奠边府被俘的重伤人员
    【新华社日内瓦十日电】出席日内瓦会议的越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外交部长范文同，在今天的会议上发表了一项声明。声明说：
    “越南民主共和国代表团奉本国政府之命特作如下声明：
    越南民主共和国政府在此次战争中，向持人道政策，对于俘虏和伤兵尤其是如此。基于这种人道政策，我政府准备允许运走在奠边府俘获的法国远征军重伤人员。
    如果法国政府愿意运走这些重伤人员，双方司令部的代表将对此事就地采取切实的措施。”
    【新华社日内瓦十日电】参加日内瓦会议的越南民主共和国代表团发表公报如下：
    一九五四年五月十日越南民主共和国代表团在日内瓦会议上所发表的声明中说：
    “……我政府准备允许运走在奠边府俘获的法国远征军重伤人员”。
    关于此事，我们作下列的澄清：
    我们的所谓“法国远征军”，系指奠边府德卡斯特莱将军统率下的所有军队，不问其属于何国籍。
