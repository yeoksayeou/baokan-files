Title: Korean Cabinet Passes Decision on Strengthening Railway Transport
Author:
Date: 1954-05-11
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, May 10th: According to the Korean Central News Agency, in order to guarantee the completion of the enormous transportation tasks stipulated in the Three-Year Plan for the Post-War Recovery and Development of the People's Economy, the Korean Cabinet recently passed a decision on strengthening railway transport.
    The decision stipulates: before June 15th, a check, repair, and rectification of all railway lines, bridges, tunnels, etc., nationwide must be carried out. Before the end of June, all original bridges on other lines, except for the Wonsan-Rajin line, must be restored to traffic.
    The decision stipulates: in order to improve and strengthen cargo transportation, the loading and unloading of goods at border stations and international joint transportation stations must be mechanized. Freight trucks and ox/horse carts must be allocated to stations in major cities and necessary stations.
    The decision stipulates: the restoration and reorganization of major stations must be completed within this year. In addition, starting from August 1st, direct express trains will begin running from Pyongyang to Chongjin and from Pyongyang to Sinuiju.

### 朝鲜内阁通过关于加强铁道运输的决定

1954-05-11
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜内阁通过关于加强铁道运输的决定
    【新华社平壤十日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：朝鲜内阁为保证完成战后人民经济恢复发展的三年计划所规定的巨大的运输任务，最近通过了关于加强铁道运输的决定。
    决定中规定：在六月十五日以前，对全国所有铁道线路、桥梁、隧道等进行一次检查、修补和整顿。在六月底以前，除元山—罗津线外，要使其他各线路原有的桥梁恢复通车。
    决定中规定：为了改善和加强货物运输，要使国境车站和国际联运车站的货物装卸机械化。要在主要城市的车站和必要的车站配置运货汽车和牛、马车。
    决定中规定：在今年内要恢复和整理各主要车站。另外，从八月一日起，开始驶行平壤至清津和平壤至新义州的直达快车。
