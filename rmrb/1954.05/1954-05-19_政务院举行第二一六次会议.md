Title: Government Administration Council Holds 216th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-05-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 17th -- The 216th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 13th. The meeting heard and approved the report by Xue Muqiao, Director of the State Statistical Bureau of the Central People's Government, on the Third National Statistical Work Conference, and approved various appointment and removal proposals.

### 政务院举行第二一六次会议

1954-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第二一六次会议
    【新华社十七日讯】中央人民政府政务院第二百一十六次政务会议在十三日下午举行。会议听了和批准了中央人民政府国家统计局薛暮桥局长所作的关于第三届全国统计工作会议的报告，通过了各项任免案。
