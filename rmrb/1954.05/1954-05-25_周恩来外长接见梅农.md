Title: Zhou Enlai Receives Menon

Author:
Date: 1954-05-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 23rd: Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, received Indian Representative to the United Nations, Menon, at 3:00 PM today.

### 周恩来外长接见梅农

1954-05-25
第4版()
专栏：

    周恩来外长接见梅农
    【新华社日内瓦二十三日电】中华人民共和国外交部部长周恩来今日下午三时接见印度驻联合国代表梅农。
