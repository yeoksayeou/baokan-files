Title: The U.S. Prepares to Convene a Five-Nation Military Conference, Attempting to Formulate a "Joint Action" Plan for Southeast Asia
Author:
Date: 1954-05-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, May 9th: A "New York Times" correspondent in Washington reports that U.S. State Department personnel say that representatives (possibly chiefs of staff) from the United States, Britain, France, Australia, and New Zealand will hold a meeting next week to formulate a plan for "joint action" in Southeast Asia. The meeting will be held in Singapore or Washington.

### 美国准备召集五国军事会议  企图制订东南亚“联合行动”计划

1954-05-11
第4版()
专栏：

    美国准备召集五国军事会议
    企图制订东南亚“联合行动”计划
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社纽约九日讯：“纽约时报”驻华盛顿记者报道，美国国务院人员说，美国、英国、法国、澳大利亚、新西兰的代表（可能是参谋长）将在下星期举行会议，以便制订在东南亚“联合行动”的计划。会议将在新加坡或华盛顿举行。
