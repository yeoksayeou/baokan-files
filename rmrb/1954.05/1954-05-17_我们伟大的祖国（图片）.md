Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-05-17
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The suburbs of major cities are developing vegetable production in a planned way to meet the needs of urban people. This is the scene of members of the Xu Hongsheng Agricultural Production Cooperative, a vegetable agricultural production cooperative in the suburbs of Hangzhou, harvesting Chinese cabbage. The vegetables produced here not only meet the needs of Hangzhou, but also meet the needs of other cities such as Shanghai.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-05-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    各大城市的郊区正在有计划地发展蔬菜的生产，以供应城市人民的需要。这是杭州市郊区的一个蔬菜农业生产合作社——许洪生农业生产合作社的社员们收获小白菜的情形。这里生产的蔬菜不仅供应杭州市的需要，而且供应上海等其他城市的需要。
