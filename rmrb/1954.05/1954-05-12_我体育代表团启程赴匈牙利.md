Title: Our Sports Delegation Departs for Hungary
Author:
Date: 1954-05-12
Page: 4

    Our Sports Delegation Departs for Hungary
    [This newspaper dispatch] To further enhance the friendship between our country and the People's Democratic countries of Eastern Europe, and to better learn from the sports experience of fraternal countries, the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission has decided to dispatch a Chinese sports delegation to visit Hungary, Poland, and Bulgaria. The delegation, led by Chairman Wang Ke, departed by plane for Hungary at 6:00 AM on May 9th.
    The Chinese sports delegation includes fifteen male basketball players and seven staff members. Every athlete was selected from the "August 1st" men's basketball team, which won the men's basketball championship at the 1953 National Basketball and Volleyball Tournament.
    The Chinese sports delegation will also be invited to participate in the Soviet Union and People's Democratic Countries basketball competition to be held in Bulgaria this June.

### 我体育代表团启程赴匈牙利

1954-05-12
第4版()
专栏：

    我体育代表团启程赴匈牙利
    【本报讯】为进一步增进我国与东欧各人民民主国家间的友谊，更好地学习兄弟国家的体育运动经验，中央人民政府体育运动委员会决定派遣中国体育代表团，前往匈牙利、波兰和保加利亚进行访问。代表团一行在团长王克率领下，已于五月九日上午六时乘飞机启程赴匈牙利。
    中国体育代表团包括十五名男子篮球运动员和七名工作人员。每一个运动员都是从获得一九五三年全国篮、排球比赛大会男子篮球冠军的“八一”男子篮球队中选拔出来的。
    这次出国的中国体育代表团还将被邀参加今年六月在保加利亚举行的苏联及人民民主国家的篮球比赛。
