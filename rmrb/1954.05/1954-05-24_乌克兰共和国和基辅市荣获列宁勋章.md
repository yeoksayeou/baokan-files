Title: Ukrainian Republic and Kiev City Awarded the Order of Lenin
Author:
Date: 1954-05-24
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 22nd: According to TASS, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union has awarded the Order of Lenin to the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic and its capital, Kiev, respectively, to commemorate the 300th anniversary of the reunification of Ukraine with Russia, to celebrate the outstanding achievements of the Ukrainian people in economic and cultural construction, and to recognize the great role of Kiev in the history of the Russian, Ukrainian, and Belarusian peoples.

### 乌克兰共和国和基辅市荣获列宁勋章

1954-05-24
第3版()
专栏：

    乌克兰共和国和基辅市荣获列宁勋章
    【新华社二十三日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科二十二日讯：为了纪念乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯三百周年，并庆祝乌克兰人民在经济和文化建设中的卓越成就，以及基辅市在俄罗斯、乌克兰和白俄罗斯人民的历史上所起的巨大作用，苏联最高苏维埃主席团特以列宁勋章分别授予乌克兰苏维埃社会主义共和国及其首都基辅市。
