Title: Spokesperson for the Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Holds Press Conference
Author: 
Date: 1954-05-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 10th: Huang Wen Huan, spokesperson for the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam attending the Geneva Conference, held a press conference at the Palais des Nations here at 5:30 p.m. on the 10th. Huang Wen Huan introduced to nearly two hundred foreign journalists the content of the speech made by Pham Van Dong, Chief Representative of the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, at the afternoon session on the 10th, and his eight-point proposal for restoring peace in Indochina. Huang Wen Huan also announced that the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, based on its consistent humanitarian policy of treating prisoners of war with leniency, will allow the severely wounded personnel of the French Expeditionary Force captured at Dien Bien Phu this time to be transported away.

Huang Wen Huan's announcement on the above points aroused deep attention from the journalists from various countries present.

### 越南民主共和国代表团发言人举行记者招待会

1954-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

    越南民主共和国代表团发言人举行记者招待会
    【新华社日内瓦十日电】出席日内瓦会议的越南民主共和国代表团发言人黄文欢十日下午五时半在此间国联大厦举行记者招待会。黄文欢向近二百名各国新闻记者介绍了越南民主共和国代表团首席代表范文同在十日下午会议上发言的内容及其关于恢复印度支那和平的八点建议。黄文欢并宣布：越南民主共和国政府本其一贯宽待战俘的人道主义政策，对此次在奠边府被俘的法国远征军重伤人员准予运走。
    黄文欢关于上述各点的宣布，引起了到会的各国记者的深刻注意。
