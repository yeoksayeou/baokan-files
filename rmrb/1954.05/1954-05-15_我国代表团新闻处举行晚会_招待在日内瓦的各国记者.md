Title: China Delegation Press Office Hosts Reception for Journalists from Various Countries in Geneva
Author:
Date: 1954-05-15
Page: 4

    China Delegation Press Office Hosts Reception for Journalists from Various Countries in Geneva

    [Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 13th] The Press Office of the People's Republic of China Delegation attending the Geneva Conference held a reception at the Saint Peter's Square Theater here on the evening of the 13th, showing the Chinese color documentary film "National Day, 1952" to entertain journalists from various countries covering the conference news here. More than 250 journalists from various countries who attended the reception were very interested in this documentary film reflecting the face of the new China.

### 我国代表团新闻处举行晚会  招待在日内瓦的各国记者

1954-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    我国代表团新闻处举行晚会
    招待在日内瓦的各国记者
    【新华社日内瓦十三日电】出席日内瓦会议的中华人民共和国代表团新闻处十三日晚在此间圣彼得广场剧院举行晚会，放映我国五彩纪录影片“一九五二年国庆节”，招待来此间采访会议新闻的各国记者。出席晚会的各国记者二百五十余人对这一反映新中国面貌的纪录影片感到很大的兴趣。
