Title: Yunnan Tin Corporation Fulfills Last Month's Production Plan
Author:
Date: 1954-05-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 16th: The Yunnan Tin Corporation exceeded its production plan for April. Among them, refined tin completed 100% of the plan; solder completed 110.56%.

　　In April, workers of various factories and mines affiliated with the Yunnan Tin Corporation studied Wang Chonglun's spirit of innovation and further launched a labor competition. Workers in the machine selection workshop of the Gejiu Mineral Processing Plant actively looked for tricks and improved the shaking table, enabling the workshop to complete its April production plan five days ahead of schedule. In April, the various factories and mines affiliated with the Tin Corporation also further promoted advanced experiences such as the production area management system, which greatly improved the level of management.

### 云南锡业公司  完成上月生产计划

1954-05-17
第1版()
专栏：

    云南锡业公司
    完成上月生产计划
    【新华社昆明十六日电】云南锡业公司四月份超额完成了生产计划。其中精锡完成计划百分之一百；焊锡完成百分之一百十点五六。
    四月份，云南锡业公司所属各厂矿职工学习王崇伦的革新精神，进一步展开了劳动竞赛。个旧选矿厂机选车间职工积极寻找窍门，改进了摇床，使这个车间四月份的生产计划提前五天就完成了。四月份，锡业公司所属各厂矿还进一步推行生产区域管理制等先进经验，大大提高了管理水平。
