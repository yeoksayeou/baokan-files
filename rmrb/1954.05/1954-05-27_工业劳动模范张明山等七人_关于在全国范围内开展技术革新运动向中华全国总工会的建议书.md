Title: Suggestion from Industrial Labor Models Zhang Mingshan and Six Others to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Regarding Launching a Technical Innovation Movement Nationwide
Author:
Date: 1954-05-27
Page: 1

To the All-China Federation of Trade Unions:

We attended the opening ceremony of the Anshan Iron and Steel Technical Innovation Exhibition and were greatly inspired and educated. We believe that adopting technical innovation methods to tap the potential of existing factories and mines' production technology and equipment, in order to increase labor productivity, improve product quality, reduce product costs, reduce workers' physical labor, improve working conditions, ensure production safety, and protect the health of employees, is entirely necessary and possible.

We, the working class, have become the masters of the country. Under the education and cultivation of the Chinese Communist Party, we have seen the brilliant prospects of socialist industrialization and the infinitely bright future. We workers should become conscious fighters for socialism and should shoulder the glorious task of socialist construction. In order to continuously improve labor productivity and promote the early realization of socialist industrialization, we suggest launching a technical innovation movement nationwide. We propose that the following points be taken as the common goals for all employees across the country:

	I. Improve existing production technology and equipment. Gradually change laborious and cumbersome manual operations into mechanical or semi-mechanical operations.

	II. Improve operating methods and fully exploit the potential of production technology and equipment.

	III. Rationally utilize working time and reduce the consumption of raw materials, materials, tools, fuel, and electricity.

	IV. Improve technical standards, technical inspections, and technical safety measures.

	V. In accordance with the requirements of the national plan, strive for the research, design, and trial production of new products.

	VI. Learn from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union, learn new technologies and new operating methods, and constantly break through quotas.

	VII. Improve production organization and labor organization, save manpower, shorten non-production time, and overcome imbalances and disconnections in production.

	VIII. Workers, engineering and technical personnel, and staff should cooperate closely, give full play to collective wisdom, and do a good job in production.

	IX. Do not be complacent or arrogant, learn from each other, and help each other.

Please consider the feasibility of the above suggestions, All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

With respect!
			Zhang Mingshan, Wang Chonglun, Tang Liyan, Huang Rongchang,
			Liu Zuwei, Zhu Shunyu, Fu Jingwen
				April 15, 1954

### 工业劳动模范张明山等七人  关于在全国范围内开展技术革新运动向中华全国总工会的建议书

1954-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

    工业劳动模范张明山等七人
    关于在全国范围内开展技术革新运动向中华全国总工会的建议书
    中华全国总工会：
    我们参加了鞍钢技术革新展览会的开幕典礼，受到了很大的启发和教育。我们认为采用技术革新的方法，发掘现有厂矿企业生产技术设备的潜力，以提高劳动生产率，提高产品质量，降低产品成本，减轻工人的体力劳动，改善劳动条件，保障生产安全，保护职工的健康，是完全必要和可能的。
    我们工人阶级已经成为国家的主人翁。在中国共产党的教育和培养下，我们看到了社会主义工业化的灿烂前景，看到了无限美好的将来。我们工人应该成为社会主义的自觉的战士，应该担负起社会主义建设的光荣任务。为了不断地提高劳动生产率，促使社会主义工业化的早日实现，我们建议在全国范围内开展技术革新运动。并提议把以下几点，作为全国职工共同努力的目标：
    一、改善旧有的生产技术设备。逐步改变费力的、笨重的手工操作为机械的或半机械的操作。
    二、改进操作方法，充分发挥生产技术设备潜力。
    三、合理地利用工作时间，减少原料、材料、工具、燃料、电力的消耗。
    四、改进技术标准、技术检查与技术安全的措施。
    五、根据国家计划的要求，努力新产品的研究、设计与试制。
    六、学习苏联先进经验，学习新技术和新操作方法，不断地突破定额。
    七、改善生产组织和劳动组织，节约人力，缩短非生产时间，克服生产中的不均衡和脱节现象。
    八、工人、工程技术人员和职员密切合作，发挥集体智慧，搞好生产。
    九、不自满，不骄傲，互相学习，互相帮助。
    以上建议是否可行，请中华全国总工会考虑。
    此致敬礼！
            张明山、王崇伦、唐立言、黄荣昌、
            刘祖威、朱顺余、傅景文
                    一九五四年四月十五日
