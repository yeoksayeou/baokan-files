Title: General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army Notifies the French Military Headquarters of the Decision to Allow the French Army to Transport Wounded Prisoners of War from Dien Bien Phu

Author:
Date: 1954-05-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 12th: Vietnam News Agency, May 12th: The General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army issued the following notification to the General Headquarters of the French Army in Indochina regarding the transportation of seriously wounded enemy prisoners of war from Dien Bien Phu:

    The General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army hereby notifies the General Headquarters of the French Army in Indochina: Based on the humanitarian policy of President Ho Chi Minh and the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and in accordance with the statement made by the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam at the Geneva Conference regarding the issue of seriously wounded French prisoners of war in Dien Bien Phu, the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army has decided to allow the General Headquarters of the French Army in Indochina to transport seriously wounded French prisoners of war from Dien Bien Phu. The General Headquarters of the French Army in Indochina may immediately send representatives to meet with representatives of the Vietnam People's Army, who will inform them of the conditions and methods for transporting the seriously wounded personnel.

    The meeting place is designated as the north of the Dien Bien Phu airport. The French representatives must arrive by helicopter marked with a Red Cross symbol.

    The meeting time shall be determined by the General Headquarters of the French Army in Indochina and notified one day in advance.

### 越南人民军总司令部通知法军总司令部  决定允许法军运走在奠边府被俘的重伤员

1954-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    越南人民军总司令部通知法军总司令部
    决定允许法军运走在奠边府被俘的重伤员
   【新华社十二日讯】越南通讯社十二日讯：越南人民军总司令部就运走在奠边府被俘的敌军重伤人员问题给印度支那法军总司令部的通知如下：
    越南人民军总司令部特通知印度支那法军总司令部：越南人民军总司令部本着胡志明主席以及越南民主共和国政府的人道政策，按照越南民主共和国代表团在日内瓦会议上发表的关于在奠边府被俘的法军重伤人员问题的声明，决定允许印度支那法军总司令部运走在奠边府被俘的法军重伤人员。印度支那法军总司令部可以立即派遣代表会晤越南人民军代表，越南人民军代表将告诉他运走重伤人员的条件和方式。
    会晤地点定在奠边府机场北部。法方代表得乘涂有红十字标记的直升飞机前来。
    会晤时间由印度支那法军总司令部规定，并早一天通知。
