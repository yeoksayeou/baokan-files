Title: Chairman Mao Receives the Delegation of the Korean Visit to China

Author: 
Date: 1954-05-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government received Kim Ung-gi, head of the Korean People's Delegation to China, and Kim Dal-hyun, Lee Young-ho, and Lee Dong-young, deputy heads of the delegation, on April 30.

Present from the Chinese side were Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Soong Ching Ling, Li Jishen, Lin Boqu, Chen Yun, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Deng Xiaoping, Peng Zhen, Zhang Bojun, Chen Shutong, and Liao Chengzhi.

Present from the Korean side were the famous writer Han Seol-ya, Kang Hou-bong, Secretary-General of the Korean People's Delegation to China, Kang Cheol-yeon, leader of the Korean People's Army Concert Troupe, Lee Seo-hyang, leader of the National Art Theater, and Ahn Young-il, leader of the National Classical Art Theater.

Also present was Choi Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China.

After the meeting, Head Kim Ung-gi presented gifts to Chairman Mao Zedong on behalf of the Korean people and delivered a dedication.

Chairman Mao Zedong personally accepted the gifts and, accompanied by Head Kim Ung-gi, viewed the gifts one by one.

### 毛主席接见朝鲜访华代表团

1954-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见朝鲜访华代表团
    据新华社三十日讯：中央人民政府毛泽东主席在四月三十日接见朝鲜人民访华代表团团长金应基、副团长金达铉、李永镐、李东英。
    在座的，中国方面有朱德、刘少奇、宋庆龄、李济深、林伯渠、陈云、郭沫若、黄炎培、邓小平、彭真、章伯钧、陈叔通、廖承志。
    在座的，朝鲜方面有著名作家韩雪野、朝鲜人民访华代表团书记长康候凤、朝鲜人民军协奏团领队姜哲渊、国立艺术剧场领队李曙乡、国立古典艺术剧场领队安英一。
    在座的还有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使崔一。
    接见后，金应基团长代表朝鲜人民向毛泽东主席献礼，并致献词。
    毛泽东主席亲自受礼并在金应基团长陪同下，逐一观看了礼品。
