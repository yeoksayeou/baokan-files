Title: Mongolian Cycling Expedition Team Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1954-05-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 18th: The Mongolian People's Republic's Ulan Bator-Beijing Cycling Expedition Team, invited to visit our country according to the 1954 Sino-Mongolian Cultural Cooperation Plan, left Beijing by bicycle along the original route on the afternoon of the 18th to return home. Zhu Debao, Deputy Director of the Sports Competition Department of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, was specially assigned to accompany them to the Sino-Mongolian border.

At 1 p.m. on the 18th, before the Mongolian Cycling Expedition Team's departure, a grand farewell meeting was held by people from all walks of life in the capital at the Working People's Cultural Palace. Those present included: Wei Que, member of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission; Huang Zhong, Deputy Secretary-General; Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Government Administration Council's Committee on Cultural and Educational Affairs; Chai Zemin, Deputy Secretary-General of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Reception Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Fu Hao, specialist of the Asian Department; and representatives from the capital's sports circles, athletes, students, and citizens, totaling more than 1,000 people.

Ochirbat, Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, and embassy staff also attended the farewell meeting.

Wei Que delivered a speech at the meeting, wishing the Mongolian Cycling Expedition Team a successful return to Ulan Bator and the completion of the meaningful cycling expedition of more than 2,800 kilometers round trip.

Danbi, the captain of the Mongolian Cycling Expedition Team, delivered a speech. He said that after returning home, he would certainly introduce the new life and labor enthusiasm of the Chinese people to his own people, and wished further development of the friendship between the Mongolian and Chinese people and athletes.

At the meeting, the Mongolian Cycling Expedition Team received flowers presented by the children of Beijing. Then, amidst warm applause and cheers, they rode their bicycles and waved goodbye to the well-wishers.

Another dispatch: Before leaving Beijing to return home, Danbi, the captain of the Mongolian People's Republic's Ulan Bator-Beijing Cycling Expedition Team, issued a written statement on his impressions of New China. He said: During this visit to China, we have seen the brilliant achievements made in China's sports cause under the great care of the Chinese Communist Party, the Central People's Government, and Chairman Mao Zedong. He finally said: We will report with admiration to our people, our youth, and athletes in detail about the glorious victories of our great friend, the Chinese people and their youth, and the Chinese people's sincere friendship and boundless love for the Mongolian people.

### 蒙古自行车远足队离京回国

1954-05-19
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古自行车远足队离京回国
    【新华社十八日讯】根据中蒙一九五四年度文化合作计划，应邀前来我国访问的蒙古人民共和国乌兰巴托—北京自行车远足队，在十八日下午自北京乘自行车沿原来路线返国。中央人民政府体育运动委员会特派运动竞赛司副司长朱德宝等人陪送他们到中蒙边境。
    十八日下午一时，在蒙古自行车远足队临行前，首都各界在劳动人民文化宫举行盛大欢送会。到会的有：中央人民政府体育运动委员会委员韦悫、副秘书长黄中，外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航，政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深，北京市人民政府副秘书长柴泽民，中央人民政府外交部交际处副处长王拓、亚洲司专员符浩以及首都体育界代表、运动员、学生、市民等共一千多人。
    蒙古人民共和国驻我国大使奥其尔巴特和大使馆人员也参加了欢送大会。
    韦悫在会上致词，他祝蒙古自行车远足队胜利返回乌兰巴托，完成往返两千八百多公里的具有重大意义的自行车远足。
    蒙古自行车远足队队长丹毕致词。他表示回国后一定要把中国人民的新的生活情况和劳动热情介绍给本国人民；并祝蒙中两国人民及运动员之间的友谊获得进一步的发展。
    会上，蒙古自行车远足队接受了北京市少年儿童们的献花。随后他们便在热烈的掌声和欢呼声中，乘上自行车向欢送者挥手告别。
    又讯：蒙古人民共和国乌兰巴托—北京自行车远足队离开北京回国前，队长丹毕发表了对新中国观感的书面谈话。他说：这次在中国，我们看到了在中国共产党、中央人民政府和毛泽东主席极大关怀下的中国体育运动事业所获得的辉煌的成就。他最后说：我们将要以敬佩的心情向我国人民、我国青年和运动员详细地介绍我们伟大的朋友——中国人民及其青年们的辉煌的胜利和中国人民对蒙古人民的真诚的友谊和无限的热爱。
