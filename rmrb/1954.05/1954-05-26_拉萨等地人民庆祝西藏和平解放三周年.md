Title: People in Lhasa and other places celebrate the third anniversary of the peaceful liberation of Tibet

Author:

Date: 1954-05-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, May 25th: People of all ethnic groups in Lhasa, Shigatse, Gyantse, Changdu, and Taizhao and other towns on the Kangzang Plateau warmly celebrated the third anniversary of the peaceful liberation of Tibet.

More than two hundred responsible cadres from the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and senior monk and secular officials from the Tibetan local government held a meeting in Lhasa on the afternoon of the 23rd to celebrate the third anniversary of the signing of the agreement on measures for the peaceful liberation of Tibet. General Zhang Jingwu, representative of the Central People's Government, also attended the celebration meeting. At the meeting, Kalon Sohkang Wangqing Geli, representative of the Tibetan local government, reported on the significance of the meeting. Monk official Tuden Tandar gave a detailed report at the celebration meeting on the significant achievements made by the Tibetan people and the People's Liberation Army entering Tibet in implementing the agreement on measures for the peaceful liberation of Tibet over the past three years. He stated that the Tibetan people should further strengthen their unity with all fraternal ethnic groups in the motherland in order to thoroughly implement the agreement on measures for the peaceful liberation of Tibet. Representatives of monks and secular officials at all levels of the Tibetan local government, including Zhasha Liuxia Tuden Tapa, Taiji Jianglejin, Zibon Langselin, Wangde, representative of the Panchen Kanpo Lija, and Duwa Kanbu, representative of the three major monasteries, spoke at the meeting one after another. In their speeches, they used various specific examples from their own personal experiences to illustrate the greatness of Chairman Mao's ethnic policy and the benefits that the agreement on measures for the peaceful liberation of Tibet had brought to the Tibetan people. Liuxia Tuden Tapa said: After the signing of the agreement on measures for the peaceful liberation of Tibet, the brilliance of Chairman Mao's ethnic policy shone all over Tibet, enabling the development of Tibet's political, economic, cultural and other construction undertakings. This is a great kindness that we Tibetan people will never forget.

General Zhang Jingwu, representative of the Central People's Government, spoke. On behalf of the staff of the Central People's Government and the People's Liberation Army entering Tibet, he thanked the Dalai Lama, the Panchen Erdeni, and all the Tibetan people for leading the Tibetan people in actively participating in supporting transportation and building the Kangzang Highway over the past three years. He also used the achievements made in implementing the peaceful agreement over the past three years to illustrate the importance of the agreement to the development of the Tibetan ethnic group and the Tibetan people towards a happy future. He called on the officials and cadres present to continue to promote the spirit of unity, mutual assistance, friendship and cooperation, and to gradually realize the policy of unity, progress and greater development under the wise leadership of the Central People's Government and Chairman Mao.

The celebration meeting finally passed a telegram of salute to Chairman Mao and a telegram of condolences to the military workers and civilians who built the Kangzang Highway.

Before the 23rd, monk and secular officials of the Tibetan local government in Lhasa, people from cultural and educational circles, the Lhasa Patriotic Youth Cultural Association, the Lhasa Patriotic Women's Association, and staff of the Lhasa organs and troops of the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army held forums and report meetings separately. At these meetings, Tibetan monks and secular officials and people from all walks of life spoke freely about the achievements made in the Tibet region in implementing the agreement on measures for the peaceful liberation of Tibet over the past three years, and discussed ways to further thoroughly implement the agreement. The headquarters and political department of the Tibet Military Region also specially held a banquet to welcome the senior officials of the Tibetan local government, and the Tibetan local government also invited the responsible cadres of the Lhasa organs and troops of the Tibet Military Region to hold a unity banquet.

On the 23rd, colored arches were erected on the streets of Shigatse, and national flags were hung. People of all ethnic groups held a celebration meeting. Nearly two thousand representatives from all walks of life in Shigatse and representatives from various zongs and creek cards participated in the meeting. Farmers and herdsmen from the northern Tibetan pastoral areas, along the Yarlung Zangbo River, and in the Himalayan mountains also came to participate in the celebration meeting. At the meeting, representatives of the people from all walks of life in Shigatse presented hadas and flowers to General Zhang Jingwu, representative of the Central People's Government, and the leaders of the Tibet Military Region to pay their respects. The business community also presented condolences to the People's Liberation Army, which were accepted by the leaders of the troops stationed in Shigatse on behalf of them. Ji Jinmei, director of the Panchen Kanpo Lija, spoke at the meeting, expressing his heartfelt gratitude to the People's Liberation Army for the benefits it had brought to the Tibetan people by faithfully implementing the agreement.

In Gyantse, Yadong, Zedang, Changdu, Bomi, Dingqing, Taizhao and other places, people of all ethnic groups also carried out celebration activities.

### 拉萨等地人民庆祝西藏和平解放三周年

1954-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

    拉萨等地人民庆祝西藏和平解放三周年
    据新华社拉萨二十五日电：康藏高原上的拉萨、日喀则、江孜和昌都、太昭等各城镇的各族人民，热烈庆祝西藏和平解放三周年。
    中国人民解放军西藏军区负责干部和西藏地方政府高级僧俗官员二百多人，二十三日下午在拉萨市举行集会，庆祝关于和平解放西藏办法的协议签订三周年。中央人民政府代表张经武将军也出席了庆祝会。会上，西藏地方政府代表噶伦索康·旺清格来报告了开会的意义。僧官土丹旦达在庆祝会上详细地报告了三年来西藏人民和进藏人民解放军在执行和平解放西藏办法的协议所获得的重大成绩。他表示，今后西藏人民要进一步加强和祖国各兄弟民族的团结，以求彻底实现和平解放西藏办法的协议。先后在会上讲话的有西藏地方政府各级僧俗官员的代表札萨柳霞·土登塔巴、台吉江乐金、孜本朗色林，班禅堪布会议厅委员会驻拉萨代表旺德和三大寺代表都瓦堪布等人。他们在讲话中，用自己亲身感受的各种具体事例，说明毛主席民族政策的伟大和和平解放西藏办法的协议给西藏人民带来的好处。柳霞·土登塔巴说：和平解放西藏办法的协议签订后，毛主席民族政策的光芒便照遍了西藏，使西藏的政治、经济、文化等各种建设事业都得到了发展，这是我们西藏人民永远忘不了的大恩情。
    中央人民政府代表张经武将军讲话。他代表进藏的中央人民政府工作人员和人民解放军，向三年来领导西藏人民积极参加支援运输、修筑康藏公路的达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼和全体西藏人民致谢。他并以三年来执行和平协议获得的成绩，说明协议对西藏民族和西藏人民向幸福前途发展的重要性。他号召到会的官员和干部，继续发扬团结互助友爱合作的精神，在中央人民政府和毛主席英明领导下，逐步实现团结、进步、更加发展的方针。
    庆祝会最后通过给毛主席的致敬电和慰问修筑康藏公路的军工和民工的电报。
    在二十三日以前，拉萨市西藏地方政府的僧俗官员、文化教育界人士和拉萨市爱国青年文化联谊会、拉萨市爱国妇女联谊会以及中国人民解放军西藏军区驻拉萨机关部队工作人员等，都分别举行了座谈会和报告会。在这些会上，藏族僧俗官员和各阶层人士，都畅述了西藏地区三年来执行和平解放西藏办法的协议所获得的成就，并讨论了进一步彻底实现协议的办法。西藏军区司令部和政治部还特地举行宴会，欢宴西藏地方政府的高级官员，西藏地方政府也邀请西藏军区驻拉萨各机关、部队的负责干部举行团结宴会。
    二十三日那天，日喀则市街上搭起彩楼，悬挂国旗，各族人民举行了庆祝大会。参加大会的有日喀则市各界代表和各宗、谿卡的代表近两千人，藏北牧区、雅鲁藏布江沿岸和喜马拉雅山区的农牧民，也赶来参加庆祝会。会上，日喀则各界人民代表向中央人民政府代表张经武将军和西藏军区首长献哈达、鲜花致敬，商业界并向人民解放军献慰问品，都由驻日喀则部队首长代表接受。班禅堪布会议厅委员会主任委员计晋美在会上讲话，表示衷心感谢人民解放军忠实执行协议给西藏人民带来的好处。
    在江孜、亚东、泽当、昌都、波密、丁青、太昭等地，各族人民也分别进行了庆祝活动。
