Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-05-24
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The Zhoujiazhuang Agricultural Production Cooperative in Jin County, a cotton-producing area in Hebei Province, has responded to the government's call to expand cotton fields and has increased its cotton planting area by 30% compared to last year. This is a picture of the newly established state-owned Hebei Province Second Tractor Station helping the cooperative plant cotton with tractors this year.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-05-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    河北省产棉区晋县周家庄农业生产合作社响应政府关于扩大棉田的号召，比去年扩大了植棉面积百分之三十。这是今年新建立的国营河北省第二拖拉机站用拖拉机帮助该社种植棉花的情形。
