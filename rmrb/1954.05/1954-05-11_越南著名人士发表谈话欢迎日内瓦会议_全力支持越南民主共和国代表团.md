Title: Prominent Vietnamese Figures Speak Out to Welcome the Geneva Conference, Fully Support the Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1954-05-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 10th: According to Vietnam News Agency on the 9th, prominent Vietnamese figures have issued statements supporting the Geneva Conference and the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

    Thi Son, member of the National Committee of the Vietnam National United Front, said: "We welcome the Geneva Conference. The participation of the delegation of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in this conference is a great encouragement to us. The voice of the delegation of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam is the voice of all the Vietnamese people, so we fully support our delegation."

    Tran Duc Thinh, Secretary-General of the Vietnam National Farmers Liaison Committee, said: "The just struggle of the Vietnamese people has won the sympathy and support of peace-loving people all over the world. Vietnamese farmers welcome the Geneva Conference and hope that the conference will help resolve the Indochina issue on the basis of recognizing the true freedom and independence of the people of Vietnam, Cambodia and Laos. Vietnamese farmers fully support the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam attending the Geneva Conference. The delegation is attending the conference with the most sincere desire of the Vietnamese people for peace and their determination to fight for true national independence, democracy and freedom."

### 越南著名人士发表谈话欢迎日内瓦会议  全力支持越南民主共和国代表团

1954-05-11
第4版()
专栏：

    越南著名人士发表谈话欢迎日内瓦会议
    全力支持越南民主共和国代表团
    【新华社十日讯】据越南通讯社九日讯：越南著名人士发表谈话，支持日内瓦会议和越南民主共和国代表团。
    越南国民联合战线全国委员会委员诗山说：我们欢迎日内瓦会议。越南民主共和国政府代表团参加这个会议，给予我们很大的鼓舞。越南民主共和国政府代表团的声音就是全越南人民的声音，因此我们全力支持我们的代表团。
    越南全国农民联络委员会总书记陈德盛说：越南人民的正义斗争已经赢得全世界爱好和平人民的同情和支持。越南农民欢迎日内瓦会议，希望会议将有助于在承认越南、高棉与寮国三国人民的真正自由与独立的基础上使印度支那问题获得解决。越南农民全力支持出席日内瓦会议的越南民主共和国代表团。代表团是抱着越南人民要求和平的最诚挚的愿望与他们为真正的民族独立、民主与自由而斗争的决心去参加会议的。
