Title: New Barley from East China Marketed
Author: Cheng Hua
Date: 1954-05-18
Page: 2

New Barley from East China Marketed

New barley has been marketed in Lianjiang County of Fujian Province, Lanxi, Yiwu, and Longyou Counties of Zhejiang Province, Dangtu, Liu'an and other counties of Anhui Province, and Gaochun County of Jiangsu Province in the East China region. This year, the barley in East China is growing well, and the general yield has increased compared to last year. In Jinhua Prefecture, the main barley producing area of Zhejiang Province, more than one million mu of barley were planted this year, and the total output of the region is estimated to increase by 10% compared to last year. Grain departments and cooperatives in various parts of East China have promptly set up grain purchasing stations, announced price lists, and launched the purchase of new barley. Grain departments and supply and marketing cooperatives in Zhejiang Province are preparing to establish more than 1,700 purchasing stations throughout the province.
(Cheng Hua)

### 华东区新大麦上市
程华
1954-05-18
第2版()
专栏：

    华东区新大麦上市
    华东区福建省连江县，浙江省兰溪、义乌、龙游等县，安徽省当涂、六安等县和江苏省高淳县等地，新大麦已经上市。今年华东区大麦生长情况良好，一般产量均较去年有所提高。浙江省主要大麦产地——金华专区，今年共种植大麦一百多万亩，全区产量估计可比去年增产一成。华东区各地粮食部门和合作社都及时地设置了购粮站，公布了牌价，展开新大麦收购工作。浙江省各地粮食部门和供销合作社即准备在全省设立收购站一千七百多个。　　　　　　　　　　　　
　（程华）
