Title: Sino-Soviet Agreement Signed on Distribution of Soviet Films in China
Author:
Date: 1954-05-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 29th: To strengthen film exchange between China and the Soviet Union, the Soviet Film Export Company and the China Film Distribution Company signed the "Agreement on Distribution of Soviet Films in China" on May 29th.

    Signing on behalf of the Soviet side were Sergei Yevgenyev, Deputy Manager and General Representative of the Soviet Film Export Company, and Gribnev, Deputy Representative of the Soviet Film Export Company's Representative Office in China. Signing on behalf of the Chinese side were Luo Guangda, Manager of the China Film Distribution Company, and Yang Shaoren, Deputy Manager. Both sides expressed congratulations on the signing of this agreement.

    Attending the signing ceremony were: Safonov, First Secretary of the Soviet Embassy in China, Makarov, Deputy Representative of the Commercial Representative Office, and Sokolov, Representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in China; Wang Lanxi, Acting Director of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, Wang Shuzhuang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Culture, Wu Qing, Specialist of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Zou Difan, Chief of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Government Administration Council's Committee on Cultural and Educational Affairs, Gao Ge, Director of the Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio, and others.

### 中苏签订苏联影片在中国发行合同

1954-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏签订苏联影片在中国发行合同
    【新华社二十九日讯】苏联影片输出公司和中国电影发行公司为加强中苏两国的电影交流，在五月二十九日签订了“苏联影片在中国发行合同”。
    代表苏联方面签字的是苏联影片输出公司副经理总代表谢尔盖也夫，苏联影片输出公司驻华总代表处副代表格里布涅夫；代表中国方面签字的是中国电影发行公司经理罗光达，副经理杨少任。双方都为这一合同的签订表示祝贺。
    参加签订仪式的有：苏联驻华大使馆一等秘书萨福隆，商务代表处副代表马卡罗和苏联对外文化协会驻华代表索柯洛夫；我中央人民政府文化部电影局代理局长王阑西，文化部办公厅副主任王书庄，外交部苏联东欧司专员吴青，政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局处长邹荻帆，中央新闻纪录电影制片厂厂长高戈等。
