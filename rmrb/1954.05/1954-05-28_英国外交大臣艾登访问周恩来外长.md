Title: British Foreign Secretary Eden Visits Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1954-05-28
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 27th: British Foreign Secretary Mr. Eden visited Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China at 10:30 AM on the 27th for a half-hour meeting.

### 英国外交大臣艾登访问周恩来外长

1954-05-28
第4版()
专栏：

    英国外交大臣艾登访问周恩来外长
    【新华社日内瓦二十七日电】英国外交大臣艾登先生二十七日上午十时半访问中华人民共和国周恩来外长，会谈半小时。
