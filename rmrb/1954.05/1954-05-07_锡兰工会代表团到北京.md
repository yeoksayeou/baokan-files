Title: Ceylon Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-05-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 6th: The Ceylon Trade Union Federation delegation and the Ceylon Labour Union delegation, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 6th. Song Chuan, member of the Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and Deputy Director of the Working Committee of the Agricultural, Forestry and Water Conservancy Trade Union of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Zhuang Yan, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, were at the airport to welcome them.

The list of the Ceylon Trade Union Federation delegation:

Leader: A. Waidyanatha, member of the Central Council of the Ceylon Trade Union Federation and Chairman of the Ceylon United Cigar Workers' Union;

Member: J.R.K. Silva, member of the Central Council of the Ceylon Trade Union Federation and Vice-Chairman of the Ceylon Toddy Workers' Union;

The list of the Ceylon Labour Union delegation:

Leader: U. Albert Perera, General Secretary of the Ceylon Labour Union;

Member: N.L. Perera, Treasurer of the Ceylon Labour Union.

### 锡兰工会代表团到北京

1954-05-07
第4版()
专栏：

    锡兰工会代表团到北京
    【新华社六日讯】应中华全国总工会邀请前来我国参观、访问的锡兰工会联合会代表团及锡兰劳工联合会代表团，已在六日下午乘飞机到达北京。中华全国总工会执行委员会委员、中华全国总工会农林水利工会工作委员会副主任宋川、中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长庄焰等曾到机场欢迎。
    锡兰工会联合会代表团名单：
    团长  阿·瓦伊德林干  锡兰工会联合会中央理事会理事兼锡兰统一雪茄烟工人工会主席；
    团员  杰·鲁·克·席尔瓦  锡兰工会联合会中央理事会理事兼锡兰椰酒工人工会副主席；
    锡兰劳工联合会代表团名单：
    团长  乌·艾伯特·培里拉  锡兰劳工联合会总书记；
    团员  纳·勒·培里拉  锡兰劳工联合会财务。
