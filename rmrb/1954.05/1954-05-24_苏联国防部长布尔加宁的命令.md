Title: Order of the Soviet Minister of Defense, Bulganin
Author:
Date: 1954-05-24
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 22nd. Soviet newspapers published on the 22nd the order of the Soviet Ministry of Defense. The full text is as follows:

Comrades, soldiers and sergeants of the Army and Navy! Comrades, lieutenants, colonels, and generals of the Army and Navy!

The entire personnel of the Soviet armed forces and the entire Soviet people are celebrating the 300th anniversary of the reunification of Ukraine with Russia. This is a historically significant event and a national holiday for the Ukrainian people, the Russian people, and all the nationalities of the Soviet Union.

On the occasion of this national holiday celebrating the 300th anniversary of the reunification of Ukraine with Russia, I congratulate you.

To commemorate the unbreakable brotherly friendship of the peoples of the Soviet Union, I order: On May 23rd, military parades will be held in Kiev, the capital of the Ukrainian Republic, in Kharkov, Pereyaslav-Khmelnitsky, Rovno, and in the hero cities of Sevastopol and Odessa, each with a 20-gun salute.

On May 30th, a military parade will be held in Moscow, the capital of our motherland, with a 20-gun salute.

Long live our great motherland—the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics!

Long live the brotherly friendship between the peoples of our country—the source of strength and power of the multi-ethnic socialist state!

Long live the Soviet armed forces, which have won many glorious victories and defend the peace and security of our motherland, and may they grow ever stronger!

Long live the Soviet government!

Long live the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Great inspiring and leading force of the Soviet people in the struggle to build communism!

	N.A. Bulganin, Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union, Marshal of the Soviet Union

### 苏联国防部长布尔加宁的命令

1954-05-24
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联国防部长布尔加宁的命令
   【新华社讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十二日讯：苏联各报在二十二日刊载了苏联国防部的命令，全文如下：陆海军战士、士官同志们！陆海军尉官、校官和将军同志们！
    苏联武装部队全体人员和全体苏联人民一同庆祝乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯三百周年，它是具有重大意义的历史事件，是乌克兰人民、俄罗斯人民以及苏联一切民族人民的全国大节日。
    值此庆祝乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯三百周年这一全国节日之际，我向你们祝贺。
    为了纪念苏联各族人民的牢不可破的兄弟友谊，我命令：五月二十三日，在乌克兰共和国首都基辅、在哈尔科夫、佩雷雅斯拉夫—赫米尔尼茨基、罗夫，以及塞瓦斯托波尔和敖德萨这两个英雄城市举行阅兵式，并各鸣放礼炮二十响。
    五月三十日，在我们祖国的首都莫斯科举行阅兵式，并鸣放礼炮二十响。
    我们伟大的祖国——苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟万岁！
    我国各族人民间的兄弟友谊——多民族社会主义国家的力量和威力的泉源万岁！
    获得许多光荣胜利的、捍卫着和平和我们祖国的安全的苏联武装部队万岁，并祝其日益强大！
    苏联政府万岁！
    苏联人民在争取建成共产主义的斗争中的伟大的鼓舞力量和领导力量——苏联共产党万岁！
              苏联国防部部长、苏联元帅  尼·亚·布尔加宁
