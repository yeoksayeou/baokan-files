Title: Zhou Gengsheng, Acting President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Hosts a Banquet to Bid Farewell to the Norwegian People's Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-05-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Zhou Gengsheng, Acting President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, held a banquet at the International Club on the 28th to bid farewell to the Norwegian People's Cultural Delegation, which is about to leave Beijing to return home.

Professor Jessing, head of the Norwegian People's Cultural Delegation, Reverend Føbech, deputy head, and all members of the delegation attended the banquet.

At the beginning of the banquet, Zhou Gengsheng delivered a farewell speech. He said that the visit of the Norwegian People's Cultural Delegation to China will strengthen the cultural exchange between the Chinese and Norwegian people, which will help the people of the two countries maintain the cause of peace. He also wished the delegation a happy and smooth journey home.

Professor Jessing, the head of the delegation, on behalf of all the members, read out their written impressions of their visit to China. They expressed their deep gratitude for the warm welcome they received in various parts of China and said that after returning home, they would definitely convey to their people that the Chinese people are building for peace.

Reverend Føbech, the deputy head of the delegation, and several other members also spoke at the meeting.

The banquet was filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere throughout.

After the meeting, the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe performed wonderful acrobatic shows.

### 中国人民外交学会代会长周鲠生  设宴欢送挪威人民文化代表团

1954-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

    中国人民外交学会代会长周鲠生
    设宴欢送挪威人民文化代表团
    据新华社讯：中国人民外交学会代理会长周鲠生于二十八日在国际俱乐部举行宴会，欢送即将离京返国的挪威人民文化代表团。
    挪威人民文化代表团团长耶辛教授、副团长福贝克牧师及代表团全体团员都参加了宴会。
    宴会开始时，周鲠生致欢送词。他说，通过挪威人民文化代表团来中国访问，将加强中挪两国人民文化的交流，这将有助于两国人民维护和平事业的。他并祝代表团在返国旅途中快乐、顺利。
    代表团团长耶辛代表全体团员在会上宣读了他们访问中国的书面观感。他们对在中国各地受到热烈欢迎表示深切感谢；并表示在回国后一定向本国人民传达：中国人民是在为和平而建设。
    代表团副团长福贝克和其他几位团员也在会上讲了话。
    宴会始终充满热烈友好气氛。
    会后，中国杂技团表演了精采的杂技节目。
