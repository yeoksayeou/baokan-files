Title: Molotov Receives Deputy Prime Minister Pham Van Dong

Author:
Date: 1954-05-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 6th. Soviet Foreign Minister V.M. Molotov hosted a banquet on the 6th in honor of Pham Van Dong, head of the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Geneva Conference and Deputy Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Nam Il, and other members of the delegations of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the People's Republic of China attended the banquet.

### 莫洛托夫外长招待范文同副总理

1954-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫外长招待范文同副总理
    【新华社日内瓦六日电】苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫六日设宴招待出席日内瓦会议的越南民主共和国代表团团长、越南民主共和国副总理范文同。
    出席宴会的有中华人民共和国政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务相南日，以及越南民主共和国和中华人民共和国代表团其他团员。
