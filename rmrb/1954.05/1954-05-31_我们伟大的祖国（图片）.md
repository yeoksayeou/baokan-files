Author:
Date: 1954-05-31

    Our Great Motherland
    Young Pioneers of the Geng Changsuo Agricultural Production Cooperative in Wu Gong Village, Raoyang County, Hebei Province, often visit the Wu Gong Village Tractor Station, hoping to learn how to drive tractors when they grow up. This is Wang Youquan, the deputy head of the tractor station's mechanical department, explaining the basics of driving a tractor to them.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    河北省饶阳县五公村耿长锁农业生产合作社的少年先锋队员，经常到五公村拖拉机站去参观，希望长大后也能学会开拖拉机。这是拖拉机站机务副站长王友泉，正在向他们讲解驾驶拖拉机的常识。
