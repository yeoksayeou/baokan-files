Title: Four Indonesian Student Representatives Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-05-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency report:
Four Indonesian student representatives, invited by the All-China Students' Federation to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by train on the evening of the 4th. The four representatives are: Suroso (Secretary-General of the Indonesian National University Students Association), Jawadi Hadi Pradoko Syukur (Organization Minister of the Indonesian National University Students Association), Effendi Lubis (Secretary-General of the Student Union of Gadjah Mada University), and Sasama Sutikyo (Chairman of the Central Council of the All-Indonesia Teachers' College Students Association).

### 印尼学生代表四人到京

1954-05-07
第4版()
专栏：

    印尼学生代表四人到京
    据新华社讯：应中华全国学生联合会邀请前来我国访问的四位印尼学生代表已在四日晚乘火车到北京。四位代表是：苏洛莫（印尼民族大学生协会秘书长）、雅瓦迪·哈迪普拉多科·修库尔（印尼民族大学生协会组织部长）、埃芬迪·卢比斯（印尼国立加查马达大学学生会秘书长）、萨萨马·苏蒂克约（全印尼师范学校学生协会中央理事会主席）。
