Author:
Date: 1954-05-13

    Our Great Motherland
    Local governments are vigorously helping well-established perennial mutual aid teams to transition into agricultural production cooperatives. The Fifth Production Cooperative, newly formed by perennial mutual aid teams in Yingchengzi District, a suburb of Lüda City, received a loan from the government and built a new electric-powered well. This is a scene of the electric well irrigating the vegetable fields.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    各地政府大力帮助条件较好的常年互助组转入农业生产合作社。旅大市郊区营城子区新由常年互助组组成的第五生产合作社，得到了政府的贷款，新建了一座电力水井。这是电力水井向菜地灌溉的情形。
