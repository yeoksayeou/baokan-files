Title: Korean Delegation Visits and Tours East China and Other Regions

Author:

Date: 1954-05-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency:

    The Korean People's Delegation to China and the accompanying art troupe, after participating in the "May Day" celebrations in Beijing and conducting visits, tours, performances, and other activities, departed Beijing by train on the morning of the 7th, accompanied by Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Xu Zhe, Counselor of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, to visit and perform in East China, Central South, and Northeast regions.

    More than 500 people went to the station to see them off, including Liao Chengzhi, Qi Yanming, Ding Xilin, Liu GuanYi, Yu Xinqing, He Ying, Xu Ying, Chai Zemin, Li Chao, and others.

    Choi Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, and embassy staff also went to the station to see them off.

    During their stay in Beijing, the Korean People's Delegation to China and the accompanying art troupe, in addition to participating in the "May Day" celebrations, visited and toured many schools, cultural and educational institutions, and farms. On the 6th, they continued to visit and tour Peking University, Beijing Institute of Iron and Steel Technology, the Institute of Entomology and the Institute of Botany of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, the Central Institute of Hygiene, People's Publishing House, the Photography Department of the Xinhua News Agency, the New China Opera Costume Factory, and toured the Forbidden City and the Western Suburbs Park.

### 朝鲜访华团赴华东等地参观

1954-05-08
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜访华团赴华东等地参观
    据新华社讯：朝鲜人民访华代表团和随行艺术团，在北京参加了“五一”节观礼和进行访问、参观、演出等活动后，已在中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使馆参赞徐哲的陪同下，七日上午乘火车离京，赴华东、中南以及东北各地进行访问、演出。
    前往车站欢送的有：廖承志、齐燕铭、丁西林、刘贯一、余心清、何英、徐英、柴泽民、李超等五百多人。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使崔一和使馆人员也到车站欢送。
    朝鲜人民访华代表团和随行艺术团在京期间，除参加了“五一”节观礼外，曾访问和参观了很多学校、文教机关和农场等。六日，他们又继续访问和参观了北京大学、北京钢铁工业学院、中国科学院昆虫研究所及植物研究所、中央卫生研究院、人民出版社、新华通讯社摄影部、新中国戏装工厂，和游览了故宫、西郊公园等处。
