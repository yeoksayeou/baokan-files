Title: Molotov and Dulles Hold Talks on Atomic Energy Issues
Author:
Date: 1954-05-03
Page: 4

    Molotov and Dulles
    Hold Talks on Atomic Energy Issues
   [Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 2nd] TASS, Geneva, 1st: V.M. Molotov and Mr. Dulles held another meeting on atomic energy issues on May 1st. They discussed the Soviet government's April 27th response to the U.S. government's March 19th, 1954 memorandum. The U.S. government in Washington will further study this response.

### 莫洛托夫和杜勒斯  举行关于原子能问题的会谈

1954-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫和杜勒斯
    举行关于原子能问题的会谈
   【新华社二日讯】塔斯社日内瓦一日讯：维·米·莫洛托夫和杜勒斯先生五月一日又举行了一次关于原子能问题的会谈。他们讨论了苏联政府四月二十七日对美国政府一九五四年三月十九日的备忘录的复文。华盛顿的美国政府将进一步研究这个复文。
