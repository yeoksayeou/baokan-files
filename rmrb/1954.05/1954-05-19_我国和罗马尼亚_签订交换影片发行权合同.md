Title: China and Romania Sign Contract on Exchange of Film Distribution Rights
Author:
Date: 1954-05-19
Page: 1

    China and Romania
    Sign Contract on Exchange of Film Distribution Rights
   【Xinhua News Agency, 17th】In order to further strengthen film exchanges between China and Romania, and in accordance with the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, the Romanian People's Republic Film Distribution Administration and the China Film Distribution Company signed a contract on the exchange of film distribution rights between China and Romania and a contract on the exchange of newsreel footage between China and Romania on the 17th.
    Mr. Meng Tiannu, First Secretary of the Embassy of the Romanian People's Republic in China, signed on behalf of the Romanian side; Mr. Luo Guangda, Manager of the China Film Distribution Company, signed on behalf of the Chinese side. Both parties expressed their congratulations on the signing of these two contracts.
    Those who attended the signing ceremony included Mr. An Ding, Economic Counselor of the Embassy of the Romanian People's Republic in China, Mr. Zhao Feng, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, Mr. Wu Qing, Special Commissioner of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Mr. Zou Difan, Director of the Department of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government, and Mr. Gao Ge, Director of the Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio, etc.

### 我国和罗马尼亚  签订交换影片发行权合同

1954-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我国和罗马尼亚
    签订交换影片发行权合同
   【新华社十七日讯】罗马尼亚人民共和国电影发行管理处与中国电影发行公司为了进一步加强中罗两国电影的交流，并根据中罗文化合作协定，在十七日签订了中罗交换影片发行权合同和中罗交换新闻片素材合同。
    代表罗方签字的是罗马尼亚人民共和国驻华大使馆一等秘书孟天努；代表我方签字的是中国电影发行公司经理罗光达。双方都为这两个合同的签订表示了祝贺。
    参加签字仪式的有罗马尼亚人民共和国驻华大使馆经济参赞安定和我中央人民政府文化部办公厅主任赵沨，外交部苏联东欧司专员吴青，中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局处长邹荻帆，中央新闻纪录电影制片厂厂长高戈等。
