Title: Molotov Visits Chairman of the Swiss Federal Government
Author:
Date: 1954-05-30
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 29th) TASS, Geneva, May 28th: On May 28th, Soviet Foreign Minister V.M. Molotov visited R. Rubattel, Chairman of the Swiss Federal Government, in Bern. Rubattel and Molotov held talks. Present at the talks were M. Petitpierre, Minister of the Swiss Political Department (i.e., Ministry of Foreign Affairs) and member of the Federal Political Council, and F.F. Molochkov, Soviet Envoy to Switzerland.

At 1 p.m., Chairman of the Swiss Federal Government, R. Rubattel, hosted a banquet in honor of V.M. Molotov.

Attending the banquet were M. Petitpierre, F. Etter, and G. Streuli, members of the Swiss Federal Political Council, E. Zhender, Director of Political Affairs of the Swiss Federal Political Department, R. Moris, Head of Protocol of the Political Department, and other personnel.

On the Soviet side, V.V. Kuznetsov, F.F. Molochkov, L.F. Ilyichev, A.A. Lavrishev, and A.I. Kulepov, Soviet Trade Representative to Switzerland, attended the banquet.

Chairman Rubattel and V.M. Molotov both spoke at the banquet.

On the morning of May 28th, V.M. Molotov also met and held talks with M. Petitpierre, Minister of the Swiss Political Department, in Bern.

### 莫洛托夫访瑞士联邦政府主席

1954-05-30
第4版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫访瑞士联邦政府主席
     【新华社二十九日讯】塔斯社日内瓦二十八日讯：五月二十八日，苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫到伯尔尼访问了瑞士联邦政府主席鲁·陆巴特尔。陆巴特尔和莫洛托夫进行了谈话。谈话时在座的还有：瑞士政治部（即外交部）部长、联邦政务委员会委员马·彼蒂彼爱和苏联驻瑞士公使费·弗·莫洛奇科夫。
    下午一时，瑞士联邦政府主席鲁·陆巴特尔设宴招待维·米·莫洛托夫。
    参加宴会的有：瑞士联邦政务委员会委员马·彼蒂彼爱、菲·埃特、格·斯特勒利，瑞士联邦政府政治部政务司司长艾·琴德尔，政治部礼宾处处长罗·莫里斯和其他人员。
    苏联方面参加宴会的有：瓦·瓦·库兹涅佐夫、费·弗·莫洛奇科夫、勒·弗·伊利切夫、阿·阿·拉夫里谢夫、苏联驻瑞士贸易代表阿·伊·库列波夫。
    陆巴特尔主席和维·米·莫洛托夫都在宴会上讲了话。
    五月二十八日上午，维·米·莫洛托夫也在伯尔尼和瑞士政治部部长马·彼蒂彼爱晤谈。
