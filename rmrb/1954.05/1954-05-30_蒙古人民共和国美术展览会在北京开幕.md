Title: Mongolian People's Republic Art Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-05-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 29th: The "Mongolian People's Republic Art Exhibition," sponsored by the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government's State Council, according to the 1954 Sino-Mongolian Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan, held its opening ceremony on the 29th at the Zhongshan Hall in Zhongshan Park.

    Those attending the opening ceremony included: Lu Dingyi, Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government's State Council; Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Ding Xilin and Zheng Zhenduo, Deputy Ministers of Culture; Sa Kongliao, Deputy Director of the General Administration of Publications; Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the State Council; Chen Zhongjing, Deputy Director; Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; He Ying, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department; and Fu Hao, Commissioner of the Asian Department.

    Liu Kaiqu and Wu Zuoren, Vice Chairmen of the Chinese Artists Association, artists Hua Junwu and Gu Yuan, Zhou Libo, head of the former Chinese literary and art delegation that visited the Mongolian People's Republic, and writers Zhao Shuli and Chen Baichen, and poet Ai Qing also attended the opening ceremony.

    Also attending the opening ceremony were: Ochirbat, Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China; Soviet and various People's Democratic countries' envoys to China; the Ambassador of the Republic of India to China; officials from the Indonesian, Pakistani, and other countries' embassies in China, and Mongolian artists led by Dorjiin Choidok, who came to China to visit and assist in the preparation of the exhibition.

    Deputy Director Lu Dingyi delivered an opening speech at the opening ceremony. He said that this exhibition will not only be warmly welcomed by the Chinese people and help the Chinese people further understand the Mongolian people, but also provide Chinese artists with a good opportunity to observe and learn, thereby contributing to the development of China's art work. He also congratulated the successful exhibition of the Mongolian People's Republic Art Exhibition in China.

    Ambassador Ochirbat said in his speech: The purpose of holding this exhibition is to introduce the paintings of the Mongolian people to the Chinese people and Chinese artists, and to obtain suggestions and criticisms from art workers and all audiences; the exhibition will certainly make some contributions to the mutual exchange of experience and the development of cultural cooperation between the people of Mongolia and China.

    Ambassador Ochirbat then cut the ribbon for the opening of the exhibition.

    The exhibition displays nearly 300 works of art, including oil paintings, sculptures, ethnic patterns, stage design drawings, secular paintings, landscape paintings, and works by students of Mongolian art schools.

### 蒙古人民共和国美术展览会在北京开幕

1954-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民共和国美术展览会在北京开幕
     【新华社二十九日讯】根据中蒙一九五四年文化合作执行计划，中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局主办的“蒙古人民共和国美术展览会”二十九日在中山公园中山堂举行揭幕仪式。
    参加揭幕仪式的有：中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会副主任陆定一，外交部副部长章汉夫，文化部副部长丁西林、郑振铎，出版总署副署长萨空了，政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深、副局长陈忠经，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗以及中央人民政府外交部亚洲司副司长何英、交际处副处长王拓、亚洲司专员符浩等。
    中国美术家协会副主席刘开渠、吴作人，美术家华君武、古元，曾去过蒙古人民共和国访问的前中国文艺代表团团长周立波以及作家赵树理、陈白尘，诗人艾青等也参加了揭幕仪式。
    参加揭幕仪式的还有：蒙古人民共和国驻我国大使奥其尔巴特，苏联和各人民民主国家驻我国使节，印度共和国驻我国大使，印度尼西亚、巴基斯坦等国驻我国大使馆的官员以及以多吉因·却多克为首的前来我国访问并协助筹备此次展览会的蒙古美术家等。
    陆定一副主任在揭幕仪式上致开幕词。他说，这个展览会不仅将得到中国人民的热烈欢迎，有助于中国人民对蒙古人民的进一步了解，而且将使中国的美术家们得到很好的观摩和学习的机会，从而有助于我国美术工作的发展。他并祝贺蒙古人民共和国美术展览会在中国的展出获得成功。
    奥其尔巴特大使致词说：这个展览会举办的目的，是向中国人民及中国艺术家们介绍蒙古人民的绘画作品，并借以取得美术工作者及所有观众的建议和批评；展览会一定会对蒙中两国人民之间的相互交流经验及发展文化合作的事业作出一些贡献。
    展览会继由奥其尔巴特大使剪彩。
    展览会上展出了油画、雕塑、民族图案、舞台设计图、世俗画、风景画以及蒙古的美术学校学生们的习作等近三百件美术作品。
