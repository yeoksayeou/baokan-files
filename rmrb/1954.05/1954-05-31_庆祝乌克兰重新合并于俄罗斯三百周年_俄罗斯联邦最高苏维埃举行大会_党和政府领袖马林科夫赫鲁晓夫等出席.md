Title: Celebrating the 300th Anniversary of Ukraine's Reunification with Russia The Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation Holds a Grand Meeting Party and Government Leaders Malenkov, Khrushchev, and Others Attend
Author: 
Date: 1954-05-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 30th dispatch: According to TASS, the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation held a grand meeting in the Kremlin, Moscow, on the 29th to celebrate the 300th anniversary of Ukraine's reunification with Russia. Among those attending the meeting were representatives of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation, delegations from the Ukrainian Republic and other Soviet republics, a delegation from the Polish Parliament, workers from Moscow factories, prominent scientists, writers, and artists, as well as members of foreign diplomatic missions in the Soviet Union.

Malenkov, Khrushchev, Bulganin, Mikoyan, Pervukhin, Shvernik, Suslov, and Pospelov were seated on the government platform, greeted by a storm of sustained applause.

The meeting was presided over by Solovyov, Chairman of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation.

At the meeting, a decree from the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union was read out, announcing the awarding of the Order of Lenin to the Russian Federation in recognition of the outstanding contributions of the Russian people and the various ethnic groups of the Russian Federation to the economic and cultural development of the country.

Puzanov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Russian Federation, delivered a report at the meeting. He recounted the historical circumstances of Ukraine's reunification with Russia and the great significance of this event for the development of the Russian and Ukrainian peoples in all aspects. He also spoke of the common struggle of the Ukrainian and Russian peoples for the victory of the October Revolution, their concerted creative efforts in communist construction, and the economic and cultural achievements of Ukraine and the Russian Federation. He concluded by saying that no force in the world could shake the alliance of the Russian and Ukrainian peoples, and the alliance of all nationalities on the free Soviet land. His words were met with sustained applause from the entire audience.

The head of the Ukrainian Republic delegation, A.I. Kirichenko, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Ukrainian Communist Party, the heads of the delegations of other Soviet republics, and the head of the Polish parliamentary delegation delivered congratulatory speeches at the meeting.

In a very warm atmosphere, the meeting adopted telegrams of tribute to the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, as well as a congratulatory telegram to the Ukrainian people.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 30th dispatch: According to TASS, an artistic performance was held on the 29th at the Bolshoi Theatre of the Soviet Union in Moscow to entertain the participants of the celebration meeting of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation marking the 300th anniversary of Ukraine's reunification with Russia. Many wonderful programs were performed at the meeting, including Russian, Ukrainian, and Belarusian folk songs and dances, as well as works by classical and modern composers.

Malenkov, Khrushchev, Bulganin, Mikoyan, Pervukhin, Shvernik, Kirichenko, Suslov, Pospelov, and others attended the performance.

### 庆祝乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯三百周年  俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃举行大会  党和政府领袖马林科夫赫鲁晓夫等出席

1954-05-31
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯三百周年
    俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃举行大会
    党和政府领袖马林科夫赫鲁晓夫等出席
   【新华社三十日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃二十九日在莫斯科的克里姆林宫举行大会，庆祝乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯三百周年。参加大会的有：俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃的代表们、乌克兰共和国及苏联其他加盟共和国代表团、波兰议会代表团、莫斯科工厂的工人、杰出的科学家、作家和艺术家，以及各国驻苏联外交使团人员。
    马林科夫、赫鲁晓夫、布尔加宁、米高扬、别尔乌辛、什维尔尼克、苏斯洛夫、波斯伯洛夫在政府席上就座，全场响起了暴风般的、经久不息的掌声。
    大会由俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃主席索洛维耶夫主持。
    会上宣读了苏联最高苏维埃主席团的命令，宣布以列宁勋章授予俄罗斯联邦，以表彰俄罗斯人民和俄罗斯联邦各族人民对国家经济和文化发展的杰出贡献。
    俄罗斯联邦部长会议主席普扎诺夫在会上作了报告。他叙述了乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯的历史情况，以及这一事件对于俄罗斯人民和乌克兰人民在各方面发展的重大意义。他还谈到了乌克兰人民和俄罗斯人民争取十月革命的胜利的共同斗争，他们在共产主义建设中的同心协力的创造性努力，以及乌克兰和俄罗斯联邦的经济和文化成就。他最后说，世界上没有一种力量能够动摇俄罗斯人民和乌克兰人民的联盟，以及自由的苏维埃国土上各族人民的联盟。他的话博得了全场经久不息的掌声。
    乌克兰共和国代表团团长、乌克兰共产党中央委员会第一书记阿·伊·基里钦科，苏联其他加盟共和国代表团团长和波兰议会代表团团长相继在会上致了贺辞。
    大会在非常热烈的气氛中通过了向苏联最高苏维埃主席团、苏联部长会议和苏联共产党中央委员会致敬的电文，以及给乌克兰人民的贺电。
   【新华社三十日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：二十九日，在莫斯科的苏联大剧院举行了艺术表演会，招待俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃举行的乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯三百周年庆祝大会的参加者。会上表演了许多精采的节目，其中有俄罗斯、乌克兰和白俄罗斯的民族歌曲和舞蹈，以及古典作曲家和现代作曲家的作品。
    马林科夫、赫鲁晓夫、布尔加宁、米高扬、别尔乌辛、什维尔尼克、基里钦科、苏斯洛夫、波斯伯洛夫等出席了表演会。
