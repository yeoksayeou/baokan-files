Title: Nam Il Hosts Banquet in Honor of Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1954-05-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 7th: Foreign Minister Nam Il, head of the delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 7th in honor of Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai, head of the delegation of the People's Republic of China.

    The Chinese guests included: Zhang Wentian, Wang Jiaxiang, Li Kenong, Wang Bingnan, Qiao Guanhua, Chen Jiakang, Huang Hua, and Wang Zhuoru.
  Korean guests attending the banquet also included: Bai Nan-un, Ki Sok-bok, Chang Chun-san, Tian Donghe, Kim Taek-yong, Kim Myong-gui, and Pang Seung-jik.
    The banquet was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 南日外务相设宴招待周恩来外长

1954-05-09
第1版()
专栏：

    南日外务相设宴招待周恩来外长
    【新华社日内瓦七日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国代表团团长南日外务相七日晚设宴招待中华人民共和国代表团团长周恩来外长。
    中国方面应邀参加的有：张闻天、王稼祥、李克农、王炳南、乔冠华、陈家康、黄华、王倬如。
  朝鲜方面参加宴会的还有：白南云、奇石福、张春山、田东赫、金泽泳、金明龟、方承直。
    宴会在热烈友好空气中进行。
