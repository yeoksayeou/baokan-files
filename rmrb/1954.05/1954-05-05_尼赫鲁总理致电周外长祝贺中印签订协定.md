Title: Prime Minister Nehru Sends Telegram to Foreign Minister Zhou Congratulating on the Signing of the Sino-Indian Agreement
Author:
Date: 1954-05-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 4th: Indian Prime Minister Nehru sent a telegram to Zhou Enlai, Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China, to congratulate on the recent signing of the agreement between China and India on trade and transportation issues between the Tibet region of China and India. The telegram is as follows:
To His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China:

Thank you for your telegram that I received in Colombo.

I am very pleased that our two countries have signed an agreement on trade and transportation issues between India and the Tibet region of China. This agreement, based on the principles of mutual respect for territorial sovereignty, mutual non-aggression, mutual non-interference in internal affairs, equality and mutual benefit, and peaceful coexistence, strengthens and consolidates the friendship between the peoples of China and India. I would like to take this opportunity to congratulate you and, through you, to extend my greetings to the Chinese government and the Chinese people.

Nehru, May 3, 1954

### 尼赫鲁总理致电周外长祝贺中印签订协定

1954-05-05
第4版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁总理致电周外长祝贺中印签订协定
    【新华社四日讯】印度共和国总理尼赫鲁为最近中印两国就中国西藏地方与印度的通商和交通问题签订协定，致电我国中央人民政府外交部周恩来部长祝贺。电文如下：中华人民共和国外交部部长周恩来阁下：
    我在科伦坡收到您的来电，谨此致谢。
    我很高兴，我们两国业就印度和中国西藏地方间的通商和交通问题签订协定。此一基于互相尊重领土主权，互不侵犯，互不干涉内政，平等互惠，和平共处的原则而缔结的协定加强并巩固了中印两国人民的友谊。我极愿借此机会向您祝贺，并通过您向中国政府和中国人民致意。
    尼赫鲁　一九五四年五月三日
