Title: Preparatory Meeting of the Nine-Nation Representatives on the Indochina Issue
Author:
Date: 1954-05-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 28th: Our special correspondent reports: The representatives of the nine-nation delegations discussing the Indochina issue held a preparatory meeting on the 28th. The participants in the meeting included: Marquess of Reading (United Kingdom), Chauvel (France), Phleger (United States), Gromyko (Soviet Union), Zhang Wentian (People's Republic of China), Chen Gongxiang (Democratic Republic of Vietnam), as well as representatives of Bao Dai Vietnam, the Kingdom of Cambodia, and the Kingdom of Laos. According to information obtained here, the meeting exchanged views on the proposal put forward at the previous restricted meeting of the nine-nation delegations regarding direct contact between representatives of the commanders of the warring parties in Indochina.

### 讨论印度支那问题的九国代表开准备会议

1954-05-30
第4版()
专栏：

    讨论印度支那问题的九国代表开准备会议
    【新华社日内瓦二十八日电】本社特派记者报道：讨论印度支那问题的九国代表团的代表二十八日举行准备会议。参加会议的有：里丁侯爵（英国）、萧维尔（法国）、菲莱格（美国）、葛罗米柯（苏联）、张闻天（中华人民共和国）、陈公祥（越南民主共和国）以及保大越南、高棉王国和寮国王国的代表。据此间获得的消息说，会议对于过去九国代表团限制性的会议上所提出的关于印度支那交战双方司令官的代表的直接接触问题的提案，交换了意见。
