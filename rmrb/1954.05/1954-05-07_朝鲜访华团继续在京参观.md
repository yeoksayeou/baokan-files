Title: Korean Delegation Continues Visit in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-05-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency:

    The Korean People's Delegation and accompanying art troupe continued their visits and tours in the capital on the 5th, giving reports and performances to various circles.
    On the morning of the 5th, the delegation visited the Renmin University of China, Beijing Agricultural University, the Institute of Applied Physics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and toured the Temple of Azure Clouds and the Temple of the Reclining Buddha. In the afternoon of the 5th, Kim Ung-gi, head of the delegation, Lee Dong-yeong, deputy head, and some members of the delegation visited the Central Institute for Nationalities.
    On the evening of the 5th, the delegation gave a report to more than 5,000 people, including representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, representatives of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Korean residents in Beijing, and people from all walks of life in Beijing. On the same evening, the Korean National Art Theater and the Korean National Classical Art Theater held grand soirees to entertain representatives from various countries invited to China to attend the National Day celebrations, as well as people from the literary and art circles in the capital.

### 朝鲜访华团继续在京参观

1954-05-07
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜访华团继续在京参观
    据新华社讯：朝鲜人民访华代表团和随行艺术团，五日继续在首都进行访问、参观，并向各界作报告和演出。
    代表团在五日上午分别参观了中国人民大学、北京农业大学、中国科学院应用物理研究所，和游览了碧云寺、卧佛寺等处。五日下午，代表团团长金应基、副团长李东英及部分团员前往中央民族学院参观。
    代表团在五日晚上，向中国人民志愿军观礼代表、中国人民解放军观礼代表、在京朝鲜侨民以及北京市各界人民五千多人作了报告。在同日晚上，朝鲜国立艺术剧场和朝鲜国立古典艺术剧场举行盛大晚会，招待各国应邀来我国观礼的代表及首都文艺界人士。
