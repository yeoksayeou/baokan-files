Title: Vietnamese People's Army Captures French Stronghold Two Kilometers Southeast of Phủ Lý; Lao People's Liberation Army Defeats Three Enemy Companies; Cambodian People's Liberation Army Destroys Two Enemy Strongholds

Author:
Date: 1954-05-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: According to the Vietnam News Agency on the 15th: On the evening of the 12th, the Vietnamese People's Army captured a French stronghold located two kilometers southeast of Phủ Lý city. The stronghold was defended by the headquarters and two companies of the 2nd Battalion of the 6th Moroccan Regiment. According to initial reports, the People's Army annihilated most of the enemy troops defending the stronghold.

Xinhua News Agency, 15th: According to the Vietnam News Agency on the 14th: While the Vietnamese People's Army completely eliminated the French Điện Biên Phủ Group stronghold on the 7th, the People's Armed Forces in Hà Nam Province, North Vietnam, launched an attack on enemy troops stationed in Phủ Lý city, more than 50 kilometers south of Hanoi, killing and wounding 65 enemy soldiers. On the morning of the 8th, the People's Armed Forces also eliminated an enemy stronghold seven kilometers south of Phủ Lý city, killing and capturing 66 enemy soldiers. The People's Armed Forces seized 50 machine guns and rifles among other spoils of war.

Xinhua News Agency, 17th: According to a report from the Vietnam News Agency: The Lao Resistance Government issued a communiqué on the 16th stating that on May 8th, the Lao People's Liberation Army defeated three enemy companies at Kung Keu, 20 kilometers south of Muong Khoua in Upper Laos, killing and capturing Captain Mair Milard, Lieutenant Badois, and Second Lieutenant Petrard, the commanders of the puppet army's 4th Battalion, among others.

The communiqué stated that these enemy troops belonged to three battalions dispatched by the enemy from Luang Prabang in Upper Laos. These three battalions were sent to occupy Muong Khoua, about 50 kilometers southwest of Điện Biên Phủ, in order to rescue the French troops then besieged at Điện Biên Phủ. After the Vietnamese People's Army completely annihilated the enemy at Điện Biên Phủ, these three enemy battalions hastily retreated towards Luang Prabang on May 8th and were intercepted by the Lao People's Liberation Army in the Muong Khoua area.

Xinhua News Agency, 17th: According to a report from the Vietnam News Agency: The Cambodian Resistance Government issued a communiqué on the 16th stating that in the ten days up to May 4th, the Cambodian People's Liberation Army eliminated two enemy strongholds each defended by two platoons in the provinces of Svay Rieng and Prey Veng. At the same time, the Cambodian People's Liberation Army also forced the enemy to withdraw from fifteen observation towers along the highways in this area and seized 110 various weapons from the enemy.

### 越南人民军攻下府里东南二公里法军据点  寮国解放军击溃敌军三连、高棉解放军拔除敌军据点两处

1954-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

    越南人民军攻下府里东南二公里法军据点
    寮国解放军击溃敌军三连、高棉解放军拔除敌军据点两处
    【新华社十六日讯】据越南通讯社十五日讯：十二日晚，越南人民军攻下位于府里城东南二公里的一个法军据点。该据点是由第六摩洛哥团第二营的营部和两个连防守的。据初步消息说，人民军歼灭了该据点的大部守敌。
    【新华社十五日讯】据越南通讯社十四日讯：北越河南省的人民武装在七日越南人民军完全拔除法军奠边府集团据点的同时，对驻扎在河内以南五十余公里府里城的敌军发动进攻，毙、伤敌军六十五名。八日晨，人民武装并拔除了位于府里城以南七公里的敌军据点一处，毙、俘敌军六十六名。人民武装缴获的战利品包括机枪和步枪五十挺（支）。
    【新华社十七日讯】据越南通讯社讯：寮国抗战政府十六日发表的公报说：五月八日，寮国人民解放军在上寮芒苛以南二十公里弓剧地方击溃敌军三个连，并击毙和俘掳伪军第四营的指挥官麦尔密拉德上尉、巴多瓦中尉、彼特拉德少尉等人。
    公报说：这些敌军属于被敌人从上寮琅勃剌邦派出的三个营。这三个营被派去占领奠边府西南约五十多公里的芒苛，以便援救当时被围在奠边府的法军。在越南人民军全歼奠边府的敌军后，这三营敌军于五月八日急忙向琅勃剌邦回窜，在芒苛地区遭到寮国人民解放军的截击。
    【新华社十七日讯】据越南通讯社讯：高棉抗战政府十六日发表的公报说，高棉人民解放军在到五月四日为止的十天中，在柴桢和波萝勉两省拔除敌军各以两个排据守的据点两处。同时，高棉人民解放军还迫使敌军撤出这个地区内公路上的了望塔十五处，并缴获敌军各种武器一百一十件。
