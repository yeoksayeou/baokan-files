Title: Trade Union Representatives from Various Countries Visit Different Places
Author:
Date: 1954-05-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 8th: Trade union delegations and representatives from the Soviet Union, North Korea, Vietnam, Mongolia, Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, Romania, Hungary, India, Indonesia, Burma, Ceylon, France, Italy, Israel, Greece, and other countries, who were invited to our country, accompanied by Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, left by train on the evening of the 8th to visit and tour the Northeast, Shanghai, Nanjing, Hangzhou, and other places.

Zhu Xuefan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and heads of various industrial trade unions went to the station to see them off.

In addition: The delegation of the Scottish Mineworkers' Union had already left for the Northeast on the evening of the 5th for a prior visit.

### 各国工会代表到各地参观

1954-05-10
第4版()
专栏：

    各国工会代表到各地参观
    【新华社八日讯】应邀前来我国的苏联、朝鲜、越南、蒙古、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、印度、印尼、缅甸、锡兰、法国、意大利、以色列、希腊等国工会代表团及工会代表，在中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一陪同下，于八日晚乘火车去东北及上海、南京、杭州等地参观、游览。
    中华全国总工会副主席朱学范及各产业工会负责人曾到车站送行。
    又讯：苏格兰矿工工会代表团已在五日晚先行去东北参观。
