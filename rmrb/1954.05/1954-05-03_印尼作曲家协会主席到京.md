Title: Chairman of the Indonesian Composers Association Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-05-03
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: Emil Pasaribu, Chairman of the Indonesian Composers Association, sent by the Government of the Republic of Indonesia to visit and investigate music and opera in our country, arrived in Beijing on April 30. Pasaribu also attended the "May Day" celebrations.

### 印尼作曲家协会主席到京

1954-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

    印尼作曲家协会主席到京
    据新华社讯：印度尼西亚共和国政府派来我国访问并考察音乐、歌剧的印尼作曲家协会主席埃米尔·帕沙里布，已于四月三十日抵京。帕沙里布并参加了“五一”节的观礼。
