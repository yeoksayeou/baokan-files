Title: Wireless Telephone Service Opens Between Lhasa and Qamdo
Author:
Date: 1954-05-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, May 17th: Wireless telephone service between Lhasa and Qamdo opened on May 10th, and communication is good. Long-distance telephone service between Qamdo and Chengdu, Ya'an, and Kangding and Garzê in the Tibetan Autonomous Region of Xikang Province, also opened at the end of April.

### 拉萨和昌都间开放无线电话

1954-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

    拉萨和昌都间开放无线电话
    【新华社重庆十七日电】五月十日，拉萨和昌都之间已开放无线电话，通话情形良好。昌都和成都、雅安及西康省藏族自治区的康定、甘孜等地，也在四月底开放了长途电话。
