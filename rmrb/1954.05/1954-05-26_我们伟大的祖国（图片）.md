Our Great Motherland
Agricultural technology extension stations in various regions are playing a significant role in promoting new agricultural tools and assisting farmers in using new-style agricultural implements. This is a scene from Liujiadian Village in Pingquan County, Rehe Province, where the agricultural technology extension station is demonstrating sowing machine planting to the director and mutual aid team leaders of the village's agricultural producers' cooperative.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    各地农业技术推广站在推广新农具和帮助农民使用新式农具的工作中起着很大的作用。这是热河省平泉县农业技术推广站在刘家店村为该村农业生产合作社主任和互助组组长们表演播种机播种的情形。
