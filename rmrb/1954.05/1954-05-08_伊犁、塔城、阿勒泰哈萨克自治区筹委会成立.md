Title: Preparatory Committee for the Ili, Tacheng, and Altay Kazakh Autonomous Region Established
Author: 
Date: 1954-05-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

    Preparatory Committee for the Ili, Tacheng, and Altay Kazakh Autonomous Region Established
    The Xinjiang Provincial People's Government convened a symposium from April 20th to 21st on the establishment of a preparatory committee for the Kazakh Autonomous Region, equivalent to an administrative office, in the three special districts of Ili, Tacheng, and Altay. At the meeting, the Preparatory Committee for the Ili, Tacheng, and Altay Kazakh Autonomous Region was formally established.  Wang Enmao, First Secretary of the Xinjiang Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Saifuddin, Fourth Secretary, Baorhan, Chairman of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government and Director of the Xinjiang Provincial Preparatory Committee for the Promotion of National Regional Autonomy, Gao Jinchun, Vice Chairman of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government, and others attended the symposium.  Representatives from all ethnic groups and circles from the three special districts of Ili, Tacheng, and Altay, members of the Xinjiang Provincial Preparatory Committee for the Promotion of National Regional Autonomy in Urumqi, and heads of autonomous region preparatory committees equivalent to the county level and above in Xinjiang Province, totaling 120 people, were invited to participate in this symposium.
    The symposium was presided over by Baorhan. At the meeting, Saifuddin made a report on "Uniting the People of All Ethnic Groups in Xinjiang to Strive for the Victorious Implementation of the Party's General Task on Nationalities Issues During the Transition Period," and put forward the proposal to establish a Kazakh Autonomous Region, equivalent to an administrative office, in the three special districts of Ili, Tacheng, and Altay.
    The representatives attending the meeting conducted a heated discussion based on Saifuddin's report, and unanimously agreed that the establishment of the Kazakh Autonomous Region is a major joyous event in the history of the Kazakh people, and that they should use the greatest enthusiasm and strength to do a good job in the preparatory work for the autonomous region, and strive for the early establishment of the autonomous region.
    After full consultation, the meeting unanimously approved the establishment of the Kazakh Autonomous Region Preparatory Committee, with 51 members including Batihan, Li Quan (Han), and Abudureyim Aisa (Uyghur), and formally established the Preparatory Committee for the Kazakh Autonomous Region. Batihan (Kazakh) was elected as Chairman, and Li Quan (Han), Abudureyim Aisa (Uyghur), Jakulin (Kazakh), and Nosufuhan (Kazakh) were elected as Vice Chairmen. The Autonomous Region Preparatory Committee also decided to set up its office in Yining City, an important town in northern Xinjiang Province.

### 伊犁、塔城、阿勒泰哈萨克自治区筹委会成立

1954-05-08
第3版()
专栏：

    伊犁、塔城、阿勒泰哈萨克自治区筹委会成立
    新疆省人民政府四月二十到二十一日召开了关于伊犁、塔城、阿勒泰三个专区建立相当于行署级的哈萨克自治区筹备委员会座谈会，会上正式成立了伊犁、塔城、阿勒泰哈萨克自治区筹备委员会。出席座谈会的有：中共中央新疆分局第一书记王恩茂、第四书记赛福鼎、新疆省人民政府主席、新疆省推行民族区域自治筹委会主任委员鲍尔汉、新疆省人民政府副主席高锦纯等。被邀请参加这次座谈会的有伊犁、塔城、阿勒泰三个专区的各族各界代表和新疆省推行民族区域自治筹备委员会在乌鲁木齐市的委员以及新疆省相当于县级以上自治区筹备委员会的负责人等共一百二十人。
    座谈会由鲍尔汉主持。会上，赛福鼎作了“关于新疆各族人民团结起来，为胜利贯彻国家过渡时期在民族问题方面的总任务而奋斗”的报告，并提出了以伊犁、塔城、阿勒泰三个专区建立相当于行署级的哈萨克自治区的意见。
    到会代表根据赛福鼎的报告进行了热烈讨论，一致认为成立哈萨克自治区，是哈萨克族人民历史上的一件大喜事，应该用最大的热情和力量来做好自治区的筹备工作，争取自治区早日成立。
    会议经过充分协商后，一致通过了以巴提汉、李铨（汉族）、阿不都热衣木艾沙（维吾尔族）等五十一人为自治区筹备委员会委员，正式成立了哈萨克自治区筹备委员会，还推选巴提汉（哈萨克族）为主任委员，李铨（汉族）、阿不都热衣木艾沙（维吾尔族）、贾库林（哈萨克族）、诺素甫汉（哈萨克族）为副主任委员。自治区筹备委员会并决定在新疆省北部重镇——伊宁市办公。（新华社）
