Title: Geneva Conference Holds Restricted Meeting on Indochina Issue
Author:
Date: 1954-05-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 17th: The delegations of the nine countries participating in the discussion of the Indochina issue at the Geneva Conference held a restricted meeting on the afternoon of the 17th and issued the following communique: "The delegations of the nine countries participating in the conference on Indochina held a restricted meeting at 3:00 PM on May 17th. They heard statements regarding the evacuation of the wounded from Dien Bien Phu. The meeting then proceeded to discuss the restoration of peace in Indochina. Another restricted meeting will be held on May 18th."

### 日内瓦会议举行印度支那问题限制性会议

1954-05-19
第4版()
专栏：

    日内瓦会议举行印度支那问题限制性会议
    【新华社日内瓦十七日电】参加日内瓦会议关于印度支那问题讨论的九个国家的代表团十七日下午举行了限制性的会议后发表公报如下：“参加关于印度支那的会议的九个国家的代表团在五月十七日下午三时举行限制性会议。他们听取了关于从奠边府撤出伤员问题的发言。会议然后进行关于在印度支那恢复和平的讨论。在五月十八日将再举行限制性会议。”
