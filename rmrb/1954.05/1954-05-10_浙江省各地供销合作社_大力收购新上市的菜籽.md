Title: Supply and Marketing Cooperatives in Zhejiang Province Vigorously Purchase Newly Marketed Rapeseed

Author: 
Date: 1954-05-10
Page: 2

    Supply and Marketing Cooperatives in Zhejiang Province
    Vigorously Purchase Newly Marketed Rapeseed
    The supply and marketing cooperatives in various areas of the Wenzhou Special District of Zhejiang Province are vigorously organizing the purchase of newly marketed rapeseed. Rapeseed planted by farmers in Zhejiang Province will be fully available on the market in May. This year, the state's rapeseed purchase volume in Zhejiang Province accounts for approximately 75 percent of the province's total rapeseed production this year. The purchasing operations have all been entrusted to the local supply and marketing cooperatives. The supply and marketing cooperatives in various areas will widely establish purchasing points in rural areas for purchasing, while taking into account transportation, processing, and storage conditions. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 浙江省各地供销合作社  大力收购新上市的菜籽

1954-05-10
第2版()
专栏：

    浙江省各地供销合作社
    大力收购新上市的菜籽
    浙江省温州专区各地供销合作社正大力组织收购刚上市的菜籽。浙江省农民种植的菜籽将在五月份全部上市。今年国家在浙江省收购菜籽数量约占全省今年菜籽总产量的百分之七十五。收购业务已全部委托各地供销合作社办理。各地供销合作社将在兼顾运输、加工、仓储等条件下，在农村广泛设立收购点，进行收购。　　（新华社）
