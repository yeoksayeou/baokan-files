Title: German Delegation of the Sino-German Standing Committee for Technical and Technological Scientific Cooperation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-05-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 20th: According to the Sino-German Agreement on Technical and Technological Scientific Cooperation, the German delegation to the first meeting of the Sino-German Standing Committee for Technical and Technological Scientific Cooperation, led by Richard Goschuetz, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 20th.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them were: Wang Daohan, head of the Chinese group of the Sino-German Standing Committee for Technical and Technological Scientific Cooperation and Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building of the Central People's Government; Guan Hantao, member of the Chinese group and Deputy Director of the Technical Cooperation Bureau of the State Planning Commission of the Central People's Government; Chang Yanqing, acting member of the Chinese group and Deputy Director of the Second Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government; as well as representatives from the Ministry of Heavy Industry, the Second Ministry of Machine Building, the Ministry of Construction Engineering, the Ministry of Textile Industry, the Ministry of Agriculture, and the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government.

Also present at the airport to welcome them were Mr. Koenig, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, and embassy staff.

List of the German Delegation to the Sino-German Standing Committee for Technical and Technological Scientific Cooperation:

Head: Richard Goschuetz (Head of the German Group of the Sino-German Committee for Technical and Technological Scientific Cooperation), State Secretary of the Ministry of Heavy Industry.

Members: Fritz Karau (Member of the German Group), Director of the Transportation Vehicle Planning Bureau of the State Planning Commission.

Hans Siegers (Member of the German Group), Director of the Optical Precision Instrument Division of the Ministry of Machine Building.

Walter Mostz (Secretary of the German Group), Staff Member of the Technical Scientific Cooperation Bureau of the State Planning Commission.

Hardt Springer (Expert Advisor to the Delegation), Director of the Heavy Machinery Design Bureau.

Paul Klaus (Expert Advisor to the Delegation), Vice Minister of the Ministry of Light Industry.

Ruebsahm (Expert Advisor to the Delegation), Director of the Research Institute of the Ministry of Agriculture and Forestry.

Margarete Thurn (Technical Assistant, female)

### 中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会德国代表团到京

1954-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

    中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会德国代表团到京
    【新华社二十日讯】根据中德技术与技术科学合作协定，前来北京参加中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会第一届会议的德国代表团，在团长理夏德·戈舒埃茨的率领下，于二十日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有：中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会中国组组长、中央人民政府第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵、中国组委员、中央人民政府国家计划委员会技术合作局副局长管寒涛，中国组代理委员、中央人民政府对外贸易部第二局副局长常彦卿以及中央人民政府重工业部、第二机械工业部、建筑工程部、纺织工业部、农业部、邮电部代表等。
    前往机场欢迎的还有德意志民主共和国驻我国大使柯尼希和使馆人员。
    中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会德国代表团名单
    团长：理夏德·戈舒埃茨（中德技术与技术科学合作委员会德国组组长）重工业部国务秘书。
    团员：弗里茨·卡劳（德国组委员）国家计划委员会运输工具计划局局长。
    汉斯·西格尔斯（德国组委员）机械工业部光学精密仪器处处长。
    华尔特·莫斯特兹（德国组秘书）国家计划委员会技术科学合作局职员。
    哈德·斯普林格（代表团顾问专家）重型机械设计局局长。
    保罗·克劳斯（代表团顾问专家）轻工业部副部长。
    吕柏萨姆（代表团顾问专家）农林部研究院院长。
    玛格丽特·图恩（技术协理、女）
