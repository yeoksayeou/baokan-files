Title: Hungarian Working People's Party Congress Discusses Rákosi Report
Author:
Date: 1954-05-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 26th: According to TASS, the Third Congress of the Hungarian Working People's Party continued on the 25th. The morning session began with a discussion of the report by Comrade Mátyás Rákosi, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Party. The first speaker was László Farkas, Secretary of the Budapest City Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party. He criticized the shortcomings in Budapest's industrial sector and the shortcomings in the leadership of the industrial sector by the Party organizations.

Árpád Kiss, Minister of Light Industry of the Hungarian People's Republic, pointed out in his speech that the staff of the light industry sector had implemented the Party's resolutions and achieved some success in increasing the production of daily necessities. Kiss particularly focused on the issue of training skilled cadres.

At the end of the meeting, Mihály Farkas, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee, made a speech.

### 匈劳动人民党代表大会讨论拉科西报告

1954-05-27
第4版()
专栏：

    匈劳动人民党代表大会讨论拉科西报告
    【新华社二十六日讯】据塔斯社布达佩斯讯：匈牙利劳动人民党第三次代表大会二十五日继续开会。上午会议开始讨论党中央委员会第一书记马加斯·拉科西同志的报告。首先发言的是匈牙利劳动人民党布达佩斯市委书记拉斯洛·法卡斯。他批评了布达佩斯工业部门中的缺点和党组织领导工业部门工作的缺点。
    匈牙利人民共和国轻工业部部长阿帕德·基什在发言中指出，轻工业部门工作人员执行了党的决议，在增加日用品生产方面获得了某些成就。基什特别着重谈到了培养熟练干部的问题。
    会议结束时，中央委员会政治局委员米哈伊·法卡斯作了发言。
