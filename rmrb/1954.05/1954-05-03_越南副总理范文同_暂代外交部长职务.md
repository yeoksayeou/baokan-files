Title: Vietnamese Deputy Prime Minister Pham Van Dong Temporarily Assumes Post of Foreign Minister

Author:

Date: 1954-05-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 1st: Vietnam News Agency reports:
The Prime Minister's Office of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam issued the following communiqué on April 30th:

Following consultations with the Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly, the President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Ho Chi Minh, has decided to appoint Deputy Prime Minister Pham Van Dong to temporarily serve as Foreign Minister during the period of medical treatment of Vietnamese Foreign Minister Hoang Minh Giam.

### 越南副总理范文同  暂代外交部长职务

1954-05-03
第1版()
专栏：

    越南副总理范文同
    暂代外交部长职务
   【新华社一日讯】越南通讯社讯：越南民主共和国总理府于四月三十日发表下列公报：
    越南民主共和国主席胡志明在与国民大会常务委员会主席协商之后，决定在越南外交部长黄明鉴养病期间，任命副总理范文同暂时代理外交部长的职务。
