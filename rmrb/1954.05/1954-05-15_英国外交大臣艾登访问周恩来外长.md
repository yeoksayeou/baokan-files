Title: British Foreign Secretary Eden Visits Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1954-05-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 14th. British Foreign Secretary Eden, chief representative of the British delegation attending the Geneva Conference, visited Zhou Enlai, Foreign Minister and chief representative of the People's Republic of China delegation, at 10:00 a.m. on the 14th for a thirty-minute meeting.

### 英国外交大臣艾登访问周恩来外长

1954-05-15
第1版()
专栏：

    英国外交大臣艾登访问周恩来外长
    【新华社日内瓦十四日电】参加日内瓦会议的英国代表团首席代表艾登外交大臣，在十四日上午十时来访问中华人民共和国代表团首席代表周恩来外长，晤谈三十分钟。
