Title: Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs, Hosts Menon at Banquet
Author:
Date: 1954-05-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 28th. Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, hosted Mr. Menon, the Indian Representative to the United Nations, at a banquet at 7:30 PM on the 28th. Mr. S. Sen, the Indian Consul-General in Geneva, was also present. On the Chinese side, Feng Xuan, Chen Jiakang, Wang Zhuoru, and Wen Pengjiu were present as companions.

### 周恩来外交部长宴请梅农

1954-05-30
第4版()
专栏：

    周恩来外交部长宴请梅农
    【新华社日内瓦二十八日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来二十八日下午七时半宴请印度驻联合国代表梅农。出席的还有印度驻日内瓦总领事斯·塞恩。中国方面出席作陪的有冯铉、陈家康、王倬如、温朋久。
