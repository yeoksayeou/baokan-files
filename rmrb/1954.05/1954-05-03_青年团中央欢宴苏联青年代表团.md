Title: Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Holds Banquet for Soviet Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-05-03
Page: 3

    The Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League held a banquet on the evening of the 30th to welcome the Soviet youth delegation invited to visit our country. The entire Soviet youth delegation, headed by Comrade Shelepin, attended the banquet.

    The Chinese side attending the banquet included: Hu Yaobang, Liao Chengzhi, Rong Gaotang, Liu Daosheng, Luo Yi, Qu Tangliang, Zhang Ze, and Hu Keshi, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League; Qian Sanqiang and Wu Han, Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Youth Federation; Tian Demin, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation; Ge Buhai, Secretary of the North China Working Committee of the Communist Youth League; Zhang Dazhong, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League; cultural and artistic figures from the capital; and combat heroes from the People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers, as well as model workers from industry and agriculture in Beijing.

    A total of ninety people attended the banquet.

    The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 青年团中央欢宴苏联青年代表团

1954-05-03
第3版()
专栏：

    青年团中央欢宴苏联青年代表团中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会在三十日晚设宴欢迎应邀来我国访问的苏联青年代表团。以谢列平同志为首的苏联青年代表团全体人员出席了宴会。
    中国方面出席宴会的有：青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡耀邦、廖承志、荣高棠、刘导生、罗毅、区棠亮、章泽、胡克实，中华全国民主青年联合会副主席钱三强、吴晗，中华全国学生联合会主席田德民，青年团华北工作委员会书记葛步海、青年团北京市委员会书记张大中，首都文化艺术界人士及解放军、志愿军战斗英雄和北京市工、农业劳动模范等。
    出席宴会的共九十人。
    宴会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。　（新华社）
