Title: India Holds Conference Against U.S.-Pakistan Military Treaty, Advocating for Easing International Tensions; Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee Sends Congratulatory Telegram; Chinese People's Committee for World Peace Sends Congratulatory Telegram Simultaneously
Author:
Date: 1954-05-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: The Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee sent a telegram on the 7th to congratulate the All-India Conference Against the U.S.-Pakistan Military Treaty, Advocating for Easing International Tensions. The telegram is as follows: To the All-India Peace Council, forwarding to the All-India Conference Against the U.S.-Pakistan Military Treaty, Advocating for Easing International Tensions:
    The Indian people's movement against the U.S.-Pakistan military treaty and striving for the easing of international tensions will surely receive the warm support of the people of Asia and all countries of the world. Every achievement made by the Indian people in the struggle against aggressors is an important contribution to defending peace in Asia and the world.
    Wishing the conference success!
                                    Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, May 7, 1954

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th: The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace sent a telegram on the 7th to congratulate the All-India Conference Against the U.S.-Pakistan Military Treaty, Advocating for Easing International Tensions. The telegram is as follows: To the All-India Peace Council, forwarding to the All-India Conference Against the U.S.-Pakistan Military Treaty, Advocating for Easing International Tensions:
    The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace extends warm congratulations to the All-India Conference of people from all walks of life in India against the U.S.-Pakistan military treaty and demanding the easing of international tensions, and is willing to work together with the Indian people to strive for further easing international tensions and defending peace in Asia and the world.
                                        Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, May 7, 1954

### 印度举行反对美巴军事条约、主张缓和国际紧张局势大会  亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会致电祝贺  中国人民保卫世界和平委员会同时致电祝贺

1954-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

    印度举行反对美巴军事条约、主张缓和国际紧张局势大会
    亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会致电祝贺
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会同时致电祝贺
    【新华社七日讯】亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会七日电贺全印反对美巴军事条约、主张缓和国际紧张局势大会。电文如下：全印和平理事会转全印反对美巴军事条约、主张缓和国际紧张局势大会：
    印度人民反对美巴军事条约和争取缓和国际紧张局势的运动，定将获得亚洲及世界各国人民的热烈支持。印度人民在反对侵略者斗争中所取得的每一个成就，是对保卫亚洲与世界和平的重要贡献。
    祝大会成功！
                                    亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会　一九五四年五月七日
    【新华社七日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会七日电贺全印反对美巴军事条约、主张缓和国际紧张局势大会。电文如下：全印和平理事会转全印反对美巴军事条约、主张缓和国际紧张局势大会：
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会向印度各界人民反对美巴军事条约和要求缓和国际紧张局势全国大会致以热烈的祝贺，并愿与印度人民在一起，为争取进一步缓和国际紧张局势、保卫亚洲及世界的和平共同奋斗。
                                        中国人民保卫世界和平委员会　一九五四年五月七日
