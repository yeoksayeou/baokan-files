Title: Pyongyang Rally of Over 200,000 People Supports Nam Il's Proposal for Peaceful Resolution of the Korean Question
Author:
Date: 1954-05-07
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: A rally of over 200,000 people from all walks of life in Pyongyang was held at the square in front of the Pyongyang People's Committee on the afternoon of May 5th, to enthusiastically support the proposal for a peaceful resolution of the Korean question put forward by the Foreign Minister of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Nam Il, at the Geneva Conference.
    Present at the conference were the chairmen of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Democratic Front for the Unification of the Fatherland of Korea, Li Young, Chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Pak Chong-ae, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, responsible persons of various democratic parties and social organizations, and prominent figures from society. Vice Premiers of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Pak Chang-ok, Kim Il, Choi Chang-ik, Hong Myung-hee, Jung Il-ryong, and Park Ui-wan, also attended the meeting.
    Pak Chong-ae, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, spoke at the meeting, saying: The proposal for a peaceful settlement of the Korean question put forward by our government delegation at the Geneva Conference has received enthusiastic support from the people of all strata of our country. This proposal contains the principles for a peaceful resolution of the Korean question. However, the United States and the Syngman Rhee clique have openly opposed the withdrawal of American troops from Korea, and opposed free elections in North and South Korea without foreign interference, attempting to obstruct the peaceful reunification of Korea. Therefore, we must vigilantly watch their activities. She called on all Korean people to mobilize as one and fight for the peaceful reunification of the Fatherland.
    Representatives of the North Korean Democratic Party, the Korean Chondoist Chongu Party, the Korean Kunmin Association and other democratic parties, as well as representatives of workers, peasants, women and youth, spoke afterwards. They unanimously expressed their enthusiastic support for the proposal for a peaceful resolution of the Korean question put forward by the delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea at the Geneva Conference, and expressed their intention to further unite around the Labor Party, the Republic government, and Marshal Kim Il Sung, contributing all their strength to the struggle for the peaceful reunification of the Fatherland.
    The conference finally adopted a resolution calling on all Korean people to fight for the support of the proposal for a peaceful resolution of the Korean question put forward by the delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea at the Geneva Conference, and for the peaceful reunification of the Fatherland.

### 平壤市二十多万人举行集会  支持南日提出的和平解决朝鲜问题方案

1954-05-07
第4版()
专栏：

    平壤市二十多万人举行集会
    支持南日提出的和平解决朝鲜问题方案
    据新华社讯：平壤市各界人民二十多万人五日下午在平壤市人民委员会前面的广场集会，热烈支持朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务相南日在日内瓦会议上提出的和平解决朝鲜问题的方案。
    出席大会的有朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会议长团的议长们，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议议长李英，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱，各民主党派、社会团体的负责人，社会著名人士等。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相朴昌玉、金一、崔昌益、洪命熹、郑一龙、朴义琓等也出席了大会。
    朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱在大会上讲话，她说：我国政府代表团在日内瓦会议上提出的和平解决朝鲜问题的方案，得到了我国各阶层人民的热烈支持。这个方案包含着和平解决朝鲜问题的原则。但是，美国和李承晚集团竟公然反对美军撤离朝鲜，反对在南北朝鲜进行没有外力干涉的自由选举，企图阻挠朝鲜的和平统一。因此，我们要警惕地注视着他们的活动。她号召全朝鲜人民要一致动员起来，为祖国的和平统一而斗争。
    接着讲话的有北朝鲜民主党、朝鲜天道教青友党、朝鲜健民会等民主党派的代表，以及工人、农民、妇女、青年的代表。他们一致表示热烈支持朝鲜民主主义人民共和国代表团在日内瓦会议上提出的和平解决朝鲜问题的方案，并表示要进一步团结在劳动党、共和国政府和金日成元帅的周围，贡献全部力量，为祖国的和平统一而斗争。
    大会最后通过了决议，号召全朝鲜人民为支持朝鲜民主主义人民共和国代表团在日内瓦会议上提出的和平解决朝鲜问题的方案，为祖国的和平统一而斗争。
