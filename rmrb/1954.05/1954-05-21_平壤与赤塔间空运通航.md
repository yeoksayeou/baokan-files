Title: Air Transport Inaugurated Between Pyongyang and Chita
Author:
Date: 1954-05-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, May 20th: TASS, Pyongyang, May 19th—According to the protocol signed at the end of last year between Korea and the Soviet Union regarding the resumption of work by the Korean-Soviet Aviation Transport Company, transport planes have begun flying the route between Pyongyang, Shenyang, Qiqihar, Hailar, and Chita.

### 平壤与赤塔间空运通航

1954-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

    平壤与赤塔间空运通航
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社平壤十九日讯：根据去年年底签订的朝鲜和苏联关于恢复朝苏航空运输会社工作的议定书，运输机已开始沿平壤、沈阳、齐齐哈尔、海拉尔与赤塔线路飞行。
