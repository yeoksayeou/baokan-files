Our Great Motherland
The newly built No. 2 open-hearth furnace at the Chongqing No. 101 Steel Plant has commenced production. It will improve the imbalance between Chongqing's smelting capacity and its rolling capacity. This is the newest Soviet-made automatic feeder replacing manual labor, sending tons of materials into the furnace on the afternoon of May 8.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-05-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    重庆一零一钢铁厂新建二号平炉已经开工生产。它将改善重庆冶炼能力低于轧钢能力的不平衡状况。这是五月八日下午最新式的苏联自动进料机代替人力劳动，把成吨的料送进炉去。
