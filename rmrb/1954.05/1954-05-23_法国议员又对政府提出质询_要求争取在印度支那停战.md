Title: French Parliamentarians Again Question the Government, Demanding Efforts to Achieve an Armistice in Indochina
Author:
Date: 1954-05-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, May 21st, TASS reported: French newspapers report that National Assembly member Pierre André (Independent Republican) has submitted a question to the government, asking what measures the government intends to take to achieve an armistice in Indochina and to prevent France from "joining new defense treaties without the consent of Britain or consulting parliament (this refers to the Southeast Asian military bloc cobbled together by the United States)."
    "L'Aurore" believes that André's questioning may lead to a third parliamentary debate on the French government's policy on the Indochina issue.

### 法国议员又对政府提出质询  要求争取在印度支那停战

1954-05-23
第4版()
专栏：

    法国议员又对政府提出质询
    要求争取在印度支那停战
    【新华社二十二日讯】塔斯社巴黎二十一日讯：法国各报报道，国民议会议员皮埃尔·安德烈（独立共和党）向政府提出一项质询，问政府打算采取什么措施，以争取在印度支那停战和预防法国“不得到英国同意，也不征询议会的意见就加入新的防御条约（这指的是美国拼凑的东南亚军事集团）”。
    “震旦报”认为，安德烈的质询可能使得议会就法国政府对印度支那问题的政策进行第三次辩论。
