Title: Press Office of Chinese Delegation Hosts Evening Reception
Author:
Date: 1954-05-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 21st: The Press Office of the Delegation of the People's Republic of China hosted an evening reception at the Hotel du Lac here on the 20th to show the color Yue Opera film "Liang Shanbo and Zhu Yingtai" to the journalists covering the Geneva Conference. More than 250 people attended, and the venue was packed. During the cocktail party after the screening, they unanimously praised the film's plot, colors, music, and the actors' skills. Many British, French, and West German journalists were deeply impressed by the beautiful style and high artistic level of Chinese folk art embodied in the Chinese film's adaptation of folk drama to the screen.

### 我国代表团新闻处举行晚会

1954-05-23
第4版()
专栏：

    我国代表团新闻处举行晚会
    【新华社日内瓦二十一日电】中华人民共和国代表团新闻处二十日晚在此间湖滨旅馆举行晚会，放映五彩越剧故事片“梁山伯与祝英台”，招待现在在日内瓦采访会议新闻的新闻记者。到会有二百五十余人，场内座无虚席。他们在放映后的鸡尾酒会上，对于该影片的情节、色彩、音乐以及演员的技巧等各方面，一致给予好评。有许多英国、法国和西德的记者，对于中国电影把民间戏剧搬上银幕所体现出的中国民间艺术的优美风格和高度艺术水平，获得了深刻的印象。
