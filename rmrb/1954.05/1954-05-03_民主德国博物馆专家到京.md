Title: East German Museum Experts Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-05-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency report: According to the 1954 implementation plan of the Sino-German cultural cooperation agreement, Miss Kete Finshtebush and Mrs. Eric Fritcher, museum experts from the German Democratic Republic who study Oriental art, were invited to China to study classical art and participate in the "May 1st" celebration, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of April 30.

### 民主德国博物馆专家到京

1954-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国博物馆专家到京
    据新华社讯：按照中德文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划，应邀来我国研究古典艺术并参加“五一”节观礼的德意志民主共和国研究东方艺术的博物馆专家克特·芬什特布什小姐和埃里克·弗里彻夫人，在四月三十日下午乘飞机到达北京。
