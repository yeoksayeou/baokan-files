Title: Indian Government Sends Personnel to China to Investigate Water Conservancy: Sain and Law Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-05-10
Page: 4

    Indian Government Sends Personnel to China to Investigate Water Conservancy
    Sain and Law Arrive in Beijing
   Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Sain, Chairman of the Central Water and Power Commission of the Indian Ministry of Irrigation and Power, and Law, Director of Design of the Commission, sent by the Indian government to China to investigate water conservancy, arrived in Beijing on the evening of the 7th. Hao Zhizhai, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Water Conservancy of the Central People's Government, and others went to the station to greet them.
    On the evening of the 8th, Zhang Hanying, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Water Conservancy of the Central People's Government, hosted a banquet in honor of Sain and Law.

### 印度政府派员来我国考察水利  赛因和劳氏到达北京

1954-05-10
第4版()
专栏：

    印度政府派员来我国考察水利
    赛因和劳氏到达北京
   【新华社九日讯】印度政府派遣来我国考察水利的印度水利和电力部中央水利和电力委员会主席赛因和该会设计主任劳氏，在七日晚到达北京。前往车站迎接的有我中央人民政府水利部办公厅副主任郝执斋等人。
    八日晚，我中央人民政府水利部副部长张含英设宴招待赛因和劳氏。
