    Our Great Motherland
    In Tianjia Village, Xiaopingdao District, Dalian City, a new "Advanced Animal Husbandry Production Cooperative" has been established. It can supply the city with 140 catties of cow and goat milk every day, and the output will continue to increase. This is a picture of the members milking goats.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    旅大市小平岛区田家村新成立了一个“先进畜牧业生产合作社”，每天可以供给城市一百四十斤牛羊奶，产量将继续增加。这是社员挤羊奶的情况。
