Title: International Student Union and Vietnamese Youth Organizations Send Congratulatory Messages for China's Youth Day

Author: 
Date: 1954-05-04
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, May 3rd:

    The Vietnamese youth organizations, the Vietnam Youth Union, the Vietnam National Salvation Youth League, and the Vietnam Student Union, sent telegrams to the All-China Youth Federation, the China New Democratic Youth League, and the All-China Students' Federation, congratulating them on China's Youth Day. The telegram stated: Vietnamese youth and students extend warm, united, and militant greetings to Chinese youth and students. Under the wise leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, you have waged heroic struggles in the long and arduous revolution to win national liberation and establish the People's Republic of China. Inheriting this glorious tradition, in recent years you have achieved brilliant successes in the movement to resist America and aid Korea and in the socialist construction of the motherland.
    Chinese youth and students have set a shining example for Vietnamese youth. Your achievements have greatly inspired us, provided us with valuable experience, and strengthened our determination and will to overthrow the French colonialists, defeat the American interventionists, and overthrow the feudal system.
    China is implementing its Five-Year Plan. Vietnamese youth and students warmly congratulate you on achieving even more brilliant success in building a happy life, building a strong China, and safeguarding Asian and world peace.

    Xinhua News Agency, May 3rd:

    The International Student Union sent a telegram to the All-China Students' Federation congratulating them on China's Youth Day. The telegram stated:
    On the occasion of the 35th anniversary of China's "May Fourth" Youth Day, the International Student Union extends warm congratulations to the youth and students of China. We wish you further achievements in your studies and work, in realizing the Five-Year Plan for building your great motherland, in consolidating friendship with the youth and students of other countries, and in striving for world peace.

### 国际学联和越南青年组织电贺我国青年节

1954-05-04
第4版()
专栏：

    国际学联和越南青年组织电贺我国青年节
    【新华社三日讯】越南青年组织越南青年联盟、越南救国青年团和越南学生联合会致电中华全国民主青年联合会、中国新民主主义青年团和中华全国学生联合会祝贺我国青年节。电文说：越南青年学生热烈地向中国青年学生致以团结、战斗的敬礼。在中国共产党和毛主席的英明领导下，你们在争取民族解放、建立中华人民共和国的长期艰苦的革命中，曾进行了英勇的斗争。继承着这光荣的传统，最近几年来你们又在抗美援朝运动和祖国的社会主义建设事业中获得光辉的成就。
    中国青年学生给越南青年树立了光辉的榜样。你们的成就大大鼓舞了我们、给我们提供了宝贵的经验并增强了我们打倒法国殖民者、击败美国干涉者和推翻封建制度的决心和意志。
    中国正在执行五年计划。越南青年学生热忱祝贺你们在建设幸福的生活、建设强大的中国、保卫亚洲及世界和平的事业中得到更加辉煌的成就。
    【新华社三日讯】国际学生联合会致电中华全国学生联合会祝贺我国青年节。电文说：
    值此“五四”中国青年节三十五周年纪念日，国际学生联合会谨向中国的青年和学生致以热烈的祝贺。祝你们在学习与工作中、在实现建设你们伟大的祖国的五年计划中、在巩固与其他国家的青年和学生的友谊及争取世界和平中获得进一步的成就。
