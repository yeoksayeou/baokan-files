Title: The State-Owned Shijiazhuang No. 1 Cotton Mill Starts Production (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1954-05-21
Page: 2

The State-Owned Shijiazhuang No. 1 Cotton Mill Starts Production

    Above: Zhao Minglai, a farmer representative and head of the Xisanjiao Village Agricultural Production Cooperative in the suburban area, presents large bales of cotton to the factory at the meeting on behalf of the farmers, expressing that the farmers will produce even better to support national construction in the future. The workers also presented a piece of cloth and a bundle of yarn woven by them to the farmer representatives.
    Hebei Daily Manuscript, Ke Zhang, Yuan Hao Photos

    Below: The warping machine starts production. Shijiazhuang No. 1 Culture Center Manuscript, Yang Bozhi Photo

    After more than a year of intense construction, the State-Owned Shijiazhuang No. 1 Cotton Mill officially started production on April 29, two months ahead of schedule. At 8:30 a.m. that day, a grand opening ceremony was held in front of the factory building and office building. This is a panoramic view of the meeting. Hebei Daily Manuscript, Ke Zhang, Yuan Hao Photos

### 国营石家庄第一棉纺厂开工生产（图片）

1954-05-21
第2版()
专栏：

    国营石家庄第一棉纺厂开工生产
    上：农民代表、市郊区西三教村农业生产合作社社长赵明来在大会上代表农民把大包棉花赠献给工厂，表示农民们要在今后更好地生产支持国家的建设。工人们也以织出的一匹布和一捆纱送给农民代表。
    河北日报稿  克章  袁浩摄
    下：整经机开始生产。　石家庄第一文化馆稿　杨博之摄    国营石家庄第一棉纺织厂经过一年多的紧张施工后，已于四月二十九日提前二个月正式开始生产了。这天上午八时半在该厂厂房和办公大楼前隆重举行了开工典礼大会，这是大会全景。　河北日报稿  克章  袁浩摄
