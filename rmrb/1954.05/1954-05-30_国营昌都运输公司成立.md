Title: State-owned Qamdo Transportation Company Established
Author:
Date: 1954-05-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency: The state-owned Qamdo Transportation Company has been formally established. The company is responsible for passenger and freight transportation from the Gangtuo ferry crossing on the west bank of the Jinsha River to the current terminus of the Kangzang Highway, which will greatly facilitate the exchange of goods between the interior and the Kangzang Plateau. After the establishment of the transportation company, the animal power previously used for transportation on this section of the highway will gradually shift to branch line transportation.

### 国营昌都运输公司成立

1954-05-30
第2版()
专栏：

    国营昌都运输公司成立
    国营昌都运输公司已正式成立。公司担负金沙江西岸的岗沱渡口到康藏公路目前汽车终点站的客、货运输任务，将大大便利内地和康藏高原的物资交流。运输公司成立后，以前在这一段公路线上运输的兽力，便逐步转到支线运输。　（新华社）
