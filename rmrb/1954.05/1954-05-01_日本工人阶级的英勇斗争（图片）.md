Title: The Heroic Struggle of the Japanese Working Class (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1954-05-01
Page: 6

The heroic struggle of the Japanese working class
    1.  60,000 workers belonging to the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan held a rally in Hibiya Park, Tokyo, against the Yoshida government's "Anti-Strike Law," and then demonstrated and marched through the streets of Tokyo.
    2.  On the day the Japanese Yoshida government forced the passage of the "Anti-Strike Law" in the House of Representatives, worker representatives from the Japan Coal Miners' Union and the Japan Government and Public Workers' Union Council demonstrated in the courtyard of the House of Representatives.
    3.  Japanese workers employed by the U.S. occupation forces in northern Tokyo held a "Workers' Rally Against the Anti-Strike Law."

### 日本工人阶级的英勇斗争（图片）

1954-05-01
第6版()
专栏：

    日本工人阶级的英勇斗争
    一、属于日本工会总评议会的六万工人，在东京日比谷公园举行反对吉田政府的“禁止罢工法”大会后，在东京街头示威游行。
    二、日本吉田政府强制国会众议院通过“禁止罢工法”的当天，日本煤矿工会、日本官公厅工会协议会的工人代表在众议院的院子中示威。
    三、东京北部美国占领军雇用的日本工人，举行“反对禁止罢工法工人大会”。
