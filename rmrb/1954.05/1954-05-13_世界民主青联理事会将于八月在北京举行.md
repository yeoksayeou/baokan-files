Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Council to be Held in Beijing in August
Author: 
Date: 1954-05-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, May 11th: The Executive Committee of the World Federation of Democratic Youth has accepted the invitation of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth to hold a council meeting of the World Federation of Democratic Youth in Beijing.

According to the decision of the Executive Committee, the meeting will be held from August 9th to 15th this year, and the meeting will include three agenda items: 1. The struggle of young people in colonial and backward countries for their rights, for national independence, and for peace, and the tasks of the World Federation of Democratic Youth; 2. The issue of organizations of the World Federation of Democratic Youth participating in the preparation of the International Rural Youth Conference; 3. Preparing for the Fifth World Youth and Student Festival and the Second World Youth Friendship Sports Meet.

### 世界民主青联理事会将于八月在北京举行

1954-05-13
第4版()
专栏：

    世界民主青联理事会将于八月在北京举行
    【新华社十二日讯】据塔斯社布达佩斯十一日讯：世界民主青年联盟执行委员会接受了中华全国民主青年联合会的邀请，将在北京举行世界民主青年联盟理事会会议。
    根据执行委员会的决议，会议将在今年八月九日至十五日举行，会议将包括三项议程：一、殖民地和落后国家的青年争取自己的权利、争取民族独立及和平的斗争和世界民主青年联盟的任务；二、世界民主青年联盟的各国组织参加筹备国际农村青年大会的问题；三、筹备第五届世界青年与学生联欢节和第二届世界青年友谊体育大会。
