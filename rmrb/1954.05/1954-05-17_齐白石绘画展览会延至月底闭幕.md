Title: Qi Baishi Painting Exhibition Extended to End of Month
Author:
Date: 1954-05-17
Page: 3

The Qi Baishi Painting Exhibition, sponsored by the Chinese Artists Association, opened on April 28th at the Qianqing Palace on the east side of the Forbidden City Museum and has been extremely popular with visitors. As of May 14th, the number of visitors has exceeded 40,000. The exhibition was originally scheduled to end on May 18th, but it has now been decided to extend it to May 31st.

### 齐白石绘画展览会延至月底闭幕

1954-05-17
第3版()
专栏：

    齐白石绘画展览会延至月底闭幕
    中国美术家协会主办的齐白石绘画展览会，自四月二十八日在首都故宫博物院东路承乾宫开幕以来，观众非常踊跃，截至五月十四日止，参观人数已超过四万人。展览会原定五月十八日结束，现已决定延至五月三十一日闭幕。
