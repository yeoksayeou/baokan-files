Title: German Ambassador König Holds Reception to Celebrate German Liberation Day
Author:
Date: 1954-05-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. German Democratic Republic Ambassador to China, König, held a film reception at the embassy on the evening of the 8th to celebrate German Liberation Day.

Those invited to attend the reception included: Guo Moruo, Vice Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government, Shen Junru, President of the Supreme People's Court, Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and many others.

Also invited to attend the reception were diplomatic envoys and embassy staff from various countries stationed in China.

At the reception, a film depicting the revolutionary struggle of Ernst Thälmann was shown.

To celebrate German Liberation Day, Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government, sent a telegram of congratulations to Grotewohl, Premier of the German Democratic Republic.

### 德大使柯尼希举行招待会庆祝德解放日

1954-05-09
第1版()
专栏：

    德大使柯尼希举行招待会庆祝德解放日
    【新华社八日讯】德意志民主共和国驻我国大使柯尼希八日晚在大使馆举行电影招待会，庆祝德国解放日。
    应邀出席招待会的有：中央人民政府政务院副总理郭沫若，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，外交部副部长伍修权等多人。
    应邀出席招待会的，还有各国驻华使节和使馆人员。
    招待会上，放映了描写恩斯特·台尔曼从事革命斗争的影片。
    为庆祝德国解放日，中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理特致电德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥，表示祝贺。
