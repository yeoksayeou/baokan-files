Title: PLA Railway Engineering Corps Holds Third Meritorious Service Celebration Conference
Author:
Date: 1954-05-19
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, May 18th: The Third Meritorious Service Celebration Conference of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Railway Engineering Corps was held in Beijing on the 14th, lasting five days and closing victoriously on the 18th.

    Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army attended the conference and gave important instructions.

    Teng Daiyuan, Minister of the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government, and Li Zaiwen, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, attended the conference.

    Four hundred and seventy-four representatives of heroes, models, and meritorious individuals from various units of the Railway Engineering Corps participated in the conference, including some representatives of the Railway Engineering Corps of the Chinese People's Volunteers who were invited to attend.

    The People's Liberation Army Railway Engineering Corps has achieved tremendous results in restoring and building the motherland's railways. In the later stages of the Liberation War, the People's Liberation Army Railway Engineering Corps repaired 1,623 kilometers of lines destroyed by the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang, 976 bridges (with a total extension of 26,294.62 meters), and 46,608 kilometers of communication lines, making a significant contribution to the victory of the war. After the national victory, the Railway Engineering Corps actively participated in the permanent restoration projects of national railways, and together with railway workers across the country, basically restored the railway network on the Chinese mainland, playing a significant role in the recovery of the national economy. The People's Liberation Army Railway Engineering Corps also participated in the construction of the Tianshui-Lanzhou Railway, the southern section of the Baoji-Chengdu Railway, the Northeast Forest Railway, and the Wuhan Han River Railway Bridge.

    Among the meritorious representatives of the People's Liberation Army Railway Engineering Corps, there is Wang Zhilin, a first-class meritorious individual who established outstanding achievements in the construction of a certain section of railway; Jiang Fusheng, a second-class meritorious individual who put forward rationalization proposals in the construction of a certain forest railway in the Northeast, thereby completing the roadbed construction task ahead of schedule; and Zhang Jun, a second-class meritorious individual who learned to operate a large crane in three days during the Han River Railway Bridge project; Wang Juncai and Fu Rongshan, meritorious representatives of the Fifth Company, who created the highest record of driving 260 meters of bridge pile foundations in six hours.

    Among the meritorious representatives of the Railway Engineering Corps of the Volunteers, there are Li Zhenyuan, the meritorious representative of the nationally famous "Yang Lian Di Company"; Qiu Yongfu, the representative of the Ninth Company of a certain unit, who was awarded a collective second-class merit in the struggle against the enemy's "strangulation war"; Qi Qingquan, who was awarded a first-class merit for helping the Korean people restore the Qingchuan River Bridge project; and Ma Shouchang, Li Yunlong, and Yu Shen, second-class heroes and second-class models who made meritorious service in the war.

    At the opening of the conference, Li Shouxuan, Deputy Commander of the Railway Engineering Corps, delivered the opening speech. After describing the achievements made by the People's Liberation Army Railway Engineering Corps in recent years, he said that the main task of the Railway Engineering Corps in the future is to conscientiously study and implement the general task of the country's transition period, actively participate in the motherland's railway construction cause, and strive to build itself into a regularized and modernized railway engineering corps. He demanded that all commanders and fighters must guard against arrogance and impatience, continue to carry forward the spirit of heroic tenacity and hard work, learn with humility, and establish greater merits for the motherland.

    Teng Daiyuan, Minister of the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government, delivered a congratulatory speech at the meeting. He hoped that the People's Liberation Army Railway Engineering Corps would make more brilliant contributions in participating in the motherland's railway construction cause with its own glorious and creative labor.

    Commander-in-Chief Zhu De gave important instructions at the opening. He said: The Railway Engineering Corps is an indispensable part of the modern national defense force. Therefore, the task of the Railway Engineering Corps is to step up construction and make itself a strong force that can shoulder the important responsibility of defending the motherland and supporting the country's economic construction. He encouraged all commanders and fighters of the Railway Engineering Corps to strengthen their political, military, cultural, and technical studies, humbly learn advanced Soviet military science, and carry forward the glorious traditions of the People's Liberation Army of hard struggle and unity, develop the spirit of collective heroism, and constantly heighten their vigilance to defend the motherland and participate in the motherland's socialist construction.

    During the meeting, Chen Li, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Railway Engineering Corps, made a summary report on the meritorious service campaign to the conference, Cui Tianmin, Deputy Political Commissar, made a report on the future work tasks of the Railway Engineering Corps, and Liu Ke, Deputy Commander of Technology of the Railway Engineering Corps, made a report on participating in the task of new railway construction projects in the motherland. The representatives enthusiastically discussed these reports and expressed their determination to resolutely implement Commander-in-Chief Zhu's instructions and strive to defend and build the motherland.

    At the meeting, Gan Siqi, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government, gave a report on the current situation. Wang Zhen, Commander and Political Commissar of the Railway Engineering Corps, also gave instructions to the heroes and meritorious individuals present. He encouraged everyone to always stand on the glorious position of defending and building the motherland and complete the tasks assigned by the people of the motherland and Chairman Mao.

    At the end of the conference, awards were presented to the heroes, models, and meritorious individuals present. The leaders of the Railway Engineering Corps also hosted a banquet on the evening of the 18th to bid them farewell.

### 解放军铁道兵举行三届庆功大会

1954-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

    解放军铁道兵举行三届庆功大会
    【新华社十八日讯】中国人民解放军铁道兵第三届庆功大会于十四日在北京召开，历时五天，在十八日胜利闭幕。
    中国人民解放军朱德总司令曾到会作了重要指示。
    中央人民政府铁道部部长滕代远、中华全国总工会书记处书记栗再温出席了这次大会。
    参加大会的有铁道兵各部队的英雄、模范、功臣代表四百七十四人，其中有一部分是应邀参加此次大会的中国人民志愿军铁道兵的代表。
    人民解放军铁道兵在恢复和建筑祖国铁路线中取得了巨大的成绩。在解放战争后期，人民解放军铁道兵曾抢修被蒋介石匪帮破坏的线路一千六百二十三公里，桥梁九百七十六座（总延长二万六千二百九十四点六二公尺），通讯线路四万六千六百零八条公里，对战争的胜利作了重大贡献。全国胜利后，铁道兵又积极参加了全国铁路线的永久性复旧工程，和全国铁路职工一道基本上恢复了中国大陆上的铁路网，对国民经济的恢复工作起了很大的作用。人民解放军铁道兵还参加了天兰铁路、宝成铁路南段、东北森林铁路等和武汉汉水铁桥等建设工程。
    在解放军铁道兵的功臣代表中，有在修建某段铁路中建立卓越功绩的一等功臣王志林；有在东北建筑某森林铁路的工程中提出合理化建议，因而提前完成修筑路基任务的二等功臣江富生以及在汉水铁桥工程中三天学会操纵大型吊船的二等功臣张君；创造了在六小时中桥台打桩二百六十公尺最高纪录的第五连功臣代表王俊才、傅荣山等。
    在志愿军铁道兵功臣代表中，有全国著名的“杨连第连”的功臣代表李振远、有在反对敌人“绞杀战”的斗争中荣立集体二等功的某部第九连代表邱永福、有在帮助朝鲜人民恢复清川江大桥工程中荣立一等功的齐清泉以及在战争中荣立功勋的二级英雄马寿昌、李云龙、二级模范于深等。
    大会开幕时，铁道兵副司令员李寿轩致开幕词。他在叙述了人民解放军铁道兵几年来所取得的成绩后说，铁道兵今后的主要任务是认真学习和贯彻国家过渡时期的总任务，积极参加祖国的铁道建设事业，并为把自己建设成一支正规化、现代化的铁道兵而努力。他要求全体指挥员、战斗员必须戒骄戒躁，继续发扬英勇顽强、艰苦奋斗的精神，虚心学习，为祖国建立更大的功勋。
    中央人民政府铁道部滕代远部长在会上致祝词。他希望人民解放军铁道兵在参加祖国铁道建设事业中，以自己光荣的创造性的劳动，作出更光辉的贡献。
    朱德总司令在开幕时作了重要指示。他说：铁道兵是现代化国防军不可缺少的组成部分，因此，铁道兵的任务就是要加紧建设，使自己成为一支坚强的、能够担负起保卫祖国、支援国家经济建设的重要力量。他勉励铁道兵的全体指挥员、战斗员加强政治、军事、文化、技术学习，虚心学习苏联先进的军事科学，并发扬人民解放军艰苦奋斗、团结一致的光荣传统，发扬集体英雄主义精神，时刻提高警惕，为保卫祖国和参加祖国的社会主义建设事业而努力。
    会议期间，铁道兵政治部副主任陈力向大会作了立功运动的总结报告，副政治委员崔田民作了关于铁道兵今后工作任务的报告，铁道兵技术副司令员刘克作了关于参加祖国新建铁路工程任务的报告。代表们热烈地讨论了这些报告，并纷纷表示要坚决执行朱总司令的指示，为保卫祖国和建设祖国而奋斗。
    会上，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任甘泗淇作了目前形势的报告。铁道兵司令员兼政治委员王震也向到会的英雄、功臣们作了指示。他勉励大家永远站在保卫祖国和建设祖国的光荣岗位上，完成祖国人民和毛主席交给的任务。
    大会最后给到会的英雄、模范、功臣们授奖。铁道兵首长并在十八日晚设宴欢送他们。
