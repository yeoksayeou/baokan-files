Title: Celebrations of the 300th Anniversary of Ukraine's Reunification with Russia: Grand Gatherings Held by People Across the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1954-05-24
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency: The Soviet people are continuing to carry out various activities to warmly celebrate the 300th anniversary of Ukraine's reunification with Russia. Celebrations are being held in various places these days. A grand celebration hosted by the Soviet Composers Association was held on the 18th in the Great Hall of the Moscow Conservatory. At the meeting, Ukrainian artists currently visiting Moscow were warmly welcomed by Moscow musicians, composers, actors, and artists. Kozytsky, Chairman of the Board of the Ukrainian Republic Soviet Composers Association, gave a report at the meeting. He focused on the long and solid connections between the Ukrainian and Russian people in musical art, and the positive influence of advanced Russian classical music on the development of Ukrainian music. The Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Medical Sciences also held a celebration in Moscow on the 19th. Professor Nesterov, Vice President of the Soviet Academy of Medical Sciences, spoke in his opening remarks about the great achievements of the Ukrainian people in culture, and emphasized the significant contributions of outstanding Ukrainian scientists to the development of Soviet medicine. In addition, the Soviet capital also held a Ukrainian children's literature exhibition to celebrate the 300th anniversary of Ukraine's reunification with Russia.

In Pereyaslav-Khmelnitsky, a historical city of Ukraine (where the historic resolution on the reunification of Ukraine with Russia was passed 300 years ago), the Kiev Oblast Soviet of Workers' Deputies, together with the Pereyaslav-Khmelnitsky City Soviet of Workers' Deputies, held a celebration meeting on the 17th. Kirichenko, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Ukrainian Communist Party, Kalchenko, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Ukrainian Republic, and Korotchenko, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet, attended the meeting. Grishko, Secretary of the Kiev Oblast Committee of the Ukrainian Communist Party, gave a report on the 300th anniversary of Ukraine's reunification with Russia. Korotchenko delivered a congratulatory message to the meeting on behalf of the Central Committee of the Ukrainian Communist Party and the Republic Government.

### 庆祝乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯三百周年  苏联各地人民举行盛大集会

1954-05-24
第3版()
专栏：

    庆祝乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯三百周年
    苏联各地人民举行盛大集会
    据新华社讯：苏联人民继续展开各种活动，热烈庆祝乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯三百周年。这几天，各地正陆续举行庆祝会。苏联作曲家协会主持的盛大庆祝会十八日在莫斯科音乐院大厅举行。会上，目前正在莫斯科访问的乌克兰艺术家，受到莫斯科音乐家、作曲家、演员和艺术家的热烈欢迎。乌克兰共和国苏联作曲家协会理事会主席科齐茨基在会上作了报告。他着重谈到了乌克兰和俄罗斯人民在音乐艺术方面的悠久而巩固的联系，以及先进的俄罗斯古典音乐对乌克兰音乐的发展所起的良好影响。苏联医学科学院主席团也于十九日在莫斯科举行了庆祝会。苏联医学科学院副院长湼斯捷罗夫教授在开会词中谈到了乌克兰人民在文化方面获得的巨大成就，并强调指出了杰出的乌克兰科学家对发展苏联医学所作的重大贡献。此外，苏联首都还为庆祝乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯三百周年举办了乌克兰儿童文学展览会。
    在乌克兰的历史名城佩雷雅斯拉夫—赫米尔尼茨基（三百年前在这儿通过了乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯的历史性决议），基辅州劳动人民代表苏维埃十七日会同佩雷雅斯拉夫—赫米尔尼茨基城劳动人民代表苏维埃举行了庆祝大会。乌克兰共产党中央委员会第一书记基里钦科、乌克兰共和国部长会议主席卡尔钦科、最高苏维埃主席团主席科罗特钦科出席了这次会议。乌克兰共产党基辅州委员会书记格里希科在会上作了关于乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯三百周年的报告。科罗特钦科代表乌克兰共产党中央委员会和共和国政府向会议致贺词。
