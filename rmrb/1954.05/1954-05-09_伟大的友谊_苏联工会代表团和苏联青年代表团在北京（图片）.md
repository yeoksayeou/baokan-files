Title: Great Friendship – Soviet Trade Union Delegation and Soviet Youth Delegation in Beijing (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1954-05-09
Page: 5

Great Friendship
——Soviet Trade Union Delegation and Soviet Youth Delegation in Beijing

The Soviet Youth Delegation, invited by the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by air on April 29. Alexander N. Shelepin, head of the Soviet Youth Delegation (second from the left in the front row), posed for a photo with Hu Yaobang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, after disembarking. China Youth Daily, Photo by Jia Huamin

The Soviet Trade Union Delegation, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the May Day International Labor Day celebrations in our country in 1954, arrived in Beijing by plane on April 28. The fifth person from the left is the head of the delegation, V.I. Krestyaninov. Xinhua News Agency reporter Qian Sijie

The Soviet Trade Union Delegation visits the "Angang Technological Innovation Exhibition" being held in Beijing. Edited by the China Workers Painting Collection Editorial Office, Photo by Yang Ziyi

The Soviet Trade Union Delegation on the Tiananmen reviewing stand during the 1954 May Day International Labor Day celebrations in our country. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zheng Xiaozhen

Alexander N. Shelepin, head of the Soviet Youth Delegation and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, speaks at a rally held in Zhongshan Park in our capital to commemorate the "May Fourth" Movement and welcome the Soviet Youth Delegation. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zou Jiandong

The Soviet Youth Delegation applauds and waves to the parading crowds from the Tiananmen reviewing stand during the 1954 May Day International Labor Day celebrations in our country. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zou Jiandong

V.G. Kucherova, member of the Soviet Trade Union Delegation, vice foreman of the Ivanovo Miranki United Factory, and representative of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, visits the Beijing No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory. Edited by the China Workers Painting Collection Editorial Office, Photo by Ma Chunian

V.G. Blazhenov, member of the Soviet Trade Union Delegation, driver of the Moscow-Sogirov locomotive depot, and representative of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, exchanges experiences with Guo Shude, national railway labor model of our country, during a visit to the Fengtai Locomotive Depot of the Beijing Railway Bureau. Edited by the China Workers Painting Collection Editorial Office, Photo by Yang Ziyi

Members of the Soviet Youth Delegation and Chinese youth sing and dance to their hearts' content at the garden party celebrating the "May Fourth" Youth Day. Xinhua News Agency reporters Ye Hua and Yu Huiru

Some members of the Soviet Youth Delegation visit the Red Flag Agricultural Production Cooperative in Liangjiadian Village, Haidian District, a suburb of Beijing. This is the scene when they visited the vegetable fields. China Youth Daily, Photo by Hong Ke

### 伟大的友谊——苏联工会代表团和苏联青年代表团在北京（图片）

1954-05-09
第5版()
专栏：

    伟大的友谊
    ——苏联工会代表团和苏联青年代表团在北京
    应中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会的邀请来我国访问的苏联青年代表团，于四月二十九日飞抵北京。苏联青年代表团团长亚历山大·纳·谢列平（前排左起第二人）下机后，和中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡耀邦合影。   中国青年报稿　贾化民摄
    应中华全国总工会的邀请来我国参加一九五四年“五一”国际劳动节观礼的苏联工会代表团，于四月二十八日乘飞机到达北京。左起第五人是代表团团长瓦·伊·克雷斯特雅尼诺夫。新华社记者　钱嗣杰摄
    苏联工会代表团参观正在北京举行的“鞍钢技术革新展览会”。中国工人画集编辑室稿　杨子颐摄
    苏联工会代表团在我国一九五四年“五一”国际劳动节天安门观礼台上。　新华社记者　郑小箴摄
    苏联青年代表团团长、苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会第一书记亚历山大·纳·谢列平在我国首都中山公园举行的纪念“五四”和欢迎苏联青年代表团大会上讲话。　新华社记者　邹健东摄
    苏联青年代表团在我国一九五四年“五一”国际劳动节天安门观礼台上向游行队伍鼓掌、招手致意。新华社记者　邹健东摄
    苏联工会代表团团员、依万诺佛米蓝基联合厂副领班、苏联最高苏维埃代表维·格·库契罗娃参观北京第一棉纺织厂。  中国工人画集编辑室稿　马椿年摄
    苏联工会代表团团员、莫斯科——索吉罗夫机务段司机长、苏联最高苏维埃代表维·格·布拉仁诺夫参观北京铁路分局丰台机务段时，和我国全国铁路劳动模范郭树德交流经验。  中国工人画集编辑室稿　杨子颐摄
    在纪念“五四”青年节游园联欢晚会上苏联青年代表团团员和我国青年尽情歌舞。　新华社记者　叶华　喻惠如摄
    苏联青年代表团部分团员访问北京市郊海甸区亮甲店村红旗农业生产合作社。这是他们参观菜田时的情形。　中国青年报稿　洪克摄
