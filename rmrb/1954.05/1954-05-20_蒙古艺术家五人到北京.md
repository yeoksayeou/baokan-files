Title: Five Mongolian Artists Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-05-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 19th: At the invitation of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the State Council of the Central People's Government, the artists of the Mongolian People's Republic, who came to China to visit and prepare for the Art Exhibition of the Mongolian People's Republic, arrived in Beijing on the 18th (Gelejigin·Audun had already arrived in Beijing on May 9th).  Liu Kaiqu, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Artists Association, Hua Junwu, Secretary-General, Zou Difan, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, and more than a dozen Chinese artists including Gu Yuan, Wang Xun, Ai Zhongxin, Zhong Ling, and Li Qun, went to the station to welcome them.
    The exhibits of the Art Exhibition of the Mongolian People's Republic include more than 200 original works of oil paintings and sculptures, which will be publicly exhibited in the Zhongshan Hall of Zhongshan Park in the capital before the end of May.
    The list of Mongolian People's Republic artists visiting China is as follows:
    Dojin·Quedok: Art Director of the Art Bureau of the Art Committee of the Mongolian People's Republic, Representative of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, Honored Artist, Recipient of the Choibalsan Prize.
    Jiaoyidanjiin·Baldandorj: Staff member of the Art Committee of the Mongolian People's Republic, painter.
    Dorgolun·Manibadar: Pattern designer, Honored Artist, Recipient of the Choibalsan Prize.
    Barbarun·Gombosuren: Painter
    Gelejigin·Audun: Painter

### 蒙古艺术家五人到北京

1954-05-20
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古艺术家五人到北京
    【新华社十九日讯】应我中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局的邀请前来我国访问并筹备蒙古人民共和国美术展览会的蒙古人民共和国艺术家已在十八日到达北京（其中格列吉因·奥敦已在五月九日先行抵京）。前往车站欢迎的有中国美术家协会副主席刘开渠、秘书长华君武、对外文化联络事务局处长邹荻帆，以及我国美术家古元、王逊、艾中信、钟灵、力群等十余人。
    蒙古人民共和国美术展览会的展品包括油画、雕塑等原作二百余件，将在五月底以前在首都中山公园中山堂公开展出。
    蒙古人民共和国来华美术家名单如下：
    多吉因·却多克  蒙古人民共和国艺术委员会美术局美术指导，蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔代表，功勋美术家，乔巴山奖金获得者。
    缴伊丹吉因·巴尔丹多尔日  蒙古人民共和国艺术委员会工作者，画家。
    多尔高伦·马尼巴达尔  图案画家，功勋美术家，乔巴山奖金获得者。
    巴尔巴伦·高姆波苏伦  画家
    格列吉因·奥敦  画家
