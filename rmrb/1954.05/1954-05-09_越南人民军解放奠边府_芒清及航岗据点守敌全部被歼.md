Title: The Vietnamese People's Army Liberates Dien Bien Phu; All Enemy Troops Defending Muong Thanh and Hong Cong Strongholds Annihilated
Author:
Date: 1954-05-09
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, May 8th: According to Vietnam News Agency on May 8th: On the evening of May 6th and the 7th, the Vietnamese People's Army launched a general offensive against the French army's central stronghold in Dien Bien Phu, Muong Thanh. Under the powerful offensive of the Vietnamese People's Army, enemy troops in various fortifications surrendered one after another. By 5 p.m. on the 7th, the People's Army had completely occupied Muong Thanh, and all defending enemy troops were annihilated.
    On the same day at 7 p.m., nearly 2,000 enemy troops from the Hong Cong stronghold in southern Dien Bien Phu—the last stronghold of the French army in Dien Bien Phu—attempted to break through and escape, but were completely annihilated by the People's Army at 10 p.m. that day. By this point, Dien Bien Phu was completely liberated.
    Xinhua News Agency, May 8th: Vietnam News Agency reports: The spokesperson of the General Headquarters of the Vietnamese People's Army announced at 8 a.m. on May 8th: The French army's Dien Bien Phu group stronghold, which had been surrounded, has been captured.
    On the evening of May 6th, the Vietnamese People's Army launched a general offensive against the French army's heavily fortified group stronghold in Dien Bien Phu. After a night and a day of fighting, the Vietnamese People's Army completely annihilated the defending troops of the enemy's command stronghold in Muong Thanh at 5 p.m. on the 7th. At 10 p.m. on the same day, the Vietnamese People's Army completely annihilated the French troops in the Hong Cong stronghold in southern Dien Bien Phu.
    Since the Vietnamese People's Army launched an offensive against this group stronghold nearly two months ago, the enemy troops annihilated in Dien Bien Phu have totaled seventeen infantry battalions (including seven parachute battalions), three artillery battalions, and several engineering units. During the same period, a total of fifty-seven enemy aircraft were shot down or destroyed. (Photo attached)

### 越南人民军解放奠边府  芒清及航岗据点守敌全部被歼

1954-05-09
第1版()
专栏：

    越南人民军解放奠边府
    芒清及航岗据点守敌全部被歼
    【新华社八日讯】据越南通讯社八日讯：越南人民军在五月六日晚上和七日，向奠边府法军中央堡垒芒清发动总攻。在越南人民军的强大攻势下，各处工事中的敌军纷纷投降。到七日下午五时，人民军完全占领芒清，守敌全部被歼。
    同日下午七时，奠边府南部航岗据点——法军在奠边府最后的一个据点的将近二千名敌军妄图突围逃跑，但在当天下午十时就全部被人民军歼灭。至此，奠边府全部重获解放。
   【新华社八日讯】越南通讯社讯：越南人民军总司令部发言人五月八日上午八时宣布：被包围的法军奠边府集团据点已被攻克。
    五月六日晚上，越南人民军对法军奠边府强固的集团据点发动总攻击。经过一夜又一天的战斗后，越南人民军在七日下午五时全歼敌军指挥据点芒清的守军。同日下午十时，越南人民军全歼奠边府南部据点航岗的法军。
    自从越南人民军在将近两个月以前对这个集团据点发动攻势以来，在奠边府歼灭的敌军共达十七个步兵营（包括七个伞兵营）、三个炮兵营和几支工兵部队。在同一时期内，击落或击毁的敌机共达五十七架。（附图片）
