Title: Rapeseed Bumper Harvest in Fujian Province

Author: 
Date: 1954-05-14
Page: 2

    Fujian Province has achieved a widespread bumper harvest of rapeseed. The total rapeseed harvesting area in the province reached over 400,000 mu, an increase of more than 18% compared to last year. The average yield is about 50 jin of rapeseed per mu. Based on this calculation, the province can harvest over 20 million jin of rapeseed. If all these oilseeds are used for oil extraction, it can produce about 6 million jin of rapeseed oil and over 10 million jin of rapeseed cake.
                                  (Xinhua News Agency)

### 福建省油菜籽普遍丰收

1954-05-14
第2版()
专栏：

    福建省油菜籽普遍丰收
    福建省各地油菜籽普遍丰收。全省油菜收获面积达四十万多亩，比去年增加百分之十八以上。一般每亩收油菜籽五十斤左右，如按此计算，全省约可收油菜籽二千万斤以上。这些油料如全部用来榨油，可产菜油六百万斤左右和油菜饼一千多万斤。
                                  （新华社）
