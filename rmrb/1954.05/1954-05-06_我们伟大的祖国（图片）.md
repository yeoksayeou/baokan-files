    Our Great Motherland
    Yulong Mountain is located in Lijiang Prefecture in northwestern Yunnan Province. It is a branch of the Daxue Mountains in the Hengduan Mountains. It rises 5,914 meters above sea level. The mountain peaks are covered with snow all year round, and the scenery is beautiful. It is inhabited by various fraternal ethnic groups such as the Naxi, Bai, Lisu, and Yi. This is a Naxi woman living at the foot of Yulong Mountain.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    玉龙山在云南省西北部丽江专区，是横断山脉区的大雪山的支脉，拔海五千九百十四公尺，山峰终年积雪，风景秀丽。这里居住着麽些、民家、傈僳、彝等各兄弟民族。这是居住在玉龙山麓的麽些族妇女。
