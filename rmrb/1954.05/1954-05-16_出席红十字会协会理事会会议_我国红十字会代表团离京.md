Title: Chinese Red Cross Delegation Leaves Beijing to Attend League of Red Cross Societies Board of Governors Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-05-16
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, May 15th: The Chinese Red Cross delegation of eight people, led by head Li Dequan, departed from Beijing by plane on the morning of the 15th to attend the 23rd Board of Governors Meeting of the League of Red Cross Societies, which will be held in Oslo, Norway, starting on May 24th. Those who went to the airport to see them off included Hu Lansheng, Vice President and Secretary-General of the Red Cross Society of China, Gong Pusheng, Standing Director, and Lin Shixiao, Deputy Secretary-General, and others.

### 出席红十字会协会理事会会议  我国红十字会代表团离京

1954-05-16
第1版()
专栏：

    出席红十字会协会理事会会议
    我国红十字会代表团离京
    【新华社十五日讯】出席五月二十四日起在挪威奥斯陆举行的红十字会协会第二十三届理事会会议的中国红十字会代表团一行八人，由团长李德全率领，在十五日晨乘飞机离京。前往机场欢送的，有中国红十字会总会副会长兼秘书长胡兰生、常务理事龚普生、副秘书长林士笑等多人。
