Title: Zhou Enlai Again Meets with Menon
Author:
Date: 1954-05-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, May 25th. Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, again met with Mr. Menon, the representative of India to the United Nations, at 10:30 a.m. on the 25th.

### 周恩来外长再次接见梅农

1954-05-26
第4版()
专栏：

    周恩来外长再次接见梅农
    【新华社日内瓦二十五日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来二十五日上午十时三十分再次接见印度驻联合国代表梅农先生。
