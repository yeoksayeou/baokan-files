Title: Zhou Enlai, Foreign Minister, Visits British Foreign Secretary Eden
Author:
Date: 1954-05-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 20th -- Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, visited British Foreign Secretary Eden at 11:00 a.m. today for a one-hour meeting.

### 周恩来外长访问英国外交大臣艾登

1954-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

    周恩来外长访问英国外交大臣艾登
    【新华社日内瓦二十日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来在二十日上午十一时访问英国外交大臣艾登，会谈一小时。
