Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Holds Grand Banquet to See Off Trade Union Delegations and Representatives from Various Countries; French General Confederation of Labor Delegation Issues Statement Eagerly Hoping for Swift Restoration of Peace in Indochina
Author:
Date: 1954-05-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 30th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions held a grand banquet at the Peace Hotel on the evening of the 30th to bid farewell to the trade union delegations and representatives from various countries who are about to leave Beijing and return home. The banquet was hosted by Liu Ningyi and Zhu Xuefan, vice-chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.
    Attending the banquet were representatives of trade union delegations and trade union representatives from Korea, Mongolia, Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, Romania, Hungary, India, France, Italy, Israel, Greece, and other countries.
    Also present were Li Jiebo, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, chairmen and vice-chairmen of various industrial trade unions, and heads of various departments of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.
    At the beginning of the banquet, Zhu Xuefan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, delivered a farewell speech. He said: "During the month we have spent together, you have left an unforgettable impression on the Chinese working class. Now that you need to leave us due to work reasons, we will always remember you, unite with you, and work together for trade union rights, for democracy and freedom, and for safeguarding peace in the Far East and the world." Henri Raynaud, head of the French General Confederation of Labor delegation and Secretary of the French General Confederation of Labor, read a written statement from the French General Confederation of Labor delegation at the meeting, expressing warm support for the proposal made by Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Foreign Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, at the Geneva Conference, and sincerely hoping for a swift end to the war in Indochina and the restoration of peace.
    The statement read by Henri Raynaud was warmly welcomed by the trade union representatives from various countries, and the applause at the meeting lasted for a long time.
    Trade union representatives from other countries also spoke at the meeting, expressing their impressions of their visit to New China and their determination to work for the cause of peace.
    At the banquet, Liu Ningyi also delivered a speech to bid farewell to the distinguished guests. He said: "The visits of the distinguished guests to China have greatly encouraged the Chinese working class and the laboring people." Liu Ningyi said: "The Asian representatives have expressed their solidarity and will of the Asian working class and people who love independence, democracy, and peace, and resolutely oppose the U.S. warmongers' sabotage of peace in Asia and the world." Liu Ningyi said: "We are about to part and return to our respective posts to strive for the unity of the working class, world peace, people's democracy, national independence, and the happiness of mankind."
    The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 中华全国总工会举行盛大宴会  欢送各国工会代表团及工会代表  法国总工会代表团发表声明热望迅速恢复印度支那和平

1954-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

    中华全国总工会举行盛大宴会
    欢送各国工会代表团及工会代表
    法国总工会代表团发表声明热望迅速恢复印度支那和平
    【新华社三十日讯】中华全国总工会三十日晚在和平宾馆举行盛大宴会，欢送即将离京返国的各国工会代表团及工会代表。宴会由中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一、朱学范主持。
    出席宴会的有：朝鲜、蒙古、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、印度、法国、意大利、以色列、希腊等国的工会代表团代表及工会代表。
    出席作陪的有：中华全国总工会书记处书记李颉伯，各产业工会主席、副主席，中华全国总工会各部门负责人。
    宴会开始，由中华全国总工会副主席朱学范致欢送词。他说：在一个月的相处中，你们给我们中国工人阶级的印象是永远难忘的。现在大家因为工作的关系需要离开我们，我们将永远记得你们，与你们团结在一起，为工会权利，为民主、自由，为保卫远东与世界和平而共同努力。法国总工会代表团团长、法国总工会书记亨利·雷诺在会上宣读了法国总工会代表团的书面声明，表示热烈支持越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外长范文同在日内瓦会议上的建议，热诚希望迅速结束印度支那战争和恢复和平。
    亨利·雷诺宣读的声明受到了各国工会代表们的热烈欢迎，会上掌声经久不息。
    其他各国的工会代表也都相继在会上讲话，发表在新中国访问的观感，和表达为和平事业而努力的决心。
    在宴会上，刘宁一也致词欢送贵宾们。他说：贵宾们在中国的访问，给我国工人阶级和劳动人民以很大的鼓舞。刘宁一说：亚洲的代表们以由衷的友谊座谈，表示了亚洲工人阶级和人民的热爱独立、民主与和平的团结意志，坚决反对美国好战分子破坏亚洲和世界的和平。刘宁一说：我们将要分别了，各自回到自己的工作岗位，为了工人阶级的团结、世界和平、人民民主、民族独立和人类的幸福而奋斗。
    宴会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
