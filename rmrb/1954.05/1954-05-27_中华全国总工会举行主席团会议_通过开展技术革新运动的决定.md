Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Holds Presidium Meeting, Adopts Decision on Launching Technical Innovation Movement
Author: 
Date: 1954-05-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 26th. The Fifth Meeting of the Presidium of the Seventh Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions passed the "Decision of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions on Launching a Technical Innovation Movement Throughout the Country."

The Fifth Meeting of the Presidium of the Seventh Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions was held from April 20th to 23rd. Heads of various departments and industrial unions of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, as well as model workers Zhang Mingshan, Liu Zuwei, Huang Rongchang, Zhu Shunyu, and Fu Jingwen, attended the meeting. The meeting heard a report by Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, on "Labor Emulation Has Begun to Enter a New Stage—Technical Innovation." At the same time, it discussed the proposal to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions made by seven industrial model workers, Zhang Mingshan, Wang Chonglun, Tang Liyan, Huang Rongchang, Liu Zuwei, Zhu Shunyu, and Fu Jingwen, on launching a technical innovation movement nationwide. After discussion and research, the Presidium fully agreed with Chairman Lai Ruoyu's report, and fully endorsed and supported the proposal of the seven model workers, unanimously believing that the trade union organization should consciously lead this technical innovation movement. The meeting then passed the aforementioned decision.

The meeting also heard and approved a report by Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, on strengthening the international liaison work of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

The meeting also studied the issue of improving the leadership of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions in a spirit of criticism and self-criticism.

In addition, the Presidium of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions also approved the "Instructions of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions on the Methods of Managing and Using Labor Insurance Funds" and the "Provisional Regulations of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions on Several Issues Concerning Sanatorium Undertakings."

### 中华全国总工会举行主席团会议  通过开展技术革新运动的决定

1954-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

    中华全国总工会举行主席团会议
    通过开展技术革新运动的决定
    【新华社二十六日讯】中华全国总工会第七届执行委员会主席团第五次会议通过了“中华全国总工会关于在全国范围内开展技术革新运动的决定”。
    中华全国总工会第七届执行委员会主席团第五次会议是在四月二十日到二十三日举行的。中华全国总工会各部门、各产业工会负责人以及劳动模范张明山、刘祖威、黄荣昌、朱顺余、傅景文等列席了会议。会议听取了中华全国总工会主席赖若愚关于“劳动竞赛已经开始走上一个新的阶段——技术革新”的报告，同时讨论了张明山、王崇伦、唐立言、黄荣昌、刘祖威、朱顺余、傅景文等七位工业劳动模范关于在全国范围内开展技术革新运动向中华全国总工会的建议。主席团经过讨论和研究后，完全同意赖若愚主席的报告，并完全赞成和支持张明山等七位劳动模范的建议，一致认为工会组织应自觉地领导这个技术革新运动。会议接着通过了上述的决定。
    这次会议还听取并同意了中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一关于加强全国总工会国际联络工作的报告。
    会议还以批评和自我批评精神研究了改进中华全国总工会书记处的领导的问题。
    此外，中华全国总工会主席团还批准了“中华全国总工会关于劳动保险金掌握使用办法的指示”和“中华全国总工会关于疗养事业若干问题的暂行规定”。
