Title: Volunteer Army Leadership Actively Discusses Draft Constitution
Author:
Date: 1954-05-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 18th. The leadership of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army is actively discussing the first draft of the Constitution of the People's Republic of China.

    The Volunteer Army leadership's draft constitution discussion group began its discussions on April 9th, chaired by Yang Dezhi, Deputy Commander of the Volunteer Army. Participants included Li Zhimin, Deputy Political Commissar of the Volunteer Army, Yang Yong, Deputy Commander, responsible cadres from the command organs of various armed services, and departments such as the Volunteer Army Headquarters, Political Department, Logistics Department, and Cadre Department.

### 志愿军领导机关积极讨论宪法草案初稿

1954-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军领导机关积极讨论宪法草案初稿
    【新华社平壤十八日电】中国人民志愿军领导机关正积极讨论中华人民共和国宪法草案初稿。
    志愿军领导机关的宪法草案初稿讨论小组自四月九日开始讨论，由志愿军副司令员杨得志主持。参加讨论的有志愿军副政治委员李志民、副司令员杨勇和各兵种指挥机关以及志愿军司令部、政治部、后勤部、干部部等部门的负责干部。
