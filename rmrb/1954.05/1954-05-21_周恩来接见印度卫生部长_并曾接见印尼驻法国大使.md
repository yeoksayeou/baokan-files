Title: Zhou Enlai Receives Indian Health Minister and Previously Received Indonesian Ambassador to France
Author:
Date: 1954-05-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 20th: Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, received Mrs. Kaur, the Indian Minister of Health, on the evening of May 18th. Mrs. Kaur was passing through Geneva on her way to Oslo to attend a meeting of the Board of Governors of the League of Red Cross Societies.
    Prior to this, on May 15th, Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai received Mr. Anak Agung, the Indonesian Ambassador to France. On the morning of May 18th, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian received Mr. Gundevia, the Indian Ambassador to Switzerland.

### 周恩来接见印度卫生部长  并曾接见印尼驻法国大使

1954-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

    周恩来接见印度卫生部长
    并曾接见印尼驻法国大使
    【新华社日内瓦二十日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部周恩来部长于五月十八日晚接见了印度卫生部部长考尔夫人，考尔夫人是路过日内瓦前往奥斯陆出席红十字会协会理事会会议的。
    在此之前，五月十五日，周恩来外长接见了印度尼西亚驻法国大使阿纳克·阿贡先生。十八日上午，外交部张闻天副部长接见了印度驻瑞士大使贡德维亚先生。
