Title: Lao Free National United Front Issues Statement Supporting Soviet, Chinese, and Vietnamese Propositions on Restoring Peace in Indochina
Author: 
Date: 1954-05-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 21st: Vietnam News Agency, May 21st: Phoumi Vongvichit, Secretary-General of the Lao Free National United Front, issued a statement on May 15th, supporting the propositions put forward by the Soviet Union, China, and Vietnam at the Geneva Conference regarding the restoration of peace in Indochina. The full text of the statement is as follows:

    On behalf of all members of the Lao Free National United Front, I welcome and support the propositions put forward by Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov, Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai, and Acting Foreign Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Pham Van Dong at the Geneva Conference regarding the issue of restoring peace in Indochina.

    We believe that:

    These reasonable and practical propositions fully conform to the wishes of the Lao people who have been fighting for peace, national independence, unification, and a democratic life for eight years, and clearly reflect our just demands.

    The speeches and propositions of the American and French delegations at the Geneva Conference are unreasonable and are not based on the current facts and situation in Indochina, especially not on the current facts and situation in Laos.

    The above-mentioned propositions put forward by the Foreign Ministers of the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam can serve as an appropriate basis for the discussions at the Geneva Conference on the issue of achieving a ceasefire and restoring peace in Indochina.

### 寮国自由民族统一战线发表声明  支持苏中越关于恢复印度支那和平的主张

1954-05-22
第4版()
专栏：

    寮国自由民族统一战线发表声明
    支持苏中越关于恢复印度支那和平的主张
    【新华社二十一日讯】越南通讯社二十一日讯：寮国自由民族统一战线总书记富米冯维希在五月十五日发表声明，支持苏联、中国和越南在日内瓦会议上提出的关于恢复印度支那和平的主张。声明全文如下：
    我代表寮国自由民族统一战线全体成员，表示欢迎并支持苏联外交部长莫洛托夫、中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来和越南民主共和国代理外交部长范文同在日内瓦会议上提出的关于恢复印度支那和平问题的主张。
    我们认为：
    这些合理的、切合实际的主张完全符合为争取和平、民族独立、统一和民主生活而进行了八年斗争的寮国人民的愿望，并且清楚地反映了我们的正义的要求。
    美国代表团和法国代表团在日内瓦会议上的发言和主张是不合理的，是没有以印度支那目前的事实和局势，特别是没有以寮国目前的事实和局势为根据的。
    上述苏联、中华人民共和国和越南民主共和国的外交部长提出的主张可以作为日内瓦会议为实现停战和恢复印度支那和平问题而进行的讨论的适当基础。
