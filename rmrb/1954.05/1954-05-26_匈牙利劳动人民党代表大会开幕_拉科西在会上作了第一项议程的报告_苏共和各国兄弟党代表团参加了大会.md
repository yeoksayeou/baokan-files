Title: Hungarian Working People's Party Congress Opens; Rakosi Delivers Report on the First Item on the Agenda; Delegations from the Soviet Communist Party and Fraternal Parties of Various Countries Attend the Congress
Author:
Date: 1954-05-26
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, May 25th: According to TASS, the Third Congress of the Hungarian Working People's Party opened in Budapest on the 24th. The delegation of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, headed by Voroshilov, and delegations from the Communist and Workers' parties of more than twenty other countries attended the congress.

　　The opening ceremony of the congress was presided over by I. Nagy, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party. He suggested that all delegates to the congress stand and observe a moment of silence for Comrade Stalin, the successor to Lenin's cause, and Comrade Gottwald, a prominent activist in the international workers' movement.

　　In his opening remarks, I. Nagy pointed out: Under the leadership of the Hungarian Working People's Party, the Hungarian people are confidently moving towards socialism. Every delegate to the Third Party Congress is filled with determination to further strengthen the unity of our people and the unity of our party.

　　This congress is being held at a time when the cause of defending peace and the struggle against instigators of war are being waged with particular intensity. He said: Our party is filled with the determination to march in the ranks of the peace camp led by the Soviet Union and to fight for the relaxation of international tensions and for the cause of peace.

　　The congress elected a presidium of thirty-five people. Amidst stormy applause, the delegation of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the delegations of other fraternal Communist and Workers' parties were invited to take their seats on the presidium. Subsequently, the congress elected the working bodies of the congress.

　　The congress adopted the following agenda:

　　1. Report on the summary of the work of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party and the tasks of the Hungarian Working People's Party, presented by Comrade M. Rakosi;

　　2. Tasks of state organs and local people's councils, presented by Comrade I. Nagy;

　　3. Report on the summary of the work of the Central Auditing Committee, presented by Comrade K. Kish;

　　4. Report on the revision of the Charter of the Hungarian Working People's Party, presented by Comrade L. Acs;

　　5. Election of the leading organs of the Hungarian Working People's Party.

　　After the agenda of the congress was adopted, M. Rakosi, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, delivered a report on the first item on the agenda.

### 匈牙利劳动人民党代表大会开幕  拉科西在会上作了第一项议程的报告  苏共和各国兄弟党代表团参加了大会

1954-05-26
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利劳动人民党代表大会开幕
　　拉科西在会上作了第一项议程的报告
    苏共和各国兄弟党代表团参加了大会
    【新华社二十五日讯】据塔斯社布达佩斯讯：匈牙利劳动人民党第三次代表大会二十四日在布达佩斯开幕。以伏罗希洛夫为首的苏联共产党代表团，以及其他二十多个国家的共产党和工人党的代表团参加了大会。
    大会开幕式由匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会政治局委员伊·纳吉主持。他建议大会全体代表起立，为列宁事业的继承者斯大林同志和国际工人运动的著名活动家哥特瓦尔德同志默哀。
    伊·纳吉在开幕词中指出：匈牙利人民在匈牙利劳动人民党的领导下正满怀信心地走向社会主义。第三次党代表大会的每一个代表都充满了决心来进一步加强我国人民的团结及我们党的团结。
    这次代表大会是在保卫和平事业和反对战争挑拨者的斗争进行得特别激烈的时候召开的。他说：我们党充满了决心，要走在以苏联为首的和平阵营的行列中，为缓和国际紧张局势、为和平事业而斗争。
    大会选出了三十五人组成大会主席团。在暴风雨般的掌声中，苏联共产党代表团和其他兄弟共产党及工人党的代表团被邀请到主席台上就座。接着，大会选出了代表大会的工作机构。
  代表大会通过议程如下：
    一、匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会工作总结和匈牙利劳动人民党的任务的报告，报告人：马·拉科西同志；
    二、国家机关和地方人民会议的任务，报告人：伊·纳吉同志；
    三、中央检查委员会工作总结报告，报告人：卡·基什同志；
    四、关于修改匈牙利劳动人民党党章的报告，报告人：拉·阿契同志；
    五、选举匈牙利劳动人民党的领导机构。
    大会议程通过后，匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会第一书记马·拉科西在会上作了第一项议程的报告。
