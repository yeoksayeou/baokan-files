Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-05-05
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Local people's governments are focusing on establishing pumping stations to help farmers increase grain production. This is the newly built Yinjiang Pumping Station on the north bank of the Nantang River in Yinxian County, Ningbo Prefecture, Zhejiang Province, which can replace animal and human power for irrigating more than 7,000 mu of land north of the Nantang River.

### 我们伟大的祖国(图片)

1954-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    各地人民政府正在有重点地建立抽水机站，帮助农民提高粮食的产量。这是浙江宁波专区鄞县南塘河北面新建的鄞江抽水机站，能够代替南塘河以北七千余亩土地的畜力和人力的灌溉。
