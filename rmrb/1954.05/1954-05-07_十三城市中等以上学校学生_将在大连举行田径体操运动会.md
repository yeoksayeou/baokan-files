Title: Students from Secondary and Higher Education Institutions in Thirteen Cities to Hold Track and Field and Gymnastics Meet in Dalian
Author:
Date: 1954-05-07
Page: 3

Students from Secondary and Higher Education Institutions in Thirteen Cities
To Hold Track and Field and Gymnastics Meet in Dalian

    The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission has decided to hold the "1954 Track and Field and Gymnastics Meet for Students of Secondary and Higher Education Institutions in Thirteen Cities" in Dalian in August this year.
    The purpose of this conference is to implement Chairman Mao’s call to young people to be “good in health, good in study, and good in work,” to improve the physical health of students to increase learning efficiency, to cultivate the fine qualities of bravery, perseverance, agility, optimism, and diligence in the younger generation, and the spirit of collectivism, to prepare for labor and defend the motherland, and to promote the development of student sports and improve their technical level.
    The units participating in the competition include thirteen cities: Beijing, Tianjin, Lushun-Dalian, Shenyang, Harbin, Changchun, Shanghai, Nanjing, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Xi'an, Chongqing, and Chengdu.
    Currently, major cities are actively preparing for student sports meets, selecting their best student athletes to welcome the upcoming national competition. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 十三城市中等以上学校学生  将在大连举行田径体操运动会

1954-05-07
第3版()
专栏：

    十三城市中等以上学校学生
    将在大连举行田径体操运动会
    中央人民政府体育运动委员会决定在今年八月在大连举行“一九五四年十三城市中等以上学校学生田径、体操运动大会”。
    这次大会的目的是为了贯彻毛主席对青年“身体好、学习好、工作好”的号召，改善学生身体健康状况以提高学习效率，培养青年一代的勇敢、坚毅、机敏、乐观、勤劳等优良品质和集体主义精神，准备劳动和保卫祖国，推动学生体育运动的开展和提高其技术水平。
    参加竞赛的单位有北京、天津、旅大、沈阳、哈尔滨、长春、上海、南京、武汉、广州、西安、重庆和成都等十三城市。
    目前，各大城市正积极筹备召开学生运动会，选拔出自己最优秀的学生运动员来迎接即将到来的全国性竞赛大会。　（新华社）
