Title: China and North Korea Sign Agreement on Exchange Rate Between Renminbi and Korean Won
Author:
Date: 1954-05-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 20th: Of great significance for the development of economic cooperation between China and North Korea, the agreement between the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea on the exchange rate between Renminbi and Korean Won, and the protocol on border currency exchange and the protocol on non-trade remittances between the People's Bank of China and the Central Bank of North Korea, were signed in Beijing on May 20th.

Signing on behalf of the Chinese side was Cao Juru, Vice President of the Head Office of the People's Bank of China; signing on behalf of the Korean side were Li Changchun, Vice Minister of Finance of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and Kim Jae-pil, Vice Governor of the Central Bank of North Korea.

Participating in the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were: Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Wu Bo, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Finance, Hu Renkui, Deputy Director of the General Administration of Customs of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, Shang Ming, Director of the General Office of the People's Bank of China, He Ying, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Zhan Wu, Director of the Foreign Business Bureau of the People's Bank of China, and Commissioner Chen Dabang. On the Korean side were: Choi Il, Korean Ambassador to China, and Kim Kuk-bo, Counselor of the Embassy. After the signing, Cao Juru hosted a banquet.

### 中朝两国签订人民币与朝鲜币比价的协定

1954-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

    中朝两国签订人民币与朝鲜币比价的协定
    【新华社二十日讯】对于发展中朝两国经济合作有重要意义的中华人民共和国中央人民政府与朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府关于人民币与朝鲜币比价的协定，中国人民银行与北朝鲜中央银行关于边境货币兑换议定书与非贸易汇兑议定书，已于五月二十日在北京签字。
    代表中国方面签字的是：中央人民政府中国人民银行总行副行长曹菊如；代表朝鲜方面签字的是：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁财政省副相李长春、北朝鲜中央银行副理事长金在弼。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有：中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫，财政部副部长吴波，对外贸易部海关总署副署长胡仁奎，中国人民银行办公厅主任尚明，外交部亚洲司副司长何英，外交部交际处副处长王拓，中国人民银行国外业务局局长詹武、专员陈达邦。朝鲜方面有：朝鲜驻华大使崔一、大使馆参赞金国辅等。签字毕，由曹菊如设宴招待。
