Title: Indian Ambassador Nedyam Raghavan Hosts Banquet for Chinese Government Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1954-05-04
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, May 3rd: To celebrate the success of the Sino-Indian negotiations on relations concerning the Tibetan region of China, Indian Ambassador and head of the Indian government delegation, Nedyam Raghavan, hosted a banquet on April 30th for the Chinese government delegation. Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, He Ying, Deputy Director of the Asian Department, and Yang Gongsu, Assistant in Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government's Representative in Tibet, were invited to attend the banquet.
    Also invited to the banquet were Lai Yali, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department.
    The Indian side attending the banquet included representatives from the Indian government delegation and personnel from the Indian Embassy in China.

### 印度大使赖嘉文设宴招待我政府代表团

1954-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

    印度大使赖嘉文设宴招待我政府代表团
    【新华社三日讯】为了庆祝中印两国关于在中国西藏地方的关系问题的谈判获得成功，印度政府代表团团长赖嘉文大使曾在四月三十日设宴招待中国政府代表团，我中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫、亚洲司副司长何英、中央人民政府驻西藏代表外事帮办杨公素等应邀参加了宴会。
    应邀参加宴会的还有：外交部办公厅副主任赖亚力、交际处副处长沈平等。
    印度方面参加宴会的有印度政府代表团代表和印度驻华大使馆人员。
