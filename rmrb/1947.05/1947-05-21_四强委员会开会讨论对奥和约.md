Title: Four-Power Committee Meets to Discuss Austrian Treaty
Author:
Date: 1947-05-21
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 17th—According to TASS, Vienna: The Four-Power Committee, established by the Moscow Foreign Ministers Conference to discuss the unresolved clauses in the draft Austrian treaty, began its meeting in Vienna on the 12th. The second meeting was held on the 13th, chaired by the Soviet representative Novikov. The main topic of the meeting was the work of the committee of experts. Representatives from the Soviet Union, Britain, and France all put forward proposals. After heated debate, it was decided that the representatives would meet again on May 15th for further discussion.

### 四强委员会开会讨论对奥和约

1947-05-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　四强委员会开会讨论对奥和约
    【新华社陕北十七日电】据塔斯社维也纳讯：莫斯科外长会议所决定的讨论对奥和约草案中未获协议条款之四强委员会，于十二日开始在维也纳举行。十三日举行第二次会议，由苏联代表诺维科夫主席，会议讨论主题为专家委员会之工作。苏英法代表均有提案，经激辩后，决定各代表于五月十五日再行开会研讨。
