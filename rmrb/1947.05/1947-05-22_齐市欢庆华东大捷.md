Title: Qiqihar Celebrates the Great Victory in East China
Author:
Date: 1947-05-22
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, West Manchuria, 20th: Upon hearing the news that the East China People's Liberation Army had annihilated the entire three brigades of the 74th Division, a main force of the Chiang Kai-shek army, in Menglianggu, the people of Qiqihar celebrated joyously. A special edition of the West Manchuria Daily quickly spread throughout the city. The municipal government, the Joint Middle School, and various elementary schools posted victory bulletins. Telephone operators were overwhelmed with inquiries about the victory news. At 12 noon, the city of Qiqihar sounded its sirens. All government offices and shops throughout the city simultaneously raised flags and fired cannons in celebration. Elementary school students, along with workers, members of the People's Liberation Army, labor union members, and military bands conducted street propaganda. People's faces radiated with the joy of victory.

### 齐市欢庆华东大捷

1947-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　齐市欢庆华东大捷
    【新华社西满二十日电】华东人民解放军在孟良崮歼灭蒋军主力之七十四师三个旅全部，捷报传来，齐齐哈尔市人民欢呼庆祝，西满日报号外迅速传遍全市。市政府，联中及各小学纷纷张贴捷报。传达员询问胜利消息的电话忙碌异常。正午十二时，齐市上空高鸣汽笛。全市各机关商店均同时悬旗鸣炮庆祝。各小学学生及军文区工团军乐队进行街头宣传。人们脸上荡漾着胜利的欢笑。
