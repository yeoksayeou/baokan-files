Title: Anniversary Congratulatory Message for the People's Daily
Author: Liu Ziju
Date: 1947-05-15
Page: 4

　　After the People's Daily was founded,
　　May 15th is the anniversary.
　　The newspaper is launching a commemoration,
　　Soliciting opinions from readers.
　　Criticism and suggestions require expertise,
　　I am a layman when it comes to running a newspaper.
　　Now everyone is talking about quick rhythmic talk,
　　I'll also learn a thing or two from talented elder brothers.

　　Among all kinds of spiritual food,
　　The newspaper should occupy the first place.
　　Large circulation, far spread,
　　Reaching large and small villages.
　　News is fast, stories are fresh,
　　Published daily without interruption.
　　Printing is clear, writing is simple,
　　Literate men and women can all read.
　　Postage is low, price is cheap,
　　It's really convenient without spending much money.

　　Reading the newspaper every day becomes a habit,
　　Not reading is like not eating.
　　The benefits of newspapers are endless,
　　Its role is even more obvious.
　　It can educate, it can publicize,
　　Organization and leadership are not trivial!

　　The policy of propaganda and education,
　　I will also mention it here:
　　The purpose of propaganda and education,
　　Is to strengthen unity and fighting spirit.
　　In our People's Liberation Area,
　　Everything is bright.
　　If there are individual shortcomings,
　　They are also flaws in the jade.
　　If you compare the advantages with the disadvantages,
　　It's like a drop in the ocean;
　　If you insist on using that one drop as the topic,
　　It's like splitting hairs to find faults.

　　Now the shortcomings of the people,
　　Should all be blamed on Chiang Kai-shek.
　　What should we choose to praise or expose?
　　Chairman Mao already has instructions.
　　The meaning of praise is encouragement,
　　The purpose of exposure is to attack.
　　The enemy should be attacked,
　　Workers and peasants should be encouraged.
　　We should encourage instead of attacking,
　　The enemy will definitely laugh.

　　Why is the enemy laughing,
　　Laughing at myself for scolding myself.
　　If you don't believe what I said,
　　The "Wild Lily" incident is a precedent.
　　How to deal with the shortcomings of the people,
　　Persuasion and enlightenment to educate.
　　When he realizes it himself,
　　Self-criticism is more beneficial.
　　Everyone likes to make progress,
　　Who would willingly abandon oneself?
　　Boldly praise the advantages,
　　Shortcomings are easily removed by comparison.

　　This method is very good!
　　Look to the good to learn.
　　What to publicize and educate?
　　Reasonable, beneficial and powerful:
　　Reasonable is to talk about policies,
　　Beneficial is like dividing land.
　　In all kinds of revolutionary struggles,
　　Only with strength can we win.
　　Speaking must also be restrained,
　　Ensure that words and deeds are consistent.
　　Only those fascists,
　　Rely on boasting to eat.

　　The newspaper in organization and leadership,
　　Is equal to many inspection teams.
　　Conveying the instructions of the superiors,
　　It is called the throat and tongue, not in vain.
　　Reflecting the opinions below,
　　Like a glass mirror.
　　After each movement is decided,
　　It undertakes the call for propaganda.
　　When everyone understands it,
　　It will be done enthusiastically.

　　Typical introductions have great effects,
　　The demonstration effect is extraordinary.
　　Absorb, summarize and transmit experience,
　　The progress of cadres and the masses has its roots.
　　All kinds of labor models are on the newspaper,
　　Everyone is like winning the top prize in the imperial examination.
　　The more encouraged, the more motivated,
　　Promoting work further forward.
　　Who advocated this method?
　　Chairman Mao told us.

　　What is the main content of the newspaper,
　　It changes according to time and place;
　　The current center is three things,
　　Fighting, turning over, and production.
　　Even if we only talk about these three things,
　　The importance and urgency are not the same.
　　Egalitarianism cannot be committed,
　　There should always be a focus.

　　The newspaper must be popular,
　　Workers and peasants can understand it.
　　We must be familiar with the lives of the people,
　　The language of the people must be accessible.
　　Familiarity is necessary to avoid being a layman,
　　Accessibility is necessary to use it skillfully.
　　Words must be familiar, sentences must be short,
　　Punctuation marks should be simple.
　　Men, women, old and young can understand,
　　To penetrate into the folk.

　　How to carry out the mass line,
　　We still have to ask Chairman Mao for instructions.
　　"From the masses, to the masses,"
　　Comprehend and apply it thoroughly.
　　"From" relies entirely on the communication network,
　　"To" relies on the post office.

　　What is the standard of good and bad?
　　Class stand is the first.
　　What is the main content and form?
　　Technology serves politics.
　　We must be vigilant against ideological poisoning,
　　We must not be numb and indifferent.

　　If you want to run the newspaper well,
　　Many issues need attention:
　　News must be reviewed,
　　Editorials must be "machine-started."
　　The content must of course be discussed,
　　The writing must also be considered.
　　Thousands of words are flawed,
　　Being self-righteous is not allowed.

　　After various manuscripts are drafted,
　　Adding, deleting and modifying are necessary.
　　Even the title and layout,
　　Everything cannot be taken lightly.
　　Position, front and back, font size,
　　Indicate importance, urgency and slowness.
　　Printing technology needs to be improved,
　　Blurring and unclearness can lead to big mistakes.
　　Proofreaders must be careful,
　　Mistakes, omissions and reversals violate discipline.

　　Whether articles or news,
　　We must connect theory with practice.
　　Leaders should be responsible,
　　Personally use their eyes and pens.
　　Who established this rule?
　　Marx, Engels, Lenin and Chairman Mao.

　　If you want to run the Party newspaper well,
　　It is really not easy.
　　The responsibility of the whole Party is not enough,
　　The people must contribute together.
　　The power of the people is infinite,
　　The future is bright and promising.
　　The People's Daily belongs to the people,
　　To seek benefits for the people.
　　I'll stop here,
　　To express congratulations and wish efforts.

### 人民日报周年纪念祝词
刘子久
1947-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　人民日报周年纪念祝词
    刘子久
    人民日报创刊后，
    五月十五是周年。
    报社发起来纪念，
    征求读者的意见。
    批评建议须内行，
    办报俺是门外汉。
    而今都兴说快板，
    俺也来向有才大哥学一番。
    各种精神食粮内，
    报纸要占第一端。
    发行多，传播远，
    大小村庄都普遍。
    消息灵，故事鲜，
    天天出版不问断。
    印刷清，文字浅，
    识字男女都能看。
    邮费低，定价廉，
    花钱不多真方便。
    每天看报成习惯，
    不看就像没吃饭。
    报纸好处说不尽，
    它的作用更显然。
    能教育，能宣传，
    组织领导非等闲！
    宣传教育的政策，
    也在这里提一番：
    宣传教育的目的，
    加强团结与斗志。
    在我人民解放区，
    样样都是光明的。
    若有个别的缺点，
    也是美中的不足。
    若拿优点比缺点，
    好似沧海之一粟；
    硬拿一粟作论题，
    就是吹毛去求疵。
    现在人民的缺点，
    都应归罪蒋介石。
    歌颂暴露何所择？
    毛主席早已有指示。
    歌颂意义是鼓励，
    暴露目的是打击。
    敌人应该受打击，
    工农应该受鼓励。
    应该鼓励的反打击，
    敌人一定笑嘻嘻。
    敌人为何笑嘻嘻，
    笑我自己骂自己。
    我说这话你不信，
    “野百合花”有前例。
    人民缺点如何办，
    说服启发去教育。
    等他自己觉悟了，
    自我批评更有益。
    人人都喜欢上进，
    谁甘自暴与自弃？
    优点大胆去表扬，
    缺点相形易革去。
    这种方式为甚好！
    向着好的去看齐。
    宣传教育些什么？
    有理有利与有力：
    有理就是讲政策，
    有利好比分土地。
    各种革命斗争中，
    有力才能够胜利。
    说话还须有节制，
    保证言行要齐一。
    只有那些法西斯，
    吃饭专靠吹牛皮。
    报在组织领导上，
    等于许多巡视团。
    传达上级的指示，
    号称喉舌不虚传。
    反映下面的意见，
    如同玻璃镜一般。
    每个运动决定后，
    号召宣传它承担。
    等到人人懂得了，
    就会搞得热火朝天。
    典型介绍效果大，
    示范作用不平凡。
    吸收总结传经验，
    干群进步有根源。
    各种劳模上了报，
    个个好比中状元。
    越鼓励来越加油，
    推动工作更向前。
    这种办法谁提倡，
    毛主席来告诉咱。
    报纸内容何为主，
    因时因地有变换；
    当前中心三件事，
    打仗翻身和生产。
    就是单说这三件，
    轻重缓急不一般。
    平均主义不能犯，
    经常应该有重点。
    报纸必须大众化，
    工农才能解得下。
    人民生活要熟悉，
    人民语言要通达。
    熟悉才能不外行，
    通达运用才熟练。
    字要熟，句要短，
    标点符号要简单。
    男女老幼能听懂，
    才能深入到民间。
    群众路线怎搞起，
    还得请示毛主席。
    “从群众中来，到去群众中”，
    领会运用要彻底。
    “从来”全凭通讯网，
    “到去”依靠邮政局。
    好坏以何为标准？
    阶级立场是第一。
    内容形式何为主，
    技术服务于政治。
    思想放毒要警惕，
    麻木不仁要不的。
    想把报纸办得好，
    许多问题要注意：
    新闻固须要审查，
    社论更要“开机器”。
    内容当然要讨论，
    文字也须加考虑。
    下笔千言有毛病，
    自以为是要不的。
    各种稿件草完后，
    增减删改是必须。
    就是标题与排版，
    样样都不能儿戏。
    地位前后字大小，
    表示轻重与缓急。
    印刷技术要提高，
    模糊不清误大事。
    校对人员须仔细，
    错漏颠倒犯纪律。
    无论文章和消息，
    要联系理论与实际。
    首长应当要负责，
    亲自动眼又动笔。
    这种规矩谁建立，
    马恩列斯毛主席。
    党报要想办得好，
    的的确确不容易。
    全党负责还不够，
    必须人民齐出力。
    人民力量无限大，
    前途光明有出息。
    人民日报是人民的，
    为了人民谋利益。
    诌到这里就打住，
    略表庆贺祝努力。
