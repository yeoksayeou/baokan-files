Title: Local Armed Forces of the Third Military Subdistrict of Luzhong Annihilate Over Two Thousand Enemies in Two Months
Author:
Date: 1947-05-24
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shandong, 21st: The local armed forces of the Third Military Subdistrict of Luzhong persisted in fighting alongside the main forces on the periphery in 146 engagements in March and April, killing 351 enemies, wounding 372 enemies, capturing 1,374 enemies, and seizing five 82mm mortars, six 60mm guns, two 75mm field guns, two bazookas, one grenade launcher, one hand cannon, 68 light and heavy machine guns, 58 submachine guns, four Thompson submachine guns, and 40 million in Kuomintang currency.

### 鲁中三分区地方兵团  两月歼敌二千余

1947-05-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　鲁中三分区地方兵团
    两月歼敌二千余
    【新华社山东二十一日电】鲁中三分区地方兵团三、四两月份坚持边沿配合主力共作战一百四十六次，毙敌三百五十一名，伤敌三百七十二名，俘敌一千三百七十四名，缴获八二迫击炮五门，六零炮六门，七五钢炮二门，火箭筒两个，掷弹筒一个，手炮一门，轻重机枪六十八挺，冲锋枪五十八支，汤姆式四支，蒋币四千万元。
