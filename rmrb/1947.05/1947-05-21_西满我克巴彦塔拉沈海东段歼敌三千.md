Title: West Manchuria, Woke, Bayantala, and the Eastern Section of the Shenhai Line Annihilate 3,000 Enemies
Author:
Date: 1947-05-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 19th (Delayed Dispatch): Military News: A part of the West Manchuria Democratic Allied Army recovered Bayantala, an important town 40 miles northwest of Zhengjiatun, i.e. Wendulu Wangfu, on the 14th. The defending Chiang troops fled in panic.
Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 19th: In the battle to recover Shuangshan City on the 15th, the West Manchuria Democratic Allied Army killed 43 Chiang soldiers including company commanders and below, captured 312 including Wu Zubo, deputy head of the 259th regiment of the 87th Division, seized 2 eight-one mortars, 3 anti-tank guns, 22 light and heavy machine guns, 1331 long and short guns, 36 submachine guns, 15,000 rounds of ammunition, 1 jeep, and 36 mules and horses.
Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 19th: The Liaodong Democratic Allied Army's self-defense counterattack on the eastern section of the Shen (Yang) Hai (Long) line, after a day and night of fierce fighting from the 13th to the 14th, has controlled more than 100 li of the railway line. It is reported that the strongholds of Shanchengzhen, Caoshi, and Heishantou stations have been captured. According to preliminary statistics, this campaign captured over 2,700 Chiang soldiers including Wan Binglin, deputy head of the 552nd regiment of the 184th Division, and Shi Zaikuan, battalion commander, killed and wounded over 300 people, seized 8 mortars, 10 sixty-mm guns, 3 rocket launchers, 36 heavy machine guns, 86 light machine guns, 1335 rifles, 33 short guns, 1 anti-tank rifle, 10 cars, and over 1 million catties of grain.
Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 19th: A certain unit of the Democratic Allied Army recovered the small Lashanzi stronghold near Jilin City on the 15th, annihilating over 300 enemies. On the 16th, the enemy dispatched a regiment to counterattack, firing over a thousand shells, but the Democratic Allied Army's position remained unshaken.

### 西满我克巴彦塔拉沈海东段歼敌三千

1947-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　西满我克巴彦塔拉沈海东段歼敌三千
    【新华社东北十九日电】（迟到）军息：西满民主联军一部，于十四日收复郑家屯西北四十里之重镇巴彦塔拉即温都噜王府，守城蒋军狼狈逃窜。
    【新华社东北十九日电】西满民主联军在十五日收复双山城战斗中，计毙蒋军连长以下四十三人，俘八十七师二五九团副团长吴祖伯以下三百一十二名，缴获八一迫击炮二门，战防炮三门，轻重机枪二十二挺，长短枪一三一支，冲锋式机枪三十六挺，弹药一万五千发，吉普车一辆，骡马三十六匹。
    【新华社东北十九日电】辽东民主联军在沈（阳）海（龙）线东段之自卫反击，经十三日至十四日一昼夜激战，已控制铁路线百余里。计攻克山城镇、草市、黑山头等车站据点。据初步统计，是役计俘蒋军一八四师五五二团副团长万炳麟、营长史在宽以下二千七百余名，毙伤三百余人。缴获迫击炮八门，六零炮十门，火箭炮三门，重机枪三六挺，轻机枪八六挺，步枪一三三五支，短枪三十三支，战防枪一支，汽车十辆，粮食一百余万斤。
    【新华社东北十九日电】民主联军某部十五日收复吉林城附近之小拉山子据点，歼敌三百余人。十六日敌出动一个团反扑，发炮千余发，而民主联军阵地屹然未动。
