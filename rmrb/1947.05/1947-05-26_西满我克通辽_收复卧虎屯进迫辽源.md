Title: Western Manchuria Recaptures Tongliao, Recovers Wohutun and Advances on Liaoyuan
Author:
Date: 1947-05-26
Page: 1

　　Western Manchuria Recaptures Tongliao
  　Recovers Wohutun and Advances on Liaoyuan
    [Xinhua News Agency, Western Manchuria, Urgent Telegram, 23rd] A unit of the Western Manchurian Democratic Allied Army, at 3 PM on the 22nd, once again liberated Tongliao County, an important town in Western Manchuria, annihilating a portion of the enemy.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northeast Front, 23rd] In the Zhengjiatun (Liaoyuan) area, a unit of the Northeast Democratic Allied Army has recovered Wohutun (over 30 *li* north of Zhengjiatun) railway station, an important stronghold of the enemy. The defending enemy burned the trains gathered at the station and fled towards Zhengjiatun. The Democratic Allied Army is pursuing them and has now advanced on the city.

### 西满我克通辽　收复卧虎屯进迫辽源

1947-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　西满我克通辽
  　收复卧虎屯进迫辽源
    【新华社西满二十三日急电】西满民主联军一部，于二十二日下午三时再度光复西满重镇通辽县城，歼敌一部。
    【新华社东北前线二十三日电】郑家屯（辽源）方面，东北民主联军一部已收复敌重要据点卧虎屯（郑家屯以北三十余里）车站，守敌将停集该站之火车焚毁，向郑家屯逃窜；民主联军跟踪追击，现已进迫该城。
