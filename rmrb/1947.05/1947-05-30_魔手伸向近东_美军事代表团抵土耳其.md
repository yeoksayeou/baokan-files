Title: Sinister Hands Reach the Near East: U.S. Military Delegation Arrives in Turkey
Author:
Date: 1947-05-30
Page: 3

　　Sinister Hands Reach the Near East
  　U.S. Military Delegation Arrives in Turkey
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, May 26th: Ankara dispatch: A joint U.S. Army and Navy delegation executing Truman's "aid" bill arrived here on the evening of the 22nd. The head of the delegation is Army Group Leader Oliver. Their arrival has made Ankara very nervous.
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, May 26th: TASS Cairo dispatch: Reports state that Egypt has signed an agreement with Britain to purchase a large number of firearms, which will be handed over to the Egyptian authorities in Alexandria. However, when the ships carrying the firearms arrived at the port, they were ordered to continue sailing to Turkey. This practice has caused dissatisfaction among the Egyptian military authorities. Last week, three cargo ships fully loaded with British tanks, armored vehicles, and other items left Port Said for Turkey, and the dissatisfaction of the Egyptian military authorities continues to increase.

### 魔手伸向近东　美军事代表团抵土耳其

1947-05-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　魔手伸向近东
  　美军事代表团抵土耳其
    【新华社陕北二十六日电】安哥拉讯：执行杜鲁门“援助”法案的美陆海军联合代表团，于二十二日晚抵达此间，代表团团长为奥立佛莱陆军组长。彼等此来使安哥拉甚为紧张。
    【新华社陕北二十六日电】塔斯社开罗讯：报载埃及已与英国签订购买大批枪炮之协定，该项枪炮将在亚历山大港交与埃及当局。但是当载运枪炮之船只抵达该港时，又奉命继续驶往土耳其。此一做法已引起埃及军事当局之不满。上周又有满载英国坦克，装甲车等物之货船三艘离开塞德港驶赴土耳其，埃军当局之不满继续增加。
