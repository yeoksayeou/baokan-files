Title: Our Army Counterattacks West of Jinan, Liberates Yancheng, Annihilates 2,000 Enemies
Author:
Date: 1947-05-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, East China Front, 16th: A unit of the East China People's Liberation Army victoriously captured Yancheng, an important stronghold on the Jinan-Yucheng section of the Jinpu Railway, on the afternoon of the 13th. The 1,600-strong Shandong Security Brigade 5th Regiment under the command of the Chiang Kai-shek puppet army were almost completely wiped out. Over a thousand officers and soldiers, including Li Daxiang, were captured alive. Three mortars, three heavy machine guns, and over a thousand rifles were seized.
Xinhua News Agency, East China Front, 16th: A unit of the East China People's Liberation Army counterattacked the Chiang Kai-shek puppet troops in the area south of Changqing on the 5th. After a day and night of fighting, over 700 Chiang Kai-shek soldiers were captured alive, along with 21 light machine guns and over 500 rifles.

### 我军反击济南西侧解放晏城歼敌二千

1947-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　我军反击济南西侧解放晏城歼敌二千
    【新华社华东前线十六日电】华东人民解放军某部于十三日下午一举攻克津浦线上济南禹城间重要据点晏城，驻守蒋伪军山东保安大队第五团李达祥部一千六百余就歼，生俘李达祥以下官兵千余名，缴获迫击炮三门，重机枪三挺，步枪千余支。
    【新华社华东前线十六日电】华东人民解放军一部于五日反击长清以南地区蒋伪，战斗历一昼夜，生俘蒋军七百余名，缴获轻机枪二十一挺，步枪五百余支。
