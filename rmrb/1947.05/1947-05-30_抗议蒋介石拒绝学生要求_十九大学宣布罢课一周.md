Title: Protesting Chiang Kai-shek's Rejection of Student Demands, Nineteen Universities Announce One-Week Strike
Author:
Date: 1947-05-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, May 28th: Shanghai Report: This city's Ta Kung Pao reports that more than 30 professors from Yenching University in Peiping issued a declaration on May 25th, expressing sympathy and support for the statement of professors from Peking University. The declaration stated: They are willing to join students in fighting against hunger and civil war; professors from Peking University and Yenching University will jointly issue a declaration soon, making statements on the current situation in China. Nineteen universities in the five regions of Beijing, Shanghai, Jiangsu, Zhejiang, and Henan have announced a one-week strike starting on May 26th to protest Chiang Kai-shek's continued civil war and rejection of student demands. Furthermore, universities in Peiping and Tianjin, Nanchang Chung Cheng University, Shenyang Northeast University, and other schools continue their strikes. Another dispatch: The U.S. Military Advisory Group in China, in view of the continuing surge of the Chinese student movement, has specifically ordered all U.S. military personnel stationed in Nanjing, Shanghai, Peiping, Tianjin, and Qingdao to remain in their barracks and not leave without permission from superiors.

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, May 28th: Shanghai News: Wenhui Bao, United Evening News, and Xinmin Evening News were ordered to cease publication by Chiang Kai-shek's Songhu Garrison Headquarters. Within two hours, their printing plants and offices were occupied by military police. Shanghai's other famous newspaper, Tie Bao, was also ordered to cease publication for revealing that Chiang Kai-shek had approved this matter.

### 抗议蒋介石拒绝学生要求　十九大学宣布罢课一周

1947-05-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　抗议蒋介石拒绝学生要求
  　十九大学宣布罢课一周
    【新华社陕北二十八日电】沪讯：此间大公报载：北平燕京大学教授三十余人于五月二十五日发表宣言，表示同情与支持北京大学教授之声明，宣言称：他们愿与学生合作为反饥饿反内战而奋斗；北京大学及燕京大学教授最近将联合发表宣言，对中国当前局势发展，有所陈述。京、沪、苏、浙、豫五区十九个大学已宣布自五月二十六日起罢课一周，对蒋介石之继续内战及拒绝学生要求，表示抗议。又平津各大学、南昌中正大学、沈阳东北大学等校仍继续罢课。另讯：美国驻华军事顾问团，鉴于中国学生运动继续高涨，特下令南京、上海、北平、天津、青岛各地美军全体人员守在兵营里，非有上级允许不得擅自外出。
    【新华社陕北二十八日电】沪息：文汇报、联合晚报，及新民晚报——被蒋介石之淞沪警备司令部勒令停刊后，两小时内其印厂、办事处等即为军警所占据，上海之另一著名报纸《铁报》，因透露蒋介石曾批准此事，也被勒令停刊。
