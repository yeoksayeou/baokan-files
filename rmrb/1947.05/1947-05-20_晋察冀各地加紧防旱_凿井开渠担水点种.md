Title: Jin-Cha-Ji Areas Intensify Drought Prevention: Digging Wells, Opening Channels, Carrying Water for Planting

Author:
Date: 1947-05-20
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji, May 17th: Due to lack of rain, drought conditions have appeared in various parts of the Jin-Cha-Ji region. The entire region is launching a drought prevention campaign. After the Rongzhen Canal in Qu Yang County released water, they are now rushing to irrigate wheat fields and autumn fields. Farmers in Renqiu, Hejian, Dacheng, Gaoyang, and other places in Central Hebei are digging wells on a large scale and using waterwheels for irrigation. Farmers in the mountainous areas of Southern Chahar and Western Hebei are carrying water for planting.

### 晋察冀各地加紧防旱  凿井开渠担水点种

1947-05-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　晋察冀各地加紧防旱
    凿井开渠担水点种
    【新华社晋察冀十七日电】晋察冀各地缺雨，已呈旱象，全区正展开防旱运动。曲阳县之荣臻大渠放水后，现正赶灌麦田与秋田。冀中任邱、河间、大城、高阳等地农民正大批凿井，利用水车灌溉。察南、冀晋山地农民则纷纷担水点种。
