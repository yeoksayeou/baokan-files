Title: Over a Hundred Professors from Peking University and Tsinghua University Comfort and Support Demonstrating Students
Author:
Date: 1947-05-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 23rd. A Shanghai dispatch of the 22nd reports: This paper carries news that sixty-two professors from National Peking University recently sent a letter to students who were marching and demonstrating against civil war and against hunger, expressing their comfort and encouragement. Over eighty professors from National Tsinghua University also sent a letter to the students expressing their support, and guaranteeing their support for their demands to immediately cease the civil war and resume peace talks.

### 北大清华百余教授慰勉声援示威学生

1947-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　北大清华百余教授慰勉声援示威学生
    【新华社陕北二十三日电】二十二日沪息，此间报载：国立北京大学六十二位教授，顷致函反内战反饥饿游行示威之学生表示慰勉。国立清华大学八十余教授亦致函学生表示声援，并保证支持彼等立即停止内战及恢复和谈之要求。
