Author:
Date: 1947-05-21
Page: 3

　　Chiang Controlled Area News Briefs
　　[Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 18th] According to an Associated Press dispatch from Shanghai on the 17th: In order to avoid unmanageable economic chaos and strengthen its control, the Chiang Kai-shek's Shanghai Municipal Government recently forced all Shanghai stores and companies to join trade associations, otherwise they would be ordered to close down and not be issued business licenses. This matter will arouse widespread resentment in Shanghai's commercial circles.
　　[Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 17th] Shanghai News: The Kuomintang Central Executive Committee announced on the 14th that Li Jishen had been expelled from the party because he vehemently criticized Chiang Kai-shek's traitorous civil war policy in Hong Kong.

### 蒋区零讯

1947-05-21
第3版()
专栏：蒋区零讯

　　蒋区零讯
    【新华社陕北十八日电】据美联社上海十七日电称：蒋记上海市政府为避免经济混乱的无法收拾并加强其控制，近迫令所有上海商店公司参加同业公会，否则即勒令停业，不发予营业证，此事将引起上海商业界普遍反感。
    【新华社陕北十七日电】沪息：国民党中央执行委员会已于十四日宣布开除李济琛氏党籍。因李氏在港猛烈抨击蒋介石的卖国内战政策。
