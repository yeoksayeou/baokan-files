Title: Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu Send a Congratulatory Telegram on Inner Mongolia's March Towards Brightness
Author:
Date: 1947-05-22
Page: 1
　
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 20th: Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu sent a telegram in reply to the Inner Mongolia People's Representative Conference. The original telegram is as follows: Dear representatives of the Inner Mongolia People's Representative Conference! Your telegram of May 17th has been received. The Inner Mongolian compatriots who have suffered so much are beginning to create a new history of freedom and brightness under your leadership. We believe that the Mongolian people will unite closely with the Han people and other ethnic groups in the country to strive for the elimination of national oppression and feudal oppression, and to build a new Mongolia and a new China. Congratulations on your victory.
    Mao Zedong
    Zhu De, May 19th

### 毛主席朱总司令电贺内蒙走向光明
毛主席  朱德
1947-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席朱总司令电贺内蒙走向光明
    【新华社陕北二十日电】毛主席、朱总司令顷电复内蒙人民代表大会。原电如下：亲爱的内蒙人民代表大会全体代表们！你们五月十七日来电收到了。曾经饱受困难的内蒙同胞，在你们领导之下，正在开始创造自由光明的新历史。我们相信：蒙古民族将与汉族和国内其他民族亲密团结，为着扫除民族压迫与封建压迫，建设新蒙古与新中国而奋斗。庆祝你们的胜利。
    毛泽东
    朱德  五月十九日
