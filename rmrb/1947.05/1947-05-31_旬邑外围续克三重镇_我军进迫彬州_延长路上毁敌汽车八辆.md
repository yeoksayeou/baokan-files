Title: Continuing to Capture Three Towns Outside Xunyi, Our Army Advances on Binzhou, Destroying Eight Enemy Vehicles on the Yan'an Road
Author:
Date: 1947-05-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi Front, May 29th: After liberating Xunyi County and the important town of Zhanghong, a unit of the Northwest People's Liberation Army took advantage of the victory to expand its gains in all directions. On the 23rd, it captured Taiyu, ten miles northwest of the city, and Shidian, twenty-five miles northeast of Binzhou. On the 25th, Longgao Town, forty miles southwest of Xunyi, was also liberated. A part of the Chiang Kai-shek and Hu Zongnan troops defending these towns were captured, and more than 500 shi of grain were seized. The Chiang Kai-shek and Hu Zongnan troops in Binzhou are now extremely alarmed.
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi Front, May 29th: Local guerrillas on the southern front of the Border Region have recently destroyed a total of eight transport vehicles belonging to Chiang Kai-shek and Hu Zongnan's army. On the 22nd, the Guanzhong guerrillas destroyed three enemy vehicles between Mazijie and Zhenjiawan, south of Fu County, killing and wounding four Chiang Kai-shek and Hu Zongnan soldiers. On the evening of the 24th, the Fu County guerrillas ambushed the enemy at Anzitou, which belongs to中部县 [unclear district name], destroying five enemy vehicles and killing and wounding seven enemy soldiers.
Xinhua News Agency, Northwest, May 29th: Commanders and fighters of a certain unit of the Northwest People's Liberation Army recently presented Peng Dehuai, Deputy Commander-in-Chief, and Xi Zhongxun, Political Commissar, with the spoils of victory seized in the Panlong battle as a token of appreciation. The gifts included a pair of shoes, three sets of shirts, five pairs of socks, a walking stick, a pair of binoculars, as well as canned goods, tea, sugar, and cigarettes. A letter of appreciation was also presented, which stated: "Under your wise leadership, we have become stronger and bigger with each battle. With your leadership, we fear nothing, and we will surely win the battle, eliminate Hu Zongnan, and liberate the Great Northwest." Commanders Peng and Xi immediately replied with letters of encouragement.

### 旬邑外围续克三重镇　我军进迫彬州　延长路上毁敌汽车八辆

1947-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　旬邑外围续克三重镇
  　我军进迫彬州
  　延长路上毁敌汽车八辆
    【新华社陕北前线二十九日电】西北人民解放军某部于解放旬邑县城及张洪重镇后，乘胜向四周扩大战果。二十三日一举攻克该城西北十里之太峪及彬州东北二十五里之世店两大镇。二十五日续解放旬邑西南四十里之龙高镇。俘虏守备各该镇蒋胡军一部，并缴获粮食五百余石。现彬州蒋胡军极感恐慌。
    【新华社陕北前线二十九日电】边区南线地方游击队，近日共击毁蒋胡军之运输汽车八辆。二十二日，关中游击队在富县南麻子街、真家湾间击毁敌汽车三辆，毙伤蒋胡军四名。二十四日晚，富县游击队在中部县属之安子头设伏，击毁敌汽车五辆，毙伤敌军七名。
    【新华社西北二十九日电】西北人民解放军某部指战员，顷以蟠龙战斗缴获之胜利品慰问彭德怀副总司令及习仲勋政委。计送给鞋一双，衬衣三套，袜子五双，手杖一根，望远镜一付，还有罐头、茶叶、糖、纸烟等。并呈送慰问信一封，信中称：“我们在你们英明领导下，已越打越强，越打越大。有你们领导，我们什么也不怕，一定打胜仗，把胡宗南消灭，解放大西北。”彭、习两首长当即回信予以鼓励。
