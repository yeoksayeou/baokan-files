Title: Changli Battle Annihilates 1,700 Enemies; Our Army Captures Jingou Station Northeast of Chaoyang
Author:
Date: 1947-05-30
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji, 28th: In the battle to recover Changli city on the Beining Railway on the 17th of this month, the Jidong People's Liberation Army annihilated the pseudo-Independent 3rd Division (formerly the puppet Manchukuo Punitive Force) headquarters, the Chiang Kai-shek county government, and Chiang's puppet forces stationed at the three stations of Liushouying, totaling 1,740 people. Among them, 1,273 were captured alive. They seized 33 light machine guns, 2,470 rifles and pistols, 24 small cannons, 100,000 rounds of various bullets, more than 62 million Chiang currency, and more than 10,000 bags of white flour. They also destroyed more than 40 pillboxes along the railway line, destroyed about 70 li of railway tracks, three bridges, two locomotives, and one train. After completing its mission, the unit took the initiative to evacuate the city on the night of the 19th.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Ji-Cha-Re-Liao, 28th: (Delayed) A unit of the Rehe People's Liberation Army continued its counterattack on the Jin-Cheng Railway. On the night of the 12th, they captured Taohuashan, 30 li northeast of Chaoyang, and Jingou Station on the railway northeast of Chaoyang. They also attacked the Sanbao Coal Mine, capturing 85 enemies, destroying one railway bridge, and recovering more than 4,000 catties of electric wire. On the night of the 13th, the unit recaptured Chuitouying Village (2 li east of Jingou) and Nengjia Station, capturing six enemies and destroying two wooden bridges on the Chaoyang Highway. There are now no electric lights in Chaoyang city at night. Furthermore, a unit of the People's Liberation Army, on the 11th, braved the rain to launch a surprise attack on Lingershijiazi in Chaoyang. A puppet army company in the area fled in desperation, and all the stronghold fortifications were completely destroyed.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji, 28th: A unit of the Ji-Re-Cha People's Liberation Army completely annihilated the 3rd Battalion of the 12th Regiment of the 4th Division of the Chiang army at Shanghuangqi, about 100 li northwest of Fengning in western Rehe, on the 17th.

### 昌黎战役歼敌千七百　朝阳东北我军攻克金沟车站

1947-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　昌黎战役歼敌千七百
  　朝阳东北我军攻克金沟车站
    【新华社晋察冀二十八日电】本月十七日收复北宁线昌黎城之役，冀东人民解放军歼灭驻守昌黎城内之伪独三师（原伪满洲国讨伐队）师部、蒋记县政府及驻守留守营等三车站之蒋伪共一千七百四十人。内生俘者一千二百七十三人。缴轻机枪三十三挺，长短枪二千四百七十支，小炮二十四门，各种子弹十万发，蒋币六千二百余万元，白面一万余袋。并摧毁铁路沿线碉堡四十余处。毁路轨约七十华里，桥梁三座，火车头两个，火车一列。该部于达成任务后，即于十九日夜主动撤离该城。
    【新华社冀察热辽二十八日电】（迟到）热河人民解放军一部续向锦承铁路反击。于十二日夜攻克朝阳东北三十里之桃花山，及朝阳东北铁路上之金沟车站。并袭击三宝煤矿。计俘敌八十五名，破铁桥一座，收回电线四千多斤。十三日夜，该部再克垂头营村（金沟东二里）及能家车站。俘敌六名，破朝阳公路上木桥两座。现朝阳城夜间已无电灯。又：人民解放军一部，十一日冒雨奔袭朝阳之另二十家子，该地伪军一个中队拚命逃窜，据点工事当被全部摧毁。
    【新华社晋察冀二十八日电】冀热察人民解放军某部，十七日于热西丰宁西北约百里之上黄旗地方，完全歼灭蒋军十三军第四师十二团之第三营。
