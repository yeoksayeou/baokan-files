Title: Everything for Victory at the Front! Cadres and Miscellaneous Staff of Various Agencies Continue to Practice Production and Thrift
Author: Meiling, Huamin, Huazhong, Jianye
Date: 1947-05-14
Page: 2

　　Everything for Victory at the Front!
　　Cadres and Miscellaneous Staff of Various Agencies
　　Continue to Practice Production and Thrift
　　[Our Newspaper's Comprehensive Report] Everything is for the victory at the front, and to quickly eliminate the Chiang army. All departments continue to implement the production and thrift movement. Production and thrift in the military region's directly subordinate units has become a conscious mass movement. All cadres, miscellaneous staff, female comrades with two or three young children, and convalescing cadres have participated in the movement. Their production methods include more than thirty types, such as spinning yarn, grinding tofu, sprouting soybeans, repairing houses, and repairing watches. According to a preliminary inspection of nine units, including the Headquarters, Political Department, and the Second Bureau, there are a total of 145 spinning wheels, with 228 participants, and 257.25 jin of thread have been spun. The Second Bureau is the best organized and has the greatest achievements. They have 30 spinning wheels, 70 participants, and have spun more than 80 jin of thread, and have all established mutual aid groups. The entire body of cadres of the Taiyue County Commissioner's Office, the Taiyue Post Office, the Industry and Tax Bureau, the First Commissioner's Office, and the Qinshui and Shimin County political and civilian agencies have all made specific plans for production and thrift. Female cadre Wang Yuhua and her husband from the Fourth Commissioner's Office donated all of their property, enough to cover the expenses of one soldier for the whole year, and she also wants to complete a 2,000 yuan public contribution task. The Joint Industry and Tax Bureau decided that each person would raise one pig, and the agency's production could earn 250,000 yuan throughout the year. The First Commissioner and Deputy Commissioner personally took the lead in carrying coal. County Magistrate Wang of Qinshui saved a year's worth of supplies, influencing many cadres to also save on clothing and dye expenses. In the past, personal difficulties relied on public solutions, but now they no longer request preferential treatment from the public and are determined to mobilize their families to produce. Cadres in the Fourth District of Gaoping saved 221,800 yuan in grain and vegetable funds in half a year to support the front, and are stepping up production for self-sufficiency. The Qinshui Chengguan School District resolved that each teacher would save half a dou of rice to support the front. The Changzi County Government formulated a plan to reduce the people's burden and support the front through production and thrift, and has now cultivated 31 mu of land. All cadres decided not to receive clothing and firewood expenses, which could save 1,770 chi of cloth, equivalent to 272,600 yuan in Jilu Yu     【本报综合讯】一切为了前线胜利，迅速消灭蒋军，各部继续贯彻生产节约运动。军区直属队生产节约已成为自觉的群众运动，全体干杂人员以及带有二、三个小孩子的女同志和休养干部都参加了运动。他们的生产办法有纺纱、磨豆腐、生豆芽、修房子、修表等三十种以上。据初步检查，司令部、政治部、二局等九个单位，共有纺车一百四十五辆，参加人数二百廿八个，已纺出线子二百五十七斤四两。以二局组织最好，成绩也最大，他们共有三十辆纺车，七十人参加，纺了八十余斤线子，并且都成立了互助组。太岳县专署、太岳邮局、工税局、一专署机关及沁水、士敏等县政民机关，全体干部均作出生产节约具体计划。四专署女干部王玉华夫妇献出自己全部财产，足供一个战士全年费用，她并要完成两千元交公任务。工税联合局决定每人养一口猪，机关生产全年可赚二十五万元。一专署正副专员亲自带头担炭。沁水王县长节约全年供给，影响许多干部也节约衣服和染料等费用。过去个人困难依靠公家解决，现在不再要求公家优待，决心动员家属生产。高平四区干部节约半年粮食菜金二十二万一千八百元支援前线，并加紧生产自给。沁水城关学区决议每个教员节约斗半米支援前线。长子县府制定生产节约减轻人民负担支援前线计划，现已垦种土地三十一亩。全体干部决定不领服装柴草费，可节约一千七百七十尺布，合冀钞二十七万二千六百元。冀鲁豫各地机关学校热烈开展节衣缩食支援前线运动。一专署各直属机关提出节省一粒米、一文钱、一滴油、一页纸运动。规定五人一灯，一两油点三夜。行署公安局、保干校学生用纺织、卷烟、做豆腐、生豆芽、做鞋底、编草帽等手工生产办法节约自给。荏平救联会一个交通员献米四百斤，火夫班长王秀林献米四百八十二斤。寿张邮局老火夫张传顺，本来他是不舍得多花一文钱的人，这次他自报奋勇献米二百五十斤。七十四岁参议员吴国卿先生将三个月的衣服、鞋袜、津贴、菜金、粮柴草料一律节约支援前线。冀南建国学院会计队，在生产节约支援前线号召下，队与队、组与组互相竞赛，决议每人每天节约一两米，全体学员鞋袜费一律支援前线。安阳政治处杂务人员精简后，人少了一倍，工作却做得比先更好了，特别在收复水冶后，往各区送信特别多，来往吃饭的人也特别多，通讯员伙夫同志不但没有丝毫怨言，把工作搞好后又种了三亩菜，伙夫来山更积极的去掀敌人的炮楼，得柴三千多斤，至少节省三万多元。
                                    （梅岭、化民、华中、建业）
