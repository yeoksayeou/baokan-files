Title: Auspicious Rain in Shandong
Author:
Date: 1947-05-20
Page: 1
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Shandong, 18th: On the evening of the 16th, abundant rain fell here, approximately three inches. The farmers joyfully told each other, predicting a bumper harvest.

### 山东喜雨

1947-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　山东喜雨
    【新华社山东十八日电】十六日黄昏，此间甘霖普降，约三寸许；农民欣喜相告，预卜丰收。
