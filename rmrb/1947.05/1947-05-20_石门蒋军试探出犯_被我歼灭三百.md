Title: Shijiazhuang Chiang Troops Test the Waters with an Attack; 300 Annihilated by Our Forces
Author:
Date: 1947-05-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji, 18th: On the 11th, the 94th Regiment of the 32nd Division of the 33rd Army of Chiang Kai-shek, along with a contingent of puppet troops numbering about 2,000, made a probing attack on the area of ​​Dongxi Yangling, more than ten miles southeast of Shizhuang. They were fiercely attacked by our army. After half an hour of fierce fighting, more than 300 enemy soldiers were killed or wounded, and the remnants fled in panic back to Baifo Village, fifteen miles east of Shizhuang.

### 石门蒋军试探出犯  被我歼灭三百

1947-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　石门蒋军试探出犯
    被我歼灭三百
    【新华社晋察冀十八日电】十一日上午，石庄蒋军第三十三十二师九十四团，配合伪军一部约二千人，试探出犯石庄东南十余里之东西洋陵一带，遭我军痛击，激战半小时，毙伤敌三百余名，残敌即仓皇窜回石庄东十五里之白佛村。
