Title: Britain Increasingly Impoverished, Tens of Thousands of Coal Miners Strike, Food Shortages Increase Social Unrest
Author:
Date: 1947-05-13
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 11th: Agence France-Presse, London dispatch: Recently, tens of thousands of British coal miners went on strike on the day the five-day work week was implemented. In Durham County, 30,000 miners went on strike, bringing production to a standstill in seventeen mines. In Lancashire, 2,000 miners went on strike, halting production in three mines. According to the strikers, the implementation of the five-day work week has resulted in an economic loss of approximately two pounds per week for them.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 10th: London dispatch: Britain is facing the difficulty of a food panic. Former British Coalition Cabinet Food Minister Woolton recently stated in the House of Lords that the future food crisis will reduce the physical strength required for national work and seriously increase social unrest. Woolton warned that the danger of Britain's increasing food shortages is no less serious than the coal shortage, and the government should frankly announce the extent of the danger.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 11th: Hamburg dispatch: In protest of food prices, workers in all factories in Hamburg held a large-scale strike on the 8th. Hamburg Radio stopped broadcasting, and trams also stopped running in the afternoon. Workers held large-scale demonstrations and marches, protesting the food policy of the British occupation authorities.

### 英国日益穷困  数万煤矿工人举行罢工  粮食不足增加社会不安

1947-05-13
第3版()
专栏：

　　英国日益穷困
    数万煤矿工人举行罢工
    粮食不足增加社会不安
    【新华社陕北十一日电】法国新闻社伦敦讯：最近英国数万煤矿工人于实行五日工作周之日举行罢工，在达哈谟郡三万矿工罢工，使十七个矿坑之生产陷于停顿。在郎卡郡则有二千矿工罢工，使三个矿坑停止生产。据罢工者称：实行五日工作周使他们每周在经济上受到两磅左右的损失。
    【新华社陕北十日电】伦敦讯：英国正面临粮食恐慌之困难。前英国联合内阁粮食部大臣乌尔顿顷在上院发言称：未来之粮食危机将减退全国工作所需之体力，并严重增加社会扰攘不安。乌氏提出警告谓：英国粮食日益不足的危险，将不下于煤荒之严重，政府应坦白公告危险的程度。
    【新华社陕北十一日电】汉堡讯：为抗议粮食价格，汉堡所有各工厂工人于八日举行大罢工。汉堡广播电台停止播音，电车亦于下午停驶。工人举行声势浩大的示威游行，抗议英占领当局的粮食政策。
