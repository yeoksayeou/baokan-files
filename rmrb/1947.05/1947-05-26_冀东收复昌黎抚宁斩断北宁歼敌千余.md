Title: Jidong Recovers Changli and Funing, Cuts Off Beining Railway, Annihilates Over 1,000 Enemies

Author:

Date: 1947-05-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji, Urgent Telegram, 24th: The Jidong People's Liberation Army successively recovered the two county towns of Changli and Funing on the 18th, cutting off the Beining Railway. At midnight on the 17th, a unit of the People's Liberation Army launched a self-defense counterattack west of Beidaihe on the vital North China artery, the Beining Railway. After fierce fighting until 4 p.m. on the 18th, they successively liberated Changli County town and the three stations of Zhangjiazhuang, Liushouying, and Houfengtai to its left and right, annihilating over 1,000 Chiang Kai-shek puppet troops, including a deputy regimental commander and chief of staff, and seizing two American-style heavy machine guns and over 1,400 rifles and carbines. On the same day, the Chiang Kai-shek puppet troops stationed about 60 li northeast of Changli in Funing city fled in panic, and the city was also recovered.

### 冀东收复昌黎抚宁斩断北宁歼敌千余

1947-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　冀东收复昌黎抚宁斩断北宁歼敌千余
    【新华社晋察冀二十四日急电】冀东人民解放军于十八日先后收复昌黎、抚宁两县城，切断北宁路。十七日午夜，人民解放军一部向华北大动脉北宁路北戴河以西实行自卫反击，激战至十八日下午四时，先后解放昌黎县城及其左右之张家庄、留守营、后封台三车站，歼蒋伪军副团长、参谋长以下千余名，缴美式重机枪两挺，步马枪一千四百余枝。同日，驻守昌黎东北约六十华里之抚宁城蒋伪仓惶逃窜，该城遂亦告收复。
