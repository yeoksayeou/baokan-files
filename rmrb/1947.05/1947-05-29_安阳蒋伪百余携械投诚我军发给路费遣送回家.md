Title: Over a Hundred Chiang Kai-shek Troops in Anyang Surrender to Our Army with Weapons; Given Travel Expenses and Sent Home

Author:

Date: 1947-05-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Henan Front, 25th—On the evening of the 14th, 121 officers and soldiers of the 3rd Company, 3rd Battalion, Artillery Regiment, 39th Brigade, 40th Division of the Chiang Kai-shek army at Yuanfen, north of Anyang city, led by ammunition Captain Zhang Yitong, surrendered to the People's Liberation Army with one machine gun, eight light machine guns, and over seventy rifles. On the 16th, three squads of puppet troops from the same stronghold also surrendered to the People's Liberation Army with two light machine guns, twenty-six rifles, three pistols, and over 4,000 rounds of ammunition. They expressed their willingness to return home to farm, and the People's Liberation Army provided each of them with travel expenses ranging from 2,000 to 5,000 Jireh banknotes and sent them back to their villages.

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Henan Front, 27th—Wang Suiqing, the Major General Commander of the 6th Regiment of Chiang Kai-shek's 3rd Column, has been identified in a field hospital of the People's Liberation Army. Wang, who was wounded in the abdomen, has gradually recovered after more than twenty days of treatment in the field hospital. Wang expressed his deep gratitude. The hospital where he is staying houses over 500 wounded officers and soldiers of the Third Column, all of whom are receiving careful treatment.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanxi-Hebei-Shandong-Henan, 27th—During the recent Zhengtai Campaign, the masses in the Taihang region not only completed transportation, demolition, and rescue tasks, but also actively cooperated with the troops in combat, pursuing fleeing enemies, and clearing out enemy stragglers, creating glorious results. They captured one mortar, two heavy machine guns, nineteen light machine guns, ten grenade launchers, six pistols, over a thousand rounds of ammunition, over a thousand grenades, and 10,300 pounds of wire. The Zuoquan Militia Regiment repelled a counterattack of over a thousand enemies near Shouyang. The 7th Company of the regiment alone captured two machine guns, two grenade launchers, and over ten rifles.

### 安阳蒋伪百余携械投诚我军发给路费遣送回家

1947-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　安阳蒋伪百余携械投诚我军发给路费遣送回家
    【新华社豫北前线二十五日电】十四日晚，安阳城北袁坟之蒋军四十师三十九旅炮兵营三营三连官兵一百二十一人，由弹药队长张义同率领，携同机枪一挺、轻机枪八挺、步枪七十多支，投奔人民解放军。十六日，又有该据点伪军三个班，携轻机枪两挺、步枪二十六支、手枪三支、子弹四千多发，投降解放军。彼等表示愿回家务农，人民解放军即发给他们每人路费两千至五千元冀钞。遣送还乡。
    【新华社豫北前线二十七日电】蒋伪三纵队六总队少将总队长王遂庆，已在解放军某野战医院中被查出。王腹部受伤，经野战医院二十余日的医治，已渐痊愈。王表示十分感激。他所住的医院共收容五百余第三纵队的负伤官兵，都受到细心的治疗。
    【新华社晋冀鲁豫二十七日电】太行参战群众在这次正太战役中，不仅完成运输，破击与抢救彩号等任务，并积极配合部队作战，追击溃退之敌、肃清敌人散兵，创造光辉战果。计缴获迫击炮一门，重机枪两挺，轻机枪十九挺，掷弹筒十个，手枪六支，子弹千余发，手榴弹千余颗，电线一万零三百斤。左权民兵团在寿阳附近，击退敌千余人的反扑。仅该团七连即缴获机枪两挺，掷弹筒两个，步枪十余支。
