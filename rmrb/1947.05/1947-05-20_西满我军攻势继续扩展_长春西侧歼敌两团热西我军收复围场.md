Author:
Date: 1947-05-20
Page: 1

　　West Manchuria PLA Offensive Continues to Expand
    Two Enemy Regiments Annihilated West of Changchun; Jehol-West PLA Recovers Weichang
    [New China News Agency Northeast Dispatch, 17th] The West Manchuria Democratic Allied Army, at 11:00 AM on the 17th, completely annihilated the entire 90th Regiment of the 30th Division of the New 1st Army of the Chiang Kai-shek troops and the 17th Security Regiment, which were entrenched in the old Huaide City (70 li west of Changchun). The commander of the 90th Regiment, Xiang Dianyuan, was captured alive. Detailed battle results are currently being verified.
    [New China News Agency Jehol-Chahar-Liaoning Dispatch, 18th] A certain unit of the Jehol-Chahar-Liaoning People's Liberation Army recovered Weichang at 4:00 PM on the 16th. The battle began at 24:00 on the 14th. The defending enemy, the 10th Regiment of the 4th Division of the 13th Army, and a bandit unit, totaling more than 1,200 men, were completely annihilated. Qipan Mountain, an important stronghold 50 li north of Weichang, was also liberated. Our army is continuing to expand the gains. Weichang was occupied by Chiang Kai-shek's troops last October. The city is an important town in western Jehol, 280 li from Chengde, Chifeng, and Dolon. It is the second county town recovered by the Jehol-Chahar-Liaoning People's Liberation Army in May, following Lingyuan.

### 西满我军攻势继续扩展  长春西侧歼敌两团热西我军收复围场

1947-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　西满我军攻势继续扩展
    长春西侧歼敌两团热西我军收复围场
    【新华社东北十七日急电】西满地区民主联军，于十七日上午十一时全部歼灭据守旧怀德城（长春以西七十里）之蒋军新一军三十师九十团及保安十七团两个整团，生俘九十团团长项殿元，详细战果正清查中。
    【新华社冀察热辽十八日电】冀察热辽人民解放军某部十六日下午四时收复围场。战争于十四日二十四时开始，守敌十三军第四师第十团及土匪一部共一千二百余人全部被歼；围场北五十里之重要据点棋盘山亦告光复，我军正继续扩大战果。围场系去年十月被蒋军侵占，该城为热西重镇，距承德、赤峰、多伦均二百八十里。为五月份内继凌源之后冀察热辽人民解放军收复之第二座县城。
