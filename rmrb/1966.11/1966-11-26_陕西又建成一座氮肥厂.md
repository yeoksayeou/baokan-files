Title: Shaanxi Completes Another Nitrogen Fertilizer Plant
Author:
Date: 1966-11-26
Edition: 2

Shaanxi Province has completed the construction of another nitrogen fertilizer plant, which has recently commenced production. This plant has an annual production capacity of over 20,000 tons of ammonium bicarbonate. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 陕西又建成一座氮肥厂

1966-11-26
第2版()
专栏：

    陕西又建成一座氮肥厂
    陕西省又建成一座氮肥厂，已在最近投入生产。这座氮肥厂每年可以生产二万多吨碳酸氢铵。　（据新华社讯）
