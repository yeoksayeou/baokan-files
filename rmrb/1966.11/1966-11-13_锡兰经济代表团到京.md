Title: Sri Lankan Economic Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-11-13
Edition: 5

For the purpose of negotiating China's assistance in the construction of the "Bandaranayake Memorial International Conference Hall" in Sri Lanka, a Sri Lankan economic delegation led by the Director of Public Works and Telecommunications of Sri Lanka's Ministry of Public Works and Telecommunications, Mr. De Silva, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 8th.

### 锡兰经济代表团到京

1966-11-13
第5版()
专栏：

    锡兰经济代表团到京
    新华社讯　为谈判我国援助锡兰修建“班达拉奈克纪念国际会堂”工程，以锡兰公共工程邮电部公共工程局长德席尔瓦为首的锡兰经济代表团一行二人，八日乘飞机到达北京。
