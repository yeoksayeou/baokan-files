Title: Vietnamese People Show Great Interest in China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1966-11-11
Edition: 4

The Vietnamese people are paying close attention to China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. The picture shows two Vietnamese spectators eagerly viewing an exhibition of Chairman Mao's reception of revolutionary young Red Guards.
