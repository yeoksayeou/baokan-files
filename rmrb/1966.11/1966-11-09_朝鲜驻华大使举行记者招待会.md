Title: North Korean Ambassador Holds Press Conference
Author:
Date: 1966-11-09
Edition: 5

North Korean Ambassador Holds Press Conference

	According to Xinhua News Agency, on the 7th, the Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, Pak Se-chang, held a press conference for Chinese and foreign journalists today. He exposed and condemned the recent armed attacks and various military provocations by the U.S. imperialist aggressor forces and the puppet army of South Korea on the military demarcation line in Korea against the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

### 朝鲜驻华大使举行记者招待会

1966-11-09
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜驻华大使举行记者招待会
    据新华社七日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使朴世昌今天举行中外记者招待会，揭露和谴责美帝国主义侵略军和南朝鲜伪军最近在朝鲜军事分界线上，对朝鲜民主主义人民共和国肆无忌惮地进行武装攻击和各种军事侵犯的行为。
