Title: Lao People's Party Supports Vietnamese People's Accusations Against U.S. Imperialism
Author:
Date: 1966-11-21
Edition: 5

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Khamkeut, November 17th: According to the Pathet Lao radio broadcast, the spokesperson for the Central Committee of the Lao People's Party issued a statement on November 13th, strongly condemning the U.S. imperialist aggression against Vietnam and firmly supporting the statement issued by the Vietnam Democratic Republic's Committee to Investigate U.S. Imperialist War Crimes in Vietnam on October 31st.

	The spokesperson for the Central Committee of the Lao People's Party stated, "The aggressive war launched by U.S. imperialism in Vietnam is unprecedented in its vile and filthy nature in history."

	The statement pointed out, "The heinous crimes committed by U.S. imperialism in Vietnam are a provocation and insult to progressive humanity."

	The statement said, "As a neighboring country of Vietnam and a people with a long-standing friendship with the Vietnamese people, the Lao people fully support the Vietnamese people's just struggle against the U.S. imperialist invaders."

	The statement concluded, "The Lao people and the Lao People's Party are determined to strengthen unity with the Vietnamese people and thoroughly defeat the U.S. imperialist invaders."

### 老挝爱国战线党支持越南人民对美帝的控诉  老挝越南人民加强团结打败美帝

1966-11-21
第5版()
专栏：

    老挝爱国战线党支持越南人民对美帝的控诉
    老挝越南人民加强团结打败美帝
    据新华社康开十七日电　据巴特寮电台广播：老挝爱国战线党中央委员会发言人十三日发表声明，强烈谴责美帝国主义侵略越南的罪行，坚决支持越南民主共和国调查美帝国主义在越南的战争罪行委员会十月三十一日发表的声明。
    老挝爱国战线党中央委员会发言人的声明说，“美帝国主义在越南发动的侵略战争，具有历史上前所未有的卑鄙性和肮脏性。”
    声明指出，“美帝国主义在越南所犯下的滔天罪行，是对进步人类的蔑视和挑衅。”
    声明说，“作为越南的邻邦和同越南人民有着悠久的友谊关系的老挝人民完全支持越南人民反对美帝国主义侵略者的正义斗争。”
    声明最后说，老挝人民和老挝爱国战线党决心加强和越南人民的团结，彻底打败美帝国主义侵略者。
