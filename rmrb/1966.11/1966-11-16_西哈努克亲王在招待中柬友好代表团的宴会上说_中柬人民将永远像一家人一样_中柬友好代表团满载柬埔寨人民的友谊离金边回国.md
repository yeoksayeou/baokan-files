Title: Prince Sihanouk Says Chinese and Cambodian Peoples Will Forever Be Like One Family
Author:
Date: 1966-11-16
Edition: 6

Prince Sihanouk of Cambodia said at a banquet held for the Sino-Cambodian Friendship Delegation that the Chinese and Cambodian peoples will forever be like one family. The Sino-Cambodian Friendship Delegation left Phnom Penh for home, carrying with them the friendship of the Cambodian people.

According to a report from Xinhua News Agency in Phnom Penh on November 15, Prince Sihanouk, the Head of State of Cambodia, received the Sino-Cambodian Friendship Delegation at the tourist attraction of Bokor City in the middle of the day on November 14. This was the fourth time the delegation had been received by Prince Sihanouk during their visit to Cambodia.

Prince Sihanouk warmly received the delegation at his villa in Bokor City and had a cordial conversation with Zhang Qi, the leader of the delegation, the delegation members, and the Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia, Chen Shuliang, and his wife, who accompanied them on their visit. He also hosted a luncheon for the delegation.

At the banquet, Prince Sihanouk wished Chairman Mao Zedong good health and long life and extended his best wishes to other Chinese leaders and the Chinese people. Prince Sihanouk once again stated, "No force can separate China and Cambodia; China and Cambodia will forever be like one family." He also sincerely thanked the Chinese guests for their visit.

According to a report from Xinhua News Agency in Phnom Penh on November 14, Prince Sihanouk, the Head of State of Cambodia, and his wife received the Sino-Cambodian Friendship Delegation again on November 12 and 13.

The Sino-Cambodian Friendship Delegation, led by Zhang Qi, was received by Prince Sihanouk at his villa in Prek Sbauv while en route to Sihanoukville on the morning of November 12. The prince also invited them to take a boat ride. After the boat ride, Prince Sihanouk hosted a banquet for the delegation.

Princess Sihanouk also received the delegation at the Sihanoukville Guest House in the evening of November 12.

When the delegation arrived in Sihanoukville, they were warmly welcomed by Mayor Heng Saba and the local people. Accompanied by Heng Saba, the delegation visited the People's Socialist Alliance Exhibition Hall, the port, and local factories in Sihanoukville.

On November 13 at noon, Prince Sihanouk received the delegation again at his residence in Ochoeteal and hosted a banquet. During the banquet, Prince Sihanouk once again pointed out that the visit of the delegation was an honor and a pleasure for the Cambodian people and himself.

According to a report from Xinhua News Agency in Phnom Penh on November 15, Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia Chen Shuliang and his wife hosted a farewell banquet on the evening of November 14 for the Sino-Cambodian Friendship Delegation, marking the end of their friendly visit to Cambodia.

Guests at the banquet included Princess Sihanouk, Prince Sihanouk's personal secretary Bin Nuon, Vice President of the Royal Council Kantol, Speaker of the National Assembly Chou Sengkhosal, Speaker of the Royal Assembly Mondana, and Prime Minister Lon Nol, among others, totaling over 300 people.

According to a report from Xinhua News Agency in Phnom Penh on November 15, the Sino-Cambodian Friendship Delegation, led by Zhang Qi, a member of the National People's Congress and Vice Chairman of the China-Cambodia Friendship Association, concluded their visit to Cambodia and departed from Phnom Penh by plane on November 15.

Those who came to the airport to see off the delegation included Princess Sihanouk, Vice President of the Cambodian National Assembly Pich Thieng, Minister of Health Pham Binh Chong's wife, Secretary of State for National Education Didi Na's wife, General Secretary of the Royal Socialist Youth Union of Cambodia Yand, Chairman of the Cambodia-China Friendship Association Lan Neth, First Vice Chairman Sunan, Second Vice Chairman Hu Ning, Secretary-General Putchai, other members of the Cambodia-China Friendship Association, and many other notable figures.

The national flags of both countries fluttered in the wind at the airport. Several hundred members of the Royal Socialist Youth Union of Cambodia, youth, and students lined up to see off the delegation, shouting "Long live Cambodian-Chinese friendship!" A group of Cambodian girls presented flowers to Zhang Qi and the delegation members.

The representative of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to Cambodia, Nguyen Thang, also came to the airport to see off the delegation.

Chinese Ambassador Chen Shuliang and diplomatic officials from the Chinese Embassy also came to the airport to see off the delegation.

Those who came to the airport to see off the delegation also included overseas Chinese students and representatives.

### 西哈努克亲王在招待中柬友好代表团的宴会上说  中柬人民将永远像一家人一样  中柬友好代表团满载柬埔寨人民的友谊离金边回国

1966-11-16
第6版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王在招待中柬友好代表团的宴会上说
    中柬人民将永远像一家人一样
    中柬友好代表团满载柬埔寨人民的友谊离金边回国
    据新华社金边十五日电　柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王十四日中午在柬埔寨游览胜地波哥儿城接见了中柬友好代表团。这是代表团在柬埔寨访问期间第四次受到西哈努克亲王的接见。
    西哈努克亲王在波哥儿城的别墅里热情地接待了代表团，同代表团团长张茜、代表团团员以及陪同他们参观访问的中国驻柬埔寨大使陈叔亮和夫人进行了亲切的谈话，并设午宴招待代表团。
    西哈努克亲王在宴会上祝毛泽东主席健康长寿，并向中国其他领导人和中国人民表示良好的祝愿。西哈努克亲王再次表示：“任何力量都不能把中国和柬埔寨分开，中国和柬埔寨将永远像一家人一样。”他还衷心感谢中国客人们的这次访问。
    据新华社金边十四日电　柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王和夫人十二日和十三日再次热情接见正在柬埔寨访问的中柬友好代表团。
    由张茜率领的中柬友好代表团在十二日早晨前往西哈努克市进行访问途中路过磅沙拉时，西哈努克亲王在别墅接见了代表团的全体团员，并且招待他们乘游艇在水上游览。游览后，西哈努克亲王设便宴招待了代表团。
    西哈努克夫人还在十二日晚上，在西哈努克市迎宾馆接待了代表团。
    在代表团到达西哈努克市时，受到市长亨沙空以及各界人民的热烈欢迎。代表团在亨沙空陪同下先后参观了西哈努克市人民社会同盟展览馆、港口和当地的工厂。
    十三日中午，西哈努克亲王在他的奥乔提住所再一次接见代表团，并设便宴招待。西哈努克亲王在席间再次指出，代表团的访问使柬埔寨人民和他本人感到荣幸和愉快。
    据新华社金边十五日电　中国驻柬埔寨大使陈叔亮和夫人，十四日晚上为中柬友好代表团结束在柬埔寨的友好访问举行告别宴会。
    出席宴会的有，拉斯美·索巴娜王姑、柬埔寨国家元首私人秘书宾努、王位最高委员会副主席康托尔亲王、国民议会议长周森古沙、王国议会议长蒙达那亲王、内阁首相朗诺等三百多人。
    据新华社金边十五日电　由全国人民代表大会代表、中国柬埔寨友好协会副会长张茜率领的中柬友好代表团，结束了在柬埔寨的访问，于十五日乘飞机离开金边回国。
    到机场欢送代表团的有：拉斯美·索巴娜王姑，柬埔寨国民议会副议长披天来，卫生大臣方炳桢夫人，国民教育国务秘书狄弟娜夫人，高棉王家社会主义青年联盟总专员严迪；柬中友协主席兰·涅特，第一副主席苏南，第二副主席胡宁，秘书长蒲才，柬中友协其他理事以及许多著名人士。
    机场上柬中两国国旗迎风飘扬。几百名高棉王家社会主义青年联盟盟员、青年和学生列队欢送代表团，他们高呼“柬中友谊万岁！”一群柬埔寨姑娘向张茜和代表团成员献了花。
    越南民主共和国政府驻柬埔寨代表阮商也到机场欢送。
    中国大使陈叔亮，中国大使馆的外交官员等也到机场欢送。
    到机场欢送的还有华侨学生和代表。
