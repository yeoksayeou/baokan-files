Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets Athletes Participating in the First Asian New Forces Games
Author:
Date: 1966-11-02
Edition: 4

Vice Premier Chen Yi met tonight with Chinese athletes who are about to participate in the First Asian New Forces Games and had a cordial conversation with them.

Vice Premier Chen Yi encouraged the athletes to hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, emphasize politics, be modest and prudent, guard against arrogance and impatience, and enhance friendship with athletes from various Asian countries, promote mutual progress, and develop together. He urged them to unite against imperialism and make contributions to the success of the First Asian New Forces Games.

Vice Premier Chen Yi also posed for a group photo with the athletes.

### 陈毅副总理接见我参加亚新会的运动员

1966-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见我参加亚新会的运动员
    新华社一日讯　陈毅副总理今晚接见了即将参加第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会的我国运动员，并同他们进行了亲切的谈话。
    陈毅副总理勉励运动员们高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，突出政治，谦虚谨慎，戒骄戒躁，与亚洲各国运动员增进友好，互相促进，共同发展，团结反帝，为开好第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会作出贡献。
    陈毅副总理还同运动员们一起合影留念。
