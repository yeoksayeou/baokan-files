Title: President Dior Congratulates China on Successful Nuclear Missile Test
Author:
Date: 1966-11-01
Edition: 1

Chinese leaders have received a telegram from President Ahmed Sekou Toure of the Republic of Guinea congratulating China on the successful test launch of a nuclear missile. The telegram reads as follows:

	“On the occasion of the successful launch of an atomic warhead rocket by the People's Republic of China, we are pleased to extend our heartfelt congratulations to you and to convey the sincere congratulations of the people, the party, and the government of Guinea to all Chinese scientists and technicians. China's achievements in the fields of atomic energy and science mark a decisive stage in the restoration of the dignity of the peoples of the Third World and are another guarantee for the maintenance of world peace. In addition, the People's Republic of China has pledged not to be the first to use atomic weapons, and we express our respect for this.

	With the highest regards.”

### 杜尔总统致电祝贺我发射核导弹试验成功  中国的新成就是世界和平又一保证

1966-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    杜尔总统致电祝贺我发射核导弹试验成功
    中国的新成就是世界和平又一保证
    新华社三十一日讯　我国国家领导人收到了几内亚共和国总统艾哈迈德·塞古·杜尔发来的电报，对我国发射导弹核武器试验成功表示衷心的祝贺。电文如下：
      “在中华人民共和国成功地发射原子弹头火箭的时候，我们高兴地请你接受并请向全体中国科学家和技术人员转达几内亚人民、几内亚党和几内亚政府的衷心的祝贺。中国在原子和科学方面所取得的成就标志着恢复第三世界各国人民尊严的决定性的阶段，也是维护世界和平的又一保证。此外，中华人民共和国还保证决不首先使用原子武器，我们对此表示敬意。
    顺致最崇高的敬意。”
