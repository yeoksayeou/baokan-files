Title: U.S. Warships and Aircraft Intrude into Our Territorial Waters and Airspace
Author:
Date: 1966-11-17
Edition: 5

From 8:44 a.m. on November 13 to 12:35 a.m. on November 15, a U.S. warship intruded into our territorial waters in the region east of the mouth of the Yangtze River eight times. From 10:30 a.m. to 10:50 a.m. on November 15, from 2:45 p.m. to 2:46 p.m. on the same day, and from 7:49 a.m. to 8:01 a.m. on November 16, a total of three U.S. military aircraft intruded into our airspace over Yongxing Island and Dong Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province.

Regarding the military provocation by the U.S. warship and military aircraft, the spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the ministry, has issued the 416th serious warning.

### 美舰美机侵入我领海领空  我提出第四百一十六次严重警告

1966-11-17
第5版()
专栏：

    美舰美机侵入我领海领空
    我提出第四百一十六次严重警告
    新华社十六日讯　十一月十三日八时四十四分至十五日零时三十五分，美国军舰一艘，先后八次侵入我长江口以东地区领海。十五日十时三十分至十时五十分，十四时四十五分至十四时四十六分和十六日七时四十九分至八时零一分，共有美国军用飞机三架，先后侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛、东岛地区领空。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百一十六次严重警告。
