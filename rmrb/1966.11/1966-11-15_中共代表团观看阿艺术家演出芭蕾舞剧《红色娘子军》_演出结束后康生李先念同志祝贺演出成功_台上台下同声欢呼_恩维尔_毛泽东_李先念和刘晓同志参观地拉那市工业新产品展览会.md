Title: Chinese Communist Party Delegation Watches Albanian Artists Perform the Ballet "The Red Detachment of Women"
Author:
Date: 1966-11-15
Edition: 5

By a delegation led by Comrade Kang Sheng, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Secretary of the Central Secretariat, and Comrade Li Xiannian, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Secretary of the Central Secretariat, the Chinese Communist Party delegation watched the Albanian National Opera and Ballet Theater's performance of the modern revolutionary ballet "The Red Detachment of Women" at the Cultural Palace in Tirana on the evening of the 12th.

	Other delegations invited to attend the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Party of Labor included representatives from the Vietnamese Labor Party, the Indonesian Communist Party, the New Zealand Communist Party, the Malayan Communist Party, the Belgian Communist Party, the Brazilian Communist Party, the Communist Party of Australia (Marxist-Leninists), the Colombian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninists), the Italian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninists), the French Communist Movement (Marxist-Leninists), and the Swiss Communist Organization (Marxist-Leninists).

	Comrade Alija, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor, Comrade Kellezi, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee, and Comrade Dejana, Minister of Education and Culture of Albania, also watched the performance with the Chinese Communist Party delegation and others.

	The revolutionary content of the ballet and the excellent performance by the Albanian actors were warmly received by the audience.

	The evening was filled with a very warm and friendly atmosphere. After the performance, Comrades Kang Sheng, Li Xiannian, Liu Xiao, and Wu Xiuquan of the delegation, accompanied by Comrades Kellezi and Dejana, went on stage to shake hands and embrace the Albanian artists, congratulating them on their successful performance and presenting them with flower baskets. At this moment, the stage and the audience erupted in enthusiastic applause and shouted in unison, "Enver—Mao Zedong."

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, November 13th, Chinese Communist Party delegation deputy leader, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Secretary of the Central Secretariat, Comrade Li Xiannian, and member of the Chinese Communist Party delegation, Central Committee member Comrade Liu Xiao, this morning visited the Tirana Industrial New Products Exhibition.

	Comrade Kellezi, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor and Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Tirana People's Congress, Comrade Pacirami, member of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor and Secretary of the Tirana Municipal Committee, and Comrade Dedja, Vice Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Tirana People's Congress, accompanied the visit. Comrade Kellezi personally explained the exhibition to the Chinese comrades.

### 中共代表团观看阿艺术家演出芭蕾舞剧《红色娘子军》  演出结束后康生李先念同志祝贺演出成功　台上台下同声欢呼“恩维尔—毛泽东”  李先念和刘晓同志参观地拉那市工业新产品展览会

1966-11-15
第5版()
专栏：

    中共代表团观看阿艺术家演出芭蕾舞剧《红色娘子军》
    演出结束后康生李先念同志祝贺演出成功　台上台下同声欢呼“恩维尔—毛泽东”
    李先念和刘晓同志参观地拉那市工业新产品展览会
    新华社地拉那十三日电　由团长、中共中央政治局常务委员、中央书记处书记康生和副团长、中共中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记李先念率领的中国共产党代表团，十二日晚在地拉那文化宫观看了地拉那国家歌剧和芭蕾舞剧院演出的中国现代革命芭蕾舞剧《红色娘子军》。
    观看演出的还有应邀参加阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第五次代表大会的其他共产党、工人党和马克思列宁主义组织的代表团，它们是：越南劳动党代表团，印度尼西亚共产党代表团，新西兰共产党代表团，马来亚共产党代表团，比利时共产党代表团，巴西共产党代表团，澳大利亚共产党（马克思列宁主义者）代表团，哥伦比亚共产党（马克思列宁主义）代表团，意大利共产党（马克思列宁主义）代表团，法国共产主义运动（马克思列宁主义）代表团和瑞士共产党人组织（马克思列宁主义）代表团。
    同中共代表团等一起观看演出的有：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员阿利雅，中央政治局候补委员凯莱齐，阿尔巴尼亚教育和文化部长德雅纳等。
    这个芭蕾舞剧的革命内容和阿尔巴尼亚演员的精采演出，受到了观众的热烈欢迎。
    演出晚会充满了十分热烈友好的气氛。演出结束后，康生同志、李先念同志以及代表团团员刘晓和伍修权同志，在凯莱齐和德雅纳等同志的陪同下走上舞台，同阿尔巴尼亚艺术家握手拥抱，祝贺他们演出成功，并向他们赠送了花篮。这时，台上台下热烈鼓掌，同声欢呼“恩维尔—毛泽东”。
    据新华社地拉那十三日电　中国共产党代表团副团长、中共中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记李先念和中共代表团员、中央委员刘晓，今天上午参观了地拉那市工业新产品展览会。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局候补委员、地拉那市人民会议执行委员会主席凯莱齐，劳动党中央委员、地拉那市委书记帕奇拉米，地拉那市人民会议执行委员会副主席戴迪亚陪同参观。凯莱齐同志亲自向中国同志做了讲解。
