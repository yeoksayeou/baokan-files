Title: Chinese Government Trade Delegation Visits Ceylon
Author:
Date: 1966-11-23
Edition: 4

A delegation from the Chinese government, led by Minister of Foreign Trade Lin Haiyun, departed from Beijing by plane today to visit Ceylon. The delegation will also negotiate the 1967 Sino-Ceylonese barter agreement. Those who went to the airport to see them off included Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang, and Acting Charge d'Affaires of the Ceylonese Embassy in China, Korus, among others.

### 我政府贸易代表团去锡兰访问

1966-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

    我政府贸易代表团去锡兰访问
    新华社二十二日讯　由对外贸易部代理部长林海云率领的中国政府贸易代表团，今天乘飞机离开北京去锡兰访问，并商订一九六七年度中锡换货议定书。到机场送行的，有对外贸易部副部长李强，锡兰驻中国大使馆临时代办柯鲁斯等。
