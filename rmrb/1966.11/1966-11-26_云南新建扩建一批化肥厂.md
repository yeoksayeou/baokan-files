Title: Yunnan to Build and Expand a Batch of Fertilizer Plants
Author:
Date: 1966-11-26
Edition: 2

Yunnan Province is currently building and expanding a batch of medium and small-sized fertilizer plants. Some of these have already been completed and put into production. Once all these fertilizer plants are completed and put into production, Yunnan Province's fertilizer production capacity will increase by more than onefold.

### 云南新建扩建一批化肥厂

1966-11-26
第2版()
专栏：

    云南新建扩建一批化肥厂
    云南省正在新建和扩建一批中小型化肥厂，其中的一部分已经建成投入生产。这批化肥厂全部完工投产后，云南省的化肥生产能力将增加一倍以上。
