Title: The Third Session of the Asia-Africa Journalists Training Class Begins
Author:
Date: 1966-11-06
Edition: 3

The third session of the Asia-Africa Journalists Training Class, organized by the Asia-Africa Journalists Association, began today in Beijing. This session includes students selected from countries such as Azania (South Africa), Southwest Africa, Mozambique, Basutoland, Zimbabwe, and Ceylon.

The Secretary-General of the Asia-Africa Journalists Association, Chard, the Secretary-General Morrison, Karun Jumaier, and Sugiura Ichihei attended today's opening ceremony. Also present were Wang Minzhao, Acting President of the All-China Journalists Association, and officials from the Foreign Affairs Department's Press Bureau and various news organizations in the capital. Tang Pingzhu, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association, on behalf of the All-China Journalists Association, presented the students with English editions of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong."

### 第三期亚非记者进修班开学

1966-11-06
第3版()
专栏：

    第三期亚非记者进修班开学
    新华社五日讯　亚非新闻工作者协会主办的第三期亚非记者进修班今天在北京开学。本期进修班有阿扎尼亚（南非）、西南非洲、莫三鼻给、巴苏陀兰、津巴布韦和锡兰等国家选派的学员参加学习。
    亚非新闻工作者协会总书记查禾多、驻会书记摩里逊、卡荣朱迈勒、杉山市平等出席了今天的开学典礼。出席开学典礼的还有：中华全国新闻工作者协会代主任王敏昭，以及外交部新闻司和首都各新闻单位的负责人。中华全国新闻工作者协会副主任唐平铸，代表中华全国新闻工作者协会向学员们赠送了英文版《毛泽东选集》。
