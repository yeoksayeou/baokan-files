Title: Burmese Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-11-02
Edition: 4

At the invitation of China, a Burmese trade delegation, led by team leader Colonel Ye Min, arrived in Beijing by plane this evening to participate in the Guangzhou China Export Commodities Fair and conduct a visit.

### 缅甸贸易代表团到京

1966-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸贸易代表团到京
    新华社二十九日讯　应邀前来我国参加广州中国出口商品交易会和进行访问的缅甸贸易代表团，在团长耶敏中校率领下，今晚乘飞机到达北京。
