Title: North Korean Ambassador to China Leaves Post and Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1966-11-22
Edition: 4

North Korean Ambassador to China Leaves Post and Returns Home

Xinhua News Agency, November 21st

The Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, Pak Se-chang, has been recalled and will leave his post. Today, he departed Beijing by train to return to his home country.

### 朝鲜驻华大使离任回国

1966-11-22
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜驻华大使离任回国
    新华社二十一日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使朴世昌奉调离任，今天乘火车离开北京回国。
