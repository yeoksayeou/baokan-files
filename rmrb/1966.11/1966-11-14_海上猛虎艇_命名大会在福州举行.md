Title: "Sea Tiger Boat" Naming Ceremony Held in Fuzhou
Author:
Date: 1966-11-14
Edition: 2

	“This army possesses an indomitable spirit. It will vanquish all enemies and will never be subdued by them.”
	“The Sea Tiger Boat” Naming Ceremony Held in Fuzhou

	The guidance officer of the "Sea Tiger Boat," Fu Jinqiuan, on behalf of all the comrades on the boat, expressed that they will definitely remain humble and cautious, guard against arrogance and impatience, and turn our boat into a grand school of Mao Zedong Thought.

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, November 13th: The leadership of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy held a naming ceremony today in Fuzhou. According to the order of the Ministry of National Defense, the 588 boat of the Navy was awarded the honorable title of "Sea Tiger Boat."

	The naming ceremony began with the resounding melody of "The East Is Red." The entire crew of the "Sea Tiger Boat" stood up and collectively recited Chairman Mao's words: "This army possesses an indomitable spirit. It will vanquish all enemies and will never be subdued by them."

	At the meeting, Vice Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Han Xianchu, on behalf of the Ministry of National Defense, read the order awarding the "Sea Tiger Boat" the honorable title and presented the flag to the "Sea Tiger Boat" on behalf of the Ministry of National Defense. The Navy's responsible comrade Wang Hongkun, on behalf of the Navy Party Committee, presented a portrait of our most beloved leader, Chairman Mao, to the "Sea Tiger Boat." The responsible comrade of the Fuzhou troops, Liu Peishan, and the Secretary of the Fujian Provincial Committee and Governor of Fujian Province, Wei Jinshui, each presented a banner to the "Sea Tiger Boat." The East Sea Fleet Party Committee of the Navy presented a copy of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" to each comrade of the "Sea Tiger Boat."

	Comrade Ye Fei, Secretary of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the East China Bureau, on behalf of the Central Committee of the East China Bureau, extended the warmest congratulations to all comrades of the "Sea Tiger Boat." He said, the heroic deeds of the "Sea Tiger Boat" once again prove that Comrade Lin Biao's wise judgment: the best weapon is Mao Zedong Thought, and the greatest combat effectiveness is the invincible power of people armed with Mao Zedong Thought.

	Ye Fei said, the U.S. imperialists, modern revisionists, and the reactionary Chiang Kai-shek clique will not accept defeat. Especially in Fujian, which is on the front line of the struggle against the enemy, we must double our vigilance, strengthen our preparedness, and be ready at any time to smash all the enemy's plots and sabotage. He said: The current Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution is advancing towards the direction indicated by Chairman Mao. We must stand together with the broad masses, thoroughly expose and criticize the bourgeois reactionary line, eliminate its influence, resolutely defend the proletarian revolutionary line represented by Chairman Mao, and carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution to the end!

	Comrade Wang Hongkun, in his speech, called on all officers and soldiers of the Navy to most resolutely implement Comrade Lin Biao's instructions on raising the study of Chairman Mao's works to a new level, to launch a new high tide of studying and applying Chairman Mao's works, and to carry out a thorough ideological revolution.

	Comrade Pi Dingjin, the responsible comrade of the Fuzhou troops, in his speech, called on the frontline troops in Fujian to learn from the "Sea Tiger Boat" and encouraged all comrades of the "Sea Tiger Boat" to remain humble and cautious, guard against arrogance and impatience, and to continuously revolutionize and advance.

	More than a thousand representatives from the famous hero units and model individuals of the Army, Navy, and Air Force on the Fujian frontline, such as the "Red Spearhead Company" and Feng Fusheng, Ye Zhongyang, as well as various units, attended the ceremony. Fu Jinqiuan, the guidance officer of the "Sea Tiger Boat," expressed the determination of the entire crew on behalf of all comrades at the meeting. He said, we will definitely remember Chairman Mao's teachings: "Humility makes one progress, arrogance makes one fall behind." We will learn from all heroic units and heroic individuals, and learn from the heroic warriors who defend the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, Cai Yongqiang. We will most resolutely and enthusiastically respond to Comrade Lin Biao's great call, push the mass movement of studying and applying Chairman Mao's works to a new stage, focus on the "use" aspect, and truly turn our boat into a grand school of Mao Zedong Thought.

### “海上猛虎艇”命名大会在福州举行

1966-11-14
第2版()
专栏：

    “这个军队具有一往无前的精神，它要压倒一切敌人，而决不被敌人所屈服。”
    “海上猛虎艇”命名大会在福州举行
    “海上猛虎艇”指导员傅金泉代表全艇同志表示，一定谦虚谨慎，戒骄戒躁，把我们艇真正办成毛泽东思想的大学校
    据新华社福州十三日电　中国人民解放军海军领导机关，今天在福州举行命名大会，根据国防部命令授予海军某部五八八艇以“海上猛虎艇”的光荣称号。
    命名大会在宏亮的《东方红》乐曲声中开始。“海上猛虎艇”全体同志起立，集体高声朗诵毛主席语录：“这个军队具有一往无前的精神，它要压倒一切敌人，而决不被敌人所屈服。”
    会上，中国人民解放军副总参谋长韩先楚，代表国防部宣读了授予“海上猛虎艇”光荣称号的命令，并代表国防部向“海上猛虎艇”授旗。海军负责人王宏坤代表海军党委向“海上猛虎艇”赠送我们最敬爱的领袖毛主席像。福州部队负责人刘培善和福建省委书记处书记、福建省长魏金水分别向“海上猛虎艇”赠送了锦旗。海军东海舰队党委向“海上猛虎艇”全体同志每人赠送了一部《毛泽东选集》。
    中共中央华东局书记处书记叶飞，代表中共中央华东局向“海上猛虎艇”全体同志致以最热烈的祝贺。他说，“海上猛虎艇”的英雄战绩，再一次证明了，林彪同志的英明论断：最好的武器是毛泽东思想，最大的战斗力是用毛泽东思想武装起来的人的无比正确。
    叶飞说，美帝国主义、现代修正主义和蒋介石反动派，是不会甘心失败的。特别是福建，处在对敌斗争的最前线，更要加倍提高警惕，加强战备，随时准备粉碎敌人的一切阴谋破坏。他说：当前无产阶级文化大革命，正朝着毛主席所指引的方向胜利前进。我们一定要和广大群众在一起，彻底揭发批判资产阶级反动路线，肃清其影响，坚决保卫以毛主席为代表的无产阶级革命路线，把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底！
    海军负责人王宏坤在讲话中号召海军全体指战员要最坚决地贯彻林彪同志关于把学习毛主席著作的群众运动提高到一个新的阶段的指示，掀起大学大用毛主席著作的新高潮，大搞思想革命化。
    福州部队负责人皮定钧在讲话中，号召福建前线部队向“海上猛虎艇”学习，并勉励“海上猛虎艇”全体同志要谦虚谨慎，戒骄戒躁，不断革命，不断前进。
    福建前线部队陆海空三军著名英雄单位和模范人物“红色尖刀连”和丰福生、叶中央等以及各部队代表共一千多人，出席了大会。“海上猛虎艇”指导员傅金泉，代表全艇同志在大会上表示决心。他说，我们一定牢牢记住毛主席“虚心使人进步，骄傲使人落后”的教导，谦虚谨慎，戒骄戒躁，向一切英雄单位和英雄人物学习，向保卫无产阶级文化大革命的英雄战士蔡永祥学习。我们一定最坚决最热烈地响应林彪同志的伟大号召，把活学活用毛主席著作的群众运动推向新阶段，在“用”字上狠下功夫，把我们艇真正办成毛泽东思想的大学校。
