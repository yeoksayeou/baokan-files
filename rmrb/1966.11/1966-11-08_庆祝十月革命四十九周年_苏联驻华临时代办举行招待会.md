Title: Celebrating the 49th Anniversary of the October Revolution: The Soviet Chargé d'Affaires Holds a Reception
Author:
Date: 1966-11-08
Edition: 6

For the celebration of the 49th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, the Soviet Chargé d'Affaires in China, Razdukhov, held a reception at the embassy this evening.

Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Chairman Guo Moruo, and relevant officials such as Zhang Aiping, Qiao Guanhua, Jiang Ming, and Song Yiping attended the reception. The delegation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association led by Mayevsky, as well as diplomats from various countries stationed in China, also attended the reception.

### 庆祝十月革命四十九周年  苏联驻华临时代办举行招待会

1966-11-08
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝十月革命四十九周年
    苏联驻华临时代办举行招待会
    新华社七日讯　为庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十九周年，苏联驻中国大使馆临时代办拉兹杜霍夫今晚在大使馆举行了招待会。
    陈毅副总理、郭沫若副委员长，以及张爱萍、乔冠华、江明、宋一平等有关方面负责人出席了招待会。以马耶夫斯基为首的苏中友协代表团，以及各国驻中国使节也出席了招待会。
