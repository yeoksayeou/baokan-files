Title: Comrade Ray Nunes Leaves Beijing for His Home Country
Author:
Date: 1966-11-30
Edition: 1

Comrade Ray Nunes of the Political Committee of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of New Zealand has concluded his visit to our country. Today, he departed Beijing by airplane to return to his home country. Those who went to the airport to see him off included Comrade Li Xiannian, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Comrade Zhao Yimin, an alternate member of the Central Committee.

### 纽恩斯同志离京回国

1966-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

    纽恩斯同志离京回国
    新华社二十九日讯　新西兰共产党全国委员会政治委员会委员雷·纽恩斯同志，结束了在我国的访问，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。到机场欢送的，有中共中央政治局委员李先念同志，中央候补委员赵毅敏同志等。
