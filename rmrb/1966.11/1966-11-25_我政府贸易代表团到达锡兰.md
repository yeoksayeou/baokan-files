Title: Chinese Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Ceylon
Author:
Date: 1966-11-25
Edition: 4

A trade delegation led by Lin Haiyun, acting minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, arrived in Ceylon on November 23 at the invitation of the Ceylonese government.

Ceylon's Minister of Commerce and Trade, Peries, and others welcomed the delegation at the airport.

China's acting ambassador, Guo Ying, and commercial counselor, Xi Yingsheng, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 我政府贸易代表团到达锡兰

1966-11-25
第4版()
专栏：

    我政府贸易代表团到达锡兰
    新华社科伦坡二十三日电　由对外贸易部代理部长林海云率领的中国政府贸易代表团应锡兰政府的邀请，二十三日到达这里访问。
    锡兰商业和贸易部长佩里斯等到机场欢迎。
    中国大使馆临时代办国鹰和商务参赞奚业胜也到机场欢迎。
