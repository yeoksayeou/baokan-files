Title: Chairman Mao Receives the Guinean Government Economic Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-11-18
Edition: 1

Chairman Mao received the Minister of Economic Development of Guinea, Ismail Touré, and the members of the Guinean government economic delegation led by him, and had a friendly and cordial conversation with them. Vice Premier Chen Yi was present at the reception. The Guinean Ambassador to China, Sekou Camara, was also present. (Accompanying photograph)

Chairman Mao receives the Minister of Economic Development of Guinea, Ismail Touré (third from the left), and the members of the Guinean government economic delegation led by him. The photo shows them posing together during the reception.

### 毛主席接见几内亚政府经济代表团

1966-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见几内亚政府经济代表团
    新华社十七日讯　毛泽东主席今晚接见几内亚经济发展部部长伊斯梅尔·杜尔，和由他率领的几内亚政府经济代表团的成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的有陈毅副总理。几内亚驻中国大使塞古·卡马拉也在座。（附图片）
    毛主席接见几内亚经济发展部部长伊斯梅尔·杜尔（左三）和由他率领的几内亚政府经济代表团的成员。图为接见时合影。
    　新华社记者摄
