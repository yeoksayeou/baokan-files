Title: Comrade Mao Zedong's Telegram to the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour
Author:
Date: 1966-11-08
Edition: 1

    Let the Communist Party of China and the Communist Party of Albania, the peoples of China and Albania unite! Let all Marxist-Leninists of the world unite! Let all revolutionary peoples of the world unite! Down with imperialism! Down with modern revisionism! Down with all reactionaries!

    Mao Zedong
