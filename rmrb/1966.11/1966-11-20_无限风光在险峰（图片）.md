Title: Boundless Scenery Lies on the Perilous Peaks
Author: Wú Mǐn
Date: 1966-11-20
Edition: 2

The scenery of mountains and rivers is gathered in my eyes,
The winds and clouds of the five continents fill my heart.
Millions sing "The East is Red,"
The four seas praise Mao Zedong.
The hearts of the Long March warriors surge,
Evergreen like the sturdy pine in winter and summer.
The revolution does not choose the easy path,
"Boundless scenery lies on the perilous peaks!"

### 无限风光在险峰（图片）
诗信　吴敏
1966-11-20

    无限风光在险峰
