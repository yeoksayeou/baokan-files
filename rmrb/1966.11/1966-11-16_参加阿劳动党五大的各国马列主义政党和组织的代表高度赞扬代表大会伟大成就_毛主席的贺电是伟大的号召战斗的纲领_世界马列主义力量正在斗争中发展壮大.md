Title: Representatives of Marxist-Leninist Parties and Organizations from Various Countries Highly Praise the Great Achievements of the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour
Author:
Date: 1966-11-16
Edition: 5

Representatives of Marxist-Leninist parties and organizations from various countries who attended the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour highly praised the great achievements of the congress. Mao Zedong's congratulatory message is a great call to action and a program for struggle. The forces of Marxism-Leninism in the world are growing stronger in the struggle.

	NEW DELHI, Nov. 14 (Xinhua) -- Representatives of the Communist Party of Indonesia, the Communist Party of New Zealand, the Communist Party of Australia (Marxist-Leninists), the Communist Party of Belgium, the Communist Party of Bolivia, the Revolutionary Communist Party of Chile, the Communist Party of Italy (Marxist-Leninists), the Marxist-Leninist Communist Movement of France, and the Socialist Revolutionary Party of Lebanon, who were invited to attend the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour, recently spoke to Xinhua reporters. They enthusiastically praised the successful conclusion of the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour and highly evaluated Mao Zedong's congratulatory message to the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour.

	The head of the Indonesian Communist Party delegation, Central Political Bureau member and Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee, Yu. Ajitoro, said that the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour demonstrated the close unity of the Albanian Party of Labour and the Albanian people, and showed the people's love and trust for the party. This congress has great international historical significance. Representatives from five continents of Marxist-Leninists attended and praised it. This shows that the Marxist-Leninist movement in the world has grown stronger and more consolidated in the struggle against imperialism led by the United States, modern revisionism centered on the Soviet Communist Party leadership, and the reactionaries of various countries. This congress also clearly reflects the fighting unity of Marxist-Leninists around the world.

	He said that Comrade Mao Zedong's congratulatory message to the congress is of great significance not only to Albanian communists but also to the international communist movement.

	Comrade Mao Zedong's congratulatory message correctly expresses the high evaluation of Marxist-Leninists and revolutionary people around the world for the Albanian Party of Labour led by Comrade Enver Hoxha and the heroic Albanian people. The message is right: People's Albania has become a great socialist beacon in Europe. This message correctly expresses the fighting friendship between the two parties and the two peoples of China and Albania, based on proletarian internationalism and genuine Marxist-Leninist principles.

	Ajitoro said: The experience of the Indonesian Communist Party and people in the struggle against the brutal white terror of the Suharto-Nasution fascist military dictatorship, and in the true united action against imperialism and modern revisionism helping the fascist military dictatorship, has proven that the call made by Comrade Mao Zedong in the congratulatory message is correct and urgent. This call is: All Marxist-Leninists in the world unite, all revolutionary people in the world unite, overthrow imperialism, overthrow modern revisionism, and overthrow the reactionaries of various countries.

	The head of the New Zealand Communist Party delegation, National Committee Political Commission member Ray Nunes, said that the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour is an event of great domestic and international significance. The congress has fully adhered to the line of resolute and ruthless struggle against imperialism and modern revisionism. It has drawn a clear line between Marxism-Leninism and modern revisionism, declaring that there is no middle ground between Marxism-Leninism and modern revisionism.

	He said that the attendance of many representatives of Marxist-Leninist parties and organizations from various countries at this congress shows that new forces are rising all over the world, determined to fight imperialism and completely break with the revisionists who betray the interests of the working class and the laboring people.

	Nunes said that the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour shows that the Albanian Party of Labour and all Marxist-Leninists around the world, together with the Communist Party of China led by the contemporary greatest Marxist-Leninist, Comrade Mao Zedong, are advancing in unity, will end imperialism, and build a new world of socialist family.

	He emphasized that Comrade Mao Zedong's congratulatory message to the congress deeply embodies the spirit of proletarian internationalism and Marxist-Leninist revolution. The message clearly shows the unity of the Communist Party of China with the Albanian Party of Labour and all revolutionaries. It shows that the Communist Party of China has unlimited trust in the people of various countries, believing that they can defeat imperialism, achieve national liberation, and carry out socialist revolution.

	Nunes said that Mao Zedong's congratulatory message is a call to arms for the people of various countries to unite and advance towards the only correct path of victory, which is the class struggle and national liberation struggle under the banner of Marxism-Leninism. It has given and will continue to give new inspiration to Marxist-Leninists and revolutionaries around the world.

	The head of the Australian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninists) delegation, Central Committee member H. E. O'Sullivan, pointed out that China's successful launch of missile nuclear weapons tests, the great success of the revolution, and the achievements of the Red Guards have left the American imperialists and the Khrushchev revisionists dumbfounded. Then, the Albanian Party of Labour held its Fifth Congress, with representatives from many countries' communist parties, workers' parties, and Marxist-Leninist organizations attending the congress, which is another great blow to imperialism and modern revisionism.

	He said that the great Mao Zedong is an outstanding Marxist-Leninist of our time, who has made great contributions to the scientific development of Marxism-Leninism. The great Mao Zedong's congratulatory message to the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour is very profound. We suggest studying it seriously.

	In his speech, O'Sullivan particularly emphasized the issue of building a new type of party based on Mao Zedong Thought. He said that we must strive to build a new type of, genuine Marxist-Leninist revolutionary party. In this regard, we believe that studying Chairman Mao's works, especially "On Contradiction" and "On Practice," is very important, of course, all of Chairman Mao's works are extremely important.

	O'Sullivan criticized the so-called middle-of-the-road fallacy in the international communist movement. He said that there is no middle-of-the-road. You either support Marxism-Leninism and the world revolution, or you oppose it. If you oppose Marxism-Leninism and the world revolution, you are either consciously or unconsciously a counter-revolutionary.

	The head of the Belgian Communist Party delegation, Central Committee Secretary J. Griva, said: Comrade Mao Zedong correctly praised the Albanian Party of Labour and the heroic Albanian people in the congratulatory message, pointing out that the modern revisionists centered on the Soviet Communist Party leadership are merely "a handful of earth," while socialist Albania is "a towering mountain," thus inspiring all revolutionaries around the world to draw inspiration from the courage and firmness of the Albanian Party of Labour and the Albanian people.

	Griva emphasized that Comrade Mao Zedong pointed out in the congratulatory message the development, victory, and glorious future of the world revolution, calling on all Marxist-Leninists around the world to unite, all revolutionary people around the world to unite, to overthrow imperialism, to overthrow modern revisionism, to overthrow the reactionaries of various countries, and to build a new world without imperialism, without capitalism, without an exploitative system, thus greatly inspiring all revolutionaries to redouble their efforts and fight.

	The head of the Bolivian Communist Party delegation, Central Secretariat member Oscar Samora, said that this congress is of great historical significance to the Albanian people, and it has also made great contributions to the international communist movement. From this congress, we see that the Marxist-Leninist forces around the world are developing and growing stronger, while modern revisionism is on the decline. We see that the Communist Party of China and the Albanian Party of Labour are respected and loved by Marxist-Leninists and revolutionary people around the world, while the Soviet Communist Party leadership's attempt to isolate China and Albania in collusion with American imperialism has once again failed.

	In exposing the reactionary "united action" fallacy of the Soviet revisionist clique, he said that the Soviet revisionists accuse Marxist-Leninists and revolutionary people of not wanting to support the Vietnamese people by not joining their so-called "united action," which is a complete clich√©. The Soviet revisionists shout "support Vietnam" and give some material aid on the surface; but this cannot cover up their true purpose of sabotaging the Vietnamese people's struggle against American aggression for national salvation. Many facts show that the Soviet revisionists have long been colluding with American imperialists in "united action." The Soviet revisionists dream of using the "united action" label to act as the boss of the world revolution, in order to make a deal with the imperialist boss‚ÄîAmerica‚Äîto dominate the world together, which is absolutely deceiving Marxist-Leninists and revolutionary people.

	In talking about Chairman Mao's congratulatory message to the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour, he said that Chairman Mao's congratulatory message vividly embodies the great Mao Zedong Thought, which is an important document of Marxism-Leninism. This is not only a congratulatory message to the Albanian comrades but also to all Marxist-Leninists and revolutionary people around the world. We are greatly inspired.

	He said that our task is to make our party and our people learn and master the great Mao Zedong Thought‚Äîthe contemporary Marxism-Leninism. We believe that when the Bolivian people are armed with Mao Zedong Thought, they will be invincible.

	Samora said that we must work hard to strive for the great call of Comrade Mao Zedong: "All Marxist-Leninists in the world unite, all revolutionary people in the world unite, overthrow imperialism, overthrow modern revisionism, overthrow the reactionaries of various countries."

	The head of the Revolutionary Communist Party of Chile delegation, Central Secretariat member Jorge Diaz, said that the resolutions of the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour will mobilize the Albanian people to achieve greater successes in the socialist revolution and socialist construction, and will further strengthen the struggle against imperialism and modern revisionism, making new contributions to the world revolution and the defense and development of Marxism-Leninism.

	He said that Comrade Mao Zedong's congratulatory message to the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour is a great encouragement and support to all Marxist-Leninist parties and revolutionary people around the world. It will arouse their revolutionary fighting spirit and, with the ideas of the congratulatory message as a great weapon, strive for new victories.

	He pointed out that our party believes that the experience of the world revolution has proven the correctness of Mao Zedong Thought, which is the pinnacle of Marxism-Leninism. We are making great efforts to learn and apply Mao Zedong Thought in our actual struggle. We are firmly convinced that once the people of the world are armed with Mao Zedong Thought, they will definitely achieve a thorough victory over imperialism, revisionism, and reactionaries.

	The head of the Communist Party of Italy (Marxist-Leninists) delegation, General Secretary F. Dinocci, said that the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour is not only a historic event for the Albanian people who are striving to build socialism but also a historic event for all the people of various countries who are struggling against imperialism, modern revisionism, and reactionaries.

	He said that Marxist-Leninists of various countries must resolutely struggle against modern revisionism while struggling against imperialism and the reactionaries of various countries. There is no middle ground and no neutral path between Marxism-Leninism and revisionism. Any indecisive and compromising position will be beneficial to the conspiracies of imperialism and Khrushchev revisionism.

	He pointed out that Chairman Mao's congratulatory message to the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour has caused a great upsurge among the Albanian representatives and the representatives of brother parties. This congratulatory message is an important document of the glorious Communist Party of China, which takes Mao Zedong Thought as its guide, and is a creative development of Marxism-Leninism.

	Dinocci said that Chairman Mao's congratulatory message calls People's Albania "a great socialist beacon in Europe," which has greatly boosted the morale of European Marxist-Leninists because they are taking the Albanian Party of Labour as an example to build a resolute and indomitable vanguard of the proletariat against imperialism, the bourgeoisie, and modern revisionism.

	He said that Chairman Mao's congratulatory message points out the path of class struggle in all aspects. The call in the congratulatory message, "All Marxist-Leninists in the world unite, all revolutionary people in the world unite..." is our most basic teaching. Every Marxist-Leninist should use the congratulatory message to enrich his revolutionary ideological consciousness.

	The head of the Marxist-Leninist Communist Movement of France delegation, Political Bureau member and Secretariat member Ray Cassas, said that the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour is a great inspiration to the representatives of various Marxist-Leninist parties and organizations, including our delegation.

	He said that the congratulatory message of Comrade Kang Sheng, read at the congress, which is from Chairman Mao‚Äîcontemporary Lenin‚Äîstrongly armed our minds. The statement in the congratulatory message that "the traitors of the revolution are only 'a handful of earth,' while the revolutionary forces are 'a towering mountain,'" clearly draws a line between the new Marxist-Leninist forces and the decaying forces that betray the revolution.

	The head of the Socialist Revolutionary Party of Lebanon delegation, Central Committee Chairman Yu. Mubarak, pointed out that Chairman Mao's congratulatory message to the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour has great international significance and is a very important document. It vividly reflects the fraternal fighting friendship between the Communist Party of China and the Albanian Party of Labour. It shows the genuine unity of Marxist-Leninists on the basis of Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism, in the common struggle against American imperialism and modern revisionism represented by the Soviet leadership.

	He said that for us Lebanese communists, the congratulatory message is a great contribution from the brotherly Communist Party of China and its beloved teacher, our dear Comrade Mao Zedong, to strengthening the struggle against our common enemies‚ÄîAmerican imperialism and Soviet modern revisionism. We Lebanese communists regard Chairman Mao's congratulatory message as a program and as the Marxist-Leninist line of struggle in politics, ideology, theory, and organization. Chairman Mao's congratulatory message points out: "The truth of Marxism-Leninism is on our side. The international proletariat is on our side. The oppressed nations and peoples are on our side. More than 90% of the people of the world are on our side. Our friends are all over the world. We are not afraid of isolation, nor will we be isolated. We are invincible. A handful of miserable anti-China and anti-Albania people are doomed to failure." This is the truth!

	Mubarak said that imperialism, modern revisionism, and the reactionaries of various countries all oppose China and Albania; while all revolutionaries, all Marxist-Leninists, all oppressed peoples and nations, and all upright people in the world stand with China, with the Communist Party of China and Chairman Mao, with Albania and its Party of Labour and Comrade Hoxha.

	He said that the entire progressive humanity, all oppressed and exploited peoples, and all those who truly want to make a revolution under the red flag of Marxism-Leninism, achieve liberation, and strive for the victory of socialism, wholeheartedly welcome Chairman Mao's congratulatory message to the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour.
