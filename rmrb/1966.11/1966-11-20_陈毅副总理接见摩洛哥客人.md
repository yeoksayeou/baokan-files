Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Moroccan Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-11-20
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi today met with Hajji Ahmed Benani, Special Envoy of the Moroccan Prime Minister's Office, and had a friendly conversation with him.

Present at the meeting were Ding Xilin, Vice Chairman of the China-Africa Friendship Association, and others.

El Aroui, Chargé d'Affaires of the Moroccan Embassy in China, was also present.

### 陈毅副总理接见摩洛哥客人

1966-11-20
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见摩洛哥客人
    新华社十九日讯　陈毅副总理今天接见摩洛哥首相府特派员哈吉·艾哈迈德·贝纳尼，同他进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中非友协副会长丁西林等。
    摩洛哥驻中国大使馆临时代办埃尔·阿洛伊也在座。
