Title: Jinan Successfully Produces Single-Drum Shaft-Sinking Winch
Author:
Date: 1966-11-26
Edition: 2

During the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, the workers of Jinan Heavy Machinery Factory seized the revolution and promoted production, successfully designing and manufacturing China's first 16-ton single-drum shaft-sinking winch.

The 16-ton single-drum shaft-sinking winch is suitable for sinking mine shafts with depths of up to 800 meters. It can suspend heavy shaft-sinking equipment such as platforms and pumps, with a maximum lifting capacity of 16 tons. It is a product urgently needed by China's mining industry.

### 济南制成单筒凿井绞车

1966-11-26
第2版()
专栏：

    济南制成单筒凿井绞车
    在无产阶级文化大革命中，济南重型机械厂职工，抓革命，促生产，自己设计制造成功我国第一台十六吨单筒凿井绞车。
    十六吨单筒凿井绞车，适用于开凿深度在八百米以内的矿井井筒，可以悬吊吊盘、水泵等较重的凿井设备，最大悬吊重量为十六吨，是我国采掘工业急需的产品。
