Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-11-19
Edition: 4

	Vice Premier Chen Yi today met with the chairman of the Japan-China Friendship Association, Miyazaki Kiyoshi, and the advisor to the Japanese delegation commemorating the 100th anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's birth, Ito Takeo, the delegation leader, Makino Uchi Takeru, and other delegation members. He had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

	The president of the China-Japan Friendship Association, Liao Chengzhi, and others participated in the meeting.

### 陈毅副总理接见日本客人

1966-11-19
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见日本客人
    新华社十八日讯　陈毅副总理今天接见日中友协理事长宫崎世民，日本纪念孙中山诞生一百周年访华代表团顾问伊藤武雄、团长牧野内武人和代表团团员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    中日友协会长廖承志等参加了接见。
