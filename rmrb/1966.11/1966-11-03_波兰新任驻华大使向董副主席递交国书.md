Title: New Polish Ambassador to China Presents Credentials to Vice Chairman Dong
Author:
Date: 1966-11-03
Edition: 4

New Polish Ambassador to China Presents Credentials to Vice Chairman Dong

	Xinhua News Agency, November 2nd: The new Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Polish People's Republic to the People's Republic of China, Wladyslaw Rozynski, presented his credentials to Vice Chairman Dong Biwu this afternoon, accompanied by officials from the embassy. After presenting his credentials, Vice Chairman Dong Biwu had a friendly conversation with the ambassador. Present were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qiao Guanhua and others.

### 波兰新任驻华大使向董副主席递交国书

1966-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰新任驻华大使向董副主席递交国书
    新华社二日讯　波兰人民共和国新任驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使韦托德·罗津斯基，今天下午偕同大使馆官员向董必武副主席递交国书。递交国书后，董必武副主席同大使进行了友好的谈话。在场的有外交部副部长乔冠华等。
