Title: Celebrating the 49th Anniversary of the October Revolution
Author:
Date: 1966-11-07
Edition: 5

The China-Soviet Friendship Association, Beijing Municipal China-Soviet Friendship Association, and the State Council's Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries held a movie evening tonight to celebrate the 49th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution.

Vice Premier Chen Yi, Deputy Foreign Minister Qiao Guanhua, Vice Chairman of the China-Soviet Friendship Association Shi Ximin, and Deputy Director of the State Council's Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Song Yiping attended the movie evening.

The Chargé d'Affaires of the Soviet Embassy in China, Razdukhov, and the Soviet-China Friendship Association delegation led by Mayorovsky were invited to attend the movie evening. The movie "In the Name of the Revolution" was screened at the evening. At the beginning of the evening, Shi Ximin delivered a speech.

### 庆祝十月革命四十九周年  中苏友协等举行电影晚会

1966-11-07
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝十月革命四十九周年
    中苏友协等举行电影晚会
    新华社六日讯　中苏友协总会、北京市中苏友协和对外文委今天晚上举行电影晚会，庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十九周年。
    国务院副总理陈毅，外交部副部长乔冠华，中苏友协总会副会长石西民，对外文委副主任宋一平等，出席了电影晚会。
    苏联驻中国大使馆临时代办拉兹杜霍夫，以马耶夫斯基为首的苏中友协代表团，应邀出席电影晚会。晚会上放映故事影片《以革命的名义》。晚会开始时，石西民致了词。
