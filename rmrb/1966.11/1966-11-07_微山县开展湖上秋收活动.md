Title: Wei County Launches Autumn Harvest Activities on the Lake
Author:
Date: 1966-11-07
Edition: 2

Wei County in Shandong Province, which is rich in aquatic plants, has seen its commune members actively engaging in autumn harvest activities on the lake. According to incomplete statistics, the county has already collected over 15 million jin of various lake grasses, more than 400,000 jin of medicinal chickweed seeds, and a large amount of lotus seeds, water chestnuts, and lotus leaves, among other things.

### 微山县开展湖上秋收活动

1966-11-07
第2版()
专栏：

    微山县开展湖上秋收活动
    水生植物极为丰富的山东省微山县，各人民公社社员积极开展湖上的秋收活动。据不完全统计，这个县已采集各种湖草一千五百多万斤，可供药用的鸡头米四万多斤，此外，还有大量的莲蕊、菱角、荷叶等。
