Title: Comrade Enver Hoxha Meets with Foreign Delegations and Observers
Author:
Date: 1966-11-03
Edition: 4

	Comrade Enver Hoxha Meets with Foreign Delegations and Observers

	Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, November 3 — On the afternoon of October 31, Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, received at the Central Committee of the Labor Party the delegations and observers from various countries' communist and workers' parties and Marxist-Leninist organizations who were invited to attend the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Labor Party.

	They included: the Chinese Communist Party delegation led by Kang Sheng, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat, and Li Xiannian, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat, as well as delegations and observers from the communist and workers' parties and Marxist-Leninist organizations of Vietnam, North Korea, Romania, South Vietnam, Indonesia, New Zealand, Burma, Malaya, Belgium, Brazil, Australia, Peru, Lebanon, Ceylon, Colombia, Sudan, Ecuador, Bolivia, Chile, Italy, France, Austria, Haiti, Switzerland, Portugal, the Netherlands, Britain, and the United States, totaling 29 parties and organizations.

	Present at the reception were members, alternate members, and members of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party.

	The reception was held in a very warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 霍查同志接见外国代表团和观察员

1966-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    霍查同志接见外国代表团和观察员
    新华社地拉那一日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央第一书记恩维尔·霍查，十月三十一日下午在劳动党中央大厦接见了应邀参加阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第五次代表大会的各国共产党、工人党以及马克思列宁主义组织的代表团和观察员。
    他们是：由中共中央政治局常务委员、中央书记处书记康生和中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记李先念率领的中国共产党代表团，以及来自越南、朝鲜、罗马尼亚、越南南方、印度尼西亚、新西兰、缅甸、马来亚、比利时、巴西、澳大利亚、秘鲁、黎巴嫩、锡兰、哥伦比亚、苏丹、厄瓜多尔、玻利维亚、智利、意大利、法国、奥地利、海地、瑞士、葡萄牙、荷兰、英国和美国等二十九个共产党和工人党与马克思列宁主义组织的代表团和观察员。
    接见时在座的有阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、候补委员和中央委员们等。
    接见是在十分亲切和友好的气氛中进行的。
