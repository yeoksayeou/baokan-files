Title: Lao Writer Souvanthong Composes a Poem Full of Passion to Praise Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1966-11-15
Edition: 5

Lao writer Souvanthong, with boundless joy, composed a poem to enthusiastically acclaim China's successful launch of a missile and nuclear weapon test, hailing it as a brilliant victory of the great Mao Zedong Thought. The poem reads:

	China's brothers launch missiles,
	The world is astonished by their mighty power.
	It tells people: China's development
	Has far surpassed expectations.
	This powerful weapon for safeguarding world peace
	Is a brilliant victory of Mao Zedong Thought.
	Uncle Mao's thought, like the sun, is so bright,
	It is the eternal force that turns the world around,
	It is the ultimate truth.
	It is like a great bell, echoing across the universe.

### 老挝作家苏万吞满怀激情赋诗歌颂毛主席  毛泽东思想是扭转乾坤的永恒力量

1966-11-15
第5版()
专栏：

    老挝作家苏万吞满怀激情赋诗歌颂毛主席
    毛泽东思想是扭转乾坤的永恒力量
    新华社康开十二日电　老挝作家苏万吞怀着无比喜悦的心情写了一首诗，热烈欢呼中国发射导弹核武器试验成功，是伟大的毛泽东思想的光辉胜利。诗中写道：中国兄弟发射导弹，巨大威力世界惊叹。它告诉人们：中国的发展远远超过了预想。这保卫世界和平的强大武器，是毛泽东思想的光辉胜利。毛伯伯的思想啊，像太阳一样明亮，它是扭转乾坤的永恒的力量呵，是登峰造极的真理。它像一座洪钟，响彻宇宙大地。
