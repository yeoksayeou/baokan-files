Title: Three Scholars from Mali Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-11-06
Edition: 3

According to the China-Mali Cultural Cooperation Agreement, three scholars from Mali, invited to visit China for a friendly visit, arrived in Beijing on November 1. They are the Director of the National Museum of Mali and historian Moussa Oumar Cisse, anthropologist Youssouf Toure, and linguist Kanté. Relevant academic circles in China went to the airport to welcome them.

### 马里三位学者到京

1966-11-06
第3版()
专栏：

    马里三位学者到京
    新华社讯　根据中国马里文化合作协定，应邀来我国进行友好访问的马里国家博物馆馆长、历史学家穆萨·奥马·西，人种学家优素福·西塞，语言学家康塔夫人一行三人，在一日到达北京。我国有关学术界人士前往机场迎接。
