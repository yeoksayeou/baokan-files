Title: Qinghai Agricultural Machinery Factory Produces Hand-Held Tractor
Author:
Date: 1966-11-21
Edition: 2

The workers of Qinghai Agricultural Machinery Factory have displayed a revolutionary spirit of daring to think, daring to act, and daring to innovate. They have overcome numerous difficulties in technology, equipment, and raw materials, successfully developing a hand-held tractor. During the development process, whenever they encountered difficulties, they turned to Chairman Mao's works for guidance, drawing wisdom and strength from them. After more than 140 days of repeated practice, they finally overcame all obstacles and successfully developed the tractor. This hand-held tractor can be used with a plow to till the land or with a trailer to transport goods.

### 青海农牧机械厂制成手扶拖拉机

1966-11-21
第2版()
专栏：有所发现  有所发明  有所创造  有所前进

    青海农牧机械厂制成手扶拖拉机
    青海农牧机械厂职工，发扬敢想、敢干、敢创的革命精神，克服技术设备、原料材料等方面的种种困难，试制成功手扶拖拉机。在试制过程中，他们遇到困难，就向毛主席著作请教，从毛主席著作中汲取智慧和力量，经过一百四十多天的反复实践，终于排除万难，试制成功。这种手扶拖拉机可以挂犁耕地，也可以挂上拖斗拖运东西。
