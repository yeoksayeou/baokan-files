Title: Comrade Kang Sheng and Li Xiannian Receive Personnel from the Chinese Embassy in Albania
Author:
Date: 1966-11-15
Edition: 5

Comrade Kang Sheng, head of the Chinese Communist Party delegation, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and secretary of the Central Secretariat, and Comrade Li Xiannian, deputy head of the delegation, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and secretary of the Central Secretariat, received the entire staff of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, Chinese experts, and students studying in Albania on November 9. They also took a commemorative photo together.

### 康生和李先念同志接见我驻阿使馆人员等

1966-11-15
第5版()
专栏：

    康生和李先念同志接见我驻阿使馆人员等
    新华社地拉那十二日电　中国共产党代表团团长、中共中央政治局常务委员、中央书记处书记康生，副团长、中共中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记李先念等同志，九日接见了中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆全体工作人员、在阿尔巴尼亚的中国专家和留学生，并同他们一起摄影留念。
