Title: Another Great Victory Against Imperialist Monopoly in International Sports: The First Asian New Games Open Today in Phnom Penh
Author:
Date: 1966-11-25
Edition: 1

A grand-scale First Asian New Games will open on November 25 in Phnom Penh, the capital of Cambodia. This is the first comprehensive international sports competition independently and autonomously organized by the Asian emerging forces to break through the imperialist control and monopoly of international sports.

Athletes totaling more than 2,000 from 17 countries and regions, including Cambodia, China, Vietnam, Laos, North Korea, Mongolia, Pakistan, Nepal, Ceylon, Palestine, Syria, Iraq, Yemen, Indonesia, Lebanon, and Japan, will participate in the competition.

The decision to hold the First Asian New Games was unanimously adopted at the founding meeting of the Asian New Games Committee held in Beijing in September 1965. Since then, U.S. imperialism and its followers have been trying every means to sabotage and disrupt the event. However, their despicable plots have met with shameful failures time and again. The successful opening of this Games is a new victory for the unity and anti-imperialist struggle of the Asian emerging forces and a demonstration of strength against U.S. imperialism.

Cambodian Head of State Prince Norodom Sihanouk, the Royal Government, and the Cambodian people have made tremendous contributions to the successful convening of the First Asian New Games.

The First Asian New Games will include competitions in basketball, volleyball, athletics, swimming, diving, gymnastics, fencing, shooting, archery, tennis, table tennis, badminton, cycling, football, water polo, boxing, wrestling, judo, weightlifting, rowing, and sailing, totaling 21 events. All competition events are scheduled to end on December 6.

		BEIJING, Nov. 24 (Xinhua) -- Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram on Nov. 23 to Cambodian Head of State Prince Norodom Sihanouk and the First Asian New Games, extending warm congratulations on the opening of the Games. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

		To His Highness Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Head of State of Cambodia, and the First Asian New Games:

		On the occasion of the opening of the First Asian New Games, I, on behalf of the Government and people of China, extend warm congratulations to Your Highness and the Games, and pay high tribute to the athletes and sports workers from all participating countries.

		The First Asian New Games is another great victory for the Asian people in their struggle against imperialist monopoly of international sports. I am convinced that the holding of the First Asian New Games will greatly promote friendship and unity among the Asian people and athletes in their common struggle against imperialism, and will greatly promote the independent and autonomous development of sports in Asian countries.

		Under Your Highness's leadership, the Cambodian Government and people have done outstanding work in preparing and hosting the First Asian New Games. This is a major contribution to the New Games movement and the anti-imperialist struggle of the Asian people.

		The New Games movement is a new force. It will surely overcome the decadent forces of imperialism and grow stronger in the struggle. I wish the First Asian New Games a great success!

		Zhou Enlai
		Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
		Beijing, November 23, 1966

### 反对帝国主义垄断国际体育事业的又一次大胜利  第一届亚洲新运会今日在金边开幕  周恩来总理致电西哈努克亲王并亚新会表示热烈祝贺

1966-11-25
第1版()
专栏：

    反对帝国主义垄断国际体育事业的又一次大胜利
    第一届亚洲新运会今日在金边开幕
    周恩来总理致电西哈努克亲王并亚新会表示热烈祝贺
    据新华社金边二十四日电　规模盛大的第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会，将于二十五日在柬埔寨首都金边如期开幕。这是亚洲新兴力量人民冲破帝国主义对国际体育事业的控制和垄断，第一次独立自主举办的新型的综合性国际体育比赛。
      包括柬埔寨、中国、越南、老挝、朝鲜、蒙古、巴基斯坦、尼泊尔、锡兰、巴勒斯坦、叙利亚、伊拉克、也门、印度尼西亚、黎巴嫩、日本等十七个国家和地区的二千多名运动员将参加比赛。
    举办第一届亚洲新运会是一九六五年九月在北京举行的新运会亚洲委员会成立大会上一致通过的。从那时以来，美帝国主义及其追随者千方百计地阴谋阻挠和破坏。但是，它们卑鄙的破坏阴谋一次又一次地遭到可耻的失败。这届运动会的如期举行是亚洲新兴力量人民团结反帝的新胜利，也是对美帝国主义的一次示威。
    柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王、王国政府和柬埔寨人民，对第一届亚洲新运会的胜利召开作出了巨大的贡献。
    第一届亚洲新运会将进行篮球、排球、田径、游泳、跳水、体操、击剑、射击、射箭、网球、乒乓球、羽毛球、自行车、足球、水球、拳击、摔跤、柔道、举重、赛艇、帆船等二十一项比赛。全部比赛项目预定将于十二月六日结束。
    新华社二十四日讯　周恩来总理二十三日打电报给柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会，热烈祝贺这届亚洲新运会的开幕。电报全文如下：金边柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王并第一届
  亚洲新兴力量运动会：
    值此第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会开幕之际，我代表中国政府和中国人民，向亲王和大会表示热烈的祝贺，向参加大会的各国运动员和体育工作者致以崇高的敬意。
    第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会是亚洲各国人民反对帝国主义垄断国际体育事业的又一次大胜利。我深信，第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会的举行，对促进亚洲各国人民和运动员之间的友谊和团结反帝的共同事业，对促进亚洲各国体育事业独立自主的发展，必将起巨大的作用。
    在亲王的领导下，柬埔寨政府和人民为筹备和主办第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会进行了杰出的工作。这是对新兴力量运动和亚洲人民反帝斗争的重大贡献。
    新兴力量运动会是一种新生力量。它必将战胜帝国主义的腐朽力量，在斗争中不断茁壮成长。　祝第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会成功！
                            中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                             一九六六年十一月二十三日于北京
