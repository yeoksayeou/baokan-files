Title: Bravely Fighting the Enemy in the Sea Battle East of Chongwu, the Soldiers and Officers of a Certain Naval Vessel of the People's Liberation Army Navy Earn the Honorable Title of "Sea Tiger Boat"
Author:
Date: 1966-11-20
Edition: 3

	The soldiers and officers of a certain naval vessel of the People's Liberation Army Navy, who bravely fought the enemy in the sea battle east of Chongwu and earned the honorable title of "Sea Tiger Boat," are holding high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought. They are more consciously studying and applying Chairman Mao's works, vigilantly defending our country's maritime frontier. This is a scene of the soldiers studying Chairman Mao's quotations before training.
