Title: Liu Ningyi Meets with Australian Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-11-13
Edition: 5

Liu Ningyi, president of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, met with the entire delegation of the Australian Postal Workers' Union, headed by George Sleet, today and had a warm and friendly conversation with them.

### 刘宁一接见澳大利亚客人

1966-11-13
第5版()
专栏：

    刘宁一接见澳大利亚客人
    新华社十一日讯　中华全国总工会主任刘宁一，今天接见以乔治·斯雷特为首的澳大利亚邮政工人工会代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
