Title: The United States Sends "Special Forces" to Thailand to Massacre People
Author:
Date: 1966-11-14
Edition: 5

According to a report from Xinhua News Agency on the 12th, a message from Bangkok states: The United States recently sent more than 360 "special forces" to Thailand to serve as military advisors, strengthening control and training of the Thai military, in order to intensify the massacre of the Thai people.

According to a report from the United Press International, the members of this "special forces" unit were the so-called "guerrilla experts" who were first sent to South Vietnam six years ago to train the South Vietnamese puppet army in "counter-insurgency tactics." The report says that they will train "counter-insurgency warfare units" for the Thai Army.

### 美国派“特种部队”到泰国屠杀人民

1966-11-14
第5版()
专栏：

    美国派“特种部队”到泰国屠杀人民
    据新华社十二日讯　曼谷消息：美国最近派了三百六十多名“特种部队”到泰国充当军事顾问，加强对泰国军队的控制和训练，以便加紧屠杀泰国人民。
    据合众国际社报道，参加这支“特种部队”的是在六年以前第一次派到南越去训练南越伪军掌握“反游击战术”的所谓“游击专家”。报道说，他们将为泰国陆军训练“反游击战战术部队”。
