Title: The General Headquarters of the People's Liberation Army on the Reissuance of the Three Main Disciplines and Eight Points for Attention
Author:
Date: 1966-11-17
Edition: 6

The three main disciplines are as follows:

(1) All actions must follow orders;
(2) Do not take a single needle or thread from the people;
(3) All captured items must be turned over to the public.

The eight points for attention are as follows:

(1) Speak politely;
(2) Conduct fair trade;
(3) Return borrowed items;
(4) Compensate for damaged property;
(5) Do not beat or verbally abuse anyone;
(6) Do not damage crops;
(7) Do not harass women;
(8) Do not mistreat prisoners.

### 《中国人民解放军总部关于重行颁布三大纪律八项注意的训令》

1966-11-17
第6版()
专栏：

    三大纪律如下：
    （一）一切行动听指挥；（二）不拿群众一针一线；（三）一切缴获要归公。
    八项注意如下：
    （一）说话和气；（二）买卖公平；（三）借东西要还；（四）损坏东西要赔；（五）不打人骂人；（六）不损坏庄稼；（七）不调戏妇女；（八）不虐待俘虏。
    ——《中国人民解放军总部关于重行颁布三大纪律八项注意的训令》
