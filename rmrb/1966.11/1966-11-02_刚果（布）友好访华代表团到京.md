Title: Congolese (Brazzaville) Friendship Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-11-02
Edition: 4

The Congolese (Brazzaville) Friendship Delegation to China, led by the delegation head and member of the Central Committee of the Congolese National Revolutionary Movement, Prosper Kousakana, arrived in Beijing by airplane this evening. The delegation consists of five members. They are in China at the invitation of the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee for a friendly visit.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included the president of the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee, Xiao Qing, and other relevant officials.

The Chargé d'Affaires of the Congolese (Brazzaville) Embassy in China, Jean Daniel Bakula, and other diplomatic staff from the embassy also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 刚果（布）友好访华代表团到京

1966-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

    刚果（布）友好访华代表团到京
    新华社一日讯　由团长、刚果（布）全国革命运动中央委员会委员库萨卡纳·普罗斯佩尔率领的刚果（布）友好访华代表团一行五人，今晚乘飞机到达北京。代表团是应中国亚非团结委员会邀请，来我国进行友好访问的。
    到机场欢迎的，有中国亚非团结委员会主任廖承志以及其他有关方面负责人。
    刚果（布）驻中国大使馆临时代办让·丹尼尔·巴库拉及使馆其他外交官员也到机场迎接。
