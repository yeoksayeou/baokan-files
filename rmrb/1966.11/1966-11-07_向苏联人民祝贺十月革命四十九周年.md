Title: Congratulations to the Soviet People on the 49th Anniversary of the October Revolution
Author:
Date: 1966-11-07
Edition: 1

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and the State Council sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, and the Council of Ministers of the USSR on November 6 to congratulate the 49th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union,
To the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR,
To the Council of Ministers of the USSR:

On the 49th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, we, on behalf of the Chinese people, extend our heartfelt congratulations to the fraternal Soviet people.

We fervently hope that the Soviet people will inherit and carry forward the glorious revolutionary tradition of the great October Revolution.

We fervently hope that the Soviet people will defend the fruits of the October Revolution and continue to advance along the path of the great October Socialist Revolution opened by the great Lenin.

We fervently hope that the peoples of our two countries will work together to maintain the great friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples.

	Chinese Communist Party Central Committee
	Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China
	State Council of the People's Republic of China

November 6, 1966

### 向苏联人民祝贺十月革命四十九周年

1966-11-07
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央、人大常委会和国务院
    向苏联人民祝贺十月革命四十九周年
    热烈希望苏联人民继承和发扬伟大十月革命的光荣革命传统，捍卫十月革命的胜利果实；热烈希望我们两国人民共同努力，维护中苏人民的伟大友谊。
    新华社六日讯　中共中央、中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会和国务院六日打电报给苏共中央、苏联最高苏维埃主席团和苏联部长会议，祝贺伟大的十月社会主义革命四十九周年，贺电全文如下：苏联共产党中央委员会苏联最高苏维埃主席团苏联部长会议：
    在伟大的十月社会主义革命四十九周年的时候，我们代表中国人民向兄弟的苏联人民致以衷心的祝贺。
    我们热烈希望，苏联人民继承和发扬伟大十月革命的光荣革命传统。
    我们热烈希望，苏联人民捍卫十月革命的胜利果实，沿着伟大列宁所开辟的十月社会主义革命的道路继续前进。
    我们热烈希望，我们两国人民共同努力，维护中苏两国人民的伟大友谊。
            中国共产党中央委员会
            中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会
            中华人民共和国国务院
                                    一九六六年十一月六日
