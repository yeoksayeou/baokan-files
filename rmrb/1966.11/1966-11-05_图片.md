Title: Chairman Mao Reviews Revolutionary Students and Red Guards at Tiananmen
Author:
Date: 1966-11-05
Edition: 6

To the stirring melody of "The East Is Red," our great teacher, great leader, great supreme commander, and great helmsman Chairman Mao ascended the Tiananmen Gate to review revolutionary students and Red Guards from all over the country who had come to Beijing.
