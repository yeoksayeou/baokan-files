Title: Guinean Government Economic Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-11-11
Edition: 5

At the invitation of the Chinese government, the Guinean Republic's government economic delegation, led by the Minister of Economic Development of Guinea, Ismail Touré, arrived in Beijing by plane this evening. Vice Premier Chen Yi, along with over a thousand people from various sectors of the capital, went to the airport to give them a warm welcome.

People waved copies of "Quotations from Chairman Mao," along with bouquets, flags, and ribbons, shouting slogans: "Long live the friendship between the peoples of China and Guinea!" "Long live the unity of the peoples of Asia and Africa!" "Long live the unity of all the peoples of the world!" "Down with American imperialism and old and new colonialism!"

Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation also included Liao Chengzhi, Qi Pengfei, Yang Lin, Lu Xiuzhang, Ding Xilin, and other relevant officials.

Members of the Guinean government economic delegation, the Guinean ambassador to China, Kamara Sekou, and other diplomatic staff from the embassy also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

On November 10, the Guinean Republic's government economic delegation arrived in Beijing. Vice Premier Chen Yi and over a thousand people from various sectors of the capital went to the airport to welcome them. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter)

### 几内亚政府经济代表团到京  陈毅副总理和首都千余群众到机场热烈欢迎

1966-11-11
第5版()
专栏：

    几内亚政府经济代表团到京
    陈毅副总理和首都千余群众到机场热烈欢迎
    据新华社十日讯　应我国政府邀请来我国进行友好访问的几内亚共和国政府经济代表团，在几内亚经济发展部长伊斯梅尔·杜尔率领下，今晚乘飞机到达北京。国务院副总理陈毅，以及首都各界一千多人前往机场热烈欢迎。
    人们挥动着《毛主席语录》，挥舞着花束、彩旗和彩带，高呼口号：“中国—几内亚两国人民友好万岁！”“亚非人民大团结万岁！”“全世界人民大团结万岁！”“打倒美帝国主义和新老殖民主义！”
    前往机场欢迎的，还有廖承志、姬鹏飞、杨琳、卢绪章、丁西林等有关方面的负责人。
    几内亚政府经济代表团团员、几内亚驻中国大使卡马拉·塞古和大使馆其他外交官员，也到机场迎接。（附图片）
    十一月十日，几内亚共和国政府经济代表团到达北京。国务院副总理陈毅和首都各界一千多人前往机场迎接。　新华社记者摄
