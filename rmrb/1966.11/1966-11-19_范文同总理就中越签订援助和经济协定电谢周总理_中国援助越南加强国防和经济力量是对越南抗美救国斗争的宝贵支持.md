Title: Premier Phan Van Dong Thanks Premier Zhou for Aid and Economic Agreement
Author: Fan Wen Tong
Date: 1966-11-19
Edition: 1

New China News Agency, November 18th: Premier Zhou Enlai recently received a telegram from Premier Phan Van Dong of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The telegram expresses gratitude for China's support and aid to the Vietnamese people's struggle against American aggression and for national salvation. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

To Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:

Recently, the Vietnamese government's economic delegation led by Comrade Le Thanh Kiet, together with the Chinese government's economic delegation, successfully completed negotiations on aid and economic matters for 1967.

At a time when American imperialism is intensifying and expanding its aggression against our country, our Party Central Committee and government highly value the aid and economic agreement signed between our two countries. We regard it as a precious assistance from China to help us strengthen our national defense and economy, and to defeat the American aggressors. This is an active contribution to consolidating and developing the combat unity and brotherly cooperation between our two peoples for the common revolutionary cause.

On behalf of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and the Vietnamese people, I sincerely thank the Communist Party of China, the Government of the People's Republic of China, and the Chinese people for their valuable support and assistance to the Vietnamese people's just and victorious struggle against American aggression and for national salvation, in the spirit of proletarian internationalism. We also thank the Communist Party of China and the Government of the People's Republic of China for their warm and cordial reception of our government's economic delegation.

I extend my warmest regards to you and wish you good health.

Phan Van Dong
Premier of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
November 1, 1966, Hanoi

### 范文同总理就中越签订援助和经济协定电谢周总理  中国援助越南加强国防和经济力量是对越南抗美救国斗争的宝贵支持
范文同
1966-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

    范文同总理就中越签订援助和经济协定电谢周总理
    中国援助越南加强国防和经济力量是对越南抗美救国斗争的宝贵支持
    新华社十八日讯　周恩来总理最近收到了越南民主共和国政府总理范文同的一份电报。电报对中国给予越南人民抗美救国斗争的支持和援助表示感谢。电报全文如下：中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志：
    最近，由黎清毅同志率领的我国政府经济代表团同中华人民共和国政府经济代表团一起圆满地完成了关于一九六七年援助和经济方面的谈判。
    在美帝国主义加强和扩大对我国的侵略战争的时候，我们党中央和政府对我们两国之间这次签订的援助和经济协定给予很高的评价，并且把它看作是中国为使我们加强自己的国防和经济力量，打败美国侵略者而给予的宝贵援助。这是对巩固和发展我们两国人民为共同的革命事业而建立起来的战斗团结和兄弟合作所做出的积极贡献。
    我代表越南劳动党中央委员会、越南民主共和国政府和越南人民，衷心地感谢中国共产党、中华人民共和国政府和中国人民本着无产阶级国际主义精神，对越南人民正义的和必胜的抗美救国斗争所做出的宝贵支持和援助。我们感谢中国共产党和中华人民共和国政府对我国政府经济代表团的亲切和热情的接待。
    谨向您致以亲切的敬礼，并祝身体健康。
              越南民主共和国政府总理　范文同
                  一九六六年十一月一日于河内
