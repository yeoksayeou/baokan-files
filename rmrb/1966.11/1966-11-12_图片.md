Title: The Red Sun Rises in the East
Author:
Date: 1966-11-12
Edition: 6

The red sun rises in the East, how warm and benevolent it is, illuminating the hearts of all our ethnic peoples. We sing with all our hearts about the great leader Chairman Mao, wishing Chairman Mao eternal life!
