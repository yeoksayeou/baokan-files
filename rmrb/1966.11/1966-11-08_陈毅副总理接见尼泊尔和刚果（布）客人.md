Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Guests from Nepal and Congo (Brazzaville)
Author:
Date: 1966-11-08
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Chen Yi today received the entire delegation of the Nepalese Road Engineers, headed by Bishnu Bahadur Karki, and held a cordial and friendly conversation with them. Nepalese Ambassador to China, Rendra Subba, was also present at the reception.

On the same day, Vice Premier Chen Yi received the entire delegation of the Congolese (Brazzaville) Friendship Visit to China, headed by Kousakana Prospere, and held a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

### 陈毅副总理接见尼泊尔和刚果（布）客人

1966-11-08
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见尼泊尔和刚果（布）客人
    据新华社七日讯  陈毅副总理今天接见了以比斯努·巴哈杜尔·卡其为首的尼泊尔公路工程师代表团的全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时，尼泊尔驻中国大使伦迪尔·苏巴也在座。
    同日陈毅副总理接见以库萨卡纳·普罗斯佩尔为首的刚果（布）友好访华代表团的全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
