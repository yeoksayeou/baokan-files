Title: Comrade Nishizawa Ryūji and His Wife Depart Beijing for Their Home Country
Author:
Date: 1966-11-27
Edition: 5

Comrade Nishizawa Ryūji and his wife, who were invited by the China-Japan Friendship Association to visit China for friendly exchanges, departed Beijing by plane today to return to their home country.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Comrade Liao Chengzhi, president of the China-Japan Friendship Association, and others.

Also present to see them off were Japanese peace activist Nishizonoji Kōichi and others.

### 西泽隆二同志和夫人离京回国

1966-11-27
第5版()
专栏：

    西泽隆二同志和夫人离京回国
    新华社二十六日讯　应中国日本友好协会邀请来我国进行友好访问的西泽隆二同志和夫人，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的有中日友协会长廖承志等同志。
    前往欢送的还有日本和平人士西园寺公一等。
