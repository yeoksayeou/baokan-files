Title: The "Soybean Capital" Reaps Another Bountiful Harvest
Author:
Date: 1966-11-29
Edition: 2

The county of Yutushu in Jilin Province, renowned as the "Soybean Capital," has once again seen a bountiful harvest of soybeans this year. The broad masses of commune members are meticulously selecting the finest soybeans to sell to the state. According to inspections by relevant departments, the soybeans sold by Yutushu County this year are all of the first and second grade.

### “大豆之乡”大豆又丰收

1966-11-29
第2版()
专栏：

    “大豆之乡”大豆又丰收
    被誉为“大豆之乡”的吉林省榆树县，今年大豆又获丰收。广大社员正在精心挑选上等大豆向国家交售。据有关部门检查，今年榆树县交售的大豆，都是一、二等品。
