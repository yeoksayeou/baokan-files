Title: Singing the Hero Cai Yongxiang
Author:
Date: 1966-11-20
Edition: 4

Cai Yongxiang, a soldier from the People's Liberation Army, is a model of revolutionary heroism. His life is a shining example of the spirit of selfless dedication to the people and the revolutionary cause. Cai Yongxiang's songs are not just melodies; they are the voice of the revolution, echoing the aspirations and struggles of the masses.

Born into a poor family, Cai Yongxiang joined the army at a young age. He witnessed firsthand the hardships and sufferings of the people under the old society. This experience ignited a fiery passion within him to fight for a better future. He joined the Communist Party and became a soldier, dedicating his life to the revolutionary cause.

Cai Yongxiang's songs are deeply rooted in the lives of the common people. He sings about their struggles, their hopes, and their dreams. His music resonates with the hearts of the masses, inspiring them to stand up and fight for their rights. His songs are a powerful weapon in the cultural revolution, spreading the ideals of socialism and communism.

Cai Yongxiang's life and work are a testament to the power of revolutionary spirit. He is a true hero, a model for all soldiers and revolutionaries. His songs will continue to inspire generations to come, reminding them of the sacrifices made by those who fought for a better world.

Let us learn from Cai Yongxiang's example. Let us sing his songs, carry forward his spirit, and strive for the great cause of communism. Long live the revolutionary hero Cai Yongxiang! Long live the People's Liberation Army! Long live the Communist Party of China!
