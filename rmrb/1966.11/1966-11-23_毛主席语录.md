Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1966-11-23
Edition: 1

The working class must, in the struggle against the class enemy and in the struggle to transform nature, transform the whole of society, and at the same time transform itself. The working class must keep on learning in its work, and it must never stop learning.

		"On the Correct Handling of Contradictions Among the People"
