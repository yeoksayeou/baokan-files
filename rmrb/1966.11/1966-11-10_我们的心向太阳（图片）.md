Title: Our Hearts Turn to the Sun
Author: Zhou Yuying
Date: 1966-11-10
Edition: 6

In the vast expanse of the sky, the sun shines brightly, illuminating the earth with its radiant light. The sun represents the truth, the light, and the warmth of life. It symbolizes the great leader Chairman Mao and the brilliant light of Mao Zedong Thought.

The sun is the source of life. Without the sun, there would be no life on earth. Similarly, without Chairman Mao and Mao Zedong Thought, there would be no new China, no socialist revolution, and no socialist construction. The sun is the symbol of progress and development. It shines brightly, driving away the darkness and bringing light to the world. Chairman Mao and Mao Zedong Thought are the guiding principles for the Chinese people to advance towards a brighter future.

The sun is eternal and unchanging. It rises every day, bringing light and warmth to the earth. Chairman Mao and Mao Zedong Thought are also eternal and unchanging. They will forever guide the Chinese people on the path to socialism and communism.

Let us all unite under the banner of Chairman Mao and Mao Zedong Thought. Let us all strive to make our country prosperous and strong. Let us all work together to build a socialist China that is modern, powerful, and prosperous.

Our hearts turn to the sun, our hearts turn to Chairman Mao, our hearts turn to Mao Zedong Thought. Let us all follow the guidance of Chairman Mao and Mao Zedong Thought, and work together to build a bright future for our country and our people.
