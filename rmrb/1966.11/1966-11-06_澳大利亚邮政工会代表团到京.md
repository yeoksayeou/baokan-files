Title: Delegation of the Australian Postal Workers' Union Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-11-06
Edition: 3

At the invitation of the All-China Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union, a five-member delegation from the Australian Postal Workers' Union, led by George Sleet, General Secretary of the Australian Postal Workers' Union and Vice Chairman of the Australian Support Vietnam Committee, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 2nd.

### 澳大利亚邮政工会代表团到京

1966-11-06
第3版()
专栏：

    澳大利亚邮政工会代表团到京
    新华社讯　应中国邮电工会全国委员会邀请来我国进行友好访问的澳大利亚邮政工人工会代表团一行五人，由澳大利亚邮政工人工会总书记、澳大利亚支援越南行动委员会副主席乔治·斯雷特率领，二日乘飞机到北京。
