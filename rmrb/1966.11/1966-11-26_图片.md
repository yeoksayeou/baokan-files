Title: Members of Yuncheng County's Chengguan Commune Remember Chairman Mao's Instructions, Grasp the Revolution, and Promote Production
Author:
Date: 1966-11-26
Edition: 2

Members of Yuncheng County's Chengguan Commune in Shandong Province are firmly keeping in mind Chairman Mao's instructions. They are seizing the revolutionary momentum, promoting production, and actively participating in the well-digging campaign. This is the well-digging professional team of Xinzhuang Brigade engaged in well-digging.
