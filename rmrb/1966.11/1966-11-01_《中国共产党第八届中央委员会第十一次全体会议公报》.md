Title: Communiqué of the Eleventh Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1966-11-01
Edition: 6

We firmly believe that, under the leadership of the great leader Comrade Mao Zedong and the Communist Party of China, the entire military and civilian population of the nation, through self-reliance and striving for progress, will certainly be able to overcome all difficulties and obstacles. We will certainly be able to fulfill the mission entrusted to us by history and will not disappoint the expectations of the revolutionary people of the world.
