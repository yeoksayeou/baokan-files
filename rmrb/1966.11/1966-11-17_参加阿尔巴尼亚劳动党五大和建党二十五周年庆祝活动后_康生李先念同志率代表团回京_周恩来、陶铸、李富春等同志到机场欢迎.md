Title: After Attending the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour and the 25th Anniversary Celebrations of Its Founding, Comrades Kang Sheng and Li Xiannian Lead the Delegation Back to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-11-17
Edition: 1

The delegation attending the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour and the celebrations of the 25th anniversary of its founding, led by Kang Sheng, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and secretary of the Secretariat, and Li Xiannian, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and secretary of the Secretariat, returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon. The delegation members include Liu Xiao, Wu Xiuquan, and Peng Shaohui.

Comrades Zhou Enlai, Tao Zhu, and Li Fuchun, members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Tan Zhenlin, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Liu Ningyi, member of the Secretariat, Luo Guibo, member of the Central Committee, Zhao Yimin, alternate member of the Central Committee, Li Tianyou, vice chief of the General Staff of the People's Liberation Army, and Wang Li, Cao Yifu, and other comrades went to the airport to welcome them.

Diplomats from the Albanian Embassy in China and the Pakistani Embassy in China also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 参加阿尔巴尼亚劳动党五大和建党二十五周年庆祝活动后  康生李先念同志率代表团回京  周恩来、陶铸、李富春等同志到机场欢迎

1966-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

    参加阿尔巴尼亚劳动党五大和建党二十五周年庆祝活动后
    康生李先念同志率代表团回京
    周恩来、陶铸、李富春等同志到机场欢迎
    新华社十六日讯　参加阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第五次代表大会和阿尔巴尼亚劳动党成立二十五周年庆祝活动的中国共产党代表团团长、中共中央政治局常务委员、中央书记处书记康生，副团长、中共中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记李先念，代表团团员刘晓、伍修权、彭绍辉，今天下午乘飞机回到北京。
    到机场欢迎的有，中共中央政治局常务委员周恩来、陶铸、李富春，中央政治局委员谭震林，中央书记处书记刘宁一，中央委员罗贵波，中央候补委员赵毅敏，中国人民解放军副总参谋长李天佑，以及王力、曹轶欧等同志。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使馆和巴基斯坦驻中国大使馆外交官员，也到机场欢迎。
