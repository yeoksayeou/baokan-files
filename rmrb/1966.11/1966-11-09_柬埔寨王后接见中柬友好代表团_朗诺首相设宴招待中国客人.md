Author:
Date: 1966-11-09
Edition: 5

Cambodian Queen Receives Chinese-Cambodian Friendship Delegation

Lon Nol Premier Hosts Dinner for Chinese Guests

According to Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, November 7th: Cambodian Queen Sisowath Kossamak Nearireath Serey Vatthana received Zhang Qi, Vice Chairman of the China-Cambodia Friendship Association, and the entire delegation of the Chinese-Cambodian Friendship Delegation led by her this afternoon.

Zhang Qi conveyed greetings to the Cambodian Queen regarding her health. She conveyed the greetings of Premier Zhou Enlai and his wife, as well as Vice Premier Chen Yi, to the Cambodian Queen, and handed over two letters written by them to the Cambodian Queen.

The Cambodian Queen expressed her great pleasure in receiving the delegation led by Zhang Qi. She said, "You are our friends. When our country faced difficulties, Vice Premier Chen Yi gave us firm support. We will never forget this." She requested Zhang Qi to convey her greetings to Chairman Mao Zedong and other Chinese leaders.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, November 7th: Cambodian Prime Minister General Lon Nol held a banquet on the evening of the 6th to entertain the entire delegation of the Chinese-Cambodian Friendship Delegation led by Zhang Qi.

Lon Nol and Zhang Qi both spoke at the banquet.

Lon Nol, after praising the remarkable progress made by the Chinese people in many areas and the great influence they have had on the world, pointed out: "These progress and changes were achieved under the outstanding leadership and brilliant inspiration of Chairman Mao Zedong. We express our highest respect and deepest affection for him."

Zhang Qi, in her speech, praised the Cambodian people, under the leadership of Prince Norodom Sihanouk, for their resolute struggle to defend independence, sovereignty, and territorial integrity, and for their resolute struggle against the aggression, interference, and subversive activities of American imperialism and its lackeys. She also praised Cambodia's development and progress in national construction, firmly adhering to the path of self-reliance.

She emphasized that the continuous development of Sino-Cambodian friendship is a significant contribution to the unity of Asia and Africa in opposing imperialism and safeguarding world peace.

### 柬埔寨王后接见中柬友好代表团  朗诺首相设宴招待中国客人

1966-11-09
第5版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨王后接见中柬友好代表团
    朗诺首相设宴招待中国客人
    据新华社金边七日电  柬埔寨王后西索瓦·哥沙曼·尼亚里丽七日下午接见中国柬埔寨友好协会副会长张茜和由她率领的中柬友好代表团全体团员。
    张茜向柬埔寨王后问候健康。她转达了周恩来总理和夫人以及陈毅副总理对柬埔寨王后的问候，并且转交了他们写给柬埔寨王后的两封信。
    柬埔寨王后表示非常高兴接见由张茜率领的代表团。她说：“你们是我们的朋友。在我们国家遇到困难的时候，陈毅副总理给了我们坚决的支持。我们对此永远也忘记不了。”她请张茜转达她对毛泽东主席和中国其他领导人的问候。
    据新华社金边七日电柬埔寨王国政府首相朗诺将军六日晚上举行宴会，招待由张茜率领的中柬友好代表团全体成员。
    朗诺和张茜先后在宴会上讲了话。
    朗诺在赞扬中国人民目前在许多方面取得了了不起的进步，和在世界上发生了巨大的影响后指出：“这些进步和变化是在杰出的毛泽东主席的卓越的推动和天才的鼓舞下取得的，我们对他表示最崇高的敬意和最真挚的爱戴。”
    张茜在讲话中赞扬柬埔寨人民在西哈努克亲王领导下，为坚决地捍卫独立、主权和领土完整而进行的斗争，以及为反对美帝国主义及其走狗的侵略、干涉和颠覆活动而坚决进行的斗争。她还赞扬柬埔寨在国家建设方面，坚定不移地走自力更生的道路取得的发展和进步。
    她强调指出中柬友谊的不断发展是对亚非团结反帝和保卫世界和平的重大贡献。
