Title: Comrade Gu Dasun Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1966-11-07
Edition: 2

Comrade Gu Dasun, a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and a member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, passed away in Guangzhou on November 4, 1966, at the age of 69 due to illness despite medical treatment.

Comrade Gu Dasun joined the Communist Party of China in 1924. He served in various positions, including County Committee Secretary of the Communist Party, Secretary of the Seven-County Joint Committee, Military Department Head of the East River Soviet Government, Commander and Acting Political Commissar of the Eleventh Army of the Red Army, Commander-in-Chief of the East River Region Red Army Guerrillas, Director of the First Department of the Central Party School, Secretary and Secretary-General of the Central Committee's Northeast Bureau, Deputy Director of the Organization Department of the Northeast Bureau, and Director of the Transportation Department of the Northeast Administrative Committee. After the liberation of the entire country, he served as Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, Deputy Secretary of the Central Committee's South China Bureau, Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, and Vice Chairman of the Provincial Political Consultative Conference. In 1945, he was elected as an alternate member of the Central Committee at the Seventh National Congress of the Communist Party of China.

### 古大存同志逝世

1966-11-07
第2版()
专栏：

    古大存同志逝世
    新华社广州六日电　中国共产党中央委员会候补委员、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员古大存同志，因病经医治无效，于一九六六年十一月四日在广州逝世，享年六十九岁。
    古大存同志于一九二四年参加中国共产党，历任中共县委书记，中共七县联委书记，东江苏维埃政府军事部长，红军第十一军军长兼代政委，东江地区红军游击总指挥，中共中央党校一部主任，中共中央西满分局常委、秘书长，中共中央东北局组织部副部长，东北行政委员会交通部部长等职。全国解放以后，曾任广东省人民政府副主席，中共中央华南分局副书记，中共广东省委书记处书记，广东省副省长，省政协副主席等职。一九四五年在中国共产党第七届全国代表大会上被选为候补中央委员。
