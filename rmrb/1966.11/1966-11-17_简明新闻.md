Pakistan Changes County Name Imposed by British Colonizers

On November 14, Muhammad Musa, the governor of West Pakistan, announced at a centenary celebration ceremony in Sahiwal that the Pakistani government has decided to rename Montgomery County to Sahiwal County. During the British colonial rule, the British colonial authorities forcibly changed the name of this county to Montgomery County.

American Students in Rhode Island Attempt to Assault War Criminal Wheeler

On November 15, Chairman of the U.S. Joint Chiefs of Staff Wheeler, while defending the U.S. government's policy of aggression against Vietnam at a university in Providence, the capital of Rhode Island, was confronted by dozens of young students opposing U.S. aggression against Vietnam who rushed onto the stage to assault him. Amidst the jeers of the crowd, Wheeler, under the protection of numerous police officers who had been pre-arranged, hastily escaped through a side door of the auditorium and fled back to Washington.

U.S. and Japanese Reactionaries Conduct Joint Military Exercises

Just as the U.S. is escalating its aggression in Southeast Asia, the U.S. Air Force stationed in Japan and the Japanese "Air Self-Defense Force" began a three-day exercise in Japan on November 14. The exercise involved 360 U.S. aircraft and 500 Japanese "Air Self-Defense Force" aircraft.

Egypt Suffered Heavy Losses from Severe Storm

Heavy rain, hail, and storms hit large areas of Egypt on November 7, causing significant damage.

In the Cairo area, hailstones as large as eggs, a phenomenon not seen in 71 years, fell. In just half an hour, 16 millimeters of rain fell, causing disruptions to telephone, electricity, and traffic. In Alexandria, the heavy rain was accompanied by winds of 45 to 80 kilometers per hour. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

    巴基斯坦更改英殖民者定的县名
    西巴基斯坦省督穆罕默德·穆萨十四日在萨希瓦尔镇成为县府百年庆祝仪式上，宣布了巴基斯坦政府把蒙哥马利县重新命名为萨希瓦尔县的决定。在英国殖民统治期间，英国殖民当局硬把这个县改名为蒙哥马利县。
    美国罗得岛的学生要揍战犯惠勒
    美国参谋长联席会议主席惠勒十五日在罗得岛州首府普罗维顿斯的一个大学里，为美国政府侵略越南的政策辩解时，有好几十个反对美国侵略越南的青年学生登上讲台，要去揍他。在群众怒斥声中，惠勒在预先布置好的许多警察的保护下，仓惶从礼堂旁门溜出，匆匆逃回华盛顿。
    美日反动派又举行联合军事演习
    正当美国在东南亚加紧扩大侵略战争的时候，驻日本的美国空军和日本“航空自卫队”十四日开始在日本举行为期三天的演习。参加这次演习的有：美国飞机三百六十架和日本“航空自卫队”飞机五百架。
    阿联遭特大暴风雨袭击损失重大
    大雨、冰雹和风暴七日袭击阿联大片地区，造成了重大损失。
    在开罗地区，冰雹有鸡蛋那么大，是七十一年来从未见过的。半小时内下雨十六毫米，使电话、电灯和交通都中断了。在亚历山大港，大雷雨中还夹着时速四十五到八十公里的大风。　（据新华社讯）
