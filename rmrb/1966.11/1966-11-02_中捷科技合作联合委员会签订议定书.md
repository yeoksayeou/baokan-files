Title: Sino-Czechoslovak Joint Committee on Scientific and Technological Cooperation Signs Protocol
Author:
Date: 1966-11-02
Edition: 4

The thirteenth session of the Sino-Czechoslovak Joint Committee on Scientific and Technological Cooperation was recently held in Beijing. On October 28, the session signed the protocol of the meeting.

### 中捷科技合作联合委员会签订议定书

1966-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

    中捷科技合作联合委员会签订议定书
    新华社二十八日讯  中华人民共和国和捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国科学技术合作联合委员会第十三届会议最近在北京举行，并于十月二十八日签订了会议议定书。
