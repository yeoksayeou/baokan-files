Title: Chairman Mao Receives Mr. Sannanayake and His Wife
Author:
Date: 1966-11-08
Edition: 1

Chairman Mao received Mr. Sannanayake, Secretary-General of the Afro-Asian Writers' Permanent Bureau, and his wife. He had a cordial and friendly conversation with them. (Photo attached)

Chairman Mao received Mr. Sannanayake, Secretary-General of the Afro-Asian Writers' Permanent Bureau, and his wife, and at their request, signed his name in the "Quotations from Chairman Mao Tse-tung."

### 毛泽东主席接见森纳那亚克和夫人

1966-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席接见森纳那亚克和夫人
    新华社七日讯  毛泽东主席接见了亚非作家常设局秘书长森纳那亚克先生及夫人，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。（附图片）
    毛主席接见亚非作家常设局秘书长森纳那亚克先生及其夫人，并且应他们的要求，在《毛主席语录》上签名留念。
                                      新华社记者摄
