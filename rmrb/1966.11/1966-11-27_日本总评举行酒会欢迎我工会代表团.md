Title: The Japanese Trade Union Congress Holds a Reception to Welcome the Chinese Trade Union Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-11-27
Edition: 5

Tokyo, Nov. 26 (Xinhua) — The Japanese Trade Union Congress held a reception in Tokyo on the evening of November 25 to welcome a delegation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions led by Kang Yonghe, vice-chairman of the ACFTU.

More than 50 people attended the reception, including Satoshi Horii, chairman of the Congress, Futoshi Kaneta, vice-chairman, Sho Iwa, secretary-general, Minoru Takano, vice-chairman of the Japan Federation of Metal Workers' Unions, and leaders from various unions affiliated with the Congress.

Horii and Kang both spoke at the reception, emphasizing the fighting friendship between the peoples of China and Japan and the unity between the workers of the two countries.

The Chinese trade union delegation is in Japan at the invitation of the Japanese Trade Union Congress for a friendly visit.

### 日本总评举行酒会欢迎我工会代表团

1966-11-27
第5版()
专栏：

    日本总评举行酒会欢迎我工会代表团
    新华社二十六日讯  东京消息：日本工会总评议会二十五日晚上在东京举行酒会，欢迎由中华全国总工会副主任康永和率领的中国工会代表团。
    出席酒会的五十多人中包括总评议长堀井利胜、副议长兼田富太郎、事务局长岩井章、日本全国金属工会副委员长高野实和总评所属一些工会的领导人。
    堀井利胜和康永和先后在酒会上讲了话。他们在讲话中一致强调中日两国人民的战斗友谊和中日两国工人的团结。
    中国工会代表团是应日本工会总评议会的邀请到日本进行友好访问的。
