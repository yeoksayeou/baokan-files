Title: Always Marching Forward Without Halt
Author: Lǐ Xuāndé
Date: 1966-11-07
Edition: 6

	Always Marching Forward Without Halt
	By a soldier of the People's Liberation Army, Li Xuande

	Chairman Mao gave me a gun,
	I stand guard for my country.
	Under the scorching sun and rain,
	Through winds, snow, and frost,
	I bear the people's hopes,
	Defending the country's borders.
	My eyes are like lightning,
	My will is like steel,
	I watch the movements of wild beasts,
	I capture tigers, leopards, and wolves.
	The soldier's youth is as red as fire,
	I devote my whole heart to the Party!

	Chairman Mao gave me a pen,
	I strive for the revolution.
	I praise the working people,
	I record their great achievements,
	I sing of revolutionary heroes,
	I praise socialism;
	I refute the bourgeois fallacies,
	I strip the enemy's disguise,
	I attack the ghosts and snakes,
	I strike down the evil winds and poisons,
	I sweep away all harmful creatures,
	Revolutionary soldiers are invincible!

	Chairman Mao gave me a book,
	Every word shines like a pearl.
	The truth illuminates my heart,
	I will not lose my way on the path forward.
	I hold my head high and follow the Party,
	I overcome all difficulties and dangers.
	I am the east wind,
	I am the spring rain,
	I nourish the red sprouts,
	They grow into towering trees;
	The soldier's red heart is with the Party,
	Always marching forward without halt!

### 永远前进不停步
李宣德
1966-11-07
第6版()
专栏：

    永远前进不停步
    解放军某部　李宣德
    毛主席给我一杆枪，
    我为祖国来站岗。
    头顶骄阳暴雨，
    身披风雪冰霜，
    肩负人民希望，
    守卫祖国边疆。
    眼似电，
    志如钢，
    监视野兽行踪，
    捕杀虎豹豺狼。
    战士青春火样红，
    全心全意献给党！
    毛主席给我一支笔，
    我为革命来出力。
    颂扬劳动人民，
    记录丰功伟绩，
    歌唱革命英雄，
    赞美社会主义；
    斥资产阶级谬论，
    剥掉敌人画皮，
    讨伐牛鬼蛇神，
    打击妖风毒气，
    横扫一切害人虫，
    革命战士天下无敌！
    毛主席给我一部书，
    字字闪光像珍珠。
    真理照得我心里亮，
    前进路上不迷途。
    昂首挺胸跟党走，
    千难万险全排除。
    是东风，
    是春雨，
    滋润红色幼芽，
    长成参天大树；
    战士红心向着党，
    永远前进不停步！
