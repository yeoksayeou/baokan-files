Title: Vietnamese and Japanese Representatives Hold Separate Meetings to Solemnly Commemorate the 100th Anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's Birth
Author:
Date: 1966-11-16
Edition: 5

Vietnamese and Japanese representatives from various sectors held separate meetings to solemnly commemorate the 100th anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's birth.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 13th - Representatives from various sectors of the people of Hanoi and overseas Chinese representatives held a grand meeting on the evening of the 12th to commemorate the 100th anniversary of the birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen.

Huang Guo Yue, a member of the Standing Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front and President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, delivered the opening remarks. Subsequently, Vice President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, Nguyen Van Hien, gave a lengthy report at the meeting, detailing the revolutionary deeds of Mr. Sun Yat-sen.

Ambassador Zhu Qiwen praised Mr. Sun Yat-sen's revolutionary spirit and condemned the treacherous betrayal of the world people's revolutionary cause by the Soviet revisionist leadership.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 13th - The Vietnam-China Friendship Association and the Vietnam Overseas Chinese General Association jointly held a ceremony to unveil the memorial monument of Mr. Sun Yat-sen in Hanoi on the afternoon of the 12th.

The ceremony was held at No. 22 Hang Canh Street, where Mr. Sun Yat-sen resided during his revolutionary activities in Vietnam.

The inscription on the monument, written in both Vietnamese and Chinese, reads: "The great pioneer of the Chinese democratic revolution, Mr. Sun Yat-sen, resided here in 1904."

Chairman Ho Chi Minh sent a floral basket for the unveiling of the memorial monument of Mr. Sun Yat-sen.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo - More than 200 prominent figures from various sectors in Japan held a meeting on the afternoon of the 12th in Tokyo to solemnly commemorate the 100th anniversary of the birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen.

Miyazaki Ryosuke, representing the Sun Yat-sen 100th Anniversary Commemoration Committee, spoke, praising Sun Yat-sen's role in the Chinese national democratic revolution and the development of Sino-Japanese friendly relations.

Nakajima Kenzo said that the Chinese people have inherited Sun Yat-sen's will, not only completing the tasks of liberation and democratic revolution but also carrying out a great socialist revolution on an unprecedented scale in history.

Sun Pinghua said that the friendly relationship Mr. Sun Yat-sen established with the Japanese people in his time has, under new historical conditions, developed into a fighting friendship between the peoples of China and Japan in the common struggle against American imperialism. This friendship has become an irresistible historical current that no force or person can ever destroy.

### 越南和日本各界代表分别举行集会  隆重纪念孙中山诞生一百周年

1966-11-16
第5版()
专栏：

    越南和日本各界代表分别举行集会
    隆重纪念孙中山诞生一百周年
    据新华社河内十三日电　河内市越南各界人民代表和华侨代表十二日晚上隆重集会，纪念孙中山先生诞生一百周年。
    越南祖国战线中央委员会主席团委员、越中友好协会会长黄国越在致开幕词后，越中友好协会副会长阮文煊在会上作了长篇报告，详细地介绍了孙中山先生的革命事迹。
    朱其文大使在讲话中赞扬了孙中山先生的革命精神，并谴责了苏联修正主义领导集团对世界人民革命事业的可耻叛卖。
    据新华社河内十三日电　越中友好协会和越南华侨联合总会，十二日下午在河内联合举行了孙中山先生纪念碑揭幕仪式。
    仪式是在孙中山先生在越南进行革命活动期间，曾经居住的河内行帆街二十二号举行的。
    用越中两国文字写成的碑文是：“中国伟大的民主革命先行者孙中山先生于一九○四年曾居于此”。
    胡志明主席为孙中山先生纪念碑的揭幕赠送了花篮。
    据新华社讯　东京消息：日本各界著名人士二百多人十二日下午，在东京举行集会，隆重纪念孙中山先生诞辰一百周年。
    宫崎龙介代表孙中山先生诞生一百周年纪念事业委员会讲话，他赞扬孙中山对中国民族民主革命和开展日中友好关系所起的作用。
    中岛健藏说，中国人民继承了孙中山的遗志，不仅完成了解放和民主革命的任务，而且以历史上空前的规模，深入地进行着伟大的社会主义革命。
    孙平化讲话说，孙中山先生当年在日本人民中建立的友好关系，在新的历史条件下，在反对美帝国主义的共同斗争中，发展成为中日两国人民的战斗友谊。这一友谊已形成一个不可阻挡的历史洪流，是任何势力，任何人也永远破坏不了的。
