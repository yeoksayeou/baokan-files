Title: Chinese Embassy Staff in Ghana Arrive in Paris En Route to China
Author:
Date: 1966-11-07
Edition: 5

After the reactionary military junta in Ghana unilaterally announced the severance of diplomatic relations between China and Ghana, the Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Ghana, Huang Shixian, and all embassy staff, en route back to China, arrived in Paris by airplane this afternoon.

The correspondent from Xinhua News Agency in Accra also arrived on the same flight.

They were warmly welcomed at the airport by Chinese Ambassador to France Huang Zhen, embassy staff, and representatives of Chinese students studying in France.

### 我驻加纳使馆人员在回国途中到达巴黎  黄镇大使和使馆人员及留学生到机场热烈欢迎

1966-11-07
第5版()
专栏：

    我驻加纳使馆人员在回国途中到达巴黎
    黄镇大使和使馆人员及留学生到机场热烈欢迎
    新华社巴黎五日电　在加纳反动军事政变当局片面宣布中断中国和加纳两国关系后，中国驻加纳大使馆临时代办黄世燮和使馆全部人员在撤离阿克拉回国途中今天下午乘飞机到达巴黎。
    新华社驻阿克拉记者也同机到达。
    他们在机场上受到中国驻法国大使黄镇和使馆工作人员以及中国留学生代表的热烈欢迎。
