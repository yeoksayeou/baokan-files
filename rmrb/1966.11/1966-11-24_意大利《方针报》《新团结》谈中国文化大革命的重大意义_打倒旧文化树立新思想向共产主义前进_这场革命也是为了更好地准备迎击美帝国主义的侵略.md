Title: Italian "Guideline" and "New Unity" Discuss the Great Significance of China's Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1966-11-24
Edition: 4

	Rome, Nov. 24 (Xinhua) - The fifth and sixth issues of the Italian "Guideline" published an article titled "Long Live the Proletarian Cultural Revolution," praising China's proletarian cultural revolution as an "example and model" that encourages those fighting for world socialism.

	The article pointed out that China's proletarian cultural revolution is a "new stage of continuous revolution, a higher stage." In light of the lessons from the emergence of revisionism in the Soviet Union, China's cultural revolution is "extremely necessary" for the entire international communist movement and will have a "great international influence."

	The article further stated that in the face of increasing war provocations by U.S. imperialism against China, the question is whether to prepare to thoroughly repel the aggression of U.S. imperialists or, conversely, to avoid conflicts, turning the path of fighting to the end for the liberation of the peoples and oppressed classes of various countries into compromise and concession. The article said, "The purpose of the cultural revolution is also to better prepare the entire nation to repel U.S. aggression and, when the U.S. launches an invasion, to defeat this aggression."

	The article condemned the despicable actions of the Soviet revisionist clique and their Italian revisionist followers in slandering China's cultural revolution. The article said, "The proletariat has a good instinct: they know that those praised by the bourgeoisie are their enemies, and all those slandered by them are their friends. The more intense the slander, the better these friends are."

	The article concluded, "Today, the cultural revolution taking place in China is supported by tens of millions of people around the world, who regard it as the most advanced symbol of the liberation of oppressed peoples and the world socialist revolution."

	Rome, Nov. 24 (Xinhua) - The tenth issue of the Italian "New Unity" published an article pointing out, "The proletarian cultural revolution is not only important for China but also for the international communist movement."

	The article, titled "Communism, Revisionism, and the Proletarian Cultural Revolution," pointed out that the experience of the international communist movement, especially the emergence of Khrushchev's modern revisionism in the Soviet Union, shows that in building a socialist society, it is not enough to merely destroy the old bourgeois state machine. "It is also necessary to conduct socialist education, to ensure that the younger generation always maintains a vigorous revolutionary spirit, to change people's thoughts, to struggle against and overthrow the old ideas, old culture, old customs, and old habits of the old bourgeois society that still exist in the socialist society period. If these old social ideologies are allowed to spread freely and not overthrown, they can create conditions for counter-revolutionary development, endangering the survival of socialist countries."

	The article highly praised the Chinese Red Guards and revolutionary youth for "boldly carrying out the proletarian cultural revolution," making their parents' revolutionary epic even more vivid, adding new content, and making all imperialists, bourgeoisie, and revisionists around the world tremble with fear.

	The article said that through the proletarian cultural revolution, the Chinese people will establish new socialist thoughts. With these new thoughts, they will achieve rock-solid unity in both thought and action; they will be able to resist and defeat any attacks launched by imperialism; and they will be able to advance more rapidly toward socialism and communism.

	The article said that various bourgeois elements and revisionists are violently opposing the proletarian cultural revolution, "because they know that this revolution signifies a new failure for them, a failure that will inevitably accelerate their doom."

### 意大利《方针报》《新团结》谈中国文化大革命的重大意义  打倒旧文化树立新思想向共产主义前进  这场革命也是为了更好地准备迎击美帝国主义的侵略

1966-11-24
第4版()
专栏：

    意大利《方针报》《新团结》谈中国文化大革命的重大意义
    打倒旧文化树立新思想向共产主义前进
    这场革命也是为了更好地准备迎击美帝国主义的侵略
    新华社讯　罗马消息：意大利《方针报》第五——六期发表题为《无产阶级文化大革命万岁》的文章，赞扬中国无产阶级文化大革命是“鼓励那些为世界的社会主义而斗争的人们的榜样和典范”。
    文章指出，中国的无产阶级文化大革命是“不断革命的一个新阶段，一个更高的阶段”。鉴于苏联出现修正主义的教训，中国的文化大革命对于整个国际共产主义运动来说是“极其需要的”，将具有“巨大的国际影响”。
    文章还说，在美帝国主义日益加紧对中国进行的战争挑衅的情况下，“是准备彻底迎击美帝国主义者的侵略，还是相反，力图避免冲突，使为各国人民和被压迫阶级的解放而斗争到底的路线变成妥协和让步”。文章说，“文化革命的目的也是为了更好地使全国准备迎击美国的侵略，一旦美国进行侵略时，就击败这种侵略”。
    文章谴责苏联修正主义集团及其追随者意大利的修正主义分子诽谤中国文化大革命的卑鄙行径。文章说，无产阶级有一个好的本能：懂得那些受到资产阶级赞扬的人都是他们的敌人，而所有受到他们诽谤的人都是自己的朋友。受到的诽谤愈强烈，这些朋友就愈好。
    文章最后说：“今天中国正在进行的文化革命，得到全世界千百万人的支持，他们把它看作被压迫人民解放和世界社会主义革命的最先进的象征。”
    据新华社讯　罗马消息：意大利《新团结》第十期发表文章指出：“无产阶级文化革命不仅对中国，而且对国际共产主义运动都是有重要意义的。”
    这篇题为《共产主义、修正主义和无产阶级文化大革命》的文章指出，国际共产主义运动的经验，特别是苏联出现赫鲁晓夫现代修正主义的经验，说明了建设社会主义社会单单摧毁旧的资产阶级的国家机器是不够的，“还必须进行社会主义教育、必须使年青一代始终保持着朝气蓬勃的革命精神，必须改变人的思想，必须与社会主义社会时期还存在的资产阶级社会的旧思想、旧文化、旧风俗和旧习惯进行斗争，并把它们打倒。这些旧社会的思想意识，如果让它自由泛滥，不打倒它，就可能产生反对社会主义的舆论，造成反革命发展的条件，危害社会主义国家的生存。”
    文章盛赞中国的红卫兵和革命青年“大胆地进行无产阶级文化革命”，“使他们父辈写下的革命史诗更为生动，增添了新的内容，使全世界的帝国主义者、资产阶级和修正主义者心惊胆颤。”
    文章说，中国人民通过无产阶级文化大革命，将树立起新的社会主义思想。有了这种新思想，就将达到思想上和行动上的磐石般的团结；就能抵抗和击败帝国主义所发动的任何进攻；就能日益迅速地向社会主义和共产主义前进。
    文章说，形形色色的资产阶级分子和修正主义者狂暴地反对无产阶级文化革命，“他们知道，这场革命对他们来说，标志一种新的失败，这一失败将无可挽救地加速他们的灭亡。”
