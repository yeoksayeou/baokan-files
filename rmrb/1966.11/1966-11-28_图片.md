Title: The Great Leader Chairman Mao Trusts, Cares for, and Supports the Masses' Revolutionary Movement
Author:
Date: 1966-11-28
Edition: 6

The great leader Chairman Mao most trusts the masses, most cares for the masses, and most supports the masses' revolutionary movement. In just over three months, Chairman Mao has reviewed the cultural revolution army eight times. Under Chairman Mao's personal leadership, the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution is developing in a deeper, broader, and healthier direction.
