Title: Commemorating the 100th Anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's Birth
Author:
Date: 1966-11-15
Edition: 6

To commemorate the 100th anniversary of the birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen, the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications began issuing commemorative stamps featuring Mr. Sun Yat-sen's portrait on the 12th.

The commemorative stamp issued by the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications for the 100th anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's birth.

### 纪念孙中山诞生百周年  邮电部发行纪念邮票

1966-11-15
第6版()
专栏：

    纪念孙中山诞生百周年
    邮电部发行纪念邮票
    新华社讯　为纪念孙中山先生诞生一百周年，邮电部从十二日开始发行了有孙中山先生像的纪念邮票。（附图片）
