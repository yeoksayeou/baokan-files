Title: The People Are the Motive Force of History, the Masses Are the True Heroes
Author:
Date: 1966-11-27
Edition: 2

The people, and only the people, are the motive force in the creation of world history.

		*On the Coalition Government* (April 24, 1945)
		*Selected Works of Mao Zedong*, Volume 3, Page 1031

Among all things in the world, people are the most precious. Under the leadership of the Communist Party, as long as there are people, any miracle can be created.

		*The Failure of Idealist Historiography* (September 16, 1949)
		*Selected Works of Mao Zedong*, Volume 4, Page 1516

It must be understood that the masses are the true heroes, while we ourselves are often naive and laughable. Without understanding this, one cannot gain even the most basic knowledge.

		*Preface and Postscript to Investigations of the Rural Economy* (March and April 1941)
		*Selected Works of Mao Zedong*, Volume 3, Page 790

“Three cobblers with their wits combined make a Wu Zixu,” which means the masses have great creative power. Among the Chinese people, there are indeed tens of thousands of “Wu Zixu.” Every village and every town has its own “Wu Zixu.”

		*Organize* (November 29, 1943)
		*Selected Works of Mao Zedong*, Volume 3, Page 936

Apart from the leadership of the Party, the 600 million people are a decisive factor. The more people, the more discussions, the higher the enthusiasm, and the greater the effort.

		*Introducing a Cooperative* (April 15, 1958)
		*Selected Readings from the Works of Mao Zedong*, Volume A, Page 381

The masses have limitless creative power. They can organize themselves, advance to every place and department where they can exert their strength, advance in the depth and breadth of production, and create more and more welfare for themselves.

		*Commentary on the Article “Surplus Labor Finds an Outlet”* (1955)
		*The Socialist Upsurge in China’s Countryside*, Volume 2, Page 578

What is the true iron wall? It is the masses, the thousands and tens of thousands of people who genuinely support the revolution. This is the true iron wall, which no force can break, completely unbreakable. The counter-revolutionaries cannot break us, but we will break the counter-revolutionaries. By rallying thousands and tens of thousands of masses around the revolutionary government, developing our revolutionary war, we can annihilate all counter-revolutionaries and seize all of China.

		*Care for the People’s Livelihood, Pay Attention to Work Methods* (January 27, 1934)
		*Selected Works of Mao Zedong*, Volume 1, Page 134

Every comrade should understand that as long as we rely on the people, firmly believe in the boundless creative power of the masses, trust the people, and unite with them, then any difficulty can be overcome, and any enemy can be defeated by us, rather than defeating us.

		*On the Coalition Government* (April 24, 1945)
		*Selected Works of Mao Zedong*, Volume 3, Page 1097

As long as we can master Marxism-Leninism, trust the masses, closely unite with them, and lead them forward, we can completely overcome any obstacle and defeat any difficulty. Our power is invincible.

		*The Present Situation and Our Tasks* (December 25, 1947)
		*Selected Works of Mao Zedong*, Volume 4, Page 1260

We must believe in the masses, and we must believe in the Party. These are two fundamental principles. If we doubt these two principles, then we cannot accomplish anything.

		*On the Issue of Agricultural Cooperativization* (July 31, 1955)
		*Selected Readings from the Works of Mao Zedong*, Volume A, Page 300

The Chinese people will see that once the destiny of China is in the hands of the people themselves, China will shine like the rising sun in the east, with its brilliant light illuminating the earth, quickly cleansing the muddy waters left by the reactionary government, healing the wounds of war, and building a new, strong, and genuine People’s Republic.

### 人民是创造历史的动力，群众是真正的英雄

1966-11-27
第2版()
专栏：毛主席关于群众路线的语录

    人民是创造历史的动力，群众是真正的英雄
    人民，只有人民，才是创造世界历史的动力。
                《论联合政府》（一九四五年四月二十四日）
                《毛泽东选集》第三卷第一○三一页
    世间一切事物中，人是第一个可宝贵的。在共产党领导下，只要有了人，什么人间奇迹也可以造出来。
                《唯心历史观的破产》（一九四九年九月十六日）
                  《毛泽东选集》第四卷第一五一六页
    必须明白：群众是真正的英雄，而我们自己则往往是幼稚可笑的，不了解这一点，就不能得到起码的知识。
                《〈农村调查〉的序言和跋》（一九四一年三月、四月）
                《毛泽东选集》第三卷第七九○页
    “三个臭皮匠，合成一个诸葛亮”，这就是说，群众有伟大的创造力。中国人民中间，实在有成千成万的“诸葛亮”，每个乡村，每个市镇，都有那里的“诸葛亮”。
                《组织起来》（一九四三年十一月二十九日）
                《毛泽东选集》第三卷第九三六页
    除了党的领导之外，六亿人口是一个决定的因素。人多议论多，热气高，干劲大。
                《介绍一个合作社》（一九五八年四月十五日）
                  《毛泽东著作选读》甲种本第三八一页
    人民群众有无限的创造力。他们可以组织起来，向一切可以发挥自己力量的地方和部门进军，向生产的深度和广度进军，替自己创造日益增多的福利事业。
                  《多余劳动力找到了出路》一文的按语（一九五五年）
                  《中国农村的社会主义高潮》中册第五七八页
    真正的铜墙铁壁是什么？是群众，是千百万真心实意地拥护革命的群众。这是真正的铜墙铁壁，什么力量也打不破的，完全打不破的。反革命打不破我们，我们却要打破反革命。在革命政府的周围团结起千百万群众来，发展我们的革命战争，我们就能消灭一切反革命，我们就能夺取全中国。
                  《关心群众生活，注意工作方法》（一九三四年一月二十
                        七日）
                  《毛泽东选集》第一卷第一三四页
    应该使每一个同志懂得，只要我们依靠人民，坚决地相信人民群众的创造力是无穷无尽的，因而信任人民，和人民打成一片，那就任何困难也能克服，任何敌人也不能压倒我们，而只会被我们所压倒。
                    《论联合政府》（一九四五年四月二十四日）
                  《毛泽东选集》第三卷第一○九七页
    只要我们能够掌握马克思列宁主义的科学，信任群众，紧紧地和群众一道，并领导他们前进，我们是完全能够超越任何障碍和战胜任何困难的，我们的力量是无敌的。
                    《目前形势和我们的任务》（一九四七年十二月二十五日）
                    《毛泽东选集》第四卷第一二六○页
    我们应当相信群众，我们应当相信党，这是两条根本的原理。如果怀疑这两条原理，那就什么事情也做不成了。
                    《关于农业合作化问题》（一九五五年七月三十一日）
                    《毛泽东著作选读》甲种本第三○○页
    中国人民将会看见，中国的命运一经操在人民自己的手里，中国就将如太阳升起在东方那样，以自己的辉煌的光焰普照大地，迅速地荡涤反动政府留下来的污泥浊水，治好战争的创伤，建设起一个崭新的强盛的名副其实的人民共和国。
                    《在新政治协商会议筹备会上的讲话》（一九四九年六月
                        十五日）
                    《毛泽东选集》第四卷第一四七○——一四七一页
