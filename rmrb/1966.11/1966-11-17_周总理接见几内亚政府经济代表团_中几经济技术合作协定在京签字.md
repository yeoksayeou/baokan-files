Title: Premier Zhou Receives Guinean Government Economic Delegation; Sino-Guinean Economic and Technical Cooperation Agreement Signed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-11-17
Edition: 1

Premier Zhou today afternoon received Guinean Minister of Economic Development Ismail Dior and the members of the Guinean government economic delegation he led, and had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Vice Premier Chen Yi and other relevant department heads Qi Pengfei, Yang Lin, and Lu Xiuzhang.

Guinean Ambassador to China Segou Camara was also present.

After the reception, Premier Zhou hosted a banquet to entertain the Guinean friends.

The economic and technical cooperation agreement and protocol between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Guinea, as well as the agreement on trade credit provided by the Government of the People's Republic of China to the Government of the Republic of Guinea and the 1967 trade protocol between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Republic of Guinea, were signed in Beijing this afternoon.

Premier Zhou attended the signing ceremony.

Vice Premier Chen Yi and Guinean Minister of Economic Development Ismail Dior signed the agreements and protocols on behalf of both sides.

Also present at the signing ceremony from the Chinese side were: Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qi Pengfei, Vice Chairman of the State Economic Commission Yang Lin, and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Lu Xiuzhang.

Present from the Guinean side were: Guinean Ambassador to China Segou Camara and members of the Guinean government economic delegation.

### 周总理接见几内亚政府经济代表团  中几经济技术合作协定在京签字

1966-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见几内亚政府经济代表团
    中几经济技术合作协定在京签字
    新华社十六日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了几内亚经济发展部部长伊斯梅尔·杜尔和由他率领的几内亚政府经济代表团成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有陈毅副总理，其他有关方面负责人姬鹏飞、杨琳、卢绪章等。
    几内亚驻中国大使塞古·卡马拉也在座。
    接见后，周恩来总理设便宴招待几内亚朋友。
    新华社十六日讯　中华人民共和国和几内亚共和国经济技术合作协定和议定书，今天下午在北京签字。同时签字的还有中华人民共和国政府向几内亚共和国政府提供贸易贷款的协定和中华人民共和国政府和几内亚共和国政府一九六七年贸易议定书。
    周恩来总理出席了签字仪式。
    国务院副总理陈毅和几内亚经济发展部长伊斯梅尔·杜尔，分别代表双方在协定和议定书上签字。
    中国方面出席签字仪式的还有：外交部副部长姬鹏飞，对外经委副主任杨琳，对外贸易部副部长卢绪章等。
    几内亚方面出席签字仪式的有：几内亚驻中国大使塞古·卡马拉，几内亚政府经济代表团团员。
