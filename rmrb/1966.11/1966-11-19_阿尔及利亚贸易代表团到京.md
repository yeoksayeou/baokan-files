Title: Algerian Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-11-19
Edition: 4

An Algerian trade delegation led by Zidour Amhed, a specialist from the Algerian Ministry of Commerce, arrived in Beijing by plane this evening. The delegation is here to discuss issues related to China's aid in the construction of an exhibition hall in Algeria.

### 阿尔及利亚贸易代表团到京

1966-11-19
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚贸易代表团到京
    新华社十八日讯　以阿尔及利亚商业部专员齐德尔·阿默德为首的阿尔及利亚贸易代表团，今天晚间乘飞机到达北京。代表团是来商谈我国援助阿尔及利亚修建展览馆项目的有关问题的。
