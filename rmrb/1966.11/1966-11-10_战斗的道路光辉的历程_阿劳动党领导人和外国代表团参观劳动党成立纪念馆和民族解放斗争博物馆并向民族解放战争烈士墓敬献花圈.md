Title: The Glorious Path of Struggle
Author:
Date: 1966-11-10
Edition: 4

In the wake of the successful conclusion of the Fifth Congress of the Party of Labour of Albania and on the occasion of the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Party of Labour, the leaders of the Party of Labour of Albania and the delegations of the Communist Party of China and other parties, who were invited to attend the Fifth Congress of the Party of Labour of Albania, visited the Party of Labour of Albania Museum and the Museum of the National Liberation Struggle on November 8.

Those who went to visit included: Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee Adil Carcani, Behar Balluku, Haxhi Toska, Hysni Kapo, Manush Myftiu, Mehmet Shehu, Ramiz Alia, Rrahman Marmullaku, Spiro Koleka, and alternate members of the Political Bureau Adil Carcani, Kadri Hazbiu, Koço Theodhosi, Pilo Peristeri, and others.

Kang Sheng, head of the delegation of the Communist Party of China, member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat, Li Xiannian, deputy head of the delegation, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat, as well as members of the delegation Liu Xiao and Wu Xiuquan also went to visit.

During the visit to the Party of Labour of Albania Museum, Comrade Hoxha gave a detailed introduction to Comrade Kang Sheng, Comrade Li Xiannian, and others about the situation where various communist groups in Albania held a meeting here 25 years ago and founded the Communist Party of Albania (the predecessor of the Party of Labour of Albania). The visit by the leaders of the Party of Labour of Albania and various foreign delegations was warmly welcomed by the nearby residents. Many old people who had witnessed the birth, growth, and strengthening of the Party rushed out from their homes to shake hands and embrace Comrade Hoxha and Comrade Kang Sheng. Children waved flowers and frequently waved to the leaders of the Party of Labour and the foreign guests, shouting, "Long live the Party of Labour!"

		Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, November 8

On the occasion of the successful conclusion of the Fifth Congress of the Party of Labour of Albania and the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Party of Labour, the leaders of the Party of Labour of Albania and the delegations of the Communist Party of China and other parties, who were invited to attend the Fifth Congress of the Party of Labour of Albania, laid wreaths at the Martyrs' Cemetery of the National Liberation War in Tirana on November 8.

Those who participated in the laying of wreaths included: Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee Adil Carcani, Behar Balluku, Haxhi Toska, Hysni Kapo, Manush Myftiu, Mehmet Shehu, Ramiz Alia, Rrahman Marmullaku, Spiro Koleka, and alternate members of the Political Bureau Adil Carcani, Kadri Hazbiu, Koço Theodhosi, Pilo Peristeri, and others. The wreath laid by the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania had a ribbon with the words: "Glory to the martyrs who sacrificed for the cause of national liberation!"

The wreath laid by the delegation of the Communist Party of China, headed by Kang Sheng, member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat, and Li Xiannian, deputy head of the delegation, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat, had a ribbon with the words: "The martyrs who sacrificed for the cause of the liberation of the Albanian people will be immortal!"

Today, wreaths were also laid at the Martyrs' Cemetery by 28 delegations of communist parties, workers' parties, and Marxist-Leninist organizations.

### 战斗的道路光辉的历程  阿劳动党领导人和外国代表团参观劳动党成立纪念馆和民族解放斗争博物馆并向民族解放战争烈士墓敬献花圈

1966-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

    战斗的道路光辉的历程
    阿劳动党领导人和外国代表团参观劳动党成立纪念馆和民族解放斗争博物馆并向民族解放战争烈士墓敬献花圈
    新华社地拉那八日电  在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第五次代表大会胜利闭幕和劳动党成立二十五周年之际，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党领导人和应邀参加劳动党第五次代表大会的中国共产党代表团以及其他一些代表团，八日参观了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党成立纪念馆和民族解放斗争博物馆。
    前往参观的有：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央第一书记恩·霍查，中央政治局委员阿·查尔查尼、贝·巴卢库、哈·托斯卡、希·卡博、马·穆夫蒂乌、穆·谢胡、拉·阿利雅、里·马尔科、斯·科列加，中央政治局候补委员阿·凯莱齐、卡·哈兹比乌、科·蒂奥多西、彼·杜米等。
    中国共产党代表团团长、中共中央政治局常务委员、中央书记处书记康生，副团长、中共中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记李先念以及团员、中共中央委员刘晓和伍修权也前往参观。
    在参观阿尔巴尼亚劳动党成立纪念馆时，霍查同志向康生、李先念等同志详细介绍了阿尔巴尼亚各个共产主义小组二十五年前在这里开会，成立阿尔巴尼亚共产党（阿尔巴尼亚劳动党前身）的情况。阿尔巴尼亚劳动党领导人和各个外国代表团的参观，受到了附近居民的热烈欢迎。许多亲眼看着党诞生、成长和壮大的老人从家里跑出来，同霍查同志和康生同志握手、拥抱。儿童们举着鲜花，频频向劳动党的领导人和外国来宾们招手并高呼：“劳动党万岁！”
    新华社地拉那八日电  在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第五次代表大会胜利闭幕和劳动党成立二十五周年之际，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党领导人和应邀参加劳动党第五次代表大会的中国共产党代表团以及其他一些代表团，八日向地拉那民族解放战争烈士墓敬献花圈。
    参加献花圈的有：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央第一书记恩·霍查，中央政治局委员阿·查尔查尼、贝·巴卢库、哈·托斯卡、希·卡博、马·穆夫蒂乌、穆·谢胡、拉·阿利雅、里·马尔科、斯·科列加，中央政治局候补委员阿·凯莱齐、卡·哈兹比乌、科·蒂奥多西、彼·杜米、皮·皮里斯特里等。阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会献的花圈的缎带上写着：“光荣属于为祖国解放事业而牺牲的烈士们！”
    由团长、中共中央政治局常务委员、中央书记处书记康生和副团长、中共中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记李先念率领的中国共产党代表团敬献的花圈的缎带上写着：“为阿尔巴尼亚人民解放事业而牺牲的烈士永垂不朽！”
    今天向烈士墓献花圈的还有其他二十八个共产党、工人党和马克思列宁主义组织的代表团。
