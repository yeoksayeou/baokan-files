Title: Shenyang Develops New High-Temperature Fire Retardant
Author:
Date: 1966-11-08
Edition: 3

Shenyang has successfully developed a new fire-retardant material called the "401" fire retardant. This new fire retardant, when applied to the surface of flammable materials in a layer as thin as a hair, can withstand direct flames of 700 degrees Celsius for 15 minutes without burning. If the layer is thickened two to three times, it can withstand direct flames of 900 to 1000 degrees Celsius for 15 minutes without burning. This new fire retardant was developed by a chemical group in the Dongsheng People's Commune of the Honggong District in Shenyang.

### 沈阳制成新耐高温防火剂

1966-11-08
第3版()
专栏：

    沈阳制成新耐高温防火剂
    沈阳试制成功防火新材料——“四○一”防火剂。这种新防火剂涂在易燃物表面头发丝那样厚的一层，可耐七百度火力直接烧十五分钟而不燃；涂层增厚二三倍，可耐九百度到一千度火力直接烧十五分钟而不燃。这种新防火剂是沈阳红工区东升人民公社的一个化工小组试制出来的。
