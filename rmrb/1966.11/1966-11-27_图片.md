Title: Chairman Mao Receives Revolutionary Students and Red Guards
Author:
Date: 1966-11-27
Edition: 6

The news of Chairman Mao's reception of revolutionary students and Red Guards reached a Red Guard Long March team from Kalaqin Banner in Inner Mongolia, who were on their way. The young warriors were even more encouraged, quickened their pace, and finally arrived at the side of the great leader Chairman Mao before the meeting to accept Chairman Mao's inspection.

The photograph on this page was taken by a reporter from this newspaper.
