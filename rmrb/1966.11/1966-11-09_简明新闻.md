	Brief News
	North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Five Invading U.S. Aircraft

	On the afternoon of the 8th, the military and civilians of the Vietnamese capital, Hanoi, shot down another invading U.S. unmanned high-altitude reconnaissance aircraft. On the 4th and 7th, the military and civilians of Quang Ninh Province shot down two invading U.S. aggressor aircraft. The military and civilians of Nghe An Province also shot down two U.S. aircraft on the 4th and 6th.

	Egypt and Syria Sign Joint Defense Agreement

	On the 4th, Egyptian Prime Minister Sidqi Sulayman and Syrian Prime Minister Yusuf Zayin signed a joint defense agreement between Egypt and Syria in Cairo.

	The agreement states: "The signatory parties believe that aggression against either party or its armed forces is aggression against the other party." The other party will actively assist the aggrieved country, taking emergency measures, including the use of force, to repel the aggression.

	Singapore Athletes to Participate in the Asian New Forces Games

	According to the executive headquarters of the first Asian New Forces Games, Singapore athletes will participate in the first Asian New Forces Games, which will open in Phnom Penh on November 25. They will compete in badminton, tennis, table tennis, and archery.

	Italian Socialist Party and Socialist Democratic Party Merge

	After being split for nineteen years, the Italian Socialist Party and the Socialist Democratic Party officially merged on October 30, forming the Unified Socialist Party, with Nenni as the chairman. The merger of the two parties further indicates their decline and bankruptcy.

	Panama Experiences Worst Flood in 30 Years

	Panama recently experienced the worst flood in thirty years. This flood was caused by the overflowing of the Chepo and Pacora rivers after heavy rains.

	Due to the flooding caused by the heavy rains, three villages and large tracts of tobacco plantations were submerged, and several large bridges were washed away.

### 简明新闻

1966-11-09
第4版()
专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    越南北方军民击落入侵美机五架
    越南首都河内市军民在八日下午又击落入侵的美国无人驾驶高空侦察机一架。四日和七日，广宁省军民击落入侵的美国强盗飞机两架。义安省军民在四日和六日也击落两架美国飞机。
    阿联和叙利亚签订联合防御协定
    阿联总理西德基·苏莱曼和叙利亚总理优素福·扎因四日在开罗签订了一项阿联和叙利亚联合防御协定。
    协定说：“签字国双方认为，对任何一方或它的武装力量的侵略就是对另一方的侵略。”另一方将主动援助被侵略国，采取包括使用武力在内的紧急措施来击退侵略。
    新加坡运动员将参加亚新会比赛
    据第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会执行总部消息，新加坡运动员将参加十一月二十五日在金边开幕的第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会。他们将参加羽毛球、网球、乒乓球和射箭等项比赛。
    意大利社会党同社会民主党合并
    意大利社会党和社会民主党在分裂了十九年之后，已经在十月三十日正式重新合并，组成统一社会党，由南尼任主席。两党的合流进一步表明了它们的没落和破产。
    巴拿马发生三十年来最大的水灾
    巴拿马最近发生三十年来所没有过的大水灾。这次水灾是由切波和帕科拉两条河水在暴雨后泛滥造成的。
    由于暴雨引起的河水泛滥，淹没了三个村庄和大片的烟草种植园，冲垮了几座大桥。
                            （据新华社讯）
