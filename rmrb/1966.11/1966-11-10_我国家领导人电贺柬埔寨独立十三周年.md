Title: Chinese Leaders Send Congratulatory Message on the 13th Anniversary of Cambodian Independence
Author:
Date: 1966-11-10
Edition: 1

New China News Agency, November 9th: Chinese leaders sent a telegram on the 8th to Prince Norodom Sihanouk, the Head of State of Cambodia, to express warm congratulations to the Prince, the Cambodian people, and the Royal Government of Cambodia on the 13th anniversary of Cambodian independence.

The telegram stated: "Under the leadership of Prince Sihanouk, the Cambodian people have achieved great successes in developing their national economy, national culture, and in building their own country. The Cambodian people have bravely fought against the aggression, interference, subversion, and threats of American imperialism and its lackeys, defending their national independence, sovereignty, and national dignity. The Kingdom of Cambodia has consistently adhered to a policy of independence, autonomy, and peaceful neutrality, firmly opposing the aggressive and war policies of American imperialism, supporting the Vietnamese people's struggle against American aggression for national salvation, and making valuable contributions to the unity of Asian and African peoples against imperialism and to the defense of Asian peace."

The telegram concluded: "The traditional friendship between the peoples of China and Cambodia has continued to develop well. In the struggle against our common enemy, American imperialism, the peoples of China and Cambodia have always supported and encouraged each other, working closely together. We are confident that this friendship and friendly cooperative relationship between our two peoples will continue to be strengthened and developed. The Cambodian people can be assured that the 700 million Chinese people will forever be the most loyal and reliable friends of the Cambodian people."

### 我国家领导人电贺柬埔寨独立十三周年

1966-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我国家领导人电贺柬埔寨独立十三周年
    新华社九日讯　我国家领导人八日打电报给柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王，向亲王、柬埔寨人民和柬埔寨王国政府热烈祝贺柬埔寨独立十三周年。
    电报说：“柬埔寨人民在亲王的领导下，在发展民族经济、民族文化和建设自己国家的事业中，取得了很大的成就。柬埔寨人民对美帝国主义及其仆从的侵略、干涉、颠覆和威胁，进行了英勇的斗争，捍卫了国家的独立、主权和民族尊严。柬埔寨王国始终不渝地奉行独立自主和和平中立的政策，坚决反对美帝国主义的侵略政策和战争政策，支持越南人民的抗美救国斗争，为亚非人民团结反帝和保卫亚洲和平的事业作出了宝贵的贡献。”
    电报最后说：“中柬两国人民亲密的传统友谊，一直很好地向前发展着。在反对我们共同敌人美帝国主义的斗争中，中柬两国人民一向相互支持，相互鼓舞，密切合作。我们深信，我们两国人民的这种友谊和友好合作关系将继续不断地巩固和发展。柬埔寨人民可以指望，七亿中国人民将永远是柬埔寨人民最忠实、可靠的朋友。”
