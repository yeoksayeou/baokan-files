Author:
Date: 1966-11-04
Edition: 5

Friendly Exchanges

	△ The Japanese delegation, led by Takashi Takaoka, arrived in Beijing by plane on the evening of the 3rd to discuss the fifth-year trade agreement memorandum with Liao Chengzhi.

	△ Professor Yamet and his wife, a French radiologist, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 2nd at the invitation of the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences for a friendly visit to China.

	△ The delegation of the Japan-China Friendship Association of Nagano Prefecture, consisting of 16 people led by team leader Xiaogai Zhengxian, arrived in Beijing by plane from Guangzhou on the 2nd at the invitation of the China-Japan Friendship Association for a friendly visit to China. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 友好往来

1966-11-04
第5版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    △来我国商谈廖承志、高碕达之助备忘录第五年度贸易协议的日本代表团，三日晚上乘飞机到达北京。
    △应中国医学科学院邀请，前来我国进行友好访问的法国放射学家雅梅特教授和他的夫人二日乘飞机到达北京。
    △应中日友协邀请来我国进行友好访问的日中友协长野县友好代表团一行十六人，在团长小笠原正贤率领下，于二日由广州乘飞机到达北京。　（据新华社讯）
