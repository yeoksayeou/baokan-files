Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1966-11-11
Edition: 1

The struggle of the proletariat and revolutionary people to change the world includes the accomplishment of the following tasks: to change the objective world, and also to change one's subjective world—to change one's capacity for cognition and the relationship between one's subjective world and the objective world.

					*From "On Practice"*
