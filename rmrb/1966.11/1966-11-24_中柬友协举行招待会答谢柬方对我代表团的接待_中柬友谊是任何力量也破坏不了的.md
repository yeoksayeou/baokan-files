Title: Chinese-Cambodian Friendship Association Holds a Reception to Thank the Cambodian Side for Their Hospitality to Our Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-11-24
Edition: 4

The Chinese-Cambodian Friendship Association held a reception this afternoon to express gratitude for the warm hospitality extended to the Chinese-Cambodian Friendship Delegation, led by Vice Chairman Zhang Qixia, during their visit to Cambodia.

Vice Premier Chen Yi attended the reception.

Prince Norodom Sihanouk's son, Prince Norodom Sihanouk, and Cambodian Ambassador to China, Zhang Gang, were invited to attend the reception.

Zhang Qixia and Zhang Gang both spoke at the reception. They both emphasized that no matter what happens in the world, the peoples of China and Cambodia will stand together; the friendship between China and Cambodia is unbreakable by any force. They both wished the upcoming Asian New Forces Games in Phnom Penh a great success. The ambassador pointed out that, with China's support, the Asian New Forces Games will shatter the lies and sabotage of imperialism, contributing to the strengthening of anti-imperialist solidarity among Asian peoples.

Attendees also included Han Nianlong, Ding Xilin, Zhou Yiping, and other relevant officials.

Diplomats from the Cambodian Embassy in China and other Cambodian friends were also invited to attend.

After the reception, a large-scale documentary film "Chairman Mao Reviews 150,000 Parading Troops" celebrating the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, and a Cambodian documentary "Chinese-Cambodian Friendship Association Delegation Visits Cambodia" were screened.

### 中柬友协举行招待会答谢柬方对我代表团的接待  中柬友谊是任何力量也破坏不了的

1966-11-24
第4版()
专栏：

    中柬友协举行招待会答谢柬方对我代表团的接待
    中柬友谊是任何力量也破坏不了的
    据新华社二十三日讯  中柬友协今天下午举行招待会，答谢由中柬友协副会长张茜率领的中柬友好代表团访问柬埔寨时所受到的热情接待。
    陈毅副总理出席了招待会。
    在北京的西哈努克亲王的儿子纳拉迪波王子，柬埔寨驻中国大使张岗等，应邀出席了招待会。
    张茜和张岗在招待会上先后讲话。他们都谈到，不管世界上发生什么事情，中柬两国人民都会站在一起；中柬友谊是任何力量也破坏不了的。他们都祝愿即将在金边举行的亚洲新兴力量运动会取得成功。大使指出，由于中国的支持，亚洲新运会将粉碎帝国主义的造谣和破坏，为加强亚洲人民的反帝团结做出贡献。
    出席招待会的，还有韩念龙、丁西林、周一萍等有关方面负责人。
    柬埔寨驻中国大使馆的外交官员及其他柬埔寨朋友也应邀出席。
    招待会后，放映庆祝中华人民共和国成立十七周年的大型纪录片《毛主席检阅一百五十万游行大军》，和柬埔寨纪录片《中柬友协代表团访问柬埔寨》。
