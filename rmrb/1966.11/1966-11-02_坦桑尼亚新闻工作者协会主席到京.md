Title: Chairman of the Tanzania Journalists Association Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-11-02
Edition: 4

At the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association, Chairman of the Tanzania Journalists Association, Kaishi, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

### 坦桑尼亚新闻工作者协会主席到京

1966-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

    坦桑尼亚新闻工作者协会主席到京
    新华社三十一日讯　应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请到我国访问的坦桑尼亚新闻工作者协会主席卡伊锡，今天乘飞机到达北京。
