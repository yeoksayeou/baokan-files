Title: The Sino-Sri Lankan Barter Agreement Signed in Colombo
Author:
Date: 1966-11-30
Edition: 4

The 1967 Sino-Sri Lankan Barter Agreement was signed in Colombo on the morning of the 29th.

The signatories were Lin Haiyun, Head of the Chinese Government Trade Delegation and Acting Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, for the Chinese side; and P. de S. Kularatne, Head of the Sri Lankan Government Trade Delegation and Minister of Commerce and Trade, for the Sri Lankan side.

The Charge d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Sri Lanka, Guo Ying, attended the signing ceremony.

The Sri Lankan Ambassador to China, Rupasinghe Gunawadane, also attended the ceremony.

Sri Lankan Governor-General William Gopallawa held a tea party in the afternoon of the 29th to entertain the Chinese Government Trade Delegation.

### 中锡换货议定书在科伦坡签字

1966-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

    中锡换货议定书在科伦坡签字
    新华社科伦坡二十九日电　中华人民共和国和锡兰一九六七年换货议定书二十九日上午在科伦坡签字。
    在议定书上签字的，中国方面是中国政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部代理部长林海云；锡兰方面是锡兰政府贸易代表团团长、商业和贸易部长佩里斯。
    中国驻锡兰大使馆临时代办国鹰出席了签字仪式。
    锡兰驻中国大使鲁帕辛格·古纳瓦德纳也出席了仪式。
    锡兰总督威廉·高伯拉瓦二十九日下午举行茶会，招待中国政府贸易代表团。
