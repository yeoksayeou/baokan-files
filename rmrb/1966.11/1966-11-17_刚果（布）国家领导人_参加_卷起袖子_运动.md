Title: Leaders of Congo (Brazzaville) Participate in "Roll Up Your Sleeves" Campaign
Author:
Date: 1966-11-17
Edition: 5

Leaders of Congo (Brazzaville) Participate in "Roll Up Your Sleeves" Campaign

Xinhua News Agency, Brazzaville: The President of Congo (Brazzaville), Alphonse Massamba-Débat, the President of the National Assembly, M'bemba, and the Prime Minister, Ambroise Noumazalaye, recently participated in the "Roll Up Your Sleeves" campaign in the suburbs of Brazzaville. They repaired a 200-meter section of the Brazzaville-Mayama Road.

Members of the Political Bureau of the National Revolutionary Movement of Congo, government officials, and assembly members also took part in the road repair work.

These leaders, armed with shovels and dressed in work clothes, labored for two hours.

The "Roll Up Your Sleeves" campaign is being carried out nationwide in Congo (Brazzaville). The Congo News Agency reported on the seventh that we must rely on ourselves to transform our country, as no one else can do the work for the Congolese people.

### 刚果（布）国家领导人  参加“卷起袖子”运动

1966-11-17
第5版()
专栏：

    刚果（布）国家领导人
    参加“卷起袖子”运动
    新华社布拉柴维尔电　刚果（布）总统马桑巴一代巴、国民议会议长穆亚比和总理努马扎莱最近在布拉柴维尔郊区参加“卷起袖子”运动，在布拉柴维尔—马雅马公路上修理了二百米的一段路面。
    刚果（布）全国革命运动政治局委员们、政府成员和议会议员也参加了修路。
    这些领导人手执铁锹，身穿劳动服，劳动了两小时。
      “卷起袖子”运动正在刚果（布）全国展开。刚果新闻社七日在一篇关于这方面的报道中说，我们应当依靠自己来改造我们的国家，因为没有别人能够为刚果人代劳。
