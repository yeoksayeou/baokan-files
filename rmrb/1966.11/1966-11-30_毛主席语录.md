	Mao Zedong Quotations

	The truth of Marxism-Leninism is on our side. The international proletariat is on our side. The oppressed nations and peoples are on our side. More than ninety percent of the people of the world are on our side. Our friends are all over the world. We are not afraid of isolation, nor will we ever be isolated. We are invincible. A handful of miserable anti-China, anti-Albania elements are doomed to failure.
