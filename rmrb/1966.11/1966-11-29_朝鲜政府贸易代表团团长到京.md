Title: Head of North Korean Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-11-29
Edition: 5

The head of the North Korean government trade delegation, Deputy Minister of Trade Ryu Sung-yi, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

Those who went to the airport to welcome him included Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang and others.

The acting charge d'affaires of the North Korean embassy in China, Kim Jae-suk, and other members of the North Korean government trade delegation who had arrived earlier, also went to the airport to welcome him.

### 朝鲜政府贸易代表团团长到京

1966-11-29
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜政府贸易代表团团长到京
    新华社二十八日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府贸易代表团团长、贸易省副相柳成义，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有对外贸易部副部长李强等。
    朝鲜驻中国大使馆临时代办金在淑，先期到达北京的朝鲜政府贸易代表团其他成员，也到机场迎接。
