Title: New Iraqi Ambassador to China Presents Credentials to Vice Chairman Dong
Author:
Date: 1966-11-03
Edition: 4

Iraq's new Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the People's Republic of China, Monir Rashid, presented his credentials to Vice Chairman Dong Biwu this afternoon, accompanied by embassy officials. After presenting his credentials, Vice Chairman Dong Biwu had a friendly conversation with the ambassador. Present were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qiao Guanhua and others.

### 伊拉克新任驻华大使向董副主席递交国书

1966-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    伊拉克新任驻华大使向董副主席递交国书
    新华社二日讯　伊拉克共和国新任驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使莫尼尔·拉希德，今天下午偕同大使馆官员向董必武副主席递交国书。递交国书后，董必武副主席同大使进行了友好的谈话。在场的有外交部副部长乔冠华等。
