Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Meets with Guests from Mali
Author:
Date: 1966-11-10
Edition: 4

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Guo Moruo today met with Mali historian Moussa Oumar Sy, ethnologist Youssouf Toure, and linguist Kanté. He had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见马里客人

1966-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见马里客人
    新华社九日讯　人大常委会副委员长、中国科学院院长郭沫若，今天接见马里历史学家穆萨·奥马·西，人种学家优素福·西塞，语言学家康塔夫人，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
