Title: Liao Chengzhi Hosts Dinner for Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-11-05
Edition: 5

Liao Chengzhi hosted a dinner party this evening to entertain the Japanese delegation that came to China to discuss the fifth-year trade agreement between Liao Chengzhi and Takasugi Tatsunosuke. In the morning, Liao Chengzhi received this delegation.

### 廖承志宴请日本客人

1966-11-05
第5版()
专栏：

    廖承志宴请日本客人
    新华社四日讯　廖承志今晚举行酒会，招待来我国商谈廖承志、高碕达之助备忘录第五年度贸易协议的日本代表团。上午，廖承志接见了这个代表团。
