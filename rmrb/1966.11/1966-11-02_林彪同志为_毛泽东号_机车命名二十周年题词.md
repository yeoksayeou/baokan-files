Title: Comrade Lin Biao's Inscription for the 20th Anniversary of the Naming of the "Mao Zedong" Locomotive
Author:
Date: 1966-11-02
Edition: 1

The New China News Agency reported on November 1st that the great leader Chairman Mao's close comrade-in-arms, Comrade Lin Biao, has written an inscription for the 20th anniversary of the naming of the "Mao Zedong" locomotive of the Beijing Railway Bureau. The full text of the inscription is:

	Mao Zedong Thought is the locomotive driving history forward in the people's revolution

	Lin Biao
	October 27, 1966

### 林彪同志为“毛泽东号”机车命名二十周年题词

1966-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

    林彪同志为“毛泽东号”机车命名二十周年题词
    新华社一日讯　伟大领袖毛主席的亲密战友林彪同志，为北京铁路局“毛泽东号”机车命名二十周年题了词。题词全文是：
    毛泽东思想指引下的人民革命是历史前进的火车头
                              林彪　一九六六，十月廿七日
