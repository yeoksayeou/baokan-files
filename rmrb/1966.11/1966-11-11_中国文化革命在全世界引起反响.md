Title: The Chinese Cultural Revolution Resonates Around the World
Author: Gēbó'ěr
Date: 1966-11-11
Edition: 4

The Chinese Cultural Revolution, under the personal leadership of the great Chairman Mao Zedong, has resonated around the world. People praise the great Mao Zedong so highly. I hope that the Chinese Cultural Revolution achieves complete victory and brings the fruits of victory to other peoples who are on the road to development, so that they can organize themselves and achieve human liberation worldwide.

	[[Morocco]] Gēbó'ěr

### 中国文化革命在全世界引起反响
哥伯尔
1966-11-11
第4版()
专栏：国外来信

    中国文化革命在全世界引起反响
    由于有以伟大的毛泽东主席的亲自领导，中国的文化大革命在全世界引起了反响。人们对伟大的毛泽东是如此赞颂。我希望中国的文化革命取得完全的胜利，并把胜利果实带给其他正在发展道路上的人民，以便使他们组织起来，在全世界实现人类的解放。
  　〔摩洛哥〕  哥伯尔
