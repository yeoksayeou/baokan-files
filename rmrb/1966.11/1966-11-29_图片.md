Title: Chairman Mao and His Close Comrade Comrade Lin Biao, Along with Other Central Leaders, Inspect Revolutionary Students and Red Guards from All Over the Country
Author:
Date: 1966-11-29
Edition: 6

Chairman Mao and his close comrade Comrade Lin Biao, along with other central leaders, rode in an open-top car to inspect revolutionary students and Red Guards from all corners of the country. The young revolutionaries cheered loudly, and the shouts of "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years! Ten thousand times ten thousand years!" echoed through the skies.

	Xinhua News Agency Photographer
