New Ambassador of the Congo (Brazzaville) Presents Credentials to Vice Chairman Song Qingling

BEIJING, Nov. 24 (Xinhua) — Apollinaire Bazanga, the new Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Congo (Brazzaville) to the People's Republic of China, presented his credentials to Vice Chairman Song Qingling this afternoon. Accompanying the ambassador were other officials from the embassy.

After presenting his credentials, Vice Chairman Song Qingling had a friendly conversation with Ambassador Bazanga. Present at the ceremony were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qiao Guanhua and others.

### 刚果（布）新任驻华大使向宋庆龄副主席递交国书

1966-11-25
第4版()
专栏：

    刚果（布）新任驻华大使
    向宋庆龄副主席递交国书
    新华社二十四日讯　刚果（布）共和国新任驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使阿波利奈尔·巴赞加，今天下午偕同大使馆外交官员，向中华人民共和国副主席宋庆龄递交国书。
    递交国书后，宋庆龄副主席同巴赞加大使进行了友好的谈话。在场的有外交部副部长姬鹏飞等。
