Title: Chinese-Cambodian Friendship Delegation Visits Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1966-11-05
Edition: 5

A delegation from the Chinese-Cambodian Friendship Association, led by the vice-president Zhang Qi, departed from Beijing by plane today. The delegation, consisting of eight members, was invited to visit Cambodia for a friendly exchange.

Those who came to the airport to see them off included Vice Premier Chen Yi and relevant officials Han Nianlong, Ding Xilin, and Zhou Yiping.

Also present at the airport to see them off were Prince Norodom Naradipo, Cambodian Ambassador to China Zhang Gang, and Cultural Counselor Shen Lisu.

### 中柬友好代表团赴柬埔寨访问

1966-11-05
第5版()
专栏：

    中柬友好代表团赴柬埔寨访问
    新华社四日讯　由中柬友协副会长张茜率领的中柬友好代表团一行八人，今天乘飞机离开北京，应邀前往柬埔寨进行友好访问。
    到机场送行的，有陈毅副总理，以及有关方面负责人韩念龙、丁西林、周一萍等。
    到机场送行的，还有诺罗敦·纳拉迪波王子，柬埔寨驻中国大使张岗，文化参赞沈力速等。
