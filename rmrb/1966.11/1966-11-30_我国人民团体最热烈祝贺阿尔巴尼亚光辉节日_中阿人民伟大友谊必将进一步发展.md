Title: Chinese People's Organizations Warmly Congratulate Albania's Glorious National Day
Author:
Date: 1966-11-30
Edition: 4

Chinese people's organizations have sent warm congratulations to Albania on the occasion of the 22nd anniversary of its liberation.

	New China News Agency, November 29 — Various people's organizations in China have sent telegrams to the relevant people's organizations in Albania, extending their warmest congratulations on the 22nd anniversary of Albania's liberation.

	The All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China, the All-China Women's Federation, and the China-Albania Friendship Association, in their telegrams to the relevant people's organizations in Albania, all expressed their warmest congratulations to the brotherly Albanian people for their new, brilliant achievements in the socialist revolution and socialist construction under the leadership of the experienced Albanian Party of Labour led by Comrade Enver Hoxha. They sincerely wished the Albanian people greater victories under the guidance and inspiration of the historically significant Fifth Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour.

	The telegrams praised the friendship between the Chinese and Albanian peoples as a revolutionary and fighting friendship, a friendship based on Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism. The telegrams stated that in the common struggle against imperialism and modern revisionism, and in the common cause of socialist revolution and socialist construction, the great friendship between the Chinese and Albanian peoples will be further consolidated and developed.

### 我国人民团体最热烈祝贺阿尔巴尼亚光辉节日  中阿人民伟大友谊必将进一步发展

1966-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

    我国人民团体最热烈祝贺阿尔巴尼亚光辉节日
    中阿人民伟大友谊必将进一步发展
    新华社二十九日讯　我国各人民团体致电阿尔巴尼亚各人民团体，最热烈地祝贺阿尔巴尼亚解放二十二周年。
    中华全国总工会，中国共产主义青年团中央委员会，中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会和中国阿尔巴尼亚友好协会，在给阿尔巴尼亚各有关人民团体的贺电中，都最热烈地祝贺兄弟的阿尔巴尼亚人民在以恩维尔·霍查同志为首的久经考验的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的领导下，在社会主义革命和社会主义建设事业中取得新的辉煌成就，最衷心地祝愿阿尔巴尼亚人民在具有重大历史意义的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第五次代表大会的指引和鼓舞下，取得更大的胜利。
    贺电赞颂中阿两国人民的友谊是革命的友谊，战斗的友谊，是马列主义和无产阶级国际主义的友谊。贺电表示，在反对帝国主义和现代修正主义的共同斗争中，在社会主义革命和社会主义建设的共同事业中，中阿两国人民的伟大友谊必将获得进一步的巩固和发展。
