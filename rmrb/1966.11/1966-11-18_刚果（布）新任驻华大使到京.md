Title: New Ambassador from the Republic of the Congo (Brazzaville) Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-11-18
Edition: 6

New China News Agency, November 17th

The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of the Congo (Brazzaville) to the People's Republic of China, Apollinaire Bazanga, arrived in Beijing by airplane this evening.

### 刚果（布）新任驻华大使到京

1966-11-18
第6版()
专栏：

    刚果（布）新任驻华大使到京
    新华社十七日讯　刚果（布）共和国新任驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使阿波利奈尔·巴赞加，今晚乘飞机到达北京。
