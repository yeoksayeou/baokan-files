Title: Warm Congratulations on the 25th Anniversary of the Founding of the Albanian Party of Labour
Author:
Date: 1966-11-08
Edition: 1

The Chinese Communist Party Central Committee sends a telegram to the Albanian Party of Labour Central Committee to warmly congratulate the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Albanian Party of Labour.

The glorious Albanian Party of Labour, led by Comrade Enver Hoxha, has held high the banner of Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism, persisted in the struggle against imperialism and revisionism, and set a brilliant example for all Marxist-Leninists and revolutionary people around the world.

		November 7th, Xinhua News Agency reported: The full text of the telegram from the Chinese Communist Party Central Committee congratulating the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Albanian Party of Labour is as follows:

Dear Comrades of the Albanian Party of Labour Central Committee:

The Chinese Communist Party warmly congratulates the Albanian Party of Labour on its 25th anniversary and warmly congratulates the Albanian Party of Labour on its great achievements in revolution and socialist construction over the past 25 years.

The glorious Albanian Party of Labour, under the leadership of the Central Committee headed by the tried and tested leader Comrade Enver Hoxha, has gone through 25 years filled with heroic struggles and brilliant victories. The Albanian Party of Labour is a truly Marxist-Leninist party, a party with high principles, a party loyal to the people, and a brave and tenacious proletarian revolutionary party that dares to fight and dare to win.

Under the leadership of the Albanian Party of Labour, the heroic Albanian people, after a bitter revolutionary war, liberated their country, and after establishing a people's government, overcame numerous difficulties on the road ahead and won great victories in the socialist revolution and socialist construction. Socialist Albania is a great and invincible red stronghold standing in Europe.

The Albanian Party of Labour adheres to the socialist road and has taken a series of revolutionary measures in the political, economic, military, and cultural fields. These measures have significant theoretical and practical importance for enriching the experience of the international proletarian dictatorship.

The Albanian Party of Labour holds high the banner of Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism. The Albanian Party of Labour is not afraid of imperialism, not afraid of revisionism, not afraid of the threat of war, not afraid of isolation. The Albanian Party of Labour resolutely opposes imperialism led by the United States and reactionaries in various countries, resolutely opposes modern revisionism centered on the Soviet Communist Party leadership, resolutely supports the Vietnamese people's heroic anti-American national salvation war, and resolutely supports the revolutionary struggles of all oppressed peoples and nations. The Albanian Party of Labour adheres to principles and fights resolutely, setting a brilliant example for all Marxist-Leninists and revolutionary people around the world.

The two parties and peoples of China and Albania have always supported each other and fought side by side in the struggle against our common enemies and in the struggle for the cause of socialism and communism. This friendship is a revolutionary friendship, a fighting friendship, a friendship of Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism. We are deeply convinced that this great friendship between our two parties and peoples will be further consolidated and developed in the years to come, radiating even more brilliant light.

		Chinese Communist Party Central Committee
		November 5, 1966

### 热烈祝贺阿尔巴尼亚劳动党成立二十五周年

1966-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    中国共产党中央委员会致电阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会
    热烈祝贺阿尔巴尼亚劳动党成立二十五周年
    以恩维尔·霍查同志为首的光荣的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党，高举马克思列宁主义和无产阶级国际主义的旗帜，坚持反帝反修斗争，为全世界马克思列宁主义者和革命人民树立了光辉的榜样。
    新华社七日讯　中国共产党中央委员会庆祝阿尔巴尼亚劳动党成立二十五周年的贺电。全文如下：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会亲爱的同志们：
    中国共产党热烈祝贺阿尔巴尼亚劳动党成立二十五周年，热烈祝贺阿尔巴尼亚劳动党二十五年来在革命和社会主义建设中取得了伟大成就。
    光荣的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党，在以久经考验的领袖恩维尔·霍查同志为首的中央委员会的领导下，经历了充满英勇战斗和光辉胜利的二十五年。阿尔巴尼亚劳动党，是真正马克思列宁主义的党，是有高度原则性的党，是忠于人民的党，是英勇顽强、敢于斗争、敢于胜利的无产阶级革命党。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党领导英雄的阿尔巴尼亚人民，经过艰苦的革命战争，取得了祖国的解放，在建立了人民政权之后，又克服了前进道路上的重重困难，赢得了社会主义革命和社会主义建设的巨大胜利。社会主义的阿尔巴尼亚是屹立在欧洲的一个伟大的、不可战胜的红色根据地。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党坚持社会主义道路，在政治、经济、军事、文化等领域内采取了一系列革命化措施。这些措施，对于丰富国际无产阶级专政的经验，具有重大的理论和实践意义。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党高举马克思列宁主义和无产阶级国际主义的旗帜。阿尔巴尼亚劳动党，不怕帝国主义，不怕修正主义，不怕战争威胁，不怕孤立。阿尔巴尼亚劳动党，坚决反对以美国为首的帝国主义和各国反动派，坚决反对以苏共领导集团为中心的现代修正主义，坚决支持越南人民英勇的抗美救国战争，坚决支持一切被压迫人民和被压迫民族的革命斗争。阿尔巴尼亚劳动党坚持原则，坚持斗争，为全世界马克思列宁主义者和革命人民树立了一个光辉的榜样。
    中阿两党、两国人民，在反对我们共同敌人的斗争中，在为社会主义和共产主义事业的斗争中，一贯互相支持，并肩战斗。这种友谊是革命的友谊，是战斗的友谊，是马克思列宁主义和无产阶级国际主义的友谊。我们深信，我们两党、两国人民之间的这种伟大友谊，在今后的岁月里，必将获得进一步的巩固和发展，放射出更加灿烂的光辉。
                                中国共产党中央委员会
                                    一九六六年十一月五日
