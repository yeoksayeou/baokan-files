Title: Resolutely Sever Ties with Elements That Sabotage Sino-Japanese Friendship
Author:
Date: 1966-11-22
Edition: 5

In recent days, various local branches of the Japan-China Friendship Association across Japan have held meetings to express their support for the Japan-China Friendship Association (Orthodox) Headquarters and to resolutely sever ties with elements that sabotage Sino-Japanese friendship.

The Hyogo Prefecture Federation of the Japan-China Friendship Association held a board meeting on the 13th, where board members led by Chairman Nakamura Kuichiro, after struggling against the splitists, declared a break with them and established the Japan-China Friendship Association (Orthodox) Hyogo Prefecture Headquarters.

On the 10th, 250 members of the Wakamatsu City branch of the Japan-China Friendship Association in Fukuoka Prefecture, after expelling the saboteurs, established the Japan-China Friendship Association (Orthodox) Wakamatsu City branch.

At the temporary members' meeting of the Japan-China Friendship Association (Orthodox) Fukuoka branch held on the 13th in Fukuoka City, all anti-Chinese elements were purged, and a resolution was passed to strive for the strengthening of the fighting friendship between the peoples of Japan and China, and it was announced that the branch would join the Japan-China Friendship Association (Orthodox) Headquarters.

At the board meeting of the Japan-China Friendship Association Suginami branch in Tokyo, held on the 14th, the overwhelming majority who insisted on genuine Sino-Japanese friendship decided to sever ties with the saboteurs.

Japanese youth hold a demonstration in Tokyo to protest against the Sato government's sabotage of the Sino-Japanese Youth Friendship Grand Festival.

### 坚决同破坏日中友好的分子断绝关系  日本各地日中友协纷纷建立新组织

1966-11-22
第5版()
专栏：

    坚决同破坏日中友好的分子断绝关系
    日本各地日中友协纷纷建立新组织
    新华社讯　东京消息：连日来，日本各地的日中友好协会组织纷纷举行集会，表示支持日中友好协会（正统）总部，坚决同破坏日中友好的分子断绝关系。
    日中友好协会兵库县联合会在十三日举行的理事会上，以会长中村九一郎为首的理事们，在同分裂主义者进行了斗争以后，声明同他们断绝关系，新建立了日中友好协会（正统）兵库县本部。
    福冈县若松市日中友好协会支部所属的二百五十名会员，十日在排除了破坏分子后，建立了日中友好协会（正统）若松市支部。
    十三日在福冈市举行的日中友好协会（正统）福冈支部临时会员大会，清除了所有的反华分子，表示决心为加强日中两国人民的战斗友谊而奋斗，并宣布加入日中友协（正统）总部。
    在十四日举行的日中友协东京都杉并支部理事会上，占绝对多数的坚持真正日中友好的人，决定同破坏分子断绝关系。（附图片）
    日本青年在东京举行示威游行，抗议佐藤政府破坏日中青年友好大联欢。　新华社发
