Title: Chairman Mao Has Arrived!
Author:
Date: 1966-11-13
Edition: 6

Chairman Mao has arrived! Chairman Mao has arrived! Our most beloved leader is warmly waving and greeting us. Chairman Mao is the greatest revolutionary of the proletariat, the reddest sun in our hearts. Let us shout a thousand times, ten thousand times: "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand times long live Chairman Mao!"

Our reporter took this photo.
