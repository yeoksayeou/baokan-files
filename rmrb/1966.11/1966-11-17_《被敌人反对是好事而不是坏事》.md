Title: Being Opposed by the Enemy Is a Good Thing, Not a Bad Thing
Author:
Date: 1966-11-17
Edition: 6

The educational policy of the Anti-Japanese Military and Political University is: a firm and correct political direction, a hardworking and plain-living work style, and flexible and mobile strategies and tactics. These three elements are indispensable for forging a revolutionary soldier fighting against Japan.
