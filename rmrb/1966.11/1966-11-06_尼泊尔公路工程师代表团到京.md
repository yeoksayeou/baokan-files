Title: Nepalese Road Engineers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-11-06
Edition: 3

At the invitation of the Road Engineering Administration Bureau of the Ministry of Communications, a delegation of seven Nepalese road engineers, led by the Chief Engineer of the Nepal Department of Roads, Bishnu Bahadur Karki, arrived in Beijing yesterday. Minister of Communications Sun Daguan received and hosted a banquet for the entire delegation today. Nepalese Ambassador to China Subba was also invited to attend the reception and banquet.

### 尼泊尔公路工程师代表团到京

1966-11-06
第3版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔公路工程师代表团到京
    新华社五日讯　应交通部公路工程管理局邀请来我国访问的尼泊尔公路工程师代表团一行七人，在尼泊尔交通部公路局总工程师比斯努·巴哈杜尔·卡其率领下，于昨天到达北京。交通部部长孙大光今天接见并宴请了代表团全体成员。尼泊尔驻中国大使苏巴应邀参加了接见和宴会。
