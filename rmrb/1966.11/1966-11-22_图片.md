Title: Pump Operator Wang Zhaohua of Daqing Oilfield and Excellent Worker on the Petroleum Front
Author:
Date: 1966-11-22
Edition: 3

Pump operator Wang Zhaohua of Daqing Oilfield and an excellent worker on the petroleum front, arms himself with Mao Zedong Thought, and accomplishes extraordinary deeds in his ordinary work post. This is Wang Zhaohua (right) studying "On Contradiction" with apprentice Tian Xiaoyun.  Xinhua News Agency Photographer.
