Title: In Praise of the Red Guards
Author:
Date: 1966-11-18
Edition: 5

The Red Guards, who are currently engaged in revolution, are our closest comrades.

The Red Guards oppose the Four Olds, becoming a driving force for building a new China. They are confidently engaged in the class struggle against revisionism.

	Japan	Shimizu Masao

The Chinese Red Guards understand revolutionary truth and are brave in breaking away from old thoughts and habits, setting a good example for revolutionary youth around the world.

	Pakistan	Abbas

The Red Guards are the hope of the revolutionary people of the world. Therefore, imperialism, revisionism, and reactionaries are very afraid of them and slander them.

Revolutionary people love the Red Guards very much because they are doing the right thing, and their actions are very revolutionary.

	Nepal	A Friend

The revolutionary spirit of the Red Guards greatly inspires revolutionary youth around the world. The Red Guards are right and have done a good job. The Red Guards have made great achievements in the Cultural Revolution.

	Sri Lanka	Samaranayake

The revolutionary actions of the Red Guards are very good. The Red Guards thoroughly smash the Four Olds of the exploiting class and firmly establish the Four News of the proletariat, resolutely clearing away the mud and filth left over from the old society. Only in this way can the socialist revolution continue to achieve victory.

	Congo (Brazzaville)	Madouba

The Red Guards who emerged during China's Cultural Revolution are full of revolutionary vigor. They are currently eliminating all the old things that stubbornly resist China's socialist system.

	Somalia	Jama

The Red Guards are reliable revolutionary successors. After the Cultural Revolution, China will be even stronger and more united.

	Bolivia	Alimando

The Red Guards are the most revolutionary. Imperialism, revisionism, and reactionaries are most afraid of them.

	Long live the Cultural Revolution!
	Long live the Red Guards!
	Long live Comrade Mao Zedong!

	Finland	A Friend

### 红卫兵赞

1966-11-18
第5版()
专栏：

    红卫兵赞
    正在进行革命的红卫兵，是我们最亲密的战友。
    红卫兵们反对四旧，成为建设新中国的动力，他们满怀信心地在进行反对修正主义的阶级斗争。
                                日本　清水正夫
    中国的红卫兵懂得革命真理，勇于破除旧思想和旧习惯，为全世界的革命青年树立了良好的榜样。
                            巴基斯坦　阿巴斯
    红卫兵是世界革命人民的希望。所以，帝国主义、修正主义和反动派都非常害怕他们，污蔑他们。
    革命人民都非常爱红卫兵，因为他们做得很正确，他们的行动是很革命的。
                              尼泊尔一位朋友
    红卫兵的革命精神大大地鼓舞了全世界的革命青年。红卫兵做得对，做得好。红卫兵在文化大革命中建立了很大的功绩。
                          锡兰　萨马拉纳亚克
    红卫兵的革命行动，好得很。红卫兵大破剥削阶级的四旧，大立无产阶级的四新，坚决把旧社会遗留下来的污泥浊水清除掉。这样，社会主义革命才能继续取得胜利。
                              刚果（布）　马东巴
    在中国文化大革命中出现的红卫兵充满着革命的朝气。他们正在清除顽固地反抗中国社会主义制度的一切旧东西。
                                    索马里　贾马
    红卫兵就是可靠的革命接班人。经过文化大革命后，中国将要更加强大，更加团结。
                            玻利维亚　阿里曼多
    红卫兵是最革命的，帝国主义、修正主义和反动派最害怕他们。
    文化大革命万岁！
    红卫兵万岁！
    毛泽东同志万岁！
                              芬兰　一位朋友
