Title: Yugoslav Chargé d'Affaires Hosts National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1966-11-29
Edition: 5

Yugoslav Chargé d'Affaires Kolob held a reception to celebrate the national day of Yugoslavia this evening.

Vice Minister of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Zhou Min and others were invited to attend the reception. Diplomatic envoys from various countries in China were also invited to attend.

### 南斯拉夫临时代办举行国庆招待会

1966-11-29
第5版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫临时代办举行国庆招待会
    新华社二十八日讯　南斯拉夫驻我国大使馆临时代办哥洛布，今晚举行南斯拉夫国庆招待会。
    外交部礼宾司副司长周敏等应邀出席招待会。各国驻华使节也应邀出席。
