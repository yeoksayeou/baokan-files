Title: Kang Keqing Meets Albanian Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-11-30
Edition: 4

Vice Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation Kang Keqing held a meeting with Shadiqu, an advanced worker from the "Mao Zedong Textile Factory" in Berat, Albania, who is a member of the Albanian-China Friendship Association delegation. The two sides had a warm and friendly conversation. Present at the meeting were members of the All-China Women's Federation Standing Committee Chen Zizhu and Yang Fuzhen, an advanced worker in studying Chairman Mao's works from Shanghai textile workers. The wife of Albanian Ambassador to China Vasili Natanaeli was also present.

### 康克清会见阿尔巴尼亚客人

1966-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

    康克清会见阿尔巴尼亚客人
    新华社二十九日讯　全国妇联副主任康克清今天下午会见阿尔巴尼亚中国友好协会代表团团员、培拉特“毛泽东纺织厂”先进工作者什·沙迪古，双方进行了亲切友好的谈话。会见时在座的，有全国妇联主席团委员沈兹九，上海纺织女工、全国学习毛主席著作积极分子杨富珍等。阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使瓦西里·纳塔奈利的夫人也在座。
