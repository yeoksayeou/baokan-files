Title: Delegation of the (Orthodox) Japan-China Friendship Association Study Tour Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-11-30
Edition: 4

At the invitation of the China-Japan Friendship Association, the fourth delegation of study tour members from the (Orthodox) Japan-China Friendship Association, consisting of eight people led by team leader Iwa-mura Sanzen'ō, arrived in Beijing by airplane on the 28th.

### 日中友协（正统）学习活动家代表团到京

1966-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

    日中友协（正统）学习活动家代表团到京
    新华社二十九日讯　应中日友协邀请来我国访问的日中友协（正统）第四次学习活动家代表团一行八人，在团长岩村三千夫率领下，二十八日乘飞机到达北京。
