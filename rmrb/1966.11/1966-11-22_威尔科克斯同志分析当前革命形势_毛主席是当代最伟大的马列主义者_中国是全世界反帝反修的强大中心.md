Title: Comrade Wilcock Analyzes the Current Revolutionary Situation
Author:
Date: 1966-11-22
Edition: 5

	Wellington, Nov. 21 (New China News Agency) — New Zealand Communist Party General Secretary Wilcock, in a report to the Political Bureau of the New Zealand Communist Party at the end of September, praised Chairman Mao as the greatest Marxist-Leninist of our time and China as a powerful center of anti-imperialism and anti-revisionism in the world.

	The November issue of the New Zealand Communist Party organ, "New Zealand Communist Review," published Wilcock's report titled "New Methods, New Strategies."

	In discussing the current international situation, Wilcock said: "We must clearly recognize that, according to the views of Marx, Engels, Lenin, Stalin, and Mao Zedong, the people are invincible. While American imperialism and its allies, the Soviet revisionists, are doomed to die. In fact, today the world presents a revolutionary situation."

	After analyzing the great victories of the Vietnamese people in their struggle against American aggression for national salvation and the failures of American imperialism, as well as the disintegration of the imperialist camp, Wilcock pointed out: "How correct Mao Zedong is! He said, in struggle, strategically we must despise all enemies, but tactically, in each specific problem, we must take all enemies seriously. To despise the enemy strategically, we must have firm determination and confidence: we can thoroughly defeat it, have the great courage to overcome all difficulties, and carry the struggle through to the end, possess the revolutionary spirit of daring to struggle and daring to win. To take the enemy seriously tactically, we must carefully study and perfect the art of struggle in each specific problem, adopt appropriate forms of struggle, concentrate all our forces for the struggle, and thus we can defeat the enemy step by step."

	The general secretary of the New Zealand Communist Party said: "We must always remember that although the American imperialists have airplanes, tanks, cannons, and nuclear bombs, and appear formidable, they are not strong. Ultimately, the strong are the people, as long as they organize and unite, and engage in various forms of struggle."

	Wilcock pointed out that modern revisionism centered on the Soviet Communist Party actually helps American imperialism when it faces increasing difficulties. "They join the anti-China chorus led by American imperialism because the People's Republic of China, led by the Communist Party of China with Mao Zedong at the helm, is the powerful center of the increasingly large-scale anti-imperialist struggle in the world, because it is the Communist Party of China that not only upholds the principles of Marxism-Leninism and opposes revisionism around the world but also continues to build a socialist society on a solid foundation."

	Wilcock pointed out: "We must realize that it is not enough to generally oppose revisionism. 'We must first oppose the leaders of the Soviet Communist Party, because they are the core today helping the Americans the most.'"

	In exposing the essence of the so-called "united action" of the new leadership of the CPSU, Wilcock said, "Revolutionary unity is the victory of the working class, the death of imperialism; revisionist 'unity' is the defeat of the working class, the preservation of imperialism."

### 威尔科克斯同志分析当前革命形势  毛主席是当代最伟大的马列主义者  中国是全世界反帝反修的强大中心

1966-11-22
第5版()
专栏：

    威尔科克斯同志分析当前革命形势
    毛主席是当代最伟大的马列主义者
    中国是全世界反帝反修的强大中心
    新华社二十一日讯　奥克兰消息：新西兰共产党总书记威尔科克斯九月底在新共政治局委员会会议上作报告时，称赞毛主席是当代最伟大的马克思列宁主义者，中国是反对帝国主义和现代修正主义的强大中心。
    新西兰共产党机关刊物《新西兰共产主义评论》十一月号刊登了威尔科克斯的这篇题为《新方法，新策略》的报告。
    威尔科克斯在谈到目前的国际局势时说：“我们必须清楚地认识到，根据马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林和毛泽东的观点，人民是不可战胜的。而美帝国主义和它的盟友苏联修正主义者是注定要死亡的。事实上，今天全世界呈现一片革命形势。”
    威尔科克斯在分析了越南人民抗美救国斗争取得的伟大胜利和美帝国主义的失败以及帝国主义阵营的分崩离析之后指出：“毛泽东是多么正确啊！他说，在斗争中，在战略上我们要藐视一切敌人，在战术上，在一个一个的具体问题上我们一定要重视一切敌人。要在战略上藐视敌人，我们必须有坚定的决心和信心：我们能够彻底击败它，有巨大的勇气克服一切困难，把斗争进行到底，要具有敢于斗争、敢于胜利的革命精神。要在战术上重视敌人，我们必须在一个一个的具体问题上慎重、仔细地研究和完善斗争的艺术，采取适当的斗争形式，集中我们的一切力量进行斗争，这样我们就能够一步一步地击败敌人。”
    新共总书记说：“我们要时刻记住，美帝国主义者尽管有飞机、坦克、大炮、核弹，看起来可怕，但是他们并不强大。归根结蒂，强大的是人民，只要他们组织起来，团结一致，进行各种形式的斗争。”
    威尔科克斯指出，以苏联共产党为中心的现代修正主义，实际上在美帝国主义面临越来越大的困难时帮助美帝国主义。“他们参加以美帝国主义为首的反华大合唱，因为由中国共产党领导的、以毛泽东为首的人民中国是世界上规模越来越大的反帝斗争的强大中心，因为正是中国共产党不仅坚持马克思列宁主义原则、反对（世界）各地的修正主义，而且在坚实的基础上继续建设社会主义社会。”
    威尔科克斯指出：“我们必须认识到，我们一般地反对修正主义是不够的。”“我们首先反对苏联共产党领导人，因为他们是今天给美国帮大忙的核心。”
    威尔科克斯在揭露苏共新领导的所谓“联合行动”的本质时说，“革命的团结是工人阶级的胜利，帝国主义的死亡；修正主义的‘联合’就是工人阶级的失败，使帝国主义保持下来。”
