Title: Mao Zedong Thought Illuminates the Entire World
Author:
Date: 1966-11-06
Edition: 5

Mao Zedong Thought illuminates the entire world. Chairman Mao Zedong is a most outstanding revolutionary leader of our time. The path led by Mao Zedong to liberate all of China has become a beacon for the peoples of the world, especially those in Asia, Africa, and Latin America, in their struggle to cast off imperialism and colonialism. The brilliance of Chairman Mao Zedong not only illuminates all of China but also illuminates the entire world.

	Cambodia
	A Student

### 毛泽东思想照亮了全世界

1966-11-06
第5版()
专栏：国外来信

    毛泽东思想照亮了全世界
    毛泽东主席是当代的一位最杰出的革命导师。毛泽东领导中国人民解放整个中国的道路，成为全世界人民，特别是亚洲、非洲和拉丁美洲人民为摆脱帝国主义和殖民主义而斗争的灯塔。毛泽东主席的光辉不仅照亮了全中国，而且照亮了全世界。　柬埔寨　一学生
