Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-11-23
Edition: 4

Vice Premier Chen Yi met with the Japanese delegation led by the ninth-degree Go player from the Japanese Go Association, Shimaura Toshihiro, this morning. He had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.

### 陈毅副总理接见日本客人

1966-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见日本客人
    新华社二十二日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见由日本棋院九段围棋手岛村俊宏率领的日本围棋代表团，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
