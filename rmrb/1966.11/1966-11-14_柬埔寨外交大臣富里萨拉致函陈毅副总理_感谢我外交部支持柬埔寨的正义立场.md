Cambodian Foreign Minister Prak Sokhon Writes to Vice Premier Chen Yi

	Thanks China's Foreign Ministry for Supporting Cambodia's Just Stance

	PHNOM PENH, Nov. 13 (Xinhua) -- According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on the 12th, Cambodian Foreign Minister Prak Sokhon wrote a letter on November 7 to Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, expressing gratitude for the statement issued by the Chinese Foreign Ministry on October 16 supporting Cambodia's just stance.

	The statement by the Chinese Foreign Ministry condemned the provocative activities of the Thai authorities against Cambodia, fully endorsed the serious stance taken by the Royal Government of Cambodia in its statement on October 6, and resolutely supported the just struggle of the Cambodian people to defend their national sovereignty and territorial integrity.

### 柬埔寨外交大臣富里萨拉致函陈毅副总理  感谢我外交部支持柬埔寨的正义立场

1966-11-14
第5版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨外交大臣富里萨拉致函陈毅副总理
    感谢我外交部支持柬埔寨的正义立场
    新华社金边十三日电　据柬埔寨新闻社十二日报道，柬埔寨王国政府外交大臣富里萨拉十一月七日写信给中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅，感谢中国外交部十月十六日发表的支持柬埔寨正义立场的声明。
    中国外交部的声明谴责了泰国当局对柬埔寨进行的挑衅活动，完全赞同柬埔寨王国政府在十月六日声明中所阐明的严正立场，坚决支持柬埔寨人民捍卫国家主权和领土完整的正义斗争。
