Title: Prominent Cambodian Figures Warmly Praise China's Successful Missile and Nuclear Weapons Test
Author:
Date: 1966-11-01
Edition: 6

Cambodian figures warmly congratulate China on the successful launch of missile and nuclear weapons tests.

According to Xinhua News Agency from Phnom Penh, Cambodian figures warmly congratulate China on the successful launch of missile and nuclear weapons tests.

Dr. Hun Neng, Chairman of the Economic and Planning Committee of the Cambodian National Assembly and Vice Chairman of the Cambodian-Chinese Friendship Association, stated in a declaration to Xinhua News Agency reporters on the 30th, "This magnificent achievement of great significance is a great victory for the Cultural Revolution and Mao Zedong Thought." It further proves the correctness of China's general line.

The declaration points out that this great achievement has a very favorable impact on the global struggle against imperialism. It strengthens the struggle against imperialism and its various accomplices, promotes national liberation movements around the world, and encourages the Vietnamese people who are waging a sacred struggle against the international bandits and aggressors, American imperialism. The declaration also points out that this achievement is a severe blow to Johnson and his accomplices. It makes the running dogs of the United States tremble and strikes a blow to the anti-China conspiracies of revisionism.

Yin Tan, Chairman of the Internal Affairs and Defense Committee of the National Assembly, said to Cambodian reporters on the 29th, "China possessing missile and nuclear weapons is a good thing, a contribution to the cause of peace." He said he admires Chairman Mao Zedong, who is leading China to a better future. We all wish him good health and longevity.

According to Xinhua News Agency from Rawalpindi, the Pakistani people warmly welcome China's successful launch of missile and nuclear weapons tests.

On the evening of the 27th, at a banquet held by the Chinese Embassy for the visiting Chinese trade union delegation, the news spread that China had successfully launched a missile with a nuclear warhead. The workers and trade union workers attending the banquet were overjoyed and warmly shook hands and embraced with Chinese friends.

A worker named Yasin said, "This great achievement belongs to you and also to us. China's achievements strengthen the power of the oppressed people who are fighting for justice."

Kushid Ahmad, Joint Secretary of the All-Pakistan Labor Federation, said, "Every Pakistani is delighted by China's successful launch of a missile with a nuclear warhead."

On the morning of the 28th, workers at the Attock Refinery in Rawalpindi heard the news of China's successful launch of a missile with a nuclear warhead and the entire factory was filled with joy. Hundreds of workers shouted, "Long live Chairman Mao Zedong!" and "Congratulations to China on this great achievement," expressing their heartfelt joy.

On the 28th, wherever Chinese reporters went, Pakistani friends warmly congratulated them. They said, "China is the pillar of the world's anti-imperialist forces. China's great victory in successfully launching missile and nuclear weapons is a major blow to the imperialist aggression plot and further shatters the illusions of American imperialists and Soviet revisionists to contain China."

The Albanian News Agency's correspondent in Beijing excitedly came to Tiananmen Square and, together with the revolutionary masses of our country, warmly cheered: The successful launch of missile and nuclear weapons tests is a great victory for Mao Zedong Thought! (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter)

### 柬埔寨著名人士热烈赞扬中国导弹核武器试验成功  中国给了约翰逊当头一棒  巴基斯坦公众说中国粉碎了帝修遏制中国的幻想

1966-11-01
第6版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨著名人士热烈赞扬中国导弹核武器试验成功
    中国给了约翰逊当头一棒
    巴基斯坦公众说中国粉碎了帝修遏制中国的幻想
    据新华社金边电　柬埔寨人士热烈祝贺中国发射导弹核武器试验成功。
    柬埔寨国民议会经济和计划委员会主席、柬中友好协会副主席胡宁博士，三十日向新华社记者发表的声明指出，中国发射导弹核武器试验成功“这一具有巨大意义的辉煌成就，是文化大革命和毛泽东思想的伟大胜利。”它进一步证明了中国总路线的正确性。
    声明指出，这次试验的伟大成就对世界范围的反对帝国主义的斗争产生了极为有利的影响。它加强了反对帝国主义及其形形色色的帮凶的斗争，推动了世界上的民族解放运动，鼓舞了正在为反对国际强盗和侵略者美帝国主义而进行神圣斗争的越南人民。声明还指出，这个成就对约翰逊及其同谋者是狠狠的当头一棒。它使美国的走狗发抖，使修正主义的反华阴谋受到打击。
    国民议会内政和国防委员会主席殷坦二十九日对柬埔寨记者说，中国拥有导弹核武器，这是一件好事情，是对和平事业的贡献。他说，他很钦佩毛泽东主席，毛泽东主席正领导中国走向更美好的前途。我们大家都祝他健康和长寿。
    据新华社拉瓦尔品第电　巴基斯坦人民热烈欢呼中国发射导弹核武器试验成功。
    二十七日晚上，在中国大使馆为来访的中国工会代表团举行的宴会上传出这一消息时，出席宴会的工人和工会工作者欣喜若狂，同中国朋友热烈地握手和拥抱。
    一位名叫亚辛的工人说，这一伟大成就是属于你们的，也是属于我们的。中国的成就加强了正在进行正义斗争的被压迫人民的力量。
    全巴基斯坦劳工联合会联合书记库希德·艾哈迈德说，每个巴基斯坦人对中国成功地发射带有核弹头的导弹都感到欢欣鼓舞。
    二十八日上午，拉瓦尔品第的阿托克炼油厂的工人听到中国成功地发射带有核弹头导弹的消息后，全厂喜气洋洋。数百名工人高呼：“毛泽东主席万岁！”、“祝贺中国取得这一伟大成就”，以表达他们发自内心的喜悦。
    二十八日，中国记者走到那里，那里的巴基斯坦朋友就向他热烈祝贺。他们说，中国是世界反帝力量的支柱，中国成功地发射导弹核武器这一巨大胜利，是对帝国主义侵略阴谋的一个巨大打击，同时进一步粉碎了美帝国主义者和苏联修正主义者遏制中国的幻想。（附图片）
    阿尔巴尼亚通讯社驻北京记者兴奋地来到天安门广场，和我国革命群众一起热烈欢呼：发射导弹核武器试验成功是毛泽东思想的伟大胜利！　新华社记者摄
