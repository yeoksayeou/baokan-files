Title: Representatives of a Border Defense Unit Attend the Conference of Active Learners of Chairman Mao's Works in Kunming
Author:
Date: 1966-11-06
Edition: 6

A representative from a certain border defense unit attending the Conference of Active Learners of Chairman Mao's Works in Kunming listens eagerly to the news of Chairman Mao's sixth review of the Cultural Revolution army, joyfully celebrating the victory of the proletarian revolutionary line represented by Chairman Mao.
