Title: Eight Red Guards from Chifeng Normal School in Inner Mongolia Form the "Grasslands Long March Red Guards" and March to Other Places for Revolutionary Link-Up
Author:
Date: 1966-11-14
Edition: 6

Eight revolutionary youngsters from Chifeng Normal School in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region formed the "Grasslands Long March Red Guards" and set out on foot to other places for revolutionary link-up. During their long march, they passed through the capital and also received the inspection of the great leader Chairman Mao. They repeatedly shouted, "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years, ten thousand times ten thousand years!"
