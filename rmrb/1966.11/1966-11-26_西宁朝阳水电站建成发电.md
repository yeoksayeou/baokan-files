Title: The Qinghai Xining Chaoyang Hydropower Station Begins Generating Electricity
Author:
Date: 1966-11-26
Edition: 2

The newly constructed Chaoyang Hydropower Station in Xining City has recently begun generating electricity. The completion of this, the largest hydropower station in Qinghai at present, has provided the Xining region with electrical power for industrial and agricultural production. Along with the power station, an irrigation canal has also been completed, which can irrigate over 13,000 mu (approximately 867 hectares) of farmland.

### 西宁朝阳水电站建成发电

1966-11-26
第2版()
专栏：

    西宁朝阳水电站建成发电
    西宁市新建的朝阳水电站，已在最近开始发电。这座青海目前最大的水电站的建成，使西宁地区的工农业生产获得了电力；同电站一起建成的引水渠道还能灌溉一万三千多亩农田。
