Title: Prince Sihanouk Exposes the Aggressive Nature of US "Aid"
Author:
Date: 1966-11-20
Edition: 5

According to Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, November 17th, the Cambodian News Agency reported today that Cambodian Head of State Prince Sihanouk severely criticized the evil intentions of US "aid," saying: "Uncle Sam provides his lackeys with dollars and various benefits only to better use them to serve imperialist purposes." The fate of recipients of US aid is "to be cannon fodder for the United States."

Prince Sihanouk emphasized: "We would rather remain poor, but we insist on being absolutely free. We do not envy the fate of those who sell themselves to American imperialism. We prefer to build our country and create our future with our limited strength, progressing slowly, but doing so in peace, freedom, and happiness, rather than 'enjoying' the false, humiliating, and dangerous prosperity that the United States bestows."

He made these remarks during an impromptu speech at the opening ceremony of a school in Takeo Province on the 16th.

Prince Sihanouk said: "President Johnson held a meeting in Manila with his lackeys and some Asian traitors, using this opportunity to engage in extensive propaganda to deceive world public opinion and the Asian people. He also promised 'peace and prosperity' to the Asian people, but with a condition that he did not hesitate to state, that is, they must agree to become lackeys of the United States."

The prince pointed out: "American economic aid is also a cunning blackmail, because if you examine it carefully, you will find that no country in the world truly benefits from its national interests because of American economic aid. In fact, if the United States gives you bread and sugar with one hand, its other hand is secretly harming your sovereignty, your independence, making your economy wither, not to mention bringing chaos, war, and endless disasters to your country at the same time." The prince said: "What are the Americans doing every day in South Vietnam, what are the increasing number of planes in front of us doing, if not to massacre the Vietnamese people so that the United States can freely station troops on South Vietnamese soil?"

He emphasized: "Our noble and brave army understands the price of selling oneself to the dollar god, accepting various benefits generously provided by the United States (cars, villas, beautiful uniforms, etc.), because our soldiers will never be cannon fodder for the United States, and the fate of recipients of US aid is precisely to be cannon fodder for the United States. Uncle Sam provides his lackeys with dollars and various benefits only to better use them to serve imperialist purposes."

Prince Sihanouk said: "Once you peel off its facade, the above-mentioned points are all aspects of this imperialism, this new colonialism, and this is why we will forever fight against it."

### 西哈努克亲王揭露美“援”的侵略实质  柬埔寨要自力更生不要美国施舍  美“援”接受者的命运恰恰就是替美国当炮灰

1966-11-20
第5版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王揭露美“援”的侵略实质
    柬埔寨要自力更生不要美国施舍
    美“援”接受者的命运恰恰就是替美国当炮灰
    据新华社金边十七日电　据柬埔寨新闻社今天报道，柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王严厉斥责美“援”的罪恶目的，他说：“山姆大叔之所以向他的走狗提供美元和种种好处，只是为了更好地利用他们为帝国主义的目的效劳。”美“援”的受惠者的命运“恰恰就是替美国当炮灰”。
    西哈努克亲王强调说：“我们宁愿贫穷下去，但是我们坚持要绝对地自由。我们不羡慕向美帝国主义卖身投靠的那些人的命运，我们宁可用我们的有限的力量建设我们的国家和缔造我们的未来，慢慢地向前进展，但是要在和平、自由和欢乐中这样做，而不愿去‘享受’美国所施舍的那种虚假的、受凌辱和危险的繁荣。”
    他是在十六日在柴桢省的一个学校的落成仪式上的即席讲话中讲这番话的。
    西哈努克亲王说：“约翰逊总统在马尼拉同他的走狗和一些亚洲叛徒开了一个会，他利用这次会议大肆进行宣传，以欺骗世界舆论和亚洲人民，他还向亚洲人民许诺‘和平与繁荣’，但是有一个他简直不讳言的条件，那就是要他们同意充当美国的走狗。”
    亲王指出：“美国的经济援助也是一种阴险的讹诈，因为只要仔细审查一番就会发现，世界上没有一个国家的真正民族利益是真正因为美国的经济援助而得到裨益。事实上，如果美国一手向你提供面包和糖，它的另一只手却在暗害你的主权，你的独立，使你的经济枯竭，更不用说与此同时给你的国家带来混乱和战争及其没完没了的无穷的灾祸了。”亲王说：“美国人每天在南越干的是什么勾当，呈现在我们眼前的日益增多的飞机在搞什么，不是为了屠杀越南人民以便美国能为所欲为地在南越土地上驻军，那又是为了什么呢？”
    他强调指出：“我们的高尚和英勇的军队懂得，把自己出卖给金元上帝，接受美国慷慨供给的各种好处（汽车，别墅，漂亮的制服等等）要付出什么代价，因为我国的军人永远不会替美国充当炮灰，而美援的受惠者的命运恰恰就是替美国充当炮灰。山姆大叔之所以向他的走狗提供美元和种种好处，那是为了更好地利用他们为帝国主义的目的效劳。”
    西哈努克亲王说：“一旦剥开它的画皮，以上所说的就是这个帝国主义，这个新殖民主义者的各个方面，这也就是为什么我们将永远同它进行斗争。”
