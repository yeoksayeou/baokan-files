Title: Agreement on Barter and Payment Between China and Vietnam Signed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-11-24
Edition: 4

The governments of the People's Republic of China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam recently held talks in Beijing regarding the mutual supply of goods for 1967. The talks were conducted in a warm and sincere atmosphere of friendship. Through the talks, both sides signed the "Agreement on the Mutual Supply of Goods and Payment Between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam for 1967" today. Vice Premier Li Xiannian attended the signing ceremony.

Li Qiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, and Li Ban, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective governments. After the signing ceremony, Li Qiang hosted a banquet in honor of the Vietnamese guests.

### 中越换货和付款协定在京签订

1966-11-24
第4版()
专栏：

    中越换货和付款协定在京签订
    新华社二十三日讯　中华人民共和国政府代表和越南民主共和国政府代表，最近在北京就一九六七年相互供应货物等问题举行了会谈。会谈是在亲切、诚挚的友好气氛中进行的。通过会谈，双方在今天签订了“中华人民共和国政府和越南民主共和国政府关于一九六七年相互供应货物和付款的协定”。国务院副总理李先念参加了签字仪式。
    中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长李强，越南民主共和国对外贸易部副部长李班，分别代表两国政府在协定上签字。签字仪式后，李强设宴招待越南客人。
