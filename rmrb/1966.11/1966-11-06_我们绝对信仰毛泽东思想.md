Title: We Absolutely Believe in Mao Zedong Thought
Author: Ruan Bin
Date: 1966-11-06
Edition: 5

Mao Zedong Thought is like the sun of the East, illuminating our hearts and adding to our fighting will in our thorough struggle against the aggressors of U.S. imperialism and modern revisionism. Mao Zedong Thought has helped the people of the whole world to recognize a truth, that is, we must struggle against our common enemy, U.S. imperialism.

We absolutely believe in Mao Zedong Thought because it can bring happiness to us youth and to the people of the whole world.

### 我们绝对信仰毛泽东思想
阮彬
1966-11-06
第5版()
专栏：国外来信

    我们绝对信仰毛泽东思想
    毛泽东思想好像东方的太阳，照亮我们的心，为我们彻底反对美帝国主义侵略者、现代修正主义者的斗争，增添了战斗毅力。毛泽东思想帮助全世界人民认识了一条真理，这就是必须同我们共同敌人美帝国主义作斗争。
    我们绝对信仰毛泽东思想，因为它能够使我们青年和全世界人民得到幸福。　越南　阮彬
