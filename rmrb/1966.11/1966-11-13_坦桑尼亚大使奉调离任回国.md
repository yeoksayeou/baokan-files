Title: Tanzanian Ambassador Leaves Post and Returns to Country
Author:
Date: 1966-11-13
Edition: 5

Tanzanian Ambassador Leaves Post and Returns to Country

	New China News Agency, November 12th: The Ambassador of the United Republic of Tanzania to China, Waziri Juma, has been recalled and will leave his post. He departed Beijing by airplane this afternoon and returned to his home country.

### 坦桑尼亚大使奉调离任回国

1966-11-13
第5版()
专栏：

    坦桑尼亚大使奉调离任回国
    新华社十二日讯　坦桑尼亚联合共和国驻中国大使瓦齐里·朱马奉调离任，已于今天下午乘飞机离京回国。
