Title: Vice Chairman Song Qingling Receives Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-11-18
Edition: 6

Vice Chairman Song Qingling received Japanese guests this afternoon. According to Xinhua News Agency, on the 17th, Vice Chairman Song Qingling received the Chairman of the Japan-China Friendship Association, Miyazaki Seiken, and his wife, as well as the advisor to the Japanese delegation commemorating the 100th anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's birth, Ito Takeo, the delegation leader, Makino Uchi Tadahito, and all the delegation members. Vice Chairman Song Qingling had a warm and friendly conversation with the Japanese guests. She said, "I vividly remember that Sun Yat-sen consistently emphasized the importance of Sino-Japanese friendship and unity. Even before his death, when he went to Japan for a meeting, he specially emphasized this issue. In this regard, the peoples of China and Japan are both striving to achieve this goal."

Vice Chairman Song Qingling said, "Chairman Mao has consistently emphasized the need to establish and strengthen a genuine, not a false, anti-American united front internationally. The visit of these Japanese friends to China will further promote the unity and friendship between the peoples of China and Japan."

Present at the reception were the President of the China-Japan Friendship Association, Liao Chengzhi, and others.

### 宋庆龄副主席接见日本客人

1966-11-18
第6版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄副主席接见日本客人
    据新华社十七日讯  宋庆龄副主席今天下午接见日中友协理事长宫崎世民和夫人，以及日本纪念孙中山诞生一百周年访华代表团顾问伊藤武雄、团长牧野内武人和全体团员。宋庆龄副主席同日本客人进行了亲切友好的谈话。她说，我深刻地记忆着孙中山一贯重视中日友好团结，他在去世前到日本开会的时候，还特别强调这一问题。在这方面，我们中日两国人民都是为实现这一目标而努力着的。
    宋庆龄副主席说，毛主席一贯强调建立和加强国际间的真正而不是虚伪的反美统一战线。这次日本朋友前来中国，将会进一步促进中日两国人民的团结和友谊。
    接见时在座的，有中日友好协会会长廖承志等。（附图片）
