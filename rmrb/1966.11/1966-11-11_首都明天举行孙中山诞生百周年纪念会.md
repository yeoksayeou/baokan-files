Title: Capital to Hold Commemoration for the 100th Anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's Birth
Author:
Date: 1966-11-11
Edition: 1

The Preparatory Committee for the Centennial Commemoration of the Birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen held a meeting today for its members in Beijing.

The meeting heard a report by Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee, Comrade Liao Chengzhi, on the preparations for the centennial commemoration of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's birth.

The meeting approved Comrade Liao Chengzhi's report and decided to hold a grand commemoration ceremony for the 100th anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's birth in the capital on November 12.

### 首都明天举行孙中山诞生百周年纪念会

1966-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

    首都明天举行孙中山诞生百周年纪念会
    新华社十日讯  孙中山先生诞生一百周年纪念筹备委员会，今天举行了在北京的筹备委员的会议。
    会议听取了筹委会秘书长廖承志同志关于孙中山先生诞生一百周年纪念筹备情况的报告。
    会议同意廖承志同志的报告，并且决定于十一月十二日在首都隆重举行孙中山先生诞生一百周年纪念大会。
