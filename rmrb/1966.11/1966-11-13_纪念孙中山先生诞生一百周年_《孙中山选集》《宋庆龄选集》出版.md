Title: In Commemoration of the 100th Anniversary of Dr. Sun Yat-sen's Birth: Publication of "Selected Works of Sun Yat-sen" and "Selected Works of Soong Ching-ling"
Author:
Date: 1966-11-13
Edition: 2

In commemoration of the 100th anniversary of the birth of Dr. Sun Yat-sen, the People's Publishing House has published "Selected Works of Sun Yat-sen" and "Selected Works of Soong Ching-ling," which were released on November 11.

### 纪念孙中山先生诞生一百周年  《孙中山选集》《宋庆龄选集》出版

1966-11-13
第2版()
专栏：

    纪念孙中山先生诞生一百周年
    《孙中山选集》《宋庆龄选集》出版
    新华社十二日讯　为了纪念孙中山先生诞生一百周年，由人民出版社出版的《孙中山选集》、《宋庆龄选集》，已于十一日出版。
