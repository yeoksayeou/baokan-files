Title: Japanese Delegation of Social Scientists Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-11-27
Edition: 5

	Japanese Delegation of Social Scientists Arrives in Beijing

	Xinhua News Agency, November 25th—At the invitation of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, a delegation of nine Japanese social scientists led by Baba Katsumi arrived in Beijing today by airplane for a friendly visit to China.

### 日本社会科学家代表团到京

1966-11-27
第5版()
专栏：

    日本社会科学家代表团到京
    新华社二十五日讯　应中国科学院邀请前来我国进行友好访问的以马场克三为首的日本社会科学家代表团一行九人，今天乘飞机到达北京。
