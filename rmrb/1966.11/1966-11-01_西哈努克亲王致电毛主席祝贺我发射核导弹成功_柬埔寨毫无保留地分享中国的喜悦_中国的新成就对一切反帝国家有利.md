Title: Prince Sihanouk Cables Chairman Mao to Congratulate China on Successful Nuclear Missile Launch
Author:
Date: 1966-11-01
Edition: 1

	Prince Norodom Sihanouk of Cambodia has sent a cable to Chairman Mao to congratulate China on the successful launch of a nuclear missile. Cambodia shares China's joy unreservedly. China's new achievements are beneficial to all anti-imperialist countries.

	Xinhua News Agency, November 31st: Chairman Mao has received a cable from Norodom Sihanouk, Head of State of Cambodia, congratulating China on the successful test launch of a nuclear missile. The text of the cable is as follows:

	"Chairman Mao Zedong:

	On the occasion of the successful launch of a nuclear-tipped missile by the People's Republic of China, I, on behalf of the entire Khmer people and in my personal capacity, extend my most sincere congratulations to you on this new and highly brilliant achievement in Chinese science and technology. I and all my compatriots firmly believe that, in the face of imperialist threats in Asia and other regions, this outstanding achievement will make a valuable contribution to the cause of peace. Therefore, Cambodia unreservedly shares the great joy and justified pride of the great Chinese people. Under the wise leadership of its outstanding national leader, the great Chinese people are making continuous progress in all fields, and these outstanding advances are an example for the whole of Asia. The Khmer people are particularly pleased to see this. We are very glad to see the new and extraordinary achievements of the People's Republic of China, which can only be beneficial to the cause of all peace-loving and progressive countries that, like Cambodia, are determined to resist all imperialist pressures. On this occasion, I express to you my highest respect."

		The article ends here.

### 西哈努克亲王致电毛主席祝贺我发射核导弹成功  柬埔寨毫无保留地分享中国的喜悦  中国的新成就对一切反帝国家有利

1966-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王致电毛主席祝贺我发射核导弹成功
    柬埔寨毫无保留地分享中国的喜悦  中国的新成就对一切反帝国家有利
    新华社三十一日讯　毛主席收到了柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王发来的电报，对我国发射导弹核武器试验成功表示最诚挚的祝贺。电文如下：
    “毛泽东主席：值此中华人民共和国成功地发射一枚带有核弹头导弹之际，我谨代表全体高棉人民，并以我个人的名义，对中国科学技术所取得的这一新的、十分光辉的成就，向阁下表示最诚挚的祝贺。我和我的全体同胞坚信，面临着帝国主义在亚洲和其他地区的威胁，这一十分卓越的成就对和平事业将是珍贵的贡献。因此，柬埔寨毫无保留地分享伟大中国人民的喜悦和正当的骄傲。伟大的中国人民在它杰出的全国领袖的英明领导下，在各个领域不断取得新的进步，这些卓越的进步是整个亚洲的榜样，高棉人民对此感到异常高兴。我们十分欣慰地看到中华人民共和国取得的这一新的非凡的成就，这一成就只能对一切爱好和平和进步并像柬埔寨一样决心抵抗帝国主义一切压力的国家的事业有利。趁此机会，我谨向阁下表示我最崇高的敬意。”
