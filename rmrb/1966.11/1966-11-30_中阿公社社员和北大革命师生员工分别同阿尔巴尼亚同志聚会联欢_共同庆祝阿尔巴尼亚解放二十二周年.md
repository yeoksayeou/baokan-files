Title: Members of the Sino-Albanian Commune and Revolutionary Teachers and Students in the North Celebrate the 22nd Anniversary of Albania's Liberation Together with Albanian Comrades
Author:
Date: 1966-11-30
Edition: 4

Members of the Sino-Albanian Commune in the Beijing suburbs and revolutionary teachers and students in the capital held separate gatherings with Albanian comrades to celebrate the 22nd anniversary of Albania's liberation.

According to Xinhua News Agency, on November 27, members of the Sino-Albanian Commune, along with Albanian comrades in Beijing, sang Chinese songs such as "The Boat Sails on with the Helmsman at the Rudder" and held a lively gathering to celebrate the 22nd anniversary of Albania's liberation.

The gathering was attended by the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association delegation led by P. Doda, Albanian Charge d'Affaires in China Melchior Babani, and other staff from the Albanian Embassy in China.

The atmosphere at the gathering was filled with a deep sense of revolutionary friendship. The members and Albanian comrades repeatedly shouted, "Long live Chairman Mao!" and "Long live Comrade Enver Hoxha!"

During the gathering, the members presented copies of "Quotations from Chairman Mao" to the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association delegation. The delegation presented a banner to the commune. The members and Albanian comrades also performed cultural programs from both countries and sang "The Boat Sails on with the Helmsman at the Rudder" together.

According to Xinhua News Agency, on November 26, revolutionary teachers and students from Peking University held a warm gathering with Albanian comrades in Beijing to celebrate the 22nd anniversary of Albania's liberation.

The Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association delegation led by P. Doda, Albanian Charge d'Affaires in China Melchior Babani, and others attended the celebration.

The celebration was filled with the atmosphere of proletarian revolutionary friendship. Portraits of Comrade Mao Zedong and Comrade Enver Hoxha were prominently displayed in the center of the venue.

Everyone enthusiastically shouted, "Long live Chairman Mao!" and "Long live Comrade Enver Hoxha!"

### 中阿公社社员和北大革命师生员工分别同阿尔巴尼亚同志聚会联欢  共同庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放二十二周年

1966-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

    中阿公社社员和北大革命师生员工分别同阿尔巴尼亚同志聚会联欢
    共同庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放二十二周年
    据新华社讯　北京郊区中阿友好人民公社社员二十七日和在京的阿尔巴尼亚同志一起，高唱《大海航行靠舵手》等中国歌曲，热烈地进行联欢，共庆阿尔巴尼亚解放二十二周年。
    参加联欢会的有以彼·多德为首的阿中友协代表团，阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使馆参赞梅莱契·巴巴尼以及大使馆其他同志。
    联欢会上洋溢着一片极其亲切的革命友情，社员们和阿尔巴尼亚同志不断高呼“毛主席万岁！”“恩维尔·霍查同志万岁！”
    会上，社员们向阿中友协代表团同志赠送了《毛主席语录》。阿中友协代表团向公社赠送了锦旗。社员们和阿尔巴尼亚同志还在会上表演了中阿两国的文艺节目，并一起齐声高唱《大海航行靠舵手》。
    据新华社讯　北京大学革命师生员工，二十六日同在北京的阿尔巴尼亚同志亲切聚会，共庆阿尔巴尼亚解放二十二周年。
    以彼·多德为首的阿中友协代表团，阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使馆临时代办梅莱契·巴巴尼等，出席了庆祝会。
    庆祝会洋溢着无产阶级革命友谊的气氛。会场中央悬挂毛泽东同志和恩维尔·霍查同志的画像。
    会场上，大家热情地高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“恩维尔·霍查同志万岁！”
