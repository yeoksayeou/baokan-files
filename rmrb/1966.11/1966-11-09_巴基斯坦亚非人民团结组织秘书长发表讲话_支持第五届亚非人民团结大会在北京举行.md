Title: Secretary-General of the Pakistan Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Organization Speaks Out in Support of the Fifth Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Conference in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-11-09
Edition: 4

	The Secretary-General of the Pakistan Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Organization, Mirja Khaleed, recently stated in Lahore that the people of Pakistan will strongly support the holding of the Fifth Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Conference in Beijing and wish it every success.

	The Fourth Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Conference, held in Accra, Ghana, decided in May 1965 that the Fifth Conference would be held in Beijing in 1967. This decision has received widespread support.

	In his speech, Mirja Khaleed pointed out that China has made a tremendous contribution to the Afro-Asian solidarity movement. China firmly supports all just causes of the Afro-Asian peoples. It takes a resolute stand in exposing and opposing U.S. imperialism and its lackeys. This is something all the peoples of Afro-Asian countries can be proud of. China's contributions to the achievements of the Afro-Asian solidarity movement have won high praise.

### 巴基斯坦亚非人民团结组织秘书长发表讲话  支持第五届亚非人民团结大会在北京举行

1966-11-09
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦亚非人民团结组织秘书长发表讲话
    支持第五届亚非人民团结大会在北京举行
    据新华社拉合尔电　巴基斯坦亚非人民团结组织秘书长米拉杰·哈立德最近在这里说，巴基斯坦人民将大力支持第五届亚非人民团结大会在北京举行，并祝大会获得成功。
    在加纳温尼巴举行的第四届亚非人民团结大会一九六五年五月决定，第五届大会一九六七年在北京举行。这个决定受到了广泛的支持。
    米拉杰·哈立德在讲话中指出，中国对亚非团结运动作出了巨大的贡献。中国坚决支持亚非人民的一切正义事业。它采取揭露和反对美帝国主义和它的帮凶的坚定立场。这是所有亚非国家的人民值得自豪的。中国对亚非团结运动的成就的贡献赢得了高度赞赏。
