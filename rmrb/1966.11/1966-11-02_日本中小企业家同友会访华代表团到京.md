Title: Delegation from the Japan Small and Medium Enterprises Friendship Association Visits Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-11-02
Edition: 4

A delegation from the Japan Small and Medium Enterprises Friendship Association, led by Arakawa Bunki, arrived in Beijing yesterday. The China Council for the Promotion of International Trade will host a banquet in their honor this evening.

### 日本中小企业家同友会访华代表团到京

1966-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

    日本中小企业家同友会访华代表团到京
    新华社一日讯　以荒贺文吉为首的日本中小企业家同友会第四次访华代表团昨天到达北京，中国国际贸易促进委员会今晚设宴招待。
