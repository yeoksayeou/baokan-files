Title: Comrade Kang Sheng and Li Xiannian Lead Delegation to Leave Tirana and Return to China
Author:
Date: 1966-11-15
Edition: 1

By delegation head, Politburo Standing Committee member, and Central Secretariat member Kang Sheng, and deputy delegation head, Politburo member, and Central Secretariat member Li Xiannian, the Chinese Communist Party delegation, invited to attend the Fifth Congress of the Party of Labour of Albania and the celebrations of the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Party of Labour, and to visit various places in Albania, left Tirana by special plane at 10 a.m. on the 14th and returned to China. The Chinese Communist Party delegation was warmly seen off at the airport by the central leadership of the Party of Labour of Albania and people from all walks of life in the capital.

People waved bouquets and the national flags of China and Albania, shouting "Enver-Hoxha, Mao Zedong" repeatedly. A group of Young Pioneers presented flowers to the delegation members.

The following were present at the airport to see them off: Politburo members of the Party of Labour of Albania, Muhamet Shehu, Hysni Kapo, Beqir Balluku, Liri Belishova, Spiro Koleka, candidate member of the Politburo Adil Carcani, and Central Committee members Beqir Shytula, Fatmir Pashko, Nasi Nasiu, and the head of the International Department of the Party Central Committee, Pali Miska.

Also present at the airport to see them off were: Charge d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, Wang Jinchuan, embassy staff, representatives of Chinese experts and students in Albania. The ambassadors of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Socialist Republic of Romania to Albania, and the Charge d'Affaires of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to Albania were also present at the airport to see them off.

### 康生李先念同志率代表团离地拉那回国  谢胡、卡博、巴卢库、阿利雅、科列加等同志和各界群众到机场热烈欢送

1966-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

    康生李先念同志率代表团离地拉那回国
    谢胡、卡博、巴卢库、阿利雅、科列加等同志和各界群众到机场热烈欢送
    新华社地拉那十四日电　由团长、中共中央政治局常务委员、中央书记处书记康生和副团长、中共中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记李先念率领的中国共产党代表团，在应邀参加了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第五次代表大会和劳动党建党二十五周年庆祝活动，并在阿尔巴尼亚各地进行了访问之后，十四日上午乘专机离开地拉那回国。中国共产党代表团在机场上受到阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央领导人和阿尔巴尼亚首都各界群众的热烈欢送。人们手持花束和中、阿两国国旗，不断欢呼“恩维尔—毛泽东”。一群少先队员向代表团成员献了花。
    今天到机场送行的有：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员穆·谢胡、希·卡博、贝·巴卢库、拉·阿利雅、斯·科列加，政治局候补委员阿·凯莱齐，中央委员贝·什图拉、法·帕奇拉米、奈·纳赛以及党中央国际部长皮·比塔。
    到机场送行的还有：中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办王锦川、使馆工作人员、中国在阿尔巴尼亚的专家和留学生代表。越南民主共和国和罗马尼亚社会主义共和国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办也到机场送行。
