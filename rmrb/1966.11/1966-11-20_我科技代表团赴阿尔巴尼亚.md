Title: Chinese Technical Delegation to Albania
Author:
Date: 1966-11-20
Edition: 5

A delegation from the China-Albania Technical and Scientific Cooperation Joint Committee, led by the Chinese side's chairman, Vice Minister of the Textile Industry Chen Weiji, departed today in the afternoon by plane for Tirana to attend the 11th session of the China-Albania Technical and Scientific Cooperation Joint Committee.

Relevant officials from our country and the acting charge d'affaires of the Albanian Embassy in China, Baba Ni, were present at the airport to see them off.

### 我科技代表团赴阿尔巴尼亚

1966-11-20
第5版()
专栏：

    我科技代表团赴阿尔巴尼亚
    新华社十九日讯　由中国阿尔巴尼亚技术和技术科学合作联合委员会中国组主席、纺织工业部副部长陈维稷率领的中国科学技术代表团，今天下午乘飞机前往地拉那，参加中国阿尔巴尼亚技术和技术科学合作联合委员会第十一届会议。
    我国有关方面负责人和阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使馆临时代办巴巴尼等，到机场送行。
