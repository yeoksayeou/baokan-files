Title: Workers of Tianjin Garment Factory No. 2 Respond to Comrade Lin Biao's Call
Author:
Date: 1966-11-19
Edition: 3

Workers of Tianjin Garment Factory No. 2 have resolutely responded to Comrade Lin Biao's call, taking the "Three Constantly Read Articles" as their guiding principles. They are earnestly working to reform their worldview and have decided to dedicate their lives to the revolution.

	New China News Agency photographer.
