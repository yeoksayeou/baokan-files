Ambassador Chai Zemin Calls on President Sekou Toure
China Firmly Supports the Guinean People's Struggle Against U.S. Imperialism

	NEW DELHI, Oct. 31 (Xinhua) -- Chinese Ambassador to Guinea Chai Zemin called on President Sekou Toure of the Republic of Guinea this afternoon in Conakry. He expressed, on behalf of the Chinese Government, the strongest indignation at the U.S. imperialists' collusion with the reactionary regime in Ghana to kidnap the Guinean delegation led by Foreign Minister Lansana.

	Ambassador Chai Zemin strongly condemned this bandit-like act of U.S. imperialism and declared that the Chinese Government and people fully support the Guinean Government's resolute struggle against U.S. imperialism and its lackeys. Chai Zemin said that in this struggle, the Chinese Government and people will always stand firmly with the Guinean Government and people.

	President Sekou Toure expressed his heartfelt thanks to the Chinese Government for its firm support to the Guinean Government and people.

### 柴泽民大使拜会杜尔总统  中国坚决支持几内亚人民反美斗争

1966-11-02
第5版()
专栏：

    柴泽民大使拜会杜尔总统
    中国坚决支持几内亚人民反美斗争
    新华社科纳克里三十一日电　中国驻几内亚大使柴泽民十月三十一日下午在科纳克里拜会了几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔，代表中国政府，对美帝国主义伙同加纳反动当局绑架由几内亚外交部长朗萨纳率领的几内亚代表团一事，表示最强烈的愤怒。
    柴泽民大使强烈谴责美帝国主义的这种强盗行径，并表示完全支持几内亚政府对美帝国主义及其走狗的坚决斗争。柴泽民大使说，在这一斗争中，中国政府和人民始终坚决地同几内亚政府和人民站在一起。
    塞古·杜尔总统向中国大使表示，他衷心感谢中国政府对几内亚政府和人民的坚决支持。
