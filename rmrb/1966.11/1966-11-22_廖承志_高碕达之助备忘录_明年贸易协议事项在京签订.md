Liaozhengzhi-Kotaro Takasaki Memorandum: Trade Agreement Items for Next Year to be Signed in Beijing

	Xinhua News Agency, November 21st: The trade agreement items for the Liaozhengzhi-Kotaro Takasaki Memorandum for 1967 were signed in Beijing today.

	Liu Xihuan and Kawai Yoshikazu signed the agreement on behalf of the Chinese and Japanese sides, respectively.

	Those who attended the signing ceremony on the Chinese side included Liaozhengzhi and other relevant personnel such as Xiao Fangzhou, Zhao Anbo, Wang Xiaoyun, Wang Yinyu, and Li Xinong. Those who attended the signing ceremony on the Japanese side included members of the Japanese Liaozhengzhi-Kotaro Takasaki Memorandum Trade Delegation and staff from the Takasaki Office in Beijing. Japanese peace activist Saionji Kichinosuke, who is in Beijing, also attended the signing ceremony. After the signing, Kawai Yoshikazu of the Japanese Liaozhengzhi-Kotaro Takasaki Memorandum Trade Delegation held a farewell reception.

### 廖承志—高碕达之助备忘录  明年贸易协议事项在京签订

1966-11-22
第4版()
专栏：

    廖承志—高碕达之助备忘录
    明年贸易协议事项在京签订
    新华社二十一日讯  廖承志—高碕达之助备忘录一九六七年度贸易协议事项，今天在北京签订。
    刘希文和河合良一分别代表中日双方在协议事项上签字。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的，有廖承志及其他有关方面人士萧方洲、赵安博、王晓云、王荫圃、李新农等。日本方面参加签字仪式的，有日本廖承志—高碕达之助备忘录贸易代表团成员和高碕办事处驻北京人员。在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也参加了签字仪式。签字后，日本廖承志—高碕达之助备忘录贸易代表团河合良一举行了告别招待会。
