Title: Jiangxi Achieves Good Harvest of Late Rice in One Season
Author:
Date: 1966-11-03
Edition: 2

Jiangxi Province is currently harvesting its late rice in one season. According to the harvesting and threshing situation in many areas, although this year's late rice in various places has been severely affected by drought, the yield is as good as last year, and some areas even surpass last year. In some counties of Ganzhou and Fuzhou Prefectures that were affected by drought, the late rice that has been harvested generally increased by about 10% compared to last year. In Shangrao Prefecture, the yield per unit area of the more than 800,000 mu that has been harvested increased by 20% compared to last year.

### 江西一季晚稻获得好收成

1966-11-03
第2版()
专栏：

    江西一季晚稻获得好收成
    江西省正在收割一季晚稻。据许多地区收打的情况表明，今年各地的一季晚稻虽然遭受严重干旱的影响，但收成同去年一样好，部分地区还超过去年。赣州、抚州等专区一些受旱县社，已收的一季晚稻一般比去年增产一成左右；上饶专区已收的八十多万亩，单位面积产量比去年增长二成。　（据新华社讯）
