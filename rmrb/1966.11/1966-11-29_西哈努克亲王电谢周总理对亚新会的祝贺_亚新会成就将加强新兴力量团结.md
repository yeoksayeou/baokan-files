Title: Prince Sihanouk Cables Thanks to Premier Zhou for Congratulations on the Asian New Force Games
Author:
Date: 1966-11-29
Edition: 5

	Premier Zhou En Lai received a cable from Prince Sihanouk, Head of State of Cambodia, on November 27th. The cable expressed gratitude for Premier Zhou's enthusiastic congratulations on the first Asian New Force Games.

	In the cable, Prince Sihanouk, on behalf of Cambodia and the Cambodian people, and in his personal capacity, expressed heartfelt thanks to Premier Zhou, the Chinese government, and the Chinese people.

	The cable stated that the first Asian New Force Games "hold profound significance for the people of all Asian countries and will be recorded in the annals of Asia, which is struggling for independence and liberation, and against American imperialism and neo-colonialist forces and their accomplices. The first Asian New Force Games will demonstrate to the world that our peoples have made great progress in sports, just as in other areas, and have broken the new monopolies that imperialist countries have attempted to maintain."

	The cable continued: "Your Excellency's friendly evaluation of the work we have done to prepare for the New Force Games deeply moves us. The Royal Government and the Cambodian people recognize the importance of this Asian Games, and have therefore spared no effort to ensure that we are worthy of the honor and trust bestowed upon Cambodia. However, I must once again emphasize the significant role played by our Chinese friends, whose material assistance and advice are extremely valuable."

	The cable concluded by wishing that the achievements of the first Asian New Force Games will strengthen the unity of the new emerging forces.

### 西哈努克亲王电谢周总理对亚新会的祝贺  亚新会成就将加强新兴力量团结

1966-11-29
第5版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王电谢周总理对亚新会的祝贺
    亚新会成就将加强新兴力量团结
    新华社二十八日讯　周恩来总理二十七日收到了柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王的复电。感谢周总理对第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会的热情祝贺。
    西哈努克亲王在电报中代表柬埔寨和柬埔寨人民，并以他个人的名义，向周总理、中国政府和中国人民致以衷心的谢意。
    电报说，第一届亚洲新运会“对亚洲所有国家的人民具有深远的意义，并将载入正在为争取独立解放、为反对美帝国主义和新殖民主义势力及其帮凶而斗争的亚洲的史册。第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会将向全世界表明，我们各国人民在体育方面，如同在其他方面一样，也取得了巨大的进步，并打破了帝国主义国家企图保持的新的垄断。”
    电报接着说：“阁下对我们为筹备新运会所做的工作的友好评价，使我们深为感动。王国政府和柬埔寨人民认识到亚洲的这次运动会的重要性，因此全力以赴，争取无愧于给予柬埔寨的荣誉和信任。但是我仍要再次强调中国朋友所起的重大作用，他们提供的物质援助和建议是极其宝贵的。”
    电报最后祝第一届亚洲新运会的成就能加强新兴力量的团结。
