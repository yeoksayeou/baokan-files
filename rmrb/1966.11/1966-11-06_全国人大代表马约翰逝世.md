Title: National People's Congress Representative Ma John Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1966-11-06
Edition: 2

The National People's Congress representative and president of the All-China Sports Federation, Ma John, passed away due to illness at 31 October 1966, at the age of 84.

### 全国人大代表马约翰逝世

1966-11-06
第2版()
专栏：

    全国人大代表马约翰逝世
    新华社五日讯　全国人民代表大会代表、中华全国体育总会主任马约翰，因病于一九六六年十月三十一日凌晨逝世，享年八十四岁。
