Title: To Celebrate the Fifth Congress of the Party of Labour of Albania and the 25th Anniversary of Its Founding
Author:
Date: 1966-11-04
Edition: 5

To celebrate the convening of the Fifth Congress of the Party of Labour of Albania and the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Party of Labour of Albania, the China-Albania Friendship Association held a report meeting on the afternoon of October 31. Mu Lin, the leader of the recent delegation of the China-Albania Friendship Association, gave a report on his visit to Albania.

Mu Lin enthusiastically introduced to the more than 800 attendees the great achievements made by the Albanian people in the socialist revolution and socialist construction under the wise leadership of Comrade Enver Hoxha and the Party of Labour of Albania.

In his report, Mu Lin said that the Albanian people have a deep affection for our great leader Chairman Mao.

Delegation members Chen Yonggui and Li Suwen also reported on their impressions of their visit to Albania.

The Charge d'Affaires of the Albanian Embassy in China, Melechi Babani, and other Albanian comrades attended the report meeting.

### 为庆祝阿劳动党五大和成立二十五周年  中阿友好协会举行报告会

1966-11-04
第5版()
专栏：

    为庆祝阿劳动党五大和成立二十五周年
    中阿友好协会举行报告会
    据新华社讯　为庆祝阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第五次代表大会的召开和阿尔巴尼亚劳动党成立二十五周年，中国阿尔巴尼亚友好协会在十月三十一日下午举行了报告会。最近回国的中阿友协代表团团长穆林在会上作了访阿报告。
    穆林向到会的八百多人热情地介绍了阿尔巴尼亚人民在以恩维尔·霍查同志为首的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的英明领导下，在社会主义革命和社会主义建设中所取得的巨大成就。
    穆林在报告中说，阿尔巴尼亚人民非常热爱我们的伟大领袖毛主席。
    代表团团员陈永贵和李素文在会上报告了他们访阿观感。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使馆临时代办梅莱契·巴巴尼和其他阿尔巴尼亚同志出席了报告会。
