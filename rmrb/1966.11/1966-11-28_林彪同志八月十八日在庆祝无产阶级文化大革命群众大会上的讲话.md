Title: Comrade Lin Biao's Speech at the Celebration Rally for the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1966-11-28

Mao Zedong is the most outstanding leader of the proletariat in our time, and the greatest genius of our time. Chairman Mao trusts the masses the most, cares for the masses the most, and supports the masses' revolutionary movement the most. He is truly heart to heart with the revolutionary masses!

Lin Biao's speech on August 18th at the celebration rally for the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.
