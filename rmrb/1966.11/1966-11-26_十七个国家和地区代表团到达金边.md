Title: Seventeen Countries and Regions' Delegations Arrive in Phnom Penh
Author:
Date: 1966-11-26
Edition: 4

The Syrian, Lebanese, Ceylonese, and Iraqi sports delegations participating in the first Asian New Forces Games held in Cambodia arrived in Phnom Penh on November 24 and 25.

With this, all 17 countries and regions' sports delegations participating in the first Asian New Forces Games have arrived in Phnom Penh.

### 十七个国家和地区代表团到达金边

1966-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    十七个国家和地区代表团到达金边
    据新华社金边二十五日电　参加在柬埔寨举行的第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会的叙利亚、黎巴嫩、锡兰和伊拉克体育代表团，已于十一月二十四日和二十五日先后到达金边。
    至此，参加第一届亚洲新运会的十七个国家和地区的体育代表团，已全部到达金边。
