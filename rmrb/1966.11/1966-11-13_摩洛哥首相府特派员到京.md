Title: Moroccan Prime Minister's Special Envoy Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-11-13
Edition: 5

At the invitation of the China-Africa Friendship Association, a well-known Moroccan friend, Special Envoy of the Prime Minister's Office, Hajj Ahmad Benani, arrived in Beijing on November 11. Those who went to the airport to welcome him included Ding Xilin, Vice Chairman of the China-Africa Friendship Association, and others. The Chargé d'Affaires of the Moroccan Embassy in China, El Aloui, was also at the airport to greet him.

### 摩洛哥首相府特派员到京

1966-11-13
第5版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥首相府特派员到京
    新华社十二日讯　应中非友协邀请来我国进行友好访问的摩洛哥知名友好人士、首相府特派员哈吉·艾哈迈德·贝纳尼，于十一月十一日到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有中非友协副会长丁西林等。摩洛哥驻华大使馆临时代办埃尔·阿洛伊也到机场迎接。
