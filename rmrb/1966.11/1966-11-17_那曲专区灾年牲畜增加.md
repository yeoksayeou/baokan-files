Title: Livestock Increase in the Ngari Prefecture Despite Disasters
Author:
Date: 1966-11-17
Edition: 2

The vast liberated herdsmen of the Ngari Prefecture in the Tibet Autonomous Region, armed with Mao Zedong Thought, with the strong support of the stationed troops, have overcome an unprecedented severe windstorm and drought, resulting in an increase of over three percent in the total number of livestock this year compared to last year.

### 那曲专区灾年牲畜增加

1966-11-17
第2版()
专栏：

    那曲专区灾年牲畜增加
    西藏自治区那曲专区广大翻身牧民，以毛泽东思想为武器，在驻军大力支持下，战胜了历史上罕见的大风雪和严重干旱，使今年牲畜总头数比去年净增百分之三以上。
