Title: Senanayake Leads Friendly Delegation to Visit Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1966-11-16
Edition: 6

To enhance understanding and friendship among Asian and African writers and to promote the exchange of literature and culture between Asia and Africa, the Secretary-General of the Permanent Bureau of Asian and African Writers, Senanayake, led a friendly delegation to leave Beijing today for a visit to Pakistan.

The delegation members include: Cruz from Angola, Kair from Sudan, and Wang Li and Xu Huizhong from China.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Guo Moruo, Liao Chengzhi, Ding Xilin, and other relevant officials, as well as many Chinese writers.

Diplomatic officials from the Pakistani Embassy in China also went to the airport to see them off.

### 森纳那亚克率友好代表团去巴基斯坦访问

1966-11-16
第6版()
专栏：

    森纳那亚克率友好代表团去巴基斯坦访问
    新华社十五日讯　为加强亚非作家之间的了解和友谊，并促进亚非文学和文化的交流，亚非作家常设局秘书长森纳那亚克率领友好代表团今天离开北京去巴基斯坦访问。
    代表团成员有：安哥拉达克鲁斯，苏丹凯尔，中国王力、徐怀中。
    到机场欢送的，有郭沫若、廖承志、丁西林等有关方面负责人以及中国作家多人。
    巴基斯坦驻华大使馆外交官员也到机场欢送。
