Title: Under the Radiant Light of Mao Zedong Thought, Foreign Experts Full of Revolutionary Passion Go to the Countryside for Labor and Tempering
Author:
Date: 1966-11-26
Edition: 5

The sun rises, and the sky is filled with red light.
Facing the rising sun, fifty-eight foreign friends of different nationalities and skin colors, singing "The East Is Red," came to the fields of a people's commune in the suburbs of Beijing, starting a new class in studying and applying Chairman Mao's works.

For a while, the fields presented a bustling scene of labor. Just like the commune members, the foreign friends rolled up their sleeves and trouser legs, not minding the dirt or the hard work. Some gripped their shovels to dig up soil clumps, while others carried baskets to transport cabbages. With beads of sweat on their cheeks, they wiped them off with their sleeves and continued to work tirelessly; even with blisters and bleeding hands, they just stuck on a piece of tape and did not pause for a moment. Amidst the intense and joyful labor, they sang over and over again: "Rain and dew nourish the seedlings to grow strong, doing revolution relies on Mao Zedong Thought..."

### 在毛泽东思想光辉照耀下  外国专家满怀革命激情下乡劳动锻炼

1966-11-26
第5版()
专栏：

    在毛泽东思想光辉照耀下
    外国专家满怀革命激情下乡劳动锻炼
    红日升起，霞光满天。
    迎着初升的太阳，五十八名国籍不同、肤色不同的外国朋友，高唱着《东方红》来到北京郊区人民公社的田野，开始了他们活学活用毛主席著作的新的一课。
    一时，田野上呈现一派热气腾腾的劳动景象。和社员们一样，外国朋友们挽起衣袖和裤腿，不嫌脏，不怕累，有的紧攥铁锨挖土块，有的抬着箩筐运白菜。脸颊上挂满了汗珠，他们用衣袖擦擦，还是一股劲猛干；手上打了泡，流血了，他们贴一块胶布，也不肯歇一口气。伴随着紧张而愉快的劳动，他们一遍又一遍地唱着：“雨露滋润禾苗壮，干革命靠的是毛泽东思想。……”
