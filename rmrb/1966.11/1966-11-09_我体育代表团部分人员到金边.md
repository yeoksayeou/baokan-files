Title: Part of the Chinese Sports Delegation Arrives in Phnom Penh
Author:
Date: 1966-11-09
Edition: 5

Part of the Chinese sports delegation participating in the First Asian New Forces Games arrived in Phnom Penh on November 7. The delegation, consisting of 177 athletes and staff members, arrived on two special flights. They were the first foreign athletes to arrive in Phnom Penh for this event. They were warmly welcomed at the airport by officials from the executive headquarters of the First Asian New Forces Games, including Bo Sen An and Tao Suo, as well as Cambodian athletes. Yang Hui Sheng, the cultural counselor from the Chinese Embassy in Cambodia, was also present to welcome them.

### 我体育代表团部分人员到金边

1966-11-09
第5版()
专栏：

    我体育代表团部分人员到金边
    新华社金边七日电　参加第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会的中国体育代表团一部分运动员和工作人员一百七十七人，七日分乘两架专机到达金边。这是第一批到达金边参加这届亚新会的外国运动员。他们在机场受到第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会执行总部负责人卜森安、执行总部接待小组委员会主席陶索以及柬埔寨运动员的热烈欢迎。中国驻柬埔寨大使馆文化参赞杨惠生也到机场欢迎。
