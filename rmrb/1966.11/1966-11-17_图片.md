Title: Premier Zhou Enters Receives Guinean Economic Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-11-17
Edition: 5

On November 16, Premier Zhou Enlai received the Minister of Economic Development of Guinea, Ismail Touré, and members of the Guinean government economic delegation led by him. The photo shows a group photo taken during the reception. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter)
