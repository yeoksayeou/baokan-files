Title: Vice Governor of Guangdong Province Huang Jie Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1966-11-16
Edition: 4

Vice Governor of Guangdong Province Huang Jie, a representative of the National People's Congress and a member of the Committee of the State Council for Overseas Chinese Affairs, passed away in Guangzhou on November 9, 1966, due to illness. He was 56 years old at the time of his death.

### 广东省副省长黄洁病逝

1966-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

    广东省副省长黄洁病逝
    新华社广州十五日电　全国人民代表大会代表、中华人民共和国华侨事务委员会委员　广东省副省长黄洁，因病于一九六六年十一月九日在广州逝世，逝世时五十六岁。
