Title: Trade Delegation from Zanzibar, Tanzania Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-11-05
Edition: 5

A trade delegation from Zanzibar, Tanzania, led by the Deputy Minister of Trade and Industry of Zanzibar, Tanzania, Shaban Sudi Mpanda, arrived in Beijing today. The delegation, consisting of three members, had previously participated in the China Export Commodities Fair in Guangzhou and flew to Beijing from there.

### 坦桑尼亚桑给巴尔贸易代表团到京

1966-11-05
第5版()
专栏：

    坦桑尼亚桑给巴尔贸易代表团到京
    新华社四日讯　以坦桑尼亚桑给巴尔贸易和工业部副部长沙班·苏迪·姆庞达为首的坦桑尼亚桑给巴尔贸易代表团一行三人，在广州参加了中国出口商品交易会后，今天由广州乘飞机到达北京。
