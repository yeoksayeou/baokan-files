Title: Tanzanian Zanzibar Trade Delegation Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-11-07
Edition: 5

A trade delegation from Zanzibar, led by the Deputy Minister of Trade and Industry of Tanzania, Zanzibar, Shaban Sudi Mpanda, consisting of three members, departed Beijing by plane today to visit and tour Shanghai. The delegation will return to their country after visiting Shanghai.

Relevant officials from the Ministry of Foreign Trade and the Acting Charge d'Affaires of the Tanzanian Embassy in China, Ali Mingogo, were at the airport to see them off.

### 坦桑尼亚桑给巴尔贸易代表团离京

1966-11-07
第5版()
专栏：

    坦桑尼亚桑给巴尔贸易代表团离京
    新华社六日讯　以坦桑尼亚桑给巴尔贸易和工业部副部长沙班·苏迪·姆庞达为首的坦桑尼亚桑给巴尔贸易代表团一行三人，今天乘飞机离开北京去上海参观访问。代表团将在访问上海后回国。
    对外贸易部有关负责人和坦桑尼亚驻中国大使馆临时代办阿里·明尼戈戈等到机场送行。
