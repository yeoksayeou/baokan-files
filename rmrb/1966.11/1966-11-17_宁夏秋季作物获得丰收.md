Title: Ningxia Achieves Bumper Autumn Harvest
Author:
Date: 1966-11-17
Edition: 2

The broad masses of community members in the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region, holding high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, have achieved a universal increase in autumn crop yields. The famous grain-producing region of the Yellow River Valley has seen increases of around 20% in crops such as rice, proso millet, and legumes compared to last year's bumper harvest. Even the vast mountainous areas have seen a historically rare good harvest of grain crops.

### 宁夏秋季作物获得丰收

1966-11-17
第2版()
专栏：

    宁夏秋季作物获得丰收
    宁夏回族自治区广大社员，高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，夺得秋季作物普遍增产。著名的粮食产区河套地区的水稻、糜子、豆类等作物都比丰收的去年增产两成左右。广大山区的粮食作物也获得了历史上少有的好收成。
