Title: Meeting of the Sino-Soviet Committee on Scientific and Technical Cooperation Signs Meeting Minutes
Author:
Date: 1966-11-07
Edition: 5

The fifteenth session of the Sino-Soviet Committee on Scientific and Technical Cooperation between the People's Republic of China and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics was recently held in Beijing and signed the meeting minutes on November 6.

### 中苏科技合作委员会会议签订会议议定书

1966-11-07
第5版()
专栏：

    中苏科技合作委员会会议签订会议议定书
    新华社六日讯　中华人民共和国和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟科学技术合作委员会第十五届会议最近在北京举行，并于十一月六日签订了会议议定书。
