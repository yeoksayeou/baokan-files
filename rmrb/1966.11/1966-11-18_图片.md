Title: Exhibition of Dazhai Pictures in Bamako, Mali
Author:
Date: 1966-11-18
Edition: 5

At the exhibition of Dazhai pictures held in Bamako, the capital of Mali, the Malian people enthusiastically praised, "The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution led by Chairman Mao is excellent!" The photo shows Malian audiences eagerly seeking pictures of Chairman Mao receiving the Red Guards.  (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter)
