	Brief News

	Algeria and Syria Reaffirm Support for the Struggle of the Palestinian People

	Algerian Prime Minister Sidiki Slimane and Syrian Prime Minister Yusuf Zu'ayyin issued a joint statement on the evening of the 7th, reaffirming their support for the Palestinian people's struggle to return to their homeland. The statement said: "Both sides believe that all Arab fighting masses should unite to engage in their primary mission—the war of liberation of Palestine. The Palestinian people have the right to organize themselves and prepare for the legitimate struggle to liberate their usurped homeland."

	McNamara Surrounded by American Protesting Students

	One of the chief culprits of the U.S. aggression in Vietnam, Defense Secretary McNamara, was surrounded by hundreds of students opposing the Vietnam War during his visit to the famous U.S. university, Harvard, on the 7th. He was subjected to severe questioning by the students, who left him speechless and unable to escape. He managed to sneak out through an underground tunnel used for heating pipes, only with the protection of a large police escort.

	Ecuadorian Student Union Condemns U.S. Imperialism

	The Ecuadorian Student Union recently held its 22nd National Congress. The congress passed a resolution strongly condemning U.S. imperialism's aggressive crimes around the world. The resolution expressed the determination to carry the struggle against U.S. imperialism to the end.

	Denmark Announces Dissolution of Parliament and Upcoming Elections

	The Danish Krag government, after its tax reform bill was rejected by parliament, announced its resignation on the 2nd. On the same day, it announced the dissolution of parliament and scheduled general elections for November 22nd.

	One-Third of Italy's Land Affected by Flooding

	Floods caused by storms have affected 100,000 square kilometers of land in Italy (one-third of the country's total land area). Preliminary statistics indicate that over a hundred people have died in this flood, with material losses amounting to 100 to 150 billion lire. This is the 12th flood to hit Italy in the past 20 years.

### 简明新闻

1966-11-10
第4版()
专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    阿叙重申支持巴勒斯坦人民斗争
    阿联总理西德基·苏莱曼和叙利亚总理优素福·扎因七日晚上发表了一项联合公报，重申支持巴勒斯坦人民争取返回家园的斗争。公报说：“双方认为，全体阿拉伯战斗的群众应当团结起来，从事他们的主要事业——巴勒斯坦的解放战争。巴勒斯坦人民有权把自己组织起来，准备进行解放被篡夺的家园的合法斗争。”
    麦克纳马拉被美国示威学生包围
    美国侵越战争的罪魁祸首之一、国防部长麦克纳马拉七日在访问美国著名学府——哈佛大学时，被数百名反对侵越战争的学生团团围困，受到了学生们的严辞责问。他被问得理屈辞穷，无法脱身，好不容易才在大批警察的护卫下，偷偷从一条安置暖气管道的地下隧道溜了出去。
    厄瓜多尔大学生联合会谴责美帝
    厄瓜多尔大学生联合会最近举行了第二十二届全国代表大会。大会通过的决议强烈谴责美帝国主义在世界各地的侵略罪行。
    决议表示要把反对美帝国主义的斗争进行到底。
    丹麦宣布解散议会即将举行大选
    丹麦克拉格政府由于改革税收制度的法案被议会否决，在二日宣布辞职。
    同一天，宣布解散议会，并在十一月二十二日举行大选。
    意大利有三分之一土地遭受水灾
    暴风雨引起的洪水，使意大利十万平方公里土地（占全国土地面积的三分之一）受到灾害。
    据初步统计，已经有一百多人死于这次水灾，物质损失达一万到一万五千亿里拉。
    这是意大利二十年来发生的第十二次水灾。
                          （据新华社讯）
