Title: Chinese Government Economic Delegation Arrives in Mali
Author:
Date: 1966-11-23
Edition: 4

A Chinese government economic delegation led by Deputy Director of the Foreign Economic Liaison Commission, Wang Daohan, arrived in Bamako on the 19th at the invitation of the Malian government for a friendly visit.

### 我政府经济考察团到达马里

1966-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

    我政府经济考察团到达马里
    据新华社巴马科电　由对外经济联络委员会副主任汪道涵率领的中国政府经济考察团应马里政府的邀请，十九日到达巴马科对马里进行友好访问。
