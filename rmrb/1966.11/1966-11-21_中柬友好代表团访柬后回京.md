Title: Chinese-Cambodian Friendship Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1966-11-21
Edition: 4

The Chinese-Cambodian Friendship Delegation, which was invited to visit Cambodia, consisting of eight members, concluded their visit and returned to Beijing today, led by the delegation head and Vice President of the Chinese-Cambodian Friendship Association, Zhang Qi.

Vice Premier Chen Yi, along with relevant officials Han Nianlong, Ding Xilin, and Zhou Yiping, were at the airport to welcome them.

Also present at the airport to welcome the delegation were Prince Norodom Naradip, Cambodian Ambassador to China Zhang Gang, and Minister Plenipotentiary Shen Lisu.

### 中柬友好代表团访柬后回京

1966-11-21
第4版()
专栏：

    中柬友好代表团访柬后回京
    新华社二十日讯　应邀去柬埔寨进行友好访问的中柬友好代表团一行八人，在结束访问后，由团长、中柬友协副会长张茜率领，今天乘飞机回到北京。
    到机场迎接的，有陈毅副总理，以及有关方面负责人韩念龙、丁西林、周一萍等。
    到机场迎接的，还有诺罗敦·纳拉迪波王子，柬埔寨驻中国大使张岗，公使衔参赞沈力速等。
