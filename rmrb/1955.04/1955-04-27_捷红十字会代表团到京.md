Title: Czechoslovak Red Cross Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-04-27
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 24th – At the invitation of the Red Cross Society of China, the Czechoslovak Red Cross delegation, led by Eduard Tuma, Chairman of the Czechoslovak Red Cross, and including member Dr. Pannick, member of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Red Cross, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 24th to attend the "May Day" Labor Day celebrations in our country. Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China, Cui Yitian, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Public Health of the People's Republic of China, and others were at the airport to welcome them.

### 捷红十字会代表团到京

1955-04-27
第4版()
专栏：

    捷红十字会代表团到京
    新华社二十四日讯　应中国红十字会总会的邀请前来我国参加“五一”劳动节观礼的捷克斯洛伐克红十字会代表团团长、捷克斯洛伐克红十字会主席爱德华·图马和团员、捷克斯洛伐克红十字会中央委员会委员潘尼克医生，在二十四日乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有中国红十字会总会会长李德全、中华人民共和国卫生部副部长崔义田等人。
