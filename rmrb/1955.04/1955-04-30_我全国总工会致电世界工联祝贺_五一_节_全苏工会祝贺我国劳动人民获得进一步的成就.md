Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends a Congratulatory Message to the World Federation of Trade Unions on "May Day" / All-Soviet Central Council of Trade Unions Congratulates the Chinese Laboring People on Further Achievements

Author: 
Date: 1955-04-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram to the World Federation of Trade Unions on April 28th, congratulating them on "May Day" International Labor Day. The telegram reads as follows:

To the World Federation of Trade Unions:

On the occasion of the "May Day" International Labor Day, a festival of solidarity for the working people of the world, I extend warm congratulations on behalf of the Chinese working class to the World Federation of Trade Unions, which struggles for the interests of the working class all over the world.

Since its establishment, the World Federation of Trade Unions has achieved significant successes in promoting the unity and solidarity of the international working class and safeguarding the interests of the working class. I wish the World Federation of Trade Unions even greater achievements in the struggle to improve the living standards of working people, defend trade union rights, democracy and freedom, strengthen the solidarity of the international working class, and defend lasting world peace.

Long live the solidarity of the working class of the world!
                          All-China Federation of Trade Unions

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th. The All-Soviet Central Council of Trade Unions sent a telegram to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions on April 26th, congratulating them on "May Day" International Labor Day. The telegram reads as follows:

To the All-China Federation of Trade Unions:

Warm congratulations on "May Day," the festival of international solidarity of the working people!

We wish our brothers, the laboring people of the People's Republic of China, further achievements in the struggle to complete the first five-year plan for developing the national economy, for the industrialization of the country, and for laying the foundation of socialism.

May the unbreakable friendship between the People's Republic of China and the Soviet Union be further consolidated.
                                    All-Soviet Central Council of Trade Unions

### 我全国总工会致电世界工联祝贺“五一”节  全苏工会祝贺我国劳动人民获得进一步的成就

1955-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    我全国总工会致电世界工联祝贺“五一”节
    全苏工会祝贺我国劳动人民获得进一步的成就
    新华社二十八日讯　中华全国总工会二十八日致电世界工会联合会，祝贺“五一”国际劳动节。电文如下：
    世界工会联合会：
    值此全世界劳动人民团结的节日——“五一”国际劳动节之际，谨代表中国工人阶级向为全世界工人阶级利益而斗争的世界工会联合会致以热烈的祝贺。
    世界工会联合会自成立以来，在促进国际工人阶级的统一、团结、保卫工人阶级利益的事业中已获得了重大的成就。谨祝世界工会联合会在争取改善劳动人民的生活水平、保卫工会权利、民主自由、加强国际工人阶级的团结与保卫世界持久和平的斗争中获得更伟大的成就。
    全世界工人阶级团结万岁！
                          中华全国总工会
    新华社二十七日讯　全苏工会中央理事会二十六日致电中华全国总工会，祝贺“五一”国际劳动节。电文如下：
    中华全国总工会：
    热烈地祝贺劳动人民国际团结的节日——“五一”节。
    祝我们的兄弟中华人民共和国劳动人民在为完成发展国民经济的第一个五年计划、为国家工业化、为建立社会主义基础而进行的斗争中获得进一步的成就。
    愿中华人民共和国和苏联之间牢不可破的友谊日益巩固。全苏工会中央理事会
