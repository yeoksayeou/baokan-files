Title: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu Receives Chinese Buddhist Delegation to Burma
Author:
Date: 1955-04-10
Page: 1
　　
    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 9th: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu hosted a vegetarian feast for the Chinese Buddhist Delegation to Burma in Pegu at noon on the 7th. Before the feast, Prime Minister U Nu and his wife met with the head of the delegation, Xirao Gyatso, and others according to Buddhist etiquette.

    The Chinese Buddhist Delegation to Burma was accompanied to Pegu on the 7th by U Win, the Burmese Minister of Religious Affairs and Minister of Culture, and his wife, as well as Thekin Lu Min, the Director of Religious Guidance. In Pegu, they visited the Shwemawdaw Pagoda. The Chinese Buddhist Delegation to Burma donated 1,200 yuan in alms and presented hadas to the reclining Buddha.

### 缅甸总理吴努招待我国佛教访缅代表团

1955-04-10
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸总理吴努招待我国佛教访缅代表团
    新华社九日讯　仰光消息：缅甸联邦总理吴努七日中午在勃固设斋招待中国佛教访缅代表团。斋前，吴努总理和夫人依照佛教礼仪会见了喜饶嘉错团长等。
    中国佛教访缅代表团一行，是在七日由缅甸联邦政府宗教事务部长兼联邦文化部长吴温和夫人以及宗教指导处长德钦卢敏陪同前往勃固访问的。在勃固，他们参观了瑞摩都佛塔。中国佛教访缅代表团共捐献了缘金一千二百元，并向卧佛献哈达。
