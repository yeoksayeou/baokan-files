Title: Austrian Government Issues Communiqué on Tripartite Declaration of US, UK, and France
Author:
Date: 1955-04-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, April 7th: According to TASS, after the High Commissioners of the UK, US, and France in Austria presented a joint declaration to Austrian Chancellor Raab, the Austrian News Agency published an official Austrian communiqué. The communiqué stated: "The Austrian side notes with satisfaction the tripartite declaration."

The communiqué said: "The Austrian government recognizes that a treaty with Austria can only be concluded on the basis of an agreement between the four powers. Therefore, it believes that the main purpose of this visit is to achieve a result: to enable the four occupying powers to meet again at the negotiating table after the Austrian government delegation returns from Moscow."

### 奥政府就美英法三国宣言发表公报

1955-04-09
第4版()
专栏：

    奥政府就美英法三国宣言发表公报
    新华社八日讯　据塔斯社维也纳七日讯：在英、美、法三国驻奥地利高级专员向奥地利总理拉布提出联合宣言后，奥地利通讯社发表了奥地利的一个官方公报。这个公报说：“奥地利方面满意地注意到三国宣言”。
    公报说：“奥地利政府认识到，只有在四国达成协议的基础上才有可能缔结对奥和约。因此，它认为这次访问的主要目的是获致这样一种结果：使四个占领国在奥地利政府代表团从莫斯科回来以后，能够重新在会议桌上会面。”
