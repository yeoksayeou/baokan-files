Title: North Korean, Hungarian, and Romanian Newspapers Publish News of the Chinese Communist Party's National Conference

Author:
Date: 1955-04-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 7th. North Korean newspapers such as "Rodong Sinmun," "Minju Choson," "Korean People's Army Daily," and "Minbo" all prominently published the full text of the Chinese Communist Party Central Committee's Communiqué on the National Conference of the Party and related resolutions on the 7th.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 7th. Budapest dispatch: The Hungarian newspaper "Szabad Nép" published the full text of the Chinese Communist Party Central Committee's Communiqué on the National Conference of the Party and the Resolution of the National Conference on the Anti-Party Alliance of Gao Gang and Rao Shushi on the 6th. Other newspapers in Budapest also published news of the Chinese Communist Party's National Conference.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 7th. Bucharest dispatch: All major newspapers in Bucharest, the capital of Romania, published news of the Chinese Communist Party's National Conference on the 6th.

### 朝、匈、罗报纸  刊载中共全国代表会议的消息

1955-04-08
第1版()
专栏：

    朝、匈、罗报纸
    刊载中共全国代表会议的消息
    新华社平壤七日电　朝鲜“劳动新闻”、“民主朝鲜报”、“朝鲜人民军报”和“朝鲜民报”等报纸，都在七日以显著地位全文刊载了中国共产党中央委员会关于党的全国代表会议的公报和有关的决议。
    新华社布拉格七日电　布达佩斯消息：匈牙利“自由人民报”六日全文刊载了中国共产党中央委员会关于党的全国代表会议的公报，全国代表会议关于高岗、饶漱石反党联盟的决议。布达佩斯其他各报也刊载了中国共产党全国代表会议的消息。
    新华社布拉格七日电　布加勒斯特消息：罗马尼亚首都布加勒斯特各大报纸在六日都刊载了中国共产党全国代表会议的消息。
