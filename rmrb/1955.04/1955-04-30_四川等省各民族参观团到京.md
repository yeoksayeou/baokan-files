Title: Visiting Delegations of Various Nationalities from Sichuan and Other Provinces Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-04-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: Visiting delegations of various nationalities from Sichuan, Xikang, Guangxi, and Yunnan provinces, who will attend the "May 1st" International Labor Day celebrations, arrived in Beijing on April 26th and 27th respectively.

    The Sichuan and Xikang provinces' visiting delegation, totaling fifty-six people, is led by delegation leader Luo Zhiqing (Yi), and deputy delegation leaders Liu Ruquan (Han), Yang Wenzhen (Tibetan), and Zhang Zhicheng (Tibetan). The Guangxi province's visiting delegation, totaling forty-six people, is led by delegation leader Qin Baolong (Zhuang), and deputy delegation leader Xu Pengnian (Zhuang). The Yunnan province's visiting delegation, totaling twenty-three people, is led by delegation leader Ai Qun (Han), and deputy delegation leaders Lu Guoliang (Hani), Mo Xiaoguai (Dai), Hei Mojia (Lisu), and Li Qun (Han). The members of these three visiting delegations are labor models and work models from the Han, Tibetan, Yi, Zhuang, Yao, Miao, Dong, Dai, Jingpo, Hani, Lisu, Bai, and Lahu nationalities.

    Representatives from the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China welcomed each visiting delegation at the railway station upon their arrival in Beijing.

### 四川等省各民族参观团到京

1955-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    四川等省各民族参观团到京
    新华社二十九日讯　参加“五一”国际劳动节观礼的四川、西康、广西、云南四省各民族参观团分别于四月二十六、二十七日先后抵京。
    四川、西康两省各民族参观团共五十六人，由团长罗志清（彝）、副团长刘汝荃（汉）、杨文珍（藏）、张志成（藏）率领。广西省各民族参观团共四十六人，由团长覃宝龙（僮）、副团长徐彭年（僮）率领。云南省各民族参观团共二十三人，由团长艾群（汉）、副团长卢国亮（哈尼）、莫小乖（傣）、黑墨加（傈僳）、李群（汉）率领。这三个参观团的团员是汉、藏、彝、僮、瑶、苗、侗、傣、景颇、哈尼、傈僳、民家、拉祜等民族的劳动模范和工作模范。
    各参观团抵京时，中华人民共和国民族事务委员会曾派代表到车站欢迎。
