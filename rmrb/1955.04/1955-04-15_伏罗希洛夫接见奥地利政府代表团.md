Title: Voroshilov Receives Austrian Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-04-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 14th: According to TASS, the President of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, K.Y. Voroshilov, received the Austrian government delegation led by Austrian Federal Chancellor Julius Raab and members of the delegation on the 13th. Austrian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, N. Bischoff, accompanied the delegation during the reception.

Present at the reception were Soviet Foreign Minister V.M. Molotov and Soviet Ambassador to Austria and High Commissioner I.I. Ilyichev.

During the reception, Raab handed Voroshilov a letter from the President of the Austrian Republic, Körner. This letter requested the pardon of Austrians sentenced in the Soviet Union for crimes committed against the Soviet people during the war. The letter from the President of the Austrian Republic stated: If "the Soviet Union can grant a magnanimous pardon, it would be the best gift to the Austrian Republic on the tenth anniversary of Austria's revival. The Soviet people can be assured that all Austrians will express sincere and deep gratitude for such a magnanimous act."

The President of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Voroshilov, told Austrian Chancellor Raab that he believed the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR would treat this request from the President of the Austrian Republic favorably.

### 伏罗希洛夫接见奥地利政府代表团

1955-04-15
第4版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫接见奥地利政府代表团
    新华社十四日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席克·叶·伏罗希洛夫十三日接见了奥地利政府代表团团长、奥地利联邦总理尤·拉布和代表团团员们。陪同代表团受接见的有奥地利驻苏大使纳·比萧夫。
    接见时在座的有苏联外交部长维·米·莫洛托夫和苏联驻奥地利大使兼高级专员伊·伊·伊利切夫。
    在接见时，拉布把奥地利共和国总统柯尔纳的一封信交给伏罗希洛夫。这封信要求赦免那些因在战时曾犯有反对苏联人民的罪行而在苏联被处徒刑的奥地利人。奥地利共和国总统的信中说：如果“苏联能作出宽宏大量的赦免，那将是苏联在奥地利复兴十周年的时候对奥地利共和国最好的馈赠。苏联人民可以相信：全体奥地利人会对这种宽宏大量的行为表示真挚的深切的谢忱。”
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫向奥地利总理拉布说，他想，苏联最高苏维埃主席团会善意地对待奥地利共和国总统的这一要求的。
