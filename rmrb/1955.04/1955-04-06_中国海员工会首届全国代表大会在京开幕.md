Title: First National Congress of the China Seamen's Union Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-04-06
Page: 1

    The First National Congress of the China Seamen's Union Opens in Beijing

    (Our Newspaper News) The First National Congress of the China Seamen's Union opened in Beijing on the 4th. After the opening of the meeting, the congratulatory message from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China was read. Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Li Yunchang, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Communications of the People's Republic of China, spoke at the meeting.

    At this conference, the work of the preparatory committee of the China Seamen's Union over the past five years will be reviewed and summarized, the guidelines and tasks of the union's work in the future will be formulated, and the national leading body of the Seamen's Union will be elected.

### 中国海员工会首届全国代表大会在京开幕

1955-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    中国海员工会首届全国代表大会在京开幕
    本报讯　中国海员工会第一届全国代表大会四日在北京开幕。会议开幕后，宣读了中共中央的祝词。中华全国总工会赖若愚主席和中华人民共和国交通部李运昌副部长在会上讲了话。
    在这次大会上，将要检查和总结中国海员工会筹备委员会五年来的工作，制定今后工会工作的方针和任务，选举海员工会全国领导机构。
