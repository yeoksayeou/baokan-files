Title: Pham Van Dong and Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Laos Issue Joint Statement Vietnam and Laos will Develop Bilateral Relations Based on the Five Principles
Author:
Date: 1955-04-25
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Bandung, April 23rd: Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Ket Sasorith, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Laos, issued a joint statement on the 23rd. The full text is as follows:

His Excellency Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and His Excellency Ket Sasorith, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Laos, held friendly talks, attended by His Excellency Jawaharlal Nehru, Prime Minister of the Republic of India, and His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Premier of the People's Republic of China.

They reached an agreement and issued the following statement:

1.  The Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam believes that the political solution to be sought by the Government of the Kingdom of Laos and "Laos" in accordance with the Geneva Accords is a matter of internal order, and the Government of the Kingdom of Laos and "Laos" are completely free to resolve this issue in the best way in accordance with the supreme interests of the country and the Lao people.

2.  The Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Government of the Kingdom of Laos will develop and coordinate the good-neighborly relations that exist and should exist between the two countries within the scope of the Five Principles stipulated in the Sino-Indian Agreement of April 29, 1954.

            Bandung, April 23, 1955

### 范文同和老挝王国首相发表联合声明  越南和老挝将根据五项原则发展两国关系

1955-04-25
第4版()
专栏：

    范文同和老挝王国首相发表联合声明
    越南和老挝将根据五项原则发展两国关系
    新华社万隆二十三日电　越南民主共和国副总理兼外交部长范文同和老挝王国首相克特·萨索里斯二十三日发表了联合声明，全文如下：
    越南民主共和国副总理兼外交部长范文同阁下和老挝王国首相克特·萨索里斯阁下友好地举行了会谈，会谈时在场的有印度共和国总理贾瓦哈拉尔·尼赫鲁阁下和中华人民共和国总理周恩来阁下。
    他们取得协议，发表如下的声明：
    一、越南民主共和国政府认为，老挝王国政府和“寮国”根据日内瓦协定应当谋求的政治解决是一个内部秩序问题，老挝王国政府和“寮国”完全有自由以最好的方式按照这个国家和老挝人民的最高利益来解决这个问题。
    二、越南民主共和国政府和老挝王国政府将在一九五四年四月二十九日的中印协议所规定的五项原则的范围内发展和协调在这两个国家之间存在着和应当存在的睦邻关系。
            一九五五年四月二十三日于万隆
