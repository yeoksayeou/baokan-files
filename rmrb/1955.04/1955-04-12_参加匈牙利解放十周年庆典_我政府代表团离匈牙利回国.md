Title: Our Government Delegation Leaves Hungary for Home After Attending the 10th Anniversary Celebration of the Liberation of Hungary
Author:
Date: 1955-04-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, April 11th - The Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China, led by Deng Zihui, Vice Premier of the State Council, left Budapest by plane on the 10th to return home after attending the celebrations for the 10th anniversary of the liberation of Hungary.

Those who saw the delegation off at the airport included Antal Hegedüs, Acting Chairman of the Hungarian Council of Ministers, Antal Apró, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Rezső Nyers and János Matolcsi, Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, Foreign Minister János Boldoczki, Sándor Rónai, Speaker of the National Assembly, and Károly Pongrácz, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Budapest City Council, among others.

Hao Deqing, the Chinese Ambassador to Hungary, and the diplomatic envoys of the Soviet Union and other people's democratic countries to Hungary were also present at the airport to see them off.

### 参加匈牙利解放十周年庆典  我政府代表团离匈牙利回国

1955-04-12
第1版()
专栏：

    参加匈牙利解放十周年庆典
    我政府代表团离匈牙利回国
    新华社布达佩斯十一日电　参加匈牙利解放十周年庆祝典礼的中华人民共和国政府代表团，在团长、国务院副总理邓子恢的率领下，十日乘飞机离开布达佩斯回国。
    到机场欢送代表团的有匈牙利部长会议代理主席安·赫格居斯，部长会议副主席安·阿普罗，匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会书记处书记拉·阿奇和雅·马托尔奇，外交部长雅·波尔多奇基，国民议会议长桑·罗奈伊和布达佩斯市会议执行委员会主席卡·庞格拉茨等人。
    中国驻匈牙利大使郝德青和苏联及各人民民主国家驻匈牙利的外交使节也到机场送行。
