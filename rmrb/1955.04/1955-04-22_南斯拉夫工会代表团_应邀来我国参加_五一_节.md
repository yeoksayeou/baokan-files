Title: Yugoslav Trade Union Delegation Invited to China to Participate in "May Day"
Author:
Date: 1955-04-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 20th: According to a report by the Yugoslav News Agency, the Yugoslav trade union delegation, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to come to China to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations, will depart from Belgrade on the 20th and come to Beijing.

Draghi Stamenkovic, head of the Yugoslav trade union delegation, member of the Presidium of the Central Council of the Confederation of Trade Unions of Yugoslavia, and Chairman of the Trade Union Council of the Republic of Serbia, stated to the Yugoslav trade union newspaper "Rad": The working people of Yugoslavia have followed the Chinese people's struggle for liberation with concern. The delegation will do its best to understand the general situation of the working class in this distant country, the achievements of the working class, and the role of trade unions. Stamenkovic expressed that the visit of the Yugoslav trade union delegation to China will help promote a better understanding between the working classes and their organizations of the two countries.

### 南斯拉夫工会代表团  应邀来我国参加“五一”节

1955-04-22
第1版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫工会代表团
    应邀来我国参加“五一”节
    新华社二十日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社报道，应中华全国总工会邀请来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节观礼的南斯拉夫工会代表团将在二十日动身离开贝尔格莱德前来北京。
    南斯拉夫工会代表团团长、南斯拉夫工会联合会中央理事会主席团委员兼塞尔维亚共和国工会理事会主席德拉吉·斯塔门科维奇向南斯拉夫工会报纸“拉德报”发表声明说：南斯拉夫劳动人民曾以关切的心情注视中国人民的解放斗争。代表团将尽力去了解这个遥远的大国中的工人阶级的一般情况，工人阶级的成就以及工会的作用。斯塔门科维奇表示：南斯拉夫工会代表团来中国访问，将有助于促进两国工人阶级及其组织相互间更好的了解。
