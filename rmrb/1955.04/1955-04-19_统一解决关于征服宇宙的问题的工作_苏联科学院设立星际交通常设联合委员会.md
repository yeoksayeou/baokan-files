Title: Unified Work to Solve Problems Regarding the Conquest of the Universe: Soviet Academy of Sciences Establishes Permanent Joint Commission on Interstellar Communication
Author:
Date: 1955-04-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 17th (TASS dispatch): The Astrometry Committee of the Soviet Academy of Sciences has established a permanent joint commission on interstellar communication to unify the work of solving problems regarding the conquest of the universe. The commission is chaired by L. Sedov.

The scientific secretary of the Permanent Joint Commission on Interstellar Communication, A. Karpenko, told a TASS reporter: The problem of interplanetary travel is the most difficult problem that mankind must solve in the work of conquering nature, and it can only be solved with the active participation of many scientific, engineering, and technical institutions.

The progress of human knowledge in the past decade has created the premise that fantastic plans for navigating the universe can be developed into planned scientific research based on the consolidation of modern technological achievements. One of the initial tasks of the commission is to organize the establishment of an automatic scientific laboratory on an artificial satellite. Such a laboratory can orbit the Earth outside the atmosphere for a long period of time. In this laboratory, it will be possible to study phenomena that cannot be studied on Earth. Therefore, the automatic equipment of this laboratory will enable biologists to obtain materials on the conditions of life without geocentric gravity; astrophysicists will be able to study the ultraviolet and X-ray spectra of the sun and planets to obtain more materials on the changes occurring on these celestial bodies; and radiophysicists will be able to study the changes in the stratosphere more fully and determine the best way to establish radio communication with future spacecraft.

This cosmic laboratory will enable scientists to explore the secrets of the universe more deeply, and it will be the first step in solving the problem of interstellar communication.

### 统一解决关于征服宇宙的问题的工作  苏联科学院设立星际交通常设联合委员会

1955-04-19
第1版()
专栏：

    统一解决关于征服宇宙的问题的工作
    苏联科学院设立星际交通常设联合委员会
    新华社十八日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科十七日讯：苏联科学院天体测量委员会已经成立了一个星际交通常设联合委员会，统一解决关于征服宇宙的问题的工作。委员会由列·塞多夫任主席。
    星际交通常设联合委员会的科学秘书安·卡尔本科向塔斯社记者说：星球之间的交通问题是人类在征服自然的工作中必须解决的最困难的问题，只有在许多科学、工程和技术机构的积极参加下才能获得解决。
    人类的知识在最近十年中的进步已经创造了这样一个前提，使得在宇宙中航行的幻想性的计划，有可能发展到在现代技术成就的巩固基础上进行有计划的科学研究。委员会的最初步的工作之一就是组织在一个人造的卫星上建立一个自动科学实验室的工作。这样一个实验室可以在很长一段时期内在大气之外围绕地球运转。在这种实验室里将有可能研究那些在地球上无法研究的现象。因此，这种实验室的自动设备将使得生物学家能够获得关于在没有地心吸力的情况下生活的条件的材料；天体物理学家将能够研究太阳和星球的紫外线和爱克斯光线的光谱，以获得更多的关于在这些天体上发生的变化的材料；无线电物理学家将能够更加充分地研究同温层中的变化，并确定同将来的宇宙船只建立无线电联系的最好的办法。
    这种宇宙实验室将使科学家能够更深地探讨宇宙的秘密，而且它将是解决星际交通问题的第一个步骤。
