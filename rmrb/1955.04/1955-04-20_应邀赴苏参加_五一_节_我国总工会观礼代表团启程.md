Title: Invited to the Soviet Union to Participate in "May Day": Delegation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to Attend the Observance Sets Out
Author:
Date: 1955-04-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 18th - The delegation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, invited by the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions to visit the Soviet Union and attend the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations, departed from Beijing by plane on the morning of the 18th. The delegation consists of twelve members, led by Zhong Min, Executive Member of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and Chairman of the Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions, and deputy leader Du Daozhou, member of the Funding Review Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

### 应邀赴苏参加“五一”节  我国总工会观礼代表团启程

1955-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀赴苏参加“五一”节
    我国总工会观礼代表团启程
    新华社十八日讯　中华全国总工会应全苏工会中央理事会邀请赴苏联参加“五一”国际劳动节观礼代表团在十八日晨由北京乘飞机启程。代表团共十二人，团长为中华全国总工会执行委员，上海市工会联合会主席钟民，副团长为中华全国总工会经费审查委员会委员杜道周。
