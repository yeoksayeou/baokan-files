Title: Ambassador Huang Zhen Holds Reception in Jakarta for Premier Zhou Enlai's Visit to Indonesia
Author:
Date: 1955-04-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 28th. The Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Indonesia, Huang Zhen, held a reception in Jakarta at 8:00 PM on the 27th for Premier Zhou Enlai's visit to Indonesia at the invitation of the Indonesian government.

    Indonesian President Sukarno and Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo were invited to attend the reception.

    Also invited to attend the reception were: Indonesian Foreign Minister Sunarjo, Defense Minister Kusumasumantri, Economic Minister Roesino, Education and Culture Minister Muhammad Yamin, Information Minister Dubin, Transportation Minister Gani, Public Works Minister Muhammad Hasan, Labor Minister Abidin, Minister of National Welfare Abbas, Army Chief of Staff Bambang Sugan, Navy Chief of Staff Subijakto, Air Force Chief of Staff Suryadama, and Indonesian Ambassador to China Mononutu.

    Also invited were: Indonesian Parliament Speaker Sardjono and parliamentarians, leaders of various political parties and mass organizations, and prominent figures from all walks of life.

    Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Indonesia also attended the reception.

    At the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai raised his glass to wish President Sukarno good health. President Sukarno raised his glass to wish Chairman Mao Zedong good health.

    The reception was held in a friendly atmosphere.

### 为周恩来总理访问印度尼西亚  黄镇大使在雅加达举行招待会

1955-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    为周恩来总理访问印度尼西亚
    黄镇大使在雅加达举行招待会
    新华社雅加达二十八日电　中华人民共和国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇为周恩来总理应邀到印度尼西亚访问，在二十七日下午八时在雅加达举行招待会。
    印度尼西亚总统苏加诺和总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约应邀出席了招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的还有：印度尼西亚外交部长苏纳约、国防部长库素马素曼特里、经济部长罗西诺、文教部长穆罕默德·雅明、情报部长杜斌、交通部长干尼、公共工程部长穆罕默德·哈桑、劳工部长阿比丁、国家福利部长阿巴斯、陆军参谋长班邦·苏甘、海军参谋长苏比雅克托、空军参谋长苏尔亚达马和印度尼西亚驻中国大使莫诺努图。
    应邀出席的还有：印度尼西亚国会议长沙多诺和国会议员，各政党和群众团体的领袖以及各界著名人士。
    出席招待会的还有各国驻印度尼西亚的外交使节。
    在招待会上，周恩来总理举杯祝苏加诺总统健康。苏加诺总统举杯祝毛泽东主席健康。
    招待会在友好的气氛中进行。
