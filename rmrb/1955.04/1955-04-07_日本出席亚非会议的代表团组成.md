Title: Composition of the Japanese Delegation to the Asian-African Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-04-07
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 5th: The Japanese government has decided to send a delegation led by State Minister Takasaki Tatsunosuke to attend the Asian-African Conference. The Japanese delegation includes Masayuki Tani, advisor to the Japanese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Shunichi Kase, councillor of the Japanese Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
    The Japanese Diet has decided to send five Diet members, respectively elected by the Democratic Party, the Liberal Party, the Left-wing Socialist Party, the Right-wing Socialist Party, and Ryokufukai, to attend the Asian-African Conference with the Japanese delegation in an advisory capacity.

### 日本出席亚非会议的代表团组成

1955-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

    日本出席亚非会议的代表团组成
    新华社五日电　东京消息：日本政府已经决定由国务相高碕达之助率领日本代表团出席亚非会议。日本代表团包括日本外务省顾问谷正之、日本外务省参议加濑俊一。
    日本国会已经决定派遣由民主党、自由党、左派社会党、右派社会党、绿风会分别选出的五个国会议员、以顾问资格随同日本代表团出席亚非会议。
