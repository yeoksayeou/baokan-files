Title: Chinese Academy of Sciences Hosts Banquet to Welcome Soviet Academy of Sciences Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1955-04-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th: The Chinese Academy of Sciences held a banquet on the evening of the 27th to welcome the Soviet Academy of Sciences delegation, which had been invited to visit China.

All members of the Soviet Academy of Sciences delegation, headed by Academician I.P. Bardin, Vice President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, attended the banquet.

Also invited to the banquet were Academician Yudin, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to China, embassy staff, and many Soviet experts in Beijing.

Those attending the banquet included: Chen Boda, Zhang Jiafu, Zhu Kezhen, Wu Youxun, Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and heads of various departments; Zeng Zhaolun and Chen Kangbai, Vice Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies; Liang Xi, Chairman of the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization; and prominent figures from the capital's scientific community.

Also attending the banquet were: Lü Dong, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Heavy Industry of the People's Republic of China; Wang Daohan, Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building; Liu Lanbo, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Fuel Industry; He Changgong and Xu Jie, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Geology; and Zhou Jianren, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Higher Education.

Also attending the banquet were: Zhou Yang, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Liao Jingtian, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Ma Yinchu, President of Peking University, and other heads of universities.

At the banquet, Vice President Chen Boda and Academician Bardin both spoke.

During the banquet, the guests repeatedly raised their glasses to toast the close cooperation between Chinese and Soviet scientists, and to the increasingly consolidated and developed great friendship between China and the Soviet Union.

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 中国科学院设宴欢迎苏联科学院代表团

1955-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    中国科学院设宴欢迎苏联科学院代表团
    新华社二十七日讯　二十七日晚，中国科学院举行宴会，欢迎应邀来我国访问的苏联科学院代表团。
    以苏联科学院副院长伊·普·巴尔金院士为首的苏联科学院代表团全体人员都应邀出席了宴会。
    应邀出席宴会的还有：苏联驻我国特命全权大使尤金院士和使馆人员，以及在京的苏联专家多人。
      出席宴会的有：中国科学院副院长陈伯达、张稼夫、竺可桢、吴有训和各部门负责人，中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会副主席曾昭抡、陈康白，中华全国科学技术普及协会主席梁希，以及首都科学界著名人士。
    出席宴会的还有：中华人民共和国重工业部副部长吕东，第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵，燃料工业部副部长刘澜波，地质部副部长何长工、许杰，高等教育部副部长周建人。
    出席宴会的还有：中共中央宣传部副部长周扬，中苏友好协会总会副秘书长廖经天，以及北京大学校长马寅初和其他高等院校的负责人。
    宴会上，陈伯达副院长和巴尔金院士都讲了话。
    席间，宾主一再举杯，祝中苏两国科学家的亲密合作，祝中苏两国的伟大友谊日益巩固和发展。
    宴会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
