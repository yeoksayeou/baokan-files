Title: Albanian and Other Countries' Newspapers Welcome the Asian-African Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-04-15
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 13th – Albanian News Agency dispatch: The Albanian General Trade Union's central organ newspaper "Labor News" wrote on the 12th that the Asian-African Conference will be a new and great contribution to the realization of the hopes of the people of the countries participating in the conference. The newspaper emphasized that profound changes have already taken place in Asia and Africa, and the era of colonialist rule has passed. The newspaper pointed out that the people of Asia and Africa have enough strength to decide their own destiny and defend peace.
    Xinhua News Agency, 13th – Sydney dispatch: The Australian "Tribune" weekly published an editorial on April 5th, stating: "The Asian-African Conference to be held in Bandung, Indonesia this month is a historically significant event."
    The editorial stated that the conference will defend the peace of the vast Asian-African region, which has nearly two-thirds of the world's population, and will plan the next steps in the struggle against colonialism and racial discrimination, and for the economic and cultural rejuvenation of the Asian and African continents.
    The editorial pointed out that the decision to hold the Asian-African Conference has caused panic among the American warmongers. The editorial finally called on everyone to rise up and defeat America's war policy against Asians.

### 阿尔巴尼亚等国报纸欢迎举行亚非会议

1955-04-15
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚等国报纸欢迎举行亚非会议
    新华社布拉格十三日电　阿尔巴尼亚通讯社消息：阿尔巴尼亚总工会中央机关报“劳动报”十二日写道，亚非会议对于参加会议的国家的人民的希望的实现，将是新的、伟大的贡献。报纸强调说，目前亚洲和非洲已经发生了深刻的变革，殖民主义者的统治时代已经过去了。报纸指出，亚洲和非洲人民有足够的力量自己来决定自己的命运和保卫和平。
    新华社十三日讯　悉尼消息：四月五日一期的澳大利亚“论坛”周报发表社论说：“将于本月中在印度尼西亚万隆举行的亚非会议是一件具有历史意义的大事。”
    社论说，会议将捍卫拥有全球的将近三分之二人口的广阔亚非地区的和平，并将为今后反对殖民主义和种族歧视以及促进亚洲和非洲大陆的经济和文化复兴的斗争，规划下一步的步骤。
    社论指出：举行亚非会议的决定，已经在美国战争贩子中间引起了惊慌。社论最后号召大家起来击败美国的反对亚洲人的战争政策。
