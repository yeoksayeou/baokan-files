Title: US Christian Churches Write to Eisenhower, Requesting No Military Adventure in the Taiwan Area
Author:
Date: 1955-04-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, April 2nd (TASS): According to a United Press report, the National Council of Churches of Christ in the United States, with over 35 million members, has written a letter to Eisenhower requesting him to reject any proposals to use armed forces in the Taiwan area situation, because such a policy "could trigger World War III."

The committee requested further efforts to seek negotiations with China, and asked the President to "take all honorable measures to seek a peaceful resolution of the Asian crisis."

The committee requested the President to be "wary of the danger of taking unilateral action" on Far East issues.

### 美国基督教会写信给艾森豪威尔  要求不要在台湾地区进行军事冒险

1955-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

    美国基督教会写信给艾森豪威尔
    要求不要在台湾地区进行军事冒险
    新华社三日讯　据塔斯社纽约二日讯：据合众社报道，拥有三千五百万以上会员的美国基督教会全国委员会写了一封信给艾森豪威尔，要求他拒绝关于在台湾地区局势中使用武装力量的任何建议，因为这种政策“可能引起第三次世界大战”。
    委员会要求再作努力谋求同中国进行谈判，并且要求总统“采取一切体面的措施以求和平解决亚洲危机”。
    委员会要求总统在远东问题上“谨防采取片面行动的危险”。
