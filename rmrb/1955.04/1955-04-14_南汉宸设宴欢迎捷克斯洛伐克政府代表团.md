Title: Nan Hanchen Hosts Banquet to Welcome Czechoslovak Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-04-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 13th: Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 13th to welcome the Czechoslovak Republic Government Delegation, which was invited to China to attend the opening ceremony of the "Exhibition of Achievements of Ten Years of Socialist Construction in Czechoslovakia" held in Beijing.

Invited to the banquet were: Head of the Czechoslovak Republic Government Delegation and Deputy Prime Minister of the Czechoslovak Republic, Jankovcová; Deputy Head of the delegation and Minister of Metallurgy and Mining Industry of the Czechoslovak Republic, Reitmajer; Delegation members: Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Gregor; Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, Kozol; Deputy Minister of Machine Industry, Šmok; Academician of the Academy of Sciences, Ježek; Painter, Bělčík; Sculptor, Lauda, and officials from the Czechoslovak Embassy in China.

Present at the banquet were: Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Li Xiannian; Minister of Heavy Industry, Wang Heshou; Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ji Pengfei; Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Li Zheren; Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building, Wang Daohan, and other vice ministers from various ministries, as well as Vice Mayor of Beijing, Feng Jiping.

Also present at the banquet were: Vice Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Li Dequan; Chairman of the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization, Liang Xi; Vice Chairman of the Chinese Artists Association, Liu Kaiqu, and people from all walks of life in the capital.

More than 300 people attended the banquet.

At the banquet, Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, first delivered a speech, warmly welcoming all members of the Czechoslovak Republic Government Delegation headed by Chairman Jankovcová. He said, "The 'Exhibition of Achievements of Ten Years of Socialist Construction in Czechoslovakia' will open victoriously as scheduled on April 15th. This exhibition is the largest Czechoslovak Republic overseas exhibition, and undoubtedly we will learn a lot of industrialization experience from this exhibition, which will be of great help to our socialist construction."

Next, Chairman Jankovcová delivered a reply speech. She said that due to the friendly cooperation between us, we were able to organize this exhibition in your beautiful country. We sincerely hope that this exhibition will further consolidate the friendly relations between the people of Czechoslovakia and China and will help consolidate the cause of world peace.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests repeatedly raised their glasses to toast the great friendship between the people of China and Czechoslovakia.

The banquet was conducted in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

After the banquet, a dance party was held.

### 南汉宸设宴欢迎捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团

1955-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    南汉宸设宴欢迎捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团
    新华社十三日讯　十三日晚，中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸设宴欢迎应邀前来我国参加在北京举行的“捷克斯洛伐克十年社会主义建设成就展览会”开幕式的捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团。
    应邀出席宴会的有：捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团团长、捷克斯洛伐克共和国副总理扬科夫卓娃，副团长、捷克斯洛伐克共和国冶金工业与采矿部部长雷特马耶尔，团员：捷克斯洛伐克驻我国特命全权大使格里哥尔、对外贸易部副部长科佐尔、机器工业部副部长什莫克、科学院院士叶希基克、画家贝尔契、雕刻家劳达，以及捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使馆的官员。
    出席宴会的有：中华人民共和国国务院副总理李先念，重工业部部长王鹤寿，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，对外贸易部副部长李哲人，第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵，以及其他各部副部长，北京市副市长冯基平。
    出席宴会的还有：中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全，中华全国科学技术普及协会主席梁希，中国美术家协会副主席刘开渠以及首都各界人士等。
    出席宴会的共有三百多人。
    宴会上，中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸首先致词，对以扬科夫卓娃团长为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团全体人员表示热烈欢迎。他说，“捷克斯洛伐克十年社会主义建设成就展览会”将在四月十五日如期胜利开幕。这个展览会是捷克斯洛伐克共和国出国展览中规模最大的一个，无疑地我们将从这个展览会中学习到许多工业化的经验，对我们的社会主义建设事业会有很大的帮助。
    接着，扬科夫卓娃团长致答词。她说，由于我们之间友好合作的关系，使我们能够在你们美丽的国家里组织这一展览会。我们真诚地希望这次展览会能进一步地巩固捷中两国人民之间的友好关系，并能有助于巩固世界和平事业。
    席间，宾主一再举杯为中捷两国人民的伟大友谊干杯。
    宴会在热烈和友好的空气中进行。
    宴会后，举行了舞会。
