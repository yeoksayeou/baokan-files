Title: Vietnamese Ministry of Interior Issues Statement Condemning the Other Side for Forcing Residents to Move South
Author:
Date: 1955-04-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 27th - According to the Vietnam News Agency: The Ministry of Interior of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam issued a statement on the 25th, condemning the American imperialists, French colonialists, and the Ngo Dinh Diem clique for coercing and deceiving residents of the northern region of Vietnam into moving to South Vietnam.

The statement listed facts demonstrating that the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam has consistently guaranteed freedom of movement for residents. The statement condemned the other side for violating the Geneva Accords, engaging in deceptive propaganda, and even using violence to force residents of North Vietnam to move south. The statement said: Such incidents have occurred in the provinces of Bac Ninh, Bac Giang, Son Tay, Ha Dong, Hung Yen, Hai Duong, Quang Binh, Quang Tri, and the city of Hanoi, and now forced relocation of residents is also occurring in the Haiphong area. The statement pointed out: The other side's purpose in forcing residents to move south is to recruit more soldiers for the Ngo Dinh Diem army in preparation for launching new wars; and to forcibly recruit labor for southern plantations, attempting to hinder the economic recovery and increased production in the northern region of Vietnam. The statement emphasized that the conspiracy to force residents to move was directed and instigated by the American imperialists.

The statement stated: The Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam adheres to the firm policy of guaranteeing the freedom of movement for the people, and at the same time is determined to punish those who abuse and distort the freedom of movement to carry out forced evacuations and undermine peace and security in the North. The statement said that in order to expose the conspiracy of the American imperialists and their lackeys, it is necessary to expose their crimes and actions violating the Geneva Accords to the Vietnamese people and world public opinion, and called on all people to unite in struggle to smash their conspiracy.

### 越南内务部发表声明谴责对方强迫居民南迁

1955-04-28
第4版()
专栏：

    越南内务部发表声明谴责对方强迫居民南迁
    新华社河内二十七日电　据越南通讯社讯：越南民主共和国内务部二十五日发表声明，谴责美帝国主义者、法国殖民主义者和吴庭艳集团强迫和欺骗越南北部地区居民迁往南越。
    声明列举事实说明越南民主共和国政府一贯保证居民的迁移自由。声明谴责对方违反日内瓦协定，进行欺骗宣传，甚至通过暴力迫使北越居民南迁。声明说：在北宁、北江、山西、河东、兴安、海阳、广平、广治等省以及河内市都发生过这种事件，现在在海防地区也发生强迫居民迁移事件。声明指出：对方强迫居民南迁的目的是要为吴庭艳军队招募更多的士兵，以准备进行新的战争；并为南方种植园强迫征募劳动力，企图阻碍越南北部地区恢复经济、增加生产。声明强调指出，强迫居民迁移的阴谋是由美帝国主义者指使和策动的。
    声明表示：越南民主共和国政府奉行保证人民迁移自由的坚定政策，同时决心惩处那些滥用和曲解迁移自由以进行强迫撤退并破坏北部的和平及安全的人。声明说，为揭露美帝国主义者及其仆从的阴谋，有必要向越南人民和世界舆论揭发他们的罪行和违反日内瓦协定的行动，并号召全体人民在斗争中团结起来，粉碎他们的阴谋。
