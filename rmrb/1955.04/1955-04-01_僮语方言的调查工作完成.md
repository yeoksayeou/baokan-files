Title: Zhuang Language Dialect Survey Completed
Author:
Date: 1955-04-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency:
    The Zhuang Language Work Team of the Guangxi Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Region completed the survey of Zhuang language dialects for the creation of Zhuang writing at the end of February. More than thirty language workers are now conducting research to determine the basic Zhuang language dialect.
    For six months, the Zhuang Language Work Team, led by Professor Yuan Jiahua of the Institute of Linguistics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, surveyed Zhuang language dialects in forty-seven counties in the Zhuang area of Guangxi. In each county, they collected more than two thousand words, many short sentences, folk songs, and stories.

### 僮语方言的调查工作完成

1955-04-01
第3版()
专栏：

    僮语方言的调查工作完成
    桂西僮族自治区僮文工作队，在二月底已完成创造僮族文字的僮语方言调查工作。现在三十多个语文工作者正在进行整理研究，确定僮语基本方言。
    六个月来，僮文工作队在中国科学院语言研究所袁家骅教授等率领下，调查了广西僮族地区四十七个县的僮语方言。他们在每个县都搜集了两千多个单词、许多短句、民歌和故事。　（新华社）
