Title: German Tibetologist Schubert Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-04-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 5th—Professor Johannes Schubert, a Tibetan language expert from the German Democratic Republic, has arrived in Beijing on April 5th to conduct scientific research in China according to the Sino-German cultural cooperation agreement.
    Professor Schubert teaches Tibetan at Humboldt University in Berlin and Karl Marx University in Leipzig. He will stay in China for three months, engaging in scientific research related to the Tibetan language.

### 德藏文专家舒伯特到北京

1955-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    德藏文专家舒伯特到北京
    新华社五日讯　根据中德文化合作协定来我国进行科学研究的民主德国西藏文专家约翰尼斯·舒伯特教授已在四月五日抵达北京。
    舒伯特教授在柏林洪堡大学及来比锡卡尔·马克思大学担任西藏文的教授工作，他将在中国逗留三个月，从事有关西藏语文的科学研究工作。
