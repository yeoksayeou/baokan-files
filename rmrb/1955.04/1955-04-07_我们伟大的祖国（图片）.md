Author:
Date: 1955-04-07

    Our Great Motherland
    Zhong Chengming, a worker in the milling cutter and reamer workshop of the Harbin Measuring & Cutting Tool Factory, was transferred from the army last July. He diligently studied his profession and quickly mastered the operating techniques of the grinder, and was therefore named a model producer in the workshop. This year, he completed his first quarter production plan more than twenty days ahead of schedule.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    哈尔滨量具刃具厂铣刀铰刀车间工人钟成明，是去年七月从部队转业的。他刻苦钻研业务，很快就掌握了磨床的操作技术，因而被评为车间模范生产者。今年他提前二十多天完成了第一季度的生产计划。
