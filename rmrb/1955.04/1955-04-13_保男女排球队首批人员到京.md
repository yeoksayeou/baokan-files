Title: First Group of Bulgarian Men's and Women's Volleyball Teams Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-04-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 12th. The first group of twenty members of the Bulgarian National Men's and Women's Volleyball Mixed Team, who have been invited to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 12th, led by Deputy Head of Delegation G. Georgiev.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included leading members of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Military Sports Department of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, and the Beijing Physical Culture and Sports Commission, as well as representatives of our athletes, totaling more than 50 people.

Personnel from the Bulgarian Embassy in China also went to the airport to welcome them.

The second group of eleven members of the Bulgarian National Men's and Women's Volleyball Mixed Team, led by Head of Delegation I. Nikolov, will also arrive in Beijing in the coming days.

### 保男女排球队首批人员到京

1955-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    保男女排球队首批人员到京
    新华社十二日讯　应邀来我国访问的保加利亚国家男、女排球混合队首批人员一行二十人在副领队格·格奥尔基耶夫率领下，已在十二日下午乘机抵达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有中华人民共和国体育运动委员会、中国人民解放军总政治部文化部、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会军事体育部和北京市体育运动委员会等单位的负责人，以及我国运动员代表等共五十多人。
    保加利亚驻我国大使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
    保加利亚国家男、女排球混合队第二批人员十一人在领队伊·尼科洛夫率领下，也将在日内到京。
