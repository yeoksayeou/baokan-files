Title: Czechoslovak State Song and Dance Ensemble Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-04-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, April 19th. The Czechoslovak State Song and Dance Ensemble, a group of more than 180 people, arrived in Beijing by special train on the afternoon of the 19th, led by the head of the ensemble, Pavel Dubovsky, Deputy Minister of Culture of the Slovak Administrative Committee, at the invitation of the Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

Those who went to the station to welcome them included Zheng Zhenduo, Deputy Minister of Culture of the People's Republic of China, Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, Feng Jiaping, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and many people from the capital's cultural and artistic circles. Šmíd, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, and embassy staff also went to the station to welcome them.

### 捷克斯洛伐克国家歌舞团到达北京

1955-04-20
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克国家歌舞团到达北京
    新华社十九日讯　根据中捷文化合作协定，应邀来我国访问的捷克斯洛伐克国家歌舞团一行一百八十多人，在歌舞团团长、斯洛伐克行政委员会文化部副部长帕维尔·杜博夫斯基率领下，十九日下午乘专车到达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有中华人民共和国文化部副部长郑振铎，对外文化联络局局长洪深，北京市副市长冯基平和首都文化艺术界人士多人。捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使馆临时代办石密茨和使馆人员也曾到车站欢迎。
