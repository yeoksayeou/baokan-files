Title: Zhang Guohua Holds Celebration Banquet and Receives Chinese and Indian Personnel Involved in the Handover
Author:
Date: 1955-04-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, April 1st. General Zhang Guohua, Acting Representative of the Government of the People's Republic of China in Tibet, held a banquet on the afternoon of the 1st to celebrate the smooth completion of the work of the Indian government in handing over the postal, telegraph, and relay station services originally operated by them in the Tibet region of China to the Chinese government, and to entertain officials from both China and India who participated in the handover work.

Invited to attend the banquet were: Mr. Menon, Consul General of the Republic of India in Lhasa, Vice Consul Gyatso, and Indian officials involved in the inventory, such as Chaiber.

Invited to attend the banquet were: Yang Gongsu, Assistant for Foreign Affairs of the Representative of the Government of the People's Republic of China in Tibet, and Chinese personnel involved in the takeover work, including Hou Jie, Chen Yang, and Ding Jia.

Invited to attend the banquet were: Langdun Gongga Wangqug, the outgoing Siji (official) of the Tibetan local government; Galun (Cabinet Minister) Dorga Puncog Raojie; Assistant Galun Xiasu Jumo Ciren Dorje; Acting Galun Gazhang Losang Renzeng; Acting Galun Zhaxilinba Qinrao Wangqug; outgoing Mu Galun Ranba Tubdan Gonqing; Ze Cang Da Secretary, Great Lama Ronglangse Tubdan Losang; and Dele Raodeng, Deputy Director of the Panchen Kanpo Lija Committee.

Responsible cadres of the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army were present as hosts.

A total of more than fifty people attended the banquet.

During the banquet, General Zhang Guohua delivered a speech. After expressing his gratitude to the handover staff from both sides, he said: "The smooth completion of this handover not only demonstrates the earnest implementation of the agreement signed by the Chinese and Indian governments in Beijing on April 29, 1954, but also demonstrates that the governments and peoples of China and India, in the spirit of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence advocated by Premier Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister Nehru, have developed the existing friendly relations between China and India."

During the banquet, Mr. Menon, Consul General of the Republic of India in Lhasa, also made a brief speech. He said: The friendship between China and India has been uninterrupted since ancient times, and this handover of postal, telegraph, and relay station services will further promote the exchange and development of politics, economy, and culture between the two countries. The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere, and the hosts and guests frequently toasted to the friendship between China and India and to peace in Asia and the world.

### 张国华举行庆祝宴会并招待中印接交人员

1955-04-02
第1版()
专栏：

    张国华举行庆祝宴会并招待中印接交人员
    新华社拉萨一日电　代理中华人民共和国政府驻西藏代表张国华将军在一日下午举行宴会，庆祝印度政府将其原在中国西藏地方所经营的邮电、驿站交给我国政府的工作顺利完成，并招待中印双方参加交接工作的官员。
    应邀出席宴会的有：印度共和国驻拉萨总领事梅农、副领事嘉错和印度方面参加清点工作的官员蔡伯尔等。
    应邀出席宴会的有：中华人民共和国政府驻西藏代表外事帮办杨公素、我方参加接管工作的人员侯杰、晨阳、丁甲等。
    应邀出席宴会的有：西藏地方政府卸任司候朗顿·滚噶旺曲、噶伦朵噶·彭错饶杰、助理噶伦夏苏·举墨策仁多吉、代理噶伦噶章·罗桑仁增、代理噶伦札西林巴·钦饶旺曲、卸任母噶伦然巴·图丹滚清、泽仓大秘书大喇嘛绒朗色·土登洛桑和班禅堪布会议厅委员会副主任委员德勒饶登等。
    出席作陪的有：中国人民解放军西藏军区负责干部。
    参加宴会的共五十多人。
    席间，张国华将军致词。他在向双方交接工作人员致感谢之意后说：“这次接交工作的顺利完成，不仅说明了中印两国政府切实地执行了一九五四年四月二十九日在北京签订的协定，也表现了中印两国政府和人民本着周恩来总理和尼赫鲁总理所倡导的和平共处五项原则精神，发展了中印两国已有的友好关系。”
    宴会进行中，印度共和国驻拉萨总领事梅农也作了简短讲话。他说：中印两国友谊自古以来未间断过，这次邮电、驿站接交，会更促进两国政治、经济、文化的交流和发展。宴会在友好气氛中进行，宾主频频为中印两国友谊，为亚洲和世界和平干杯。
