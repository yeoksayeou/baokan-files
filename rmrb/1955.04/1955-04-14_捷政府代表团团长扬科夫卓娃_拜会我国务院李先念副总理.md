Title: Czechoslovak Government Delegation Head Jankovcova Visits Chinese State Council Vice Premier Li Xiannian
Author:
Date: 1955-04-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 13th:
    Jankovcova, head of the Czechoslovak Republic Government Delegation and Vice Premier of the Czechoslovak Republic, visited Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, on the afternoon of the 13th. Accompanying her were: Retmajer, Deputy Head of the Delegation and Minister of Metallurgical Industry and Mining of the Czechoslovak Republic; members: Grigor, Czechoslovak Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China; Kozor, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade; Šmok, Deputy Minister of Machine Industry; Academician Ješík; Painter Belč; and Sculptor Lauda.
    Also present at the meeting were: Wang Heshou, Minister of Heavy Industry of the People's Republic of China; Li Zheren, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade; Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Minister of Culture; and He Wei, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs.

### 捷政府代表团团长扬科夫卓娃  拜会我国务院李先念副总理

1955-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    捷政府代表团团长扬科夫卓娃
    拜会我国务院李先念副总理
    新华社十三日讯　捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团团长、捷克斯洛伐克共和国副总理扬科夫卓娃，在十三日下午拜会了中华人民共和国国务院副总理李先念。随同前往拜会的有：代表团副团长、捷克斯洛伐克共和国冶金工业与采矿部部长雷特马耶尔，团员：捷克斯洛伐克驻我国特命全权大使格里哥尔、对外贸易部副部长科佐尔、机器工业部副部长什莫克、科学院院士叶希基克、画家贝尔契、雕刻家劳达。
    拜会时在座的有：中华人民共和国重工业部部长王鹤寿，对外贸易部副部长李哲人，文化部副部长张致祥，外交部部长助理何伟。
