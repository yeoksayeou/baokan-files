Title: Burmese Delegation Arrives in Delhi, India
Author:
Date: 1955-04-09
Page: 1
　
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 8th. The Burmese delegation to the Asian Countries Conference, led by lawyer U Hla Kyaing, arrived in Delhi on the 7th.

### 缅甸代表团到达印度德里

1955-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸代表团到达印度德里
    新华社新德里八日电　出席亚洲国家会议的缅甸代表团由律师吴拉叫率领，七日到达德里。
