Title: Picture
Author: Liu Qi
Date: 1955-04-21
Page: 2

    Young people and children in various regions are actively responding to the call of the Ministry of Education of the People's Republic of China and the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League to carry out planting activities, vigorously launching a tree planting and afforestation campaign. This is a group of students from Fangjiaxiang Elementary School in Nanjing planting trees in the Yuhuatai afforestation area.

　　　Photo by Liu Qi (Manuscript from Nanjing Xinhua Daily)
