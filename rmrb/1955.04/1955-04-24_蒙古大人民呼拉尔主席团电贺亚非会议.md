Title: Presidium of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia Sends Congratulatory Telegram to the Asian-African Conference
Author: 
Date: 1955-04-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, April 21st. The Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, Chairman Z. Sambu, sent a telegram to the Asian-African Conference to express congratulations. The text of the telegram is as follows:

	The Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, on behalf of the peace-loving Mongolian people, extends the warmest congratulations to the participants of the Asian-African Conference.

	The Mongolian people are deeply convinced that the conference of Asian and African countries will undoubtedly promote the relaxation of international tensions, consolidate universal peace and security among nations, and help the people of Asian and African countries defend the cause of freedom and national independence.

	I wish the participants of the Asian-African Conference success in the struggle to consolidate international peace.

### 蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团电贺亚非会议

1955-04-24
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团电贺亚非会议
    新华社二十一日讯　蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席扎·桑布打电报给亚非会议表示祝贺。电文如下：
    蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团代表爱好和平的蒙古人民谨向亚非会议的参加者致以最热烈的祝贺。
    蒙古人民深信：亚非国家的会议，毫无疑问的将会促进国际紧张局势的缓和，巩固国际间的普遍和平与安全，并将有助于亚非各国人民保卫自由与民族独立的事业。
    谨祝亚非会议的参加者在为巩固国际和平而进行的斗争中获得成就。
