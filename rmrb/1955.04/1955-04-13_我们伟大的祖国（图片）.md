Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-04-13
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The Nanqiao Agricultural Production Cooperative in Taishan County, Guangdong Province, is actively engaged in spring plowing and production. This year, the planned yield for double-cropping rice fields is 1,000 catties per mu. The transplanting of rice seedlings has begun on over 1,000 mu of land. The picture shows cooperative members transplanting seedlings using the close planting method.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    广东省台山县南乔农业生产合作社积极进行春耕生产。今年计划双造田每亩产量为一千斤，现在一千多亩水稻已开始插秧。这是社员们采用小科密植法插秧的情形。
