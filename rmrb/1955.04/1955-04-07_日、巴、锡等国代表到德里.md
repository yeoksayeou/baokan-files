Title: Representatives from Japan, Pakistan, Ceylon, and Other Countries Arrive in Delhi
Author:
Date: 1955-04-07
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 6th: The head of the Japanese delegation to the Asian Countries Conference, Matsumoto Jiichiro, a member of the House of Councillors, and his party of twenty-two people arrived in Delhi on the 6th. They were welcomed at the airport by representatives of the International Preparatory Secretariat of the Asian Countries Conference and members of the Reception Committee. Prior to this, fourteen Japanese representatives had already arrived in Delhi on the 5th.
    Also arriving in Delhi on the 6th were the Pakistani representative, Bashani, Chairman of the East Pakistan People's Muslim League, and some members of the Ceylon delegation.

### 日、巴、锡等国代表到德里

1955-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

    日、巴、锡等国代表到德里
    新华社新德里六日电　出席亚洲国家会议的日本代表团团长、参议院议员松本治一郎和团员一行二十二人在六日到达德里。他们在机场受到亚洲国家会议国际筹备秘书处代表和招待委员会委员的欢迎。在这以前，已有十四个日本代表在五日到达德里。
    在六日到达德里的还有巴基斯坦代表、东巴基斯坦人民回教联盟主席巴沙尼和锡兰代表团部分团员。
