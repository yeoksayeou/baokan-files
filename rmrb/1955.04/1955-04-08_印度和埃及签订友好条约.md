Title: India and Egypt Sign Treaty of Friendship

Author:
Date: 1955-04-08
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 6th, TASS dispatch: India and Egypt signed a treaty of friendship in Cairo on April 6th. The treaty stipulates the consolidation of friendly and peaceful cooperation between the two countries and further expansion of cultural exchanges between India and Egypt.
    The treaty also stipulates that India and Egypt will cooperate in industry, agriculture, trade and navigation.

### 印度和埃及签订友好条约

1955-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    印度和埃及签订友好条约
    新华社七日讯　塔斯社开罗六日讯：印度和埃及两国四月六日在开罗签订了友好条约。条约规定巩固两国间的友好与和平合作，进一步扩大印度和埃及的文化交流。
  条约还规定，印度和埃及将在工业、农业、贸易和航海方面进行合作。
