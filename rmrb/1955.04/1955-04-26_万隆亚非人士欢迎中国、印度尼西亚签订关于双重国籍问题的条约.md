Title: Bandung Afro-Asian Figures Welcome the Treaty on Dual Nationality Signed by China and Indonesia
Author: 
Date: 1955-04-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: According to a report by the Indian Press Trust, Afro-Asian figures in Bandung welcomed the treaty on the issue of dual nationality signed by China and Indonesia on the 22nd, considering it a symbol of the Asian solidarity that China and Indonesia have consistently strived for. The news stated that it is generally considered a symbol of resolving all disputes through peaceful negotiations.

### 万隆亚非人士欢迎中国、印度尼西亚签订关于双重国籍问题的条约

1955-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    万隆亚非人士欢迎中国、印度尼西亚签订关于双重国籍问题的条约
    新华社二十二日讯　据印度报业托辣斯报道，万隆的亚非人士对中国和印度尼西亚二十二日签订关于双重国籍问题的条约表示欢迎，认为这是中国和印度尼西亚一贯争取的亚洲团结的一个征象。消息说，一般都认为这是通过和平谈判解决一切争端的一个征象。
