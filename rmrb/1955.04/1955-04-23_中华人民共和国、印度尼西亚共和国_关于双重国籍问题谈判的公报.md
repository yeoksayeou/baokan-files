Title: Communiqué of the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia on Negotiations Regarding the Issue of Dual Nationality
Author:
Date: 1955-04-23
Page: 1

The governments of the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia, in order to reasonably resolve the issue of dual nationality, held preliminary negotiations in Beijing from November 2, 1954, to December 23, 1954. Based on the agreement reached in Beijing, the two governments continued negotiations in Jakarta and Bandung from March 29, 1955, to April 20, 1955.

During the negotiations, both sides, based on the principles of equality, mutual benefit, and non-interference in internal affairs, conducted full consultations on the basis of friendly cooperation and unanimously agreed to conclude the "Treaty between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia on the Issue of Dual Nationality."

The aforementioned treaty was signed in Bandung on April 22, 1955, by the plenipotentiary representatives of the two governments. Signing on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China was Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China. Signing on behalf of the Government of the Republic of Indonesia was Sunario, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Indonesia. The aforementioned treaty shall enter into force after ratification by both countries and the exchange of instruments of ratification in Beijing. The negotiations were conducted in a harmonious atmosphere from beginning to end.

### 中华人民共和国、印度尼西亚共和国  关于双重国籍问题谈判的公报

1955-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国、印度尼西亚共和国
    关于双重国籍问题谈判的公报
    中华人民共和国政府和印度尼西亚共和国政府为了合理解决双重国籍问题，曾于一九五四年十一月二日至一九五四年十二月二十三日在北京举行初步谈判，两国政府并以在北京达成的协议为基础，于一九五五年三月二十九日至一九五五年四月二十日在雅加达和万隆继续进行谈判。
    双方在谈判中根据平等、互利和不干涉内政的原则，在友好合作的基础上，进行了充分的协商，并一致同意缔结“中华人民共和国和印度尼西亚共和国关于双重国籍问题的条约”。
    上述条约已在一九五五年四月二十二日在万隆由两国政府的全权代表签字。代表中华人民共和国政府签字的是中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来。代表印度尼西亚共和国政府签字的是印度尼西亚共和国外交部长苏纳约。上述条约在两国批准并在北京互交批准书后生效。谈判自始至终在融洽的气氛中进行。
