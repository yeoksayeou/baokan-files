Title: Soviet Newspapers Publish China's Foreign Ministry Statement
Author:
Date: 1955-04-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 14th. "Pravda," "Izvestia," "Trud," "Krasnaya Zvezda," "Moskovskaya Pravda," and other newspapers on April 14th published the statement of our Foreign Ministry regarding the destruction of the plane carrying our delegation members and Chinese and foreign journalists by American-Chiang Kai-shek special agents. The newspapers also carried news of Indian Prime Minister Nehru expressing his sorrow and condolences over the matter. "Pravda" and "Komsomolskaya Pravda" also carried excerpts from the People's Daily editorial regarding the plane's destruction.

### 苏联报纸刊登我国外交部声明

1955-04-15
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联报纸刊登我国外交部声明
    新华社莫斯科十四日电　“真理报”、“消息报”、“劳动报”、“红星报”、“莫斯科真理报”和其他报纸十四日刊载我外交部关于我代表团人员和中外记者座机遭受美蒋特务破坏失事的声明。报纸还刊载印度尼赫鲁总理对此事表示悲痛和慰问的消息，“真理报”、“共青团真理报”还刊载了人民日报关于飞机遭受破坏失事的社论摘要。
