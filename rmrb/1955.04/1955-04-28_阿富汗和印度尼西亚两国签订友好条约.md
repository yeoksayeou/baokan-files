Title: Afghanistan and Indonesia Sign Treaty of Friendship

Author:

Date: 1955-04-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 26th - According to the Indonesian News Agency, Afghanistan and Indonesia signed a treaty of friendship between the Kingdom of Afghanistan and the Republic of Indonesia in Bandung on the 24th.

    Signing on behalf of the Kingdom of Afghanistan was Naim Khan, head of the Afghan delegation to the Asian-African Conference, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of Afghanistan. Signing on behalf of Indonesia was Indonesian Foreign Minister Sunaryo.

### 阿富汗和印度尼西亚两国签订友好条约

1955-04-28
第4版()
专栏：

    阿富汗和印度尼西亚两国签订友好条约
    新华社雅加达二十六日电　据印度尼西亚新闻社报道，阿富汗和印度尼西亚二十四日在万隆签订了阿富汗王国和印度尼西亚共和国之间的友好条约。
    代表阿富汗王国签字的是阿富汗出席亚非会议代表团团长、阿富汗副首相兼外交大臣纳伊姆汗，代表印度尼西亚签字的是印度尼西亚外交部长苏纳约。
