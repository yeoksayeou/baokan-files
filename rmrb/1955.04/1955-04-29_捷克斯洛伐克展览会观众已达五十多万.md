Title: Czechoslovak Exhibition Attracts Over 500,000 Visitors
Author:
Date: 1955-04-29
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 28th – As of April 28th, the Czechoslovak Exhibition on Achievements in Ten Years of Socialist Construction has attracted over 520,000 visitors. The visitors include workers, farmers, students, engineering technicians, and government cadres. On the 28th, over 40 members of the Zhangguozhuang Agricultural Production Cooperative in Fengtai District, Beijing, which has established friendship ties with the Vinarsice village of the Prague region in Czechoslovakia, visited the exhibition. Pok, the director of the Czechoslovak Exhibition on Achievements in Ten Years of Socialist Construction, and the staff warmly explained agricultural machinery and other exhibits to the cooperative members, and they exchanged commemorative badges.
    Xinhua News Agency, April 27th – The Czechoslovak Exhibition on Achievements in Ten Years of Socialist Construction has extensively carried out experience exchange activities. As of the 26th, it has resolved 60 written questions raised by relevant units in Beijing through on-site Q&A, seminars, and factory guidance. On the 23rd, the Beijing Municipal Bureau of Building Materials Industry invited relevant Czechoslovak timber technicians to a seminar to discuss experiences in saving wood. During their visits to timber factories such as Guanghua, Czechoslovak technicians made many suggestions for improving work. On the 26th, Czechoslovak technicians were invited to the Tianjin No. 2 Rubber Factory to help solve the maintenance and usage problems of a three-roll calender. Czechoslovak experts and technicians have also drawn up a plan to give more than 30 academic and technical reports to people from all walks of life in the capital.
    Since the launch of experience exchange, this exhibition has screened 14 documentary films related to machine manufacturing and distributed over 20,000 technical documents. (Attached photo)
    Comrade Mao Zedong and other leaders of the Chinese Communist Party visited the Czechoslovak Exhibition on Achievements in Ten Years of Socialist Construction in Beijing on April 28. The photo shows Comrade Mao Zedong (second from the right in the front row) visiting the exhibits in the industrial hall, accompanied by Jankovcova, the head of the Czechoslovak government delegation (first from the right in the front row).
              Xinhua News Agency reporter Qi Guanshan

### 捷克斯洛伐克展览会观众已达五十多万

1955-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克展览会观众已达五十多万
    新华社二十八日讯　到二十八日止，参观捷克斯洛伐克十年社会主义建设成就展览会的观众已达五十二万多人。参观的群众中有工人、农民、学生、工程技术人员和机关干部。二十八日，与捷克斯洛伐克布拉格州维纳尔日采村建立了友谊的北京市丰台区张郭庄农业生产合作社社员四十多人前往参观，捷克斯洛伐克十年社会主义建设成就展览会主任波克和工作人员热情地为社员们讲解农业机器和其他展品，他们并互送了纪念章。
    新华社二十七日讯　捷克斯洛伐克十年社会主义建设成就展览会广泛开展经验交流活动，到二十六日止，已通过现场解答、座谈和下厂指导等方式解决了北京市各有关单位以六十份书面提出的问题。二十三日，北京市建筑材料工业局邀请捷克斯洛伐克有关木材技术人员座谈节约木材的经验。捷克斯洛伐克技术人员在参观光华等木材厂过程中向各厂提出了许多改进工作的建议。二十六日，捷克斯洛伐克技术人员应邀去天津市第二橡胶厂，帮助解决三滚筒压延机的维修和使用问题。捷克斯洛伐克专家和技术人员并已订出计划，准备向首都各界作三十多个学术和技术性的报告。
    这个展览会自开展经验交流以来，已放映十四场有关机器制造等纪录影片，并分发了两万多份技术资料。（附图片）
    中国共产党领导者毛泽东同志等四月二十八日在北京参观捷克斯洛伐克十年社会主义建设成就展览会。图为毛泽东同志（前排右起第二人）在捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团团长扬科夫卓娃（前排右起第一人）陪同下，参观工业馆大厅展出的展品。
              新华社记者　齐观山摄
