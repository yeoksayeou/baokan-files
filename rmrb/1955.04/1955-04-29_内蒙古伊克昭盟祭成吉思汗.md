Title: Eke Zhao League in Inner Mongolia Celebrates Genghis Khan
Author:
Date: 1955-04-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency:
    Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Eke Zhao League, held a grand ceremony to celebrate Genghis Khan in Ejin Horo from April 13th to 20th. More than 200 herdsmen participated in the grand ceremony, which was presided over by Ma Fugang (Mongolian), Deputy Leader of the Eke Zhao League, and others. After the grand ceremony, material exchange activities and folk sports activities such as horse racing and wrestling were held.

### 内蒙古伊克昭盟祭成吉思汗

1955-04-29
第2版()
专栏：

    内蒙古伊克昭盟祭成吉思汗
    内蒙古自治区伊克昭盟四月十三日到二十日，在伊金霍洛举行祭奠成吉思汗大会。参加大祭的牧民有二百多人，由伊克昭盟副盟长马富纲（蒙族）等主祭。大祭仪式结束后，举行了物资交流活动；和赛马、摔跤等民间体育活动。　　　　　　（新华社）
