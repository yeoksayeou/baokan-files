Title: Czech and Bulgarian Volleyball Teams Leave Beijing for Shanghai Visit; Czech and Bulgarian Volleyball Teams Play Friendly Matches with Central Institute of Physical Education Volleyball Teams in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-04-19
Page: 2

The entire personnel of the Czechoslovak Army Central House Men's Volleyball Team and the Bulgarian National Mixed Men's and Women's Volleyball Team, who were invited to visit our country, left Beijing by train on the evening of April 14 and arrived in Shanghai on the afternoon of the 16th.

Accompanying them were the men's and women's volleyball teams from the Competitive Sports Guidance Department of the Central Institute of Physical Education and the "August 1st" men's and women's volleyball teams of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Before leaving Beijing, the Czechoslovak Army Central House Men's Volleyball Team and the Beijing Men's Volleyball Joint Team held a friendly volleyball match on April 8.

On April 10, the Czechoslovak Army Central House Men's Volleyball Team played its third friendly match in the capital. The team participating in the match was the men's volleyball team of the Competitive Sports Guidance Department of the Central Institute of Physical Education.

The opening ceremony of the performance matches of the Bulgarian National Mixed Men's and Women's Volleyball Team in our country was held on April 14 at the Xian Nong Tan Stadium in the capital. Members of various countries' embassies in China attended the opening ceremony. After the opening ceremony, the Bulgarian National Mixed Men's and Women's Volleyball Team played two friendly volleyball matches with the men's and women's volleyball teams of the Competitive Sports Guidance Department of the Central Institute of Physical Education, respectively. The matches attracted many sports enthusiasts in the capital. The Czechoslovak Army Central House Men's Volleyball Team also came to watch the matches. The two teams' wonderful performances often won warm applause and cheers from the audience. The Bulgarian team won both matches. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 捷、保排球队离京去上海访问  捷、保排球队在京同中央体育学院排球队作了友谊比赛

1955-04-19
第2版()
专栏：

    捷、保排球队离京去上海访问
    捷、保排球队在京同中央体育学院排球队作了友谊比赛
    应邀来我国访问的捷克斯洛伐克军队中央之家男子排球队和保加利亚国家男、女排球混合队全体人员已在四月十四日晚乘车离开北京，于十六日下午到上海。
    陪同前往的还有中央体育学院竞技指导科男、女排球队和中国人民解放军“八一”男、女排球队。
    在离开北京以前，捷克斯洛伐克军队中央之家男子排球队和北京市男子排球联队于四月八日举行了一场排球友谊赛。
    四月十日，捷克斯洛伐克军队中央之家男子排球队在首都作了第三次友谊比赛。参加比赛的是中央体育学院竞技指导科男子排球队。
    保加利亚国家男、女排球混合队在我国的表演比赛，于四月十四日在首都先农坛体育场举行开幕式。各国驻我国使馆人员出席了开幕式。开幕式后，保加利亚国家男、女排球混合队分别和中央体育学院竞技指导科男、女排球队进行了两场排球友谊赛。比赛吸引了首都许多体育运动的爱好者。捷克斯洛伐克军队中央之家男子排球队也到场观看比赛。两队的精采表演，常常博得观众的热烈掌声和欢呼声。两场比赛结果都是保加利亚队获胜。（据新华社讯）
