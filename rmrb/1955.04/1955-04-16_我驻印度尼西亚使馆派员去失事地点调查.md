Title: Our Embassy in Indonesia Sends Personnel to Investigate the Site of the Incident
Author:
Date: 1955-04-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 15th -- Our embassy in Indonesia has dispatched officials Li Xiaocun and Hu Xuntao to the sea crash site of the "Kashmir Princess" aircraft, which was sabotaged by US-Chiang Kai-shek secret agents, to conduct an investigation. The Indonesian government's investigation team has also departed for the location. The investigation team consists of officials from the Indonesian Civil Aviation Authority and Air Force officials. The military attache of the Indian Embassy in Indonesia is accompanying them.
According to an announcement by the Indonesian Navy authorities on the 14th, another six victims' bodies have been recovered from the wreckage of the "Kashmir Princess" aircraft, including two personnel from Air India International. Including those previously found, a total of nine victims' bodies have been found. Indonesian naval vessels are still conducting deep-sea salvage operations.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 15th -- Indian Minister of Transport Jagjivan Ram said in the People's House on the 14th that a thorough investigation would be conducted immediately into the crash of the Air India International aircraft in the South China Sea. Two senior officials from the Indian Civil Aviation Bureau are scheduled to travel to Singapore on the 16th to investigate the aircraft crash.

### 我驻印度尼西亚使馆派员去失事地点调查

1955-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我驻印度尼西亚使馆派员去失事地点调查
    新华社雅加达十五日电　我国驻印度尼西亚使馆已派黎笑村和胡训陶两位官员前往被美蒋特务破坏失事的“克什米尔公主号”飞机坠海地点进行调查。印度尼西亚政府派出的调查小组也已动身前往。调查小组由印度尼西亚民航机构官员和空军官员等组成。印度驻印度尼西亚大使馆陆军武官随同前往，据印度尼西亚海军当局十四日宣布，已从
  “克什米尔公主号”飞机残骸中找出另外六位遇难者的尸体，其中两人是印度国际航空公司的人员。连同以前发现的，已经一共找到了九位遇难者的尸体。印度尼西亚海军舰只仍在进行深水打捞工作。
    新华社新德里十五日电　印度交通部长贾格克凡·拉姆十四日在人民院说，将立即对印度国际航空公司飞机在南中国海坠毁事件进行彻底调查。印度民航局的两名高级官员定于十六日前往新加坡调查飞机坠毁事件。
