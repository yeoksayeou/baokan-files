Title: Trade Union Delegations from the Soviet Union and Other Countries Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-04-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 27th. At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Soviet trade union delegation, which is visiting China to attend the "May Day" celebrations and for a visit, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 27th. The delegation is headed by P.N. Korobova, Secretary of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions. The members include I. Saripov, Chairman of the Kazakh Republic Trade Union Federation; N.N. Motova, Secretary of the Moscow Oblast Trade Union Federation; N.M. Kuzmin, lathe operator of the Moscow "Red Proletarian Factory", Deputy to the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Republic, Stalin Prize winner; Nu. Maksumova, female weaver of the Tashkent Stalin Textile Factory; and V.P. Sakovich, staff member of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions.

Also arriving by plane on the same afternoon, at the invitation to attend the "May Day" celebrations and for a visit, were the All-German Trade Union delegation headed by Erich Glückauf, the Bulgarian trade union delegation headed by Plahov, and the Mexican Confederation of Workers and Peasants delegation headed by Otda Guelelo.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Liu Ningyi and Zhu Xuefan, Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Li Jiebo, Li Zaiwen, and Dong Xin, Secretaries of the Secretariat, and the heads of the International Liaison Department and various industrial trade unions of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

### 苏联等国工会代表团到京

1955-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联等国工会代表团到京
    新华社二十七日讯　应中华全国总工会的邀请，来我国参加“五一”节观礼和访问的苏联工会代表团在二十七日下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团由全苏工会中央理事会书记普·尼·柯罗鲍娃任团长，团员有哈萨克共和国工会联合会主席伊·萨里波夫，莫斯科州工会联合会书记纳·尼·莫托娃，莫斯科“红色无产者工厂”镟工、俄罗斯共和国最高苏维埃代表、斯大林奖金获得者尼·米·库兹明，塔什干斯大林纺织工厂女织工努·马克苏莫娃和全苏工会中央理事会工作人员维·彼·萨科维奇。
    同日下午乘飞机到达的，还有应邀来我国参加“五一”节观礼和访问的以爱利希·格吕茨纳为首的全德国工会代表团、以普拉霍夫为首的保加利亚工会代表团和以渥达·圭莱洛为首的墨西哥工农联合总会代表团。
    到机场欢迎的有：中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一、朱学范，书记处书记李颉伯、栗再温、董昕，中华全国总工会国际联络部和各产业工会的负责人。
