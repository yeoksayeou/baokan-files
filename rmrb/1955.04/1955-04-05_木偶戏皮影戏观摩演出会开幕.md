Title: Puppet Show and Shadow Play Performance and Exhibition Opens

Author: 
Date: 1955-04-05
Page: 3
    
    The Puppet Show and Shadow Play Performance and Exhibition, sponsored by the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, opened in Beijing on the evening of April 1st.
    Attending the opening ceremony were literary and art workers from the capital and puppet show and shadow play actors from various places who came to participate in the performances. The entire troupe of the Czechoslovakian Lipele Puppet Theater, which arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of April 1st, and personnel from the Czechoslovakian Embassy in China were also invited to attend the opening ceremony.
    Zheng Zhenduo, Deputy Minister of Culture, said at the opening ceremony: Puppet shows and shadow plays have a long history of development in China and are an indispensable part of our country's precious national artistic heritage. This distinctive and infectious dramatic art is well-loved and heard by the broad masses of the people. He hoped that through this performance, they could observe and learn from each other, exchange experiences, and create more favorable conditions for the development of puppet shows and shadow plays. Zheng Zhenduo expressed a warm welcome to the Czechoslovakian Lipele Puppet Theater for visiting China.
    At the opening ceremony, Pelipi, the head of the Czechoslovakian Lipele Puppet Theater, also spoke.
    Participating in this performance and exhibition are representative puppet show and shadow play troupes from twelve provinces: Fujian, Zhejiang, Guangdong, Hunan, Shandong, Shanxi, Shaanxi, Jiangsu, Hebei, Qinghai, Heilongjiang, and Sichuan. There are more than 180 actors participating in the performances. In addition to historical dramas, their performances include fairy tales and modern dramas.
    During the performance period, the Ministry of Culture, the Chinese Dramatists Association, and the Chinese Artists Association will jointly hold a puppet show and shadow play exhibition. Chinese actors will watch the performances of the Czechoslovakian Lipele Puppet Theater. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 木偶戏皮影戏观摩演出会开幕

1955-04-05
第3版()
专栏：

    木偶戏皮影戏观摩演出会开幕
    中华人民共和国文化部主办的木偶戏、皮影戏观摩演出会四月一日晚在北京开幕。
    参加开幕式的有首都的文艺工作者和各地前来参加演出的木偶戏、皮影戏的演员们。四月一日下午到达北京的捷克斯洛伐克利培勒兹木偶剧团全体人员和捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使馆人员也应邀参加了开幕式。
    文化部副部长郑振铎在开幕式上讲话说：木偶戏、皮影戏在我国有很长的发展历史，是我国珍贵的民族艺术遗产中不可忽视的一部分。这一富有特点并具有感染力的戏剧艺术为广大人民群众所喜见乐闻。他希望能通过这次演出，相互观摩，交流经验，为木偶戏、皮影戏的发展创造更有利的条件。郑振铎对捷克斯洛伐克利培勒兹木偶剧团前来我国访问，表示热烈的欢迎。
    在开幕式上，捷克斯洛伐克利培勒兹木偶剧团团长裴利皮也讲了话。
    参加这次演出会的有福建、浙江、广东、湖南、山东、山西、陕西、江苏、河北、青海、黑龙江、四川十二个省具有代表性的木偶戏、皮影戏剧团，参加演出的演员代表共一百八十多人。他们演出的节目中除历史剧外，还有童话剧和现代剧。
    在演出期间，文化部、中国戏剧家协会、中国美术家协会将联合举办木偶戏、皮影戏展览会，我国的演员们将观摩捷克斯洛伐克利培勒兹木偶剧团的演出。　（新华社）
