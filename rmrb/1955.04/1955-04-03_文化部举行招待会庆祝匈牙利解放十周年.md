Title: Ministry of Culture Holds Reception to Celebrate the Tenth Anniversary of Hungary's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1955-04-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd. The Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China held a film reception at the Capital Cinema on the evening of April 2nd to celebrate the tenth anniversary of Hungary's liberation.

    The Hungarian film "Fourteen Lives Were Saved" was screened at the reception.

    Hungarian Ambassador to China Skala and embassy staff, as well as other foreign envoys and diplomatic officials in China, were invited to attend the reception.

    Those attending the reception included Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Minister of Culture of the People's Republic of China, Xu Xuehan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Zeng Zhaolun, Vice Minister of Higher Education, He Wei, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Liaison, and Wang Lanxi, Deputy Director of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture.

    Before the film screening, Vice Minister Zheng Zhenduo and Ambassador Skala both delivered speeches.

### 文化部举行招待会庆祝匈牙利解放十周年

1955-04-03
第1版()
专栏：

    文化部举行招待会庆祝匈牙利解放十周年
    新华社二日电　中华人民共和国文化部二日晚上在首都电影院举行电影招待会，庆祝匈牙利解放十周年。
    会上放映了匈牙利影片“十四条生命被拯救了”。
    匈牙利驻我国大使斯克拉丹和使馆人员，以及其他各国驻华使节和外交官员都应邀出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的有：中华人民共和国文化部副部长郑振铎，对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒，高等教育部副部长曾昭抡，外交部部长助理何伟，对外文化联络局局长洪深，文化部电影事业管理局副局长王阑西等。
    影片放映前，郑振铎副部长和斯克拉丹大使都讲了话。
