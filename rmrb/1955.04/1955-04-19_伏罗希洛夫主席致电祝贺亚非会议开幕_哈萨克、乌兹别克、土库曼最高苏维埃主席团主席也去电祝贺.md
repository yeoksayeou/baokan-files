Title: Voroshilov Sends Telegram to Congratulate the Opening of the Asian-African Conference. Chairmen of the Supreme Soviets of Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, and Turkmenistan Also Sent Telegrams to Congratulate.
Author:
Date: 1955-04-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 18th, Moscow Dispatch from TASS: K.Y. Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, sent a telegram to the Presidium of the Asian-African Conference held in Bandung to express congratulations. The telegram reads as follows: I would like to express my congratulations to the participants of the Asian-African Conference which opened today. I sincerely hope that your work will be fruitful and successful in accomplishing the lofty tasks before the conference.

Xinhua News Agency, April 18th, Moscow Dispatch from TASS, April 17th: Sh. Rashidov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Kazakh Soviet Socialist Republic, and A. Sariyev, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Turkmen Soviet Socialist Republic, sent telegrams to the Presidium of the Asian-African Conference to express congratulations.

The congratulatory telegram from the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Kazakh Soviet Socialist Republic stated that the Kazakh people firmly believe that all countries in Asia and Africa will establish close cooperation and achieve complete national independence despite the conspiracies of the imperialists.

Rashidov's congratulatory telegram expressed deep conviction that the decisions of the conference will promote friendly cooperation between Asian and African countries, consolidate the freedom and national independence of all peoples, ease international tensions and consolidate world peace.

Sariyev's congratulatory telegram stated that the peace-loving Turkmen people, who had personally suffered from heavy colonial oppression in the past, hoped that the Bandung Conference would play a historically significant role in the cause of national development of Asian and African countries.

These congratulatory telegrams all wished the Asian-African Conference success.

### 伏罗希洛夫主席致电祝贺亚非会议开幕  哈萨克、乌兹别克、土库曼最高苏维埃主席团主席也去电祝贺

1955-04-19
第1版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫主席致电祝贺亚非会议开幕
    哈萨克、乌兹别克、土库曼最高苏维埃主席团主席也去电祝贺
    新华社十八日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟最高苏维埃主席团主席克·叶·伏罗希洛夫打电报给在万隆举行的亚非会议的主席团表示祝贺。电文如下：我谨向今天开幕的亚非会议的参加者表示祝贺。我真诚地希望在完成摆在会议面前的崇高任务中，工作富有成果并取得胜利。
    新华社十八日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科十七日讯：哈萨克苏维埃社会主义共和国最高苏维埃主席团、乌兹别克苏维埃社会主义共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席夏·拉希多夫和土库曼苏维埃社会主义共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席阿·萨里耶夫分别打电报给亚非会议主席团表示祝贺。
    哈萨克苏维埃社会主义共和国最高苏维埃主席团的贺电说，哈萨克人民坚信，亚非两洲所有的国家将不顾帝国主义者的阴谋，建立起密切的合作，并取得完全的民族独立。
    拉希多夫的贺电表示深信，会议的各项决定将促进亚非国家之间的友好合作，巩固各国人民的自由和民族独立，缓和国际紧张局势和巩固世界和平。
    萨里耶夫的贺电说，过去曾亲身受到沉重的殖民压迫的、爱好和平的土库曼人民，希望万隆会议在亚非国家民族发展的事业中发挥具有历史意义的作用。
    这些贺电都祝亚非会议成功。
