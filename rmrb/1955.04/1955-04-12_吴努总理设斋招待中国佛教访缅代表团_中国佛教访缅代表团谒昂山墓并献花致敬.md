Title: Prime Minister U Nu Hosts a Feast for the Chinese Buddhist Delegation to Burma; The Chinese Buddhist Delegation to Burma Visits Aung San's Tomb and Presents Flowers in Tribute
Author:
Date: 1955-04-12
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 11th, Rangoon Dispatch: Burmese Union Prime Minister U Nu and his wife hosted a vegetarian feast at their official residence on April 9th for the Chinese Buddhist Delegation to Burma. Accompanying the delegation were U Than Swe, Parliamentary Secretary of the Ministry of Religious Affairs of the Burmese Union Government, U Ba Rye, Religious Affairs Officer, Thakin Lu Min, Director of the Religious Guidance Department, and others. Yao Zhongming, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, and embassy staff were also invited to attend the reception.
    Prime Minister U Nu discussed the Buddhist situation in China and Burma with the head of the delegation, Xiraojiacuo, Secretary-General Zhao Puchu, and others. The delegation presented Prime Minister U Nu with gifts such as Chinese Buddhist pictures, prayer beads, and cloisonné vases.
    Xinhua News Agency, April 11th, Rangoon Dispatch: The Chinese Buddhist Delegation to Burma visited Aung San's Tomb on April 8th and presented flowers in tribute.
    On the same day, the Chinese Buddhist Delegation to Burma paid homage to the Shwedagon Pagoda in Rangoon.

### 吴努总理设斋招待中国佛教访缅代表团  中国佛教访缅代表团谒昂山墓并献花致敬

1955-04-12
第1版()
专栏：

    吴努总理设斋招待中国佛教访缅代表团
    中国佛教访缅代表团谒昂山墓并献花致敬
    新华社十一日讯　仰光消息：缅甸联邦总理吴努夫妇四月九日在官邸设斋招待中国佛教访缅代表团。陪同代表团前往的有缅甸联邦政府宗教事务部国会秘书吴丹盛、宗教事务官吴巴瑞礼、宗教指导处长德钦卢敏等。中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和使馆人员也应邀出席了招待会。
    吴努总理同代表团团长喜饶嘉错、秘书长赵朴初等交谈了中缅两国佛教的情况。代表团将中国佛教图片、佛珠和景泰蓝瓶等礼物赠送给吴努总理。
    新华社十一日讯　仰光消息：中国佛教访缅代表团四月八日谒昂山墓并献花致敬。
    同一天，中国佛教访缅代表团朝拜了仰光大金塔。
