    Our Great Motherland
    Our country's seven Nationalities Institutes have achieved great success in training cadres from minority nationalities. More than 7,600 graduates have participated in various aspects of the motherland's construction. This is a scene of Dai student Yu Jianxiang (left) and Lisu student Dong Er'mei reviewing their lessons together after class at the Yunnan Nationalities Institute.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-04-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国七个民族学院培养少数民族干部的工作有很大成绩。七千六百多个毕业学生已参加祖国各方面的建设。这是云南民族学院的傣族学员玉间香（左）同傈僳族学员栋二妹一起在课后复习功课的情形。
