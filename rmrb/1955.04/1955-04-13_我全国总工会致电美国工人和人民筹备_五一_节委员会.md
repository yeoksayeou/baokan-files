Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends a Message to the U.S. Workers and People's "May Day" Festival Preparatory Committee
Author:
Date: 1955-04-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency report: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions cabled the American 1955 Workers and People's "May Day" Festival Preparatory Committee in New York on the 7th. The cable stated: "We extend our fraternal greetings to the American workers and working people preparing to celebrate 'May Day.' This great festival will be a day to demonstrate the will and strength of American workers and working people in striving for peace and democracy, demanding improved living standards, and promoting domestic and international labor solidarity. We wish that the friendship between the working classes of China and the United States, working for peace, will grow stronger every day."

Note: The American 1955 Workers and People's "May Day" Festival Preparatory Committee is composed of union workers from the AFL, CIO, and independent unions, as well as representatives from social groups such as youth, women, Black people, peace, and culture. The committee is preparing to hold a peaceful demonstration in New York City this "May Day" to express the American people's desire to strive for peaceful coexistence among all nations and to ease international tensions, to abolish all McCarthyist persecutory legislation, to implement a genuine anti-economic depression program, and to strive for civil rights.

### 我全国总工会致电美国工人和人民筹备“五一”节委员会

1955-04-13
第4版()
专栏：

    我全国总工会致电美国工人和人民筹备“五一”节委员会
    新华社讯　中华全国总工会七日致电纽约美国一九五五年工人和人民筹备“五一”节委员会。电文说：谨向筹备庆祝“五一”节的美国工人和劳动人民致以兄弟的敬意。这个伟大的节日将是表现出美国工人和劳动人民争取和平、民主，要求改善生活及促进国内国际劳工团结的意志和力量的日子。祝中美两国工人阶级为和平而努力的友谊与日俱增。
    按：美国一九五五年工人和人民筹备“五一”节委员会是由劳联、产联和独立工会的工会工作者以及青年、妇女、黑人、和平、文化等社会团体的代表组成的。委员会正筹备在今年“五一”节在纽约市举行和平示威，表示美国人民争取各国和平共处和缓和国际紧张局势、争取废止一切麦卡锡主义的迫害性立法、争取实施真正的反经济萧条纲领、争取公民权利的愿望。
