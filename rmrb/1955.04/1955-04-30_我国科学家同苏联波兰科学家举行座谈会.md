Title: Chinese Scientists Hold Symposiums with Soviet and Polish Scientists
Author:
Date: 1955-04-30
Page: 1

Chinese Scientists Hold Symposiums with Soviet and Polish Scientists

    This newspaper reports: The Chinese Academy of Sciences held a symposium on April 28 to receive the delegation from the Soviet Academy of Sciences. Present were Academician Bardin, head of the delegation of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, and all members of the delegation, as well as Chen Boda, Zhang Jiafu, Zhu Kezhen, Wu Youxun, vice presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and more than 30 well-known scientists in Beijing, including Pan Zinian, Yan Jici, Xu Jie, and Yang Zhongjian.

    The meeting was presided over by Chen Boda. Zhang Jiafu expressed his gratitude for the great help the Soviet Academy of Sciences had given to China's scientific work in recent years and hoped that the delegation's visit would further promote scientific cooperation between China and the Soviet Union and the development of China's scientific undertakings. Subsequently, the guests raised questions regarding the cooperation between the Academy of Sciences and industrial departments, comprehensive survey work, the training of scientific cadres, scientific popularization work, and scientific publishing work, which were answered by the scientists present. The meeting went through four hours of enthusiastic conversation.

    On April 29, Bardin, head of the delegation of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, and members Kostenko and Tananayev visited the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant; Betehtin, Gerasimov, and Belousov visited the Institute of Geology of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Voronin and Tcherkashen visited the Institute of Psychology of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Mishustin visited the Central Institute of Biological Products of the Ministry of Health; and Astafiev visited the Beijing Library, and also participated in a historical symposium sponsored by the Institute of History of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

    This newspaper reports: The Chinese Academy of Sciences held two symposiums on April 26 and 29 to receive the delegation from the Polish Academy of Sciences. The meetings were presided over by Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. Present were Academician Wierzbicki, Vice President of the Polish Academy of Sciences and head of the delegation of the Polish Academy of Sciences, and all members of the delegation. Chinese scientists attending the symposium included Zhu Kezhen, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Yan Jici, Director of the Department of Technical Sciences of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Pan Zinian, Deputy Director of the Department of Social Sciences, and Xu Jie, Deputy Director of the Department of Biology and Earth Sciences.

    At the first meeting, Wu Heng, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, expressed his gratitude for the help the Polish Academy of Sciences had given to China's scientific work in recent years, and hoped that the delegation of the Polish Academy of Sciences would offer valuable opinions on China's scientific work during this visit. Subsequently, the guests raised many important questions regarding scientific work, which were answered by the Chinese scientists present. At the second symposium, Wierzbicki introduced the organizational structure and work of the Polish Academy of Sciences and expressed the hope to further strengthen the cooperative relationship between the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Polish Academy of Sciences. Wierzbicki presented a batch of books published by the Polish Academy of Sciences to the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

    On April 27, the delegation of the Polish Academy of Sciences visited Peking University; on the 28th, they visited Tsinghua University; on the same day, member Kuratowski also visited the Institute of Mathematics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and gave a report on "Continuous Functions on the Complex Field." On the 29th, the head of the delegation, Wierzbicki, gave a report to the Chinese Society of Civil Engineering on the "Semi-Probabilistic Method of Studying Building Safety," which aroused great interest among the audience.

### 我国科学家同苏联波兰科学家举行座谈会

1955-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我国科学家同苏联波兰科学家举行座谈会
    本报讯　中国科学院在四月二十八日举行座谈会，招待苏联科学院代表团。到会的有苏联科学院代表团团长巴尔金院士和全体团员，中国科学院副院长陈伯达、张稼夫、竺可桢、吴有训，在京著名科学家潘梓年、严济慈、许杰、杨锺健、赵九章等三十多人。
    会议由陈伯达主持。张稼夫对苏联科学院几年来给我国科学工作的巨大帮助表示感谢，并希望通过这次代表团的访问，进一步促进中苏两国科学的合作和中国科学事业的发展。接着，贵宾们提出了有关科学院和产业部门的合作、综合考察工作、培养科学干部、科学普及工作及科学出版工作等问题，都分别由到会的科学家们作了答复。会议经过了四小时热烈的交谈。
      四月二十九日，苏联科学院代表团团长巴尔金、团员科斯琴科、塔纳纳耶夫等参观了石景山钢铁厂；别捷赫金、格拉西莫夫、别洛乌索夫等访问了中国科学院地质研究所，沃罗宁、契尔卡申访问了中国科学院心理研究室；米舒斯金参观了卫生部中央生物制品研究所；阿斯塔菲耶夫参观了北京图书馆等处，还参加了中国科学院历史研究所主办的历史座谈会。
    本报讯　中国科学院在四月二十六日和二十九日举行了两次座谈会招待波兰科学院代表团。会议由中国科学院副院长吴有训主持。到会的有波兰科学院副院长、波兰科学院代表团团长维日比茨基院士和全体团员，出席座谈会的中国科学家有中国科学院副院长竺可桢、中国科学院技术科学部主任严济慈、社会科学部副主任潘梓年、生物地学部副主任许杰等。
    在第一次会上，中国科学院副秘书长武衡对波兰科学院几年来给我国科学工作的帮助表示感谢，并希望波兰科学院代表团在这次访问中，能对我国科学工作多多提出宝贵的意见。接着贵宾们提出了许多有关科学工作的重要问题，都分别由到会的我国科学家们作了答复。在第二次座谈会上，维日比茨基介绍了波兰科学院的组织机构和工作情况，并且提出了进一步加强中国科学院和波兰科学院之间的合作关系的愿望。维日比茨基把波兰科学院出版的一批图书送给了中国科学院。
    四月二十七日，波兰科学院代表团访问了北京大学；二十八日又访问了清华大学；同日，团员库拉托夫斯基还访问了中国科学院数学研究所，并作关于“复数域上的连续函数”的报告。二十九日，团长维日比茨基向我国土木工程学会作了关于“研究房屋安全的半或然率方法”的报告，引起了到会听众的极大兴趣。
