Title: Pham Van Dong Arrives in Bandung and Issues Statement at Jakarta Airport
Author:
Date: 1955-04-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Bandung, April 20th: Pham Van Dong, Head of the Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Asian-African Conference and Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, arrived in Bandung by plane from Jakarta on the morning of the 20th.

At the airport to welcome him were Indonesian Foreign Minister Sunarjo, Minister of Transportation Gani, members of the Chinese delegation to the Asian-African Conference Zhang Hanfu and Huang Zhen, and Secretary-General Wang Zhuoru.

Deputy Prime Minister Pham Van Dong flew from Yangon to Jakarta on the 19th. Upon arrival, he issued a statement at the airport, saying: "I would like to extend my warmest respects to the government and people of Indonesia. I am convinced that through the efforts of all the delegates, the Asian-African Conference will be a success and will contribute to the peaceful coexistence of the people of Asian and African countries, to the maintenance and consolidation of peace in Asia and Africa, and to the strengthening of friendship and economic and cultural ties between Asian and African countries."

### 范文同到达万隆并在雅加达机场发表声明

1955-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    范文同到达万隆并在雅加达机场发表声明
    新华社万隆二十日电　越南民主共和国出席亚非会议代表团团长、越南民主共和国副总理兼外交部长范文同于二十日上午从雅加达乘飞机到达万隆。
    在机场欢迎的有印度尼西亚外交部长苏纳约、交通部长干尼，出席亚非会议的中国代表团团员章汉夫、黄镇及秘书长王倬如等。
    范文同副总理在十九日从仰光乘飞机到雅加达。抵达时在机场发表声明说：“我愿意向印度尼西亚政府和人民致最热烈的敬意。我深信在各位代表的努力下，亚非会议将获得成功，并将对亚非各国人民的和平共处、对亚洲和非洲和平的维持和巩固、对亚非国家间的友谊以及经济和文化联系的加强有所贡献。”
