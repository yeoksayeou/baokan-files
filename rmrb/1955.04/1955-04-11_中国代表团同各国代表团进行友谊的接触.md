Title: Chinese Delegation Conducts Friendly Contacts with Delegations from Various Countries
Author: 
Date: 1955-04-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 10th: The Chinese delegation attending the Asian Countries Conference engaged in friendly contacts with delegations from various countries attending the conference.

    On April 9th, Chinese representatives met with the Japanese delegation at a luncheon. They mutually expressed the friendship between the Chinese and Japanese people.

    The Chinese representatives also met with representatives from Burma, Ceylon, Pakistan, and Nepal, as well as representatives from Egypt, Jordan, Lebanon, and Syria.

    Prior to this, the Chinese delegation gathered with the Indian delegation at a luncheon on the 8th. Guo Moruo, head of the Chinese delegation, and Mrs. Rameshwari Nehru, head of the Indian delegation, exchanged sincere congratulations.

### 中国代表团同各国代表团进行友谊的接触

1955-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

    中国代表团同各国代表团进行友谊的接触
    新华社新德里十日电　出席亚洲国家会议的中国代表团在会议期间和出席会议的各国代表团进行了友谊的接触。
    四月九日，中国代表们在午餐会上会见了日本代表团。他们相互表达了中日两国人民的友情。
    中国代表们也会见了缅甸、锡兰、巴基斯坦和尼泊尔的代表，以及埃及、约旦、黎巴嫩和叙利亚等国的代表。
    在这以前，中国代表团在八日同印度代表团在午餐会上聚会。中国代表团团长郭沫若和印度代表团团长拉米希瓦里·尼赫鲁夫人互致诚挚的祝贺。
