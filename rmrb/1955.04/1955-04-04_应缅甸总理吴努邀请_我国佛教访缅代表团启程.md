Title: At the Invitation of Burmese Prime Minister U Nu, Chinese Buddhist Delegation Departs for Burma

Author:

Date: 1955-04-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd News: The Chinese Buddhist Delegation, at the invitation of Burmese Prime Minister U Nu, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 2nd for a visit to Burma.

The delegation members include: Head of the Delegation, Xiraojiacuo (Tibetan, Acting President of the Buddhist Association of China, Geshe); Secretary-General, Zhao Puchu (Han Chinese, Vice President and Secretary-General of the Buddhist Association of China, Layman); Delegation Members, Galahzang (Mongolian, Vice President of the Buddhist Association of China, Living Buddha); Weifang (Han Chinese, Director of the Buddhist Association of China, Abbot of the Jade Buddha Temple in Shanghai, Dharma Master); Yifang (Han Chinese, Director of the Buddhist Association of China, Abbot of the Dongya Temple on Mount Jiuhua in Anhui, Dharma Master); Jigang (Han Chinese, Abbot of the Falun Temple in Nanchang, Jiangxi, Dharma Master).

Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Wang Feng, Sa Kongliao, and Yang Jingren, Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China; He Chengxiang, Director of the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council; Tang Haiguang, Special Commissioner of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; directors of the Buddhist Association of China in Beijing; and heads of units such as the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Dalai Lama's Office in Beijing, and the Panchen Erdeni's Office in Beijing, as well as representatives of monks, nuns, and lamas in Beijing, and many others.

Burmese Ambassador to China U Myint Maung and First Secretary of the Embassy U Maung Maung Toe also went to the airport to see them off.

### 应缅甸总理吴努邀请  我国佛教访缅代表团启程

1955-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    应缅甸总理吴努邀请
    我国佛教访缅代表团启程
    新华社二日讯　中国佛教访缅代表团应缅甸总理吴努邀请，在二日上午乘飞机离开北京前往缅甸访问。
    代表团组成人员包括：团长喜饶嘉错（藏族、中国佛教协会代理会长、格西），秘书长赵朴初（汉族、中国佛教协会副会长兼秘书长、居士），团员噶喇藏（蒙族、中国佛教协会副会长、活佛），苇舫（汉族、中国佛教协会理事、上海玉佛寺住持、法师）、义方（汉族、中国佛教协会理事、安徽九华山东崖寺住持、法师）、济广（汉族、江西南昌法轮庵住持、法师）。
    前往机场欢送的有：中华人民共和国民族事务委员会副主任汪锋、萨空了、杨静仁，国务院宗教事务局局长何成湘，外交部亚洲司专员唐海光，中国佛教协会在京理事和中国人民对外文化协会、达赖喇嘛驻京办事处、班禅额尔德尼驻京办事处等单位负责人以及北京市僧、尼、喇嘛代表等多人。
    缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂和使馆一等秘书吴茂茂铎等也前往机场欢送。
