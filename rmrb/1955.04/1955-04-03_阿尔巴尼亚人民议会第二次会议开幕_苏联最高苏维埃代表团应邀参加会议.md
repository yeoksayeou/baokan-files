Title: Second Session of the Albanian People's Assembly Opens; Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation Invited to Attend

Author: 
Date: 1955-04-03
Page: 4

    Albanian People's Assembly Second Session Opens
    Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation Invited to Attend
    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, April 2nd (TASS dispatch from Tirana, April 1st): The second session of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania opened in Tirana on March 31st .
    The First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labour Party, Hoxha, the Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Shehu, the Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly, Lechi, and other leaders of the Labour Party and the government attended the meeting.
    The delegation of the Soviet Supreme Soviet, headed by Tarasov, was invited to attend the meeting. The agenda of the meeting included: the foreign policy of the Albanian People's Republic, the 1955 national budget, etc. Xinhua News Agency, April 1st: According to the Albanian Telegraph Agency, Tirana: The delegation of the Soviet Supreme Soviet, headed by Tarasov, arrived in Tirana by plane on March 31st at the invitation of the Presidium of the Albanian People's Assembly. Greeting the delegation at the airport were: the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labour Party, Enver Hoxha, the Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly, Haxhi Lleshi, the Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Mehmet Shehu, and other leaders of the Albanian Labour Party and the state. Diplomatic envoys of the Soviet Union and various people's democratic countries to Albania also greeted the delegation at the airport.

### 阿尔巴尼亚人民议会第二次会议开幕  苏联最高苏维埃代表团应邀参加会议

1955-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚人民议会第二次会议开幕
    苏联最高苏维埃代表团应邀参加会议
    新华社二日讯　塔斯社地拉那一日讯：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会第二次会议于三月三十一日在地拉那开幕。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查，部长会议主席谢胡，人民议会主席团主席列希，劳动党和政府的其他领导人参加了会议。
    以塔拉索夫为首的苏联最高苏维埃代表团应邀参加了会议。会议议程包括：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府的外交政策、一九五五年国家预算等。新华社一日讯　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社地拉那讯：以塔拉索夫为首的苏联最高苏维埃代表团，应阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团的邀请，在三月三十一日乘飞机到达地拉那。到机场欢迎代表团的有：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记恩·霍查，人民议会主席团主席哈·列希，部长会议主席穆·谢胡和阿尔巴尼亚劳动党及国家的其他领导人。苏联和各人民民主国家驻阿尔巴尼亚的外交使节也到机场欢迎。
