Title: Burmese President Hosts Banquet for Delegations from China, India, and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1955-04-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 16th: The President of the Union of Burma, Dr. Ba U, hosted a banquet at 8 PM on the 15th to welcome the Premier of the State Council of the People’s Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, Indian Prime Minister Nehru, Egyptian Prime Minister Nasser, Afghan Deputy Prime Minister Naim Khan, and the Vice Premier of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Pham Van Dong, along with the principal members of their respective delegations, who were en route to the Asian-African Conference in Indonesia and passing through Rangoon.

    Those attending and accompanying them included the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, Foreign Minister Sao Khun Hkio, and ministers from various departments of the Burmese government. The Chinese and Indian Ambassadors to Burma were also invited to attend the banquet.

    The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 缅甸总统设宴招待中印等国代表团

1955-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸总统设宴招待中印等国代表团
    新华社仰光十六日电　缅甸联邦总统巴宇博士在十五日晚八时设宴招待赴印度尼西亚出席亚非会议路经仰光的中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来、副总理陈毅、印度总理尼赫鲁、埃及总理纳赛尔、阿富汗副首相纳伊姆汗、越南民主共和国副总理范文同以及各代表团的主要团员。
    出席作陪的有缅甸联邦总理吴努、外交部长藻昆卓及缅甸联邦政府各部部长。中国和印度驻缅甸大使也应邀出席了宴会。
    宴会在友好空气中进行。
