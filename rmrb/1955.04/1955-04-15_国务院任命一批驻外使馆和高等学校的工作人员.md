Title: State Council Appoints Personnel to Foreign Embassies and Universities
Author:
Date: 1955-04-15
Page: 2
    The State Council of the People's Republic of China issued an order on April 6, appointing a number of personnel to foreign embassies and universities. The list is as follows:
    Zhou Qiuye appointed as Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the People's Republic of Yugoslavia
    Long Qian appointed as Vice President of Zhongshan University
    Wang Weiqi appointed as Vice President of Northwestern Polytechnical University
    Sun Zhongde appointed as President of Shanghai Second Medical College
    Yang Mingxian appointed as Vice President of Hunan Medical College
    Zhu Liangfu appointed as Vice President of Kunming Institute of Technology

### 国务院任命一批驻外使馆和高等学校的工作人员

1955-04-15
第2版()
专栏：

    国务院任命一批驻外使馆和高等学校的工作人员
    中华人民共和国国务院四月六日发布命令，任命了一批驻外使馆和高等学校工作人员，名单如下：
    周秋野为中华人民共和国驻南斯拉夫人民共和国大使馆参赞
    龙潜为中山大学副校长
    王维祺为西北工学院副院长
    孙仲德为上海第二医学院院长
    杨明显为湖南医学院副院长
    竺良甫为昆明工学院副院长
