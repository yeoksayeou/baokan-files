Title: Korean Workers' Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session
Author:
Date: 1955-04-06
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 5th. "Rodong Sinmun" published a communiqué from the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party on the 5th, stating that the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party held a plenary session for four days, starting on April 1st.

    The plenary session discussed the following issues:

    I. On further strengthening the class education of party members;

    II. On overcoming the bureaucratism that still exists among some staff members in party and state organs;

    III. On practicing strict economy, strengthening discipline in financial and material control, and strengthening the struggle against corruption and waste;

    IV. Organizational issues.

    Regarding the first and second issues, Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, delivered a report. Central Committee members and staff from party, government, social organizations, and scientific and cultural institutions engaged in discussions and unanimously supported Kim Il-sung's report.

    Regarding the third issue, Park Chang-ok, member of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, delivered a report. Following the report, staff from party, government, and economic organs participated in the discussions.

    On the last day of the meeting, Kim Il-sung delivered a concluding speech on the three issues.

    The plenary session made corresponding decisions on the various issues.

### 朝鲜劳动党中央委员会举行全体会议

1955-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜劳动党中央委员会举行全体会议
    新华社平壤五日电　“劳动新闻”五日刊载朝鲜劳动党中央委员会的公报说，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会在四月一日起的四天内举行了全体会议。
    全体会议讨论了如下的问题：
    一、关于进一步加强对党员的阶级教育工作；
    二、关于克服党和国家机关中一部分工作人员仍然存在的官僚主义；
    三、关于厉行节约，加强对金融、物资管制的纪律和加强反贪污、反浪费的斗争；
    四、组织问题。
    关于第一、第二两项问题由朝鲜劳动党中央委员会委员长金日成做了报告。中央委员们和党、政机关和社会团体以及科学、文化机关工作人员进行了讨论，一致支持金日成的报告。
    关于第三项问题，朝鲜劳动党中央常务委员会委员朴昌玉做了报告。报告后，党和政权机关、经济机关工作人员参加了讨论。
    在会议的最后一天，金日成做了关于三项问题的总结发言。
    全体会议对各项问题做出了相应的决定。
