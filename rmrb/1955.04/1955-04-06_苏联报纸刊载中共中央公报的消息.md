Title: News of Soviet Newspapers Publishing the Communiqué of the CCP Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1955-04-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 5th. Newspapers such as "Pravda," "Izvestia," "Trud," and "Krasnaya Zvezda" all published on the 5th the news of the Communiqué of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China regarding the convocation of the Party's National Conference from March 21st to March 31st and the Fifth Plenary Session of the Seventh Central Committee of the Communist Party of China held on April 4th.

### 苏联报纸刊载中共中央公报的消息

1955-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联报纸刊载中共中央公报的消息
    新华社莫斯科五日电　“真理报”、“消息报”、“劳动报”、“红星报”等报纸五日都刊载了中国共产党中央委员会关于在三月二十一日至三月三十一日召集了党的全国代表会议和中国共产党中央委员会四月四日举行了第七届第五次全体会议的公报消息。
