Title: Zhang Linzhi Appointed Minister of the Third Ministry of Machine Building

Author:
Date: 1955-04-10
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 9th—Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, appointed Zhang Linzhi as Minister of the Third Ministry of Machine Building of the People's Republic of China on the 9th, according to the decision passed by the 11th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.

### 张霖之被任命为第三机械工业部部长

1955-04-10
第1版()
专栏：

    张霖之被任命为第三机械工业部部长
    新华社九日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东九日根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会第十一次会议通过的决定，任命张霖之为中华人民共和国第三机械工业部长。
