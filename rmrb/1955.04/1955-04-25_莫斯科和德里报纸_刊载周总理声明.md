Title: Moscow and Delhi Newspapers Publish Premier Zhou's Statement
Author:
Date: 1955-04-25
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 24th: Moscow newspapers on the 24th published the statement made by Zhou Enlai, Chief Delegate of the Chinese Delegation to the Asian-African Conference, on the 23rd regarding the issue of easing the situation in the Far East and the Taiwan area.
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 24th: Delhi newspapers on the 24th universally published, in prominent positions, the statement made by Premier Zhou Enlai on the 23rd at the meeting of the heads of delegations from the eight countries participating in the Asian-African Conference, regarding the issue of easing tensions in the Far East, particularly in the Taiwan area.

### 莫斯科和德里报纸  刊载周总理声明

1955-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科和德里报纸
    刊载周总理声明
    新华社莫斯科二十四日电　莫斯科报纸二十四日登载了中国出席亚非会议代表团首席代表周恩来总理二十三日就和缓远东及台湾地区局势问题发表的声明。
    新华社新德里二十四日电　德里报纸二十四日普遍以显著地位刊载了周恩来总理二十三日在参加亚非会议的八国代表团团长会议上，就和缓远东紧张局势的问题特别是台湾地区紧张局势的问题发表的声明。
