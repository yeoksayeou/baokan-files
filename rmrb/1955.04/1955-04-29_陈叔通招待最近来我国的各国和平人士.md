Title: Chen Shutong Hosts Peace Activists from Various Countries Visiting China
Author:
Date: 1955-04-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 27th: Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, hosted a banquet on the evening of April 27th to entertain peace activists from various countries who have recently come to China.

Invited to the banquet were: the Korean delegation led by Park Chung-ai, who came to China after attending the Asian Countries Conference; the Soviet representatives, the famous writers Korneychuk and Vasilevskaya; and the Mongolian delegation led by Jagvaral Nyim.

Also invited to the banquet were: the Vietnamese delegation led by Lê Đình Thám, the Cambodian delegation led by Mey Phuong, and the Laotian delegation led by Singkapo, who were passing through China after attending the World Peace Conference.

Present at the banquet were: Xu Deheng, Xu Baoju, Liu Ningyi, and Luo Longji, standing committee members of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace.

Also present at the banquet were: Saifudin, Li Dequan, Chu Tunan, Wu Hongbin, as well as the heads of various people's organizations.

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 陈叔通招待最近来我国的各国和平人士

1955-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    陈叔通招待最近来我国的各国和平人士
    新华社二十七日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通二十七日晚举行宴会，招待最近来我国的各国和平人士。
    应邀出席宴会的有：出席亚洲国家会议后来我国的以朴正爱为首的朝鲜代表团，苏联代表、著名作家考涅楚克和华西列夫斯卡娅，以雅格瓦拉尔·尼姆为首的蒙古代表团。
    应邀出席宴会的还有：出席世界和平大会路经我国的以黎廷探为首的越南代表团，以梅普为首的高棉代表团，以辛卡波为首的寮国代表团。
    出席宴会的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员许德珩、许宝驹、刘宁一、罗隆基。
    出席宴会的还有：赛福鼎、李德全、楚图南、吴鸿宾，以及各人民团体的负责人。
    宴会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
