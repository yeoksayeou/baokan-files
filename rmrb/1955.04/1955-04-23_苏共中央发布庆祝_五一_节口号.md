Title: CPC Central Committee Issues Slogans for "May Day" Celebration
Author:
Date: 1955-04-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 20th, TASS: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union has issued the following slogans for the celebration of the 1955 "May Day" International Labor Day:

	I. Long live "May Day," the festival of solidarity of the working people of the world, the festival of fraternal friendship of workers in all countries!
	Raise higher the banner of proletarian internationalism! Workers of all countries, unite!
	II. A fraternal salute to all peoples who are fighting for peace, democracy, and socialism!
	III. Working people of all countries! If the people of all countries take up the cause of defending peace and persevere in this cause to the end, peace can be maintained and consolidated! Consolidate the unity of the people of all countries in the struggle for peace!
	Long live lasting peace among the people of all countries!
	IV. Long live the powerful movement to defend world peace! Working people of all countries, actively fight against the threat of a new world war, and strive for peaceful cooperation among the people of all countries! Expand and unite the ranks of peace supporters!
	V. Working people of all countries! Strive for the prohibition of atomic weapons, hydrogen weapons, and other weapons of mass destruction, and strive for general disarmament!
	VI. May the unity of the people of all countries in the struggle against the revival of German militarism be increasingly consolidated! May the people of European countries achieve lasting peace and collective security!
	VII. Working people of the Soviet Union and the People's Democracies! Further unite our forces and strengthen the economic and defense power of the countries of the democratic camp, to answer the conspiracies of the enemies of peace! Cooperate more closely to strive for peace and against the threat of aggression!
	VIII. A fraternal salute to the great Chinese people who are victoriously struggling to complete the first five-year plan for the development of the national economy, for the industrialization of the country, and for building the foundation of socialism!
	May the unbreakable friendship and cooperation between the Soviet and Chinese people, a powerful factor for world peace, flourish and develop!
	IX. A fraternal salute to the working people of the People's Democracies who are victoriously struggling for the further development of the national economy and culture, for strengthening the strength of the People's Democracies and constantly improving the welfare of the people, and for building socialism!
	May the unbreakable friendship and cooperation between the People's Democracies and the Soviet Union continue to develop and consolidate!
	X. A fraternal salute to the heroic people of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea who are fighting for the restoration of the national economy, for peace, and for the national reunification of Korea on a democratic basis!
	XI. A fraternal salute to the heroic people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam who are fighting for peace and democracy and for the restoration of the national economy of their motherland!
	XII. Salute to the peace-loving forces of Germany who are fighting against the evil policy of turning West Germany into a hotbed for a third world war!
	Long live the German Democratic Republic, a reliable pillar in the struggle for the establishment of a unified, independent, democratic, and peaceful Germany!
	XIII. Salute to the Indian people! For world peace, may the friendship and cooperation between the Soviet Union and the Indian people be increasingly consolidated!
	XIV. Salute to the people of the colonies and dependent countries who are fighting against imperialist oppression and for their freedom and national independence!
	XV. Salute to the people of Asian and African countries who are fighting against colonial oppression and racial oppression and for the relaxation of international tensions!
	XVI. Long live the friendship and cooperation of the people of the United Kingdom, the United States, and the Soviet Union in the struggle to ease international tensions, to strive for peaceful coexistence among countries, and to guarantee lasting world peace!
	XVII. May the friendship and cooperation between the Soviet people and the people of France and Italy in the struggle for peace, against the revival of German militarism, and for the establishment of European collective security be consolidated!
	XVIII. Salute to the Japanese people who are heroically fighting for national independence, for the democratic development of their country, against the revival of militarism, and against turning Japan into an imperialist military springboard in the Far East!
	XIX. Salute to the Austrian people who are striving to establish a democratic, independent, and peaceful Austria!
	XX. Long live the Soviet Union's peace-loving foreign policy—the unwavering policy of defending and consolidating peace, the policy of opposing the preparation and launching of new wars, and the policy of international cooperation and developing relations between countries!
	XXI. Soldiers and sailors of the Soviet Army and Navy! Unswervingly enrich your military and political knowledge, improve your combat skills, and master the latest combat technology and modern weapons!
	Long live the heroic Soviet armed forces, which enjoy the glory of victory and defend the peace and security of our motherland! May the Soviet armed forces be increasingly strengthened!
	XXII. Working people of the Soviet Union! Rally more closely around the Communist Party and the Soviet government, and mobilize our strength and creativity to engage in the great cause of building a communist society!
	XXIII. Long live the unbreakable alliance of the working class and the collective farmers, the unshakable foundation of the Soviet system! And may it be increasingly strengthened!
	XXIV. May the fraternal friendship of all nationalities of the Soviet Union, the source of the strength and might of our multinational socialist country, continue to develop and flourish!
	XXV. Working people of the Soviet Union! Resolutely implement the Party and government's policy of giving priority to the development of heavy industry, which is the foundation for the further development of the entire national economy, for improving the material and cultural welfare of the people, and for strengthening the strength and security of our motherland.
	XXVI. Workers, engineers, technicians, and foremen! Constantly achieve new successes in the socialist competition to complete the Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule! Launch a nationwide movement to increase labor productivity! Tirelessly strive for the further development of technology, for the promotion of scientific achievements in production, and for the promotion of the latest technology and advanced experience!
	XXVII. Workers in socialist industry! Strive to complete production plans and ensure the rhythmic operation of enterprises! Discover and utilize the potential in industry, strive to further strengthen labor discipline, improve product quality, and reduce costs!
	XXVIII. Coal industry workers, engineers, and technicians! Strive for all mines to complete production plans, promote the method of using circular charts to organize production, and improve the utilization of equipment! Build new mines and open-pit coal mines more quickly, and master their designed production capacity! Supply more coal for the national economy!
	XXIX. Oil industry workers, engineers, and technicians! Drill wells more quickly and develop new oil and natural gas mines! Promote advanced new methods of extracting and refining oil in every oilfield and factory! Supply the country with more oil, natural gas, and high-quality petroleum products!
	XXX. Soviet metallurgical workers! Better utilize the production capacity of metallurgical and mining enterprises, and improve equipment and production technology! Strive for every enterprise to complete its production plans! Supply the national economy with more pig iron, steel, steel products, and non-ferrous metals!
	XXXI. Workers, engineers, and technicians in the electrical and radio industries! Vigorously increase the production of high-quality equipment, instruments, and appliances!
	XXXII. Soviet power workers! Put new capacity of power stations and power grids into production more quickly, better utilize this capacity, and reduce fuel consumption! Promote advanced technology!
	XXXIII. Workers, engineers, and technicians in the machine-building industry! Increase the production of automobiles, tractors, combines, and other agricultural machinery! Produce more efficient machines, machine tools, and presses for the national economy!
	XXXIV. Workers in the chemical industry! Increase the production of mineral fertilizers and other chemical products, and increase their variety to meet the needs of the national economy and residents!
	XXXV. Construction workers, engineers, and technicians! Build new enterprises, state farms, machine tractor stations, residences, schools, hospitals, children's institutions, and cultural institutions more quickly! Strive to increase the level of industrialization of the construction industry and more widely use reinforced concrete structures! Reduce costs and improve project quality!
	XXXVI. Workers in the building materials industry! Increase the production of cement, prefabricated reinforced concrete structures, and new building materials! Produce more high-quality building materials for the construction of our motherland!
	XXXVII. Workers, engineers, and technicians in the timber, paper, and wood processing industries! Increase the felling and transportation of timber and the production of paper! Improve labor productivity, improve production organization, and fully utilize machinery! Produce more timber, paper, and high-quality furniture for the country!
	XXXVIII. Workers, engineers, and technicians in the daily necessities industry! Expand the production and variety of goods, and use raw materials more economically! Produce more durable and beautiful textiles, beautiful clothing, durable and beautiful shoes, and other daily necessities!
	XXXIX. Workers in the food and meat and dairy products industries! Strive to expand food production, improve food quality, and reduce product costs! Produce more sugar, oils, meat and dairy products, and other products for residents!
	XL. Fishery workers! Strive to complete the plan for fishing and processing fish products! Better utilize fishing fleets and tools! Develop aquaculture, improve product quality, and reduce product costs!
	XLI. Male and female members of collective farms, workers of machine tractor stations and state farms, and agricultural experts! Strive to make the annual output of grain reach no less than 10 billion poods! Expand the planting area of grain, increase the yield per unit area of grain, and reduce losses during grain harvesting! Ensure the fulfillment and overfulfillment of the 1955 plan for reclaiming virgin and fallow land!
	XLII. Male and female members of collective farms, workers of machine tractor stations and state farms! Strive to expand the planting area of corn, which is the most important source for rapidly increasing grain production and establishing a solid feed base for animal husbandry.
	XLIII. Agricultural workers! Fight for the large-scale development of all sectors of socialist agricultural production! On the basis of the new planning system, better utilize land and existing potential in collective farms and state farms! Produce more grain, meat, milk, wool, cotton, flax, sugar beet, and other agricultural products for the country!
	XLIV. Male and female members of collective farms! Increase labor productivity and strengthen labor discipline! Study scientific achievements and advanced experience and widely apply them to production! Ensure the further strengthening and vigorous development of the collective ownership economy of collective farms, increase the income of collective farms, and improve the welfare of collective farm members.
	XLV. Agricultural workers! Increase the output of livestock products, strive to rapidly increase product productivity and increase the total number of livestock! Produce more livestock products for the country!
	XLVI. Male and female members of collective farms, workers of machine tractor stations and state farms! Expand the production of industrial raw material crops, vegetables, and potatoes! Strive to increase the yield per unit area!
	XLVII. Workers of machine tractor stations! Strive to increase the yield per unit area of all crops, strive to develop collective ownership livestock farming in collective farms, and strive for collective farms to complete the task of selling agricultural products to the country on time! Better utilize tractors, combine harvesters, and other machinery! Strive to reduce product costs!
	XLVIII. Workers of state farms! Better utilize land, increase the yield per unit area of fields, and increase livestock product productivity! Establish your own feed base in state farms! Improve production organization and the utilization of machinery, strive to reduce production costs, and strive to make every state farm not require state subsidies! Make all state farms model enterprises with high commercial output!
	XLIX. Workers in local industries and handicraft cooperatives! Increase the production of consumer goods, household appliances, and building materials! Improve the quality of products and reduce costs, and better utilize local raw materials! Strive to expand enterprises that serve the daily life needs of residents and improve the work of these enterprises!
	L. Workers in state-owned and cooperative trade! Strive to develop Soviet urban and rural trade and maximize the satisfaction of the needs of the working people for necessities! Fight to improve the service level of Soviet trade!
	LI. Railway transportation workers! Fight to increase the volume of transportation and improve services to passengers! Strive to fully utilize the carrying capacity of vehicles, increase the weight of trains, and increase the speed of trains, and improve the turnover rate of vehicles and locomotives! Ensure that railway work is accurate and uninterrupted!
	LII. Maritime and inland river transportation workers! Strive to increase the volume of transportation! More vigorously promote advanced methods of using ships and ports! Perform the 1955 navigation work in a model manner!
	LIII. Automobile transportation and highway workers! Increase the volume of transportation and improve services to passengers! Maximize the productive utilization of automobiles! Maintain highways in a model manner and expand the construction of new highways!
	LIV. Postal and telecommunications workers! Tirelessly develop and improve postal equipment! Strive to ensure that postal, telegraph, telephone, and radio work is free of loopholes! Better serve residents!
	LV. Workers in Soviet agencies! Improve the work of state organs and eradicate bureaucracy and procrastination! Keenly care about the needs of the working people! Strengthen state discipline and strictly abide by socialist laws! Strive to streamline state organs and further strengthen their ties with the masses!
	LVI. Workers in scientific research institutions and institutions of higher learning! Advance Soviet science and technology! More boldly launch criticism of shortcomings in scientific work! Enhance the role of Soviet science in developing the national economy and ensuring our country's technological progress! Improve the work of training specialized personnel!
	LVII. Workers in literature and art! Strive for the high ideological content of literature and art, and tirelessly improve your artistic skills! Create works worthy of our great people!
	LVIII. Workers in national education! Improve the quality of school education! Educate children in the spirit of love and loyalty to the Soviet motherland and in the spirit of friendship among all nationalities! Cultivate citizens of socialist society who are cultured, educated, and love labor, and active builders of communism!
	LIX. Medical workers! Improve and develop the people's health care, and improve the working level of medical and health institutions! Apply medical achievements to practical work!
	LX. Soviet trade unions! More widely launch socialist competition, strive to increase labor productivity, and strive to complete and overfulfill national economic plans! Promote the experience of production innovators! Constantly care about continuing to improve the material welfare and cultural level of workers and staff!
	Long live the school of communism - the Soviet trade unions!
	LXI. Soviet women! Strive to achieve new achievements in all aspects of the national economy, science, and culture, and in the noble cause of educating children for the welfare and happiness of the Soviet people!
	Long live the active builders of communism - Soviet women!
	LXII. Long live the vanguard of young builders of communism, the organizer of Soviet youth, the active helper and reserve force of the Communist Party - the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union!
	LXIII. Young men and women! Our glorious Soviet youth! Actively participate in economic construction and cultural construction, and actively participate in the entire social and political life of the country! Persistently study advanced scientific and technological achievements, and master production knowledge in industry and agriculture! Resolutely and bravely fight for the victory of the great communist cause in our country.
	LXIV. Young Pioneers and students! Strive to master knowledge tenaciously! Love labor and observe discipline, and constantly achieve achievements in learning!
	LXV. Communist Party members and Communist Youth League members! Tirelessly master Marxist-Leninist theory, stand at the forefront of the struggle, and strive to further develop our socialist industry, to rapidly improve agriculture, to constantly increase the welfare of the Soviet people, and to build communism in the Soviet Union!
	LXVI. Long live the friendship and glorious fortress of the people of all nationalities of our country, the indestructible pillar of world peace - the great Union of Soviet Socialist Republics!
	LXVII. Long live the great Soviet people, the builders of communism!
	LXVIII. Long live the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the great inspiring force and guiding force of the Soviet people in the struggle to build communism!
	LXIX. Under the banner of Marx-Engels-Lenin-Stalin, under the leadership of the Communist Party, advance towards the victory of communism!

		Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union

### 苏共中央发布庆祝“五一”节口号

1955-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

    苏共中央发布庆祝“五一”节口号
    新华社讯塔斯社莫斯科二十日电：苏联共产党中央委员会发布庆祝一九五五年“五一”国际劳动节口号如下：
    一、全世界劳动人民团结的节日，一切国家工人兄弟友爱的节日——“五一”节万岁！
    更高地举起无产阶级国际主义的旗帜！全世界无产者，联合起来！
    二、向一切为和平、民主、社会主义而奋斗的人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    三、各国的劳动人民！如果各国人民将维护和平的事业担当起来，并且把这一事业坚持到底，和平就能够保持和巩固！巩固各国人民在争取和平的斗争中的团结！
    各国人民之间的持久和平万岁！
    四、强大的保卫世界和平的运动万岁！各国劳动人民，积极地为反对新的世界大战的威胁而斗争，争取各国人民之间的和平合作！扩大并团结和平拥护者的队伍！
    五、各国劳动人民！争取禁止原子武器、氢武器和其他大规模毁灭性武器，争取普遍裁减军备！
    六、祝各国人民在反对复活德国军国主义的斗争中的团结日益巩固起来！祝欧洲各国人民获得持久和平和集体安全！
    七、苏联和人民民主国家的劳动人民！进一步团结我们的力量，增强民主阵营国家的经济力量和国防力量，来回答和平的敌人的阴谋！更加密切地协力行动，来争取和平，反对侵略威胁！
    八、向为完成发展国民经济的第一个五年计划、为国家的工业化、为建成社会主义基础而胜利地斗争的伟大的中国人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    祝世界和平的强有力的因素——苏中两国人民牢不可破的友谊和合作发展昌盛！
    九、向为进一步发展国民经济和文化、为加强人民民主国家的实力和不断提高人民的福利、为建成社会主义而胜利地斗争的人民民主国家的劳动人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    祝人民民主国家同苏联的牢不可破的友谊和合作不断发展和巩固！
    十、向为恢复国民经济、为和平、为在民主基础上求得朝鲜的民族统一而斗争的英雄的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    十一、向为和平和民主、为恢复自己祖国的国民经济而斗争的英雄的越南民主共和国的人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    十二、向为反对变西德为第三次世界大战的温床的罪恶政策而斗争的德国爱好和平的力量致敬！
    争取建立统一、独立、民主和和平的德国的斗争中的可靠支柱——德意志民主共和国万岁！
    十三、向印度人民致敬！为了世界和平，祝苏联同印度人民的友谊和合作日益巩固！
    十四、向为反对帝国主义压迫、争取自己的自由和民族独立而斗争的殖民地和附属国的人民致敬！
    十五、向为反对殖民压迫和种族压迫、争取缓和国际紧张局势而斗争的亚非国家人民致敬！
    十六、英、美、苏三国人民在为缓和国际紧张局势、争取各国和平共处和保障世界持久和平的斗争中的友谊和合作万岁！
