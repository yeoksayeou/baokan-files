Title: National New Currency Issuance Largely Completed; 10,000 Yuan and 50,000 Yuan Old Currency Ceases Circulation in the Market from Today
Author:
Date: 1955-04-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 31st: As of the 29th, the new Renminbi issued nationwide has accounted for 80% of the total currency in circulation in the market. A corresponding amount of old Renminbi has been withdrawn, of which 10,000 yuan and 50,000 yuan denominations account for over 90% of the total old currency withdrawn. In major cities such as Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, and Guangzhou, new currency accounts for over 90% of the currency circulating in the market, and the 10,000 yuan and 50,000 yuan denominations of old currency have been basically completely withdrawn.

The People's Bank of China has notified all regions that, according to the State Council's order, from April 1st, the 10,000 yuan and 50,000 yuan denominations of old currency will cease circulation in the market, and no commodity transactions or other cash payments may use the 10,000 yuan and 50,000 yuan denominations of old currency. However, before April 30th, the People's Bank or other authorized exchange institutions will still be responsible for exchanging the 50,000 yuan and 10,000 yuan denominations of old currency at the statutory exchange rate. The date for stopping the circulation of various old currencies of 5,000 yuan and below will be determined separately depending on the redemption situation.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, March 31st: The work of issuing the new Renminbi in Shanghai has been basically completed. As of March 28th, the new Renminbi has accounted for more than 90% of the total currency in circulation in the city. According to the State Council's order on issuing the new Renminbi and withdrawing the existing Renminbi, from April 1st, the two denominations of old Renminbi, 10,000 yuan and 50,000 yuan, will cease circulation in the Shanghai market, and the exchange work of the People's Bank will continue until April 30th.

The Shanghai Municipal Supervisory Bureau and other departments are supervising the People's Bank to sort out the old currency that has been recovered, preparing for its destruction.

### 全国新币发行工作大部完成  从今天起一万元和五万元旧币停止在市场流通

1955-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

    全国新币发行工作大部完成
    从今天起一万元和五万元旧币停止在市场流通
    新华社三十一日讯  到二十九日止，全国发行的新人民币已占市场货币流通总量的百分之八十，相应数量的旧人民币已经收回，其中一万元和五万元的旧币占收回旧币总数的百分之九十以上。北京、天津、上海、广州等大城市市场上流通的货币中，新币已占百分之九十以上，一万元和五万元的旧币基本上全部收回。
    中国人民银行已通告各地：按照国务院命令规定，自四月一日起，一万元和五万元的旧币停止在市场流通，一切商品交易和其他现款收付都不得使用一万元和五万元的旧币。但在四月三十日以前，人民银行或其他代理兑换机构仍负责按法定比率兑换五万元和一万元的旧币。五千元和五千元以下的各种旧币停止流通使用的时间，将视收兑情形另行规定。
    新华社上海三十一日电　上海市发行新人民币的工作已基本完成。截至三月二十八日止，全市货币流通总量中，新人民币已占百分之九十以上。根据国务院关于发行新的人民币和收回现行的人民币的命令，从四月一日起，一万元和五万元的两种旧人民币将在上海市场停止流通，人民银行的兑换工作将继续到四月三十日。
    上海市监察局等部门正监督人民银行把已收回的旧币加以整理，准备销毁。
