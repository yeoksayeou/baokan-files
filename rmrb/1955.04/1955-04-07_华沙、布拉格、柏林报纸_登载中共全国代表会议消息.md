Title: Warsaw, Prague, and Berlin Newspapers Publish News of the CCP National Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-04-07
Page: 1

    Warsaw, Prague, and Berlin Newspapers
    Publish News of the CCP National Conference
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, April 6th. Warsaw newspapers published the communique of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China regarding the National Conference of the Party and the various resolutions of the National Conference on the 5th.
  Xinhua News Agency, Prague, April 6th. Major Prague newspapers published news of the National Conference of the Communist Party of China on the 5th.
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, April 6th. Newspapers in the Berlin Democratic Sector prominently published the communique of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China regarding the National Conference of the Party and various resolutions on the 5th and 6th. "Neues Deutschland" published the conference communique on the first page on the 5th, and on the 6th, published the full text of the resolution on the Gao Gang, Rao Shushi anti-Party alliance under the headline "Bourgeois Agents Eliminated in China."

### 华沙、布拉格、柏林报纸  登载中共全国代表会议消息

1955-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

    华沙、布拉格、柏林报纸
    登载中共全国代表会议消息
    新华社华沙六日电　华沙报纸五日登载了中国共产党中央委员会关于党的全国代表会议的公报和全国代表会议的各项决议。
  新华社布拉格六日电　布拉格各大报纸在五日刊载了中国共产党全国代表会议的消息。
    据新华社柏林六日电　柏林民主区各报五日和六日以显著地位刊载中国共产党中央委员会关于党的全国代表会议的公报和各项决议。“新德意志报”五日在第一版刊登会议公报，六日以“资产阶级代理人在中国被清除”为标题登载了关于高岗、饶漱石反党联盟的决议的全文。
