Title: Treaty on the Question of Dual Nationality Signed in Bandung
Author:
Date: 1955-04-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Bandung, April 22nd: The treaty between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia on the question of dual nationality was signed at 9:00 a.m. on April 22nd in Bandung by the plenipotentiary representatives of the two governments.

    Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, signed on behalf of the government of the People's Republic of China.

    Sunarjo, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Indonesia, signed on behalf of the government of the Republic of Indonesia.

    Attending the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were Chen Yi, Ye Jizhuang, Zhang Hanfu, and Huang Zhen, representatives of the delegation of the People's Republic of China attending the Asian-African Conference; advisors Liao Chengzhi, Yang Qiqing, Chen Jiakang, Huang Hua, and Da Punsheng; Secretary-General Wang Zhuoru; and Zhao Zhongshi and Lin Zhaonan, the negotiating representatives on the question of dual nationality.

    On the Indonesian side were Zodi Gonggokusumo, Minister of Justice of the Indonesian government; Ruslan Abdulgani, Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Besar, Secretary-General of the Ministry of Justice; Sukarjo Wijopranoto, Director of the Asia-Pacific Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Deputy Director Guo Yuhe; Suwito, Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and the negotiating representatives Suharjo and Taib Napis.

    Also attending the ceremony were Mononutu, Indonesian Ambassador to China, and Sanusi, Governor of West Java Province.

    Indonesian Foreign Minister Sunarjo and Chinese Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai delivered speeches at the signing ceremony.

    The signing ceremony proceeded in a harmonious atmosphere.

### 关于双重国籍问题的条约在万隆签字

1955-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

    关于双重国籍问题的条约在万隆签字
    新华社万隆二十二日电　中华人民共和国和印度尼西亚共和国关于双重国籍问题的条约二十二日上午九时在万隆由两国政府全权代表签字。
    中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来代表中华人民共和国政府签字。
    印度尼西亚共和国外交部长苏纳约代表印度尼西亚共和国政府签字。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有出席亚非会议的中华人民共和国代表团代表陈毅、叶季壮、章汉夫和黄镇，顾问廖承志、杨奇清、陈家康、黄华、达浦生，秘书长王倬如，以及关于双重国籍问题的谈判代表赵仲时和林兆南。
    印度尼西亚方面有印度尼西亚政府司法部长佐迪·龚多古苏莫、外交部秘书长鲁斯兰·阿卜杜加尼、司法部秘书长贝萨尔、外交部亚洲—太平洋司司长苏卡佐·维约普拉诺托、副司长郭裕和、外交部情报司司长苏维托、谈判代表苏哈佐和塔伊布·纳皮斯。
    参加仪式的还有印度尼西亚驻中国大使莫诺努图和西爪哇省省长萨努西。
    印度尼西亚外交部长苏纳约和中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来在签字仪式上致词。
    签字仪式在融洽的气氛中进行。
