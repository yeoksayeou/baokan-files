Date: 1955-04-11

    Our Great Motherland
    Spring wheat sowing in the Northeast region has begun successively. The Changxin Village First Agricultural Production Cooperative in Yushu County, Jilin Province, changed the past custom of "spring wheat before Qingming in the second month, spring wheat after Qingming in the third month," and started sowing eight days before Qingming. This is the scene of the cooperative members sowing.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    东北地区春小麦播种工作已陆续开始。吉林省榆树县长新村第一农业生产合作社改变了过去“二月清明麦在前，三月清明麦在后”的习惯，在清明前八天开始了播种。这是合作社社员们正在播种的情形。
