Title: Sukarno Hosts Premier Zhou and Holds Talks
Author:
Date: 1955-04-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 27th: Indonesian President Sukarno held a banquet on the evening of the 26th to host Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China.

    Also invited to the banquet were Chen Yi, Ye Jizhuang, Zhang Hanfu, Liao Chengzhi, Yang Qiqing, Qiao Guanhua, Chen Jiakang, Huang Hua, Da Puhan, Wang Zhuoru, and Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia Huang Zhen.

    The Indonesian side present at the banquet included: Vice President Hatta, Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo, Foreign Minister Sunaryo, Interior Minister Sunarjo, Defense Minister Kusumasumantri, Justice Minister Gondokusumo, Minister of Economic Affairs Roosino, Finance Minister Ong Eng Die, Agriculture Minister Sadjarwo, Transportation Minister Gani, Minister of Social Affairs Suroso, Minister of Education and Culture Muhammad Yamin, Minister of Health Lie Kiat Teng, Minister of Public Works Muhammad Hassan, Minister of National Welfare Abas, Army Chief of Staff Major General Bambang Sugan, Speaker of Parliament Sartono, and Indonesian Ambassador to China Mononutu.

    Also attending the banquet were diplomatic envoys to Indonesia from countries such as Afghanistan, India, the Soviet Union, Yugoslavia, and Czechoslovakia.

    The banquet proceeded in a warm atmosphere. At the banquet, toasts were repeatedly made to the ever-growing friendship between China and Indonesia and to the health of the leaders of the two countries.

    After the banquet, Indonesian dances were enjoyed.

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 27th: Indonesian President Sukarno, Vice President Hatta, Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo, Foreign Minister Sunaryo, and Indonesian Ambassador to China Mononutu, held talks with Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, and Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia Huang Zhen of the People's Republic of China at the Independence Palace from 9:00 to 11:00 a.m. on the 27th.

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 27th: On the afternoon of the 26th, Premier Zhou Enlai, accompanied by Indonesian President Sukarno, toured the city of Jakarta. Accompanying them on the tour were Vice Premier Chen Yi and Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia Huang Zhen from the Chinese side; and Army Chief of Staff Bambang Sugan, Jakarta Mayor Sudiro, and Indonesian Ambassador to China Mononutu from the Indonesian side.

    Premier Zhou Enlai and President Sukarno stood together in an open-top car. They passed through the main streets of the Indonesian capital. Tens of thousands of people stood on both sides of the street, applauding and cheering. Premier Zhou Enlai and President Sukarno frequently waved to the cheering crowds.

### 苏加诺招待周总理并举行会谈

1955-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    苏加诺招待周总理并举行会谈
    新华社雅加达二十七日电　印度尼西亚共和国总统苏加诺二十六日晚举行宴会，招待中华人民共和国总理周恩来。
    应邀参加宴会的还有陈毅、叶季壮、章汉夫、廖承志、杨奇清、乔冠华、陈家康、黄华、达浦生、王倬如和中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇。
    印度尼西亚方面出席宴会的有：副总统哈达、总理沙斯特罗阿米佐约、外交部长苏纳约、内政部长苏纳里约、国防部长库素马素曼特里、司法部长龚多古苏莫、经济部长罗西诺、财政部长王永利、农业部长萨茄乌、交通部长干尼、社会事务部长苏罗梭、文教部长穆罕默德·雅明、卫生部长李杰定、公共工程部长穆罕默德·哈桑、国家福利部长阿巴斯、陆军参谋长班邦·苏甘少将、国会议长沙多诺和印度尼西亚驻中国大使莫诺努图。
    参加宴会的还有阿富汗、印度、苏联、南斯拉夫和捷克斯洛伐克等国驻印度尼西亚的外交使节。
    宴会在热烈的气氛中进行。在宴会上，一再为中国和印度尼西亚两国不断增长的友谊和两国领袖的健康而干杯。
    宴会后，观赏了印度尼西亚舞蹈。
    新华社雅加达二十七日电　印度尼西亚共和国总统苏加诺、副总统哈达、总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约、外交部长苏纳约和印度尼西亚驻中国大使莫诺努图，同中华人民共和国总理周恩来、副总理陈毅、对外贸易部长叶季壮、外交部副部长章汉夫和中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇在二十七日上午九时到十一时在独立宫举行了会谈。
    新华社雅加达二十七日电　周恩来总理二十六日下午在印度尼西亚总统苏加诺陪同下游览了雅加达市区。同他们一起游览的，中国方面有副总理陈毅和中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇；印度尼西亚方面有陆军参谋长班邦·苏甘，雅加达市长苏迪罗和印度尼西亚驻中国大使莫诺努图。
    周恩来总理和苏加诺总统一同站在一辆敞篷汽车上。他们经过印度尼西亚首都的主要街道。成千成万的人站在街道两旁鼓掌欢呼。周恩来总理和苏加诺总统频频向欢呼的群众挥手示意。
