Title: British Prime Minister Churchill Resigns
Author:
Date: 1955-04-07
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, London, April 6th, TASS dispatch from London, April 5th: London Radio broadcast: On the afternoon of April 5th, Winston Churchill went to Buckingham Palace and submitted his resignation from the post of British Prime Minister to Queen Elizabeth.
    According to an official announcement issued by the Buckingham Palace Office, Winston Churchill has submitted his request to the Queen to be relieved of his duties as Prime Minister and First Lord of the Treasury, and the Queen has accepted his resignation.

### 英国首相邱吉尔辞职

1955-04-07
第4版()
专栏：

    英国首相邱吉尔辞职
    新华社六日讯　塔斯社伦敦五日讯：伦敦电台广播：四月五日下午，温斯顿·邱吉尔到白金汉宫把要求辞去英国首相职务的辞呈交给了伊丽莎白女王。
    据白金汉宫办公厅发表的官方消息说，温斯顿·邱吉尔已把请求解除他的首相和财政首席大臣职务的辞呈呈交女王，女王已接受了他的辞呈。
