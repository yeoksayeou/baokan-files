Title: Indian Parliament Parties Jointly Host Banquet for Parliamentarians Attending Asian Nations Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-04-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 13th. All parties and factions of the Indian Parliament jointly hosted a banquet at 5:00 PM on April 11th to entertain parliamentarians from various countries attending the Asian Nations Conference. The Speaker of the People's House of the Indian Parliament, G.V. Mavalankar, met with them at the banquet.

### 招待参加亚洲国家会议的各国议员  印国会各党团联合举行宴会

1955-04-15
第4版()
专栏：

    招待参加亚洲国家会议的各国议员
    印国会各党团联合举行宴会
    新华社新德里十三日电　印度国会各党团在四月十一日下午五时联合设宴招待参加亚洲国家会议的各国议员。印度国会人民院议长加·瓦·马瓦兰卡在宴会上和他们见了面。
