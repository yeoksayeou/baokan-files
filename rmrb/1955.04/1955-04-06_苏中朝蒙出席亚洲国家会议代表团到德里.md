Title: Soviet, Chinese, Korean and Mongolian Delegations to the Asian Countries Conference Arrive in Delhi
Author: 
Date: 1955-04-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 5th. The head of the Soviet delegation to the Asian Countries Conference, Tikhonov, arrived in Delhi by train on April 4th. Those who welcomed him at the station included representatives of various countries from the International Preparatory Secretariat of the Asian Countries Conference, representatives of some delegations to the Asian Countries Conference who had already arrived in Delhi, as well as members of the Indian Parliament, and members of the Legislative Assemblies of many states.

The eleven members of the Soviet delegation also arrived in Delhi by plane on April 4th, and they were warmly welcomed at the airport.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 5th. The head of the delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Asian Countries Conference, Guo Moruo, and delegation members Liao Chengzhi and Liu Guan-yi, the head of the Korean delegation, Park Jung-ai, the head of the Mongolian delegation, Yagvaral Nyim, and other members of the three delegations arrived in Delhi by plane on the 5th. Also arriving in Delhi on the same plane were members of the Soviet delegation, the famous writers Korneichuk and Vasilevskaya.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included the Chairman of the Reception Committee of the Asian Countries Conference and Chairman of the Delhi Municipal Committee, Agarwal, representatives from the Soviet Union, China, India, Japan, and other countries from the International Preparatory Secretariat, representatives from various countries attending the Asian Countries Conference who had already arrived in Delhi, Vice Chairman of the All-India Peace Council Sok, Chairman of the National Executive Committee of the India-China Friendship Association Jian Chand, and members of the Indian Parliament Nambiar, Gidwani, and Sigal.

The Chinese Ambassador to India, Yuan Zhongxian, and the Chargé d'Affaires of the Soviet Embassy in India also went to the airport to welcome them.

As the delegates stepped off the plane, the welcomers shouted slogans such as "Long live the solidarity of Asia" and "Long live world peace." Amidst the enthusiastic cheers, the delegates accepted flowers presented by the Reception Committee of the Asian Countries Conference and other organizations.

Prior to this, a part of the Chinese and Korean delegations had already arrived in Delhi on April 2nd, including Chinese delegation members Ding Xilin and Liu Ningyi, and Korean delegation member Han Xueye.

### 苏中朝蒙出席亚洲国家会议代表团到德里

1955-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    苏中朝蒙出席亚洲国家会议代表团到德里
    新华社新德里五日电　出席亚洲国家会议的苏联代表团团长吉洪诺夫在四月四日乘火车到达德里。到车站欢迎的有亚洲国家会议国际筹备秘书处的各个国家的代表，已经到达德里的一些参加亚洲国家会议的代表团的代表，以及印度国会议员，许多邦的立法会议议员等。
    苏联代表团的十一个团员也在四月四日乘飞机到达德里，他们在机场上受到了热烈的欢迎。
    新华社新德里五日电　出席亚洲国家会议的中华人民共和国代表团团长郭沫若和团员廖承志、刘贯一，朝鲜代表团团长朴正爱，蒙古代表团团长雅格瓦拉尔·尼姆和三个代表团的其他团员在五日乘飞机到达德里。同机到德里的还有苏联代表团团员、名作家考涅楚克和华西列夫斯卡娅。
    到机场欢迎的有亚洲国家会议招待委员会主席、德里市政委员会主席阿加瓦尔，国际筹备秘书处的苏联、中国、印度、日本和其他国家的代表，已经到达德里的出席亚洲国家会议的各国代表，全印和平理事会副主席索克，印中友好协会全国执行委员会主席吉安·昌德，印度国会议员南比亚尔、吉德瓦尼、西格尔等人。
    中国驻印度大使袁仲贤和苏联驻印度大使馆临时代办也到机场欢迎。
    当代表们走下飞机的时候，欢迎者高呼“亚洲的团结一致万岁”、“世界和平万岁”的口号。代表们在热烈的欢呼声中接受了亚洲国家会议招待委员会和其他团体的献花。
    在此之前，中国、朝鲜代表团的一部分团员已在四月二日到达德里，其中有中国代表团团员丁西林、刘宁一，朝鲜代表团团员韩雪野。
