    Our Great Motherland
    The broadcasting industry in New China has seen tremendous development. Ten years ago, Yan'an established the first people's radio station. Now, there are already fifty-seven people's radio stations nationwide. This is the shift foreman of a transmitter at the Central People's Broadcasting Station, and she is currently working.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新中国的广播事业有了巨大的发展。十年前延安建立了第一座人民广播电台，到现在，全国已有五十七座人民广播电台了。这是中央人民广播电台某发射机的值机班长，她正在进行工作。
