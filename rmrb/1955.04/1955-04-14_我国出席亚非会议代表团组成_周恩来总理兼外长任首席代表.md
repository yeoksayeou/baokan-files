Title: Composition of Chinese Delegation to the Asian-African Conference: Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai to Serve as Chief Delegate
Author:
Date: 1955-04-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 13th -- Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, in accordance with the decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, appointed Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of the State Council as the Chief Delegate of the People's Republic of China to the Asian-African Conference, and Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jiuzhuang, Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, and Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Indonesia, Huang Zhen, as delegates.

Xinhua News Agency, April 13th -- Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China approved: During the period when Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of the State Council participates in the Asian-African Conference abroad, the duties of the Premier will be performed by Vice Premier Chen Yun, and the duties of the Foreign Minister will be performed by Vice Minister Zhang Wentian.

### 我国出席亚非会议代表团组成  周恩来总理兼外长任首席代表

1955-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我国出席亚非会议代表团组成
    周恩来总理兼外长任首席代表
    新华社十三日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会的决定，任命国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来为中华人民共和国出席亚非会议代表团首席代表，国务院副总理陈毅、对外贸易部部长叶季壮、外交部副部长章汉夫和中华人民共和国驻印度尼西亚共和国特命全权大使黄镇为代表。
    新华社十三日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东批准：在国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来参加亚非会议的出国期间，总理的职务由陈云副总理代行，外交部长的职务由张闻天副部长代行。
