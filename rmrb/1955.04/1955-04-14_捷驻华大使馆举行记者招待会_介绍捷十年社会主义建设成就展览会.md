Title: Czechoslovak Embassy in China Holds Press Conference to Introduce Exhibition on Achievements of Ten Years of Socialist Construction in Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1955-04-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 13th. The Czechoslovak Embassy in China held a press conference on the afternoon of the 13th for the upcoming Exhibition on Achievements of Ten Years of Socialist Construction in Czechoslovakia.

At the meeting, Commercial Counselor Šal of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China introduced the Exhibition on Achievements of Ten Years of Socialist Construction in Czechoslovakia to more than 100 Chinese and foreign journalists attending the press conference, saying: This is the largest exhibition Czechoslovakia has held abroad, and its purpose is to provide Chinese friends with a comprehensive and clear introduction to the achievements of the Czechoslovak people over the past ten years. The products exhibited this time prove that Czechoslovakia is a highly industrialized country.

Šal then said: The mutual assistance and cooperation between China and Czechoslovakia is reflected in the continuous growth of material exchanges in the economic field year by year. The materials supplied by the People's Republic of China have helped the economic development and improvement of living standards of the Czechoslovak Republic; while Czechoslovakia uses industrial products to help China carry out socialist industrialization. He said that this is a mutually beneficial cooperation that develops the economic construction of the two countries and further strengthens the power of the peace camp.

Commercial Attaché Humopolaitz of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China and Hofmeister, the head of the cultural hall of the Exhibition on Achievements of Ten Years of Socialist Construction in Czechoslovakia, also introduced the preparations for the exhibition and the content of the exhibits at the meeting.

They also led reporters to visit the exhibits of the exhibition.

### 捷驻华大使馆举行记者招待会  介绍捷十年社会主义建设成就展览会

1955-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    捷驻华大使馆举行记者招待会
    介绍捷十年社会主义建设成就展览会
    新华社十三日讯　捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆在十三日下午为即将举行的捷克斯洛伐克十年社会主义建设成就展览会举行记者招待会。
    会上，捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆商务参赞沙尔向出席招待会的一百多名中外记者介绍捷克斯洛伐克十年社会主义建设成就展览会说：这是捷克斯洛伐克在国外举办的规模最大的展览会，它的目的是把捷克斯洛伐克人民十年来的成就向中国朋友作全面而明晰的介绍。这次展出的产品证明捷克斯洛伐克是一个高度工业化的国家。
    沙尔接着说：中捷两国的互助合作表现在经济上是逐年持续增长的物资交流。中华人民共和国供给的物资，帮助了捷克斯洛伐克共和国的经济发展和生活水平的提高；而捷克斯洛伐克则以工业产品来帮助中国进行社会主义工业化的建设。他说，这是一种互利的、发展两国经济建设和更进一步增强和平阵营力量的合作。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆商务专员胡摩波赖茨和捷克斯洛伐克十年社会主义建设成就展览会文化馆负责人霍夫梅斯特也在会上分别介绍了展览会的筹备情况和展品内容。
  他们并引导记者参观了展览会的展品。
