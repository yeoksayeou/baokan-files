Title: Czechoslovak Newly Appointed Ambassador Grigor Pays a Courtesy Call on Vice Minister Zhang Wentian of Our Ministry of Foreign Affairs
Author:
Date: 1955-04-09
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 8th. Antonín Gregor, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Czechoslovak Republic to China, and Šmíd, the embassy counselor, paid a courtesy call on Vice Minister Zhang Wentian of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs on the afternoon of the 8th to discuss matters related to the presentation of credentials. Also present were Chen Chu, Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 捷新任驻我国大使格里哥尔  拜会我外交部张闻天副部长

1955-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

    捷新任驻我国大使格里哥尔
    拜会我外交部张闻天副部长
    新华社八日讯　捷克斯洛伐克共和国新任驻我国特命全权大使安托宁·格里哥尔和大使馆参赞石密茨，在八日下午到我国外交部拜会张闻天副部长，商谈递交国书事宜。在座的还有外交部苏联东欧司司长陈楚，礼宾司司长柯华。
