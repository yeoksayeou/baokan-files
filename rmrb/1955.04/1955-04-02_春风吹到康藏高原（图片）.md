Author: Xin Chang
Date: 1955-04-02
Page: 2

Spring Breeze Blows to the Kangzang Plateau

The Kangzang Plateau has entered the thawing season. In the colder northern regions, farmers are actively preparing for spring plowing; in the warmer southern regions, farmers have already begun sowing.

The Kangzang Plateau's first state-owned farm, the Xinduqiao Mechanical Farm, has also begun spring production. The farm's Basang work area and the Xindu, Juri, and Dong'eluo production teams plan to sow 9,029 mu of land this spring, of which about 6,000 mu will be cultivated by tractors. (Photo by Xin Chang)

	Tractors at the state-owned Xinduqiao Farm are harrowing the land.
	The Basang work area of the farm uses horse-drawn disc harrows for spring plowing.
	Farm workers are sun-drying wheat seeds to improve the germination rate.
	More than 110,000 jin of "Nanda 2419" improved wheat seeds sown by the farm have been selected using mud and water.

### 春风吹到康藏高原（图片）
辛昌
1955-04-02
第2版()
专栏：

    春风吹到康藏高原
    康藏高原已到了解冻的季节。在气候较冷的北部地区，农民正积极进行春耕准备工作；在气候比较温暖的南部地区，农民已经开始播种。
    康藏高原上的第一个国营农场——新都桥机械农场也开始了春耕生产。这个农场所属的柏桑作业区和新都、居里、东俄洛三个生产队计划在今年春天播种九千零二十九亩土地，其中用拖拉机耕作的面积就有六千亩左右。　辛昌摄
    国营新都桥农场的拖拉机正在耙地。
    农场柏桑作业区的马拉圆盘耙在春耕。
    农场工人正在翻晒小麦种，争取提高发芽率。
    农场播种的十一万多斤“南大二四一九”小麦良种都进行了泥水选种。
