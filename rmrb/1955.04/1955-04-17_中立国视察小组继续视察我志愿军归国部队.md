Title: Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission Continues to Inspect the Return of Chinese People's Volunteers
Author:
Date: 1955-04-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 16th—The Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission stationed in Sinŭiju continued its inspection of the returning Chinese People's Volunteers from the 12th to the 15th.

The inspection work in the past few days proceeded as smoothly as before. The Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission did not raise any questions or request any re-examinations to the liaison officers of the Korean and Chinese sides.

### 中立国视察小组继续视察我志愿军归国部队

1955-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

    中立国视察小组继续视察我志愿军归国部队
    新华社平壤十六日电　驻新义州的中立国视察小组在十二日到十五日继续对中国人民志愿军归国部队进行视察。
    这几天的视察工作像过去一样进行得很顺利，中立国视察小组没有向朝中方面的联络官提出任何疑问或进行复查的要求。
