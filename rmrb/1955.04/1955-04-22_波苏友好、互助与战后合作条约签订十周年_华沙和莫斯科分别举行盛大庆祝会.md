Title: Tenth Anniversary of the Signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Mutual Assistance and Post-War Cooperation: Grand Celebrations Held in Warsaw and Moscow

Author:
Date: 1955-04-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 21st, Warsaw Dispatch: According to a TASS report from Warsaw, the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party and the Polish Council of Ministers jointly held a celebration meeting in Warsaw on the 20th to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Mutual Assistance and Post-War Cooperation.

Attending the celebration meeting were Polish United Workers' Party and state leaders Bierut and Zawadzki, representatives of various political parties and people's organizations, and advanced workers, cultural and scientific workers from Warsaw.

The Soviet government delegation, headed by Nikita Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, attended the celebration meeting.

Chairman of the Polish Council of State, Zawadzki, delivered the opening speech, warmly congratulating the Soviet people, the government, and the Communist Party of the Soviet Union on the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty.

First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Bierut, delivered a report on the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Mutual Assistance and Post-War Cooperation. He emphasized the great historical significance of the Sino-Soviet Treaty for People's Poland. Bierut's report was repeatedly interrupted by thunderous applause from the attendees.

Subsequently, Khrushchev addressed the meeting, extending heartfelt congratulations to all the Polish people on behalf of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, the Council of Ministers, and the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. His congratulatory message was warmly welcomed by the attendees.

Finally, a grand artistic performance was held, featuring artists from Poland and the Soviet Union.

Xinhua News Agency, April 21st, Moscow Dispatch: According to a TASS report from Moscow, the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and the Soviet Ministry of Culture jointly held a grand meeting in the Hall of Columns of the Trade Union House in Moscow on the 20th, to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Mutual Assistance and Post-War Cooperation. Representatives from various social organizations and working people of Moscow, as well as diplomatic envoys from many countries stationed in the Soviet Union, attended the meeting.

The government delegation of the People's Republic of Poland, headed by Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Cyrankiewicz, attended the meeting.

Soviet Minister of Culture, N. Mikhailov, delivered a report on the mutual assistance, cooperation, and friendship between the peoples of the Soviet Union and Poland.

Subsequently, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Poland, Cyrankiewicz, delivered a long speech. He congratulated the Soviet people on behalf of People's Poland and thanked the Soviet people for the assistance they had given to Poland in various fields.

Finally, Cyrankiewicz read out a congratulatory telegram from the State Council of the People's Republic of Poland, the Polish government, and the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party to the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, the Council of Ministers, and the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

Finally, a grand performance was held, featuring artists from Poland and the Soviet Union.

### 波苏友好、互助与战后合作条约签订十周年  华沙和莫斯科分别举行盛大庆祝会

1955-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    波苏友好、互助与战后合作条约签订十周年
    华沙和莫斯科分别举行盛大庆祝会
    新华社二十一日讯　据塔斯社华沙讯：波兰统一工人党中央委员会和波兰部长会议，二十日在华沙联合举行庆祝大会，庆祝波苏友好、互助与战后合作条约签订十周年。
    出席庆祝大会的有波兰统一工人党和国家的领导人贝鲁特和萨瓦茨基，各政党和人民团体的代表，华沙的先进工人、文化和科学工作者。
    以苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫为首的苏联政府代表团参加了庆祝大会。
    波兰国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基致开幕词，他向苏联人民、政府和苏联共产党热烈祝贺波苏条约签订十周年。
    波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记贝鲁特作了波苏友好、互助与战后合作条约签订十周年的报告。他着重指出了波苏条约对于人民波兰的巨大历史意义。贝鲁特的报告一再为到会者暴风雨般的掌声所打断。
    接着，赫鲁晓夫在会上致词。他代表苏联最高苏维埃主席团，部长会议和苏联共产党中央委员会向全体波兰人民致衷心的祝贺。他的贺词受到了到会者的热烈欢迎。
    最后举行了盛大的艺术表演会，参加表演的有波兰和苏联的艺术家。
    新华社二十一日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：全苏工会中央理事会、苏联对外文化协会和苏联文化部，二十日在莫斯科工会大厦圆柱大厅联合举行盛大集会，庆祝波苏友好、互助与战后合作条约签订十周年。参加会议的有莫斯科各社会团体和劳动人民的代表，以及许多国家驻苏联的外交使节。
    以部长会议主席西伦凯维兹为首的波兰人民共和国政府代表团参加了会议。
    苏联文化部部长尼·米哈伊洛夫在会上作了关于苏联和波兰两国人民之间的互助合作和友谊的报告。
    接着，波兰人民共和国部长会议主席西伦凯维兹在会上作了长篇演说。他代表人民波兰向苏联人民祝贺，并感谢苏联人民在各方面给予波兰的帮助。
    西伦凯维兹最后宣读了波兰人民共和国国务委员会、波兰政府和波兰统一工人党中央委员会给苏联最高苏维埃主席团、苏联部长会议和苏联共产党中央委员会的贺电。
    最后，举行了盛大的演出，参加演出的有波兰和苏联的艺术家。
