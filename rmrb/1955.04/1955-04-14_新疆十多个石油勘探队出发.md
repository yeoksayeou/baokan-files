Title: More Than Ten Petroleum Exploration Teams Set Off in Xinjiang
Author:
Date: 1955-04-14
Page: 2

    More Than Ten Petroleum Exploration Teams Set Off in Xinjiang
    More than ten petroleum exploration teams from the Xinjiang Petroleum Company and the Ministry of Geology dispatched to Xinjiang have successively left Urumqi to search for oil in the Junggar Basin and the Turpan Basin. The Petroleum Prospecting Brigade of the Ministry of Geology has also sent out a reconnaissance team planning to circle the Junggar Basin to survey routes for the subsequent prospecting teams.

    Some geological workers will, within 1955, ascertain the oil-bearing conditions of the Junggar Basin and the Turpan Basin, find several valuable oil-bearing structures, and conduct research on the formation of the basins and their history, drawing preliminary conclusions on oil-bearing properties from a geological perspective. Some personnel will conduct further detailed surveys, detailed measurements, and drilling in previously prospected and thoroughly investigated areas, providing the Xinjiang Petroleum Company with sufficient oil fields for mining next year. Another group of personnel will be searching this year for barite ore needed for drilling and the raw material for synthetic petroleum—oil shale.
                              (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 新疆十多个石油勘探队出发

1955-04-14
第2版()
专栏：

    新疆十多个石油勘探队出发
    新疆石油公司和地质部派到新疆的石油勘探队中，已有十多个队陆续离开乌鲁木齐去准噶尔盆地和吐鲁番盆地找寻石油。地质部石油普查大队还派出一个踏勘队计划绕准噶尔盆地一周，为后面的各个普查队踏勘路线。
    一部分地质工作人员将要在一九五五年内查明准噶尔盆地和吐鲁番盆地的含油情况，找到几个有价值的储油构造，并对盆地的形成和它的历史进行研究，从地质条件上作出含油的初步结论。一部分人员将在过去普查和详查地区，作进一步详查、细测和钻探，为新疆石油公司提供明年足够开采的油田。还有一部分人员今年将要找寻钻井所需的重晶石矿和人造石油的原料——油母页岩。
                              （据新华社讯）
