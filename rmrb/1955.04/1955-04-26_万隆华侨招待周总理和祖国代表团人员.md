Title: Overseas Chinese in Bandung Host Premier Zhou and Members of the Homeland Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-04-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Bandung, April 25th: Overseas Chinese representatives in Bandung held a reception at 11:30 a.m. on the 25th to host Premier Zhou Enlai, chief representative of the Chinese delegation to the Asian-African Conference, and all members of the delegation.

    More than two hundred people attended the reception.

    Thousands of people outside the reception hall repeatedly cheered Premier Zhou Enlai upon his arrival and departure.

### 万隆华侨招待周总理和祖国代表团人员

1955-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    万隆华侨招待周总理和祖国代表团人员
    新华社万隆二十五日电　万隆华侨代表在二十五日十一时三十分举行招待会，招待中国出席亚非会议代表团首席代表周恩来总理和代表团全体人员。
    出席招待会的有二百多人。
    招待会大厅外面数以千计的人在周恩来总理到达和离开时都一再向他欢呼。
