    Our Great Motherland
    The North Fei River Experimental Farm of the Huai River Harnessing Committee, in conjunction with the North Fei River irrigation and drainage project, has been carrying out work such as soil improvement and experiments with wheat and rice in the lower reaches of the North Fei River. The farm is currently conducting experiments on the water requirements of wheat.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    治淮委员会北淝河试验农场结合北淝河排灌工程，在北淝河下游进行了改良土壤、试验小麦和水稻等工作。农场现在正在进行小麦需水量的试验工作。
