Title: Grotewohl Sends Telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai Expressing Condolences
Author: Otto Grotewohl
Date: 1955-04-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 15th. German Democratic Republic Premier Grotewohl sent a telegram to our country's Premier Zhou Enlai expressing condolences for the losses suffered due to the destruction of the Chinese delegation's aircraft by American-Chiang Kai-shek special agents. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

Dear Comrade Premier, on behalf of the Council of Ministers and the people of the German Democratic Republic, I express my deepest condolences to the Government of the People's Republic of China and the entire Chinese people for the serious losses suffered by the delegation of the People's Republic of China attending the Asian-African Conference and journalists who were vilely and shamelessly murdered en route to Bandung.

All peace-loving German people are deeply convinced that this assassination crime of the imperialists to sabotage the Bandung Conference, and any other conspiracy, will never prevent the Chinese people and the people of Asia and Africa from turning this very important conference into a great victory in the struggle to defend peace in Asia and the world.

Premier of the German Democratic Republic, Otto Grotewohl

Berlin, April 15, 1955

### 格罗提渥总理致电周恩来总理表示慰问
奥托·格罗提渥
1955-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

    格罗提渥总理致电周恩来总理表示慰问
    新华社十五日讯　德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥致电我国周恩来总理，对中国代表团人员座机被美蒋特务破坏失事而遭到的损失表示慰问。电文如下：北京中华人民共和国总理周恩来同志：
    敬爱的总理同志，我代表德意志民主共和国部长会议和人民对中华人民共和国政府和全中国人民，由于中华人民共和国出席亚非会议代表团人员和记者在飞往万隆途中遇到卑鄙无耻的谋害而遭到的严重损失，向您表示深切的慰问。
    所有爱好和平的德国人民深信，帝国主义破坏万隆会议的这一暗杀罪行和任何其他阴谋绝不会阻止中国人民以及亚洲和非洲人民把这一非常重要的会议变成保卫亚洲和世界和平的斗争中的一个巨大的胜利。
    德意志民主共和国总理  奥托·格罗提渥
    一九五五年四月十五日于柏林
