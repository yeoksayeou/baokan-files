Title: Resolution of the First National Congress of the China Democratic National Construction Association on the Work Report of the General Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association
Author:
Date: 1955-04-14
Page: 1

The First National Congress of the China Democratic National Construction Association listened to and reviewed the work report of the General Committee and unanimously expressed its agreement. It believes that the work report summarizes the work of the Association since its establishment, clarifies three basic understandings, proposes five main experiences in cultivating core members, and has significant guiding significance for future work. The Congress decided that the Central Committee and the Standing Committee of the Central Committee should study and analyze the criticisms and suggestions on the work of the Association raised at the Congress as a reference for improving the Association's affairs, and implement the content of the work report into various specific tasks, and earnestly lead local organizations and all members to strive for further implementation of the Association's work guidelines and central tasks.

### 中国民主建国会第一次全国代表大会关于中国民主建国会总会委员会工作报告的决议

1955-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    中国民主建国会第一次全国代表大会关于中国民主建国会总会委员会工作报告的决议
    中国民主建国会第一次全国代表大会听取并审查了总会委员会的工作报告，一致表示同意；认为工作报告总结了本会成立以来的工作，明确了三点基本认识，提出了培养骨干分子的五点主要经验，对今后的工作是有重大的指导意义的；大会决定由中央委员会、中央常务委员会对大会中所提出的对于会的工作的批评和建议，加以研究、分析，作为改进会务的参考，并将工作报告的内容贯彻到各种具体工作当中去，切实领导各地方组织和全体会员为进一步贯彻执行会的工作方针和中心任务而努力奋斗。
