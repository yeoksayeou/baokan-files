Title: Bulganin Receives Indian Ambassador to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-04-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 9th: According to TASS, the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, N.A. Bulganin, received the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of India to the Soviet Union, K.P.S. Menon, on the 8th.

### 布尔加宁接见印度驻苏大使

1955-04-10
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁接见印度驻苏大使
    新华社九日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席尼·亚·布尔加宁，八日接见了印度共和国驻苏联特命全权大使克·普·西·梅农。
