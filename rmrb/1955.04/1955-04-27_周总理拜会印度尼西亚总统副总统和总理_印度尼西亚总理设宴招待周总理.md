Title: Premier Zhou Calls on Indonesian President, Vice President, and Prime Minister; Indonesian Prime Minister Hosts Banquet in Honor of Premier Zhou
Author:
Date: 1955-04-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 26th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China, accompanied by Indonesian Republic Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo, visited Indonesian Republic President Sukarno at the Independence Palace in Jakarta at 10:15 AM on the 26th. Accompanying Premier Zhou Enlai were Chen Yi, Ye Jizhuang, and Zhang Hanfu.

At 11:00 AM, Premier Zhou Enlai, accompanied by Indonesian Foreign Minister Sunarjo, called on Indonesian Vice President Hatta.

At 11:30 AM, Premier Zhou Enlai, accompanied by Indonesian Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo, laid a wreath at the Kalibata Heroes Cemetery.

At 12:15 PM, Premier Zhou Enlai, accompanied by Indonesian Foreign Minister Sunarjo, visited Indonesian Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo at the Prime Minister's residence.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 26th: Indonesian Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo hosted a banquet in honor of Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai at 12:30 PM. Also invited to the banquet were Chen Yi, Ye Jizhuang, Zhang Hanfu, and others.

Indonesian attendees at the banquet included: Minister of Finance Wang Yongli, Minister of Economic Affairs Roosino, Minister of Education and Culture Yamin, Speaker of the Parliament Sardono, and others.

Also present at the banquet were: Indonesian Ambassador to China Mononutu and Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia Huang Zhen. (Photo attached)

The Chinese delegation attending the Asian-African Conference, led by Chief Delegate Premier Zhou Enlai, arrived in Bandung by plane on April 17th. The photo shows Premier Zhou Enlai (left) in the airport lounge and Indonesian Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo (right), who came to welcome him.
                                Xinhua News Agency

### 周总理拜会印度尼西亚总统副总统和总理  印度尼西亚总理设宴招待周总理

1955-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理拜会印度尼西亚总统副总统和总理
    印度尼西亚总理设宴招待周总理
    新华社雅加达二十六日电　中华人民共和国总理周恩来在印度尼西亚共和国总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约陪同下，于二十六日上午十时十五分到雅加达独立宫拜会了印度尼西亚共和国总统苏加诺。在拜会时，随同周恩来总理前往的有陈毅、叶季壮和章汉夫。
    在上午十一时，周恩来总理在印度尼西亚外交部长苏纳约陪同下拜会了印度尼西亚副总统哈达。
    在十一时三十分，周恩来总理在印度尼西亚总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约陪同下，向加里巴塔烈士墓献了花圈。
    十二时十五分，周恩来总理在印度尼西亚外交部长苏纳约陪同下，到总理官邸拜会了印度尼西亚总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约。
    新华社雅加达二十六日电　印度尼西亚总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约在十二时三十分设宴招待中国总理周恩来。应邀参加宴会的还有陈毅、叶季壮、章汉夫等人。
    印度尼西亚方面出席宴会的有：财政部长王永利、经济部长罗西诺、文教部长雅明、国会议长沙多诺等人。
    出席宴会的还有：印度尼西亚驻中国大使莫诺努图和中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇。（附图片）
    出席亚非会议的我国代表团在首席代表周恩来总理率领下，于四月十七日乘飞机到达万隆。图为在机场休息室中的周恩来总理（左）和前往欢迎的印度尼西亚总理沙斯特罗阿米佐约（右）。
                                新华社稿
