Title: Chiang Kai-shek's Planes and Warships Constantly Harass Coastal Areas
Author:
Date: 1955-04-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Frontlines in Fujian, April 6th: In March, Chiang Kai-shek's planes and warships continuously harassed the coastal areas of Fujian and Zhejiang.

From March 1st to March 31st, American-made Chiang Kai-shek F-84 and F-47 aircraft intruded into the airspace over Fujian and Zhejiang coastal areas in a total of 451 batches and 649 sorties. They strafed fishermen and fishing boats engaged in production on the sea near Lianjiang, Fuqing, Putian, Jinjiang, Yunxiao, Dongshan in Fujian, and Dongtoushan in Zhejiang, a total of nine times. Eleven fishing boats were destroyed or damaged, nineteen fishermen were killed, and twenty-six fishermen were injured. American-made Chiang Kai-shek warships and gunboats also intruded into the sea near Xiapu, Putian, Hui'an, Haicheng, Zhangpu and other counties on several occasions during this period, and shelled fishing boats that were fishing. Two fishing boats were seized and more than twenty fishermen were kidnapped from the sea near Dongding Island, which is occupied by Chiang Kai-shek's troops.

The People's Liberation Army's anti-aircraft artillery units shot down a total of three Chiang Kai-shek aircraft in the coastal areas of Changle, Jinjiang, etc. on March 7th, 15th, and 27th respectively.

### 蒋贼飞机和舰艇不断骚扰沿海地区

1955-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

    蒋贼飞机和舰艇不断骚扰沿海地区
    新华社福建前线六日电　三月份，蒋贼飞机、舰艇不断骚扰福建、浙江沿海地区。
    从三月一日到三十一日，美造蒋贼F—八四型、F—四七型飞机共四百五十一批六百四十九架次窜入福建和浙江沿海上空，并先后在连江、福清、莆田、晋江、云霄、东山及浙江之洞头山等地附近海面上向正在生产的渔民和渔船扫射九次。打毁打伤渔船十一只，打死渔民十九人，打伤渔民二十六人。美造蒋贼军舰和炮艇，在这一时期中也多次窜入霞浦、莆田、惠安、海澄、漳浦等县附近海面，并炮击正在捕鱼的渔船；在蒋贼军盘踞的东椗岛附近海面抓去渔船二只，劫走渔民二十多人。
    中国人民解放军高射炮部队曾于三月七日、十五日、二十七日分别在长乐、晋江等沿海地区击伤蒋贼飞机共三架。
