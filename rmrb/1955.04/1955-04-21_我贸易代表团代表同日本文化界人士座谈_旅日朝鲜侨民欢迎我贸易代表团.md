Title: Representatives of Our Trade Delegation Hold Discussions with Japanese Cultural Figures; Korean Residents in Japan Welcome Our Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-04-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 20th. On the 19th, Li Zhuchen, Deputy Head of the Chinese Trade Delegation to Japan, and others, attended a Sino-Japanese cultural exchange forum jointly organized by over one hundred Japanese cultural organizations.

About two hundred and sixty representatives from various sectors of Japanese literature, art, drama, dance, music, astronomy, archeology, geography, law, medicine, education, and news publication attended the forum.

Tsukage Hideomi, Secretary-General of the Japan Democratic Scientists Association, delivered a welcoming speech, saying: The Japanese people are eager for cultural and academic exchanges between China and Japan. This is necessary for Asian peace and the cultural development of both China and Japan. He said that many Japanese cultural and press figures have visited China in the past. Now, we in the Japanese cultural circles are working hard to invite Chinese scholars to visit Japan.

Li Zhuchen said in his speech that China and Japan have had a long history of cultural exchange since ancient times. Cultural exchange between the two countries is beneficial to the people of both countries. We hope to realize Sino-Japanese cultural exchange. Li Zhuchen also introduced the situation of cultural construction in New China after liberation.

At the forum, many Japanese cultural figures unanimously expressed their desire to promote cultural exchange between China and Japan.

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 20th. On the 19th, Li Zhuchen, Deputy Head of the Chinese Trade Delegation to Japan, and others, attended a forum organized by the Japanese publishing industry. Representatives from about one hundred Japanese publishing companies attended the forum.

At the meeting, Kanehara Kinji, Vice Chairman of the Asian Cultural Exchange Publishing Association, delivered a welcoming speech, saying: The Japanese people earnestly hope that Sino-Japanese relations will normalize as soon as possible and that close economic and cultural exchanges will be carried out in peace and friendship.

Deputy Head Li Zhuchen and delegation advisor Fu Hao introduced the cultural development of New China at the meeting and expressed to the Japanese publishing industry the Chinese people's desire to strengthen cultural exchanges between China and Japan.

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 20th. On the 19th, Lei Renmin, Head of the Chinese Trade Delegation to Japan, attended a welcoming meeting held by Korean residents in Japan.

At the meeting, representatives of the Korean residents in Japan presented a letter of salute to Lei Renmin. The letter stated that over six hundred thousand Korean residents in Japan warmly welcome the trade delegation from the People's Republic of China and hope that the Sino-Japanese trade agreement negotiations will be successful.

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 20th. The Chinese Trade Delegation to Japan visited the Hakone scenic area on the 19th.

When the delegation passed through Yokohama, they visited the Chinese School run by the Yokohama Overseas Chinese Affairs Office, and received a warm welcome from the overseas Chinese in Yokohama. Head Lei Renmin addressed the welcoming compatriots and students of the Chinese School, hoping that the overseas Chinese students of the Chinese School would study hard, exercise, and prepare to participate in the economic construction of the motherland in the future.

On their way back to Tokyo, the delegation also laid flowers in front of the Nie Er Monument on Kugenuma Beach in Fujisawa City.

The delegation returned to Tokyo on the evening of the 19th.

### 我贸易代表团代表同日本文化界人士座谈  旅日朝鲜侨民欢迎我贸易代表团

1955-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我贸易代表团代表同日本文化界人士座谈
    旅日朝鲜侨民欢迎我贸易代表团
    新华社东京二十日电　十九日，中国访问日本贸易代表团副团长李烛尘等应邀出席了由一百多个日本文化团体联合主办的日中文化交流座谈会。
    出席座谈会的有日本文学、艺术、戏剧、舞蹈、音乐、天文、考古、地理、法律、医学、教育、新闻出版各界的代表约二百六十人。
    日本民主主义科学者协会干事长柘植秀臣致欢迎词说：日本国民热望中国和日本进行文化学术的交流。这对于亚洲和平和中日两国的文化发展都是必要的。他说，过去已有不少日本文化界和新闻界人士访问过中国。现在，我们日本文化界人士正在为邀请中国学者来日本访问而努力。
    李烛尘致词说，中日两国自古以来就有着悠久的文化交流关系。两国的文化交流对两国人民都有好处。我们希望能实现中日文化交流。李烛尘并介绍了解放后新中国在文化建设方面的情形。
    在座谈会上，许多日本文化界人士一致表示促进中日两国文化交流的愿望。
    新华社东京二十日电　十九日，中国访问日本贸易代表团副团长李烛尘等应邀出席了日本出版界主办的座谈会。出席座谈会的有约一百个日本出版公司的代表。
    会上，亚洲文化交流出版会副会长金原金二致欢迎词说：日本国民热烈希望尽早地使中日两国关系正常化，在和平友好中进行紧密的经济、文化交流。
    李烛尘副团长和代表团顾问符浩在会上介绍了新中国的文化发展情况，并向日本出版界表达了中国人民希望加强中日两国文化往来的愿望。
    新华社东京二十日讯　十九日，中国访问日本贸易代表团团长雷任民出席了旅日朝鲜侨民的欢迎会。
    会上，旅日朝鲜侨民的代表把一封致敬信交给了雷任民。信中说，我们六十多万旅日朝鲜侨民热烈地欢迎来自中华人民共和国的贸易代表团，并希望中日两国的贸易协议谈判获得成功。
    新华社东京二十日电　中国访问日本贸易代表团在十九日游览了箱根风景区。
    代表团路经横滨市时，参观了横滨市旅日华侨办的中华学校，受到横滨华侨的热烈欢迎。雷任民团长向欢迎的侨胞和中华学校学生致词，希望中华学校的华侨学生努力学习，锻炼身体，准备将来参加祖国的经济建设。
    代表团在返东京途中，还在藤泽市鹄沼海边的聂耳纪念碑前献了鲜花。
    代表团在十九日晚回到了东京。
