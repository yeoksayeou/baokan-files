Title: Eden Succeeds as British Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1955-04-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, London, 7th April: Buckingham Palace issued an official statement on the 6th saying: Queen Elizabeth of Britain summoned Eden on the 6th, asking him to take the position of Prime Minister and First Lord of the Treasury. Eden has accepted the Queen's appointment.

Another dispatch: On the evening of the 6th, Prime Minister Eden was unanimously elected as the leader of the Conservative Party's parliamentary group.

### 艾登继任英国首相

1955-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    艾登继任英国首相
    新华社七日讯  伦敦消息：白金汉宫六日发表官方声明说：英国女王伊丽莎白六日召见了艾登，要他担任首相和财政首席大臣的职位。艾登已经接受了女王的任命。
    又讯　六日晚，艾登首相被一致选为保守党议会党团的领袖。
