Title: Madame Eugénie Cotton, President of the Women's International Democratic Federation, Arrives in Beijing

Author: 
Date: 1955-04-23
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd. Madame Eugénie Cotton, President of the Women's International Democratic Federation, Vice-President of the World Peace Council, and President of the French Women's Union, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 22nd at the invitation of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation for a visit to China. Accompanying her were three French women: Madame Vilderme, Madame Alexandre, and Madame Balantini.
    Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Cai Chang, President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Vice-Presidents Deng Yingchao, Li Dequan, Xu Guangping, and Zhang Yun; Kang Keqing, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's National Committee for the Defense of Children; Wu Maosun, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace; Ma Yinchu and Lu Shijia from the capital's scientific and educational circles; Zhang Xiaomei, Director of the Beijing Municipal Democratic Women's Federation; and over a hundred women from the capital.
    At the airport, Madame Cotton received flowers presented by children of Beijing.
    Cai Chang delivered a welcoming speech first. On behalf of Chinese women, she extended a warm welcome to Madame Cotton for her visit to China. She said: Madame Cotton's deeds in fighting to defend world peace and the rights of women and children around the world have left a deep and cordial impression on Chinese women and children. Chinese women will unite closely with women from all countries around the world around the Women's International Democratic Federation and fight to the end to defend world peace, defend women's rights, and defend the cause of children.
    Next, Madame Cotton delivered a reply. She said: I am very grateful for the warm and generous invitation from Chinese women. She said: We admire infinitely your great country, your country with a great and rich history is undertaking even greater undertakings than in the past. In your past struggles and future struggles, the hearts of the French people are closely united with you. In particular, as President of the Women's International Democratic Federation, my heart is closely linked with the hearts of Chinese women. I hope my visit will further enhance the friendship and feelings between the people and women of France and China.

### 国际民主妇联主席戈登夫人等到京

1955-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

    国际民主妇联主席戈登夫人等到京
    新华社二十二日讯　应中华全国民主妇女联合会邀请，前来访问我国的国际民主妇女联合会主席、世界和平理事会副主席、法兰西妇女联盟主席欧仁妮·戈登夫人于二十二日下午到达北京。和她同来的还有法国妇女维尔德美夫人、亚历山大夫人和巴朗蒂尼夫人等三人。
    前往机场欢迎的有中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅，副主席邓颖超、李德全、许广平、章蕴，中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会秘书长康克清，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副秘书长吴茂荪，首都科学界、教育界人士马寅初、陆士嘉，北京市民主妇女联合会主任张晓梅及首都妇女群众等共百余人。
    在机场上，戈登夫人接受了北京市少年儿童的献花。
    蔡畅首先致欢迎词。她代表中国妇女对戈登夫人来中国访问表示热烈的欢迎，她说：戈登夫人为保卫世界和平、保卫全世界妇女儿童权利而奋斗的事迹，给我们中国妇女和儿童的印象是深刻而亲切的。我们中国妇女将和全世界各国妇女一起紧密地团结在国际民主妇女联合会的周围，为保卫世界和平、保卫妇女权利和保卫儿童的事业奋斗到底。
    接着，戈登夫人致答词。她说：我十分感谢中国妇女热情而慷慨的邀请。她说：我们无限钦佩你们这个伟大的国家，你们这个有着伟大而丰富历史的国家正在进行着比过去更伟大的事业，在你们过去的斗争和今后的斗争中，法国人民和你们的心都是紧密地团结在一起的，特别是作为国际民主妇女联合会主席的我，是和中国妇女的心紧紧地联在一起的。我期望我的访问能更进一步增进法中两国人民和妇女的友谊和感情。
