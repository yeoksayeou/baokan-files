Title: Indian Government Establishes Consulate General in Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1955-04-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 6th: The Indian Government Information Bureau issued a press release stating that the Indian government has established a Consulate General in Hanoi, Vietnam, which began operations on February 12th of this year. Anand Mohan Sahay has been appointed as Consul General.
    The Indian government has also established a Consulate General in Vientiane, Laos, which began operations on March 18th of this year. Dr. Rajkumar has been appointed as Consul General.

### 印度政府在河内设总领事馆

1955-04-07
第4版()
专栏：

    印度政府在河内设总领事馆
    新华社新德里六日电　印度政府新闻局发表新闻公报说，印度政府已经在越南河内设立总领事馆，从今年二月十二日起开始办公。阿南德莫汉·萨海被任命为总领事。
    印度政府也已经在老挝万象设立总领事馆，从今年三月十八日起开始办公。拉杰库马尔博士被任命为总领事。
