Title: China's Delegation to the Lyon International Fair Holds Reception
Author:
Date: 1955-04-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, April 25th, Lyon Telephone: The Chinese delegation to the Lyon International Fair held a reception at the Chinese pavilion on the 22nd. Approximately 300 guests attended.

    Jules Julien, Vice-President of the French National Assembly and Chairman of the French National Committee of Foreign Trade Advisors, military and political leaders from the Rhone Department and the city of Lyon, Chardin, President of the Lyon International Fair, and heads of various departments of the fair attended the reception.

    Also present were Bariot, President of the Lyon Chamber of Commerce, Depla, President of the French Committee for the Promotion of International Trade, French business figures, and representatives from science, religion, education, journalism, and trade union organizations.

    Diplomatic envoys, commercial representatives, and consuls from Romania, the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Poland, the United Kingdom, Sweden, Denmark, the Netherlands, Syria, and other countries stationed in France also attended the reception.

    Yang Haolu, head of the Chinese exhibition delegation, delivered a speech at the reception. He first expressed gratitude on behalf of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade to Herriot, Honorary President of the French National Assembly, Honorary Chairman of the Lyon International Fair, and Mayor of Lyon, the authorities of the Rhone Department and the Lyon Municipality, the heads of the fair, and French friends who assisted in the exhibition work. He then said, "The friendship between the Chinese and French people has a long history, and there is great potential for developing trade relations between the two countries. I believe that this comprehensive exhibition of the People's Republic of China pavilion will further strengthen mutual understanding and friendship, and I hope that normal trade relations between the two countries will gradually recover and increase."

    Finally, delegation head Yang Haolu expressed his willingness to bring the French people's congratulations and friendship for China's exhibition back to the Chinese people.

### 我参加里昂国际博览会展览团举行招待会

1955-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我参加里昂国际博览会展览团举行招待会
    新华社柏林二十五日电　里昂电话：中国参加里昂国际博览会展览团二十二日在中国馆举行招待会。到会宾客约三百人。
    法国国民议会副议长、法国全国对外贸易顾问委员会主席儒勒·余立安、罗尼省和里昂市的军政界领袖、里昂国际博览会主席夏丹和博览会各部门负责人出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的还有里昂商会主席巴里奥、法国国际贸易促进委员会主席德普拉、法国工商界人士和科学、宗教、教育、新闻界及工会组织的代表。
    出席招待会的还有罗马尼亚、苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、英国、瑞典、丹麦、荷兰、叙利亚等国驻法国外交使节、商务代表和领事。
    中国展览团团长杨浩庐在会上致词。他首先代表中国国际贸易促进委员会向法国国民议会荣誉议长、里昂国际博览会名誉主席和里昂市长赫里欧，罗尼省和里昂市政当局，博览会负责人和协助展览工作的法国朋友们致谢。他接着说：“中法两国人民的友谊是有长久历史的，发展两国的贸易关系存在着很大的可能性。相信这次中华人民共和国馆综合性的展出，将进一步加强彼此的了解和友谊，希望两国正常贸易关系逐步恢复和增进。”
    最后，杨浩庐团长表示愿意把法国人民对中国这次展出的祝贺和友谊带回给中国人民。
