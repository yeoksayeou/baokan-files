Title: Iranian Prime Minister Zahedi Resigns
Author:
Date: 1955-04-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tehran, April 6th (TASS): According to well-informed journalistic circles in Tehran, the Shah of Iran accepted the resignation of Prime Minister General Zahedi on the evening of April 5th and entrusted Hossein Ala, the current Minister of the Royal Court, with forming a new government.

Since Hossein Ala will be traveling to Switzerland for medical treatment on April 11th, Finance Minister Amini will temporarily act as Prime Minister.

General Zahedi will be going to West Germany on April 7th.

### 伊朗首相萨希迪辞职

1955-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    伊朗首相萨希迪辞职
    新华社七日讯　塔斯社德黑兰六日讯：据德黑兰消息灵通的新闻界人士说，伊朗国王在四月五日晚间接受了首相萨希迪将军辞职的辞呈，并委托现任宫内大臣侯赛尼·阿拉组织新政府。
    由于侯赛尼·阿拉将在四月十一日前往瑞士医疗，财政大臣阿米尼将暂时代理首相的职务。
    萨希迪将军将在四月七日去西德。
