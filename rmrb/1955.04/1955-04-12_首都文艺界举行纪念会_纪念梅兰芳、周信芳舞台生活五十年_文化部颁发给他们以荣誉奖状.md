Title: Literary and Art Circles in the Capital Hold Commemoration Meeting Commemorating the 50th Anniversary of Mei Lanfang and Zhou Xinfang's Stage Life The Ministry of Culture Awards Them Honorary Certificates
Author:
Date: 1955-04-12
Page: 3

The commemoration meeting jointly organized by the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and the Chinese Dramatists Association to commemorate the 50th anniversary of the stage life of Mei Lanfang and Zhou Xinfang, outstanding patriotic and realist artists of our country, was held yesterday (the 11th) afternoon at the Tianqiao Theater in the capital. At the meeting, the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China also awarded Mei Lanfang and Zhou Xinfang honorary certificates.

A total of 1,400 literary, drama, film, fine arts, music, and dance workers from the capital attended the commemoration meeting. Soviet art experts in Beijing, cultural counselors from various countries' embassies in China, and Japanese famous drama actor Kanya Nakamura were also invited to attend.

Yang Hansheng, Secretary-General of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and Executive Director of the Chinese Dramatists Association, delivered the opening speech. He said: Today's commemoration meeting demonstrates the new society's love and encouragement for its own artists and will encourage art workers to better implement the literary and art policy put forward by Comrade Mao Zedong and serve the workers, peasants, and soldiers more.

Xia Yan, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, said in his speech: Mr. Mei Lanfang and Mr. Zhou Xinfang, in their long fifty years, have inherited and promoted the fine traditions of realism and patriotism in our country's opera art through creative labor; until today, they not only still maintain the highest level of Peking opera art, but also continue to develop. In their artistic life, they are still full of youthful spirit. Xia Yan said: The drama art community should value their and other senior outstanding people's artists' realistic performance art experience, learn from their rich experience in singing art and typical and characteristic performance methods, so that our realistic art tradition can be continuously enriched, innovated, and carried forward.

Next, Ouyang Yuqian, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Dramatists Association, made a report on Mei Lanfang entitled "The True Actor - Creator of Beauty"; Tian Han, Chairman of the Chinese Dramatists Association, made a report entitled "Fighting Performing Artist - Zhou Xinfang." In their reports, they briefly introduced the experiences of Mei Lanfang and Zhou Xinfang, introduced the excellent qualities of Mei Lanfang and Zhou Xinfang as patriotic artists, analyzed in detail their artistic achievements and contributions to Chinese opera art, and called on opera workers to learn from their tireless spirit of diligent learning, faithful artistic labor, and artistic reform for decades.

Cheng Yanqiu, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Traditional Opera and a famous dramatist, delivered a congratulatory speech on behalf of all opera workers at the meeting.

Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, presented Mei Lanfang and Zhou Xinfang with honorary certificates on behalf of the Ministry of Culture amidst thunderous applause from the audience.

The young generation of drama workers in the capital went on stage to present flowers and pay tribute to these two outstanding drama predecessors. Professor Lesli, a Soviet drama expert, presented gifts on behalf of the Soviet art experts in Beijing and wished them greater contributions to the great Chinese people and to all mankind.

Finally, Mei Lanfang and Zhou Xinfang delivered speeches in reply. In their speeches, they both said: This commemoration meeting is not only an individual honor but also a common honor for all opera workers in the country. They both expressed their heartfelt thanks to the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao. After recounting his feelings after fifty years of stage life, Mei Lanfang encouraged young opera workers and said: Love your work, honestly engage in learning, work hard in artistic practice, constantly labor, constantly exercise, and constantly create; constantly humbly accept mass opinions and strictly conduct self-criticism; for the people, for the bright and beautiful future of the motherland, contribute everything we have! Zhou Xinfang said: Although Mr. Mei and I are both sixty years old, our spirit will never grow old. We are willing to join hands with comrades in the national opera community to move forward and fight to the end for the great socialist construction cause of the motherland.

### 首都文艺界举行纪念会  纪念梅兰芳、周信芳舞台生活五十年  文化部颁发给他们以荣誉奖状

1955-04-12
第3版()
专栏：

    首都文艺界举行纪念会
    纪念梅兰芳、周信芳舞台生活五十年
    文化部颁发给他们以荣誉奖状
    中华人民共和国文化部、中国文学艺术界联合会、中国戏剧家协会为纪念我国杰出的爱国主义、现实主义艺术家梅兰芳、周信芳舞台生活五十年联合主办的纪念大会，昨天（十一日）下午在首都天桥剧场举行。会上，中华人民共和国文化部并颁发给梅兰芳和周信芳以荣誉奖状。
    参加纪念会的，有首都文学、戏剧、电影、美术、音乐、舞蹈工作者共一千四百人。在京的苏联艺术专家、各国驻我国使馆文化参赞以及日本著名戏剧演员中村玩右卫门等国际友人也应邀出席。
    中国文学艺术界联合会秘书长、中国戏剧家协会常务理事阳翰笙致开会词。他说：今天的纪念会表现了新社会对人民自己的艺术家的热爱和鼓励，并将鼓励艺术工作者更好地执行毛泽东同志提出的文艺方针，更多地为工农兵服务。
    中华人民共和国文化部副部长夏衍在讲话中说：梅兰芳、周信芳两位先生在悠长的五十年中，以创造性的劳动，继承并且发扬了我国戏曲艺术的现实主义和爱国主义的优良传统；直到今天，他们不仅依然保持着京剧艺术的最高水平，而且继续向前发展，在他们的艺术生活的天地里，依然充满一片青春气象。夏衍说：戏剧艺术界应该重视他们和其他一些高年的优秀人民艺术家的现实主义表演艺术经验，学习他们演唱艺术的丰富经验和典型化、性格化的表演方法，使我们的现实主义艺术传统能得到不断的丰富、革新和发扬光大。
    接着，中国戏剧家协会副主席欧阳予倩作了关于梅兰芳的题为“真正的演员——美的创造者”的报告；中国戏剧家协会主席田汉作了题为“战斗的表演艺术家——周信芳”的报告。他们在报告中，扼要地介绍了梅兰芳和周信芳的经历，介绍了梅兰芳和周信芳的作为爱国主义艺术家的优良的品质，详尽地分析了他们的艺术成就和对中国戏曲艺术的贡献，号召戏曲工作者学习他们孜孜不倦、数十年如一日的虚心学习、忠实于艺术劳动和艺术改革的精神。
    中国戏曲研究院副院长、著名的戏剧家程砚秋，代表全体戏曲工作者在会上致贺词。
    文化部部长沈雁冰代表文化部在全场掌声雷动中颁发给梅兰芳、周信芳以荣誉奖状。
    首都年青一代的戏剧工作者上台给这两位卓越的戏剧先辈献花致敬。苏联戏剧专家列斯里教授代表在京的苏联艺术专家们献礼，并预祝他们为伟大的中国人民、为全人类作出更大的贡献。
    最后，由梅兰芳、周信芳致答词。他们在发言中都说：这个纪念会，不仅是个人的荣誉，而且是全国戏曲工作者共同的荣誉，他们都向中国共产党和毛主席致衷心的谢意。梅兰芳在叙述自己从事舞台生活五十年的感受之后，勉励青年戏曲工作者说：热爱你的工作，老老实实地从事学习，努力艺术实践，不断地劳动，不断地锻炼，不断地创造；不断地虚心接受群众意见，严格进行自我批评；为着人民，为着祖国灿烂美好的未来，贡献出我们的一切！周信芳说：梅先生和我虽然都是六十岁的人了，但我们的精神永远不老，我们愿与全国戏曲工作者同志携手前进，为祖国的伟大的社会主义建设事业奋斗到底。
