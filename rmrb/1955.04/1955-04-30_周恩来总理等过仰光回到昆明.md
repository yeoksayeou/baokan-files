Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and Others Passed Through Rangoon and Returned to Kunming
Author:
Date: 1955-04-30
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 29th—Rangoon dispatch: The delegation of the People's Republic of China attending the Asian-African Conference, led by Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Rangoon at 5 p.m. on the 28th on its way back to Beijing from Jakarta and left Rangoon for home at 8 a.m. on the 29th.
    When Premier Zhou Enlai and his party arrived and left Rangoon, the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, personally went to the airport to greet and see them off. On the evening of the 28th, Prime Minister U Nu also hosted a banquet to entertain Premier Zhou Enlai and other members of the delegation of the People's Republic of China.
    Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 29th—The delegation of the People's Republic of China attending the Asian-African Conference, headed by Premier Zhou Enlai, arrived in Kunming by plane at 2:15 p.m. on the 29th.
    Also arriving in Kunming on the same plane were: Chen Yi, Ye Jizhuang, Zhang Hanfu, representatives of the delegation of the People's Republic of China, and Liao Chengzhi, Yang Qiqing, Qiao Guanhua, Chen Jiakang, Huang Hua, Da Punsheng, advisors, and Wang Zhuoru, Secretary-General.
    Yao Zhongming, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Burma, also accompanied them on their return.
    Those who welcomed them at the airport were: Guo Yingqiu, Governor of Yunnan Province; Liu Minghui, Liu Daifeng, Gong Zizhi, and Wu Zuomin, Vice Governors; Xie Fuzhi, First Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China; Yu Yichuan, Second Secretary; Ma Jikong, Deputy Secretary; Qin Jiwei, Deputy Commander of the Kunming Military Region; Bai Xiaosong, Su Honggang, Xie Chongwen, heads of the Yunnan Provincial organizations of the Provincial Political Consultative Conference and various democratic parties, and responsible cadres of the government and various people's organizations, totaling more than 100 people.

### 周恩来总理等过仰光回到昆明

1955-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理等过仰光回到昆明
    新华社二十九日讯　仰光消息：中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来率领的中华人民共和国出席亚非会议代表团在从雅加达返北京途中，于二十八日下午五时抵达仰光，并已在二十九日晨八时离仰光返国。
    周恩来总理等一行在到达和离开仰光时，缅甸联邦总理吴努均亲到机场迎送，二十八日晚间吴努总理并曾设宴招待周恩来总理和中华人民共和国代表团其他人员。
    新华社昆明二十九日电　以周恩来总理为首的中华人民共和国出席亚非会议代表团在二十九日下午二时十五分乘飞机到达昆明。
    同机到达昆明的，有：中华人民共和国代表团代表陈毅、叶季壮、章汉夫，顾问廖承志、杨奇清、乔冠华、陈家康、黄华、达浦生，秘书长王倬如等。
    中华人民共和国驻缅甸大使姚仲明也陪同返国。
    到机场欢迎的，有：云南省省长郭影秋，副省长刘明辉、刘岱峰、龚自知、吴作民；中共云南省委员会第一书记谢富治，第二书记于一川，副书记马继孔；昆明军区副司令员秦基伟；省政协和各民主党派云南省组织的负责人白小松、苏鸿纲、谢崇文等，政府和各人民团体的负责干部共一百多人。
