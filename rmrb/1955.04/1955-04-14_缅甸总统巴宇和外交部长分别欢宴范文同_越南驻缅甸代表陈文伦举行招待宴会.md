Title: Burmese President Ba U and Foreign Minister Host Banquets for Pham Van Dong Separately, Vietnamese Representative in Burma Tran Van Luan Holds Reception

Author:
Date: 1955-04-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 13th.—Rangoon news: The President of the Union of Burma, Dr. Ba U, held a banquet at the Presidential Palace at noon on the 12th to entertain Pham Van Dong, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

On the same day at 6:00 PM, Burmese Foreign Minister Sao Khun Kyi hosted a cocktail reception at "Lam Chintzong Mansion" to welcome Pham Van Dong, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, on his visit to Burma. Burmese government ministers and senior officials from various departments were invited to attend. Ambassadors, ministers, and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in Burma were also invited to attend.

The banquet and reception took place in a friendly atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 13th.—Rangoon news: Tran Van Luan, representative of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Burma, held a reception at the Strand Hotel in Rangoon on the evening of the 12th for Vice Premier Pham Van Dong's visit to Burma.

Those invited to the banquet included Burmese Prime Minister U Nu, Foreign Minister Sao Khun Kyi, Kachin State Affairs Minister Duwa Zau Lawn, Burmese government officials, and Chairman of the Burmese Committee for the Defense of World Peace, Thakin Kodaw Hmaing, as well as figures from cultural and educational circles. The ambassadors of the People's Republic of China, the Soviet Union, and India stationed in Burma, the ministers of Israel and the Netherlands, and the chargé d'affaires of Indonesia, as well as diplomatic officials from various embassies, also attended the banquet.

During the banquet, Vice Premier Pham Van Dong had cordial conversations with the guests.

### 缅甸总统巴宇和外交部长分别欢宴范文同  越南驻缅甸代表陈文伦举行招待宴会

1955-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸总统巴宇和外交部长分别欢宴范文同
    越南驻缅甸代表陈文伦举行招待宴会
    新华社十三日讯　仰光消息：缅甸联邦总统巴宇博士在十二日中午在总统府设宴招待越南民主共和国副总理兼外交部长范文同。
    同日下午六时，缅甸外交部长藻昆卓在“林振宗大厦”举行了鸡尾酒会，欢迎越南民主共和国副总理兼外交部长范文同的访问缅甸。应邀出席的有缅甸政府部长和各部高级官员。应邀出席的还有各国驻缅甸的大使、公使和外交官员。
    宴会和招待会在友好空气中进行。
    新华社十三日讯　仰光消息：越南民主共和国驻缅甸代表陈文伦为范文同副总理的访问缅甸，十二日晚在仰光海滨旅馆举行招待宴会。
    应邀参加宴会的有缅甸联邦政府总理吴努、外交部长藻昆卓、克钦邦事务部长杜瓦藻朗、缅甸政府官员和缅甸保卫世界和平委员会主席德钦哥都迈和文化教育界人士。驻缅甸的中华人民共和国、苏联、印度的大使，以色列、荷兰的公使和印度尼西亚的代办以及各国使馆外交官员，也出席了宴会。
    席间，范文同副总理同来宾们作了亲切的交谈。
