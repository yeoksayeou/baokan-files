Title: Indonesian President Sukarno Hosts Delegations from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1955-04-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Bandung, April 19th: Indonesian President Sukarno held a reception at the Provincial Governor's residence on the evening of the 18th to welcome the delegations from various countries participating in the Asian-African Conference. Also attending the reception were observers attending the meeting, staff of the Joint Secretariat, and foreign and Indonesian journalists covering the conference in Bandung.

On the Indonesian side, senior Indonesian government officials, local government officials, and celebrities from all walks of life attended the reception.

Indonesian songs and dances were performed at the reception. The guests showed great interest in the Indonesian songs and dances.

While the reception was in progress, tens of thousands of people gathered outside the Provincial Governor's residence. As the guests passed by, they cheered loudly to express their welcome.

### 印度尼西亚总统苏加诺招待各国代表团

1955-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚总统苏加诺招待各国代表团
    新华社万隆十九日电　印度尼西亚总统苏加诺十八日晚在省长官邸举行招待会，欢迎参加亚非会议的各国代表团。出席招待会的还有列席会议的观察员、联合秘书处工作人员、在万隆采访会议新闻的外国和印度尼西亚记者。
    印度尼西亚方面出席招待会的有印度尼西亚政府高级官员、地方政府官员以及各界名流。
    招待会上表演了印度尼西亚歌舞。来宾们对印度尼西亚歌舞感到很大的兴趣。
    在招待会进行时，成千上万的人聚集在省长官邸外面。当来宾们走过时，他们高声欢呼表示欢迎。
