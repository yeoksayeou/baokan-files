    The treaty between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia on the issue of dual nationality was signed in Bandung on April 22nd by the plenipotentiary representatives of the two governments. Zhou Enlai (left), Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, signed on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China. Sunaryo (right), Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Indonesia, signed on behalf of the Government of the Republic of Indonesia.

专栏：

    出席亚非会议的我国代表团首席代表周恩来总理四月十九日下午在亚非会议全体会议上发言。
    新华社记者　钱嗣杰摄
    中华人民共和国和印度尼西亚共和国关于双重国籍问题的条约于四月二十二日在万隆由两国政府的全权代表签字。中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来（左边）代表中华人民共和国政府签字。印度尼西亚共和国外交部长苏纳约（右边）代表印度尼西亚共和国政府签字。
    新举社记者　钱嗣杰摄（无线电传真）
