Title: Trade Union Delegations from Various Countries Arrive in Beijing One After Another; British Women's Delegation Visits China
Author:
Date: 1955-04-27
Page: 4
	Xinhua News Agency, April 25th: The Korean Trade Union Delegation, headed by Jiang Xiemo, arrived in Beijing by train on the evening of the 25th, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to participate in the "May Day" celebrations and visit China. Li Zaiwen, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others went to the station to welcome them.
	Xinhua News Agency, April 25th: The Romanian Trade Union Delegation, headed by Andrei Ion, the French Trade Union Delegation (headed by Montmousseau, who arrived in Beijing earlier), and the Chilean Trade Union Delegation, headed by Gilberto Sea, as well as two Iranian trade union representatives and one Lebanese trade union representative, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 25th, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to participate in the "May Day" celebrations and visit China.
	Li Jiebo, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and the heads of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and various industrial trade unions went to the airport to welcome them.
	Xinhua News Agency, April 26th: The Indonesian Trade Union Delegation and trade union representatives, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the China Education Trade Union to participate in the "May Day" celebrations and visit China, arrived in Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 26th. They are: the Indonesian General Trade Union Delegation headed by Yoko Sujono, the Indonesian Teachers' Trade Union Delegation headed by Subiadynata, the Indonesian Trade Union Federation Delegation headed by Rachmad, the Indonesian People's Rights United Trade Union Delegation headed by Rasi Sultan Rajamas, the Indonesian Trade Union Federation Delegation headed by I.H. Jacob, the Indonesian Trade Union Congress Delegation headed by Rivai Atmachya, the Information Department Trade Union Delegation headed by Ismail Hassan, the Indonesian Bank Trade Union Delegation headed by Muhammad Ali, the Indonesian Customs Employee Trade Union representative Hacha Wiyoto, and the Indonesian Pawnbroking Industry Trade Union representative Sajono.
	The heads of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and industrial trade unions such as the China Education Trade Union welcomed them at the station.
	Xinhua News Agency, April 25th: The British Women's Delegation, invited by the All-China Democratic Women's Federation to visit China and participate in the "May Day" celebrations, arrived in Beijing on the 25th. The head of the delegation is Mrs. Frida Greenbo, Honorary Secretary of the British Women's Conference and Secretary of the National Association of British Children's Nurseries. Members of the delegation include: Mrs. Ashley, Miss Cannell, Mrs. Steel, Mrs. Dorothy Morgan, Mrs. Silcock, Mrs. Violet Stratton, etc.
	Li Dequan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and many others went to the airport to welcome them.

### 各国工会代表团陆续到京  英国妇女代表团来我国访问

1955-04-27
第4版()
专栏：

    各国工会代表团陆续到京
    英国妇女代表团来我国访问
    新华社二十五日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请，来我国参加“五一”节观礼和访问的以姜禼模为首的朝鲜工会代表团，在二十五日晚乘火车到达北京。前往车站欢迎的有：中华全国总工会书记处书记栗再温等人。
    新华社二十五日讯　应中华全国总工会的邀请，来我国参加“五一”节观礼和访问的以安德烈·伊昂为首的罗马尼亚工会代表团、法国工会代表团（团长蒙慕梭已先期到京）和以希尔伯托·塞阿为首的智利工会代表团，以及伊朗工会代表二人和黎巴嫩工会代表一人在二十五日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有：中华全国总工会书记处书记李颉伯，中华全国总工会国际联络部和各产业工会的负责人。
    新华社二十六日讯　应中华全国总工会和中国教育工会的邀请，来我国参加“五一”节观礼和访问的印度尼西亚各工会代表团和工会代表，在二十六日下午乘火车到达北京。他们是：以约柯苏佐诺为首的印度尼西亚总工会代表团、以苏比亚迪纳塔为首的印度尼西亚教师工会代表团、以拉奇马德为首的印度尼西亚工会联合会代表团、以拉席·苏丹拉查玛斯为首的印度尼西亚民权统一工会代表团、以伊·赫·雅各布为首的印度尼西亚职工联合会代表团、以里瓦伊·阿特马查为首的印度尼西亚工会大会代表团、以伊斯梅尔·哈桑为首的情报部工会代表团、以穆罕默德·阿里为首的印度尼西亚银行工会代表团、印度尼西亚关税雇员工会代表哈查·维约托和印度尼西亚典当业工会代表萨佐诺。
    到车站欢迎的，有中华全国总工会国际联络部和中国教育工会等产业工会的负责人。
    新华社二十五日讯　应中华全国民主妇女联合会邀请，前来我国访问并参加“五一”观礼的英国妇女代表团在二十五日到达北京。代表团团长为英国妇女大会名誉秘书长、英国儿童保育院全国协会秘书长弗里达·格林波夫人。代表团团员有：艾希莱夫人、坎内尔小姐、斯蒂尔夫人、道乐赛·摩根夫人、西尔考克夫人、维奥列特·斯特拉顿夫人等。
    前往机场欢迎的有，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全等多人。
