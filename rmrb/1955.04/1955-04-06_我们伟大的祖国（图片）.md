    Our Great Motherland
    The entire project of the state-owned Baotou Sugar Factory is nearing completion. After the factory goes into operation, it can process 1,000 tons of sugar beets per day, producing a large amount of sugar for the needs of the people. This is a scene of workers doing painting work.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营包头糖厂的全部工程即将完工。这个厂投入生产后，每天可处理一千吨甜菜，生产大量的砂糖供人民的需要。这是工人们正在进行粉饰工作的情形。
