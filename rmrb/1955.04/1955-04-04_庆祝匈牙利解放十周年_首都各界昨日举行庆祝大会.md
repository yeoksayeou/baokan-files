Title: Celebrating the Tenth Anniversary of the Liberation of Hungary, All Circles in the Capital Held a Celebration Meeting Yesterday
Author: 
Date: 1955-04-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 3rd. All circles in the capital held a celebration meeting last night to grandly celebrate the tenth anniversary of the liberation of Hungary. The celebration meeting was organized by the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China.

    Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, attended the celebration meeting.

    Also attending the celebration meeting were: He Long and Chen Yi, Vice Premiers of the State Council, Bo Yibo, Director of the State Construction Commission, Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, as well as ministers and vice ministers of various ministries, and deputy directors of various commissions.

    Also attending the celebration meeting were: Li Jishen, Huang Yanpei, and Peng Zhen, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Wang Kunlun, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and heads of various people's organizations, and more than 600 people from scientific, cultural, artistic, and journalistic circles in the capital.

    Skladan, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Hungary to China, and officials of the embassy were invited to attend the meeting.

    Also attending the celebration meeting were diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries to China. Minister Shen Yanbing presided over the celebration meeting. At the beginning of the meeting, the band played the national anthems of Hungary and China. Bo Yibo, Director of the State Construction Commission of the People's Republic of China, spoke amidst warm applause (full text on page 3).

    Skladan, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Hungary to China, then spoke (full text on page 3), and warm applause repeatedly resounded throughout the hall.

    At the celebration meeting, Hungarian musicians and the Central Song and Dance Troupe finally performed wonderful musical and dance programs.

### 庆祝匈牙利解放十周年  首都各界昨日举行庆祝大会

1955-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝匈牙利解放十周年
    首都各界昨日举行庆祝大会
    据新华社三日讯　首都各界三日晚举行庆祝大会，隆重庆祝匈牙利解放十周年。庆祝大会由中华人民共和国文化部举办。
    中华人民共和国副主席朱德出席了庆祝大会。
    出席庆祝大会的还有：国务院副总理贺龙、陈毅，国家建设委员会主任薄一波，文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，以及各部部长、副部长，各委员会副主任。
    出席庆祝大会的还有：全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、黄炎培、彭真，北京市副市长王昆仑，及其各人民团体负责人，首都科学、文化、艺术、新闻界人士等六百多人。
    匈牙利人民共和国驻我国大使斯克拉丹和使馆官员应邀出席了大会。
    出席庆祝大会的还有各国驻华使节和外交官员。庆祝大会由沈雁冰部长主持。大会开始，乐队奏匈、中两国国歌。中华人民共和国国家建设委员会主任薄一波在热烈掌声中讲话（全文见第三版）。
    匈牙利人民共和国驻我国大使斯克拉丹接着讲话（全文见第三版），全场一再响起热烈的掌声。
    在庆祝大会上，最后由匈牙利音乐家和中央歌舞团表演了精采的音乐歌舞节目。
