Title: Soviet Academy of Sciences and Polish Academy of Sciences Delegations Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-04-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 26th: The delegation of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, headed by Academician I.P. Bardin, Vice President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, arrived in Beijing by train on the afternoon of April 26th, at the invitation of the Chinese Academy of Sciences for a visit to China.

The delegation members include: Academicians of the Soviet Academy of Sciences I.P. Gerasimov, M.P. Kostenko, A.G. Betekhtin, Corresponding Members V.V. Belousov, E.N. Mishustin, I.V. Tananaev, Professor L.L. Voronin, Researchers R.V. Astafiev, and A.N. Cherkashen.

Those who greeted them at the station included: Chen Boda, Zhang Jiafu, Zhu Kezhen, Wu Youxun, Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and heads of various departments, Liang Xi, Chairman of the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization, and well-known figures from the capital's scientific community, including Fan Wenlan, Pan Zinian, Zheng Zhenduo, Yan Jici, Xu Jie, and many others.

Also greeting them at the station were: Xia Yun, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Heavy Industry of the People's Republic of China, Wang Daohan, Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building, Liu Lanbo, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Fuel Industry, He Changgong, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Geology, Fu Lianzhang, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Health, and Wu Han, Vice Mayor of Beijing.

Also greeting them at the station were: Hong Shen, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Li Zhanwu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Ma Yinchu, President of Peking University, and heads of other institutions of higher learning.

Academician Yudin, the Soviet Ambassador to China, and embassy staff members also greeted them at the station.

After the members of the Soviet Academy of Sciences delegation, headed by Academician Bardin, got off the train, they shook hands with the greeters one by one.

At the station, Zhu Kezhen, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, delivered a welcoming speech, and Academician Bardin delivered a reply. They wished the fraternal friendship between Chinese and Soviet scientists would be increasingly consolidated and developed, and wished that this friendship would bear rich fruits for the development of the common cause of China and the Soviet Union, for the promotion of world peace, and for the benefit of mankind.

Xinhua News Agency, April 25th: The delegation of the Polish Academy of Sciences, headed by Academician Wierzbicki, Vice President of the Polish Academy of Sciences and a civil engineer, and members: Academician Kuratowski, a mathematician, Corresponding Member Nowacki, a civil engineer, and Professor Rozinski, a historian, and three others, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 24th. Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and a physicist, and more than 20 people from the capital's scientific community, including Pan Zinian, Zhou Peiyuan, and Qian Weichang, welcomed them at the airport.

On the afternoon of the 25th, the Chinese Academy of Sciences held a welcome reception to welcome the four Polish scientists. The reception was presided over by Wu Youxun, and attended by more than 30 people, including well-known scientists in Beijing and heads of various departments of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

### 苏联科学院和波兰科学院代表团到京

1955-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联科学院和波兰科学院代表团到京
    新华社二十六日讯　以苏联科学院副院长伊·普·巴尔金院士为首的苏联科学院代表团，应中国科学院邀请来我国访问，已在二十六日下午乘火车到达北京。
    代表团团员包括：苏联科学院院士伊·普·格拉西莫夫、麦·普·科斯琴科、阿·格·别捷赫金，通讯院士维·维·别洛乌索夫、叶·纳·米舒斯金、伊·维·塔纳纳耶夫，教授勒·鲁·沃罗宁，研究员鲁·维·阿斯塔菲耶夫、阿·纳·契尔卡申。
    到车站欢迎的有：中国科学院副院长陈伯达、张稼夫、竺可桢、吴有训和各部门负责人，中华全国科学技术普及协会主席梁希，首都科学界著名人士范文澜、潘梓年、郑振铎、严济慈、许杰等多人。
    到车站欢迎的还有：中华人民共和国重工业部副部长夏耘，第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵，燃料工业部副部长刘澜波，地质部副部长何长工，卫生部副部长傅连暲和北京市副市长吴晗。
    到车站欢迎的还有：中国人民对外文化协会副会长洪深，中苏友好协会总会副秘书长李霑吾，北京大学校长马寅初和其他高等院校的负责人。
    苏联驻我国大使尤金院士和使馆人员也到车站欢迎。
    以巴尔金院士为首的苏联科学院代表团人员下车后，与欢迎者一一握手。
    在车站上，中国科学院副院长竺可桢致欢迎词，巴尔金院士致答词。他们祝中苏两国科学家之间兄弟般的友谊日益巩固和发展，祝这种友谊对于发展中苏两国的共同事业，对于促进世界和平和造福人类，结出丰硕的果实。
    新华社二十五日讯　波兰科学院代表团团长、波兰科学院副院长、土木工程学家维日比茨基院士，团员：数学家库拉托夫斯基院士，土木工程学家诺瓦茨基通讯院士，历史学家罗津斯基教授等四人，已于二十四日乘飞机抵京，到机场欢迎的有中国科学院副院长、物理学家吴有训和首都科学界潘梓年、周培源、钱伟长等二十余人。
    二十五日下午，中国科学院举行欢迎会，欢迎四位波兰科学家。欢迎会由吴有训主持，参加的有在北京的著名科学家以及中国科学院各部门负责人共三十多人。
