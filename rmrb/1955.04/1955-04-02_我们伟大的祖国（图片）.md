Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-04-02

Our Great Motherland
Troops from six divisions of the Chinese People's Volunteers have begun to return to the motherland from Korea in succession. This is the train carrying a unit of the Volunteers arriving in Andong City, where Young Pioneers are welcoming them.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-04-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国人民志愿军六个师的部队已开始从朝鲜陆续返回祖国。这是志愿军某部所乘列车到达安东市，少年先锋队员们在欢迎他们。
