Title: Our Army Shot Down and Damaged One Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Plane Each that Intruded into Guangdong

Author: 
Date: 1955-04-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, April 25th. Our People's Liberation Army anti-aircraft artillery units shot down and damaged one Chiang Kai-shek bandit plane each on the 24th, which intruded into the airspace over the coastal areas of Guangdong.

　　From 13:36 to 15:49 on the 24th, a total of five Chiang Kai-shek army F-84 and RT-33 aircraft, in two batches, intruded into the airspace over the coastal areas of Shantou City and Chenghai County, Guangdong Province. Our People's Liberation Army anti-aircraft artillery units opened fire fiercely, immediately shooting down and damaging one Chiang Kai-shek bandit F-84 aircraft each. The downed enemy plane crashed into the sea 20 kilometers east of Nan'ao Island, and the damaged one flew away trailing smoke and fire.

### 我军击落击伤窜扰广东的蒋贼飞机各一架

1955-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我军击落击伤窜扰广东的蒋贼飞机各一架
    新华社广州二十五日电　我人民解放军高射炮部队二十四日击落、击伤窜扰广东沿海地区上空的蒋贼飞机各一架。
    二十四日十三时三十六分到十五时四十九分，蒋贼军F—八四型和RT—三三型飞机共五架，分两批窜扰广东省汕头市及澄海县一带沿海地区上空。我人民解放军高射炮部队猛烈开火，当即击落、击伤蒋贼F—八四型飞机各一架。击落的一架敌机坠入南澳岛以东二十公里的海中，击伤的一架拖着烟火飞逃。
