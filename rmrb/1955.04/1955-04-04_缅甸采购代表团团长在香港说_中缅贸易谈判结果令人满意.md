Title: Burmese Purchasing Delegation Head Says in Hong Kong: Sino-Burmese Trade Negotiation Results Satisfactory
Author:
Date: 1955-04-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd -- According to Hong Kong's "Ta Kung Pao" report: The head of the Burmese Federal Government Purchasing Delegation, Burmese Minister of Public Works and Reconstruction U Ba Maung Gale, told reporters in Hong Kong on March 31 that the trade negotiations with the representatives of the People's Republic of China went very smoothly and the results were completely satisfactory. He said that this kind of trade is mutually beneficial. The quality and price of Chinese products are satisfactory. He said that various steel products, construction materials, cotton yarn, and other commodities purchased by the Burmese Federation from China will be used in construction.

### 缅甸采购代表团团长在香港说  中缅贸易谈判结果令人满意

1955-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸采购代表团团长在香港说
    中缅贸易谈判结果令人满意
    新华社二日讯　据香港“大公报”报道：缅甸联邦政府采购代表团团长、缅甸公共工程和恢复重建部部长波敏贡三月三十一日在香港对记者们发表谈话说：这次同中华人民共和国政府的代表进行的贸易谈判，进行很顺利，结果完全令人满意。他说，这种贸易是互利的。中国产品的质量和价格都令人满意。他说，缅甸联邦从中国购入的各种钢材、建筑器材、棉纱等商品，将用到建设方面去。
