    The delegation of the People's Republic of China attending the Asian-African Conference, led by Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Bandung by plane on April 17. Indonesian Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo (wearing a dark hat on the right) was at the airport to welcome them. Xinhua News Agency reporter Qian Sijie (faxed manuscript)
