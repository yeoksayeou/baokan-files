Title: Citizens of Bangalore, India, Hold Rally Supporting the Asian Countries Conference and the Asian-African Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-04-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 5th. Citizens of Bangalore, the capital of Mysore State, India, held a grand rally on the 3rd in support of the upcoming Asian Countries Conference in Delhi and the Asian-African Conference in Bandung.

S. Sastri, the chairman of the meeting, said at the meeting that the purpose of the Asian-African Conference and the Asian Countries Conference is to consolidate the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. Chief Minister of Mysore State, Hanumanthaiya, delivered a speech at the meeting, hoping that the two conferences would be successful in creating a peaceful atmosphere throughout the world.

The meeting passed a resolution wishing the Asian Countries Conference and the Asian-African Conference success.

### 印度班加罗尔市民举行集会  支持亚洲国家会议和亚非会议

1955-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

    印度班加罗尔市民举行集会
    支持亚洲国家会议和亚非会议
    新华社新德里五日电　印度买索尔邦首府班加罗尔的市民在三日举行了一次盛大的集会，支持即将在德里举行的亚洲国家会议和在万隆举行的亚非会议。
    会议的主持人斯·萨斯特里在会上说，亚非会议和亚洲国家会议的目的在于巩固和平共处的五项原则。买索尔邦首席部长哈努曼塔亚在会上发表了演说，希望这两个会议在世界各地创造和平气氛方面获得成功。
    会议通过决议，预祝亚洲国家会议和亚非会议获得成功。
