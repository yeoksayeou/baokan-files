Title: At the Closing Session of the Asian Countries Conference on April 10, Delegates Authorized the Presidium to Prepare for the Establishment of a Liaison Agency
Author:
Date: 1955-04-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 11th. The Asian Countries Conference, which opened in New Delhi on April 6th, closed on April 10th. Representatives and observers from sixteen countries including Burma, Cambodia, Ceylon, China, Egypt, India, Japan, Jordan, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Lebanon, Mongolia, Nepal, Pakistan, Syria, the Soviet Union, and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam participated in the work of this conference.

This conference was presided over by Mrs. Rameshwari Nehru, head of the Indian delegation.

The representatives unanimously adopted the resolutions on political, cultural, scientific, economic, and social issues put forward by the Presidium, and unanimously adopted the appeal by the religious representatives attending the conference for cooperation among believers in Asian countries in striving for peace.

When the resolution on granting the People's Republic of China its legitimate seat in the United Nations was adopted, the representatives stood up and applauded warmly in welcome.

Dr. Anup Singh, the Indian representative, announced at the meeting that the representatives had authorized the Presidium to hold further discussions to devise a method for the contacts already established between the delegations of various countries to continue through a liaison agency. The representatives also requested the Presidium to draft a resolution on promoting friendship between India and Pakistan.

Following this, Mrs. Rameshwari Nehru delivered a closing speech. She expressed the hope that this conference would greatly help to ease international tensions, and expressed her belief that the friendship of the Asian people reflected in this conference would grow even further.

At the beginning of the meeting, a large group of children from Delhi and representatives from several women's organizations in Delhi congratulated the Presidium and the representatives. Guo Moruo, head of the Chinese delegation, on behalf of the Presidium and all the representatives, expressed his gratitude to the Indian children and women for their congratulations.

At the end of the meeting, the representatives enthusiastically shouted slogans: "Long live the Asian Countries Conference!" "Long live world peace!" "Long live the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence!" "Long live Asian solidarity!"

### 在亚洲国家会议四月十日闭幕会议上  各国代表授权主席团筹备建立联络机构

1955-04-12
第4版()
专栏：

    在亚洲国家会议四月十日闭幕会议上
    各国代表授权主席团筹备建立联络机构
    新华社新德里十一日电　四月六日在新德里开幕的亚洲国家会议已经在四月十日闭幕。参加这次会议工作的有缅甸、柬埔寨、锡兰、中国、埃及、印度、日本、约旦、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、黎巴嫩、蒙古、尼泊尔、巴基斯坦、叙利亚、苏联、越南民主共和国等十六个国家的代表和观察员。
    这次会议由印度代表团团长拉米希瓦里·尼赫鲁夫人主持。
    代表们一致通过了主席团提出的关于政治问题、文化问题、科学问题、经济问题和社会问题的决议，并且一致通过了出席会议的宗教界的代表提出的关于亚洲国家的教徒为争取和平而进行合作的呼吁书。
    在通过关于给予中华人民共和国以在联合国中的合法地位的决议时，代表们起立热烈鼓掌表示欢迎。
    印度代表阿努普·辛格博士在会上宣布，代表们授权主席团举行进一步讨论以拟出办法使各国代表团之间已经建立起来的接触通过联络机构继续下去。代表们还要求主席团草拟关于促进印度—巴基斯坦的友谊的决议。
    接着，拉米希瓦里·尼赫鲁夫人致闭幕词。她表示希望这次会议将大大有助于缓和国际紧张局势，并且表示相信，在这次会议上反映出来的亚洲人民的友谊将更进一步地增长。
    在会议开始的时候，一大群德里的儿童和德里的若干妇女组织的代表向主席团和代表们祝贺。中国代表团团长郭沫若代表主席团和全体代表对印度儿童和妇女的祝贺表示感谢。
    在会议结束时，代表们热烈地高呼口号：“亚洲国家会议万岁！”“世界和平万岁！”“和平共处五项原则万岁！”“亚洲团结万岁！”
