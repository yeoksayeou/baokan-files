Title: Czechoslovakia Holds Third Congress for the Defence of Peace
Author:
Date: 1955-04-21
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, April 18th: The Third Czechoslovak Congress for the Defence of Peace opened in Prague on the 17th. More than two thousand peace fighters from all parts of the country participated in the congress, as well as representatives from the World Peace Council, and delegations from the Soviet Union, Poland, France, Britain, the German Democratic Republic, West Germany, and other countries.
    Josef Hromádka, a member of the Presidium of the Czechoslovak Committee for the Defence of Peace, delivered a report at the meeting on the work of the Czechoslovak Committee for the Defence of Peace and the new tasks of the national peace movement.
    Speaking of the situation in the Far East, Hromádka said that the United States' desperate attempts to turn Taiwan into a military fortress and blockade the Chinese mainland are a serious provocation to the Chinese people. Common sense and conscience cannot understand why American interests should be defended in Taiwan, or why China's demands for Taiwan are a threat to the "free world." Moreover, no one understands how it is possible to refuse to grant China its seat in the United Nations.
    The congress passed a resolution expressing the firm determination of the Czechoslovak people to fight against the remilitarization of West Germany and against the preparation of atomic warfare.
    The resolution stated: We warmly welcome the great progress made by the Soviet Union in the peaceful use of atomic energy and thank it for its generous assistance to us in this regard. We welcome the decision of the World Peace Council to hold the World Peace Congress in Helsinki.
    The congress elected a new Committee for the Defence of Peace.

### 捷克斯洛伐克举行第三次保卫和平大会

1955-04-21
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克举行第三次保卫和平大会
    新华社布拉格十八日电　捷克斯洛伐克第三次保卫和平大会十七日在布拉格举行。参加大会的有来自全国各地的两千多个和平战士，以及世界和平理事会的代表，苏联、波兰、法国、英国、德意志民主共和国、西德和其他国家的代表团。
    捷克斯洛伐克保卫和平委员会主席团委员约·赫鲁玛德卡在会上作了关于捷克斯洛伐克保卫和平委员会的工作和全国和平运动的新任务的报告。
    赫鲁玛德卡在谈到远东局势时说，美国拼命把台湾变成军事堡垒和封锁中国大陆的行动是对中国人民的严重挑衅。常识和良知都不能了解为什么要在台湾保卫美国的利益，为什么中国对台湾的要求是对“自由世界”的威胁。而且谁也不了解怎么能够拒绝给予中国在联合国组织中的席位。
    大会通过了一项决议，决议表示了捷克斯洛伐克人民为反对西德重新军国主义化和反对准备原子战争而斗争的坚定决心。
    决议说，我们热烈欢迎苏联在和平使用原子能方面所获得的巨大进展，并且感谢它在这方面给我们的慷慨帮助。我们欢迎世界和平理事会关于在赫尔辛基举行世界和平大会的决定。
    大会选出了新的保卫和平委员会。
