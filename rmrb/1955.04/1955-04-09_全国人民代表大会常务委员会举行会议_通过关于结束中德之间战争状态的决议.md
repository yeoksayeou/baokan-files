Title: Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Holds Meeting; Resolution on Ending the State of War Between China and Germany Adopted
Author:
Date: 1955-04-09
Page: 1

    Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Holds Meeting
    Resolution on Ending the State of War Between China and Germany Adopted
    Xinhua News Agency, April 8th - The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its ninth meeting on April 7th.
    The meeting, based on the proposal submitted by the State Council, adopted the Resolution on Ending the State of War between the People's Republic of China and Germany.
    The meeting also heard a report by Zeng Xisheng, a member of the Anhui Provincial People's Committee, on the three agricultural production reform efforts being implemented in Anhui Province, and approved appointments, approvals of appointments, and several other items.
    Xinhua News Agency, April 8th - The ninth meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on April 7th approved the appointment of Jiang Wen, Zhu Jianming, Lü Wanji, Hou Zheng, and Zhao Pengfei as procurators of the Supreme People's Procuratorate.

### 全国人民代表大会常务委员会举行会议  通过关于结束中德之间战争状态的决议

1955-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常务委员会举行会议
    通过关于结束中德之间战争状态的决议
    新华社八日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在四月七日举行第九次会议。
    会议根据国务院提出的议案，通过了关于结束中华人民共和国同德国之间的战争状态的决议。
    会议还听了安徽省人民委员会委员曾希圣关于安徽省实行农业生产的三项改革工作的报告，并通过任命和批准任命的事项和其他事项多起。
    新华社八日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会四月七日第九次会议通过任命江文、朱剑明、吕万吉、侯政、赵鹏飞为最高人民检察院检察员。
