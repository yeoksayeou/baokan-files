Title: U Nu and Pham Van Dong to Lead Burmese and Vietnamese Delegations to the Asian-African Conference Respectively
Author:
Date: 1955-04-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 3rd dispatch: According to a PTI report from Rangoon: The Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, will lead the Burmese delegation to the Asian-African Conference to be held in Bandung, Indonesia, on April 18. The Burmese delegation will include the Minister of Information of the Union of Burma, U Tun Win, the Minister of Trade, U Raschid, and ten others.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 3rd: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu and his wife flew back to Rangoon on the 2nd after a week-long visit to India.

Before leaving India, Prime Minister U Nu told reporters in Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh, on the 1st that the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence are an effective way to consolidate world peace.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 3rd: According to the Vietnam News Agency, Hanoi, April 3rd: Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, will lead the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the upcoming Asian-African Conference in Bandung.

### 吴努、范文同将分别率领缅甸、越南代表团出席亚非会议

1955-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    吴努、范文同将分别率领缅甸、越南代表团出席亚非会议
    新华社三日讯　据印度报业托辣斯仰光消息：缅甸联邦总理吴努将率领缅甸代表团出席将于四月十八日在印度尼西亚的万隆举行的亚非会议。缅甸代表团将包括缅甸联邦政府宣传部长吴顿温和贸易部长吴拉实等十二人。
    新华社新德里三日电　缅甸总理吴努和他的夫人在印度进行了一个星期的访问以后，已在二日乘机飞回到仰光。
    吴努总理在离开印度以前，一日曾在印度北方省的哥拉克浦尔对新闻记者们说，和平共处的五项原则是巩固世界和平的有效方法。
    新华社三日讯　据越南通讯社河内三日讯：越南民主共和国副总理兼外交部长范文同将率领越南民主共和国代表团出席即将在万隆举行的亚非会议。
