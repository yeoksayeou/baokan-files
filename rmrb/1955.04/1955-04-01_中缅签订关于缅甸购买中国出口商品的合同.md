Title: China and Burma Sign Contract on Burma's Purchase of Chinese Export Goods
Author:
Date: 1955-04-01
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 31st. According to the provisions of the Trade Agreement between the People's Republic of China and the Union of Burma on the exchange of Burmese rice for Chinese export commodities in November 1954, the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China and the Government Procurement Delegation of the Union of Burma conducted negotiations on the Burmese government procurement of Chinese export commodities, and reached an agreement on March 28 in a friendly and harmonious atmosphere, and signed three contracts for the purchase of Chinese hardware and steel, building materials, sanitary equipment, paper, cotton yarn, raw silk, etc.
    Signing the above contracts on behalf of the Chinese side were: Zhou Huamin, Manager of China National Metals and Electrical Machinery Import Corporation, Sun Liji, Deputy Manager of China National Sundries Export Corporation, and Liu Jingtao, Deputy Manager of China National Silk Corporation. Signing the contracts on behalf of the Burmese side was U Ye Gaung, Secretary of the Government Procurement Delegation of the Union of Burma.
    After the signing, Li Zheren, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, on behalf of Minister Ye Jizhuang, exchanged letters with Bo Min Gaung, Head of the Government Procurement Delegation of the Union of Burma and Minister of Public Works and Reconstruction of the Government of the Union of Burma, regarding the confirmation of the Joint Communique.
    The following attended the signing ceremony on the Chinese side: Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Kong Yuan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and other responsible persons from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Ministry of Foreign Trade.
    The following attended the signing ceremony on the Burmese side: U Hla Maung, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Union of Burma to China, all members of the Government Procurement Delegation of the Union of Burma, and personnel from the Embassy of the Union of Burma in China.

### 中缅签订关于缅甸购买中国出口商品的合同

1955-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

    中缅签订关于缅甸购买中国出口商品的合同
    新华社三十一日讯　根据一九五四年十一月中华人民共和国和缅甸联邦关于缅甸大米同中国出口商品换货议定书的规定，中华人民共和国对外贸易部和缅甸联邦政府采购代表团进行了关于缅甸联邦政府采购中国出口商品的谈判，已于三月二十八日在友好融洽气氛中达成协议，并分别签订了购买中国五金钢材、建筑器材、卫生设备、纸张、棉纱、生丝等三个合同。
    代表中国方面分别在上述合同上签字的是：中国五金电工进口公司经理周化民、中国杂品出口公司副经理孙立基、中国丝绸公司副经理刘敬陶。代表缅甸方面在合同上签字的是缅甸联邦政府采购代表团秘书吴容模。
    签字后由中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长李哲人代表叶季壮部长同缅甸联邦政府采购代表团团长、缅甸联邦政府公共工程和恢复重建部部长波敏贡交换了关于确认联合公报的函件。
    参加签字典礼的：中国方面有外交部副部长章汉夫，对外贸易部副部长孔原，及外交部、对外贸易部的其他负责人员。
    缅甸方面参加签字典礼的有缅甸联邦驻我国特命全权大使吴拉茂，缅甸联邦政府采购代表团全体团员和缅甸联邦驻我国大使馆人员。
