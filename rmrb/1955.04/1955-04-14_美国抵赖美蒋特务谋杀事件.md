Title: The United States Denies the US-Chiang Kai-shek Clique's Special Agents' Murder Plot

Author:

Date: 1955-04-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 13th. The US State Department spokesman on the 13th denied the fact that American and Chiang Kai-shek special agents murdered personnel and reporters from the Chinese delegation attending the Asian-African Conference.

However, the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique dared not deny this murder incident. According to a United Press International report from Taipei on April 13th, when reporters asked the Chiang gang’s "Foreign Ministry" spokesman about this murder, he refused to comment.

### 美国抵赖美蒋特务谋杀事件

1955-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    美国抵赖美蒋特务谋杀事件
    新华社十三日讯　美国国务院发言人十三日抵赖美国和蒋介石特务谋杀中国出席亚非会议代表团人员和记者的事实。
    但是，蒋介石卖国集团不敢抵赖这一谋杀事件。据合众社台北十三日消息，蒋贼“外交部”的发言人在有记者问到这一谋杀事件时，拒绝表示意见。
