Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-04-26
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Workers in various regions are enthusiastically launching labor competitions to welcome the "May 1st" International Labor Day. The No. 55015 scraper-loader of the Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway Mechanical Road Construction Team has been operating safely for more than 4,000 hours, saving the state more than 32,000 yuan in repair costs. Now, the drivers and mechanical repairmen have held meetings and proposed to do a good job in machinery maintenance and save gasoline consumption to welcome their festival.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    各地工人正在热烈展开劳动竞赛，迎接“五一”国际劳动节。兰新铁路机械筑路队五五○一五号铲运机已安全运转了四千多小时，为国家节省了修理费用三万二千多元。现在驾驶员和机械修理员们又开会提出要做好机械保养工作、节省汽油消耗来迎接自己的节日。
