Title: Nehru Receives Heads of Delegations and Others from Asian Countries Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-04-11
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 10th: Indian Prime Minister Nehru received the heads of delegations and some 50 delegates from the Asian Countries Conference yesterday afternoon.
    The Chinese representatives who were received together with Head of Delegation Guo Moruo included Liao Chengzhi, Ding Xilin, Saifudin, and Chen Hansheng.

### 尼赫鲁接见亚洲国家会议各国代表团长等

1955-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁接见亚洲国家会议各国代表团长等
    新华社新德里十日电　印度总理尼赫鲁九日下午接见了参加亚洲国家会议的各国代表团团长和部分代表五十人。
    同郭沫若团长一起被接见的中国代表有廖承志、丁西林、赛福鼎、陈翰笙。
