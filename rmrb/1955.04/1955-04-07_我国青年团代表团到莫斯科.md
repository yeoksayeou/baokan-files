Title: Chinese Youth League Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1955-04-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 6th: A twenty-member delegation of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, led by Head of Delegation Hu Keshi, arrived in Moscow by plane on the 4th at the invitation of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union.

The delegation will conduct a tour of the Soviet Union for approximately two months to gain a broad understanding of the lives of Soviet youth and learn from the work experience of the Komsomol.

### 我国青年团代表团到莫斯科

1955-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我国青年团代表团到莫斯科
    新华社莫斯科六日电　中国新民主主义青年团代表团一行二十人，应苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会的邀请，在团长胡克实率领下，于四日乘飞机抵达莫斯科。
    代表团将在苏联进行为期约两个月的参观访问，广泛了解苏联青年的生活，学习共青团的工作经验。
