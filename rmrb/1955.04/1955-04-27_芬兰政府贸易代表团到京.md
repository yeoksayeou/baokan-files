Title: Finnish Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-04-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, April 25th

    The Finnish government trade delegation, led by Mr. R. Enckell, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of April 25th.
    Liu Xiwen, Deputy Director of the Third Bureau of our Ministry of Foreign Trade, and Li Kefu, our Commercial Counselor to the Finnish Embassy currently in Beijing, were at the airport to welcome them.
    Mr. Seireke, Counselor, and Mr. Mattila, Commercial Counselor of the Finnish Embassy in China, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 芬兰政府贸易代表团到京

1955-04-27
第4版()
专栏：

    芬兰政府贸易代表团到京
    新华社二十五日讯　芬兰政府贸易代表团由团长鲁·恩克尔先生率领，在四月二十五日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场迎接的有我对外贸易部第三局副局长刘希文及现在北京的我国驻芬兰大使馆商务专员李克夫等。
    芬兰驻华大使馆参赞谢列克及商务参赞马狄拉也前往机场迎接。
