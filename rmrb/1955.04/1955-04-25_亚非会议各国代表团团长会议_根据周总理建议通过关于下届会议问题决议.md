Title: Asian-African Conference Heads of Delegations Meeting: Resolution on the Next Meeting Adopted Based on Premier Zhou's Suggestion
Author:
Date: 1955-04-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Bandung, April 24th. The meeting of heads of delegations attending the Asian-African Conference continued from early morning to 5:00 PM on the 24th.

    Zhou Enlai, the chief representative of the Chinese delegation, suggested at the meeting that the Asian-African Conference should be held again in the future. Based on this suggestion, the meeting of heads of delegations adopted the following resolution and included it in the joint communiqué:

    "The Asian-African Conference suggests that the five sponsoring countries, in consultation with the participating countries, consider the issue of convening the next session of the Asian-African Conference."

### 亚非会议各国代表团团长会议  根据周总理建议通过关于下届会议问题决议

1955-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    亚非会议各国代表团团长会议
    根据周总理建议通过关于下届会议问题决议
    新华社万隆二十四日电　出席亚非会议的各国代表团团长会议在二十四日从早晨一直继续到下午五时。
    中国代表团首席代表周恩来在会上建议亚非会议在将来应再次举行。各国代表团团长会议根据这一建议通过了下列决议并把它列入联合公报中：
    “亚非会议建议五个发起国在同与会国协商之下，考虑召开亚非会议下届会议的问题。”
