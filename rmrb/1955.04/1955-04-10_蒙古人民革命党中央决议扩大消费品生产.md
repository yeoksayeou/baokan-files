Title: Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party Central Committee Resolution on Expanding Production of Consumer Goods
Author:
Date: 1955-04-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, April 7th (TASS): Mongolian newspapers published the resolution of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party on increasing the variety and improving the quality of consumer goods produced by domestic enterprises.

    In order to more fully meet the growing material and cultural needs of the working people, the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party passed a resolution to expand the production of the most needed goods for residents. The resolution paid special attention to the issue of improving product quality.

    Many domestic enterprises will organize the production of new types of consumer goods.

### 蒙古人民革命党中央决议扩大消费品生产

1955-04-10
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民革命党中央决议扩大消费品生产
    新华社八日讯　塔斯社乌兰巴托七日讯：蒙古报纸刊载了蒙古人民革命党中央委员会关于增加国内企业生产的消费品种类和改进质量的决议。
    为了更充分地满足劳动人民不断增长的物质和文化需求，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会通过了扩大居民最需要的物品的生产的决议。决议特别注意到改进产品质量的问题。
    国内的许多企业将组织新种类消费品的生产。
