Title: Rakosi Receives Deng Zihui
Author:
Date: 1955-04-04
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, April 3rd: Mátyás Rákosi, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, received Deng Zihui, head of the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China and Vice Premier of the State Council, on the 2nd, who was attending the celebrations for the 10th anniversary of the liberation of Hungary.
    Hao Deqing, China's Ambassador to Hungary, was also present at the reception.
    Also present at the reception were Antal Apró, Acting Chairman of the Hungarian Council of Ministers, and Rajk Ács, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party.

### 拉科西接见邓子恢

1955-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    拉科西接见邓子恢
    新华社三日讯　布达佩斯消息：匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会第一书记马·拉科西二日接见了参加匈牙利解放十周年庆祝典礼的中华人民共和国政府代表团团长、国务院副总理邓子恢。
    随同受接见的有我国驻匈牙利大使郝德青。
    接见时在座的有匈牙利部长会议代理主席安·赫格居斯和劳动人民党中央委员会书记处书记拉·阿奇。
