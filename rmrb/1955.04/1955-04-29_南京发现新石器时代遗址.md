Title: Neolithic Site Discovered in Nanjing
Author:
Date: 1955-04-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency:

    Neolithic Site Discovered in Nanjing
    More than 1,000 pieces of stone tools, pottery, bone tools, and a large number of animal bones have been excavated from the Beiyinyangying construction site in Nanjing. According to preliminary research by the East China Cultural Relics Work Team, this is a Neolithic site. The unearthed cultural relics have been on display in Nanjing since April 21st.

### 南京发现新石器时代遗址

1955-04-29
第2版()
专栏：

    南京发现新石器时代遗址
    南京市内北阴阳营建筑工地发掘出一千多件石器、陶器、骨器以及大量兽骨。据华东文物工作队初步研究，这是一处新石器时代的遗址。出土文物，从四月二十一日起在南京市展览。　　　　（新华社）
