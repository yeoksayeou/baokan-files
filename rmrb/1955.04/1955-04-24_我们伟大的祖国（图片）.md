Our Great Motherland
1955 is a crucial year for our country to complete its first five-year economic construction plan. In the first quarter of this year, many factories and mines across the country have completed or exceeded their state plans. The workers of the First Automobile Manufacturing Plant exceeded the construction plan by 142% during winter construction and have now switched to normal construction. The workers of the 106 work zone are pouring concrete.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-04-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    一九五五年是我国完成第一个五年经济建设计划关键性的一年。今年第一季度，全国很多厂矿已完成和超额完成了国家计划。第一汽车制造厂的职工在冬季施工中超额完成了施工计划的百分之一百四十二，现在已转入正常施工，一○六工区的工人们正在浇灌混凝土。
