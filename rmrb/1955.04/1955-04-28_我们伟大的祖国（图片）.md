Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-04-28
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The State-owned Qingdao Aquatic Products Company's fishing vessel repair and manufacturing plant successfully trial-produced a 29-horsepower motorized sailboat for fishermen. The four motorized sailboats manufactured for the four fishery production cooperatives in the suburbs of Qingdao this March have also successively been launched to welcome the spring fishing season. This is a scene of another newly built motorized sailboat on April 2nd, sliding down the dock for a sea trial.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营青岛水产公司渔轮修造厂，为渔民试制的二十九匹马力机帆船成功。今年三月给青岛郊区的四个渔业生产合作社制造的四只机帆船也已先后下水迎接春季鱼汛。这是在四月二日新造的又一艘机帆船，正滑下船坞出海试航的场面。
