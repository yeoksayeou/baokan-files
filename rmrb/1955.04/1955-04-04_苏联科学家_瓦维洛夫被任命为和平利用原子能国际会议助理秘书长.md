Title: Soviet Scientist Vavilov Appointed Assistant Secretary-General of International Conference on Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy
Author: 
Date: 1955-04-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New York, April 3rd. TASS, New York, April 2nd: United Nations Secretary-General Dag Hammarskjöld has announced the appointment of Soviet scientist V.S. Vavilov as Assistant Secretary-General of the International Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy (to be held in Geneva on August 8 this year). Vavilov will assume his duties in New York around mid-April.

### 苏联科学家  瓦维洛夫被任命为和平利用原子能国际会议助理秘书长

1955-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联科学家
    瓦维洛夫被任命为和平利用原子能国际会议助理秘书长
    新华社三日讯　塔斯社纽约二日讯：联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德已经宣布任命苏联科学家维·斯·瓦维洛夫为和平利用原子能国际会议（今年八月八日在日内瓦召开）的助理秘书长。瓦维洛夫将在四月中旬左右到纽约就任。
