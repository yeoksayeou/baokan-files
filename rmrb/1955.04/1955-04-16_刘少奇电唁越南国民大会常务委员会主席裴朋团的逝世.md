Title: Liu Shaoqi Sends Condolences on the Death of Bui Bang Doan, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of Vietnam
Author: Liu Shaoqi
Date: 1955-04-16
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 15th: Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram to Ton Duc Thang, Acting Chairman, Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, to express condolences on the passing of Mr. Bui Bang Doan. The text of the telegram is as follows: Comrade Ton Duc Thang, Acting Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam:

    Upon learning of the unfortunate passing of Mr. Bui Bang Doan, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, I, on behalf of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, express my deepest condolences.

    Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China
                  April 13, 1955, in Beijing

### 刘少奇电唁越南国民大会常务委员会主席裴朋团的逝世
刘少奇
1955-04-16
第4版()
专栏：

    刘少奇电唁越南国民大会常务委员会主席裴朋团的逝世
    新华社十五日讯　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇致电越南民主共和国国民大会常务委员会代理主席孙德胜，悼唁裴朋团先生的逝世。电文如下：越南民主共和国国民大会常务委员会代理主席孙德胜同志：
    获悉越南民主共和国国民大会常务委员会主席裴朋团先生不幸逝世，我谨代表中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会表示深切的悼唁。
    中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长  刘少奇
                  一九五五年四月十三日于北京
