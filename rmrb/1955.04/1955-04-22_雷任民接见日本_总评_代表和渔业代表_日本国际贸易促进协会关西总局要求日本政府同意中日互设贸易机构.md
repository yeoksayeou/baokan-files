Title: Lei Renmin Receives Representatives of Japanese "Sohyo" and Fisheries Representatives; Kansai General Bureau of Japan International Trade Promotion Association Requests Japanese Government to Agree to Mutual Establishment of Trade Organizations between China and Japan
Author:
Date: 1955-04-22
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, April 21st. Lei Renmin, head of the Chinese Trade Delegation visiting Japan, received Takano Minoru, Kitagawa Yoshiyuki, and Sakae Tokutaro, representatives of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan (Sohyo), on the morning of the 20th.
    Takano stated during the meeting that Japanese workers unanimously demand the promotion of Sino-Japanese trade and fervently hope that this visit by the Chinese trade delegation to Japan will help further develop Sino-Japanese trade. Japanese workers will unite with the people nationwide who demand Sino-Japanese friendship and strive to break down the barriers to Sino-Japanese trade.
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, April 21st. Lei Renmin, head of the Chinese Trade Delegation visiting Japan, received Shichida Suekichi, head of the Japanese-China Fisheries Council delegation, along with deputy heads Murayama Satarō, Yamazaki Kinoshuke and others, on the morning of the 21st; these individuals had just returned to Tokyo from China.
    Shichida Suekichi and others expressed their gratitude to the Chinese side for the successful conclusion of the Sino-Japanese fisheries negotiations. They hoped that the Chinese trade delegation's visit to Japan would successfully complete its mission and enhance Sino-Japanese friendship.
    Xinhua News Agency, April 21st. Tokyo dispatch: The Kansai General Bureau of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association adopted a resolution on the 19th, requesting the Japanese government to agree to the mutual establishment of permanent trade organizations and the holding of sample exhibitions between China and Japan.
    The resolution has been submitted to the Ministry of International Trade and Industry and the Speaker of the House of Representatives.

### 雷任民接见日本“总评”代表和渔业代表  日本国际贸易促进协会关西总局要求日本政府同意中日互设贸易机构

1955-04-22
第1版()
专栏：

    雷任民接见日本“总评”代表和渔业代表
    日本国际贸易促进协会关西总局要求日本政府同意中日互设贸易机构
    新华社东京二十一日电　中国访问日本贸易代表团团长雷任民在二十日上午接见了日本工会总评议会的代表高野实、北川义行和坂尾德太郎。
    高野实在会见时表示，日本工人一致要求促进日中贸易，并热烈希望这次中国贸易代表团的访问日本能够有助于日中贸易的进一步发展。日本的工人要和要求中日友好的全国人民团结起来，为打破中日贸易的障碍而努力。
    新华社东京二十一日电　中国访问日本贸易代表团团长雷任民在二十一日上午接见了刚从中国回到东京的日本日中渔业协议会代表团团长七田末吉，副团长村山佐太郎、山崎喜之助等人。
    七田末吉等为中日渔业谈判的圆满结束向中国方面表示感谢。他们希望中国访日贸易代表团能顺利完成任务，并增进中日友好。
    新华社二十一日讯　东京消息：日本国际贸易促进协会关西总局在十九日通过一项决议，要求日本政府同意中国和日本双方互设常驻的贸易机构和举行样品展览会。
    决议已提交通商产业省和众议院议长。
