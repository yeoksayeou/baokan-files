Title: Shen Yanbing Hosts Banquet to Welcome Czechoslovak State Song and Dance Ensemble
Author:
Date: 1955-04-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 20th. Minister of Culture of the People's Republic of China, Shen Yanbing, held a banquet at the Beijing Peace Hotel on the evening of April 20th to welcome Pavel Dubovsky, head of the Czechoslovak State Song and Dance Ensemble, and all members of the ensemble.

Also invited to the banquet were Šmíd, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, and embassy staff.

On the Chinese side, those present included Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Minister of Culture; Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations; Feng Ji-ping, Deputy Mayor of Beijing; Chen Yi, head of the Song and Dance Ensemble of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, who had visited Czechoslovakia; and more than 70 writers, playwrights, musicians, dancers, and artists from the capital.

Minister Shen Yanbing delivered a speech at the banquet, welcoming the Czechoslovak artists to visit China and wishing them success in their performances in China. Head of the Ensemble Dubovsky also spoke at the banquet, saying that the song and dance ensemble would use its artistic activities to promote the close friendship between China and Czechoslovakia.

### 沈雁冰设宴欢迎捷克斯洛伐克国家歌舞团

1955-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    沈雁冰设宴欢迎捷克斯洛伐克国家歌舞团
    新华社二十日讯　中华人民共和国文化部部长沈雁冰二十日晚在北京和平宾馆举行宴会，欢迎捷克斯洛伐克国家歌舞团团长帕维尔·杜博夫斯基和歌舞团全体人员。
    应邀出席宴会的还有捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使馆临时代办石密茨和使馆人员。
    我国方面出席作陪的有文化部副部长郑振铎，对外文化联络局局长洪深，北京市副市长冯基平和曾赴捷克斯洛伐克访问的中国人民解放军歌舞团团长陈沂以及首都的作家、戏剧家、音乐家、舞蹈家、美术家共七十多人。
    沈雁冰部长在宴会上致词，欢迎捷克斯洛伐克的艺术家来我国访问，并预祝他们在我国的演出获得成功。杜博夫斯基团长也在宴会上讲了话，他说，歌舞团将以自己的艺术活动，来促进中捷两国的亲密友谊。
