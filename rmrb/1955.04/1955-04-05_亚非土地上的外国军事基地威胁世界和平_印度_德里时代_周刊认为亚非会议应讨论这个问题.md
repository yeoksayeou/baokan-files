Title: Foreign Military Bases on Asian and African Soil Threaten World Peace; Indian "Delhi Times" Weekly Believes the Asian-African Conference Should Discuss This Issue
Author:
Date: 1955-04-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 3rd: The "Delhi Times" weekly of April 1st stated that one of the important issues to be raised for discussion and decision at the upcoming Asian-African Conference is the existence of foreign military bases on Asian and African soil, and the threat they pose to the independence, integrity, and maintenance of world peace of these countries.

The weekly said that Asian countries feel that these foreign bases on their land—mostly American—are dangerous to them. Asian countries regained their freedom after a long period of struggle. Therefore, responsible political figures in Asian and African countries are now eager to ask and debate: if they allow foreign countries to establish military or any other kind of bases on their land under any pretext, will they suffer the same fate as centuries ago? The newspaper said that everyone is very uneasy about this issue, and the Bandung Conference must make a wise and statesmanlike decision on it.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 2nd: "Indonesia News" published an editorial on the 1st commenting on the upcoming Asian-African Conference, saying: "The convening of the Asian-African Conference shows that the Asian and African people hope to consult and solve all issues related to war and peace, including the colonial issue." The editorial said that it is now an era of rapid development towards ending colonial rule. "In this era, all oppression and poverty brought about by war and colonial rule must be eradicated from the earth."

### 亚非土地上的外国军事基地威胁世界和平  印度“德里时代”周刊认为亚非会议应讨论这个问题

1955-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

    亚非土地上的外国军事基地威胁世界和平
    印度“德里时代”周刊认为亚非会议应讨论这个问题
    新华社新德里三日电　四月一日的“德里时代”周刊说：即将举行的亚非会议将提出讨论和决定的重要问题之一，是外国军事基地在亚洲和非洲土地上的存在，以及它们对于这些国家的独立、完整和对于维护世界和平所构成的威胁。
    周刊说，亚洲国家感到在它们土地上的这些外国基地——大部分是美国的——对它们是有危险的。亚洲国家是在经过长时期的斗争以后才重新获得了它们的自由的。因此，各亚非国家的负责的政界人士现在所急于要询问和辩论的就是：如果他们容许外国以任何借口在他们的土地上建立起军事或任何其他种类的基地的话，那么他们是否会重新遭到数世纪以前的同样的命运。报纸说，大家对这个问题都感到非常不安，而万隆会议必须对此作出一个明智的和具有政治家风度的决定。
    新华社雅加达二日电　“印度尼西亚新闻”一日发表社论评论即将召开的亚非会议说：“亚非会议的召开，表明亚非人民希望进行磋商，来解决所有有关战争与和平的问题，其中包括殖民地问题。”社论说，现在是向着结束殖民统治急剧发展的时代。“在这个时代中，必须把战争和殖民统治带来的一切压迫和贫困从地球上铲除干净”。
