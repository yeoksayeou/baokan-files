Transmitting new agricultural technology to farmers is one of the important conditions for promoting new agricultural implements such as the double-wheeled, double-shared plow. The People's Committee of Jieyu County, Shanxi Province, held a short-term agricultural technology training class to teach farmers how to use the double-wheeled, double-shared plow.
