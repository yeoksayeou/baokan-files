Title: British Liberal Party Leader in the House of Lords Speaks, Advocating for the Restoration of Our Country's Legitimate Rights in the Security Council
Author:
Date: 1955-04-03
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, London, 2nd - British Liberal Party Leader in the House of Lords, Samuel, spoke on the issue of China's seat in the United Nations in a speech on the 29th. Samuel said that the United Nations Security Council should have five major power council members, but currently there are only four major powers plus Taiwan. He said: "This situation cannot continue."

    Samuel said: "Since the United Nations Charter stipulates that China, the real China, should be a permanent member of the council, China, which possesses a quarter of humanity's population, cannot willingly be excluded."

### 英国上院自由党领袖发表谈话  主张恢复我国在安理会合法权利

1955-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    英国上院自由党领袖发表谈话
    主张恢复我国在安理会合法权利
    新华社二日讯　伦敦消息：英国议会上院自由党领袖塞缪尔在二十九日发表的一次谈话中谈到中国在联合国中的席位问题。塞缪尔说，联合国安全理事会应该有五个大国理事国，但是，目前只有四个大国再加上台湾。他说：“这种情况是不能继续下去的。”
    塞缪尔说：“既然联合国宪章规定中国，真正的中国，应该是常任理事国，拥有全人类四分之一人口的中国就不可能甘心于被排除在外。”
