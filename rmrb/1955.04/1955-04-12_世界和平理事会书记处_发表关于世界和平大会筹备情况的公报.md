Title: World Peace Council Secretariat Issues Communiqué on Preparations for the World Peace Congress
Author:
Date: 1955-04-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, April 10th (Tass dispatch): The World Peace Council's proposal to hold the World Peace Congress in Helsinki from May 22nd to 29th, 1955, has aroused enthusiastic response from broad sections of people around the world.

The Secretariat of the World Peace Council issued a "Press Communiqué" on the preparations for the World Peace Congress. The communiqué stated that preparations for the World Peace Congress are now underway in many areas. National conferences have already been held in France, Burma, Colombia, Mexico, and Poland, where issues related to the preparation of the World Peace Congress were discussed.

Sixty-one countries have now announced their agreement to send delegations to the World Peace Congress.

The communiqué stated that the Finnish delegation will have 200 people, the French delegation 150, the British, Indian, and Swedish delegations 100 each, the Canadian delegation 50, the Polish delegation 35, the Japanese delegation 60, and the Belgian delegation 30. If the many organizations that have announced their willingness to send their representatives to Helsinki as guests and observers are also taken into account, then more than 2,000 people will participate in the World Peace Congress.

All countries are strengthening the preparatory work for the World Peace Congress under the slogan of opposing preparations for atomic war and striving to consolidate world peace.

### 世界和平理事会书记处  发表关于世界和平大会筹备情况的公报

1955-04-12
第4版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会书记处
    发表关于世界和平大会筹备情况的公报
    新华社十一日讯　塔斯社维也纳十日讯：世界和平理事会提出的在一九五五年五月二十二日到二十九日在赫尔辛基举行世界和平大会的倡议，引起了全世界广泛阶层人民的热烈反应。
    世界和平理事会书记处发表了关于世界和平大会筹备情况的“新闻公报”。公报中说，现在正在多方面展开筹备世界和平大会的工作。在法国、缅甸、哥伦比亚、墨西哥和波兰都已经举行了全国性的会议，会上讨论了有关筹备世界和平大会的问题。
    现在已经有六十一个国家宣布同意派代表团出席世界和平大会。
    公报中说，芬兰代表团将有二百人，法国代表团有一百五十人，英国、印度和瑞典的代表团各有一百人，加拿大代表团有五十人，波兰代表团有三十五人，日本代表团有六十人，比利时代表团有三十人。如果把许多组织宣布愿意派自己的代表以来宾和观察员身份前往赫尔辛基也估计在内，那末，参加世界和平大会的将在两千人以上。
    各国都在加强反对准备原子战争的运动和争取巩固世界和平的口号下进行世界和平大会的筹备工作。
