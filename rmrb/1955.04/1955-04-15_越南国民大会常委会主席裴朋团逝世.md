Title: Chairman Bùi Bằng Đoàn of the National Assembly Standing Committee of Vietnam Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1955-04-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 14th: According to the Vietnam News Agency, the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam announced in a communiqué on the 13th that Chairman Bùi Bằng Đoàn of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam passed away in Hanoi on the morning of April 13th at the age of 66.

The Council of Ministers of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam held a special meeting on the same day and passed a resolution to hold a funeral service for Bùi Bằng Đoàn on the 14th. The Council of Ministers also decided to posthumously award Bùi Bằng Đoàn the First-Class Independence Medal and designated a government delegation to attend the funeral to be held in his hometown, Liên Bật village, Ứng Hòa district, Hà Đông province.

### 越南国民大会常委会主席裴朋团逝世

1955-04-15
第4版()
专栏：

    越南国民大会常委会主席裴朋团逝世
    新华社河内十四日电　据越南通讯社讯：越南民主共和国国民大会常务委员会十三日发表公报宣布，越南民主共和国国民大会常务委员会主席裴朋团四月十三日上午在河内逝世，享年六十六岁。
    越南民主共和国部长会议同日举行的特别会议通过决议，在十四日举行裴朋团的丧礼。部长会议还决定追赠裴朋团以一级独立勋章，并且指定一个政府代表团参加将在他的故乡河东省应和县联弼村举行的葬礼。
