Title: Ulaanbaatar-Jining Railway Track-Laying Completed in Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1955-04-11
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, April 9th (TASS dispatch): The Ulaanbaatar-Jining railway tracks have been laid from Ulaanbaatar to the railway station on the Chinese border.

    The first passenger train left Ulaanbaatar on the evening of the 8th, heading towards the Chinese border.

### 乌兰巴托—集宁铁路蒙古境内铺轨完成

1955-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

    乌兰巴托—集宁铁路蒙古境内铺轨完成
    新华社十日讯　据塔斯社乌兰巴托九日讯：乌兰巴托—集宁铁路的路轨已从乌兰巴托铺到中国边境上的火车站。
    第一列客车已经在八日晚离开乌兰巴托驶向中国边境。
