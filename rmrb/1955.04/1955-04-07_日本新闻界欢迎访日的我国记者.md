Title: Japanese Press Circles Welcome Visiting Chinese Journalists

Author:
Date: 1955-04-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, April 5th. Wang Xi and three other Chinese journalists accompanying the Chinese trade delegation to Japan were invited to attend a welcome forum held by Japanese press circles on the 5th.

Approximately 180 representatives from organizations such as the Japan Congress of Journalists, the Trade Reporters Association, the China Issues Reporters Association, the Joint Editorial Department of Organ Newspapers, and the Small and Medium Enterprise Editors Club attended the forum. These included leading figures and editors/reporters from newspapers such as "Asahi Shimbun," "Mainichi Shimbun," "Yomiuri Shimbun," "Sangyo Keizai Shimbun," "Nihon Keizai Shimbun," "Chubu Nihon Shimbun," "Nishi Nippon Shimbun," "Hokkaido Shimbun," "Akahata," and "Sohyo News," as well as individuals in charge and editors/reporters from Kyodo News, "Chuo Koron," Iwanami Shoten, and Heibonsha. Discussions were held at the meeting on strengthening ties between Chinese and Japanese journalists, promoting understanding between the peoples of the two countries, and the work of newspapers.

### 日本新闻界欢迎访日的我国记者

1955-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

    日本新闻界欢迎访日的我国记者
    新华社东京五日电　随中国访问日本贸易代表团访问日本的中国新闻记者汪溪等四人，五日应邀出席了日本新闻界人士主办的欢迎座谈会。
    出席座谈会的有日本新闻工作者会议、贸易记者会、中国问题记者会、机关报共同编辑部和中小企业编辑俱乐部等组织的代表约一百八十人。其中包括“朝日新闻”、“每日新闻”、“读卖新闻”、“产业经济新闻”、“日本经济新闻”、“中部日本新闻”、“西日本新闻”、“北海道新闻”、“赤旗报”、“总评新闻”等报纸，以及共同通讯社、“中央公论”、岩波书店、平凡社等单位的负责人及编辑记者。会上，就加强中日新闻工作者的联系、促进两国人民的了解以及报纸工作情况进行了座谈。
