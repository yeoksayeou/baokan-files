Title: Celebrating the Tenth Anniversary of Hungary's Liberation: Hungarian Working People's Party Central Committee and Others Hold Grand Reception
Author:
Date: 1955-04-07
Page: 4

    Celebrating the Tenth Anniversary of Hungary's Liberation
    Hungarian Working People's Party Central Committee and Others Hold Grand Reception

    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, April 5th: According to TASS, the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, the Council of Ministers, and the National Council of the Hungarian Patriotic People's Front held a grand reception in the National Assembly Building in Budapest on April 4th to celebrate the tenth anniversary of Hungary's liberation.

    Among those attending the reception were members of the Politburo headed by Mátyás Rákosi, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, members of the Hungarian National Assembly, members of the Presidium of the Republic headed by István Dobi, Chairman of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, government officials, generals of the People's Army, prominent figures from the literary, artistic, and scientific circles, and advanced producers.

    The reception was attended by the Soviet government delegation headed by K.Y. Voroshilov and government delegations from other countries participating in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of Hungary's liberation.

    Also attending the reception were diplomatic envoys from various countries to Hungary.

    The reception was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 庆祝匈牙利解放十周年  匈劳动人民党中央等举行盛大招待会

1955-04-07
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝匈牙利解放十周年
    匈劳动人民党中央等举行盛大招待会
    新华社六日讯　据塔斯社布达佩斯五日讯：匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会、匈牙利人民共和国主席团、部长会议和匈牙利爱国人民阵线全国委员会，四月四日在布达佩斯国民议会大厦举行盛大招待会，庆祝匈牙利解放十周年。
    出席招待会的有以匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会第一书记马·拉科西为首的政治局委员，匈牙利国民议会议员，以匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席伊·道比为首的共和国主席团人员，政府官员，人民军将领，文学、艺术、科学界著名人士和先进生产者。
    参加招待会的有以克·叶·伏罗希洛夫为首的苏联政府代表团和其他国家参加匈牙利解放十周年庆祝典礼的政府代表团。
    出席招待会的还有各国驻匈牙利外交使节。
    招待会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
