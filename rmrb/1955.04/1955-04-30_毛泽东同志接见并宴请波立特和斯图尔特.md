Title: Comrade Mao Zedong Receives and Hosts Pollitt and Stewart

Author:
Date: 1955-04-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, received and hosted Harry Pollitt, General Secretary of the Communist Party of Great Britain, and Robert Stewart, Chairman of the Central Control Commission, on the afternoon of April 29th, and held lengthy talks. Liu Shaoqi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, Deng Xiaoping, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary-General of the CPC Central Committee, Wang Jiaxiang, member of the CPC Central Committee, and Yang Shangkun, Director of the General Office of the CPC Central Committee, attended the reception and banquet.

### 毛泽东同志接见并宴请波立特和斯图尔特

1955-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东同志接见并宴请波立特和斯图尔特
    新华社二十九日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东在二十九日下午接见和宴请了英国共产党总书记哈里·波立特和中央监察委员会主席罗伯特·斯图尔特，并进行了长时间的谈话。参加接见和宴会的有中共中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记刘少奇，中共中央政治局委员、中共中央秘书长邓小平，中共中央委员王稼祥，中共中央办公厅主任杨尚昆。
