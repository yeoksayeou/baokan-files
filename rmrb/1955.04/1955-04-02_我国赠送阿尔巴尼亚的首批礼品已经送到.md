Title: First Batch of Gifts from China to Albania Arrives
Author:
Date: 1955-04-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 1st. The first batch of gifts from the government delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Albanian people on the occasion of the tenth anniversary of Albania's liberation arrived at the Albanian port of Durrës on March 29th aboard the Polish ship "Bytom."

    A ceremony was held in Durrës that afternoon to mark the arrival of the gifts. The ceremony was attended by Tu Jakova, Vice Chairman of the Albanian Council of Ministers, Jo Pashi, Minister of Construction and Transport, Ki Engjela, Minister of Trade, Va. Natanaeli, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Durrës party and government leaders.

    Xu Yixin, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Albania, attended the ceremony.

    On the 30th, "Voice of the People" and "Unity" published editorials on the arrival of these gifts, pointing out that this is a noble manifestation of the great friendship between the Albanian and Chinese peoples.

### 我国赠送阿尔巴尼亚的首批礼品已经送到

1955-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

    我国赠送阿尔巴尼亚的首批礼品已经送到
    新华社一日讯　地拉那消息：中华人民共和国政府代表团在阿尔巴尼亚解放十周年赠送给阿尔巴尼亚人民的礼品的第一批，已经由波兰轮船“贝托姆号”在三月二十九日运抵阿尔巴尼亚港口都拉斯。
    当天下午在都拉斯为礼品的到达举行了仪式。参加仪式的有阿尔巴尼亚部长会议副主席图·雅科瓦，建筑和交通部部长约·帕希科，贸易部部长基·恩杰拉，外交部副部长瓦·纳塔奈利，以及都拉斯的党政领导人等。
    中华人民共和国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使徐以新参加了仪式。
    三十日，“人民之声报”和“团结报”就这些礼品的到达发表了社论，指出这是阿中两国人民的伟大友谊的崇高表现。
