Title: All-China Women's Federation Establishes Secretariat

Author:

Date: 1955-04-20
Page: 2

    The Standing Committee of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation held a meeting on the 14th. The meeting discussed the issue of establishing a secretariat in accordance with the Constitution of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation. Based on the resolution of the first meeting of the Second Executive Committee of the All-China Women's Federation, the meeting elected Zhang Yun, Xu Guangping, Kang Keqing, Luo Qiong, Zeng Xianzhi, Cao Mengjun, Shen Zijiu, and Cao Guanqun to form the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation.
    The meeting also discussed and approved the main points of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation's work plan for 1955. The work plan for 1955 proposes: to further strengthen the work of women in rural and urban areas in accordance with the general task of the state during the transition period; to further strengthen socialist ideological and political education for women, and in particular to strengthen publicity and education on socialist moral principles regarding marriage, family, mothers and children to all sectors of society; and to cooperate with the international movement to defend peace, strengthen and expand friendship between women of all countries. The plan also stipulates various necessary specific measures.
    In addition, the meeting discussed and approved the specific plan for the upcoming First National Urban Women's Work Conference. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 全国妇联成立书记处

1955-04-20
第2版()
专栏：

    全国妇联成立书记处
    中华全国民主妇女联合会常务委员会在十四日举行会议。会议根据中华全国民主妇女联合会章程讨论了组织书记处的问题。会议根据全国妇联第二届执行委员会第一次会议的决议，选出章蕴、许广平、康克清、罗琼、曾宪植、曹孟君、沈兹九、曹冠群等八人组成全国妇联书记处。
    会议还讨论和通过了中华全国民主妇女联合会一九五五年的工作计划要点。一九五五年的工作计划提出：根据国家在过渡时期的总任务，进一步加强农村和城市妇女工作；进一步加强对妇女进行社会主义思想和政治教育，特别要向社会各方面加强进行以社会主义道德原则对待婚姻、家庭、母亲和儿童的宣传教育；并要配合国际保卫和平运动，加强、扩大和各国妇女之间的友谊。计划还规定了各项必要的具体措施。
    另外，会议讨论和通过了即将召开的全国第一次城市妇女工作会议的具体计划。　（新华社）
