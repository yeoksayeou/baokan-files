Title: Pakistani Prime Minister Ali to Lead Delegation to Asian-African Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-04-05
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 4th: A Karachi dispatch: According to official announcements, Pakistani Prime Minister Ali will lead the Pakistani delegation to attend the Asian-African Conference to be held in Bandung. The Pakistani delegation will include Minister of Justice H. S. Suhrawardy, Minister of Finance Chaudhri Muhammad Ali, and others.

### 巴基斯坦总理阿里  将率领代表团出席亚非会议

1955-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦总理阿里
    将率领代表团出席亚非会议
    新华社新德里四日电　喀喇蚩消息：据官方宣布，巴基斯坦总理阿里将率领巴基斯坦代表团出席即将在万隆召开的亚非会议。巴基斯坦代表团将包括司法部长侯·夏·苏拉瓦底、财政部长乔德里·穆罕默德·阿里等人。
