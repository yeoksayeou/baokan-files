Title: Celebrating the Tenth Anniversary of the Signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Post-War Cooperation: Polish Ambassador to China, Kirilocek, Holds Reception in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-04-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd. Polish Ambassador to China, Kirilocek, held a reception at the Soviet Exhibition Hall in Beijing on the evening of April 22nd to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Post-War Cooperation.

Academician Yudin, the Soviet Ambassador to China, and embassy staff were invited to attend the reception.

He Long and Li Xiannian, Vice Premiers of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, ministers and vice ministers of various ministries, directors and deputy directors of various commissions, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs He Wei, Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Su Yu, Deputy Director of the General Political Department Xiao Hua, and Vice Mayor of Beijing Feng Jiping attended the reception.

Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and members, Wu Yuzhang, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and other leaders of various people's organizations, as well as people from the capital's science, education, literature, art, and journalism circles also attended the reception.

Diplomatic envoys and embassy staff from various People's Democratic countries were also invited to attend the reception.

At the meeting, Ambassador Kirilocek, Ambassador Yudin, and Vice Premier He Long all spoke. They all expressed warm congratulations on the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty.

After the meeting, a Polish film was shown.

### 庆祝波苏友好、互助与战后合作条约签订十周年  波兰驻我国大使基里洛克在京举行招待会

1955-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝波苏友好、互助与战后合作条约签订十周年
    波兰驻我国大使基里洛克在京举行招待会
    新华社二十二日讯　波兰驻我国大使基里洛克二十二日晚在北京苏联展览馆举行招待会，庆祝波苏友好、互助与战后合作条约签订十周年。
    苏联驻我国大使尤金院士和使馆人员应邀出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的有中华人民共和国国务院副总理贺龙、李先念，各部部长、副部长，各委员会主任、副主任，外交部部长助理何伟，中国人民解放军总参谋长粟裕、总政治部副主任萧华，北京市副市长冯基平。
    出席招待会的还有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长陈叔通和委员，中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章，以及其他各人民团体负责人，首都科学、教育、文学、艺术和新闻界人士。
    各人民民主国家驻华使节和使馆人员也应邀出席了招待会。
    会上，基里洛克大使，尤金大使，贺龙副总理都讲了话。他们对波苏条约签订十周年都表示热烈祝贺。
    会后，放映了波兰影片。
