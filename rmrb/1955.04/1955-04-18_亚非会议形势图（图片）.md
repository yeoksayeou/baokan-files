Title: Map of the Asian-African Conference
Author: Shushi
Date: 1955-04-18
Page: 4

    Map of the Asian-African Conference Drawn by Shu Shi
    The Asian-African Conference opens today in Bandung, Indonesia. A total of twenty-nine countries are participating in this conference, with a total land area of more than 31 million square kilometers, accounting for nearly a quarter of the world's land area; the population totals 1.44 billion, accounting for nearly two-thirds of the world's population.

### 亚非会议形势图（图片）
舒适
1955-04-18
第4版()
专栏：

    亚非会议形势图　　　　　　舒适绘
    亚非会议今天在印度尼西亚的万隆开幕了。参加这次会议的共有二十九个国家，它们的土地面积共三千一百多万平方公里，占全世界土地面积将近四分之一；人口共十四亿四千万，占全世界人口将近三分之二。
