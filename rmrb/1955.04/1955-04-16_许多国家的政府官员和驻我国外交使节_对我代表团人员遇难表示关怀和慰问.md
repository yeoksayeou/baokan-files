Title: Government Officials of Many Countries and Diplomatic Envoys to Our Country Express Concern and Condolences for the Death of Our Delegation Members
Author:
Date: 1955-04-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 15th. After the plane carrying staff members and reporters of the Chinese delegation to the Asian-African Conference crashed due to sabotage by American-Chiang Kai-shek special agents, government officials of many countries and diplomatic envoys to China expressed their sympathy to us and condolences to the families of the victims.

So far, the following have sent notes or letters to our Ministry of Foreign Affairs and our embassies abroad expressing concern and condolences: Prime Minister Nehru of the Republic of India, Prime Minister Grotewohl of the German Democratic Republic, Ambassador König of the German Democratic Republic to China, and Minister of Transport Jagjivan Ram of the Republic of India.

Ambassador Choi Il of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, Chargé d'Affaires Czattorday of the Hungarian People's Republic Embassy in China, Chargé d'Affaires Zhou Liang of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Embassy in China, and Chargé d'Affaires Sudibjo of the Republic of Indonesia Embassy in China, specifically visited our Ministry of Foreign Affairs to express their condolences to the families of the victims. Ambassador R.K. Nehru of the Republic of India to China and Chargé d'Affaires U Maung Maung Toe of the Embassy of the Union of Burma in China also expressed their concern about this incident during their visits to our Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

The Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic visited our Ambassador to Mongolia to express condolences to our delegation.

### 许多国家的政府官员和驻我国外交使节  对我代表团人员遇难表示关怀和慰问

1955-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

    许多国家的政府官员和驻我国外交使节
    对我代表团人员遇难表示关怀和慰问
    新华社十五日讯　我国出席亚非会议代表团工作人员和记者所乘座机因美蒋特务破坏失事后，许多国家的政府官员和驻华外交使节纷纷向我表示同情，并慰问罹难者的家属。
    到现在为止，已经向我国外交部和我国驻外使馆致送照会或函电表示关怀和慰问的有：印度共和国总理尼赫鲁、德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥、德意志民主共和国驻华大使柯尼希、印度共和国交通部长贾格克凡·拉姆。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使崔一、匈牙利人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办察托尔道伊、越南民主共和国驻华大使馆临时代办周亮、印度尼西亚共和国驻华大使馆临时代办苏迪比约都特地访问了我外交部，表示对被难者家属的慰问。印度共和国驻华大使赖嘉文和缅甸联邦驻华大使馆临时代办吴茂茂铎在访问我外交部时，也对这一事件表示关怀。
    蒙古人民共和国外交部副部长访问了我国驻蒙古大使，对我国代表团表示慰问。
