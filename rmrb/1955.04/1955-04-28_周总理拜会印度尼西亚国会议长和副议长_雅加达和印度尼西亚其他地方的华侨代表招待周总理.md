Title: Premier Zhou Pays Courtesy Visit to Indonesian Speaker and Deputy Speakers of Parliament, Overseas Chinese Representatives from Jakarta and Other Parts of Indonesia Host Premier Zhou
Author:
Date: 1955-04-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 27th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China and Vice Premier Chen Yi, accompanied by Indonesian Foreign Minister Sunarjo, paid a courtesy visit to Indonesian Speaker of Parliament Sardjono, Deputy Speakers Tambunan, Arudji Kartawinata, and Chairman of the Parliamentary Foreign Affairs Subcommittee, Mois, at 11:15 AM on April 27th.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 27th: Over six hundred representatives of overseas Chinese from Jakarta and other parts of Indonesia held a reception in Jakarta at 2:00 PM on April 27th to welcome Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China and other personnel accompanying Premier Zhou Enlai on his visit to Indonesia.

Premier Zhou Enlai delivered a speech at the reception.

Sudiro, the Mayor of Jakarta, who was invited to attend the reception, also delivered a speech.

### 周总理拜会印度尼西亚国会议长和副议长  雅加达和印度尼西亚其他地方的华侨代表招待周总理

1955-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理拜会印度尼西亚国会议长和副议长
    雅加达和印度尼西亚其他地方的华侨代表招待周总理
    新华社雅加达二十七日电　中华人民共和国总理周恩来和副总理陈毅由印度尼西亚外交部长苏纳约陪同，于二十七日上午十一时十五分拜会了印度尼西亚国会议长沙多诺，副议长谭布兰、阿鲁齐·卡塔韦纳塔和国会外交小组委员会主席莫伊斯。
    新华社雅加达二十七日电　雅加达和印度尼西亚其他地方的华侨代表六百多人二十七日下午二时在雅加达举行招待会，招待中华人民共和国总理周恩来和随同周恩来总理访问印度尼西亚的其他人员。
    周恩来总理在会上讲了话。
    应邀参加招待会的雅加达市长苏迪罗也在会上讲了话。
