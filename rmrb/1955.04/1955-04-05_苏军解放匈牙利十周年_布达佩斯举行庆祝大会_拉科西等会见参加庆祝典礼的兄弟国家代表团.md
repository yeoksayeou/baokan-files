Title: Tenth Anniversary of the Soviet Army's Liberation of Hungary: Budapest Holds Celebration Meeting; Rákosi and Others Meet with Delegations from Fraternal Countries Attending the Celebration Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1955-04-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th, dispatch from Budapest: According to TASS, the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, the Council of Ministers, and the National Council of the Hungarian Patriotic People's Front jointly held a celebration meeting at the Budapest National Opera and Ballet Theatre on April 3rd to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the Soviet Army's liberation of Hungary.

The celebration meeting was opened by I. Dobi, Chairman of the Presidium of the Republic.

Mátyás Rákosi, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, delivered a report on the tenth anniversary of the Soviet Army's liberation of Hungary at the meeting
(Full text appears on page 3 of today's newspaper). In his report, he spoke about the achievements Hungary had made in various aspects of socialist construction over the past ten years and the peaceful policy of the Hungarian People's Republic.

Subsequently, Voroshilov, the head of the Soviet government delegation, spoke, congratulating the Hungarian people on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, the Soviet government, and the entire Soviet people, and wishing them new achievements in developing the national economy, improving culture, and consolidating the people's democratic system.

The heads of government delegations from other countries also delivered congratulatory messages at the meeting.

On the same day, a ceremony was held in Budapest's Liberty Square to lay wreaths at the Soviet Army Martyrs' Memorial.

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, April 4th: A grand ceremony to lay wreaths at the Soviet Army Martyrs' Memorial was held at Liberty Square in Budapest at 10:00 a.m. on the 3rd.

Mátyás Rákosi, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, I. Dobi, Chairman of the Presidium of the Republic, András Hegedüs, Acting Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Ernő Gerő, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and General I. Bata, Minister of National Defense, and other party, government, and military leaders attended the ceremony.

Rákosi, Dobi, and Hegedüs laid wreaths on behalf of the Central Committee of the Working People's Party, the Presidium of the Republic, and the Council of Ministers, respectively.

The Soviet government delegation, the government delegation of the People's Republic of China, and the government delegations of other people's democratic countries, who were attending the tenth anniversary celebration of Hungary's liberation, also participated in the ceremony and laid wreaths.

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, April 4th: Mátyás Rákosi, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, I. Dobi, Chairman of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, András Hegedüs, Acting Chairman of the Council of Ministers, István Hidas and András Apró, Vice-Chairmen of the Council of Ministers, General I. Bata, Minister of National Defense, and other party, government, and military leaders met at noon on the 3rd with the Soviet government delegation headed by K.E. Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, the government delegation of the People's Republic of China headed by Vice Premier of the State Council Deng Zihui, and the government delegations of various people's democratic countries.

### 苏军解放匈牙利十周年  布达佩斯举行庆祝大会  拉科西等会见参加庆祝典礼的兄弟国家代表团

1955-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

    苏军解放匈牙利十周年
    布达佩斯举行庆祝大会
    拉科西等会见参加庆祝典礼的兄弟国家代表团
    新华社四日讯　据塔斯社布达佩斯讯：匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会、匈牙利人民共和国主席团、部长会议和匈牙利爱国人民阵线全国委员会，四月三日在布达佩斯国立歌剧和巴蕾舞剧院联合举行庆祝大会，庆祝苏军解放匈牙利十周年。
    庆祝大会由共和国主席团主席伊·道比主持开幕。
    匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会第一书记马·拉科西在会上做了关于苏军解放匈牙利十周年的报告
  （全文见今日本报第三版）。他在报告中谈到了匈牙利十年来在社会主义建设各方面所取得的成就和匈牙利人民共和国的和平政策。
    接着苏联政府代表团团长伏罗希洛夫讲话，他代表苏联共产党中央委员会、苏联最高苏维埃、苏联政府和全体苏联人民向匈牙利人民祝贺，并祝他们在发展国民经济、提高文化和巩固人民民主制度中取得新的成就。
    其他国家的政府代表团团长也在会上相继致了贺词。
    同一天，在布达佩斯自由广场举行了向苏军烈士纪念碑献花圈的典礼。
    新华社布达佩斯四日讯　三日上午十时在布达佩斯自由广场举行了向苏军烈士纪念碑献花圈的隆重典礼。
    匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会第一书记马·拉科西，共和国主席团主席伊·道比，部长会议代理主席安·赫格居斯、部长会议第一副主席埃·格罗，国防部长伊·巴塔上将等党政军领导人，都参加这次典礼。
    拉科西、道比和赫格居斯分别代表劳动人民党中央委员会、共和国主席团和部长会议献了花圈。
    参加匈牙利解放十周年庆祝典礼的苏联政府代表团、中华人民共和国政府代表团和其他人民民主国家政府代表团也参加了典礼并献了花圈。
    新华社布达佩斯四日电　匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会第一书记马·拉科西、匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席伊·道比、部长会议代理主席安·赫格居斯、部长会议副主席伊·希达什和安·阿普罗、国防部长伊·巴塔上将等党政军领导人，三日中午会见了以苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席克·叶·伏罗希洛夫为首的苏联政府代表团、以国务院副总理邓子恢为首的中华人民共和国政府代表团和各人民民主国家政府代表团。
