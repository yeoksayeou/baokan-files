Title: At the invitation of Prime Minister Nehru, Vietnam will send a goodwill mission to India
Author:
Date: 1955-04-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 1st. The Vietnam News Agency reported on March 31st: At the invitation of Prime Minister Nehru during his talks with President Ho Chi Minh in Hanoi, the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam has decided to send a goodwill mission to the Republic of India in early April. The goodwill mission will be led by Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Pham Van Dong and will be composed of several senior officials from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

### 应尼赫鲁总理的邀请  越南将派友好使团访问印度

1955-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

    应尼赫鲁总理的邀请
    越南将派友好使团访问印度
    新华社河内一日电　越南通讯社三月三十一日讯：应尼赫鲁总理在河内同胡志明主席举行会谈时所作的邀请，越南民主共和国政府已经决定在四月初派遣一个友好使团访问印度共和国。友好使团将由副总理兼外交部长范文同率领，并由越南民主共和国外交部的若干高级官员组成。
