Title: U Nu and Phạm Văn Đồng Issue Joint Communiqué of Talks, Phạm Văn Đồng Returns to Hanoi and Issues Written Statement
Author:
Date: 1955-04-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 14th: Burmese Federal Prime Minister U Nu and Vietnamese Democratic Republic Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Phạm Văn Đồng concluded their talks on the 13th and issued a joint communiqué.

The communiqué stated that Prime Minister U Nu and Deputy Prime Minister Phạm Văn Đồng reaffirmed their support for the Geneva Accords. They agreed that these agreements must be faithfully implemented in order to achieve a satisfactory resolution of the political problems in Vietnam, Cambodia, and Laos.

The communiqué stated that they agreed that efforts should be made to promote the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, which provide a basis for establishing good relations between countries in Southeast Asia and other regions.

The communiqué stated that they were both convinced that the friendly relations between Burma and the Vietnamese Democratic Republic should be further strengthened to promote the mutual interests of the two peoples.

The communiqué stated that they expressed their full support for the upcoming Asian-African Conference, which would be the first time in history that the peoples of Asia and Africa would have the opportunity to come together, thereby breaking down the barriers created by colonialism between them and promoting the cause of peace.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 14th: Vietnamese Democratic Republic Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Phạm Văn Đồng returned to Hanoi by plane on the afternoon of the 14th.

Those who went to Gia Lam Airport to welcome him included the heads of various departments and people's organizations of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic government, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Hanoi and representatives of the International Supervisory and Control Commission.

After disembarking from the plane, Phạm Văn Đồng issued a written statement saying: "I am very satisfied with my visits to India and Burma. We clearly see the sympathy and admiration of the Indian and Burmese people for the heroic resistance of the Vietnamese people, and their support for the Vietnamese people's heroic struggle to implement the Geneva Accords, consolidate peace, achieve unification, and complete national independence and democracy."

Phạm Văn Đồng said: "This visit has played a role in further strengthening the friendship between the Vietnamese people and the people of India and Burma. The Vietnamese people, who have gained the sympathy of the people of India, the people of Burma, and all peace-loving and just people in the world, will more actively struggle to consolidate peace, achieve unification, and complete national independence and democracy."

### 吴努和范文同发表会谈联合公报  范文同回到河内发表书面谈话

1955-04-15
第4版()
专栏：

    吴努和范文同发表会谈联合公报
    范文同回到河内发表书面谈话
    新华社十四日讯　仰光消息：缅甸联邦总理吴努和越南民主共和国副总理兼外交部长范文同在十三日结束了他们的会谈，并且发表了会谈的联合公报。
    公报说，吴努总理和范文同副总理重申他们对于日内瓦协议的支持。他们一致认为必须忠实履行这些协议，以便使越南、柬埔寨和老挝的政治问题获得令人满意的解决。
    公报说，他们一致认为，应该努力来促进和平共处的五项原则，这五项原则为东南亚和其他地区各国之间建立良好关系提供了基础。
    公报说，他们都确信，应该进一步加强缅甸和越南民主共和国之间的友好关系，以促进两国人民的相互利益。
    公报说，他们表示完全支持即将举行的亚非会议，这个会议将是历史上第一次使亚洲和非洲人民有机会相聚一堂，从而打破殖民主义在他们之间所制造的障碍，并促进和平事业。
    新华社河内十四日电　越南民主共和国副总理兼外交部长范文同在十四日下午乘飞机返抵河内。
    前往嘉林机场欢迎的有越南民主共和国政府各部门、各人民团体负责人、以及各国驻河内使节和国际监督和监察委员会的代表。
    范文同在下机后发表书面谈话说：我对于在印度和缅甸的访问，感到非常满意。我们清楚地看到，印度人民和缅甸人民对于越南人民英勇抗战的同情和钦佩以及对于越南人民执行日内瓦协定以巩固和平、实现统一、完成全国独立和民主的英勇斗争的支持。
    范文同说：这一次的访问有着进一步密切越南人民同印度、缅甸人民之间的友谊的作用。得到印度人民、缅甸人民和全世界爱好和平与正义同情的越南人民，将更加积极为巩固和平、实现统一、完成全国独立和民主而斗争。
