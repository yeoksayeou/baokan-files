Title: Czechoslovak Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing at the Invitation of the Chinese Government to Attend the Opening Ceremony of the Exhibition of Achievements in Socialist Construction of Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1955-04-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 12th. The head of the Czechoslovak Republic Government Delegation, and Vice Premier of the Czechoslovak Republic, Jankovcova, arrived in Beijing by plane at 4:00 PM on the 12th. Also arriving in Beijing were the deputy head of the delegation, Minister of Metallurgy and Mining of the Czechoslovak Republic, Reitmajer; members: Deputy Minister of Machine Industry of the Czechoslovak Republic Šmok, Academician Ježík, painter Bělski, and sculptor Lauda. Members, the Czechoslovak Republic's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Gregor, and Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Kozor, are already in Beijing.
    The delegation is visiting at the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China to attend the opening ceremony of the "Exhibition of Achievements in Ten Years of Socialist Construction of Czechoslovakia" held in Beijing.
    Those who welcomed them at the airport included: Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Li Xiannian, Minister of Heavy Industry Wang Heshou, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Fan Ziwen, Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building Wang Daohan, Vice Minister of Culture Zhang Zhixiang, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs He Wei, Director of the Protocol Department Ke Hua, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department Liu Jingyu, and Vice Mayor of Beijing Feng Jiping.
    Also at the airport to welcome them were: Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade Nan Hanchen, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Wu Youxun, Deputy Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade Xiao Fangzhou, and others.
    Members of the Czechoslovak Republic Government Delegation, the Czechoslovak Republic's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Gregor, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Kozor, and officials from the Czechoslovak Republic's Embassy in China were also at the airport to welcome them.
    Also at the airport to welcome them was Academician Yudin, the Soviet Union's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China.
    After the members of the Czechoslovak Republic Government Delegation headed by Jankovcova disembarked from the plane, they shook hands with the welcomers one by one. Head of Delegation Jankovcova also received flowers. (Attached picture)
    The Czechoslovak Republic Government Delegation arrived in Beijing on April 12. The picture shows the head of the delegation, Vice Premier of the Czechoslovak Republic Jankovcova (left), shaking hands with Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian (right) at the airport who went to welcome them.
                              Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Qingrui

### 应我国政府邀请参加捷社会主义建设成就展览会开幕式  捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团到达北京

1955-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    应我国政府邀请参加捷社会主义建设成就展览会开幕式
    捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团到达北京
    新华社十二日讯　捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团团长、捷克斯洛伐克共和国副总理扬科夫卓娃，在十二日下午四时乘飞机到达北京。同时到京的有代表团副团长、捷克斯洛伐克共和国冶金工业与采矿部部长雷特马耶尔，团员：捷克斯洛伐克共和国机器工业部副部长什莫克、科学院院士叶希基克、画家贝尔契、雕刻家劳达。团员、捷克斯洛伐克驻我国特命全权大使格里哥尔和对外贸易部副部长科佐尔已在北京。
    代表团是应中华人民共和国政府邀请，前来参加在北京举行的“捷克斯洛伐克十年社会主义建设成就展览会”开幕式的。
    到机场欢迎的有：中华人民共和国国务院副总理李先念，重工业部部长王鹤寿，对外贸易部副部长范子文，第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵，文化部副部长张致祥，外交部部长助理何伟、礼宾司司长柯华、苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇，以及北京市副市长冯基平。
    到机场欢迎的还有：中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸，中国科学院副院长吴有训，中国国际贸易促进委员会副秘书长萧方洲等。
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团团员、捷克斯洛伐克驻我国特命全权大使格里哥尔、对外贸易部副部长科佐尔和捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使馆官员也到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的，还有苏联驻我国特命全权大使尤金院士。
    以扬科夫卓娃为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团人员下机后，与欢迎者一一握手。扬科夫卓娃团长并接受了献花。（附图片）

                      新华社记者　刘庆瑞摄
