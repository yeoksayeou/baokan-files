Title: New Czechoslovak Ambassador Gregor Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-04-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 5th. Antonín Gregor, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Czechoslovak Republic to China, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 5th. Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, went to the airport to welcome him.

Members of the diplomatic corps and staff of various countries' embassies in China, as well as Šmíd, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Czechoslovak Republic in China, and all embassy staff members also went to the airport to welcome him.

### 捷新任大使格里哥尔到北京

1955-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    捷新任大使格里哥尔到北京
    新华社五日讯　捷克斯洛伐克共和国新任驻我国特命全权大使安托宁·格里哥尔在五日乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有我中华人民共和国外交部礼宾司司长柯华。
    各国驻我国使节、外交人员和捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办石密茨和全体馆员也到机场欢迎。
