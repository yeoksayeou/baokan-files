Title: Agreement Signed Between Vietnam and France on the Transfer of Quảng Ngãi and Bình Định Regions
Author:
Date: 1955-04-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 15th: According to Vietnam News Agency on the 14th, the agreement on the transfer of Quảng Ngãi and Bình Định regions (the last section of the temporary stationing area of the Vietnamese People's Army in Central Vietnam) stipulates the same principles and methods as the agreement on the transfer of the Hải Phòng area.

The Quảng Ngãi and Bình Định regions will be divided into nine districts, to be transferred to the French Union forces from April 22 to May 16, 1954.

The advance guard and police team of the French Union forces will enter the Quảng Ngãi and Bình Định regions 48 hours before the transfer date, with a total of 420 personnel, including 380 military police and 40 civil police.

Both parties will request the International Commission to send four mobile inspection teams to monitor the progress of the transfer work.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 15th: According to Vietnam News Agency on the 14th, the delegation of postal experts from the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the delegation of postal experts from the South Vietnamese authorities held talks in Hải Phòng on April 11th and 12th. Both parties have reached an agreement on the exchange of ordinary postcards between the northern and southern regions.

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 15th: According to TASS, Paris, 14th: According to Agence France-Presse, French Prime Minister Edgar Faure spoke at a press conference on the 13th about France's plans for nuclear energy production. He reiterated that France must "eliminate backwardness" in this area and become "a powerful atomic nation." At the same time, he said that France does not intend to conduct scientific research on the use of atomic energy for military purposes and does not intend to manufacture atomic bombs or hydrogen bombs.

Edgar Faure also spoke about the situation in Indochina. He assured that France would "fulfill the Geneva Accords and pay attention to the implementation of these agreements."

### 关于移交广义、平定地区  越法双方签订议定书

1955-04-16
第4版()
专栏：

    关于移交广义、平定地区
    越法双方签订议定书
    新华社河内十五日讯　据越南通讯社十四日讯：关于移交广义、平定地区（越南人民军中越临时驻扎地段最后地段）的议定书，规定了同关于移交海防地区的议定书一样的原则和方式。
    广义、平定地区将分成九个区，从一九五四年四月二十二日起到五月十六日止移交给法兰西联邦部队。
    法兰西联邦部队的先遣警卫和警察队将在移交日期以前四十八小时进入广义、平定地区，人员总数为四百二十人，其中宪兵三百八十人，民警四十人。
    双方将要求国际委员会派遣四个机动视察小组监察移交工作的进行。
    新华社河内十五日讯　据越南通讯社十四日讯：越南民主共和国政府邮政专家代表团同南越当局的邮政专家代表团，四月十一日和十二日在海防进行会谈。双方已经就在北部地区和南部地区之间交换家常明信片问题达成协议。
    新华社十五日讯　据塔斯社巴黎十四日讯：据法新社消息，法国总理埃加·富尔十三日在记者招待会上就法国在核子能生产方面的计划发表了谈话。他重申法国在这一方面必须“消灭落后情况”，并且成为“一个强大的原子国家”。同时，他说，法国不打算进行把原子能用于军事目的的科学研究工作，不打算制造原子弹和氢弹。
    埃加·富尔还谈到印度支那的局势。他保证说，法国将“履行日内瓦协议和注意这些协议的执行情况”。
