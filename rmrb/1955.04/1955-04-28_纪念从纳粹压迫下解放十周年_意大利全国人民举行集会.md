Title: Nationwide Rallies Held in Italy to Commemorate the Tenth Anniversary of Liberation from Nazi Oppression
Author:
Date: 1955-04-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, April 27th: According to TASS: On the 25th, the Italian people commemorated the tenth anniversary of Italy's liberation from Nazi oppression. On this day, rallies and demonstrations were held throughout the country, including in Rome, Venice, Genoa, Verona, and other towns.

    At a rally in Biella, Vercelli Province, Giorgio Amendola, a member of the Secretariat of the Italian Communist Party, spoke. He called on the people to use a united front to oppose all attempts to revive Fascism.

### 纪念从纳粹压迫下解放十周年  意大利全国人民举行集会

1955-04-28
第4版()
专栏：

    纪念从纳粹压迫下解放十周年
    意大利全国人民举行集会
    新华社二十七日讯　据塔斯社讯：二十五日，意大利人民纪念意大利从纳粹压迫下解放十周年。在这个节日，全国各地如罗马、威尼斯、热那亚维罗那和其他城镇都举行了集会和示威游行。
      在维切利省比埃拉的一次集会上，意大利共产党书记处书记乔治·阿门多拉讲了话。他号召人民用统一战线来反对复活法西斯主义的一切企图。
