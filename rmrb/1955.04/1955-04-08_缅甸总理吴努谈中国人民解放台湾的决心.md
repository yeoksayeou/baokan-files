Title: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu on the Chinese People's Determination to Liberate Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1955-04-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th dispatch from Rangoon: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu held a press conference on the 5th. When discussing China's determination to liberate Taiwan, he stated, "People's China, in the recent past, has not just once but repeatedly and emphatically stated its determination to liberate Taiwan. These words are very clear."

On the 2nd, Burmese Minister of Defense U Ba Swe delivered a speech at a mass meeting in Tavoy. When discussing Asian issues, he pointed out, "Taiwan is Chinese territory, and the Chinese people's liberation of Taiwan is purely an internal affair."

### 缅甸总理吴努谈中国人民解放台湾的决心

1955-04-08
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸总理吴努谈中国人民解放台湾的决心
　　新华社七日讯　仰光消息：缅甸总理吴努五日举行记者招待会。他在谈到中国一定要解放台湾时说，“人民中国在最近的过去不止一次而是再三再四地表明，它决心要解放台湾。这些话是非常明白的。”
　　二日，缅甸国防部长吴巴瑞在丹老群众大会上发表演说。他在谈到亚洲问题时指出，“台湾是中国领土，中国人民解放台湾纯然是内政问题”。
