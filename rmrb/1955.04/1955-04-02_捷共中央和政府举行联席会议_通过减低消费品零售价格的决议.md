Title: Joint Meeting of the Central Committee and Government of the Czechoslovak Communist Party Passes Resolution on Reducing Retail Prices of Consumer Goods
Author:
Date: 1955-04-02
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, April 1st. The Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and the Republic Government held a joint meeting in Prague on March 29th. The meeting discussed measures to further improve the living standards of the working people on the basis of the development already achieved in the national economy, and passed a resolution on implementing the fourth reduction of retail prices of consumer goods from April 1st.
    The resolution pointed out that this time the retail prices of more than 16,000 kinds of goods will be reduced. The working people can benefit from this price reduction by 1.3 billion crowns (Czech currency) in one year. If the previous three retail price reductions are added, the people can benefit by 12 billion crowns a year.
    According to the resolution, among the goods with reduced prices, textiles and furniture will be reduced by 30 percent, socks will be reduced by 27 percent, toys will be reduced by 16 percent, cameras will be reduced by 10 percent, watches will be reduced by 8.8 percent, and vegetable oil and rice will be reduced by 10 percent and 8.5 percent respectively.
    The meeting also passed a resolution on partially adjusting the wages of workers in industrial, cultural and educational, and medical departments.

### 捷共中央和政府举行联席会议  通过减低消费品零售价格的决议

1955-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

    捷共中央和政府举行联席会议
    通过减低消费品零售价格的决议
    新华社布拉格一日电　捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会和共和国政府，于三月二十九日在布拉格举行联席会议。会议讨论了关于在国民经济已经获得发展的基础上进一步改善劳动人民生活的措施，并且通过了关于从四月一日起实行第四次减低消费品零售价格的决议。
    决议指出，这一次减低零售价格的货品共有一万六千多种，劳动人民在一年之内可以从这次减价中得到十三亿克朗（捷币名）的好处，如果加上前三次减低零售价格，人民一年可以得到一百二十亿克朗的好处。
    按照决议规定，在减价的货品中，纺织品和家具将减价百分之三十，袜类将减价百分之二十七，玩具将减价百分之十六，照像机将减价百分之十，手表将减价百分之八点八，植物油和大米将分别减价百分之十和八点五。
    会议还通过了关于部分调整工业部门、文化教育和医疗部门工作者的工资的决议。
