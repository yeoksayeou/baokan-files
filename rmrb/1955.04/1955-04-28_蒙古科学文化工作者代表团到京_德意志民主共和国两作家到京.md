Title: Mongolian Scientific and Cultural Workers Delegation Arrives in Beijing; Two Writers from the German Democratic Republic Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-04-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 27th – The Mongolian Scientific and Cultural Workers Delegation, invited to visit our country according to the 1955 executive plan of the Sino-Mongolian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 27th. The head of the delegation is Mashlai, President of the Mongolian National Teachers College. The members include: Nachagdarri, Master of History and Vice Chairman of the Mongolian Science Committee, and Zewigehzabu, Mongolian Meritorious Artist and painter.
Those who went to the airport to welcome them were: Wei Que, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Education of the People's Republic of China, Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Liaison, and many others.

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th – The writers of the German Democratic Republic, Willi Bredel and Willi Meinck, who were invited to visit our country according to the 1955 executive plan of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement, arrived in Beijing by plane on April 27.

Willi Bredel is an academician of the German Academy of Arts, a first-class National Prize winner, an honorary doctor of Rostock University, and a member of the Central Committee of the German Socialist Unity Party. His works "Fathers", "Fifty Days", "A German Soldier's Testament" and others have been translated into Chinese. Willi Meinck is the Director of the Department of Children's and Youth Publications of the German Writers Association. They will stay in China for two months. In addition to general visits, they will also participate in the commemoration meeting of world cultural celebrities to be held in Beijing in early May.

### 蒙古科学文化工作者代表团到京  德意志民主共和国两作家到京

1955-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古科学文化工作者代表团到京
    德意志民主共和国两作家到京
    新华社二十七日讯　根据中蒙文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划，应邀来我国访问的蒙古科学文化工作者代表团在二十七日下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团团长是蒙古国立师范学院院长马什拉伊，团员有：历史学硕士、蒙古科学委员会副主席纳查格达尔日，蒙古功勋艺术家、画家泽维格扎布。
    到机场欢迎的有：中华人民共和国教育部副部长韦悫，对外文化联络局局长洪深等多人。
    新华社二十七日讯　根据中德文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划应邀来我国访问的德意志民主共和国作家威利·布莱德尔和威利·迈恩克在四月二十七日乘飞机到达北京。
    威利·布莱德尔是德国艺术学院院士，一级国家奖金获得者，罗斯托克大学荣誉博士，并为德国统一社会党中央委员。他的作品“父亲们”、“五十天”、“一个德国兵的遗嘱”等已译成中文。威利·迈恩克是德国作家联盟儿童与青年读物部主任。他们在中国将逗留两个月，除作一般参观访问外，并将参加五月初在北京举行的世界文化名人的纪念会。
