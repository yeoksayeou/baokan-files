Title: The People's Bank of China Issues Notice: Recalling Old Currency with Denominations of 5,000 Yuan and Below by a Deadline

Author:

Date: 1955-04-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 10th: Cao Juru, Governor of the People's Bank of China, today issued a notice regarding the recall of various old currencies with denominations of 5,000 yuan and below by a deadline. The full text of the notice is as follows:

According to Article 3 of the order of the State Council of the People's Republic of China on February 21, 1955, concerning the issuance of the new Renminbi and the recall of the existing Renminbi, the time for stopping the circulation of old currency with denominations of 5,000 yuan and below will be appropriately determined separately by the People's Bank of China depending on the exchange situation.

Now that most of the old currency has been recalled, in order to facilitate market circulation and people's exchange, it is hereby decided that various old currencies with denominations of 5,000 yuan and below will cease to circulate in the market on May 10, 1955. However, they can still be exchanged for new currency at the legal ratio at the People's Bank of China and its authorized exchange institutions before June 10, after which exchanges will cease from June 11, 1955.

### 中国人民银行发出通告  限期收回票面额五千元以下的各种旧币

1955-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民银行发出通告
    限期收回票面额五千元以下的各种旧币
    新华社十日讯　中国人民银行行长曹菊如，今天发出关于限期收回票面额五千元以下的各种旧币的通告。通告全文如下：
    根据中华人民共和国国务院一九五五年二月二十一日关于发行新的人民币和收回现行的人民币的命令第三项规定，票面五千元及五千元以下各种旧币停止流通使用的时间，由中国人民银行视收兑情形另行适当规定。
    现旧币已大部收回，为了便于市场流通和人民兑换，特决定票面额五千元及五千元以下各种旧币截至一九五五年五月十日停止在市场上流通使用，但在六月十日前仍可到中国人民银行及代理兑换机构按法定比率兑换新币，从一九五五年六月十一日起停止收兑。
