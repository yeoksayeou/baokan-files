Title: All-China Women's Federation Chairman Cai Chang Hosts Banquet for Mrs. Gordon
Author:
Date: 1955-04-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 24th. Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, Cai Chang, held a banquet on the evening of the 24th to entertain Mrs. Gordon, who is visiting our country, and French women Mrs. Vildeme, Mrs. Alexander, and Mrs. Balantini.

Those present at the banquet included Deng Yingchao, Li Dequan, Xu Guangping, and Zhang Yun, vice-chairpersons of the All-China Women's Federation, and Kang Keqing, secretary-general of the Chinese People's National Committee for the Defence of Children.

Also present were Chen Shutong, vice-chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Zhang Xiruo, president of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Ma Yinchu, president of Peking University; Zeng Zhaolun, vice-chairman of the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies; Hong Shen, vice-president of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, as well as people from the capital's scientific, educational, literary, artistic, medical, and religious circles, and model workers from Beijing.

### 全国妇联主席蔡畅举行宴会招待戈登夫人

1955-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    全国妇联主席蔡畅举行宴会招待戈登夫人
    新华社二十四日讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅在二十四日晚举行宴会，招待前来我国访问的戈登夫人和法国妇女维尔德美夫人、亚历山大夫人和巴朗蒂尼夫人。
    出席作陪的有，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超、李德全、许广平、章蕴，中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会秘书长康克清。
    出席作陪的还有，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，北京大学校长马寅初，中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会副主席曾昭抡，中国人民对外文化协会副会长洪深以及首都科学、教育、文学艺术、医务、宗教界人士和北京市劳动模范等。
