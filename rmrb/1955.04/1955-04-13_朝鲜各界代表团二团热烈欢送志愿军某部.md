Title: Korean People's Delegation No. 2 Warmly Sees Off a Unit of the Chinese People's Volunteers

Author:
Date: 1955-04-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, April 11th: The Korean people's delegation No. 2 for bidding farewell to the Chinese People's Volunteers returning home, along with the Ministry of Transportation's Art Troupe, held a grand farewell meeting at 1:00 PM on April 9th at the headquarters of a unit of the Chinese People's Volunteers returning home. The meeting was attended by all members of the delegation, representatives of the CPV units returning home, representatives of the CPV units remaining in Korea, and representatives from the Korean People's Army Air Force, anti-aircraft artillery units, and local governments.

    The head of the delegation, Baek Nam-un, Minister of Education of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, read out a letter of gratitude from the Central Committee of the Democratic Front for the Reunification of the Fatherland of Korea to all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteers returning home at the farewell meeting. Jeong Su-yeong, Vice Chairman of the North Pyongan Province Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, delivered a farewell speech at the meeting. Subsequently, delegation members Park Tae-joon, Vice Minister of Labor of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and Major General Yoon Kun-chang, Deputy Director of the General Political Bureau of the Korean People's Army, presented flags to the returning CPV units on behalf of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, the Korean Cabinet, the Central Committee of the Democratic Front for the Reunification of the Fatherland of Korea, and the Supreme Command of the Korean People's Army, respectively. The banner of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party was inscribed with the words: "Deep gratitude and honor to the brave warriors who, under the sacred banner of resisting America and aiding Korea, have made brilliant combat achievements in the Fatherland Liberation War of the Korean people. We wish you even more glorious achievements in your future endeavors." Representatives of the People's Committees of various counties in North Pyongan Province also presented sixteen banners and two hundred and nineteen congratulatory telegrams from the people of various places to the returning CPV units at the meeting. An air force unit of the Korean People's Army also presented the returning CPV units with a commemorative album. The album contained twenty-two consolation letters, decorated with color, written by commanders, pilots, and ground crew of the People's Army Air Force.

    After a representative of the CPV units remaining in Korea delivered a farewell speech at the meeting, Yang Zhonghang, a representative of a unit of the CPV returning home, delivered a farewell speech at the meeting. He asked the delegation to convey the respect and gratitude of all commanders and fighters of the returning units to the heroic Korean people and Marshal Kim Il-sung.

    After the farewell meeting, the actors of the Korean Ministry of Transportation's Art Troupe, who had come with the delegation, gave a performance.

### 朝鲜各界代表团二团热烈欢送志愿军某部

1955-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜各界代表团二团热烈欢送志愿军某部
    新华社平壤十一日电　朝鲜各界人民组成的欢送中国人民志愿军归国部队代表团第二团和随行的交通省文艺工作团，四月九日下午一时在中国人民志愿军归国部队某部领导机关驻地举行盛大的欢送大会。参加大会的有代表团全体团员、志愿军归国部队代表、留朝部队代表和朝鲜人民军空军部队、高射炮部队以及地方政府的代表等。
    代表团团长、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁教育相白南云在欢送会上宣读了朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会给中国人民志愿军归国部队全体指挥员、战斗员的感谢信。朝鲜劳动党平安北道委员会副委员长郑洙永在会上作了送别讲话。接着，代表团团员、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国劳动省副相朴泰俊和朝鲜人民军总政治局副局长尹君昌少将分别代表朝鲜劳动党中央委员会、朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会、朝鲜内阁、朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会和朝鲜人民军最高司令部向归国的志愿军部队献旗。朝鲜劳动党中央委员会的锦旗上写着：“向在抗美援朝的神圣旗帜下，在朝鲜人民的祖国解放战争中立下了灿烂的战斗伟勋的勇士们致以深深的感谢和荣誉。预祝你们在今后的事业中，取得更光辉的成就。”平安北道各郡人民委员会代表也在会上向志愿军归国部队献赠了十六面锦旗和各地人民给志愿军归国部队的致敬电二百一十九件。朝鲜人民军空军某部还赠给志愿军归国部队一本纪念册。纪念册里夹着人民军空军指挥员、飞行员和地勤人员写的、用彩色装饰着的二十二封慰问信。
    中国人民志愿军留朝部队代表在会上致欢送词以后，志愿军归国部队某部代表杨中行在会上作了告别讲话。他请代表团把归国部队全体指挥员、战斗员的敬意和谢意转致英雄的朝鲜人民和金日成元帅。
    欢送会结束后，随代表团前来的朝鲜交通省文艺工作团的演员们，进行了慰问演出。
