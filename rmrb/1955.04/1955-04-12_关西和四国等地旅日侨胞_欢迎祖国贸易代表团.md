Title: Overseas Chinese in Kansai and Shikoku Welcome Homeland Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-04-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Osaka, April 11th: Overseas Chinese in Kansai and Shikoku held a grand banquet in Osaka on the evening of the 10th to welcome the Chinese Trade Delegation visiting Japan.

A total of 170 representatives of overseas Chinese from Osaka, Kobe, Kyoto, Wakayama, Shikoku and other places attended the banquet.

Wang Juncai, Chairman of the Kansai Overseas Chinese Welcome Homeland Delegation Committee, delivered a welcoming speech, expressing the overseas Chinese's support and love for the motherland.

Head of Delegation Lei Renmin, in his speech, conveyed the cordial care and regards of the motherland to the overseas Chinese, and expressed his respect for the overseas Chinese's love for the motherland and their contributions to the cause of the motherland. Lei Renmin told the overseas Chinese about the great socialist construction that the people of the motherland are engaged in with unparalleled enthusiasm. His speech was met with prolonged applause from the attending overseas Chinese representatives.

During the banquet, Chinese students studying in Japan sang songs such as "Ode to the Motherland." The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere throughout.

### 关西和四国等地旅日侨胞  欢迎祖国贸易代表团

1955-04-12
第1版()
专栏：

    关西和四国等地旅日侨胞
    欢迎祖国贸易代表团
    新华社大阪十一日电　关西和四国等地的旅日华侨十日晚在大阪举行盛大的宴会，欢迎中国访问日本贸易代表团。
    出席宴会的有大阪、神户、京都、和歌山、四国等地的华侨代表共一百七十人。
    关西华侨欢迎祖国代表团委员会委员长王俊才致欢迎词，表示了旅日华侨对祖国的拥护和热爱。
    雷任民团长在致词时转达了祖国对侨胞们的亲切关怀和慰问，并对旅日侨胞热爱祖国和对祖国事业的贡献表示敬意。雷任民把祖国人民正以无比的热情从事于伟大的社会主义建设的情况告诉了华侨。他的讲话获得了到会华侨代表们经久不息的掌声。
    席间，中国留日学生演唱了“歌唱祖国”等歌曲。宴会始终在热情友爱的空气中进行。
