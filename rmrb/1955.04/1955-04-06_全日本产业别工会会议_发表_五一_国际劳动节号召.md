Title: All Japan Federation of Industrial Labor Unions Issues May Day Appeal
Author:
Date: 1955-04-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, April 5th, TASS reported from Tokyo on the 4th in the "Akahata" newspaper: The Executive Committee of the All Japan Federation of Industrial Labor Unions has issued an appeal for the "May Day" International Labor Day. The main calls are: Oppose preparations for atomic war, defend trade union rights and democratic freedoms, and achieve unity of action by the working class in the struggle to increase wages and eliminate unemployment.

### 全日本产业别工会会议  发表“五一”国际劳动节号召

1955-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

    全日本产业别工会会议
    发表“五一”国际劳动节号召
    新华社五日电　塔斯社东京四日讯：“赤旗报”四日报道：全日本产业别工会会议执行委员会已经发表“五一”国际劳动节的号召。主要的号召是：反对准备原子战争，保卫工会权利和民主自由，在争取提高工资和消灭失业的斗争中工人阶级的行动统一。
