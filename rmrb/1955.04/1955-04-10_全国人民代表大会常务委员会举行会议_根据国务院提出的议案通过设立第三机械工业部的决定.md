Title: Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Holds Meeting; Decision to Establish the Third Ministry of Machine Building Approved Based on the State Council's Proposal

Author: 
Date: 1955-04-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 9th—The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its tenth and eleventh meetings successively on April 8th and 9th.

    At the tenth meeting, the committee heard a report from Li Dazhang, Governor of Sichuan Province, on agricultural production and rural work in Sichuan Province.

    At the eleventh meeting, the committee heard a work report from Tao Zhu, Governor of Guangdong Province, on agricultural production and issues concerning overseas Chinese in Guangdong Province. At this meeting, based on the proposal submitted by the State Council, a decision was approved to establish the Third Ministry of Machine Building of the People's Republic of China, responsible for leading local state-owned, joint state-private, cooperative, and private machinery and electrical industries; approved the State Council's establishment of the Urban Construction General Bureau; and passed appointments.

### 全国人民代表大会常务委员会举行会议  根据国务院提出的议案通过设立第三机械工业部的决定

1955-04-10
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常务委员会举行会议
    根据国务院提出的议案通过设立第三机械工业部的决定
    新华社九日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在四月八日、九日先后举行了第十、第十一次会议。
    在第十次会议上，听取了四川省省长李大章关于四川省农业生产和农村工作的报告。
    在第十一次会议上，听取了广东省省长陶铸关于广东省农业生产和华侨问题的工作报告。这次会议还根据国务院提出的议案，通过决定设立中华人民共和国第三机械工业部，负责领导地方国营、公私合营、合作社营和私营的机械、电机工业；批准国务院设立城市建设总局；通过了任命事项。
