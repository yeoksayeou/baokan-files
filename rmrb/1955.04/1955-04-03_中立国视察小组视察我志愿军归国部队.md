Title: Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission Inspects Chinese People's Volunteers Returning Troops
Author: 
Date: 1955-04-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, April 2nd—According to the Korean Central News Agency, Sinuiju, April 1st: Personnel from the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission stationed in Sinuiju inspected the Chinese People's Volunteers returning troops by train on March 31st.

　　The Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission was divided into two groups, one consisting of Polish and Swiss members, and the other consisting of Czechoslovak and Swedish members. Each group was accompanied by staff from the four countries. The inspection teams investigated each carriage.

　　When the inspection team members arrived at the door of each carriage, the person in charge of the carriage reported the number of returning personnel and the classification of weapons and ammunition in the carriage. Each group entered the carriages and inspected according to the report to determine whether the report was accurate.

　　The Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission enjoyed complete freedom and full convenience during this inspection, as in the past. Liaison officers from the Korean and Chinese sides actively cooperated and assisted the work of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission.

　　The first day of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission's inspection work was successfully completed within three hours.

　　The Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission will continue its inspection work.

### 中立国视察小组视察我志愿军归国部队

1955-04-03
第1版()
专栏：

    中立国视察小组视察我志愿军归国部队
    新华社平壤二日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社新义州一日讯：驻在新义州的中立国视察小组人员，在三月三十一日视察了乘车返国的中国人民志愿军归国部队。
    中立国视察小组分为两个分组，一个分组由波兰和瑞士成员组成，另一分组由捷克斯洛伐克和瑞典成员组成，每一分组都带有四国工作人员。视察小组对每节车厢进行了调查。
    视察小组人员到达每节车厢门口的时候，车内负责人员就向他们报告车厢内归国人员的数目和武器弹药的分类数字。每一分组都进入车厢，根据报告进行视察，借以断定报告是否确实。
    中立国视察小组这次进行视察时同以往一样，享有完全的自由和充分的便利。朝中方面的联络官对中立国视察小组的工作给以积极的合作和帮助。
    中立国视察小组第一天的视察工作在三小时内顺利完毕。
    中立国视察小组将继续进行视察工作。
