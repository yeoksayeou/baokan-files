Title: Chen Shutong Hosts a Banquet in Honor of Mrs. Gordon
Author:
Date: 1955-04-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 29th to welcome Mrs. Eugénie Cotton, Vice-President of the World Peace Council, and French women Mrs. Wildemeersch, Mrs. Alexandre, and Mrs. Barantin, who are visiting our country.

Present at the banquet were Mao Dun, Ma Yinchu, Cai Tingkai, and Li Dequan, members of the World Peace Council in Beijing.

Also present at the banquet were Xu Deheng, Xu Baoku, and Luo Longji, standing committee members of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Zhang Xiruo, chairman of the Beijing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace.

Also present at the banquet were Xu Guangping, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and Kang Keqing, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's National Committee for the Defense of Children.

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 陈叔通举行宴会招待戈登夫人

1955-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    陈叔通举行宴会招待戈登夫人
    新华社二十九日　讯中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通在二十九日晚举行宴会，招待前来我国访问的世界和平理事会副主席欧仁妮·戈登夫人和法国妇女维尔德美夫人、亚历山大夫人、巴朗蒂尼夫人。
    出席宴会的有，世界和平理事会在京理事茅盾、马寅初、蔡廷锴、李德全。
    出席宴会的还有，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员许德珩、许宝驹、罗隆基，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会北京市分会主席张奚若。
    出席宴会的还有，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平，中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会秘书长康克清。
    宴会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
