Title: Vice Premier Pham Van Dong Visits Burma at the Invitation of India After His Visit to India
Author:
Date: 1955-04-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 12th: After visiting India, Pham Van Dong, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, arrived in Rangoon by plane at 5:00 PM on the 11th to visit Burma at the invitation of Burmese Prime Minister U Nu.
　　Prime Minister U Nu personally welcomed him at the airport.
　　Also at the airport to welcome him were Burmese Foreign Minister Sao Khun Hkio and other government officials, the Soviet, Chinese, and Indian ambassadors to Burma, and other foreign diplomatic representatives to Burma.
　　The representative of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to Burma also welcomed him at the airport.
　　After disembarking, Vice Premier Pham Van Dong received flowers presented by Burmese children.
　　That evening, Prime Minister U Nu held a reception to welcome Vice Premier Pham Van Dong. Burmese music and dances were performed at the reception.

### 范文同副总理访问印度后应邀到缅甸访问

1955-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    范文同副总理访问印度后应邀到缅甸访问
    新华社十二日讯　仰光消息：越南民主共和国副总理兼外交部长范文同，在访问印度以后，应缅甸联邦总理吴努的邀请，在十一日下午五时乘飞机到达仰光访问。
    吴努总理亲自到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的还有缅甸外交部长藻昆卓和其他政府官员，苏联、中国、印度驻缅甸的大使，以及其他国家驻缅甸的外交代表。
    越南民主共和国驻缅甸代表也到机场欢迎。
    范文同副总理下飞机后，接受了缅甸儿童的献花。
    当天晚上，吴努总理举行了招待会欢迎范文同副总理。会上演出了缅甸音乐和舞蹈。
