Title: Economic and Cultural Committees of the Asian-African Conference Hold Separate Meetings
Author:
Date: 1955-04-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Bandung, April 19th. The Economic Committee of the Asian-African Conference held its first meeting on the morning of the 19th, presided over by the Indonesian Minister of Economic Affairs, Roosseno. Chinese representative Ye Jizhuang attended the meeting. The next meeting will be held on the morning of the 20th.

Xinhua News Agency, Bandung, April 19th. A press communique issued by the Economic Committee of the Asian-African Conference stated that the Economic Committee held a meeting on the morning of the 19th, at which the meeting unanimously elected Professor Roosseno, the Indonesian Minister of Economic Affairs, as the chairman of the committee.

Roosseno emphasized in his speech the importance of the economic problems facing Asian and African countries, and requested everyone to concentrate manpower and material resources to achieve common goals. He emphasized the urgency of development, especially mentioning technological advancements and skills aimed at raising living standards. The committee adopted the following agenda: 1. Cooperation in economic development. 2. Cooperation in developing trade. 3. Cooperation in other areas. 4. Promoting the peaceful uses of nuclear energy. 5. Organizational issues. The committee decided to include summaries of discussions on each of the five agenda items in the report to the conference.

Xinhua News Agency, Bandung, April 19th. The Cultural Committee of the Asian-African Conference held its first meeting on the morning of the 19th, presided over by the Indonesian Minister of Education and Culture, Muhammad Yamin. Chinese representative Huang Zhen attended the meeting of the Cultural Committee. The next meeting will be held on the morning of the 20th.

### 亚非会议经济和文化委员会分别举行会议

1955-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    亚非会议经济和文化委员会分别举行会议
    新华社万隆十九日电　亚非会议经济委员会十九日上午在印度尼西亚经济部长罗西诺主持下召开第一次会议。中国代表叶季壮出席了会议。下一次会议将在二十日上午举行。
    新华社万隆十九日电　亚非会议经济委员会发表的新闻公报说，经济委员会在十九日早晨举行了会议，会议一致选举印度尼西亚经济部长罗西诺教授为委员会主席。
    罗西诺在发言中强调指出亚非国家所面临的经济问题的重要性，要求大家集中人力和物力以达到共同的目标。他强调指出发展的迫切性，特别提到目的在于提高生活水平的技术进步和技能。委员会通过下列议程：一、经济发展方面的合作问题。二、发展贸易方面的合作问题。三、其他方面的合作问题。四、促进核子能的和平用途问题。五、组织方面的问题。委员会决定把就五项议程中的每一项所进行的讨论的摘要编入向会议提出的报告中。
    新华社万隆十九日电　亚非会议文化委员会十九日早晨在印度尼西亚文教部部长穆罕默德·雅明主持下举行了第一次会议。中国代表黄镇出席了文化委员会会议。下次会议将在二十日早晨举行。
