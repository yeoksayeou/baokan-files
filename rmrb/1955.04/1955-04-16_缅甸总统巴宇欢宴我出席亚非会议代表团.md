Title: Burmese President Ba U Hosts Banquet for Chinese Delegation to Asian-African Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-04-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 15th: The President of the Union of Burma, Dr. Ba U, hosted a banquet at 8:00 PM on the 14th in Rangoon to entertain Zhou Enlai, the chief representative of the People's Republic of China delegation to the Asian-African Conference and Premier and Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of China, as well as representatives Chen Yi, Ye Jizhuang, Zhang Hanfu, and all advisors. From the Burmese side, those attending the banquet included Prime Minister U Nu, Foreign Minister Sao Khun Hkio, Chief Justice of the Supreme Court U Thein Maung, Burmese Ambassador to the United States U Ba Lwin, and other senior officials.

    The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 缅甸总统巴宇欢宴我出席亚非会议代表团

1955-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸总统巴宇欢宴我出席亚非会议代表团
    新华社仰光十五日电　缅甸联邦总统巴宇博士，在十四日下午八时设宴招待现在仰光的中华人民共和国出席亚非会议代表团首席代表、中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来，代表陈毅、叶季壮、章汉夫以及全体顾问。缅甸方面出席宴会的有总理吴努，外交部长藻昆卓、最高法院首席法官吴登貌、缅甸驻美国大使巴林顿等高级官员。
    宴会在友好空气中进行。
