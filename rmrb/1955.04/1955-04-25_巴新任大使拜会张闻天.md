Title: New Pakistani Ambassador Calls on Zhang Wentian
Author:
Date: 1955-04-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd — Pakistan's newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Sultanuddin Ahmad, accompanied by Counselor Sultan Muhammad Khan, paid a visit to Vice Minister Zhang Wentian of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs on the morning of April 22nd to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials. Also present were Zhang Wenjin, Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 巴新任大使拜会张闻天

1955-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    巴新任大使拜会张闻天
    新华社二十二日讯　巴基斯坦新任驻我国特命全权大使苏尔丹乌丁·阿哈默德偕参赞苏尔唐·穆罕默德·汗，在二十二日上午到我国外交部拜会张闻天副部长，商谈递交国书事宜。在座的有外交部第二亚洲司司长章文晋、礼宾司司长柯华。
