Title: Zhang Zhixiang Hosts Banquet for the People's Song and Dance Troupe of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1955-04-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 21st—Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Minister of Culture of the People's Republic of China, held a banquet at the Beijing International Club at noon on the 21st to welcome all members of the People's Song and Dance Troupe of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, headed by Director Liu Zhonglu.

Zhou Liang, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, and embassy staff were also invited to the banquet.

Among those present were Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the People's Republic of China, Feng Ji-ping, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and more than 60 people from literary and art circles in the capital.

At the banquet, Vice Minister Zhang Zhixiang and Director Liu Zhonglu delivered speeches successively, wishing the great friendship between the people of China and Vietnam further consolidation and development.

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 张致祥欢宴越南民主共和国人民歌舞团

1955-04-22
第1版()
专栏：

    张致祥欢宴越南民主共和国人民歌舞团
    新华社二十一日讯　二十一日中午，中华人民共和国文化部副部长张致祥在北京国际俱乐部举行宴会，欢迎以刘仲庐团长为首的越南民主共和国人民歌舞团全体人员。
    应邀出席宴会的还有越南民主共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办周亮和使馆人员。
    出席作陪的有中华人民共和国对外文化联络局局长洪深，北京市副市长冯基平和首都文学艺术界人士等六十多人。
    宴会上，张致祥副部长和刘仲庐团长先后致词，祝中越两国人民的伟大友谊进一步巩固和发展。
    宴会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
