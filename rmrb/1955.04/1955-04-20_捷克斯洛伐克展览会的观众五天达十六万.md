Title: The Czechoslovak Exhibition Attracts 160,000 Visitors in Five Days
Author: 
Date: 1955-04-20
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 19th: The Exhibition of Achievements in Ten Years of Socialist Construction in Czechoslovakia has been warmly welcomed by the Chinese people. In the past five days, 160,000 people in the capital have visited the exhibition. In addition, more than 290 factories, schools, organizations, and military units have registered with the exhibition office and will come to visit one after another.

### 捷克斯洛伐克展览会的观众五天达十六万

1955-04-20
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克展览会的观众五天达十六万
    新华社十九日讯　捷克斯洛伐克十年社会主义建设成就展览会受到我国人民热烈欢迎。五天来，首都人民参观展览会的已达十六万人。另有二百九十多个工厂、学校、机关和部队已经到展览会办公室登记，将陆续前来参观。
