Title: Eden Announces Date of British General Election
Author:
Date: 1955-04-17
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, London, April 16th: British Prime Minister Eden announced in a special broadcast in London on the evening of the 15th that the British general election would be held on May 26th. The current parliament will be dissolved on May 6th. The new parliament will begin its work on June 7th.

### 艾登宣布英国大选日期

1955-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

    艾登宣布英国大选日期
    新华社十六日讯　伦敦消息：英国首相艾登十五日晚在伦敦发表特别广播演说时宣布：英国将在五月二十六日举行大选。本届议会将在五月六日解散。新的议会将在六月七日开始工作。
