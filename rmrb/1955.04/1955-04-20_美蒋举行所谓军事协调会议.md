Title: U.S.-Chiang Hold So-Called Military Coordination Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-04-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 19th dispatch: A representative of Pacific U.S. Forces Commander Stuempfle and representatives of the Chiang bandit "Ministry of National Defense" began holding a so-called military coordination conference in Taipei on the 18th to discuss taking joint measures to obstruct the Chinese people's liberation of Taiwan and the coastal islands. Before the meeting, Stuempfle had been summoned to report to U.S. President Eisenhower on the situation in the Taiwan area.

It is reported that the purpose of this U.S.-Chiang military conference is to "carry out military integration and cooperation." The agenda includes issues such as training, logistics, intelligence, and the establishment of a "joint center" to uniformly allocate U.S. and Chiang armed forces.

### 美蒋举行所谓军事协调会议

1955-04-20
第4版()
专栏：

    美蒋举行所谓军事协调会议
    新华社十九日讯　太平洋美军总司令斯图普的代表和蒋贼“国防部”代表十八日起在台北举行所谓军事协调会议，商讨采取共同措施来阻挠中国人民解放台湾和沿海岛屿。在会议举行以前，斯图普曾经奉召向美国总统艾森豪威尔报告台湾地区局势。
    有消息说，这次美蒋军事会议的目的在于“进行军事统筹和合作”，议程有训练、后勤、情报和设立一个“联合中心”以便统一调配美蒋武装部队等问题。
