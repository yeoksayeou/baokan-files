Title: Indonesian and British Ships Salvage Destroyed and Crashed Indian Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1955-04-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 25th. According to information already received, the Indonesian investigation committee responsible for investigating the destruction and crash of the Indian aircraft "Kashmir Princess" has concluded its investigation in Singapore. Committee Chairman Imawang said on the 23rd that Indonesian and British ships will participate in the salvage of the aircraft wreckage.

The Indian Ministry of External Affairs issued an announcement on the 24th stating that at the request of the Indian and Indonesian governments, the British ship "Balfour" had sailed from Singapore to the scene of the incident on the 23rd to carry out salvage operations. All salvaged wreckage and other possible evidence will be submitted to the Indonesian investigation committee.

### 印度尼西亚和英国船只  打捞被破坏失事的印度飞机

1955-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚和英国船只
    打捞被破坏失事的印度飞机
    新华社二十五日讯　根据已经得到的消息，负责调查印度飞机“克什米尔公主号”被破坏失事事件的印度尼西亚调查委员会已经结束了在新加坡进行的调查。委员会主席伊马汪二十三日说，印度尼西亚和英国船只将参加打捞飞机残骸的工作。
    印度外交部二十四日发表公告说，在印度和印度尼西亚政府的要求下，英船“巴尔弗号”二十三日已由新加坡驶往出事地点从事打捞。所有打捞起来的残骸和其他可能得到的证据将提交印度尼西亚的调查委员会。
