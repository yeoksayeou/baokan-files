Title: Delegations from the Soviet Union, China, Japan, North Korea, Mongolia, Vietnam, etc., visit Gandhi's cremation site and lay wreaths
Author:
Date: 1955-04-08
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 7th. Leaders and many members of the delegations from the Soviet Union, China, Japan, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Mongolia, and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, attending the Asian Countries Conference, visited Gandhi's cremation site and laid wreaths on the morning of the 7th.

### 苏、中、日、朝、蒙、越等国代表团  谒甘地火葬处并献花圈

1955-04-08
第1版()
专栏：

    苏、中、日、朝、蒙、越等国代表团
　　谒甘地火葬处并献花圈
　　新华社新德里七日电　出席亚洲国家会议的苏联、中国、日本、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、蒙古和越南民主共和国代表团的领导人及许多团员七日上午谒甘地火葬处并献花圈。
