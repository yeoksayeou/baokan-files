Title: Wladyslaw Jóźwiak-Witold Appointed Deputy Chairman of the Polish Council of Ministers
Author:
Date: 1955-04-20
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, April 18th. According to a report by the Polish Press Agency on the 17th, the State Council of the Polish People's Republic, at the request of Chairman of the Council of Ministers Cyrankiewicz, decided to appoint Wladyslaw Jóźwiak-Witold, the former Minister of State Control, as Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and to appoint Roman Zambrowski as Minister of State Control.

### 弗·约兹维亚克—韦托德被任命为波兰部长会议副主席

1955-04-20
第4版()
专栏：

    弗·约兹维亚克—韦托德被任命为波兰部长会议副主席
    新华社华沙十八日讯　据波兰通讯社十七日报道：波兰人民共和国国务委员会根据部长会议主席西伦凯维兹的要求，决定任命原任国家监察部部长弗·约兹维亚克—韦托德为部长会议副主席，并任命罗·萨姆布罗夫斯基为国家监察部部长。
