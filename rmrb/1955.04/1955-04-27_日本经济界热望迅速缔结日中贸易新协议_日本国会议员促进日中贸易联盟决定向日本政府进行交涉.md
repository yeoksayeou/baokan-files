Title: Japanese Economic Circles Eager for the Rapid Conclusion of a New Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement; League of Japanese Diet Members to Promote Sino-Japanese Trade Decides to Negotiate with the Japanese Government
Author:
Date: 1955-04-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, April 26th: The League of Japanese Diet Members to Promote Sino-Japanese Trade held an extraordinary general meeting on the 25th to discuss how to expedite an agreement in the Sino-Japanese trade talks.

    Katsumada Seiichi, chairman of the extraordinary general meeting, told the Japanese press after the meeting that the conclusion reached at the meeting was that the Japanese government needed to make further efforts to resolve issues such as the conclusion of a payment agreement between China and Japan and the mutual establishment of permanent trade representative offices. The extraordinary general meeting decided to establish a negotiating committee to negotiate with the Japanese government on this issue and to make the decision of this meeting a joint resolution of all parties, to be submitted to the Diet for approval.

    While the extraordinary general meeting was being held, seven organizations, including the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, the welcoming committee for the delegation of small and medium-sized enterprises related to trade with China, the Sino-Japanese Trade Local Countermeasures Committee, the China-Japan Trade Association, the National Council of Trade Unions for Promoting Sino-Japanese Trade, the Japan-China Friendship Association, and the National Conference for the Restoration of Diplomatic Relations between Japan and China and Japan and the Soviet Union, submitted petitions to the League of Japanese Diet Members to Promote Sino-Japanese Trade. The petition stated that Japan's economic circles are eagerly hoping for the rapid conclusion of a trade agreement that can expand Sino-Japanese trade. We hope that the parliamentary league will urge the Japanese government to ensure the lifting of the embargo, the conclusion of a payment agreement between the national banks of the two countries, and the mutual establishment of permanent trade representative offices with official authority.

    According to Xinhua News Agency Tokyo dispatch, five organizations, including the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, the welcoming committee for the delegation of small and medium-sized enterprises related to trade with China, the Sino-Japanese Trade Local Countermeasures Committee, the China-Japan Trade Association, and the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan (Sohyo), submitted a petition to the Japanese Prime Minister, the Ministry of International Trade and Industry, the Ministry of Finance, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Ministry of Transport, the Ministry of Agriculture and Forestry, and various Japanese political parties on the 21st, requesting the Japanese government to show sincerity towards the new Sino-Japanese trade agreement in order to meet the expectations of the Japanese people.

### 日本经济界热望迅速缔结日中贸易新协议  日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟决定向日本政府进行交涉

1955-04-27
第4版()
专栏：

    日本经济界热望迅速缔结日中贸易新协议
    日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟决定向日本政府进行交涉
    新华社东京二十六日电　日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟二十五日召开了临时总会，讨论怎样促使日中贸易谈判迅速达成协议的问题。
    临时总会主席胜间田清一在会后向日本报界发表谈话说：这次会议得出的结论认为，关于缔结中日两国间的支付协定和互设常驻贸易代表机构等问题的解决，需要日本政府作进一步的努力。临时总会决定成立一个交涉委员会就这个问题向日本政府进行交涉，并使这次会议的决定成为各党派的共同决议案，以便提交国会通过。
    在临时总会举行时，日本国际贸易促进协会、中小企业关系中国贸易代表团欢迎会、日中贸易地方对策委员会、中日贸易会、促进日中贸易全国工会协议会、日中友好协会和恢复日中、日苏邦交国民会议七个团体向日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟提出了请愿书。请愿书说：日本的经济界热烈希望迅速缔结一个能够扩大日中贸易的贸易协议。我们希望议员联盟督促日本政府保证作到解除禁运、缔结两国国家银行间的支付协定和互设有官方权限的常驻贸易代表机构。
    据新华社东京电　日本国际贸易促进协会、中小企业关系中国贸易代表团欢迎会、日中贸易地方对策委员会、中日贸易会和日本工会总评议会五个团体，二十一日向日本首相、通商产业省、大藏省、外务省、运输省、农林省以及日本各政党提出一项请愿书，要求日本政府对新的中日贸易协议表现诚意，以符合日本国民的期望。
