Title: Bulgarian Newspapers Publish News of the Chinese Communist Party National Congress
Author:
Date: 1955-04-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, April 8th. Sofia News: Bulgarian "Workers' Affairs Newspaper" and other newspapers in Sofia successively published news of the Chinese Communist Party National Congress on the 6th and 7th.

### 保加利亚报纸  刊载中共全国代表会议的消息

1955-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

    保加利亚报纸
    刊载中共全国代表会议的消息
    新华社布拉格八日电　索非亚消息：保加利亚“工人事业报”和索非亚其他报纸，在六日和七日相继刊载了中国共产党全国代表会议的消息。
