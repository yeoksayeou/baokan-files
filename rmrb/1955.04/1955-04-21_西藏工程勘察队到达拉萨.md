Title: Tibet Engineering Survey Team Arrives in Lhasa
Author:
Date: 1955-04-21
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency

    Tibet Engineering Survey Team Arrives in Lhasa

    The majority of the members of the Tibet Engineering Survey Team, jointly formed by the Ministry of Fuel Industry, the Ministry of Geology, the Ministry of Water Resources, and the Ministry of Local Industry, arrived in Lhasa on the 16th. According to the State Council's decision to assist Tibet in construction matters, they will conduct surveying and design work for projects such as the Lhasa and Xigaze hydropower stations, the Lhasa River and Nyang Chu River embankment and dam construction projects, the Xigaze small thermal power plant, and the Lhasa small iron factory and leather factory.

    While passing through Qamdo, Zhamu and other places, the Tibet Engineering Survey Team helped the Qamdo Regional People's Liberation Committee and its Bomi Office with preliminary surveys of local water sources, providing some design data for the construction of hydropower stations. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 西藏工程勘察队到达拉萨

1955-04-21
第2版()
专栏：

    西藏工程勘察队到达拉萨
    由燃料工业部、地质部、水利部和地方工业部联合组成的西藏工程勘察队的大部分队员，十六日到达拉萨。他们将根据国务院关于帮助西藏地方进行建设事项的决定，给拉萨、日喀则水力发电站，拉萨河、年楚河的河堤水坝建筑工程，日喀则小型火力发电厂，拉萨小型铁工厂和皮革厂等项建设进行勘测、设计工作。
    西藏工程勘察队在途经昌都、札木等地时，曾帮助昌都地区人民解放委员会及其波密办事处初步勘察了当地水源，为建筑水力发电站提供了部分设计资料。　　　　　　　　　　（新华社）
