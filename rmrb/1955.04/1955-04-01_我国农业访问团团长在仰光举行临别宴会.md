Title: Head of Chinese Agricultural Delegation Holds Farewell Banquet in Rangoon
Author: 
Date: 1955-04-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, March 30th: Liu Rui-long, head of the Chinese agricultural delegation currently visiting Burma, held a farewell banquet in Rangoon on the 29th.

Among those invited to the banquet were: Burmese Union Government Minister of Agriculture and Forestry Thakin Kyaw Dun, Minister of Foreign Affairs Sao Khun Kyoe, Supreme Court Judge U Myint Thein, Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities of Parliament Sao Hkun Hkio, Honorary Chairman of the Burma-China Friendship Association Thakin Kodaw Hmaing, Chairman Dr. Htin Baw, and Chairman of the Burma-Soviet Cultural Association U Aung Ra, among others.

Also invited to the banquet were the ambassadors of the Soviet Union, India, Yugoslavia, Pakistan and other countries to Burma, as well as other foreign envoys and diplomatic officials.

Chinese Ambassador to Burma Yao Zhongming and personnel from the embassy also attended the banquet.

In his speech at the banquet, head of delegation Liu Rui-long said that the delegation had extensively exchanged views on agricultural production with relevant parties in Burma during the visit. He expressed his hope that this kind of exchange and sharing of experiences, as well as the peaceful and friendly relations between China and Burma, would be further developed.

In his speech, Thakin Kyaw Dun pointed out that the exchange of experience between Burma and China will contribute to the economic development of both countries and will be conducive to maintaining peace in Asia and the world.

At the banquet, head of delegation Liu Rui-long presented the Burmese Union Government with Chinese films and pictures about agricultural production, double-wheeled double-shared plows, and crop seeds. After accepting these gifts on behalf of the Burmese Union Government, Thakin Kyaw Dun presented the Chinese government with a Burmese baby elephant, crop seeds, books and publications on agriculture, and Burmese native handicrafts.

The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere throughout.

### 我国农业访问团团长在仰光举行临别宴会

1955-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我国农业访问团团长在仰光举行临别宴会
    新华社三十日讯　仰光消息：正在访问缅甸的中国农业访问团团长刘瑞龙二十九日在仰光举行临别宴会。
    应邀出席宴会的有：缅甸联邦政府农林部长德钦觉顿、外交部长藻昆卓、最高法院法官吴敏登，国会民族院议长萧恢塔、缅中友好协会名誉主席德钦哥都迈、主席信波博士和缅苏文化协会主席吴昂拉等。
    应邀出席宴会的还有：苏联、印度、南斯拉夫、巴基斯坦等国驻缅甸大使以及其他各国的使节和外交官员。
    中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和大使馆人员也出席了宴会。
    刘瑞龙团长在宴会上致辞说，代表团在访问过程中和缅甸有关方面广泛地交换了有关农业生产方面的意见。他表示希望这种来往和经验交流以及中缅两国的和平友好关系得到进一步的发展。
    德钦觉顿在致辞中指出，缅中两国经验的交流，将有助于两国经济的发展，并有利于维护亚洲和世界的和平。
    在宴会上，刘瑞龙团长将中国有关农业生产的电影片、图片、双轮双铧犁和农作物种籽赠给缅甸联邦政府。德钦觉顿代表缅甸联邦政府接受这些礼品后，将缅甸小象一头、农作物种籽、有关农业的书刊以及缅甸土产手工艺品赠给中国政府。
    宴会始终在友好的气氛中进行。
