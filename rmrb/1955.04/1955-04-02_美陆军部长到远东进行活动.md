Title: US Army Secretary Conducts Activities in the Far East
Author:
Date: 1955-04-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 31st: U.S. Army Secretary Stevens has been in the Far East for the past few days arranging American war plans. After spending two days in South Korea on the 29th and 30th, he is now in Tokyo preparing to hold talks with the Director General of Japan's "Defense Agency," Arao Sugihara. Stevens will fly to Taiwan via Okinawa on April 2nd. According to reports, Stevens' trip to Taiwan is for a final "battlefield review" of detailed plans for American aggressive activities in the Taiwan Strait.

At the same time, Lou A. Shaw, Deputy Director of Intelligence for the U.S. Army Department, is currently in Taiwan discussing the establishment of a joint intelligence agency with the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique. Sources indicate that his activities are related to those of Stevens.

### 美陆军部长到远东进行活动

1955-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

    美陆军部长到远东进行活动
    新华社三十一日讯　美国陆军部长斯蒂文斯这几天正在远东安排美国的战争计划。他在二十九日和三十日在南朝鲜活动了两天后，又在东京准备和日本“防卫厅”长官杉原荒太举行会谈。斯蒂文斯将在四月二日经冲绳飞往台湾。据消息透露，斯蒂文斯的台湾之行，是对美国的在台湾海峡进行侵略活动的详尽计划作最后的“战场审查”。
    同时，美国陆军部情报处副处长鲁·阿·萧近日正在台湾同蒋介石卖国集团讨论建立联合情报机构。消息指出，他的活动和斯蒂文斯的活动是有联系的。
