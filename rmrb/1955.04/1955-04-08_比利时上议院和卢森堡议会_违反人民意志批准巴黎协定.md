Title: Belgian Senate and Luxembourg Parliament Approve Paris Agreements Against the Will of the People
Author:
Date: 1955-04-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Brussels: After two days of discussion, the Belgian Senate passed a bill on the 6th to ratify the Paris Agreements. Communist senators voted against it.

A broad movement against the ratification and implementation of the Paris Agreements has unfolded throughout Belgium. The plan to arm West Germany has aroused increasingly strong opposition from the Belgian people. The Belgian ruling clique forcibly passed the bill ratifying the Paris Agreements in complete disregard of the people's will.

Furthermore, the Luxembourg Parliament approved the Paris Agreements on the 6th.

### 比利时上议院和卢森堡议会  违反人民意志批准巴黎协定

1955-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    比利时上议院和卢森堡议会
    违反人民意志批准巴黎协定
    据新华社讯　布鲁塞尔消息：比利时上议院在经过两天讨论后，六日通过了批准巴黎协定的法案。共产党议员投票反对。
    比利时全国已经展开反对批准和实施巴黎协定的广大运动。武装西德的计划引起比利时人民愈来愈强烈的反对。比利时统治集团是在完全违反人民意志的情况下强行通过批准巴黎协定的法案的。
    又讯　卢森堡议会六日批准了巴黎协定。
