Title: Comrade Mao Zedong and Others Visit Czechoslovak Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1955-04-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th. The leaders of the Chinese Communist Party, Comrades Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, Zhu De, Chen Yun, Kang Sheng, Peng Zhen, Lin Boqu, Zhang Wentian, Peng Dehuai, Deng Xiaoping and others, visited the Czechoslovak Ten Years of Socialist Construction Achievements Exhibition on the evening of the 28th.

They were accompanied by the Czechoslovak Republic government delegation headed by Jankovcova, Czechoslovak Ambassador to China Grigor, and the director of the Czechoslovak Ten Years of Socialist Construction Achievements Exhibition, Pok.

Comrade Mao Zedong and others spent a total of one and a half hours visiting the exhibition. They toured all sections of the exhibition.

### 毛泽东同志等参观捷克斯洛伐克展览会

1955-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东同志等参观捷克斯洛伐克展览会
    新华社二十八日讯　中国共产党的领导者毛泽东、刘少奇、朱德、陈云、康生、彭真、林伯渠、张闻天、彭德怀、邓小平等同志，在二十八日晚间参观捷克斯洛伐克十年社会主义建设成就展览会。
    以扬科夫卓娃为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团、捷克斯洛伐克驻我国特命全权大使格里哥尔和捷克斯洛伐克十年社会主义建设成就展览会主任波克陪同参观。
    毛泽东同志等在展览会参观历时共一时半。他们参观了展览会的各个部分。
