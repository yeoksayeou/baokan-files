Title: Indonesian Foreign Minister Speaks on the International Situation
Author:
Date: 1955-04-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 6th. The "Indonesia Torch" newspaper published on the 5th a statement by Foreign Minister Sunarjo to the press on the international situation.

Sunarjo said that the source of the current gloomy international situation is the rearmament of West Germany in Europe and the explosive situation in the Taiwan area of Asia. But there is still a glimmer of hope.

Sunarjo said that it is understandable that the People's Republic of China will not give up Taiwan. He pointed out that until now, the People's Republic of China has been implementing a rational policy on this issue. Sunarjo also said that the position of the People's Republic of China in international affairs cannot be ignored. Its participation in the United Nations (Indonesia supports its entry into the United Nations) would ease international tensions.

### 印度尼西亚外长就国际局势发表谈话

1955-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚外长就国际局势发表谈话
    新华社雅加达六日讯　“印度尼西亚火炬报”五日刊登了外交部长苏纳约就国际局势向报界发表的谈话。
    苏纳约说，目前的阴暗国际局势的来源是欧洲的西德的重新武装以及亚洲的爆炸性的台湾地区的局势问题。但是仍然存在一线希望。
    苏纳约说，可以理解的是，中华人民共和国是不会放弃台湾的。他指出，直到现在，中华人民共和国对于这个问题还是在执行一种合理的政策。苏纳约又说，中华人民共和国在国际事务中的地位是不容忽视的。它之参加联合国（印度尼西亚支持它加入联合国）会缓和国际紧张局势。
