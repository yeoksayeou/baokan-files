Our Great Motherland
    Industrial labor models from various regions are playing an active and pioneering role in increasing production, practicing economy, and fulfilling national production plans. Meng Tai, an Anshan Iron and Steel Works special-grade labor model at the Anshan Iron and Steel Works, actively implemented the suggestions of Soviet experts and led the workers in the repair shop to manufacture blast furnace tuyeres, each of which can save about 300 yuan.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    各地工业劳动模范在增产节约和完成国家生产计划中，起着积极和先锋的作用。鞍钢炼铁厂鞍钢特等劳动模范孟泰积极贯彻苏联专家建议，领导修理场职工制造高炉风口，每个可以节省三百元左右。
