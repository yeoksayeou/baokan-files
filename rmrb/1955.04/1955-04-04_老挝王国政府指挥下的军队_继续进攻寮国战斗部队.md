Title: Armies Under the Command of the Royal Lao Government Continue to Attack the Lao Fighting Units
Author:
Date: 1955-04-04
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 3rd – News from Laos states that the armies under the command of the Royal Lao Government continue to launch provocative attacks against the Lao Fighting Units. On March 27th, they used machine guns in Na Khang to fire upon the positions of the Lao Fighting Units southeast of Na Xin. On the same day, the armies under the command of the Royal Lao Government, entrenched in Huai Tou, launched a provocative attack on the Lao Fighting Units west of Huai Tou with mortars and machine guns.

### 老挝王国政府指挥下的军队  继续进攻寮国战斗部队

1955-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝王国政府指挥下的军队
    继续进攻寮国战斗部队
    新华社河内三日电　从老挝来的消息说，老挝王国政府指挥下的军队继续向寮国战斗部队发动挑衅性的进攻。三月二十七日，他们在那康用机枪射击那馨东南的寮国战斗部队的阵地。同日，盘踞会头的王国政府指挥下的军队，用迫击炮和机关枪向会头以西的寮国战斗部队进行挑衅攻击。
