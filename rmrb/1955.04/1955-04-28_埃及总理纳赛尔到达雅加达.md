Title: Egyptian Prime Minister Nasser Arrives in Jakarta
Author:
Date: 1955-04-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 26th: Egyptian Prime Minister Nasser arrived in Jakarta on the 25th at the invitation of the Indonesian government, after departing from Bandung. Indonesian Foreign Minister Sunarjo and the Egyptian Ambassador to Indonesia accompanied him from Bandung.

After arriving in Jakarta, Nasser was received by President Sukarno.

### 埃及总理纳赛尔到达雅加达

1955-04-28
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及总理纳赛尔到达雅加达
    新华社雅加达二十六日电　埃及总理纳赛尔应印度尼西亚政府的邀请，在二十五日从万隆到达雅加达。从万隆陪同他前来的有印度尼西亚外交部长苏纳约和埃及驻印度尼西亚大使。
    纳赛尔在到达雅加达以后受到了苏加诺总统的接见。
