Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-04-20
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    A newly built modern state-owned Hulan Flax Raw Material Factory in Heilongjiang Province has officially started production. The flax produced by this factory will be supplied to the Harbin Flax Textile Factory to weave high-quality linen. This is a corner of the workshop, where female workers are feeding raw materials into the short fiber preparation machine.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在黑龙江省新建的一座近代化国营呼兰亚麻原料厂已正式投入生产。这个厂生产的亚麻将供给哈尔滨亚麻纺织厂，织出质量良好的亚麻布。这是车间的一角，女工正给短纤维准备机输送原料。
