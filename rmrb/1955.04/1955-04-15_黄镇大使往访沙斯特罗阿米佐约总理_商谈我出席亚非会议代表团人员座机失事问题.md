Title: Ambassador Huang Zhen Visits Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo to Discuss the Plane Crash Involving Personnel of the Delegation to the Asian-African Conference

Author:

Date: 1955-04-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 14th: Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia Huang Zhen visited Indonesian Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo on the 13th to discuss the plane crash involving staff members and journalists of the Chinese delegation to the Asian-African Conference.

Ambassador Huang Zhen said that the government of the People's Republic of China considers this incident to be extremely serious and believes that it was an act of sabotage by the enemies of peace. Ambassador Huang Zhen requested the Indonesian government to pay close attention to preventing further subversive activities by saboteurs. He said, "This is not only a hostile act against the People's China, but also a hostile act against all countries participating in the Asian-African Conference."

Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo expressed his deep sorrow over the incident. He assured that efforts would be made to take security measures to protect the safety of the personnel of the various delegations and to be vigilant against the subversive activities of the enemies of peace.

When Ambassador Huang Zhen visited the Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs on the 12th, Foreign Minister Sunaryo expressed his regret over the plane crash.

### 黄镇大使往访沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理  商谈我出席亚非会议代表团人员座机失事问题

1955-04-15
第1版()
专栏：

    黄镇大使往访沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理
    商谈我出席亚非会议代表团人员座机失事问题
    新华社雅加达十四日电　中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇十三日下往见印度尼西亚总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约，商谈中国出席亚非会议代表团工作人员和记者所乘坐的飞机失事的问题。
    黄镇大使说，中华人民共和国政府认为这次事件非常严重，并且认为这是出于和平的敌人的破坏。黄镇大使要求印度尼西亚政府密切注意防止破坏分子进一步的阴谋活动。他说：“这不仅是对人民中国的敌对行为，也是对一切参加亚非会议的国家的敌对行为。”
    沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理对这一事件表示深切悲痛。他保证将努力采取安全措施以保护各国代表团人员的安全，并且警惕和平的敌人的破坏活动。
    黄镇大使十二日到印度尼西亚外交部的时候，外交部长苏纳约对这次飞机失事的事件表示惋惜之情。
