Title: Comrade Harry Pollitt, General Secretary of the Communist Party of Great Britain, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-04-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 6th—Comrade Harry Pollitt, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Great Britain, and Comrade Robert Stewart, Chairman of the Central Control Commission of the Communist Party of Great Britain, arrived in Beijing on April 6, 1955. Present at the airport to welcome them were Liu Shaoqi, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee; Deng Xiaoping, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and Secretary-General of the Central Committee; Wang Jiaxiang, member of the Central Committee; Yang Shangkun, Director of the General Office of the Central Committee; and Li Chuli and Li Qixin, responsible personnel from the central organs.

### 英国共产党总书记波立特同志等到京

1955-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

    英国共产党总书记波立特同志等到京
    新华社六日讯　英国共产党中央委员会总书记哈里·波立特和英国共产党中央监察委员会主席罗伯特·斯图尔特，在一九五五年四月六日到达北京。到飞机场欢迎的有中国共产党中央委员会政治局委员、中央书记处书记刘少奇，中央政治局委员、中央秘书长邓小平，中央委员王稼祥，中央办公厅主任杨尚昆和中央机关负责工作人员李初梨、李启新等。
