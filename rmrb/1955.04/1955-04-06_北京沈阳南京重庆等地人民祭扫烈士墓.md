Title: People in Beijing, Shenyang, Nanjing, Chongqing and other places sweep the tombs of martyrs

Author: 
Date: 1955-04-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 5th - Today, responsible persons and staff from the General Office of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, the Beijing Municipal People's Committee and other organs, as well as soldiers from the People's Liberation Army stationed in Beijing, workers, students and others, more than 1,100 people, went to the Babaoshan Revolutionary Cemetery to mourn the revolutionary martyrs. The tombs of Ren Bishi, Zhang Lan, and Yang Lisan were covered with wreaths presented by the people. Two days before Qingming Festival, more than 1,200 workers from the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant, as well as college and middle school students and Young Pioneers in the capital who were enjoying their spring break, also came to the cemetery to sweep the tomb of Ren Bishi.

On this fifth day, many people also went to the Wanan Cemetery in Xiangshan to sweep the tomb of martyr Li Dazhao. Staff from the Ministry of Public Security of the People's Republic of China and commanders and fighters from the North China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army laid wreaths at the tomb.

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, April 5th - Today is Qingming Festival. Representatives from various organs, local organizations of democratic parties, people's organizations in Shenyang, and the Northeast Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army went to the Beiling Martyrs Cemetery to pay respects and sweep the tombs of Volunteer Army martyrs such as Huang Jiguang, Qiu Shaoyun, Sun Zhanyuan, and Yang Lian and the tombs of the People's Liberation Army martyrs, and presented wreaths.

Today, more than 500 representatives of groups went to the martyrs' tombs in Zhongshan Park, Dongling and other places to pay their respects and sweep the tombs.

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, April 5th - Before Qingming Festival, more than 100,000 people from all walks of life in Jiangsu Province and Nanjing City went to the Yuhuatai Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery to sweep the tombs.

Yuhuatai is the place where about 100,000 revolutionary martyrs and patriotic people were killed during the reactionary rule of the Kuomintang. Deng Zhongxia, one of the founders of the Chinese workers' movement, Yun Daiying, the leader of the Chinese youth movement, Luo Dengxian, a general of the Northeast Anti-Japanese Allied Army, and Sun Jinchuan, the secretary of the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China twenty-eight years ago, all sacrificed themselves here. On the morning of the 5th, more than 300 leaders of the Party and government of Jiangsu Province and Nanjing City and representatives of the provincial and municipal people's congresses went to Yuhuatai to pay their respects and presented wreaths. Zhang Yumin, the wife of Lu Zhiying, one of the "Wen Cui" three martyrs, made a special trip from Shanghai to sweep the tomb. Many middle school Youth League members and Young Pioneers held ceremonies to admit new League members and new team members in front of the martyrs' tombs after sweeping the tombs.

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, April 5th - More than 3,500 people from all walks of life in Chongqing City went to the Zhazidong Martyrs Cemetery in the suburbs today to sweep the tombs, pay tribute to the martyrs who were killed by the US-Chiang Kai-shek secret service organization "Sino-American Special Technical Cooperation Organization" before liberation, and pay respects to the places where the martyrs were killed.

### 北京沈阳南京重庆等地人民祭扫烈士墓

1955-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    北京沈阳南京重庆等地人民祭扫烈士墓
    新华社五日讯　今日，中共中央办公厅、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、北京市人民委员会等机关的负责人和工作人员，以及中国人民解放军驻京部队的战士和工人、学生等一千一百多人，到八宝山革命公墓悼念革命先烈。在任弼时、张澜、杨立三的墓前放满了人们献的花圈。清明节前两天，石景山钢铁厂的工人和正在欢度春假的首都大、中学生、少年先锋队队员一千二百多人，也到公墓来给任弼时扫墓。
    五日这一天，很多人还到香山万安公墓给李大钊烈士扫墓。中华人民共和国公安部的工作人员和中国人民解放军华北军区的指战员在墓前献了花圈。
    新华社沈阳五日电　今天是清明节，沈阳市各机关、各民主党派地方组织、各人民团体和中国人民解放军东北军区的代表，前往北陵烈士陵园向黄继光、邱少云、孙占元、杨连第等志愿军烈士墓和解放军烈士墓扫墓致敬，并献花圈。
    今天，前往中山公园、东陵等烈士墓前凭吊、扫墓的团体代表有五百多人。
    新华社南京五日电　清明节前，江苏省和南京市各界人民十万多人前往雨花台革命烈士陵扫墓。
    雨花台是在国民党反动统治年代里先后约十万革命先烈和爱国志士殉难的地方。中国工人运动的创始人之一邓中夏，中国青年运动的领导人恽代英，东北抗日联军将领罗登贤及二十八年前中共南京市委书记孙津川等都在这里牺牲。五日上午，江苏省和南京市党和政府的领导人及省、市人民代表大会代表三百多人到雨花台凭吊，并献花圈。“文萃”三烈士之一卢志英的妻子张育民特地从上海赶来扫墓。很多学校中青年团员和少年先锋队员扫墓后在烈士陵前举行吸收新团员、新队员的仪式。
    新华社重庆五日电　重庆市各界人民三千五百多人今日前往市郊渣滓洞烈士墓地扫墓，向解放前夕被美蒋特务组织“中美特种技术合作所”杀害的烈士致敬，并凭吊了烈士们殉难的地方。
