Title: Pollitt and Stewart Visit Liu Shaoqi, Zhu De, and Chen Yun; The CPC Central Committee Hosts a Banquet for Pollitt and Stewart
Author:
Date: 1955-04-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 10th. Harry Pollitt, General Secretary of the Communist Party of Great Britain, and Robert Stewart, Chairman of the Central Supervisory Committee, who arrived in Beijing on the 6th of this month, visited Liu Shaoqi, Zhu De, and Chen Yun, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, on the afternoon of the 10th. Also present were Peng Dehuai, Lin Boqu, Dong Biwu, Peng Zhen, Zhang Wentian, and Deng Xiaoping, members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Wu Yuzhang and Wang Jiaxiang, members of the CPC Central Committee, and Yang Shangkun, Director of the General Office of the CPC Central Committee.
    Xinhua News Agency, April 10th. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China hosted a banquet on April 10th to welcome Harry Pollitt, General Secretary of the Communist Party of Great Britain, and Robert Stewart, Chairman of the Central Supervisory Committee, who are currently in Beijing.
    Attending the banquet were Liu Shaoqi, Zhu De, Chen Yun, Peng Dehuai, Lin Boqu, Dong Biwu, Peng Zhen, Zhang Wentian, Deng Xiaoping, members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Wu Yuzhang and Wang Jiaxiang, members of the CPC Central Committee, and Yang Shangkun, Zhao Yimin, Li Chuli, Li Qixin, and other responsible staff members from relevant departments.

### 波立特和斯图尔特访问刘少奇、朱德、陈云  中共中央宴请波立特和斯图尔特

1955-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

    波立特和斯图尔特访问刘少奇、朱德、陈云
    中共中央宴请波立特和斯图尔特
    新华社十日讯　本月六日到达北京的英国共产党总书记哈里·波立特和中央监察委员会主席罗伯特·斯图尔特，在十日下午访问中国共产党中央委员会书记处书记刘少奇、朱德、陈云。在座的有中共中央政治局委员彭德怀、林伯渠、董必武、彭真、张闻天、邓小平，中共中央委员吴玉章、王稼祥，中共中央办公厅主任杨尚昆。
    新华社十日讯　中国共产党中央委员会在四月十日设宴欢迎刻在北京的英国共产党总书记波立特和中央监察委员会主席斯图尔特。
    出席宴会的有中共中央政治局委员刘少奇、朱德、陈云、彭德怀、林伯渠、董必武、彭真、张闻天、邓小平，中共中央委员吴玉章、王稼祥和有关部门负责工作人员杨尚昆、赵毅敏、李初梨、李启新等人。
