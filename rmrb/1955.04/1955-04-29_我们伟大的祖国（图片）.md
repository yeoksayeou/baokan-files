    Our Great Motherland
    The technical improvement group of the No. 2 Blast Furnace of the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant and the technical workers of the repair workshop successfully trial-produced a tool for crushing "sintered ore" - a pneumatic hammer - in ten days from April 11 to 20, as a gift for the "May Day" holiday.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    石景山钢铁厂二号高炉技术改进小组和修理工场的技术工人在四月十一日至二十日十天中，试制成功了一种破碎“烧结矿”的工具——风锤，作为“五一”节的献礼。
