Title: Trade Union Delegations from Various Countries Arrive in Beijing One After Another
Author:
Date: 1955-04-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th. Upon the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the Chinese Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union, the head of the Japanese Workers' Delegation to China, Mr. Minoru Takano, and the first group of delegates, as well as the delegation from the All India Postal and Telegraph Employees Union, led by Mr. Mukherjee, and the head of the Iranian Trade Union Delegation, Mr. Khadjavie (two delegates had already arrived in Beijing), arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 28th.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Li Jiebo, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, as well as the heads of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and various industrial unions.

### 各国工会代表团陆续到京

1955-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    各国工会代表团陆续到京
    新华社二十八日讯　应中华全国总工会和中国邮电工会的邀请，来我国参加“五一”节观礼和访问的日本工人访华代表团团长高野实和第一批代表，以慕克吉为首的全印邮电雇员联合会代表和伊朗工会代表团团长卡德贾维（代表二人已先行到京）在二十八日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有：中华全国总工会书记处书记李颉伯，以及中华全国总工会国际联络部和各产业工会负责人。
