Title: Vietnamese Democratic Republic People's Song and Dance Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-04-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 20th — The People's Song and Dance Troupe of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, a group of over fifty people led by团长刘仲庐, arrived in Beijing by train on the morning of the 20th.

Those who welcomed them at the station included Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, Hong Shen, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and many people from the capital's literary and art circles.

Zhou Liang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, and embassy staff also welcomed them at the station.

### 越南民主共和国人民歌舞团到达北京

1955-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    越南民主共和国人民歌舞团到达北京
    新华社二十日讯　越南民主共和国人民歌舞团一行五十多人，由团长刘仲庐率领，在二十日上午乘火车到达北京。
    到车站欢迎的有中华人民共和国文化部副部长张致祥，中国人民对外文化协会副会长洪深和首都文艺界人士多人。
    越南民主共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办周亮和使馆人员也到车站欢迎。
