Title: Pham Van Dong Leaves Delhi and Issues Statement at Airport
Author:
Date: 1955-04-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 10th: Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, left Delhi at noon on the 10th for Agra to visit the famous Indian classical architecture "Taj Mahal" after concluding his talks with Indian Prime Minister Nehru.

Pham Van Dong will fly to Calcutta at 6:30 am on April 11th. On the same day, he will fly to Yangon at the invitation of the Government of the Union of Burma.

Those seeing him off at Delhi Airport included: Major General Yadunath Singh, Military Secretary to the President of the Republic of India, V.K. Krishna Menon, Indian Representative to the United Nations, N.R. Pillai, Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, S. Dutt, Commonwealth Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Y.K. Chopra, Chief of Protocol of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Also present at the airport to see him off was A.D. Pandit, Administrative Commissioner of Delhi State.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries were also at the airport to see him off, including the Chinese Ambassador, the Burmese Ambassador, the Polish Ambassador, the Hungarian Minister, the Romanian Minister, the Soviet Chargé d'Affaires, and the Czechoslovak Chargé d'Affaires.

Before leaving Delhi, Pham Van Dong issued the following statement at the airport:

“I am very satisfied with my short but fruitful visit to this country. This visit has helped to faithfully implement the Geneva Accords aimed at restoring peace in Indochina on the basis of recognizing the national rights of the people of Vietnam, Cambodia, and Laos.

    “Everywhere, I have been received with brotherly hospitality, and everywhere, I have found full support for the just struggle of the Vietnamese people, who are currently striving to consolidate peace and achieve national unification in accordance with the provisions of the Geneva Accords.

    “My visit has helped to strengthen the friendly relations between the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Republic of India.

    “I would like to once again express my gratitude to the Government of the Republic of India for the warm welcome extended to the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.”

### 范文同离开德里并在机场发表声明

1955-04-12
第1版()
专栏：

    范文同离开德里并在机场发表声明
    新华社新德里十日电　越南民主共和国副总理兼外交部长范文同在结束了同印度总理尼赫鲁的会谈后，十日中午离开德里赴亚格拉去访问著名的印度古典建筑“泰吉·玛哈尔陵”。
    范文同将在四月十一日上午六时三十分乘飞机赴加尔各答。同日，他将应缅甸联邦政府的邀请飞往仰光。
    在德里机场欢送的有：印度共和国总统军事秘书雅度那特·辛格少将、印度驻联合国代表维·克·克里希纳·梅农、外交部秘书长纳·鲁·皮莱，外交部英联邦秘书斯·杜特、外交部交际司长英·塞·乔普拉。
    到机场欢送的还有德里邦行政专员阿·德·潘迪特。
    在机场欢送的还有各国外交使节，其中有中国大使、缅甸大使、波兰大使、匈牙利公使、罗马尼亚公使、苏联代办和捷克斯洛伐克代办。
    范文同在离开德里以前在机场发表声明如下：
  “我对于我对这个国家所作的短暂然而颇有收获的访问感到很满意。这次访问有助于忠实执行目的在于在承认越南、柬埔寨和老挝人民的民族权利的基础上恢复印度支那和平的日内瓦协议。
      “在每一个地方，我都受到兄弟般的接待，在每一个地方，我都发现对于越南人民目前根据日内瓦协议的规定争取巩固和平和获得民族统一的正义斗争的完全支持。
      “我的访问有助于加强越南民主共和国和印度共和国之间的友好关系。
      “我愿再一次向印度共和国政府给予越南民主共和国代表团的热诚欢迎表示感谢。”
