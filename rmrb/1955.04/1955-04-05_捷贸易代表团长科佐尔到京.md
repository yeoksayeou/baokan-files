Title: Czechoslovak Trade Delegation Head Kozel Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-04-05
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 4th. Kozel, the head of the Czechoslovak Republic Trade Delegation and First Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Czechoslovak Republic, arrived in Beijing by plane on April 4th.
    Those who greeted him at the airport included Xu Xuehan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of China, Zhang Huadong, Director of the Import Bureau, Li Yusheng, Deputy Director of the Second Bureau, and Yang Mian, Director of the Protocol Department.
    Also greeting him at the airport were Šmíd, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Czechoslovak Republic Embassy in China, and Šál, Commercial Counselor.
    Kitrich, Deputy Head of the Czechoslovak Republic Trade Delegation and Director of the Department of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Czechoslovak Republic, arrived in Beijing in 1954.

### 捷贸易代表团长科佐尔到京

1955-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

    捷贸易代表团长科佐尔到京
    新华社四日讯　捷克斯洛伐克共和国贸易代表团团长、捷克斯洛伐克共和国对外贸易部第一副部长科佐尔，四月四日乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场迎接的有我国对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒、进口局局长张化东、第二局副局长李郁生和交际处处长杨勉等。
    到机场迎接的还有捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻华大使馆临时代办石密茨、商务参赞沙尔。
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国贸易代表团副团长、捷克斯洛伐克共和国对外贸易部司长基特里赫已在一九五四年到达北京。
