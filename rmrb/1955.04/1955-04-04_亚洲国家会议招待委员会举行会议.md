Title: Asian Countries Conference Reception Committee Holds Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1955-04-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 2nd. The Asian Countries Conference Reception Committee held a meeting on March 31st and elected the Chairman of the Delhi Municipal Committee, Ru. Na. Agrawal, as the Chairman of the Reception Committee. The meeting was hosted by Mrs. Rameshwari Nehru, who asked everyone to express a warm welcome to the representatives attending the Asian Countries Conference.

### 亚洲国家会议招待委员会举行会议

1955-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    亚洲国家会议招待委员会举行会议
    新华社新德里二日电　亚洲国家会议招待委员会在三月三十一日举行会议，选出德里市政委员会主席鲁·纳·阿加瓦尔为招待委员会主席。会议主持人拉米希瓦里·尼赫鲁夫人在会上要求大家对参加亚洲国家会议的代表们表示热诚的欢迎。
