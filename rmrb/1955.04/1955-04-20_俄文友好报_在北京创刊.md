Title: "Russian Language Friendship News" Launched in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-04-20
Page: 2

    "Russian Language Friendship News" Launched in Beijing
    The daily "Russian Language Friendship News," founded by the headquarters of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, was launched in Beijing on April 15th. The newspaper is a broadsheet, one full page across. The task of this newspaper is to promote the great achievements of the Chinese and Soviet people in political, economic, and cultural construction, to report on the lives of the Soviet people and the advanced experiences in various aspects of the Soviet Union, and to report on the vigorous development of the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people, thereby promoting the great friendship and cooperation between China and the Soviet Union. The newspaper also publishes news of major events at home and abroad. The inaugural issue published congratulatory messages from Soong Ching Ling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Denisov, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. (Xinhua News Agency)

### “俄文友好报”在北京创刊

1955-04-20
第2版()
专栏：

    “俄文友好报”在北京创刊
    中苏友好协会总会创办的日刊“俄文友好报”已于四月十五日在北京创刊，篇幅为对开一大张。这个报纸的任务是宣传中苏两国人民在政治、经济和文化建设方面的伟大成就，报道苏联人民的生活和苏联各方面的先进经验，报道中苏两国人民友谊的蓬勃发展，从而促进中苏两国的伟大友谊和合作。这个报纸还刊登国内外重大事件的新闻。创刊号上发表有中苏友好协会会长宋庆龄和苏联对外文化协会理事会主席杰尼索夫的贺词。（新华社）
