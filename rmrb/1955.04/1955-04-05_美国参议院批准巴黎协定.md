Title: US Senate Approves Paris Agreement
Author: 
Date: 1955-04-05
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 3rd. TASS Washington: On April 1st, the US Senate discussed the Foreign Affairs Committee's recommendation to ratify the Paris Agreements regarding the arming of West Germany and the permission for West Germany to join the North Atlantic Alliance. After a short discussion, the Senate passed the Foreign Affairs Committee's recommendation to ratify the Paris Agreements. Republican Senators Langer and Malone voted against it.

### 美国参议院批准巴黎协定

1955-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

    美国参议院批准巴黎协定
    新华社三日讯　塔斯社华盛顿讯：四月一日，美国参议院讨论外交委员会建议批准关于武装西德和准许西德参加北大西洋联盟的巴黎协定的报告。经过短时间的讨论后，参议院通过了外交委员会关于批准巴黎协定的建议。共和党参议员朗杰和马隆投票反对。
