Title: Phạm Văn Đồng Visits Nehru and Meets with Other Indian Government Officials; Chinese Ambassador to India Yuan Zhongxian Holds Reception to Welcome Phạm Văn Đồng
Author:
Date: 1955-04-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 10th. Phạm Văn Đồng, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, visited the Indian Parliament at 3:00 PM on the 9th. At 3:30 PM, Phạm Văn Đồng held talks with Indian Prime Minister Nehru for one hour in the Parliament.

On the morning of the 9th, Phạm Văn Đồng met with Indian Finance Minister Deshmukh and Minister of Commerce and Industry Krishnamachari at 9:30 AM and 10:00 AM respectively. At 10:30 AM, Phạm Văn Đồng visited Dr. Syed Mahmud, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Indian Government.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 10th. Indian Prime Minister Nehru hosted a banquet at his official residence on the evening of the 8th to entertain Phạm Văn Đồng, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Among those present at the banquet were: Indian Vice President Dr. Radhakrishnan, Finance Minister Deshmukh, Health Minister Mrs. Kaur, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Dr. Mahmud, Head of the Indian Delegation to the United Nations Menon, Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Pillai, and others.

Nehru's daughter, Mrs. Indira Gandhi, also attended the banquet.

Nehru and Phạm Văn Đồng delivered speeches at the banquet.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 10th. Chinese Ambassador to India Yuan Zhongxian held a reception on the evening of the 9th to welcome Phạm Văn Đồng, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, who was visiting India at the invitation of the Indian government. All members of the delegation of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam attended the reception.

Among those present at the reception were: senior officials of the Indian government, diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in Delhi, personnel from the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Chinese delegation participating in the Asian countries conference held in Delhi.

The reception was held in a friendly atmosphere.

### 范文同访问尼赫鲁并会见印政府其他官员  我国驻印度大使袁仲贤举行招待会欢迎范文同

1955-04-11
第4版()
专栏：

    范文同访问尼赫鲁并会见印政府其他官员
    我国驻印度大使袁仲贤举行招待会欢迎范文同
    新华社新德里十日电　越南民主共和国副总理兼外交部长范文同九日下午三时访问了印度国会。下午三时三十分，范文同在国会同印度总理尼赫鲁会谈了一小时。
    九日上午，范文同在九时三十分和十时分别会见了印度政府财政部长德希穆克和工商部长克里希纳马查里。上午十时三十分，范文同访问了印度政府外交部副部长赛·马茂德博士。
    新华社新德里十日电　印度总理尼赫鲁八日晚在他的官邸设宴招待越南民主共和国副总理兼外交部长范文同。
    出席宴会的有：印度副总统拉德哈克里希南博士、财政部部长德希穆克、卫生部部长考尔夫人、外交部副部长马茂德博士、印度驻联合国代表团团长梅农、外交部秘书长皮莱等。
    尼赫鲁的女儿英迪拉·甘地夫人也出席了宴会。
    尼赫鲁和范文同在宴会上曾先后致词。
    新华社新德里十日电　中国驻印度大使袁仲贤九日晚举行招待会欢迎应邀访问印度的越南民主共和国副总理兼外交部长范文同。越南民主共和国政府代表团的全体团员，都出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的有：印度政府的高级官员和各国驻德里的使节和外交官员，参加在德里举行的亚洲国家会议的越南民主共和国代表团人员和中国代表团人员。
    招待会在友好的气氛中举行。
