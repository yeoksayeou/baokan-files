Title: Celebrating the Tenth Anniversary of the Soviet Army's Liberation of Hungary: Hungarian Ambassador to China, Skalaidan, Holds Reception
Author:
Date: 1955-04-05
Page: 4

　　Celebrating the Tenth Anniversary of the Soviet Army's Liberation of Hungary
　　Hungarian Ambassador to China, Skalaidan, Holds Reception

　　Xinhua News Agency, April 4th. The Ambassador of the Hungarian People's Republic to China, Skalaidan, held a grand reception on the evening of April 4th to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the liberation of Hungary.

　　The Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Zhu De, was invited to attend the reception.

　　Also present at the reception were: Vice Premiers of the State Council He Long, Chen Yi, and Ulanhu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, as well as ministers and vice ministers of various government ministries, and directors and deputy directors of various commissions.

　　Also present at the reception were: Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Chen Shutong, and members of the committee.

　　Also present at the reception were: President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng, and senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

　　Also present at the reception was Vice Mayor of Beijing, Wang Kunlun.

    Also present at the reception were: members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People’s Political Consultative Conference, heads of various people’s organizations, figures from the capital’s scientific, educational, literary, artistic, and journalistic circles, and model workers.

　　Heads of diplomatic missions and diplomatic officials from various countries in China also attended the reception.

　　A total of more than six hundred people attended the reception.

　　At the reception, Ambassador Skalaidan and Vice Premier Chen Yi delivered speeches successively (full text to be published separately).

　　The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

　　A dance was held after the reception.

### 庆祝苏军解放匈牙利十周年  匈牙利驻我国大使斯克拉丹举行招待会

1955-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝苏军解放匈牙利十周年
    匈牙利驻我国大使斯克拉丹举行招待会
    新华社四日讯　匈牙利人民共和国驻我国大使斯克拉丹四日晚举行盛大招待会，庆祝匈牙利解放十周年。
    中华人民共和国副主席朱德应邀出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的还有：国务院副总理贺龙、陈毅、乌兰夫，外交部副部长张闻天，以及政府各部部长、副部长，各委员会主任、副主任。
    出席招待会的还有：全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、陈叔通和委员。
    出席招待会的还有：最高人民法院院长董必武，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，以及中国人民解放军高级将领。
    出席招待会的还有北京市副市长王昆仑。
    出席招待会的还有：中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员，各人民团体负责人，首都科学、教育、文学、艺术、新闻界人士和劳动模范等。
    各国驻华使节和外交官员也出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的共有六百多人。
    招待会上，斯克拉丹大使和陈毅副总理先后讲了话（全文另发）。
    招待会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
    招待会后，举行了舞会。
