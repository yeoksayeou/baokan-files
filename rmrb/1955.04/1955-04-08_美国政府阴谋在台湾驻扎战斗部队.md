Title: US Government Conspires to Station Combat Troops in Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1955-04-08
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 7th: TASS, New York, April 6th: The New York-published "American Daily" carried a dispatch from Kingsbury Smith, a Paris-based reporter for International News Service. The dispatch stated that diplomatic sources in Washington revealed that the US government is considering the possibility of stationing a strong US combat force in Taiwan. Kingsbury Smith said that although no decision has been made, it is understood that this possible action is being considered in connection with efforts to "strengthen" Chiang Kai-shek in the event that China "attacks and occupies" Jinmen and Mazu.

### 美国政府阴谋在台湾驻扎战斗部队

1955-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    美国政府阴谋在台湾驻扎战斗部队
    新华社七日讯　塔斯社纽约六日讯：纽约出版的“美国人日报”刊载了国际新闻社驻巴黎记者金斯伯里·史密斯发的一条电讯。这条电讯说，来自华盛顿的外交界消息透露：美国政府正在考虑在台湾驻扎一支强有力的美国战斗部队的可能性。金斯伯里·史密斯说，虽然还没有作出决定，但是据悉，现在正在联系到在一旦中国“进攻并占领”金门和马祖时如何努力“加强”蒋介石的问题，来考虑这种可能的行动。
