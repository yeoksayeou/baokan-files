Title: Chinese Government Delegation Arrives in Budapest to Attend the 10th Anniversary Celebration of Hungary's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1955-04-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, April 2nd: Budapest Dispatch: At the invitation of the Government of the Hungarian People's Republic, the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China, led by Vice Premier of the State Council Deng Zihui, arrived in Budapest by plane on April 1 to attend the 10th Anniversary Celebration of Hungary's Liberation.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included: Hegedüs, Acting Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Hungary; Apró, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers; Aczél and Marosán, Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party; General Bata, Minister of National Defense; Boldoczki, Minister of Foreign Affairs; Hay, Minister of Foreign Trade; and Rónai, Speaker of the National Assembly, among others.

Also present to welcome the delegation were Hao Deqing, Chinese Ambassador to Hungary, and embassy staff, as well as diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union and various People's Democratic countries to Hungary.

After disembarking from the plane, Vice Premier Deng Zihui, accompanied by Hegedüs, Acting Chairman of the Hungarian Council of Ministers, and General Bata, Minister of National Defense, reviewed the guard of honor.

### 应邀参加匈牙利解放十周年庆祝典礼  我国政府代表团到达布达佩斯

1955-04-03
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加匈牙利解放十周年庆祝典礼
    我国政府代表团到达布达佩斯
    新华社布拉格二日电　布达佩斯消息：应匈牙利人民共和国政府的邀请参加匈牙利解放十周年庆祝典礼的中华人民共和国政府代表团，由国务院副总理邓子恢率领，在四月一日乘飞机到达布达佩斯。
    到机场欢迎的有：匈牙利部长会议代理主席赫格居斯、部长会议副主席阿普罗、劳动人民党中央委员会书记处书记阿奇和马托尔奇、国防部部长巴塔上将、外交部长波尔多奇基、对外贸易部部长海伊和国民议会议长罗奈伊等多人。
    前往欢迎的还有我国驻匈牙利大使郝德青和大使馆人员，苏联和各人民民主国家驻匈牙利外交使节等。
    邓子恢副总理等下机后，在匈牙利部长会议代理主席赫格居斯、国防部部长巴塔上将陪同下，检阅了仪仗队。
