Title: Asian Countries Conference International Preparatory Secretariat Issues Communiqué Announcing Conference Preparations Nearly Complete and Publishing Conference Program
Author:
Date: 1955-04-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 3rd. The chairman of the International Preparatory Secretariat of the Asian Countries Conference, Mrs. Rameshwari Nehru, and representatives of various countries working in the Secretariat held a press conference in New Delhi on April 2nd.

The news communiqué issued by the Secretariat stated that the preparations for the Asian Countries Conference are nearing completion. Approximately 225 delegates from eighteen countries will participate in the Asian Countries Conference to be held in New Delhi from April 6th to 10th.

The communiqué stated that the first item on the conference agenda will be a discussion of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence in the joint statement by the Prime Ministers of China and India. The conference will establish committees to discuss political, economic, social, and cultural issues, as well as a specialized committee to discuss the scientific issues of the peaceful utilization of atomic energy.

The announced program of the Asian Countries Conference includes: an opening session on April 6th, and plenary sessions on April 7th and 10th. Committee meetings will be held on April 8th and 9th, along with various welcome receptions for delegates from various countries that are being prepared. At the close of the conference, a mass rally will be held at Gandhi Square on April 10th.

### 亚洲国家会议国际筹备秘书处发表公报  宣布会议筹备工作已将近完成并公布会议程序

1955-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    亚洲国家会议国际筹备秘书处发表公报
    宣布会议筹备工作已将近完成并公布会议程序
    新华社新德里三日电　亚洲国家会议国际筹备秘书处主席拉米希瓦里·尼赫鲁夫人和各国参加秘书处工作的代表四月二日在新德里举行了记者招待会。
    秘书处发表的新闻公报说，亚洲国家会议的筹备工作已将近完成。来自十八个国家的约二百二十五名代表将参加从四月六日到十日在新德里举行的亚洲国家会议。
    公报说，会议的第一项议程将是讨论中印两国总理联合声明中的和平共处五项原则。会议将成立讨论政治、经济、社会和文化问题以及专门讨论和平利用原子能的科学问题的各种委员会。
    已宣布的亚洲国家会议程序包括：四月六日举行开幕会议，四月七日和十日举行全体会议。四月八日和九日将举行各委员会的会议和正在筹备的欢迎各国代表的各种欢迎会。会议闭幕时将于四月十日在甘地广场举行群众大会。
