Title: Premier Zhou Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Hegedüs on Election as Chairman of the Hungarian Council of Ministers
Author:
Date: 1955-04-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 21st. Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, sent a telegram congratulating András Hegedüs on his election as Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Hungarian People's Republic. The telegram reads as follows:

To Comrade András Hegedüs, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Hungarian People's Republic:

I am delighted to learn of your election as Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Hungarian People's Repubic, and I extend my warm congratulations to you on behalf of the government of the People's Republic of China and in my own name.

I wish you new achievements in your future work.

Zhou Enlai, April 21, 1955

### 周总理电贺赫格居斯当选匈部长会议主席

1955-04-22
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电贺赫格居斯当选匈部长会议主席
    新华社二十一日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来电贺安·赫格居斯当选为匈牙利人民共和国部长会议主席。电文如下：匈牙利人民共和国部长会议主席亲爱的安·赫格居斯同志：
    欣悉您当选为匈牙利人民共和国部长会议主席，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府并以我个人名义向您致热烈的祝贺。
    祝您在今后的工作中获得新的成就。
    周恩来　一九五五年四月二十一日
