Title: Our Anti-Aircraft Artillery Units in the Coastal Areas of Guangdong Shot Down an Intruding Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Plane

Author:

Date: 1955-04-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, April 28th. On the 26th, our People's Liberation Army anti-aircraft artillery units shot down a Chiang Kai-shek bandit F-84 type aircraft that intruded into the northeast coastal area of Guangdong. From 8:50 to 14:35 on this day, four Chiang Kai-shek bandit F-84 type aircraft, one F-86 type aircraft, and one RT-33 type reconnaissance aircraft successively intruded into the Shantou and Chenghai areas in the northeast coastal area of Guangdong and were repelled by our anti-aircraft artillery units. Among them, one Chiang Kai-shek bandit F-84 type aircraft was hit, caught fire, and crashed ten kilometers northeast of Nan'ao Island.

### 我高射炮部队在广东沿海地区  击落窜扰的蒋贼飞机一架

1955-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我高射炮部队在广东沿海地区
    击落窜扰的蒋贼飞机一架
    新华社广州二十八日电　二十六日我人民解放军高射炮部队击落窜扰广东东北沿海地区的蒋贼军F—八四型飞机一架。这天八时五十分到十四时三十五分，蒋贼军F—八四型飞机四架、F—八六型飞机和RT—三三型侦察机各一架先后窜扰广东省东北沿海的汕头和澄海地区，被我高射炮部队击退，其中蒋贼军F—八四型飞机一架被击中起火，坠于南澳岛东北十公里处。
