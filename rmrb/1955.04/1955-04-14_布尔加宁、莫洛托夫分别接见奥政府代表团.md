Title: Bulganin and Molotov Receive Austrian Government Delegation Respectively
Author:
Date: 1955-04-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 13th April. TASS, Moscow, 12th April: The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, N.A. Bulganin, received the head of the Austrian government delegation, Austrian Federal Chancellor Julius Raab, and delegation members: Vice-Chancellor Adolf Schärf, Foreign Minister Leopold Figl, and Deputy Foreign Minister Bruno Kreisky, on the 12th. The delegation was accompanied by the Austrian Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Soviet Union, N. Bischoff.

Present at the reception were Soviet Foreign Minister V.M. Molotov and Soviet Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to Austria and High Commissioner, I.I. Ilyichev.

According to Xinhua News Agency: Soviet Foreign Minister V.M. Molotov received the Austrian government delegation headed by Austrian Federal Chancellor Julius Raab on the 12th.

According to Xinhua News Agency, 13th April: The First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov, held a reception on the 12th for the Austrian government delegation that came to Moscow at the invitation of the Soviet government.

All members of the Austrian government delegation, headed by Austrian Federal Chancellor Raab, attended the reception.

Also present at the reception were Soviet Communist Party and Soviet government leaders Bulganin, Kaganovich, Mikoyan, Pervukhin, Soviet Ambassador to Austria and High Commissioner Ilyichev, Soviet government ministers and deputy ministers, senior generals of the Soviet army, and people from all walks of life, including culture and news.

Foreign envoys stationed in Moscow also attended the reception.

According to Xinhua News Agency: Austrian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Bischoff, hosted a banquet on the 12th for all members of the Austrian government delegation, headed by Austrian Federal Chancellor Raab.

### 布尔加宁、莫洛托夫分别接见奥政府代表团

1955-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁、莫洛托夫分别接见奥政府代表团
    新华社十三日讯　塔斯社莫斯科十二日讯：苏联部长会议主席尼·亚·布尔加宁十二日接见了奥地利政府代表团团长、奥地利联邦总理尤·拉布和代表团团员：副总理阿·谢尔夫、外交部长利·费格尔和外交部副部长布·克赖斯基。陪同代表团受接见的有奥地利驻苏联特命全权大使纳·比萧夫。
    接见时在座的有苏联外交部长维·米·莫洛托夫和苏联驻奥地利特命全权大使兼高级专员伊·伊·伊利切夫。
    据新华社讯　苏联外交部长维·米·莫洛托夫十二日接见了以奥地利联邦总理尤·拉布为首的奥地利政府代表团。
    据新华社十三日讯　苏联部长会议第一副主席、苏联外交部长莫洛托夫十二日为应苏联政府邀请来莫斯科的奥地利政府代表团举行招待会。
    出席招待会的有以奥地利联邦总理拉布为首的奥地利政府代表团全体人员。
    出席招待会的还有苏联共产党和苏联政府的领导人布尔加宁、卡冈诺维奇、米高扬、别尔乌辛，苏联驻奥大使兼高级专员伊利切夫，苏联政府部长和副部长，苏联军队高级将领以及文化和新闻等各界人士。
    出席招待会的还有驻在莫斯科的外国使节。
    据新华社讯　奥地利驻苏大使比萧夫十二日宴请了以奥地利联邦总理拉布为首的奥地利政府代表团全体人员。
