Title: China and North Korea Sign Film Exchange Contract
Author:
Date: 1955-04-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd – A contract between China and North Korea for the mutual purchase and distribution of films was signed at the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China on the afternoon of the 21st. Representing the Chinese side was Luo Guangda, General Manager of the China Film Distribution Company, and representing the North Korean side was Kim Young-ryul, representative of the Film Representative Office of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China.
    During the signing ceremony, both representatives stated in their speeches that the signing of this contract marks a further consolidation and development of exchanges in the film industry between China and North Korea.

### 中朝签订互购影片发行合同

1955-04-24
第1版()
专栏：

    中朝签订互购影片发行合同
    新华社二十二日讯　中朝互相购买影片发行合同二十一日下午在中华人民共和国文化部举行了签字仪式。代表中方签字的是中国电影发行公司总经理罗光达，代表朝方签字的是朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华电影代表部代表金泳烈。
    在签字仪式上，双方代表在发言中一致认为，这一合同的签订标志着中朝两国在电影事业的交流上获得了更进一步的巩固和发展。
