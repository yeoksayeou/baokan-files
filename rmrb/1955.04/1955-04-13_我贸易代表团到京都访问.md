Title: Our Trade Delegation Visits Kyoto
Author:
Date: 1955-04-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Osaka, April 12th: The Chinese Trade Delegation to Japan visited Kyoto on the 11th after leaving Osaka.

    The delegation visited the product exhibition held by the Kyoto Prefecture industrial and commercial circles, and held discussions with representatives from industrial and commercial circles.
    The delegation attended a welcome dinner held by various circles in Kyoto. During the dinner, Yoshimura Magosaburo, chairman of the Kyoto branch of the Japan Association for the Promotion of International Trade, and Yoshimi Takayama, the mayor of Kyoto, delivered welcome speeches. They expressed their hope that Sino-Japanese trade could be further developed. The chairman of the Kyoto Prefectural Assembly and the chairman of the Kyoto City Council also presented the resolution passed by the assembly welcoming the Chinese Trade Delegation to Japan to the head of the delegation, Lei Renmin. The resolution expressed hope that the negotiations for a new Sino-Japanese trade agreement could reach an agreement quickly.
    Part of the delegation left Kyoto on the evening of the 11th and returned to Osaka, while another group of members, led by Deputy Head Lu Xuzhang, returned to Tokyo from Kyoto on the evening of the 11th to continue discussions with the Japanese side on issues related to the signing of a new Sino-Japanese trade agreement.

### 我贸易代表团到京都访问

1955-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我贸易代表团到京都访问
    据新华社大阪十二日电　中国访问日本贸易代表团曾在十一日从大阪到京都访问。
    代表团在京都参观了京都府工商界举办的商品展览会，并和工商界的代表进行了座谈。
    代表团出席了京都各界的欢迎晚餐会。席间，日本国际贸易促进协会京都分会会长吉村孙三郎和京都市市长高山义三致欢迎词，他们希望中日贸易能得到进一步的发展。京都府议会议长和京都市议会议长还把议会所通过的欢迎中国访问日本贸易代表团的决议交给了雷任民团长。决议希望这次新的中日贸易协议谈判能够迅速达成协议。
    代表团的一部分在十一日晚离开京都回到大阪，另一部分团员在副团长卢绪章率领下，十一日晚由京都返回东京，继续就签订新的中日贸易协议的有关问题同日本方面进行商谈。
