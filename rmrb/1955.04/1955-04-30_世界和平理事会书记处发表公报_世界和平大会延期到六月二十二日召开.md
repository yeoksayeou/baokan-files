Title: World Peace Council Secretariat Issues Communiqué: World Peace Congress Postponed to June 22nd
Author:
Date: 1955-04-30
Page: 4

    World Peace Council Secretariat Issues Communiqué
    World Peace Congress Postponed to June 22nd
    Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, April 28th: TASS reports: The World Peace Council Secretariat issued the following communiqué: Influential groups in various countries have requested the World Peace Council to postpone the World Peace Congress originally scheduled to be held in Helsinki in May. Many people who hoped to attend the congress also expressed the same request. Based on these opinions, it has been decided that the World Peace Congress will be held in Helsinki from June 22nd to 29th, 1955. (Signed) Frédéric Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the World Peace Council.

### 世界和平理事会书记处发表公报  世界和平大会延期到六月二十二日召开

1955-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会书记处发表公报
    世界和平大会延期到六月二十二日召开
    新华社二十九日讯　塔斯社维也纳二十八日讯：世界和平理事会书记处发表公报如下：各国有影响的团体请求世界和平理事会延期召开原定五月在赫尔辛基举行的世界和平大会。很多希望参加这次大会的人也表示了同样的要求。根据这些意见，决定：世界和平大会在一九五五年六月二十二日到二十九日在赫尔辛基举行。世界和平理事会主席弗雷德里克·约里奥—居里。
