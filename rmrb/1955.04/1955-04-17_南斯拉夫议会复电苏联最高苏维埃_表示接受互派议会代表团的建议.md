Title: Yugoslav Parliament Replies to the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Expressing Acceptance of the Proposal to Exchange Parliamentary Delegations
Author:
Date: 1955-04-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. According to a report by the Yugoslav News Agency, the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia has sent a reply to the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union regarding its declaration issued on February 9th of this year. The reply stated that the Presidium of the Yugoslav National Assembly particularly noted the Supreme Soviet's proposal that the mutual exchange of parliamentary delegations between countries is beneficial, and that representatives of one country's parliament should give speeches in the parliament of another country. The reply said: The Presidium of the Yugoslav National Assembly, on behalf of the Assembly, declares that it is willing to accept the proposal of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union to exchange parliamentary delegations.

### 南斯拉夫议会复电苏联最高苏维埃  表示接受互派议会代表团的建议

1955-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫议会复电苏联最高苏维埃
    表示接受互派议会代表团的建议
    新华社十五日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社报道，南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国国民议会主席团已经把对苏联最高苏维埃今年二月九日发表的宣言的一份答复送交苏联最高苏维埃。复电表示，南斯拉夫国民议会主席团特别注意到苏联最高苏维埃关于各国议会互派议会代表团是有益的以及一国议会的代表在另一国的议会中发表演说的主张。复电说：南斯拉夫国民议会主席团代表议会宣布，它愿意接受苏联最高苏维埃的互派议会代表团的建议。
