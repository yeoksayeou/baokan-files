Title: Indian Linguist Professor Vira Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-04-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th. Professor Raghu Vira, Member of the Council of States of the Indian Parliament, Director of the International Academy of Indian Culture, and a renowned Indian linguist, and his daughter, Miss Sudarshana Devi, arrived in Beijing by train on the 27th at the invitation of the Chinese Academy of Sciences for a visit to our country.

Those who greeted them at the station included: Zhou Taixuan, Director of the Compilation and Translation Bureau of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Luo Changpei, Director of the Institute of Linguistics, Lü Shuxiang, Deputy Director, and representatives from the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 印度语言学家维拉教授到京

1955-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    印度语言学家维拉教授到京
    新华社二十八日讯　应中国科学院邀请来我国访问的印度国会联邦院议员、印度文化国际学院院长、印度著名语言学家拉古·维拉教授和他的女儿苏达尔·夏娜·德维小姐已在二十七日乘火车抵京。
    到车站欢迎的有：中国科学院编译局局长周太玄、语言研究所所长罗常培、副所长吕叔湘等人和中国人民对外文化协会的代表。
