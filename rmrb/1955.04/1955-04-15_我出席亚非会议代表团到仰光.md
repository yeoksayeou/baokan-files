Title: Delegation Attending Asian-African Conference Arrives in Rangoon
Author:
Date: 1955-04-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 14th - The delegation of the People's Republic of China attending the Asian-African Conference, led by Zhou Enlai, the Chief Representative of the delegation, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, left Beijing by plane on April 7th, en route to Indonesia via Burma. Accompanied on the same plane were U Ohn Maung, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Union of Burma to the People's Republic of China, and Mononutu, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Indonesia to the People's Republic of China.
    The composition of the delegation of the People's Republic of China is as follows:
    Chief Representative of the delegation: Zhou Enlai; Representatives: Chen Yi, Ye Jizhuang, Zhang Hanfu, Huang Zhen (already in Indonesia); Advisors: Liao Chengzhi, Yang Qiqing, Qiao Guanhua, Chen Jiakang, Huang Hua, Da Pusheng; Secretary-General: Wang Zhuoru.
    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 14th - The delegation of the People's Republic of China attending the Asian-African Conference, led by Zhou Enlai, the Chief Representative, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Rangoon, the capital of Burma, at 10:15 AM on the 14th en route to Indonesia.
    U Ohn Maung, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Union of Burma to the People's Republic of China, and Mononutu, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Indonesia to the People's Republic of China, also arrived on the same plane.
    U Nu, the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, personally welcomed them at the airport.
    Also present at the airport to welcome them were Burmese Foreign Minister Sao Khun Hkio and other Burmese government officials.
    Yao Zhongming, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Burma, and embassy staff also welcomed them at the airport.
    The ambassadors of the Soviet Union, India, and the United Kingdom to Burma also welcomed them at the airport.
    The delegation of the People's Republic of China attending the Asian-African Conference, led by Zhou Enlai, the Chief Representative, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, left Beijing on April 7th en route to Indonesia via Burma. The picture shows Premier Zhou Enlai acknowledging the well-wishers before the plane took off. (Attached image)

### 我出席亚非会议代表团到仰光

1955-04-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我出席亚非会议代表团到仰光
    新华社十四日讯　出席亚非会议的中华人民共和国代表团在代表团首席代表、中华人民共和国总理兼外交部长周恩来率领下，已经在四月七日乘飞机离开北京，取道缅甸前往印度尼西亚。随机同行的有缅甸联邦驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使吴拉茂和印度尼西亚共和国驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使莫诺努图。
    中华人民共和国代表团组成人员如下：
    代表团首席代表：周恩来，代表：陈毅、叶季壮、章汉夫、黄镇（已在印度尼西亚），顾问：廖承志、杨奇清、乔冠华、陈家康、黄华、达浦生，秘书长：王倬如。
    新华社仰光十四日电　出席亚非会议的中华人民共和国代表团在首席代表、中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来率领下，在赴印度尼西亚途中在十四日上午十时一刻到达缅甸首都仰光。
    随机同来的有缅甸联邦驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使吴拉茂和印度尼西亚共和国驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使莫诺努图。
    缅甸联邦政府总理吴努亲到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的还有缅甸外交部长藻昆卓和缅甸政府其他官员。
    中华人民共和国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的还有苏联、印度和英国驻缅甸大使。
    出席亚非会议的中华人民共和国代表团在代表团首席代表、中华人民共和国总理兼外交部长周恩来率领下，四月七日离京取道缅甸前往印度尼西亚。图为在飞机起飞前，周恩来总理向欢送者答礼。（附图片）
