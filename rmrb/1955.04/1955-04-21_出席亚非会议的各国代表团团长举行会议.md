Title: Heads of Delegations to the Asian-African Conference Hold Meeting
Author:
Date: 1955-04-21
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Bandung, 20th – The heads of delegations to the Asian-African Conference held a meeting on the morning of the 20th. The meeting will continue at 3 p.m. on the afternoon of the 20th.
    The Economic Committee and the Cultural Committee also held meetings separately on the morning of the 20th.

### 出席亚非会议的各国代表团团长举行会议

1955-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    出席亚非会议的各国代表团团长举行会议
    新华社万隆二十日电　出席亚非会议的各国代表团团长二十日上午举行了会议。会议将在二十日下午三时继续举行。
    经济委员会和文化委员会二十日上午也分别举行了会议。
