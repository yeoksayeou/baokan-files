Title: Commemorative Activities for Lenin Unfold Across China

Author:

Date: 1955-04-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 20th: The 85th anniversary of the birth of the great revolutionary teacher Lenin falls on April 22nd. Commemorative activities are unfolding across China.

　　In recent days, newspapers in the capital have published articles and pictures commemorating Lenin. Many magazines published in Beijing have also featured special articles and pictures commemorating Lenin in their latest issues.

　　Exhibitions introducing Lenin's life are being held in Beijing and many other major cities. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association of Beijing has launched exhibitions of the pictures "The Great Lenin" and "Lenin's Life" at the Soviet Picture Exhibition Room in Beihai Park and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Gallery in the Working People's Cultural Palace, starting today. The Beijing Library also started exhibiting the picture "The Great Lenin" and displaying Lenin's works in the library from today. The Lenin's life and deeds exhibition held by the Dalian-Lushun Sino-Soviet Friendship Association opened on the 19th.

　　On the anniversary of Lenin's birth, commemorative gatherings will be held in Beijing and all provinces and cities across the country.

　　All provinces and cities will screen films about Lenin's life around the anniversary.

### 我国各地展开纪念列宁的活动

1955-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我国各地展开纪念列宁的活动
    据新华社二十日讯　四月二十二日是伟大的革命导师列宁诞辰八十五周年纪念日，我国各地正在展开纪念活动。
    连日来首都各报刊载了纪念列宁的文章和图片。在北京出版的许多杂志也在最近一期上刊载了纪念列宁的专文和图片。
    北京和许多大城市都正在举办介绍列宁生平的展览会。北京市中苏友好协会从今日起，已在北海公园苏联图片展览室和劳动人民文化宫中苏友好画廊等处展出了图片“伟大的列宁”和“列宁的生平”。北京图书馆也从今日起在馆内展出了图片“伟大的列宁”，并陈列了列宁的著作。旅大市中苏友好协会举办的列宁生平事迹展览会已在十九日开幕。
    在列宁诞辰纪念日，北京和全国各省、市都将举行纪念集会。
    各省、市在纪念日前后将放映有关列宁生平的电影。
