Title: Moscow Newspapers Publish Full Text of Chairman Mao's Order Ending the State of War Between China and Germany
Author:
Date: 1955-04-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 9th. Moscow newspapers all published on the 9th the full text of the order issued by Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China on the 7th regarding the end of the state of war between the People's Republic of China and Germany.

### 莫斯科各报全文刊登  毛主席结束中德战争状态命令

1955-04-10
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科各报全文刊登
    毛主席结束中德战争状态命令
    新华社莫斯科九日电　莫斯科各报九日都刊登了中华人民共和国毛泽东主席七日发布的关于结束中华人民共和国同德国之间的战争状态的命令全文。
