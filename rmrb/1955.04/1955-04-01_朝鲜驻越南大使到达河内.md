Title: Korean Ambassador to Vietnam Arrives in Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1955-04-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 30th:  The newly-appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Xu Zhe, and his embassy staff arrived in Hanoi on the afternoon of the 30th.

　　范文同, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Wu Tingying, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others were present to welcome them.

　　Diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union, China, and other People's Democratic countries to Vietnam also came to welcome them.

### 朝鲜驻越南大使到达河内

1955-04-01
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜驻越南大使到达河内
    新华社河内三十日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻越南民主共和国特命全权大使徐哲和使馆人员一行在三十日下午抵达河内。
    前往欢迎的有越南民主共和国外交部长范文同、外交部典礼局局长武廷莹等人。
    苏联、中国和其他人民民主国家驻越南的外交使节也前往欢迎。
