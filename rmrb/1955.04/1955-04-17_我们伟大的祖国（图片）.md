Our Great Motherland
The Daye Steel Plant has built a "Workers' Village" for its workers in the beautiful scenic area of Huangshi City. There are twenty-one three-story buildings and a primary school for the workers' children. The steel plant's workers live here.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    大冶钢厂在黄石市幽美的风景区为工人们兴建了一个“工人村”。这里有二十一幢三层楼房和一所子弟学校，钢厂的工人们就居住在这里。
