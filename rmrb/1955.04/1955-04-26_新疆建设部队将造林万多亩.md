Title: Xinjiang Construction Corps to Afforest Over Ten Thousand Mu
Author:
Date: 1955-04-26
Page: 2
The People's Liberation Army Xinjiang Military Region Production and Construction Corps plans to afforest over 18,000 mu this year. The area to be afforested this year will be nearly three times larger than in 1954, with windbreak and farmland protection forests accounting for over 16,000 mu. At present, the corps has made various preparations and will soon begin tree planting and afforestation work. (Our correspondent in Urumqi)

### 新疆建设部队将造林万多亩

1955-04-26
第2版()
专栏：

    新疆建设部队将造林万多亩
      人民解放军新疆军区生产建设部队今年计划造林一万八千多亩。今年造林的面积将比一九五四年扩大将近三倍，其中防风护田林占一万六千多亩。目前，这个部队已经作好各种准备，不久即将开始植树造林工作。（本报驻乌鲁木齐记者）
