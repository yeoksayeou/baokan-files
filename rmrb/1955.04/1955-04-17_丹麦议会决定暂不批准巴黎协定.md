Title: Danish Parliament Decides to Temporarily Not Approve Paris Agreements
Author:
Date: 1955-04-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Copenhagen, April 15th: The unicameral Danish Parliament, during a debate on the Paris Agreements concerning West Germany's participation in the North Atlantic Treaty Organization on the 14th, decided to temporarily not approve the bill, and instead return it to a special committee for "further study."

During the debate, Danish Communist Party members and spokespersons for the Radical Left party opposed the ratification of the agreement. Radical Left leader Jørgensen said: Rearming West Germany will bring fatal consequences to Europe.

### 丹麦议会决定暂不批准巴黎协定

1955-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

    丹麦议会决定暂不批准巴黎协定
    新华社十五日讯　哥本哈根消息：一院制的丹麦议会十四日在辩论使西德参加北大西洋集团的巴黎协定时，决定暂不批准这个法案，先退交一个专门委员会作“进一步的研究”。
    在辩论中，丹麦共产党议员和激进党的发言人反对批准这个协定。激进党领袖约根森说：重新武装西德将给欧洲带来致命的后果。
