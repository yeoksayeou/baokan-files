Title: PLA on the Fujian Front Shoots Down and Damages One Chiang Kai-shek Plane Each

Author:
Date: 1955-04-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 3rd—The People's Liberation Army anti-aircraft artillery units on the Fujian front shot down and damaged one Chiang Kai-shek plane each.

　　At 9:30 AM on the 3rd, two Chiang Kai-shek F-47 type planes intruded into the airspace over Jinjiang County, Fujian Province. The People's Liberation Army anti-aircraft artillery units immediately launched fierce fire on these two Chiang Kai-shek planes, shooting down one of them. The other was damaged and fled in panic towards the sea southeast of Quanzhou Bay.

　　The downed Chiang Kai-shek plane crashed near Suqian Village, more than 30 kilometers south of Jinjiang County, and was burned on the spot. The plane was named "Little Dragon" and its number was "318". The pilot, Luo Muwen, parachuted and was captured immediately. According to Luo Muwen's confession: He is a lieutenant pilot of the 7th Squadron of the 3rd Brigade of the Chiang Kai-shek Air Force, and his military number is "2045".

### 福建前线解放军击落击伤蒋贼飞机各一架

1955-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    福建前线解放军击落击伤蒋贼飞机各一架
    新华社福建前线三日电  福建前线中国人民解放军高射炮部队击落、击伤蒋贼飞机各一架。
    三日上午九时三十分，蒋贼F—47型飞机两架，窜扰福建省晋江县境上空。中国人民解放军高射炮部队当即对这两架蒋贼飞机展开猛烈射击，击落其中的一架，另一架被击伤，慌忙向泉州湾东南海面上空逃去。
    被击落的这架蒋贼飞机坠于晋江县城以南三十多公里的苏前村附近，当场焚毁，机名为“小龙”，机号是“318”。机上的驾驶员罗穆文跳伞降落后当即被俘。据罗穆文自供：他是蒋贼空军三大队七中队的少尉飞行员，军号是“2045”。
