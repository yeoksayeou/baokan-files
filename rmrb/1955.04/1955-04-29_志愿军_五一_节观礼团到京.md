Title: Volunteer Army "May Day" Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-04-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th – The Chinese People's Volunteer Army "May Day" Homecoming Observation Delegation, led by General Zhao Zhangcheng, arrived in Beijing by train on April 27th.

### 志愿军“五一”节观礼团到京

1955-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军“五一”节观礼团到京
    新华社二十八日讯　中国人民志愿军“五一”节归国观礼代表团在团长赵章成将军率领下，在四月二十七日乘火车到达北京。
