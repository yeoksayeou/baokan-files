Title: Trade Union Delegations from Various Countries Arrive in Beijing One After Another
Author:
Date: 1955-04-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 24th: At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Vietnamese Trade Union Delegation headed by Pei Taiyang, the Mongolian Trade Union Delegation headed by Sangri Surun, the Czechoslovak Trade Union Delegation headed by Kao Er, the Hungarian Trade Union Delegation headed by Olohi Istvan, the Albanian Trade Union Delegation headed by Piro Piristri, and the Yugoslav Trade Union Delegation headed by Dragi Stamenkovic arrived in Beijing by train and plane on the afternoon of the 24th to participate in the "May Day" celebrations and pay visits to China.

When the trade union delegations from various countries arrived, Li Jiebo, Liu Ziju, and Dong Xin, secretaries of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and heads of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and various industrial trade unions went to welcome them.

Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd: At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the industrial trade unions of China's heavy industry, railways, coal mines, seafarers, and road transport, the British Railway Union Delegation headed by Bell, the Australian Trade Union Delegation headed by Ross, and the New Zealand Trade Union Delegation headed by Thor arrived in Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 23rd to participate in the "May Day" celebrations and pay visits to China.

Heads of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the industrial trade unions of China's heavy industry, railways, coal mines, seafarers, and road transport were at the station to welcome them.

The Australian Trade Union Delegation is composed of representatives from the Amalgamated Engineering Union and the Central Council of the Miners' Federation of Australia, and the New Zealand Trade Union Delegation is composed of representatives from the New Zealand Railway Tradesmen's Association, the New Zealand Drivers' Union, and the New Zealand West Coast Buller Miners' Union.

### 各国工会代表团陆续到京

1955-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    各国工会代表团陆续到京
    新华社二十四日讯　应中华全国总工会的邀请，来我国参加“五一”节观礼和访问的以裴泰杨为首的越南工会代表团、以桑日苏伦为首的蒙古工会代表团，以高考尔为首的捷克斯洛伐克工会代表团，以奥洛希·伊斯特万为首的匈牙利工会代表团、以皮洛·皮里斯特里为首的阿尔巴尼亚工会代表团和以德拉吉·斯塔门科维奇为首的南斯拉夫工会代表团，在二十四日下午分别乘火车和飞机到达北京。
    各国工会代表团到达时，分别前往欢迎的有：中华全国总工会书记处书记李颉伯、刘子久、董昕，中华全国总工会国际联络部和各产业工会负责人。
    新华社二十三日讯　应中华全国总工会和中国重工业、铁路、煤矿、海员和公路运输等产业工会的邀请，来我国参加“五一”节观礼和访问的以贝尔为首的英国铁路工会代表团、以罗斯为首的澳大利亚工会代表团和以托尔为首的新西兰工会代表团在二十三日下午乘火车到达北京。
    到车站欢迎的，有中华全国总工会国际联络部和中国重工业、铁路、煤矿、海员和公路运输等产业工会的负责人。
    澳大利亚工会代表团由澳大利亚五金工人工会联合会和澳大利亚矿工联合会中央理事会的代表组成，新西兰工会代表团由新西兰铁路员工联合会、新西兰司机工会和新西兰西海岸布勒矿工工会的代表组成。
