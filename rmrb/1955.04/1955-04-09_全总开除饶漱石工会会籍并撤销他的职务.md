Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Expels Rao Shushi from Trade Union Membership and Revokes His Positions
Author:
Date: 1955-04-09
Page: 1
 
Xinhua News Agency, April 8th—The Presidium of the Seventh Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions held its seventh meeting on April 8th. The Presidium meeting considered that Rao Shushi's anti-Party criminal activities demonstrated that he was the most dangerous enemy undermining the unity of the working class and sabotaging the revolutionary cause of the working class. In accordance with Article 5 of the Trade Union Constitution of the People's Republic of China, the Presidium unanimously decided to expel Rao Shushi from trade union membership and revoke his position as a member of the Seventh Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

### 全总开除饶漱石工会会籍并撤销他的职务

1955-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

    全总开除饶漱石工会会籍并撤销他的职务
    新华社八日讯　中华全国总工会第七届执行委员会主席团于四月八日举行第七次会议。主席团会议认为，饶漱石反党的罪恶活动，说明他是破坏工人阶级团结、破坏工人阶级革命事业的最危险的敌人。主席团根据中华人民共和国工会章程第五条的规定，一致决议开除饶漱石的工会会籍，并撤销他的中华全国总工会第七届执行委员的职务。
