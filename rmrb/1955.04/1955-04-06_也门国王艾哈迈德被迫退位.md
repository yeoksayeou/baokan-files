Title: Yemen King Ahmed Forced to Abdicate
Author:
Date: 1955-04-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 5th: According to TASS, on the 4th, Yemen King Ahmed has been forced to abdicate, and his brother, Emir Abdullah, the former Minister of Foreign Affairs, has succeeded to the throne.

At the time of the coup, the Prime Ministers of Egypt, Syria, and Saudi Arabia were in Cairo discussing the establishment of a new Arab League. During the discussions, Yemen Prime Minister Hassani repeatedly stated that Yemen supported the alliance and expressed its opposition to the Turkish-Iraqi military treaty.

Cairo Radio reported that the new King of Yemen favors Western methods and ideas, and also favors granting foreign companies unlimited power to develop Yemen's natural resources.

According to Reuters, the abdicated King of Yemen's son, Edbar Dar, the governor of Hodeida province, is trying to gather tribes from northern and central Yemen to support his father.

### 也门国王艾哈迈德被迫退位

1955-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

    也门国王艾哈迈德被迫退位
    新华社五日讯　据塔斯社开罗四日讯：也门国王艾哈迈德已被迫退位，而由原任外交大臣的他的兄弟埃米尔·阿卜杜拉继任王位。
    在这次政变发生的时候，埃及、叙利亚和沙特阿拉伯的总理们正在开罗商谈建立一个新的阿拉伯国家联盟。在商谈过程中，也门首相哈萨尼一再声明说，也门支持这个联盟，并表示它对土耳其—伊拉克军事条约持反对态度。
    开罗电台报道，也门的新国王赞成西方的方法和观念，也赞成给予外国公司以开发也门自然资源的无限权力。
    据路透社报道，已退位的也门国王的儿子、荷得达省省长埃德巴达尔正在设法纠集也门北部和中部的部落来支持他的父亲。
