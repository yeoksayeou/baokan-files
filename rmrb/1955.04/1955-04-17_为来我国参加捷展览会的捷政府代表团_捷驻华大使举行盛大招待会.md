Title: Czechoslovak Ambassador to China Holds Grand Reception for Czechoslovak Government Delegation Attending the Czechoslovak Exhibition in Our Country
Author:
Date: 1955-04-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 16th. Czechoslovak Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Gregor, held a grand reception at the Moscow Restaurant in the Soviet Exhibition Center on the evening of the 16th for the Czechoslovak Republic Government Delegation invited to China to attend the opening ceremony of the "Exhibition of Achievements in Ten Years of Socialist Construction of Czechoslovakia".

The head of the Czechoslovak Republic Government Delegation, Vice Premier of the Czechoslovak Republic, Jankovcova, the deputy head, Minister of Metallurgical Industry and Mining of the Czechoslovak Republic, Rejtmajer, and delegation members: Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of Czechoslovakia, Kozol, Deputy Minister of Machine Industry, Šmok, Academician of the Academy of Sciences, Ješikič, painter Belč, sculptor Lauda, and the director of the "Exhibition of Achievements in Ten Years of Socialist Construction of Czechoslovakia," Pok, and staff members, all attended the reception.

Invited to attend the reception were: Vice Premiers of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Ulanfu, Li Xiannian, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, Minister of Heavy Industry Wang Heshou, Minister of Fuel Industry Chen Yu, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Forestry Liang Xi, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Li Zheren, and other deputy ministers of various ministries, and Vice Mayor of Beijing, Feng Jiping.

Also invited to attend the reception were: Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Nan Hanchen, and other leaders of people's organizations, as well as people from all walks of life in the capital.

Diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China were also invited to attend the reception.

A total of more than 300 people attended the reception.

At the reception, Ambassador Gregor delivered a speech first. Then, Vice Premier Li Xiannian and Delegation Leader Jankovcova delivered speeches successively.

During the banquet, the guests and hosts frequently stood up and toasted to the great friendship between the people of China and Czechoslovakia, to the health of the leaders of the two parties and governments, and to the increasing development and strengthening of the strength of the peaceful, democratic, and socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 为来我国参加捷展览会的捷政府代表团  捷驻华大使举行盛大招待会

1955-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

    为来我国参加捷展览会的捷政府代表团
    捷驻华大使举行盛大招待会
    新华社十六日讯　捷克斯洛伐克驻我国特命全权大使格里哥尔，为应邀来我国参加“捷克斯洛伐克十年社会主义建设成就展览会”开幕式的捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团，特于十六日晚在苏联展览馆莫斯科餐厅举行盛大招待会。
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团团长、捷克斯洛伐克共和国副总理扬科夫卓娃，副团长、捷克斯洛伐克共和国冶金工业与采矿部部长雷特马耶尔，团员：捷克斯洛伐克对外贸易部副部长科佐尔、机器工业部副部长什莫克、科学院院士叶希基克、画家贝尔契、雕刻家劳达，以及“捷克斯洛伐克十年社会主义建设成就展览会”主任波克和工作人员，都出席了招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的有：中华人民共和国国务院副总理乌兰夫、李先念，秘书长习仲勋，外交部副部长张闻天，重工业部部长王鹤寿，燃料工业部部长陈郁，邮电部部长朱学范，林业部部长梁希，对外贸易部副部长李哲人和其他各部副部长，北京市副市长冯基平。
    应邀出席招待会的还有：中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸和其他人民团体的负责人，以及首都各界人士。
    各国驻华使节和外交官员也应邀出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的共有三百多人。
    招待会上，格里哥尔大使首先致词。接着，李先念副总理和扬科夫卓娃团长先后致词。
    席间，宾主频频起立，为中捷两国人民的伟大友谊和两国党和政府领袖的健康，为以苏联为首的和平民主社会主义阵营的力量的日益发展和壮大而干杯。
    招待会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
