Title: The Five Prime Ministers of the Asian-African Conference Host a Reception
Author: 
Date: 1955-04-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Bandung, April 24th—The prime ministers of the five initiating countries of the Asian-African Conference, U Nu, Kotelawala, Nehru, Ali Sastroamidjojo, and Muhammad Ali, hosted a reception at the Homann Hotel at 10:00 PM tonight to mark the end of the conference. The heads and members of the delegations from the 29 participating countries, as well as all conference staff, attended the reception.

### 亚非会议五个发起国总理举行招待会

1955-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    亚非会议五个发起国总理举行招待会
    新华社万隆二十四日电　亚非会议五个发起国家的总理吴努、科特拉瓦拉、尼赫鲁、阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约和穆罕默德·阿里二十四日晚十时在霍曼旅馆举行招待会，以表示会议的结束。参加招待会的有与会二十九国代表团团长、团员和会议的全体工作人员。
