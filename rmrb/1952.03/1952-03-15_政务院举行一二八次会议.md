Title: Government Administration Council Holds 128th Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1952-03-15
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, March 14th—The 128th session of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 14th. At the meeting, He Cheng, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government, gave a report on the situation of the insect and poison germs spread by American imperialism in Northeast China, and our epidemic prevention measures.

### 政务院举行一二八次会议

1952-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行一二八次会议
    【新华社十四日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百二十八次政务会议于十四日下午举行。会上，中央人民政府卫生部贺诚副部长作了关于美帝国主义在我国东北撒布传染细菌的昆虫、毒物的情况及我防疫措施的报告。
