Title: The People's Bank of China Lhasa Office Opens

Author: 
Date: 1952-03-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 11th: The People's Bank of China Lhasa Office officially opened on February 22nd. Zhang Jingwu, representative of the Central People's Government, and Generals Zhang Guohua, Commander of the People's Liberation Army Tibet Military Region, and Alapei Apei Awang Jinmei, First Deputy Commander and Kalon of the Tibet Local Government, personally attended the opening ceremony of the office. More than 80 representatives of Tibetan, Hui, and Han merchants from Lhasa City, and representatives of foreign merchants residing in Lhasa, attended to offer their congratulations.

At the opening ceremony, Generals Zhang Jingwu and Zhang Guohua said that the establishment of the People's Bank of China Lhasa Office would provide strong assistance to the people of Tibet in their economic lives. In his speech, General Zhang Guohua specifically explained to foreign merchants that on the basis of equality and mutual benefit, we welcome the establishment and development of fair commercial and trade relations with the people of neighboring countries. In his speech, Apei Awang Jinmei placed great hope on the People's Bank of China Lhasa Office, saying that with the strong help and support of the People's Bank of China Lhasa Office, the economic construction undertakings in the Tibet region will surely achieve rapid development in the future.

The establishment of the People's Bank of China Lhasa Office has been warmly congratulated by the people of Tibet, and merchants of all ethnic groups in Lhasa have flocked to the office to handle remittance procedures. Within a few days after the opening, more than 50 payments for goods were remitted.

### 中国人民银行拉萨办事处开业

1952-03-12
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民银行拉萨办事处开业
    【新华社拉萨十一日电】中国人民银行拉萨办事处已在二月二十二日正式开业。中央人民政府代表张经武将军和人民解放军西藏军区司令员张国华将军、第一副司令员兼西藏地方政府噶伦阿沛·阿旺晋美都亲自前往参加了该办事处的开业典礼。前往祝贺的有拉萨市的藏、回、汉各族商人代表八十多名，和住拉萨的外侨商人代表。
    开业典礼上，张经武和张国华两将军讲话说：中国人民银行拉萨办事处的成立，使西藏人民将在经济生活上获得有力的帮助。张国华将军在讲话中特别向外侨商人说明：在平等互利的基础上，我们是欢迎跟友邻各国人民建立和发展公平的通商贸易关系的。阿沛·阿旺晋美在讲话中对中国人民银行拉萨办事处给予重大的希望，他说：在中国人民银行拉萨办事处的大力帮助和支持下，今后西藏地区的经济建设事业一定会得到飞跃的发展。
    中国人民银行拉萨办事处的成立，受到西藏人民的热烈祝贺，拉萨的各族商人纷纷前往该办事处办理汇兑手续。在开业后几天之内就汇出五十多笔货款。
