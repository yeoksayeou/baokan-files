Title: Stalin International Peace Prize Winner Anna Seghers Receives Prize in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1952-03-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 24th dispatch: A prize-giving ceremony was held on the 19th in the Moscow Kremlin to award the "Strengthening International Peace" Stalin International Prize to the famous peace fighter and renowned German female writer Anna Seghers. Representatives from all circles of the Soviet Union, famous writers, scientists, artists, and Moscow's Stakhanovite workers attended the ceremony. Skobeltsyn, Chairman of the Stalin International Peace Prize Committee, read out the committee's decision to award the prize and then presented Anna Seghers with the certificate and gold medal, along with a congratulatory speech. Subsequently, representatives from the Soviet Peace Defense Committee, Yershova, the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, Leontieva, and the Soviet Writers' Association, Surkov, delivered congratulatory speeches one after another. In her acceptance speech, Anna Seghers expressed her heartfelt gratitude for being awarded this prestigious prize and pledged to continue the struggle for peace with renewed vigor.

### 斯大林国际和平奖金获得者  西格斯在莫斯科接受奖金

1952-03-25
第4版()
专栏：

    斯大林国际和平奖金获得者
    西格斯在莫斯科接受奖金
    【新华社二十四日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：十九日莫斯科克里姆林宫内举行了授奖典礼，以“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金授予著名的和平战士、德国著名女作家安娜·西格斯。参加典礼的有苏联各界的代表，著名的作家、科学家、艺术家和莫斯科的斯塔哈诺夫式工人。斯大林国际和平奖金委员会主席斯科贝尔琴在会上宣读了委员会的授奖决定之后，就把奖状和金质奖章授予安娜·西格斯，并致祝辞。接着，苏联拥护和平委员会代表叶尔硕娃、苏联妇女反法西斯委员会代表里昂捷娃和苏联作家协会代表苏尔科夫，相继致词祝贺。安娜·西格斯在致答词中对授予这个崇高的奖金表示衷心的感谢，并保证将以新的力量进行争取和平的斗争。
