Title: Attending the Executive Bureau Meeting of the World Peace Council, Chinese Representatives Guo Moruo and Others Depart Beijing for Oslo
Author:
Date: 1952-03-22
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 21st: The Executive Bureau Meeting of the World Peace Council will be held in Oslo, the capital of Norway, from March 29th to 31st. The Chinese representatives attending the meeting include Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairman of the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council; Mao Dun, member of the Executive Bureau; Qian Sanqiang, member of the China Peace Committee; and staff members, a total of six people. They departed Beijing by plane on the 21st to attend the meeting in Oslo. Those who went to the airport to see them off included Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairman of the China Peace Committee; Liu Guan, Secretary-General; Cui Yueli, Deputy Secretary-General; Meng Yu, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department; Zhu Kezhen and Wu Youxun, Vice-Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Chen Zhongjing, Deputy Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Government Administration Council's Culture and Education Committee; Sha Kefu, Secretary of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles; and Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

### 出席世界和平理事会执行局会议  我国代表郭沫若等离京赴奥斯陆

1952-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

    出席世界和平理事会执行局会议
    我国代表郭沫若等离京赴奥斯陆
    【新华社二十一日讯】世界和平理事会执行局会议将在三月二十九日到三十一日在挪威首都奥斯陆举行，出席会议的我国代表：世界和平理事会执行局副主席郭沫若、执行局委员茅盾，中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会委员钱三强及工作人员等一行六人，已在二十一日乘飞机启程离京前往奥斯陆参加会议。到机场欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会副主席陈叔通、秘书长刘贯一、副秘书长崔月犁、联络部副部长孟雨，中国科学院副院长竺可桢、吴有训，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局副局长陈忠经，中华全国文学艺术界联合会秘书长沙可夫，中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长金子明等人。
