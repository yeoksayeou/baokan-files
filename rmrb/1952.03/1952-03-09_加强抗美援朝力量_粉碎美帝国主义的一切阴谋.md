Title: Strengthen the Resistance to America and Aid Korea, Smash All Conspiracies of American Imperialism
Author:
Date: 1952-03-09
Page: 2

Reader's Letter

    Strengthen the Resistance to America and Aid Korea
    Smash All Conspiracies of American Imperialism
    Editor: Our entire school's female faculty, staff, students, and family members angrily protest the towering crime of the American invading army spreading bacteria in our Northeast.
    The inhumane American invading army is actually conducting bacteriological warfare on the sacred territory of our great motherland—the Northeast—disregarding everything and seriously violating and undermining international law prohibiting the use of all bacteriological weapons. We sternly protest the insane and beastly behavior of the American invaders with incomparable anger! We want to solemnly tell the American invaders: Your arrogant provocative behavior will absolutely not escape the just condemnation and sanctions of the people of the world!
    We must be a hundred times more vigilant against all possible atrocities by the American invading army, continue to carry out the work of resisting America and aiding Korea, do a good job in our respective posts, and especially do a good job in the anti-corruption and anti-theft movement at present, and strive to finally smash all the conspiracies of American imperialism!
    The entire female faculty, staff, students, and family members of the Beijing Institute of Northern Jiaotong University

### 加强抗美援朝力量  粉碎美帝国主义的一切阴谋

1952-03-09
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    加强抗美援朝力量
    粉碎美帝国主义的一切阴谋
    编辑同志：我们全校女教职员工同学家属愤怒地抗议美国侵略军在我国东北撒布细菌的滔天罪行。
    灭绝人性的美国侵略军，竟在我们伟大祖国的神圣领土上——东北，不顾一切地进行细菌战，严重地违反了和破坏了禁止使用一切细菌武器的国际公法。我们以无比愤怒的心情严厉地抗议美国侵略者丧心病狂的兽行！我们要严正地告诉美国侵略者：你们这种狂妄的挑衅行为是绝对逃不出全世界人民正义的谴责和制裁的！
    我们要百倍警惕美国侵略军一切可能的暴行，继续进行抗美援朝工作，做好各人岗位上的工作，在目前尤其要把反贪污、反盗窃运动搞好，为最后粉碎美帝国主义一切阴谋而奋斗！
    北方交通大学京院全体女教职工家属同学
