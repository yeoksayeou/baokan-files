    Our Great Motherland
    The democratic reform of factories plays a significant role in developing production. After democratic reform, the workers of a steelmaking plant in a certain steel company in Central and Southern China actively adopted the assembly line method, bringing their production potential into full play. This is a scene of the workers working hard to produce under the plan of "increasing production and practicing economy by 2,050 billion yuan for the country."

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    工厂的民主改革对于发展生产有重大作用。中南某钢铁公司炼钢厂的工人们，经过民主改革以后，积极采用了流水作业法，发挥了生产的潜在力。这是工人们在“为国家增产节约二千零五十亿元”的计划下，正在努力生产的一个场面。
