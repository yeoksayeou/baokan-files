    Our Great Motherland
    After arriving in the capital, the visiting delegation of ethnic minorities from Southwest China has been visiting various construction projects in Beijing for several days. This is a scene of some of the female youth league members of the visiting delegation of ethnic minorities from Southwest China visiting the universal wood testing machine at Tsinghua University.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国西南各民族参观团到达首都后，连日参观了北京的各项建设。这是西南各民族参观团的一部分女青年团员在清华大学参观木材全能试验机的情形。
