Title: The Government Administration Council Holds its 126th Meeting; Passes Decision on Unified Handling of Organ Production
Author:
Date: 1952-03-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 29th - The Central People's Government Administration Council held its 126th meeting on the afternoon of February 29th. After hearing a statement by Rong Zihe, Deputy Minister of the Central People's Government Ministry of Finance, the meeting passed the Decision on Unified Handling of Organ Production and decided to establish the Central People's Government Organ Production Handling Committee. The meeting heard and approved the report made by Li Lisan, Minister of the Central People's Government Ministry of Labor, summarizing the ministry's work in providing relief to unemployed workers over the past year and a half, and passed supplementary instructions on the issue of providing relief to unemployed workers. In addition, the meeting approved several appointment cases and submitted several appointment and removal cases to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第一二六次政务会议  通过关于统一处理机关生产的决定

1952-03-01
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一二六次政务会议
    通过关于统一处理机关生产的决定
    【新华社二十九日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百二十六次政务会议在二月二十九日下午举行。会议在听取中央人民政府财政部戎子和副部长所作的说明后，通过了关于统一处理机关生产的决定，并决定成立中央人民政府机关生产处理委员会。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府劳动部李立三部长所作的该部关于一年半以来救济失业工人工作总结的报告，通过了关于救济失业工人问题的补充指示。此外，会议通过任命案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
