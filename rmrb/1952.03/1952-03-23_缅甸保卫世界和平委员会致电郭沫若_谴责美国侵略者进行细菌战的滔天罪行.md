Title: Burma Peace Committee for World Peace Sends a Telegram to Guo Moruo Condemning the Heinous Crimes of American Aggressors in Conducting Germ Warfare
Author:
Date: 1952-03-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd: The Secretary of the Burma Peace Committee for World Peace, Maung Maung Gyee, sent a telegram on the 17th to Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, condemning the crimes of American aggressors in conducting germ warfare. The telegram reads as follows:

    The Central Committee of the Burma Peace Committee for World Peace believes that the use of bacteriological weapons by American aggressors in Korea is not only inhumane, but also, like the bombing of Hiroshima, openly threatens to exterminate all Asian people who oppose American rule.

    The Central Committee of the Burma Peace Committee for World Peace condemns this monstrous crime, demands that the Burmese government lodge a protest with the American government, and calls on all people's organizations to unite and protest.

### 缅甸保卫世界和平委员会致电郭沫若  谴责美国侵略者进行细菌战的滔天罪行

1952-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸保卫世界和平委员会致电郭沫若
    谴责美国侵略者进行细菌战的滔天罪行
    【新华社二十三日讯】缅甸保卫世界和平委员会秘书貌貌叫在十七日致电中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会主席郭沫若，谴责美国侵略者进行细菌战的罪行。电文如下：
    缅甸保卫世界和平委员会中央委员会认为：美国侵略者在朝鲜使用细菌武器不但是灭绝人性的，而且正如它轰炸广岛一样，公然地威胁要灭尽一切反对美国统治的亚洲人民。
    缅甸保卫世界和平委员会中央委员会谴责这一滔天罪行，要求缅甸政府向美国政府提出抗议，并号召各人民团体一致起来抗议。
