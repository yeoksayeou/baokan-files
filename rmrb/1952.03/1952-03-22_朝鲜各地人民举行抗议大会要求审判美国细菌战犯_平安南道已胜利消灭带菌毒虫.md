Title: People in Various Parts of Korea Hold Protest Rallies Demanding the Trial of American Germ Warfare Criminals; South Pyongan Province has Successfully Eliminated Germ-Carrying Insects
Author:
Date: 1952-03-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 20th: Korean newspapers continue to publish news of rallies held by people in various parts of Korea protesting the use of bacteriological weapons by the American aggressors. The meetings in various places have all passed resolutions demanding that American criminals be tried by the people's courts.
    "Democratic Korea" reported that protest rallies were held in Anju, Sariwon, Anak, Wonsan, and other places.
    The Korean Central News Agency reported that preventive measures have been taken throughout the country. The agency also published a statement by the Vice-Chairman of the South Pyongan Provincial Anti-Epidemic Committee, who said: Under the guidance of the anti-epidemic teams, the people have successfully eliminated the germ-carrying insects dropped in various areas of the province.

### 朝鲜各地人民举行抗议大会要求审判美国细菌战犯  平安南道已胜利消灭带菌毒虫

1952-03-22
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜各地人民举行抗议大会要求审判美国细菌战犯
    平安南道已胜利消灭带菌毒虫
    【新华社二十一日讯】据塔斯社平壤二十日讯：朝鲜报纸继续登载朝鲜各地人民集会抗议美国侵略者使用细菌武器的消息。各处的会议都通过决议，要求由人民的法庭来审判美国罪犯们。
    “民主朝鲜”报道：在安州、沙里院、安岳、元山和其他地方，都举行了抗议大会。
    朝鲜中央通讯社报道了全国都采取了防止受害的措施，该社并发表了平安南道防疫委员会副委员长的声明，他说：在防疫队指导下的人民，已经胜利地消灭了投在该道各地区的带有病菌的毒虫。
