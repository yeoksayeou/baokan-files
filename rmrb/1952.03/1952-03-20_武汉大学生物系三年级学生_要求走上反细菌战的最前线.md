Title: Wuhan University Biology Department Third-Year Students Request to Go to the Forefront of the Anti-Bacterial Warfare Effort
Author:
Date: 1952-03-20
Page: 2

Reader's Letters

    Wuhan University Biology Department Third-Year Students
    Request to Go to the Forefront of the Anti-Bacterial Warfare Effort
    We have already achieved certain victories in the anti-corruption movement. Now, with even stronger determination, we request to go to the forefront of the anti-bacterial warfare effort. We study biological sciences, and together with the broad masses of the people, we have the confidence to crush the U.S. bandits' conspiracy to wage bacterial warfare.
    All Third-Year Students of the Biology Department, Wuhan University

### 武汉大学生物系三年级学生  要求走上反细菌战的最前线

1952-03-20
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    武汉大学生物系三年级学生
    要求走上反细菌战的最前线
    我们在反贪污运动中已经取得了一定的胜利。现在，我们以更加坚强的决心，要求走上反细菌战的最前线。我们是学习生物科学的，我们同广大人民在一起，有信心粉碎美国强盗进行细菌战的阴谋。
    武汉大学生物系三年级全体同学
