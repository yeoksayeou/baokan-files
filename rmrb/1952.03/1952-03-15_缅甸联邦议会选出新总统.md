Title: The Union Parliament of Burma Elects New President
Author: 
Date: 1952-03-15
Page: 4
    
    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, March 14th: The Union Parliament of Burma's Chamber of Deputies and Chamber of Nationalities held a joint session on the 12th and elected former Chief Justice Dr. Ba U as the new President. Dr. Ba U was sworn in on the 13th.

### 缅甸联邦议会选出新总统

1952-03-15
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸联邦议会选出新总统
    【新华社十四日讯】仰光消息：缅甸联邦议会的众议院和民族院在十二日举行两院联席会议，选出前最高法院院长巴宇博士为新总统。巴宇博士已在十三日宣誓就职。
