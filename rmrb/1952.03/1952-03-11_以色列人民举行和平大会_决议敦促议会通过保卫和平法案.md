Title: Israeli People Hold Peace Conference; Resolution Urges Parliament to Pass Law Defending Peace
Author:
Date: 1952-03-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tel Aviv, March 9th: The Israeli Peace Conference was held in Tel Aviv on March 7th. More than two thousand representatives attended, including many prominent representatives of progressive society, youth, and soldiers.

    The conference summarized the work completed by the Peace Committee in collecting signatures for the World Peace Council's declaration calling on the five major powers to conclude a peace pact. Four hundred thousand people in Israel have signed the declaration. The conference enthusiastically passed a resolution on concluding a peace pact. The conference urged the Israeli parliament to pass a law defending peace. The Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council sent a congratulatory telegram to the conference.

### 以色列人民举行和平大会  决议敦促议会通过保卫和平法案

1952-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    以色列人民举行和平大会
    决议敦促议会通过保卫和平法案
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社特拉维夫九日讯：以色列和平大会已于三月七日在特拉维夫举行，出席的有两千多名代表，其中包括许多进步的社会人士、青年和军人的著名代表。
    大会总结了和平委员会完成为世界和平理事会要求五大国缔结和平公约宣言征集签名运动的工作。以色列人民在该项宣言上签名的已有四十万人。大会热烈地通过了一项关于缔结和平公约的决议。大会敦促以色列议会通过一个保卫和平法案。世界和平理事会执行局曾致电大会祝贺。
