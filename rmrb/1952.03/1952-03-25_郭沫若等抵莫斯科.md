Title: Guo Moruo and Others Arrive in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1952-03-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 24th: Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council Executive Bureau, and Mao Dun, member of the Executive Bureau, and Qian Sanqiang, member of the Chinese People's Committee for Defending World Peace and Opposing American Aggression, representatives of our country attending the World Peace Council Executive Bureau meeting, arrived in Moscow by plane on the 22nd. Among those who went to the airport to welcome them were Kotov, Executive Secretary of the Soviet Peace Committee, Obalin, member of the committee, and Kalishyan, Vice-Chairman of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 郭沫若等抵莫斯科

1952-03-25
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若等抵莫斯科
    【新华社莫斯科二十四日电】出席世界和平理事会执行局会议的我国代表、世界和平理事会执行局副主席郭沫若、执行局委员茅盾和中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会委员钱三强等，在二十二日飞抵莫斯科。前往机场欢迎的有苏联拥护和平委员会执行书记科托夫，委员奥巴林和苏联对外文化协会副主席卡利什扬等。
