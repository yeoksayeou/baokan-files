Title: Czechoslovak Workers Donate One Billion Units of Penicillin to Korean Mothers and Children
Author:
Date: 1952-03-27
Page: 4

    Czechoslovak Workers Donate One Billion Units of Penicillin
    to Korean Mothers and Children
    [Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 26th] According to a report from the Telegraphic Agency of Czechoslovakia on the 24th, workers at the Czechoslovak "Biogena" Penicillin Factory have produced one billion units of penicillin as a gift to Korean mothers and children. These many units of penicillin were produced because workers adopted new working methods, allowing them to exceed their usual production standards.

### 捷工人以十亿单位盘尼西林  赠给朝鲜的母亲和儿童

1952-03-27
第4版()
专栏：

    捷工人以十亿单位盘尼西林
    赠给朝鲜的母亲和儿童
    【新华社二十六日讯】据电通社布拉格二十四日讯：捷克斯洛伐克的“比奥吉那”盘尼西林工厂的工人们，已经制造了十亿单位的盘尼西林，作为送给朝鲜的母亲和儿童们的礼物。这许多单位的盘尼西林是因为工人们采用了新式工作方法，得以超出他们的平常生产标准而生产出来的。
