Title: Visits to Chairman Mao and Commander Peng Dehuai's Hometowns by Representatives of Returning Volunteers and Representatives of the Korean People's Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1952-03-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, March 3rd: The Central-South Delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation and the Central-South Delegation of the Korean People's Delegation to China, a group of over 40 people, visited Shaoshan Township, Xiangtan County, Chairman Mao's hometown, on the 16th.

The representatives gave reports to the people of Shaoshan Township and held a symposium for families of revolutionary martyrs, families of revolutionary soldiers, and labor models. The representatives were respected and loved by the people of Shaoshan Township.賀恩生, an old mother who is already 59 years old, sent the representatives ten eggs and a basket of sweet potatoes. She said that she planted the sweet potatoes herself and insisted that the representatives accept them. 刘秀华, a military family member and labor model, gave the medal she received for participating in the county labor model conference to 黃龜漢, the head of the Central-South Delegation of the Korean People's Delegation to China. 少先队员成丽华 and 林国南, Young Pioneer members, gave their red scarves to Zhang Lüqin, a representative of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation, and Xu Jinyu, a representative of the Korean People's Delegation to China, respectively. After listening to the representatives' reports, the people of Shaoshan Township expressed that they would work hard to increase production and practice thrift to support the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army.

Zhang Lie, the head of the Central-South Delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation, and 黃龜漢, the head of the Central-South Delegation of the Korean People's Delegation to China, visited Wushi Township, Xiangtan County, Commander Peng Dehuai's hometown, on February 18th. The people of Wushi Township braved wind and snow to hold a welcome rally. At the meeting, Zhang Lie, on behalf of Commander Peng and all comrades of the Volunteer Army, paid tribute to the glorious people of Wushi Township. 黃龜漢 said: The Korean people will never forget the brotherly friendship of the Chinese people and the people of Commander Peng's hometown. Representatives of the people of Wushi Township also reported on their situation in resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea and their future work plans.

### 志愿军归国代表和朝鲜人民访华代表  访问毛主席和彭德怀司令员的故乡

1952-03-04
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军归国代表和朝鲜人民访华代表
    访问毛主席和彭德怀司令员的故乡
    【新华社长沙三日电】中国人民志愿军归国代表团中南分团和朝鲜人民访华代表团中南分团一行四十多人，在十六日访问了毛主席的故乡——湘潭县韶山乡。
    代表们向韶山乡人民作了报告；并召开了革命烈士家属、革命军人家属和劳动模范座谈会。代表们受到了韶山乡人民的尊敬和热爱。年已五十九岁的老妈妈贺恩生，送给代表十个鸡蛋和一篮红薯。她说她的红薯是自己亲手种的，一定要代表们收下。军属、劳动模范刘秀华，把自己参加县劳动模范大会得到的奖章，送给朝鲜人民访华代表团中南分团团长黄龟汉。少年儿童队员成丽华和林国南，把自己的红领巾分别献给志愿军归国代表团代表张绿琴和朝鲜人民访华代表团代表许金玉。韶山乡的人民听了代表们的报告后，纷纷表示要努力增产节约，支援中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军。
    中国人民志愿军归国代表团中南分团团长张烈和朝鲜人民访华代表团中南分团团长黄龟汉，在二月十八日访问了彭德怀司令员的故乡——湘潭县乌石乡。乌石乡人民冒着风雪举行了欢迎大会。会上，张烈代表彭司令员和志愿军全体同志向光荣的乌石乡人民致敬。黄龟汉说：朝鲜人民永远也不会忘记中国人民和彭司令员故乡人民兄弟般的友谊。乌石乡人民代表也报告了他们抗美援朝的情况和今后的工作计划。
