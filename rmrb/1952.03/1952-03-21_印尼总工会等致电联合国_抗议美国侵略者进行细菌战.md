Title: Indonesian Trade Union Federation, etc., Cables United Nations to Protest Bacteriological Warfare by American Aggressors
Author:
Date: 1952-03-21
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 20th—The Central Bureau of the Indonesian Trade Union Federation cabled the United Nations on the 15th, severely protesting the crimes of the American aggressors in conducting bacteriological warfare. The cable pointed out: This action by the United States has openly violated the basic humanitarian principles of the United Nations Charter and constitutes the most serious crime. The cable demanded that the United Nations severely sanction this criminal behavior of the United States. At the same time, the headquarters of the Indonesian People's Youth League has sent letters to the Indonesian Prime Minister, the Speaker of the Parliament, and the Indonesian Red Cross, denouncing the United States for using bacteriological weapons and demanding that the crime of the United States in conducting bacteriological warfare be stopped and punished.
    In addition, the Indonesian Association in Bombay, India, has also cabled the Secretary-General of the United Nations to protest the bacteriological warfare conducted by the American army.

### 印尼总工会等致电联合国  抗议美国侵略者进行细菌战

1952-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

    印尼总工会等致电联合国
    抗议美国侵略者进行细菌战
    【新华社二十日讯】雅加达消息：印尼总工会中央局在十五日致电联合国，严重抗议美国侵略者进行细菌战的罪行。电文指出：美国这种行动已公然破坏联合国宪章的基本人道原则，乃是一种最严重的罪行。电文要求联合国严厉制裁美国这种犯罪行为。同时，印尼人民青年团总部已致函印尼总理、国会议长和印尼红十字会，斥责美国使用细菌武器，并要求制止和惩办美国进行细菌战的罪行。
    此外，在印度孟买的印尼协会也致电联合国秘书长，抗议美国军队进行细菌战。
