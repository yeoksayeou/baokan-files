Title: Marshal Vasilevsky of the Soviet Union Replies to Commander-in-Chief Zhu, Thanking Him for His Congratulations on the 34th Anniversary of the Founding of the Soviet Armed Forces
Author:
Date: 1952-03-01
Page: 1

(Xinhua News Agency, 29th) Marshal Vasilevsky, the Soviet Minister of the Army, telegraphed his thanks to Commander-in-Chief Zhu De for his congratulations on the 34th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Armed Forces. The telegram is as follows:

To Comrade Zhu De, Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army:

Thank you for your congratulations and good wishes on the 34th anniversary of the Soviet Army and Navy.

Marshal of the Soviet Union, Minister of the Army, A. Vasilevsky, February 27th

### 苏联华西列夫斯基元帅复电朱总司令  感谢对苏联武装部队建军三十四周年的祝贺

1952-03-01
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联华西列夫斯基元帅复电朱总司令
    感谢对苏联武装部队建军三十四周年的祝贺
    【新华社二十九日讯】苏联陆军部长华西列夫斯基元帅电谢朱德总司令对苏联武装部队建军三十四周年的祝贺。电文如下：中国人民解放军总司令朱德同志：
    感谢您对苏联陆海军三十四周年纪念的祝贺和良好的愿望。
    苏联元帅陆军部长阿·华西列夫斯基  二月二十七日
