Title: American Aggressors Extend Germ Warfare to Qingdao; Continue to Frenziedly Scatter Bacterial Insects in Northeastern China; 62 American Planes Intrude into Northeastern China's Airspace in Successive Groups
Author:
Date: 1952-03-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Qingdao, March 14th: The American aggressors have recently further extended their heinous germ warfare from Northeast China to the vicinity of Qingdao in Shandong Province.

　　At 21:00 on March 6th, an American aircraft intruded into the airspace over Taipingjiao, Fushansuo, Damaidao, and Shazikou in the suburbs of Qingdao, scattering bacterial insects. After the enemy plane passed, residents in Taipingjiao and Shazikou in the eastern suburbs of Qingdao discovered a large number of suddenly appearing flies, spiders, small beetles, grasshoppers, soil bees, ants, and other poisonous insects.

　　At 11:00 on March 7th, an American aircraft intruded into the airspace over Cangkou and Licun in the northeast of Qingdao, scattering a large number of poisonous insects such as flies, mosquitoes, fleas, and spiders.

　　The city's Bacterial Warfare Defense Special Committee and the city's epidemic prevention team have been established, and epidemic prevention personnel have gone to the areas where poisonous insects were discovered to carry out work. The people of the city are engaging in an intense anti-bacterial warfare struggle with extreme anger.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, March 14th: From March 6th to 8th, the American aggressors continued to shamelessly dispatch military aircraft to intrude into Northeast China and scatter bacterial insects, as follows:

　　From 10:06 to 16:00 on March 6th, American aircraft in four groups, totaling nineteen sorties, successively intruded into the airspace over Andong, Jiuliancheng, Fengcheng, Shuifeng, Dadonggou, and Changdianhekou, scattering large numbers of flies, mosquitoes, fleas, spiders, beetles, ticks, sandflies, lice, ants, earthworms, small black insects and other bacterial-spreading poisonous insects.

　　On March 7th, an American aircraft intruded into the airspace over Ji'an and other places, scattering bacteria. On the same day, poisonous insects such as mosquitoes, flies, fleas, and butterflies were successively discovered in Taipinggou, Zhuanghe, Kuandian, Jinzhou, Shenyang, and Xinmintun in Ji'an.

　　On March 8th, poisonous insects dropped by American aircraft, such as spiders, centipedes, toads, locusts, and bees, were continuously discovered in Shenyang, Tonghua, Linjiang, Fusong, Ji'an, Xinbin, Fengcheng, Andong, and Fushun.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, March 14th: Sixty-two American aircraft in eleven groups intruded into the airspace of Northeast China on the 12th, harassing Andong City, Anping River, Yongdian River Estuary, Dadonggou, Dagushan, Qingduizi, Tangshancheng, Taipingshao, Huangbaidianzi, Gaolichengzi, Fengcheng, Xiuyan, and Ji'an in Liaodong Province. One of them dropped six bombs and strafed Huangbaidianzi and Sandaogou, injuring three residents.

### 美侵略者竟把细菌战扩展到青岛  并继续在我东北地区疯狂撒布细菌毒虫  美机六十二架分批窜扰我国东北领空

1952-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

    美侵略者竟把细菌战扩展到青岛
    并继续在我东北地区疯狂撒布细菌毒虫
    美机六十二架分批窜扰我国东北领空
    【新华社青岛十四日电】美国侵略者最近又进一步把罪恶的细菌战从东北扩展到我国山东的青岛附近地区。
    三月六日二十一时，美国飞机一架侵入我青岛市郊太平角、浮山所、大麦岛、沙子口一带上空，撒布细菌毒虫。敌机过后，青岛市东郊太平角及沙子口等地居民发现大批突然出现的苍蝇、蜘蛛和小甲盖虫、蚂蚱、土蜂、蚂蚁等毒虫。
    三月七日十一时，美机一架侵入青岛市东北的沧口、李村等地上空，撒布大量苍蝇、蚊子、跳蚤、蜘蛛等毒虫。
    现该市细菌战防御专门委员会及市防疫队已成立，防疫人员已前往发现毒虫地区进行工作，全市人民怀着万分愤怒的心情投入紧张的反细菌战斗争中去。
    【新华社沈阳十四日电】自三月六日至八日，美国侵略者继续无耻派遣军用飞机侵入我国东北地区，撒布细菌毒虫，计：
    三月六日十时六分至下午四时，美机四批、十九架次先后侵入安东、九连城、凤城、水丰、大东沟、长甸河口等地上空，撒布大量苍蝇、蚊子、跳蚤、蜘蛛、硬盖虫、壁虱、白蛉子、虱子、蚂蚁、蚯蚓、黑色小虫等传播细菌的毒虫。
    三月七日，美机一架侵入辑安等地上空撒布细菌。当天，辑安太平沟、庄河、宽甸、锦州、沈阳、新民屯等地都先后发现蚊子、苍蝇、跳蚤、蝴蝶等毒虫。
    三月八日，沈阳、通化、临江、抚松、辑安、新宾、凤城、安东、抚顺等地续发现美机投下的蜘蛛、蜈蚣、蛤蟆、蝗虫、蜜蜂等毒虫。
    【新华社沈阳十四日电】美机六十二架，十二日分十一批侵入我东北领空，窜扰辽东省安东市、安平河、永甸河口、大东沟、大孤山、青堆子、汤山城、太平哨、黄柏甸子、高丽城子、凤城、岫岩、辑安等地。其中一架在黄柏甸子、三道沟投弹六枚，并施行扫射，伤居民三人。
