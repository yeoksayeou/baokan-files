Title: Fourth Agenda Staff Meeting of Korean Negotiations: Both Sides Continue to Exchange Views on the Issue of Prisoner Repatriation
Author:
Date: 1952-03-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, March 27th: At the staff meeting on the fourth agenda item of the Korean armistice negotiations on March 27th, both sides continued to exchange views on the issue of prisoner repatriation. At the staff meeting on the third agenda item, the two sides discussed the wording of the draft armistice agreement.

### 朝鲜谈判第四项议程参谋会上  双方续就遣俘问题交换意见

1952-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜谈判第四项议程参谋会上
    双方续就遣俘问题交换意见
    【新华社开城二十七日电】三月二十七日朝鲜停战谈判第四项议程参谋会议上，双方继续就遣俘问题交换意见。第三项议程参谋会议上，双方就停战协定草案的文字进行了讨论。
