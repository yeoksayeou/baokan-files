Title: Hungarian National Folk Ensemble to Perform in China via the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1952-03-26
Page: 4

　　Hungarian National Folk Ensemble
　　To Perform in China via the Soviet Union
　　【Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 25th】 The Hungarian National Folk Ensemble, invited to perform in China, departed Moscow for Beijing by train on the 23rd. The ensemble, led by Director Feleki, consists of one hundred and thirty-five people.
　　Editor's Note: To meet the needs of readers in various regions, this newspaper will publish an international commentary once a week, starting today.

### 匈牙利国家文工团  经苏联来我国表演

1952-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利国家文工团
    经苏联来我国表演
    【新华社莫斯科二十五日电】应我国邀请到
    我国表演的匈牙利国家文工团在二十三日经莫
    斯科乘火车赴北京。文工团由团长菲列西率领，
    共有一百三十五人。
