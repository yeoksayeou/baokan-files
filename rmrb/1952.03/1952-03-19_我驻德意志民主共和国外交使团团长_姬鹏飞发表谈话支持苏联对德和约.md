Title: Head of the Chinese Diplomatic Mission to the German Democratic Republic, Ji Pengfei, Issues Statement Supporting Soviet Peace Treaty with Germany

Author:
Date: 1952-03-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 17th. Ji Pengfei, head of the Chinese diplomatic mission to the German Democratic Republic, with the rank of ambassador, issued a statement to the editor of the Berlin "Daily Review" on the 14th regarding the letter from the German Democratic Republic to the Four Powers and the Soviet government's note on the peace treaty with Germany.

    Ambassador Ji Pengfei said: The Soviet note is of great significance for restoring German unity and European peace. He said: "The existence of an independent, unified, democratic, and peace-loving Germany is conducive to consolidating peace in Europe and the world. Therefore, the Soviet note proposing the conclusion of a peace treaty with Germany not only conforms to the interests of the German and European people, but also to the interests of the peace-loving people of Asia." He concluded, "The Chinese people fully support the Soviet note advocating the conclusion of a peace treaty with Germany and the German people's desire for peace and unity."

### 我驻德意志民主共和国外交使团团长  姬鹏飞发表谈话支持苏联对德和约

1952-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

    我驻德意志民主共和国外交使团团长
    姬鹏飞发表谈话支持苏联对德和约的照会
    【新华社柏林十七日电】我国驻德意志民主共和国外交使团大使衔团长姬鹏飞在十四日就德意志民主共和国致四大国公函及苏联政府关于对德和约的照会对柏林“每日评论报”编者发表谈话。
    姬鹏飞使团长说：苏联的照会对恢复德国的统一与欧洲的和平具有重大意义。他说：“一个独立、统一、民主与爱好和平的德国的存在有助于巩固欧洲与世界和平。因此，建议缔结对德和约的苏联照会不仅符合德国和欧洲人民的利益，而且也符合亚洲爱好和平的人民的利益。”他最后说：“中国人民完全支持主张缔结对德和约的苏联照会和德国人民对于和平与统一的愿望。”
