Title: All Staff of the Beijing Public Security Bureau Public Security Hospital Prepare to Go to the Forefront of the Anti-Bacterial Warfare

Author:

Date: 1952-03-19
Page: 2

Reader's Letters

    To the Editor: The inhumane bacteriological warfare launched by the American imperialists has aroused our immense indignation. We have the determination and confidence to chop off the bloody claws of the American aggressors. We have organized ourselves, and in addition to strengthening sanitation and epidemic prevention within the organization, we are prepared to respond to the call of the motherland at any time and go to the forefront of the anti-bacterial war!

    All Staff of the Public Security Hospital of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Public Security Bureau
    (Signatures omitted)

### 北京市公安局公安医院全体人员  准备到反细菌战的最前线去

1952-03-19

    北京市公安局公安医院全体人员
