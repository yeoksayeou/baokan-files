    Our Great Motherland
    Improving varieties is an important condition for increasing the yield per unit area of ​​crops. One of the important methods for improving corn varieties is "detasseling and seed selection." It is based on the hybridization of varieties, plus mixed selection and artificial pollination methods. It can increase corn yield by about 10%, improve quality, and reduce pests and diseases. This is a scene of female workers from the North China Agricultural Science Institute collecting tassel pollen with an auxiliary pollinator.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    改良品种是提高农作物单位面积产量的重要条件。改良玉米品种的重要方法之一是“去雄选种”。它是以品种的杂交为基础，加以混合选种及人工辅助授粉的方法。它可以使玉米产量增加百分之十左右，并提高了质量，减少了病虫害。这是华北农业科学研究所的女工正在用辅助授粉器采集雄穗花粉的情形。
