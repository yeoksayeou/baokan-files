Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-03-15
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    After the peaceful liberation of Lhasa, a strategic town on the border of our great motherland, the Tibetan people celebrated enthusiastically. On the day the People's Liberation Army arrived in Lhasa, the Tibetan people paraded in teams. This is a scene of them parading in front of the Potala Palace, holding the national flag high.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我们伟大祖国的边疆重镇——拉萨和平解放之后，藏族人民热烈欢庆。当人民解放军到达拉萨的那一天，藏族人民纷纷结队游行。这是他们高举着国旗在布达拉宫前游行的情形。
