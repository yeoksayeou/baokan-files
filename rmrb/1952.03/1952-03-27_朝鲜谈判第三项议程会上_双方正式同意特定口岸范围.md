Title: At the Korean Negotiations on the Third Agenda Item: Both Sides Formally Agreed on the Scope of Specific Ports
Author:
Date: 1952-03-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, March 26th. At the staff meeting on the third agenda item of the Korean armistice negotiations on the 26th, both sides formally agreed on the scope of specific rear-area ports for each side. At the staff meeting on the fourth agenda item, the two sides continued to exchange views on the issue of prisoner repatriation.

### 朝鲜谈判第三项议程会上  双方正式同意特定口岸范围

1952-03-27
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜谈判第三项议程会上
    双方正式同意特定口岸范围
    【新华社开城二十六日电】二十六日朝鲜停战谈判第三项议程参谋会议上，双方正式同意了各方后方特定口岸的范围。第四项议程参谋会议上，双方继续就遣俘问题交换意见。
