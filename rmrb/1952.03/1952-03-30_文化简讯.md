Cultural Briefs

    ▲ The sixth issue of "Current Events Handbook" has been published. This issue contains a propaganda outline called "Strive for a Complete Victory in the Anti-Corruption Struggle," which explains the Central Committee for Economy and Inspection's regulations on handling corruption, waste, and overcoming bureaucratic errors. There is also a Q&A section that explains the standards and methods for classifying and handling industrial and commercial households in the "Five-Anti" movement.

    This issue contains three articles and materials explaining some issues related to opposing American bacteriological warfare.

    This issue also has a current affairs Q&A, explaining the 1952 Soviet Union's national budget, and comparing the Soviet Union's national budget with the American budget, illustrating the Soviet Union's strength, prosperity, and love of peace.

### 文化简讯

1952-03-30
第3版()
专栏：文化简讯

    文化简讯
    ▲“时事手册”第六期已出版。这一期有一篇宣传提纲叫做“争取反贪污斗争的彻底胜利”，它解释了中央节约检查委员会关于处理贪污、浪费及克服官僚主义错误的若干规定。有一篇问答，解释了“五反”运动中工商户分类处理的标准和办法。
    这一期有三篇文章和资料，解释了反对美国细菌战的一些问题。
    这一期还有一篇时事问答，解说一九五二年苏联的国家预算，并把苏联的国家预算和美国预算作了对比，说明苏联的强大繁荣和爱好和平。
