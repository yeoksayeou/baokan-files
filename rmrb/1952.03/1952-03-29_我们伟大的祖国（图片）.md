Our Great Motherland

Zhang Shuyun, a female worker at the Hengda Cigarette Factory in Tianjin and a Special-Class Labor Model, concentrated the wisdom of the masses and created a complete set of advanced work methods, which increased the factory's production by 9.5%. Zhang Shuyun's work method is now being promoted throughout the country. This is Zhang Shuyun packing cigarettes.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    天津市特等劳动模范、恒大烟厂女工张淑云，集中群众智慧，创造了一套完整的先进工作法，使全厂生产量提高了百分之九·五。现在张淑云的工作法已在全国各地推广起来了。这是张淑云正在包烟的情形。
