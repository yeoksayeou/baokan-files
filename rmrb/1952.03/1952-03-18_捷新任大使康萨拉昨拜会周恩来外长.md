Title: Newly Appointed Czechoslovak Ambassador Kang Sala Pays Courtesy Call on Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-03-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 17th: Newly appointed Czechoslovak Ambassador to China Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary Kang Sala, accompanied by First Secretary Yablos, paid a courtesy call on Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs at 7:00 PM on March 17th to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials. Also present were Xu Yixin, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wei Baoshan, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department.

### 捷新任大使康萨拉昨拜会周恩来外长

1952-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

    捷新任大使康萨拉昨拜会周恩来外长
    【新华社十七日讯】新任捷克斯洛伐克驻华特命全权大使康萨拉偕一等秘书雅布罗斯基于三月十七日下午七时赴外交部拜会周恩来部长，商谈呈递国书事宜。在座者尚有：外交部苏联东欧司副司长徐以新及交际处副处长魏宝善。
