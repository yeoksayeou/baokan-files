Title: Please Accept the Glory Dedicated to You - Congratulating the Victory of Chinese Brothers Donating Weapons
Author: Hong Chunzhe
Date: 1952-03-16
Page: 4

Please Accept the Glory Dedicated to You
-- Congratulating the Victory of Chinese Brothers Donating Weapons
Head of the Korean People's Delegation to China, Hong Chunzhe

	Birds intentionally
	Sing high towards the spring sky,
	Crisp high notes permeate the warm air;
	Grass sprouts begin to stretch,
	Springs sing to their heart's content,
	They are also congratulating this great victory!
	The volcano-like roar of 500 million people,
	Shakes the earth,
	Excites the ocean.
	Thinking of the sky over fighting Korea,
	Propellers turn in my heart;
	Now, I seem to see:
	500 million people place thousands of fighter planes on the airport runway,
	--Facing the enemy.
	I want to cheer this great victory of donation,
	I want to salute the great Chinese people!
	Unquenchable flames are rising,
	Unstoppable pulses are beating,
	To aid us with all their strength, every heart,
	Are countless fervent hearts.
	I clearly see and hear:
	Every person, every street, every village,
	The boundless fields, the lush forests, and the eastward-flowing rivers…
	Are all aiding us.
	Cordial and powerful support,
	Is contained in "Resist America, Aid Korea";
	The shouts of righteous friends,
	Will shake the foundation of the White House.
	The continuous support and encouragement of 500 million people,
	Further enhances our fighting will,
	Further strengthens our confidence in victory.
	Everything I have seen and heard,
	Besides gratitude, I cannot say a second word.
	From the towering chimneys rises the smoke of increased production,
	In the wide and vast fields grow the grains of a bountiful year,
	On the front lines of tireless labor and creation, day and night,
	You never forget Korea;
	Although you are not directly fighting with weapons,
	You are all overcoming the enemy with us.
	Blood, sweat, and the will to fight with steel are condensed together,
	Overcoming all the trials and tribulations,
	Leaving behind a beautiful epic.
	My eternal brothers!
	Dear mothers, sisters!
	I regret that I cannot shake hands with each of the 500 million people,
	I regret that I cannot shake every hand filled with excitement—
	Shake hard, shake hard.
	Please accept--
	The honor our entire nation dedicates to you.
	Please accept--
	The victorious hero's respect and glory from my brothers who have earned the name "Matrosov"
	and my sisters who have earned the name "Danya"!
	Forever shining brightly in the history of billions of years
	Dear comrades!
	We do not want any more
	Humiliating history where even bone fossils cannot be found.
	Let us pull our hands together with force, with more force,
	Bravely strive for victory,
	Let the light of victory forever illuminate the world!
	We will use your fervent support
	To completely eliminate the cruel American beasts
	who gouge out eyes and cut off breasts!
	We will wipe out from the earth the American executioner murderers
	who spread bacteria and are enemies of all mankind!
	I write this poem of gratitude for the victory of the donation with hot tears,
	At the same time,
	I also angrily bite my lip in hatred of the enemy,
	Even if my lip is bitten through.
	(Translated by Bing Wei)

### 请接受献给你们的光荣——祝贺中国弟兄们捐献武器的胜利
洪淳哲
1952-03-16
第4版()
专栏：

    请接受献给你们的光荣
    ——祝贺中国弟兄们捐献武器的胜利
    朝鲜人民访华代表团团长　洪淳哲
    群鸟有意地
    朝着春之苍穹高歌，
    清脆的尖音渗透和暖的空气；
    草芽在开始舒展，
    泉水在尽情吐唱，
    他们也在祝贺这一伟大的胜利啊！
    火山爆发似的五万万人民的呼声，
    震撼着大地，
    激动着海洋。
    想起战斗的朝鲜的天空，
    螺旋桨在心中转动；
    现在，我彷佛看到：
    五万万人民把几千架战斗机放在机场的跑道上，
    ——头朝着敌人。
    我要欢呼这一伟大的捐献的胜利，
    我要向伟大的中国人民致敬！
    扑不灭的火焰在上升，
    止不住的脉膊在跳动，
    尽最大力量援助我们的每颗心，
    是无数火热的心。
    我清楚地看见了、听到了：
    每个人、每条街、每座村庄，
    无垠的田野、苍郁的树林和东流的江水……
    都在援助着我们。
    亲切的有力的支援，
    蕴藏在“抗美援朝”里；
    正义的朋友们的呐喊，
    将震动白宫的基石。
    五万万人民源源不绝的支援与鼓舞，
    更加提高了我们的战斗意志，
    更加坚定了我们的胜利信心。
    我所见到的听到的一切，
    除了感激我再也说不出第二句话。
    高耸入云的烟囱里冒着增产的长烟，
    宽广辽阔的田地里结着丰年的谷粒，
    在不分昼夜的勤劳与创造的火线上，
    你们一刻也不忘记朝鲜；
    虽然没直接拿着武器作战，
    但是多喒都是和我们一起战胜着敌人。
    血汗与钢铁的战斗意志凝结在一起，
    战胜了一切艰难与困苦的考验，
    留下了美丽的史诗。
    我的永远的弟兄们！
    亲爱的母亲们、姊妹们！
    恨我不能和五万万人个个都握手，
    恨我不能和每一个同怀着激昂的手——
    用力地握，用力地握。
    请接受——
    我们全民族献给你们的荣誉。
    请接受——
    荣膺了“马特洛索夫”名字的我的弟兄们
    和荣膺了“丹孃”名字的我的姊妹们的——胜利的英雄的敬意和光荣！
    在亿万年历史上永远光辉灿烂的
    亲爱的战友们！
    我们不愿再有
    连骨化石都找不到的屈辱的历史。
    让我们用力、再用力地拉紧手，
    勇敢地争取胜利，
    让胜利的光芒永远照耀着世界！
    我们要以你们热烈的声援
    把挖人眼、割人乳房的
    残忍的美国野兽消灭干净！
    把撒布细菌、和全人类为敌的
    美国刽子手杀人犯从地球上清洗出去！
    我用热泪写下这一祝贺捐献胜利底感激的诗篇，
    同时，
    也愤怒地咬着嘴唇痛恨着敌人，
    尽管嘴唇要被我咬破了。
    （冰蔚译）
