Title: Introducing Experience in Saving Mimeograph Stencils

Author: Tong Jiansu

Date: 1952-03-26

Page: 6

    Introducing Experience in Saving Mimeograph Stencils

    I work in the writing section of the Tangshan Prefecture Office in Hebei Province. Recently, I discovered a way to save mimeograph stencils. We often encounter situations where we need to type a document, and using half a stencil is not enough, but using a whole stencil is wasteful, as the latter half only contains two or three lines of text, along with the leader's name, and the date. After printing, we can cut off this half stencil, cover the inked portion with paper, then use correction fluid to cover the few sparse characters. We then cover the two or three lines of text on the edge with paper. In this way, this half-used stencil can be reused.

    Secretariat of the Tangshan Prefecture Office, Hebei Province, Tong Jiansu

### 介绍节省打字腊纸的经验
佟见素
1952-03-26
第6版()
专栏：来信摘要

    介绍节省打字腊纸的经验
    我是在河北省唐山专署作文书工作的。最近，我发现了一种节省腊纸的办法。我们常碰到要打一个文件时，若用半张腊纸则不够，若用一张腊纸则又等于多下半张。因为在这后半张纸上只有两、三行字和首长名衔与年、月、日。如在印完以后，把这半张腊纸裁下来，把有油墨的部分用纸贴起来，然后用修正液把那稀稀的几个字涂了，再把边上的两、三行字用纸贴上，这样，这半张用过的腊纸就可以再用了。
    河北省唐山专署秘书室  佟见素
