Title: Danish Chargé d'Affaires ad Interim Holds National Day Reception Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-03-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 11th: The Chargé d'Affaires ad Interim of the Danish Legation, Mr. Meis Torp, held a reception in Beijing on the evening of March 11th to celebrate the National Day of the Kingdom of Denmark.

Huang Yanpei, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government, Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications, Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, Lei Renmin, Deputy Minister of Trade, Yu Xinqing, Director of the Bureau of Ceremonies, and Zhu Zaoguan, Deputy Director of the General Office of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, were invited to attend.

Diplomatic envoys to China from various countries who attended were: Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, Indian Ambassador Panikkar, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Burmese Ambassador U Law Maung, Pakistani Ambassador Raza, Swiss Minister Renzo L. Zolli, Chargé d'Affaires ad Interim of the Indonesian Embassy Isha Mahdi, Chargé d'Affaires ad Interim of the Swedish Embassy Beve, Chargé d'Affaires ad Interim of the Finnish Legation Brondsted, Chargé d'Affaires ad Interim of the Czechoslovakian Embassy Jabroski, and personnel from the Romanian, Polish, and Hungarian Embassies, as well as the negotiating representatives of Norway and the Netherlands.

The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 丹麦公使馆临时代办  昨天举行国庆招待会

1952-03-12
第4版()
专栏：

    丹麦公使馆临时代办
    昨天举行国庆招待会
    【新华社十一日讯】三月十一日是丹麦王国国庆日。丹麦驻华公使馆临时代办迈思妥特于十一日晚在北京举行招待会志庆。
    中央人民政府政务院副总理黄炎培，外交部副部长章汉夫，邮电部部长朱学范，文化部部长沈雁冰，贸易部副部长雷任民，典礼局局长余心清，人民革命军事委员会办公厅副主任朱早观等应邀赴会。
    到会各国驻华外交使节有：苏联大使罗申，印度大使潘尼迦，保加利亚大使彼得科夫，缅甸大使吴拉茂，巴基斯坦大使罗查，瑞士公使任佐立，印度尼西亚大使馆临时代办伊沙·玛蒂，瑞典大使馆临时代办贝凡，芬兰公使馆临时代办勃朗斯坦特，捷克斯洛伐克大使馆临时代办雅布罗斯基和罗马尼亚、波兰、匈牙利各国使馆人员及挪威、荷兰的谈判代表。
    招待会在友好气氛中进行。
