Title: Marshal Stalin Sends Telegram to Premier Kim Il-sung Congratulating on the Third Anniversary of the Signing of the Soviet-Korean Agreement on Economic and Cultural Cooperation

Author: 
Date: 1952-03-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 19th: Chairman Stalin of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union sent a telegram to Premier Kim Il-sung of the Korean Cabinet, congratulating on the third anniversary of the signing of the Soviet-Korean Agreement on Economic and Cultural Cooperation. The telegram reads as follows: To Comrade Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Pyongyang:

    On the occasion of the third anniversary of the signing of the Soviet-Korean Agreement on Economic and Cultural Cooperation, I extend my congratulations to you, Comrade Premier.

    I wish the heroic Korean people success in their struggle for freedom and independence.

    J. Stalin

### 斯大林大元帅致电金日成首相  祝贺苏朝经济文化互助协定签订三周年

1952-03-20
第4版()
专栏：

    斯大林大元帅致电金日成首相
    祝贺苏朝经济文化互助协定签订三周年
    【新华社平壤十九日电】苏联部长会议主席斯大林致电朝鲜内阁首相金日成，祝贺苏朝经济文化互助协定签订三周年。电文如下：平壤朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成同志：
    值此苏朝经济文化互助协定签订三周年，我向首相同志致以祝贺。
    祝英雄的朝鲜人民在争取自己的自由独立的斗争中有所成就。
    约·斯大林
