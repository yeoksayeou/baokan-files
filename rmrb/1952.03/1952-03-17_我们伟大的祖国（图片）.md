Author:
Date: 1952-03-17

    Our Great Motherland
    It is now the tree planting season, and the people in the mountainous areas must seize the opportunity to plant trees on a large scale. These are the male and female students of the Rehe Provincial Agricultural and Forestry College, and they are working hard to plant trees to turn this barren mountain into a green forest.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    现在是植树的季节，山区的人民更要抓紧时期大量植树。这是热河省农林专科学校的男女学生，他们正在努力植树，要把这一片荒山变成绿林。
