Title: Congratulations on World Youth Week: Youth from Germany, Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria, and Other Countries Extend Greetings to Chinese Youth
Author:
Date: 1952-03-28
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: March 21st to 28th is World Youth Week. The Free German Youth League, the Czechoslovak Youth League, the Bulgarian Dimitrov People's Youth League, the Romanian Labor Youth League, and the Albanian Labor Youth League have each sent telegrams to the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth and the China New Democratic Youth League, extending their greetings to Chinese youth.
    In their congratulatory telegrams, they said: Chinese youth are victoriously carrying out the construction of their motherland, and the heroic Chinese People's Volunteers are heavily striking at the American imperialists in Korea. They warmly express their fraternal friendship to the Chinese young peace fighters and pledge to resolutely fight alongside Chinese youth, under the leadership of the glorious Soviet Union's Komsomol – the great example for youth worldwide – to strive for peace until final victory.

### 祝贺世界青年周  德捷保等国青年向我国青年致敬

1952-03-28
第4版()
专栏：

    祝贺世界青年周
    德捷保等国青年向我国青年致敬
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：三月二十一日到二十八日是世界青年周。自由德国青年联盟、捷克青年联盟、保加利亚季米特洛夫人民青年联盟、罗马尼亚劳动青年联盟、阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟，顷分别致电中华全国民主青年联合总会和中国新民主主义青年团，向我国青年致敬。
    他们在贺电中说：中国青年正在胜利地进行祖国的建设工作，中国人民志愿军的英雄们正在朝鲜沉重地打击美帝国主义者。他们热烈地向中国的青年和平战士表示兄弟般的友谊，并保证坚决和中国青年一起，在世界青年伟大榜样——光荣的苏联共产主义青年团的前导下，为争取和平而斗争到最后胜利。
