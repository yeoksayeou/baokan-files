Title: Government Administration Council Holds Enlarged Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-03-12
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 11th. The 127th meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held in an enlarged session on the afternoon of the 8th. After listening to the reports made by Liu Jingfan, Secretary-General of the Central Thrift Examination Committee, and Peng Zhen, Mayor of Beijing (published separately), the meeting approved the Central Thrift Examination Committee's Several Provisions on Handling Corruption, Waste, and Overcoming Bureaucratic Errors, and the Beijing Municipal People's Government's standards and methods for classifying and handling industrial and commercial households in the "Five-Anti" movement. In addition, the meeting approved several appointment and removal cases and submitted several cases for approval by the Central People's Government Committee.

### 政务院举行扩大会议

1952-03-12
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行扩大会议
    【新华社十一日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百二十七次政务会议于八日下午扩大举行。会议在听取了中央节约检查委员会刘景范秘书长和北京市彭真市长所作的报告（另文发表）后，批准了中央节约检查委员会关于处理贪污、浪费及克服官僚主义错误的若干规定和北京市人民政府在“五反”运动中关于工商户分类处理的标准和办法。此外，会议通过了任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
