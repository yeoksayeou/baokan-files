Title: At the invitation of the Finnish Student Union and the Finland-China Friendship Association, our student delegation left Norway for a visit to Finland
Author:
Date: 1952-03-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, 29th: TASS, Helsinki, 27th: The Chinese student delegation has arrived in Helsinki after visiting Britain and Norway. The delegation is here at the invitation of the National Union of Students of Finland and the Finland-China Friendship Association. The members of the delegation are learning about the study and life of Finnish students.

### 应芬兰学联和芬中友协邀请  我学生代表团离挪赴芬访问

1952-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    应芬兰学联和芬中友协邀请
    我学生代表团离挪赴芬访问
    【新华社二十九日讯】塔斯社赫尔辛基二十七日讯：中国学生代表团在访问英国和挪威后已抵达赫尔辛基。代表团是应芬兰全国学生联合会和芬中友好协会的邀请来到这里的。代表团的团员们正在了解芬兰学生的学习与生活情况。
