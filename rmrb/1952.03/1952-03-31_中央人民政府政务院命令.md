Title: Order of the State Council of the Central People's Government

Author: 
Date: 1952-03-31
Page: 1

Order of the State Council of the Central People's Government
    The "Regulations on the Establishment of People's Courts in the Three-Anti Movement" are hereby formulated and promulgated. It is hoped that all localities will abide by and implement them.

    Premier Zhou Enlai
    March 30, 1952
