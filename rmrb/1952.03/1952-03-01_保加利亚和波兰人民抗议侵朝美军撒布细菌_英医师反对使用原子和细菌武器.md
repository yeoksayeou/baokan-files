Title: Bulgarian and Polish People Protest U.S. Military's Spreading of Bacteria in Korea; British Physicians Oppose the Use of Atomic and Bacteriological Weapons
Author:
Date: 1952-03-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 29th: The monstrous crime of the American aggressors using bacteriological weapons in Korea has aroused anger and protests from the people of Bulgaria and Poland. According to TASS: The Bulgarian people express anger at the new crime of the American murderers in using bacteriological weapons in the evil war against the freedom-loving Korean people. The Bulgarian National Peace Defense Committee receives many telegrams every day from working people all over the country demanding that the murderers who use bacteriological weapons be severely punished. The telegram of the shipbuilders of Stalin City says: We firmly protest the crime of the American aggressors in using bacteriological weapons in Korea. The American warmongers should know that the heroic Korean people are fighting to defend their freedom and independence, and their will cannot be broken by anything.

According to the Polish News Agency, the Central Committee of the Polish Red Cross sent a letter to the International Committee of the Red Cross and the League of Red Cross Societies, supporting the statement issued by Park Heon-yeong, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, regarding the American aggressors' use of bacteriological weapons in Korea, and firmly protesting this inhuman method of warfare that violates international law and international rules of war. The Polish Red Cross demands that the International Committee of the Red Cross and the League of Red Cross Societies take strong measures to prevent the recurrence of this crime against the Korean people.

Xinhua News Agency, 29th: TASS London, 25th: The "Daily Worker" stated: The Physicians' Meeting convened by the "Socialist Medical Association" in Reigate passed a resolution requiring the British government to declare its opposition to the use of atomic and bacteriological weapons and to withdraw its support for countries that want to use such weapons. The resolution also called on the government to make every effort to restore "meetings between the Great Powers in order to eliminate all threats of war."

### 保加利亚和波兰人民抗议侵朝美军撒布细菌  英医师反对使用原子和细菌武器

1952-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    保加利亚和波兰人民抗议侵朝美军撒布细菌
    英医师反对使用原子和细菌武器
    【新华社二十九日讯】美国侵略者在朝鲜使用细菌武器的滔天罪行，激起保加利亚和波兰人民的愤怒和抗议。据塔斯社消息：保加利亚人民对于美国杀人凶犯在反对爱好自由的朝鲜人民的罪恶战争中使用细菌武器的新罪行，表示愤怒。保加利亚保卫和平全国委员会每天收到全国各地劳动人民发来的许多电报，要求严惩使用细菌武器的凶犯。斯大林城造船工人的电报说：我们坚决地抗议美国侵略者在朝鲜使用细菌武器的罪行。美国战争贩子们应该知道：英勇的朝鲜人民是为了保卫自己的自由和独立而战斗，他们的意志是任何东西都打不破的。
    据波兰通讯社报道，波兰红十字会中央委员会致函红十字国际委员会和日内瓦红十字会协会，支持朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务相朴宪永所发表的关于美国侵略者在朝鲜使用细菌武器的声明，坚决抗议美国这种违背国际公法和国际作战规则的惨无人道的作战方法。波兰红十字会要求红十字国际委员会和红十字会协会采取有力的措施，防止这种对朝鲜人民的罪行的重演。
    【新华社二十九日讯】塔斯社伦敦二十五日讯：“工人日报”称：“社会主义者医师联合会”在里子召集的医师会议通过决议，要求英国政府宣布反对使用原子和细菌武器，撤销对那些要使用这种武器的国家的支持。决议并要求政府尽一切力量恢复“大国间的会议，以便消除一切战争威胁”。
