Title: Order of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council
Author: Chen Yun
Date: 1952-03-15
Page: 2

    Order of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council
    (52) Finance-Secret-Plan (Finance) No. 51
    In view of the basically stable price situation, in order to ensure state tax revenue, regarding Article 6, Paragraph 2 of the Provisional Regulations on Goods Tax: for goods taxed according to market wholesale prices, the provision that tax prices and tax amounts are assessed monthly and adjusted at any time when prices rise or fall by more than 10%; it has now been submitted to the Government Administration Council for approval to modify the adjustment range to 5%, effective from March 15, 1952.
    This order
    Director Chen Yun
    March 4

### 中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会命令
陈云
1952-03-15
第2版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会命令
    （52）财经密计（财）字第五十一号
    根据目前物价基本上已趋稳定的情况，为保证国家税收，关于货物税暂行条例第六条第二款内：按市场批价核税的货物，每月评定税价税额后，遇物价涨落超过百分之十随时调整的规定；现经呈奉政务院批准，将其调整幅度修改为百分之五，自一九五二年三月十五日起实行。
    此令主任　陈云
    三月四日
