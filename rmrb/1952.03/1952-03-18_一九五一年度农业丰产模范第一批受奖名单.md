    First Batch of Award Recipients for 1951 Agricultural Abundant Harvest Models

    I. All-Around Abundant Harvest Model Mutual Aid Teams and All-Around Abundant Harvest Model Agricultural Production Cooperatives

    Li Shunda Mutual Aid Team, Xigou Village, Pingshun County, Shanxi Province. The team consists of 23 households, cultivating 224 mu of land, with an average grain yield of 382 jin per mu, an increase of 109.9% compared to pre-war levels.

    Han En Mutual Aid Team, Bao'an Village, Jiaohe County, Jilin Province. The team consists of 24 households, cultivating 761.4 mu of land, with an average grain yield of 315 jin per mu, an increase of 103.3% compared to pre-war levels.

    Geng Changsuo Agricultural Production Cooperative, Wugong Village, Raoyang County, Hebei Province. The cooperative consists of 17 households, cultivating 213.2 mu of land, with an average grain yield of 510 jin per mu, an increase of 131.8% compared to pre-war levels.

    Jin Shilong Agricultural Production Cooperative, Yingcheng Village, Yanji County, Jilin Province. The cooperative consists of 22 households, cultivating 655.7 mu of land, with an average grain yield of 417 jin per mu, an increase of 65% compared to 1950.

    Wangmang Village Mutual Aid Joint Group, Chang'an County, Shaanxi Province. The village consists of 22 groups, 143 households, cultivating 1406.9 mu of land, with an average grain yield of 474 jin per mu, an increase of 63.9% compared to 1950.

    (Each of the above groups, cooperatives, and joint groups will be awarded a Patriotic Abundant Harvest Certificate and a bonus of 5 million yuan; each group leader and cooperative head will also be awarded a Patriotic Abundant Harvest Medal.)

    山西运城专区农场。八○四亩旱硷地棉田，每亩平均产籽棉二九五点八六斤；另一点○八亩丰产水地，每亩产籽棉九二八斤。
    湖南省常德稻场。二百亩稻田，每亩平均产稻七○九斤，超过当地产量一倍；其中有四点一三亩丰产田，每亩产稻一、○五○斤。
    山西阳曲县农场。十四亩玉米地，每亩平均产玉米八六二点三斤；其中三亩丰产地，每亩产玉米一、二三○斤。其余谷子地十五亩，每亩产谷子七六五斤。高粱地八亩，每亩产高粱八二五斤。均高出当地产量一倍以上。
    山东博兴县农场。三一点八五亩旱地棉花，每亩产籽棉三二八斤；其中有四点○九亩丰产地，每亩产籽棉五○四点四斤，高出当地产量六倍以上。
    山东莱芜县农场。二亩丰产水浇地，每亩产玉米一、○二八斤，其余地亩所种玉米，每亩平均产四七○斤，较当地产量高出二百斤。
    （以上五场各奖给爱国丰产锦旗一面。）
