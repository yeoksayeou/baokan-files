Title: People's Organizations and Progressive Public Opinion Around the World Unanimously Protest Against American Aggressors' Germ Warfare
Author:
Date: 1952-03-11
Page: 4

People's organizations and progressive public opinion around the world
    Unanimously protest against American aggressors' germ warfare
    Soviet "Komsomolskaya Pravda" publishes commentary
    Denouncing the criminal actions of American aggressors
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 9th] According to TASS, Moscow, March 9th: "Komsomolskaya Pravda," in its international commentary, commented on the crimes of American aggressors conducting germ warfare in Korea, saying: The American interventionist army has suffered military defeats on the Korean front and has resorted to the most despicable means. They have used bacteriological weapons, which has once again exposed their barbarity, their shamelessness, and their disregard for the fundamental principles of international morality. American aggressors have embarked on the same evil path that led to the defeat of Japanese imperialists.
    The commentary said: The crime of the American command using bacteriological weapons has aroused great indignation among all people with a sense of justice around the world. People of all countries are angrily denouncing the masterminds and executors of these crimes. The Soviet people, like the people of other countries around the world, express their great indignation at the criminal actions of the American military authorities in Korea.
    People in various parts of Korea hold protest rallies
    Demanding severe punishment for American germ warfare criminals
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 10th] People in various parts of Korea have held rallies to angrily protest the US government's crime of conducting germ warfare. At a protest rally held by workers at a factory in North Pyongan Province, epidemic prevention personnel from the provincial people's committee reported on the investigation into the enemy's dissemination of germ-carrying insects. The workers expressed unparalleled anger at the heinous crimes of the American aggressors and unanimously passed a protest resolution, resolutely demanding severe punishment for the germ warfare criminals.
    Farmers in Munchon County, Kangwon Province, Sinchon County, Hwanghae Province, and Sinuiju City demanded at protest rallies that peace-loving people all over the world rise up to stop the US government's most barbaric and brutal acts of slaughter. They stressed: No atrocities by the enemy can conquer the Korean people. Korean farmers must continue to wage a relentless struggle against the brutal enemy.
    All health workers in Hamhung City, South Hamgyong Province, also held a protest rally on the 6th to condemn the US government's crime of openly trampling on international law and human morality by conducting germ warfare. They called on health workers throughout Korea to do their utmost to prevent epidemics and sterilize, and together with the heroic people of China and Korea, smash the US imperialists' brutal ambition to murder peaceful people in Korea on a large scale.
    Albanian people believe that conducting germ warfare
    Is a hateful act of robbery by American aggressors
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, March 10th] TASS, Tirana, March 10th: The Albanian people were extremely indignant upon learning that American aggressors had expanded germ warfare to the territory of the People's Republic of China.
    The Albanian people unanimously demand an end to the criminal acts of American murderers. Newspapers continue to publish protests from working people against the use of bacteriological weapons by US imperialists. Staff of the Academy of Sciences wrote in the "People's Voice" newspaper: "The use of bacteriological weapons against the heroic Korean people and the Chinese People's Volunteers is a new and hateful act of robbery, the culmination of the hysteria of American aggressors. American aggressors must be held responsible for the consequences of their crimes."
    Italian scientist calls on the entire civilized world
    To protest against America's use of the most despicable bacteriological weapons
    [Xinhua News Agency, Rome] According to TASS, Rome: Italian parliamentarian and renowned scientist Professor Marchetti published an article in "Unità" on March 9th, protesting against the crimes of American imperialists in conducting germ warfare in Korea and China. Marchetti wrote: Bacteria are not weapons. This is the most vile and shameful tool invented in American laboratories, used by American imperialists to carry out mass slaughter. Marchetti called on the entire civilized world to protest the heinous crimes committed by American imperialists.

### 世界各国人民团体和进步舆论  一致抗议美国侵略者进行细菌战

1952-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    世界各国人民团体和进步舆论
    一致抗议美国侵略者进行细菌战
    苏联“共青真理报”发表评论  斥责美国侵略者的犯罪行动
    【新华社十日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科九日讯：“共青真理报”在国际述评中评论美国侵略者在朝鲜进行细菌战的罪行说：美国干涉军在朝鲜前线遭受军事失败，就采取了最卑鄙的手段。他们使用了细菌武器，这就再度暴露了他们的野蛮，就再度暴露了他们毫不知耻，对国际道德的基本原则毫不在意。美国侵略者已经走上了使日本帝国主义者遭到失败的同一罪恶道路。
    评论说：美国司令部使用细菌武器的罪行，已经引起全世界一切有正义感的人士的极大愤慨。各国人民都愤怒地斥责这些罪行的主谋者和执行者。苏联人民像全世界其他国家人民一样，对美国军事当局在朝鲜的犯罪行动，表示莫大的愤慨。
    朝鲜各地人民举行抗议集会  要求严厉制裁美国细菌战犯
    【新华社平壤十日电】朝鲜各地人民纷纷集会，愤怒地抗议美国政府进行细菌战的罪行。在平安北道某工厂工人举行的抗议大会上，道人民委员会的防疫工作人员报告了敌人撒布细菌毒虫的调查情形。工人们对美国侵略者的万恶暴行表示无比的愤怒，一致通过抗议书，坚决要求给细菌战犯以严厉的制裁。
    江原道文川郡、黄海道信川郡以及新义州市等地的农民，在抗议大会上都要求全世界爱好和平的人民奋起制止美国政府这种最野蛮、最残暴的屠杀行为。他们调强指出：敌人的任何暴行都不能征服朝鲜人民。朝鲜农民一定要继续和凶暴的敌人展开无情的斗争。
    咸镜南道咸兴市全体卫生工作人员在六日也举行了抗议大会，控诉美国政府公然蹂躏国际公法和人类道德进行细菌战的罪行。他们号召全朝鲜的卫生工作者全力进行防疫灭菌工作，和朝中两国英雄的人民一起，粉碎美帝国主义阴谋大规模杀害朝鲜和平人民的残暴野心。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民认为进行细菌战  是美国侵略者可恶的强盗行为
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社地拉那十日讯：阿尔巴尼亚人民得悉美国侵略者把细菌战争扩大到中华人民共和国领土上的消息，极为愤慨。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民一致要求制止美国杀人犯的这种罪恶行动。报纸继续登载劳动人民对美帝国主义者使用细菌武器的抗议。科学院的工作人员在“人民之声”报上写道：“对英勇的朝鲜人民和中国人民志愿军使用细菌武器，是新的可恶的强盗行为，是美国侵略者歇斯底里的最高峰。美国侵略者必须对他们的罪行的后果负责。”
    意科学家号召整个文明世界  抗议美国使用最可耻的细菌武器
    【新华社十日讯】据塔斯社罗马讯：意大利议员、著名科学家马凯西亚教授于三月九日在“团结报”上发表文章，抗议美帝国主义者在朝鲜及中国进行细菌战的罪行。马凯西亚写道：细菌并不是武器。这是美国实验室中发明的最卑鄙和最可耻的工具，给美帝国主义者用来进行大规模的屠杀。马凯西亚号召整个文明世界抗议美帝国主义者所犯的滔天罪行。
