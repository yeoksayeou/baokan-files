Title: Chinese Delegation Arrives in Moscow to Attend International Economic Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-03-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 27th. The Chinese delegation to the International Economic Conference, led by delegation leader Nan Hanchen, arrived in Moscow on the 27th. Those who greeted the delegation at the station included Nesterov, Chairman of the All-Soviet Chamber of Commerce; Berezin, Director of the International Department of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions; Chamberland, Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee of the International Economic Conference; Zhang Wentian, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union; and Sha Qianli, leader of the Chinese Commercial Delegation.

### 参加国际经济会议  我国代表团抵莫斯科

1952-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

    参加国际经济会议
    我国代表团抵莫斯科
    【新华社莫斯科二十七日电】我国参加国际经济会议的代表团由南汉宸团长率领在二十七日到达莫斯科。到车站欢迎的有全苏商会主席奈斯特洛夫、全苏工会中央理事会国际部部长别列金、国际经济会议筹备委员会秘书长香贝朗、我驻苏大使张闻天、我国商务代表团团长沙千里等。
