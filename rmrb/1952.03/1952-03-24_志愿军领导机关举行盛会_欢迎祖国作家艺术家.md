Title: Volunteer Army Leadership Holds Grand Gathering to Welcome Homeland Writers and Artists
Author:
Date: 1952-03-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, March 23rd. The leadership of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army held a grand gathering to welcome the seventeen writers and artists from the homeland, led by the famous writer Ba Jin, who arrived on the Korean front on the 20th. At the meeting, the representative of the leadership of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army delivered a welcoming speech. He said: The most outstanding writers and artists dispatched by the people of the motherland have come to the Korean front despite hardships to collect stories of the Volunteer Army's heroic battles and portray the heroic image of the Volunteer Army. This will be a great encouragement to all commanders and fighters of the Volunteer Army and a powerful promotion and improvement of the Volunteer Army's cultural work. The famous writer Ba Jin, on behalf of all the writers and artists who came to Korea, paid tribute to the commanders and fighters of the Volunteer Army. He said: The people of the whole country regard the Volunteer Army as an example for them to learn from. Because of you, the people of the motherland have had a happy life for the past two years. We have come to the Korean front to learn from you and to report your great struggle to the people of the whole country. The writers Ge Luo and the artist Gu Yuan successively stated at the meeting that they would go deep into the troops to experience life and transform their own thinking in order to complete the creative tasks given to them by the people of the motherland. After listening to relevant reports from the leadership of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, the writers and artists who came to Korea will go to various units to work.

### 志愿军领导机关举行盛会  欢迎祖国作家艺术家

1952-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军领导机关举行盛会
    欢迎祖国作家艺术家
    【新华社朝鲜前线二十三日电】中国人民志愿军领导机关举行盛会，欢迎由著名作家巴金率领在二十日抵达朝鲜前线的祖国十七位文艺家。会上，中国人民志愿军领导机关代表致欢迎词。他说：祖国人民派遣的最优秀的作家、艺术家不辞艰苦地到朝鲜前线来收集志愿军英勇作战的事迹，刻划志愿军英雄形象，这对志愿军全体指挥员、战斗员将是一个极大的鼓舞，对志愿军部队的文化工作将是一个有力的推动和提高。著名作家巴金代表全体来朝作家、艺术家向志愿军指挥员、战斗员致敬。他说：全国人民都以志愿军作为自己学习的榜样。有了你们，祖国人民才有了两年来的幸福生活。我们来朝鲜前线就是要向你们学习，要把你们伟大的斗争报告给全国人民。作家葛洛、美术家古元在会上相继表示要深入部队体验生活，改造自己的思想，来完成祖国人民所给的创作任务。来朝作家、艺术家将在中国人民志愿军领导机关听取有关报告后，分赴各部队工作。
