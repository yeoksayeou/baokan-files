Title: The Ministry of Railways Decides to Adjust Freight Rates on Northeastern Railways Starting Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1952-03-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 30th: The Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government has decided to adjust freight rates for all classes of goods on the Northeastern Railways, effective April 1st. This has been approved by the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government. The current freight rates on the Northeastern Railways differ greatly from those on the Northern and Southern Railways. After this adjustment, the difference in freight rates between the Northeastern Railways and the Northern and Southern Railways will be reduced, but will still be lower than those on the Northern and Southern Railways.

### 铁道部决定从明天起  调整东北铁路货运运价

1952-03-31
第1版()
专栏：

    铁道部决定从明天起
    调整东北铁路货运运价
    【新华社三十日讯】中央人民政府铁道部决定自四月一日起调整东北铁路各等货运运价，中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会已经批准。东北铁路现行货运运价和北、南方铁路比较相差甚大。这次调整之后，东北铁路和北、南方铁路货运运价差额已经缩小，但仍低于北、南方铁路。
