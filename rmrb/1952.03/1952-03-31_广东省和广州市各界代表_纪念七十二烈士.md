Title: Representatives from Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City Commemorate the Seventy-Two Martyrs
Author:
Date: 1952-03-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, March 30th. On the 29th, the anniversary of the martyrdom of the Seventy-Two Martyrs of Huanghuagang, more than two hundred representatives from various agencies, people's organizations, and democratic parties in Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City went to the Huanghuagang Martyrs Cemetery at 9:00 AM to hold a public memorial ceremony and present wreaths to pay their respects.

### 广东省和广州市各界代表  纪念七十二烈士

1952-03-31
第1版()
专栏：

    广东省和广州市各界代表
    纪念七十二烈士
    【新华社广州三十日电】二十九日是黄花岗七十二烈士殉难纪念日。广东省和广州市各机关、各人民团体及各民主党派的代表二百多人曾在上午九时前往黄花岗烈士陵园举行公祭，并献花圈致敬。
