Order of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government

    The "Regulations on the Establishment of People's Tribunals in the Five-Anti Movement" are hereby formulated and promulgated, and it is hoped that all localities will abide by them.

    Premier Zhou Enlai
    March 24, 1952
