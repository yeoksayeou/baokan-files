Title: Medical Personnel of the Harbin Hui People's Health Center Resolve to Stop the Heinous Crime of Bacteriological Warfare by U.S. Imperialism
Author:
Date: 1952-03-20
Page: 2

    Reader's Letters:

    Medical Personnel of the Harbin Hui People's Health Center
    Resolve to Stop the Heinous Crime of Bacteriological Warfare by U.S. Imperialism
    We are the health workers of the Harbin Hui People's Health Center. We resolutely protest the crime of U.S. imperialism in spreading bacteria in Korea and Northeast China.
    Our epidemic prevention workers in Northeast China have rich experience in epidemic prevention. We have the confidence and the strength to crush the conspiracy of U.S. imperialism to wage bacteriological warfare.
    Now, all of our medical personnel have taken action, and we are determined to fight to the end to stop the heinous crime of U.S. imperialism in spreading bacteria. All medical personnel of the Harbin Hui People's Health Center

### 哈尔滨回民保健所医务人员  决心制止美帝国主义撒布细菌的滔天罪行

1952-03-20
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    哈尔滨回民保健所医务人员
    决心制止美帝国主义撒布细菌的滔天罪行
    我们是哈尔滨市回民保健所卫生工作人员。我们坚决抗议美帝国主义在朝鲜和我国东北地区撒布细菌的罪行。
    我们东北的防疫工作者，是有丰富的防疫经验的。我们有信心，也有力量，粉碎美帝国主义进行细菌战的阴谋。
    现在，我们全体医务人员已经行动起来，我们决心为制止美帝国主义撒布细菌的滔天罪行而斗争到底。哈尔滨市回民保健所全体医务人员
