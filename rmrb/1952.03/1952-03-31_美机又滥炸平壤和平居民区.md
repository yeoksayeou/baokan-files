Title: American Planes Again Indiscriminately Bomb Peaceful Residential Areas of Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1952-03-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 30th:
American planes again indiscriminately bomb peaceful residential areas of Pyongyang.
According to TASS, Pyongyang, March 29th: American jet fighter-bombers strafed and bombed southwestern districts of Pyongyang on the afternoon of March 28th. According to confirmed materials, American Flying Fortresses dropped many bombs in the area on the night of March 26th. Due to the recent cowardly air raids by American aerial bandits, some Pyongyang citizens were killed or injured, and some houses were destroyed.

### 美机又滥炸平壤和平居民区

1952-03-31
第4版()
专栏：

    美机又滥炸平壤和平居民区
    【新华社三十日讯】美国侵略者在继续使用细菌武器的同时，又连续滥炸平壤和平居民区。据塔斯社平壤二十九日讯：美国喷气式战斗轰炸机于三月二十八日下午扫射并轰炸平壤西南各区。据已经证实的材料，美国飞行堡垒于三月二十六日夜曾在该地区投掷许多炸弹。由于美国空中强盗最近的卑怯的空袭，平壤有若干市民死伤，并有一些房屋被炸毁。
