Title: Speeches by Representatives of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers Investigation Team at the Welcome Rally Held by People from All Walks of Life in Beijing

Author:
Date: 1952-03-30
Page: 4

Editor's Note: On the evening of March 22nd, legal professionals and representatives from all walks of life in Beijing gathered to welcome the investigation team sent by the International Association of Democratic Lawyers to Korea to investigate the atrocities of the American aggressors. At the meeting, the head of the investigation team, Brandweiner (Austria), the deputy head, Cavallari (Italy), and team members De Brito (Brazil), Gastel (UK), Jascue (France), Mrs. Morens (Belgium), and Mrs. Wasilkowska (Poland) all spoke. Their speeches proved to the people of the world the various crimes committed by the American aggressors, including the use of bacteriological weapons, poison gas, and other violations of human justice and international law. The following are excerpts from their speeches. The titles are added by the editor.
