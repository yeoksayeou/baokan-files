Title: Amado and Guillén Left Beijing for the Soviet Union Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-03-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. “Strengthening International Peace” Stalin International Prize winner, renowned Brazilian writer Amado and his wife, World Peace Council Cultural Exchange Committee member, Cuban poet Guillén and his wife, after visiting Beijing, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other places in our country for a month, left Beijing by plane for the Soviet Union on the morning of the 29th. Those who went to the airport to see them off included Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, Secretary-General Liu Guanyi, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department Meng Yu, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Mao Dun, Deputy Director of the International Work Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Liao Mengxing, and Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Wu Maosun, and others. Dr. Hedlicka, Cultural Attaché of the Czechoslovakian Embassy in China, also went to the airport to see them off.

Also reported: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, and Vice Chairman Chen Shutong hosted a banquet on the afternoon of the 28th to bid farewell to Amado and Guillén, and to welcome Mr. James Gareth Endicott, Chairman of the Canadian Peace Congress, who recently visited our country.

### 亚马多和吉里安昨天离北京赴苏联

1952-03-01
第1版()
专栏：

    亚马多和吉里安昨天离北京赴苏联
    【新华社二十九日讯】“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金得奖人、巴西著名作家亚马多及其夫人，世界和平理事会文化交流委员会委员、古巴诗人吉里安及其夫人在我国北京、上海、杭州等地参观访问一个月后，已于二十九日晨乘飞机离京赴苏。到机场欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会主席郭沫若、秘书长刘贯一、联络部副部长孟雨，中华全国文学艺术界联合会副主席茅盾、中华全国民主妇女联合会国际工作部副部长廖梦醒、中国人民外交学会秘书长吴茂荪等人。捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆文化专员赫德利奇卡博士亦到机场欢送。
    又讯：中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会主席郭沫若、副主席陈叔通曾于二十八日下午设宴欢送亚马多和吉里安，并欢迎最近来我国访问的加拿大和平大会主席文幼章。
