    Our Great Motherland
    Under the slogan of serving the country's construction with science and technology, the creativity and enthusiasm of our country's science and technology workers are increasing day by day. The "vacuum tube" self-made by the Northeast Institute of Science has been successful and the trial effect is very good. This is a staff member of the Northeast Institute of Science, who is checking the effectiveness of the self-made "vacuum tube".

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在科学技术为祖国建设服务的口号下，我国科学技术工作者的创造性和积极性日益提高。东北科学研究所自制的“真空管”已经获得成功，试用的效果很好。这是东北科学研究所的一位工作人员，他正在检查自制“真空管”的效能。
