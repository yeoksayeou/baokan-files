Title: Southwest Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1952-03-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 17th: The Southwest Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation, consisting of 178 people, departed from Chongqing on February 27th and arrived in Beijing on the morning of the 17th. Greeting them at the station were Liu Geping, Vice Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, Zhu Zaoguan, Deputy Director of the General Office of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission and member of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, and many cadres from the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government.

    The visiting delegation is composed of members of the Tibetan, Yi, Hui, Miao, Dai (Bu), Mosuo, Dong, Lisu, Shantou, Zhuang, Gelao, Zhongjia, Minjia, Zhu ?, Qiu, Nong, Sha, Akha, and Yao ethnic groups. The purpose of their visit to Beijing is to pay their respects to Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government, and to visit the various constructions of the capital. The delegation visited Wuhan for more than ten days on its way, and was warmly welcomed by the local People's Government and people from all walks of life.

### 西南各民族参观团抵京

1952-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

    西南各民族参观团抵京
    【新华社十七日讯】西南各民族参观团一行一百七十八人在上月二十七日由重庆起程，已在十七日上午到达北京。前往车站欢迎的有中央人民政府民族事务委员会副主任委员刘格平、人民革命军事委员会办公厅副主任兼中央人民政府民族事务委员会委员朱早观及中央人民政府民族事务委员会干部多人。
    参观团由藏、彝、回、苗、傣（僰）、摩些、侗、傈僳、山头、僮、仡佬、仲家、民家、侏?、俅、侬、沙、阿佧、瑶等族人员组成。他们这次来京的目的是向毛主席和中央人民政府致敬，并参观首都的各项建设。参观团途经武汉时，曾在当地参观十多日，受到当地人民政府及各界人民的热烈欢迎。
