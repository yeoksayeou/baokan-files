Title: Supreme Soviet of the USSR Approves 1952 Budget
Author:
Date: 1952-03-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 11th: The Soviet of the Union and the Soviet of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR held their final sessions on the 8th. After concluding discussions on the draft of the USSR's 1952 national budget, the meetings of the two chambers adopted the revised budget. The total revenue of this revised national budget is 509,911,160,008 rubles, and the total expenditure is 476,920,588,000 rubles.

The meeting of the Soviet of the Union was held on the afternoon of the 8th. Representatives from Lithuania, Kyrgyzstan, Uzbekistan, the Karelo-Finnish Republic, the Mordovian Autonomous Republic, and the Chelyabinsk Oblast spoke during the discussion of the budget draft. They unanimously advocated the adoption of the budget draft as revised by the budget committee, and emphasized that this budget vividly reflects the great achievements of the Soviet Union and the great tasks of the Soviet people in building communism.

The meeting of the Soviet of Nationalities was held on the evening of the 8th, continuing the discussion of the 1952 national budget draft. Comrade Kuznetsov, Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, cited many facts in his speech to illustrate the continuous improvement of the living standards and welfare of the Soviet working people. Soviet Minister of Agriculture, Venediktov, discussed the great achievements of Soviet agriculture last year in his report. Many other representatives also spoke at the meeting.

Soviet Minister of Finance Zverev made concluding remarks at the meetings of both chambers respectively. He pointed out that the Council of Ministers of the USSR had studied the amendments proposed by the budget committees of the two chambers and agreed to increase budget revenue by 1,009,220,000 rubles. Zverev said: As the representatives pointed out, the 1952 budget of the Soviet Union guarantees the financial resources necessary for developing the Soviet national economy and improving the material and cultural standards of the people. This budget is in the interests of all the Soviet people. The Soviet people firmly believe that under the leadership of Comrade Stalin, they will achieve new successes in safeguarding world peace and building a communist society.

After passing the budget draft, the meetings of the two chambers also passed the law on the 1952 national budget of the USSR and the decrees issued by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR between the previous and current sessions of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR. Thereupon, the meetings of the two chambers were declared closed.

### 苏联最高苏维埃批准一九五二年预算

1952-03-13
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃批准一九五二年预算
    【新华社十一日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院在八日分别举行了末次会议。两院会议在结束了苏联一九五二年国家预算案的讨论后，已通过了预算的修正案。这个修正后的国家预算，计收入总额五千零九十九亿一千一百六十万零八千卢布，支出总额四千七百六十九亿二千零五十八万八千卢布。
    联盟院的会议在八日下午举行。在讨论预算案时发言的有：立陶宛、吉尔吉斯、乌兹别克、卡累利阿—芬兰各共和国、摩得维尼亚自治共和国，以及车里雅宾斯克州等地的代表。他们一致主张通过预算委员会所修正的预算案，并强调指出：这个预算生动地反映出苏联的伟大成就，和苏联人民在建设共产主义方面的伟大任务。
    民族院会议在八日晚间举行，继续讨论一九五二年国家预算案。全苏工会中央理事会主席、代表库兹涅佐夫在发言中列举了许多事实，说明苏联劳动人民生活福利不断增进的情况。苏联农业部长维尼迪克托夫在报告中论述了去年苏联农业的伟大成就。在会上发言的还有许多代表。
    苏联财政部长兹维列夫分别在两院的会议上作了总结发言。他指出：苏联部长会议已经研究了两院预算委员会所提出的修正案，同意把预算收入增加十亿零九千二百二十万卢布。兹维列夫说：正如代表们所指出的，苏联一九五二年的预算保证了发展苏联国民经济和提高人民物质与文化水平所必需的财源。这个预算是符合全苏联人民的利益的。苏联人民坚信：他们在斯大林同志的领导下，必将在维护世界和平和建设共产主义社会的工作中获得新的成就。
    两院会议在通过预算案之后，并通过了关于苏联一九五二年国家预算的法律，和苏联最高苏维埃主席团在苏联最高苏维埃上届会议和本届会议之间所颁布的法令。至此，两院会议即宣告闭幕。
