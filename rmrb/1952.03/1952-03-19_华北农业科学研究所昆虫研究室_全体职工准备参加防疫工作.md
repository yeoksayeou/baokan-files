Title: All Staff of the Insect Research Department of the North China Agricultural Science Institute Prepare to Participate in Epidemic Prevention Work
Author:
Date: 1952-03-19
Page: 2

Reader's Letters

    All Staff of the Insect Research Department of the North China Agricultural Science Institute Prepare to Participate in Epidemic Prevention Work
    To the Editor: We were originally workers engaged in eliminating agricultural pests and protecting various crops. We are prepared to obey the call of the motherland at any time and participate in epidemic prevention work in Korea or the Northeast. We eagerly hope for the opportunity to contribute our strength.
    All Staff of the Insect Research Department of the North China Agricultural Science Institute

### 华北农业科学研究所昆虫研究室  全体职工准备参加防疫工作

1952-03-19
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    华北农业科学研究所昆虫研究室
    全体职工准备参加防疫工作
    编辑同志：我们原是扑灭农业害虫、保护各种作物的工作者。我们准备随时遵从祖国的召唤，参加朝鲜或东北的防疫工作。我们热烈地希望着有贡献自己力量的机会。
    华北农业科学研究所昆虫研究室全体职工
