Title: People's Air Force Female Soldiers Invited to Beijing to Participate in "March 8th" Celebrations; All-China Women's Federation to Hold "March 8th" Inaugural Flight Ceremony for First Group of Female Aviation Personnel
Author:
Date: 1952-03-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 6th: Female soldiers of the People's Air Force, including flight, navigation, mechanical, communications and other air and ground personnel, have flown to the capital, Beijing, on March 3rd at the invitation of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, to participate in the capital's "March 8th" International Women's Day celebrations.

This is the first group of female aviation personnel to appear in People's China. They have opened up a path for women in New China to master aviation technology.

The All-China Democratic Women's Federation has decided to hold a "March 8th" inaugural flight ceremony in the capital on March 8th to celebrate the first group of female aviation personnel. Female pilots will then fly over Tiananmen Square and perform flight demonstrations.

After March 8th, the female Air Force soldiers will visit factories and schools in the capital for tours and propaganda activities.

### 人民空军女战士应邀来京参加“三八”节  全国妇联将举行庆祝首批女航空人员“三八”起飞典礼

1952-03-07
第1版()
专栏：

    人民空军女战士应邀来京参加“三八”节
    全国妇联将举行庆祝首批女航空人员“三八”起飞典礼
    【新华社六日讯】人民空军的女战士，包括飞行、领航、机械、通讯等空地勤人员，应中华全国民主妇女联合会的邀请，已在三月三日飞到首都北京，准备参加首都的“三八”国际妇女节。
    这是人民中国出现的第一批女航空人员，她们为新中国妇女掌握航空技术开辟了一条道路。
    中华全国民主妇女联合会已决定三月八日在首都举行庆祝第一批女航空人员“三八”起飞典礼。女飞行员们届时将驾驶飞机通过天安门上空，作飞行表演。
    三月八日后，空军女战士们将到首都各工厂、学校去参观，并进行宣传。
