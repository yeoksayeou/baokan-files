Title: "Investigation Team on the Crimes of Bacteriological Warfare by U.S. Imperialism" Arrives in Shenyang, Begins Inspecting Evidence of Bacteriological Warfare Crimes by American Aggressors, Northeast People Warmly Welcome the Investigation Team and Will Fully Assist the Investigation
Author:
Date: 1952-03-17
Page: 1
 
    Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 16th. The "Investigation Team on the Crimes of Bacteriological Warfare by U.S. Imperialism," led by Director Li Dequan and Deputy Directors Liao Chengzhi and Chen Qiyuan, arrived in Shenyang at 2:30 p.m. on the 16th.  Those who went to the station to welcome them included: Lin Feng, Vice Chairman of the Northeast People's Government; Gao Chongmin, Chairman of the Northeast General Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Opposition to American Aggression; He Jinnian, Deputy Commander of the Northeast Military Region of the People's Liberation Army; Ma Hong, Deputy Secretary-General of the Northeast Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Ning Wu, Director of the Northeast Provisional Working Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang; Li Youwen, Secretary-General of the Northeast People's Government; Che Xiangchen, Minister of Education; Liu Zhiming, Minister of Culture; Wang Bin, Minister of Health; Mo Wenhua, Director of the Political Department of the Northeast Military Region of the People's Liberation Army; Li Ziping, Minister of Health; Yan Jici, Acting President of the Northeast Branch of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Jiao Ruoyu, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, and more than 30 others. Xu Minghe, Third Secretary of the Korean Embassy in China in Shenyang, also went to welcome them. After all members of the investigation team got off the train, they held a forum with the welcoming personnel at the Northeast People's Government Reception Office. Li Dequan, the head of the investigation team, made a short speech at the meeting, saying: "We have come this time to conduct on-the-spot investigations into the crimes of U.S. imperialism in spreading bacteria in Korea and various parts of the Northeast. We will expose to the people of the world the evidence of U.S. imperialism's bacteriological warfare. The investigation team, composed of representatives from various democratic parties and people's organizations across the country, as well as nationally renowned scientific experts, will do everything possible to complete the above tasks." Lin Feng, Vice Chairman of the Northeast People's Government, delivered a welcome speech, saying: "Our people in the Northeast warmly welcome the arrival of the comrades of the investigation team. At present, all the people in the Northeast have formed a mass front against bacteriological warfare, which will vigorously assist the work of the investigation team." Finally, Wang Bin, Minister of Health of the Northeast People's Government, reported to all members of the team on the process of the American imperialists spreading bacteria-carrying insects in various parts of the Northeast.
    After the forum, all members of the investigation team inspected various evidences of the bacteriological warfare crimes committed by U.S. imperialism. In the evening, the investigation team and scientific and medical workers in the Northeast region held a technical symposium to initially exchange views.

### “美帝国主义细菌战罪行调查团”抵沈阳  开始视察美国侵略者细菌战罪证  东北人民热烈欢迎调查团并将全力协助调查

1952-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

    “美帝国主义细菌战罪行调查团”抵沈阳
    开始视察美国侵略者细菌战罪证
    东北人民热烈欢迎调查团并将全力协助调查
    【新华社沈阳十六日电】“美帝国主义细菌战罪行调查团”在团长李德全、副团长廖承志、陈其瑗率领下，十六日午后二时三十分到达沈阳。前往车站欢迎的有：东北人民政府副主席林枫、中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会东北总分会主席高崇民、人民解放军东北军区副司令员贺晋年、中共中央东北局副秘书长马洪、中国国民党革命委员会东北临时工作委员会主任委员宁武、东北人民政府秘书长栗又文、教育部部长车向忱、文化部部长刘芝明、卫生部部长王斌、人民解放军东北军区政治部主任莫文骅、卫生部部长李资平、中国科学院东北分院代理院长严济慈、沈阳市副市长焦若愚等三十多人。在沈阳的朝鲜驻华大使馆三等秘书许鸣鹤也前往欢迎。调查团全体人员下车后，即与欢迎人员在东北人民政府交际处举行座谈。调查团团长李德全在会上发表简短演说，她说：“我们这次来实地调查美帝国主义在朝鲜和东北各地撒布细菌的罪行，我们将向全世界人民揭露美帝国主义进行细菌战的罪证。由全国各民主党派、人民团体代表和全国闻名的科学专家等组成的调查团，将尽一切努力完成上述任务。”东北人民政府副主席林枫致欢迎词说：“我们东北人民非常欢迎调查团同志们的来临，目前全东北人民已组成了一个群众性的反细菌战的战线，将会有力地协助调查团的工作。”最后，由东北人民政府卫生部部长王斌向全体团员报告美帝国主义者在东北各地撒布细菌毒虫的经过。
    座谈后，调查团全体人员视察了美帝国主义撒布细菌的各种罪证。晚间，调查团和东北区科学医务工作者举行技术座谈会，初步交换意见。
