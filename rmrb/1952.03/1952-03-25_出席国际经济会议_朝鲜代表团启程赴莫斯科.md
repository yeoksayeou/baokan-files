Title: North Korean Delegation Departs for Moscow to Attend International Economic Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-03-25
Page: 4

    Attending the International Economic Conference
    North Korean Delegation Departs for Moscow
    [Dispatch from this newspaper] According to Xinhua News Agency: The delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Yu Dao-seong and Deputy Head Yoon Hang-joong, departed for Moscow on the 15th to attend the International Economic Conference to be held in Moscow on April 3rd, and was warmly greeted and sent off by people from all walks of life in Pyongyang.

### 出席国际经济会议  朝鲜代表团启程赴莫斯科

1952-03-25
第4版()
专栏：

    出席国际经济会议
    朝鲜代表团启程赴莫斯科
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：出席四月三日在莫斯科举行的国际经济会议的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国代表团，在团长俞道承、副团长尹行重的率领下，在十五日启程赴莫斯科，受到平壤各界人士的热烈欢送。
