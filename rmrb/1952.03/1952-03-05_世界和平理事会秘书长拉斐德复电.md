Title: Reply Telegram from Jean Lafitte, Secretary-General of the World Peace Council
Author:
Date: 1952-03-05
Page: 1

Secretary-General Jean Lafitte of the World Peace Council replied to Chairman Guo Moruo of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace:

    I have forwarded your telegram to Chairman Joliot-Curie, requesting him to publish it. I have also forwarded the original text to all members of the World Peace Council and the peace committees of various countries.

    John Lafitte, 21:00, March 1, Prague

### 世界和平理事会秘书长拉斐德复电

1952-03-05
第1版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会秘书长拉斐德复电中国保卫世界和平委员会郭沫若主席：
    我已把您的电报转交约里奥—居里主席，请他发表，我又把原文转交世界和平理事会全体理事及各国和平委员会。
    约翰·拉斐德  三月一日二十一时于布拉格
