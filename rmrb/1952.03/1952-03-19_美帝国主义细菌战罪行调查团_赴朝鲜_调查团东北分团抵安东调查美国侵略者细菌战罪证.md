Title: "Investigation Group on Crimes of Bacteriological Warfare by U.S. Imperialism" Goes to Korea; Northeast Branch of Investigation Group Arrives in Andong to Investigate Evidence of Bacteriological Warfare Crimes by U.S. Aggressors
Author:
Date: 1952-03-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 18th: The "Investigation Group on Crimes of Bacteriological Warfare by U.S. Imperialism" has departed from Shenyang to conduct investigations in Korea and in Andong and Kuandian, Liaodong Province. The group going to Korea, led by the head of the group, Li Dequan, and the deputy head, Liao Chengzhi, departed on the 18th and will travel to Korea via Andong City.

Xinhua News Agency, Andong, 18th: The Northeast Branch of the "Investigation Group on Crimes of Bacteriological Warfare by U.S. Imperialism," a group of forty-two people led by the head of the branch, Chen Qiyuan, left Shenyang by train on the 17th and arrived in Andong on the 18th. Gao Yang, Chairman of the Liaodong Provincial People's Government, Liu Zizai, Deputy Secretary of the Liaodong Provincial Party Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Chen Beichen, Mayor of Andong City, Zhang Tie, Director of the Health Department of the Liaodong Provincial People's Government, and heads of various people's organizations in Andong City welcomed them at the station. The branch group listened to a report by Gao Yang, the person in charge of the local epidemic prevention committee, on the U.S. imperialists' continuous scattering of bacteria-carrying insects and inspected the evidence of the crimes on the spot.

Before leaving Shenyang, the Northeast Branch extensively collected evidence of U.S. imperialists scattering bacteria-carrying insects in various parts of Northeast China. The Northeast Epidemic Prevention Committee, the Northeast literary and art circles, and the press all sent many people to participate in the work of the branch group. Li Guisen, Director of the Reception Office of the Northeast People's Government, accompanied the branch group to Andong.

### “美帝国主义细菌战罪行调查团”赴朝鲜  调查团东北分团抵安东调查美国侵略者细菌战罪证

1952-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

    “美帝国主义细菌战罪行调查团”赴朝鲜
    调查团东北分团抵安东调查美国侵略者细菌战罪证
    【新华社沈阳十八日电】“美帝国主义细菌战罪行调查团”已由沈阳出发，分赴朝鲜及辽东省安东、宽甸等地进行调查。赴朝鲜调查的由该团团长李德全、副团长廖承志率领，在十八日出发，将经安东市前往朝鲜。
    【新华社安东十八日电】“美帝国主义细菌战罪行调查团”东北分团一行四十二人，在分团团长陈其瑗率领下，在十七日乘车离开沈阳，十八日到达安东。辽东省人民政府主席高扬、中共辽东省委副书记刘子载、安东市市长陈北辰、辽东省人民政府卫生厅厅长张铁和安东市各人民团体负责人都到车站欢迎。该分团听取了该地防疫委员会负责人高扬报告美帝国主义连日撒布细菌毒虫的经过，并实地视察罪证。
    东北分团离开沈阳前，曾广泛搜集美帝国主义在东北各地撒布细菌毒虫的罪证。东北防疫委员会、东北文艺界、新闻界都派许多人参加分团工作。东北人民政府交际处处长李桂森陪同该分团到安东。
