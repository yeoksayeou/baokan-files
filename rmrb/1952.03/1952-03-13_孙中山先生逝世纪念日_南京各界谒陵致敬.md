Title: Nanjing Circles Pay Respects at Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum on the Anniversary of His Death
Author:
Date: 1952-03-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, March 12th: Representatives from all circles in Nanjing paid respects at the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum to commemorate the 27th anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's death. Li Leping, Deputy Mayor of Nanjing; He Jifeng, Vice Chairman of the Nanjing Municipal People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee; Liu Shuzhou, representative of the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China; Shao Hengqiu, head of the Nanjing Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang; and more than 30 representatives from local organizations of various democratic parties in Nanjing and people's organizations in Nanjing, gathered solemnly in front of the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum on the morning of the 12th to hold a memorial ceremony and present wreaths to pay their respects. Members of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang in Nanjing also held a memorial meeting on the afternoon of the 12th.

### 孙中山先生逝世纪念日  南京各界谒陵致敬

1952-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

    孙中山先生逝世纪念日
    南京各界谒陵致敬
    【新华社南京十二日电】南京市各界代表晋谒中山陵，纪念孙中山先生逝世二十七周年。南京市人民政府副市长李乐平、南京市各界人民代表会议协商委员会副主席何基沣、中国共产党南京市委员会代表刘述周、中国国民党革命委员会南京市分部负责人邵恒秋及各民主党派南京地方组织和南京市各人民团体代表三十多人，在十二日上午齐集中山陵前，隆重举行谒陵仪式，献花圈致敬。中国国民党革命委员会在南京的党员并在十二日下午举行了纪念会。
