Title: Secretary-General of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers Calls Shen Junru to Express Serious Protest to the United Nations Against the US Military's Germ Warfare in the Invasion of Korea

Author:

Date: 1952-03-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 17th: The Secretary-General of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, lawyer of the Paris Court of Appeal, Joe Nordmann, and others, telegraphed Shen Junru, President of the Supreme People's Court of China, expressing a serious protest against the crime of the US military conducting germ warfare. The telegram is as follows: President Shen Junru of the Supreme People's Court, Beijing, China: We, representing seventy lawyers of the Paris Court of Appeal, express our great indignation at the germ warfare conducted by American pilots against the Chinese and Korean people. We now lodge a serious protest with the United Nations against these crimes.

Joe Nordmann, Marcel Willard-Lederman
Sent from Paris, 3:20 PM on the 15th.

### 国际民主法律工作者协会总书记致电沈钧儒表示  向联合国严重抗议侵朝美军进行细菌战

1952-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

    国际民主法律工作者协会总书记致电沈钧儒表示
    向联合国严重抗议侵朝美军进行细菌战
    【新华社十七日讯】国际民主法律工作者协会总书记、巴黎上诉法院律师乔·诺德曼等致电我国最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，表示严重抗议美军进行细菌战的罪行。电文如下：中国北京最高人民法院沈钧儒院长：我们代表巴黎上诉法院的七十位律师，对于美国飞行员对中朝人民所进行的细菌战，表示极大的愤怒。我们现向联合国提出严重的抗议，反对这些罪行。
    乔·诺德曼，马塞尔·威拉特·列德曼
    巴黎十五日下午四时二十分发
