Title: The Vietnamese People's Army Holds a Grand Military Parade in Hoa Binh City; General Vo Nguyen Giap Calls for Preparations to Crush the Enemy's New Conspiracies; The Vietnamese Council of Ministers Decides to Launch a Production Increase and Enemy Elimination Competition This Year
Author:
Date: 1952-03-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 4th: According to the Vietnam News Agency: On February 27th, the Vietnamese People's Army held a large-scale military parade in the newly liberated Hoa Binh City. Vietnamese People's Army troops who defeated the French invaders on the Black River, Hoa Binh front, and Highway 6 participated in the review. The military parade was presided over by General Vo Nguyen Giap, Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnamese People's Army. Representatives from the National United Front of Vietnam, representatives from the northern Vietnamese mountainous regions and the Red River Delta regions, representatives from the Vietnamese People's Army troops in north-central Vietnam, representatives from war service personnel, and many residents of Hoa Binh City attended the ceremony.

At the celebration, General Vo Nguyen Giap read congratulatory messages from President Ho Chi Minh, the Central Executive Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, and the National United Front of Vietnam. He also issued an order to the Vietnamese People's Army, emphasizing the importance of the recent three-month campaign to liberate Hoa Binh City, and calling on Vietnamese People's Army soldiers to prepare to crush any new conspiracies and retaliatory actions that the enemy might undertake after their serious defeat.

Xinhua News Agency, 4th: According to a Vietnam News Agency report on the 1st: The government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam held a meeting of the Council of Ministers in mid-February. The meeting discussed the international situation and listened to a military report by General Vo Nguyen Giap, Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnamese People's Army.

The Council of Ministers approved the budget for 1952 and decided to launch a new competition movement in 1952 to increase production, kill enemies for merit, and support the army and give preferential treatment to families of revolutionary soldiers and martyrs. The meeting also approved a draft plan to adjust the central and local government structures at all levels, as well as regulations for reducing and reassigning personnel in government agencies in various districts, counties, and provinces.

President Ho Chi Minh, at the conclusion of the meeting, particularly emphasized the significance of the recent military victories. He also pointed out that the Vietnamese People's Government's production and frugality plan is the central task of the Vietnamese people and cadres in 1952, and everyone must launch a competition to complete this plan.

### 越南人民军在和平城举行盛大阅兵典礼  武元甲将军号召准备粉碎敌人的新阴谋  越南部长会议决定今年展开增产和杀敌竞赛运动

1952-03-05
第4版()
专栏：

    越南人民军在和平城举行盛大阅兵典礼
    武元甲将军号召准备粉碎敌人的新阴谋
    越南部长会议决定今年展开增产和杀敌竞赛运动
    【新华社四日讯】据越南通讯社消息：二月二十七日，越南人民军在新解放的和平城举行了大规模的阅兵典礼。在黑水江和平战线上和第六号公路上击败法国侵略者的越南人民军部队参加了检阅。阅兵典礼由越南人民军总司令武元甲将军主持。参加典礼的有越南国民联合战线全国委员会的代表、北越山区和红河三角洲地区的代表、中越北部越南人民军部队的代表、战勤工作人员的代表以及和平城的许多居民。
    在庆祝会中，武元甲将军宣读了胡志明主席、越南劳动党中央执行委员会和越南国民联合战线全国委员会的贺电，并向越南人民军发出命令，强调指出最近三月来解放和平城战役的重要意义，并号召越南人民军战士准备粉碎敌人在严重失败以后所可能进行的新阴谋和报复行动。
    【新华社四日讯】据越南通讯社一日消息：越南民主共和国政府在二月中旬举行部长会议。会议讨论了国际形势，并听取了越南人民军总司令武元甲将军的军事报告。
    部长会议通过了一九五二年度的预算，并决定在一九五二年展开增加生产、杀敌立功和拥军优属的新的竞赛运动。会议又通过了调整中央和地方各级政府机构的计划草案，以及减少和重新配备各区、县、省政府机关人员的条例。
    胡志明主席在会议结束时特别强调指出最近军事胜利的重要意义。他并指出，越南人民政府的生产节约计划，是越南人民和干部在一九五二年中的中心任务，大家必须展开竞赛来完成这个计划。
