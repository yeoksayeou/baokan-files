Title: Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Convenes; Both Chambers Will Separately Approve This Year's National Budget and Adopt Decrees
Author:
Date: 1952-03-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 6th: The Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union convened in the Kremlin on the 5th. The meeting of the Union Council was held at 2 p.m. When Malenkov, Beria, Voroshilov, Andreyev, Kaganovich, Khrushchev, Shvernik, Suslov, Ponomarin, etc., appeared on the government platform, all delegates and guests warmly applauded. Subsequently, the members of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet and ministers of the government also took their seats. Guests attending the meeting included members of foreign diplomatic missions, as well as Soviet and foreign journalists. The meeting first heard and approved the report of the Credentials Committee of the Union Council on the examination of the qualifications of the nine newly elected deputies, and then approved the agenda of the meeting.

The meeting of the Council of Nationalities was opened at 4 p.m. by the Chairman of the Council of Nationalities, Shayakhmetov. Mikoyan, Kosygin, Shvernik, and Shkiryatov attended the meeting, and all delegates and guests responded with warm applause. The meeting heard and approved the report of the Credentials Committee of the Council of Nationalities on the examination of the qualifications of the nine newly elected deputies.

The Union Council and the Council of Nationalities adopted the same meeting agenda as follows: 1. Approve the Soviet Union's national budget for 1952; 2. Adopt the decrees promulgated by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. The two chambers have decided to hold a joint meeting at 7 p.m. on the 6th to hear a report on the Soviet Union's national budget for 1952, and the reports of the budget committees of the two chambers, and then convene separately for discussion and voting. The procedure for approving the decrees promulgated by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union will also be carried out separately by the two chambers.

### 苏联最高苏维埃会议开幕  两院将分别批准今年国家预算和通过法令

1952-03-07
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃会议开幕
    两院将分别批准今年国家预算和通过法令
    【新华社六日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃会议于五日在克里姆林宫开幕。联盟院的会议在下午二时举行，当马林科夫、贝利亚、伏罗希洛夫、安德列耶夫、卡冈诺维奇、赫鲁舍切夫、什维尔尼克、苏斯洛夫、波诺马林科等出现在政府席上时，全场代表和来宾都热烈鼓掌欢迎。接着，最高苏维埃主席团各委员和政府各部长也相继就座。参加会议的来宾有各国外交使团人员，以及苏联和外国的新闻记者。会议首先听取并通过了联盟院资格审查委员会关于审查九位补选代表的资格的报告，接着通过了会议的议程。
    民族院的会议于下午四时由民族院主席沙亚赫米托夫主持开幕。米高扬、柯西金、什维尔尼克和施基利亚托夫出席会议，全体代表和来宾们报以热烈的掌声。会议听取并通过了民族院资格审查委员会关于审查九位补选代表的资格的报告。
    联盟院和民族院通过相同的会议议程如下：一、批准苏联一九五二年国家预算；二、通过苏联最高苏维埃主席团颁布的法令。两院已决定在六日下午七时举行联席会议，听取关于苏联一九五二年国家预算的报告，和两院预算委员会的报告，然后分别集会进行讨论和表决。批准苏联最高苏维埃主席团颁布的法令的手续，也由两院分别进行。
