    Our Great Motherland
    In order to cultivate the interest of children and teenagers in aviation and to learn the principles of flight, many middle schools in Beijing have established model airplane research groups. This is a scene of a model airplane flying competition held by students of Beijing No. 11 Middle School in Tiananmen Square.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-03-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为了培养少年儿童对航空事业的爱好和学习飞行的原理，北京市的许多中学校，都成立了模型飞机研究组。这是北京市第十一中学的学生，在天安门广场举行模型飞机飞行比赛的情形。
