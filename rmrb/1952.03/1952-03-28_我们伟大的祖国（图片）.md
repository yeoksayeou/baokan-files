Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-03-28
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Chen Yongkang, a model of high rice yield in East China, initiated the establishment of a mutual aid team in January of this year. Here, their mutual aid team is holding a meeting. They unanimously raised their hands to approve the patriotic production increase plan, requiring the average yield per mu of all the team's land to reach 1,000 jin, with three mu creating a new record of a maximum yield of 1,600 jin per mu.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    华东水稻丰产模范陈永康，在今年一月发起成立了互助组。在这里，他们的互助组正在开会。他们一致举手通过爱国增产计划，要全组土地每亩平均产量达到一千市斤，其中有三亩要创造每亩最高产量一千六百市斤的新纪录。
