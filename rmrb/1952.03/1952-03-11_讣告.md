Author:

Date: 1952-03-11
Page: 1

Obituary
Mr. Li Xijiu, a member of the Central People's Government Committee, a member of the Supreme People's Procuratorate of the Central People's Government, Vice Chairman of the People's Government of Hebei Province, and a member of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, passed away due to illness in Beijing at 6:00 PM on March 10, 1952. A memorial service will be held on a selected date. This obituary is hereby announced.

Li Xijiu Funeral Committee: Li Jishen, Lin Boqu, Shen Junru, Chen Shutong, Luo Ronghuan, Dong Biwu, Huang Yanpei, He Xiangning, Tan Pingshan, Li Weihan, Zhang Bojun, Cai Tingkai, Jiang Guangnai, Liu Lantao, Yang Xiufeng, Wang Baozhen, Xing Xiping, Qi Yanming, Yu Xinqing
