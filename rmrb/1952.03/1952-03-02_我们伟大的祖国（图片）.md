Author:

Zhang Lin, a cadre of the China Changchun Railway Administration, is a model Communist Party member who lost his eyesight for the sake of revolutionary work. He always maintains an optimistic spirit, works selflessly, and strives to introduce the advanced experience of Soviet industrial construction, making outstanding achievements. This is a picture of Zhang Lin using a 75x microscope to read.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-03-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中长铁路管理局的干部张林是一个为革命工作而两眼失明的模范共产党员。他始终抱着乐观主义的精神，忘我地工作，并努力介绍苏联工业建设的先进经验，做出了优越的成绩。这是张林正用七十五倍的显微镜看书的情形。
