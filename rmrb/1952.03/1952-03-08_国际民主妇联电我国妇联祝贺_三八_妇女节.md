Title: International Democratic Women's Federation Sends Telegram to All-China Women's Federation to Congratulate "March 8th" Women's Day
Author:
Date: 1952-03-08
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The International Democratic Women's Federation sent a telegram to the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, congratulating "March 8th" Women's Day. The telegram is as follows:
    On the occasion of the "March 8th" International Women's Day, I wish you success in the construction of New China and the struggle for peace, and greater victories for the heroic Chinese People's Volunteers in Korea.
    International Democratic Women's Federation

### 国际民主妇联电我国妇联祝贺“三八”妇女节

1952-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

    国际民主妇联电我国妇联祝贺“三八”妇女节
    【新华社七日讯】国际民主妇联致电中华全国民主妇女联合会，祝贺“三八”妇女节。电文如下：
    值此“三八”国际妇女节，谨祝新中国建设及争取和平获得成功，并祝英勇的中国人民志愿军在朝鲜获得更大的胜利。
    国际民主妇联
