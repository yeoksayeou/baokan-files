Title: Delegation to International Children's Defense Conference Departs for Vienna; Will Represent Parents Nationwide in Accusing American Aggressors of Germ Warfare Atrocities Against Our Country
Author:
Date: 1952-03-28
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 27th: The Chinese delegation to the International Children's Defense Conference, scheduled to be held in Vienna in April, led by Kang Keqing (Secretary-General of the Chinese People's National Committee for the Defense of Children) as head and Liu Shi (Director of the Department of Teacher Education of the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government) as deputy head, left Beijing for Vienna on the 25th. Those who saw them off at the station included Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's National Committee for the Defense of Children, and committee members Luo Qiong, Li Lilian, Liao Mengxing, Zuo Lin, representatives of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Luo Shuzhang, Zeng Xianzhi, and representatives from the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League and other people's organizations, totaling over a hundred people.
    The Chinese delegation to the conference is composed of representatives from the Chinese People's National Committee for the Defense of Children, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the All-China Youth Federation, and representatives from education, justice, literature and art, medical services, social welfare, and childcare. The delegation includes one head, one deputy head, fifteen members, and four staff members. The members include: Zhang Yunying (All-China Federation of Trade Unions), Ke Zaishuo (All-China Youth Federation, already abroad), Wang Ruqi (Ministry of Justice of the Central People's Government), Wu Chao Ren (Deputy Dean of the Beijing University Medical College), Tao Shufan (Primary School Teacher), Huang Shujun (Kindergarten Educator) and Wang Mingfeng (Model Children's Caregiver), among others. Before their departure, the delegation told reporters that they would faithfully convey the wishes and will of all people and parents across the country who care about the well-being of children to defend peace, and discuss all issues related to the defense of children with representatives from various countries. The delegates will also represent parents nationwide in accusing the American aggressors of their germ warfare atrocities against the Chinese people.

### 我出席国际保卫儿童大会代表团赴维也纳  将代表全国父母们控诉美国侵略者对我国进行细菌战暴行

1952-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我出席国际保卫儿童大会代表团赴维也纳
    将代表全国父母们控诉美国侵略者对我国进行细菌战暴行
    【新华社二十七日讯】我国出席定于四月间在维也纳举行的国际保卫儿童大会的代表团，由团长康克清（中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会秘书长），副团长柳湜（中央人民政府教育部师范教育司司长）率领，已在二十五日离北京赴维也纳。到车站送行的有：中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会副主席邓颖超、委员罗琼、李丽莲、廖梦醒、左林，中华全国民主妇女联合会代表罗叔章、曾宪植，以及中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会和其他人民团体的代表一百余人。
    我国出席大会的代表团是由中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会、中华全国总工会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中华全国民主青年联合总会，以及教育、司法、文艺、医务、社会福利、儿童保育等方面的代表组成。代表团计有团长、副团长各一人、团员十五人和工作人员四人。团员包括：张云莹（全国总工会）、柯在铄（全国青联，已在国外）、王汝琪（中央人民政府司法部）、吴朝仁（北京大学医学院副院长）、陶淑范（小学教师）、黄淑俊（幼稚教育工作者）和王鸣凤（模范儿童教养员）等人。代表团在临行前对本社记者表示：代表们将忠实地传达全国一切关怀儿童幸福的人士和父母们保卫和平的愿望和意志，和各国代表共同商讨一切有关保卫儿童的问题。代表们并将代表全国的父母们，控诉美国侵略者对中国人民进行细菌战的暴行。
