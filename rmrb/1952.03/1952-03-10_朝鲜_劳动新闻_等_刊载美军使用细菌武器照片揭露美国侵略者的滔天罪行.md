Title: North Korean "Rodong Sinmun" and Others Publish Photos of US Military Using Bacteriological Weapons, Exposing the Heinous Crimes of the American Aggressors
Author:
Date: 1952-03-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 6th: "Rodong Sinmun," "Democratic Korea," and other newspapers have published many photos concerning the American aggressors' use of bacteriological weapons. The first photo published by "Democratic Korea" shows the scene of disease-carrying insects spreading on the snow after the American aggressors dropped bacteriological bombs. The second is the internal structure of this bomb, with the shell divided into four compartments, each containing poisonous insects. The other two photos depict fragments of the bomb and the external shape of the bacteriological bomb.

The newspapers reported that the Korean people expressed great anger at the American aggressors' crimes of conducting bacteriological warfare. People in various places held rallies to protest, demanding that the people's courts try these crimes. The newspapers published numerous statements from workers, farmers, intellectuals, and leaders from all walks of life, unanimously denouncing the vicious and shameless crimes committed by the American aggressors under the banner of the United Nations.

### 朝鲜“劳动新闻”等  刊载美军使用细菌武器照片揭露美国侵略者的滔天罪行

1952-03-10
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜“劳动新闻”等
    刊载美军使用细菌武器照片揭露美国侵略者的滔天罪行
    【新华社七日讯】据塔斯社平壤六日讯：“劳动新闻”、“民主朝鲜”和其他报纸登出许多关于美国侵略者使用细菌武器的照片。“民主朝鲜”所刊载的第一幅照片的内容是：在美国侵略者投下细菌炸弹后，带有病菌的毒虫散布在雪地上的情形。第二幅是这个炸弹的内部构造，弹壳内分为四格，每格中都放有毒虫。另外两幅照片摄取的是炸弹碎片和细菌炸弹的外形。
    各报报道：朝鲜人民对于美国侵略者进行细菌战的罪行，表示极大的愤怒。各地人民纷纷集会抗议，他们要求由人民法庭来审判这种罪行。各报登载了许多工人、农民、知识分子和各阶层人民领袖的声明，他们一致痛斥美国侵略者在联合国的蓝旗下犯下的这种恶毒无耻的罪行。
