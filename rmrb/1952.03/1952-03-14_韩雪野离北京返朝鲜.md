Title: Han Xueye Departs Beijing to Return to North Korea
Author:
Date: 1952-03-14
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 13th: Han Xueye, Chairman of the Korean National Committee for the Promotion of Peace, left Beijing by train on the 13th to return to North Korea. Those who saw him off at the station included Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the China Peace Committee, Choe Young, Counselor of the Korean Embassy in China, and the famous Korean dancer Choi Seung-hee, among others.
    In other news: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, hosted a banquet on the 12th to bid farewell to Han Xueye. Those in attendance included Mao Dun, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the China Peace Committee, Wu Maosun, Deputy Secretary-General, Tan Tiwu, Deputy Director of the Organization Department, and the famous Korean dancer Choi Seung-hee, among others.

### 韩雪野离北京返朝鲜

1952-03-14
第4版()
专栏：

    韩雪野离北京返朝鲜
    【新华社十三日讯】朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会委员长韩雪野于十三日乘火车离北京返朝鲜。到车站欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会秘书长刘贯一、朝鲜驻华大使馆参赞崔英及朝鲜名舞蹈家崔承喜等。
    又讯：中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会主席郭沫若曾于十二日设宴欢送韩雪野，作陪者有中华全国文学艺术联合会副主席茅盾、中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会秘书长刘贯一、副秘书长吴茂荪、组织部副部长谭惕吾及朝鲜名舞蹈家崔承喜等。
