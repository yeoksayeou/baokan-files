Title: Korean Ambassador Kwon O-jik Pays a Visit to Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1952-03-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th.
New Korean Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Kwon O-jik, accompanied by Counselor Choi Young, visited Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs at 6:00 PM on the 11th to discuss matters related to presenting his credentials. Also present were Chen Jiakang, Acting Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wei Baoshan, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department.

### 朝鲜大使权五稷拜会周恩来外长

1952-03-12
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜大使权五稷拜会周恩来外长
    【新华社十一日讯】新任朝鲜驻华特命全权大使权五稷偕参赞崔英于十一日下午六时赴外交部拜会周恩来部长，商谈呈递国书事宜。在座者尚有：外交部亚洲司代司长陈家康及交际处副处长魏宝善。
