Our Great Motherland
Scientific workers and medical and health workers from all over the country have requested to go to the forefront of the anti-bacterial warfare to fight against the poisonous flames of the American aggressors' bacterial warfare. This is a picture of students from Peking University Medical College signing a resolution with indignation.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-03-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    全国各地科学工作者和医药卫生工作者纷纷要求到反细菌战的最前线，为扑灭美国侵略者细菌战的毒焰而斗争。这是北京大学医学院的学生们怀着愤激的心情，正在决心书上签名。
