    Cultural Briefs
    ▲To provide propaganda materials for various regions to organize the masses to prevent and combat drought, the Agricultural Film Studio of the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government has specially produced two slideshows, "Preserving Soil Moisture and Preventing Drought" and "Carrying Water for Planting." At the same time, the two slideshows "Cotton Cultivation Techniques" and "Turning Poor Mountains into Rich Mountains" are printed into cellophane sheets using photographic copperplate color printing, to be supplied to agricultural agencies in various regions as propaganda for carrying out spring plowing and afforestation work.

### 文化简讯

1952-03-21
第3版()
专栏：文化简讯

    文化简讯
    ▲中央人民政府农业部农业电影社为了供给各地做好组织群众防旱抗旱的宣传材料，特编制“保墒防旱”“挑水点种”两部幻灯片，同时把“棉花栽培术”和“把穷山变成富山”两部灯片，采用照像铜版套色印成玻璃纸片，供给各地农业机关作为开展春耕和造林工作的宣传之用。
