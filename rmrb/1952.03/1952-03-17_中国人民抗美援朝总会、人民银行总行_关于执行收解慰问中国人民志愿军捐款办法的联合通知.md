Title: Joint Notice from the China People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea and the Head Office of the People's Bank of China Regarding the Implementation of Measures for Collecting and Delivering Donations to Comfort the Chinese People's Volunteer Army
Author: 
Date: 1952-03-17
Page: 1

    China People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea, Head Office of the People's Bank of China
    Joint Notice Regarding the Implementation of Measures for Collecting and Delivering Donations to Comfort the Chinese People's Volunteer Army

    In order to meet the needs of people across the country to comfort the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and to simplify procedures, this Committee and the Bank have jointly formulated the "Measures for the China People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea to Entrust the People's Bank of China to Collect Donations to Comfort the Chinese People's Volunteer Army," which is attached hereto and should be implemented accordingly.

    China People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea
    Head Office of the People's Bank of China

### 中国人民抗美援朝总会、人民银行总行  关于执行收解慰问中国人民志愿军捐款办法的联合通知

1952-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民抗美援朝总会、人民银行总行
    关于执行收解慰问中国人民志愿军捐款办法的联合通知
    为了满足全国各地人民慰问中国人民志愿军的需要和简便手续起见，本会、行共同商订了“中国人民抗美援朝总会委托中国人民银行代收慰问中国人民志愿军捐款办法”，随文附发，即希遵照实行。
    中国人民抗美援朝总会
    中国人民银行总行
