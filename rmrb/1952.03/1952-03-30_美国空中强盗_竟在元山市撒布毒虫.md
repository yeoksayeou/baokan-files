Title: American Aerial Bandits Actually Spread Poisonous Insects in Wonsan

Author:

Date: 1952-03-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 29th. American aerial bandits, on the evening of the 19th, again dropped large numbers of poisonous insects carrying bacteria, such as earthworms, ground silkworms, snails, grasshoppers, and mosquitoes, over the areas of Chungchong-ri, Shinpoong-ri, Shinheung-ri, and Sanje-ri in Wonsan city, Korea. After these poisonous insects landed, the epidemic prevention team members and citizens of the area immediately rose up to quickly exterminate them. The people of Wonsan City, who personally witnessed the heinous crimes committed by the American bandits, have become more vigilant and are further carrying out effective epidemic prevention work with utmost anger.

### 美国空中强盗  竟在元山市撒布毒虫

1952-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

    美国空中强盗
    竟在元山市撒布毒虫
    【新华社平壤二十九日电】美国空中强盗十九日晚又在朝鲜元山市区中清里、新丰里、新兴里、山祭里等地上空，投掷了大量带有细菌的蚯蚓、地蚕、蜗牛、蚱蜢、蚊子等毒虫。这些毒虫落地后，该地的防疫队员和市民就一致奋起迅速加以扑灭。亲眼看到美国强盗制造了万恶罪行的元山市人民都更加提高警惕，以万分愤怒的心情进一步展开了有效的防疫工作。
