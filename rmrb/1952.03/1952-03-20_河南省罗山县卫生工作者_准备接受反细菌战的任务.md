Title: Henan Province, Luoshan County Health Workers Prepare to Accept Anti-Bacterial Warfare Mission
Author:
Date: 1952-03-20
Page: 2

Reader's Letters:

    Henan Province, Luoshan County Health Workers
    Prepare to Accept Anti-Bacterial Warfare Mission
    We severely protest the crime of American imperialism spreading bacteria. We are health workers, soldiers who eliminate bacteria and infectious diseases. We are ready at any time to respond to the call of relevant leaders and unconditionally accept the tasks assigned to us by the motherland, and use practical actions to smash the heinous crimes of the American bandits.
    Luoshan County Branch of Henan Provincial Health Workers Association
    Luoshan County Branch of the Red Cross Society of China

### 河南省罗山县卫生工作者  准备接受反细菌战的任务

1952-03-20
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    河南省罗山县卫生工作者
    准备接受反细菌战的任务
    我们严重抗议美帝国主义撒布细菌的罪行。我们是卫生工作者，是消灭细菌及传染病的战士。我们随时准备响应有关领导方面的号召，无条件地接受祖国交给我们的任务，用实际行动去粉碎美国强盗的毒恶罪行。
    河南省卫生工作者协会罗山县分会
    中国红十字会罗山县分会
