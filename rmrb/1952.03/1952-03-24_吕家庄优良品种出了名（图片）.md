Author: Peng Bin, Ren Qianqiao, Peng Hanchun

Page: 3

(4) Great changes in Lüjia Village,
    Every household exchanges seeds.
    For a good harvest of crops,
    They all select seeds in the fields.

(5) Lüjia Village harvested more grain,
    Passing the method to other villages.
    15,000 jin of good seeds were exchanged,
    Extended to 250 villages, large and small.

(6) Promote and propagate diligently,
    Superior varieties spread to all directions;
    Old and young,
    Everyone rejoices when they see the good seeds.

### 吕家庄优良品种出了名（图片）

1952-03-24
第3版()
专栏：

    吕家庄优良品种出了名
    彭彬  任迁乔  彭翰春  作
    （四）吕家庄大转变，
    家家户户把种籽换。
    为了庄稼收成好，
    都到田里把种选。
    （五）吕家庄多打了粮，
    把办法传给外庄上。
    好种籽换出去一万五千斤，
    推广到大小二百五十个庄。
    （六）勤宣传多推广，
    优良品种走四乡；
    年老的、年轻的，
    谁见了好种谁欢喜。
