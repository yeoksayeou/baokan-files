Title: Chairman Mao Sends Telegram to Comrade Rákosi to Congratulate Him on His Sixtieth Birthday
Author:
Date: 1952-03-09
Page: 1

    [News from this newspaper] March 9th is the sixtieth birthday of Mátyás Rákosi, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party. Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram of congratulations. The telegram reads as follows:

    To the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party,
    Dear Comrade Mátyás Rákosi:

    On the occasion of your sixtieth birthday, I, on behalf of the Communist Party of China and myself personally, extend fraternal congratulations to you - the loyal son of the Hungarian people and the outstanding fighter of the international workers' movement!

    I wish you good health and further success in leading the Hungarian people towards socialism and defending world peace.

    Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Mao Zedong
    March 7, 1952, Beijing

### 毛主席电贺拉科西同志六十寿辰

1952-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席电贺拉科西同志六十寿辰
    【本报讯】三月九日是匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会总书记玛吉阿斯·拉科西六十寿辰。中国共产党中央委员会毛泽东主席特致电祝贺。电文如下：匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会亲爱的玛吉阿斯·拉科西同志：
    在你六十寿辰之际，我代表中国共产党和我个人向你——匈牙利人民的忠实儿子和国际工人运动的卓越战士，致兄弟般的祝贺！
    敬祝你身体健康，并祝你在领导匈牙利人民走向社会主义和保卫世界和平的事业上获得进一步的成功。
    中国共产党中央委员会主席  毛泽东
    一九五二年三月七日，北京
