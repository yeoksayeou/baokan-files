Title: Political Department of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army posthumously awards Martyr Luo Shengjiao a Special Class Merit and confers the honorary title of "First-Class Model of Cherishing the People"
Author:
Date: 1952-03-11
Page: 1

    Political Department of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army
    Posthumously awards Martyr Luo Shengjiao a Special Class Merit
    And confers the honorary title of "First-Class Model of Cherishing the People"
    [This newspaper dispatch] The Political Department of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army has decided to posthumously award Martyr Luo Shengjiao, who heroically saved a drowning Korean boy, a Special Class Merit and confer the honorary title of "First-Class Model of Cherishing the People."
    The decision states: Martyr Luo Shengjiao was a clerk in the reconnaissance company of a certain unit of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. He was from Tongzi Village, Zishan District, Xinhua County, Hunan Province. He was 21 years old, came from a poor peasant family, and had worked as an apprentice. He was an outstanding member of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League. On the morning of January 2nd of this year, at 7:00 AM, Martyr Luo Shengjiao heroically sacrificed his life in Chengchuan County, northern Korea, while rescuing the drowning Korean boy, Cui ?.
    The decision praises Martyr Luo Shengjiao's heroic act, pointing out that he fully demonstrated the noble qualities of the great internationalism and revolutionary heroism of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, carried forward the glorious tradition of the Chinese People's Revolution, and further strengthened the friendly unity between the Chinese and Korean peoples. In order to commemorate and commend Martyr Luo Shengjiao's merits and deeds, the Political Department of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army has decided to posthumously award Martyr Luo Shengjiao a Special Class Merit and confer the honorary title of "First-Class Model of Cherishing the People."

### 中国人民志愿军政治部  追记罗盛教烈士特等功一次  并授予“一级爱民模范”的光荣称号

1952-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民志愿军政治部
    追记罗盛教烈士特等功一次
    并授予“一级爱民模范”的光荣称号
    【本报讯】中国人民志愿军政治部决定对舍身抢救朝鲜溺水少年的罗盛教烈士，追记特等功一次，并授予“一级爱民模范”的光荣称号。
    决定称：罗盛教烈士为中国人民志愿军某部侦察连文书，湖南新化县紫山区桐子村人，年二十一岁，出身于贫农家庭，曾做过学徒，是一个优秀的中国新民主主义青年团团员。今年一月二日早晨七时，罗盛教烈士在朝鲜北部成川郡某村驻地，为援救溺水的朝鲜少年崔?而壮烈牺牲。
    决定中表扬罗盛教烈士的壮烈行为，指出他充分表现了中国人民志愿军伟大的国际主义和革命英雄主义的崇高品质，发扬了中国人民革命的光荣传统，进一步加强了中朝人民的友爱团结。中国人民志愿军政治部为了追记和表扬罗盛教烈士的功勋和事迹，决定追记罗盛教烈士特等功一次，并授予“一级爱民模范”的光荣称号。
