Title: The People's Republic of Romania Presents Two Tractors to Our Country - Presentation Ceremony Held Yesterday in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-03-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 22nd:
The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Romania, Groza, as a celebration of the signing of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, has specially presented two tractors to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government. The presentation ceremony was held in Beijing on the afternoon of the 22nd, with Ambassador Rudenko representing the government of the People's Republic of Romania in presenting the gift, and Director Yu Xinqing of the Central People's Government's Bureau of Ceremonies representing our side in accepting it.

### 罗马尼亚人民共和国  赠我国拖拉机两部  赠送典礼昨日在北京举行

1952-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚人民共和国
    赠我国拖拉机两部
    赠送典礼昨日在北京举行
    【新华社二十二日讯】罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席格罗查，为庆祝中罗文化合作协定的签订，特赠送我中央人民政府毛泽东主席拖拉机两部。赠送典礼于二十二日下午在北京举行，由鲁登科大使代表罗马尼亚人民共和国政府授礼，我方由中央人民政府典礼局余心清局长代表接受。
