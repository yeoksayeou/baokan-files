Title: Sixteen Trade Unions, including Coal Mining, Electricity, Posts and Telecommunications, and Railways, Firmly Protest Against the US Aggressors' Germ Warfare in Korea

Author:
Date: 1952-03-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 5th:

    The National Committee of the China Coal Miners' Union and other organizations issued statements protesting the American aggressors' germ warfare in Korea. The statement is as follows:
    The American aggressors continue to spread large amounts of bacteria in the front and rear of Korea, killing peaceful Korean residents, in a vain attempt to reverse their disastrous defeat on the Korean battlefield. This behavior is completely inhumane and violates international law, and is intolerable to peace-loving and just people all over the world.
    The Chinese trade unions of coal mining, electricity, posts and telecommunications, railways, transportation, shop employees, hardware smelting, machine manufacturing, ordnance, construction, seamen, food, news publishing and printing, finance, salt industry, and civil aviation, firmly protest against the towering crimes of the American bandits in Korea.
    We fully support Premier Zhou's statement, and vow to support the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean People's Army with practical actions to achieve the final victory in the War to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea as soon as possible.
    The National Committee of the China Coal Miners' Union
    The National Committee of the China Electricity Trade Union
    The National Committee of the China Postal and Telecommunications Trade Union
    The National Committee of the China Railway Trade Union
    The National Committee of the China Transportation Trade Union
    The National Committee of the China Store Employees Trade Union
    The Preparatory Committee of the China Hardware Smelting Trade Union
    The Preparatory Committee of the China Machine Manufacturing Trade Union
    The National Committee of the China Ordnance Trade Union
    The Working Committee of the China Construction Trade Union
    The Preparatory Committee of the China Seamen's Trade Union
    The National Committee of the China Food Trade Union
    The Preparatory Committee of the China News Publishing and Printing Trade Union
    The National Committee of the China Financial Trade Union
    The National Committee of the China Salt Industry Trade Union
    The Working Committee of the China Civil Aviation Trade Union
    March 3rd

### 我煤矿、电业、邮电、铁路等十六个工会  坚决抗议侵朝美军进行细菌战

1952-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我煤矿、电业、邮电、铁路等十六个工会
    坚决抗议侵朝美军进行细菌战
    【新华社五日讯】中国煤矿工会全国委员会等发表声明，对美国侵略者在朝鲜进行细菌战争提出抗议，声明如下：
    美国侵略者在朝鲜前线和后方，继续不断地大量撒布细菌，杀害朝鲜和平居民，妄想借此来挽回他们在朝鲜战场上的惨败。这种行为，完全是丧失人性，破坏国际公法，为全世界爱好和平和正义的人民所绝不能容忍的。
    中国煤矿、电业、邮电、铁路、搬运、店员、五金冶炼、机器制造业、兵工、建筑、海员、食品、新闻出版印刷、金融、盐业、民航等工会，对美国强盗在朝鲜的这种滔天罪行，坚决抗议。
    我们完全拥护周总理的声明，并誓以实际行动，支持中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军，早日取得抗美援朝的最后胜利。
