Title: Pak Chong-ae Sends Telegram to All-China Women's Federation Congratulating International Women's Day; Soviet Women Send Telegrams to Women of Various Countries with Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1952-03-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 7th: Pak Chong-ae, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Women's League, sent a telegram to Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, congratulating her on International Women's Day, "March 8th." The telegram reads as follows: Chairman Cai Chang of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation:

	On the occasion of the friendship and solidarity day of democratic women all over the world – International Women's Day, "March 8th" – I, on behalf of all Korean women, extend warm and militant congratulations to you and the great women of all of China.

	Korean women, who have risen up to participate in the Fatherland Liberation War against the armed aggression of the American and British imperialists for the unification, independence, and freedom of their country, are determined to strive for final victory in order to reciprocate your noble assistance.

	I wish you even greater achievements in the cause of striving for lasting world peace and the happy future of women and children.

	Pak Chong-ae, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Women's League

Xinhua News Agency, 9th: According to TASS, Moscow: On International Women's Day, "March 8th," the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee sent telegrams to democratic women's organizations in countries such as Korea, China, Czechoslovakia, Romania, the United Kingdom, and France, warmly congratulating them on the festival and wishing them further success in the noble cause of striving for peace and democracy.

	In a telegram to Pak Chong-ae, Chairman of the Korean Democratic Women's League, the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee said that Soviet women are proud of the achievements of Korean women in the liberation war, both on the front lines and in the rear. We wish our dear sisters, the worthy daughters of the heroic Korean people, new victories in their current just struggle for the unification, freedom, and independence of their country, and for the victory of the just cause of the Korean people.

### 朴正爱致电我全国妇联  祝贺国际妇女节  苏联妇女致电各国妇女祝贺

1952-03-10
第4版()
专栏：

    朴正爱致电我全国妇联
    祝贺国际妇女节
    苏联妇女致电各国妇女祝贺
    【新华社平壤七日电】朝鲜民主妇女同盟中央委员会委员长朴正爱致电中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅，祝贺“三八”国际妇女节。电文如下：中华全国民主妇女联合会蔡畅主席：
    值此全世界民主妇女的友谊团结日——“三八”国际妇女节，我谨代表全朝鲜妇女向您和伟大的全中国妇女，致以热烈的战斗的祝贺。
    为了祖国的统一、独立和自由而奋起参加反抗美英帝国主义武装侵略者的祖国解放战争的朝鲜妇女，决心争取最后的胜利，以答谢你们崇高的援助。
    祝您在争取世界持久和平与妇女儿童幸福的将来的事业中，有更巨大的成就。
    朝鲜民主妇女同盟中央委员会委员长  朴正爱
    【新华社九日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联妇女反法西斯委员会在“三八”国际妇女节致电朝鲜、中国、捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、英国、法国等国家的民主妇女团体，热烈祝贺佳节，并祝她们在争取和平与民主的崇高的事业中获得进一步的成功。
    苏联妇女反法西斯委员会在给朝鲜民主妇女同盟委员长朴正爱的电报里说，苏联妇女对朝鲜妇女在解放战争中在前线与后方所表现的功绩感到骄傲。我们祝我们亲爱的姊妹们，英勇的朝鲜人民无愧的女儿，为了他们国家的统一、自由和独立，为了朝鲜人民正义事业的胜利，在她们目前的正义斗争中获得新的胜利。
