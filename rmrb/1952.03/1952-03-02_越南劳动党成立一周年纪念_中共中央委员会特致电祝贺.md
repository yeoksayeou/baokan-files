Title: Anniversary of the Founding of the Vietnam Workers' Party: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Sends a Congratulatory Message
Author:
Date: 1952-03-02
Page: 1

    To mark the first anniversary of the founding of the Vietnam Workers' Party, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a congratulatory message. The text of the message is as follows:
    To the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party:
    On the occasion of the first anniversary of the founding of the Vietnam Workers' Party, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends brotherly congratulations to you, the Vietnamese working class, and the Vietnamese people!
    We respectfully wish the Vietnam Workers' Party complete victory in its struggle to unite and lead the entire Vietnamese people in expelling the imperialist aggressors and completing the national liberation, and further success in developing people's democracy and building an independent, unified, democratic, and prosperous Vietnam.
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
    March 1, 1952, Beijing.

### 越南劳动党成立一周年纪念  中共中央委员会特致电祝贺

1952-03-02
第1版()
专栏：

    越南劳动党成立一周年纪念
    中共中央委员会特致电祝贺
    【本报讯】三月三日是越南劳动党成立一周年纪念日，中国共产党中央委员会特致电祝贺。电文如下：越南劳动党中央委员会：
    在越南劳动党成立一周年之际，中国共产党中央委员会谨向你们及越南工人阶级和越南人民致兄弟般的祝贺！
    我们敬祝越南劳动党在团结和领导全越南人民驱逐帝国主义侵略者、完成民族解放的斗争中获得完全的胜利，在发展人民民主，建设独立、统一、民主、繁荣的越南的事业上获得进一步的成功。
    中国共产党中央委员会
    一九五二年三月一日，北京。
