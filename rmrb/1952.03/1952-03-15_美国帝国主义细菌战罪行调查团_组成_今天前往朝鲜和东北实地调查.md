Title: "American Imperialist Germ Warfare Crimes Investigation Team" Assembled, to Go to Korea and Northeast Today for On-the-Spot Investigation
Author:
Date: 1952-03-15
Page: 1

    "American Imperialist Germ Warfare Crimes Investigation Team" Assembled
    To Go to Korea and Northeast Today for On-the-Spot Investigation
    [Xinhua News Agency, 14th] The "American Imperialist Germ Warfare Crimes Investigation Team" has now been formed. The list is as follows: The head of the team is Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China; the deputy heads are Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, and Chen Qiyuan, Director of the Supervisory Committee of the China Relief Association; the secretary-general is Gong Naiquan, a medical surgery expert; the deputy secretaries-general are Shen Qiyi, Executive Director of the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization, and Wu Maosun of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs. The team members are Peng Zemin and Yan Jingqing of the Red Cross Society of China; Zeng Zhaolun and Zhang Jingyue of the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies; Fang Shishan, Li Zongen, and Yan Renying of the Chinese Medical Association; Liao Gailong of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression; Yang Meizhen of the Young Women's Christian Association; Tian Demin of the All-China Students' Federation; Wang Zizhong, a Christian pastor; Wang Runfeng of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Lü Huang of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Mei Ru'ao, an expert in international law; Zhou Jianren, a biology expert; Yang Xiandong, an agricultural expert; Xie Shaowen, Wei Xi, Liu Weitong, Yang Shuya, Guo Chengzhou, Shen Dinghong, Xie Zhimu, and Fang Liang, bacteriology experts; He Qi, Liu Chongle, and Liu Zhiying, entomology experts; Wu Guang, a parasitology expert; Yu Huanwen, an epidemiology expert; Cheng Shaozheng, a veterinary expert; Yan Jiagui, a pathologist; He Guanqing, a public health expert; Yang Enfu, a nutrition expert; and Du Zhen, Li Zhihua, and Zhuang Yan, literary and artistic workers; and Lin Lin, Sun Chengpei, and Yao Liwen, news reporters. The entire team, along with staff members, numbers approximately seventy people, and is scheduled to depart from Beijing on the afternoon of March 15th for the Korean front lines and various parts of Northeast China to conduct on-the-spot investigations into the towering crimes of American imperialism in carrying out germ warfare.

专栏：

    今天前往朝鲜和东北实地调查
