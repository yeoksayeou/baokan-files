Title: World Peace Council Receives Numerous Telegrams and Letters Demanding Severe Punishment for American Germ Warfare Criminals; World Federation of Democratic Youth Calls on Youth to Rise Up and Stop U.S. Military Crimes
Author:
Date: 1952-03-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 29th: According to a TASS dispatch from Prague on the 28th: The World Peace Council has received numerous telegrams and letters in which people all over the world angrily protest the heinous crimes of the American imperialists in waging germ warfare.

The "Information Bulletin" published by the World Peace Council has, in recent days, published excerpts from these telegrams and letters, including protests from international and national people's organizations and prominent progressive leaders from many countries. They firmly demand an immediate end to germ warfare and severe punishment for the Americans who initiated and organized it.

Those lodging protests include organizations and peace movement leaders from Britain, France, Belgium, Vietnam, India, Burma, Cyprus, Algeria, Japan, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Iran, the German Democratic Republic, the Netherlands, Italy, China, and other countries; representatives attending the American Peace Congress; the International Federation of Chemical Workers' Unions; the Australian Waterside Workers' Federation and the Building Workers' Industrial Union; the Central Bureau of the Indonesian General Federation of Trade Unions; the Women's International Democratic Federation; and mass organizations from many countries in the fields of trade unions, science, youth, women, culture, education, and religion.

Prominent figures who protested the heinous crimes of American imperialists and denounced the use of inhuman bacteriological weapons include: American singer Paul Robeson, French scientist Armand, Cuban poet Guillén, British legal worker Platts-Mills, Polish professor Infeld, Indian writer Anand, American writer Fast, Japanese scientist Yoshitaro Hirano, Vice-Dean of Canterbury Hewlett Johnson, Brazilian writer Amado, former Mexican government minister Uribe, American artist Kent, Soong Ching Ling, and many others.

Xinhua News Agency, March 29th: TASS dispatch from Budapest, March 29th: World Federation of Democratic Youth General Secretary Terni issued a statement denouncing the crimes of the American aggressors in using bacteriological weapons against the Korean and Chinese people.

The statement pointed out: The American armed forces have actually used bacteriological weapons against the Korean and Chinese people, weapons that even the most heinous murderers, the Hitlerites, did not dare to use. The world's youth are shocked and indignant at the news of this new, monstrous crime. The aggressors intend to turn all countries in the world into cemeteries, and they do not hesitate to subject all of humanity to their threats.

The statement said: We call on the youth of the world, regardless of views and race, we call on the entire younger generation to use all means to force the American government to immediately stop using bacteriological weapons and other weapons of mass slaughter. The World Federation of Democratic Youth calls on young people to fight for the banning of such weapons and for the punishment of those guilty of using them, and calls on young people to demand the conclusion of a peace pact to guarantee human peace and happiness. The statement also called on young American peace fighters to rise up and stop the hands of the criminals, to stop the policy they are pursuing that will subject American and world youth to indescribable suffering.

### 世界和平理事会接到许多电报和信件  要求严惩美国细菌战罪犯  世界民主青联号召青年起来制止美军罪行

1952-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会接到许多电报和信件
    要求严惩美国细菌战罪犯
    世界民主青联号召青年起来制止美军罪行
    【新华社二十九日讯】据塔斯社布拉格二十八日讯：世界和平理事会接到了许多电报和信件，全世界人民在这些电报和信件里愤怒地抗议美帝国主义者进行细菌战的滔天罪行。
    世界和平理事会出版的“情况公报”几天来已把这些电报和信件摘录发表了一部分，其中有国际的与各国国内的人民团体和许多国家的著名进步领袖的抗议。他们坚决要求立即制止细菌战，严厉惩罚发动与组织细菌战的美国人。
    提出抗议的有英国、法国、比利时、越南、印度、缅甸、塞浦路斯、阿尔及利亚、日本、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、伊朗、德意志民主共和国、荷兰、意大利、中国及其他国家的团体与和平运动领袖，参加美洲和平大会的代表，国际化学工人工会联合会，澳大利亚码头工会联合会和建筑工人工会，印尼总工会中央局，国际民主妇女联合会以及许多国家的工会、科学、青年、妇女、文化、教育、宗教等方面的群众团体。
    对美帝国主义者的滔天罪行提出抗议并斥责使用非人道的细菌武器的著名人士有：美国歌唱家罗伯逊，法国科学家阿马德，古巴诗人纪廉（前译为吉里安），英国法律工作者普拉茨—米尔斯，波兰教授英费尔德，印度作家安纳德，美国作家法斯特，日本科学家平野义太郎，坎特伯雷副主教约翰逊，巴西作家亚玛多，前墨西哥政府部长艾里拜托，美国艺术家肯特，宋庆龄和其他许多人士。
    【新华社二十九日讯】塔斯社布达佩斯二十九日讯：世界民主青年联盟总书记特尼发表声明，斥责美国侵略者对朝鲜和中国人民使用细菌武器的罪行。
    声明指出：美国武装部队竟然对朝鲜和中国人民使用了连最凶恶的杀人凶犯希特勒分子也未敢使用的细菌武器，世界青年听到这种新的滔天罪行的消息，都十分震惊和忿慨。侵略者打算把世界各国都变成墓场，他们不惜让全体人类遭受他们的威胁。
    声明说：我们号召世界青年，不分见解和种族，号召整个青年一代用一切方法迫使美国政府立即停止使用细菌武器和其他大批屠杀人民的武器。世界民主青年联盟号召青年们进行斗争，以期这种武器终被禁止，犯有使用这种武器之罪行的人受到惩罚，并号召青年们起来要求缔结保障人类和平和幸福的和平公约。声明又号召美国青年和平战士们起来制止罪犯们的手，制止他们所奉行的要使美国和全世界青年遭受言语无法形容的痛苦的政策。
