Title: Central Ministry of Internal Affairs Directs Localities to Help Families of Martyrs and Soldiers with Spring Plowing in a Timely Manner
Author:
Date: 1952-03-17
Page: 2

The Central People's Government Ministry of Internal Affairs, in order to ensure that the land of revolutionary martyrs' families and revolutionary servicemen's families is not delayed for spring plowing, has issued instructions to various localities, requiring them to immediately check the arrangement and progress of the substitute plowing work, strengthen the leadership of the village (township) preferential treatment committees or substitute plowing committees, formulate plans, mobilize and organize the masses to do a good job in substitute plowing, solve the difficulties of seeds and fertilizers, and not allow the land of martyrs' families and servicemen's families to be left uncultivated; at the same time, it is also necessary to educate the able-bodied martyrs' families and servicemen's families to actively participate in production. The instruction emphasized: In disaster areas, "more attention should be paid to doing a good job in substitute plowing and organizing the production of martyrs' families and servicemen's families." (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中央内务部指示各地及时帮助烈军属春耕

1952-03-17
第2版()
专栏：

    中央内务部指示各地及时帮助烈军属春耕
    中央人民政府内务部为保证革命烈士家属、革命军人家属的土地不误春耕，特向各地发出指示，要求各地立即检查代耕工作的布置与进行情况，加强对村（乡）优抚委员会或代耕委员会的领导，订出计划，发动与组织群众把代耕工作做好，解决籽种与肥料的困难，不使烈属、军属荒芜一亩地；同时也要教育有劳动力的烈属、军属积极参加生产。指示强调指出：在灾区“更应注意做好代耕工作与组织烈属、军属生产”。（新华社）
