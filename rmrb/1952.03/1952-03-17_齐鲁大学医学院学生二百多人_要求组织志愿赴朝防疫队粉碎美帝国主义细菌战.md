Title: More than 200 Students from Cheeloo University Medical School Request to Organize a Volunteer Anti-epidemic Team to Korea to Crush the American Imperialist Germ Warfare
Author:
Date: 1952-03-17
Page: 2

    More than 200 Students from Cheeloo University Medical School
    Request to Organize a Volunteer Anti-epidemic Team to Korea to Crush the American Imperialist Germ Warfare
    The American imperialists are shamelessly conducting inhumane germ warfare in Korea and Northeast China, which we absolutely cannot tolerate. All the students of Cheeloo University Medical School, with boundless excitement and anger, already have 272 people requesting to organize an anti-epidemic team to Korea. We request the relevant leaders to allow us to go to Korea or the Northeast to give the American imperialists a powerful counterattack! We have strong confidence that we can undertake this glorious and great task. Finally, let us shout: Medical personnel across the country, take action and use our unparalleled strength to completely crush the American imperialists' inhumane germ warfare.
    Cheeloo University Medical School Students Volunteer to Resist America and Aid Korea
    Anti-epidemic Team Preparatory Committee

### 齐鲁大学医学院学生二百多人  要求组织志愿赴朝防疫队粉碎美帝国主义细菌战

1952-03-17
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    齐鲁大学医学院学生二百多人
    要求组织志愿赴朝防疫队粉碎美帝国主义细菌战
    美帝国主义者卑鄙无耻地在朝鲜和我国东北进行灭绝人性的细菌战争，我们决不能容忍。我们齐鲁大学医学院全体同学在无比的激动与愤怒下，已有二百七十二人要求组织赴朝防疫队。我们要求有关领导方面，准许我们到朝鲜或东北去，给美帝国主义者一个有力的回击！我们有坚强的信心，能够担当起这个光荣伟大的任务。最后，让我们高呼：全国医学界人士行动起来，以我们无比的力量彻底粉碎美帝国主义的灭绝人性的细菌战争。
    齐鲁大学医学院学生志愿抗美援朝
    防疫队筹委会
