Title: Letters from Soldiers and Trainees of Various PLA Branches: Resolutely Extinguish the Venomous Flames of American Bacteriological Warfare – Summary of Readers' Letters
Author:
Date: 1952-03-30
Page: 2

Letters from Soldiers and Trainees of Various PLA Branches
Resolutely Extinguish the Venomous Flames of American Bacteriological Warfare
— Summary of Readers' Letters

This newspaper continues to receive letters from a broad range of readers from various branches of the People's Liberation Army, denouncing the crime of American imperialism in conducting bacteriological warfare, and expressing their determination to extinguish the bacteria.

The following nine individuals from a certain unit of the People's Air Force, including Xu Gaogong, wrote: "The heinous crime of the American aggressors in spreading bacteria has aroused our boundless anger. We must adhere to our posts, hone our skills, and be ready at any time to respond to the call of the motherland and go to the forefront of Resist America, Aid Korea, to completely eliminate the American bacteriological war criminals." The following twelve soldiers from a certain unit of the People's Navy, including Chen Yunpeng, wrote: "For the sake of our great motherland and people, for the sake of human justice and peace, and for the sake of science and civilization, we express our boundless anger and resolute protest against the heinous crime of American imperialism in spreading bacteria. In addition to striving to improve our technology and defend our sacred and inviolable coastal defense, we are ready at any time to go to the front line of the anti-bacterial warfare and directly strike the American aggressors." All the trainees of the 11th team of a certain tank school wrote: "We are not afraid of the bacteriological warfare waged by American imperialism. We are determined to hone our skills and wait for the call of the motherland at any time to fight to crush the man-eating American aggressors." The following nine soldiers from a certain artillery unit of North China, including Gao Zhenying, wrote: "We have cleaned our cannons and polished our guns, waiting for the call of the motherland. No matter what conspiracies and tricks American imperialism engages in, we have the confidence and ability to defeat it." All the trainees of the fourth team of a certain communications school of the People's Liberation Army wrote: "The American aggressors' launching of bacteriological warfare has aroused our boundless anger. We are determined to strive to complete our study tasks ahead of schedule and be ready to strike the American aggressors at any time." The following twenty-eight people from the Health Department of a certain artillery unit of East China, including Li Huamin, wrote: "We are a group of health workers. We demand to go to the forefront of the anti-bacterial warfare and use our scientific knowledge and strong will to smash the conspiracy of the American aggressors."

### 解放军各兵种战士、学员来信  坚决扑灭美国细菌战的毒焰——读者来信综述

1952-03-30
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    解放军各兵种战士、学员来信
    坚决扑灭美国细菌战的毒焰
    ——读者来信综述
    本报继续收到人民解放军各兵种广大读者的来信，声讨美帝国主义进行细菌战的罪行，并表示扑灭细菌的决心。
    人民空军某部许高恭等九人来信说：“美国侵略者撒布细菌的滔天罪行，激起了我们无比的愤怒。我们一定要坚守工作岗位，练好本领，随时准备着响应祖国的号召，到抗美援朝的最前线去，彻底消灭美国细菌战犯。”人民海军某部战士陈云鹏等十二人来信说：“为了伟大祖国和人民，为了人类的正义与和平，为了科学和文明，我们对美帝国主义撒布细菌的滔天罪行，表示无比的愤怒和坚决的抗议。我们除了努力提高技术，保卫我们神圣的不可侵犯的海防外，我们随时准备到反细菌战的前线去，直接打击美国侵略者。”某战车学校第十一队全体学员来信说：“我们对于美帝国主义进行的细菌战，毫无畏惧。我们决心练好本领，随时等待祖国的号召，为粉碎那吃人生番的美国侵略者而斗争。”华北炮兵某部战士高振英等九人来信说：“我们擦好炮，抹好枪，等待着祖国的召唤。不管美帝国主义搞什么阴谋诡计，我们都有把握、有信心战胜它。”解放军某通讯学校第四队全体学员来信说：“美国侵略者发动细菌战争，激起了我们无比的愤怒。我们决心努力提前完成学习任务，随时准备打击美国侵略者。”华东炮兵某部卫生部李华民等二十八人来信说：“我们是一批卫生工作者。我们要求到反细菌战的最前线去，用我们的科学知识和坚强的意志，粉碎美国侵略者的阴谋。”
