Marching (Sketches from the Korean Battlefield, No. 8)
Shao Yu
    We are marching on the snowy fields of Korea,
    Korean children and mothers stand before the ruins;
    There is no need to ask where the children's fathers have gone,
    They have gone to join the Korean People's Army.
    From the children and hardworking mothers,
    We see the stubbornness and resilience of the Korean people,
    They look at us with hope, confidence, and pride;
    We know why we left our beloved motherland,
    Every ruin tells us—go and resolutely eliminate the enemy!

### 行军（朝鲜战场素描之八）（图片）
邵宇
1952-03-30
第3版()
专栏：

    行军（朝鲜战场素描之八）
    邵宇
    我们在朝鲜的雪地上行军，
    废墟前站立着朝鲜的孩子和母亲；
    用不着再问孩子的爸爸到那儿去啦，
    他们去参加了朝鲜人民军。
    从孩子和勤劳的母亲身上，
    看到了朝鲜人民的倔强和坚忍，
    他们用希望、信心和骄傲望着我们；
    我们知道为什么要离开亲爱的祖国，
    每一块废墟都告诉我们——去坚决地消灭敌人！
