Title: New Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Kousal, Arrives in Beijing Yesterday; Mr. Weiskopf Recalled
Author:
Date: 1952-03-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 15th: Mr. Weiskopf, the first Czechoslovak Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, was recalled on March 11th. The Government of the Czechoslovak Republic has now appointed Mr. Kousal as the Czechoslovak Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China.

Ambassador Kousal arrived in Beijing by plane at 5:00 PM on the 15th. Those who went to the airport to welcome him included Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Wei Baoshan, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department; Wu Qing, Specialist of the Soviet and Eastern European Affairs Department; Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; and Zeng Mei, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command. Also present were Soviet Ambassador to China, Mr. Roshchin; Romanian Ambassador to China, Mr. Rudenko; Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic to China with the rank of Ambassador, Mr. König; Mongolian Ambassador to China, Mr. Jalkhasaikhan; Hungarian Ambassador to China, Mr. Saffronko; Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Mr. Petkov; Burmese Ambassador to China, Mr. U Law Maung; Swiss Minister to China, Mr. Renzo Lardelli; Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy, Mr. Isa Matari; Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy, Mr. Dodin; Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Delegation, Mr. Zhou Liang; Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Korean Embassy, Mr. Choi Young; Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Danish Legation, Mr. Mejstrup; Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Swedish Embassy, Mr. Befvenstedt; Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Finnish Legation, Mr. Brongstedt; Counselor of the Indian Embassy, Mr. Gore; and Counselor of the Pakistani Embassy, Mr. Ahmed Ali. Mr. Jabroski, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Czechoslovak Embassy, and all members of the embassy also went to welcome him.

### 捷新任驻我国大使  康萨拉昨天抵北京  魏斯柯普夫已奉调返国

1952-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

    捷新任驻我国大使
    康萨拉昨天抵北京
    魏斯柯普夫已奉调返国
    【新华社十五日讯】捷克斯洛伐克首任驻华特命全权大使魏斯柯普夫已在三月十一日奉调返国，捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府现已任命康萨拉为捷克斯洛伐克驻我国特命全权大使。
    康萨拉大使在十五日下午五时乘飞机抵京。赴机场欢迎的有中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权、交际处副处长魏宝善、苏联东欧司专员吴青，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，京津卫戍司令部副参谋长曾美。此外还有苏联驻华大使罗申，罗马尼亚驻华大使鲁登科，德意志民主共和国驻华外交使团大使衔团长柯尼希，蒙古驻华大使贾尔卡赛汗，匈牙利驻华大使夏法朗柯，保加利亚驻华大使彼得科夫，缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂，瑞士驻华公使任佐立，印度尼西亚大使馆临时代办伊沙·玛蒂，波兰大使馆临时代办道定，越南代表团临时代办周亮，朝鲜大使馆临时代办崔英，丹麦公使馆临时代办迈思妥，瑞典大使馆临时代办贝凡，芬兰公使馆临时代办勃朗斯坦特，印度大使馆参赞高尔，巴基斯坦大使馆参赞阿默德·艾礼等。捷克斯洛伐克大使馆临时代办雅布罗斯基及全体馆员亦前往欢迎。
