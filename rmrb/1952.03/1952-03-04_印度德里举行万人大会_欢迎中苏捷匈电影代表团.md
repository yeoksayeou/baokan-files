Title: Ten Thousand Person Rally Held in Delhi, India to Welcome Film Delegations from China, the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia and Hungary
Author:
Date: 1952-03-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 3rd: According to TASS, a ten thousand person rally was held in Delhi, India on February 26th to welcome the film delegations from the Soviet Union, China, Czechoslovakia and Hungary who were participating in the Indian International Film Exhibition. The rally was jointly held by the Democratic Women's Federation, the Democratic Youth League, the Delhi General Labor Union, the Progressive Writers Association, and the Soviet Union Friendship Association.

    Sharma, the representative of the Delhi General Labor Union, expressed sincere respect to the film delegations from various countries on behalf of the hundreds of thousands of workers in Delhi. He said: "Whether it is the film works of the Soviet Union or the People's Republic of China, they are a symbol of great hope for us."

### 印度德里举行万人大会  欢迎中苏捷匈电影代表团

1952-03-04
第4版()
专栏：

    印度德里举行万人大会
    欢迎中苏捷匈电影代表团
    【新华社三日讯】据塔斯社讯：二月二十六日印度德里曾举行了一个一万人的大会，欢迎参加印度国际电影展览会的苏联、中国、捷克斯洛伐克和匈牙利的电影代表团。这个大会是由民主妇女联合会、民主青年联盟、德里总工会、进步作家协会和苏联之友协会联合举行的。
    德里总工会代表夏尔马代表德里数十万工人，向各国电影代表团表示热诚的敬意。他说：“无论是苏联或是中华人民共和国的电影作品，对于我们都是一个伟大希望的象征。”
