Title: Order of the Ministry of Finance of the Central People's Government on Revising the Adjustment Range of Commodity Tax Valuation
Author:
Date: 1952-03-15
Page: 2

Based on the Order No. (52) Cai Jing Mi Ji (Cai) 51 of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council on March 4, 1952, which was approved by the Government Administration Council: Article 6, Paragraph 2 of the Provisional Regulations on Commodity Tax is amended to change the valuation adjustment range from "10 percent" to "5 percent", effective from March 15, 1952. In accordance with the above order, Article 18, Paragraph 7 and Article 19 of the Detailed Rules for the Implementation of the Provisional Regulations on Commodity Tax, concerning the valuation adjustment range of "10 percent", are hereby amended to "5 percent", and will be effective from March 15, 1952. It is hoped that all local tax authorities and relevant public and private manufacturers will comply with and implement this order. For this purpose.

This order,
Minister Bo Yibo
Deputy Ministers Rong Zihe
Wang Shao'ao

### 中央人民政府财政部  关于修改货物税评价调整幅度的命令

1952-03-15
第2版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府财政部
    关于修改货物税评价调整幅度的命令
    奉政务院财政经济委员会一九五二年三月四日（52）财经密计（财）字第五十一号命令，以奉政务院批准：将货物税暂行条例第六条第二款内的评价调整幅度“百分之十”修改为“百分之五”，自一九五二年三月十五日起实行。等语；根据上项命令，兹将货物税暂行条例施行细则第十八条第七款及第十九条内有关评价调整幅度“百分之十”的规定，一并修改为“百分之五”，并自一九五二年三月十五日起实行。希各地税务机关及有关公私厂商一体遵照执行。为要。
    此令部长　薄一波  副部长　戎子和
    王绍鏊
