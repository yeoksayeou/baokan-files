Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-03-06
Page: 1

Our great motherland
　　Zheng Xikun's locomotive crew, a national railway model worker group, having completed the annual transportation task ahead of schedule on December 20th of last year, is now striving with a high degree of patriotic enthusiasm to complete the new transportation task for 1952. This is the scene of Zheng Xikun's crew intensifying their work after writing a letter challenging the locomotive maintenance crews across the country. All crew members are inspecting the locomotives and strengthening their maintenance.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    全国铁路劳动模范郑锡坤机车包乘组，在去年十二月二十日提前完成全年运输任务后，正以高度的爱国热情为完成一九五二年新的运输任务而奋斗。这是郑锡坤包车组写信向全国铁路机务段包车组挑战后加紧工作的情形。全组的乘务员们都正在检查机车和加强机车的保养工作。
