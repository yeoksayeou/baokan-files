Title: Government Administration Council's 129th Meeting Passes Instructions on This Year's Water Conservancy Work, etc.
Author:
Date: 1952-03-28
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 27th—The 129th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 21st. After listening to the report on water conservancy work given by Fu Zuoyi, Minister of the Ministry of Water Conservancy, the meeting passed instructions on water conservancy work for 1952. Subsequently, after listening to the explanation by Peng Zhen, Deputy Director of the Political and Legal Committee of the Government Administration Council, the meeting passed regulations on the establishment of people's courts during the "Five-Anti" campaign.

### 政务院第一二九次会议  通过今年水利工作的指示等

1952-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院第一二九次会议
    通过今年水利工作的指示等
    【新华社二十七日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百二十九次政务会议在二十一日下午举行。会议在听取中央人民政府水利部傅作义部长所作的水利工作报告后，通过了关于一九五二年水利工作的指示。会议继在听取政务院政治法律委员会彭真副主任的说明后，通过了关于“五反”运动中成立人民法庭的规定。
