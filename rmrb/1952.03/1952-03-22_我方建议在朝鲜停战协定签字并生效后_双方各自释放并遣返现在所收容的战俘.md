Title: Our Side Proposes That After the Korean Armistice Agreement is Signed and Takes Effect, Both Sides Should Release and Repatriate All Prisoners of War Currently Held

Author:

Date: 1952-03-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, March 21st: At the staff meeting on the fourth agenda item of the Korean armistice talks on March 21st, in order to seek a concrete solution to the existing differences between the two sides on the fourth agenda item as soon as possible, our side, based on our proposal on March 5th, put forward principled articles on the release and repatriation of prisoners of war held by both sides. The specific suggestion is that after the armistice agreement is signed and takes effect, both sides should release and repatriate the prisoners of war currently held by them. As for the list of the aforementioned prisoners of war, it will be finalized by the staff of both sides. The other side stated that they would express their opinions after studying it.

At the staff meeting on the third agenda item, the two sides entered into discussions on the specific scope of designated ports and reached an agreement on the main lines of communication for neutral nations inspection teams to reach the designated ports of both sides from the headquarters of the Supervisory Commission.

### 我方建议在朝鲜停战协定签字并生效后  双方各自释放并遣返现在所收容的战俘

1952-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我方建议在朝鲜停战协定签字并生效后
    双方各自释放并遣返现在所收容的战俘
    【新华社开城二十一日电】三月二十一日朝鲜停战谈判第四项议程参谋会议上，我方为了求得双方对第四项议程的现存分歧早日获得具体的解决，根据我方三月五日的建议，提出了有关双方释放并遣返其所收容之战俘的原则性的条文，具体建议在停战协定签字并生效后，双方应各自释放并遣返其现在所收容的战俘，至于上述战俘的名单，则由双方参谋人员予以最后校正。对方表示将在研究后表示意见。
    第三项议程参谋会议上，双方进入了特定口岸具体范围的讨论，对中立国视察小组从监察委员会总部到达双方各特定口岸的主要交通线达成了协议。
