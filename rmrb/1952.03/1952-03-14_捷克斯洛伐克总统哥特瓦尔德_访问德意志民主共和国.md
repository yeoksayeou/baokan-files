Title: Czechoslovak President Gottwald Visits the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1952-03-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, March 13th: According to TASS, Czechoslovak Republic President Gottwald, accompanied by members of a government delegation, traveled to Berlin on the 11th to meet with German Democratic Republic President Pieck for a courtesy visit. Also present were German Democratic Republic People's Chamber Chairman Dieckmann, Prime Minister Grotewohl, Deputy Prime Ministers Ulbricht, Nuschke, Bolz, Loch, and Heinrich Rau, other heads of the German Democratic Republic government, members of the National Council of the National Front of Democratic Germany, and leaders of various parties.

Gottwald first delivered a congratulatory speech, followed by President Pieck's reply, saying: This visit coincides with another event of great significance for Germany and the world, namely the Soviet government's proposal regarding the basic principles of a German peace treaty. The Czechoslovak Republic government should also directly participate in the work of convening the peace conference proposed by the Soviet Union. This new peace step by the Soviet government has major decisive significance for peace in Europe. This new peace step gives the friendly visit of the President and government delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic to the German Democratic Republic particularly great significance.

### 捷克斯洛伐克总统哥特瓦尔德  访问德意志民主共和国

1952-03-14
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克总统哥特瓦尔德
    访问德意志民主共和国
    【新华社十三日讯】据塔斯社柏林讯：捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统哥特瓦尔德偕政府代表团团员于十一日前往柏林，会见德意志民主共和国总统皮克，作礼仪上的访问。当时在场的并有德意志民主共和国人民议会主席狄克曼，总理格罗提渥，副总理乌布利希、努舍克、博尔茨、洛赫和亨利·劳，德意志民主共和国政府其他首长，民主德国全国阵线全国委员会委员及各党派领袖。
    哥特瓦尔德首先致祝词，随后由皮克总统致答词说：这次访问和另一个对于德国和全世界都有重大意义的事件巧合，这个事件便是苏联政府提出了关于对德和约基本原则的建议。捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府也应该直接参加召开苏联所建议举行的和会的工作。苏联政府这一个新的和平步骤，对于欧洲的和平有着重大的决定性的关系，这一个新的和平步骤，使得捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统和政府代表团对德意志民主共和国的友好访问有了特别重大的意义。
