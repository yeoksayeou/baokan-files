Title: Premier Zhou Hosts a Banquet for the Southwest Nationalities Visiting Group
Author:
Date: 1952-03-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd: Premier Zhou Enlai hosted a banquet at 6 p.m. on the 23rd to entertain the entire Southwest Nationalities Visiting Group. Accompanying him were Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Huang Yanpei, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council; Peng Zhen and Zhang Xiruo, Deputy Directors of the Political and Legal Committee of the Government Administration Council; Li Weihan, Secretary-General of the Government Administration Council and concurrently Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission; Shao Lizi, Member of the Government Administration Council; Ye Jizhuang, Minister of the Central Trade Department; Li Shucheng, Minister of the Central Agriculture Department; Liu Geping, Deputy Chairman of the Central Nationalities Affairs Commission; Tao Xijin, Secretary-General of the Political and Legal Committee of the Government Administration Council; Su Jinguan, Deputy Minister of the Central Health Department; Wei Que, Deputy Minister of the Central Education Department; Yu Xinqing, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Central People's Government Committee; Zheng Zhenduo, Director of the Bureau of Social Culture of the Central Ministry of Culture, and all members of the Central Nationalities Affairs Commission in Beijing. Premier Zhou Enlai delivered a speech at the meeting. In his speech, Premier Zhou expressed a warm welcome to the Southwest Nationalities Visiting Group for coming to Beijing and gave a concise explanation of national unity and patriotism. After Premier Zhou's speech, Guoji Mugu, an Yi representative, addressed the gathering on behalf of the Southwest Nationalities Visiting Group. He said: "Chairman Mao's great nationalities policy has enabled us to forever escape the disasters of inter-ethnic hatred and mutual slaughter, and embark on a happy road of ethnic equality, mutual assistance, and cooperation. We sincerely thank Chairman Mao. Chairman Mao is the great savior of all our ethnic groups."
    The banquet concluded in a warm, cordial, and pleasant atmosphere.

### 周总理欢宴西南各民族参观团

1952-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理欢宴西南各民族参观团
    【新华社二十三日讯】周恩来总理在二十三日下午六时举宴招待西南各民族参观团全体人员。作陪的有中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈叔通，政务院副总理黄炎培，政务院政治法律委员会副主任彭真、张奚若，政务院秘书长兼民族事务委员会主任委员李维汉，政务委员邵力子，中央贸易部部长叶季壮，中央农业部部长李书城，中央民族事务委员会副主任委员刘格平，政务院政治法律委员会秘书长陶希晋，中央卫生部副部长苏井观，中央教育部副部长韦悫，中央人民政府委员会办公厅副主任余心清，中央文化部社会文化事业管理局局长郑振铎及中央民族事务委员会全体在京委员等。会上，周恩来总理作了讲话。周总理在讲话中，对西南各民族参观团到北京来表示热烈的欢迎，并就民族团结和爱国主义作了扼要说明。周总理讲话后，彝族人士果基木古代表西南各民族参观团致词。他说：“毛主席伟大的民族政策，使我们永远脱离了民族间互相仇视、彼此残杀的灾难，走上了民族平等，互助合作的幸福道路。我们衷心感谢毛主席。毛主席是我们各族人民的大救星。”
    宴会在热烈、亲切、愉快的气氛中结束。
