Title: Smash the American Aggressors' Germ Warfare

Author: 
Date: 1952-03-21
Page: 2

Reader's Letters

    Smash the American Aggressors' Germ Warfare
    We unanimously express our boundless indignation at the towering crime of the American aggressor's aircraft frantically spreading bacteria in our Northeast region and the Korean front. We have vivid memories of the tragic fact that Japanese imperialism used bacteria to massacre our people in the past, and now American imperialism is inhumanely launching a new germ warfare. We will never allow the bloody history to repeat itself, and we vow to express our protest and anger with practical actions. We must continue to deepen the movement to resist America and aid Korea, and obey the needs of the motherland, going to the positions where we are needed at any time, to completely crush the enemy's arrogant conspiracy to carry out germ warfare.
    All staff members of the Department of Public Health, Peking University School of Medicine

### 粉碎美国侵略军的细菌战

1952-03-21
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    粉碎美国侵略军的细菌战
    我们对于美国侵略军的飞机在我东北地区和朝鲜前线疯狂撒布细菌的滔天罪行，一致表示无比的愤恨。过去日本帝国主义利用细菌屠杀我国人民的惨痛事实，我们记忆犹新，而美帝国主义又在灭绝人性地发动新的细菌战争了。我们决不允许血腥的历史重演，我们誓以实际行动来表示我们的抗议和愤怒，我们要继续深入开展抗美援朝运动，并服从祖国的需要，随时到需要我们的岗位上去，彻底粉碎敌人进行细菌战的狂妄阴谋。
    北京大学医学院公共卫生学系全体工作人员
