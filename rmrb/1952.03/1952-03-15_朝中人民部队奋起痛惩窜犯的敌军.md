Title: The Korean and Chinese People’s Forces Resolutely Punish the Enemy Troops Who Fled and Invaded

Author:
Date: 1952-03-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, March 14th: Our front-line reporter reports: The Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers are deeply angered by the crimes of the American aggressors in spreading bacteria, and are rising up to severely punish the enemy troops who have fled and invaded our army's positions. On the morning of March 2nd, two platoons of enemy troops fled to the Gaoyangbao area southwest of ShuoNing and were ambushed by our army, resulting in more than 40 enemy casualties. On the night of the 3rd, an enemy platoon fled to Lou Tai, north of Gaolangpu-ri, and its vanguard squad was completely wiped out by our pre-ambushed forces. At 2:00 AM on the 5th, an enemy company continuously fled towards a certain high ground northwest of Wendengli three times but were repelled by us each time. On the night of the 6th, an enemy squad fled to the Jiapuli area northwest of Jinhua and were completely annihilated by our outpost forces. On the night of the 7th, an enemy platoon ambushed a certain position of our army northwest of Gaowangli but were repelled by us. On the morning of the 10th, a group of enemy troops fled to a mountain east of Wendengli and were routed by our army.

### 朝中人民部队奋起痛惩窜犯的敌军

1952-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

    朝中人民部队奋起痛惩窜犯的敌军
    【新华社朝鲜前线十四日电】本社前线记者报道：朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军为美国侵略者撒布细菌的罪行深深激怒，奋起痛惩向我军阵地窜犯的敌军。三月二日晨，敌军两个排窜至朔宁西南高阳堡地区，遭我军伏击，毙伤敌军四十多人。三日夜，敌军一个排向高浪浦里以北的楼垈窜犯，其一个先头班被我预伏部队全部歼灭。五日二时，敌军一个连向我文登里西北某高地连续窜犯三次，都被我击退。六日晚，敌军一个班窜至金化西北地甲里地区，被我前哨部队全部歼灭，七日夜，敌军一个排偷袭高旺里西北我军某阵地，被我击退。十日清晨，敌军一股窜至文登里以东一个山头，被我军击溃。
