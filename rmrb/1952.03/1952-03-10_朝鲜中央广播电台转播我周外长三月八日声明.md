Title: Korean Central Broadcasting Station Broadcasts Our Foreign Minister Zhou's March 8th Statement
Author:
Date: 1952-03-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang Dispatch: The Korean Central Broadcasting Station broadcast on the 8th and 9th my Central People's Government Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's solemn protest against the U.S. government's use of bacteriological weapons to slaughter Chinese people and violate Chinese airspace.
