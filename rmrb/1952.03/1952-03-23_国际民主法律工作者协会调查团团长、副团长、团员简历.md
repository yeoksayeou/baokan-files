Title: Biographies of the Head, Deputy Head, and Members of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers Investigation Mission
Author:
Date: 1952-03-23
Page: 4

    Biographies of the Head, Deputy Head, and Members of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers Investigation Mission
    Head: Brandweiner (Austria), Doctor of Law, Professor of International Law at the University of Graz, Austria, Member of the World Peace Council, Member of the Council of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers.
    Deputy Head: Cavallari (Italy), Lawyer of the Italian Supreme Court, former commander of the guerrillas, Vice Chairman of the National Association of Guerrilla Fighters, member of the Socialist Party.
    Member: De Britto (Brazil), Lawyer of the Rio de Janeiro Court, Secretary of the Brazilian Association of Democratic Lawyers.
    Gaster (United Kingdom), Lawyer, Member of the Haldane Society of Socialist Lawyers, Member of the Executive Committee of the London Peace Council.
    Gaskay (France), Lawyer of the Paris Court, Secretary of the Progressive Association, Member of the French National Council for the Defense of Peace.
    Ke Bonian (China), Director of the China New Law Research Association, Director of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Director of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers.
    Morens (Belgium), Lawyer in Brussels, the capital of Belgium, graduated from the Journalism Department of the University of Brussels, Secretary of the Belgian Branch of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers.
    Wasilewska (Poland), Judge of the Supreme Court, Member of the World Peace Council, Vice Chairman of the Polish Women's League.

### 国际民主法律工作者协会调查团团长、副团长、团员简历

1952-03-23
第4版()
专栏：

    国际民主法律工作者协会调查团团长、副团长、团员简历
    团长：布兰德魏纳（奥地利），法学博士，奥地利格拉夫大学国际法教授，世界和平理事会理事，国际民主法律工作者协会理事会理事。
    副团长：卡瓦莱里（意大利），意大利高等法院律师，前游击队司令，全国游击队员协会副主席，社会党党员。
    团员：狄布里托（巴西），里约热内卢法院律师，巴西民主法律工作者协会书记。
    加斯特尔（英国），律师，霍尔登社会主义法律工作者协会会员，伦敦和平委员会执行委员会委员。
    贾斯盖（法国），巴黎法院律师，进步人士协会书记，法国全国保卫和平理事会理事。
    柯柏年（中国），中国新法学研究会理事，中国人民外交学会理事，国际民主法律工作者协会理事。
    莫伦斯（比利时），比利时首都—布鲁塞尔律师，布鲁塞尔大学新闻系毕业，国际民主法律工作者协会比利时分会书记。
    华西尔考夫斯卡（波兰），最高法院法官，世界和平理事会理事，波兰妇女联合会副主席。
