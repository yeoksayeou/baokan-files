Title: Korean Negotiations: Staff Meeting on the Third Agenda Continues Discussion on the Specific Scope of Rear Area Ports
Author:
Date: 1952-03-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, March 24th:
    The staff meeting on the fourth agenda item of the Korean armistice negotiations on March 24th was delayed by three hours at the request of the other side. At the meeting, the two sides exchanged views on the scope and form of future staff meetings.
    At the staff meeting on the third agenda item, the two sides continued to discuss the specific scope of the rear area ports. Our side agreed to the other side's revised proposal regarding the main transportation lines for the neutral nations inspection teams from the headquarters of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission to the specific ports of both sides.

### 朝鲜谈判第三项议程参谋会议  继续讨论后方口岸具体范围

1952-03-25
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜谈判第三项议程参谋会议
    继续讨论后方口岸具体范围
    【新华社开城二十四日电】三月二十四日朝鲜停战谈判第四项议程参谋会议由于对方的要求，延迟三小时开会，双方在会上就今后参谋会议讨论的范围和形式交换了意见。
    第三项议程参谋会议上，双方继续就后方口岸的具体范围问题进行了讨论。我方同意了对方经过修正后而提出的中立国视察小组自中立国监察委员会总部到双方各特定口岸的主要交通线。
