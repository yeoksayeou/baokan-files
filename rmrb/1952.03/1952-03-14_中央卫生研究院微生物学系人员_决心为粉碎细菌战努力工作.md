Title: Personnel of the Microbiology Department of the Central Institute of Health Resolve to Work Hard to Crush Bacteriological Warfare
Author: Fang Gang
Date: 1952-03-14
Page: 2

Column: Reader's Letter

    Personnel of the Microbiology Department of the Central Institute of Health
    Resolve to Work Hard to Crush Bacteriological Warfare

    Comrade Editor: More than 140 working comrades at the Central Institute of Health have already signed up, requesting to go to the Northeast or Korea to participate in the work of eradicating germs and poisonous insects. Our Microbiology Department has united with the Parasitology Department to strengthen the research on defensive techniques. Personnel from both departments are working day and night in the laboratory, using selfless spirit and practical actions to crush the aggressive conspiracy of American imperialism and to fight for the defense of world peace and the defense of mankind!

    Microbiology Department of the Central Institute of Health Fang Gang

### 中央卫生研究院微生物学系人员  决心为粉碎细菌战努力工作
方纲
1952-03-14
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    中央卫生研究院微生物学系人员
    决心为粉碎细菌战努力工作
    编辑同志：中央卫生研究院已经有一百四十多工作同志签名，要求赴东北或朝鲜参加扑灭病菌和毒虫的工作。我们微生物学系已经和寄生虫学系团结在一起，加强防御技术的研究，我们两系的人员正在不分昼夜地在实验室中工作着，用忘我的精神和实际的行动来粉碎美帝国主义的侵略阴谋，为保卫世界和平，保卫人类而奋斗！
    中央卫生研究院微生物学系  方纲
