Title: Korean Armistice Talks Made No Progress Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-03-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, March 23rd—At the staff meeting on the fourth item of the agenda of the Korean armistice talks on March 23, the other party was still studying and considering the principle clauses put forward by our side on the 21st, and could not put forward specific opinions, so today's meeting made no progress.

At the staff meeting on the third item of the agenda, the two sides continued to exchange views on the specific scope of the rear area ports.

### 朝鲜停战谈判昨日无进展

1952-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判昨日无进展
    【新华社开城二十三日电】三月二十三日朝鲜停战谈判第四项议程参谋会议上，因对方对我方二十一日所提的原则条文尚在研究考虑，不能提出具体意见，今日会议无进展。
    第三项议程参谋会议上，双方继续就后方口岸具体范围的问题交换了意见。
