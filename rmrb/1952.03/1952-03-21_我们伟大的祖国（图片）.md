    Our Great Motherland
    The worker masses in many state-owned factories and enterprises have seen their production enthusiasm further increased in the struggle against corruption, waste, and bureaucracy. The workers of the locomotive section of the Nankou Railway Factory not only exposed and captured major corrupt elements, but also overfulfilled their locomotive task for January by 115.06%. This is a scene of their group discussing and drafting anti-corruption and production competition challenge letters.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-03-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    许多国营工厂企业的工人群众，在反对贪污浪费和反对官僚主义的斗争中，生产积极性也更加提高了。南口铁路工厂机车所的工人们不但检举和捕捉了大贪污犯，并在一月份超额完成机车任务百分之一百一十五·○六。这是他们的小组在议订反贪污和生产竞赛挑战书的情形。
