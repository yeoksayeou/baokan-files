Title: All-China Women's Federation Hosts Female Members of the Southwest Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation
Author:
Date: 1952-03-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 28th:

    The All-China Women's Federation held a tea party on the 28th to host all the female members of the Southwest Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation. At the meeting, Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, extended a warm welcome to all the delegation members. She briefly introduced the international and domestic situation and the situation of women's organizations, and called on representatives of all ethnic groups to vigorously publicize the policies of the People's Government and the current situation after returning, unite the women masses, and assist the government in doing a good job in ethnic minority areas. Luo Qiong, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Women's Federation, introduced the various policies and laws of the People's Government protecting women, and explained that due to the government's implementation of the policy of equality between men and women, women have made outstanding achievements and contributions in various fields. Subsequently, Yi ethnic group representative Yi Mu Niu Niu, Miao ethnic group representative Yang Guangqin, and Sani ethnic group representative Luo Shiying reported on the living, production, and marriage conditions of women from various ethnic groups in the Southwest. Yi ethnic group representative Wang Yufen also stated on behalf of women from various ethnic groups in the Southwest: Women from various ethnic groups in the Southwest urgently demand to learn culture and improve maternal and child health. In their talks, they all unanimously stated that the Communist Party and Chairman Mao have liberated them from a life of being insulted and enslaved, and have given them political rights and equality between men and women. They sincerely thank the Communist Party and Chairman Mao.

### 中华全国民主妇女联合会  招待西南各族参观团女团员

1952-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中华全国民主妇女联合会
    招待西南各族参观团女团员
    【新华社二十八日讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会在二十八日举行茶会，招待西南各民族参观团全体女团员。会上，全国妇联副主席邓颖超向全体团员表示热烈的欢迎。她简略地介绍了国际、国内的形势和妇女组织的情况，号召各民族代表回去以后，要大力宣传人民政府的政策和目前的形势，团结妇女群众，协助政府做好少数民族地区的工作。全国妇联副秘书长罗琼介绍了人民政府保护妇女的各项政策法令，并说明由于政府实施了男女平等的政策，妇女在各方面都有了卓越的成就和贡献。接着，彝族代表依姆妞妞、苗族代表杨光琴、撒尼族代表罗世英分别报告了西南各民族妇女的生活、生产和婚姻情况。彝族代表王玉芬并代表西南各族妇女提出：西南各族妇女迫切要求学习文化和改善妇婴卫生。她们在谈话中都一致表示：共产党和毛主席使她们脱离了被侮辱被奴役的生活，得到了政治上的权利和男女平等，她们衷心感谢共产党和毛主席。
