Title: All-China Women's Federation Slogans Commemorating "March 8th" International Women's Day
Author:
Date: 1952-03-02
Page: 1
    All-China Women's Federation
    Slogans Commemorating "March 8th" International Women's Day

    1. Women, mobilize! Actively participate in the movement against corruption, waste, and bureaucracy and the movement against bribery, tax evasion, theft of state assets, shoddy workmanship and cutting corners, and theft of state economic intelligence. Under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and the working class, resolutely repel the bourgeoisie's rampant attacks, and consolidate the leadership of the working class from all aspects!
    2. Women, mobilize! Strive to participate in various production and construction projects, practice strict economy, enthusiastically support the Chinese People's Volunteers, and continue to strengthen the work of resisting America and aiding Korea!
    3. Women, unite! Actively participate in the country's political, economic, and cultural construction undertakings, fight for your legitimate rights, oppose those who violate the Marriage Law and disregard the interests of women and children, and strive for the complete liberation of women!
    4. Female workers, female shop assistants, and female staff members! Family members of workers and staff and other laborers! Actively participate in the movement against corruption, waste, and bureaucracy and the movement against bribery, tax evasion, theft of state assets, shoddy workmanship and cutting corners, and theft of state economic intelligence, and bravely expose the crimes of illegal capitalists and corrupt elements!
    5. Rural women! Work hard in spring plowing, actively participate in mutual aid teams and cooperatives, learn and improve production techniques, and strive for increased agricultural production!
    6. Women in industry and commerce! Faithfully support and participate in the movement against corruption, waste, and bureaucracy and the movement against bribery, tax evasion, theft of state assets, shoddy workmanship and cutting corners, and theft of state economic intelligence, thoroughly confess, or mobilize your relatives and friends to confess, criminal acts!
    7. Female students! Eliminate the bourgeoisie's decadent ideas, arm yourselves with proletarian thought, establish and consolidate a world view of serving the people, study hard, and prepare to participate in the country's construction undertakings!
    8. Female educators, eliminate bourgeois educational viewpoints and do a good job in people's education!
    9. Female literary and art workers, liquidate bourgeois ideology and implement the literary and art work policy of serving workers, peasants, and soldiers!
    10. Female medical and health workers, and child welfare workers, eliminate bourgeois ideology and strive to improve the health of the broad masses of working women and their children!
    11. Women workers, resolutely oppose bureaucracy that is divorced from working women and divorced from reality, closely connect with the masses of women, care about the suffering of working women, and fight to protect the rights and interests of women and children and strive for the complete liberation of women!
    12. Learn from Soviet women, cherish the motherland, actively participate in national construction, and safeguard the shining example of world peace!
    13. Warmly support the call of the Women's International Democratic Federation to defend world peace and protect the rights and interests of women and children!
    14. Actively welcome the upcoming International Conference for the Defence of Children!
    15. Women of the world unite, oppose wars of aggression, and defend world peace!
    16. Long live the Chinese Communist Party!
    17. Long live the People's Republic of China!
    18. Long live the Chinese People's Volunteers! Long live the Chinese People's Liberation Army!
    19. Long live Chairman Mao Zedong, the wise leader of the Chinese people!
    20. Long live Marshal Stalin, the teacher of all mankind in the world!
    21. Long live women's liberation!
    22. Long live the great unity of women all over the world!

### 中华全国民主妇女联合会  纪念“三八”国际妇女节的口号

1952-03-02
第1版()
专栏：

    中华全国民主妇女联合会
    纪念“三八”国际妇女节的口号
    一、妇女们动员起来，积极参加反贪污、反浪费、反官僚主义运动和反行贿、反偷税漏税、反盗窃国家资财、反偷工减料、反盗窃国家经济情报运动，在中国共产党和工人阶级领导下，坚决打退资产阶级的猖狂进攻，从各方面巩固工人阶级的领导权！
    二、妇女们动员起来，努力参加各种生产建设，厉行节约，热烈支援中国人民志愿军，继续加强抗美援朝工作！
    三、妇女们团结起来，积极参加祖国的政治、经济、文化的各种建设事业，争取自己的合法权利，反对违反婚姻法和漠视妇女儿童利益的分子，为争取妇女彻底解放而奋斗！
    四、女工、女店员、女职员们！职工家属及其他劳动者家属们！积极参加反贪污、反浪费、反官僚主义运动和反行贿、反偷税漏税、反盗窃国家资财、反偷工减料、反盗窃国家经济情报运动，勇敢检举不法资本家和贪污分子的罪行！
    五、农村妇女们！努力春耕，积极参加互助组、合作社，学习和提高生产技术，争取农业增产！
    六、工商界妇女们！要忠诚拥护和参加反贪污、反浪费、反官僚主义运动和反行贿、反偷税漏税、反盗窃国家资财、反偷工减料、反盗窃国家经济情报运动，彻底坦白或动员自己的亲友坦白犯罪的行为！
    七、女学生们！要清除资产阶级的腐朽思想，用无产阶级思想武装自己，建立和巩固为人民服务的人生观，努力学习，准备参加祖国的建设事业！
    八、女教育工作者，要清除资产阶级教育观点，做好人民教育工作！
    九、女文艺工作者，要清算资产阶级思想，贯彻为工农兵服务的文艺工作方针！
    十、女医药卫生工作者，儿童福利工作者，要清除资产阶级思想，为增进广大劳动妇女及其子女的健康而努力！
    十一、妇女工作者，要坚决反对脱离劳动妇女，脱离实际的官僚主义，密切联系妇女群众，关心劳动妇女的疾苦，为保护妇女儿童权益，争取妇女彻底解放而斗争！
    十二、学习苏联妇女，热爱祖国，积极参加祖国建设，保卫世界和平的光辉榜样！
    十三、热烈拥护国际民主妇女联合会保卫世界和平，保卫妇女和儿童权益的号召！
    十四、积极迎接即将召开的国际保卫儿童会议！
    十五、全世界妇女团结起来，反对侵略战争，保卫世界和平！
    十六、中国共产党万岁！
    十七、中华人民共和国万岁！
    十八、中国人民志愿军万岁！中国人民解放军万岁！
    十九、中国人民的英明领袖毛泽东主席万岁！
    二十、全世界人类的导师斯大林大元帅万岁！
    二十一、妇女解放万岁！
    二十二、全世界妇女大团结万岁！
