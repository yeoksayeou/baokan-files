Title: All-China Youth Federation and All-China Students' Federation Demand that Young People and Students Worldwide Stop U.S. Germ Warfare Crimes
Author: Tian Demin
Date: 1952-03-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 11th: The All-China Youth Federation and the All-China Students' Federation Demand that Young People and Students Worldwide Stop U.S. Germ Warfare Crimes

    Xinhua News Agency, March 11th: The All-China Youth Federation sent a telegram to the Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, requesting all young peace fighters around the world to take active action to stop the crimes of germ warfare being carried out by the U.S. aggressors. The telegram is as follows: To the Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth and to all peace-loving youth around the world:

    After the U.S. invading army launched a large-scale germ warfare in Korea, it again invaded Northeast China with 68 batches of planes, 448 sorties, starting from February 29th, spreading a large number of germ-carrying insects and indiscriminately bombing. We demand that all young peace fighters around the world take active action to jointly stop this insane and brutal crime of the U.S. aggressors.

    All-China Youth Federation
    March 11th

    Xinhua News Agency, March 11th: Tian Demin, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, sent a telegram to Grosman, Chairman of the International Union of Students, requesting all students around the world to unanimously stop the U.S. aggressors from carrying out germ warfare crimes. The telegram is as follows: To Comrade Grosman, Chairman of the International Union of Students:

    After the U.S. invading troops in Korea conducted germ warfare in Korea, they recently sent planes to invade our Northeast airspace, spreading a large number of germ-carrying poisonous insects and carrying out bombing and strafing. The people and students of our country are all extremely angry and demand that students all over the world unanimously and resolutely stop this crime that blatantly violates international law and seriously endangers human safety.

    Tian Demin, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation
    March 11th

### 我全国民主青联总会和全国学联  要求全世界青年、学生制止美国细菌战罪行
田德民
1952-03-12
第4版()
专栏：

    我全国民主青联总会和全国学联
    要求全世界青年、学生制止美国细菌战罪行
    【新华社十一日讯】中华全国民主青年联合总会致电世界民主青年联盟书记处，要求全世界青年和平战士积极行动制止美国侵略者进行细菌战的罪行。电文如下：世界民主青年联盟书记处并转全世界爱好和平的青年：
    美国侵略军在朝鲜发动了大规模的细菌战之后，又从二月二十九日起以飞机六十八批、四百四十八架次侵入我国东北，撒布大量传播细菌的昆虫并滥施轰炸。我们要求全世界青年和平战士采取积极行动，共同制止美国侵略者这种疯狂的残暴罪行。
    中华全国民主青年联合总会
    三月十一日
    【新华社十一日讯】中华全国学生联合会主席田德民致电国际学生联合会主席格罗曼，要求全世界学生一致制止美国侵略者进行细菌战的罪行。电文如下：国际学生联合会主席格罗曼同志：
    美国侵朝军队在朝鲜进行细菌战争以后，最近竟又派遣飞机侵入我东北领空，撒布大量传播细菌的毒虫并进行轰炸、扫射。我国全国人民和学生都无比愤怒，要求全世界学生一致坚决制止这种公然破坏国际公法和严重危害人类安全的罪行。
    中华全国学生联合会主席　田德民
    三月十一日
