Title: Lhasa Military and Civilians Celebrate the Tibetan New Year
Author:
Date: 1952-03-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 1st: On February 26th, the first day of the Tibetan New Year, all monks, laypeople and the People's Liberation Army stationed in Lhasa celebrated the festival with great joy. On this day, the brilliant and beautiful national flags fluttered everywhere in Lhasa. Lhasa citizens, lamas from various monasteries, and farmers and herdsmen from the city's suburbs and pastures were out and about in the city in groups. Young Tibetan men and women dressed in festive costumes freely formed many singing and dancing troupes to perform in the streets. Commanders and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Lhasa celebrated the festival with the people of Lhasa according to Tibetan customs. They put on dramas and showed movies at their garrisons and held get-togethers with Tibetan compatriots. Groups of Tibetans were seen reading wall newspapers written in both Tibetan and Chinese posted on the streets by various Lhasa organizations.
The day before the New Year, the Dalai Lama sent his personal representative, the chamberlain Palha, to pay respects to Zhang Jingwu, representative of the Central People's Government, and Zhang Guohua, commander of the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and others; and sent various New Year gifts. Before the New Year, the people of all ethnic groups in Lhasa enthusiastically launched a patriotic donation campaign to support the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and had donated more than 10 million yuan by February 26th.

### 拉萨军民欢度藏历新年

1952-03-04
第1版()
专栏：

    拉萨军民欢度藏历新年
    【新华社拉萨一日电】二月二十六日，是藏历正月初一。拉萨全体僧俗人民和驻拉萨的人民解放军在这天以极愉快的心情，欢度佳节。这一天，拉萨市区到处飘扬着灿烂美丽的国旗。拉萨市民和各寺院的喇嘛以及市郊农村和牧场的农、牧民们，都成群结队地在市区游玩。穿着节日盛装的藏族男女青年们，自由组成了许多唱游队在街头演出。人民解放军驻拉萨部队的指挥员和战斗员按照西藏风俗习惯和拉萨人民一起欢度节日。他们在驻地演出戏剧，放映电影和藏胞联欢。成群的藏民在看拉萨各机关贴在街头的用藏、汉文字写出的墙报。
    新年前一天，达赖喇嘛派私人代表侍从官长帕拉向中央人民政府代表张经武将军和中国人民解放军西藏军区司令员张国华将军等致敬；并送贺年礼品多种。拉萨的各族人民在新年之前，热烈展开爱国捐献运动，支援中国人民志愿军，到二月二十六日止已捐献一千多万元。
