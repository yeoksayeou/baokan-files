Title: Investigation Team of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers Arrives in Shenyang; All Circles in Northeast China Warmly Welcome the Investigation Team to Expose the Crimes of American Aggressors in Conducting Germ Warfare
Author:
Date: 1952-03-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, March 26th: The investigation team of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, which came to Northeast China to investigate the crimes of American imperialism in spreading bacteria-carrying insects, arrived in Shenyang on the 26th. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Gao Chongmin, Chairman of the Northeast Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression and President of the Northeast Branch of the Supreme People's Court of the Central People's Government; Liu Zhiming, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the Northeast Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Ning Wu, Chairman of the Northeast Provisional Working Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang; Guan Mengjue, Secretary-General of the Northeast General Branch of the China Democratic League; Che Xiangchen, Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Shenyang Branch of the China Association for Promoting Democracy; Wang Bin, Minister of Health of the Northeast People's Government; Bai Xiqing, Deputy Minister; Zhang Weizhen, Chairman of the Northeast Federation of Trade Unions; Zhang Ze, Secretary of the Northeast Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League; Guo Mingqiu, Director of the Northeast Democratic Women's Federation; Jiao Ruoyu, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang; representatives of journalists in the Northeast region, and others.

    After the investigation team got off the train, they were accompanied by the welcoming personnel to the Northeast People's Government Guesthouse and held a brief discussion. At the meeting, Gao Chongmin delivered a welcome speech on behalf of the Northeast Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression and legal workers in the Northeast region, expressing warm welcome and high respect to the investigation team. After praising the investigation team's righteous actions in investigating the various crimes committed by the American aggressor army and its accomplices in Korea, he said: "American imperialism is not only conducting germ warfare on the Korean front and in the rear, but also spreading a large number of bacteria-carrying insects in various parts of our Northeast China. This inhuman crime of American imperialism is a serious destruction of human justice, international conventions, scientific civilization, and a serious threat to the survival of all mankind. Our people are determined and confident to smash this beastly germ warfare of American imperialism. Peace-loving people all over the world will also rise up to defend themselves and effectively stop this monstrous crime of American imperialism, especially all righteous legal workers have a natural duty to accuse this crime to all mankind. This investigation team's hard work in coming to our Northeast China to investigate the crime of American imperialism's germ warfare is a powerful blow to the American aggressors."
    The head of the investigation team, Brandweiner, said in his speech: "We are very honored to be invited to the Northeast region of China to investigate the crimes of American aggressors in conducting germ warfare. I believe we have considerable experience in investigating the use of bacteriological weapons. We believe that our investigation work will contribute to the cause of defending world peace and opposing germ warfare."

### 国际民主法律工作者协会调查团抵沈阳  东北各界热烈欢迎调查团揭发美国侵略者进行细菌战罪行

1952-03-27
第1版()
专栏：

    国际民主法律工作者协会调查团抵沈阳
    东北各界热烈欢迎调查团揭发美国侵略者进行细菌战罪行
    【新华社沈阳二十六日电】前来东北调查美帝国主义撒布带菌昆虫罪行的国际民主法律工作者协会调查团在二十六日抵达沈阳。前往车站欢迎的有中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会东北总分会主席、中央人民政府最高人民法院东北分院院长高崇民、中共中央东北局宣传部副部长刘芝明、中国国民党革命委员会东北临时工作委员会主任委员宁武、中国民主同盟东北总支部秘书长关梦觉、中国民主促进会沈阳市分会筹备委员会主任委员车向忱、东北人民政府卫生部部长王斌、副部长白希清、东北总工会主席张维桢、中国新民主主义青年团东北委员会书记章泽、东北民主妇女联合会主任郭明秋、沈阳市副市长焦若愚、东北区新闻工作者代表等。
    调查团下车后，即由欢迎人员陪同前往东北人民政府交际处，并举行了简短的座谈会。会上，高崇民代表中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会东北总分会及东北地区法律工作者致欢迎词，对调查团表示热烈的欢迎和崇高的敬意。他在盛赞调查团在朝鲜从事调查美国侵略军及其帮凶们所犯各种罪行的正义行为之后，说：“美帝国主义不仅在朝鲜前线和后方进行细菌战，并且更恶毒地在我国东北各地撒布大量带有细菌的昆虫，美帝国主义这种绝灭人性的罪行，是对人类正义、国际公约、科学文明的严重破坏，也是对全人类生存的严重威胁。我国人民有决心、有信心粉碎美帝国主义这种野兽般的细菌战。全世界爱好和平的人民也必将起来自卫，有效地制止美帝国主义这种滔天的罪行，尤其是一切正义的法律工作者更有向全人类控诉这种罪行的天职。此次调查团不辞辛劳来到我国东北调查美帝国主义细菌战的罪行，更是对美国侵略者的一个有力的打击。”
    调查团团长布兰德魏纳致词说：“我们很荣幸地被邀请到中国东北地区调查美国侵略者进行细菌战的罪行，我相信我们对于调查使用细菌武器的工作已经有了相当经验。我们相信我们的调查工作，将对于保卫世界和平、反对细菌战的事业有所贡献。”
