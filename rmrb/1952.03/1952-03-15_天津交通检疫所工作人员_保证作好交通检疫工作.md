Title: Tianjin Traffic Quarantine Station Staff Guarantee to Do a Good Job in Traffic Quarantine
Author: Tong Yuheng
Date: 1952-03-15
Page: 2

Column: Reader's Letter

    Tianjin Traffic Quarantine Station Staff
    Guarantee to Do a Good Job in Traffic Quarantine
    We protest against the use of bacteriological weapons by American imperialism, which are absolutely forbidden by international law and human morality.
    All staff members of the Tianjin Traffic Quarantine Station of our Central Ministry of Health, in addition to strictly carrying out their own quarantine work, have also made preparations to participate in quarantine work on the battlefield. We make a guarantee to the people of the whole country that when the motherland needs us, we will strive to be the first group of national traffic quarantine workers to rush to the front line.
    Tong Yuheng, Director of Tianjin Traffic Quarantine Station, Central Ministry of Health

### 天津交通检疫所工作人员  保证作好交通检疫工作
佟愚恒
1952-03-15
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    天津交通检疫所工作人员
    保证作好交通检疫工作
    我们抗议美帝国主义使用国际公法和人类道德所绝对禁止的细菌武器。
    我们中央卫生部天津交通检疫所的全体职工，除了严格执行本身的检疫工作外，并已作好准备，要求参加战地的检疫工作。我们在全国人民面前提出保证，祖国需要我们的时候，我们就争取作为全国交通检疫工作人员奔赴前线的第一批。
    中央卫生部天津交通检疫所所长　佟愚恒
