International Briefs

    Our Art Exhibition Held in Tambov
    A Chinese art exhibition opened on the 16th in Tambov (in the Russian Federal Republic). The exhibits include bone, wood, porcelain, and stone artworks, fine art works by famous Chinese artists, and Russian-language magazines published in China. The exhibition has been warmly welcomed by visitors.

    Our Youth Art Troupe Arrives in Romania for Performances
    The Chinese Youth Art Troupe, which participated in the World Youth and Student Festival for Peace held in Berlin last August, arrived in Romania on February 19th after touring the German Democratic Republic, Hungary, Poland, and the Soviet Union. The art troupe gave its first performance in Bucharest on the 25th, with more than 2,500 people in attendance. Leaders of the Romanian government and the Workers' Party, representatives of people's organizations, and foreign diplomatic envoys to Romania were present. After the performances in Bucharest, the art troupe has traveled to other cities in Romania to perform.

    Pakistan International Industrial Exhibition
    Chinese Pavilion Receives Warm Welcome
    The People's Republic of China Pavilion at the Pakistan International Industrial Exhibition has attracted tens of thousands of visitors since its opening on the 9th. In the first two days after the opening alone, 190,000 people visited. The Chinese Pavilion is the largest and most comprehensive exhibition hall in the exhibition, displaying more than 5,000 pieces of various industrial raw materials and manufactured goods, including various daily necessities, chemical and electrical materials, various precision tools and instruments, various machine tools, and various special crafts. Visitors have praised the achievements created by the Chinese working people and expressed admiration for the rapid progress of the new China in economic construction.

### 国际简讯

1952-03-22
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    我国艺术展览会在坦波夫举行
    中国艺术展览会于十六日在坦波夫（在俄罗斯联邦共和国）开幕。展览品中有骨、木、磁、石等艺术品，中国名艺术家的美术作品和在中国出版的俄文杂志。展览会受到参观者的热烈欢迎。
    我国青年文艺工作团抵罗演出
    参加去年八月间在柏林举行的世界青年与学生和平联欢节的我国青年文艺工作团，在德意志民主共和国、匈牙利、波兰和苏联四国巡回演出后，已于二月十九日抵罗马尼亚访问。文艺工作团二十五日在布加勒斯特作了第一次演出，到场观众二千五百多人。罗马尼亚政府和工人党的领袖、人民团体代表和各国驻罗外交使节，都曾出席。在布加勒斯特表演结束之后，文艺工作团已到罗马尼亚其他各城市演出。
    巴基斯坦国际工业展览会
    中国馆受到热烈欢迎
    巴基斯坦国际工业展览会中的中华人民共和国馆，自九日开放以来，吸引了成千成万的观众。仅在开馆以后的两天之内，前往参观的就有十九万人。中国馆是这次展览会中规模最大、内容最丰富的展览馆，里面陈列着各种工业原料和制成品共五千多件，包括各种日用品、化工电器材料、各种精密工具仪表、各种工作母机以及各种特种工艺品等。观众无不赞扬中国劳动人民创造的这些成果，并对新中国在经济建设方面的飞速进步表示钦佩。
