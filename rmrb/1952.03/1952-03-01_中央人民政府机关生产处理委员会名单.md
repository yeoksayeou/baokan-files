    List of Members of the Production and Processing Committee of the Central People's Government Organs
    The following is the list of members of the Production and Processing Committee of the Central People's Government Organs, approved by the 126th Administrative Council Meeting of the Central People's Government: Director: Li Fuchun; Secretary-General: Song Shaowen; Members: Yang Shangkun, Yang Lisan, Qi Yanming, Liu Xiufeng, Xu Zirong, Rong Zihe, Hu Jingyun, Deng Jie, Zhou Rongxin.
