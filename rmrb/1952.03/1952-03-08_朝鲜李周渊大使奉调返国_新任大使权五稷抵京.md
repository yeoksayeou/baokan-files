Title: Korean Ambassador Li Zhouyuan Recalled; New Ambassador Kwon O-jik Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-03-08
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Korean first ambassador to China Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary Li Zhouyuan was recalled on November 13th last year. The government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea has now appointed Kwon O-jik as the Korean Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to our country.
    Ambassador Kwon O-jik arrived in Beijing by car at 12:15 noon on the 7th. Those who went to the station to welcome him included Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Chen Jiakang, Acting Director of the Asian Department, Wei Baoshan, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and Zeng Mei, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command. Also present were Soviet Ambassador to China Roshchin, Romanian Ambassador to China Rudenko, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic to China, König, Mongolian Ambassador to China, Jalkhasaikhan, Hungarian Ambassador to China, Saffronko, Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Petkov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy, Daoding, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Delegation, Zhou Liang, and First Secretary of the Czech Embassy, Jabroski, etc. Choi Young, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Korean Embassy in China, and all members of the embassy also went to welcome him.

### 朝鲜李周渊大使奉调返国  新任大使权五稷抵京

1952-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜李周渊大使奉调返国
    新任大使权五稷抵京
    【新华社七日讯】朝鲜首任驻华特命全权大使李周渊已于去年十一月十三日奉调返国，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府现已任命权五稷为朝鲜驻我国特命全权大使。
    权五稷大使于七日中午十二时十五分乘车抵京。赴车站欢迎者有我中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫、亚洲司代司长陈家康、交际处副处长魏宝善，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，京津卫戍司令部副参谋长曾美。此外尚有苏联驻华大使罗申，罗马尼亚驻华大使鲁登科，德意志民主共和国驻华外交使团大使衔团长柯尼希，蒙古驻华大使贾尔卡赛汗，匈牙利驻华大使夏法朗柯，保加利亚驻华大使彼得科夫，波兰大使馆临时代办道定，越南代表团临时代办周亮，捷克大使馆一等秘书雅布罗斯基等。朝鲜驻华大使馆临时代办崔英及全体馆员亦前往欢迎。
