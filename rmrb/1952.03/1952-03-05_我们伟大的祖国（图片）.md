Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-03-05
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    In order to support the Chinese and Korean People's Forces and the Korean people, and resolutely extinguish the poisonous flames of the American aggressors' bacteriological warfare, our medical and health science workers have actively taken action. The staff of the Beijing Central Biological Products Institute are working day and night to rush produce a large number of anti-epidemic vaccines to be shipped to Korea. This is a scene of them preparing to manufacture anti-epidemic vaccines.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-03-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为了支援中朝人民部队和朝鲜人民，坚决扑灭美国侵略者细菌战的毒焰，我国医药卫生科学工作者纷纷积极行动起来。北京中央生物制品研究所的职工们，日夜赶制大批防疫菌苗运往朝鲜。这是他们正在准备制造防疫菌苗的情形。
