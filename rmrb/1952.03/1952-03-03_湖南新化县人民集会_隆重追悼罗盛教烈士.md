Title: People's Gathering in Xinhua County, Hunan Province, Solemnly Commemorates Martyr Luo Shengjiao
Author:
Date: 1952-03-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, February 2nd:
    The hometown of internationalist fighter Luo Shengjiao, Xinhua County in Hunan Province, recently solemnly held a memorial meeting to commemorate Martyr Luo Shengjiao. Those attending included Zhang Lie, head of the Central South sub-group of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation, Huang Guihan, head of the Korean People's Delegation to China, delegate Xu Jinyu, representatives from the Hunan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Hunan Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Hunan Provincial Anti-American Aid Korea Branch, and the Hunan Provincial Working Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and 1,500 people from Xinhua County.

    Martyr Luo Shengjiao heroically sacrificed himself on January 2nd of this year in a certain place in Korea while rescuing a drowning Korean boy, Cui ?. When the news of his selfless sacrifice reached Xinhua, the county people's government immediately sent representatives to Tongzi Village, Songshan Township (previously mistakenly reported as Xiangzi Village), more than 100 li from the county seat, to express condolences to the family of Martyr Luo, distributing 1,800 jin of grain as a pension, and calling on people from all walks of life to learn from the example of Martyr Luo Shengjiao.

    At the memorial meeting, after Zhang Lie, head of the sub-group, recounted Luo Shengjiao's heroic deeds with deep sorrow, he said: "Thousands upon thousands of commanders and fighters of the Volunteer Army will learn from and carry forward the heroic example of Comrade Luo Shengjiao!" Group leader Huang Guihan said: "The exemplary deeds of Comrade Luo Shengjiao will forever remain in the hearts of the Korean people and all people who love freedom and peace around the world." Luo Diekai, Luo Shengjiao's father, stated at the meeting: In order to commemorate his son and repay everyone's concern for him, he will work hard to increase production this year, increase yields, support the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army, and maintain and carry forward the martyr's glory.

### 湖南新化县人民集会  隆重追悼罗盛教烈士

1952-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

    湖南新化县人民集会
    隆重追悼罗盛教烈士
    【新华社长沙二日电】国际主义战士罗盛教故乡——湖南省新化县，最近隆重举行追悼大会，追悼罗盛教烈士。到会的有中国人民志愿军归国代表团中南分团团长张烈、朝鲜人民访华代表团团长黄龟汉、代表许金玉，中国共产党湖南省委员会、中国人民解放军湖南军区、湖南省抗美援朝分会和中国新民主主义青年团湖南省工作委员会等机关的代表和新化县人民共一千五百多人。
    罗盛教烈士是在今年一月二日为抢救溺水的朝鲜少年崔?而壮烈牺牲在朝鲜某地的。当他舍己成仁的消息传到新化后，该县人民政府立即派代表前往离县城一百多里的松山乡桐子村（前误为相子村），慰问罗烈士的家属，发给一千八百斤谷子的抚恤粮，并号召各界人民学习罗盛教烈士的榜样。
    在追悼会上，张烈分团长在沉痛地叙述了罗盛教的壮烈事迹后说：“千千万万个志愿军的指挥员、战斗员，都将学习和发扬罗盛教同志的英雄榜样！”黄龟汉分团长说：“罗盛教同志的模范事迹，将永远地留在朝鲜人民和全世界爱好自由、和平的人们的心里。”罗盛教的父亲罗迭开在会上表示：为了纪念自己的儿子和报答大家对自己的关切，今年要努力生产，提高产量，支援中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军，保持和发扬烈士的光荣。
