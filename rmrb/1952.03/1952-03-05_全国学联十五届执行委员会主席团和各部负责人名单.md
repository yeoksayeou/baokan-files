Title: List of Chairman and Heads of Departments of the 15th Executive Committee of the All-China Students' Federation

Author:

Date: 1952-03-05
Page: 1

(According to Xinhua News Agency dispatch)
    The 15th Executive Committee of the All-China Students' Federation elected its Presidium at its first meeting held last August. It is composed of one representative each from the student federations of nine provinces and cities, namely Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, Nanjing, Wuhan, Shenyang, Xi'an, Yunnan, and Xinjiang, with Beijing as the Chairman and the other eight provinces and cities such as Shanghai and Tianjin as Vice-Chairmen. The list of the Presidium and heads of departments is as follows:
    Chairman: Tian Demin (Beijing)
    Vice-Chairmen: Song Xiheng (Shanghai), Fang Guangyu (female) (Tianjin), Guan Ruoluan (female) (Nanjing), Chen Zhenlei (Wuhan), Feng Yinfu (Shenyang), Zhang Wenhao (Xi'an), Dong Xuelong (Yunnan), Tuohuti Aili (Xinjiang, Uyghur)
    Secretary-General: Xie Bangding
    Director of the Organization Department: Feng Yinfu
    Deputy Director: Xiong Qifang
    Director of the Propaganda Department: Guan Ruoluan
    Director of the International Liaison Department: Ke Zaishuo
    Deputy Director: Cai Zujing
    Director of the Welfare Department: Zhang Huai
    Director of the Recreation Department: Zhang Wenhao
    Deputy Director: Zhu Shihe

### 全国学联十五届执行委员会主席团和各部负责人名单

1952-03-05
第1版()
专栏：

    全国学联十五届执行委员会主席团和各部负责人名单
    中华全国学生联合会第十五届执行委员会，在去年八月举行的第一次会议上选出了主席团，由北京、上海、天津、南京、武汉、沈阳、西安、云南、新疆等九个省市学生联合会各选一人组成，以北京为主席，以上海、天津等八个省市为副主席。主席团和各部负责人名单如下：
    主席  田德民（北京）
    副主席  宋锡恒（上海）  方光宇（女）（天津）  关若鸾（女）（南京）  陈震雷（武汉）    冯荫复（沈阳）  张文浩（西安）  董学隆（云南）  托乎的艾力（新疆、维吾尔族）
    秘书长  谢邦定
    组织部部长  冯荫复
    副部长  熊启芳
    宣传部部长  关若鸾
    国际联络部部长  柯在铄  副部长  蔡祖静
    福利部部长  张淮
    康乐部部长  张文浩  副部长  朱世和
