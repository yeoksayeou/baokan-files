Title: Hope to Continue Screening the Film "Lenin in 1918"
Author: Ma Qingyun, Sun Jianxia, Qian Chunwei, Zhang Bingxun, Wu Yuying, Li Mei, Cheng Chunxi
Date: 1952-03-12
Page: 6

    Hope to Continue Screening the Film "Lenin in 1918"

    To the Editor: After reading Comrade Jia Ji's review of the Soviet film "Lenin in 1918" published in the People's Daily, we regret not having seen this film. In order to enable more viewers to learn from this film and help everyone carry out the anti-corruption struggle, we suggest that the China Film Distribution and Exhibition Corporation re-screen this film for a period of time.

    International News Bureau Ma Qingyun Sun Jianxia Qian Chunwei
    Zhang Bingxun Wu Yuying Li Mei
    Cheng Chunxi

### 希望继续放映“列宁在一九一八”影片
马卿云　孙剑霞　钱春暐  张炳勋　武毓英　黎枚  程纯熙
1952-03-12
第6版()
专栏：

    希望继续放映“列宁在一九一八”影片
    编辑同志：看了人民日报发表的贾霁同志对苏联影片“列宁在一九一八”的评介后，我们很后悔没有去看这一影片。为了使更多的观众能从这部影片里得到教育，帮助大家进行反贪污斗争，我们建议中国影片经理公司再把这个影片放映一个时期。
    国际新闻局　马卿云　孙剑霞　钱春暐
    张炳勋　武毓英　黎枚
    程纯熙
