Title: International Association of Democratic Lawyers Investigation Team to Go to Northeast China to Investigate the Crimes of the U.S. Military Using Bacteriological Weapons
Author: 
Date: 1952-03-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 21st: The International Association of Democratic Lawyers Investigation Team is about to leave Beijing for Northeast China to investigate the crimes of U.S. imperialism in waging bacteriological warfare. The investigation team issued a communique on the 21st, stating:

    The investigation team appointed by the International Association of Democratic Lawyers has completed its investigation into the crimes of the U.S. military and its allies in Korea, and the investigation team is about to go to Northeast China to investigate the crimes of the U.S. military in using bacteriological weapons on Chinese soil.

### 国际民主法律工作者协会调查团  将赴东北调查美军使用细菌武器的罪行

1952-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

    国际民主法律工作者协会调查团
    将赴东北调查美军使用细菌武器的罪行
    【新华社二十一日讯】国际民主法律工作者协会调查团即将离北京前往东北调查美帝国主义进行细菌战的罪行。调查团二十一日发表公报说：
    国际民主法律工作者协会指派的调查团已完成对美军及其盟军在朝鲜的罪行的调查，调查团即将前赴中国的东北调查美军在中国土地上使用细菌武器的罪行。
