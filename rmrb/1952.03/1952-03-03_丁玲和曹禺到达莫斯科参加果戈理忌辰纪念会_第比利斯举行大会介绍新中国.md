Title: Ding Ling and Cao Yu Arrive in Moscow to Attend Gogol's Death Anniversary Commemoration Ceremony; Tbilisi Holds Conference to Introduce New China
Author: 
Date: 1952-03-03
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 2nd. The literary and art representatives from our country, Ding Ling and Cao Yu, who are participating in the Soviet Union's commemoration ceremony for the 100th anniversary of the death of the great Russian writer Gogol, arrived in Moscow on February 29th after leaving Beijing by plane on February 27th. Representatives from the Soviet Peace Committee, the Soviet Writers' Union, and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries were at the airport to welcome them.
    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. According to TASS news agency: People from all walks of life in Tbilisi, the capital of the Soviet Georgian Republic, held a conference at the Rustaveli Theater on the 1st to introduce New China. Many scientists, artists, writers, and representatives from all walks of life in Tbilisi attended the conference. Stalin Prize winner and President of the Georgian Academy of Sciences, Muskhelishvili, delivered the opening speech, emphasizing that the friendly alliance between the Chinese and Soviet people is a powerful force for defending world peace. The attendees listened with great interest to two reports: "Lenin and Stalin on the Chinese Revolution" and "The Achievements of the People's Republic of China." A grand concert was held after the meeting, featuring works by Chinese and Soviet composers. An exhibition was also held in the venue, displaying literary works and posters from New China. The Chinese translation of "The Knight in the Panther's Skin," the immortal masterpiece by the ancient Georgian writer Rustaveli, published in Shanghai, was also on display at the exhibition and received great attention from the audience.

### 丁玲和曹禺到达莫斯科参加果戈理忌辰纪念会  第比利斯举行大会介绍新中国

1952-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

    丁玲和曹禺到达莫斯科参加果戈理忌辰纪念会
    第比利斯举行大会介绍新中国
    【新华社莫斯科二日电】我国参加苏联举行的俄罗斯伟大作家果戈理逝世一百周年纪念仪式的文艺界代表丁玲和曹禺，在二月二十七日搭飞机离开北京之后，已在二月二十九日抵达莫斯科。苏联拥护和平委员会、苏联作家协会和苏联对外文化协会，都派代表到机场欢迎。
    【新华社二日讯】据塔斯社消息：苏联格鲁吉亚共和国首都第比利斯的各界人民，一日在路斯赫威里剧院举行了介绍新中国的大会。出席大会的有第比利斯的科学家、艺术家、文学家和各界代表多人。斯大林奖金得奖人、格鲁吉亚科学院院长慕斯赫利施维利致开会辞，他强调指出中苏两国人民的友好同盟是保卫世界和平的强大力量。到会者以极大的兴趣听取了
    “列宁和斯大林论中国革命”和“中华人民共和国的成就”两个报告。会后举行了盛大的音乐会，演奏了中国和苏联作曲家的作品。会场内并举办了展览会，陈列着新中国的文学作品和招贴画。在上海出版的格鲁吉亚古代文学家路斯赫威里的不朽名著“虎皮骑士”的中文译本，也在展览会上陈列，受到观众很大的注意。
