Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-03-19
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Medical and health workers across the country have been actively demanding to take practical actions to oppose the American aggressors' bacteriological warfare. All the more than 400 doctors, nurses, and staff members of Shanghai Renji Hospital unanimously signed a letter to Chairman Mao, requesting to go to the forefront of the anti-bacteriological warfare. This is a picture of them signing the letter.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    全国各地医药卫生工作者纷纷要求以实际行动来反对美国侵略者的细菌战。上海仁济医院的全体医生、护士、职工四百余人，一致签名上书毛主席，要求到反细菌战的最前线去。这是他们正在签名的情形。
