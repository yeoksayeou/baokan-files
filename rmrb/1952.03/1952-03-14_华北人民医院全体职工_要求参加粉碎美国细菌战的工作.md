Title: All Staff of North China People's Hospital Demand to Participate in the Work of Crushing America's Bacteriological Warfare

Author: 
Date: 1952-03-14
Page: 2

Column: Reader's Letter

    To the Editor: We are medical and health workers, and we express our serious protest against the American aggressors' violation of international conventions and inhumane conduct of bacteriological warfare. Now, all the staff of our hospital unanimously demand that the task of sterilization and epidemic prevention be given to us first. We are ready to go. We want to go to the forefront of the anti-bacteriological warfare in the posture of exterminating bacterial warriors and give the American imperialists and bacteriological war criminals a devastating blow. We believe that the final victory belongs to us.
    Trade Union Committee of North China People's Hospital

### 华北人民医院全体职工  要求参加粉碎美国细菌战的工作

1952-03-14
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    华北人民医院全体职工
    要求参加粉碎美国细菌战的工作
    编辑同志：我们是医药卫生工作者，对美国侵略者违反国际公约、毫无人性地进行细菌战的暴行，提出严重抗议。现在，我院全体职工一致要求把灭菌防疫的任务首先交给我们，我们已整装待发。我们要以扑灭细菌战士的姿态到反细菌战的最前线上去，给美帝国主义者及细菌战犯们以毁灭性打击。我们相信最后胜利是属于我们的。
    华北人民医院工会委员会
