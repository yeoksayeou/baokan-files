Our Great Motherland
The Northwest Delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation and the Northwest Delegation of the Korean People's Delegation to China arrived in Xi'an on February 1st and were welcomed by people from all walks of life in Xi'an. The young students of Xi'an are lifting the comrades of the delegation high in the air.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-02-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国人民志愿军归国代表团西北分团和朝鲜人民访华代表团西北分团于二月一日到达西安，受到西安各界人民的欢迎。西安的青年学生们，正把代表团的同志们高高地抬起。
