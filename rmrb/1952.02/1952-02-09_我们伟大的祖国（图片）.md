Our Great Motherland
The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has held many exhibitions throughout the country, introducing pictures of Sino-Soviet friendship, Soviet construction, and the lives of the Soviet people, strengthening the friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union, and spreading the advanced experience of Soviet construction. This is a picture exhibition held by the Rehe Sino-Soviet Friendship Association in Ongniud Banner. The Mongolian people are listening to the interpreter's explanation.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中苏友好协会在全国各地举办了许多展览会，介绍中苏友好、苏联建设和苏联人民生活的图片，加强着中苏两国人民的友谊，传播着苏联建设的先进经验。这是热河中苏友好协会在翁牛特旗举办的图片展览会，蒙族人民正在听讲解员解释的情形。
