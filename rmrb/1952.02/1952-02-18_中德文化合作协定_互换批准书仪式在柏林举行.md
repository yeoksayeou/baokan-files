Title: Exchange of Instruments of Ratification Ceremony for the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement Held in Berlin
Author:
Date: 1952-02-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, February 16th: The ceremony for the exchange of instruments of ratification for the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement, signed in Beijing on October 9, 1951, was held at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic on the morning of the 14th of this month. Our plenipotentiary representative was Ji Pengfei, head of the diplomatic mission of the People's Republic of China to the German Democratic Republic, with the title of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary. The plenipotentiary representative of the German Democratic Republic was Foreign Minister Dertinger. Those who attended the ceremony also included Mrs. Heisel, Deputy Minister of Education of the German Democratic Republic, Mrs. Rentmeister, Vice Chairman of the State Art Committee, the Director of the First Department and the Director of the Legal Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, as well as the Counselor, Secretary, and attendants of our diplomatic mission.

### 中德文化合作协定  互换批准书仪式在柏林举行

1952-02-18
第4版()
专栏：

    中德文化合作协定
    互换批准书仪式在柏林举行
    【新华社柏林十六日电】一九五一年十月九日在北京签订的中德文化合作协定互换批准书仪式，已于本月十四日晨在德意志民主共和国外交部举行。我方全权代表为我国驻德意志民主共和国外交使团特命全权大使衔团长姬鹏飞，德意志民主共和国全权代表为外交部长窦丁格尔。参加仪式者尚有德意志民主共和国教育部副部长柴塞尔夫人、国家艺术委员会副主席伦特迈斯特夫人、外交部第一司司长和法律司司长以及我外交使团的参赞、秘书和随员等多人。
