Title: Eliminating American Air Bandits (Korean Battlefield Sketch No. 7) (Picture)
Author: Shao Yu
Date: 1952-02-22
Page: 3

    Eliminating American Air Bandits
    (Korean Battlefield Sketch No. 7)
    Shao Yu
    On December 14th of last year, an air battle unfolded in the skies over Yongdam-ri, Korea, resulting in the destruction of four enemy aircraft. The enemy planes that fled south were also shot down by our nearby anti-aircraft units. One enemy plane crashed two miles west of our garrison, shattering into pieces. Every soldier who passed by the wreckage of the enemy plane had a smile on their face. Someone asked, "Comrade, what are you smiling about?" A soldier replied, "Look, America's 'air superiority' has been shattered here."

### 消灭美国空中强盗（朝鲜战场素描之七）（图片）
邵宇
1952-02-22
第3版()
专栏：

    消灭美国空中强盗
    （朝鲜战场素描之七）
    邵宇
    去年十二月十四日，在朝鲜龙潭里的上空，展开了一场空战，有四架敌人的飞机被击毁了；向南逃窜的敌机，又被附近我高射部队击毁。有一架敌机，在我们驻地以西二里处摔得粉碎；每个从敌机残骸走过去的战士，脸上都显出微笑。是谁在问道：“同志你笑什么？”一个战士说道：“你看，美国的‘空中优势’，在这里摔碎了。”
