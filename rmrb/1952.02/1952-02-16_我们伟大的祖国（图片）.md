Our great motherland
In order to thoroughly defeat the bourgeoisie's wild attacks and consolidate the leadership of the working class, the masses of workers in various regions are actively engaged in the struggle against corruption, waste, and bureaucracy. This is a joint meeting of group leaders convened by the Thrift Inspection Committee of the Beijing Railway Station of the Tianjin Railway Administration. They are carefully studying the materials of accusations and confessions, preparing to capture major embezzlers.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-02-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为了彻底打退资产阶级的猖狂进攻，巩固工人阶级的领导权，各地工人群众正积极投入反贪污、反浪费、反官僚主义的斗争。这是天津铁路管理局北京站节约检查委员会召开的小组长联席会议。他们正缜密地研究检举和坦白的材料，准备捉捕大贪污犯。
