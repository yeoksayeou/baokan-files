Date: 1952-02-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency

    Snowfall in Hebei, Shanxi, Suiyuan and Other Provinces
    [Xinhua News Agency News] Hebei, Shanxi, Suiyuan and other provinces have experienced widespread snowfall on the 5th and 6th. Hebei experienced about an inch of snowfall in various locations on the afternoon of the 5th, with larger snowfalls in the coastal areas of Cangxian, Tangshan and other regions. From 8:00 PM on the 5th to 11:00 AM on the 6th, Shanxi experienced 76 millimeters of snowfall, with 7.2 millimeters of meltwater, exceeding the amount of the two snowfalls since the beginning of winter last year. Suiyuan Province experienced snowfall from 3:00 AM to 10:00 AM on the 6th. Guisui and Baotou cities experienced two inches of snowfall, and the Yikezhao League experienced six inches. Since the beginning of winter last year, there has been little rain and snow in North China. This snowfall is very beneficial to the growth of wheat seedlings and the killing of insects and preservation of moisture in autumn fields.

### 河北山西绥远等省降雪

1952-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

    河北山西绥远等省降雪
    【新华社讯】河北、山西、绥远等省已在五日和六日普遍降雪。河北各地在五日下午降雪一寸左右，沿海的沧县、唐山等专区雪量较大。山西各地自五日晚八时到六日上午十一时止，降雪达七十六公厘，溶水量七·二公厘，超过去年入冬后两次降雪的数量。绥远省在六日晨三时到十时降雪。归绥和包头市降雪二寸，伊克昭盟六寸。去年入冬以来，华北各地雨雪都少。这次落雪对麦苗成长和秋田杀虫保墒有很大好处。
