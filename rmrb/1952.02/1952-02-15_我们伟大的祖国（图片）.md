    Our Great Motherland
    Widely mobilizing the masses to help each other dig wells and universally using well water for irrigation is an effective method for developing water conservancy and preventing drought in North China. This is a scene of the Yuluoshan Land Cooperative, an agricultural labor model in Shagedan Village, Ji County, Hebei Province, digging a well.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-02-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    广泛发动群众互助打井，普遍利用井水灌溉，是华北地区发展水利和防旱抗旱的有效办法。这是河北省冀县沙疙疸村农业劳动模范郁洛善土地合作社打井的情形。
