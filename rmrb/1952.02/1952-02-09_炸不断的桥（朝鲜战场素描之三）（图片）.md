Title: The Bridge That Cannot Be Blown Up (Korean War Sketch Three)
Author: Shao Yu
Date: 1952-02-09
Page: 3

    The Bridge That Cannot Be Blown Up (Korean War Sketch Three)
    Shao Yu
    On the Korean battlefield, how many such bridges are there? How many times have they been bombed by the enemy? Look! The bridge is still a bridge! The bridge—stands firm and proud, cars drive over it, and the troops are advancing!
    It—is the embodiment of our heroic people's engineers.
    It—is a bridge that can never be blown up!

### 炸不断的桥（朝鲜战场素描之三）（图片）
邵宇
1952-02-09
第3版()
专栏：

    炸不断的桥  （朝鲜战场素描之三）
    邵宇
    在朝鲜战场上，有多少这样的桥，它被敌人炸过多少次了，你看！桥还是桥！桥——坚实骄傲地站立着，车开过去，队伍在前进！
    它——是我们英雄的人民工兵的化身
    它——是一座永远炸不断的桥！
