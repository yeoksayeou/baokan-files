Author:
Date: 1952-02-19

    Our Great Motherland
    The troops of the Chinese People's Liberation Army entering Tibet are a heroic force that has overcome numerous difficulties. They have crossed the desolate grasslands and the snow-covered Tibetan plateau for many years, completing the task of advancing. This is a scene of the People's Liberation Army advancing in the snow-capped mountains of the Tibetan Plateau.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-02-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国人民解放军入藏部队，是一支战胜重重困难的英雄部队。他们曾越过了杳无人烟的荒凉草原和长年积雪的西藏高原，完成了进军的任务。这是解放军在西藏高原的雪山中前进时的情景。
