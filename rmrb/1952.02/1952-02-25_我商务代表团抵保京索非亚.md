Title: Our Business Delegation Arrives in Sofia, Bulgaria
Author:
Date: 1952-02-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 23rd. The business delegation from the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, headed by Deputy Head of the Delegation Wang Hanmin, arrived in Sofia, the capital of Bulgaria, on the evening of the 20th. Those who went to the railway station to welcome them included Bulgarian Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Mirushev and other senior officials, as well as personnel from our embassy in Bulgaria.

### 我商务代表团抵保京索非亚

1952-02-25
第4版()
专栏：

    我商务代表团抵保京索非亚
    【新华社布拉格二十三日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府派赴保加利亚人民共和国的商务代表团，在副团长王汉民率领下，于二十日晚抵达保首都索非亚。赴火车站欢迎的有保加利亚对外贸易部副部长米鲁谢夫和其他高级官员，以及我驻保加利亚大使馆人员等。
