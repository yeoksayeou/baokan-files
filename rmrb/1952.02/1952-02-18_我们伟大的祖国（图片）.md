Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-02-18
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The young Chinese People's Air Force is growing stronger day by day. After joining the air force schools, a large number of outstanding young people have learned many skills, and they are determined to further develop the People's Air Force. These are young students at the Air Force School. Before practicing flying, they are discussing flying techniques.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-02-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    年轻的中国人民空军正日益发展壮大。大批优秀的青年参加空军学校以后，学会了许多本领，他们决心进一步发展人民空军。这是空军学校的青年学员们。他们在练习飞行之前，正讨论着飞行的技术。
