Title: Celebrating Hungarian Publication Day: Hungarian Ambassador Hosts Reception for Journalists in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-02-01
Page: 4

    Celebrating Hungarian Publication Day
    Hungarian Ambassador Hosts Reception for Journalists

    [Xinhua News Agency] To celebrate February 1st, Hungarian Publication Day, the Hungarian Ambassador to China, Száfrankó, held a reception for journalists in Beijing on January 31st. At the reception, Ambassador Száfrankó introduced the origin of Hungarian Publication Day and described the current situation of Hungarian newspapers and magazines and their important role in socialist construction. Ambassador Száfrankó then answered various questions raised by the journalists. After the meeting, the feature film "Underground Colony," a story about the Hungarian people smashing the sabotage conspiracy of American imperialist spies, was shown.

    Note: February 1st marks the tenth anniversary of the founding of "Free People's Daily," the central organ of the Hungarian Working People's Party. The Hungarian government specially designated it as Hungarian Publication Day last November in commemoration.

### 庆祝匈牙利出版节  匈大使在京招待记者

1952-02-01
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝匈牙利出版节  
    匈大使在京招待记者
    【新华社讯】匈牙利驻我国大使夏法朗柯，为庆祝二月一日——匈牙利出版节，特于一月三十一日在北京举行记者招待会。夏法朗柯大使在会上介绍了匈牙利出版节的由来，并叙述了匈牙利报纸和杂志的近况及其在社会主义建设中所起的重要作用。夏法朗柯大使随后答复了记者所提出的各项问题。会后并放映了匈牙利人民粉碎美帝国主义间谍分子怠工破坏阴谋的故事片“地下殖民地”。
    按：二月一日为匈牙利劳动人民党中央机关报“自由人民报”创刊十周年纪念日，匈牙利政府特在去年十一月定为匈牙利出版节以示纪念。
