Title: The Newly Appointed Dutch Government Negotiator, Mr. van der Pepp, Arrived in Beijing by Car Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-02-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 2nd: Mr. Hou Weimin, the negotiator dispatched by the Dutch government to negotiate with the Central People's Government of our country on the establishment of diplomatic relations, was transferred from Beijing on November 26th of last year. Mr. van der Pepp, the newly appointed negotiator dispatched by the Dutch government, arrived in Beijing by car at 12:15 noon on the 2nd. Lang Xinkang, Deputy Section Chief of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, went to the station to greet him.

### 荷兰政府新派遣的谈判代表  范德普昨日乘车抵达北京

1952-02-03
第1版()
专栏：

    荷兰政府新派遣的谈判代表  
    范德普昨日乘车抵达北京
    【新华社二日讯】荷兰政府为与我国中央人民政府就建立外交关系问题进行谈判而派遣的谈判代表侯卫民先生，已在去年十一月二十六日奉调离北京。荷兰政府新派遣的谈判代表范德普先生在二日中午十二时十五分乘车抵京，中央人民政府外交部交际处副科长郎新康赴站迎接。
