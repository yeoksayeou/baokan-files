    The Traitorous Merchants Who Harmed the Volunteers Should Be Punished as Running Dogs of Imperialism
    Editor: When we saw the news that the traitorous merchant Li Yinting harmed our most beloved people—the volunteer comrades—we immediately felt an indescribable anger in our hearts. We sadly recited: "Our most beloved wounded comrades were disabled when they shouldn't have been, sacrificed when they shouldn't have been." From this, we further saw the hideous faces of the bourgeoisie. When the most outstanding sons and daughters of the motherland were fighting selflessly in the harsh environment of Korea for the motherland, for the Korean people, and for world peace, when the people of the motherland were celebrating the great victory to the volunteers with victorious hearts, when the people of the motherland were supporting the volunteers with their own bits and pieces of sweat, the traitorous merchants shot poisonous arrows at our most beloved people, causing them undue losses. We suggest that the government punish them as traitors to the motherland, the most despicable traitors, and the loyal running dogs of imperialism who are undermining world peace. Let us work together to suppress the bourgeoisie's attack.

    Let the People of the Whole Country Judge the Traitorous Merchants Who Harmed the Volunteers
    Editor: The people of the motherland have done their utmost to support the volunteers. The great working class put forward the slogan "Transform the factory into a battlefield" and worked overtime day and night. The great peasant class paid public grain in advance and sent good rice to the volunteers. All patriotic people have demonstrated their loyalty to the motherland and contributed their strength. The volunteer comrades are very grateful for this noble spirit of the people of the motherland. They not only contributed their blood and lives in the battle, but also carefully protected and used the materials of the motherland. A certain unit completely utilized waste materials to create a considerable iron factory, which made 1,500 horseshoes, 20,000 horseshoe nails, 7,500 anti-skid nails, 186 tools, and 210 steel picks; the comrades of a certain telephone platoon only slept for one hour in six days and six nights, and picked up 115,000 meters of broken wire, more than 1,000 pairs of broken rubber shoes, and more than 1,800 poles in half a month, completing a communication network of more than 230 li. There are countless volunteer heroes who were injured and sacrificed in order to protect the property of the motherland.
    The volunteers cherish every grain of rice and every thread of the motherland. But what about the bourgeoisie? In order to satisfy their selfish desires, they tried every means to attack the working class. They even extended their poisonous hands to our People's Liberation Army's close brothers, the beloved children of the people of the whole country, and the heroes admired by the people of the world—the great Chinese People's Volunteers. As revealed in the newspaper, Shanghai traitorous merchant Wang Kangnian, Hankou traitorous merchant Li Yinting, the capital of Lixing Egg Factory in Tianjin, Wuhan traitorous merchant Peng Xicheng, and Beijing traitorous merchant Shi Liru dared to be so insane and conscienceless to harm the volunteers and undermine our sacred cause of resisting America and aiding Korea.
    I demand that the people of the whole country judge them! Demand that the government severely punish them! If not, what is justice!
    All those who do not fully recognize the bourgeoisie's rampant attacks and whose stand is not firm should wake up! If you still have a sense of responsibility for the fate of the motherland and for the volunteers, you should immediately stand up and wage a ruthless struggle against the lawless elements in the bourgeoisie!

    我要求全国人民来裁判他们！要求政府严厉地惩办他们！不如此还有什么天理！
    一切对资产阶级猖狂进攻认识不足、立场不坚定的人们，应该醒醒！如果你们对祖国的命运、对志愿军还有责任感的话，就应当立即起来，跟资产阶级中的不法分子做无情的斗争！

    北京市第十区不法奸商
    胆敢解雇工人抵抗运动
    当地政府应迅速调查处理
    编辑同志：北京市第十区许多不法奸商，曾偷漏税收，投机倒把，向机关工作人员行贿。他们在这次反行贿、反偷漏税运动中，不但不彻底坦白，向人民低头认罪，反而千方百计地解雇工人，或压制和威胁检举他们的工人，来抵抗这一运动。例如：朝阳门外南中街长顺公牛羊店经理李长有，过去私屠耕牛，牟取暴利，并克扣工人工资，他在这次运动中，害怕工人检举，解雇了六个工人。该同业公会负责人李春等袒护资方，从中进行非法活动，促使双方妥协，由资方给了工人一点钱了事。文星永油粮店经理韩寿臣因为害怕被检举，关了铺子，五个工人现已失业。启泰永烟铺张经理，最近解雇了三个工人，并且欠下工人两个月的工资。最可恶的是福增祥服装店经理黄福增，他抗拒运动，竟狠毒地一天只给工人吃一顿饭（在晚上九点吃），工人饿得直哭。此外，天成粮店、东顺和饭铺等商号也发生了解雇工人的情况。我们认为这是值得注意的。我们除要求工人同志们继续向这些无法无天的奸商作坚决斗争外，并要求政府迅速派人进行调查，对这些破坏运动的不法奸商，按法律给以严厉制裁，以保障运动的顺利开展。
                          邱福昌　李洪涛
    编者按：北京市第十区不法奸商，以解雇、压制和威胁工人来抵抗反行贿、反偷税漏税运动，是令人不能容忍的。当地政府应该重视这一情况，迅速制裁不法奸商。
    要求政府严惩大成煤矿不法资本家华问渠
    编辑同志：我们读了二月六日人民日报刊载的贵阳市私营大成煤矿资方华问渠残酷剥削工人的报道以后，立即写了一封慰问信给大成煤矿工人，坚决支持他们对不法资本家的正义斗争。通过这一事件，我们深深感到资产阶级的进攻是十分猖狂的。像华问渠这样的资本家，身居政府要职，竟藐视政府法令，任意胡作非为，完全违背了共同纲领。这是绝对不能容忍的。我们要求政府严厉惩办不法资本家华问渠。解放两年多来，该矿工人还一直受着惨无人道的压榨，连最起码的福利保障都没有，这是什么原因呢？因此，我们认为各有关领导机关也应作深刻检讨。
