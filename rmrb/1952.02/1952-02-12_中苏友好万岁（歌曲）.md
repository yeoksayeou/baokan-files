Title: Long Live Sino-Soviet Friendship (Song)
Author: Xiao He, Luo Xin
Date: 1952-02-12
Page: 3

    Long Live Sino-Soviet Friendship
    Lyrics by Xiao He
    Music by Luo Xin
    Editor's Note: The All-China Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has decided to recommend this song for the masses in various regions to practice singing when commemorating the second anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance. It is hoped that local radio stations will teach this song in their arts and entertainment programs.
