Our Great Motherland

The Han En mutual aid team in Bao'an Village, Jiaohe County, Jilin Province, which is famous throughout the country, is steadily embarking on the road to agricultural cooperatives starting this year. This is Han En leading the members of the mutual aid team in a meeting. They are discussing the method of contributing land as shares.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-02-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    全国有名的吉林省蛟河县保安屯的韩恩互助组，从今年开始，正稳步地走上农业合作社的道路。这是韩恩领导着互助组的组员开会。他们正在讨论土地入股的办法。
