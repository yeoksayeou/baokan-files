    Our Great Motherland
    Zhang Mingliang, a special-class agricultural labor model in Shaanxi Province, led a mutual aid team that, since its formation last spring, has taken the lead not only in production but also in grain tax payment, cotton sales and storage, increased production and donations. This mutual aid team has thirty-four mu of cotton fields, all of which have now been plowed once with new-style walking plows. This is a picture of them plowing the land.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-02-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    陕西省特等农业劳动模范张明亮领导的互助组，从去年春季组织起来以后，不但在生产上，而且在缴公粮、售棉储棉、增产捐献各项工作中都起了带头作用。这个互助组有三十四亩棉田，现在已全部用新式步犁深耕了一次。这是他们正在耕地的情形。
