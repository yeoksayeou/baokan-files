Title: Actively Prepare to Participate in the International Economic Conference: Nan Hanchen, Ma Yinchu, and Others Organize National Preparatory Committee
Author:
Date: 1952-02-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 26th: To promote international economic cooperation, the International Economic Conference initiated by industrialists and business people from various countries is scheduled to be held in Moscow in early April. China has organized a national preparatory committee consisting of national financial and economic workers, people from industry and commerce, cooperative workers, trade union workers, and economists. The members of the National Preparatory Committee consist of Nan Hanchen, President of the People's Bank of China; Ma Yinchu, President of Peking University; Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Zhang Naiqi, member of the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce; Ji Chaoding, Director and Deputy General Manager of the Bank of China; Lei Renmin, Deputy Minister of Trade; Chen Weiji, Deputy Minister of the Textile Industry; Meng Yongqian, Vice Chairman of the Board of Directors of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives; Li Zhuchen, Chairman of the Tianjin Federation of Industry and Commerce; Sheng Pihua, Chairman of the Shanghai Federation of Industry and Commerce; Xu Dixin, Deputy Director of the East China Financial and Economic Committee; Chen Hansheng, Deputy Director of the China Economic Society; Liu Zijiu, Minister of Culture and Education of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; and Lu Xuzhang, Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, totaling fourteen people. The National Preparatory Committee is in contact with the International Preparatory Committee and the preparatory committees of various countries to carry out preparatory work.

### 积极准备参加国际经济会议  南汉宸马寅初等组织全国筹备委员会

1952-02-27
第1版()
专栏：

    积极准备参加国际经济会议
    南汉宸马寅初等组织全国筹备委员会
    【新华社二十六日讯】为促进国际间经济合作，各国工商业人士所发起的国际经济会议定于四月初在莫斯科举行。中国方面已由国家财政经济工作者、工商业界人士、合作社工作者、工会工作者、以及经济学者等组织全国筹备委员会。全国筹备委员会委员由中国人民银行行长南汉宸、北京大学校长马寅初、中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一、中华全国工商业联合会筹备委员会委员章乃器、中国银行董事兼副总经理冀朝鼎、贸易部副部长雷任民、纺织工业部副部长陈维稷、中华全国合作社联合总社理事会副主任孟用潜、天津市工商业联合会主任委员李烛尘、上海市工商业联合会主任委员盛丕华、华东财政经济委员会副主任许涤新，中国经济学会副主任委员陈翰笙、中华全国总工会文教部部长刘子久、中国进出口公司经理卢绪章等十四人组成。全国筹备委员会正与国际筹备委员会及各国筹备委员会联系，进行筹备工作。
