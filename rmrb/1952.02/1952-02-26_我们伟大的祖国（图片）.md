Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-02-26
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Wounded and sick soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteers returning home for recuperation are very concerned about the news from the Korean front, and the comrades at the front are also concerned about them. This is a scene of the representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteers Returning Delegation and the Southwest Branch of the Korean People's Delegation to China visiting Guo Jiaqun, a wounded soldier of the Chinese People's Volunteers, in a hospital in Southwest China. Comrade Zhang Boying, a female soldier of the Chinese People's Volunteers, is reading Commander Peng Dehuai's letter of condolence to Comrade Guo Jiaqun.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-02-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国人民志愿军回国休养的伤病员非常关心朝鲜前线的消息，前线的同志们也关心着他们。这是志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团西南分团的代表们在西南某医院慰问志愿军伤员郭家群的情形。志愿军女战士张伯英同志正在读彭德怀司令员的慰问信给郭家群同志听。
