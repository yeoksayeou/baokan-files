Title: Congratulations on the Anniversary of the Founding of the Korean People's Army: Commander-in-Chief Zhu De Sends a Telegram to General Kim Il-sung
Author:
Date: 1952-02-08
Page: 1

    Congratulations on the Anniversary of the Founding of the Korean People's Army
    Commander-in-Chief Zhu De Sends a Telegram to General Kim Il-sung
    [Xinhua News Agency, 7th] General Zhu De, Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, sent a telegram to General Kim Il-sung, Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army, congratulating him on the anniversary of the founding of the Korean People's Army. The text of the telegram is as follows:
    To General Kim Il-sung, Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army:
    On the occasion of the anniversary of the founding of the Korean People's Army, I extend my warm congratulations and respect to you and all the officers and men of the Korean People's Army on behalf of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.
    In order to resist the aggression of American imperialism and its accomplices, the Korean People's Army, with a high degree of heroism and tenacity, has overcome various difficulties, repelled the arrogant attacks of American imperialism, and achieved brilliant victories. I respectfully wish the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers continued joint operations and great victories in the cause of resisting aggression and defending world peace.
    Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Zhu De, February 7th

### 祝贺朝鲜人民军建军节  朱德总司令致电金日成将军

1952-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺朝鲜人民军建军节
    朱德总司令致电金日成将军
    【新华社七日讯】中国人民解放军总司令朱德将军致电朝鲜人民军最高司令官金日成将军，祝贺朝鲜人民军建军节，电文如下：朝鲜人民军最高司令官金日成将军：
    值此朝鲜人民军建军节之际，谨代表中国人民解放军向您及朝鲜人民军全体将士，致热烈的祝贺并表示敬意。
    为反对美帝国主义及其帮凶的进攻，朝鲜人民军以高度的英勇顽强精神，克服了种种困难，打退美帝国主义的狂妄进攻，取得了辉煌的胜利。敬祝朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军继续并肩作战，在反对侵略和保卫世界和平的事业中取得伟大胜利。
    中国人民解放军总司令  朱德  二月七日
