Title: Beijing Public Trial of Major Embezzlers Scheduled for 11 AM Today; All Sectors Should Organize to Listen to the Live Broadcast of the Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-02-01
Page: 1

    【Our Newspaper News】The Beijing Public Trial of Major Embezzlers will be held at 11:00 AM today (the 1st) at the Music Hall in Zhongshan Park, Beijing. Beijing People's Broadcasting Station and Tianjin People's Broadcasting Station will both broadcast the live proceedings of the meeting at that time. All sectors of the two cities, including factories, government offices, organizations, schools, and shops, should organize to listen. The frequency of Beijing People's Broadcasting Station is 850 kilocycles and 1080 kilocycles, and the frequency of Tianjin People's Broadcasting Station is 770 kilocycles and 920 kilocycles.

### 北京公审大贪污犯大会定今天上午十一时举行  各界应组织收听大会实况广播

1952-02-01
第1版()
专栏：

    北京公审大贪污犯大会定今天上午十一时举行
    各界应组织收听大会实况广播
    【本报讯】北京公审大贪污犯大会于今日（一日）上午十一时在北京中山公园音乐堂举行。北京人民广播电台和天津人民广播电台届时均转播大会实况，两市各工厂、机关、团体、学校、商店等各界市民，均应组织收听。北京人民广播电台周率为八五○千周及一○八○千周，天津人民广播电台周率为七七○千周及九二○千周。
