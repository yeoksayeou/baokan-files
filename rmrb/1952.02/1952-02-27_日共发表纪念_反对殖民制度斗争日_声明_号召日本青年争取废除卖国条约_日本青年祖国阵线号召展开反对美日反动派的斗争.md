Title: CCP Issues Statement Commemorating "Day of Struggle Against Colonial System," Calls on Japanese Youth to Strive for the Abolition of Treacherous Treaties. Japanese Youth Patriotic Front Calls for Launching a Struggle Against the US-Japan Reactionaries

Author:

Date: 1952-02-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 26th: The Japanese Communist Party's Provisional Central Guidance Department issued a statement commemorating the "Day of Struggle Against the Colonial System." The statement calls for a struggle to abolish the "Peace Treaty" and the "Security Treaty," overthrow the traitorous Yoshida government, dissolve the current Diet, withdraw all occupation forces, strive for national independence, and strive to establish a national liberation democratic government.

The "Metal Worker" newspaper published an editorial commemorating the "Day of Struggle Against the Colonial System," calling on Japanese youth to stand at the forefront of the struggle against rearmament and to prevent the implementation of conscription.

Prior to this, the National Committee of the Japanese Youth Patriotic Front also issued a call to Japanese youth, demanding that young people across the country resolutely rise up and launch a struggle against the US-Japan reactionaries. The call stated: Japanese youth must, on the "Day of Struggle Against the Colonial System," resolve to take action and drive the warmongers out of the motherland.

### 日共发表纪念“反对殖民制度斗争日”声明  号召日本青年争取废除卖国条约  日本青年祖国阵线号召展开反对美日反动派的斗争

1952-02-27
第4版()
专栏：

    日共发表纪念“反对殖民制度斗争日”声明
    号召日本青年争取废除卖国条约
    日本青年祖国阵线号召展开反对美日反动派的斗争
    【新华社二十六日讯】据塔斯社东京讯：日本共产党临时中央指导部发表纪念“反对殖民制度斗争日”的声明。声明中号召进行斗争，争取废除“和约”和“安全条约”，推翻吉田卖国政府，解散目前的国会，撤退一切占领军，争取民族独立，争取成立民族解放民主政府。
    “金属劳动者”报为纪念“反对殖民制度斗争日”发表社论，号召日本青年起来站在反对重新武装的斗争最前列和阻止兵役制的实行。
    在此以前，日本青年祖国阵线全国委员会也向日本青年发出号召，要求全国青年坚决起来向美日反动派展开斗争。这个号召说：日本青年必须在“反对殖民制度斗争日”下定决心，采取行动，把战争贩子从祖国驱逐出去。
