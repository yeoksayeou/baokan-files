Title: Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation and Korean People's Delegation to China Visit Various Places Across the Country and Receive a Warm Welcome from the People

Author:
Date: 1952-02-27
Page: 1

The sub-delegations of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation and the Korean People's Delegation to China are visiting and reporting in various parts of the country and receiving a warm welcome from the people. Representatives of the North China sub-delegation of the two delegations have made reports to various organizations, groups, troops, schools, and citizens in Beijing and Tianjin, and have since departed for the five provinces of North China and the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. The Northwest sub-delegation, the Northeast sub-delegation, and the Central South sub-delegation have now concluded their work in Xi'an, Shenyang, and Wuhan, and departed for various parts of Northwest, Northeast, and Central South on the 11th, 12th, and 13th respectively to make reports. The Southwest sub-delegation also left Chongqing on the 17th to go to various parts of the Southwest. Representatives of the East China sub-delegation, after visiting Nanjing and Shanghai, left Shanghai in groups on the 20th and 21st respectively to go to various parts of East China.

The sub-delegations made a total of 365 reports in the seven cities of Beijing, Tianjin, Xi'an, Shenyang, Chongqing, Nanjing, and Shanghai, with an audience of over 1,073,000 people; they also made many radio reports, with over 400,000 people listening to the broadcasts in Tianjin and Chongqing alone. The representatives visited the wounded and sick of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the People's Liberation Army who were recuperating in various places, visited mass organizations, families of revolutionary martyrs, families of military personnel, visited factories, schools, etc., and held symposiums with people from all walks of life to exchange experiences in various struggles and construction. The exhibitions of physical objects and pictures from the front lines of the War to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea, held by the representatives in various places, were visited by 172,000 people in Tianjin, Xi'an, and Nanjing alone.

The reports of the representatives have enabled the people in various places to more profoundly understand the significance of increasing production and practicing economy, and have strengthened their confidence in resisting US aggression and aiding Korea, and have also powerfully promoted the struggle against corruption and bribery among the people in various places. All the "tiger-fighting" team members of the Xi'an Telecommunications Bureau unanimously stated that they would learn from the heroic spirit of the Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army to repel the rampant attacks of the bourgeoisie. All the staff and workers of the Shenyang Clothing Factory No. 1 proposed to produce products with completely qualified quality to welcome the representatives. After listening to the report, the residents of Nanjing held discussions one after another, expressing their determination to resolutely plunge into the struggle against corruption and bribery.

### 志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团各分团  在全国各地进行访问受到人民热烈欢迎

1952-02-27
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团各分团
    在全国各地进行访问受到人民热烈欢迎
    【本报讯】中国人民志愿军归国代表团和朝鲜人民访华代表团各个分团正在全国各地进行访问和报告，受到各地人民的热烈欢迎。两代表团的华北分团代表，先后在北京、天津两市向各机关、团体、部队、学校和市民作报告之后，已分赴华北五省和内蒙古自治区。西北分团、东北分团、中南分团现已结束在西安、沈阳、武汉的工作，分别在十一日、十二日、十三日赴西北、东北、中南各地作报告。西南分团也在十七日离开重庆分赴西南各地。华东分团的代表在分访南京和上海后，已在二十日、二十一日先后分组离开上海，赴华东各地。
    各分团在北京、天津、西安、沈阳、重庆、南京、上海七市，共作报告三百六十五次，听众达一百零七万三千多人；同时还作了多次广播报告，仅天津、重庆两市收听广播的就有四十多万人。代表们分别在各地慰问了正在休养中的人民志愿军和人民解放军的伤病员，访问了群众团体、革命烈士家属、军人家属，参观了工厂、学校等，并和各界人民举行座谈会，交流各种斗争和建设的经验。代表们在各地举办的抗美援朝前线实物及图片展览，仅在天津、西安、南京三地，就有十七万二千人参观。
    代表们的报告，使各地人民更加深刻地认识到增产节约的意义，更加坚定了抗美援朝的信心，而且有力地推进了各地人民反贪污、反行贿斗争。西安电信局全体“打虎”队员一致表示：要学习志愿军和朝鲜人民军的英勇精神，来打退资产阶级的猖狂进攻。沈阳被服一厂全体职工提出要生产质量全部合格的产品来欢迎代表们。南京市居民在听完报告之后纷纷召开座谈会，表示要坚决投入反贪污、反行贿的斗争。
