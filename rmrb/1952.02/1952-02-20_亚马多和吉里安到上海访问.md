Title: Amado and Guillén Visit Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1952-02-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, February 19th: Jorge Amado, the famous Brazilian writer and winner of the Stalin International Peace Prize "For Strengthening Peace Among Peoples," and Nicolás Guillén, a Cuban poet and member of the Cultural Exchange Committee of the World Peace Council, arrived in Shanghai on the morning of the 17th from Beijing, accompanied by artist Wang Zhaowen. They were welcomed at the station by Liu Changsheng, Chairman of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression; Jin Zhonghua, Vice Chairman; Xia Yan, Chairman of the Shanghai Federation of Literary and Art Circles; and cultural and artistic workers including Huang Yuan, Zhou Erfu, Liu Simu, Lu Yi, and Shi Fangyu.

### 亚马多和吉里安到上海访问

1952-02-20
第4版()
专栏：

    亚马多和吉里安到上海访问
    【新华社上海十九日电】“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金得奖人、巴西著名作家亚马多及其夫人，世界和平理事会文化交流委员会委员、古巴诗人吉里安，由文艺家王朝闻陪同，在十七日上午九时由北京到达上海访问。到车站欢迎的有：中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会上海分会主席刘长胜、副主席金仲华，上海市文学艺术界联合会主席夏衍，以及文化艺术工作者黄源、周而复、刘思慕、陆诒、石方禹等多人。
