Title: Chinese People's Volunteer Army soldier Luo Shengjiao heroically saves a drowning Korean boy; broad masses of Chinese and Korean soldiers resolve to learn from the martyr's great internationalist spirit
Author:
Date: 1952-02-05
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, 4th. Our correspondent reports: The heroic deed of the immortal internationalist fighter Luo Shengjiao, who gave his life to save the drowning Korean boy Cui Ying, has been widely spread among the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army, and has become a model for the broad masses of commanders and fighters to learn from.
    After receiving the report about this incident, the leading organ of a certain unit of the Volunteer Army to which Martyr Luo Shengjiao belonged immediately issued a circular to all its subordinate units, commending him. The unit's newspaper also published a short commentary, calling on all commanders, fighters, and staff to learn from Martyr Luo Shengjiao's great internationalist spirit and the noble qualities of a revolutionary soldier, and to further strengthen the unity between the Chinese and Korean peoples. The unit's cultural troupe also sent people to the site of Martyr Luo's sacrifice to collect materials, preparing to write a play for performance in the troops. At the memorial service held by a certain unit, the commanders and fighters all expressed their pride in having a comrade who so typically demonstrated the noble qualities of the Chinese people, and everyone expressed their determination to learn from Luo's internationalist spirit.
    Martyr Luo's heroic deeds have also spread to the Korean People's Army. The General Political Bureau of the People's Army has sent people to the site of Luo Shengjiao's heroic sacrifice to collect Luo's exemplary deeds.

### 中国人民志愿军战士  罗盛教舍身抢救朝鲜溺水少年  广大中朝战士决心学习烈士伟大的国际主义精神

1952-02-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民志愿军战士  罗盛教舍身抢救朝鲜溺水少年  
    广大中朝战士决心学习烈士伟大的国际主义精神
    【新华社朝鲜前线四日电】本社记者报道：不朽的国际主义战士罗盛教舍身抢救朝鲜溺水少年崔?的壮烈事迹，已在中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军中被人们广泛地传播着，并已成为广大指挥员、战斗员的学习榜样。
    罗盛教烈士生前所属的志愿军某部领导机关在接到这件事的报告后，即向所属各部通报表扬。该部的报纸也发表了短评，号召全体指挥员、战斗员和工作人员学习罗盛教烈士伟大的国际主义精神和革命军人的崇高品质，进一步加强中朝人民的团结。该部文工团并派人到罗烈士牺牲地点收集材料，准备编写剧本在部队中演出。在某部举行的追悼会上，指挥员、战斗员们都以自己有了如此典型地表现出中国人民的高贵品质的同志而感到骄傲，大家表示决心学习罗烈士的国际主义精神。
    罗烈士的壮烈事迹也传到了朝鲜人民军中。人民军总政治局已派人到罗盛教壮烈牺牲的地点，收集罗烈士的模范事迹。
