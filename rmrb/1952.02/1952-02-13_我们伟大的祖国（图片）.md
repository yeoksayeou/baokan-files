Our Great Motherland
　　The State-run Lutai Farm is a relatively large mechanized farm directly under the leadership of the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government. It was established in 1949 and covers an area of ​​52,200 mu, all irrigated by electricity. This is the exterior view of the farm's nine-hole large water gate.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-02-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营芦台农场是中央人民政府农业部直接领导的一个较大的机械化农场。它建立于一九四九年，共占地五万二千二百亩，全部用电力灌溉。这是农场的九孔大水闸的外景。
