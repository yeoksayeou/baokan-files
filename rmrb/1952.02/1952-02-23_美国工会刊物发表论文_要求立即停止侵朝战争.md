Title: US Union Publication Publishes Article Demanding Immediate End to War of Aggression Against Korea
Author:
Date: 1952-02-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: According to a report by Diantong News Agency: The "Black Gang News," a publication for grassroots marine stoker workers published in San Francisco, recently published an editorial that clearly expressed the opposition of American lower-level workers to the American war of aggression against Korea. The editorial stated: "As the American armistice negotiators intend to find some bigger and better excuses to keep the war going, more American men are being killed every hour. Arms merchants know that if peace is achieved and all the gunfire stops, they will not get any benefit. When bullets and shells are whistling, the big shots sit at home, calculating the profits they have earned, and writing magnificent speeches discussing why we should continue to slaughter. Those who are killed, made homeless, and crippled are ordinary people, working people; while on the other hand, women in fur coats and their wealthy husbands, living on Park Avenue, intend to make the people of other countries accept 'our way of life.' If the people work hard enough, Korea can have peace and our children can come back. It is necessary to write to the big shots in Washington and tell them that we want peace and want our people back from Korea. You can save lives by writing immediately."

### 美国工会刊物发表论文  要求立即停止侵朝战争

1952-02-23
第4版()
专栏：

    美国工会刊物发表论文
    要求立即停止侵朝战争
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：据电通社报道：在旧金山发行的基层海船司炉工人的刊物“黑伙伴新闻”，最近发表一篇社论，很明显地表示了美国下层工人对于美国侵朝战争的反对。这篇社论说：“当美国停战谈判代表打算找一些更大的和更好的藉口来使战争继续下去的时候，每一小时都有更多的美国男儿被打死。军火商人知道假使和平实现而所有的炮声都停止了的话，他们就得不到什么利益了。当枪弹、炮弹在呼啸的时候，大头子们却坐在家里，计算着他们赚来的利润，并且写着论述为什么我们应该继续不断屠杀的洋洋大观的讲演辞。那些被杀死的，被弄得无家可归的，被弄成残废的是普通人民，是劳动人民；而另一方面，住在公园路穿着貂皮大衣的女人和她们的阔丈夫，却打算使其他国家的人民接受‘我们的生活方式’。假使人民能努力的话，朝鲜是可以得到和平的，我们的孩子们是可以回来的。必需写信告诉华盛顿的大头子们说，我们要和平，要我们的人从朝鲜回来。你们立即写信就可以挽救生命。”
