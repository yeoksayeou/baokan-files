Title: Our Government Delegation Travels to Mongolia to Attend Marshal Choibalsan's Funeral
Author:
Date: 1952-02-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 8th:
    To express its condolences on the death of Mongolian people's leader, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, Marshal Choibalsan, the Central People's Government of our country has specially appointed Nie Rongzhen, member of the Central People's Government Committee and Acting Chief of General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, to lead a government delegation which flew from Beijing to the capital of the Mongolian People's Republic by special plane on the 8th to attend the funeral. Members of the delegation include Ulanhu, member of the Central People's Government Committee and Chairman of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. The government delegation also includes Ji Yatai, our country's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Mongolian People's Republic, who is currently in Ulaanbaatar. Yang Shangkun, Director of the General Office of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Zhu Zaoguan, Deputy Director of the General Office of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, and Peng Sike, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, went to the airport to see them off. Darharabu, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Mongolian People's Republic in China, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 我政府代表团赴蒙参加乔巴山元帅葬礼

1952-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我政府代表团赴蒙参加乔巴山元帅葬礼
    【新华社八日讯】我国中央人民政府为表示对蒙古人民领袖、蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席乔巴山元帅逝世的哀悼，特派中央人民政府委员会委员、人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻为首的政府代表团于八日由北京乘专机飞往蒙古人民共和国首都参加葬礼。代表团团员包括中央人民政府委员会委员、内蒙古自治区人民政府主席乌兰夫，外交部副部长伍修权，外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航。政府代表团还包括现在乌兰巴托的我国驻蒙古人民共和国特命全权大使吉雅泰。前往机场送行的有：中国共产党中央办公厅主任杨尚昆，外交部办公厅主任王炳南，人民革命军事委员会办公厅副主任朱早观，中央人民政府民族事务委员会办公厅副主任朋斯克。蒙古人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办达尔哈热布等亦往机场送行。
