Title: Korean and Chinese Troops Capture Two Heights on the North Han River, Annihilating 103 Enemy Soldiers, and Killing or Wounding Over 100 Counterattacking Enemy Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1952-02-22
Page: 1

(Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, 21st) Our front-line reporter reports: From February 11th to 15th, the largest battle on the front line in these five days was on the night of the 14th when a unit of our army captured Height 662 east of the North Han River and another height to its southwest in one fell swoop, completely annihilating one platoon each from the reconnaissance company of the Rhee [Syngman Rhee's] puppet army's 3rd Division, the reconnaissance platoon of the 23rd Regiment, and the 10th company of the 3rd Battalion, as well as part of one firepower platoon, totaling 103 enemy soldiers. On the morning of the 15th, an enemy battalion counterattacked the area but was repelled by our army's fierce artillery fire, with over 100 more enemy soldiers killed or wounded.

### 朝中部队攻占北汉江两高地  全歼守敌百零三名  并毙伤反扑敌一百多名

1952-02-22
第1版()
专栏：

    朝中部队攻占北汉江两高地
    全歼守敌百零三名
    并毙伤反扑敌一百多名
    【新华社朝鲜前线二十一日电】本社前线记者报道：自二月十一日至十五日，这五天中在前线上最大的一次战斗是十四日夜我军一部一举攻占北汉江以东六六二高地及其西南另一高地，全歼守敌李伪军第三师搜索营、二十三团搜索连、三营十连的各一个排，并一个火器排的一部，共计一百零三人。十五日晨，敌军一个营向该地反扑，复被我军猛烈炮火击退，又毙伤敌一百多人。
