Title: People from All Walks of Life in Shenyang, Chongqing, Guangzhou, Dihua and Other Cities Grandly Celebrate the Second Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance; Railway Workers in Various Places Hold Rallies to Celebrate and Express Warm Gratitude to Soviet Experts for Their Assistance
Author:
Date: 1952-02-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Report: People from all walks of life in cities such as Shenyang, Dalian, Chongqing, Guangzhou, Dihua, and Fuzhou warmly celebrate the second anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance. People of Northeast China and Shenyang held a grand celebration meeting on the afternoon of the 14th. Gao Gang, Chairman of the Northeast People's Government and President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Northeast Branch, Lin Feng and Gao Chongmin, Vice Chairmen of the Northeast People's Government, Li Zhuoran, Minister of Propaganda of the Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and representatives from various democratic parties and people's organizations, totaling more than 1,000 people, attended. Soviet Consul General in Shenyang, Ledav, and many Soviet experts in Shenyang also attended the celebration meeting. Chairman Gao Gang spoke at the meeting, saying: The people of Northeast China have a particularly deep understanding of the Soviet Union's assistance to China's construction cause. In the past two years, according to the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance, the Soviet Union has provided us with many new types of machinery and equipment, and sent many experienced experts to help us, enabling the economic construction of Northeast China to achieve great achievements in a short period. Under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Communist Party of China, the working class in Northeast China has learned from the advanced experience and technology of the Soviet Union, and has brought its creative power into greater play. Therefore, last year, the working class in Northeast China over-fulfilled the state plan by increasing production and practicing economy by more than 14 million tons of grain. We should especially thank the assistance of our Soviet allies. After Soviet Consul General in Shenyang, Ledav, spoke, Liu Zhiming, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Northeast Branch, read the telegram of respect to Chairman Mao and Marshal Stalin from the assembly. After the meeting, the Northeast People's Government, the Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Northeast Branch jointly held a grand banquet and evening party.

More than 1,500 representatives from all walks of life in Dalian held a grand celebration meeting on the evening of the 14th. Representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation and the Korean People's Delegation to China who arrived in Dalian also participated in the celebration. Qiao Chuanjue, President of the Dalian Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Ouyang Qin, Secretary of the Dalian Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Major General Marsayev, representative of the Soviet Army Command in Lushun, and Soviet Consul General in Dalian, Brodzov, spoke at the meeting.

More than a thousand representatives from all walks of life in Southwest China and Chongqing held a celebration meeting on the afternoon of the 14th. Participants in the meeting included Soviet experts, members of the Southwest Branch of the Korean People's Delegation to China, members of the Southwest Branch of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation, and members of the Xikang Minority Nationalities Visiting Group. He Long, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Southwest Branch, spoke at the meeting. He called for learning from the Soviet Union's construction experience and welcoming the construction work of the great motherland.

At the celebration meeting held by people from all walks of life in Guangzhou on the 14th, Ye Jianying, Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, called for vigorously strengthening Sino-Soviet friendship, building our great motherland, and smashing any aggressive conspiracy of the reactionary camp headed by US imperialism.

At the celebration meeting held by people from all ethnic groups in Dihua on the evening of the 13th, Gao Jinchun, Vice Chairman of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government, said: The people of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang have the deepest understanding of the assistance given to us by the friendly Soviet Union. The establishment of the Sino-Soviet Petroleum Company and the Nonferrous Metals Company, and the restoration and development of trade relations between China and the Soviet Union in Xinjiang have greatly influenced the economic construction of Xinjiang and the improvement of people's lives.

Representatives from all walks of life in Fujian Province and Fuzhou City also held celebration meetings.

Various celebration activities were held in various cities before and after the festival.

Xinhua News Agency Report: Railway workers in various places enthusiastically celebrate the second anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance. More than 2,000 employees of the Central Ministry of Railways held a grand celebration meeting on the 14th. Soviet bridge experts who helped with China's railway construction also participated in the meeting. Minister of Railways Teng Daiyuan pointed out in his speech: Due to the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance, the Chinese people have received great help from the Soviet Union, and in the past two years, they have been able to carry out the restoration and construction of the motherland at an astonishing speed. Thanks to the help of Soviet experts, the demonstration of the China Changchun Railway, and the efforts of hardworking Chinese railway employees, China's old railways have been rapidly restored, and 700 kilometers of new railways have been built. The efficiency of railway departments has also been greatly improved due to learning from Soviet advanced experience. Teng Daiyuan said: However, the wealth created by the bloodshed and sweat of hundreds of thousands of railway employees has been stolen in large quantities by unlawful merchants colluding with corrupt elements. Therefore, he called on all employees to carry the current struggle against corruption and bribery through to the end. Soviet expert Liabiqai, in his speech, assured the assembly on behalf of all Soviet workers working on Chinese railways that they would contribute all their knowledge and strength to China's railway cause. More than 1,000 Chinese and Soviet employees of the China Changchun Railway Administration held a grand celebration meeting on the 14th. Railway workers in Shenyang, Tianjin, Zhengzhou, Jinan, Hengyang and other regions also held meetings to celebrate warmly.

### 沈阳重庆广州迪化等城市各界人民  隆重庆祝中苏友好同盟两周年  各地铁路职工集会庆祝并热烈感谢苏联专家的帮助

1952-02-16
第1版()
专栏：

    沈阳重庆广州迪化等城市各界人民
    隆重庆祝中苏友好同盟两周年
    各地铁路职工集会庆祝并热烈感谢苏联专家的帮助
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：沈阳、旅大、重庆、广州、迪化、福州等城市各界人民热烈庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订两周年。东北和沈阳各界在十四日下午举行了隆重的庆祝会。东北人民政府主席、中苏友好协会东北总分会会长高岗，东北人民政府副主席林枫、高崇民，中共中央东北局宣传部长李卓然及各民主党派、各人民团体代表等一千多人出席。苏联驻沈阳总领事列达夫和在沈苏联专家多人也出席这一庆祝大会。高岗会长在会上讲话，他说：苏联对中国建设事业的帮助，东北人民体会得特别深刻。两年来，根据中苏友好同盟互助条约，苏联供给我们许多新式的机器装备，派来了许多富有经验的专家帮助我们，使东北的经济建设在短期内获得了巨大成就。东北工人阶级在毛主席和中国共产党的领导下，学习了苏联的先进经验和先进技术，也就更加发挥了自己的创造力量。因此，去年东北工人阶级超计划地为国家增产节约了一千四百多万吨粮食。我们应当特别感谢苏联盟友的援助。苏联驻沈阳总领事列达夫讲话后，中苏友好协会东北总分会副会长刘芝明宣读了大会向毛主席和斯大林大元帅致敬的电文。会后，东北人民政府、中共中央东北局和中苏友好协会东北总分会联合举行盛大的宴会和晚会。
    旅大市各界人民代表一千五百余人在十四日晚举行隆重的庆祝会，抵达大连的中国人民志愿军归国代表团代表和朝鲜人民访华代表团代表也参加庆祝。在会上讲话的有旅大中苏友好协会会长乔传玨、中国共产党旅大市委员会书记欧阳钦、驻旅顺苏军指挥部代表马尔赛夫少将、苏联驻大连总领事勃罗卓夫等。
    西南和重庆各界代表千余人在十四日下午举行庆祝大会。参加大会的有苏联专家和朝鲜人民访华代表团西南分团、中国人民志愿军归国代表团西南分团的全体团员，西康少数民族参观团团员等。中苏友好协会西南总分会会长贺龙在大会上讲话。他号召学习苏联的建设经验，迎接伟大祖国的建设工作。
    在广州市各界人民十四日举行的庆祝集会上，广东省人民政府主席叶剑英号召大力加强中苏友谊，建设我们伟大的祖国，粉碎以美帝国主义为首的反动阵营的任何侵略阴谋。
    在迪化各族各界人民十三日晚举行的庆祝集会上，新疆省人民政府副主席高锦纯说：新疆各族人民对友邦苏联给我们的帮助，体会最深。中苏石油公司和有色金属公司的成立，中苏两国在新疆的贸易关系的恢复和发展，对于新疆经济建设的进行和人民生活的改善发生了很大的影响。
    福建省和福州市各界代表也举行了庆祝会。
    各城市并在节日前后分别举行了各种庆祝活动。
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：各地铁路职工热烈庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订二周年。中央铁道部机关二千余职工在十四日举行了隆重的庆祝大会。帮助我国铁路建设的苏联桥梁专家们也参加了大会。铁道部部长滕代远在讲话中指出：由于中苏友好同盟互助条约的签订，中国人民得到苏联很大帮助，两年来得以惊人速度进行祖国的恢复和建设。由于苏联专家的帮助、中国长春铁路的示范，以及勤劳的中国铁路员工的努力，我国旧有铁路已迅速恢复，并且新修了七百公里铁路。铁路各部门工作也因学习了苏联先进经验而效率大大提高。滕代远说：但是几十万铁路员工流血流汗所创造的财富却被不法奸商和贪污分子勾结起来而大量盗窃去了。因此他号召全体职工必须把目前的反贪污、反行贿斗争进行到底。苏联专家俩比才夫在讲话中，代表全体在中国铁路上工作的苏联籍工作者向大会保证，要把所有知识和力量贡献给中国的铁路事业。中国长春铁路管理局中苏职工一千余人，十四日举行盛大庆祝会。沈阳、天津、郑州、济南、衡阳等地区铁路职工也都集会热烈庆祝。
