Title: Iranian Anti-Imperialist Oil Company Struggle Association Resolves to Oppose US Plunder of Iranian Oil
Author:
Date: 1952-02-28
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tehran, February 22nd: The Second Congress of the Iranian National Anti-Imperialist Oil Company Struggle Association has been held in Tehran. After discussing the situation in Iran last year and the report on the association's activities, the representatives and members present passed five resolutions. These resolutions were published in the "Bisu Ayandeh" newspaper on February 21st.

　　In the first resolution, the congress called on the Iranian people to more fully understand the dangers posed by US imperialism and to expose the various anti-national conspiracies being carried out in Iran.

　　The second resolution decided to rename the association the "Iranian National Anti-Imperialist Struggle Association."

　　The third resolution concerned organizational issues.

　　The fourth resolution concerned Iran's general policy, especially its policy on oil. The resolution stated: 1. The congress believes that any foreign participation in the exploration, refining, or marketing of Iranian oil is contrary to the interests of the Iranian people and their motherland; 2. The transfer of southern Iranian oil enterprises to US imperialists is a serious act of betraying the people and the motherland; 3. The Iranian government must give countries that are willing to purchase Iranian oil on acceptable terms equal opportunities; 4. The Iranian government must resist foreign conspiracies aimed at seizing Iranian oil, and it should also be aware that the "International Bank for Reconstruction and Development" is an agency of US imperialism, and any dealings with it are contrary to Iran's interests.

　　The congress resolutions put forward the following demands: in foreign policy, to no longer blindly follow the war and imperialist camp, but to join the democratic countries' bloc; in domestic policy, to abolish treaties that bind Iran, expel foreign advisors and spies, and guarantee freedom of speech, parties, and trade unions; in economic policy, to reduce war expenditure, increase education and public health expenditure in the budget, and stop importing unnecessary goods, etc.

　　The fifth resolution expressed support for the anti-imperialist struggles of the Egyptian and Tunisian people.

### 伊朗反帝国主义石油公司斗争协会  决议反对美国掠夺伊朗石油

1952-02-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　伊朗反帝国主义石油公司斗争协会
    决议反对美国掠夺伊朗石油
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：塔斯社德黑兰二十二日讯：伊朗全国反帝国主义石油公司斗争协会第二届大会已在德黑兰举行。在讨论了去年的伊朗局势和关于协会的活动的报告以后，到会的代表和协会会员通过了五项决议。这些决议都已在二月二十一日的《比苏·阿杨第报》上发表。
    在第一个决议中，大会号召伊朗人民更充分地了解美帝国主义所带来的危险，并号召伊朗人民揭露在伊朗进行的各种反民族阴谋。
    第二个决议决定把协会改名为“伊朗全国反帝国主义斗争协会”。
    第三个决议是关于组织问题的。
    第四个决议是关于伊朗的一般政策的，特别是关于石油问题的政策。决议说：一、大会认为任何外国人参加伊朗石油的开采、提炼或推销，都是和伊朗人民及其祖国的利益相违背的；二、将伊朗南部石油企业出让给美帝国主义者就是严重的出卖人民、出卖祖国的行为；三、伊朗政府必须给予那些愿意根据可以接受的条件购买伊朗石油的国家以平等的机会；四、伊朗政府必须抵抗目的在于攫取伊朗石油的外国阴谋，同时它也应该知道“国际复兴开发银行”是美帝国主义的机构，而和它打任何交道都是违反伊朗的利益的。
    大会决议提出下列要求：在外交政策方面，不再盲目地跟随战争和帝国主义阵营，而应该加入民主国家集团；在内政政策方面，废除束缚伊朗的条约，驱逐外国顾问和间谍，保证言论、党派及工会的自由；在经济政策方面，减少战争开支，增加预算中的教育和公共卫生经费，停止输入不必需的货物等。
    第五个决议表示支持埃及和突尼斯人民的反帝国主义斗争。
