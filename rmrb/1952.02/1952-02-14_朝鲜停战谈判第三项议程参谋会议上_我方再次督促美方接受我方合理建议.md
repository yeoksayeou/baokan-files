Title: On the Third Item Agenda of the Korean Armistice Talks, Our Side Again Urges the U.S. Side to Accept Our Reasonable Proposal
Author:
Date: 1952-02-14
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 13th—At the参谋会议 on the third item on the agenda of the Korean armistice talks on the 13th, our side once again urged the other side to seriously consider and accept our reasonable proposals for a monthly limit of 30,000 military personnel for rotation and four specific rear ports for each side.
    At the 参谋会议 on the fourth item on the agenda, on the basis of comparative study of our proposal of February 3 and the other side's working draft, the two sides completed the exchange of specific opinions. The two sides agreed to negotiate specific wording for the articles that have already been mutually agreed upon, starting tomorrow.

### 朝鲜停战谈判第三项议程参谋会议上  我方再次督促美方接受我方合理建议

1952-02-14
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判第三项议程参谋会议上
    我方再次督促美方接受我方合理建议
    【新华社开城十三日电】十三日朝鲜停战谈判第三项议程参谋会议上，我方再次督促对方郑重考虑并接受我方所提出的每月三万人的军事人员的轮换限额以及每方四个特定后方口岸的合理建议。
    第四项议程参谋会议上，在比较研究我方二月三日提案及对方工作草案的基础上，双方完毕了具体意见的交换。双方同意自明天起就已经互相同意了的各款，商订具体的文字规定。
