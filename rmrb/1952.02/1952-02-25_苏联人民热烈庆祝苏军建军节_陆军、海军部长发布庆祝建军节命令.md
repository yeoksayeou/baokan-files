Title: Soviet People Warmly Celebrate Soviet Army Day; Army and Navy Ministers Issue Orders Celebrating Army Day
Author:
Date: 1952-02-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 23rd: According to TASS, the Soviet people warmly celebrated the 34th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army. On February 22nd, celebration meetings were generally held in Soviet cities and villages. In Moscow, the Soviet Ministry of the Army, together with representatives of the working people, held a celebration meeting at the Central Red Army Theater. The meeting place was decorated solemnly and beautifully. The slogans "Long Live Our Powerful Socialist Motherland!" and "Long Live the Soviet Armed Forces!" were emblazoned on crimson velvet. Portraits of Lenin and Stalin, the founders of the Soviet Armed Forces, hung on the rostrum. An honor guard of infantry, sailors, pilots, tank crewmen, artillerymen, and cavalrymen stood at attention under their flags. The meeting was presided over by Soviet Marshal Sokolovsky, followed by a report by Soviet Marshal Govorov on the 34th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy. When Govorov finally shouted "Long Live the Great Marshal Stalin, the brilliant leader of the Soviet Armed Forces!" a storm of cheers resounded in the hall. The attendees passed a congratulatory telegram to Stalin with great enthusiasm. On the same day, the Soviet Ministry of the Navy also held a celebration meeting with representatives of the working people of Moscow in the Pillar Hall of the House of Trade Unions.

In the days leading up to the festival, grand exhibitions and film screenings were held in many Soviet cities. These days, the Central Museum of the Soviet Army in Moscow has been particularly crowded, with visitors paying tribute to the heroes of the Soviet Army, the outstanding sons and daughters of the Soviet people, with a sense of pride.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 23rd: TASS reports from Moscow: Soviet Army Minister Vasilevsky issued the following order on the 34th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Armed Forces, February 23rd:

Comrade soldiers and sergeants of the Army! Comrade officers, colonels, and generals of the Army!

Today, the Soviet people and their armed forces celebrate the holiday of the Soviet Army and Navy.

On the occasion of the 34th anniversary of the founding of our armed forces, I salute and congratulate you.

I sincerely wish all members of the Soviet Army new and greater achievements in perfecting their military and political knowledge, in further strengthening the combat readiness of the army, and in perfectly fulfilling their military duties of guarding the national interests of our great motherland.

In commemoration of the 34th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy, I order: Today, February 23rd, in Moscow, the capital of our motherland, in the capitals of all the constituent republics, in Kaliningrad, Lviv, Khabarovsk, Vladivostok, and in the hero cities of Leningrad, Stalingrad, Sevastopol, and Odessa, a twenty-gun salute shall be fired in each city.

Long live the Soviet Army and Navy!

Long live the heroic Soviet people and our powerful motherland!

Long live the Soviet government!

Long live the party of Lenin and Stalin, our victorious inspirers and organizers!

Long live the great leader and teacher, the brilliant helmsman, Marshal of the Soviet Union, Comrade Stalin!

Soviet Army Minister, Marshal of the Soviet Union, Vasilevsky

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 23rd: TASS reports from Moscow: Soviet Navy Minister Kuznetsov issued the following order on the 34th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Armed Forces, February 23rd:

Comrade sailors and sergeants of the Navy! Comrade officers, colonels, and generals of the Navy!

Today, the sailors, together with all the Soviet people, celebrate the 34th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy.

The Soviet Army and Navy, organized and nurtured by the great Lenin-Stalin party, have undergone a heroic fighting path and have proven themselves to be reliable defenders of the interests of the Soviet state. Under the leadership of the Bolshevik Party, under the leadership of the great leader and teacher Comrade Stalin, the Soviet Army and Navy greet this glorious festival with new achievements of all personnel in combat training and in military and political education.

Naval personnel must continue to work tirelessly to strengthen combat readiness, cultivate outstanding experts, cultivate those who have outstanding achievements in combat and political training, and continue to strengthen the military discipline, vigilance, and organization of the entire army.

Comrade sailors and sergeants of the Navy! Comrade officers, colonels, and generals of the Navy!

On the occasion of the 34th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy, I salute and congratulate you, and wish you new and greater achievements in combat and political training.

Long live our Soviet motherland!

Long live the heroic Soviet people and their ever-victorious Army and Navy!

Long live the Soviet government!

Long live the party of Lenin and Stalin!

Glory to our great and wise leader and helmsman, Comrade Stalin!

Soviet Navy Minister, Vice Admiral Kuznetsov

### 苏联人民热烈庆祝苏军建军节  陆军、海军部长发布庆祝建军节命令

1952-02-25
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联人民热烈庆祝苏军建军节
    陆军、海军部长发布庆祝建军节命令
    【新华社二十三日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联人民热烈庆祝苏军建军三十四周年。二十二日，苏联各城市和乡村普遍举行了庆祝大会。在莫斯科，苏联陆军部会同劳动人民代表，在中央红军剧院举行了庆祝会。大会会场布置得庄严美丽。鲜红色的丝绒上缀着“我们强大的社会主义祖国万岁！”和“苏联武装部队万岁！”的口号。主席台上挂着苏联武装部队的缔造者列宁和斯大林的画像。步兵、海员、飞行员、坦克手、炮兵和骑兵仪仗队在军旗下肃立。大会由苏联元帅索科洛夫斯基主持开会，接着由苏联元帅戈沃罗夫作了关于苏联陆、海军建军三十四周年纪念的报告。当戈沃罗夫最后高呼苏联武装部队的天才领袖斯大林大元帅万岁时，会场上响起了暴风雨般的欢呼。到会者以极大的热情通过了致斯大林的贺电。同日，苏联海军部也和莫斯科劳动人民代表，在工会大厦圆柱大厅举行了庆祝会。
    在节日前夕，苏联许多城市都举行了盛大的展览会和电影放映会。这些天来，莫斯科苏军中央博物馆特别拥挤，参观的人们都怀着骄傲的心情向苏联人民的优秀儿女——苏军英雄们致敬。
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联陆军部长华西列夫斯基，在二十三日苏联武装部队建军三十四周年纪念日，发布命令如下：陆军战士和士官同志们！陆军尉官、校官和将军同志们！
    今天，苏联人民和他们的武装部队庆祝苏联陆、海军的节日。
    值此我们的武装部队建军三十四周年之际，我向你们致敬和祝贺。
    谨祝苏联军队的全体人员，在使他们的军事政治知识日臻完备上、在进一步加强军队的战斗准备上、在十全十美地克尽警卫我们伟大祖国的国家利益的军事职责上，获得新的更大的成绩。
    为了纪念苏联陆、海军建军三十四周年，我命令：今天，二月二十三日，在我们祖国的首都——莫斯科，在各加盟共和国的首都，在加里宁格勒、罗夫、伯力、海参崴，在列宁格勒、斯大林格勒、塞瓦斯托波尔和敖德萨等英雄城市，各鸣放礼炮二十响。
    苏联陆海军万岁！
    英雄的苏联人民和我们的强大祖国万岁！
    苏维埃政府万岁！
    我们的胜利的鼓舞者和组织者——列宁和斯大林的党万岁！
    伟大的领袖和导师、天才的舵手、苏联大元帅斯大林同志万岁！
    苏联陆军部长、苏联元帅华西列夫斯基
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联海军部长库兹聂佐夫，在二十三日苏联武装部队建军三十四周年纪念日，发布命令如下：海军战士和士官同志们！海军尉官、校官和将军同志们！
    今天，海军战士和苏联全体人民一道，庆祝苏联陆、海军建军三十四周年。
    苏联陆军和海军在伟大的列宁—斯大林党的组织和培育下，曾经经历了英雄的战斗道路，并且已经证明了它们是苏维埃国家利益的可靠的保卫者。苏联陆军和海军在布尔什维克党的领导下，在伟大的领袖和导师斯大林同志的领导下，以全体人员在战斗教练、在军事和政治教育方面的新成就来迎接这个光荣的节日。
    海军人员们必须继续不倦地努力，加强战斗准备，造就优秀的专家，造就在战斗和政治训练方面有卓越成就的人，并继续加强全军的军纪、警惕和组织。
    海军战士和士官同志们！海军尉官、校官和将军同志们！
    值此苏联陆、海军建军三十四周年，我向你们致敬和祝贺，祝你们在战斗和政治训练方面取得新的更大的成就。
    我们的苏维埃祖国万岁！
    英雄的苏联人民和他们的常胜的陆海军万岁！
    苏维埃政府万岁！
    列宁和斯大林的党万岁！
    光荣归于我们伟大贤明的领袖和舵手斯大林同志！
    苏联海军部长、海军中将库兹聂佐夫
