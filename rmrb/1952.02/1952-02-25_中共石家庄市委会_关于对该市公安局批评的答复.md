Title: CPC Shijiazhuang Municipal Committee: Reply Regarding Criticism of the City's Public Security Bureau
Author:
Date: 1952-02-25
Page: 2

To the Editor:

The readers' exposure of certain leading cadres of our city's Public Security Bureau obstructing the anti-corruption, anti-waste, and anti-bureaucracy movement is true. This matter has been resolved in a timely manner. The Municipal Committee personally went to assist the bureau in rectifying the command headquarters for the anti-corruption, anti-waste, and anti-bureaucracy movement, and placed those with serious suspicions of corruption and leading cadres who suppressed democracy on suspension, removal from office, and ordered them to reflect within a time limit. After the obstacles to the anti-corruption, anti-waste, and anti-bureaucracy movement were removed, the masses actively launched a siege against corrupt elements.

The CPC Shijiazhuang Municipal Committee

### 中共石家庄市委会  关于对该市公安局批评的答复

1952-02-25
第2版()
专栏：对人民日报读者批评建议的反应

    中共石家庄市委会
    关于对该市公安局批评的答复
    编辑同志：读者揭发我市公安局某些领导干部阻碍反贪污、反浪费、反官僚主义运动，所述事实都是真实的。这件事已及时得到解决。市委曾亲自前往帮助该局整顿了反贪污、反浪费、反官僚主义运动的指挥部，把有严重贪污嫌疑的分子和压制民主的领导干部分别停职、撤职、限期反省。反贪污、反浪费、反官僚主义的障碍被扫除后，群众积极向贪污分子展开了围剿。中共石家庄市委员会
