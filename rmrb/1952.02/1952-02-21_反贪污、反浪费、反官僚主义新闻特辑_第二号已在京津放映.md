Title: "Anti-Corruption, Anti-Waste, Anti-Bureaucracy News Special" No. 2 Screened in Beijing and Tianjin

Author:
Date: 1952-02-21
Page: 3

    "Anti-Corruption, Anti-Waste, Anti-Bureaucracy News Special" No. 2 Screened in Beijing and Tianjin
    The Beijing Film Studio's "Anti-Corruption, Anti-Waste, Anti-Bureaucracy News Special" No. 2 has been completed and is being screened in cinemas in Beijing and Tianjin. The content of No. 2 is: "Beijing Public Trial of Major Corrupt Elements Conference." The film fully illustrates that the People's Government will severely punish major corrupt elements who have committed serious crimes and refuse to confess according to law; and will leniently treat major corrupt elements who voluntarily confess and can perform meritorious service to atone for their crimes. The Beijing Film Studio's "Anti-Corruption, Anti-Waste, Anti-Bureaucracy News Special" No. 1 was warmly welcomed by the majority of the audience after it was shown in various places. (Xinhua News Agency)

### “反贪污、反浪费、反官僚主义新闻特辑”第二号已在京津放映

1952-02-21
第3版()
专栏：

    “反贪污、反浪费、反官僚主义新闻特辑”第二号已在京津放映
    北京电影制片厂的“反贪污、反浪费、反官僚主义新闻特辑”第二号已完成，并已在京津两地电影院放映。第二号内容为：“北京公审大贪污犯大会”。该片充分说明了：人民政府对罪恶严重拒不坦白的大贪污犯必依法严惩；对自动坦白并能立功赎罪的大贪污犯则从宽处理。北京电影制片厂的“反贪污、反浪费、反官僚主义新闻特辑”第一号在各地演出后，曾受到广大观众的热烈欢迎。    （新华社）
