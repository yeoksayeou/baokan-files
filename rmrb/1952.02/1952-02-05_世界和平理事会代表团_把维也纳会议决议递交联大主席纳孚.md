Title: World Peace Council Delegation Submits Vienna Conference Resolutions to UN General Assembly President Nasrollah Entezam

Author:
Date: 1952-02-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 1st dispatch from Paris: TASS reports: On the evening of January 30th, UN General Assembly President Nasrollah Entezam received a delegation from the World Peace Council composed of Joliot-Curie, Nenni, Gott, Farge, Mrs. Gordon, Professors Bruhm and Bonnerde. They presented to Entezam the resolutions passed at the Second Session of the World Peace Council held in Vienna and delivered the World Peace Council's letter to the United Nations, as adopted at the conference.

Entezam and the representatives of the World Peace Council conferred for more than an hour.

Xinhua News Agency, January 1st dispatch from Paris: TASS reports: The World Peace Council delegation issued the following report after visiting the President of the United Nations General Assembly:

At 16:00 today, Mr. Nasrollah Entezam, President of the United Nations General Assembly, received a delegation from the World Peace Council. The delegation consisted of Joliot-Curie, President of the World Peace Council, Mrs. Gordon and Nenni, Vice-Presidents of the World Peace Council, and Bruhm, Professor Bonnerde, Gott, and Farge, all members of the World Peace Council. The World Peace Council delegation presented Mr. Entezam with the resolutions passed by the World Peace Council at its Vienna meeting, especially the resolution on the simultaneous reduction of armaments by the major powers under international supervision. Mr. Entezam expressed his concern for the solutions proposed by the World Peace Council. This meeting clearly demonstrated the important role of public opinion in the discussion of international issues.

Paris, January 30, 1952

### 世界和平理事会代表团  把维也纳会议决议递交联大主席纳孚

1952-02-05
第4版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会代表团  
    把维也纳会议决议递交联大主席纳孚
    【新华社一日讯】塔斯社巴黎讯：一月三十日晚间，联合国大会主席纳孚接见了由约里奥—居里、南尼、戈特、法奇、戈登夫人、布伦姆和波纳德教授组成的世界和平理事会代表团。他们向纳孚提出了在维也纳召开的世界和平理事会第二次会议所通过的决议，并且把这次会议通过的世界和平理事会告联合国书递交纳孚。
    纳孚和世界和平理事会的代表晤谈了约一小时有余。
    【新华社一日讯】塔斯社巴黎讯：世界和平理事会代表团在访问了联合国大会主席后发表报告如下：
    今天十六时，联合国大会主席纳孚先生接见了世界和平理事会代表团。代表团系由世界和平理事会主席约里奥—居里，世界和平理事会副主席戈登夫人和南尼，世界和平理事会理事布伦姆、波纳德教授、戈特和法奇诸人组成。世界和平理事会代表团向纳孚先生递交了世界和平理事会维也纳会议所通过的决议，特别是关于各大国在国际监督下同时裁减军备的决议。纳孚先生表示关心世界和平理事会提出的办法。这次会晤极其明确地证明了舆论在国际问题的讨论上所起的重要作用。
    一九五二年一月三十日于巴黎
