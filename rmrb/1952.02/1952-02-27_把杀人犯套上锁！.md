Title: Put the Murderers in Chains!
Author: Yuan Shuipai
Date: 1952-02-27
Page: 3

Put the Murderers in Chains!
	Yuan Shuipai

	What the Japanese devils did,
		He does it all;
	What Hitler did,
		He does on a larger scale!
	Electric light blinds the eyes,
	Gasoline burns the body,
	Killing, branding, steaming,
	Treating our people as germ warfare test subjects,
	We are angry!
	We denounce!
	We shout to the people of the world:
	"Arise, put the murderers in chains!"

	What the Japanese devils did,
		He does it all;
	What Hitler did,
		He does on a larger scale!
	In Buchenwald, in Auschwitz,
	Bone meal from the German Nazis burning people still remains in the grass roots;
	In Suwon, on Geoje Island,
	The American bandits' killing fields are even darker and more cruel!
	We are angry!
	We denounce!
	We shout to the people of the world:
	"Arise, put the murderers in chains!"

	What the Japanese devils did,
		He does it all;
	What Hitler did,
		He does on a larger scale!
	The jackals sit in airplanes, pull out paper packets,
	Dropping poisonous mosquitoes, flies, fleas,
	Enemies of all mankind,
	Stretching out his evil blood claws to all mankind!
	We are angry!
	We denounce!
	We shout to the people of the world:
	"Arise, put the murderers in chains!"

### 把杀人犯套上锁！
袁水拍
1952-02-27
第3版()
专栏：

    把杀人犯套上锁！
    袁水拍
    是日本鬼子干过的，
    他都干；
    是希特勒干过的，
    他更大规模地干！
    电光射眼，
    汽油烧身，
    杀的杀，烙的烙，蒸的蒸，
    把我们的人当细菌武器试验品，
    我们愤怒！
    我们控诉！
    我们向全世界人民高呼：
    “起来，把杀人犯套上锁！”
    是日本鬼子干过的，
    他都干；
    是希特勒干过的，
    他更大规模地干！
    在布痕瓦尔德，在奥斯威辛，
    草根里还留着德国纳粹烧人的骨粉；
    在水原，在巨济岛，
    美国强盗的杀人场更加黑暗、残忍！
    我们愤怒！
    我们控诉！
    我们向全世界人民高呼：
    “起来，把杀人犯套上锁！”
    是日本鬼子干过的，
    他都干；
    是希特勒干过的，
    他更大规模地干！
    豺狼坐着飞机，摸出纸包，
    投下有毒的蚊子，   苍蝇，跳蚤，
    和全人类为敌，
    向全人类伸出他的罪恶的血爪！
    我们愤怒！
    我们控诉！
    我们向全世界人民高呼：
    “起来，把杀人犯套上锁！”
