Title: Important Step in Implementing the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet; Establishment of the People's Liberation Army Tibet Military Region; General Zhang Jingwu Announces Order of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Representatives of the Tibetan Local Government and Others Express Warm Congratulations and Support
Author:
Date: 1952-02-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 19th: In accordance with Article 15 of the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet, the People's Liberation Army Tibet Military Region was established on the 10th. Attending the establishment meeting were cadres at all levels of the People's Liberation Army troops stationed in Lhasa and the Tibetan local troops, as well as more than 300 people including officials of the Tibetan local government and representatives of the three major monasteries who came to congratulate. At the meeting, General Zhang Jingwu, representative of the Central People's Government, first announced the order of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government appointing cadres of the People's Liberation Army Tibet Military Region, followed by a speech by General Zhang Guohua, Commander of the Tibet Military Region. He first pointed out that the establishment of the Tibet Military Region is an important step in implementing the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet. Then, he pointed out that the tasks of the Tibet Military Region are to defend the national defense, build national defense, consolidate national defense, drive the imperialist aggressor forces out of Tibet, maintain local public security, protect the interests of the Tibetan monks and lay people, build people's armed forces, and reorganize the Tibetan local troops into the People's Liberation Army; and the most urgent task at present is to actively complete the construction and production tasks and strive for self-sufficiency in grain in order to reduce the burden on the Tibetan people. Finally, he called on all commanders and fighters of the troops entering Tibet to actively unite with the cadres of the Tibetan local government, patiently listen to their opinions, and actively help the Tibetan local troops with their exemplary actions, so that they can be reorganized into the People's Liberation Army as soon as possible. Apei Awang Jinmei, the first deputy commander of the Tibet Military Region, and Duo Ge Peng Cuo Rao Jie, the second deputy commander, both unanimously agreed in their speeches that the establishment of the Tibet Military Region is a great joyous event in the history of the Tibetan monks and lay people, an important step in thoroughly implementing the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet, and a powerful guarantee for driving the imperialist forces out of Tibet, consolidating national defense, and protecting the lives and property of people of all strata in Tibet. They also emphasized that under the leadership of the Tibet Military Region, the Tibetan local army must closely unite with the People's Liberation Army, strive to learn the fine style of the People's Liberation Army, and strive to be reorganized into the People's Liberation Army as soon as possible. Garon Lhalu Tsewang Dorje, representative of the Tibetan local government, and Pingcuo Wangjie, deputy director of the Changdu Area People's Liberation Committee, also spoke successively, expressing their warm congratulations and support for the establishment of the Tibet Military Region. Garon Lhalu, representative of the Tibetan local government, also stated that he would vigorously assist the People's Liberation Army in consolidating national defense and jointly build a prosperous and happy new Tibet in the future.
    Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 19th: Gesang Wangdui, the ninth generation Ben Zhu official of the former Tibetan local army and all officers and soldiers who gloriously revolted in the Changdu area in 1950, sent a telegram to General Zhang Guohua, Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Tibet Military Region, and General Tan Guansan, Political Commissar, and all the leaders of the Military Region, congratulating the establishment of the Tibet Military Region. The telegram said: "We respectfully hear that the Chinese People's Liberation Army Tibet Military Region was established on the 10th of this month. This is a major victory for our Tibetan people after the peaceful liberation of all Tibet; it is a concrete implementation of the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet; it is a powerful guarantee for our Tibetan people to achieve complete liberation and a happy life. Gesang Wangdui and all officers and soldiers extend their congratulations with incomparable joy and love." The telegram said: "In the year since our officers and soldiers revolted, with the strong help of the People's Liberation Army troops entering Tibet, we have initially learned revolutionary theory, and we all feel infinitely happy that the Tibetan people can enjoy the warmth of the motherland's big family. In order to defend the happy life that the motherland and the Tibetan people have already obtained, we resolutely, under the direct leadership of the Tibet Military Region, drive the imperialist aggressor forces out of Tibet, consolidate national defense, and defend our hometown." The telegram concluded: "Now we would like to celebrate the establishment of the Tibet Military Region by doing a good job in local public security work and practicing production and thrift."

### 实现和平解放西藏办法协议的重要步骤  人民解放军西藏军区成立  张经武将军宣布人民革命军事委员会命令西藏地方政府代表等表示热烈祝贺和拥护

1952-02-20
第1版()
专栏：

    实现和平解放西藏办法协议的重要步骤
    人民解放军西藏军区成立
    张经武将军宣布人民革命军事委员会命令西藏地方政府代表等表示热烈祝贺和拥护
    【新华社拉萨十九日电】依照和平解放西藏办法的协议第十五条规定，中国人民解放军西藏军区已在十日成立。参加成立大会的有人民解放军驻拉萨部队和西藏地方部队的各级干部，及前来祝贺的西藏地方政府官员和三大寺的代表共三百多人。会上首由中央人民政府代表张经武将军宣布中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会任命人民解放军西藏军区干部的命令，继由西藏军区司令员张国华将军讲话。他首先指出西藏军区的成立，是实现和平解放西藏办法的协议的重要步骤，接着他指出西藏军区的任务是保卫国防、建设国防、巩固国防，驱逐帝国主义的侵略势力出西藏，维护地方治安，保护西藏僧俗人民的利益，建设人民武装，改编西藏地方部队为人民解放军；而目前急需要做的事是积极完成修建生产任务，争取粮食自给，以减轻西藏人民负担。最后他号召进藏部队全体指挥员和战斗员主动团结西藏地方政府干部，耐心听取他们的意见，并以自己的模范行动积极帮助西藏地方部队，使他们早日改编为人民解放军。西藏军区第一副司令员阿沛·阿旺晋美和第二副司令员朵噶·彭错饶杰在讲话中都一致认为，西藏军区的成立是西藏僧俗人民历史上的大喜事，是彻底实现和平解放西藏办法的协议的一项重要步骤，也是驱逐帝国主义势力出西藏，巩固祖国国防，保护西藏各阶层人民生命财产的有力保证。他们并着重指出，在西藏军区的领导下，西藏地方军队必须和人民解放军亲密团结，努力学习人民解放军的优良作风，争取早日改编为人民解放军。西藏地方政府代表噶伦拉鲁·泽旺多吉和昌都地区人民解放委员会副主任凭错旺阶也相继讲话，他们对西藏军区的成立一致表示热烈的祝贺和拥护。西藏地方政府代表噶伦拉鲁并表示，今后要大力协助人民解放军巩固国防，共同建设繁荣幸福的新西藏。
    【新华社拉萨十九日电】一九五零年在昌都地区光荣起义的前西藏地方军队第九代本主官格桑旺堆和全体官兵，致电中国人民解放军西藏军区司令员张国华和政治委员谭冠三两将军及军区各首长，祝贺西藏军区的成立。电文说：“敬闻中国人民解放军西藏军区于本月十日成立，这是我藏族人民在得到全西藏和平解放后的重大胜利；是和平解放西藏办法的协议的具体实施；是我藏族人民获得彻底解放和幸福生活的有力保障。格桑旺堆暨全体官兵谨以无比欢欣和热爱的心意致以祝贺。”电文说：“我全体官兵起义一年多来，在人民解放军入藏部队的大力帮助之下，已初步学习了革命理论，我们都为藏族人民享受到祖国大家庭的温暖而感到无限幸福。为了捍卫祖国和藏族人民业已获得的幸福生活，我们坚决在西藏军区的直接领导下，驱逐帝国主义侵略势力出西藏，巩固国防，保卫家乡。”电文最后说：“现在我们谨以做好地方治安工作和厉行生产节约的实际行动，来庆祝西藏军区的成立。”
