Title: Chongqing's Second Surgical Team for Korea Set Off to Work on the Korean Front

Author:
Date: 1952-02-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, February 5th: The ten members of Chongqing's Second Surgical Team for Korea set off on the 4th to work on the Korean front. Before their departure, more than 400 health workers from Chongqing held a farewell meeting. Representatives Zhang Kaizhen and Zhang Boying from the Southwest Sub-delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation, as well as representatives Shen Donggen and Zhao Shengzhe from the Korean People's Delegation to China's Southwest, also attended the meeting and reported on the heroic deeds of the soldiers at the front.

### 重庆市援朝第二外科手术队  启程赴朝鲜前线工作

1952-02-07
第4版()
专栏：

    重庆市援朝第二外科手术队
    启程赴朝鲜前线工作
    【新华社重庆五日电】重庆市志愿援朝第二外科手术队队员十人在四日出发到朝鲜前线工作。出发前，重庆市卫生工作者四百多人举行欢送会；中国人民志愿军归国代表团西南分团代表张凯镇、张伯英以及朝鲜人民访华代表团西南分团代表申东根、赵圣哲等也参加了大会，并报告了前线战士们的英勇事迹。
