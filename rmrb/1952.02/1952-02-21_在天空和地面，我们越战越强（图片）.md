    In the Sky and on the Ground, We Grow Stronger as We Fight
    (Sketches from the Korean Battlefield, Part 6) Shao Yu
    Following the enthusiastic movement by the people of the motherland to donate airplanes and cannons to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea, the Chinese and Korean People's forces on the Korean battlefield have seen increasingly powerful air forces, artillery, and tank units. The fate of destruction for the imperialist aggressor bandits is sealed.

专栏：

    在天空和地面，我们越战越强
    （朝鲜战场素描之六）  邵宇
    在祖国人民轰轰烈烈的抗美援朝捐献飞机大炮运动之后，朝鲜战场上中、朝人民部队，出现了日益强大的空军、炮兵、和坦克部队。帝国主义侵略强盗毁灭的命运是注定了的。
