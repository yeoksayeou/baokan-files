Title: Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace Protests Japanese-Chiang Preparation for Illegal Treaty
Author:
Date: 1952-02-19
Page: 4

    Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace
    Protests Japanese-Chiang Preparation for Illegal Treaty
    [Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, 15th] According to Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, on the 15th: The Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace issued a statement on the 14th, protesting the Japanese reactionary government's guarantee to conclude an illegal separate "peace treaty" with the Chiang Kai-shek remnant bandit gang.
    The statement said: On December 24, 1951, the Yoshida government notified its American masters that it would sign a separate peace treaty with Chiang Kai-shek—the faithful running dog of American imperialism in Taiwan—to continue the farce of San Francisco.
    The people and peace fighters of Vietnam lodge a serious protest against this and pledge to work closely with the Chinese people to oppose the reactionary conspiracies of the imperialists and their running dogs, and are determined to fight harder, kill more enemy troops, restore independence and defend world peace.

### 越南保卫世界和平委员会  抗议日蒋准备非法缔和

1952-02-19
第4版()
专栏：

    越南保卫世界和平委员会
    抗议日蒋准备非法缔和
    【新华社十八日讯】据越南通讯社北越十五日讯：越南保卫世界和平委员会在十四日发表声明，抗议日本反动政府保证和蒋介石残余匪帮缔结非法单独“和约”。
    声明说：一九五一年十二月二十四日，吉田政府通知它的美国主子们说：它要和蒋介石——美帝国主义在台湾的忠实走狗——签订单独和约，继续进行旧金山的滑稽剧。
    越南的人民与和平战士们对此提出严重抗议，并保证要和中国人民密切合作，反对帝国主义者及其走狗们的反动阴谋，决心更加努力战斗，杀死更多的敌军，恢复独立和保卫世界和平。
