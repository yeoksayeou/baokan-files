Title: Second Anniversary of the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between China and Vietnam; President Ho Chi Minh Sends a Telegram to Chairman Mao to Congratulate

Author:
Date: 1952-02-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. On the occasion of the second anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between the People's Republic of China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, President Ho Chi Minh of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of China, extending friendly congratulations. Chairman Mao Zedong replied to President Ho Chi Minh on February 4th, expressing his gratitude. The two telegrams are as follows:

(1) Telegram from President Ho Chi Minh to Chairman Mao Zedong
To His Excellency Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:

On the occasion of the second anniversary of the People's Republic of China's recognition of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, I would like to extend, on behalf of the Vietnamese people and government, our friendly congratulations to you, your government, and the Chinese people.

I wish the Chinese people brilliant achievements under your leadership in the movement to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea and in the construction of the new China. Due to the victory of the Chinese people and their warm friendship, I am confident that the Vietnamese people will surely achieve victory in the struggle against the imperialist aggressors for national independence and freedom. Wishing you good health.

Ho Chi Minh
President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam

(2) Chairman Mao Zedong's Reply to Comrade Ho Chi Minh, Chairman of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam:

Thank you for your friendly congratulations on the second anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between the People's Republic of China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. I am confident that the Vietnamese people's resolute and unremitting, persistent struggle against imperialism and for national independence and freedom will, under your leadership and with the sympathy of peace-loving people in Asia and around the world, ultimately achieve final victory.

Mao Zedong
February 4, 1952, Beijing

### 我国和越南建交二周年纪念  胡志明主席致电毛主席祝贺

1952-02-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我国和越南建交二周年纪念  
    胡志明主席致电毛主席祝贺
    【新华社四日讯】越南民主共和国政府胡志明主席曾于中华人民共和国与越南民主共和国建交二周年纪念时，致电我国中央人民政府毛泽东主席，致以友谊的祝贺。毛泽东主席已于二月四日电复胡志明主席，表示感谢。两电文如下：
    （一）胡志明主席致毛泽东主席的贺电中华人民共和国主席毛泽东阁下：
    值此中华人民共和国承认越南民主共和国二周年纪念之际，我谨代表越南人民与政府向您、贵国政府及中国人民致以友谊的祝贺。
    我预祝中国人民在您的领导下，在抗美援朝运动及新中国的建设中获得辉煌的成就。由于中国人民的胜利以及他们亲切的友谊，我确信越南人民必将在为争取民族独立与自由的反对帝国主义侵略者的斗争中得到胜利。谨祝健康。
    越南民主共和国主席　胡志明
    （二）毛泽东主席复电越南民主共和国政府主席胡志明同志：
    感谢您在中华人民共和国与越南民主共和国建交二周年纪念时的友谊祝贺。我确信：越南人民反对帝国主义、争取祖国独立与自由的坚决不懈的持久斗争，在您的领导下，在亚洲及全世界爱好和平人民的同情下，终将获得最后的胜利。  
    毛泽东  一九五二年二月四日  北京
