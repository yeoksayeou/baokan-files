Title: British King George VI Dies

Author:

Date: 1952-02-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, 7th. British King George VI died on the morning of the 6th in Sandringham, Norfolk. Princess Elizabeth, the King's eldest daughter, formally inherited the throne on the 7th and became Queen Elizabeth II.

### 英国国王乔治六世病逝

1952-02-08
第4版()
专栏：

    英国国王乔治六世病逝
    【新华社七日讯】伦敦消息：英国国王乔治六世于六日清晨在诺福克郡桑德棱汉地方病逝。英王长女伊丽莎白公主七日正式继承王位，为女王伊丽莎白二世。
