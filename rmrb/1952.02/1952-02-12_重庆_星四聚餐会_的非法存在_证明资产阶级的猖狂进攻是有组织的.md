Title: The Illegal Existence of the Chongqing "Star Four Dinner Party" Proves that the Bourgeoisie's Frenzied Attack is Organized
Author: Ge Jingzhi, An Ruixia
Date: 1952-02-12
Page: 2

Reader's Letters

    The Illegal Existence of the Chongqing "Star Four Dinner Party"
    Proves that the Bourgeoisie's Frenzied Attack is Organized
    Editor: From the news about the Chongqing big capitalists Gao Gongmao and others secretly and illegally organizing the "Star Four Dinner Party" published in the People's Daily on February 4th, we can see that the bourgeoisie's attack on us is not only conscious and planned, but also organized. Some people say: The bourgeoisie hasn't even held a meeting together, how could they "planned" to attack us? The fact that the illegal organization "Star Four Dinner Party" of capitalists was uncovered in Chongqing is a powerful explanation, and this fact fully refutes any sophistry of the bourgeoisie.
    We hate these capitalists from the bottom of our hearts, they are the enemies of the people!
    Ge Jingzhi An Ruixia

### 重庆“星四聚餐会”的非法存在  证明资产阶级的猖狂进攻是有组织的
葛景智  安瑞霞
1952-02-12
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    重庆“星四聚餐会”的非法存在
    证明资产阶级的猖狂进攻是有组织的
    编辑同志：我们从二月四日人民日报刊载的关于重庆大奸商高功懋等秘密非法组织“星四聚餐会”的消息，可以看出资产阶级向我们进攻，不但是有意识、有计划的，而且是有组织的。有人说：资产阶级又没有一起开过会，怎么会“有计划”地向我们进攻呢？重庆破获的这个奸商非法组织“星四聚餐会”的事实就是一个有力的说明，这一事实充分驳斥了资产阶级的任何诡辩。
    我们从心底里仇恨这些奸商，他们是人民的敌人！
    葛景智  安瑞霞
