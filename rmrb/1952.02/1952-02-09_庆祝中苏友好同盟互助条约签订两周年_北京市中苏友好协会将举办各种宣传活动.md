Title: Celebrating the Second Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance: Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to Hold Various Promotional Activities
Author:
Date: 1952-02-09
Page: 1

    Celebrating the Second Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance
    Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to Hold Various Promotional Activities
    [This newspaper's dispatch] To celebrate the second anniversary of the signing of the Sino- Soviet Treaty Of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance on the fourteenth of this month, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association will hold celebrations and various promotional activities before and after this great holiday.
    1. The Association will jointly hold a celebration and a report meeting introducing the Soviet Union with the Soviet Union Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries: On February 12th, Comrade Sinilov will speak on "The Improvement of the Welfare of the Soviet People," followed by a screening of the film "Prosperous Ukraine" with Chinese narration; on the 14th, Comrade Sgotsko, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy, ​​will give report on celebrating the second anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance; on the 16th, Comrade Vinogradova will speak on "The Face of the Soviet People in Literature," followed by Soviet music and dance performances, all at the Soviet Union Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries on Nanheyan Street.
    2. From February 10th to 16th, all cinemas in the city will show more Soviet films. Members of the Association will receive a 20% discount with their membership cards.
    3. From February 14th to 16th, from 7:30 PM to 8:00 PM every day, the Beijing People's Broadcasting Station (850 and 1080 kilocycles) will teach the songs "Great Unity of the Chinese and Soviet Peoples" and other songs.
    4. On February 13th and 15th, Soviet film evenings will be held at the Soviet Union Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries every day.
    5. All branch associations will universally exhibit photos of "Friendship and Cooperation between the Chinese and Soviet Peoples" and "The Life of Soviet Farmers."

### 庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订两周年  北京市中苏友好协会将举办各种宣传活动

1952-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订两周年
    北京市中苏友好协会将举办各种宣传活动
    【本报讯】本月十四日是中苏友好同盟互助条约签订两周年，北京市中苏友好协会将于这个伟大节日的前后，举行庆祝和各种宣传活动。
    一、该会与苏联对外文化协会联合举行庆祝会和介绍苏联的报告会：二月十二日由西尼洛夫同志讲“苏联人民福利的提高”，会后放映华语解说的“繁荣的乌克兰”影片；十四日由苏联大使馆参赞司高磋同志作关于庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订两周年的报告；十六日由维诺格拉多娃同志讲“苏联人民在文学中的面貌”，会后并有苏联音乐舞蹈等表演，地点皆在南河沿苏联对外文化协会。
    二、全市各电影院自二月十日至十六日多放映苏联电影，该会会员凭会员证八折优待。
    三、二月十四至十六日每日下午七时半至八时，在北京人民广播电台（八五○和一○八○千周）教唱“中苏人民大团结”等歌曲。
    四、二月十三、十五日，每日在苏联对外文化协会举办苏联电影晚会。
    五、各支会并普遍展览“中苏两国人民的友好与合作”和“苏联农民生活”的照片。
