Title: USSR Establishes Preparatory Committee for International Economic Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-02-15
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 13th: According to TASS, the Soviet Union has established a national preparatory committee in view of the International Economic Conference to be held in Moscow in April. The preparatory committee was initiated by the All-Union Chamber of Commerce, the All-Union Cooperative Association, the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, the Institute of Economics of the USSR Academy of Sciences, some industrial and commercial groups, and people from scientific and economic circles. The committee members include: Nesterov, Chairman of the All-Union Chamber of Commerce (Chairman of the Committee); Kuznetsov, Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions; Kokholov, Chairman of the All-Union Cooperative Association; Sakharov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union; Kuzmin, Deputy Minister of the Iron and Steel Industry of the Soviet Union; Lyov, Deputy Minister of the Light Industry of the Soviet Union; Grashchenko, Deputy Governor of the State Bank of the Soviet Union; Nichkov, Chairman of the "Exporter" Organization; Vlasov, Director of the Stalin Automobile Factory; Ostrovityanov, Director of the Institute of Economics of the USSR Academy of Sciences; Academician Strumilin, Academician Bardin, and Orlov, Director of the Scientific Research Institute attached to the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union.

### 苏联成立国际经济会议筹备委员会

1952-02-15
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联成立国际经济会议筹备委员会
    【新华社十三日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：鉴于国际经济会议将于四月在莫斯科举行，苏联已成立了全国筹备委员会。筹备委员会是由全苏商会、全苏合作社协会、全苏工会中央理事会、苏联科学院经济研究所、一些工商业团体和科学界、经济界人士等倡议成立的。委员会的委员包括：全苏商会主席奈斯特洛夫（委员会主席），全苏工会中央理事会主席库兹涅佐夫，全苏合作社协会主席贺何洛夫，苏联对外贸易部副部长萨哈洛夫，苏联钢铁工业部副部长库兹明，苏联轻工业部副部长李若夫，苏联国家银行副行长格拉申科，“出口商”组织主席尼契阔夫，斯大林汽车工厂厂长伏拉索夫，苏联科学院经济研究所所长奥斯特罗维捷扬诺夫，经济学家斯特鲁米林院士、巴丁院士和苏联对外贸易部附设科学研究所所长奥尔洛夫。
