    Our Great Motherland
    Plum blossom is a famous ornamental plant native to China and blooms everywhere around the Spring Festival every year. It has been planted since the Han Dynasty, and after a long period of cultivation, nearly two hundred varieties have been produced. Its fruit is edible and medicinal. This is a white plum blossom from Mudu, Suzhou.

    我们伟大的祖国
    梅花是我国原产的著名观赏植物的一种，每年春节前后到处盛开。它从汉代起就开始被种植，经过人们长期的培育，产生了近二百样的品种。它的果实可供食用和药用。这是苏州木渎的白梅花。
