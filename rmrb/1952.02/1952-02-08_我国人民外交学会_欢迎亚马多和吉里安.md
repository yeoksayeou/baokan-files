Title: China People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Welcomes Amado and Guillén
Author:
Date: 1952-02-08
Page: 4

    China People's Institute of Foreign Affairs
    Welcomes Amado and Guillén

    [Xinhua News Agency, 7th] The Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs held a reception on the evening of the 7th to welcome Jorge Amado, the renowned Brazilian writer and recipient of the Stalin International Peace Prize, and Nicolás Guillén, member of the Cultural Exchange Committee of the World Peace Council, who are visiting China. Among those present at the reception were Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, and members of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, including Zhou Gengsheng, Chen Hansheng, Shao Lizi, Qian Duansheng, Mei Ru'ao, Zhang Jiongbo, Qu Wu, Liu Guanyi, Wu Maosun, Meng Xianzhang, Ma Jian, and Jin Kemu, totaling more than 140 people. Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, delivered a speech first, extending a warm welcome to the two famous peace warriors. He said: The close friendship between the Chinese people and the people of Brazil, Cuba, and other Latin American countries in the struggle to defend world peace will be further developed due to the visit of Amado and Guillén. Subsequently, Amado and Guillén respectively reported on the situation of the struggles of the people of Brazil, Cuba, and other Latin American countries against the aggression of U.S. imperialism and for peace, democracy, and national independence. Guillén also recited his famous poems – "A Black Man Sings in New York" and "Song of China." Guo Moruo composed a poem on the spot and recited it himself. The reception concluded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

    Since arriving in Beijing on January 31, Amado and Guillén have visited rural areas near Beijing, met with emancipated peasants, and toured scenic spots in the capital.

### 我国人民外交学会  欢迎亚马多和吉里安

1952-02-08
第4版()
专栏：

    我国人民外交学会
    欢迎亚马多和吉里安
    【新华社七日讯】中国人民外交学会七日晚举行欢迎会，招待前来我国访问的斯大林国际和平奖金得奖人、巴西著名作家亚马多和世界和平理事会文化交流委员会委员、古巴诗人吉里安。出席欢迎会的有中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会主席郭沫若，中国人民外交学会会员周鲠生、陈翰笙、邵力子、钱端升、梅汝璈、张絅伯、屈武、刘贯一、吴茂荪、孟宪章、马坚、金克木等一百四十余人。首由中国人民外交学会会长张奚若致词，对两位著名的和平战士的来临表示热烈的欢迎，他说：中国人民与巴西、古巴和拉丁美洲各国人民之间在保卫世界和平斗争中的亲密友谊必将因亚马多和吉里安的来访而获得进一步发展。接着由亚马多和吉里安分别报告了巴西、古巴和拉丁美洲各国人民反对美帝国主义的侵略和争取和平、民主与民族独立的斗争的情况。吉里安并朗诵了所作名诗——“一个黑人在纽约歌唱”和“中国之歌”。郭沫若当场和诗一首并亲自朗读。欢迎会在热烈友好的气氛中结束。
    亚马多和吉里安自一月三十一日抵京后，曾访问了北京近郊农村与翻身农民晤谈并赴首都各名胜区游览。
