Title: Communist Party Member Zhang Ziliang Consistently Maintains a Hardworking and Simple Style (Picture)
Author: Wang Jiao, Shao Guohuan
Date: 1952-02-02
Page: 3

     A Communist Party Member, Zhang Ziliang, Consistently Maintains a Hardworking and Simple Style
     Written by Wang Jiao and Shao Guohuan (with picture)
    (10) When he was still living on the supply system, his father suddenly came to see him. Someone advised him to buy some things for his father, which could be reimbursed by the public. He said, "What qualifications do I have to spend public money?"
    (Attached picture)
    (11) After the salary system was changed, he was rated at over 800 points. But he still lived a very frugal life, with a lot of monthly savings.
    (Attached picture)
    (12) Someone advised him not to be too austere and to replace his tattered cotton quilt. He said, "What I ate and wore in the past, I feel this is already very good." Because he never forgot the instructions the Party gave him when he went from Yan'an to the Northeast: "After going from the countryside to the city, maintain the Party's tradition of hard work and simplicity."

### 一贯保持艰苦朴素作风的  共产党员张子良（图片）
王角  邵国寰
1952-02-02
第3版()
专栏：

     一贯保持艰苦朴素作风的  
     共产党员张子良
     王角  邵国寰  作（附图片）   
    （十）当他还过着供给制生活的时候，他父亲忽然来看他。有人劝他买些东西送他父亲，可以由公家报销。他说，“我有什么资格支公家钱用？”
    （附图片）    
    （十一）改了工薪制度，他被评给八百多分。可是他仍然过着十分俭朴的生活，每月有很多节余。
    （附图片）    
    （十二）有人劝他不要太刻苦，把那床破棉被换一换，他说：“从前吃什么，穿什么，我觉得这样已经满好了。”因为他时刻也没忘记，他从延安去东北时，党给他的指示“从农村到城市以后，要保持党的坚苦朴素的传统”。
