Title: All-China Sports Federation Cables International Olympic Committee

Author: 
Date: 1952-02-13
Page: 4

All-China Sports Federation
    Cables International Olympic Committee
    Notifying Participation in the Current Olympic Games
    [Xinhua News Agency, February 12th] The All-China Sports Federation cabled the Secretariat of the International Olympic Committee, notifying its continued participation in the International Olympic Games organization and participation in the current Olympic Games. The text of the cable is as follows: To the Secretariat of the International Olympic Committee:
    In accordance with China's past participation in previous Olympic Games, the All-China Sports Federation has decided to continue participating in the International Olympic Games organization and has decided to participate in the International Olympic Games to be held in Helsinki in July this year. We now notify your committee that the All-China Sports Federation is the only sports organization representing the People's Republic of China. No other group, including the sports representatives of the Kuomintang reactionary clique in Taiwan, can be regarded as any legitimate representative of China, nor can they be allowed to participate in this Olympic Games and its related meetings. We have now learned that your committee will hold an Olympic meeting in Oslo on February 15th, and we are preparing to participate. Please inform us of the agenda of the meeting and the number of personnel required for us to attend the meeting, which would be greatly appreciated.
    All-China Sports Federation
    February 5, 1952, in Beijing

### 我国体育总会  致电国际奥林匹克委员会

1952-02-13
第4版()
专栏：

    我国体育总会
    致电国际奥林匹克委员会
    通知参加本届奥林匹克运动大会
    【新华社十二日讯】中华全国体育总会致电国际奥林匹克委员会秘书处，通知继续参加国际奥林匹克大会的组织并参加本届奥林匹克运动大会，电文如下：国际奥林匹克委员会秘书处：
    中华全国体育总会根据中国在过去参加历届奥林匹克大会的关系，决定仍继续参加国际奥林匹克大会的组织，并决定参加今年七月间在赫尔辛基举行的国际奥林匹克运动大会。我们现在通知贵会：中华全国体育总会是代表中华人民共和国的唯一体育组织，任何其他团体，包括台湾中国国民党反动集团的体育代表在内，不能作为中国的任何合法代表，亦不能容许其参加此届奥林匹克运动大会及其有关的会议。现闻贵会将在二月十五日在奥斯陆举行奥林匹克会议，我们准备参加，请即将该会议程及须要我们参加会议之人员数目通知我们，实为至盼。中华全国体育总会
    一九五二年二月五日于北京
