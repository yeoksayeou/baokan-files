Author:
Date: 1952-02-17
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Gao Guandou, an agricultural labor model in Shen County, Hebei Province, responded to the call of the People's Government and went to various villages to help transform alkaline land in order to realize the county's plan to transform 10,000 mu of alkaline land. This is a picture of Gao Guandou and the members of the alkaline land transformation committee of Gaoguzhuang Village studying the soil.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-02-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    河北深县农业劳动模范高贯斗响应人民政府的号召，为了实现全县改造一万亩碱地的计划，到各村去帮助改造碱地。这是高贯斗和高古庄村改造碱地委员会的委员们研究土质的情形。
