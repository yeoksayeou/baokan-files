Title: General Kim Il-sung Awarded the DPRK First Class Order of Freedom and Independence; Supreme People's Assembly Awards Orders to Park Heon-yeong and Others with Special Meritorious Service
Author:
Date: 1952-02-15
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 14th: The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, on the occasion of the fourth anniversary of the founding of the Korean People's Army, awarded General Kim Il-sung, Supreme Commander of the People's Army, the First Class Order of Freedom and Independence, praising his great achievements in the work of creating, organizing, and strengthening the People's Army, as well as in the Fatherland Liberation War against the American aggressors and their running dog, the Syngman Rhee bandit gang.
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 14th: The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea issued an order on the eve of the fourth anniversary of the founding of the Korean People's Army, awarding the First Class Order of Freedom and Independence and the National Flag Order to forty-three party, government, and military personnel who had made special meritorious service in the Fatherland Liberation War.
    Park Heon-yeong made special contributions to the strengthening of political organizational work; the People's Army and government security organ generals Kim Ung, Kim Man, Kim Kwang-hyop, Nam Il, Park Il-u, Park Jeong-deok, Bang Hak-se, Ryu Kyung-soo and twenty-one others, and People's Army senior officers Nam Kyung-soo, Lee Hyun-sang and eight others, displayed patriotic devotion and military creativity, and achieved great victories in the war; Vice Minister of Internal Affairs Lee Pil-gyu and Ko Hui-man, the leader of special production departments, also made special meritorious service in their work; all of the above were awarded the First Class Order of Freedom and Independence.
    Park Chang-ok, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, Lee Byung-nam, Minister of Public Health, and Heo Jeong-suk, Minister of Culture and Propaganda, and six officers of the People's Army and public security organs, Han Myung-shin, and non-commissioned officer Ahn Bong-sun, were also awarded the First Class National Flag Order for special meritorious service. Ki Seok-bok, Deputy Minister of Culture and Propaganda, and Lee Mun-jo, Deputy Director of the Propaganda and Agitation Department of the Central Committee of the Labor Party, were awarded the Second Class National Flag Order.

### 金日成将军荣获朝鲜一级自由独立勋章  最高人民会议以勋章授予朴宪永等有特殊功勋人员

1952-02-15
第4版()
专栏：

    金日成将军荣获朝鲜一级自由独立勋章
    最高人民会议以勋章授予朴宪永等有特殊功勋人员
    【新华社平壤十四日电】朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会在纪念朝鲜人民军建军四周年之际，授予人民军最高司令官金日成将军以一级自由独立勋章，表扬他在创建、组织和加强人民军的工作中以及在反抗美国侵略者及其走狗李承晚匪帮的祖国解放战争中所建立的伟大功勋。
    【新华社平壤十四日电】朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会在朝鲜人民军建军四周年前夕发布命令，分别以一级自由独立勋章和国旗勋章授予在祖国解放战争中建立了特殊功勋的党、政、军工作人员四十三人。
    朴宪永对政治组织工作的加强有特殊的贡献；人民军及政府保安机关将领金雄、金万、金光侠、南日、朴一禹、朴正德、方学世、柳京洙等二十一人及人民军高级军官南庆洙、李铉相等八人，发挥了爱国主义的献身精神和军事创造性，在战争中取得了巨大胜利；内务省副相李弼圭及特殊生产部门的领导者高熙万，也在工作中建立了特殊功勋；上述诸人都荣获一级自由独立勋章。
    朝鲜劳动党中央委员会书记朴昌玉、内阁保健相李炳南和文化宣传相许贞琡三人及人民军和公安机关的军官韩明信、下士军官安凤善等六人，也因特殊功勋荣获一级国旗勋章。文化宣传省副相奇石福、劳动党中央委员会宣传鼓动部副部长李文朝两人荣获二级国旗勋章。
