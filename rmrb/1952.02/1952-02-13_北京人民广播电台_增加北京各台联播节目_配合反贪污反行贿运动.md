Title: Beijing People's Broadcasting Station Increases Joint Broadcasts from Beijing Stations to Cooperate with the Anti-Corruption and Anti-Bribery Movement
Author:
Date: 1952-02-13
Page: 2

In order to cooperate with the anti-corruption, anti-waste, and anti-bureaucracy movement, and the anti-bribery, anti-tax evasion, anti-theft of state property, anti-shoddy work and use of inferior materials, and anti-theft of state economic intelligence movement, the Beijing People's Broadcasting Station, in addition to strengthening coordinated propaganda in its original radio programs, has added a joint broadcast program for all broadcasting stations in the city. The time is from 1:00 PM to 1:30 PM every day, reporting news, important recordings, commentaries, and reports related to the movement. All broadcasting stations in the city will broadcast simultaneously. The broadcasting stations simultaneously broadcasting this special program are: Beijing People's Broadcasting Station, frequencies 850 kHz and 1080 kHz; Beijing People's Broadcasting Station Second, Third, and Fourth Stations, frequencies 920 kHz, 750 kHz, and 1320 kHz; and the privately-owned Huasheng Broadcasting Station, frequency 1470 kHz. It is hoped that all people in the city, especially the industrial and commercial sectors of the city, will pay attention to listening.

### 北京人民广播电台  增加北京各台联播节目  配合反贪污反行贿运动

1952-02-13
第2版()
专栏：

    北京人民广播电台
    增加北京各台联播节目
    配合反贪污反行贿运动
    北京人民广播电台为了配合反贪污、反浪费、反官僚主义运动和反行贿、反偷税漏税、反盗骗国家资财、反偷工减料、反盗窃国家经济情报运动，除了在原有各广播节目内加强配合宣传外，特增加一项全市各广播电台联播节目，时间为每天下午一点到一点三十分，报道有关运动的新闻、重要录音和评论、通讯等。全市广播电台同时联播。联播这项特别节目的广播电台有：北京人民广播电台周率为八五○千周、一○八○千周，北京人民广播电台第二、三、四台周率为九二○千周、七五○千周、一三二○千周，私营华声广播电台周率为一四七○千周，希望全市人民，尤其是本市工商业界注意收听。
