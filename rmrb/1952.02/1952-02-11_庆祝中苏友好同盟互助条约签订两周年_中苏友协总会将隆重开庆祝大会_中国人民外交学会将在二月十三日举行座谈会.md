Title: Celebrating the Second Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance; Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters to Hold Grand Celebration Rally; Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to Hold Symposium on February 13th

Author: 
Date: 1952-02-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. In order to celebrate the second anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship Alliance, and mutual assistance. the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters has decided to hold a grand celebration rally in Beijing on February 14th; Li Jishen, Huang Yanpei, and Shen Junru, vice presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters, Xu Deheng, director, Guo Moruo, vice president, and Zhang Bojun, director, will successively give radio speeches on the Central People's Broadcasting Station from February 10th to 15th. The radio lectures will begin at 8:00 PM every day. More than 90,000 sets of pictures of "Soviet Farmers' Lives" and "Friendship and Cooperation Between the People of China and the Soviet Union" and 4,000 sets of slides of "Soviet Collective Farms" and "Soviet Advanced Production Increase Experience - Granular Fertilizer" printed by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters have now been distributed throughout the country for widespread exhibition and screening.

The Central People's Broadcasting Station has decided to hold a "Sino-Soviet Friendship" music broadcast from February 11th to 17th: broadcasting Chinese music to the Soviet Union once every day from 7:45 AM to 8:00 AM; broadcasting Soviet music for 20 minutes to the whole country every night from 7:00 PM to 8:00 PM.

The Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs will hold a symposium on the second anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance at 7:00 PM on February 13th at the association.

### 庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订两周年  中苏友协总会将隆重开庆祝大会  中国人民外交学会将在二月十三日举行座谈会

1952-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订两周年
    中苏友协总会将隆重开庆祝大会
    中国人民外交学会将在二月十三日举行座谈会
    【新华社十日讯】为庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订两周年，中苏友好协会总会决定于二月十四日在京举行隆重的庆祝大会；中苏友协总会副会长李济深、黄炎培、沈钧儒、理事许德珩、副会长郭沫若和理事章伯钧将依次于二月十日至十五日在中央人民广播电台作广播讲演。广播讲演每天下午八时开始。中苏友协总会印制的“苏联农民生活”和“中苏两国人民的友好与合作”图片九万多套及“苏联的集体农庄”和“苏联先进增产经验—颗状肥料”幻灯片四千套，现已分发全国各地广泛展览和放映。
    中央人民广播电台决定自二月十一日至十七日举办“中苏友好”音乐广播：每天早晨七点四十五分至八点对苏联广播中国音乐一次；每晚七至八时间向全国广播苏联音乐二十分钟。
    中国人民外交学会将在二月十三日晚七时在该会举行关于中苏友好同盟互助条约签订两周年的座谈会。
