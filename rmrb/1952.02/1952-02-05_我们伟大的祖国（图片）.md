Author:
Date: 1952-02-05

Comrade Yu Chunyin is the leader of the patrol team at the Maigen Road Freight Depot in Shanghai. He is loyal, honest, and incorruptible in his service to the people's cause. In his struggle against unscrupulous merchants and villains to protect state property, he demonstrated the excellent qualities of the working class and was approved to join the glorious Chinese Communist Party. This is Comrade Yu Chunyin carefully inspecting the goods.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-02-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    于春银同志是上海麦根路货栈巡守队的小组长。他对人民事业忠诚老实，廉洁奉公。他为了保护国家财产，在和奸商、恶棍作斗争中，表现了工人阶级的优秀品质，并被批准参加了光荣的中国共产党。这是于春银同志在认真地检查货物的情形。
