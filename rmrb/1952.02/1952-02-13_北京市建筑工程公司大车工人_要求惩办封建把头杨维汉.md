Title: Beijing Municipal Construction Engineering Company Truck Workers Demand Punishment for Feudal Foreman Yang Weihan
Author: Zhang Fa
Date: 1952-02-13
Page: 2

Reader's Letters:

    Beijing Municipal Construction Engineering Company Truck Workers
    Demand Punishment for Feudal Foreman Yang Weihan
    Comrade Editor: We are truck workers from the 5119 construction site of the Beijing Municipal Construction Engineering Company. Since July of last year, the truck team leader, feudal foreman Yang Weihan, has deducted 30,000 yuan from each of our wages every month, totaling 960,000 yuan over two months. Yang said, "This is to give gifts to cadre Huang Zihui, who manages and allocates the trucks, as a 'reward.' Anyone who disagrees shouldn't think about hauling trucks here." He managed the workers' meals, and in just two months, embezzled more than 3 million yuan. Yang Weihan used this money to bribe cadres. As far as we know, cadres Huang Zihui, Ma Yingbo, Sun Yongsheng, Yang Shutang, Duan Tongyuan, etc., have all received bribes from him. Therefore, Yang obtained convenience and began to steal state property. Yang stole more than 1,900 jin of scrap steel from the construction site, four large carts of wooden boards, and more than forty sacks. Regarding the illegal actions of feudal foreman Yang Weihan in exploiting workers and bribing cadres, we demand that the Beijing Municipal Construction Engineering Company immediately handle this matter. Zhang Fa

### 北京市建筑工程公司大车工人  要求惩办封建把头杨维汉
张发
1952-02-13
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    北京市建筑工程公司大车工人
    要求惩办封建把头杨维汉
    编辑同志：我们是北京市建筑工程公司五一一九工地的大车工人。去年七月以后，大车组长、封建把头杨维汉，在我们的工资内，每人每月扣发三万元，两个月共扣了九十六万元。杨说：“这是给管理调配大车的干部黄子辉送礼‘酬劳’用的。谁不同意，谁就别想在这里拉车。”他管理工人的伙食，只两个月，就贪污了三百多万元。杨维汉用这些款子贿赂干部。据我们知道，干部黄子辉、马英波、孙永升、杨树唐、段同元等，都受过他的贿赂，因此，杨得到了方便，开始偷盗国家财产。杨曾偷走工地的碎钢筋头一千九百多斤，木板四大车，麻袋四十多条。对封建把头杨维汉剥削工人和贿赂干部的不法行为，我们要求北京市建筑工程公司立即予以处理。  张发
