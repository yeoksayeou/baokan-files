Title: Our Government Delegation Returns to Beijing After Attending Marshal Choibalsan's Funeral
Author:
Date: 1952-02-13
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, February 12th: The four-member government delegation dispatched by the Central People's Government of our country to the capital of the Mongolian People's Republic to attend Marshal Choibalsan's funeral, consisting of Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Ulanhu, Chairman of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, returned to Beijing by special plane on February 12. Those who went to the airport to welcome them included: Yang Shangkun, Director of the General Office of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Wei Baoshan, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department, and An Dong, Director of the Foreign Liaison Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission. Darhazhav, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Mongolian People's Republic in China, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 参加乔巴山元帅葬礼后  我政府代表团返京

1952-02-13
第1版()
专栏：

    参加乔巴山元帅葬礼后
    我政府代表团返京
    【新华社十二日讯】我国中央人民政府派赴蒙古人民共和国首都参加乔巴山元帅葬礼的政府代表团一行四人：人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻，内蒙古自治区人民政府主席乌兰夫，外交部副部长伍修权，外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航，已于二月十二日乘专机返抵北京。前往机场欢迎的有：中国共产党中央办公厅主任杨尚昆，外交部办公厅主任王炳南，交际处副处长魏宝善，人民革命军事委员会对外联络处处长安东。蒙古人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办达尔哈热布等亦往机场欢迎。
