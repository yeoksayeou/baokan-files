Title: At the Invitation of Student Unions in England and Other Places, Chinese Student Delegation Arrives in Britain for a Visit
Author: 
Date: 1952-02-01
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, London, 27th—A Chinese student delegation arrived in London on the 25th for a three-week visit at the invitation of the National Union of Students of England, Wales, and Northern Ireland. The head of the delegation is Yuan Yongxi, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Students' Federation. The delegation includes student representatives from Renmin University of China, Beijing, Shanghai, and other cities. The delegation will visit universities in major British cities to enhance friendship and mutual understanding between Chinese and British students.

### 应英格兰等地学联的邀请  我国学生代表团抵英访问

1952-02-01
第4版()
专栏：

    应英格兰等地学联的邀请  
    我国学生代表团抵英访问
    【新华社二十七日讯】伦敦消息：中国学生代表团应英格兰、威尔士、北爱尔兰全国学生联合会的邀请，在二十五日抵伦敦作三个星期的访问。代表团团长是中华全国学生联合会副秘书长袁永熙，团员包括中国人民大学、北京、上海和其他城市各大学的学生代表多人。代表团将到英国各大城市访问各大学，以增进中英两国学生之间的友谊和相互了解。
