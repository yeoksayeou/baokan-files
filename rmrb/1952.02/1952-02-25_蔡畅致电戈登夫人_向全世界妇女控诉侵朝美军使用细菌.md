Title: Cai Chang Calls Mrs. Gordon, Accusing American Troops in Korea of Using Bacteria to the Women of the World
Author:
Date: 1952-02-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd: Cai Chang, President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, sent a telegram to Mrs. Gordon, President of the Women's International Democratic Federation, accusing the American aggressors of the heinous crime of using bacteria in Korea. The telegram is as follows: Dear Mrs. Gordon:

　　We accuse the American aggressors of the heinous crime of using bacteria in Korea to the women and mothers of the world with boundless anger. The American aggressors disregarded human justice and international law, and openly and on a large scale used bacteriological weapons to carry out inhumane bacteriological warfare against the heroic Korean People's Army, the Chinese People's Volunteers, and the peaceful residents of Korea. We call on all peace-loving, humanitarian, and just people in the world, especially the peace-loving women and mothers of all countries under your leadership, to immediately condemn the American aggressors and impose severe sanctions on them.

President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Cai Chang

### 蔡畅致电戈登夫人  向全世界妇女控诉侵朝美军使用细菌

1952-02-25
第1版()
专栏：

    蔡畅致电戈登夫人
    向全世界妇女控诉侵朝美军使用细菌
    【新华社二十三日讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅致电国际民主妇女联合会主席戈登夫人，控诉美国侵略者在朝鲜使用细菌作战的滔天罪行，电文如下：亲爱的戈登夫人：
    我们以无比的愤怒向全世界的妇女和母亲们控诉美国侵略者在朝鲜使用细菌作战的滔天罪行。美国侵略者竟置人类的正义与国际公法于不顾，公开大规模地使用细菌武器向英勇的朝鲜人民军、中国人民志愿军及朝鲜的和平居民实行灭绝人性的细菌战争，我们要求全世界爱好和平、主张人道与正义的人们，特别是您领导下的各国爱好和平的妇女们，母亲们，立即对美国侵略者加以声讨并予以严厉的制裁。
    中华全国民主妇女联合会主席  蔡畅
