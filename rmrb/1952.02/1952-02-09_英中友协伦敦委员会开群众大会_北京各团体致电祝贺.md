Title: Mass Meeting Held by the London Committee of the Britain-China Friendship Association; Beijing Organizations Send Congratulatory Telegrams
Author: 
Date: 1952-02-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The London Committee of the Britain-China Friendship Association will hold a mass meeting at Beaver Hall, London, on February 9th. The main aim of the meeting is to strive for peace in the Far East and the world, and to demand peace, friendship, and trade between Britain and China. People's organizations in Beijing have jointly sent a telegram of congratulations, which reads as follows:
    To Mr. Joseph Needham, President; Mr. Illsley, Secretary; Mr. Pritt, of the London Committee of the Britain-China Friendship Association:
    We are delighted to learn that the London Committee of the Britain-China Friendship Association will hold a mass meeting on February 9th, aimed at striving for peace in the Far East and the world, and demanding friendly trade between Britain and China. We represent all the people of Beijing, the capital of the People's Republic of China, in extending our congratulations to the meeting, and take this opportunity to pay our respects to the British people and the citizens of London who are friendly to China!

    We warmly endorse the meeting's call for the British people to work hard to maintain and develop normal peaceful, friendly, and trade relations with China, and to jointly strive for and defend peace in the Far East and the world. This is entirely in the interests of the people of both China and Britain, and it is the consistent wish of the people of both countries. We wish the conference success.

    China Peace Committee
    Beijing Branch
    Beijing Federation of Trade Unions
    Beijing Peasants Association
    Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce
    Beijing Students Federation
    Beijing Democratic Youth Federation
    Beijing Democratic Women's Federation
    Beijing Federation of Literary and Art Workers
    February 7, 1952 Beijing

### 英中友协伦敦委员会开群众大会  北京各团体致电祝贺

1952-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    英中友协伦敦委员会开群众大会
    北京各团体致电祝贺
    【新华社八日讯】英中友好协会伦敦委员会订于二月九日在伦敦碧佛厅举行群众大会，会议的主旨在争取远东及世界和平并要求英国与中国和平、友好及贸易。北京市各人民团体特联名去电致贺，电文如下：英中友好协会伦敦委员会会长  李约瑟主席  伊勒贝秘书  德里邦先生：
    欣闻英中友好协会伦敦委员会订于二月九日举行旨在争取远东及世界和平并要求英国与中国友好贸易的群众大会。我们代表中华人民共和国首都北京的全体人民向大会致贺，并借此机会向对中国友好的英国人民及伦敦市民致敬！
    我们热情赞成会议号召英国人民努力维护并发展对中国和平、友好与贸易的正常关系，并为争取与保卫远东及世界和平而共同奋斗。这是完全符合中英两国人民的利益的，也是中英两国人民一贯的意愿。我们预祝大会成功。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会
    北京市分会
    北京市总工会
    北京市农民协会
    北京市工商业联合会
    北京市学生联合会
    北京市民主青年联合会
    北京市民主妇女联合会
    北京市文学艺术工作者联合会
    一九五二年二月七日  北京
