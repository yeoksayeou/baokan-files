Author:
Date: 1952-02-03

    Our Great Motherland
    In extremely difficult combat environments, the Chinese People's Volunteer Army has carried forward the spirit of hard work and plain living, demonstrated the noble quality of cherishing the property of the motherland, and used various methods to avoid the loss of materials. At the front, Volunteer Army soldiers are inspecting empty gasoline drums. They pour out every last drop of gasoline from the bottom, unwilling to waste a single drop.

### 我们伟大的祖国(附图片）

1952-02-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国人民志愿军在极端艰苦的战斗环境中，发扬了艰苦朴素的作风，表现了热爱祖国财产的高贵品质，用种种办法避免物资的损失。在前方，志愿军战士们正在检查空的汽油桶。他们把油底子一点一点地倒出来，不肯浪费一滴汽油。
