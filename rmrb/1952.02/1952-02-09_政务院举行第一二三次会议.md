Title: Government Administration Council Holds 123rd Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-02-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 8th:

    The 123rd meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 8th. After listening to the explanation made by Li Shucheng, Minister of Agriculture of the Central People's Government, the meeting approved the instruction on vigorously launching a mass drought prevention and anti-drought campaign.

### 政务院举行第一二三次会议

1952-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一二三次会议
    【新华社八日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百二十三次政务会议在八日下午举行。会议在听取中央人民政府农业部李书城部长所作的说明后，通过了关于大力开展群众性的防旱、抗旱运动的指示。
