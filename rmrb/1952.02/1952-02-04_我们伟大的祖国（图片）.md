    Our Great Motherland
    In the great struggle of the people of the whole country against corruption, waste, and bureaucracy, the broad masses of Communist Youth League members have also played an active role. Dong Wanli, a member of the Communist Youth League at the Northeast Commercial Specialized School, waged an unyielding struggle against the corrupt and degenerate element Su Zhengjia and finally achieved victory. This is a picture of Comrade Dong Wanli exposing the crimes of corrupt elements at a League branch meeting.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-02-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在全国人民反对贪污、反对浪费、反对官僚主义的伟大斗争中，广大的青年团员也起了积极的作用。东北商业专门学校青年团员董万里，向着贪污蜕化分子苏正家进行不屈不挠的斗争，终于取得了胜利。这是董万里同志正在团小组会上揭发贪污分子罪行时的情形。
