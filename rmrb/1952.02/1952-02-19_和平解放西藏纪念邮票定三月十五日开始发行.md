Title: Commemorative Stamps for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet to be Issued Starting March 15th
Author:
Date: 1952-02-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 17th:

The Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government will begin issuing commemorative stamps for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet on March 15th of this year. These stamps are horizontal rectangles, with the words "Peaceful Liberation of Tibet" printed side-by-side in both Han and Tibetan languages; there are two designs: a panoramic view of the Potala Palace in Lhasa and Tibetan farmers plowing with yaks; the face values are 400 yuan (orange-red), 800 yuan (purple-red) (emerald green), and 1,000 yuan (blue-violet); sales will cease on September 30th of this year.

### 和平解放西藏纪念邮票定三月十五日开始发行

1952-02-19
第1版()
专栏：

    和平解放西藏纪念邮票定三月十五日开始发行
    【新华社十七日讯】中央人民政府邮电部将在今年三月十五日开始发行和平解放西藏纪念邮票。这种邮票是横式长方形，上面并排地印着“和平解放西藏”字样的汉、藏两族文字；
    有拉萨的布达拉宫全景和西藏农民用牦牛耕作的两种图案；面值分四百元（橘红）、八百元（紫红）（翠绿）、一千元（青莲）三种；到今年九月三十日停止发售。
