Title: Ministry of Light Industry Issues Notice to All Regions to Conscientiously Implement "Measures for Inspecting Military Food"
Author:
Date: 1952-02-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd: The Central Ministry of Light Industry issued a notice on the 20th, requesting that the industrial departments of all major administrative regions and the industrial and commercial bureaus of all provinces and cities, in conjunction with the movement against corruption, waste, and bureaucracy, conscientiously implement the "Measures for Inspecting Military Food." The goal is to thoroughly expose the sabotage of unscrupulous merchants and corrupt elements in the production of military food and other military supplies, in order to protect the health of the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

### 轻工业部通知各地切实执行  “军用食品检查办法”

1952-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

    轻工业部通知各地切实执行
    “军用食品检查办法”
    【新华社二十三日讯】中央轻工业部在二十日发出通知，要求各大行政区工业部及各省市工业厅工商局结合反贪污、反浪费、反官僚主义运动，切实执行“军用食品检查办法”，彻底揭露不法奸商和贪污分子在承制军用食品及其他军需品中的暗害行为，以保障中国人民志愿军和中国人民解放军的健康。
