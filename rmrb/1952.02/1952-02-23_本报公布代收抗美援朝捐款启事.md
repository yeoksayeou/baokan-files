Title: This Newspaper Announces Solicitation of Donations for Resistance Against U.S. Aggression and Aid to Korea
Author:
Date: 1952-02-23
Page: 8

Since the Chinese People's Volunteers went to Korea to fight, this newspaper has successively received consolation money and weapon donations from readers to the Volunteers. This newspaper has transferred these funds to the China Resist America Aid Korea Headquarters and the People's Bank of China Beijing Branch, and most of the receipts from the China Resist America Aid Korea Headquarters and the People's Bank have been sent to the donors. However, some donations were given to the Wangfujing office of the People's Bank of China Beijing Branch, and the office only gave this newspaper a general receipt, so this receipt could not be sent to the donors separately. Now, the names and donation amounts of these donors are published below. Those who have already sent receipts will not be published again. If you have not received a receipt and are not listed below, please write to this newspaper for inquiries.

### 本报公布代收抗美援朝捐款启事

1952-02-23
第8版()
专栏：

    本报公布代收抗美援朝捐款启事
    自从中国人民志愿军赴朝作战以来，本报陆续收到读者捐献给志愿军的慰问金及武器捐款。本报已将这些款项，分别转交抗美援朝总会及人民银行北京分行，并将抗美援朝总会及人民银行的收据，大部寄给捐款人。惟其中有部分捐款，在送交人民银行北京分行王府井办事处时，该办事处仅付给本报一总收据，以致此项收据，无法分别寄给捐款人。现将这部分捐款人的姓名及捐款额公布于后，凡已经寄给收据者，则不再公布。如有未收到收据，且未在下列名单中公布者，请捐款人迳函本报查询。
