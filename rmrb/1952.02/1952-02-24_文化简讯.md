Cultural Briefs

    ▲ More than 7,000 educational workers from middle and primary schools in Guangzhou organized winter vacation study classes, starting from February 1st, to study documents related to opposing corruption and waste, etc. More than 5,000 of them participated in propaganda work. Leading their studies was a study committee jointly formed by the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Cultural and Educational Department of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, the Guangzhou Municipal Bureau of Culture and Education, the Preparatory Committee of the Guangzhou Federation of Trade Unions, the Preparatory Committee of the Guangzhou Education Trade Union, and the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League.
    ▲ The fourth issue of the "New Observer" magazine this year has been published. This issue focuses on the anti-corruption, anti-waste, and anti-bureaucracy movement, featuring articles such as Liu Ningyi's "Bravely and Resolutely Fight Against Illegal Merchants," Xie Juezai's "Public Trial of Tigers," and seven articles written by shop employees themselves exposing and accusing the crimes of illegal capitalists.
    In addition, this issue also contains Liu Geping's special article "Unprecedented Great Unity of All Nationalities in China," Xia Guang's Korean correspondence "The Tremendous Power Generated by Class Friendship," Zhang Xiaohu's rural correspondence "Han En Mutual Aid Group Embarks on the Road of Agricultural Cooperatives," Xu Guangyao's "To the Soviets," Zang Kejia's book review "Gratifying Harvest," and six life stories by Lü Risheng and others. The cover of this issue is Shao Yu's sketch "Volunteer Army Soldiers and Comfort Bags."

### 文化简讯

1952-02-24
第3版()
专栏：文化简讯

    文化简讯
    ▲广州市各中、小学校教育工作者七千多人，组织了寒假学习班，由二月一日起分别进行有关反对贪污浪费等文件的学习，其中有五千多人曾出动参加宣传工作。领导他们学习的是中共广州市委会、广东省人民政府文教厅、广州市人民政府文教局、广州市总工会筹备会、广州市教育工会筹备会、青年团广州市工作委员会联合组成的学习委员会。
    ▲新观察杂志今年第四期已经出版。这期以反贪污、反浪费、反官僚主义运动为中心，登有刘宁一的“勇敢地、坚决地和不法商人作斗争”，谢觉哉的“公审老虎”等文章，还有店员工人自己写的揭露和控诉不法资本家罪恶的文章七篇。
    此外，这一期还登载有刘格平的专文“中国各民族空前大团结”、夏光的朝鲜通讯“阶级友爱产生的巨大力量”、张啸虎的农村通讯“韩恩互助组走上农业合作社的道路”、徐光耀的“寄苏联人”、臧克家的书评“可喜的收获”、以及吕日生等的生活小故事六则。这期的封面是邵宇的速写“志愿军战士和慰问袋”。
