Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Voices Support for General Strike in Tunisia
Author:
Date: 1952-02-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, January 29th: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram to the Tunisian General Labour Union to express its support for the nationwide general strike in Tunisia. The telegram reads as follows:
    To the Tunisian General Labour Union: On behalf of the Chinese working class, we extend our heartfelt respect and warm support to the workers and staff throughout Tunisia who are engaged in a nationwide general strike against the barbaric suppression by the French colonial authorities, and we wish you even greater victories in this struggle against the cruel rule of French imperialism and for national liberation.
    All-China Federation of Trade Unions
    January 29th

### 我全国总工会声援突尼斯总罢工

1952-02-02
第4版()
专栏：

    我全国总工会  
    声援突尼斯总罢工
    【新华社二十九日讯】中华全国总工会为突尼斯全国举行总罢工事致电突尼斯工会联合会，以示支援。电文如下：
    突尼斯工会联合会：谨代表中国工人阶级向突尼斯全国各地为反对法国殖民当局的野蛮镇压而进行的全国总罢工的工人和职员致以衷心的敬意与热烈的支援，并祝你们在这次反对法帝国主义残酷统治，争取民族解放的斗争中获得更伟大的胜利。
    中华全国总工会
    一月二十九日
