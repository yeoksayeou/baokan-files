Title: Japanese Communist Party Parliamentary Group Demands Dissolution of the Police Reserve Force
Author:
Date: 1952-02-18
Page: 4

    Japanese Communist Party Parliamentary Group
    Demands Dissolution of the Police Reserve Force
    (Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 16th) TASS, Tokyo, 15th: According to the Japanese Communist Party Parliamentary Group's bulletin "Parliamentary Information," the Japanese Communist Party Parliamentary Group has submitted a proposal to the Diet regarding the dissolution of the Police Reserve Force. The proposal points out that this is what all peace-loving Japanese people demand.
    The explanation of this proposal states: "Japan must engage in peaceful cooperation with all countries in the world, including the Soviet Union and China. All Japanese people want to live peaceful lives and oppose rearmament and conscription."

### 日共国会议员团  要求解散警察后备队

1952-02-18
第4版()
专栏：

    日共国会议员团
    要求解散警察后备队
    【新华社十六日讯】塔斯社东京十五日讯：据日本共产党国会议员团的公报“国会情报”报道：日本共产党国会议员团已向国会提出一项关于解散警察后备队的提案，提案中指出：这是希望和平的全体日本人民所要求的。
    这个提案的说明说：“日本必须与世界各国，包括苏联和中国在内，从事和平合作。所有的日本人民都希望过和平生活、并且反对重新武装和实行征兵。”
