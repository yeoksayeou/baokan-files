Title: Government Administration Council Holds 125th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-02-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 22nd: The 125th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 22nd. The meeting heard and approved the report of Li Weihan, Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, on the Second (Enlarged) Meeting of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, and then, in principle, approved the "Outline for the Implementation of National Regional Autonomy," the "Provisional Organizational Regulations for Nationalities Affairs Commissions at All Levels of People's Governments," the "Decision of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government on Measures for the Implementation of Local National Democratic United Governments," and the "Decision of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government on Guaranteeing the Rights of National Equality for All Scattered Ethnic Minority Components." The "Outline for the Implementation of National Regional Autonomy" and the "Provisional Organizational Regulations for Nationalities Affairs Commissions at All Levels of People's Governments" will be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval before implementation.

### 政务院举行一二五次会议

1952-02-23
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行一二五次会议
    【新华社二十二日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百二十五次政务会议在二十二日下午举行。会议在听取和批准了中央人民政府民族事务委员会李维汉主任委员所作的中央人民政府民族事务委员会第二次（扩大）会议的报告后，原则通过了“民族区域自治实施纲要”、“各级人民政府民族事务委员会试行组织通则”、“中央人民政府政务院关于地方民族民主联合政府实施办法的决定”、“中央人民政府政务院关于保障一切散居的少数民族成份享有民族平等权利的决定”。“民族区域自治实施纲要”及“各级人民政府民族事务委员会试行组织通则”将于提请中央人民政府委员会批准后施行。
