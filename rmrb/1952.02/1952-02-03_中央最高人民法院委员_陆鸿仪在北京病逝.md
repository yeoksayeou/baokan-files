Title: Central Supreme People's Court Member Lu Hongyi Dies in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-02-03
Page: 1

    Central Supreme People's Court Member
    Lu Hongyi Dies in Beijing
    [By our staff] Lu Hongyi, member of the Central People's Government Supreme People's Court and Chief of the Civil Division, passed away at 8:00 AM on February 1st at Peking Union Medical College Hospital. The Lu Hongyi Funeral Committee has issued an obituary and set the memorial service for 10:00 AM today (the 3rd) at Jiaxing Temple in the north of the city.

### 中央最高人民法院委员  陆鸿仪在北京病逝

1952-02-03
第1版()
专栏：

    中央最高人民法院委员  
    陆鸿仪在北京病逝
    【本报讯】中央人民政府最高人民法院委员兼民庭庭长陆鸿仪，于二月一日上午八时病逝于北京协和医院。陆鸿仪委员治丧委员会顷发出讣告，定于今天（三日）上午十时假北城嘉兴寺举行追悼会。
