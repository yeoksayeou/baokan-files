Title: Mercilessly Strike American Imperialism

Author:

Date: 1952-02-24
Page: 2

Reader's Letters

    Mercilessly Strike American Imperialism
    We accuse! We accuse with boundless anger to peace-loving people all over the world! The atrocities of American imperialism have developed to the point of exterminating humanity. American imperialism, colluding with notorious Japanese bacteriological warfare criminals, dropped a large number of bacteria in Korea. This utterly shameless behavior precisely demonstrates the desperate mood of American imperialism's death throes.
    Medical workers across the country! The imperialism is savage and outrageous, we must resolutely smash the enemy's sinister plot. Bacteria are not impossible to defend against. This is the best opportunity for us to take active action and respond to the call to resist America and aid Korea. Let us unite and give the conscienceless American imperialists a merciless blow!
    All Staff Members of the Department of Bacteriology and Immunology, Peking Union Medical College

### 给美帝国主义以无情打击

1952-02-24
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    给美帝国主义以无情打击
    我们控诉！我们以无比的愤怒向全世界爱好和平的人民控诉！美帝国主义者的暴行已经发展到灭绝人性的地步了。美帝国主义勾结臭名昭彰的日本细菌战犯，在朝鲜投下大量细菌。这种无耻透顶的行为，正好显示了美帝国主义垂死挣扎的绝望心情。
    全国医务工作者们！帝国主义野蛮透顶，我们要坚决粉碎敌人罪恶阴谋。细菌并不是不能防御的。这是我们积极行动，响应抗美援朝的口号的最好机会。让我们团结起来，给丧尽天良的美帝国主义者以无情打击！
    中国协和医学院细菌免疫学科全体工作人员
