Title: Commemorating the 34th Anniversary of the Founding of the Soviet Armed Forces - Chairman Mao's Congratulatory Telegram to Marshal Stalin

Author:

Date: 1952-02-23
Page: 1

    Commemorating the 34th Anniversary of the Founding of the Soviet Armed Forces
    Chairman Mao's Congratulatory Telegram to Marshal Stalin

To Comrade Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics:

    On the glorious occasion of the 3 fourth anniversary of the founding of the Armed Forces of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, please accept from me my heartfelt congratulations and best wishes.

    Mao Zedong
    February 21, 1952, Beijing

### 苏联武装部队建军三十四周年纪念  毛主席致斯大林大元帅贺电

1952-02-23
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联武装部队建军三十四周年纪念
    毛主席致斯大林大元帅贺电苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议主席斯大林同志：
    值此苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟武装部队建军三十四周年的光荣节日，请接受我衷心的祝贺和最好的愿望。
    毛泽东  一九五二年二月二十一日北京
