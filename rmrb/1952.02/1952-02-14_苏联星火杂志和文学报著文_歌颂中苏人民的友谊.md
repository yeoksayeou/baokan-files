Title: Soviet Spark Magazine and Literary Gazette Publish Articles Praising the Friendship Between the Chinese and Soviet People
Author:
Date: 1952-02-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 12th:

    The Soviet Spark magazine and Literary Gazette recently published articles to welcome the second anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance. The latest issue of Spark magazine published a special page titled "The Friendship of the People of Two Great Nations," containing many photos of Soviet experts and advanced Soviet experience helping China's construction. The magazine also commented: The Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance is of great significance for increasing and strengthening the friendly cooperation between the Chinese and Soviet people. The Sino-Soviet Treaty has opened a new era in the development of friendly relations between the people of the two great nations. Over the past two years, this treaty has demonstrated its great vitality and its great international significance for the cause of peace. The Literary Gazette published the first section, "The Friendship of the Great People," of the Soviet composer Kabalevsky's travelogue "In New China." Kabalevsky described the feelings he personally experienced in New China last October when he accompanied the Soviet People's Delegation to participate in the celebration of our National Day, regarding the sentiments of the Chinese people. He said with deep emotion: "The friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people is truly a permanent, unbreakable, great friendship."

### 苏联星火杂志和文学报著文  歌颂中苏人民的友谊

1952-02-14
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联星火杂志和文学报著文
    歌颂中苏人民的友谊
    【新华社莫斯科十二日电】苏联星火杂志和文学报顷发表文章，迎接中苏友好同盟互助条约签订二周年。星火杂志最近一期出版了题为“两大国家人民的友谊”的专页，登载了多幅关于苏联专家和苏联先进经验帮助中国建设的照片。该杂志并著论说：中苏友好同盟互助条约对于增加和加强中苏人民的友好合作，是有巨大的意义的。中苏条约已在发展两大国人民的友好关系上开辟了新纪元。过去两年这个条约已显示出它的巨大活力和对和平事业的巨大国际意义。文学报发表了苏联作曲家卡巴列夫斯基的游记“在新中国”的第一节“伟大人民的友谊”。卡巴列夫斯基叙述了他去年十月随苏联人民观礼代表团参加我国国庆节庆祝典礼，在新中国所亲身感觉到的中国人民的情谊。他深深感动地说：“中苏人民的友谊，真正是永久的、牢不可破的伟大友谊。”
