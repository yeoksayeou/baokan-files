    Our Great Motherland
    The exhibition held in Tianjin on anti-corruption, anti-waste, and anti-bureaucracy has profoundly educated every audience member and aroused the hatred of the broad masses of people for profiteers and embezzlers. This is a crowd of viewers at the entrance of the exhibition, all of whom say that this exhibition is a large classroom.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-02-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    天津市举办的反贪污、反浪费、反官僚主义的展览会，深刻地教育了每一个观众，激起了广大人民对于奸商和贪污分子的憎恨。这是在展览会门前拥挤着的观众，他们都说这个展览会是一个大课堂。
