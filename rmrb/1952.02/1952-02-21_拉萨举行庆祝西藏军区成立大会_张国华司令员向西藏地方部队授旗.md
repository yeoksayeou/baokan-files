Title: Lhasa Holds Celebration of the Founding of the Tibet Military Region; Commander Zhang Guohua Presents Flag to Tibetan Local Troops
Author:
Date: 1952-02-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, February 20th: More than 10,000 people, including the People's Liberation Army stationed in Lhasa, Tibetan local troops, and monks and laypeople, held a celebration of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Tibet Military Region in front of the Potala Palace on the 11th. At the meeting, all commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army and officers and soldiers of the Tibetan local troops took a solemn oath in both Tibetan and Chinese languages under the supervision of General Zhang Jingwu, representative of the Central People's Government, and led by General Zhang Guohua, Commander of the Tibet Military Region. After the oath, a grand flag presentation ceremony was held. First, Commander Zhang Guohua presented the flag to the Tibetan local troops, which was accepted by Keimo Soinam Wangdu, Acting Commander of the Tibetan local troops, the first Daibon master officer of the Dalai Lama's guard, and the sixth and seventh Daibon master officers. Subsequently, the Dalai Lama and Ranba Tudan Gongqing, the chief Kalon representative of the Tibetan local government, Hopaton, representative of the Panchen Kampus, representatives of the People's Liberation Committee of Changdu and other regions, and representatives of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Lhasa successively presented flags, hadas, and flowers to the leaders of the military region. Commander Zhang Guohua, after elaborating on the significance of the establishment of the Tibet Military Region and the current tasks, emphasized: Under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu of the Central People's Government and under the direct leadership of the Southwest Military Region, we must unite closely, overcome all difficulties, and complete our tasks. First Deputy Commander Aphe Awang Jinmei and Second Deputy Commander Dorga Pengcuo Raojie unanimously agreed in their speeches that the establishment of the Tibet Military Region is an unprecedented major event. Ranba Tudan Gongqing, the chief Kalon of the Tibetan local government, said: "The establishment of the Tibet Military Region is another great victory for the Tibetan people after peaceful liberation, and from now on, the Tibetan people will no longer be subjected to imperialist aggression." At the meeting, General Zhang Jingwu called on the PLA troops entering Tibet and the Tibetan local troops to achieve true unity, progress, and unification under the unified leadership of the leaders of the Tibet Military Region. He also pointed out: The Tibet Military Region and the Tibetan people must always heighten their revolutionary vigilance and prevent imperialist elements and their running dogs from taking open and secret measures from the outside and inside to sow discord and sabotage our national unity and the agreement on the peaceful liberation of Tibet. The conference passed a congratulatory telegram to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu. After the meeting, the People's Liberation Army stationed in Lhasa, Tibetan local troops, and monks and laypeople held a demonstration parade.

### 拉萨举行庆祝西藏军区成立大会  张国华司令员向西藏地方部队授旗

1952-02-21
第1版()
专栏：

    拉萨举行庆祝西藏军区成立大会
    张国华司令员向西藏地方部队授旗
    【新华社拉萨二十日电】驻拉萨人民解放军、西藏地方部队和僧俗人民一万多人，十一日在布达拉宫前举行庆祝中国人民解放军西藏军区成立大会。会上，人民解放军全体指挥员、战斗员和西藏地方部队官兵，在中央人民政府代表张经武将军监誓和西藏军区司令员张国华将军领誓下，用藏汉两种语言庄严宣誓；誓毕，隆重地举行了授旗式。首由张国华司令员向西藏地方部队授旗，受旗的有西藏地方部队代总司令凯墨·索安旺堆、达赖警卫部队第一代本主官和第六、第七代本主官。接着达赖和西藏地方政府代表首席噶伦然巴·图丹滚清、班禅行辕代表霍巴顿和昌都等地区的人民解放委员会代表及驻拉萨人民解放军的代表，相继向军区首长献旗、献哈达、献花。张国华司令员在阐述西藏军区成立的意义和当前任务后，着重指出：我们在中央人民政府毛主席和朱总司令英明领导下，在西南军区直接领导下，亲密地团结起来，一定可以克服一切困难，一定可以完成任务。第一副司令员阿沛·阿旺晋美、第二副司令员朵噶·彭错饶杰在致词中一致认为，西藏军区的成立是一件史无前例的大事。西藏地方政府首席噶伦然巴·图丹滚清说：“西藏军区的成立，是藏族人民继和平解放之后的又一伟大胜利，从此西藏人民再不受帝国主义的侵略。”张经武将军在大会上号召人民解放军入藏部队和西藏地方部队在西藏军区首长统一领导下，做到真正的团结进步和统一，他并指出：西藏军区和西藏人民要随时提高革命的警惕性，防止帝国主义分子及其走狗采取公开的和秘密的从外部和内部来进行挑拨离间，阴谋破坏我们民族团结与和平解放西藏办法的协议。大会通过了向毛主席和朱总司令的致敬电。会后，驻拉萨的人民解放军、西藏地方部队和僧俗人民举行了示威大游行。
