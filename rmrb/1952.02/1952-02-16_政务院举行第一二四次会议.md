Title: Government Administration Council Holds 124th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-02-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 15th
The 124th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 15th. After listening to the explanation made by Li Shucheng, Minister of Agriculture of the Central People's Government, the meeting approved the "Instructions on Agricultural Production in 1952".

### 政务院举行第一二四次会议

1952-02-16
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一二四次会议
    【新华社十五日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百二十四次政务会议在十五日下午举行。会议在听取中央人民政府农业部李书城部长所作的说明后，通过了关于一九五二年农业生产的指示。
