Title: The 30th Anniversary of the Founding of the Labour-Progressive Party of Canada: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Sends Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1952-02-01
Page: 1
    The 30th Anniversary of the Founding of the Labour-Progressive Party of Canada
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Sends Congratulations
    [This newspaper dispatch] On February 1st, the anniversary of the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Labour-Progressive Party of Canada, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram of congratulations. The telegram reads as follows:
    To the National Committee of the Labour-Progressive Party of Canada, dear comrades:
    On the occasion of the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Labour-Progressive Party of Canada, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm congratulations to you and the Canadian working class. We wish the Labour-Progressive Party of Canada new and great success in leading the Canadian people in the struggle to defend world peace and strive for social progress.
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
    Beijing, January 31, 1952

### 加拿大劳工进步党成立三十周年  中国共产党中央委员会致电祝贺

1952-02-01
第1版()
专栏：

    加拿大劳工进步党成立三十周年
    中国共产党中央委员会致电祝贺
    【本报讯】二月一日是加拿大劳工进步党成立三十周年纪念日，中国共产党中央委员会特致电祝贺。电文如下：加拿大劳工进步党全国委员会亲爱的同志们：
    当加拿大劳工进步党成立三十周年纪念日之际，中国共产党中央委员会谨向你们和加拿大工人阶级致热烈的祝贺。祝加拿大劳工进步党在领导加拿大人民保卫世界和平和争取社会进步的斗争中获得新的伟大的成功。
    中国共产党中央委员会
    一九五二年一月三十一日北京
