Title: Algerian Communist Party Holds Sixth National Congress; Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Sends Congratulatory Telegram

Author:
Date: 1952-02-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 26th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram on February 15th congratulating the Algerian Communist Party on its Sixth National Congress. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To the Central Committee of the Algerian Communist Party and to all delegate comrades of the Sixth National Congress of the Algerian Communist Party:

On the happy occasion of the opening of your party's Sixth National Congress, we extend our congratulations on the success of your congress, and wish you victory in the struggle against imperialist enslavement and for national liberation.

Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
February 15, 1952

### 阿尔及利亚共产党举行六次全国代表大会  中国共产党中央委员会致电祝贺

1952-02-27
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚共产党举行六次全国代表大会
    中国共产党中央委员会致电祝贺
    【新华社二十六日讯】中国共产党中央委员会在二月十五日电贺阿尔及利亚共产党第六次全国代表大会，电文如下：
    阿尔及利亚共产党中央委员会并转阿尔及利亚共产党第六次全国代表大会全体代表同志们：
    欣逢贵党举行第六次全国代表大会之际，谨祝贺你们的大会成功，并祝你们在反对帝国主义奴役和争取民族解放的斗争中获得胜利。
    中国共产党中央委员会  一九五二年二月十五日
