Title: China Coal Construction Company Central South District Company Blindly Transfers Timber, Wasting 1.4 Billion Yuan
Author: Zhu Kuanfu
Date: 1952-02-06
Page: 2

    China Coal Construction Company Central South District Company
    Blindly Transfers Timber, Wasting 1.4 Billion Yuan
    Editor: Last June, the China Coal Industry Building Materials Company Central South District Company, without investigation or research, blindly transferred pine planking and 3.5-inch fir timber from Guangzhou to Wuhan. After this batch of timber arrived in Wuhan, it was transferred to Henan and North China because it was not suitable for market needs, which increased unnecessary burdens on loading, unloading, and transportation costs, wasting 1.436 billion yuan of state assets. I believe that the company's blind transfer and irresponsible attitude towards state assets cannot be tolerated. The company should conduct an in-depth inspection and make a self-criticism.
    Zhu Kuanfu

### 中国煤建公司中南区公司  盲目调运木材浪费十四亿元
朱宽夫
1952-02-06
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    中国煤建公司中南区公司
    盲目调运木材浪费十四亿元
    编辑同志：去年六月，中国煤业建筑器材公司中南区公司没有调查研究，就盲目地从广州向武汉调运松分板和三吋半的杉木。这批木材运到武汉，因不适合市场需要，又把它转运到河南和华北各地，以致在装卸、搬运等费用上，增加了不应有的负担，浪费了国家资财十四亿三千六百多万元。我认为这个公司盲目调运和对国家资财不负责任的态度，是不能容许的，这个公司应深入检查并作检讨。
    朱宽夫
