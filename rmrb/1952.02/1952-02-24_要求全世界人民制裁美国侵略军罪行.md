Title: Demand the People of the World Sanction the Crimes of the American Aggressor Troops

Author: 
Date: 1952-02-24
Page: 2

Reader's Letter

    Demand the People of the World Sanction the Crimes of the American Aggressor Troops
    Editor: We, the entire staff of the Department of Parasitology of the Beijing University School of Medicine, with immense anger, protest the heinous crime of the American aggressor troops in Korea in spreading bacteria. The use of such despicable, shameless, and inhumane methods by American imperialism can only arouse the anger of peace-loving people all over the world and will absolutely not weaken the confidence of peace-loving people in the world in striving for peace. We unanimously demand that all justice-loving people in the world give the American imperialists, who have committed heinous crimes, the most severe sanctions and punishments. Let the national medical and health workers unite and, together with the peace-loving people of the world, wage a resolute struggle against the shameless atrocities of American imperialism!
    The Entire Staff of the Department of Parasitology of the Beijing University School of Medicine

### 要求全世界人民制裁美国侵略军罪行

1952-02-24
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    要求全世界人民制裁美国侵略军罪行
    编辑同志：我们北京大学医学院寄生生物学科全体人员，以无比愤怒的心情，抗议侵朝美军疯狂撒布细菌的灭绝人性的暴行。美帝国主义使用这种卑鄙无耻、灭绝人性的手段，只有激起全世界爱好和平人民的愤怒，是绝对不可能减弱世界爱好和平人民争取和平的信心的。我们一致要求全世界爱好正义的人民，给罪恶滔天的美帝国主义以最严厉的制裁和惩罚。全国医务卫生工作者团结起来，和世界爱好和平人民一起，向美帝国主义的无耻暴行作坚决的斗争！
    北京大学医学院寄生生物学科全体人员
