Title: Participation in the Indian International Film Exhibition; My film "The White Haired Girl" is welcomed
Author:
Date: 1952-02-15
Page: 4

    Participating in the Indian International Film Exhibition
    My film "The White Haired Girl" is welcomed
    (Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi) The Chinese film "The White Haired Girl", which participated in the International Film Exhibition held in India, was screened in Mumbai on January 29th and February 4th. The film was warmly welcomed by the audience. The screenings were packed on both days, with a total audience of more than 5,000 people.
    The Chinese film delegation briefly introduced the content of "The White Haired Girl" at the beginning of the screenings. During the screening, when the scene of Dachun being forced to flee and finding the Eighth Route Army appeared, the theater suddenly erupted in warm applause in the midst of a tense and heavy atmosphere. After watching the film, the audience discussed the content of the film and praised the music and the artistic accomplishment of the actors.

### 参加印度国际电影展览会  我影片“白毛女”受到欢迎

1952-02-15
第4版()
专栏：

    参加印度国际电影展览会
    我影片“白毛女”受到欢迎
    【新华社新德里电】参加在印度举行的国际电影展览会的我国影片“白毛女”，已于一月二十九日和二月四日在孟买放映。这部影片受到观众的热烈欢迎。在这两天中场场满座，观众共有五千多人。
    中国电影代表团在“白毛女”影片开始放映时，简略地介绍了它的内容。影片放映中，当大春被迫逃亡后找到了八路军的镜头出现时，剧场在紧张和沉重的气氛中突然响起了热烈的掌声。观众看完这部影片后，纷纷谈论影片的内容，并称赞片中的音乐和演员的艺术修养。
