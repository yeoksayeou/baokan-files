Title: Doing One Good Deed: Beijing Children's Spring Festival Greetings to Families of Martyrs and Military Personnel (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1952-02-06
Page: 5

    Doing One Good Deed
    Beijing Children's Spring Festival Greetings to Families of Martyrs and Military Personnel
    Young Pioneers from the Beijing May 1st Girls' Middle School visited the family of Ming Baishi, a volunteer combat hero from Dengcao Hutong No. 24. They helped the military family clean the house and scrubbed the windows until they sparkled.
    Young Pioneers in Beijing responded to the call of the Chinese People's Committee for Resisting America and Aiding Korea and the Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of China by conducting large-scale consolation activities for the families of martyrs and military personnel during the Spring Festival. Here, Young Pioneers from Beijing No. 1 Girls' Middle School pin a large red flower on the chest of Military Family Elder Chen, clapping their hands and singing for her.
    Young Pioneers from the Beijing May 1st Girls' Middle School help a military family wash their cotton quilts.
    Young Pioneers from Beijing Yucai Middle School write a letter for Military Family Elder Sun, of Xitangzi Hutong No. 15, to a volunteer soldier on the Korean front.
    Young Pioneers from the Second Affiliated Elementary School of Beijing Normal University perform a 快板 (clapper talk) for Military Family Elder Yang.
    Young Pioneers from Beijing Baozi Hutong Elementary School used the money they saved from buying candy to purchase a portrait of Chairman Mao, which they presented to Qingquan, the elderly family member of Wang Xueshi, a soldier in the Chinese People's Volunteers.

### 做好一件事情  北京少年儿童春节慰问烈军属（图片）

1952-02-06
第5版()
专栏：

    做好一件事情
    北京少年儿童春节慰问烈军属
    北京市五一女子中学少年儿童队员慰问了灯草胡同二十四号志愿军战斗英雄明白石的家属，她们帮助军属们打扫房子，把玻璃都擦得干干净净的。
    北京市少年儿童队队员响应中国人民抗美援朝总会、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会的号召，在春节期间在规模地进行了慰问烈、军属工作。这是北京市女子第一中学儿童队员们把大红花戴在军属陈老太太的胸前，并拍手唱歌给陈老太太听的情形。
    北京市五一女子中学儿童队员帮助军属拆洗棉被。
    北京市育英中学少年儿童队员替西堂子胡同十五号军属孙老太太写信给朝鲜前线的志愿军战士。
    北京师范大学第二附属小学少年儿童队员给军属杨老太太演唱快板。
    北京市报子胡同小学的少年儿童队员们把节省下来的糖果钱买了毛主席像，送给中国人民志愿军王学实的家属清泉老大爷。
