Title: Chinese and Soviet Artists Hold Joint Soiree in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1952-02-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 13th: The Chinese Youth Arts Troupe and Soviet artists held a joint soiree at the Soviet Artists' House on the 11th. Zhang Wentian, the Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, was also invited to attend. The soiree began with a welcoming speech by Soviet People's Artist Barsova. Ren Hong, the deputy head of the Chinese Youth Arts Troupe, said in his reply that during the tour performances in Moscow, Leningrad, Minsk, Kiev, Kharkov, Kazan, and Stalin, the delegation carefully examined the Soviet people's construction of communism, visited many theaters and museums, and saw the remarkable achievements of Soviet artists. We have learned a lot from the outstanding Soviet art. We will widely apply the experience we have gained in the Soviet Union after we return to our country. The soiree proceeded in an atmosphere of unity and friendship.

### 我国和苏联艺术工作者在莫斯科举行联欢晚会

1952-02-14
第4版()
专栏：

    我国和苏联艺术工作者在莫斯科举行联欢晚会
    【新华社莫斯科十三日电】中国青年文艺工作团和苏联艺术工作者，于十一日在苏联艺术工作者之家举行了联欢晚会。我国驻苏联大使张闻天也应邀参加。晚会由苏联人民艺术家巴尔索娃致欢迎词。中国青年文艺工作团副团长任虹在致答词中指出：代表团在莫斯科、列宁格勒、明斯克、基辅、哈尔科夫、喀山、斯大林诺等地作巡回演出期间，曾仔细地考察了苏联人民进行共产主义建设的情形，访问了许多剧院和博物馆，看到了苏联艺术家们的卓越成就。苏联杰出的艺术已使我们学到了许多东西。我们将来回国之后，将广泛地运用我们在苏联所得到的经验。晚会在团结友好的气氛中进行。
