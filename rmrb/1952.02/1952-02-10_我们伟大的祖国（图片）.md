Author:
Date: 1952-02-10

    Our Great Motherland
    Model female teacher Yu Yanping founded a private school in Yaohua Village, Yangsi District, Shanghai, teaching many farmers to learn culture and training a large number of mass teachers, setting an example for consolidating and adhering to year-round private schools. This is a picture of Yu Yanping patiently helping Grandpa Gong Haihe learn to write.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-02-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    模范女教师俞燕平，在上海杨思区耀华村创办了民校，教许多农民学文化，并培养了大批的群众教师，为巩固和坚持常年民校作了范例。这是俞燕平正在耐心地帮助龚海和老伯伯学习写字的情形。
