Title: Celebrating the Second Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance - Soviet Ambassador to China, Mr. Roshchin, Hosts Reception - Vice Chairmen Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi and Others and Foreign Envoys Attend by Invitation

Author:

Date: 1952-02-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. To commemorate the second anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance, the Soviet Ambassador to China, Mr. Roshchin, held a reception at the Soviet Embassy at 5 p.m. on the 14th.

Those invited to attend were: Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, and Li Jishen; Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government Zhou Enlai; Vice Premiers Guo Moruo and Huang Yanpei; Acting Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Nie Rongzhen; President of the Supreme People's Court Shen Junru; Deputy Chief Procurator of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Lan Gongwu; Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Wu Yuzhang; Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression Chen Shutong; and heads of various departments of the Central People's Government and leaders of democratic parties and people's organizations, including Li Lisan, He Xiangning, Bo Yibo, Xu Teli, Situ Meitang, Zhang Xiruo, Liu Yazi, Shao Lizi, Peng Zemin, Lu Dingyi, Zhang Hanfu, Wu Xiuquan, Ye Jizhuang, Zhu Xuefan, Zhang Bojun, Liang Xi, Fu Zuoyi, Ding Xilin, Li Dequan, Shi Liang, Yu Xinqing, Liu Ningyi, Liao Chengzhi, Feng Wenbin, Deputy Mayor of Beijing Zhang Youyu, Wu Han, and others, totaling more than 650 people.

The foreign envoys to China who were invited to attend were: Czechoslovak Ambassador Weiskopf, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic with the Title of Ambassador Koenig, Hungarian Ambassador Saffronko, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Burmese Ambassador U La Maung, Swiss Minister Renzo Zehnder, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy Isa Matari, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy Doding, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Delegation Zhou Liang, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Korean Embassy Choi Young, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Swedish Embassy Befan, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Mongolian Embassy Darhazhav, Counselor of the Romanian Embassy Xilaid, Counselor of the Indian Embassy Gaul, Norwegian Government Negotiating Representative Golan, Dutch Government Negotiating Representative Van der Put, and personnel from various embassies.

The reception was conducted in a warm and friendly atmosphere. During the event, the hosts and guests frequently toasted to the unbreakable friendship between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union, to the health of the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao Zedong, and to the health of the leader of the working people of the world and the best friend of the Chinese people, Marshal Stalin.

### 纪念中苏友好同盟互助条约签订两周年  苏联驻我国大使罗申举行酒会  朱德刘少奇副主席等和各国使节应邀参加

1952-02-15
第1版()
专栏：

    纪念中苏友好同盟互助条约签订两周年
    苏联驻我国大使罗申举行酒会
    朱德刘少奇副主席等和各国使节应邀参加
    【新华社十四日讯】为纪念中苏友好同盟互助条约签订二周年，苏联驻我国大使罗申于十四日下午五时在苏联大使馆举行酒会。
    应邀到会的有：中央人民政府副主席朱德、刘少奇、李济深，中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来，副总理郭沫若、黄炎培，人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，最高人民检察署副检察长蓝公武，中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章，中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会副主席陈叔通和中央人民政府各部门首长及民主党派、人民团体负责人李立三、何香凝、薄一波、徐特立、司徒美堂、张奚若、柳亚子、邵力子、彭泽民、陆定一、章汉夫、伍修权、叶季壮、朱学范、章伯钧、梁希、傅作义、丁西林、李德全、史良、余心清、刘宁一、廖承志、冯文彬，北京市人民政府副市长张友渔、吴晗等六百五十多人。
    应邀到会的各国驻华使节有：捷克斯洛伐克大使魏斯柯普夫、德意志民主共和国外交使团大使衔团长柯尼希、匈牙利大使夏法朗柯、保加利亚大使彼得科夫、缅甸大使吴拉茂、瑞士公使任佐立、印尼大使馆临时代办伊沙·玛蒂、波兰大使馆临时代办道定、越南代表团临时代办周亮、朝鲜大使馆临时代办崔英、瑞典大使馆临时代办贝凡、蒙古大使馆临时代办达尔哈热布、罗马尼亚大使馆参赞席拉德、印度大使馆参赞高尔、挪威政府谈判代表高兰、荷兰政府谈判代表范德普和各国使馆人员。
    招待会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。席间宾主频频为中苏两国人民牢不可破的友谊，为中国人民的伟大领袖毛泽东主席的健康，为世界劳动人民的领袖、中国人民最好的朋友斯大林大元帅的健康而干杯。
