Title: List of Cadres Appointed to the Tibet Military Region of the People's Liberation Army by the Central People's Government People's Revolutionary Military Commission
Author:
Date: 1952-02-20
Page: 1

The Central People's Government People's Revolutionary Military Commission
List of Cadres Appointed to the Tibet Military Region of the People's Liberation Army

Commander: Zhang Guohua, formerly Commander of the People's Liberation Army troops entering Tibet.

First Deputy Commander: Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, formerly a Kalon of the Tibetan Local Government.

Second Deputy Commander: Dorje Pengtso Rabgye, served as Commander-in-Chief of the Tibetan Local Army in 1945, currently a Kalon of the Tibetan Local Government.

Third Deputy Commander: Chang Binggui, formerly Deputy Commander of a certain army of the People's Liberation Army.

Political Commissar: Tan Guansan, formerly Political Commissar of the People's Liberation Army troops entering Tibet.

Deputy Political Commissar: Fan Ming, formerly Commander of a certain detachment of the People's Liberation Army troops entering Tibet.

Deputy Political Commissar: Wang Qimei, formerly Deputy Political Commissar of a certain army of the People's Liberation Army.

Chief of Staff: Li Jue, formerly Chief of Staff of a certain army of the People's Liberation Army.

Director of the Political Department: Liu ZhengGuo, formerly Director of the Political Department of a certain army of the People's Liberation Army.
