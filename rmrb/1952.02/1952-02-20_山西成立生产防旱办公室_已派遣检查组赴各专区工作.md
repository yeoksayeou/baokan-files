Title: Shanxi Establishes Production and Drought Prevention Office; Inspection Teams Dispatched to Various Special Districts

Author:

Date: 1952-02-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Taiyuan, 19th. To implement the Central Committee's decision on launching a drought prevention and resistance movement, the Shanxi Provincial People's Government held an administrative meeting on the 15th, passed resolutions on various specific measures, and established the Provincial Production and Drought Prevention Office on the 16th, with Wu Guangtang, Director of the Provincial People's Government's Department of Agriculture and Forestry, as Director, and Zhang Junxuan, Director of the Department of Civil Affairs, as Deputy Director. The various departments of the Provincial People's Government have transferred 22 cadres to form five inspection teams, which departed on the 18th to inspect drought prevention work in Yuncheng, Linfen, Yuci, Xinxian, and Xingxian Special Districts.

Editor's Note: The Shanxi Provincial People's Government has rapidly organized a production and drought prevention office and begun work in accordance with the State Council's decision. Other areas with drought conditions should take immediate action like Shanxi Province.

### 山西成立生产防旱办公室  已派遣检查组赴各专区工作

1952-02-20
第1版()
专栏：

    山西成立生产防旱办公室
    已派遣检查组赴各专区工作
    【新华社太原十九日电】为贯彻中央关于开展防旱、抗旱运动的决定，山西省人民政府在十五日举行行政会议，通过了各项具体措施的决议，并于十六日成立了省生产防旱办公室，由省人民政府农林厅厅长武光汤任主任，民政厅厅长张隽轩任副主任。省人民政府各部门已抽调干部二十二人组成五个检查组，在十八日分赴运城、临汾、榆次、忻县、兴县等专区检查防旱工作。
    编者按：山西省人民政府按照政务院的决定，迅速组织了生产防旱办公室，展开工作。其他凡有旱象的地区，都应该像山西省一样立即行动起来。
