Reader's Letters

    Whoever Harms Our Most Beloved People is Our Enemy
    Tsinghua University Students Demand Severe Punishment for Unscrupulous Merchants
    To the Editor: Recent reports of unscrupulous merchants harming the Volunteer Army have aroused great anger among all the students of our university. We cannot tolerate the unscrupulous merchants who harm our most beloved Volunteer Army comrades for a moment! Whoever harms our most beloved people is our most hated enemy. They do not deserve to be Chinese; they are, in effect, accomplices of American imperialism. All of us students unanimously demand that the government give the most severe punishment to these heinous and shameless villains—the unscrupulous merchants. We firmly believe that this is the unanimous demand of the people of the whole country. We fully feel that we have the right and responsibility to make such a demand, and we ask the People's Government to severely punish these most odious people as soon as possible.
    All students of Tsinghua University

### 谁危害我们最可爱的人，谁就是我们的敌人  清华大学学生要求严惩奸商

1952-02-21
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    谁危害我们最可爱的人，谁就是我们的敌人
    清华大学学生要求严惩奸商
    编辑同志：最近报上发表的许多不法奸商暗害志愿军的痛心事实，激起了我们全校同学极大的愤怒。我们对暗害我们最可爱的志愿军同志的奸商，一刻也不能容忍！他们危害我们最可爱的人，他们就是我们最可恨的人。他们不配做一个中国人，他们实际上是美帝国主义的帮凶。我们全体同学一致要求政府，给这些罪恶滔天的无耻之徒——奸商以最严厉的处分。我们深信这是全国人民一致的要求。我们充分感到我们有这样要求的权利和责任，我们要求人民政府尽速地严惩这些最可恶的人。
    清华大学全体学生
