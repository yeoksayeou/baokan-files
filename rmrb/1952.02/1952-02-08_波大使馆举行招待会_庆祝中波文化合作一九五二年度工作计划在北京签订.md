Title: Polish Embassy Holds Reception Celebrating the Signing of the 1952 Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Work Plan in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-02-08
Page: 4

    Polish Embassy Holds Reception
    Celebrating the Signing of the 1952 Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Work Plan in Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, 7th] The Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Republic's Embassy in China, Mr. Dowding, and the Chairman of the Polish Subsidiary Committee of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Joint Committee, Mr. Winder, held a reception at the Polish Embassy at 7 p.m. on the 7th to celebrate the signing of the 1952 Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Work Plan in Beijing in January.

    Those invited to attend included: Mr. Shen Yanbing, Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, Mr. Ding Xilin, Vice Minister, Mr. Zeng Zhaolun, Vice Minister of Education, Mr. Peng Mingzhi, our country's Ambassador to Poland currently in Beijing, Mr. Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Mr. Sha Kefu, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Culture, Mr. Hong Shen, Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Government Administration Council's Cultural and Educational Committee, and dozens of heads of various departments of the Central People's Government.

    At 2 p.m. on the same day, Mr. Winder also held a press conference. He delivered a written statement at the meeting, elaborating on the great significance of Sino-Polish cultural cooperation, and describing the impressions gained by the Polish representatives during their visit to New China, and the current situation of the cultural circles of People's Poland.

    Mr. Winder and his party arrived in Beijing in January of this year. They successively visited factories, rural areas, schools, and other cultural and educational institutions in Beijing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Nanjing, Tianjin and other places.

### 波大使馆举行招待会  庆祝中波文化合作一九五二年度工作计划在北京签订

1952-02-08
第4版()
专栏：

    波大使馆举行招待会
    庆祝中波文化合作一九五二年度工作计划在北京签订
    【新华社七日讯】波兰共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办道定、中波文化合作联合委员会波兰附属委员会主席温德先生，为庆祝中波文化合作一九五二年度工作计划于一月在北京签订，特于七日下午七时在波兰大使馆举行招待会。
    应邀到会的有：我中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰、副部长丁西林、教育部副部长曾昭抡、现在北京的我国驻波兰大使彭明治、外交部办公厅主任王炳南、文化部办公厅主任沙可夫、政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深，以及中央人民政府各部门首长等数十人。
    同日下午二时，温德先生并举行了记者招待会。他在会上发表了书面谈话，阐明中波文化合作的重大意义，并叙述波兰代表们访问新中国所获得的印象，和人民波兰文化界的近况。
    温德先生等一行是在今年一月间到达北京的。他们曾先后访问了北京、上海、杭州、南京、天津等地的工厂、农村、学校和其他文化教育机关。
