Title: Mongolian Chairman Bumantsend and Foreign Minister Lhamsuren Telegraph Thanks to Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou for Condolences on the Death of Marshal Choibalsan
Author:
Date: 1952-02-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th: Mongolian People's Republic Great Hural Presidium Chairman Bumantsend and Foreign Minister Lhamsuren, on the 15th, sent telegrams to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of China and Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of the State Council, respectively, thanking them for their condolences on the death of Marshal Choibalsan. The telegrams are as follows:

To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:
    We are deeply grateful for your fraternal and profound condolences on the premature death of Marshal Choibalsan, the leader of our people, the founder and organizer of our people's country, the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic. On behalf of the Presidium of the Great Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, the Mongolian people, and myself personally, I would like to express my heartfelt gratitude to you and, through you, to the friendly Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people.

    Following the path indicated by Comrade Choibalsan, the respected leader of our people and the great builder of our people's democratic country, the working people of our country are consolidating the friendship with the great Chinese people and all peace-loving people, and will forever strive more vigorously for the cause of peace, democracy, and socialism, headed by our common friendly nation—the powerful Soviet Union.

                            Bumantsend, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic
                                            Ulaanbaatar, February 15, 1952

To Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:
    Please accept my sincere thanks for your fraternal condolences on the death of Marshal Choibalsan, the leader of our people, the founder of our independent people's democratic country, and the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic. In further consolidating the friendship between the people of our two countries, further seeking the prosperity of our countries, and making every effort to strengthen the cause of peace, democracy, and socialism, headed by our common friendly nation—the great Soviet Union, the heroic life of Comrade Choibalsan will forever be an inspiring example.

                        Lhamsuren, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic
                          Ulaanbaatar, February 15, 1952

### 蒙古布曼增迪主席和拉姆苏伦外长  电谢毛主席和周总理对乔巴山元帅的吊唁

1952-02-18
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古布曼增迪主席和拉姆苏伦外长
    电谢毛主席和周总理对乔巴山元帅的吊唁
    【新华社十七日讯】蒙古人民共和国大呼拉尔主席团主席布曼增迪和外交部长拉姆苏伦，十五日分别复电我中央人民政府主席毛泽东和政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来，答谢他们对乔巴山元帅逝世的吊唁。电文如下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东同志：
    我国人民的领袖、我们人民国家的创始人及组织者、蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席乔巴山元帅过早的逝世，承蒙致以兄弟般的深切哀悼，谨代表蒙古人民共和国大呼拉尔主席团、蒙古人民及我个人向您并通过您向与我们友好的中华人民共和国中央人民政府及中国人民表示衷心的谢意。
    我国劳动人民走着我国人民敬爱的领袖、我们人民民主国家的伟大建设者乔巴山元帅所指示的道路，巩固着与伟大中国人民及一切爱好和平的人民之间的友谊，并将永远更加坚强地为着以我们共同的友邦——强大的苏联为首的和平、民主及社会主义事业而奋斗。
                    蒙古人民共和国大呼拉尔主席团主席　布曼增迪
                                            一九五二年二月十五日于乌兰巴托中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来同志：
    对于我国人民的领袖、我们独立的人民民主国家的创始人、蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席乔巴山元帅的逝世，承蒙致以兄弟般的哀悼，总理同志，请接受我深挚的谢意。在进一步巩固我们两国人民之间的友谊，进一步求得我们国家的繁荣并全力加强以我们共同的友邦——伟大的苏联为首的和平、民主及社会主义阵营的事业中，乔巴山同志英勇的一生将永远是富有鼓舞力量的榜样。
                        蒙古人民共和国外交部部长　拉姆苏伦
                          一九五二年二月十五日于乌兰巴托
