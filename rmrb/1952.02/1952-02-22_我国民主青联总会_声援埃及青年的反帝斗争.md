Title: All-China Youth Federation Voice Support for Egyptian Youth's Anti-Imperialist Struggle
Author:
Date: 1952-02-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, February 21st: The All-China Democratic Youth Federation sent a telegram to Egyptian youth and students to commemorate "Day of Struggle Against Colonial System." The text of the telegram is as follows:

    To "Egyptian Gazette," Cairo, to be forwarded to Egyptian youth and students:

    On the occasion of "Day of Struggle Against Colonial System," Chinese youth extend fraternal greetings to the heroic Egyptian youth and students. We deeply sympathize with and firmly support your struggle, together with the Egyptian people, to safeguard sovereignty and strive for national independence, and we are willing to unite with you and advance in the struggle against imperialism and in defense of world peace.

    All-China Democratic Youth Federation

### 我国民主青联总会  声援埃及青年的反帝斗争

1952-02-22
第4版()
专栏：

    我国民主青联总会
    声援埃及青年的反帝斗争
    【新华社二十一日讯】中华全国民主青年联合总会为纪念“反对殖民制度斗争日”致电埃及青年学生，电文如下：
    开罗“埃及人报”转埃及青年学生
    际此“反对殖民制度斗争日”，中国青年谨向英勇的埃及青年与学生致以兄弟般的敬礼。我们深切同情并坚决支持你们与埃及人民一起所进行的维护主权、争取民族独立的斗争，并愿与你们在反对帝国主义、保卫世界和平的斗争中团结前进。
    中华全国民主青年联合总会
