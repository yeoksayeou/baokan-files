Author:
Date: 1952-02-11

    Our Great Motherland
    The establishment of the Sino-Soviet Civil Aviation Joint Stock Company is of great importance for developing China's aviation industry and strengthening economic cooperation between China and the Soviet Union. This is a giant passenger plane of the Sino-Soviet Civil Aviation Joint Stock Company at Beijing Station, carrying mail and passengers, preparing to take off.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中苏民用航空股份公司的创办，对于发展我国航空事业、加强中苏两国的经济合作有重要的作用。这是中苏民航股份公司的巨型客机在北京站运载邮件和旅客准备起飞时的情形。
