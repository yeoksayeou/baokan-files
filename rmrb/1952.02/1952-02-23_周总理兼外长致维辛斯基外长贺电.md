Title: Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's Congratulatory Telegram to Foreign Minister Vyshinsky
Author:
Date: 1952-02-23
Page: 1

    Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's Congratulatory Telegram to Foreign Minister Vyshinsky
    To Comrade Vyshinsky, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics:
    On the occasion of the glorious 34th anniversary of the Armed Forces of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, please accept my warm congratulations, Comrade Minister.
    Zhou Enlai, February 21, 1952, Beijing
