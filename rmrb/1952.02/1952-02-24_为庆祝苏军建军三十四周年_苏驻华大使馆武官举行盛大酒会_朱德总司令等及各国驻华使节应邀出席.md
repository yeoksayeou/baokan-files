Title: To Celebrate the 34th Anniversary of the Soviet Army, the Military Attaché of the Soviet Embassy in China Holds a Grand Reception; Commander-in-Chief Zhu De and Other Foreign Envoys to China are Invited to Attend
Author:
Date: 1952-02-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd. To celebrate the 34th anniversary of the Soviet Army, Lieutenant General Kotov, the Military Attaché of the Soviet Embassy in China, held a grand cocktail party at the embassy on the afternoon of the 23rd.

Invited to attend were: Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government, Vice Chairman of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, and Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the Government Administration Council and Vice Chairman of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Guo Moruo and Huang Yanpei, Vice Premiers of the Government Administration Council; Shen Junru, President of the Supreme People's Court; Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Su Yu, Deputy Chief of the General Staff; Luo Ronghuan, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate and Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Liu Yalou, Commander of the Air Force; Xiao Jingguang, Commander of the Navy; Chen Xilian, Commander of the Artillery Corps; Xu Guangda, Commander of the Armored Corps; Zhang Hanfu and Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Ministers of Foreign Affairs; Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the China Peace Committee and Against American Aggression; as well as senior officers of the Army, Navy and Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, heads of various departments of the Central People's Government, responsible persons of various democratic parties and people's organizations, Zhang Youyu and Wu Han, Deputy Mayors of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and more than 500 others.

Foreign envoys to China who were invited to attend were: Czechoslovak Ambassador Weiskopf, Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Indian Ambassador Panikkar, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic with the rank of Ambassador Koenig, Hungarian Ambassador Saffronko, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Burmese Ambassador U La Maung, Pakistani Ambassador Rochar, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy Isha Mahdi, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy Doting, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Delegation Zhou Liang, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Korean Embassy Choi Young, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Danish Legation Meister, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Swedish Embassy Bevan, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Mongolian Embassy Darhazhabu, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Finnish Legation Brandstedt, Counselor of the Swiss Legation Schitina, as well as embassy staff, Norwegian government negotiation representative Goran, Dutch government negotiation representative Van der Put, and others.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere. During the reception, the hosts and guests frequently toasted to the friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union and the two countries' armies, to the health of the leader of the working people of the world, the genius commander of the Soviet Army, Marshal Stalin, and to the health of the great leader of the Chinese people, the creator and leader of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Chairman Mao Zedong.

### 为庆祝苏军建军三十四周年  苏驻华大使馆武官举行盛大酒会  朱德总司令等及各国驻华使节应邀出席

1952-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

    为庆祝苏军建军三十四周年
    苏驻华大使馆武官举行盛大酒会
    朱德总司令等及各国驻华使节应邀出席
    【新华社二十三日讯】为庆祝苏军建军三十四周年，苏联驻我国大使馆武官柯托夫中将，特在二十三日下午在大使馆举行盛大鸡尾酒会。
    应邀到会的有：中央人民政府副主席、人民革命军事委员会副主席、中国人民解放军总司令朱德，中央人民政府副主席李济深，政务院总理兼人民革命军事委员会副主席周恩来，政务院副总理郭沫若、黄炎培，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻、副总参谋长粟裕，最高人民检察署检察长、中国人民解放军总政治部主任罗荣桓，中国人民解放军总政治部副主任萧华，空军部队司令刘亚楼，海军部队司令萧劲光，炮兵部队司令陈锡联，装甲部队司令许光达，外交部副部长章汉夫、伍修权，中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会副主席陈叔通，以及中国人民解放军陆空海军高级军官、中央人民政府各部门首长和各民主党派、人民团体负责人，北京市人民政府副市长张友渔、吴晗等共五百余人。
    应邀出席的各国驻华使节有：捷克斯洛伐克大使魏斯柯普夫、罗马尼亚大使鲁登科、印度大使潘尼迦、德意志民主共和国外交使团大使衔团长柯尼希、匈牙利大使夏法朗柯、保加利亚大使彼得科夫、缅甸大使吴拉茂、巴基斯坦大使罗查、印尼大使馆临时代办伊沙·玛蒂、波兰大使馆临时代办道定、越南代表团临时代办周亮、朝鲜大使馆临时代办崔英、丹麦公使馆临时代办迈思妥、瑞典大使馆临时代办贝凡、蒙古大使馆临时代办达尔哈热布、芬兰公使馆临时代办勃朗斯坦特、瑞士公使馆参赞施禔纳、以及各使馆人员和挪威政府谈判代表高兰、荷兰政府谈判代表范德普等。
    酒会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。席间宾主频频为中苏两国人民和两国军队的友谊，为世界劳动人民的领袖、苏联军队的天才统帅斯大林大元帅的健康，为中国人民的伟大领袖、中国人民解放军的创造者和领导者毛泽东主席的健康而干杯。
