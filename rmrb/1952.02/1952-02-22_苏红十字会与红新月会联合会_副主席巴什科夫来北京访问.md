Title: Vice Chairman Bashkov of the Union of Soviet Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies Visits Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-02-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 21st:

Vice Chairman of the Union of Soviet Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies, Professor of Dermatology and Venereology at Moscow University, Bashkov, and his delegation arrived in Beijing on January 17th at the invitation of the Red Cross Society of China. During the visit, they visited various medical and health institutions in Beijing, toured the capital's scenic spots, and held discussions with personnel from the Red Cross Society of China, Beijing medical, health, and educational institutions, and the Communist Youth League. They also gave two academic lectures on medical prevention issues at Peking Union Medical College Hospital. With the assistance of the Red Cross Society of China, Bashkov and others are in Beijing preparing for the establishment of the Soviet Red Cross Hospital.

Bashkov's visit to Beijing has come to an end, and he is scheduled to return home on the 22nd. Before his departure, he spoke to reporters about his impressions of visiting New China, praising the high patriotic enthusiasm of the Chinese working people in engaging in economic construction, and expressing his firm belief that under the leadership of the Central People's Government headed by Mao Zedong, the Chinese people will surely be able to build a beautiful and happy life.

### 苏红十字会与红新月会联合会  副主席巴什科夫来北京访问

1952-02-22
第4版()
专栏：

    苏红十字会与红新月会联合会
    副主席巴什科夫来北京访问
    【新华社二十一日讯】苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会副主席、莫斯科大学皮肤花柳科教授巴什科夫等一行，应我国红十字会总会的邀请，在一月十七日抵达北京访问。在访问期间，曾参观北京各医疗卫生机关，游览首都的名胜，并和中国红十字会、北京医疗、卫生、教育机关及青年团的人员举行了座谈，又在北京协和医院作了两次有关医疗预防问题的学术讲演。巴什科夫等在中国红十字会总会的协助下，在北京进行筹办苏联红十字会医院的工作。
    巴什科夫等访问北京已告完毕，定于二十二日返国。他在临行前曾向记者畅谈了访问新中国的印象，盛赞中国劳动人民从事经济建设的高度爱国热情，并深信中国人民在以毛泽东为首的中央人民政府的领导下，必将能够建设美好和幸福的生活。
