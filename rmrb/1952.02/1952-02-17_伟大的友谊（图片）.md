    (X) This last discovered child was finally rescued by Durasov, the bridge-guarding soldiers, and the workers.
    (XI) The next day, the Kaiyuan County People's Government organized rescue boats to transport Durasov, the soldiers, the workers, and the disaster victims from the bridgehead surrounded by floods to Kaiyuan. When the disaster victims separated from Durasov, they said with tears of emotion, "The Soviet comrades and soldiers treat us so well, even better than our own relatives!" (The End)

### 伟大的友谊（图片）
冯真  乌恩  苗地  曾善庆  郝宏章
1952-02-17
第3版()
专栏：

    伟大的友谊
    冯真、乌恩、苗地  曾善庆、郝宏章合作
    （十）这个最后被发现的孩子，终于被杜拉索夫和守桥战士、工人们营救上来了。
    （十一）第二天开原县人民政府组织了救生船，从洪水包围的桥头把杜拉索夫、战士、工人和灾民们送到开原去。当灾民和杜拉索夫分开时，他们感动地流着泪说：“苏联同志和战士们待我们这样好，简直比亲人还亲哩。”（完）
