Title: CPPCC National Committee Member Guo Guanjie Passes Away in Beijing; Interment and Transfer of Remains Scheduled for Today
Author:
Date: 1952-02-13
Page: 1

    CPPCC National Committee Member
    Guo Guanjie Passes Away in Beijing
    Interment and Transfer of Remains Scheduled for Today
    [This newspaper's report] Guo Guanjie, member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, member of the Political and Legal Affairs Committee of the Central People's Government, and member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, passed away at 2:50 PM on the twelfth of this month at Peking Union Medical College Hospital. Comrade Guo Guanjie was a native of Meixian, Guangdong, born in 1892, at the age of sixty-one. He participated in the Xinhai Revolution and the Great Revolution of 1927 and served as a professor at Beijing Normal University and other schools. The funeral committee of Comrade Guo Guanjie issued an obituary, stating that the interment will be held at Peking Union Medical College Hospital at 1:00 PM today (the 13th), and the remains will be temporarily placed at Jiaxing Temple.

### 人民政协全国委员会委员  郭冠杰在北京病逝  定今天入殓并移灵

1952-02-13
第1版()
专栏：

    人民政协全国委员会委员
    郭冠杰在北京病逝
    定今天入殓并移灵
    【本报讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会委员、中央人民政府政治法律委员会委员、中国农工民主党中央委员郭冠杰，在本月十二日下午二时五十分病逝于北京协和医院。郭冠杰委员为广东梅县人，生于一八九二年，享年六十一岁，曾参加辛亥革命和一九二七年大革命，历任北京师范大学等校教授。郭冠杰委员治丧委员会发出讣告，定今天（十三日）下午一时在北京协和医院入殓，并移灵嘉兴寺暂厝。
