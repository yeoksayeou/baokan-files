Title: The US and the Soviet Union Step Up "Détente" With No Worries on the Western Front; The United States Again Transfers Ships from the Atlantic Fleet to Participate in the Vietnam War

Author: 
Date: 1966-01-23
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, January 22nd: While the Johnson administration is transferring large numbers of troops from Western Europe to South Vietnam, it has also transferred a batch of warships from the Atlantic Fleet to participate in the Vietnam War.
    According to a report by United Press International on the 21st, six destroyers from the US Atlantic Fleet left the Newport Naval Base on the US Atlantic coast a few days ago, "They received orders to go to South Vietnam" and have been "assigned tasks in the South China Sea." The report also said that another four destroyers also sailed from Newport at the same time, but the "destination of these four was not revealed." Another telegram issued by the same news agency on the same day also revealed that the United States "is currently removing machine guns from tanks stationed in Europe and rushing them to South Vietnam" because "this type of weapon is extremely scarce (in South Vietnam)."
    With the US and the Soviet Union stepping up "détente" in Western Europe and the US having no worries on the Western Front, the Johnson administration has been transferring military personnel, ships, artillery, and ammunition from Western Europe to the Vietnam War since the middle of last year to alleviate the difficulties of insufficient troops and supply shortages in the Vietnam War.

### 美苏加紧搞“缓和”  西线无后顾之忧  美国又抽调大西洋舰队舰只参加侵越战争

1966-01-23
第3版()
专栏：

    美苏加紧搞“缓和”  西线无后顾之忧
    美国又抽调大西洋舰队舰只参加侵越战争
    新华社二十二日讯  华盛顿消息：约翰逊政府在从西欧抽调大量兵员去南越的同时，又从大西洋舰队抽调了一批军舰去参加侵越战争。
    据合众国际社二十一日报道，美国大西洋舰队的六艘驱逐舰已经在前几天离开了美国在大西洋海岸的新港海军基地，“它们接到了去南越的命令”，并且已“在南中国海分配了任务”。报道还说，另外还有四艘驱逐舰也在同时驶离了新港，但是这四艘的“目的地没有透露”。这家通讯社在同一天发出的另外一条电讯中还透露，美国“目前正从驻在欧洲的坦克上把机枪拆下来，赶运去南越”，因为“这种武器（在南越）极其缺乏”。
    在美苏加紧在西欧搞“缓和”、美国在西线无后顾之忧的情况下，约翰逊政府从去年年中起，一直在从西欧方面抽调军事人员，舰只、大炮、弹药投入侵越战争，以解救侵越兵力不足和供应短缺的困难。
