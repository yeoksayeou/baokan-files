Title: Johnson Government Intensifies Pace of Expanding War of Aggression Against Vietnam; US Pacific Air Force Commander Boasts Readiness to Bomb Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-01-29
Edition: 5

    The Johnson Government Intensifies Pace of Expanding War of Aggression Against Vietnam
    US Pacific Air Force Commander Boasts Readiness to Bomb Vietnam
    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 28th – U.S. Pacific Air Forces Commander Harris boasted on the 27th that his forces are ready to resume bombing North Vietnam.

    Speaking in Honolulu, Harris also said that the U.S. Air Force strength in the Pacific region would increase from the current 88,000 to 110,000 personnel.

    On the 27th, White House Press Secretary Moyers again announced that Johnson was considering resuming bombing of North Vietnam. He said that Johnson is "studying the diplomatic and military facts" and "attempting to weigh the possible consequences of each step and each decision in order to determine what is in the best interest of the United States."

    At the same time, the American propaganda machine continues to report news of the imminent resumption of bombing and further expansion of the war, cooperating with the authorities in conducting blatant war blackmail against the Vietnamese people. Associated Press White House correspondent Cormier reported on the 27th that "the President himself has no doubt that the pause in bombing has failed to bring peace closer," and "it is said that Johnson has decided to reaffirm America's resolve in Vietnam." He revealed that Johnson is currently considering "when to act and whether to expand the air strikes."

    On the same day, United Press International also reported that Johnson "is nearing a decision to resume bombing of North Vietnam. His main concerns now are timing and scale." The report also said that Johnson hopes "to display resolve to the greatest extent possible at the lowest possible cost in manpower and materiel."

    An advance copy of the January 31st issue of *U.S. News & World Report* in a lengthy report from Saigon, even more brazenly threatened that "the United States is preparing for a larger-scale war while opening the door to gestures of reconciliation," and "if peace efforts fail completely, the prospect is a greatly escalated war—one that many here believe will engulf all of Southeast Asia before it is over."

    Referring to the issue of resuming bombing, the report wrote: "The demand to resume bombing is strong among the military here," and "if the U.S. resumes bombing—which most believe is only a matter of time—the target list is likely to expand"; it said: "Senior American officials believe that more punishment is needed to convince Hanoi that it has more to gain through peaceful negotiations than through war."

### 约翰逊政府加紧扩大侵越战争步伐  美国太平洋空军司令扬言已作好轰炸越南的准备

1966-01-29
第5版()
专栏：

    约翰逊政府加紧扩大侵越战争步伐
    美国太平洋空军司令扬言已作好轰炸越南的准备
    新华社二十八日讯　华盛顿消息：美国驻太平洋地区空军司令哈里斯二十七日扬言，他的部队已经作好恢复轰炸越南北方的准备。
    哈里斯在檀香山发表谈话时还说，美国在太平洋地区的空军兵力，将由目前的八万八千人增加到十一万人。
    二十七日，白宫新闻秘书莫耶斯再次宣布，约翰逊正在考虑恢复对越南北方的轰炸的问题。他说：约翰逊“正在研究外交方面和军事方面的事实”，他“试图斟酌每一个步骤以及每一种决定所可能引起的后果，以便断定什么对美国最有利”。
    与此同时，美国的宣传机器也在继续报道美国即将恢复轰炸和进一步扩大战争的消息，配合官方对越南人民进行露骨的战争讹诈。美联社驻白宫记者科米尔二十七日报道说：“总统本人对于暂停轰炸未能使和平临近这一点已经毫无怀疑”，“据说，约翰逊决定要重新显示美国在越南的坚定态度”。他透露，约翰逊目前正在考虑“何时行动以及是否扩大空袭”的问题。
    同一天，合众国际社也报道说，约翰逊“正在接近于决定恢复轰炸北越。他现在主要关心的问题是时间和规模”。消息还说，约翰逊希望“以最低限度的人力、物力为代价来最大程度地显示坚定态度”。
    提前出版的一月三十一日一期的《美国新闻与世界报道》在一篇发自西贡的长篇报道中，甚至更加明目张胆地威胁说：“美国正在准备进行更大规模的战争，同时敞开作出和解姿态的大门”，“如果和平努力完全失败，前景是战争大大升级——这里的许多人相信，这场战争在结束以前将把整个东南亚卷入。”
    这篇报道在谈到恢复轰炸的问题时写道：“恢复轰炸的要求在这里的军人当中是强烈的”，“如果美国恢复轰炸——大部分人认为这只是时间问题——目标清单可能扩大”；它说：“高级美国官员认为，需要更多的惩罚才能使河内相信，它通过战争得到的东西要比通过和平谈判得到的为少。”
