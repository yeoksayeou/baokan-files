Title: Actively Preparing to Participate in America's War of Aggression in Asia: Japanese "Self-Defense Forces" Hold Large-Scale Map Exercise; "Defense Agency" Secretly Builds Missile Base Under the Guise of Constructing an American Military Ballfield

Author:

Date: 1966-01-30
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, January 24th: Some officers of the Japanese "Self-Defense Forces" are holding large-scale map exercises, stepping up war preparations.
    According to reports from newspapers such as the *Tokyo Shimbun*, the "Eastern方面隊司令部 [Tōbu hōmen sō shireibu]" (Eastern方面隊 Command) of the Japanese "Ground Self-Defense Force" has convened 150 officers, including those from the "Eastern方面隊 Command," the "1st Division," and the "12th Division," to hold a five-day large-scale map exercise simulating nuclear war from the 24th to the 28th at the Sōmagahara "12th Division Command" in Gunma Prefecture.
    This map exercise targets a hypothetical enemy possessing considerable modern equipment. The exercise includes artillery battles and close combat, communication and liaison, and logistical support.
    The *Akahata* reported on the 23rd that this map exercise shows that the Japanese "Self-Defense Forces" want to participate in wars launched by U.S. imperialism in places like Vietnam and the Korean Peninsula. This exercise is a further step beyond the "Three Arrows Operation Plan" previously formulated by the Japanese "Defense Agency" with China and Korea as hypothetical enemies.
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, January 24th: According to a report in the *Tokyo Shimbun* on the 23rd, the Japanese "Defense Agency" is attempting to secretly build a missile base in Tokyo under the guise of constructing an American military golf course. According to reports, the Japanese "Defense Agency" recently notified the Tokyo Metropolitan Government that the U.S. military intends to build a "golf course" near a new residential area in Minami-Tama, Tokyo. The "Defense Agency" once designated this location as the site for a Hawk missile base in the Second Expansion Plan, but this attempt failed due to strong opposition from the Tokyo Metropolitan Assembly and the masses. Now, the "Defense Agency" has changed its tactics and wants to build a so-called "golf course" for American military use here.

### 为参加美国侵略亚洲的战争积极准备  日本“自卫队”举行大规模图上作业演习  “防卫厅”假借修建美军球场为名秘密修建导弹基地

1966-01-30
第4版()
专栏：

    为参加美国侵略亚洲的战争积极准备
    日本“自卫队”举行大规模图上作业演习
    “防卫厅”假借修建美军球场为名秘密修建导弹基地
    据新华社东京二十四日电  日本“自卫队”的一些军官正在举行大规模图上作业演习，加紧进行战争准备。
    据《东京新闻》等报纸报道，日本“陆上自卫队东部方面司令部”召集了包括“东部方面司令部”、“第一师团”和“第十二师团”的一百五十名军官，从二十四日到二十八日，在群马县相马原“第十二师团司令部”举行五天的假想核战争的大规模图上作业演习。
    这次图上演习以拥有相当现代化装备的假想敌为对象。演习的内容包括炮战和近战，通讯联络和兵站工作等。
    《赤旗报》二十三日报道说，这次图上作业演习表明，日本“自卫队”想要参加由美帝国主义在越南和朝鲜半岛等地方发动的战争。这次演习比日本“防卫厅”过去制定的以中国和朝鲜为假想敌的“三矢作战计划”更进了一步。
    据新华社东京二十四日电据《东京新闻》二十三日报道，日本“防卫厅”企图假借修建美国军用高尔夫球场的名义，在东京秘密修建一个导弹基地。据报道，日本“防卫厅”最近通知东京都当局说，美军要在东京南多摩的新住宅区附近修建一处“高尔夫球场”。“防卫厅”曾经把这个地点指定为第二次扩军计划中的隼式导弹基地用地，但是，由于遭到东京都议会和群众的强烈反对，这个企图失败了。现在，“防卫厅”又变换手法，要在这里修建一所美国军用的所谓“高尔夫球场”。
