Title: De Gaulle Announces Inauguration as Next French President
Author:
Date: 1966-01-11
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, January 8th - De Gaulle announced his official inauguration as the next French President on the morning of the 8th, and simultaneously appointed the new government.
    De Gaulle was re-elected as French President on December 19th of last year.
    He reappointed Georges Pompidou as Prime Minister, and based on Pompidou's recommendation, appointed the ministers of the new French government.
    According to a communiqué from the Presidential Palace of the Republic, the key ministers and ministers of state of the previous government who remain in office are: Minister of State for Cultural Affairs André Malraux, Minister of State for Administrative Reform Louis Joxe, Minister of Foreign Affairs Maurice Couve de Murville, Minister of the Interior Roger Frey, and Minister of the Armed Forces Pierre Messmer.
    The main changes in the new government are: Former Prime Minister and one of the main leaders of the Union for the New Republic, Michel Debré, was appointed Minister of Finance and Economy, replacing Valéry Giscard d'Estaing, who is no longer a member of the new government; former Cabinet Prime Minister Edgar Faure was appointed Minister of Agriculture, and he was not a member of the previous government.

### 戴高乐宣布就任下届法国总统

1966-01-11
第4版()
专栏：

    戴高乐宣布就任下届法国总统
    新华社巴黎八日电  戴高乐八日上午宣布正式就任下一届法国总统，同时任命了新政府。
    戴高乐是在去年十二月十九日再次当选为法国总统的。
    他重新任命乔治·蓬皮杜为总理，并且根据蓬皮杜的建议，任命了法国新政府的部长。
    根据共和国总统府的公报说，上届政府的主要部长和国务部长留任的有：文化事务国务部长安德烈·马尔罗，负责行政改革的国务部长路易·若克斯，外交部长莫里斯·顾夫·德姆维尔，内政部长罗歇弗雷，武装部队部长皮埃尔·梅斯梅尔。
    新政府的主要变动是：前总理、保卫新共和联盟主要领导之一米歇尔·德勃雷被任命为财政经济部长，代替不再成为新政府成员的吉斯卡尔·德斯坦；前内阁总理埃加·富尔被任命为农业部长，他不是上届政府的成员。
