Title: Zeng Yongquan Appointed as China's Ambassador to Romania
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-29
Edition: 1

    Zeng Yongquan Appointed as China's Ambassador to Romania
    Xinhua News Agency, January 28th -- Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has appointed Zeng Yongquan as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Socialist Republic of Romania. Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has removed Liu Fang from the position of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Socialist Republic of Romania.

### 曾涌泉被任命为我驻罗马尼亚大使

1966-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    曾涌泉被任命为我驻罗马尼亚大使
    新华社二十八日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇任命曾涌泉为中华人民共和国驻罗马尼亚社会主义共和国特命全权大使。中华人民共和国主席刘少奇免去刘放的中华人民共和国驻罗马尼亚社会主义共和国特命全权大使的职务。
