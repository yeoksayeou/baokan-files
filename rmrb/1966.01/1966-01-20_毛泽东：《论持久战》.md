Title: Mao Zedong: On Protracted War
Author: Mao Zedong
Date: 1966-01-20
Edition: 6

    The deepest source of power in war lies within the masses. The main reason why Japan dares to bully us lies in the disorganized state of the Chinese people. Overcoming this shortcoming will place the Japanese invaders before our hundreds of millions of people who have stood up, making them like a wild bull rushing into a fire array. Even our slightest shout will startle it greatly, and this wild bull will inevitably be burned to death.
    Mao Zedong: On Protracted War

### 毛泽东：《论持久战》

1966-01-20
第6版()
专栏：

    战争的伟力之最深厚的根源，存在于民众之中。日本敢于欺负我们，主要的原因在于中国民众的无组织状态。克服了这一缺点，就把日本侵略者置于我们数万万站起来了的人民之前，使它象一匹野牛冲入火阵，我们一声唤也要把它吓一大跳，这匹野牛就非烧死不可。
    毛泽东：《论持久战》
