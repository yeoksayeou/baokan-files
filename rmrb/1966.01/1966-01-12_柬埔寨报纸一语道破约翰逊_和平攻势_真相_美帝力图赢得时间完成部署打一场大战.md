Title: Cambodian Newspaper Exposes the Truth of Johnson's "Peace Offensive": US Imperialism Attempts to Buy Time to Complete Deployment for a Major War
Author:
Date: 1966-01-12
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 10th: *Cambodge Actualités* published an editorial on the 10th regarding Johnson's "peace offensive," stating, "American leaders do not genuinely seek peace in Vietnam; they merely aim to create a convenient pretext to deceive world opinion."

    The editorial stated, "The Americans are actually continuing strategic deployments of unprecedented scale in Indochina and the Pacific region." The editorial said, "Some have long since cleverly interpreted Johnson's current 'bombing pause' and 'diplomatic ballet' as due to his need to gain six weeks to complete these deployments, transporting all necessary means there for a major battle." The editorial pointed out, "This is the truth."

    The newspaper also published a commentary stating, "The aim of the US imperialists' war of aggression against Vietnam is to attempt to turn Vietnam into a new type of colony. To oppress and exploit in Vietnam is the true purpose of the imperialists."

    The commentary said: The United States "knows very well what it is doing in Vietnam, and they also know why they are clinging to Vietnam. But unlike ordinary criminals, they attempt to reverse black and white, flaunting their hypocrisy, cunning tricks, and the heinous crimes they have committed."

### 柬埔寨报纸一语道破约翰逊“和平攻势”真相  美帝力图赢得时间完成部署打一场大战

1966-01-12
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨报纸一语道破约翰逊“和平攻势”真相
    美帝力图赢得时间完成部署打一场大战
    据新华社金边十日电  《柬埔寨电讯报》十日对约翰逊的“和平攻势”发表社论说，“美国领导人并不是真的想要在越南谋求和平，他们只是想制造一个方便的借口，以欺骗世界舆论。”
    这篇社论说，“美国人实际上继续在印度支那和太平洋地区作规模空前的战略部署。”社论说，“有些人早已把约翰逊目前的‘暂停轰炸’和‘外交芭蕾舞’奇妙地解释为由于他需要赢得六周时间，以便完成这些部署，为一场大战斗，把一切必要的手段运到那里去。”社论指出，“这就是事实真相。”
    这家报纸还发表评论说，“美帝国主义者对越南进行侵略战争的目的，是企图把越南变成新型的殖民地。在越南进行压迫和剥削，这就是帝国主义者的真正目的。”
    评论说：美国“自己很清楚在越南干的是什么，他们也知道自己赖在越南是为了什么。但是他们同一般的罪犯不同，企图颠倒黑白。以伪善、狡诈的伎俩和所犯下的滔天罪行，在这里大显身手。”
