Title: Lamentations

Author:
Date: 1966-01-01
Edition: 4

    Lamentations
      United States: Everything Looks Discouraging
      (In Vietnam), we have reached a point where there will never be a "good" solution, let alone an easy one. No matter which direction we look, the scene is discouraging.
    …Currently, no one doubts that the end of this year marks only the beginning. It is now clear that what will happen in the future will be painful….
                    *The Wall Street Journal*, December 17th of last year
                      ×××
    The world is a mess…the earth is full of trouble spots…. In the eyes of American officials, the world's chaos seems to be getting worse and worse.
    Vietnam is not the only place that is worrying…The situation in sub-Saharan Africa is very unstable, and Rhodesia is a new powder keg…Just look at South America near the United States and you will find a turbulent continent gradually moving towards real crisis and trouble…. NATO, as an alliance, is in deep trouble….
                  *U.S. News & World Report*, December 6th of last year
      Britain: International Status at Its Lowest Point
    As far as Britain is concerned, of course, many things happened in 1965, but nothing was resolved—economically (the root cause of all our difficulties) even more so than in other areas. But for us, it was also the year our international status reached its lowest point. Britain has reached a point where it is beginning to lose confidence due to being strapped for cash and heavily indebted.
                  *Financial Times*, December 29th of last year
                      ×××
    The British economy and currency need American support. British defense and foreign policy are dependent on Washington's defense and foreign policy…. The British Commonwealth of twenty-two nations is crumbling.
                            Associated Press, December 26th of last year
      West Germany: New Year Faces New Difficulties
      (West Germany) will not be easier internationally next year than this year. Perhaps it will have to deal with some new difficulties, which is entirely possible.
              *Frankfurter Allgemeine Zeitung*, December 29th of last year
      Japan: Deeply Trapped in a Mire of Suffering
      The "Year of Suffering," 1965, is about to pass.
    It can be said that this year was a heavy "gray year." Whether from a political, economic, or social perspective, how to climb out of this deep mire and stand up will be the biggest issue in the coming year. Japan Kyodo News Agency, December 11th of last year
      India: Greater Grain Shortages Are About to Appear
    There are some disturbing signs that India is about to experience its worst food crisis in more than two decades. …The political consequences of the famine that may occur next year cannot be quietly ignored.
                      *Financial Times*, December 9th of last year

### 哀鸣录

1966-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

    哀鸣录
      美国：一切景象都令人丧气
      （在越南），我们已经处于这样一种境地：再也不会有什么“良好的”解决办法，更不用说容易的解决办法了。我们不管朝哪个方向看，景象都是令人丧气的。
    ……目前肯定没有人怀疑，今年年底标志着仅仅是开端。现在清楚地看出，今后会发生的事情是很痛苦的……。
                    美国《华尔街日报》去年十二月十七日
                      ×××
    世界一团混乱，……地球到处是麻烦地点……。在美国官员眼中，世界的混乱看来还会越来越糟。
    越南并不是唯一使人忧虑的地点……。非洲大陆撒哈拉以南的局势是很不稳定的，罗得西亚就是一个新的火药桶……。只要看看临近本国（美国）的南美洲就会发现一个动荡不定的大陆正在逐渐走向真正的危机和麻烦……。北大西洋公约组织作为一个联盟，正陷在深深的麻烦中……。
                  《美国新闻与世界报道》去年十二月六日
      英国：国际地位降到最低点
    就英国来说，当然，一九六五年发生了许多事情，但是没有解决任何事情——经济方面（我们全部困难的根本原因）比其他方面更其如此。但是对我们来说，也是我们的国际地位降到最低点的一年，英国已经到了因捉襟见肘和负债累累而开始失去自信的地步。
                  英国《金融时报》去年十二月二十九日
                      ×××
    英国的经济和货币需要美国支持。英国的防务和对外政策依附于华盛顿的防务和对外政策……。英国的由二十二个国家组成的联邦正在摇摇欲坠中。
                            美联社去年十二月二十六日
      西德：新的一年面临新困难
      （西德）明年在国际方面不会比今年轻松。也许它还要对付一些新添的困难，这是完全可能的。
              西德《法兰克福汇报》去年十二月二十九日
      日本：深陷在苦难的泥沼中
      “苦难的一年”的一九六五年，即将逝去。
    可以说今年是沉重的“灰色的一年”。无论是从政治方面、经济方面、社会方面来看，怎样能从这个深陷的泥沼中爬出来、站起来，将是即将到来的一年的最大的课题。日本共同社去年十二月十一日
      印度：更大的粮荒将要出现
    有一些令人不安的迹象表明，印度即将发生它二十多年来的最严重的粮食危机。……来年可能发生的饥馑的政治后果是不能悄悄地忽略过去的。
                      英国《金融时报》去年十二月九日
