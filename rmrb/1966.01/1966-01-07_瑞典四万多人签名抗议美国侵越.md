Title: More Than 40,000 Swedes Sign Petition Protesting U.S. Aggression in Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-01-07
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, January 4th: More than 40,000 people from all walks of life in Sweden, as well as some organizations, have signed a letter protesting U.S. aggression against Vietnam. The letter demands that the United States cease its war of aggression in Vietnam.
    This letter was delivered to the U.S. Embassy in Sweden on the 3rd.

### 瑞典四万多人签名抗议美国侵越

1966-01-07
第3版()
专栏：

    瑞典四万多人签名抗议美国侵越
    新华社斯德哥尔摩四日电　瑞典各界四万多人和一些团体，在一封抗议美国侵略越南的信件上签了名。信中要求美国停止在越南的侵略战争。
    这封信已经在三日交给美国驻瑞典大使馆。
