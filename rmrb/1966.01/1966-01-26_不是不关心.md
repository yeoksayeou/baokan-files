    Not Indifference
    Walter Lippmann said in an article that he recently discovered during a visit to South America that anti-American sentiment is widespread in Latin America. What is the reason for this? Lippmann believes it is mainly because
  “people feel that Washington does not care much about Latin America.”
    In fact, the reason why anti-American sentiment is so strong in Latin America today is precisely because Washington "cares too much" about Latin America. American imperialism regards Latin America as its own "backyard" and its own "exclusive preserve," interfering everywhere. Can it still be said that it "doesn't care much"? If it "cared" less, perhaps anti-American sentiment in Latin America would not be so strong.

### 不是不关心

1966-01-26
第3版()
专栏：三言两语

    不是不关心
    李普曼在一篇文章中说，他最近访问南美时发现，拉丁美洲普遍存在着反美情绪。这是什么原因呢？李普曼认为主要是因为
  “人们感觉到华盛顿并不很关心拉丁美洲”。
    事实上，今天拉丁美洲反美情绪之所以如此强烈，正是由于华盛顿对拉丁美洲“太关心”之故。美帝国主义把拉丁美洲看作是自己的“后院”、自己的“禁脔”，到处干涉。还能说是“不很关心”吗？如果它少“关心”一些，也许拉丁美洲的反美情绪还不致于强烈到这样的程度。
