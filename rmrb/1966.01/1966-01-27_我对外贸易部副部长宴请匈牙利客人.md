Title: My Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Hosts Hungarian Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-01-27
Edition: 5

    My Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Hosts Hungarian Guests
    Xinhua News Agency, January 26th – Jiang Ming, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, hosted a simple banquet tonight for Kódé, deputy head of the Hungarian government trade delegation, and members of the delegation.

**Named Entities**

江明	Jiang Ming
匈牙利	Hungary
新华社	Xinhua News Agency
科德门	Kódé
对外贸易部	Ministry of Foreign Trade
