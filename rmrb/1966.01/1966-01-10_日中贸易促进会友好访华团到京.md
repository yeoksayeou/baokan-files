Title: Japan-China Trade Promotion Association Friendship Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-01-10
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, January 9th—The Japan-China Trade Promotion Association Friendship Delegation, invited by the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The delegation consists of eighteen people, led by head Kawase Kazuo and Secretary-General Sumi Masanori.
    Zhang Huaidong, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and responsible persons from relevant departments, went to the airport to welcome them. Representatives of Japanese friendly trading companies in Beijing also welcomed them at the airport.

### 日中贸易促进会友好访华团到京

1966-01-10
第4版()
专栏：

    日中贸易促进会友好访华团到京
    新华社九日讯　应中国国际贸易促进委员会邀请前来我国访问的日中贸易促进会友好访华代表团一行十八人，在团长川濑一贯、秘书长隅井正典率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席张化东和有关方面负责人等。到机场欢迎的，还有在京的日本友好商社代表。
