Title: Sino-Albanian Friendship Association Reception Celebrates the 20th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of Albania. The Chinese and Albanian People Hold High the Banner of Anti-Imperialism and Anti-Revisionism and Advance Shoulder to Shoulder to Victory.
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-11
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, January 10th -- The Chinese-Albanian Friendship Association held a reception this evening to warmly celebrate the 20th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of Albania.
    Jiang Nanxiang, President of the Sino-Albanian Friendship Association, and leading figures from various sectors Wang Bingnan, Ding Xilin, Zhao Xinchu, Yang Xiandong, gathered with the Albanian Ambassador to China, Nesti Nase, the Albanian Deputy Minister of Agriculture, Mendu Permeti, who is currently visiting Beijing, and other Albanian comrades in Beijing, to celebrate the glorious festival of the Albanian people.
    At the reception, filled with an atmosphere of cordial friendship, Jiang Nanxiang and Ambassador Nesti Nase delivered speeches successively. They warmly praised the great friendship and militant solidarity between the Chinese and Albanian peoples, and wished that the two peoples would hold even higher the banner of Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism, hold even higher the banner against imperialism and modern revisionism, and advance shoulder to shoulder to victory in their common struggle.

### 中阿友协招待会庆祝阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国成立二十周年  中阿人民高举反帝反修旗帜并肩胜利前进

1966-01-11
第4版()
专栏：

    中阿友协招待会庆祝阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国成立二十周年
    中阿人民高举反帝反修旗帜并肩胜利前进
    新华社十日讯  中国阿尔巴尼亚友好协会今晚举行招待会，热烈庆祝阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国成立二十周年。
    中阿友协会长蒋南翔，各方面的负责人王炳南、丁西林、赵辛初、杨显东，同阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使奈斯蒂·纳赛，正在北京访问的阿尔巴尼亚农业部副部长门道尔·佩尔梅蒂，以及在北京的阿尔巴尼亚其他同志，欢聚一堂，共庆阿尔巴尼亚人民的光辉节日。
    在洋溢着亲切友好气氛的招待会上，蒋南翔和奈斯蒂·纳赛大使先后讲了话。他们热情地颂扬中阿两国人民的伟大友谊和战斗团结，并祝两国人民更高地举起马克思列宁主义和无产阶级国际主义的旗帜，更高地举起反对帝国主义和现代修正主义的旗帜，在共同的斗争中并肩胜利前进。
