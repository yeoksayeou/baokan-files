Title: Thai Troops Invade Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1966-01-24
Edition: 3

    Thai Troops Invade Cambodia
    According to a Xinhua News Agency dispatch from Phnom Penh on the 23rd, preliminary reports indicate that on the 21st, around 15:00 hours, approximately one hundred Thai armed forces personnel, divided into several groups, invaded Cambodian territory in the direction of the Cambodian provincial defense army outpost in Chi Ne Sa, Koh Kong Province, along the Cambodian coast. These Thai invaders encountered Cambodian troops at the forward outpost.
    Cambodian reinforcements were immediately dispatched to the location. The two sides exchanged fire until 18:15 hours. One Cambodian provincial defense army soldier sustained minor injuries.

### 泰国军队侵犯柬埔寨

1966-01-24
第3版()
专栏：

    泰国军队侵犯柬埔寨
    据新华社金边二十三日电  据柬埔寨新闻社二十三日报道，据初步报告，二十一日将近十五点钟，泰国武装部队约一百人，分几路朝柬埔寨沿海戈公省奇内萨地方的柬埔寨省防军哨所方向侵入柬埔寨领土。这些泰国入侵者同柬埔寨前沿哨所的柬埔寨部队遭遇。
    柬埔寨增援部队立即开到那里。双方交火直到十八点一刻。一名柬埔寨省防军受轻伤。
