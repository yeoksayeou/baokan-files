Title: Liao Chengzhi Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-01-05
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, January 3rd: Liao Chengzhi, President of the China-Japan Friendship Association, met this morning with all members of the Japanese Fukushima Prefecture Trade Union Delegation to China, led by Kazuhisa Wada, and held friendly talks with them. Present at the meeting were Li Shubin, Wang Xiaoyun, and other responsible persons from relevant departments.
    Xinhua News Agency, January 3rd: Liao Chengzhi, President of the China-Japan Friendship Association, met this morning with all members of the Japanese Kawasaki City Workers' Delegation to China, led by Toshio Takahashi, and held friendly talks with them.
    Present at the meeting were Peng Siming, Xin Yi, and other responsible persons from relevant departments.

### 廖承志会长接见日本客人

1966-01-05
第4版()
专栏：

    廖承志会长接见日本客人
    新华社三日讯中日友协会长廖承志今天上午接见由和田敬久率领的日本福岛县工会访华代表团全体成员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有栗树彬、王晓云等有关方面负责人。
    新华社三日讯中日友协会长廖承志今天上午接见由高桥敏夫率领的日本川崎市工人访华团全体成员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有彭思明、辛毅等有关方面负责人。
