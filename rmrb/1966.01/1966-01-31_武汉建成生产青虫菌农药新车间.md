Title: Wuhan Builds New Workshop for Producing Bacillus Thuringiensis Pesticide

Author:
Date: 1966-01-31
Edition: 3

    Wuhan has recently put into production a newly built workshop for Bacillus Thuringiensis.
    Bacillus Thuringiensis is a type of bacterial pesticide produced artificially. Experiments have shown that Bacillus Thuringiensis has a relatively high killing power against more than ten kinds of pests that attack grains, cotton, vegetables, and forests, such as rice stem borers, corn borers, cotton bollworms, pine caterpillars, tea hairy caterpillars, stinging caterpillars, bagworms, and cabbage caterpillars. It is harmless to humans, livestock, and beneficial birds and insects.
    Bacillus Thuringiensis has low production costs, readily available raw materials, convenient storage and transportation, and is easy to use. It can be used alone or mixed with pesticides such as 666, 1605, and Dipterex. It can also be recycled. The dead insects poisoned by Bacillus Thuringiensis can be collected, mashed, and filtered, with one pound of dead insects mixed with 1,000 to 2,000 pounds of water, and its insecticidal efficiency is roughly the same as using new pesticide.
    "Controlling pests with bacteria" is a new science of the present era. Agricultural scientific researchers in Hubei Province worked alongside workers in the factory, thereby finding an ideal method for producing Bacillus Thuringiensis in large quantities.

### 武汉建成生产青虫菌农药新车间

1966-01-31
第3版()
专栏：新建设  新产品

    武汉建成生产青虫菌农药新车间
    武汉新建的青虫菌车间已在最近投入生产。
    青虫菌是人工制造的一种细菌农药。试验证明，青虫菌对稻苞虫、玉米螟、红铃虫、松毛虫、茶毛虫、刺蛾、避债虫，以及菜青虫等粮、棉、菜、林十几种害虫，都有较高的灭杀力。而对人、畜和益鸟益虫无害。
    青虫菌的生产成本低，原料来源容易，储存运输方便，而且使用简便。它既可以单独使用，也可以和六六六、一六○五、敌百虫等农药混用。它还可以回收使用。被青虫菌毒死的害虫被收集起来，经过捣烂过滤，每斤死虫兑水一二千斤，其杀虫效率和使用新药大体相同。
    “以菌治虫”是当代一门新科学。湖北省农业科学研究人员到工厂同工人一起值班，从而找到了大量生产青虫菌的理想方法。
