Title: Snowfall in Northeast China
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-14
Edition: 2

    Northeast China Snowfall
    Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, January 13th – In the first ten days of January, snowfall was widespread in Northeast China. Areas with heavier snowfall included the eastern part of Liaoning Province and the Shenyang area; in Jilin Province, the Siping, Tonghua, and Jilin areas saw precipitation of 8 to 12 millimeters; in Heilongjiang Province, the Songhua River, Mudanjiang, and other areas saw precipitation of 5 millimeters. Snowfall in other areas was generally lighter.
    Since last autumn, rainfall and snowfall have been scarce in many areas of Northeast China. The snowfall in the first ten days of this month is beneficial to the region's efforts to achieve increased agricultural production this year.

### 东北降雪

1966-01-14
第2版()
专栏：

    东北降雪
    新华社沈阳十三日电　一月上旬，东北地区普遍降雪。其中降雪较大的地区，为辽宁省的东部地区和沈阳地区；吉林省的四平、通化、吉林地区，降水量八毫米到十二毫米；黑龙江省的松花江、牡丹江等地区降水量五毫米。其它地区降雪一般较小。
    东北不少地区自去秋以来雨雪稀少，本月上旬的降雪，对这个地区争取今年农业增产有利。
