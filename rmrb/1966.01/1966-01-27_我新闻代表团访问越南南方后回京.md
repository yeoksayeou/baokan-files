Title: Our News Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-01-27
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, January 26th. The Chinese News Delegation, led by Feng Su, has returned to Beijing after visiting South Vietnam at the invitation of the South Vietnam Patriotic and Democratic Journalists Association.
    During its visit to South Vietnam, the Chinese News Delegation had extensive contact with leaders of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front, leading cadres at all levels, and the broad masses of soldiers and civilians in the liberated areas, and received their warm welcome.

### 我新闻代表团访问越南南方后回京

1966-01-27
第4版()
专栏：

    我新闻代表团访问越南南方后回京
    新华社二十六日讯　由冯素率领的中国新闻代表团，应越南南方爱国与民主新闻工作者协会的邀请，在越南南方进行访问以后，已回到北京。
    中国新闻代表团在越南南方访问期间，同越南南方民族解放阵线的负责人、各级领导干部和解放区的广大军民进行了广泛接触，并且受到了他们的热情欢迎。
