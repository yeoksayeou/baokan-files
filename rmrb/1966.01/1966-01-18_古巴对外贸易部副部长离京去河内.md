Title: Cuban Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Leaves Beijing for Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1966-01-18
Edition: 4

　　Cuban Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Leaves Beijing for Hanoi

　　Xinhua News Agency, January 17th: Cuban Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, Reguiera, and others left Beijing by plane today for Hanoi. They arrived in Beijing by train on January 13th from Pyongyang, en route from Korea to visit Vietnam.

　　When the guests arrived and departed from Beijing, Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Zhu Jianbai, Deputy Director, Yu Liguang, Commercial Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Cuba, and others were present at the station and airport to greet them. On the evening of the 13th, Lei Renmin hosted a banquet for the guests.

### 古巴对外贸易部副部长离京去河内

1966-01-18
第4版()
专栏：

    古巴对外贸易部副部长离京去河内
    新华社十七日讯　古巴对外贸易部副部长瑞格依拉等今天乘飞机离开北京前往河内。他们是由朝鲜前往越南访问途中，在一月十三日由平壤乘火车到达北京的。
    客人们在到达和离开北京时，前往车站和机场迎送的，有对外贸易部副部长雷任民、副局长朱剑白、中国驻古巴商务参赞于黎光等。十三日晚，雷任民曾设宴招待客人。
