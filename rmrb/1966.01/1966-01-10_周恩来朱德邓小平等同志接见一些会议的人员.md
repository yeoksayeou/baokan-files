Title: Comrades Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Deng Xiaoping, and others Receive Personnel from Various Conferences
Author:
Date: 1966-01-10
Edition: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Comrades Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Deng Xiaoping, Peng Zhen, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, Tan Zhenlin, Lu Dingyi, Kang Sheng, Bo Yibo, Ye Jianying, Xie Fuzhi, and other leaders, this afternoon received all personnel attending some conferences held in Beijing by the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Chinese People's Public Security Forces, the Ministry of Agriculture, the Ministry of Building Materials Industry, the Ministry of Construction Engineering, the Ministry of Chemical Industry, and the Ministry of Higher Education, as well as all personnel of the Avant-Garde Cultural Troupe of the Jinan Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army who came to Beijing to perform. (Picture attached)
　　Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Deng Xiaoping, Peng Zhen, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, Tan Zhenlin, Lu Dingyi, Kang Sheng, Bo Yibo, Ye Jianying, Xie Fuzhi and other leaders received all the personnel from some meetings held in Beijing by the Chinese People's Liberation Army and other departments, as well as all the personnel of the Jinan Military Region's Avant-Garde Cultural Troupe.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency Reporter Du Xiuxian Photography

### 周恩来朱德邓小平等同志接见一些会议的人员

1966-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来朱德邓小平等同志接见一些会议的人员
    新华社九日讯　周恩来、朱德、邓小平、彭真、李富春、李先念、谭震林、陆定一、康生、薄一波、叶剑英、谢富治等领导人，今天下午接见了出席中国人民解放军、中国人民公安部队、农业部、建筑材料工业部、建筑工程部、化学工业部、高等教育部在北京召开的一些会议的全体人员，以及来北京演出的中国人民解放军济南部队前卫文工团的全体人员。（附图片）
    周恩来、朱德、邓小平、彭真、李富春、李先念、谭震林、陆定一、康生、薄一波、叶剑英、谢富治等领导人，接见了中国人民解放军等部门在北京召开的一些会议的全体人员和济南部队前卫文工团的全体人员。
                              新华社记者　杜修贤摄
