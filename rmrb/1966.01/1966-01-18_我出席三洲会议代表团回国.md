Title: Our Delegation Attending the Tricontinental Conference Returns Home
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-18
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 16th – The Chinese delegation attending the Asian, African, and Latin American People's Solidarity Conference, led by head of delegation Wu Xueqian, left Havana by plane on the 16th to return home.
    The Tricontinental Conference was held here from January 3rd to January 15th. Some members of the delegation left Havana on the 15th to return home.

### 我出席三洲会议代表团回国

1966-01-18
第4版()
专栏：

    我出席三洲会议代表团回国
    新华社哈瓦那十六日电　出席亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲人民团结会议的中国代表团在团长吴学谦的率领下十六日乘飞机离哈瓦那回国。
    三洲会议是从一月三日到一月十五日在这里举行的。代表团部分团员已于十五日离开哈瓦那回国。
