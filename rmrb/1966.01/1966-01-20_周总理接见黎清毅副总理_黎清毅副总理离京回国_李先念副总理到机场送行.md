Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Deputy Prime Minister Le Thanh Nghi; Deputy Prime Minister Le Thanh Nghi Leaves Beijing for Home; Vice Premier Li Xiannian Sees Him Off at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1966-01-20
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, January 19th: Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai received Le Thanh Nghi, head of the Economic Delegation of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and Deputy Prime Minister, and Li Ban, deputy head of the delegation and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, on the 18th. They had a cordial and friendly conversation.  Also present at the meeting were Vice Premier Li Xiannian and responsible comrades from relevant departments, Fang Yi, Han Nianlong, and Li Qiang.
    Hoang Bac, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, was also present.
    Xinhua News Agency, January 19th: The Economic Delegation of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by Deputy Prime Minister Le Thanh Nghi, left Beijing by special plane on the 19th to return home. The delegation arrived in Beijing on the 18th en route home from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.
    Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian hosted a banquet on the evening of the 18th to entertain the Vietnamese comrades. Premier Zhou Enlai attended the banquet. Vice Premier Li Xiannian and Deputy Prime Minister Le Thanh Nghi delivered speeches at the banquet. They jointly wished for the continuous development of the militant friendship between the peoples of China and Vietnam. Also present at the banquet were responsible comrades from relevant departments, Fang Yi, Han Nianlong, Peng Shaohui, and Li Qiang. Hoang Bac, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, and Tran Van Thanh, head of the Permanent Delegation of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front to China, were invited to attend the banquet.
    When Deputy Prime Minister Le Thanh Nghi arrived in and departed from Beijing, Vice Premier Li Xiannian and responsible comrades from relevant departments were at the airport to greet and see him off. Charge d'Affaires ad interim Hoang Bac, Delegation Head Tran Van Thanh, and DPRK Ambassador to China Pak Se-chang also were at the airport to greet and see him off.

### 周总理接见黎清毅副总理  黎清毅副总理离京回国　李先念副总理到机场送行

1966-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见黎清毅副总理
    黎清毅副总理离京回国　李先念副总理到机场送行
    新华社十九日讯　国务院总理周恩来十八日接见了越南民主共和国政府经济代表团团长、政府副总理黎清毅，副团长、对外贸易部副部长李班，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有李先念副总理和有关方面负责人方毅、韩念龙、李强等。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办黄北也在座。
    新华社十九日讯　由黎清毅副总理率领的越南民主共和国政府经济代表团，十九日乘专机离开北京回国。代表团是十八日由朝鲜民主主义人民共和国回国途中到达北京的。
    国务院副总理李先念在十八日晚上设宴招待越南同志。周恩来总理出席了宴会。李先念副总理和黎清毅副总理在宴会上先后致词。他们共祝中越两国人民的战斗友谊不断发展。出席宴会作陪的，还有各有关方面的负责人方毅、韩念龙、彭绍辉、李强等。越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办黄北，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长陈文成，应邀出席了宴会。
    黎清毅副总理到达和离开北京时，李先念副总理以及各有关方面的负责人，到机场迎送。黄北临时代办、陈文成团长，以及朝鲜驻中国大使朴世昌，也到机场迎送。
