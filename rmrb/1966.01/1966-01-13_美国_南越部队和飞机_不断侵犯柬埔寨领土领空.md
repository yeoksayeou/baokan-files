Title: US-South Vietnamese Troops and Aircraft Constantly Violate Cambodian Territory and Airspace
Author:
Date: 1966-01-13
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, December 12th - According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on the 12th, on the evening of December 26th last year, US-South Vietnamese troops from the Dong Duc post in South Vietnam fired on Cambodian territory with automatic weapons and mortars. Bullets and six mortar shells flew over a Cambodian post in Kandal Province. The Cambodian garrison returned fire with light machine guns at the aforementioned South Vietnamese post, forcing the invaders to cease firing.
    Another message issued by the news agency on the 12th stated that US-South Vietnamese aircraft had intruded into Cambodian airspace in Kratie Province and Kandal Province eight times between December 27th last year and January 4th this year.
    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, December 10th - According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on the 10th, on the morning of December 31, 1965, US-South Vietnamese combat troops from the Cai Wen post fired nineteen mortar and artillery shells from South Vietnamese territory at the provincial defense forces post in Peam Montea, Prey Veng Province, Cambodia. The Cambodian provincial defense forces at the post responded forcefully with mortars.

### 美国—南越部队和飞机  不断侵犯柬埔寨领土领空

1966-01-13
第4版()
专栏：

    美国—南越部队和飞机
    不断侵犯柬埔寨领土领空
    新华社金边十二日电　据柬埔寨新闻社十二日报道，去年十二月二十六日晚上，南越同德哨所的美国—南越部队用自动武器和迫击炮向柬埔寨领土进行射击。子弹和六发炮弹飞过干丹省柬埔寨哨所的上空。柬埔寨哨所的守军用轻机枪向上述南越哨所进行了还击，迫使侵略者停止射击。
    这家新闻社十二日发的另一条消息说，美国—南越飞机从去年十二月二十七日到今年一月四日曾经八次侵入桔井省和干丹省的柬埔寨领空。
    据新华社金边十日电　据柬埔寨新闻社十日报道，一九六五年十二月三十一日上午，丐文哨所的美国—南越作战部队从南越境内向柬埔寨波萝勉省佩昂蒙特亚地方的省防军哨所发射了十九发迫击炮和大炮炮弹，哨所的柬埔寨省防军用迫击炮予以有力的回击。
