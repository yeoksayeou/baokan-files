Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Premier Zhou Enlai Send Telegrams to Congratulate the Tenth Anniversary of the Independence of the Republic of Sudan
Author:
Date: 1966-01-01
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, December 31st—Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram to Ismail al-Azhari, Chairman of the Supreme Council of State of the Republic of Sudan, on December 31st, congratulating the tenth anniversary of the independence of the Republic of Sudan. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Excellency Ismail al-Azhari, Chairman of the Supreme Council of State of the Republic of Sudan, Khartoum:
    On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of the independence of the Republic of Sudan, I extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of Sudan. I wish the Republic of Sudan prosperity and its people happiness. I wish the friendship between the people of China and Sudan will grow day by day.
                        Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                       December 31, 1965

    Xinhua News Agency, December 31st—Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram to Muhammad Ahmad Mahjoub, Prime Minister of the Republic of Sudan, on December 31st, congratulating the tenth anniversary of the independence of the Republic of Sudan. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Excellency Muhammad Ahmad Mahjoub, Prime Minister of the Republic of Sudan, Khartoum:
    On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of the independence of the Republic of Sudan, I extend my warm congratulations to the Government and people of Sudan. I wish the Government and people of Sudan new achievements in the cause of safeguarding national independence and building their own country. I wish the friendly relations between China and Sudan will be increasingly consolidated and developed.
                          Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                                    December 31, 1965

### 刘少奇主席和周恩来总理  电贺苏丹共和国独立十周年

1966-01-01
第3版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席和周恩来总理
    电贺苏丹共和国独立十周年
    新华社三十一日讯  中华人民共和国主席刘少奇三十一日打电报给苏丹共和国国家最高委员会主席伊斯梅尔·阿扎里，祝贺苏丹共和国独立十周年。电文如下：喀土穆苏丹共和国国家最高委员会主席伊斯梅尔·阿扎里阁下：
    在苏丹共和国独立十周年的时候，我向阁下和苏丹人民表示热烈的祝贺。祝苏丹共和国繁荣，人民幸福。祝中国和苏丹两国人民之间的友谊日益发展。
                        中华人民共和国主席刘少奇
                       一九六五年十二月三十一日
    新华社三十一日讯  中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来三十一日打电报给苏丹共和国总理穆罕默德·艾哈迈德·马哈古卜，祝贺苏丹共和国独立十周年。电文如下：喀土穆苏丹共和国总理穆罕默德·艾哈迈德·马哈古卜阁下：
    在苏丹共和国独立十周年的时候，我向苏丹政府和苏丹人民致以热烈的祝贺。祝苏丹政府和苏丹人民在维护民族独立和建设自己国家的事业中，取得新的成就。祝中国和苏丹两国的友好关系日益巩固和发展。
                          中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来
                                    一九六五年十二月三十一日
