Title: Rushing to Learn from Daqing
Author: Wei Chun
Date: 1966-01-22
Edition: 4

    Rushing to Learn from Daqing
    Worker of the First Tractor Factory Wei Chun
    Whistle blasts resoundingly—
    The bugle calls urgently;
    Machine tools, one after another—
    Whips crack, urging forward, flying with speed.
    Parts turn on the assembly line,
    A surge of enthusiasm rolls on the assembly line.
    Oh, tractor workers,
    A mighty army rushes to learn from Daqing!
    The Daqing spirit, the Daqing people,
    Self-reliance sets the example.
    Learn from Daqing's hard bones,
    No matter how great the difficulties, we dare to fight!
    With our hearts set on the motherland, we look at the world,
    Produce more high-quality "Dongfanghong."
    The whole factory learns the Daqing spirit,
    Holding high the red flag, never stopping!
    〔Selected from "Tractor News"〕



**Named Entities**


第一拖拉机厂	Diyi Tuolaji Chang (First Tractor Factory)
大庆	Daqing
东方红	Dongfanghong (East is Red, tractor brand)
拖拉机报	Tuolaji Bao (Tractor News)

### 赶大庆
魏淳
1966-01-22
第4版()
专栏：

    赶大庆
    第一拖拉机厂工人  魏淳
    汽笛声声——
    号角吹得紧；
    台台机床——
    扬鞭催马直飞腾。
    流水线上零件翻，
    装配线上热潮滚。
    拖拉机工人呵，
    千军万马赶大庆！
    大庆精神大庆人，
    自力更生树标兵。
    学习大庆硬骨头，
    困难再大咱敢拼！
    心怀祖国看世界，
    多造优质“东方红”。
    大庆精神全厂学，
    高擎红旗永不停！
    〔选自《拖拉机报》〕
