Title: Cambodian Border Guards Valiantly Repel Armed Invasion by Thailand; Prince Sihanouk Cables Vice Premier Chen Yi Thanking China for Support; Central Presidium of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front, etc. Support Cambodia's Firm Opposition to U.S. Imperialist Aggression
Author:
Date: 1966-01-04
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 3rd. Cambodian News Agency reported on the 3rd that, according to initial reliable reports, on the evening of January 2nd, about 30 Thai soldiers invaded Cambodian territory, attempting to launch an attack on the Chhne Sa province defense force post in Koh Kong Province, located approximately 800 meters from the border.

    However, when the enemy entered about 300 meters into Cambodian territory, they were met with resistance from the Cambodian outpost troops. The outpost troops opened fire on the enemy, immobilizing them. Later, the enemy, unable to withstand the attack, retreated into Thai territory.

    Cambodian News Agency also reported that on December 12th and 21st, 1965, several Thai aircraft violated Cambodian airspace over Battambang and Koh Kong Provinces on two separate occasions.

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 1st. According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on the 1st, between December 8th and 19th, 1965, American-South Vietnamese aircraft intruded into the airspace of Svay Rieng Province, Mondulkiri Province, Takeo Province, Kampot Province, and Prey Veng Province in Cambodia a total of 14 times.

    The news agency also reported on the 1st that on the morning of December 13th last year, South Vietnamese puppet troops stationed at the Lang Tao post in South Vietnam invaded the village of Bot Ma in Svay Rieng Province, Cambodia. On the same day, South Vietnamese puppet troops also shelled Cambodian territory near the Ben Bania Cambodian post in Takeo Province.

    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Chen Yi, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, received a cable from Cambodian Head of State Prince Norodom Sihanouk on the 1st, thanking the Chinese government and people for their support of Cambodia's decision to repel any encroachment on Cambodian territorial integrity by U.S. imperialism and its lackeys. The full text of the cable is as follows: To His Excellency Chen Yi, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

    Your Excellency's support on behalf of the Chinese government for our decision to repel any encroachment on Cambodian territorial integrity by U.S. imperialism and its lackeys greatly encourages us. On behalf of the Cambodian people, I extend my deepest gratitude to the government of the People's Republic of China and the great Chinese people.

    Cambodia's sole wish is to live peacefully on its own land in strict neutrality. However, if imperialism dares to expand the war into our territory, the Cambodian people will fight to defend their rights and freedom. The fraternal support of the People's Republic of China further strengthens our confidence in the final victory over the aggressors.

          Norodom Sihanouk
          Head of State of Cambodia
    Phnom Penh, January 1, 1966

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi. According to a report by the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency: The Presidium of the Central Committee of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front issued a statement condemning the U.S. government's reckless authorization of U.S. troops to invade Cambodian territory.

    The statement expressed full support for the statements issued by the Royal Government of Cambodia on December 25th and 26th, which refuted the slander of the U.S. State Department and declared that Cambodia is determined to resist any act of aggression against Cambodia by U.S. imperialists and their lackeys.

    The statement said that if the U.S. imperialists recklessly expand the war of aggression into Cambodia, the people of South Vietnam and their liberation armed forces will join forces with the Cambodian people and the Royal Cambodian Army to drive their common enemy out of their countries, in order to rebuild true peace in Indochina and Southeast Asia.

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 3rd. According to a report by the Vietnam News Agency, Tran Huu Duyet, Secretary General of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front, wrote to the Secretary General of the Cambodian People's Social Alliance on the 2nd, expressing warm support for the Cambodian people's just struggle against U.S. imperialism.

    The letter strongly condemned the recent statement by the U.S. government authorizing U.S. aggressor troops in South Vietnam to invade Cambodian territory at any time, and expressed full support for the resolution of the Cambodian People's Social Alliance Congress on this issue. The letter demanded that U.S. imperialism immediately cease all acts of aggression against Cambodia.

    The letter emphasized that all the Vietnamese people guarantee that in the anti-U.S. struggle to defend peace in Indochina and Southeast Asia, they will strengthen solidarity with the fraternal Cambodian people.

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana. The Albanian newspaper "People's Voice" published a commentary on the 31st, expressing full support for the solemn position of the Cambodian people and government to prepare to repel the military aggression of U.S. imperialism.

    The commentary said that the Albanian people and their government highly appreciate the Cambodian people's struggle and efforts for national progress and for true peace and security in Asia and the world, and resolutely condemn the hostile activities of U.S. imperialism against the freedom and independence of Cambodia. The Cambodian people's resolute response to the conspiracies of U.S. imperialism has received the full support of the Albanian people.

### 柬埔寨边防军奋勇击退泰国的武装入侵  西哈努克亲王致电陈毅副总理感谢中国支持  南越民族解放阵线中央主席团等支持柬埔寨坚决反对美帝侵略

1966-01-04
第3版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨边防军奋勇击退泰国的武装入侵
    西哈努克亲王致电陈毅副总理感谢中国支持
    南越民族解放阵线中央主席团等支持柬埔寨坚决反对美帝侵略
    新华社金边三日电　柬埔寨新闻社三日报道，据初步的可靠消息，一月二日晚上，泰国军队约三十人侵入柬埔寨领土，企图对柬埔寨戈公省沿海距边境约八百米地方的奇内萨省防军哨所发动进攻。
    但是敌人进入柬埔寨境内约三百米时，遭到了柬埔寨前哨部队的抗击。前哨部队向敌人开火，使他们动弹不得。后来，敌人抵挡不住，退到泰国境内。
    柬埔寨新闻社还报道，一九六五年十二月十二日和二十一日，泰国飞机数架先后两次在马德望和戈公省侵犯柬埔寨领空。
    新华社金边一日电　据柬埔寨新闻社一日报道：美国—南越飞机在一九六五年十二月八日到十九日共侵入柬埔寨柴桢省、蒙多基里省、茶胶省、唝吥省和波萝勉省的领空达十四次之多。
    这家新闻社一日还报道，去年十二月十三日上午，驻扎在南越朗道哨所的南越伪军侵入柬埔寨柴桢省的博特马村。同一天，南越伪军还炮轰了茶胶省本巴尼亚柬埔寨哨所附近的柬埔寨领土。
    新华社三日讯　中华人民共和国副总理兼外交部长陈毅收到柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王一日打给他的电报，感谢中国政府和人民对柬埔寨回击美帝国主义及其走狗对柬埔寨领土完整的任何侵犯的决定的支持。电报全文如下：北京中华人民共和国副总理兼外交
  部长陈毅阁下：
    阁下代表中国政府支持我们回击美帝国主义及其走狗对柬埔寨领土完整的任何侵犯的决定，使我们感到很大的鼓舞。我谨代表柬埔寨人民，向中华人民共和国政府和伟大的中国人民，致以深切的谢意。
      柬埔寨唯一的愿望，就是在严格中立的情况下在自己的土地上和平生活。但是，如果帝国主义胆敢把战争扩大到我国境内，柬埔寨人民将为保卫自己的权利和自由而战斗。中华人民共和国的兄弟般的支持，更加增强了我们最后战胜侵略者的信心。
          柬埔寨国家元首
          诺罗敦·西哈努克
  一九六六年一月一日于金边
    新华社河内电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团发表声明，谴责美国政府悍然授权美军入侵柬埔寨领土。
    声明表示完全支持柬埔寨王国政府十二月二十五日和二十六日发表的声明，这两个声明驳斥了美国国务院的诽谤，并且宣告：柬埔寨决定反击美帝国主义者及其走狗对柬埔寨的任何侵略行动。
    声明说，如果美帝国主义者肆无忌惮地把侵略战争扩大到柬埔寨，越南南方人民和他们的解放武装力量将同柬埔寨人民和王国军队协力把他们的共同敌人从自己的国家赶出去，以便在印度支那和东南亚重建真正的和平。
    新华社河内三日电　据越南通讯社报道，越南祖国战线中央委员会秘书长陈友悦二日写信给柬埔寨人民社会同盟总书记，表示热烈支持柬埔寨人民反对美帝国主义的正义斗争。
    信件强烈谴责美国政府最近授权在南越的美国侵略军可以随时入侵柬埔寨领土的声明，并表示完全支持柬埔寨人民社会同盟代表大会关于这个问题的决议。信件要求美帝国主义立即停止侵略柬埔寨的一切行动。
    信件强调说，全体越南人民保证在保卫印度支那和东南亚和平的反美斗争中，要同兄弟的柬埔寨人民加强团结。
    新华社地拉那电　阿尔巴尼亚《人民之声报》三十一日发表评论，表示完全支持柬埔寨人民和政府准备回击美帝国主义的军事侵略的严正立场。
    评论说，阿尔巴尼亚人民及其政府，高度评价柬埔寨人民为争取国家进步、争取亚洲和世界的真正和平和安全而进行的斗争与努力，坚决谴责美帝国主义反对柬埔寨的自由和独立的敌对活动。柬埔寨人民对美帝国主义的阴谋的坚决回答，得到了阿尔巴尼亚人民的完全支持。
