Title: General Ne Win Holds Reception to Celebrate 18th Anniversary of Burmese Independence
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-06
Edition: 4

    According to a New China News Agency report from Rangoon on the 4th, General Ne Win, Chairman of the Revolutionary Council of the Union of Burma, and his wife held a grand reception here on the evening of the 4th to celebrate the 18th anniversary of Burmese independence.

    Approximately two thousand people attended the reception. Among them were members of the Revolutionary Council, government ministers, and representatives from various sectors.

    Foreign diplomatic envoys, including Geng Biao, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, also attended the reception. The six-day Rangoon Independence Day celebration officially began on the evening of the 3rd.

### 奈温将军举行招待会庆祝缅甸独立十八周年

1966-01-06
第4版()
专栏：

    奈温将军举行招待会庆祝缅甸独立十八周年
    据新华社仰光四日电　缅甸联邦革命委员会主席奈温将军和夫人四日晚上在这里举行盛大招待会，庆祝缅甸独立十八周年。
    约有两千人出席了招待会。其中有革命委员会委员、政府部长和各界代表。
    包括中国驻缅甸大使耿飚在内的各国外交使节也出席了招待会。仰光庆祝独立日的六天活动是从三日晚上正式开始的。
