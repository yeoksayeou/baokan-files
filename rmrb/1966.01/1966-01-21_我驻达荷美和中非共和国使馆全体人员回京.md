Title: All Personnel from Our Embassies in Dahomey and Central African Republic Return to Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-21
Edition: 3

    All Personnel from Our Embassies in Dahomey and Central African Republic Return to Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, January 20th: Li Yunchuan, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Dahomey, and embassy staff, and Zhu Junyi, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Central African Republic, and embassy staff, returned to Beijing by plane today. Ambassador Li Yunchuan and Chargé d'affaires Zhu Junyi withdrew from Dahomey and the Central African Republic on the 5th and 8th of this month respectively.
    Also returning to Beijing from the Central African Republic at the same time were Chinese agricultural experts who were helping with construction in the Central African Republic, staff who held the Chinese Economic Construction Exhibition in the Central African Republic, and Xinhua News Agency reporters and staff stationed in the Central African Republic.
    Today, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Vice Chairman of the Foreign Economic Relations Commission Xie Huaide, other responsible persons from relevant departments Du Ganquan, Wang Yizhi, Liang Fengzhu, Tian Renjie, Zhao Gang, and Meng Ying, our ambassador to the Central African Republic, who had returned to Beijing earlier, and his wife, went to the Capital Airport to greet and express their sincere condolences to all personnel returning from Dahomey and the Central African Republic. Young women presented flowers to all personnel returning to Beijing.
    Joseph-Gilbert Mamadou, Ambassador of the Central African Republic to China, and embassy staff left Beijing for home on January 10th.

### 我驻达荷美和中非共和国使馆全体人员回京

1966-01-21
第3版()
专栏：

    我驻达荷美和中非共和国使馆全体人员回京
    新华社二十日讯  中华人民共和国驻达荷美大使李云川和使馆工作人员，中华人民共和国驻中非共和国大使馆临时代办朱君毅和使馆工作人员，今天乘飞机返抵北京。李云川大使和朱君毅临时代办是分别在本月五日和八日撤离达荷美和中非共和国的。
    同时从中非共和国回到北京的，还有帮助中非共和国建设的中国农业专家，在中非共和国举办中国经济建设展览会的工作人员，新华社驻中非共和国记者及工作人员。
    今天，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，对外经委副主任谢怀德，其他有关方面的负责人杜干全、王毅之、梁丰珠、田人杰、赵纲，以及先期回到北京的我国驻中非共和国大使孟英和夫人，前往首都机场迎接，向从达荷美和中非共和国返国的全体人员表示亲切慰问。女青年向回到北京的全体人员献了花。
    中非共和国驻中国大使约瑟夫—吉勒贝尔·马马杜和使馆工作人员，已于一月十日离开北京回国。
