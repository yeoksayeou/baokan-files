Title: Strengthening Combat Readiness and Raising Revolutionary Vigilance: Vietnamese Air Force and Air Defense Units Determined to Severely Punish Air Pirates

Author: 
Date: 1966-01-30
Edition: 3

    HANOI, January 28 (Xinhua) – The Vietnam News Agency reported that the Vietnamese People's Army Air Force and Air Defense Forces are extensively carrying out a competition movement to strengthen combat readiness and enhance revolutionary vigilance, in order to shoot down intruding American aircraft as soon as the guns fire. The movement is being carried out under the slogan: Determined to defeat the American aggressors, unafraid of sacrifice and difficulties, prepared to fight a long-term battle until final victory is achieved.
    A certain unit of the Air Defense Forces has launched a movement to promote military democracy, in order to fully utilize the wisdom of all gunners in training and formulating combat plans. All units of this force have reinforced their fortifications and combat positions, and strengthened vigilance.
    Personnel of various Air Force units are actively studying tactics, determined to shoot down intruding enemy aircraft under any circumstances. Many mechanics have launched a competition with pilots to ensure that aircraft are accident-free in training and combat.

### 加强战斗准备　提高革命警惕  越南空军和防空部队决心严惩飞贼

1966-01-30
第3版()
专栏：

    加强战斗准备　提高革命警惕
    越南空军和防空部队决心严惩飞贼
    新华社河内二十八日电  据越南通讯社报道，越南人民军空军部队和防空部队广泛开展加强战斗准备和提高革命警惕的竞赛运动，以便做到炮声一响就把来犯的美机打下来。这个运动是在这样一个口号下进行的：决心打败美国侵略者，不怕牺牲和困难，准备进行长期的战斗，直到取得最后胜利为止。
    防空部队某部开展了发扬军事民主的运动，以便在训练和制订战斗计划中充分发挥全体炮手的智慧。这个部队的所有单位都加固了他们的工事和战斗场地，并加强了警戒。
    各空军部队的人员都在积极研究战术，决心在任何情况下把来犯的敌机打下来。许多机械员同驾驶员开展了竞赛，保证飞机在训练和战斗中不出事故。
