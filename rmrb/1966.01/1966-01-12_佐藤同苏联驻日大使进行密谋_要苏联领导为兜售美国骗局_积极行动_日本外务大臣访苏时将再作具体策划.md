Title: Sato Conspires with Soviet Ambassador to Japan, Demanding Soviet Leaders "Actively Act" to Peddle American Deceit, Specific Plans to be Further Developed During Japanese Foreign Minister's Visit to the Soviet Union
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-12
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, January 11th: Tokyo News: Japanese Prime Minister Eisaku Sato, in a meeting with Soviet Ambassador to Japan, Vinogradov, on the 10th, demanded that the Soviet Union "take active actions" to serve the American "peace talks" deception.

    According to a Kyodo News report, Sato told Vinogradov that he "had fully understood the enthusiasm of the United States for seeking a peaceful solution to the (Vietnam) problem during his exchange of views with U.S. Special Envoy Harriman." He said, "It is hoped that the Soviet Union, as an important country with influence on this issue, must take active action. It is necessary to get the parties concerned in the dispute to sit down at the negotiating table in order to peacefully resolve the Vietnam problem." Sato asked the Soviet Ambassador to "convey his remarks to Premier Kosygin," and also said that Foreign Minister Shiina's upcoming visit to the Soviet Union will allow them to "fully consult with the Soviet government on the peaceful settlement of the Vietnam problem."

### 佐藤同苏联驻日大使进行密谋  要苏联领导为兜售美国骗局“积极行动”  日本外务大臣访苏时将再作具体策划

1966-01-12
第4版()
专栏：

    佐藤同苏联驻日大使进行密谋
    要苏联领导为兜售美国骗局“积极行动”
    日本外务大臣访苏时将再作具体策划
    新华社十一日讯　东京消息：日本政府首相佐藤荣作十日同苏联驻日大使维诺格拉多夫举行会谈时，要苏联“采取积极行动”为美国的“和谈”骗局效劳。
    据共同社报道，佐藤对维诺格拉多夫说，他在“同美国特使哈里曼交换意见时，已经充分了解了美国争取和平解决（越南）问题的热情。”他说，“希望苏联作为对这个问题有影响力的重要国家务必采取积极行动，有必要让争端有关人员坐到谈判桌子上来，以和平解决越南问题。”佐藤要求苏联大使把他的讲话“转告给柯西金总理”，他还说，不久椎名外务大臣访问苏联时，将“同苏联政府充分协商和平解决越南问题”。
