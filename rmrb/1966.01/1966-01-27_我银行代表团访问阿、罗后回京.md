Title: My Bank Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Albania and Romania
Author:
Date: 1966-01-27
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, January 23rd – The Chinese People's Bank delegation of four members, led by the Vice President of the Bank, Fang Gao, returned to Beijing by plane today after concluding their visit to Albania and Romania.


**Named Entities**

中国人民银行	Zhongguo Renmin Yinhang (People's Bank of China)
方皋		Fang Gao
阿尔巴尼亚	Albaniya (Albania)
罗马尼亚	Luomaniya (Romania)
北京		Beijing
新华社	Xinhua News Agency

### 我银行代表团访问阿、罗后回京

1966-01-27
第5版()
专栏：

    我银行代表团访问阿、罗后回京
    新华社二十三日讯　中国人民银行副行长方皋率领的银行代表团一行四人，结束了在阿尔巴尼亚、罗马尼亚的访问后，今天乘飞机回到北京。
