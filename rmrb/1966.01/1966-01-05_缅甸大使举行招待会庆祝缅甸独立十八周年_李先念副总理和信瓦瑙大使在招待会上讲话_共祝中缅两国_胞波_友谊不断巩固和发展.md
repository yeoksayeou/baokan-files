Title: Burmese Ambassador Holds Reception Celebrating the 18th Anniversary of Burmese Independence; Vice Premier Li Xiannian and Ambassador Hsin Wa Nau Speak at the Reception, Jointly Wishing the Continuous Consolidation and Development of the "Pauk-Phaw" Friendship Between China and Burma

Author:
Date: 1966-01-05
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, January 4th. Burmese Ambassador to China, Sama Duwa Sinwa Nau, held a reception at the Beijing Hotel this evening to celebrate the 18th anniversary of the independence of the Union of Burma.

    Vice Premier Li Xiannian, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Shen Yanbing and Xu Deheng, and Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Fu Zuoyi, were invited to attend the reception.

    Ambassador Sinwa Nau and Vice Premier Li Xiannian spoke at the reception successively. They wished that the "Pauk-Phaw" friendship between the people of China and Burma would be continuously consolidated and developed.

    In his speech, Ambassador Sinwa Nau spoke about Burma's neutral policy and the achievements Burma has made in various fields. He said that the people of Burma firmly believe that only through great efforts based on a policy of self-reliance can the Union of Burma achieve modernization and attain a prominent position in all aspects.

    The Ambassador praised the friendly relations between Burma and China and their peoples. He pointed out that the mutual visits of Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Chairman Ne Win, as well as other leaders of Burma and China, have further expanded and developed the friendship and cooperation between Burma and China in various fields, and further strengthened the kinship-like "Pauk-Phaw" friendship between the two peoples.

    The Ambassador said that the governments of Burma and China, through the conclusion of a treaty of friendship and mutual non-aggression, and the issuance of various declarations and joint statements regarding the treaty and the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, have expressed the lofty ideals of relations between countries and have achieved many outstanding and mutually beneficial results.

    Ambassador Sinwa Nau concluded by saying that General Ne Win, Chairman of the Revolutionary Council of the Union of Burma, and the Revolutionary Government of the Union of Burma, warmly wish the Government and people of the People's Republic of China continued and even greater and more prominent achievements in the future under the outstanding and firm leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong.

    In his speech, Vice Premier Li Xiannian praised the Burmese people for their persistent struggle against new and old colonialism and for the consolidation of national independence over the past eighteen years. He said that under the leadership of Chairman Ne Win, the Union of Burma has made remarkable achievements in opposing imperialist interference and subversion, defending national sovereignty and independence, eradicating the remaining forces of colonialism, and developing the national economy and culture. In international affairs, the Burmese government has adhered to a policy of peace and neutrality and has made positive contributions to promoting Asian-African solidarity and safeguarding world peace.

    Vice Premier Li Xiannian said that the new year will be a year in which the people of Asian countries will achieve greater victories in their anti-imperialist revolutionary struggles. The Chinese people are determined to unite more closely with the people of all Asian countries, and in supporting the great cause of the Vietnamese people in resisting American aggression and saving the nation, and in the common struggle against US imperialism, safeguarding national independence, and defending Asian and world peace, to advance towards new victories.

    Vice Premier Li Xiannian said that China and Burma are close and friendly neighbors, and our two countries have consistently adhered to the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and the Ten Principles of the Bandung Conference, living in harmony and treating each other as equals. Frequent mutual visits between the leaders of the two countries have continuously added splendor to the "Pauk-Phaw" friendship between the peoples of China and Burma.

    In his speech, Vice Premier Li Xiannian also expressed, on behalf of the Chinese government and the Chinese people, sincere gratitude to the Burmese government and people for their consistent support of the just struggle of the Chinese government and people to recover their own territory of Taiwan, and for opposing the imperialist conspiracy to create "two Chinas." The Chinese people cherish the friendship between China and Burma and will continue to work tirelessly to enhance this friendship.

    Among those invited to attend the reception were responsible persons from our Ministry of Foreign Affairs and other departments, responsible persons from the Chinese People's Liberation Army, people's organizations, democratic parties, and Beijing Municipality. Among them were Zeng Shan, Zhang Xiruo, Fang Yi, Han Nianlong, Peng Shaohui, Wang Xinting, Fu Zhong, Lei Renmin, Le Songsheng, Jian Bozan, etc. Diplomatic envoys from various countries to China were also invited to attend the reception.

### 缅甸大使举行招待会庆祝缅甸独立十八周年  李先念副总理和信瓦瑙大使在招待会上讲话  共祝中缅两国“胞波”友谊不断巩固和发展

1966-01-05
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸大使举行招待会庆祝缅甸独立十八周年
    李先念副总理和信瓦瑙大使在招待会上讲话
    共祝中缅两国“胞波”友谊不断巩固和发展
    新华社四日讯缅甸驻中国大使沙马杜瓦·信瓦瑙今天晚上在北京饭店举行招待会，庆祝缅甸联邦独立十八周年。
    李先念副总理，郭沫若副委员长，政协全国委员会副主席沈雁冰、许德珩，国防委员会副主席傅作义等，应邀出席了招待会。
    信瓦瑙大使和李先念副总理在招待会上先后讲了话。他们祝中缅两国人民的“胞波”友谊不断地巩固和发展下去。
    信瓦瑙大使在讲话中谈到了缅甸的中立政策以及缅甸在各方面取得的成就。他说，缅甸人民坚决认为只有通过基于自力更生政策所作出的巨大努力，缅甸联邦才能取得现代化的进展和在各方面获得显著的地位。
    大使赞扬了缅中两国和两国人民之间的友好关系。他指出，刘少奇主席和奈温主席以及缅中两国其他领导人的互相访问，进一步扩大和发展了缅甸和中国之间的友好和各方面的合作，进一步加强了两国人民亲戚般的“胞波”友谊。
    大使说，缅甸和中国政府通过缔结友好和互不侵犯条约，发表有关该条约和和平共处五项原则的各项宣言和联合声明，已经表达了国与国之间关系的崇高理想，并取得了许多突出的互利的成果。
    信瓦瑙大使最后说，缅甸联邦革命委员会主席奈温将军和缅甸联邦革命政府热烈地祝中华人民共和国政府和人民在毛泽东主席的卓越和坚定的领导下，今后继续取得比以往更为伟大和突出的成就。
    李先念副总理在讲话中赞扬缅甸人民十八年来为反对新老殖民主义和巩固民族独立进行了坚持不懈的斗争。他说，缅甸联邦在奈温主席的领导下，在反对帝国主义的干涉和颠覆、捍卫国家主权和独立方面，在铲除殖民主义残余势力、发展民族经济和文化方面，都取得了显著的成就。在国际事务中，缅甸政府坚持奉行和平中立政策，为促进亚非团结和维护世界和平，作出了积极的贡献。
    李先念副总理说，新的一年将是亚洲各国人民反帝革命斗争取得更大胜利的一年。中国人民决心同亚洲各国人民更加紧密地团结在一起，在支援越南人民抗美救国的伟大事业中，在反对美帝国主义、维护民族独立、保卫亚洲和世界和平的共同斗争中，向新的胜利前进。
    李先念副总理说，中缅两国是亲密的友好邻邦，我们两国一贯遵循和平共处五项原则和万隆会议十项原则，和睦相处，平等相待。两国领导人频繁的相互访问，不断为中缅两国人民的“胞波”情谊增添光辉。
    李先念副总理在讲话中还代表中国政府和中国人民，衷心感谢缅甸政府和人民一贯支持中国政府和人民收复自己领土台湾的正义斗争，反对帝国主义制造“两个中国”的阴谋。中国人民十分珍视中缅友谊，并将继续为增进这一友谊而不断努力。
    应邀出席招待会的，还有我国外交部和其他各部门的负责人，中国人民解放军、人民团体、民主党派和北京市的负责人。他们当中有曾山、张奚若、方毅、韩念龙、彭绍辉、王新亭、傅钟、雷任民、乐松生、翦伯赞等。各国驻中国的使节也应邀出席了招待会。
