Title: Chairman Liu Appoints Ambassadors to Syria, Iraq, and Sudan
Author:
Date: 1966-01-05
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, January 4th: Chairman of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi has appointed Chen Tan as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Arab Republic of Syria.
    Chairman of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi has removed Xu Yixin from his post as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Arab Republic of Syria.
    Xinhua News Agency, January 4th: Chairman of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi has appointed Cao Chi as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Iraq.
    Chairman of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi has removed Zhang Weilie from his post as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Iraq.
    Xinhua News Agency, January 4th: Chairman of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi has appointed Yu Peiwen as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Sudan.
    Chairman of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi has removed Gu Xiaobo from his post as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Sudan.

### 刘主席任命我驻叙利亚伊拉克苏丹大使

1966-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席任命我驻叙利亚伊拉克苏丹大使
    新华社四日讯中华人民共和国主席刘少奇任命陈坦为中华人民共和国驻阿拉伯叙利亚共和国特命全权大使。
    中华人民共和国主席刘少奇免去徐以新的中华人民共和国驻阿拉伯叙利亚共和国特命全权大使职务。
    新华社四日讯中华人民共和国主席刘少奇任命曹痴为中华人民共和国驻伊拉克共和国特命全权大使。
    中华人民共和国主席刘少奇免去张伟烈的中华人民共和国驻伊拉克共和国特命全权大使职务。
    新华社四日讯中华人民共和国主席刘少奇任命俞沛文为中华人民共和国驻苏丹共和国特命全权大使。
    中华人民共和国主席刘少奇免去谷小波的中华人民共和国驻苏丹共和国特命全权大使职务。
