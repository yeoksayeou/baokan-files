Title: Swedish Newspapers Don't Believe the U.S. Has Any Sincerity in "Peace Talks" - Johnson Talks Peace Today, Expands War Tomorrow
Author: Miao Di
Date: 1966-01-06
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, January 4th. The Swedish newspaper *Dagens Nyheter* published an editorial on January 3rd titled "Peace, Threats, and Poison Gas," exposing that while the United States is playing the "peace talks" trick on the Vietnam issue, it is further expanding its war of aggression. The editorial said that the U.S.'s hope to end its war in Vietnam and achieve peace is false and hypocritical. While shouting to resolve the Vietnam issue through peace talks, it also clamors to expand the war. Therefore, there is reason to doubt whether the U.S. is truly sincere in hoping for peace talks.
    The editorial also said that U.S. troops used poison gas to kill South Vietnamese guerrillas during the U.S.'s "peace offensive." "Using poison gas for military purposes is so disgusting that people cannot believe that the U.S. would conduct negotiations in this way."
    The editorial concluded, "The U.S. has made the world doubt its desire for peace, because it shouts peace today, and tomorrow it clamors to expand the war. People will certainly not believe that the U.S. sincerely hopes for peace in Vietnam." (Attached picture)

### 瑞典报纸不相信美国有什么“和谈”诚意  约翰逊今天高谈和平明天扩大战争

1966-01-06
第5版()
专栏：

    瑞典报纸不相信美国有什么“和谈”诚意
    约翰逊今天高谈和平明天扩大战争
    新华社斯德哥尔摩四日电　瑞典《每日新闻报》三日在一篇题为《和平、威胁及毒气》的社论中，揭露美国在越南问题上玩弄“和谈”的同时，正在进一步扩大侵略战争。社论说，美国希望结束它在越南的战争和实现越南和平是虚假的、伪善的。它一面高呼用和谈解决越南问题，一面又扬言要扩大战争。因此，人们有理由怀疑美国是否真心诚意希望和谈。
    社论又说，美国军队在美国的“和平攻势”期间是用毒气杀害南越游击队的。“把毒气用于军事目的是如此令人作呕，人们不能相信美国竟会以这种方式来进行谈判。”
    社论最后说，“美国已经使全世界怀疑它的和平愿望，因为它今天高呼和平，而明天又扬言要扩大战争。人们必然不会相信美国真心诚意地希望越南和平。”（附图片）
