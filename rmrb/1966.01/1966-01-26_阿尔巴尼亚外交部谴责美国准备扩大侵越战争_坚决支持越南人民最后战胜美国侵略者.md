Title: Albanian Foreign Ministry Condemns U.S. Preparation to Expand War of Aggression Against Vietnam, Resolutely Supports Vietnamese People's Final Victory Over U.S. Aggressors
Author:
Date: 1966-01-26
Edition: 3

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, January 23rd—The Albanian People's Republic Foreign Ministry issued a statement on January 22nd, strongly condemning and protesting the barbaric crimes of the U.S. imperialists in expanding their war of aggression in Vietnam.
    The statement said that the U.S. imperialist aggressors, following the slogan of "kill all, burn all, and destroy all," are implementing a criminal "scorched earth" policy in South Vietnam. To this end, they are not only conducting bombings and dropping incendiary bombs, but also using the most barbaric means prohibited by international agreements, such as poison gas and chemical agents, to destroy life in vast areas of South Vietnam. These heinous crimes of U.S. imperialism are even more ferocious and cruel than those of the Hitler Nazis.
    The statement pointed out that in the face of these facts, no one believes Johnson's deceptive statements, appeals, and proposals about resolving the Vietnam issue through "peaceful means." In his "State of the Union" address, he said, "We must stay there," "stay until the aggression stops." How hypocritical and shameless his words are, because everyone knows that the aggressor in Vietnam is none other than U.S. imperialism itself, and it is the U.S. imperialists who are continuing to massacre the Vietnamese people, desperately burning and destroying everything.
    The statement said that the Albanian People's Republic Foreign Ministry most strongly condemns these double-dealing criminal and deceptive activities of the U.S. imperialists and their servants, who intend to expand U.S. aggression in Vietnam and throughout Southeast Asia. The Albanian people and the government of the Albanian People's Republic support the just cause of the heroic Vietnamese people, and fully support the January 5, 1966 statement of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front and the January 11, 1966 statement of the Foreign Ministry of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, both of which exposed and condemned the new crimes committed by the U.S. aggressors in implementing the "scorched earth" policy in Vietnam and their insidious conspiracies to make people believe in their "desire for peace talks."
    The statement pointed out that the new U.S. military adventurism is doomed to failure in the face of the determination of the people of Southeast Asian countries to defeat U.S. aggression, just as the glorious South Vietnamese people, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and the entire Vietnamese people, united as one with the strength of iron, have victoriously repelled U.S. aggression.
    The statement said that the Vietnamese people, in their heroic and patriotic struggle, have adopted an unwavering stance against imperialist aggression; they are not isolated, and they have friends all over the world. The Albanian people have always regarded the struggle of the fraternal Vietnamese people as their own struggle, and will forever stand by the heroic Vietnamese people until the Vietnamese people finally defeat the U.S. imperialist aggressors. The statement concluded by saying that the Albanian People's Republic Foreign Ministry, in expressing the Albanian people's love and deep respect for the fraternal Vietnamese people, once again expresses its firm belief that final victory will surely belong to the heroic Vietnamese people. The U.S. imperialists will inevitably suffer complete defeat and be driven out of Vietnam.

### 阿尔巴尼亚外交部谴责美国准备扩大侵越战争  坚决支持越南人民最后战胜美国侵略者

1966-01-26
第3版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚外交部谴责美国准备扩大侵越战争
    坚决支持越南人民最后战胜美国侵略者
    据新华社地拉那二十三日电　阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国外交部二十二日发表声明，强烈谴责和抗议美帝国主义者在越南扩大侵略战争的野蛮罪行。
    声明说，美帝国主义侵略者根据“杀光、烧光和毁光”的口号，在南越实行罪恶的“焦土”政策。为此，他们不仅进行轰炸和投掷燃烧弹，而且使用毒气和化学毒剂等国际协定所禁止的最野蛮的手段来毁灭南越广大地区的生命。美帝国主义的这些滔天罪行，其凶恶残酷甚至超过了希特勒纳粹分子。
    声明指出，在这种种事实面前，谁也不相信约翰逊关于通过“和平途径”解决越南问题的骗人的声明、呼吁和建议。他在“国情咨文”中说：“我们一定要留下来”，“留到侵略停止为止”。他的言谈是多么虚伪无耻，因为大家知道，在越南的侵略者正是美帝国主义自己，正是美帝国主义者在继续屠杀越南人民，拼命烧光和毁光。
    声明说，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国外交部最强烈地谴责美帝国主义者及其仆从的这种两面三刀的罪恶和欺骗活动，他们打算扩大美国在越南和整个东南亚的侵略。阿尔巴尼亚人民和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府声援英雄的越南人民的正义事业，完全支持越南南方民族解放阵线一九六六年一月五日的声明和越南民主共和国外交部一九六六年一月十一日的声明，这两项声明揭露和谴责了美国侵略者在越南实行“焦土”政策所犯下的新罪行以及他们为了使人相信他们的“和谈愿望”而策划的恶毒阴谋。
    声明指出，在东南亚各国人民要打败美国侵略的决心面前，美国的新的军事冒险政策是注定要遭到失败的，正如光荣的越南南方人民、越南民主共和国、钢铁般地团结一致的全体越南人民胜利地击退美国的侵略一样。
    声明说，越南人民在英勇的爱国斗争中对帝国主义的侵略采取了坚定不移的立场，他们不是孤立的，他们的朋友遍天下。阿尔巴尼亚人民始终把兄弟的越南人民的斗争看作是自己的斗争，并将永远站在英雄的越南人民一边，直到越南人民最后战胜美帝国主义侵略者。声明最后说，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国外交部在表达阿尔巴尼亚人民对兄弟的越南人民的热爱和深深尊敬的同时，再一次表示深信，最后胜利必将属于英雄的越南人民。美帝国主义者必将遭到彻底的失败和被赶出越南。
