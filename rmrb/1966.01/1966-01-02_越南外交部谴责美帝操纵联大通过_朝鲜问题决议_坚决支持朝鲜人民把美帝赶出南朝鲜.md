Title: The Vietnamese Foreign Ministry Condemns U.S. Imperialism for Manipulating the UN General Assembly to Pass the "Korean Question Resolution" and Firmly Supports the Korean People in Driving U.S. Imperialism out of South Korea

Author:

Date: 1966-01-02
Edition: 3

    According to a report by the New China News Agency in Hanoi on the 31st, the spokesperson of the Foreign Ministry of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam issued a statement on the 31st, strongly condemning the 20th session of the United Nations General Assembly for illegally passing the so-called "Korean Question Resolution." The statement pointed out: "This 'resolution' is purely a deceptive trick designed to justify the U.S. occupation of South Korea."

    The statement said, "The Vietnamese people and the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam fully support the correct position of the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea as elucidated in the statement of the spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea on December 23, 1965. The government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam resolutely rejects the so-called 'Korean Question Resolution' passed by the United Nations General Assembly under the manipulation of the United States, and considers this 'resolution' to be invalid."

    The statement said, "The Vietnamese people fully sympathize with and unreservedly support the fraternal Korean people's just struggle to drive U.S. imperialism out of South Korea and peacefully reunify their motherland."

### 越南外交部谴责美帝操纵联大通过“朝鲜问题决议”  坚决支持朝鲜人民把美帝赶出南朝鲜

1966-01-02
第3版()
专栏：

    越南外交部谴责美帝操纵联大通过“朝鲜问题决议”
    坚决支持朝鲜人民把美帝赶出南朝鲜
    据新华社河内三十一日电　据越南通讯社报道，越南民主共和国外交部发言人三十一日发表声明，强烈谴责第二十届联合国大会非法通过所谓“朝鲜问题决议”。声明指出：“这个‘决议’纯粹是一种骗人的手法，目的是为了给美国霸占南朝鲜进行辩解。”
    声明说，“越南人民和越南民主共和国政府完全支持朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务省发言人一九六五年十二月二十三日声明中阐明的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府的正确立场。越南民主共和国政府断然拒绝联合国大会在美国的操纵下通过的所谓‘朝鲜问题决议’，并认为这个‘决议’是无效的。”
    声明说，“越南人民完全同情和毫无保留地支持兄弟的朝鲜人民把美帝国主义赶出南朝鲜以及和平统一祖国的正义斗争。”
