Title: Thai Authorities Ready to Invade Cambodia at Any Time
Author:
Date: 1966-01-29
Edition: 5

    According to a dispatch from Xinhua on the 27th, Bangkok: While intensifying armed provocations against Cambodia, the Thai authorities are strengthening military deployments on the Thai-Cambodian border in preparation for further aggression against Cambodia.
    It is reported that after the Thai Cabinet held a meeting on the 25th to listen to a report by Thai Navy Deputy Commander-in-Chief Charoon and others on the border situation in Trat Province, which is close to Cambodia, it ordered the three armed forces to strengthen their vigilance and be ready to mobilize at any time.

### 泰国当局准备随时入侵柬埔寨

1966-01-29
第5版()
专栏：

    泰国当局准备随时入侵柬埔寨
    据新华社二十七日讯　曼谷消息：泰国当局在加紧对柬埔寨进行武装挑衅的同时，正在加强泰—柬边境的军事部署，为进一步侵略柬埔寨进行准备。
    据报道，泰国内阁在二十五日召开会议听取泰国海军副总司令乍仑等人关于接近柬埔寨的达叻府的边境形势报告后，命令三军加强戒备，随时准备出动。
