Title: Prince Sihanouk Cables the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association, Thanking Them for Their Support of Cambodia's Anti-American Struggle
Author:
Date: 1966-01-16
Edition: 5

    Prince Sihanouk Cables the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association
    Thanking Them for Their Support of Cambodia's Anti-American Struggle
    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, January 14th (via Cambodian News Agency, January 14th) - Cambodian Head of State Prince Sihanouk sent a cable to the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association, thanking them for their support of Cambodia's anti-American struggle.
    The cable said: "We are deeply gratified by your telegram expressing support and solidarity with our resolute struggle against American imperialism and its Bangkok and Saigon lackeys. We are particularly pleased that the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association has exposed Thailand, a tool of the American warmongers—whose leaders have long pursued a policy of war and territorial annexation against its neighbors."
    Prince Sihanouk said: "I warmly wish the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association new achievements in its excellent work of better exposing the true face of American imperialism and conveying the truth of the fierce struggles of the Indochinese peoples against aggression to our Asian, African, and Latin American brothers and sisters."

### 西哈努克亲王复电亚非记协书记处  感谢对柬埔寨反美斗争的支持

1966-01-16
第5版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王复电亚非记协书记处
    感谢对柬埔寨反美斗争的支持
    新华社金边十四日电  据柬埔寨新闻社十四日报道：柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王打电报给亚非新闻工作者协会书记处，感谢对柬埔寨反美斗争的支持。
    电报说：“你们对我们反对美帝国主义及其曼谷和西贡走狗的坚决斗争表示支持和声援的电报，使我们深感欣慰。亚非新闻工作者协会揭露了美国好战分子的工具泰国——它的领导人对邻国长期以来奉行战争政策和吞并领土的政策，对此我们特别感到高兴。”
    西哈努克亲王说：“我热烈祝愿亚非新闻工作者协会在更好地揭露美帝国主义的真象，和把印度支那各国人民反对侵略的激烈斗争的真象告诉给亚洲、非洲和拉丁美洲兄弟人民的出色的工作中取得新的成就。”
