Title: Urgent Telegram from the Liaison Delegation of the Vietnam People's Army to the International Commission: Strongly Protesting US Imperialism's Reinforcements and Continued Violation of Vietnamese Airspace
Author:
Date: 1966-01-07
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi – The Liaison Delegation of the General Command of the Vietnam People's Army sent an urgent telegram on December 30th of last year to the International Commission in Vietnam, strongly protesting the US government's dispatch on December 29th of the Third Brigade of the US 25th Infantry Division, consisting of 4,000 troops, to the Pleiku region of South Vietnam.
    The telegram pointed out that this is an action by the US government to intensify the war in South Vietnam. It once again exposes the bellicose nature of US imperialism and proves that the United States is plotting to intensify and expand the war of aggression in South Vietnam.
    The Liaison Delegation of the General Command of the Vietnam People's Army also sent a telegram on December 30th to the International Commission in Vietnam, strongly protesting the continued violation of the airspace of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on December 29th by US aircraft, which conducted reconnaissance and provocative activities over many areas in the provinces of Quang Binh, Thai Binh, Thanh Hoa, Nghe An, and Quang Ninh.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 1st – The Liaison Delegation of the General Command of the Vietnam People's Army sent an urgent telegram on January 1st to the International Commission in Vietnam, strongly protesting and denouncing the continued violation of the airspace of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on January 1st by US aircraft, which conducted reconnaissance and provocative activities over some areas in the provinces of Ha Bac, Nam Ha, Thanh Hoa, Nghe An, Ha Tinh, and Quang Binh.

### 越南人民军联络代表团急电国际委员会  强烈抗议美帝增兵并继续侵犯越南领空

1966-01-07
第3版()
专栏：

    越南人民军联络代表团急电国际委员会
    强烈抗议美帝增兵并继续侵犯越南领空
    新华社河内电　越南人民军总司令部联络代表团去年十二月三十日打紧急电报给越南国际委员会，强烈抗议美国政府十二月二十九日增派美国第二十五步兵师第三旅四千人到越南南方波来古地区。
    电报指出，这是美国政府在南越进行的强化战争的行动。它再一次暴露了美帝国主义的好战本质，证明了美国正在阴谋强化和扩大越南南方的侵略战争。
    越南人民军总司令部联络代表团十二月三十日还打电报给越南国际委员会，强烈抗议美国飞机十二月二十九日继续侵犯越南民主共和国的广平、太平、清化、义安和广宁等省许多地方，进行侦察和挑衅活动。
    新华社河内一日电　越南人民军总司令部联络代表团一日打紧急电报给越南国际委员会，强烈抗议和控诉美国飞机一日继续侵犯越南民主共和国领空，在河北、南河、清化、义安、河静、广平等省一些地区进行侦察和挑衅活动。
