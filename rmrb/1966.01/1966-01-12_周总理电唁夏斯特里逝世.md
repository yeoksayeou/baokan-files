Title: Premier Zhou Sends Condolences on Shastri's Death
Author: Zhou Enlai
Date: 1966-01-12
Edition: 5

    Premier Zhou Sends Condolences on Shastri's Death
    Xinhua News Agency, January 11th. Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to Indian President Radhakrishnan on January 11th, expressing condolences on the death of Indian Prime Minister Shastri. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Excellency Dr. Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan, President of the Republic of India, New Delhi:
    I was shocked to learn of the unfortunate passing of His Excellency Mr. Lal Bahadur Shastri, Prime Minister of the Republic of India. Please accept my condolences.
                      Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                                    January 11, 1966

### 周总理电唁夏斯特里逝世
周恩来
1966-01-12
第5版()
专栏：

    周总理电唁夏斯特里逝世
    新华社十一日讯　周恩来总理十一日打电报给印度总统拉达克里希南，吊唁印度总理夏斯特里逝世。电文如下：新德里印度共和国总统萨瓦帕利·拉达克里希南博士阁下：
    惊悉印度共和国总理拉尔·巴哈杜尔·夏斯特里先生阁下不幸逝世，谨致吊唁。
                      中华人民共和国国务院总理  周恩来
                                    一九六六年一月十一日
