Title: Jilin Completes a Pair of Modernized Mines

Author:
Date: 1966-01-09
Edition: 2

    Jilin Completes a Pair of Modernized Mines
    The newly built pair of modernized mines of the Tonghua Mining Bureau of Jilin Province have recently been officially put into production.
    From coal mining to transportation, the entire production process of these mines is mechanized, with an annual coal production capacity of 450,000 tons.

**Named Entities**

吉林省  Jilin Province
通化矿务局    Tonghua Mining Bureau

### 吉林建成一对现代化矿井

1966-01-09
第2版()
专栏：新建设  新产品

    吉林建成一对现代化矿井
    吉林省通化矿务局新建的一对现代化矿井最近正式投入生产。
    这对矿井从采煤到运输，全部生产过程都是机械化的，每年能产煤四十五万吨。
