Title: New Dutch Chargé d'Affaires Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-01-22
Edition: 3

    New Dutch Chargé d'Affaires Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, January 21st—The new Dutch Chargé d'Affaires to China, Mr. Jan Krens, arrived in Beijing by train this morning. Li Tianmin, a specialist from the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, greeted him at the station.

### 荷兰新任驻华代办到京

1966-01-22
第3版()
专栏：

    荷兰新任驻华代办到京
    新华社二十一日讯  荷兰新任驻华代办扬克仁今天上午乘火车到达北京。外交部礼宾司专员李天民到车站迎接。
