Title: Lao People's Liberation Army Supreme Command Protests US Imperialism and Right-Wing Clique's Attack on Liberated Areas; Calls on Patriotic Military and Civilians to Resolutely Crush the Enemy's Dry Season Offensive; South Vietnam National Liberation Front Condemns US Imperialism for Using B-52 Aircraft to Bomb Lao Liberated Areas
Author:
Date: 1966-01-04
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Kang Kai Telegram, According to a Pathet Lao Radio broadcast on December 30th of last year: A spokesperson for the Lao People's Liberation Army Supreme Command issued a statement on December 29th, strongly protesting the war actions of US imperialism and the Lao right-wing clique in concentrating more than twenty battalions of troops to attack the liberated areas of Khammouane Province and Savannakhet Province.

    The statement said that in order to implement the dry season offensive plan, US imperialism and the Lao right-wing clique recently concentrated fourteen battalions of troops along Route 9 in Savannakhet Province and seven battalions of troops in Thakhek in Khammouane Province, conducting "sweeps" along Route 9, the Bangfai River, and Route 12, launching large-scale offensives against the liberated areas.

    The statement pointed out that after suffering a disastrous defeat in the offensive against the Khammouane Province liberated areas in November, US imperialism, the Thai reactionaries, and the Lao right wing colluded, transporting two battalions of Thai troops into Thakhek, and concentrating a large number of American and Thai troops along the Thai-Lao border. US imperialism has intensified the dispatch of aircraft to bomb highways such as Routes 9, 7, and 6. US imperialism is also fabricating rumors on a large scale to prepare conditions for expanding its war of aggression in Laos. The statement pointed out that these actions by US imperialism and the right-wing clique show that they are deliberately creating a large-scale war in Laos.

    The statement warned US imperialism and its running dogs that they must immediately cease this reckless action, and called on the Lao patriotic military and civilians to be vigilant, ready to fight at any time, and resolutely smash the US imperialism and its running dogs' dry season offensive plan.

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 3rd Electric report. According to the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency, the Foreign Liaison Committee of the Central Committee of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front issued a statement on December 28th of last year, condemning US imperialism for using B-52 strategic bombers to bomb the liberated areas of Laos.

    The statement said, "As neighbors of the Lao people, the South Vietnamese people will never stand idly by in the face of the actions of the American aggressors trampling on Lao territory. They guarantee to stand closely with the fraternal Lao people, go all out to fight the American aggressors, until complete victory is achieved."

    The statement pointed out that the Johnson administration's use of B-52 aircraft to bomb the Lao liberated areas is yet another new step in its war of aggression in Laos. The United States is doing this to strengthen and further expand the war of aggression in Laos, in order to escape from the predicament it faces in South Vietnam. The United States, in collusion with the Lao right-wing forces, is stepping up the intrusion and encroachment on the liberated areas of the Lao patriotic forces in central and lower Laos, attempting to control this area and connect this area with the American battlefield in South Vietnam and the bases in Thailand. The statement strongly protested the United States' intensified bombing of central and lower Laos, the dispatch of more American expeditionary forces to Laos, and the direct command of the Lao puppet army to slaughter the Lao people. The statement demanded that the American ruling clique immediately stop this barbaric action.

### 老挝解放军最高指挥部抗议美帝和右派集团进攻解放区  号召爱国军民坚决粉碎敌人旱季进攻  南越民族解放阵线谴责美帝用B—52型飞机轰炸老挝解放区

1966-01-04
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝解放军最高指挥部抗议美帝和右派集团进攻解放区
    号召爱国军民坚决粉碎敌人旱季进攻
    南越民族解放阵线谴责美帝用B—52型飞机轰炸老挝解放区
    新华社康开电　据巴特寮电台去年十二月三十日广播：老挝人民解放军最高指挥部发言人十二月二十九日发表声明，强烈抗议美帝国主义和老挝右派集团集中二十多个营的军队进攻甘蒙省和沙湾拿吉省解放区的战争行动。
    声明说，为了实现旱季进攻计划，美帝国主义和老挝右派集团最近在沙湾拿吉省沿第九号公路集中了十四个营的兵力，在甘蒙省的他曲集中了七个营的兵力，沿第九号公路、邦费河和第十二号公路一带进行“扫荡”，对解放区发动大规模进攻。
    声明指出，在十一月间发动的对甘蒙省解放区的进攻遭到惨败后，美帝国主义、泰国反动派和老挝右派勾结一起，把两个营的泰国军队运进他曲，并且在泰国—老挝边界一带集中了大批美国和泰国的军队。美帝国主义加紧派遣飞机轰炸第九、第七和第六号等公路。美帝国主义还大肆制造谣言，为它扩大侵略老挝战争准备条件。声明指出，美帝国主义和右派集团的这些行动表明，它们正在蓄意在老挝制造大规模战争。
    声明警告美帝国主义和它的走狗必须立即停止这种冒险行动，并且号召老挝爱国军民提高警惕，随时准备战斗，坚决粉碎美帝国主义和它的走狗的旱季进攻计划。
    新华社河内三日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会对外联络委员会去年十二月二十八日发表一项声明，谴责美帝国主义用B—52型战略轰炸机轰炸老挝解放区。
    声明说，“作为老挝人民的邻居的越南南方人民对美国侵略者践踏老挝国土的行动决不袖手旁观。他们保证同兄弟的老挝人民紧密地站在一起，全力以赴地同美国侵略者斗争，直到取得完全胜利。”
    这项声明指出，约翰逊政府用B—52型飞机轰炸老挝解放区，是它在老挝进行侵略战争的又一个新的步骤。美国这样作是为了强化和进一步扩大侵略老挝的战争，为了摆脱它在越南南方所处的困境。美国正伙同老挝右派力量在加紧侵袭和蚕食老挝爱国力量在中、下寮的解放区，妄图控制这个地区，并把这个地区同美国在越南南方的战场和在泰国的基地连成一片。声明强烈抗议美国加紧轰炸中、下寮，派遣更多的美国远征军去老挝，并直接指挥老挝仆从军屠杀老挝人民。声明要美国统治集团立即停止这种野蛮行动。
