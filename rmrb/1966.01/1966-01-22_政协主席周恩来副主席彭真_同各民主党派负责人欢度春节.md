Title: CPPCC Chairman Zhou Enlai and Vice Chairman Peng Zhen Celebrate Spring Festival with Leaders of Democratic Parties
Author:
Date: 1966-01-22
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, January 21st. Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee and Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC, and Peng Zhen, Member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC, celebrated the Spring Festival together this morning with leaders of various democratic parties and non-party democratic人士 at a Spring Festival gathering held by the National Committee of the CPPCC.
    The gathering was held in the third-floor hall of the CPPCC Auditorium, which was filled with a joyful atmosphere. When Chairman Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman Peng Zhen, and other Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the CPPCC, Xu Bing, Gao Chongmin, Deng Zihui, Fu Zuoyi, Xie Juezai, Shen Yanbing, Xu Deheng, Li Dequan, entered the hall, warm applause broke out throughout the venue. Chairman Zhou Enlai and others warmly shook hands with everyone and extended festive greetings.
    Vice Chairman Peng Zhen spoke at the gathering. He congratulated the people of our country on the great achievements they had made in the past year in the socialist revolution and socialist construction; he wished everyone could unite more closely in the new year, study Chairman Mao's works diligently, study Marxism-Leninism, and while transforming society, also transform our thinking, so that we could forge ahead courageously in the development of the situation.
    Also attending the gathering were members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the CPPCC, members of the Standing Committee of the NPC, leaders of various democratic parties and non-party democratic人士, and leaders of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce.
    At the gathering, literary and art workers from the capital performed excerpts from Peking Opera, Henan Opera, and musical performances. (Attached picture)

### 政协主席周恩来副主席彭真  同各民主党派负责人欢度春节

1966-01-22
第1版()
专栏：

    政协主席周恩来副主席彭真  同各民主党派负责人欢度春节
    新华社二十一日讯  中共中央副主席、政协全国委员会主席周恩来，中共中央政治局委员、政协全国委员会副主席彭真，今天上午在政协全国委员会举行的春节联欢会上，同各民主党派负责人和无党派民主人士一起欢度春节。
    联欢会在政协礼堂三楼大厅举行，大厅里洋溢着欢乐的气氛。当周恩来主席，彭真副主席和其他政协全国委员会副主席徐冰、高崇民、邓子恢、傅作义、谢觉哉、沈雁冰、许德珩、李德全等进入大厅时，全场响起了热烈的掌声。周恩来主席等同大家亲切握手，致以节日的祝贺。
    彭真副主席在联欢会上讲话。他祝贺我国人民在去年一年里，在社会主义革命和社会主义建设中所取得的巨大成就；祝愿大家在新的一年中，更加紧密地团结起来，努力学习毛主席的著作，学习马克思列宁主义，在改造社会中，同时改造我们的思想，让我们在形势发展中奋勇前进。
    参加联欢会的，还有政协全国委员会常委，人大常委，各民主党派负责人和无党派民主人士，全国工商联负责人等。
    在联欢会上，首都文艺工作者演出了京剧、豫剧清唱和音乐歌舞等精采的节目。（附图片）
    中共中央副主席、政协全国委员会主席周恩来同大家一起欢度春节。
