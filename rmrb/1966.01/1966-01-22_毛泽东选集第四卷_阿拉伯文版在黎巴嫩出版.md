Title: Selected Works of Mao Zedong, Volume Four, Published in Arabic in Lebanon
Author:
Date: 1966-01-22
Edition: 3

    Selected Works of Mao Zedong, Volume Four
    Arabic Edition Published in Lebanon
    Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, 20th—The Arabic edition of Volume Four of the Selected Works of Mao Zedong has been published in Lebanon.
    The Arabic edition of Volume Four of the Selected Works of Mao Zedong was published in early January 1966 by the Lebanese "Sina Publishing House." After publication, the local people competed to buy it. The Arabic edition of Volume One of the Selected Works of Mao Zedong has been reprinted and distributed in Syria.

### 毛泽东选集第四卷  阿拉伯文版在黎巴嫩出版

1966-01-22
第3版()
专栏：

    毛泽东选集第四卷
    阿拉伯文版在黎巴嫩出版
    新华社二十日讯  贝鲁特消息：毛泽东选集第四卷阿拉伯文版已在黎巴嫩出版。
    毛泽东选集第四卷阿拉伯文版是在一九六六年一月上旬，由黎巴嫩“西纳出版社”出版的，出版后，当地人民争相购买。毛泽东选集第一卷阿拉伯文版已在叙利亚再版发行。
