Title: Premier Zhou Cables Congratulations to Bitar on Assuming Syrian Premiership
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-11
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 10th January. Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, sent a cable on January 8 to the Prime Minister of the Arab Syrian Republic, Salah al-Din Bitar, congratulating him on assuming the premiership. The text of the cable is as follows:
    To His Excellency Salah al-Din Bitar, Prime Minister of the Arab Syrian Republic, Damascus:
        On the occasion of Your Excellency's assumption of the Premiership of the Arab Syrian Republic, please accept my sincere congratulations. I wish the Syrian people new achievements in the cause of opposing imperialism and old and new colonialism, safeguarding national independence and building their own country. I wish the friendly relations between China and Syria will be consolidated and developed.
        Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                            January 8, 1966

### 周总理电贺比塔尔就任叙总理

1966-01-11
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电贺比塔尔就任叙总理
    新华社十日讯  中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来八日打电报给阿拉伯叙利亚共和国总理萨拉赫丁·比塔尔，祝贺他就任总理。电文如下：大马士革阿拉伯叙利亚共和国总理萨拉赫丁·比塔尔阁
  下：
    在阁下就任阿拉伯叙利亚共和国总理的时候，请接受我诚挚的祝贺。祝叙利亚人民在反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义、维护民族独立和建设自己国家的事业中取得新的成就。祝中叙两国之间的友好关系巩固和发展。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来
                    一九六六年一月八日
