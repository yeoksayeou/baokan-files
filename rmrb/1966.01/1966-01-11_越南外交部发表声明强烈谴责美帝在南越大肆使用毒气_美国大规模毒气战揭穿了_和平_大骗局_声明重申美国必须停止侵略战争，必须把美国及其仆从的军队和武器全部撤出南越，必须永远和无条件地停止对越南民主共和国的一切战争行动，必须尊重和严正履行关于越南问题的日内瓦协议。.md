Title: Vietnamese Foreign Ministry Issues Statement Strongly Condemning U.S. Imperialism's Rampant Use of Poison Gas in South Vietnam; U.S.'s Massive Chemical Warfare Exposes the "Peace" Hoax; Statement Reaffirms that the U.S. Must Stop Its War of Aggression, Must Withdraw All U.S. and Puppet Troops and Weapons from South Vietnam, Must Forever and Unconditionally Cease All Acts of War Against the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Must Respect and Strictly Implement the Geneva Accords on Vietnam.
Author:
Date: 1966-01-11
Edition: 3

	Hanoi, January 9 (Xinhua) - The Spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam issued a statement on the evening of the 9th, strongly condemning the monstrous crime of U.S. imperialism in conducting a large-scale chemical warfare in South Vietnam. The full text of the statement is as follows:

	The U.S. imperialists, in a vain attempt to salvage their failure and predicament in the war of aggression in South Vietnam, resort to any cruel means and any barbaric crimes. The U.S. aggressors, regardless of the severe condemnation of world public opinion, are massively using chemical poisons and gases in South Vietnam as weapons of war to carry out massacres.

	During the "sweep" on January 2, 1966, U.S. aggressors used helicopters to indiscriminately drop grenades with poison gas on the Bao Trai area of Long An Province, more than 30 kilometers from Saigon. Following this, paratroopers of the 173rd Airborne Brigade sprayed poison gas into the entire area using grenade launchers. The U.S. aggressors are also experimenting with the use of "Mighty Mite" sprayers, a machine that uses air pressure to spray 285 kilometers per hour and can spread airflow over a wide area, to spray powdered CS gas. As everyone remembers, in the past few years, U.S. imperialism has used high concentrations and amounts of 2,4-D dichlorophenoxyacetic acid, 2’·4·5—T trichlorophenoxyacetic acid, D.N.C. dinitrocresol and other chemical agents, causing serious poisoning and damage to the people, livestock, plants, and crops of South Vietnam.

	From the beginning of 1965 to the present, U.S. aggressors have secretly transported thousands of tons of new chemical poisons into South Vietnam, which have dangerous damaging effects, including CH, DM, CS and other models. CH is phenyl chloroacetophenone, a substance that causes suffocation, sneezing, and tearing, and is a war gas that was used in World War I. British troops once called DM the intensely irritating and suffocating Adamsite gas, a gas that killed hundreds of thousands of people in World War I. CS is a compound that causes tearing, suffocation, and irritates the human respiratory tract, causing difficulty in breathing and dizziness. The U.S. military began equipping themselves with this gas in 1960. U.S. aggressors have widely used this gas in South Vietnam, spraying high concentrations of poison into villages and bomb shelters, causing thousands of innocent people to be poisoned, vomit, suffocate, and suffer from pneumonia, and many people have died. On September 5 of last year alone, U.S. troops used poison gas in Yongdang Village, Binh Dinh Province, causing 35 people to die tragically and 19 to be severely poisoned, including 26 women and 28 children under the age of ten.

	There is no doubt that the poisons and gases currently being indiscriminately used by the U.S. in South Vietnam are the most barbaric and inhumane chemical weapons, condemned by humanity and strictly prohibited by international conventions.

	The U.S. aggressors are deliberately repeating the evil path of the past Hitler fascists, committing extremely barbaric crimes that progressive humanity cannot forgive.

	While the U.S. authorities are carrying out a large-scale "peace" deceptive offensive, they are blatantly and massively using poison gas to kill innocent people in South Vietnam. Obviously, this action exposes the hypocritical face of the U.S. aggressors.

	The use of poison gas as a weapon of war by U.S. imperialism in South Vietnam is a blatant trampling on the 1954 Geneva Accords on Vietnam and the 1922 and 1925 international conventions prohibiting the use of asphyxiating, poisonous, or other similar agents in war.

	The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam strongly accuses world public opinion of the above-mentioned monstrous crimes of U.S. imperialism. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam calls on the people of the world who love peace, freedom, and uphold justice, including the American people, to further take vigorous action to promptly stop the bloody claws of the U.S. aggressors and severely condemn the extremely inhumane war methods of the U.S. government.

	The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam firmly demands that the U.S. government must immediately stop using poison gas and poisons as weapons of war in South Vietnam. The U.S. government must stop its war of aggression, must withdraw all U.S. and puppet troops and weapons from South Vietnam, must forever and unconditionally cease all acts of war against the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and must respect and strictly implement the 1954 Geneva Accords on Vietnam.

	Hanoi, January 8 (Xinhua) - According to a report by the Vietnam News Agency, the Liaison Delegation of the General Command of the Vietnam People's Army wrote to the International Commission for Vietnam on the 8th, condemning the recent barbaric "sweeps" launched by the U.S. and its puppet troops against Bao Trai and other places in the Xin'an-Di'an area of South Vietnam.

	The letter said that the U.S. 173rd Airborne Brigade and the South Korean, Australian, and New Zealand accomplice troops implemented a "burn all, destroy all, and kill all" policy during the "sweep," using the most barbaric methods, including the use of napalm bombs, phosphorus incendiary bombs, chemical agents, and various poison gases to massacre civilians.

### 越南外交部发表声明强烈谴责美帝在南越大肆使用毒气  美国大规模毒气战揭穿了“和平”大骗局  声明重申美国必须停止侵略战争，必须把美国及其仆从的军队和武器全部撤出南越，必须永远和无条件地停止对越南民主共和国的一切战争行动，必须尊重和严正履行关于越南问题的日内瓦协议。

1966-01-11
第3版()
专栏：

    越南外交部发表声明强烈谴责美帝在南越大肆使用毒气
    美国大规模毒气战揭穿了“和平”大骗局
    声明重申美国必须停止侵略战争，必须把美国及其仆从的军队和武器全部撤出南越，必须永远和无条件地停止对越南民主共和国的一切战争行动，必须尊重和严正履行关于越南问题的日内瓦协议。
    新华社河内九日电  越南民主共和国外交部发言人九日晚上发表声明，强烈谴责美帝国主义在越南南方进行大规模毒气战争的滔天罪行。声明全文如下：
    美帝国主义妄图挽救它在越南南方的侵略战争的失败和困境，不择任何残暴手段，不择任何野蛮罪行。美国侵略者竟不顾世界舆论的严厉谴责，在越南南方大规模使用化学毒药和毒气作为进行成批地屠杀人的战争武器。
    在一九六六年一月二日的“扫荡”中，美国侵略者用直升飞机对距离西贡三十多公里的隆安省保寨地区滥肆投下带有毒气的手榴弹，接着，第一七三空降旅的伞兵又用榴弹枪向上述整个地区空间喷射毒气。美国侵略者还正在试验使用“迈蒂迈特”喷射机，这是用气压每小时喷射达二百八十五公里和能够散发广大面积的气流的一种机器，以喷射粉状的CS毒气。谁都记得，在过去的几年中，美帝国主义已经使用了高浓度和分量的2·4—D二氯苯氧乙酸、2’·4·5—T三氯苯氧乙酸、D．N．C．二硝基甲酚等化学药品，使越南南方的人民和家畜、植物、庄稼严重中毒和遭到损害。
    从一九六五年年初到现在，美国侵略者已经偷偷地把成千吨新的化学毒药运进越南南方，这些毒药具有危险的损害作用，其中有CH、DM、CS等型号，CH质是使人窒息、喷嚏、流眼泪的苯氯乙酮物质，是第一次世界大战中曾经使用过的一种战争毒气。英国军队曾经把DM称为剧烈刺激和窒息的亚当氏毒气，是在第一次世界大战中屠杀成十万人的一种毒气。CS质是使人流眼泪、窒息，并刺激人体呼吸管道、使人呼吸困难和头晕的一种化合物，是美国军队从一九六○年开始装备的；美国侵略者在越南南方广泛地使用了这种毒气，把高浓度的毒剂喷撒到乡村、防空洞，使数以千计的无辜人民中毒、呕吐、窒息、患胸炎，许多人死亡。仅去年九月五日美军在平定省永党乡使用一次毒气，就使三十五人惨死和十九人严重中毒，其中包括二十六名妇女和二十八名十岁以下的儿童。
    毫无疑问，目前美国在越南南方滥肆使用的毒药和毒气，是受到人类谴责和国际公约严禁的最野蛮、最无人道的化学武器。
    美国侵略者正在蓄意重蹈过去希特勒法西斯的罪恶道路，犯下了进步人类所不能饶恕的极端野蛮的罪行。
    当美国当局正在大搞“和平”欺骗攻势的时候，它们却在南越明目张胆地大肆使用毒气杀害无辜人民。很显然，这种行动揭穿了美国侵略者假仁假义的面目。
    美帝国主义在越南南方使用毒气作为战争工具，是明目张胆地践踏了一九五四年关于越南问题的日内瓦协议和一九二二年及一九二五年关于在战争中禁止使用窒息气、毒气、毒药以及各种类似毒剂的国际公约。
    越南民主共和国外交部向世界舆论强烈控诉美帝国主义的上述滔天罪行。越南民主共和国外交部呼吁世界爱好和平、自由和主持公理的人民，包括美国人民，进一步大力行动起来，及时制止美国侵略者的血腥魔爪，严厉谴责美国政府的极其无人道的战争手段。
    越南民主共和国外交部坚决要求美国政府必须立即停止在越南南方使用毒气和毒药作为战争工具。美国政府必须停止侵略战争，必须把美国及其仆从的军队、武器全部撤出越南南方，必须永远和无条件地停止对越南民主共和国的一切战争行动，必须尊重和严正履行一九五四年关于越南问题的日内瓦协议。
    据新华社河内八日电据越南通讯社报道：越南人民军总司令部联络代表团八日写信给越南国际委员会，谴责美国及其仆从军最近对越南南方新安—堤岸地区的保寨等地发动野蛮“扫荡”。
    信中说，美国第一七三空降旅以及南朝鲜、澳大利亚和新西兰帮凶军在进行“扫荡”时，实行了“烧光、毁光和杀光”政策，用最野蛮的手段，包括使用凝固汽油弹、磷燃烧弹、化学毒剂和各种毒气屠杀平民。
