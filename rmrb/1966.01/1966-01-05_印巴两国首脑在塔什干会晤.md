Title: Indo-Pakistani Leaders Meet in Tashkent
Author:
Date: 1966-01-05
Edition: 4

    New China News Agency, Tashkent, January 4th—According to a TASS report, Pakistani President Ayub Khan and Indian Prime Minister Shastri began their meeting in Tashkent, the capital of the Soviet Uzbekistan Republic, on the afternoon of the 4th. Soviet Council of Ministers Chairman Kosygin attended the meeting.
    The Indo-Pakistani leaders arrived in Tashkent on the 3rd.

### 印巴两国首脑在塔什干会晤

1966-01-05
第4版()
专栏：

    印巴两国首脑在塔什干会晤
    新华社四日讯塔什干消息，据塔斯社报道，巴基斯坦总统阿尤布·汗和印度总理夏斯特里四日下午开始在苏联乌兹别克共和国首都塔什干举行会晤。苏联部长会议主席柯西金出席了会晤。
    印巴两国首脑是在三日先后到达塔什干的。
