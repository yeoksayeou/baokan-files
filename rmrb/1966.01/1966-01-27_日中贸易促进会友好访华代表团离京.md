Title: Japan-China Trade Promotion Association Friendly Delegation Leaves Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-27
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, January 24th – The 18-member friendly delegation of the Japan-China Trade Promotion Association, led by Chairman Kazuo Kawase and Secretary-General Masanori Sumi, left Beijing by plane today to visit Hangzhou and other places.

    Present at the airport to see them off were Zhang Huadong, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and responsible persons from relevant departments.

    Also at the airport to see them off were Kazumi Kimura, Managing Director of the Kansai Headquarters of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, and Japanese economic and trade personnel in Beijing.

### 日中贸易促进会友好访华代表团离京

1966-01-27
第5版()
专栏：

    日中贸易促进会友好访华代表团离京
    新华社二十四日讯　日中贸易促进会友好访华代表团一行十八人，在团长川濑一贯、秘书长隅井正典率领下，今日乘飞机离京去杭州等地参观访问。
    到机场欢送的有：中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席张化东及有关方面负责人。
    到机场欢送的还有日本国际贸易促进协会关西本部专务理事木村一三及在京日本经济贸易界人士。
