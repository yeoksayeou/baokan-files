Title: Heads of Chinese and Vietnamese Economic and Cultural Delegations to Laos Visit Phoumi Vongvichit, Warmly Congratulating the 10th Anniversary of the Lao Patriotic Front

Author:
Date: 1966-01-08
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Kang Kai, 7th -- Zhu Ying, head of the Chinese Economic and Cultural Delegation to Laos, and Vu Van Thanh, head of the Economic and Cultural Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to Laos, paid a visit to Nouhak Phoumsavan, representative of the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front, on the morning of the 6th, warmly congratulating the 10th anniversary of the Lao Patriotic Front.
    During the visit, the heads of the Chinese and Vietnamese delegations successively congratulated the Lao Patriotic Front on leading the Lao people to achieve great victories in the anti-American patriotic struggle.

### 中越驻老挝经济文化代表团负责人拜会冯沙万  热烈祝贺老挝爱国战线党成立十周年

1966-01-08
第4版()
专栏：

    中越驻老挝经济文化代表团负责人拜会冯沙万
    热烈祝贺老挝爱国战线党成立十周年
    新华社康开七日电　中国驻老挝经济文化代表团负责人朱英和越南民主共和国驻老挝经济文化代表团负责人武文清六日上午拜会老挝爱国战线党中央代表诺哈·冯沙万，热烈祝贺老挝爱国战线党成立十周年。
    拜会时，中国和越南代表团的负责人先后祝贺老挝爱国战线党领导老挝人民进行反美爱国斗争所取得的巨大胜利。
