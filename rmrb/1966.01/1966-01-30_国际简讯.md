　　International Briefs
　　The United States Gives Yugoslavia Another Batch of Wheat
　　Belgrade Dispatch: Yugoslavia recently signed a new agreement with the United States to beg for 1.35 million tons of wheat from the United States. This batch of wheat, including freight, is worth a total of $86.4 million. According to the agreement signed by Yugoslavia and the United States in Belgrade on January 21, the United States decided to increase the amount of wheat it promised to supply to Yugoslavia last November from 700,000 tons to 1.35 million tons.
　　The Park Chung-hee Clique Forces University Students to Receive Military Training
　　Seoul Dispatch: The South Korean Park Chung-hee clique, in order to adapt to the needs of the United States' expanding war of aggression against Vietnam, is forcing South Korean university students to receive military training.
　　It is reported that the South Korean puppet Ministry of National Defense and puppet Ministry of Education have decided to conduct military training for all university students starting this semester. The puppet Ministry of National Defense has prepared to transfer 300 active-duty officers to serve as instructors.
　　New Australian Cabinet Announced and Sworn In
　　Canberra Dispatch: New Australian Prime Minister Harold Holt was formally sworn in on the 26th.
　　In the new cabinet, John McEwen serves as Deputy Prime Minister and Minister for Trade and Industry. Other key cabinet members include: Minister for Foreign Affairs Paul Hasluck, Minister for Defence Allen Fairhall, and Treasurer William McMahon.

### 国际简讯

1966-01-30
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    美国又给南斯拉夫一批小麦
    贝尔格莱德消息：南斯拉夫最近同美国签订了一项向美国乞求到一百三十五万吨小麦的新协定。这批小麦加运费在内共值八千六百四十万美元。根据南斯拉夫和美国一月二十一日在贝尔格莱德签订的这个协定，美国决定把去年十一月答应供给南斯拉夫小麦的数量从七十万吨增加到一百三十五万吨。
    朴正熙集团强迫大学生受训
    汉城消息：南朝鲜朴正熙集团为了适应美国扩大侵略越南战争的需要，正在强迫南朝鲜的大学生接受军训。
    据报道，南朝鲜伪国防部和伪文教部已经决定，自本学期起对所有大学生进行军训。伪国防部已准备抽调三百名现役军官担任教官。
    澳大利亚新内阁已宣布就职
    堪培拉消息：澳大利亚新总理霍尔特二十六日正式宣布就职。
    在新的内阁中，约翰·麦克尤恩任副总理兼贸易和工业部长。其他主要内阁成员有：外交部长保罗·哈斯勒克，国防部长艾伦·费尔霍尔，财政部长威廉·麦克马洪。
