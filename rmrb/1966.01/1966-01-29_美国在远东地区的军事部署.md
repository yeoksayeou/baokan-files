Title: U.S. Military Deployments in the Far East
Author:
Date: 1966-01-29
Edition: 4

    Editor's Note: U.S. imperialism is gradually shifting the focus of its global strategy from Europe to Asia, with China as its main enemy. While stepping up preparations for further expanding the war of aggression against Vietnam, the Johnson administration is also strengthening its military deployments and concentrating its forces throughout Asia, attempting to complete the so-called "crescent encirclement" of our country. Recently, some American military and political leaders have openly clamored to expand the war to China. We need to take this war threat from U.S. imperialism seriously.
    The Chinese people have never harbored any illusions about U.S. imperialism. We have seen through the aggressive nature of U.S. imperialism and also seen through the paper tiger essence of its outward strength. No matter how many troops, planes, warships, and missiles it dispatches to encircle China, it can never strengthen its strategic position. Today, the revolutionary movements of the Asian people are flourishing, and the aggressive positions of U.S. imperialism are being attacked everywhere. As Chairman Mao pointed out long ago, "All U.S. military bases abroad are nooses around the neck of U.S. imperialism." It is not others, but U.S. imperialism itself that is trapped in the encirclement of the people of Asia and the world. If U.S. imperialism wants to wage a full-scale war of aggression in Asia, it can only accelerate its own demise.
    Vice Premier Chen Yi said a few months ago, "We are ready for everything to oppose U.S. aggression. If U.S. imperialism is determined to impose a war of aggression on us, then welcome them to come sooner, welcome them to come tomorrow." The 650 million Chinese people will take all necessary measures to defeat the U.S. aggressors and make their due contribution to overthrowing U.S. imperialism, the contemporary instigator of aggression and war.

### 美国在远东地区的军事部署

1966-01-29
第4版()
专栏：

    编者按：美帝国主义把全球战略的重点，正在从欧洲逐渐转移到亚洲，把中国作为美国的主要敌人。约翰逊政府在加紧准备进一步扩大侵略越南战争的同时，也在整个亚洲加强军事部署，集结兵力，企图完成对我国的所谓“新月形包围圈”。最近，美国的一些军政头目纷纷公开叫嚷要把战争扩大到中国。对于美帝国主义这种战争威胁，是需要我们认真对待的。
    中国人民对美帝国主义从来不存任何幻想。我们看透了美帝国主义的侵略本性，同时也看透了美帝国主义纸老虎的外强中干的实质。不管它调派多少军队、飞机、军舰、导弹来包围中国，也决不能加强它的战略地位。今天，亚洲人民的革命运动蓬勃高涨，美帝国主义的侵略阵地到处受到冲击。正如毛主席早就指出的“所有美国在外国的军事基地，都是套在美帝国主义脖子上的绞索。”不是别人，而正是美帝国主义陷在亚洲和全世界人民的重重包围之中。美帝国主义要在亚洲进行一场全面的侵略战争，只能加速它的灭亡。
    陈毅副总理在几个月前就曾经说过，“为了反对美国侵略，我们一切都准备好了。如果美帝国主义决心要把侵略战争强加于我们，那就欢迎他们早点来，欢迎他们明天就来。”六亿五千万中国人民将采取一切必要的手段打败美国侵略者，为打倒美帝国主义这个当代的侵略和战争祸首，作出自己应有的贡献。
