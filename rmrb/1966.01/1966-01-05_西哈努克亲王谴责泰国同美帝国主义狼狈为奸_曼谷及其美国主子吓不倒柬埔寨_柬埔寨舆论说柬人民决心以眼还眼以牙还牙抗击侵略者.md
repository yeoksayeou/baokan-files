Title: Prince Sihanouk Condemns Thailand's Collusion with U.S. Imperialism; Bangkok and its American Masters Cannot Intimidate Cambodia; Cambodian Public Opinion Says Cambodian People are Determined to Retaliate Eye for an Eye, Tooth for a Tooth, to Resist the Aggressors

Author: 
Date: 1966-01-05
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 4th Dispatch: According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on the 4th, Cambodian Head of State Prince Sihanouk stated in a speech delivered on the 3rd in Klaing Roleap village, Kampong Chhnang Province, that "Thailand has seriously betrayed Asia, to which it belongs, and colluded with U.S. imperialists, allowing them to establish various facilities in Thailand, enabling the U.S. military to use all kinds of war materials to slaughter innocent Asian people in Laos and Vietnam."

    He said, "The Americans have once again confessed that imperialism faces a very serious threat, namely the massive people's movement that is sweeping across countries. They are in fact confessing that the current situation in Thailand is increasingly developing into a situation similar to that in South Vietnam."

    Prince Sihanouk said, "Bangkok and its American masters cannot intimidate us."

  "We will continue to complete our national construction tasks, but we are also prepared to give the 'Free Khmer' elements and their masters a proper 'welcome' should they dare to attack us."

    Cambodian National Radio, in a commentary broadcast on the evening of the 3rd, stated: "If the Thais, the 'Free Khmer' elements, and the South Vietnamese believe that they can intimidate us through their current increasing military pressure on our borders, they are gravely mistaken. As the Prince emphasized in his speech yesterday, we are fully capable of dealing with these aggressors and are determined to 'repay any attack with an eye for an eye and a tooth for a tooth.'"

    The radio station said: "Our enemies should have believed that their current threats cannot make us change our freely chosen policy of non-alignment, which their Washington masters and protectors are very annoyed with."

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 3rd Dispatch: Cambodian newspapers published editorials and commentaries on the 3rd, condemning U.S. imperialism, South Vietnam, and Thailand for invading Cambodia and reiterating Cambodia's determination to resist aggression.

    The editorial in *Cambodge Soir* on the 3rd, in discussing Cambodia's repelling of recent Thai and South Vietnamese aggression in O'Smach and Bat Deng villages, stated: "We will not stop here. If our enemies continue to provoke us, we will take other measures for legitimate defense, and these neighboring countries will bear the consequences." The editorial stated: "We will rise up and resist the aggressors with the weapons we possess. But if it proves that only the use of other weapons can force the enemy to abandon their plans to continue to annex Cambodia, we have other weapons to use. Our enemies must not think that we will only take defensive measures." The editorial quoted the resolution of the Twentieth National Congress of the People's Socialist Community saying that we will pay them back in their own coin, an eye for an eye, a tooth for a tooth. The editorial said that Cambodia will never be swallowed up. Cambodia will rather die than yield, and will fight resolutely. (Attached image)

### 西哈努克亲王谴责泰国同美帝国主义狼狈为奸  曼谷及其美国主子吓不倒柬埔寨  柬埔寨舆论说柬人民决心以眼还眼以牙还牙抗击侵略者

1966-01-05
第3版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王谴责泰国同美帝国主义狼狈为奸
    曼谷及其美国主子吓不倒柬埔寨
    柬埔寨舆论说柬人民决心以眼还眼以牙还牙抗击侵略者
    新华社金边四日电据柬埔寨新闻社四日报道，柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王三日在磅清扬省克拉因洛菲村发表讲话说，“泰国已十分严重地背叛了自己所属的亚洲，同美帝国主义者狼狈为奸，听任他们在泰国建立各种设施，使美军得以用各种战争物资屠杀老挝和越南的无辜的亚洲人民。”
    他说，“美国人再一次供认帝国主义面临着十分严重的威胁，那就是日益席卷各国的声势浩大的人民运动。他们实际上供认目前的泰国局势日益发展成同南越相象的局势。”
    西哈努克亲王说，“曼谷及其美国主子是吓不倒我们的”。
  “我们将继续完成我们的国家建设任务，但是，我们也准备给予‘自由高棉’分子及其主子以应有的‘欢迎’，一旦他们敢于进攻我们的话。”
    柬埔寨国家电台三日晚上发表评论说：“如果泰国人、‘自由高棉’分子和南越人认为，通过他们目前对我们边境日益加紧的军事压力，就能够把我们吓倒，他们就大错而特错了。正如亲王昨天在讲话中强调指出的，我们完全能够对付这些侵略者，而且决定‘以牙还牙回敬他们的任何进攻’。”
    这家电台说：“我们的敌人本该相信，他们目前施加威胁决不能使我们改变我们自由选择的不结盟政策，他们的华盛顿主子和保护者对我们的这种政策是十分恼火的。”
    新华社金边三日电柬埔寨报纸三日发表社论和评论，谴责美帝国主义、南越和泰国侵略柬埔寨并且重申柬埔寨抗击侵略的决心。
    《柬埔寨电讯报》三日的社论在谈到柬埔寨击退泰国和南越最近在奥斯马和巴图村进行侵略以后说：“我们不会到此为止。如果我们的敌人继续向我们挑衅，我们为了进行正当防御，将采取其他措施，我们的这些邻国将自食其果。”社论说：“我们将奋起以我们所拥有的武器抗击侵略者。但是，如果事实证明只有运用其他武器才能迫使敌人放弃继续推行它们的并吞柬埔寨的计划的话，我们还有其他武器运用。我们的敌人切勿认为我们将仅仅以采取防御为限。”社论引述人民社会同盟第二十次全国代表大会的决议说，我们将以其人之道还治其人之身，以眼还眼、以牙还牙。社论说，柬埔寨决不会任人吞食。柬埔寨将宁死不屈，坚决战斗。（附图片）
