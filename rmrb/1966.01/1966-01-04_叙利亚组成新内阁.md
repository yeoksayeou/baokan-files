Title: Syria Forms New Cabinet
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-04
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, January 2nd -- According to Syrian newspapers, a new cabinet headed by Salah al-Din Bitar was formed in Syria on the evening of the 1st.

    The new cabinet consists of twenty-six members. Among them, Bitar serves as Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, former Vice-Chairman of the Presidential Council, Muhammad Omran, serves as Minister of Defense, and Fahmi al-Ashuri serves as Minister of Interior.

    Salah al-Din Bitar has formed cabinets three times since March 1963.

    The Zain cabinet resigned on December 21, 1965.

### 叙利亚组成新内阁

1966-01-04
第3版()
专栏：

    叙利亚组成新内阁
    新华社大马士革二日电　据叙利亚报纸报道，一日晚间，叙利亚成立了以萨拉赫丁·比塔尔为首的新内阁。
    新内阁由二十六人组成。其中比塔尔担任总理兼外交部长，总统会议前副主席穆罕默德·奥姆兰担任国防部长，法赫米·阿舒里担任内政部长。
    萨拉赫丁·比塔尔自从一九六三年三月以来曾先后三次组阁。
    扎因内阁已于一九六五年十二月二十一日辞职。
