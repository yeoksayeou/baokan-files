Title: Nepalese Cultural Delegation Head Sama Says Chinese Literature and Art Serve the People; Chinese People Have a Common Ideology and Can Create Any Miracle

Author:

Date: 1966-01-21
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu - Jānardan Sama, head of the Nepalese Cultural Delegation and Deputy Director of Nepal Radio, recently told a Xinhua News Agency reporter about his impressions of visiting China: In China, people are the most valuable. There is mutual respect between people. They are all working hard, and there is no unemployment or hunger. The great Chinese people have a common purpose and a common ideology, and therefore can create any miracle in the world.
    Jānardan Sama led the Nepalese Cultural Delegation to visit China in September 1965. He praised the achievements of Chinese culture and art, saying that Chinese culture and art are beautiful and prosperous. It promotes various art forms that are loved by the broad masses of people and reflects the lives of the broad masses of people. China's new culture and art serve the broad masses of people.
    While praising the friendship between China and Nepal, he said: The friendship between the people of China and Nepal is heartfelt. Our two peoples have common characteristics, hoping to live in harmony, love labor, peace, and friendship, and fight against injustice and untruth. During our visit to China, the Chinese people's reception made us feel that we were not abroad, but at home.

### 尼泊尔文化代表团长萨玛说中国文艺为人民服务  中国人民有着共同思想能创造出任何奇迹

1966-01-21
第3版()
专栏：外国朋友谈中国

    尼泊尔文化代表团长萨玛说中国文艺为人民服务
    中国人民有着共同思想能创造出任何奇迹
    新华社加德满都电  尼泊尔文化代表团团长，尼泊尔电台副台长贾纳丹·萨玛最近对新华社记者谈到他访问中国的印象时说：在中国，人是最可贵的。人与人之间是相互尊重的。他们都在努力工作，没有失业和饥饿。伟大的中国人民群众有着共同的目的和共同的思想，因此可以创造出任何世界上的奇迹来。
    贾纳丹·萨玛曾在一九六五年九月率领尼泊尔文化代表团访问中国。他赞扬中国的文化艺术的成就说，中国的文化艺术是美丽的和繁荣的。它发扬了广大人民喜闻乐见的各种艺术形式，反映了广大人民的生活。中国的新文化艺术是为广大的人民服务的。
    他在赞扬中国和尼泊尔两国友谊时说：中尼两国人民的友谊是心连心的。我们两国人民有着共同的特点，希望和睦相处、热爱劳动、和平和友谊，同非正义和非真理进行斗争。在访问中国期间中国人民对我们的接待，使我们感到我们不象是在国外，而是象在自己的家里一样。
