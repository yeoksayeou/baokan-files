Title: President of Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Hosts Banquet for Japanese Guests
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-01
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, 31st — Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, hosted a banquet tonight in honor of Okada Haruo, member of the Japanese Socialist Party Diet, and Tsukada Shohei, Vice Chairman of the Hokkaido Assembly of Japan.
    The Japanese guests arrived in Beijing by plane on the evening of the 30th.

### 我人民外交学会会长宴请日本客人

1966-01-01
第3版()
专栏：

    我人民外交学会会长宴请日本客人
    新华社三十一日讯中国人民外交学会会长张奚若今晚设宴招待日本社会党国会议员冈田春夫和日本北海道议会副议长塚田庄平。
    日本客人是在三十日晚乘飞机到达北京的。
