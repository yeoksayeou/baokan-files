Title: Deputy Head of Polish Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-18
Edition: 4

    Deputy Head of Polish Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    (Xinhua News Agency, January 17th) Kubiczek, Deputy Head of the Polish Government Trade Delegation and Deputy Director of the First Treaty Division of the Polish Ministry of Foreign Trade, and five others, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning.
    Gao Shirong, Deputy Director of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and others went to the airport to welcome them.  Minczewski, Commercial Counselor of the Polish Embassy in China, and others also greeted them at the airport.

### 波兰贸易代表团副团长到京

1966-01-18
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰贸易代表团副团长到京
    新华社十七日讯　波兰政府贸易代表团副团长、波兰对外贸易部第一条约司副司长库比切克等六人，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的，有对外贸易部副局长高仕融等。波兰驻华大使馆商务参赞明成斯基等也到机场迎接。
