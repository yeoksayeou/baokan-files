Title: The Liaison Delegation of the Vietnam People's Army Sent a Letter to the International Commission Strongly Protesting the United States' Use of Poison Gas in South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-01-22
Edition: 3

    According to a report from Hanoi by the New China News Agency on the 20th, the Liaison Delegation of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army recently wrote a letter to the International Commission in Vietnam, strongly protesting the savage crimes of the American aggressors in using poison gas and chemical agents to massacre the people of South Vietnam on a large scale.
    The letter said that on January 11, when 8,000 American aggressor troops, along with Australian and New Zealand puppet troops, conducted a "sweep" of the liberated area more than 30 kilometers northwest of Saigon, they actually released poison gas into various shelters, poisoning many civilians to death. This is irrefutable evidence of the heinous crime of American aggressors using poison gas to massacre the people of South Vietnam.
    The letter pointed out that the U.S. imperialists, disregarding the condemnation of world public opinion, not only do not cease their crimes but even say that they will continue to use poison gas in their "sweeps."
    The protest letter demanded that the United States must stop its war of aggression against South Vietnam and withdraw all troops, all weapons and other war materials of the United States and its satellite countries, including all poison gas and other chemical agents, from South Vietnam.

### 越南人民军联络代表团致函国际委员会  强烈抗议美国在南越使用毒气

1966-01-22
第3版()
专栏：

    越南人民军联络代表团致函国际委员会
    强烈抗议美国在南越使用毒气
    据新华社河内二十日电  据越南通讯社报道，越南人民军总司令部联络代表团最近写信给越南国际委员会，强烈抗议美国侵略者使用毒气和化学毒剂大规模屠杀越南南方人民的野蛮罪行。
    信件说，一月十一日，八千名美国侵略军伙同澳大利亚和新西兰仆从军对西贡西北三十多公里的解放区进行“扫荡”时，竟向各个掩蔽所施放毒气，毒死了许多老百姓。这就是美国侵略者使用毒气屠杀越南南方人民滔天罪行的无可抵赖的证据。
    信件指出，美帝国主义者不顾世界舆论的谴责，不但不停止他们的罪行，竟然说他们要在“扫荡”中继续使用毒气。
    抗议信要求美国必须停止对越南南方的侵略战争，把美国及其仆从国的所有军队，所有武器和其他作战物资，包括所有的毒气和其他化学毒剂撤出越南南方。
