Title: Herdsmen Love New Year Pictures
Author: Wulijitu
Date: 1966-01-21
Edition: 4

    Herdsmen Love New Year Pictures
    Mongolian Nationality Wulijitu
    Before liberation, the people in the vast pastoral areas of Inner Mongolia had never seen any New Year pictures. After liberation, New Year pictures with revolutionary content began to appear.
    Every time the Spring Festival arrives, herdsmen put up their favorite New Year pictures in every household. These New Year pictures fully reflect the real life of the pastoral areas, and sing the praises of the heroic figures and advanced deeds of the laboring herdsmen.
    With the development of socialist revolution and socialist construction, the life of the herdsmen is constantly improving, and their cultural life is becoming increasingly rich and colorful, the need for New Year pictures by herdsmen is also more urgent. Art workers in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, in order to better serve the workers, peasants, soldiers and herdsmen, are determined to continue to follow Comrade Mao Zedong's orientation of literature and art serving the workers, peasants and soldiers, go deep into the masses, continuously transform their thinking, strengthen artistic practice, and strive to create new and beautiful New Year pictures.

### 牧民喜爱新年画
乌力吉图
1966-01-21
第4版()
专栏：

    牧民喜爱新年画
    蒙古族  乌力吉图
    解放前，内蒙古广大牧区人民从未见过什么年画。解放以后，开始有了革命内容的新年画。
    每当春节到来，牧民家家户户都张贴起他们喜爱的新年画。这些年画，充分反映了牧区人民的现实生活，歌颂劳动牧民的英雄人物和先进事迹。
    随着社会主义革命和社会主义建设事业的发展，牧民的生活不断改善，文化生活日益丰富多彩，牧民对新年画的需要也更加迫切了。内蒙古自治区的美术工作者，为了更好地为工、农、牧、兵服务，决心继续遵循毛泽东同志指出的为工农兵服务的文艺方向，深入群众，不断改造思想，加强艺术实践，为创作出又新又美的新年画而奋斗。
