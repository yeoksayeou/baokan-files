Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Japanese Friends
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-23
Edition: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, January 22nd – Premier Zhou Enlai received today the Friendly Delegation of the Japan-China Trade Promotion Association, currently visiting here, including the head of the delegation, Kawase Kazuharu, the Secretary-General, Sumii Masanori, and all members of the delegation, as well as Kimura Ichizo, Executive Director of the Kansai Headquarters of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association and others. Premier Zhou Enlai had a friendly conversation with the Japanese friends. Present at the reception were leading members of the concerned departments including Liao Chengzhi, Nan Hanchen, Lei Renmin, Lian Guan, Zhang Huadong, Yong Longgui, Wang Xiaoyun, Sun Pinghua, etc. (Attached picture)

### 周恩来总理接见日本朋友

1966-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见日本朋友
    新华社二十二日讯  周恩来总理今天接见了正在这里访问的日中贸易促进会友好代表团团长川濑一贯、秘书长隅井正典和代表团全体成员，以及日本国际贸易促进协会关西本部专务理事木村一三等人。周恩来总理同日本朋友进行了友好的谈话。接见时在座的有关方面人士有：廖承志、南汉宸、雷任民、连贯、张化东、勇龙桂、王晓云、孙平化等。（附图片）
