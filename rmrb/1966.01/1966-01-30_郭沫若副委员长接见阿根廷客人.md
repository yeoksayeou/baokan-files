Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Receives Argentine Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-01-30
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, January 29th: Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, today received Rolando Victor Garcia, Vice President of the Argentine National Council for Scientific and Technical Research and Dean of the Faculty of Science of the University of Buenos Aires, and his wife, Emilia, who holds a Master's degree in Psychology, and had a friendly conversation with them. Zhou Peiyuan, Zhang Jin, and others were present at the reception.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见阿根廷客人

1966-01-30
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见阿根廷客人
    新华社二十九日讯　人大常委会副委员长、中国科学院院长郭沫若，今天接见了阿根廷科学研究委员会副主席、布宜诺斯艾利斯大学理学院院长罗兰多·维克多·加尔西亚，和他的夫人心理学硕士艾米利亚，同他们进行了友好谈话。接见时在座的有周培源、张进等。
