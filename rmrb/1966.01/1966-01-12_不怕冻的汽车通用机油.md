Title: Cold-Resistant Universal Motor Oil for Automobiles

Author:
Date: 1966-01-12
Edition: 2

    Cold-Resistant Universal Motor Oil for Automobiles
    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, January 8th – A type of cold-resistant universal motor oil for automobile engines has recently been successfully trial-produced at the Shanghai Refinery.
    In the Shanghai Refinery's laboratory, people are conducting freezing tests on this motor oil. The temperature has been lowered to minus 45 degrees Celsius, and the motor oil still has not frozen.
    In China's Northeast, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, and high mountain areas, if automobiles use ordinary motor oil, it easily freezes below minus 10 degrees, making it difficult to start the car, and drivers have to bake related parts with fire. The cold-resistant universal motor oil is trial-produced by the staff of the Shanghai Refinery to help drivers overcome this difficulty.

### 不怕冻的汽车通用机油

1966-01-12
第2版()
专栏：新建设新产品

    不怕冻的汽车通用机油
    新华社上海八日电　一种不怕冻的汽车发动机通用机油最近在上海炼油厂试制成功。
    在上海炼油厂的试验室里，人们正在对这种机油进行冷冻试验。温度已经降到摄氏零下四十五度，这种机油仍然没有凝冻。
    在我国东北、内蒙古自治区和高山地带，如果汽车使用普通机油，在零下十度以下便容易凝冻，汽车不好发动，驾驶员只好用火烘烤有关机件。不怕冻的通用机油，就是上海炼油厂职工为帮助驾驶员克服这种困难而试制的。
