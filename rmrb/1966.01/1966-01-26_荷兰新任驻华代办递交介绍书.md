Title: New Dutch Chargé d'Affaires to China Presents Credentials

Author: 
Date: 1966-01-26
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, January 25th—The new Dutch Chargé d'Affaires to China, Mr. J. Vixseboxse, presented his credentials to Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Wang Bingnan this afternoon. Present at the occasion were Lai Yali, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Hu Shudu, Deputy Director of the Department of West European Affairs.
