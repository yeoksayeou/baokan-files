Title: South Vietnam National Liberation Front Representative in Algeria Refutes US Newspaper's Rumors, Reaffirms the National Liberation Front's Solemn Position Remains Unchanged
Author:
Date: 1966-01-16
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 15th (via Vietnamese News Agency) -- Huang Wenxin, head of the Permanent Delegation of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front to Algeria, issued a statement in Algiers on the 13th, condemning the US newspaper *The New York Times* for spreading rumors and slanders against the South Vietnam National Liberation Front, and reaffirming that the solemn position of the National Liberation Front remains unchanged.
    The statement refuted a false report published by *The New York Times* on the 12th. This report claimed that a representative of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front stationed in Algiers had hinted to a reporter from the newspaper that if the United States recognized the South Vietnam National Liberation Front, then the South Vietnam National Liberation Front and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam might abandon the demand for the withdrawal of US troops from South Vietnam as a condition for starting negotiations.
    The statement emphasized that this was a completely unfounded fabrication. It said: "We have not had any contact with any *New York Times* reporters stationed in Algiers or coming from abroad, whether direct or indirect, formal or informal."
    The statement pointed out that this shameless rumor-mongering by *The New York Times* is a new probe in the grand conspiracy of the "peace offensive" launched by US imperialism on the Vietnam issue. The statement reaffirmed that the position expressed in the March 22, 1965 statement of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front remains unchanged.

### 南越民族解放阵线驻阿尔及利亚代表驳美报造谣  重申民族解放阵线严正立场从未改变

1966-01-16
第4版()
专栏：

    南越民族解放阵线驻阿尔及利亚代表驳美报造谣
    重申民族解放阵线严正立场从未改变
    新华社河内十五日电　据越南通讯社报道，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻阿尔及利亚代表团团长黄文心十三日在阿尔及尔发表声明，谴责美国《纽约时报》对越南南方民族解放阵线的造谣诬蔑，重申民族解放阵线的严正立场没有改变。
    声明驳斥了《纽约时报》十二日发表的一则虚假的消息。这则消息说什么越南南方民族解放阵线驻阿尔及尔的一位代表曾向这家报纸的记者暗示：如果美国承认越南南方民族解放阵线，那末越南南方民族解放阵线和越南民主共和国可能放弃要求美军撤出越南南方的主张作为开始谈判的条件。
    声明着重指出，这完全是毫无根据的捏造。它说：“我们没有同任何驻在阿尔及尔或者从外国来的《纽约时报》记者进行过任何接触，不论是直接的还是间接的，正式的还是非正式的。”
    声明指出，《纽约时报》这种无耻的造谣行径是美帝国主义在越南问题上发动的“和平攻势”大阴谋中的一个新的试探。声明重申，越南南方民族解放阵线一九六五年三月二十二日声明中所表示的立场没有改变。
