Title: Grand Gala Performance in Bucharest Commemorates the 107th Anniversary of Romania's Unification
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-28
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, January 25th—The Romanian State Committee for Culture and Art held a grand gala performance at the Bucharest Opera and Ballet Theatre on the evening of the 24th to commemorate the 107th anniversary of Romania's unification.

    Romanian Communist Party and state leaders Nicolae Ceaușescu, Chivu Stoica, Ion Gheorghe Maurer, as well as heads of Party and state departments and mass organizations, a total of over one thousand people, attended and watched the performance.

    Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Romania also attended and watched the performance.

### 纪念罗马尼亚统一一百零七周年  布加勒斯特举行盛大文艺演出

1966-01-28
第4版()
专栏：

    纪念罗马尼亚统一一百零七周年
    布加勒斯特举行盛大文艺演出
    新华社布加勒斯特二十五日电　罗马尼亚国家文化艺术委员会为纪念罗马尼亚统一一百零七周年，二十四日晚在布加勒斯特歌剧和芭蕾舞剧院举行盛大的文艺演出。
    罗马尼亚共产党和国家领导人尼·齐奥塞斯库、基伏·斯托伊卡、扬·格·毛雷尔以及党和国家各部门、各群众团体的负责人等一千多人，出席观看了演出。
    各国驻罗马尼亚的外交使节也出席观看了演出。
