Title: I Donate Books to the Bangla Academy of Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1966-01-01
Edition: 3

    I Donate Books to the Bangla Academy of Pakistan
    Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 29th – Wang Ze, Chinese Consul General in Dacca, East Pakistan, presented a collection of Chinese literature and art books to the Bangla Academy on the 29th. At the book donation ceremony, Wang Ze said that there is a close and friendly feeling between the Chinese and Pakistani people. He expressed hope that the donated books would further strengthen this fraternal friendship.
    Dr. Qudrat-E-Khuda, Director of the Bangla Academy, expressed his gratitude on behalf of the academy when accepting the books. He said: This collection contains many treasures of Chinese literature, which will bring the people of the two countries closer. Through these books, the people of Pakistan can have a more intimate understanding of China.

### 我向巴基斯坦班格拉学院赠送书籍

1966-01-01
第3版()
专栏：

    我向巴基斯坦班格拉学院赠送书籍
    新华社卡拉奇二十九日电中国驻东巴基斯坦达卡总领事王泽二十九日把一批中国的文学和文艺书籍赠给了班格拉学院。王泽在赠送这批书籍的仪式上讲话说，中巴人民之间有着亲密友好的感情。他表示希望赠送的这批书籍将使这种兄弟友谊更加紧密。
    班格拉学院院长古德拉·特·库达博士在代表这个学院接受这批书籍时表示感谢。他说：这批书里有许多中国文学的宝藏，会使两国人民更加接近。巴基斯坦人民通过这批书籍可以更亲切地了解中国。
