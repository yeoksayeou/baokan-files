Title: U.S.-Park Clique Continuously Conducts Military Exercises
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-28
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 26th. Seoul dispatch: U.S. imperialism and its running dog Park Chung-hee clique have recently conducted military exercises continuously.
    According to a broadcast from Seoul Radio, the South Korean puppet army and the U.S. aggressor troops held a joint mine-sweeping exercise in Jinhae Bay, South Korea, from the 17th to the 23rd. Participating in this exercise were the U.S. MSC7-class minesweeper of ten thousand tons, the South Korean puppet navy's "Namyeong" minesweeper, and the U.S.-Park clique's new-type aircraft. The exercises included aerial mine-laying, mine monitoring, mine exploration, and mine-sweeping.
    It is reported that the South Korean puppet army officer candidate school conducted a three-day "cold resistance combat training" in Jingan Township, Yangju County, Gyeonggi Province, starting on the 20th.
    According to Western news agencies, the Park Chung-hee clique received six M48 medium tanks from the U.S. Army on the 24th. It was revealed that these six tanks are the first batch of twenty-two tanks that the United States promised to provide to the Park Chung-hee clique as part of its army equipment renewal plan.

### 美朴集团连续进行军事演习

1966-01-28
第4版()
专栏：

    美朴集团连续进行军事演习
    新华社平壤二十六日电　汉城消息：美帝国主义及其走狗朴正熙集团最近又连续进行军事演习。
    据汉城电台广播，南朝鲜傀儡军和美国侵略军十七日到二十三日在南朝鲜镇海湾举行了联合扫雷演习。参加这次演习的有美国万吨级的MSC7号扫雷艇和南朝鲜傀儡海军的“南英号”扫雷艇以及美朴集团的新型飞机。演习的内容包括空中布雷、监视水雷、探索水雷和扫雷等。
    据报道，南朝鲜傀儡陆军士官学校从二十日起曾在京畿道杨州郡真乾面进行了三天的“耐寒作战训练”。
    另据西方通讯社报道，朴正熙集团在二十四日得到了美国提供的六辆美国陆军M48中型坦克。据透露，这六辆坦克是美国答应作为朴正熙集团陆军装备更新计划的一部分而向它提供的二十二辆坦克中的第一批。
