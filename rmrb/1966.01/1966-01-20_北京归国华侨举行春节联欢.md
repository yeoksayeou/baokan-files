Title: Beijing Returned Overseas Chinese Hold Spring Festival Gala
Author:
Date: 1966-01-20
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, January 19th. More than 2,700 returned overseas Chinese in Beijing held a Spring Festival gala this evening. Liao Chengzhi, Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, spoke at the gala and extended his Spring Festival greetings to everyone.
    Also participating in tonight's gala were Lin Xiude, Deputy Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission; Cui Yueli, Deputy Mayor of Beijing; and responsible persons from the All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese and other units.

### 北京归国华侨举行春节联欢

1966-01-20
第3版()
专栏：

    北京归国华侨举行春节联欢
    新华社十九日讯　北京归国华侨二千七百多人在今晚举行春节联欢会。华侨事务委员会主任廖承志在会上讲话，向大家祝贺春节。
    参加今晚联欢会的，还有华侨事务委员会副主任林修德，北京市副市长崔月犁，以及中华全国归国华侨联合会等单位的负责人。
