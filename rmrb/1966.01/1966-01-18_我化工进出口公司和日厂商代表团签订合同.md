Title: Our Chemical Import and Export Corporation Signs Contract with Japanese Manufacturers Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-01-18
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 15th. Wang Hanmin, Acting General Manager of the China National Chemical Import and Export Corporation, and Suzuki Haruo, head of the Japanese Ammonium Sulfate and Urea Manufacturers Delegation, signed a contract for a fertilizer transaction in Beijing this afternoon.
    Attending the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were Liao Chengzhi, Liu Xiwen, Wang Xiaoyun, Sun Pinghua, and others. On the Japanese side were Tsuneomi Soma, Chief Representative of the Kaozaki Office's Beijing Liaison Office, and all members of the delegation. Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji, who was in Beijing, also attended the signing ceremony. After the signing, Wang Hanmin hosted a banquet for the Japanese guests.

### 我化工进出口公司和日厂商代表团签订合同

1966-01-18
第4版()
专栏：

    我化工进出口公司和日厂商代表团签订合同
    新华社十五日讯　中国化工进出口公司代总经理王汉民和日本硫铵、尿素厂商代表团团长铃木治雄，今天下午在北京签订了一项化肥交易的合同。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有廖承志、刘希文、王晓云、孙平化等，日本方面有高碕办事处驻北京联络事务所首席代表相马常敏以及代表团全体成员。在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也参加了签字仪式。签字以后，王汉民设宴招待了日本客人。
