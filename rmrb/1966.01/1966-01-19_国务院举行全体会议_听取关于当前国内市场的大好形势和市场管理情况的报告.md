Title: State Council Holds Plenary Session to Hear Report on the Excellent Situation of the Current Domestic Market and Market Management

Author:

Date: 1966-01-19
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The State Council held its 160th plenary session today. The session heard reports from Minister of Commerce Yao Yilin and Deputy Director of the Central Administration of Industry and Commerce Guan Datong on the excellent situation of the current domestic market and market management.
    The meeting approved changes to the administrative divisions of some counties and cities, and also approved appointments and removals.

**Named Entities**

国务院	Guówùyuàn (State Council)
商业部	Shāngyè bù (Ministry of Commerce)
姚依林	Yáo Yīlín
中央工商行政管理局	Zhōngyāng gōngshāng xíngzhèng guǎnlǐ jú (Central Administration of Industry and Commerce)
管大同	Guǎn Datong
新华社	Xīnhuá shè (Xinhua News Agency)

### 国务院举行全体会议  听取关于当前国内市场的大好形势和市场管理情况的报告

1966-01-19
第2版()
专栏：

    国务院举行全体会议
    听取关于当前国内市场的大好形势和市场管理情况的报告
    新华社十八日讯　国务院全体会议今天举行第一百六十次会议，会议听取了商业部部长姚依林和中央工商行政管理局副局长管大同关于当前国内市场的大好形势和市场管理情况的报告。
    会议通过了部分县、市行政区划变更事项，还通过任免事项。
