Title: Confidently Embrace New Production Tasks on the Basis of Completing the Third Five-Year Plan: Albanian Enterprises Achieve a Good Start in New Year Production

Author: 
Date: 1966-01-11
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 5th—In recent days, reports of successfully completing the first few days of production tasks in 1966 have continuously poured in from all over Albania.
    1966 is the first year of Albania's Fourth Five-Year Plan. Based on the completion of the Third Five-Year Plan, the working people of various regions are confidently welcoming new production tasks.
    In the capital, Tirana, all joint enterprises and factories have successfully completed the production tasks for the first few days of this year.
    In various regions and industries across the country, many enterprises and units have overfulfilled the production tasks for the first few days of this year. These enterprises include the country's largest Vlora Lenin Cement Plant, the Gjirokastra Cigarette Factory (which completed its Third Five-Year Plan production tasks two months ahead of schedule), the coal miners of the Mborje-Drenove Coal Mine Company in Korçë, and all shifts at the Durrës Seaport Terminal.

### 在完成第三个五年计划基础上满怀信心迎接新的生产任务  阿尔巴尼亚企业新年生产开门红

1966-01-11
第4版()
专栏：

    在完成第三个五年计划基础上满怀信心迎接新的生产任务
    阿尔巴尼亚企业新年生产开门红
    新华社地拉那五日电  近几天来，从阿尔巴尼亚全国各地不断传来胜利完成一九六六年头几天生产任务的捷报。
    一九六六年是阿尔巴尼亚执行第四个五年计划的第一年。各地劳动人民在完成第三个五年计划的基础上，满怀信心地迎接新的生产任务。
    在首都地拉那，各联合企业和各工厂都胜利地完成了今年头几天的生产任务。
    在全国各地区、各行业，有许多企业单位超额完成了今年头几天的生产任务，这些企业中有全国最大的发罗拉列宁水泥厂、提前两个月完成第三个五年计划生产任务的吉罗卡斯特卷烟厂，以及科尔察的姆博雷——德雷诺煤矿公司的采煤工人和都拉斯海运码头的所有班组等。
