Title: Prince Sihanouk Reiterates He Will Never Sacrifice Interests to Gain American Respect, Sternly Condemns the United States for Instigating South Vietnam and Thailand to Invade Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1966-01-09
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, January 8th: Cambodian Head of State Prince Norodom Sihanouk recently told an American journalist: "Don't expect the Khmer people to put your (Americans') interests above their own to gain your respect. You will never succeed."
    Prince Sihanouk made these remarks in response to a question from Stanley Karnow, a journalist from the American *Washington Post*.
    The sixth issue of the Cambodian newspaper *People's Socialist Community* published Prince Sihanouk's response.
    Prince Sihanouk sternly condemned the United States for supporting the Saigon regime and Thailand, encouraging them to invade Cambodia, condemning the United States for carrying out bombings and murders, causing casualties among innocent residents on the Cambodian border, and condemning the United States for supporting the Cambodian traitor "Free Khmer," providing them with supplies, and sending American officers to train them. Prince Sihanouk pointed out: "This violates all norms of international law and reflects the extent of your hostility towards Cambodia's independence."
    He said: "We are legitimately negotiating with China, asking China to help Cambodia safeguard its territorial integrity in the future."
    He said, "Don't expect the Khmer people to put your interests above their own to gain your respect. You will never succeed, even if you continue to call them 'pseudo-neutralists, primitives, fools, madmen, servants of Beijing,' and so on."
    Prince Sihanouk said: "We have never refused to repair our broken relations with the United States. However, this reconciliation can only be lasting if it is built on a solid foundation. This foundation must have the following elements:
    First, recognition of Cambodia's territorial integrity, including its coastal islands.
    Second, the conclusion of a formal agreement to cease armed aggression against Cambodian territory.
    Third, compensation to the victims of previous aggressions against Cambodia."
    Prince Sihanouk first answered the question of why Cambodia rejected American "aid." He said, "First, I want to remind you of certain aspects of American 'aid' to Cambodia and why we rejected this 'aid.' The Cambodian state may not use American economic aid to equip industries and enterprises that are not privately owned. The local currency accumulated through American aid must be entirely distributed to private importers to purchase products or equipment that do not always meet the vital or even basic needs of Cambodia and the Cambodian people. Furthermore, materials may only be imported from countries approved by the United States.
    In terms of military assistance, we were delivered old-fashioned and outdated materials that clearly could not meet the basic needs of our national defense. We have no right to accept aid from other countries, especially socialist countries. American weapons can only be used against communists who do not attack us. They cannot be used against American mercenaries who are the only ones invading our country. ... We can no longer endure your insults."

### 西哈努克亲王重申决不牺牲利益换取美国尊重  严厉谴责美国指使南越泰国侵略柬埔寨

1966-01-09
第4版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王重申决不牺牲利益换取美国尊重
    严厉谴责美国指使南越泰国侵略柬埔寨
    新华社金边八日电　柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王最近向美国记者发表谈话说：“不要希望高棉人把你们（美国人）的利益放在自己利益之上，来换取你们的尊重。你们是永远办不到的。”
    西哈努克亲王是在答复美国《华盛顿邮报》记者斯坦利·卡诺的问题时这样说的。
    柬埔寨《人民社会同盟》报第六期刊载了西哈努克亲王的答复。
    西哈努克亲王严厉谴责美国支持西贡政权和泰国，怂恿它们侵略柬埔寨，谴责美国进行轰炸和谋杀，使柬埔寨边境的无辜居民遭受伤亡，谴责美国支持柬埔寨叛国分子“自由高棉”，提供他们物资，派美国军官训练他们。西哈努克亲王指出：“这是违反一切国际法准则的，同时反映你们敌视柬埔寨的独立到了何种地步。”
    他说：“我们正在正当地同中国进行谈判，要求中国帮助柬埔寨在今后维护自己的领土完整。”
    他说，“不要希望高棉人把你们的利益放在自己的利益之上来换取你们的尊重，你们是永远办不到的，即使你们继续把他们叫作‘假中立派，原始人，傻瓜，疯子，北京的仆从’等等。”
    西哈努克亲王说：“我们从来没有拒绝弥补我们同美国的已经破裂的关系。但是，这种和解只有建立在牢固的基础上才能持久。这个基础必须具有下列几个因素：
    一，承认柬埔寨的领土完整，包括它的沿海岛屿在内。
    二，就停止武装侵略柬埔寨领土缔结一项正式协定。
    三，赔偿以前对柬埔寨的各次侵略的受害者。”
    西哈努克亲王首先答复了柬埔寨为什么拒绝美国“援助”的问题。他说，“首先，我要提醒你注意美国对柬埔寨的‘援助’的某些方面，和我们为什么拒绝这种‘援助’。柬埔寨国家不得利用美国的经济援助来装备不是私营的工业和企业。因为美援而积聚起来的当地货币必须完全分配给私人进口商来购买并不总是符合柬埔寨和柬埔寨人民的切身或甚至基本需要的产品或装备。此外，只准从美国所同意的国家进口物资。
    在军事援助方面，运交给我们的是显然不能满足我国国防基本需要的老式和陈旧的物资。我们无权接受别国的援助，特别是社会主义国家的援助。美国的武器只能用来反对并不攻击我们的共产党人。不能用它们来反对唯一侵略我国的美国雇佣军。……我们不能再承受你们的侮辱了。”
