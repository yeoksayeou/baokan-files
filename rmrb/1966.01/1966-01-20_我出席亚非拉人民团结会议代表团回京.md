Title: Delegation to Asian, African, and Latin American People's Solidarity Conference Returns to Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-20
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 19th. The Chinese delegation to the Asian, African, and Latin American People's Solidarity Conference, led by its head, Wu Xueqian, returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon.
    Responsible persons of various people's organizations went to the airport to greet them, including Qu Tangliang, Kang Yonghe, Shen Zijiu, Zheng Senyu, Wang Chuanbin, Hu Qili, Ren Yinglun, Yan Wenjing, Qiao Shi, Wu Qing, and He Lin.

### 我出席亚非拉人民团结会议代表团回京

1966-01-20
第4版()
专栏：

    我出席亚非拉人民团结会议代表团回京
    新华社十九日讯　中国出席亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲人民团结会议代表团在团长吴学谦率领下，今天下午乘飞机回到北京。
    前往机场迎接的有区棠亮、康永和、沈兹九、郑森禹、王传斌、胡启立、任映仑、严文井、乔石、吴青、何林等各人民团体负责人。
