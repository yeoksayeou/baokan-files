Title: Albanian Leaders Receive Delegation from People's Bank of China
Author:
Date: 1966-01-13
Edition: 4

    New China News Agency, Tirana, January 11th—Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, received the delegation from the People's Bank of China, led by Fang Gao, Deputy Governor of the People's Bank of China, on the morning of the 11th, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Politburo Member and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Hysni Kapo, Minister of Finance Veiz Gjeloshi, and Governor of the State Bank, Koleka Bakali.
    New China News Agency, Tirana, January 11th—Mehmet Shehu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Albania, received all members of the delegation from the People's Bank of China, led by Fang Gao, Deputy Governor of the People's Bank of China, on the 10th, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them. Present at the reception were Albanian Minister of Finance, Veiz Gjeloshi, Governor of the State Bank, Koleka Bakali, and others.
    Xu Jianguo, Chinese Ambassador to Albania, was also present at the reception.

### 阿尔巴尼亚领导人接见我人民银行代表团

1966-01-13
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚领导人接见我人民银行代表团
    新华社地拉那十一日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央第一书记霍查，十一日上午接见了由中国人民银行副行长方皋率领的中国人民银行代表团，并且同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、中央书记托斯卡、财政部长维尔利和国家银行行长巴卡利。
    新华社地拉那十一日电　阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席谢胡，十日接见了由中国人民银行副行长方皋率领的中国人民银行代表团全体成员，并且同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的有，阿尔巴尼亚财政部长维尔利、国家银行行长巴卡利等。
    接见时中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使许建国也在座。
