Title: New Instrument for Studying Electrophysiology

Author: 
Date: 1966-01-29
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, January 28th – Guangzhou recently successfully trial-produced a precision instrument for studying electrophysiology – an electrophysiological instrument.
    The electrophysiological instrument is a basic instrument for biological research. This electrophysiological instrument, successfully trial-produced by the Guangzhou Shuguang Radio Instrument Factory in cooperation with the Institute of Physiology of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, has all of its circuits designed by the research unit itself, and all of the components used are produced in China. After preliminary evaluation by relevant departments, all technical indicators have met the design requirements.
    This electrophysiological instrument has high sensitivity and can amplify weak bioelectricity by more than one million times. Although the various electrical changes generated in the bodies of humans, animals, and plants are extremely weak, they can all be detected by this instrument, and the change curves can be displayed without distortion.
    In addition to being suitable for various needs in physiological research and medical diagnosis, this instrument can also be used for general scientific experiments and industrial testing.

### 研究电生理学的新仪器

1966-01-29
第2版()
专栏：

    研究电生理学的新仪器
    新华社广州二十八日电　广州市最近试制成功一台研究电生理学的精密仪器——电生理仪。
    电生理仪是生物学研究的一种基本仪器。由广州曙光无线电仪器厂与中国科学院生理研究所合作试制成功的这台电生理仪，线路全部是研究单位自己设计的，所采用的元件也全部是我国生产的。经有关部门初步鉴定，各项技术指标都达到设计要求。
    这台电生理仪的灵敏度很高，能把微弱的生物电放大一百万倍以上。人、动物和植物体内产生的各种电变化虽然极其微弱，但是用这种仪器都能探测出来，并且能不失真地显示出变化曲线。
    这种仪器除了适合于生理学研究与医疗诊断方面的多种需要以外，还能作为一般科学实验与工业测试之用。
