Title: Soviet Delegation Passes Through Beijing En Route to Vietnam
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-08
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th. A Soviet delegation headed by Shelepin, member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee and Central Committee Secretary, passed through Beijing at noon today on its way to visit the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
    Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State Council, and other responsible comrades Zhao Yimin, Wang Bingnan, Yu Zhan, Dai Ping, and others welcomed and saw off the Soviet delegation at the Capital Airport.
    Also at the airport to welcome and see off the delegation were: Soviet Ambassador to China, Lapin, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, Hoang Bac, and representative of the Permanent Mission in China of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front, Hoang Van Hoan.

### 苏联代表团路过北京前往越南

1966-01-08
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联代表团路过北京前往越南
    新华社七日讯　前往越南民主共和国访问的苏联代表团，在苏共中央主席团委员、中央书记谢列平率领下，今天中午路过北京。
    国务院副总理李先念，以及其他方面的负责人赵毅敏、王炳南、余湛、戴平等，到首都机场迎送苏联代表团。
    到机场迎送的还有：苏联驻中国大使拉宾，越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办黄北，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代表黄万里。
