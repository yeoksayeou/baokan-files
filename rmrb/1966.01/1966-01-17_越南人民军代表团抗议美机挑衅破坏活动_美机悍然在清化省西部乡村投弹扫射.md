Title: Delegation of the Vietnam People's Army Protests Provocative and Destructive Activities of U.S. Aircraft; U.S. Aircraft Brazenly Bombs and Strafes Villages in Western Thanh Hóa Province
Author:
Date: 1966-01-17
Edition: 5

    According to a dispatch from Hanoi by Xinhua News Agency on January 16th: The Liaison Delegation of the General Command of the Vietnam People's Army sent an urgent telegram to the International Commission of Vietnam on the 16th, strongly protesting and denouncing the continued violation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam by U.S. aircraft on January 14th. They conducted reconnaissance and provocative activities over the provinces of Quảng Bình, Hà Tĩnh, Nghệ An, and Thanh Hóa. On the evening of the same day, at 22:00 hours, they dropped four bombs and strafed some villages in western Thanh Hóa Province, causing some loss of life, property, and homes to the local people.

    According to a dispatch from Hanoi by Xinhua News Agency on January 15th: The Liaison Delegation of the General Command of the Vietnam People's Army sent an urgent telegram to the International Commission of Vietnam on the 15th, strongly protesting the continued violation of the airspace of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam by U.S. aircraft on January 14th, conducting reconnaissance and provocative activities. The telegram pointed out that these activities, carried out by the Johnson administration while it constantly trumpets its "quest for peace" on the Vietnam issue, prove that its actions are preparations to intensify and expand the U.S. war of aggression in Vietnam.

### 越南人民军代表团抗议美机挑衅破坏活动  美机悍然在清化省西部乡村投弹扫射

1966-01-17
第5版()
专栏：

    越南人民军代表团抗议美机挑衅破坏活动
    美机悍然在清化省西部乡村投弹扫射
    据新华社河内十六日电　据越南通讯社报道：越南人民军总司令部联络代表团十六日打紧急电报给越南国际委员会，强烈抗议和控诉美国飞机十四日继续侵犯越南民主共和国，对广平、河静、义安、清化等省进行侦察和挑衅活动，并于当天晚上二十二时，向清化省西部的一些乡投下四颗炸弹并进行扫射，使当地人民的生命、房屋和财产遭受一些损失。
    据新华社河内十五日电　越南人民军总司令部联络代表团十五日打紧急电报给越南国际委员会，强烈抗议美国飞机十四日继续侵犯越南民主共和国领空，进行侦察和挑衅活动。电报指出，约翰逊政府在它不断宣扬在越南问题上“谋求和平”的论调的时候进行的这些活动证明，它的行动是准备加强和扩大美国在越南的侵略战争。
