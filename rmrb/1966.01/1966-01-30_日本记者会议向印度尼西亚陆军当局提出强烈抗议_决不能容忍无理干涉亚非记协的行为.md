Title: Japanese Journalists' Conference Lodges Strong Protest with Indonesian Army Authorities: Unreasonable Interference with the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association Absolutely Cannot Be Tolerated
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-30
Edition: 4

    New China News Agency, Tokyo, 28th – The Japanese Journalists' Conference strongly protests the Indonesian Army authorities' interference and persecution of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association and Indonesian journalists.
    This protest was made in a letter written by Yuichi Kobayashi, Chairman of the Japanese Journalists' Conference, to Indonesian Foreign Minister Subandrio on the 28th.
    The letter stated that the Japanese Journalists' Conference absolutely cannot tolerate the unreasonable interference and persecution of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association and Indonesian journalists by the Indonesian Army authorities since the "September 30th Movement." The letter stated that this is an unforgivable act of infringing upon the basic rights of journalists.
    The letter pointed out that the Indonesian Army authorities' interference with the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association is a serious insult to all Afro-Asian journalists.
    The letter also demanded that the Indonesian Army authorities cease their acts of persecution, immediately release those arrested Indonesian journalists, and guarantee the freedom of their activities.

### 日本记者会议向印度尼西亚陆军当局提出强烈抗议  决不能容忍无理干涉亚非记协的行为

1966-01-30
第4版()
专栏：

    日本记者会议向印度尼西亚陆军当局提出强烈抗议
    决不能容忍无理干涉亚非记协的行为
    新华社二十八日讯　东京消息：日本记者会议强烈抗议印度尼西亚陆军当局干涉和迫害亚非新闻工作者协会书记处和印度尼西亚新闻工作者。
    这个抗议是日本记者会议议长小林雄一在二十八日写给印度尼西亚外交部长苏班德里约的信中提出的。
    信中说，日本记者会议决不能容忍印度尼西亚陆军当局自“九月三十日运动”以来对亚非新闻工作者协会书记处和印度尼西亚新闻工作者进行的无理干涉和迫害。信中说，这是侵犯新闻工作者的基本权利的不可饶恕的行为。
    信中指出，印度尼西亚陆军当局干涉亚非新闻工作者协会书记处的行为，是对全体亚非新闻工作者的严重侮辱。
    这封信还要求印度尼西亚陆军当局停止迫害行为，立即释放那些被捕的印度尼西亚新闻工作者并保证他们活动的自由。
