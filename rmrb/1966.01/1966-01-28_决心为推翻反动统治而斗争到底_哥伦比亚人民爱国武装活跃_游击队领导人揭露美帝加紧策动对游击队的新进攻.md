Title: Determined to Fight to the End to Overthrow Reactionary Rule: Patriotic Armed Forces of the Colombian People Active; Guerrilla Leaders Expose US Imperialism's Intensified Efforts to Plan New Offensives Against Guerrillas
Author:
Date: 1966-01-28
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Bogotá, January 26th Dispatch: Guerrilla forces in northern and central Colombia have recently been actively engaging and striking at reactionary armed forces suppressing the people.
    According to reports, a guerrilla unit operating in the Magdalena province of northern Colombia attacked a district government in the city of Pelotas on the evening of the 20th, engaging in a battle with local police and government troops sent to suppress them, seizing some firearms. Prior to this, the guerrilla force had also attacked two other towns, causing alarm among the reactionary military authorities.
    Guerrilla forces fighting in the provinces of Tolima and Huila in central Colombia have been launching continuous attacks from the end of last year to mid-January this year. A communiqué from the Sixth Brigade of the government army admitted that on January 1st, a government patrol was attacked by guerrillas on a highway in Tolima province, resulting in two deaths and the burning of two vehicles. From January 2nd to 9th, government forces in the southern part of the province were subjected to several surprise attacks by guerrillas. In one battle in Ataco, an army captain was seriously injured. In the Chaparral region, a police outpost was also attacked.
    Guerrilla forces operating in the Guayabero and Caquetá regions issued a statement to the press on the 19th, strongly criticizing the pro-American reactionary rule and expressing their determination to fight to the end to overthrow it. The statement also exposed the crimes of "the high-level military command of the United States" in directly participating in the suppression of guerrilla activities in the provinces of Tolima and Huila. According to reports, guerrilla activities in the two provinces have been very active recently.
    Xinhua News Agency, Bogotá, January 26th Dispatch: Amidst the active armed struggle of the patriotic people of Colombia, the United States is intensifying its efforts in Colombia to plan new offensives against the patriotic armed forces.
    A statement issued to the press in Huila province on the 19th by leaders of the guerrilla movement in the El Pato, Guayabero, and Caquetá regions of central Colombia revealed that "currently, the high-level military command of the United States is directly directing the Sixth Brigade in its operational actions (against the guerrillas) in the provinces of Tolima and Huila." The statement also stated that, in addition, some American military personnel participated in the so-called "civil-military operations" conducted by the Colombian army in the Tame, Casanare region.
    According to reports, Roberto Porter, Commander of the U.S. Southern Command, recently arrived in Colombia and held secret talks with Colombian Armed Forces Commander Fajardo Pizón and Defense Minister Reyes Pizarro, among others, on the 13th. They formulated a plan to attack the patriotic guerrilla forces in the new year. It was revealed that the talks focused on military operations to suppress the peasants.
    In his New Year's greeting to the Colombian people, the famous guerrilla leader Manuel Marulanda exposed the crimes of high-ranking U.S. military personnel who participated in the suppression of the guerrillas in Macatalia, El Pato, Guayabero, and Río Chiquito. He pointed out that the planes that bombed and strafed the guerrillas were provided by the United States and flown by Americans. (Attached Picture)
    The patriotic armed forces of the Colombian people are constantly striking at the armed forces of the pro-American reactionary government. This is a patriotic armed force in a mountainous area of Colombia, fully equipped and ready to depart.

### 决心为推翻反动统治而斗争到底  哥伦比亚人民爱国武装活跃  游击队领导人揭露美帝加紧策动对游击队的新进攻

1966-01-28
第4版()
专栏：

    决心为推翻反动统治而斗争到底
    哥伦比亚人民爱国武装活跃
    游击队领导人揭露美帝加紧策动对游击队的新进攻
    新华社二十六日讯　波哥大消息：哥伦比亚北部和中部地区的游击队最近积极活动，打击镇压人民的反动武装。
    据报道，在这个国家北部马格达莱纳省活动的一支游击队，在二十日晚上袭击了派利塔斯市的一个区政府，同当地的警察和前往镇压的政府军发生一场战斗，缴获了一些枪枝。在这以前，这支游击武装还曾经袭击过另外两个城镇，使反动军事当局惶恐不安。
    战斗在哥伦比亚中部托利马和乌伊拉省的游击队，从去年年底到今年一月中不断出击。政府军第六旅的公报承认，今年一月一日，一支政府巡逻队在托利马省的一条公路上遭到游击队袭击，死亡两人，还有两辆汽车被烧毁。从一月二日到九日，政府军在这个省的南部几次受到游击队的突然袭击，其中在阿塔科的一次战斗中，一个陆军上尉受重伤。在查帕拉尔地区，一个警察哨所也遭到袭击。
    在瓜亚贝罗和卡克塔地区活动的游击队在十九日向新闻界发表声明，强烈抨击亲美反动统治，并且表示决心为推翻这一统治而斗争到底。这项声明还揭露了“美国的高级军事指挥部”直接参与对托利马省和乌伊拉省游击队镇压活动的罪恶勾当。据报道，最近两个省的游击活动十分活跃。
    据新华社二十六日讯　波哥大消息：在哥伦比亚爱国人民武装斗争活跃的情况下，美国又在哥伦比亚加紧策动对爱国武装力量进行新的进攻。
    哥伦比亚中部埃尔帕托、瓜亚贝罗和卡克塔地区游击运动领导人在十九日向乌伊拉省报界发表的一项声明揭露说，“现在，美国的高级军事指挥部正在直接指挥第六旅在托利马省和乌伊拉省的（对游击队的）作战行动。”这项声明还说，此外，还有一些美国军事人员参与了哥伦比亚军队在塔梅市卡萨纳雷地区进行的所谓“民间军事行动”。
    据报道，美国南方司令部司令罗伯托·波特尔不久前到达哥伦比亚，在十三日同哥伦比亚武装部队司令法哈尔多·皮松及国防部长雷维斯·皮萨罗等举行了秘密会谈。他们制定了在新的一年进攻爱国游击武装的计划。据透露，会谈着重讨论了镇压农民的军事行动。
    著名游击队领导人曼努埃尔·马鲁兰达在给哥伦比亚人民的新年贺信中揭露：美国高级军事人员参与了镇压马克塔利亚、埃尔帕托、瓜亚贝罗、里奥奇基托游击队的罪恶活动。他指出，对游击队员进行轰炸扫射的飞机，是由美国提供、由美国人驾驶的。（附图片）
    哥伦比亚人民爱国武装力量不断打击亲美反动政府的武装部队。这是哥伦比亚某山区一支整装待发的爱国武装力量。
