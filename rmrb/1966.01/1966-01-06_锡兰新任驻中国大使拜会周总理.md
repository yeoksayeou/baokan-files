Title: New Ceylonese Ambassador to China Calls on Premier Zhou

Author:

Date: 1966-01-06
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, January 5th - The new Ceylonese Ambassador to China, Don Ben. Ru. Gunawardena, paid a courtesy call on Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council this evening.

    Premier Zhou Enlai held a cordial and friendly conversation with Ambassador Gunawardena.

    Present at the meeting were Chen Mianzhi, Deputy Director of the First Asian Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Zhou Min, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department.

### 锡兰新任驻中国大使拜会周总理

1966-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

    锡兰新任驻中国大使拜会周总理
    新华社五日讯　锡兰新任驻中国大使唐·本·鲁·古纳瓦德纳，今晚拜会了国务院总理周恩来。
    周恩来总理同古纳瓦德纳大使进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    拜会时在座的，有外交部第一亚洲司副司长陈勉之，礼宾司副司长周敏。
