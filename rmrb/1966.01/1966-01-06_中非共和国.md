Title: Central African Republic
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-06
Edition: 4

　　Central African Republic is located in central Africa and is a landlocked country. It is bordered by Chad to the north, Congo (Brazzaville) and Congo (Leopoldville) to the south, Cameroon to the west, and Sudan to the east. It has an area of 617,000 square kilometers and a population of 1.3 million. The population is mainly composed of tribes of the Sudanese language family. The capital is Bangui, with a population of approximately 100,000.

### 中非共和国

1966-01-06
第4版()
专栏：资料

    中非共和国
    中非共和国位于非洲中部，是一个内陆国家。它北靠乍得，南与刚果（布）和刚果（利）接壤，西是喀麦隆，东是苏丹。面积六十一万七千平方公里，人口一百三十万。主要是苏丹语系的部族。首都在班吉，约有十万人。
