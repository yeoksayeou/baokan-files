    Three Short Remarks
    American commentator Walter Lippmann admitted that the American war of aggression against Vietnam "actually backfired." Because the United States originally wanted to "stop revolutionary wars," the war has made it "very difficult for the United States to launch anti-revolutionary activities anywhere."
    This Washington strategist seems to have a relatively clear head, because he has after all realized that the United States' war of aggression against Vietnam backfired. However, one thing that people like Lippmann will never understand is that the reactionary policies implemented by imperialism and reactionaries will always lead to their opposite. The reason why the US imperialism's counter-revolutionary global strategy to dominate the world is doomed to failure is precisely because it is overreaching.

    Some advisors to the Sato government advocate so-called "seeking new approaches—re-understanding Japan."
    How to "seek" it? It is said that it is necessary to strengthen "defense awareness" and "Self-Defense Forces" in order to "assist" the United States in dealing with China.
    In this way, there is no question of "advocating" anything; it is nothing more than bringing out the Bushido spirit again; this "approach" is not "new" at all, but merely repeating the mistakes of Tojo.

### 三言两语

1966-01-12
第4版()
专栏：

    三言两语
    美国评论员李普曼承认，美国侵越战争“实际上起了搬起石头砸了自己脚的作用”。因为美国原来想“制止革命战争”，结果却使美国在“任何地方发动反对革命的活动感到很困难”。
    这个华盛顿的策士似乎头脑还是比较清醒的，因为他毕竟看到了美国进行侵越战争是搬起石头砸了自己的脚。但是，有一点是李普曼之流永远不会懂得的，这就是：帝国主义和反动派实行的反动政策，总是要走向它的反面。美帝国主义妄图独霸世界的反革命全球战略之所以注定要失败，就是因为它力不从心。
                              ×　　　×
    佐藤政府的一些顾问提倡所谓“寻求新的进路——重新认识日本”。
    怎样“寻求”法呢？据说就是要加强“防卫意识”和“自卫队”，以便“协助”美国对付中国。
    这样，根本谈不上什么“提倡”，那不过是把武士道重新搬出来；如此“进路”也一点不“新”，不过是重蹈东条的覆辙。
