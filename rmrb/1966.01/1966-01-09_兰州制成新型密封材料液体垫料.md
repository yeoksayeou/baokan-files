    Lanzhou Manufactures New Type of Sealing Material: Liquid Gasket
    A new type of sealing material with wide applications – liquid gasket – has recently been successfully trial-produced at the Lanzhou Refinery.
    This gasket is made by mixing various high molecular compounds with fillers, additives, and other raw materials. Applying a thin layer of liquid gasket to the connections of various pipelines, equipment, etc., can completely seal and prevent leakage.
    In petroleum and chemical enterprises, how to ensure tight and leak-proof connections between pipelines and equipment, and avoid waste of raw materials and production safety hazards, is a very important issue. In the past, China generally used solid gaskets, such as asbestos boards, rubber sheets, metal washers, or other gaskets for sealing. Solid gaskets could not be used for threaded connections, pipe clamp connections, and socket connections of pipes. In these cases, methods such as improving mechanical processing precision were mostly used to compensate. However, these methods are not ideal, and some waste manpower and increase costs. Using liquid gasket sealing can fundamentally eliminate leakage, which is convenient and economical.

### 兰州制成新型密封材料液体垫料

1966-01-09
第2版()
专栏：新建设  新产品

    兰州制成新型密封材料液体垫料
    具有广泛用途的新型密封材料——液体垫料，最近在兰州炼油厂试制成功。
    这种垫料是由许多种高分子化合物加充填剂和助剂等原料混合搅拌制成的。在各类管线、设备等连接处涂上一层很薄的液体垫料，就能完全密封，防止泄漏。
    在石油和化工等企业中，管线和设备在连接的地方如何做到严密不漏，避免造成原料的浪费和生产上的不安全，这是一个很重要的问题。过去我国一般都是采用固体垫料，如石棉板、橡胶片、金属垫圈或其它垫片等进行密封。在遇到管子的丝扣连接、管箍连接以及承插连接等地方，固体垫料还不能使用。在这种情况下，大都采用提高机械加工精度等办法来补救。但是这些办法都不够理想，有的还浪费人力，增大成本。采用液体垫料密封，就可以从根本上杜绝泄漏，既方便，又经济。
