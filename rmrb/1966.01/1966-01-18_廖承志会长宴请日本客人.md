Title: Liao Chengzhi Hosts Banquet for Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-01-18
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, January 17th: Liao Chengzhi, President of the China-Japan Friendship Association, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the delegation from the Japan-China Trade Promotion Association, led by head Kawase Ikkan and Secretary-General Sumi Masanori, on their friendly visit to China.
    Liao Chengzhi and Kawase Ikkan spoke at the banquet. They wished for further development of non-governmental trade between China and Japan and the friendly relations between the peoples of the two countries.
    Responsible persons from relevant departments, including Zhang Huadong, Liu Xiwu, and Wang Xiaoyun, attended the banquet. Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji, who is in Beijing, was also invited to attend the banquet.
    Before the banquet, Liao Chengzhi met with all members of the delegation and held friendly talks with them.

### 廖承志会长宴请日本客人

1966-01-18
第4版()
专栏：

    廖承志会长宴请日本客人
    新华社十七日讯　中国日本友好协会会长廖承志今晚举行宴会，欢迎由团长川濑一贯、秘书长隅井正典率领的日中贸易促进会友好访华代表团。
    廖承志和川濑一贯在宴会上先后讲话。他们祝愿中日民间贸易和中日两国人民的友好关系进一步发展。
    出席宴会的，有张化东、刘希文、王晓云等有关方面负责人。在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也应邀出席了宴会。
    宴会前，廖承志接见了代表团全体成员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
