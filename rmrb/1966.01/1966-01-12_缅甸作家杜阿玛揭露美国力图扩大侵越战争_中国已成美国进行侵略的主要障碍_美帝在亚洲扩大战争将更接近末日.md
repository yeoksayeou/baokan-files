Title: Burmese Writer Daw Amar Exposes U.S. Efforts to Expand the War of Aggression Against Vietnam; China Has Become the Main Obstacle to U.S. Aggression; Expanding the War in Asia Will Bring U.S. Imperialism Closer to Its Doomsday
Author:
Date: 1966-01-12
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 10th – Burmese renowned female writer Daw Amar published articles in the *People's Daily* on the 9th and 10th consecutively, warning the Johnson administration, saying, "The more U.S. imperialism expands its war of aggression in Asia, the closer it will get to its doomsday."

    Commenting on Johnson's recent "peace" swindle, she said, "The activities of the U.S. imperialists have shown that if they cannot impose that form of 'negotiation' on the Vietnamese people, they will expand their war of aggression against Vietnam."

    Daw Amar said, "When U.S. imperialists formulate strategies to exploit and dominate the world, they see that the main obstacle to implementing this strategy is none other than China. Therefore, they are eager to attack and crush China."

    She said, "China firmly holds high the banner of Marxism-Leninism and will never surrender or compromise with imperialism. China earnestly practices proletarian internationalism and supports all national liberation movements in the world. These are incompatible with the U.S. policy of aggression and war." Therefore, U.S. imperialism is eager to topple China, this towering pillar that hinders its aggression.

    She said that once a Sino-U.S. war breaks out, the United States will have to face a "ground war in Asia," which is what the United States fears most. Since the U.S. imperialists, equipped with first-class weapons, cannot even defeat the people of South Vietnam, they will certainly not be able to defeat countries like China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, whose people are well-organized and like an impregnable wall of bronze and iron.

    She said that if the U.S. imperialists expand the Vietnam War into an Asian war, then they will certainly not get what they want, but rather get consequences they do not expect.

    She said, "The flames of anti-imperialism will burn brighter and brighter, and the imperialists, their accomplices, and international right-wing reactionaries will surely be engulfed by the flames of anti-imperialism."

### 缅甸作家杜阿玛揭露美国力图扩大侵越战争  中国已成美国进行侵略的主要障碍  美帝在亚洲扩大战争将更接近末日

1966-01-12
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸作家杜阿玛揭露美国力图扩大侵越战争
    中国已成美国进行侵略的主要障碍
    美帝在亚洲扩大战争将更接近末日
    新华社仰光十日电  缅甸著名女作家杜阿玛在《人民报》九、十两日连续发表文章警告约翰逊政府说：“美帝国主义越扩大它在亚洲的侵略战争，就会越接近它的末日。”
    她在评论约翰逊最近的“和平”骗局时说：“美帝国主义者的活动已经表明，如果他们不能把那种形式的‘谈判’强加给越南人民，他们就要扩大对越南的侵略战争。”
    杜阿玛说：“美帝国主义者在制定剥削和统治全世界的战略的时候，他们看到执行这一战略的主要障碍莫过于中国。因此，他们急于进攻和粉碎中国。”
    她说：“中国坚定地高举马克思列宁主义，决不向帝国主义屈服或妥协。中国切实地执行无产阶级国际主义，支持全世界一切民族解放运动，这些都是同美国的侵略和战争政策不相容的。”因此，美帝国主义就急于要推倒妨碍它进行侵略的中国这个擎天巨柱。
    她说，一旦爆发中、美战争，美国就不得不面对“在亚洲的地面战争”，而这是美国所最害怕的。既然用第一流的武器装备起来的美帝国主义者，连南越人民都打不赢，他们决战胜不了象中国和越南民主共和国这样的国家，这两个国家的人民组织得很好，象一堵铜墙铁壁。
    她说，如果美帝国主义者把越南战争扩大为亚洲战争，那么他们肯定不能如愿以偿，而是得到他们料想不到的后果。
    她说：“反帝的火焰将越烧越旺，帝国主义者、他们的同谋者和国际右翼反动派肯定会被反帝的火焰所吞没。”
