Title: Johnson Madly Expands Military Under the Guise of Peace: US Monthly Draft to Increase to 80,000
Author:
Date: 1966-01-11
Edition: 3

    Johnson Madly Expands Military Under the Guise of Peace
    US Monthly Draft to Increase to 80,000
    (Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 10th) Lewis Hershey, Director of the US National Selective Service System, announced on the 9th that the conscription authorities plan to increase the monthly draft quota from the current 30,000-40,000 to 80,000, which is the peak level during the Korean War.
    This new war measure further exposes that the Johnson administration's current so-called "peace offensive" is merely to buy time to prepare for further expanding the war of aggression against Vietnam.
    Since the end of last year, Hershey has convened a series of meetings with heads of conscription agencies from all states to plan measures to expand the scope of conscription and intensify the search for cannon fodder to cope with the difficulty of insufficient troops for the invasion of Vietnam and to prepare manpower for further expansion of the war.
    In a statement to reporters after the meeting, Hershey said that he estimated that the demand for US military manpower in the Vietnam War would be "similar" to that during the Korean War, i.e., about 80,000 per month. He also revealed that after the conclusion of this series of meetings currently being held, it might be decided to reinstate the conscription regulations of the Korean War period and further relax the scope of conscription. He indicated that university students would be the first to bear the brunt in this regard. Malone, head of the Tennessee conscription agency who participated in the meeting, also revealed that in order to greatly increase the conscription quota, "we will have to squeeze students."
    The Associated Press pointed out in its report on the conscription conference that the need to expand the scope of conscription is because "conscripting approximately 40,000 people a month in recent months has reduced the number of eligible nineteen-year-olds."

### 约翰逊在和平烟幕下疯狂扩军  美国每月征兵将增至八万人

1966-01-11
第3版()
专栏：

    约翰逊在和平烟幕下疯狂扩军
    美国每月征兵将增至八万人
    据新华社十日讯  华盛顿消息：美国全国征兵局局长刘易斯·赫尔希九日宣布，征兵当局计划把每月征兵名额由目前的三四万人增加到八万人，即侵朝战争时期的最高峰。
    这一新的战争措施，进一步暴露约翰逊政府目前发起所谓“和平攻势”，只不过是为了争取时间，为进一步扩大侵越战争作准备。
    赫尔希从去年年底起，召集全国各州征兵机构负责人举行了一系列会议，策划扩大征兵范围，加紧搜罗炮灰的措施，以应付侵越兵力不足的困难，为进一步扩大战争准备人力。
    赫尔希在会后对记者发表谈话时说，他估计越南战争对美军人力的需求，将会同朝鲜战争期间“相似”，即每月约八万人。他还透露，在目前举行的这一系列会议结束之后，可能决定恢复朝鲜战争时期的征兵条例，进一步放宽征兵范围。他表示，在这方面，首当其冲的将是大学学生。参加会议的田纳西州征兵机构负责人马龙也透露，为了大大增加征兵名额，“我们将不得不去压榨学生。”
    美联社在报道这次征兵会议时指出，需要扩大征兵范围，是因为“最近几个月每月征大约四万人入伍，已经使适龄的十九岁的人减少了”。
