Title: U.S. and South Vietnamese Puppet Army Aircraft and Warships Continuously Invade Cambodia; Cambodian Border Army and Civilians Heroically Repel the Enemy

Author: 
Date: 1966-01-26
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, January 25th: According to the Cambodian News Agency on January 25th: On January 5th, a South Vietnamese puppet army vessel intruded along the Mekong River near Cham Samnar village in Kandal province, Cambodia. Local Cambodian armed forces opened fire on the vessel, forcing it to turn around and flee.
    According to reports from the Cambodian News Agency on January 22nd and 23rd, U.S.—South Vietnamese puppet army aircraft intruded into the airspace of Takeo, Svay Rieng, Prey Veng, Kampong Cham, and Kandal provinces in Cambodia a total of 23 times from the 3rd to the 19th. On the afternoon of January 9th, the South Vietnamese puppet army shelled the border area of Prey Veng province in Cambodia. On the same afternoon, two South Vietnamese puppet soldiers intruded into the Tbaeng Wein area of Svay Rieng province, Cambodia.
    On the morning of January 10th, thirty South Vietnamese puppet soldiers intruded approximately one kilometer into Svay Rieng province, Cambodia. After exchanging fire with local Cambodian armed villagers, these intruders retreated back into South Vietnamese territory.
    On the morning of January 14th, twenty-two South Vietnamese puppet soldiers intruded approximately two kilometers into the village of Va Bavet in Svay Rieng province, Cambodia. After a half-hour exchange of fire with Cambodian border guards, the invaders withdrew into South Vietnamese territory. On the morning of January 10th, thirty South Vietnamese puppet soldiers intruded approximately one kilometer into Svay Rieng province, Cambodia. The intruders exchanged fire with Cambodian armed villagers for more than twenty minutes, and then withdrew into South Vietnamese territory. On the morning of January 15th, a Dakota aircraft of the South Vietnamese puppet army intruded approximately three kilometers into Cambodian airspace. After being hit by artillery fire from the local border guards, the plane fled, trailing a plume of black smoke.

### 美国和南越伪军飞机舰艇不断侵入柬埔寨  柬边境军民英勇击退敌人

1966-01-26
第3版()
专栏：

    美国和南越伪军飞机舰艇不断侵入柬埔寨
    柬边境军民英勇击退敌人
    据新华社金边二十五日电　据柬埔寨新闻社二十五日报道：一月五日，一艘南越伪军的舰艇沿湄公河侵入到柬埔寨干丹省湛三纳村附近。当地柬埔寨武装部队向这只舰艇开火，迫使它转身逃走。
    另据柬埔寨新闻社二十二和二十三日报道，美国—南越伪军飞机从三日到十九日侵入柬埔寨茶胶、柴桢、波萝勉、磅湛、干丹等省上空共达二十三次。九日下午，南越伪军向柬埔寨波萝勉省边境地区开炮。同一天下午，两名南越伪军侵入柬埔寨柴桢省塔卞温地方。
    十日上午，南越伪军三十人侵入柬埔寨柴桢省境内深达约一公里。在同当地柬埔寨武装村民交火后，这些入侵者退回南越境内。
    一月十四日上午，南越伪军二十二人侵入柬埔寨境内达两公里左右的柴桢省瓦巴韦恰村。侵略者在同柬埔寨边防人员交火半小时之后，撤回南越境内。一月十日上午，南越伪军三十人侵入柬埔寨柴桢省境内达一公里左右。入侵者同柬埔寨武装村民交火二十多分钟，然后撤回南越境内。一月十五日上午，南越伪军的一架达科他式飞机侵入柬埔寨领空达三公里左右。这架飞机被当地边防军的炮火击中后，拖着一股黑烟溜走。
