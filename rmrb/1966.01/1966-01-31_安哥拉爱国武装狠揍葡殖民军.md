Title: Angolan Patriotic Armed Forces Vigorously Beat Portuguese Colonial Army
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-31
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Brazzaville - Angolan patriotic armed forces, active in the northern Cabinda region, continuously attacked Portuguese colonial troops during the last week of last year.
    The People's Movement for the Liberation of Angola announced here on the 8th that a reconnaissance patrol team of the People's Movement for the Liberation of Angola engaged in a battle with a Portuguese colonial army patrol on December 27th, two kilometers away from the enemy barracks of Sanga in Cabinda. Under the heroic attack of the patriotic armed forces, the enemy fled in all directions, leaving behind five bodies.
    On December 30th, two platoons of the enemy attempted to cross the Mikonje region of Cabinda (the border area of the controlled area of the Angolan Patriotic Armed Forces). They were met with head-on mortar fire from the Angolan patriotic armed forces, suffered heavy losses, and were forced to retreat to their own outposts.

### 安哥拉爱国武装狠揍葡殖民军

1966-01-31
第5版()
专栏：

    安哥拉爱国武装狠揍葡殖民军
    新华社布拉柴维尔电　活跃在北部喀奔达地区的安哥拉爱国武装力量，在去年最后一周连续打击葡萄牙殖民军。
    安哥拉人民解放运动八日在这里发表战报说，安哥拉人民解放运动的一支侦察巡逻队十二月二十七日在离喀奔达的桑加的敌人兵营两公里的地方同葡萄牙殖民军巡逻队发生遭遇战。在爱国武装英勇打击下，敌人四散逃命，留下五具尸体。
    十二月三十日，两排敌人企图越过喀奔达的米孔热地区（安哥拉爱国武装部队控制区的边界地区）。他们遭到安哥拉爱国武装的迫击炮火的迎头痛击，受到重大损失，被迫退回到他们自己的哨所。
