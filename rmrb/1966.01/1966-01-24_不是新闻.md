Title: Not News
Author:
Date: 1966-01-24
Edition: 3

    Not News
    The American *New York Times* published an article on the 16th, saying that large American monopoly capitalist groups are making huge profits from the Johnson administration's ever-increasing war budget. The newspaper wrote: "Just look at the list of the largest contractors in defense in the last fiscal year, and it is clear who this money ends up with and who it will end up with in the next fiscal year."
    Everyone knows that America's monopoly capitalist groups have always made fortunes from war. Without even looking at the contractor list, one can imagine who this money ends up with. What the *New York Times* considers news is actually not news at all.

### 不是新闻

1966-01-24
第3版()
专栏：三言两语

    不是新闻
    美国《纽约时报》十六日刊登文章说，美国大垄断资本集团从约翰逊政府的不断增大的战争预算中大赚其钱。这家报纸写道：“只要看一看上一财政年度在国防方面最大的承包商名单，就可以一目了然地看出，这笔钱落在谁手里，在下一财政年度这些钱又将落在谁手里。”
    谁都知道，美国的垄断资本集团从来就是靠战争发财的。用不着看承包商名单，也就可以想象得到这笔钱落在谁手里了。《纽约时报》认为是新闻其实并不是什么新闻。
