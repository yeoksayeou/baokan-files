Title: West Germany: Severe Setbacks and Deep Worries

Author: 
Date: 1966-01-20
Edition: 5

    The mood in our country at the end of this year is not good. Public opinion researchers confirm this to us.
    What happened this year? What is it that has led to a low mood in these twelve months? Public opinion researcher Allenbach says it is a worry about economic welfare. This worry can be found in the significant loss of purchasing power of our Deutsche Mark.
    Tonight, the New Year's bell announces the beginning of a new year, but within the bell sounds old worries are rippling.
                        West German *Bonn Review*, December 31, 1965
    1965 was a year full of political and diplomatic setbacks for the Federal Republic of Germany.
    The "economic miracle" that the Federal Republic was so proud of has begun to falter for the first time.
                        West German *Frankfurt Review*,
                            December 31, 1965

### 西德：挫折严重忧虑重重

1966-01-20
第5版()
专栏：

    西德：挫折严重忧虑重重
    今年年终在我国的情绪是不好的。民意研究者向我们证实这一点。
    这一年发生了什么事呢？是什么东西在这十二个月中导致了情绪的低落呢？民意研究家阿伦巴赫说，是对经济福利的忧虑。这种忧虑可以在我们的德国马克购买力的大大丧失中寻找。
    今天夜里新年的钟声宣告了新的一年的开始，但在钟声里面却荡漾着老的忧虑。西德《波恩评论报》一九六五年十二月三十一日
    一九六五年对于德意志联邦共和国来说是充满着政治和外交挫折的一年。
    联邦共和国足以炫耀的“经济奇迹”已第一次开始动摇。
                        西德《法兰克福评论报》
                            一九六五年十二月三十一日
