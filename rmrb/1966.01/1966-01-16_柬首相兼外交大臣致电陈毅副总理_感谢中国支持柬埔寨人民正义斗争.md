Title: Cambodian Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Cables Vice Premier Chen Yi to Thank China for Supporting the Cambodian People's Just Struggle
Author:
Date: 1966-01-16
Edition: 4

    Cambodian Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Cables Vice Premier Chen Yi
    Thanking China for Supporting the Cambodian People's Just Struggle
    Xinhua News Agency, January 15th – Chen Yi, Vice Premier and Foreign Minister of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, received a cable from the Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, Norodom Kantol, on January 13th. The cable expressed gratitude for the Chinese Foreign Ministry's statement on January 3rd, which strongly condemned U.S. imperialism's instigation of Thailand to invade Cambodia and expressed full support for Cambodia's just struggle to defend its independence. The full text of the cable is as follows:
    To His Excellency Chen Yi, Vice Premier and Foreign Minister of the Government of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:
    Your Excellency,
    I am honored to inform Your Excellency that I was very pleased to learn of the statement issued by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China on January 3, 1966, in support of our country following the Thai aggression against the O Smach border post in Cambodia on December 30 and 31, 1965. On behalf of the Cambodian people and the Royal Government, and in my own name, I would like to express to Your Excellency my most sincere and deepest gratitude for the unwavering and consistent support that the great and fraternal people and government of the People's Republic of China have always given us. This is a precious encouragement to us peace-loving Cambodians, encouraging us in our struggle against U.S. imperialism and its agents who are attempting to extend the war to the peaceful and neutral Kingdom of Cambodia.
    Please accept my highest consideration.
            Prime Minister and Foreign Minister
            Norodom Kantol, January 13, 1966, Phnom Penh

### 柬首相兼外交大臣致电陈毅副总理  感谢中国支持柬埔寨人民正义斗争

1966-01-16
第4版()
专栏：

    柬首相兼外交大臣致电陈毅副总理
    感谢中国支持柬埔寨人民正义斗争
    新华社十五日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅收到了柬埔寨王国首相兼外交大臣诺罗敦·康托尔一月十三日发来的电报，电报表示感谢中国外交部一月三日发表的强烈谴责美帝国主义策动泰国侵略柬埔寨，并且表示全力支持柬埔寨保卫独立的正义斗争的声明。电报全文如下：北京中华人民共和国政府副总理兼
  外交部长陈毅先生阁下阁下：
    我荣幸地告诉阁下，我非常愉快地获悉中华人民共和国外交部在一九六五年十二月三十日和三十一日泰国向柬埔寨奥斯马边防哨所发动侵略后于一九六六年一月三日发表了支持我国的声明。我谨代表柬埔寨人民和王国政府，并以我本人的名义，向阁下表示最热诚的、最深切的谢意，感谢伟大的、兄弟的中华人民共和国人民和政府始终不渝地给予我们的一贯支持。这是对我们爱好和平的柬埔寨人的珍贵的鼓舞，鼓舞着我们为反对企图把战争扩大到和平中立的柬埔寨王国的美帝国主义及其代理人而进行的斗争。
    顺致最崇高的敬意。
        首相兼外交大臣
          诺罗敦·康托尔一九六六年一月十三日于金边
