Title: Patriotic Soldiers of Portuguese Guinea Inflict Heavy Losses on the Enemy

Author: 
Date: 1966-01-31
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Dakar, January 27th: Patriotic soldiers of Portuguese Guinea have recently launched successive attacks in the northern region of Portuguese Guinea, killing 21 Portuguese colonial soldiers and wounding more than 40.

    A communiqué issued here on January 27th by the General Secretariat of the African Independence Party of Portuguese Guinea and Cape Verde stated that on January 8th and 10th, guerrillas launched two attacks on enemy troops not far from the Portuguese military base in Balo, killing a total of six enemy soldiers and wounding more than ten. The communiqué said that on January 12th, the guerrillas ambushed a Portuguese colonial military unit 5 kilometers from Balo, destroying a vehicle carrying enemy troops, killing 15 enemies, and wounding more than 30.

    Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, reports: According to a communiqué issued here on the 14th by Amílcar Cabral, Secretary-General of the African Independence Party of Guinea (Portuguese) and Cape Verde, patriotic armed forces of Portuguese Guinea once again repelled the Portuguese colonial army's invasion of the liberated areas last December.

    The communiqué said that in the Gabú region of the east, patriotic armed forces attacked Portuguese colonial troops in Buluntuma and Kangkuilifa on December 13th and 22nd of last year, killing more than 80 enemy soldiers, capturing two African mercenaries and one Portuguese soldier, and seizing a large quantity of weapons and supplies.

### 葡属几内亚爱国战士予敌重创

1966-01-31
第5版()
专栏：

    葡属几内亚爱国战士予敌重创
    新华社达喀尔二十七日电　葡属几内亚爱国战士最近在葡属几内亚北部地区连续出击，打死葡萄牙殖民军二十一名，打伤四十多名。
    葡属几内亚和佛得角非洲独立党总书记处二十七日在这里发表的公报说，一月八日和十日，游击队在距离巴罗的葡萄牙军基地不远的地方两次出击敌军，共打死敌军六名，打伤十多名。公报说，游击队一月十二日在距巴罗五公里的地方，伏击了一支葡萄牙殖民军，击毁一辆载着敌军的车辆，打死敌人十五名，打伤三十多名。
    据新华社科纳克里电　据（葡属）几内亚和佛得角非洲独立党总书记阿米卡尔·卡布拉尔十四日在这里发表的公报，葡属几内亚爱国武装在去年十二月内再一次击退了葡萄牙殖民军对解放区的进犯。
    公报说，在东部加布地区，爱国武装去年十二月十三日、二十二日在布伦图马和康奎利法等地袭击了葡萄牙殖民军，打死敌军八十多名，活捉两名非洲雇佣兵和一名葡萄牙士兵，缴获了大量武器和物资。
