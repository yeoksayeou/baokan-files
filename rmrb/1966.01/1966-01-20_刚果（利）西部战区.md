　　The Congo (L) Western War Zone
　　In January 1964, Congolese patriots first ignited the flames of armed struggle in the Kwilu region of the west, opening up the first battlefield in Africa to directly oppose US imperialism and its lackeys.
　　The Congo Western War Zone includes Léopoldville Province and Kasai Province. The people of the Kwilu region in this war zone have a long tradition of glorious struggle and strong revolutionary sentiments, and are extremely dissatisfied with the rule of the puppet regime.
　　Although the people's armed forces in the Western War Zone initially numbered only dozens of people and possessed only bows, arrows, spears, and machetes, they grew rapidly in battle. By the time they raised the flag of uprising in Kwilu in January 1964, they had grown to several hundred people, and by the end of February, they had grown to 10,000, constantly seizing weapons from the enemy to equip themselves.
　　For two years, the patriotic armed forces of the Congo Western War Zone have overcome numerous difficulties and persisted in fighting. They often strike the enemy in unpredictable ways, establishing the Congo's first revolutionary base area in the vast rural and jungle areas, and constantly crushing the puppet regime and starting to establish new government organs everywhere.
　　The leader of the Congo Western War Zone is Pierre Mulele. He was a comrade-in-arms of Lumumba, the Secretary-General of the African Solidarity Party, and is now one of the leaders of the Congo Revolutionary Supreme Council.

### 刚果（利）西部战区

1966-01-20
第4版()
专栏：资料

    刚果（利）西部战区
    一九六四年一月，刚果爱国者在西部克韦卢地区首先燃起了武装斗争的烽火，在非洲开辟了第一个用武装直接反对美帝国主义及其走狗的战场。
    刚果西部战区包括利奥波德维尔省和开赛省。这个战区的克韦卢地区人民，有着长期光荣斗争的传统和强烈的革命情绪，对于傀儡政权的统治十分不满。
    尽管开始时西部战区人民武装只有数十人，并且只拥有弓箭、长矛和弯刀，但是，它在战斗中迅速成长壮大。到了一九六四年一月，在克韦卢首举义旗时，已发展至数百人，二月底更发展至一万人，并且不断地从敌人手里夺取武器来装备自己。
    两年来，刚果西部战区的爱国武装克服了重重困难，始终坚持战斗，他们经常神出鬼没地打击敌人，在广大农村和丛林地带建立了刚果的第一个革命根据地，并不断地在各地粉碎傀儡政权和着手建立新的政权机构。
    刚果西部战区的领导人是皮埃尔·谬勒尔。他是卢蒙巴的战友，非洲团结党总书记，现在是刚果革命最高委员会的领导人之一。
