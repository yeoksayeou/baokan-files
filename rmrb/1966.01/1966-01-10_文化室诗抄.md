    Poetry Selections from Cultural Centers
    The red and bright sun shines overhead,
    Chairman Mao's works are infinitely good,
    Every word and every sentence is remembered in our hearts,
    Every sentence is a precious treasure.
        Feng Ying, Xuanhua Commune, Gaotai County, Gansu Province

      Chairman Mao's works in hand,
      The sun rises in the heart;
      Literacy textbook in hand,
      Eyes shine brightly;
      The sun rises, eyes shine brightly,
      The cultural center is our university.
        Folk songs compiled by the youth of Zhuhe Township, Zhaojue County, Liangshan Yi Autonomous Prefecture

    A blackboard hangs in the street,
    With articles and pictures.
    The blackboard is like a golden key,
    The masses feel happy at first glance.
        Masses of Hezhang Brigade, Nanhan Commune, Xiangyuan County, Shanxi Province

    The grass on Mount Naipa is green and verdant,
    Flocks of sheep graze on the mountain,
    The sheep are fat, the wool is thick,
    Select five jin of the best from ten thousand jin of wool,
    Make the best and most beautiful Charwa,
    Send it across mountains and rivers to Beijing,
    To be presented to our beloved Chairman Mao,
    Please accept this heart of the Yi people.
      Note (Charwa: A type of felt cloak) Compiled by the Cultural Center of Zhongxin Township, Tuowu Yi Autonomous Region, Mianning County, Sichuan Province

### 文化室诗抄

1966-01-10
第6版()
专栏：

    文化室诗抄红通通的太阳当头照，毛主席著作无限好，一言一语记心中，句句都是宝中宝。
    甘肃省高台县宣化公社冯应
      基毛主席著作捧手上，心窝里面出太阳；识字课本捧手上，眼睛里面放光芒；出太阳，放光芒，文化室是我们的大学堂。
    凉山彝族自治州昭觉县竹核
      乡青年们编的民歌一块黑板街头挂，有文章来有图画。板报象把金钥匙，群众一看心开花。
    山西省襄垣县南邯公社合漳
      大队群众编乃帕山的草儿青又青，山上的羊儿一群群，羊儿肥，羊毛深，万斤羊毛选五斤，做件最好最美的察尔瓦，千山万水送北京，献给敬爱的毛主席，请你收下彝家的一片心。
  注（察尔瓦：披毡的一种）四川省冕宁县拖乌彝族自治区中心乡文化室编
