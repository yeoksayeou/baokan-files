Title: President Ayub Khan Thanks Chairman Liu and Premier Zhou for Condolences on Hurricane Disaster in East Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1966-01-09
Edition: 4

    Thanking for Condolences on Hurricane Disaster in East Pakistan
    President Ayub Khan Cables Chairman Liu and Premier Zhou
    Xinhua News Agency, January 8th. Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Premier Zhou Enlai received a cable from President Ayub Khan of Pakistan, thanking them for the telegram of condolence sent to him regarding the recent hurricane disaster in East Pakistan.
    President Ayub Khan's telegram said: "On behalf of the Government and people of Pakistan, and in my own name, I thank Their Excellencies, the Chinese Government, and the Chinese people for the telegram of condolence issued regarding the recent hurricane disaster in East Pakistan. We are deeply moved by the concern shown by Their Excellencies for the unfortunate victims of the hurricane.
    We are also very grateful for the generous donation made by the Chinese Red Cross Society to help the hurricane victims."



**Named Entities**


刘少奇 Liu Shaoqi
周恩来 Zhou Enlai
阿尤布·汗 Ayub Khan
巴基斯坦 Pakistan
东巴基斯坦 East Pakistan
中国红十字会 Chinese Red Cross Society

### 感谢对东巴基斯坦遭受飓风灾害的慰问  阿尤布·汗总统电刘主席周总理

1966-01-09
第4版()
专栏：

    感谢对东巴基斯坦遭受飓风灾害的慰问
    阿尤布·汗总统电刘主席周总理
    新华社八日讯　刘少奇主席和周恩来总理收到了巴基斯坦阿尤布·汗总统的一封电报，感谢他们对东巴基斯坦最近遭受飓风灾害打给他的慰问电。
    阿尤布·汗总统的电报说：“我代表巴基斯坦政府和人民，并且以我个人的名义，感谢两位阁下、中国政府和中国人民为东巴基斯坦最近遭受飓风灾害所发出的慰问电。两位阁下对不幸的飓风受难者所表示的关心使我们深为感动。
    我们也十分感谢中国红十字会为帮助飓风灾民所作的慷慨捐献。”
