Title: Albanian Deputy Minister of Agriculture Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1966-01-18
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, January 17th -- Mendor Permeti, Albanian Deputy Minister of Agriculture, left Beijing by plane this afternoon via Moscow to return home.
    Among those who saw him off at the airport were Jiang Ming, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and Zhu Rong, Vice Minister of Agriculture. Albanian Ambassador to China, Nesti Nase, and Commercial Counselor Keço Kasabi, also went to the airport to see him off.

### 阿尔巴尼亚农业部副部长离京回国

1966-01-18
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚农业部副部长离京回国
    新华社十七日讯　阿尔巴尼亚农业部副部长门道尔·佩尔梅蒂，今天下午乘飞机离开北京经莫斯科回国。
    前往机场送行的，有对外贸易部副部长江明、农业部副部长朱荣等。阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使奈斯蒂·纳赛和商务参赞凯乔·卡萨比等也到机场送行。
