Title: Colorful Brush: Written to Welcome the Third Five-Year Plan
Author: Tian Jian
Date: 1966-01-26
Edition: 6

    Colorful Brush
    Written to Welcome the Third Five-Year Plan
    Tian Jian
    I
    New people,
    New world –
    Colorful brush,
    Draw a heavenly ladder.
    Our hands were liberated by the Party,
    Our unwavering loyalty is dyed red by the Party.
    I ride high mountains like horses,
    I scoop up the seas as if from a ladle.
    The fiery years reflect the red flag,
    Daqing and Dazhai are legendary miracles.
    Our hands are the golden key,
    To dress the earth in new clothes again.
    Ah, my motherland,
    Youth should be dedicated to you.
    Ah, socialism,
    Life should be defended for you.

    II
    Worker-peasant courage,
    Soldier's heart –
    Most heroic,
    Most bright.
    Glad to see the knife at the jackal's neck,
    Red clouds in the sky squeeze out dark clouds.
    Who says one can coexist with beasts?
    I say, I'd rather have "humanity reborn."
    Build a red pavilion,
    For comrades all over the world to sing together.
    Red mountains, red waters, Red Plum Ridge,
    Each branch striving to be evergreen.
    Our hands move mountains,
    New Yu Gong of socialism.
    The clang of hammers,
    Playing the strongest sound of the era.

    III
    Mountains beat drums,
    Winds blow horns –
    Leap forward!
    New China.
    A cry on the heroes' bridge,
    Friends from all over the world smile with you.
    A laugh on the heroes' bridge,
    Ten thousand miles of wind and clouds beckoned in hand.
    To climb dangerous peaks, please build bridges,
    To split high mountains, please light the cannon.
    News of victory is like snowflakes,
    Instantly turning into a great river tide.
    Imperialism, dare you block the way?
    You cannot stop this cannon fire.
    Class enemies, dare you mock?
    You cannot suppress this laughter.

    IV
    Revolutionary horn,
    Horns sounding –
    East wind sings,
    Great river flows.
    "Singing of one rice stalk bearing nine ears,"
    The sun rises gradually in our hearts.
    "Singing makes the spring breeze smile,"
    The furnace roars, steel sings.
    A new generation walks under the red flag,
    Blooming like flowers, in their youth.
    Never looking back in the face of raging waves,
    Seeing a red heart in the wind and waves.
    Listen to us blow the revolutionary horn,
    Singing of a sky full of red.
    Listen to us sing of Mao Zedong,
    To be the masters of the universe.

    V
    New people,
    New world –
    Colorful brush,
    Draw a heavenly ladder.
    Where does the heavenly ladder lead?
    Step by step, climbing to the peak.
    The colorful brush writes poems of fire,
    To leave no slaves in the world.
    Sing an international song,
    The great river flows east, waves for thousands of miles.
    The crescent moon becomes a bridge, the stars become flowers,
    The shackles of slaves are cast into golden plows.
    Hundreds of millions of proletarian brothers,
    Self-reliance is the weapon;
    Look, the world of tomorrow,
    Must be communism!
    Written in the winter of 1965

### 七彩笔  为迎接第三个五年计划作
田间
1966-01-26
第6版()
专栏：

    七彩笔
    为迎接第三个五年计划作
    田间
    一
    新的人，
    新天地——
    七彩笔，
    绘天梯。
    双手是党解放的，
    赤胆是党染红的。
    高山当马任我骑，
    大海当瓢任我提。
    火红的年月映赤旗，
    大庆大寨啊传奇迹。
    双手就是金钥匙，
    再为大地取新衣。
    啊，我的祖国，
    青春应当献给你。
    啊，社会主义，
    生命应当捍卫你。
    二
    工农胆，
    战士心——
    最英勇，
    最光明。
    喜看刀在豺狼颈，
    天上红云挤乌云。
    谁说和野兽能共处，
    我说宁肯“人类再生”。
    造一座红色楼台，
    天下战友同歌吟。
    红色山水红梅岭，
    枝枝争作万年春。
    双手拔山啊，
    社会主义新愚公。
    铁锤当当啊，
    高奏时代最强音。
    三
    山擂鼓，
    风吹号——
    跃进啊，
    新中国。
    英雄桥上一声唤，
    天南海北伴你笑。
    英雄桥上一声笑，
    万里风云手上招。
    要攀险峰请架桥，
    要劈高山请点炮。
    胜利消息似雪片，
    顷刻化作大江潮。
    帝国主义你敢挡道？
    这炮声你挡不了。
    阶级敌人你敢嘲笑？
    这笑声你压不倒。
    四
    革命号，
    号声声——
    东风唱，
    大江行。
    “唱得一禾生九穗”
    胸中冉冉太阳升。
    “唱得春风笑开口”，
    炉火熊熊钢铁鸣。
    红旗下走来新一代，
    如花似火正年青。
    惊涛骇浪里头不回，
    风吹浪打啊见红心。
    听我们高吹革命号，
    唱出满天红。
    听我们歌唱毛泽东，
    做宇宙的主人翁。
    五
    新的人，
    新天地——
    七彩笔，
    绘天梯。
    天梯通往哪里去？
    步步攀向高峰去。
    彩笔写下火焰诗，
    不让世界有奴隶。
    国际高歌歌一曲，
    大江东去浪万里。
    弯月变桥啊星变花，
    奴隶的镣铐铸金犁。
    无产阶级亿万兄弟，
    自力更生是武器；
    看吧，明天的世界，
    一定是共产主义！
    一九六五年冬天作
