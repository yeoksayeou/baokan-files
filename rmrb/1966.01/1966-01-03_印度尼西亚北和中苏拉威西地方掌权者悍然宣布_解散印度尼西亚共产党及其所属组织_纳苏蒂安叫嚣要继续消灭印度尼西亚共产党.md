Title: Indonesian North and Central Sulawesi Local Authorities Brazenly Announce Dissolution of the Indonesian Communist Party and its Affiliated Organizations; Nasution Yells to Continue Eliminating the Indonesian Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1966-01-03
Edition: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 2nd dispatch: Jakarta news: According to a report by Antara News Agency on December 31, 1965, Sunandar Prijo Sudarmo, Commander of the 13th Military Region of Indonesia and Local Authority of North and Central Sulawesi, decided on December 25, 1965, to dissolve the Indonesian Communist Party and its affiliated mass organizations.

　　He also announced a ban on members of the local Indonesian Communist Party and its affiliated mass organizations from participating in any form of political activity, and announced the management and review of all property rights of the Indonesian Communist Party and its affiliated mass organizations.

　　It is reported that eleven first-level regional (equivalent to provinces) authorities in Indonesia have now announced the dissolution of the Indonesian Communist Party and its affiliated mass organizations.

　　The other ten first-level regions are: West Java, Maluku, South and Southeast Sulawesi, West Kalimantan, Greater Jakarta and its surrounding areas, Central Kalimantan, South Sumatra (including Jambi and Lampung), South Kalimantan, Aceh Special Region of Sumatra, and East Kalimantan.

　　In addition, the commander of the Indonesian Third Naval Regional Command, the commander of the Fifth Naval Regional Command, and the commander of the Third Air Force Operational Zone Command have also successively announced the dissolution or banning of the Indonesian Communist Party and its affiliated mass organizations within their respective jurisdictions.

　　Xinhua News Agency, 2nd dispatch: Jakarta news: According to a report by Antara News Agency on the 1st, Indonesian Minister Coordinator of the Department of Defense and Security Nasution said at the inauguration ceremony of young naval officers held in Jakarta on December 31 that "the work of eliminating the September 30th Movement - the Indonesian Communist Party - still needs to be continued."

　　Speaking of the "dissolution" of the Indonesian Communist Party, Nasution said, "In various regions, its dissolution is already a fact, both in fact and in law."

　　Nasution emphasized "taking practical actions" to "eliminate" "counter-revolutionaries." He "demanded that whoever made mistakes, regardless of who they are, should be brought to a special military court, and that personnel should be cleansed and rectified." He said, "Now various regions are resuming various actions."

### 印度尼西亚北和中苏拉威西地方掌权者悍然宣布  解散印度尼西亚共产党及其所属组织  纳苏蒂安叫嚣要继续消灭印度尼西亚共产党

1966-01-03
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚北和中苏拉威西地方掌权者悍然宣布
    解散印度尼西亚共产党及其所属组织
    纳苏蒂安叫嚣要继续消灭印度尼西亚共产党
    新华社二日讯　雅加达消息：据安塔拉通讯社一九六五年十二月三十一日报道，印度尼西亚第十三军区司令、北和中苏拉威西地方掌权者苏南达尔·普里约·苏达尔莫在一九六五年十二月二十五日决定，解散印度尼西亚共产党和它所属的群众组织。
    他还宣布禁止当地印度尼西亚共产党和它所属的群众组织的成员参加任何形式的政治活动，并且宣布管理和审查印度尼西亚共产党和它所属的群众组织的全部财产权。
    据报道，现在，印度尼西亚已经有十一个一级地区（相当于省）当局宣布解散印度尼西亚共产党和它所属的群众组织。
    其它十个一级地区是：西爪哇、马鲁古、南和东南苏拉威西、西加里曼丹、大雅加达和它的周围地区、中加里曼丹、南苏门答腊（包括占碑和楠榜）、南加里曼丹、苏门答腊亚齐特区和东加里曼丹。
    此外，印度尼西亚第三海军区域司令部司令、第五海军区域司令部司令和空军第三作战区司令部司令也先后宣布了解散或取缔在他们所管辖的地区内的印度尼西亚共产党和它所属的群众组织。
    新华社二日讯　雅加达消息：据安塔拉通讯社一日报道，印度尼西亚国防和安全部组统筹部长纳苏蒂安十二月三十一日在雅加达举行的航海学院青年军官就职典礼上说，“消灭九月三十日运动——印度尼西亚共产党（的工作），还有必要继续进行”。
    纳苏蒂安在谈到“解散”印度尼西亚共产党时说，“在各个地区事实上和法律上，它的解散都已经是事实。”
    纳苏蒂安强调“采取实际行动”，“消灭”“反革命”。他“要求把犯错误的人不管是谁都带到特别军事法庭上去，对人员进行清洗和整顿”。他说，“现在各地区在恢复采取各项行动”。
