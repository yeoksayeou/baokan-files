Title: Oppose Britain's Collusion with the Smith Colonial Regime: Zambian Students Demonstrate, Shouting "Wilson, Get Out!"
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-15
Edition: 4

    Oppose Britain's Collusion with the Smith Colonial Regime
    Zambian Students Demonstrate, Shouting "Wilson, Get Out!"
    Xinhua News Agency, Lusaka, 13th – A dispatch from Lusaka: Zambian students demonstrated in the capital, Lusaka, on the 13th, opposing Britain's collusion with the Smith colonial regime in Southern Rhodesia.
    During the demonstration, British Prime Minister Wilson, who was visiting Lusaka, was leaving a press conference venue by car. Students angrily shouted at the British Prime Minister, "Wilson, get back!" Demonstrators held up placards that read: "Implement African Majority Rule in Southern Rhodesia," "Take Immediate Action" against the Smith regime.
    British Prime Minister Wilson arrived in Zambia on the morning of the 13th. Before this, he attended the Commonwealth Prime Ministers' Conference held in Lagos, the capital of Nigeria, to discuss the issue of Southern Rhodesia.

### 反对英国同史密斯殖民政权狼狈为奸  赞比亚学生示威高呼威尔逊滚蛋！

1966-01-15
第4版()
专栏：

    反对英国同史密斯殖民政权狼狈为奸
    赞比亚学生示威高呼威尔逊滚蛋！
    新华社十三日讯　卢萨卡消息：赞比亚学生十三日在首都卢萨卡举行示威，反对英国同南罗得西亚的史密斯殖民政权狼狈为奸。
    示威时，正在卢萨卡访问的英国首相威尔逊正乘车离开一个记者招待会的会场。学生们向这位英国首相愤怒地高呼：“威尔逊，滚回去！”示威者高举的标语牌上写着：“在南罗得西亚实行非洲人多数执政”，“立即采取行动”反对史密斯政权。
    英国首相威尔逊是十三日清晨到达赞比亚的。在这以前，他出席了在尼日利亚首都拉各斯举行的讨论南罗得西亚问题的英联邦总理会议。
