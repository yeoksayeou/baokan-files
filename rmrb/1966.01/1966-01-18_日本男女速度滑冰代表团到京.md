Title: Japanese Men's and Women's Speed Skating Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-01-18
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, January 15th: The Japanese men's and women's speed skating delegation, comprising fifteen members and led by Kuramachi Taro, director of the Japan Skating Federation, arrived in Beijing by plane tonight at our invitation for a visit and competition in China.

**Named Entities**

日本  Japan
北京  Beijing
新华社  Xinhua News Agency
仓町太郎 Kuramachi Taro
日本滑冰协会  Japan Skating Federation

### 日本男女速度滑冰代表团到京

1966-01-18
第4版()
专栏：

    日本男女速度滑冰代表团到京
    据新华社十五日讯　应邀来我国访问比赛的日本男女速度滑冰代表团一行十五人，由日本滑冰协会理事仓町太郎率领，今晚乘飞机到北京。
