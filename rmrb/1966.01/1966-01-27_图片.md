Title: New Construction, New Products

Author: Wu Muhua

Date: 1966-01-27
Edition: 3

    The Wuhan Heavy Machine Tool Plant has independently developed a precision gantry planer. It is mainly used to process the planes, guide rail surfaces, inclined surfaces, etc. of large parts on machine tools or machines, and the smoothness can reach level 7. This is a worker using a precision gantry planer to process a flat ruler.
            Photo by Wu Muhua (Xinhua News Agency)
