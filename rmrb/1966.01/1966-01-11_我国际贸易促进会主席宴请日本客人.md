Title: Chairman of China Council for the Promotion of International Trade Hosts Banquet for Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-01-11
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, January 10th – Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the friendly delegation of the Japan-China Trade Promotion Association, led by head of delegation Ichiro Kawase and Secretary-General Masanori Sumii, on their visit to China.
    Those present at the banquet included Lei Renmin, Zhang Huadong, Yong Longgui, Zhao Anbo, Wang Xiaoyun and others. Japanese peace advocate Kinkazu Saionji, who is currently in Beijing, was also invited to attend the banquet.

### 我国际贸易促进会主席宴请日本客人

1966-01-11
第4版()
专栏：

    我国际贸易促进会主席宴请日本客人
    新华社十日讯  中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸今天晚上设宴欢迎由团长川濑一贯、秘书长隅井正典率领的日中贸易促进会友好访华代表团。
    出席作陪的有：雷任民、张化东、勇龙桂、赵安博、王晓云等。在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也应邀出席了宴会。
