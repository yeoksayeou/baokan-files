　　　　玉米双杂交种
    Corn is a cross-pollinated crop. People can hybridize corn by utilizing this characteristic.
    Corn double hybrid is a type of corn hybrid. It is produced by crossing two single hybrids and is suitable for field cultivation.
  Double hybrid corn plants grow robustly and uniformly, are highly disease-resistant, and have significant yield performance. Therefore, they are welcomed by the masses.

### 玉米双杂交种

1966-01-10
第2版()
专栏：小资料

    玉米双杂交种
    玉米是异花授粉作物，人们利用它的这一特点，可以给玉米进行杂交。
    玉米双杂交种是玉米杂交种的一种。它是由两个单杂交种杂交产生的，适于大田栽培。
  双杂交种玉米的植株生长粗壮整齐，高度抗病，有显著的丰产性能，因此受到群众的欢迎。
