Title: Southern Vietnam Liberation News Agency Supplements Report on New Victories in Đồng Dương, Southern Vietnam Liberation Army Annihilates Four Puppet Army Battalions and One US Army Battalion, Mỹ Tho Province Liberation Army Engages in Fierce Close Combat with the Enemy, Annihilating Over 200 Enemy Troops in One Stroke
Author:
Date: 1966-01-05
Edition: 3

    Southern Vietnam Liberation News Agency Supplements Report on New Victories in Đồng Dương
    Southern Vietnam Liberation Army Annihilates Four Puppet Army Battalions and One US Army Battalion
    Mỹ Tho Province Liberation Army Engages in Fierce Close Combat with the Enemy, Annihilating Over 200 Enemy Troops in One Stroke
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 3rd – According to supplementary reports from the Southern Vietnam Liberation News Agency, the Southern Vietnam Liberation Armed Forces annihilated four battalions of puppet troops and one battalion of the United States Marine Corps during the battles in the Đồng Dương area of Quảng Nam Province from December 8th to 18th, 1965.
    According to reports, on December 8th and 9th, the Liberation Armed Forces annihilated three battalions of puppet troops sent to rescue the besieged Việt An stronghold, namely the 11th Ranger Battalion, the 1st and 3rd Battalions of the 45th Regiment, and destroyed the enemy's command post.
    On December 9th, 10th, and 12th, the Liberation Armed Forces and guerrillas attacked several enemy strongholds near Đồng Dương and annihilated 300 American invaders.
    On December 18th, the Liberation Armed Forces ambushed nearly a battalion of United States Marines and a battalion of puppet troops. After three hours of fierce fighting, one battalion of puppet troops was completely annihilated, and nearly a battalion of US troops was almost completely wiped out.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 2nd – According to reports from the Southern Vietnam Liberation News Agency, the Liberation Armed Forces of Mỹ Tho Province in Southern Vietnam launched a heroic counterattack against a large-scale "mopping-up" operation by the US-puppet clique on December 28th of last year. In the battle, they engaged in close combat with the enemy, annihilating over 200 enemy troops in one stroke.
    On that day, seven main-force battalions of puppet troops and three companies of the puppet "security forces," under the direct command of the American invaders and under the cover of an M-113 armored vehicle squadron, launched a large-scale "mopping-up" operation against Long Định commune in Châu Thành district of Mỹ Tho Province.
    As soon as the enemy climbed out of the helicopters, they were immediately met with a fierce attack by the Liberation Armed Forces. The fearless fighters of the Liberation Armed Forces, regardless of the enemy's crazy bombing by aircraft and the intense shelling by artillery, bravely rushed towards the enemy and engaged in fierce close combat, fighting from 2 pm until dark. They finally crushed the enemy's "mopping-up" operation, annihilating more than 200 enemy troops, including many American invaders and puppet officers.

### 越南南方解放通讯社补充报道同阳大捷的新战果  南越解放军歼灭四营伪军和一营美军  美萩省解放军同敌人展开激烈近战一举歼敌二百多名

1966-01-05
第3版()
专栏：

    越南南方解放通讯社补充报道同阳大捷的新战果
    南越解放军歼灭四营伪军和一营美军
    美萩省解放军同敌人展开激烈近战一举歼敌二百多名
    新华社河内三日电　据越南南方解放通讯社补充报道，越南南方解放武装力量一九六五年十二月八日到十八日在广南省同阳地区的战斗中，歼灭了伪军四个营和美国海军陆战队一个营。
    据报道，解放武装力量在十二月八日和九日歼灭了前往援救被包围的越安据点的伪军三个营，即第十一别动营、第四十五团的第一营和第三营，并摧毁了敌军的指挥所。
    十二月九日、十日和十二日，解放武装力量和游击队在同阳附近进攻了敌人的几个据点，并歼灭了美国侵略者三百名。
    十二月十八日，解放武装力量又伏击了将近一个营的美国海军陆战队和一营的伪军，经过三小时的激烈战斗之后，一营伪军被全歼，近一个营的美军也几乎全军覆没。
    新华社河内二日电据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方美萩省解放武装力量去年十二月二十八日在英勇反击美伪集团军队一次大规模“扫荡”的战斗中，同敌人展开近战，一举消灭敌人二百多名。
    这一天，伪军七个主力营和伪“保安队”三个连，在美国侵略者的直接指挥和一个M—113型装甲车队的掩护下，对美萩省周城县龙定乡进行大规模“扫荡”。
    敌人刚从直升飞机上爬下来，立即遭到解放武装力量的猛烈袭击，解放武装力量的无畏战士们不顾敌人飞机的疯狂轰炸和大炮的猛烈轰击，勇敢地冲向敌人，展开激烈的近战，从下午两点一直打到天黑，终于粉碎了敌人的“扫荡”，共消灭敌人二百多名，其中包括美国侵略者和伪军官多名。
