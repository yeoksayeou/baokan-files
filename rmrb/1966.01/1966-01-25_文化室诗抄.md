Author:
Date: 1966-01-25
Edition: 4

    Culture Room Poetry Selections
    Poor and Lower-Middle Peasants Take Charge
    Poor and lower-middle peasants take charge,
    Even barren mountains can bloom red flowers,
    With the Party to support us,
    We can hold up the sky if it falls.
    Anhui Guichi County
    Xuqiao Brigade Club
    Sunflowers Face the Sun, Everywhere is Spring
    Chairman Mao's words warm the heart,
    Better than the sun in the sky,
    Everyone reads the revolutionary "treasure book,"
    Sunflowers face the sun, everywhere is spring.
    Guangdong Puning County Nigou
    Brigade Culture Room
    Commune Members Love to Sing Harvest Tunes
    Holding red books, hearts ablaze,
    The cold wind feels like a cool fan,
    Carrying burdens with wings,
    Not worried about steep slopes or high mountains.
    Swinging hoes with wind-like speed,
    Not afraid of hard soil or solid rocks,
    Snowflakes cover our heads,
    Hot sweat soaks the ground.
    Three Red Banners shine brightly,
    Commune members love to sing harvest tunes,
    Learn from and catch up with Dazhai, set targets,
    Come back and see next autumn.
    Shanxi Xiyang County Longhuzhai Club Li Jisheng
    Weeding Poem
    Songs burst out like fiery balls,
    Singing makes the sun stand still to watch,
    Watch me turn the weeder in the field,
    For a great increase in grain production.
    Fujian Fuan County Fankeng Brigade Club
    Flowers Don't Wither, Planted by the Party
    Living on a cliff,
    Door facing the sun,
    Wearing a green robe all year round,
    Belted with a jade sash.
    Not afraid of the biting cold,
    Revolutionary fire in our hearts,
    Grasping the red flag high,
    Flowers don't wither, planted by the Party.
    Liaoning Faku County Hongqi Field Brigade Commune Member Jin Yuting

### 文化室诗抄

1966-01-25
第4版()
专栏：

    文化室诗抄
    贫下中农来当家
    贫下中农来当家，
    秃山也能开红花，
    有党给我撑腰杆，
    天掉下来顶回它。
    安徽贵池县
    许桥大队俱乐部
    葵花向阳处处春
    主席话儿暖人心，
    胜似天上日一轮，
    革命“宝书”人人读，
    葵花向阳处处春。
    广东普宁县泥沟
    大队文化室
    社员爱唱丰收调
    怀揣红书心火烧，
    寒风权当凉扇摇，
    担起担儿长翅膀，
    不愁坡陡和山高。
    抡起镢头风赶火，
    那怕土硬石头牢，
    漫天雪花盖顶来，
    浑身热汗遍地浇。
     三面红旗红光照，
    社员爱唱丰收调，
    学赶大寨订指标，
    明年秋后再来瞧。
    山西昔阳县龙虎寨俱乐部李济胜
    耘草诗
    歌声喷出火团团，
    唱得太阳站着看，
    看我耘耙田里转，
    为的粮食大增产。
    福建福安县范坑大队俱乐部
    鲜花不谢是党栽
    居住在山崖，
    大门向阳开，
    身穿四季青袍，
    腰扎一条玉带。
    不怕风寒刺骨，
    革命烈火心中在，
    紧握红旗高高举，
    鲜花不谢是党栽。
    辽宁法库县红旗田大队社员金玉廷
