Title: Battle Song
Author: Gan Ge
Date: 1966-01-22
Edition: 4

    Battle Song
    Worker of Lanzhou Chemical Industry Company  Gan Ge
    Snowflakes flutter, spreading joyous news,
    Great wind happily blows its horn,
    Chimney forests wave colorful silk,
    Motors hum a new tune,
    Cheering the arrival of the Third Five-Year Plan!
    Hands manipulate valves busily,
    Red hearts leap on pledge letters!
    Iron fists are clenched at the oath-taking rally,
    Heroic feelings surge like a tide.
    Chairman Mao points out the direction for us,
    Ten thousand horses gallop, red flags flutter.
    The workshop rumbles, setting up the battlefield,
    Where diligence prevails and difficulties fall,
    One hand waves tools,
    One hand sharpens the battle knife;
    Good news fills the sky with red clouds,
    Turning into red flags flying everywhere!
    〔Selected from "Lanhua Worker"〕

### 战斗歌
甘戈
1966-01-22
第4版()
专栏：

    战斗歌
    兰州化学工业公司工人  甘戈
    雪花飞舞传喜报，
    大风欢乐忙吹号，
    囱林滔滔挥彩绸，
    马达声声唱新调，
    欢呼第三个五年计划到来了！
    手掌活门忙操作，
    保证书上红心跳！
    誓师会上挥铁拳，
    豪情似海涨大潮。
    毛主席给咱指方向，
    万马奔腾红旗飘。
    车间隆隆摆战场，
    干劲到处困难倒，
    一手挥工具，
    一手磨战刀；
    捷报满天红霞飞，
    化作红旗遍地飘！
    〔选自《兰化工人》〕
