Title: Indian Troops Continuously Violate the Ceasefire and Bombard Pakistani Positions
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-05
Edition: 4

    Indian Troops Continuously Violate the Ceasefire and Bombard Pakistani Positions
    According to a Xinhua News Agency report from Karachi on the 3rd: A Pakistani official spokesperson revealed in Rawalpindi on the 3rd that Indian troops again violated the ceasefire in Kashmir on the 2nd, opening fire and shelling Pakistani positions in the Nikial and Koi Ratta areas.
    The spokesperson said that Indian troops have been continuously bombarding these positions for more than a month now. They have also opened fire in the Burki and Hussainiwala areas.
    According to this news agency, Indian troops attempted to invade Pakistani positions in the northern region of Kashmir on New Year's Day this year.

### 印军不断破坏停火轰击巴阵地

1966-01-05
第4版()
专栏：

    印军不断破坏停火轰击巴阵地
    据新华社卡拉奇三日电据巴基斯坦联合通讯社报道，巴基斯坦官方发言人三日在拉瓦尔品第揭露，印度军队二日又在克什米尔破坏停火，开炮轰击尼基亚尔和库伊拉塔地区的巴基斯坦阵地。
    发言人说，印度军队不断轰击这些阵地到现在已经有一个多月了。他们还在布尔基和侯赛尼瓦拉地区开火。
    据这家通讯社报道，印度军队在今年元旦那天就曾试图侵入克什米尔北部地区的巴基斯坦阵地。
