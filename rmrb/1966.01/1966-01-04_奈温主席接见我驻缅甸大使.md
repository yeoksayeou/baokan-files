Title: Chairman Ne Win Receives Chinese Ambassador to Burma
Author:
Date: 1966-01-04
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 2nd—General Ne Win, Chairman of the Revolutionary Council of the Union of Burma, received Geng Biao, Chinese Ambassador to Burma, here on the morning of the 2nd, and held cordial and friendly talks.



**Named Entities**


奈温	Nài Wēn (Ne Win)
耿飚	Gěng Biāo
仰光	Yǎngguāng (Rangoon)
缅甸联邦革命委员会	Miǎndiàn Liánbāng Gémìng Wěiyuánhuì (Revolutionary Council of the Union of Burma)
缅甸	Miǎndiàn (Burma)

### 奈温主席接见我驻缅甸大使

1966-01-04
第3版()
专栏：

    奈温主席接见我驻缅甸大使
    新华社仰光二日电　缅甸联邦革命委员会主席奈温将军二日上午在这里接见了中国驻缅甸大使耿飚，并进行了亲切友好的谈话。
