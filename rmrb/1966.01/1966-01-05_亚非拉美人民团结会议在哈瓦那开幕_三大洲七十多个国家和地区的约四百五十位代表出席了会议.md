Title: Afro-Asian-Latin American People's Solidarity Conference Opens in Havana; Approximately 450 Representatives from More Than 70 Countries and Regions of the Three Continents Attended the Conference

Author:
Date: 1966-01-05
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Havana, January 3rd: The First Afro-Asian-Latin American People's Solidarity Conference opened here on the evening of the 3rd.

    According to the decision of the heads of delegations meeting, Cuba served as the chairman of the conference, and the South Vietnam National Front for Liberation, Ghana, and Venezuela served as vice-chairmen.

    At the opening session, Cuban President Dorticós delivered the opening speech. Conference Secretary-General Youssef El Sebai delivered a political report.

    At the meeting, congratulatory telegrams from Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai and other national leaders to the conference were read.

    Approximately 450 representatives from more than 70 countries and regions of the three continents attended the conference, including the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the South Vietnam National Front for Liberation, China, Korea, the Lao Patriotic Front, Indonesia, Japan, Cambodia, the Patriotic Front of Thailand, the Supreme Revolutionary Council of Congo (Brazzaville), Tanzania, Algeria, Guinea, the United Arab Republic, Ghana, Cuba, Dominica, Venezuela, Panama, and Peru, among others.

    Cuban Premier Castro attended the meeting.

    The agenda for the conference was adopted at the heads of delegations meeting held on the afternoon of the 3rd.

    According to the scheduled plan, the conference will conclude on January 12th.

### 亚非拉美人民团结会议在哈瓦那开幕  三大洲七十多个国家和地区的约四百五十位代表出席了会议

1966-01-05
第4版()
专栏：

    亚非拉美人民团结会议在哈瓦那开幕
    三大洲七十多个国家和地区的约四百五十位代表出席了会议
    新华社哈瓦那三日电第一届亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲人民团结会议三日晚在这里开幕。
    根据各国代表团团长会议的决定，古巴担任会议主席，越南南方民族解放阵线、加纳和委内瑞拉担任会议副主席。
    在开幕会议上，古巴总统多尔蒂科斯致了开幕词。会议秘书长优素福·西巴伊作了政治报告。
    会上宣读了中国总理周恩来和其他国家领导人致大会的贺电。
    来自三大洲七十多个国家和地区的约四百五十位代表出席了会议，其中包括越南民主共和国、越南南方民族解放阵线、中国、朝鲜、老挝爱国战线党、印度尼西亚、日本、柬埔寨、泰国爱国阵线、刚果
  （利）最高革命委员会、坦桑尼亚、阿尔及利亚、几内亚、阿联、加纳、古巴、多米尼加、委内瑞拉、巴拿马和秘鲁等。
    古巴总理卡斯特罗出席了会议。
    在三日下午举行的代表团团长会议上通过了这次会议的议程。
    根据预定计划，会议将在一月十二日结束。
