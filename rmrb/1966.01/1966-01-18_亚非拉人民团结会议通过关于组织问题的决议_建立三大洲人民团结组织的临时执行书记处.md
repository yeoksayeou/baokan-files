Title: Asian, African, Latin American People's Solidarity Conference Passes Resolution on Organizational Issues Establishing Interim Executive Secretariat of the Organization of Solidarity of the Three Continents
Author:
Date: 1966-01-18
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 16th - The First Asian, African, Latin American People's Solidarity Conference passed a resolution on organizational issues, deciding to establish an interim executive secretariat of the Asian, African, Latin American People's Solidarity Organization temporarily in Havana.

    The resolution stated that the purpose of this organization is: "To unite, coordinate and inspire the peoples of Asia, Africa, and Latin America in their struggle against imperialism, led by the United States, and against old and new colonialism."

    The resolution said that this organization will effectively support national liberation movements in the three continents, support revolutionary struggles, and provide firm and reliable support for the anti-imperialist struggles of the liberated countries in the three continents.

    The interim executive secretariat established in Havana will consist of a secretary-general and twelve secretaries (four from each continent). Its responsibilities include: implementing the decisions of the conference; preparing for the Second Asian, African, Latin American People's Solidarity Conference to be held in Cairo in 1968. This conference will decide the location of the organization; submitting to the Second Asian, African, Latin American People's Solidarity Conference a draft of the final organizational structure of the organization of the three continents.

    In addition, the resolution also decided to establish a committee to assist and aid national liberation movements and the struggle against neocolonialism. This committee will consist of twelve members, elected by the conference, and will select a chairman, a vice-chairman, and a secretary from among the members.

    The resolution said that this committee will provide moral, political, and material assistance to national liberation movements, especially to those engaged in armed struggles against imperialism, colonialism, and neocolonialism.

### 亚非拉人民团结会议通过关于组织问题的决议  建立三大洲人民团结组织的临时执行书记处

1966-01-18
第4版()
专栏：

    亚非拉人民团结会议通过关于组织问题的决议
    建立三大洲人民团结组织的临时执行书记处
    新华社哈瓦那十六日电  第一届亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲人民团结会议通过了关于组织问题的决议，决定临时在哈瓦那建立一个亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲人民团结组织的执行书记处。
    决议说，这个组织的宗旨是：“团结、协调和鼓舞亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲人民反对以美国为首的帝国主义和新老殖民主义的斗争。”
    决议说，这个组织将有效地支持三大洲的民族解放运动，支持革命斗争，对三大洲解放了的国家的反帝斗争给予坚定和可靠的支持。
    在哈瓦那建立的临时性执行书记处由一名总书记和十二名书记（每大洲四名）组成。它的职责包括：执行大会的决定；筹备将于一九六八年在开罗召开的第二次亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲人民团结会议。这次会议将决定这个组织的所在地；向第二次亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲人民团结会议提出三大洲组织的最后组织机构草案。
    此外，决议还决定成立协助和援助民族解放运动与反对新殖民主义斗争委员会。这个委员会由十二名委员组成，由大会选举产生，并从委员中选择一位主席，一位副主席，一位书记。
    决议说，这个委员会将对民族解放运动，特别是进行武装斗争反对帝国主义、殖民主义和新殖民主义的民族解放运动提供道义上、政治上和物质上的援助。
