    To Discuss the Convening of a Plenary Session of the Secretariat and Discuss Future Work Issues
    Permanent Secretaries of the Asian-African Journalists Association Gather in Beijing After Evacuating Jakarta
    Xinhua News Agency, 6th – After the Secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists Association evacuated Jakarta, the permanent secretaries have arrived in Beijing one after another. The permanent secretaries told Xinhua reporters: After obtaining the consent of the All-China Journalists Association, they decided to temporarily gather in Beijing to discuss convening a plenary session of the Secretariat to discuss future work issues.
    The permanent secretaries who have arrived in Beijing are: A. R. Aboukous (Arab Republic of Syria), Chen Quanbi (People's Republic of China), D. Manuweera (Ceylon), Lee Morison (South Africa), and Sugiyama Ichihira (Japan).
    When they arrived at Beijing Airport, they were warmly welcomed by Wang Yi, First Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Journalists Association, Secretaries Deng Gang, Li Wu, Li Bingquan, and people from the capital's press circles.

### 商讨召开书记处全体会议和讨论今后工作问题  亚非记协各常驻书记撤离雅加达后来京会合

1966-01-07
第5版()
专栏：

    商讨召开书记处全体会议和讨论今后工作问题
    亚非记协各常驻书记撤离雅加达后来京会合
    新华社六日讯　亚非新闻工作者协会书记处撤离雅加达后，各常驻书记先后来到了北京。各常驻书记对新华社记者说：他们在征得中华全国新闻工作者协会的同意后，决定暂到北京会合，以商讨召开书记处全体会议讨论今后工作问题。
    来到北京的常驻书记是：阿·勒·阿布库斯（阿拉伯叙利亚共和国）、陈泉璧（中华人民共和国）、德·曼努维拉（锡兰）、里·摩里逊（南非）和杉山市平（日本）。
    他们到达北京机场时，受到中华全国新闻工作者协会书记处第一书记王揖，书记邓岗、李伍、李炳泉，以及首都新闻界人士的热烈欢迎。
