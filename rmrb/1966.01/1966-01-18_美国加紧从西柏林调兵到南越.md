Title: U.S. Intensifies Troop Transfers from West Berlin to South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-01-18
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, January 17th, West Berlin News: The U.S. occupation authorities in West Berlin announced that the U.S. Army infantry troops dispatched to West Berlin during the 1961 "Berlin Crisis" will begin their "permanent withdrawal" starting on the 17th.

    This measure was announced at a time when the United States and the Soviet Union tacitly agree to maintain "détente" on the German and Berlin issues, enabling the United States to divert forces to expand the war of aggression against Vietnam. United Press International said that the U.S. reduction of its troops in West Berlin is "because the direct communist threat to the city has ceased."

    It is reported that about six to seven hundred U.S. troops are to be withdrawn this time, and the first batch already left for West Germany by truck on the 17th. After the withdrawal of this batch of troops, there will be six thousand U.S. troops remaining in West Berlin.

    Before announcing this withdrawal, a U.S. Army spokesman in Western Europe revealed on the 14th that in the past twelve months, more than 10,000 U.S. troops stationed in Western Europe had expressed "voluntary" service in South Vietnam, and about 6,300 of them had already gone to South Vietnam to accept "missions."

### 美国加紧从西柏林调兵到南越

1966-01-18
第5版()
专栏：

    美国加紧从西柏林调兵到南越
    新华社十七日讯　西柏林消息：美国驻西柏林占领军当局宣布，在一九六一年“柏林危机”期间增派西柏林的美国陆军步兵部队，从十七日开始“永远撤出”。
    这个措施是在这样的时候宣布的：美国同苏联默契在德国和柏林问题上保持“缓和”，使美国得以抽调兵力去扩大侵略越南的战争。合众国际社说，美国削减它在西柏林的军队，是“因为共产党对这个城市的直接威胁已经停止”。
    据报道，这次要撤出的美军有六七百人，其中第一批已经在十七日乘卡车前往西德。在这批部队撤出后，留在西柏林的美军还有六千人。
    在宣布这次撤军前，美国驻西欧陆军发言人在十四日曾经透露，在过去十二个月里，美国驻西欧的军队有一万多人表示“自愿”去南越服役，其中有大约六千三百人已经到南越接受了“任务”。
