Title: U.S. Warships and Aircraft Invade Our Territorial Waters and Airspace, We Issue the 397th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1966-01-08
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, January 7th: On January 7th, from 4:19 to 5:37 and from 6:15 to 7:40, a U.S. warship intruded into our territorial waters south of Pingtan Island, Fujian Province. On the same day, from 12:24 to 12:44, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island and Dong Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. Regarding these military provocations by U.S. warships and military aircraft, the spokesman of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 397th serious warning.

### 美军舰飞机侵入我领海领空  我提出第三百九十七次严重警告

1966-01-08
第4版()
专栏：

    美军舰飞机侵入我领海领空
    我提出第三百九十七次严重警告
    新华社七日讯　一月七日四时十九分至五时三十七分和六时十五分至七时四十分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省平潭岛以南地区领海。同日十二时二十四分至十二时四十四分美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛、东岛地区领空。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第三百九十七次严重警告。
