Title: President Ayub Ends Visit to Afghanistan
Author:
Date: 1966-01-04
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, January 3rd - Pakistani President Ayub Khan concluded his two-day visit to Afghanistan and left Kabul on the morning of the 3rd.
    He arrived in Afghanistan on the 1st at the invitation of Afghan King Mohammad Zahir Shah.
    Accompanying President Ayub were Pakistani Foreign Minister Bhutto, Commerce Minister Ghulam Faruque, and other senior officials.
    During the visit, the Afghan King and the Pakistani President held talks.

### 阿尤布总统结束对阿富汗的访问

1966-01-04
第3版()
专栏：

    阿尤布总统结束对阿富汗的访问
    新华社喀布尔三日电　巴基斯坦总统阿尤布·汗结束了对阿富汗的两天访问，三日早晨离开喀布尔。
    他是应阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿的邀请，在一日到达阿富汗的。
    陪同阿尤布总统的有，巴基斯坦外交部长布托，商业部长古拉姆·法鲁克和其他高级官员。
    在访问期间，阿富汗国王和巴基斯坦总统举行了会谈。
