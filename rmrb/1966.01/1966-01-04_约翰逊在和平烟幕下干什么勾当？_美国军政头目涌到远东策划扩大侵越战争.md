Title: What is Johnson up to under the smoke screen of peace? US Military and Political Chiefs Flock to the Far East to Plot Expansion of the War of Aggression Against Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-01-04
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd January: Seoul Dispatch—After further plotting with the Park Chung-hee clique to expand the war of aggression against Vietnam, U.S. Vice President Humphrey left Seoul on the 2nd to return home.
    Humphrey arrived in Seoul on the 1st. He held "talks" on the Vietnam issue separately with South Korean puppet President Park Chung-hee and puppet Prime Minister Chung Il-kwon. The Park Chung-hee clique expressed its appreciation for the United States' further expansion of the war of aggression against Vietnam. Puppet Prime Minister Chung Il-kwon clamored that "the Vietnam War must be victoriously concluded with the cooperation of the free world, and must never end in another Panmunjom truce."
    In a statement issued before leaving Seoul on the 2nd, Humphrey praised the Park Chung-hee clique for willingly serving the United States' policy of aggression. He said that the Park Chung-hee clique's "bold and active actions in promoting the security of the free world and cooperation in the Far East" were a "remarkable contribution."
    Humphrey also stated that "the United States is interested in providing South Korea with more economic and military assistance."
    During his stay in South Korea, Humphrey also went to the vicinity of the Military Demarcation Line to conduct "inspections," touring U.S. aggressor troops and South Korean puppet troops.
    Before going to South Korea, Humphrey had been active in Tokyo, Manila, and Taipei, attempting to gather Japan, the Philippines, the Chiang Kai-shek clique, and the Park Chung-hee puppet, etc., to organize an aggressive military group to serve the United States' plot to expand the war.
    In Honolulu on the 2nd, Humphrey received briefings on U.S. intelligence and military activities in Vietnam and other places in Southeast Asia. Participants included Admiral Sharp, Commander-in-Chief of the U.S. Pacific Command, commanders of various services in the Pacific region, presidential assistant Valenti, and other civilian and military officials.
    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd January: Washington Dispatch—The Johnson administration has recently dispatched military and political figures to the Far East one after another. On the 1st, U.S. Secretary of the Army Resor, Secretary of the Air Force Brown, and Administrator of the International Development Agency Bell arrived in Saigon.
    They arrived in Saigon shortly after Chairman of the U.S. Joint Chiefs of Staff Wheeler and Army Chief of Staff Harold Johnson concluded their activities in South Vietnam. According to reports, Resor and Brown will be active in South Vietnam for three or four days, respectively "inspecting" the "U.S. military forces" that have been battered by the South Vietnam Liberation Armed Forces. Before going to South Vietnam on December 30, they announced that in addition to their activities in South Vietnam, they would also go to Thailand, Okinawa, Hawaii, and other places that serve as strategic bases for the aggression against Vietnam.
    Wheeler conducted four days of activities in South Vietnam from December 22 to 25. After leaving South Vietnam, he went to Thailand and Taiwan. While in Taiwan, he held secret talks with Chiang Kai-shek and senior military and political leaders of the Chiang Kai-shek clique.
    Harold Johnson was active in South Vietnam for a week. When he left Saigon for Thailand on December 29, he clamored that the United States would "win victory" in South Vietnam.
    As these military and political figures were rushing to visit, the U.S. military announced that the 3rd Brigade of the 25th Infantry Division of the U.S. Army stationed in Hawaii, consisting of 4,000 men, began to depart for South Vietnam on December 28, bringing the strength of U.S. troops invading Vietnam to approximately 185,000 men.

### 约翰逊在和平烟幕下干什么勾当？  美国军政头目涌到远东策划扩大侵越战争

1966-01-04
第4版()
专栏：

    约翰逊在和平烟幕下干什么勾当？
    美国军政头目涌到远东策划扩大侵越战争
    新华社三日讯　汉城消息：美国副总统汉弗莱在同朴正熙集团进一步策划扩大侵越战争的阴谋之后，已于二日离汉城回国。
    汉弗莱是在一日到达汉城的。他曾同南朝鲜伪总统朴正熙和伪总理丁一权分别就越南问题进行了“会谈”。朴正熙集团对美国进一步扩大侵越战争表示赞赏。伪总理丁一权叫嚣说，“越南战争必须在自由世界的合作下胜利结束，而绝不能以另一个板门店的停战形式而告终”。
    汉弗莱在二日离开汉城前发表的声明中，夸奖朴正熙集团甘心为美国的侵略政策效劳。他说，朴正熙集团在“增进自由世界的安全和远东地区合作所采取的果敢的、积极的行动”是一种“显著的贡献”。
    汉弗莱还表示，“美国对给予南朝鲜以更多的经济和军事援助是感兴趣的”。
    汉弗莱在南朝鲜期间还去军事分界线附近进行“视察”，巡视了美国侵略军和南朝鲜伪军。
    汉弗莱在去南朝鲜之前，曾到东京、马尼拉和台北进行活动，企图把日本、菲律宾、蒋介石集团和朴正熙傀儡等纠集在一起，组织侵略性的军事集团，为美国扩大战争的阴谋效劳。
    汉弗莱二日在檀香山听取了关于美国在越南和东南亚其它地方的情报和军事活动的汇报。参加汇报会的有美国太平洋地区武装部队总司令夏普、太平洋地区各军种司令、总统助理瓦伦蒂和其他文武官员。
    新华社三日讯　华盛顿消息：约翰逊政府最近相继派出军政要员到远东活动。一日，美国陆军部长里索，空军部长布朗和国际开发署署长贝尔又到达西贡。
    他们是在美国参谋长联席会议主席惠勒和陆军参谋长哈·约翰逊刚刚结束在南越的活动后到达西贡的。据报道，里索和布朗将在南越活动三、四天，分别“视察”被南越解放武装力量打得狼狈不堪的“美国军事部队”。他们在十二月三十日去南越前夕，曾宣布他们除到南越活动外，还要到作为侵越战略基地的泰国、冲绳、夏威夷等地活动。
    惠勒在十二月二十二日到二十五日在南越进行了四天活动。他在离开南越后，接着又去泰国和台湾。他在台湾时，同蒋介石及蒋介石集团的高级军政头目进行了密谈。
    哈·约翰逊在南越活动了一个星期。他在十二月二十九日离开西贡去泰国时，叫嚷美国要在南越“赢得胜利”。
    在这些军政要员匆匆出访的时候，美国军方宣布，驻夏威夷的美国陆军第二十五步兵师第三旅四千人从十二月二十八日开始开赴南越，使侵越美军的兵力将达到十八万五千人左右。
