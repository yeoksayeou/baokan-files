Title: People of All Nationalities Celebrate the Spring Festival and Encourage Each Other: Better Study and Apply Chairman Mao's Writings

Author:

Date: 1966-01-23
Edition: 1

    According to Xinhua News Agency, January 22nd: People of all nationalities across the country are carrying out various meaningful activities with boundless joy to celebrate the Spring Festival. In their New Year greetings, people are encouraging each other to better study Chairman Mao's writings, to better highlight politics, and to bravely advance in order to do a good job in the first year of the Third Five-Year Plan.

    During the festival, grand and enthusiastic Spring Festival celebrations were held in Beijing, Tianjin, Shenyang, Xi'an, Wuhan, Xining, Lhasa and other places. Those participating in the celebrations included labor models and advanced workers from the industrial, agricultural, financial and trade, cultural and educational fronts, activists in studying Chairman Mao's writings, combat heroes, "Five-Good" soldiers, "Five-Good" militiamen, and representatives of revolutionary martyrs' families, military families, and demobilized soldiers.

    Responsible persons from various provinces, cities, and autonomous regions, along with representatives from all walks of life, celebrated the festival together and gave speeches. In their speeches, they spoke freely about the current excellent situation, celebrated the great victories achieved last year, and expressed their determination to hold high the red banner of Mao Zedong Thought even higher and to do an even better job this year.

    Comrade Ichizo Sunama, Secretary of the Central Secretariat of the Japanese Communist Party, and his wife, who are currently in Guangzhou, as well as Mr. Li Zongren and his wife, accompanied by Comrade Tao Zhu and others, attended the Guangzhou Spring Festival Sports Viewing Meeting and celebrated the New Year with people from all walks of life.

    Discussing experiences in studying Chairman Mao's writings, worker-peasant-soldier get-togethers, performing revolutionary modern dramas, singing revolutionary songs, telling revolutionary stories, carrying out sports activities, and exercising are the main contents of this year's Spring Festival activities in various places.

    In Shanghai, on the morning of the Spring Festival, more than 2,500 workers held tea parties and forums at the Hudong Workers' Cultural Palace to exchange their experiences and insights in studying Chairman Mao's writings. Li Yulin, the Party Branch Secretary of the Weihai Iron Factory, which is famous in Shanghai for its ambition despite its small size and its daring to break through and surpass, invited Liu Jintang from Shanghai Boiler Factory and Qian Fendi from State Cotton Factory No. 21, who are activists in studying Chairman Mao's writings, to exchange experiences in studying Chairman Mao's writings at the Hudong Workers' Cultural Palace along with other workers from the factory. In the Fuzhou Workers' Cultural Palace, more than 700 people from all walks of life in Fuzhou City, Fujian Province, gathered to discuss their experiences in studying and applying Chairman Mao's writings and exchanged learning experiences. Pan Wumei, a labor model and veteran electrician from the Fuzhou Water Company, who is famous for his flexible study and application of Chairman Mao's writings, introduced how their "Five-Good" maintenance team, through studying Chairman Mao's writings, improved their awareness, established the idea of serving the Chinese revolution and the world revolution, and overfulfilled their production tasks year after year. Some Communist Party members in Beijing also gathered together during the festival to discuss their study experiences and insights, planning to further study Chairman Mao's writings and improve production this year.

    Workers, peasants, and the People's Liberation Army carried out extensive get-togethers in Beijing, Shenyang, Guangzhou and other places.

    Workers from the Beijing No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory, commanders and fighters from a certain unit of the People's Liberation Army, and commune members from the Gaobeidian People's Commune held a grand worker-peasant-soldier get-together on the eve of the Spring Festival and performed many self-compiled revolutionary literary and artistic programs. Many cadres and students in Beijing who participated in the rural socialist education movement went to the rural areas around Beijing on the 21st to visit poor and lower-middle peasants and wish them a happy holiday. Seventy-four factories, including the Shenyang Heavy Machinery Factory and the Shenyang Transformer Factory, organized seventy-four delegations to go to production teams of seventy-three people's communes in the suburbs to celebrate with the farmers. Some veteran workers and technicians from units such as the Shenyang Tractor Factory, the Shenyang Water Pump Factory, and the Agricultural Machinery Trial Production Factory also used the opportunity of the get-together to solicit farmers' opinions on the factory's products and to repair agricultural tools and water pumps for the production teams.

    During the Spring Festival, mass cultural and artistic activities were very active everywhere. The festive stage in Shanghai was colorful and full of the atmosphere of the times. More than 40 professional art troupes in the city performed revolutionary modern dramas, some of which were newly rehearsed. In recent days, many factories have held large-scale mass cultural performances.

    Cultural palaces, clubs, and cultural centers in Tianjin have been very lively in recent days. The First Workers' Cultural Palace held evenings such as "University Liberation Army" and "University Daqing," which were most popular with the masses. Rural cultural centers in various parts of Hubei Province carried out extensive activities during the Spring Festival, some organized commune members to learn from Wang Jie and read red books; some wrote Spring Festival couplets for the families of martyrs and soldiers; some performed literary and artistic programs; and some showed slides.

    Exercise and defend the motherland. Sports activities were rich and varied throughout the Spring Festival. During the Spring Festival, sports activities in Guangzhou were particularly active. On the 22nd, more than 4,300 militiamen and sports enthusiasts in Xi'an participated in a military sports performance. The performance included shooting, parachute jumping, and anti-aircraft shooting. On the Spring Festival day, Nanchang city launched an armed swimming activity across the Gan River. As the athletes broke through the cold wind and waves and crossed the Gan River, the audience on the shore applauded warmly. More than 100 People's Liberation Army soldiers, government cadres, and students in Lhasa held a men's 5,000-meter and women's 3,200-meter cross-country race on the wide street below the Potala Palace.

### 各族人民欢度春节互相勉励：  更好地活学活用毛主席著作

1966-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    各族人民欢度春节互相勉励：
    更好地活学活用毛主席著作
    据新华社二十二日讯  全国各地各族人民怀着无限喜悦的心情，开展各种有意义的活动，欢度新春佳节。人们在新春祝贺中争相勉励：要更好学习毛主席著作，更好地突出政治，为做好第三个五年计划第一年的各项工作而奋勇前进。
    节日期间，北京、天津、沈阳、西安、武汉、西宁、拉萨等地都举行了盛大的、热烈的春节联欢会。参加联欢会的，有工业、农业、财贸、文教战线的劳动模范和先进工作者，学习毛主席著作积极分子，战斗英雄、五好战士，五好民兵，烈属、军属、复员军人的代表。
    各地省、市、自治区的负责人和各界代表一起欢度佳节，并且讲了话。他们在讲话中畅谈当前的大好形势，欢庆去年取得的伟大胜利，并表示要更高地举起毛泽东思想红旗，把今年的工作做得更加出色。
    正在广州的日本共产党中央书记处书记砂间一良同志和夫人，以及李宗仁先生和夫人，由陶铸同志等陪同，参加了广州的春节体育观赏会，和各界人民一起欢度新春佳节。
    畅谈学习毛主席著作的心得，工农兵联欢，大演革命现代戏、唱革命歌、讲革命故事，开展体育运动、锻炼身体，是今年各地春节活动的主要内容。
    在上海，春节这天上午，有两千五百多名职工在沪东工人文化宫举行茶话会、座谈会，交流学习毛主席著作的心得和经验。以厂小志大、敢闯敢超闻名上海的卫海铁工厂党支部书记李玉林，这天在沪东工人文化宫和厂里其他职工一起，邀请了学习毛主席著作积极分子上海锅炉厂刘金堂、国棉二十一厂钱芬娣等，相互交流学习毛主席著作的经验。在福州市工人文化宫里，福建省福州市各界七百多人欢聚一堂，谈了活学活用毛主席著作的体会，并且交流了学习经验。以活学活用毛主席著作出名的福州市自来水公司劳动模范、老电工潘五妹，介绍了他们五好检修班组通过学习毛主席著作，提高觉悟，树立一切为中国革命和世界革命的思想，连年超额完成生产任务的事迹。北京的一些共产党员，也在节日里欢聚一起，座谈学习心得、体会，打算在今年里进一步学好毛主席著作，搞好生产。
    北京、沈阳、广州等地，工人、农民、解放军进行了广泛的联欢活动。
    北京第一棉纺织厂职工、解放军某部指战员和高碑店人民公社的社员们，春节前夕举行了工农兵大联欢，演出了许多自编的革命文艺节目。北京市许多参加过农村社会主义教育运动的干部、学生，二十一日赶到京郊农村去拜访贫下中农，向他们祝贺节日。沈阳重型机器厂，沈阳变压器厂等七十四个工厂职工组织了七十四个代表团，到郊区七十三个人民公社的生产队，和农民联欢。沈阳拖拉机厂、沈阳水泵厂、农业机械试制厂等单位的一些老工人和技术人员，还利用联欢的机会，征求农民对本厂产品的意见，并且给生产队检修农具和抽水机。
    春节期间，各地群众性文艺活动十分活跃。上海的节日舞台，万紫千红，充满时代气息。全市四十多个专业文艺团体，都演出了革命现代戏，有些戏是新排的。连日来，许多工厂都举行了规模较大的群众文艺演出。
    天津市各文化宫、俱乐部、文化馆连日来热闹非常。第一工人文化宫举办了大学解放军、大学大庆等晚会，最受群众欢迎。湖北各地农村文化室春节期间广泛开展活动，有的组织社员学王杰，读红书；有的为烈军属写春联；有的演出文艺节目；有的放映幻灯。
    锻炼身体，保卫祖国。各地春节期间的体育活动丰富多采。春节期间，广州市的体育运动特别活跃。二十二日，西安市四千三百多名民兵和体育爱好者，参加了军事体育表演。共表演了射击、飞机跳伞、对空射击等项节目。春节这天，南昌全市开展了武装泅渡赣江的活动。当健儿们冲破寒风激浪，横渡过赣江时，岸边观众热烈鼓掌。拉萨市解放军战士、机关干部和学生一百多人，在布达拉宫下面的宽阔街道上，举行了男子五千米和女子三千二百米的越野赛跑。
