Title: Taiyuan Acrobatic Arts Troupe of China Leaves for Sudan for Performance Tour

Author: 
Date: 1966-01-08
Edition: 4

    The Taiyuan Acrobatic Arts Troupe of China Leaves for Sudan for Performance Tour
    Xinhua News Agency, January 6th: At the invitation of the Sudanese government, the 40-member Chinese Taiyuan Acrobatic Arts Troupe, led by Zhou Guangyao, left Beijing by plane on the 5th for a performance tour in Sudan.
    Those who saw them off at the airport included Wu Qing, Ding Bo and other responsible persons from relevant departments.

### 我太原杂技艺术团前往苏丹访问演出

1966-01-08
第4版()
专栏：

    我太原杂技艺术团前往苏丹访问演出
    新华社六日讯　应苏丹政府邀请，中国太原杂技艺术团一行四十人，在团长周光耀的率领下五日乘飞机离开北京，前往苏丹访问演出。
    到机场送行的，有吴青、丁波等有关方面负责人。
