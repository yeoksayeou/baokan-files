Title: US Aggressors Continuously Use Poison and Massacre People in South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-01-12
Edition: 4

    US Aggressors Continuously Use Poison and Massacre People in South Vietnam
    According to a report from the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency in Hanoi on the 11th, the US aggressors and their running dogs, in the three days from January 1st to 3rd, successively dispatched multiple batches of aircraft to spray chemical poisons in many areas of Vinh Long and Sa Dec provinces in South Vietnam, committing new barbarous crimes. On the 1st, US aircraft sprayed brown chemical poisons in My Thuan, Tan Khai, Tan Luoc, Thanh Loi, and Phong Hoa communes of Binh Minh district in Vinh Long province, as well as Hoa Tan commune of Sa Dec province, poisoning many people and causing many crops to wither.
    On the 2nd and 3rd, the US bandits and their running dogs dispatched aircraft to spray large quantities of chemical poisons in the Tam Vu area of Binh Minh district and the Kinh Mai area of Tra On district in Vinh Long province.
    In addition, on December 27th of last year, US aircraft also sprayed chemical poisons in Long Hai commune of Ba Ria province, poisoning many people in this area.

### 美侵略者接连在南越放毒屠杀人民

1966-01-12
第4版()
专栏：

    美侵略者接连在南越放毒屠杀人民
    据新华社河内十一日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，美国侵略者及其走狗在一月一日到三日的三天中，接连派遣多批飞机向越南南方永隆和沙沥两省的许多地方撒放化学毒药，犯下新的野蛮罪行。一日，美国飞机向永隆省平明县美顺、新盖、新略、成利、风和等乡以及沙沥省和新乡撒放棕色化学毒药，使很多人民中毒，许多庄稼枯萎。
    二日和三日，美国强盗及其走狗又派遣飞机向永隆省平明县寻芜地区和三平县京梅地区撒放了大批化学毒药。
    此外，去年十二月二十七日，美国飞机还向巴地省隆海乡撒放了化学毒药，使这个地区的许多人民中毒。
