Title: Chinese and Albanian Artists Celebrate New Year Together, Celebrating Victory in the Anti-American and Anti-Revisionist Struggle; Premier Zhou Enlai and Ambassador Nesti Nase Attend

Author:
Date: 1966-01-02
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, December 31st -- To celebrate the New Year of 1966, literary and art workers in the capital and artists from the Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe held a get-together at the Beijing Hotel this evening. Chinese and Albanian artists jointly celebrated the great victories achieved by the people of both countries in the past year in the struggle against American imperialism and modern revisionism, and in the struggle for socialist revolution and socialist construction, and mutually wished for greater victories in the new year of combat.
    Premier Zhou Enlai attended the get-together. Premier Zhou warmly shook hands with the Albanian comrades and extended New Year's greetings.
    Albanian Ambassador to China, Nesti Nase, and embassy officials were invited to attend the get-together.
    After the New Year's bell rang on the Central People's Broadcasting Station, cheers of "Long live Comrade Enver! Long live Comrade Mao Zedong!" resounded throughout the venue. The get-together began with the music of "Socialism is Good." Chinese and Albanian artists enjoyed themselves to the fullest while performing songs and dances from both countries.
    Fu Zhong, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, congratulated the Albanian comrades on the beginning of the new year. He proposed a toast to the unbreakable unity and fighting friendship between the people and armies of China and Albania.
    Also present at the evening were Zhao Yimin, Kong Yuan, Wang Bingnan, Li Chang, Song Yiping, Li Qitao, and other responsible persons from relevant departments, as well as figures from the literary and art circles.

### 中阿艺术家新年联欢共庆反美反修斗争胜利  周恩来总理和奈斯蒂·纳赛大使出席

1966-01-02
第3版()
专栏：

    中阿艺术家新年联欢共庆反美反修斗争胜利
    周恩来总理和奈斯蒂·纳赛大使出席
    新华社三十一日讯　为欢庆一九六六年新年，首都文艺工作者和阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团的艺术家们，今晚在北京饭店举行联欢。中阿两国艺术家们共庆两国人民过去一年在反对美帝国主义、现代修正主义的斗争中，在社会主义革命和社会主义建设的斗争中取得的伟大的胜利，互祝在战斗的新的一年中取得更大的胜利。
    周恩来总理出席了联欢晚会。周总理同阿尔巴尼亚同志亲切握手，祝贺新年。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使奈斯蒂·纳赛和大使馆官员应邀参加了联欢。
    在中央人民广播电台响起了新年钟声以后，“万岁，恩维尔同志！万岁，毛泽东同志！”的欢呼声响彻会场。联欢会在《社会主义好》的乐曲声中开始。中阿两国艺术家们一面尽情欢乐，一面表演中阿两国的歌舞。
    中国人民解放军总政治部副主任傅钟向阿尔巴尼亚同志祝贺新的一年开始。他提议为中阿两国人民、两国军队牢不可破的团结和战斗友谊干杯。
    出席晚会的，还有赵毅敏、孔原、王炳南、李昌、宋一平、李琦涛等有关方面负责人以及文艺界人士。
    联欢晚会是对外文化联络委员会和中国人民解放军总政治部联合举办的。
