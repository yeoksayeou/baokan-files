Title: Afro-Asian Journalists' Association Sends Telegram to Prince Sihanouk, Firmly Supporting the Cambodian People's Struggle Against the United States

Author:
Date: 1966-01-10
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, January 9th: The Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association (AAJA) sent a telegram to Cambodian Head of State Prince Norodom Sihanouk on January 9th, strongly condemning the aggression against Cambodia by US imperialism and its lackeys, and firmly supporting the heroic struggle of the Cambodian people against US imperialism. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

    To His Royal Highness Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Head of State of Cambodia, Phnom Penh:

    The Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association, on behalf of its forty-seven member countries and all progressive Afro-Asian journalists, fully appreciates and wholeheartedly supports the decisions of the 20th Congress of the People's Socialist Community and the Cambodian people, as well as Your Highness's speech in Kompong Chhnang, which strongly condemned the United States for seeking to expand the war, in which it is failing in Vietnam, to peace-loving and anti-imperialist Cambodia. The AAJA condemns the aggression against O Smach, Oddar Meanchey province by the Thai lackeys of US imperialism. The AAJA admires the uncompromising and firm position of the heroic Cambodian people under Your Highness's leadership, using a "tooth for a tooth" approach to deal with the aggression of US imperialism and its lackeys. The AAJA fully supports Your Highness's correct statement that "if US imperialism and its lackeys invade Cambodia, they will face not only the resolute six million Cambodians, because the Cambodians are by no means isolated."

    The AAJA strongly condemns US imperialism's war policy and calls on all AAJA member countries and progressive Afro-Asian journalists to fully support the Cambodian people's struggle against American imperialism.

    Permanent Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association:

    A.L. Abukus (Arab Republic of Syria)
    Chen Quanbi (People's Republic of China)
    D. Manuvila (Ceylon)
    Lee Morisson (South Africa)
    Sugiyama Ichiei (Japan)
          Beijing, January 9, 1966

### 亚非记协致电西哈努克亲王  坚决支持柬埔寨人民反美斗争

1966-01-10
第4版()
专栏：

    亚非记协致电西哈努克亲王
    坚决支持柬埔寨人民反美斗争
    新华社九日讯　亚非新闻工作者协会书记处九日打电报给柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王，强烈谴责美帝国主义及其走狗对柬埔寨的侵略，并且坚决支持柬埔寨人民反对美帝国主义的英勇斗争。电报全文如下：金边柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王殿下：
    亚非新闻工作者协会书记处代表它四十七个成员国和所有进步亚非新闻工作者，完全赞赏和全力支持人民社会同盟第二十次代表大会和柬埔寨人民的决定以及殿下在磅清扬的讲话，这次讲话强烈谴责了美国要把在越南遭到失败的战争扩大到爱好和平和反帝的柬埔寨。亚非新闻工作者协会谴责美帝国主义的泰国走狗对奥多棉藏省奥斯马的侵略。亚非新闻工作者协会钦佩在殿下领导下的英雄柬埔寨人民不妥协和坚定的立场，用“以牙还牙”的方针来对付美帝国主义和它的走狗的侵略。亚非新闻工作者协会全力支持殿下的正确声明，“假如美帝国主义和它的走狗入侵柬埔寨，他们面临的不只是坚决的六百万的柬埔寨人，因为柬埔寨人决不是孤立的。”
    亚非新闻工作者协会强烈谴责美帝国主义的战争政策，并号召所有亚非新闻工作者协会成员国和进步亚非新闻工作者全力支持柬埔寨人民反对美国帝国主义的斗争。
    亚非新闻工作者协会书记处常驻书记
    阿·勒·阿布库斯（阿拉伯叙利亚共和国）
    陈泉璧（中华人民共和国）
    德·曼努维拉（锡兰）
    里·摩里逊（南非）
    杉山市平（日本）
          一九六六年一月九日于北京
