Title: U.S. Continues Underground Nuclear Tests
Author:
Date: 1966-01-25
Edition: 3

    New China News Agency dispatch from Washington: The U.S. Atomic Energy Commission announced that the United States conducted an underground nuclear weapons test at the Nevada Test Site on the 13th.

    This is the first official U.S. nuclear weapons test announced this year, and the fifty-eighth test since the Partial Nuclear Test Ban Treaty was signed in 1963.

### 美国继续进行地下核试验

1966-01-25
第3版()
专栏：

    美国继续进行地下核试验
    据新华社讯　华盛顿消息：据美国原子能委员会宣布，美国十三日在内华达试验场进行了一次地下核武器试验。
    这是今年以来美国官方宣布的第一次核武器试验，也是部分禁止核试验条约在一九六三年签订以来的第五十八次试验。
