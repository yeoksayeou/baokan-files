Title: Eleventh Volume of "Khrushchev's Speeches" Published
Author:
Date: 1966-01-13
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 12th. The eleventh volume of "Khrushchev's Speeches" has been published by World Knowledge Press and is being distributed throughout the country. This collection includes 64 reports, speeches, letters, telegrams, and Q&A sessions with reporters publicly released by Khrushchev from January to April 1959.

### 《赫鲁晓夫言论》第十一集出版

1966-01-13
第4版()
专栏：

    《赫鲁晓夫言论》第十一集出版
    新华社十二日讯　《赫鲁晓夫言论》第十一集，已由世界知识出版社出版，在全国各地发行。这一集的内容，包括赫鲁晓夫在一九五九年一月至四月期间公开发表的报告、讲话、函电、答记者问等，共六十四篇。
