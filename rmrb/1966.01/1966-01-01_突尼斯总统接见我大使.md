Title: Tunisian President Receives Chinese Ambassador
Author:
Date: 1966-01-01
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, 30th – Tunisian President Habib Bourguiba received Chinese Ambassador Yao Nian at the Government Palace on the morning of the 30th.


**Named Entities**

突尼斯    Tunisia
哈比卜·布尔吉巴  Habib Bourguiba

### 突尼斯总统接见我大使

1966-01-01
第3版()
专栏：

    突尼斯总统接见我大使
    新华社突尼斯三十日电突尼斯总统哈比卜·布尔吉巴三十日上午在政府大厦接见了中国驻突尼斯大使姚念。
