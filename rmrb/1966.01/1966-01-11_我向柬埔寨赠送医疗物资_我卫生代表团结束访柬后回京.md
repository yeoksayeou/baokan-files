Title: China Donates Medical Supplies to Cambodia; Chinese Health Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visit to Cambodia
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-11
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh – The handover ceremony for medical supplies donated by the Chinese Ministry of Health to the Cambodian Ministry of Social Welfare and Labor was held here on the morning of the sixth.
    Huang Shuze, head of the Chinese Health Delegation and Vice Minister of the Chinese Ministry of Health, presented the list of these medical supplies to Madame Tioulong Pouch, Secretary of State of the Cambodian Ministry of Social Welfare and Labor, on behalf of the Chinese Ministry of Health. In his speech, Huang Shuze praised the achievements of the Royal Government of Cambodia in developing the welfare of the Cambodian people.
    In her speech, Madame Tioulong Pouch expressed her gratitude on behalf of the Royal Government of Cambodia for the medical supplies donated by the Chinese government.
    Zhou Sou, Secretary of State of the Cambodian Ministry of Planning, and officials from the Cambodian Ministry of Social Welfare and Labor attended the ceremony.
    Zheng Sixiong, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Cambodia, also attended the ceremony.
    Xinhua News Agency, 7th – The Chinese Health Delegation, led by Vice Minister of Health Huang Shuze, consisting of eight people, returned to Beijing by plane today after completing its inspection visit to Cambodia. Shi Shuhan, Vice Minister of Health, and Ouyang Jing, Director of the General Office, were at the airport to welcome them. Cambodian Ambassador to China, Zhang Gang, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 我向柬埔寨赠送医疗物资  我卫生代表团结束访柬后回京

1966-01-11
第4版()
专栏：

    我向柬埔寨赠送医疗物资
    我卫生代表团结束访柬后回京
    新华社金边电  中国卫生部赠送给柬埔寨社会福利和劳工部的医疗物资的交接仪式六日上午在这里举行。
    中国卫生代表团团长、中国卫生部副部长黄树则代表中国卫生部把这批医疗物资的清单交给了柬埔寨社会福利和劳工部国务秘书狄普曼夫人。黄树则在讲话中赞扬柬埔寨王国政府在发展柬埔寨人民的福利事业方面所取得的成就。
    狄普曼夫人在讲话中代表柬埔寨王国政府对中国政府赠送的医疗物资表示感谢。
    参加仪式的有柬埔寨计划国务秘书周索以及柬埔寨社会福利和劳工部的官员。
    中国驻柬埔寨大使馆临时代办郑斯雄也参加了仪式。
    新华社七日讯卫生部副部长黄树则率领的中国卫生代表团一行八人，结束了在柬埔寨考察访问后，今天乘飞机回到北京。前往机场迎接的，有卫生部副部长史书翰和办公厅主任欧阳竞。柬埔寨驻中国大使张岗也到机场迎接。
