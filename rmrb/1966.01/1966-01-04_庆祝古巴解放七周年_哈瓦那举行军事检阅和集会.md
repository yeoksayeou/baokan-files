Title: Celebrating the Seventh Anniversary of Cuban Liberation: Military Parade and Rally Held in Havana
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-04
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Havana, January 2nd—A military parade and mass rally were held in the Cuban capital on the morning of the 2nd to commemorate the seventh anniversary of Cuban Liberation.

    Cuban Prime Minister Fidel Castro, President Dorticós, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Revolutionary Armed Forces Raúl Castro, as well as other leaders of the Cuban government and party, attended the rally.

    Also attending the rally were representatives attending the Tricontinental Conference of Peoples of Asia, Africa and Latin America, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Cuba.

    Fidel Castro delivered a speech at the rally.

### 庆祝古巴解放七周年  哈瓦那举行军事检阅和集会

1966-01-04
第3版()
专栏：

    庆祝古巴解放七周年
    哈瓦那举行军事检阅和集会
    新华社哈瓦那二日电　古巴首都二日上午举行军事检阅和群众集会，纪念古巴解放七周年。
    古巴总理菲德尔·卡斯特罗、总统多尔蒂科斯、副总理兼革命武装部长劳尔·卡斯特罗以及古巴政府和党的其他领导人出席了这次集会。
    参加集会的还有出席亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲人民团结会议的代表和各国驻古巴的外交使节。
    菲德尔·卡斯特罗在集会上发表了演说。
