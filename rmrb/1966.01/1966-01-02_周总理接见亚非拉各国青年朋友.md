Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Young Friends from Asia, Africa, and Latin America
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-02
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 1st January – Premier Zhou Enlai received young friends from Asia, Africa, and Latin America who are currently visiting Beijing this afternoon. They are: the Guinean African Democratic Revolution Youth Organization delegation headed by Idrissa Traoré, the South Vietnamese Youth Representative Le Phong, the Angolan People's Liberation Movement Youth Organization representative Alexandre, the Indonesian Youth Delegation, the Japanese Youth League Council Delegation headed by Lin Zhaoxiong, and youth representatives from Brazil, Dominica, Ecuador, and Haiti.
    Premier Zhou Enlai had a cordial and friendly conversation with the young friends from various countries.
    Present at the reception were Yang Haibo, Hu Qili, Li Wenyuan, Wu Shaozu, and other responsible persons from relevant departments.

### 周总理接见亚非拉各国青年朋友

1966-01-02
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见亚非拉各国青年朋友
    新华社一日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了正在北京访问的亚洲、非洲和拉丁美洲各国的青年朋友。他们是：以伊德里萨·特拉奥雷为首的几内亚非洲民主革命青年组织代表团，越南南方青年代表黎风，安哥拉人民解放运动青年组织代表阿莱克桑达，印度尼西亚青年代表团，以林照雄为首的日本青年团协议会代表团，以及巴西、多米尼加、厄瓜多尔、海地的青年代表。
    周恩来总理同各国青年朋友进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有杨海波、胡启立、李文耀、伍绍祖等有关方面的负责人。
