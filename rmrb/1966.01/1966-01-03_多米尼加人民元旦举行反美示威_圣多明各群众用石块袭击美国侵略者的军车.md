Title: Dominican People Hold Anti-US Demonstrations on New Year's Day; Masses in Santo Domingo Attack American Aggressors' Military Vehicles with Stones

Author: 
Date: 1966-01-03
Edition: 4

    New China News Agency, Santo Domingo, 2nd - Patriotic residents of the Dominican capital held anti-US demonstrations in the streets again on January 1st, and attacked a jeep carrying American intervention troops with stones. The "Pan-American Forces" threw tear gas at the demonstrating masses in an attempt to disperse the demonstration.
    Since the incident in Santiago in mid-December, when right-wing troops attacked the original rebel forces, the indignant Dominican people have continuously launched fierce anti-American actions. Faria Tavares, leader of the "June 14th Revolutionary Movement," revealed at a press conference on December 29th that the incident in which right-wing troops attacked the original rebel forces was a conspiracy by the United States to eliminate the leaders of the original rebel forces.

### 多米尼加人民元旦举行反美示威  圣多明各群众用石块袭击美国侵略者的军车

1966-01-03
第4版()
专栏：

    多米尼加人民元旦举行反美示威
    圣多明各群众用石块袭击美国侵略者的军车
    新华社二日讯　圣多明各消息：多米尼加首都爱国居民一月一日又在街上举行反美示威，并且用石块袭击一辆坐着美国干涉军的吉普车。“泛美部队”向示威群众投掷催泪瓦斯，力图驱散示威的队伍。
    从去年十二月中在圣地亚哥城发生右翼部队袭击原起义部队的事件后，激愤的多米尼加人民连续展开激烈的反美行动。“六·一四革命运动”领导人法里亚·塔瓦雷斯十二月二十九日在记者招待会上揭露说，右翼部队袭击原起义部队的事件，是美国企图消灭原起义部队领导的一个阴谋。
