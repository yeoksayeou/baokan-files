Title: Prominent Colombian Figure Torres Announces Participation in National Liberation Army; Will Not Lay Down Arms Until People Seize Power
Author:
Date: 1966-01-31
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Bogota, 30th dispatch: Bogota news: Prominent Colombian figure Camilo Torres recently issued a declaration saying that he has joined the Colombian National Liberation Army, which is currently engaged in armed struggle.

    Camilo Torres was formerly a priest and university professor, and is now one of the leaders of the Colombian "United Front Movement."

    In his declaration, Torres strongly condemned the brutal rule of the Colombian people by U.S. imperialism and the Colombian reactionaries. The declaration pointed out that the struggle of the Colombian people is for "future Colombians to have their own motherland, independent of American power."

    Torres said in the declaration that he joined the National Liberation Army because this organization "seeks to liberate the people from exploitation, oligarchy, and imperialism. It will not lay down its arms until power is completely in the hands of the people."

### 哥伦比亚著名人士托雷斯宣布参加民族解放军  人民不夺取政权就不放下武器

1966-01-31
第5版()
专栏：

    哥伦比亚著名人士托雷斯宣布参加民族解放军
    人民不夺取政权就不放下武器
    新华社三十日讯　波哥大消息：哥伦比亚著名人士卡米洛·托雷斯最近发表一项宣言说，他已经参加了正在进行武装斗争的哥伦比亚民族解放军。
    卡米洛·托雷斯过去是一位神父，担任过大学教授，现在是哥伦比亚“统一战线运动”领导人之一。
    托雷斯在他的宣言中强烈谴责了美帝国主义和哥伦比亚反动派对哥伦比亚人民的残酷统治。宣言指出，哥伦比亚人民的斗争，是为了“使未来的哥伦比亚人能够有一个自己的、不依靠于美国强权的祖国”。
    托雷斯在宣言中说，他参加民族解放军是因为这个组织“所寻求的是使人民摆脱剥削、寡头集团和帝国主义而取得解放。只要政权没有完全落到人民的手中，它就不放下武器”。
