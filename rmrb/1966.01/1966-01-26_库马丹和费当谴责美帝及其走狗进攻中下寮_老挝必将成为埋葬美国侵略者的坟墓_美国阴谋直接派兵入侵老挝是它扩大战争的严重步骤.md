Title: Koumadane and Phaydang Condemn US Imperialists and Their Lackeys for Attacking Central and Lower Laos: Laos Will Surely Become a Graveyard for American Aggressors; US Plot to Directly Send Troops to Invade Laos is a Grave Step in Its Expansion of the War
Author:
Date: 1966-01-26
Edition: 4

    Koumadane and Phaydang Condemn US Imperialists and Their Lackeys for Attacking Central and Lower Laos
    Laos Will Surely Become a Graveyard for American Aggressors
    US Plot to Directly Send Troops to Invade Laos is a Grave Step in Its Expansion of the War
    According to a Xinhua News Agency report from Khang Khay on January 24th: Radio Pathet Lao broadcast on the 24th that Sithon Koumadane, Vice Chairman of the Lao Patriotic Front Central Committee, warned on the 21st that if US imperialism dares to directly send troops to invade Laos, Laos will become a graveyard for American imperialist aggressors.
    Sithon Koumadane made these remarks in response to questions from a Radio Pathet Lao reporter.
    He pointed out that just as US imperialism is frantically expanding the war of aggression against South Vietnam, US imperialism and the Kouprasith-Sananikone traitorous reactionary clique in Laos have also concentrated dozens of battalions of troops, and with the coordination of Thai and South Vietnamese troops, are frantically attacking the liberated areas of Central and Lower Laos. At the same time, the US Air Force has been relentlessly bombing and strafing the liberated areas of Central and Lower Laos, and releasing chemical poisons. Recently, US imperialism has been plotting to directly send its ground troops to attack Central and Lower Laos. The Sananikone and Kouprasith cliques are also desperately clamoring to bring American troops into Laos.
    Koumadane said: The Lao Patriotic Front's statement of January 17, 1966, clearly pointed out the firm will of the Lao Patriotic Front and the Lao people: to resist America and save the nation to the end. The Lao people cherish peace very much, but will never exchange their independence and sovereignty for false and deceptive "peace."
    Sithon Koumadane demanded that Prince Souvanna Phouma and Prince Boun Oum put the interests of the motherland first, meet with Prince Souphanouvong, and on the basis of mutual understanding, jointly seek to get the country out of the dangerous situation deliberately created by US imperialism and the Kouprasith-Sananikone clique.
  According to a Xinhua News Agency report from Khang Khay on January 25th: Radio Pathet Lao broadcast on the 24th: Phaydang, Vice Chairman of the Lao Patriotic Front Central Committee, in a recent speech, strongly protested against the US imperialist plot to directly send troops to invade and occupy the liberated areas of Central and Lower Laos, the new military adventure. Phaydang said: US imperialism, disregarding the sovereignty of Laos, is plotting to directly send troops to invade Laos, which is clearly an extremely serious new step in its preparation to expand the war of aggression against Laos to the entire Indochina.
    Phaydang pointed out: This conspiracy of US imperialism has created an extremely serious new crisis. It makes it possible for Laos and the entire Indochina to be turned into a fierce battlefield.
    Phaydang called on the people of Laos to be vigilant, closely unite around the Lao Patriotic Front, and smash all the vicious plots of US imperialism and its running dogs.

### 库马丹和费当谴责美帝及其走狗进攻中下寮  老挝必将成为埋葬美国侵略者的坟墓  美国阴谋直接派兵入侵老挝是它扩大战争的严重步骤

1966-01-26
第4版()
专栏：

    库马丹和费当谴责美帝及其走狗进攻中下寮
    老挝必将成为埋葬美国侵略者的坟墓
    美国阴谋直接派兵入侵老挝是它扩大战争的严重步骤
    据新华社康开二十四日电　据巴特寮电台二十四日广播，老挝爱国战线党中央委员会副主席西吞·库马丹二十一日警告说，如果美帝国主义胆敢直接派兵入侵老挝，老挝将成为埋葬美帝国主义侵略者的坟墓。
    西吞·库马丹是在回答巴特寮电台记者的问题时说这番话的。
    他指出，正当美帝国主义疯狂地扩大侵略越南南方的战争的时候，美帝国主义和库帕拉西—萨纳尼空卖国反动集团在老挝也集中了几十个营的兵力，在泰国和南越军队的配合下，疯狂地进攻中寮和下寮解放区。同时，美国空军一直在对中寮和下寮解放区进行狂轰滥炸，撒放化学毒药。最近，美帝国主义更在阴谋派遣它的地面部队直接进攻中寮和下寮。萨纳尼空和库帕拉西集团也在竭力叫嚷，要把美国军队引进老挝。
    库马丹说：老挝爱国战线党一九六六年一月十七日声明明确地指出老挝爱国战线党和老挝全国人民的坚强意志是：抗美救国到底，老挝人民非常热爱和平，但决不拿自己的独立和主权去换取虚假和骗人的“和平”。
    西吞·库马丹要求富马亲王和文翁亲王把祖国的利益摆在第一位，同苏发努冯亲王会晤，在互相谅解和互相了解的基础上，共同寻求使国家摆脱美帝国主义和库帕拉西—萨纳尼空集团蓄意造成的危险局面。
  据新华社康开二十五日电　据巴特寮电台二十四日广播：老挝爱国战线党中央委员会副主席费当在最近发表的一次谈话中，强烈抗议美帝国主义阴谋直接派兵侵占老挝中寮、下寮解放区的新的军事冒险行动。费当说：美帝国主义不顾老挝的主权，阴谋直接派兵侵入老挝，显然是它准备把侵略老挝的战争扩大到整个印度支那的一个极其严重的新步骤。
    费当指出：美帝国主义的这一阴谋已经造成了极其严重的新危机。它使老挝和整个印度支那有被变成剧烈的战场的可能。
    费当号召老挝全国人民提高警惕，紧密地团结在老挝爱国战线党的周围，粉碎美帝国主义及其走狗的一切恶毒的阴谋。
