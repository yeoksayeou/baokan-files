Title: China's Foreign Cultural Commission Holds Farewell Banquet for Albanian Army Song and Dance Troupe; Vice Premier Lu Dingyi and Ambassador Nasse Attend the Banquet
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-06
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 5th – The Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries held a banquet this evening to warmly bid farewell to the Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe.
    Vice Premier of the State Council, Lu Dingyi, attended the banquet and proposed a toast at the meeting.
    Albanian Ambassador to China, Nesti Nase, and officials of the embassy were invited to attend.
    At the banquet, filled with fraternal friendship, Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Colonel Vani Baba, head of the Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe, spoke successively. They enthusiastically praised the unbreakable great friendship between the Chinese and Albanian people, built on the foundation of Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism.
    Zhang Xiruo said that the Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe's visit and performances in our country introduced the heroic spirit and revolutionary optimism displayed by the Albanian people in the socialist revolution and socialist construction to the Chinese people. He wished the Albanian people greater achievements in the struggle against US imperialism, against modern revisionism, and in defending the purity of Marxism-Leninism, under the leadership of the Albanian Labor Party headed by Comrade Enver Hoxha, and in building socialism and spreading socialist culture.
    In his speech, Colonel Vani Baba said that the Albanian people and army will, as always, join hands with the 650 million Chinese people and army to advance until we achieve final victory, until the final demise of our common enemy - US imperialism and its running dog, Khrushchev's modern revisionism. He wished the Chinese people and the Chinese People's Liberation Army greater achievements in the struggle to build socialism and strengthen the national defense forces of the socialist motherland.
    Gathering with the Albanian comrades tonight were Wang Bingnan, Li Qi, Li Qitao, Zhang Jinhui, Yu Ji, Wang Yuanfang and other responsible persons from relevant departments, as well as people from the capital's literary and art circles.

### 我对外文委饯别阿军歌舞团  陆定一副总理纳赛大使出席宴会

1966-01-06
第4版()
专栏：

    我对外文委饯别阿军歌舞团
    陆定一副总理纳赛大使出席宴会
    新华社五日讯　对外文化联络委员会今晚举行宴会，热烈欢送阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团。
    国务院副总理陆定一出席了宴会，并在会上祝酒。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使奈斯蒂·纳赛和大使馆官员应邀出席。
    在洋溢着兄弟情谊的宴会上，对外文委主任张奚若和阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团团长弗尼·巴巴上校先后讲话。他们热情颂扬中阿两国人民建立在马克思列宁主义和无产阶级国际主义基础上的牢不可破的伟大友谊。
    张奚若说，阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团在我国的访问演出，向中国人民介绍了阿尔巴尼亚人民在社会主义革命和社会主义建设中所表现的英雄气概和革命乐观主义精神。他祝阿尔巴尼亚人民在以恩维尔·霍查同志为首的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的领导下，在反对美帝国主义，反对现代修正主义，捍卫马克思列宁主义纯洁性的斗争中，在建设社会主义和传播社会主义文化中取得更大的成就。
    弗尼·巴巴上校在讲话中说，阿尔巴尼亚人民和军队将一如既往，同六亿五千万中国人民和军队携手前进，直到我们取得最后胜利，直到我们的共同敌人——美帝国主义及其走狗赫鲁晓夫现代修正主义的最后灭亡。他祝愿中国人民、中国人民解放军在建设社会主义、壮大社会主义祖国国防力量的斗争中取得更大的成就。
    今晚同阿尔巴尼亚同志们欢聚的，还有王炳南、李琦、李琦涛、张金辉、虞棘、王元方等有关方面负责人以及首都文艺界人士。
