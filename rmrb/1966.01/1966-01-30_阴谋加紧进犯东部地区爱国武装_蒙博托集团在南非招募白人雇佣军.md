Title: Conspiracy to Intensify Attacks on Patriotic Armed Forces in Eastern Region: Mobutu Group Recruits White Mercenaries in South Africa
Author:
Date: 1966-01-30
Edition: 4

    Plotting to Intensify Attacks on Patriotic Armed Forces in Eastern Region
    Mobutu Group Recruits White Mercenaries in South Africa
    Xinhua News Agency, Johannesburg, 24th – The Mobutu puppet regime of Congo (Leopoldville), in collusion with the South African colonial authorities, is stepping up its recruitment of white mercenaries in South Africa in order to suppress the Congolese people's patriotic armed forces.
    According to the South African *Sunday Express* on the 22nd, the large-scale recruitment of white mercenaries for the Mobutu Group is underway in Johannesburg, Durban, Cape Town, and other places in South Africa. Recently, 300 mercenaries have already been recruited in South Africa, 200 of whom have been transported to Congo (Leopoldville) and are undergoing training there.
    The newspaper said that the Mobutu Group's large-scale recruitment of mercenaries this time is in preparation for launching a large-scale offensive against the patriotic armed forces in the eastern region of Congo (Leopoldville).

### 阴谋加紧进犯东部地区爱国武装  蒙博托集团在南非招募白人雇佣军

1966-01-30
第4版()
专栏：

    阴谋加紧进犯东部地区爱国武装
    蒙博托集团在南非招募白人雇佣军
    新华社二十四日讯　约翰内斯堡消息：刚果（利）蒙博托傀儡政权勾结南非殖民当局加紧在南非招募白人雇佣军，以便镇压刚果人民爱国武装。
    据南非《星期日快报》二十二日透露，为蒙博托集团大规模招募白人雇佣军的工作正在南非约翰内斯堡、德班和开普敦等地进行。最近以来，已经在南非招募了三百名雇佣军，其中二百名已经运到刚果（利），正在那里接受训练。
    这家报纸说，蒙博托集团这次大量招募雇佣军，是为了准备向刚果（利）东部地区的爱国武装发动一次大规模的进攻。
