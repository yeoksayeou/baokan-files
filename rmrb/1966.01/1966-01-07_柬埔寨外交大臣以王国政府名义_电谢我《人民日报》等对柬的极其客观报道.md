Title: Cambodian Foreign Minister, on Behalf of the Royal Government, Cables Gratitude to Our *People's Daily* and Others for Their Extremely Objective Reporting on Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1966-01-07
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 6th – According to the Cambodian News Agency on the 6th, Cambodian Head of State Prince Sihanouk instructed the Foreign Minister, on behalf of the Khmer people and the Royal Government, to express gratitude to the chief editors of *People’s Daily*, *Guangming Daily*, and *Ta Kung Pao*, because the article entitled “Cambodia, Standing at the Forefront of the Anti-American Struggle in Southeast Asia” published by them contained extremely objective reporting on Cambodia.
    The cable also expressed gratitude to the Chinese journalists delegation led by Chang Zhiqing, pointing out that this delegation, which recently visited Cambodia, fully supported Cambodia's policy of neutrality and introduced to the world the concrete achievements of the People's Socialist Community since Cambodia's independence.

### 柬埔寨外交大臣以王国政府名义  电谢我《人民日报》等对柬的极其客观报道

1966-01-07
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨外交大臣以王国政府名义
    电谢我《人民日报》等对柬的极其客观报道
    新华社金边六日电　据柬埔寨新闻社六日报道，柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王责成外交大臣以高棉人民和王国政府的名义向《人民日报》、《光明日报》和《大公报》的各位总编辑表示感谢，因为他们刊载的一篇题为《屹立在东南亚反美斗争前哨的柬埔寨》的文章中对柬埔寨作了极其客观的报道。
    电报还向常芝青率领的中国新闻工作者代表团表示感谢，它指出，这个最近访问了柬埔寨的代表团完全支持柬埔寨的中立政策，向世界介绍了柬埔寨独立以来人民社会同盟所取得的具体成就。
