Title: The Liaison Delegation of the Vietnam People's Army Strongly Protests the Bombing of the North by American Aircraft; The American Rhetoric of "Seeking Peace" is a Complete Deception

Author:

Date: 1966-01-08
Edition: 3

    The Liaison Delegation of the Vietnam People's Army Strongly Protests the Bombing of the North by American Aircraft
    The American Rhetoric of "Seeking Peace" is a Complete Deception
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 7th. The head of the Liaison Delegation of the General Command of the Vietnam People's Army sent an urgent telegram to the Chairman of the International Commission of Vietnam on the 7th, strongly protesting and denouncing the bombing and strafing of the Na Meo area of Thanh Hoa Province in North Vietnam by American aircraft on the afternoon of the 5th.
    The telegram stated that at 17:30 on the 5th, three American F-105 jet fighters flew in from the direction of Laos and dropped four bombs and fired more than 30 rockets at the Na Meo area in the western part of Thanh Hoa Province, near the Vietnam-Laos border.
    The telegram said that just as the American government was clamoring about so-called "peaceful intentions" and "suspending the bombing of North Vietnam," American aircraft continued to violate the airspace of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, conducting reconnaissance and provocative activities, and even bombing and strafing the territory of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. This proves that the American authorities' rhetoric and methods of so-called "seeking peace" are completely deceptive, and their purpose is to deceive the American and world people, and to cover up the conspiracy and actions of the United States to strengthen and expand the war of aggression against Vietnam.

### 越南人民军联络代表团强烈抗议美机轰炸北方  美国“寻求和平”论调完全是骗人的

1966-01-08
第3版()
专栏：

    越南人民军联络代表团强烈抗议美机轰炸北方
    美国“寻求和平”论调完全是骗人的
    新华社河内七日电　越南人民军总司令部联络代表团团长七日打紧急电报给越南国际委员会主席，强烈抗议和控诉美国飞机五日下午轰炸和扫射越南北方清化省纳苗地区。
    电报说，五日十七时三十分，美国F—105型喷气机三架，从老挝方向飞来，向清化省西部靠近越南—老挝边境的纳苗地区投掷了四颗炸弹和发射了三十多枚火箭。
    电报说，正当美国政府叫嚷所谓“和平诚意”和“暂停轰炸北越”的时候，美国飞机仍然继续侵犯越南民主共和国领空，进行侦察和挑衅活动，甚至轰炸、扫射越南民主共和国的领土。这证明了美国当局的所谓“寻求和平”的论调和手段，完全是骗人的，其目的是迷惑美国和世界人民，掩盖美国强化和扩大侵略越南战争的阴谋和行动。
