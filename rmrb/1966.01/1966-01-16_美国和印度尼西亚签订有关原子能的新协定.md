Title: US and Indonesia Sign New Agreement on Atomic Energy
Author:
Date: 1966-01-16
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, January 13th: The United States and Indonesia signed an agreement in Washington on the 12th to extend the agreement on the peaceful uses of atomic energy signed between the two countries in June 1960 for another five years.

    The new agreement stipulates that the atomic reactors provided by the United States to Indonesia will be used under the supervision of the International Atomic Energy Agency.

    Assistant Secretary of State for Far Eastern Affairs Bundy and Indonesian Ambassador to the United States Lambertus N. Palar signed the agreement on behalf of their respective countries.

### 美国和印度尼西亚签订有关原子能的新协定

1966-01-16
第5版()
专栏：

    美国和印度尼西亚签订有关原子能的新协定
    新华社十三日讯　华盛顿消息：美国和印度尼西亚十二日在华盛顿签订一项协定，决定把一九六○年六月间两国签订的关于和平利用原子能的协定再延长五年。
    新协定规定，美国向印度尼西亚提供的原子反应堆将在国际原子能机构的监督下使用。
    美国负责远东事务助理国务卿邦迪和印度尼西亚驻美国大使兰贝图斯·尼·巴拉分别代表两国在协定上签字。
