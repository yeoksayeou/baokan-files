Title: Comrade Hill Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1966-01-05
Edition: 1

    Comrade Hill Leaves Beijing for Home
    Comrades Deng Xiaoping, Kang Sheng, and Others See Him Off at the Railway Station
    Xinhua News Agency, 4th. E.F. Hill, Chairman of the Communist Party of Australia (Marxist-Leninist), and his wife, Joyce Hill, who were invited to visit our country, left Beijing by train today to return home.
    Those who saw them off at the station included Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, and his wife, Zhuo Lin; Kang Sheng, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat, and his wife, Cao Yi'ou; Liu Ningyi, Member of the CPC Central Committee; Zhao Yimin, Alternate Member of the CPC Central Committee; and Tang Mingzhao, Yu Zhiying, and Wang Wenzhong, responsible working personnel of the organs of the CPC Central Committee. Also present to see them off was F. Russell, a member of the leading body of the Communist Party of Australia (Marxist-Leninist), who is currently in Beijing.

### 希尔同志离京回国

1966-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

    希尔同志离京回国
    邓小平康生等同志到车站欢送
    新华社四日讯应邀来我国访问的澳大利亚共产党（马克思列宁主义者）主席爱·弗·希尔和夫人乔伊斯·希尔，今天乘火车离北京回国。
    到车站送行的，有中共中央总书记邓小平和夫人卓琳，中共中央政治局候补委员、书记处书记康生和夫人曹轶欧，中共中央委员刘宁一，中共中央候补委员赵毅敏，中共中央机关负责工作人员唐明照、俞志英、王文忠等。前往送行的，还有正在北京的澳大利亚共产党（马克思列宁主义者）领导机构成员弗·罗素。
