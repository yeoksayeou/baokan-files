Title: Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1966-01-07
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, January 6th – The Albanian People’s Army Song and Dance Troupe concluded its visit and performances in our country and left Beijing for home tonight by special train.
    Li Qiutao, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Li Qi, Vice Minister of Culture, and responsible persons from relevant departments, including Chen Boqing, Zhang Jinhui, Yu Ji, Wang Yuanfang, Ding Bo, and Jin Guangzu, as well as hundreds of literary and art workers from the capital, warmly saw them off at the station.
    Tonight, Beijing Railway Station was filled with the sound of gongs and drums, brimming with fraternal friendship. When the Albanian artists arrived at the station, the people seeing them off warmly shook hands and embraced them, then bid them farewell with reluctance.
    Albanian Ambassador to China, Nesti Nase, and his wife, Military Attaché Colonel Feriq Hatu of the Embassy, and diplomatic officials from the Vietnamese Embassy in China, also went to the station to see them off.

### 阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团离京回国

1966-01-07
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团离京回国
    新华社六日讯　阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团结束了在我国的访问演出，今晚乘专车离开北京回国。
    对外文化联络委员会副主任李琦涛、文化部副部长李琦，有关方面负责人陈伯清、张金辉、虞棘、王元方、丁波、金光祖，以及首都文艺工作者数百人，到车站热烈欢送。
    今晚，北京车站锣鼓喧天，洋溢着兄弟般的情谊。当阿尔巴尼亚艺术家们到达车站时，欢送的人们同他们热情握手、拥抱，然后依依话别。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使奈斯蒂·纳赛和夫人、大使馆武官费利克·哈图上校，越南驻中国大使馆外交官员，也到车站送行。
