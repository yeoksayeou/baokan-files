Title: Spring Festival Gathering of Taiwan Compatriots in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-01-23
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, January 22nd - Over two hundred Taiwan compatriots in Beijing held a Spring Festival gathering this afternoon to celebrate the Lunar New Year.
    At the gathering, Xu Mengshan, Secretary-General of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League headquarters, congratulated everyone on their contributions to the socialist revolution and socialist construction of the motherland in the past year, and wished everyone even greater achievements in 1966. The atmosphere at the gathering was filled with joy. Taiwan compatriots performed songs and other artistic programs such as "Taiwanese People Long for Liberation" and "We Must Plant the Banner of Victory on Taiwan."

### 在京台湾籍同胞春节联欢

1966-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    在京台湾籍同胞春节联欢
    新华社二十二日讯  在北京的台湾省籍同胞二百多人，今天下午举行联欢会，欢度新春佳节。
    会上，台湾民主自治同盟总部秘书长徐萌山对大家在过去一年参加祖国社会主义革命和社会主义建设作出的贡献，表示祝贺，并祝大家在一九六六年中，取得更大的成绩。联欢会上喜气洋洋。台湾省籍同胞演出了《台湾人民盼解放》、《一定要把胜利的旗帜插到台湾》等歌曲和其他文艺节目。
