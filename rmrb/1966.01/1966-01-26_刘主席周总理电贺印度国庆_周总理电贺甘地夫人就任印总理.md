Title: Chairman Liu and Premier Zhou Send Congratulatory Messages on India's National Day; Premier Zhou Sends Congratulatory Message to Mrs. Gandhi on Assuming Office as Prime Minister of India
Author:
Date: 1966-01-26
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, January 25th—Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram on January 25th to Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan, President of the Republic of India, congratulating him on the National Day of the Republic of India (January 26th).
    The full text of the telegram is as follows:
    To His Excellency Dr. Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan, President of the Republic of India, New Delhi:
    On the occasion of the National Day of the Republic of India, I extend my congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of India. I wish your country prosperity and your people happiness.
          Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                January 25, 1966
    Xinhua News Agency, January 25th—Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram on January 25th to Mrs. Indira Gandhi, Prime Minister of the Republic of India, congratulating her on the National Day of the Republic of India.
    The full text of the telegram is as follows:
    To Her Excellency Mrs. Indira Gandhi, Prime Minister of the Republic of India, New Delhi:
    On the occasion of the National Day of the Republic of India, I extend my congratulations to Your Excellency and the Government of the Republic of India. I wish the people of your country happiness.
    Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                January 25, 1966
    Xinhua News Agency, January 25th—Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram on January 25th to Mrs. Indira Gandhi, Prime Minister of the Republic of India, congratulating her on assuming office as Prime Minister of the Republic of India.
    The full text of the telegram is as follows:
    To Her Excellency Mrs. Indira Gandhi, Prime Minister of the Republic of India, New Delhi:
    As Your Excellency assumes office as Prime Minister of the Republic of India, please accept my heartfelt congratulations and best wishes. I wish the Republic of India prosperity and its people happiness.
    Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                January 25, 1966

### 刘主席周总理电贺印度国庆  周总理电贺甘地夫人就任印总理

1966-01-26
第4版()
专栏：

    刘主席周总理电贺印度国庆
    周总理电贺甘地夫人就任印总理
    新华社二十五日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇二十五日打电报给印度共和国总统萨瓦帕利·拉达克里希南，祝贺印度共和国国庆（一月二十六日）。
    电报全文如下：新德里印度共和国总统萨瓦帕利·拉达克里希南博士阁下：
    在印度共和国国庆的时候，我向阁下和印度人民致以祝贺。祝贵国繁荣，人民幸福。
          中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                一九六六年一月二十五日
    新华社二十五日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来二十五日打电报给印度共和国总理英迪拉·甘地夫人，祝贺印度共和国国庆。
    电报全文如下：新德里印度共和国总理英迪拉·甘地夫人阁下：
    在印度共和国国庆的时候，我向阁下和印度共和国政府表示祝贺。祝贵国人民幸福。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                一九六六年一月二十五日
    新华社二十五日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来二十五日打电报给印度共和国总理英迪拉·甘地夫人，祝贺她就任印度共和国总理。
    电报全文如下：新德里印度共和国总理英迪拉·甘地夫人阁下：
    在阁下就任印度共和国总理的时候，请接受我衷心的祝贺和良好的祝愿。祝印度共和国繁荣、人民幸福。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                一九六六年一月二十五日
