Title: Soviet Delegation Arrives in Beijing on its Way Home from Vietnam

Author:

Date: 1966-01-14
Edition: 4

    Soviet Delegation Arrives in Beijing on its Way Home from Vietnam
    Xinhua News Agency, January 13th—The Soviet delegation led by Shelepin, member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the CPSU and Secretary of the Central Committee, arrived in Beijing today on its way home after concluding its visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
    Li Xiannian, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the CPC and Vice Premier of the State Council, as well as other responsible persons including Zhao Yimin and Wang Bingnan, went to the airport to greet them.
    Also present at the airport were: Soviet Ambassador to China, Lapin, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, Hoang Bac, representative of the Permanent Delegation of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front in China, Hoang Van Loi, as well as envoys to China from some other countries.
    Tonight, Comrade Li Xiannian hosted a banquet for the Soviet guests. Afterwards, the guests and hosts watched a Chinese acrobatic performance together.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 13th—The Soviet delegation led by Shelepin, member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the CPSU and Secretary of the Central Committee, visited the Democratic Republic of Vietnam from January 7th to 13th. The delegation left Hanoi by plane this morning, the 13th, to return home.

### 苏联代表团从越南回国途中到北京

1966-01-14
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联代表团从越南回国途中到北京
    新华社十三日讯　由苏共中央主席团委员、中央书记谢列平率领的苏联代表团，结束了对越南民主共和国的访问，在回国途中今天到达北京。
    前往机场迎接的，有中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理李先念，以及其他方面的负责人赵毅敏、王炳南等。
    到机场迎接的还有：苏联驻中国大使拉宾，越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办黄北，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代表黄万里，以及其他一些国家驻中国的使节。
    今天晚上，李先念同志设宴招待苏联客人。随后，宾主一同观看了中国杂技表演。
    新华社河内十三日电　由苏共中央主席团委员、中央书记谢列平率领的苏联代表团，在一月七日到十三日对越南民主共和国进行了访问。代表团在十三日上午乘飞机离开河内回国。
