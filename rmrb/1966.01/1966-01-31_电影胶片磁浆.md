Title: Film Magnetic Slurry

Author:
Date: 1966-01-31
Edition: 3

	New Construction New Products

    Film Magnetic Slurry
    The Shanghai 革新 Plastic Factory recently successfully trial-produced a film magnetic slurry. By coating this magnetic slurry on the edge of the film, it is possible to record the required dialect, so that people of relevant nationalities or regions can understand the words in the film when it is shown. If this film needs to be changed to another dialect, it is only necessary to erase the original sound and record the new dialect.
    In the past, with the photosensitive sound recording method, one film could only fix one language. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 电影胶片磁浆

1966-01-31
第3版()
专栏：新建设  新产品

    电影胶片磁浆
    上海革新塑料厂最近试制成功一种电影胶片磁浆。把这种磁浆涂在电影胶片边上，就可以录下所需要的方言，放映电影时有关的民族或地区的人便能听懂电影里的话。如果这部电影需要调换另外一种方言，只要抹去原音，录上新的方言就行了。
    过去感光发音的办法，一部胶片只能固定一种语言。 　（据新华社讯）
