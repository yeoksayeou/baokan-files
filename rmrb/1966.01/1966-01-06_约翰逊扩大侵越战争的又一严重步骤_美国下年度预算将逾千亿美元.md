Title: Johnson Takes Another Serious Step in Expanding the War of Aggression Against Vietnam; U.S. Budget for Next Year to Exceed $100 Billion

Author:

Date: 1966-01-06
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 5th -- White House Press Secretary Moyers revealed to reporters on the 4th that the U.S. government's total budget expenditure will reach between $110 billion and $115 billion in the 1967 fiscal year, which begins on July 1st this year.
    This figure will set the highest record for federal budget expenditure in U.S. history. It reflects the increasingly heavy burden on U.S. finances brought about by the "escalation" of the U.S. war of aggression against Vietnam.
    Moyers said he expected President Johnson to submit budget proposals to Congress within the legally prescribed deadline (January 25th this year).
    Moyers also said that before submitting the new budget to Congress, the President will again request Congress to approve additional appropriations for Vietnam expenditures within the current fiscal year. He declined to specify how much additional funding would be requested.
    However, according to Western news agencies citing "qualified authorities" in Washington, Johnson will soon request nearly $13 billion in additional military appropriations to meet the expenditure needs of the expanded war of aggression against Vietnam.
    United Press International reported that "this request is much larger than previously predicted, which will increase the total military appropriations for the current fiscal year to more than $62 billion."
    In addition, according to Moyers, expenditure within the current fiscal year is estimated to reach about $105 billion to $107 billion, with revenue of approximately $96.5 billion. That is to say, the budget deficit will reach $8.5 billion to $10.5 billion.

### 约翰逊扩大侵越战争的又一严重步骤  美国下年度预算将逾千亿美元

1966-01-06
第5版()
专栏：

    约翰逊扩大侵越战争的又一严重步骤
    美国下年度预算将逾千亿美元
    新华社五日讯　华盛顿消息：据白宫新闻秘书莫耶斯四日向新闻记者透露，在今年七月一日开始的一九六七财政年度内，美国政府的预算开支总额将达一千一百亿美元到一千一百五十亿美元之间。
    这个数字将开创美国有史以来联邦预算开支的最高纪录。它反映了美国侵越战争“不断升级”给美国财政带来的日益沉重的负担。
    莫耶斯说，他预料约翰逊总统将按照法律规定的限期
  （今年一月二十五日）向国会提出预算建议。
    莫耶斯还说，在向国会提出新预算之前，总统将再次要求国会对本财政年度内的越南开支追加拨款。他拒绝说明具体要求追加多少。
    但是据西方通讯社引华盛顿“有资格的权威方面”的消息说，约翰逊不久将要求追加将近一百三十亿美元的军事拨款来应付扩大侵越战争的开支需要。
    合众国际社在报道中说，“这个要求比事先的预测要大得多，这样就将使本财政年度的军事拨款总额增加到六百二十亿美元以上”。
    另外，据莫耶斯说，本财政年度内的开支估计将达到一千零五十亿到一千零七十亿美元左右，收入大约为九百六十五亿美元。这就是说，预算赤字将达到八十五亿到一百零五亿美元。
