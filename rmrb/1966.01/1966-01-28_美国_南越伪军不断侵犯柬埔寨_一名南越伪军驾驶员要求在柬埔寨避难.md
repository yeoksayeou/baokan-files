Title: US-South Vietnamese Puppet Troops Continuously Invade Cambodia; A South Vietnamese Puppet Army Pilot Requests Asylum in Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1966-01-28
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 26th - According to the Cambodian News Agency on the 26th, US-South Vietnamese puppet troops have recently been continuously invading Cambodian territory and airspace.
    On the evening of January 3rd, US-South Vietnamese puppet troops fired three mortar shells into Cambodian territory, landing near the village of Shea Pa Dai in Takeo Province.
    On the afternoon of January 13th, South Vietnamese puppet troops fired nine mortar shells at a Cambodian outpost in Prey Veng Province.
    On the evening of January 20th, South Vietnamese puppet troops fired on Song Village in Kiri Vong, Takeo Province, with automatic weapons and mortars. A peasant woman was shot and wounded in the right arm.
    On the morning of January 16th, South Vietnamese puppet troops fired five mortar shells at a provincial defense army outpost in Ba Dei, Cambodia.
    On January 8th and 10th, US-South Vietnamese puppet army aircraft violated Cambodian airspace in Kampong Cham Province three times.
    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 27th - Cambodian Secretary of State for Information, Xie Sang, announced at a press conference on the 26th that a South Vietnamese puppet army pilot voluntarily landed in Rattanakiri Province, Cambodia, on January 20th in an L-19 observation aircraft and requested asylum from the Cambodian authorities.

### 美国—南越伪军不断侵犯柬埔寨  一名南越伪军驾驶员要求在柬埔寨避难

1966-01-28
第4版()
专栏：

    美国—南越伪军不断侵犯柬埔寨
    一名南越伪军驾驶员要求在柬埔寨避难
    新华社金边二十六日电　据柬埔寨新闻社二十六日报道，美国—南越伪军最近又不断侵犯柬埔寨的领土和领空。
    一月三日晚上，美国—南越伪军向柬埔寨境内发射了三发迫击炮弹，这些炮弹落在距茶胶省谢帕代村附近。
    一月十三日下午，南越伪军向波萝勉省的一个柬埔寨哨所发射了九发迫击炮弹。
    一月二十日晚上，南越伪军用自动武器和迫击炮向茶胶省吉里翁地方松村射击。一个农妇的右臂被击中受伤。
    一月十六日上午，南越伪军向柬埔寨境内巴代地方的省防军哨所发射了五发迫击炮弹。
    一月八日和十日，美国—南越伪军飞机曾经三次侵犯磅湛省的柬埔寨领空。
    据新华社金边二十七日电　柬埔寨新闻国务秘书谢桑在二十六日举行的记者招待会上宣布，一名南越伪军驾驶员一月二十日驾着一架L—19型观察机，自愿在柬埔寨腊塔纳基里省降落，并且要求柬埔寨当局给予避难。
