Title: Two Argentine Scholars Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-01-27
Edition: 5

    Two Argentine Scholars Arrive in Beijing
    (Xinhua News Agency, January 25th) Professor Rolando Victor Garcia, Vice Chairman of the Argentine National Council for Scientific and Technical Research, Dean of the Faculty of Sciences of the University of Buenos Aires, and mathematician, and his wife, Emilia, a Master of Psychology, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 24th at the invitation of the National Committee of the Scientific and Technological Association of the People's Republic of China for a visit to our country. Hua Luogeng, Chairman of the Chinese Mathematical Society, and others were at the airport to greet them.

### 阿根廷两学者到京

1966-01-27
第5版()
专栏：

    阿根廷两学者到京
    新华社二十五日讯　应中华人民共和国科学技术协会全国委员会邀请来我国访问的阿根廷科学研究委员会副主席、布宜诺斯艾利斯大学理学院院长、数学家罗兰多·维克多·加尔西亚教授和他的夫人心理学硕士艾米利亚二十四日乘飞机到达北京。前往机场迎接的有中国数学会理事长华罗庚等。
