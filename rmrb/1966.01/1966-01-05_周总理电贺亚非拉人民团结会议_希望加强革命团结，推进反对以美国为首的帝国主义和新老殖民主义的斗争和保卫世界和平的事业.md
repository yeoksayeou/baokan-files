Title: Premier Zhou Sends Congratulatory Telegram to the Tricontinental Conference, Hoping to Strengthen Revolutionary Unity, Advance the Struggle Against Imperialism Headed by the United States and Old and New Colonialism, and Safeguard the Cause of World Peace
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-05
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, sent a telegram on the 1st to congratulate the Asian, African, and Latin American People's Solidarity Conference opening in Havana on the 3rd. The full text of the congratulatory telegram is as follows:
    Havana, Cuba, Asian, African, and Latin American People's Solidarity Conference:
    As the Asian, African, and Latin American People's Solidarity Conference is held in Havana, I, on behalf of the Chinese government and the Chinese people, extend warm congratulations to the conference, hoping that the conference will make positive contributions to strengthening the revolutionary unity of the Asian, African, and Latin American people, advancing the struggle against imperialism headed by the United States and old and new colonialism, and safeguarding the cause of world peace.
      Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai
                      January 1, 1966

### 周总理电贺亚非拉人民团结会议  希望加强革命团结，推进反对以美国为首的帝国主义和新老殖民主义的斗争和保卫世界和平的事业

1966-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电贺亚非拉人民团结会议
    希望加强革命团结，推进反对以美国为首的帝国主义和新老殖民主义的斗争和保卫世界和平的事业
    新华社四日讯中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来一日打电报祝贺三日在哈瓦那开幕的亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲人民团结会议，贺电全文如下：古巴哈瓦那亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲人民团结会议
    当亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲人民团结会议在哈瓦那举行之际，我代表中国政府和中国人民向会议表示热烈的祝贺，希望会议对加强亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲人民的革命团结、推进反对以美国为首的帝国主义和新老殖民主义的斗争和保卫世界和平的事业，作出积极的贡献。
      中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来
                      一九六六年一月一日
