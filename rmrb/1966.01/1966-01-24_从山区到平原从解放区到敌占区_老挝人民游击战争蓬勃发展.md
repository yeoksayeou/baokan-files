Title: From the Mountains to the Plains, From Liberated Areas to Enemy-Occupied Areas: The Laotian People's Guerrilla Warfare Flourshes

Author: 
Date: 1966-01-24
Edition: 3

    According to a dispatch from Kang Kai by Xinhua News Agency, Radio Pathet Lao broadcasted that guerrilla forces in various parts of Laos have rapidly developed and grown stronger in the past year in the battles against the attacks of U.S. imperialism and its running dogs. From the mountains to the plains, from liberated areas to enemy-occupied areas, the movement of people's guerrilla warfare is developing more vigorously.
    The radio station said that these guerrilla forces, from sporadic attacks on the enemy to coordinating with the troops to annihilate enemy forces, have made the enemy suffer blows everywhere and become panic-stricken. In the first half of last year alone, the guerrilla forces carried out more than 200 counter-attack and "mopping-up" battles, killing 214 enemy soldiers, wounding 43, capturing 5, and shooting down 5 enemy planes, capturing 1 U.S. pilot alive.
    Since October of last year, the Vientiane Province guerrilla forces have engaged in more than 20 battles, killing 33 enemy soldiers, wounding 15, shooting down 2 U.S. helicopters, and seizing a large number of military supplies.
    In December of last year, the guerrilla forces in the provinces of Xieng Khouang, Khammouane, and the lower Laotian region killed 142 enemy soldiers and bandits, wounded 80, captured 78, and seized 192 pieces of various weapons.
    The guerrilla forces in the lower Laotian region, in coordination with local troops, carried out many "mopping-up" battles last year, killing 464 enemy soldiers, wounding 218, capturing 108, and seizing 278 pieces of various weapons.
    The Savannakhet Province guerrilla forces were very active in November and December of last year. The guerrilla forces in Lakhonpheng County attacked many enemy strongholds, forcing the defending enemy to flee. Recently, the guerrilla forces in the Gengkok County region destroyed many bridges on Highway 13, killing 96 enemy soldiers, wounding many, capturing 1, and damaging an enemy plane.

### 从山区到平原从解放区到敌占区  老挝人民游击战争蓬勃发展

1966-01-24
第3版()
专栏：

    从山区到平原从解放区到敌占区
    老挝人民游击战争蓬勃发展
    据新华社康开电  据巴特寮电台广播：老挝各地的游击队去年在反击美帝国主义和它的走狗军队进攻的战斗中迅速发展壮大，从山区到平原，从解放区到敌占区，人民游击战争的运动日益蓬勃地发展。
    电台说，这些游击队从零星地袭击敌人，到配合部队歼灭成股的敌人，使敌人到处都挨打，惊慌万状。仅去年上半年，游击队就进行了二百多次反击战和反“扫荡”战斗，打死敌军二百一十四名，打伤四十三名，俘虏五名，并打落敌机五架，活捉美国飞行员一名。
    从去年十月份以来，万象省游击队共进行了二十多次战斗，打死敌人三十三名，打伤十五名；打落美国直升飞机两架；并且缴获大批军用物资。
    川圹、甘蒙省和下寮地区的游击队，在去年十二月份，共打死敌军和土匪一百四十二名，打伤八十名，俘虏七十八名，缴获各种武器一百九十二件。
    下寮地区游击队同地方部队配合，在去年一年中进行了很多次反“扫荡”战斗，共打死敌人四百六十四名，打伤二百一十八名，俘虏一百零八名，缴获各种武器二百七十八件。
    沙湾拿吉省游击队在去年十一、十二两个月中非常活跃。拉哈南县游击队袭击了敌人的许多据点，迫使守敌逃窜。最近，更谷县地区游击队在十三号公路上破坏了许多桥梁，打死敌人九十六名，打伤多名，俘虏一名；并击伤敌机一架。
