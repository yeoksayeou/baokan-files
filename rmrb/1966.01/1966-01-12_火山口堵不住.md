Title: The Volcano Cannot Be Blocked

Author:
Date: 1966-01-12
Edition: 5

    The Volcano Cannot Be Blocked
    More than 30,000 people in Panama's capital held a large-scale demonstration on the 9th, reiterating their demand to reclaim sovereignty over the Canal Zone, which they raised two years ago. The people of the Dominican Republic's capital responded to the strike call issued by various trade unions on the 10th and launched a heroic struggle against the American aggressor troops. The anti-American tidal wave in the "backyard" of U.S. imperialism has once again shaken the Washington ruling clique.
    The new struggles of the people of Panama and the Dominican Republic show that these two places, which are occupied by U.S. imperialism, are still full of explosive situations, which may be triggered at any time, igniting a new anti-American anger.
    This situation is determined by the irreconcilable contradiction between the aggressor and the aggressed. U.S. imperialism can use various means, either armed suppression or political deception, to temporarily suppress or alleviate the resistance of the invaded people for a time, but it cannot solve the existing contradictions. On the contrary, it can only deepen the contradictions. This is why the anti-American struggle in Latin America always rises and falls. For U.S. imperialism, there are volcanoes everywhere, and these volcanoes cannot be blocked.

### 火山口堵不住

1966-01-12
第5版()
专栏：短评

    火山口堵不住
    巴拿马首都三万多人九日举行大规模示威，重申他们在两年前提出的收回运河区主权的要求。多米尼加首都人民响应各工会组织在十日发出的罢工号召，向美国侵略军展开了英勇的搏斗。美帝国主义“后院”掀起的反美巨浪，再一次震动了华盛顿统治集团。
    巴拿马和多米尼加人民的新的斗争说明，在美帝国主义霸占下的这两个地方，仍然充满着爆炸性的局势，随时都可能一触即发，燃起一场新的反美怒火。
    这种局势，是由侵略者同被侵略者之间不可调和的矛盾决定的。美帝国主义可以以各种各样的手段，或武力镇压，或政治欺骗，把被侵略的人民的反抗暂时压下去，或缓和于一时，但是并不可能解决存在的矛盾，相反的，只能使得矛盾日益加深。这就是为什么在拉丁美洲，反美斗争的浪潮总是此伏彼起。对美帝国主义来说，到处都是火山，这些火山口是堵塞不住的。
