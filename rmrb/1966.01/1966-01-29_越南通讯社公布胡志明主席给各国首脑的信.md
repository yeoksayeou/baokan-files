Title: Vietnam News Agency Publishes Ho Chi Minh's Letter to Heads of State

Author:

Date: 1966-01-29
Edition: 2

    Vietnam News Agency Publishes Ho Chi Minh's Letter to Heads of State
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 28th (dispatch): According to Vietnam News Agency, Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, wrote letters on January 24th to the heads of all socialist countries, many countries associated with the 1954 Geneva Agreements on Vietnam, many nationalist countries, and many other countries concerned about the situation in Vietnam.
    The Vietnam News Agency today published President Ho Chi Minh's letter.

### 越南通讯社公布胡志明主席给各国首脑的信

1966-01-29
第2版()
专栏：

    越南通讯社公布胡志明主席给各国首脑的信
    新华社河内二十八日电　据越南通讯社报道：越南民主共和国主席胡志明在二十四日写信给各社会主义国家、许多同一九五四年关于越南问题的日内瓦协议有关的国家、许多民族主义国家、许多关心越南形势的其他国家的首脑。
    越南通讯社今天公布了胡志明主席的信件。
