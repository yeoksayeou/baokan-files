Title: Western European Public Opinion Exposes Johnson's Hypocrisy and Duplicity; The United States Conceals the Expansion of War Under the Guise of Sham "Peace Talks"
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-11
Edition: 3

    According to Xinhua News Agency, January 9th, some Western European public opinion has pointed out the hypocrisy of the American "peace offensive." They believe that the United States is using the name of "peace negotiations" to conceal the reality of expanding the war.

    The French newspaper *Les Échos* published an editorial on January 7th, saying that Johnson's "peace offensive" is merely a "smokescreen" to conceal the American determination to continue the war. The editorial said: "In the final analysis, the United States is not interested in peace," and the reason it expresses its willingness to "negotiate peace" is because American policy has "lost popular support and the support of some public opinion" domestically. Secondly, it is because "the allies are also divided" and are being criticized by some allies.

    The Swedish newspaper *Dagens Nyheter* said in a commentary on January 3rd: "The American desire for peace is hypocritical and duplicitous. Peace negotiations are impossible at present." The Swedish *Aftonbladet* said, "If the United States really wants to abide by the Geneva Accords, it should abandon its military position in South Vietnam."

    The West German *Deutsche Volkszeitung* said on January 7th: "It seems that despite all the vows of peace, the US government has actually firmly resolved to continue spiraling upwards in the Vietnam War... The United States' military, financial and political preparations in 1966 also demonstrate this."

### 西欧国家舆论揭露约翰逊的虚伪和伪善  美国假“和谈”之名掩盖扩大战争之实

1966-01-11
第3版()
专栏：

    西欧国家舆论揭露约翰逊的虚伪和伪善
    美国假“和谈”之名掩盖扩大战争之实
    据新华社九日讯  一些西欧国家的舆论指出了美国“和平攻势”的虚伪性。它们认为，美国是在借“和平谈判”之名，掩盖扩大战争之实。
    法国《回声报》七日发表社论说，约翰逊的“和平攻势”仅仅是掩盖美国决心继续进行战争的“烟幕”。社论说：“归根结蒂，美国对和平是不感兴趣的”，它所以表示愿意“和谈”，是因为美国的政策在国内“失去民心和一些舆论的支持”。其次，是因为“盟国也是四分五裂”，并受到一些盟国的批评。
    瑞典报纸《每日新闻》在三日的一篇评论中说：“美国的和平愿望是虚伪和伪善的。在目前不可能进行和平谈判。”瑞典《晚报》说，“如果美国真的要遵守日内瓦协议，就应该放弃它在南越的军事地位。”
    西德的《德意志人民报》一月七日说：“看来，尽管有一切和平誓言，美国政府实际上已坚定地下了决心，继续在越南战争的螺旋线上打转……美国一九六六年在军事、财政和政治上的准备也表明这一点。”
