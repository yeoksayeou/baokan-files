Title: The United States Intensifies Preparations to Expand the War of Aggression Against Vietnam: Training Reserve Forces, Requisitioning Reserve Ships, and Expediting the Shipment of Munitions and Supplies

Author: 
Date: 1966-01-24
Edition: 3

    According to a dispatch from Xinhua News Agency on the 23rd from Washington: The Johnson administration is stepping up the training of reserve forces and helicopter pilots, and plans to mobilize another batch of reserve ships to rush munitions and supplies to South Vietnam, actively preparing to expand the war of aggression against Vietnam.

    According to a report by United Press International on the 22nd, a "specially selected" reserve force of 150,000 men, established by US Secretary of Defense McNamara last summer, is undergoing intensive training and has received "extra funds and equipment." Citing military sources, the report stated that this reserve force, mainly composed of three infantry divisions and six brigades, is "approaching combat readiness" and will be ready for combat within thirty days of receiving orders by this autumn. Prior to this, according to a statement on the 17th by Thorson, the commander of the Alabama Army Aviation Center, the US Army is stepping up the training of helicopter pilots and mechanics in preparation for expanding the war of aggression against Vietnam.

    According to a report by Reuters, officials in Washington revealed that the US government is considering using twenty-five old ships built more than twenty years ago to cope with the increasingly serious supply problems in the war of aggression against Vietnam.

### 美国加紧准备扩大侵越战争  训练后备部队，调用后备船只，赶运军火物资

1966-01-24
第3版()
专栏：

    美国加紧准备扩大侵越战争
    训练后备部队，调用后备船只，赶运军火物资
    据新华社二十三日讯  华盛顿消息：约翰逊政府正在加紧训练后备部队和直升飞机驾驶员，并且计划再动用一批后备船只向南越赶运军火、物资，积极准备扩大侵越战争。
    据合众国际社二十二日报道，美国国防部长麦克纳马拉去年夏天下令建立的一支“经过挑选”的十五万名后备部队，正在接受紧张的训练，并且得到了“额外的款项和装备”。消息引军方人士的话说，这支主要包括三个步兵师和六个旅的后备部队，正“接近具有战斗实力”，到今年秋天就可以在接到命令三十天之内，作好战斗准备。在这以前，据亚拉巴马州陆军航空中心的司令官托尔森十七日透露，美国陆军正为扩大侵越战争加紧训练直升飞机驾驶员和机械士。
    另据路透社报道，华盛顿的官员们透露，美国政府正在考虑动用二十五艘在二十多年前建造的老船，来应付侵越战争中越来越严重的供应问题。
