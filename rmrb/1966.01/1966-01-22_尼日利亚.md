    Nigeria
    Nigeria is located in West Africa, bordering the Gulf of Guinea in the Atlantic Ocean, with an area of 923,772 square kilometers. According to the 1963 statistics, the population is 55.65 million, making it the most populous country in Africa. There are more than 250 tribes in the country, and the capital is Lagos.
    The climate in Nigeria is hot, and the country is rich in natural resources. The economy is mainly agricultural. British capital occupies an important position in the Nigerian economy, but in recent years, American capital has also vigorously penetrated. Nigeria is divided into four regions: East, West, North, and Mid-West. Each region has a regional government and a regional assembly, with ruling and opposition parties.
                                  (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 尼日利亚

1966-01-22
第3版()
专栏：资料

    尼日利亚
    尼日利亚位于非洲西部，濒临大西洋的几内亚湾，面积九十二万三千七百七十二平方公里。人口据一九六三年统计是五千五百六十五万人，是非洲人口最多的一个国家。全国共有二百五十多个部族，首都是拉各斯。
    尼日利亚气候炎热，物产富饶。经济以农业为主。在尼日利亚经济中，英国资本占重要地位，但近年来，美国资本也大力渗入。尼日利亚分东西北和中西四个区，每区都设区政府和区议会，有执政党和反对党。
                                  （据新华社）
