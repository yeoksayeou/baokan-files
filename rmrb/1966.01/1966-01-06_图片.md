Title: Zhang Gengxin
Author: Zhang Gengxin
Date: 1966-01-06
Edition: 2

    The simple inclined plane boat lift built at the Datong Weir in Ningbo, Zhejiang, is transporting boats over the dam. Using this type of boat lift increases the speed of boats crossing the dam by six times compared to the previous manual method, and also saves labor and costs. Xinhua News Agency Reporter Zhang Gengxin photographed.
