Title: Sino-Myanmar Friendship Association Holds Reception Celebrating the 18th Anniversary of Myanmar's Independence and the 6th Anniversary of the Sino-Myanmar Treaty of Friendship and Non-Aggression
Author:
Date: 1966-01-13
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, January 12th, the China-Myanmar Friendship Association held a reception this afternoon to warmly celebrate the 18th anniversary of the independence of the Union of Burma and the 6th anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Burmese Treaty of Friendship and Non-Aggression.
    Zhang Youyu, president of the China-Myanmar Friendship Association, spoke at the reception. He congratulated the Union of Burma, under the leadership of Chairman Ne Win, on the significant achievements it has made in defending national independence and state sovereignty, opposing imperialist interference and subversion, and in developing national economy and national culture. Zhang Youyu said that the Burmese government has consistently pursued a policy of peace and neutrality. The Sino-Burmese Treaty of Friendship and Non-Aggression, signed by the two countries six years ago, embodies the great vitality of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence jointly advocated by the two countries and has made a valuable contribution to maintaining peace in Asia and the world.
    Burmese Ambassador to China, Sama Duwa Sinwa Nawng, praised the great achievements of the Chinese people under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong, and praised the profound "Paukphaw" friendship between the peoples of Burma and China in his speech. He expressed that the friendly and cooperative relations between Burma and China will surely be further developed in the future.
    Attending the reception were leading members from relevant departments including Ding Xilin, Han Nianlong, Jian Bozan, Zhao Xinchu, and Zhou Shujia.

### 中缅友协举行招待会  庆祝缅甸独立十八周年、中缅友好和互不侵犯条约签订六周年

1966-01-13
第4版()
专栏：

    中缅友协举行招待会
    庆祝缅甸独立十八周年、中缅友好和互不侵犯条约签订六周年
    据新华社十二日讯　中国缅甸友好协会今天下午举行招待会，热烈庆祝缅甸联邦独立十八周年及中国缅甸友好和互不侵犯条约签订六周年。
    中缅友协会长张友渔在招待会上讲话。他祝贺缅甸联邦在奈温主席的领导下，为捍卫民族独立和国家主权、反对帝国主义干涉和颠覆、在发展民族经济和民族文化方面，取得了显著成就。张友渔说，缅甸政府一贯奉行和平中立政策。六年前中缅两国签订的中缅友好和互不侵犯条约，体现了两国共同倡导的和平共处五项原则的伟大生命力，对维护亚洲和世界和平作出了宝贵的贡献。
    缅甸驻中国大使沙马杜瓦·信瓦瑙在讲话中赞扬中国人民在毛泽东主席领导下获得的巨大成就，赞扬缅中两国人民深厚的“胞波”情谊。他表示，缅中两国的友好合作关系今后必将获得进一步的发展。
    出席招待会的，有丁西林、韩念龙、翦伯赞、赵辛初、周叔迦等有关方面负责人。
