Title: Chairman Liu Appoints Yang Gongsu as Ambassador to Nepal
Author:
Date: 1966-01-28
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 27th -- Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has appointed Yang Gongsu as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Nepal.
    Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has relieved Zhang Shijie of his post as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Nepal.

### 刘主席任命杨公素为驻尼泊尔大使

1966-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席任命杨公素为驻尼泊尔大使
    新华社二十七日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇任命杨公素为中华人民共和国驻尼泊尔王国特命全权大使。
    中华人民共和国主席刘少奇免去张世杰的中华人民共和国驻尼泊尔王国特命全权大使的职务。
