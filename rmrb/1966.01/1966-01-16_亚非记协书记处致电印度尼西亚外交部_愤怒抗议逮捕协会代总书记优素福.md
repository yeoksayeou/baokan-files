Title: Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association Sends Telegram to Indonesian Foreign Ministry, Angrily Protesting the Arrest of Association's Acting Secretary-General Yusuf
Author:
Date: 1966-01-16
Edition: 5

    Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association Sends Telegram to Indonesian Foreign Ministry
    Angrily Protesting the Arrest of Association's Acting Secretary-General Yusuf
    Xinhua News Agency, January 15th. The Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association today released a telegram to the Foreign Ministry of the Republic of Indonesia regarding the arrest of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association Acting Secretary-General, Mr. Yusuf, the full text of which is as follows: Jakarta, Foreign Ministry of the Republic of Indonesia:
    The Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association protests with extreme indignation the arbitrary arrest of Mr. Yusuf, Acting Secretary-General of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association, by the Indonesian Armed Forces. This arrest is a grave provocation and insult to the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association and Afro-Asian journalists. The Afro-Asian Journalists' Association strongly condemns this act of persecution by the Indonesian Armed Forces. This arrest can only further expose the right-wing of the Indonesian military and prove once again that the Secretariat cannot continue to work in Jakarta. The Afro-Asian Journalists' Association demands the immediate release of Mr. Yusuf and other Indonesian journalists who have been unreasonably persecuted.

### 亚非记协书记处致电印度尼西亚外交部  愤怒抗议逮捕协会代总书记优素福

1966-01-16
第5版()
专栏：

    亚非记协书记处致电印度尼西亚外交部
    愤怒抗议逮捕协会代总书记优素福
    新华社十五日讯　亚非新闻工作者协会书记处今天发表关于亚非新闻工作者协会代总书记优素福被捕致印度尼西亚共和国外交部的电报，电报全文如下：雅加达印度尼西亚共和国外交部：
    亚非新闻工作者协会书记处以极大愤怒的心情抗议亚非新闻工作者协会代总书记优素福先生被印度尼西亚武装部队专横逮捕。这一逮捕是对亚非新闻工作者协会和亚非新闻工作者的严重挑衅和侮辱。亚非新闻工作者协会严厉谴责印度尼西亚武装部队这一迫害行为。逮捕只能进一步暴露印度尼西亚军方右派，又一次证明书记处无法在雅加达继续工作。亚非新闻工作者协会要求立即释放优素福先生和其他被无理迫害的印度尼西亚新闻工作者。
