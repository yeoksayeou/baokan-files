Title: Thai Authorities Deploy Air Force and Large Numbers of Military Police to Suppress Patriotic Forces on a Large Scale

Author:
Date: 1966-01-01
Edition: 3

    Thai Authorities Deploy Air Force and Large Numbers of Military Police
    to Suppress Patriotic Forces on a Large Scale
    According to Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok dispatch, 31st: The Thai government is conducting large-scale armed suppression of patriotic forces in the northeastern region.
    According to Western news agencies, Thai officials revealed on the 30th that, with the support of the air force, 800 armed police and paratroopers launched a large-scale "anti-communist offensive" in Nakhon Phanom Province in northeastern Thailand. The message revealed that military police had exchanged fire with people's armed forces.
    The police chief of the capital, Bangkok, Major General Phee Vichitphan, already departed on the 29th to Nakhon Phanom Province to personally command the operation.

### 泰国当局出动空军和大批军警  大规模镇压泰国爱国力量

1966-01-01
第3版()
专栏：

    泰国当局出动空军和大批军警
    大规模镇压泰国爱国力量
    据新华社三十一日讯  曼谷消息：泰国政府正在东北部地区大规模武装镇压爱国力量。
    据西方通讯社报道，泰国官员三十日透露，在空军的支援下，八百名武装警察和伞兵，在泰国东北部的那空拍侬府发动了大规模“反共攻势”。消息透露，军警和人民武装进行了交火。
    首都曼谷警察局局长费·维差攀少将已经在二十九日动身去那空拍侬府亲自指挥。
