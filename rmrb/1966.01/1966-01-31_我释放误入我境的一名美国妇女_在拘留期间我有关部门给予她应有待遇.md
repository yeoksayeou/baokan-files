Title: We Release American Woman Who Mistakenly Entered Our Territory; During Detention, Our Relevant Departments Gave Her Due Treatment
Author:
Date: 1966-01-31
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 30th – American woman Beverly Joan Riley mistakenly entered the territory of Bao'an County, Guangdong Province, on the afternoon of January 11th and was immediately detained by our police. After our side pointed it out, she admitted her border crossing error. This afternoon, our side released her in Shenzhen. During the detention, our relevant departments gave her due treatment.

### 我释放误入我境的一名美国妇女  在拘留期间我有关部门给予她应有待遇

1966-01-31
第4版()
专栏：

    我释放误入我境的一名美国妇女
    在拘留期间我有关部门给予她应有待遇
    新华社广州三十日电　美国妇女贝弗利·琼·赖利在一月十一日下午，误入我国广东省宝安县境内，当即被我民警拘留。经我方指出后，她已承认越境错误。今天下午，我方已在深圳将她释放。在拘留期间，我有关部门给了她应有的待遇。
