Title: Oppose US Expansion of the War of Aggression Against Vietnam; Prevent US Nuclear-Powered Fleets from Intrusion: 7,000 Workers in Yokosuka, Japan Demonstrate and March; People from All Walks of Life Angrily Protest the Eighth Intrusion of US Nuclear Submarine into Sasebo
Author:
Date: 1966-01-25
Edition: 3

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 24th: Approximately 7,000 workers held a rally near the US military base in Yokosuka Naval Port, Kanagawa Prefecture on the 23rd, opposing the US war of aggression against Vietnam and opposing the US nuclear-powered fleet's plot to enter this port.
    The participants unanimously passed an appeal calling on all democratic forces in Japan to unite, prevent US nuclear-powered fleets from entering Japanese ports, and not allow Yokosuka to become a US nuclear base.
    The appeal condemned the United States for expanding its war of aggression against Vietnam and the Sato Cabinet for serving the US policy of aggression. The appeal also strongly protested against the Sato Cabinet's permission for US nuclear submarines to enter Japanese ports eight times and its agreement for US nuclear-powered fleets to dock at Yokosuka Port.
    After the meeting, the workers held a demonstration march in Yokosuka City. In front of the headquarters of the US naval base, demonstrators repeatedly shouted slogans: "Yankees go home!"
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo: Amidst shouts of "Yankees, go home!" on the morning of the 18th, people from all walks of life in Japan angrily protested the intrusion of the US nuclear submarine "Barb" into Sasebo Port.
    This is the eighth time a US nuclear submarine has entered Sasebo Port.
    When the "Barb" nuclear submarine entered Sasebo Port, angry Japanese people from all over Nagasaki Prefecture gathered at the pier to hold a protest rally.
    The rally leaders called on people at the meeting to hold protest activities every time a US nuclear submarine enters this port.
    After the rally, the participants sent representatives to the Nagasaki City Government to request the mayor to refuse US nuclear submarines and nuclear-powered aircraft carriers from entering the port. (Attached picture)

### 反对美国扩大侵越战争　阻止美国核动力舰队闯入  日本横须贺七千工人示威游行  各界人民愤怒抗议美国核潜艇第八次闯进佐世保

1966-01-25
第3版()
专栏：

    反对美国扩大侵越战争　阻止美国核动力舰队闯入
    日本横须贺七千工人示威游行
    各界人民愤怒抗议美国核潜艇第八次闯进佐世保
    据新华社二十四日讯　东京消息：大约七千名工人二十三日在神奈川县横须贺军港的美军基地附近举行集会，反对美国侵越战争和反对美国核动力舰队图谋驶进这个港口。
    与会者一致通过了呼吁书，号召日本一切民主力量团结起来，阻止美国核动力舰队闯进日本港口，不让横须贺变成美国的核基地。
    呼吁书谴责美国扩大侵略越南的战争和佐藤内阁为美国侵略政策效劳。呼吁书还强烈抗议佐藤内阁准许美国核潜艇先后八次闯进日本港口，和同意美国核动力舰队在横须贺港停泊。
    会后，工人们在横须贺市举行了示威游行。示威者在美国海军基地总部门前，不断高呼口号：“美国佬滚回去！”
    新华社讯　东京消息：日本各界人民十八日早晨在高呼“美国佬，滚回去！”的声浪中，愤怒抗议美国核潜艇“棘鬣鱼号”闯进佐世保港口。
    这是美国核潜艇第八次开进佐世保港。
    当“棘鬣鱼号”核潜艇驶入佐世保港口的时候，从长崎县各地赶来的愤怒的日本人民聚集在码头上举行了抗议集会。
    集会领导人在会上号召每当美国核潜艇驶入这个港口的时候都要举行抗议活动。
    集会后，与会者派出代表到长崎市政府要求市长拒绝让美国核潜艇和核动力航空母舰进港。（附图片）
