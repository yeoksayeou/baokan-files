Title: PLA on Fujian Front Ceases Bombardment During Spring Festival

Author: 
Date: 1966-01-21
Edition: 2

    Fujian Front, Xinhua News Agency, January 20th – A spokesperson for the Chinese People's Liberation Army (PLA) Fujian Front Command announced today that January 21st is the traditional Spring Festival holiday for the Chinese people. In order to allow compatriots on the islands of Greater Jinmen, Lesser Jinmen, Greater Dan, and Lesser Dan, as well as officers and soldiers of the Kuomintang army, to peacefully enjoy the holiday, the artillery units of the PLA Fujian Front have been ordered to cease bombardment on the two single-numbered days of January 21st and 23rd as a gesture of concern.

### 福建前线我军春节停止炮击

1966-01-21
第2版()
专栏：

    福建前线我军春节停止炮击
    新华社福建前线二十日电  中国人民解放军福建前线司令部发言人今天宣布，一月二十一日是我国人民传统的春节佳日，为了让大金门、小金门、大担、二担等岛屿上的祖国同胞以及国民党军官兵安度佳节，中国人民解放军福建前线炮兵部队奉命在一月二十一日和二十三日两个单日停止炮击，以示关怀。
