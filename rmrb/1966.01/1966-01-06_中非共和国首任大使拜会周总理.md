Title: Central African Republic's First Ambassador Calls on Premier Zhou

Author: 
Date: 1966-01-06
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, January 5th. Joseph-Gilbert Mamadou, the first ambassador of the Central African Republic to China, paid a courtesy call on Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai this evening.

    Premier Zhou Enlai held a cordial and friendly conversation with Ambassador Mamadou.

    Present at the meeting were Xie Feng, Deputy Director of the African Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Zhou Min, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department.

### 中非共和国首任大使拜会周总理

1966-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

    中非共和国首任大使拜会周总理
    新华社五日讯　中非共和国首任驻中国大使约瑟夫—吉勒贝尔·马马杜，今晚拜会了国务院总理周恩来。
    周恩来总理同马马杜大使进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    拜会时在座的，有外交部非洲司副司长谢丰，礼宾司副司长周敏。
