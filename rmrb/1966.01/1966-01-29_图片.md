Title: Yuan Ruxun
Author: Yuan Ruxun
Date: 1966-01-29
Edition: 2

    Our country's first hot rolling drill bit machine has been successfully trial-produced at the Tianjin Tool Factory. It is used to manufacture twist drills, the most commonly used tool in mechanical industrial production. The production efficiency is twenty times higher than the original method of producing drill bits, and it saves one-third of the steel. This is a worker using a hot rolling drill bit machine to produce twist drills. Xinhua News Agency reporter Yuan Ruxun photographed.
