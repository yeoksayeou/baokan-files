Title: Spring Song of the哨所 (Outpost)
Author: Yao Chengyou
Date: 1966-01-27
Edition: 6

    The overbearing north wind howls,
    The continuous cold rain pours down.
    A coastal defense post in the misty mountains,
    Laughter abounds, depicting the scenery of spring.
    Northern wintersweet greets the flying snow,
    Southern coconut trees touch the clouds;
    The sentry chief spreads out the world map,
    Unveiling a vibrant picture of spring:
    The flames of war in the Venezuelan mountains and forests,
    Are like blossoming sprigs of spring flowers;
    The gunshots of Santo Domingo,
    Are like spring thunder splitting the sky!
    The spring tide in the Congo River,
    Carries warships advancing towards the colonialists;
    The red flags in the bamboo forests of South Vietnam,
    Illuminate the sky with morning glow!
    Ah, steel guns, battle, victory,
    Ah, east wind, red flag, steeds,
    Ah, the spring song in the soldiers' hearts,
    Can melt ten thousand zhang of ice!
    The shadows of soldiers flash through the wind and rain,
    The patrol path is sprinkled with blossoming hoof prints;
    The bayonets of the soldiers gleam in the grass,
    Eyes shining, watching the changes in the situation!

### 哨所春歌
姚成友
1966-01-27
第6版()
专栏：

    哨所春歌
    战士　姚成友
    霸道的北风呼呼刮，
    连夜的冷雨哗哗下。
    云雾山中海防哨，
    笑语滔滔把春光描画。
    北方腊梅迎飞雪，
    南方椰树顶云霞；
    哨长摊开世界地图，
    展现一幅春光明媚的图画：
    委内瑞拉山林的烽火，
    象枝枝绽开的春花；
    圣多明各的枪声，
    象春雷漫空劈炸！
    刚果河中的春潮，
    托战船向殖民主义者进发；
    南越竹林里的红旗，
    映透了漫天的朝霞！
    呵，钢枪、战斗、胜利，
    呵，东风、红旗、骏马，
    战士心中的春歌呵，
    能把万丈冰融化！
    风雨中掠过战士的身影，
    巡逻小路洒下朵朵马蹄花；
    草丛中亮着战士的刺刀，
    双目炯炯注视风云变化！
