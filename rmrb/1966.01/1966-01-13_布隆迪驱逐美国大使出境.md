Title: Burundi Expels US Ambassador

Author: 
Date: 1966-01-13
Edition: 4

    Burundi Expels US Ambassador
    According to a Xinhua News Agency dispatch from Bujumbura on the 11th: Bujumbura Radio reported that the government of the Kingdom of Burundi on the 10th ordered the US Ambassador to Burundi, Donald Dumont, and his two senior aides to leave the country within twenty-four hours because they interfered in Burundi's internal affairs.
    That morning, the Burundi Foreign Ministry delivered the expulsion order to the US Embassy.
    US State Department spokesman McCloskey threatened at a press conference in Washington on the 10th that the United States was considering taking "appropriate action" regarding Burundi's measure.

### 布隆迪驱逐美国大使出境

1966-01-13
第4版()
专栏：

    布隆迪驱逐美国大使出境
    据新华社十一日讯　布琼布拉消息：据布琼布拉电台广播，布隆迪王国政府十日勒令美国驻布隆迪大使当纳德·杜蒙和他的两个高级助手在二十四小时内离境，因为他们干涉了布隆迪的内政。
    当天早晨，布隆迪外交部把这一驱逐令交给了美国大使馆。
    美国国务院新闻发布官麦克洛斯基十日在华盛顿记者招待会上威胁说，美国正就布隆迪的这一措施考虑采取“适当行动”。
