Title: Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the NPC Standing Committee, Receives Australian Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-01-22
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, January 20th  Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received Australian lawyer Cedric Cuthbert Ralph and his daughter Barbara, who were invited to visit China, this evening and had a friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Wu Defeng, Lian Guan, Wang Jiren, and others.

### 刘宁一副委员长接见澳客人

1966-01-22
第3版()
专栏：

    刘宁一副委员长接见澳客人
    新华社二十日讯  人大常委会副委员长刘宁一今晚接见了应邀来我国访问的澳大利亚律师塞德里克·卡斯伯特·拉尔夫和他的女儿巴巴拉，并同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有吴德峰、连贯、王吉仁等。
