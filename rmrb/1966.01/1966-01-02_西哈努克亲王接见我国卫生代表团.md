Title: Prince Sihanouk Receives China's Health Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-01-02
Edition: 3

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, January 1st, Cambodian Head of State Prince Sihanouk received all members of the Chinese Health Delegation led by Huang Shuze on the morning of the 1st. Prince Sihanouk awarded medals to all members of the Chinese Health Delegation individually and held sincere and friendly talks with them.
    Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia, Chen Shuliang, was also present at the reception.
    On the evening of December 31st, Cambodian Secretary of State for Planning, Chau Sau, and his wife held a grand reception for the Chinese Health Delegation.

### 西哈努克亲王接见我国卫生代表团

1966-01-02
第3版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王接见我国卫生代表团
    据新华社金边一日电　柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王一日上午接见了由黄树则率领的中国卫生代表团全体成员。西哈努克亲王给中国卫生代表团全体成员一一颁发了勋章，并同他们进行了真挚友好的谈话。
    接见时，中国驻柬埔寨大使陈叔亮也在座。
    十二月三十一日晚上，柬埔寨计划国务秘书周索和夫人曾经为中国卫生代表团举行盛大招待会。
