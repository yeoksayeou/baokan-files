Title: Soviet Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1966-01-15
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, January 14th—The Soviet delegation, led by Shelepin, member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee and Secretary of the Central Committee, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.
    Li Xiannian, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, and other responsible persons such as Zhao Yimin and Wang Bingnan, saw them off at the airport.
    Also at the airport to see them off were Soviet Ambassador to China, Lapin, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, Hoang Bac, Representative of the Permanent Mission of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front to China, Hoang Van Hoan, and some other countries' envoys to China.

### 苏联代表团离北京回国

1966-01-15
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联代表团离北京回国
    新华社十四日讯　由苏共中央主席团委员、中央书记谢列平率领的苏联代表团，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理李先念，以及其他方面的负责人赵毅敏、王炳南等，到机场送行。
    到机场送行的，还有苏联驻中国大使拉宾，越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办黄北，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代表黄万里，以及其他一些国家驻中国的使节。
