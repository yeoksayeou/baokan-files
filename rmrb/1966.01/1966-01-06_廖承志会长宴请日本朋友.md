Title: Liao Chengzhi Hosts Banquet for Japanese Friends
Author:
Date: 1966-01-06
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, January 5th: Liao Chengzhi, President of the China-Japan Friendship Association, hosted a banquet tonight for Okada Haruo, a member of the Japanese Socialist Party Diet, and Tsukada Shohei, Vice Chairman of the Hokkaido Prefectural Assembly of Japan. Zhao Anbo, Wang Xiaoyun, Zhao Zhengyi, Ding Tuo, and others were present as guests.
    Japanese peace figure in Beijing, Kinkazu Saionji, also attended the banquet.

### 廖承志会长宴请日本朋友

1966-01-06
第4版()
专栏：

    廖承志会长宴请日本朋友
    新华社五日讯　中日友好协会会长廖承志今晚设宴招待日本社会党国会议员冈田春夫和日本北海道议会副议长塚田庄平。出席作陪的，有赵安博、王晓云、赵政一、丁拓等。
    在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也出席了宴会。
