Title: Lao People and Army Warmly Celebrate PLA Army Day, Troops in Various Locations Hold Rallies Determined to Strike Even Heavier Blows Against U.S. Aggressors
Author:
Date: 1966-01-24
Edition: 3

    (Xinhua News Agency, Kang Kai, 23rd) The Lao people and the Lao People's Liberation Army (LPLA) have been holding various activities in recent days to warmly celebrate the 17th anniversary of the founding of the LPLA.
    Units of the LPLA's regular forces, local armed forces, and guerrillas have held celebrations of various forms in the front lines, behind enemy lines, in guerrilla zones, and in the rear areas of the liberated zones. At the meetings, each unit summarized the achievements made in the past year in combat, military and political study, production labor, mass work, and other aspects, and expressed their determination to strike even heavier blows against the U.S. aggressors and their running dogs in the new year.
    The LPLA units in the Xieng Khouang area, which recently won medals and certificates of merit at all levels awarded by the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front and the Supreme Command of the LPLA, held awarding ceremonies in their respective驻地 [zhùdì - locations of troops]. Representatives of the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front and the Supreme Command of the LPLA awarded medals and prizes to these troops, and encouraged them to maintain their glorious traditions, continue their heroic struggle, and strive for greater victories.
    Around Army Day, representatives of the masses in various places sent representatives to the LPLA units to express their condolences and to hold joint celebrations with the troops.
    The leading organs of the LPLA recently hosted banquets for the Chinese and Vietnamese economic and cultural delegations stationed in Laos. The heads of the Chinese and Vietnamese delegations praised the LPLA at the banquet for the great victories it has achieved in the construction and development of the army and in the people's war over the past seventeen years. ### 老挝军民热烈庆祝解放军建军节  各地部队举行集会决心要更沉重打击美国侵略者

1966-01-24
第3版()
专栏：

    老挝军民热烈庆祝解放军建军节
    各地部队举行集会决心要更沉重打击美国侵略者
    据新华社康开二十三日电  老挝人民和人民解放军连日来举行各种活动，热烈庆祝老挝人民解放军建军十七周年。
    解放军各正规部队、地方武装和游击队等单位，纷纷在前线、敌后、游击区和解放区的后方各地举行了各种形式的庆祝会。会上，各单位都总结了一年来在战斗、军事和政治学习、生产劳动、群众工作等方面所取得的成就，并且表示要在新的一年中更加沉重地打击美国侵略者和他们的走狗。
    最近荣获老挝爱国战线党中央和老挝人民解放军最高指挥部颁发的各级勋章和奖状的解放军川圹地区各部队，分别在驻地举行了受勋受奖大会。爱国战线党中央和解放军最高指挥部的代表分别到这些部队授勋和授奖，并且勉励他们保持光荣传统，继续英勇奋斗，争取更大的胜利。
    在建军节前后，各地群众派代表到解放军各部队表示慰问，并且同部队联欢。
    老挝人民解放军领导机关最近先后设宴招待中国和越南驻老挝经济文化代表团。中国和越南代表团负责人在宴会上赞扬老挝人民解放军十七年来在军队的建设和发展方面，以及在人民战争中所取得的巨大胜利。（附图片）
