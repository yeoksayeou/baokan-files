Title: Australian Prime Minister Menzies Resigns
Author:
Date: 1966-01-25
Edition: 3

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Canberra dispatch: Australian Prime Minister Robert Menzies announced his immediate resignation on the 20th. The Deputy Leader of the Liberal Party Parliamentary Group, and Minister of Finance, Harold Holt, succeeded Menzies as Prime Minister.

    During his sixteen years in office, Menzies consistently followed the United States in both domestic and foreign policies, serving Wall Street's policies of aggression and war.

### 澳总理孟席斯辞职

1966-01-25
第3版()
专栏：

    澳总理孟席斯辞职
    据新华社讯　堪培拉消息：澳大利亚总理罗伯特·孟席斯二十日宣布立即辞职。自由党议会党团副领袖、财政部长哈罗德·霍尔特接替孟席斯为总理。
    孟席斯任职十六年中，在国内、外政策上一贯追随美国，为华尔街的侵略政策和战争政策效劳。
