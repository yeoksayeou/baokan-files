Title: The People of Yemen are Delighted to Watch My Documentary "Glorious Holiday" - Chairman Mao's Leadership Has Made China a Strong Country

Author:
Date: 1966-01-23
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Sana'a - Since the large-scale Chinese color documentary "Glorious Holiday" began showing in Taiz, Yemen on December 11, every showing has been packed, receiving a warm welcome from the public.

    People lined up early in the morning to buy tickets. Due to the large number of viewers, many people bought tickets and stood to watch.

    When Chairman Mao appeared on the screen, the audience erupted in thunderous applause. Some viewers said after watching the film: "The reason why China has become a strong country in a short period of fifteen years is because it has strong people and strong leadership." Some viewers said: "Chairman Mao is a great man, he has made China, with its 650 million people, into such an extraordinary country."

### 也门人民喜看我纪录片《光辉的节日》  毛主席的领导使中国成为强大的国家

1966-01-23
第3版()
专栏：中国文化在国外

    也门人民喜看我纪录片《光辉的节日》
    毛主席的领导使中国成为强大的国家
    新华社萨那电  中国大型彩色纪录片《光辉的节日》自十二月十一日在也门塔伊兹放映以来，场场客满，受到公众的热烈欢迎。
    人们一清早就排着长队等候买票。由于观众太多，有许多人买了票站着看。
    当毛主席出现在银幕上的时候，观众发出暴风雨般的掌声。有些观众在看完电影后说：“中国所以能在短短的十五年时间里成为一个强大的国家，是因为它有坚强的人民和坚强的领导。”有的观众说：“毛主席是一个伟大的人物，他使有六亿五千万人民的中国成了这样了不得的国家。”
