Title: Chairman Liu Appoints Huang Hua and Chen Chu as Ambassadors to the United Arab Republic and Ghana Respectively
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-20
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 19th -- Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has appointed Huang Hua as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the United Arab Republic. Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has removed Chen Jiakang from his position as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the United Arab Republic.

    Xinhua News Agency, 19th -- Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has appointed Chen Chu as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Ghana. Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has removed Huang Hua from his position as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Ghana.

### 刘主席分别任命黄华陈楚为驻阿联加纳大使

1966-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席分别任命黄华陈楚为驻阿联加纳大使
    新华社十九日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇任命黄华为中华人民共和国驻阿拉伯联合共和国特命全权大使。中华人民共和国主席刘少奇免去陈家康的中华人民共和国驻阿拉伯联合共和国特命全权大使职务。
    新华社十九日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇任命陈楚为中华人民共和国驻加纳共和国特命全权大使。中华人民共和国主席刘少奇免去黄华的中华人民共和国驻加纳共和国特命全权大使的职务。
