Title: What Tricks is U.S. Imperialism Plotting Against China?
Author:
Date: 1966-01-29
Edition: 4

    U.S. Imperialism is Plotting Against China
    Shouting that Dealing with China is America's Top Problem
    China and containing Chinese aggression are the biggest problems for the free world.
                   U.S. Secretary of Defense McNamara
    Russia is not the biggest problem in America's path. It is the second problem, our first problem is China.
　　　　Former U.S. Ambassador to the United Nations Adlai Stevenson
    In the next thirty years, the main strength of the United States will be to contain China in Asia.
                          Prominent U.S. Commentator James Reston
    Raving that the United States Will Sooner or Later Launch a War of Aggression Against China
    (The United States) will inevitably have a showdown with Communist China in the end. Strategically speaking, it is easier to carry out this showdown now than later.　　　《The Washington Post》
    We will clash with Red China at the appropriate time... (The United States) will have a war with Red China sooner or later.　　　　《The Wall Street Journal》
    The Pentagon's latest plan for conflict with the CCP is ready, if conflict with the CCP cannot be avoided.　　　　　《The Christian Science Monitor》
    Advocating that the U.S. and the Soviet Union Unite to "Contain" China
    The center of world turmoil has moved from the Iron Curtain of Western Europe to the area surrounding Communist China... Some major difficult choices await us. In preparing for these choices, the first thing to remember is that we are not alone in Asia. A sound policy must start from the entire periphery of China, which means including the Soviet Union, Japan, India, Britain, and France. All these countries have a common interest with us to varying degrees in containing China's expansion and pacifying the area around China.
                              Prominent U.S. Commentator Walter Lippmann
    If an armed conflict occurs between Russia and China, we will become Russia's ally. In any case, we have already been allies once in the last great war.
                  U.S. South Atlantic Fleet Commander Arthur Gralla

### 美帝国主义在对中国打什么主意

1966-01-29
第4版()
专栏：

    美帝国主义在对中国打什么主意
    叫嚷对付中国是美国头号问题
    中国和遏制中国的侵略是自由世界的最大问题。
                   美国国防部长　麦克纳马拉
    俄国不是美国前途中最大的问题。它是第二号问题，我们的第一号问题是中国。　
　　　　美国前驻联合国大使　史蒂文森
    在今后三十年中，美国的主要力量是要在亚洲遏制中国。
                          美国著名评论员　赖斯顿
    扬言美国迟早要发动侵华战争
    （美国）最后必然要与共产党中国摊牌。从战略上说，现在就进行这种摊牌比往后要容易。　　　《华盛顿邮报》
    我们将在适当的时候同赤色中国交战……（美国）迟早要同赤色中国发生一场战争。　　　　《华尔街日报》
    五角大楼关于同中共发生冲突的最新计划已经准备好，如果不能避免同中共发生冲突的话。　　　　　《基督教科学箴言报》
    鼓吹美苏联合起来“遏制”中国
    世界动乱的中心已从西欧的铁幕移动到共产党中国周围地区……将有一些重大的困难的抉择有待我们作出。在为这些抉择作准备的时候，首先要记住的是，我们在亚洲并不是孤独的，一项健全的政策必须从中国的整个周围地区开始，这意味着将包括苏联、日本、印度、英国和法国。所有这些国家在遏制中国的扩张和绥靖中国周围地区方面都在不同程度上同我们有共同的利益。
                              美国著名评论员　李普曼
    如果发生俄国和中国的武装冲突，我们将成为俄国的盟国。不管怎样，我们在上次大战中已经做了一次同盟者了。
                  美国南大西洋舰队司令　阿瑟·格拉拉
