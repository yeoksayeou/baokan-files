Title: Mao Zedong's Work "Foreword to "The Communist"" Published in Ceylon
Author:
Date: 1966-01-29
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, 26th -- The Sinhala version of Chairman Mao Zedong's work, "Foreword to "The Communist"", published by the Colombo People's Publishing House, has now been released. This book was translated by Ananda Kumara, Secretary-General of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association.

### 毛主席著作《〈共产党人〉发刊词》在锡出版

1966-01-29
第4版()
专栏：

    毛主席著作《〈共产党人〉发刊词》在锡出版
    新华社科伦坡二十六日电　科伦坡人民出版社出版的毛泽东主席著作《〈共产党人〉发刊词》僧伽罗文版现在已经发行。这本书是由锡中友好协会秘书长阿南达·库马拉翻译的。
