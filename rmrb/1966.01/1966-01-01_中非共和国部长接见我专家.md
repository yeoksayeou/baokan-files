Title: Central African Republic Minister Receives Chinese Experts
Author:
Date: 1966-01-01
Edition: 3

    Central African Republic Minister Receives Chinese Experts
    Xinhua News Agency, Bangui, 31st—Albert Payao, Minister of Development of the Central African Republic, received the Chinese agricultural expert group consisting of Zhang Bingjin and three others on the afternoon of the 30th.
    The Chinese experts arrived in Bangui on the morning of the 30th. N'Zala, Director of the Political Affairs Office of the Presidential Palace of the Central African Republic, went to the airport to welcome them and hosted a luncheon.

### 中非共和国部长接见我专家

1966-01-01
第3版()
专栏：

    中非共和国部长接见我专家
    新华社班吉三十一日电中非共和国发展部长阿尔贝·帕亚奥三十日下午接见了由张秉金等四人组成的中国农业专家小组。
    中国专家们是三十日上午到达班吉的。中非共和国总统府政务办公厅主任恩扎拉前往机场欢迎并设午宴招待。
