    Brief Remarks

    Lippmann is very pessimistic about Johnson's prospects for expanding the war of aggression against Vietnam. This famous commentator wrote: "He is on the verge of committing the kind of devastating historical mistake that criminals make. The Athenians made this mistake when they attacked Syracuse, and Napoleon and Hitler made this mistake when they attacked Russia."

    The Peloponnesian War, the French-Russian War, and the Soviet-German War have many differences from America's current war of aggression against Vietnam. However, the assassination of Alcibiades in Asia Minor, Napoleon's death on Saint Helena, and Hitler's suicide in a Berlin bunker do indeed foreshadow Johnson's fate.
          ×　×　×
    The Japanese newspaper *Tokyo Shimbun* revealed that the United States has concluded a secret agreement with the Saigon puppet regime to "lease" Cam Ranh Bay for ninety-nine years.

    Judging from the United States' extravagant construction in Cam Ranh Bay, it is entirely possible that the United States and the South Vietnamese puppet group signed such a secret agreement. The American propaganda machine has already made no secret of the fact that the United States wants to turn Cam Ranh Bay into a "permanent base" of its own.

    However, in its so-called "Fourteen Points" plan, the Johnson government said that "we do not want bases in Southeast Asia" and that "we do not want to keep American troops in South Vietnam after peace is ensured." It is probably speaking of at least ninety-nine years later.
          ×　×　×
    The American magazine *Newsweek* wrote: "While the United States was conducting a noisy peace offensive with one hand last week, its other hand was preparing for a broader battle in Vietnam if necessary."

    Isn't this what we often say is the counter-revolutionary dual tactics of American imperialism? However, Rusk said that "there is no trickery or deception in this regard." Rusk's yelling is precisely one hand of the two hands being played by the Johnson government.

### 三言两语

1966-01-08
第3版()
专栏：三言两语

    三言两语
    李普曼对于约翰逊扩大侵越战争的前景十分悲观。这个著名评论员写道：“他正处在要犯那种毁灭性的历史性错误的边缘上，雅典人进攻叙拉古犯了这种错误，拿破仑和希特勒进攻俄国也犯了这种错误。”
    伯罗奔尼战争、法俄战争、苏德战争同美国今天的侵越战争有许多不尽相同的地方。但是，亚西比得的被刺杀于小亚细亚，拿破仑的死于圣赫勒拿岛，希特勒的自杀于柏林地窖，这些人的结局，倒的确预示着约翰逊的下场。
          ×　×　×
    日本《东京新闻》透露，美国已同西贡傀儡集团缔结了一个“租借”金兰湾九十九年的秘密协定。
    从美国在金兰湾不惜工本地大兴土木看来，美国和南越傀儡集团签订这种密约是完全可能的。美国的宣传机器早已毫不讳言，美国要把金兰湾变成为它的一个“永久性基地”了。
    但是，约翰逊政府在它的所谓“十四点”计划中，却说什么“我们不希望在东南亚有基地”、什么“我们并不希望在和平得到确保以后把美国部队继续留在南越”。它大概说的至少是九十九年以后吧。
          ×　×　×
    美国《新闻周刊》写道：“当美国上周用一只手从事一场喧嚣一时的和平攻势的同时，它的另一只手正在为一旦必要时在越南进行更广泛的战斗作准备”。
    这不就是我们常说的，美帝国主义的反革命两手政策吗？但是，腊斯克却说什么“这方面是没有什么诡计或骗局的”。腊斯克的这种叫嚷，正是约翰逊政府在玩弄的两手中的一手。
