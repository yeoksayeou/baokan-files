Title: Vietnamese People's Army Shoots Down a US Aircraft

Author: 
Date: 1966-01-28
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 27th – It was announced here on the 27th that on the afternoon of the 26th, the army and people of Quang Binh Province in North Vietnam shot down one invading American jet fighter, damaged several others, and captured one American pilot alive.

### 越南军民击落美机一架

1966-01-28
第3版()
专栏：

    越南军民击落美机一架
    新华社河内二十七日电　据这里二十七日公布，越南北方广平省军民二十六日下午击落入侵的美国喷气式飞机一架，击伤多架，并活俘美国飞行员一名。
