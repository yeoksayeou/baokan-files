Title: Sato Government Serving US Aggression in Vietnam: Manufacturing Ammunition and Transporting Weapons to Kill
Author:
Date: 1966-01-27
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 23rd dispatch: Japanese newspapers reveal that the Japanese Sato government is serving the US aggression in Vietnam by transporting war materials.
    The US Far East Military Maritime Transport Office revealed in Tokyo on the 18th that it will send another tank landing ship from Japan to transport weapons and ammunition to South Vietnam. This action is facing increasing resistance from Japanese crew members. According to a recent report in the Socialist Party's organ newspaper, *The Socialist News*, a Japanese man from Chiba Prefecture named Hiroi Kinichi was once employed by a US transport ship. He revealed that many of the sailors on his transport ship were high-ranking former Japanese Navy personnel. This transport ship had loaded weapons and ammunition from ports in Japan and the Philippines and sailed to ports such as Da Nang and Chu Lai in South Vietnam. He said that he had seen Japanese-made ammunition in Da Nang. Hiroi Kinichi also said that the transport ships sometimes fought alongside US warships and even distributed weapons to Japanese sailors to participate in combat.
    According to the *Akahata* report, the US military printing plant in Kawasaki City, Kanagawa Prefecture, is printing a large number of leaflets for the US military to drop in the liberated areas of South Vietnam. This printing plant is the largest of all US military printing plants in Asia.
    The *Asahi Shimbun* recently revealed that Japan's Mitsui & Co. had "sold" a dredger to South Korea's Hyundai Construction for use by the US military in constructing military ports in Da Nang and Qui Nhon.

### 佐藤政府正为美国侵越效劳  既给美国制造侵越弹药又帮它运输武器杀人

1966-01-27
第5版()
专栏：

    佐藤政府正为美国侵越效劳
    既给美国制造侵越弹药又帮它运输武器杀人
    新华社二十三日讯　东京消息：日本报刊透露，日本佐藤政府在运送作战物资方面为美国侵略越南效劳。
    美国驻远东军事海运处十八日在东京透露，它将派另一艘坦克登陆舰从日本运送武器和弹药到南越去。这一行动正在遇到日本船员越来越大的抵抗。据最近出版的社会党机关报《社会新报》报道说，千叶县的一个名叫广居欣一的日本人曾经被一艘美国运输舰雇用。他透露，在他所属的运输舰上的海员中，有不少是日本旧海军的高级人员。这只运输舰曾经从日本和菲律宾港口装上武器和弹药开到南越的岘港、朱莱等港口。他说，他曾在岘港看到过日本造的弹药。广居欣一还说，运输船只有时还同美国军舰一起作战，还发武器给日本水手去参加战斗。
    据《赤旗报》报道，在神奈川县川崎市的美军印刷厂，正在为美军印制投在南越解放区的大量传单。这所印刷厂是美军在亚洲的所有印刷厂中最大的一所。
    《朝日新闻》最近透露，日本三井物产公司曾把一只挖泥船“卖给”南朝鲜现代建设公司，供美军修建岘港和归仁军港使用。
