Title: Increased Production of Large Timber Lacquer in Northwestern Guizhou
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-07
Edition: 2

    Large timber lacquer production in the Wumeng mountain region of Northwestern Guizhou increased again last year, and the quality also improved significantly.
    Northwestern Guizhou is the main producing area of large timber lacquer in our country. Production has increased continuously in recent years. Large timber lacquer is a type of raw lacquer with relatively good quality. This lacquer has less moisture, a bright color, and strong anti-corrosion properties, making it a high-quality coating for industrial use.

### 黔西北大木漆增产

1966-01-07
第2版()
专栏：

    黔西北大木漆增产
    黔西北乌蒙山区的大木漆去年又获增产，质量也有显著提高。
    黔西北是我国大木漆的主要产区。最近几年连年增产。大木漆是生漆中质量比较好的一种。这种漆水分少，色泽鲜明，防腐性强，是工业上的上等涂料。
