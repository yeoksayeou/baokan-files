Title: Central African Education Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1966-01-07
Edition: 4

    The Central African Education Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

    Xinhua News Agency, 6th January. The Central African Education Delegation, led by E Bondi Marie-Francois, Director of the Office of the Ministry of Education, Youth and Sports of the Central African Republic, left Beijing by plane on the 5th to return home.

    Among those seeing them off at the airport were Zhang Guiru, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Education, and other responsible persons from relevant departments. Joseph-Gilles Gilbert Mamadou, Ambassador of the Central African Republic to China, also saw them off at the airport.

### 中非教育代表团离京回国

1966-01-07
第4版()
专栏：

    中非教育代表团离京回国
    新华社六日讯由中非共和国教育、青年和体育部办公厅主任埃邦迪·马里—弗朗索瓦率领的中非教育代表团五日乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场送行的有教育部办公厅主任张桂如等有关方面负责人。中非共和国驻中国大使约瑟夫—吉勒贝尔·马马杜也到机场送行。
