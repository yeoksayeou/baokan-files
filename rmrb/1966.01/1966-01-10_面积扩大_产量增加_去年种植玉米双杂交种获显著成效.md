Title: Expanded Area, Increased Production: Significant Results Achieved in Planting Double-Cross Corn Hybrids Last Year
Author:
Date: 1966-01-10
Edition: 2

    According to Xinhua News Agency, the planting area of double-cross corn hybrids in major corn producing areas of our country further expanded last year. According to incomplete statistics from sixteen provinces (autonomous regions) and municipalities including Shandong, Shanxi, and Xinjiang, the planting area of first-generation seeds of double-cross corn hybrids increased threefold compared to the previous year, and universally achieved significant increases in production.
    Planting double-cross corn hybrids is one of the most effective measures for increasing corn production. Since 1958, agricultural scientific research institutions in our country have successively bred many excellent double-cross corn hybrid combinations, of which more than thirty have been widely promoted for production. Last year, the Shuangyue No. 3 promoted in Shandong Province, the Xinxhuang No. 1 promoted in Henan Province, the Chunza No. 12 promoted in Shanxi Province, the Nongda No. 7 promoted in Hebei Province and Beijing Municipality, the Liaoshuang 558 promoted in Liaoning Province, the Jishuang No. 2 promoted in Jilin Province, the Heiyu No. 46 promoted in Heilongjiang Province, and the Chuannong Shuangjiao No. 1 promoted in Sichuan Province all achieved a 20 to 30 percent increase in production compared to local improved corn varieties.
    In the process of promoting double-cross corn hybrids, all regions have adopted the method of multi-point demonstration and gradual promotion. Therefore, the promotion has been very rapid. (Attached picture)

### 面积扩大　产量增加  去年种植玉米双杂交种获显著成效

1966-01-10
第2版()
专栏：粮食新品种

    面积扩大　产量增加
    去年种植玉米双杂交种获显著成效
    据新华社讯　去年我国玉米主要产区玉米双杂交种的种植面积又有扩大。据山东、山西、新疆等十六个省（区）、市的不完全统计，种植玉米双杂交种一代种子的面积比前年扩大三倍，而且普遍获得了显著的增产。
    种植玉米双杂交种，是增产玉米的最有效的措施之一。一九五八年以来，我国的农业科学研究机关先后选育出许多优良的玉米双杂交种组合。其中供生产上大面积推广的有三十多个。去年山东省推广的双跃三号，河南省推广的新双一号，山西省推广的春杂十二号，河北省和北京市推广的农大七号，辽宁省推广的辽双五五八，吉林省推广的吉双二号，黑龙江省推广的黑玉四十六号，四川省推广的川农双交一号等玉米双杂交一代种子，都比当地玉米良种增产百分之二十至三十。
    在推广玉米双杂交种的过程中，各地都采取了多点示范逐步推广的办法。因此，推广得很快。（附图片）
