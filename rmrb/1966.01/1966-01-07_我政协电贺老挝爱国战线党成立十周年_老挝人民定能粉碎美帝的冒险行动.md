Title: CPPCC Sends Congratulatory Telegram on the 10th Anniversary of the Lao Patriotic Front; The Lao People Will Surely Crush the Adventurous Actions of U.S. Imperialism
Author:
Date: 1966-01-07
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front on the 5th, warmly congratulating the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Lao Patriotic Front. The telegram reads as follows:

    To the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front:

    On the occasion of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Lao Patriotic Front, we, on behalf of the Chinese people, extend warm congratulations and fraternal greetings to the Lao Patriotic Front and the Lao people who are heroically fighting against U.S. imperialism and its running dogs.

    For ten years, the Lao Patriotic Front, headed by Prince Souphanouvong, has led the Lao people in a heroic struggle to safeguard Laos' national independence and has achieved great victories. Now, U.S. imperialism is stepping up its armed intervention in Laos, gradually expanding the flames of the war of aggression against Vietnam to the entire Indochina region. The modern revisionists, colluding with U.S. imperialism, are plotting to undermine the righteous anti-U.S. struggle of the Indochinese people. The Lao people still face serious tasks of struggle. We believe that under the leadership of the Lao Patriotic Front, the heroic Lao people will surely be able to crush the adventurous actions of U.S. imperialism and its running dogs to expand the war.

    The Chinese and Lao people are neighbors and brothers. The Chinese people always stand firmly with the Lao people, fully support the Lao people's patriotic and just struggle against U.S. imperialism and its running dogs until final victory.

    The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
            January 5, 1966, Beijing

### 我政协电贺老挝爱国战线党成立十周年  老挝人民定能粉碎美帝的冒险行动

1966-01-07
第4版()
专栏：

    我政协电贺老挝爱国战线党成立十周年
    老挝人民定能粉碎美帝的冒险行动
    新华社六日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会五日打电报给老挝爱国战线党中央委员会，热烈祝贺老挝爱国战线党成立十周年。电文如下：老挝爱国战线党中央委员会：
    在老挝爱国战线党成立十周年的时候，我们代表中国人民向正在同美帝国主义及其走狗进行英勇斗争的老挝爱国战线党和老挝人民，致以热烈的祝贺和兄弟的敬礼。
    十年来，以苏发努冯亲王为首的老挝爱国战线党领导老挝人民，为维护老挝的民族独立进行了英勇的斗争，并且取得了巨大的胜利。现在，美帝国主义正在加紧对老挝进行武装干涉，逐步把侵略越南的战火扩大到整个印度支那地区。而现代修正主义者同美帝国主义进行勾结，阴谋破坏印度支那人民的反美正义斗争。老挝人民仍然面临着严重的斗争任务。我们相信，在老挝爱国战线党的领导下，英勇的老挝人民一定能够粉碎美帝国主义及其走狗扩大战争的冒险行动。
    中国人民同老挝人民是近邻，是兄弟。中国人民始终坚定不移地同老挝人民站在一道，全力支持老挝人民反对美帝国主义及其走狗的爱国正义斗争，直到取得最后胜利。
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会
            一九六六年一月五日于北京
