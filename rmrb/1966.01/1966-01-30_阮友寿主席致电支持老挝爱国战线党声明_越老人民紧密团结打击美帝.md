Title: Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho Sends Telegram Supporting Lao Patriotic Front's Statement: Vietnamese and Lao People Closely United to Combat US Imperialism
Author:
Date: 1966-01-30
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 27th (Reported) – According to the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency, Nguyen Huu Tho, Chairman of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front, sent a telegram on January 22nd to Prince Souphanouvong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front, expressing complete support for the Lao Patriotic Front Central Committee's statement issued on January 17th protesting the US imperialist conspiracy to expand the war of aggression in Laos.

    The telegram stated: "We guarantee to strengthen the solidarity with the fraternal Lao people, and are determined to more severely strike against US imperialism, defeat all its schemes and actions of aggression in South Vietnam, in order to liberate South Vietnam, and thus, together with the fraternal Lao and Cambodian people, make effective contributions to defending the independence, sovereignty, and territorial integrity of the three peoples, and to defending peace in Indochina, Southeast Asia, and the world."

    The telegram strongly condemned the increasingly serious crimes of aggression committed by the US imperialists and their lackeys in Laos, and sternly warned against the US imperialist plan to send its ground troops to occupy central and lower Laos and use this area as a springboard for aggression against Indochina.

    The telegram pointed out, "This is the most dangerous new aggressive step trampling on the sovereignty and independence of the Lao people, which threatens to turn the whole of Indochina into a battlefield. This serious aggression threat of US imperialism exposes the true face of the Johnson government's so-called 'peace efforts'."

    The telegram expressed firm belief that the Lao people's heroic struggle against US aggression and for national salvation will surely win complete victory.

### 阮友寿主席致电支持老挝爱国战线党声明  越老人民紧密团结打击美帝

1966-01-30
第3版()
专栏：

    阮友寿主席致电支持老挝爱国战线党声明
    越老人民紧密团结打击美帝
    新华社河内二十七日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团主席阮友寿二十二日打电报给老挝爱国战线党中央委员会主席苏发努冯亲王，表示完全支持爱国战线党中央委员会一月十七日发表的抗议美帝国主义在老挝扩大侵略战争阴谋的声明。
    电报说：“我们保证加强同老挝兄弟人民的团结，决心更加严厉地打击美帝国主义，打败它在越南南方的一切侵略阴谋和行动，以便解放越南南方，从而同兄弟般的老挝人民和柬埔寨人民一道，对保卫三国人民的独立、主权和领土完整，对保卫印度支那、东南亚和全世界的和平作出有效的贡献。”
    电报强烈谴责美帝国主义者及其走狗在老挝所犯的越来越严重的侵略罪行，并且严正警告美帝国主义阴谋派遣它的地面部队占领中寮和下寮并利用这一地区作为侵略印度支那的跳板的计划。
    电报指出，“这是一个蹂躏老挝人民的主权和独立、势将使整个印度支那变成战场的最危险的新侵略步骤。美帝国主义的这种严重的侵略威胁暴露了约翰逊政府的所谓‘和平努力’的真面目”。
    电报表示坚信，老挝人民抗美救国的英勇斗争一定会赢得完全的胜利。
