Title: Amidst the Roars of Anger from the Japanese People, American Nuclear Submarine Rolls Out of Sasebo
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-26
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 25th - News: On the 24th, the American nuclear submarine "Tautog" sailed out of Sasebo Port amidst the shouts of "Nuclear Submarines Get Out!" from the Japanese people.

    On the Sasebo docks, citizens once again held a demonstration against the American nuclear submarine. Nagasaki Zenji, Vice Chairman of the Sasebo Area Executive Committee for the Abolition of the U.S.-Japan "Security Treaty," said to the people participating in the demonstration: "No matter how frequently American nuclear submarines enter and station in the port, we must organize protest actions."

    Hashimoto, the representative of the Japanese Communist Party in the Kyushu region, also addressed the demonstrators, saying: "In the struggle against the frequent visits of nuclear submarines, the strength of the Sasebo area Communist Party organization is constantly growing, and the number of party members has increased to the highest record since the war."

### 在日本人民一片怒吼声中  美国核潜艇滚出佐世保

1966-01-26
第4版()
专栏：

    在日本人民一片怒吼声中
    美国核潜艇滚出佐世保
    新华社二十五日讯　东京消息：美国核潜艇“棘鬣鱼号”二十四日在日本人民高呼：“核潜艇滚出去！”的口号声中开出了佐世保港。
    在佐世保码头上，市民又一次举行反对美国核潜艇的示威。废除日美“安全条约”佐世保地区执行委员会副委员长长崎善次向参加示威的人们说：“不管美国核潜艇怎样经常地进驻港口，我们都一定要组织抗议行动。”
    九州地方日本共产党代表桥本也对示威群众发表讲话，他说：“在抗议核潜艇频繁进驻的斗争中，佐世保地区共产党组织的力量不断壮大，党员数字已经增加到战后的最高纪录。”
