Author:
Date: 1966-01-18
Edition: 4

    Ambassador Gheorghiu, on behalf of the Romanian Academy,
    presents honorary and corresponding academician certificates to President Guo Moruo and others
    Xinhua News Agency, January 17th: Romanian Ambassador to China, Dumitru Gheorghiu, today, on behalf of the Romanian Socialist Republic Academy, presented honorary academician certificates and badges to Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Zhu Kezhen, Vice President, and corresponding academician certificates and badges to Huang Bingwei, Director of the Institute of Geography of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.
    The ceremony for awarding the certificates and badges was held this evening at the Romanian Embassy in China. Ambassador Gheorghiu spoke at the ceremony. He said that the ties and cooperation between the Academies of Sciences of Romania and China, and between scientific figures of the two countries, will contribute to consolidating and developing the friendship between the peoples of Romania and China, and promoting the development of scientific undertakings in the two countries.
    Zhu Kezhen spoke at the ceremony on behalf of the scientists receiving the certificates and badges. He said that the Romanian Academy of Sciences awarding honorary and corresponding academician certificates and badges to three Chinese scientists is an honor for all Chinese scientific workers and also symbolizes the strengthening of the friendship between the Chinese and Romanian people and scientists of the two countries. Zhu Kezhen expressed his gratitude to the Romanian Academy of Sciences for this and wished the Romanian scientific undertaking continuous development in the country's socialist construction.
    Invited to attend the ceremony were Zhang Xiruo, Zhang Jingfu, Wu Youxun, Wang Bingnan, Shi Ximin, and Li Qitao, responsible persons of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and relevant government departments, and well-known figures in the Chinese scientific community, including Zhou Peiyuan, Huang Jiasi, Yan Jici, Yin Zanxun, Tong Dizhou, Liu Daosheng, and Gao Yuan. To present the certificates and badges to the Chinese scientists, Ambassador Gheorghiu held a reception at the embassy.

### 乔治乌大使代表罗马尼亚科学院  授予郭沫若院长等名誉院士和通讯院士证件

1966-01-18
第4版()
专栏：

    乔治乌大使代表罗马尼亚科学院
    授予郭沫若院长等名誉院士和通讯院士证件
    新华社十七日讯　罗马尼亚驻中国大使杜米特鲁·乔治乌今天代表罗马尼亚社会主义共和国科学院，把名誉院士的证书及证章授予中国科学院院长郭沫若、副院长竺可桢，把通讯院士的证书及证章授予中国科学院地理研究所所长黄秉维。
    授予证书及证章的仪式今晚在罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆举行。乔治乌大使在仪式上讲话。他说，罗中两国科学院和两国科学界人士的联系和合作，将对巩固和发展罗中两国人民的友谊，促进两国科学事业的发展作出贡献。
    竺可桢代表接受证书及证章的科学家在仪式上讲话。他说，罗马尼亚科学院分别授予三名中国科学家以名誉院士和通讯院士的证书及证章，这是全体中国科学工作者的荣誉，也象征了中罗两国人民和两国科学家友谊的加强。竺可桢为此向罗马尼亚科学院表示感谢，并祝罗马尼亚科学事业在国家的社会主义建设中不断获得发展。
    应邀出席仪式的，有中国科学院和政府有关部门的负责人张奚若、张劲夫、吴有训、王炳南、石西民、李琦涛，中国科学界著名人士周培源、黄家驷、严济慈、尹赞勋、童第周、刘导生、高原等。为给中国科学家授予证书及证章，乔治乌大使在使馆举行了酒会。
