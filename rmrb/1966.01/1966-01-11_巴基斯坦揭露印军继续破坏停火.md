Title: Pakistan Exposes Continued Ceasefire Violations by Indian Troops
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-11
Edition: 4

    Pakistan Exposes Continued Ceasefire Violations by Indian Troops
    (Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, January 9) According to a Pakistan Press International report, an official Pakistani spokesperson in Rawalpindi said that Indian troops have continued to violate the ceasefire in some areas in recent days.
    From January 5th to 8th, Indian troops continuously shelled Pakistani positions in some areas of Kashmir and opened fire in some areas of East and West Pakistan.

### 巴基斯坦揭露印军继续破坏停火

1966-01-11
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦揭露印军继续破坏停火
    新华社卡拉奇九日电  据巴基斯坦联合通讯社报道，巴基斯坦官方发言人在拉瓦尔品第说，印度军队在最近几天继续在一些地区破坏停火。
    印度军队从五日到八日不断在克什米尔的一些地区炮轰巴基斯坦阵地，并在东、西巴基斯坦一些地区开火。
