Title: Lao Patriotic Armed Forces and Civilians Crush U.S.-Puppet Group's "Mopping-Up" Operation, Annihilating Over 300 Enemy Troops, Shooting Down Three and Damaging Seven U.S. Aircraft. Khamsouk Keola Says Lao People are Determined to Fight Aggression Shoulder to Shoulder with Cambodian People
Author:
Date: 1966-01-08
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Khang Khay, 6th -- According to a Pathet Lao Radio broadcast: In December of last year, patriotic armed forces and civilians in the liberated areas of the three Lao provinces of Luang Prabang, Vientiane, and Xieng Khouang, in several counterattacks against the U.S.-puppet group's "mopping-up" operations and offensives, annihilated more than 300 enemy troops and captured a large quantity of weapons and military supplies.
    The patriotic armed forces and civilians in the Nam Bo, Pa Seng and Chuan Yin areas of Luang Prabang province smashed several enemy "mopping-up" operations, eliminating a total of 154 enemy troops and capturing 57 various types of guns.
    The patriotic armed forces and civilians in the Vang Vieng area of Vientiane province actively launched attacks to smash the enemy's "mopping-up" operations, eliminating 48 enemy troops, shooting down two U.S. helicopters, and capturing a large quantity of weapons.
    The radio station added that on the 17th, in a battle south of Xieng Khouang city, the patriotic armed forces and civilians of Xieng Khouang province annihilated the headquarters and two battalion headquarters of the enemy puppet 13th Mobile Regiment, eliminating a total of 104 enemy troops (previously reported as 78), including 49 enemy troops killed, among whom were two major battalion commanders and a Thai advisor. One U.S. aircraft was shot down and seven were damaged. Five guns of various types and a large quantity of other weapons and military supplies were captured.
    Xinhua News Agency, Khang Khay, 7th -- According to a "Voice of Laos" radio broadcast: Khamsouk Keola, Minister of Health, Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Lao National United Government, and representative of the Lao Patriotic Neutralist Forces, issued a statement on the 5th, strongly condemning the U.S. imperialists' plot to expand the war to Cambodia, and pointed out that this is part of the U.S. government's expansion of the Indochina war of aggression.
    The statement pointed out that U.S. imperialism is the culprit in undermining the 1954 Geneva Accords on Indochina and is an extremely dangerous enemy of the Indochinese people. The statement said that the Lao people are determined to fight alongside the fraternal Cambodian people and all the Indochinese people against U.S. imperialism until final victory.
    The statement said that the Cambodian army and people, under the leadership of Cambodian Head of State Prince Norodom Sihanouk, will certainly be able to crush the aggressive plots of U.S. imperialism and its running dogs.

### 老挝爱国军民粉碎美伪集团“扫荡”  歼敌三百多名击落美机三架击伤七架  坎苏·高拉说老挝人民坚决同柬人民并肩抗击侵略

1966-01-08
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝爱国军民粉碎美伪集团“扫荡”
    歼敌三百多名击落美机三架击伤七架
    坎苏·高拉说老挝人民坚决同柬人民并肩抗击侵略
    新华社康开六日电　据巴特寮电台广播：老挝琅勃拉邦、万象、川圹三个省的一些解放区的爱国军民，去年十二月在多次反击美伪集团的“扫荡”和进攻中，共歼灭敌人三百多名，并且缴获大批武器和军用物资。
    琅勃拉邦省南博、巴森和川银等地区的爱国军民粉碎了敌人的多次“扫荡”，共消灭敌人一百五十四名，缴获各种枪枝五十七件。
    万象省万荣地区的爱国军民，积极出击粉碎了敌人的“扫荡”，消灭敌人四十八名，打落美国直升飞机两架，并且缴获大批武器。
    电台补充报道，川圹省爱国军民十七日在川圹市以南歼灭敌伪第十三机动团团部和两个营部的战斗中，共歼灭敌人一百零四名（前报七十八名），其中被打死的敌人四十九名，被打死的敌人中有两名少校营长和一名泰国顾问。打落美国飞机一架，打伤七架。缴获各种炮五门以及大批其他武器和军用物资。
    据新华社康开七日电　据“老挝之声”电台广播：老挝民族团结政府卫生大臣、代理外交大臣和老挝爱国中立力量代表坎苏·高拉五日发表声明，强烈谴责美帝国主义阴谋把战争扩大到柬埔寨，并且指出，这是美国政府扩大印度支那侵略战争的一部分。
    声明指出，美帝国主义是破坏一九五四年关于印度支那问题的日内瓦协议的凶手，它是印度支那人民极其危险的敌人。声明说，老挝人民坚决同兄弟的柬埔寨人民和全体印度支那人民并肩同美帝国主义斗争到取得最后胜利。
    声明说，柬埔寨军队和人民在柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王的领导下，一定能够粉碎美帝国主义及其走狗的侵略阴谋。
