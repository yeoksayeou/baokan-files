Title: Premier Pham Van Dong Receives Chinese Health Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-01-04
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 2nd—Premier Pham Van Dong of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam received the Chinese Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Health Delegation led by Ye Fusun, and Chinese medical experts Zou Bangzhu, Liu Chunqian, and Du Benye, who are currently visiting Vietnam, on the morning of January 2nd. He held cordial and friendly talks with them.
    Present at the meeting was Pham Ngoc Thach, Minister of Health of Vietnam. Chen Liang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim, and Du Zhanchao, Cultural Counselor, of the Chinese Embassy in Vietnam were also present.
    The Chinese Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Health Delegation arrived in Hanoi on December 14th last year and returned to China on the afternoon of January 2nd after a twenty-day study visit to Vietnam.

### 范文同总理接见我卫生考察团

1966-01-04
第3版()
专栏：

    范文同总理接见我卫生考察团
    新华社河内二日电　越南民主共和国政府总理范文同二日上午接见了正在越南进行访问的由叶馥荪率领的中国广西壮族自治区卫生考察团和中国医学家邹邦柱、刘纯谦、都本业，并同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有越南卫生部部长范玉石。中国驻越南大使馆临时代办陈亮、文化参赞杜展潮也在座。
    中国广西壮族自治区卫生考察团是在去年十二月十四日到达河内的，在越南进行了二十天的考察访问，已于二日下午回国。
