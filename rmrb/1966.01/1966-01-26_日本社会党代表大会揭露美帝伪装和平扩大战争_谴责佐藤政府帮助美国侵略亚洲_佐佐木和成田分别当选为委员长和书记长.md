Title: Japanese Socialist Party Congress Exposes U.S. Imperialism's Disguised Peace, Expansion of War; Condemns Sato Government for Aiding U.S. Aggression in Asia; Sasaki and Narita Elected as Chairman and Secretary-General Respectively
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-26
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 25th Dispatch: The 27th Congress of the Japanese Socialist Party, held in Tokyo, concluded on the 22nd. The congress passed a declaration and the 1966 action plan, and re-elected the party's Central Executive Committee.

    The declaration passed by the congress stated: "Under the guise of a peace offensive, U.S. imperialism seeks to expand its aggression and armed intervention in Vietnam to the entire Indochina peninsula, and is threatening to transform this war into an all-out war against China."

    The declaration also pointed out: "The reactionary Sato cabinet, externally placing Japan under the U.S. nuclear umbrella and assisting U.S. aggression in Asia, is internally intensifying inflation, enterprise 'rationalization,' and suppression measures to attack the working people, attempting to establish a militarist and fascist ruling system." During the discussion of the draft of the 1966 action plan, the delegates engaged in fierce debates. Left-wing delegates advocated that the struggle against imperialism and for national liberation must be strengthened. Right-wing delegates attacked the draft action plan, arguing that it excessively emphasized the national liberation struggle and that the assessment of the international situation was not based on the position of "peaceful coexistence"; they praised the Sato government's collusion with Soviet leaders; they insisted on adding absurd arguments such as "opposing any nuclear tests by any country" to the draft.

    Later, the left and right wings of the party reached a compromise on the amendment to this draft on the 21st. Currently, the newly elected Central Executive Committee is making final revisions to the action plan passed by the congress.

    The congress elected Sasaki Kozo as Chairman of the Socialist Party, Kono Mitsu as Vice-Chairman, Narita Tomomi as Secretary-General, and Kuroda Hisao as Chairman of the Inspection and Audit Committee. All of them were re-elected.

### 日本社会党代表大会揭露美帝伪装和平扩大战争  谴责佐藤政府帮助美国侵略亚洲  佐佐木和成田分别当选为委员长和书记长

1966-01-26
第4版()
专栏：

    日本社会党代表大会揭露美帝伪装和平扩大战争
    谴责佐藤政府帮助美国侵略亚洲
    佐佐木和成田分别当选为委员长和书记长
    新华社二十五日讯　东京消息：日本社会党在东京举行的第二十七次代表大会在二十二日结束。大会通过了一项宣言和一九六六年的行动方针，并且改选了党的中央执行委员会。
    大会通过的宣言说：“美帝国主义在和平攻势的伪装下，要把它在越南进行的侵略和武装干涉扩大到整个印度支那半岛，并且正在进行威胁，要把这个战争转变为针对中国的全面战争。”
    宣言还指出：“反动的佐藤内阁，对外把日本放在美国的核保护伞下，帮助美国侵略亚洲，对内正在加紧采取通货膨胀、企业‘合理化’和镇压措施，向劳动人民进攻，企图建立军阀主义和法西斯主义的统治体制。”代表们在讨论一九六六年行动方针草案时，展开了激烈的争论。左派代表主张必须加强反对帝国主义和争取民族解放的斗争。右派代表攻击这个方针草案，认为草案过分强调民族解放斗争，对国际形势的估计没有立足在“和平共处”的立场上；他们赞扬佐藤政府同苏联领导人勾搭的做法；他们坚持要在草案中加进“反对任何国家进行任何核试验”等荒谬论点。
    后来，党内左派和右派在二十一日就这个草案的修正案达成了妥协。目前，新选出的中央执行委员会正在对大会通过的行动方针再作最后的修改。
    大会选出佐佐木更三为社会党委员长，河野密为副委员长，成田知巳为书记长，黑田寿男为监察检查委员长，他们都是再次当选。
