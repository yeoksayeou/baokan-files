Title: Shanghai Produces Transistor Megaphones in Batches
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-06
Edition: 2

    Shanghai Produces Transistor Megaphones in Batches
    The Shanghai Radio Factory No. 11 is currently mass-producing a new industrial product: hand-held transistor megaphones.
    This megaphone consists of three parts: a microphone, an amplifier, and a loudspeaker. The power source used is four ordinary dry-cell batteries, making it usable in places without AC power. The megaphone's casing is made of lightweight aluminum, and the total weight, including the dry batteries, is only two kilograms.
    This megaphone is suitable for command in the military, transportation, fire control, and other fields. It can also be used for short-range communication in ports, stations, sports fields, and construction sites.



**Named Entities**

上海  Shanghai
上海无线电十一厂  Shanghai Radio Factory No. 11

### 上海成批生产手提式晶体管扩声器

1966-01-06
第2版()
专栏：

    上海成批生产手提式晶体管扩声器
    上海无线电十一厂正在成批生产一种工业新产品——手提式晶体管扩声器。
    这种扩声器是由传声器（话筒）、放大器和扬声器（喇叭）三个部分组成的。所用的电源，是四节一般干电池，在没有交流电的地方也可使用。扩声器的外壳是用质地很轻的铝材做的，连干电池在内，全部重量只有两公斤。
    这种扩声器适用于部队、交通、消防等方面的指挥，也可以在港口、车站、运动场、建筑工地作近距离的传话。
