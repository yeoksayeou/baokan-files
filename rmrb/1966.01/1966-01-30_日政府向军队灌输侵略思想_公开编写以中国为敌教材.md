Title: Japanese Government Instills Aggressive Ideology in the Military; Openly Compiles Textbooks Hostile to China
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-30
Edition: 4

    The Japanese Government Instills Aggressive Ideology in the Military
    Openly Compiles Textbooks Hostile to China
    (Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo) The Japanese "Defense Agency" is currently compiling a new textbook to instill militaristic and foreign aggression ideologies into "Self-Defense Forces" members.
    The outline of this new textbook advocates hostility towards China, pointing out that "the focus of international tension has transitioned from 'US-Soviet confrontation' to 'US-China confrontation'. Direct and indirect aggression crises from foreign countries are developing." The outline of the new textbook emphasizes Japan's position as "a member of the free world," adhering to its stance of following the United States, and clamoring that the revival of militarism "does not violate the constitution."

### 日政府向军队灌输侵略思想  公开编写以中国为敌教材

1966-01-30
第4版()
专栏：

    日政府向军队灌输侵略思想
    公开编写以中国为敌教材
    据新华社东京电　日本“防卫厅”正在编写一部新教材，向“自卫队”队员灌输军国主义和对外侵略的思想。
    这部新教材大纲鼓吹以中国为敌，它指出“国际紧张局势的焦点已从‘美苏对立’过渡到‘美中对立’。来自外国的直接、间接的侵略危机正在发展”。新教材大纲强调日本作为“自由主义阵营一员”，坚持它追随美国的立场，并且叫嚣复活军国主义“并不违反宪法”。
