Title: Publication of "Vice Premier Chen Yi Answers Questions from Reporters"

Author:
Date: 1966-01-09
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th - The People's Publishing House today published the book "Vice Premier Chen Yi Answers Questions from Reporters." This book includes Vice Premier Chen Yi's important talk at the press conference for Chinese and foreign journalists on September 29, 1965, and his answers to questions from Mr. Takano Yoshhisa, the Beijing correspondent of the Japanese newspaper "Akahata," on December 30, 1965. It will be distributed across the country starting from the 8th.

### 《陈毅副总理答记者问》一书出版

1966-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

    《陈毅副总理答记者问》一书出版
    新华社七日讯　人民出版社今天出版了《陈毅副总理答记者问》一书。这本书，包括陈毅副总理一九六五年九月二十九日在中外记者招待会上的重要谈话和一九六五年十二月三十日答日本《赤旗报》驻北京记者高野好久问。从八日起，在全国各地发行。
