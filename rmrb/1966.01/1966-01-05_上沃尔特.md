Title: Upper Volta
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-05
Edition: 4

    Upper Volta is located in central West Africa, with an area of 274,200 square kilometers and a population of 4.5 million, about half of whom are Mossi people. The capital is Ouagadougou.
    Upper Volta is one of the ancient countries of Africa. At the end of the nineteenth century, French colonists began to invade, and in 1909 it became a French colony. In 1933, it was divided into three parts by France and merged into Ivory Coast, Mali, and Niger respectively. In 1947, the original boundaries were restored. It became independent on August 5, 1960. ### 上沃尔特

1966-01-05
第4版()
专栏：资料

    上沃尔特
    上沃尔特位于西非中部，面积二十七万四千二百平方公里，人口四百五十万，其中约一半是莫西族人。首都是瓦加杜古。
    上沃尔特是非洲古老国家之一。十九世纪末叶，法国殖民者开始入侵，一九○九年沦为法国殖民地。一九三三年曾被法国分割为三部分，分别被合并于象牙海岸、马里和尼日尔。一九四七年重新恢复原来疆界。一九六○年八月五日独立。（附图片）
