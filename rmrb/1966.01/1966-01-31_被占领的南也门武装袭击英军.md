Title: Armed Forces in Occupied Southern Yemen Attack British Troops
Author:
Date: 1966-01-31
Edition: 5

    Armed Forces in Occupied Southern Yemen Attack British Troops
    (Xinhua News Agency) A military spokesman for the National Liberation Front of occupied Southern Yemen said in Taiz, Yemen, on the 13th that Southern Yemeni (the British-occupied Aden Colony and Aden Protectorate) nationalists have, in the past week, attacked four British patrols with hand grenades on the main streets of Aden, killing five British soldiers and wounding eleven.
    The Liberation Front has also recently secretly distributed leaflets warning British colonial officers and soldiers not to persecute the local people.
    Recently, the people in this region have been increasingly vocal in their opposition to British colonial rule and demanding the removal of British military bases. The British *Evening Standard* stated that the British troops stationed in Aden are now "more concerned with protecting their families, residences, and themselves" than with playing any so-called "strategically important role."

### 被占领的南也门武装袭击英军

1966-01-31
第5版()
专栏：

    被占领的南也门武装袭击英军
    据新华社讯　据中东通讯社报道，被占领的南也门民族解放阵线军事发言人十三日在也门的塔伊兹说，南也门（英国占领下的亚丁殖民地和亚丁保护地）民族主义者在过去一个星期中，在亚丁的主要街道用手榴弹袭击了四支英国巡逻队，炸死英国兵五名，炸伤十一名。
    解放阵线最近还秘密散发传单，警告英国殖民官兵不许迫害当地人民。
    最近以来这个地区的人民反对英国殖民统治和要求撤除英国军事基地呼声高涨。英国《旗帜晚报》说，现在驻亚丁的英国军队中，“更多的是保护他们的家眷、住所和他们自己”，而不是起所谓“战略上的重要作用”。
