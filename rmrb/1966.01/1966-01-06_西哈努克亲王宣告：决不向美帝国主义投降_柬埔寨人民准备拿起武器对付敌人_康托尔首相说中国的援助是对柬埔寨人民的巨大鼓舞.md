Title: Prince Sihanouk Declares: Never Surrender to U.S. Imperialism
Cambodian People Prepare to Take Up Arms Against the Enemy
Prime Minister Kantol Says China's Aid is a Great Encouragement to the Cambodian People
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-06
Edition: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, January 5th (via Cambodia News Agency) – Cambodian Head of State Prince Sihanouk said, "We are not afraid of war," and "If U.S. imperialism and its running dogs impose war upon us, we will fight."

　　Prince Sihanouk, speaking at a mass rally in Kampong Chhnang on the 4th, said that Cambodia is "ready" for the invasion of U.S. imperialism and its lackeys, and it "will fight to the last man, never surrendering to the U.S. imperialists and their servants."

　　He said, "Their atomic bombs cannot scare us. Their atomic bombs cannot scare people who are not afraid of death and will never surrender."

　　Prince Sihanouk said, "We adhere to our current system, but since the U.S. imperialists do not allow us to maintain this system, we have no choice but to deal with them, first by defeating them."

　　Prince Sihanouk said: "Some people doubt: How can we Khmers, without (decent) planes and warships (our current air and naval forces are fundamentally unable to participate in combat), resist the well-equipped Americans and their running dogs?"

　　He said, "We do not rely on the weapons we possess to resist the enemy, but on our determination to die rather than yield, and to sacrifice the last person rather than surrender to them. As long as there is one Khmer who picks up a wooden stick to break the enemy's head, the struggle will continue."

　　Prince Sihanouk pointed out that if U.S. imperialism and its servants invade Cambodia, they "will face not only six million Khmers who are determined, because the Khmers will never be isolated in a war." He said, "As long as we can rely on our own means to fight the war, we will rely on ourselves, but in the opposite case, we will appeal to our friends."

　　He said, "Besides the full assistance we are sure to receive from our Chinese brothers, we are sure to also receive assistance from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea."

　　Prince Sihanouk said, "The traitor Son Ngoc Thanh stubbornly says that I am pushing my country into war, but the opposite is true. It is he who serves his master, carrying out frequent aggression against our borders, the purpose of which is clearly to provoke a war, and we must resolutely defend our peace and do everything possible to maintain peace."

　　He said, "If we all have to take up arms against the enemy, we will gladly do so. I firmly believe that the people of the whole country, civilians and soldiers, men and women, old and young, will then prepare to make the greatest sacrifices together with the Prince."

　　Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, January 5th – Cambodian Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Prince Kantol said that China's provision of assistance in all aspects is a great encouragement to Cambodia at a time when U.S. imperialism and its running dogs are wantonly insulting the Cambodian people and hindering Cambodia's development.

　　Prince Kantol made these remarks at a banquet on the evening of the 4th to welcome the Chinese health delegation led by Huang Shuze.

　　Prince Kantol said at the banquet that China's assistance in all aspects will be warmly welcomed and will be a great encouragement. He said, "This assistance is very gratifying at a time when the U.S. imperialists and their running dogs are desperately dealing with brotherly people, wantonly insulting us, and attempting to hinder our development."

### 西哈努克亲王宣告：决不向美帝国主义投降  柬埔寨人民准备拿起武器对付敌人  康托尔首相说中国的援助是对柬埔寨人民的巨大鼓舞

1966-01-06
第4版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王宣告：决不向美帝国主义投降
    柬埔寨人民准备拿起武器对付敌人
    康托尔首相说中国的援助是对柬埔寨人民的巨大鼓舞
    新华社金边五日电　据柬埔寨新闻社五日报道，柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王说，“我们不怕战争”，“如果美帝国主义及其走狗把战争强加于我们，我们就应战。”
    西哈努克亲王四日在磅清扬市的一次群众大会上发表讲话说，柬埔寨对美帝国主义及其仆从的入侵“已经作好准备”，它“将斗争到最后一个人，决不向美帝国主义者及其仆从投降。”
    他说，“他们的原子弹是吓不倒我们的。他们的原子弹是吓不倒不怕死、决不投降的人民的。”
    西哈努克亲王说，“我们坚持我们的现行制度，但是，既然美帝国主义者不让我们保持这种制度，我们没有别的选择，只好对付他们，首先是打败他们。”
    西哈努克亲王说：“有些人怀疑：我们高棉人没有（象样的）飞机和军舰（我们目前所拥有的空军和海军都是根本不能参加作战的），怎么能够抵抗武装精良的美国人及其走狗呢？”
    他说，“我们并不依靠我们拥有的武器来抗击敌人，而是依靠我们宁死不屈的决心，不惜牺牲最后一个人，决不向他们投降。只要还有一个高棉人拿起木棍来打破敌人的脑袋，斗争就将继续下去。”
    西哈努克亲王指出，如果美帝国主义及其仆从侵入柬埔寨，它们“将面对的不只是六百万下定了决心的高棉人，因为高棉人决不会在一场战争中孤立。”他说，“只要我们能依靠自己的手段来进行抗战，那我们就依靠自己，但是在相反的情况下，我们就将向我们的朋友发出呼吁。”
    他说，“除了我们肯定从我们的中国兄弟那里得到充分的援助之外，我们肯定还可以得到朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的援助。”
    西哈努克亲王说，“卖国贼山玉成硬说什么我把我的国家推向战争，其实正好相反，正是他为他的主子效劳，对我国的边境进行频繁的侵略，其目的显然是为了挑起一场战争，而我们要坚决保卫我们的和平，竭尽全力维护和平。”
    他说，“如果我们大家必须拿起武器对付敌人，我们将乐意这样做。我深信，全国人民，文职人员和军人，男女老少，那时都将同亲王一起准备作出最大的牺牲。”
    据新华社金边五日电　柬埔寨首相兼外交大臣康托尔亲王说，正当美帝国主义及其走狗大肆侮辱柬埔寨人民和阻挠柬埔寨发展的时候，中国要在各方面提供援助是对柬埔寨的一个巨大的鼓舞。
    康托尔亲王是在四日晚上招待由黄树则率领的中国卫生代表团的宴会上讲这番话的。
    康托尔亲王在宴会上说，中国要在各方面提供的援助将受到热烈欢迎，并且将是一个巨大的鼓舞。他说，“正当美帝国主义者及其走狗拼命对付兄弟人民，大肆侮辱我们和企图阻挠我们的发展的时候，这种援助是非常令人欣慰的。”
