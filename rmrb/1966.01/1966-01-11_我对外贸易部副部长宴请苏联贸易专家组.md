Title: My Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Hosts Soviet Trade Expert Group
Author:
Date: 1966-01-11
Edition: 4

    My Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Hosts Soviet Trade Expert Group
    Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang hosted a banquet this evening to welcome the Soviet trade expert group led by Shiliaev, Director of the Eastern Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Trade. The Soviet trade expert group arrived in Beijing on January 7th.



**Named Entities**

李强	Li Qiang

北京	Beijing

### 我对外贸易部副部长宴请苏联贸易专家组

1966-01-11
第4版()
专栏：

    我对外贸易部副部长宴请苏联贸易专家组
    新华社十日讯  对外贸易部副部长李强今天晚上设宴招待由苏联对外贸易部东方司司长施利亚耶夫率领的苏联贸易专家组。苏联贸易专家组是在一月七日到达北京的。
