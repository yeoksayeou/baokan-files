Title: Guinean Economic Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-20
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, the Guinean economic delegation, led by Baba Camara, Director of the Guinean Foreign Trade Bureau, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

### 几内亚经济代表团到京

1966-01-20
第4版()
专栏：

    几内亚经济代表团到京
    据新华社十九日讯　几内亚经济代表团在团长、几内亚对外贸易局局长巴巴·卡马拉率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
