Title: Asian, African, Latin American People's Solidarity Conference Begins Subcommittee Meetings
Author: 
Date: 1966-01-09
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Havana, January 7th—The plenary session of the Asian, African, and Latin American People's Solidarity Conference adjourned on the morning of the 7th. In the afternoon, the subcommittees began their meetings.
    The conference established four subcommittees: political, economic, socio-cultural, and organizational issues.
    Under the political subcommittee, three subordinate groups were established to address the Vietnam issue, pressing issues of anti-imperialist struggle, and issues of neo-colonialism and old colonialism.
    In addition, the delegations from the eighteen countries and regions that made up the preparatory committee are responsible for drafting the general declaration of the conference.

### 亚非拉人民团结会议开始举行小组委员会会议

1966-01-09
第4版()
专栏：

    亚非拉人民团结会议开始举行小组委员会会议
    新华社哈瓦那七日电　亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲人民团结会议全体会议七日上午休会。下午，各小组委员会开始举行会议。
    会议成立了政治、经济、社会文化以及组织问题等四个小组委员会。
    在政治小组委员会下又成立了越南问题、反帝斗争的紧迫问题和新老殖民主义问题等三个下属小组。
    此外，由组成筹备委员会的十八个国家和地区的代表团负责起草会议总宣言。
