Title: Czechoslovak Government Trade Delegation Head Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-01-18
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, January 17th - The head of the Czechoslovak Government Trade Delegation and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of Czechoslovakia, Mr. M. Honusek, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning.  Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Jiang Ming and others went to the airport to greet him.

    Mr. V. Jansa, deputy head of the Czechoslovak Government Trade Delegation who arrived earlier, all the delegation members, and Mr. Kotzmann, counsellor of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, also went to the airport to greet them.

### 捷政府贸易代表团团长到京

1966-01-18
第4版()
专栏：

    捷政府贸易代表团团长到京
    新华社十七日讯　捷克斯洛伐克政府贸易代表团团长、捷克斯洛伐克对外贸易部副部长米·霍努塞克，今天上午乘飞机到京。前往机场迎接的有对外贸易部副部长江明等。
    先期到京的捷克斯洛伐克政府贸易代表团副团长符·杨萨和全体团员以及捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使馆参赞柯茨曼等也到机场迎接。
