    The EZ-1 type semiconductor hearing aid, weighing only 75 grams, has been successfully trial-produced at the Shanghai Telecommunications Equipment Factory. This hearing aid can adjust the volume for the impaired ear, select different tones, and also be used to listen to the telephone.
