Title: All-China Journalists Association Holds Reception to Welcome Resident Secretary of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association; Wishing the Afro-Asian Journalists Association to Carry Forward its Militant Tradition and Strengthen United Struggle; Liao Chengzhi Says at the Meeting: Any Conspiracy to Sabotage and Split the Afro-Asian Solidarity Movement Will Not Succeed

Author:

Date: 1966-01-16
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, January 15th – The All-China Journalists Association held a cold buffet reception this evening to welcome the resident secretaries of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association who have come to Beijing to meet.

      The resident secretaries of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association, A. L. Abukus (Arab Syrian Republic), Chen Quanbi (China), Lee Morrison (South Africa), and Sugiyama Ichidaira (Japan), attended the reception. They were warmly welcomed at the reception by Wu Lengxi, Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, and heads of various news organizations in the capital.

    Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, was invited to attend the reception.

    Wu Lengxi spoke at the reception, extending a warm welcome on behalf of Chinese journalists to the resident secretaries of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association. Wu Lengxi said that since its establishment, the Afro-Asian Journalists Association has been fighting against imperialism and old and new colonialism, and for strengthening the unity of the Afro-Asian people and journalists, and has achieved great results. Chinese journalists express their deep respect for these achievements of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association.

    He said that since last October, the work of the Secretariat has encountered difficulties and has been obstructed by the right-wing forces in Indonesia, and it cannot continue to work normally. The decision of the Secretariat to temporarily withdraw from Jakarta is not the fault of the Secretariat and the progressive journalists of Indonesia, but the fault of the right-wing forces in Indonesia.

    Wu Lengxi said that Chinese journalists express their gratitude to the progressive journalists of Indonesia for their past assistance and cooperation with the Afro-Asian Journalists Association, and express their sympathy for the persecution and difficult situation they have encountered since last October. As the All-China Journalists Association, a member of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association, we have an obligation to provide convenience for the Secretariat's temporary withdrawal from Jakarta to meet in Beijing. We express our firm support for the statement issued by the Secretariat.

    Wu Lengxi said that Chinese journalists will do their best to help the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association convene a full meeting of the Secretariat as soon as possible in the near future. We wish the Afro-Asian Journalists Association to continue to carry forward its militant tradition, which is to fight against imperialism, especially US imperialism, against old and new colonialism, and to strengthen the unity of the Afro-Asian people and Afro-Asian journalists.

    A. L. Abukus spoke at the reception, thanking the All-China Journalists Association for its support and assistance on behalf of all members of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association.

    He said that our Chinese colleagues have stood with us from the beginning in the struggle against the common enemy. The Afro-Asian Journalists Association has encountered great difficulties after the dramatic changes in the political situation in Indonesia. We must fight to the end, and we believe that this struggle will surely be victorious.

    He proposed a toast to the past, present, and future victories of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association, to the victory of the struggle against imperialism, old and new colonialism, especially against US imperialism, to the victory of the Vietnamese people's struggle, and to the victory of the American people's struggle against their rulers.

    Liao Chengzhi proposed a toast at the reception, expressing that the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity fully supports the work of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association. He said that any attempt to usurp the leadership of the Afro-Asian solidarity movement, any conspiracy to sabotage and split the Afro-Asian solidarity movement, will not succeed. The temporary withdrawal of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association from Jakarta to continue its work under new conditions marks a new development in the Afro-Asian solidarity and anti-imperialist cause.

    Also invited to attend the reception were peace activists and journalists from various countries in Beijing, including Kinkazu Saionji and Kazuo Asami from Japan, Rewi Alley from New Zealand, and Mrs. Willy from Indonesia. Journalists from various Afro-Asian countries stationed in Beijing also attended the reception.

    Also present at the gathering with friends from various countries tonight were Wang Yunsheng, Chen Jun, Miao Haileng, Jin Zhao, Mu Xin, Bi Jilong, Wang Yi, Deng Gang, Li Wu, and Li Bingquan, heads of various news organizations and relevant departments in the capital.

### 我记协举行招待会欢迎亚非记协书记处驻会书记  祝亚非记协发扬战斗传统加强团结斗争  廖承志在会上说：任何企图破坏和分裂亚非团结运动的阴谋都是不能得逞的

1966-01-16
第5版()
专栏：

    我记协举行招待会欢迎亚非记协书记处驻会书记
    祝亚非记协发扬战斗传统加强团结斗争
    廖承志在会上说：任何企图破坏和分裂亚非团结运动的阴谋都是不能得逞的
    新华社十五日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会今晚举行冷餐招待会，欢迎来北京会合的亚非新闻工作者协会书记处驻会书记。
      亚非新闻工作者协会书记处驻会书记阿·勒·阿布库斯（阿拉伯叙利亚共和国）、陈泉璧（中国）、里·摩里逊（南非）和杉山市平（日本），出席了招待会。他们在招待会上受到中华全国新闻工作者协会主席吴冷西以及首都各新闻单位负责人的热烈欢迎。
    中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志应邀出席了招待会。
    吴冷西在招待会上讲话，他代表中国新闻工作者向亚非新闻工作者协会书记处驻会书记表示热烈欢迎。吴冷西说，亚非新闻工作者协会成立以来，一直为反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义，为加强亚非人民和新闻工作者的团结而斗争，并取得了巨大的成绩。中国新闻工作者对亚非新闻工作者协会所取得的这些成就表示深切的敬意。
    他说，自从去年十月以来，书记处的工作遇到了困难，遭到了印度尼西亚右派势力的阻挠，不能继续正常工作。书记处决定暂时撤离雅加达，这不是书记处和印度尼西亚进步新闻工作者的过错，而是印度尼西亚右派势力的过错。
    吴冷西说，中国新闻工作者对印度尼西亚进步新闻工作者在过去给予亚非新闻工作者协会的帮助和合作表示感谢，对他们自去年十月以来遭到的迫害和困难处境，表示同情。作为亚非新闻工作者协会书记处成员的中华全国新闻工作者协会，对书记处暂时撤离雅加达来北京会合，当然有义务提供方便。对书记处所发表的声明，我们表示坚决的支持。
    吴冷西说，中国新闻工作者将尽自己的力量，帮助亚非新闻工作者协会书记处尽快在不久的将来召开书记处全体会议。我们祝愿亚非新闻工作者协会继续发扬它的战斗传统，这就是为反对帝国主义特别是美帝国主义，反对新老殖民主义，加强亚非人民和亚非新闻工作者的团结而斗争。
    阿·勒·阿布库斯在招待会上讲话，他代表亚非新闻工作者协会书记处全体成员感谢中华全国新闻工作者协会的支持和帮助。
    他说，我们的中国同行一开始就在反对共同敌人的斗争中同我们站在一起。亚非新闻工作者协会在印度尼西亚政治局势发生了急剧的变化以后，遭到了很大的困难。我们一定要斗争到底，我们相信这个斗争一定能取得胜利。
    他提议，为亚非新闻工作者协会过去、现在和将来的胜利，为反对帝国主义、新老殖民主义、尤其是反对美帝国主义斗争的胜利，为越南人民斗争的胜利，为美国人民反对他们的统治者的斗争的胜利，干杯。
    廖承志在招待会上祝酒，他表示中国亚非团结委员会全力支持亚非新闻工作者协会书记处的工作。他说，任何篡夺亚非团结运动的领导，任何企图破坏和分裂亚非团结运动的阴谋，都是不能得逞的。亚非新闻工作者协会书记处暂时撤离雅加达，在新的条件下继续工作，这标志着亚非团结反帝事业的新发展。
    应邀出席招待会的，还有在北京的各国和平人士和新闻工作者，他们当中有日本的西园寺公一、浅海一男，新西兰的艾黎，印度尼西亚的威利夫人。常驻北京的亚非各国的记者也出席了招待会。
    今晚，同各国朋友一起欢聚的首都各新闻单位和有关方面负责人，有王芸生、陈浚、缪海棱、金照、穆欣、毕季龙、王揖、邓岗、李伍、李炳泉等。
