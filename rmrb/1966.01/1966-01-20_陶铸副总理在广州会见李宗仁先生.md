Title: Vice Premier Tao Zhu Meets with Mr. Li Zongren in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1966-01-20
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, January 19th -- Vice Premier of the State Council and First Secretary of the Central South Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Tao Zhu, and his wife, Zeng Zhi, met with Mr. Li Zongren and his wife, Ms. Guo Dejie, who are currently visiting Guangzhou, on the afternoon of the 18th.
    Mr. Cheng Siyuan was also invited to the meeting.
    Also present at the meeting were Chen Yu, Third Secretary of the CPC Central South Bureau and Governor of Guangdong Province; Zhao Ziyang, First Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Party Committee; Lin Liming, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Guangdong Provincial Party Committee and Vice Governor of Guangdong Province; Zeng Sheng, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province and Mayor of Guangzhou; Zhao Zhuoyun, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province and Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Guangdong Provincial Committee; Xu Chongqing, Deng Wenzhao, and Huang Jie, Vice Governors of Guangdong Province; Zhang Boquan, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the CPPCC; Xiao Junying, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the CPPCC and Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang; Luo Peiyuan, Deputy Mayor of Guangzhou and Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Guangzhou Municipal Committee; and Li Dachao, Vice Chairman of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the CPPCC.
    Also present at the meeting were Ping Jiesan, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, who accompanied Mr. Li Zongren and his wife, Ms. Guo Dejie, on their visit, and Liu Zhongrong, an old friend of Mr. Li Zongren.
    After the meeting, Tao Zhu and his wife, Zeng Zhi, hosted a banquet for Mr. Li Zongren and Ms. Guo Dejie, as well as Mr. Cheng Siyuan. At the banquet, Comrade Tao Zhu welcomed Mr. Li Zongren and Ms. Guo Dejie to visit the Central South region and wished them a happy Spring Festival in Guangzhou. He also hoped that Mr. Li Zongren and Ms. Guo Dejie would take a good look during this revisit to the old place and offer more suggestions on various aspects of the work. After the banquet, Mr. Li Zongren and his wife, Ms. Guo Dejie, and others, watched the Guangzhou Spring Festival Flower Fair.
    Mr. Li Zongren and Ms. Guo Dejie arrived in Guangzhou from Beijing on the 7th.

### 陶铸副总理在广州会见李宗仁先生

1966-01-20
第3版()
专栏：

    陶铸副总理在广州会见李宗仁先生
    新华社广州十九日电　国务院副总理、中共中央中南局第一书记陶铸和夫人曾志，十八日下午会见了正在广州参观访问的李宗仁先生和夫人郭德洁女士。
    同时被邀会见的还有程思远先生。
    会见时在座的，有中共中央中南局第三书记、广东省省长陈郁，中共广东省委第一书记赵紫阳，中共广东省委书记处书记、广东省副省长林李明，广东省副省长、广州市市长曾生，广东省副省长、中共广东省委统一战线工作部部长赵卓云，广东省副省长许崇清、邓文钊、黄洁，政协广东省委员会副主席张?村，政协广东省委员会副主席、中国国民党革命委员会广东省委员会主任委员萧隽英，广州市副市长、中共广州市委统一战线工作部部长罗培元，政协广州市委员会副主席李达潮。
    会见时在座的，还有陪同李宗仁先生和夫人郭德洁女士前来参观访问的中共中央统一战线工作部副部长平杰三，李宗仁先生的旧友刘仲容。
    会见后，陶铸和夫人曾志设宴招待李宗仁先生和郭德洁女士，以及程思远先生。陶铸同志在宴会上对李宗仁先生和郭德洁女士前来中南地区参观访问表示欢迎，祝他们在广州欢乐地度过春节。他还希望李宗仁先生和郭德洁女士这次重游旧地，多看一看，对各方面的工作多多提意见。宴会结束后，李宗仁先生和夫人郭德洁女士等，观赏了广州迎春花市。
    李宗仁先生和郭德洁女士一行是在七日从北京到达广州的。
