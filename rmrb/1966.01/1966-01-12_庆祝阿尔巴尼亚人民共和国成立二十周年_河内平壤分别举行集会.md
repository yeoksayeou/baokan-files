Title: Hanoi and Pyongyang Hold Rallies to Celebrate the 20th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of Albania
Author:
Date: 1966-01-12
Edition: 5

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 11th: The Vietnam News Agency reported that people from all walks of life in Hanoi held a rally on the evening of the 10th to celebrate the 20th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of Albania.
    Pham Khac Quang, Vice Chairman of the Hanoi City Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front, spoke at the meeting. He praised the tremendous achievements made by the Albanian people in socialist construction over the past twenty years. He emphasized the close friendly relations between the Vietnamese and Albanian peoples, and expressed gratitude for the wholehearted support of the Albanian Labor Party, government, and people for the Vietnamese people's struggle against the American imperialist aggressors.
    Shefqet Hekali, the Albanian Ambassador to Vietnam who was invited to attend the celebration, said in his speech that the Albanian Labor Party, government, and people unreservedly support the Vietnamese people's struggle against American imperialist aggression, and wished the Vietnamese people greater victories in their struggle to resist American aggression and save the nation.
    According to Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, January 10th: A rally was held in Pyongyang on the evening of the 10th to celebrate the 20th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of Albania.
    Li Shangshan, Vice Chairman of the Pyongyang City People's Committee, said in his speech that he believes the fraternal friendly and cooperative relations between the Korean and Albanian peoples will be further developed and consolidated on the basis of Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism.
    Hashia, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Albanian Embassy in North Korea, emphasized in his speech that the Albanian people, the Albanian Labor Party, and the Albanian government have always fully supported the Korean people's just struggle to drive the American imperialists out of South Korea and achieve the reunification of their motherland.

### 庆祝阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国成立二十周年  河内平壤分别举行集会

1966-01-12
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国成立二十周年
    河内平壤分别举行集会
    据新华社河内十一日电　据越南通讯社报道，河内各界人民十日晚上举行集会，庆祝阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国成立二十周年。
    越南祖国战线河内市委员会副主席范克广在会上讲了话。他称赞阿尔巴尼亚人民二十年来在社会主义建设中取得的巨大成就。他着重指出了越南人民和阿尔巴尼亚人民之间的密切友好关系，并且对阿尔巴尼亚劳动党、政府和人民对越南人民反对美帝国主义侵略者的斗争的全心全意的支持表示感谢。
    应邀出席庆祝会的阿尔巴尼亚驻越南大使谢夫切特·赫卡利在讲话中表示，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党、政府和人民无保留地支持越南人民反对美帝国主义侵略的斗争，并祝越南人民在抗美救国的斗争中取得更大的胜利。
    据新华社平壤十日电　平壤市十日晚上举行集会，庆祝阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国成立二十周年。
    平壤市人民委员会副委员长李尚善在讲话中表示相信，朝阿两国人民兄弟般的友好合作关系将在马克思—列宁主义和无产阶级国际主义原则的基础上得到进一步的发展和巩固。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻朝鲜大使馆临时代办哈希亚在讲话中强调指出，阿尔巴尼亚人民、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和阿尔巴尼亚政府一向完全支持朝鲜人民为把美帝国主义者赶出南朝鲜和争取祖国统一而进行的正义斗争。
