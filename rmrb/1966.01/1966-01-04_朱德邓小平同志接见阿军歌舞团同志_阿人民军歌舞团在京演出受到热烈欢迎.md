Title: Zhu De and Deng Xiaoping Receive Comrades from the Albanian Army Song and Dance Troupe; Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe's Performance in Beijing Receives Warm Welcome
Author:
Date: 1966-01-04
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, January 3rd - Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, received Colonel Vani Baba, Head of the Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe, Colonel Gaqo Avrazi, Artistic Director, Lieutenant Colonel Hamet Myrdali, Deputy Head, Lieutenant Colonel Spiro Kristo, Deputy Artistic Director, and principal performers of the song and dance troupe this evening, and held cordial and friendly talks with them.
    Present at the reception were Zhang Xiruo, Liu Zhijian, Wang Bingnan, Li Chang and other responsible persons of relevant departments.
    Nesti Nase, Albanian Ambassador to China, and Colonel Feliq Hatu, Military Attaché of the Embassy, were also present.
    Xinhua News Agency, January 3rd - The performance of the Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe in Beijing this evening received a warm welcome.
    Party and state leaders Zhu De and Deng Xiaoping watched the performance.
    When the Albanian artists sang "Song of Enver Hoxha," "The East is Red," and "Tirana-Beijing," thunderous applause resounded throughout the theater. Tonight, the artists also performed many Albanian and Chinese revolutionary songs and dances, as well as songs, dances, and folk songs praising the great friendship between the Albanian and Chinese peoples, all of which were warmly welcomed and highly praised by the audience.
    At the end of the performance, Comrades Zhu De and Deng Xiaoping, among others, went onto the stage to shake hands cordially with the performers and congratulate them on their successful performance.
    Nesti Nase, Albanian Ambassador to China, and Colonel Feliq Hatu, Military Attaché of the Embassy, also watched the performance.
    Also watching the performance were Zhang Xiruo, Liu Zhijian, Wang Bingnan, Li Chang, Zhang Jinhui, Dai Bixiang, Ding Bo and other responsible persons of relevant departments.

### 朱德邓小平同志接见阿军歌舞团同志  阿人民军歌舞团在京演出受到热烈欢迎

1966-01-04
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德邓小平同志接见阿军歌舞团同志
    阿人民军歌舞团在京演出受到热烈欢迎
    新华社三日讯　中共中央副主席、人大常委会委员长朱德，中共中央总书记邓小平，今晚接见了阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团团长弗尼·巴巴上校、艺术指导加乔·阿乌拉基上校、副团长哈梅特·穆尔达里中校、副艺术指导斯皮罗·克里斯多少校以及歌舞团的主要演员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有张奚若、刘志坚、王炳南、李昌等有关方面负责人。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使奈斯蒂·纳赛和大使馆武官费利克·哈图上校也在座。
    新华社三日讯　阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团今晚在北京举行的演出，受到热烈欢迎。
    党和国家领导人朱德、邓小平观看了演出。
    当阿尔巴尼亚艺术家们演唱《恩维尔·霍查之歌》、《东方红》和《地拉那—北京》歌曲的时候，剧场内响起一阵又一阵热烈的掌声。今晚，艺术家们还演出了许多阿尔巴尼亚和中国的革命歌曲和舞蹈，以及颂扬阿中两国人民伟大友谊的歌舞和民歌等节目，都受到观众的热烈欢迎和高度评价。
    演出结束时，朱德、邓小平同志等走上舞台同演员们亲切握手，祝贺他们演出成功。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使奈斯蒂·纳赛和大使馆武官费利克·哈图上校也观看了演出。
    观看演出的，还有张奚若、刘志坚、王炳南、李昌、张金辉、戴碧湘、丁波等有关方面负责人。
