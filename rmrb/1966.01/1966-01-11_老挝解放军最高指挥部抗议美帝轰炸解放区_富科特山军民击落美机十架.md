Title: Lao People's Liberation Army Supreme Command Protests US Imperialist Bombing of Liberated Areas; Armed Forces and Civilians in Phou Kout Mountain Shoot Down Ten US Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-01-11
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Kang Kai – According to a Pathet Lao Radio broadcast on the 7th, the spokesman for the Supreme Command of the Lao People's Liberation Army issued a statement on the 5th, strongly protesting the savage crimes of US imperialism and its lackeys in continuously dispatching aircraft to frantically bomb and strafe the liberated areas of Laos recently.
    The statement said that from December 24th, 27th to 31st of last year, US imperialism and its lackeys dispatched multiple batches of aircraft to bomb and strafe the liberated areas of Savannakhet Province, Khammouane Province, and Luang Prabang Province, causing many losses to the lives and property of the local people.
    The statement pointed out that some American aircraft took off from bases in Thailand and South Vietnam. The statement said that the Lao military and civilians expressed great indignation at these barbaric actions of US imperialism.
    The statement called on all Lao military and civilians to remember this blood debt and resolutely repulse all attacks by US imperialism and its lackeys to avenge the fallen compatriots.
    Xinhua News Agency, Kang Kai, 10th – According to a "Voice of Laos" radio broadcast on the 10th: The patriotic armed forces in the Phou Kout Mountain area of ​​Xieng Khouang Province, Laos, severely damaged the American air pirates, shooting down ten American aircraft and damaging one on the 7th. From 15:40 to 17:20 on the afternoon of the 7th, thirty-six American aircraft carried out indiscriminate bombing of the Phou Kout Mountain area. The local military and civilians bravely resisted the invading enemy aircraft. After nearly two hours of fierce fighting, the patriotic military and civilians shot down ten American bandit aircraft. (Attached picture)
    The Lao People's Liberation Army's anti-aircraft artillery is always ready to annihilate enemy aircraft that dare to invade. Chen Han (Xinhua News Agency Manuscript)

### 老挝解放军最高指挥部抗议美帝轰炸解放区  富科特山军民击落美机十架

1966-01-11
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝解放军最高指挥部抗议美帝轰炸解放区
    富科特山军民击落美机十架
    新华社康开电  据巴特寮电台七日广播：老挝人民解放军最高指挥部发言人五日发表声明，强烈抗议美帝国主义及其走狗最近连续不断地派遣飞机疯狂轰炸和扫射老挝解放区的野蛮罪行。
    声明说，去年十二月二十四日、二十七日到三十一日，美帝国主义及其走狗派遣飞机多批轰炸、扫射了沙湾拿吉省、甘蒙省和琅勃拉邦省的解放区，使当地人民的生命和财产受到了许多损失。
    声明指出，有些美国飞机是从泰国和南越的基地起飞的。声明说，老挝军民对美帝国主义的这些野蛮行动表示极大的愤慨。
    声明号召老挝全体军民要记住这笔血债，坚决回击美帝国主义及其走狗的一切进攻，为死难同胞报仇。
    新华社康开十日电据“老挝之声”电台十日广播：老挝川圹省富科特山地区的爱国武装力量重创美国飞贼，七日击落了美国飞机十架，击伤一架。七日下午十五时四十分至十七时二十分，美国飞机三十六架，对富科特山地区进行狂轰滥炸。当地军民英勇抗击入侵敌机。经过近两个小时的激烈战斗，爱国军民击落美国强盗飞机十架。（附图片）
    老挝人民解放军的高射炮兵随时准备歼灭敢来侵犯的敌机。　陈汉摄（新华社稿）
