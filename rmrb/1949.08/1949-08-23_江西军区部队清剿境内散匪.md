Title: Jiangxi Military Region Troops Clear Out Remaining Bandits Within Territory
Author:
Date: 1949-08-23
Page: 1
　
Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, 22nd—Local troops of the Jiangxi Military Region of the People's Liberation Army are clearing out remaining bandits within the territory. A certain regiment and guard company of the Nanchang sub-district, from the 5th to the 9th, pursued and annihilated the special agent bandit Xie Guangming's unit in Qing'an County, northwest of Nanchang, killing or wounding 17 bandits including the deputy regiment commander and capturing 88 bandits. The Jiujiang sub-district has captured 18 scattered bandits including Deputy Battalion Commander Yan Meizhong in recent days. The Guo Kan unit of the Jishui County pseudo-police headquarters and the Zhang Shoulu bandit unit, a total of 96 people, surrendered with their weapons a few days ago in the Ji'an sub-district. A certain regiment of the Poyang sub-district pursued and annihilated a part of the special agent bandit Li Fengchun's unit in late last month.

### 江西军区部队清剿境内散匪

1949-08-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　江西军区部队清剿境内散匪
    【新华社南昌二十二日电】人民解放军江西军区地方部队，清剿境内散匪。南昌分区某团及警卫连，自五日至九日在南昌西北的清安县境内，追歼特务土匪谢光明部，毙伤匪副团长以下十七名，俘匪八十八名。九江分区连日来俘散匪副营长阎梅忠以下十八名。吉安分区吉水县伪警察总队郭侃部及匪张受禄部共九十六人，日前携械投诚。鄱阳分区某团于上月下旬追歼特务土匪李逢春部一部。
