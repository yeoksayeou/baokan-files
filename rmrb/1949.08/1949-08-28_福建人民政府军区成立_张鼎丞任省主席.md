Title: Fujian Provincial People's Government Military Region Established; Zhang Dingcheng Appointed Provincial Chairman
Author:
Date: 1949-08-28
Page: 1

　　Fujian Provincial People's Government Military Region Established
　　Zhang Dingcheng Appointed Provincial Chairman
    [Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, 27th] The Fujian Provincial People's Government has been established in Fuzhou upon the order of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission and the Headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Zhang Dingcheng is the Chairman of the People's Provincial Government, and Ye Fei and Fang Yi are the Vice Chairmen. The Fujian Military Region has also been established simultaneously. The Headquarters of the People's Liberation Army has appointed Ye Fei as Commander, Zhang Dingcheng as Political Commissar, Wei Guoqing as Deputy Political Commissar, Liu Peishan as Director of the Political Department, and Chen Tiejun as Deputy Chief of Staff. The responsible persons of the Provincial Government and the Military Region have all arrived at their posts and are working.

### 福建人民政府军区成立　张鼎丞任省主席

1949-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　福建人民政府军区成立
  　张鼎丞任省主席
    【新华社福州二十七日电】福建省人民政府已奉中国人民革命军事委员会及中国人民解放军总部命令在福州成立。人民省政府主席为张鼎丞，副主席为叶飞、方毅。福建军区亦同时成立，人民解放军总部委任叶飞为司令员，张鼎丞为政治委员，韦国清为副政治委员，刘培善为政治部主任，陈铁君为副参谋长。现省府及军区各负责人均已到职办公。
