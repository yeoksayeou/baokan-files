Title: Lüda Entrusts Middle School Teachers - Enrollment Now Open

Author:

Date: 1949-08-22
Page: 4

　　Lüda Entrusts Middle School Teachers
  　Enrollment Now Open
    [Our Newspaper] As of now, there are 479 registered unemployed teachers and staff members. The Education Bureau and the Education Union will convene a meeting at Xiangshan Orphanage at 3:00 PM on the 27th of this month to discuss employment issues.

    Another report: Lüshun Dalian entrusts the city to hire forty-two middle school teachers. Enrollment will be accepted at the Education Union this afternoon (the 22nd). (Qing)

### 旅大托聘中学教员　今接洽报名

1949-08-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　旅大托聘中学教员
  　今接洽报名
    【本报讯】失业教职员登记者至目前止，已有四七九人，准备在本月二十七号下午三点于香山慈幼院由教育局、教联会召集商讨工作问题。
    又讯：旅顺大连托本市聘请中学教师四十二人，定今天（廿二日）下午在教联会接洽报名。（清）
