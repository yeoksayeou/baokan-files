Title: Youth League Propaganda Department and China Youth Society Invite Heads of Xinhua Bookstore Branches for Discussion

Author:
Date: 1949-08-04
Page: 1

    [Ping City News] The Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League and the China Youth Society, taking advantage of the North China Xinhua Bookstore holding a branch meeting in Ping, specially invited the heads of various branches to the China Youth Society yesterday (3rd) to exchange distribution experience, in order to more quickly and universally distribute "China Youth" and the China Youth Series to various regions in the future. Those present included the Publishing Committee, the head office of the North China Xinhua Bookstore, and the heads of various local branches. The meeting discussed issues such as publication, distribution, and content. In terms of distribution, in addition to emphasizing the need to develop subscription services, the Xinhua Bookstore expressed its willingness to do its best to deliver "China Youth" to the vast number of readers in a timely and in-depth manner.

### 青年团中宣部及中国青年社邀新华书店各分店负责人座谈

1949-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    青年团中宣部及中国青年社邀新华书店各分店负责人座谈
    【平市讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会宣传部及中国青年社，乘华北新华书店在平召开分店会议之际，特邀请各分店负责人于昨日（三日）在中国青年社交换发行经验，以求今后能更迅速地普遍地把“中国青年”及中国青年丛书发行到各地区去。到会者有出版委员会，华北新华书店总店及各地分店负责人。会上讨论了出版、发行及内容等问题，在发行上，除着重指出要发展预订户外，新华书店表示愿尽力把“中国青年”及时地深入地送到广大读者手中。
