Title: Shanghai Municipal People's Government Holds Evening Party to Celebrate the Fourth Anniversary of the Liberation of Northeast China
Author:
Date: 1949-08-17
Page: 1

　　Shanghai Municipal People's Government Holds Evening Party
  　Celebrating the Fourth Anniversary of the Liberation of Northeast China
    [This newspaper dispatch] Shanghai News: August 15th was the fourth anniversary of the liberation of Northeast China. The Shanghai Municipal People's Government invited more than 200 representatives from all walks of life to hold a celebration party that evening. Those present included Rao Shushi, Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chen Yi, Mayor of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government, Feng Zhongyun, Chairman of the Songjiang Province of Northeast China, and representatives from various democratic parties and people's organizations. Vice Mayor Pan Hannian delivered the opening speech at the meeting. Mayor Chen Yi and Feng Zhongyun, Chairman of Songjiang Province, who had just arrived in Shanghai from Northeast China, both spoke at the meeting. Chairman Feng recounted the history of the arduous struggle of the Northeast People's Anti-Japanese Army over the past fourteen years. After the meeting, the Northeast film "The Bridge" was shown to add to the festive atmosphere.

### 沪市人民政府举行晚会　庆祝东北解放四周年

1949-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　沪市人民政府举行晚会
  　庆祝东北解放四周年
    【本报讯】上海消息八月十五号是东北解放四周年纪念日，上海市人民政府在当天晚上邀集各界代表二百多人，举行庆祝晚会，到会的有中共上海市委员会书记饶漱石、上海市人民政府陈毅市长、东北松江省主席冯仲云，以及各民主党派各人民团体代表。会上由潘汉年副市长致开会词。陈毅市长和刚从东北到上海的松江省主席冯仲云都在会上讲话，冯主席讲述了东北人民抗日军在十四年中艰苦斗争的历史，会后放映东北影片“桥”助兴。
