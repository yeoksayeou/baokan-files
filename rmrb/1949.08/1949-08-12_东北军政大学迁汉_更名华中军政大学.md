Title: Northeast Military and Political University Moves to Wuhan, Renamed Central China Military and Political University
Author:
Date: 1949-08-12
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 11th. The Northeast Military and Political University has moved to Hankou and been renamed the Central China Military and Political University. It is currently actively surveying school sites and preparing for enrollment in order to continue to cultivate a large number of cadres for the construction of a new China.

### 东北军政大学迁汉  更名华中军政大学

1949-08-12
第2版()
专栏：

    东北军政大学迁汉
    更名华中军政大学
    【新华社汉口十一日电】东北军政大学已迁来汉口，并更名为华中军政大学，现正积极勘定校址，准备招生，以继续大量培养建设新中国的各种干部。
