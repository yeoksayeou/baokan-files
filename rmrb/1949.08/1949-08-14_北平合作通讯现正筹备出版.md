Title: Beiping Cooperative Newsletter is Now Being Prepared for Publication
Author: Zheng Zhong
Date: 1949-08-14
Page: 8

    Beiping Cooperative Newsletter is Now Being Prepared for Publication
    [Our Dispatch] Currently, there are already over one hundred grassroots cooperatives in Beiping, with more than 200,000 members. It is planned to establish a city-wide cooperative network in the second half of the year, reaching 600,000 cooperative members. In order to further consolidate and develop cooperative undertakings and rapidly disseminate cooperative news, the Municipal General Cooperative is now preparing to publish a monthly publication called "Beiping Cooperative Newsletter," as a forum for criticism and suggestions from the vast number of members; it will also serve as a forum for educating cadres, exchanging experiences, and studying business operations. The direct target audience of this publication is the cadres and members of various cooperatives in the city.
          (Zheng Zhong)

### 北平合作通讯现正筹备出版
郑重
1949-08-14
第8版()
专栏：

    北平合作通讯现正筹备出版
    【本报讯】现在北平的各基层合作社已达到一百多个，拥有社员二十余万人，下半年并计划建立全市的合作网，达到六十万合作社员。为了进一步巩固与发展合作事业，迅速传播合作消息，市总社现正筹备出版一种月刊，名为“北平合作通讯”，作为广大社员批评建议的园地；也作为教育干部，交流经验，研究业务的园地。该刊的直接对象是本市的各种合作社干部和社员。
          （郑重）
