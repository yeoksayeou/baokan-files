Title: Hui People's College Ready for Establishment; Enrollment to Begin at the End of August
Author: Du Qing
Date: 1949-08-06
Page: 2

    Hui People's College Ready for Establishment
    Enrollment to Begin at the End of August
    [This newspaper's dispatch] To enhance the culture of the Hui people and cultivate revolutionary cadres from the Hui population, the North China People's Government previously decided to merge the Chengda Normal School and the Northwest Middle School to form the National Hui People's College. The reorganization of the college has been prepared. The first enrollment will begin in the North China region at the end of August, with registration from the 10th to the 16th and examinations on the 18th.
                  (Du Qing)

### 回民学院筹备就绪  八月底开始招生
杜青
1949-08-06
第2版()
专栏：

    回民学院筹备就绪
    八月底开始招生
    【本报讯】为提高回民文化，培养回民革命干部，前经华北人民政府决定，将成达师范与西北中学合并，组成国立回民学院。该院改组工作业已筹备就绪。订于八月下旬先在华北区范围内第一次招生，十日至十六日报名，十八日考试。
                  （杜青）
