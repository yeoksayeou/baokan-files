Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-08-04
Page: 1

    Tonight's program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station: At 19:50 to 20:10, an address by Engineer Shen Hong to friends in the national scientific community. At 19:30 to 19:50, Women's Time: Introducing Shen Sanma, a rural working woman who bravely sacrificed herself in the struggle against the Kuomintang reactionaries. At 22:20 to 22:50, an arts and culture program, broadcast by the Second Art Troupe of the Third Field Army of the People's Liberation Army: a comic dialogue and small opera "Meritorious Service Flower Drum Lantern."

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚十九点五十分到二十点十分演讲节目，沈鸿工程师对全国科学界朋友们的讲话。十九点三十分到十九点五十分妇女时间：介绍一位和国民党反动派斗争中壮烈牺牲的农村劳动妇女沈三妈。二十二点二十分到二十二点五十分文艺节目，由人民解放军第三野战军第二文工团播送快板小戏“立功花鼓灯”。
