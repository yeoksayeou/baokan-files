Title: National Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Symposium to be Held in Beiping This Month; All-China Federation of Trade Unions Issues Notice
Author:
Date: 1949-08-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, July 7th: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions has decided to convene a National Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Symposium in Beiping on August 26th to discuss the establishment of a nationwide postal and telecommunications trade union organization and to resolve major difficulties existing in postal and telecommunications services in various regions. This notice was issued on July 31st. The notice stipulates that the Northeast Postal and Telecommunications Bureau, the North China Postal Administration, the North China Telecommunications Administration, the Central China Telecommunications Administration, the Shanghai Telecommunications Administration, the postal administrations of Shandong, Jiangsu, Henan, Hubei, and Songjiang provinces, and the postal administrations of Shanghai, Beiping, Tianjin, Taiyuan, Changchun, and Xi'an shall each send one representative from their respective postal or telecommunications trade unions to attend. If a local postal trade union organization already exists, the trade union shall elect the representative. If there is no trade union organization, the local Federation of Trade Unions (or Preparatory Committee) shall consult with the local postal and telecommunications workers to elect the representative.

The notice also stipulates that the attending representatives should collect materials on the organizational situation of trade unions, business conditions before and after liberation, as well as problems that have been resolved and still exist, and prepare opinions on the establishment of a national postal and telecommunications general trade union organization.

### 全国邮电工人工作座谈会本月在平召开  全国总工会发出通知

1949-08-08
第1版()
专栏：

    全国邮电工人工作座谈会本月在平召开
    全国总工会发出通知
    【新华社北平七日电】中华全国总工会决定于八月二十六日在平召开全国邮电工人工作座谈会，讨论产生全国性的邮电工会组织及解决各地邮电业务上所存在的重大困难等问题。此项通知已于上月三十一日发出，通知规定东北邮电总局、华北邮政总局、华北电信总局、华中电信总局、上海电信总局、山东、江苏、河南、湖北、松江五省邮政管理局、上海、北平、天津、太原、长春、西安六市邮政管理局等的邮政工会或电信工会各选派代表一人出席。凡当地已有邮政工会组织者由工会推选，无工会组织者由当地总工会（或筹委会）与该地邮电工人商量推选之。
    通知还规定出席代表搜集关于解放前后的工会组织情况、业务情况以及已经解决的和还存在的问题的材料，并准备对成立全国性邮电总工会组织的意见。
