Title: Former News Attaché of the Yugoslav Embassy in Sweden, Immovic, Refuses to Return Home and Criticizes Tito's Terrorist Policies
Author:
Date: 1949-08-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th: TASS Stockholm reports: The former news attaché of the Yugoslav Embassy in Stockholm, Immovic, has refused to return home and has written articles fiercely criticizing the terrorist regime established by the Tito clique. Immovic, after returning to Belgrade from Stockholm, worked in the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and was later transferred to the Propaganda Department. In July 1949, Immovic went to Stockholm again as a member of the Yugoslav delegation attending the international sports competition held there.

### 南驻瑞典使馆前任新闻参赞　伊姆毛维区拒绝回国  抨击铁托恐怖政策

1949-08-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　南驻瑞典使馆前任新闻参赞
  　伊姆毛维区拒绝回国
    抨击铁托恐怖政策
    【新华社北平十九日电】塔斯社斯德哥尔摩讯：驻斯德哥尔摩的南斯拉夫公使馆前任新闻参赞伊姆毛维区，已拒绝回国，并著文猛烈抨击铁托集团所建立的恐怖政权。按伊姆毛维区从斯德哥尔摩返回贝尔格莱德之后，曾在外交部供职，随后又调到宣传部。一九四九年七月，伊姆毛维区又前赴斯德哥尔摩，作为出席在该地举行的国际运动竞赛的南斯拉夫代表团团员。
