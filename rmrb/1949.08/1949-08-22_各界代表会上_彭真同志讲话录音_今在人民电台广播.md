Title: Speech Recording of Comrade Peng Zhen at the All-Circles Representatives Conference to be Broadcast on People's Radio Today
Author:
Date: 1949-08-22
Page: 4

　　Speech Recording of Comrade Peng Zhen at the All-Circles Representatives Conference to be Broadcast on People's Radio Today

　　[This newspaper reports] The Beiping People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the recording of the summary report by Comrade Peng Zhen, Secretary of the Beiping Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, at the Beiping Municipal All-Circles Representatives Conference at 20:10 this evening (the 22nd). Furthermore, starting tomorrow (the 23rd), it will successively invite Vice Mayor of the People's Government Zhang Youyu, Xiao Ming of the Trade Union Preparatory Committee, Xu Liqun, Director of the Beiping Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League Preparatory Committee, Gu Qizong, Chairman of the Student Federation, the Director of the Women's Federation Preparatory Committee, and others to broadcast. During "Workers' Time," it will successively broadcast the work situation of the city's Chinese medicine industry trade union and the process and experience of signing collective contracts.

### 各界代表会上　彭真同志讲话录音　今在人民电台广播

1949-08-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　各界代表会上
  　彭真同志讲话录音
  　今在人民电台广播
    【本报讯】北平人民广播电台今（二十二）日晚间二十点十分播送中共北平市委书记彭真同志在北平市各界代表会议上总结报告的录音。并自明日（二十三日起）陆续请人民政府张友渔副市长、职工总会筹委会萧明、青年团北平市筹委会主任许立群、学联主席古奇踪、妇联筹委会主任等广播。并于“职工时间”内，陆续广播本市国药业职工会工作情况和订立集体合同的经过及经验。
