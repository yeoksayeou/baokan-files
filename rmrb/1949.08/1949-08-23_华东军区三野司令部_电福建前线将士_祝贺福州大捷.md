Title: East China Military Region Third Field Army Headquarters Congratulates Fujian Frontline Soldiers on the Great Victory of Fuzhou
Author:
Date: 1949-08-23
Page: 1

　　East China Military Region Third Field Army Headquarters
  　Sends Telegram to Fujian Frontline Soldiers
  　Congratulating Them on the Great Victory of Fuzhou
    [Report from this newspaper] According to a broadcast from the Shanghai People's Broadcasting Station on the 22nd, the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army Headquarters and Political Department sent a telegram congratulating all commanders and fighters on the Fujian front. The telegram stated: "Congratulations on your great victory in liberating Fuzhou, the capital of Fujian Province. It is hoped that you will continue to carry forward the spirit of not avoiding the summer heat, crossing mountains and ridges, overcoming fatigue, and marching thousands of miles. With a heroic and tenacious style of continuous fighting, you should resolutely implement Chairman Mao's and Commander-in-Chief Zhu's orders, thoroughly eliminate the remaining enemies, liberate all of Fujian, liberate Taiwan, and strive unremittingly to liberate all of China."

### 华东军区三野司令部　电福建前线将士　祝贺福州大捷

1949-08-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　华东军区三野司令部
  　电福建前线将士
  　祝贺福州大捷
    【本报讯】据上海人民广播电台二十二日广播，华东军区和第三野战军司令部、政治部，打电报祝贺福建前线的全体指战员。电文称：“庆贺你们解放福建省会福州的伟大胜利。尚望继续发扬不避暑热，翻山越岭，克服疲劳，千里进军精神，以英勇顽强连续战斗的作风，为坚决执行毛主席、朱总司令命令，彻底肃清残敌，解放全福建、解放台湾、解放全中国而奋斗不懈。”
