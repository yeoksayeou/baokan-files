Title: Foreign Exchange Rates Revised Starting Today
Author:
Date: 1949-08-01
Page: 2

    Foreign Exchange Rates Revised Starting Today
    [This newspaper dispatch] The Bank of China's foreign exchange rates will be revised starting today as follows: US dollar 1:2700 yuan (People's Currency, same below), British pound 1:7560 yuan, Hong Kong dollar 1:400 yuan.

### 外汇牌价今起重订

1949-08-01
第2版()
专栏：

    外汇牌价今起重订
    【本报讯】中国银行外汇牌价今日起重新改订为：美钞一比二七○○元（人民券下同），英镑一比七五六○元，港币一比四○○元。
