Title: Moving Towards Building a New Xi'an: Representatives from All Circles in Xi'an Gather for Discussion; General He Long Proposes Five Major Tasks; Transforming a Consumer City into a Productive City
Author:
Date: 1949-08-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 6th: The Xi'an City All-Circles Representative Conference was held from July 28th to August 1st. A total of 302 representatives attended. Xi Zhongxun, Secretary of the Northwest Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Yang Mingxuan, Vice Chairman of the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Government, Zhao Shoushan, Deputy Commander of the First Field Army of the People's Liberation Army, and Yang Zilian, Secretary-General of the Northwest General Branch of the China Democratic League, also attended the meeting. He Long, Chairman of the conference, stated in his opening speech that the primary tasks of the people of Xi'an at present are: (1) To fully support the front lines in order to quickly eliminate the Hu-Ma bandit troops. (2) To eliminate bandits and special agents, and establish a revolutionary order. (3) To actively restore and develop production, overcome various difficulties, and pay attention to correctly and systematically resolving the problems of public factories and the labor-capital relations of private factories. (4) To begin planning and organizing unemployed citizens to go to various production fronts, in order to gradually transform Xi'an from a consumer city into a productive city, and to rationally adjust the personnel institutions of certain factories, so as to avoid wasting manpower and materials. (5) To stabilize finance and commodity prices.

Jia Tuofu, Deputy Director of the Xi'an Military Control Commission and Mayor of Xi'an, reported on the takeover and municipal work in Xi'an over the past two months. The entire body of representatives first divided into nineteen groups to discuss this report. Then, representatives from each group were selected to report the discussion situation to the conference. A total of 209 proposals related to civil affairs, finance, construction, culture and education, public security and justice, and health were adopted by the conference and submitted to the Military Control Commission and the Municipal People's Government for implementation. At the meeting, a telegram was unanimously passed to pay tribute to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, support the prompt convening of the New Political Consultative Conference, the establishment of a democratic coalition government, support the establishment of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and pay tribute to all commanders and fighters of the First Field Army. The conference also elected Zhao Boping as the Chairman of the All-Circles Representative Conference, and Yang Zilian, Zhu Zitong, Ma Dehan, and Wang Jingqiu (female) as Vice Chairmen. Zhao Boping delivered the closing speech, pointing out that in addition to worker representatives occupying the main seats, peasants, rickshaw pullers, the poor, ethnic minorities, and democratic figures also had their own representatives attending. Although the representatives had different opinions on certain specific issues, the meeting was conducted towards the common political goal of building a new democratic Xi'an. He hoped that the representatives would assist the government in implementing the conference's proposals after the meeting, regularly reflect the opinions of the masses to the Military Control Commission and the Municipal Government, and be responsible for publicizing and explaining the policies and decrees of the People's Government to the people.

### 向建设新西安迈进  西安各界代表集会讨论  贺龙将军提出五大任务  变消费城市为生产城市

1949-08-07
第2版()
专栏：

    向建设新西安迈进
    西安各界代表集会讨论
    贺龙将军提出五大任务
    变消费城市为生产城市
    【新华社西安六日电】西安市各界代表会于七月二十八日至八月一日举行。出席代表共三百零二名。中共中央西北局书记习仲勋、陕甘宁边区政府副主席杨明轩、人民解放军第一野战军副司令员赵寿山、民盟西北总支部秘书长杨子廉等亦到会参加。大会主席贺龙在开幕词中说明西安人民当前的首要任务是：（一）全力支援前线，以便迅速消灭胡马匪军。（二）肃清匪特，建立革命秩序。（三）积极恢复和发展生产，克服各种困难，注意正确地有步骤地解决公营工厂的问题和私营工厂的劳资关系问题。（四）着手计划组织无职业的市民到各种生产战线上去，以便逐步地把西安市由消费城市变为生产城市，并合理调整某些工厂人事机构，以免浪费人力物力。（五）稳定金融物价。
    由西安市军管会副主任兼西安市长贾托夫在会上报告了西安两月来接管及市政工作。先由全体代表分为十九个小组就这一报告进行讨论。然后由各组推选代表向大会报告讨论情形。经大会通过成立的有关民政、财政、建设、文教、公安司法、卫生等方面议案共达二百零九件，交军管会及市人民政府采行。会上并一致通过通电向毛主席、朱总司令致敬，拥护迅速召开新政协，成立民主联合政府，拥护成立中苏友好协会及向第一野战军全体指战员致敬等提案。大会并选出赵伯平为各界代表会主席，杨子廉、朱子彤、马德涵、王竞秋（女）等四人为副主席。赵伯平致闭幕词，指出：这次代表会除工人代表占主要席位外，农民、洋车夫、贫民、少数民族及民主人士亦均有自己的代表出席。各位代表虽然在某些具体的问题上也有着不同意见，但会议是朝着建设一个新民主主义的新西安这个共同的政治目标进行的。他希望各位代表在会后应协助政府实行大会的议案，经常向军管会及市政府反映群众意见，并负责向人民宣传解释人民政府的政策、法令。
