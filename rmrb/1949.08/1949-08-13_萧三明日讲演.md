Title: Xiao San to Give Lecture Tomorrow
Author: 
Date: 1949-08-13
Page: 8

    [This Municipality News] The fifth Chinese Youth Lecture Series, sponsored by the China Youth Society, is scheduled to be held at 3:00 PM on the 14th (Sunday) in the auditorium of the Yiwen Middle School on Fuqian Street. Xiao San, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, will speak on the topic of youth learning. All are welcome to attend. No admission tickets will be issued, and entry will be limited to the seating capacity of the auditorium.

### 萧三明日讲演

1949-08-13
第8版()
专栏：

    萧三明日讲演
    【本市讯】中国青年社主办的第五次中国青年讲演会，订于十四日（星期日）午后三时在府前街艺文中学大礼堂，请中苏友好协会副总干事萧三讲青年学习问题。欢迎听讲，不另发入场券，以礼堂座满为限。
