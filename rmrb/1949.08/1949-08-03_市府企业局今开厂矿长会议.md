Title: Municipal Enterprise Bureau to Hold Factory and Mine Director Meeting Today
Author:
Date: 1949-08-03
Page: 5

    Municipal Enterprise Bureau
    To Hold Factory and Mine Director Meeting Today
    [This City News] The Municipal People's Government Enterprise Bureau has scheduled a joint meeting of factory and mine directors (managers) for 9:00 AM today (the 3rd) to report on the completion status of each factory and mine's July work plan, the reasons for any failures to complete the plan, and the central focus of each factory and mine's work in August.

### 市府企业局今开厂矿长会议

1949-08-03
第5版()
专栏：

    市府企业局
    今开厂矿长会议
    【本市讯】市人民政府企业局，定于今（三）日上午九时，召集各厂、矿长（经理）联席会议，汇报各厂矿七月份工作计划完成程度，和未能完成的原因，并报告各厂矿八月份工作中心。
