Title: National Federation of Literary and Art Circles to Publish "People's Literature"
Author:
Date: 1949-08-04
Page: 1

The All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles has decided to publish a monthly magazine entitled "People's Literature." Mao Dun, Ouyang Shan, Feng Xuefeng, Ai Qing, and He Qifang will serve as members of the editorial board, with Mao Dun as the chairman. The editorial office has been located at 16 Duofu Lane, Wangfujing Street, Beiping (the former site of the "North China Literature and Art" office). Preparations are underway, and it is expected to be available to readers on October 1st.

### 全国文协出版“人民文学”

1949-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    全国文协出版“人民文学”
    【本报讯】中华全国文学工作者协会顷决定出版月刊一种，定名为“人民文学”，由茅盾、欧阳山、冯雪峰、艾青、何其芳分任编辑委员，茅盾任主任委员，社址已觅定北平王府大街多福巷十六号（“华北文艺”社旧址），现正积极筹备中，闻十月一日即可与读者见面。
