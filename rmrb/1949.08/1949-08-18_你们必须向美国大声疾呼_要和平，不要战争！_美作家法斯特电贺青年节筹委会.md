Title: You Must Cry Out Loud to America: We Want Peace, Not War! American Writer Fast Sends Congratulations to Youth Day Preparatory Committee
Author:
Date: 1949-08-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 17th: Budapest dispatch: The famous American progressive writer Howard Fast sent a letter to the World Youth Day Preparatory Committee to congratulate them. He wrote: In America, youth means facing the horrors of war and unemployment. Most American youth, like you, are yearning for a better life. Fast emphasized: Young people, the future of the world belongs to you. You must cry out loud: you don't want war, you don't want fascism; you crave a peaceful life so that young people all over the world can work together to build a new world. Say these things loudly so that these words can be heard on this side of the ocean, here in America.

### 你们必须向美国大声疾呼　要和平，不要战争！  美作家法斯特电贺青年节筹委会

1949-08-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　你们必须向美国大声疾呼
  　要和平，不要战争！
    美作家法斯特电贺青年节筹委会
    【新华社北平十七日电】布达佩斯讯：著名的美国进步作家法斯特致书世界青年节筹委会祝贺。他写道：在美国，青年就意味着面对战争的恐怖和失业。大多数美国青年正同你们一样渴求美好的生活。法斯特着重地说：青年们，世界的将来是属于你们的。你们必须大声疾呼：你们不要战争，不要法西斯主义；你们渴求和平的生活，以便全世界青年能通力合作，建立新的世界。把这些大声说出来，好让这些话在海洋的这边，在美国这儿，也能听到。
