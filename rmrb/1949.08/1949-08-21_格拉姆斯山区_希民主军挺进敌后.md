Title: Grammos Mountains: Greek Democratic Army Advances Behind Enemy Lines
Author:
Date: 1949-08-21
Page: 3
　　
　　Grammos Mountains
  　Greek Democratic Army Advances Behind Enemy Lines
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 20th: TASS, Bucharest, August 18th: Free Greece Radio broadcast a communique issued by the Supreme Command of the Greek Democratic Army, stating: Greek Democratic Army troops within the Vitsi region have been ordered to move laterally and advance into the enemy's rear. In the Vitsi area, units of the Democratic Army remain to conduct smaller-scale warfare.
    In the Grammos Mountains, the enemy continues to concentrate manpower and equipment. Democratic Army troops, in accordance with the Supreme Command's plan, have advanced behind enemy lines and are now reorganizing.

### 格拉姆斯山区　希民主军挺进敌后

1949-08-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　格拉姆斯山区
  　希民主军挺进敌后
    【新华社北平二十日电】塔斯社布加勒斯特十八日讯，自由希腊电台广播希腊民主军最高统帅部所颁布的公报称：维齐境内希腊民主军部队已奉命由侧翼运动而挺进敌人的后方。在维齐地区，仍留有民主军的部队，以进行规模较小的战争。
    在格拉姆斯山区，敌人继续集中人力与装备。民主军部队依照最高统帅部的计划，已挺进敌后，现正重整队伍中。
