Title: Protecting the City is Protecting Production: Shanghai Liberation Army Visits Factories, Class Consciousness Greatly Enhanced
Author:
Date: 1949-08-03
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 3rd: The People's Liberation Army unit garrisoning Shanghai has been using factory visits and inviting workers to give reports to raise the awareness of the working class among commanders and fighters, the vast majority of whom are of peasant origin. Since the unit entered Shanghai, various units have visited many state-owned and private factories and invited male and female workers from various factories to report to the troops on their lives and past struggles with Kuomintang secret agents. After visiting and listening to reports, the troops initially realized the oppression and suffering the workers endured in the old society, and the high degree of unity and tenacious struggle of the working class, greatly enhancing the class consciousness of the troops. Soldiers of the tenth squad of the third company of a certain unit said after having a get-together with the workers: "We only knew that capitalists exploited workers before, but we didn't know how they exploited them. After listening to the reports from the worker brothers, we really understood." The soldiers deeply felt the hardship and greatness of the workers' work during the visits. Workers at the Fufeng Iron Factory spoke about how the factory workers endured hardship in order to resume production as soon as possible after the liberation of Shanghai, which deeply moved many soldiers. The troops who visited the shipyard, after listening to the workers' reports about their heroic struggle against imperialism and the Kuomintang reactionaries without weapons, said at a discussion meeting, Zhao Zhan, the political instructor of the sixth company, said: "We have weapons in our hands today, and some comrades still want to lay down their weapons and leave the ranks, which is really wrong." In the past, many soldiers always thought that the workers' lives were good and that they had not suffered much. After listening to the workers' reports, this idea also changed. When the direct team of a certain regiment visited the workers' residential area of ​​the Dachunghua Match Factory, they saw worker Wu Fuyou, who had worked for twenty-eight years, and his family of seven still lived in a small shack. After returning from the visit, a small number of commanders and fighters with hedonistic ideas began to realize that the glamorous things in the city were all the playthings of the exploiting class, and initially corrected their wrong ideas. Wu Yunnan, a soldier of the tenth squad of a certain unit, said: "After entering the city, I always wanted to eat good food and wear good clothes. After listening to the workers' reports and seeing the workers' lives, I thought about my past ideas and felt that they were all wrong. We must learn from the workers and not from the exploiting class." After visiting the factories, the commanders and fighters personally saw the greatness of industrial production. For example, after a certain unit visited the Hengda Yarn Factory, they learned that the factory's daily output could provide clothes for 300 people for a year, and the machine production of 800 ### 保护城市就是保护生产  沪解放军参观工厂  阶级觉悟大大提高

1949-08-03
第2版()
专栏：

    保护城市就是保护生产
    沪解放军参观工厂  阶级觉悟大大提高
    【新华社上海三日电】警备上海的人民解放军某部，利用参观工厂和请工人作报告的方法，提高绝大部分是农民出身的指挥员战斗员对工人阶级的认识。该部自进驻上海以来，各单位曾分别参观了国营和私营的许多工厂，邀请了各工厂的男女工人，来部队报告他们的生活和过去与国民党特务斗争的情形。经过了参观和听报告的部队，都初步认识到工人在旧社会里所受的压迫和痛苦，和工人阶级高度的团结性和顽强的斗争性，大大提高部队的阶级觉悟，某部三连十班的战士们，在和工人们开过联欢会以后说：“以前光知道资本家剥削工人，但不知道怎样剥削的，听了工人兄弟的报告以后，我们才真正懂了”。战士们在参观中深深感觉到工人们工作的艰苦和伟大。阜丰铁工厂的工人讲到上海解放后，该厂工人为了尽早恢复生产忍受艰苦的情形，使许多战士深为感动。参观造船厂的部队，在听了工人们报告他们手无寸铁和帝国主义及国民党反动派进行英勇斗争的情形后，在讨论会上六连政治指导员赵瞻说：“我们今天手里有了武器，有的同志竟还想放下武器脱离队伍，真是错误。”过去不少战士总以为工人的生活好，没吃什么苦，听了工人的报告后，这种想法也转变了。某团直属队在访问大中华火柴厂的工人住区时，看到工人吴福有，做了二十八年工，现在全家七口人还是挤在一间小棚房里。访问回来后，少数有享乐思想的指战员们，开始认识了城市中的花花绿绿，都是剥削阶级的玩意儿，初步纠正了自己的错误思想。某部十班战士吴云南说：“进了城老想吃好的，穿好的。这次听了工人报告，看了工人的生活，想想过去的想法，觉得都错了。我们要向工人看齐，不要向剥削阶级看齐。”经过参观工厂，指战员们亲眼看到了工业生产的伟大。如某部参观恒大纱厂，知道了该厂每天的产量可供三百人一年的衣服，八百名工人的机器生产可抵五万人的手工业生产，开始懂得要建设新中国一定先要发展工业生产，并进一步认识了保护城市就是保护生产，就是保护人民祖国的财富，纠正了过去某些同志以为保护城市是保护有钱人的错误想法。许多连队并因此加强了自己的警备工作。某部三连提出“不让有偷盗抢劫的事件在我们地区发生”。该连个别战士过去站岗不起劲，现在也改变了。
