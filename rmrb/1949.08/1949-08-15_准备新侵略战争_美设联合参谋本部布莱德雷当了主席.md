Title: Preparing for a New War of Aggression: US Establishes Joint Chiefs of Staff with Bradley as Chairman
Author:
Date: 1949-08-15
Page: 3

    Preparing for a New War of Aggression
    US Establishes Joint Chiefs of Staff with Bradley as Chairman
    [Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 14th] Washington Dispatch: Truman appointed Bradley, an active implementer and advocate of the new US war policy, as the chairman of the newly established Joint Chiefs of Staff on the 11th. On the 12th, Truman also appointed Army Vice Chief of Staff Collins as Army Chief of Staff to fill Bradley's vacancy. According to the new National Military Establishment Act signed by Truman on the 10th, Bradley will lead the three chiefs of staff of the US Army, Navy, and Air Force to implement the US military policy of preparing for a new war of aggression. At the same time, Truman appointed Eagan Fritz, the vice president of US Steel Corporation, as the chairman of the Munitions Board on the 12th. The above three appointments are subject to Senate approval.

### 准备新侵略战争  美设联合参谋本部布莱德雷当了主席

1949-08-15
第3版()
专栏：

    准备新侵略战争
    美设联合参谋本部布莱德雷当了主席
    【新华社北平十四日电】华盛顿讯：杜鲁门于十一日任命美国新战争政策的积极执行者与鼓吹者布莱德雷为新设立的联合参谋本部的主席。十二日，杜鲁门又任命陆军副参谋长柯林斯为陆军参谋总长，以补布莱德雷的遗缺。根据杜鲁门于十日签署的新军事统一法，布莱德雷将领导美国海陆空军三个参谋总长，以执行美国准备新侵略战争的军事政策。杜鲁门同时于十二日任命美国钢公司的副总经理伊根佛利兹为军火委员会的主席。上述三人的任命，尚待参院的批准。
