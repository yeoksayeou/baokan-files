Title: Natural Science Workers in Beiping to Hold Zonal Symposiums on the White Paper; Calls on Local Branches to Also Initiate Discussions
Author:
Date: 1949-08-31
Page: 1

　　Natural Science Workers in Beiping
　　To Hold Zonal Symposiums on the White Paper
　　Calls on Local Branches to Also Initiate Discussions
　　[This newspaper's dispatch] The Preparatory Committee of the All-China Natural Science Workers Representative Assembly, in order to further expand the discussion of the "White Paper" issue, has decided that before September 5th, scientific workers in various schools, organizations, and groups in Beiping will be divided into twelve zones, including Tsinghua University, Peking University, Xiehe Hospital, Beiping Research Institute, Mining and Metallurgical Research Institute, and Geological Survey Institute, to hold separate symposiums. At the same time, it calls on local branches to also launch discussions on the "White Paper." It is also preparing to compile all symposium records and publish a special issue in "Science Communication."

### 在平自然科学工作者  将分区座谈白皮书  号召各地分会亦展开讨论

1949-08-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　在平自然科学工作者
    将分区座谈白皮书
    号召各地分会亦展开讨论
    【本报讯】中华全国自然科学工作者代表大会筹备委员会为进一步展开“白皮书”问题的讨论，决定于九月五日以前将在平各学校、机关、团体科学工作者划分为清华大学、北京大学、协和医院、北平研究院、矿冶研究所及地质调查所等十二区分别举行座谈会。同时号召各地分会亦展开“白皮书”的讨论。并准备将所有座谈会记录汇集起来于“科学通讯”刊登专辑。
