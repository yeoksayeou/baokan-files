Title: People of the Four Nordic Countries to Jointly Hold Peace Week
Author:
Date: 1949-08-21
Page: 3

　　People of the Four Nordic Countries
  　Will Jointly Hold Peace Week
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 20th] TASS, Copenhagen reports: The Danish newspaper "People's Land" reports that the people of the four Nordic countries of Norway, Sweden, Denmark, and Finland have decided to hold a grand "Week for Strengthening the World Peace Movement" in September and October. This movement was initiated by the Norwegian Provisional Peace Committee, which was established after the World Peace Congress in Paris. Norway's Peace Week is scheduled to be held at the end of September and the beginning of October, Sweden from September 11th to 18th, Finland from September 25th to October 2nd, and Denmark from October 2nd to 9th. At that time, the four countries will organize various peace conferences and invite famous foreign figures who support peace to attend. According to a Polish Press Agency report from Stockholm, the chairman of the Swedish Peace Committee, Mr. Balmar, stated that the committee will invite Wallace, Curie, Zilliacus, and the Archbishop of Canterbury, Johnson, to give speeches.

### 北欧四国人民　将联合举行和平周

1949-08-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　北欧四国人民
  　将联合举行和平周
    【新华社北平二十日电】据塔斯社哥本哈根讯：丹麦“人民园地”报报道：北欧挪威、瑞典、丹麦、芬兰四国人民已定于九、十月间先后举行盛大的“巩固世界和平运动周”。这一运动系由在巴黎世界和平大会后成立的挪威临时和平委员会发起。挪威的和平周定于九月底十月初举行，瑞典九月十一日至十八日，芬兰九月二十五日至十月二日，丹麦十月二日至九日，届时四国将组织各种和平大会，并将邀请外国著名拥护和平的人士出席。据波兰通讯社斯德哥尔摩消息，瑞典和平委员会主席巴尔马称，该会将邀请华莱士、居里、齐里雅库斯及坎特柏里大主教约翰逊等演讲。
