Title: Military Commission Railway Department Adjusts Railway Management Organizations
Author:
Date: 1949-08-18
Page: 1

　　Military Commission Railway Department
  　Adjusts Railway Management Organizations
    [Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station Broadcast] Beijing, 17th: The Railway Department of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission, due to work needs, has recently decided to slightly change the original railway management organizations. The Zhengzhou Railway Administration will establish a Xi'an Branch Bureau; in addition, offices will be established in Hankou and Xinxiang. The Jinan Railway Administration will establish Qingdao, Xuzhou, and Bengbu Branch Bureaus. The Xuzhou Office originally established by the Zhengzhou Administration will be abolished, and the Huainan Railway Bureau will be abolished.

### 军委铁道部　调整铁路管理机构

1949-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　军委铁道部
  　调整铁路管理机构
    【北平新华广播电台广播】北平十七日消息：中国人民革命军事委员会铁路部，最近因为工作需要，决定将原有铁路管理机构略予改变。郑州铁路管理局成立西安分局；另外在汉口、新乡两个地方设立办事处。济南铁路管理局成立青岛、徐州、蚌埠三个分局。郑州管理局原来设立的徐州办事处撤销，淮南铁路局撤销。
