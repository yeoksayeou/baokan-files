Title: Cheng Qian and Chen Mingren Host Banquet for Changsha Military and Political Leaders
Author:
Date: 1949-08-31
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, 21st (Delayed Dispatch) Generals Cheng Qian and Chen Mingren hosted a banquet on the evening of the 19th in the Provincial Government Auditorium to honor Xiao Jingguang, Director of the Changsha Military Control Commission, Wang Shoudao, Deputy Director, and all members of the Military Control Commission, as well as the newly appointed directors of various departments and bureaus of the Provincial Government. Also invited were the heads of military and political organs such as the 12th Corps of the Fourth Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Changsha Garrison Command, along with prominent figures from all walks of life in Hunan, totaling over 140 guests. During the banquet, General Cheng Qian delivered a speech, expressing deep praise for Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu's correct leadership and the victorious advance of the People's Liberation Army, which enabled the peaceful liberation of Changsha and Hunan. He also declared: From now on, he is willing to work under the leadership of Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu to completely annihilate the Chiang Kai-shek and Bai Chongxi bandit troops and build a new Hunan. Director Xiao Jingguang gave an impromptu reply, expressing his utmost respect for the resolute uprising of Generals Cheng and Chen. He stated that the current spirit of unanimous cooperation in Hunan is most commendable. In the future, the Communist Party side in Hunan is willing to cooperate sincerely with Generals Cheng and Chen and prominent figures from all walks of life to build a prosperous and progressive New Democratic Hunan. During the banquet, guests repeatedly raised their glasses to congratulate each other, and the event concluded joyfully at 8:30 PM.

### 程潜陈明仁欢宴长沙军政首长

1949-08-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　程潜陈明仁欢宴长沙军政首长
    【新华社长沙二十一日电】（迟到）程潜、陈明仁两将军十九日晚，在省府大礼堂欢宴长沙军管会萧劲光主任、王首道副主任及军管会各委员暨省府新任各厅处长，及中国人民解放军第四野战军十二兵团、长沙警备司令部等军政机关首长，并邀湖南各界贤达作陪，宾主共一百四十余人。席间程潜将军致词，对毛主席、朱总司令的正确领导，人民解放军的胜利进军，使长沙和湖南和平解放得以实现，深致赞颂。并声称：今后愿在毛主席、朱总司令领导下为尽歼蒋白匪军，建设新湖南而努力。萧劲光主任即席答词，对程、陈两将军毅然起义，极表敬佩。并谓湖南当前一致合作的精神最值钦幸。今后湖南中共方面，愿开诚布公与程、陈二将军及各界贤达推诚合作，以谋建成繁荣进步的新民主主义的湖南。席间宾主屡屡举杯互贺，至八点半始尽欢而散。
