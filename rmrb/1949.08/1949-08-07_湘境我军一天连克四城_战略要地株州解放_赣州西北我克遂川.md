Title: Our Army in Xiang Province Liberates Four Cities in One Day; Strategic Stronghold of Zhuzhou Liberated; Our Forces Capture Suichuan Northwest of Ganzhou
Author:
Date: 1949-08-07
Page: 1

    Our Army in Xiang Province Liberates Four Cities in One Day
    Strategic Stronghold of Zhuzhou Liberated
    Our Forces Capture Suichuan Northwest of Ganzhou
    [Xinhua News Agency, Central China Front, 6th] The People's Liberation Army on the Central China Front captured Zhuzhou, a vital military town southeast of Changsha, on the morning of the 3rd, and liberated the cities of Yiyang, Yuanjiang, and Huarong north and south of Dongting Lake on the same day. Zhuzhou is the intersection of the Xiang-Gan, Yue-Han, and Xiang-Qian railways, and is the site of the largest locomotive factory in Central China, holding an important strategic position. Yiyang is the central city in the lower reaches of the Zi River, and tea, hemp, tobacco, and cotton from the Zi River basin are all gathered and distributed there. Banxi, southwest of the county, is a famous antimony mine producing area.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Central China Front, 6th] A unit of the People's Liberation Army liberated Suichuan County, northwest of Ganzhou, on the 1st of this month, defeating the majority of the enemy's 211th and 213th Divisions of the 23rd Army, and capturing more than 900 enemy troops, including the commander of the Third Column of the Jiangxi Pacification Headquarters.

### 湘境我军一天连克四城  战略要地株州解放  赣州西北我克遂川

1949-08-07
第1版()
专栏：

    湘境我军一天连克四城
    战略要地株州解放
    赣州西北我克遂川
    【新华社华中前线六日电】华中前线人民解放军三日晨占领长沙东南军事要镇株州，并于同日解放洞庭湖南北的益阳、沅江、华容等城。株州系湘赣、粤汉、湘黔三铁路的交叉点，为华中最大的机车厂所在地，在战略上居重要地位。益阳为资江下游的中心城市，资江流域的茶、麻、烟草、棉花均以该地为集散地。该县西南的板溪为著名锑矿的出产地。
    【新华社华中前线六日电】人民解放军某部本月一日解放赣州西北的遂川县城，击溃敌二十三军二一一、二一三师等大部，俘敌江西绥署第三纵队司令以下九百余名。
