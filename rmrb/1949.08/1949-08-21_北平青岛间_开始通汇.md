Title: Currency Exchange Between Beiping and Qingdao Begins

Author:

Date: 1949-08-21
Page: 4

　　Currency Exchange Between Beiping and Qingdao
　　Begins
　　[This newspaper's dispatch] Currency exchange between Beiping and Qingdao has begun. The business department of the People's Bank of China's Beiping branch has started undertaking currency exchange business between Beiping and Qingdao. Exchange rates will be posted and announced daily.

### 北平青岛间　开始通汇

1949-08-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　北平青岛间
  　开始通汇
    【本报讯】北平与青岛间已通汇。人民银行北平分行营业部已开始承作北平青岛间汇兑业务，汇率每日挂牌公布。
