Title: Siamese Government's Anti-Chinese Atrocities Include Sealing Off Overseas Chinese Schools
Author:
Date: 1949-08-21
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 19th: The Siamese government has once again begun anti-Chinese atrocities. According to Agence France-Presse, Bangkok, the Siamese Ministry of Education, under the pretext of so-called "violations of the Private School Organization Act," has sealed off several overseas Chinese schools. Several overseas Chinese newspapers in Bangkok have condemned this autocratic action by the Siamese government.

### 暹政府排华暴行竟查封华侨学校

1949-08-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　暹政府排华暴行竟查封华侨学校
    【新华社北平十九日电】暹罗政府又开始排华暴行。据法新社曼谷讯，暹教育部借口所谓“违犯私立学校组织法案”，已将数家华侨学校查封。曼谷若干华侨报纸对暹政府此种专制行动提出谴责。
