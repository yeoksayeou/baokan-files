Title: Farewell

Author: Huang Wei

Date: 1949-08-12
Page: 6

     Farewell
     Goodbye, Beiping
     Goodbye, Comrades
     Remembering your囑咐 (zhǔfù - instructions, exhortations),
     Carrying the people's hopes.
     We are going to the Southwest,
     To the frontier!
     "Go to the border region,
     Work hard.
     The people's army is welcomed everywhere
     By the people,
     Living among the masses
     You will be tempered to be stronger!"
     Comrades, don't worry,
     We have nothing to hold us back.
     Mao Zedong's great banner
     Has withstood storms,
     Mao Zedong's good students,
     Can also withstand tests!
     In the Central Plains, on the frontier,
     All are doing revolutionary work.
     Everyone work hard,
     Show some results
     Let's compare!
     Chairman Mao,
     We are leaving,
     To the Southwest,
     To the frontier,
     Planting rice seedlings on the Pearl River Delta,
     Learning Miao dances on the Yunnan-Guizhou Plateau.
     Humbly learning from the masses,
     Three years, five years,
     Ten years, eight years,
     Then returning to Beiping.
     Chairman Mao,
     At that time,
     You will inspect!

### 辞行
荒苇
1949-08-12
第6版()
专栏：

     辞行
     别了，北平
     再见，同志
     记着你们的嘱咐，
     带了人民的希望。
     我们到西南去，
     到边疆去！
     “边区去，
     好好工作。
     人民的队伍到处受人
     民的欢迎，
     生活在群众里
     你会炼的更坚强！”
     同志，不要惦念，
     我们没有什么牵挂。
     毛泽东的大旗子
     抵挡过狂风暴雨，
     毛泽东的好学生，
     也经得住考验！
     在中原，在边疆，
     做的都是革命工作。
     大家好好干，
     拿出成绩来
     比比看！
     毛主席，
     我们走了，
     到西南，
     到边疆，
     在珠江三角洲上插秧苗，
     在云贵高原上学习苗家舞。
     向群众低头学习，
     三年，五年，
     十年，八年，
     再回到北平来。
     毛主席，
     那时，
     你来检阅！
