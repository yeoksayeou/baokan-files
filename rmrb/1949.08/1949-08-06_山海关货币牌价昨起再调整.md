Title: Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate Adjusted Again Starting Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-08-06
Page: 2

Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate Adjusted Again Starting Yesterday
[Shanhaiguan Electric] The Shanhaiguan Joint Office of the People's Bank of China and the Northeast Bank has adjusted the exchange rate to 1 RMB yuan equals 50 Northeast yuan, effective August 5th.

### 山海关货币牌价昨起再调整

1949-08-06
第2版()
专栏：

    山海关货币牌价昨起再调整
    【山海关电】中国人民银行、东北银行山海关联合办事处自八月五日起牌价改为人民券一元兑换东北券五十元。
