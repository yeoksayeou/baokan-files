Author:
Date: 1949-08-17
Page: 1

　　Beiping Xinhua Radio Station Tonight's Program
    [From Our Newspaper] Beiping Xinhua Radio Station's lecture program from 7:50 PM to 8:10 PM will feature Zhang Huaquan (Wang Xiuluan), a Chinese youth representative attending the World Youth Festival, speaking on "How I Became a Labor Hero." The youth time from 7:30 PM to 7:50 PM will broadcast: (1) "The League's Activities Must Be Integrated with Central Work"; (2) Correspondence – "A Model Factory League Branch." The arts and entertainment program from 10:20 PM to 10:50 PM will feature songs and instrumental music – Pinghan Railway Ballad, @@ Medley, Push Cart, Dialogue of Flowers.

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚七点五十分到八点十分演讲节目，由出席世界青年节的中国青年代表张化泉（王秀鸾）讲：“我是怎样成为劳动英雄的”。七点三十分到七点五十分青年时间，播送：（一）“团的活动必须结合中心工作”；（二）通讯——“一个模范的工厂团支部”。十点二十分到十点五十分文艺节目，唱歌和器乐——平汉路小唱，＠＠联奏，推车，对花。
