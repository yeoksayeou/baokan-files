Title: Jiaotong University Application Deadline: Nearly 4,000 Applicants
Author:
Date: 1949-08-13
Page: 8

Jiaotong University Application Deadline

Nearly 4,000 Applicants

[This newspaper] News: The application deadline for the North China region admissions of China Jiaotong University was yesterday (12th). A total of 1,891 people applied for freshman admission to Tangshan Engineering College, including 1,592 applicants for various engineering departments and 299 applicants for specialized courses. A total of 1,940 people applied for the Beiping Management College, including 1,219 for the university division and 721 for the secondary technical courses. The examination dates for Beiping Management College are the 17th and 18th, and for Tangshan Engineering College, the 19th and 20th. The examination venues are Beiping Management College and Normal University. The schedule for Tangshan Engineering College's examination venue will be announced on the 17th and 18th at Beiping Management College and Normal University.

### 交大报名截止  近四千人投考

1949-08-13
第8版()
专栏：

    交大报名截止
    近四千人投考
    【本报讯】中国交通大学北平区招生报名已于昨（十二）日截止，报考唐山工学院新生共一八九一人，内投考各工程学系者一五九二人，投考专修科者二九九人。报考北平管理学院的共一九四○人，内大学部一二一九人。中等技术科七二一人。考试日期平院为十七、十八两日，唐院为十九、二十两日。试场均在北平管理学院及师范大学。唐院试场次序表，定十七、十八在平院、师大两处公布。
