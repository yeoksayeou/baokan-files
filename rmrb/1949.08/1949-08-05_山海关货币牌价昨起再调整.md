Title: Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate Adjusted Again Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-08-05
Page: 2
Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate
Adjusted Again Yesterday
[Shanhaiguan Telegraph] The Shanhaiguan Joint Office of the People's Bank of China, Northeast Bank, has changed its exchange rate to one RMB yuan兑换 [exchanges for] 55 Northeast yuan, effective August 4th.

### 山海关货币牌价昨起再调整

1949-08-05
第2版()
专栏：

    山海关货币牌价
    昨起再调整
    【山海关电】中国人民银行，东北银行山海关联合办事处自八月四日起牌价改为人民券一元兑换东北券五十五元。
