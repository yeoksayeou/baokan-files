Title: Shenyang Liberated for Eight Months: Private Industry and Commerce Increase Twofold; People's Government Organizes Inspection Team to Understand the Situation of Various Industries
Author:
Date: 1949-08-11
Page: 1
    Shenyang Liberated for Eight Months
    Private Industry and Commerce Increase Twofold
    People's Government Organizes Inspection Team to Understand the Situation of Various Industries
    [Report from this Newspaper] Shenyang News: The Shenyang Municipal People's Government Private Industry and Commerce Inspection Team and the representatives of labor and management from various private industry and commerce sectors in Shenyang, totaling more than 600 people, held a forum on the 5th of this month. Zhu Qiwen, the Mayor of Shenyang, and Li Liguo, the Deputy Secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, both spoke at the meeting. When discussing the situation of industry and commerce in Shenyang, they pointed out that both state-owned and private industry and commerce have recovered and developed in the eight months since liberation. Before liberation, there were more than 7,000 private commercial households and only more than 4,000 private industrial households. Now there are 11,484 private industrial households and 22,284 private commercial households. Comrade Li Liguo explained the industrial and commercial policies of the democratic government, and finally emphasized that "all private industrial and commercial enterprises that are beneficial to the national economy and the people's livelihood should be protected and assisted by the democratic government, and private industrialists and businessmen can only have a bright future and outlet in the New Democratic economic construction."
    [Another Report] In order to implement the economic policy of "facing all directions" to develop industry and commerce in Shenyang, the Shenyang Municipal People's Government established a private industry and commerce inspection team on the 1st of this month, led by Comrade Li Liguo, Deputy Secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Comrade Ma Hong of the Northeast Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, with about 80 cadres. They are divided into an industrial inspection group and a commercial inspection group to investigate the situation of various industries separately, and the work is expected to be completed on the 20th of this month.

### 沈阳解放八个月  私营工商业增加两倍  人民政府组织考察团了解各业情况

1949-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

    沈阳解放八个月
    私营工商业增加两倍
    人民政府组织考察团了解各业情况
    【本报讯】沈阳消息：沈阳市人民政府私营工商业考察团和沈阳市私营工商业各行业劳资双方代表一共六百多人，本月五日举行座谈会，沈阳市朱其文市长和中共沈阳市委副书记李力果都在会上发表谈话，他们谈到沈阳市工商业情况的时候，指出在解放后八个月中国营私营工商业都恢复发展，解放以前私营商业有七千多户，私营工业只有四千多户，现在私营工业已经有一万一千四百八十四户，私营商业有二万二千二百八十四户。李力果同志说明了民主政府的工商业政策，最后并着重指出“一切有利于国计民生的私营工商业都应该受到民主政府的保护和扶助，而且私营工商业者只有在新民主主义经济建设中，才有它的光明前途和出路”。
    【又讯】为了贯彻“四面八方”的经济政策以发展沈阳市工商业，本月一日，沈阳市人民政府成立了私营工商业考察团，由中共沈阳市委副书记李力果同志和中共中央东北局马洪同志负责率领干部约八十人，分为工业考察组和商业考察组，分别调查各业情况，预计在本月二十日结束工作。
