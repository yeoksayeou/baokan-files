Title: Truman Supports Quirino in Plotting the "Pacific Union"
Author:
Date: 1949-08-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 14th -- After arriving in the US, the Philippine puppet president Quirino, under the direct encouragement of Truman, has been engaging in activities to organize a "Pacific Union." After delivering an anti-communist speech in the US Senate on the 9th, Quirino met with the US ambassadors of the Kuomintang bandit gang, South Korea, India, Pakistan, Burma, Thailand, Afghanistan, Ceylon, Syria, Lebanon, and Iraq at the Philippine Embassy in Washington on the 10th to discuss issues related to the organization of the "Pacific Union." On the same day, Quirino met with US Secretary of Defense Johnson, requesting the US to increase military "aid" to the Philippines. On the 11th, Quirino and Truman issued a joint statement. In this statement, Truman expressed that the United States would actively support the Philippine reactionary ruling group and once again promised Quirino that he would "provide all possible assistance" to his organization of the "Pacific Union." Quirino flew from Washington to New York on the 11th, and on his way back to the Philippines, he will go to Los Angeles and San Francisco to appeal for "US aid."

### 杜鲁门扶植季里诺  从事“太平洋联盟”阴谋

1949-08-15
第3版()
专栏：

    杜鲁门扶植季里诺
    从事“太平洋联盟”阴谋
    【新华社北平十四日电】菲律宾傀儡总统季里诺抵美后，即在杜鲁门的直接鼓励下，从事组织“太平洋联盟”的活动。季里诺于九日在美参院发表反共演说后，十日在华盛顿菲大使馆与国民党匪帮及南朝鲜、印度、巴基斯坦、缅甸、泰国、阿富汗、锡兰、叙利亚、黎巴嫩、伊拉克等十一国的驻美使节商谈与组织“太平洋联盟”有关的问题。同日，季里诺会见美国防部长詹森，要求美国增加对菲律宾的军事“援助”。十一日，季里诺与杜鲁门发表一个联合声明。在这个声明中，杜鲁门表示美国将积极扶植菲律宾反动统治集团，并再次答应季里诺对他组织“太平洋联盟”将“给以一切可行的援助”。季里诺已于十一日自华盛顿飞抵纽约，并将在返菲的活动途中，前往洛杉矶与旧金山，为吁请“美援”奔走。
