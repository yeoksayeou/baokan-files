Title: Beijing-Shanghai Telegraphic Money Transfer

Author:

Date: 1949-08-03
Page: 8

[This City News] The Beiping Postal Savings and Remittance Bureau and its four branch offices in Dongcheng, Xisi, Peking University, and Beijing Normal University have started telegraphic money transfer services with Shanghai on August 1st. For information on the amount of remittance and procedures, please call 5-3695 or visit the Bureau's Business Section at 3 Dongjiaomin Lane and its branch offices for consultation.

        (Postal and Remittance Bureau Communications Group)
