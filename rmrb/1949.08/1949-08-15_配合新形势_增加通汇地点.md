Title: Adapting to the New Situation: Increasing Remittance Locations
Author:
Date: 1949-08-15
Page: 2

    To adapt to the new situation,
    Increase Remittance Locations
    [Further News] As the domestic remittance market has recently become more prosperous, with smooth flow of goods between urban and rural areas, the People's Bank of China, to cooperate with the new economic situation, will soon add remittance services to the following locations: Wenzhou (exchange rate of 40 yuan per 1,000 yuan), Haining (formerly 硖石) (10 yuan), Shengxian (15 yuan), Zhuji (15 yuan), Pinghu (15 yuan), Shangrao (40 yuan), and Heze (40 yuan). The exchange rates for the following locations will also be adjusted: Jinan 10 yuan (down 40 yuan), Jining 40 yuan (down 10 yuan), Weifang 40 yuan (down 10 yuan), Shaoxing 15 yuan (up 5 yuan), Xiaoshan and Yuhang 15 yuan (up 5 yuan), Nanchang 30 yuan (down 10 yuan), and Taiyuan 60 yuan (down 20 yuan).

### 配合新形势  增加通汇地点

1949-08-15
第2版()
专栏：

    配合新形势
    增加通汇地点
    【又讯】由于最近内汇市场趋于繁荣，城乡物资流畅，人民银行为配合新的经济形势，日内将增加温州（汇率每千元四十元），硖石（十元），嵊县（十五元），诸暨（十五元），平湖（十五元），上饶（四十元），菏泽（四十元）等地汇兑业务，并调整下列各地汇率，济南十元（跌四十元），济宁四十元（跌十元），潍坊四十元（跌十元），绍兴十五元（涨五元），萧山、余杭十五元（涨五元），南昌三十元（跌十元），太原六十元（跌二十元）。
