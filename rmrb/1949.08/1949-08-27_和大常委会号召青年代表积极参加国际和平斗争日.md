Title: All-China Federation of Democratic Youth Calls on Youth Representatives to Actively Participate in International Day of Struggle for Peace
Author:
Date: 1949-08-27
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 26th: Budapest Dispatch: The Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace recently sent a letter to the World Federation of Democratic Youth, urging young people from various countries participating in the conference to exert greater efforts in the struggle to win peace and cooperation among all nations and to oppose the instigators of a new war. The letter also calls on participants in the Youth and Student Festival to become the most active organizers of the International Day of Struggle for Peace on October 2nd.

### 和大常委会号召青年代表积极参加国际和平斗争日

1949-08-27
第3版()
专栏：

　　和大常委会号召青年代表积极参加国际和平斗争日
    【新华社北平二十六日电】布达佩斯讯：世界拥护和平大会常设委员会顷致书世界青年与学生联欢大会，力促参加大会的各国青年在争取各国间的和平与合作及反对新战争挑拨者的斗争中，尽更大的力量。该书并号召联欢大会的参加者要成为十月二日国际和平斗争日最积极的组织者。
