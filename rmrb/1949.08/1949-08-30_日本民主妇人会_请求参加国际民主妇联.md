Title: Japan Democratic Women's Association Requests to Join the Women's International Democratic Federation
Author: 
Date: 1949-08-30
Page: 3

　　Japan Democratic Women's Association
  　Requests to Join the Women's International Democratic Federation
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 29th. The Dentsu Agency, Tokyo, reported: The First Congress of the Japan Democratic Women's Association, which includes forty women's groups and has 800,000 members, recently resolved to request to join the Women's International Democratic Federation. The congress issued a draft program urging the struggle for universal peace, national independence, the complete liberation of Japanese women, and the establishment of a people's democratic government.

### 日本民主妇人会　请求参加国际民主妇联

1949-08-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　日本民主妇人会
  　请求参加国际民主妇联
    【新华社北平二十九日电】电通社东京讯：包括四十个妇女团体，拥有八十万会员的日本民主妇人会第一届代表大会顷议决请求参加国际民主妇联。大会发表纲领草案，敦促为普遍和平、国家独立、日本妇女完全解放及建立人民民主政府而斗争。
