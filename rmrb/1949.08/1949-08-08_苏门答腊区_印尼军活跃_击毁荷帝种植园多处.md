Title: Sumatra Area: Indonesian Army Active, Destroys Many Dutch Plantations
Author:
Date: 1949-08-08
Page: 3

    Sumatra Area
    Indonesian Army Active
    Destroys Many Dutch Plantations

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 7th (TASS, The Hague, June 6th): Aneta news agency quoted a dispatch from Batavia stating that the Indonesian army is becoming increasingly active in Sumatra. Recently, they attacked many Dutch plantations in the Medan area, causing great damage. It is reported that Dutch patrols clashed with Indonesian troops in Borneo. The news agency reported on the situation in Java, stating that an official announcement declared that nineteen Dutch soldiers were killed in battle in the past few days.

### 苏门答腊区  印尼军活跃  击毁荷帝种植园多处

1949-08-08
第3版()
专栏：

    苏门答腊区
    印尼军活跃
    击毁荷帝种植园多处
    【新华社北平七日电】塔斯社海牙六日电：安普通讯社引巴城消息称：印尼军在苏门答腊正日渐活跃。最近，他们攻击棉兰地区的许多荷兰种植园，使之受很大的损失。据悉：荷兰巡逻队曾于婆罗洲与印尼军冲突。通讯社报道爪哇情势称：官方公告宣布在过去数日的战斗中，有十九名荷军被击毙。
