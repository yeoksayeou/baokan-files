Title: Literary and Art Congress Northeast Visiting Group Arrives in Shenyang
Author: 
Date: 1949-08-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Beiping Broadcast: Shenyang, 5th—The Northeast Visiting Group of representatives from the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Workers Congress, including Huang Yaomian, Chen Zhongfan, Jiang Tianzuo, Nie Gannu, Zhao Wangyun, Bai Yang, Lao Zhicheng, and others, a total of thirty people, arrived in Shenyang on the afternoon of the 3rd. Liu Zhiming, Sai Ke, Shu Qun, and others from the Northeast Literary and Art Association, along with more than thirty representatives from various cultural and performing arts troupes, welcomed them at the station.

### 文代大会东北参观团到达沈阳

1949-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

    文代大会东北参观团
    到达沈阳
    【北平新华广播电台广播】沈阳五号消息：中华全国文学艺术工作者代表大会代表所组成的东北参观团黄药眠、陈中凡、蒋天佐、聂绀弩、赵望云、白杨、老志诚等一行三十人，三号下午到达沈阳，东北文艺协会刘芝明、塞克、舒群等和各文工团代表三十多人，都到车站欢迎。
