Title: Northeast China Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations Select Representatives to Attend Representative Conference
Author: 
Date: 1949-08-14
Page: 3

    Northeast China Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations
    Select Representatives to Attend Representative Conference
    [Report from this newspaper] Shenyang News: Dalian and Harbin, Jilin and other places have selected representatives to attend the Northeast Representative Conference of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. The Dalian area's city and county Sino-Soviet Friendship Association representatives, numbering thirty, have formally formed a delegation, led by Wang Qun, Vice President of the Dalian Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Kang Minzhuang, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the Dalian District Party Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, as Deputy Leader. It was decided that they would travel to Shenyang on a special train on the 12th, bringing with them photographic materials and other items related to the history of friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people, in preparation for an exhibition at the Representative Conference. The Harbin Sino-Soviet Friendship Association selected Li Jianping and thirteen others as representatives. The Jilin City Sino-Soviet Friendship Association selected Wang Yuan and five others as representatives. In addition, the cities of Siping and Fushun also successively established branches of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association on the 3rd and 4th of this month, passed the organizational charter, and elected executive committee members.

### 东北各地中苏友好协会  纷选代表出席代表大会

1949-08-14
第3版()
专栏：

    东北各地中苏友好协会
    纷选代表出席代表大会
    【本报讯】沈阳消息：旅大地区和哈尔滨吉林等地纷纷选出出席中苏友好协会东北代表大会的代表。旅大地区各市县中苏友好协会代表三十人，已经正式组成代表团，由旅大中苏友好协会副会长王群为团长，中共旅大区党委宣传部副部长康敏庄为副团长，决定十二日乘专车来沈阳，并将带来有关中苏人民友谊史迹的照片材料等，准备在代表大会展览。哈尔滨市中苏友好协会选出李建平等十三人为代表。吉林市中苏友好协会王瑗等五人为代表。另外四平和抚顺等市本月三日和四日也先后建立了中苏友好协会分会，通过了组织章程和选出执行委员等。
