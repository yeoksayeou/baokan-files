    This messy account, after writing for so long, seems tiring to read, but it yields a simple conclusion: the US indeed spent quite a lot of capital in order to invade China. This sum is almost half of the total $20 billion in "aid" given by US imperialism to reactionaries around the world after the war, and almost twice the $3.75 billion "aid" to Britain.
    But, regrettably, US imperialism spent so much capital, investing in China's civil war, but the result was that it fell into a state of being "out of control"! US imperialism and its running dogs did not defeat the Chinese people; instead, they were defeated by the heroic Chinese people. (The End)
