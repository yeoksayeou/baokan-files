Title: People's Radio Tonight's Highlights
Author:
Date: 1949-08-21
Page: 4

　　People's Radio Tonight's Highlights
　　18:20—18:35 Listener Service: (1) Continuing to answer Mr. Wang Wenshu's questions about the differences in customs between the Soviet Union and the Chinese people; (2) Unclaimed telegrams from the Beiping Telecommunications Bureau. 18:35—18:50 Youth Time: Broadcasting two short commentaries from the Beiping Youth Society: (1) From the World Youth Program to the American White Paper; (2) Supporting the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. 18:50—19:10 Entertainment: The Combat Drama Troupe of the First Field Army of the People's Liberation Army sings four new songs: "The Army Marches Forward," "The People's Liberation Army and the People are One Family," "The Great March," and "Singing of Chairman Mao." 19:10—19:30 Workers' Time: Inviting Qiu Fangyu, the trade union representative of the Beiping Municipal People's Representatives Conference, to speak on the topic: "Understanding After Listening to Vice Chairman Zhou's Political Report."

### 人民电台今晚要目

1949-08-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　人民电台今晚要目
    一八、二○—一八、三五听众服务：（一）继续回答王文叔先生关于苏联和中国人民风俗习惯不同的问题，（二）北平电信局电报招领。一八、三五—一八、五○青年时间：播送北平青年社的两篇短评：（一）从世界青年的节目说到美国白皮书，（二）拥护中苏友好协会。一八、五○—一九、一○文娱活动：人民解放军第一野战军战斗剧社演唱“军队向前进”“解放军老百姓是一家”“大进军”“歌唱毛主席”四支新歌。一九、一○—一九、三○职工时间：请北平市各界代表会议工会代表仇方域讲话，题目是：“听了周副主席的政治报告以后的认识”。
