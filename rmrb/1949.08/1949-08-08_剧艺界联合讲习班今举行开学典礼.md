Title: Joint Training Class for Performing Artists Holds Opening Ceremony
Author: Bai Sheng
Date: 1949-08-08
Page: 5
    [This newspaper report]

    Joint Training Class for Performing Artists Holds Opening Ceremony
    [Our Newspaper] The joint training class for the old opera and Quyi [Chinese ballad singing] circles of Beiping Municipality, sponsored by the Old Opera Department of the North China Cultural Committee and the Old Opera Section of the Beiping Municipal Cultural Committee, has been formally established. The opening ceremony is scheduled to be held at the Democratic Theater this morning (8th) at 10:00 AM. Responsible comrades from the North China Cultural Committee and the Propaganda Department of the Beiping Municipal Party Committee will attend and deliver speeches. Four hundred old artists have voluntarily signed up to participate in this training class. The training period is set for two months, starting from today. Classes will be held every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday from 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM at the Democratic Theater. The course subjects include "New Outlook on Life" and special lectures, group discussions, etc.
          (Bai Sheng)

### 剧艺界联合讲习班今举行开学典礼
柏生
1949-08-08
第5版()
专栏：

    剧艺界联合讲习班今举行开学典礼
    【本报讯】由华北文委会旧剧处、北平市文委会旧剧科主办的北平市旧剧界、曲艺界联合讲习班，已正式成立，定于今（八）日上午十时在民主戏院举行开学典礼。有华北文委会与北平市委宣传部负责同志出席讲话。自动报名参加此次讲习班的旧艺人有四百名，讲习期间定为两个月，自即日起，每星期一、三、五上午十时至十二时在民主戏院上课，课目有“新人生观”及专题讲演，小组讨论等。
          （柏生）
