Title: Northeast China-Soviet Friendship Association Holds First Congress; Launches Signature Campaign to Thank Stalin
Author:
Date: 1949-08-15
Page: 1

    Northeast China-Soviet Friendship Association
    Holds First Congress
    Launches Signature Campaign to Thank Stalin
    [Shenyang People's Broadcasting Station, Fourteenth Broadcast] The first congress of the Northeast China-Soviet Friendship Association solemnly opened in Shenyang today. Eleven units participated in the congress, with 159 representatives, representing 430,000 members. The congress first approved Comrade Stalin, Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, Ms. Soong Ching Ling, and General Lin Biao as honorary presidium members of the congress, and Gao Gang, Li Fuchun, Lin Feng, Gao Chongmin, Wu Xiuquan, and 26 others as members of the presidium of the congress. The congress presented flowers to the presidium and paid tribute to the heroic Red Army who sacrificed themselves for the liberation of the Northeast, as well as the martyrs and people who sacrificed themselves in the War of Resistance Against Japan. First, Comrade Gao Gang, Secretary of the Northeast Bureau, reported, pointing out the great significance of holding the congress, and listing the profound friendship and assistance of the Soviet Union to the Chinese people from the October Revolution to the present. Now the people of the Northeast have signed a one-year trade agreement with the Soviet Union, which is the beginning of a new stage in the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples. Then, Chairman Lin Feng of the Northeast Administrative Committee and representatives of workers, peasants, and business circles spoke respectively. After that, the congress listened to the work reports of the China-Soviet Friendship Associations in the Lushun-Dalian area and Harbin. The congress is expected to last for four days and will officially establish the Northeast General Branch of the China-Soviet Friendship Association.
    [Another News] In order to commemorate the fourth anniversary of "August 15th" and celebrate the signing of the trade agreement between the Northeast and the Soviet Union, and the establishment of the Northeast China-Soviet Friendship Association, the Northeast Daily and the preparatory committee of the Northeast China-Soviet Friendship Association jointly launched a signature campaign in factories, institutions, and schools in Shenyang to thank Marshal Stalin, the Soviet Red Army, Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and the People's Liberation Army, from the 10th to the 21st of this month. As of the 13th, more than 8,000 signed lists from employees, students, and others in Shenyang have been received, and the signature campaign is still ongoing.

### 东北中苏友好协会  首次代表大会开幕  发起签名运动向斯大林致谢

1949-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    东北中苏友好协会
    首次代表大会开幕
    发起签名运动向斯大林致谢
    【沈阳人民广播电台十四日广播】东北中苏友好协会第一次代表大会，今天在沈阳隆重开幕。参加大会的有十一个单位，代表一百五十九名，代表了四十三万会员。大会首先通过斯大林大元帅、毛主席、朱总司令、宋庆龄女士、林彪将军为大会名誉主席团，高岗、李富春、林枫、高崇民、伍修权等三十一名为大会主席团。大会向主席团献花，向为解放东北而牺牲的英勇红军，和抗日战争中牺牲烈士和人民致哀后，首先由东北局书记高岗同志报告，他指出召开代表大会的重大意义，并列举了苏联十月革命直到现在对中国人民的深厚友谊和援助，现在东北人民又和苏联订定了一年贸易协定，这是中苏两国人民友谊进入新阶段的开始。接着由东北行政委员会林枫主席和工人、农民、商业界代表分别讲话以后，大会听取了旅大地区和哈尔滨市中苏友好协会的工作报告。大会预计进行四天，将正式成立中苏友好协会东北总分会。
    【又讯】为了纪念“八一五”四周年，并庆祝东北和苏联贸易协定的签订，东北中苏友好协会的成立，东北日报和东北中苏友好协会筹备会联合在沈阳进行各工厂、机关、学校中发起向斯大林大元帅、苏联红军、毛主席、朱总司令和人民解放军致谢的签名运动，从本月十日开始到廿一日。截止到十三日为止，已经收到沈阳市职工学生等约计八千多名的签名单，现在签运名动还在继续进行中。
