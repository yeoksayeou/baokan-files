Title: Nanchang Garrison Shoots Down Bandit Plane
Author:
Date: 1949-08-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, 5th. The Nanchang garrison shot down an enemy plane that attacked the city on the morning of the 1st. At 10 a.m. that morning, the enemy plane flew over the city and strafed the citizens. A certain unit of our garrison immediately retaliated fiercely, and the bandit plane was unable to escape and was shot down in a certain place in the suburbs of Nanchang. The fuselage was destroyed by fire, and the three pilots were killed.

### 南昌驻军击落匪机一架

1949-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

    南昌驻军击落匪机一架
    【新华社南昌五日电】南昌驻军一日晨击落来袭的敌机一架。是日上午十时，该敌机飞临本市上空，扫射市民。我驻军某部当即予以猛烈还击，匪机不及逃逸，被击落于南昌市郊某地，机身焚毁，驾驶员三名毙命。
