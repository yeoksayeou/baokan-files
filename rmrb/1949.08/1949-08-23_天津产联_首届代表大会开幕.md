Title: Tianjin Production Union Opens First Congress
Author:
Date: 1949-08-23
Page: 2

　　Tianjin Production Union
  　First Congress Opens
    [Tianjin, 22nd dispatch] The first member representative congress of the Tianjin Municipal Production Union opened on the 21st. Attending were 295 representatives from 73 member units in six industries, including clothing, tobacco, flour, and printing.

### 天津产联　首届代表大会开幕

1949-08-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　天津产联
  　首届代表大会开幕
    【天津二十二日消息】天津市产联工会第一届会员代表大会，在二十一号开幕。出席的有被服、烟草、面粉、印刷等六个行业七十三个单位会员代表二百九十五人。
