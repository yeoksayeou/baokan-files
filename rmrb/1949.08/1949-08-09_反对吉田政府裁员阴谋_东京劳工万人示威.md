Title: Ten Thousand Tokyo Workers Demonstrate Against Yoshida Government's Layoff Conspiracy
Author:
Date: 1949-08-09
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 8th: According to a United Press dispatch from Tokyo: Ten thousand workers in Tokyo held a demonstration rally at the Imperial Palace Plaza on the 6th, protesting the government's large-scale layoff plan, and calling for the overthrow of the reactionary Yoshida government and the establishment of a people's government to improve the people's living standards. Tochiashi Kazuyoshi, chairman of the All-Japan Postal Workers' Union and Communist member of the Diet, in a speech to the assembly, denounced the Yoshida government as the public enemy of the Japanese working people, stating that this government is using large-scale layoff plans to threaten and suppress workers. Kim Chao (transliteration), a representative of the Federation of Korean Residents in Japan, also addressed the assembly, condemning the attempt by Syngman Rhee and Chiang Kai-shek to form a so-called Pacific Alliance. He said: Reactionaries like Syngman Rhee and Chiang Kai-shek must be driven out of the Asian region.

### 反对吉田政府裁员阴谋  东京劳工万人示威

1949-08-09
第3版()
专栏：

    反对吉田政府裁员阴谋
    东京劳工万人示威
    【新华社北平八日电】据合众社东京讯：东京劳工一万人，于六日在皇宫广场举行示威大会，抗议政府大批裁员计划，并号召打倒反动的吉田政府，建立人民政府，以改善人民的生活。全日递信工人工会主席、共产党众议员土桥一吉在向大会演说中，痛斥吉田政府为日本劳动人民的公敌，这个政府正利用大批裁员计划，威胁与镇压工人。旅日朝鲜侨民联合会代表金朝（译音）也向大会发表了演说，谴责李承晚与蒋介石筹组所谓太平洋联盟的企图。他说：象李承晚、蒋介石这样的反动派，必须逐出亚洲地区。
