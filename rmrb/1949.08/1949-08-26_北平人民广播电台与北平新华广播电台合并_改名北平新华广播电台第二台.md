Title: Merger of Beiping People's Broadcasting Station and Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station; Renamed Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Second Station
Author:
Date: 1949-08-26
Page: 1

[Beiping News] The Beiping People's Broadcasting Station will merge with the Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station starting September 1st and will be renamed Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Second Station. The original Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station will be the First Station. The Second Station's wavelengths are 353 meters, 850 kilocycles; 222 meters, 1350 kilocycles. The opening and closing music for the morning programs will be "The East is Red," and the opening and closing music for the noon and evening programs will be "I Love New China." The full-day program schedule is as follows:

Morning:
	7:00-7:15 Opening music, announcement of the morning program.
	7:15-7:30 Western music.
	7:30-7:45 Joint broadcast of the First Station's first Mandarin news bulletin.
	7:45-8:00 First local news bulletin.
	8:00-8:15 Time check, Peking Opera.
	8:15-8:30 Market report.
	8:30-8:45 Preview of the evening program, Cantonese opera, closing music.
	8:30-9:00 Joint broadcast of the First Station's arts and literature program (Sundays and holidays only, preview of the evening program after the arts and literature program).

Noon:
	12:00-12:15 Opening music, announcement of the noon program.
	12:15-12:30 Peking Opera.
	12:30-12:45 Joint broadcast of the First Station's second Mandarin news bulletin.
	12:45-13:00 Cantonese opera.
	13:00-13:30 Time check, storytelling, local variety music.
	13:30-13:45 Preview of the evening program, closing music.

Evening:
	17:00-17:15 Opening music, announcement of the evening program.
	17:15-17:30 Women's hour (Tuesdays, Saturdays), Children's hour (Mondays, Wednesdays, Thursdays, Fridays, Sundays).
	17:30-17:45 Peking Opera.
	17:45-18:00 Workers' hour (Mondays, Wednesdays, Thursdays, Saturdays), Youth hour (Tuesdays, Fridays, Sundays).
	18:00-18:15 Time check, music program (Mondays, Wednesdays, Fridays, Sundays). New song practice (Tuesdays, Thursdays, Saturdays).
	18:15-18:30 Joint broadcast of the First Station's worker (Tuesdays, Fridays, Sundays), youth (Wednesdays, Saturdays), women (Mondays, Thursdays) rotating programs.
	18:30-19:00 Joint broadcast of the First Station's first arts and literature program.
	19:00-19:15 Joint broadcast of the First Station's lectures on social sciences or natural science knowledge.
	19:15-19:30 Local variety music.
	19:30-19:45 Second local news bulletin.
	19:45-20:00 Western music.
	20:00-20:15 Time check, commentary, Voice of the Municipality.
	20:15-20:30 Listener service (Tuesdays, Saturdays), Peking Opera (Mondays, Wednesdays, Thursdays, Fridays, Sundays).
	20:30-21:30 Joint broadcast of the First Station's third Mandarin news bulletin, international current events, commentary or comprehensive reports, speeches or dispatches.
	21:30-21:45 Cantonese opera.
	21:45-22:00 Preview of tomorrow's program, closing music.

### 北平人民广播电台与北平新华广播电台合并  改名北平新华广播电台第二台

1949-08-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平人民广播电台与北平新华广播电台合并
    改名北平新华广播电台第二台
    【本市讯】北平人民广播电台，自九月一日起，与北平新华广播电台合并；改名为北平新华广播电台第二台，原北平新华广播电台为第一台。第二台波长３５３公尺，８５０千周；２２２公尺，１３５０千周。早晨节目的开始曲及终了曲为“东方红”，中午和晚间节目的开始曲及终了曲为“我爱新中国”。全天节目如下：
    早晨：七、○○—七、一五开始曲，报告早晨节目。七、一五—七、三○西乐。七、三○—七、四五联播第一台第一次普通话新闻。七、四五—八、○○第一次本市新闻。八、○○—八、一五对时，平剧。八、一五—八、三○报告行情。八、三○—八、四五预告晚间节目，粤曲，终了曲。八、三○—九、○○联播第一台文艺节目（只限星期日及假日，文艺节目以后预告晚间节目）。
    中午：一二、○○—一二、一五开始曲，报告中午节目。一二、一五—一二、三○平剧。一二、三○—一二、四五联播第一台第二次普通话新闻。一二、四五—一三、○○粤曲。一三、○○—一三、三○对时，故事讲述，地方杂曲。一三、三○—一三、四五预告晚间节目，终了曲。
    晚间：一七、○○—一七、一五开始曲，报告晚间节目。一七、一五—一七、三○妇女时间（星期二、六），儿童时间（星期一、三、四、五、日）。一七、三○—一七、四五平剧。一七、四五—一八、○○职工时间（星期一、三、四、六），青年时间（星期二、五、日）。一八、○○—一八、一五对时，音乐节目（星期一、三、五、日）。新歌练习（星期二、四、六）。一八、一五—一八、三○联播第一台职工（星期二、五、日），青年（星期三、六），妇女（星期一、四）轮回节目。一八、三○—一九、○○联播第一台第一次文艺节目。一九、○○—一九、一五联播第一台社会科学或自然科学常识讲座。一九、一五—一九、三○地方杂曲。一九、三○—一九、四五第二次本市新闻。一九、四五—二○、○○西乐。二○、○○—二○、一五对时，言论，市政之声。二○、一五—二○、三○听众服务（星期二、六），平剧（星期一、三、四、五、日）。二○、三○—二一、三○联播第一台第三次普通话新闻，国际时事，评论或综合报导，演讲或通讯。二一、三○—二一、四五粤曲。二一、四五—二二、○○预告明天节目，终了曲。
