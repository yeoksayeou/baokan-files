Title: US Unemployment Figures: 5.9 Million

Author:
Date: 1949-08-11
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 10th -- According to a United Press report: The United Electrical, Radio and Machine Workers of America (UE), affiliated with the Congress of Industrial Organizations (產联), issued a statement on the 7th condemning the US Census Bureau for undercounting the number of unemployed in the United States in July by more than 1.8 million. The union stated that the actual number of unemployed in the United States has reached 5.9 million, while the bureau reported only 4,095,000 unemployed.

### 美失业人数  五百九十万

1949-08-11
第3版()
专栏：

    美失业人数
    五百九十万
    【新华社北平十日电】据合众社消息：属于产联的电气工人工会联合会于七日发表声明，谴责美国人口统计局把美国七月份失业人数少算了一百八十万人以上。该工会称：美国实际失业人数已达五百九十万人，而该局报道的失业人数，只是四百零九万五千人。
