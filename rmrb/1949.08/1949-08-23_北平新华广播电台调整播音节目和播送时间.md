Title: Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Adjusts Broadcast Programs and Broadcast Times
Author:
Date: 1949-08-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, August 22nd: Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station has decided to adjust its broadcast programs and broadcast times from September 1st due to the increase in lectures and the requests from listeners in various regions to adjust the broadcast times for Japanese news, South China local language news, and arts and entertainment programs, as follows:
　　First Broadcast Time (5:00-5:30)
　　　　5:00-5:15　　Opening Music: Japanese Music
　　　　5:15-5:30　　Japanese News
　　Second Broadcast Time (7:00-8:30)
　　　　7:00-7:15　　Opening Music
　　　　7:15-7:30　　Preview of the Day's Programs, Time Signal
　　　　7:30-7:45　　First Mandarin News
　　　　7:45-8:00　　Xiamen (Amoy) Dialect News
　　　　8:00-8:15　　Chaozhou Dialect News
　　　　8:15-8:30　　Guangzhou (Cantonese) Dialect News
　　　　8:30-9:00　　Arts and Entertainment Program (Sundays and Holidays Only)
　　Third Broadcast Time (12:00-13:00)
　　　　12:00-12:15　　Opening Music: Music
　　　　12:15-12:30　　Preview of Noon and Evening Programs, Time Signal
　　　　12:30-12:45　　Second Mandarin News
　　　　12:45-13:00　　Music
　　Fourth Broadcast Time (13:30-16:30)
　　　　13:30-13:45　　Opening Music: Report on the Summary of News Records for the Field Army
　　　　13:45-16:30　　News Records for the Field Army
　　Fifth Broadcast Time (17:30-24:00)
　　　　17:30-17:45　　Opening Music: Japanese Music
　　　　17:45-18:00　　Japanese News
　　　　18:00-18:15　　Preview of the Evening Programs, Time Signal
　　　　18:15-18:30　　Rotating Programs for Workers, Youth, and Women
　　　　18:30-19:00　　First Arts and Entertainment Program
　　　　19:00-19:15　　Lectures on Common Knowledge of Social Science or Natural Science
　　　　19:15-19:30　　Music (or other non-fixed programs)
　　　　19:30-19:45　　Xiamen (Amoy) Dialect News
　　　　19:45-20:00　　Chaozhou Dialect News
　　　　20:00-20:15　　Guangzhou (Cantonese) Dialect News
　　　　20:15-20:30　　Music
　　　　20:30-20:50　　Third Mandarin News
　　　　20:50-21:00　　International Current Events
　　　　21:00-21:15　　Commentary or Comprehensive Report
　　　　21:15-21:30　　Speeches or Correspondences
　　　　21:30-22:00　　English News
　　　　22:00-22:30　　Second Arts and Entertainment Program
　　　　22:30-22:50　　Fourth Mandarin News
　　　　22:50-23:00　　Preview of Tomorrow's Programs, Time Signal, Ending Music
　　　　23:00-24:00　　Recorded News
　　(The station broadcasts five times a day, for a total of twelve hours and thirty minutes, and thirteen hours on Sundays and holidays. The opening music for the first, second, third, and fifth broadcast times is the "People's Liberation Army March," and the opening music for the fourth broadcast time is the "Three Main Rules of Discipline and Eight Points for Attention Song." The ending music for the first, second, third, and fourth broadcast times is "March Forward to Victory." The closing music for the entire day's broadcast is the ending music "Carry the Revolution Through to the End.")
