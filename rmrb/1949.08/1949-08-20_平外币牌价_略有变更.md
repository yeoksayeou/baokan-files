Title: Flat Foreign Exchange Rates Slightly Changed
Author:
Date: 1949-08-20
Page: 4

　　Economic News

　　Flat Foreign Exchange Rates
  　Slightly Changed
    [Report from this newspaper] The People's Bank of Beiping Branch's foreign exchange rates will be revised starting today to 2,500 yuan per US dollar, a decrease of 200 yuan from the original rate, while US drafts will remain at 2,700 yuan. The pound sterling remains at 7,560 yuan per pound, and the Hong Kong dollar at 400 yuan per dollar.

### 平外币牌价　略有变更

1949-08-20
第4版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　平外币牌价
  　略有变更
    【本报讯】人民银行北平分行外币牌价今起改订为美钞一兑二千五百元，比原牌价下落二百元，美汇则仍为二千七百元。英镑仍为一兑七千五百六十元，港币一兑四百元。
