Title: First Meeting of the Northeast People's Government Committee: Comrade Gao Gang Elected Chairman of the Government, Li Fuchun, Lin Feng, and Gao Chongmin as Vice Chairmen
Author:
Date: 1949-08-30
Page: 2

　　First Meeting of the Northeast People's Government Committee
  　Comrade Gao Gang Elected Chairman of the Government
    Li Fuchun, Lin Feng, and Gao Chongmin as Vice Chairmen
    Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, August 29th: The Northeast People's Government Committee held its first plenary meeting on the 27th. All government committee members unanimously elected Gao Gang as Chairman of the Northeast People's Government, and Li Fuchun, Lin Feng, and Gao Chongmin as Vice Chairmen. The meeting also approved the following key personnel for various committees, departments, institutes, banks, and bureaus: Director of the General Office: Lin Feng (concurrent), Deputy Director: Li Youwen (concurrent). Secretary-General: Li Youwen, Deputy Secretary-General: Wang Guangwei. Director of the Finance and Economy Committee: Gao Gang (concurrent), Deputy Directors: Li Fuchun, Lin Feng (concurrent), Ye Jizhuang, Committee Members: Wang Heshou, Gu Zhuoxin, Du Zheheng, Zhu Lizhi, Wang Xingrang, Yu Guangsheng, Zhang Weizhen, Lü Dong, Wei Zhenwu, Yi Xiuxiang, Liu Juying, Zhang Huadong, Ni Wei, Tang Yunchao, An Zhiwen, Wang Qizhi. Director of the Supervisory Committee: Lin Feng (concurrent), Committee Members: Zhang Xiushan, Zhou Huan, Wang Jinxiang, Li Yimin. (Two positions vacant, to be determined later.) Minister of Civil Affairs: Wang Yifu, Minister of Finance: Gu Zhuoxin, Deputy Minister: Ni Wei, Minister of Heavy Industry: Wang Heshou, Deputy Ministers: Lü Dong, An Zhiwen, Minister of Light Industry: Undetermined, Minister of Agriculture and Forestry: Du Zheheng, Deputy Minister: Wei Zhenwu, Minister of Commerce: Wang Xingrang, Deputy Minister: Yi Xiuxiang, Minister of Foreign Trade: Ye Jizhuang (concurrent), Deputy Minister: Zhang Huadong, Minister of Culture and Education: Che Xiangchen, Deputy Ministers: Dong Chuncai, Zou Lufeng, Minister of Public Security: Wang Jinxiang, Deputy Minister: Chen Long, Minister of Justice and concurrently President of the Northeast People's Court: Gao Chongmin (concurrent), Minister of Health: Wang Bin, Deputy Ministers: Dai Zhenghua, Bai Xiqing, General Manager of the Northeast Bank: Zhu Lizhi, Deputy General Managers: Wang Qizhi, Shen Yujie, Director of the Postal and Telecommunications Administration: Chen Xianzhou, Director of the Labor Bureau: Tang Yunchao, Director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau: Lu Xi.
    In order to better implement and carry out the decisions of the Northeast People's Representative Conference, the plenary meeting of government committee members is prepared to continue discussing economic construction and conservation issues, the People's Representative Conference, government organizations at all levels, urban political organizations, and cadres, among other issues.

### 东北人民政府委员会首次会议　高岗同志当选政府主席  李富春林枫高崇民任副主席

1949-08-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　东北人民政府委员会首次会议
  　高岗同志当选政府主席
    李富春林枫高崇民任副主席
    【新华社沈阳二十九日电】东北人民政府委员会于二十七日举行第一次全体会议。全体政府委员一致推选高岗为东北人民政府主席，李富春、林枫、高崇民为副主席。会议并通过各会、部、院、行、局主要人选如下：办公厅主任林枫（兼），副主任栗又文（兼）。秘书长栗又文，副秘书长王光伟。财政经济委员会主任高岗（兼），副主任李富春、林枫（兼）、叶季壮，委员王鹤寿、顾卓新、杜者蘅、朱理治、王兴让、余光生、张维桢、吕东、魏震五、易秀湘、刘居英、张化东、倪伟、唐韵超、安志文、王企之。监察委员会主任林枫（兼），委员张秀山、周桓、汪金祥、李逸民。（空两个名额，以后再定。）民政部长王一夫，财政部长顾卓新，副部长倪伟，重工业部长王鹤寿，副部长吕东、安志文，轻工业部长未定，农林部长杜者蘅，副部长魏震五，商业部长王兴让，副部长易秀湘，对外贸易部长叶季壮（兼），副部长张化东，文化教育部长车向忱，副部长董纯才、邹鲁风，公安部长汪金祥，副部长陈龙，司法部长兼东北人民法院院长高崇民）（兼），卫生部长王斌，副部长戴正华、白希清，东北银行总经理朱理治，副经理王企之、申玉洁，邮电管理总局局长陈先舟，劳动总局长唐韵超，外事局长陆曦。
    为了更好地执行与贯彻东北人民代表会议决定，全体政府委员会议准备继续讨论经济建设与节约问题、人民代表会议、各级政府机构、城市政权组织及干部等问题。
