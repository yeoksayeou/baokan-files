Title: Four Hundred Shanghai Primary and Secondary School Teachers Join the League
Author:
Date: 1949-08-24
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, August 23rd. Four hundred and forty-eight primary and secondary school teachers in Shanghai have joined the New Democratic Youth League, and a grand admission ceremony was held on the 20th. More than 1,400 primary and secondary school teachers in Shanghai and representatives of various mass organizations attended. Dai Bai＠, representative of the Shanghai Working Committee of the Youth League, said in his speech that he hoped that the teachers who joined the League would work hard to carry out revolutionary education for children and young people, cultivate new intellectuals and new citizens, and become model workers in New Democratic education.

### 上海中小学教员四百人入团

1949-08-24
第2版()
专栏：

　　上海中小学教员四百人入团
    【新华社上海二十三日电】上海中小学教员四百四十八人加入新民主主义青年团，并在二十日举行隆重入团典礼。上海中小学教员一千四百多人和各群众团体代表都来参加。青年团上海工作委员会代表戴白＠在讲话中希望参加团的教员们，努力对儿童和青年进行革命教育，培养新的知识分子和新的公民，成为新民主主义教育的模范工作者。
