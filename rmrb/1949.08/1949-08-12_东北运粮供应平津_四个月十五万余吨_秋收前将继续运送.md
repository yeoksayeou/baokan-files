Title: Northeast Transports Grain to Supply Beijing and Tianjin: Over 150,000 Tons in Four Months; Shipments to Continue Before Autumn Harvest
Author: 
Date: 1949-08-12
Page: 1

    Northeast Transports Grain to Supply Beijing and Tianjin
    Over 150,000 Tons in Four Months
    Shipments to Continue Before Autumn Harvest
    [This newspaper's dispatch] Shenyang News: Since the liberation of Beijing and Tianjin, the units under the Commercial Department of the Northeast Administrative Committee have continuously transported grain into the region, exchanging it for some of the cotton cloth and other materials needed by the Northeast, actively supporting the war effort and coordinating economic construction in both regions. According to incomplete statistics, from April to July, the Northeast has transported 157,860 tons of grain to Beijing, Tianjin, Tangshan, and other places. If vehicles permit, it is planned to continue shipping to the interior before the autumn harvest.

### 东北运粮供应平津  四个月十五万余吨  秋收前将继续运送

1949-08-12
第1版()
专栏：

    东北运粮供应平津
    四个月十五万余吨
    秋收前将继续运送
    【本报讯】沈阳消息：平津解放以后，东北行政委员会商业部所属各单位，不断运送粮食进关，换回一部东北所需要的纱布等物资，积极支援战争和配合两地经济建设。据不完全统计从四月到七月四个月间，东北已向平津唐山等地运送粮食十五万七千八百六十吨，如车辆许可，在秋收前还准备陆续向关内运送。
