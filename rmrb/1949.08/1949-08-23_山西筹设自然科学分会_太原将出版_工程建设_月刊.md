Title: Shanxi Prepares to Establish Natural Science Branch; Taiyuan to Publish "Engineering Construction" Monthly
Author:
Date: 1949-08-23
Page: 2

Shanxi Prepares to Establish Natural Science Branch
Taiyuan to Publish "Engineering Construction" Monthly

　　[Taiyuan Dispatch] Natural science workers in Taiyuan City held a forum on the 18th to discuss the establishment of the Shanxi Branch of the Preparatory Committee for the First National Congress of Natural Science Workers of China. Seventy-six natural science workers from various agencies, factories, and schools attended. Chairman Wei Bin read the declaration of the Scientific Workers Association and Commander-in-Chief Zhu's speech at the preparatory meeting. Lai Jifa, director of the Taiyuan Industrial Management Office, spoke, pointing out that Shanxi is a major industrial base in North China with abundant resources and a great future for industrial development, providing a heroic arena for natural science workers. The meeting tentatively confirmed 52 members of the Taiyuan Natural Science Workers Preparatory Committee and discussed the branch's charter, the method of selecting preparatory committee members, and their tasks.
    [Taiyuan Dispatch] In order to strengthen technical research, exchange engineering experience, and improve technical standards, the Taiyuan Industrial Management Office has decided to publish the "Engineering Construction" monthly magazine through its Engineering Department. The content will include technical research, inventions, improvements, and introductions to industrial overviews from other regions. Manuscript collection has already begun, and the inaugural issue is scheduled for publication in October.

### 山西筹设自然科学分会　太原将出版“工程建设”月刊

1949-08-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　山西筹设自然科学分会
  　太原将出版“工程建设”月刊
    【太原电】太原市自然科学工作者于十八日举行座谈会，商议成立中华全国第一次自然科学工作者代表大会筹委会山西分会，到有各机关、工厂、学校自然科学工作者七十六人，主席韦彬宣读科学工作者协会的宣言及朱总司令在筹备会上的讲话后，由太原工业管理处赖际发处长讲话，他指出山西系华北主要工业基地，资源丰富，工业发展前途极大，为自然科学工作者英雄用武之地。会议中暂确定了太原市自然科学工作者筹委会委员五十二人，并讨论了分会简章及筹委会委员产生办法及任务。
    【太原电】太原市工业管理处为加强技术研究，交换工程经验，提高技术水平，特决定由该处工程室出版“工程建设”月刊，内容为技术的研究、发明、改进，及介绍其它各地工业概况。现已开始征集稿件，创刊号定于十月出版。
