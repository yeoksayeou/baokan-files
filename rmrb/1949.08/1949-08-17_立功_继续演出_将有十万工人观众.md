Title: "Establishing Merit" Continues Performances; Will Have 100,000 Worker Audience
Author: Lida
Date: 1949-08-17
Page: 8

　　"Establishing Merit" Continues Performances; Will Have 100,000 Worker Audience
　　Lida
　　[This newspaper's report] The Northeast Cultural and Educational Troupe came to Beiping to participate in the Literary and Artistic Congress, performing the worker opera "Establishing Merit" and the rural drama "Conspiracy," receiving praise from all sectors. In particular, the play "Establishing Merit" won enthusiastic welcome from workers throughout the city. The Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, based on worker requests, has specially requested the troupe to remain in Beiping. Starting from the 14th of this month, they will perform the play "Establishing Merit" at the Zhongshan Park Music Hall to entertain workers. It is estimated that 100,000 workers will come to see this play, including industrial, handicraft, and transport workers.
　　　　　　(Lida)

### “立功”继续演出　将有十万工人观众
力达
1949-08-17
第8版()
专栏：

　　“立功”继续演出　将有十万工人观众
    力达
    【本报讯】东北文教队来平参加文代会，演出工人歌剧“立功”及农村剧“阴谋”，获得各界赞誉。尤以“立功”一剧博得全市工人热烈欢迎。市总工会根据工人要求，特请该队留平，自本月十四日起在中山公园音乐堂演出“立功”剧，招待工人。据估计此次来观此剧的工人将有十万，包括产业、手工业、搬运等工人。
    　　　　　　（力达）
