Title: National Committees Convene Successively: Electing Chairmen, Vice-Chairmen, and Standing Committee Members
Author:
Date: 1949-08-02
Page: 2

    [Ping Shi News] The national committees of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the Chinese Dramatists Association, the China Film Association, the China Artists Association, the Chinese Musicians Association, and the Chinese Dancers Association have successively convened meetings to discuss work and elect chairmen, vice-chairmen, and standing committees. In addition to the list of the chairman, vice-chairman, and standing committee of the Dramatists Association, which was published on July 28, the following are the results: Chairman of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles: Mao Dun; Vice-Chairmen: Ding Ling, Ke Zhongping; Standing Committee Members: Mao Dun, Zheng Zhenduo, Ding Ling, Ba Jin, Ai Qing, Sha Kefu, Cao Jinghua, Zhao Shuli, Xiao San, Zhou Yang, Xue Feng, Ke Zhongping, Hu Feng, He Qifang, Feng Naichao, Feng Zhi, Ouyang Shan, Liu Zhiming, Yu Pingbo, Huang Yaomian, Zhong Jingwen; Chairman of the China Film Association: Yang Hansheng; Vice-Chairmen: Yuan Muzhi; Standing Committee Members: Yang Hansheng, Yuan Muzhi, Cai Chusheng, Shi Dongshan, Chen Boer, Huang Zuolin, Chen Baichen, Wu Yinxian, Su Yi; Chairman of the China Artists Association: Xu Beihong; Vice-Chairmen: Jiang Feng, Ye Qianyu; Standing Committee Members: Xu Beihong, Jiang Feng, Ye Qianyu, Cai Ruohong, Liu Kaiqu, Wu Zuoren, Li Hua, Gu Yuan, Wang Chaowen, Ni Yide, Li Qun, Zhu Dan, Yefu; Chairman of the Chinese Musicians Association: Lü Ji; Vice-Chairmen: Ma Sicong, He Luding; Standing Committee Members: Lü Ji, Ma Sicong, He Luding, Li Ling, Xiang Yu, Jiang Dingxian, He Shide, An Bo, Meng Bo, Zhang Fei, Shen Zhibai, Miao Tianrui, Lao Zhicheng; Chairman of the Chinese Dancers Association: Dai Ailian; Vice-Chairmen: Wu Xiaobang; Standing Committee Members: Dai Ailian, Wu Xiaobang, Hu Guogang, Li Bozhao, Liang Lun, Zhao Yunge, Jia Zuoguang, Yue Ke, Xu Husha, Chen Jinqing, Jin Renhai.

### 全国委员会先后召开  选出正副主席和常委

1949-08-02
第2版()
专栏：

    全国委员会先后召开
    选出正副主席和常委
    【平市讯】全国文协、剧协、影协、美协、音协、舞协的全国委员会已先后召集会议，讨论工作，并选出了主席、副主席和常务委员会。除剧协主席、副主席和常务委员会名单已于七月二十八日见报外，计文协主席为茅盾、副主席为丁玲、柯仲平、常务委员为茅盾、郑振铎、丁玲、巴金、艾青、沙可夫、曹靖华、赵树理、萧三、周扬、雪峰、柯仲平、胡风、何其芳、冯乃超、冯至、欧阳山、刘芝明、俞平伯、黄药眠、钟敬文；影协主席为阳翰笙、副主席为袁牧之、常务委员为阳翰笙、袁牧之、蔡楚生、史东山、陈波儿、黄佐临、陈白尘、吴印咸、苏怡；美协主席为徐悲鸿、副主席为江丰、叶浅予、常务委员为徐悲鸿、江丰、叶浅予、蔡若虹、刘开渠、吴作人、李桦、古元、王朝闻、倪贻德、力群、朱丹、野夫；音协主席为吕骥、副主席为马思聪、贺绿汀、常务委员为吕骥、马思聪、贺绿汀、李凌、向隅、江定仙、何士德、安波、孟波、张非、沈知白、缪天瑞、老志诚；舞协主席为戴爱莲、副主席为吴晓邦、常务委员为戴爱莲、吴晓邦、胡果刚、李伯钊、梁伦、赵郓哥、贾作光、岳克、徐胡沙、陈锦清、金仁海。
