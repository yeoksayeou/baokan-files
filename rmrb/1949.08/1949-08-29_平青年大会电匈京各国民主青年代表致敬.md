Title: Ping Youth Congress Sends Greetings to Democratic Youth Representatives from Hungary, Beijing, and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1949-08-29
Page: 4

　　【This City News】The Beijing Youth Congress yesterday (28th) held a celebration of the Second World Democratic Youth Congress, the World Youth and Student Festival, and the International Youth Day Congress, sending a telegram of greetings to the representatives of various countries at the Second World Democratic Youth Congress and the World Youth and Student Festival. The original telegram is as follows:

　　We, the youth of Beijing, celebrate the festival of democratic youth worldwide with boundless excitement, and extend our highest and sincere respect to you!

　　Under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and the leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao Zedong, the Chinese people, after a long and heroic struggle, have liberated the vast people of their motherland. This victory is inseparable from the victory of democratic forces worldwide. We sincerely thank the Soviet people, the people of the new democratic countries, and the people of all countries for their fraternal friendship towards the Chinese people.

　　Dear friends, today, we, the youth of Beijing, the capital of the Chinese people, are fighting alongside the youth of all China to eliminate the remaining forces of Chiang Kai-shek, thwart the sabotage plots of American imperialism, and build an independent, free, and happy New China.

　　Our struggle is not isolated; our hearts are forever linked with yours. Let us unite around the incomparably powerful world peace fortress of the Soviet Union and strive for lasting world peace, people's democracy, and youth happiness!

### 平青年大会电匈京各国民主青年代表致敬

1949-08-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　平青年大会电匈京各国民主青年代表致敬
    【本市讯】北平市各界青年昨（二十八）日举行的庆祝世界民主青年第二次代表大会、世界青年及学生联欢大会及国际青年节大会电世界民主青年第二次代表大会、世界青年及学生联欢大会的各国代表们致敬，原电如下：
    我们北平市青年以无限的兴奋，庆祝全世界民主青年的节日，同时向你们致崇高、诚挚的敬礼！
    在中国共产党和中国人民领袖毛泽东主席的领导下，中国人民经过了长期英勇艰苦的斗争，已经把自己祖国的广大人民解放了出来。这个胜利和全世界民主力量的胜利是不可分的。我们衷心的感谢苏联人民，感谢各新民主主义国家的人民和世界各国人民对中国人民的兄弟般的友谊。
    亲爱的朋友们，今天，我们中国人民首都——北平的青年们，正和全中国青年一道，为消灭蒋介石的残余力量，击破美国帝国主义的破坏阴谋，建设独立、自由、幸福的新中国而斗争。
    我们的斗争不是孤立的，我们的心永远和你们联在一起。让我们在无比强大的世界和平堡垒苏联的周围团结起来，争取全世界的持久和平、人民民主和青年幸福！
