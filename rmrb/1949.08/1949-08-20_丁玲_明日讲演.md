Title: Ding Ling to Lecture Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1949-08-20
Page: 2

　　Ding Ling
  　Tomorrow's Lecture
    [Ping City News] The Sixth China Youth Lecture Series, sponsored by the China Youth Society, will be held at 2:30 p.m. on the 21st (Sunday) in the auditorium of the Shuai Fu Yuan Art Academy. The female writer Ding Ling will give a lecture on "On the Road of Progress." All are welcome to attend. No admission tickets will be issued; seating is limited to the capacity of the auditorium.

### 丁玲　明日讲演

1949-08-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　丁玲
  　明日讲演
    【平市讯】中国青年社主办的第六次中国青年讲演会，订于二十一日（星期日）午后两点半钟在帅府园艺专大礼堂，请女作家丁玲讲“在前进的路上”。欢迎听讲，不另发入场券，以礼堂座满为限。
