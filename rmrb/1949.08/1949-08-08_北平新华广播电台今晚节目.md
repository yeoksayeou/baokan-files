Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-08-08
Page: 1

    [This newspaper's report] Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station's program for tonight: From 7:50 PM to 8:10 PM, a lecture program featuring international affairs expert Hu Yuzhi, who will speak on "Sino-Soviet Cooperation in Defending Pacific Peace." From 7:30 PM to 7:50 PM, the women's hour will broadcast introductions to women in Asian countries, the first installment being "The Life and Struggle of Vietnamese Women." From 10:20 PM to 10:50 PM, the arts and literature program will commemorate the fourth anniversary of the Soviet Union's declaration of war against Japan, broadcasting Soviet military music.

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-08-08
第1版()
专栏：

    北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚七点五十分到八点十分演讲节目，请国际问题专家胡愈之演讲：“中苏合作保卫太平洋和平”。七点三十分到七点五十分妇女时间，播送亚洲各国妇女介绍，第一篇——越南妇女的生活和斗争。十点二十分到十点五十分文艺节目，纪念苏联对日宣战四周年，播送苏联军乐。
