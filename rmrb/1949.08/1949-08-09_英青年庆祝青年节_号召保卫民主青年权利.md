Title: British Youth Celebrate Youth Day, Call to Defend Democratic Youth Rights
Author:
Date: 1949-08-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 8th: London dispatch: The British World Youth Council, affiliated with the World Federation of Democratic Youth, recently convened a conference in London to celebrate World Youth Day. Approximately 500 people attended the conference. Speakers included Walter Davis, chairman of the British World Youth Day Committee, John Moss of the British Peace Committee, and young worker Brown. Brown was arrested and imprisoned for participating in a May Day demonstration in front of the American Embassy in London to protest the speech of U.S. Congressman—vampire Cannon. The speakers called on British democratic youth to unite around the World Federation of Democratic Youth, to fight for the defense of peace against the aggressive plans of the North Atlantic Treaty, and to fight for the rights of democratic youth.

### 英青年庆祝青年节  号召保卫民主青年权利

1949-08-09
第3版()
专栏：

    英青年庆祝青年节
    号召保卫民主青年权利
    【新华社北平八日电】伦敦讯：世界民主青年联合会所属的英国世界青年理事会，顷在伦敦召开大会，庆祝世界青年节。大会参加者约五百人。在会上发言的，有英国世界青年节委员会主席华尔脱·台维斯，英国和平委员会委员约翰·茅斯，青年工人勃朗。勃朗曾因参加“五一”在伦敦美大使馆前的示威，抗议美国国会议员——吸血鬼康农的演说而被捕下狱。演讲者都号召英国民主青年团结在世界民主青联的周围，为保卫和平反对北大西洋公约的侵略计划而斗争，为民主青年的权利而斗争。
