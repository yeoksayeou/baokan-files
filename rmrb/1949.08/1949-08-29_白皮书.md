Title: White Paper
Author: Gao Shiqi
Date: 1949-08-29
Page: 6

　　White Paper
　　Gao Shiqi
　　American imperialism is just a paper tiger
　　It looks formidable from afar
　　But it can't stand the wind when you get close.
　　× × ×
　　The White Paper is a pretentious show
　　It's its confession
　　It's the heinous crime of its aggression against the Chinese people
　　× × ×
　　Where is the traditional friendship?
　　In the sound of airplanes and cannons
　　Where is democratic individualism?
　　In the stench of dollar bills
　　× × ×
　　Discard illusions, prepare for struggle
　　Strengthen unity, support the front lines
　　Throw the remnants of the American-Chiang clique into the bottom of the Pacific Ocean

　　August 23, 1949
