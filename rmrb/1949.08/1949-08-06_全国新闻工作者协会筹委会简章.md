Title: Regulations of the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Journalists Association
Author:
Date: 1949-08-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 5th: The regulations of the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Journalists Association were principally approved by the plenary meeting of the committee on July 13th, and the text was revised and approved today by the Standing Committee of the committee. The full text of the regulations is as follows:

Article 1: This association is named the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Journalists Association, and its purpose is to prepare for and convene the National Congress of Journalists and form the All-China Journalists Association.

Article 2: The term "journalist" in the preceding article refers to:

    1. Editors, commentators, reporters, data editors, and photojournalists working in newspapers nationwide;
    2. Editors, commentators, reporters, data editors, and photojournalists working in news agencies nationwide;
    3. Editors, commentators, reporters, and broadcasters working in radio stations;
    4. Editors, commentators, reporters, and photojournalists working in current affairs periodicals (every other day, every three days, weekly, bi-weekly, semi-monthly, monthly) and news pictorials;
    5. Supervisors, directors, editors, cutters, photographers, and dubbing staff participating in the production of news films;
    6. Heads and teachers of news education institutions;
    7. Staff members in news agencies of governments at all levels;
    8. Other staff members in the above news agencies who have not participated in other national trade union organizations and have worked continuously for three years or more.
    9. Those who have worked continuously in the above news agencies for three years or more and have been away from journalistic work for less than three years.

Article 3: The following personnel may apply to join this association as members:

    1. Chinese personnel working in foreign news agencies stationed in China;
    2. Foreign employed personnel working in Chinese news agencies;
    3. Chinese personnel working in newspapers or other overseas news agencies run by Chinese people abroad.

Article 4: The tasks of this association are as follows:

    1. To determine the date and location for convening the National Congress of Journalists;
    2. To determine the number of delegates to the National Congress of Journalists and the method of their selection;
    3. To assist and guide the preparatory work in various regions;
    4. To convene the National Congress of Journalists;
    5. To review the qualifications of delegates to the National Congress of Journalists;
    6. To draft the agenda of the congress;
    7. To draft the task report of the All-China Journalists Association;
    8. To draft the constitution of the All-China Journalists Association;
    9. To solicit information and suggestions related to journalistic work from all parties;
    10. To handle other preparatory matters.

Article 5: This association shall establish a Standing Committee to be responsible for handling the daily work of the association and convening plenary meetings of the association, to be organized by nine members mutually elected by all members of the Preparatory Committee.
    The Standing Committee shall have one director and two deputy directors, who shall be mutually elected by all members of the Standing Committee.

Article 6: This association shall establish the following working bodies:

    1. Secretariat;
    2. Drafting Committee for the Task Report of the All-China Journalists Association;
    3. Drafting Committee for the Constitution of the All-China Journalists Association;
    4. Organization Committee.

Article 7: This association may establish preparatory sub-committees in various regions as needed.

Article 8: These regulations shall be implemented after being adopted by the plenary meeting of this association.

### 全国新闻工作者协会筹委会简章

1949-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

    全国新闻工作者协会筹委会简章
    【新华社北平五日电】中华全国新闻工作者协会筹备委员会简章，经七月十三日该会全体会议作原则通过后，已于今日由该会常务委员会修正文字通过。简章全文如下：
    第一条  本会定名为中华全国新闻工作者协会筹备委员会，其目的为筹备并召开全国新闻工作者代表大会，组成中华全国新闻工作者协会。
    第二条  前条所称新闻工作者，系指：
    一、在全国各报社工作之编辑、评论作者、采访记者、资料编辑、摄影记者；
    二、在全国各新闻通讯社工作之编辑、评论作者、采访记者、资料编辑、摄影记者；
    三、在广播电台工作之编辑、评论员、采访记者、广播员；
    四、在时事性期刊（隔日、三日、周刊、旬刊、半月刊、月刊）及新闻画报工作之编辑、评论作者、采访记者、摄影记者；
    五、参加制作新闻影片工作之监制人、导演、编辑、剪接、摄影、配音人员；
    六、新闻教育机构之主持人及教师；
    七、各级政府新闻机构中的工作人员；
    八、以上新闻机构中其他工作人员，未曾参加其他全国性职工团体而连续工作达三年以上者。
    九、在以上新闻机构中曾经连续工作达三年以上，而现在脱离新闻工作未满三年者。
    第三条  左列人员得申请加入本会为会员：
    一、在驻华外国新闻机构中工作之华籍人员；
    二、在中国新闻机构中工作之外籍雇用人员；
    三、在国外的中国人民所办的报社或其他海外新闻机构中工作之华籍人员。
    第四条  本会之任务如左：
    一、决定全国新闻工作者代表大会的召开日期及地点；
    二、决定出席全国新闻工作者代表大会的代表数额及其产生方法；
    三、协助并指导各地筹备工作的进行；
    四、召开全国新闻工作者代表大会；
    五、审查全国新闻工作者代表大会代表的资格；
    六、拟订大会议事日程；
    七、草拟全国新闻工作者协会的任务报告草案；
    八、草拟全国新闻工作者协会章程草案；
    九、征集各方面有关新闻工作的资料和建议；
    十、办理其他筹备事项。
    第五条  本会设常务委员会，负责处理本会日常工作，并召集本会全体会议，由全体筹备委员互推九人组织之。
    常务委员会设主任一人，副主任二人，由全体常务委员互推之。
    第六条  本会设下列各种工作机构：
    一、秘书处；
    二、中华全国新闻工作者协会任务报告草案起草委员会；
    三、中华全国新闻工作者协会章程草案起草委员会；
    四、组织委员会。
    第七条  本会得按实际需要，在各地成立筹备会分会。
    第八条  本简章经本会全体会议通过后实施。
