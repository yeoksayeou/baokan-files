Title: Baoding People's Broadcasting Station to Begin Broadcasting on September 1st
Author:
Date: 1949-08-26
Page: 2

　　Baoding People's Broadcasting Station
  　To Begin Broadcasting on September 1st
    [Baoding News] The Hebei Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party has decided, with the approval of the North China Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, to establish the Baoding People's Broadcasting Station. The station will officially begin broadcasting on September 1st. On the 17th, the Hebei Provincial Committee issued instructions emphasizing that the leading departments at the provincial level should fully utilize the radio station to lead and promote work; at the same time, the local, municipal, and county committees must conscientiously organize listening to ensure that the intentions and instructions of the provincial leadership are delivered on time and thoroughly implemented.

### 保定人民广播电台　九一开始播音

1949-08-26
第2版()
专栏：

　　保定人民广播电台
  　九一开始播音
    【保定讯】中共河北省委决定并经中共中央华北局批准，建立保定人民广播电台。该台九月一日正式播音。十七日河北省委发出指示，强调省级各领导部门要充分使用广播电台以领导与推动工作；同时各地、市、县委必须认真组织收听，保证省级领导意图和指示按时下达和深入贯彻。
