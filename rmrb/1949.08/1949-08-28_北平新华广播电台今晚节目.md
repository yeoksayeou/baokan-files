Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-08-28
Page: 1

　　Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Program
    [This newspaper dispatch] Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station's speech program from 7:50 PM to 8:10 PM will feature Xia Kangzhi, Standing Committee Member of the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Representative Conference of Workers in Natural Sciences, speaking on "The Class Nature of Natural Science Work." The workers' program from 7:30 PM to 10:50 PM will introduce a steel army in transportation construction - the Railway Corps. The arts and entertainment program from 10:20 PM to 10:50 PM will feature the Huoxian (Firefront) Theater Troupe of the Political Department of the Ji-Zhong Military Region broadcasting "Production Chants." In addition, the arts program on Sunday morning at 8:00 AM will feature the Literary and Art Work Troupe of the North China Military and Political University broadcasting the new opera "Red Cloth Strip."

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚七点五十分到八点十分演讲节目，中华全国自然科学工作者代表大会筹备会常务委员夏康之讲：“自然科学工作的阶级性”。七点三十分到十点五十分职工节目，介绍交通建设上的一支铁军—铁道兵团。十点二十分到十点五十分文艺节目，冀中军区政治部火线剧社播送“生产联唱”。另外，本日上午八点星期文艺节目，由华北军政大学文艺工作团播送新歌剧“红布条”。
