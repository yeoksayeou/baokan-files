Title: Occupy Qiannan on the Guangdong-Jiangxi Border, Liberate Huichang Southwest of Ruijin, Conquer Putian and the Naval Port of Sandu'ao in Fujian

Author:

Date: 1949-08-26
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Central China Front, 25th—The People's Liberation Army on the southern Jiangxi front, having victoriously joined forces with the South China People's Guerrilla Force, captured Qiannan County, located at the junction of Jiangxi and Guangdong provinces east of Qujiang, on the 20th, annihilating part of the enemy. Another unit of the Liberation Army occupied Huichang County, southwest of Ruijin, the capital of the Central Soviet Government during the civil war era, on the 22nd.
    Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 25th—The People's Liberation Army, which liberated Fuzhou, liberated Putian County, northeast of Xiamen, on the 20th. Furthermore, Sandu'ao, a famous naval port on the Fujian coast, was also occupied by the People's Liberation Army on the 11th.

### 粤赣边境攻占虔南瑞金西南解放会昌  闽境克莆田及军港三都澳

1949-08-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　粤赣边境攻占虔南瑞金西南解放会昌
    闽境克莆田及军港三都澳
    【新华社华中前线二十五日电】与华南人民游击队胜利会师的赣南前线人民解放军，二十日攻占曲江以东江西和广东两省交界的虔南县城，歼敌一部。另部解放军二十二日占领内战时代中央苏维埃政府首都瑞金西南的会昌县城。
    【新华社福建前线二十五日电】解放福州的人民解放军，二十日解放厦门东北的莆田县城。又福建沿海著名的军港三都澳亦于十一日被人民解放军占领。
