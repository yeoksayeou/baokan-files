Title: Guo Moruo to Broadcast Today on the Fourth Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Alliance; Xinhua Broadcasting Station in Beiping to Establish "Popular Science Lectures"
Author:
Date: 1949-08-14
Page: 1

    Guo Moruo to Broadcast Today
    On the Fourth Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Alliance
    Xinhua Broadcasting Station in Beiping to Establish
    "Popular Science Lectures"
    [This newspaper's dispatch] Guo Moruo, vice-chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, will speak on "The Fourth Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Alliance" in the Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station's program tonight from 7:50 PM to 8:10 PM.
    [This newspaper's dispatch] The Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station will add "Popular Science Lectures" starting in September, broadcasting various popular science knowledge, especially general basic knowledge related to production and construction. The lecture's programming plan was initially decided yesterday afternoon at a joint symposium held by the Propaganda Department of the Preparatory Committee for the First National Congress of Natural Scientists of China and the Editorial Department of the Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station, concerning the lecture and the "Science Communications" (a publication of the Propaganda Department of the Preparatory Committee for the Congress of Natural Scientists). Special editors attending yesterday included Lu Yudao, Tao Hong, Meng Qingzhe, Xia Kangnong, Zi Qiang, Li Qiang, Qian Sanqiang, Yuan Hanqing, Yan Jici, Le Tianyu, Huang Dingchen, Wen Jize, and more than ten others. The meeting decided that the Popular Science Lectures should include various knowledge of science, engineering, agriculture, and medicine, as well as stories of scientists and inventors, and should answer various questions from listeners related to natural science and technology at any time. The meeting decided that in addition to the already hired special editors being responsible for manuscript selection, special editors would continue to be widely hired and contributions would be widely solicited.

### 郭沫若今日播讲  中苏同盟四周年  北平新华广播电台将设  “自然科学常识讲座”

1949-08-14
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若今日播讲
    中苏同盟四周年
    北平新华广播电台将设
    “自然科学常识讲座”
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚七点五十分到八点十分演讲节目，中苏友好协会筹备委员会副主任郭沫若讲：“中苏同盟四周年”。
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台将从九月起增设“自然科学常识讲座”，播讲各种自然科学常识，特别是有关生产建设的一般的基本的知识。该讲座编播计划，已于昨日下午在中华全国第一次自然科学代表大会筹委会宣传部及北平新华广播电台编辑部联合召开的该讲座及“科学通讯”（科代筹委宣传部刊物）特约编辑座谈会上初步决定。昨日到会的特约编辑有卢于道、陶宏、孟庆哲、夏康农、子强、李强、钱三强、袁翰青、严济慈、乐天宇、黄鼎臣、温济泽等十余人。会上决定自然科学常识讲座应包括理、工、农、医各种常识及科学家发明家的故事等，并应随时解答听众提出的各种有关自然科学和技术的问题。会上决定除由已聘请的特约编辑分别担任择搞外，继续广泛聘请特约编辑并广泛征稿。
