Title: Tram Fare Adjustment
Author:
Date: 1949-08-24
Page: 4

　　Tram Fare Adjustment
    [This City News] The Beiping City Tram Company will adjust tram fares starting August 24th. The fares are divided into three categories: Category A (one section) is 30 yuan, Category B (two or three sections) is 50 yuan, and Category C (four or five sections) is 70 yuan. At the same time, the West Third Road trams will be extended to Chongwenmen (directly from Pinganli to Chongwenmen), with the entire route priced as a five-section fare.

### 电车调整票价

1949-08-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　电车调整票价
    【本市讯】北平市电车公司自八月二十四日起开始调整票价，计分甲种（一段）三十元，乙种（两段三段）五十元，丙种（四段五段）七十元。同时将西三路车辆延长至崇文门（自平安里直达崇文门），全程按五段售票。
