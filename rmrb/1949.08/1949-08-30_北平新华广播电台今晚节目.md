Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-08-30
Page: 1

　　Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Program
    [Our Newspaper] Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station's lecture program from 7:50 PM to 8:10 PM, Tie Duansheng, representative of the preparatory committee of the joint association of university professors of the Beiping All-Circles Representatives Conference and Dean of the Law School of Peking University, will speak on: "Looking Forward to the People's Democratic Dictatorship from the Beiping All-Circles Representatives Conference". From 7:30 PM to 7:50 PM, during the workers' time slot, a speech by Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, will be broadcast – "Great Feelings in a New Democratic Country". From 10:20 PM to 10:50 PM, the arts and literature program will feature an introduction to Korean music.

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-08-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚七点五十分到八点十分演讲节目，北平各界代表会议院校教授联合会筹备会代表、北京大学法学院院长铁端升讲：“从北平各界代表会议瞻望人民民主专政”。七点三十分到七点五十分职工时间，播送中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一的一篇讲话——“在新民主主义国家的伟大感受”。十点二十分到十点五十分文艺节目，朝鲜音乐介绍。
