Title: South Korean Patrol Boat Defects
Author:
Date: 1949-08-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 14th - TASS reports, citing the North Korean Central News Agency, that the signal patrol boat "NR-DI" of the first patrol boat team of the so-called "South Korean Defense Forces" Navy sailed into Mongji Port (transliteration) in North Korea on August 8th. Under the goal of serving their country and people, the sailors have handed over all the equipment of the boat to the representatives of the General Headquarters of the Korean People's Democratic Republic People's Army.

### 南鲜巡艇一艘起义

1949-08-15
第3版()
专栏：

    南鲜巡艇一艘起义
    【新华社北平十四日电】据塔斯社引北朝鲜中央电讯社消息，属于所谓“南朝鲜国防军”海军第一巡逻艇队的信号巡逻艇“ＮＲ—ＤＩ”号，于八月八日开入北朝鲜的蒙吉港（译音）。水手们在为他们的国家与人民服务的目标下，已将该艇的全部装备交予朝鲜人民民主共和国人民军总部的代表。
