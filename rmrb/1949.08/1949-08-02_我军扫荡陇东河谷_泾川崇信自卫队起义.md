Title: Our Army Sweeps the Longdong River Valley; Jingchuan and Chongxin Self-Defense Forces Uprising
Author:
Date: 1949-08-02
Page: 1

    Our Army Sweeps the Longdong River Valley
    Jingchuan and Chongxin Self-Defense Forces Uprising
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northwest Front, 1st dispatch] A unit of the People's Liberation Army First Field Army's cavalry swept through the local bandit troops in the Longdong River Valley. On the 26th and 27th of last month, a total of 203 bandit members of the Zhengning County Self-Defense Force were captured, over 50 bandits were killed or wounded, and 194 rifles and pistols were seized.
    [Xi'an People's Broadcasting Station broadcast] News from the Northwest Front: When the People's Liberation Army launched an offensive in eastern Gansu, 440 members of the self-defense forces of Jingchuan and Chongxin counties, on July 29th, respectively killed the reactionary puppet Jingchuan County Chief and arrested the puppet Chongxin County Chief, and then staged an uprising. The self-defense forces of the two counties brought with them 391 rifles and pistols, and 9 light machine guns, and were warmly welcomed by the local people.

### 我军扫荡陇东河谷  泾川崇信自卫队起义

1949-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我军扫荡陇东河谷
    泾川崇信自卫队起义
    【新华社西北前线一日电】人民解放军第一野战军骑兵部队一部，扫荡陇东河谷地方匪军，上月二十六、二十七两日内共俘匪正宁县自卫队二百零三名，毙伤匪五十余名，缴获长短枪一百九十四支。
    【西安人民广播电台广播】西北前线消息：当解放军在甘肃东部发动攻势时，泾川、崇信两县的自卫队四百四十人，在七月二十九日，分别打死反动的伪泾川县长和逮捕伪崇信县长后，举行起义，两县自卫队，携来长短枪三百九十一支，轻机枪九挺，受到当地人民热烈欢迎。
