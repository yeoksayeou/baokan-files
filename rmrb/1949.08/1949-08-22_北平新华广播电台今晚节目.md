Author:
Date: 1949-08-22
Page: 1

　　Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
    [This Dispatch] Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station's lecture program from 7:50 PM to 8:10 PM will feature Wang Zheng, Director of the Telecommunications Bureau of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission, speaking on "Several Issues Concerning the Future of Telecommunications in the New Democratic Regime." The Women's Hour from 7:30 PM to 7:50 PM will introduce the lives of women in Iran – one of the countries soon to participate in the Asian Women's Conference to be held in Beiping. The arts and entertainment program from 10:20 PM to 10:50 PM will feature the Northeast Lu Xun Academy of Arts and Literature Music Troupe performing orchestral pieces.

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚七点五十分到八点十分演讲节目，中国人民革命军事委员会电信总局局长王诤讲：“新民主主义政权下电信事业今后的几个问题”。七点三十分到七点五十分妇女时间，介绍伊朗——即将参加在北平召开的亚洲妇女会议的国家之一——的妇女生活。十点二十分到十点五十分文艺节目，东北鲁迅文艺学院音乐工作团演奏管弦乐曲。
