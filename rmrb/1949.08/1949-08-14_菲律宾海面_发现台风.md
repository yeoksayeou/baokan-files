Title: Typhoon Spotted in the Philippine Sea
Author:
Date: 1949-08-14
Page: 3

    Philippine Sea
    Typhoon Spotted
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 12th] The Shanghai Meteorological Observatory reported at 10:00 AM on August 12th: At 8:00 AM this morning, a typhoon of medium intensity was located 200 miles east of the northern tip of the Philippines (20 degrees North latitude, 124 degrees East longitude), moving north and northwest. It is expected to reach Taiwan by the morning of the 13th, and may strike the coastal areas of Fujian and Zhejiang from late night on the 13th to the morning of the 14th.

### 菲律宾海面  发现台风

1949-08-14
第3版()
专栏：

    菲律宾海面
    发现台风
    【新华社上海十二日电】上海气象台八月十二日上午十时报告：十二日晨八时，强度中级之台风已在菲律宾北端以东二百哩海面（北纬二零度、东经一二四度）向北、西北行进，预计十三日晨可至台湾，十三日深夜至十四日晨可能进袭闽浙沿海。
