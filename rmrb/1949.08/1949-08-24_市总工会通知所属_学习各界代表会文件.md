Title: Municipal Federation of Trade Unions Notifies Affiliates to Study Documents from the People's Representative Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-08-24
Page: 4

　　Municipal Federation of Trade Unions Notifies Affiliates
  　Study Documents from the People's Representative Conference
    [This Municipality News] The Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions issued a notice to factories, workers' night schools, worker training classes, and trade union cadres in various districts on August 20, stipulating that starting from August 22, they should study the summary report by Peng Zhen, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, at the People's Representative Conference, as well as Mayor Ye Jianying's report on the takeover and administrative work in the past six months, and the supplementary report, within a week. The study method may adopt lectures and reports. The Federation of Trade Unions also notified the representatives from various factories who attended the People's Representative Conference to convey the spirit and resolutions of the conference in their respective factories as soon as possible. Furthermore, Xiao Ming, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, broadcast on the People's Broadcasting Station at 8:10 PM yesterday (August 23), with the topic: "All Workers in Beijing, Take Action and Implement the Resolutions of the People's Representative Conference," calling on Beijing workers to study the conference documents and implement the conference resolutions.

### 市总工会通知所属　学习各界代表会文件

1949-08-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　市总工会通知所属
  　学习各界代表会文件
    【本市讯】北平市职工总会筹委会，于八月二十日向各工厂、工人夜校、工人训练班及各区工会干部发出通知，规定自八月二十二日起一周内学习中共北平市委书记彭真在各界代表会上的总结报告、叶剑英市长关于半年来接管与施政工作报告及补充报告。学习方法可采上课和报告等方式，总工会并通知各工厂出席各界代表会的代表，尽速在本厂传达大会精神及决议。又：市总工会筹委会副主任萧明，于昨（二十三日）晚八时十分在人民广播电台广播，讲题是：“全北平市工人行动起来，执行各界代表会议的决议”，号召北平工人，学习大会文件，执行大会决议。
