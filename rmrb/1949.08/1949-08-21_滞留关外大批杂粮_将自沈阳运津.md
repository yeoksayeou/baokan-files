Title: Large Quantities of Coarse Grains Held Up Outside the Customs, To Be Transported from Shenyang to Tianjin

Author:
Date: 1949-08-21
Page: 4

　　Large Quantities of Coarse Grains Held Up Outside the Customs
  　To Be Transported from Shenyang to Tianjin
    [Tianjin News] The Tianjin-bound train has now reached Jinzhou. Currently, approximately 1,380,000 jin of coarse grains (including corn, sorghum, broad beans, and red beans) held up at Shanhaiguan have been transported to this city on the 18th and will be available for sale on the market immediately. According to general grain merchants, the train will soon reach Shenyang, where about 200 cars (approximately 12,000,000 jin) of coarse grains have already been loaded and will soon be transported to Tianjin.

### 滞留关外大批杂粮　将自沈阳运津

1949-08-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　滞留关外大批杂粮
  　将自沈阳运津
    【天津讯】自津东行火车已通锦州，目前被阻于山海关之杂粮（包括玉米、红粮、元豆、红小豆等）约一百三十八万余斤，十八日已运抵本市即日应市销售。据一般粮商谈：最近火车将直达沈阳，该地约有二百余车（约合一千二百余万斤）杂粮业已装车即将运津。
