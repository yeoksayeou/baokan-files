Title: Nanchang, the Birthplace of the People's Liberation Army, Celebrates Army Day with 50,000 People; Workers Present Production Achievements as Gifts

Author: 
Date: 1949-08-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, 3rd: Nanchang City, the birthplace of the People's Liberation Army's predecessor, the Workers' and Peasants' Red Army, warmly celebrated the first "August 1st" Army Day after liberation. On the morning of the 1st, Yangko teams from various agencies and factory workers went to the city's garrison troops and the People's Government to offer congratulations. A certain artillery regiment of the People's Liberation Army held a solemn parade with captured American weapons and various cannons. At noon, the municipal government held a banquet for the families of revolutionary martyrs and military dependents. The 73-year-old mother of the martyr Fang Zhimin and the 68-year-old father of CCP Central Committee member Rao Shushi were the most respected guests at the table. At 6 p.m., a memorial meeting was held in "August 1st" Park, attended by more than 50,000 people from the troops and all walks of life. The meeting presented flags to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and all commanders and fighters of the Second and Fourth Field Armies of the People's Liberation Army. Chen Zhengren, Secretary of the Jiangxi Provincial Committee of the CCP, Shao Shiping, Chairman of the Jiangxi Provincial People's Government, and Deng Hua, Commander of a certain corps of the People's Liberation Army, spoke at the meeting. The meeting finally adopted congratulatory telegrams to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, General Lin Biao, and all commanders and fighters on the Central China Front. A grand victory parade was held after the meeting and lasted until late at night. Workers from the Railway Bureau, Highway Bureau, Water and Electricity Company, and Telecommunications Bureau in the city launched a production competition to welcome this victorious festival and achieved remarkable results. The workers of the Nanchang Main Station Locomotive Section of the Zhejiang-Jiangxi Railway and the workers of the carriage workshop of the Niuhang Station of the Nanchang-Xunyang Railway successfully test-ran the "August 1st" and "Nanchang Liberation" locomotives on the 30th. The "August 1st," "Army Day," and "Zedong" trucks repaired by the workers of the Highway Bureau have also been completed. The workers of the Telecommunications Bureau erected power lines between Nanchang and Gaoan, Shanggao, and Qingjiang. All the staff of the Water and Electricity Company repaired the second Shuiquan sluice gate, which was broken by the flood, on the 30th.

### 人民解放军诞生地南昌  五万人庆祝建军节  工人们以生产成绩作献礼

1949-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    人民解放军诞生地南昌
    五万人庆祝建军节
    工人们以生产成绩作献礼
    【新华社南昌三日电】人民解放军前身——工农红军的诞生地南昌市党政军民，热烈庆祝解放后第一个“八一”建军节。一日清早，各机关、工厂职工的秧歌队，就纷向市内驻军及人民政府祝贺。人民解放军某炮兵团，携带缴获的美式武器及各种大炮，举行了庄严的游行式。中午，市府欢宴革命先烈的家属和军属。先烈方志敏同志的七十三岁的母亲和中共中央委员饶漱石同志的六十八岁的父亲是座上最受尊敬的宾客。下午六时，在“八一”公园举行纪念大会，到会部队及各界人民五万余人。大会向毛主席、朱总司令及人民解放军第二、第四野战军全体指战员献旗。会上并有中共江西省委员会书记陈正人、江西省人民政府主席邵式平、人民解放军某兵团司令员邓华等讲话。大会最后通过致毛主席、朱总司令、林彪将军及华中前线全体指战员的贺电。会后举行盛大的胜利游行，直至深夜始散。市内铁路局、公路局、水电公司及电讯局等处的工人们，为了迎接这个胜利的节日，曾发动生产竞赛，均获得显著成绩。浙赣路南昌总站机务段工人及南浔路牛行站车房工人赶修的“八一”和“南浔解放”两号机车，均于三十日试车成功。公路局工人赶修的“八一”、“建军”、“泽东”号三辆汽车，亦全部竣工。电讯局工人则架通了南昌至高安、上高和清江间的电线。水电公司全体职工于三十日修好了被大水冲断的第二水泉闸门。
