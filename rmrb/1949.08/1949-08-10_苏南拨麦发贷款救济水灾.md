Title: Southern Jiangsu Allocates Wheat and Issues Loans to Aid Flood Victims
Author:
Date: 1949-08-10
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, August 8th: In order to enable farmers in flood-stricken areas to replant in a timely manner, the Southern Jiangsu Branch of the People's Bank has issued seven hundred million yuan in kind seed loans in July, with a monthly interest rate of only three per mille. At the same time, the Southern Jiangsu Administrative Office has allocated 90,000 catties of wheat to resettle disaster victims in the Suzhou Special District.

### 苏南拨麦发贷款救济水灾

1949-08-10
第2版()
专栏：

    苏南拨麦发贷款救济水灾
    【新华社上海八日电】人民银行苏南分行为使水灾区农民及时补种，已于七月间先后发放折实种籽贷款七千万元，月息仅三厘。同时苏南行署并曾拨麦九万斤，安置苏州专区灾民。
