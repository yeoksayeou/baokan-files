Title: North China Government Staff Training Class: Four Thousand Trainees Hold Graduation Ceremony, Chairman Dong Delivers Speech Encouraging Continued Learning
Author: Lin Hong
Date: 1949-08-15
Page: 2

North China Government Staff Training Class
    Four Thousand Trainees Hold Graduation Ceremony
    Chairman Dong Delivers Speech Encouraging Continued Learning

    [Our Paper's News] The North China People's Government Staff Training Class held a graduation ceremony at the Zhongshan Park Music Hall on August 12th. Over four thousand three hundred trainees, along with one thousand plus retained personnel from various departments, attended. Dong Biwu, Chairman of the North China People's Government, and responsible comrades from various departments were present to provide guidance. The meeting was first opened by Comrade Yi Jiaju, Chairman of the assembly, who stated: This training has been successful. After studying revolutionary theory, everyone has distinguished the boundaries between revolution and counter-revolution, reviewed many past incorrect viewpoints, and laid a preliminary foundation for transforming themselves; and began to establish a worldview of serving the people. Chairman Dong then delivered a speech, providing a detailed analysis of the current international and domestic situation. He especially earnestly advised all trainees on the importance of continuous learning, urging them to never forget to study in the future.

    After Chairman Dong's speech, trainees from seven units, including the Public Enterprise Department and the North China Agricultural Science Research Institute, presented flags to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and Chairman Dong respectively. Finally, Jin Mosheng, Director of the Training Department, delivered a summary report on the training, and a trainee representative gave a reply.
                                                          (Lin Hong)

### 华北政府工作人员训练班  四千学员举行毕业典礼  董主席讲话勖勉继续学习
林洪
1949-08-15
第2版()
专栏：

    华北政府工作人员训练班
    四千学员举行毕业典礼
    董主席讲话勖勉继续学习
    【本报讯】华北人民政府工作人员训练班于八月十二日在中山公园音乐堂举行毕业典礼，到全体学员四千三百余人，各部门留用人员一千余人，华北人民政府董必武主席及各部负责同志均亲临指导。首先由大会主席易家驹同志致开会词，他说：这一次的学习是有成绩的，大家在学习了革命的理论后，分清了革命与反革命的界线，检讨了过去许多错误的看法，初步打下了改造自己的基础；开始建立了为人民服务的人生观。继由董主席讲话，他对目前国际国内的形势作了详尽的分析。特别对学习问题谆谆地告诫全体学员，今后要继续学习，时时不忘学习。
    董主席讲完话后，公营企业部、华北农业科学研究所等七个单位的训练班学员，分别向毛主席、朱总司令和董主席献旗最后训练处处长金默生同志作学习总结报告，和学员代表致答词。      
      （林洪）
