Title: Enemy Agents in Wuhan Register and Repent
Author:
Date: 1949-08-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 3rd. Since the Wuhan Military Control Commission promulgated the "Implementation Measures for Kuomintang Secret Service Personnel Applying for Repentance Registration" on the 11th of last month, over a hundred Kuomintang "Military Intelligence" and "Central Statistics Bureau" secret service personnel have applied for registration at various registration offices established by the Public Security Bureau by the end of July. Some of these secret agents have also reported lists of secret agents潜伏潜伏 in Wuhan to the public security organs, and some have persuaded their accomplices to register and repent with the public security organs.

### 武汉敌特登记悔过

1949-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    武汉敌特登记悔过
    【新华社汉口三日电】武汉市军管会于上月十一日颁布关于国民党特务人员申请悔过登记实施办法后，至七月底向公安局设立之各登记处申请登记之国民党“军统”“中统”等特务人员已达百余人。其中一部分特务分子且向公安机关报告了潜伏在武汉的特务分子名单，有的并劝说其同伙向公安机关登记悔过。
