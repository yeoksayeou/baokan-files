Title: Upon Hearing News of the Northeast's Trade Agreement with the Soviet Union, All Circles in Shanghai are Elated, Unanimously Believing it is a Resolute Blow to Imperialist Blockade
Author:
Date: 1949-08-04
Page: 1

    [Shanghai People's Broadcasting Station, 3rd Broadcast] Following the Northeast People's Government's conclusion of a barter agreement with the Soviet Union, all circles in Shanghai have expressed unanimous elation. Leaders from the Democratic Youth Federation, Vocational Youth Federation, Student Union, Democratic Women's Federation, teachers from universities, middle schools, and primary schools, as well as figures from the literary and art circles, technical fields, and publishing industry, have successively issued statements. All believe this is a resolute blow to the imperialist blockade, further encouraging our confidence in struggle and enabling us to more clearly recognize who are our enemies and who are our friends.

### 听到东北与苏联缔结通商协定  沪市各界欢欣鼓舞  咸认是对帝国主义封锁的无情打击

1949-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    听到东北与苏联缔结通商协定
    沪市各界欢欣鼓舞
    咸认是对帝国主义封锁的无情打击
    【上海人民广播电台三日广播】东北人民政府和苏联缔结易货协定后，本市各界一致表示欢欣。民主青年联合会、职业青年联合会、学联、民主妇联、大中小学教师以及文艺界、技术界、出版界等负责人纷纷发表谈话，都认为这是对帝国主义封锁的无情打击，更加鼓舞了我们斗争的信心，并且使我们更清楚的认识谁是我们的敌人，谁是我们的友人。
