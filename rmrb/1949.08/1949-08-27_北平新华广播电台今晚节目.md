Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-08-27
Page: 1

　　Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Program
    [Our Newspaper] Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station tonight from 7:30 to 7:50 PM Youth Time: Broadcast "China Youth" issue 16 editorial: "Long Live the Friendship and Unity of Democratic Youth Worldwide"; Introduction: "World Federation of Democratic Youth", "International Student Union", "International Youth Day" and "World Youth and Student Festival". From 7:50 to 8:10 PM Lecture Program, Beiping Meteorological Observatory will present: "The Weather in North China in Autumn". From 10:20 to 10:50 PM Arts and Entertainment Program, the Inner Mongolia Arts Troupe will broadcast "Introduction to Mongolian Songs".

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-08-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚七点三十分到七点五十分青年时间：播送“中国青年”十六期社论：“全世界民主青年友谊团结万岁”；介绍：“世界民主青年联盟”，“国际学生联合会”，“国际青年节”和“世界青年和学生联欢大会”。七点五十分到八点十分演讲节目，北平气象台主讲：“华北秋天的天气”。十点二十分到十点五十分文艺节目，内蒙古文艺工作团播送“蒙古歌曲介绍”。
