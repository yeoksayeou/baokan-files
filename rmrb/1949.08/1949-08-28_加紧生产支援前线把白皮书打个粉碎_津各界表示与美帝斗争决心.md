Title: Step Up Production to Support the Front Lines and Smash the White Paper: People from All Walks of Life in Tianjin Express Resolve to Fight Against U.S. Imperialism
Author:
Date: 1949-08-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, August 27th—When commenting on the American "White Paper," people from all walks of life in Tianjin unanimously expressed their firm confidence in crushing the new conspiracies of U.S. imperialism and building a new China. After reading or hearing the content of the U.S. "White Paper," the vast number of workers and staff in various public and private factories in the city expressed: The working class should strengthen solidarity, strive to develop production, and no matter what tricks the United States plays, we will smash them to pieces. Liu Yan, Chairman of the Tianjin Student Union, said: In order to achieve a complete victory in our great revolutionary struggle as soon as possible, and for the bright future of our younger generation, we students vow to stand firmly with the Chinese people in the socialist camp led by the Soviet Union under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, and fight to the end for the liberation of the Chinese people and the victory of the construction of a new China. Luo Yun, Director of the Tianjin Women's Federation, said: We women in Tianjin have long recognized the face of U.S. imperialism and no longer hold any illusions about it. We must step up production, actively support the front lines, and completely eliminate the remaining forces of the Chiang Kai-shek bandits, so that national liberation can be achieved sooner. Li Zhuchen, Manager of Jiuda Refined Salt Company, pointed out: Now that the Chinese people have stood up, the imperialists can no longer use their tricks. Yu Keji, Engineer of the Tianjin Branch of North China Electric Power Company, pointed out: "U.S. imperialism is actually a dying beast today, but it will still struggle to the death. We should be more vigilant."

### 加紧生产支援前线把白皮书打个粉碎　津各界表示与美帝斗争决心

1949-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　加紧生产支援前线把白皮书打个粉碎
  　津各界表示与美帝斗争决心
    【新华社天津二十七日电】津市各界人士在评论美国“白皮书”时，都一致表示出对粉碎美帝国主义新阴谋和建设新中国的坚强信心。本市各公私营工厂广大职工，在读了或听了美帝“白皮书”的内容后，都表示：工人阶级应该加强职工团结，努力发展生产，不管美国再打什么鬼主意，都一定将它打个粉碎。津市学联主席刘焱说：为了使我们伟大的革命斗争早日得到全部胜利，为了我们年青一代的美好的将来，我们学生誓与中国人民一道，在中共和毛主席领导下坚决站在以苏联为首的和平民主阵营，为争取中国人民的解放，为新中国建设的胜利，奋斗到底。津市妇联主任罗云表示：我们天津妇女早就认识了美帝国主义的面目，不再对美帝国主义抱任何幻想，我们要加紧生产，积极支援前线，彻底消灭蒋匪的残余力量，使全国解放能更早实现。久大精盐公司经理李烛尘指出：在中国人民已经站了起来的今天，帝国主义者是再不能施其技了。华北电业津分公司工程师余克稷指出：“美帝国主义在今天实际已是一个垂死的野兽，但它还会作最后挣扎，我们应提高警惕。”
