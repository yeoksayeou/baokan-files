    Publication Briefs One
  "Essential Readings for Cadres": Eleven Titles Corrected or Compiled and Sent for Publication
    Of the twelve theoretical books designated by the Party Central Committee as "Essential Readings for Cadres," except for "Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin on China," which is still being compiled, the remaining eleven titles, including Social Development History, Political Economy, The Communist Manifesto, Socialism: Utopian and Scientific, Imperialism, the Highest Stage of Capitalism, State and Revolution, "Left-Wing" Communism: An Infantile Disorder, Foundations of Leninism, A Short History of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks), Lenin and Stalin on Socialist Economic Construction, and On Practice, have all been corrected or compiled and sent for publication by Liberation Press.
  Three Classical Works by Marx and Engels Soon to Be Published
    Marx's "The Poverty of Philosophy" and "Critique of the Gotha Program" have been re-translated by Comrade He Sijing. Engels' "Ludwig Feuerbach and the End of Classical German Philosophy" has also been revised by Comrade Zhang Zhongshi. All three classical works will be published by Liberation Press.
  "On the Self-Cultivation of a Communist" Revised and Republished
    Comrade Liu Shaoqi's "On the Self-Cultivation of a Communist" has been revised in some wording by the author and submitted to Liberation Press for republication.

### 出版简报之一

1949-08-15
第6版()
专栏：

    出版简报之一
  “干部必读”已校正或编就发稿十一种
    党中央指定的“干部必读”十二种理论书，除“马、恩、列、斯论中国”、一种尚待整理外，其余社会发展史、政治经济学、共产党宣言、社会主义从空想到科学的发展、帝国主义是资本主义的最高阶段、国家与革命、“左派”幼稚病、论列宁主义基础、苏联共产党（布）历史简要读本、列宁斯大林论社会主义经济建设、思想方法论等十一种，解放社都已校正或编就发稿。
  马恩古典著作三种即将出版
    马克思著“哲学底贫困”与“哥达纲领批判”，已由何思敬同志重新译过。恩格斯著“费尔巴哈德国古典哲学的终结”，原译本亦经张仲实同志校改。以上三种古典著作，均将由解放社出版。
  “论共产党员修养”订正重版
    刘少奇同志的“论共产党员的修养”，作者已作了若干文字上的修改交解放社重版。
