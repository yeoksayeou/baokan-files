Title: Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Party Congress Sets Policy: Develop Agricultural Production
Author:
Date: 1949-08-21
Page: 2

　　Xi'an People's Broadcasting Station Broadcast: Yan'an News: The First Congress of the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Committee of the Communist Party of China opened on July 26th and closed on August 8th after Li Hebang, Secretary of the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Committee and Chairman of the Congress, made a concluding report. The conference unanimously agreed with and supplemented Comrade Li Hebang's report on land reform and party consolidation, as well as Comrade Li Jinglin's report on the 1950 economic construction plan. The conference reviewed and summarized past work experience and put forward guidelines for future work. The conference determined that the central task of the entire Party in the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region in the future is to restore and develop production and promote the economy, but first and foremost, it should focus on improving and developing agricultural production in order to lay a solid foundation for the development of industry. The conference pointed out that in a considerable period to come, agriculture and handicrafts in the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region will still be scattered and individual. It is necessary to appropriately develop towards collectivization and industrialization through the road of cooperatives.

### 陕北党代会确定方针　发展农业生产

1949-08-21
第2版()
专栏：

　　陕北党代会确定方针
  　发展农业生产
    【西安人民广播电台广播】延安消息：中共陕北区第一次党代表会议，从七月二十六日开幕，到八月八日由大会主席陕北区党委书记李合邦作总结报告后闭幕。会议一致同意和补充了李合邦同志关于土改、整党问题的报告，以及李景林同志报告的一九五○年经济建设计划。会议检讨和总结了过去的工作经验，提出今后的工作方针。会议确定陕北全党今后的中心任务，是恢复和发展生产、繁荣经济，但首先应以提高和发展农业生产为重心，为发展工业打下稳固的基础。会议指出在今后一个相当长的时期内，陕北农业和手工业还将是分散的、个体的，必须适当的通过合作社的道路，向集体化、工业化方向发展。
