Title: *Pravda* Article Points Out: Soviet Films' Ideological and Artistic Level Improves Daily; American Films are Specimens of Human Shame
Author:
Date: 1949-08-12
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 11th: TASS, Moscow: *Pravda* reporter Krusinsky commented on the achievements of the Fourth International Film Festival, saying: The achievements of the film festival provide new evidence that the ideological and artistic level of Soviet films is increasing daily. At the same time, the developing art of the people's democratic countries has also been widely displayed.

    Krusinsky emphasized: The film festival also proves that American films, typical products of monopoly capitalists, are all waste and garbage, only serving to arouse disgust in objective audiences with noble tastes. The reporter divided the monotonous productions of those business-minded film studios into two main categories: the first is detective films, in which one hears the sound of human skulls being smashed, accompanied by frivolous and noisy music; the second is so-called psychological and "philosophical" films, in which the sound of skulls cracking is accompanied by sluggish melodies. This is a philosophy that reverses psychology and mocks humanity. Pure pornography and naturalism are the inherent characteristics of such films. This specimen of human shame represents the characteristics of contemporary bourgeois art.

### 真理报著文指出  苏联电影思想艺术水平日高  美国电影是人类耻辱的标本

1949-08-12
第3版()
专栏：

    真理报著文指出
    苏联电影思想艺术水平日高
    美国电影是人类耻辱的标本
    【新华社北平十一日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：真理报记者克鲁辛斯基评论第四届国际电影节的成果说：电影节的成果，提供了新的证据，说明苏联电影的思想与艺术水平日渐增高。同时，人民民主国家正在发展着的艺术，也广泛地表现了出来。
    克鲁辛斯基强调指出：电影节同时也证明了，垄断资本家典型产品的美国电影尽是些废物和垃圾，只不过徒然使有高尚趣味的客观的观众产生厌恶的印象罢了。记者把那些讲究生意经的制片厂的千篇一律的出品，分作主要的两类：第一类是侦探片，在这种影片里，人们听到击破人类头盖骨的声音，伴奏着轻佻的喧闹的音乐；第二类就是所谓心理与“哲学”电影。在这类影片里，头盖骨的破裂声伴奏着迟滞的旋律。这是颠倒心理和嘲弄人类的哲学。纯粹的色情主义和自然主义，是这种影片所必然固有的性质。这种人类耻辱的标本，正代表着当代的资产阶级艺术的特性。
