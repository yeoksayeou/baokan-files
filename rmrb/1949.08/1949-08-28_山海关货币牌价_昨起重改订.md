Title: Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate Revised Again Starting Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-08-28
Page: 4

　　Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate
  　Revised Again Starting Yesterday
    [Shanhaiguan News] The Shanhaiguan Joint Office of the People's Bank of China and the Northeast Bank has revised the exchange rate starting August 27th to be 1 RMB equaling 56 Northeast Yuan.

### 山海关货币牌价　昨起重改订

1949-08-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　山海关货币牌价
  　昨起重改订
    【山海关讯】中国人民银行、东北银行山海关联合办事处自八月二十七日起牌价改为人民券一元兑东北券五十六元。
