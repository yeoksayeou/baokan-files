Title: Democratic Youth Closely United: World Youth Representatives to Hold Get-Together
Author:
Date: 1949-08-25
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 24th: Budapest dispatch: The delegations of various countries participating in the World Youth and Students Festival are arranging to hold get-togethers with representatives of other countries. The recent gathering of the Chinese delegation and Southeast Asian representatives was of this nature. The Soviet delegation has already held friendly consultations with the delegations of China, Italy, Bulgaria, and other countries in order to hold a get-together. These gatherings will more closely unite the youth of the world who are fighting for peace, democracy, national independence, and a bright future.
    At the same time, 600 male and female members of the delegations from Poland, Romania, Czechoslovakia, Germany, Austria, France, Indonesia, and Albania, in order to express the international solidarity of youth, voluntarily participate in the construction project of the Budapest People's Sports Arena every day.

### 民主青年紧密团结　世界青年代表将举行联谊会

1949-08-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　民主青年紧密团结
  　世界青年代表将举行联谊会
    【新华社北平二十四日电】布达佩斯讯：参加世界青年与学生联欢大会的各国代表团，正布置和其他国家的代表举行联谊会。中国代表团和东南亚代表最近的聚会，就是这样的性质。苏联代表团已经和中国、意大利、保加利亚和其他国家的代表团作了友好的会商，以便举行联谊会。这些聚会将把全世界正为和平民主为民族独立和美好的将来而斗争的青年，更紧密地团结在一起。
    同时，波、罗、捷、德、奥、法、印尼以及阿尔巴尼亚的代表团男女团员六百人，为了表现青年的国际团结，自愿每日参加布达佩斯人民体育馆的建筑工程。
