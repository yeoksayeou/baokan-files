Title: Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate Revised Starting Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-08-27
Page: 4

　　Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate
　　Revised Starting Yesterday
　　[Shanhaiguan News] The Shanhaiguan Joint Office of the People's Bank of China and the Northeast Bank revised its exchange rate starting August 26th to 1 RMB equaling 52 Northeast Yuan.

### 山海关货币价牌　昨起改订

1949-08-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　山海关货币价牌
  　昨起改订
    【山海关讯】中国人民银行、东北银行山海关联合办事处自八月二十六日起牌价改为人民券一元兑东北券五十二元。
