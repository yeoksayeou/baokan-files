Title: Political Department of the North China Military Region: Zhu Liangcai and Others Appointed as Director and Deputy Directors
Author:
Date: 1949-08-03
Page: 2

    Political Department of the North China Military Region
    Zhu Liangcai and Others Appointed as Director and Deputy Directors
    [This newspaper's dispatch] According to news from the Headquarters of the North China Military Region: Yesterday (the 2nd), an order was received announcing that Luo Ruiqing, Director of the Political Department of the North China Military Region, and Cai Shufan, Deputy Director, have been assigned to other posts. Zhu Liangcai will serve as the Director, and Zhang Nansheng and Zhang Zhixiang will serve as Deputy Directors, respectively.

### 华北军区政治部  朱良才等任正副主任

1949-08-03
第2版()
专栏：

    华北军区政治部
    朱良才等任正副主任
    【本报讯】华北军区司令部消息：昨（二日）日奉命宣布华北军区政治部主任罗瑞卿、副主任蔡树藩另有任用。正主任由朱良才、副主任由张南生、张致祥分别担任。
