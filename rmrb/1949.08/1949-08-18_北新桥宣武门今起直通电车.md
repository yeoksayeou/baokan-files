Title: Tram Direct Service Between Beixinqiao and Xuanwumen Starts Today
Author:
Date: 1949-08-18
Page: 4

　　Tram Direct Service Between Beixinqiao and Xuanwumen Starts Today
    [This Newspaper Reported] Starting today (the 18th), the No. 3 East Route tram will extend to Xuanwumen. The entire route will sell tickets in five sections, running directly from Beixinqiao to Xuanwumen. (Tram Company Traffic Section)

### 北新桥宣武门今起直通电车

1949-08-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　北新桥宣武门今起直通电车
    【本报讯】本市东三路电车自今（十八）日起延长至宣武门，全路按五段售票自北新桥直驶宣武门。（电车公司车务课）
