Title: New Hunan Daily Launched in Changsha
Author: 
Date: 1949-08-20
Page: 2

　　New Hunan Daily
  　Launched in Changsha
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 17th] The New Hunan Daily, the organ of the Hunan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, was launched in Changsha on the 15th. The Hunan branch of Xinhua News Agency was established on the 14th and began sending稿 (manuscripts/news items).

### 新湖南报　在长沙创刊

1949-08-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　新湖南报
  　在长沙创刊
    【新华社北平十七日电】中国共产党湖南省委会机关报新湖南报，已于十五日在长沙创刊。新华社湖南分社于十四日成立，并开始发稿。
