Title: Commercial Telegrams Between Beiping and Changsha Open Yesterday; Wireless Telephone Service Available Within Days
Author:
Date: 1949-08-07
Page: 5

    Commercial Telegrams Between Beiping and Changsha Open Yesterday;
    Wireless Telephone Service Available Within Days
    [Our Newspaper's Report] After the liberation of Changsha, the capital of Hunan Province, on the 5th, the Beiping Telecommunications Bureau immediately conducted an experimental telegraph connection with Changsha, and the results were excellent. Starting yesterday (the 6th), the official sending and receiving of commercial telegrams began. Wireless telephone testing between Beiping and Changsha is also being actively conducted, and telephone service should be available within days.
            (Telecommunications Bureau)

### 北平长沙间昨起通商报  无线电话日内可通

1949-08-07
第5版()
专栏：

    北平长沙间昨起通商报
    无线电话日内可通
    【本报讯】湖南省会长沙于五日解放后，北平电信局立即与长沙试验电报联系，结果良好。自昨（六）日起开始正式收发商报。北平与长沙间无线电话，亦正积极进行试话，日内即可通话。
            （电信局）
