Title: Our Delegation Hosts Journalists; Comrade Xiao Hua Delivers Report; Journalists from Various Countries Wish Us Greater Victory
Author:
Date: 1949-08-25
Page: 1
　　
　　Our Delegation Hosts Journalists
    Comrade Xiao Hua Delivers Report
  　Journalists from Various Countries Wish Us Greater Victory
    [Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 24th] The Chinese Democratic Youth Delegation held a press conference in Budapest on the 22nd, attended by about 70 journalists from various countries. At the meeting, Xiao Hua, deputy head of the delegation, reported on the victories of the Chinese People's Liberation War in the past three years and the reasons for the victories, as well as the role played by Chinese youth in the national struggle. He also said: Chinese youth are extremely willing to unite with the democratic youth of the Soviet Union and Eurasian people's democratic countries, colonial countries, and bourgeois countries, and jointly strive for lasting peace, national independence, people's democracy, and a bright future. We firmly believe that victory will surely belong to us. Xiao Hua's report was met with warm applause. The reporters also shouted "Mao Zedong! Mao Zedong!" repeatedly. The reporters then successively asked questions about China's land reform, armed struggle, and the construction of the liberated areas. Finally, a Czech journalist, representing all the journalists present, thanked the Chinese delegation and congratulated the Chinese people on achieving greater victories.

### 我代表团招待记者  萧华同志作报告　各国记者祝我获得更大胜利

1949-08-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　我代表团招待记者
    萧华同志作报告
  　各国记者祝我获得更大胜利
    【新华社布拉格二十四日电】中国民主青年代表团二十二日在布达佩斯举行记者招待会，到有各国记者约七十人。会上，代表团副团长萧华报告了中国人民解放战争三年来的胜利和胜利的原因，以及中国青年在民族斗争中所起的作用。他并说：中国青年极愿与苏联和欧亚人民民主国家、殖民地国家、及资产阶级国家的民主青年团结一致，共同为持久和平、民族独立、人民民主和美好的将来而奋斗。我们深信胜利是一定属于我们的。萧华的报告博得热烈的掌声。记者们并高呼“毛泽东！毛泽东！”不已。记者随即纷纷对中国的土地改革、武装斗争及解放区建设等方面提出询问。最后，捷克记者代表全体到会记者，向中国代表团致谢，并祝贺中国人民获得更大的胜利。
