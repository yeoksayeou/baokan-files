Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-08-07
Page: 1

    [This newspaper's dispatch] Tonight's program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station: From 7:30 PM to 7:50 PM, worker's time: The national working class celebrates the "August 1st" Army Day with practical actions of stepping up production and practicing thrift to support the army. From 7:50 PM to 8:10 PM, lecture program, Mr. Zhang Yan from Beiping Meteorological Observatory will speak on: "The Basic Reasons for Recent Rains in Beiping". From 10:20 PM to 10:50 PM, entertainment program, the Huoxian (Frontline) Theater Troupe of the Political Department of the Jizhong Military Region will broadcast and sing production medleys and other songs.

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-08-07
第1版()
专栏：

    北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚七点三十分到七点五十分职工时间：全国工人阶级以加紧生产节约劳军的实际行动来庆祝“八一”建军节。七点五十分到八点十分演讲节目，北平气象台章淹先生讲：“北平最近下雨的基本原因”。十点二十分到十点五十分文艺节目，冀中军区政治部火线剧社播唱生产联唱及其他歌曲。
