Title: Celebrating the Fifth Anniversary of Soviet Army's Liberation of the Homeland: Romanian People Joyfully Celebrate National Day. Grand Military Parade Held in Bucharest, Representatives from Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, and Bulgaria Attend and Offer Congratulations

Author:

Date: 1949-08-26
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 25th: TASS, Bucharest dispatch: On August 23rd, the Romanian people joyfully celebrated their National Day, the fifth anniversary of the Soviet army's liberation of Romania from Nazi German enslavement. The Romanian people adorned the capital with flowers and red flags, creating a magnificent scene. A military parade and a demonstration of over 400,000 working people were held in Victory Square, Bucharest. Representatives from the Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria, Hungary, and Albania attended to offer congratulations. The Soviet delegation was led by Voroshilov. When Groza, Dej, and Soviet Marshal Voroshilov appeared on the reviewing stand, thunderous cheers erupted, and shouts of "Salute the great Stalin" and "Salute the ever-victorious Soviet Army" echoed for a long time. The military parade lasted for one and a half hours, with infantry, cavalry, mechanized units, artillery, and tank units under review. Dozens of trainer and fighter planes swept across the sky above the square.

### 纪念苏军解放国土五周年　罗马尼亚人民欢度国庆  布加勒斯特举行隆重阅兵式苏波捷保等国代表参加祝贺

1949-08-26
第3版()
专栏：

　　纪念苏军解放国土五周年
  　罗马尼亚人民欢度国庆
    布加勒斯特举行隆重阅兵式苏波捷保等国代表参加祝贺
    【新华社北平二十五日电】塔斯社布加勒斯特讯：八月二十三日，罗马尼亚人民欢乐地庆祝了他们的国庆日，这一天是苏联军队从纳粹德国的奴役下解放罗马尼亚的五周年纪念日。罗马尼亚人民用鲜花和红旗把京城装饰成一片灿烂，布加勒斯特胜利广场举行了阅兵式和四十万人以上劳动人民的示威游行。苏联、波、捷、保、匈、阿各国均派代表参加祝贺，苏联代表团由伏罗希洛夫率领。当格罗查、德治和苏联元帅伏罗希洛夫出现在检阅台上时，会上响起了震天的欢呼，“向伟大的斯大林致敬”、“向常胜的苏军致敬”的呼声历久不息。阅兵式进行一小时半，受检阅的有步兵、骑兵、机械化部队、炮兵及坦克队。数十架教练机与战斗机掠过广场的上空。
