Title: Northeast People's Representative Conference Concludes; Northeast People's Government Elected; Gao Gang and 40 Others Elected as Government Members; Conference Sends Telegrams of Respect to Stalin, Chairman Mao, and Commander-in-Chief Zhu

Author:

Date: 1949-08-28
Page: 1

[Shenyang People's Radio Station broadcast on the 27th] The Northeast People's Representative Conference, which lasted for six days, concluded on the 26th. The conference elected Gao Gang, Li Fuchun, Lin Feng, Gao Chongmin, Zhang Weizhen, Zhang Lofu, Wu Xiuquan, Che Xiangchen, Zhang Xuesi, Chen Xianzhou, Yu Yifu, Liu Yaxiong, Han Tianshi, Zhu Dehai, Liu Yingyuan, Li Guojun and 25 others as members of the Northeast People's Government. Li Yanlu, Si Hanmin, Bai Xiqing, Wang Zhixi, Deng Zhaoxiang, and 6 others were elected as alternate members. The conference unanimously approved the suggestions of the Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee regarding the Northeast People's Government's administrative policies, approved the Northeast Administrative Committee's three-year work report, endorsed the New Political Consultative Conference and other six resolutions, and passed a telegram of respect to Marshal Stalin, Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and all officers and soldiers of the Fourth Field Army.

[Shenyang People's Radio Station Broadcast] At the closing ceremony of the Northeast People's Representative Conference, Comrade Li Fuchun, Deputy Secretary of the Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, delivered the closing speech on behalf of the Presidium. He began by saying: "The conference has achieved great success after six days of intense work. This is a symbol of the unity of the people of all strata in the Northeast." Then, he pointed out that the success of the conference was reflected in three aspects: (1) The conference approved the Northeast Administrative Committee's summary of the government's work in the Northeast over the past three years. (2) The conference adopted the suggestions of the Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee regarding the administrative policies of the Northeast People's Government. (3) The conference elected the Northeast People's Government. Next, Comrade Li Fuchun emphasized that the administrative policies stipulate arduous and heavy economic construction tasks for the future, and called on all representatives to face up to difficulties and overcome them. Finally, he called on all representatives to mobilize and organize the people of the Northeast to transform the conference's policies into practical actions of the people after the closing of the conference.

### 东北人民代表会议闭幕　选出东北人民政府  高岗等四十一人当选为政府委员  大会电斯大林毛主席朱总司令致敬

1949-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　东北人民代表会议闭幕
  　选出东北人民政府
    高岗等四十一人当选为政府委员
    大会电斯大林毛主席朱总司令致敬
    【沈阳人民广播电台二十七日广播】东北人民代表会议，开了六天，已经在二十六日闭幕。大会选出高岗、李富春、林枫、高崇民、张维祯、张洛甫、伍修权、车向忱、张学思、陈先舟、于毅夫、刘亚雄、韩天石、朱德海、刘英源、李国钧等四十一人为东北人民政府委员，李延录、司汉民、白希清、王之玺、邓兆祥等十一人为候补委员，大会一致通过，接受中共中央东北局关于东北人民政府施政方针的建议，批准东北政委会三年工作报告，拥护新政协等六个决议，并通过给斯大林大元帅和毛主席、朱总司令以及第四野战军全体指战员致敬的电文。
    【沈阳人民广播电台广播】东北人民代表会议闭幕典礼上，中共中央东北局副书记李富春同志代表主席团致闭幕词，他首先说：会议经过六天紧张的工作，获得重大成功。这是东北各阶层人民团结的象征，接着，他指出会议的成功表现在三方面，就是（一）会议批准了东北行政委员会对东北三年来政府工作的总结。（二）会议通过了中共中央东北局关于东北人民政府施政方针的建议。（三）会议选出了东北人民政府。接着李富春同志强调指出，施政方针规定了今后艰巨繁重的经济建设任务，向全体代表提出要正视困难，克服困难。最后并且号召全体代表在会议闭幕以后，必须动员组织东北人民把会议的方针变为人民的实际行动。
