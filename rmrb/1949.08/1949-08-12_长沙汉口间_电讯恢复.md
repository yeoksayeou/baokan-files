Title: Telecommunications Restored Between Changsha and Hankou
Author:
Date: 1949-08-12
Page: 2

    Changsha-Hankou
    Telecommunications Restored
    [This Newspaper Dispatches] Wuhan News: Telecommunications between Hankou and Changsha have been restored. After the liberation of Changsha, the local Telecommunications Bureau, following instructions from the Central China Telecommunications Administration, opened wireless telegraph and telephone services from the 7th. Now, telegrams between Hankou and Changsha can be transmitted at any time, 24 hours a day. In addition, wired telephone lines from Changsha, Polo, Liling, Zhuzhou, and other places have also been fully repaired and are in operation.

### 长沙汉口间  电讯恢复

1949-08-12
第2版()
专栏：

    长沙汉口间
    电讯恢复
    【本报讯】武汉消息：汉口长沙间的电讯已经恢复。长沙解放以后，该地电信局就奉华中电信管理局的指示，从七日起即开放了无线电报电话营业。现在汉口长沙间的电报，每天二十四小时都能随时传递。另外，从长沙、泊罗、醴陵、株州等地的有线电话，也都完全修好通话。
