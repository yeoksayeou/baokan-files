Title: Tito Clique Panics, Forbids Publication of Soviet Reply
Author:
Date: 1949-08-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 17th: The Soviet government's reply to the Tito government has caused panic and unease among the Tito clique. The Tito government has strictly ordered Yugoslav newspapers to refrain from publishing the Soviet reply. The Tito government's high-level leading bodies hastily convened a meeting in Belgrade on the 13th to discuss countermeasures. According to TASS reports, as of the 14th, the Tito government had remained silent on the Soviet reply.

### 铁托集团恐慌　竟禁载苏联复文

1949-08-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　铁托集团恐慌
  　竟禁载苏联复文
    【新华社北平十七日电】苏联政府致铁托政府的复文，引起铁托集团的恐慌不安。铁托政府严令南斯拉夫报纸禁登苏联复文。铁托政府的高级领导机构于十三日慌张地在贝尔格莱德举行会议，以讨论应付之策。据塔斯社报道，至十四日为止，铁托政府对苏联复文仍一声不响。
