Title: Preparatory Committee of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association: List of Candidates for the Secretariat Determined; Introduction of Soviet Nation-Building Experience and Work to Enhance Friendship Between the Two Peoples Begins

Author:

Date: 1949-08-03
Page: 1

    [Ping City News] In order to prepare for the formal establishment of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and to begin introducing Soviet nation-building experience and other work related to enhancing friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples, the Secretariat of the Preparatory Committee of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has decided to temporarily establish the following institutions: 1. Secretariat: In charge of general affairs, finance, documentation, personnel, and other work, with Huan Xiang as Director and Lai Yali as Deputy Director. 2. Organization Department: In charge of developing membership, guiding local branches, and other work, with Qian Junrui concurrently serving as Director, and Wang Kunlun and Xin Zhichao as Deputy Directors. 3. Liaison Department: In charge of various contacts with the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, receiving guests, organizing visits, and other liaison work, with Xiao San as Director, and Ding Zan, Chen Jiakang, Lu Cui, and Ge Baoquan as Deputy Directors. 4. Research and Publishing Department: In charge of research, editing, translation, and publishing work, with Zhang Zhongshi as Director, and Cao Jinghua, Shen Zhiyuan, and Fan Hong as Deputy Directors. 5. Service Department: In charge of organizing exhibitions, soirees, film screenings, managing clubs and reading rooms, running Russian language night schools, subscribing to Soviet books and newspapers, and other work, with Yan Baohang as Director, and Cao Yu and Li Dequan as Deputy Directors. 6. Library and Archives, with Wang Zhixiang as Director.

### 中苏友好协会筹委会  干事会机构人选确定  开始进行苏联建国经验介绍及有关增进两国人民友谊工作

1949-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友好协会筹委会
    干事会机构人选确定
    开始进行苏联建国经验介绍及有关增进两国人民友谊工作
    【平市讯】中苏友好协会筹委会干事会为了筹备正式成立中苏友好协会，并开始进行苏联建国经验之介绍及其他有关增进中苏两国人民友谊的工作，决定暂设以下各种机构：一、秘书处：掌理总务、财务、文书、人事等工作，以宦乡为主任，赖亚力为副主任。二、组织部：掌理发展会员、指导各地分会等工作，由钱俊瑞兼主任，王昆仑、辛志超为副主任。三、联络部：掌理与苏联对外文化协会各种联系、招待来宾、组织参观及其他联络工作，以萧三为主任，丁瓒、陈家康、陆璀、戈宝权为副主任。四、研究出版部：掌理研究、编辑、翻译及出版等工作，以张仲实为主任，曹靖华、沈志远、樊弘为副主任。五、服务部：掌理组织展览会、晚会、演放电影，管理俱乐部、阅览室，办俄文夜校，代订苏联书报杂志等工作，以阎宝航为主任，曹禺、李德全为副主任。六、图书资料室，以王之相为主任。
