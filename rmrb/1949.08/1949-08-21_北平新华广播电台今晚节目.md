Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-08-21
Page: 1

　　Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Program
    [This Newspaper's News] Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station's speech program from 7:50 PM to 8:10 PM will feature a recording of the summary report by Peng Zhen, Secretary of the Beiping Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, at the Beiping Municipal People's Representative Conference. From 7:30 PM to 7:50 PM, the worker's time will discuss how all specific work of the trade union must proceed from production, based on the lessons of the Shenyang Smelting Plant. From 10:20 PM to 10:50 PM, the arts program will feature the singing of new songs by the choir from the five districts of the Beiping Municipal Middle and High School Summer Camp.

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-08-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚七点五十分到八点十分演讲节目，中共北平市委书记彭真在北平市各界代表会议上的总结报告录音。七点三十分到七点五十分职工时间，根据沈阳冶炼厂的教训，谈谈工会的一切具体工作必须从生产出发。十点二十分到十点五十分文艺节目，由北平市大中学生暑期学园五个区的歌咏队演唱新歌曲。
