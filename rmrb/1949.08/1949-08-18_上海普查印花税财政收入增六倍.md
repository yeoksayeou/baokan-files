Title: Shanghai Census Increases Stamp Tax Revenue Sixfold
Author:
Date: 1949-08-18
Page: 4

　　Shanghai Census Increases Stamp Tax Revenue Sixfold
    [Shanghai News] The Shanghai Direct Taxation Bureau's census of stamp tax work for more than twenty days has increased fiscal revenue sixfold (before the inspection, the average daily tax revenue was 6 million yuan; after the inspection, the average daily revenue increased to more than 36 million yuan). This has enabled the vast majority of industrialists and business owners to correctly understand the people's government's serious and responsible attitude towards tax work.

### 上海普查印花税财政收入增六倍

1949-08-18
第4版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　上海普查印花税财政收入增六倍
    【上海讯】上海市直接税局对印花税工作经二十余日普查，使财政收入增加六倍（未检查前每日平均纳库税款六百万元，检查后平均每日增至三千六百余万元。）广大工商业主从而正确认识了人民政府对税收工作的认真负责态度。
