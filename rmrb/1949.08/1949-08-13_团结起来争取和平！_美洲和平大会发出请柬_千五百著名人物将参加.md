Title: Unite to Strive for Peace! The American Peace Congress Sends Out Invitations - 1,500 Prominent Figures to Participate
Author:
Date: 1949-08-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 12th: TASS, New York, 10th: The Secretary-General of the American Peace Congress, Delano, announced that approximately 1,500 prominent figures from the United States and other countries will participate in the work of the Congress, scheduled to be held in Mexico City on September 5th. According to reports, the chairmen of the Congress, the poet Martinier and the president of the Latin American Labor Federation, Toledano, jointly announced that Americans supporting the Congress include Wallace, Chaplin, Thomas Mann, Robeson, astronomy professor Shapley, and 200 other prominent figures. Latin American initiators include the Chilean poet Mistral and other prominent figures. Invitations to convene the Congress have already been sent out. The invitation points out: The purpose of our convening the American Congress in support of peace is that the people of the Americas unite to express their desire for peace, will, and strength.

### 团结起来争取和平！  美洲和平大会发出请柬  千五百著名人物将参加

1949-08-13
第3版()
专栏：

    团结起来争取和平！
    美洲和平大会发出请柬
    千五百著名人物将参加
    【新华社北平十二日电】塔斯社纽约十日讯：美洲和平大会秘书长德兰诺宣布：约有一千五百位著名人物来自美国及其他国家，参加定于九月五日在墨西哥城召开的大会工作。据报载，大会主席——诗人马丁尼亚和拉丁美洲劳工联合会主席托列达诺联合宣布：支持大会的美国人有华莱士、卓别麟、汤玛斯曼、罗伯逊、天文学教授沙普莱和其他两百位著名人物。拉丁美洲的发起人有智利女诗人米斯德拉尔和其他一些著名人物。召开大会的请柬业已发出。请贴内指出：美洲人民团结起来表示他们渴望和平的意志和力量，这就是我们召开美洲拥护和平大会的目的。
