Title: Celebrating International Youth Solidarity: Over Ten Thousand Young People to Gather Today
Author:
Date: 1949-08-28
Page: 4

　　Celebrating International Youth Solidarity
  　Over Ten Thousand Young People to Gather Today
    [Our Newspaper's News] The Preparatory Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of China in Beiping Municipality, the Preparatory Committee of the Beiping Municipal Democratic Youth Federation, and the Beiping Municipal Student Union, three youth organizations, are jointly hosting a celebration rally to celebrate international youth solidarity. It is scheduled to be held today (the 28th) at the large sports field of Huiwen Middle School. Over ten thousand young people from all walks of life will participate in the rally. After the opening ceremony, there will be performances such as the Yangko dance by young workers, bayonet exercises by young soldiers, model glider demonstrations by young students, and a tug-of-war competition. In the evening, Comrade Qian Junrui will give a speech, and a film will be shown for entertainment.

### 庆祝国际青年大团结　万余青年今集会

1949-08-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　庆祝国际青年大团结
  　万余青年今集会
    【本报讯】中国新民主主义青年团北平市筹委会、北平市民主青联筹委会和北平市学联等三个青年团体，为了庆祝国际青年的大团结所主办的庆祝大会，定于今日（二十八日）假汇文中学大操场举行，大会将有各界青年万余人参加。开幕式之后，有青年工人的大秧歌舞、青年军人的劈刺表演、青年学生的滑翔模型机表演和拔河比赛等节目。晚上有钱俊瑞同志讲演，并放映电影助兴。
