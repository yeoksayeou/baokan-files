Title: All-India Peace Conference to be Held in November
Author:
Date: 1949-08-04
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, August 3rd: TASS, Delhi, 2nd: The All-India Trade Union Congress passed a resolution to convene an All-India Peace Conference in November.

### 全印和平大会十一月间召开

1949-08-04
第3版()
专栏：

    全印和平大会十一月间召开
    【新华社北平三日电】塔斯社德里二日电，全印职工大会通过关于在十一月间召开全印和平大会的决议。
