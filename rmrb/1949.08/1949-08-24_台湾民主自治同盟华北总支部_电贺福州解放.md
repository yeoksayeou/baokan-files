Title: Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League North China General Branch Cables Congratulations on the Liberation of Fuzhou
Author:
Date: 1949-08-24
Page: 1

　　Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League North China General Branch
  　Cables Congratulations on the Liberation of Fuzhou
    [This newspaper dispatch] According to Xinhua News Agency: The North China General Branch of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League cabled congratulations on the liberation of Fuzhou. The congratulatory telegram stated: The complete liberation of Fuzhou leaves Taiwan completely exposed to the advancing spearhead of the victorious Southern Expeditionary Army, which will be a favorable beginning for the liberation of Taiwan. We vow, under the wise leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, to assist the People's Liberation Army in wiping out the remnants of the enemy who are甘当困兽, liberating all of Taiwan, and avenging the February 28th Incident!

### 台湾民主自治同盟华北总支部　电贺福州解放

1949-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　台湾民主自治同盟华北总支部
  　电贺福州解放
    【本报讯】据新华社电：台湾民主自治同盟华北总支部电贺福州解放。贺电中称：福州的全部解放，使台湾完全暴露在南征胜利军的进军矛头之前，这将成为解放台湾的有利开端。我们誓在中国共产党的英明领导下，协助人民解放军，将甘当困兽之残敌消灭干净，解放全台湾，以雪二、二八之仇！
