Date: 1949-08-03
Page: 6

    Selected Poems of Warriors
    (I) Charge Style  Machine Gun Company of the Guard Battalion  Li Weifeng
    Charge style, da-da-da, fires bursts, the enemy is scared, in the daytime, I carry it, at night, I hug it, I wipe it every day, it and I, are of one mind, to the battlefield, to kill the enemy, mouth open, great strength, the Kuomintang, cried for its mama, and laid down all its weapons.
    (II) Hand Grenade  (Same as above)
    Hand grenade, big head, I carry it every day, it fears nothing, on the battlefield, truly brave, seeing the enemy, eyes turn red, tongue sticking out, it explodes, the Kuomintang, is scared, those bombed, heads split open, and can no longer, be reactionary!
    (III) Hydraulic Press  Machine Gun Company of the Guard Battalion  Han Lianyi
    Hydraulic press, American style, Chiang Kai-shek, sent it, I take it, use it, to oppose feudalism, and oppose imperialism, it sees the enemy, throws a tantrum, mouth jabbering, the enemy is knocked down, belly drinking water, the enemy is completely surrounded.

### 战士诗选

1949-08-03
第6版()
专栏：

    战士诗选
    （一）冲锋式  警卫营机炮连  栗维凤
    冲锋式，哒哒哒，打连发，敌害怕，在白天，我背它，到晚上，我抱它，我一天，一擦它，我和它，一条心，到战场，把敌杀，嘴一张，气力大，国民党，叫了妈，把武器全放下。
    （二）手榴弹  （同前）
    手榴弹，脑袋大，我天天，背着它，什么事，它不怕，在战场，真勇敢，见敌人，红了眼，舌一伸，就爆炸，国民党，就害怕，被炸的，头开花，再不能，反动啦！
   （三）水压机  警卫营机炮连  韩连义
    水压机，美国式，蒋介石，送来的，我拿它，有用它，反封建，又反帝，它见敌，发脾气，嘴里呱呱叫，敌人被打倒，肚里一喝水，敌人全被围。
