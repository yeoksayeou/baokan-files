Title: Important Correction
Author:
Date: 1949-08-28
Page: 1

　　Important Correction
    In the article "Concerning the Destruction of the Longjiabao Mine, the CCP Chahar Provincial Committee's Self-Criticism and Decision on Handling" published on page two of this newspaper on the 27th, the sentence at the beginning, "The CCP Chahar Provincial Committee recently conducted a profound self-criticism of the serious damage suffered by the Longjiabao Mine of the Longyan Iron Mine," should be followed by "and made a decision on handling the incident on August 20th." We hereby solemnly correct this.

### 重要补正

1949-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　重要补正
    二十七日本报二版所载“关于龙家堡矿场被毁事件，中共察哈尔省委的检讨和处理的决定”一文，开头“中共察哈尔省委顷对龙烟铁矿龙家堡矿场漕受严重破坏事件，作了深刻的检讨”句下，应为“并于八月二十日作出对该事件处理的决定”。特此郑重补正。
