Title: Preparatory Committee of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Cables Back to the German-Soviet Friendship Association, Expressing Sincere Gratitude for Friendship and Congratulations. All Circles in Lüda Celebrate the Fourth Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Alliance

Author:

Date: 1949-08-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 15th -- The Preparatory Committee of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association received a congratulatory telegram from Jürgen Kuczynski, Chairman of the German-Soviet Friendship Association. The telegram reads:

“To Madam Sun Yat-sen, Preparatory Committee of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association:

The German-Soviet Friendship Association extends its heartfelt congratulations to you and your organization.

All members of this association watch with great excitement the heroic struggle of the Chinese people against the enemies of peace and progress, and wish the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association success.

　　　　　　　　Chairman of the German-Soviet Friendship Association, Jürgen Kuczynski”

The Preparatory Committee of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association immediately telegraphed Jürgen Kuczynski, expressing gratitude. The telegram reads: "To Mr. Jürgen Kuczynski, Chairman of the German-Soviet Friendship Association: We sincerely thank you for your congratulations and are willing to unite with you to strive for the further development and consolidation of the world's democratic and peaceful camp headed by the Soviet Union.

　　　　　　　　Chairwoman of the Preparatory Committee of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Soong Ching Ling."

Xinhua News Agency, Dalian, 15th -- On the 14th, major enterprises, factories, organizations, schools, and groups in the Lüda area held seminars, recollection meetings, entertainment gatherings, etc., to commemorate the fourth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship and Alliance. Speakers at these meetings unanimously emphasized the great significance of this treaty for safeguarding peace in the Far East and the world, and unanimously agreed that only the Soviet Union is the friend of the Chinese people. The Dalian "People's Daily," the organ of the CPC Lüda District Committee, published an editorial entitled "Lofty Friendship, Loyal Assistance" and a special commemorative issue to express its celebration.

### 中苏友好协会筹委会　电复德苏友好协会衷诚感谢友谊祝贺  旅大各界欢庆中苏同盟四周年

1949-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏友好协会筹委会
  　电复德苏友好协会衷诚感谢友谊祝贺
    旅大各界欢庆中苏同盟四周年
    【新华社北平十五日电】中苏友好协会筹委会接到德国德苏友好协会主席尤尔耿·库庆斯基贺电一则。电称：
    “中苏友好协会筹备委员会孙逸仙夫人：
    德苏友好协会特向您和您的组织致衷心的祝贺。
    本会全体会员以莫大的兴奋，注视中国人民反对和平与进步之敌人的英勇斗争，并祝中苏友好协会之成功。
  　　　　　　　　德苏友好协会主席尤尔耿·库庆斯基”
    中苏友好协会筹委会当即致电尤尔耿·库庆斯基，表示感谢，电称：“德苏友好协会主席尤尔耿·库庆斯基先生：我们衷诚地感谢你的祝贺，并愿和你们团结一致，为进一步发展与巩固以苏联为首的世界民主和平阵营而奋斗。
  　　　　　　　　中苏友好协会筹委会主任宋庆龄”。
    【新华社大连十五日电】十四日旅大地区各大企业工厂、机关、学校、团体，纷纷举行座谈会、回忆会、娱乐会等，纪念中苏友好同盟条约签订四周年。这些会议的发言者一致强调这个条约对保卫远东及世界和平的重大意义，并一致认为只有苏联才是中国人民的朋友。中共旅大区党委机关报大连“人民日报”特以“崇高的友谊忠诚的援助”为题发表社论，并出纪念专刊，表示庆祝。
