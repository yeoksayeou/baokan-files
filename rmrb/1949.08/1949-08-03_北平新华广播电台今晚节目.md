Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author: 
Date: 1949-08-03
Page: 1

[This newspaper report] Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station tonight at 19:30 Youth Time, introduces the activities of the Qiqihar Railway Youth League, 19:50 lecture program, 22:20 cultural program is the Hebei-Shandong-Henan Folk Art Association performing local Luozihu "Liang Shiying Sacrifices Herself to Kill the Enemy."

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚十九点三十分青年时间，介绍齐齐哈尔铁路青年团的活动，十九点五十分演讲节目，二十二点二十分文艺节目是冀鲁豫民间艺术联合会演唱地方落子“梁世英舍身杀敌。”
