Title: Shanghai Daxin Steel Plant Produces Steel
Author:
Date: 1949-08-25
Page: 1

　　Shanghai Daxin Steel Plant Produces Steel
    [Shanghai People's Broadcasting Station, 24th broadcast] Shanghai News: The publicly-owned Daxin Steel Plant resumed work on the 22nd after being reorganized by the Heavy Industry Department. By the 23rd, it had produced a total of three tons of four-inch steel ingots. The foundry department is rushing to make models and is expected to start casting iron work before the 25th.

### 沪大鑫钢铁厂出钢

1949-08-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　沪大鑫钢铁厂出钢
    【上海人民广播电台二十四日广播】上海消息：公营大鑫钢铁厂经重工业处整理后，已在二十二号复工。至二十三号止，已生产四英寸钢锭共三吨。铸铁部正在赶制模型，二十五号前可开始铸铁工作。
