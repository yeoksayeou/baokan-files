Title: This Newspaper Begins Serializing "A History of American Aggression Against China" Today
Author: 
Date: 1949-08-26
Page: 1

　　This Newspaper Begins Serializing
  　"A History of American Aggression Against China" Today
    This book is a recent work by Comrade Liu Danian, Deputy Director of the History Research Office of North China University. The entire book is divided into four parts, totaling more than 50,000 words, and systematically exposes the essence of American imperialism's aggression against China from a historical perspective. Starting today, it will be published serially on page six of this newspaper.

### 本报自今日起连载　“美国侵华史”

1949-08-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　本报自今日起连载
  　“美国侵华史”
    该书为华北大学历史研究室副主任刘大年同志近著。全书分四部分，共五万余言，从历史上有系统地揭发美帝国主义侵华的本质。自今日起，在本报第六版连续刊载。
