Title: Printed Matter, etc., Can Be Sent to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1949-08-21
Page: 4

　　Printed Matter, etc., Can Be Sent to the Soviet Union

　　[Our Newspaper] From today, all post offices in this city will begin accepting newspapers, books, and printed matter addressed to the Soviet Union and the new democratic countries of Eastern Europe. All such mail will be forwarded via Harbin and Siberia. (Public Service Group, Beiping Postal Administration)

### 印刷品等可寄苏联
北平邮政管理局公众服务组
1949-08-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　印刷品等可寄苏联
    【本报讯】本市各邮局自即日起开始收寄寄往苏联及东欧各新民主主义国家的新闻纸和书籍、印刷品，该项邮件一律发由哈尔滨经西伯利亚转递。（北平邮政管理局公众服务组）
