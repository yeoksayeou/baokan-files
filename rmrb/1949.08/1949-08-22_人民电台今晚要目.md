People's Radio Tonight's Programs

　　18:35-18:50 Youth Time: Reports on youth activities in various regions. 18:50-19:10 Entertainment: The North China People's Art Troupe Orchestra performs orchestral pieces. 19:10-19:30 Workers' Time: Introduction to the life of Soviet workers: (1) Soviet female workers, (2) Soviet agricultural workers. 19:30-19:50 Children's Time: Speeches and songs by children from the central primary school in the Sixth District of this city. 20:10-20:30 Commentary - Voice of the Municipality: Broadcasting of a recording of the summary report by Comrade Peng Zhen, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, at the Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference.

### 人民电台今晚要目

1949-08-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　人民电台今晚要目
    一八、三五—一八、五○青年时间：报告各地青年动态。一八、五○—一九、一○文娱活动：华北人民文艺工作团管弦乐队演奏管弦乐曲。一九、一○—一九、三○职工时间：介绍苏联工人生活：（一）苏联的女工，（二）苏联的农业工人。一九、三○—一九、五○儿童时间：由本市六区中心小学的小朋友们讲话和唱歌。二○、一○—二○、三○言论市政之声：播送中共北平市委书记彭真同志在北平市各界代表会议上总结报告的录音。
