Title: Northeast China Recruits Professors and Technical Personnel in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1949-08-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, August 23rd. Following the arrival of the Northeast Recruitment Delegation in Shanghai on the 12th, led by Feng Zhongyun, Chairman of Songjiang Province and President of Harbin Institute of Technology, preparations are underway to recruit a large number of university professors and personnel skilled in science, technology, agriculture, medicine, literature and art to go to Northeast China to participate in cultural and educational and economic construction work. The Shanghai China Technical Association and the Shanghai Branch of the China Association of Scientific Workers have begun registering technical personnel since the 15th to introduce various technical personnel to participate in inland production and construction.

### 东北在沪招聘教授技术人材

1949-08-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　东北在沪招聘教授技术人材
    【新华社上海二十三日电】东北招聘团在松江省主席兼哈尔滨工业大学校长冯仲云率领下于十二日抵达上海后，正准备招聘大批大学教授和理、工、农、医、文艺、科学技术人材赴东北参加文化教育和经济建设工作。上海中国技术协会、中国科学工作者协会上海分会为介绍各种技术人员参加内地生产建设，已自十五日起举办技术人材登记。
