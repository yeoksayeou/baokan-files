Title: Shortened Vehicle Turnover Rate in Railway Bureaus of the Ministry of Railways Indicates Increased Productivity of People's Railways
Author:
Date: 1949-08-10
Page: 1

    The improvement of productivity in railway enterprises is mainly manifested in the increase of transportation capacity and the reduction of transportation costs, and its concentrated manifestation is the shortening of vehicle turnover rate. Because vehicle turnover rate refers to the number of days each freight car takes from its first loading to its second loading. The shorter the turnover rate, the greater the vehicle utilization efficiency, the more freight cars are saved, and the lower the transportation cost. In the past, during the Kuomintang period, the railway was a tool for exploiting the people. It only desperately increased freight rates to satisfy the private pockets of bureaucratic capitalists, and did not pay attention to improving transportation efficiency, so it never calculated vehicle turnover rates.

    Since the railway business has been under the management of the people, the awareness of all employees has been raised, and a high degree of enthusiasm and creativity has been brought into play, the railway transportation capacity has been rapidly enhanced, and the vehicle turnover rate has been gradually shortened. ### 铁道部各路局车辆周转率缩短  标志人民铁道生产力提高

1949-08-10
第1版()
专栏：

    铁道部各路局车辆周转率缩短
    标志人民铁道生产力提高
    【本报讯】铁道企业的生产力的提高，主要表现在于运输能力增加和运输成本的减低，而其集中表现则在于缩短车辆周转率。因为车辆周转率，是指每一货车自第一次装车到第二次装车所经天数。周转率愈短，表示车辆运用效率愈大，节省了车皮，运输成本随之减低。过去国民党时期，铁路是剥削人民的工具，只在运价方面拚命提高以满足官僚资本家的私囊，不讲究提高运输效能，所以也从不计算车辆周转率。
    铁道事业自从归属人民自己管理后，由于全体员工觉悟提高，发挥了高度的积极性和创造性，铁路运输能力迅速增强，车辆周转率遂逐渐缩短。兹将东北总局所属五个铁路管理局车辆周转率列表如下：局别＼月份　1　　　　2　　　　3　　　　4　　　　5　　　　6　　　　7哈尔滨局　　3．3　　2．8　　3．2　  3．08　　2．92　　3．01　　2．89齐齐哈尔局　2．3　　2．5　　2．4　　2．10　  2．01　　2．12　　1．82吉林局　  　3．6　　3．4　　2．7　  2．31　  2．35　  2．23　　2．33沈阳局　  　3．8　　3　3　　2．9　　3．06　　2．53　  2．41　　2．45锦州局　　　3．7　　2．4　　2．4　  2．01　  2．06　  2．38　　1．78东北总局　　5．4　　4．9　　4．6　　4．30　　3．86　　3．90　　3．44
