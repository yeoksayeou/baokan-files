Title: Northeast Mail Delivered to Guannei by Sea
Author:
Date: 1949-08-18
Page: 1

　　Northeast Mail Delivered to Guannei by Sea
    [Shenyang People's Broadcasting Station, 17th Broadcast] Shenyang News: Before the restoration and opening of the Beining Railway, mail to Guannei has been sent by the Northeast Postal and Telecommunications Administration through the Northeast Trading Steamship Company, by sea to Tianjin, and then forwarded to various parts of Guannei. On the 12th and 16th of this month, a total of more than 1.96 million letters and 428 packages were sent.

### 东北邮件由海路寄递关内

1949-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　东北邮件由海路寄递关内
    【沈阳人民广播电台十七日广播】沈阳消息：在北宁铁路没有修复通车前，寄往关内的邮件，已经由东北邮电管理总局通过东北贸易轮船公司，由海路发到天津，再转寄关内各地，本月十二日和十六日两次，一共发出信件一百九十六万多封，包裹四百二十八件。
