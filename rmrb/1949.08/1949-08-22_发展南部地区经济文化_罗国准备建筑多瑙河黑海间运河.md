Title: Romania Prepares to Build Danube-Black Sea Canal to Develop Southern Region's Economy and Culture
Author:
Date: 1949-08-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 21st: Bucharest dispatch, Romanian News Agency reported: Preparations for the construction of the Danube-Black Sea Canal have begun. The construction of the canal was decided by the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party and the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic. The construction of the canal is of great importance. This canal will guarantee rapid and inexpensive transportation, the industrialization of the southern region of the country and its economic and cultural development; create conditions for improving agricultural production and raising the living standards of farmers; facilitate the fight against frequent droughts in southern Romania; improve sanitary conditions in the malaria-ridden Calarasi (western Romania) marsh area, and improve the health of the people in the area. Thousands of people are now participating in the preparatory work for the excavation of the canal.

### 发展南部地区经济文化　罗国准备建筑多瑙河黑海间运河

1949-08-22
第3版()
专栏：

　　发展南部地区经济文化
  　罗国准备建筑多瑙河黑海间运河
    【新华社北平二十一日电】布加勒斯特讯，罗马尼亚通讯社报道：建筑多瑙河—黑海运河的准备工作业已开始。开筑运河系罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会政治局及罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议所决定的。运河的建筑具有非常大的重要性。这条运河将保证迅速与廉价的运输，国家南部地区的工业化及其经济与文化的发展；创造改进农业生产和提高农民生活水平的条件；便于与罗马尼亚南部经常受到旱灾作斗争；改善疟疾流行的卡拉斯（罗国西部）沼泽地区的卫生条件，增进该区人民的健康。开凿运河的准备工作现已有成千人参加进行。
