Title: At the China Day Conference, the Chinese youth's struggle experience was praised as a good example for the world's youth
Author:
Date: 1949-08-29
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 28th: Budapest Dispatch: The 25th was China Day as stipulated by the World Youth and Student Festival. A conference was held at the Hungarian Capital Sports Palace at 5:00 PM, attended by 2,500 people. Speakers at the meeting unanimously agreed that the Chinese youth's struggle experience is a good example for the youth of the world. China's victory is not only the victory of the Chinese people but also the victory of the people of the world. Xiao Hua, deputy head of the Chinese delegation, reported at the conference on the recent victorious advance of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and representatives from more than thirty countries rushed to present him with flowers. The meeting adjourned at 7:30 PM.
　　The Chinese Youth Delegation, at the invitation of the Soviet Youth Delegation, visited Soviet youth on the 19th. Representatives of the two countries' youth held a free and lively meeting. K. K. Kochomarov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, personally welcomed the young Chinese fighters. Xiao Hua, the deputy head of the Chinese delegation, thanked the Soviet youth, saying: The Chinese youth will certainly strive to enhance the eternal friendship between the Chinese and Soviet youth. After exchanging gifts, the Chinese and Soviet youth talked and sang happily together.

### 中国日大会上　中国青年的斗争经验被誉为世界青年好榜样

1949-08-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国日大会上
  　中国青年的斗争经验被誉为世界青年好榜样
    【新华社布拉格二十八日电】布达佩斯讯，二十五日是世界青年与学生联欢大会所规定的中国日。下午五时在匈京体育宫举行大会，到会的有二千五百人。会上的发言者一致认为中国青年的斗争经验是全世界青年的好榜样。中国的胜利，不仅是中国人民的胜利，也是全世界人民的胜利。中国代表团副团长萧华在大会上报告了中国人民解放军最近胜利的进军，有三十几个国家的代表拥向他献花。七时半散会。
    中国青年代表团曾于十九日应苏联青年代表团的邀请，访问了苏联青年。两国青年代表举行了自由活泼的会晤。苏联共产主义青年团中央书记柯乔马佐夫亲自欢迎中国年青战斗员们。中国代表团副团长萧华向苏联青年答谢时说：中国青年一定努力增进中苏青年之间的永恒友谊。中苏两国青年在交换礼物后，又在一起愉快地谈心和歌唱。
