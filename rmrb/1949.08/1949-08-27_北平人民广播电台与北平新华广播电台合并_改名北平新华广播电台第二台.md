Title: Merger of Peiping People's Broadcasting Station and Peiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station, Renamed Peiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Second Station
Author:
Date: 1949-08-27
Page: 1

　　Merger of Peiping People's Broadcasting Station and Peiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
    Renamed Peiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Second Station
    Peiping People's Broadcasting Station will merge with Peiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station starting from September 1st; it will be renamed Peiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Second Station, and the original Peiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station will be the First Station. The Second Station's wavelengths are 353 meters, 850 kilocycles; 222 meters, 1350 kilocycles. The opening and closing music for the morning program is "The East is Red," and the opening and closing music for the noon and evening programs is "I Love New China." The full-day program is as follows:
    Morning: 7:00-7:15 Opening music, report on the morning program. 7:15-7:30 Western music. 7:30-7:45 Joint broadcast of the First Station's first Mandarin news. 7:45-8:00 First local news. 8:00-8:15 Time check, Peking Opera. 8:15-8:30 Report on market prices. 8:30-8:45 Preview of the evening program, Cantonese music, closing music. 8:30-9:00 Joint broadcast of the First Station's arts and entertainment program (only on Sundays and holidays, followed by a preview of the evening program after the arts and entertainment program).
    Noon: 12:00-12:15 Opening music, report on the noon program. 12:15-12:30 Peking Opera. 12:30-12:45 Joint broadcast of the First Station's second Mandarin news. 12:45-13:00 Cantonese music. 13:00-13:30 Time check, storytelling, local variety show. 13:30-13:45 Preview of the evening program, closing music.
    Evening: 17:00-17:15 Opening music, report on the evening program. 17:15-17:30 Women's Time (Tuesdays, Saturdays), Children's Time (Mondays, Wednesdays, Thursdays, Fridays, Sundays). 17:30-17:45 Peking Opera. 17:45-18:00 Workers' Time (Mondays, Wednesdays, Thursdays, Saturdays), Youth Time (Tuesdays, Fridays, Sundays). 18:00-18:15 Time check, music program (Mondays, Wednesdays, Fridays, Sundays). New song practice (Tuesdays, Thursdays, Saturdays). 18:15-18:30 Joint broadcast of the First Station's workers' (Tuesdays, Fridays, Sundays), youth (Wednesdays, Saturdays), women's (Mondays, Thursdays) rotating programs. 18:30-19:00 Joint broadcast of the First Station's first arts and entertainment program. 19:00-19:15 Joint broadcast of the First Station's lecture on social sciences or natural science knowledge. 19:15-19:30 Local variety show. 19:30-19:45 Second local news. 19:45-20:00 Western music. 20:00-20:15 Time check, commentary, Voice of the Municipality. 20:15-20:30 Listener service (Tuesdays, Saturdays), Peking Opera (Mondays, Wednesdays, Thursdays, Fridays, Sundays). 20:30-21:30 Joint broadcast of the First Station's third Mandarin news, international current affairs, commentary or comprehensive report, speech or newsletter. 21:30-21:45 Cantonese music. 21:45-22:00 Preview of tomorrow's program, closing music.

### 北平人民广播电台与北平新华广播电台合并  改名北平新华广播电台第二台

1949-08-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平人民广播电台与北平新华广播电台合并
    改名北平新华广播电台第二台
    北平人民广播电台，自九月一日起，与北平新华广播电台合并；改名为北平新华广播电台第二台，原北平新华广播电台为第一台。第二台波长３５３公尺，８５０千周；２２２公尺，１３５０千周。早晨节目的开始曲及终了曲为“东方红”，中午和晚间节目的开始曲及终了曲为“我爱新中国”。全天节目如下：
    早晨：七、○○—七、一五开始曲，报告早晨节目。七、一五—七、三○西乐。七、三○—七、四五联播第一台第一次普通话新闻。七、四五—八、○○第一次本市新闻。八、○○—八、一五对时，平剧。八、一五—八、三○报告行情。八、三○—八、四五预告晚间节目，粤曲，终了曲。八、三○—九、○○联播第一台文艺节目（只限星期日及假日，文艺节目以后预告晚间节目）。
    中午：一二、○○—一二、一五开始曲，报告中午节目。一二、一五—一二、三○平剧。一二、三○—一二、四五联播第一台第二次普通话新闻。一二、四五—一三、○○粤曲。一三、○○—一三、三○对时，故事讲述，地方杂曲。一三、三○—一三、四五预告晚间节目，终了曲。
    晚间：一七、○○—一七、一五开始曲，报告晚间节目。一七、一五—一七、三○妇女时间（星期二、六），儿童时间（星期一、三、四、五、日）。一七三○—一七、四五平剧。一七、四五—一八、○○职工时间（星期一、三、四、六），青年时间（星期二、五、日）。一八、○○—一八、一五对时，音乐节目（星期一、三、五、日）。新歌练习（星期二、四、六）。一八、一五—一八、三○联播第一台职工（星期二、五、日），青年（星期三、六），妇女（星期一、四）轮回节目。一八、三○—一九、○○联播第一台第一次文艺节目。一九、○○—一九、一五联播第一台社会科学或自然科学常识讲座。一九、一五—一九、三○地方杂曲。一九、三○—一九、四五第二次本市新闻。一九、四五—二○、○○西乐。二○、○○—二○、一五对时，言论，市政之声。二○、一五—二○、三○听众服务（星期二、六），平剧（星期一、三、四、五、日）。二○、三○—二一、三○联播第一台第三次普通话新闻，国际时事，评论或综合报导，演讲或通讯。二一、三○—二一、四五粤曲。二一、四五—二二、○○预告明天节目，终了曲。
