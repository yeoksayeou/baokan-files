Title: Lenin and Stalin's Works to be Published in Polish Translations
Author:
Date: 1949-08-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 3rd: Warsaw dispatch from the Polish News Agency: The Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party has decided to immediately begin publishing Polish translations of the works of Lenin and Stalin. Lenin's works will be translated based on the latest Russian fourth edition, and Stalin's works will be translated based on the newly published Russian version. The resolution of the Polish United Workers' Party states: The Central Committee believes that educating party cadres in the spirit of Marxism-Leninism is necessary to fulfill the great historical tasks facing the Polish United Workers' Party.

### 列宁斯大林著作即在波出版译本

1949-08-04
第3版()
专栏：

    列宁斯大林著作即在波出版译本
    【新华社北平三日电】波兰通讯社华沙讯：波兰统一工人党中央委员会已决定立即开始出版列宁及斯大林著作的波兰文译本，列宁的著作根据最近的俄文第四版翻译，斯大林的著作根据现在刚出版的俄文本翻译。波统一工人党的决议说：中央委员会认为以马克思主义——列宁主义的精神来教育党的干部，是履行波兰统一工人党面临的伟大历史任务所必需的。
