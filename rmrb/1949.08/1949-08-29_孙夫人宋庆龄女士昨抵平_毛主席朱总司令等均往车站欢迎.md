Title: Madam Sun Yat-sen, Soong Ching-ling, Arrives in Beiping Yesterday; Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu De Among Those Welcoming Her at the Station
Author:
Date: 1949-08-29
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 28th—Madam Sun Yat-sen, Soong Ching-ling, arrived in Beiping from Shanghai at 4:00 PM today. Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu De, Vice Chairman Zhou, Lin Boqu, Dong Biwu, Li Jishen, He Xiangning, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Zhang Bojun, Huang Yanpei, Ma Xulun, Tan Pingshan, Liu Yazi, Peng Zemin, Cai Tingkai, Nie Rongzhen, Li Lisan, Liao Chengzhi, Cai Chang, Shen Yanbing, Li Dequan, Chen Qiyuan, Hu Yuzhi, Zhang Zhizhong, Qi Yanming, Kang Keqing, Xu Guangping, Luo Shuzhang, Shen Zijiu, and over fifty others went to the station to welcome her. After Madam Sun alighted from the train, she shook hands with each of the welcomers and accepted flowers presented by children from the Los Angeles Children's Nursery. Madam Sun departed from Shanghai on the 26th of this month, accompanied by Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chairman of the National Democratic Women's Federation, Liao Mengxing, daughter of Mr. Liao Zhongkai, and Guan Yiwen, Director of the Reception Department of the Shanghai Military Control Commission.

### 孙夫人宋庆龄女士昨抵平　毛主席朱总司令等均往车站欢迎

1949-08-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　孙夫人宋庆龄女士昨抵平
  　毛主席朱总司令等均往车站欢迎
    【新华社北平二十八日电】孙夫人宋庆龄女士已于今日下午四时自上海抵平。毛主席、朱总司令、周副主席、林伯渠、董必武、李济深、何香凝、沈钧儒、郭沫若、章伯钧、黄炎培、马叙伦、谭平山、柳亚子、彭泽民、蔡廷锴、聂荣臻、李立三、廖承志、蔡畅、沈雁冰、李德全、陈其瑗、胡愈之、张治中、齐燕铭、康克清、许广平、罗叔章、沈兹九等五十余人均往车站欢迎。孙夫人下车后，与欢迎者一一握手，并接受洛杉矶儿童保育院儿童的献花。孙夫人系于本月二十六日从上海启程，由全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超、廖仲恺先生女公子廖梦醒、上海市军管会交际处处长管易文伴送来平。
