Title: Pingliang Tianshui Military Sub-district Established
Author:
Date: 1949-08-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northwest Front, 7th. Following the liberation of Pingliang, an important city on the Xi'an-Lanzhou highway, on July 28th, the Pingliang Military Sub-district of the Gansu Military Region of the People's Liberation Army has been formally established by order and has entered the city to conduct its work. Furthermore, following the liberation of Tianshui County, a major town in southern Gansu, on August 3rd, the Tianshui Military Sub-district of the Gansu Military Region has also been established by order.

### 平凉天水军分区成立

1949-08-08
第1版()
专栏：

    平凉天水军分区成立
    【新华社西北前线七日电】西兰公路上重要城市平凉于七月二十八日获得解放后，人民解放军甘肃军区平凉军分区已奉命正式成立，并已入城办公。又陇南重镇天水县城于八月三日解放后，甘肃军区天水军分区亦已奉命成立。
