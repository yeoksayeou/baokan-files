Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-08-15
Page: 1

Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Program
[This newspaper dispatch] Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station will broadcast the following: From 7:50 PM to 8:10 PM, a speech by Liu Shanben to the remaining Kuomintang air force personnel before his departure abroad. From 7:30 PM to 7:50 PM, during Youth Time, "An Overview of Women in the Lushun and Dalian Areas" will be broadcast. From 10:20 PM to 10:50 PM, during the Arts and Entertainment program, songs commemorating "August 15th" and others will be broadcast.

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚七点五十分到八点十分演讲节目，刘善本出国前对残余国民党空军人员讲话。七点三十分到七点五十分青年时间，播送“旅顺大连地区妇女概况”。十点二十分到十点五十分文艺节目，播送纪念“八一五”等歌曲。
