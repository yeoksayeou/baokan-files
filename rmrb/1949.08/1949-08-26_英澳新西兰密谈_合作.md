Title: Britain, Australia, New Zealand in Secret Talks on "Cooperation"
Author:
Date: 1949-08-26
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, The Hague, August 23rd: Dutch newspapers published news from Australia stating that secret talks on the so-called "Southwest Pacific Defense" have begun in Melbourne. Representatives from Britain, Australia, and New Zealand are negotiating a so-called "cooperation" plan in this area.

### 英澳新西兰密谈“合作”

1949-08-26
第3版()
专栏：

　　英澳新西兰密谈“合作”
    【新华社北平二十五日电】塔斯社海牙二十三日讯：荷兰各报刊载澳大利亚消息称：关于所谓“西南太平洋防务”的秘密谈判，已在墨尔钵开始。英、澳及新西兰的代表正谈判这一方面的所谓“合作”计划。
