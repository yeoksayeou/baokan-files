Title: China University of Political Science and Law to Begin Enrollment

Author: 
Date: 1949-08-09
Page: 1

China University of Political Science and Law to Begin Enrollment

[Ping City News] After more than a month of preparation, the Beiping College of Political Science and Law is ready. With the approval of the North China People's Government, it has been renamed China University of Political Science and Law. Xie Juezai, Minister of Justice, has been appointed as the兼任校长 (concurrent) president, and Li Da and Zuo Zonglun as vice presidents. The university will be located at the former Chaoyang College site at Haiyuncang, East City of Beiping, and will soon begin enrollment.

China University of Political Science and Law is a specialized school that takes Marxism-Leninism-Mao Zedong Thought as its guiding principle, studies new jurisprudence, implements new legal education, and cultivates new judicial talents. Based on revolutionary needs and students' levels, it will establish several departments; and will also add necessary departments and specialized courses such as forensic medicine, prison management, and fingerprinting. The university has drafted the first phase of its teaching plan, the content of which is to study the theoretical knowledge of Marxism-Leninism-Mao Zedong Thought, the policy knowledge of New Democracy, and the legal knowledge and judicial experience of the People's Republic of China, in order to establish a new outlook on life for serving the people and a new legal perspective for serving the people's democratic dictatorship, and to enable students to master the professional knowledge and skills that meet China's actual needs.

### 中国政法大学即将招生

1949-08-09
第1版()
专栏：

    中国政法大学即将招生
    【平市讯】北平政法学院经月余筹备，业已就绪，并经华北人民政府决定，改称中国政法大学，任命司法部部长谢觉哉兼任校长，李达、左宗纶为副校长。校址设北平东城海运仓前朝阳学院旧址，即将开始招生。
    中国政法大学是以马列主义——毛泽东思想为指导原则，研究新法学，实施新的法律教育，培养新司法人材的专门学校。根据革命需要及学生程度，分设若干部；另增设必要部门及法医、监狱、指纹等专修科。该校已拟定第一期教学计划，内容为学习研究马列主义——毛泽东思想的理论知识，新民主主义的政策知识及中华人民共和国的法律知识与司法经验，以确立学员为人民服务的新人生观及为人民民主专政服务的新法律观，并使之掌握切合于中国实际需要的业务知识与技术。
