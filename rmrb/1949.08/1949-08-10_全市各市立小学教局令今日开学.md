Title: Municipal Education Bureau Orders City's Municipal Elementary Schools to Open Today
Author: Ji Qing
Date: 1949-08-10
Page: 8

    Municipal Education Bureau Orders City's Municipal Elementary Schools to Open Today
    [This newspaper reported] The Beiping Municipal Education Bureau recently announced that Beiping's municipal elementary schools (including urban and suburban areas) are scheduled to open today (the 10th), and formal classes will begin on the 15th. It was also decided that the miscellaneous fees for this semester will be ten jin of millet. (Ji Qing)

### 全市各市立小学教局令今日开学
纪清
1949-08-10
第8版()
专栏：

    全市各市立小学教局令今日开学
    【本报讯】北平市教育局顷宣布，北平市立各小学（包括市区、郊区）定于今日（十日）开学，十五日正式上课。并决定此学期杂费为十斤小米。（纪清）
