Title: Two Thousand Indian Peasants Riot, Demanding the Release of Communists
Author:
Date: 1949-08-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 2nd. TASS Delhi, July 30th: Peasant riots in India are becoming increasingly fierce. Two thousand peasants in Chavazpa Village, United Province, clashed with police in order to demand the release of several communists who had been arrested for inciting resistance against landlord oppression; a number of people were killed or injured. In Ranipa Village, East Bengal Province, police opened fire on a group of peasants, killing twelve.

### 印二千农民暴动  要求释放共产党人

1949-08-03
第3版()
专栏：

    印二千农民暴动
    要求释放共产党人
    【新华社北平二日电】塔斯社德里七月三十日讯：印度农民暴动日益剧烈。联合省夏瓦兹帕村的二千名农民为了要求释放被以鼓动反抗地主压迫的罪名而被捕的若干共产党人，与警察发生冲突，若干人死伤。在东孟加拉省的拉尼帕村，警察向一群农民开枪，农民死十二名。
