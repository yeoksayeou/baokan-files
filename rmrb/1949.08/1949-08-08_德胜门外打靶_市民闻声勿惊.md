Title: Shooting Practice Outside Deshengmen: Citizens Should Not Be Alarmed by the Sound
Author:
Date: 1949-08-08
Page: 5

    Shooting Practice Outside Deshengmen
    Citizens Should Not Be Alarmed by the Sound
    [This Newspaper's Report] The North China People's Government Public Security Brigade will be conducting shooting practice outside Deshengmen on the 8th and 9th. Citizens are requested not to be alarmed by the sound.

### 德胜门外打靶  市民闻声勿惊

1949-08-08
第5版()
专栏：

    德胜门外打靶
    市民闻声勿惊
    【本报讯】华北人民政府公安大队于八、九两日在德胜门外打靶，希市民闻声勿惊。
