Title: Shanghai-Prague Direct Communication Announcement
Author:
Date: 1949-08-23
Page: 1

　　Shanghai-Prague
  　Direct Communication Announcement
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 22nd] Shanghai International Radio Station has initiated direct communication with Prague, the capital of Czechoslovakia, starting from the 15th. This is a pioneering effort in communication between Shanghai and the new democratic countries of Eastern Europe.

### 上海布拉格　直达通报

1949-08-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　上海布拉格
  　直达通报
    【新华社上海二十二日电】上海国际无线电台已于十五日起与捷克京城布拉格直达通报。这是上海与东欧新民主国家通报的创举。
