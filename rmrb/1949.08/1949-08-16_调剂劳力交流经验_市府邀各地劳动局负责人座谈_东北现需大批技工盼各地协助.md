Title: Coordinating Labor and Exchanging Experiences: Municipal Government Invites Heads of Local Labor Bureaus to Symposium; Northeast Currently Needs Large Numbers of Technicians, Hoping for Assistance from Various Regions
Author: Cheng Jie
Date: 1949-08-16
Page: 5
    
　　Coordinating Labor and Exchanging Experiences
  　Municipal Government Invites Heads of Local Labor Bureaus to Symposium
    Northeast Currently Needs Large Numbers of Technicians, Hoping for Assistance from Various Regions
    [Reported by This Newspaper] The People's Government of Beiping Municipality invited the heads of labor bureaus from five regions – Northeast, North China, Northwest, East China, and Central Plains – who came to Beiping to attend the All-China Federation of Trade Unions' work conference, to a symposium at 5:00 PM on the 14th. Participants included the heads of the Northeast Labor General Bureau and the labor bureaus of Shenyang, Harbin, Qiqihar, Anshan, Tianjin, Shanghai, Tangshan, Shijiazhuang, Jinan, Nanjing, Hangzhou, and other cities. Comrade Liu Laifu of the Beiping Municipal Federation of Trade Unions also participated. During the meeting, issues such as the coordination of labor forces in various regions and the future exchange of experiences were discussed. Representatives from the Northeast mentioned that the Northeast currently has a labor shortage and needs a large number of miners and various technical personnel (such as in chemical industry, coal mine technology, etc.), and hope to receive assistance from various regions. Everyone hopes to use the Beiping Municipal Labor Bureau as the center to form a communication network, and for the Northeast and Beiping to introduce experiences in labor (protection) work and experiences in establishing collective contracts, etc. Specific methods for future mutual contact have also been agreed upon recently. The symposium did not end until 8:00 PM. All participating comrades also agreed to strengthen contact in the future. (Cheng Jie)

### 调剂劳力交流经验　市府邀各地劳动局负责人座谈  东北现需大批技工盼各地协助
程捷
1949-08-16
第5版()
专栏：

　　调剂劳力交流经验
  　市府邀各地劳动局负责人座谈
    东北现需大批技工盼各地协助
    【本报讯】北平市人民政府邀请来平参加全国总工会召开的工会工作会议的东北、华北、西北、华东、中原等五个地区的劳动局负责人于十四日下午五时举行座谈会。参加者包括东北劳动总局及沈、哈、齐齐哈尔、鞍山、津、沪、唐、石、济、宁、杭等市劳动局负责人，北平市总工会刘莱夫同志亦参加。会间讨论各地劳动力之调剂和今后各地经验之交流等问题。东北代表谈及现东北劳动力不足，需要大批矿工和各种技术人员（如化学工业、煤窑技术等），希各地予以协助。大家希望以平市劳动局为中心，组成通讯网，并由东北及北平介绍劳（动）保（护）工作经验、订立集体合同经验等。最近并商定今后相互联系之具体办法。座谈会直至晚八时始散。各出席同志并互相约定今后加强联络。（程捷）
