Title: Youth Academy to Organize Sports Meet
Author:
Date: 1949-08-13
Page: 8

    Youth Academy
    筹 To Organize Sports Meet
    [Our newspaper] The Summer Youth Academy is scheduled to hold a sports meet at the Temple of Agriculture at 2:00 PM on the 18th of this month. All sectors are welcome to visit and provide guidance. Preparations are currently underway.
    (Summer Youth Academy)

### 青年学园  筹开运动会

1949-08-13
第8版()
专栏：

    青年学园
    筹开运动会
    【本报讯】暑期青年学园定本月十八日下午二时在先农坛举行一次运动会，请各界参观指导。现正积极筹备中。
    （暑期青年学园）
