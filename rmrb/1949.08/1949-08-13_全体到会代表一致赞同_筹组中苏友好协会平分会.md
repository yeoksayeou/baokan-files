Title: All Delegates Unanimously Approve Preparing to Organize the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association's Beiping Branch
Author:
Date: 1949-08-13
Page: 5

    [This City News] At the Beiping Municipal People's Representative Conference on the 12th, over a hundred representatives submitted a temporary written motion to initiate preparations for organizing the Beiping Municipal Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. The motion stated: "In order to further consolidate and promote the revolutionary friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union, strengthen the political, economic, and cultural ties between the two peoples, and unite together to jointly strive to defend world peace, we support the establishment of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and propose that the conference prepare to organize the Beiping Municipal Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association." The entire body of delegates unanimously approved, and an unprecedentedly enthusiastic signature campaign was launched.

### 全体到会代表一致赞同  筹组中苏友好协会平分会

1949-08-13
第5版()
专栏：

    全体到会代表一致赞同
    筹组中苏友好协会平分会
    【本市讯】北平市各界代表会十二日会议中，代表百余人临时书面动议发起筹备组织中苏友好协会北平市分会。该动议案称：“为了进一步巩固和发扬中苏两国人民的革命友谊，加强两国人民在政治上、经济上、文化上的联系，团结一致，共同为保卫世界和平而奋斗，我们拥护成立中苏友好协会，并提议大会筹备组织中苏友好协会北平市分会。”经全体到会代表一致赞同，展开了空前热烈的签名运动。
