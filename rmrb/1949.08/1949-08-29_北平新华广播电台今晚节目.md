Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-08-29
Page: 1

　　[This News] Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station, tonight from 7:30 PM to 7:50 PM for women's time, will broadcast excerpts from a speech given by Qu Mengjue, Secretary-General of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, on August 5th at a women's federation meeting. The topic is "Our Understanding of the New China and the Efforts We Should Make." From 7:50 PM to 8:10 PM, the lecture program will feature Xia Kangnong, Standing Committee Member of the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Representative Assembly of Natural Science Workers, speaking on "The Class Nature of Natural Science" (originally scheduled for last night, postponed to tonight due to circumstances). From 10:20 PM to 10:50 PM, the arts and literature program will feature the Second Cultural Troupe of the Political Department of the Third Field Army of the People's Liberation Army performing the "Huaihai Campaign Suite."

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-08-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台，今晚十九点三十分到十九点五十分妇女时间，择要播送中华全国民主妇女联合会秘书长区梦觉八月五号在一个妇女联合会上的演讲，题目是“我们对新中国的认识和应有的努力”。十九点五十分到二十点十分演讲节目，中华全国自然科学工作者代表大会筹备会常务委员夏康农讲“自然科学的阶级性”（原定昨晚播讲，因事移至今晚）。二十二点二十分到二十二点五十分文艺节目，人民解放军第三野战军政治部第二文工团演唱“淮海战役组歌”。
