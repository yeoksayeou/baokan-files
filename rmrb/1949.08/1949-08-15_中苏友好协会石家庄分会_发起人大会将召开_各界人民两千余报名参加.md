Title: Shijiazhuang Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to Convene Inaugural Meeting; Over Two Thousand People from All Walks of Life Register to Participate
Author:
Date: 1949-08-15
Page: 1

    Shijiazhuang Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
    Inaugural Meeting to be Convened
    Over Two Thousand People from All Walks of Life Register to Participate
    [Shijiazhuang Dispatch] After the initiators of the Shijiazhuang Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association issued a notice, people from all walks of life in the city have actively registered to participate. As of August 14th, the number of participants has reached 2,168, including representatives from factories, government offices, schools, and citizens. Preparations are currently underway to convene the inaugural meeting to formally establish the preparatory committee for the Shijiazhuang City Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

### 中苏友好协会石家庄分会  发起人大会将召开  各界人民两千余报名参加

1949-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友好协会石家庄分会
    发起人大会将召开
    各界人民两千余报名参加
    【石家庄专电】中苏友好协会石家庄分会发起人发出通知后，全市各界人民纷纷报名参加，截至八月十四日止，参加者已达两千一百六十八人，包括各工厂、机关、学校和市民代表。现正准备开发起人大会，以正式成立中苏友好协会石家庄市分会筹备会。
