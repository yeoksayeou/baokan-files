Title: Plain Daily Starts Publication in Xinxiang
Author:
Date: 1949-08-23
Page: 2

　　【Xinxiang Electric】After the establishment of the Pingyuan Provincial People's Government, the former Ji-Lu-Yu Daily of the Ji-Lu-Yu District was ordered to cease publication on August 21st. On August 22nd, the Pingyuan Daily, covering the entire province, was published in Xinxiang as the Party newspaper of the Provincial Party Committee to guide the work of the province. At the same time, the Xinxiang branch of the Xinhua News Agency was also established in Xinxiang on the 21st and began sending out news稿.

### 平原日报在新乡创刊

1949-08-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　平原日报在新乡创刊
    【新乡电】平原省人民政府成立后，原冀鲁豫区的冀鲁豫日报，奉命于八月二十一日终刊。八月二十二日，在新乡出版全省范围的平原日报，作为该省省委指导全省工作的党报。同时新华社平原分社亦于二十一日在新乡成立，开始发稿。
