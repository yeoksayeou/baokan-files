Title: U.S. Suffers Third Defeat; Returns to Washington
Author:
Date: 1949-08-11
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 10th: Washington dispatch: The American warmongers – Army Chief of Staff Bradley, Naval Chief of Staff Denfeld, and Air Force Chief of Staff Vandenberg – who bear the mission of planning to use the people of Western Europe as cannon fodder for the US imperialists' aggressive wars, have flown back to Washington from Vienna on the 9th after holding talks with the military authorities of the nine countries participating in the North Atlantic Treaty Organization and inspecting US troops within Germany and Austria. Later, they will report on their trip to Europe to the Senate Armed Services Committee.

### 美国三战败  返回华盛顿

1949-08-11
第3版()
专栏：

    美国三战败
    返回华盛顿
    【新华社北平十日电】华盛顿讯：负有筹划使西欧人民充当美帝国主义发动侵略战争的炮灰的使命的美国众谋长们——陆军参谋长布莱德雷、海军参谋长邓菲尔德、空军参谋长范登堡等，在与九个参加北大西洋公约国家的军事当局举行商谈并视察德奥境内的美军之后，已于九日自维也纳飞返华盛顿。稍后，他们将向美参院军事委员会报告欧洲之行。
