Title: At the Hague "Round Table Conference," US-British-Dutch Imperialists Sharply Contend for Indonesia

Author:
Date: 1949-08-28
Page: 3

　　At the Hague "Round Table Conference"
  　US-British-Dutch Imperialists
  　Sharply Contend for Indonesia
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 26th] Hague dispatch: Negotiations at the so-called "Round Table" conference between the Netherlands and Indonesia are continuing secretly in The Hague. According to a TASS dispatch on the 25th, citing the Dutch Communist newspaper De Waarheid, American representatives are engaged in extensive activities in The Hague. "The United States hopes to use the Hague 'Round Table' conference to include Hatta and his subordinates in the Southeast Asian military bloc led by Washington." De Waarheid, the Dutch Communist newspaper, strongly asserted: A fierce struggle for Indonesia is taking place between Dutch, British, and American imperialists, each of whom wants to obtain huge profits at the expense of the Indonesian people through the "Round Table" conference.

### 海牙“圆桌会议”上  美英荷帝国主义者　争夺印尼斗争尖锐

1949-08-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　海牙“圆桌会议”上
  　美英荷帝国主义者
  　争夺印尼斗争尖锐
    【新华社北平二十六日电】海牙讯：荷兰和印尼的所谓“圆桌”会议的谈判，正继续在海牙秘密进行中。据塔斯社二十五日电引荷兰共产党真理报报道，美国代表们在海牙展开很大的活动。“美国希望借海牙‘圆桌’会议之助将哈达及其部下包括到华盛顿所领导下的东南亚军事集团里”。荷共真理报力称：荷兰、英国与美国帝国主义之间正在进行争夺印尼的尖锐斗争，这三个帝国主义每一个都想通过“圆桌”会议，以牺牲印尼人民，取得大量利润。
