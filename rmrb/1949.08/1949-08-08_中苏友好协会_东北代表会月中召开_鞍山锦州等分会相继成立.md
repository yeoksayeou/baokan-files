Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Northeast Representative Conference to be Held Mid-Month; Anshan, Jinzhou and Other Branches Established Successively
Author:
Date: 1949-08-08
Page: 1

    Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
    Northeast Representative Conference to be Held Mid-Month
    Anshan, Jinzhou and Other Branches Established Successively
    [Report from this newspaper] The Preparatory Committee of the Northeast General Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association recently issued a notice to provinces, cities, and local branches or preparatory committees of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association regarding the convocation of the Northeast Representative Conference of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, stating briefly:
    The Preparatory Committee of the Northeast General Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has decided to convene the Northeast Representative Conference of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association in Shenyang on the 14th of this month to establish the Northeast General Branch (the conference is scheduled to last for three days). Representatives from various regions are requested to arrive in Shenyang before August 13th and report to the Secretariat of the Preparatory Committee (temporarily located at the Northeast Guest House). The number of representatives selected from various regions, their names, resumes, and the heads of the delegation should be telegraphed to the Preparatory Committee before the 10th of this month. Proposals and work reports for the representative conference should also be submitted in written form to the Secretariat of the Preparatory Committee in advance. Congratulatory gifts and messages from mass organizations, government agencies, schools, and people from all walks of life for the conference should be delivered before August 12th.
    [Comprehensive report from this newspaper] Northeast Dispatch: The Anshan City branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association was officially established at the end of last month. There are now forty-two branches in the city, with 7,434 members. The Jinzhou City Sino-Soviet Friendship Association officially established its representative conference on the 28th of last month. The conference was attended by all members of the preparatory committee and 101 representatives from 28 units including government agencies, factories, schools, and mass organizations. The conference announced its future work plan: to organize branches in factories, schools, government agencies, and organizations, and to widely recruit members; to establish Russian language training classes; and to hold Soviet lectures and exhibitions. The Xi'an City branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association was officially established. At the founding meeting, it was decided to convene the first general membership meeting on August 15th. Since the Harbin City Sino-Soviet Friendship Association began recruiting members on July 11th, it has established 172 branches with over 54,000 members as of July 31st. The association decided to convene its first membership representative conference on August 7th, and a celebration party will be held that evening.

### 中苏友好协会  东北代表会月中召开  鞍山锦州等分会相继成立

1949-08-08
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友好协会
    东北代表会月中召开
    鞍山锦州等分会相继成立
    【本报讯】中苏友好协会东北总分会筹委会顷为召开中苏友好协会东北代表大会事，向各省市及各地中苏友好协会分会或筹委会发出通知，略称：
    中苏友好协会东北总分会筹委会决定于本月十四日在沈阳召开中苏友好协会东北代表大会，成立东北总分会（会期预定为三天），希各地代表于八月十三日前一律到达沈阳并向本筹委会秘书处（暂设东北招待所）报到。各地选出之代表人数、姓名、简历及代表团负责人等均请于本月十日前电告筹委。对代表大会的提案及工作报告，亦请先写成书面材料送交筹委秘书处。各地群众团体机关学校及各界人士如有赠送大会的贺礼、贺词等均请于八月十二日前送到。
    【本报综合报导】东北讯：鞍山市中苏友好协会分会，已于上月底正式成立。现全市已有四十二个支会，会员七千四百三十四名。锦州市中苏友好协会已于上月二十八日正式成立代表大会，到有全体筹委及各机关、工厂、学校、群众团体等二十八个单位的代表一百零一人。大会宣布今后工作计划：组织各工厂、学校、机关、团体的支会，广泛吸收会员；成立俄文补习班；举办苏联讲座和展览会等。中苏友好协会西安市分会正式成立。成立会上，决定“八一五”召开第一次全体会员大会。哈尔滨市中苏友好协会自七月十一日开始发展会员以来，截至七月三十一日为止，已建立起一百七十二个支会，入会者已达五万四千多人。该会决定于八月七日召开第一次会员代表大会，当晚将举行庆祝晚会。
