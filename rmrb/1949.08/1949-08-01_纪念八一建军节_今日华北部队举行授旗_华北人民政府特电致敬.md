Title: Commemorating the August 1st Army Day, North China Troops Hold Flag Presentation Ceremony Today; North China People's Government Sends Special Telegram of Respect
Author: Bai Sheng
Date: 1949-08-01
Page: 1

    Commemorating the August 1st Army Day
    North China Troops Hold Flag Presentation Ceremony Today; North China People's Government Sends Special Telegram of Respect
    [This Newspaper's Dispatch] Today, the 1st, is the founding day of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. In order to solemnly commemorate this festival, all units under the North China Military Region will hold flag presentation ceremonies and commemorative meetings in their respective garrisons. Units stationed in Beiping will hold flag presentation ceremonies by division from the 1st to the 3rd, with leaders of the North China Military Region representing the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission to present flags and review the troops. Some units will also inspect military training achievements and sports activities. People's organizations and democratic parties in Beiping will present flags and pay tribute to the People's Liberation Army units in Beiping at that time. In addition, the North China Pictorial will hold a photo exhibition of the troops at Zhongshan Park starting today, and theaters in Beiping will also provide free movie viewings for the troops today. (Bai Sheng)
    [Another Dispatch] The Suiyuan Military Region has decided to hold a grand military parade, military and political tests, and discipline inspections today, which will also be held simultaneously.
    [Beiping Dispatch] Today is the 22nd anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The North China People's Government has sent a special telegram to all commanders and fighters of the North China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army to congratulate them. The text of the telegram is as follows: To Commander Nie and Political Commissar Bo of the North China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and to all commanders, fighters, and political workers, comrades:
    Our North China People's Liberation Army liberated all of North China, enabling the people of North China to embark on peaceful construction. On the occasion of the 22nd anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, we extend our respect and heartfelt greetings to you! We wish you glorious completion of the tasks of consolidating national defense and safeguarding revolutionary order in the future.
        North China People's Government        August 1st

### 纪念八一建军节  今日华北部队举行授旗  华北人民政府特电致敬
柏生
1949-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    纪念八一建军节
    今日华北部队举行授旗
    华北人民政府特电致敬
    【本报讯】今（一）日为中国人民解放军建军节，华北军区所属各部队为了隆重纪念这一节日，均将于各该驻地举行授旗典礼和纪念会。在平各部队定于一日至三日，以师为单位分别举行授旗典礼，由华北军区首长代表中国人民革命军事委员会授旗和检阅部队。有的单位并将检查军事训练成绩、体育活动等。平市各人民团体、民主党派届时将向人民解放军在平各部队献旗致敬。此外，华北画报社将于今日起在中山公园举办部队照片展览，平市各影院今日亦免费分别招待部队观影。（柏生）
    【又讯】绥远军区决定于今日举行大阅兵、军事、政治测验及纪律检查，亦将同时举行。
    【平市讯】今日为中国人民解放军二十二周年建军节，华北人民政府特致电中国人民解放军华北军区全体指战员祝贺，电文如下：中国人民解放军华北军区聂司令员、薄政委转全体指挥员、战斗员、全体政治工作人员同志们：
    我华北人民解放军，解放了全华北，使华北人民得以着手和平建设，际此中国人民解放军二十二周年建军节，特向你们致敬并致亲切地慰问！祝你们今后光荣完成巩固国防与保卫革命秩序底任务。
        华北人民政府        八月一日
