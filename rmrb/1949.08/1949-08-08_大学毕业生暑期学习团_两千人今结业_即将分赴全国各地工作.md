Title: University Graduate Summer Study Group: 2,000 Graduates Today, Soon to Depart for Work Across the Country
Author: Bai Sheng
Date: 1949-08-08
Page: 5

    University Graduate Summer Study Group
    2,000 Graduates Today
    Soon to Depart for Work Across the Country
    [Dispatch from This Newspaper] The 2,000 students in the North China University Graduate Summer Study Group have completed their studies and have been specifically assigned work. The group is scheduled to hold a graduation ceremony at 9:00 AM today (the 8th) in the auditorium of Tsinghua University. Responsible comrades from the North China Higher Education Committee, the head of the study group, Feng Naichao, and guests from all walks of life will speak at the meeting, along with student reports on their studies. The university graduates have been studying since July 10th, for a month, with students from the Faculty of Arts and Law accounting for one-half and those from the Faculties of Science, Engineering, Agriculture, and Medicine accounting for the other half. The work assignment areas this time include Northeast China, North China, East China, and directly subordinate organs of the central government.
        (Bai Sheng)

### 大学毕业生暑期学习团  两千人今结业  即将分赴全国各地工作
柏生
1949-08-08
第5版()
专栏：

    大学毕业生暑期学习团
    两千人今结业
    即将分赴全国各地工作
    【本报讯】华北各大学毕业生暑期学习团学员二千人，学习完毕，并已具体分配工作。该团定今（八）日上午九时在清华大学礼堂举行结业典礼。会上将有华北高等教育委员会负责同志、学习团团长冯乃超以及各界来宾的讲话和学员的学习报告。按此次各大学毕业生自七月十日开始学习以来已经一月，学员中文法学院与理、工、农、医学院各占二分之一。此次分配工作地区包括东北、华北、华东等地及中央直属各机关。
        （柏生）
