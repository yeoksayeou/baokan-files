Title: Tito's Atrocities Persecute Cominform Sympathizers
Author:
Date: 1949-08-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 30th: The Tito clique is currently persecuting sympathizers of the Cominform on Yugoslav territory within Trieste. According to a report by the Trieste correspondent of the London Times, cited by TASS: Yugoslav political police are arresting supporters of the Cominform. On August 25th, Tito's military authorities in Trieste interrogated three people said to be supporters of the Cominform.

### 铁托暴行竟迫害共产党情报局同情者

1949-08-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　铁托暴行竟迫害共产党情报局同情者
    【新华社北平三十日电】铁托集团现正在的里雅斯特境内南斯拉夫区，大肆迫害共产党情报局的同情者。据塔斯社引伦敦泰晤士报驻的里雅斯特记者报道：南斯拉夫政治警察正逮捕情报局的支持者，二十五日，铁托军事当局在的里雅斯特审讯三名据说是共产党情报局支持者的人。
