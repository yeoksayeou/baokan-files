Date: 1949-08-23

　　Notice to Contributors:
    Do not write on both sides of the paper!
    Do not write in pencil!
    Do not write horizontally!
    Do not write cursive script, simplified characters, or tiny characters!
    Do not use ×× or English letters to replace names of people or places!
    Leave wider spaces between lines!
    Write numbers, names, and place names particularly clearly!
    Write table numbers in Chinese vertically!
    Indicate your mailing address and telephone number at the end of the manuscript!
    Please keep a copy of your manuscript and be sure to include postage stamps!

### 来稿注意：

1949-08-23
第6版()
专栏：

　　来稿注意：
    不要一纸两面写！
    不要用铅笔写！
    不要横写！
    不要写草字、简笔字，或芝麻大的字！
    不要用××或英文字母替代人名地名！
    行与行间空白留宽些！
    数目字、姓名、地名要特别写清楚！
    表格数字用中文直写！
    稿末注明通讯处和电话号码！
    请自留底稿，必附邮资邮票！
