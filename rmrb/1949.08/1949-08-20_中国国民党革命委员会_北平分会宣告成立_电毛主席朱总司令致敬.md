Title: Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, Peiping Branch, Announces Establishment; Sends Respects to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu

Author:

Date: 1949-08-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 19th: The Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang held a membership meeting in Peiping this month on the 14th and announced the establishment of the Peiping Branch. The conference passed a telegram to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, as follows:

Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu:

As the Chinese People's Liberation War is about to achieve national victory, we are holding a membership meeting in Peiping, the people's capital, and formally establishing the Peiping Municipal Branch. We sincerely thank the Chinese Communist Party for its correct leadership and the heroic fighting of all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, which has enabled the Chinese people to achieve great victory. We firmly adhere to Mr. Sun Yat-sen's Three Great Policies and are willing to follow our Central Committee, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, to intensify our studies, work hard, and strive to thoroughly complete the three major tasks of anti-imperialism, anti-feudalism, and anti-bureaucratic capitalism, and build a new democratic China. We extend our highest respects!

Membership Meeting of the Peiping Municipal Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, August 14th.

### 中国国民党革命委员会　北平分会宣告成立  电毛主席朱总司令致敬

1949-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国国民党革命委员会
  　北平分会宣告成立
    电毛主席朱总司令致敬
    【新华社北平十九日电】中国国民党革命委员会于本月十四日举行北平市会员大会，并宣告北平分会成立。大会通过致毛主席、朱总司令电文如下：
    毛主席朱总司令：
    当中国人民解放战争即将获得全国胜利的今天，我们在人民的首都北平举行会员大会，正式成立北平市分会。我们衷心感谢中国共产党的正确领导和中国人民解放军全体指战员的英勇奋战，使中国人民获得伟大胜利。我们坚决奉行孙中山先生的三大政策，愿跟随我们中央总会，在中国共产党领导之下，加紧学习，努力工作，为彻底完成反帝反封建反官僚资本三大任务，建设新民主主义的新中国而奋斗。谨致崇高的敬礼！
    中国国民党革命委员会北平市会员大会叩八月十四日
