Title: Staff Study Session Concludes Today
Author:
Date: 1949-08-21
Page: 4

　　Staff Study Session
  　Concludes Today
    [Our Dispatch] The municipal primary and secondary school staff summer study session is scheduled to hold a graduation ceremony at 8:00 AM today (the 21st) at the Sitsun Middle School assembly hall. Comrades Peng Zhen and Deputy Mayor Zhang Youyu, among others, will speak at the ceremony. An evening party will also be held in the afternoon. (Summer Study Session)

### 教职员学习会　今日结业
暑期学习会
1949-08-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　教职员学习会
  　今日结业
    【本报讯】本市中小学教职员暑期学习会定于今（二十一）日上午八时在四存中学总会举行结业典礼；届时将有彭真同志、张友渔副市长等讲话。午后并举行晚会。  （暑期学习会）
