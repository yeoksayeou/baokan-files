Title: Cultivating Aviation Technical Talents: Shanghai Military Control Commission Registers Students
Author:
Date: 1949-08-02
Page: 1

    Cultivating Aviation Technical Talents
    Shanghai Military Control Commission Registers Students
    [Broadcast by Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Shanghai, 1st – In order to cultivate aviation technical talents, the Aviation Department of the Shanghai Military Control Commission has been conducting registration since July 18th for graduates of aviation engineering, electrical engineering, and mechanical engineering departments from specialized schools and above, who are dedicated to the people's aviation cause. As of the 30th, 179 people have registered. The Aviation Department of the Military Control Commission has decided to hold examinations on the 1st and 5th.

### 造就航空技术人材  沪军管会登记学员

1949-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    造就航空技术人材
    沪军管会登记学员
    【北平新华广播电台广播】上海一日消息：上海市军管营航空部，为了造就航空技术人材，从七月十八日起，举办专科以上学校航空工程和电机机械等系毕业、有志于人民航空事业的学员登记。到三十号为止，前往报名的已经有一百七十九人。军管会航空部决定在一号、五号两天举行考试。
