Title: Southern Jiangxi Continuously Captures Five Cities Including Longnan; More Than One Hundred Local Bandits in Southern Shaanxi Surrender to Us
Author:
Date: 1949-08-29
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Central China Front, August 28th: In three days, from the 19th to the 21st, the People's Liberation Army on the Central China Front successively captured four cities on the border of Guangdong and Jiangxi: Longnan, Chongyi, Anyuan, and Dingnan. On the 23rd, they occupied Dajishan, a tungsten ore producing area. Prior to this, the People's Liberation Army captured Shangyou City, northwest of Ganzhou, on the 11th. Currently, with the exception of a few cities in the southeastern border of Jiangxi, such as Guangchang, Ningdu, Shicheng, Ruijin, and Xunwu, the rest of the province has been liberated.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, August 28th: More than one hundred local bandits from the Ren Xiaotu gang, entrenched in the area east of Shang County and the Xiping area in southern Shaanxi, surrendered to the People's Liberation Army on the 10th of this month, led by Deputy Regiment Commander Liu Jingchun and Battalion Commander Xiang Lianfen, handing over two light machine guns, ninety-eight rifles, and ten pistols. Furthermore, on the 12th, the People's Liberation Army advancing into western Hunan annihilated the 3rd Brigade of the bandit Temporary First Division, which was looting in the Bieshan and Baitazi area southeast of Feng County, killing and wounding some and capturing over a hundred enemy soldiers.

### 赣南连克龙南等五城　陕南股匪百余向我投诚

1949-08-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　赣南连克龙南等五城
  　陕南股匪百余向我投诚
    【新华社华中前线二十八日电】华中前线人民解放军十九日至二十一日三天中连克粤赣边境的龙南、崇义、安远、定南等四城并于二十三日占领钨砂产地大吉山。在此以前，解放军于十一日占领赣州西北的上犹城。现江西全省除东南边境的广昌、宁都、石城、瑞金、寻邬等少数城市外，其余地区均已获得解放。
    【新华社汉口二十八日电】盘据陕南商县以东及西坪地区股匪任小秃部百余人，本月十日在副团长刘景春、营长相连芬等率领下向解放军投诚，缴出轻机枪两挺，步枪九十八枝，手枪十枝。又挺进湘西的解放军十二日在沣县东南别山、白台子一带歼灭正在抢掠的股匪暂一师第三旅，除毙伤外，生俘敌百余名。
