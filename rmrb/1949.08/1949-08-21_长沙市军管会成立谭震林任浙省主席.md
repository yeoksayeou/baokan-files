Title: Changsha Military Control Commission Established, Tan Zhenlin Appointed Chairman of Zhejiang Province

Author:

Date: 1949-08-21
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, August 20th: The Changsha Military Control Commission was formally established on the 19th, by order of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission. Within the area under the jurisdiction of Changsha City, the Commission will implement the city policies formulated by the Chinese Communist Party, abide by the Eight Articles of Agreement of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, implement military control, and serve as the highest authority in the area during the period of military control, unifying military, political, economic, cultural, and other controlled matters throughout the city. Xiao Jinguang is appointed as the Director of the Military Control Commission, and Wang Shoudao and Chen Mingren are appointed as Deputy Directors. The Commission consists of fourteen members: Huang Kecheng, Xiao Jinguang, Wang Shoudao, Chen Mingren, Tang Xing, Zhou Li, Yuan Renyuan, Li Minghao, Wang Jinxin, Xie Peiran, Cao Ying, Yan Zixiang, Song Naide, and Chen Geng.
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, August 20th: The Zhejiang Provincial People's Government was established on the 19th by order. Tan Zhenlin, the Chairman of the Provincial Government, has assumed his post and begun work on this day.

### 长沙市军管会成立谭震林任浙省主席

1949-08-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　长沙市军管会成立谭震林任浙省主席
    【新华社长沙二十日电】长沙市军事管制委员会奉中国人民革命军事委员会命令，于十九日正式成立。该会在长沙市所辖区内奉行中国共产党所制定的城市政策，遵照中国人民解放军的约法八章，实行军事管制，为该区军事管制期间的最高权力机关，统一全市军事、政治、经济、文化等管制事宜。军管会由萧劲光任主任，王首道、陈明仁任副主任。委员共十四人，为：黄克诚、萧劲光、王首道、陈明仁、唐星、周礼、袁任远、李明灏、王劲信、解沛然、曹瑛、阎子祥、宋乃德、陈庚。
    【新华社杭州二十日电】浙江省人民政府已于十九日奉命成立。省政府主席谭震林已于是日到职工作。
