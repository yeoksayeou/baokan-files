Title: Famous Play "Meritorious Service" Continues to be Staged in the City
Author: Bai Sheng
Date: 1949-08-08
Page: 5

    Famous Play "Meritorious Service"
    Continues to be Staged in the City
    [This Newspaper's Dispatch] In response to the enthusiastic requests from workers in various factories and summer school students and teachers in Beiping, the Beiping City Federation of Trade Unions and the Beiping City Cultural Committee invited the Northeast Cultural and Educational Team to continue performing the famous play "Meritorious Service" depicting workers. The team has accepted the request and has been performing at the Zhongshan Park Music Hall since the 5th to entertain workers and students. It has won unanimous praise and enthusiastic support from the audience, who all believe that this is the direction for future theatrical performances. It is reported that the team has decided to continue giving several more performances to entertain workers and students.
          (Bai Sheng)

### 名剧“立功”续在本市上演
柏生
1949-08-08
第5版()
专栏：

    名剧“立功”
    续在本市上演
    【本报讯】北平市总工会与北平市文委会应北平各工厂工人与暑期学习学生、教员的热烈要求，邀请东北文教队继续演出描写工人的名剧“立功”，该队已接受要求，已于五日起在中山公园音乐堂演出，招待工人、学生。博得观众一致好评与热烈拥护，大家均认为这是今后戏剧演出的方向。闻该队决定仍继续公演数场招待工人、学生。
          （柏生）
