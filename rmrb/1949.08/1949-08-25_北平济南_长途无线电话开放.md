Title: Beiping-Jinan Long-Distance Radio Telephone Service Opens

Author: 
Date: 1949-08-25
Page: 4

　　Beiping-Jinan
  　Long-Distance Radio Telephone Service Opens
    [This Newspaper's Report] The Beiping Telecommunications Bureau announced on the 24th that the long-distance radio telephone line between Beiping and Jinan had achieved excellent results after testing. It has now been decided to open it for commercial use today (the 25th). The daily call time is from 9:00 AM to 10:00 AM. The call rate for ordinary dialing is 1,100 yuan per call.

### 北平济南　长途无线电话开放

1949-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　北平济南
  　长途无线电话开放
    【本报讯】北平电信局二十四日发表：北平济南间长途无线电话，经试验成绩良好。现已决定自今日（二十五日）开放商用。每日通话时间为上午九时至十时。话价寻常叫号，每次一千一百元。
