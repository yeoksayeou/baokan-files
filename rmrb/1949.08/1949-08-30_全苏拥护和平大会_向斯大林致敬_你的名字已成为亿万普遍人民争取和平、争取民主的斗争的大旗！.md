Title: All-Soviet Peace Conference Pays Tribute to Stalin: Your Name Has Become the Banner of Hundreds of Millions of Ordinary People in the Struggle for Peace and Democracy!
Author:
Date: 1949-08-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 29th: TASS, Moscow: At its final session, the All-Soviet Peace Conference adopted a letter of tribute to Stalin. The full text is as follows: Dear Comrade Stalin! We, the representatives of the Soviet Republic's working class, collective farmers, and intellectuals, gathered at the All-Soviet Peace Conference, sincerely extend our most sincere and warm respects to you—our teacher, the great fighter for peace and friendship among all nations.

	Your name has become the banner of hundreds of millions of ordinary people in the struggle for peace and democracy!

	You have ignited the flame in the hearts of all people around the world with your unwavering confidence in the great and just cause of the struggle for peace, national independence, and the promotion of friendship and goodwill among the peoples of all countries.

	Your persistence in the struggle for peace, your steadfastness, and your noble courage have become our example and model.

	Comrade Stalin, under the leadership of the heroic Bolshevik Party you have guided, the Soviet people defeated fascism, preserved the glory, freedom, and independence of their motherland, and saved all of humanity from the threat of shameful fascist enslavement.

	Only because of this epoch-making victory can the people of the world hold their heads high with hope, look forward to the future, and be deeply convinced that the dark forces of imperialist barbarism will ultimately be defeated.

	This explains why, in every speech defending peace delivered here, the speakers call out your name with love and gratitude.

	We are well aware that the struggle for peace is not an easy one.

	We see how the imperialists of the world, first and foremost the American imperialists, are doing everything in their power to obstruct our struggle for peace. However, no force can shake our will and our conviction, and no force can divide the people in this struggle against the warmongers and against the man-eating savages of the imperialist camp.

	We have fully resolved to dedicate all our strength to the altar of peace.

	We have gathered here in our glorious capital, Moscow, to hold a peace conference and seriously discuss our plans for struggling for peace.

	We listened with satisfaction to the speeches of our foreign friends, who assured us that the people in their countries are fully determined to carry out the struggle against the warmongers and to strengthen friendship with the great Soviet people by all means.

	We have established the Standing Committee of the All-Soviet Peace Conference and entrusted it with the lofty task of consolidating friendship among nations, exposing warmongers, and fighting for peace by all means, namely, the methods taught to us by you, our dear leader and teacher.

	Long live our motherland, the Soviet Union, the first and greatest defender of peace in the world! Long live the great Bolshevik Party that leads us to victory! Long live our beloved Comrade Stalin!

### 全苏拥护和平大会　向斯大林致敬  你的名字已成为亿万普遍人民争取和平、争取民主的斗争的大旗！

1949-08-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　全苏拥护和平大会
  　向斯大林致敬
    你的名字已成为亿万普遍人民争取和平、争取民主的斗争的大旗！
    【新华社北平二十九日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：全苏拥护和平大会在末次会议上通过了向斯大林致敬书。全文如下：亲爱的斯大林同志！我们，在全苏拥护和平大会上集会的、苏维埃共和国工人阶级、集体农民和知识分子的代表，诚心诚意地向你——我们的导师、为和平与各国友好而斗争的伟大斗士，致最诚恳热烈的敬意。
    你的名字已成为亿万普遍人民争取和平、争取民主的斗争的大旗。
    你用对全世界争取和平的斗争、争取民族独立和增进各国人民间友谊与善意的斗争这一伟大和正义的事业的坚定信心，燃起了全世界一切人民心中的火焰。
    你对争取和平的斗争的坚持，你的坚定和你崇高的勇敢，已成为我们的榜样与模范。
    斯大林同志，在你所指导的英勇的布尔什维克党的领导下，苏联人民战胜了法西斯主义，保住了他们祖国的光荣、自由与独立，并从可耻的法西斯奴役的威胁中拯救了全人类。
    只因为有这个划时代的胜利，全世界的人民才能满怀希望地昂首向前，瞻望将来，并且深信终将战胜帝国主义野蛮的黑暗势力。
    这就说明了为什么在这里发表的每一篇保卫和平的演说中，演说者都满怀热爱与感激的心情来呼唤你的名字。
    我们深知：争取和平的斗争并不是一个容易的斗争。
    我们看到：全世界的帝国主义者们，首先是美国帝国主义者正在怎样地用尽一切努力来阻挠我们争取和平的斗争。然而，没有任何力量能动摇我们的意志和我们的信念，没有任何力量能分裂在反对战争贩子，反对帝国主义阵营的吃人生番的这一斗争中的人民。
    我们已经完全决定以我们的全部力量奉献在和平的圣坛上。
    我们聚集在这里，在我们光荣的首都莫斯科，举行拥护和平大会，并且认真地讨论了我们为和平而斗争的计划。
    我们满意地倾听了我们的外国朋友的讲话，他们向我们保证：他们国内的人民已完全决心进行反对战争贩子的斗争，并用各种方法来增强对伟大的苏联人民的友谊。
    我们已经成立了全苏拥护和平大会常设委员会并赋予以崇高的任务：用种种方法，即你——我们亲爱的领袖和导师所教导我们的方法，来巩固各国间的友谊，揭发战争贩子，并为和平而斗争。
    我们的祖国——苏联，世界上第一个和最伟大的和平保卫者——万岁！领导我们走向胜利的伟大的布尔什维克党万岁！我们亲受的、热爱的斯大林同志万岁！
