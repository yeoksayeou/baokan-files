Title: Democratic National Construction Association Beiping Branch Formally Established
Author:
Date: 1949-08-22
Page: 1

　　Democratic National Construction Association
  　Beiping Branch Formally Established
    [Beiping City News] The Beiping City Branch of the Democratic National Construction Association was formally established yesterday (the 21st). The inaugural meeting was held at Xiehe Auditorium at 3:00 PM. Attendees included Cui Yueli, representative of the Beiping Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Yu Xinqing, representative of the Beiping Municipal Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang; Chen Dingwen and Shen Yifan, representatives of the Beiping Branch of the China Democratic League; Han Zhao'e, representative of the Beiping Municipal Organizing Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party; Xu Deheng, representative of the Jiusan Society; Huang Yanpei, Yang Weiyu, and Feng Yunhe, directors of the Association's General Headquarters; and more than 130 members of the branch. During the meeting, Chairman Ling Qijun delivered an opening speech, hoping that public and private enterprises would unite within the Democratic National Construction Association to work together to produce, prosper the economy, and implement the policy of considering both public and private interests and benefiting both labor and capital. Subsequently, Sun Qimeng reported on the preparatory work of the branch. Guests Cui Yueli, Chen Dingwen, Yu Xinqing, and Han Zhao'e delivered speeches successively. Huang Yanpei, representative of the General Headquarters, stated in his speech that the mission of the Association is to unite national industrialists and business people across the country to jointly develop production, prosper the economy, unite with various democratic parties, and build a New Democratic China under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong of the Chinese Communist Party. The meeting then approved the draft charter of the branch and a telegram of respect to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu. Ling Qijun, Pu Jiexiu, Nan Hanchen, Liu Yifeng and others were elected as directors and supervisors; Pan Yuyan, Li Peizhi, and Hua Nangui and others were elected as supervisors.

### 民主建国会　北平分会正式成立

1949-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　民主建国会
  　北平分会正式成立
    【平市讯】民主建国会北平市分会昨（二十一）日正式成立。成立大会于下午三时假协和礼堂举行。到有来宾中国共产党北平市委会代表崔月犁，中国国民党革命委员会北平市分会代表余心清，中国民主同盟北平市支部代表陈鼎文、沈一帆，中国农工民主党北平市整理委员会代表韩兆鹗，九三学社代表许德珩，该会总会理事黄炎培、杨卫玉、酆云鹤及该分会会员等一百三十余人。会中，主席凌其峻致开会词，希望公私营企业要在民建会内团结起来，共同努力生产，繁荣经济，执行公私兼顾、劳资两利政策。继由孙起孟报告该分会筹备工作。来宾崔月犁、陈鼎文、余心清、韩兆鹗相继讲话。总会代表黄炎培致词时，说明该会任务为团结全国民族工商业者来共同发展生产，繁荣经济，联合各民主党派，在中国共产党毛主席领导下，建设新民主主义的中国。大会继通过该分会章程草案，及向毛主席、朱总司令致敬电。并选出凌其峻、浦洁修、南汉宸、刘一峰等为理监事；潘禹言、李培之、华南圭等为监事。
