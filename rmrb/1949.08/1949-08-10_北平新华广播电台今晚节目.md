Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-08-10
Page: 1

    【This newspaper's dispatch】Tonight's program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station: From 7:50 PM to 8:10 PM, the speech program will feature Liu Feng, Deputy Minister of Education of the North China Government, speaking on "How to Develop Social Education in Cities." From 7:30 PM to 7:50 PM, the Youth Time segment will broadcast the "China Youth" editorial – "Regarding Summer Learning"; and the news report – "A Record of the Summer Learning Group of Beiping College and University Students." From 10:20 PM to 10:50 PM, the Arts and Entertainment program will feature selections from the opera "Establishing Merit," broadcast by the Northeast Cultural and Educational Work Team.

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-08-10
第1版()
专栏：

    北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚七点五十分到八点十分演讲节目，华北政府教育部副部长刘风讲：“怎样开展城市的社会教育”。七点三十分到七点五十分青年时间，播送“中国青年”社论——“关于暑期学习”；通讯——“记北平大中学生暑期学习团”。十点二十分到十点五十分文艺节目，东北文化教育工作队播送“立功”歌剧选曲。
