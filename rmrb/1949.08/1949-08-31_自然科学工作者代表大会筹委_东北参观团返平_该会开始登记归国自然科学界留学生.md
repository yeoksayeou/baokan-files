Title: Natural Science Workers Representative Assembly Preparatory Committee - Northeast Visiting Group Returns to Beijing; The Assembly Begins Registration of Returned Natural Science Students

Author: Bai Sheng
Date: 1949-08-31
Page: 1

　　Natural Science Workers Representative Assembly Preparatory Committee
  　Northeast Visiting Group Returns to Beijing
    The Assembly Begins Registration of Returned Natural Science Students
    [Our Dispatch] The Preparatory Committee members of the All-China Natural Science Workers Representative Assembly, including Zhu Kezhen, Xie Jiarong, Shi Jiayang, Wu Xuezhou, Jin Shanbao, Zhang Changshao, Zhang Mengwen, Deng Shuqun, Huang Zongzhen, Zhou Ren, and others, a group of over forty people, visited the Northeast at the end of last month and returned to Beijing yesterday (the 29th) afternoon. In the evening, the Chairman of the Preparatory Committee, Wu Yuzhang, and others hosted a dinner. It is reported that this trip involved a very detailed visit and inspection of industrial construction, agriculture, and medicine in the Northeast. They will stay in Beijing for a few days before returning to their respective work locations. The Preparatory Committee has also scheduled a forum at 3:00 PM tomorrow (the 31st) at the China Engineers Society in Dengshikou, inviting the Preparatory Committee members who returned from the Northeast to report on their visit and impressions.
    [Another Dispatch] In order to specifically care for the returned natural science students, the Assembly has decided to begin registration procedures starting from the 30th at the Assembly's address in the European and American Returned Students Association on Nanheyan, in order to arrange and allocate work.
    (Bai Sheng)

### 自然科学工作者代表大会筹委　东北参观团返平  该会开始登记归国自然科学界留学生
柏生
1949-08-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　自然科学工作者代表大会筹委
  　东北参观团返平
    该会开始登记归国自然科学界留学生
    【本报讯】中华全国自然科学工作者代表大会筹备委员会筹委竺可桢、谢家荣、施嘉炀、吴学周、金善宝、张昌绍、张孟闻、邓叔群、黄宗甄、周仁等一行四十余人上月底赴东北参观，已于昨（廿九）日下午返平。当晚由筹委会主任委员吴玉章等招待晚餐。据闻此行对东北工业建设、农业、医学等参观考查颇为详尽，他们将在平停留数日后，即分别返各地工作。筹委会并定于明（三十一）日下午三时在灯市口前中国工程师学会举行座谈会，请东北参观归来的筹委报告参观情况和感想。
    【又讯】该会为具体照顾归国的自然科学界的留学生，决定自三十日起开始在南河沿欧美同学会该会会址办理登记手续，以便安置和分配工作。
    （柏生）
