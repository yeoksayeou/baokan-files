Title: North China Literary and Art Work Committee's Old Opera Division Reorganized into China Opera Improvement Committee; All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and All-China Association of Literary Workers Request Government to Record and Subsidize Expenses
Author:
Date: 1949-08-27
Page: 2

　　North China Literary and Art Work Committee's Old Opera Division
  　Reorganized into China Opera Improvement Committee
    All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and All-China Association of Literary Workers
  　Request Government to Record and Subsidize Expenses
    [Our Newspaper's Report] In order to adapt to the needs of work development, the North China Cultural and Art Work Committee, upon approval from the North China People's Government, has adjusted and expanded its original organization. The original Old Opera Division has been reorganized into the China Opera Improvement Committee, and two new divisions, the Art Division and the Foreign Cultural Liaison Division, have been added, with Zhou Yang and Xiao San concurrently serving as heads of these divisions, respectively.
    Further News: The All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and the All-China Association of Literary Workers have already submitted reports to the North China People's Government for approval and record. The expenses of these two associations are currently being budgeted, and the government will be requested to review and subsidize them.
    (North China People's Government Press Office)

### 华北文艺工作委员会旧剧处　改为中国戏曲改进委员会  全国文联暨全国文协　呈请政府备案补助经费

1949-08-27
第2版()
专栏：

　　华北文艺工作委员会旧剧处
  　改为中国戏曲改进委员会
    全国文联暨全国文协
  　呈请政府备案补助经费
    【本报讯】华北文化艺术工作委员会为适应工作发展之需要，经呈准华北人民政府将原有机构加以调整和扩充，原有旧剧处改为中国戏曲改进委员会，并添设艺术处与对外文化联络处两处，由周扬、萧三分别兼任处长。
    又讯：中华全国文学艺术界联合会暨中华全国文学工作者协会业已呈报华北人民政府批准备案。该两会经费，正制造预算，拟请政府审核补助。
    （华北人民政府新闻发布室）
