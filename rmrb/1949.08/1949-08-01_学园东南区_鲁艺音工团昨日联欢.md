Title: Southeast District of the Academy: Lu Yi Music and Labor Troupe Held a Joint Celebration Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-08-01
Page: 5

    Southeast District of the Academy
    Lu Yi Music and Labor Troupe
    Held a Joint Celebration Yesterday
    [This Newspaper's Report] The Southeast District of the Youth Academy and the Lu Yi Music and Labor Troupe held a joint celebration at 3:00 PM yesterday (the 31st) in the auditorium of the Art Academy. More than 1,000 students from various schools and members of the Lu Yi Music and Labor Troupe attended. The entertainment program included songs performed by students from various schools, and farm work dances, miner dances, the comic dialogue “Yang Guixiang Persuades Her Fiancé to Join the Army,” and songs performed by the Lu Yi Music and Labor Troupe. The students were very satisfied with the songs performed by the Lu Yi Music and Labor Troupe. The celebration continued until 6:00 PM and concluded with the solemn singing of the Internationale.
          (Southeast District of the Academy Communications Group)

### 学园东南区  鲁艺音工团昨日联欢

1949-08-01
第5版()
专栏：

    学园东南区
    鲁艺音工团
    昨日联欢
    【本报讯】青年学园东南区和鲁艺音工团昨日（三十一日）下午三时在艺专大礼堂举行联欢会，到各校同学及鲁艺音工团团员一千多人。游艺节目有各校同学演出的歌咏，鲁艺音工团演出的农作舞、矿工舞、说唱“杨桂香劝未婚夫参军”、歌咏等。同学们对鲁艺音工团的歌咏很满意，联欢一直到六点在庄严的国际歌声中散会。          （学园东南区通讯组）
