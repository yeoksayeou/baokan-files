Title: Strengthening the Peace Front Everywhere "Peace Advocate" Published; All-India Peace Congress to be Held

Author:
Date: 1949-08-15
Page: 3

    Strengthening the Peace Front Everywhere
    "Peace Advocate" Published
    All-India Peace Congress to be Held
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 14th] Polish News Agency, Paris dispatch: The first issue of "Peace Advocate," the organ of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress, has been published in Paris in French, English, and Spanish. The editorial is entitled "We are strengthening the peace front everywhere." Major articles in the publication include Ehrenburg's "This is only a beginning," Gromyko's "Atomic energy must be used for peaceful purposes," Sciarone's "The experience of Italy's struggle for peace," and J.B.S. Haldane's "The World Federation of Trade Unions opposes war." The publication also reported on news of the peace movement in forty-nine countries.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 14th] TASS, Delhi dispatch: It is learned here that the Peace Congress will be held in Calcutta in September under the auspices of the West Bengal branch of the All-India Trade Union Congress.

### 处处都加强着和平阵线  “和平拥护者”出版  全印将举行拥护和平大会

1949-08-15
第3版()
专栏：

    处处都加强着和平阵线
    “和平拥护者”出版
    全印将举行拥护和平大会
    【新华社北平十四日电】波兰通讯社巴黎讯：世界拥护和平大会常设委员会机关刊“和平拥护者”的第一期已在巴黎以法文、英文和西班牙文出版。社论题为“我们处处都加强着和平阵线”。该刊的主要专文包括爱伦堡的“这只是一个开端”，葛氏的“原子能必须用于和平目的”，昔兰尼的“意大利争取和平斗争的经验”，吉伯特的“世界工联反对战争”。该刊物并报道了关于四十九个国家和平运动的消息。
    【新华社北平十四日电】塔斯社德里讯：据此间获悉，拥护和平大会将于九月间在全印职工大会西孟加拉分会主持下在加尔各答举行。
