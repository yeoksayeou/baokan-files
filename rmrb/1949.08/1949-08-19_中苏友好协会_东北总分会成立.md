Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Northeast Branch Established
Author:
Date: 1949-08-19
Page: 3

　　Marks the beginning of a new stage of solidarity and friendship between the people of Northeast China and the people of the Soviet Union
    Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
  　Northeast Branch Established
    [Shenyang People's Broadcasting Station, 18th broadcast] Shenyang News: The Northeast Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has been formally established. At the closing meeting of the association on the 17th, Liu Zhiming delivered the closing address, saying: The establishment of the Northeast Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association marks the beginning of a new stage of solidarity and friendship between the people of Northeast China and the people of the Soviet Union. To do a good job in the work of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, it is necessary to eliminate the narrow bourgeois nationalist ideas in thought and establish the spirit of internationalism. The conference adopted telegrams to Marshal Stalin, Chairman Mao, and the preparatory committee of the National Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. Finally, Gao Gang, Li Fuchun, Lin Feng, Li Zhuoran, Gao Chongmin, Che Xiangchen, Li Yunchang, Wu Xiuquan, Zhu Qiwen and other fifty-one people were elected as executive members of the Northeast Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

### 中苏友好协会　东北总分会成立

1949-08-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　标志着东北人民与苏联人民团结友好新阶段的开始
    中苏友好协会
  　东北总分会成立
    【沈阳人民广播电台十八日广播】沈阳消息：中苏友好协会东北总分会，已经正式成立。在十七号该会闭幕会上，刘芝明致闭会词说：中苏友好协会东北总分会的成立，标志着东北人民和苏联人民团结友好新阶段的开始。要把中苏友好协会的工作做好，就必须在思想上去掉资产阶级狭隘的民族主义思想，树立国际主义精神。大会通过了给斯大林大元帅、毛主席和全国中苏友好协会筹委会的通电。最后选出高岗、李富春、林枫、李卓然、高崇民、车向忱、李运昌、伍修权、朱其文等五十一人，为东北中苏友好协会执行委员。
