Title: Over Ten Thousand Liberated Officers and Soldiers from Zhejiang Join Our Army
Author:
Date: 1949-08-26
Page: 1
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 25th—Since crossing the river, the Training Department for Liberated Officers and Soldiers of the Zhejiang Military Region of the People's Liberation Army has enlisted 10,661 liberated officers and soldiers of the Kuomintang army into the People's Liberation Army, including over 260 technical personnel. Before joining the army, the Kuomintang liberated officers and soldiers underwent short-term study, which initially raised their political awareness. After joining the army, they expressed their willingness to render meritorious service to the people in the great advance to the southeast and south China. In addition to enlisting over 10,000 people, the department also sent 59,510 Kuomintang officers and soldiers back to their hometowns for production, including 2,204 officers of the rank of colonel or higher, and 23,318 officers of the rank of lieutenant. Before being sent back, they were all issued certificates and travel expenses, and transfer stations were set up in Hangzhou, Jinhua and other places to provide food and accommodation and transportation convenience.

### 浙江万余解放官兵　参加我军

1949-08-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　浙江万余解放官兵
  　参加我军
    【新华社杭州二十五日电】人民解放军浙江军区解放官兵训练处，自渡江以来已争取了一万零六百六十一名国民党军解放官兵，先后参加解放军，其中二百六十余名为技术人员。参加解放军的国民党解放官兵入伍前都经过短期学习，初步提高了政治觉悟，入伍后纷纷表示愿在向东南和华南的伟大进军中为人民立功。除争取一万余人入伍外，该处又分别遣送国民党军官兵五万九千五百一十名回籍生产，其中包括校级以上军官二千二百零四名，尉级军官二万三千三百十八名。遣送之前，均发给证明书和路费，并在杭州、金华等地设立遣送站，给予膳宿照顾和乘车方便。
