Title: Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Program
Author:
Date: 1949-08-31
Page: 1

【Our Newspaper News】Tonight's program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station from 7:50 PM to 8:10 PM features a lecture program by Fei Xiaotong, a professor at Tsinghua University and representative of the preparatory committee of the Beiping All-Circles Representative Conference University Professors Joint Association, on the topic: "I Participated in the Beiping All-Circles Representative Conference." From 7:30 PM to 7:50 PM, during youth time, an article by Jiang Nanxiang, "The Exchange of Chinese Youth Movement and International Youth Movement," will be broadcast. From 10:20 PM to 10:50 PM, a cultural program will be broadcast by the Anti-Enemy Drama Troupe of the Political Department of the North China Military Region: "Song Introductions."

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-08-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚七点五十分到八点十分演讲节目，北平各界代表会议院校教授联合会筹备会代表、清华大学教授费孝通演讲，题目是：“我参加了北平各界代表会议”。七点三十分到七点五十分青年时间，播送蒋南翔的一篇文章——“中国青年运动和国际青年运动的交流”。十点二十分到十点五十分文艺节目，华北军区政治部抗敌剧社播送：“歌曲介绍”。
