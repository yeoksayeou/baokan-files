Title: With Soviet Youth as the Vanguard, Democratic Youth from Various Countries Actively Prepare to Participate in the Youth Festival; 600,000 Soviet Youths Complete the Five-Year Plan
Author:
Date: 1949-08-06
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 5th – Reports from various sources: With Soviet youth as the vanguard, democratic youth from various countries are continuing to actively prepare to participate in the World Youth Festival and Youth Congress.
    The representative of the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee told a TASS reporter: Several amateur art groups, young male and female actors, artists, writers, and composers from Soviet factories and plants are preparing special programs to participate in the Youth Festival performances. Two years ago, the Soviet youth delegation participated in the International Youth Festival held in Prague. At that time, the Soviet delegation showed the world the achievements of Soviet culture, and they achieved success at that Youth Festival. In the past two years, Soviet youth have achieved new and outstanding achievements in various aspects of political, economic, and cultural life. Six hundred thousand young male and female laborers have completed the Five-Year Plan, and the most outstanding young Soviet patriots – Stakhanovite workers, scientists, and representatives of the literary and artistic circles – have been awarded Stalin Prizes. The Soviet section of the exhibition that will open in Budapest will inform representatives and guests from various countries of these achievements. The photos that the Soviet Union will exhibit will illustrate the role played by Soviet youth in the Soviet post-war peaceful construction. The Soviet delegation will also include a large number of student athletes – basketball and volleyball teams, swimming, wrestling, and boxing athletes, etc.

### 以苏联青年为首的各国民主青年  积极准备参加青年节  苏六十万青年完成五年计划

1949-08-06
第3版()
专栏：

    以苏联青年为首的各国民主青年
    积极准备参加青年节
    苏六十万青年完成五年计划
    【新华社北平五日电】各方报导：以苏联青年为首的各国民主青年，正继续积极准备参加世界青年节与青年大会。
    苏联青年反法西斯委员会代表对塔斯社记者谈称：苏联制造厂与工厂的若干业余艺术团体、青年男女演员、艺术家、作家与作曲家，正在准备着参加青年节表演的特别节目。两年前，苏联青年代表团曾参加在布拉格举行的国际青年节。苏联代表团那时曾向全世界显示了苏联文化的成就，他们在那次青年节获得了成功。两年来的苏联青年在政治、经济与文化生活各方面又获得了新的卓越的成就。已有六十万青年男女劳动者完成了五年计划，苏联最优秀的青年爱国者——斯塔哈诺夫式的工人、科学家、文学与艺术界代表，曾荣获斯大林奖金。将在布达佩斯开幕的展览会的苏联之部，将把这些成就告诉各国的代表与来宾。苏联所将展览的照片，将说明苏联青年在苏联战后和平建设的工作中所起的作用。苏联代表团亦将包括大批学生运动员——篮球与排球队、游泳、角力与拳斗选手等。
