Title: World Peace Congress Standing Committee Suggests October 2nd as International Day of Struggle for Peace
Author:
Date: 1949-08-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 4th: Paris dispatch: The Executive Committee of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress held a meeting here on July 29th. Delegations from the World Federation of Trade Unions Secretariat, the International Democratic Women's Federation, the World Federation of Democratic Youth, and representatives of the Latin American peace movement also attended the meeting. D'Arboussier, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Peace Congress, reported on the situation of peace supporters in 71 countries conducting the struggle to defend peace, and emphasized the significance of the upcoming Soviet Peace Congress and the American Congress for Peace. After Saillant delivered a report on the International Day of Struggle for Peace, the Executive Committee of the Standing Committee of the Congress decided: to suggest October 2nd as the International Day of Struggle for Peace, pending final confirmation after consultation with peace committees in various countries.

### 世界和大常委会建议  十月二日为国际和平斗争日

1949-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    世界和大常委会建议
    十月二日为国际和平斗争日
    【新华社北平四日电】巴黎讯：七月二十九日，世界和平大会常设委员会执委会，在此间举行会议，世界工联秘书处、国际民主妇联、世界民主青联的代表团及拉丁美洲和平运动的代表们亦列席会议。和平大会常委会副主席达波赛在会上报告七十一国的和平拥护者进行保卫和平斗争的情况，并着重指出即将来临的苏联拥护和平大会及美洲争取和平大会的意义。在赛扬作了关于国际和平斗争日的报告之后，大会常委会执委会决定：建议以十月二日为国际和平斗争日，待与各国的拥护和平委员会商讨后，即作最后确定。
