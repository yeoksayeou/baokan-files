　　Normal University and Fu Jen Catholic University Teachers and Staff Angrily Denounce White Paper
    Resolutely Crush the New Conspiracy of American Imperialism, Lean Towards the Soviet Union and Implement One-Sided Policy
    [Report from this newspaper] To protest against the White Paper of American imperialism, teachers and staff of Normal University jointly issued a declaration, the original text of which is as follows: On the fifth of this month, the U.S. State Department published a so-called White Paper on Sino-American relations, totaling 1,554 pages, attempting to split Chinese territory and provoke Sino-Soviet relations. After five years of actively assisting Chiang Kai-shek in his failure, they are still unwilling to give up and vainly attempt to use so-called "democratic individualists" to conduct anti-Soviet and anti-Chinese people's espionage activities in various places. Furthermore, their malicious intent is revealed as they attempt to seize Taiwan from the hands of the Chinese people and stuff it into the mouths of the Japanese; this is tantamount to admitting that they will take the place of the German and Italian accomplices of Japan. Although their conspiracies and tricks will inevitably meet with the same failure as their aid to Chiang Kai-shek, we, standing on the position of the Chinese people, cannot but solemnly inform American imperialism of the following facts: 1. It was you who transported a large amount of military supplies to slaughter the Chinese people when the Japanese invaders frantically invaded China. 2. It was you who did not allow the invaded Chinese people to settle accounts with the Japanese after the Japanese surrendered. 3. It was you who dispatched aircraft and warships to actively help the Chiang bandits transport troops to various liberated areas to carry out civil war. Your relationship with China is no different from that of Japan, only with countless blood debts. In the eyes of the Chinese people, you are the same as Japan after the September 18th Incident, with nothing but hatred.
    We, the colleagues of Normal University, in addition to lodging a serious protest against the shameless confession of American imperialism, must also be vigilant. The vulgarity and shamelessness of the White Paper are consistent with the Atlantic Pact and Pacific Pact it has instigated. Its purpose is nothing more than to be enemies with the Chinese people and enemies with the people of the world. We must thoroughly smash it. In order to thoroughly smash the aggressive conspiracy of American imperialism, we must lean towards the Soviet Union, lean towards the new democratic countries of Eastern Europe, and lean towards the working class and peasants of the world. Therefore, we must support Chairman Mao's one-sided policy of people's democratic dictatorship published on July 1st. Furthermore, we are willing to respond to the recent government's call for the Sino-Soviet friendship movement. And especially important is that the intellectuals of our country should no longer fantasize about the middle road, because American imperialism is trying to use our weaknesses to coerce and lure us into carrying out anti-people crimes in our motherland. In the past, Wang Jingwei failed as a running dog for Japanese imperialism, and today Chiang Kai-shek has failed as a running dog for American imperialism. If we do not fall on the side of the people, we will definitely fall on the side of the anti-people, and there is no third way. Is it not yet clear?
    Finally, we propose that the White Paper is just a shameless confession. In order to smash its conspiracy, we must raise our vigilance, thoroughly implement Chairman Mao's one-sided policy, respond to the government's call, and unite the workers and peasants of the world to overthrow American imperialism.

### 师大辅大教职员愤斥白皮书  坚决粉碎美帝新阴谋靠拢苏联实行一面倒

1949-08-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　师大辅大教职员愤斥白皮书
    坚决粉碎美帝新阴谋靠拢苏联实行一面倒
    【本报讯】为抗议美帝白皮书，师大教职员联名发表宣言，原文如下：本月五日美帝国务院发表了长达一千五十四页的所谓中美关系的白皮书，企图分裂中国领土，挑拔中苏关系。在五年来积极援助蒋介石失败之后，又还是死不甘心，妄想利用什么“民主的个人主义者”在各地进行其反苏反中国人民的特务活动，并且图穷匕见，想把台湾从中国人手里夺出来，塞到日本人嘴里去；这无异于自承将取日本帮凶的德意的地位而代之。虽其阴谋诡计，必然遭到与援助蒋介石同样的失败，可是我们站在中国人民的立场，不能不以下列事实正告美帝：一、当日寇疯狂侵略中国时输送大量军火屠杀中国人民的是你们。二、日寇投降以后不许受侵略的中国人向日本人算帐的是你们。三、在各地发生强奸妇女事件，派遣飞机军舰积极帮助蒋匪运输军队到各地解放区进行内战的还是你们。你们和中国的关系与日本并没两样，只是数不清的血债。你们在中国人民的眼里已经和九一八以后的日本相同，除去愤恨并无其他。
    我们师大教职员同仁，除对美帝无耻的供状提出严重的抗议以外，还要自己警惕。白皮书的下流无耻，是与其所策动大西洋公约、太平洋公约是一致的。其目的不外与中国人民为敌，与全世界的人民为敌，我们必须彻底粉碎它。为了澈底粉碎美帝的侵略阴谋，我们必须靠拢苏联，靠拢东南欧新民主主义国家，靠拢全世界工农大众，因而我们必须拥护毛主席七一发表的论人民民主专致的一面倒政策。更进一步，我们愿意响应近来政府对中苏友好运动之号召。而尤其重要的是，我们全国的知识分子，再不要幻想什么中间路线，因为美帝正想利用我们的弱点，威迫利诱，在祖国进行反人民的罪行。过去汪精卫给日本帝国主义作走狗失败了，今天蒋介石给美帝作走狗又失败了。我们不倒在人民的这一面，就一定倒向反人民的那一面，而第三条路是没有的，难道说还不明白吗？
    最后，我们提出，白皮书只是一纸无耻的供状，为了粉碎它的阴谋，就必须自己提高警觉，彻底实行毛主席的一面倒政策，响应政府的号召，全世界职工农民团结起来打倒美帝。
