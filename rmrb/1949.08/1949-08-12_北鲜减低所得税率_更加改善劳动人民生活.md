Title: North Korea Reduces Income Tax Rates, Further Improving the Living Standards of Working People
Author:
Date: 1949-08-12
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 9th: The Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Korean People's Democratic Republic has recently issued an order stipulating that, starting from October 1949, income tax rates for workers, staff, small merchants, and small employers will be reduced in North Korea. This decision aims to further improve the living standards of the working people. The new income tax rates are 49 percent lower for workers and staff than the current rates implemented since February 1947, and an average of 19.9 percent lower for all levels of the population.

### 北鲜减低所得税率  更加改善劳动人民生活

1949-08-12
第3版()
专栏：

    北鲜减低所得税率
    更加改善劳动人民生活
    【新华社北平十一日电】塔斯社平壤九日讯：朝鲜人民民主共和国最高人民议会主席团顷颁布命令，规定自一九四九年十月份开始，在北朝鲜减低工人、职员、小商人及小雇主的所得税税率。这一决定旨在进一步改善劳动人民的生活。新的所得税率比之一九四七年二月以来实施的现行税率，对工人与职员减低了百分之四十九，对各阶层人民平均减低了百分之十九点九。
