Title: Bitolj Region: Greek Democratic Army Recaptures Positions; Enemy Forces Near Grammos Suffer Heavy Losses
Author:
Date: 1949-08-27
Page: 3

　　Bitolj Region
  　Greek Democratic Army Recaptures Positions
  　Enemy Forces Near Grammos Suffer Heavy Losses
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 26th: TASS, Sofia, 23rd: The Ellas News Agency reports: The General Headquarters of the Greek Democratic Army issued a communique stating: The enemy continues to prepare for a new offensive towards Grammos. Our infantry is preventing the concentration of enemy forces, causing significant losses to the royalist fascists. The enemy's offensive in the Robescu region was repelled. In the Bitolj region, units of the Democratic Army have recaptured lost positions.

### 比特科尼茨地区　希民主军收复阵地　格拉姆斯附近敌军受重创

1949-08-27
第3版()
专栏：

　　比特科尼茨地区
  　希民主军收复阵地
  　格拉姆斯附近敌军受重创
    【新华社北平二十六日电】塔斯社索非亚二十三日讯：艾拉斯通讯社报道：希腊民主军总司令部发表公报称：敌人继续准备向格拉姆斯的新攻势。我步兵正阻止敌军的集中，使保皇法西斯受到重大损失。敌人在罗贝斯库地区的进攻被击退。在比特科尼茨地区，民主军所部克复失去的阵地。
