Title: Managing Private Banking and Money Industries: East China Military Region Announces Measures
Author:
Date: 1949-08-30
Page: 4

Economic News

　　Managing Private Banking and Money Industries
  　East China Military Region Announces Measures
    Local People's Banks are designated as inspection authorities to assist governments at all levels in management.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 29th] In order to stabilize finance, support production, and ensure normal social credit, the Headquarters of the East China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army promulgated and implemented the "Provisional Measures for the Management of Private Banking and Money Industries in the East China Region" on the 21st. The measures consist of 25 articles. Among them, it is stipulated that the Headquarters of the East China Military Region authorizes the East China Financial and Economic Committee as the competent authority for the management of private banking and money industries in the East China Region, and designates the People's Banks of China in various places as the management and inspection authorities for the banking and money industries in various places, to assist governments at all levels in managing the banking and money industries.

### 管理私营银钱业　华东军区公布办法  各地人民银行指定为检查机关，协助各级政府管理。

1949-08-30
第4版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　管理私营银钱业
  　华东军区公布办法
    各地人民银行指定为检查机关，协助各级政府管理。
    【新华社上海二十九日电】中国人民解放军华东军区司令部为稳定金融，扶植生产，保障社会正常信用，特于二十一日公布并施行“华东区管理私营银钱业暂行办法”，该办法全文二十五条。其中规定华东军区司令部授权华东财政经济委员会为华东区管理私营银钱业主管机关，并指定各地中国人民银行为各地银钱业之管理检查机关，协助各级政府管理银钱业。
