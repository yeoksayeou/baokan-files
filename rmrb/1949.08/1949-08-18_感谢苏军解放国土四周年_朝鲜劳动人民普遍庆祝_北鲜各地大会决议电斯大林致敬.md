Title: To Thank the Soviet Army for Liberating the Homeland for the Fourth Anniversary, the Korean Working People Universally Celebrate; Rallies in Various Parts of North Korea Resolve to Send a Telegram of Respect to Stalin
Author:
Date: 1949-08-18
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 15th (TASS dispatch): The Korean working people universally celebrate the fourth anniversary of the Soviet Army's liberation of Korea from the shackles of Japanese imperialism. The cities and villages of North Korea are decorated as if for a festival. Commemorative meetings were held in large and small factories in the cities and in the villages. At these meetings, the working people reviewed the progress of Korea in the past four years, and expressed deep gratitude to the Soviet Army and Marshal Stalin. At the meetings, everyone unanimously emphasized the desire to further consolidate the friendship between the Soviet Union and the Korean people. On August 14th, the Pyongyang City People's Assembly, as well as various parties and public organizations, held a commemorative meeting at the National Art Theater. The Deputy Prime Minister of the Korean People's Democratic Republic, Park Heon-young, gave a report to the meeting. The meeting unanimously adopted a telegram of respect to Marshal Stalin.

### 感谢苏军解放国土四周年　朝鲜劳动人民普遍庆祝  北鲜各地大会决议电斯大林致敬

1949-08-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　感谢苏军解放国土四周年
  　朝鲜劳动人民普遍庆祝
    北鲜各地大会决议电斯大林致敬
    【新华社北平十七日电】塔斯社平壤十五日讯：朝鲜劳动人民普遍庆祝苏军自日本帝国主义束缚下解放朝鲜的四周年。北朝鲜的城市与乡村装饰得象节日一样。城市中的大小工厂和乡村，都举行了纪念大会。在这些会议上，劳动人民回顾过去四年中朝鲜的进展，并向苏军与斯大林大元帅表示深深的感谢，会议上大家一致强调进一步巩固苏联与朝鲜人民之间的友谊愿望。八月十四日，平壤市人民议会以及各党派和公众团体，在国家艺术剧院举行纪念大会。朝鲜人民民主共和国副总理朴宪永向大会作了报告。大会一致通过给斯大林大元帅的致敬电。
