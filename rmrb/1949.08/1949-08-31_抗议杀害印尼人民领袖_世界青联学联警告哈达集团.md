Title: Protesting the Murder of Indonesian People's Leaders: World Federation of Democratic Youth and International Union of Students Warn Hatta Group
Author:
Date: 1949-08-31
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 30th: According to a report from TASS, Budapest, August 27th: The leading bodies of the World Federation of Democratic Youth and the International Union of Students have sent a letter to Hatta, expressing their firm protest on behalf of the world's democratic youth against the Hatta group's despicable act of brutally murdering prominent leaders of the Indonesian working class and the Communist Party, such as Shariffudin, Suripno, and Harino.

### 抗议杀害印尼人民领袖　世界青联学联警告哈达集团

1949-08-31
第3版()
专栏：

　　抗议杀害印尼人民领袖
  　世界青联学联警告哈达集团
    【新华社北平三十日电】据塔斯社布达佩斯二十七日讯：世界民主青年联盟与世界学生联合会的领导机关，顷致函哈达，代表世界民主青年对哈达集团残杀印尼工人阶级与共产党的卓越领袖沙利佛丁、苏利普诺、哈利诺等人的卑鄙行为，表示坚决抗议。
