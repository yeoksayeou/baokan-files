Title: Northeast Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to be Established on "August 15th"
Author:
Date: 1949-08-03
Page: 1
    [Broadcast from Shenyang Radio on the 2nd] The preparatory committee of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Northeast Branch held a preparatory meeting on the 26th of last month. Representatives from the initiating organizations and groups such as the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Shenyang Branch, the Northeast Federation of Trade Unions, the Preparatory Committee of the Northeast New Democratic Youth League, the Northeast Democratic Women's Federation, and the Northeast Literary Association attended. Li Fuchun, Deputy Secretary of the Northeast Bureau, Lin Feng, Chairman of the Northeast People's Government Committee, and Gao Chongmin, Vice Chairman, also attended. After discussion at the meeting, it was decided to hold a representative meeting in Shenyang on "August 15th," the fourth anniversary of the Soviet Red Army's liberation of Northeast China, to formally establish the Northeast Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

### 中苏友好协会东北总分会将于“八一五”成立

1949-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友好协会东北总分会将于“八一五”成立
    【沈阳电台二日广播】中苏友好协会东北总会筹备会，上月廿六日召开筹备会议。到会的有该会发起人中苏友好协会沈阳分会、东北职工总会、新民主主义青年团东北筹备委员会、东北民主妇女联合会、东北文艺协会等机关团体代表多人。东北局副书记李富春、东北政委会主席林枫、副主席高崇民等都亲临参加。会上经讨论决定：于“八一五”苏联红军解放东北四周年纪念日，在沈阳举行代表大会，正式成立中苏友好协会东北总分会。
