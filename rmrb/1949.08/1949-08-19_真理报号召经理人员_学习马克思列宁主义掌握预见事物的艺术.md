Title: Pravda Calls on Managers to Study Marxism-Leninism and Master the Art of Predicting Things
Author:
Date: 1949-08-19
Page: 6
　　
　　Moscow Dispatch: The Pravda published an editorial on the 10th, emphatically requiring managers to master Marxism-Leninism and the ability to predict things. The Pravda pointed out: In the Soviet Union, not every manager has yet mastered the art of predicting things, and not everyone can work with a long-term vision. The Pravda said: "The great strength of our Party lies in the fact that, under the guidance of the advanced Marxist-Leninist theory (which provides knowledge of the laws of social development), we can understand the internal relations of the things around us, not only knowing how things develop at the present stage and where they are developing, but also knowing how they will inevitably develop in the future and where they will develop."
　　The Pravda continued: The entire history of the Bolshevik Party, the entire history of socialist construction in the Soviet Union, provides countless examples proving the foresight of Leninism and Stalinism. The Communist Party cultivates Party, state, and economic talents, requiring them to have the ability to predict things, requiring them to look forward and work with a long-term vision. Workers without this ability and who act blindly without foresight cannot meet the lofty requirements of Bolshevik-style managers. The Communist Party and the Soviet people require every manager not only to complete the tasks assigned by the state today, but also to see tomorrow, and to exert creativity to create conditions for further industrial development, and to ensure the completion of increasingly important new tasks. [Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 16th]

### 真理报号召经理人员　学习马克思列宁主义掌握预见事物的艺术

1949-08-19
第6版()
专栏：

　　真理报号召经理人员
  　学习马克思列宁主义掌握预见事物的艺术
    莫斯科讯：真理报十日发表社论，着重要求经理人员应掌握马克思列宁主义和预见事物的能力。真理报指出：在苏联，至今还不是每个经理人员都已掌握了预见事物的艺术的，并不是所有的人都能看得见远景地工作的。真理报说：“我党的伟大力量就在于：它是在先进的马克思列宁主义理论的指导下（这一理论提供了关于社会发展规律的知识），使我们能理解周围事物的内在关系，不但知道现阶段中的事物怎样发展，向那里发展，并且还知道它们将来一定要怎样发展，向什么地方发展。”
    真理报继称：布尔什维克党的全部历史，苏联社会主义建设的全部历史，提供了无数例子证明列宁、斯大林主义的预见。共产党培养党的、国家的和经济的人才，要求他们具有预见事物的能力，要求他们向前看，看得见远景地工作。没有这种能力的和看不见远景地盲目行事的工作者，是不能满足对布尔什维克式的经理人员的崇高要求的。共产党和苏维埃人民要求每个经理人员，不但能完成国家所给予的今天的任务，而且还要看到明天，并发挥创造性，创造进一步发展工业的条件，并保证完成越来越重大的新任务。　　　　【新华社北平十六日电】
