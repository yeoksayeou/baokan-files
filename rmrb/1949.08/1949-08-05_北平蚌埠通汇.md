Title: Ping-Pong Remittances Between Beiping and Bengbu
Author:
Date: 1949-08-05
Page: 5
   
    [This Newspaper's News] The People's Bank of Beiping Branch has begun handling remittances between Beiping and Bengbu, with an exchange rate of six per thousand. Currently, only postal money orders are processed.

### 北平蚌埠通汇

1949-08-05
第5版()
专栏：

    北平蚌埠通汇
    【本报讯】北平与蚌埠间汇兑，人民银行北平分行已开始承办，汇率千分之六，目前仅办理票汇一种。
