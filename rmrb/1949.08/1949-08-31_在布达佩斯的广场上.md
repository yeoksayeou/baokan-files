Title: In the Squares of Budapest
Author: Ma Qi
Date: 1949-08-31
Page: 6

　　In the Squares of Budapest
    Ma Qi
    At this moment, peace-loving and democratic people are focusing their attention on the squares of Budapest, Eastern Europe.
    The citizens of Budapest
     are thronging the streets
     holding flowers
     wearing beautiful new clothes
     wanting to see the young people from foreign lands
     to see how they celebrate the festival
     to prepare to offer them flowers and kisses
     In the squares of Budapest
     youth representatives from more than eighty countries
     different skin colors
     different languages
     holding hands, shoulder to shoulder
     embracing, kissing
     In the squares of Budapest
     the peace-loving and democratic young generation
     are displaying their阵营 (zhenying, can mean either camp or front)
     reviewing their own strength
     Red flags are flying in the air
     Under the red flags
     are millions upon millions of young people
     and the giant portraits of
     their highly esteemed people's leaders
     Thunderous applause rises
     A Soviet boy appears in front of the microphone
     calling on the world's youth to oppose the war-mongers
     A Chinese young man
     announced on the stage
     the bankruptcy of American imperialism in China
     A young man from free Greece
     tells the world
     the story of the Greek people's refusal to submit
     Soviet youth
     use a collective chorus
     to sing the happy song of socialism
     Chinese youth use simple dances
     to display the power of the new China's workers
     An African-American boy sings a song
     People hear
     the tragic plight of the oppressed black people
     Speeches, singing
     dancing, art competitions
     sports meets, parades and demonstrations
     They are spending the festival in joy
     In the festival they
     learn, exchange revolutionary experiences
     Listen
     tens of thousands of people sing the Internationale in unison
     "Arise, ye prisoners of starvation!
     Arise, ye wretched of the earth!"
     Listen
     tens of thousands of people shout in unison
     "Stalin, Mao Zedong
     Rákosi, Ulbricht!"
     This is an iron army defending peace
     This contingent represents
     youth, represents
     the great hope of humanity
     What connects them together
     is a common ideal
     --For the true liberation of all mankind
    　　August 24th, at 平

### 在布达佩斯的广场上
马其
1949-08-31
第6版()
专栏：

　　在布达佩斯的广场上
    马其
    这时候，爱好和平民主的人民把他们的目光凝聚在东欧布达佩斯的广场
    布达佩斯的市民
     拥塞在大街上
     抱着鲜花
     穿着漂亮的新装
     要看看来自异国的年青人
     看看他们怎么欢渡佳节
     要准备献给他们鲜花和吻
     在布达佩斯的广场上
     八十多国的青年代表
     不同的肤色
     不同的语言
     拉着手，并着肩
     拥抱着，亲吻着
     在布达佩斯的广场上
     爱好和平民主的年青一代
     摆出了他们的阵营
     检阅他们自己的力量
     红旗在空中飞扬
     在红旗下面
     有千千万万的青年人
     和他们高举着的
     人民领袖们的巨像
     雷动的掌声起来了
     一个苏联少年出现在播音机前面
     号召世界青年反对战争贩子
     一个中国的年青人
     在台上宣布了
     美帝在中国的破产
     一个自由希腊的青年
     告诉世界
     希腊人民不屈服的故事
     苏联青年们
     用集体大合唱
     唱出了社会主义的幸福歌
     中国青年用朴素的舞蹈
     显示出新中国工人的力量
     美国的黑人少年唱一首歌
     人们听见了
     黑人被压迫的悲惨境遇
     讲演，歌唱
     舞蹈，艺术竞赛
     运动会，游行示威
     他们在欢乐里渡佳节
     他们在佳节里
     学习，交换革命经验
     听啊
     几万人齐声合唱国际歌
     “起来饥寒交迫的奴隶
     起来全世界的罪人！”
     听啊
     几万人齐声呼喊
     “斯大林，毛泽东
     拉科西，乌拉！”
     这是一支保卫和平的铁军
     这支队伍代表着
     青春，代表着
     人类伟大的希望
     联系他们在一起的
     是一个共同的理想
     ——为了全人类真正的解放
    　　八月二十四日于平
