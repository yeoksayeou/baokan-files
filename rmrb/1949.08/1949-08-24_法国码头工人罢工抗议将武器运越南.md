Title: French Dockworkers Strike to Protest Weapons Shipments to Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1949-08-24
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 23rd. TASS, Paris: Dockworkers in Dunkirk, on the northern coast of France, have decided to organize one-hour strikes twice a day, morning and afternoon, to protest the French authorities' shipment of weapons to Vietnam. They are calling on dockworkers in all ports across the country to refuse to load ships carrying goods destined to continue the colonial war in Vietnam.

### 法国码头工人罢工抗议将武器运越南

1949-08-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　法国码头工人罢工抗议将武器运越南
    【新华社北平二十三日电】塔斯社巴黎讯，法国北部沿海岸敦刻尔刻的码头工人已决定每天组织上下午各一次的一小时罢工，以抗议法当局将武器送往越南。他们号召全国各港口码头工人拒绝装载继续运往越南进行殖民地战争的货物上船。
