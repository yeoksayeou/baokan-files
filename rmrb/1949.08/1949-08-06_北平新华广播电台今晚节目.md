Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Radio Station
Author:
Date: 1949-08-06
Page: 1

Beiping Xinhua Radio Station Tonight's Program
    [This News Report] Beiping Xinhua Radio Station will broadcast a lecture program from 7:50 PM to 8:10 PM tonight, where Mr. Zhang Yan of the Beiping Meteorological Observatory will speak on "The Basic Reasons for Recent Rainfall in Beiping." The youth program from 7:30 PM to 7:50 PM will introduce the editorial of "China Youth" issue No. 13 - "Do Not Keep Advanced Youth Outside the Doors of the League." The art and literature program from 10:20 PM to 10:50 PM will feature the Northeast Cultural and Educational Team singing Ode to the Chinese Communist Party and other songs.

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

    北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚十九点五十分到二十点十分演讲节目，由北平气象台章湮先生讲：“最近北平下雨的几个基本原因”。十九点三十分到十九点五十分青年节目：介绍“中国青年”第十三期的社论——“不要将先进青年关在团的大门以外”。二十二点二十分到二十二点五十分文艺节目，东北文教队演唱中国共产党颂及其他歌曲。
