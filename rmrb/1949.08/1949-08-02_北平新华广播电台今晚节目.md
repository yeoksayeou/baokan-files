Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-08-02
Page: 1

    Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Program
    [This newspaper] Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station's lecture program from 19:50 to 20:10 tonight will feature female labor hero Li Fenglian broadcasting to workers across the country in response to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions' call to "August 1st" to save money and support the army. The employee program from 19:30 to 19:50 will broadcast the opening speech of Li Lisan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, at the trade union work conference. The arts and entertainment program from 22:20 to 22:50 will feature Liu Tongxi of the Northeast Lu Xun Academy of Arts' music troupe singing the Liaoning Drum "Ode to Train Driver Zhang Fulin."

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚十九点五十分到二十点十分演讲节目，女劳动英雄李凤连为响应中华全国总工会号召“八一”节约劳军向全国工人广播。十九点三十分到十九点五十分职工节目，播送中华全国总工会副主席李立三在工会工作会议上的开幕词。二十二点二十分到二十二点五十分文艺节目，由东北鲁迅文艺学院音乐工作团刘桐玺演唱辽宁大鼓“歌唱火车司机张福林。”
