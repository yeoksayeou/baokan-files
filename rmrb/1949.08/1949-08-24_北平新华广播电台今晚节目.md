Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-08-24
Page: 1

　　【Our Newspaper News】Tonight, the Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station will broadcast "Recollections of the First World Youth Festival" during the youth program from 19:30 to 19:50. From 22:20 to 22:50, the arts program will broadcast new songs sung by the First Cultural Troupe of the Third Field Army of the People's Liberation Army.

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚十九点三十分到十九点五十分青年时间，播送“回忆第一次世界青年联欢大会”，二十二点二十分到二十二点五十分文艺节目播送人民解放军第三野战军第一文工团演唱的新歌曲。
