Title: Central University Renamed Nanjing University
Author:
Date: 1949-08-12
Page: 2

(Broadcast from Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station) Nanjing, August 11th: The former National Central University has now been ordered to be renamed National Nanjing University. The university has now decided to enroll new students for this year. Registration will begin on the 20th of this month, and the examination will be held on the 24th.

### 中央大学改名  南京大学

1949-08-12
第2版()
专栏：

    中央大学改名
    南京大学
    【北平新华广播电台广播】南京十一日消息：原来的国立中央大学现在奉令改名国立南京大学。该校现已决定招收本年度新生，从本月二十日起报名，二十四日举行考试。
