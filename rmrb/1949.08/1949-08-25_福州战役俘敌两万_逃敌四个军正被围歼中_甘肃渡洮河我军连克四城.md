Title: Fuzhou Campaign Captures 20,000 Enemy Troops; Four Escaping Enemy Corps are Being Encircled and Annihilated; Our Army Crosses the Tao River in Gansu and Captures Four Cities in Succession
Author:
Date: 1949-08-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 24th -- In the battle to liberate Fuzhou, the People's Liberation Army's preliminary results show that over 23,300 enemy troops were captured. The number of enemy killed and wounded is still being verified. The annihilated Nationalist bandit troops include the Fuzhou Pacification Headquarters, the 6th Army Corps direct units, the entire 216th Division of the 74th Army, and parts of the 73rd Army, 25th Army, and 96th Army. The remnant bandits of the 25th, 96th, 106th, and 121st Armies, who fled south from Fuzhou, are being encircled and annihilated by our powerful forces in the mountainous areas north of Fuqing.

North Ping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Broadcast -- Northwest Front, 24th Message: The People's Liberation Army, advancing south of Lanzhou, crossed the Tao River westward and liberated the four county towns of Kangle, Ningding, Hezheng, and Linxia successively within three days from the 20th to the 22nd. In the battle to liberate Kangle, over 180 of the defending Ma bandit's newly formed 2nd Cavalry Regiment surrendered to the Liberation Army, led by the regiment commander Zhang Ganjian. The Liberation Army was warmly welcomed by the Hui and Han people along the way as they advanced westward. Residents put up slogans, supported the old and carried the young, offering boiled water and food to welcome the Liberation Army, and they also accused the Ma bandits of their crimes.

### 福州战役俘敌两万　逃敌四个军正被围歼中  甘肃渡洮河我军连克四城

1949-08-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　福州战役俘敌两万
  　逃敌四个军正被围歼中
    甘肃渡洮河我军连克四城
    【新华社福建前线二十四日电】人民解放军在解放福州的战役中，初步战果经查明共俘敌二万三千三百余人，毙伤敌数目尚在清查中。被歼灭的国民党匪军计有福州绥署、第六兵团直属队及七十四军二百一十六师全部、七十三军、二十五军、九十六军各一部。由福州南逃的残匪二十五军、九十六军、一零六军、一二一军等部，正被我强大部队围歼于福清以北山区中。
    【北平新华广播电台广播】西北前线二十四号消息：进到兰州以南的人民解放军，向西渡过洮河，在二十号到二十二号三天之内，先后解放了康乐、宁定、和政、临夏等四座县城。在解放康乐的战斗中，守敌马匪新编骑兵第二团一部一百八十多人，由该团团长张干鉴率领向解放军投降。解放军在西进路上，受到沿途回汉人民的热烈欢迎。居民张贴标语，扶老携幼，提着开水和食物欢迎解放军，并且纷纷向解放军控诉马匪统治的罪恶。
