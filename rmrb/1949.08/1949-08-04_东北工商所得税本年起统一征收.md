Title: Northeast Region to Implement Unified Collection of Industrial and Commercial Income Tax This Year
Author:
Date: 1949-08-04
Page: 2

    Northeast Region to Implement Unified Collection of Industrial and Commercial Income Tax This Year
    [Shenyang News] The Northeast Administrative Committee convened its 61st Standing Committee Meeting on the 29th of last month. At the meeting, in order to unify industrial and commercial tax revenues across the Northeast, the "Provisional Regulations on Industrial and Commercial Income Tax in the Northeast Liberated Area" were discussed and approved, and it was decided to implement them starting this year.

### 东北工商所得税本年起统一征收

1949-08-04
第2版()
专栏：

    东北工商所得税本年起统一征收
    【沈阳消息】东北行政委员会上月二十九日召开第六十一次常务委员会议，会议上为了统一东北各地工商业税收讨论和通过“东北解放区工商所得税暂行条例”决定从本年度起实行。
