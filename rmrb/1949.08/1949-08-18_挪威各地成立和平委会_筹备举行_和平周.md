Title: Peace Committees Established Throughout Norway; Preparing to Hold "Peace Week"
Author: 
Date: 1949-08-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 17th. Diantong Agency Oslo dispatch: Local "Peace Committees" are being established in cities, towns, and enterprises throughout Norway to prepare for the "Peace Week" scheduled to be held in Norway at the end of September. This is part of the overall Scandinavian "Peace Week" movement. Local committees will send representatives to a gathering scheduled to be held in Oslo in early October, where a permanent "Peace Committee" will be elected. During "Peace Week," meetings will be held simultaneously throughout Norway. Various circles in Norway have now invited many foreign representatives to participate.

### 挪威各地成立和平委会　筹备举行“和平周”

1949-08-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　挪威各地成立和平委会
  　筹备举行“和平周”
    【新华社北平十七日电】电通社奥斯陆讯：挪威城乡各地与各企业正纷纷成立地方性的“和平委员会”，筹备定于九月底在挪威举行的“和平周”。这是整个斯坎的那维亚“和平周”运动的一部分。各地委员会将派遣代表出席定于十月初在奥斯陆举行的集会，会上将选出常设“和平委员会”。“和平周”期间全挪各地将同时开会。现挪威各界已邀请许多外国代表参加。
