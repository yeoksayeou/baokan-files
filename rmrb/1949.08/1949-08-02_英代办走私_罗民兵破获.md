Title: British Chargé d'Affaires Involved in Smuggling, Romanian Militia Cracks Down
Author:
Date: 1949-08-02
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 1st – TASS, Bucharest: On the night of July 25th, the Romanian militia arrested a group of smugglers in Bucharest who, in addition to smuggling, were also attempting to organize various criminals to cross the border illegally. Among those detained was the British Chargé d'Affaires, Roderick Sarell, who was released after his identity was verified. Interrogated, the arrested individuals confessed that they intended to cross the Romanian border illegally with the help of Sarell. The arrested individuals are still being interrogated.

### 英代办走私  罗民兵破获

1949-08-02
第3版()
专栏：

    英代办走私
    罗民兵破获
    【新华社北平一日电】塔斯社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚民兵于七月二十五日夜在布加勒斯特逮捕了一伙走私者，除走私之外，他们还企图组织各种犯罪分子非法地通过边境。被拘禁者中有英国代办罗德列克·萨列尔，他在验明身份后已被释放。被捕者经审讯供认：他们意图在萨列尔的帮助下非法穿过罗马尼亚边境。被捕者仍在被审讯中。
