Title: Central China Establishes Streamlining and Thrift Committee
Author:
Date: 1949-08-10
Page: 2

    Central China Establishes
    Streamlining and Thrift Committee
    [Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 9th] Following the decision of the Central China Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to launch a streamlining and thrift campaign, the Central China Region Streamlining and Thrift Campaign Committee has been established. Lin Biao, Deng Zihui, Zhao Erlu, Tan Zheng, Xiao Ke, Tao Zhu, Wu Defeng, and others, fourteen in total, are members of the committee, with Lin Biao serving as chairman, Deng Zihui as vice chairman, and Zhao Erlu as secretary-general. The committee is responsible for leading the streamlining and thrift campaign throughout the Central China Region, its directly affiliated organizations, and the city itself.

### 华中成立精简节约委员会

1949-08-10
第2版()
专栏：

    华中成立
    精简节约委员会
    【新华社汉口九日电】中共中央华中局发布开展精简节约运动的决定后，华中区精简节约运动委员会业经成立。林彪、邓子恢、赵尔陆、谭政、萧克、陶铸、吴德峰等十四人为该会委员，林彪任主任委员、邓子恢任副主任委员，赵尔陆为秘书长。该委员会负责领导全华中区及各直属机关和本市的精简节约运动。
