Title: Jiu San Society Sends Congratulations on "August 1st"

Author:

Date: 1949-08-01
Page: 1

Jiu San Society Sends Congratulations on "August 1st"
[This newspaper] news: The Jiu San Society sent a telegram to congratulate the Army Day on August 1st. The telegram reads in brief: "Chairman Mao of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Commander-in-Chief Zhu of the General Headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and all commanders and fighters:

    On the great day of the August 1st Army Day anniversary, we extend our highest respect and warmest congratulations to you!" The telegram also stated: "All members of the Jiu San Society, at this moment when the people of the whole country are jubilantly welcoming victory, commemorating this great day, will surely become more united and more energetic, and continue to support the Chinese People's Liberation Army under the leadership of the Communist Party of China and Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, to eliminate the remaining forces of the Chiang Kai-shek fascist bureaucratic comprador, smash the US imperialist blockade and aggression against China, and strive to build a new democratic China with national independence, civil rights, freedom, and people's livelihood and happiness."

### 九三学社电贺“八一”

1949-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    九三学社电贺“八一”
    【本报讯】九三学社电贺八一建军纪念节，略称：“中国人民革命军事委员会毛主席，中国人民解放军总部朱总司令暨全体指战员：
    欣逢八一建军纪念的伟大日子，谨向你们致崇高的敬礼和热烈的祝贺！”电文又称：“九三学社全体社员在此全国人民欢欣鼓舞迎接胜利的时刻，纪念这一伟大日子，必将愈益团结，愈益奋发，继续在中共和毛主席、朱总司令的领导下，拥护中国人民解放军，为肃清蒋介石法西斯官僚买办残余势力，粉碎美帝对华的封锁和侵略，建设民族独立民权自由民生幸福的新民主主义的中国而奋斗。”
