Title: General Yuan Zhongxian's Statement Published in Soviet Newspapers
Author:
Date: 1949-08-09
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 8th: Moscow dispatch: The Pravda and Izvestia newspapers on the 5th, in prominent positions in their international columns, published the statement of Commander Yuan Zhongxian of the People's Liberation Army's Zhenjiang Front regarding the illegal escape of the British warship Amethyst.
