Title: World Peace Congress Executive Committee Calls for Opposition to War, Launches Peace Defense Demonstration
Author:
Date: 1949-08-23
Page: 3

　　World Peace Congress Executive Committee Calls for Opposition to War
  　Launches Peace Defense Demonstration
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 22nd] Paris Dispatch: The Executive Committee of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress held a meeting in Paris on the 19th, heard reports on determining October 2nd as the International Day of Struggle for Peace and on the launching of preparations for the International Day of Struggle for Peace by various democratic groups, and decided to call on men and women of all countries to unite and fight against war, and to further intensify the struggle for peace. The Executive Committee proposed organizing a demonstration to defend peace in early September. The Executive Committee also decided to send delegations to participate in or send telegrams to the upcoming Soviet Peace Conference, the American Peace Conference, the World Democratic Youth Congress, the Dutch National Peace Conference, and the Peace Week to be organized by peace supporters in Sweden and Norway. The Executive Committee decided to hold a plenary meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress from August 27th to 29th.

### 和大执委会号召反对战争　发起保卫和平示威

1949-08-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　和大执委会号召反对战争
  　发起保卫和平示威
    【新华社北平二十二日电】巴黎讯：世界和平大会常设委员会执委会于十九日在巴黎举行会议，听取了关于确定十月二日为国际和平斗争日及各民主团体发起筹备国际和平斗争日的报告，并决定号召一切国家的男女团结起来，进行反对战争的斗争，进一步加紧为和平而斗争。执委会提议在九月初组织保卫和平的示威。执委会也决定派代表团参加或致电即将召开的苏联拥护和平大会、美洲拥护和平大会、世界民主青年大会、荷兰全国拥护和平大会以及瑞典、挪威的和平拥护者将组织的和平周。执委会决定于八月二十七——二十九日举行世界拥护和平大会常设委员会全体会议。
