Title: Shanghai Industrial and Commercial Circles Comfort Troops and Help Disaster Relief: Donations Reach Thirty Billion

Author:

Date: 1949-08-16
Page: 2

　　Shanghai Industrial and Commercial Circles Comfort Troops and Help Disaster Relief: Donations Reach Thirty Billion
　　[Shanghai News] As of the 10th, donations from Shanghai's industrial and commercial circles to comfort troops and provide disaster relief have reached over 3 billion in cash, along with over 27,000 enamel bowls and thousands of items such as towels, toothbrushes, and undershirts.

### 上海工商界劳军救灾　捐献达三十亿

1949-08-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　上海工商界劳军救灾　捐献达三十亿
    【上海讯】上海工商界劳军救灾捐款，至十日止已达到现款三十亿元以上，另有搪瓷碗二万七千余个，毛巾、牙刷、汗衫等数千件。
