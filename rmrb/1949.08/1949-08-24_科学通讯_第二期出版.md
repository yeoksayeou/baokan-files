　　"Science Bulletin" Second Issue Published
　　[From Our Newspaper] The inaugural issue of "Science Bulletin," published by the Preparatory Committee of the National Congress of Natural Science Workers, was released on July 11th, on the eve of the preparatory meeting of the congress. Now, the second issue of the bulletin was published on the 16th of this month. Its content is a special collection reporting on the preparatory meetings of the National Congress of Natural Science Workers, relating to the future unity and cooperation of Chinese natural science workers, and planned texts of scientific work. In addition to the Standing Committee of the Preparatory Committee of Science Congress distributing the bulletin to preparatory branches across the country, relevant academic groups, and schools, it is also entrusted to the Sanlian Bookstore for distribution and sale.

### “科学通讯”第二期出版

1949-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　“科学通讯”第二期出版
    【本报讯】全国自然科学工作者代表大会筹备会发行的“科学通讯”创刊号，在该筹备大会前夕七月十一日出刊。现在该通讯第二期又已在本月十六日出版。内容为报导全国自然科学工作者代表大会筹备会议的特辑。关系中国自然科学工作者今后之团结合作及科学工作之计划性的文字。该通讯除由科代筹备会常委会分寄全国各地筹备分会，各有关学术团体及各学校外，另交三联书店代为发行出售。
