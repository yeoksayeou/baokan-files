Title: Changsha Military Control Commission Begins Taking Over Public Enterprises
Author:
Date: 1949-08-27
Page: 2

　　Changsha Military Control Commission
  　Begins Taking Over Public Enterprises
    [Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, 26th] The Changsha Municipal Military Control Commission has begun taking over public enterprises such as the Hunan Electric Company, Antimony Manufacturing Factory, Yu Xiang Cotton Mill, the Telecommunications Bureau, and the Central Bank, Bank of China, Bank of Communications, and Farmers Bank. Employees of these enterprises have expressed enthusiastic welcome for the takeover. After the takeover began at the Antimony Manufacturing Factory, all departments except the foundry workshop, which only partially resumed work due to a shortage of raw materials, have fully resumed work. The factory's employees also voluntarily formulated seven production increase covenants, stipulating punctuality, strict economy, and launching competitions. Employees of the Central Bank, Bank of China, Bank of Communications, and Farmers Bank have all established assistance takeover groups to help the takeover personnel count materials and review account books.

### 长沙军管会　开始接管公营企业

1949-08-27
第2版()
专栏：

　　长沙军管会
  　开始接管公营企业
    【新华社长沙二十六日电】长沙市军管会已开始接管湖南电气公司、锑品制造厂、裕湘纱厂、电信局及中央、中国、交通、农民四银行等公营企业。各企业中的职工对接管表示热烈欢迎。锑品制造厂职工在接管开始后，除翻砂间因原料缺少只部分复工外，其他各部门已全部复工。该厂职工并自动订出七项增产公约，规定准时工作，厉行节约，并展开竞赛。中、中、交、农四行职工都成立了协助接管小组，帮助接管人员清点物资、审查帐册。
