Title: Hankou-Zhengzhou "August 1st" Line Opens to Traffic
Author:
Date: 1949-08-03
Page: 1
    [Broadcast by Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Wuhan, 2nd – The Hankou-Zhengzhou section of the Pinghan Railway officially opened to traffic on August 1st. The first train, hauled by the newly repaired "August 1st" locomotive, departed from Hankou for Zhengzhou yesterday afternoon at 2:30 PM. Yesterday evening at 7:00 PM, the Wuhan Military Control Commission Railway Department held a traffic opening ceremony and a memorial meeting for the martyrs who died during the repair work, attended by more than 1,000 employees. At the meeting, Guo Weicheng, director of the Wuhan Military Control Commission Railway Department, called on everyone to work hard, support the front lines, and carry forward the glorious tradition of the Pinghan Railway's "February 7th" strike.

### 汉口郑州“八一”通车

1949-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    汉口郑州“八一”通车
    【北平新华广播电台广播】武汉二日消息：平汉路汉口、郑州段，已经在八月一日正式通车。第一次列车，在昨天下午两点半钟，由新修好的“八一”号机车牵引，从汉口开往郑州。下午七点钟，武汉市军管会铁道处曾经举行通车典礼，和追悼在抢修工作中殉职烈士大会，到有员工一千多人。会上，武汉市军管会铁道处处长郭维城号召大家努力工作，支援前线，发扬平汉路“二七”的光荣传统。
