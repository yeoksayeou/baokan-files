Title: World Democratic Youth Representatives to Convene Soon; Chinese and Soviet Delegations Arrive in Hungarian Capital; Eastern European Democratic Youth Warmly Celebrate Youth Day
Author:
Date: 1949-08-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 11th: Delegations from the Soviet Union, China, Mongolia, and Korea participating in the World Youth Festival arrived in Budapest on the 9th. Delegations from Czechoslovakia, France, Italy, Britain, Spain, the Netherlands, Belgium, the United States, Poland, Sweden, Norway, Denmark, Finland, Bulgaria, Germany, Romania and other countries will also arrive successively. Delegations from Reunion Island, a French colony in Africa, and Vietnam are also en route, and they were warmly welcomed by Polish youth upon their arrival in Poland.

The Peace Relay Race from Northern Europe to Budapest, passing through Sweden, Norway, Denmark, Germany, and Poland, is about to reach its destination. Young athletes continuously transmit the message of peace from the youth of these countries, and grand handover ceremonies are held at each relay point. The report given by the Polish Youth League to the Warsaw relay team to take to Budapest stated: Unity and struggle among all forces for peace can invalidate the sinister plans of the imperialist warmongers.

The youth of the Eastern European people's democratic countries are stepping up their preparations for the Youth Festival and expressing warm congratulations. Romanian youth have raised 500,000 lei (Romanian currency) for travel expenses for delegations from Greece, Spain, and colonial countries. Youth across Poland are holding mass rallies to celebrate Youth Day, demonstrating solidarity with the international movement for peace. They warmly welcomed the African and Vietnamese delegations passing through Poland on their way to Budapest. A Vietnamese representative said at the meeting: "The struggle for progress and freedom waged by Polish youth inspires our struggle against the colonial aggression of the French government." The Bulgarian National Peace Committee sent a telegram to the World Federation of Democratic Youth, congratulating the Youth Festival and the Second World Youth Congress, and called on the delegates of the Congress to speak out loudly and protest against the conspiracy of British and American imperialists to instigate a new war. Youth across Hungary are electing delegates to attend the congress. The Federation of Young Workers and the Federation of Young Peasants have issued declarations, emphasizing the great tasks facing the world's youth in relation to the struggle for peace.

### 世界民主青年代表即将会师  中苏代表团已抵匈京  东欧民主青年热烈庆祝青年节

1949-08-12
第1版()
专栏：

    世界民主青年代表即将会师
    中苏代表团已抵匈京
    东欧民主青年热烈庆祝青年节
    【新华社北平十一日电】苏联、中国、蒙古和朝鲜的参加世界青年节的代表团，已于九日到达布达佩斯。捷、法、意、英、西、荷、比、美、波、瑞（典）、挪、丹、芬、保、德、罗等国的代表团，也将陆续到达。非洲法属殖民地留尼汪岛和越南的代表团，也正在途中，他们到波兰时，受到波兰青年的热烈欢迎。
    从北欧到布达佩斯的和平接力跑，经过瑞典、挪威、丹麦、德国和波兰，即将到达目的地。青年运动员们连续传着这些国家青年的和平音信，并在各个交接点举行盛大的传递典礼。波兰青年联盟交给华沙接力跑小组带到布达佩斯去的报告里说：一切和平力量团结起来进行斗争，就能使帝国主义战争贩子的罪恶计划归于无效。
    东欧人民民主国家的青年，正加紧进行青年节的准备工作，并表示热烈的祝贺。罗马尼亚青年为希腊、西班牙和殖民地青年代表团所募集的旅费，已达到五十万列伊（罗币）。波兰各地青年纷纷举行庆祝青年节群众大会，表现与争取和平的国际运动的团结一致。他们在会上热烈欢迎经过波兰到布达佩斯去的非洲和越南代表团。一位越南代表在会上说：“波兰青年所进行的争取进步与自由的斗争，鼓舞着我们对法国政府的殖民侵略所进行的斗争。”保加利亚全国和平委员会致电世界民主青年联盟，祝贺青年节和第二次世界青年大会，并号召大会的代表们大声疾呼，抗议英美帝国主义者挑拨新战争的阴谋。匈牙利各地青年正在选举出席大会的代表。青年工人联合会和青年农民联合会已发出宣言，着重地指出世界青年所面临的与和平斗争有关的伟大任务。
