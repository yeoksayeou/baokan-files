Title: Viet Minh Victory: French Troops Driven Out of Lan Phi and Four Other Locations
Author:
Date: 1949-08-26
Page: 3

　　Viet Minh Victory
　　French Troops Driven Out of Lan Phi and Four Other Locations
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 25th: TASS Sydney, August 23rd: It is learned here that French troops have been driven out of Lan Phi, Ma Na, Bo Lai, Tai Ding, and Ke Guan (approximately 170 miles north of Hanoi, all place names transliterated) in northern Vietnam. Furthermore, when the Vietnamese army annihilated French troops attacking a village 100 miles north of Saigon, they killed 100 French soldiers.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 25th: TASS Beirut: On August 21st, residents of Damascus, the capital of Syria, held demonstrations against the plan to establish a "Greater Syria" and against the actions of British and American imperialists. The demonstrators demanded that Syria become an independent democratic republic. Police dispersed the demonstrators and arrested eight people.

### 越军获胜　兰菲等五地法军被逐出

1949-08-26
第3版()
专栏：

　　越军获胜
  　兰菲等五地法军被逐出
    【新华社北平二十五日电】塔斯社悉尼二十三日讯，据此间获悉，越南北部兰菲、马拿、伯莱、台定及克关（河内以北一百七十哩，地名均译音）等地法军均被逐出。又越南军于歼灭进攻西贡以北百哩某村的法军时，击毙法军百名。
    【新华社北平二十五日电】塔斯社贝鲁特讯：八月二十一日，叙利亚首都大为士革居民举行示威，反对建立“大叙利亚”的计划，反对英美帝国主义者的行为。示威者要求使叙利亚成为独立的民主共和国。警察曾驱逐示威者，并逮捕了八人。
