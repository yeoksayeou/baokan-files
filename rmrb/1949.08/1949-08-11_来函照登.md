    Letters to the Editor
    Regarding the news item "National Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Symposium to be Held in Beiping This Month; All-China Federation of Trade Unions Issues Notice" published on the first page of your newspaper on August 8th, please correct the names of the telecommunications organizations as follows: "North China Telecommunications Bureau" should be "Telecommunications Bureau of the Central Military Commission of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission" (abbreviated as "Military Commission Telecommunications Bureau"); "Central China Telecommunications Bureau" should be "Central China Telecommunications Administration"; "Shanghai Telecommunications Bureau" should be "East China Telecommunications Administration."
            Secretariat of the Military Commission Telecommunications Bureau, August 9th

### 来函照登
军委电信总局秘书室
1949-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

    来函照登
    贵报八月八日第一版刊载“全国邮电工人工作座谈会本月在平召开全国总工会发出通知”新闻一则，文内之有关电信机构名称应请代为更正如下：“华北电信总局”，应为“中国人民革命军事委员会电信总局”（简称“军委电信总局”）；“华中电信总局”，应为“华中电信管理局”；“上海电信总局”，应为“华东电信管理局”。
            军委电信总局秘书室启  八月九日
