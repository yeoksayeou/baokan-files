Title: People in Various Places Actively Help; South Korean Guerrillas Active; Attack on Gimcheon County Station Annihilates a Squad of Puppet Military Police
Author:
Date: 1949-08-02
Page: 3

    People in Various Places Actively Help
    South Korean Guerrillas Active
    Attack on Gimcheon County Station Annihilates a Squad of Puppet Military Police
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 1st – TASS, Pyongyang dispatch: Guerrilla forces in many counties of various provinces in South Korea, with the help of local residents, are conducting victorious battles against the South Korean puppet "government's" police. On July 22nd, a guerrilla force attacked a station in Gimcheon County, annihilating a squad of police and puppet soldiers, seizing rifles, grenades, and pistols. Another force cut off telephone communications on a highway and blew up several bridges. Guerrillas are also actively operating in Gangwon Province. At the same time, South Korean guerrillas are distributing leaflets in various places, calling on the people to unite under the banner of the Democratic Fatherland Front and fight for the complete unification of Korea.
      (The above place names are all transliterations.)

### 各地人民积极帮助  南鲜游击队活跃  袭击金都县车站歼伪军警一队

1949-08-02
第3版()
专栏：

    各地人民积极帮助
    南鲜游击队活跃
    袭击金都县车站歼伪军警一队
    【新华社北平一日电】塔斯社平壤讯：南朝鲜各道许多县的游击队，在当地居民的帮助下，正对南朝鲜傀儡“政府”的警察进行胜利的战斗。一支游击队在七月二十二日进攻金都县的一个车站时，歼灭了一队警察与伪军，缴获步枪、手榴弹和手枪若干支。另一支则切断了一条公路上的电话交通并炸毁了几座桥梁。游击队也在康温道积极活动着。同时，南朝鲜游击队在各地散布传单，号召人民在民主祖国联合阵线的旗帜下团结起来，为朝鲜的完全统一而斗争。
      （以上地名均系译音）
