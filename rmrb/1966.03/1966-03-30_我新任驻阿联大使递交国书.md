Title: My Newly Appointed Ambassador to the United Arab Republic Presents Credentials

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-30
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 29th: Huang Hua, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of China to the United Arab Republic, presented his credentials to President Nasser of the United Arab Republic on the 28th.

    Present at the presentation of credentials were Minister of Foreign Affairs Mahmoud Riad, the President's acting aide-de-camp Major General Saad Eddin Mitwalli, and the President's Chamberlain Salah Shahed.

    In his speech, Ambassador Huang Hua pointed out that the friendly and cooperative relations between the people of China and the United Arab Republic continue to develop and strengthen on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. Huang Hua said: "The friendly and cooperative relations between our two countries will achieve further development in the noble cause of opposing imperialism, supporting national independence movements, and building our own countries."

    President Nasser said in his reply that he was satisfied with the close relations and friendship between the two countries.

    President Nasser asked Ambassador Huang Hua to convey his and the people of the United Arab Republic's greetings to the People's Republic of China and its leaders.

### 我新任驻阿联大使递交国书

1966-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

    我新任驻阿联大使递交国书
    新华社开罗二十九日电 中国新任驻阿联特命全权大使黄华二十八日向阿联总统纳赛尔递交国书。
    递交国书时在座的有外交部长马哈茂德·里亚德、总统的代理侍从武官萨德·埃丁·米特瓦利少将和总统府侍从长萨拉赫·沙赫德。
    黄华大使在讲话中指出，中国同阿联两国人民的友好和合作关系在和平共处五项原则的基础上不断发展和加强。黄华说：“我们两国的友好合作关系将在反对帝国主义、支持民族独立运动和建设自己国家的崇高事业中取得进一步发展。”
    纳赛尔总统致答词说，他对于两国的亲切关系和友谊表示满意。
    纳赛尔总统要求黄华大使向中华人民共和国及其领导人转达他和阿联人民的问候。
