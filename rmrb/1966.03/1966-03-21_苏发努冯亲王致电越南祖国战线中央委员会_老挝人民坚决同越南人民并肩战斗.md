Title: Prince Souphanouvong Sends Telegram to the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front: The Lao People are Determined to Fight Shoulder to Shoulder with the Vietnamese People

Author:

Date: 1966-03-21
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Kang Kai, 19th. (Dispatch) According to a broadcast by the Pathet Lao Radio on the 19th: Prince Souphanouvong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front, sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front on March 12, expressing warm support for the Vietnamese people's struggle against American aggression and for national salvation.

    The telegram said that the U.S. imperialists are currently carrying out activities to slander the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, creating a pretext for sending U.S. ground forces into Laos and plotting to expand the war of aggression to the entire Indochina. Faced with these cunning schemes and tactics of the U.S. imperialists, the Lao people are determined to fight shoulder to shoulder with the Vietnamese people to resist U.S. imperialism until complete victory is achieved.

    The telegram reiterated its warm support for the clear and just four-point position of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, as well as President Ho Chi Minh's letter to the heads of state of various countries on January 24 this year. The telegram said that the Lao people are determined to further strengthen the solidarity between the Lao and Vietnamese peoples, and resolutely fulfill their sacred responsibility to all fraternal nations.

    Xinhua News Agency, Kang Kai, 19th. (Dispatch) Khamsouk Keola, Minister of Health and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Lao National Union Government, recently made a statement to reporters on the "Week of Solidarity with the Vietnamese People" campaign, saying: The Lao patriotic neutral forces will, together with the Lao Patriotic Front, actively support the struggle of the fraternal Vietnamese people against U.S. imperialism and its lackeys.

### 苏发努冯亲王致电越南祖国战线中央委员会  老挝人民坚决同越南人民并肩战斗

1966-03-21
第3版()
专栏：

    苏发努冯亲王致电越南祖国战线中央委员会
    老挝人民坚决同越南人民并肩战斗
    新华社康开十九日电　据巴特寮电台十九日广播：老挝爱国战线党中央委员会主席苏发努冯亲王三月十二日打电报给越南祖国战线中央委员会，表示热烈支持越南人民的抗美救国斗争。
    电报说，目前，美帝国主义正在展开诬蔑越南民主共和国的活动，为把美国陆军开进老挝制造借口，并阴谋把侵略战争扩大到整个印度支那。面对着美帝国主义的这些狡猾阴谋和手段，老挝人民坚决同越南人民一道并肩战斗，抗击美帝国主义，直到取得完全胜利。
    电报再次表示热烈支持越南民主共和国政府的明确和正义的四项主张，以及胡志明主席在今年一月二十四日给各国首脑的信。电报说，老挝人民决心进一步加强同越南人民之间的团结，并且坚决完成自己对各兄弟民族的神圣责任。
    据新华社康开十九日电　老挝民族团结政府卫生大臣兼代理外交大臣坎苏·高拉最近就开展“声援越南人民周”活动向记者发表谈话时说：老挝爱国中立力量将和老挝爱国战线党一起，积极支持越南兄弟人民反对美帝国主义及其走狗的斗争。
