Title: The Liaison Delegation of the Vietnamese People's Army Cables the International Commission Protesting the Pirate Acts of American-Puppet Warships Attacking Fishing Boats and Abducting Fishermen
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-11
Edition: 3

    New China News Agency, Hanoi, March 10th. The head of the Liaison Delegation of the General Command of the Vietnamese People's Army sent an urgent telegram on the 9th to the Chairman of the International Commission in Vietnam, strongly condemning the pirate acts of the warships of American imperialism and its running dogs that have recently continuously invaded the northern coast of Vietnam, attacking fishing boats and abducting fishermen.
    The telegram pointed out that from February 28th to March 3rd, warships of the United States and its running dogs successively invaded the coastal areas of Thanh Hóa and other provinces of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, attacking thirteen fishing boats and rafts that were fishing at sea, abducting thirteen fishermen, killing three fishermen, and injuring many others.
    The telegram pointed out that these piratical acts of American imperialism and its running dogs are blatant new acts of aggression and war against the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, proving that American imperialism is intensifying and expanding its war of aggression against Vietnam.
    The telegram firmly demanded that the United States and its running dogs must immediately release all the abducted fishermen.

### 越南人民军联络代表团致电国际委员会  抗议美伪军舰袭击渔船劫走渔民的海盗行为

1966-03-11
第3版()
专栏：

    越南人民军联络代表团致电国际委员会
    抗议美伪军舰袭击渔船劫走渔民的海盗行为
    新华社河内十日电  越南人民军总司令部联络代表团团长九日打紧急电报给越南国际委员会主席，严厉谴责美帝国主义和它的走狗的军舰最近接连侵入越南北方沿海袭击渔船、劫走渔民的海盗行径。
    电报指出，从二月二十八日到三月三日，美国及其走狗的军舰接连侵入越南民主共和国清化等省沿海，袭击了正在海上捕鱼的十三只渔船和竹筏，劫走渔民十三人，并打死渔民三人、打伤多人。
    电报指出，美帝国主义和它的走狗的这些海盗行径，是对越南民主共和国的极其明目张胆的新的侵略和战争行动，它证明了美帝国主义正在加强和扩大对越南的侵略战争。
    电报坚决要求美国和它的走狗必须立即释放所有被劫走的渔民。
