Title: Lao Patriotic Front Art Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-31
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, March 30th – The Lao Patriotic Front Art Troupe, a group of forty-eight led by troupe leader Khamma Phoukhong, arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon.
    The Lao Patriotic Front Art Troupe is visiting China and performing at the invitation of the China-Laos Friendship Association. This is their second visit and performance tour in our country.
    Greeting the Lao friends at the station were Lian Guan, President of the China-Laos Friendship Association, Ren Yinglun, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Wang Yuanfang, Ding Bo, Zhou Weizhi, Li Chuan and other figures from the capital's literary and art circles. Madame Kou Ning, wife of Quinim Pholsena, who is in Beijing, was at the station to welcome them.
    Today, the station was filled with the sound of gongs and drums, and artists from the capital played lively Chinese folk tunes to welcome and thank the Lao artists for once again bringing the Lao people's fighting friendship.

### 老挝爱国战线党文工团到京

1966-03-31
第2版()
专栏：

    老挝爱国战线党文工团到京
    新华社三十日讯  老挝爱国战线党文工团一行四十八人，在团长坎马·富贡率领下，今天下午乘火车到达北京。
    老挝爱国战线党文工团是应中国老挝友协邀请来中国访问演出的，这是他们第二次来我国访问演出。
    到车站迎接老挝朋友的，有中老友协会长连贯，对外文协副会长任映仑，以及首都文艺界人士王元方、丁波、周巍峙、李椽等。在北京的老挝贵宁·奔舍那夫人到车站迎接。
    今天车站上锣鼓喧天，首都文艺工作者吹奏起欢快的中国民间曲调，欢迎和感谢老挝艺术家再一次带来老挝人民的战斗友情。
