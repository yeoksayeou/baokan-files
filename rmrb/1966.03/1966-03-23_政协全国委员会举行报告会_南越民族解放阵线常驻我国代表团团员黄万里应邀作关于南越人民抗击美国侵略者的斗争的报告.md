Title: CPPCC National Committee Holds Report Meeting; Huang Wanli, Member of the Permanent Delegation of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front to China, Invited to Give a Report on the South Vietnamese People's Struggle Against the American Aggressors
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-23
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 22nd - The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference held a report meeting this afternoon. Huang Wanli, a member of the permanent delegation of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front to China, was invited to give a report on the heroic South Vietnamese people's struggle against the American aggressors. The report meeting was presided over by Gao Chongmin, Vice Chairman of the CPPCC National Committee. More than 1,500 people attended the report meeting, including members of the CPPCC National Committee, heads of various democratic parties, and people from all walks of life.

### 政协全国委员会举行报告会  南越民族解放阵线常驻我国代表团团员黄万里应邀作关于南越人民抗击美国侵略者的斗争的报告

1966-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会举行报告会
    南越民族解放阵线常驻我国代表团团员黄万里应邀作关于南越人民抗击美国侵略者的斗争的报告
    新华社二十二日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会今天下午举行报告会，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团员黄万里，应邀在会上作了关于英雄的越南南方人民抗击美国侵略者的斗争的报告。报告会由政协全国委员会副主席高崇民主持。参加报告会的，有政协全国委员会委员，各民主党派负责人，以及各界人士等一千五百多人。
