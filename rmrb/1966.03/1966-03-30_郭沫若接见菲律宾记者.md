Title: Guo Moruo Meets with Philippine Journalist
Author:
Date: 1966-03-30
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 29th. Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, met this afternoon with Antonio Araneta, a journalist from the Philippine weekly *The Realist*, who is visiting China at the invitation of our organization. Present at the meeting were Chang Zhiqing, Standing Director of the All-China Journalists Association and Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the Ta Kung Pao.

### 郭沫若接见菲律宾记者

1966-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见菲律宾记者
    新华社二十九日讯  中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若今天下午接见了应邀来我国访问的菲律宾《写实》周刊记者安东尼奥·阿兰尼塔。接见时在座的有中华全国新闻工作者协会常务理事、大公报副社长兼总编辑常芝青等。
