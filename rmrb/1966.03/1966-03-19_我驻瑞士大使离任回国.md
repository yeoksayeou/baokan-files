Title: Our Ambassador to Switzerland Leaves Post for Home

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-19
Edition: 4

    Our Ambassador to Switzerland Leaves Post for Home
    Xinhua News Agency, Bern, March 17th—Li Qingquan, Chinese Ambassador to Switzerland, left his post for home on the 17th. Wettewald, Head of the Protocol Department of the Swiss Federal Political Department, and some diplomatic envoys to Switzerland went to the Chinese Embassy to bid farewell to Ambassador Li Qingquan.

### 我驻瑞士大使离任回国

1966-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

    我驻瑞士大使离任回国
    新华社伯尔尼十七日电　中国驻瑞士大使李清泉十七日离任回国。瑞士联邦政治部交际处处长韦特瓦尔德和一些国家驻瑞士使节到中国大使馆向李清泉大使告别。
