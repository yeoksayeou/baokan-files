Title: Ghana President Nkrumah Leaves Beijing; Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Others See Him Off at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1966-03-05
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 4th—Ghanaian President Dr. Kwame Nkrumah departed Beijing by special plane on February 28th.
    Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman Lin Feng and others went to the airport to see him off.

### 加纳总统恩克鲁玛离开北京  刘少奇主席周恩来总理等到机场欢送

1966-03-05
第1版()
专栏：

    加纳总统恩克鲁玛离开北京
    刘少奇主席周恩来总理等到机场欢送
    新华社四日讯　加纳共和国总统克瓦米·恩克鲁玛博士已在二月二十八日乘专机离开北京。
    刘少奇主席，周恩来总理，林枫副委员长等前往机场欢送。
