Title: List of Personnel Accompanying Chairman Liu and Madame on Their Visit Abroad

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-27
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 26th – In addition to Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi and his wife Zhang Qian, the following personnel are accompanying Chairman of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi and his wife Wang Guangmei on their visit to the Islamic Republic of Pakistan:
    Zhang Hanfu Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs
    Wang Dongxing Vice Minister of Public Security
    Feng Xuan Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress
    Wang Daohan Vice Chairman of the Commission for Economic Relations with Foreign Countries
    Lai Yali Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs
    Zhang Tong Director of the First Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs

**Named Entities**

### 陪同刘主席和夫人出国访问人员名单

1966-03-27

    汪道涵　对外经济联络委员会副主任
    赖亚力　外交部礼宾司司长
    张　彤　外交部第一亚洲司司长
