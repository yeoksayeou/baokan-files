Title: Chairman Ho and Premier Pham Van Dong Watch Performance by Chinese Acrobatic Troupe and Receive All Comrades of the Acrobatic Troupe
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-28
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 26th  President Ho Chi Minh and Premier Pham Van Dong of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam watched a performance by the Chinese Railway Art Troupe's Acrobatic Troupe on the evening of the 26th and received all the comrades of the Acrobatic Troupe.
    During the reception, President Ho Chi Minh and Premier Pham Van Dong had cordial talks with the acrobatic troupe's performers.
    Present at the performance and reception were Vietnamese leaders Hoang Van Hoan, To Huu, and Phan Trong Tue.
    Zhu Qiwu, the Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam, and Du Zhanchao, the cultural counselor of the embassy, were also present. On the morning of the 26th, Phan Trong Tue, Minister of Transportation of Vietnam, on behalf of the Vietnamese government, awarded the First Class Labor Medal to the Chinese Railway Art Troupe's Acrobatic Troupe.

### 胡主席范文同总理观看我杂技团演出  并接见了杂技团全体同志

1966-03-28
第3版()
专栏：

    胡主席范文同总理观看我杂技团演出
    并接见了杂技团全体同志
    新华社河内二十六日电  越南民主共和国主席胡志明和政府总理范文同二十六日晚上观看了中国铁路文工团杂技团的演出，并接见了杂技团全体同志。
    在接见时，胡志明主席和范文同总理同杂技团演员们进行了亲切的谈话。
    观看演出和接见时在座的还有越南领导人黄文欢、素友和潘仲慧。
    中国驻越南大使朱其文，大使馆文化参赞杜展潮也在座。二十六日上午，越南交通运输部长潘仲慧代表越南政府把一级劳动勋章授予中国铁路文工团杂技团。
