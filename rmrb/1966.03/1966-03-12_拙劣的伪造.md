    A Clumsy Forgery

    Recently, an Arabic booklet titled "The Role Played by the Soviet Union in the Establishment of Israel" has been circulating in some Arab countries. The content of this booklet consists of speeches made by Soviet representatives Gromyko, Malik, and others at the United Nations between 1947 and 1949 regarding the Soviet Union's support for the Anglo-American proposal for the partition of Palestine and the establishment of the State of Israel. This booklet was published by imperialists and their running dogs under the false pretenses of the Palestine Liberation Organization's Beijing office and the China Foreign Languages Press in Beijing. The China Foreign Languages Press has never published such a booklet, nor has the Palestine Liberation Organization's Beijing office or the Palestine Liberation Organization requested the China Foreign Languages Press to publish such a booklet. This booklet is a complete forgery. (Attached picture)

### 拙劣的伪造

1966-03-12
第4版()
专栏：

    拙劣的伪造
    最近，在一些阿拉伯国家流传一本《苏联在伊色列建立时所扮演的角色》阿拉伯文小册子。这本小册子的内容是一九四七至一九四九年期间苏联代表葛罗米柯、马立克等人在联合国发表的关于苏联支持英、美提出的巴勒斯坦分治方案和建立以色列国的一些言论。这本小册子是帝国主义及其走狗假冒巴勒斯坦解放组织驻北京办事处和中国北京外文出版社的名义出版的。中国外文出版社从来没有出版过这样一本小册子，巴勒斯坦解放组织驻北京办事处或者巴勒斯坦解放组织也没有要求过中国外文出版社出版这样一本小册子。这本小册子完全是伪造的。（附图片）
    上图为伪造的《苏联在伊色列建立时所扮演的角色》阿拉伯文小册子的封底里页下半部分——中文书名和出版者等的放大字样。书名把我们通常所译的“以色列”写成“伊色列”，说明这本小册子的伪造技巧十分拙劣。　　　　新华社稿
