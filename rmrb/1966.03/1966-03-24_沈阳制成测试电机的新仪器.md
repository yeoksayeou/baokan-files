Title: Shenyang Manufactures New Instrument for Testing Motors
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-24
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang - A new type of instrument for testing motors of different capacities and various speeds, the motor torque-speed tester, has been successfully trial-produced by the Shenyang Electrical Scientific Research Institute.
    Using this instrument, the performance of motors can be correctly inspected and judged. At present, most motor manufacturers in China do not have this testing equipment; although some factories have imported dynamometers, it takes three to five skilled personnel two to three days to complete the test of a motor's torque and speed. The newly manufactured motor torque-speed tester uses a charge converter as the speed measuring element. It has no mechanical connection, making it simple to use, and only requires one person and a few minutes to complete the test of a motor.
    Organized by the Shenyang Municipal Science and Technology Commission, relevant domestic scientific research and production units have evaluated the instrument and believe that it has a novel structure, good performance, and strong completeness. With slight improvements, it can be put into production.

### 沈阳制成测试电机的新仪器

1966-03-24
第2版()
专栏：

    沈阳制成测试电机的新仪器
    新华社沈阳电　用来测试电机的不同容量和各种转速的新型仪器——电机转矩转速测试仪，已经由沈阳市电气科学研究所试制成功。
    使用这种仪器，可以正确地检查判别电机的性能。目前我国大部分电机制造厂没有这种测试设备；有些工厂虽然有进口的测功机，但是测试一台电机的转矩、转速，需要三、五个熟练人员花两三天时间才能完成。新制成的电机转矩转速测试仪，是采用充电变换器作为测速元件的，它没有机械连接，因而使用简便，测试一台电机只需要一个人用几分钟就可以完成。
    由沈阳市科学技术委员会组织的国内有关科学研究和生产单位鉴定，认为这种仪器结构新颖、性能良好，成套性强，稍加改进就可投入生产。
