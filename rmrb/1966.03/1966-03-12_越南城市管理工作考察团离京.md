Title: Vietnamese Urban Management Delegation Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-03-12
Edition: 4

    Vietnamese Urban Management Delegation Leaves Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, March 11th: The Vietnamese Urban Management Delegation, headed by Wu Gongfu, left Beijing this morning to visit and inspect southern China. Those who saw them off at the airport included Zhao Fan, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Vice Mayor of Beijing, as well as responsible persons from the Ministry of Internal Affairs and relevant departments. Su An, Commercial Counselor of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, also went to the airport to see them off.
    Last night, Zhao Fan held a banquet to bid farewell to the Vietnamese friends. The banquet was filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere. The delegation came to China for an inspection visit based on the Sino-Vietnamese Scientific and Technological Cooperation Agreement.

### 越南城市管理工作考察团离京

1966-03-12
第4版()
专栏：

    越南城市管理工作考察团离京
    新华社十一日讯　以武公辅为首的越南城市管理工作考察团，今晨离开北京去我国南方考察访问。前往机场送行的，有中共北京市委书记处书记、北京市副市长赵凡，以及内务部和各有关方面负责人。越南驻中国大使馆商务参赞苏安等也前往机场送行。
    昨晚，赵凡举行宴会欢送越南朋友，宴会上充满热烈友好气氛。考察团是根据中越两国科学技术合作协定来我国进行考察的。
