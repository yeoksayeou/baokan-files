Title: My New Ambassador Presents Credentials to the King of Nepal
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-18
Edition: 5

    My New Ambassador Presents Credentials to the King of Nepal
    Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, March 17th – Yang Gongsu, the new Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, presented his credentials to King Mahendra of Nepal on the afternoon of the 16th.
    After the presentation of credentials, King Mahendra held friendly talks with Yang Gongsu.
    Yang Gongsu conveyed greetings from Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, to the King. King Mahendra also conveyed his greetings to Chairman Liu Shaoqi.



**Named Entities**


尼泊尔 Nepal
马亨德拉 Mahendra
杨公素 Yang Gongsu
刘少奇 Liu Shaoqi
新华社 Xinhua News Agency

### 我新任大使向尼泊尔国王递交国书

1966-03-18
第5版()
专栏：

    我新任大使向尼泊尔国王递交国书
    新华社加德满都十七日电　中国新任驻尼泊尔大使杨公素，十六日下午向尼泊尔国王马亨德拉递交了国书。
    递交国书后，马亨德拉国王同杨公素进行了友好的谈话。
    杨公素向马亨德拉国王转达了中华人民共和国主席刘少奇对国王的问候。马亨德拉国王也向刘少奇主席表示问候。
