Title: Syrian Chargé d'Affaires Farah Holds Reception Commemorating the Syrian March 8th Revolution Day; Vice Premier Chen Yi and Others Invited to Attend; Hosts and Guests Jointly Wish for the Continuous Development of Friendship Between the Two Peoples
Author:
Date: 1966-03-15
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 14th – Farah, the Chargé d'Affaires of the Embassy of the Arab Syrian Republic in China, held a reception tonight to commemorate the Syrian March 8th Revolution Day.
    Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, and responsible persons from relevant departments Kong Yuan, Nan Hanchen, Li Tianyou, Lei Renmin, Ding Xilin, Yang Lin, and others, were invited to attend the reception.
    Chargé d'Affaires Farah and Vice Premier Chen Yi spoke at the reception, jointly wishing for the continuous development of friendship between the Chinese and Syrian peoples.
    In his speech, Chargé d'Affaires Farah introduced the achievements made in various aspects of Syria in the three years since the March 8th Revolution. He also talked about the Palestinian issue. He said that Syria firmly believes that the entire Arab people are capable of settling accounts with Israel, the running dog of colonialism, and its backers. The Chargé d'Affaires pointed out that Syria's position on international issues is based on opposing world imperialism and world colonialism. Syria pursues a policy of neutrality and non-alignment, and supports and stands in solidarity with all national liberation movements of people in the world against colonialism. He congratulated the friendly Chinese people for the great victories and achievements made due to the implementation of the doctrines and instructions of their wise revolutionary leader Mao Zedong.
    In his speech, Vice Premier Chen Yi said that in recent years, Syria has made considerable achievements in safeguarding national independence, developing the national economy, and building its own country. In international affairs, the Syrian government and people oppose the aggressive and war policies of U.S. imperialism, support national liberation movements, and sympathize with and support the heroic Vietnamese people in their struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation. The Chinese people appreciate and support your just stance.
    Vice Premier Chen Yi said that China and Syria are very friendly countries. In the past year, the leaders of our two countries have held many friendly meetings, enhanced mutual understanding, and promoted friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries. This conforms to the wishes of the people of both countries and is also conducive to the common cause of Asian and African people uniting against imperialism and safeguarding world peace. We firmly believe that in the struggle against imperialism headed by the United States and in the construction of our own countries, the friendly and cooperative relations between our two countries will be further developed, and the profound friendship between the two peoples will be continuously strengthened.
    Vice Premier Chen Yi reiterated that the Chinese people firmly support the struggle of the Arab people and the Palestinian people against imperialism and new and old colonialism.
    Diplomatic envoys from various countries to China were also invited to attend the reception.

### 叙利亚临时代办法拉赫举行招待会  纪念叙利亚三月八日革命节  陈毅副总理等应邀出席  宾主共祝两国人民友谊不断发展

1966-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

    叙利亚临时代办法拉赫举行招待会
    纪念叙利亚三月八日革命节
    陈毅副总理等应邀出席  宾主共祝两国人民友谊不断发展
    新华社十四日讯　阿拉伯叙利亚共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办法拉赫今晚举行招待会，纪念叙利亚三月八日革命节。
    国务院副总理陈毅，人大常委会副委员长郭沫若，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，各有关方面负责人孔原、南汉宸、李天佑、雷任民、丁西林、杨琳等，应邀出席了招待会。
    法拉赫临时代办和陈毅副总理在招待会上讲了话，共祝中叙两国人民的友谊不断发展。
    法拉赫临时代办在讲话中介绍了叙利亚三月八日革命以来的三年中各方面取得的成就。还谈到巴勒斯坦问题。他说，叙利亚完全相信，全体阿拉伯人民是有能力同殖民主义的走狗以色列及其扶植者算账的。临时代办指出，叙利亚在国际问题上的立场，是建立在反对世界帝国主义和世界殖民主义之上的。叙利亚奉行中立和不结盟政策，支持和声援世界上一切反殖民主义的人民解放运动。他祝贺友好的中国人民由于执行他的英明的革命领袖毛泽东的学说和指示所取得的巨大胜利和成就。
    陈毅副总理在讲话中说，几年来，叙利亚在维护国家独立、发展民族经济和建设自己国家等方面取得了不少成就。在国际事务中，叙利亚政府和人民反对美帝国主义的侵略政策和战争政策，支持民族解放运动，同情和声援英雄的越南人民抗美救国斗争。中国人民赞赏和支持你们这种正义的立场。
    陈毅副总理说，中叙两国是很友好的国家。近一年来，我们两国领导人进行了多次友好会晤，增进了相互了解，促进了两国友好合作关系。这符合两国人民的愿望，也有助于亚非人民团结反帝和维护世界和平的共同事业。我们深信，在反对以美国为首的帝国主义和建设自己国家的斗争中，我们两国友好合作关系将进一步发展，两国人民间的深厚友谊将不断加强。
    陈毅副总理重申，中国人民坚决支持阿拉伯人民和巴勒斯坦人民反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义的斗争。
    各国驻中国的使节也应邀出席了招待会。
