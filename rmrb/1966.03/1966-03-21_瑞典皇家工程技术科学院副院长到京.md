Title: Vice President of the Royal Swedish Academy of Engineering Sciences Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-21
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 20th – At the invitation of the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Metrology Technology and Instrument Manufacturing Society, Mr. Malmström, Vice President of the Royal Swedish Academy of Engineering Sciences, who is visiting our country, arrived in Beijing today. He was greeted at the airport by Gao Gewu, Vice Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Metrology Technology and Instrument Manufacturing Society, and others.

### 瑞典皇家工程技术科学院副院长到京

1966-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典皇家工程技术科学院副院长到京
    新华社二十日讯　应中国计量技术与仪器制造学会筹备委员会的邀请，来我国访问的瑞典皇家工程技术科学院副院长马姆斯特罗姆今天到达北京。前往机场迎接的，有中国计量技术与仪器制造学会筹备委员会副主任委员高戈伍等。
