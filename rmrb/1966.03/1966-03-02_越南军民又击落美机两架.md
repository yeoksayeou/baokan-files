Title: Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Two More American Planes

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-02
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 1st (electric dispatch) – It was announced here on the 1st that on the afternoon of February 27th, several American bandit planes bombed and strafed a residential area in the northern part of Quang Binh Province, North Vietnam. The local military and civilians shot down an American jet fighter with their first volley of fire.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 28th (electric dispatch) – It was announced here on the 28th that the military and civilians of Nghe An Province shot down an American plane that was bombing a residential area in Hung Nguyen County of this province on the 25th.

### 越南军民又击落美机两架

1966-03-02
第3版()
专栏：

    越南军民又击落美机两架
    新华社河内一日电  据这里一日公布，美国强盗飞机多架，二月二十七日下午对越南北方广平省北部的一个居民区进行轰炸和扫射。当地军民用第一轮炮火就击落了一架美国喷气式飞机。
    新华社河内二十八日电据这里二十八日公布，义安省军民二十五日击落轰炸这个省兴源县居民区的美国飞机一架。
