Title: The Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Hosts a Banquet to Welcome the Pakistani Cultural and Art Troupe; Vice Premier Tan Zhenlin Receives Pakistani Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-03-25
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 24th. Vice Premier of the State Council Tan Zhenlin received the Pakistani Cultural and Art Troupe led by Head of the Delegation Rahman and Deputy Head of the Delegation Madam Aziz this evening, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Yang Chun, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Zhao Xinchu, Vice Minister of Culture; Xu Jingwu, Deputy Director of the First Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Fang Shishan, Vice President of the Sino-Pakistani Friendship Association; and others.
    Durrani, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Pakistani Embassy in China, and Madam Rocha, wife of the Ambassador, were also present.
    Xinhua News Agency, March 24th. The Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries hosted a banquet this evening to warmly welcome the Pakistani Cultural and Art Troupe led by Head of the Delegation Rahman and Deputy Head of the Delegation Madam Aziz.
    Vice Premier of the State Council Tan Zhenlin attended the banquet.
    At the banquet, which was filled with a friendly and warm atmosphere, Yang Chun, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, delivered a speech. She said: Just as our Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Vice Premier Chen Yi are about to visit our friendly neighbor, the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, we are very pleased that the Pakistani Cultural and Art Troupe, headed by Mr. Rahman and Madam Aziz, has come to our country for a visit and performance.
    Yang Chun said, we believe that the visit and performance of Pakistani artists to our country will make a greater contribution to promoting the friendship and cultural exchange between the people of the two countries.
    Rahman said in his speech, we bring the warmest congratulations and greetings from 100 million Pakistani people to the 650 million Chinese people. You are our most friendly and great neighbor. When we were in the most difficult time, when our country was subjected to foreign aggression, and the Pakistani people united to resist the aggressors, the Chinese people gave us support. Our determination to resist the enemy and the help of the Chinese people are a great source of strength for the Pakistani people.
    Rahman mentioned that the main purpose of this visit to China was not to perform, but to learn from Chinese artists.
    He said that the mutual visits of art troupes from China and Pakistan are good opportunities for mutual learning.
    Also present at the banquet were Zhao Xinchu, Fang Shishan, Xu Jingwu, Zhou Weizhi, Ding Bo, Wang Yuanfang, Dai Ailian and other responsible persons from relevant departments and people from literary and art circles in the capital.
    Durrani, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Pakistani Embassy in China, and Madam Rocha, wife of the Ambassador, were invited to attend the banquet.
    The Pakistani guests arrived in Beijing by plane this morning. They were invited by our Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit and perform in our country. (Attached picture)

### 我对外文委设宴欢迎巴基斯坦文化艺术团  谭震林副总理接见巴基斯坦客人

1966-03-25
第4版()
专栏：

    我对外文委设宴欢迎巴基斯坦文化艺术团
    谭震林副总理接见巴基斯坦客人
    新华社二十四日讯 国务院副总理谭震林今晚接见由团长拉赫曼、副团长阿奇兹夫人率领的巴基斯坦文化艺术团，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有对外文委副主任杨纯、文化部副部长赵辛初，外交部第一亚洲司副司长徐净武，中巴友协副会长方石珊等。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使馆临时代办杜拉尼和罗查大使夫人也在座。
    据新华社二十四日讯对外文化联络委员会今晚举行宴会，热烈欢迎由团长拉赫曼、副团长阿奇兹夫人率领的巴基斯坦文化艺术团。
    国务院副总理谭震林出席了宴会。
    在充满友好热烈气氛的宴会上，对外文委副主任杨纯讲话。她说：正当我国刘少奇主席和陈毅副总理即将访问我们友好的邻邦巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国的时候，以拉赫曼先生和阿奇兹夫人为首的巴基斯坦文化艺术团来到我国进行访问和演出，我们感到十分高兴。
    杨纯说，我们相信，巴基斯坦艺术家们又一次来我国访问演出，必将对促进两国人民的友谊和文化交流做出更大的贡献。
    拉赫曼在讲话中说，我们带来一亿巴基斯坦人民对六亿五千万中国人民最热烈的祝贺和问候。你们是我们最友好的伟大邻邦。当我们在最困难的时候，我们国家遭到外来侵略、巴基斯坦人民团结一致抵抗侵略者的时候，中国人民给了我们支持。我们抵抗敌人的决心和中国人民的帮助，是巴基斯坦人民很大的力量的源泉。
    拉赫曼谈到这次来中国访问，主要不是为了表演，而是为了向中国艺术家们学习的。
    他说，巴中两国艺术团的相互访问，是互相学习的好机会。
    出席宴会的，还有赵辛初、方石珊、徐净武、周巍峙、丁波、王元方、戴爱莲等有关方面负责人和首都文艺界人士。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使馆临时代办杜拉尼和罗查大使夫人，应邀出席了宴会。
    巴基斯坦客人是今天上午乘飞机到京的。他们是应我对外文委邀请来我国进行访问演出。（附图片）
    三月二十四日，国务院副总理谭震林接见以团长拉赫曼（前左八），副团长阿奇兹夫人（前右五）率领的巴基斯坦文化艺术团。
    新华社记者  刘长忠摄
