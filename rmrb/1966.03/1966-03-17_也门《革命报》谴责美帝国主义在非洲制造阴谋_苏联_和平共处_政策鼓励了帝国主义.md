Title: Yemen's "Revolutionary News" Condemns US Imperialist Conspiracies in Africa; Soviet "Peaceful Coexistence" Policy Encourages Imperialism
Author:
Date: 1966-03-17
Edition: 4

    Sanaa, March 14th (Xinhua) – Yemen's "Revolutionary News" published an article on March 11th condemning the US imperialist policy of aggression and the Soviet leadership's so-called policy of "peaceful coexistence."
    The article stated that in recent days, Africa has become a hotbed of imperialist conspiracies. Imperialism, led by the United States, is playing the leading role in these conspiracies. US imperialism's strategy is based on the principle that "might makes right."
    After exposing a series of imperialist conspiracies in Africa, the article pointed out that "the Soviet Union's attitude toward imperialist conspiracies encourages imperialism to continue its conspiracies, and encourages imperialism to undermine the good situation in Africa. While the Soviet Union adopts the so-called policy of peaceful coexistence, the imperialists continue to carry out their conspiracies."
    The article said, therefore, "the policy of peaceful coexistence adopted by the Soviet Union shows that the Soviet Union has surrendered to imperialism."

### 也门《革命报》谴责美帝国主义在非洲制造阴谋  苏联“和平共处”政策鼓励了帝国主义

1966-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

    也门《革命报》谴责美帝国主义在非洲制造阴谋
    苏联“和平共处”政策鼓励了帝国主义
    据新华社萨那十四日电　也门《革命报》三月十一日刊登一篇文章，谴责美帝国主义侵略政策和苏联领导的所谓“和平共处”政策。
    文章说，这几天来，非洲已变成帝国主义进行阴谋活动的场所。以美国为首的帝国主义在这些阴谋中，扮演了主要的角色。美帝国主义的战略就是建立在：“权力属于强者”的基础上。
    文章在揭露帝国主义在非洲进行一系列的阴谋活动之后指出，“苏联对帝国主义阴谋的态度是鼓励帝国主义继续进行阴谋活动，鼓励帝国主义破坏非洲的好形势。当苏联采取所谓和平共处的政策的时候，帝国主义者照样进行它们的阴谋活动。”
    文章说，因此，“苏联所采取的和平共处政策表明，苏联向帝国主义投降”。
