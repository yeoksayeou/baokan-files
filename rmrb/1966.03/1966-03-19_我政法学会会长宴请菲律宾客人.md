Title: President of China Political Science and Law Society Hosts Banquet for Filipino Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-03-19
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 18th: Wu Defeng, President of the China Political Science and Law Society, and his wife hosted a banquet tonight for Mrs. Maria Kalaw Katigbak, head of the visiting Philippine study delegation and Senator of the Philippine Liberal Party, as well as delegation members Attorney Nestor Kalaw and Isagani Yambot, who are currently visiting Beijing. Attending the banquet as guests were Zhang Zhizhang, Gao Chongmin, Zhang Youyu, Wang Jiren, and Zhang Jiexun, among other figures from various fields.

### 我政法学会会长宴请菲律宾客人

1966-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

    我政法学会会长宴请菲律宾客人
    新华社十八日讯　中国政治法律学会会长吴德峰和夫人今晚设宴招待正在北京访问的菲律宾考察代表团团长、菲律宾自由党参议员玛丽亚·卡劳·卡蒂瓦克夫人以及代表团成员、内斯托尔·卡佬律师和伊萨加尼·扬博特。出席宴会作陪的，有张志让、高崇民、张友渔、王吉仁、张洁珣等各方面人士。
