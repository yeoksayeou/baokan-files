Title: Delegation of the National Union of Workers of Portuguese Guinea Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-30
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 28th: The delegation of the National Union of Workers of Portuguese Guinea, led by Luís Almeida Cabral, General Secretary of the National Union of Workers of Portuguese Guinea, arrived in Beijing by plane yesterday for a visit to our country at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. Kang Yonghe, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others welcomed them at the airport.

**Named Entities**

中国    China
北京  Beijing

### 葡属几内亚工人全国联合会代表团到京

1966-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

    葡属几内亚工人全国联合会代表团到京
    新华社二十八日讯  应中华全国总工会邀请来我国访问的以葡属几内亚工人全国联合会总书记路易斯·阿尔美达·卡布拉尔为首的葡属几内亚工人全国联合会代表团，昨天乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中华全国总工会副主席康永和等。
