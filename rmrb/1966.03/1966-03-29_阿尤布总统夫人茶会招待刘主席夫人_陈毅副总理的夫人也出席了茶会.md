Title: President Ayub's Wife Hosts Tea Party for Chairman Liu's Wife; Vice Premier Chen Yi's Wife Also Attends

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-29
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi, 27th—President Ayub Khan's wife of Pakistan held a tea party at the Presidential Palace here this afternoon to entertain Chairman Liu Shaoqi's wife.

    Vice Premier Chen Yi's wife and the wife of Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan, Ding Guoyu, also attended the tea party.

    Also present at the tea party were: the wives of the ministers of the Pakistani central government and other prominent figures in women's circles.

    The hosts and guests had a cordial conversation at the tea party.

### 阿尤布总统夫人茶会招待刘主席夫人  陈毅副总理的夫人也出席了茶会

1966-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尤布总统夫人茶会招待刘主席夫人
    陈毅副总理的夫人也出席了茶会
    新华社拉瓦尔品第二十七日电  巴基斯坦总统阿尤布·汗的夫人二十七日下午在这里的总统府举行茶会，招待刘少奇主席夫人。
    陈毅副总理的夫人和中国驻巴基斯坦大使丁国钰的夫人也出席了茶会。
    出席茶会的还有：巴基斯坦中央政府各部长的夫人和其他妇女界著名人士。
    在茶会上，宾主双方进行了亲切的交谈。
