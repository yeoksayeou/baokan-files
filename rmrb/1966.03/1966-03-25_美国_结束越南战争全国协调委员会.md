Title: U.S. "National Coordinating Committee to End the War in Vietnam"
Author:
Date: 1966-03-25
Edition: 3

    The U.S. "National Coordinating Committee to End the War in Vietnam" was formed in August 1965, with its headquarters in Madison, Wisconsin. It is a national liaison organization for the American people's movement against the war of aggression in Vietnam. At that time, thirty-three peace groups participated in the committee. Now, about two hundred groups are associated with this committee, including national peace organizations, student organizations, and political organizations. Last October, the committee organized the first three-day "International Days of Protest" demonstrations in nearly one hundred cities in the United States, with approximately 100,000 people participating. ### 美国“结束越南战争全国协调委员会”

1966-03-25
第3版()
专栏：资料

    美国“结束越南战争全国协调委员会”
    美国“结束越南战争全国协调委员会”在一九六五年八月组成，总部设在威斯康星州的麦迪逊市。它是美国人民反对侵略越南战争运动的一个全国性联络组织，当时参加这个委员会的有三十三个和平团体。现在，同这个委员会有联系的约有二百个团体，其中包括全国性的和平组织、学生组织和政治组织。去年十月，这个委员会组织了美国近一百个城市约十万人参加的连续三天的第一次“国际抗议日”示威。（附图片）
