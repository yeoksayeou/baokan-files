Title: Ji Lianbo
Author: Ji Lianbo
Date: 1966-03-22
Edition: 4

    On March 21st, the China-Pakistan Friendship Association held a reception to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan. Vice Premier Chen Yi talked with Durrani (second from the right), Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Pakistani Embassy in China, and his wife at the reception. The first from the right is Zhang Jie, Vice President of the China-Pakistan Friendship Association. Xinhua News Agency reporter Ji Lianbo took the photo.

专栏：

    三月二十一日，中国巴基斯坦友好协会举行招待会，庆祝巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国成立十周年。陈毅副总理同巴基斯坦驻华大使馆临时代办杜拉尼（右二）和夫人在招待会上交谈。右一是中巴友协副会长张杰。　新华社记者　冀连波摄
