Title: Japanese Communist Party Delegation Leaves Beijing for Guangzhou; Zhu De, Peng Zhen, Tan Zhenlin and Other Comrades Welcomed and Saw Them Off at the Airport

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-30
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 29th—The Japanese Communist Party delegation led by Comrade Kenji Miyamoto, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, concluded its visit to China and left Beijing for Guangzhou.
    Those who saw them off at the airport included Comrade Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Comrades Peng Zhen and Tan Zhenlin, members of the Political Bureau and Secretaries of the Secretariat, Comrade Kang Sheng, alternate member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat, Comrades Liao Chengzhi, Liu Ningyi, members of the Central Committee, and Comrade Zhao Yimin, alternate member of the Central Committee.

### 日共代表团离京前往广州  朱德彭真谭震林等同志到机场欢送

1966-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

    日共代表团离京前往广州
    朱德彭真谭震林等同志到机场欢送
    新华社二十九日讯  由日本共产党中央委员会总书记宫本显治同志率领的日本共产党代表团，结束了在中国的访问，离开北京前往广州。
    到机场送行的，有中共中央副主席朱德，政治局委员、书记处书记彭真、谭震林，政治局候补委员、书记处书记康生，中央委员廖承志、刘宁一，中央候补委员赵毅敏等同志。
