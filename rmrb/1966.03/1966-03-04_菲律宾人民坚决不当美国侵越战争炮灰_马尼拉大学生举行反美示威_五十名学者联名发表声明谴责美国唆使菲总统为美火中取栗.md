Title: Philippine People Resolutely Refuse to Be Cannon Fodder for the US War of Aggression Against Vietnam; Manila University Students Hold Anti-American Demonstrations; Fifty Scholars Jointly Issue Statement Condemning the US for Instigating the Philippine President to Pull Chestnuts out of the Fire for the US

Author:

Date: 1966-03-04
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 3rd, Manila Dispatch: The struggle of the Philippine people against US imperialism's attempt to lure the Philippine authorities into providing cannon fodder for its war of aggression against Vietnam continues to develop.
    According to a report in the *Manila Times* on the 2nd, university students in Manila once again held an anti-American demonstration in front of the Congress building on the 1st, protesting against US imperialism's aggression against Vietnam and the Philippine authorities' preparation to dispatch two thousand Philippine "engineers" and "security forces" to South Vietnam.
    This is the eighth large-scale anti-American demonstration held by people in various parts of the Philippines since the beginning of this year.
    The *Manila Chronicle* reported on the 1st that fifty professors and researchers from Philippine universities recently jointly issued a statement opposing the provision of cannon fodder for the US war of aggression against Vietnam. The statement pointed out that "no amount of platitudes and demagogic propaganda can obscure the obvious fact that President Marcos made the decision to provide military assistance to South Vietnam (the puppet regime) under the instigation of US forces."
    Salvador Araneta, president of Araneta University in the Philippines, and Alejandro Lichauco, dean of the university's Economic Research Institute, also wrote to members of Congress on February 22, opposing the Philippine authorities' dispatch of troops to South Vietnam.
    According to a report in the *News Evening Post* on the 1st, a Philippine women's organization wrote to Philippine President Marcos on February 28 opposing the dispatch of troops to South Vietnam.
    Francisco Nepomuceno, Governor of Pampanga Province in the Philippines, told reporters on the 1st that the plan to send Philippine troops to South Vietnam has aroused strong opposition in this country because this plan is "to sell out our compatriots and carve up the flesh and blood of Filipinos for a mere tens of millions of dollars."

### 菲律宾人民坚决不当美国侵越战争炮灰  马尼拉大学生举行反美示威  五十名学者联名发表声明谴责美国唆使菲总统为美火中取栗

1966-03-04
第4版()
专栏：

    菲律宾人民坚决不当美国侵越战争炮灰
    马尼拉大学生举行反美示威
    五十名学者联名发表声明谴责美国唆使菲总统为美火中取栗
    新华社三日讯　马尼拉消息：菲律宾人民反对美帝国主义诱迫菲律宾当局为它侵越战争提供炮灰的斗争继续发展。
    据《马尼拉时报》二日报道，马尼拉的大学生一日再一次在国会大厦前举行反美示威，抗议美帝国主义侵略越南和菲律宾当局准备派遣二千名菲律宾“工兵”和“安全部队”去南越。
    这是今年以来菲律宾各地人民举行的第八次规模较大的反美示威。
    《马尼拉纪事报》一日报道，五十名菲律宾大学的教授和研究人员最近联名发表声明，反对为美国侵越战争提供炮灰。声明指出，“不管多少泛泛空谈和蛊惑人心的宣传都不能掩盖这样一个显而易见的事实，即马科斯总统是在美国势力唆使下作出向南越（傀儡政权）提供军事援助的决定的。”
    菲律宾阿拉内塔大学校长萨尔瓦多·阿拉内塔和这家大学的经济研究学院院长亚历杭德罗·利查科二月二十二日也写信给国会议员，反对菲律宾当局派兵去南越。
    据《新闻晚报》一日报道，菲律宾的一个妇女组织二月二十八日写信给菲总统马科斯反对派兵去南越。
    菲律宾邦板牙省省长弗朗西斯科·内波穆塞诺一日对记者说，派遣菲律宾部队去南越的计划已在这个国家引起了强烈的反对，因为这一计划是“为了区区几千万美元而出卖我们的同胞和宰割菲律宾人的血肉”。
