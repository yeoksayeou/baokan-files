Title: Chinese Communist Youth League Delegation Arrives in Romania
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-24
Edition: 4

    Our Chinese Communist Youth League Delegation Arrives in Romania
    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, March 21st – The Chinese Communist Youth League delegation led by Wang Daoyi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, arrived in Bucharest on the 21st. The delegation is invited to attend the Fourth Congress of the Romanian Communist Youth League.
    Those who greeted the delegation at the airport included: Enache, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Communist Youth League, and Secretaries Nicolcioiu, Poparad, Stoica, and Popescu. Wang Dong, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Romania, also greeted the delegation at the airport.

### 我共青团代表团到罗马尼亚

1966-03-24
第4版()
专栏：

    我共青团代表团到罗马尼亚
    新华社布加勒斯特二十一日电由中国共产主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记王道义率领的中国共青团代表团二十一日抵达布加勒斯特。代表团是应邀参加罗马尼亚共产主义青年团第四次代表大会的。
    到机场迎接的有：罗马尼亚共青团中央第一书记埃纳克，书记尼科尔乔尤、波帕拉德、斯托伊卡和波佩斯库等人。中国驻罗马尼亚大使馆参赞王栋也到机场迎接。
