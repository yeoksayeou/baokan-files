Title: President Ayub Khan Says Pakistan Wishes to Have Peace with India; India Must Change its Policy of Resolving Disputes with Neighboring Countries by Force

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-21
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi, 19th -- According to a report by the Pakistan Press International, President Ayub Khan said on the 19th that India must change its policy of resolving its disputes with neighboring countries through the use of force or the display of force.
    President Ayub Khan made these remarks at the third meeting of the Pakistan Muslim League Council held in Dacca. He said that no matter what happens, no matter what the cost, Pakistan will not be intimidated.
    He pointed out that despite India's oppression and tyranny, the people of Kashmir are still demanding to exercise their right to determine their own future. Jammu and Kashmir is a disputed territory, and one day, this issue will definitely be resolved.
    The President recalled the recent war with India and said that India's aggression was aimed at Pakistan's survival. He further said that Pakistan will never forget the goodwill and friendship that some countries in the world showed it during the war. This is a unique expression of friendship in international relations.
    The President said that Pakistan's foreign policy aims to guarantee its survival. Some countries have requested a change in this policy. But we explained that this is part of our continued struggle for survival. He said that Pakistan hopes for peace with India. But this peace must be based on decent justice. This peace will be achieved in three stages: first, India changes its mind and becomes amicable with Pakistan; second, resolving the Jammu and Kashmir dispute; and finally, reducing forces.
    But President Ayub regretted that there was no indication that Indian leaders were prepared to resolve the Jammu and Kashmir dispute.

### 阿尤布·汗总统说巴基斯坦愿与印度和平  印度必须改变以武力解决与邻国争端的政策

1966-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尤布·汗总统说巴基斯坦愿与印度和平
    印度必须改变以武力解决与邻国争端的政策
    新华社拉瓦尔品第十九日电　据巴基斯坦联合通讯社报道，阿尤布·汗总统十九日说，印度必须改变它通过使用武力或显示武力来解决它与邻国的争端的这种政策。
    阿尤布·汗总统是在巴基斯坦穆斯林联盟理事会在达卡举行的第三次会议上讲这番话的。他说，不管发生什么情况，不管要付出什么代价，巴基斯坦是不会被吓倒的。
    他指出，尽管印度进行压迫和实行暴政，克什米尔人民仍然要求行使他们决定自己的未来的权利。查谟和克什米尔是一块有争议的领土，总有一天，这个问题是一定会解决的。
    总统回顾了最近与印度进行的战争，并且说，印度的侵略是针对巴基斯坦的生存的。他又说，巴基斯坦决不会忘记世界上的一些国家在战争期间对它表示的亲善和友谊。这是国际关系中的友谊的一个独特的表示。
    总统说，巴基斯坦的对外政策旨在保证它的生存。有些国家要求改变这个政策。但是我们解释说，这是我们为生存而继续进行的斗争的一部分。他说，巴基斯坦希望与印度和平。但是这种和平必须在体面的正义的基础之上。这种和平将分三个阶段实现，即，印度改变心意而与巴基斯坦和睦。第二，解决查谟和克什米尔争端。最后，减少部队。
    但是阿尤布总统感到遗憾的是，没有迹象表明印度领导人准备解决查谟和克什米尔争端。
