Title: Afghan Women's Delegation Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-03-20
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 19th -- The Afghan women's delegation of five people, led by Saliha Farouk Etemadi, President of the Afghan Women's Association, accompanied by Shen Ziji, member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation, and Xu Keli, executive member, left Beijing by plane this morning to visit other places before returning to their country.
    Those who went to the airport to see them off included Kang Keqing, Vice President of the All-China Women's Federation, and other responsible persons from relevant departments Luo Shuzhang, Hu Zi'ying, Zhang Jie, Shen Qiyi, Li Bozhao, Zeng Xianzhi, Yang Yunyu, Zhao Feng, Cai Lian and others.
    Mohammad Shuaib Miskainyah, the Afghan Ambassador to China, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 阿富汗妇女代表团离京

1966-03-20
第4版()
专栏：

    阿富汗妇女代表团离京
    新华社十九日讯  以阿富汗妇女会会长萨利哈·法鲁克·埃特马迪为首的阿富汗妇女代表团一行五人，由全国妇联主席团委员沈兹九、执行委员徐克立陪同，今天上午乘飞机离开北京去外地参观访问，然后回国。
    前往机场送行的有全国妇联副主席康克清，其他有关方面负责人罗叔章、胡子婴、张杰、沈其益、李伯钊、曾宪植、杨蕴玉、赵烽、蔡濂等。
    阿富汗驻华大使穆罕默德·舒埃卜·米斯凯尼亚也到机场送行。
