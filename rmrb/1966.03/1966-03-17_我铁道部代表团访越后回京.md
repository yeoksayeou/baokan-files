Title: Our Ministry of Railways Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-03-17
Edition: 4

    Our Ministry of Railways Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Vietnam
    Xinhua News Agency, 14th – The Chinese Ministry of Railways delegation, led by Vice Minister of Railways Guo Lu, returned to Beijing by train this morning after concluding a friendly visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.  Vice Minister of Railways Wu Jingtian and others were at the station to welcome them.
    Su An and Le Quang Tuong, commercial counselors of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, were also at the station to welcome them.

### 我铁道部代表团访越后回京

1966-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

    我铁道部代表团访越后回京
    新华社十四日讯　由铁道部副部长郭鲁率领的中国铁道部代表团结束了在越南民主共和国的友好访问后，今天上午乘火车回到北京。到车站迎接的有铁道部副部长武竞天等。
    越南驻中国大使馆商务参赞苏安、黎光相也到车站迎接。
