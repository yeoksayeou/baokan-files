Title: Guinean Newspapers Say Ideological Mobilization of the People is Needed Now More Than Ever; African People Must Arm Themselves and Resolutely Struggle Against Imperialism; President of the All-African Trade Union Federation Condemns Imperialist-Engineered Coup in Ghana
Author:
Date: 1966-03-14
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 13th Dispatch from Casablanca: The President of the All-African Trade Union Federation, Ben Seddik, issued a statement on the 12th, after the special meeting of the All-African Trade Union Federation held in Casablanca, Morocco, stating that the All-African Trade Union Federation strongly condemns the recent imperialist-engineered coup in Ghana and calls on all Ghanaian and African workers to mobilize against the new pro-imperialist puppet regime, providing all necessary support to the Ghanaian workers and people, as well as President Nkrumah.
    The statement pointed out that the African continent is currently at a moment characterized by a colonialist and imperialist counteroffensive, attempting to nullify the revolutionary gains obtained by the peoples of African countries in their heroic struggles for national liberation.
    The statement pointed out that the African people's policy of striving for African liberation is contradictory to the policies of imperialism and is "absolutely irreconcilable."
    In its statement, the All-African Trade Union Federation demanded the immediate release of all trade union activists arrested in Accra by the "National Liberation Council."

    Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, March 10th Dispatch: The Guinean newspaper *Liberté* published an editorial on the 10th, calling on the Guinean people to be "doubly vigilant and doubly resolute" and emphasizing the need to "arm the people" to fight against imperialism and its dark forces.
    The editorial stated: "At a time when illegal seizures of power and military dictatorships are emerging on the African continent, at a revolutionary moment, there is a greater need than ever before for armed mobilization and ideological mobilization of the people."
    The editorial said: "We are now fighting against imperialism and its dark forces," and the members of the Democratic Party of Guinea and the Guinean people "should raise its ideological level and receive appropriate military training."
    The editorial stated: "Guinea won its sovereignty because its sons and daughters who do not wear uniforms have paid diverse sacrifices, often unknown to people, but forever noble."
    The editorial called on the Guinean revolutionary youth, members of the Democratic Party of Guinea, and the Guinean people to "hold a hoe or compass in one hand and a gun in the other; be doubly vigilant, doubly resolute!" "Live for work, die for battle!"

    Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi, March 11th Dispatch: The President of the Pakistani African Students' Union, Okullo Epak, said in a letter to the editor of the *Pakistan Times* that the recent coup in Ghana was orchestrated by the enemies of African unity and the struggle for freedom, unity, and revival of Africa – the imperialists.
    The letter, published in today's *Pakistan Times*, stated: "But, it should be told to London and Washington that their planning of such vile schemes can only help strengthen our conviction and determination to fight for African unity and liberation. We shall continue to struggle relentlessly to wipe out every trace of Verwoerd, Smith, and other such imperialist stooges."
    In the letter, Epak praised the stance taken by Guinea, Tanzania, Mali, and other African countries regarding this rebellion.

### 几内亚报纸说现在比过去更需要对人民实行思想动员  非洲人民武装起来同帝国主义坚决斗争  全非工会联合会主席谴责帝国主义在加纳策动政变

1966-03-14
第4版()
专栏：

    几内亚报纸说现在比过去更需要对人民实行思想动员
    非洲人民武装起来同帝国主义坚决斗争
    全非工会联合会主席谴责帝国主义在加纳策动政变
    新华社十三日讯　卡萨布兰卡消息：全非工会联合会主席本·西迪克在全非工会联合会特别会议在摩洛哥的卡萨布兰卡举行以后，十二日发表声明说，全非工会联合会强烈谴责帝国主义最近在加纳策动的政变，并且呼吁全体加纳工人和非洲工人动员起来，反对新的亲帝国主义的傀儡政权，给予加纳工人和人民以及恩克鲁玛总统一切必要的支持。
    声明指出，目前非洲大陆处于这样一个时刻，其特点是殖民主义和帝国主义发动反攻，企图取消非洲各国人民在争取民族解放的英勇斗争中所获得的革命成果。
    声明指出，非洲人民的争取非洲解放的方针同帝国主义的方针是矛盾的，并且是“绝对不可调和的”。
    全非工会联合会在声明中要求立即释放“全国解放委员会”在阿克拉逮捕的所有工会积极分子。
    新华社科纳克里十日电几内亚《自由报》十日发表社论号召几内亚人民“加倍警惕和加倍坚决”，并强调“把人民武装起来”，同帝国主义及其黑暗势力作战。
    社论说：“在非洲大陆上出现非法夺取政权和军人专政的时候，在革命的时刻，就比过去任何时候都更需要对人民实行武装动员和思想动员。”
    社论说：“我们现在正同帝国主义及其黑暗势力作战”，几内亚民主党党员和几内亚人民“应当提高它的思想水平并接受适当的军事训练。”
    社论说：“几内亚是由于它的不穿军装的儿女们付出了多方面的、常常为人们所不知道的、但永远是高尚的牺牲才赢得主权的。”
    社论号召几内亚革命青年、几内亚民主党党员和几内亚人民“一手拿锄头或圆规，一手拿枪；加倍警惕，加倍坚决！”“为工作而生，为战斗而死！”
    新华社拉瓦尔品第十一日电　巴基斯坦非洲学生会主席奥库洛·埃帕克在给《巴基斯坦时报》编者的一封信中说：加纳最近的政变是非洲统一和非洲争取自由、统一和复兴的斗争的敌人——帝国主义者策划的。
    这封登载在今天的《巴基斯坦时报》上的信说：“但是，应该告诉伦敦和华盛顿，他们策划这种卑鄙的阴谋只能帮助加强我们为非洲统一和解放而斗争的信念和决心。我们将继续不懈地进行斗争，以扫除维沃尔德、史密斯和其他这一类的帝国主义走狗的一切痕迹。”
    埃帕克在信中赞扬了几内亚、坦桑尼亚、马里和其他非洲国家对这次叛乱采取的立场。
