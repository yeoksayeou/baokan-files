Title: Pathet Lao Radio Condemns Indonesian Reactionary Forces for Killing Progressive Figures; Lao People Resolutely Support the Indonesian People's Struggle
Author:
Date: 1966-03-02
Edition: 4

　　新华社康开电　巴特寮电台二月二十五日发表评论，强烈谴责印度尼西亚反动势力杀害和监禁印度尼西亚进步人士的罪行。
　　评论指出，印度尼西亚反动势力的所谓“审讯”同九月三十日运动有联系的人，实际上是一种欺骗宣传，企图以此欺骗国际舆论，掩盖它们迫害印度尼西亚共产党人和爱国人民的罪行和它们的不可告人的阴谋。
　　评论指出，在美帝国主义的唆使下，印度尼西亚反动势力最近几个月来的镇压和迫害行动，破坏了印度尼西亚人民的团结反美阵线，同时它还破坏了印度尼西亚同世界爱好和平国家的友好关系。
　　评论说，老挝人民坚决控诉印度尼西亚反动势力迫害印度尼西亚人民的残暴行动，要求它们必须立即停止这些镇压和迫害活动，释放目前被监禁的人。
　　评论最后表示，老挝人民坚决支持印度尼西亚人民为反对印度尼西亚反动派的镇压、迫害而进行的正义斗争。

　　Xinhua News Agency, Kang Kai Electric Dispatch. Pathet Lao Radio broadcast a commentary on February 25th, strongly condemning the crimes of the Indonesian reactionary forces in killing and imprisoning Indonesian progressive figures.
　　The commentary pointed out that the Indonesian reactionary forces' so-called "interrogation" of people connected with the September 30th Movement is actually a deceptive propaganda, attempting to deceive international public opinion, conceal their crimes of persecuting Indonesian communists and patriotic people, and their ulterior motives.
　　The commentary pointed out that, incited by U.S. imperialism, the Indonesian reactionary forces' suppression and persecution actions in recent months have undermined the united anti-U.S. front of the Indonesian people, and at the same time, it has damaged Indonesia's friendly relations with peace-loving countries in the world.
　　The commentary said that the Lao people resolutely condemn the Indonesian reactionary forces' brutal actions in persecuting the Indonesian people, demanding that they immediately stop these suppression and persecution activities and release the people currently imprisoned.
　　The commentary concluded by stating that the Lao people resolutely support the Indonesian people's just struggle against the suppression and persecution of the Indonesian reactionaries.
