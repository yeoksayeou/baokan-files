Title: Japanese Communist Party Delegation Concludes Visit to North Korea, Departs Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1966-03-22
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 21st - The Japanese Communist Party delegation, led by Kenji Miyamoto, General Secretary and Standing Committee Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee, departed Pyongyang by special plane on March 21st after concluding its visit to North Korea. The delegation was invited by the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party to visit North Korea.
    Those who saw the delegation off at the airport included Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party; Pak Kum-chol, Ri Hyo-sun, and Kim Kwang-hyop, Vice-Chairmen and Political Committee Members of the Central Committee; Ri Song-un, Central Committee Member and Chairman of the Pyongyang City Committee of the Workers' Party; and Pak Yong-kuk and Ko Hyok, Central Committee Members and Ministers of various departments of the Party Central Committee.
    Jiao Ruoyu, Chinese Ambassador to North Korea, and Phan Chanh Giang, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Embassy in North Korea, also saw the delegation off at the airport.
    Tens of thousands of Pyongyang residents also went to the airport to bid farewell to the delegation.
    On the evening of the 20th, Chairman Kim Il-sung hosted a banquet in honor of all members of the Japanese Communist Party delegation.
    Also present at the banquet were Choi Yong-kun, Pak Kum-chol, Ri Hyo-sun, and Kim Kwang-hyop, Vice-Chairmen and Political Committee Members of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, and others.
    The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere. (Photo attached)
    On March 21st, the Japanese Communist Party delegation headed by Comrade Kenji Miyamoto, General Secretary of the Japanese Communist Party, arrived in Beijing by special plane after concluding its visit to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. Comrade Peng Zhen warmly welcomed Comrade Kenji Miyamoto at the airport.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Changzhong (photograph)

### 日共代表团结束在朝鲜访问离开平壤

1966-03-22
第4版()
专栏：

    日共代表团结束在朝鲜访问离开平壤
    新华社平壤二十一日电　由中央委员会总书记、政治局常务委员宫本显治率领的日本共产党代表团在结束了在朝鲜的访问后二十一日乘专机离开平壤。代表团是应朝鲜劳动党中央委员会的邀请到朝鲜进行访问的。
    到机场欢送代表团的有朝鲜劳动党中央委员会委员长金日成，中央委员会副委员长、政治委员会委员朴金喆、李孝淳和金光侠，中央委员、劳动党平壤市委员会委员长李松云，中央委员、党中央各部部长朴容国和高赫等。
    中国驻朝鲜大使焦若愚和越南驻朝鲜大使馆临时代办潘正江也到机场送行。
    到机场欢送代表团的还有成千上万的平壤居民。
    二十日晚，金日成委员长设宴招待日本共产党代表团的全体成员。
    出席宴会的还有朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长、政治委员会委员崔庸健、朴金喆、李孝淳和金光侠等。
    晚宴是在友好的气氛中进行的。（附图片）
    三月二十一日，以日本共产党总书记宫本显治同志为首的日本共产党代表团结束在朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访问后乘专机到达北京。宫本显治同志在机场受到彭真同志的热烈欢迎。
    新华社记者　刘长忠摄
