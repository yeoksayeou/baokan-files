Title: Chairman Liu Receives Four Kashmiri Public Leaders; Kashmiri Public Leaders Warmly Welcome Chairman Liu's Visit to Pakistan, Praising China's Role in Supporting Oppressed Nations in Asia and Africa
Author:
Date: 1966-03-29
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi, March 27th – Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, received four Kashmiri public leaders here this afternoon. They were: Sardar Abdul Qayyum, Ali Muhammad Kanwar, Muhammad Aslam Khan, and Maqbool Shah.
    Chairman Liu Shaoqi had friendly talks with them.
    Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi, March 27th – Kashmiri public leaders warmly welcomed Chairman Liu Shaoqi's visit to Pakistan.
    President of the Azad Jammu and Kashmir government, Abdul Hamid Khan, broadcast a congratulatory telegram on the evening of the 26th through the "Free Kashmir" radio station, extending warm greetings to the Chinese leaders. He said that the great revolutionary leaders of China wholeheartedly and sincerely support the Kashmiri people's struggle for freedom.
    He also said that the greatness of the Chinese leaders can be seen from the great revolution and the profound impact this revolution has had worldwide.
    He pointed out that the Chinese revolution has shaken the foundations of Western imperialism.
    Muhammad Yusuf Shah, spokesman for the Kashmir Revolutionary Committee, said that we Kashmiris believe that Sino-Pakistani friendship is not only an important factor in ensuring lasting peace in Asia, but will also prove to be an important factor in the Kashmiri people's struggle to free themselves from Indian rule.
    In his congratulatory telegram broadcast on the evening of the 26th through the "Free Kashmir" radio station, he expressed his heartfelt welcome to the Chinese leaders' visit to Pakistan. He said that the Chinese revolutionaries, like the Kashmiri people, are engaged in the struggle against fascism and neo-colonialism. This is a very satisfying and encouraging thing.
    Leaders of the Kashmir Liberation Movement, Khadim Hussain and Sheikh Khalid Mahmood, said in their congratulatory telegram that the People's Republic of China has played a glorious role in supporting the oppressed nations of Asia and Africa. We, the Kashmiri people, pay tribute to the great and peace-loving Chinese people. (Attached picture)

### 刘主席接见四位克什米尔公众领袖  克什米尔公众领袖热烈欢迎刘主席访问巴基斯坦，赞扬中国支持亚非被压迫民族的作用

1966-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席接见四位克什米尔公众领袖
    克什米尔公众领袖热烈欢迎刘主席访问巴基斯坦，赞扬中国支持亚非被压迫民族的作用
    新华社拉瓦尔品第二十七日电  中华人民共和国主席刘少奇二十七日下午在这里接见了四位克什米尔公众领袖。他们是：萨达尔·阿卜杜勒·加尧姆、阿里·穆罕默德·坎瓦尔、穆罕默德·阿斯拉姆汗和马克布尔·沙阿。
    刘少奇主席同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    新华社拉瓦尔品第二十七日电  克什米尔公众领袖热烈欢迎刘少奇主席访问巴基斯坦。
    查谟和克什米尔自由政府主席阿卜杜勒·哈米德汗二十六日晚上通过“自由克什米尔”电台广播了一封贺电，向中国领导人热烈致意。他说，中国的伟大革命领导人无保留地和真诚地支持克什米尔人民争取自由的斗争。
    他还说，中国领导人的伟大可从伟大的革命和这种革命在全世界产生的深远影响中看出来。
    他指出，中国的革命动摇了西方帝国主义的基础。
    克什米尔革命委员会发言人穆罕默德·优素福·沙阿说，我们克什米尔人认为，中巴友谊不仅是确保亚洲的持久和平的一个重要因素，而且还将证明是克什米尔人民在争取摆脱印度的统治的斗争中的一个重要因素。
    他在二十六日晚上通过“自由克什米尔”电台发表的贺电中表示衷心欢迎中国领导人访问巴基斯坦。他说，中国的革命者和克什米尔人民一样，正在从事反对法西斯主义和新殖民主义的斗争。这是一个非常令人满意和鼓舞的事情。
    克什米尔解放运动领导人哈迪姆·侯赛因和谢赫·哈利德·马哈茂德在贺电中说，中华人民共和国在支持亚洲、非洲被压迫民族方面起了光荣的作用。我们克什米尔人民向伟大和爱好和平的中国人民致敬。（附图片）
