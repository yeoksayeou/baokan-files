Title: Australian "Vanguard" Condemns Australian Government for Increasing Troops as Accomplices to American Imperialist Aggression in Vietnam, Calls on People to Further Support the Vietnamese People's Struggle
Author:
Date: 1966-03-15
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Melbourne, 10th. The Australian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist) organ "Vanguard" on the 10th condemned the Australian government's decision to send more troops to South Vietnam to help the United States carry out its war of aggression. It said that Prime Minister Holt had drawn Australia deeper into the American imperialist war of aggression.
    The newspaper exposed the government's lies to create a pretext for sending more troops. It pointed out that the government's actions "actually demonstrate the widespread opposition of the Australian people to further involvement in the dirty Vietnam War. The feelings of the Australian people have now been clearly expressed through demonstrations and many other means, they openly oppose this war and are moving towards openly opposing American imperialist rule over Australia."
    "Vanguard" called on all Australians to oppose all deception, to raise their voices more, to demand that all American and Australian troops withdraw from South Vietnam, and to fully support the conditions for resolving the war put forward by the South Vietnam National Liberation Front.

### 澳《先锋报》谴责澳政府增兵充当美帝侵越帮凶  号召人民进一步支持越南人民斗争

1966-03-15
第3版()
专栏：

    澳《先锋报》谴责澳政府增兵充当美帝侵越帮凶
    号召人民进一步支持越南人民斗争
    新华社墨尔本十日电　澳大利亚共产党（马克思列宁主义者）机关报《先锋报》十日谴责澳大利亚政府决定增派军队到南越去帮助美国进行侵略战争。它说，霍尔特总理已经使澳大利亚更深地卷入美帝国主义的侵略战争。
    这家报纸揭露政府撒谎以便为增派军队制造口实。它指出，政府这样做，“实际上正好表明澳大利亚人民普遍反对进一步卷入肮脏的越南战争。澳大利亚人的感情现在已经用示威及其他许多方式清楚地表现出来，他们公开反对这场战争，而且正在进而公开反对美帝国主义统治澳大利亚。”
    《先锋报》要求全体澳大利亚人反对一切欺骗，更多地发出他们的呼声，要求美国和澳大利亚的军队全部撤出南越，并完全支持越南南方民族解放阵线提出的解决战争的条件。
