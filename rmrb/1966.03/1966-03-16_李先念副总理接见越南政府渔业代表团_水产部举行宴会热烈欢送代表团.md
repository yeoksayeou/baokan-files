Title: Vice Premier Li Xiannian Receives Vietnamese Government Fisheries Delegation; Ministry of Aquatic Products Holds Banquet to Warmly Welcome the Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-03-16
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 15th – Vice Premier Li Xiannian received the Fisheries Delegation of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, headed by Nguyen Trong Tinh, Director-General of the General Department of Aquatic Products of Vietnam, this afternoon and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were responsible persons from relevant departments including Gao Wenhua, Li Qiang, Shi Min, Dong Xuelin, and Du Ganquan.
    Hoang Bac, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, was also present.
    Xinhua News Agency, March 15th – The Ministry of Aquatic Products held a banquet tonight to warmly welcome the Fisheries Delegation of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, headed by Nguyen Trong Tinh, Director-General of the General Department of Aquatic Products of Vietnam.
    Vice Premier Li Xiannian attended the banquet.
    At the banquet, Vice Minister of Aquatic Products Gao Wenhua and Delegation Head Nguyen Trong Tinh spoke successively. They wished the solidarity and friendship between the people of China and Vietnam in the struggle against U.S. imperialism would be further consolidated and developed.
    Responsible persons from relevant departments including Li Qiang, Shi Min, Dong Xuelin, and Du Ganquan attended the banquet. Hoang Bac, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, was invited to attend the banquet. (Photo attached)
    March 15th, Vice Premier Li Xiannian receives the Fisheries Delegation of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, headed by Nguyen Trong Tinh (front left fourth), Director-General of the General Department of Aquatic Products of Vietnam.

### 李先念副总理接见越南政府渔业代表团  水产部举行宴会热烈欢送代表团

1966-03-16
第4版()
专栏：

    李先念副总理接见越南政府渔业代表团
    水产部举行宴会热烈欢送代表团
    新华社十五日讯　李先念副总理今天下午接见以越南水产总局总局长阮仲省为首的越南民主共和国政府渔业代表团，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有高文华、李强、史敏、董学林、杜干全等有关方面负责人。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办黄北也在座。
    新华社十五日讯　水产部今晚举行宴会，热烈欢送以越南水产总局总局长阮仲省为首的越南民主共和国政府渔业代表团。
    李先念副总理出席宴会。
    宴会上，水产部副部长高文华和阮仲省团长先后讲话。他们祝愿中越两国人民在反对美帝国主义斗争中的团结和友谊进一步巩固和发展。
    出席宴会的有李强、史敏、董学林、杜干全等有关方面负责人。越南驻中国大使馆临时代办黄北应邀出席了宴会。（附图片）
    三月十五日，李先念副总理接见以越南民主共和国水产总局总局长阮仲省（前左四）为首的越南民主共和国政府渔业代表团。
  　新华社记者　刘长忠摄
