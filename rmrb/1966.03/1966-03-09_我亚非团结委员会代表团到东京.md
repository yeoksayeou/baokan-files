Title: Delegation of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity Arrives in Tokyo
Author:
Date: 1966-03-09
Edition: 5

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, March 8th, the delegation of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, led by Yang Shuo, arrived in Tokyo by plane on the evening of the 7th.
    Japanese leaders of the inviting organization, including Sakamoto Tokumatsu, Chairman of the Japan Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee, Kaneko Mitsuhiro, Deputy Director of the United Front Work Department of the Japanese Communist Party Central Committee, Honjo Kakuo, Chairman of the Joint Struggle Conference of the Japan National Public Service Employees' Union, Miyoshi Hajime, Secretary-General of the Japan-China Friendship Association, and well-known commentator Matsuoka Yoko, warmly welcomed the delegation of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity at the airport. Sakamoto Tokumatsu said in his welcoming speech: The visit of the delegation of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity to Japan will further strengthen the friendship between the people of Japan and China. Yang Shuo, the head of the delegation and Vice-Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, said in his speech that the Chinese delegation's visit to Japan is to strengthen the friendship and unity between the people of China and Japan, and to further strengthen the unity of the Afro-Asian people.

### 我亚非团结委员会代表团到东京

1966-03-09
第5版()
专栏：

    我亚非团结委员会代表团到东京
    据新华社东京八日电  由杨朔率领的中国亚非团结委员会代表团七日晚乘飞机到达东京。
    日本邀请团体负责人、日本亚非团结委员会理事长坂本德松和日共中央统战部副部长金子满广、日本国家公务员工会共同斗争会议主席本条嘉久雄、日中友好协会事务局长三好一、著名评论家松冈洋子等到机场热烈欢迎中国亚非团结委员会代表团。坂本德松在致欢迎词时说：中国亚非团结委员会代表团访问日本，将进一步加强日中两国人民的友好。代表团团长、中国亚非团结委员会副主席杨朔在讲话中说，中国代表团到日本访问，是为了加强中日两国人民的友好和团结，并为了进一步加强亚非人民的团结。
