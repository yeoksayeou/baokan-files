Title: Korean Rainbow Trout Settles in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-03-24
Edition: 2

	Good News for Diversified Economy

	Korean Rainbow Trout Settles in Beijing
	The two hundred and twenty-four rainbow trout that Kang Hi-won, Chairman of the Pyongyang City People's Committee of Korea, gave to Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen a year ago have now propagated more than 20,000 fry in Beijing.
	Rainbow trout is a precious cold-water fish. Its abdomen has a red line, and its back is covered with green-based black-spotted scales. The fish meat contains more protein and fat, and it tastes delicious. The yield is relatively high.
					(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 朝鲜虹鳟鱼落户北京

1966-03-24
第2版()
专栏：多种经营喜讯

    朝鲜虹鳟鱼落户北京
    朝鲜平壤市人民委员会委员长姜希源在一年前送给北京市彭真市长的二百二十四条虹鳟鱼，现在已在北京繁殖了两万多条鱼苗。
    虹鳟鱼是一种名贵的冷水性鱼类，它的腹部有一条红线，脊背上布满绿底黑斑的鱼鳞。鱼肉含蛋白质和脂肪较多，味道鲜美。产量较高。
                    （据新华社讯）
