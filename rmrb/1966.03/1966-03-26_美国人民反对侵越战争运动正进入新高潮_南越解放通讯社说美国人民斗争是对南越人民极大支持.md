Title: The American People's Movement Against the War of Aggression Against Vietnam is Entering a New Climax; South Vietnam Liberation News Agency Says the American People's Struggle is a Great Support for the South Vietnamese People

Author:
Date: 1966-03-26
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, March 25th: The American people's movement against the war of aggression against Vietnam is entering a new climax. On March 25th and 26th, the American people will once again hold a national-scale protest movement – "International Protest Day" – to launch a tit-for-tat struggle against Johnson's policy of constantly expanding the war of aggression against Vietnam.

    According to a report in the March 19th issue of the *National Guardian* weekly, on March 25th and 26th, tens of thousands of men, women, and children in dozens of cities across the United States, from New York in the east to Berkeley in the west, from Chicago in the north to Tugaloo in the south, will take action under slogans such as "End the Vietnam War Immediately!" and "Bring American Troops Home Immediately!" They will hold protest demonstrations, protest rallies, torchlight parades, all-night vigils, lectures, seminars, anti-war poetry readings, anti-war song concerts, etc., to express their protest against the war of aggression against Vietnam. According to the "National Coordinating Committee to End the War in Vietnam," the organizers of "International Protest Day," this struggle is expected to receive a response and support from the public in dozens of countries.

    According to a dispatch from Xinhua News Agency in Hanoi on March 25th, the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency issued a commentary on March 23rd on the "International Protest Day" held by the American people on March 25th and 26th against the American aggression against Vietnam, pointing out that the American people's struggle against the Johnson clique's policies of aggression and war is a great support for the people of South Vietnam.

    The commentary pointed out that the Johnson clique talks incessantly about "peace." But they are sending American and puppet troops to the Vietnam battlefield to expand the war of aggression, suppress the domestic movement for peace and against the war, plot counter-revolutionary coups around the world, create international tensions, and trample on the peace aspirations of the people of all countries.

    The commentary said that the struggle of the American people is a great support for the people of South Vietnam and is a close coordination with the struggle of the people of South Vietnam.

    The commentary stated that the Vietnamese people will unite with the American people, resolutely fight, and defeat American imperialism. (Attached picture)

### 美国人民反对侵越战争运动正进入新高潮  南越解放通讯社说美国人民斗争是对南越人民极大支持

1966-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

    美国人民反对侵越战争运动正进入新高潮
    南越解放通讯社说美国人民斗争是对南越人民极大支持
    据新华社二十五日讯　美国人民反对侵越战争的运动，正在进入一个新的高潮。三月二十五日和二十六日，美国人民将再一次举行全国规模的抗议运动——“国际抗议日”，同约翰逊不断扩大侵越战争的政策，展开针锋相对的斗争。
    据十九日的《国民前卫》周刊报道，在三月二十五日和二十六日，从东部的纽约到西部的伯克利、从北部的芝加哥到南部的图加洛，美国数十个城市数以万计的男女老少将在“立即结束越南战争！”“立即把美国兵撤回家来！”等口号下行动起来。他们将举行抗议示威、抗议集会、火炬游行、通宵守夜、讲演会、座谈会、反战诗歌朗诵会、反战歌曲演唱会等，以表示对侵越战争的抗议。据“国际抗议日”的组织者“结束越南战争全国协调委员会”宣布，这个斗争预计还将得到数十个国家的公众的响应和支持。
    据新华社河内二十五日电越南南方解放通讯社二十三日就美国人民三月二十五日和二十六日举行反对美国侵略越南的“国际抗议日”发表评论指出，美国人民反对约翰逊集团的侵略和战争政策的斗争，是对越南南方人民的极大支持。
    评论指出，约翰逊集团喋喋不休地谈到“和平”。但是他们却在把美国及其仆从的军队派到越南战场去扩大侵略战争，镇压美国国内要求和平、反对战争的运动，在世界各地策划反革命政变，制造国际紧张局势，蹂躏世界各国人民的和平愿望。
    评论说，美国人民的斗争是对越南南方人民的极大支持，是同越南南方人民斗争的紧密配合。
    评论表示，越南人民将同美国人民团结起来，坚决进行斗争，打败美帝国主义。（附图片）
