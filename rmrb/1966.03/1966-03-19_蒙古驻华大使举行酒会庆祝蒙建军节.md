Title: Mongolian Ambassador to China Holds Reception to Celebrate Mongolian Army Day
Author:
Date: 1966-03-19
Edition: 4

    The Mongolian Ambassador to China Holds a Reception to Celebrate Mongolian Army Day
    Xinhua News Agency, March 18th. The Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, Dun. Tsebgeemid, held a reception this evening to celebrate the 45th anniversary of the founding of the Mongolian People's Army.
    Xu Guangda, Vice Minister of National Defense, and responsible persons from relevant departments, including Zhang Zongxun, Tang Tianji, Du Yide, Chang Qiankun, Jia Ruoyu, and Liu Xianglun, were invited to attend the reception.
    Dun. Tsebgeemid and Xu Guangda toasted each other at the reception.
    Diplomatic envoys and military attachés from various countries to China also attended the reception.

### 蒙古驻华大使举行酒会庆祝蒙建军节

1966-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古驻华大使举行酒会庆祝蒙建军节
    新华社十八日讯　蒙古人民共和国驻中国大使敦·策伯格米德今晚举行酒会，庆祝蒙古人民军建军四十五周年。
    国防部副部长许光达，以及有关方面负责人张宗逊、唐天际、杜义德、常乾坤、贾若瑜、刘祥纶等应邀出席了酒会。
    敦·策伯格米德和许光达在酒会上先后祝了酒。
    各国驻华使节和武官也出席了酒会。
