Title: Argentine Guests Leave Beijing to Visit Southern China

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-20
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 19th.  Emilio Jáuregui, Secretary-General of the Argentine Federation of Press Workers, and Eduardo Jozami, Assistant Secretary, who were invited by the All-China Journalists Association to visit China, left Beijing by plane today to continue their visit to southern China before returning home.
    Before their departure, Ren Yinglun, Vice President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, hosted a banquet to bid them farewell.

### 阿根廷客人离京去我国南方访问

1966-03-20
第4版()
专栏：

    阿根廷客人离京去我国南方访问
    新华社十九日讯  应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来我国访问的阿根廷报刊工作者联合会总书记埃米略·豪雷吉和助理书记爱德华多·何萨米，今天乘飞机离开北京去我国南方继续访问，然后回国。
    行前，中拉友协副会长任映仑曾设宴为他们送行。
