Title: Vice Minister of Public Security Hosts Albanian Guests

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-29
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 26th: Yang Qiqing, Vice Minister of Public Security and Director of the Chinese "Pioneer" Sports Association, hosted a banquet tonight to warmly welcome the entire Albanian Ministry of Internal Affairs Sports Delegation led by Niko Çeta.
    Meleqi Babani, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Albanian Embassy in China, was invited to attend the banquet.
    Also present at the banquet were Ling Yun, Vice Minister of Public Security; Ma Xingwu, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Public Security Forces and Deputy Director of the Chinese "Pioneer" Sports Association; Mu Zuoyun, Deputy Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, and others.
    The Albanian Ministry of Internal Affairs Sports Delegation, consisting of four members, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Chinese "Pioneer" Sports Association. Ma Xingwu and others welcomed them at the airport.

### 我公安部副部长宴请阿尔巴尼亚客人

1966-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

    我公安部副部长宴请阿尔巴尼亚客人
    新华社二十六日讯 公安部副部长、中国“前卫”体育协会主任杨奇清今晚举行宴会，热烈欢迎由尼科·采塔率领的阿尔巴尼亚内务部体育代表团全体人员。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻我国大使馆临时代办梅莱契·巴巴尼应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的还有公安部副部长凌云，公安部队政治部副主任、中国“前卫”体育协会副主任马星五，国家体委副司长牟作云等。
    应中国“前卫”体育协会邀请来我国访问的阿尔巴尼亚内务部体育代表团一行四人，是今天下午乘飞机到达北京的。马星五等到机场欢迎他们。
