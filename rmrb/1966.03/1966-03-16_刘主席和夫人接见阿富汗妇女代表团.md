Title: Chairman Liu and his Wife Receive the Afghan Women's Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-16
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 15th: Chairman Liu Shaoqi and his wife Wang Guangmei received this afternoon Ms. Saliha Farouk Etemadi, President of the Afghan Women's Association, and all members of the Afghan Women's Delegation led by her, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Shen Zijiu and Xu Keli. (Attached photo)
    Right picture: Chairman Liu Shaoqi and his wife Wang Guangmei receive all members of the Afghan Women's Delegation, headed by Saliha Farouk Etemadi (front left three), President of the Afghan Women's Association.
      Xinhua News Agency reporter

### 刘主席和夫人接见阿富汗妇女代表团

1966-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席和夫人接见阿富汗妇女代表团
    新华社十五日讯刘少奇主席和夫人王光美今天下午接见阿富汗妇女会会长萨利哈·法鲁克·埃特马迪，以及由她率领的阿富汗妇女代表团全体成员，同她们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有沈兹九、徐克立。（附图片）
    右图：刘少奇主席和夫人王光美接见以阿富汗妇女会会长萨利哈·法鲁克·埃特马迪（前左三）为首的阿富汗妇女代表团全体成员。
      新华社记者
            张　彬摄
