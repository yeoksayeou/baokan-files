Title: US Military Occupation of Japanese Airspace Endangers Civil Aviation; Three Civilian Airliners Recently Crash in Japan, Killing Over 300 People
Author:
Date: 1966-03-09
Edition: 5

    According to a Xinhua News Agency dispatch from Tokyo, March 8th, the Japanese Communist Party Central Committee’s newspaper, *Akahata*, published an article on March 7th revealing that the fundamental reason for the recent series of airliner crashes in Japan involving Japanese and foreign airlines is that Japanese people cannot freely use Japan's airspace. The United States, utilizing the U.S.-Japan "Security Treaty," occupies many excellent airports in Japan and establishes "U.S. military-only airspace" in Japanese skies, creating "extremely congested" civilian air routes, and therefore, accidents frequently occur.

    According to reports, on February 4th, a Boeing 727 of All Nippon Airways on a domestic route crashed into Tokyo Bay, killing all 133 pilots and passengers on board; on March 4th, a Canadian Pacific Airlines Douglas DC8 jet collided with ground facilities and caught fire while landing at Tokyo's Haneda Airport, killing 64 people; and on March 5th, a British Overseas Airways Corporation Boeing 707 jet crashed near Mount Fuji, killing all 124 people on board.

    The article states: Recently, in order to expand its war of aggression against Vietnam, the United States has chartered a large number of transport aircraft from American civilian airlines to transport weapons, ammunition, and soldiers to South Vietnam through Japan, resulting in a significant increase in the number of flights taking off and landing at Haneda Airport every day. According to statistics, in 1955, the average number of takeoffs and landings was 82 flights per day; by August of last year, this had increased to 300 to 330 flights per day, equivalent to four times the previous number, resulting in "severe congestion" of civil aviation routes and civilian airports.

### 美军霸占日本空域危害民航交通  三架民用客机最近在日本失事使三百多人死亡

1966-03-09
第5版()
专栏：

    美军霸占日本空域危害民航交通
    三架民用客机最近在日本失事使三百多人死亡
    据新华社东京八日电  日共中央机关报《赤旗报》七日发表文章揭露最近在日本连续发生日本和外国航空公司的客机失事的根本原因是日本人不能自由使用日本的领空。美国利用日美“安全条约”，占用日本许多优良的机场，并且在日本天空设置“美军专用空域”，造成民用航线“拥挤不堪”，因此，经常发生事故。
    据报道，二月四日，全日本航空公司国内航线的波音727型客机，坠落于东京湾，飞机上的驾驶员、旅客等共一百三十三人全部死亡；三月四日，加拿大太平洋航空公司的道格拉斯DC8型喷气式客机在东京羽田机场降落时与地面设施相撞起火，死亡六十四人；三月五日，英国海外航空公司波音707型喷气式客机在富士山附近坠毁，机上一百二十四人全部死亡。
    文章说：最近美国为了扩大侵略越南战争，大量包租美国民航公司的运输机，通过日本向南越运送武器、弹药和兵员，致使每天从羽田机场起飞和降落的飞机班次大大增加。据统计，一九五五年平均每天起飞和降落的班机八十二架次，到去年八月，每天增加到三百到三百三十架次，相当于过去的四倍，造成了民航机线路和民用机场“严重拥挤”。
