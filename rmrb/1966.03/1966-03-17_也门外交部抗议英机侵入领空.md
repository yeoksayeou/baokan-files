Title: Yemen Ministry of Foreign Affairs Protests British Aircraft Intrusion into Airspace
Author:
Date: 1966-03-17
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Sana'a, March 14th (Electric Dispatch) The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Arab Republic of Yemen issued a statement on the 13th protesting the intrusion of British aircraft into Yemeni airspace.

    The statement pointed out that on March 9th, British aircraft intruded into Yemeni territory over the Qatabah area for up to one hour. This is one of a series of events in which Britain has been aggressing against the Arab Republic of Yemen.

    The statement said that the Yemeni government strongly protested this act of aggression and reserved the right to defend its territorial integrity. The statement pointed out that the British government must bear full responsibility for the consequences that its aggression may cause.

### 也门外交部抗议英机侵入领空

1966-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

    也门外交部抗议英机侵入领空
    新华社萨那十四日电　阿拉伯也门共和国外交部十三日发表一项声明，抗议英国飞机侵入也门领空。
    声明指出，三月九日，英国飞机在卡塔巴地区上空侵入也门境内达一小时，这是英国侵略阿拉伯也门共和国的一系列事件之一。
    声明说，也门政府强烈抗议这种侵略行动，并保留保卫领土完整的权利。声明指出，英国政府必须对它的侵略可能造成的后果承担全部责任。
