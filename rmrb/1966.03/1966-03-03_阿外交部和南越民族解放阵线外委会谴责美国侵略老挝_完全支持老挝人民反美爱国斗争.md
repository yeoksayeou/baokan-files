Title: Albanian Foreign Ministry and South Vietnam National Liberation Front Foreign Affairs Committee Condemn US Aggression Against Laos, Fully Support Lao People's Anti-American Patriotic Struggle
Author:
Date: 1966-03-03
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 1st, the Albanian Foreign Ministry issued a statement on the 1st, firmly condemning the United States' acts of aggression against Laos and fully supporting the Lao people's resolute struggle against US imperialism and its puppets in Laos.

    The statement said that US imperialism blatantly continues to interfere in Laos' internal affairs, transporting weapons, aircraft, and military personnel into Laos, and instigating and supporting Lao right-wing forces to trample on the 1962 Geneva Agreement on the peaceful settlement of the Laos issue and the tripartite agreement on the establishment of a national unity government. American planes taking off from US military bases in Thailand and South Vietnam bombed agricultural areas and villages, massacring the people there. Recently, American planes carried out several bandit-like attacks on Phongsaly, the capital of Upper Laos, causing serious damage to the buildings and property of the Chinese Consulate General. Under the instigation and support of the United States, Kong Le's troops launched a series of attacks on the patriotic forces in the Phou Kout liberated area.

    The statement pointed out that the Albanian people fully support and stand in solidarity with the Lao people's resolute struggle against US imperialism and its puppets in Laos to safeguard independence, sovereignty, national unity, neutrality, and peace. The Albanian Foreign Ministry firmly condemns the United States' acts of aggression in Laos, unreservedly supports the legitimate demands of the Lao people, the Lao Patriotic Front, and the Lao Patriotic Neutralist Forces, and demands that the United States cease all forms of interference and aggression against Laos.

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 2nd, according to a report from the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency: The Foreign Liaison Committee of the Central Committee of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front issued a statement on February 22, strongly protesting the continuous large-scale offensives by US imperialism and the Lao right-wing army against the Lao liberated areas.

    The statement pointed out that the various "sweeps" launched by US imperialism and the Lao right-wing army against the Lao liberated areas since the beginning of February are part of the sinister conspiracy of the US warmongering clique to clamor for opening a second front in Indochina, attempting to occupy Central and Lower Laos, and connect Thailand, an important US logistics base in Southeast Asia, with South Vietnam.

    The statement expressed full support for the Lao Patriotic Front's just stand in fighting for a truly peaceful, independent, and neutral Laos.

### 阿外交部和南越民族解放阵线外委会谴责美国侵略老挝  完全支持老挝人民反美爱国斗争

1966-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

    阿外交部和南越民族解放阵线外委会谴责美国侵略老挝
    完全支持老挝人民反美爱国斗争
    据新华社地拉那一日电  阿尔巴尼亚外交部一日发表声明，坚决谴责美国对老挝的侵略行动，完全支持老挝人民对美帝国主义及其在老挝的傀儡进行的坚决斗争。
    声明说，美帝国主义公然继续干涉老挝内政，把武器、飞机和军事人员运进老挝，怂恿和支持老挝右派势力践踏一九六二年关于和平解决老挝问题的日内瓦协议以及关于建立民族团结政府的三方面协议。从泰国和南越的美国军事基地起飞的美国飞机轰炸农业地区和村庄，屠杀那里的人民。最近，美国飞机对上寮首府丰沙里进行了多次强盗式的袭击，使中华人民共和国总领事馆的建筑物和财产遭到严重损失。贡勒部队在美国的唆使和支持下，对富科特解放区的爱国力量进行了一系列的进攻。
    声明指出，阿尔巴尼亚人民完全支持和声援老挝人民为维护独立、主权、民族团结、中立与和平而对美帝国主义及其在老挝的傀儡进行的坚决斗争。阿尔巴尼亚外交部坚决谴责美国在老挝的侵略行动，无保留地支持老挝人民、老挝爱国战线党和老挝爱国中立力量的正当要求，并且要求美国停止对老挝进行任何形式的干涉和侵略。
    据新华社河内二日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会对外联络委员会二月二十二日发表声明，强烈抗议美帝国主义和老挝右派军队连续大规模进攻老挝解放区。
    声明指出，美帝国主义和老挝右派军队从二月初以来对老挝解放区发动的各次“扫荡”，是美国好战集团叫嚣的所谓在印度支那开辟第二条战线的不可告人的阴谋的一部分，妄图占领中寮和下寮，使美国在东南亚的重要后勤基地泰国同越南南方连接起来。
    声明表示完全支持老挝爱国战线党为建立一个真正和平、独立和中立的老挝而斗争的正当立场。
