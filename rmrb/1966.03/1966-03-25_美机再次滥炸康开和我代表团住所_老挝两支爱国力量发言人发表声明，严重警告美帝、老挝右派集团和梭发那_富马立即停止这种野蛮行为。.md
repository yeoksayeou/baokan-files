Title: U.S. Planes Again Indiscriminately Bomb Kang Khai and the Residence of Our Delegation; Spokesmen for the Two Lao Patriotic Forces Issue Statements, Severely Warning U.S. Imperialism, the Lao Right-Wing Group, and Souvanna Phouma to Immediately Cease This Barbaric Behavior.
Author:
Date: 1966-03-25
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Kang Khai, March 24th – American air pirates once again frenziedly bombed the city of Kang Khai, the former seat of the Royal Lao Government, and the residence of the Chinese Economic and Cultural Delegation to Laos today. The residence of the Chinese delegation caught fire in the bombing.
    At 10:15 AM on the 24th, the American aggressors brazenly dispatched four jet planes that intruded into the airspace above Kang Khai city and indiscriminately bombed the residence of the Chinese delegation and the homes of Kang Khai citizens.
    This is the third large-scale crime committed by U.S. imperialism in bombing Kang Khai city and the Chinese Economic and Cultural Delegation. In June 1964 and November 1965, U.S. imperialism dispatched planes to bomb Kang Khai city and the Chinese Economic and Cultural Delegation on two separate occasions.
    U.S. imperialism's barbaric behavior of continuing to bomb Kang Khai city and the Chinese Economic and Cultural Delegation, disregarding the repeated strong protests of the Lao people, the Chinese people, and the people of other countries, has aroused great anger among people from all walks of life here.
    Spokesmen for the two Lao patriotic forces issued a statement on the 24th, strongly protesting the barbaric behavior of the American air pirates.
    The statement severely warned U.S. imperialism, the Lao right-wing group, and Souvanna Phouma to immediately cease this barbaric behavior. The statement said that if they are bent on having their own way, they will be responsible for all consequences arising therefrom.

### 美机再次滥炸康开和我代表团住所  老挝两支爱国力量发言人发表声明，严重警告美帝、老挝右派集团和梭发那·富马立即停止这种野蛮行为。

1966-03-25
第1版()
专栏：

    美机再次滥炸康开和我代表团住所
    老挝两支爱国力量发言人发表声明，严重警告美帝、老挝右派集团和梭发那·富马立即停止这种野蛮行为。
    新华社康开二十四日电　美国空中强盗二十四日又一次疯狂轰炸老挝前王国政府所在地康开市和中国驻老挝经济文化代表团。中国代表团住所被炸中起火。
    二十四日上午十时十五分，美国侵略者公然出动四架喷气式飞机侵入康开市上空，并向中国代表团住所和康开市民住宅狂轰滥炸。
    这是美帝国主义第三次犯下的大规模轰炸康开市和中国经济文化代表团的罪行。在一九六四年六月间和一九六五年十一月间，美帝国主义曾先后两次出动飞机轰炸康开市和中国经济文化代表团。
    美帝国主义这种不顾老挝人民、中国人民和其他各国人民一再强烈抗议而继续轰炸康开市和中国经济文化代表团的野蛮行为，激起了此间各界人民的极大愤怒。
    老挝两支爱国力量发言人二十四日发表声明，强烈抗议美国空中强盗的野蛮行为。
    声明严重警告美帝国主义、老挝右派集团和梭发那·富马立即停止这种野蛮行为。声明说，如果他们一意孤行，由此而产生的一切后果将由他们负责。
