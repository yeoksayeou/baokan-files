Title: Vice Premier and Defense Minister Lin Biao Sends Congratulations on the 10th Anniversary of the Founding of the National People's Army of the German Democratic Republic
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-01
Edition: 4

    Vice Premier and Defense Minister Lin Biao
    Sends Congratulations on the 10th Anniversary of the Founding of the National People's Army of the German Democratic Republic
    Xinhua News Agency, February 28th—Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense Lin Biao sent a telegram today to General Heinz Hoffmann, Minister of National Defense of the German Democratic Republic, congratulating the German Democratic Republic's National People's Army on its tenth anniversary. The telegram reads as follows: To Comrade General Heinz Hoffmann, Minister of National Defense of the German Democratic Republic,
    Respected Comrade Minister:
    On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the National People's Army of the German Democratic Republic, I, on behalf of all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, extend warm festive congratulations to all officers and soldiers of the National People's Army of the German Democratic Republic.
    The Chinese people and the Chinese People's Liberation Army have consistently and resolutely supported the just struggle of the people and the National People's Army of the German Democratic Republic against U.S. imperialism and West German militarism, for the conclusion of a peace treaty with Germany, for safeguarding national sovereignty, and for the reunification of Germany on the basis of peace and democracy. We sincerely wish you continuous new achievements in this just struggle and in strengthening your national defense capabilities. May the fraternal friendship between the peoples and armies of China and Germany be increasingly consolidated and developed.
            Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China
                                                    Lin Biao
                              February 28, 1966

### 林彪副总理兼国防部长  电贺德国家人民军建军十周年

1966-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    林彪副总理兼国防部长
    电贺德国家人民军建军十周年
    新华社二十八日讯　国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪今天打电报给德意志民主共和国国防部长海因茨·霍夫曼大将，祝贺德意志民主共和国国家人民军建军十周年。电文如下：德意志民主共和国国防部长海因茨·霍夫曼大将尊敬的部长同志：
    在德意志民主共和国国家人民军建军十周年的时候，我谨代表中国人民解放军全体指战员，向德意志民主共和国国家人民军全体官兵，致以热烈的节日祝贺。
    中国人民和中国人民解放军始终不渝地坚决支持德意志民主共和国人民和国家人民军反对美帝国主义和西德军国主义、争取缔结对德和约、维护国家主权和争取在和平、民主的基础上重新统一德国的正义斗争，并且衷心地祝愿你们在这一正义斗争中和加强自己的国防威力的工作中不断取得新的成就。愿中德两国人民和两国军队之间的兄弟友谊日益巩固和发展。
            中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长
                                                    林　彪
                              一九六六年二月二十八日
