Title: Albanian Ambassador to China Recalled Home

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-13
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 12th. The Ambassador of the People's Republic of Albania to China, Nasse, was recalled from his post and left Beijing by plane at noon today to return to his country.

### 阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使奉调回国

1966-03-13
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使奉调回国
    新华社十二日讯　阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国驻中国大使纳赛奉调离任，今天中午乘飞机离开北京回国。
