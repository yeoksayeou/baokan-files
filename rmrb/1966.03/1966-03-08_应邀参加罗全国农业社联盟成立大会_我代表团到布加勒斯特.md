Title: Invited to Attend the Inaugural Meeting of the Romanian National Union of Agricultural Cooperatives, Our Delegation Arrives in Bucharest
Author:
Date: 1966-03-08
Edition: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, March 7th -- At the invitation to attend the inaugural meeting of the Romanian National Union of Agricultural Production Cooperatives, the Chinese delegation, led by Cheng Zhaoxuan, the Vice Minister of Agriculture and head of the delegation, arrived in Bucharest by train on the morning of the 7th.
　　S. Matei, Minister of Agriculture of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee and member of the Preparatory Committee of the Romanian National Union of Agricultural Production Cooperatives, An. Miculescu, First Vice Chairman of the Romanian Supreme Agricultural Council, and Ni. Ionescu, Vice Chairman, welcomed the delegation at the station.
　　Wang Dong, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Romania, also greeted the delegation at the station.

### 应邀参加罗全国农业社联盟成立大会  我代表团到布加勒斯特

1966-03-08
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀参加罗全国农业社联盟成立大会
    我代表团到布加勒斯特
    新华社布加勒斯特七日电应邀参加罗马尼亚全国农业生产合作社联盟成立大会的中国代表团，在团长、农业部副部长程照轩率领下，七日晨乘火车抵达布加勒斯特。
    罗共中央农业部部长、罗马尼亚全国农业生产合作社联盟筹备委员会委员斯·马特、罗马尼亚最高农业委员会第一副主席安·米库列斯库和副主席尼·约内斯库等到车站欢迎。
    中国驻罗马尼亚使馆临时代办王栋也到车站迎接。
