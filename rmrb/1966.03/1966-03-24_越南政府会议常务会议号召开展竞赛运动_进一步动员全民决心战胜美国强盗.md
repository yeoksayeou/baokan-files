Title: The Standing Committee of the Vietnamese Government Council Calls for a Competition Movement to Further Mobilize the Entire People's Determination to Defeat the American Robbers

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-24
Edition: 3

    The Standing Committee of the Vietnamese Government Council Calls for a Competition Movement
    To Further Mobilize the Entire People's Determination to Defeat the American Robbers
    (Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 22nd) The Standing Committee of the Government Council of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam recently held a meeting to discuss the issue of launching a national competition in the struggle against U.S. aggression and national salvation, and determined the direction and tasks of the competition, in order to further mobilize the entire people's determination to defeat the American aggressor robbers under any circumstances.
    The Standing Committee of the Government Council believes that because the production and combat competition for resisting U.S. aggression and saving the nation is developing with a magnificent revolutionary momentum, the Vietnamese people have achieved many great victories in all aspects.
    The Standing Committee of the Government Council decided that the direction and tasks of the competition during the period of resisting U.S. aggression and national salvation are: to actively organize and mobilize all the forces of the state and the people, vigorously promote production and combat, continue to carry out the construction of the socialist material and technical foundation in accordance with the new situation, so as to enhance economic and defense potential; to be determined to defeat the destructive war of U.S. imperialism and actively defend the North; to wholeheartedly serve the Southern liberation revolution; and at the same time, to prepare to deal with all situations and be determined to victoriously complete the national plan and defeat the American robbers under any circumstances.
    The decision calls on workers, co-op members, staff, cadres, soldiers and people of all strata across the country to enthusiastically participate in this competition movement and victoriously complete the above-mentioned tasks.

### 越南政府会议常务会议号召开展竞赛运动  进一步动员全民决心战胜美国强盗

1966-03-24
第3版()
专栏：

    越南政府会议常务会议号召开展竞赛运动
    进一步动员全民决心战胜美国强盗
    新华社河内二十二日电  越南民主共和国政府会议常务会议最近举行会议，讨论了在抗美救国斗争中开展全民竞赛的问题，并确定了竞赛的方向和任务，以进一步动员全民决心在任何情况下战胜美国侵略强盗。
    政府会议常务会议认为，由于抗美救国的生产和战斗竞赛以磅礴的革命气势波澜壮阔地发展着，所以使越南人民在各方面取得了许多巨大的胜利。
    政府会议常务会议决定：在抗美救国时期中的竞赛方向和任务是：积极组织和动员国家和人民的一切力量，大力促进生产和战斗，继续进行符合新形势下的社会主义物质和技术基础的建设，以增强经济和国防潜力；决心打败美帝国主义的破坏性战争，积极保卫北方；全心全力为南方解放革命服务；同时准备对付一切情况，决心在任何情况下胜利完成国家计划和战胜美国强盗。
    决定号召全国的工人、社员、职员、干部、战士和各阶层人民热烈参加这一竞赛运动，胜利地完成上述任务。
