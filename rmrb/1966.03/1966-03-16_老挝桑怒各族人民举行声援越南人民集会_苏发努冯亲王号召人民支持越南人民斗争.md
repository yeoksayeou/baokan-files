Title: Lao Sang Neua People of All Ethnic Groups Hold Rally in Support of the Vietnamese People; Prince Souphanouvong Calls on People to Support the Vietnamese People's Struggle
Author:
Date: 1966-03-16
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Kang Khai, 14th - According to a Pathet Lao radio broadcast: On the 12th, more than 800 people from all ethnic groups and sectors in Sang Neua Province, Laos, held a rally to support the struggle of the Vietnamese people.
    Prince Souphanouvong, Chairman of the Lao Patriotic Front and Vice Premier of the Tripartite National Unity Government, called on the Lao people at the meeting to further strengthen and develop the struggle against US imperialist aggression in Laos, and to support with practical actions the just struggle of the Vietnamese people against US imperialist aggression, to defend the North, liberate the South, and then unify the country.
    The rally unanimously adopted a resolution strongly condemning the US imperialist crimes of aggression against Vietnam and resolutely supporting the just struggle of the fraternal Vietnamese people against US aggression and for national salvation.
    After the rally, the participants watched a film reflecting the heroic struggle of the Vietnamese people.

### 老挝桑怒各族人民举行声援越南人民集会  苏发努冯亲王号召人民支持越南人民斗争

1966-03-16
第3版()
专栏：

    老挝桑怒各族人民举行声援越南人民集会
    苏发努冯亲王号召人民支持越南人民斗争
    新华社康开十四日电　据巴特寮电台广播：老挝桑怒省各族各界人民八百多人十二日举行集会，声援越南人民的斗争。
    老挝爱国战线党主席、三方民族团结政府副首相苏发努冯亲王在会上号召老挝人民进一步加强和开展反对美帝国主义侵略老挝的斗争，以实际行动支持越南人民反对美帝国主义侵略、保卫北方、解放南方、进而统一祖国的正义斗争。
    集会一致通过决议，强烈谴责美帝国主义对越南的侵略罪行，坚决支持兄弟越南人民抗美救国的正义斗争。
    集会后，与会者观看了反映越南人民英勇斗争的影片。
