Title: Albanian Paper Says U.S. Global Strategy Targets China and National Liberation Movements; Soviet Leadership Provides New Opportunities for U.S. Strategic Shift to the East
Author:
Date: 1966-03-02
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, February 28th. An article published in the Albanian newspaper *People's Voice* on the 27th stated that U.S. Vice President Humphrey's press conference in Washington after his trip to Asia, and Defense Secretary McNamara's report at a secret discussion of the Senate Armed Services Committee, showed that the U.S. global strategy is aimed at the People's Republic of China and the liberation movements of all countries.
    The article said that in order to cover up the preparatory activities for the expansion of U.S. aggression in the Far East, Humphrey and McNamara continued to frantically slander China, describing the peace-loving socialist country of the People's China as the "most important source of tension in the Far East." The United States directs its spearhead of aggression at China because China is a powerful fortress of socialism, freedom, and peace for all countries, and is the main force preventing the U.S. imperialist aggressors from realizing their plans and conspiracies to enslave all countries.
    The article pointed out that the U.S. imperialists' dirty war of expanding aggression in Vietnam and their efforts to establish a encirclement against China are encouraged and supported by the policy of Soviet-U.S. rapprochement. McNamara openly expressed satisfaction with the Soviet Union in his report, because all of the Soviet Union's current activities provide new opportunities for the U.S. global strategy to shift its center of gravity from Europe to Asia.
    The article concluded that the people of Asian countries who are determined to defend freedom and independence will not be intimidated or deceived. It is not the people of all countries who are being encircled, but U.S. imperialism itself. The heroic Vietnamese people are bravely resisting the largest military power, U.S. imperialism, thereby setting a great example for all oppressed people and making a brilliant contribution to smashing the U.S. imperialist aggression plan.

### 阿报说美帝全球战略针对中国和各国人民解放运动  苏联领导为美国战略东移提供新机会

1966-03-02
第3版()
专栏：

    阿报说美帝全球战略针对中国和各国人民解放运动
    苏联领导为美国战略东移提供新机会
    新华社地拉那二十八日电  阿尔巴尼亚《人民之声报》二十七日发表文章说，美国副总统汉弗莱在亚洲之行后在华盛顿举行的记者招待会和国防部长麦克纳马拉在参议院军事委员会的秘密讨论中所作的报告表明，美国的全球战略针对着中华人民共和国和各国人民的解放运动。
    文章说，为了掩盖美国在远东扩大侵略的准备活动，汉弗莱和麦克纳马拉对中国继续进行疯狂的诬蔑，把爱好和平的社会主义国家人民中国说成是“远东紧张局势的最重要根源”。美国把它的侵略矛头指向中国，是因为中国是社会主义、各国人民的自由与和平的强大堡垒，是制止美帝国主义侵略者实现其奴役各国人民的计划和阴谋的主要力量。
    文章指出，美帝国主义扩大侵略越南的肮脏战争和竭力建立对中国的包围圈，得到苏美相互靠拢的政策的鼓励和支持。麦克纳马拉在报告中公开对苏联表示满意，因为苏联目前的所有活动为美国全球战略的重心从欧洲移到亚洲提供了新的机会。
    文章最后说，坚决地保卫自由和独立的亚洲各国人民不会被吓倒，也不会被欺骗。被包围的不是各国人民，而是美帝国主义自己。英雄的越南人民英勇地抗击着最大的军事强国美帝国主义，从而为所有被压迫人民做出了伟大的榜样，为粉碎美帝国主义的侵略计划做出了辉煌的贡献。
