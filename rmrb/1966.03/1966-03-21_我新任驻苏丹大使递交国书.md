Title: My New Ambassador to Sudan Presents Credentials

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-21
Edition: 4

    Our New Ambassador to Sudan Presents Credentials
    (Xinhua News Agency, Khartoum, March 19th) Yu Peiwen, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of China to Sudan, presented his credentials here today to Ismail al-Azhari, Chairman of the Supreme Council of State of Sudan.
    After presenting his credentials, the Chinese Ambassador held a friendly conversation with Chairman Ismail al-Azhari. Both emphasized the importance of further developing the friendly relations between the two countries. Also present at the presentation of credentials were all members of the Supreme Council of State of Sudan and Acting Foreign Minister Ahmed Mahdi.

### 我新任驻苏丹大使递交国书

1966-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

    我新任驻苏丹大使递交国书
    据新华社喀土穆十九日电　中国驻苏丹特命全权大使俞沛文今天在这里向苏丹国家最高委员会主席伊斯梅尔·阿扎里递交了国书。
    中国大使在递交国书后同伊斯梅尔·阿扎里主席进行了友好的谈话。他们都强调了进一步发展两国友好关系的重要意义。递交国书时在场的还有苏丹国家最高委员会的全体委员和代理外交部长艾哈迈德·马赫迪。
