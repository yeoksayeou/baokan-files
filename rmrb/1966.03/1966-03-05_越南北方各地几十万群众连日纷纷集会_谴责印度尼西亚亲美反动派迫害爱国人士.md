Title: Hundreds of Thousands of People in Various Parts of Northern Vietnam Have Been Holding Rallies in Recent Days to Condemn the Pro-American Reactionaries in Indonesia for Persecuting Patriots
Author:
Date: 1966-03-05
Edition: 3

    Hanoi, March 2nd (Xinhua) – According to the Vietnam News Agency on March 2nd, hundreds of thousands of people in various parts of Northern Vietnam have been holding rallies in recent days to condemn the insane atrocities committed by the pro-American reactionaries in Indonesia against Indonesian patriots and progressive people.
    These rallies have all passed resolutions demanding that the Indonesian reactionaries immediately cease their atrocities, release all patriots imprisoned, and respect the democratic rights of the Indonesian people.
    The resolutions expressed confidence that the Indonesian people will be able to overcome the current difficulties and withstand the trials, and win the final victory.
    On February 28th, four hundred representatives of literary and artistic workers in Hanoi held a rally and issued a statement expressing deep sympathy for the Indonesian people and artists, and calling on literary and artistic workers who love freedom and justice around the world to denounce and condemn the barbaric crimes of the pro-American reactionaries in Indonesia and force them to immediately stop their evil deeds.

### 越南北方各地几十万群众连日纷纷集会  谴责印度尼西亚亲美反动派迫害爱国人士

1966-03-05
第3版()
专栏：

    越南北方各地几十万群众连日纷纷集会
    谴责印度尼西亚亲美反动派迫害爱国人士
    相信印度尼西亚人民必将克服目前困难经受考验赢得最后胜利
    新华社河内二日电　据越南通讯社二日报道，越南北方各地几十万群众，近日来纷纷举行集会，谴责印度尼西亚的亲美反动派对印度尼西亚的爱国者和进步人民采取的疯狂暴行。
    这些集会都通过决议，要求印度尼西亚反动派立即停止他们的暴行，释放被关在狱中的所有爱国者，尊重印度尼西亚人民的民主权利。
    决议表示相信，印度尼西亚人民必将能克服目前的困难和经受考验，赢得最后胜利。
    河内的四百名文学艺术工作者代表二月二十八日举行集会，并发表了一项声明，对印度尼西亚人民和艺术家表示深切同情，呼吁全世界爱好自由和正义的文学艺术工作者声讨和谴责印度尼西亚亲美反动派的野蛮罪行，迫使他们立即停止他们的罪恶行为。
