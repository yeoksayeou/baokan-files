Title: Invited to Attend the Inaugural Meeting of the Romanian Agricultural Cooperative Alliance, Our Delegation Leaves Beijing for Romania
Author:
Date: 1966-03-06
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 5th. At the invitation to attend the inaugural meeting of the Romanian National Agricultural Production Cooperative Alliance, the Chinese delegation, led by Cheng Zhaoxuan, Vice Minister of Agriculture, left Beijing by plane today for Romania. Among those who saw them off at the airport were Zhu Rong, Vice Minister of Agriculture, and responsible persons from relevant departments, including Ye Huosheng and Yu Zhan.
    Dumitru Gheorghiu, the Romanian Ambassador to China, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 应邀参加罗农业社联盟成立大会  我代表团离京赴罗马尼亚

1966-03-06
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀参加罗农业社联盟成立大会
    我代表团离京赴罗马尼亚
    新华社五日讯　应邀参加罗马尼亚全国农业生产合作社联盟成立代表大会的中国代表团，在团长、农业部副部长程照轩率领下，今天乘飞机离开北京前往罗马尼亚。到机场欢送的，有农业部副部长朱荣及有关方面负责人叶蠖生、余湛等。
    罗马尼亚驻中国大使杜米特鲁·乔治乌也到机场送行。
