Title: Strong Earthquake Occurs in Uganda; Magnitude Reaches Seven, Intensity Approximately Ten Degrees
Author:
Date: 1966-03-22
Edition: 5

    Strong Earthquake Occurs in Uganda
    Magnitude Reaches Seven, Intensity Approximately Ten Degrees
    Xinhua News Agency, Kampala, 21st - A strong earthquake occurred in Uganda, Africa, before dawn on the 20th. According to Agence France-Presse, the earthquake reached a magnitude of seven and an estimated intensity of ten degrees, with the epicenter in the Lake Albert region.
    When the earthquake occurred, residents of Kampala, the capital of Uganda, were awakened from their sleep. Houses shook, doors and windows rattled, and furniture and utensils collided with each other.

### 乌干达发生强烈地震  震级达七级，烈度约十度

1966-03-22
第5版()
专栏：

    乌干达发生强烈地震
    震级达七级，烈度约十度
    新华社二十一日讯　坎帕拉消息：二十日破晓前，非洲的乌干达发生了一次强烈地震。据法新社报道，这次地震震级达七级，烈度估计为十度，震中在艾伯特湖区域。
    这次地震发生时，乌干达首都坎帕拉的居民在睡梦中被惊醒，房屋震动，门窗格格作响，家具和器皿被震得互相撞击。
