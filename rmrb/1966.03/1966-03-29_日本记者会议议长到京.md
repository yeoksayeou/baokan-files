Title: Japanese Journalists Conference Chairman Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-03-29
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 27th—Koichi Kobayashi, Chairman of the Japan Journalists Conference, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to China at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association.

    Welcoming him at the airport were Wang Yi, First Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Journalists Association, and Secretaries Deng Gang and Li Bingquan, among others.

    Sugiyama Hei, the Japanese Secretary of the Asian-African Journalists Association in Beijing, also went to the airport to welcome him.

### 日本记者会议议长到京

1966-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

    日本记者会议议长到京
    新华社二十七日讯  日本记者会议议长小林雄一，应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来我国访问，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中华全国新闻工作者协会书记处第一书记王揖和书记邓岗、李炳泉等人。
    在京的亚非新闻工作者协会日本书记杉山市平等也到机场迎接。
