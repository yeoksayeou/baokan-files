Title: Women's Rallies in Various Countries Commemorate "March 8th" International Women's Day, Supporting the Struggle of the Vietnamese People; Northern Vietnamese Women Express Determination to Fight Shoulder-to-Shoulder with their Southern Sisters to Defeat the American Aggressors
Author:
Date: 1966-03-09
Edition: 5

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 8th - Hanoi held a meeting of representatives from all walks of life on the evening of March 7th, warmly celebrating "March 8th" International Women's Day and the 5th anniversary of the founding of the South Vietnam Women's Liberation Federation.

    Nguyen Thi Thap, Chairman of the Vietnam Women's Union, spoke at the meeting, warmly praising the revolutionary heroism displayed by the women of the North in the anti-American, national salvation struggle over the past year, and the great achievements they have made. She called on the women of the North to launch a competition to learn from the women of the South, further develop the "Three Responsibilities" movement, and join their sisters in the South to defeat the American aggressors.

    The conference finally passed a resolution, enthusiastically responding to the launch of the competition to learn from the women of the South, and contributing their greatest strength to the cause of resisting America and saving the nation.

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 8th - Women in Pyongyang held a rally on March 7th to commemorate "March 8th" International Women's Day, expressing their firm support for the struggle of Vietnamese women against American imperialist aggression.

    Choe Kum-ja, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Women's League, gave a report at the meeting. She pointed out that no matter how unscrupulously American imperialism wages its barbaric struggle, it will absolutely not be able to stop the heroic struggle of the Vietnamese women and people.

    Choe Kum-ja said that the Korean people and women, who are determined to share life and death with the Vietnamese people, will further strengthen their militant solidarity with their Vietnamese brothers, and are deeply convinced that the heroic Vietnamese people and women, with the strong support and backing of the people of the world, will surely achieve final victory.

    The Central Committee of the Korean Women's League sent a telegram warmly congratulating the 5th anniversary of the founding of the South Vietnam Women's Liberation Federation.

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Khang Khay, March 8th - Women from all walks of life in Khang Khay, Laos, held a meeting on March 8th to commemorate "March 8th" International Women's Day and celebrate the 5th anniversary of the founding of the South Vietnam Women's Liberation Federation.

    Niang Duosi Sisathi, Second Secretary of the Secretariat of the Lao Women's Federation, praised the great contributions made by the patriotic women of Laos and the patriotic women of South Vietnam in their respective struggles against American aggression and for national salvation, and in their production struggles. She strongly condemned American imperialism for continuously expanding its war of aggression in Indochina, especially its large-scale military offensives against the liberated areas of central and lower Laos and the Phou Khout Mountain-Xieng Khouang Plateau region in January and February this year. She pointed out that the more disastrous the defeats suffered by American imperialism, the more rampant it becomes, therefore all Lao women must further heighten their vigilance and continue to wage the most resolute struggle.

    According to a Pathet Lao Radio broadcast on March 8th: The Preparatory Committee of the Lao Patriotic Women's Federation issued a call to women across the country on March 8th in commemoration of "March 8th" International Women's Day, encouraging them to further strengthen unity, promote patriotism, and deepen their hatred of the enemy, so as to make positive contributions to thoroughly defeating imperialism and its lackeys and building Laos into a peaceful, neutral, independent, unified, and prosperous country.

    According to Xinhua News Agency, March 7th - Tokyo: Approximately 7,000 Japanese women held a rally near the US 7th Fleet base in Yokosuka on the 6th to commemorate "March 8th" International Women's Day. They opposed the American aggression against Vietnam and demanded that the American troops get out of Japan. The meeting passed resolutions opposing the entry of American nuclear fleets into Yokosuka and opposing the visit of South Vietnamese puppet Prime Minister Nguyen Cao Ky to Japan. After the meeting, the participating women held banners and flags and held a demonstration march on the streets of Yokosuka.

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Brazzaville, March 7th - Women in Congo (Brazzaville) held a grand mass rally and parade here on the 6th to celebrate the first anniversary of the founding of the Revolutionary Women's League of Congo (Brazzaville) and International Working Women's Day.

    Celine Yanza, Chairman of the Revolutionary Women's League of Congo (Brazzaville), said in a speech at the rally that Congolese women relentlessly condemned the imperialists for staging a coup in Ghana while Nkrumah was away. She also said, "The Revolutionary Women's League of Congo (Brazzaville) relentlessly condemns the United States for continuing to wage a war of aggression on the land of Vietnam."

    The women held a parade. In the long procession were members of various departments of women's organizations, female militia, female students, and Young Pioneers.

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Ottawa, March 7th - A group of Canadian women began an all-night demonstration at Prime Minister Pearson's residence in Ottawa on the 6th, protesting American aggression against Vietnam and demanding that Canada take a tough stance against American policies in Vietnam.

### 各国妇女集会纪念“三八”节支持越南人民斗争  越南北方妇女表示坚决同南方姐妹并肩斗争打败美国侵略者

1966-03-09
第5版()
专栏：

    各国妇女集会纪念“三八”节支持越南人民斗争
    越南北方妇女表示坚决同南方姐妹并肩斗争打败美国侵略者
    据新华社河内八日电  河内市各界妇女代表七日晚上举行集会，热烈庆祝“三八”国际妇女节和越南南方妇女解放联合会成立五周年。
    越南妇女联合会主席阮氏十在会上讲话，热烈表扬一年来北方妇女在抗美救国斗争中表现的革命英雄主义气概和她们所取得的巨大成绩。她号召北方妇女展开学习南方妇女的竞赛，进一步开展“三承担”运动，与南方姐妹们一起，打败美国侵略者。
    大会最后通过决议，热烈响应开展学习南方妇女的竞赛运动，为抗美救国的事业贡献出自己最大的力量。
    据新华社平壤八日电  平壤市妇女七日举行集会，纪念“三八”国际劳动妇女节，表示坚决支持越南妇女反对美帝国主义侵略的斗争。
    朝鲜妇女同盟中央委员会副委员长崔锦子在会上作了报告。她指出，不管美帝国主义怎样不择手段地进行野蛮挣扎，都绝对阻挡不了越南妇女和人民的英勇斗争。
    崔锦子说，决心同越南人民生死与共的朝鲜人民和妇女，将进一步加强同越南兄弟的战斗团结，并且深信得到全世界人民有力支持和声援的英雄的越南人民和妇女，一定能取得最后胜利。
    朝鲜妇女同盟中央委员会打电报热烈祝贺越南南方妇女解放联合会成立五周年。
    据新华社康开八日电  老挝康开市各界妇女八日集会，纪念“三八”国际劳动妇女节和庆祝越南南方解放妇女联合会成立五周年。
    老挝妇女联合会书记处第二书记娘·杜西·西沙堤在讲话中赞扬了老挝爱国妇女和越南南方爱国妇女各自在抗美救国斗争和生产斗争中所作出的巨大贡献，强烈谴责了美帝国主义不断扩大侵略印度支那战争，特别是今年一月和二月先后向老挝中下寮解放区和富科特山—查尔平原地区发动大规模军事进攻的罪行。她指出，美帝国主义遭到的失败越惨重，就越猖狂，因此全体老挝妇女必须更加提高警惕，继续进行最坚决的斗争。
    据巴特寮电台八日广播：老挝爱国妇女联合会筹委会八日就纪念“三八”国际劳动妇女节向老挝全国妇女发出号召书，勉励她们进一步加强团结，发扬爱国精神和加深对敌人的仇恨，为彻底打败帝国主义及其走狗和把老挝建设成为和平、中立、独立、统一和繁荣的国家作出积极的贡献。
    据新华社七日讯  东京消息：约七千名日本妇女，六日在横须贺美国第七舰队基地附近举行纪念“三八”国际妇女节集会。她们反对美国侵略越南，要求美国军队从日本滚出去。会上通过了反对美国核舰队闯进横须贺和反对南越傀儡总理阮高其访问日本的决议。会后，与会妇女举着标语牌和旗帜，在横须贺的大街上举行了示威游行。
    据新华社布拉柴维尔七日电  刚果（布）妇女六日在这里举行盛大的群众集会和游行，庆祝刚果（布）革命妇女联盟成立一周年和国际劳动妇女节。
    刚果（布）革命妇女联盟主席赛利纳·扬扎夫人在集会上的讲话中说，刚果妇女毫不留情地谴责帝国主义者利用恩克鲁玛不在的情况下，在加纳策动了政变。她还说，“刚果（布）革命妇女联盟无情地谴责美国继续在越南的土地上进行侵略战争。”
    妇女们举行了游行。在长长的队伍中，有妇女组织的各部门成员、女民兵、女学生和少先队员。
    据新华社渥太华七日电  一群加拿大妇女六日开始在渥太华皮尔逊总理的宅邸举行通宵示威，抗议美国侵略越南，要求加拿大采取强硬的态度反对美国的越南政策。
