Title: Miyazaki Seimin Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-03-24
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd: Miyazaki Seimin, chairman of the Japan-China Friendship Association, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to our country at the invitation of the China-Japan Friendship Association. Zhao Anbo, Secretary-General of the China-Japan Friendship Association, and others greeted him at the airport.

    China-UAR Friendship Association Delegation Leaves for UAR for a Visit
    Xinhua News Agency, March 22nd: At the invitation of the UAR-China Friendship Association, a delegation of four members of the China-UAR Friendship Association, led by Zhang Jie, Vice President of the China-UAR Friendship Association, left Beijing by plane today for a friendly visit to the UAR.

    Those who saw them off at the airport included Ren Yinglun, Vice Minister of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhang Tiesheng, Vice President of the China-UAR Friendship Association, Liu Pinyi, Vice Chairman of the China Islamic Association, Wu Qing, Secretary-General of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, and other relevant responsible persons. Zakaria Adly Imam, the UAR Ambassador to China, and embassy officials also saw them off at the airport.

### 宫崎世民到京

1966-03-24
第4版()
专栏：

    宫崎世民到京
    新华社二十三日讯　应中日友好协会邀请来我国访问的日中友好协会理事长宫崎世民，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。中日友协秘书长赵安博等到机场迎接。中国阿联友协代表团赴阿联访问
    新华社二十二日讯　应阿联中国友好协会邀请，中国阿联友好协会代表团一行四人，在团长、中国阿联友协副会长张杰率领下，今天乘飞机离开北京前往阿联进行友好访问。
    到机场送行的，有对外文委副主任任映仑、中国阿联友协副会长张铁生、中国伊斯兰教协会副主任刘品一、中非友协总干事吴青等有关方面负责人。阿联驻中国大使查卡里亚·阿德利·伊马姆和使馆官员也到机场送行。
