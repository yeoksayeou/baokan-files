Title: Guinea's Capital Holds 70,000-Strong Rally to Welcome President Nkrumah; African People Will Counter Violence with Revolutionary Violence
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-06
Edition: 3

    Guinea's Capital Holds 70,000-Strong Rally to Welcome President Nkrumah
    African People Will Counter Violence with Revolutionary Violence
    President Touré Announces Nkrumah Will Assume the Highest Responsibilities of the Guinean State and Party
    Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, March 5th: Approximately 70,000 residents of Guinea's capital gathered at the "September 28th Stadium" on the afternoon of the 2nd for a mass rally, warmly welcoming Ghanaian President Nkrumah and expressing their firm support for him.
    The rally was organized by the National Political Bureau of the Guinea Democratic Party. President Touré and President Nkrumah addressed the assembly.
    In his speech, President Touré sternly condemned the crimes of imperialists and their running dogs in subverting young African nations. Speaking of the recent military coup in Ghana, he said, "It is the radio stations in places like Paris, Washington, London, and Bonn that are rejoicing at the illegal, unlawful, and most despicable acts of betrayal that are happening in Africa."
    President Touré pointed out: "We want to explain to all countries in the world that each country's position on the Ghanaian military rebellion will be the yardstick we use to measure whether each country's relationship with Africa is sincere."
    President Touré pointed out that what is decisive is obviously the awakening of the people. "There are 250 million Africans in Africa who oppose betrayal, oppose imperialism, and oppose neo-colonialism."
    He said: "We want to tell our brother Nkrumah that here, it is as if you are in your own country, and from today onwards, you will assume the highest responsibilities of the Guinean state and party."
    President Touré called on the Guinean people to redouble their revolutionary vigilance. He said, "You must recognize the enemy, and once you recognize the enemy, surround him. Once you surround the enemy, render him immobile. If the enemy does not take your strength seriously, then eliminate him."
    He said, "We will counter violence with revolutionary violence."
    He emphasized: "We will be able to defend the fruits of our national revolution, we will be able to defend our goals, and we will take bold action to achieve them as much as possible."
    In his speech, President Nkrumah expressed his deep gratitude to President Sékou Touré and the people of Guinea for their welcome and firm support. He said, "I want to take advantage of this grand gathering here to announce to the people of the world that I will soon return to Ghana."
    The rally ended with slogans such as "Down with imperialism!" "Down with colonialism!" "Down with neo-colonialism!" "Victory belongs to the African people!"

### 几内亚首都七万人集会欢迎恩克鲁玛总统  非洲人民将以革命暴力来对付暴力

1966-03-06
第3版()
专栏：

    几内亚首都七万人集会欢迎恩克鲁玛总统
    非洲人民将以革命暴力来对付暴力
    杜尔总统宣布恩克鲁玛担负几内亚国家和党的最高职责
    新华社五日讯　科纳克里消息：几内亚首都约七万居民二日下午在“九月二十八日体育场”举行群众大会，热烈欢迎加纳总统恩克鲁玛并对他表示坚决支持。
    这次大会是由几内亚民主党全国政治局组织的。杜尔总统和恩克鲁玛总统在大会上讲了话。
    杜尔总统在讲话中严厉谴责帝国主义者及其走狗颠覆年轻的非洲国家的罪行。在谈到最近在加纳发生的军事政变时，他说，“当非洲发生非法的、违法的、最卑鄙的背叛行为的时候，感到高兴的正是巴黎、华盛顿、伦敦和波恩等地的电台。”
    杜尔总统指出：“我们要对世界上所有国家说明，每个国家对加纳军人叛乱的立场，将是我们用来衡量每个国家同非洲的关系是否诚恳的准则。”
    杜尔总统指出：显然，起决定作用的是人民的觉悟。“在非洲有二亿五千万非洲人，他们反对背叛，反对帝国主义和新殖民主义。”
    他说：“我们要向我们的恩克鲁玛兄弟表示，在这里就象在你自己的国家一样，从今天起，你担负几内亚国家和党的最高职责。”
    杜尔总统号召几内亚人民加倍提高革命警惕性。他说，“你们要认清敌人，认清了敌人，就把他包围起来，把敌人包围起来，叫他动弹不得。如果敌人不把你们的力量放在眼里，那就把他消灭掉。”
    他说，“我们将以革命的暴力来对付暴力”。
    他强调说：“我们将能够保卫我们民族革命的成果，我们将能够保卫我们的目标，并且尽量采取果敢的行动使之实现。”
    恩克鲁玛总统在讲话中，对塞古·杜尔总统和几内亚人民对他的欢迎和坚决支持表示十分感激。他说：“我要利用这个盛大的集会在这里向全世界人民宣布，不久我将回到加纳。”
    大会在“打倒帝国主义！”“打倒殖民主义！”“打倒新殖民主义！”“胜利属于非洲人民！”等口号声中结束。
