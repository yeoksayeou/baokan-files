Title: Bulgarian National Assembly Holds Meeting
Author:
Date: 1966-03-14
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, March 12th - The first session of the Fifth Bulgarian National Assembly was held here from the 11th to the 12th.
    The meeting adopted a resolution to change the structure of the Council of Ministers, elected a new Presidium of the National Assembly, and approved the composition of the Council of Ministers proposed by the Chairman of the Council of Ministers. G. Traykov and T. Zhivkov were re-elected as Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, respectively.

### 保加利亚国民议会举行会议

1966-03-14
第3版()
专栏：

    保加利亚国民议会举行会议
    新华社索非亚十二日电　保加利亚第五届国民议会第一次会议于十一日到十二日在这里举行。
    会议通过了改变部长会议结构的决议，选出了新的国民议会主席团，批准了由部长会议主席提议的部长会议的组成。格·特莱科夫和托·日夫科夫继续当选为国民议会主席团主席和部长会议主席。
