Title: The American War of Aggression Against Vietnam Mobilizes World People to Strengthen the Anti-American Struggle; People of Various Countries Hold Rallies and Demonstrations to Support the Vietnamese People's Struggle

Author:
Date: 1966-03-24
Edition: 3

    According to Xinhua News Agency: The Political Bureau of the African Independence Party of Portuguese Guinea and Cape Verde sent a telegram to the Vietnamese Labor Party and the South Vietnam National Liberation Front on the 16th, saying: "We reiterate to you that our fighting party and people, who share the same heart with the heroic Vietnamese people in the common struggle against imperialism, unswervingly support you."
      "Radio Palestine Voice" issued a commentary on the evening of the 17th, strongly condemning the American aggression against Vietnam. The radio emphasized: "The United States will ultimately fail."
  According to Xinhua News Agency on the 23rd: In recent days, the masses in many Latin American countries have expressed their firm support for the Vietnamese people's struggle against American aggression and for national salvation by holding rallies, demonstrations, and other activities.
    Many students from the Normal School of Coahuila State, Mexico, held a rally and demonstration in Torreón City on the 19th to support the Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. imperialism.
    At the rally, the students condemned the aggressive war launched by U.S. imperialism against Vietnam, and held a demonstration in the streets of the city. When the demonstrating masses passed the "Mexico-United States Cultural Association" office in Torreón City, they threw bottles filled with gasoline at the office and then set it on fire.
    Prior to this, demonstrations against U.S. aggression against Vietnam, attended by thousands of people, were held successively in Mexico City, the capital, and Puebla City, Puebla State.
    On the 21st, Uruguayan youth students took sudden action and hung a Vietnamese flag on the balcony opposite the U.S. Consulate General building on the main street to express their support for the Vietnamese people and protest against the U.S. aggression against Vietnam.
    In San Jose, Costa Rican women sent a delegation to the U.S. Embassy to hand over the signatures of thousands of women to the U.S. Ambassador, demanding that U.S. troops withdraw from Vietnam immediately. When the U.S. Ambassador, feeling guilty and unreasonable, rudely rejected their request, the women representatives refuted him forcefully.
    In Santo Domingo, Dominican workers, student organizations, and progressive parties with a tradition of anti-American struggle have issued statements supporting the Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. imperialist aggression. Students also held demonstrations.
    In Buenos Aires, the Argentine people bravely hung a flag of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front on the fourth floor of the U.S. Embassy building in Argentina on the night of the 21st. The masses in this city had also held demonstrations a few days earlier in support of the Vietnamese people's anti-American struggle.
    According to Xinhua News Agency, Brussels, 22nd: From March 12th to 19th, at the call of the Action Organization for the Defense of Peace and Independence of All Peoples and the Committee for Solidarity with the Vietnamese People, many demonstrations and other activities were carried out to express the Belgian people's firm support for the heroic Vietnamese people and their anger at U.S. imperialist aggression.
    Many lectures and film screenings were held in many towns, moving the masses of all strata. The Belgian Communist Party organized mass rallies in the streets and at the entrances of enterprises in industrial cities such as Herstal, Boussue, Quaregnon, and La Louvière. On March 19th—Vietnam's Anti-American Struggle Day—in Leuven, an ancient university town in the Flanders region, university students and workers gathered together to hold street demonstrations.
    That afternoon, a demonstration was also held in Charleroi—the center of the important industrial area of Wallonia. The youth were all excited.
    After the demonstration, a mass rally was held. André Despontin, Secretary of the Regional Committee of the Action Organization for the Defense of Peace and Independence of All Peoples, Stefan Struelens, Secretary of the Belgian Committee for Solidarity with the Vietnamese People, Désiré Trifaux, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Belgian Communist Party, and Father Paul Carrette spoke at the meeting. They all emphasized the important significance of the Vietnamese people's heroic struggle against the American aggressors to the people of all countries in the world. They linked the Vietnamese people's struggle with the Belgian workers' and working people's struggle against the United States' attempt to carry out political and economic control, and against the occupation of bases and airports in Belgium by American and West German troops. They called on the participants to further strengthen their full and active solidarity with the Vietnamese people, to constantly expose the United States' aggressive activities, its hypocritical "peace offensive," and to expose the conspiracy of U.S. imperialism's partners to prevent U.S. imperialism from suffering an irreparable defeat.
    Belgian Communist Party members have never stopped organizing mass meetings and raising funds for medicines and cash. For several weeks, they have repeatedly delivered medicines and cash to the South Vietnam National Liberation Front.

### 美国侵越战争动员了世界人民加强反美斗争  各国人民集会示威支援越南人民斗争

1966-03-24
第3版()
专栏：

    美国侵越战争动员了世界人民加强反美斗争
    各国人民集会示威支援越南人民斗争
    据新华社讯　葡属几内亚和佛得角非洲独立党政治局十六日给越南劳动党和越南南方民族解放阵线一份电报说：“我们向你们重申，在反对帝国主义的共同斗争中同英雄的越南人民心心相印的我们战斗的党和人民，始终不渝的支持你们。”
      “巴勒斯坦之声电台”十七日晚发表评论，强烈谴责美国侵略越南。电台强调指出：“美国到头来终将失败。”
  据新华社二十三日讯　拉丁美洲许多国家的群众这几天来以举行集会、示威游行和其他各种活动的方式，表示坚决支持越南人民的抗美救国斗争。
    墨西哥科阿韦拉州师范学校的许多学生十九日在托雷翁城举行集会和示威游行，声援越南人民反对美帝国主义的斗争。
    学生们在集会上谴责了美帝国主义对越南发动的侵略战争，并且在这个城市的大街上举行了示威游行。示威群众经过“墨西哥—美国文化协会”在托雷翁城的办事处时，把装有汽油的瓶子扔向这个办事处，然后点起了火。
    在这以前，首都墨西哥城和普埃布拉州的普埃布拉市相继举行了有几千人参加的反对美国侵略越南的示威游行。
    二十一日，乌拉圭青年学生采取突然行动，在座落主要大街上的美国总领事馆大楼对面的阳台上挂起了一面越南旗帜，表示支持越南人民，抗议美国对越南的侵略。
    在圣约瑟，哥斯达黎加妇女们派了一个代表团到美国大使馆去，要把几千个妇女的签名交给美国大使，表示要求美国军队立即从越南撤退。当这个理屈心虚的美国大使粗暴地拒绝她们的要求时，妇女代表们向他作了强有力的驳斥。
    在圣多明各，富有反美斗争传统的多米尼加工人、学生组织和各进步党派纷纷发表声明，支持越南人民反对美帝国主义侵略的斗争。学生们还举行了示威游行。
    在布宜诺斯艾利斯，阿根廷人民在二十一日夜间勇敢地将一面越南南方民族解放阵线的旗帜挂到了美国驻阿根廷大使馆建筑物的第四层楼上。这个城市的群众在几天以前还举行了声援越南人民反美斗争的示威游行。
    据新华社布鲁塞尔二十二日电　从三月十二日到十九日，在保卫各国人民和平与独立行动组织和声援越南人民委员会的号召下，开展了许多示威游行和其他活动，以表示比利时人民对英雄的越南人民的坚决支持和对美帝国主义侵略的愤怒。
    在许多城镇举办了许多演讲会和电影放映，感动了各阶层的群众。比利时共产党在赫斯特雷、布絮、夸雷尼翁、卢维埃等工业城市的大街上和企业门口组织了群众集会。三月十九日——越南人民反美斗争日——那一天，在佛兰德地区卢万这个古老的大学城，大学生们和工人们聚集在一起在大街上举行了游行示威。
    这天下午，在沙勒罗瓦市——瓦隆重要工业区的中心——也举行了游行示威。青年们都情绪激昂。
    游行结束后，举行了群众集会，保卫各国人民和平与独立行动组织地区委员会书记安德烈·德斯庞坦、比利时声援越南人民委员会书记斯特凡·斯特律伦斯、比利时共产党中央委员会政治局委员德西雷·特里福和保罗·卡雷特神父都在会上讲了话。他们都强调了越南人民反对美国侵略者的英勇斗争对世界各国人民的重要意义。他们把越南人民的这场斗争同比利时工人和劳动群众反对美国要在政治和经济上进行控制的企图、反对美国和西德的军队在比利时占用基地和飞机场的斗争联系起来。他们呼吁与会者进一步加强对越南人民的全力和积极的声援，不断揭露美国的侵略活动，它的虚伪的“和平攻势”以及揭露美帝国主义的合伙者为了使美帝国主义免遭不可挽救的失败而策划的阴谋。
    比利时共产党党员们从未停止过组织群众大会、募捐药品和现金的活动。几周来，他们已经多次把药品和现金送交越南南方民族解放阵线。
