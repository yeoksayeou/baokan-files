Title: Chinese Education Delegation to Visit the United Arab Republic and Guinea
Author:
Date: 1966-03-10
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, March 9th – A Chinese education delegation led by Liu Aifeng, Vice Minister of Education, left Beijing by plane today for friendly visits to the United Arab Republic, Guinea, and other countries. He Wei, Minister of Education, Song Yiping, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and diplomatic officials from the embassies of the United Arab Republic and Guinea in China saw them off at the airport.

### 我教育代表团去阿联几内亚访问

1966-03-10
第5版()
专栏：

    我教育代表团去阿联几内亚访问
    新华社九日讯  由教育部副部长刘皑风率领的中国教育代表团，今天乘飞机离开北京，前往阿联、几内亚等国进行友好访问。教育部部长何伟，对外文委副主任宋一平，以及阿联、几内亚驻中国大使馆的外交官员等前往机场送行。
