Title: My Minister of Internal Affairs Meets and Fetes Vietnamese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-03-08
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 7th -- Zeng Shan, Minister of Internal Affairs, met this afternoon with the Vietnamese Urban Management Work Delegation headed by Wu Gongfu. Present at the meeting were: Huang Qingxi, Vice Minister of Internal Affairs, Cui Yueli, Vice Mayor of Beijing, and others.
    Su An, Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, was also present. After the meeting, Zeng Shan hosted a banquet to welcome the Vietnamese guests.
    The delegation arrived in Beijing on February 18th.

### 我内务部长会见并欢宴越南客人

1966-03-08
第4版()
专栏：

    我内务部长会见并欢宴越南客人
    新华社七日讯　内务部部长曾山今天下午会见了以武公辅为首的越南城市管理工作考察团。会见时在座的有：内务部副部长黄庆熙，北京市副市长崔月犁等。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆商务参赞苏安等也在座。会见后，曾山设宴欢迎越南客人。
    考察团是在二月十八日到达北京的。
