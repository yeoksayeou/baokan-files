Title: China Mass Produces Prestressed Concrete Poles
Author:
Date: 1966-03-24
Edition: 2

    China Mass Produces Prestressed Concrete Poles
    (Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan) Many places in China have begun mass production of prestressed concrete poles.
    This type of pole utilizes the shrinkage force of the steel reinforcement frame itself, causing the surface concrete to withstand a pre-stress, and therefore has the advantages of compact structure and high strength. Compared with ordinary cement poles, this type of pole can save 30% to 70% of steel, reduce weight by 15% to 20%, reduce cost by 10% to 30%, and does not crack. Its service life is longer than that of ordinary poles.

### 我国成批生产预应力水泥电杆

1966-03-24
第2版()
专栏：

    我国成批生产预应力水泥电杆
    据新华社武汉电　我国好多地方已开始成批生产预应力水泥电杆。
    这种电杆利用钢筋骨架本身的收缩力，使表面的混凝土承受一种预应压力，因而具有结构紧密、强度大的优点。这种电杆同普通水泥电杆比较，可以节省钢材百分之三十到七十，减轻重量百分之十五到二十，降低成本百分之十到三十，而且不裂纹，使用寿命比普通电杆长。
