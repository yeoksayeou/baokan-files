Title: Tibet's First Part-Work, Part-Study Normal School Opens

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-10
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 9th: Tibet's first part-work, part-study normal school, the Tibet Autonomous Region Normal School, officially opened in Lhasa recently. This school mainly trains teachers for the people-run primary schools in Tibet's agricultural and pastoral areas. Over 95 percent of the students are children of emancipated serfs and slaves.
    In order to meet the demands of the emancipated serfs in Tibet for mastering scientific and cultural knowledge, the relevant departments of the Autonomous Region Party Committee and the Autonomous Region People's Committee began to trial-run this school a year ago. During the trial period, the teachers and students combined labor with the study of cultural and scientific knowledge, and have made significant progress in production, study, and ideology.

### 西藏第一所半农半读师范学校开学

1966-03-10
第2版()
专栏：

    西藏第一所半农半读师范学校开学
    新华社拉萨九日电  西藏第一所半农半读的师范学校——西藏自治区师范学校，最近在拉萨正式开学。这所学校主要是为西藏农牧区的民办小学培养师资的，学生中百分之九十五以上是翻身农奴和奴隶的子女。
    为满足西藏翻身农奴对掌握科学文化知识的要求，自治区党委和自治区人民委员会的有关部门在一年前就着手试办这所学校。试办期间，师生们结合劳动学习文化科学知识，在生产、学习和思想上都有显著进步。
