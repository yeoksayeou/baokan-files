Title: Guinean Ambassador Sekou Pays Courtesy Call on Chairman Zhu

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-26
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, March 25th: Kamara Sekou, the newly appointed Ambassador of the Republic of Guinea to China, paid a courtesy call on Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, this afternoon. Chairman Zhu De held a friendly conversation with him.


**Named Entities**

几内亚 Guinea
卡马拉·塞古	Kamara Sekou
朱德 Zhu De
全国人民代表大会常务委员会	Standing Committee of the National People's Congress

### 几内亚大使塞古拜会朱委员长

1966-03-26
第2版()
专栏：

    几内亚大使塞古拜会朱委员长
    新华社二十五日讯　几内亚共和国新任驻中国大使卡马拉·塞古今天下午拜会了全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德。朱德委员长同他进行了友好的谈话。
