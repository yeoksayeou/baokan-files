Title: Our Government Delegation Leaves Berlin for Home

Author:
Date: 1966-03-19
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, March 17th. The Chinese government delegation, led by Li Yilin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Chemical Industry, left Berlin for home on the 17th. Those seeing them off at the airport included Gruber, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Chemical Industry of the German Democratic Republic, Wegner, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade and Intra-German Trade, and others. Liu Pu, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the German Democratic Republic, and Chen Yang, Deputy Head of the Chinese Trade Delegation who is here for trade negotiations, also saw them off at the airport. The delegation was invited to participate in the Leipzig Spring Fair. During their stay, the delegation also visited factories and enterprises in some cities.

### 我政府代表团离柏林回国

1966-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

    我政府代表团离柏林回国
    新华社柏林十七日电　由化学工业部副部长李艺林率领的中国政府代表团十七日离开柏林回国。到机场送行的有德意志民主共和国化学工业部副部长格鲁柏，对外和东西德贸易部副部长韦格内等。中国驻德意志民主共和国大使馆临时代办刘溥，正在这里进行贸易谈判的中国贸易代表团副团长陈扬等也到机场送行。代表团是应邀来参加来比锡春季博览会的。代表团在逗留期间还参观了一些城市的工厂企业。
