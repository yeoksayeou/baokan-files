Title: Chen Yi Receives Afghan Guests

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-16
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 15th. Vice Premier Chen Yi received all members of the Afghan Women's Delegation headed by Saliha Faruq Etemadi, President of the Afghan Women's Association, this morning and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Kang Keqing, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, as well as Shen Zijiu, Xu Keli, and others.

### 陈毅副总理接见阿富汗客人

1966-03-16
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见阿富汗客人
    新华社十五日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了以阿富汗妇女会会长萨利哈·法鲁克·埃特马迪为首的阿富汗妇女代表团全体成员，同她们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有全国妇联副主席康克清，以及沈兹九、徐克立等。
