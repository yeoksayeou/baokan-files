Title: Politics is the Soul
Author: Xiao Jinzhou
Date: 1966-03-10
Edition: 6

    Politics is the Soul
    Worker of Wuhan Heavy Machine Tool Plant, Xiao Jinzhou
    In the workshop cadre debate, what is the soul?
    Compete to speak out,
    Relate to reality very seriously.
    Study theory to add vigor,
    Arm your minds to carry out the revolution,
    Compare your work and thoughts,
    Chairman Mao's teachings are firmly remembered.
    In the workshop cadre debate,
    Political work must come first,
    Overcome difficulties with courage,
    Learn from the revolutionary people of Daqing.
    Teaching by example and word is well combined,
    Wholeheartedly serve the people,
    Not afraid of hardship or death,
    Work relies on struggle.

**Named Entities**

武汉重型机床厂	Wuhan Heavy Machine Tool Plant
大庆	Daqing
毛主席	Mao Zhuxi

### 政治是灵魂
萧金洲
1966-03-10
第6版()
专栏：

    政治是灵魂
    武汉重型机床厂工人  萧金洲车间干部大辩论，什么东西是灵魂？争先恐后把言发，联系实际很认真。学习理论添干劲，武装头脑干革命，对照工作和思想，主席教导牢记心。车间干部大辩论，政治工作要先行，克服困难有勇气，学习大庆革命人。身教言教结合好，一心一意为人民，不怕苦来不怕死，工作就是靠斗争。
