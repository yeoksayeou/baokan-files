Title: Afghan Ambassador Sohail Calls on Premier Zhou Enlai
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-23
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 22nd – The new Afghan Ambassador to China, Mohammad Asef Sohail, paid a call on Premier Zhou Enlai this morning. Premier Zhou held friendly talks with him.
    Present at the meeting were Vice Foreign Minister Han Nianlong and others.

### 阿富汗新任大使苏海尔拜会周恩来总理

1966-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

    阿富汗新任大使苏海尔拜会周恩来总理
    新华社二十二日讯　阿富汗新任驻中国大使穆罕默德·阿沙夫·苏海尔今天上午拜会了周恩来总理。周总理同他进行了友好的谈话。
    拜会时在座的有外交部副部长韩念龙等。
