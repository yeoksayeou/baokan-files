    This is the Chinese Folk Art Troupe giving an open-air performance of the Chinese folk song and dance "Women Militia" in Sikasso, Mali. The Malian people greatly appreciate the revolutionary spirit of Chinese women who love military uniforms more than beautiful dresses. After each performance, many audience members shout: "Long live China! Long live Sino-Malian friendship!"
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Xianquan photographed.

专栏：外国朋友谈中国
