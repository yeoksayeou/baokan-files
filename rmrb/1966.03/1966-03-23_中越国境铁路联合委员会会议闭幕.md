Author:
Date: 1966-03-23
Edition: 3

    Sino-Vietnamese Border Railway Joint Committee Meeting Concludes
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 22nd – The ninth meeting of the Sino-Vietnamese Border Railway Joint Committee concluded in Hanoi on the 21st.
    Zhang Dewen, head of the Chinese railway delegation and Director of the Kunming Railway Bureau, and Truong Quang Giao, head of the Vietnamese railway delegation and Deputy Director of the Vietnam Railways General Department, signed the protocol, respectively.
    Attending the signing ceremony were Duong Bach Lien, Deputy Minister of Transport and Communications of Vietnam, and Wang Baoxuan, Commercial Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Vietnam.
    The Chinese railway delegation left Hanoi on the afternoon of the 22nd to return to China.

### 中越国境铁路联合委员会会议闭幕

1966-03-23
第3版()
专栏：

    中越国境铁路联合委员会会议闭幕
    新华社河内二十二日电　中越国境铁路联合委员会第九次会议二十一日在河内闭幕。
    中国铁路代表团团长、昆明铁路局局长张德温和越南铁路代表团团长、越南铁路总局副局长张光羲分别在议定书上签了字。
    参加签字仪式的有越南交通运输部副部长杨白莲和中国驻越南大使馆商务参赞王保宣。
    中国铁路代表团在二十二日下午离开河内回国。
