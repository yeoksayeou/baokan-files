Title: Our Leaders Send Congratulatory Telegrams to Bulgarian Leaders on Their Re-election

Author:

Date: 1966-03-14
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 13th. Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Premier Zhou Enlai sent telegrams on March 13th to Georgi Traikov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Bulgarian National Assembly, and Todor Zhivkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, congratulating them on their re-election as Chairman of the Presidium of the Bulgarian National Assembly and Chairman of the Council of Ministers. The telegram reads as follows:
    To Comrade Georgi Traikov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Bulgarian People's Republic National Assembly, and Comrade Todor Zhivkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Bulgarian People's Republic:
    On the occasion of your continued tenure as Chairman of the Presidium of the Bulgarian People's Republic National Assembly and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, please accept our sincere congratulations. May the friendship between the people of China and Bulgaria be increasingly consolidated and developed.

      Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
      Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                    March 13, 1966

### 我领导人电贺保加利亚领导人继续当选

1966-03-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我领导人电贺保加利亚领导人继续当选
    新华社十三日讯　刘少奇主席、周恩来总理十三日打电报给保加利亚国民议会主席团主席格·特莱科夫、部长会议主席托·日夫科夫，祝贺他们继续当选为保加利亚国民议会主席团主席和部长会议主席。电文如下：索非亚保加利亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席格·特莱科夫同志，保加利亚人民共和国部长会议主席托·日夫科夫同志：
    在你们继续担任保加利亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席和部长会议主席的时候，谨向你们表示诚挚的祝贺。祝中保两国人民的友谊日益巩固和发展。
      中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
      中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                    一九六六年三月十三日
