Title: Protest Against U.S. Plot to Establish Pan-American Force to Interfere in Latin American People's Struggles: Panamanian People Rise Up Again in Anti-U.S. Demonstrations
Author:
Date: 1966-03-01
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Panama City, February 26th: The Panamanian people held demonstrations and rallies in front of the National Assembly building on the 25th to protest the special committee meeting of the Organization of American States, which attempted to establish a so-called Pan-American force, planned by the United States, to suppress Latin American national liberation movements.
    The demonstrating masses held up placards with slogans such as "Yankee intervention troops get out of the Dominican Republic," and "Oppose U.S. military bases."
    This special committee meeting was convened in Panama City on the 25th at the instigation of U.S. imperialism. At the beginning of the meeting, the United States put forward a proposal to establish a so-called Pan-American force.
    According to Xinhua News Agency, San José, February 26th: Panamanian student groups issued a statement protesting the U.S. instigation of the Organization of American States special committee meeting, conspiring to establish a Pan-American force to interfere in Latin American countries.
    The statement, placed on the seats of the representatives of Latin American countries attending the meeting, said, "Any attempt to stop the Latin American revolution will be swept away by the people of all countries."

### 抗议美国策划建立泛美部队干涉拉美人民斗争  巴拿马人民再次奋起反美示威

1966-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    抗议美国策划建立泛美部队干涉拉美人民斗争
    巴拿马人民再次奋起反美示威
    据新华社二十六日讯　巴拿马城消息：巴拿马人民二十五日在国会大厦门前举行示威游行和集会，抗议美洲国家组织特别委员会会议，企图建立由美国策划的镇压拉丁美洲民族解放运动的所谓泛美部队。
    示威群众高举标语牌，上面写着：“（美国）干涉军从多米尼加滚出去”、“反对美国军事基地”等口号。
    这次特别委员会会议是在美帝国主义的嗾使下二十五日在巴拿马城召开的。会议一开始，美国就提出了一项成立所谓泛美部队的提案。
    据新华社圣约瑟二十六日电　巴拿马学生团体发表声明，抗议美国策动美洲国家组织特别委员会开会，阴谋成立干涉拉丁美洲国家的泛美部队。
    这项放在拉丁美洲各国参加会议的代表们的座位上的声明说，“阻止拉丁美洲革命的任何企图都将被各国人民扫清。”
