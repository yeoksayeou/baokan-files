Title: French Women's Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-03-03
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, February 2nd. A five-member French women's delegation led by Madame Ylen de Lippkowski, President of the French Women's Groups International Liaison Committee, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. They are visiting our country at the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation. Those who welcomed them at the airport included Zhang Yun, Vice-President of the All-China Women's Federation, and Ding Xilin, Peng Yan, Zhang Xiaomei, Lu Shijia, Du Jinfang, Wu Quanheng, and other responsible persons from relevant departments. The wife of the French Ambassador to China, Mr. Peyre, also greeted them at the airport.

### 法国妇女代表团到京

1966-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

    法国妇女代表团到京
    新华社二日讯　由法国妇女团体国际联络委员会主席伊兰·德·李普考斯基夫人率领的法国妇女代表团一行五人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。她们是应全国妇联的邀请前来我国进行友好访问的。到机场欢迎的，有全国妇联副主席章蕴，有关方面负责人丁西林、彭炎、张晓梅、陆士嘉、杜近芳、吴全衡等。法国驻中国大使佩耶的夫人也到机场迎接。
