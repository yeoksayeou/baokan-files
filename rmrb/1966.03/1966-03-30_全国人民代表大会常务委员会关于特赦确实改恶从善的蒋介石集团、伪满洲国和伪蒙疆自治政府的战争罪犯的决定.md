Title: Decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on Granting Special Amnesty to War Criminals of the Chiang Kai-shek Clique, the Puppet Manchuguo, and the Puppet Mengjiang Autonomous Government Who Have Truly Turned Over a New Leaf

Author:
Date: 1966-03-30
Edition: 1

    The Twenty-ninth Meeting of the Standing Committee of the Third National People's Congress, having discussed the State Council's proposal on granting special amnesty to war criminals of the Chiang Kai-shek clique, the puppet Manchuguo, and the puppet Mengjiang Autonomous Government who have truly turned over a new leaf, decides: Special amnesty shall be granted to war criminals of the Chiang Kai-shek clique, the puppet Manchuguo, and the puppet Mengjiang Autonomous Government who, after a certain period of reformation, have truly turned over a new leaf.



**Named Entities**


蒋介石集团 Chiang Kai-shek Clique
伪满洲国	Puppet Manchuguo
伪蒙疆自治政府	Puppet Mengjiang Autonomous Government
全国人民代表大会常务委员会 Standing Committee of the National People's Congress
国务院 State Council

### 全国人民代表大会常务委员会关于特赦确实改恶从善的蒋介石集团、伪满洲国和伪蒙疆自治政府的战争罪犯的决定

1966-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常务委员会关于特赦确实改恶从善的蒋介石集团、伪满洲国和伪蒙疆自治政府的战争罪犯的决定
                （一九六六年三月二十九日通过）
    第三届全国人民代表大会常务委员会第二十九次会议，讨论了国务院关于特赦确实改恶从善的蒋介石集团、伪满洲国和伪蒙疆自治政府的战争罪犯的建议，决定：对于经过一定期间的改造、确实改恶从善的蒋介石集团、伪满洲国和伪蒙疆自治政府的战争罪犯，实行特赦。
