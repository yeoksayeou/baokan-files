Title: New Afghan Ambassador Sohail Pays a Courtesy Call on Chairman Zhu

Author:
Date: 1966-03-25
Edition: 1

    New Afghan Ambassador Sohail Pays a Courtesy Call on Chairman Zhu
    Xinhua News Agency, March 24th—Sohail, the newly appointed Ambassador of the Kingdom of Afghanistan to China, paid a courtesy call on Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, this afternoon. Chairman Zhu De had a friendly conversation with him.

### 阿富汗新任大使苏海尔拜会朱委员长

1966-03-25
第1版()
专栏：

    阿富汗新任大使苏海尔拜会朱委员长
    新华社二十四日讯　阿富汗王国新任驻中国大使苏海尔今天下午拜会了全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德。朱德委员长同他进行了友好的谈话。
