Title: CPC Central Committee Hosts Banquet for Vietnamese Labor Party Delegation; Sino-Vietnamese Party Delegations Led by Comrades Zhou Enlai and Le Duan Held Cordial Talks
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-28
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 27th. The Vietnamese Labor Party delegation, headed by Comrade Le Duan, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party, paid a friendly visit to China from March 22nd to 25th, 1966. The delegation members included: Nguyen Duy Trinh, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party; Tran Tu Binh and Nguyen Van Kinh, members of the Central Committee; Hoang Tung, alternate member of the Central Committee; and accompanying staff.
    On March 22nd, the CPC Central Committee hosted a banquet to welcome all comrades of the Vietnamese Labor Party delegation. The banquet was presided over by Zhou Enlai and Zhu De, Vice-Chairmen of the CPC Central Committee. The Chinese side attending the banquet also included: Peng Zhen, Li Xiannian, and Tan Zhenlin, members of the Political Bureau and Secretaries of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee; Kang Sheng, alternate member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee; Liu Ningyi, member of the CPC Central Committee; Yang Chengwu and Zhao Yimin, alternate members of the CPC Central Committee; and responsible comrades from relevant departments.
    During the Vietnamese Labor Party delegation's visit to China, the Chinese Communist Party delegation headed by Comrade Zhou Enlai held cordial talks with the Vietnamese Labor Party delegation headed by Comrade Le Duan in an atmosphere full of brotherly friendship.
    The Vietnamese Labor Party delegation was welcomed and seen off by responsible comrades of the CPC Central Committee headed by Comrade Zhou Enlai upon arrival and departure from Beijing.

### 中共中央宴请越南劳动党代表团  以周恩来黎笋同志为首的中越两党代表团举行了亲密会谈

1966-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央宴请越南劳动党代表团
    以周恩来黎笋同志为首的中越两党代表团举行了亲密会谈
    新华社二十七日讯  以越南劳动党中央委员会第一书记黎笋同志为首的越南劳动党代表团，一九六六年三月二十二日到二十五日在中国进行了友好访问。代表团的成员有：越南劳动党中央政治局委员阮维桢，中央委员陈子平、阮文镜，中央候补委员黄松以及随行人员。
    三月二十二日，中共中央设宴欢迎越南劳动党代表团全体同志。宴会由中共中央副主席周恩来和朱德主持。中国方面出席宴会的还有：中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记彭真、李先念、谭震林，中共中央政治局候补委员、书记处书记康生，中共中央委员刘宁一，中共中央候补委员杨成武、赵毅敏以及有关部门负责同志。
    在越南劳动党代表团访问中国期间，以周恩来同志为首的中国共产党代表团，同以黎笋同志为首的越南劳动党代表团，在充满兄弟般友谊的气氛中，进行了亲密的会谈。
    越南劳动党代表团到达和离开北京的时候，受到以周恩来同志为首的中共中央负责同志们的欢迎和欢送。
