Title: Romanian Ambassador to China Departs Post

Author:
Date: 1966-03-20
Edition: 4

　　罗马尼亚驻华大使奉调离任
　　新华社十九日讯　罗马尼亚社会主义共和国驻中国大使乔治乌奉调离任，今天下午乘火车离京回国。

Romanian Ambassador to China Departs Post
　　Xinhua News Agency, March 19th: Gheorghe, the Ambassador of the Romanian Socialist Republic to China, departed his post upon being recalled. He left Beijing by train this afternoon to return to his country.
