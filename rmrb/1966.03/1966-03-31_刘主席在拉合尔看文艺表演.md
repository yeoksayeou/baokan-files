Title: Chairman Liu Watches Cultural Performance in Lahore
Author:
Date: 1966-03-31
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Lahore, March 29th. Chairman Liu Shaoqi and his wife, accompanied by the Governor of West Pakistan Province, Malik Amir Muhammad Khan, and the Pakistani Foreign Minister Bhutto and his wife, watched a cultural performance here tonight.
    The performance was held in the auditorium of Punjab University. The first program was a chorus praising the friendship between China and Pakistan, sung to the tune of the famous Chinese revolutionary song "Production Cantata." This program was greeted with a storm of applause from the audience.
    Other programs included various folk songs and dances from Punjab, Sindh, Balochistan, and other parts of Pakistan.
    The performance was held by the West Pakistan Minister of Information, Ghulam Nabi Memon, for Chairman Liu Shaoqi and his wife. Some of Chairman Liu's entourage also watched the performance.
    More than a thousand people watched the performance, including senior government officials and prominent figures from all walks of life.

### 刘主席在拉合尔看文艺表演

1966-03-31
第2版()
专栏：

    刘主席在拉合尔看文艺表演
    新华社拉合尔二十九日电  刘少奇主席和夫人二十九日晚上在西巴基斯坦省督马利克·阿密尔·穆罕默德·汗、巴基斯坦外交部长布托和夫人的陪同下，在这里观看了文艺表演。
    这次表演是在旁遮普大学礼堂举行的，第一个节目是以中国著名革命歌曲《生产大合唱》的调子演唱的颂扬中巴两国友谊的大合唱，这个节目博得了观众暴风雨般的掌声。
    其他的节目包括旁遮普、信德、俾路支和巴基斯坦其他地方的各种民间歌舞。
    这次表演是由西巴基斯坦新闻部长吴拉姆·纳比·梅蒙为刘少奇主席和夫人举行的，刘主席的一部分随行人员也观看了这次表演。
    观看表演的有一千多人，其中有高级政府官员和各界知名人士。
