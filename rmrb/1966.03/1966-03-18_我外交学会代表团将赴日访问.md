Title: Delegation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to Visit Japan
Author:
Date: 1966-03-18
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, March 17th -- The Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, at the invitation of the Japanese Socialist Party, has decided to send a delegation to Japan in the near future for a friendly visit.
    Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, telegraphed Sasaki Kōzō, Chairman of the Japanese Socialist Party, today, saying that during the Chinese delegation's visit to Japan, it will exchange views with the Japanese Socialist Party on further developing the struggle against U.S. imperialism and enhancing friendship between the two sides, based on the four joint statements issued in the past.
    The list of the delegation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to Japan is: Head of the delegation, Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Deputy Head of the delegation, Zhao Anbo, Standing Director of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; members include Zhao Zhengyi, Wu Xuewen, Jin Sucheng, and Li Mengjing.

### 我外交学会代表团将赴日访问

1966-03-18
第5版()
专栏：

    我外交学会代表团将赴日访问
    新华社十七日讯　中国人民外交学会应日本社会党邀请，决定派代表团于近期内去日本进行友好访问。
    中国人民外交学会会长张奚若今天打电报给日本社会党委员长佐佐木更三说，中国代表团在访日期间，将同日本社会党在过去发表的四次共同声明的基础上，就进一步开展反对美帝国主义的斗争、增进双方的友谊交换意见。
    中国人民外交学会访日代表团的名单是：团长、中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之，副团长、中国人民外交学会常务理事赵安博，团员有赵政一、吴学文、金苏城、李孟竞等。
