Title: Shanghai Football Team Plays in Syria

Author:

Date: 1966-03-30
Edition: 4

    The Shanghai Football Team Plays in Syria
    (Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, March 28) The Chinese Shanghai Football Team played its first match in Syria against the Syrian Tobacco Company Football Team in Damascus, the Syrian capital, on the 28th. The Shanghai Football Team won with a score of four to three.
    The atmosphere on the field was consistently warm and friendly. Over seven thousand spectators continuously applauded and cheered the splendid performances of the athletes from both countries.
    The Shanghai Football Team arrived in Syria on the 27th.

### 上海足球队在叙利亚比赛

1966-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

    上海足球队在叙利亚比赛
    据新华社大马士革二十八日电 中国上海足球队二十八日在叙利亚首都大马士革同叙利亚烟草公司足球队进行了访叙的首场比赛。上海足球队以四比三获胜。
    比赛场上始终充满了热烈友好的气氛。七千多名观众不断为两国运动员的精采表演鼓掌喝采。
    上海足球队是二十七日到达叙利亚的。
