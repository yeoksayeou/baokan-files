Title: Vietnamese Army and People Shot Down Four More American Planes

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-24
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 23rd (electric dispatch) According to an announcement here on the evening of the 23rd, the army and people of Nghe An, Ha Tinh, and Quang Binh provinces in North Vietnam shot down a total of four American planes today. Among them, Nghe An province shot down two, Ha Tinh province shot down one, and Quang Binh province shot down one.

### 越南军民又击落美机四架

1966-03-24
第3版()
专栏：

    越南军民又击落美机四架
    新华社河内二十三日电　据这里二十三日晚上公布，越南北方义安、河静和广平三省军民今天共击落美国飞机四架，其中义安省打落两架，河静省打落一架、广平省打落一架。
