Title: Actively Preparing for the Centennial Anniversary of Dr. Sun Yat-sen’s Birth: Nanjing Branch's First Meeting Approves Commemorative Activity Plan
Author:
Date: 1966-03-21
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 20th – The Nanjing Branch of the Preparatory Committee for the Centennial Anniversary of Dr. Sun Yat-sen's Birth held its first meeting this afternoon.

    Jiang Weiqing, First Secretary of the Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Director of the Nanjing Branch of the Preparatory Committee for the Centennial Anniversary of Dr. Sun Yat-sen's Birth, presided over the meeting.

    The meeting discussed and approved the plan for commemorative activities for the centennial anniversary of Dr. Sun Yat-sen's birth: 1. Hold a commemorative meeting for the centennial anniversary of Dr. Sun Yat-sen's birth in the Nanjing People's Great Hall in November 1966; 2. Hold ceremonies to visit the Zhongshan Mausoleum on the day of and the day before the centennial anniversary of Dr. Sun Yat-sen's birth; 3. Repair the Zhongshan Mausoleum, the Sun Yat-sen Bronze Statue Square in Xinjiekou, and Dr. Sun Yat-sen's office and bedroom when he assumed the post of Provisional Grand President; 4. Collect Dr. Sun Yat-sen's relics and materials, solicit commemorative articles, hold an exhibition of Dr. Sun Yat-sen's revolutionary historical sites, and produce a documentary of the commemorative activities; 5. Hold various symposiums to carry out propaganda work related to Dr. Sun Yat-sen's life and deeds; 6. The Preparatory Committee will establish an office under the Secretary-General and Deputy Secretary-General. The office will set up secretarial, publicity, general affairs, and reception groups to carry out specific preparatory work.

    Those attending today's meeting included Peng Chong, Chen Yang, Zhang Zhen, Liu Zhong, Wu Yifang, Liu Guojun, Gao Yihan, Ren Chonggao, Chen Heqin, Chen Zhongfan, Liu Shuxun, Kuang Yaming, Ji Yuting, Chen Suiheng, Fan Cunzhong, Liao Yunsheng, Shi Zhongqi, and Ding Guangxun, all Deputy Directors of the Preparatory Committee.

    The meeting also approved the list of responsible persons for the office, secretarial, publicity, general affairs, and reception groups of the Nanjing Branch of the Preparatory Committee for the Centennial Anniversary of Dr. Sun Yat-sen's Birth.

    The Nanjing Branch of the Preparatory Committee for the Centennial Anniversary of Dr. Sun Yat-sen's Birth was established on December 19, 1965.

### 积极筹备孙中山先生诞辰一百周年纪念活动  南京分会首次会议通过纪念活动方案

1966-03-21
第2版()
专栏：

    积极筹备孙中山先生诞辰一百周年纪念活动
    南京分会首次会议通过纪念活动方案
    新华社南京二十日电　孙中山先生诞辰一百周年纪念筹备委员会南京分会，今天下午举行首次会议。
    中共江苏省委第一书记、孙中山先生诞辰一百周年纪念筹委会南京分会主任江渭清主持会议。
    会议讨论通过了关于孙中山先生诞辰一百周年纪念活动的方案：一、一九六六年十一月在南京人民大会堂举行孙中山先生诞辰一百周年纪念大会；二、在孙中山先生诞辰一百周年当天和前一天，举行参谒中山陵的仪式；三、修缮中山陵、新街口孙中山先生铜象广场、孙中山先生就任临时大总统的办公室和卧室；四、搜集孙中山先生遗物和资料，征集纪念文章，举办纪念孙中山先生革命史迹展览，摄制纪念活动的实况纪录片；五、举行各种座谈会，进行有关孙中山先生生平事迹的宣传工作；六、筹委会在秘书长、副秘书长之下设立办公室。办公室设秘书、宣传、总务、接待等小组，进行具体筹备工作。
    出席今天会议的有筹委会副主任彭冲、陈扬、张震、刘中、吴贻芳、刘国钧、高一涵、任崇高、陈鹤琴、陈中凡、刘树勋、匡亚明、计雨亭、陈邃衡、范存忠、廖运升、史钟奇、丁光训等。
    会议还通过了孙中山先生诞辰一百周年纪念筹备委员会南京分会办公室和秘书、宣传、总务、接待等组负责人名单。
    孙中山先生诞辰一百周年纪念筹备委员会南京分会于一九六五年十二月十九日成立。
