Title: Leader of All-China Journalists Association Hosts Algerian Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-03-29
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, March 28th -- Wang Yijin, First Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Journalists Association and Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the People's Daily, held a banquet tonight to welcome the Algerian journalists delegation led by Semmiche Abderrahim. Algerian Ambassador to China, Mohamed Cherif Sahli, was also invited to attend the banquet.
    The Algerian journalists delegation arrived in Beijing on the 26th.

### 我记协领导人宴请阿尔及利亚客人

1966-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

    我记协领导人宴请阿尔及利亚客人
    据新华社二十八日讯  中华全国新闻工作者协会书记处第一书记、人民日报副总编辑王揖今晚举行宴会，欢迎由塞米什·阿布德拉哈米德率领的阿尔及利亚新闻工作者代表团。阿尔及利亚驻华大使穆罕默德·谢里夫·萨赫利也应邀出席了宴会。
    阿尔及利亚新闻工作者代表团是在二十六日到京的。
