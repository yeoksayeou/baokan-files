Title: A New Zealand Guest Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-24
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd. Mr. Ian Alistair Ballantine, Chairman of the New Zealand-China Society, Dunedin Branch, and his wife, arrived in Beijing today for a visit to China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 新西兰一客人到京

1966-03-24
第4版()
专栏：

    新西兰一客人到京
    新华社二十三日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请，前来我国访问的新西兰—中国协会达尼丁分会主席伊恩·阿利斯特·巴兰坦及夫人今天到达北京。
