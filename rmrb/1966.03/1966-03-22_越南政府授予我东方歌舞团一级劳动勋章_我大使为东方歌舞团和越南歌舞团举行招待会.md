Title: Vietnamese Government Awards First-Class Labor Medal to China Oriental Song and Dance Ensemble; Chinese Ambassador Holds Reception for China Oriental Song and Dance Ensemble and Vietnamese Song and Dance Troupe
Author:
Date: 1966-03-22
Edition: 4

    Hanoi, March 21 (Xinhua) -- The government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam awarded the First-Class Labor Medal to the China Oriental Song and Dance Ensemble, which is currently in Vietnam for a friendly performance tour, on the 20th, to commend its contribution to enhancing the militant unity and friendship between the people of Vietnam and China. Nguyen Van Tao, Minister of Labor of Vietnam, presented the medal on behalf of the Vietnamese government at the award ceremony.
    Also attending the award ceremony were: Pham Ngoc Thuan, Director of the Vietnam Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations; Vu Quoc Uy, Deputy Director; Ha Huy Giap, Vice Minister of Culture; Cu Huy Can, and Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam Zhu Qiwu.
    That evening, the Vietnam Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations and the Ministry of Culture hosted a banquet to bid farewell to the China Oriental Song and Dance Ensemble. At the enthusiastic banquet, Director Pham Ngoc Thuan and Zhang Xing, head of the China Oriental Song and Dance Ensemble, delivered speeches, jointly wishing the great friendship and militant unity between the people of China and Vietnam would last forever.
    Hanoi, March 21 (Xinhua) -- Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam Zhu Qiwu held a reception on the evening of the 21st for the China Oriental Song and Dance Ensemble's visit and performance tour in Vietnam, and for the Vietnamese Song and Dance Art Troupe's return to Hanoi after a performance tour in China.
    Those attending the reception included Huang Quoc Viet, Ha Huy Giap, Tran Tu Binh, Pham Ngoc Thuan, Pham Hong, Vu Quoc Uy, Ton Quang Phiet, Tran Huy Lieu, Nguyen Co Thach, Xuan Truong and other responsible persons from relevant departments, as well as Vietnamese artists.
    Qu Huy Can, head of the Vietnamese Song and Dance Art Troupe, Pham Dinh Loc, deputy head, Doan Kim Phuc, and all the actors attended the reception.
    Chinese Ambassador Zhu Qiwu, Zhang Xing, head of the China Oriental Song and Dance Ensemble, and Ha Huy Giap, Vice Minister of Culture of Vietnam, delivered speeches at the reception.

### 越南政府授予我东方歌舞团一级劳动勋章  我大使为东方歌舞团和越南歌舞团举行招待会

1966-03-22
第4版()
专栏：

    越南政府授予我东方歌舞团一级劳动勋章
    我大使为东方歌舞团和越南歌舞团举行招待会
    新华社河内二十一日电　越南民主共和国政府二十日把一级劳动勋章授予正在越南进行友好访问演出的中国东方歌舞团，以表扬它对增强越中两国人民之间的战斗团结和友谊作出的贡献。在授勋仪式上，越南劳动部长阮文造代表越南政府授勋。
    参加授勋仪式的还有：越南对外文化联络委员会主任范玉纯、副主任武国威，文化部副部长何辉甲、瞿辉瑾，以及中国驻越南大使朱其文。
    当天晚上，越南对外文化联络委员会和文化部设宴欢送东方歌舞团。在热情洋溢的宴会上，范玉纯主任和东方歌舞团团长张星先后讲话，共祝中越两国人民的伟大友谊和战斗团结万古长青。
    据新华社河内二十一日电　中国驻越南大使朱其文二十一日晚上为中国东方歌舞团到越南访问演出和越南歌舞艺术团访华演出后回到河内举行招待会。
    出席招待会的有黄国越、何辉甲、陈子平、范玉纯、范鸿、武国威、孙光阀、陈辉燎、阮基石、春长等各有关方面负责人以及越南文艺界人士。
    越南歌舞艺术团团长瞿辉瑾、副团长范庭六、团金平以及全体演员出席了招待会。
    中国大使朱其文，中国东方歌舞团团长张星和越南文化部副部长何辉甲先后在招待会上讲了话。
