Title: Dakar Students Demonstrate Against Anglo-American Masterminded Ghanaian Coup; Africans in London Demonstrate in Support of President Nkrumah
Author:
Date: 1966-03-07
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, 6th dispatch from Conakry: According to a broadcast from Radio Conakry, students at the University of Dakar in the Senegalese capital held demonstrations in front of the British and American embassies to oppose the Anglo-American imperialist instigation of a military coup in Ghana.

    The demonstrating students chanted slogans such as "Down with British imperialism," "Yankees, get out," and "Africans unite."

    Radio Conakry commented on the demonstration on the 2nd, saying it was an example of patriotic action by African youth. The radio called on African youth to take action to counter the imperialist plot to threaten the fate of the peoples of African countries.

    Xinhua News Agency, 6th dispatch from London: Forty Africans in London held a demonstration on the 2nd in support of Ghanaian President Nkrumah. The demonstrators carried placards with the slogans: "Africa supports Nkrumah," "Imperialists, let Africa decide its own destiny."

### 达喀尔学生示威反对英美策动加纳政变  伦敦非洲人示威支持恩克鲁玛总统

1966-03-07
第3版()
专栏：

    达喀尔学生示威反对英美策动加纳政变
    伦敦非洲人示威支持恩克鲁玛总统
    新华社六日讯　科纳克里消息：据科纳克里电台广播，塞内加尔首都达喀尔大学学生在英国和美国大使馆前面举行了示威游行，反对英、美帝国主义在加纳策动军事政变。
    示威学生在游行时高呼：“打倒英帝国主义”、“美国佬，滚回去”、“非洲人团结起来”等口号。
    科纳克里电台二日就这次示威游行发表评论说，这是非洲青年爱国行动的一个例子。电台号召非洲青年采取行动来对付帝国主义威胁非洲各国人民的命运的阴谋。
    新华社六日讯　伦敦消息：在伦敦的四十名非洲人二日举行示威游行，支持加纳总统恩克鲁玛。示威者拿着标语牌，上面写着：“非洲支持恩克鲁玛”，“帝国主义者让非洲决定自己的命运”。
