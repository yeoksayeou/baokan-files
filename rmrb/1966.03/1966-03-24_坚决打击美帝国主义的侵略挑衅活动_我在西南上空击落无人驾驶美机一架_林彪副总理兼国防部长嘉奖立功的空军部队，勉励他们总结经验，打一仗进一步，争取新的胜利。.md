    Resolutely Combat the Aggression and Provocative Activities of U.S. Imperialism
    We Shot Down a U.S. Unmanned Aircraft Over Southwest China
    Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense Lin Biao Commended the Air Force Units for Their Meritorious Service, Encouraging Them to Summarize Experience, Advance Further After Each Battle, and Strive for New Victories.
    Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd – A U.S. imperialist military unmanned high-altitude reconnaissance aircraft intruded into the airspace over Southwest China this afternoon to carry out reconnaissance and provocative activities and was shot down by the People's Liberation Army Air Force units.
    Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd – Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense Lin Biao issued a commendation order today, praising the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force units for shooting down a U.S. imperialist military unmanned high-altitude reconnaissance aircraft over Southwest China.
    The commendation order stated that this victory is the result of your holding high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought and resolutely implementing the five principles of giving prominence to politics. It is hoped that you will guard against arrogance and impetuosity, further strengthen war preparedness, conscientiously summarize experience, and advance further after each battle to strive for new victories.

1966-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

    坚决打击美帝国主义的侵略挑衅活动
    我在西南上空击落无人驾驶美机一架
    林彪副总理兼国防部长嘉奖立功的空军部队，勉励他们总结经验，打一仗进一步，争取新的胜利。
    新华社二十三日讯　美帝国主义军用无人驾驶高空侦察机一架，二十三日下午，侵入我国西南地区上空，进行侦察挑衅活动，被我人民解放军空军部队击落。
    新华社二十三日讯　国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪今天颁发嘉奖令，表扬在西南地区上空击落美帝国主义军用无人驾驶高空侦察机一架的中国人民解放军空军部队。
    嘉奖令说，这次胜利，是你们高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，坚决贯彻突出政治的五项原则所取得的成果。希望你们戒骄戒躁，进一步加强战备工作，认真总结经验，做到打一仗进一步，为争取新的胜利而奋斗。
