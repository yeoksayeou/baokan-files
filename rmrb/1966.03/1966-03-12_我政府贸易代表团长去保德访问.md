Title: Head of Chinese Government Trade Delegation Visits Bulgaria and East Germany

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-12
Edition: 4

　　Our Government Trade Delegation Head Goes to Bulgaria and East Germany for Visit
　　Xinhua News Agency, March 11th—Fu Shenglin, head of the Chinese Government Trade Delegation and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, left Beijing by plane at noon today for visits to Bulgaria and the German Democratic Republic.
　　Among those seeing him off at the airport were Jiang Ming, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and others. Commercial Counselor Gumnerov of the Bulgarian Embassy in China and Commercial Commissioner Sachs of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China also saw him off at the airport.

### 我政府贸易代表团长去保德访问

1966-03-12
第4版()
专栏：

    我政府贸易代表团长去保德访问
    新华社十一日讯　中国政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长傅生麟今天中午乘飞机离开北京前往保加利亚和德意志民主共和国访问。
    前往机场送行的有对外贸易部副部长江明等。保加利亚驻华大使馆商务参赞古姆奈罗夫等外交官员，德意志民主共和国驻华大使馆商务专员萨克思也到机场送行。
