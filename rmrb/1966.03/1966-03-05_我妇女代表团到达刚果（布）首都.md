Title: Chinese Women's Delegation Arrives in the Capital of Congo (Brazzaville)
Author:
Date: 1966-03-05
Edition: 4

    Brazzaville, March 1st (Xinhua News Agency) – A Chinese women's delegation of three people, headed by Guo Jian, arrived in Brazzaville, the capital of Congo (Brazzaville), on February 28th.
    The delegation is visiting at the invitation of the Revolutionary Women's Union of the Congo (Brazzaville) to participate in the celebrations of the first anniversary of the founding of the Revolutionary Women's Union of the Congo (Brazzaville) and the "March 8th" International Working Women's Day.
  The delegation was warmly welcomed at the airport by Mrs. Celina Yanza, Chairman of the Revolutionary Women's Union of the Congo (Brazzaville), Third Secretary Josephine N'Djaye, and other officials of the Revolutionary Women's Union of the Congo (Brazzaville).

### 我妇女代表团到达刚果（布）首都

1966-03-05
第4版()
专栏：

    我妇女代表团到达刚果（布）首都
    据新华社布拉柴维尔一日电　以郭建为首的中国妇女代表团一行三人二月二十八日到达刚果（布）首都布拉柴维尔。
    代表团是应刚果（布）革命妇女联盟的邀请，前来参加庆祝刚果（布）革命妇女联盟成立一周年和“三八”国际劳动妇女节活动的。
  代表团在机场受到刚果（布）革命妇女联盟主席塞利纳·扬扎夫人，第三书记约瑟芬·恩贾耶和刚果（布）革命妇女联盟的其他负责人的热烈欢迎。
