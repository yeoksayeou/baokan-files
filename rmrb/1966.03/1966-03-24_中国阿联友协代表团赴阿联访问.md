Title: Chinese-United Arab Republic Friendship Association Delegation Visits the United Arab Republic
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-24
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 22nd – At the invitation of the United Arab Republic-China Friendship Association, a four-member delegation from the Chinese-United Arab Republic Friendship Association, led by Zhang Jie, the head of the delegation and Vice President of the Chinese-United Arab Republic Friendship Association, left Beijing by plane today for a friendly visit to the United Arab Republic.
    Seeing them off at the airport were Ren Yinglun, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Zhang Tiesheng, Vice President of the Chinese-United Arab Republic Friendship Association; Liu Pinyi, Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association; Wu Qing, Secretary-General of the Chinese-African Peoples' Friendship Association, and other responsible persons from relevant departments. Zakaria Adli Imam, the United Arab Republic's Ambassador to China, and embassy officials also saw them off at the airport.

### 中国阿联友协代表团赴阿联访问

1966-03-24
第4版()
专栏：

    中国阿联友协代表团赴阿联访问
    新华社二十二日讯　应阿联中国友好协会邀请，中国阿联友好协会代表团一行四人，在团长、中国阿联友协副会长张杰率领下，今天乘飞机离开北京前往阿联进行友好访问。
    到机场送行的，有对外文委副主任任映仑、中国阿联友协副会长张铁生、中国伊斯兰教协会副主任刘品一、中非友协总干事吴青等有关方面负责人。阿联驻中国大使查卡里亚·阿德利·伊马姆和使馆官员也到机场送行。
