Title: Glorious Title of "American Destroyer Hero"
Author:
Date: 1966-03-16
Edition: 3

　　Under the battle cry of "Seek out the Americans and annihilate them," the heroic South Vietnamese army and people, filled with profound hatred, are constantly and painfully annihilating the American aggressors. In the fierce battles, countless brilliant heroes have emerged. To commend their meritorious deeds and to mobilize the South Vietnamese army and people to more heroically and massively annihilate the American invading bandits, the Command of the South Vietnam Liberation Armed Forces has specially formulated regulations for awarding the glorious title of "American Destroyer Hero" to individuals who have achieved outstanding results in the battles to annihilate American aggressors.

　　For Liberation Army soldiers, those who annihilate 20 or more American aggressors will be awarded the title of Excellent Class "American Destroyer Hero"; those who annihilate 15 American aggressors will be awarded the First Class "American Destroyer Hero" title; those who annihilate 10 American aggressors will be awarded the Second Class "American Destroyer Hero" title; those who annihilate 5 American aggressors will be awarded the Third Class "American Destroyer Hero" title.

　　For guerrilla and militia fighters, those who annihilate 15 or more American aggressors will be awarded the title of Excellent Class "American Destroyer Hero"; those who annihilate 10 American aggressors will be awarded the First Class "American Destroyer Hero" title; those who annihilate 6 American aggressors will be awarded the Second Class "American Destroyer Hero" title; those who annihilate 3 American aggressors will be awarded the Third Class "American Destroyer Hero" title.



**Named Entities**


越南南方  Yuènán Nánfāng (South Vietnam)

歼美勇士  Jiān Měi Yǒngshì (American Destroyer Hero)
找美国佬歼  Zhǎo Měiguó lǎo jiān (Seek out the Americans and annihilate them)

### 光荣的“歼美勇士”称号

1966-03-16
第3版()
专栏：资料

    光荣的“歼美勇士”称号
    在“找美国佬歼”的战斗口号下，英雄的越南南方军民，怀着刻骨的深仇大恨，不断地痛歼美国侵略者。在激烈的战斗中，涌现出许许多多光辉灿烂的英雄人物。为了表彰他们的功勋，动员南越军民更加英勇地、大量地歼灭美国侵略强盗，越南南方解放武装力量指挥部特制定出授予在歼灭美国侵略者的战斗中成绩突出的个人以“歼美勇士”光荣称号的条例。
    对于解放军战士，凡歼美国侵略者二十人以上的，即可荣获优秀级“歼美勇士”的称号；歼美国侵略者十五人的，可获一级
  “歼美勇士”称号；歼美国侵略者十人的，可获二级“歼美勇士”称号；歼美国侵略者五人的，可获三级“歼美勇士”的称号。
    对于游击队和民兵战士，凡歼灭美国侵略者十五人以上的，即可荣获优秀级“歼美勇士”的称号；歼灭美国侵略者十人的，可获一级“歼美勇士”称号；歼灭美国侵略者六人的，可获二级“歼美勇士”称号；歼灭美国侵略者三人的，可获三级“歼美勇士”的称号。
