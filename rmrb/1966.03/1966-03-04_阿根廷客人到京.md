Title: Argentine Guests Arrive in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-04
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, February 2nd—The Argentine journalist delegation, consisting of two members, arrived in Beijing tonight for a friendly visit to our country at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association. They are Emilio Jáuregui, Secretary-General of the Federation of Argentine Newspaper Workers, and Eduardo Jozami, Assistant Secretary.
    Chang Zhiqing, Editor-in-Chief of the Ta Kung Pao, and relevant individuals from the capital's journalistic circles were present at the airport to welcome them.

### 阿根廷客人到京

1966-03-04
第4版()
专栏：

    阿根廷客人到京
    新华社二日讯　应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来我国进行友好访问的阿根廷新闻工作者代表团一行二人今晚到达北京。他们是阿根廷报刊工作者联合会总书记埃米略·豪雷吉和助理书记爱德华多·何萨米。
    到机场迎接的有大公报总编辑常芝青和首都新闻界有关人士。
