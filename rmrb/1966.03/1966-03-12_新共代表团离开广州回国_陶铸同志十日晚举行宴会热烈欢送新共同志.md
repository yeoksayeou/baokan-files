Title: New Zealand Communist Party Delegation Leaves Guangzhou for Home; Comrade Tao Zhu Hosts Banquet on the 10th to Warmly See Off Comrade New Zealand Communist

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-12
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 11th – Comrade V.G. Wilcox, General Secretary of the Communist Party of New Zealand, and the delegation of the Communist Party of New Zealand he led, left Guangzhou by train this morning via Hong Kong to return home.
    Comrade Tao Zhu, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and First Secretary of the Central South Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, held a banquet last night to warmly see off Comrade Wilcox and the delegation of the Communist Party of New Zealand he led. The banquet proceeded in a cordial and friendly atmosphere. Attending the banquet were Lin Liming, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the CPC; Yong Wentao, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the CPC and First Secretary of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the CPC; Zhang Gensheng, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the CPC; and Zeng Sheng, Third Secretary of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the CPC. Tang Mingzhao, a responsible worker from the organs of the CPC Central Committee, also attended the banquet.
    Those who saw them off at the station today included Comrades Lin Liming, Yong Wentao, Zhang Gensheng, and Zeng Sheng, as well as responsible comrades from relevant departments. Young women presented the distinguished guests with flowers.
    Tang Mingzhao, a responsible worker from the organs of the CPC Central Committee, and Ma Fu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the CPC, accompanied the distinguished guests to Shenzhen.

### 新共代表团离开广州回国  陶铸同志十日晚举行宴会热烈欢送新共同志

1966-03-12
第1版()
专栏：

    新共代表团离开广州回国
    陶铸同志十日晚举行宴会热烈欢送新共同志
    新华社广州十一日电　新西兰共产党总书记维·乔·威尔科克斯同志和由他率领的新西兰共产党代表团，今天上午乘火车离开广州取道香港回国。
    中共中央委员、中共中央中南局第一书记陶铸同志昨天晚上曾举行宴会，热烈欢送威尔科克斯同志和由他率领的新西兰共产党代表团。宴会在亲切友好的气氛中进行。出席宴会作陪的，有中共广东省委书记处书记林李明，中共广东省委书记处书记、中共广州市委第一书记雍文涛，中共广东省委书记处候补书记张根生，中共广州市委第三书记曾生等。中共中央机关负责工作人员唐明照也出席了宴会。
    今天到车站送行的，有林李明、雍文涛、张根生、曾生等同志，以及有关方面负责人。女青年向贵宾们献了鲜花。
    中共中央机关负责工作人员唐明照和中共广东省委副秘书长马甫陪同贵宾前往深圳。
