Author:
Date: 1966-03-29
Edition: 4

    Our Transportation Delegation Visits Vietnam
    Xinhua News Agency, March 28th. At the invitation of Phan Trong Tue, Minister of the Ministry of Transport and Communications of Vietnam, the Chinese Transportation Delegation, headed by Sun Da-guang, Minister of the Ministry of Communications of the People’s Republic of China, left Beijing by plane today for Hanoi for a friendly visit. Those seeing them off at the airport included Ma Yaoji, Yu Mei, Peng Deqing, Mei Shengwei, and other responsible persons from relevant departments. Vietnamese Ambassador to China, Tran Tu Binh, also saw them off at the airport.

### 我交通代表团赴越访问

1966-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

    我交通代表团赴越访问
    新华社二十八日讯  应越南交通运输部部长潘仲慧邀请，以中华人民共和国交通部部长孙大光为首的中国交通代表团，今天乘飞机离开北京前往河内进行友好访问。到机场送行的，有关方面负责人有马耀骥、于眉、彭德清、梅盛伟等。越南驻中国大使陈子平等也到机场送行。
