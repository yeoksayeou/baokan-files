Title: Swedish LKB Company Biochemical Instrument Exhibition Opens
Author:
Date: 1966-03-22
Edition: 4

    Swedish LKB Company Biochemical Instrument Exhibition Opens
    Xinhua News Agency, 21st. The opening ceremony of the Swedish LKB Company Biochemical Instrument Exhibition was held this morning at the China Medical University in Beijing.
    At the opening ceremony, Zhang Huadong, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and Swedish Ambassador to China, Mr. Petrén, delivered speeches successively. They congratulated the opening of the exhibition and expressed the hope that the exhibition would further promote the friendship, trade relations, and cultural exchanges between the peoples of China and Sweden.
    Biochemical instruments were exhibited at the exhibition. Zhang Huadong and others, accompanied by Ambassador Petrén, visited the exhibits.
    Mr. Malmström, General Manager of the Swedish LKB Company participating in the exhibition, and Professor Tiselius, a famous Swedish biochemist, were also invited to attend the opening ceremony.
    Shen Qizhen, Tang Haiguang and other responsible persons from relevant departments also attended the opening ceremony.
    Tonight, Zhang Huadong held a banquet to entertain Swedish friends and congratulate the opening of the exhibition.

### 瑞典勒克贝公司生物化学仪器展览会开幕

1966-03-22
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典勒克贝公司生物化学仪器展览会开幕
    新华社二十一日讯　瑞典勒克贝公司生物化学仪器展览会，今天上午在北京中国医科大学举行开幕式。
    开幕式上，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席张化东和瑞典驻中国大使佩特里先后讲话。他们对展览会的开幕表示祝贺，并希望通过展览会进一步促进中瑞两国人民的友谊、贸易关系和文化交流。
    展览会上展出了生物化学仪器。张化东等由佩特里大使陪同参观了展品。
    参加展览会的瑞典勒克贝公司总经理马姆斯特罗姆和瑞典著名生物化学家蒂西利斯教授，也应邀出席了开幕式。
    参加开幕式的，还有沈其震、唐海光等有关方面负责人。
    今晚，张化东举行宴会招待瑞典朋友，祝贺展览会开幕。
