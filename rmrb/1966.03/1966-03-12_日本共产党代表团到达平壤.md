Title: Japanese Communist Party Delegation Arrives in Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1966-03-12
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 11th (Report) According to the Korean Central News Agency, the Japanese Communist Party delegation, invited by the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, arrived in Pyongyang by special plane on the afternoon of March 11th.
    The delegation is led by Miyamoto Kenji, General Secretary and Standing Committee Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party. The delegation members include: Oka Masayoshi, Standing Committee Member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat; Kurahara Koreto, Member of the Political Bureau; Yonehara Sho; Sunama Ichiro, Secretary of the Secretariat; Ueda Koichiro, Alternate Member of the Central Committee; Ueda Kenjiro; and Kudo Akira.
    Welcoming the delegation at the airport were Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party; Park Kum-chul, Li Hyo-sun, and Kim Kwang-hyup, Members of the Political Committee and Vice-Chairmen of the Central Committee; Li Song-un, Central Committee Member and Chairman of the Pyongyang Municipal Committee of the Workers' Party; Park Yong-guk, Ko Hyuk, and Chae Hui-jong, Central Committee Members and Ministers of various departments of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party; and others.
    Jiao Ruoyu, Chinese Ambassador to North Korea, and Phan Chanh Giang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Embassy in North Korea, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation. Tens of thousands of Pyongyang residents also welcomed the delegation at the airport. (See picture).

### 日本共产党代表团到达平壤

1966-03-12
第3版()
专栏：

    日本共产党代表团到达平壤
    新华社平壤十一日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社报道，日本共产党代表团应朝鲜劳动党中央委员会的邀请十一日下午乘专机到达平壤。
    代表团是由日本共产党中央委员会总书记、政治局常务委员宫本显治率领的。代表团团员有：政治局常务委员、书记处书记冈正芳，政治局委员藏原惟人、米原昶，书记处书记砂间一良，中央候补委员上田耕一郎、上田建二郎、工藤晃。
    到机场欢迎代表团的有朝鲜劳动党中央委员会委员长金日成，政治委员会委员和中央委员会副委员长朴金喆、李孝淳和金光侠，中央委员、劳动党平壤市委员会委员长李松云，中央委员、劳动党中央各部部长朴容国、高赫和蔡喜正等。
    中国驻朝鲜大使焦若愚和越南驻朝鲜大使馆临时代办潘正江也前往机场欢迎。到机场欢迎代表团的还有成千上万的平壤居民。（附图片）
    三月十一日，日本共产党中央委员会总书记宫本显治（右四）和由他率领的日本共产党代表团乘专机离开北京前往朝鲜民主主义人民共和国进行友好访问。中共中央副主席周恩来等同志到机场欢送。
        新华社记者　刘少山摄
    三月十日，中共中央委员、中共中央中南局第一书记陶铸同志举行宴会，热烈欢送新西兰共产党总书记威尔科克斯同志（左四）和由他率领的新西兰共产党代表团。这是宾主在宴会前合影。
