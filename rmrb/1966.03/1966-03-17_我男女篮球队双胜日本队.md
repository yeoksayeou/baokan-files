Title: Our Men's and Women's Basketball Teams Both Defeat Japanese Teams
Author:
Date: 1966-03-17
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, March 15th—The "Avant-Garde" Sports Association Men's Basketball Team of China and the Beijing Institute of Physical Education Women's Basketball Team separately defeated the Waseda University Men's Basketball Team of Japan and the Hirano Women's Basketball Team of the Japanese "Nichibo" Company in Beijing tonight. The score for the men's team was 119 to 44, and the score for the women's team was 65 to 64.

    These two exciting friendly matches, held at the Beijing Workers' Gymnasium, frequently elicited enthusiastic applause from more than 15,000 people.

### 我男女篮球队双胜日本队

1966-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

    我男女篮球队双胜日本队
    据新华社十五日讯　中国“前卫”体育协会男子篮球队和北京体育学院女子篮球队，今晚在北京分别战胜了日本早稻田大学男子篮球队和日本“尼吉波”公司平野女子篮球队。男子队的比数是一百一十九比四十四，女子队的比数是六十五比六十四。
    在北京工人体育馆举行的这两场精采的友谊赛，不时博得一万五千多人的热烈掌声。
