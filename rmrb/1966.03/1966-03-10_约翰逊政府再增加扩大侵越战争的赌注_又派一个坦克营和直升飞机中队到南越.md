Title: Johnson Administration Increases Stakes in Expanding War of Aggression Against Vietnam; Sends Another Tank Battalion and Helicopter Squadron to South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-03-10
Edition: 4

    Johnson Administration Increases Stakes in Expanding War of Aggression Against Vietnam
    Sends Another Tank Battalion and Helicopter Squadron to South Vietnam
    Seventh Fleet Commander Yells About Preparing to Fight a Long War in South Vietnam
    Xinhua News Agency, 9th dispatch: Saigon news: The American authorities have dispatched another tank battalion and a new type of helicopter squadron to South Vietnam.
    According to Western news agency reports, a tank battalion of the U.S. 25th Division began landing in Saigon on the 8th. Two infantry brigades of this division have already arrived in South Vietnam.
    According to the Associated Press, the U.S. Marine Corps invading Vietnam added twenty-seven medium-sized "Sea Knight" helicopters on the 8th.
    With the arrival of this new squadron, the number of helicopters in the U.S. Marine Corps invading Vietnam has increased to nearly two hundred.
    At the same time, the commander of the U.S. Seventh Fleet, Hyland, who recently conducted activities in Manila, the capital of the Philippines, said at a press conference on the 8th that the United States "must prepare to fight a long war in Vietnam."
    He also said: "We are prepared to stay there for a long time. I just told my subordinates not to have the idea that this war will end soon. I don't think this war will end soon."

### 约翰逊政府再增加扩大侵越战争的赌注  又派一个坦克营和直升飞机中队到南越

1966-03-10
第4版()
专栏：

    约翰逊政府再增加扩大侵越战争的赌注
    又派一个坦克营和直升飞机中队到南越
    第七舰队司令叫嚷准备在南越打一场长期战争
    新华社九日讯  西贡消息：美国当局又增派一个坦克营和一个新型直升飞机中队到南越。
    据西方通讯社报道，美国第二十五师的一个坦克营八日开始在西贡登陆。这个师的两个步兵旅已经到达南越。
    据美联社报道，侵越美国海军陆战队八日增加了二十七架中型“海上骑士”式直升飞机。
    由于这个新的中队的到达，侵越美国海军陆战队的直升飞机增加到将近两百架。
    同时，最近到菲律宾首都马尼拉活动的美国第七舰队司令海兰八日在记者招待会上说，美国“必须准备在越南打一场长期战争”。
    他还说：“我们准备在那里呆很长的时间。我刚刚告诉我的部下不要抱这样的想法，即这场战争将很快结束。我认为这场战争不会很快结束。”
