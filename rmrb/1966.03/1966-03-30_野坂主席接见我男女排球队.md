Title: Chairman Nosaka Receives Chinese Men's and Women's Volleyball Teams
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-30
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, March 28th. Comrade Nosaka Sanzō, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, received the Chinese men's and women's volleyball teams, led by Zhang Zhihuai, in Hiroshima on the 28th while they were in Japan for friendly matches.
    During the reception of the Chinese men's and women's volleyball teams, Chairman Nosaka said that the Japanese Communist Party and the Chinese Communist Party are genuine Marxist-Leninist parties cooperating closely, and the people of Japan and China are strongly united. Chairman Nosaka pointed out that not only should sports exchanges be conducted between the people of Japan and China, but cultural and political exchanges should also be carried out.
    Chairman Nosaka praised the Chinese volleyball players for playing bravely in the matches, leaving a good impression on the Japanese people, and contributing to strengthening the friendship between the people of Japan and China. Finally, Chairman Nosaka shook hands with the members of the Chinese volleyball team one by one, encouraging them to continue their efforts and achieve even better results.
    The reception was filled with a cordial and friendly atmosphere.

### 野坂主席接见我男女排球队

1966-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

    野坂主席接见我男女排球队
    新华社东京二十八日电  日本共产党中央委员会主席野坂参三同志，二十八日在广岛接见了正在日本进行友谊比赛的由张之槐率领的中国男女排球队。
    野坂主席在接见中国男女排球队时说，日本共产党和中国共产党是紧密携手合作的真正的马克思列宁主义的党，日中两国人民是坚强团结的。野坂主席指出，日中两国人民之间不仅要进行体育交流，而且也应该开展文化和政治交流。
    野坂主席称赞中国排球运动员在比赛中打得勇敢，给日本人民留下了良好的印象，并对加强日中两国人民的友谊作出了贡献。野坂主席最后同中国排球队队员一一握手，鼓励他们再接再厉，取得更好的成绩。
    接见时充满了亲切友好的气氛。
