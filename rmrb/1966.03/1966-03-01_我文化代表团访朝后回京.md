Title: Our Cultural Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting North Korea
Author:
Date: 1966-03-01
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 27th. The Chinese Cultural Delegation, led by Song Yiping, Vice-Minister of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, returned to Beijing by train today after concluding its friendly visit to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. Responsible persons from relevant departments, including Shi Huaibi, Li Qi, Zhang Yingwu, and Liu Xianglun, were at the station to greet them. The Korean Ambassador to China, Pak Se-chang, was also at the station to greet them.

### 我文化代表团访朝后回京

1966-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    我文化代表团访朝后回京
    新华社二十七日讯　由对外文委副主任宋一平率领的中国文化代表团结束了在朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的友好访问后，今天乘火车回到北京。到车站迎接的，有史怀璧、李琦、张映吾、刘祥纶等有关方面负责人。朝鲜驻中国大使朴世昌也到车站迎接。
