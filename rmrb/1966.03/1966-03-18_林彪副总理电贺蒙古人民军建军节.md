Title: Lin Biao, Vice Premier, Sends Congratulations on the Anniversary of the Founding of the Mongolian People's Army
Author: Lin Biao
Date: 1966-03-18
Edition: 5

    Lin Biao, Vice Premier, Sends Congratulations on the Anniversary of the Founding of the Mongolian People's Army
    Xinhua News Agency, 17th — Lin Biao, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, sent a telegram today to General Zh. Lkhagvasuren, Minister of People's Army Affairs and Commander of the People's Army of the Mongolian People's Republic, congratulating him on the 45th anniversary of the founding of the Mongolian People's Army. The text of the telegram is as follows:
    To Comrade General Zh. Lkhagvasuren, Minister of People's Army Affairs and Commander of the People's Army of the Mongolian People's Republic:
    On the occasion of the 45th anniversary of the founding of the Mongolian People's Army, I, on behalf of all commanders and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, extend warm festive congratulations to all officers and men of the Mongolian People's Army. May the Mongolian People's Army achieve new successes in defending the socialist revolutionary achievements of the Mongolian people and participating in the cause of socialist construction.
    May the fraternal friendship between the people and armies of China and Mongolia be increasingly consolidated and developed.
        Lin Biao, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China
                                      March 17, 1966

### 林彪副总理电贺蒙古人民军建军节
林彪
1966-03-18
第5版()
专栏：

    林彪副总理电贺蒙古人民军建军节
    新华社十七日讯　国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪，今天打电报给蒙古人民共和国人民军事务部长兼人民军司令员扎·勒哈格瓦苏伦上将，祝贺蒙古人民军建军四十五周年。电文如下：蒙古人民共和国人民军事务部长兼人民军司令员扎·勒哈格瓦苏伦上将尊敬的部长同志：
    在蒙古人民军建军四十五周年的时候，我谨代表中国人民解放军全体指战员，向蒙古人民军全体官兵，致以热烈的节日祝贺。愿蒙古人民军在保卫蒙古人民的社会主义革命成果和参加社会主义建设事业中取得新的成就。
    愿中蒙两国人民和两国军队之间的兄弟友谊日益巩固和发展。
        中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长　林彪
                                      一九六六年三月十七日
