Title: The Soviet Union to Provide India with Forty Helicopters
Author:
Date: 1966-03-10
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 8th: According to a broadcast by the "All India Radio" on the evening of the 7th, the Soviet Union will provide India with forty MI-4 helicopters, according to an agreement signed in Moscow on the 7th between the Soviet government and the Indian government.
    The value of these helicopters is four million five hundred thousand U.S. dollars.
    According to an earlier report by the official Indian news agency, the Soviet Union provided India with three squadrons of MiG fighter jets in 1965.

### 苏联将提供印度四十架直升飞机

1966-03-10
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联将提供印度四十架直升飞机
    新华社八日讯  新德里消息：据“全印广播电台”七日晚上广播：根据苏联政府和印度政府七日在莫斯科签订的一项协议，苏联将向印度提供四十架MI—4型直升飞机。
    这批直升飞机价值四百五十万美元。
    据早些时候印度官方新闻机构报道，苏联在一九六五年向印度提供了三个中队的米格战斗机。
