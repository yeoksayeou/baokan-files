Title: Chinese Government Delegation Arrives in Berlin to Visit the Leipzig Fair
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-06
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 4th—The Chinese Government Delegation led by Li Yilin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Chemical Industry, arrived in Berlin on the afternoon of the 4th. The delegation is visiting the Leipzig Spring Fair at the invitation of the organizers.
    Those who greeted them at the airport included Schaffenstein, Minister of the Guidance and Supervision Department of the Special Zone and County Committee of the German Democratic Republic; Enkelmann, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign and East German Trade; and Kosel, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Construction.
    Also greeting them at the airport were: Liu Pu, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the German Democratic Republic, a member of the delegation; Wang Wenlin, head of the Chinese Exhibition Group participating in the Leipzig Fair exhibition, also a member of the delegation; and Chen Yang, deputy head of the Chinese Trade Delegation who is currently in Berlin for the 1966 trade negotiations.

### 我政府代表团到柏林参观来比锡博览会

1966-03-06
第4版()
专栏：

    我政府代表团到柏林参观来比锡博览会
    据新华社柏林四日电　由化学工业部副部长李艺林率领的中国政府代表团四日下午到达柏林。代表团是应邀前来参观来比锡春季博览会的。
    到机场迎接的有德意志民主共和国专区和县委员会指导和监督部部长沙芬斯坦，对外和东西德贸易部副部长恩克尔曼，建设部副部长科塞尔等。
    到机场迎接的还有：代表团团员、中国驻德意志民主共和国大使馆临时代办刘溥，代表团团员、参加来比锡博览会展出的中国展览团团长王文林，正在柏林进行一九六六年贸易谈判的中国贸易代表团副团长陈扬等。
