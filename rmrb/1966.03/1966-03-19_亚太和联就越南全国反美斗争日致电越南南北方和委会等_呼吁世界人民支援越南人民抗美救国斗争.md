Title: Asia-Pacific Peace Liaison Committee Sends Telegram to North and South Vietnam Committees, etc., on Vietnam National Anti-US Struggle Day, Calling on World People to Support the Vietnamese People's Struggle Against US Aggression and for National Salvation

Author:
Date: 1966-03-19
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 18th: The Asia and Pacific Peace Liaison Committee today called on the people of the Asia and Pacific region and appealed to the people of the world to further unite and strive to support the just struggle of the Vietnamese people against US aggression and for national salvation, thoroughly defeat the US aggressors and their lackeys, and defend peace in the Asia and Pacific region and the world.
    The Asia-Pacific Peace Liaison Committee issued the above call in a telegram sent today to the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace, the South Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace, and the Permanent Bureau of the International Conference for Support of the Vietnamese People Against US Imperialist Aggression and Defense of Peace, on the occasion of the "March 19" Vietnam National Anti-US Struggle Day. The telegram strongly condemned US imperialism for frantically expanding the war of aggression against Vietnam and even barbarously using poison gas and chemical poisons. The telegram said that it is worth noting that the United States is stepping up the expansion of the war in Laos and carrying out armed provocations against Cambodia, deliberately expanding the flames of war to the entire Indochina, threatening the peace and security of vast areas of Asia.
    The Asia-Pacific Peace Liaison Committee expressed great indignation and protest in the telegram against the crimes committed by the US aggressors in Vietnam, and expressed its respect to the Vietnamese people who are bravely fighting against US imperialist aggression.

### 亚太和联就越南全国反美斗争日致电越南南北方和委会等  呼吁世界人民支援越南人民抗美救国斗争

1966-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

    亚太和联就越南全国反美斗争日致电越南南北方和委会等
    呼吁世界人民支援越南人民抗美救国斗争
    新华社十八日讯　亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会今天号召亚洲和太平洋区域人民并呼吁全世界人民进一步团结起来，努力支援越南人民抗美救国的正义斗争，彻底打败美国侵略者及其走狗，保卫亚洲和太平洋区域及全世界的和平。
    亚太和联是在今天为“三·一九”越南全国反美斗争日致越南保卫世界和平委员会、越南南方保卫世界和平委员会和声援越南人民反对美帝国主义侵略、保卫和平国际会议常设局的电报中发出上述号召的。电报中强烈谴责美帝国主义正在疯狂地扩大侵越战争，甚至野蛮地使用毒气和化学毒药。电报说，值得人们警惕的是，美国正加紧扩大老挝的战争和对柬埔寨进行武装挑衅，蓄意把战火扩大到整个印度支那，威胁着整个亚洲广大地区的和平与安全。
    亚太和联在电报中对美国侵略者在越南所犯下的罪行表示极大的愤慨和抗议，并且向英勇抗击美帝国主义侵略的越南人民表示敬意。
