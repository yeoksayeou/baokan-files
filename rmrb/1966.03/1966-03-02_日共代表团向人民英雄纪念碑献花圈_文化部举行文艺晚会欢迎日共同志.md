Title: Japanese Communist Party Delegation Lays Wreath at Monument to the People's Heroes; Ministry of Culture Holds Literary and Art Soiree to Welcome Japanese Comrades
Author:
Date: 1966-03-02
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 1st. The Japanese Communist Party delegation, headed by Comrade Kenji Miyamoto, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, laid a wreath at the Monument to the People's Heroes in Tiananmen Square this afternoon. The inscription on the wreath read: "Dedicated to the Revolutionary People's Heroes of China."

    In front of the Monument to the People's Heroes, Comrade Kenji Miyamoto and all members of the delegation, accompanied by Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Wan Li, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, also visited the reliefs around the monument, which reflect the history of the Chinese people's revolutionary struggle over the past century.

    Xinhua News Agency, March 1st. The Ministry of Culture held a literary and art soiree tonight to welcome the Japanese Communist Party delegation.

    Masayoshi Oka, Deputy Head of the Japanese Communist Party delegation, Standing Committee Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the JCP, and Secretary of the Central Secretariat, along with delegation members Tadao Kurahara, Akira Yonehara, etc., accompanied by Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Kang Sheng, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat, as well as Comrades Liu Ningyi, Zhao Yimin, etc., attended the soiree and watched the modern ballet "The Red Detachment of Women."

### 日共代表团向人民英雄纪念碑献花圈  文化部举行文艺晚会欢迎日共同志

1966-03-02
第1版()
专栏：

    日共代表团向人民英雄纪念碑献花圈
    文化部举行文艺晚会欢迎日共同志
    新华社一日讯  以日本共产党中央委员会总书记宫本显治同志为首的日本共产党代表团，今天下午到天安门广场，向人民英雄纪念碑献了花圈。花圈上写着：“献给中国革命的人民英雄”。
    在人民英雄纪念碑前，宫本显治同志和代表团全体同志，还由中共中央委员刘宁一、中共北京市委书记处书记万里陪同，参观了纪念碑四周的反映中国人民一百多年来革命斗争历史的浮雕。
    新华社一日讯文化部今天举行文艺晚会，欢迎日本共产党代表团。
    日本共产党代表团副团长、日共中央政治局常务委员、中央书记处书记冈正芳，代表团团员藏原惟人、米原昶等，由中共中央总书记邓小平，政治局候补委员、书记处书记康生，以及刘宁一、赵毅敏等同志陪同出席晚会，观看了现代芭蕾舞剧《红色娘子军》。
