Title: Protesting US Imperialist Aggression Against Vietnam and the Dominican Republic, Students Demonstrate in the Argentine Capital
Author:
Date: 1966-03-24
Edition: 4

    Protesting US Imperialist Aggression Against Vietnam and the Dominican Republic
    Students Demonstrate in the Argentine Capital
    Xinhua News Agency, 20th dispatch: Buenos Aires news: Students in the Argentine capital held a demonstration in the city center on the evening of the 18th to protest against the American aggression against Vietnam and the Dominican Republic.
    According to Western news agencies, hundreds of students participated in the demonstration. During the demonstration, they distributed leaflets demanding that "American murderers get out of Vietnam and Santo Domingo."
    When the marching crowd passed the venue where the "Alliance for Progress" was holding a meeting, the students set off firecrackers to protest against the American representatives attending the meeting.

### 抗议美帝侵略越南多米尼加  阿根廷首都学生示威

1966-03-24
第4版()
专栏：

    抗议美帝侵略越南多米尼加
    阿根廷首都学生示威
    新华社二十日讯　布宜诺斯艾利斯消息：阿根廷首都学生十八日晚在市中心举行示威，抗议美国侵略越南和多米尼加。
    据西方通讯社报道，数百名学生参加了这次示威。他们在示威途中散发传单，要求“美国杀人犯滚出越南和圣多明各”。
    游行队伍经过“争取进步联盟”正在举行会议的场所时，学生鸣放爆竹对参加会议的美国代表表示抗议。
