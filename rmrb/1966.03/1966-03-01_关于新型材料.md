    On New Materials
    Er Wu
    In recent years, many new varieties have been developed in alloy materials, synthetic materials, inorganic non-metallic materials, and composite materials. These new types of new materials have been promoted and applied in national defense cutting-edge technology and in the design revolution of electromechanical products.
    To develop new alloy materials, the most important thing is to comprehensively study various symbiotic metal minerals, and implement comprehensive development and comprehensive utilization. For example, by utilizing symbiotic iron ore containing elements such as copper, vanadium, titanium, and rare earths, and utilizing resources such as manganese, silicon, molybdenum, and boron, a variety of new alloy steels can be created. For example, copper ore generally contains gold, selenium, thallium, etc., lead-zinc ore generally contains silver, cadmium, bismuth, etc., nickel ore generally contains copper, cobalt, platinum, etc., tin ore generally contains copper, lead, zinc, iron, etc., tungsten ore generally contains molybdenum, etc., copper ore generally contains rhenium, etc... By implementing comprehensive utilization, materials can be used to their full potential, and new varieties of new non-ferrous metal materials can be developed.
    To develop new chemical synthetic materials, the most important thing is to develop the petrochemical industry, comprehensively utilize major raw materials such as petroleum, natural gas, and coal, and process them into complex polymer polymers through in-depth chemical processing, and process them into synthetic rubber, synthetic fiber or plastic products. For example, there are now various types of synthetic rubber, including styrene-butadiene rubber, chloroprene rubber, nitrile rubber, butadiene rubber, butyl rubber, polyisobutylene rubber, polyisoprene rubber, ethylene-propylene rubber, silicone rubber, fluororubber, etc. Each has different properties and strengths, and can be used according to their respective characteristics.
    Composite materials, such as copper-clad steel plates and polyvinyl chloride plastic composite sheets, not only save steel but also improve performance and have a wide range of uses. Fiberglass in new materials is a glass fiber product treated with unsaturated resin. It can replace steel and has many superior properties, which has great development prospects. This is because comprehensive processing products already have comprehensive properties.
    Comprehensive utilization of resources and development of new materials are of great significance in developing social productivity.

### 关于新型材料
二吴
1966-03-01
第5版()
专栏：

    关于新型材料
    二吴
    近年来，在合金材料、合成材料、无机非金属材料、复合材料等方面，发展了许多新的品种。这些新品种的新型材料，已经在国防尖端技术方面、在机电产品设计革命方面得到推广应用。
    要发展新型的合金材料，最要紧的是综合研究各种共生金属矿物，实行综合开发，综合利用。例如，利用含有铜、钒、钛、稀土等元素的共生铁矿，利用锰、硅、钼、硼等的资源，就能创造出多种新型合金钢材。又如，铜矿一般含有金、硒、铊等，铅锌矿一般含有银、镉、铋等，镍矿一般含有铜、钴、铂等，锡矿一般含铜、铅、锌、铁等，钨矿一般含钼等，铜矿一般含铼等……，实行综合利用，才能物尽其用，并发展新品种的新型有色金属材料。
    要发展新型的化学合成材料，最要紧的是发展石油化学工业，综合利用石油、天然气、煤炭等主要原料，经过深度化学加工，制成复杂的高分子聚合物，加工为合成橡胶、合成纤维或塑料产品。例如，现在合成橡胶的品种，已有丁苯橡胶、氯丁橡胶、丁腈橡胶、顺丁二烯橡胶、丁基橡胶、聚异丁烯橡胶、聚异戊二烯橡胶、乙丙橡胶、硅橡胶、氟橡胶等，性能各有不同，各有所长，可以因材使用。
    复合材料，如复铜钢板、如聚氯乙烯塑料复合薄板，既节约了钢材，又改善了性能，用途广。新型材料中的玻璃钢，是用不饱和树脂处理的玻璃纤维制品，既可代替钢材，又有许多优越的性能，很有发展前途。这是因为综合性的加工产品，已具有综合性的性能。
    综合利用资源，发展新型的材料，在发展社会生产力方面来说，是有重大意义的。
