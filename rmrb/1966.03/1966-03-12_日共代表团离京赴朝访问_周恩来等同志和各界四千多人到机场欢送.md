Title: Japanese Communist Party Delegation Departs Beijing for Visit to North Korea; Zhou Enlai and Other Comrades and Over Four Thousand People from All Walks of Life See Them Off at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1966-03-12
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 11th. Miyamoto Kenji, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, and the Japanese Communist Party delegation he led, left Beijing by special plane this afternoon for a visit to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.
    Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and more than four thousand people from all walks of life in the capital, went to the airport to see them off.
    The masses from all walks of life who came to see off the Japanese Communist Party delegation beat drums and gongs at the airport, waved colored flags and bouquets, and shouted slogans such as "Long live the militant friendship between the Chinese Communist Party and the Japanese Communist Party," "Long live invincible Marxism-Leninism."
    Those who went to the airport to see them off also included Peng Zhen and Li Xiannian, members of the Political Bureau and secretaries of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, Kang Sheng, alternate member of the Political Bureau and secretary of the Secretariat, Liao Chengzhi and Liu Ningyi, members of the Central Committee, Zhao Yimin, alternate member of the Central Committee, and Zhao Fan, secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPC.
    Pak Se-chang, the Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, and some Japanese comrades in Beijing also went to the airport to see them off.

### 日共代表团离京赴朝访问  周恩来等同志和各界四千多人到机场欢送

1966-03-12
第1版()
专栏：

    日共代表团离京赴朝访问
    周恩来等同志和各界四千多人到机场欢送
    新华社十一日讯　日本共产党中央委员会总书记宫本显治和由他率领的日本共产党代表团，今天下午乘专机离开北京，前往朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访问。
    中共中央副主席周恩来，以及首都各界四千多人，前往机场欢送。
    欢送日本共产党代表团的各界群众，在机场上打起锣鼓，挥舞彩旗和花束，高呼
  “中国共产党和日本共产党的战斗友谊万岁”、“战无不胜的马克思列宁主义万岁”等口号。
    到机场欢送的，还有中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记彭真、李先念，政治局候补委员、书记处书记康生，中央委员廖承志、刘宁一，中央候补委员赵毅敏，中共北京市委书记处书记赵凡等同志。
    朝鲜驻中国大使朴世昌，在北京的一部分日本同志，也到机场欢送。
