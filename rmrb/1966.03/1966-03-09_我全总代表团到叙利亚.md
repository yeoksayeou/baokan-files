Title: ACFTU Delegation Arrives in Syria
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-09
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 6th. At the invitation of the General Federation of Syrian Workers' Trade Unions, the delegation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions (ACFTU) led by Wang Jiacong arrived here on the evening of the 5th.
    The delegation was warmly welcomed at the airport by Khalid Jundi, Chairman of the General Federation of Syrian Workers' Trade Unions, and other leaders.
    Li Huanong, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy, also went to the airport to greet the delegation.

### 我全总代表团到叙利亚

1966-03-09
第5版()
专栏：

    我全总代表团到叙利亚
    新华社大马士革六日电  应叙利亚工人工会总联合会的邀请，由王家宠率领的中华全国总工会代表团五日晚到达这里。
    代表团在机场受到叙利亚工人工会总联合会的主席哈利德·琼迪和其他领导人的热烈欢迎。
    中国大使馆临时代办李华农也到机场迎接。
