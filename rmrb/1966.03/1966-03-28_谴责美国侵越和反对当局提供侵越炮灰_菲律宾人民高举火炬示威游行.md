Title: Philippine People Hold Torchlight Demonstrations, Condemning US Aggression Against Vietnam and Opposing the Authorities' Provision of Cannon Fodder for the Invasion

Author:
Date: 1966-03-28
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Manila, March 25th: Manila news - Filipino workers and students again took to the streets of Manila on the evening of the 25th, holding aloft torches and placards in a demonstration condemning US aggression against Vietnam and opposing the dispatch of Filipino troops to participate in the US war of aggression against Vietnam.

    This demonstration was part of the activities organized by Filipino mass organizations in response to the "International Day of Protest" against US aggression against Vietnam.

    The demonstrating masses held massive protest rallies in front of the US Embassy in the Philippines and the Capitol Building. They shouted: "Yankees, go home!" "Don't send Filipinos to Vietnam!"

    Speakers at the protest rallies angrily condemned the US imperialists' conspiracy to drag the Philippines into the war of aggression against Vietnam. They also condemned the US Central Intelligence Agency for engaging in conspiratorial activities in the Philippines. The demonstrating masses distributed pamphlets denouncing the crimes of US imperialism in its aggression against the Philippines.

### 谴责美国侵越和反对当局提供侵越炮灰  菲律宾人民高举火炬示威游行

1966-03-28
第5版()
专栏：

    谴责美国侵越和反对当局提供侵越炮灰
    菲律宾人民高举火炬示威游行
    新华社二十五日讯  马尼拉消息：菲律宾工人和学生群众二十五日晚上又在马尼拉的大街上高举火炬和标语牌进行游行示威，谴责美国侵略越南和反对派遣菲律宾军队参加美国侵越战争。
    这次示威是菲律宾群众组织为响应举行反对美国侵略越南的“国际抗议日”而组织的活动的一部分。
    示威群众在美国驻菲律宾大使馆和国会大厦前举行了声势浩大的抗议集会。他们高呼：“美国佬，滚回去！”“不要派菲律宾人到越南！”
    在抗议集会上讲话的人都愤怒谴责美帝国主义竭力要把菲律宾拖进侵越战争的阴谋。他们还谴责美国中央情报局在菲律宾从事阴谋活动。示威群众散发了控诉美帝国主义侵略菲律宾罪行的小册子。
