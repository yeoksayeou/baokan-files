Title: Chairman Liu Continues Talks with President Ayub Khan; Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi Holds Talks with Foreign Minister Bhutto
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-29
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi, 27th—Chairman of the People’s Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, continued his talks with President of Pakistan, Ayub Khan, at the Presidential Palace on the evening of the 27th.
    Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi, 27th—Chinese Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi held talks here with Pakistani Foreign Minister Bhutto on the morning of the 27th.
    The Chinese side participating in the talks included Vice Director of the Commission for Economic Relations with Foreign Countries Wang Daohan, as well as other personnel. The Pakistani side participating in the talks included: Minister of Commerce Ghulam Faruque, Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Aziz Ahmed, Pakistani Ambassador to China Rochan, as well as other personnel.
    The talks were conducted in a cordial and friendly atmosphere.
    That afternoon, Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi and Foreign Minister Bhutto held separate talks.

### 刘主席同阿尤布·汗总统继续会谈  陈毅副总理兼外长同布托外长会谈

1966-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席同阿尤布·汗总统继续会谈
    陈毅副总理兼外长同布托外长会谈
    新华社拉瓦尔品第二十七日电  中华人民共和国主席刘少奇同巴基斯坦总统阿尤布·汗二十七日晚上在总统府继续进行了会谈。
    新华社拉瓦尔品第二十七日电  中国副总理兼外交部长陈毅二十七日上午在这里和巴基斯坦外交部长布托举行了会谈。
    中国方面参加会谈的有对外经济联络委员会副主任汪道涵以及其他人员。巴基斯坦方面参加会谈的有：商业部长吴拉姆·法鲁克，外交部秘书阿齐兹·艾哈迈德，巴基斯坦驻中国大使罗查以及其他人员。
    会谈是在亲切友好的气氛中进行的。
    当天下午，陈毅副总理兼外交部长和布托外交部长单独举行了会谈。
