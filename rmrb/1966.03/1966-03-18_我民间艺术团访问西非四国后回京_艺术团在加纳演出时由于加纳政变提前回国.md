Title: Our Folk Art Troupe Returns to Beijing After Visiting Four West African Countries; Art Troupe Returns Home Early from Ghana Due to Ghanaian Coup

Author:

Date: 1966-03-18
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 16th – The Chinese Folk Art Troupe, led by Cheng De, returned to Beijing this afternoon after visiting and performing in Mali, Guinea, Mauritania, and Ghana.
    The art troupe returned home early from Ghana due to the coup d'état in Ghana.
    Greeting them at the station today were Yang Chun, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Li Qi, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture; and Yu Ji, Ding Bo, and other responsible persons from relevant departments, as well as more than two hundred literary and art workers from the capital. Diplomatic officials from the Malian and Guinean embassies in China also greeted them at the station. The Chinese Folk Art Troupe is the first Chinese song and dance troupe to visit West Africa. The art troupe performed forty-eight shows in the four West African countries, with a total audience of more than 70,000 people.

### 我民间艺术团访问西非四国后回京  艺术团在加纳演出时由于加纳政变提前回国

1966-03-18
第5版()
专栏：

    我民间艺术团访问西非四国后回京
    艺术团在加纳演出时由于加纳政变提前回国
    新华社十六日讯　由于承德率领的中国民间艺术团，先后在马里、几内亚、毛里塔尼亚和加纳访问演出后，今天下午回到北京。
    艺术团是在加纳演出时，由于加纳政变而提前回国的。
    今天到车站迎接的，有对外文委副主任杨纯、文化部副部长李琦、以及虞棘、丁波等有关方面负责人和首都文艺工作者二百多人。马里和几内亚驻中国大使馆的外交官员，也到车站迎接。中国民间艺术团是我国首次到西非访问的歌舞团。艺术团在西非四国演出四十八场，共有七万多人观看了演出。
