Title: Comrade Ai Siqi, Deputy Principal of the Central Party School of the Communist Party of China and Deputy to the National People's Congress, Passes Away

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-23
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, March 22nd: Comrade Ai Siqi, Deputy Principal of the Central Party School of the Communist Party of China, Deputy to the National People's Congress, and Academician of the Department of Philosophy and Social Sciences of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, passed away due to illness in Beijing at 9:15 PM on March 22, 1966, at the age of 56.
    Comrade Ai Siqi joined the Communist Party of China in 1935. From 1935 to 1937, he served as an editor for the Shanghai "Reading Life" magazine. From September 1937 to 1947, he successively held positions as a chief instructor at the Anti-Japanese Military and Political University in Yan'an, Director of the Cultural Research Office of the Central Research Institute of the Communist Party of China, Secretary-General of the Central Cultural Committee, and Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the Yan'an Liberation Daily. After the national liberation, he successively served as Director of the Philosophy Teaching and Research Office and Deputy Principal of the Central Party School of the Communist Party of China. He was elected as a delegate to the National People's Congress in every session from 1954 onwards.
    Comrade Ai Siqi was a loyal fighter on the party's theoretical front. From his youth, he worked diligently throughout his life, actively participating in the propaganda and education of Marxist-Leninist philosophy under the leadership of the Party. He tirelessly studied Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought and waged a resolute struggle against bourgeois academic thought and modern revisionism. (Attached picture)

### 中共中央高级党校副校长、全国人大代表  艾思奇同志逝世

1966-03-23
第2版()
专栏：

    中共中央高级党校副校长、全国人大代表
    艾思奇同志逝世
    新华社二十二日讯　中共中央高级党校副校长，全国人民代表大会代表，中国科学院哲学社会科学部学部委员艾思奇同志，因病于一九六六年三月二十二日下午九时十五分在北京逝世，年五十六岁。
    艾思奇同志于一九三五年参加中国共产党。在一九三五至一九三七年间，担任上海《读书生活》杂志编辑。从一九三七年九月到一九四七年，历任延安抗日军政大学主任教员、中共中央研究院文化研究室主任、中央文委秘书长、延安解放日报副总编辑。全国解放后，历任中共中央高级党校哲学教研室主任、副校长。从一九五四年起被选为历届全国人民代表大会代表。
    艾思奇同志是党在理论战线上的忠诚战士。他从青年时期起，毕生勤勤恳恳，在党的领导下，积极参加马克思列宁主义哲学的宣传教育工作。他不懈地学习马克思列宁主义、毛泽东思想，同资产阶级学术思想、现代修正主义进行了坚决的斗争。（附图片）
