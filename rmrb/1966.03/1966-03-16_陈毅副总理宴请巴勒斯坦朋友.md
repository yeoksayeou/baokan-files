Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Hosts a Banquet for Palestinian Friends
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-16
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 15th – Vice Premier Chen Yi received Rashid Sa'id Jarbu, Deputy Director of the Palestine Liberation Organization's Beijing Office, and his wife this afternoon, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    After the meeting, Vice Premier Chen Yi hosted a banquet for Mr. and Mrs. Jarbu.
    Present at the reception and banquet were Qu Tangliang, Wang Chuanbin, Zhu Ziqi, Lin Zhaonan and others.

### 陈毅副总理宴请巴勒斯坦朋友

1966-03-16
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理宴请巴勒斯坦朋友
    新华社十五日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见巴勒斯坦解放组织驻北京办事处副主任拉希德·赛义德·加尔布和夫人，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见后，陈毅副总理设宴招待加尔布夫妇。
    接见和宴请时在座的有区棠亮、王传斌、朱子奇、林兆南等。
