Title: French Edition of Volume I of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" Published
Author:
Date: 1966-03-01
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 28th -- The French edition of Volume I of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" has recently been published by Foreign Languages Press and will be distributed domestically by Foreign Languages Bookstore and internationally by China International Book Trading Corporation starting from March 1st.

    The French edition of Volume I of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" is translated from the Chinese edition published by People's Publishing House in July 1952. Like the Chinese edition, this volume contains seventeen works by Comrade Mao Zedong from the First and Second Revolutionary Civil Wars.

    The French edition of Volume IV of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" has already been translated and published.

### 《毛泽东选集》第一卷法文本出版

1966-03-01
第1版()
专栏：

    《毛泽东选集》第一卷法文本出版
    新华社二十八日讯　《毛泽东选集》第一卷法文本已在最近由外文图书出版社出版，并将从三月一日起，在国内由外文书店、在国外由中国国际书店分别发行。
    《毛泽东选集》第一卷法文本，是根据人民出版社一九五二年七月出版的中文本翻译的。这一卷的外文本同中文本一样，共收进了毛泽东同志在第一次和第二次国内革命战争时期的十七篇著作。
    《毛泽东选集》第四卷的法文本前已翻译出版。
