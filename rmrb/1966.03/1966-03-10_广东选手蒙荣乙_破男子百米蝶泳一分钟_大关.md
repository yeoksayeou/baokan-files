Title: Guangdong Athlete Meng Rongyi Breaks the One-Minute Barrier in Men's 100m Butterfly

Author:
Date: 1966-03-10
Edition: 2

    According to Xinhua News Agency, March 7th, Guangdong butterfly swimmer Meng Rongyi broke the one-minute barrier in the men's 100-meter butterfly for the first time today while participating in the swimming competition celebrating International Women's Day "March 8th" in Beijing, achieving a time of 59.8 seconds. This result is 0.2 seconds faster than his time in yesterday's competition in the same event.
    Meng Rongyi also clocked in at 2 minutes 19.6 seconds, breaking the national record of 2 minutes 20.1 seconds in the men's 200-meter butterfly.
    In today's women's 100-meter butterfly competition, Li Lifen, a 16-year-old athlete from Heilongjiang, finished first with a time of 1 minute 11.7 seconds, breaking the national record of 1 minute 11.8 seconds. At the same time, she also broke her own national youth women's 100-meter butterfly record of 1 minute 12.6 seconds.
    Yesterday, Guangdong athlete Gao Shenqing broke the national record in the women's 100-meter breaststroke. Guangdong newcomer Xu Jinzhu broke the national youth record in the men's 1,500-meter freestyle.

### 广东选手蒙荣乙  破男子百米蝶泳一分钟“大关”

1966-03-10
第2版()
专栏：体育

    广东选手蒙荣乙
    破男子百米蝶泳一分钟“大关”
    据新华社七日讯  广东蝶泳选手蒙荣乙今天在北京参加庆祝“三八”国际妇女节游泳比赛时，第一次突破了男子百米蝶泳一分钟的“大关”，成绩达到了五十九秒八。这个成绩比他昨天参加这个项目比赛时的成绩又提高了零点二秒。
    蒙荣乙今天还以二分十九秒六的成绩，刷新了男子二百米蝶泳二分二十秒一的全国纪录。
    在今天的女子一百米蝶泳比赛中，黑龙江十六岁的选手李丽芬用一分十一秒七的时间首先游完全程，刷新了一分十一秒八的全国纪录。同时刷新了李丽芬自己保持的一分十二秒六的少年女子百米蝶泳全国纪录。
    昨天，广东选手高慎卿刷新了女子百米蛙泳的全国纪录。广东新手徐锦柱在男子一千五百米自由泳中，刷新了少年全国纪录。
