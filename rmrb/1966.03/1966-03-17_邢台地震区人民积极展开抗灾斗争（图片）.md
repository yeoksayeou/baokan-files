Title: People in the Xingtai Earthquake Area Actively Engage in Disaster Relief Efforts (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1966-03-17
Edition: 2

    The masses in the disaster area are both arranging their lives, rebuilding their homes, and, without missing the farming season, throwing themselves into production.
