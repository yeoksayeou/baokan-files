    State Council Appointment and Dismissal List
    Xinhua News Agency, March 15th – The 161st plenary meeting of the State Council held on March 9, 1966, passed the appointment and dismissal of a batch of personnel from state organs and universities. The list is as follows:
    Appointment:
    Wu Hao as Vice Minister of the Fifth Ministry of Machine Building;
    Zou Tong as Vice Minister of the Ministry of Coal Industry;
    Shi Huaibi, Zhou Yiping, Ren Yinglun, and Yang Chun as Deputy Directors of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries;
    Liu Dai as Counselor of the State Council;
    Shi Xiangsheng and Wang Mingyuan as Vice Presidents of Beijing Agricultural University;
    Feng Peizhi as President of Beijing Normal College;
    Wu Jieping as President of Beijing Second Medical College;
    Tian Renping as Vice President of East China Institute of Textile Science and Technology;
    Zhang Fengfu as Vice President of Chongqing Institute of Technology;
    Wang Zhaoji as Vice President of Shenyang Institute of Technology;
    Wang Weibai as Vice President of Wuhan Iron and Steel Institute;
    Wang Jingfan as Vice President of Chongqing Posts and Telecommunications Institute.
    Removal of Liu Yongru from the position of Vice President of Beijing Normal University and Lin Qishen from the position of Vice President of Nanjing Institute of Posts and Telecommunications.

### 国务院任免名单

1966-03-16
第2版()
专栏：

    国务院任免名单
    新华社十五日讯　一九六六年三月九日举行的国务院全体会议第一百六十一次会议，通过任免一批国家机关和高等学校工作人员，名单如下：
    任命：
    吴皓为第五机械工业部副部长；
    邹桐为煤炭工业部副部长；
    史怀璧、周一萍、任映仑、杨纯为对外文化联络委员会副主任；
    刘岱为国务院参事；
    史向生、王明远为北京农业大学副校长；
    冯佩之为北京师范学院院长；
    吴阶平为北京第二医学院院长；
    田任平为华东纺织工学院副院长；
    张丰富为重庆工业学院副院长；
    王兆麒为沈阳工业学院副院长；
    王蔚柏为武汉钢铁学院副院长；
    王敬璠为重庆邮电学院副院长。
    免去刘墉如的北京师范大学副校长职务，林启深的南京邮电学院副院长职务。
