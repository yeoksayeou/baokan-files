    See Who the Soviet Leaders are Standing With?
    The Soviet Union and the United States Once Again Voted in Favor of Extending the Mandate of the UN Forces in Cyprus
    Soviet Representatives Also Held a Meeting in Bangkok with Representatives of the Chiang Gang, South Vietnam, and the South Korean Puppet Clique
    Xinhua News Agency, 17th Dispatch from New York: The United Nations Security Council passed a resolution on the 16th, deciding to extend the mandate of the “United Nations Peacekeeping Force” stationed in Cyprus for another three months, until June 25, 1966. The representatives of the Soviet Union and the United States once again voted together in favor.
    During the debate, the U.S. representative, James Roosevelt, welcomed the extension of the mandate of this force, emphasizing that the United States contributed the most money to this interventionist force.
    In the discussion, the Soviet representative, Fedorin, on the one hand, pretended, saying that the Cyprus issue must be resolved by the Cypriot people themselves in the absence of external interference, respecting the principles of the country’s independence, sovereignty, and territorial integrity; on the other hand, he voted in favor of extending the mandate of this interventionist force.
    Since this UN force was stationed in Cyprus on March 27, 1964, to interfere in the country's internal affairs, this is the seventh time its mandate has been extended.
    Xinhua News Agency, 17th Dispatch from Bangkok: The so-called Asian Children and Youth Work Conference, organized by the United Nations Children’s Fund and other institutions, was held in Bangkok from the 8th to the 15th. Soviet representatives again sat together with representatives of the Taiwan Chiang Kai-shek clique, South Vietnam, South Korea, "Malaysia," and other puppet groups to participate in this conference.
    Representatives from the United States, Britain, Japan, India, Australia, and other countries also participated in this conference. The Soviet Union sent a delegation led by Alipov, Deputy Minister of Health of Uzbekistan, to participate in the conference.
    The conference was jointly organized by the United Nations Children's Fund, the Economic Commission for Asia and the Far East, and the Asian Institute for Economic Development and Planning.
    From October to December of last year, the Soviet government sent delegations four times to participate in meetings held by UN agencies in the Southeast Asian region together with puppet representatives from South Vietnam, South Korea, etc., three of which were attended by representatives of the Chiang Kai-shek clique.

第5版()
专栏：

    请看苏联领导人跟谁站在一起？
    苏美再一次投票赞成延长联合国部队在塞浦路斯的驻扎期限
    苏联代表又同蒋帮南越南朝鲜傀儡集团代表在曼谷举行会议
    新华社十七日讯　纽约消息：联合国安全理事会十六日通过一项决议，决定把驻在塞浦路斯的“联合国维持和平部队”的驻扎期限再延长三个月，到一九六六年六月二十五日。苏联和美国的代表又一次一起投赞成票。
    在辩论中，美国代表詹姆斯·罗斯福表示欢迎延长这支部队的驻扎期限，并强调美国对这支干涉部队出钱最多。
    苏联代表费德林在讨论中一方面装模作样，说什么塞浦路斯问题必须在尊重这个国家的独立、主权和领土完整的原则下，在没有外力干涉的情况下由塞浦路斯人民自己解决；另一方面投票赞成延长这支干涉部队的驻扎期限。
    自从联合国这支部队在一九六四年三月二十七日进驻塞浦路斯干涉这个国家的内部事务以来，这已经是第七次延长它的驻扎期限。
    据新华社十七日讯　曼谷消息：由联合国儿童基金会等机构主办的所谓亚洲儿童和青年工作会议八日到十五日在曼谷举行。苏联代表又同台湾蒋介石集团、南越、南朝鲜、“马来西亚”等傀儡集团的代表坐在一起，参加了这个会议。
    参加这个会议的，还有美国、英国、日本、印度、澳大利亚等国家的代表。苏联派出由乌兹别克卫生部副部长阿里波夫率领的代表团参加会议。
    会议是由联合国儿童基金会、亚洲远东经济委员会、亚洲经济发展和计划机构联合主办的。
    苏联政府从去年十月到十二月曾经四次派遣代表团同南越、南朝鲜等傀儡代表一起，参加联合国机构在东南亚地区举行的会议，其中三次有蒋介石集团的代表参加。
