Title: Zhang Bin (photo)
Author: Zhang Bin
Date: 1966-03-19
Edition: 2

    Zhang Fengzhu, Second Secretary of the Commercial Counselor's Office of the Chinese Embassy in Indonesia, Li Ruifeng, reporter of the Jakarta branch of Xinhua News Agency, and Shi Chaoyuan, Guo Zhixiu, Qiu Guowu, Wang Qisheng and other staff members of these units, who were injured by thugs organized by the right-wing forces in Indonesia, returned to Beijing by plane on the evening of March 18. Comrade Zhang Fengzhu and others are photographed with the heads of relevant departments who went to the airport to welcome them. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Bin.
