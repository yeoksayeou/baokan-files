Edition: 5

    Our Railway Delegation Arrives in Hanoi
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 9th. The Chinese railway delegation attending the 9th meeting of the Sino-Vietnamese Border Railway Joint Committee, led by Zhang Dewen, Director of the Kunming Railway Bureau, arrived in Hanoi by train on the 8th. Zhang Guangxi, Deputy Director of the Vietnam General Railway Administration, Wang Baoxuan, Commercial Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Vietnam, and others greeted the delegation at the station.

### 我铁路代表团到达河内

1966-03-10
第5版()
专栏：

    我铁路代表团到达河内
    新华社河内九日电  出席中越国境铁路联合委员会第九次会议的中国铁路代表团，由昆明铁路局局长张德温率领，八日乘火车到达河内。越南铁路总局副局长张光熙，中国驻越南大使馆商务参赞王保宣等到车站迎接。
