Title: My Economic and Trade Exhibition in Karachi Opens with Warm Welcome; 30,000 Visitors on the First Day Praise China's Self-Reliance in Creating Great Miracles
Author:
Date: 1966-03-19
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 16th—The People's Republic of China Economic and Trade Exhibition grandly opened on the 15th in Karachi, Pakistan's largest city.

    The exhibition was held at the invitation of the Pakistani government.

    Pakistan's Minister of Commerce, Ghulam Faruque, attended the opening ceremony and cut the ribbon for the exhibition. In his speech at the opening ceremony, Ghulam Faruque pointed out that the exhibition reflects the brilliant achievements China has made in its economic development. "These exhibits will be of great educational value to us and will strengthen our economic ties." Faruque said that the relationship between Pakistan and China continues to grow, and the holding of this exhibition is a major step forward in consolidating Sino-Pakistani friendship.

    Xiao Fangzhou, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and head of the Chinese delegation attending the exhibition, delivered a welcome speech. He expressed hope that the exhibition would make a positive contribution to enhancing mutual understanding and friendship between the Chinese and Pakistani peoples, and to promoting friendly cooperation and economic and trade relations between the two countries.

    After Ghulam Faruque cut the ribbon, accompanied by Xiao Fangzhou, Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan Ding Guoyu, and other guests, he entered the exhibition hall to view the exhibits. The various exhibits in the exhibition hall reflect the great achievements of China's socialist construction in recent years, attracting great interest from the guests and receiving high praise. One guest said that the exhibition demonstrates the victory of the self-reliance policy implemented by People's China in national construction. A guest named Faridi wrote in the guestbook, "China is moving forward—the progress made in such a short period is astonishing."

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, March 16th—Approximately 30,000 people visited the People's Republic of China Economic and Trade Exhibition being held here on March 16th. This was the first day the exhibition was open to the public.

    A steady stream of people poured into the exhibition from all directions.

    Visitors warmly praised the great achievements of China's socialist construction reflected in the exhibition.

    One visitor said, "I am proud of China's great achievements." He kissed a displayed tractor and said that China's great achievements inspire those who are fighting for a bright future for the people.

    A professor wrote in the guestbook: "Self-reliance has created great miracles. Truly remarkable!"

    A visitor named Masun wrote: "The people of Asia are doing well, we are proud of you."

    One visitor wrote: "Long live Chairman Mao Zedong and Chairman Liu Shaoqi! Congratulations to People's China."

### 我经济贸易展览会在卡拉奇开幕受到热烈欢迎  第一天三万人参观，赞扬中国自力更生创造了巨大奇迹

1966-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

    我经济贸易展览会在卡拉奇开幕受到热烈欢迎
    第一天三万人参观，赞扬中国自力更生创造了巨大奇迹
    据新华社卡拉奇十六日电  中华人民共和国经济和贸易展览会十五日在巴基斯坦最大的城市——卡拉奇隆重开幕。
    展览会是应巴基斯坦政府的邀请而举行的。
    巴基斯坦商业部长吴拉姆·法鲁克出席了开幕式，并为展览会剪彩。吴拉姆·法鲁克在开幕式上讲话指出，展览会反映了中国在经济发展过程中所取得的辉煌成就。“这些展品将对我们具有很大的教育价值，并将加强我们之间的经济联系。”法鲁克说，巴基斯坦同中国的关系在继续增长，这次展览会的举行在巩固中国—巴基斯坦友好关系方面迈进了一大步。
    中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席、前来参加展览会的中国代表团团长萧方洲致欢迎词，他表示希望展览会对增进中国人民和巴基斯坦人民之间的相互了解和友谊，对促进两国的友好合作和经济及贸易关系作出积极的贡献。
    吴拉姆·法鲁克剪彩之后，由萧方洲和中国驻巴基斯坦大使丁国钰以及其他来宾陪同，进入展览馆大厅参观。展览馆的各种展品反映了近年来中国社会主义建设的伟大成就，引起来宾的极大兴趣，并且获得了高度的评价。一位来宾说，展览会表明人民中国在国家建设中所执行的自力更生政策的胜利。一位来宾法里迪在留言簿上写道，“中国正在前进中——在这样短短的时期里取得的进步是惊人的。”
    据新华社卡拉奇十六日电三月十六日约有三万人参观了在这里举办的中华人民共和国经济和贸易展览会。这一天是展览会向公众开放的第一天。
    川流不息的人群从四面八方涌向展览会。
    参观者热烈赞扬展览会所反映的中国社会主义建设的伟大成就。
    一位参观者说：“我对中国的伟大成就感到骄傲。”他吻了一台展出的拖拉机说，中国的伟大成就鼓舞了为人民的光明前途而奋斗的人们。
    一位教授在留言簿上写道：“自力更生创造了巨大的奇迹。真了不起！”
    一位名叫马松的参观者写道：“亚洲的人民干得好，我们为你们感到骄傲。”
    有一位参观者写道：“毛泽东主席和刘少奇主席万岁！向人民中国祝贺。”
