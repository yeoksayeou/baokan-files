Title: Celebrating the 10th Anniversary of the Founding of the National People's Army of the German Democratic Republic, Military Attaché of the German Democratic Republic in China Holds Reception
Author:
Date: 1966-03-02
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 1st. Lieutenant Colonel Kautzsch, Military Attaché of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China, held a reception tonight to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the National People's Army of the German Democratic Republic. Xu Guangda, Vice Minister of National Defense, as well as responsible comrades from relevant departments, including Zeng Shan, Wang Xinting, Zhang Tianyun, Du Yide, Liu Zhen, Liu Shaowen, Jia Ruoyu, and Yu Zhan, were invited to attend the reception. Lieutenant Colonel Kautzsch and Wang Xinting, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, delivered toasts at the reception. Kurt, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, and diplomatic envoys and military attachés from various countries to China also attended the reception.

### 庆祝德国家人民军建军十周年  民主德国驻华武官举行招待会

1966-03-02
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝德国家人民军建军十周年
    民主德国驻华武官举行招待会
    新华社一日讯  德意志民主共和国驻中国大使馆武官考奇中校今晚举行招待会，庆祝德意志民主共和国国家人民军建军十周年。国防部副部长许光达，以及有关方面负责人曾山、王新亭、张天云、杜义德、刘震、刘少文、贾若瑜、余湛等应邀出席了招待会。考奇中校和中国人民解放军副总参谋长王新亭，在招待会上先后祝了酒。德意志民主共和国驻中国大使柯尔特，各国驻中国的使节和武官，也出席了招待会。
