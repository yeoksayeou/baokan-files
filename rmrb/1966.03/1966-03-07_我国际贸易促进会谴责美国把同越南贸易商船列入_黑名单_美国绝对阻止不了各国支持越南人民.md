Title: China Council for the Promotion of International Trade Condemns the U.S. for Blacklisting Merchant Ships Trading with Vietnam; The U.S. Will Absolutely Not Be Able to Stop Countries from Supporting the Vietnamese People
Author:
Date: 1966-03-07
Edition: 4

    China Council for the Promotion of International Trade Condemns the U.S. for Blacklisting Merchant Ships Trading with Vietnam
    The U.S. Will Absolutely Not Be Able to Stop Countries from Supporting the Vietnamese People
    Chairman of the Vietnam Chamber of Commerce Points Out that the U.S. Action Exposes the U.S. Imperialist "Peace" Deception and the Face of War Criminals
    (Xinhua News Agency, March 6) A spokesperson for the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade (CCPIT) today commented on the U.S. imperialists' blacklisting of foreign merchant ships trading with the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The spokesperson pointed out that U.S. imperialism's attempt to use intimidation and threats to force countries around the world to stop trade with the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and to stop supporting the Vietnamese people's just struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation is absolutely impossible.
    The spokesperson for the CCPIT stated that the CCPIT fully supports the statement issued by the Vietnam Chamber of Commerce strongly condemning this illegal decision by U.S. imperialism, and firmly protests this new act of robbery by U.S. imperialism.
    He pointed out that this illegal decision by U.S. imperialism infringes upon the right to legitimate trade of countries owning ships and the traditional principle of freedom of the seas, and is a brutal interference in the sovereignty of these countries. This illegal decision is also an illegal economic blockade against the people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and is another serious step by U.S. imperialism in expanding its aggression against Vietnam. It seriously infringes upon and tramples on the independence and sovereignty of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and once again exposes the Johnson administration's conspiracy to expand the war under the smokescreen of sham peace talks. This illegal decision is also a serious infringement on the interests of the people of all countries that have friendly trade and shipping relations with the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
    The spokesperson for the CCPIT reiterated that the Chinese people stand with the fraternal Vietnamese people and all peace-loving people to jointly struggle against this criminal conspiracy of U.S. imperialism and to support the Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation.
    (Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi) According to a report by the Vietnam News Agency, Duong Van Dam, Chairman of the Vietnam Chamber of Commerce of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, strongly condemned the U.S. government for blacklisting five foreign ships trading with the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and prohibiting these ships from entering U.S. ports to load goods for shipment to other countries.
    Duong Van Dam said that this is part of the U.S.'s practice of threatening and pressuring Western and other countries to stop trading with the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. This action by the U.S. government infringes upon the sovereignty and independence of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, brutally violates the freedom of trade and navigation on the high seas, and violates the right of countries that have trade relations with each other to have transportation and exchange of goods.
    Duong Van Dam pointed out that this old trick of U.S. imperialism to boycott ships trading with the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, in the context of their expanding war against North Vietnam, is the first step in blockading and isolating the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
    This action by the United States can only prove its weakness and its helplessness in the face of the struggle of the Vietnamese people and the people of the world, including the international business and maritime communities.
    He said: The above-mentioned actions expose the "peace" fraud of U.S. imperialism and the true face of their war criminals to world opinion.
    Duong Van Dam called on the ship-owning countries to condemn this illegal measure of U.S. imperialism and demand the revocation of these measures in order to protect their sovereignty and independence and their trade interests.

### 我国际贸易促进会谴责美国把同越南贸易商船列入“黑名单”  美国绝对阻止不了各国支持越南人民

1966-03-07
第4版()
专栏：

    我国际贸易促进会谴责美国把同越南贸易商船列入“黑名单”
    美国绝对阻止不了各国支持越南人民
    越商会主席指出美国的行动暴露了美帝“和平”骗局和战犯面目
    新华社六日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会发言人今天在评论美帝国主义把同越南民主共和国进行贸易的外国商船列入“黑名单”一事时指出，美帝国主义企图用恐吓、威胁的手段，迫使世界各国停止对越南民主共和国的贸易往来、停止支持越南人民抗美救国的正义斗争，这是绝对办不到的。
    中国国际贸易促进委员会发言人表示，中国国际贸易促进委员会完全支持越南民主共和国商会发表的强烈谴责美帝国主义这一非法决定的声明，并坚决抗议美帝国主义这一新的强盗行径。
    他指出，美帝国主义这一非法决定，侵犯了拥有船舶国家合法贸易的权利和传统的海上自由的原则，是对这些国家主权的粗暴干涉。这一非法决定，也是对越南民主共和国人民进行的非法的经济封锁，是美帝国主义扩大侵略越南的又一个严重步骤，它严重侵犯和蹂躏了越南民主共和国的独立和主权，并且再一次暴露了约翰逊政府在虚假和谈烟幕下掩盖其扩大战争的阴谋。这一非法决定，也是对与越南民主共和国有友好贸易和航运往来关系的各国人民利益的严重损害。
    中国国际贸易促进委员会发言人重申，中国人民同兄弟的越南人民和一切爱好和平的人民站在一起，为反对美帝国主义的这一罪恶阴谋，为支持越南人民的抗美救国斗争而共同斗争。
    新华社河内电　据越南通讯社报道，越南民主共和国商会主席杨文谈强烈谴责美国政府把五艘同越南民主共和国进行贸易的外国船只列入黑名单，并且禁止这些船只进入美国港口装载运往其他国家的货物。
    杨文谈说，这是美国威胁和压迫西方和其他国家停止同越南民主共和国进行贸易的做法的一部分。美国政府的这个行动侵犯了越南民主共和国的主权和独立，粗暴地侵犯了贸易和公海航行自由，侵犯了彼此有贸易关系的国家间交通和货物交流的权利。
    杨文谈指出，美帝国主义抵制同越南民主共和国进行贸易的船只这种老手法，在他们对越南北方扩大战争的情况下，是封锁和孤立越南民主共和国的第一步。
    美国的这个行动只能证明它的虚弱和它在越南人民和包括国际商业和海运界在内的全世界人民的斗争面前的一筹莫展。
    他说：上述行动向世界舆论暴露了美帝国主义的“和平”骗局和他们的战争罪犯的真面目。
    杨文谈呼吁各个船主国为了他们的主权和独立以及贸易上的利益，谴责美帝国主义的这种非法措施，并要求撤销这些措施。
