Title: The CPC Central Committee's March 22, 1966, Reply to the CPSU Central Committee Published
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-25
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 24th. "The Reply of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, March 22, 1966," has been published by the People's Publishing House. This book will be distributed throughout the country starting from the 25th.

### 中共中央一九六六年三月二十二日给苏共中央的复信出版

1966-03-25
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央一九六六年三月二十二日给苏共中央的复信出版
    新华社二十四日讯《中国共产党中央委员会一九六六年三月二十二日给苏联共产党中央委员会的复信》，已由人民出版社出版。这本书，从二十五日起在全国各地发行。
