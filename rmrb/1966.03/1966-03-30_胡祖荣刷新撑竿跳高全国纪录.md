Title: Hu Zurong Breaks National Pole Vault Record
Author:
Date: 1966-03-30
Edition: 2

    Hu Zurong, the national record holder for the men's pole vault, created a new national record of 4.85 meters in a track and field competition held in Beijing on the 29th, according to a Xinhua News Agency report on the 29th.  His previous national record of 4.80 meters was set in June of last year.
    This is the second national record broken by Chinese track and field athletes so far this year.  Anhui athlete Dong He broke the national record for women's javelin throw in a track and field competition on the 28th.

### 胡祖荣刷新撑竿跳高全国纪录

1966-03-30
第2版()
专栏：

    胡祖荣刷新撑竿跳高全国纪录
    新华社二十九日讯 男子撑竿跳高全国纪录保持者胡祖荣二十九日在北京参加的一次田径比赛，创造了四米八五的全国新成绩。原来的全国纪录——四米八十，是他在去年六月间创造的。
    这是我国田径选手今年以来刷新的第二项全国纪录。安徽选手董荷在二十八日的一次田径比赛中，曾经打破女子标枪掷远的全国纪录。
