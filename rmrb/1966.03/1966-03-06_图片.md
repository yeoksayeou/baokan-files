Title: Photo
Author: Dù Xiūxián
Date: 1966-03-06
Edition: 2

    On March 5th, Premier Zhou Enlai met with representatives of advanced individuals from the national industrial and transportation front. This is a group photo taken during the meeting. Front row, from right: Liu Yuanfu, Wang Jinxi, Premier Zhou, Feng Yuping, Tian Fenglin, Chen Xinyu, Huang Rongchang. These six representatives of advanced individuals were invited to Beijing to give reports at the National Industrial and Transportation Work Conference and the National Industrial and Transportation Political Work Conference.
                                    Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian
