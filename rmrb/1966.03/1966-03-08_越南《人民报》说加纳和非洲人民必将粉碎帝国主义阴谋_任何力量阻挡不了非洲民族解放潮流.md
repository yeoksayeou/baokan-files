Title: Vietnamese *People's Daily* Says Ghana and African People Will Surely Crush Imperialist Conspiracy; No Force Can Stop the African National Liberation Tide
Author:
Date: 1966-03-08
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 6th. The Vietnamese *People's Daily* published an editorial on the 6th, pointing out that imperialists cannot stop Ghana and other African countries from continuing to advance to consolidate their national independence.

    The editorial pointed out that the military coup in Ghana on February 24th against President Nkrumah fully demonstrated the brutal policies of the imperialists to undermine the independence of African countries. The reactionary nature of this coup was exposed from the very beginning.

    The editorial said that the Ghanaian military coup, manipulated behind the scenes by U.S. and British imperialism, has aroused immense indignation in African progressive public opinion. African public opinion widely expressed support for Nkrumah and the Ghanaian people, emphasizing that the military coup of February 24th was an extremely serious action by imperialism against the independence and sovereignty of African countries, and a conspiracy to undermine the great unity of Africa against imperialism.

    The editorial pointed out that the Ghanaian military coup proves more than ever that imperialism will never automatically give up Africa. For African countries, the issue of uniting to struggle resolutely against imperialism, headed by the United States, is currently a matter of life and death.

    The editorial said that the Vietnamese people are extremely angry about the imperialists' orchestration of the Ghanaian coup. The Vietnamese people resolutely support the Ghanaian people and President Nkrumah in their new struggle to defeat all the conspiracies of imperialism and its lackeys.

    The editorial pointed out that the blatant subversive methods adopted by the imperialists, headed by the United States, only prove their panic about the surging tide of national liberation revolution in Africa. No force can stop this tide. The Vietnamese people, like the revolutionary people of Africa, firmly believe that Ghana and Africa will continue to advance, will surely crush all the destructive means of the imperialists, and win true independence and freedom.

### 越南《人民报》说加纳和非洲人民必将粉碎帝国主义阴谋  任何力量阻挡不了非洲民族解放潮流

1966-03-08
第3版()
专栏：

    越南《人民报》说加纳和非洲人民必将粉碎帝国主义阴谋
    任何力量阻挡不了非洲民族解放潮流
    新华社河内六日电　越南《人民报》六日发表社论指出，帝国主义者阻挡不了加纳和其他非洲国家为巩固民族独立而继续前进的步伐。
    社论指出，加纳二月二十四日发生的反对恩克鲁玛总统的军事政变，充分证明了帝国主义者破坏非洲国家独立的粗暴政策。这个政变的反动性质从一开始就暴露无遗。
    社论说，由美、英帝国主义在幕后操纵的加纳军事政变，激起了非洲进步舆论的无比愤慨。非洲的舆论广泛表示支持恩克鲁玛和加纳人民，强调指出二月二十四日的军事政变是帝国主义反对非洲国家的独立和主权的一个极其严重的行动，是破坏非洲反对帝国主义的大团结的一种阴谋。
    社论指出，加纳的军事政变比任何时候都更加证明：帝国主义是永远不会自动放弃非洲的。对非洲国家来说，为坚决反对以美国为首的帝国主义而团结斗争的问题是当前关系生死存亡的问题。
    社论说，越南人民对帝国主义者制造加纳政变一事感到异常愤怒。越南人民坚决支持加纳人民和恩克鲁玛总统为击败帝国主义及其走狗的一切阴谋而进行的新的斗争。
    社论指出，以美国为首的帝国主义者采取的明目张胆的颠覆办法，只能证明它们对非洲汹涌澎湃的民族解放革命潮流的惊惶不安。任何力量也不能阻挡这个潮流。越南人民同非洲革命人民一样坚信，加纳和非洲必将继续前进，必将粉碎帝国主义者的一切破坏手段，赢得真正的独立和自由。
