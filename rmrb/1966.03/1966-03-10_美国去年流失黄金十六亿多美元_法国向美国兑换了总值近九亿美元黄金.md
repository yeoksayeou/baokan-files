Title: The United States Lost More Than 1.6 Billion US Dollars in Gold Last Year; France Exchanged Gold Worth Nearly 900 Million US Dollars from the United States

Author:

Date: 1966-03-10
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 9th: According to a statement released by the U.S. Treasury Department on the 8th, the United States sold 1.664 billion U.S. dollars worth of gold in 1965, of which more than three-quarters was exchanged for by seven Western European countries, mainly France, using U.S. dollars.
    Materials released by the U.S. Treasury Department indicate that France exchanged a total of 884 million U.S. dollars worth of gold from the United States last year, accounting for more than half of the gold sold by the United States. In addition, Austria, Belgium, Italy, the Netherlands, Spain, and Switzerland exchanged over 500 million U.S. dollars.
    During the same period, the United States purchased nearly 200 million U.S. dollars worth of gold from countries such as Brazil, Colombia, Morocco, and the United Kingdom.

### 美国去年流失黄金十六亿多美元  法国向美国兑换了总值近九亿美元黄金

1966-03-10
第5版()
专栏：

    美国去年流失黄金十六亿多美元
    法国向美国兑换了总值近九亿美元黄金
    新华社九日讯  华盛顿消息：据美国财政部八日宣布，一九六五年内美国售出黄金十六亿六千四百万美元，其中四分之三以上是由法国为主的七个西欧国家用美元换去的。
    美国财政部发表的材料表明，在去年内，法国一共向美国兑换了总值八亿八千四百万美元的黄金，占美国售出黄金的半数以上；另外奥地利、比利时、意大利、荷兰、西班牙和瑞士六国共兑换了五亿多美元。
    同一时期，美国曾向巴西、哥伦比亚、摩洛哥和英国等国购进黄金近两亿多美元。
