Title: Albanian People's Assembly Presidium Decides to Re-establish Political Commissars in Armed Forces and Abolish Military Ranks
Author:
Date: 1966-03-09
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, March 8th - Albanian newspapers published today the decision of the Presidium of the Albanian People's Assembly regarding the re-establishment of political commissars in the armed forces and the order to abolish the military rank system in the armed forces.
    The decision regarding the re-establishment of political commissars in the armed forces states that, under the conditions of new and greater tasks facing the armed forces, in order to further improve the ideological level, revolutionary consciousness, and vigilance of all officers and soldiers, and to strengthen collective leadership and collective wisdom, taking into account the experience of the National Liberation War and the experience of the significant role of political commissars in the early days of liberation, the Presidium of the People's Assembly has decided to re-establish the position of political commissar in all units and military institutions.
    This decision shall take effect immediately upon promulgation.
    The decision of 1955 to abolish the position of political commissar and establish a unified command system is declared invalid.
    The order regarding the abolition of the military rank system in the armed forces stipulates the abolition of the military rank system of the Albanian People's Republic's armed forces, with military levels determined according to the duties performed in the corresponding institutions within the armed forces.
    This order shall take effect on May 1st of this year.

### 阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团决定  武装部队重新设立政委取消军衔制

1966-03-09
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团决定
    武装部队重新设立政委取消军衔制
    新华社地拉那八日电  阿尔巴尼亚报纸八日登载了阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团关于在武装部队内重新设立政治委员的决定和关于取消武装部队军衔制的命令。
    关于在武装部队内重新设立政治委员的决定说，在武装部队面临着新的更大的任务的条件下，为了进一步提高全体指战员的思想水平、革命的觉悟和警惕性，加强集体领导和集体智慧，考虑到民族解放战争的经验和解放初期关于政治委员的重大作用的经验，人民议会主席团决定在所有部队和军事机构中重新设立政治委员的职务。
    这个决定在公布后立即生效。
    决定宣布一九五五年关于取消政治委员职务并建立单一指挥制的决议失效。
    关于取消武装部队军衔制的命令规定取消阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国武装部队的军衔制，军人级别按照在武装部队相应机构内所担负的职务确定。
    这个命令在今年五月一日起生效。
