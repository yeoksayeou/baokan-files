Title: At the Invitation of President Ayub Khan, Chairman Liu and His Wife Will Visit Pakistan; Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi and His Wife Will Accompany Them

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-14
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 13th: At the invitation of Field Marshal Mohammad Ayub Khan, President of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and his wife, Wang Guangmei, will pay a friendly visit to Pakistan. Accompanying them on the visit will be Chen Yi, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, and his wife, as well as other personnel.

### 应阿尤布·汗总统的邀请  刘主席和夫人将访问巴基斯坦  陈毅副总理兼外长和夫人将陪同前往访问

1966-03-14
第1版()
专栏：

    应阿尤布·汗总统的邀请
    刘主席和夫人将访问巴基斯坦
    陈毅副总理兼外长和夫人将陪同前往访问
    新华社十三日讯  中华人民共和国主席刘少奇和夫人王光美，应巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国总统穆罕默德·阿尤布·汗元帅的邀请，将前往巴基斯坦进行友好访问。陪同访问的有中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅和夫人以及其他人员。
