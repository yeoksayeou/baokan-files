Title: Communist Party of Peru Holds Fifth National Congress
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-09
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Lima, March 8th -- The Communist Party of Peru held its Fifth National Congress from November 15th to 16th, 1965. General Secretary Saturnino Paredes delivered a report at the meeting entitled "The Political Situation and the Tasks of the Communist Party of Peru." The meeting finally adopted the "Conclusions and Resolutions of the Fifth National Congress of the Communist Party of Peru."

### 秘鲁共产党举行第五次全国代表会议

1966-03-09
第3版()
专栏：

    秘鲁共产党举行第五次全国代表会议
    新华社八日讯  利马消息：秘鲁共产党在一九六五年十一月十五日至十六日召开了第五次全国代表会议。总书记萨图尼诺·帕雷德斯在会上作了关于《政治形势和秘鲁共产党的任务》的报告。会议最后通过了《秘鲁共产党第五次全国代表会议的结论和决议》。
