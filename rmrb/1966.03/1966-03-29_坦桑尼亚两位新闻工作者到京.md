Title: Tanzanian Journalists Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-03-29
Edition: 4

    Tanzanian Journalists Arrive in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, March 26th. Mr. Mwamukwamwa, journalist of the Tanzanian bi-weekly "African Vigilant," and Mr. Masimomosi, correspondent of the "Agricultural Today" newspaper, arrived in Beijing by plane tonight for a visit to China at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association.
    Li Wu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Journalists Association, and others welcomed them at the airport.

### 坦桑尼亚两位新闻工作者到京

1966-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

    坦桑尼亚两位新闻工作者到京
    新华社二十六日讯  应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来我国访问的坦桑尼亚《非洲警惕》双周刊记者姆旺坎姆瓦和《今日农业》报通讯员马辛莫希，今天晚上乘飞机到达北京。
    中华全国新闻工作者协会书记处书记李伍等到机场欢迎。
