Title: New Algerian Ambassador to China Calls on Vice Premier Chen Yi
Author:
Date: 1966-03-06
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 5th—Algeria's new Ambassador to China, Mohammed Cherif Sahli, paid a courtesy call on Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi this morning. Vice Premier Chen Yi held a friendly conversation with him.

### 阿尔及利亚新任驻华大使  拜会陈毅副总理

1966-03-06
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚新任驻华大使
    拜会陈毅副总理
    新华社五日讯　阿尔及利亚新任驻华大使穆罕默德·谢里夫·萨赫利今天上午拜会国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅。陈毅副总理同他进行了友好的谈话。
