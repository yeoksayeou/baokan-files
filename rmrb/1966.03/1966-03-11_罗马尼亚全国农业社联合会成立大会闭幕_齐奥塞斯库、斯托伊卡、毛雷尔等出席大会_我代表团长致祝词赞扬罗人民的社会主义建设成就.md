Title: Romanian National Federation of Agricultural Cooperatives Inaugural Congress Concludes, Ceaușescu, Stoica, Maurer, and Others Attend, Our Delegation Head Delivers Congratulatory Speech Praising the Achievements of the Romanian People in Socialist Construction
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-11
Edition: 4

    Romanian National Federation of Agricultural Cooperatives Inaugural Congress Concludes
    Ceaușescu, Stoica, Maurer, and Others Attend
    Our Delegation Head Delivers Congratulatory Speech Praising the Achievements of the Romanian People in Socialist Construction
    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, March 9th – The inaugural congress of the Romanian National Federation of Agricultural Production Cooperatives concluded here on the 9th.
    The congress opened on the 7th. More than 5,500 representatives from agricultural cooperative federations across the country, along with 3,500 non-voting delegates from industrial, cultural, scientific, and artistic sectors, attended the congress.
    Romanian party and state leaders Ceaușescu, Chivu Stoica, Maurer, and others also attended the congress.
    Nicolae Ceaușescu, General Secretary of the Romanian Communist Party, spoke at the meeting on the 7th. In his speech, he pointed out that the establishment of the National Federation of Agricultural Production Cooperatives is an event of great significance in the national life. It will promote the new development of agriculture and contribute to the overall development of the motherland. In his report, Ceaușescu also discussed the significant achievements made by Romanian industry and agriculture during the Six-Year Plan (1960-1965), and pointed out the future guidelines and tasks for further developing agricultural production.
    Vasile Vîlcu, Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Romanian National Federation of Agricultural Production Cooperatives, delivered a report at the meeting on the tasks of agricultural production cooperatives in improving agricultural and animal husbandry production and further developing and strengthening agricultural production cooperatives.
    The congress elected the Council of the National Federation of Agricultural Production Cooperatives, and Vasile Vîlcu was elected Chairman of the Council.
    Cheng Zhaoxuan, head of the Chinese delegation and Vice Minister of Agriculture, who was invited to attend the congress, delivered a congratulatory speech at the meeting. In his speech, he praised the great victories achieved by the Romanian people under the leadership of the Romanian Communist Party in socialist construction and spoke of the profound friendship established between the people of China and Romania in the long-term revolutionary struggle.
    Romanian Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Maurer, also spoke at the close of the meeting.

### 罗马尼亚全国农业社联合会成立大会闭幕  齐奥塞斯库、斯托伊卡、毛雷尔等出席大会  我代表团长致祝词赞扬罗人民的社会主义建设成就

1966-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚全国农业社联合会成立大会闭幕
    齐奥塞斯库、斯托伊卡、毛雷尔等出席大会
    我代表团长致祝词赞扬罗人民的社会主义建设成就
    新华社布加勒斯特九日电  罗马尼亚全国农业生产合作社联合会成立大会九日在这里闭幕。
    大会是在七日开幕的。来自全国各农业社联合会的五千五百多名代表以及工业、文化、科学、艺术等部门的三千五百多名列席代表出席了大会。
    罗马尼亚党和国家领导人齐奥塞斯库、基伏·斯托伊卡、毛雷尔等也出席了大会。
    罗共中央总书记齐奥塞斯库七日在会上讲了话。他在讲话中指出，成立全国农业生产合作社联合会是全国生活中具有重要意义的事件。它将促进农业的新的发展，并为祖国的全面发展作出贡献。齐奥塞斯库在报告中还谈到罗马尼亚工农业在六年计划期间（一九六○——一九六五年）所取得的重大成就，并且指出了今后进一步发展农业生产的方针和任务。
    罗马尼亚全国农业生产合作社联合会筹备委员会主席瓦西里·维尔库七日在会上作了关于农业生产合作社为提高农牧业生产以及进一步发展和加强农业生产合作社的任务的报告。
    大会选出了全国农业生产合作社联合会理事会，瓦西里·维尔库当选为理事会主席。
    应邀参加大会的中国代表团团长、农业部副部长程照轩在会上致了祝词。他在祝词中赞扬了罗马尼亚人民在罗马尼亚共产党领导下，在社会主义建设中所取得的巨大胜利，并且谈到了中罗两国人民在长期革命斗争中所建立的深厚友谊。
    罗马尼亚部长会议主席毛雷尔在会议结束时也讲了话。
