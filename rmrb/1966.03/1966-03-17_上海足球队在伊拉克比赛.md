Title: Shanghai Football Team Plays in Iraq

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-17
Edition: 4

    Shanghai Football Team Plays in Iraq
    (Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad) Approximately 8,000 spectators in Baghdad, the capital of Iraq, watched the friendly match between the Chinese Shanghai Football Team and Baghdad B Team on the afternoon of the 11th.
    The Shanghai Football Team defeated the Baghdad B Team with a score of 4-0. This wonderful match won warm cheers and applause from the audience.
    The Shanghai Football Team arrived in Baghdad on the 8th.

### 上海足球队在伊拉克比赛

1966-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

    上海足球队在伊拉克比赛
    据新华社巴格达电　伊拉克首都巴格达十一日下午大约有八千名观众观看了中国上海足球队同巴格达乙队的友谊比赛。
    上海足球队以四比零胜巴格达乙队。这场精彩的比赛博得了观众热烈的欢呼声和掌声。
    上海足球队是八日到达巴格达的。
