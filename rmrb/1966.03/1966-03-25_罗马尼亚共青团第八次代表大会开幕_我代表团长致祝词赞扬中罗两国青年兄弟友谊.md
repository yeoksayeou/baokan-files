Title: Romanian Communist Youth League's Eighth Congress Opens; Our Delegation Leader Delivers Congratulatory Speech Praising the Brotherly Friendship Between Chinese and Romanian Youth

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-25
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, March 23rd - The Eighth Congress of the Romanian Communist Youth League opened here on the morning of the 23rd. More than 3,000 official delegates and observers from all over the country attended the congress. The Chinese Communist Youth League delegation, led by Wang Daoyi, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, as well as representatives from youth organizations from more than 30 countries, including Albania, North Korea, and Japan, also attended the congress.
    Romanian party and government leaders Nicolae Ceaușescu, Chivu Stoica, Ion Gheorghe Maurer, and others attended the opening ceremony of the congress.
    Nicolae Ceaușescu, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Communist Party, delivered a congratulatory speech to the congress on behalf of the Central Committee of the Romanian Communist Party. He highly praised the contributions made by the Romanian Communist Youth League and all young people in socialist construction.
    Enache, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Communist Youth League, delivered a report on the work and tasks of the Communist Youth League at the meeting, and Maria Apostol, Chairman of the Inspection Committee of the Central Committee of the League, delivered a report on the work of the Inspection Committee of the Central Committee of the League.
    In his report, Enache suggested that, in view of the fact that the Romanian Socialist Youth Congress held in 1922 laid the foundation for the Romanian revolutionary youth organization, this congress should be renamed the Eighth Congress of the Romanian Communist Youth League (originally called the Fourth Congress). All the representatives unanimously adopted this proposal.
    Wang Daoyi, head of the Chinese Communist Youth League delegation, delivered a congratulatory speech to the congress on the evening of the 23rd. He warmly praised the close brotherly friendship between the people and youth of China and Romania forged in the long revolutionary struggle. He said that in recent years, the friendly cooperative relations between our two countries' youth organizations have further developed. The Chinese youth cherish and will strive to develop the fraternal friendship between our two peoples and youth.
    The congress will last for four days and will close on the 26th.

### 罗马尼亚共青团第八次代表大会开幕  我代表团长致祝词赞扬中罗两国青年兄弟友谊

1966-03-25
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚共青团第八次代表大会开幕
    我代表团长致祝词赞扬中罗两国青年兄弟友谊
    新华社布加勒斯特二十三日电  罗马尼亚共产主义青年团第八次代表大会二十三日上午在这里开幕。出席代表大会的有来自全国的三千多名正式代表和列席代表。由中国共青团中央书记王道义率领的中国共青团代表团以及来自阿尔巴尼亚、朝鲜、日本等共三十多个国家的青年组织的代表，也出席了大会。
    罗马尼亚党政领导人尼·齐奥塞斯库、基伏·斯托伊卡、扬·格·毛雷尔等出席了大会开幕式。
    罗马尼亚共产党中央委员会总书记尼·齐奥塞斯库代表罗共中央向大会致祝词。他高度赞扬了罗马尼亚共青团和全体青年在社会主义建设中作出的贡献。
    罗马尼亚共青团中央第一书记埃纳克在会上作了关于共青团的工作和任务的报告，团中央检查委员会主席玛丽亚·阿波斯托尔作了关于团中央检查委员会的工作的报告。
    埃纳克在报告中建议，鉴于一九二二年举行的罗马尼亚社会主义青年大会为罗马尼亚革命青年组织奠定了基础，这次代表大会应该改称为罗马尼亚共青团第八次代表大会（原先称为第四次代表大会）。全体代表一致通过了这一建议。
    中国共青团代表团团长王道义二十三日晚上向大会致了祝词。他热烈赞扬中罗两国人民和青年在长期的革命斗争中结成的亲密的兄弟友谊。他说，近几年来，我们两国青年组织的友好合作关系有了进一步的发展。中国青年十分珍视并将努力发展我们两国人民和青年之间的兄弟友谊。
    代表大会将举行四天，到二十六日闭幕。
