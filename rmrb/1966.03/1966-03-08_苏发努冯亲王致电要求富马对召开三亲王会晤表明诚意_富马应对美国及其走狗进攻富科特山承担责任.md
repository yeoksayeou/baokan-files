Title: Prince Souphanouvong Sends a Telegram Demanding Phouma Show Sincerity in Convening a Three-Prince Meeting; Phouma Should Bear Responsibility for the Attacks by the United States and its Lackeys on Phou Kout Mountain
Author:
Date: 1966-03-08
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Kang Khai, 7th—According to Radio Pathet Lao, Prince Souphanouvong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front and Deputy Prime Minister of the National Union Government, pointed out in a telegram to Prince Phouma on February 24 that Prince Phouma should bear responsibility for the blatant attacks launched by the United States and its lackeys against Phou Kout Mountain in Xieng Khouang Province.

    The telegram pointed out that on February 4, under the direct command of American advisors, the Kong Le troops and Thai troops, with the cooperation of American aircraft taking off from bases in Thailand, launched a large-scale attack on the positions of the Lao People's Liberation Army and Patriotic Neutralist Forces on Phou Kout Mountain in Xieng Khouang Province. This was an extremely blatant and premeditated action to attack the liberated areas. This action is clearly linked to the new military conspiracies plotted by US imperialism against central and lower Laos. The telegram said that the aforementioned attack on Phou Kout Mountain has made the Lao situation more tense and serious, and is also a major obstacle to the holding of a meeting of the three Lao princes.

    The telegram said that the Lao Patriotic Front has in the past and now demands that the three Lao parties hold negotiations on the basis of the various agreements signed by the three Lao parties in order to seek a solution to the Lao problem.

    The telegram requested that Prince Phouma show sincerity on the issue of a three-prince meeting and not insist on Luang Prabang, the royal capital controlled by the right-wing group, as the meeting venue, but rather should jointly find a meeting location that can guarantee the safety of all three parties and be approved by all three parties.

### 苏发努冯亲王致电要求富马对召开三亲王会晤表明诚意  富马应对美国及其走狗进攻富科特山承担责任

1966-03-08
第4版()
专栏：

    苏发努冯亲王致电要求富马对召开三亲王会晤表明诚意
    富马应对美国及其走狗进攻富科特山承担责任
    新华社康开七日电　据巴特寮电台广播：老挝爱国战线党中央委员会主席、民族团结政府副首相苏发努冯亲王在二月二十四日打给富马亲王的电报中指出，富马亲王应对美国及其走狗向川圹省富科特山发动的明目张胆的进攻承担责任。
    电报指出，二月四日，在美国顾问直接指挥下，贡勒军队和泰国军队在从泰国基地起飞的美国飞机配合下，大举进攻川圹省富科特山老挝人民解放军和爱国中立力量的阵地，这是极其明目张胆的和蓄谋已久的进攻解放区的行动。这一行动显然是同美帝国主义策划的对中寮和下寮地区的新军事阴谋相联系的。电报说，上述对富科特山的进攻使得老挝局势更加紧张和严重，并且也是老挝三亲王举行会晤的巨大障碍之一。
    电报说，老挝爱国战线党过去和现在都要求老挝三方面在老挝三方所签订的各项协议的基础上，举行谈判，以便寻求解决老挝问题的办法。
    电报要求富马亲王在三亲王会晤的问题上表明诚意，不要坚持以右派集团所控制的琅勃拉邦王都为会谈的地点，而是应该共同找到一个能够保证三方安全、并得到三方赞成的会晤地点。
