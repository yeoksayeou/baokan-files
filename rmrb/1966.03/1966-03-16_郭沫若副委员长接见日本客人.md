Title: Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the NPC Standing Committee, Receives Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-03-16
Edition: 4

    Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the NPC Standing Committee, Receives Japanese Guests

    (Xinhua News Agency, March 15) Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the NPC Standing Committee, received all members of the Japanese Folk Performing Artists Delegation headed by Okamotobun'ya this morning and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.



**Named Entities**

郭沫若	Guo Moruo
冈本文弥	Okamotobun'ya
日本民族艺能家代表团	Japanese Folk Performing Artists Delegation
新华社	Xinhua News Agency
全国人民代表大会常务委员会	National People's Congress Standing Committee

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人

1966-03-16
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人
    据新华社十五日讯　郭沫若副委员长今天上午接见了以冈本文弥为首的日本民族艺能家代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
