Title: Prince Sihanouk Sends Telegram of Support to President Nkrumah
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-10
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, March 9th -- According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on the 9th, Cambodian Head of State Prince Sihanouk sent a telegram to President Nkrumah expressing his support for him.
    The telegram said, "We have been closely following the recent military coup in Ghana, and we express our concern about this event, which weakens Asian-African solidarity and strengthens the enemies of imperialism and neo-colonialism. At this moment of trial, I would like to assure you of our moral support and fervently pray that Ghana will restore peace and stability and regain its position among those Asian and African countries that are fighting with all their might against imperialist aggression and conspiracy."

### 西哈努克亲王致电支持恩克鲁玛总统

1966-03-10
第5版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王致电支持恩克鲁玛总统
    新华社金边九日电  据柬埔寨通讯社九日报道，柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王打电报给恩克鲁玛总统，表示对他的支持。
    电报说，“我们一直密切注视着加纳最近军事政变的发展，我们对这种削弱亚非团结、长帝国主义和新殖民主义敌人威风的事件，表示忧虑。在这个考验的时刻，我愿向你保证我们的道义支持，并且热烈祝祷加纳恢复和平与稳定，和在那些为反对帝国主义侵略和阴谋进行全力斗争的亚非国家中重新获得它的地位。”
