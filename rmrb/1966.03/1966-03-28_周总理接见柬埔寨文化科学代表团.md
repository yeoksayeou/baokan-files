Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Cambodian Cultural and Scientific Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-03-28
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, March 27th: Premier Zhou Enlai received the entire Cambodian Cultural and Scientific Delegation led by Mr. Sim Var, Undersecretary of State of the Cambodian Ministry of National Education, at noon today and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were responsible persons from relevant departments including Shi Huaibi and Wu Heng. The Cambodian Ambassador to China, Mr. Chau Seng, was also present. (Photo attached)
    March 27th, Premier Zhou Enlai receives the Cambodian Cultural and Scientific Delegation led by Mr. Sim Var (fourth from right), Undersecretary of State of the Cambodian Ministry of National Education.
      Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Changzhong [photograph]

### 周总理接见柬埔寨文化科学代表团

1966-03-28
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理接见柬埔寨文化科学代表团
    新华社二十七日讯  周恩来总理今天中午接见了柬埔寨国民教育部副国务员沈力速率领的柬埔寨文化科学代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有：史怀璧、武衡等有关方面负责人。柬埔寨驻中国大使张岗也在座。（附图片）
    三月二十七日，周恩来总理接见由柬埔寨国民教育部副国务员沈力速（右四）率领的柬埔寨文化科学代表团。
      新华社记者  刘长忠摄
