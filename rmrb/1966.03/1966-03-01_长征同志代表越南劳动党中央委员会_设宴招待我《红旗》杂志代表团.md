Title: Comrade Trường Chinh, on Behalf of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party, Hosts a Banquet for the Delegation of Our Magazine "Red Flag"
Author:
Date: 1966-03-01
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 28th – Trường Chinh, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 26th on behalf of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party to receive the delegation of the Chinese magazine "Red Flag," led by Deputy Editor-in-Chief Fan Ruoyu.

    Attending the banquet were Hà Huy Giáp, member of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party, Vũ Tuân, Director of the General Office of the Party Central Committee, Song Tùng, Deputy Director of the Foreign Liaison Department of the Party Central Committee, Trần Song, Vice President of the Nguyễn Ái Quốc Party School, Đào Duy Tùng, Editor-in-Chief of the magazine "Học Tập," and Hồng Chương, Deputy Editor-in-Chief, among others.

    Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam, Zhu Qiwén, was also invited to attend the banquet.

    The banquet was filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 长征同志代表越南劳动党中央委员会  设宴招待我《红旗》杂志代表团

1966-03-01
第3版()
专栏：

    长征同志代表越南劳动党中央委员会
    设宴招待我《红旗》杂志代表团
    新华社河内二十八日电　越南劳动党中央政治局委员长征，二十六日晚上代表越南劳动党中央委员会设宴招待由副总编辑范若愚率领的中国《红旗》杂志代表团。
    参加宴会的有越南劳动党中央委员何辉甲，党中央办公厅主任武遵，党中央对外联络委员会副主任双松，阮爱国党校副校长陈宋，《学习》杂志总编辑陶维松和副总编辑鸿章等。
    中国驻越南大使朱其文也应邀出席了宴会。
    宴会洋溢着热情友好的气氛。
