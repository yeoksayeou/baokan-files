Title: The Liaison Delegation of the Vietnam People's Army Protests US Imperialism's Expansion of Bombing of the North; US Imperialism's New Military Adventure Once Again Exposes Johnson's "Humanitarian" Lies
Author:
Date: 1966-03-07
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 4th. The head of the Liaison Delegation of the General Command of the Vietnam People's Army sent an urgent telegram to the Chairman of the International Commission of Vietnam on the 4th, strongly condemning the war actions of US imperialism in expanding the bombing of North Vietnam.
    The telegram stated that on March 4th, multiple batches of American fighter planes successively invaded the airspace of Nghia Lo, Son La, Yen Bai, Phu Tho, Hoa Binh, Thanh Hoa, Nghe An provinces in North Vietnam, and bombed and strafed many residential areas and economic facilities in Phu Tho, Hoa Binh, Thanh Hoa provinces.
    On March 3rd, American aircraft also barbarously bombed the Mai Song Primary School and the Dan Thuong School in Ha Hoa County, Phu Tho Province, killing and injuring eleven students and teachers of Dan Thuong School.
    The telegram pointed out that recently, US imperialism has intensified its bandit-like attacks on the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, expanding the scope of bombing in many provinces of North Vietnam, and indiscriminately bombing densely populated residential areas, hospitals, schools, dams, etc.
    The telegram pointed out that US imperialism, disregarding the strong condemnation of world public opinion, is intensifying its war of aggression in Vietnam. These blatant "escalation" actions by the United States once again expose the deceptive nature of Johnson's so-called "peace" and "humanitarianism."
    The telegram emphasized that the US government must bear full responsibility for all the consequences caused by its new military adventures.

### 越人民军联络代表团抗议美帝扩大对北方的轰炸  美帝新军事冒险再次揭穿约翰逊“人道”谎言

1966-03-07
第4版()
专栏：

    越人民军联络代表团抗议美帝扩大对北方的轰炸
    美帝新军事冒险再次揭穿约翰逊“人道”谎言
    新华社河内四日电　越南人民军总司令部联络代表团团长四日打紧急电报给越南国际委员会主席，严厉谴责美帝国主义对越南北方扩大轰炸的战争行动。
    电报说，三月四日，多批美国战斗机接连侵入越南北方的义路、山萝、安沛、富寿、和平、清化、义安等省上空，并轰炸扫射富寿、和平、清化等省的许多居民区和经济设施。
    三月三日，美国飞机还野蛮轰炸了富寿省下和县的梅松小学和丹上学校，炸死、炸伤丹上学校的十一名学生和教师。
    电报指出，最近，美帝国主义加紧对越南民主共和国的强盗进攻，对越南北方许多省份扩大轰炸范围，并对人口稠密的居民区、医院、学校、水坝等狂轰滥炸。
    电报指出，美帝国主义不顾世界舆论的强烈谴责，加紧扩大在越南的侵略战争。美国这些明目张胆的“升级”行动，再次揭穿了约翰逊所谓“和平”、“人道”等论调的欺骗性。
    电报强调说，美国政府必须对它的新军事冒险行动所引起的一切后果担负全部责任。
