Title: People from all walks of life in the capital will hold a rally today to support the American people in opposing U.S. imperialist aggression against Vietnam
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-27
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 26th. People from all walks of life in the capital will hold a rally on the 27th to support the American people in opposing U.S. imperialism's aggression against Vietnam. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the live proceedings of the rally starting at 2:25 PM on the 27th, using the frequency of its first program. Beijing Television and Beijing People's Broadcasting Station will also broadcast it simultaneously.



**Named Entities**


首都   Shǒudū (Capital, referring to Beijing)
美国   Měiguó (United States)
美帝国主义  Měi Dìguózhǔyì (U.S. Imperialism)
越南   Yuènán (Vietnam)
新华社   Xīnhuá Shè (Xinhua News Agency)
中央人民广播电台  Zhōngyāng Rénmín Guǎngbō Diàntái (Central People's Broadcasting Station)
北京电视台  Běijīng Diànshìtái (Beijing Television)
北京人民广播电台  Běijīng Rénmín Guǎngbō Diàntái (Beijing People's Broadcasting Station)

### 首都各界人民今日举行大会支持美国人民反对美帝侵越

1966-03-27
第1版()
专栏：

    首都各界人民今日举行大会支持美国人民反对美帝侵越
    新华社二十六日讯　首都各界人民将在二十七日举行支持美国人民反对美帝国主义侵略越南大会。中央人民广播电台将从二十七日下午两点二十五分开始，用第一套节目的频率，转播大会的实况。北京电视台、北京人民广播电台也将同时转播。
