Title: Changes in National Administrative Divisions from October 15, 1965 to February 28, 1966
Author:
Date: 1966-03-23
Edition: 2

    Changes in National Administrative Divisions
    October 15, 1965 to February 28, 1966
    Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region:
    I. Established Erenhot City. Erenhot City belongs to Xilin Gol League.
    II. Established Ergun Left Banner and Ergun Right Banner, abolishing Ergun Banner. Ergun Left Banner and Ergun Right Banner belong to Hulunbuir League.
    Liaoning Province:
    I. Established Liaonan Administrative Office, stationed in Gaixian County. It leads Liaoyang and Haicheng counties, originally under the leadership of Anshan City; Yingkou and Gaixian counties, originally under the leadership of Yingkou City; and Fuxian, Jinxian, and Xinjin counties, originally under the leadership of Lüda City. A total of seven counties.
    II. Established Jinzhou Administrative Office, stationed in Jinzhou City. It leads Heishan, Beizhen, Yixian, Jinxian, Jinxi, Xingcheng, and Suizhong counties, originally under the leadership of Jinzhou City.
    III. Zhuanghe County, originally under the leadership of Lüda City, is placed under the leadership of Dandong City.
    IV. Liaoyang City, originally under the leadership of Anshan City, is now directly under the leadership of the province.
    Jilin Province:
    I. Established Dehui Administrative Office, stationed in Dehui County. Dehui, Yushu, Nongan, Jiutai, and Shuangyang counties, originally under the leadership of Changchun City, are placed under the leadership of Dehui Administrative Office.
    II. Established Yongji Administrative Office, stationed in Jilin City. Yongji, Shulan, Huadian, Panshi, and Jiaohe counties, originally under the leadership of Jilin City, are placed under the leadership of Yongji Administrative Office.
    Heilongjiang Province:
    The cities of Mudanjiang and Jixi, originally under the leadership of Mudanjiang Administrative Office, and the cities of Jiamusi, Hegang, and Shuangyashan, originally under the leadership of Hejiang Administrative Office, are all placed directly under the leadership of the province.
    Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region:
    I. Abolished Shuiding County (formerly known as Suiding County), and the administrative region of Shuiding County is incorporated into Horgos County.
    II. Hojing County is renamed Hejing County.
    Jiangsu Province:
    Established Liuhe Administrative Office, stationed in Liuhe County. It leads Liuhe, Yizheng, Jiangpu, and Jinhu counties, originally under the leadership of Yangzhou Administrative Office, and Xuyi County, originally under the leadership of Huaiyin Administrative Office, a total of five counties.
    Henan Province:
    Established Sheqi County. Sheqi County belongs to Nanyang Special District.
    Hunan Province:
     I. Abolished Nanyue County and established Hengdong County. Hengdong County belongs to Hengyang Special District.
    II. Established Miluo County. Miluo County belongs to Yueyang Special District.
    Guizhou Province:
    I. Established Anlong Buyi and Miao Autonomous County, abolishing Anlong County.
    II. Established Zhenfeng Buyi and Miao Autonomous County, abolishing Zhenfeng County.
    III. Established Ceheng Buyi Autonomous County, abolishing Ceheng County.
    IV. Established Wangmo Buyi and Miao Autonomous County, abolishing Wangmo County.
    V. The above four ethnic minority autonomous counties belong to Xingyi Special District.
    VI. Established Ziyun Miao and Buyi Autonomous County, abolishing Ziyun County. Ziyun Miao and Buyi Autonomous County belongs to Anshun Special District.
    VII. Restored Liuzhi County to its original name of Langdai County.
    Tibet Autonomous Region:
    I. Heihe County is renamed Nagqu County.
    II. Ningjing County is renamed Markam County.
    III. Lajiali County is renamed Qusong County.
    IV. Zhegu County is renamed Cuomei County.
    V. Sangangqu County is renamed Zayu County.
                      (Xinhua News Agency)

### 全国行政区划变动情况  一九六五年十月十五日至一九六六年二月二十八日

1966-03-23
第2版()
专栏：

    全国行政区划变动情况
    一九六五年十月十五日至一九六六年二月二十八日
    内蒙古自治区：
    一、设立二连浩特市。二连浩特市属锡林郭勒盟。
    二、设立额尔古纳左旗和额尔古纳右旗，撤销额尔古纳旗。额尔古纳左旗和额尔古纳右旗属呼伦贝尔盟。
    辽宁省：
    一、设立辽南专员公署，驻盖县。领导原由鞍山市领导的辽阳、海城两个县；原由营口市领导的营口、盖县两个县和原由旅大市领导的复县、金县、新金三个县。共七个县。
    二、设立锦州专员公署，驻锦州市。领导原由锦州市领导的黑山、北镇、义县、锦县、锦西、兴城、绥中七个县。
    三、旅大市领导的庄河县划归丹东市领导。
    四、鞍山市领导的辽阳市改由省直接领导。
    吉林省：
    一、设立德惠专员公署，驻德惠县。将长春市领导的德惠、榆树、农安、九台、双阳五个县划归德惠专员公署领导。
    二、设立永吉专员公署，驻吉林市。将吉林市领导的永吉、舒兰、桦甸、磐石、蛟河五个县划归永吉专员公署领导。
    黑龙江省：
    将牡丹江专员公署领导的牡丹江、鸡西两个市和合江专员公署领导的佳木斯、鹤岗、双鸭山三个市均改由省直接领导。
    新疆维吾尔自治区：
    一、撤销水定县（绥定县改称），将水定县的行政区域划归霍城县。
    二、和靖县改名为和静县。
    江苏省：
    设立六合专员公署，驻六合县。领导原由扬州专员公署领导的六合、仪征、江浦、金湖四个县及淮阴专员公署领导的盱眙县，共五个县。
    河南省：
    设立社旗县。社旗县属南阳专区。
    湖南省：
     一、撤销南岳县，设立衡东县。衡东县属衡阳专区。
    二、设立汨罗县。汨罗县属岳阳专区。
    贵州省：
    一、设立安龙布依族苗族自治县，撤销安龙县。
    二、设立贞丰布依族苗族自治县，撤销贞丰县。
    三、设立册亨布依族自治县，撤销册亨县。
    四、设立望谟布依族苗族自治县，撤销望谟县。
    五、以上四个少数民族自治县属兴义专区。
    六、设立紫云苗族布依族自治县，撤销紫云县。紫云苗族布依族自治县属安顺专区。
    七、将六枝县恢复原名为郎岱县。
    西藏自治区：
    一、黑河县改名为那曲县。
    二、宁静县改名为芒康县。
    三、拉加里县改名为曲松县。
    四、哲古县改名为措美县。
    五、将桑昂曲县改名为察隅县。
                      （新华社）
