Title: Johnson Escalates the War of Aggression Against Vietnam; Domestic Anti-War Movement Also Rises: American People Will Launch Larger-Scale Anti-War Demonstrations
Author:
Date: 1966-03-10
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 9th. According to a report in the American weekly, *The National Guardian* on the 5th, broad sections of the American people will hold demonstrations on March 25th and 26th, the "International Days of Protest," to protest the American aggression against Vietnam.

    This will be another nationwide demonstration and march by the American people against the war of aggression against Vietnam, following the massive "International Days of Protest" held in mid-October of last year.

    The report said that since March 1st, anti-war groups in fifty cities including New York, Washington, Boston, Philadelphia, Detroit, Chicago, Berkeley, San Francisco, and Los Angeles, have notified the organizers of the demonstration – the "National Coordinating Committee to End the War in Vietnam" – of their preparations to hold demonstrations during these two days.

    Ames Park, chairman of the "National Coordinating Committee to End the War in Vietnam," which is responsible for organizing the demonstration, said: "The purpose of our protest is to emphasize to the Johnson government that as the war gradually escalates, domestic opposition to the war will also gradually escalate."

    According to reports, the New York City "March Committee" is organizing this march. In Chicago, a committee similar to the New York City "March Committee" has been established to organize the march.

    The report said that during the first "International Days of Protest" held last October, an estimated 100,000 Americans demonstrated in at least ninety-three cities. The report quoted Ames Park as saying: "We believe that since that time, there has been a tremendous increase in anti-war sentiment [in the United States]." He pointed out that in the past six months, the number of peace organizations joining the "National Coordinating Committee to End the War in Vietnam" has "roughly doubled." This organization was formed in August of last year by thirty-three peace groups. Now, approximately two hundred groups, including national peace organizations, student organizations, and political organizations, are affiliated with this committee. (Attached picture)

### 约翰逊侵越战争在升级  国内反战运动也在高涨  美国人民将掀起更大规模的反战示威

1966-03-10
第4版()
专栏：

    约翰逊侵越战争在升级  国内反战运动也在高涨
    美国人民将掀起更大规模的反战示威
    新华社九日讯  据美国《国民前卫》周刊五日报道，美国广大阶层人民将在三月二十五日和二十六日两天举行抗议美国侵略越南的“国际抗议日”示威活动。
    这将是美国人民在去年十月中旬举行声势浩大的“国际抗议日”活动以后，又一次全国规模的反对侵略越南战争的示威游行。
    报道说，自三月一日以来，纽约、华盛顿、波士顿、费城、底特律、芝加哥、伯克利、旧金山、洛杉矶等五十个城市的反战团体，已经通知示威活动的组织者——“结束越南战争全国协调委员会”，表示准备在这两天内举行示威。
    负责组织这次示威活动的“结束越南战争全国协调委员会”主席埃姆斯帕克说：“我们抗议的目的是要向约翰逊政府强调指出，在这场战争逐步升级的同时，国内对这场战争的反对也将逐步升级。”
    据报道，纽约市“游行委员会”正在组织这次游行。在芝加哥，已成立了一个同纽约市“游行委员会”相似的委员会来组织这次游行。
    报道说，在去年十月举行的第一次“国际抗议日”期间，估计有十万美国人在至少九十三个城市举行了示威。报道援引埃姆斯帕克的话说：“我们认为，从那个时候开始，（在美国）反战情绪有了巨大的增长。”他指出，在过去半年中，加入“结束越南战争全国协调委员会”的和平组织“大约增加了一倍”。这个组织是在去年八月由三十三个和平团体组成的。现在，大约有二百个团体，包括全国性的和平组织、学生组织和政治组织，都同这个委员会有联系。（附图片）
    二月二十三日，美国总统约翰逊在纽约华道夫·亚斯多里亚饭店发表演说鼓吹侵越政策时，七千纽约公众在饭店外面举行盛大反战示威的情况。
