Title: US and Thailand Intensify Collusion to Expand Military Deployment for Aggression Against Vietnam; US Sends Another Batch of Military Heads to Thailand for Conspiracy Activities
Author:
Date: 1966-03-01
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok Dispatch: According to Thai newspapers, the United States has recently dispatched another batch of high-ranking military officers to Thailand to strengthen military collusion between the two countries and make deployments for expanding the US imperialist war of aggression against Vietnam.
    According to Thai newspapers, US Pacific Armed Forces Deputy Chief of Staff Passey discussed the Southeast Asian situation, strengthening the Thai Navy, and strengthening "military ties" between the two countries with Thai Acting Navy Commander-in-Chief Charoon on the 21st. Senior officials from the US Department of Defense's "Advanced Research Projects Agency" and a battlefield military research institution visited Bangkok in mid-February for talks with Thai Deputy Minister of Defense Thawi.
    According to the Associated Press, the United States plans to increase its troop presence in Thailand from 14,000 to 16,000 when the large air base being built in Sattahip is completed in a few months. Thai Deputy Prime Minister Praphat recently told reporters, when referring to US Vice President Humphrey's activities in Thailand in mid-February, that the United States had promised to supply Thailand with another batch of light weapons suitable for jungle warfare.
    In early February, the United States transferred a gunboat and a minesweeper to the Thai Navy.
    In order to step up coordination with the US deployment to expand its aggression against Vietnam, the Thai authorities announced in mid-February the conscription of young people born in 1945.

### 美泰加紧勾结进行扩大侵越军事部署  美国又派一批军事头目到泰国阴谋活动

1966-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    美泰加紧勾结进行扩大侵越军事部署
    美国又派一批军事头目到泰国阴谋活动
    新华社讯　曼谷消息：据泰国报纸报道，美国最近又派遣了一批高级军官到泰国去加强两国军事勾结，为扩大美帝国主义侵越战争进行部署。
    据泰国报纸报道，美国太平洋武装部队副参谋长帕西二十一日同泰国代理海军总司令乍仑讨论了东南亚局势、加强泰国海军和加强两国“军事联系”等问题。美国国防部“高级研究计划局”和一个战地军事研究机构的高级官员在二月中到曼谷同泰国国防部副部长他威进行了会谈。
    据美联社透露，美国准备过几个月在梭桃邑修筑的大空军基地建成的时候，把美国在泰国的驻军从一万四千人增加到一万六千人。泰国副总理巴博最近对新闻记者谈到二月中美国副总统汉弗莱到泰国活动的时候说，美国又答应了供应泰国一批适合于丛林战的轻型武器。
    二月初，美国移交了一艘炮艇和一艘扫雷艇给泰国海军。
    为了加紧配合美国扩大侵越战争的部署，泰国当局在二月中宣布征召一九四五年出生的青年入伍。
