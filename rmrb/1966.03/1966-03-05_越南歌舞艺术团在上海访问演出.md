Title: Vietnamese Song and Dance Ensemble Visits and Performs in Shanghai

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-05
Edition: 3

    The Vietnamese Democratic Republic Song and Dance Ensemble Visits and Performs in Shanghai
    (Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, March 3rd) The Vietnamese Democratic Republic Song and Dance Ensemble gave its premiere performance in Shanghai on the evening of March 3rd. The revolutionary and passionate songs and dances with national characteristics performed by the Vietnamese artists were warmly welcomed by the Shanghai audience.
    Cao Diqiu, Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Mayor of Shanghai; Zhang Chunqiao, Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China; Song Richang, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai; Liu Wenxue, Political Commissar of the Shanghai Garrison District; and responsible persons from relevant departments watched the performance.
    Vietnamese students and interns in Shanghai also watched the performance.
    At the end of the performance, Comrades Cao Diqiu and Zhang Chunqiao went on stage to congratulate the Vietnamese artists on their successful performance. Amidst the music of "Liberate the South" and enthusiastic applause, Shanghai literary and art workers presented flowers and flower baskets to the envoys of friendship from the Vietnamese people.
    The Vietnamese Democratic Republic Song and Dance Ensemble arrived in Shanghai on the afternoon of the 2nd after visiting and performing in Beijing.
    Song Richang, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, Meng Bo, Director of the Municipal Bureau of Culture, and other responsible persons and people from literary and art circles warmly welcomed the art envoys of the Vietnamese people at the station. Young women presented flowers to their Vietnamese comrades-in-arms. In the evening, Cao Diqiu, Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Mayor of Shanghai, held a reception to warmly welcome the Vietnamese Democratic Republic Song and Dance Ensemble.
    Cao Diqiu and Đoàn Kim Bình, head of the ensemble, spoke at the reception. They unanimously pointed out that in the common struggle against the aggression of U.S. imperialism, the fighting friendship between the Chinese and Vietnamese peoples is eternal. Cao Diqiu said that the people of Shanghai, like the people of the whole country, resolutely support the just struggle of the Vietnamese people until U.S. imperialism is driven out of Vietnam and Vietnam is unified. Đoàn Kim Bình said that as long as the troops of U.S. imperialism do not withdraw from South Vietnam, we will fight, fight until they are completely defeated, and let the Vietnamese people solve their own internal affairs.
    Yang Xiguang, Alternate Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Song Richang, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, and others attended the reception. The reception was filled with a cordial and warm atmosphere.
    Before the reception, Comrades Cao Diqiu and Yang Xiguang met with Đoàn Kim Bình, head of the art troupe, Phạm Đình Sáu, deputy head, Đoàn Kim Bình, and the main actors of the song and dance troupe, and had cordial conversations with them.

### 越南歌舞艺术团在上海访问演出

1966-03-05
第3版()
专栏：

    越南歌舞艺术团在上海访问演出
    据新华社上海三日电　越南民主共和国歌舞艺术团三日晚上在上海举行首场演出。越南艺术家们演出的充满革命战斗激情和富有民族特色的歌舞，受到上海观众热烈欢迎。
    中共上海市委书记处书记、上海市市长曹荻秋，中共上海市委书记处书记张春桥，上海市副市长宋日昌，上海警备区政委刘文学以及有关方面负责人观看了演出。
    在上海的越南留学生和实习生也观看了演出。
    演出结束时，曹荻秋、张春桥同志等走上舞台，祝贺越南艺术家演出成功。在《解放南方》乐曲声和热烈的掌声中，上海文艺工作者向越南人民的友谊使者献了鲜花和花篮。
    越南民主共和国歌舞艺术团是在北京访问演出后于二日下午到达上海的。
    上海市副市长宋日昌，市文化局局长孟波等有关方面负责人和文艺界人士到车站热烈欢迎越南人民的艺术使者。女青年向越南战友献花。晚上，中共上海市委书记处书记、上海市市长曹荻秋举行酒会，热烈欢迎越南民主共和国歌舞艺术团。
    曹荻秋和瞿辉瑾团长在酒会上先后讲话。他们一致指出，在反对美帝国主义侵略的共同斗争中，中越两国人民的战斗友谊是永恒的。曹荻秋说，上海人民和全国人民一样，坚决支持越南人民的正义斗争，直到把美帝国主义赶出越南，实现越南统一。瞿辉瑾团长说，只要美帝国主义的军队不撤出越南南方，我们就要打，打得他们彻底崩溃，让越南人民自己解决自己国家的内部事务。
    中共上海市委书记处候补书记杨西光、上海市副市长宋日昌等出席了酒会。酒会上充满了亲切、热烈的气氛。
    酒会前，曹荻秋、杨西光同志等会见了艺术团团长瞿辉瑾、副团长范庭六、团金平和歌舞团的主要演员，和他们亲切交谈。
