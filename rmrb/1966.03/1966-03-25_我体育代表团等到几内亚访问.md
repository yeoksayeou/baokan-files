Title: Chinese Sports Delegation Arrives in Guinea for Visit

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-25
Edition: 4

    Our Sports Delegation Arrived in Guinea for Visit
    Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, March 23rd – The Chinese Sports Delegation, Guangdong Women's Basketball Team, and Chinese National Youth Football Team, invited to visit and compete in Guinea, arrived in Conakry by plane on the morning of the 23rd.
    Welcoming the Chinese guests at the airport were: Mohamed Sako, member of the National Committee of the Guinean African Democratic Revolution Youth Organization; Ali Sanhon, Deputy Director of the Sports Bureau; Dour Kemoko, Chairman of the National Basketball Association; Jean Dien, Director of the Information Office of the Presidential Palace and member of the National Football Association; and Guinean athletes.
    Chai Zemin, Chinese Ambassador to Guinea, also welcomed them at the airport.

### 我体育代表团等到几内亚访问

1966-03-25
第4版()
专栏：

    我体育代表团等到几内亚访问
    新华社科纳克里二十三日电 应邀到几内亚访问比赛的中国体育代表团、广东女子篮球队和中国国家青年足球队，二十三日上午乘飞机到达科纳克里。
    到机场欢迎中国客人的有：几内亚非洲民主革命青年组织全国委员会成员穆罕默德·萨科，体育局副局长阿里·桑洪，全国篮球协会主席杜尔·克莫科，驻总统府新闻办公室主任和全国足球协会成员让·迪恩和几内亚运动员。
    中国驻几内亚大使柴泽民也到机场欢迎。
