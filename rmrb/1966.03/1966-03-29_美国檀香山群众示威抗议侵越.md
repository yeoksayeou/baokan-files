Title: Honolulu Masses Demonstrate Against the Invasion of Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-03-29
Edition: 5

    Honolulu Masses Demonstrate Against the Invasion of Vietnam
    Xinhua News Agency, March 28th, Honolulu dispatch: About two hundred Honolulu citizens held a rally and demonstration on the 27th, protesting the Johnson administration's invasion of Vietnam and demanding the withdrawal of American troops from South Vietnam.
    Demonstrators held a protest rally at Honolulu International Airport. One of the organizers of the rally read out an open letter condemning the barbarous behavior of the United States government in invading Vietnam. Many speakers at the rally demanded the withdrawal of American troops from South Vietnam.
    The demonstrators then marched to the U.S. Air Force Base and Naval Base in Honolulu, where they delivered petitions against the invasion of Vietnam.

### 美国檀香山群众示威抗议侵越

1966-03-29
第5版()
专栏：

    美国檀香山群众示威抗议侵越
    新华社二十八日讯  檀香山消息：约二百名檀香山公众二十七日举行集会和示威，抗议约翰逊政府侵略越南和要求美军从南越撤退。
    示威者在檀香山国际机场举行抗议大会，大会的一个组织者宣读了一封公开信，谴责美国政府侵略越南的野蛮行为，在会上发言的许多人都要求美国军队撤出南越。
    示威者接着游行到美国在檀香山的空军基地和海军基地，并且在那里递交了反对侵略越南的请愿书。
