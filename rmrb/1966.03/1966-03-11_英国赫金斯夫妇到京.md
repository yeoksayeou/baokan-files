Title: British Hutchings Couple Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-03-11
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 10th. British friends Mr. and Mrs. Hutchings, who are visiting our country at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, arrived in Beijing by plane today.
