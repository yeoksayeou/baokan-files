Title: National Table Tennis Championships to be Held in Shanghai This Year; More Than 290 Athletes from 24 Provinces and Municipalities to Participate

Author:

Date: 1966-03-30
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, March 27th. The 1966 National Table Tennis Championships will be held in Shanghai from April 5th to 15th. The "seed" list for each event of the championships has been announced, and the order of competition was determined by drawing lots on the 26th.

    More than 290 athletes from 24 provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions have registered to participate in this competition, including renowned players such as Zhuang Zedong, Li Furong, Zhang Xielin, Wang Jiasheng, Lin Huiqing, Zheng Minzhi, Liang Lizhen, Li Henan, and Li Li, as well as a large number of up-and-coming talents.

    This championships will feature a total of seven events: men's team, women's team, men's singles, men's doubles, women's singles, women's doubles, and mixed doubles. The men's and women's team events will be conducted in three stages according to the format of the 28th World Table Tennis Championships. The five singles events will still be held using a single-elimination format.

    This championships has made some special provisions for the team events: each team must have one defensive player, who must play at least three times. At the same time, each player registered for the team event must play at least twice, so that more people can get training.

    The group assignments for the men's and women's team events are as follows:

    Men's Team Event:

    Group 7 – Zhejiang Team 1, Liaoning, Shanghai Team 2.
    Group 8 – Sichuan, Guizhou, Jiangsu Team 2.
    Group 9 – Guangdong Team 1, Guangxi, Hebei Team 3.
    Group 10 – Jiangsu Team 1, Fujian Team 1, Shanghai Team 4.

    Women's Team Event:

    Group 1 – Shanghai Team 1, Hunan, Shaanxi, Henan Team 2.
    Group 2 – Sichuan, Fujian Team 1, Henan Team 1, Shandong Team 2.
    Group 3 – Guangdong Team 1, Zhejiang Team 1, Gansu.
    Group 4 – Shandong Team 1, Guangxi, Qinghai, Hebei Team 2.
    Group 5 – Jilin, Jiangxi, Shanghai Team 4, Beijing Team 2.
    Group 6 – Beijing Team 1, Guizhou, Shanghai Team 3.
    Group 7 – Jiangsu, Anhui, Shanghai Team 2.
    Group 8 – Hubei, Heilongjiang, Guangdong Team 2.
    Group 9 – Liaoning Team 1, Yunnan, Fujian Team 2, Zhejiang Team 2.

    The men's and women's seeded players for the five singles events are (arranged by draw area):

### 今年全国乒乓球锦标赛将在上海举行  参加比赛的有二十四个省市区的二百九十多名选手

1966-03-30
第2版()
专栏：

    今年全国乒乓球锦标赛将在上海举行
    参加比赛的有二十四个省市区的二百九十多名选手
    新华社上海二十七日电  一九六六年全国乒乓球锦标赛将于四月五日到十五日在上海举行。锦标赛各项比赛的“种子”名单已经公布，并且在二十六日抽签确定了比赛秩序。
    报名参加这次比赛的有二十四个省、市、自治区的二百九十多名选手，其中包括庄则栋、李富荣、张燮林、王家声、林慧卿、郑敏之、梁丽珍、李赫男、李莉等名手，而且还有为数众多的后起之秀。
    这次锦标赛将进行男子团体、女子团体、男子单打、男子双打、女子单打、女子双打和混合双打共七项比赛。其中男女团体赛将按第二十八届世界乒乓球锦标赛的举行办法，分成三个阶段进行。五个单项仍采取单淘汰赛的办法。
    这次锦标赛对团体赛作了一些特殊规定：要求每队必须有一名守球选手，这位守球手至少出场三次，同时，各队报名参加团体赛的选手每人至少出场二次，以便使更多的人能得到锻炼。
