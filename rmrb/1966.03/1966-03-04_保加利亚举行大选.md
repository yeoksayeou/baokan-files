Title: Bulgaria Holds Elections

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-04
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, February 2 (date as printed). Bulgaria held general elections on February 27 to elect deputies to the Fifth National Assembly, deputies to people's assemblies at all levels, as well as judges and jurors. The final results of the election, published in Sofia newspapers on the 1st, stated that a total of 415 deputies to the National Assembly and 51,275 deputies to the people's assemblies were elected in this election.

### 保加利亚举行大选

1966-03-04
第4版()
专栏：

    保加利亚举行大选
    新华社索非亚二日电　保加利亚二月二十七日举行了大选，选举第五届国民议会议员、各级人民议会议员以及法官和陪审员。索非亚报纸一日登载的选举最后结果说，这次选举共选出四百一十五名国民议会议员和五万一千二百七十五名人民议会议员。
