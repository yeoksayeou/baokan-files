Title: Vietnam Journalists Association General Secretary and South Vietnam Democratic Patriotic Journalists Association Express Support for the AAJA Secretariat's Withdrawal from Jakarta
Author:
Date: 1966-03-11
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 10th - The Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association (AAJA) received a telegram on March 8th from the South Vietnam Democratic Patriotic Journalists Association stating that they strongly condemned the pro-American Indonesian reactionaries for obstructing and interfering with the activities and affairs of the Secretariat and for openly taking terrorist measures against its staff. The telegram also expressed full support for the AAJA Secretariat's decision to temporarily withdraw from Jakarta and for other measures taken to safeguard the principles of the Jakarta Declaration of the AAJA.
    Earlier, the AAJA Secretariat received a telegram from Liu Guiqi, General Secretary of the Vietnam Journalists Association, stating that he fully supported the measures taken by the AAJA Secretariat and reiterated full support for the AAJA, expressing full confidence in its Secretariat.

### 越南记协总书记和南越民主爱国记协  表示支持亚非记协书记处撤出雅加达

1966-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    越南记协总书记和南越民主爱国记协
    表示支持亚非记协书记处撤出雅加达
    新华社十日讯  亚非新闻工作者协会书记处八日接到越南南方民主爱国新闻工作者协会发来的电报，电报指出，他们强烈谴责亲美的印度尼西亚反动派对书记处的活动和事务进行阻挠和干涉以及对它的工作人员公开采取恐怖手段。电报还表示全力支持亚非新闻工作者协会书记处暂时撤离雅加达的决定和为保卫亚非新闻工作者协会雅加达宣言的原则所采取的其他措施。
    前些时候，亚非新闻工作者协会书记处接到越南新闻工作者协会总书记刘贵淇的来电，电报表示完全支持亚非新闻工作者协会书记处采取的措施，并重申对亚非新闻工作者协会全力支持，对它的书记处充满信心。
