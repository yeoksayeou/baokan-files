Title: Prominent Swedish Biochemist and Others Arrive in Beijing

Author:

Date: 1966-03-21
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 20th:  Invited by the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences, prominent Swedish biochemist Professor Tiselius and his wife, along with thermochemist Associate Professor Sunner, arrived in Beijing by plane today.  Welcoming them at the airport were Shen Qizhen, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences, and related personnel.  Swedish Ambassador to China Petri and his wife were also present at the airport to greet them.

### 瑞典著名生物化学家等人到京

1966-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典著名生物化学家等人到京
    新华社二十日讯　应中国医学科学院邀请，瑞典著名生物化学家蒂西利斯教授和夫人以及热化学家松内副教授，今天乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中国医学科学院副院长沈其震和有关方面人士。瑞典驻中国大使佩特里和夫人也到机场迎接。
