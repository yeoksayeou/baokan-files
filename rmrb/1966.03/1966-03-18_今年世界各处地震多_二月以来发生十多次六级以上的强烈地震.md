Title: Many Earthquakes Around the World This Year: More Than Ten Strong Earthquakes of Magnitude 6 or Higher Since February
Author:
Date: 1966-03-18
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 17th dispatch: Recently, earthquakes have occurred in many regions of the world.
    According to information obtained by our reporter from relevant sources: Since February of this year, there have been more than ten strong earthquakes with magnitudes of 6 or higher and epicentral intensities of approximately 8 degrees or higher, including the strong earthquake that occurred in the Xingtai area of Hebei Province, China on March 8.
    Several of these strong earthquakes occurred in the Pacific seismic belt, while others occurred in the Atlantic Ocean and West Pakistan. The most recent strong earthquake occurred on March 13 in the sea area northeast of Taiwan, China, with a magnitude of 7.8 and an epicentral intensity of 10 to 11 degrees.
    Earthquakes occur very frequently around the world every year. On average, there are two to three hundred strong earthquakes with magnitudes of 6 or higher, one to two thousand with magnitudes of 5 or higher, and more than ten thousand with magnitudes of 4 or higher each year.
    Since March 6, earthquakes of varying degrees have occurred in the northeastern United Arab Republic, eastern Turkey, and Japan.

### 今年世界各处地震多  二月以来发生十多次六级以上的强烈地震

1966-03-18
第5版()
专栏：

    今年世界各处地震多
    二月以来发生十多次六级以上的强烈地震
    新华社十七日讯　最近世界上许多地区发生地震。
    据本社记者从有关方面获悉：今年二月以来发生的震级在六级以上、震中烈度大约在八度以上的强烈地震达十余次之多，其中包括三月八日在我国河北省邢台专区发生的强烈地震。
    这些强烈地震有几次发生在太平洋地震带，还有的发生在大西洋和西巴基斯坦。最新的一次强烈地震三月十三日发生在我国台湾东北的海区，震级为七点八级，震中烈度为十度到十一度。
    世界各地每年发生的地震十分频繁。平均每年发生的震级在六级以上的强烈地震就有二、三百次，五级以上的有一两千次，而四级以上的则在一万次以上。
    三月六日以来，在阿联东北部、土耳其东部以及日本都曾发生过程度不同的地震。
