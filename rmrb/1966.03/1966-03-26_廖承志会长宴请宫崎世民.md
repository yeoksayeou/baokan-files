Title: Liao Chengzhi Hosts Banquet for Miyazaki Seimin
Author:
Date: 1966-03-26
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 25th—Liao Chengzhi, President of the China-Japan Friendship Association, and his wife, Jing Puchun, hosted a banquet this evening to welcome Miyazaki Seimin, Chairman of the Japan-China Friendship Association.
    Among those present at the banquet were Peng Yan, Lin Lin, Sun Pinghua, Wang Xiaoyun, and others. Japanese peace advocate Kinkazu Saionji, who is in Beijing, was invited to attend the banquet.

### 廖承志会长宴请宫崎世民

1966-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

    廖承志会长宴请宫崎世民
    新华社二十五日讯　中日友好协会会长廖承志和夫人经普椿今晚设宴欢迎日中友好协会理事长宫崎世民。
    出席宴会的，有彭炎、林林、孙平化、王晓云等。在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一应邀出席了宴会。
