Title: Philippine Inspection Delegation Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-03-21
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, March 20th, the 14-member Philippine inspection delegation led by Mrs. Maria Kalaw Katigbak, Senator of the Philippine Liberal Party, left Beijing by plane this morning.  Among those who went to the airport to see them off were Zhang Yun, Luo Shuzhang, Huang Changshui, and Chang Zhiqing.

### 菲律宾考察代表团离京

1966-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

    菲律宾考察代表团离京
    据新华社二十日讯　由菲律宾自由党参议员玛丽亚·卡劳·卡蒂瓦克夫人率领的菲律宾考察代表团一行十四人，今天上午乘飞机离开北京。前往机场欢送的有章蕴、罗叔章、黄长水、常芝青等。
