Title: Indira Gandhi Again Ranted Against China in Indian Lower House; Indian Government's New Budget Submitted to Parliament Includes Unprecedentedly Huge Military Expenditure
Author:
Date: 1966-03-03
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd dispatch: New Delhi dispatch: According to Indian newspapers, in the budget for the fiscal year 1966-1967 submitted to Parliament, the Indian government proposed an unprecedentedly huge military budget of 7.977 billion rupees, an increase of nearly 300 million rupees over the previous year's military expenditure.
    During the discussion of this unprecedentedly huge military budget in the Lower House of the Indian Parliament, according to a report in the *Hindustan Times*, Indira Gandhi, speaking in Parliament on the 1st, slandered that China "continues to adopt a threatening attitude on the northern border" and attacked China for "creating tension in the world and pursuing a policy unfavorable to peace."
    According to a report by the Indian Information Service on the 1st, Dinesh Singh also repeated the cliches of so-called Chinese "threats" in his speech. He alarmistically claimed that "the Chinese threat is not only military, but a bigger problem." He clamored, "Make every effort to contain China's expansionism politically, economically and militarily."

### 甘地夫人又在印度下院叫嚷反华  印政府向议会提出的新预算中军事预算空前庞大

1966-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

    甘地夫人又在印度下院叫嚷反华
    印政府向议会提出的新预算中军事预算空前庞大
    新华社二日讯　新德里消息：据印度报纸报道，印度政府在提交议会的一九六六——一九六七财政年度的预算中，提出了七十九亿七千七百万卢比的空前庞大的军事预算，比上一年度的军费又增加了将近三亿卢比。
    在印度议会下院讨论这个空前庞大的军事预算的时候，据《印度斯坦时报》报道，英迪拉·甘地夫人一日在议会讲话时，诬蔑中国“在北部边界上继续采取威胁的态度”，并且攻击中国“在世界上制造紧张局势和奉行一种不利于和平的政策”。
    据印度新闻处一日报道，迪内希·辛格在讲话中也重复所谓中国“威胁”的滥调，他耸人听闻地说什么“中国的威胁不仅仅是军事性的，而是一个更大的问题”。他叫嚷，“尽一切努力从政治、经济和军事方面来遏制中国的扩张主义。”
