Title: Vice Chairman Dong Receives Meritorious Personnel Who Shot Down Unmanned American Aircraft, Praises Their Good Fight, and Encourages Them to Continue Their Efforts for Even Greater Victories
Author:
Date: 1966-03-07
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 6th – Dong Biwu, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, today received Sun Xiaoqing and other combat-meritorious personnel from the Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army who shot down an American imperialist military unmanned high-altitude reconnaissance aircraft over Central-South China on the 5th, in a certain location in Central-South China. During the reception, Vice Chairman Dong Biwu praised them for fighting well and encouraged them to continue their efforts, strengthen combat readiness, resolutely eliminate all invading enemy aircraft, and strive for even greater victories. After the reception, Vice Chairman Dong Biwu took a photo with Sun Xiaoqing and others.
    Those accompanying the reception included Wei Guoqing, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central-South Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Wang Dinglie, leader of the Air Force of the Guangzhou Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and others.

### 董副主席接见击落无人驾驶美机的有功人员  赞扬他们打得好，勉励他们再接再厉争取更大胜利

1966-03-07
第1版()
专栏：

    董副主席接见击落无人驾驶美机的有功人员
    赞扬他们打得好，勉励他们再接再厉争取更大胜利
    新华社广州六日电　中华人民共和国副主席董必武，今天在中南某地接见了五日在中南地区上空击落美帝国主义军用无人驾驶高空侦察机的中国人民解放军空军部队作战有功人员孙孝庆等。接见时，董必武副主席赞扬他们打得好，并勉励他们再接再厉，加强战备，坚决消灭一切入侵敌机，争取更大的胜利。接见后，董必武副主席和孙孝庆等一起照了相。
    陪同接见的，有中共中央中南局书记处书记韦国清，中国人民解放军广州部队空军领导人王定烈等。
