Title: China and Japan Men's and Women's Basketball Teams Hold Friendly Matches; Mayor Peng Zhen Watches the Games and Receives Japanese Players
Author:
Date: 1966-03-13
Edition: 4

    China and Japan Men's and Women's Basketball Teams Hold Friendly Matches
    Mayor Peng Zhen Watches the Games and Receives Japanese Players
    Xinhua News Agency, March 12th. The Japanese "Nichibo" Company Hirano Women's Basketball Team and the Japanese Waseda University Men's Basketball Team, visiting China for the first time on a friendly visit, played their first friendly matches in China tonight at the Beijing Workers' Gymnasium against the Beijing Men's Basketball Team and the Beijing Women's Basketball Team. As a result of the matches, the home team men's team won 106-51, and the women's team lost 61-64.
    Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen watched tonight's games with more than 15,000 people from the capital. After the games, Mayor Peng Zhen received the head of the Japanese Men's and Women's Basketball Delegation, Makiyama Keishu, and went to the sports field to shake hands with all the athletes from both sides. (Photo attached)
    On March 12, Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen took a group photo with athletes from both sides after watching the China-Japan men's and women's basketball games. Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Jingying.

### 中日男女篮球队进行友谊赛  彭真市长观看比赛并接见日本队员

1966-03-13
第4版()
专栏：

    中日男女篮球队进行友谊赛
    彭真市长观看比赛并接见日本队员
    新华社十二日讯　第一次来我国进行友好访问的日本“尼吉波”公司平野女子篮球队和日本早稻田大学男子篮球队，今天晚上在北京工人体育馆同北京男子篮球队和北京女子篮球队进行访华首次友谊比赛。比赛结果，主队男子队以一○六比五十一获胜，女子队以六十一比六十四失利。
    北京市市长彭真同首都一万五千多人观看了今晚的比赛。比赛结束后，彭真市长接见了日本男女篮球代表团团长牧山圭秀，并且到运动场同双方运动员一一握手。（附图片）
    三月十二日，北京市市长彭真观看中日男女篮球赛以后，和双方运动员合影。　　　　　　新华社记者　王景英摄
