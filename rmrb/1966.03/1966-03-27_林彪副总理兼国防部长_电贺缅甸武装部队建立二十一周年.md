Title: Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense Lin Biao Sends Telegram to Congratulate the 21st Anniversary of the Founding of the Burmese Armed Forces
Author:
Date: 1966-03-27
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, 26th. Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense Lin Biao today sent a telegram to General Ne Win, Chairman of the Revolutionary Council and Minister of Defense of the Union of Burma, congratulating the 21st anniversary of the founding of the Armed Forces of the Union of Burma. The telegram is as follows:
    To His Excellency General Ne Win, Chairman of the Revolutionary Council and Minister of Defense of the Union of Burma:
    On the occasion of the 21st anniversary of the founding of the Armed Forces of the Union of Burma, I, on behalf of all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, extend warm congratulations to all officers and soldiers of the Armed Forces of the Union of Burma.
    I wish that the traditional friendship between the peoples of China and Burma will be increasingly consolidated and developed, and I wish Your Excellency good health.
          Lin Biao
      Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China
                    March 26, 1966

### 林彪副总理兼国防部长  电贺缅甸武装部队建立二十一周年

1966-03-27
第3版()
专栏：

    林彪副总理兼国防部长
    电贺缅甸武装部队建立二十一周年
    新华社二十六日讯　国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪今天打电报给缅甸联邦革命委员会主席兼国防部长奈温将军，祝贺缅甸联邦武装部队建立二十一周年。电文如下：缅甸联邦革命委员会主席兼国防部长奈温将军尊敬的将军阁下：
    在缅甸联邦武装部队建立二十一周年的时候，我谨代表中国人民解放军全体指战员，向缅甸联邦武装部队全体官兵，致以热烈的祝贺。
    祝中缅两国人民的传统友谊日益巩固和发展，并祝阁下身体健康。
      中华人民共和国国务院
        副总理兼国防部长　林　彪
                一九六六年三月二十六日
