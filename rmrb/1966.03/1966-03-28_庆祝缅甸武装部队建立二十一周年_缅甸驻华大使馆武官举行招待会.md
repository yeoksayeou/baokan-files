Title: Reception Held by Military Attaché of the Burmese Embassy in China to Celebrate the 21st Anniversary of the Founding of the Burmese Armed Forces
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-28
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, March 27th – Colonel Maung Kyi, Military Attaché of the Embassy of the Union of Burma in China, held a reception this evening to celebrate the 21st anniversary of the founding of the Burmese Armed Forces.
    Xu Guangda, Vice Minister of National Defense, Zhang Aiping, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and others were invited to attend the reception.
    Colonel Maung Kyi and Zhang Aiping proposed toasts at the reception. They proposed a toast to the friendship between the peoples and the armies of China and Burma. The Burmese Ambassador to China, Sama Duwa Sinwa Nawng, diplomatic envoys and military attachés from various countries to China attended the reception.

### 庆祝缅甸武装部队建立二十一周年  缅甸驻华大使馆武官举行招待会

1966-03-28
第3版()
专栏：

    庆祝缅甸武装部队建立二十一周年
    缅甸驻华大使馆武官举行招待会
    新华社二十七日讯  缅甸联邦驻中国大使馆武官貌基上校今晚举行招待会，庆祝缅甸武装部队建立二十一周年。
    国防部副部长许光达，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张爱萍等，应邀出席了招待会。
    貌基上校和张爱萍在招待会上先后祝酒。他们提议，为中缅两国人民和两国军队之间的友谊干杯。缅甸驻中国大使沙马杜瓦·信瓦瑙，各国驻中国使节和武官，出席了招待会。
