Title: Comrade Liu Shaoqi Meets with Comrades Wilcox and Others and Hosts a Banquet for Comrade Xin

Author:

Date: 1966-03-08
Edition: 1

    Liu Shaoqi Meets with Comrades Wilcox and Others
    and Hosts a Banquet for Comrade Xin
    Xinhua News Agency, 7th March: Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, met this afternoon with the New Zealand Communist Party delegation headed by V.J. Wilcox, General Secretary of the New Zealand Communist Party, and delegation member A. Ostler. They had a cordial and friendly conversation.
    Present at the meeting were Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, as well as Comrades Peng Zhen, Kang Sheng, Liu Ningyi, Zhao Yimin, and others.
    After the meeting, Comrade Liu Shaoqi hosted a banquet in honor of Comrade Wilcox and Comrade Ostler.
    In the evening, the Ministry of Culture held a cultural performance to welcome the New Zealand Communist Party delegation. (Attached Picture)

### 刘少奇同志会见威尔科克斯等同志  并在会见后设宴招待新共同志

1966-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇同志会见威尔科克斯等同志
    并在会见后设宴招待新共同志
    新华社七日讯　中共中央副主席刘少奇今天下午会见了新西兰共产党代表团团长、新西兰共产党总书记维·乔·威尔科克斯和代表团团员亚·奥斯特勒。双方进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    会见时在座的，有中共中央总书记邓小平，以及彭真、康生、刘宁一、赵毅敏等同志。
    会见以后，刘少奇同志设宴招待威尔科克斯同志和奥斯特勒同志。
    晚上，文化部举行文艺晚会，欢迎新西兰共产党代表团。（附图片）
    刘少奇同志设宴招待新共同志。左起：维·乔·威尔科克斯、刘少奇、亚·奥斯特勒、邓小平。
      新华社记者　刘长忠摄
