Title: Chairman Liu, Premier Zhou Send Congratulations on Morocco's National Day
Author:
Date: 1966-03-03
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, February 2nd - Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council, sent a telegram on the 2nd to King Hassan II of Morocco, congratulating the Kingdom of Morocco on its National Day. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Majesty King Hassan II of Morocco, Rabat:
    On the occasion of the National Day of the Kingdom of Morocco, we extend our heartfelt congratulations and best wishes to Your Majesty and the Moroccan people. We wish your people happiness and your country prosperity. We wish that the friendly and cooperative relations between China and Morocco will be consolidated and developed on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence.
      Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                              Liu Shaoqi
      Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                              Zhou Enlai
                March 2, 1966

### 刘主席  周总理  电贺摩洛哥国庆

1966-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席  周总理
    电贺摩洛哥国庆
    新华社二日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇和国务院总理周恩来二日打电报给摩洛哥国王哈桑二世，祝贺摩洛哥王国国庆。电文如下：拉巴特摩洛哥国王哈桑二世陛下：
    在摩洛哥王国国庆的时候，我们谨向陛下和摩洛哥人民致以衷心的祝贺和良好的祝愿。祝贵国人民幸福，国家昌盛。祝中国和摩洛哥两国的友好合作关系在和平共处五项原则的基础上获得巩固和发展。
      中华人民共和国主席
                              刘少奇
      中华人民共和国国务院总理
                              周恩来
                一九六六年三月二日
