Title: Dong He Breaks National Women's Javelin Record
Author:
Date: 1966-03-30
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, March 28th: In an excellent track and field athletes competition held in Beijing today, Anhui female athlete Dong He achieved a javelin throw of 52.13 meters, breaking her own national women's javelin record of 50.51 meters set in April of last year.
    This is the first national record broken by Chinese track and field athletes this year.
    In today's competition, Fujian's famous male high jumper Ni Zhiqin cleared a height of 2.23 meters, approaching his national record of 2.25 meters set in November of last year. No other athlete in the world has cleared this height so far this year. On March 1st, Ni Zhiqin also cleared a height of 2.20 meters in another competition.

### 董荷打破女子标枪全国纪录

1966-03-30
第2版()
专栏：

    董荷打破女子标枪全国纪录
    新华社二十八日讯 今天在北京举行的一次优秀田径运动员比赛中，安徽女选手董荷掷标枪的成绩达到了五十二米十三，刷新了她本人在去年四月间创造的五十米五一的女子标枪掷远全国纪录。
    这是我国田径选手今年以来打破的第一项全国纪录。
    在今天的比赛中，福建著名男子跳高选手倪志钦跳过了二米二三的高度，接近了他去年十一月间创造的二米二五的全国纪录。今年以来，世界上还没有另外的运动员能跳过这个高度。三月一日，倪志钦在另一次比赛中曾经跳过二米二十的高度。
