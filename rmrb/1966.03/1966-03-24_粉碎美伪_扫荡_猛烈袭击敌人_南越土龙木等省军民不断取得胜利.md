Title: Crushing the US-Puppet "Mopping-Up" Operations, Fiercely Attacking the Enemy: The Military and Civilians of Thu Dau Mot and Other Provinces in South Vietnam Continuously Achieve Victories
Author:
Date: 1966-03-24
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi Dispatch: According to the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency, the military and civilians of Thu Dau Mot, Sa Dec, Thua Thien, Gia Lai and other provinces in South Vietnam have recently achieved new victories in combat.

    On March 5, the South Vietnam Liberation Armed Forces launched a fierce attack on the enemy in the Binh Long military sub-district in the northern part of Thu Dau Mot province, destroying 60 enemy barracks and an office of the puppet regime, killing and wounding more than 250 enemies.

    On the night of March 9, the guerrillas of Thu Dau Mot province attacked the enemy in Thu Dau Mot city, destroying many offices of the puppet regime and eliminating 156 enemies, including 101 killed. Among the killed enemies were two lieutenant-level officers of the South Vietnamese puppet army and a lieutenant of the Australian accomplice army.

    On the night of March 16, the Sa Dec province Liberation Armed Forces launched a surprise attack on the enemy's Tan Dinh Airport, located about 100 kilometers southwest of Saigon, wiping out one puppet army company, routing one platoon, destroying one enemy L-19 reconnaissance plane, and capturing many weapons.

    From February 25 to 28, the military and civilians of Phu Vang District, Thua Thien Province, crushed three large-scale "mopping-up" operations by the US and puppet troops, inflicting heavy casualties on the enemy. In the fighting on February 25 alone, 233 enemies were killed and wounded. On February 27, the Liberation Armed Forces intercepted and crushed a "mopping-up" operation by a battalion of enemy troops, killing and wounding a large number of enemies. After the battle, the enemy used fifteen helicopters to carry the corpses.

    On February 14, the Gia Lai province Liberation Armed Forces conducted a heroic counter-"mopping-up" battle in an area 11 kilometers northwest of Pleiku. In just 20 minutes, they eliminated 67 enemy troops. On the night of February 15, the Liberation Armed Forces of this province fiercely bombarded the enemy's Tan Lac stronghold with mortars, killing more than 100 enemy troops.

### 粉碎美伪“扫荡”　猛烈袭击敌人  南越土龙木等省军民不断取得胜利

1966-03-24
第3版()
专栏：

    粉碎美伪“扫荡”　猛烈袭击敌人
    南越土龙木等省军民不断取得胜利
    据新华社河内电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方土龙木、沙沥、承天、嘉莱等省军民最近在战斗中，不断取得新的胜利。
    越南南方解放武装力量三月五日对土龙木省北部的平隆军事小区的敌人发动猛烈进攻，炸毁敌军营房六十间和伪政权办公处一所，打死、打伤敌人二百五十多名。
    三月九日夜间，土龙木省游击队袭击土龙木市的敌军，炸毁伪政权的多处办事处，歼灭敌人一百五十六名，其中打死一百零一名。在打死的敌人中，有南越伪军尉级军官两名和澳大利亚帮凶军少尉一名。
    沙沥省解放武装力量三月十六日夜间奇袭位于西贡西南约一百公里的敌军新迪机场，歼灭伪军一个连，击溃一个排，炸毁敌L—19型侦察机一架，缴获武器多件。
    承天省富汪县军民从二月二十五日到二十八日粉碎了美伪军三次大规模“扫荡”，使敌人伤亡惨重。仅在二月二十五日一天的战斗中，就打死、打伤敌人二百三十三名。二月二十七日，解放武装力量又截击和粉碎了一个营的敌军的“扫荡”，大量杀伤敌人。敌人在战斗结束后，用了十五架直升飞机来载运死尸。
    嘉莱省解放武装力量二月十四日在波来梅西北十一公里的地区进行英勇的反“扫荡”战斗。他们只用了二十分钟的时间，就歼灭敌军六十七名。二月十五日晚上，这个省的解放武装力量又用迫击炮猛烈轰击敌人的新乐据点，打死敌人一百多名。
