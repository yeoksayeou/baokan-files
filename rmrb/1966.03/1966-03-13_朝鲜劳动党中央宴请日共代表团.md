Title: Korean Workers' Party Central Committee Hosts Banquet for JCP Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-13
Edition: 3

    New China News Agency, Pyongyang, March 11th - The Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party hosted a banquet on the evening of the 11th to entertain the delegation of the Japanese Communist Party.

    Attending the banquet were all members of the delegation led by Kenji Miyamoto, General Secretary of the Central Committee and Standing Committee member of the Political Bureau of the Japanese Communist Party.

    Attending the banquet were Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party; Choi Yong-kun, Park Kum-chol, Ri Hyo-sun and Kim Kwang-hyup, members and vice-chairmen of the Political Committee of the Korean Workers' Party; and Ri Ju-yeon, member of the Political Committee.

    The banquet proceeded in an atmosphere filled with comradely friendship and fraternal affection.

### 朝鲜劳动党中央宴请日共代表团

1966-03-13
第3版()
专栏：

    朝鲜劳动党中央宴请日共代表团
    新华社平壤十一日电　朝鲜劳动党中央委员会十一日晚设宴招待日本共产党代表团。
    出席宴会的有由日本共产党中央委员会总书记、政治局常务委员宫本显治率领的代表团的全体成员。
    出席宴会的有朝鲜劳动党中央委员会委员长金日成；朝鲜劳动党政治委员会委员、副委员长崔庸健、朴金喆、李孝淳和金光侠和政治委员会委员李周渊等。
    宴会在洋溢着同志般的友谊和兄弟情谊的气氛中进行。
