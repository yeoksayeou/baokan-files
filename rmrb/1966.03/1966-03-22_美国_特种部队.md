Title: U.S. "Special Forces"
Author:
Date: 1966-03-22
Edition: 4

    The American imperialism has specially established so-called "Special Forces" to suppress the national liberation movements in Asia, Africa and Latin America. Members of the U.S. "Special Forces" receive specialized training to carry out suppression, sabotage, and espionage activities.
    Currently, the U.S. "Special Forces" have 10,000 personnel, organized into seven battalions, stationed in South Vietnam, Okinawa, Panama, West Germany, and the U.S. mainland.
    In South Vietnam, the U.S. "Special Forces" have at least 1,500 personnel. On the one hand, they infiltrate the liberated areas to carry out harassment; on the other hand, they conduct espionage activities in enemy-occupied areas or in North Vietnam. At the same time, they act as "advisors," training the puppet South Vietnamese authorities' special forces, or stationed on the borders of Laos, Cambodia, and other areas bordering enemy occupied territories, to conduct "surveillance" and "blockade" of the liberated areas. The U.S. "Special Forces" operate in teams (generally of twelve people), sometimes incorporating puppet troops.
    The "Special Forces" are also constantly attacked by the South Vietnamese People's Armed Forces. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 美国“特种部队”

1966-03-22
第4版()
专栏：资料

    美国“特种部队”
    美帝国主义为镇压亚洲、非洲和拉丁美洲的民族解放运动，专门成立有所谓
  “特种部队”。美国“特种部队”的成员受过专门训练，进行镇压、破坏和特务活动。
    现在美国“特种部队”已有一万人，编为七个大队，分驻南越、冲绳、巴拿马、西德和美国本土等地。
    在南越的美国“特种部队”，至少已有一千五百多人。它一方面潜入解放区进行骚扰，另一方面在敌占区或到越南北方进行特务活动。同时，它还充当“顾问”，训练南越傀儡当局的特务部队，或者，驻在老挝、柬埔寨等边境和敌占区边境，对解放区进行“监视”与“封锁”。美国“特种部队”以小组（一般为十二人）为单位，有时也把伪军编在一起。
  “特种部队”也不断遭到南越人民武装的打击。　（新华社）
