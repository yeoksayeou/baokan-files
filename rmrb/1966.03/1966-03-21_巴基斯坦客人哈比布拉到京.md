Title: Pakistani Guest Habibullah Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-03-21
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, March 20th: Habibullah, former Vice Chairman of the Karachi Municipal Committee of Pakistan, arrived in Beijing by plane last night at the invitation of our country.

### 巴基斯坦客人哈比布拉到京

1966-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦客人哈比布拉到京
    据新华社二十日讯　应邀来我国访问的巴基斯坦前卡拉奇市政委员会副主席哈比布拉，昨晚乘飞机到达北京。
