Title: Inner Mongolia Power Machinery Plant Completed and Put into Production
Author:
Date: 1966-03-24
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, March 23rd. Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region's first factory specializing in the production of diesel engines—the Inner Mongolia Power Machinery Plant—has recently been completed and put into production in Hohhot City.
    The first batch of "1105 Type" diesel engines produced by this factory meet the standards stipulated by the state in terms of performance and the quality of parts. This type of diesel engine is simple to operate, has stable speed, compact structure, is sturdy and durable, and is suitable for power generation, irrigation, threshing, and grain and grass processing in rural and pastoral areas.

### 内蒙古动力机厂建成投入生产

1966-03-24
第2版()
专栏：

    内蒙古动力机厂建成投入生产
    新华社呼和浩特二十三日电　内蒙古自治区第一座专门生产柴油机的工厂——内蒙古动力机厂，最近在呼和浩特市建成并投入生产。
    这个工厂首批生产的“一一零五型”柴油机，性能和零件部件的质量，都符合国家规定的标准。这种柴油机操作简便，转速稳定，结构紧凑，坚固耐用，适合农村牧区发电、排灌、脱粒和粮草加工作动力用。
