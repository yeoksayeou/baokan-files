Title: Vice President of the All-China Women's Federation Hosts Banquet for Chilean Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-03-29
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 28th – Zhang Yun, Vice President of the All-China Women's Federation, hosted a banquet tonight in honor of the Chilean Women's Delegation, led by Mrs. Carmen Lasso Carrera, member of the Chamber of Deputies of Chile. Present as hosts were Luo Shuzhang, Xia Zhixu, Cao Mengjun, Zeng Xianzhi, Yang Yunyu, and others. The Chilean Women's Delegation arrived in Beijing by plane yesterday.

### 我妇联副主席宴请智利客人

1966-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

    我妇联副主席宴请智利客人
    新华社二十八日讯  全国妇联副主席章蕴今晚设宴招待由智利众议员卡门·拉索·卡雷拉夫人率领的智利妇女代表团。出席作陪的有罗叔章、夏之栩、曹孟君、曾宪植、杨蕴玉等。智利妇女代表团是在昨日乘飞机到京的。
