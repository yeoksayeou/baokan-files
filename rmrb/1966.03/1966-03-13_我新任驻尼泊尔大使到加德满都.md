Title: My Newly Appointed Ambassador to Nepal Arrives in Kathmandu
Author:
Date: 1966-03-13
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, March 11th: Yang Gongsu, the newly appointed Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Nepal, arrived here by plane on the afternoon of the 11th.
    Those who went to the airport to welcome him included: Vice Chairman of the National Council, Nar K. Pradhan; Military Secretary Assistant to King Mahendra, Brigadier General Jutra Bahadur; Protocol Officer of the Nepalese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Tagdish Rana; President of the Nepal-China Friendship Association, Purna Bahadur; as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Nepal.
    Officials from the Chinese Embassy and Chinese experts in Nepal also went to the airport to welcome him.

### 我新任驻尼泊尔大使到加德满都

1966-03-13
第4版()
专栏：

    我新任驻尼泊尔大使到加德满都
    新华社加德满都十一日电　新任中华人民共和国驻尼泊尔大使杨公素十一日下午乘飞机到达这里。
    前往机场欢迎的有：全国评议会副议长纳·库·普拉丹；马亨德拉国王助理军事秘书朱特拉·巴哈杜尔准将；尼泊尔外交部礼宾官塔格迪什·拉纳；尼中友好协会会长普尔纳·巴哈杜尔以及各国驻尼泊尔外交使节。
    中国大使馆官员和在尼泊尔的中国专家也到机场欢迎。
