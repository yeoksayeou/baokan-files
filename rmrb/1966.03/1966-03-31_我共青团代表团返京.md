Title: Our Communist Youth League Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-03-31
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, March 30th. The Chinese Communist Youth League Delegation, led by Wang Daoyi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, returned to Beijing today after attending the Eighth Congress of the Romanian Communist Youth League in Romania. Wang Zhaohua, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and others greeted them at the airport. Also at the airport to greet them was Mr. Betrelescu, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Romanian Embassy in China.

### 我共青团代表团返京

1966-03-31
第2版()
专栏：

    我共青团代表团返京
    新华社三十日讯 由共青团中央书记处书记王道义率领的中国共青团代表团在罗马尼亚参加罗马尼亚共青团第八次代表大会后，今天回到北京。团中央书记处书记王照华等到机场迎接。到机场迎接的还有罗马尼亚驻华大使馆临时代办贝特勒斯库。
