Title: National People's Congress Standing Committee Meeting Passes Decision to Pardon War Criminals Who Have Genuinely Turned Over a New Leaf

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-30
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 29th. The Standing Committee of the Third National People's Congress held its 29th meeting. Based on the suggestion of the State Council, the meeting discussed and approved the decision regarding the special pardon for war criminals from the Chiang Kai-shek Group, the puppet Manchukuo, and the puppet Mengjiang Autonomous Government who have genuinely turned over a new leaf.
    The meeting also passed resolutions approving the Organizational Regulations for the People's Congresses and People's Committees at all levels of the Hainan Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture in Qinghai Province, the Organizational Regulations for the People's Congresses and People's Committees at all levels of the Kizilsu Kirghiz Autonomous Prefecture in Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, and the Organizational Regulations for the People's Congress and People's Committee of the Bayingol Mongolian Autonomous Prefecture in the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region.

### 全国人大常委会会议通过决定  特赦确实改恶从善的战争罪犯

1966-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人大常委会会议通过决定
    特赦确实改恶从善的战争罪犯
    新华社二十九日讯  第三届全国人民代表大会常务委员会举行第二十九次会议，会议根据国务院的建议，讨论通过关于特赦确实改恶从善的蒋介石集团、伪满洲国和伪蒙疆自治政府的战争罪犯的决定。
    会议还通过决议，批准青海省海南藏族自治州各级人民代表大会和各级人民委员会组织条例、新疆维吾尔自治区克孜勒苏柯尔克孜自治州各级人民代表大会和各级人民委员会组织条例、新疆维吾尔自治区巴音郭楞蒙古自治州人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例。
