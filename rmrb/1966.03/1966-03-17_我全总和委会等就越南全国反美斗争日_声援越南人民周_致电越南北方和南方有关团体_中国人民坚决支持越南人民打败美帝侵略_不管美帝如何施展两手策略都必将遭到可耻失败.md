Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions, China Peace Committee and others Send Telegrams to Relevant Groups in North and South Vietnam on Vietnam National Anti-American Struggle Day "Week of Solidarity with the Vietnamese People": Chinese People Firmly Support the Vietnamese People in Defeating American Imperialist Aggression; No Matter How American Imperialism Employs Two-Pronged Tactics, it Will Inevitably Meet With Shameful Failure
Author:
Date: 1966-03-17
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, 16th dispatch: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity sent telegrams on the 15th to relevant groups in Vietnam and South Vietnam on the 16th anniversary of the "March 19th" Vietnam National Anti-American Struggle Day and the "Week of Solidarity with the Vietnamese People," expressing firm support for the Vietnamese people's just struggle against American imperialist aggression.
    In a telegram to the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor and the South Vietnam Liberation Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions warmly hailed the significant victories achieved by the military and civilians of South and North Vietnam in their struggle against American imperialist aggression. The telegram stated that the victory of the Vietnamese people has further exposed the true face of American imperialism as a paper tiger to the oppressed people of the world, demonstrated the unparalleled power of people's war, greatly inspired their fighting spirit, and made a tremendous contribution to the anti-imperialist revolutionary struggles of the peoples of Asia, Africa, and Latin America.
    The telegram pointed out that Chinese workers firmly support the Vietnamese people's determination to fight the war of resistance to the end and are prepared for all possible expansions of the war of aggression by American imperialism. Under any circumstances, Chinese workers will resolutely and unhesitatingly stand with the Vietnamese people and fight to the end against the common enemy, American imperialism.
    The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity jointly sent a telegram on the 15th to the Vietnam Committee for World Peace, the Vietnam Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Committee, the South Vietnam Committee for World Peace, the South Vietnam Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Committee, and the Permanent Bureau of the International Conference for Solidarity with the Vietnamese People Against American Imperialist Aggression and for the Defense of Peace, extending the most lofty and fraternal respect to the Vietnamese people fighting on the front lines of the anti-American struggle.
    The telegram praised the Vietnamese people for setting a brilliant example for the anti-imperialist struggles of oppressed nations and oppressed peoples around the world through their heroic struggle. The telegram also pointed out that the victory of the people of North and South Vietnam against American imperialist aggression vividly demonstrates that American imperialism can be completely defeated. People's war is the most effective weapon to deal with American robbers. The telegram also wished the Vietnamese people new and greater victories.
    The telegram said that at present, American imperialism is intensifying its war of aggression against Vietnam under the smokescreen of "peace talks" and is preparing to extend the flames of war to Laos, Cambodia, and China. The Johnson administration has once again exposed its own "peace" facade, and also exposed the ugly faces of all kinds of accomplices who are actively peddling the "peace talks" fraud for American imperialism. The telegram expressed firm belief that no matter how American imperialism employs its two-pronged tactics of intensifying war and peace deception, and no matter how its running dogs and partners serve it, it will meet with a shameful failure, and final victory will surely belong to the heroic Vietnamese people who dare to fight and are good at fighting.

### 我全总和委会等就越南全国反美斗争日“声援越南人民周”致电越南北方和南方有关团体  中国人民坚决支持越南人民打败美帝侵略  不管美帝如何施展两手策略都必将遭到可耻失败

1966-03-17
第3版()
专栏：

    我全总和委会等就越南全国反美斗争日“声援越南人民周”致电越南北方和南方有关团体
    中国人民坚决支持越南人民打败美帝侵略
    不管美帝如何施展两手策略都必将遭到可耻失败
    新华社十六日讯　中华全国总工会和中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国亚非团结委员会，分别在十五日就“三·一九”越南全国反美斗争日十六周年和“声援越南人民周”致电越南和越南南方的有关团体，对越南人民反对美帝国主义侵略的正义斗争表示坚决的支持。
    中华全国总工会在致越南总工会和越南南方解放工会联合会的电报中，热烈欢呼越南南、北方军民在反对美帝国主义侵略的斗争中所取得的重大胜利。电报说，越南人民的胜利使全世界被压迫人民进一步看清了美帝国主义纸老虎的真面目，看到了人民战争的无比威力，大大鼓舞了他们的斗志，对亚、非、拉各国人民的反帝革命斗争作出了巨大的贡献。
    电报指出，中国工人坚决支持越南人民抗战到底的决心，并且对美帝国主义扩大侵略战争的各种可能都作了准备。在任何情况下，中国工人都坚决地、毫不犹豫地和越南人民站在一起，为反对共同敌人美帝国主义而战斗到底。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和中国亚非团结委员会，在十五日联名打电报给越南保卫世界和平委员会、越南亚非人民团结委员会、越南南方保卫世界和平委员会、越南南方亚非人民团结委员会以及声援越南人民反对美帝国主义侵略、保卫和平国际会议常设局，向战斗在反美斗争最前线的越南人民致最崇高的、兄弟般的敬意。
    电报赞扬越南人民以自己英勇的斗争，为全世界被压迫民族和被压迫人民的反帝斗争树立了光辉的榜样。电报还指出，越南南北方人民反对美帝国主义侵略的胜利生动地表明，美帝国主义是完全可以打败的。人民战争是对付美国强盗最有效的法宝。电报中还祝越南人民取得新的、更大的胜利。
    电报说，目前，美帝国主义正在“和谈”烟幕下加紧扩大侵越战争，并且准备把战火烧到老挝、柬埔寨和中国。约翰逊政府再一次揭穿了它自己的“和平”假面具，也暴露了那些积极为美帝国主义兜售“和谈”骗局的形形色色帮凶者的丑恶面目。电报表示坚信，不管美帝国主义如何施展强化战争和和平欺骗的两手策略，也不管它的走狗和合伙者如何为它效劳，都将遭到可耻的失败，最后的胜利一定属于敢于斗争、善于斗争的英雄的越南人民。
