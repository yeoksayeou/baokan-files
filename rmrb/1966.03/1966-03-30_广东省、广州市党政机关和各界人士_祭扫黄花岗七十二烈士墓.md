Title: Guangdong Province, Guangzhou City Party and Government Organs and People from All Walks of Life Pay Respects at the Tomb of the Seventy-Two Martyrs of Huanghuagang
Author:
Date: 1966-03-30
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, March 29th -- Today, despite the rain, people from all walks of life in Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City paid their respects at the Tomb of the Seventy-Two Martyrs of Huanghuagang.
    Party and government leading organs of Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City, the Guangdong Provincial and Guangzhou Municipal Committees of the CPPCC, and the local organizations of various democratic parties in Guangdong presented wreaths at the Martyrs' Tomb. During the memorial ceremony, Yao Yuping, who participated in the uprising and is now the Director of the Guangdong Provincial Counselor's Office, presented a wreath on behalf of all those attending the tomb sweeping.
    Among those who participated in today's memorial activities were Xu Chongqing and Deng Wenzhao, Vice Governors of Guangdong Province; Luo Peiyuan, Minister of the United Front Work Department of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Mayor of Guangzhou; Zhang Luchun, Feng Shen, Tan Tiandu, Xiao Junying, and Yi Meihou, Vice Chairmen of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the CPPCC; as well as responsible persons of local organizations of various democratic parties and relatives of the martyrs, totaling more than one hundred people.

### 广东省、广州市党政机关和各界人士  祭扫黄花岗七十二烈士墓

1966-03-30
第2版()
专栏：

    广东省、广州市党政机关和各界人士
    祭扫黄花岗七十二烈士墓
    新华社广州二十九日电  广东省、广州市各界人士今天上午冒雨祭扫了黄花岗七十二烈士墓。
    广东省、广州市党和政府领导机关，政协广东省、广州市委员会，以及各民主党派广东省地方组织在烈士墓前献了花圈。在举行纪念仪式时，当年起义参加者、现任广东省参事室主任姚雨平代表全体参加扫墓的人员献了花圈。
    参加今天纪念活动的，有广东省副省长许崇清、邓文钊，中共广州市委统战部部长、广州市副市长罗培元，政协广东省委员会副主席张醁村、冯燊、谭天度、萧隽英、蚁美厚，以及各民主党派地方组织负责人和烈士家属等一百多人。
