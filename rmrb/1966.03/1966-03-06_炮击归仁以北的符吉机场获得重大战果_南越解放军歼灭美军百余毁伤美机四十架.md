Title: Significant Victory Achieved in Bombardment of Phu Cat Airfield North of Qui Nhon: South Vietnamese Liberation Army Annihilates Over a Hundred American Troops and Destroys or Damages Forty American Aircraft

Author:
Date: 1966-03-06
Edition: 4

    Heavy Toll Inflicted in Bombardment of Phu Cat Airfield North of Qui Nhon
    South Vietnamese Liberation Army Annihilates Over a Hundred American Troops and Destroys or Damages Forty American Aircraft
    Guerrillas in Binh Dinh and Phu Yen Provinces Crush Enemy "Sweeps," Eliminating Over 700 Enemy Troops
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 5th (via cable) – According to supplementary reports from the South Vietnam Liberation Press Agency, the South Vietnamese Liberation Armed Forces, in battles fought on February 2nd and 21st in Binh Dinh and Binh Thuan provinces respectively, eliminated a total of over 400 enemy troops, destroyed 30 American helicopters, and shot down two American jet fighters.

    In the battle of February 2nd, when the South Vietnamese Liberation Armed Forces bombarded the Phu Cat airfield north of Qui Nhon in Binh Dinh province, they destroyed 30 American helicopters, damaged about 10, killed or wounded 111 American aggressor troops, and destroyed five enemy military trucks.

    In the ambush conducted by the Binh Thuan Provincial Liberation Armed Forces on February 21st on the highway between Phan Thiet and Ma Lam, they killed, wounded, or captured a total of 316 enemy troops, seized over 100 various weapons, shot down two American jet fighters, burned five enemy military vehicles, and destroyed two iron bridges.

    The report stated that on February 21st, the enemy launched two columns of troops to conduct "sweeps" along the highway between Phan Thiet and Ma Lam. One of these columns, consisting of a puppet security company and five additional platoons, encountered fierce resistance from the Liberation Armed Forces 12 kilometers from Ma Lam, and approximately 100 were killed or wounded on the spot. Subsequently, the Liberation Armed Forces seized the opportunity to pursue the fleeing enemy, eliminating many more. Furthermore, they routed the enemy entrenched in the "strategic hamlet" connecting Ma Lam to Phan Thiet.

    Another column, consisting of a puppet security company and four platoons under the command of four American aggressors, launched a "sweep" from Phan Thiet and also encountered resistance from the Liberation Armed Forces 6 kilometers from Phan Thiet, where half were wiped out.

    The Liberation Armed Forces also ambushed another enemy force coming from Phan Thiet to provide reinforcements, inflicting heavy casualties on them.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 5th (via cable) – According to a report by the South Vietnam Liberation Press Agency, guerrillas in Binh Dinh and Phu Yen provinces in South Vietnam crushed multiple enemy "sweeps" in February, dealing heavy blows to the U.S.-puppet troops and South Korean mercenary troops, eliminating over 700 enemy troops in total.

    Guerrillas in various locations in Binh Dinh province eliminated 510 enemy troops in the first ten days of February, including 228 American aggressor troops and South Korean mercenary troops.

    Guerrillas in various locations in Phu Yen province eliminated over 200 enemy troops in the anti-"sweep" battles in February, including 20 American aggressor troops and 146 South Korean mercenary troops. In addition, guerrillas in the two provinces shot down two American helicopters and destroyed four military vehicles.

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi (via cable) – According to a report from the South Vietnam Liberation Press Agency, the Saigon City Liberation Armed Forces in South Vietnam, with the assistance of the local people, successfully used explosives to blow up the South Vietnamese puppet army's General Staff Headquarters near Tan Son Nhut Airport at 12:00 noon on February 17th.

    According to preliminary reports, 130 enemy troops were killed or wounded, including 30 American aggressors.

    After successfully completing their mission, the Liberation Armed Forces have safely returned to their base areas.

### 炮击归仁以北的符吉机场获得重大战果  南越解放军歼灭美军百余毁伤美机四十架

1966-03-06
第4版()
专栏：

    炮击归仁以北的符吉机场获得重大战果
    南越解放军歼灭美军百余毁伤美机四十架
    平定富安两省游击队粉碎敌人“扫荡”歼敌七百余名
    新华社河内五日电　据越南南方解放通讯社补充报道，越南南方解放武装力量二月二日和二十一日先后在平定省和平顺省进行的战斗中，共歼灭敌人四百多名，击毁美国直升飞机三十架，击落美国喷气式战斗机两架。
    越南南方解放武装力量二月二日晚在炮击平定省归仁以北符吉机场的战斗中，共击毁美国直升飞机三十架，击伤十架左右，打死、打伤美国侵略军一百十一名，击毁敌军用卡车五辆。
    平顺省解放武装力量二月二十一日在藩切和麻林之间的公路上进行的阻击战中，共打死、打伤和俘虏敌人三百十六名，缴获各种武器一百多件，击落美国喷气式战斗机两架，烧毁敌军车五辆，炸毁铁桥二座。
    报道说，二月二十一日，敌人分两路出动，向藩切和麻林之间的公路沿线一带进行“扫荡”。其中一股包括一个伪保安连和另外五个排的敌军在离麻林十二公里的地方遭到解放武装力量的猛烈阻击，当场被打死、打伤一百名左右。接着，解放武装力量还乘胜追击逃敌，消灭不少敌人。并且击溃了据守在麻林通往藩切“战略村”的敌人。
    另一股一个伪保安连和四个排的敌军在四名美国侵略者的指挥下，从藩切出动“扫荡”，在离藩切六公里的地方也遭到解放武装力量的阻击，被歼灭了一半。
    解放武装力量还阻击了由藩切开来救援的另一股敌军，使他们受到重大伤亡。
    新华社河内五日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方平定和富安两省游击队二月份粉碎了敌人的多次“扫荡”，给了美伪军和南朝鲜帮凶军以沉重的打击，共歼敌七百多名。
    平定省各地游击队在二月份的头十天中就歼灭敌人五百一十名，其中包括美国侵略军和南朝鲜帮凶军二百二十八名。
    富安省各地游击队在二月份的反“扫荡”战斗中，共歼灭敌人二百多名，其中包括美国侵略军二十名、南朝鲜帮凶军一百四十六名。此外，两省游击队还击落美国直升飞机二架，摧毁军车四辆。
    新华社河内电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方西贡市解放武装力量在当地人民的协助下，在二月十七日中午十二时用炸药成功地爆炸了新山一机场附近的南越伪军总参谋部。
    据初步消息，炸死、炸伤敌人一百三十名，其中有美国侵略者三十名。
    解放武装力量在胜利完成任务后，已安全回到根据地。
