Title: A Philippine Reporter Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-03-29
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 27th: Antonio Araneta, a reporter from the Philippine weekly "Free Press," and his wife arrived in Beijing on March 27th for a visit at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association.

### 菲律宾一记者到京

1966-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

    菲律宾一记者到京
    新华社二十七日讯  菲律宾《写实》周刊记者安东尼奥·阿兰尼塔和夫人，应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来我国访问，于二十七日到京。
