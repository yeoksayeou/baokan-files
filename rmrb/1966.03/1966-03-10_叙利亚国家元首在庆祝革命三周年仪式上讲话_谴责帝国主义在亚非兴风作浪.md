Title: Syrian Head of State Speaks at Ceremony Celebrating Third Anniversary of Revolution Condemns Imperialism for Stirring Up Trouble in Asia and Africa
Author:
Date: 1966-03-10
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, March 8th. Syrian Head of State Nureddin al-Atassi stated on the 8th at a ceremony celebrating the third anniversary of the Syrian Revolution that we oppose the new waves of imperialism stirring up trouble in Asia and Africa, waves intended to provoke, divide, and destroy Asian and African countries.
    A mass parade and military review were held in Damascus on the morning of the 8th to celebrate the third anniversary of the Syrian Revolution. Al-Atassi and Minister of Defense Hafez al-Assad reviewed the parading troops. Before the parade, Al-Atassi delivered a speech. Speaking about the Palestinian issue, he said that we will mobilize all our strength to liberate Palestine. He said that the Syrian Revolution supports the Arab liberation movement and the struggle of the people of South Yemen against imperialism.
    Al-Atassi condemned the American aggression against Vietnam, believing that this aggression aims to interfere with, control, and dominate other countries.
    Also reviewing the parading troops were Prime Minister Yusuf Zuayyin, Secretary-General of the Provisional Regional Leadership of the Arab Ba'ath Socialist Party Salah Jadid, as well as ministers and senior military officers. Foreign diplomatic envoys stationed here also attended the review ceremony.

### 叙利亚国家元首在庆祝革命三周年仪式上讲话  谴责帝国主义在亚非兴风作浪

1966-03-10
第5版()
专栏：

    叙利亚国家元首在庆祝革命三周年仪式上讲话
    谴责帝国主义在亚非兴风作浪
    新华社大马士革八日电  叙利亚国家元首努尔丁·阿塔西八日在庆祝叙利亚革命三周年的仪式上说，我们反对帝国主义在亚洲和非洲掀起的新风浪，这种风浪是要对亚非国家挑衅，分裂和破坏它们。
    八日上午在大马士革举行了群众游行和军事检阅，庆祝叙利亚革命三周年。阿塔西和国防部长哈菲兹·阿萨德检阅了游行队伍。在游行前，阿塔西发表了讲话。他在谈到巴勒斯坦问题时说，我们将调动我们的全部力量来解放巴勒斯坦。他说，叙利亚革命支持阿拉伯解放运动，支持南也门人民反对帝国主义的斗争。
    阿塔西谴责美国对越南的侵略，认为这种侵略是为了干涉、控制和统治其他国家。
    检阅游行队伍的还有总理优素福·扎因、阿拉伯复兴社会党临时地区领导机构总书记萨拉赫·贾迪德以及部长们和高级军官。各国驻这里的外交使节也出席了检阅仪式。
