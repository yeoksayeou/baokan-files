Title: Head of Polish Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-03-08
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 7th. The head of the Polish government trade delegation, Polish Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Dmochowski, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning.
    Among those who went to the airport to welcome him were Jiang Ming, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and others. Jerzy Knothe, the Polish Ambassador to China, and members of the Polish government trade delegation who had arrived in Beijing earlier, also greeted him at the airport.

### 波兰政府贸易代表团团长到京

1966-03-08
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰政府贸易代表团团长到京
    新华社七日讯　波兰政府贸易代表团团长、波兰对外贸易部副部长德莫霍夫斯基今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的，有对外贸易部副部长江明等。波兰驻中国大使耶日·克诺泰和先期到达北京的波兰政府贸易代表团的成员也到机场迎接。
