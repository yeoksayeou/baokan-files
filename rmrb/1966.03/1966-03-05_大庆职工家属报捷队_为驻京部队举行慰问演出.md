Title: Daqing Workers' Family Reporting Team Performs a Consolation Performance for Troops Stationed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-03-05
Edition: 2

    Daqing Workers' Family Reporting Team
    Performs a Consolation Performance for Troops Stationed in Beijing
  Ye Jianying, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council, and others watched the performance
    Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Daqing Oilfield Workers' Family Reporting Team gave a consolation performance on the evening of the 3rd for commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Beijing. The drama they performed, "The Rising Sun," reflects the moving story of how the families of Daqing Oilfield workers, under the guidance of Mao Zedong Thought, gradually embarked on the road of laborization, collectivization, and revolutionization, and was warmly welcomed by the commanders and fighters.
    Ye Jianying, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council, watched the performance.
    Wang Xinting, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Liu Zhijian, Deputy Director of the General Political Department, and Liao Hansheng, head of the Beijing Troops, also watched the performance.

### 大庆职工家属报捷队  为驻京部队举行慰问演出

1966-03-05
第2版()
专栏：

    大庆职工家属报捷队
    为驻京部队举行慰问演出
  国防委员会副主席叶剑英等观看了演出
    新华社四日讯　大庆油田职工家属报捷队，三日晚上为人民解放军驻北京部队指战员举行慰问演出。她们演出的话剧《初升的太阳》，反映大庆油田职工家属在毛泽东思想指引下，逐步走上劳动化、集体化、革命化道路的动人事迹，受到指战员的热烈欢迎。
    国防委员会副主席叶剑英观看了演出。
    观看演出的还有中国人民解放军副总参谋长王新亭、总政治部副主任刘志坚、北京部队负责人廖汉生等。
