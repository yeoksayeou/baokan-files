Title: Learning from Comrade Jiao Yulu: Photography Exhibition Held in the Capital
Author:
Date: 1966-03-21
Edition: 2

    Learning from Comrade Jiao Yulu
    Photography Exhibition Held in the Capital
    Xinhua News Agency, March 20th. The “Learning from Chairman Mao’s Good Student – Comrade Jiao Yulu Photography Exhibition” opened today at Zhongshan Park in Beijing.
    The exhibition features over 110 photographs reflecting Comrade Jiao Yulu's glorious deeds in creatively studying and applying Chairman Mao's works, wholeheartedly serving the people, and the revolutionary spirit of the Lankao people. These photographs were taken by more than 30 photographers.
    The exhibition is divided into four parts. The first part, entitled "A Good Example of a County Party Secretary - Jiao Yulu," introduces Jiao Yulu's glorious deeds through photographs of his activities during his lifetime, his personal belongings, and the Lankao people's commemoration of Comrade Jiao Yulu. The second part, entitled "The Four Models Advocated and Established by Comrade Jiao Yulu," praises the revolutionary spirit of Hancun, Qinzhai, Zhao Duolou, and Shuangyangshu. The third part, "The People of Lankao Have Great Ambitions, Daring to Transform Heaven and Earth," extols the people of Lankao for holding high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, launching a new upsurge in eradicating the "three scourges," and the soaring revolutionary spirit of the Lankao people in transforming heaven and earth. The fourth part is "The People of the Whole Country Learn from Jiao Yulu."
    The exhibition is sponsored by the China Photographic Society.

### 向焦裕禄同志学习  首都举办摄影展览

1966-03-21
第2版()
专栏：

    向焦裕禄同志学习
    首都举办摄影展览
    新华社二十日讯　“向毛主席的好学生——焦裕禄同志学习摄影展览”今天在北京中山公园展出。
    这个展览展出的一百一十多幅照片，反映了焦裕禄同志活学活用毛主席著作、全心全意为人民服务的光辉事迹以及兰考人民的革命精神。这些照片是由三十多位摄影工作者拍摄的。
    展览分四个部分，第一部分以《县委书记的好榜样——焦裕禄》为题，通过焦裕禄生前的一些活动的照片和遗物，兰考人民对焦裕禄同志的怀念，介绍了焦裕禄的光辉事迹。第二部分以《焦裕禄同志倡导、树立的四个样板》为题，赞扬了韩村、秦寨、赵垛楼、双杨树的革命精神。第三部分是《兰考人民多奇志，敢教日月换新天》，歌颂了兰考人民高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，掀起了根治“三害”的新高潮，以及兰考人民改天换地的冲天革命气概。第四部分是《全国人民学习焦裕禄》。
    这个展览是由中国摄影学会举办的。
