Title: Malian and Tanzanian Newspapers Emphasize Vigilance Against Imperialist Conspiracies, African People Resolutely Repulse Imperialist Aggression
Author:
Date: 1966-03-07
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, March 6th dispatch: Bamako message: Mali's *Essor* published an editorial on March 4th, condemning the recent imperialist offensive against the independence and freedom of the African people.
    The editorial pointed out that the African people will resolutely resist this offensive. The editorial said: "To most effectively oppose imperialism, it is necessary to constantly and consciously regard imperialism and its accomplices as a force that is always ready to invade you, always attempting to harm the fruits of your labor, deprive you of your freedom, and destroy your dignity." The editorial said that imperialism is a demon that uses hypnosis in one hand while holding a butcher knife over your head, ready to kill you while you are asleep.
    The editorial reminded the people of African countries not to harbor any illusions about imperialism and called on them to repel imperialist aggression with firm determination.
    Xinhua News Agency, March 6th dispatch: Dar es Salaam message: The military coup in Ghana, orchestrated by imperialism, has aroused the anger of Tanzanian public opinion.
    The Tanganyika African National Union's *Nationalist* published an editorial on the 4th, pointing out that the Ghana coup was planned and manipulated by countries that viewed Nkrumah as a serious obstacle to their political and economic interests.
    The editorial said that the Ghana coup "warns all freedom-loving people – all Africans who cherish their own freedom and the freedom of the entire Africa – to be more vigilant than before and must not underestimate the strength of the enemies of Africa within and outside Africa."
    The newspaper also published a commentary on the 4th, pointing out that the events that have occurred in Africa and Asia in recent months show that imperialism is not launching an offensive from a single front. "They are launching offensives against us simultaneously on the economic, political, military, and international fronts. To effectively deal with them, we must be vigilant in all aspects." The commentary condemned imperialist aggression in Vietnam, the creation of chaos in Indonesia, and the orchestration of military coups in Africa.

### 马里和坦桑报纸强调警惕帝国主义阴谋  非洲人民坚决击退帝国主义侵略

1966-03-07
第3版()
专栏：

    马里和坦桑报纸强调警惕帝国主义阴谋
    非洲人民坚决击退帝国主义侵略
    新华社六日讯　巴马科消息：马里
  《发展报》三月四日发表社论，谴责帝国主义最近对非洲人民的独立和自由发动进攻。
    社论指出，非洲人民将坚决抵抗这种进攻。社论说：“要最有效地反对帝国主义，就必须经常自觉地把帝国主义及其帮手看作是时刻准备侵略你们、总是企图损害你们的劳动果实、剥夺你们的自由和破坏你们的尊严的一种势力。”社论说，帝国主义是一个恶魔，它一手向你们玩弄催眠术，一手在你们的头上举起屠刀，准备当你们睡着的时候就杀死你们。
    社论提醒非洲各国人民不要对帝国主义抱有任何幻想，并且号召他们以坚强的决心击退帝国主义的侵略。
    新华社六日讯　达累斯萨拉姆消息：帝国主义在加纳策动的军事政变激起了坦桑尼亚舆论的愤怒。
    坦噶尼喀非洲民族联盟的《民族主义者报》四日发表社论指出，加纳政变是由那些把恩克鲁玛看成是它们的政治和经济利益的严重障碍的一些国家策划和操纵的。
    社论说，加纳政变“警告所有爱好自由的人——所有珍视自己的自由和整个非洲的自由的非洲人——要比以前更加警惕，决不能低估在非洲境内外的非洲敌人的力量”。
    这家报纸四日还发表评论指出，非洲和亚洲最近几个月来发生的事件说明，帝国主义不是单单从一条战线发动攻势。“他们同时在经济、政治、军事和国际战线上向我们发动进攻，为了有效地对付他们，我们必须在各方面保持警惕。”评论谴责帝国主义在越南进行侵略，在印度尼西亚制造混乱和在非洲策动军事政变。
