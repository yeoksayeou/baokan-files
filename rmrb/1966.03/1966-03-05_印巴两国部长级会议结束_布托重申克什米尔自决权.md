Title: Indo-Pakistan Ministerial Meeting Concludes; Bhutto Reiterates Kashmir's Right to Self-Determination
Author:
Date: 1966-03-05
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi, March 3rd - The Indo-Pakistan ministerial meeting in Rawalpindi concluded on the evening of the 2nd, after two days of discussions. The joint communique of the meeting stated that the Indo-Pakistan ministerial meeting was held to "discuss matters directly related to the two countries in the further implementation of the Tashkent Declaration." The communique said, "These exploratory talks led to a beneficial exchange of views. Both sides agreed to meet again in the future."
    Pakistani Foreign Minister Bhutto, at a press conference held after seeing off the Indian delegation, reiterated Pakistan's position that "the people of Kashmir must enjoy the right to self-determination."
    The Indian representatives attending the meeting included Indian Foreign Minister Swaran Singh, Commerce Minister Shah, and Minister of Transport, Aviation, Shipping and Tourism, Reddy. The Pakistani side included Pakistani Foreign Minister Bhutto, Commerce Minister Faruque, and Minister of Communications Sabur Khan.

### 印巴两国部长级会议结束  布托重申克什米尔自决权

1966-03-05
第4版()
专栏：

    印巴两国部长级会议结束
    布托重申克什米尔自决权
    新华社拉瓦尔品第三日电　印度和巴基斯坦部长级会议在拉瓦尔品第举行了两天的会议后，二日晚已经结束。会议的联合公报说，举行印度和巴基斯坦的部长级会议是为了“讨论在进一步执行塔什干宣言方面同两国直接有关的事宜”。公报说，“这次属于试探性的会谈导致了有益的意见交换。双方一致同意以后再会晤。”
    巴基斯坦外交部长布托在为印度代表团送行之后举行的记者招待会上，重申了巴基斯坦主张“克什米尔人民必须享有自决权”的立场。
    出席这次会议的印度方面代表有：印度外交部长斯瓦兰·辛格，商业部长沙阿和运输、航空、海运和旅行部长雷迪等。巴基斯坦方面有：巴基斯坦外交部长布托，商业部长法鲁克和交通部长萨布尔汗等。
