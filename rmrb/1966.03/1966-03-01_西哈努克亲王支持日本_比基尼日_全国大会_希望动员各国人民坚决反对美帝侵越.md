Title: Prince Sihanouk Supports the Japanese "Bikini Day" National Convention, Hopes to Mobilize People of All Countries to Resolutely Oppose US Imperialist Aggression Against Vietnam
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-01
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, February 28th -- According to the Cambodian News Agency, Cambodian Head of State Prince Sihanouk wrote a letter to the Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs on February 19th, expressing his support for the "March 1st Bikini Day National Convention" organized by the council.
    On March 1, 1954, when the United States conducted a hydrogen bomb test near Bikini Island in the Pacific, some Japanese fishermen were injured.
    Prince Sihanouk said, "We wish you great success in this action, as you are taking courageous action against those who are openly preparing for a new nuclear war."
    He said: "We hope that your conference will help mobilize the people of all countries in the world to participate in the struggle to resolutely and effectively oppose the American imperialist aggression against Indochina, especially against Vietnam."
    Prince Sihanouk pointed out that the people of Asian and African countries are fighting to get rid of neo-colonial rule and maintain their independence. He hopes that the Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs will contribute to strengthening the unity of the people of Asian and African countries.

### 西哈努克亲王支持日本“比基尼日”全国大会  希望动员各国人民坚决反对美帝侵越

1966-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王支持日本“比基尼日”全国大会
    希望动员各国人民坚决反对美帝侵越
    新华社金边二十八日电　据柬埔寨新闻社报道，柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王曾经在二月十九日写信给日本禁止原子弹氢弹协议会，表示支持这个协议会组织的“三月一日比基尼日全国大会”。
    一九五四年三月一日，美国在太平洋比基尼岛附近进行氢弹试验时，曾经使日本的一些渔民遭到伤亡。
    西哈努克亲王说，“你们在为反对那些正在公开准备一场新的核战争的人采取勇敢行动，我们预祝您们在这一行动中取得巨大的成就。”
    他说：“我们希望你们的大会将有助于动员世界各国人民参加斗争，坚决和有效地反对美帝国主义对印度支那、特别是对越南的侵略。”
    西哈努克亲王指出，亚非各国人民正在为摆脱新殖民主义统治和维护独立进行斗争。他希望日本禁止原子弹氢弹协议会将对加强亚非各国人民的团结作出贡献。
