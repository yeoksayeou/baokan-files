Title: Vietnamese Militia Shoots Down U.S. Aircraft

Author:

Date: 1966-03-12
Edition: 3

    Hanoi, March 11 (Xinhua) – It was announced here on the 11th that on March 4th, militia of Duc Huong Commune, Duc Tho County, Ha Tinh Province in North Vietnam used light weapons to shoot down an invading U.S. jet fighter.

### 越南民兵击落美机一架

1966-03-12
第3版()
专栏：

    越南民兵击落美机一架
    新华社河内十一日电　据这里十一日公布，越南北方河静省德寿县德香乡民兵四日用轻武器击落入侵的美国喷气式飞机一架。
