Title: Guo Moruo Meets with Mexican Economist
Author:
Date: 1966-03-21
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 20th – Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People’s Committee for World Peace, received Graciela Leiters Quezada, a Mexican economist and peace activist who is currently visiting China, on the 19th. They had a friendly conversation.

### 郭沫若接见墨西哥一经济学家

1966-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见墨西哥一经济学家
    新华社二十日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若十九日接见了正在我国访问的墨西哥经济学家、和平人士格拉谢拉·莱特·克萨达，同她进行了友好的谈话。
