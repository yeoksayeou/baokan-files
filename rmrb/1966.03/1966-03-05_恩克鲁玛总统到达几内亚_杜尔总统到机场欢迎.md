Title: President Nkrumah Arrives in Guinea, President Touré Welcomes Him at the Airport
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-05
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 4th dispatch: Conakry news: Ghanaian President Kwame Nkrumah arrived in Conakry, the capital of Guinea, by plane on March 2.
    Guinean President Sékou Touré, members of the National Political Bureau of the Guinea Democratic Party, and government ministers welcomed him at the airport.
    When Nkrumah arrived, the band played the national anthems of Ghana and Guinea, and he reviewed the guard of honor.
    On the same day, a mass rally was held in the Conakry stadium to welcome Nkrumah.

### 恩克鲁玛总统到达几内亚  杜尔总统到机场欢迎

1966-03-05
第4版()
专栏：

    恩克鲁玛总统到达几内亚
    杜尔总统到机场欢迎
    新华社四日讯　科纳克里消息：加纳总统克瓦米·恩克鲁玛在三月二日乘飞机到达几内亚首都科纳克里。
    几内亚总统塞古·杜尔、几内亚民主党全国政治局委员们和政府部长们到机场欢迎。
    在恩克鲁玛到达时，乐队奏起加纳和几内亚国歌，检阅了仪仗队。
    当天，在科纳克里体育场举行了群众大会欢迎恩克鲁玛。
