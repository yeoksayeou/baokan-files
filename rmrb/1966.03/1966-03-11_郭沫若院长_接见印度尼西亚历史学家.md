Title: Guo Moruo Receives Indonesian Historian

Author:

Date: 1966-03-11
Edition: 4

    Guo Moruo Receives Indonesian Historian
    Xinhua News Agency, 10th — Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, received Dr. Li Deqing, Deputy Director of the Indonesian National Cultural Research Institute and historian, who is currently visiting China for academic exchange, this morning. They had a friendly conversation.
    Liu Danian and others were present at the meeting.

### 郭沫若院长  接见印度尼西亚历史学家

1966-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若院长
    接见印度尼西亚历史学家
    新华社十日讯  中国科学院院长郭沫若今天上午接见了正在我国进行学术访问的印度尼西亚国家文化研究所副所长、历史学家李德清博士，同他进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有刘大年等。
