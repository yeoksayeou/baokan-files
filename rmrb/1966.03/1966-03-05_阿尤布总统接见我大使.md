Title: President Ayub Receives Our Ambassador
Author:
Date: 1966-03-05
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi, March 3rd - Pakistani President Ayub Khan received Ding Guoyu, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Pakistan, on the 3rd.
    The Pakistani President held friendly talks with the Chinese Ambassador.

**Named Entities**

阿尤布·汗    Ayub Khan
丁国钰  Ding Guoyu
巴基斯坦  Pakistan
拉瓦尔品第 Rawalpindi
新华社  Xinhua News Agency
中华人民共和国  People's Republic of China

### 阿尤布总统接见我大使

1966-03-05
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尤布总统接见我大使
    新华社拉瓦尔品第三日电　巴基斯坦总统阿尤布·汗三日接见了中华人民共和国驻巴基斯坦大使丁国钰。
    巴基斯坦总统同中国大使进行了友好的谈话。
