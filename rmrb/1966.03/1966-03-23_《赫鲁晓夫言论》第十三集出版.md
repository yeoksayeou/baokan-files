Title: 《Khrushchev's Speeches》 Volume 13 Published
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-23
Edition: 4

    《Khrushchev's Speeches》 Volume 13 Published

    Xinhua News Agency, 22nd. *Khrushchev's Speeches*, Volume 13, has been published by World Knowledge Press and is being distributed throughout the country. This volume includes 151 reports, speeches, letters, Q&A sessions with reporters, etc., publicly released by Khrushchev from September to December 1959.

### 《赫鲁晓夫言论》第十三集出版

1966-03-23
第4版()
专栏：

    《赫鲁晓夫言论》第十三集出版
    新华社二十二日讯　《赫鲁晓夫言论》第十三集，已由世界知识出版社出版，在全国各地发行。这一集的内容，包括赫鲁晓夫在一九五九年九月至十二月期间公开发表的报告、讲话、函电、答记者问等，共一百五十一篇。
