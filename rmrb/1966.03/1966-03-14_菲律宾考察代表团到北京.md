Title: Philippine Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-03-14
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, March 13th -- The Philippine delegation, headed by Senator Maria Kalaw Katigbak of the Liberal Party of the Philippines, arrived in Beijing by plane tonight. They are in China for a friendly visit at the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation.
    Welcoming them at the airport were Zhang Yun, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation and Deputy to the National People's Congress, and responsible persons from relevant departments, including Luo Shuzhang, Pu Jiexiu, Chang Zhiqing, and Mu Xin. (Attached picture)
    On March 13th, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Guinea to China, Camara Sékou, presented his credentials to Chairman Liu Shaoqi. Chairman Liu Shaoqi posed for a photo with Ambassador Camara Sékou (third from left) and other diplomatic officials of the embassy.
  　Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian [photograph]

### 菲律宾考察代表团到北京

1966-03-14
第3版()
专栏：

    菲律宾考察代表团到北京
    新华社十三日讯　以菲律宾自由党参议员玛丽亚·卡劳·卡蒂瓦克夫人为首的菲律宾考察代表团，今晚乘飞机到达北京。他们是应全国妇联的邀请来我国进行友好访问的。
    到机场欢迎的，有全国妇联副主席、人大代表章蕴，和有关方面负责人罗叔章、浦洁修、常芝青、穆欣等。（附图片）
    三月十三日，几内亚共和国新任驻中国特命全权大使卡马拉·塞古向刘少奇主席递交国书。刘少奇主席同卡马拉·塞古大使（左三）及大使馆其他外交官员合影。
  　新华社记者　杜修贤摄
