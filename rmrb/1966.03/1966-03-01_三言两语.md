    Brief Comments
    Not Knowing the Enemy or Yourself
    The *New York Times* greatly lamented the ongoing quarrel in Washington. It quoted *The Art of War* by Sun Tzu, saying: " 'Know the enemy and know yourself, and you will not be defeated in a hundred battles.' We are not very clear about ourselves, and it is doubtful whether we have any understanding of our enemies." These words do indeed describe the situation of the fools in Washington.
    *The Art of War* also has a sentence: "If you know neither the enemy nor yourself, you will be defeated in every battle." Isn't the fact that the *New York Times* did not dare to quote or simply did not know this sentence a perfect illustration of why American imperialism is bound to fail?
    What "Legitimacy"?
    U.S. Vice President Humphrey said smugly that the South Vietnam National Liberation Front has "no legitimacy."
    I don't know what "law" Humphrey is referring to. Is it the South Vietnamese puppet "constitution" concocted single-handedly by the U.S.-puppet clique? The South Vietnam National Liberation Front certainly does not have, and does not need, such "legitimacy." The South Vietnamese people are fighting precisely under the leadership of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front to overthrow the puppet regime with this "legitimacy" and drive away its supporters. Otherwise, Humphrey would not need to ramble on about the issue of "legitimacy."

### 三言两语

1966-03-01
第3版()
专栏：三言两语

    三言两语
    不知彼不知己
    《纽约时报》对华盛顿正在进行的争吵大为感叹。它引用《孙子兵法》上的话说：“‘知彼知己，百战不殆’。我们对自己都不是了解得很清楚的，我们对我们的敌人，是否有任何了解是很可怀疑的。”这些话倒的确说出了华盛顿蠢人的状况。
    《孙子兵法》还有一句话是：“不知彼，不知己，每战必殆。”《纽约时报》不敢引用或者根本不晓得的这句话，不是正好说明美帝国主义之所以必败吗？
    什么“合法性”
    美国副总统汉弗莱神气十足地说：越南南方民族解放阵线“没有任何合法性”。
    不知道汉弗莱指的是什么“法”。是指美伪集团一手炮制的南越伪“宪法”吗？这样的“合法性”，越南南方民族解放阵线的确没有，而且也不需要。南越人民正是在越南南方民族解放阵线领导下为推翻有这种
  “合法性”的傀儡政权和赶走它的支持者而斗争。否则，汉弗莱也用不到要在什么“合法性”的问题上饶舌了。
