Title: Expel Chiang Gang Elements Illegally Occupying Chinese Buildings in Paris; French Foreign Ministry Spokesman Says Buildings Belong to China
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-14
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, March 12th. According to the decision of the French government, French police expelled Chiang Kai-shek elements illegally occupying two Chinese buildings in Paris on the 12th.
    A spokesman for the French Foreign Ministry said that the French police had taken over the two buildings—11 George V Avenue and 47 Pergolèse Street—because the two buildings occupied by the Chiang Kai-shek elements "belong to the Chinese nation, and for France, that nation is Beijing."
    The spokesman also said that the Chiang Kai-shek elements had been occupying the two buildings "illegally and fraudulently."
    Before France recognized China in 1964, 11 George V Avenue was the location of the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang's so-called "embassy" in France, and 47 Pergolèse Street was used by the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang's so-called "consulate general." After France recognized China, the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang adopted a disguise, continuing to occupy the two buildings in the name of the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang's so-called "delegation" to the United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization, until they were expelled by the French police this time.

### 赶走窃占中国在巴黎的建筑物的蒋帮分子  法国外交部发言人说建筑物属于中国

1966-03-14
第3版()
专栏：

    赶走窃占中国在巴黎的建筑物的蒋帮分子
    法国外交部发言人说建筑物属于中国
    新华社巴黎十二日电　根据法国政府的决定，法国警察十二日赶走了窃占中国在巴黎的两座建筑物的蒋介石分子。
    法国外交部的一个发言人说，法国警察已经接管这两座建筑物——乔治第五大街十一号和佩尔戈勒塞街四十七号，因为蒋介石分子占据的这两座建筑物“属于中国人的国家，而对法国来说，那个国家就是北京。”
    发言人还说，蒋介石分子一直是“非法地和以欺骗手段”占据这两座建筑物的。
    在法国一九六四年承认中国以前，乔治第五大街十一号是蒋介石匪帮驻法国的所谓“大使馆”的所在地，佩尔戈勒塞街四十七号曾由蒋介石匪帮的所谓“总领事馆”使用。在法国承认中国后，蒋介石匪帮采取了改头换面的办法，以蒋介石匪帮驻联合国教育、科学及文化组织所谓“代表团”的名义继续占据这两座建筑物，直到这次被法国警察赶走。
