Title: Lao Military and Civilians Shot Down Three US Aircraft, Damaged Six

Author:
Date: 1966-03-24
Edition: 4

    Laotian Military and Civilians Shot Down Three US Aircraft, Damaged Six
    Xinhua News Agency, Khang Khay, March 23rd (Dispatch) – According to a broadcast by the "Voice of Laos" radio station: On the 18th, the patriotic military and civilians of Xieng Khouang province, Laos, shot down two enemy aircraft in a battle against American aerial bandits.
    In addition, according to a Pathet Lao radio broadcast on the 23rd: On the 16th, patriotic forces in the Phou Kout mountain area of Laos shot down one American jet aircraft and damaged six.
    Xinhua News Agency, Khang Khay, March 21st (Dispatch) – Pathet Lao Radio published a commentary on the 19th, strongly condemning US imperialism for increasingly stepping up the dispatch of aircraft to carry out indiscriminate bombing of central and lower Laos. The commentary pointed out that the criminal action of US imperialism aims to further worsen the tense situation in Laos, so that the United States can use the excuse to realize a new military conspiracy of directly sending land forces to occupy central and lower Laos.
    The commentary pointed out that this new conspiracy of US imperialism has been strongly condemned by the Lao people and world public opinion. The more the US planes bomb and destroy, the more it arouses the deep-seated hatred of the people in the Lao liberated areas towards the American bandits and their lackeys. The Lao people, united in resolute struggle, with the support of peace-loving people all over the world, are fully capable of defeating the US imperialist aggressors.

### 老挝军民击落美机三架击伤六架

1966-03-24
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝军民击落美机三架击伤六架
    新华社康开二十三日电 据“老挝之声”电台广播：老挝川圹省爱国军民在十八日反击美国空中强盗的战斗中，击落敌机两架。
    另据巴特寮电台二十三日广播：老挝富科特山地区爱国部队十六日击落美国喷气式飞机一架，击伤六架。
    据新华社康开二十一日电巴特寮电台十九日发表评论，强烈谴责美帝国主义日益加紧派遣飞机对老挝中、下寮地区进行狂轰滥炸。评论指出，美帝国主义这个罪恶行动的目的是进一步恶化老挝的紧张局势，使美国可以利用借口来实现直接派遣陆军侵占中、下寮地区的新的军事阴谋。
    评论指出，美帝国主义的这个新阴谋已遭到老挝人民和世界舆论的强烈谴责。美国飞机越是轰炸破坏，就越加激起老挝解放区人民对美国强盗及其走狗的刻骨仇恨。老挝人民团结一致坚决斗争，在全世界爱好和平人民的支持下，完全能够打败美帝国主义侵略者。
