Title: Calcutta People Persist in Anti-Hunger Struggle; Indian Authorities Dispatch Large Numbers of Police to Cruelly Suppress Starving People
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-02
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Calcutta: The anti-hunger struggle of the masses in Calcutta, India, entered its eighth day on February 23rd. The Indian Congress Party authorities continued to dispatch large numbers of police to carry out suppression.

    According to a report in the *Hindustan Standard*, on February 23rd, the masses in the suburb of Calcutta, 24 Parganas, continued to hold anti-hunger demonstrations. The Indian authorities once again mobilized large numbers of police to cruelly suppress the demonstrating masses. The police used iron-clad truncheons to beat the masses, used tear gas to disperse them, and arrested more than one hundred demonstrators. On February 22nd, the police arrested 306 demonstrators in this area.

    In the Barasat and Bangaon areas, large-scale strikes were held on February 23rd, factories were closed, shops stopped business, and transportation came to a standstill. Police in Barasat arrested a large number of people. Angry demonstrators held a meeting to condemn the government's anti-people food policy and the police's atrocities. In Hooghly, Howrah, Nadia, Burdwan, Murshidabad, and other places, the masses continued to hold demonstrations, and the Indian authorities also arrested a large number of demonstrators.

### 加尔各答人民坚持反饥饿斗争  印度当局出动大批警察残酷镇压饥民

1966-03-02
第4版()
专栏：

    加尔各答人民坚持反饥饿斗争
    印度当局出动大批警察残酷镇压饥民
    新华社讯  加尔各答消息：印度加尔各答群众的反饥饿斗争，在二月二十三日进入了第八天。印度国大党当局继续出动大批警察进行镇压。
    据《印度斯坦旗报》报道，加尔各答郊区二十四——帕加纳斯的群众二十三日继续举行反饥饿示威，印度当局又调动了大批警察对示威群众进行了残酷镇压，警察用包铁皮的警棍殴打群众，用催泪瓦斯驱散他们，并且又逮捕了一百多名示威群众。在二十二日，警察在这一地区就逮捕了三百零六名示威者。
    在巴拉萨特和班冈地区，二十三日举行了大规模罢工，工厂关闭，商店停止了营业，交通陷于停顿。巴拉萨特的警察逮捕了大批群众。愤怒的示威者举行了一次会议，谴责政府反人民的粮食政策和警察的暴行。在霍格里、豪拉、纳迪亚、布德万、麦尔希达巴德等地，群众继续举行示威，印度当局也逮捕了大批示威群众。
