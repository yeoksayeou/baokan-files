Title: Guinean Ambassador Sekou Pays Courtesy Call on Premier Zhou

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-30
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 29th - The new Guinean Ambassador to China, Camara Sekou, paid a courtesy call on Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai this morning. Premier Zhou Enlai held friendly talks with him.



**Named Entities**

周恩来	Zhou Enlai

### 几内亚新任大使塞古拜会周总理

1966-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

    几内亚新任大使塞古拜会周总理
    新华社二十九日讯  几内亚新任驻中国大使卡马拉·塞古今天上午拜会了国务院总理周恩来。周恩来总理同他进行了友好的谈话。
