Title: Albanian People's Army Officers and Soldiers and Ministry of Interior Cadres Gather to Warmly Support the Decision of the Labor Party Central Committee; Hoxha and Other Party and Government Leaders Attend the Meeting
Author:
Date: 1966-03-07
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 6th—According to reports in the local newspapers, a meeting was held on the morning of May 5th in the Central Committee Building of the Albanian Labor Party. Participants included soldiers, officers, and generals from various units of the Albanian People's Army, as well as cadres from the Ministry of Interior.

    Albanian party and government leaders Enver Hoxha, Beqir Balluku, Hysni Kapo, Mehmet Shehu, Ramiz Alia, Kadri Hazbiu, and Petrit Dume, as well as Sadiq Bekteshi, Director of the Political Department of the Albanian People's Army, and others attended the meeting. Comrade Hoxha spoke to those present about the decision of the Albanian Labor Party Central Committee plenary session on March 4, 1966, regarding several important issues related to further strengthening and modernizing the Albanian People's Army. Many soldiers, officers, and generals enthusiastically participated in the discussion. With high revolutionary spirit and revolutionary enthusiasm, they unanimously expressed their complete support for the historic decision of the Albanian Labor Party Central Committee.

### 阿尔巴尼亚人民军官兵和内务部干部集会  热烈支持劳动党中央的决定  霍查等党政领导人参加了会议

1966-03-07
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚人民军官兵和内务部干部集会
    热烈支持劳动党中央的决定
    霍查等党政领导人参加了会议
    新华社地拉那六日电　据这里的报纸报道，五日上午在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会大厦召开了一次有阿尔巴尼亚人民军各部队的战士、军官和将军以及内务部机关干部参加的会议。
    参加这次会议的有阿尔巴尼亚党政领导人恩维尔·霍查、贝基尔·巴卢库、希斯尼·卡博、穆罕默德·谢胡、拉米兹·阿利雅、卡德里·哈兹比乌、彼得里特·杜米，以及阿尔巴尼亚人民军政治部主任萨迪克·别克特希等。霍查同志向出席会议的人谈到了一九六六年三月四日阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央全会关于进一步加强并使阿尔巴尼亚人民军现代化的若干重要问题的决定。许多战士、军官和将军热烈地参加了讨论。他们以高度的革命精神和革命热情，一致表示完全支持阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会的历史性决定。
