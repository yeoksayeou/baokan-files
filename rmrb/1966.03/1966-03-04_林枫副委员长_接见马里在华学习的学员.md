Title: Vice Chairman Lin Feng Receives Malian Students Studying in China
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-04
Edition: 4

    Vice Chairman Lin Feng
    Receives Malian Students Studying in China
    Xinhua News Agency, March 3rd. Vice Chairman Lin Feng received fourteen Malian students, including Idrissa Diakite, studying in China on the morning of the 2nd and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.  Present at the reception were Liu Zizai, Vice Minister of Higher Education, and Cui Yaoxian, Vice President of the Renmin University of China.
    Tenema Traore, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Malian Embassy in China, was also present.

### 林枫副委员长  接见马里在华学习的学员

1966-03-04
第4版()
专栏：

    林枫副委员长
    接见马里在华学习的学员
    新华社三日讯　林枫副委员长二日上午接见了马里在华学习的伊德里萨·迪亚基特等十四名学员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有高等教育部副部长刘子载，中国人民大学副校长崔耀先等。
    马里驻华大使馆临时代办特尼马·特拉奥雷也在座。
