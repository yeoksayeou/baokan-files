Title: Vice Chairman Dong Biwu Receives Representatives of Poor and Lower-Middle Peasants from Hubei Province, Encouraging Them to Hold High the Great Red Banner of Mao Zedong Thought, Better Study and Apply Chairman Mao's Works, and Become New "Foolish Old Men" of the Socialist Era, Carrying the Socialist Revolution and Construction Through to the End.
Author:
Date: 1966-03-13
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 12th -- Dong Biwu, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, received all the representatives attending the Second Congress of Poor and Lower-Middle Peasants of Hubei Province on the morning of the 12th. Accompanying the reception were Wang Renzhong, First Secretary of the Hubei Provincial Party Committee, and other responsible comrades from Hubei Province's Party and government leadership departments.

    After the reception, Vice Chairman Dong Biwu had a cordial conversation with some of the representatives. Vice Chairman Dong encouraged them to hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, better study and apply Chairman Mao's works, become new "Foolish Old Men" of the socialist era, and carry the socialist revolution and socialist construction through to the end.

    Before the reception, Vice Chairman Dong Biwu personally inscribed a poem to congratulate the Second Congress of Poor and Lower-Middle Peasants of Hubei Province. The full text of the poem is:

    "The congress opens well, exchanging much experience.
    Determined to learn from Dazhai, taking Mao's Selected Works as the guide.
    Ideological revolutionization, sincerely learning from each other.
    Be self-reliant, unafraid of hardship.
    The east wind blows mightily, sweeping away the demons of the world."

### 董必武副主席接见湖北省贫农下中农代表大会代表  勉励他们更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，更好地活学活用毛主席著作，做社会主义时代的新“愚公”，把社会主义革命和建设进行到底。

1966-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

    董必武副主席接见湖北省贫农下中农代表大会代表
    勉励他们更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，更好地活学活用毛主席著作，做社会主义时代的新“愚公”，把社会主义革命和建设进行到底。
    新华社武汉十二日电　中共中央政治局委员、中华人民共和国副主席董必武，十二日上午接见了出席湖北省第二次贫农、下中农代表大会的全体代表。陪同接见的，有中共湖北省委第一书记王任重以及湖北省党、政领导部门其他负责人。
    接见以后，董必武副主席同一部分代表进行了亲切谈话。董副主席勉励他们更高地举起毛泽东思想的伟大红旗，更好地活学活用毛主席著作，做社会主义时代的新“愚公”，把社会主义革命和社会主义建设进行到底。
    在接见以前，董必武副主席亲笔题了一首诗，祝贺湖北省第二次贫农、下中农代表大会。这首诗的全文是：
    “大会开得好，交流经验多。决心学大寨，毛选作先河。思想革命化，诚恳相切磋。要自力更生，不怕困难磨。东风吹浩荡，扫却世间魔。”
