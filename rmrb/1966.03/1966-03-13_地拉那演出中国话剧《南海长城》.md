Title: Chinese Play "Southern China Sea Great Wall" Performed in Tirana
Author:
Date: 1966-03-13
Edition: 4

    Chinese famous play "Southern China Sea Great Wall" premiered here on the evening of the 4th, receiving a warm welcome from the audience.
    Albanian Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Chairman of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association, Kellezi, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Natanaeli, Chairman of the Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Treska, and Chinese Ambassador to Albania Xu Jianguo, among others, watched the performance.
    "Southern China Sea Great Wall" was rehearsed by all the actors of the Tirana People's Art Theater according to the 1965-1966 execution plan of the Albanian-Chinese Cultural Cooperation Agreement, and co-directed by Albanian Meritorious Art Director Pandi Stillu and Chinese drama director Song Xingzhong.
    During the nearly three-hour performance, the audience was constantly immersed in a strong fighting atmosphere. In particular, the great scene of all the people being soldiers and the victorious ending of the South China Sea militia annihilating the invading enemy won the audience's stormy and warm applause. After the performance, the entire audience stood up and shouted "Enver-Mao Zedong", "Long live Albanian-Chinese friendship!"

### 地拉那演出中国话剧《南海长城》

1966-03-13
第4版()
专栏：

    地拉那演出中国话剧《南海长城》
    据新华社地拉那电　中国著名话剧《南海长城》于四日晚在这里首次演出，受到观众的热烈欢迎。
    阿尔巴尼亚部长会议副主席、阿中友协主席凯莱齐、外交部第一副部长纳塔奈利、对外文委主席特雷斯卡以及中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使许建国等观看了演出。
    《南海长城》是根据阿中文化合作协定一九六五——一九六六年执行计划由地拉那人民艺术剧院的全体演员排演，并由阿尔巴尼亚功勋艺术指导潘迪·斯蒂鲁和中国话剧导演宋兴中合作导演的。
    在将近三个小时的演出中，观众始终沉浸在强烈的战斗气氛中。特别是全民皆兵的伟大场面和南海民兵全歼来犯敌人的胜利结局，博得观众暴风雨般的热烈掌声。演出结束后，全场起立高呼“恩维尔—毛泽东”、“阿中友谊万岁！”
