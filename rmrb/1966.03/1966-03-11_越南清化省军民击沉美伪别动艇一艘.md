Title: Thanh Hoa Province Army and People of Vietnam Sink One US-Puppet Commando Boat
Author:
Date: 1966-03-11
Edition: 3

    Hanoi, Tenth Dispatch from Xinhua News Agency -- It was announced here on the morning of the tenth that the army and people of Thanh Hoa province in northern Vietnam sank one commando boat of the American bandits and their running dogs on the eighth.

    At 0:30 AM on the eighth, the American bandits and their running dogs took advantage of the darkness to dispatch three commando boats, intruding into the sea area of Thanh Hoa province. The army and people of Thanh Hoa province, carrying forward the spirit of "resolutely fighting and thoroughly defeating the American bandits," fiercely attacked the enemy commando boats, immediately sinking one of them, while the remaining two fled in panic.

### 越南清化省军民击沉美伪别动艇一艘

1966-03-11
第3版()
专栏：

    越南清化省军民击沉美伪别动艇一艘
    新华社河内十日电  据这里十日上午公布，越南北方清化省军民八日击沉美国强盗及其走狗的别动艇一艘。
    八日凌晨零时三十分，美国强盗及其走狗利用黑夜出动三艘别动艇，侵入清化省海面。清化省军民发扬“决战决胜美国强盗”的精神，痛击敌人别动艇，当即击沉其中一艘，剩下的两艘仓惶逃遁。
