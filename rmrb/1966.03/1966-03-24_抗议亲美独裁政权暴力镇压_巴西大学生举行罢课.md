Title: Brazilian University Students Strike to Protest Violent Suppression by Pro-U.S. Dictatorial Regime
Author:
Date: 1966-03-24
Edition: 4

    Protesting Violent Suppression by Pro-U.S. Dictatorial Regime
    Brazilian University Students Strike
    (Xinhua News Agency, Rio de Janeiro, 20th) University students in Belo Horizonte, the capital of Minas Gerais state, Brazil, have been holding a general strike against the pro-U.S. dictatorial regime since the 14th. It has been announced that the strike will continue until the 21st.
    The strike at the University of Belo Horizonte is in protest against the authorities' violent suppression of students' anti-dictatorship actions. On the 13th, thousands of students from this university held a grand demonstration against the pro-U.S. dictatorial regime. The dictatorial regime dispatched police to suppress the students, resulting in many injuries and over fifty arrests. The reactionary authorities also unreasonably handed over fourteen of those arrested to military tribunals.
    On the 19th, police broke into the local São José Church to search for students hiding there. The police's brutality aroused even greater anger among the students, who flocked to the church to protest and demonstrate.

### 抗议亲美独裁政权暴力镇压  巴西大学生举行罢课

1966-03-24
第4版()
专栏：

    抗议亲美独裁政权暴力镇压
    巴西大学生举行罢课
    据新华社二十日讯　里约热内卢消息：巴西米纳斯吉拉斯州首府贝洛奥里藏特的大学生从十四日起举行反对亲美独裁政权的总罢课。据宣布，罢课将继续到二十一日。
    贝洛奥里藏特大学的罢课是为了抗议当局对学生的反独裁行动进行暴力镇压。十三日，这所大学的数千名学生举行了反对亲美独裁政权的盛大示威。独裁政权出动警察对学生进行镇压，很多人受伤，五十多人被捕，反动当局还把十四名被捕者无理递交军事法庭。
    十九日，警察又闯进当地圣何塞教堂搜捕躲藏在那里的学生，警察的暴行激起了学生更大的愤怒，他们纷纷涌向教堂进行抗议示威。
