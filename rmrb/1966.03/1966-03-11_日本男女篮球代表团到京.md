Title: Japanese Men's and Women's Basketball Delegations Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-03-11
Edition: 4

    Japanese Men's and Women's Basketball Delegations Arrive in Beijing
    (Xinhua News Agency, 10th) The Japanese men's and women's basketball delegation, consisting of thirty-one members, arrived in Beijing by plane this evening at our invitation for a visit and competition.
    The head of the delegation is Makiyama Keishu, the managing director of the Japan Basketball Association. The delegation was warmly welcomed at the airport by Mu Zuoyun, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Basketball Association, and our country's men's and women's basketball players.
    The Japanese women's basketball team is composed of players from the Hirano Women's Basketball Team of the Japan Textile Company, which won the All-Japan Women's Basketball Championship last year. The Japanese men's basketball team is composed of the men's basketball team from Waseda University in Japan.

### 日本男女篮球代表团到京

1966-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    日本男女篮球代表团到京
    据新华社十日讯  应邀来我国访问比赛的日本男女篮球代表团一行三十一人，今晚乘飞机到达北京。
    代表团团长是日本篮球协会常务理事牧山圭秀。代表团在机场上受到中国篮球协会副主席牟作云和我国的男女篮球运动员们的热烈欢迎。
    日本女子篮球队是由去年获得全日女子篮球比赛冠军的日本纺织公司平野女子篮球队的队员组成。日本男子篮球队由日本早稻田大学男子篮球队组成。
