Title: Vice Premier Chen Receives French Women's Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-03-09
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, March 8th – Vice Premier Chen Yi received the French Women's Delegation led by Madame Irène de Lipkowski, President of the French Women's Groups International Liaison Committee, this morning and had a friendly conversation with them.

    Present at the reception were Zhang Yun and Wu Quanheng. (Attached picture)
    March 8th, Vice Premier Chen Yi receives the French Women's Delegation led by Madame Irène de Lipkowski (fifth from the left), President of the French Women's Groups International Liaison Committee. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zheng Xiaozhen.

### 陈副总理接见法国妇女代表团

1966-03-09
第5版()
专栏：

    陈副总理接见法国妇女代表团
    新华社八日讯  陈毅副总理今天上午接见由法国妇女团体国际联络委员会主席伊兰·德·李普考斯基夫人率领的法国妇女代表团，同她们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有章蕴、吴全衡。（附图片）
    三月八日，陈毅副总理接见由法国妇女团体国际联络委员会主席伊兰·德·李普考斯基夫人（左五）率领的法国妇女代表团。  新华社记者  郑小箴摄
