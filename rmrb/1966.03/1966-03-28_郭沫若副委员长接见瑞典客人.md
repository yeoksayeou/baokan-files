Title: Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Receives Swedish Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-03-28
Edition: 3

    According to Xinhua News Agency, March 26th—Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received Swedish biochemist Professor Tiselius and his wife, as well as thermochemist Associate Professor Sonner, this afternoon and held friendly talks with them.

**Named Entities**

郭沫若	Guo Moruo
全国人民代表大会常务委员会	Standing Committee of the National People's Congress
新华社	Xinhua News Agency
蒂西利斯教授	Professor Tiselius
松内副教授	Associate Professor Sonner
瑞典	Sweden

### 郭沫若副委员长接见瑞典客人

1966-03-28
第3版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见瑞典客人
    据新华社二十六日讯  全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若，今天下午接见瑞典生物化学家蒂西利斯教授和夫人以及热化学家松内副教授，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
