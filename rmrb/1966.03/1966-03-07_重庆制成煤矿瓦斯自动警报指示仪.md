    Chongqing Produces Automatic Methane Alarm Indicator for Coal Mines
    (Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 6th) The Zhongliangshan Coal Mine in Chongqing recently produced an instrument to ensure safe underground operations—an automatic methane alarm indicator.
    Its shape is similar to a radio. When it is installed in the ventilation fan room of the mine and connected to the methane extracted from underground through a rubber tube, it can reflect the increase and decrease of methane in the mine. If the amount of methane released underground exceeds safety regulations, the indicator immediately issues an alarm through a red signal, and the production command personnel can immediately take measures to prevent methane accidents.

### 重庆制成煤矿瓦斯自动警报指示仪

1966-03-07
第2版()
专栏：新建设  新产品

    重庆制成煤矿瓦斯自动警报指示仪
    据新华社重庆六日电　重庆中梁山煤矿最近制成一种保证井下安全作业的仪器——瓦斯自动警报指示仪。
    它的形状同收音机相似。把它安放在矿井的抽风机房里，用一根胶管同井下抽出的瓦斯接通，就可以反映出矿井瓦斯的升降情况。如果井下瓦斯涌出量超过安全规定，指示仪就通过红色信号立刻发出警报，生产指挥人员就可以马上采取措施，制止瓦斯事故的发生。
