Title: My Newly Appointed Ambassador to the United Arab Republic Arrives in Cairo
Author:
Date: 1966-03-24
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 22nd – Huang Hua, the newly appointed Chinese Ambassador to the United Arab Republic, arrived in Cairo by plane on the 22nd. Galaal Ezzat, Director of the Protocol Department of the UAR Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was at the airport to welcome him. He Gongkai, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the UAR, and envoys from other friendly countries were also at the airport to welcome him.

### 我新任驻阿联大使到达开罗

1966-03-24
第4版()
专栏：

    我新任驻阿联大使到达开罗
    新华社开罗二十二日电　新任命的中国驻阿拉伯联合共和国大使黄华二十二日乘飞机到达开罗。到机场欢迎他的有阿联外交部礼宾司司长加拉尔·埃扎特。中国驻阿联大使馆临时代办何功楷和其他友好国家的使节也到机场欢迎。
