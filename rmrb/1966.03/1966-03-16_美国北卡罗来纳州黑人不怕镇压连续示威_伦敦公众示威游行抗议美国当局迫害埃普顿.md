Title: Black People in North Carolina, USA, Fearlessly Demonstrate Continuously Despite Suppression; London Public Demonstrate and March to Protest Persecution of Epton by US Authorities
Author:
Date: 1966-03-16
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 12th – New York Dispatch: Black people in Halford, North Carolina, USA, have held demonstrations for two consecutive days, defying police suppression, to protest against racial discrimination.
    On the evening of the 9th, approximately 200 Black people from Halford took to the streets to protest the failure of government agencies and stores to hire Black people. The authorities dispatched 50 military police and special agents to obstruct and disrupt the Black people's march. Angry demonstrators fought the reactionary military police with whatever they could get their hands on—bricks, glass bottles, cooking utensils, etc.—and smashed a patrol car. The police used tear gas and water cannons against them, and thirty demonstrators were arrested.
    The next day, the Black people of this city continued to hold demonstrations, regardless of police suppression.
    Xinhua News Agency, London, 12th Electric: A group of London citizens and expatriates from African countries held a demonstration and march in front of the American Embassy on the afternoon of the 12th to protest the unreasonable imprisonment of American Black leader William Epton, Vice Chairman of the American Progressive Labor Party, by the US authorities, and shouted "Yankees get out of Vietnam."
    Demonstrators carried placards that read: "Epton, we support you!" "Epton is fighting for our freedom!" "Free Epton!" "End the American war in Vietnam!" "Down with US imperialism!"
    Marchers shouted angrily in the square in front of the heavily guarded American Embassy: "Free Epton!" "Yankees get out of Vietnam!"

### 美国北卡罗来纳州黑人不怕镇压连续示威  伦敦公众示威游行抗议美国当局迫害埃普顿

1966-03-16
第4版()
专栏：

    美国北卡罗来纳州黑人不怕镇压连续示威
    伦敦公众示威游行抗议美国当局迫害埃普顿
    新华社十二日讯　纽约消息：美国北卡罗来纳州哈尔福德市黑人不顾警察的镇压连续两天举行示威游行，反对种族歧视。
    九日晚上，大约二百名哈尔福德市的黑人走上街头，抗议政府机关和商店不雇用黑人。当局派出五十名军警特务对黑人的游行横加阻挠和破坏。愤怒的示威者用手头能够拿到的东西——砖块、玻璃瓶、炊事用具等——和反动军警进行了搏斗，并捣毁了一辆巡逻车。警察对他们使用催泪弹和水龙，有三十名示威者被逮捕。
    第二天，这个城市的黑人不顾警察的镇压，继续举行示威游行。
    据新华社伦敦十二日电一批伦敦市民和非洲国家侨民十二日下午在美国大使馆门前举行示威游行，抗议美国当局无理监禁美国黑人领袖、美国进步劳工党副主席威廉·埃普顿，并且高呼“美国佬滚出越南”。
    游行者拿着标语牌，上面写着：“埃普顿，我们支持你！”“埃普顿是在为我们的自由而战斗！”“释放埃普顿！”“结束美国在越南的战争！”“打倒美帝国主义！”
    游行者在警卫森严的美国大使馆门前的广场上愤怒地高呼：“释放埃普顿！”“美国佬滚出越南！”
