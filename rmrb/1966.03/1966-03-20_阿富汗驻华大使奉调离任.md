Title: Afghan Ambassador to China Leaves Post Upon Recall

Author:
Date: 1966-03-20
Edition: 4

    Afghan Ambassador to China Leaves Post Upon Recall
    Xinhua News Agency, March 19th -- The Ambassador of the Kingdom of Afghanistan to China, Mr. Miskinyar, left Beijing by train this afternoon to return to his country, having been recalled from his post.

### 阿富汗驻华大使奉调离任

1966-03-20
第4版()
专栏：

    阿富汗驻华大使奉调离任
    新华社十九日讯  阿富汗王国驻中国大使米斯凯尼亚奉调离任，今天下午乘火车离京回国。
