Title: China and Mongolia sign this year's agreement on mutual supply of goods. Vice Premier Li Xiannian receives Mongolian trade delegation

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-29
Edition: 4

    China and Mongolia sign this year's agreement on mutual supply of goods.
    Vice Premier Li Xiannian receives Mongolian trade delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, March 28th -- The governments of the People's Republic of China and the Mongolian People's Republic signed an agreement in Beijing this afternoon regarding the mutual supply of goods for the year 1966.
    Vice Premier Li Xiannian attended the signing ceremony.
    Li Qiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of China, and Z. Khishig, head of the Mongolian government trade delegation and Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective governments.
    Chinese representatives attending the signing ceremony included Lin Haiyun, Acting Minister of Foreign Trade, Liu Xianglun, Deputy Director of the Second Asian Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Zhuozhi, Commercial Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Mongolia.
    D. Tserendorj, the Mongolian Ambassador to China, and all members of the Mongolian government trade delegation, attended the signing ceremony.
    After the signing, Lin Haiyun hosted a banquet for the Mongolian guests.
    Xinhua News Agency, March 28th -- Vice Premier Li Xiannian received all members of the Mongolian government trade delegation led by Z. Khishig this afternoon, and held friendly talks with them.

### 中蒙签订今年相互供应货物议定书  李先念副总理接见蒙贸易代表团

1966-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

    中蒙签订今年相互供应货物议定书
    李先念副总理接见蒙贸易代表团
    新华社二十八日讯  中华人民共和国政府和蒙古人民共和国政府，今天下午在北京签订了关于一九六六年度相互供应货物的议定书。
    李先念副总理参加了签字仪式。
    我国对外贸易部副部长李强，蒙古政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长札·赫希格，分别代表两国政府在议定书上签字。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的，还有对外贸易部代部长林海云，外交部第二亚洲司副司长刘祥纶，中国驻蒙古大使馆商务参赞王琢之等。
    蒙古驻中国大使敦·策伯格米德，蒙古政府贸易代表团全体成员，参加了签字仪式。
    签字以后，林海云设宴招待蒙古客人。
    据新华社二十八日讯  李先念副总理今天下午接见由札·赫希格率领的蒙古政府贸易代表团全体成员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
