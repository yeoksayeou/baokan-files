Title: Lao Patriotic Armed Forces and Civilians in Xieng Khouang Repulse Three Enemy Attacks; Various Patriotic Parties and Organizations Strongly Protest U.S. Imperialism and its Running Dogs' Large-Scale Offensive Against Liberated Areas
Author:
Date: 1966-03-13
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Kang Kai, 12th. According to Pathet Lao Radio broadcasts: The patriotic armed forces and civilians of Xieng Khouang Province, Laos, successively repulsed three enemy attacks on the 2nd, 5th and 6th. In the counterattacks, a total of 62 enemies were killed, many wounded, and a large number of weapons and military supplies were seized.
    The U.S. running dog group's army launched an offensive against a certain area of the liberated zone of Xieng Khouang Province on the 2nd, attacked areas such as Fuji, Fuchuan, Fusong, and Bannabing on the 5th, and launched offensives in two routes towards the northern and southeastern parts of Height 1140 in Xieng Khouang Province on the 6th. Under the heroic counterattack of the Lao patriotic armed forces and civilians, these invasions all failed, and the remnant enemies fled in panic.
    Xinhua News Agency, Kang Kai. Lao various patriotic parties, mass organizations, and people from all walks of life strongly protested against the large-scale offensive launched by U.S. imperialism and its running dogs against the Lao liberated areas, and expressed their determination to carry the anti-U.S. patriotic struggle through to the end.
    The Lao Committee for the Peaceful Neutrality, National Harmony and Unified State, the Lao Party for Peace and Neutrality, the Lao Youth Federation, the Lao Women's Federation, the Lao Buddhist Association and other parties and mass organizations issued statements, resolutions, open letters or telegrams respectively, strongly condemning U.S. imperialism and its running dogs for launching frenzied offensives against central and lower Laos, and Mount Phou Kout, calling on peace-loving people all over the world to oppose this serious act of U.S. imperialism aimed at expanding the Indochina war and aggravating the tense situation in Southeast Asia. At the same time, they demanded that U.S. imperialism and its running dogs immediately cease their military offensives and withdraw their military personnel, weapons, and war materials from Laos, allowing the Lao people to solve their own affairs themselves.
    In recent days, Colonel Dun, the Commander-in-Chief of the Military Command of the Lao Patriotic Neutral Forces, as well as Deputy Commander Colonel Tan, Lieutenant Colonel Gong Xi, Lieutenant Colonel Zhang, and Major Tie have also given interviews to reporters, resolutely protesting against U.S. imperialism's crimes of enslaving the Lao people and expanding the Indochina war, and expressing their determination to fight alongside all patriotic forces in the country to smash U.S. imperialism's aggression and realize Laos' peace, neutrality, independence, democracy, unity, and prosperity to the end.
    According to Xinhua News Agency, Kang Kai. According to Pathet Lao Radio broadcasts: According to incomplete statistics, from January to February 20th this year, a total of 12,400 people in the upper, middle, and lower Laos regions participated in rallies and demonstrations against the American aggression against Laos.
    These rallies passed thousands of protest letters, strongly condemning the U.S. imperialist conspiracy to directly send troops to occupy the middle and lower Laos regions.

### 老挝川圹爱国军民击退敌人三次进攻  各爱国党派团体强烈抗议美帝及其走狗大规模进攻解放区

1966-03-13
第3版()
专栏：

    老挝川圹爱国军民击退敌人三次进攻
    各爱国党派团体强烈抗议美帝及其走狗大规模进攻解放区
    新华社康开十二日电　据巴特寮电台广播：老挝川圹省爱国军民在二日、五日和六日连续击退敌军三次进攻。在反击战中，共打死敌人六十二名，打伤多名，缴获大批武器和军用物资。
    美国走狗集团军队二日向川圹省解放区某地发动进攻，五日向富士、富川、富松和班纳冰等地进攻，六日又分两路向川圹省第一一四○高地北部和东南部进攻。在老挝爱国军民的英勇反击下，这些进犯均告失败，残敌狼狈溃逃。
    新华社康开电　老挝各爱国党派、群众团体和各界人士强烈抗议美帝国主义及其走狗对老挝解放区发动大规模进攻，并表示决心把反美爱国斗争进行到底。
    老挝争取和平中立、民族和睦和统一国家委员会、老挝和平中立党、老挝青年联合会、老挝妇女联合会、老挝佛教协会等党派和群众团体分别发表声明、决议，公开信或电报，严厉谴责美帝国主义及其走狗向老挝中、下寮和富科特山等地区发动疯狂进攻，呼吁全世界爱好和平人民反对美帝国主义这种旨在扩大印度支那战争和加剧东南亚紧张局势的严重行为。同时要求美帝国主义及其走狗立即停止军事进攻，并且把它们的军事人员、武器和战争物资撤出老挝，让老挝人民自己解决自己的事务。
    连日来老挝爱国中立力量军事指挥部总指挥敦上校，副总指挥坦上校、贡西中校、章中校和铁少校也都向记者发表谈话，坚决抗议美帝国主义为实现其奴役老挝人民，扩大印度支那战争的罪行，并且表示决心同全国所有爱国力量一起，为粉碎美帝国主义的侵略，实现老挝的和平、中立、独立、民主、统一和繁荣而斗争到底。
    据新华社康开电　据巴特寮电台广播：据不完全统计，今年一月到二月二十日，老挝上寮、中寮和下寮地区共有一万二千四百多人参加了反对美国侵略老挝的集会和游行示威。
    这些集会通过了成千份抗议书，强烈谴责美帝国主义阴谋直接派兵侵占老挝中寮和下寮地区。
