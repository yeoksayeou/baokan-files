Title: Our Red Cross Sends Telegram to Soviet Red Cross, Thanking Them for Condolences Regarding Earthquake in Hebei, Expressing Unwillingness to Accept Their Aid
Author:
Date: 1966-03-28
Edition: 3

    Our Red Cross Sends Telegram to Soviet Red Cross
    Thanking Them for Condolences Regarding Earthquake in Hebei Province
    Expressing Unwillingness to Accept Their Aid
    Xinhua News Agency, March 27th. The Chinese Red Cross Society telegraphed the Union of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies of the Soviet Union on the 26th, thanking them for their telegram of condolence regarding the earthquake-stricken people in Hebei Province. At the same time, it notified them that it was not prepared to accept the items sent by the Soviet organization. The telegram from the Chinese Red Cross Society stated: “Following the occurrence of the disaster, thanks to the close concern of our Party and government, and thanks to the timely support from all sectors within the country, the living arrangements and medical treatment for the affected masses have been initially arranged and will continue to be arranged. Therefore, we currently do not need any foreign medical supplies or material assistance, and are not prepared to accept the items sent by you. Thank you.”

### 我红十字会致电苏联红十字会  感谢来电慰问河北地震灾区人民  表示不准备接受该会送来的东西

1966-03-28
第3版()
专栏：

    我红十字会致电苏联红十字会
    感谢来电慰问河北地震灾区人民
    表示不准备接受该会送来的东西
    新华社二十七日讯  中国红十字会二十六日打电报给苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会，感谢该会来电慰问河北省地震地区受灾人民，同时通知该会，不准备接受该会送来的东西。中国红十字会的电报说：“在灾情发生后，由于我们党和政府的密切关怀，由于国内各方面的及时支援，受灾群众的生活和伤病治疗问题都已得到初步安排，并将继续得到安排。因此我们现在不需要国外的一切药品和物资支援，并且不准备接受你们送来的东西。谢谢。”
