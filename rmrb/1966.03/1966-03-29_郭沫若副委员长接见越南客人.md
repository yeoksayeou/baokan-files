Title: Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman, Receives Vietnamese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-03-29
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 28th -- Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, today received all members of the Vietnamese Archaeological Survey Group led by Nguyen Van Nghia, Head of the Vietnamese Department of Archaeology, and Nguyen Linh Dung, a Vietnamese microbiologist who is currently conducting research in our country. The hosts and guests engaged in a cordial and friendly conversation.
    Present at the meeting were Pei Lisheng, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Yang Zhongjian, Director of the Institute of Vertebrate Paleontology and Paleoanthropology, and Xia Nai, Director of the Institute of Archaeology.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见越南客人

1966-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见越南客人
    新华社二十八日讯  人大常委会副委员长、中国科学院院长郭沫若，今天接见了由越南考古学部负责人阮文义率领的越南考古学考察团全体成员，正在我国考察的越南微生物学家阮麟勇，宾主进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中国科学院副院长裴丽生，古脊椎动物与古人类研究所所长杨钟健和考古研究所所长夏鼐等。
