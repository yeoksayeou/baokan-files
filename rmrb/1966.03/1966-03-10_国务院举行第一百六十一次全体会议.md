Author:

Edition: 1

    The State Council Held its 161st Plenary Session
    Heard a report on the agricultural situation, and adopted a proposal on granting amnesty to a group of war criminals from the Chiang Kai-shek clique, the puppet Manchuguo, and the puppet Mengjiang Autonomous Government who have truly turned over a new leaf.
    Xinhua News Agency, 9th - The State Council held its 161st plenary session today and heard a report on the agricultural situation by Wang Guangwei, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission.
    The plenary session adopted a proposal on granting amnesty to a group of war criminals from the Chiang Kai-shek clique, the puppet Manchuguo, and the puppet Mengjiang Autonomous Government who have truly turned over a new leaf, and submitted it to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for deliberation and decision.
    The meeting also passed the "Organizational Regulations (Draft) of the People's Congresses and People's Committees at all levels of the Hainan Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture in Qinghai Province," the "Organizational Regulations (Draft) of the People's Congresses and People's Committees at all levels of the Kizilsu Kirghiz Autonomous Prefecture in Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region," and the "Organizational Regulations (Draft) of the People's Congress and People's Committee of the Bayingolin Mongol Autonomous Prefecture in Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region." These three organizational regulations will be submitted to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for consideration and approval.
    The meeting also approved some changes in the administrative divisions of counties and cities, and approved appointments and removals.

### 国务院举行第一百六十一次全体会议

1966-03-10
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行第一百六十一次全体会议
    听取了关于农业情况的报告，通过关于特赦一批确实已经改恶从善的蒋介石集团、伪满洲国和伪蒙疆自治政府的战争罪犯的建议
    新华社九日讯  国务院全体会议今天举行第一百六十一次会议，听取了国家计划委员会副主任王光伟关于农业情况的报告。
    全体会议通过关于特赦一批确实已经改恶从善的蒋介石集团、伪满洲国和伪蒙疆自治政府的战争罪犯的建议，提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议决定。
    会议还通过“青海省海南藏族自治州各级人民代表大会和各级人民委员会组织条例
  （草案）”、“新疆维吾尔自治区克孜勒苏柯尔克孜自治州各级人民代表大会和各级人民委员会组织条例（草案）”、“新疆维吾尔自治区巴音郭楞蒙古自治州人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例（草案）”。这三个组织条例将提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议批准。
    会议还通过了部分县、市行政区划变更事项，通过了任免事项。
