Title: The Johnson Government is the Common Enemy of the Vietnamese and American People; Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho Sends a Telegram Thanking the American People for Their Efforts to Stop the Aggressive War Against Vietnam; All Circles in Hanoi Hold a Rally to Warmly Welcome the "International Day of Protest" Against the War of Aggression Against Vietnam Held by the American People
Author:
Date: 1966-03-28
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 27th -- Representatives from all circles in Hanoi, numbering 2,000, held a rally on the evening of the 26th to warmly welcome the "International Day of Protest" against the U.S. government's aggression against Vietnam, held by the American people.

    Hoang Quoc Viet, member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front and Chairman of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor, Ton Quang Phiet, Chairman of the Vietnam Asian-African People's Solidarity Committee, and Ta Quang Buu, Vice Chairman of the Vietnam Peace Committee, attended the meeting.

    Pham Ngoc Thach, Minister of Health of Vietnam, spoke at the meeting. He pointed out that the movement launched by people from all walks of life in the United States against the U.S. government's war of aggression against Vietnam is developing more and more vigorously. Although the U.S. authorities have adopted various despicable fascist means to suppress it, they cannot stop the development of this movement.

    Pham Ngoc Thach pointed out that the increasingly developing movement of the American people against the U.S. government's war of aggression against Vietnam shows that the bellicose U.S. government is not only the irreconcilable enemy of the Vietnamese people but also the enemy of the American people.

    Pham Ngoc Thach expressed that the Vietnamese people sincerely thank the American people for their great support for the Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation.

    Representatives from the Vietnam Chemical Society, the Vietnam Women's Union, the Vietnam General Evangelical Church, and the Vietnam Student Union also spoke at the meeting. They unanimously and strongly condemned the crimes of U.S. imperialism in carrying out a war of aggression in Vietnam.

    The meeting unanimously passed a telegram to the U.S. "National Coordinating Committee to End the War in Vietnam," expressing the Vietnamese people's support for the American people's struggle for freedom, democracy, and human rights.

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 26th -- According to a report by the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency, Nguyen Huu Tho, Chairman of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front, sent a telegram to the U.S. "National Coordinating Committee to End the War in Vietnam" on the eve of the "International Day of Protest" against the U.S. government's war of aggression against Vietnam (March 25-26), expressing gratitude to the progressive American people for their increasingly great efforts to demand that the U.S. government unconditionally stop the war of aggression against Vietnam.

    The telegram pointed out that at present, the U.S. government is still unwilling to abandon its policy of aggression and is stubbornly trying to force the South Vietnamese people to lay down their arms and surrender with a brutal war. The telegram said that the South Vietnamese people are determined to overcome all difficulties and resolutely fight to the final victory in order to achieve true independence, freedom, and peace.

    The telegram said that the efforts of the progressive American people to demand that the U.S. government unconditionally stop the war of aggression against Vietnam are increasingly receiving the warm sympathy, support, and response from the American people and people of conscience in the world, and will surely achieve brilliant victories.

### 约翰逊政府是越南人民和美国人民共同敌人  阮友寿主席电谢美国人民为制止侵略越南战争所作的努力  河内各界集会热烈欢迎美国人民举行反对侵越战争的“国际抗议日”

1966-03-28
第4版()
专栏：

    约翰逊政府是越南人民和美国人民共同敌人
    阮友寿主席电谢美国人民为制止侵略越南战争所作的努力
    河内各界集会热烈欢迎美国人民举行反对侵越战争的“国际抗议日”
    新华社河内二十七日电 河内市各界代表二千人二十六日晚上举行集会，热烈欢迎美国人民举行反对美国政府侵略越南“国际抗议日”。
    越南祖国战线中央主席团委员、越南总工会主席黄国越，越南亚非人民团结委员会主席孙光阀，越南保卫世界和平委员会副主席谢光宝等出席了大会。
    越南卫生部长范玉石在会上讲话。他指出，美国各界人士开展的反对美国政府侵略越南战争的运动正在日益蓬勃地发展，尽管美国当局采取了各种卑鄙的法西斯手段进行镇压，但是，它无法阻挠这个运动的发展。
    范玉石指出，美国人民反对美国政府侵略越南战争的日益发展的运动，说明了好战的美国政府不仅是越南人民的不共戴天的敌人，而且也是美国人民的敌人。
    范玉石表示，越南人民衷心地感谢美国人民对越南人民抗美救国斗争的巨大支持。
    越南化学学会、越南妇女联合会、越南基督教总会和越南大学生联合会的代表也分别在大会上讲话，他们一致强烈谴责美帝国主义在越南进行侵略战争的罪行。
    大会一致通过给美国“结束越南战争全国协调委员会”一份电报，表达越南人民对美国人民为了自由、民主和人权而进行的斗争的支持。
    新华社河内二十六日电据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团主席阮友寿，在反对美国政府侵略越南战争“国际抗议日”（三月二十五日——二十六日）前夕，打电报给美国“结束越南战争全国协调委员会”，表示感谢美国进步人民为要求美国政府无条件地停止侵越战争而作出的日益巨大的努力。
    电报指出，目前，美国政府仍然不肯放弃它的侵略政策，而且还顽固地用残暴的战争企图迫使越南南方人民放下武器投降。电报说，越南南方人民决心战胜一切困难，坚决抗战到最后胜利，以取得真正的独立、自由与和平。
    电报说，美国进步人民为要求美国政府无条件地停止侵越战争而作出的努力越来越得到美国和世界上具有良知的人民的热烈同情、支持和响应，一定会取得辉煌的胜利。
