Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Telegram to Pan-Africanist Congress of Azania on Sixth Anniversary of Sharpeville Massacre: Chinese People Consistently Support the South African People's Anti-Imperialist Struggle
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-22
Edition: 5

    According to Xinhua News Agency dispatch on the 21st, the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity today sent a telegram to the Pan-Africanist Congress of Azania on the sixth anniversary of the Sharpeville Massacre in South Africa, strongly condemning the barbaric acts of the white racists in South Africa and expressing firm support for the South African people who are fighting against racial oppression and for national liberation.
    The telegram said that the South African colonial rulers, supported by imperialism headed by the United States, have committed heinous crimes, which are not only resolutely opposed by the South African people, but also angrily condemned and opposed by the people of Asia, Africa, and the world.
    The telegram said that the Chinese people have consistently supported the just struggle of the South African people against imperialism and new and old colonialism, against racial oppression, and for national liberation. The South African authorities must immediately release all South African patriots who have been unreasonably arrested, and must immediately stop the massacre of the South African people.

### 我亚非团结会就沙佩维尔事件六周年电南非泛非主义者大会  中国人民一贯支持南非人民反帝斗争

1966-03-22
第5版()
专栏：

    我亚非团结会就沙佩维尔事件六周年电南非泛非主义者大会
    中国人民一贯支持南非人民反帝斗争
    据新华社二十一日讯　中国亚非团结委员会今天就南非沙佩维尔大屠杀事件六周年，打电报给南非泛非主义者大会，严正谴责南非白人种族主义者的野蛮行为，并表示坚决支持反对种族压迫和争取民族解放而斗争的南非人民。
    电文说，南非殖民统治者在以美国为首的帝国主义支持下犯下了滔天罪行，不但为南非人民所坚决反对，也遭到亚非人民和世界人民的愤怒谴责和反对。
    电文说，中国人民一贯支持南非人民反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义，反对种族压迫，争取民族解放的正义斗争。南非当局必须立即释放一切被无理逮捕的南非爱国者，必须立即停止对南非人民的屠杀。
