    This is a worker at the Tianjin Architectural Component Assembly Company Experimental Factory hoisting a newly made ceramsite concrete wall panel. This type of wall panel can replace brick walls and is made of concrete using fly ash sintered ceramsite (replacing gravel) and slag (replacing sand) mixed with cement, then assembled into wall panels. It is lighter than brick walls, has good thermal insulation properties, and is relatively easy to process. Xinhua News Agency reporter Liang Yiding photo

专栏：

    这是天津市建筑构件装配公司实验厂工人在吊装新制成的陶粒混凝土壁板。这种壁板能代替砖墙，是由粉煤灰烧结的陶粒（代替石子）、炉渣（代替砂子）配上水泥制成的混凝土，再砌成壁板。它的重量比砖墙轻，隔热性能好，加工比较方便。　新华社记者　梁一丁摄
