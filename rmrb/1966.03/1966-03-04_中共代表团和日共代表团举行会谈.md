Title: CCP Delegation and JCP Delegation Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1966-03-04
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 3rd — The delegation of the Communist Party of China and the delegation of the Japanese Communist Party held talks in Beijing this afternoon.

    On the Chinese Communist Party side, those participating in the talks included Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee; Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary; Peng Zhen, Member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat; Kang Sheng, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat; Liao Chengzhi, Member of the Central Committee; Liu Ningyi, etc.

    On the Japanese Communist Party side, those participating in the talks included Kenji Miyamoto, General Secretary of the Central Committee; Masayoshi Oka, Standing Committee Member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat; Koreto Kurahara, Akira Yonehara, Members of the Political Bureau; Kazuyoshi Sunama, Secretary of the Secretariat; Koichiro Ueda, Tetsuzo Fuwa, Akira Kudo, Alternate Members of the Central Committee, etc.

    The talks proceeded in a cordial and friendly atmosphere. (Photo Attached)

### 中共代表团和日共代表团举行会谈

1966-03-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中共代表团和日共代表团举行会谈
    新华社三日讯　中国共产党代表团和日本共产党代表团，今天下午在北京举行会谈。
    中国共产党方面参加会谈的，有中央副主席刘少奇，总书记邓小平，政治局委员、书记处书记彭真，政治局候补委员、书记处书记康生，中央委员廖承志、刘宁一等。
    日本共产党方面参加会谈的，有中央总书记宫本显治，政治局常务委员、书记处书记冈正芳，政治局委员藏原惟人、米原昶，书记处书记砂间一良，中央候补委员上田耕一郎、不破哲三、工藤晃等。
    会谈是在亲切友好气氛中进行的。（附图片）
