Title: Algerian Ambassador Sahli calls on Chairman Zhu

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-26
Edition: 2

    Algerian Ambassador Sahli calls on Chairman Zhu
    Xinhua News Agency, March 25th—The newly appointed Ambassador of the People's Democratic Republic of Algeria to China, Mohamed Cherif Sahli, paid a visit to Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, this afternoon. Chairman Zhu De held a friendly conversation with him.

### 阿尔及利亚大使萨赫利拜会朱委员长

1966-03-26
第2版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚大使萨赫利拜会朱委员长
    新华社二十五日讯　阿尔及利亚民主人民共和国新任驻中国大使穆罕默德·谢里夫·萨赫利今天下午拜会了全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德。朱德委员长同他进行了友好的谈话。
