    Brief Comments
    Superfluous Explanation
    U.S. Senator Fulbright, who has been playing an active role in the ongoing debate in Washington surrounding the U.S. policy of aggression in Vietnam, said that on the Vietnam issue, he and Johnson "do not have different views on the ultimate goal, but rather on how to achieve that goal." He thus revealed the essence of the internal squabbles within the U.S. ruling clique.
    In fact, Fulbright's explanation is entirely superfluous. Because no one would think that a representative of the U.S. monopoly capitalist groups like him would oppose the aggressive aim of the U.S. to turn South Vietnam into a U.S. colony and military base.
    "Relying on Each Other for Survival"
    When the U.S. aggressor troops were panicked and retreated from the A Shau camp of the puppet special forces under the fierce attack of the South Vietnamese Liberation Army, they shot and killed many South Vietnamese puppet soldiers who were trying to climb onto the helicopters to escape.
    On the South Vietnamese battlefield, under normal circumstances, the U.S. military and the puppet army rely on each other for survival. The puppet army depends on the U.S. military, and the U.S. military cannot do without the puppet army. However, when the U.S. military wants to save its own life, it cannot care about any old friendship. The tragedy of being a slave can be seen from this. The U.S. military sacrifices the puppet army to save itself, which is also a kind of "relying on each other for survival."

### 三言两语

1966-03-21
第3版()
专栏：三言两语

    三言两语
    多余的表白
    一直在华盛顿围绕着美国侵越政策进行的大辩论中扮演活跃角色的美国参议员富布赖特说，在越南问题上，他同约翰逊“并不是在最后的目标上有不同的看法，而是对如何达到这种目标有不同的看法”。他这就一语道破了美国统治集团内部争吵的实质。
    其实，富布赖特的这种表白完全是多余的。因为谁也不会认为象他这样一个美国垄断资本集团的代言人，是会反对美国把南越作为美国殖民地和军事基地这一侵略目的的。
    “相依为命”
    美国侵略军在南越解放军的猛攻下，惊慌失措地撤出阿绍美伪特种部队营地时，开枪打死企图爬上直升飞机逃命的南越伪军多名。
    在南越战场上，一般的情况下，美军和伪军是相依为命的，伪军要依靠美军，美军也离不开伪军。但是，当美军为了要保住自己的性命的时候，那就顾不得什么老交情了。当奴仆者之可悲，由此可见一斑。美军牺牲伪军以保存自己，这也是一种
  “相依为命”。
