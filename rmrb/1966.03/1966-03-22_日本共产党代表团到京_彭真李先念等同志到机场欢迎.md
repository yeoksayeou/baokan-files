Title: Japanese Communist Party Delegation Arrives in Beijing; Comrades Peng Zhen, Li Xiannian, and Others Welcome at the Airport
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-22
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 21st dispatch: The Japanese Communist Party delegation, led by Kenji Miyamoto, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, arrived in Beijing by plane at noon today after concluding its visit to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.
    Those who greeted the delegation at the airport included Peng Zhen and Li Xiannian, members of the Political Bureau and Secretaries of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee; Kang Sheng, alternate member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat; Liao Chengzhi and Liu Ningyi, members of the Central Committee; Zhao Yimin, alternate member of the Central Committee; Zhao Fan, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPC, and others.
    Pak Se-chang, the Korean Ambassador to China, and some Japanese comrades in Beijing also went to the airport to greet the delegation.

### 日本共产党代表团到京  彭真李先念等同志到机场欢迎

1966-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

    日本共产党代表团到京
    彭真李先念等同志到机场欢迎
    新华社二十一日讯　由日本共产党中央委员会总书记宫本显治率领的日本共产党代表团，结束了在朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的访问，今天中午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场迎接的，有中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记彭真、李先念，政治局候补委员、书记处书记康生，中央委员廖承志、刘宁一，中央候补委员赵毅敏，中共北京市委书记处书记赵凡等。
    朝鲜驻中国大使朴世昌，在北京的一部分日本同志，也到机场迎接。
