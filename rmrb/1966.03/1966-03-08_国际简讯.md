    International Briefs
    UAR Compensates French Nationals' Property
    Cairo News: A joint statement was issued here on the 4th by delegations from the UAR and France announcing that the two countries had initialed an agreement on compensating French nationals whose property or funds had been affected or confiscated due to the UAR's nationalization decrees.
    According to the agreement, the UAR will pay for all assets of French enterprises affected by the nationalization decrees promulgated in 1961.
    US Boston Newspaper Workers Strike
    Boston News: Nearly 1,500 printing and postal workers in Boston, Massachusetts, USA, went on strike on the 6th, demanding wage increases, making it impossible for Boston's five daily newspapers to publish.
    The strike was initiated by the printing workers' union branch and the postal workers' union branch.
    It is reported that this is the third large-scale strike in Boston's newspaper industry in nine years.
    US Conducts Another Underground Nuclear Test
    Washington News: According to the US Atomic Energy Commission, the United States conducted another underground nuclear weapons test at the Nevada Test Site on the 5th.
    This is the sixth nuclear weapon test officially announced by the United States this year, and the sixty-third nuclear weapon test by the United States since the signing of the Moscow Three-Party Partial Nuclear Test Ban Treaty in August 1963.
    Austrian National Council Election Results Announced
    Vienna News: The election for the Austrian National Council (i.e., the Lower House) concluded on the 6th.
    Out of all 165 seats, the People's Party occupies 85 seats, an increase of four seats compared to the original; the Socialist Party has 74 seats, a loss of two seats compared to the original. This is the first time in twenty years that the People's Party has obtained a majority in parliamentary elections.
    The previous election in Austria was held in November 1962.

### 国际简讯

1966-03-08
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    阿联赔偿法国侨民财产
    开罗消息：阿联和法国两国代表团四日在这里发表一项联合声明宣布，两国草签了一项关于向法国侨民赔偿由于阿联国有化法令而受到影响或被查封的法国侨民的财产或资金的协定。
    根据这项协定，阿联将支付由于一九六一年颁布的国有化法令所涉及的法国企业的全部资财。
    美波士顿报业工人罢工
    波士顿消息：美国马萨诸塞州波士顿市近一千五百名印刷工人和邮递工人，六日起举行罢工，要求增加工资，使波士顿的五家日报不能出版。
    罢工是由印刷工人工会分会和邮递工人工会分会发起的。
    据报道，这是波士顿报界九年来的第三次大规模罢工。
    美国又进行地下核试验
    华盛顿消息：据美国原子能委员会宣布，美国五日又在内华达试验场进行了一次地下核武器试验。
    这是今年以来美国官方宣布的第六次核武器试验，也是一九六三年八月莫斯科三国部分禁止核试验条约签订以来美国的第六十三次核武器试验。
    奥地利国民院选举揭晓
    维也纳消息：奥地利国民院（即下议院）的选举六日结束。
    在所有一百六十五个席位中，人民党占八十五席，比原来增加了四席；社会党七十四席，比原来失去了两席。人民党二十年来第一次在议会选举中取得过半数多数。
    奥地利的上届选举是在一九六二年十一月举行的。
