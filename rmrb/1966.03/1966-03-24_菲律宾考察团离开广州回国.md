Title: Philippine Delegation Leaves Guangzhou for Home

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-24
Edition: 4

    Philippine Delegation Leaves Guangzhou for Home
    (Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 21st) The Philippine delegation, headed by Mrs. Maria Kalaw Katigbak, Liberal Party Senator of the Philippines, concluded its visit to China and left Guangzhou by train today to return home.

    Huang Ruichiu, Deputy Director of the Guangdong Provincial Women's Federation, Ye Yun, Deputy Director of the Guangzhou Municipal Women's Federation, and responsible persons from relevant departments went to the station to see them off.

### 菲律宾考察团离开广州回国

1966-03-24
第4版()
专栏：

    菲律宾考察团离开广州回国
    据新华社广州二十一日电　以菲律宾自由党参议员玛丽亚·卡劳·卡蒂瓦克夫人为首的菲律宾考察代表团，结束了在我国的访问，今天乘火车离开广州回国。
    广东省妇联副主任黄蕊秋、广州市妇联副主任叶云和有关方面负责人等到车站欢送。
