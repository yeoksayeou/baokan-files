Title: Chen Yi, Vice Premier, Receives Argentine Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-03-10
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, March 9th—Vice Premier Chen Yi received Emilio Jáuregui, Secretary General of the Argentine Federation of Press Workers, and Eduardo Jozami, Assistant Secretary, this morning and had a friendly conversation with them.

    Present at the reception were Bi Jilong, Li Bingquan, and other responsible persons from relevant departments. (See picture attached)
    (Photo) March 9th, Vice Premier Chen Yi receives Emilio Jáuregui (first from the right), Secretary General of the Argentine Federation of Press Workers, and Eduardo Jozami (second from the right).
                                  Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian

### 陈毅副总理接见阿根廷客人

1966-03-10
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见阿根廷客人
    新华社九日讯  陈毅副总理今天上午接见阿根廷报刊工作者联合会总书记埃米略·豪雷吉和助理书记爱德华多·何萨米，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有关方面负责人有毕季龙、李炳泉等。（附图片）
    三月九日，陈毅副总理接见阿根廷报刊工作者联合会总书记埃米略·豪雷吉（右一）和助理书记爱德华多·何萨米（右二）。
                                  新华社记者  杜修贤摄
