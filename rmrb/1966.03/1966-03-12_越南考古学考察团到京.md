Title: Vietnamese Archaeological Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-12
Edition: 4

    Vietnamese Archaeological Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, 11th. According to the annual executive plan for scientific cooperation between the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Vietnamese State Committee for Science and Technology and the Academy of Social Sciences, the Vietnamese archaeological delegation, a group of four led by delegation head Nguyen Van Nghi, arrived in Beijing by train today. They were warmly welcomed at the station by Yang Zhongjian, director of the Institute of Vertebrate Paleontology and Paleoanthropology of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Xia Nai, director of the Institute of Archaeology, among others.

### 越南考古学考察团到京

1966-03-12
第4版()
专栏：

    越南考古学考察团到京
    新华社十一日讯　根据中国科学院和越南国家科学技术委员会、社会科学院科学合作年度执行计划，越南考古学考察团一行四人在团长阮文义的率领下今天乘火车抵达北京。在车站上受到中国科学院古脊椎动物与古人类研究所所长杨钟健及考古研究所所长夏鼐等人的热烈欢迎。
