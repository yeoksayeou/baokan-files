Title: The CPC Central Committee Hosts a Banquet for the JCP Delegation; Comrades Zhou Enlai, Zhu De and others Hold Cordial Talks with Comrades Kenji Miyamoto, Masaaki Oka and others at the Banquet
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-28
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 27th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China hosted a banquet this evening to welcome the entire delegation of the Japanese Communist Party, led by Kenji Miyamoto, head of the delegation and General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, and Masaaki Oka, Deputy Head of the delegation, Standing Committee Member of the Political Bureau of the JCP, and Secretary of the Secretariat.
    Comrades Zhou Enlai and Zhu De, Vice Chairmen of the CPC Central Committee, Peng Zhen and Tan Zhenlin, Members of the Political Bureau and Secretaries of the Secretariat, and Kang Sheng, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat, attended the banquet.
    Also present at the banquet were Liao Chengzhi, Lin Feng, Liu Xiao, Liu Ningyi, Xiao Hua, Members of the CPC Central Committee, Zhao Yimin, Alternate Member of the Central Committee, and responsible comrades from the organs of the CPC Central Committee, state organs, people's organizations, and the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPC.
    Some Japanese comrades in Beijing were invited to attend the banquet.
    The banquet was filled with warm and friendly atmosphere as the guests and hosts engaged in cordial conversations. (Attached picture)
    Right picture: Comrades Zhou Enlai and Zhu De and others of the CPC Central Committee hold cordial talks with Kenji Miyamoto, Masaaki Oka and others at the banquet. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Bin photo

### 中共中央举行宴会招待日共代表团  周恩来朱德等同志和宫本显治冈正芳等同志在宴会上亲切交谈

1966-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央举行宴会招待日共代表团
    周恩来朱德等同志和宫本显治冈正芳等同志在宴会上亲切交谈
    新华社二十七日讯  中国共产党中央委员会今晚举行宴会，招待由团长、日本共产党中央委员会总书记宫本显治，副团长、日共中央政治局常务委员、中央书记处书记冈正芳率领的日本共产党代表团全体同志。
    中共中央副主席周恩来、朱德，政治局委员、书记处书记彭真、谭震林，政治局候补委员、书记处书记康生等同志，出席了宴会。
    出席宴会作陪的，还有中共中央委员廖承志、林枫、刘晓、刘宁一、萧华，中央候补委员赵毅敏，以及中共中央机关、国家机关、各人民团体和中共北京市委的负责同志。
    在北京的部分日本同志应邀出席了宴会。
    宴会上宾主亲切交谈，充满了热情友好的气氛。（附图片）
    右图：中共中央副主席周恩来、朱德等同志和宫本显治、冈正芳等同志在宴会上亲切交谈。  新华社记者  张  彬摄
