Title: Malian Director of the Department of Economics of the Ministry of Finance and Trade Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-03-09
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, March 8th: Oumar Coulibaly, Director of the Department of Economics of the Ministry of Finance and Trade of Mali, left Beijing by plane today to visit Southern China and then return to his country via Guangzhou. Wang Mingjun and other responsible persons from relevant departments saw him off at the airport. Tenema Traoré, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Malian Embassy in China, also saw him off at the airport.

### 马里财政和贸易部经济司司长离京

1966-03-09
第5版()
专栏：

    马里财政和贸易部经济司司长离京
    新华社八日讯  马里财政和贸易部经济司司长乌玛尔·库利巴利，今天乘飞机离开北京前往我国南方参观访问然后经广州回国。到机场送行的有王明俊等有关方面负责人。马里驻中国大使馆临时代办特尼马·特拉奥雷也到机场送行。
