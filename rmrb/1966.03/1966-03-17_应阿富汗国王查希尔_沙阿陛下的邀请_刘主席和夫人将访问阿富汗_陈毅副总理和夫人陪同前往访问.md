Title: At the Invitation of His Majesty King Zahir Shah of Afghanistan, Chairman Liu and His Wife Will Visit Afghanistan; Vice Premier Chen Yi and His Wife Will Accompany Them on the Visit
Author:
Date: 1966-03-17
Edition: 1

    At the Invitation of His Majesty King Zahir Shah of Afghanistan
    Chairman Liu and His Wife Will Visit Afghanistan
    Vice Premier Chen Yi and His Wife Will Accompany Them on the Visit
    Xinhua News Agency, 16th – At the invitation of His Majesty Mohammed Zahir Shah, King of Afghanistan, Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and his wife Wang Guangmei will pay a friendly visit to Afghanistan. Accompanying the visit are Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and his wife, as well as other personnel.

### 应阿富汗国王查希尔·沙阿陛下的邀请  刘主席和夫人将访问阿富汗  陈毅副总理和夫人陪同前往访问

1966-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

    应阿富汗国王查希尔·沙阿陛下的邀请
    刘主席和夫人将访问阿富汗
    陈毅副总理和夫人陪同前往访问
    新华社十六日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇和夫人王光美应阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿陛下的邀请，将前往阿富汗进行友好访问。陪同访问的有中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅和夫人以及其他人员。
