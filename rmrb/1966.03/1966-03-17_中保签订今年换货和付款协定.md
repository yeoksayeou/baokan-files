Title: China and Bulgaria Sign This Year's Goods Exchange and Payment Agreement
Author:
Date: 1966-03-17
Edition: 4

    China and Bulgaria Sign This Year's Goods Exchange and Payment Agreement
    Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 16th—China and Bulgaria signed the 1966 Goods Exchange and Payment Agreement in Sofia on the 16th.
    Signing the agreement on the Chinese side was Fu Shenglin, Head of the Chinese Government Trade Delegation and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and on the Bulgarian side was An. Angelov, Head of the Bulgarian Government Trade Delegation and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade.
    Lin Zhong, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Bulgaria, attended the signing ceremony.
    According to Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 14th—Fu Shenglin, Head of the Chinese Government Trade Delegation and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, arrived in Sofia by plane at noon today.

### 中保签订今年换货和付款协定

1966-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

    中保签订今年换货和付款协定
    新华社索非亚十六日电　中国和保加利亚一九六六年换货和付款协定十六日在索非亚签字。
    在协定上签字的，中国方面是中国政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长傅生麟，保加利亚方面是保加利亚政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长安·安格洛夫。
    中国驻保加利亚大使馆临时代办林中出席了签字仪式。
    据新华社索非亚十四日电　中国政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长傅生麟今天中午乘飞机抵达索非亚。
