Title: Brimming with the Militant Friendship of the Chinese and Vietnamese Peoples: Vietnamese Song and Dance Troupe Returns to Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1966-03-18
Edition: 4

    Brimming with the Militant Friendship of the Chinese and Vietnamese Peoples
    Vietnamese Song and Dance Troupe Returns to Hanoi
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 17th – The Vietnamese Song and Dance Troupe, led by Qu Huy Can, Deputy Minister of Culture of Vietnam, concluded its performance tour in China and returned to Hanoi today, filled with the friendship of the Chinese people.
    Greeting them at the station were Pham Ngoc Thuan, Director of the Vietnamese Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations, Nguyen Duc Quy, Deputy Minister of Culture, Hoang Van Loi, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and others.
    Zhu Qiwu, Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam, Du Zhanchao, Cultural Counselor, and the performers of the China Oriental Song and Dance Ensemble, which is currently on a performance tour in Vietnam, were also at the station to welcome them.
    Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, 16th – The Song and Dance Troupe of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam concluded its friendly visit to our country and returned home by special train from Nanning this afternoon.
    Lu Shaowu, Vice Chairman of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, and hundreds of people from all walks of life in Nanning went to the station to give them a warm send-off. At the station, a group of young women presented flowers to all the Vietnamese comrades. As they bid farewell, the Chinese hosts and Vietnamese guests warmly shook hands and embraced, and the people sending them off repeatedly wished the Vietnamese comrades even greater victories in their battle to resist America and save the nation.
    Le Quang Dien, Deputy Consul of the Consulate General of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Nanning, also went to the station to see them off. Lin Lin, member of the Chinese Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, who accompanied the Song and Dance Troupe to Nanning, will accompany the Song and Dance Troupe to the Sino-Vietnamese border.
    The Vietnamese Song and Dance Troupe held the closing ceremony of its performance tour in China in Nanning on the evening of the 15th and gave its last performance.
    Before arriving in Nanning, the Vietnamese Song and Dance Troupe had visited and performed in Shanghai, Changsha, and other places.

### 满载中越两国人民的战斗友谊  越南歌舞艺术团回到河内

1966-03-18
第4版()
专栏：

    满载中越两国人民的战斗友谊
    越南歌舞艺术团回到河内
    新华社河内十七日电　由越南文化部副部长瞿辉瑾率领的越南歌舞艺术团结束了在中国的访问演出，十七日满载中国人民的友谊回到河内。
    到车站欢迎的有越南对外文化联络委员会主任范玉纯、文化部副部长阮德葵、外交部副部长黄文利等。
    中国驻越南大使朱其文、文化参赞杜展潮以及正在越南访问演出的中国东方歌舞团的演员们也到车站欢迎。
    据新华社南宁十六日电越南民主共和国歌舞艺术团结束了在我国的友好访问，今天下午从南宁乘专车回国。
    广西壮族自治区副主席卢绍武和南宁各界数百人前往车站热烈欢送。在车站上，一群女青年向全体越南同志献了鲜花。中国主人和越南客人在告别的时候亲切地握手、拥抱，欢送的人们一再预祝越南同志在抗美救国的战斗岗位上取得更大的胜利。
    越南民主共和国驻南宁总领事馆副领事黎光奠也到车站送行。陪同歌舞艺术团前来南宁的我国对外文委委员林林等，将陪送歌舞艺术团到中越边境。
    越南歌舞艺术团十五日晚在南宁举行访华演出的闭幕式，并作了最后一场演出。
    越南歌舞艺术团在到达南宁之前曾先后在上海、长沙等地进行访问演出。
