　　沙佩维尔事件

　　沙佩维尔事件发生在一九六○年三月二十一日。当时南非殖民统治者在沙佩维尔残酷镇压南非人民为反对“通行证法”而举行的大示威，枪杀了七十多名非洲人，打伤了好几百人，逮捕了约二万人。
　（新华社）

Data

Sharpeville Massacre

The Sharpeville Massacre occurred on March 21, 1960. At that time, the South African colonial rulers brutally suppressed the large-scale demonstrations held by the South African people in Sharpeville to oppose the "Pass Laws," killing more than seventy Africans, wounding several hundred people, and arresting approximately twenty thousand people.
(Xinhua News Agency)
