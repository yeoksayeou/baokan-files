Title: GDR Ambassador to China, Koeltz, Departs Post for Home

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-08
Edition: 4

    GDR Ambassador to China, Koeltz, Departs Post for Home
    Xinhua News Agency, 7th – GDR Ambassador to China, Koeltz, being recalled to his country, left Beijing by plane at noon today to return home.
