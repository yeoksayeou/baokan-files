Title: New Afghan Ambassador to China Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-20
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 19th – The new Ambassador of the Kingdom of Afghanistan to China, Mohammad Azaf Soheil, arrived in Beijing by plane this evening.
