Title: Lao Liberation Army Shoots Down Three U.S. Planes
Author:
Date: 1966-03-06
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Kang Kai, 5th. According to a Pathet Lao radio broadcast on the 5th: On February 16th, more than a dozen American bandit planes indiscriminately bombed villages along Highway 7 and the Phou Kout mountain area. In the battle to counterattack enemy planes, the patriotic armed forces in the Phou Kout mountain area of ​​Xieng Khouang province shot down three American bandit planes and damaged three.

### 老挝解放军击落美机三架

1966-03-06
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝解放军击落美机三架
    新华社康开五日电　据巴特寮电台五日广播：二月十六日美国强盗飞机十多架滥肆轰炸第七号公路沿线的村庄和富科特山地区。川圹省富科特山地区爱国武装力量在反击敌机的战斗中，击落美国强盗飞机三架，击伤三架。
