Title: Bhutto Says Pakistan Will Not Forget China's Support, Chairman Liu's Visit Will Strengthen Sino-Pakistan Friendship

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-16
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi, March 15th (Correspondent) According to a Pakistan United Press Agency report on the 14th, Pakistani Foreign Minister Bhutto said at the Pakistan National Assembly meeting in Dacca on the 14th that, as President Ayub said, Pakistan's relations with China are continuing to develop. Pakistan will not forget the assistance, help and support China gave it in its time of difficulty.
    He said that Chairman Liu Shaoqi's upcoming visit to Pakistan will further strengthen the bonds of friendship between Pakistan and China and will help further promote friendly relations.
    Bhutto, speaking first in the foreign policy debate, reiterated that the people of Kashmir must be given the opportunity to exercise their right to decide whether they wish to join Pakistan. Kashmir must be given the right to decide its own choice.
    He said that unless the dispute between Pakistan and India over Kashmir is resolved, the task of establishing peace on the subcontinent will remain unfulfilled.
    Bhutto said that Pakistan wants to be friends with all countries in the world.
    Referring to Pakistan's relations with the United States and the Soviet Union, he said that Pakistan welcomes the cooperation of the United States and values ​​its relations with the United States. At the same time, Pakistan's bilateral relations with the Soviet Union are constantly developing.

### 布托外长说巴基斯坦不会忘记中国的支持  刘主席访问将加强巴中友好关系

1966-03-16
第4版()
专栏：

    布托外长说巴基斯坦不会忘记中国的支持
    刘主席访问将加强巴中友好关系
    新华社拉瓦尔品第十五日电　据巴基斯坦联合通讯社十四日报道，巴基斯坦外交部长布托十四日在达卡举行的巴基斯坦国民议会会议上说，正如阿尤布总统所说的，巴基斯坦同中国的关系在继续发展。巴基斯坦不会忘记在它困难的时候中国给予它的援助、帮助和支持。
    他说，刘少奇主席即将访问巴基斯坦，这将进一步加强巴基斯坦和中国友谊的纽带，并且将有助于进一步促进友好关系。
    布托在外交政策辩论中首先发言时重申，必须给予克什米尔人民机会，使他们行使决定他们是否愿意参加巴基斯坦的权利。必须给予克什米尔以决定自己的选择的权利。
    他说，除非巴基斯坦同印度之间关于克什米尔的争端得到解决，否则在次大陆建立和平的任务就仍然没有完成。
    布托说，巴基斯坦要同世界上一切国家做朋友。
    他在谈到巴基斯坦同美国和苏联的关系时说，巴基斯坦欢迎美国的合作，它重视同美国的关系。同时，巴基斯坦同苏联的双边关系正在不断地发展。
