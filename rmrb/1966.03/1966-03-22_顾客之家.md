Title: Customer's Home
Author: Yang Wenren Tian Jia
Date: 1966-03-22
Edition: 6

    Customer's Home
    Yang Wenren Painting Tian Jia Poem
    Faces beaming with smiles,
    The room filled with warmth,
    Haircuts for the revolution,
    Red hearts dedicated to the people.
    Welcoming diligently upon arrival,
    Seeing them off at departure,
    No distinction between old and young,
    As close as family.
    The snip-snip of the scissors,
    Like the sound of singing,
    So much affection and meaning,
    Sung to the customers.
    The little suggestion book,
    Every word expressing deep feeling,
    Praising the barbers,
    Consciously working for the revolution!

### 顾客之家
杨文仁　田家
1966-03-22
第6版()
专栏：

    顾客之家
    杨文仁画　田家诗脸上笑盈盈，室内热气腾，理发为革命，红心献人民。来时勤接待，去时送出门，无分老和少，亲密如家人。剪子吱吱响，好比唱歌声，多少情和意，唱给顾客听。小小意见簿，字字表深情，赞美理发员，自觉干革命！（附图片）
