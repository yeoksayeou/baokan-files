Title: Mongolian Government Economic Delegation Returns Home
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-21
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 20th: The members of the Mongolian Government Economic Delegation, including Mo Tao, six people in total, who came to our country to discuss economic and technical assistance issues, left Beijing by train this afternoon to return home. Yang Lin, Deputy Director of the Commission for Economic Relations with Foreign Countries, Liu Xianglun, Deputy Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and responsible persons from relevant departments went to the station to see them off. Dun·Tsebegmid, head of the Mongolian Government Economic Delegation and Mongolian Ambassador to China, also went to the station to see them off.

### 蒙古政府经济代表团回国

1966-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古政府经济代表团回国
    新华社二十日讯　前来我国商谈经济技术援助问题的蒙古政府经济代表团成员莫陶等一行六人今天下午乘火车离开北京返国。前往车站送行的有对外经济联络委员会副主任杨琳、外交部第二亚洲司副司长刘祥纶和有关部门负责人。蒙古政府经济代表团团长、蒙古驻中国大使敦·策伯格米德也到车站送行。
