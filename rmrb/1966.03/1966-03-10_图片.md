    Liaoning Province Panjin Reclamation Bureau Dawa Farm, using the "three-in-one combination" method, self-manufactured a type of small-powered rice harvester. This harvester can harvest approximately 25 mu of rice per day with one operator.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Fei photographed
