Title: Thai Troops and South Vietnamese Puppet Troops Provoke Cambodia Again
Author:
Date: 1966-03-21
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, March 19th (dispatch) - The Cambodian News Agency reported on the 19th that the military authorities of Cambodia's Koh Kong Province issued a message saying that Thai armed forces once again opened fire on the direction of the Chinei Sa outpost in Koh Kong Province on the 17th.
    The Cambodian army retaliated with all its weapons.
    At the same time, two Thai warships in the waters off Koh Lanta deliberately intruded into Cambodian territorial waters near Yor Island. They were driven away by Cambodian artillery fire.
    At noon on the 5th, a Thai air force jet flew over a village in Cambodia's Battambang Province and spread chemical poisons.
    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh (dispatch) - According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on the 17th, on the morning of March 6th, South Vietnamese puppet troops invaded Cambodian territory in Kampot Province twice. At noon on March 5th, American-South Vietnamese puppet troops' aircraft violated Cambodian airspace over Kep City in Kampot Province.
    On March 6th, Thai aircraft violated Koh Kong Province airspace twice.

### 泰国军队南越伪军又向柬埔寨挑衅

1966-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

    泰国军队南越伪军又向柬埔寨挑衅
    新华社金边十九日电　据柬埔寨新闻社十九日报道，柬埔寨戈公省军事当局发布消息说，泰国武装部队十七日再次向戈公省奇内萨哨所的方向开火。
    柬埔寨军队以一切武器进行反击。
    同时，哈勒海面的两艘泰国军舰蓄意侵入约尔岛附近的柬埔寨领海。它们被柬埔寨炮火赶走。
    五日中午，泰国空军的一架喷气式飞机飞到柬埔寨马德望省一个村庄上空，撒放化学毒药。
    新华社金边电　据柬埔寨新闻社十七日报道，三月六日上午，南越伪军两次侵入柬埔寨唝吥省境内。三月五日中午，美国—南越伪军飞机侵犯柬埔寨唝吥省白马市领空。
    三月六日，泰国飞机两次侵犯戈公省领空。
