Title: Chairman Liu Pays Tribute to Jinnah's Mausoleum

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-31
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, March 30th - Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, accompanied by Pakistani Foreign Minister Bhutto, visited the mausoleum of Muhammad Ali Jinnah, the first Governor-General of Pakistan, here this afternoon.
    Chairman Liu Shaoqi laid a wreath at the mausoleum of Muhammad Ali Jinnah.
    The ribbon on the wreath read: "To Mr. Jinnah."
    Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, and other members of Chairman Liu Shaoqi's entourage also visited the mausoleum.
    Also present at the mausoleum were Ding Guoyu, Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan, and Roza, Pakistani Ambassador to China.
    Tens of thousands of people stood on both sides of the road leading to the mausoleum, warmly cheering and applauding Chairman Liu Shaoqi.

### 刘主席向真纳陵墓献花圈

1966-03-31
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席向真纳陵墓献花圈
    新华社卡拉奇三十日电  中华人民共和国主席刘少奇三十日下午在巴基斯坦外交部长布托的陪同下，在这里访谒了巴基斯坦第一任总督穆罕默德·阿里·真纳的陵墓。
    刘少奇主席在穆罕默德·阿里·真纳的陵墓前献了花圈。
    花圈的缎带上写着：“献给真纳先生”。
    陈毅副总理、外交部副部长章汉夫和刘少奇主席的其他随行人员也一同前往谒墓。
    前往谒墓的还有中国驻巴基斯坦大使丁国钰和巴基斯坦驻中国大使罗查。
    成千上万的人民站在通往陵墓的公路两旁向刘少奇主席热烈欢呼和鼓掌。
