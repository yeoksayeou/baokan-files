Title: CCP Delegation and NZCP Delegation Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1966-03-06
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 5th -- The delegation of the Communist Party of China and the delegation of the Communist Party of New Zealand held talks in Beijing this afternoon.
    The Chinese Communist Party was represented at the talks by Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the Central Committee; Peng Zhen, Member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat; Kang Sheng, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat; Liu Ningyi, Member of the Central Committee; and Zhao Yimin, Alternate Member of the Central Committee.
    The New Zealand Communist Party delegation was represented by V.G. Wilcox, General Secretary; and A. Auster, member of the delegation.
    The talks were conducted in a cordial and friendly atmosphere. (Attached photo)
    Right picture: The CCP delegation and the NZCP delegation hold talks. The first on the right is Comrade V.G. Wilcox, General Secretary of the Communist Party of New Zealand.
                                Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Changzhong

### 中共代表团和新共代表团举行会谈

1966-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

    中共代表团和新共代表团举行会谈
    新华社五日讯　中国共产党代表团和新西兰共产党代表团，今天下午在北京举行会谈。
    中国共产党方面参加会谈的，有中央总书记邓小平，政治局委员、书记处书记彭真，政治局候补委员、书记处书记康生，中央委员刘宁一，中央候补委员赵毅敏等。
    新西兰共产党代表团方面参加会谈的，有总书记维·乔·威尔科克斯，代表团团员亚·奥斯特勒。
    会谈是在亲切友好的气氛中进行的。（附图片）
    右图：中共代表团和新共代表团举行会谈。右一是新西兰共产党总书记维·乔·威尔科克斯同志。
                                新华社记者　刘长忠摄
