Title: Algerian New Ambassador Pays a Courtesy Call on Premier Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1966-03-09
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, March 8th – Algerian’s newly appointed ambassador to China, Mohamed Cherif Sahli, paid a courtesy call on Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council this afternoon. Premier Zhou Enlai had a friendly conversation with him.

### 阿尔及利亚新任大使拜会周恩来总理

1966-03-09
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚新任大使拜会周恩来总理
    新华社八日讯  阿尔及利亚新任驻华大使穆罕默德·谢里夫·萨赫利今天下午拜会了国务院总理周恩来。周恩来总理同他进行了友好的谈话。
