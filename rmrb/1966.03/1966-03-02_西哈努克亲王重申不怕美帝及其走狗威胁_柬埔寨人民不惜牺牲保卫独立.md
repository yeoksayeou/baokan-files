Title: Prince Sihanouk Reiterates Fearlessness of U.S. Imperialist and its Lackeys' Threats; Cambodian People Prepared to Sacrifice to Defend Independence
Author:
Date: 1966-03-02
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, March 1st – According to a Cambodia News Agency report on March 1st, Cambodian Head of State Prince Sihanouk, in a speech in Pursat Province on February 26th, called on the Cambodian people to unite closely and continue to hold firm determination, willing to make the greatest sacrifices to defend Cambodia’s independence, neutrality, and territorial integrity.
    Prince Sihanouk, while condemning the provocations of the Thai and South Vietnamese puppet authorities against Cambodia, said, “A people like us who are not afraid of death will never be defeated.”
    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, February 28th – According to a Cambodia News Agency report on February 28th, Cambodian Head of State Prince Sihanouk, in a speech at a mass rally in Pursat Province on February 25th, said: No matter what tricks the imperialists and their lackeys, the expansionists, and the “Free Khmer” elements play, they cannot force us to submit.
    Sihanouk pointed out that the imperialist newspapers have already declared that the “Free Khmer” forces will invade Cambodia and that this army has increased from 10,000 to 30,000.
    Sihanouk said, “We know that this army now barely numbers three hundred. But even if it has a hundred thousand, the Cambodians of the People's Socialist Community will not worry.”
    He pointed out that there are now a total of 1.4 million troops and police from the United States, Thailand, and the Saigon and Vientiane puppet regimes surrounding Cambodia. “These are Cambodia’s 1.4 million enemies. If we are not afraid of such a large number of enemies, then why should we be afraid of 30,000 'Free Khmer' elements?”
    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, February 27th – According to a Cambodia News Agency report on February 27th, approximately fifty Thai armed forces personnel fired on the village of Chi Ring in Oddar Meanchey province of Cambodia with automatic weapons and heavy weapons for half an hour on the morning of the 17th.
    The same news agency also reported on the same day that a U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet army F-105 aircraft intruded into the airspace of a village in Kandal province of Cambodia on the morning of the 14th, flying from west to east, penetrating into the airspace of a village in Prey Veng province. When the aircraft flew over Kandal province, it was fired upon by Cambodian local forces with anti-aircraft artillery. (Attached image)
    Cambodian Royal Army intensifies military training to defend the independence, sovereignty, and national dignity of its country, preparing to give a head-on blow to invading enemies. Xinhua News Agency

### 西哈努克亲王重申不怕美帝及其走狗威胁  柬埔寨人民不惜牺牲保卫独立

1966-03-02
第4版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王重申不怕美帝及其走狗威胁
    柬埔寨人民不惜牺牲保卫独立
    新华社金边一日电  据柬埔寨新闻社一日报道，柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王二月二十六日在菩萨省发表的一次讲话中，号召柬埔寨人民紧密团结在一起，继续抱着坚定的决心，不惜以最大的牺牲来保卫柬埔寨的独立、中立和领土完整。
    西哈努克亲王在谴责泰国和南越傀儡当局对柬埔寨的挑衅时说，“象我们这样不怕死的人民，是永远打不垮的。”
    新华社金边二十八日电据柬埔寨新闻社二十八日报道，柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王二月二十五日在菩萨省举行的一次群众大会上发表讲话时说：不管帝国主义者以及它们的走狗扩张主义者和“自由高棉”分子干出什么样的勾当来，他们都不能够迫使我们屈服。
    西哈努克指出，帝国主义的报纸已经宣布，说什么“自由高棉”分子的军队将侵入柬埔寨，并且说，这支军队已从一万人增加到三万人。
    西哈努克说，“我们知道，这支军队现在刚满三百人。但是即使它拥有十万人，人民社会同盟的柬埔寨人也不会耽心。”
    他指出，现在在柬埔寨周围的有美国、泰国以及西贡和万象傀儡政权的军队和警察一共一百四十万人。“这就是柬埔寨的一百四十万敌人。如果我们对这样大量的敌人都不感到害怕，那么，为什么我们会害怕三万名‘自由高棉’分子呢？”
    新华社金边二十七日电据柬埔寨新闻社二十七日报道，泰国武装部队约五十人，十七日上午用自动武器和重武器向柬埔寨奥多棉藏省奇林村射击半小时。
    这家通讯社在同一天还报道，一架美国—南越伪军F—105型飞机十四日上午侵入柬埔寨干丹省一个村庄的上空，由西往东飞行，深入到波萝勉省的一个村庄的上空。这架飞机在干丹省上空飞行时，遭到柬埔寨地方部队高射炮的射击。（附图片）
    柬埔寨王国军队为保卫自己国家的独立、主权和民族尊严，加紧军事训练，准备给来犯的敌人以迎头痛击。新华社发
