Title: Chinese Acrobatic Troupe's First Performance in Brussels: Splendid Performances Attract Over a Thousand Spectators; Almost Every Act Receives Enthusiastic Applause

Author:

Date: 1966-03-20
Edition: 4

    My Acrobatic Troupe Performs for the First Time in Brussels
    Splendid Performances Attract Over a Thousand Spectators, Almost Every Act Receives Enthusiastic Applause
    (Xinhua News Agency, Brussels) The Chinese Acrobatic Troupe held its premiere performance in Brussels on the evening of March 8.
    The Chinese artists' splendid performances captivated over 1,400 spectators. Almost every act was greeted with enthusiastic applause. At the end of the performance, the Chinese artists unfurled a large red banner with the words "Long Live the Friendship Between the Chinese and Belgian Peoples." The mood of the entire audience was high.
    After the performance, a reception was held to welcome the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe. Williams of the Belgian Association for the Promotion of Art and Culture, and Dekeyser, Vice-Chairman of the Belgium-China Association, spoke at the reception. Dekeyser emphasized the high artistic value of the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe and spoke about the widespread popularity of various art forms among the Chinese people.
    Xu Chuanshi, head of the troupe and Director of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, expressed gratitude for the warm welcome the troupe had received.
    Also present at the reception were Madame Rellyconn and Rocher, Vice-Chairmen of the Belgium-China Association, Madame Housman, Secretary-General of the Association, Baron Arlaud, Paul Struye, Speaker of the Senate, Roland, Minister of State, as well as Griba, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Belgian Communist Party, and other leading comrades of the Belgian Communist Party.
    The Chinese Acrobatic Troupe arrived in Brussels in two groups on the 4th and 5th, respectively.

### 我杂技艺术团在布鲁塞尔首次演出  精采的表演吸引千余观众，几乎每个节目都受到热烈欢迎

1966-03-20
第4版()
专栏：中国文化在国外

    我杂技艺术团在布鲁塞尔首次演出
    精采的表演吸引千余观众，几乎每个节目都受到热烈欢迎
    据新华社布鲁塞尔电  中国杂技艺术团三月八日晚在布鲁塞尔举行首次演出。
    中国艺术家的精采表演吸引住了一千四百多名观众。几乎每个节目都受到热烈鼓掌欢迎。演出结束时，中国艺术家们展开一块大红布横幅，上面写着“中比人民友好万岁”。全场观众的情绪高涨。
    演出结束后，举行了招待会，欢迎中国杂技艺术团。比利时艺术和文化普及协会的威廉斯和比中协会副主席戴克斯在会上讲了话。戴克斯强调指出，中国杂技艺术团的高度艺术价值，并且谈到各种艺术形式在中国人民中间广泛流行的情况。
    艺术团团长、中国人民对外文化协会理事徐传实对艺术团所受到的热烈欢迎表示感谢。
    出席招待会的还有比中协会副主席雷利康夫人和罗歇，协会秘书长胡斯曼夫人，阿拉男爵，参议院议长保罗·斯特律耶，国务大臣罗兰，以及比利时共产党中央委员会书记格里巴和比共其他领导同志。
    中国杂技艺术团是分两批先后在四日和五日到达布鲁塞尔的。
