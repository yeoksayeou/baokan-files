Title: Major U.S. Military Bases in France
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-16
Edition: 4

    U.S. retains 40 military facilities, 14 air bases, and 30,000 troops stationed in France.

    France is the so-called "zone of communication" for the U.S. Army in Europe. The U.S. Army currently has more than ten warehouses, facilities, activity centers, or barracks in France. The main ports used by the U.S. Army are Saint-Nazaire, La Rochelle, and Bordeaux. These three places are also the locations of the U.S. Army's main warehouses. Other major places with U.S. military warehouses include Déols-Martinerie, Orléans, Verdun, and Nancy. U.S. military supplies, such as ammunition, are stored in France. Oil pipelines and giant oil pool areas are also located in France. The main French port used by the U.S. Navy is Villefranche.

    Of the fourteen U.S. Air Force bases in France, four are used to the maximum extent, while the rest are used or kept in reserve by rotating air force squadrons or small batches of air force troops. The airfield near Évreux is a huge U.S. Air Force base.

    According to the bilateral agreement between the United States and France, it is stipulated that the U.S. military headquarters should be established in Saint-Germain-en-Laye in France.

### 美国在法国的主要军事基地

1966-03-16
第4版()
专栏：国际知识

    美国在法国的主要军事基地
    美国在法国保有四十个军事设施、十四个空军基地和三万名驻军。
    法国是美国陆军在欧洲的所谓“交通地带”。美国陆军目前在法国拥有十多处仓库、设施、活动中心或兵营。美国陆军使用的主要港口是圣纳泽尔、拉罗舍耳、波尔多。这三个地方也是美国陆军主要仓库的所在地。其他设有美军仓库的主要地方有德奥尔—马丁奈利、奥尔良、凡尔登、南锡等。美国的军用补给，如弹药等都存放在法国。油管和巨型油池区也设在法国。美国海军使用的法国港口，主要的是维耳夫郎舍。
    美国空军在法国的十四个空军基地中，四个在最大限度地加以使用，其余的则由轮换的空军中队或小批空军部队使用或备用。埃夫勒附近的机场是美国的巨大空军基地。
    根据美国和法国达成的双边协定，规定在法国圣日耳曼设立美军司令部。
