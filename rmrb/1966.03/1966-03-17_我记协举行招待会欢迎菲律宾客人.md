Title: Chinese Journalists Association Holds Reception to Welcome Philippine Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-03-17
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 16th -- The All-China Journalists Association held a reception tonight to welcome journalists from the Philippine fact-finding delegation currently visiting Beijing.
    At the reception, Wang Yunsheng, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, and members of the capital's press circles, together with Filipino friends, toasted the friendship between the peoples of China and the Philippines and between the journalists of the two countries.

### 我记协举行招待会欢迎菲律宾客人

1966-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

    我记协举行招待会欢迎菲律宾客人
    新华社十六日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会今晚举行招待会，欢迎正在北京访问的菲律宾考察代表团中的新闻工作者。
    在招待会上，中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席王芸生和首都新闻界人士，同菲律宾朋友一起，为中菲两国人民和两国新闻工作者的友谊干杯。
