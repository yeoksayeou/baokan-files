Title: Syria Forms New Cabinet
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-03
Edition: 4

    Damascus, March 1 (Xinhua) – According to a broadcast from the local radio station this afternoon, the new Syrian head of state, Nur al-Din al-Atassi, signed an order to establish a new Syrian cabinet.

    The new cabinet consists of twenty members, eight of whom were members of the former Zein cabinet. They are Prime Minister Yusuf Zein, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Ibrahim Mahus, Interior Minister Muhammad Ashawi, Minister of Land Reform Abdul Karim Jundi, Minister of Urban and Rural Affairs Sal Mahamid, Minister of Education Mustafa Haddad, Minister of Public Works Mamdu Jaber, and Minister of Supplies Mashur Zeton.

    Air Force Commander Major General Hafez al-Assad will exercise the functions of the Minister of Defense.

### 叙利亚组成新内阁

1966-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

    叙利亚组成新内阁
    据新华社大马士革一日电　据这里电台一日下午广播，叙利亚新国家元首努尔丁·阿塔西签署一项成立叙利亚新内阁的命令。
    新内阁由二十人组成，其中八人是前扎因内阁的成员，他们是总理优素福·扎因，副总理兼外交部长易卜拉欣·马胡斯，内政部长穆罕默德·阿沙维，土地改革部长阿卜杜勒·卡里姆·琼迪，城乡事务部长萨勒·马哈米德、教育部长穆斯塔法·哈达德、公共工程部长马姆杜·贾贝尔和供应部长马舒尔·泽顿。
    空军司令哈菲兹·阿萨德少将行使国防部长的职权。
