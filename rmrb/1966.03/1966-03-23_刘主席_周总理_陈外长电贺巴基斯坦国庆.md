Title: Chairman Liu, Premier Zhou, and Foreign Minister Chen Send Congratulatory Messages on Pakistan's National Day

Author:

Date: 1966-03-23
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 22nd – Chairman of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi and Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai sent a telegram on March 22nd to Pakistani President Ayub Khan, congratulating the Islamic Republic of Pakistan on its National Day. The full text of the congratulatory telegram is as follows:

    To His Excellency Field Marshal Mohammad Ayub Khan, President of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, Rawalpindi:

    On the occasion of the National Day of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, we are pleased to extend warm congratulations to Your Excellency, the Government of Pakistan, and the people of Pakistan.

    The Government and people of Pakistan have achieved new successes in safeguarding national sovereignty, implementing an independent and autonomous policy, and developing the national economy and culture. The Chinese people are pleased with this and sincerely wish the Government and people of Pakistan greater achievements in the future.

    There is a profound traditional friendship between the peoples of China and Pakistan. In recent years, with the joint efforts of both sides, the friendly and cooperative relations between our two countries have achieved new development. This not only conforms to the common interests of the peoples of China and Pakistan, but also benefits the noble cause of Asian and African people's unity against imperialism and the safeguarding of world peace. The Chinese Government and the Chinese people firmly support Pakistan's just struggle to defend national independence and resist foreign aggression. The Chinese people will always be reliable friends of the Pakistani people.

    May the friendly and cooperative relations between China and Pakistan be continuously consolidated and developed.

    May Pakistan enjoy prosperity and its people happiness.

    Liu Shaoqi
    Chairman of the People's Republic of China

    Zhou Enlai
    Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China

    March 22, 1966

    Xinhua News Agency, March 22nd – Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Chen Yi, sent a telegram to Pakistani Foreign Minister Bhutto on March 22nd, congratulating the Islamic Republic of Pakistan on its National Day. The full text of the congratulatory telegram is as follows:

    To His Excellency Z. A. Bhutto, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, Rawalpindi:

    On the occasion of the National Day of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, please accept my sincere congratulations. I wish Your Excellency and the people of Pakistan new and greater achievements in safeguarding national independence, strengthening Asian-African unity, and maintaining world peace. May the friendly and cooperative relations between China and Pakistan continue to develop.

    Chen Yi
    Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China

    March 22, 1966

### 刘主席　周总理　陈外长电贺巴基斯坦国庆

1966-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席　周总理　陈外长电贺巴基斯坦国庆
    新华社二十二日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇和国务院总理周恩来二十二日打电报给巴基斯坦总统阿尤布·汗，祝贺巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国国庆。贺电全文如下：拉瓦尔品第巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国总统穆罕默德·阿尤布·汗元帅阁下：
    在巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国国庆的时候，我们愉快地向阁下，向巴基斯坦政府和巴基斯坦人民致以热烈的祝贺。
    巴基斯坦政府和人民在捍卫国家主权、执行独立自主政策、发展民族经济和文化的事业中，获得了新的成就，中国人民为此感到高兴，并且衷心祝愿巴基斯坦政府和人民今后取得更大的成就。
    中巴两国人民之间存在着深厚的传统友谊。近年来，在双方的共同努力下，我们两国的友好合作关系，获得了新的发展。这不仅符合中巴两国人民的共同利益，而且有利于亚非人民团结反帝和维护世界和平的崇高事业。中国政府和中国人民坚决支持巴基斯坦捍卫民族独立、抗击外来侵略的正义斗争。中国人民永远是巴基斯坦人民可靠的朋友。
    祝中巴两国的友好合作关系日益巩固和发展。
    祝巴基斯坦国家繁荣昌盛，人民幸福。
    中华人民共和国
                主席　刘少奇
    中华人民共和国国务院
                总理　周恩来
    一九六六年三月二十二日
    新华社二十二日讯　中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅二十二日致电巴基斯坦外交部长布托，祝贺巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国国庆。贺电全文如下：拉瓦尔品第巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国外交部
  长佐·阿·布托阁下：
    在巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国国庆的时候，请接受我衷心的祝贺。祝阁下和巴基斯坦人民在捍卫民族独立、加强亚非团结和维护世界和平的事业中，取得新的、更大的成就。祝中巴两国之间的友好合作关系不断向前发展。
    中华人民共和国
            外交部长　陈毅
    一九六六年三月二十二日
