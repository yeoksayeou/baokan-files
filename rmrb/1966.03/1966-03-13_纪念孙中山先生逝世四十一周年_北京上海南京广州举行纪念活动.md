Title: Commemoration of the 41st Anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's Death: Memorial Activities Held in Beijing, Shanghai, Nanjing, and Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1966-03-13
Edition: 2

    Commemoration of the 41st Anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's Death
    Memorial Activities Held in Beijing, Shanghai, Nanjing, and Guangzhou
    Xinhua News Agency, 12th—Today marks the 41st anniversary of the death of Sun Yat-sen. Liu Shutong, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, Cai Tingkai and Xiong Kewu, Vice Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, and others, visited the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall at the Biyun Temple in the Western Hills this morning to pay their respects.
    At 10:00 AM, a memorial ceremony was held at the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall. Liu Shutong, on behalf of Zhou Enlai, Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC and Premier of the State Council, and Comrade Deng Yingchao, Xu Bing, Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC, and Comrade Zhang Xiaomei, as well as the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, presented three flower baskets before the portrait of Sun Yat-sen.
    Cai Tingkai presented a flower basket on behalf of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang before the portrait of Sun Yat-sen.
    Also participating in the memorial ceremony were members of the Standing Committee and members of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, including Lu Yuwen, Zhu Yunshan, Liu Fei, Yu Xinqing, Shao Lizi, Qu Wu, Chen Cisheng, Chen Shaoxian, Chen Qiyuan, Hou Jingru, Mei Gongbin, Qian Changzhao, and others.
    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 12th—Responsible persons from the Party and government of Shanghai, as well as representatives from various sectors, visited the former residence of Sun Yat-sen to commemorate the 41st anniversary of his death.
    Those who visited the former residence of Mr. Sun Yat-sen included Cao Diqiu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee and Mayor of Shanghai, Liang Guobin, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee and Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, Yang Xiguang, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee, Song Richang, Song Jiwen, Li Gancheng, Zhang Chengzong, and Zhao Zukang, Deputy Mayors of Shanghai, Chen Wangdao, Chen Tongsheng, Shen Tilan, Liu Jingji, Li Zhaohuan, Zhou Gucheng, Wang Zhizhong, Wu Ruoan, and Lu Yudao, Vice Chairmen of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPPCC, as well as responsible persons from various democratic parties' Shanghai municipal organizations and the Municipal Women's Federation, and other sectors.
    Before the portrait of Mr. Sun Yat-sen, wreaths were placed on behalf of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee, the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee, and the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPPCC.
    Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 12th—Responsible persons from all circles in Nanjing paid their respects at the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum on the 12th to commemorate the 41st anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's death.
    At 9:00 AM, a ceremony of paying respects was held at the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum. Chen Yang, Vice Governor of Jiangsu Province, Yue Weifan, Secretary of the CPC Nanjing Municipal Committee and Mayor of Nanjing, Zhang Guangzhong, Vice Chairman of the Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the CPPCC, and Xu Meifeng, Vice Chairman of the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the CPPCC, respectfully presented wreaths before the seated statue of Sun Yat-sen on behalf of the Provincial and Municipal People's Committees, and the Provincial and Municipal Committees of the CPPCC, respectively. Subsequently, all those who paid respects at the mausoleum entered the tomb of Mr. Sun Yat-sen and circled it once to express their respect.
    Also participating in the mausoleum visit were Wu Yifang and Liu Guojun, Vice Governors of Jiangsu Province, Ye Xuzhao, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Jiangsu Provincial Committee, Liu Shuxun, Wang Zhaoquan, and Chen Suiheng, Deputy Mayors of Nanjing, as well as responsible persons from various democratic parties and local organizations of people's groups.
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 12th—People from all walks of life in Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City held a memorial ceremony in front of the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall in Guangzhou City this afternoon to commemorate the 41st anniversary of the death of Sun Yat-sen.
    Before the statue of Sun Yat-sen in the Memorial Hall, wreaths were placed on behalf of the CPC Guangdong Provincial Committee, the Guangdong Provincial People's Committee, the CPC Guangzhou Municipal Committee, the Guangzhou Municipal People's Committee, the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the CPPCC, the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the CPPCC, and the provincial and municipal local organizations of various democratic parties, as well as non-party democratic personages.
    Feng Shen, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the CPPCC, presided over the memorial ceremony and presented a wreath to the statue of Sun Yat-sen, after which all participants paid their respects to the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall.
    Participating in the memorial ceremony were Guo Dihuo and Xu Chongqing, Vice Governors of Guangdong Province, Zhang Boquan, Tan Tiandu, Xiao Junying, and Yi Meihou, Vice Chairmen of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the CPPCC, Luo Peiyuan and Chen Qiu'an, Deputy Mayors of Guangzhou, Li Dachao and Lin Zhicheng, Vice Chairmen of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the CPPCC, responsible persons from the Guangdong and Guangzhou municipal local organizations of various democratic parties, as well as friends of Mr. Sun Yat-sen during his lifetime, and others.

### 纪念孙中山先生逝世四十一周年  北京上海南京广州举行纪念活动

1966-03-13
第2版()
专栏：

    纪念孙中山先生逝世四十一周年
    北京上海南京广州举行纪念活动
    新华社十二日讯　今天是孙中山先生逝世四十一周年。中共中央统一战线工作部副部长刘述周，中国国民党革命委员会中央副主席蔡廷锴、熊克武等，今天上午前往西山碧云寺，晋谒孙中山先生纪念堂。
    十时，在孙中山先生纪念堂举行了晋谒仪式。刘述周代表政协全国委员会主席、国务院总理周恩来和邓颖超同志，中共中央统一战线工作部部长、政协全国委员会副主席徐冰和张晓梅同志，以及中共中央统一战线工作部，在孙中山先生遗象前献了三个花篮。
    蔡廷锴代表民革中央委员会在孙中山先生遗像前献了花篮。
    参加晋谒仪式的，还有民革中央常务委员会委员和中央委员卢郁文、朱蕴山、刘斐、余心清、邵力子、屈武、陈此生、陈劭先、陈其瑗、侯镜如、梅龚彬、钱昌照等。
    新华社上海十二日电　上海市党和政府以及各方面的负责人，瞻仰中山故居，纪念孙中山先生逝世四十一周年。
    前往中山先生故居的有，中共上海市委书记处书记、上海市市长曹荻秋，中共上海市委书记处书记、上海市副市长梁国斌，中共上海市委书记处候补书记杨西光，上海市副市长宋日昌、宋季文、李干成、张承宗、赵祖康，政协上海市委员会副主席陈望道、陈同生、沈体兰、刘靖基、黎照寰、周谷城、王致中、吴若安、卢于道以及各民主党派上海市地方组织和市妇联等各方面的负责人。
    在孙中山先生遗像前，放着中共上海市委员会、上海市人民委员会、政协上海市委员会献的花圈。
    新华社南京十二日电　南京各界负责人十二日谒中山陵，纪念孙中山先生逝世四十一周年。
    上午九时，在中山灵堂举行了谒陵仪式。江苏省副省长陈扬，中共南京市委书记、南京市市长岳维藩，政协江苏省委员会副主席张光中，政协南京市委员会副主席徐美峰，分别代表省、市人民委员会，政协省、市委员会，在孙中山先生坐像前敬献花圈。随后，全体谒陵人员进入孙中山先生陵寝绕行一周，以表敬意。
    参加谒陵的，还有江苏省副省长吴贻芳、刘国钧，中共江苏省委统战部副部长叶胥朝，南京市副市长刘树勋、王昭铨、陈邃衡以及各民主党派、各人民团体地方组织的负责人等。
    新华社广州十二日电　广东省、广州市各界人士今天下午在广州市中山纪念堂前举行纪念仪式，纪念孙中山先生逝世四十一周年。
    在纪念堂孙中山先生塑象前，摆着中共广东省委员会、广东省人民委员会，中共广州市委员会、广州市人民委员会，政协广东省委员会、广州市委员会和各民主党派省、市地方组织以及无党派民主人士敬献的花圈。
    政协广东省委员会副主席冯燊主持纪念仪式，向孙中山先生塑象敬献了花圈，随后全体人员瞻仰了中山纪念堂。
    参加纪念仪式的，有广东省副省长郭棣活、许崇清，政协广东省委员会副主席张?村、谭天度、萧隽英、蚁美厚，广州市副市长罗培元、陈秋安，政协广州市委员会副主席李达潮、林志澄，各民主党派广东省、广州市地方组织负责人以及孙中山先生生前友好等。
