Title: The Australian Government Willingly Acts as an Accomplice to the US Aggression Against Vietnam; Openly Decides to Send More Cannon Fodder to South Vietnam
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-10
Edition: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Canberra, 9th -- The Australian government has decided to increase the number of its accomplice troops sent to South Vietnam from 1,500 to 4,500 in May of this year.

　　Australian Prime Minister Holt announced this in the House of Representatives on the 8th. He said that this "task force" will include two infantry battalions, an air force service squadron, paratroopers, and some logistical support troops, along with eight helicopters. Holt also said that a small team of transport aircraft and one hundred army jungle warfare advisors will continue to be provided to South Vietnam.

　　When announcing the above decision, Holt also revealed to the House of Representatives that the Australian government had requested the United States to purchase a large quantity of ammunition for small arms in Australia, and this deal has been reached.

　　Inducing the Australian government to provide more cannon fodder for the U.S. war of aggression against Vietnam was the main topic when U.S. Vice President Humphrey visited Australia and met with Holt in mid-February this year. The U.S. State Department immediately expressed "heartfelt welcome" upon the announcement of Holt's decision to increase troops.

### 澳大利亚政府甘心充当美国侵越帮凶  公然决定要增派炮灰去南越

1966-03-10
第4版()
专栏：

    澳大利亚政府甘心充当美国侵越帮凶
    公然决定要增派炮灰去南越
    新华社九日讯  堪培拉消息：澳大利亚政府决定在今年五月把它派往南越的帮凶军由一千五百人增加到四千五百人。
    澳大利亚总理霍尔特八日在众议院宣布了这件事。他说，这支“特遣部队”将包括两个步兵营、一个空军勤务中队、伞兵，以及一些后勤支援部队，同时派去的还有八架直升飞机。霍尔特还说，将继续给南越提供一小队运输机和一百个陆军的丛林战顾问。
    霍尔特在宣布上述决定时，还向众议院透露，澳大利亚政府曾经要求美国在澳购买大量小型武器的弹药，这笔买卖已经达成了协议。
    诱迫澳大利亚政府为美国侵略越南的战争提供更多炮灰，是今年二月中旬美国副总统汉弗莱访问澳大利亚同霍尔特会谈时的主要议题。霍尔特增兵的决定一经宣布，美国国务院当天就立即表示“衷心的欢迎”。
