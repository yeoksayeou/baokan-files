Title: Polish Trade Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1966-03-29
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency reported that the Polish government trade delegation, led by Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of Poland, Dmochowski, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 25th to return home. Among those who went to the airport to see them off were Jiang Ming, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and others. The Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China, Stachwiak, and others also went to the airport to see them off.

### 波贸易代表团回国

1966-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

    波贸易代表团回国
    新华社讯  波兰政府贸易代表团在团长、波兰对外贸易部副部长德莫霍夫斯基率领下，二十五日上午乘飞机离京回国。前往机场送行的，有对外贸易部副部长江明等。波兰驻中国大使馆临时代办斯塔赫维亚克等也到机场送行。
