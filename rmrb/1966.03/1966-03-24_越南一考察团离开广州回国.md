Title: Vietnamese Inspection Group Leaves Guangzhou for Home

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-24
Edition: 4

    A Vietnamese Democratic Republic Urban Management Work Inspection Group concluded its friendly visit to our country and left Guangzhou for home today, according to a dispatch from Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, March 21.
    Luo Peiyuan, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou, and responsible persons from relevant departments went to the station to see them off. Nguyen Van Tac, Consul of the Vietnamese Consulate General in Guangzhou, also went to the station to see them off.

### 越南一考察团离开广州回国

1966-03-24
第4版()
专栏：

    越南一考察团离开广州回国
    据新华社广州二十一日电　越南民主共和国城市管理工作考察团，结束了在我国的友好访问，今天离开广州回国。
    广州市副市长罗培元以及有关方面负责人到车站欢送。越南驻广州总领事馆领事阮文爵也到车站送行。
