Title: Delegation of the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee Leaves for Visit to Japan
Author:
Date: 1966-03-04
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 3rd—A delegation from the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee, led by Yang Shuo, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee, left Beijing by plane today for a visit to Japan at the invitation of the Japanese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee.

 

 

**Named Entities**

中国亚非团结委员会	  Zhongguo Yafei Tuanjie Weiyuanhui	Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee
杨朔    Yang Shuo
北京    Beijing
日本亚非团结委员会	  Ribeng Yafei Tuanjie Weiyuanhui	Japanese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee
新华社   Xinhua News Agency

### 我亚非团结会代表团去日本访问

1966-03-04
第4版()
专栏：

    我亚非团结会代表团去日本访问
    新华社三日讯　由中国亚非团结委员会副主席杨朔率领的中国亚非团结委员会代表团，应日本亚非团结委员会邀请，今天乘飞机离开北京前往日本访问。
