Title: Indian Authorities' Brutal Suppression of Anti-Hunger Demonstrations Arouses Public Outrage; Calcutta Demonstrators Burn Down Government Offices
Author:
Date: 1966-03-07
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, 5th dispatch from Calcutta: Students in Nadia and Murshidabad, suburbs of Calcutta, held large-scale anti-hunger demonstrations on the 4th, angrily protesting the police authorities' brutal acts of continuously opening fire and suppressing demonstrators in Barasat and 24 Parganas since February 16th.
    According to a report in the *Indian Express* on the 5th, in Krishnanagar, Nadia, police repeatedly used tear gas and beat demonstrating students with iron-clad batons. Demonstrators valiantly resisted the police suppression in the streets. It was reported that one student was beaten to death, twenty were injured, and seventy-eight were arrested.
    The police's brutality aroused even greater anger among the demonstrators, who surrounded many government office buildings in Krishnanagar and burned down ten government offices.
    According to reports in the *Hindustan Standard* and other Indian newspapers, student and other mass demonstrations continued in Calcutta city and suburban areas such as Howrah, Barasat, Behala, Barrackpore, and Basirhat on the 3rd and 4th, demanding that the Indian government provide reasonable food supplies and conduct a judicial investigation into the police killing of demonstrating students.
    According to the Press Trust of India, the Inspector General of Police of Uttar Pradesh has, at the request of the West Bengal government, transferred two companies of police, a total of two hundred and forty men, to the Calcutta area, preparing for even more brutal suppression of the demonstrators there. ### 印度当局镇压反饥饿示威的暴行激起民愤  加尔各答示威群众烧毁政府机关

1966-03-07
第3版()
专栏：

    印度当局镇压反饥饿示威的暴行激起民愤
    加尔各答示威群众烧毁政府机关
    新华社五日讯　加尔各答消息：加尔各答郊区纳迪亚和麦尔希达巴德的学生四日举行大规模反饥饿示威，愤怒抗议警察当局从二月十六日以来不断对巴塞哈特和二十四—帕加纳斯的示威群众开枪镇压的残暴行为。
    据《印度快报》五日报道，在纳迪亚的克里希纳加尔镇，警察一再使用催泪弹，并用包铁皮的警棍殴打示威的学生，示威者在大街上英勇抗击了警察的镇压。据报道，一名学生被打死，二十人受伤，七十八人被逮捕。
    警察的暴行引起示威群众更大的愤怒，他们包围了克里希纳加尔的许多政府机关的房子，焚烧了十座政府机关的办公室。
    据《印度斯坦旗报》和其他印度报纸报道，在加尔各答市内以及豪拉、巴拉萨特、贝哈拉、巴拉克普尔、巴塞哈特等郊区各地，三日和四日不断有学生和其他群众进行示威游行，要求印度政府合理供应粮食和对警察打死示威学生一事进行司法调查。
    据印度报业托辣斯报道，北方邦警察总监已经应西孟加拉邦政府的要求，抽调了两连警察一共二百四十名前往加尔各答地区，准备对那里的示威群众进行更加残暴的镇压。（附图片）
    加尔各答妇女群众最近举行反饥饿示威游行，要求政府释放那些在反饥饿斗争中被捕的人们。　传真照片（新华社发）
