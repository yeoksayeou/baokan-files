Title: American "Challenge" Weekly Exposes US-Soviet Collusion Against China; Soviet Leaders Profess Unity But Are Actually "Uniting" with US Imperialism; US Shifts Focus of Aggression Eastward Because US-Soviet Reconciliation Assures It

Author:
Date: 1966-03-29
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 28th, New York Dispatch: The American Progressive Labor Party's organ, the "Challenge" weekly, published an article on the 22nd exposing the collusion between the United States and the Soviet Union against China.

    The article stated: In recent weeks, an increasing number of Americans have awakened and are deeply shocked to realize that Johnson's ruthless escalation in Vietnam is bringing them to the brink of a disastrous war with China. Although Washington's global strategists have hoped, and still attempt, to keep the American people and the world in the dark about the ultimate goal of their "quiet" escalation, they have finally revealed their true colors!

    The article said that Washington regards China's resolute opposition to US imperialism and China's support for the revolutionary struggles of all peoples as the main obstacle to implementing its policies of domination and control. It has shifted the focus of American aggression from Europe to Asia. At this moment, the Western front is quiet!

    The article said: In Moscow, Khrushchev's political successors grandly talk about the "unity of the socialist camp." However, in the Geneva talks on "non-proliferation" (of nuclear weapons), they sought to unite with US imperialism to monopolize nuclear weapons. In New Delhi, Kosygin, Rusk, and Humphrey held such "frank, candid, and constructive talks" that Humphrey could say to American television viewers on January 16, "It is a fact that the Soviet Union is trying to build a wall around China to contain it," "It (the Soviet Union) is primarily concerned about what Communist China, rather than the United States, might do."

    The article said that in West Berlin, the current Soviet leaders are striving for "reconciliation and more reconciliation" in an attempt to create the "right atmosphere" for US-Soviet cooperation. US-Soviet collusion is what they dream of; it is the pillar of their foreign policy; everything else is secondary. Meanwhile, Washington's military strategists confidently transfer most of their military forces from the West to the East, fully assured that, although Vietnam is bleeding and China is threatened, no "socialist unity" will threaten the security of their "rear."

    The article pointed out: Without a doubt, now, just as in the Hitler era, fascist aggression is "in action." US imperialism is today's Hitlerism. This Hitlerism has developed so enormously that its tentacles have extended to the whole world. Those who believe they can trade with US imperialism, appease it, "reconcile" with it, and those who cowardly believe they will be allowed to share the spoils, are the "Munichmen" of 1966.

    The article concluded: In the face of the rising anti-war movement in the United States, the increasingly in-depth struggle of black people, the expanding strike movement, economic instability, and disagreements within the US ruling group on how to act, the US government is continuing to try to maintain the old lie of so-called universal "consensus" through widespread propaganda of confusing and fabricated "opinion poll" results. The US government is desperately doing this precisely because there is no "consensus." The movement against the Vietnam War is increasingly combining with the domestic struggle for democracy and freedom. March 26th must become, and will become, the largest, broadest, and most in-depth mass protest action in our history.

### 美国《挑战》周报揭露美苏合谋反对中国  苏联领导口喊团结实际上是同美帝“团结”  美国侵略重点东移是因为苏美和解可以使它放心

1966-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

    美国《挑战》周报揭露美苏合谋反对中国
    苏联领导口喊团结实际上是同美帝“团结”
    美国侵略重点东移是因为苏美和解可以使它放心
    新华社二十八日讯  纽约消息：美国进步劳工党的机关刊物《挑战》周报二十二日刊载一篇文章，揭露美苏两国合谋反对中国。
    文章说：在最近几个星期里，越来越多的美国人已经觉醒过来并且深感震惊地认识到，约翰逊冷酷地在越南逐步升级正在把他们带到同中国发生一场灾难性的战争的边缘。虽然华盛顿的全球战略家曾经希望、而且现在仍然企图使美国人民和全世界摸不清他们“悄悄”逐步升级的最后目标何在，但是他们究竟还是露出了马脚！
    文章说，华盛顿把中国坚决反对美帝国主义和中国支持各国人民的革命斗争看作是推行美国的统治和控制政策的主要障碍，并且已经把美国的侵略的焦点从欧洲转移到了亚洲，可是在这个时候，西线却平静无事！
    文章说：在莫斯科，赫鲁晓夫的政治继承人振振有辞地大谈“社会主义阵营的团结”。但是在日内瓦关于“不扩散”（核武器）的会谈中，他们为了垄断核武器却设法同美帝国主义团结。在新德里，柯西金、腊斯克和汉弗莱举行了那么“坦白、坦率和建设性的会谈”，以致汉弗莱一月十六日可以对美国电视观众说，“苏联设法在中国周围修建一道遏制中国的墙，这是事实”，“它（苏联）担心的主要是共产党中国而不是美国可能干出什么事情来”。
    文章说，在西柏林，由于苏联现任领导人力图为美苏合作制造“正确气氛”，他们的心情是争取“和解和更多的和解”，美苏勾结是他们梦寐以求的东西，是他们外交政策的支柱，其他一切都是次要的。与此同时，华盛顿的军事战略家满有信心地把他们的大部分军事力量从西部调到东部，满有把握地认为，虽然越南在流血，中国受到威胁，任何“社会主义的团结”都不会威胁他们“后方”的安全。
    文章指出：毫无疑问，现在正象希特勒时代一样，法西斯侵略“正在行动中”。美帝国主义就是今天的希特勒主义。这个希特勒主义已经发展得非常庞大，它的触角已伸到全世界。那些认为自己能够同美帝国主义进行交易、姑息它、同它“和解”，和怯懦地认为他们将被允许分享赃物的人，就是一九六六年的“慕尼黑人”。
    文章最后说：面对着美国国内日益高涨的反战运动，黑人的越来越深入的斗争，日益扩大的罢工运动，经济的不稳定和美国统治集团内部在作法上的意见分歧，美国政府通过广泛地宣传混乱的和捏造的“民意测验”的结果，正在继续设法保持所谓普遍“意见一致”的陈旧的谎言。美国政府拼命这样做，正是因为并不存在什么“意见一致”。反对越南战争的运动正在日益同国内争取民主和自由的斗争结合起来。三月二十六日必须成为而且也会成为我国历史上最大、最广泛和最深入的群众抗议行动。
