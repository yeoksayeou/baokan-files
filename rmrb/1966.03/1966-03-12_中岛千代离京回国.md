Title: Nakajima Chiyo Departs Beijing for Home

Author:
Date: 1966-03-12
Edition: 4

    Nakajima Chiyo Departs Beijing for Home
    Xinhua News Agency, 10th – Nakajima Chiyo, Permanent Director of the Japan Women's Organizations Federation and Central Permanent Committee Member of the New Japan Women's Association, left Beijing by plane this morning to return to her country after visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation.
    Those who went to the airport to see her off included Yang Yunyu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation and member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and others.

### 中岛千代离京回国

1966-03-12
第4版()
专栏：

    中岛千代离京回国
    新华社十日讯　日本妇女团体联合会常任干事、新日本妇女会中央常任委员中岛千代应全国妇联邀请来我国访问后，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    前往机场欢送的有：全国妇联书记处书记、人大常委会委员杨蕴玉等。
