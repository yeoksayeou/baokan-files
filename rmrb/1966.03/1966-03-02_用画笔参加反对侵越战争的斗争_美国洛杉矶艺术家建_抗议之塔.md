Title: Using Paintbrushes to Participate in the Struggle Against the War of Aggression Against Vietnam: Los Angeles Artists in the United States Build "Protest Tower"
Author:
Date: 1966-03-02
Edition: 3

    Using Paintbrushes to Participate in the Struggle Against the War of Aggression Against Vietnam
    Los Angeles Artists in the United States Build "Protest Tower"
    Xinhua News Agency, 28th - Los Angeles News: A group of artists in Los Angeles, USA, completed a "Protest Tower" in Hollywood's "Sunset Plaza" on the 26th to protest the US government's war of aggression against Vietnam.
    This painting tower hangs more than two hundred pictures against the war of aggression against Vietnam. The tower is sixty feet high and is built of purple and yellow steel bars.
    Since the construction of this painting tower, some reactionaries have attempted to destroy it. In order to prevent the painting tower from being destroyed, the artists who built the tower have been waging a struggle to defend it.

### 用画笔参加反对侵越战争的斗争  美国洛杉矶艺术家建“抗议之塔

1966-03-02
第3版()
专栏：

    用画笔参加反对侵越战争的斗争
    美国洛杉矶艺术家建“抗议之塔”
    新华社二十八日讯　洛杉矶消息：美国洛杉矶的一批艺术家二十六日在好莱坞的“夕阳广场”建成了一座抗议美国政府侵略越南战争的“抗议之塔”。
    这座画塔挂着二百多幅反对侵越战争的图画。塔高达六十英尺，是用紫色和黄色的钢条筑成的。
    自从这座画塔修建以来，就有一些反动分子企图加以破坏。为了防止画塔遭到破坏，建塔的艺术家们一直在进行着保卫画塔的斗争。
