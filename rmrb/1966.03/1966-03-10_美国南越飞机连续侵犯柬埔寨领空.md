Title: US-South Vietnamese Planes Continuously Violate Cambodian Airspace
Author:
Date: 1966-03-10
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, March 6th: According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on the 6th, US-South Vietnamese puppet troops' planes continue to violate Cambodian airspace.
    On February 28th, a US-South Vietnamese puppet troops' plane intruded into the airspace of Ratanakiri Province and fired two rockets at a Cambodian border post.
    On February 26th, another US-South Vietnamese puppet troops' plane intruded into the airspace of Svay Rieng Province and fired a rocket at a village near the border.

### 美国南越飞机连续侵犯柬埔寨领空

1966-03-10
第4版()
专栏：

    美国南越飞机连续侵犯柬埔寨领空
    据新华社金边电  据柬埔寨新闻社六日报道，美国—南越伪军飞机继续侵犯柬埔寨领空。
    二月二十八日，一架美国—南越伪军飞机侵入腊塔纳基里省上空，并向柬埔寨边防哨所发射了两枚火箭。
    二月二十六日，另一架美国—南越伪军飞机侵入柴桢省上空，向边境附近的一个村庄发射了一枚火箭。
