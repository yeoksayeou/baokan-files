Title: Romanian Leaders Receive New Chinese Ambassador to Romania
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-25
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, March 23rd. Romanian Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Ion Gheorghe Maurer, received the new Chinese Ambassador to Romania, Zeng Yongquan, on the 23rd and held friendly talks with him.
    Present at the reception was Romanian Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, A. Măcinicí.

### 罗领导人接见我新任驻罗大使

1966-03-25
第4版()
专栏：

    罗领导人接见我新任驻罗大使
    新华社布加勒斯特二十三日电 罗马尼亚部长会议主席扬·格·毛雷尔二十三日接见了中国新任驻罗马尼亚大使曾涌泉并同他进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有罗马尼亚外交部副部长爱·米辛契斯库。
