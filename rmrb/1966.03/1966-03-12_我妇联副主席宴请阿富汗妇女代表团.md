Title: Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation Hosts Banquet for Afghan Women's Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-03-12
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 11th. Kang Keqing, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, hosted a banquet tonight in honor of the Afghan Women's Delegation led by Mrs. Saliha Farouk Etemadi, President of the Afghan Women's Association.

    Among those present at the banquet were Luo Shuzhang, Hu Ziang, Zhang Jie, Shen Qiyi, Zhang Xiaomei, and other relevant personnel.

### 我妇联副主席宴请阿富汗妇女代表团

1966-03-12
第4版()
专栏：

    我妇联副主席宴请阿富汗妇女代表团
    新华社十一日讯　全国妇联副主席康克清今晚设宴招待以阿富汗妇女会会长萨利哈·法鲁克·埃特马迪夫人率领的阿富汗妇女代表团。
    出席宴会作陪的有各有关方面人士罗叔章、胡子婴、张杰、沈其益、张晓梅等。
