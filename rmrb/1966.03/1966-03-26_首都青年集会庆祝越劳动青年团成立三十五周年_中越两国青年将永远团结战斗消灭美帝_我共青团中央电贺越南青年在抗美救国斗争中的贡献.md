Title: Capital Youth Rally Celebrates the 35th Anniversary of the Ho Chi Minh Communist Youth Union; Chinese and Vietnamese Youth Will Forever Unite and Fight to Eliminate U.S. Imperialism; Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Vietnamese Youth for Their Contributions in the Anti-American War of National Salvation
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-26
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, March 25th: More than 800 young people in the capital held a rally this afternoon to celebrate the 35th anniversary of the Ho Chi Minh Communist Youth Union and to extend warm congratulations to the heroic Vietnamese youth.
    Hoang Bac, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, and Hoang Van Hoan, member of the Permanent Delegation of the South Vietnam National Front for Liberation in China, were invited to give reports at the meeting.
    Yang Haibo, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, delivered a speech at the meeting. He said that the Ho Chi Minh Communist Youth Union unites the broad masses of young people and fights on the front lines of the Vietnamese people's great anti-American war of national salvation, demonstrating a revolutionary spirit of not fearing violence and not being afraid of sacrifice. No matter what storms they may encounter on the road ahead, the youth of China and Vietnam will always unite together, fight together, and fight to the end for the complete and utter elimination of U.S. imperialism.
    In his report, Chargé d'Affaires Hoang Bac said that under the leadership of the Vietnam Workers' Party and President Ho Chi Minh, the Ho Chi Minh Communist Youth Union has played a shock force role in the revolutionary struggle. Hoang Bac introduced the great contributions made by Vietnamese youth in the socialist construction of their motherland and in the struggle against U.S. imperialist aggression. He said that today, young people from all walks of life in Vietnam, responding to the call of the Vietnam Workers' Party, are rushing to all parts of the country to carry out all the tasks assigned to them by the Party.
    In his report, Hoang Van Hoan introduced the heroic deeds of the youth of South Vietnam in the struggle against U.S. imperialist aggression. The youth of South Vietnam will forever inherit the glorious traditions of their revolutionary predecessors, advance along the road of revolutionary struggle, and contribute to defeating U.S. imperialism and unifying the motherland.
    Attending the report meeting were Li Wanyao and Qian Dawei, Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Youth Federation, Zhang Mingqing, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, and Li Wenyi, Deputy Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, among others. Vietnamese youth friends in Beijing were also invited to attend the meeting.
    According to Xinhua News Agency, March 25th: The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China today sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Ho Chi Minh Communist Youth Union, warmly congratulating the 35th anniversary of the Ho Chi Minh Communist Youth Union.
    The telegram said that for the past thirty-five years, under the leadership of the Vietnam Workers' Party and President Ho Chi Minh, the Ho Chi Minh Communist Youth Union has always stood at the forefront of the struggle in every historical period of the Vietnamese people's revolutionary struggle, and has made immortal contributions to the Vietnamese people's revolutionary cause.
    Currently, in the Vietnamese people's great anti-American war of national salvation, the Ho Chi Minh Communist Youth Union unites the broad masses of young people, actively responds to the Party's call, inherits and carries forward the glorious revolutionary traditions of the Vietnamese people, and under the slogan of "Everything for the Frontline" and "Everything for Victory over U.S. Imperialism and its running dogs," actively launches the "Three Ready" movement and the "Anti-American National Salvation Youth Shock Brigade" movement, fighting heroically and tenaciously on the front lines, achieving one brilliant victory after another, and making significant contributions to the Vietnamese people's revolutionary cause of defending the North, liberating the South, and unifying the motherland, and to supporting the anti-American revolutionary struggles of the people of Indochina and the people of the world, setting a good example for all progressive youth in the world to dare to struggle and dare to win. The telegram said that Chinese youth will forever stand together with the fraternal Vietnamese youth and fight to the end to thoroughly defeat U.S. imperialism, the most vicious enemy of the world's people, and realize our common ideals of socialism and communism.

### 首都青年集会庆祝越劳动青年团成立三十五周年  中越两国青年将永远团结战斗消灭美帝  我共青团中央电贺越南青年在抗美救国斗争中的贡献

1966-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

    首都青年集会庆祝越劳动青年团成立三十五周年
    中越两国青年将永远团结战斗消灭美帝
    我共青团中央电贺越南青年在抗美救国斗争中的贡献
    据新华社二十五日讯　首都青年八百多人今天下午举行集会，庆祝越南劳动青年团成立三十五周年，向英雄的越南青年致以热烈的祝贺。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办黄北，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团员黄万里，应邀在会上作报告。
    共青团中央书记处书记杨海波在会上首先致词。他说，越南劳动青年团团结着广大青年群众，战斗在越南人民伟大的抗美救国斗争的第一线。表现出不畏强暴、不怕牺牲的革命精神。不管在前进的道路上会遇到多大的风浪，中越两国青年将永远团结在一起，战斗在一起，为全部、干净消灭美帝国主义斗争到底。
    黄北临时代办在报告中说，越南劳动青年团在越南劳动党和胡志明主席的领导下，在革命斗争中起了突击队的作用。黄北介绍了越南青年在祖国社会主义建设和抗击美帝国主义侵略的斗争中作出的巨大贡献。他说，今天，越南各阶层青年，响应越南劳动党的号召，奔向祖国各地，执行党交给他们的一切任务。
    黄万里在报告中介绍了越南南方青年在抗击美帝国主义斗争中的英雄事迹。越南南方青年将永远继承革命前辈的光荣传统，沿着革命斗争的道路前进，为打败美帝国主义和统一祖国作出贡献。
    出席报告会的，有全国青联副主席李文耀、钱大卫，全国学联副主席张明卿，共青团北京市委副书记李文义等。在北京的越南青年朋友也应邀出席大会。
    据新华社二十五日讯　中国共产主义青年团中央委员会今天打电报给越南劳动青年团中央委员会，热烈祝贺越南劳动青年团成立三十五周年。
    电文说，三十五年来，越南劳动青年团在越南劳动党和胡志明主席的领导下，在越南人民革命斗争的各个历史时期，始终站在斗争的前列，为越南人民的革命事业立下了不朽的功勋。
    当前，在越南人民伟大的抗美救国斗争中，越南劳动青年团团结着广大青年，积极响应党的号召，继承和发扬了越南人民光荣的革命传统，在“一切为了前线”、“一切为了战胜美帝国主义及其走狗”的口号下，积极开展“三准备”运动和“抗美救国青年突击队”运动，英勇顽强、艰苦卓绝地斗争在第一线，取得了一个又一个的光辉胜利，为越南人民保卫北方、解放南方、统一祖国的革命事业，为支持印度支那人民和全世界人民的反美革命斗争作出了重大的贡献，为全世界一切进步青年树立了一个敢于斗争、敢于胜利的好榜样。电文说，中国青年将永远同兄弟的越南青年站在一起，为彻底打败世界人民最凶恶的敌人美帝国主义，实现我们共同的理想社会主义和共产主义而奋斗到底。
