Title: Chairman Liu Meets with Staff of Chinese Embassy in Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1966-03-29
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi, 28th – Chairman Liu Shaoqi met with the staff of the Chinese Embassy in Pakistan on the 28th and took a group photo with them.

    Also present at the meeting were Vice Premier Chen Yi and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, who are accompanying Chairman Liu Shaoqi on his visit to Pakistan. (Photo attached)

    Left photo: Chairman Liu and his wife, accompanied by President Ayub Khan, visited the Taxila Museum on the morning of March 27. Along the twenty-mile route from Rawalpindi to Taxila, thousands of villagers and citizens warmly welcomed the Chinese guests.
    Xinhua News Agency Reporter Du Xiuxian

### 刘主席接见我驻巴使馆工作人员

1966-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席接见我驻巴使馆工作人员
    新华社拉瓦尔品第二十八日电  刘少奇主席二十八日接见了中国驻巴基斯坦大使馆工作人员，并且同他们一起合影。
    接见时在座的还有陪同刘少奇主席访问巴基斯坦的陈毅副总理和外交部副部长章汉夫。（附图片）
    左图：刘主席和夫人三月二十七日上午在阿尤布·汗总统陪同下，参观了泰格西拉（城）博物馆。在从拉瓦尔品第通往泰格西拉的长达二十英里的途中，数以千计的村民和市民热烈欢迎中国客人。
    新华社记者  杜修贤摄
              （传真照片）
