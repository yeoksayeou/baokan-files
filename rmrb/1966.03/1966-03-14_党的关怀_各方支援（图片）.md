Title: The Party's Care, Support from All Sides (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1966-03-14
Edition: 2

    The Central Delegation, led by Zeng Shan, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Minister of Internal Affairs, arrived in the earthquake-stricken areas. The picture shows Comrade Zeng Shan, on behalf of the Party Central Committee, Chairman Mao, and the State Council, extending cordial greetings to the masses.　Xinhua News Agency Reporter Li Changyong Photo
    A large batch of medicines sent by relevant departments to the people in the disaster area.
                                  Xinhua News Agency Reporter Wang Xudong Photo
    The troops stationed in Hebei Province are engaged in disaster relief work in a combat posture. This is the People's Liberation Army building temporary shelters for the disaster-stricken masses.
                                Xinhua News Agency Reporter Song Youmin Photo
    In order to promptly rescue the injured masses, relevant units quickly established some "field hospitals" capable of performing surgery in the epicenter of the earthquake. The picture shows the masses rushing to make cotton quilts for the "field hospitals."　Xinhua News Agency Reporter Wang Xudong Photo

### 党的关怀　各方支援（图片）

1966-03-14
第2版()
专栏：

    党的关怀　各方支援
    由中共中央委员、内务部部长曾山率领的中央慰问团，来到受地震灾害的地区。图为曾山同志代表党中央、毛主席和国务院，向群众进行亲切慰问。　新华社记者　李长永摄有关部门给灾区人民运来的大批药品
                                  新华社记者　王旭东摄
    河北驻军以战斗姿态投入救灾工作。这是解放军在为受灾群众搭临时窝棚。
                                新华社记者　宋佑民摄
    为及时抢救受伤群众，有关单位迅速在地震的中心地带，建立了一些能动手术的“战地医院”。图为群众正在给“战地医院”赶制棉被。　新华社记者　王旭东摄
