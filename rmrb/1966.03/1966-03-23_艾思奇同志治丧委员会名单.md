Title: List of Comrades of the Funeral Committee for Comrade Ai Siqi
Author:
Date: 1966-03-23
Edition: 2

    List of Comrades of the Funeral Committee for Comrade Ai Siqi
    Xinhua News Agency, 22nd. The Funeral Committee for Comrade Ai Siqi has been established.
    The public memorial service for Comrade Ai Siqi is scheduled to be held at 10:00 AM on March 25th in the auditorium of the Central Senior Party School of the Communist Party of China.
    The list of comrades of the Funeral Committee for Comrade Ai Siqi is as follows:
