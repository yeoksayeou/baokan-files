Title: Yemeni Prime Minister al-Amri and Arab trade union organizations praise President Touré for supporting President Nkrumah

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-08
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Sana'a, 7th March: Yemeni Prime Minister al-Amri praised Guinean President Touré on the 6th for his support of President Nkrumah.
    Referring to the recent military coups in Africa, al-Amri pointed out that this was an imperialist conspiracy constantly plotted against the freedom and progress of the African people, "aimed at maintaining imperialist power and exploitation on this continent."
    He emphasized that the struggling African people would gain final victory in the battle against imperialism, neocolonialism, and their running dogs.
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 7th March: The International Confederation of Arab Trade Unions and the Palestine Trade Union Federation issued statements on the 5th, condemning the imperialist instigation of a military coup in Ghana.
    The statement by the International Confederation of Arab Trade Unions condemned "the recent imperialist conspiracies in Africa," and called on the working people of Africa to be vigilant against the conspiracies of neocolonialism and their threats to the revolutionary achievements of the African people. The statement called on African workers to unite against imperialism and its running dogs.
    The International Confederation of Arab Trade Unions also expressed its support for the people of Ghana and President Nkrumah.
    The Palestine Trade Union Federation condemned "the reactionary coup in Ghana," and called on African workers to continue their struggle against imperialism and reactionaries.

### 也门总理阿姆里和阿拉伯工会组织等  赞扬杜尔总统支持恩克鲁玛总统

1966-03-08
第3版()
专栏：

    也门总理阿姆里和阿拉伯工会组织等
    赞扬杜尔总统支持恩克鲁玛总统
    新华社七日讯　萨那消息：也门总理阿姆里六日赞扬几内亚总统杜尔对恩克鲁玛总统的支持。
    阿姆里谈到最近在非洲发生的军事政变时指出，这是帝国主义不断策划的反对非洲人民的自由和进步的阴谋，“目的是保持帝国主义在这个大陆的势力和剥削”。
    他强调指出，斗争中的非洲人民在反对帝国主义、新殖民主义及其走狗的战斗中将获得最后胜利。
    新华社开罗七日电　阿拉伯工会国际联合会和巴勒斯坦工会联合会五日分别发表声明，谴责帝国主义在加纳策动军事政变。
    阿拉伯工会国际联合会的声明谴责“帝国主义最近在非洲的阴谋”，号召非洲劳动人民警惕新殖民主义的阴谋和他们对非洲人民的革命成果的威胁。声明号召非洲工人团结起来对付帝国主义及其走狗。
    阿拉伯工会国际联合会还表示支持加纳人民和恩克鲁玛总统。
    巴勒斯坦工会联合会谴责“加纳的反动政变”，并且号召非洲工人继续进行反对帝国主义和反动派的斗争。
