Author: 
Date: 1966-03-18
Edition: 2

    Wuxi Manufactures New Medical Examination Instrument
    — Radioactive Isotope Scanner
    The Wuxi Municipal Institute of Medicine in Jiangsu Province, in collaboration with the Wuxi Machine Tool Factory, has jointly developed a new medical examination instrument for modern medicine - a radioactive isotope scanner.
    Using this radioactive isotope scanner, it is possible to determine the position, size, and shape of the human thyroid gland, diagnose ectopic goiter and metastasis of thyroid cancer, as well as diagnose liver cancer, brain tumors, and spinal cord tumors, providing important analytical data for clinical diagnosis and scientific research.
    The Wuxi Municipal Institute of Medicine began the research and trial production of the radioactive isotope scanner as early as 1963. The Wuxi Machine Tool Factory specifically assigned two mechanical and electrical technicians to assist in the research and trial production. During the trial production process, they received assistance from units such as the Jiangsu Provincial Radio Research Institute, Changzhou Second Electronic Instrument Factory, and Wuxi Medical Equipment Factory. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 无锡制成医用新检查仪器——放射性同位素扫描机

1966-03-18
第2版()
专栏：

    无锡制成医用新检查仪器
    ——放射性同位素扫描机
    江苏无锡市医学研究所同无锡机床厂联合研究制成一种现代医学上的新的检查仪器——放射性同位素扫描机。
    使用这种放射性同位素扫描机，可以确定人体甲状腺的位置、大小和形状，诊断异位甲状腺肿、甲状腺癌的转移，诊断肝癌、脑肿瘤和脊髓肿瘤等，为临床诊断及科学研究提供重要的分析资料。
    无锡市医学研究所早在一九六三年就开始了放射性同位素扫描机的研究和试制工作。无锡机床厂专门抽出两名机械、电器方面的技术人员配合研究试制。在试制过程中，他们得到江苏省无线电研究所、常州第二电子仪器厂、无锡医疗器械厂等单位的协助。（据新华社讯）
