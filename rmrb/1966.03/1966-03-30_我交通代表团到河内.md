Author:
Date: 1966-03-30
Edition: 4

　　Our Communications Delegation Arrives in Hanoi
　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 29th—At the invitation of Phan Trong Tue, Minister of Transportation and Communications of Vietnam, the Chinese Communications Delegation, led by Sun Ta-kuang, Minister of Communications of China, arrived in Hanoi by plane on the 28th for a friendly visit.
　　Welcoming the delegation at the airport were: Minister Phan Trong Tue, Vice Minister Hong Xich Tam of the Ministry of Transportation and Communications of Vietnam, Vice Minister Hoang Van Tien of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Vice Minister Nghiem Ba Duc of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and Vice Minister Le Dong of the Ministry of Commerce, etc.
　　Zhu Qiwu, the Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam, also went to the airport to welcome them.
　　On the evening of the 29th, Minister Phan Trong Tue hosted a banquet to welcome the Chinese Communications Delegation.

### 我交通代表团到河内

1966-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

    我交通代表团到河内
    新华社河内二十九日电  应越南交通运输部长潘仲慧的邀请，由中国交通部部长孙大光率领的中国交通代表团，二十八日乘飞机到达河内进行友好访问。
    到机场欢迎代表团的有：潘仲慧部长、越南交通运输部副部长洪赤心，外交部副部长黄文进，对外贸易部副部长严伯德，商业部副部长黎东等。
    中国驻越南大使朱其文也到机场欢迎。
    二十九日晚上，潘仲慧部长设宴欢迎中国交通代表团。
