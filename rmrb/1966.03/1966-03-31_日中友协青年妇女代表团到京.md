Title: Japan-China Friendship Association Youth and Women Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-03-31
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, March 30th: An 18-member Japan-China Friendship Association Youth and Women Delegation, led by Sugita Hideko, Vice-Chairman of the Chiyoda Ward Branch of the New Japan Women's Association, arrived in Beijing by plane this evening.
    Yang Yunyu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation and member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, was among those who welcomed the delegation at the airport.

### 日中友协青年妇女代表团到京

1966-03-31
第2版()
专栏：

    日中友协青年妇女代表团到京
    新华社三十日讯  由新日本妇女会东京都千代田区分会副会长杉田日出子率领的日中友协青年妇女代表团一行十八人，今晚乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有全国妇联书记处书记、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员杨蕴玉等。
