Title: Boston Masses Demonstrate Against Rusk's Justification for Aggression Against Vietnam; Mexican People Rally to Protest American Aggression Against Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-03-16
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, March 15th: Boston news - When U.S. Secretary of State Dean Rusk delivered a speech at the Sheraton Hotel in Boston on the 14th, attempting to justify the Johnson administration's policy of aggression against Vietnam, more than one hundred people held a protest demonstration in front of the hotel.
    Most of the demonstrators were students from Boston University. They held up signs condemning the Johnson government's aggression against Vietnam and demanding an end to the war of aggression against Vietnam.
    Xinhua News Agency, Mexico City, March 14th - Residents of Mexico City held rallies in three different locations in the city on the 13th to protest the crimes of U.S. imperialism against the Vietnamese people. The public attending the rallies unanimously condemned the grave crimes of U.S. imperialism in aggressing against Vietnam and massacring Vietnamese women and children.
    According to reports, residents of Merida City in Yucatan State also held protest rallies against U.S. imperialism's aggression against Vietnam. In Mexicali City, Baja California, residents launched activities to support the Vietnamese people. They painted large anti-imperialist slogans on the walls of buildings in the city.

### 波士顿群众示威抗议腊斯克为侵越辩解  墨西哥人民集会抗议美国侵略越南

1966-03-16
第3版()
专栏：

    波士顿群众示威抗议腊斯克为侵越辩解
    墨西哥人民集会抗议美国侵略越南
    新华社十五日讯　波士顿消息：当美国国务卿腊斯克十四日在波士顿谢拉顿饭店发表为约翰逊侵越政策辩解的演说时，一百多人在饭店门前举行抗议示威。
    示威者大部分是波士顿大学的学生，他们高举标语牌谴责约翰逊政府侵略越南，并且要求结束侵略越南的战争。
    新华社墨西哥城十四日电墨西哥首都居民十三日在城区三个不同的地方举行集会，抗议美帝国主义侵略越南人民的罪行。参加集会的公众一致谴责美帝国主义侵略越南、屠杀越南妇女和儿童的严重罪行。
    据报道，在尤卡坦州，梅里达城的居民，也举行了抗议集会，反对美帝国主义侵略越南。在下加利福尼亚州，梅希卡里城的居民展开了声援越南人民的活动。他们在这个城市的建筑物的墙上，涂写上反帝的大字标语。
