Title: Our Second Group of Experts and Some Embassy Staff Evacuated from Ghana and Returned to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-03-10
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 9th – The second group of sixty-nine Chinese experts who were helping to build projects in Ghana under the economic and technical cooperation agreement between China and Ghana, as well as some staff members of the Chinese Embassy in Ghana, returned to Beijing by plane tonight.
    Responsible persons and staff members from relevant units were at the airport to welcome them.

### 我第二批专家和使馆部分人员撤离加纳回京

1966-03-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我第二批专家和使馆部分人员撤离加纳回京
    新华社九日讯  根据中国和加纳两国经济技术合作协定在加纳帮助建设的中国专家，以及中国驻加纳大使馆部分工作人员，第二批共六十九人，今天晚上乘飞机回到北京。
    到机场欢迎的有各有关单位负责人和工作人员。
