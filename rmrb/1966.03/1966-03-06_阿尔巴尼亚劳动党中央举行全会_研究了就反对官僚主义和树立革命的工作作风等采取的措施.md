Title: Albanian Labor Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session to Study Measures Taken to Combat Bureaucracy and Establish a Revolutionary Work Style

Author:
Date: 1966-03-06
Edition: 4

    Albanian Labor Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session
    Studied Measures Taken to Combat Bureaucracy and Establish a Revolutionary Work Style
    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, March 5th -- Newspapers here published the communique of the Sixteenth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party on the 5th.
    The communique stated that the Sixteenth Plenary Session of the Central Committee was held on the 4th under the chairmanship of Comrade Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Party Central Committee. The plenary session studied the recent measures taken in the struggle against bureaucracy and the establishment of revolutionary working methods and style; studied the measures to reorganize the party and government organs at the central and district levels; studied a number of important issues concerning further strengthening the People's Army and further modernizing it; and the instructions for the draft 1966 national plan and draft budget, which will soon be submitted to the People's Assembly for adoption.
    The communique stated that the plenary session discussed and unanimously adopted the relevant decisions on the above issues. The plenary session also decided to issue an open letter to all party members, working people, soldiers, and officers. This letter will be published soon.

### 阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央举行全会  研究了就反对官僚主义和树立革命的工作作风等采取的措施

1966-03-06
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央举行全会
    研究了就反对官僚主义和树立革命的工作作风等采取的措施
    新华社地拉那五日电　此间报纸五日发表了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央第十六次全会的公报。
    公报说，在党中央第一书记恩维尔·霍查同志的主持下，四日举行了中央第十六次全会。全会研究了最近就反对官僚主义斗争、树立革命的工作方法和作风所采取的措施；研究了改组中央和区一级的党政机构的措施；研究了进一步加强人民军并使它进一步现代化的若干重要问题，以及即将提交人民议会通过的一九六六年国家计划草案和预算草案的指示。
    公报说，全会讨论并一致通过了关于上述问题的有关决定。全会还决定向全体劳动党员、劳动人民、战士和军官发出一封公开信。这封信不久即将发表。
