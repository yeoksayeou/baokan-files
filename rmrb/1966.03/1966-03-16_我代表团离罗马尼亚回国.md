Title: Our Delegation Leaves Romania for Home

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-16
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, March 15th -- The Chinese delegation, led by Cheng Zhaoxuan, Vice Minister of Agriculture, left Romania by plane on March 15th to return home after attending the founding大会 [da hui - grand meeting, congress] of the Romanian National Federation of Agricultural Production Cooperatives and concluding its friendly visit to Romania.
    Matei, Minister of Agriculture of the Central Committee of the Romanian Communist Party and member of the Party Central Committee, and Vedeanu, Vice Chairman of the Romanian Supreme Agricultural Committee, and others saw the delegation off at the airport.
    Wang Dong, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Romania, also saw the delegation off at the airport.
    On the evening of the 14th, Wang Dong hosted a banquet for the delegation's visit to Romania.
    During its stay in Romania, the delegation also visited agricultural production cooperatives, agricultural experiment stations, and factories and enterprises in Bacău, Braşov, Ploieşti and other counties. The delegation was warmly received by relevant departments.

### 我代表团离罗马尼亚回国

1966-03-16
第4版()
专栏：

    我代表团离罗马尼亚回国
    新华社布加勒斯特十五日电　由农业部副部长程照轩率领的中国代表团在参加了罗马尼亚全国农业生产合作社联合会成立大会和结束了在罗马尼亚的友好访问后，十五日乘飞机离开罗马尼亚回国。
    罗马尼亚共产党中央委员会农业部部长、党中央委员马特，罗马尼亚最高农业委员会副主席维德亚努等到机场送行。
    中国驻罗马尼亚大使馆临时代办王栋也到机场送行。
    十四日晚，王栋为代表团访问罗马尼亚举行了宴会。
    代表团在罗马尼亚期间，还参观访问了巴克乌、布拉索夫、普洛耶什蒂等州的农业生产合作社、农业试验站以及工厂企业等单位。代表团受到了有关部门的热情接待。
