Title: U.S. Military Stationed Bacteriological and Toxic Gas Units in Okinawa; Newspaper Reveals These Units Regularly Go to Vietnam to Release Toxic Gases and Bacteria

Author:
Date: 1966-03-11
Edition: 4

    U.S. Military Stationed Bacteriological and Toxic Gas Units in Okinawa
    Newspaper Reveals These Units Regularly Go to Vietnam to Release Toxic Gases and Bacteria
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 7th – Tokyo's "Okinawa, Ogasawara News" reported that the U.S. military’s bacteriological and toxic gas units stationed in Ginoza, Okinawa, frequently go to Vietnam to carry out the heinous mission of releasing toxic gases and bacteria such as plague and cholera.
    The newspaper published the photograph of the number of this bacteriological and toxic gas unit on February 28, along with the news. The external number of this bacteriological and toxic gas unit is the First Battalion of the U.S. 503rd Airborne Division.

### 美军在冲绳驻有细菌毒气部队  日报透露这支部队常去越南撒放毒气细菌

1966-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    美军在冲绳驻有细菌毒气部队
    日报透露这支部队常去越南撒放毒气细菌
    新华社东京七日电  东京出版的《冲绳、小笠原新闻》报道，在冲绳的宜野座驻扎有美军细菌毒气部队，经常去越南执行撒放毒气和鼠疫、霍乱等细菌的罪恶任务。
    这家报纸二月二十八日在发表这条消息的同时，刊登了标志这支细菌毒气部队番号的照片。这支细菌毒气部队的对外番号是美国第五○三空降师第一营。
