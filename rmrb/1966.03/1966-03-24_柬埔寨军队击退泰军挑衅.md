Title: Cambodian Army Repulses Thai Army Provocations
Author:
Date: 1966-03-24
Edition: 4

    According to a Hsinhua News Agency dispatch from Phnom Penh, March 23: Cambodge News Agency reported on March 23 that on March 19 at noon, Thai troops stationed in Harle once again used heavy weaponry to fire upon the Chhne Sar outpost in Cambodia's Koh Kong Province. The Cambodian army fiercely retaliated, forcing the Thai troops to cease firing.
    On March 18, a Cambodian Defense Forces patrol encountered Thai troops in Cambodia's Battambang Province. The battle lasted about forty minutes, and the enemy fled towards Thailand.
    This news agency also reported on March 23: On the evening of the 19th and the morning of the 20th, South Vietnamese puppet troops shelled Cambodian territory in Prey Veng Province with mortars, wounding one Cambodian provincial defense soldier.

### 柬埔寨军队击退泰军挑衅

1966-03-24
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨军队击退泰军挑衅
    据新华社金边二十三日电　据柬埔寨新闻社二十三日报道，三月十九日中午，驻扎在哈勒的泰国军队再次用重武器向柬埔寨戈公省奇内萨哨所射击。柬埔寨军队猛烈回击，迫使泰国军队停止了射击。
    三月十八日，柬埔寨国防军巡逻队在柬埔寨马德望省同泰国军队遭遇。战斗进行约四十分钟，敌人向泰国方面逃去。
    这家通讯社二十三日还报道：十九日晚上和二十日上午，南越傀儡军用迫击炮轰击波萝勉省柬埔寨领土，一名柬埔寨省防军受伤。
