Title: Vietnamese People's Army Shoots Down Six More U.S. Aircraft

Author:

Date: 1966-03-22
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 20th – According to an announcement here on the 20th, the military and civilians of Ha Tinh province in North Vietnam shot down two U.S. bandit planes on the 20th and captured the U.S. pilots alive.
    In addition, according to reports, the military and civilians of Nghe An, Lai Chau, and Quang Binh provinces shot down a total of four invading U.S. bandit jet planes on the 15th, 16th, and 17th.

### 越南军民又击落美机六架

1966-03-22
第4版()
专栏：

    越南军民又击落美机六架
    新华社河内二十日电　据这里二十日公布，越南北方河静省军民二十日击落美国强盗飞机两架，并且活捉了美国飞行员。
    另据报道，义安、莱州、广平等省军民十五、十六和十七日三天共击落入侵的美国强盗喷气式飞机四架。
