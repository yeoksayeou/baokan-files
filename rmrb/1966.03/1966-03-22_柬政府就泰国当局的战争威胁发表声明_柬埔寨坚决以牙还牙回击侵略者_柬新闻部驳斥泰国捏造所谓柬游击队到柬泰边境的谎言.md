Title: The Cambodian Government Issues a Statement Regarding the War Threats from the Thai Authorities: Cambodia Resolves to Retaliate Against Aggressors; Cambodian Ministry of Information Refutes Thailand's Fabricated Lies About Cambodian Guerrillas Going to the Cambodian-Thai Border
Author:
Date: 1966-03-22
Edition: 5

    According to a March 21st report by the New China News Agency in Phnom Penh: The Royal Cambodian Government issued a statement on the 20th, as reported by the Cambodian News Agency on the 21st, stating that if Thailand insists on intensifying attacks and bombings of Cambodian villages and border posts, the Bangkok government must bear full responsibility for the serious consequences arising therefrom. The Royal Cambodian Government issued this statement in response to a recent “order” issued by Thai Prime Minister Thanom Kittikachorn to the Thai Army, Navy, and Air Force. This “order” by the Thai Prime Minister falsely accused Cambodian troops of bombing Thai villages and demanded that Thai troops carry out a "forceful retaliation" against Cambodia.
    The statement emphasized that the Royal Cambodian Armed Forces have never initiated the bombing of targets on the Thai border, but have only retaliated with their own means after Thailand attacked Cambodian posts and villages. The statement pointed out that the last Cambodian National Assembly clearly told Thailand that Cambodia is determined to retaliate in kind for every aggression against its territory.
    According to a March 19th report by the New China News Agency in Phnom Penh: The Cambodian Ministry of Information refuted on the 18th the lies spread by Thai Interior Minister Praphas Charusathien about Cambodia sending guerrillas to the Cambodian-Thai border area to carry out disturbances.
    On March 16th, Charusathien insisted that five Cambodian guerrillas were arrested in Sisaket Province, Thailand, and that they confessed to infiltrating under the leadership of a Cambodian lieutenant and preparing to carry out disturbances in the border area.
    The Cambodian Ministry of Information stated: "Cambodian citizens have not infiltrated Thai territory."
    The Ministry of Information pointed out that Thailand's slander is merely to damage Cambodia's reputation. (Attached photo)
    The photo shows the Cambodian people of Chineasa in Koh Kong Province tightly gripping their guns, ready to cooperate with the Royal Armed Forces at any time to strike at invading enemies. Xinhua News Agency Reporter Yang Xuehao.

### 柬政府就泰国当局的战争威胁发表声明  柬埔寨坚决以牙还牙回击侵略者  柬新闻部驳斥泰国捏造所谓柬游击队到柬泰边境的谎言

1966-03-22
第5版()
专栏：

    柬政府就泰国当局的战争威胁发表声明
    柬埔寨坚决以牙还牙回击侵略者
    柬新闻部驳斥泰国捏造所谓柬游击队到柬泰边境的谎言
    据新华社金边二十一日电 据柬埔寨新闻社二十一日报道，柬埔寨王国政府二十日发表声明说，如果泰国一定要加紧袭击和轰炸柬埔寨的村庄和边境哨所，曼谷政府必须对由此产生的严重后果负全部责任。柬埔寨王国政府是就泰国总理他侬·吉滴卡宗最近向泰国陆海空三军发布的一道“命令”发表这项声明的。泰国总理的这个“命令”诬蔑柬埔寨军队轰炸泰国的村庄，并且要泰国军队对柬埔寨进行“有力的回击”。
    声明强调指出，柬埔寨王家武装部队在任何时候都没有首先轰炸过泰国边界上的目标，而只是在泰国袭击了柬埔寨的哨所和村庄以后，才用它自己拥有的手段进行回击。声明指出，上次柬埔寨国民议会已清楚地告诉泰国，柬埔寨决定对每次对它的领土的侵略以牙还牙。
  据新华社金边十九日电　柬埔寨新闻部十八日驳斥了泰国内政部长巴博·乍鲁沙夫散布的所谓柬埔寨派遣游击队到柬泰边境地区进行骚乱的谎言。
    乍鲁沙夫在三月十六日硬说在泰国四杀吉省逮捕了五名柬埔寨游击队员，他们承认自己是在一名柬埔寨尉官的领导下渗入，并准备在边境地区进行骚乱。
    柬埔寨新闻部说：“柬埔寨公民没有渗入泰国的领土。”
    新闻部指出，泰国的诽谤只是为了破坏柬埔寨的信誉。（附图片）
