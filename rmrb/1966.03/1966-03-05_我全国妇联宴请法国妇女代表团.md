Title: All-China Women's Federation Hosts Banquet for French Women's Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-03-05
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 4th: The Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, Zhang Yun, hosted a banquet tonight in honor of the French Women's Delegation, led by Madame Hélène de Lipkowski, President of the French Committee for International Liaison of Women's Organizations. Attending the banquet as guests were Ding Xilin, Luo Shuzhang, Xia Zhixu, Lin Qiaozhi, Fan Jin, Zhang Xiaomei, Zhang Wei, Peng Yan, and other relevant figures, as well as female delegates to the National People's Congress and responsible individuals from the All-China Women's Federation who are in Beijing.

### 我全国妇联宴请法国妇女代表团

1966-03-05
第4版()
专栏：

    我全国妇联宴请法国妇女代表团
    新华社四日讯　全国妇联副主席章蕴今晚设宴招待以法国妇女团体国际联络委员会主席伊兰·德·李普考斯基夫人为首的法国妇女代表团。出席宴会作陪的，有丁西林、罗叔章、夏之栩、林巧稚、范瑾、张晓梅、张维、彭炎等各有关方面人士，以及在京的全国人民代表大会女代表和全国妇联的负责人等。
