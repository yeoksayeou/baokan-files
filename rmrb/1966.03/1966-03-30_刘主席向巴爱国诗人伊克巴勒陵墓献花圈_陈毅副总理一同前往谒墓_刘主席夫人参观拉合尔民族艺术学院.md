Title: Chairman Liu Pays Tribute to Pakistani Patriotic Poet Iqbal with Wreath; Vice Premier Chen Yi Accompanies the Visit; Chairman Liu’s Wife Visits Lahore National College of Arts
Author:
Date: 1966-03-30
Edition: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Lahore, March 29th. Chairman Liu Shaoqi and his wife, accompanied by Malik Amir Muhammad Khan, Governor of West Pakistan Province, visited the tomb of renowned Pakistani patriotic poet Allama Muhammad Iqbal (1873-1938) here this morning.

    Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, and other members of Chairman Liu Shaoqi's entourage also accompanied the visit.

    As Chairman Liu Shaoqi's motorcade passed, tens of thousands of Lahore citizens stood on both sides of the street, waving Pakistani and Chinese national flags, playing musical instruments, chanting welcoming slogans, and waving to the Chinese guests. Banners held high by the welcomers read: "Welcome Chairman Liu Shaoqi!" "Long Live People's China!" "Down with the US-Indian Collusion!"

    Chairman Liu Shaoqi laid a wreath at Iqbal's tomb, which is made of exquisite marble. The ribbon on the wreath read: "Dedicated to the outstanding Pakistani national poet Iqbal."

    Subsequently, Chairman Liu Shaoqi visited the Badshahi Mosque, one of the largest mosques in the world, capable of accommodating ten thousand Muslims praying simultaneously.

    This mosque was built three hundred years ago during the reign of the Mughal Emperor Aurangzeb. It is square in shape with three magnificent marble domes. Four tall minarets stand at each of the four corners, symbolizing the great achievements of the ancient people of this country in architecture.

    Later, Chairman Liu Shaoqi and his entourage toured the Lahore Fort, built during the reign of Emperor Akbar in 1566.

    They visited the museum inside the fort, where they saw many miniature paintings from the Mughal Dynasty.

    Accompanying Chairman Liu Shaoqi on the visit were Ding Guoyu, Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan, and his wife, as well as Rochan, Pakistani Ambassador to China.

    Xinhua News Agency, Lahore, March 29th. Wang Guangmei, wife of Chairman Liu Shaoqi, accompanied by the wife of Pakistani Foreign Minister Bhutto, visited the National College of Arts here this morning.

    Zhang Qian, wife of Vice Premier Chen Yi, and Chang Naizhi, wife of Ding Guoyu, Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan, also went.

    The Chinese guests were warmly welcomed by Shaqir Ali, President of the college, Mrs. Abassi Akhtar, Vice President, and many students.

    Guided by the hosts, the Chinese guests visited the college's classrooms, where they viewed the students' artwork, including paintings and sculptures.

    Students performed music and dances from various regions of East Pakistan and West Pakistan for the guests. Hosts and guests exchanged handicrafts as souvenirs.

### 刘主席向巴爱国诗人伊克巴勒陵墓献花圈  陈毅副总理一同前往谒墓  刘主席夫人参观拉合尔民族艺术学院

1966-03-30
第2版()
专栏：

    刘主席向巴爱国诗人伊克巴勒陵墓献花圈
    陈毅副总理一同前往谒墓  刘主席夫人参观拉合尔民族艺术学院
    新华社拉合尔二十九日电  刘少奇主席和夫人二十九日上午在西巴基斯坦省督马利克·阿密尔·穆罕默德·汗的陪同下，在这里访谒了巴基斯坦著名爱国诗人阿拉马·穆罕默德·伊克巴勒（一八七三——一九三八）的陵墓。
    陈毅副总理和夫人、外交部副部长章汉夫和刘少奇主席的其他随行人员也一同前往谒墓。
    在刘少奇主席的车队经过的时候，成千上万的拉合尔市民站在街道两旁，挥舞巴中两国国旗，演奏乐器，高呼欢迎口号，并向中国客人们挥手致意。欢迎者高举的标语牌上面写着：“欢迎刘少奇主席！”“人民中国万岁！”“打倒印美勾结！”
    刘少奇主席在用精致的大理石砌成的伊克巴勒陵墓前献了花圈。花圈的缎带上写着：“献给杰出的巴基斯坦民族诗人伊克巴勒”。
    随后，刘少奇主席参观了巴德沙希清真寺，这是世界上最大的清真寺之一，能容纳一万个穆斯林同时作祈祷。
    这座清真寺是三百年前在莫卧儿皇奥朗则布时代建成的。它是四方形，有三个堂皇的大理石圆顶。在四个角的每个角上耸立着四个高大的尖塔，象征着这个国家古代人民在建筑方面所取得的巨大成就。
    后来，刘少奇主席和他的随行人员游览了在一五六六年阿克巴尔皇帝时代修建的拉合尔堡。
    他们参观了堡内的博物馆，在那里看到了莫卧儿王朝的许多小型彩画。
    陪同刘少奇主席一起参观的有：中国驻巴基斯坦大使丁国钰和夫人以及巴基斯坦驻中国大使罗查。
    新华社拉合尔二十九日电  刘少奇主席的夫人王光美在巴基斯坦外交部长布托的夫人的陪同下，二十九日上午参观了这里的民族艺术学院。
    陈毅副总理的夫人张茜和中国驻巴基斯坦大使丁国钰的夫人常乃志也一起前往。
    中国客人受到了这所学院的院长沙基尔·阿里、副院长阿巴西·阿赫塔尔夫人和许多学生的热烈欢迎。
    中国客人在主人的引导下参观了这所学院的教室，在那里观看了学生们的艺术作品，包括绘画和雕刻。
    学生们为客人表演了东巴基斯坦和西巴基斯坦各地区的音乐和舞蹈。主人和客人互赠了工艺品以资纪念。
