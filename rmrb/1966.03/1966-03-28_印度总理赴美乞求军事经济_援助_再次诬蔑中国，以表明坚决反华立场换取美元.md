Title: Indian Prime Minister Goes to the US to Beg for Military and Economic "Aid", Slandering China Again to Show a Firm Anti-China Stance in Exchange for Dollars
Author:
Date: 1966-03-28
Edition: 5

    According to Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 25th - Indian Prime Minister Indira Gandhi left New Delhi on the evening of the 24th for a visit to the United States.

    Mrs. Gandhi's visit to the United States takes place against the backdrop of a seriously worsening food crisis and increasing economic bankruptcy in India. According to Indian newspapers, the main purpose of Mrs. Gandhi's visit is to beg for military and economic assistance. The *Hindustan Standard* said on the 22nd that Mrs. Gandhi will discuss with Johnson "American aid to India's development efforts and defense."

    According to the "Indian Information Service" on the 24th, one of the topics of the talks will be the unprecedented food crisis in India since its independence.

    The *Hindustan Standard* said on the 22nd that in her talks with Johnson, Mrs. Gandhi will discuss the so-called "threat of China to India and Southeast Asia." In order to demonstrate India's firm anti-China stance, according to the Indian Information Service on the 23rd, Mrs. Gandhi, in a recent interview with a reporter from the "Voice of America," once again slandered China for making "territorial demands" on India and adopting a so-called "threatening posture" towards India.

    It is reported that Mrs. Gandhi will hold talks with French President De Gaulle on her way to the United States. On her way back to India from the United States, she will also hold talks with British and Soviet leaders.

### 印度总理赴美乞求军事经济“援助”  再次诬蔑中国，以表明坚决反华立场换取美元

1966-03-28
第5版()
专栏：

    印度总理赴美乞求军事经济“援助”
    再次诬蔑中国，以表明坚决反华立场换取美元
    据新华社二十五日讯  新德里消息：印度总理英迪拉·甘地夫人二十四日晚离开新德里前往美国访问。
    甘地夫人是在印度国内粮食危机严重恶化和经济日益破产的情况下到美国访问的。据印度报纸报道的甘地夫人这次访问的主要目的是乞讨军事和经济援助。《印度斯坦旗报》二十二日说，甘地夫人将同约翰逊讨论“美国对印度的发展事业和国防的援助”。
    据“印度新闻处”二十四日说，双方会谈的主题之一，将是印度独立以来空前未有的粮食危机。
    《印度斯坦旗报》二十二日说，甘地夫人在同约翰逊的会谈中，将讨论所谓“中国对印度和东南亚的威胁”。为了表明印度坚决反华的立场，据印度新闻处二十三日报道，甘地夫人在最近对“美国之音”记者发表的谈话中，又一次诬蔑中国对印度提出了“领土要求”，对印度采取了所谓“威胁性的姿态”。
    据报道，甘地夫人在去美国的途中将同法国总统戴高乐举行会谈。她在从美国返回印度途中，还将同英国和苏联领导人举行会谈。
