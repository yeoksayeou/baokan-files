Title: Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Two More American Planes
Author:
Date: 1966-03-05
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 2nd—According to an announcement here on the 2nd, the military and civilians of Thanh Hoa Province and Quang Binh Province in North Vietnam each shot down an American jet fighter on March 1st, and damaged several others.
    The Liaison Delegation of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army sent an urgent telegram to the International Commission of Vietnam on the 2nd, strongly protesting the American aircraft's continued bombing raids on residential areas and economic facilities in Quang Binh, Ha Tinh, and Thanh Hoa provinces in North Vietnam on March 1st, as well as the bandit-like war actions on Hon Co Island.

### 越南军民又击落美机两架

1966-03-05
第3版()
专栏：

    越南军民又击落美机两架
    新华社河内二日电　据这里二日公布，越南北方清化省和广平省军民，三月一日各击落美国喷气式飞机一架，击伤多架。
    越南人民军总司令部联络代表团二日打紧急电报给越南国际委员会，强烈抗议美国飞机三月一日继续轰炸越南北方的广平、河静和清化等省居民区和经济设施，以及昏果岛的强盗式的战争行动。
