Title: Mai Xiande Awarded the Titles of "Combat Hero" and "Model Communist Youth League Member"; Guangzhou Holds Naming Ceremony to Award Mai Xiande Honorable Titles
Author:
Date: 1966-03-19
Edition: 2

    Mai Xiande Awarded the Titles of "Combat Hero" and "Model Communist Youth League Member"
    Guangzhou Holds Naming Ceremony to Award Mai Xiande Honorable Titles
    (Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 18th) A naming ceremony was solemnly held in Guangzhou today, where the Ministry of National Defense and the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League respectively awarded Mai Xiande, a turbine operator of a unit under the South Sea Fleet of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy, the titles of "Combat Hero" and "Model Communist Youth League Member."
    This ceremony was jointly held by the leading organs of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy and the Guangzhou Military Region, based on the order of the Ministry of National Defense awarding Mai Xiande the title of "Combat Hero" and the decision of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League awarding Mai Xiande the title of "Model Communist Youth League Member."
    Wu Zhipu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central South Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Zhou Xihan, person in charge of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy, Wen Niansheng and Yan Fusheng, persons in charge of the Guangzhou Military Region, Ma Shijiang, member of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and other responsible persons from various fields attended the ceremony.
    Many advanced collectives, hero models, and militia representatives from the army, navy, and air force units stationed on the frontier and coastal defense outposts of the motherland, carrying the enthusiasm of the broad masses of commanders and fighters and the militia to learn from Mai Xiande, came from afar to participate in the ceremony. Representatives from Mai Xiande's hometown—Jingzhou Commune in Raoping County, Guangdong Province—and Mai Xiande's father, Mai Guangjin, also attended the ceremony.
    The ceremony began in the solemn sound of the national anthem. Wen Niansheng, person in charge of the Guangzhou Military Region, read out the order of the Ministry of National Defense awarding Mai Xiande the title of "Combat Hero" on behalf of the Ministry of National Defense, and presented the certificate of "Combat Hero" to Mai Xiande on behalf of the Ministry of National Defense. Ma Shijiang, member of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, read out the decision of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League awarding Mai Xiande the title of "Model Communist Youth League Member" on behalf of the Central Committee of the League, and presented the certificate of "Model Communist Youth League Member" to Mai Xiande on behalf of the Central Committee of the League.
    Mai Xiande walked to the front of the stage to accept the awards amidst the majestic military music. At this moment, everyone stood up, and the applause lasted for a long time, expressing warm congratulations to this young combat hero armed with Mao Zedong Thought.
    Wu Zhipu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central South Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Zhou Xihan, person in charge of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy, Guo Chengzhu, person in charge of the Guangzhou Military Region, Ma Shijiang, member of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, Lin Liming, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and Acting Governor of Guangdong Province, Zeng Sheng, Third Secretary of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Mayor of Guangzhou, Wu Ruilin, person in charge of the South Sea Fleet of the Navy, and others spoke at the meeting, calling on everyone to learn from Mai Xiande.
    The famous combat heroes Hao Zhongyun and Shu Jicheng, Air Force personnel Sun Xiaoqing who had won military merits, Cui Fujun, the captain of the boat where Mai Xiande was stationed, and Ye Yongquan, the leader of the port hero militia company, unanimously stated in their speeches that they were determined to take Mai Xiande as an example, further creatively study and apply Chairman Mao's works, wholeheartedly serve the Chinese people and the people of the world, and be ready to contribute the greatest strength to defending the motherland and to the revolutionary cause of the people of the world at any time.
    Finally, Mai Xiande walked to the rostrum with excitement and spoke. He said, thank you to the Party, thank you to Chairman Mao. What I have done is not enough, I want to read Chairman Mao's books, listen to Chairman Mao's words, act according to Chairman Mao's instructions, and be a good soldier of Chairman Mao. He shouted: "Long live the Communist Party of China! Long live Chairman Mao!" At this time, warm applause erupted again for a long time.
    More than 5,000 people participated in today's naming ceremony. After the ceremony, Wu Zhipu, Wen Niansheng, Zhou Xihan and other responsible comrades met with Mai Xiande and took pictures with him. (Attached picture)

### 麦贤得荣获“战斗英雄”和“模范共青团员”称号  广州举行授予麦贤得光荣称号命名大会

1966-03-19
第2版()
专栏：

    麦贤得荣获“战斗英雄”和“模范共青团员”称号
    广州举行授予麦贤得光荣称号命名大会
    据新华社广州十八日电　国防部和共青团中央分别授予中国人民解放军海军南海舰队某部轮机兵麦贤得以“战斗英雄”和“模范共青团员”称号的命名大会，今天在广州市隆重举行。
    这个大会，是中国人民解放军海军和广州部队领导机关，根据国防部授予麦贤得以“战斗英雄”称号的命令和共青团中央授予麦贤得以“模范共青团员”称号的决定共同举行的。
    中共中央中南局书记处书记吴芝圃，中国人民解放军海军负责人周希汉，广州部队负责人文年生、晏福生，共青团中央常务委员马石江等各方面负责人出席了大会。
    坚守在祖国边防、海防前哨的陆海空军部队的许多先进集体、英雄模范和民兵的代表，带着广大指战员和民兵向麦贤得学习的热情，从远道赶来参加了大会。麦贤得的家乡——广东省饶平县汫洲公社的代表和麦贤得的父亲麦光进也出席了大会。
    大会在庄严的国歌声中开始。广州部队负责人文年生代表国防部宣读授予麦贤得以“战斗英雄”称号的命令，并代表国防部把“战斗英雄”的奖状授给麦贤得。共青团中央常务委员马石江代表团中央宣读授予麦贤得以“模范共青团员”称号的决定，并代表团中央把“模范共青团员”的奖状授给麦贤得。
    麦贤得在雄壮的军乐声中走到台前接受奖状。这时，全体起立，掌声经久不息，人们向这位用毛泽东思想武装起来的年青的战斗英雄表示热烈的祝贺。
    中共中央中南局书记处书记吴芝圃，中国人民解放军海军负责人周希汉，广州部队负责人郭成柱，共青团中央常务委员马石江，中共广东省委书记处书记、广东省代省长林李明，中共广州市委第三书记、广州市市长曾生，海军南海舰队负责人吴瑞林等都在会上讲了话，号召向麦贤得学习。
    著名的战斗英雄郝忠云、舒积成，荣立过战功的空军人员孙孝庆，麦贤得所在艇的艇长崔福俊，港口英雄民兵连连长叶永泉等，在讲话中一致表示，决心以麦贤得为榜样，进一步活学活用毛主席著作，全心全意为中国人民和世界人民服务，随时准备为保卫祖国、为世界人民革命事业贡献出最大的力量。
    最后，麦贤得怀着激动的心情走上主席台讲话。他说，感谢党，感谢毛主席。我做得还不够，我要读毛主席的书，听毛主席的话，按毛主席的指示办事，做毛主席的好战士。他高呼：“中国共产党万岁！毛主席万岁！”这时全场再一次迸发出长时间的热烈掌声。
    参加今天命名大会的共有五千多人。大会结束后，吴芝圃、文年生、周希汉等负责同志接见了麦贤得，并同他一起照了相。（附图片）
