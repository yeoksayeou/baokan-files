Title: Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling Receives Philippine Guests; All-China Women's Federation Holds Reception for Philippine Study Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-03-20
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 19th: Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling received Mrs. Maria Kalaw Katigbak, Liberal Party Senator of the Philippines, this afternoon and had a friendly conversation with her.
    Present at the reception were Zhang Yun, Vice President of the All-China Women's Federation, and others.
    Xinhua News Agency, March 19th: The All-China Women's Federation held a reception this evening to welcome the Philippine study delegation led by Mrs. Maria Kalaw Katigbak, Liberal Party Senator of the Philippines.
    Present at the reception were Xu Guangping, Zhang Yun, and Kang Keqing, Vice Presidents of the All-China Women's Federation, as well as relevant responsible persons and well-known women from all circles in the capital: Wu Defeng, Luo Shuzhang, Hu Zi'ying, Xia Zhixu, Fan Jin, Wang Yunsheng, Yong Longgui, Li Depei, and over two hundred others. (Photo attached)

### 宋庆龄副主席接见菲律宾客人  全国妇联举行酒会招待菲律宾考察代表团

1966-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄副主席接见菲律宾客人
    全国妇联举行酒会招待菲律宾考察代表团
    新华社十九日讯  宋庆龄副主席今天下午接见菲律宾自由党参议员玛丽亚·卡劳·卡蒂瓦克夫人，同她进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有全国妇联副主席章蕴等。
    新华社十九日讯全国妇联今晚举行酒会，招待由菲律宾自由党参议员玛丽亚·卡劳·卡蒂瓦克夫人率领的菲律宾考察代表团。
    出席招待会的，有全国妇联副主席许广平、章蕴、康克清，以及有关方面负责人和首都各界妇女知名人士：吴德峰、罗叔章、胡子婴、夏之栩、范瑾、王芸生、勇龙桂、李德培等二百多人。（附图片）
