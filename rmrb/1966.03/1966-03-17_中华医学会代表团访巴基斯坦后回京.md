Title: Chinese Medical Association Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1966-03-17
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 16th - The Chinese Medical Association Delegation, led by Lu Zhijun, Director of the Academy of Traditional Chinese Medicine, consisting of five people, returned to Beijing by plane today after a friendly visit to Pakistan and exchanging medical experience.



**Named Entities**

中华医学会 Chinese Medical Association
巴基斯坦 Pakistan
中医研究院 Academy of Traditional Chinese Medicine
鲁之俊 Lu Zhijun
新华社 Xinhua News Agency
北京 Beijing

### 中华医学会代表团访巴基斯坦后回京

1966-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

    中华医学会代表团访巴基斯坦后回京
    新华社十六日讯　由中医研究院院长鲁之俊率领的中华医学会代表团一行五人，应邀在巴基斯坦进行友好访问和交流医学经验后，今天乘飞机回到北京。
