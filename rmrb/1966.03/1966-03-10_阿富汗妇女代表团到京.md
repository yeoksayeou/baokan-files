Title: Afghan Women's Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-03-10
Edition: 5

    Afghan Women's Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    (Xinhua News Agency, March 9th) A five-member Afghan women's delegation, headed by Saliha Faruk Etemadi, President of the Afghan Women's Association, arrived in Beijing by plane tonight. They are visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation.
    Kang Keqing, Vice-President of the All-China Women's Federation, and other responsible persons from relevant departments, including Zhang Jie, Shen Qiyi, Zeng Xianzhi, Shen Zijiu, Zhao Feng, and Xu Keli, were at the airport to welcome them.

### 阿富汗妇女代表团到京

1966-03-10
第5版()
专栏：

    阿富汗妇女代表团到京
    新华社九日讯  以阿富汗妇女会会长萨利哈·法鲁克·埃特马迪为首的阿富汗妇女代表团一行五人，今晚乘飞机到达北京。她们是应全国妇联邀请来我国进行友好访问的。
    到机场欢迎的，有全国妇联副主席康克清，其他有关方面负责人张杰、沈其益、曾宪植、沈兹九、赵烽、徐克立等。
