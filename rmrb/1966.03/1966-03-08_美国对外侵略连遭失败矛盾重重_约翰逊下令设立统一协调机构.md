Title: US Suffers Repeated Defeats in Foreign Aggression, Contradictions Abound; Johnson Orders Establishment of Unified Coordination Agency
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-08
Edition: 4

    (Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 5th) -- Washington News: US President Johnson approved the establishment on the 4th of a high-level interdepartmental committee headed by Secretary of State Rusk to coordinate the contradictions among US government agencies engaged in foreign activities and to uniformly guide their aggressive activities in political, economic, and cultural aspects.
    The White House announced that, except for certain military activities, this committee will directly supervise the foreign activities of various departments of the US government and will have the power to make decisions. The "entire purpose" of establishing this committee "is to clarify our responsibilities in handling foreign affairs" and "to enable several departments dealing with complex foreign affairs to better and faster concentrate their efforts."

### 美国对外侵略连遭失败矛盾重重  约翰逊下令设立统一协调机构

1966-03-08
第4版()
专栏：

    美国对外侵略连遭失败矛盾重重
    约翰逊下令设立统一协调机构
    据新华社五日讯　华盛顿消息：美国总统约翰逊四日批准成立一个以国务卿腊斯克为首的高级部际委员会，协调美国政府从事对外活动各机构之间的矛盾，统一指导它们在政治、经济、文化各方面的侵略活动。
    白宫宣布，除了某些军事活动外，这个委员会将直接监督美国政府各部门的对外活动，并且有权作出决定。成立这个委员会的“全部目的，在于使我们在处理对外事务中职责分明”，“使得处理复杂的对外事务的几个部门能够更好更快地集中它们的努力”。
