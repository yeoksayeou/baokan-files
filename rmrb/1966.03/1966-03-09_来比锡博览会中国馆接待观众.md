Title: Chinese Pavilion at the Leipzig Fair Receives Visitors
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-09
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Leipzig, March 6th -- The 1966 Leipzig Spring Fair held its opening ceremony on the evening of the 5th and began receiving visitors on the 6th.
    Throughout the morning of the 6th, the Chinese pavilion received approximately 20,000 visitors. Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic, Apel, and some ministers, accompanied by Wang Wenlin, head of the Chinese Exhibition Delegation, toured the Chinese Exhibition Hall.
    China is exhibiting over 2,950 industrial products, agricultural products, handicrafts, and books this time. The total area of the Chinese pavilion is 1,500 square meters.

### 来比锡博览会中国馆接待观众

1966-03-09
第5版()
专栏：

    来比锡博览会中国馆接待观众
    新华社来比锡六日电  一九六六年来比锡春季博览会在五日晚上举行开幕式后，在六日开始接待参观者。
    六日整个上午，中国馆就接待了约两万名观众。德意志民主共和国部长会议副主席阿布施和一些部长在中国展览团团长王文林的陪同下，参观了中国展览馆。
    中国这次展出的有两千九百五十多种工业品、农产品、手工艺品和书籍等。中国馆的总面积有一千五百平方米。
