Title: Australians Hold Demonstration of 2,000 People Against Australian Government Sending Troops to South Vietnam as Accomplices of the U.S.
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-28
Edition: 5

    New China News Agency, Melbourne, March 26th - Two thousand Australians, mostly young men of conscription age, held a demonstration in Melbourne on the 26th to oppose the U.S. aggression in Vietnam and to oppose the Australian authorities conscripting young men and sending them to South Vietnam as cannon fodder.
    The demonstrators marched from outside the Victoria Barracks on St Kilda Road towards the city center, carrying hundreds of flags. The flags bore slogans such as "Australians Against the Vietnam War" and "No Conscription for Vietnam (War)." This is the largest demonstration against U.S. aggression in Vietnam in Melbourne to date.
    The police suppressed the demonstrators, tearing down some banners and arresting two protesters.

### 反对澳政府派兵去南越充当美国帮凶  澳大利亚两千人举行示威

1966-03-28
第5版()
专栏：

    反对澳政府派兵去南越充当美国帮凶
    澳大利亚两千人举行示威
    新华社墨尔本二十六日电  二千名澳大利亚人，大部分是入伍适龄青年，二十六日在墨尔本举行示威，反对美国侵略越南，反对澳大利亚当局征召青年入伍并派往南越充当炮灰。
    示威群众从斯特基尔达路的维多利亚兵营外面出发，打着数百面旗子向市区进发。旗子上写着“澳大利亚人反对越南战争”和“不许为越南（战争）征兵”等口号。这是迄今墨尔本市反对美国侵略越南的示威活动中最大的一次。
    警察对示威群众进行了镇压，撕毁了一些标语牌，逮捕了两名示威者。
