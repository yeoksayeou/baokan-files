Title: Delegation from the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-03-28
Edition: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, March 26th – The delegation from the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association, composed of Morrison (South Africa) and Sugiyama Ichiboku (Japan), recently returned to Beijing after concluding their friendly visit to Tanzania.
    Before visiting Tanzania, the delegation also visited Pakistan and the United Arab Republic.
    When the delegation arrived in Tanzania, the Chairman of the Tanganyika Journalists' Association, B.S. Karong'uju Mair, greeted them at the airport.
    During the visit, the delegation was warmly received by Tanzanian colleagues. The Tanzanian Minister of Information, Wakil, met with the delegation and had a friendly conversation with them on issues related to the work of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association.
    The delegation also met with representatives of several African national liberation organizations stationed in Dar es Salaam.

### 亚非记协书记处代表团回到北京

1966-03-28
第3版()
专栏：

    亚非记协书记处代表团回到北京
    新华社二十六日讯  由摩里逊（南非）和杉山市平（日本）组成的亚非新闻工作者协会书记处代表团，结束了在坦桑尼亚的友好访问，最近先后返回北京。
    代表团在访问坦桑尼亚前还访问了巴基斯坦和阿拉伯联合共和国。
    代表团到达坦桑尼亚时，坦噶尼喀新闻工作者协会主席贝·萨·卡荣朱迈勒曾到机场迎接他们。
    在访问期间，代表团曾受到坦桑尼亚同行的热情接待，坦桑尼亚新闻部长瓦基勒曾接见了代表团，并同他们友好地交谈了有关亚非新闻工作者协会的工作问题。
    代表团在达累斯萨拉姆还会见了常驻在那里的一些非洲民族解放组织的代表。
