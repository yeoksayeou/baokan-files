Title: Cambodian Cultural and Scientific Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-03-26
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 25th: The five-member Cambodian Cultural and Scientific Delegation, led by Mr. Sim Var, Undersecretary of State of the Cambodian National Education Ministry, arrived in Beijing by plane today. They are visiting China at the invitation of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Science and Technology Commission to discuss and sign the 1966 executive plan of the Sino-Cambodian Cultural and Scientific Cooperation Agreement and to conduct a friendly visit.
    Greeting them at the airport were Shi Huaibi, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Wu Heng, Vice-Chairman of the Science and Technology Commission, and people from relevant departments.
    Cambodian Ambassador to China, Mr. Chhang Song, and officials from the embassy were also at the airport to greet them.

### 柬埔寨文化科学代表团到京

1966-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨文化科学代表团到京
    新华社二十五日讯　柬埔寨文化科学代表团一行五人，在团长、柬埔寨国民教育部副国务员沈力速率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。他们是应对外文化联络委员会和科学技术委员会邀请，来我国商谈和签订中柬文化科学合作协定一九六六年执行计划并进行友好访问的。
    到机场欢迎的，有对外文委副主任史怀璧，科委副主任武衡，以及各有关方面人士。
    柬埔寨驻中国大使张岗和使馆官员也到机场迎接。
