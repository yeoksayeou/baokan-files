Title: At the Invitation of the CPC Central Committee, Comrade Wilcox, General Secretary of the Communist Party of New Zealand, Arrives in Guangzhou for a Friendly Visit; Lin Liming, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Guangdong Provincial Party Committee, Hosts a Banquet to Welcome Him
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-03
Edition: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 2nd—Comrade Wilcox, General Secretary of the Communist Party of New Zealand, arrived in Guangzhou by train this afternoon at the invitation of the CPC Central Committee for a friendly visit to our country.
    Welcoming him at the station were Lin Liming and Li Jianzhen, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Guangdong Provincial Party Committee; Yong Wentao, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Guangdong Provincial Party Committee and First Secretary of the Guangzhou Municipal Party Committee; Zeng Sheng, Third Secretary of the Guangzhou Municipal Party Committee; and responsible comrades from relevant departments.
    Tang Mingzhao, a responsible staff member of the CPC Central Committee organs, made a special trip from Beijing to Shenzhen to greet him. Zuo Ming, Deputy Secretary-General of the Guangdong Provincial Party Committee, also went to Shenzhen to greet him.
    In the evening, Comrade Lin Liming held a banquet to welcome Comrade Wilcox.
    After the banquet, Comrade Wilcox, accompanied by Comrade Lin Liming and others, watched the musical and dance epic "The East is Red."

### 应中共中央邀请来我国进行友好访问  新共总书记威尔科克斯同志到广州  广东省委书记处书记林李明设宴欢迎

1966-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

    应中共中央邀请来我国进行友好访问
    新共总书记威尔科克斯同志到广州
    广东省委书记处书记林李明设宴欢迎
    新华社广州二日电　新西兰共产党总书记威尔科克斯同志应中共中央的邀请，前来我国进行友好访问，今天下午乘火车到达广州。
    到车站欢迎的，有中共广东省委书记处书记林李明、李坚真，中共广东省委书记处书记、中共广州市委第一书记雍文涛，中共广州市委第三书记曾生，以及有关方面负责人等。
    中共中央机关负责工作人员唐明照专程从北京前往深圳迎接。中共广东省委副秘书长左铭也到深圳迎接。
    晚上，林李明同志举行宴会欢迎威尔科克斯同志。
    宴会后，威尔科克斯同志在林李明等同志陪同下，观看了音乐舞蹈史诗《东方红》。
