Title: I Foreign Cultural Commission, etc., Host Banquet for Cambodian Cultural and Scientific Delegation; Vice-Chairman Guo Moruo Receives Cambodian Friends
Author:
Date: 1966-03-28
Edition: 3

    The Foreign Cultural Commission and other organizations held a banquet to welcome the Cambodian Cultural and Scientific Delegation, led by the Deputy Secretary of State of the Cambodian National Education Ministry, Sim Ly Sok.
    Vice-Chairman Guo Moruo of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress met with the Cambodian Cultural and Scientific Delegation led by the Deputy Secretary of State of the Cambodian National Education Ministry, Sim Ly Sok, this evening and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them. Present at the meeting were Shi Huaibi, Wu Heng, Zhang Wei, and others. Cambodian Ambassador to China, Chhang Kong, was also present.
    According to Xinhua News Agency, March 26th: The Foreign Cultural Commission and the State Science and Technology Commission held a banquet this evening to welcome the Cambodian Cultural and Scientific Delegation led by the Deputy Secretary of State of the Cambodian National Education Ministry, Sim Ly Sok.
    Vice-Chairman Guo Moruo of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress attended the banquet. The banquet was filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere, and the hosts and guests repeatedly raised their glasses to wish for the continuous development of the friendship and scientific and cultural cooperation between the peoples of China and Cambodia.
    Vice-Chairman Shi Huaibi of the Foreign Cultural Commission and Delegation Leader Sim Ly Sok spoke at the banquet successively. Vice-Chairman Wu Heng of the State Science and Technology Commission proposed a toast at the banquet. Cambodian Ambassador to China, Chhang Kong, was also invited to attend the banquet.

### 我对外文委等欢宴柬文化科学代表团  郭沫若副委员长接见柬埔寨朋友

1966-03-28
第3版()
专栏：

    我对外文委等欢宴柬文化科学代表团
    郭沫若副委员长接见柬埔寨朋友
    新华社二十六日讯  全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若今晚接见了由柬埔寨国民教育部副国务员沈力速率领的柬埔寨文化科学代表团，和他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有史怀璧、武衡、张维等。柬埔寨驻中国大使张岗也在座。
    据新华社二十六日讯  对外文委和科委今晚举行宴会，欢迎由柬埔寨国民教育部副国务员沈力速率领的柬埔寨文化科学代表团。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若出席了宴会。宴会上洋溢着热烈友好的气氛，宾主不断举杯共祝中柬两国人民的友谊和科学文化合作不断发展。
    对外文委副主任史怀璧和代表团团长沈力速，先后在宴会上讲话。科委副主任武衡在宴会上祝酒。柬埔寨驻中国大使张岗也应邀出席了宴会。
