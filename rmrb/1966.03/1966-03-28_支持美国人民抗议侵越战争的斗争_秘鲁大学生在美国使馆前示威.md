Title: Support the Struggle of the American People Against the War of Aggression Against Vietnam; Peruvian University Students Demonstrate in Front of the US Embassy
Author:
Date: 1966-03-28
Edition: 5

    Supporting the struggle of the American people against the war of aggression against Vietnam
    Peruvian University Students Demonstrate in Front of the US Embassy
    Xinhua News Agency, Lima, 27th – Peruvian students from the renowned San Marcos University held a demonstration in front of the United States Embassy on the 26th, opposing the American aggression in Vietnam and supporting the struggle of “American people protesting against American policy.”
    The demonstrating students held signs condemning the American policy of aggression and continuously shouted anti-American slogans.
    The authorities dispatched police to suppress the students with tear gas and water cannons, and the students valiantly fought back with stones. It is reported that at least two students were arrested by the police. (Attached picture)
    Citizens of Paris demonstrate in the square in front of the US Embassy, angrily protesting the American aggression in Vietnam and the bombing of North Vietnam. The slogan in the picture reads: "Johnson Murderer!" Xinhua News Agency

### 支持美国人民抗议侵越战争的斗争  秘鲁大学生在美国使馆前示威

1966-03-28
第5版()
专栏：

    支持美国人民抗议侵越战争的斗争
    秘鲁大学生在美国使馆前示威
    新华社二十七日讯  利马消息：秘鲁著名的大学圣马科斯大学的学生二十六日在美国大使馆前面举行示威，反对美国侵略越南，并且支持“抗议美国政策的美国人民”的斗争。
    示威学生举着谴责美国侵略政策的标语牌，不断高呼反美口号。
    当局出动警察用催泪弹和水龙镇压学生，学生们用石块英勇还击。据报道，至少有两名学生被警察逮捕。（附图片）
    巴黎市民在美国大使馆前的广场上示威，愤怒抗议美国侵略越南和轰炸越南北方。图中标语上写着：“约翰逊杀人犯！”新华社发
