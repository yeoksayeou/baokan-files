Title: Ugandan Government Issues Special Communiqué Announcing Prime Minister Takes Over All Powers of President and Vice President
Author:
Date: 1966-03-05
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 3rd dispatch, Kampala: The Ugandan government issued a special communiqué on the evening of the 2nd, announcing that Ugandan Prime Minister Obote had taken over all "responsibilities, powers, and functions" of the President and Vice President of Uganda on the 2nd.
    A week ago, Obote took over all the powers of the Ugandan government and announced the temporary suspension of the Ugandan constitution. Ugandan President Mutesa is the King of the Kingdom of Buganda, one of the four regions of Uganda. The Kabaka Yekka party, the ruling party of this region, is the opposition party in Uganda. Vice President Nadiope is the head of the Busoga region.

### 乌干达政府发表特别公报宣布  乌总理接管正副总统一切权力

1966-03-05
第4版()
专栏：

    乌干达政府发表特别公报宣布
    乌总理接管正副总统一切权力
    新华社三日讯　坎帕拉消息：乌干达政府二日晚发表一项特别公报宣布，乌干达总理奥博特二日接管了乌干达总统和副总统的一切“责任、权力和职能”。
    一个星期前，奥博特接管了乌干达政府的全部权力，并宣布暂时停止实行乌干达宪法。乌干达总统穆特萨是乌干达四个地区之一的布干达王国的国王，这个地区的执政党卡巴卡耶卡党是乌干达的反对党。副总统纳迪奥普是布索加地区的首脑。
