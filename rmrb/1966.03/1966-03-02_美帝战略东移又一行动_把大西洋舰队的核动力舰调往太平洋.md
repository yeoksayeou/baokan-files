Title: U.S. Imperialism's Strategic Shift to the East: Transferring Nuclear-Powered Ships of the Atlantic Fleet to the Pacific

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-02
Edition: 3

    U.S. Imperialism's Strategic Shift to the East: Another Action
    Transferring Nuclear-Powered Ships of the Atlantic Fleet to the Pacific
    Xinhua News Agency, 1st dispatch from Washington: To meet the needs of the United States' expanding war of aggression against Vietnam, the U.S. Atlantic Fleet's nuclear-powered missile cruiser "Long Beach" was transferred to the Pacific Fleet on February 28.
    The "Long Beach," at 15,200 tons, is the first nuclear-powered missile cruiser of the United States, with nearly one thousand officers and men on board.
    After the "Long Beach" is transferred east, the U.S. Pacific Fleet will have three surface nuclear-powered warships. The other two are the "Enterprise" aircraft carrier and the "Bainbridge" destroyer.

### 美帝战略东移又一行动  把大西洋舰队的核动力舰调往太平洋

1966-03-02
第3版()
专栏：

    美帝战略东移又一行动
    把大西洋舰队的核动力舰调往太平洋
    新华社一日讯　华盛顿消息：适应美国扩大侵越战争的需要，美国大西洋舰队的核动力导弹巡洋舰“长滩号”已于二月二十八日调往太平洋舰队。
    一万五千二百吨的“长滩号”是美国第一艘核动力导弹巡洋舰，舰上有将近一千名官兵。
    “长滩号”东调后，美国太平洋舰队将有三艘水面核动力军舰。另两艘是“企业号”航空母舰和“班布里奇号”驱逐舰。
