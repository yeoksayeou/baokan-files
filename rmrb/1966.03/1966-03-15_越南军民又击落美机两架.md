Title: Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Two More US Planes

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-15
Edition: 3

    Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Two More US Planes
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 14th (Telegraph) – According to an announcement made here on the evening of the 14th, the military and civilians of Thanh Hoa Province in North Vietnam shot down one invading American jet plane and one seaplane on the 14th. The seaplane was shot down while attempting to rescue the pilot of the jet plane. (Attached image)
    South Vietnamese Liberation Army soldiers, using light weapons to weave a tight web of fire, fiercely attacked American planes. Vietnam News Agency manuscript (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 越南军民又击落美机两架

1966-03-15
第3版()
专栏：

    越南军民又击落美机两架
    新华社河内十四日电　据这里十四日晚上公布：越南北方清化省军民十四日击落入侵的美国喷气式飞机和水上飞机各一架。这架水上飞机是在前来营救喷气式飞机的飞行员时被击落的。（附图片）
    南越解放军战士，用轻武器织成严密的火网，狠揍美国飞机。　越南通讯社稿（新华社发）
