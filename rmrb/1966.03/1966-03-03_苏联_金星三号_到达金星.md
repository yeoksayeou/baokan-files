Title: Soviet "Venus 3" Reaches Venus
Author:
Date: 1966-03-03
Edition: 4

    Soviet "Venus 3" Reaches Venus
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 1 (dispatch) According to TASS reports: The Soviet "Venus 3" automatic station reached Venus on the 1st.
    This automatic station was launched on November 16 last year and flew in outer space for three and a half months.

### 苏联“金星三号”到达金星

1966-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联“金星三号”到达金星
    新华社莫斯科一日电　据塔斯社报道：苏联“金星三号”自动站一日到达金星。
    这个自动站是去年十一月十六日发射的，曾在宇宙空间飞行了三个半月。
