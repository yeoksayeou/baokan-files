Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Philippine Delegation

Author:

Date: 1966-03-17
Edition: 1

    Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Philippine Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, 16th—Vice Premier Chen Yi received Mrs. Maria Kalaw Katigbak, Liberal Party Senator of the Philippines, and the members of the Philippine delegation she led this afternoon, and held friendly talks with them.
    Present at the reception were Zhang Yun, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, and Yang Yunyu, Secretary of the Secretariat.

### 陈毅副总理接见菲律宾考察代表团

1966-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见菲律宾考察代表团
    新华社十六日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见了菲律宾自由党参议员玛丽亚·卡劳·卡蒂瓦克夫人，以及由她率领的菲律宾考察代表团的成员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有全国妇联副主席章蕴，书记处书记杨蕴玉。
