Title: Sino-Afghan Friendship Association Hosts Banquet for Afghan Women's Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-18
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, March 17th – The President of the China-Afghanistan Friendship Association, Zhang Kai, and his wife, held a banquet today to welcome the Afghan Women's Delegation headed by Saliha Faruk Etemadi. Among those attending the banquet were Song Changhua, Shen Zijiu, Xu Keli, and responsible persons from relevant departments.

### 中阿友协宴请阿富汗妇女代表团

1966-03-18
第5版()
专栏：

    中阿友协宴请阿富汗妇女代表团
    新华社十七日讯　中国阿富汗友好协会会长张凯和夫人今天举行宴会，欢迎以萨利哈·法鲁克·埃特马迪为首的阿富汗妇女代表团。出席宴会的有宋常华、沈兹九、徐克立和有关方面负责人。
