Title: 朝报谴责美朴集团增派南朝鲜伪军去南越  朝鲜人民将同越南人民并肩战斗到底  不许日本军国主义勾结美朴集团阴谋重新侵入南朝鲜 (Korean Newspaper Condemns US-Park Group for Increasing the Dispatch of South Korean Puppet Troops to South Vietnam; Korean People Will Fight Shoulder to Shoulder with Vietnamese People to the End; No Japanese Militarism Allowed to Collude with US-Park Group in a Conspiracy to Re-invade South Korea)
Author:
Date: 1966-03-12
Edition: 3

    新华社平壤十日电　《民主朝鲜报》十日发表社论，强烈谴责美帝国主义和朴正熙集团策划再派两万名南朝鲜傀儡军到南越去的罪恶活动，并且完全支持朝鲜政府七日就这件事发表的声明。
    社论说，朴正熙集团听从美国主子的命令，向南越增派傀儡军的罪恶行径，再次暴露出朴正熙集团是美帝国主义的忠实走狗，是背叛朝鲜人民的穷凶极恶的民族叛徒，是越南人民和亚非人民的敌人。
    社论警告说，如果美帝国主义和朴正熙集团无视朝鲜人民和全世界人民的一致愿望，继续把南朝鲜傀儡军派往南越，那么，这就只能加速它们的灭亡。社论表示，在今后反对共同敌人的斗争中，朝鲜人民将同越南人民并肩战斗到底。
    新华社平壤十一日电　平壤报纸十一日就日本驻南朝鲜“大使”木村九日到汉城“就职”一事分别发表评论，严厉谴责日本佐藤政府积极和南朝鲜朴正熙傀儡集团进行勾结的罪行。
    《劳动新闻》的评论指出，在美帝国主义的指使下，日本
  “大使”到南朝鲜“就职”，意味着日本军国主义重新侵入南朝鲜的阴谋活动已经走上了严重阶段。
    评论说，日本军国主义急于实现“日韩国交正常化”的主要目的是企图使它对南朝鲜的侵略合法化；按美国主子的意旨，拼凑“东北亚军事同盟”，从而实现日本军国主义向海外扩张的野心。评论指出，这个日本的所谓“新大使”木村在日本和南朝鲜发表的讲话中，狂妄的叫嚷“防止侵略”和“确立合作体系”以及说什么南朝鲜派军队到南越“对美国和对遏制中国是一件好事”等等，也如实地暴露了日本军国主义的侵略野心。
    评论最后警告说，美日反动派和朴正熙集团必须懂得，今天的朝鲜人民已经不是六十年前的朝鲜人民了，朝鲜人民绝不会容许日本军国主义者侮辱朝鲜人民的这种犯罪活动。

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 10th March. The *Minju Choson* published an editorial on the 10th, strongly condemning the heinous activities of US imperialism and the Park Chung-hee clique in plotting to dispatch another 20,000 South Korean puppet troops to South Vietnam, and fully supporting the statement issued by the Korean government on the 7th regarding this matter.

The editorial stated that the criminal act of the Park Chung-hee clique in following the orders of their American masters and increasing the dispatch of puppet troops to South Vietnam once again exposes that the Park Chung-hee clique are faithful lackeys of US imperialism, heinous national traitors who betray the Korean people, and enemies of the Vietnamese people and the people of Asia and Africa.

The editorial warned that if US imperialism and the Park Chung-hee clique disregard the unanimous wishes of the Korean people and the people of the world and continue to send South Korean puppet troops to South Vietnam, then this will only accelerate their demise. The editorial expressed that in the future struggle against the common enemy, the Korean people will fight shoulder to shoulder with the Vietnamese people to the end.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 11th March. On the 11th, Pyongyang newspapers published separate commentaries on the "inauguration" of Kimura on the 9th as the Japanese "ambassador" to South Korea, severely condemning the crimes of the Japanese Sato government in actively colluding with the South Korean Park Chung-hee puppet clique.

The *Rodong Sinmun*’s commentary pointed out that under the instigation of US imperialism, the Japanese "ambassador’s" "inauguration" in South Korea signifies that the plot of Japanese militarism to re-invade South Korea has reached a serious stage.

The commentary stated that the main purpose of Japanese militarism’s eagerness to achieve "normalization of relations between Japan and South Korea" is to attempt to legalize its aggression against South Korea; to piece together a "Northeast Asia military alliance" according to the wishes of their American masters, thereby realizing the ambition of Japanese militarism to expand overseas. The commentary pointed out that the so-called "new ambassador" Kimura's arrogant clamoring in his speeches in Japan and South Korea about "preventing aggression" and "establishing a cooperative system," as well as saying that South Korea sending troops to South Vietnam "is a good thing for the United States and for containing China," truthfully exposes the aggressive ambitions of Japanese militarism.

The commentary finally warned that the US-Japanese reactionaries and the Park Chung-hee clique must understand that the Korean people of today are no longer the Korean people of sixty years ago, and the Korean people will never allow Japanese militarists to insult the Korean people with this criminal activity.
