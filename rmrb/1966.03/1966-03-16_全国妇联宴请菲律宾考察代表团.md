Title: All-China Women's Federation Hosts Banquet for Philippine Inspection Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-16
Edition: 4

    According to Xinhua News Agency, March 14th: The All-China Women's Federation held a banquet this evening to welcome the Philippine Inspection Delegation headed by Mrs. Maria Kalaw Katigbak, Senator of the Philippine Liberal Party.
    Zhang Yun, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, spoke at the banquet. She said that there is a long tradition of friendship between the people of China and the Philippines.
    She said that the difficulties in promoting friendly exchanges and developing trade between the people of China and the Philippines do not lie on our side. We are willing to spare no effort to contribute to the promotion of friendship between the people of China and the Philippines.
    Mrs. Maria Kalaw Katigbak said in her speech that we are the first delegation from the Philippines to visit China. We hope that through this visit, the long-standing friendship between the Philippines and China will be restored. We will bring everything we see in China to the 32 million people of the Philippines, and we sincerely thank the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China for its warm reception.

### 全国妇联宴请菲律宾考察代表团

1966-03-16
第4版()
专栏：

    全国妇联宴请菲律宾考察代表团
    据新华社十四日讯　全国妇联今晚举行宴会，欢迎以菲律宾自由党参议员玛丽亚·卡劳·卡蒂瓦克夫人为首的菲律宾考察代表团。
    全国妇联副主席章蕴在宴会上讲话。她说，中菲两国人民之间有着悠久的传统友谊。
    她说，在促进中菲两国人民友好往来和发展两国贸易上，困难不在我们方面，我们愿意为促进中菲两国人民友好，不遗余力地贡献自己的力量。
    玛丽亚·卡劳·卡蒂瓦克夫人在讲话中说，我们是菲律宾第一个来中国访问的代表团，我们希望通过这次访问，使菲律宾和中国具有悠久历史的友谊恢复起来。我们将把在中国看到的一切带给三千二百万菲律宾人民，我们衷心感谢中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会的盛情接待。
