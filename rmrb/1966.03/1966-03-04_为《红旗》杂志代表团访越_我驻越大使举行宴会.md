Title: Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam Holds Banquet for《Red Flag》Magazine Delegation's Visit to Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-03-04
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 2nd – Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam, Zhu Qiwen, hosted a banquet on the evening of February 2nd for the 《Red Flag》 magazine delegation led by Fan Ruoyu, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of《Red Flag》.
    Vietnamese attendees at the banquet included: To Huu, Secretary of the Central Secretariat of the Vietnamese Labor Party; Ha Huy Giap, Central Committee Member; Vu Tuan, Director of the General Office of the Central Committee; Dao Duy Tung, Editor-in-Chief of《Hoc Tap》magazine; Hong Chuong, Deputy Editor-in-Chief; Tran Chi Hien, Deputy Director of the Foreign Liaison Committee of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party; Tran Huy Lieu, Vice-President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association; Hoang Tung, Secretary-General; Luu Qui Ky, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee; Nguyen Thanh Le, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of《Nhan Dan》; and Hoang Van Hoan, Vice-Chairman of the Vietnam Journalists Association, among others.
    At the banquet, which was filled with friendship, Ambassador Zhu Qiwen, Delegation Leader Fan Ruoyu, Comrade To Huu, and Comrade Dao Duy Tung successively raised their glasses in toasts, warmly praising the great militant friendship between the Chinese and Vietnamese Parties and peoples, and jointly wishing the Vietnamese people new and even more brilliant victories in their struggle to resist America and save the nation.

### 为《红旗》杂志代表团访越  我驻越大使举行宴会

1966-03-04
第4版()
专栏：

    为《红旗》杂志代表团访越
    我驻越大使举行宴会
    新华社河内二日电　中国驻越南大使朱其文二日晚上为由《红旗》杂志副总编辑范若愚率领的《红旗》杂志代表团举行宴会。
    越南方面出席宴会的有越南劳动党中央书记处书记素友，中央委员何辉甲，中央办公厅主任武遵，《学习》杂志总编辑陶维松、副总编辑鸿章，越南劳动党中央对外联络委员会副主任陈志贤，越中友好协会副会长陈辉燎、秘书长黄俊，对外文化联络委员会副主任刘贵琪，《人民报》副总编辑阮成黎，越南新闻工作者协会副主席黄文戬等。
    在友情洋溢的宴会上，朱其文大使、范若愚团长、素友同志和陶维松同志先后举杯祝酒，热烈称颂中越两党、两国人民之间的伟大战斗友谊，共祝越南人民抗美救国斗争取得新的更加辉煌的胜利。
