    Bolaven Plateau
    The Bolaven Plateau is located between Saravane Province, Attapu Province, and Sekong Province in Lower Laos, east of the Mekong River, with an average elevation of 1,000 meters above sea level. It is one of the four major plateaus in Laos. The Bolaven Plateau is bordered by Cambodia to the south, near South Vietnam to the east, and Thailand to the west, making its strategic position very important.
    Since February 9th of this year, U.S. imperialism and its lackeys have dispatched a large number of troops to launch a large-scale offensive against the Bolaven Plateau liberated area, attempting to occupy the entire Bolaven Plateau in order to turn Lower Laos into a springboard connecting Thailand and South Vietnam, achieving their conspiracy to threaten Cambodia and "blockade" South Vietnam.

### 波罗芬高原

1966-03-02
第4版()
专栏：新闻地理

    波罗芬高原
    波罗芬高原位于下寮沙拉湾省、阿速坡省和巴色省之间，湄公河以东，平均拔海一千米，是老挝四大高原之一。波罗芬高原南面临近柬埔寨，东边靠近越南南方，西面是泰国，战略地位很重要。
    从今年二月九日以来，美帝国主义及其走狗出动大批军队对波罗芬高原解放区发动了大规模进攻，企图占领整个波罗芬高原，以便把老挝的下寮变成联接泰国和南越的跳板，达到威胁柬埔寨、“封锁”南越的阴谋计划。
