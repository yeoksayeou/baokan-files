Title: Japanese Friendly Trade Figures Gather in Tokyo, Opposing the Sato Government's Following of the United States in "Containing" China

Author:

Date: 1966-03-17
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 16th. Nearly three hundred Japanese friendly trade figures gathered in Tokyo on the 15th, strongly condemning U.S. imperialism's aggression against Vietnam and opposing the Sato Cabinet's following of the United States in obstructing and undermining normal non-governmental trade relations between Japan and countries such as China, Vietnam, and Korea.
    Kaneko Mitsuhiro, Deputy Director of the United Front Work Department of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, spoke at the meeting. He exposed and condemned the Sato Cabinet for increasingly blatantly becoming an accomplice in U.S. imperialism's war of aggression against Vietnam, actively plotting to send troops abroad. Kaneko called on Japanese friendly trade figures to unite and struggle against the heinous plots of the U.S.-Japan reactionaries and to ensure the victory of the nationwide unified action to be held on March 20, opposing U.S. aggression against Vietnam, demanding the overthrow of the Sato Cabinet, and the dissolution of the Diet.
    Trade figures attending the meeting spoke one after another, pointing out that in order to develop friendly trade between Japan and China, Japan and Vietnam, and Japan and Korea, it is necessary to oppose U.S. imperialism's aggression against Vietnam, abolish the U.S.-Japan "Security Treaty," and smash the U.S.-Japan reactionaries' policy of "containing" China.
    The meeting also passed a letter of protest, firmly opposing the Japanese government's invitation to Nguyen Cao Ky, the puppet head of U.S. imperialism in South Vietnam, to visit Japan.

### 日本友好贸易界人士在东京集会  反对佐藤政府追随美国“遏制”中国

1966-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

    日本友好贸易界人士在东京集会
    反对佐藤政府追随美国“遏制”中国
    新华社东京十六日电　日本友好贸易界人士将近三百人十五日在东京集会，强烈谴责美帝国主义侵略越南，反对日本佐藤内阁追随美国阻挠和破坏日本同中国、越南及朝鲜等国家的正常的民间贸易往来。
    日本共产党中央委员会统一战线部副部长金子满广在会上讲了话。他揭露和谴责佐藤内阁越来越明目张胆地成为美帝国主义侵略越南战争的帮凶，积极策划派兵出国。金子号召日本友好贸易界人士团结一致同美日反动派的罪恶阴谋进行斗争，并且使将在三月二十日举行的反对美国侵略越南、要求打倒佐藤内阁、解散国会的全国统一行动获得胜利。
    出席集会的贸易界人士纷纷发言，指出要开展日中、日越、日朝友好贸易，就必须反对美帝国主义侵略越南，废除日美“安全条约”和粉碎美日反动派“遏制”中国的政策。
    会议还通过了一项抗议书，坚决反对日本政府邀请美帝国主义在南越的傀儡头子阮高其访问日本。
