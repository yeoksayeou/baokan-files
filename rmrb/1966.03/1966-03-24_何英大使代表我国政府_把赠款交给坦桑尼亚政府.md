Title: Ambassador He Ying, on behalf of the Chinese government, presents a grant to the Tanzanian government

Author:
Date: 1966-03-24
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Dar es Salaam, 22nd - Chinese Ambassador to Tanzania, He Ying, on behalf of the Chinese government, presented a grant of 30,000 pounds to the Tanzanian government on the 22nd, as funds for the expansion of the Dar es Salaam University College.
    During Premier Zhou Enlai's visit to Tanzania last June, he visited the University College and expressed that the Chinese government would provide assistance to it. This college was founded in 1961.
    At the ceremony, Dr. Chaggula, Principal of Dar es Salaam University College, accepted the grant on behalf of the Tanzanian government. In his speech, he expressed his deep gratitude to the Chinese government and Premier Zhou Enlai.
    Ambassador He Ying said in his speech that he hoped that Tanzania's education would develop rapidly, and that the university would cultivate more talent for Tanzania's national construction. He also expressed the hope that cooperation between China and Tanzania in education would be further developed.

### 何英大使代表我国政府  把赠款交给坦桑尼亚政府

1966-03-24
第4版()
专栏：

    何英大使代表我国政府
    把赠款交给坦桑尼亚政府
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆二十二日电　中国驻坦桑尼亚大使何英二十二日代表中国政府把三万英镑的赠款交给坦桑尼亚政府，作为扩建达累斯萨拉姆大学学院校舍的费用。
    去年六月周恩来总理访问坦桑尼亚期间，参观过这所大学学院，并表示中国政府将向这所大学学院提供援助。这所学院是在一九六一年创建的。
    在仪式上，达累斯萨拉姆大学学院院长查古拉博士代表坦桑尼亚政府接受了这笔赠款。他在讲话中对中国政府和周恩来总理表示深切的感谢。
    何英大使在讲话中表示希望坦桑尼亚的教育事业迅速发展，希望这所大学将为坦桑尼亚培养出更多建设国家的人才。他还表示希望中国和坦桑尼亚在教育方面的合作进一步发展。
