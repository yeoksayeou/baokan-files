Title: We Shot Down a U.S. Unmanned High-Altitude Reconnaissance Drone Over Central and Southern China

Author:
Date: 1966-03-06
Edition: 1

    Vice Premier Lin Biao Issued an Order to Commend the Air Force Units Participating in the Battle, Hoping that Commanders and Fighters Will Strengthen Combat Readiness, Resolutely Eliminate All Intrusion Enemy Planes, and Strive for Greater Victories.
    Xinhua News Agency, 5th – A U.S. imperialist military unmanned high-altitude reconnaissance drone intruded into the airspace of the central and southern regions of our country this afternoon to carry out reconnaissance and provocative activities, and was shot down by the Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.
    Xinhua News Agency, 5th – Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense Lin Biao issued an order of commendation today, praising the Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army for shooting down a U.S. imperialist military unmanned high-altitude reconnaissance drone over the central and southern regions. The order of commendation hopes that the commanders and fighters of the Air Force will hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought even higher, resolutely implement the five principles of highlighting politics, carry forward the spirit of not fearing hardship and not fearing death, be a hundred times more vigilant, strengthen combat readiness, resolutely eliminate all invading enemy planes, and strive for greater victories.

### 我在中南上空击落美无人驾驶高空侦察机一架

1966-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我在中南上空击落美无人驾驶高空侦察机一架
    林彪副总理颁令嘉奖参战空军部队，希望指战员们加强战备，坚决消灭一切入侵敌机，争取更大胜利。
    新华社五日讯　美帝国主义军用无人驾驶高空侦察机一架，今天下午侵入我国中南地区上空进行侦察挑衅活动，被我人民解放军空军部队击落。
    新华社五日讯　国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪今天颁发嘉奖令，表扬在中南地区上空击落一架美帝国主义军用无人驾驶高空侦察机的中国人民解放军空军部队。嘉奖令希望空军部队指战员们，更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，坚决贯彻突出政治的五项原则，发扬不怕苦、不怕死的精神，百倍警惕，加强战备，坚决消灭一切入侵的敌机，争取更大的胜利。
