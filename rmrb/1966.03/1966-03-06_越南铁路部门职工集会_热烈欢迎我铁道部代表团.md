Title: Vietnamese Railway Workers' Rally Warmly Welcomes Delegation from the Chinese Ministry of Railways
Author:
Date: 1966-03-06
Edition: 4

    (Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 3rd) A rally of more than 700 Vietnamese railway workers was held on the evening of the 2nd to warmly welcome the delegation from the Chinese Ministry of Railways, which is currently visiting Vietnam.
    Hong Thach Tam, Vice Minister of Transport and Communications of Vietnam, Ha Dang An, Director-General of the Railway Bureau, Nguyen Duy Ninh and Truong Quang Hy, Deputy Directors-General, and others attended the rally.
    Director-General Ha Dang An spoke at the meeting, warmly praising the fighting friendship between the railway workers of Vietnam and China.
    Guo Lu, head of the Chinese Ministry of Railways delegation, also spoke at the meeting. He warmly congratulated the Vietnamese railway workers on the brilliant achievements they have made in the struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation, and in the development of railway transport.
    After the rally, Vietnamese artists performed song and dance programs reflecting the Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation.

### 越南铁路部门职工集会  热烈欢迎我铁道部代表团

1966-03-06
第4版()
专栏：

    越南铁路部门职工集会
    热烈欢迎我铁道部代表团
    据新华社河内三日电　越南铁路部门职工七百多人二日晚上举行集会，热烈欢迎正在越南访问的中国铁道部代表团。
    越南交通运输部副部长洪赤心，铁路总局局长何登恩，副局长阮维宁、张光熙等出席了集会。
    何登恩局长在会上讲话，热烈赞扬越中两国铁路职工之间的战斗友谊。
    中国铁道部代表团团长郭鲁也在会上讲了话。他热烈祝贺越南铁路职工在抗美救国斗争中和发展铁路运输事业中所取得的辉煌成就。
    集会结束后，越南文艺工作者演出了反映越南人民抗美救国斗争的歌舞节目。
