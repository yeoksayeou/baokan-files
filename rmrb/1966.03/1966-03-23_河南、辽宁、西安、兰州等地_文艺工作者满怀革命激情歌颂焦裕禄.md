Title: Henan, Liaoning, Xi'an, Lanzhou and Other Places: Literary and Art Workers Sing the Praises of Jiao Yulu with Revolutionary Passion

Author: 
Date: 1966-03-23
Edition: 2

    According to Xinhua News Agency on the 22nd: Literary and art workers in Henan, Liaoning, Xi'an, Lanzhou, Hefei, Kunming and other provinces and cities, filled with revolutionary passion, are learning from Jiao Yulu, writing about Jiao Yulu, performing Jiao Yulu, and painting Jiao Yulu. They are using various art forms to sing the praises of Jiao Yulu's revolutionary spirit, inspiring the broad masses of the people.

    In Henan Province, more than 20 professional and amateur writers have gone to Lankao to create reportage, novels, and dramas reflecting the revolutionary spirit of Comrade Jiao Yulu. Literary and art workers in the Kaifeng area and various counties have created a large-scale Henan opera, *Jiao Yulu*, and many vocal works in a short period of time. After their public performances, they inspired the revolutionary enthusiasm of the people to struggle against difficulties.

    Art workers in these provinces and cities are also enthusiastically taking up artistic creation tools, actively creating picture stories, series of paintings, propaganda posters, and large-scale clay sculptures praising Jiao Yulu.

### 河南、辽宁、西安、兰州等地  文艺工作者满怀革命激情歌颂焦裕禄

1966-03-23
第2版()
专栏：

    河南、辽宁、西安、兰州等地
    文艺工作者满怀革命激情歌颂焦裕禄
    据新华社二十二日讯　河南、辽宁、西安、兰州、合肥、昆明等省市的文艺工作者满怀革命激情，学焦裕禄，写焦裕禄，演焦裕禄，画焦裕禄。他们运用各种文艺形式歌颂焦裕禄的革命精神，鼓舞了广大人民群众。
    河南省有二十多位专业和业余作者已奔赴兰考，创作反映焦裕禄同志革命精神的报告文学、小说和戏剧作品。开封专区和各县文艺工作者在短时间内创作出大型豫剧《焦裕禄》和许多演唱作品，公演后鼓舞了人民群众向困难作斗争的革命热情。
    这几个省市的美术工作者也热情地拿起美术创作工具，积极创作歌颂焦裕禄的连环画、组画、宣传画和大型泥塑。
