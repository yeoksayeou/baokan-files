Title: Chairman Choi Yong-kun Receives Our Ambassador
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-11
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 9th -- Choi Yong-kun, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, received Jiao Ruoyu, the Chinese Ambassador to Korea, on the afternoon of the 9th.
    Present at the meeting were Kim Yong-nam, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of Korea, and Kim Nong-hyon, Director of the Foreign Affairs Department of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly.
    Chairman Choi Yong-kun had a cordial and friendly conversation with Ambassador Jiao Ruoyu.

### 崔庸健委员长接见我大使

1966-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    崔庸健委员长接见我大使
    新华社平壤九日电  朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健九日下午接见了中国驻朝鲜大使焦若愚。
    接见时在座的有，朝鲜外务省副相金永南和最高人民会议常任委员会外事部长金农贤。
    崔庸健委员长同焦若愚大使进行了亲切友好的谈话。
