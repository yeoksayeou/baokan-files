Title: Romanian Communist Party General Secretary Receives New Chinese Ambassador to Romania
Author:
Date: 1966-03-22
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, March 21st - Nicolae Ceaușescu, General Secretary of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee, received Zeng Yongquan, the newly appointed Chinese Ambassador to Romania, on the 21st and held friendly talks with him.

    Present at the reception was An. Păcuraru, member of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee.

### 罗共中央总书记接见我新任驻罗大使

1966-03-22
第4版()
专栏：

    罗共中央总书记接见我新任驻罗大使
    新华社布加勒斯特二十一日电　罗马尼亚共产党中央委员会总书记尼·齐奥塞斯库二十一日接见了中国新任驻罗马尼亚大使曾涌泉，并同他进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有罗共中央委员安·帕库拉鲁。
