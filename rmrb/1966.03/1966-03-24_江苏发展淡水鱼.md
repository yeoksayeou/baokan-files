Title: Jiangsu Develops Freshwater Fish
Author: 
Date: 1966-03-24
Edition: 2

　　全国重点淡水渔区之一的江苏省，今年淡水鱼的养殖面积比去年增加了百分之二十左右。目前，各地群众正在积极放养鱼种。

Jiangsu Province, one of the key freshwater fishing areas in the country, has increased its freshwater fish farming area by about 20 percent this year compared to last year. At present, the masses in various places are actively stocking fish fry.



**Named Entities**


江苏省	Jiangsu Sheng
