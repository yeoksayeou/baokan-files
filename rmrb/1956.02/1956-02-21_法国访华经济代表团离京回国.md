Title: French Economic Delegation to China Departs Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1956-02-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, February 20th: The French economic delegation to China, headed by Henri Rochereau, has departed Beijing for home in batches, via Shanghai and Guangzhou. Henri Rochereau and his party left Beijing on the 20th. Those seeing them off at the airport included the heads of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, the Bank of China, and various specialized import and export corporations of China.
    Henri Rochereau held a farewell reception on the eve of his departure from Beijing. Among those attending the reception were Lei Renmin, Li Zhuchen, and Ji Chaoding, vice chairmen of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Lu Xuzhang, a member of the council; Qiao Peixin, acting deputy general manager of the Bank of China; and the heads of various specialized import and export corporations of China.

### 法国访华经济代表团离京回国

1956-02-21
第1版()
专栏：

    法国访华经济代表团离京回国
    新华社20日讯　以亨利·罗谢罗为首的法国访华经济代表团已分批离北京取道上海和广州返国。团长亨利·罗谢罗一行在20日离北京，到机场送行的有中国国际贸易促进委员会、中国银行和中国进出口各专业公司的负责人。
    亨利·罗谢罗在离北京前夕举行了辞别酒会。出席酒会的有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民、李烛尘、冀朝鼎、委员卢绪章，中国银行代副总经理乔培新和中国进出口各专业公司的负责人。
