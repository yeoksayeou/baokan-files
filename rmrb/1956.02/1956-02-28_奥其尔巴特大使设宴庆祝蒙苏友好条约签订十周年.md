Title: Ambassador Ochirbat Hosts Banquet to Celebrate the Tenth Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship
Author: 
Date: 1956-02-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 27th:

    Mongolian Ambassador to China, Ochirbat, held a banquet this evening to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance. Invited to the banquet were Soviet Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China, Li Haiqiu, and embassy staff, as well as the envoys and diplomatic officials of various People's Democratic countries and India to China.

    On the Chinese side, those invited to attend the banquet included Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia He Ying, and heads of relevant departments. The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 奥其尔巴特大使设宴庆祝蒙苏友好条约签订十周年

1956-02-28
第1版()
专栏：

    奥其尔巴特大使设宴庆祝蒙苏友好条约签订十周年
    新华社27日讯　蒙古人民共和国驻中国大使奥其尔巴特在今天晚间举行宴会，庆祝蒙苏友好互助条约签订十周年。应邀出席宴会的，有苏联驻中国大使馆临时代办李海秋和使馆人员，以及各人民民主国家和印度驻中国的使节和外交官员。
    中国方面应邀出席宴会的，有国务院副总理陈毅，文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国驻蒙古大使何英，以及有关部门的负责人。宴会在友好的空气中进行。
