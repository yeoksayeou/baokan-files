Title: Czechoslovakian Government Delegation Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1956-02-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 28th. The Czechoslovakian government delegation, headed by Grigol, arrived in Guangzhou today from Beijing, accompanied by Ji Chaoding, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.

Welcoming the distinguished guests at the station were Wen Minsheng, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, and Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou. The Soviet and Polish consuls in Guangzhou also went to welcome them.

The delegation is here to attend the opening ceremony of the Exhibition of Achievements in Socialist Construction of Czechoslovakia, which will soon be held in Guangzhou. After arriving in Guangzhou, they inspected the exhibition venue. All preparations have been completed for the exhibition, which will open on March 1st.

After arriving in Guangzhou, the delegation called on the leaders of the Provincial and Municipal People's Committees, toured Yuexiu Mountain, and watched a Cantonese opera in the evening.

### 捷政府代表团到广州

1956-02-29
第1版()
专栏：

    捷政府代表团到广州
    新华社广州28日电　以格里哥尔为首的捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团在中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎的陪同下，今天从北京到达广州。
    在车站欢迎贵宾的有广东省副省长文敏生、广州市市长朱光。苏联和波兰驻广州领事也前往欢迎。
    代表团是来参加即将在广州展出的捷克斯洛伐克社会主义建设成就展览会的开幕仪式的。他们到达广州后，就巡阅了展览会会场。这个将在3月1日开幕的展览会已经做好了一切准备工作。
    代表团到广州后，曾拜会了省、市人民委员会的领导人，并且游览了越秀山，还在晚上观看了粤剧。
