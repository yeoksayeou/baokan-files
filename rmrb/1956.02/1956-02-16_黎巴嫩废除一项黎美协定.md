Title: Lebanon Abrogates a Lebanese-American Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-02-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, February 13th, TASS: Lebanese Education Minister George Akel told reporters that the Lebanese government has abrogated the agreement concluded between the Lebanese Ministry of Education and the United States International Cooperation Administration because this agreement has damaged Lebanon's national sovereignty.

### 黎巴嫩废除一项黎美协定

1956-02-16
第4版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩废除一项黎美协定
    新华社15日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特13日讯：黎巴嫩教育部长乔治·阿克尔对新闻记者说，黎巴嫩政府废除了黎巴嫩教育部和美国国际合作署之间缔结的协定，因为这个协定使黎巴嫩的国家主权受到损害。
