Title: Indonesian Embassy Chargé d'Affaires Holds Banquet for Indonesian Naval Delegation's Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1956-02-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 28th. Anwa Sani, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy in China, held a reception and banquet on the evening of February 28th to mark the visit of the Indonesian naval delegation to China.

Present at this evening's reception and banquet were Xiao Jingguang, Vice Minister of the Ministry of National Defense of the People's Republic of China and Admiral of the Navy of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Li Da, Vice Minister of National Defense and Senior General, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs and responsible persons of other relevant ministries, Senior Generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Li Kenong, Gan Siqi, Su Zhenhua, Liu Yalou (Air Force Senior General), Wang Hongkun (Navy Senior General), Xiao Xiangrong (Lieutenant General), Luo Shunchu (Navy Lieutenant General), Zhou Xihan (Navy Lieutenant General), Wang Bingzhang (Air Force Lieutenant General) and other generals, Huang Zhen, Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia, and Colonel Mao Chen, Military Attaché, and people from all walks of life in the capital.

Heads of diplomatic missions and military attachés, acting military attachés, and deputy military attachés of various countries in China were also invited to attend.

Rear Admiral Subianto and his wife, as well as all members of the delegation, attended the reception and banquet.

### 为印度尼西亚海军代表团访问中国  印度尼西亚大使馆代办举行宴会

1956-02-29
第1版()
专栏：

    为印度尼西亚海军代表团访问中国
    印度尼西亚大使馆代办举行宴会
    新华社28日讯　印度尼西亚驻华大使馆临时代办安瓦·萨尼为印度尼西亚海军访华代表团访问中国，在28日晚举行酒会和宴会。
    出席今晚酒会和宴会的，有中华人民共和国国防部副部长、中国人民解放军海军司令员萧劲光海军大将，国防部副部长李达上将，外交部副部长章汉夫和其他有关各部负责人，中国人民解放军高级将领李克农上将、甘泗淇上将、苏振华上将、刘亚楼空军上将、王宏坤海军上将、萧向荣中将、罗舜初海军中将、周希汉海军中将、王秉璋空军中将以及其他将官们，中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇和武官茅琛上校，首都各界人士。
    各国驻华使节和武官、代理武官、副武官也应邀出席。
    苏比雅克托海军少将和夫人以及代表团全体人员出席了酒会和宴会。
