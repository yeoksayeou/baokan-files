Title: Film Production Will Generally Increase This Year
Author: 
Date: 1956-02-11
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch
    The Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture held a national film studio directors' meeting from January 28 to February 6. The meeting decided that in 1956, 36 feature films (including 10 films specifically for farmers), and 36 science and education films will be produced, doubling the output of 1955; it will also produce 78 long and short documentaries, 72 newsreels, 72 translated films, and the output of other types of films will also increase. The goal is to strive to complete the first five-year plan one to one and a half years ahead of schedule.

### 今年各种影片产量将普遍增加

1956-02-11
第3版()
专栏：

    今年各种影片产量将普遍增加
    文化部电影事业管理局1月28日到2月6日召开全国电影制片厂厂长会议。会上决定：1956年将生产艺术片36部（其中专为农民摄制的影片10部），科学教育片36部，都比1955年产量增长一倍；还要生产长、短纪录片78部，新闻简报72号，翻译片72部，其他各片种的产量也都有增加，普遍争取提前一年到一年半完成第一个五年计划。　（据新华社讯）
