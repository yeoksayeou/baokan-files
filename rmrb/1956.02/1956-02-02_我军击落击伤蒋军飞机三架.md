Title: Our Army Shoots Down and Damages Three Chiang Kai-shek Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1956-02-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. On January 29th, at 8:39 AM, four Chiang Kai-shek F-84 fighter jets intruded into the airspace over Dongshan Island, Fujian Province. The People's Liberation Army's anti-aircraft artillery units intercepted them with fierce artillery fire, and two Chiang Kai-shek aircraft were shot down and crashed into the sea north of Dongshan Island.

On January 30th, at 7:35 AM, four Chiang Kai-shek F-84 fighter jets intruded into the Shantou City area of Guangdong Province and strafed the area. One aircraft was damaged by the People's Liberation Army's anti-aircraft artillery units.

### 我军击落击伤蒋军飞机三架

1956-02-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我军击落击伤蒋军飞机三架
    新华社1日讯　1月29日上午8时39分，蒋军F—84型战斗机4架窜扰福建省东山岛上空，中国人民解放军高射炮部队以猛烈炮火截击，两架蒋军飞机被击落，坠毁在东山岛以北的海里。
    1月30日上午7时35分，蒋军F —84型战斗机4架窜扰广东省汕头市地区，进行扫射，被中国人民解放军高射炮部队击伤1架。
