Title: Tibetan Visiting Group Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-02-12
Page: 3

    The Tibetan Visiting Group returned to Beijing on February 10th. Those who welcomed them at the station included Sakongliao, Vice-Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, Gan Chunlei, Director of the General Office, Peng Sike, Deputy Director, and responsible persons from the Dalai Lama's Office in Beijing, the Panchen Lama's Office in Beijing, and the Buddhist Association of China, totaling more than 80 people.
    The visiting group departed from Beijing in October 1955 and visited factories, mines, and rural areas in Northeast, Northwest, East China, and Central South China. They also visited famous temples such as Ta'er Monastery and were warmly welcomed and received by people's committees at all levels and people of all ethnic groups. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 西藏地区参观团返京

1956-02-12
第3版()
专栏：

    西藏地区参观团返京
    西藏地区参观团在2月10日返回北京。到车站欢迎的有民族事务委员会副主任萨空了，办公厅主任甘春雷、副主任朋斯克，和西藏达赖驻京办事处、西藏班禅驻京办事处、中国佛教协会的负责人等，共80多人。
    参观团自1955年10月由北京出发，曾先后在东北、西北、华东、中南各地参观了工厂、矿山和农村，访问了塔尔寺等著名寺院，到处受到当地各级人民委员会和各族人民的热情欢迎和接待。　　　（新华社）
