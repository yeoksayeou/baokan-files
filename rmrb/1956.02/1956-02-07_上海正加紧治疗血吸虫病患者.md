Title: Shanghai Intensifies Treatment of Schistosomiasis Patients
Author:
Date: 1956-02-07
Page: 3

    Shanghai is Intensifying Treatment of Schistosomiasis Patients
    The 25 treatment teams organized by the Shanghai Schistosomiasis Prevention and Treatment Institute treated 1,070 schistosomiasis patients in rural suburban areas in January. The treatment teams, carrying complete medical equipment, set up temporary treatment stations to both screen patients and provide treatment. 400 patients have been initially cured, and schistosomiasis patients with late-stage conditions or other concurrent illnesses are mostly sent to hospitals in the city for treatment.
    Currently, Shanghai is training 800 medical personnel, preparing them to participate in schistosomiasis prevention and treatment work in rural areas after the Spring Festival. The number of beds in the city's hospitals for schistosomiasis patients will also be increased from 450 to 790. It is estimated that 10,000 patients can be treated in the first quarter. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 上海正加紧治疗血吸虫病患者

1956-02-07
第3版()
专栏：

    上海正加紧治疗血吸虫病患者
    上海市血吸虫病防治所组织的25个治疗小组，在1月份里到郊区农村治疗了1，070个血吸虫病人。治疗小组携带着完备的医疗仪器分别设置了临时治疗站，一边普查病人，一边进行治疗。已经初步治愈的有400个病人，晚期的或是兼有其他疾病的血吸虫病人，大多数送到市区各医院治疗。
    目前，上海正在训练800个医疗人员，准备在春节以后到农村中参加防治血吸虫病的治疗工作。全市各医院收容血吸虫病人的床位也将由450张增加到790张。预计在第一季度中能够治疗1万个病人。（新华社）
