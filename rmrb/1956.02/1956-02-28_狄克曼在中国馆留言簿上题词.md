Title: Dieckmann writes inscription in the Chinese Pavilion guestbook

Author: 
Date: 1956-02-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Leipzig, 27th -- On the morning of February 26th, at 8:30 AM, leaders of the government of the German Democratic Republic and members of government delegations from various countries visited the Chinese Pavilion at the Leipzig International Fair.

    After the visit, Dieckmann, Chairman of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, inscribed in the guestbook: "We visited the exhibition of our Chinese friends with great interest. It is a testament to the tremendous development of People's China."

### 狄克曼在中国馆留言簿上题词

1956-02-28
第4版()
专栏：

    狄克曼在中国馆留言簿上题词
    据新华社来比锡27日电　2月26日上午8时半，德意志民主共和国政府领导人员和各国政府代表团人员参观了在来比锡国际博览会中的中国馆。
    德意志民主共和国人民议院主席狄克曼参观后在留言簿上题词说：“我们怀着极大的兴趣参观了我们中国朋友的展出。它是人民中国巨大发展的证人。”
