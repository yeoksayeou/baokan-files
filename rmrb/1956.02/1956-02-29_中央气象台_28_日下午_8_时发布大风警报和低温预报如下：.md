Title: Central Meteorological Observatory Issues Gale Warning and Low Temperature Forecast

Author:
Date: 1956-02-29
Page: 4

The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a gale warning and low temperature forecast at 8:00 PM on the 28th:

　　It is expected that in the next 24 hours, there will still be northerly winds of force 5 to 7 in the central part of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and the eastern part of Hebei Province. Tomorrow and the day after tomorrow morning, the minimum temperature in the Beijing area may drop to around minus 10 degrees Celsius.

### 中央气象台28日下午8时发布大风警报和低温预报如下：

1956-02-29
第4版()
专栏：

    中央气象台28日下午8时发布大风警报和低温预报如下：
    预计未来24小时内内蒙古自治区的中部、河北省的东部仍有5级到7级偏北风。明天和后天早晨，北京地区的最低温度，可下降到摄氏零下10度左右。
