Title: CPPCC National Committee Plenary Session Continues

Author: 
Date: 1956-02-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Second Session of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference continued its general discussion on the morning of the 4th.

    The following spoke at the meeting: Bao Erhan, Shen Yanbing, Xu Guangping, Chen Banding, Rong Yiren, Li Congfu, Xu Chubo, Jin Baoshan, Lu Han, Liao Shicheng, Wang Yukun, Weng Wenhao, He Xian, Hu Qili, Han Quanhua, Sun Yueqi, Wu Daren, Zhang Zhijiang, Gu Chunfan, Huang Yuxian, Wang Shou, Shen Junru, Chen Shihua, Li Fengsun, Yan Jiale.

    The meeting began at 9:00 AM and ended at 12:30 PM.

    Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Second Session of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference continued its general discussion on the afternoon of the 4th.

    The following spoke at the meeting this afternoon: Liang Xi, Sheng Pihua, Yan Fuqing, Sun Yunzhu, Lu Shijia, Tang Yue, Ji Gongquan, Bao Guobao, Lu Zhonglin, Yu Zitan, Liang Shuming, Hu Yaobang, Chu Tunan, Zhang Shirang, Zhang Bingxin, Yang Shutang, Liu Shuzhou, Li Xifan, Sun Weiru, Chen Kefei, Wang Jiazhen, Dao Dongting, Peng Guangqin, Fu Baoshi, Lin Sixin, Gu Jiegang, Chen Shiyin, Li Zegang, Xu Shigao, Chen Guoda, Dai Ji, Chen Yangzhi, Ding Xuxian.

    The meeting lasted from 3:00 PM to 6:50 PM.

    The meeting will be adjourned for one day tomorrow. The meeting will continue on the 6th.

    Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference held its sixteenth meeting at 2:00 PM on the 4th. The meeting discussed the list of candidates for additional Standing Committee members and adopted the report of the Proposal Examination Committee of the Second Plenary Session on the examination of proposals.

### 政协全国委员会全体会议继续举行

1956-02-05
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会全体会议继续举行
    新华社4日讯　中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第二次全体会议4日上午继续进行大会讨论。
    在会上发言的有：鲍尔汉、沈雁冰、许广平、陈半丁、荣祥、李聪甫、徐楚波、金宝善、卢汉、廖世承、王玉坤、翁文灏、何贤、胡启立、韩权华、孙越崎、吴大任、张之江、谷春藩、黄育贤、王绶、沈钧儒、陈士骅、李凤荪、阎迦勒。
    会议在上午9时开始，12时半结束。
    新华社4日讯　中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第二次全体会议在4日下午继续进行大会讨论。
    在今天下午会上发言的有：梁希、盛丕华、颜福庆、孙云铸、陆士嘉、唐钺、冀贡泉、鲍国宝、鹿钟麟、于滋潭、梁漱溟、胡耀邦、楚图南、张士琅、张炳鑫、杨树棠、刘述周、李希凡、孙蔚如、陈克非、王家桢、刀栋庭、彭光钦、傅抱石、林斯馨、顾颉刚、陈失因、李则纲、徐士高、陈国达、戴戟、陈仰之、丁绪贤。
    会议从下午3时起到6时50分止。
    明日会议将休会一天。6日，会议继续举行。
    新华社4日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会在4日下午2时举行第十六次会议。会议讨论了增选常务委员的名单，通过了第二次全体会议提案审查委员会关于提案审查的报告。
