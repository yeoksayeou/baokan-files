Title: National Agricultural Development Program (1956-1967) Published
Author:
Date: 1956-02-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 4th. "The National Agricultural Development Program (Draft) for 1956-1967" was published by the People's Publishing House on the 4th and began distribution in Beijing. The first printing of the first edition in Beijing was 1.8 million copies. In addition, a large number of copies will also be printed in 25 cities, including Shenyang, Shanghai, Wuhan, Chongqing, Xi'an, and Urumqi.
    This book also contains news of Chairman Mao Zedong convening the Supreme State Conference to discuss the National Agricultural Development Program (Draft) for 1956-1967 proposed by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Liao Luyan's explanation of this program.

### 1956年—1967年全国农业发展纲要出版

1956-02-05
第1版()
专栏：

    1956年—1967年全国农业发展纲要出版
    新华社4日讯　“1956年到1967年全国农业发展纲要（草案）”4日由人民出版社出版，并且开始在北京发行。这本书在北京的初版第一次印刷了180万册，另外，沈阳、上海、武汉、重庆、西安和乌鲁木齐等25个城市也要大量印行。
    这本书还刊载了毛泽东主席召集最高国务会议讨论中共中央提出的1956年到1967年全国农业发展纲要草案的新闻和廖鲁言关于这一纲要的说明。
