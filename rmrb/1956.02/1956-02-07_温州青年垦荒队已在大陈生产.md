Title: Wenzhou Youth Reclamation Team Already Producing on Dachen

Author:

Date: 1956-02-07
Page: 2

Wenzhou Youth Volunteer Reclamation Team, which came from Zhejiang Province to reclaim land on Dachen Island, has now settled down and begun production. This reclamation team will cultivate approximately 1,000 mu of wasteland this year and will also begin engaging in fishery and animal husbandry production.

The reclamation team arrived at Dachen Island on January 31st and was warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life. On February 1st, youth from all walks of life on the island also held a welcoming rally. Before the reclamation team arrived at Dachen Island, farmers, fishermen, and soldiers on the island had repaired houses for the reclamation team members and prepared brand new plank beds and hoes and other tools. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 温州青年垦荒队已在大陈生产

1956-02-07
第2版()
专栏：

    温州青年垦荒队已在大陈生产
    浙江省到大陈岛垦荒的温州青年志愿垦荒队现在已经定居下来，开始生产。这个垦荒队今年将开垦荒地1，000亩左右，同时开始进行渔业和畜牧业生产。
    这个垦荒队在1月31日到达大陈岛，受到各界人民热烈欢迎。2月1日，岛上各界青年还举行了欢迎大会。在垦荒队到达大陈岛以前，岛上的农民、渔民和战士就为垦荒队队员们修理了房子，准备了崭新的铺板和锄头等工具。（据新华社讯）
