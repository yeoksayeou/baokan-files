Title: Menon Criticizes Baghdad Pact
Author:
Date: 1956-02-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 10th - In a recorded interview broadcast by All India Radio on the evening of February 8th, India's Chief Representative to the United Nations and newly appointed Indian cabinet member, Krishna Menon, vehemently criticized the Baghdad Pact. He stated that the Baghdad Pact was an "unwise move". He hoped that "even at this late stage, more sensible opinions will prevail".

He also said that if Pakistan did not participate in the Baghdad Pact, it would not affect its prosperity and independence in the slightest.

### 梅农指责巴格达条约

1956-02-12
第4版()
专栏：

    梅农指责巴格达条约
    新华社新德里10日电　在全印广播电台2月8日晚上广播的访问录音中，印度驻联合国首席代表、新任命的印度内阁阁员克里希纳·梅农激烈地指责了巴格达条约。他说，巴格达条约是一种“不智之举”。他希望“即使在这么晚的时候，比较明智的意见也还能占优势”。
    他还说，如果巴基斯坦不参加巴格达条约，并不会丝毫影响它的繁荣和独立。
