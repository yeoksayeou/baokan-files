Title: Opening of the First National Congress of Nine-Three Society Members
Author:
Date: 1956-02-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th: The first National Congress of Nine-Three Society Members opened in Beijing on the 9th. The task of this meeting is to summarize past work and determine future work guidelines and tasks, as well as to revise the constitution and elect a new central leadership.

Xu Deheng, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Nine-Three Society, delivered the opening address at the meeting. He said that the primary task of the Nine-Three Society in the face of the new situation of the upsurge of the socialist revolution is, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, to fully mobilize members and senior intellectuals associated with the Nine-Three Society in order to build a socialist society, give full play to their initiative and creativity, strive to improve their political understanding and professional level, and contribute all their strength to the socialist cause of the motherland.

Representatives from the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the United Front Work Department of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and various democratic parties also attended today's meeting.

### 九三学社首届全国社员代表大会开幕

1956-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

    九三学社首届全国社员代表大会开幕
    据新华社10日讯　九三学社第一届全国社员代表大会9日在北京开幕。这次会议的任务是总结过去的工作和确定今后的工作方针和任务，还要修改社章和选举新的中央领导机构。
    九三学社中央委员会主席许德珩在会上致开幕词。他说，九三学社在社会主义革命高潮的新形势面前的首要任务，就是要在中国共产党领导下，为了建成社会主义社会，充分发动社员和九三学社所联系的高级知识分子，发挥高度的积极性和创造性，努力提高政治认识和业务水平，为祖国的社会主义事业，贡献出一切力量。
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会、中国共产党中央统一战线工作部和各民主党派的代表也参加了今天的会议。
