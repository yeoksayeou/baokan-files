Title: Our Army on the Fujian Front Shot Down Two Chiang Planes
Author:
Date: 1956-02-09
Page: 1

    Fujian Front, Xinhua News Agency, February 8th. At 16:03 on the 7th, a batch of four Chiang Kai-shek F-84 fighter planes intruded into the Pingtan area of Fujian Province, diving and strafing fishing boats on the sea east of Wangye Mountain. At 17:18, another batch of four Chiang Kai-shek F-84 fighter planes intruded into the area south of Pingtan and Fuqing County, and two planes were damaged by the People's Liberation Army's anti-aircraft artillery units. One plane was hit in the fuselage, lost its balance, reduced its altitude, and fled southeast of Pingtan, emitting thick smoke. The other was hit in the fuselage and left wing and also fled southeast, emitting thick smoke.

### 福建前线我军击伤蒋机两架

1956-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    福建前线我军击伤蒋机两架
    新华社福建前线8日电　7日16时3分，蒋军F—84型战斗机一批4架窜扰福建省平潭地区，向王爷山以东海面上的渔船俯冲扫射。17时18分，又一批蒋军F—84型战斗机4架窜扰平潭和福清县以南地区，被人民解放军高射炮部队击伤两架。一架被击中机身，失去平衡，降低高度，冒着浓烟向平潭东南方向逃去。另一架被击中机身和左翼，也冒着浓烟向东南方向窜逃。
