Title: Indonesian Navy Delegation Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1956-02-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, February 25th: The Indonesian Navy delegation, led by Indonesian Navy Chief of Staff Admiral Subianto, arrived in Guangzhou this afternoon.

Those who welcomed them at the station included Lieutenant General Liang Xingchu, Lieutenant General Liu Xingyuan, Vice Admiral Zhao Qimin, Air Force Lieutenant General Cao Lihuai, responsible persons from the leadership organs of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Guangzhou Garrison, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou Xue Yan, and Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Guangdong Provincial People's Committee Cao Ruoming, among others.

After the distinguished guests alighted from the train, they warmly shook hands with those who came to welcome them. Members of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy Cultural Troupe presented flowers to Admiral Subianto and his wife.

In the evening, the leadership organs of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Guangzhou Garrison held a banquet at the Yingbin Hotel to welcome the distinguished guests who had traveled from afar.

When the Indonesian Navy delegation arrived in Shenzhen, Colonel Wang Qinwu of the Foreign Affairs Department of the Ministry of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, Colonel Guo Kuanren of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy, Colonel Mao Chen, Military Attache of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in Indonesia, and Colonel Duan Bingshan, representative of the leadership organs of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Guangzhou Garrison, went to greet them and accompanied the distinguished guests to Guangzhou.

### 印度尼西亚海军访华代表团到广州

1956-02-26
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚海军访华代表团到广州
    新华社广州25日讯　由印度尼西亚海军参谋长苏比雅克托将军率领的印度尼西亚海军访华代表团一行6人，今天下午到达广州。
    到车站欢迎的有中国人民解放军广州驻军领导机关负责人梁兴初中将、刘兴元中将、赵启民海军中将、曹里槐空军中将，广州市副市长薛燄和广东省人民委员会外事处处长曹若茗等人。
    贵宾下车后，同欢迎的人亲切握手。中国人民解放军海军文工团团员向苏比雅克托将军和他的夫人献花。
    晚间，中国人民解放军广州驻军领导机关在迎宾馆设宴为远道前来的贵宾们洗尘。
    印度尼西亚海军访华代表团到达深圳的时候，中华人民共和国国防部外事处王秦五上校、中国人民解放军海军郭宽仁上校、中华人民共和国驻印度尼西亚大使馆武官茅琛上校、中国人民解放军广州驻军领导机关代表段秉善上校曾前往迎接，并且陪同贵宾们到广州。
