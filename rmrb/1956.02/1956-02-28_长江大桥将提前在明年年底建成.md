Title: Yangtze River Bridge to be Completed Ahead of Schedule by the End of Next Year
Author:
Date: 1956-02-28
Page: 1

    The Yangtze River Bridge will be completed ahead of schedule by the end of 1957. According to the new plan made by the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge Engineering Bureau on the 21st, the highway bridge portion of the bridge, originally scheduled to be completed in 1958, will be completed at the same time as the railway bridge portion before the end of 1957. All eight mid-river piers of the bridge will be completed by the end of this year, four months ahead of the previous plan. The bridge girder erection project, originally scheduled to begin in September this year, will also begin three months earlier on the Hanyang bank. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 长江大桥将提前在明年年底建成

1956-02-28
第1版()
专栏：

    长江大桥将提前在明年年底建成
    武汉长江大桥将提前在1957年年底全部建成。按照武汉长江大桥工程局21日作出的新计划，原订在1958年建成的大桥公路桥部分，将和铁路桥部分同时在1957年年底前建成。大桥的8个江中桥墩，将在今年年底全部建成，比以前的计划提前4个月。原订今年9月开始的架梁工程，也将提前3个月在汉阳岸开工。　（据新华社讯）
