Title: US-UK Talks Begin in Washington
Author:
Date: 1956-02-02
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, January 31st: British Prime Minister Eden and Foreign Secretary Lloyd arrived in Washington on the 30th and began talks with US President Eisenhower and Secretary of State Dulles.

    According to US news agencies, during the first luncheon meeting held at noon on the 30th, the US and UK sides exchanged extensive views on Western relations with the Soviet Union, the European situation, the German question, the North Atlantic Treaty Organization, and the European "atomic pool" plan.

    It is understood that in the subsequent afternoon meeting, both sides focused on discussing the Middle East issue.

### 美英会谈在华盛顿开始

1956-02-02
第4版()
专栏：

    美英会谈在华盛顿开始
    新华社31日讯　华盛顿消息：英国首相艾登和外交大臣劳埃德30日到达华盛顿，同美国总统艾森豪威尔和国务卿杜勒斯开始会谈。
    据美国通讯社报道，英美双方在30日中午第一次举行的午餐会谈中，曾经就西方对苏联的关系、欧洲局势、德国问题、北大西洋公约组织和欧洲“原子共储”计划等广泛地交换了意见。
    据悉，双方在随后举行的下午会议中，着重讨论了中东问题。
