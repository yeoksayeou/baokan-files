Title: Egyptian Government Trade Representative to China, Deputy Representative Meng Dawei Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-02-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 16th - Meng Dawei, Deputy Representative of the Egyptian Government Trade Representative to China, and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane today. In the evening, they attended a banquet held by Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China.
　　Meng Dawei, Deputy Representative of the Egyptian Government Trade Representative to China, and his wife arrived in Guangzhou from Hong Kong on the 14th. They attended a banquet held by the Guangdong Provincial Foreign Trade Bureau in Guangzhou and visited places of interest.

### 埃及政府驻中国商务代表处副代表孟大维到北京

1956-02-17
第1版()
专栏：

    埃及政府驻中国商务代表处副代表孟大维到北京
    新华社16日讯　埃及政府驻中国商务代表处副代表孟大维及其夫人今天乘飞机到达北京，晚上，出席了中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长雷任民举行的宴会。
    埃及政府驻中国商务代表处副代表孟大维及其夫人是在14日从香港到达广州的。他们在广州出席了广东省对外贸易局举行的宴会，并且参观了名胜古迹。
