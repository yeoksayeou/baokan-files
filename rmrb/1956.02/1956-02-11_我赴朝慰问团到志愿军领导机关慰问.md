Title: Our Delegation to North Korea Visits the Volunteer Army Leadership

Author:
Date: 1956-02-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency report: The head of the Chinese People's Delegation to North Korea, Wang Ziyi, along with the deputy heads and members of the delegation, left Pyongyang on February 10th to visit the headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army.

The headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army was filled with a festive atmosphere. Many intersections along the main roads were adorned with pine and cypress archways decorated with the words "Celebrate the Spring Festival, Welcome Relatives." Lanterns and colorful decorations were hung inside and outside the barracks, and red flags fluttered in the wind. The Volunteer Army headquarters held a grand ceremony at 8 PM to welcome the Chinese People's Delegation to North Korea.

General Yang Yong delivered a welcoming speech on behalf of all officers and soldiers, extending a warm welcome to all comrades of the delegation and expressing heartfelt gratitude and the highest respect to the great people of the motherland and the great leader Chairman Mao. He also reported on the Volunteer Army's ongoing and enthusiastic campaign to donate to the "Volunteer Army Tractor Station."

Head of the delegation, Wang Ziyi, first extended cordial greetings and respect to all officers and soldiers of the Volunteer Army on behalf of the people of the motherland. He said, "Entrusted by the people of the whole country, we have come here to celebrate the Spring Festival with you and to convey the care and comfort of the people of the motherland. We are here to share with you the good news of the achievements of our motherland's socialist construction."

### 我赴朝慰问团到志愿军领导机关慰问

1956-02-11
第4版()
专栏：

    我赴朝慰问团到志愿军领导机关慰问
    据新华社讯　中国人民赴朝慰问团总团长王子宜、各副总团长和团员们在2月10日离开平壤，前往中国人民志愿军领导机关驻地慰问。
    中国人民志愿军领导机关驻地，充满了节日的景象，许多交通要道的路口搭起了“欢度春节，迎接亲人”的松柏彩楼、营房内外张灯结彩，红旗飘扬。志愿军领导机关在下午8时，举行了盛大的欢迎中国人民赴朝慰问团仪式。
    杨勇将军代表全体官兵在会上致欢迎词，他向慰问团全体同志表示热烈的欢迎，向伟大的祖国人民和伟大的领袖毛主席表示衷心的感谢和崇高的敬意。并且报告了志愿军全军正在轰轰烈烈地展开捐献“志愿军拖拉机站”的情况。
    总团长王子宜首先代表祖国人民向志愿军全体官兵，致以亲切的慰问和敬意。他说：“我们这次受全国人民的委托，前来同你们一起欢度春节，前来传达祖国人民对你们的关怀和慰问，我们拿祖国社会主义建设的成就，向你们报喜。”
