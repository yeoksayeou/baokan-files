Title: Yugoslav Ambassador to China Holds Reception to Welcome Yugoslav Youth Football Team
Author:
Date: 1956-02-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popovic, held a reception in Beijing on the afternoon of the 4th to welcome the Yugoslav Youth Football Team's visit to China.

    The entire Yugoslav Youth Football Team, headed by B. Pischich, attended the reception.

    Also invited to attend the reception were Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and Cai Tingkai, Cai Shufan, Huang Qixiang, Lu Han, and Rong Gaotang, Deputy Directors of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission, Chai Zemin, Director of the Beijing Physical Culture and Sports Commission, as well as more than 100 Beijing sports workers and football players.

### 南斯拉夫驻我国大使  举行酒会欢迎南斯拉夫青年足球队

1956-02-05
第1版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫驻我国大使
    举行酒会欢迎南斯拉夫青年足球队
    新华社4日讯　南斯拉夫驻中国大使波波维奇为欢迎南斯拉夫青年足球队来中国访问，4日下午在北京举行酒会。
    以勃·彼希契为首的南斯拉夫青年足球队全体人员出席了酒会。
    应邀出席酒会的还有中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅和体育运动委员会副主任蔡廷锴、蔡树藩、黄琪翔、卢汉、荣高棠，北京市体育运动委员会主任柴泽民，以及北京体育工作者、足球运动员等100多人。
