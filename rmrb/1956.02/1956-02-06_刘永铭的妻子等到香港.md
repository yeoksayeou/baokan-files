Title: Liu Yongming's Wife Arrives in Hong Kong
Author:
Date: 1956-02-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, 5th. Gui Qionghua, the wife of Liu Yongming, a Chinese student who was held up in Hong Kong, along with their daughter Liu Meimei, and his younger brother Liu Yongchang, arrived in Hong Kong this afternoon from Guangzhou, accompanied by Ji Feng, a representative of the Chinese Red Cross.

### 刘永铭的妻子等到香港

1956-02-06
第1版()
专栏：

    刘永铭的妻子等到香港
    新华社香港5日电　被留难在香港的中国留美学生刘永铭的妻子桂琼华、女儿刘美美和他的弟弟刘永錩，在中国红十字会代表纪锋陪同下，今日下午从广州到达香港。
