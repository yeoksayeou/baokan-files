Title: Harahap Announces Resignation After Announcement of Congressional Member List
Author:
Date: 1956-02-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 23rd. The "Social Messenger" reported on the 22nd that Indonesian Prime Minister Harahap told its reporters that he would return the cabinet mandate to the head of state immediately after the list of elected members of parliament is announced. He emphasized that there was absolutely no reason not to return the mandate at that time. He also said, "I have no intention of temporarily not returning the mandate when the new cabinet has not been formed."

Hadi Kusumo, chairman of the Indonesian Central Election Committee, has announced that the list of members of parliament will be officially announced on March 1.

### 哈拉哈普宣布将在国会议员名单公布后辞职

1956-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

    哈拉哈普宣布将在国会议员名单公布后辞职
    新华社雅加达23日电　“社会使者报”22日报道，印度尼西亚总理哈拉哈普对这个报纸的记者说，他将在当选的国会议员的名单公布后立即把内阁委任状退还给国家元首。他强调指出绝对没有理由在那个时候不交还委任状。他还说，“我无意在新内阁没有组成时暂不交还委任状。”
    印度尼西亚中央选举委员会主席哈迪库苏莫已经宣布，国会议员名单将在3月1日正式公布。
