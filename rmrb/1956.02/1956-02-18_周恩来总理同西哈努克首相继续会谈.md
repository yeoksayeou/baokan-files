Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Continues Talks with Prime Minister Sihanouk
Author:
Date: 1956-02-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 17th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China continued talks with His Royal Highness Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, at 3:00 PM on the 17th.

Participating in the talks on the Cambodian side were Ran L?te, Member of the Royal High Council; Chin Ti, Member of the Royal High Council; and Sang Nian, Vice-President of the National Assembly.

Participating in the talks on the Chinese side were Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, and Assistant Minister Chen Jiakang.
