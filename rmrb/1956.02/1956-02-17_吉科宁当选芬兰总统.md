Title: Kekkonen Elected President of Finland
Author:
Date: 1956-02-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, February 16th: The Republic of Finland held presidential elections on the 15th. The current Prime Minister, Kekkonen of the Agrarian League, was elected President of the Republic.

The president is elected by 300 electors. Kekkonen received 151 votes, and Faggholm of the Social Democratic Party received 149 votes.

### 吉科宁当选芬兰总统

1956-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

    吉科宁当选芬兰总统
    新华社16日讯　赫尔辛基消息：芬兰共和国在15日举行总统选举。现任总理、农民联盟的吉科宁当选为共和国总统。
    总统是由300个选举人选出的。吉科宁得到151票，社会民主党的法格霍姆得到149票。
