Title: Outstanding Commercial Worker Movement
Author:
Date: 1956-02-29
Page: 2

    The employees of the Harbin State-Owned No. 7 Department Store proposed launching an "Outstanding Commercial Worker Movement" to commercial workers in Heilongjiang Province in late January. Currently, 45 state-owned and joint state-private commercial units in the province have responded to this initiative, and have put forward goals and specific measures to strive to become outstanding commercial workers and to complete the Five-Year Plan in four years. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 优秀商业工作者运动

1956-02-29
第2版()
专栏：

    优秀商业工作者运动
    哈尔滨市国营第七百货商店职工在1月下旬，向黑龙江省商业工作者提出了开展“优秀商业工作者运动”的倡议。现在，全省有45个国营和公私合营商业单位响应这个倡议，并且提出了争取成为优秀商业工作者、争取四年完成五年计划的目标和具体措施。　　　　（据新华社讯）
