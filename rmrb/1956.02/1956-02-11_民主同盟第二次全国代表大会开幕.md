Title: Democratic League Holds Second National Congress

Author: 
Date: 1956-02-11
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, February 10th – The Second National Congress of the China Democratic League opened in Beijing on the 9th. The task of this meeting is to discuss and determine the direction and tasks of the China Democratic League in the new situation of the high tide of China's socialist revolution, to revise the League's charter, and to re-elect the new central leadership of the China Democratic League.
    Shen Junru, Acting Chairman of the China Democratic League, delivered the opening speech. He said that this congress is being held in the context of our country entering a high tide of socialist revolution. The Democratic League and all its members should give full play to their initiative and creativity, do their best to do their job well, and contribute all their strength to the great cause of socialism; they should strive to study Marxism-Leninism, criticize bourgeois idealism, participate in various revolutionary practices, and further transform themselves through their work; they should give play to the League's active role, further strengthen cooperation within the people's democratic united front, and further improve relations in all aspects; and they should more closely contact the masses and unite all forces that can be united to move forward together.
    The 9th was the first anniversary of the death of Zhang Lan, the former chairman of the China Democratic League, and the attendees stood in silence for two minutes.
    Representatives of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the United Front Work Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and various democratic parties also attended the meeting.

### 民主同盟第二次全国代表大会开幕

1956-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

    民主同盟第二次全国代表大会开幕
    据新华社10日讯　中国民主同盟第二次全国代表大会9日在北京开幕。这次会议的任务是讨论和确定中国民主同盟在中国社会主义革命高潮新形势下的方针和任务，修改盟章和改选中国民主同盟的新的中央领导机构。
    中国民主同盟代理主席沈钧儒致开幕词。他说，这次大会是在我国进入社会主义革命高潮的形势下召开的。民主同盟和全盟同志要发挥高度的积极性和创造性，尽心竭力作好自己的岗位工作，为伟大的社会主义事业贡献一切力量；要努力学习马克思列宁主义，批判资产阶级唯心主义思想，参加各种革命实践，通过业务、通过工作，更进一步地改造自己；要发挥盟的积极作用，更进一步加强人民民主统一战线内部的合作，更加改进各方面的关系；要更密切地联系群众，团结一切可以团结的力量共同前进。
    9日是中国民主同盟前主席张澜逝世一周年纪念日，到会人员起立默哀两分钟。
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会、中国共产党中央统一战线工作部和各民主党派的代表也参加了会议。
