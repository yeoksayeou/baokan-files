Title: Chinese Student Delegation Returning from Australia Passes Through Jakarta

Author:

Date: 1956-02-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 12th: The Chinese student delegation, Qian Dawei and two others, arrived in Jakarta on the 9th on their way back to China after a three-week visit to Australia.

They were invited to attend the 20th Council Meeting of the National Union of Australian University Students. During their stay in Australia, they visited Sydney, Melbourne, and Adelaide, and met with many public figures in these places.

### 我国访问澳大利亚学生代表团经过雅加达回国

1956-02-14
第4版()
专栏：

    我国访问澳大利亚学生代表团经过雅加达回国
    据新华社雅加达12日电　中国学生代表团钱大卫等3人，在澳大利亚进行了3个星期的访问以后，9日在回国途中到达雅加达。
    他们是应邀前往参加澳大利亚全国大学生联合会第二十届理事会的。在澳大利亚期间，他们访问了悉尼、墨尔本和阿得雷德等地方，会晤了这些地方的许多公众领袖。
