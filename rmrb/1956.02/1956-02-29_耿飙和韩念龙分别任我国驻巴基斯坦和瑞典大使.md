Title: Geng Biao and Han Nianlong Appointed as China's Ambassadors to Pakistan and Sweden Respectively
Author:
Date: 1956-02-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 28th: Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, has appointed Geng Biao as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Pakistan.

Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, has removed Han Nianlong from his post as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Pakistan.

Xinhua News Agency, February 28th: Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, has appointed Han Nianlong as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Sweden.

Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, has removed Geng Biao from his post as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Sweden.

### 耿飙和韩念龙分别任我国驻巴基斯坦和瑞典大使

1956-02-29
第1版()
专栏：

    耿飙和韩念龙分别任我国驻巴基斯坦和瑞典大使
    新华社28日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东任命耿飙为中华人民共和国驻巴基斯坦特命全权大使。
    中华人民共和国主席毛泽东免去韩念龙的中华人民共和国驻巴基斯坦特命全权大使的职务。
    新华社28日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东任命韩念龙为中华人民共和国驻瑞典王国特命全权大使。
    中华人民共和国主席毛泽东免去耿飙的中华人民共和国驻瑞典王国特命全权大使的职务。
