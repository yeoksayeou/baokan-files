    The signing ceremony of the Declaration of the Warsaw Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation and Mutual Assistance was held in the Vældštejn Palace in Prague, the capital of Czechoslovakia, on January 28. The left picture shows Soviet representative Molotov (seated, first from the left) signing the declaration, with Soviet representative Marshal Zhukov seated to his right. The right picture shows Marshal Nie Rongzhen, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council of the People's Republic of China, delivering a statement on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China at the signing ceremony.

    ČTK (Xinhua News Agency)
