Title: Our Delegation to North Korea for Condolences Departs
Author:
Date: 1956-02-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Chinese People's Delegation for Condolences to Korea departed from Beijing this afternoon for Korea. The delegation will represent the Chinese people in extending condolences to the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers and will participate in the celebration of the founding of the Korean People's Army.

More than 300 people, including Chen Shutong, Xie Juezai, Chen Qiyuan, Wang Yifu, Zhang Weizhen, Liu Xiyuan, Zeng Xianzhi, Liu Qiren, and Cheng Hongyi, were at the station to see off the delegation.

Choi Il, the Ambassador of North Korea to China, and embassy staff also went to the station to see them off.

The head of the Chinese People's Delegation for Condolences to Korea is Wang Ziyi, and the deputy heads are Cai Shubin, Le Songsheng, Wei Xiuying, Qian Jiaju, Luo Feng, and Zhang Zizhen. The delegation has a total of 125 representatives, including representatives of various people's organizations, model workers in industry and agriculture, advanced workers, activists in socialist construction and socialist transformation, model military dependents and families of martyrs, model demobilized soldiers, cultural and educational workers, and literary and art workers.

Accompanying the delegation for condolence performances are 13 art troupes, including song and dance troupes, drama troupes, quyi troupes, and acrobatics troupes.

### 我赴朝慰问团启程

1956-02-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我赴朝慰问团启程
    新华社4日讯　中国人民赴朝慰问团今天下午由北京启程去朝鲜。慰问团将代表中国人民慰问朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军，并将参加庆祝朝鲜人民军建军节。
    到车站欢送慰问团的有陈叔通、谢觉哉、陈其瑗、王一夫、张维桢、刘西元、曾宪植、刘其人、程宏毅等共300多人。
    朝鲜驻中国大使崔一和使馆人员，也到车站欢送。
    中国人民赴朝慰问团团长为王子宜，副团长为蔡书彬、乐松生、危秀英、千家驹、洛风、张梓桢。慰问团共有代表125人，其中包括有各人民团体代表和工农业劳动模范、先进工作者、社会主义建设和社会主义改造的积极分子、模范军属烈属、模范复员军人、文化教育工作者和文学艺术工作者。
    随这个慰问团进行慰问演出的有歌舞团、剧团、曲艺团、杂技团等13个艺术团体。
