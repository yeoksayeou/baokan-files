Author:
Date: 1956-02-10
Page: 1

    China Association for Promoting Democracy Holds Meeting to Discuss the Issue of Giving Play to the Strength of Intellectuals

    (Our newspaper's report) The China Association for Promoting Democracy (CAPD) began a meeting of its Central Standing Committee in Beijing on February 9. Participating in the meeting were the Central Standing Committee members of the CAPD, Central Committee members in Beijing, heads of various branches and preparatory committees, CAPD members attending and observing the Second Session of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), and some members with representative status in cultural and educational circles, totaling more than 90 people. The main content of the meeting is to discuss the documents of the Second Session of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC and how to implement the spirit of the meeting, assist the Party and the government in strengthening the leadership of intellectuals, promote unity, help members and the intellectuals they contact strengthen self-reform, and fully leverage their potential to serve socialist construction. In the morning, Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi attended the meeting and gave a report. In the afternoon, Vice Chairman of the CAPD Zhou Jianren spoke at the meeting.

### 中国民主促进会开会讨论发挥知识分子力量问题

1956-02-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中国民主促进会开会讨论发挥知识分子力量问题
    本报讯　中国民主促进会从2月9日起在北京召开中央常务理事会扩大会议。参加这次会议的有中国民主促进会中央常务理事、在京中央理事，各分会和筹备委员会负责人，出席和列席中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第二次全体会议的中国民主促进会会员和一部分在文化教育界有代表性的会员，共90多人。会议的主要内容是讨论中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第二次全体会议的文件和如何贯彻会议的精神，协助党和政府加强对知识分子的领导，搞好团结，帮助会员和所联系的知识分子加强自我改造，充分发挥潜在力量，为社会主义建设服务。上午，国务院副总理陈毅到会作了报告。下午，中国民主促进会副主席周建人在会上讲了话。
