Title: China Red Cross Donates to Help Yugoslav Snow Disaster Victims
Author:
Date: 1956-02-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 24th. Recently, avalanches and floods occurred in the Macedonian Republic and the Serbian Republic of Yugoslavia, causing disasters to many people and killing dozens. On February 23, the Red Cross Society of China sent a telegram to the Red Cross Society of Yugoslavia, expressing condolences to the families of the deceased and sympathy to the other disaster-stricken people. At the same time, it donated 30,000 yuan Renminbi to the Red Cross Society of Yugoslavia to help the disaster-stricken people.

### 中国红十字会  捐款帮助南斯拉夫雪灾灾民

1956-02-25
第1版()
专栏：

    中国红十字会
    捐款帮助南斯拉夫雪灾灾民
    新华社24日讯　南斯拉夫马其顿共和国和塞尔维亚共和国最近发生雪崩和水灾，有许多人受到灾害，数十人死亡。中国红十字会在23日打电报给南斯拉夫红十字会，对死难者家属表示哀悼，对其他受灾人民表示慰问，同时捐给南斯拉夫红十字会人民币3万元，以帮助受灾的人民。
