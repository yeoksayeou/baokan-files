Title: Sawatzki Holds Reception to Welcome Vice-Chairman Zhu De
Author:
Date: 1956-02-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, January 31st: The Chairman of the Polish State Council, Aleksander Zawadzki, held a grand reception at 8 PM on January 30th to welcome the Vice-Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Zhu De.

Among those present at the reception were the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Bolesław Bierut, and the Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Józef Cyrankiewicz, as well as other leaders of the Party and government, including Berman, Jóźwiak-Witold, Nowak, Ochab, Rokossowski, Zambrowski, and others. Accompanying Vice-Chairman Zhu De were the Vice-Chairman of the National Defense Council of the People's Republic of China, Marshal Nie Rongzhen, and member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Liu Lantao.

The Chinese Ambassador to Poland, Wang Bingnan, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Poland, also attended the reception.

As Zawadzki and Zhu De entered the reception hall, the more than 400 guests present greeted them with warm applause.

Zawadzki delivered a speech at the reception, saying that the friendship between the Polish people and the great Chinese people is developing and deepening day by day. He expressed his belief that Marshal Zhu De's visit will further strengthen cooperation and friendship between the two countries.

In his speech, Zhu De first paid tribute to the Polish people on behalf of the Chinese people. He said that in the past few years, China and Poland have carried out friendly cooperation and sincere mutual assistance in many aspects. In these collaborations, the Chinese people have received help and support from the Polish people. The close mutual assistance and cooperation between our two fraternal countries in the great family of the peace, democracy, and socialism camp headed by the Soviet Union is based on great internationalism and is eternal and unbreakable. Striving to develop this mutual assistance and cooperation will promote the early completion of socialist construction in our two countries and will help safeguard the cause of world peace.

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, January 31st: Zhu De, Vice-Chairman of the People's Republic of China and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, paid a visit to Bolesław Bierut, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, at 1 PM on January 30th.

Accompanying him on the visit were: Nie Rongzhen, Vice-Chairman of the National Defense Council of the People's Republic of China and member of the CPC Central Committee, Liu Lantao, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and alternate member of the CPC Central Committee, and Wang Bingnan, Chinese Ambassador to Poland.

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, January 31st: Zhu De, Vice-Chairman of the People's Republic of China, successively visited Aleksander Zawadzki, Chairman of the Polish State Council, and Józef Cyrankiewicz, Chairman of the Polish Council of Ministers, at noon and 1 PM on January 30th.

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, January 31st: Zhu De, Vice-Chairman of the People's Republic of China, visited the heroic city of Warsaw, which has been rebuilt from ruins, on the afternoon of the 30th.

Vice-Chairman Zhu De highly praised the architectural style of Warsaw's Old Town and Stalin Square.

Vice-Chairman Zhu De also visited the precious gift given to the Polish people by the Soviet Union—the Warsaw Palace of Culture and Science named after Stalin. Vice-Chairman Zhu De wrote in the Palace of Culture and Science's commemorative book: "Great Architecture, Profound Friendship."

### 萨瓦茨基举行招待会欢迎朱德副主席

1956-02-02
第4版()
专栏：

    萨瓦茨基举行招待会欢迎朱德副主席
    新华社华沙31日电　波兰国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基在1月30日晚8时举行盛大招待会，欢迎中华人民共和国副主席朱德。
    出席招待会的有波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记贝鲁特和部长会议主席西伦凯维兹以及党和政府其他领导人贝尔曼、约兹维亚克—韦托德、诺瓦克、奥哈布、罗科索夫斯基、萨姆布罗夫斯基等。陪同朱德副主席出席招待会的有中华人民共和国国防委员会副主席聂荣臻元帅和全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员刘澜涛。
    中国驻波兰大使王炳南以及各国的驻波外交使节也出席了招待会。
    萨瓦茨基和朱德走进招待会大厅时，到会的400多位来宾热烈鼓掌欢迎。
    萨瓦茨基在会上致词说，波兰人民和伟大中国人民之间的友谊在日益发展和加深。他相信朱德元帅的访问将进一步加强中波两国之间的合作和友谊。
    朱德在致词中首先代表中国人民向波兰人民致敬。他说，在过去几年中，中波两国实行了多方面的友好合作和真诚的互助。在这些合作中，中国人民得到了波兰人民的帮助和支持。我们两个兄弟国家在以苏联为首的和平民主和社会主义阵营大家庭中的亲密的互助合作关系是以伟大的国际主义为基础的，是永恒的、牢不可破的。努力发展这种互助合作关系，将促进我们两国社会主义建设的早日完成，并将有助于维护世界和平的事业。
    宴会在热烈、友好的气氛中进行。
    新华社华沙31日电　中华人民共和国副主席、中国共产党中央委员会书记处书记朱德，在1月30日下午1时拜会了波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记贝鲁特。
    陪同前去拜会的有：中华人民共和国国防委员会副主席、中共中央委员聂荣臻、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员、中共中央候补委员刘澜涛和中国驻波兰大使王炳南。
    据新华社华沙31日电　中华人民共和国副主席朱德，1月30日中午和下午1时先后拜会了波兰国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基和波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维茨。
    新华社华沙31日电　中华人民共和国副主席朱德30日下午参观了在废墟上重建起来的英雄的华沙市的市容。
    朱德副主席对华沙古城区的建筑风格和斯大林广场十分称赞。
    朱德副主席还参观了苏联送给波兰人民的宝贵礼物——华沙斯大林文化科学宫。朱德副主席在斯大林文化科学宫的纪念簿上题了字：“伟大的建筑、深厚的友谊”。
