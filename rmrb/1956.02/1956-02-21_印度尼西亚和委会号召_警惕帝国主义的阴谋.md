Title: Indonesian Peace Committee Calls for Vigilance Against Imperialist Conspiracies
Author:
Date: 1956-02-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 20th - The Indonesian National Peace Committee issued a statement on February 19th, calling on the Indonesian people to heighten their vigilance and strengthen the struggle against conspiracies attempting to pull Indonesia into the Manila Treaty Organization.

The statement pointed out that the Manila Treaty Organization held military exercises last week in a region not far from Indonesia, and that Indonesia is geographically surrounded by countries of the Manila Treaty Organization.

### 印度尼西亚和委会号召  警惕帝国主义的阴谋

1956-02-21
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚和委会号召
    警惕帝国主义的阴谋
    新华社雅加达20日电　印度尼西亚全国和平委员会2月19日发表声明，号召印度尼西亚人民提高警惕，并且加强反对企图把印度尼西亚拉进马尼拉条约组织的阴谋的斗争。
    声明指出，马尼拉条约组织上星期就在离印度尼西亚不远的地区举行了军事演习，并且印度尼西亚在地理上受到马尼拉条约组织国家的包围。
