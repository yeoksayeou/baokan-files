Title: Deng Tuo, President of the All-China Journalists Association, Hosts Banquet to See Off Yugoslav Journalists Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1956-02-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 12th. Deng Tuo, President of the All-China Journalists Association, held a banquet on the evening of February 12th to bid farewell to the head of the Yugoslav journalists delegation, Pirpic, and all members of the delegation, who are about to return home.

Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popovic, was also invited to attend the banquet.

Those present included Wu Lengxi and Wang Yunsheng, Vice Presidents of the All-China Journalists Association, Xu Huang, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, heads of various Beijing newspapers and periodicals, and members of the Chinese journalists delegation who had visited Yugoslavia, totaling more than 50 people.

Deng Tuo said in his speech at the banquet that the mutual visits of journalists delegations from China and Yugoslavia undoubtedly have a significant role in enhancing the friendship between the people of China and Yugoslavia. The unbreakable brotherly friendship between Chinese and Yugoslav journalists is higher than the mountains and deeper than the sea. Deng Tuo concluded by wishing the friendship between Chinese and Yugoslav journalists and the people of the two countries would be further consolidated and developed.

Pirpic then delivered a speech, saying that the Yugoslav journalists delegation learned about many issues and the great achievements made by the Chinese people during their visits. We felt the deep enthusiasm of the Chinese people and Chinese comrades for the Yugoslav people and Yugoslav journalists. Pirpic said that the ties and cooperation between China and Yugoslavia are at a new stage of greater consolidation and development, and he firmly believes that this delegation's visit to China will contribute to the further development of ties between China and Yugoslavia.

Yugoslav Ambassador to China Popovic also spoke at the banquet.

After the banquet, the guests exchanged gifts.

### 中国新闻工作者联谊会会长邓拓  设宴欢送南斯拉夫新闻工作者访华代表团

1956-02-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中国新闻工作者联谊会会长邓拓
    设宴欢送南斯拉夫新闻工作者访华代表团
    据新华社12日讯　中国新闻工作者联谊会会长邓拓在12日晚举行宴会，欢送即将返国的南斯拉夫新闻工作者访华代表团团长皮尔皮奇和全体团员。
    南斯拉夫驻华大使波波维奇也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席作陪的，有中国新闻工作者联谊会副会长吴冷西、王芸生，外交部新闻司副司长徐晃，北京各报刊的负责人和曾经访问南斯拉夫的中国新闻工作者代表团人员等50多人。
    邓拓在宴会上致词说，中南两国新闻工作者代表团的相互访问，对于增进中南两国人民之间的友谊无疑有着重大的作用。中南两国新闻工作者的牢不可破的兄弟友谊比高山还高，比大海还深。邓拓最后祝中南两国新闻工作者和两国人民的友谊更加巩固和发展。
    皮尔皮奇接着致词说，南斯拉夫新闻工作者代表团在参观和访问中了解了许多问题和中国人民所取得的巨大成就。我们感到了中国人民和中国同志对南斯拉夫人民、对南斯拉夫新闻工作者的深厚的热情。皮尔皮奇说，中南两国的联系和合作处在更加巩固和发展的新阶段，他深信这次代表团的访问中国，将会对进一步发展中南两国的联系作出贡献。
    南斯拉夫驻华大使波波维奇也在宴会上讲了话。
    在宴会以后，宾主互相赠送了礼品。
