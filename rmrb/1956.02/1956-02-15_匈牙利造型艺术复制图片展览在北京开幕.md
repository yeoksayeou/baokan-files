Title: Hungarian Fine Arts Reproduction Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-02-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 11th. The "Hungarian Fine Arts Reproduction Exhibition," sponsored by the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, opened at the waterside pavilion of Zhongshan Park in Beijing on the afternoon of the 11th. The exhibition features 84 art reproductions (pictures) by 64 Hungarian artists, including works by famous Hungarian painters. The exhibition will be on display in Beijing until March 2nd.

### 匈牙利造型艺术复制图片展览在北京开幕

1956-02-15
第1版()
专栏：

    匈牙利造型艺术复制图片展览在北京开幕
    新华社11日讯　对外文化联络局主办的“匈牙利造型艺术复制图片展览”于11日下午在北京中山公园水榭开始展出。这次展出了匈牙利64位艺术家的84幅艺术复制品（图片）。其中有著名的匈牙利画家的作品。展览会将在北京展出到3月2日。
