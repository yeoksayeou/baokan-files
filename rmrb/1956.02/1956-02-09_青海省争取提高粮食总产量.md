Title: Qinghai Province Strives to Increase Total Grain Output
Author:
Date: 1956-02-09
Page: 1

(According to Xinhua News Agency)

    The Qinghai Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China convened its first representative meeting of agricultural producers' cooperatives from January 28th to February 4th. At the meeting, more than 600 representatives of various ethnic groups unanimously passed a resolution to launch a large-scale, high-yield competition movement throughout the province. The resolution called for increasing the per-mu yield of grain crops on irrigated land from the existing 450-500 jin to 1,000 jin, increasing the yield on high-altitude land from 300 jin to 500 jin per mu, and increasing the yield on low-altitude and dry land from 180 jin to 300 jin per mu, so that the total grain output of the province would reach 1.37 billion jin, an increase of 23.2% over last year.
    The representatives attending the meeting, after listening to and discussing the transmitted report on the National Agricultural Development Program (Draft) and the report on Qinghai Province's agricultural production increase tasks for 1956, expressed their confidence in exceeding this year's production increase tasks.

### 青海省争取提高粮食总产量

1956-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    青海省争取提高粮食总产量
    中共青海省委员会在1月28日到2月4日召开了第一次农业生产合作社代表会议。会上，600多名各族代表一致通过决议，决定在全省开展大面积高额丰产竞赛运动。决议要求争取水浇地粮食作物每亩产量由450斤到500斤的基础上提高到1，000斤，高山地由每亩300斤提高到500斤，低山和平旱地每亩由180斤提高到300斤，使全省粮食总产量达到137，000万斤，比去年增加23.2％。
    出席会议的代表们，在听取和讨论了关于全国农业发展纲要（草案）的传达报告和青海省1956年农业增产任务的报告后，都充满信心地表示，要超额完成今年的增产任务。
                  （据新华社讯）
