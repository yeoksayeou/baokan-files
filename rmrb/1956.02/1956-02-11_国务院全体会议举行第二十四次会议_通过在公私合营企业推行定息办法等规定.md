Title: State Council Plenary Session Held the 24th Meeting; Approved Regulations on Implementing Fixed Interest Rates in Joint State-Private Enterprises, etc.
Author:
Date: 1956-02-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th dispatch: The 24th plenary session of the State Council was held on the afternoon of the 8th.

    The meeting discussed and approved the "State Council Regulations on Implementing Fixed Interest Rate Methods in Joint State-Private Enterprises" and the "State Council Regulations on Several Major Issues Concerning Property Inventory and Valuation When Private Enterprises Implement Joint State-Private Ownership." Before the discussion, Xu Dixin, Deputy Director of the Eighth Office of the State Council, made an explanation. The explanation pointed out that for investments made by overseas Chinese who remitted funds to domestic investment companies operated by the state after liberation, interest would still be paid at an annual rate of 8%.

    After listening to the explanation by Vice Premier Chen Yun, the meeting approved the "State Council Decision on Several Matters Concerning the Socialist Transformation of Private Industry and Commerce and Handicraft Industry." The meeting also approved several appointments and removals.

### 国务院全体会议举行第二十四次会议  通过在公私合营企业推行定息办法等规定

1956-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院全体会议举行第二十四次会议
    通过在公私合营企业推行定息办法等规定
    新华社10日讯　国务院全体会议第二十四次会议在8日下午举行。
    会议讨论并且通过了“国务院关于在公私合营企业中推行定息办法的规定”和“国务院关于私营企业实行公私合营的时候对财产清理估价几项主要问题的规定”。在讨论以前，由国务院第八办公室副主任许涤新作了说明。说明中指出，对华侨在解放以后汇款到国内投入国家经营的投资公司的投资，仍按照原来规定按年息8厘付息。
    会议在听了陈云副总理的说明以后，通过了“国务院关于目前私营工商业和手工业的社会主义改造中若干事项的决定”。会议还通过了任免事项多起。
