Title: At the Invitation of Our Government, Cambodian Prime Minister Sihanouk Will Visit Our Country
Author: 
Date: 1956-02-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 11th. At the invitation of the Chinese government, the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, Prince Norodom Sihanouk, will pay a friendly visit to China in mid-February.

### 应我国政府邀请  柬埔寨首相西哈努克将访问我国

1956-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

    应我国政府邀请
    柬埔寨首相西哈努克将访问我国
    新华社11日电　柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王应中华人民共和国政府邀请将于2月中旬来中国进行友好访问。
