Title: The United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee Holds Reception for Democratic Parties and Non-Party Democratic Personages
Author:
Date: 1956-02-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 13th - The United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee held a grand reception this afternoon to entertain personages from various democratic parties and non-party democratic personages.

Chairman Mao Zedong, and Secretaries of the Secretariat Liu Shaoqi and Zhou Enlai, Politburo member Peng Zhen, and Central Committee member Chen Yi of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China attended today's reception.

Those invited to attend the reception included Li Jishen, Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang; Shen Junru, Acting Chairman of the China Democratic League; Huang Yanpei, Chairman of the China Democratic National Construction Association; Ma Yinchu, a non-party democratic personage; Zhou Jianren, Vice Chairman of the China Association for Promoting Democracy; Zhang Bojun, Chairman of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party; Chen Qiyou, Chairman of the China Zhi Gong Party; Xu Deheng, Chairman of the Jiusan Society; Xie Xuehong, Chairman of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League; and Hu Yaobang, Secretary of the Central Secretariat of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League.

Also invited were Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and Cheng Qian and Fu Zuoyi, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council.

Also invited were vice chairmen, vice chairmen, central committee members, alternate central committee members, and heads of various departments of the headquarters of the democratic parties, as well as representatives attending and attending the Third National Congress of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, the Second National Congress of the China Democratic League, and the China Promotion Association. The expanded meeting of the Central Standing Council of Democracy, the First National Member Congress of the Jiusan Society, and the Second Plenary (Expanded) Meeting of the Sixth Central Committee of the Chinese Peasants’ and Workers’ Democratic Party.

Attending on behalf of the host were Li Weihan, Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, and Vice Ministers Xing Xiping, Liao Chengzhi, Liu Geping, Ping Jiesan, Wang Feng, Zhang Zhiyi, Yu Yifu, and Xu Dixin, and the persons in charge of the United Front Work Departments of the CPC Provincial and Municipal Committees in Beijing.

More than 1,000 people attended today's reception.

Chairman Mao Zedong raised his glass at the meeting and congratulated, saying: "I wish you all good health and smooth work."

At the meeting, Zhou Enlai, Li Weihan, and Li Jishen also raised their glasses to congratulate. They wished that the United Front work would be further consolidated, strengthened and developed in the high tide of socialist transformation and socialist construction.

The reception was filled with an atmosphere of unity, joy and enthusiasm. During the meeting, the hosts and guests frequently stood up and raised their glasses to each other. (Attached picture)

### 中共中央统战部举行酒会  招待民主党派和无党派民主人士

1956-02-14
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央统战部举行酒会
    招待民主党派和无党派民主人士
    新华社13日讯　中共中央统一战线工作部在13日下午举行盛大酒会，招待各民主党派人士和无党派民主人士。
    中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东和书记处书记刘少奇、周恩来，政治局委员彭真，中央委员陈毅出席了今天的酒会。
    应邀出席酒会的，有中国国民党革命委员会主席李济深，中国民主同盟代理主席沈钧儒，中国民主建国会主任委员黄炎培，无党派民主人士马寅初，中国民主促进会副主席周建人，中国农工民主党主席章伯钧，中国致公党主席陈其尤，九三学社主席许德珩，台湾民主自治同盟主席谢雪红，中国新民主主义青年团中央书记处书记胡耀邦。
    应邀出席的，还有中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈叔通，国防委员会副主席程潜、傅作义。
    应邀出席的，还有各民主党派副主席、副主任委员、中央委员、候补中央委员及其总部各部门负责人，出席和列席中国国民党革命委员会第三届全国代表大会、中国民主同盟第二次全国代表大会、中国民主促进会中央常务理事会扩大会议、九三学社第一届全国社员代表大会、中国农工民主党第六届中央委员会第二次全体会议（扩大）的代表。
    主人方面出席的，有中共中央统一战线工作部部长李维汉和副部长邢西萍、廖承志、刘格平、平杰三、汪锋、张执一、于毅夫、许涤新，中国共产党各省、市委统一战线工作部在京的负责人。
    出席今天酒会的共有一千多人。
    毛泽东主席在会上举杯祝贺说：“祝大家身体健康，工作顺利。”
    在会上，周恩来、李维汉、李济深也都举杯祝贺。他们祝统一战线工作在社会主义改造和社会主义建设高潮中更加巩固、加强和发展。
    酒会充满了团结、欢乐和热烈的空气。席间，宾主频频起立，举杯互敬。（附图片）
