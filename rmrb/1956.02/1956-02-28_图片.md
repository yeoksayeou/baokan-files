    The Song Changsheng Agricultural Production Cooperative in Yanggu County, Shandong Province, raises an average of one pig per person and has created many methods for managing, raising, and expanding feed sources. This cooperative raises many pigs, accumulates a lot of fertilizer, and grain production also increases accordingly. Photo by Zheng Hua (Manuscript from Dazhong Daily)
