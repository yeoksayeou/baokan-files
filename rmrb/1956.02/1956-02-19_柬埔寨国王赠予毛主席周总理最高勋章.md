Title: Cambodian King Presents Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou with Highest Medal
Author:
Date: 1956-02-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 18th

    Cambodian King Presents Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou with Highest Medal

    Xinhua News Agency, February 18th - Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China and Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council received the highest medal of the Kingdom of Cambodia, the Grand Cross Medal, presented by King Norodom Suramarit of the Kingdom of Cambodia this afternoon.

    The medal presentation ceremony was held solemnly at the Throne Hall at 7 p.m. today.

    At the ceremony, Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, first presented Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai with the certificate of conferment from King Norodom Suramarit. Then, he personally bestowed the highest medal of the Kingdom of Cambodia, the Grand Cross Medal, upon Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai.

    After receiving the medals, Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai warmly shook hands with Prince Norodom Sihanouk to express their gratitude.

    Members of the Cambodian delegation who attended the ceremony included: Ran Laiet, Member of the Supreme Royal Council; Qin Di, Member of the Supreme Royal Council; Sang Nian, Vice-President of the Council of the Kingdom; Enge Mias, Member of the National Assembly; Dr. Neil Smouk, Member of the Council of the Kingdom; Colonel Nu Hu, Chief of Staff of the Royal Air Force; Ron Sophon, Deputy Secretary of State for Farmer Welfare; and Phong Salen, Private Secretary to the Prince.

    Chinese attendees at the ceremony included Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Vice-Chairmen Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Li Weihan, and Chen Shutong; Vice-Premiers of the State Council Peng Dehuai, Chen Yi, and Li Fuchun; Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian; heads of various departments of state organs; Vice-Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; and senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. ### 柬埔寨国王赠予毛主席周总理最高勋章

1956-02-19
第1版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨国王赠予毛主席周总理最高勋章
    新华社18日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东和国务院总理周恩来，今天下午接受了柬埔寨王国国王诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特赠予的柬埔寨王国最高勋章——大十字勋章。
    接受勋章典礼是今日下午7时在勤政殿隆重举行的。
    在典礼上，柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王首先向毛泽东主席和周恩来总理转交了诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特国王的赠勋证书，接着，又分别为毛泽东主席和周恩来总理佩戴上柬埔寨王国最高勋章——大十字勋章。
    毛泽东主席和周恩来总理接受勋章以后，和诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王亲切握手，表示感谢。
    参加典礼的，有柬埔寨王国国家代表团团员：皇廷最高会议委员兰·?特，皇廷最高会议委员钦·迪，王国会议副议长桑·年，国民会议议员恩格·米阿斯，王国会议议员尼尔·斯莫乌克博士，皇家空军参谋长努·呼上校，农民福利副国务秘书罗恩·索方，亲王私人秘书丰·萨伦。
    中国方面参加典礼的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真、李维汉、陈叔通，国务院副总理彭德怀、陈毅、李富春、外交部副部长张闻天，以及国家机关各部门负责人，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席，中国人民解放军高级将领。（附图片）
