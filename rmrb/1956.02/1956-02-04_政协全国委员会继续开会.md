Title: CPPCC National Committee Continues Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1956-02-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The Second Plenary Session of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference continued its meeting on the afternoon of the 3rd.

    Today, the following people spoke at the meeting: Li Siguang, Fu Zuoyi, Long Donghua, Xue Dubi, Ling Qijun, Guo Linshuang, Liu Tong, Zhou Jianren, Hua Luogeng, Wei Lihuang, Fang Dingying, Hou Zonglian, Liu Wenhui, Zhao Hongzhang, Yang Jianqing, Wu Peiheng, Wang Deyi, He Siyuan, Tao Shuzeng, Liu Qiong, Qi Jianru.
    Before the adjournment, Chairman Zhou Enlai announced the death of Yang Weiyu, member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Light Industry of the People's Republic of China, and everyone stood up to express their condolences.
    Today's meeting lasted from 3:00 PM to 7:00 PM.
    Group discussions continued this morning.
    The meeting will continue on the 4th.
    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The 15th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference was held at 1:00 PM on the 3rd.
    At the meeting, Zhang Naiqi explained the report on the examination of proposals by the Proposals Examination Committee of the Second Session of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. The meeting discussed the report.

### 政协全国委员会继续开会

1956-02-04
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会继续开会
    新华社3日讯　　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会第二次全体会议3日下午继续举行。
    今天在会上发言的有：李四光、傅作义、龙冬花、薛笃弼、凌其峻、郭琳爽、刘通、周建人、华罗庚、卫立煌、方鼎英、侯宗濂、刘文辉、赵洪璋、杨鉴清、吴佩衡、王德舆、何思源、陶述曾、刘琼、齐坚如。
    会议休会前，周恩来主席宣布中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会委员、中华人民共和国轻工业部副部长杨卫玉逝世，全体起立致哀。
    今天会议从下午3时进行到7时。
    会议分组讨论在今天上午继续进行。
    4日将继续举行会议。
    新华社3日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会第十五次会议，在3日下午1时举行。
    会议上，章乃器说明了中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第二次全体会议提案审查委员会关于提案的审查报告。会议对报告进行了讨论。
