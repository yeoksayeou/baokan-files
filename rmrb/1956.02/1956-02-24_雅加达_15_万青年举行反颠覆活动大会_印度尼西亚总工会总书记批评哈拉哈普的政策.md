Title: Jakarta: 150,000 Youths Hold Anti-Subversion Rally; Indonesian Trade Union Congress Secretary-General Criticizes Harahap's Policies
Author:
Date: 1956-02-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 23rd - In Jakarta, the capital of Indonesia, 150,000 youths and students held a mass rally in Independence Square on the afternoon of February 22nd to oppose subversive activities.

Speaking at the rally were representatives of youth, students, and former armed fighters, as well as Manoppo, one of the witnesses in the case of Dutch subversives Rongslag and Schmidt. They all condemned the criminal activities of the Dutch subversives and demanded that resolute action be taken against those who undermine peace.

The resolution passed by the rally demanded: the immediate expulsion of the Dutch lawyer Van Enbel, who was allowed into Indonesia by the Harahap government; the declaration of the current Dutch High Commissioner to Indonesia as persona non grata; and the taking of strict action against those so-called witnesses from the Netherlands and the heads of some Dutch companies involved in the subversion case. The resolution also demanded that the Dutch subversives Rongslag and Schmidt be sentenced to death and that the firm attitude taken by Judge Mankom, who is hearing the case, be supported.

According to newspaper reports, similar mass rallies against subversive activities were also held in Bandung and Semarang on February 22nd and 21st, respectively.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 23rd - The National Committee meeting of the Indonesian Trade Union Congress opened in Jakarta on February 21st. The meeting will last for five days.

Yono, Secretary-General of the Indonesian Trade Union Congress, delivered an opening speech at the meeting. He gave a general account of the recent labor movement in Indonesia. He criticized the financial and economic policies of the Harahap cabinet, saying that these policies only benefited foreign monopoly capital. He said: "The working class demands lower prices and demands the resignation of the Harahap cabinet."

Yono also pointed out the good progress in unity within the Indonesian working class and the growing strength of democratic forces. He said that this meeting should aim to strengthen this unity and democracy in order to strive for better living conditions and the formation of a progressive government.

Yono called for strengthening international cooperation among working people to defend peace and national independence.

According to a statement issued by the Indonesian Trade Union Congress, the meeting will discuss issues such as improving the living conditions of working people, the danger of foreign interference, the demand for the resignation of the Harahap cabinet, and strengthening international cooperation among working people.

### 雅加达15万青年举行反颠覆活动大会  印度尼西亚总工会总书记批评哈拉哈普的政策

1956-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

    雅加达15万青年举行反颠覆活动大会
    印度尼西亚总工会总书记批评哈拉哈普的政策
    新华社雅加达23日电　在印度尼西亚首都雅加达，15万青年和学生2月22日下午在独立广场举行了反对颠覆活动的群众大会。
    在大会上发言的有青年、学生和前武装战士的代表，以及荷兰颠覆分子荣斯拉格和施密特案件的证人之一马诺波。他们都谴责了荷兰颠覆分子的罪恶活动，并且要求对那些破坏和平的人采取坚决的行动。
    大会通过的决议要求：立即驱逐哈拉哈普政府准予进入印度尼西亚的荷兰律师范恩贝尔出境，宣布现任荷兰驻印度尼西亚高级专员是不受欢迎的人，并且对那些从荷兰来的所谓证人以及同颠覆案件有关的一些荷兰企业的主管人采取严厉的行动。决议还要求判处荷兰颠覆分子荣斯拉格和施密特以死刑，并且支持审理这个案件的曼科姆法官所采取的坚定态度。
    据报纸消息说，万隆和三宝垄也分别在2月22日和21日举行了类似的反颠覆活动群众大会。
    新华社雅加达23日电　印度尼西亚总工会全国委员会会议2月21日在雅加达开幕。会议将举行五天。
    印度尼西亚总工会总书记约诺在会上致开幕词。他就印度尼西亚最近的劳工运动作了总的说明。他批评了哈拉哈普内阁的财政和经济政策。他说这种政策只有利于外国垄断资本。他说：“工人阶级要求降低物价，并且要求哈拉哈普内阁辞职。”
    约诺还指出在印度尼西亚工人阶级内部团结方面的良好进展和民主力量的日益增长。他说，这次会议应该以加强这种团结和民主为目的，以争取改善生活条件和组成进步的政府。
    约诺号召加强劳动人民的国际合作，来保卫和平和民族独立。
    根据印度尼西亚总工会发表的公告，这次会议将要讨论改善劳动人民的生活条件、外国干涉的危险和要求哈拉哈普内阁辞职、加强劳动人民的国际合作等问题。
