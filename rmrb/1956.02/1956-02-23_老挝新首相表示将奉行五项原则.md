Title: Lao New Premier States Five Principles to be Followed
Author:
Date: 1956-02-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 22nd: According to an Agence France-Presse report from Vientiane on the 21st, former Prime Minister Kote Sassorith has been entrusted by the King to organize a new government. Kote Sassorith presented the program of his new government to the newly elected National Assembly, requesting the National Assembly to authorize the new government. The program states that Laos will adhere to the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and advocates strict implementation of the 1954 Geneva Accords.

The main points of the program also include: Laos will strengthen its friendly ties and solidarity with France and the French Union "under free and equal conditions"; at the same time, Laos will seek to establish closer relations with its neighbors and all friendly and allied countries, especially Southeast Asian countries.

In addition, the program indicates that the new government will seek to restore the authority of the Royal Government in the provinces of Sam Neua and Phongsaly, which were designated as assembly areas for the Lao fighting forces under the Geneva Accords.

### 老挝新首相表示将奉行五项原则

1956-02-23
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝新首相表示将奉行五项原则
    新华社河内22日电　据法新社万象21日消息：前任首相克特·萨索里斯已经被国王委托组织新政府。克特·萨索里斯向最近选出来的国民议会提出了他的新政府的政纲，要求国民议会授权给新政府。政纲中说，老挝将奉行和平共处的五项原则，并且主张严格执行1954年日内瓦协定。
    政纲中的要点还包括：老挝将在“自由和平等的条件下”加强它同法国和法兰西联邦的友好联系和团结；同时，老挝将设法同它的邻国和一切友好和联盟的国家，特别是东南亚国家，建立较密切的关系。
  此外，政纲表示，新政府将设法恢复王国政府在日内瓦协定规定为寮国战斗部队集结区的桑怒和丰沙里两省的政权。
