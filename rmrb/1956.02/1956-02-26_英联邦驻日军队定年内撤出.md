Title: Commonwealth Forces in Japan Scheduled to Withdraw This Year
Author:
Date: 1956-02-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 23rd: The spokesman for the Commonwealth forces stationed in Japan and South Korea, B.S. Smith, announced on February 22nd that all Commonwealth troops, except for about a dozen personnel, will withdraw from Japan this year. At the same time, the military facilities in Kure Port, which have been occupied by the Commonwealth since 1945, will be returned to Japan.

Smith said that the remaining dozen or so people will be responsible for taking care of British soldiers on vacation from South Korea to Japan.

After the withdrawal of the British forces, the American army will become the only foreign army stationed on Japanese territory.

### 英联邦驻日军队定年内撤出

1956-02-26
第4版()
专栏：

    英联邦驻日军队定年内撤出
    据新华社23日讯　东京消息：英联邦驻日本和南朝鲜军队的发言人布·斯·史密斯在2月22日宣布：所有英联邦部队，除了大约十几个人以外，都将在今年撤出日本。同时，英联邦从1945年以来一直占用的吴港的军事设施也将归还日本。
    史密斯说，剩下的十几个人将负责照顾从南朝鲜到日本度假的英国士兵。
    在英军撤退以后，美国军队将成为驻扎在日本领土上的唯一的外国军队。
