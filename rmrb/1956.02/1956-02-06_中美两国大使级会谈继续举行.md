Title: Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Continue
Author:
Date: 1956-02-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, February 5th. The Sino-U.S. ambassadorial talks held their 35th meeting on February 4th. The meeting adjourned at 2:55 PM. The next meeting will be held on February 9th.

### 中美两国大使级会谈继续举行

1956-02-06
第1版()
专栏：

    中美两国大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦5日电　中美两国大使级会谈2月4日举行第35次会议。会议在下午2时55分休会，下次会议将在2月9日举行。
