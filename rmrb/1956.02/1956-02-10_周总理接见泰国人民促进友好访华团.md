Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives the Thai People's Delegation for Promoting Friendship with China

Author:
Date: 1956-02-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 9th. Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, received the Thai People's Delegation for Promoting Friendship with China on the afternoon of February 9th. The delegation included Head of the Delegation Nai Thep Chotinuchit, Deputy Head Nai Chern Buribhat, and 10 delegation members.

Present at the meeting were Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Jiakang, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Zhang Xiruo, and Secretary-General Wu Maosun. (Attached picture)

### 周总理接见泰国人民促进友好访华团

1956-02-10
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见泰国人民促进友好访华团
    新华社9日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，在9日下午接见了泰国人民促进友好访华团团长乃贴·触的努七、副团长乃琛·蒲里博和10位团员。
    接见的时候，在座的有对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部部长助理陈家康，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、秘书长吴茂荪。（附图片）
    周恩来总理和乃贴·触的努七团长（中）、乃琛·蒲里博副团长（左）谈话。
                                    新华社记者刘庆瑞摄
