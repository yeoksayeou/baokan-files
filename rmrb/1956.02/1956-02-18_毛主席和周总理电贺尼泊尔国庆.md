Title: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Telegrams Congratulating Nepal's National Day
Author:
Date: 1956-02-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 17th—Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, sent a telegram to King Mahendra Bir Bikram Shah Dev of Nepal, congratulating him on the National Day of the Kingdom of Nepal on February 18th. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Majesty King Mahendra Bir Bikram Shah Dev of Nepal,

    On the occasion of the National Day of the Kingdom of Nepal, I extend warm congratulations to Your Majesty and the people of Nepal on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish the Kingdom of Nepal increasing prosperity and the Nepalese people increasing happiness, and I wish Your Majesty good health.
            Chairman of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong
                      February 14, 1956, Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, February 17th—Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, sent a telegram to Prime Minister Tanka Prasad Acharya of the Kingdom of Nepal, congratulating him on the National Day of the Kingdom of Nepal on February 18th. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Excellency Prime Minister Tanka Prasad Acharya of the Kingdom of Nepal,

    On the occasion of the National Day of the Kingdom of Nepal, I extend sincere congratulations to the Government of the Kingdom of Nepal and to Your Excellency on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and in my own name.

    I sincerely hope that through the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Nepal, cultural and economic cooperation between the two countries will be further developed. I wish that the friendly relations between China and Nepal will be increasingly enhanced in the cause of maintaining peace in Asia and the world.
    Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai
                      February 14, 1956, Beijing

### 毛主席和周总理电贺尼泊尔国庆

1956-02-18
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席和周总理电贺尼泊尔国庆
    新华社17日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东致电尼泊尔国王马亨德拉·比尔·比克拉姆·沙阿·德瓦，祝贺2月18日尼泊尔王国国庆。电文如下：尼泊尔国王马亨德拉·比尔·比克拉姆·沙阿·德瓦陛下
    欣逢尼泊尔王国国庆，我谨代表中国人民，并且以我个人的名义向陛下和尼泊尔人民致热烈的祝贺。祝尼泊尔王国日益繁荣，尼泊尔人民日益幸福，并且祝陛下身体健康。
            中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
                      1956年2月14日　北京
    新华社17日讯　　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来致电尼泊尔王国首相唐克·普拉沙德·阿查雅，祝贺2月18日尼泊尔王国国庆。电文如下：尼泊尔王国首相唐克·普拉沙德·阿查雅阁下
    值此尼泊尔王国国庆之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府并且以我个人的名义向尼泊尔王国政府和阁下表示诚挚的祝贺。
    我热诚地希望：通过中尼两国外交关系的建立，两国间的文化和经济合作将进一步发展。祝中尼两国间的友好关系在维护亚洲和世界和平的事业中日益增进。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                      1956年2月14日　北京
