Date: 1956-02-07

    The People's Republic of China delegation, headed by Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Moscow by special train on February 4th. Greeting them at the station were First Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Molotov (first person from the left in the front row) and Mikoyan (first person from the right in the front row), among others. Vice Chairman Zhu De (second person from the left in the front row) delivered a speech at the station.
                             TASS transmitted photo (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)
