    On the morning of February 10th, Li Yuming (center), the Secretary of the Communist Party Committee of Shijingshan District in the suburbs of Beijing, sat at the switchboard of the dispatch telephone for the first time to preside over a production meeting. He issued instructions to nine agricultural production cooperatives and listened to their reports and opinions. Using the dispatch telephone to hold production meetings is very convenient; what used to take a day or two can now be resolved in an hour.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Niu Weiyu photographed

专栏：

    2月10日上午，北京市郊石景山区中共区委书记李瑜铭（中）第一次坐在调度电话总机旁主持生产会议，向9个农业生产合作社发出指示，并听取了他们的汇报和意见。利用调度电话召开生产会议，非常方便，一小时可以解决过去一天或两天才能解决的问题。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　新华社记者　牛畏予摄
