Title: Pyongyang Holds Grand Meeting to Welcome Chinese People's Delegation to Korea; Kim Il-sung and Kim Tu-bong Receive Head of Delegation Wang Ziye Respectively
Author:
Date: 1956-02-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 6th. Pyongyang held a grand welcoming rally at the Moranbong Theater on the evening of February 6th to welcome the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea.

　　The meeting was presided over by Jeong Nan-gyo, Chairman of the Pyongyang People's Committee. Minister of Culture and Propaganda Heo Jeong-suk of the Korean Cabinet first delivered a speech, expressing a warm welcome to the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea and thanking the Chinese people. She said that the delegation's visit to Korea would encourage the Korean people to create greater achievements in the struggle for the unification and independence of their motherland and the strengthening of the democratic base. She said that the American aggressors and the Syngman Rhee clique are still unreasonably opposing the peaceful reunification of Korea and a fair and reasonable settlement of the Korean issue; the American aggressors are still forcibly occupying China's territory of Taiwan and unreasonably interfering in China's internal affairs. However, the plans of the United States and its followers are bound to fail, and the Korean people will surely achieve the peaceful reunification of Korea, and the Chinese people will surely liberate Taiwan.

　　Jeong Nan-gyo, Chairman of the Pyongyang People's Committee, and Choi Jong-hak, Director of the General Political Bureau of the People's Army, presented flags to the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea on behalf of the working people of Pyongyang and the Supreme Command of the People's Army, respectively.

　　Wang Ziye, head of the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, accepted the flag and delivered a reply. He extended warm congratulations and condolences on behalf of the entire Chinese people to the heroic people of Pyongyang and the entire Korean people who have achieved brilliant achievements in the post-war reconstruction. He said that the friendship between the Chinese and Korean people is long-standing and profound. The Chinese people cherish the eternal friendship and unity between the Chinese and Korean people and will continue to work to enhance this great friendship and unity.

　　After the meeting, the Korean People's Army Ensemble performed wonderful programs.

　　The welcoming rally was attended by Park Jeong-ae, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, Choi Chang-ik, Vice Premier of the Korean Cabinet, Nam Il, Minister of Foreign Affairs, Kim Chang-man, Minister of Education, Ha Ang-cheon, Director of the Department of Social Sciences of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party, Li Ge-ru, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, Kim Kwang-hyeop, Chief of the General Staff of the People's Army, Baek Nam-un, President of the Academy of Sciences, members of the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Front for the Unification of the Fatherland, and heads of various democratic parties, social organizations, generals and officers of the People's Army, and working people of Pyongyang, totaling more than 1,000 people.

　　The delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army led by General Yang Yong, Commander of the Volunteer Army, attended the meeting.

　　Pan Zili, Chinese Ambassador to North Korea, and embassy staff also attended the meeting.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 6th. Marshal Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, respectively received Wang Ziye, head of the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, on February 6th. Wang Ziye was accompanied by Pan Zili, Chinese Ambassador to North Korea.

　　When Marshal Kim Il-sung met with the delegation, those present included Park Jeong-ae, Park Geum-cheol, Vice Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party, Ha Ang-cheon, Director of the Department of Social Sciences of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party, Heo Jeong-suk, Minister of Culture and Propaganda, and General Kim Kwang-hyeop, Chief of the General Staff of the People's Army.

　　When Kim Tu-bong met with the delegation, those present included Ri Yeong, Chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, Ri Geu-ru, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, Kang Ryang-uk, Secretary-General, Ha Ang-cheon, Director of the Department of Social Sciences of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party, and Yun Gun-chang, Deputy Director of the General Political Bureau of the People's Army.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 6th. The Chinese People's Delegation to Korea arrived in Sinuiju, a border city of North Korea, on February 6th, and was warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life in North Korea and the People's Army.

　　Now, the general delegation and its subordinate sub-delegations, accompanied by representatives from all walks of life in Korea who came to welcome them and representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, have left Sinuiju to visit various places for consolation.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 6th. The general delegation and the first sub-delegation of the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, led by Wang Ziye, head of the delegation, arrived in Pyongyang by special train on the morning of the 6th.

　　Those who welcomed the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea at the station included Heo Jeong-suk, Minister of Culture and Propaganda of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Ha Ang-cheon, Director of the Department of Social Sciences of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party, General Kim Kwang-hyeop, Chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army, heads of various parties and groups in Korea, people from all walks of life, Pyongyang citizens, and officers and soldiers of the People's Army, totaling several thousand people.

　　Pan Zili, Chinese Ambassador to North Korea, and embassy staff also came to the station to welcome the delegation.

　　A welcoming rally was held on the station platform. General Choi Jong-hak, Director of the General Political Bureau of the Korean People's Army, expressed a warm welcome to the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea on behalf of the Korean people and the officers and soldiers of the Korean People's Army.

　　Wang Ziye, head of the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, delivered a reply.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 6th. To celebrate the founding anniversary of the Korean People's Army, the leading organs and various units of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army organized 27 delegations and 6 cultural and artistic work groups to congratulate and comfort the Supreme Command of the Korean People's Army and various units of the Korean People's Army.

### 平壤举行大会欢迎我国赴朝慰问团  金日成和金枓奉分别接见总团长王子宜

1956-02-07
第4版()
专栏：

    平壤举行大会欢迎我国赴朝慰问团
    金日成和金枓奉分别接见总团长王子宜
    新华社平壤6日电　平壤市各界人民6日晚在牡丹峰剧场举行盛大欢迎会，欢迎中国人民赴朝慰问团。
    大会由平壤市人民委员会委员长郑然彪主持。朝鲜内阁文化宣传相许贞琡首先致词，他对中国人民赴朝慰问团表示热烈欢迎，并且向中国人民致谢。他说，慰问团这次来朝鲜将鼓舞朝鲜人民在为祖国的统一、独立和加强民主基地的斗争中，创造更大的成就。他说，美国侵略者和李承晚集团仍在无理地反对朝鲜的和平统一和朝鲜问题公正合理地解决；美国侵略者仍然以武力侵占着中国领土台湾，无理地干涉中国内政。但是，美国和它的仆从们的打算必然要失败的，朝鲜人民一定会实现朝鲜的和平统一，中国人民一定会解放台湾。
    平壤市人民委员会委员长郑然彪和人民军总政治局局长崔宗学分别代表平壤市劳动人民和人民军最高司令部向中国人民赴朝慰问团献旗。
    中国人民赴朝慰问团总团长王子宜受旗，并且致答词。他代表全中国人民对在战后恢复建设事业中获得辉煌成就的英雄的平壤人民和全朝鲜人民表示热烈的祝贺和慰问。他说，中朝两国人民的友谊是长远而深厚的，中国人民珍爱中朝两国人民永恒的友谊和团结，并且将继续为增进这种伟大友谊和团结而努力。
    会后，朝鲜人民军协奏团演出了精采节目。
    出席欢迎大会的有朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、朝鲜内阁副首相崔昌益、外务相南日、教育相金昌满、劳动党中央委员会社会科学部部长河仰天、最高人民会议常任委员会副委员长李克鲁、人民军总参谋长金光侠、科学院院长白南云、朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会的委员们和各民主政党、社会团体负责人、人民军将领和军官、平壤市劳动人民等，共1，000多人。
    中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇将军率领的志愿军代表团出席了大会。
    出席大会的还有中国驻朝鲜大使潘自力和使馆人员。
    新华社平壤6日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成元帅、最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉，6日分别接见了中国人民赴朝慰问团总团长王子宜。王子宜是由中国驻朝鲜大使潘自力陪同进见的。
    金日成元帅接见的时候，在座的有劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、朴金哲，劳动党中央委员会社会科学部部长河仰天、文化宣传相许贞琡、人民军总参谋长金光侠大将。
    金枓奉接见的时候，在座的有朝鲜最高人民会议议长李英、最高人民会议常任委员会副委员长李克鲁、书记长康良煜、劳动党中央委员会社会科学部部长河仰天、人民军总政治局副局长尹君昌。
    据新华社平壤6日电　中国人民赴朝慰问团6日到达朝鲜国境城市新义州，受到朝鲜各界人民和人民军的热烈欢迎。
    现在，慰问团总团及所属各分团在前来欢迎的朝鲜各界代表和中国人民志愿军代表的陪同下，已经离开新义州分别赴各地进行慰问。
    据新华社平壤6日电　中国人民赴朝慰问团总团和第一分团在总团长王子宜率领下，6日上午乘专车到达平壤。
    到车站欢迎中国人民赴朝慰问团的有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国文化宣传相许贞琡、劳动党中央委员会社会科学部部长河仰天，朝鲜人民军最高司令部总参谋长金光侠大将和朝鲜各党派、团体负责人、社会各界人士、平壤市民和人民军官兵等，共几千人。
    中国驻朝鲜大使潘自力和使馆人员也到车站欢迎。
    在车站月台上举行了欢迎大会。朝鲜人民军总政治局局长崔宗学上将代表朝鲜人民和朝鲜人民军官兵对中国人民赴朝慰问团表示热烈欢迎。
    中国人民赴朝慰问团总团长王子宜致答词。
    据新华社平壤6日电　为祝贺朝鲜人民军建军节，中国人民志愿军领导机关和各部队组织27个代表团和6个文艺工作团，到朝鲜人民军最高司令部和朝鲜人民军各部队进行祝贺和慰问。
