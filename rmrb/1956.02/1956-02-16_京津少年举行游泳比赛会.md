Title: Beijing-Tianjin Youth Swimming Competition Held
Author:
Date: 1956-02-16
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency
    Beijing-Tianjin Youth Swimming Competition Held
    The 1956 Beijing-Tianjin Youth Swimming Competition was held on the 15th in the swimming pool of the Beijing Gymnasium. More than 50 young swimmers in the boys' and girls' A and B groups participated in 21 events. Their ages ranged from fifteen or sixteen years old, with the youngest being only 11 years old.
    At the competition, Mu Xiangxiong of the Central Institute of Physical Education performed the men's 100-meter breaststroke. He achieved a time of 1 minute 10.6 seconds, breaking his own national record of 1 minute 11.8 seconds, which he set last year at the China-Soviet swimming competition in Moscow.
                      (Xinhua News Agency)

### 京津少年举行游泳比赛会

1956-02-16
第2版()
专栏：

    京津少年举行游泳比赛会
    1956年北京、天津少年游泳比赛会15日在北京体育馆的游泳馆内举行。男子甲、乙组和女子甲、乙组的50多名少年游泳运动员共进行了21项比赛。他们的年龄都在十五、六岁左右，最小的只11岁。
    在比赛会上，中央体育学院的穆祥雄进行了男子100公尺蛙泳表演。他以1分10秒6的成绩，打破了他去年在莫斯科举行的中、苏游泳比赛时创造的1分11秒8的全国最高纪录。
                      （新华社）
