Title: Indonesian Navy Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-02-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 26th—Indonesian Navy Chief of Staff Rear Admiral Subyakto and his wife, along with the Indonesian Navy delegation he leads, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of February 26th, accompanied by Colonel Mao Chen, Military Attaché of the People's Republic of China to Indonesia.

Those present at the airport to welcome them today included Vice Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China and Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy, Admiral Xiao Jingguang; Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong; representatives of the General Staff Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Major General An Dong; representatives of the General Political Department, Major General Huang Yuan; Deputy Commander of the Navy, Vice Admiral Luo Shunchu; Deputy Commander of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison, Lieutenant General Zheng Weishan; Deputy Director of the Asian Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Chen Shuliang; Chief of the Foreign Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of National Defense, Colonel Jiang Keding; Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Cao Mengjun, and others.

Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy in China, Anwar Sani, as well as military attachés, acting military attachés, and deputy military attachés of various countries' embassies in China, were also present at the airport to welcome them.

The delegation's members include the head of the delegation, Rear Admiral Subyakto and his wife, and members Lieutenant Colonel Sarin (Indonesian Military Attaché to China), Major Saleh Bratawijaya, Major Loegbregt, and Captain Sriyono Projosukanto.

Xinhua News Agency, February 26th—Indonesian Navy Chief of Staff Rear Admiral Subyakto and his wife, along with all members of the Indonesian Navy delegation, paid a courtesy call to Admiral Xiao Jingguang, Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy, on the evening of February 26th.

After the courtesy call, they were invited to have dinner with Admiral Xiao Jingguang and his wife. (Attached picture)
Rear Admiral Subyakto (left) walks out of the airport accompanied by Vice Minister of National Defense and Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy, Admiral Xiao Jingguang (right). Xinhua News Agency reporter Zou Jiandong.

### 印度尼西亚海军访华代表团到北京

1956-02-27
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚海军访华代表团到北京
    据新华社26日讯　印度尼西亚海军参谋长苏比雅克托海军少将和夫人以及他所率领的印度尼西亚海军访华代表团，由中华人民共和国驻印度尼西亚武官茅琛上校陪同，在26日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    今天到机场欢迎的，有中华人民共和国国防部副部长、中国人民解放军海军司令员萧劲光海军大将，国防部办公厅主任萧向荣中将，中国人民解放军总参谋部代表安东少将、总政治部代表黄远少将，海军副司令员罗舜初海军中将，京津卫戍副司令员郑维山中将，外交部亚洲司副司长陈叔亮，国防部外事处处长蒋克定上校，中华全国民主妇女联合会书记处书记曹孟君等。
    印度尼西亚驻华大使馆临时代办安瓦·萨尼及各国驻华大使馆武官、代理武官和副武官也到机场欢迎。
    代表团的组成人员，包括团长苏比雅克托海军少将和夫人，团员有沙林中校（印度尼西亚驻华武官）、萨勒·勃腊塔维迦亚少校、鲁格勃雷契特少校、斯里约诺·泼罗轿苏坎托上尉。
    据新华社26日讯　印度尼西亚海军参谋长苏比雅克托海军少将和夫人以及印度尼西亚海军访华代表团全体团员，在26日晚拜会了中国人民解放军海军司令员萧劲光海军大将。
    他们在拜会以后，应邀和萧劲光海军大将及他的夫人共进晚餐。（附图片）
