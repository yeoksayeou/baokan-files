Title: Our Country's慰问团 (Comfort Mission) Arrives at the Volunteer Army Garrison
Author:
Date: 1956-02-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 8th. Each sub-group of the Chinese People's 慰问团 (Comfort Mission) to Korea arrived at the headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the garrisons of various units on the 7th. They were warmly welcomed.

At the Volunteer Army leadership headquarters, more than 3,500 Volunteer Army officers and soldiers, local Korean people, and representatives of overseas Chinese welcomed the arrival of the 慰问团 (Comfort Mission) representatives.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 7th. A portion of the representatives and song and dance troupe members of the Chinese People's 慰问团 (Comfort Mission) to Korea, led by Deputy Head Luo Feng, arrived in Kaesong on the evening of the 6th. On the 7th, the 慰问团 (Comfort Mission) representatives visited and extended greetings to the station of the Korean-Chinese Delegation and the garrisons of the Korean People's Army troops stationed in the Kaesong area.

### 我国赴朝慰问团到达志愿军驻地

1956-02-09
第4版()
专栏：

    我国赴朝慰问团到达志愿军驻地
    据新华社平壤8日电　中国人民赴朝慰问团各分团在7日分别到达中国人民志愿军领导机关和各部队驻地。他们受到热烈欢迎。
    在志愿军领导机关驻地，志愿军官兵、当地朝鲜人民、华侨代表共3，500多人欢迎慰问团代表们的到来。
    据新华社平壤7日电　中国人民赴朝慰问团的一部分代表和歌舞团人员，由副总团长洛风率领在6日晚到达开城。7日，慰问团代表们先后到朝中代表团驻地、驻开城地区的朝鲜人民军部队驻地进行慰问和访问。
