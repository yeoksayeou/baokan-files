Title: Capital Overfulfills Government Bond Sales Plan
Author:
Date: 1956-02-25
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, February 24th. As of the 22nd, people from all walks of life in the capital had purchased government bonds exceeding the original sales plan by 1.63%. Currently, workers and industrialists are continuing to subscribe.

### 首都推销公债超过计划

1956-02-25
第1版()
专栏：

    首都推销公债超过计划
    新华社24日讯　首都各界人民认购的公债，到22日止已经超过原推销计划1.63％。现在，职工们和工商界人士仍在继续认购。
