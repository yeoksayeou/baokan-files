Title: Our Theatrical Troupe of the Delegation to North Korea Performs

Author: 
Date: 1956-02-08
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 7th – The Beijing Opera Troupe of the Chinese Opera School, which accompanied the Chinese People's Delegation to North Korea, gave its first performance of condolence in Pyongyang at the Moranbong Theater on the evening of February 7th.

    Marshal Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, attended the performance and met with all the actors on stage.

    Also present at the performance were leaders of the Korean Workers' Party and government, generals, officers, and soldiers of the Korean People's Army, people from all walks of life in Pyongyang, and representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteers delegation and troops stationed in the Pyongyang area.

    Diplomatic envoys from various countries to North Korea also attended the performance.

### 我赴朝慰问团剧团演出

1956-02-08
第4版()
专栏：

    我赴朝慰问团剧团演出
    据新华社平壤7日电　随中国人民赴朝慰问团到平壤的中国戏曲学校京剧团2月7日晚上在平壤牡丹峰剧场举行首次慰问演出。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国首相金日成元帅出席观看了演出，并且到舞台上会见了全体演员。
    出席观看演出的还有朝鲜劳动党和政府的负责人，朝鲜人民军的将领军官和士兵，平壤市各界人民，中国人民志愿军代表团和志愿军驻平壤地区部队的代表等。
    观剧的还有各国驻朝鲜使节。
