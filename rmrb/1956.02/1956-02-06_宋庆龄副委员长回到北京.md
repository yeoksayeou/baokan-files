Title: Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-02-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, Soong Ching Ling, returned to Beijing by special plane on the afternoon of the 5th after being invited to visit India, Burma, and Pakistan.

Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling received flowers after disembarking from the plane and cordially shook hands with those who welcomed her.

Premier of the State Council and Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Zhou Enlai, went to the airport to welcome her.

Those who came to the airport to welcome her included Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Peng Zhen, Li Weihan, and Chen Shutong, Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yun, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhang Bojun, Chen Jiageng, and Bao Erhan, Vice Presidents of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Li Dequan, Xu Guangping, Shi Liang, and Zhang Yun, and Vice Mayors of Beijing Zhang Youyu and Wang Kunlun.

Also present at the airport to welcome her were the Vice President of the Supreme People's Court, the Deputy Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, members and deputy secretaries-general of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and heads of various departments, the Secretary-General of the State Council and directors of various offices, ministers and vice ministers of various ministries, deputy directors of various committees, members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, leaders of various democratic parties, and responsible persons of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, the China People's Relief Administration, and other people's organizations, as well as representatives from all walks of life in the capital.

Pakistani Ambassador to China, Ahmed, Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru and his wife, and Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Burmese Embassy, U Maung Maung Toe, also came to the airport to welcome her.

Approximately 1,000 people came to the airport to welcome her. (Picture attached)

(Caption) Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling shakes hands with Premier Zhou Enlai, who came to welcome her at the airport. Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Qingrui.

### 宋庆龄副委员长回到北京

1956-02-06
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄副委员长回到北京
    新华社5日讯　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄，在应邀访问印度、缅甸、巴基斯坦等国后，在5日下午乘专机返回北京。
    宋庆龄副委员长下机后，接受了献花，并同欢迎者们亲切握手。
    国务院总理、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会主席周恩来前往机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的，还有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长沈钧儒、郭沫若、彭真、李维汉、陈叔通，国务院副总理陈云，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧、陈嘉庚、鲍尔汉，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全、许广平、史良、章蕴，北京市副市长张友渔、王昆仑。
    到机场欢迎的，还有最高人民法院副院长，最高人民检察院副检察长，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员、副秘书长和各部门负责人，国务院秘书长、各办公室主任，各部部长、副部长，各委员会副主任，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员，各民主党派负责人，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中苏友好协会、中印友好协会、中缅友好协会、中国印度尼西亚友好协会、中国人民救济总会和其他各人民团体负责人以及首都各界代表。
    巴基斯坦驻华大使阿哈默德，印度驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁和夫人，缅甸驻华大使馆临时代办吴茂茂铎也到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的约1，000人。（附图片）
