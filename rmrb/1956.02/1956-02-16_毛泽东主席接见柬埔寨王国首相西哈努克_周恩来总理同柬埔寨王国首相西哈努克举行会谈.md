Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Receives Cambodian Prime Minister Sihanouk; Premier Zhou Enlai Holds Talks with Cambodian Prime Minister Sihanouk
Author:
Date: 1956-02-16
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 15th — Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, received the head of the Cambodian Royal delegation, His Royal Highness Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, at 5:20 PM on the 15th.
    He also received the delegation members: Ran Neet, member of the Royal High Council; Chin Dith, member of the Royal High Council; Sang Nhean, Vice-President of the Kingdom Council; Ung Meas, Member of the National Assembly; Dr. Nhel Smouk, Member of the Kingdom Council; Colonel Nou Huk, Chief of Staff of the Royal Air Force; Ron Sophan, Deputy Undersecretary of State for Peasant Welfare; and Pho Proeung Sarun, the Prince's Private Secretary.
    Present at the reception were Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council; Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, and Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Peng Zhen, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Mayor of Beijing; Peng Dehuai and Chen Yi, Vice-Premiers of the State Council; and Zhang Wentian, Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs.
    Xinhua News Agency, 15th — Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, held talks with His Royal Highness Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, at 2:00 PM on the 15th.
    Participating in the talks on the Cambodian side were Ran Neet, member of the Royal High Council; Chin Dith, member of the Royal High Council; and Sang Nhean, Vice-President of the Kingdom Council.
    Participating in the talks on the Chinese side were Chen Yi, Vice-Premier of the State Council; Zhang Wentian, Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs; and Chen Jiakang, Assistant Minister. (Photo attached)
    Chairman Mao Zedong's group photo with Prime Minister Sihanouk and others. ### 毛泽东主席接见柬埔寨王国首相西哈努克  周恩来总理同柬埔寨王国首相西哈努克举行会谈

1956-02-16
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席接见柬埔寨王国首相西哈努克
    周恩来总理同柬埔寨王国首相西哈努克举行会谈
    新华社15日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东，在15日下午5时20分接见了柬埔寨王国国家代表团团长、柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王殿下。
    同时还接见了代表团团员：皇廷最高会议委员兰·涅特，皇廷最高会议委员钦·迪，王国会议副议长桑·年，国民会议议员恩格·米阿斯，王国会议议员尼尔·斯莫乌克博士，皇家空军参谋长努·呼上校，农民福利副国务秘书罗恩·索方，亲王私人秘书丰·萨伦。
    接见时在座的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、陈叔通，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、北京市市长彭真，国务院副总理彭德怀、陈毅，外交部副部长张闻天。
    新华社15日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王殿下，在15日下午2时举行会谈。
    柬埔寨王国方面参加会谈的，有皇廷最高会议委员兰·涅特，皇廷最高会议委员钦·迪，王国会议副议长桑·年。
    中国方面参加会谈的，有国务院副总理陈毅，外交部副部长张闻天和部长助理陈家康。（附图片）
