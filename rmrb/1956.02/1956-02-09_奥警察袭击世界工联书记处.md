Title: Austrian Police Raid World Federation of Trade Unions Secretariat

Author:

Date: 1956-02-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, February 7th: Austrian police authorities suddenly raided the office building of the World Federation of Trade Unions Secretariat on the evening of February 6th. They occupied the office building and subsequently sealed the premises.

### 奥警察袭击世界工联书记处

1956-02-09
第4版()
专栏：

    奥警察袭击世界工联书记处
    新华社据塔斯社维也纳7日讯　奥地利警察当局在2月6日晚间突然袭击世界工会联合会书记处的办公楼。他们占据了办公楼，随后并封闭了房屋。
