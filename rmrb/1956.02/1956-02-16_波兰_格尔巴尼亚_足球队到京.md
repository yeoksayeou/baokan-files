Title: Poland's "Garbarnia" Football Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-02-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: The entire 21-member Polish Krakow "Garbarnia" football team, invited to visit China and play friendly matches, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 15th. The "Garbarnia" football team is led by A. Gudowski and the deputy leader is J. Mokrzycki.

### 波兰“格尔巴尼亚”足球队到京

1956-02-16
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰“格尔巴尼亚”足球队到京
    新华社讯　应邀来中国访问和进行友谊比赛的波兰克拉科夫市“格尔巴尼亚”足球队全体人员21人，15日下午乘飞机到达北京。“格尔巴尼亚”足球队由亚·古多夫斯基任领队；由约·莫克尔净斯基任副领队。
