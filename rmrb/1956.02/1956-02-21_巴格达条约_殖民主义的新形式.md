Title: The Baghdad Pact - A New Form of Colonialism
Author: Muḥàmòdé Āqìmǎ'ěr
Date: 1956-02-21
Page: 3

    The Baghdad Pact—A New Form of Colonialism
    Syrian Ahong Muḥammad Āqìmǎ'ěr

    For decades, the Arab people have been heroically fighting against imperialism and imperialist elements. After 500 years of Turkish Ottoman rule over Arab countries and after the First World War, Syria and Lebanon fell under French rule, while other Arab countries fell under British rule.

    Since then, our people have been fighting for freedom and national independence, to eliminate the shackles of colonialism. After the end of the Second World War, Syria and Lebanon, thanks to their own sacrifices, the solidarity of the Arab people, and the free forces of the world, especially the support of the Soviet Union, finally gained freedom and independence. But our people continue the struggle and are still committed to smashing the imperialists' conspiracies and the oppression and terror they have imposed on us—using various conventions and plans to bind us again.

    Today, their conspiracies and coercions are particularly focused on Syria in order to bind it to the militarily aggressive Baghdad Pact. It is very clear that those Arab peoples who once fought for their freedom and sovereignty will continue to oppose imperialism, its treaties and conspiracies, and its political and economic domination. Therefore, the Arab people heroically oppose the Baghdad Pact, which is a new form of colonialist conquest of Arab countries, used to strengthen its bases in those countries that are still under its rule.

    We, the Syrian people, understand the meaning of the struggle for freedom and warmly welcome the historic victory of the great Chinese people in the liberation struggle to get rid of imperialism. Every victory of the great Chinese people in consolidating their sovereignty is a new blow to imperialism and a major support for our people and all the people of Asia who are struggling for national sovereignty, freedom, and independence, and to get rid of imperialist enslavement.

    We are fully convinced that the struggle of the oppressed people will eventually achieve a definite victory over the forces of war and imperialism. The unity of the people who love freedom and peace is the guarantee of achieving this great victory.

### 巴格达条约——殖民主义的新形式
穆哈默德·阿契玛尔
1956-02-21
第3版()
专栏：

    巴格达条约——殖民主义的新形式
    叙利亚阿訇　穆哈默德·阿契玛尔
    几十年来，阿拉伯人民一直英勇地进行着反对帝国主义和帝国主义分子的斗争。在土耳其奥斯曼大公统治阿拉伯国家500年后和在第一次世界大战之后，叙利亚和黎巴嫩沦落在法国的统治下，其它的阿拉伯国家则沦落在英国的统治之下。
    自从那时以来，我们的人民一直为争取自由和民族独立、消除殖民主义束缚而斗争。在第二次世界大战结束后，叙利亚和黎巴嫩由于他们自己的牺牲、由于阿拉伯人民的团结一致，以及全世界的自由的力量，特别是苏联的支持，终于获得了自由和独立。但是我们人民仍把斗争继续下去，并且仍在致力于粉碎帝国主义者的阴谋，和他们所加在我们头上的压迫和恐怖——用各种公约和计划再度束缚我们。
    如今，他们的阴谋和胁迫特别集中在叙利亚身上，以便把它束缚到军事侵略的巴格达条约里去。一切都很清楚地证明，那些过去曾经为了他们的自由和主权而斗争的阿拉伯人民，将继续反对帝国主义，反对它的条约和阴谋，反对它的政治的和经济的统治，因此阿拉伯人民英勇地反对巴格达条约，这个条约是殖民主义者征服阿拉伯国家的新形式，是用来加强它在那些仍然受它统治的国家的基地的。
    我们叙利亚人民，晓得争取自由的斗争的意义，热烈地欢迎伟大的中国人民在摆脱帝国主义的解放斗争中所获得的历史性胜利。伟大的中国人民在巩固其主权上所获得的每一个胜利，就是对帝国主义的一记新的打击，对我们人民和亚洲所有为争取民族主权、自由和独立、摆脱帝国主义奴役而斗争的人民，是一个重大的支持。
    我们十分确信：被压迫的人民的斗争终将取得对战争和帝国主义势力的肯定的胜利。热爱自由与和平的人民的团结一致，是取得这个伟大胜利的保证。
