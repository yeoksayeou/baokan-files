Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Condolences for Casualties in Yugoslav Avalanche

Author:

Date: 1956-02-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 20th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram on February 18th to the Central Council of the Confederation of Trade Unions of Yugoslavia, offering condolences for the deaths and injuries caused by the avalanche disaster in the area of the Mavrovo hydroelectric power plant construction site. The telegram stated: We express our condolences with grief for the deceased in this unfortunate event and extend our sympathy to the injured.

### 我总工会致电吊慰南斯拉夫雪崩事件中的死伤者

1956-02-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我总工会致电吊慰南斯拉夫雪崩事件中的死伤者
    新华社20日讯　中华全国总工会18日致电南斯拉夫工会联合会中央理事会，吊慰在马佛罗伏的水力发电厂建设工地地区遭受雪崩灾害的死伤者。电报里说：我们怀着悲痛的心情，为在这次不幸事件中的死难者表示吊唁以及向负伤者致以慰问。
