Title: Chiang Kai-shek Troops Bombard Coastal Villages and Fishing Boats in Fujian

Author:

Date: 1956-02-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: Around the Spring Festival, Chiang Kai-shek's naval vessels have continuously bombarded coastal villages and fishing boats in Fujian. On the 11th, 11 fishing boats from Shibi Township, Changle County, Fujian Province, were attacked by Chiang Kai-shek's gunboats while fishing in the Dongsha fishing grounds. One fishing boat was destroyed, and eight fishermen were kidnapped.

At 5:10 p.m. on the 13th, three Chiang Kai-shek warships bombarded Luciyu Island southwest of Nanri Island, firing more than 70 shells.

Around 12:00 noon on the 15th, two Chiang Kai-shek warships attacked a group of fishing boats fishing in the sea east of Liu'ao, Zhangpu County, firing more than 20 shells.

### 蒋军轰击福建沿海村镇和渔船

1956-02-23
第1版()
专栏：

    蒋军轰击福建沿海村镇和渔船
    据新华社讯　春节前后，蒋军舰艇不断轰击福建沿海村镇和海上渔船。11日，福建省长乐县石壁乡的11只渔船在东沙渔场捕鱼的时候，遭到蒋军炮艇的攻击。1只渔船被打毁，8名渔民被绑走。
    13日下午5时10分，蒋军军舰3艘向南日岛西南鹭鹚屿岛进行轰击，发射炮弹70多发。
    15日中午12时左右，蒋军军舰两艘袭击在漳浦县六鳌以东海面捕鱼的一批渔船，发射炮弹20多发。
