Title: Cambodian Parliament Sends Letter to Sihanouk Praising His Policy of Peaceful Coexistence
Author:
Date: 1956-02-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 28th. A broadcast from Phnom Penh on the 28th reported that the National Assembly of the Kingdom of Cambodia has sent a letter to Prince Sihanouk, expressing gratitude for the results of Prince Sihanouk's visit to China.

The letter stated that Prince Sihanouk visited China with friendly sentiments, and the Prince has expressed the position and policy of peaceful coexistence for maintaining world peace of Cambodia, which is led by him. The letter pointed out that the ideal announced by Prince Sihanouk is the honor of Cambodia and the entire Cambodian nation.

### 柬埔寨议会致函西哈努克  赞扬他的和平共处政策

1956-02-29
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨议会致函西哈努克
    赞扬他的和平共处政策
    新华社河内28日电　据金边电台28日广播，柬埔寨王国国民会议已经给了西哈努克亲王一封信，对西哈努克亲王访问中国的结果表示感谢。
    信中说，西哈努克亲王带着友好的感情去访问了中国，亲王已经表示了由他所领导的柬埔寨的维护世界和平的和平共处的立场和政策。信中指出，西哈努克亲王宣布的理想是柬埔寨和柬埔寨全民族的荣誉。
