Title: Sudanese Foreign Minister Announces Neutral Policy
Author:
Date: 1956-02-12
Page: 4

    Sudanese Foreign Minister Announces Neutral Policy
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, February 9th (TASS): According to a Middle East News Agency report from Khartoum, Sudanese Foreign Minister Mubarak Zarrouk announced at a press conference that Sudan advocates cooperation with all countries in the world. He emphasized that Sudan will pursue a policy of neutrality.
    He said that our foreign policy is guided solely by national interests. The Sudanese government fully supports the resolutions of the Bandung Conference and will strengthen Sudan's relations with countries in Asia and Africa.
    The Foreign Minister then pointed out that Sudan needs foreign economic and technical assistance; however, it cannot agree to conditions that "restrict its sovereignty and national independence."

### 苏丹外长宣布实行中立政策

1956-02-12
第4版()
专栏：

    苏丹外长宣布实行中立政策
    新华社10日讯　塔斯社开罗9日讯：据中东通讯社喀土穆消息，苏丹外交部长穆巴拉克·查鲁克在记者招待会上宣布，苏丹主张同世界上所有的国家进行合作。他强调说，苏丹将实行中立政策。
    他说，我们的对外政策只是以民族利益为指针。苏丹政府完全拥护万隆会议的决议，并且将巩固苏丹同亚非两洲各国的关系。
    这位外交部长接着指出，苏丹需要外国的经济援助和技术援助，然而，它不能同意那些“限制它的主权和国家独立”的条件。
