Title: Our Army Shoots Down Four Chiang Kai-shek Aircraft Harassing the Skies Over Coastal Fujian
Author:
Date: 1956-02-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 7th. Our People's Liberation Army's frontline anti-aircraft artillery units in Fujian shot down four Chiang Kai-shek aircraft on the 5th and 6th.

On the 6th, a total of 31 Chiang Kai-shek aircraft in 9 batches intruded into the skies over coastal Fujian to conduct harassment. From 16:16 to 16:52, two batches of 8 Chiang Kai-shek F-84 fighter jets intruded into the Pingtan Island area, bombing and strafing fishing boats and civilian vessels on the Niushan sea and 1,500 meters east of Guanyin Ao, dropping 8 bombs and strafing in rotation 4 times. Two civilian boats from Hanjiang Town, Putian County, were sunk, killing 11 crew members and injuring 3. From 9:02 to 9:31 on the 6th, a batch of 4 Chiang Kai-shek F-84 fighter jets harassed the skies over Weitou, Yongning and other areas of Jinjiang County. The People's Liberation Army's anti-aircraft artillery units immediately intercepted them with fierce artillery fire, shooting down 2. One was hit in the fuselage and left wing, and the other was hit in the nose, trailing black smoke as it fled towards the East China Sea. At 16:08 on the 5th, a batch of 4 Chiang Kai-shek F-84 fighter jets harassed the skies over the Pinghai area of Putian County, and two were shot down by the People's Liberation Army's anti-aircraft artillery units.

### 我军击伤窜入福建沿海上空骚扰的蒋军飞机四架

1956-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我军击伤窜入福建沿海上空骚扰的蒋军飞机四架
    新华社福建前线7日电　中国人民解放军福建前线高射炮部队在五、六两日击伤蒋军飞机4架。
    6日，蒋军飞机共9批31架次窜入福建沿海上空骚扰。16时16分到52分，蒋军F—84型战斗机2批8架次窜入平潭岛地区，向牛山海面和观音澳东1，500公尺海面上的渔船、民船轰炸扫射，投弹8枚，轮番扫射4次。炸沉莆田县涵江镇民船2只，炸死船员11人，伤3人。6日9时2分到31分，蒋军F—84型战斗机1批4架窜扰晋江县围头、永宁等地区上空。中国人民解放军高射炮部队当即以猛烈炮火截击，击伤2架。1架被击中机身和左翼，另1架被击中机头，拖着黑烟向东海方向逃去。5日16时8分，蒋军F—84型战斗机1批4架窜扰莆田县平海地区上空，被中国人民解放军高射炮部队击伤两架。
