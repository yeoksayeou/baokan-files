Title: Deng Xiaoping, Tan Zhenlin, and Wang Jiaxiang Arrive in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1956-02-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 12th: Deng Xiaoping, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Tan Zhenlin and Wang Jiaxiang, members of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, who are members of the delegation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to the upcoming 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, arrived in Moscow by plane on February 11th.
Delegation leader Zhu De and member Liu Xiao are already in Moscow.

### 邓小平、谭震林、王稼祥到达莫斯科

1956-02-13
第1版()
专栏：

    邓小平、谭震林、王稼祥到达莫斯科
    新华社莫斯科12日电　中国共产党中央委员会参加即将举行的苏联共产党第二十次代表大会的代表团团员，中共中央政治局委员邓小平、中共中央委员谭震林、王稼祥，已经在2月11日乘飞机到达莫斯科。
    代表团团长朱德、团员刘晓目前已经在莫斯科。
