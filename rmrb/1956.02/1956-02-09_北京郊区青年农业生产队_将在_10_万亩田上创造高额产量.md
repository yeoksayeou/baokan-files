Title: Youth Agricultural Production Teams in Beijing Suburbs to Create High Yields on 100,000 Mu of Land
Author:
Date: 1956-02-09
Page: 2

    Beijing suburban agricultural producers' cooperatives 568 youth production team leaders and technical instructors, at the Communist Youth League Beijing Municipal Committee held from the 4th to 7th, towards high-yield production mobilization meeting, said they would create high yields on 100,000 mu of land, to achieve the 1956 to 1967 National Agricultural Development Program (draft) proposed crop yield increase targets such as grain and cotton. They proposed that among them, 11,600 mu of corn, the average yield per mu should reach 1,000 jin, striving to reach 1,200 jin; more than 1,500 mu of sweet potatoes, each mu should produce 10,000 jin, striving to reach 12,000 jin; more than 4,600 mu of rice, each mu should produce 1,200 jin, striving to reach 1,500 jin; 939 mu of Chinese cabbage, the average yield per mu should produce 25,000 jin, striving to reach 30,000 jin.
    At the meeting, engineers and technicians from the Beijing Municipal Bureau of Agriculture, Forestry, and Water Conservancy explained in detail to the young production team members a complete set of technical measures for crops such as corn, cotton, vegetables, peanuts, sweet potatoes, and rice from planting to harvesting. The young production team members also exchanged advanced experiences in political and ideological work and increased production within the teams. The young production team leaders and technicians who attended the meeting guaranteed to thoroughly implement various technical measures to achieve the production increase plan.
    Luo Yi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and Zhao Fan, Minister of the Rural Work Department of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, both spoke at the meeting. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 北京郊区青年农业生产队  将在10万亩田上创造高额产量

1956-02-09
第2版()
专栏：

    北京郊区青年农业生产队
    将在10万亩田上创造高额产量
    北京郊区各农业生产合作社的568个青年生产队的队长和技术指导员，在青年团北京市委员会4日到7日召开的向高额产量进军动员大会上，表示要在10万亩土地上创造高额产量，实现1956年到1967年全国农业发展纲要（草案）提出的粮棉等作物的增产指标。他们提出其中11，600亩玉米，平均每亩产量要达到1，000斤，争取达到1，200斤；1，500多亩甘薯，每亩要产1万斤，争取达到12，000斤；4，600多亩水稻，每亩要产1，200斤，争取达到1，500斤；939亩白菜，平均每亩要产25，000斤，争取达到3万斤。
    会上，北京市农林水利局的工程师和技术人员给青年生产队员们详细地讲解了玉米、棉花、蔬菜、花生、甘薯、水稻等农作物从种到收的一整套技术措施。青年生产队员们也交流了队内的政治思想工作和增产的先进经验。到会的青年生产队队长和技术员保证要彻底贯彻各项技术措施，来实现增产计划。
    青年团中央书记处书记罗毅、中共北京市委农村工作部部长赵凡都在会上讲了话。　（新华社）
