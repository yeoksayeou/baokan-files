Page: 2

Hubei Province Cork Processing Plant Begins Production

    The Hubei Province Cork Processing Plant officially went into production on January 31st.

    This cork processing plant is a new type of factory equipped with machinery. It utilizes the abundant cork oak in Hubei Province as raw material to produce two products: cork bricks and cork paper. Cork bricks are the materials needed to manufacture refrigeration equipment on trains and ships, as well as in the food industry; cork paper is the material needed to manufacture tractors, automobiles, and many kinds of machines.
            (Hubei Daily Editorial Department)

### 湖北省软木加工厂开工生产

1956-02-13
第2版()
专栏：

    湖北省软木加工厂开工生产
    湖北省软木加工厂已经在1月31日正式投入生产。
    这座软木加工厂是用机械装备起来的新型工厂。它利用湖北省丰富的栓皮栎作原料，生产软木砖和软木纸两种产品。软木砖是制造火车和轮船上以及食品工业中的冷藏设备所需要的材料；软木纸是制造拖拉机、汽车和许多种机器所需要的材料。
            （湖北日报编辑部）
