Title: Shenyang City Mobilizes to Launch Advanced Producer Movement
Author:
Date: 1956-02-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, February 25th: Shenyang City held a broadcast rally for advanced producers tonight. More than 250,000 workers in more than 350 branch venues across the city listened to the live broadcast of the meeting.

    Jiao Ruoyu, Secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, delivered a report at the meeting. He pointed out that in the past month or two, 3,500 advanced producers and more than 200 advanced teams have emerged in the city, and more than 20,000 workers have exceeded their production quotas. Workers throughout the city have put forward more than 30,000 rationalization proposals. He called on workers, engineering and technical personnel, enterprise management personnel, and Party, trade union, and Communist Youth League organizations throughout the city to continue to overcome right-leaning conservative thinking, actively launch the advanced producer movement, use creative labor to greet the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China, and strive to complete the Five-Year Plan one year ahead of schedule. Jiao Ruoyu mobilized workers throughout the city to work hard to learn new technologies, master new technologies, improve technologies, promote a spirit of bold innovation, reform old technical standards and labor organization, break through all production technical quotas, and continuously strive for higher labor productivity.

    Lin Jitai, a labor model in Shenyang City, chief engineer Lü Qubing, and Wang Houshan, director of the Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Plant, also spoke at the meeting. They expressed their active participation in the advanced producer movement. Lin Jitai also assured the meeting that he would strive to implement the three technical improvement suggestions he had put forward, and work with all the workers in the workshop to strive to increase the production of crude copper by 3,000 tons and save 100 tons of heavy oil on the basis of completing this year's plan.

    The meeting read out the "Letter to All Workers in the City" proposed by the Shenyang Federation of Trade Unions.

    During the meeting, workers from more than 300 factories enthusiastically expressed their response to the call of the meeting, guaranteeing to complete the Five-Year Plan one year or more ahead of schedule.

### 沈阳市动员开展先进生产者运动

1956-02-26
第1版()
专栏：

    沈阳市动员开展先进生产者运动
    新华社沈阳25日电　沈阳市今天晚上召开了先进生产者广播大会。全市有350多个分会场25万多名职工，收听了大会实况广播。
    中共沈阳市委员会书记焦若愚，在会上作了报告。他指出，最近一两个月来，全市已经涌现出3，500名先进生产者和两百多个先进班组，有两万多工人突破了生产定额，全市职工提出的合理化建议有3万多件。他号召全市工人、工程技术人员、企业管理人员以及党、工会、青年团组织，继续克服右倾保守思想，积极开展先进生产者运动，以创造性的劳动，迎接中国共产党第八次代表大会，争取提前一年完成五年计划。焦若愚动员全市工人努力学习新技术，掌握新技术，改进技术，发扬大胆革新精神，改革旧的技术标准和劳动组织，突破一切生产技术定额，不断地为争取更高的劳动生产率而斗争。
    沈阳市劳动模范林继泰、总工程师吕去病和沈阳第一机床厂厂长王侯山也在会上讲了话。他们表示积极参加先进生产者运动。林继泰并且向大会保证：要努力实现他所提出的三项改进技术的建议，和全车间工人一起争取在完成今年计划的基础上，再增产3，000吨粗铜，节省重油100吨。
    大会宣读了沈阳市工会联合会提出的“告全市职工书”。
    大会进行期间，有300多个工厂的工人热烈表示响应大会的号召，保证提前一年或一年以上完成五年计划。
