Title:慰问团 Head Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-02-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 28th – The members of the Chinese People's Delegation to North Korea, led by the head of the delegation Wang Ziyi, returned to Beijing on the evening of the 28th.

Those who welcomed them at the station included: Xie Juezai, Chen Qiyuan, Yuan Renyuan, Yi Lirong, Zhang Weizhen, Cheng Hongyi, Liu Qiren, and others.

Choi Il, the North Korean ambassador to China, and embassy staff also welcomed them at the station.

### 赴朝慰问团总团回京

1956-02-29
第1版()
专栏：

    赴朝慰问团总团回京
    新华社28日讯　中国人民赴朝慰问团总团人员，在总团长王子宜率领下28日晚回到北京。
    到车站欢迎的有：谢觉哉、陈其瑗、袁任远、易礼容、张维桢、程宏毅、刘其人等。
    朝鲜驻中国大使崔一和使馆人员，也到车站欢迎。
