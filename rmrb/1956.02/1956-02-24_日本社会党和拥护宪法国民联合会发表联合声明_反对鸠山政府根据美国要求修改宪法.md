Title: The Japan Socialist Party and the National Federation for the Defense of the Constitution Issue a Joint Statement Opposing the Hatoyama Government's Revision of the Constitution at the Request of the United States
Author:
Date: 1956-02-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Osaka, 23rd. The Japan Socialist Party and the National Federation for the Defense of the Constitution held a press conference in Osaka on February 23rd, issuing a joint statement opposing the Hatoyama government's revision of the constitution.

The statement said: The Hatoyama government has displayed blatant activities, attempting to viciously and forcibly revise the constitution according to the demands of the United States. In order to realize this hope, the Hatoyama government wants to forcibly pass in this Diet session, with a majority, a small constituency system that conforms to the unilateral interests and strategies of one party.

The statement pointed out: If the ambition to revise the constitution is allowed to be realized, it will be tantamount to expanding the Emperor's authority again, reviving militarism in the name of the Emperor, and paving the way for a conscription system, making it possible to dispatch troops abroad as a vanguard for the United States. The statement said that the result of revising the constitution will be the revival of the feudal family system and a comprehensive threat to basic human rights. These consequences will not only threaten Japan's independence and peace as a whole, but will also fundamentally destroy the lives of the people.

The Socialist Party and the National Federation for the Defense of the Constitution expressed their determination to firmly unite all the forces of all peace-loving people to smash the conspiracy to revise the constitution.

### 日本社会党和拥护宪法国民联合会发表联合声明  反对鸠山政府根据美国要求修改宪法

1956-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

    日本社会党和拥护宪法国民联合会发表联合声明
    反对鸠山政府根据美国要求修改宪法
    新华社23日讯　大坂消息：日本社会党和日本拥护宪法国民联合会，2月23日在大坂举行记者招待会，发表反对鸠山政府修改宪法的联合声明。
    声明说：鸠山政府表现了露骨的活动，企图根据美国的要求恶劣地强行修改宪法。鸠山政府为了实现这个希望，想以多数在本届国会会议中强行通过符合于一党的单方面的利益和策略的小选举区制度。
    声明指出：如果容许修改宪法的野心实现，那就等于再度扩大天皇的权限，在天皇的名义下复活军国主义，并且将为征兵制度铺平道路，使替美国打前锋的派兵出国一事成为可能。声明说，修改宪法的结果将使封建家族制度复活，使基本人权遭到全面威胁。这些结果不仅整个威胁日本的独立和和平，并且将根本破坏国民的生活。
    社会党和拥护宪法国民联合会表示要坚决地团结爱好和平的全体国民的一切力量，粉碎修改宪法的阴谋。
