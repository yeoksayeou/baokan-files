The Qinghai Provincial Agricultural Comprehensive Experiment Station recently built a greenhouse on the plateau at an altitude of 2,300 meters. This is spring wheat suitable for planting in the Qinghai area, being cultivated in the greenhouse by the staff of the experiment station.
