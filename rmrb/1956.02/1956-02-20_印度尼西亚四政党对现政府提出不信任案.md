Title: Four Indonesian Parties File Motion of No Confidence Against Current Government
Author: 
Date: 1956-02-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 16th: According to Antara News Agency, the Indonesian Nationalist Party, the Islamic Teachers Association, the Indonesian Islamic Union Party, and the Perti Islamic Party jointly submitted a motion of no confidence in the Harahap Cabinet to the Indonesian Parliament on February 16th. The report stated that the four parties are dissatisfied with the economic, financial, and foreign policies implemented by the Harahap Cabinet.

Prior to this, five members of parliament submitted a motion to the parliament on February 15th, requesting the Harahap Cabinet to resign as soon as possible and to hand over all important issues arising from the failure of the negotiations with the Netherlands to a new government formed based on the results of universal suffrage. The motion was signed by members of the Greater Indonesian Unity Party, the Indonesian People's League Party, and the Progressive faction.

### 印度尼西亚四政党对现政府提出不信任案

1956-02-20
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚四政党对现政府提出不信任案
    新华社雅加达16日电　据安塔拉通讯社报道，印度尼西亚国民党、回教师联合会、印度尼西亚回教联盟党和白尔蒂回教党四个政党在2月16日联合向印度尼西亚国会提出表示不信任哈拉哈普内阁的议案。消息说，议案中指出，这四个政党不满意哈拉哈普内阁所实行的经济、财政和外交政策。
    在这以前，五位国会议员在2月15日曾经向国会提出动议，要求哈拉哈普内阁尽快辞职，并且把由于对荷兰谈判的失败而产生的，所有重要问题交由根据普选结果组成的新政府处理。在动议上签名的是大印度尼西亚统一党、印度尼西亚人民联盟党和进步系的议员。
