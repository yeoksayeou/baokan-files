Title: Sino-Mongolian Agreement on Mutual Supply of Goods for 1956 Signed in Ulaanbaatar
Author:
Date: 1956-02-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, February 7th: After sincere and friendly negotiations, the trade delegations of the People's Republic of China and the Mongolian People's Republic reached a complete agreement, and on February 7, 1956, in Ulaanbaatar, signed the Sino-Mongolian Agreement on Mutual Supply of Goods for 1956. The agreement stipulates that the trade volume between the two countries in 1956 will be doubled compared to 1955.

The agreement stipulates that China will supply Mongolia with rice, dried and fresh fruits, tobacco, silk fabrics and other daily necessities, while Mongolia will supply China with horses, hides and other livestock products.

He Ying, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Mongolian People's Republic, signed the agreement on behalf of the People's Republic of China. Batar, Minister of Trade of the Mongolian People's Republic, signed the agreement on behalf of the Mongolian People's Republic.

### 中蒙1956年互供货物议定书在乌兰巴托签字

1956-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    中蒙1956年互供货物议定书在乌兰巴托签字
    新华社7日讯　乌兰巴托消息：中华人民共和国贸易代表团和蒙古人民共和国贸易代表团经过诚挚友好的谈判之后，圆满地达成了协议，并且在1956年2月7日在乌兰巴托签订了中蒙1956年互供货物议定书，议定书规定1956年两国间的贸易额比1955年增加一倍。
    议定书规定中国方面将供应蒙古大米、干鲜果品、烟、丝织品和其他生活必需品等，蒙古方面将供应中国马匹、皮张和其他畜产品等。
    代表中华人民共和国方面在议定书上签字的是中华人民共和国驻蒙古人民共和国特命全权大使何英。代表蒙古人民共和国方面在议定书上签字的是蒙古人民共和国贸易部部长巴塔尔。
