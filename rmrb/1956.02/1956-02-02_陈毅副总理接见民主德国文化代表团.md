Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives the Cultural Delegation from the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1956-02-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received the cultural delegation from the German Democratic Republic on the morning of February 1st. The delegation was led by G. Harrich, and included members K. Wiese, E. Hoffmann, and secretary A. Pritzke. The delegation came to China to discuss and sign the 1956 executive plan of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

Present at the reception were Xia Yan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, Zeng Zhaolun, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Higher Education, He Wei, Assistant Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Liaison.

Also present at the reception was Eberhard, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China.

### 陈毅副总理接见民主德国文化代表团

1956-02-02
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见民主德国文化代表团
    新华社1日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅，在1日上午接见了来中国商谈和签订中德文化合作协定1956年执行计划的德意志民主共和国文化代表团团长格·哈里希，团员卡·维塞、恩·霍夫曼，代表团秘书爱·普莉琦。
    接见的时候，在座的有中华人民共和国文化部副部长夏衍，高等教育部副部长曾昭抡，外交部部长助理何伟，对外文化联络局代理局长陈忠经。
    接见的时候，德意志民主共和国驻华大使馆临时代办埃弗哈而茨也在座。
