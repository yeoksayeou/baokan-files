Title: Pakistani Foreign Minister Says Decision Made to Negotiate Trade Agreement with the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-02-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, February 10th: Pakistani Foreign Minister Chaudhri said on February 9th that Pakistan has decided to negotiate a trade agreement with the Soviet Union.

Chaudhri said that he believes there is no harm in establishing trade relations with socialist countries, if such relations are not attached to any conditions.

### 巴基斯坦外交部长说  决定同苏联谈判贸易协定

1956-02-11
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦外交部长说
    决定同苏联谈判贸易协定
    新华社10日讯　喀喇蚩消息：巴基斯坦外交部长乔德里2月9日说，巴基斯坦已经决定同苏联谈判缔结贸易协定。
    乔德里说，他认为，同社会主义国家建立贸易关系是没有害处的，如果这种关系不附有任何条件的话。
