Title: All-India Peace Council Calls for "Disarmament Fortnight"
Author:
Date: 1956-02-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, February 1st: The All-India Peace Council has called for a "Disarmament Fortnight" to be held starting from February 1st.

Dr. Kitchlew, Chairman of the All-India Peace Council, issued a statement calling on the people to actively support the demands for gradual disarmament and the prohibition of nuclear weapons mentioned in the joint statement by Bulganin, Khrushchev, and Nehru.

The statement said: "In order to eliminate suspicion and hatred between countries, especially great powers, in order to enable people to use the many existing technical knowledge for construction, and in order to eradicate poverty and distress from the world, gradual disarmament is what the world urgently needs today."

Many prominent Indian leaders have sent telegrams to congratulate the All-India Peace Council on its decision to hold the "Disarmament Fortnight."

### 全印和平理事会号召举行“要求裁军双周”

1956-02-03
第4版()
专栏：

    全印和平理事会号召举行“要求裁军双周”
    据新华社新德里1日电　全印和平理事会号召从2月1日起举行“要求裁军双周”。
    全印和平理事会主席克其鲁博士发表声明，呼吁人民积极支持布尔加宁、赫鲁晓夫和尼赫鲁联合声明中提到的争取逐步裁军和禁止核子武器的要求。
    声明说：“为了消除国与国之间、特别是大国之间的猜疑和仇恨，为了使人们能够以现有的许多技术知识用于建设方面，为了从世界上铲除贫穷和困苦，逐步裁军是今天世界上所迫切需要的事”。
    印度许多著名领袖已经致电祝贺全印和平理事会关于举行“要求裁军双周”的决定。
