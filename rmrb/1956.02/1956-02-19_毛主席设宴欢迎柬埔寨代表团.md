Title: Chairman Mao Hosts Banquet Welcoming Cambodian Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-02-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 18th – Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China hosted a banquet this evening to welcome His Royal Highness Prince Norodom Sihanouk of the Kingdom of Cambodia, Prime Minister, and the National Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia he led.

Prince Sihanouk and all members of the National Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia were invited to attend the banquet.

Also attending the banquet were Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council; Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Li Weihan, and Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Peng Zhen, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Mayor of Beijing; Peng Dehuai, Chen Yi, and Li Fuchun, Vice-Premiers of the State Council; Zhang Dingcheng, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate; Marshal Ye Jianying, Cheng Qian, Fu Zuoyi, and Long Yun, Vice-Chairmen of the National Defense Council; He Xiangning, Zhang Bojun, and Bao Erhan, Vice-Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Zhang Wentian and Zhang Hanfu, Vice-Ministers of Foreign Affairs; as well as directors of various offices of the State Council, ministers of various ministries, chairmen of various commissions, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and responsible persons of the Buddhist Association of China.

Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, and his wife were also invited to attend the banquet. (Picture Attached)

### 毛主席设宴欢迎柬埔寨代表团

1956-02-19
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席设宴欢迎柬埔寨代表团
    新华社18日讯　中华人民共和国毛泽东主席今晚举行宴会，欢迎柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王殿下和他所率领的柬埔寨王国国家代表团。
    西哈努克亲王和柬埔寨王国国家代表团全体团员都应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会作陪的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、李维汉、陈叔通，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、北京市市长彭真，国务院副总理彭德怀、陈毅、李富春，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，国防委员会副主席叶剑英元帅、程潜、傅作义、龙云，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席何香凝、章伯钧、鲍尔汉，外交部副部长张闻天、章汉夫，以及国务院各办公室主任，各部部长，各委员会主任，中国人民解放军高级将领和中国佛教协会的负责人。
    印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁和夫人也应邀出席了宴会。（附图片）
