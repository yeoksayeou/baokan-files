Title: Jiangxi and Shandong Deploy and Implement the National Agricultural Development Program; The Anhui Provincial Committee of the CPC Transfers Cadres to the Countryside to Publicize and Solicit Opinions
Author:
Date: 1956-02-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang - The Jiangxi Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China recently held a provincial committee meeting to discuss the "National Agricultural Development Program (Draft)" from 1956 to 1967 proposed by the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee. Also participating in the meeting were the heads of the CPC Gannnan District Committee and all prefectural and municipal committees in the province.

After a heated discussion, the meeting unanimously agreed that the Central Committee's proposal of this "National Agricultural Development Program (Draft)" at the present time is completely correct and timely. It presents the people of the whole country, especially the peasants, with a fighting goal and shows them a grand vision. The meeting requested that Party organizations at all levels in the province use various forms to extensively carry out propaganda activities, mobilizing the people to better strive to complete the country's first Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule. The meeting also requested that state administrative organs at all levels in the province universally organize staff to study the "Agricultural Development Program (Draft)" in order to further improve the work of economic, cultural, educational, health, political, and other departments to meet the requirements of the new situation.

The meeting requested that leading organs at all levels above the county level in the province formulate plans for implementing the "National Agricultural Development Program (Draft)" based on local conditions. The meeting requested that Party organizations at all levels focus on the current work centered on production, organize competitions, and promote advanced experiences, striving to complete the Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule and lay a favorable foundation for the realization of the 12-year agricultural development program (draft) in the future.

The meeting also called for widespread solicitation of opinions from people from all walks of life on the "National Agricultural Development Program (Draft)" for the next 12 years.

Jinan, this newspaper, 31st - The Shandong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Shandong Provincial People's Committee issued a notice on January 29 regarding the implementation of the "National Agricultural Development Program (Draft) from 1956 to 1967" proposed by the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee.

The notice stipulates that Party and government organizations at all levels in the province, as well as all Party members and state workers, must actively and conscientiously study and research the "National Agricultural Development Program (Draft)," and actively lead farmers to study and implement the requirements in the program in a planned and step-by-step manner. Party and government organizations and propaganda departments at all levels in the province must immediately promote this program widely and deeply to the people of the province, especially the peasants, to further enhance the socialist enthusiasm of the masses. Party and government leading organs at all levels in the province should, in accordance with the requirements of this program and the specific conditions of the locality, formulate specific plans for the phased development of various tasks in the locality, and at the same time, help agricultural production cooperatives formulate or revise long-term plans for the development of agricultural production. The notice also called for widespread solicitation of opinions from people from all walks of life on the draft program.

On January 31, the Shandong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China also convened a cadre meeting of more than 2,000 people from provincial-level agencies. Tan Qilong, Second Secretary of the Shandong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, made a mobilization report on studying and implementing the "National Agricultural Development Program (Draft)."

The Shandong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China has established a special committee composed of 9 people to lead the province in researching and formulating agricultural development plans.

Xinhua News Agency, Hefei - The Anhui Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China has transferred 107 cadres at the division chief level and above from the agencies directly under the Provincial Committee and the Provincial People's Committee to the countryside to publicize the 12-year "National Agricultural Development Program (Draft)." These cadres have now arrived at many agricultural production cooperatives in the southern part of the Yangtze River, the southern part of the Huai River, and the northern part of the Huai River, carefully explaining the content of the "National Agricultural Development Program (Draft)" to the members and soliciting their opinions.

### 江西山东布置实施全国农业发展纲要  中共安徽省委抽调干部下乡宣传并征求意见

1956-02-02
第1版()
专栏：

    江西山东布置实施全国农业发展纲要
    中共安徽省委抽调干部下乡宣传并征求意见
    据新华社南昌电　中共江西省委员会最近举行省委会议，讨论了中共中央政治局提出的1956年到1967年全国农业发展纲要（草案）。参加这个会议的还有中共赣南区党委和全省各地委、市委负责人。
    会议经过热烈讨论后，一致认为：党中央在当前提出这个全国农业发展纲要（草案）是完全正确的，适时的，它给全国人民特别是农民提出了战斗的目标，并在他们面前展示出了宏伟的远景。会议要求全省各级党组织要利用多种多样的形式广泛地开展宣传活动，动员人民群众更好地为提前完成国家第一个五年计划而奋斗。会议还要求全省各级国家行政机关普遍组织工作人员学习农业发展纲要（草案），以便进一步改善经济、文教卫生、政治等各部门的工作，适应新形势的要求。
    会议要求全省县以上各级领导机关根据当地的具体情况制定出实施全国农业发展纲要（草案）方面的规划。会议要求各级党组织抓住当前以生产为中心的各项工作，组织竞赛，推广先进经验，力争提前完成五年计划，为实现今后12年农业发展纲要（草案）打下有利的基础。
    会议还要求广泛地征求各界人民对今后12年全国农业发展纲要（草案）的意见。
    本报济南31日电　中国共产党山东省委员会和山东省人民委员会在1月29日发出关于贯彻执行中共中央政治局提出的“1956年到1967年全国农业发展纲要（草案）”的通知。
    通知中规定：全省各级党政组织和全体党员、国家工作人员，必须积极认真地学习和研究全国农业发展纲要（草案），并且积极领导农民有计划、有步骤地学习和贯彻实施纲要中的各项要求；全省各级党政组织和各个宣传部门必须立即向全省人民，首先是农民广泛而深入地宣传这一纲要，以进一步提高群众的社会主义积极性；全省各级党政领导机关应该根据这一纲要的要求和本地的具体条件，分别对当地各项工作进行分批分期发展的具体规划，同时帮助农业生产合作社拟订或修订发展农业生产的长期规划；省级各个经济、科学、文化、教育、卫生、政法等部门也应该根据纲要草案重新审定自己的工作规划。通知还要求广泛地征求各界人民对纲要草案的意见。
    1月31日，中共山东省委员会还召开了省级机关2，000多人的干部大会。中共山东省委第二书记谭启龙作了关于学习研究和贯彻执行全国农业发展纲要（草案）的动员报告。
    中共山东省委员会已经成立了由9人组成的领导全省研究和制定农业发展规划的专门委员会。
    新华社合肥电　中共安徽省委员会，在省委和省人民委员会直属机关中抽调了107名处长级以上的干部，下乡宣传12年的全国农业发展纲要（草案）。现在这些干部已经分别到达长江南部、淮河南部和淮河北部地区的很多农业生产合作社，向社员们仔细地讲解全国农业发展纲要（草案）的内容，并且征求社员的意见。
