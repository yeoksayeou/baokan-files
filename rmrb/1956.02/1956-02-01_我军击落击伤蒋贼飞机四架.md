Title: Our Army Shot Down and Damaged Four Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Airplanes
Author:
Date: 1956-02-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front Dispatch: On January 27th, the People's Liberation Army anti-aircraft artillery units on the Fujian Front shot down two Chiang Kai-shek bandit airplanes and damaged two others.

　　At 9:33 AM on the 27th, a total of 16 Chiang Kai-shek F-84 jet fighter planes in 4 batches intruded into the Weitou area of Jinjiang County, Fujian Province. Our anti-aircraft artillery units intercepted them with fierce artillery fire, first hitting one of the Chiang Kai-shek airplanes in the first batch. The enemy plane was hit in the head and tail, emitting black smoke, and crashed into the sea south of Beiding Island. Then, the anti-aircraft gunners accurately hit the wings of another Chiang Kai-shek airplane, which caught fire and crashed into the sea west of Beiding Island. The anti-aircraft gunners then damaged two more Chiang Kai-shek airplanes.

### 我军击落击伤蒋贼飞机四架

1956-02-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我军击落击伤蒋贼飞机四架
    据新华社福建前线电　福建前线中国人民解放军高射炮部队在1月27日击落蒋贼飞机两架，击伤两架。
    27日上午9时33分，蒋贼F—84型喷气战斗机共4批16架次窜扰福建省晋江县围头地区。我军高射炮部队以猛烈炮火截击，首先击中第一批蒋贼飞机当中的一架，敌机头部和尾部都中弹，冒起黑烟，坠毁在北碇岛南面的海里。接着，高射炮手们又以准确的炮火击中另一架蒋贼飞机的两翼，燃烧起火，坠毁在北碇岛以西的海里。高射炮手们跟着又击伤蒋贼飞机两架。
