Title: Indonesian Political Parties and People Demand Harahap Cabinet Resignation
Author:
Date: 1956-02-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 27th - According to Antara News Agency reports, the Indonesian Nationalist Party and the Nahdatul Ulama party jointly notified the Masyumi Party on the morning of February 27th, requesting it to arrange for the cabinet to resign before March 2nd. The Masyumi Party is the main party forming the current cabinet.

The report said that after that, Prime Minister Harahap immediately held a three-party meeting with the leaders of the Indonesian Nationalist Party and the Nahdatul Ulama party. The report said that the meeting did not reach an agreement.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 27th - According to Antara News Agency reports, an anti-colonial mass rally was held in Medan on the morning of February 26th. The conference passed a resolution opposing any intention to return the North Sumatra oil wells to the Bataafsche Petroleum Maatschappij, and demanded that the president dissolve the Harahap cabinet if it did not resign voluntarily by March 1st.

The conference believes that the unity of the people's strength, supported by Asian and African countries, is the only way to liberate West Irian. It called for restricting foreign monopoly capital and protecting national enterprises.

Local leaders of the Indonesian Nationalist Party, the Indonesian Communist Party, and the Indonesian Islamic Union Party spoke at the conference.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 27th - According to Antara News Agency reports, the Indonesian People's Youth League, which has more than 700,000 members, issued a statement on February 27th, expressing strong opposition to US Secretary of State Dulles' visit to Indonesia.

The statement called on Indonesian youth and students to further consolidate their unity and once again demanded the resignation of the Harahap cabinet.

### 印度尼西亚政党和人民要求哈拉哈普内阁辞职

1956-02-29
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚政党和人民要求哈拉哈普内阁辞职
    新华社雅加达27日电　据安塔拉通讯社报道，印度尼西亚国民党和回教师联合会在2月27日上午向马斯友美党提出联合通知，要求它安排在3月2日以前使内阁辞职。马斯友美党是组成目前内阁的主要政党。
    消息说，在那以后，哈拉哈普总理立刻同印度尼西亚国民党和回教师联合会的领袖举行三党会议。消息说，会议结果没有取得协议。
    新华社雅加达27日电　据安塔拉通讯社报道，2月26日早晨在棉兰举行了反殖民主义群众大会。大会通过一项决议，反对把北苏门答腊的油井归还给巴塔夫石油公司的任何意图，并且要求总统解散哈拉哈普内阁，如果它到3月1日还不自行辞职的话。
    大会认为，有着亚非国家的支持的人民力量的团结是解放西伊里安的唯一办法。它要求限制外国的垄断资本和保护民族企业。
    在大会上发言的有印度尼西亚国民党、印度尼西亚共产党和印度尼西亚回教联盟党在当地的领袖们。
    新华社雅加达27日电　据安塔拉通讯社报道，拥有70多万团员的印度尼西亚人民青年团在2月27日发表声明，表示强烈反对美国务卿杜勒斯到印度尼西亚访问。
    声明号召印度尼西亚青年和学生进一步巩固他们的团结，并且再一次要求哈拉哈普内阁辞职。
