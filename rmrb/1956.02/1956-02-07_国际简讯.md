　　* International Briefs *
　　△ Soviet Minister of Culture Mikhailov arrived in London on the 1st at the invitation of the British Council's Committee for Relations with the Soviet Union. He will discuss with representatives of the British Council the further development of scientific, cultural, and artistic exchanges between Britain and the Soviet Union and will learn about British science and cultural life.
　　△ British Prime Minister Eden and Foreign Secretary Lloyd arrived in Ottawa, the capital of Canada, by plane on the 3rd after the Anglo-American talks in Washington. They will meet with Canadian government leaders during their visit to Ottawa.
　　△ The King and Queen of Iran will arrive in New Delhi on February 16 for a three-week visit to India.

### 国际简讯

1956-02-07
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    ·国际简讯·
    △苏联文化部长米哈伊洛夫应英国文化委员会对苏关系委员会的邀请，在1日到达了伦敦。他将同英国文化委员会的代表们讨论进一步发展英、苏两国科学、文化和艺术交流的问题，并且了解英国的科学和文化生活。
    △英国首相艾登和外交大臣劳埃德在英美会谈结束以后，在3日先后由华盛顿乘飞机到达加拿大首都渥太华。他们将在访问渥太华期间同加拿大政府领袖会谈。
    △伊朗国王和王后将在2月16日到达新德里，对印度作3个星期的访问。
