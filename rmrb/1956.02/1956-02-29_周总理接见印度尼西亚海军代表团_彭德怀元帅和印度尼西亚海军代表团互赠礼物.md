Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Indonesian Naval Delegation; Marshal Peng Dehuai and the Indonesian Naval Delegation Exchange Gifts
Author:
Date: 1956-02-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 28th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council received Rear Admiral Subianto and his wife, along with all members of the Indonesian Naval Delegation visiting China, at 5 p.m. on the 28th.

    Present at the reception were Marshal Peng Dehuai, Naval Admiral Xiao Jingguang and his wife, Grand General Su Yu, Senior General Li Kenong, Senior General Gan Siqi, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Major General Zhang Xuesi, Ambassador Huang Zhen, and Zhang Wenjin.

    Also present at the reception were Chargé d'affaires ad interim Anwa Sani and his wife from the Indonesian Embassy in China.

    Xinhua News Agency, February 28th: Rear Admiral Subianto, Chief of Staff of the Indonesian Navy, and his wife, along with the Indonesian Naval Delegation visiting China led by him, paid a courtesy call on Marshal Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, at 3:30 p.m. on the 28th and presented gifts.

    During the presentation of gifts, Rear Admiral Subianto said in his speech: "We are deeply grateful for your warm hospitality, and we take this opportunity to present you with a small gift as a memento."

    Marshal Peng Dehuai expressed his gratitude in his speech. He said: "On behalf of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, I extend profound friendly respect to the Indonesian Navy and the entire Indonesian armed forces. I believe we are not temporary friends, but friends forever."

    Subsequently, Marshal Peng Dehuai presented gifts on behalf of the Chinese People's Liberation Army to the guests. Rear Admiral Subianto expressed his deep gratitude again on behalf of the entire delegation.

    Also present during the exchange of gifts were Naval Admiral Xiao Jingguang, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, and Naval Major General Zhang Xuesi, among others. (Attached picture)

### 周总理接见印度尼西亚海军代表团  彭德怀元帅和印度尼西亚海军代表团互赠礼物

1956-02-29
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见印度尼西亚海军代表团
    彭德怀元帅和印度尼西亚海军代表团互赠礼物
    新华社28日讯　国务院总理周恩来在28日下午5时接见了苏比雅克托海军少将和夫人以及印度尼西亚海军访华代表团全体人员。
    接见的时候，在座的有彭德怀元帅、萧劲光海军大将和夫人、粟裕大将、李克农上将、甘泗淇上将、萧向荣中将、张学思少将、黄镇大使和章文晋。
    接见的时候，印度尼西亚驻华大使馆临时代办安瓦·萨尼和夫人也在座。
    新华社28日讯　印度尼西亚海军参谋长苏比雅克托海军少将和夫人以及他所率领的印度尼西亚海军访华代表团，在28日下午3时半拜会中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀元帅，并赠送礼物。
    赠礼时，苏比雅克托海军少将致词说：“承阁下对我们热情招待，趁这个机会我们向你送上一点礼物，作为一种纪念。”
    彭德怀元帅致词表示感谢。他说：“我代表中国人民解放军向印度尼西亚海军及印度尼西亚全国武装力量表示深厚的友谊的敬意。我想，我们不是暂时的朋友。而是永远的朋友。”
    接着，彭德怀元帅代表中国人民解放军向客人们赠礼。苏比雅克托海军少将代表代表团全体人员再次表示深深的谢意。
    互赠礼物时，在座的有萧劲光海军大将、萧向荣中将和张学思海军少将等人。（附图片）
