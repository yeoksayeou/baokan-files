Title: U.S. Communist Party Chairman Foster's 75th Birthday: Telegram of Congratulations from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1956-02-24
Page: 1
Column:

    U.S. Communist Party Chairman Foster's 75th Birthday
    Telegram of Congratulations from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
    (Reported by this newspaper) The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram to Comrade William Foster, Chairman of the National Committee of the Communist Party of the United States, on his 75th birthday. The telegram reads as follows: Dear Comrade William Foster,
    On the occasion of your 75th birthday, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm congratulations to you, the leader of the Communist Party of the United States and the American working people.
    We congratulate the Communist Party of the United States on its new achievements in the struggle to safeguard world peace, strive for domestic democracy, and protect the interests of the working people.
    We wish you good health and longevity.
                                  The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
                                 February 23, 1956

### 美国共产党主席福斯特75岁寿辰  中国共产党中央委员会致电祝贺

1956-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

    美国共产党主席福斯特75岁寿辰
    中国共产党中央委员会致电祝贺
    本报讯　中国共产党中央委员会电贺美国共产党全国委员会主席威廉·福斯特同志75岁寿辰。电文如下：亲爱的威廉·福斯特同志：
    当您75岁寿辰之际，中国共产党中央委员会谨向您——美国共产党和美国劳动人民的领袖致以热烈的祝贺。
    我们祝贺美国共产党在维护世界和平，争取国内民主和保护劳动人民利益的斗争中取得新的成就。
    祝您健康长寿。
                                  中国共产党中央委员会
                                 1956年2月23日
