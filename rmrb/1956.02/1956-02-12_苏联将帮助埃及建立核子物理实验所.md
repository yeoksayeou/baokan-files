Title: The Soviet Union Will Help Egypt Establish a Nuclear Physics Laboratory

Author: 
Date: 1956-02-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th - TASS reports: Negotiations between the Soviet and Egyptian Republic delegations in Moscow have recently concluded. The two delegations discussed the Soviet Union's provision of scientific and technical assistance to help Egypt establish a nuclear physics laboratory in Cairo, as well as helping it to peacefully utilize atomic energy and other issues.

According to the agreement, the Soviet Union will send experts to Egypt to serve as consultants for Egypt's design and construction of the aforementioned laboratory, supply Egypt with the equipment and instruments needed for this laboratory, and will receive Egyptian scientists and experts to study at corresponding scientific research institutions in the Soviet Union. In addition, the Soviet Union will also supply Egypt with equipment and instruments for geological exploration.

### 苏联将帮助埃及建立核子物理实验所

1956-02-12
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联将帮助埃及建立核子物理实验所
    新华社11日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科10日讯：苏联和埃及共和国代表团在莫斯科举行的谈判已经在最近结束，两国代表团商谈了苏联提供科学和技术援助以便帮助埃及在开罗建立一所核子物理实验所，以及帮助它和平利用原子能等问题。
    根据协议，苏联将派专家到埃及充当埃及进行上述实验所的设计和建设工作的顾问，向埃及供应这个实验所所需的装备和仪器，并且将接待埃及的科学家和专家到苏联相应的科学研究机构进修。此外，苏联还将向埃及供应地质勘查用的装备和仪器。
