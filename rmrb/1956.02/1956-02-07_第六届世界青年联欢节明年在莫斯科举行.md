Title: Sixth World Youth Festival to be Held in Moscow Next Year
Author:
Date: 1956-02-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, 6th. TASS reports: The Executive Committee of the World Federation of Democratic Youth held a four-day meeting in Helsinki from February 1st. Participating in the meeting were members of the Executive Committee and representatives of youth organizations from 31 countries.

    In a resolution adopted by the Executive Committee, it was suggested that the Sixth World Youth and Student Festival be held in Moscow from July 28th to August 11th, 1957. It is estimated that approximately 30,000 young men and women from all strata, with different beliefs and viewpoints, will participate in this festival.

    The Executive Committee invited many international organizations to join the World Federation of Democratic Youth and the International Union of Students in the International Preparatory Committee for the festival. The Executive Committee also suggested that the first meeting of the International Preparatory Committee be held in Moscow in August 1956.

    The Executive Committee also decided to convene a regular meeting of the Council of the World Federation of Democratic Youth in Sofia in August 1956.

### 第六届世界青年联欢节明年在莫斯科举行

1956-02-07
第4版()
专栏：

    第六届世界青年联欢节明年在莫斯科举行
    新华社6日讯　据塔斯社赫尔辛基讯：世界民主青年联盟执行委员会从2月1日起在赫尔辛基举行了4天会议。参加会议的有执行委员会委员和31个国家的青年团体的代表。
    执行委员会在通过的决议中，建议在1957年7月28日到8月11日在莫斯科举行第六届世界青年与学生联欢节，估计参加这一联欢节的约有3万个属于各个阶层和具有不同信仰和观点的青年男女。
    执行委员会邀请许多国际团体同世界民主青年联盟和国际学生联合会一起参加举行联欢节的国际筹备委员会。执行委员会并且建议在1956年8月在莫斯科召开国际筹备委员会第一次会议。
    执行委员会还决定在1956年8月在索非亚召开世界民主青年联盟理事会常会。
