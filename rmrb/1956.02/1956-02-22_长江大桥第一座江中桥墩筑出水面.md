Title: The First River Pier of the Yangtze River Bridge Rises Above the Water

Author:
Date: 1956-02-22
Page: 1

    The first river pier of the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge rose above the Yangtze River's surface on the 20th. Construction of this pier began on March 15th of last year. It is the first of the eight piers in the river's center to be built, and the famous new "caisson drilling" construction method was also first tested on this pier.
    Over the past 11 months, builders, with the help of Soviet experts, have carried out a complete set of construction procedures, from sinking the steel cofferdam to pouring the concrete for the pier body. They have accumulated a wealth of experience during the construction process and have proven the superiority of the new construction method. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 长江大桥第一座江中桥墩筑出水面

1956-02-22
第1版()
专栏：

    长江大桥第一座江中桥墩筑出水面
    武汉长江大桥第一座江中桥墩在20日筑出长江水面。这个桥墩是在去年3月15日开工修筑的。它是江心8个桥墩中最先施工的一个，著名的“管柱钻孔”新施工法也首先在这个桥墩上进行试验性的运用。
    11个月来，建筑者在苏联专家帮助下，从下沉钢围囹到浇筑桥墩墩身混凝土进行了一整套的施工程序。他们在施工中积累了许多经验，并且证明新施工法的优越性。（新华社）
