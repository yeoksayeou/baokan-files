Title: Thai People's Promotion of Friendship Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:
Date: 1956-02-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 13th dispatch. The Thai People's Promotion of Friendship Delegation, headed by Nai Dhip Thepnimit, concluded its visit to Beijing and left Beijing by plane on the 13th to return home. Responsible individuals from the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs and the Buddhist Association of China went to the airport to see them off.

On the evening of the 12th, Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, held a banquet to bid farewell to the delegation.

The delegation will visit Nanjing and other places on its way home.

### 泰国人民促进友好访华团离京回国

1956-02-14
第4版()
专栏：

    泰国人民促进友好访华团离京回国
    新华社13日讯　以乃贴·触的努七为首的泰国人民促进友好访华团结束了在北京的参观访问，在13日乘飞机离开北京回国。中国人民外交学会、中国佛教协会的负责人曾到机场欢送。
    12日晚间，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若曾举行宴会欢送访华团。
    访华团在回国途中将访问南京等地。
