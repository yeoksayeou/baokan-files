Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives French Economic Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1956-02-17
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, received the French Economic Delegation to China, headed by Henri Rochereau, this afternoon.
    Present at the meeting were Lei Renmin, Ji Chao Ding, Vice-Chairmen of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Lu Xuzhang, a member of the Council, and Huang Hua, Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. (Attached picture)
    Premier Zhou Enlai talks with Henri Rochereau, head of the French Economic Delegation to China.
                                      Xinhua News Agency reporter Tan Peizhang.

### 周总理接见法国访华经济代表团

1956-02-17
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见法国访华经济代表团
    新华社16日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，在今天下午接见了以亨利·罗谢罗为首的法国访华经济代表团。
    接见时在座的，有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民、冀朝鼎和委员卢绪章，外交部西欧非洲司司长黄华。（附图片）
    周恩来总理同法国访华经济代表团团长亨利·罗谢罗谈话。
                                      新华社记者　谈培章摄
