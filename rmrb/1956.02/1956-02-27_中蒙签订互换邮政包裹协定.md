Title: China and Mongolia Sign Postal Parcel Exchange Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-02-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, February 26th: A postal parcel exchange agreement between China and Mongolia was signed on February 25th in Ulaanbaatar, the capital of the Mongolian People's Republic. Wang Zigang, head of the Chinese government postal delegation and Vice Minister of Posts and Telecommunications, signed on behalf of the People's Republic of China. Qiaomidaoerji, head of the Mongolian government postal delegation and Vice Minister of Posts and Telecommunications, signed on behalf of the Mongolian People's Republic.

Present at the signing ceremony on the Chinese side was Zheng Dechang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the People's Republic of China in the Mongolian People's Republic; on the Mongolian side were Maidar, Deputy Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, and Dakinzhabu, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications.

### 中蒙签订互换邮政包裹协定

1956-02-27
第1版()
专栏：

    中蒙签订互换邮政包裹协定
    新华社26日讯　乌兰巴托消息：中蒙两国互换邮政包裹协定2月25日在蒙古人民共和国首都乌兰巴托市签字。代表中华人民共和国方面签字的是中国政府邮电代表团团长、邮电部副部长王子纲。代表蒙古人民共和国方面签字的是蒙古政府邮电代表团团长、邮电部副部长乔米德道尔基。
    出席签字仪式的，中国方面有中华人民共和国驻蒙古人民共和国临时代办郑德昌；蒙古方面有蒙古人民共和国副总理迈达尔，邮电部长达京扎布。
