Title: Ye Jizhuang Hosts Czechoslovak Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-02-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, February 25th: Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang held a banquet on the evening of the 24th to host the Czechoslovak government delegation headed by Grigal, which came to China to preside over the opening of the Exhibition of Achievements in 10 Years of Socialist Construction in Czechoslovakia in Guangzhou.
    The Czechoslovak government delegation left Beijing for Guangzhou by train on the 25th.

### 叶季壮招待捷政府代表团

1956-02-26
第1版()
专栏：

    叶季壮招待捷政府代表团
    据新华社25日讯　对外贸易部部长叶季壮24日晚举行宴会，招待来我国主持广州捷克斯洛伐克10年社会主义建设成就展览会开幕的以格里哥尔为团长的捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团。
    捷政府代表团已在25日乘车离北京前往广州。
