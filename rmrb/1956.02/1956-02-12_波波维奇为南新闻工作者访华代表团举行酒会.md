Title: Popović Holds Reception for Yugoslav Journalists Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1956-02-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 11th -- Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popović, held a reception on the afternoon of the 11th at 5 p.m. for the Yugoslav journalists delegation currently visiting China.

The entire Yugoslav journalists delegation to China, headed by Pirpić, attended today's reception.

Those invited to attend included Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Deng Tuo, President of the All-China Journalists Association, Wu Lengxi, Mei Yi, and Wang Yunsheng, Vice Presidents, Xu Huang, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, heads of various newspapers and periodicals in Beijing, Chinese and foreign journalists, and over 100 diplomatic officials from various countries' embassies in China.

The reception proceeded in a friendly and warm atmosphere.

### 波波维奇为南新闻工作者访华代表团举行酒会

1956-02-12
第1版()
专栏：

    波波维奇为南新闻工作者访华代表团举行酒会
    新华社11日讯　南斯拉夫驻华大使波波维奇为正在中国访问的南斯拉夫新闻工作者访华代表团，在11日下午5时举行酒会。
    以皮尔皮奇为首的南斯拉夫新闻工作者访华代表团全体人员出席了今日的酒会。
    应邀出席的，有中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，中国新闻工作者联谊会会长邓拓，副会长吴冷西、梅益、王芸生，外交部新闻司副司长徐晃，北京各报刊的负责人和中外记者，以及各国驻华使馆外交官员等100多人。
    酒会在友好和热烈的空气中进行。
