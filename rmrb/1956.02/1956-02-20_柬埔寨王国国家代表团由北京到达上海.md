Title: Cambodian Royal National Delegation Arrives in Shanghai from Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-02-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 19th: The Cambodian Royal National Delegation, headed by Prince Norodom Sihanouk, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 19th, and will visit Shanghai, Guangzhou and other places before returning home. Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, accompanied them on the same plane.

At Beijing Xijiao Airport, the national flags of Cambodia and China fluttered in the wind. At 7:30, Prince Sihanouk and others arrived at the airport by car, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai. Here, the distinguished guests warmly shook hands and said goodbye to Premier Zhou Enlai, Chen Yi, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Ye Jianying, Zhang Bojun, Zhang Wentian, and others who came to see them off.

The band played the national anthems of Cambodia and China.

Prince Sihanouk, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, reviewed the guard of honor lined up at the airport.

Before boarding the plane, Prince Sihanouk and others shook hands and said goodbye to the heads of various departments of the state organs of the People's Republic of China, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, heads of various democratic parties and people's organizations, heads of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee, people from all walks of life in the capital, as well as diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries to China.

Female students presented flowers to the distinguished guests.

Prince Sihanouk stood at the door of the plane cabin and waved frequently to the people who came to see him off. At this time, warm applause rang out at the airport.

Before leaving, Prince Sihanouk issued a written statement at the airport.

The diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries to China who came to the airport today to see them off included the ambassadors of Poland, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Romania, Mongolia, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Bulgaria, Albania, Hungary, Finland, Czechoslovakia, Pakistan, Yugoslavia, India, the German Democratic Republic, Norway, and Afghanistan, the ministers of Denmark and Switzerland, the chargé d'affaires of the United Kingdom, and the temporary chargés d'affaires of Sweden, Burma, Indonesia, and the Soviet Union.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, February 19th: His Royal Highness Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, and the Cambodian Royal National Delegation he led, arrived in Shanghai by special plane this morning. More than 5,000 people from all walks of life in Shanghai welcomed them at the airport.

At 11:38 a.m., the special plane carrying the distinguished guests landed at the airport. Shanghai Vice Mayors Xu Jianguo, Sheng Pihua, Cao Diqiu, Song Richang, Lieutenant General Wang Bicheng, head of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Shanghai, and Hu Juewen and Li Zhaohuan, vice chairmen of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, walked to the plane to welcome the distinguished guests.

Also present at the airport to welcome them were the consuls general of India, Norway, Czechoslovakia, the Soviet Union, and Poland in Shanghai, the counselor of the British Office of the Chargé d'Affaires, and personnel from the Danish Consulate General in Shanghai.

His Royal Highness Prince Sihanouk delivered a speech at the airport: Ladies and gentlemen,

I am honored that the Cambodian Royal National Delegation I lead has arrived in the famous city of Shanghai as guests today after receiving grand and unparalleled hospitality in the capital, Beijing. We are very happy and very honored.

Your warm welcome has deeply moved us.

We have seen that in China, we are receiving brotherly reception everywhere from the great Chinese people.

In fact, the Cambodian people are not only neighbors of the Chinese people, but there is also a relationship of thousands of years of blood between the Cambodian people and the Chinese people.

Our enthusiastic contacts with the government of the Chinese people have made the relationship between China and Cambodia even closer.

We are entering a new era of friendly cooperation.

Our deep friendship will help consolidate peace in Asia and the world.

Ladies and gentlemen, I would like to express my sincere thanks to you once again, and warmly wish the Chinese people prosperity.

As the distinguished guests walked through the welcoming crowd accompanied by Xu Jianguo, people waved colorful flags and flowers, beat gongs and drums, and cheered and paid tribute to the distinguished guests. Dressed-up girls kept running out of the welcoming crowd to present flowers to His Royal Highness Prince Sihanouk.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, February 19th: His Royal Highness Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, and the Cambodian Royal National Delegation he led, visited Shanghai on the afternoon of the 19th.

Prince Sihanouk and others visited the large-scale Shanghai Steam Turbine Factory and the Shanghai Electric Motor Factory next to the Steam Turbine Factory.

The distinguished guests were warmly received in these two factories. The workers lined up at the gate to welcome them, and applauded and paid tribute to the distinguished guests beside the machines. Prince Sihanouk warmly shook hands with the workers. Before leaving, Prince Sihanouk asked the directors of the two factories who accompanied him on the visit to convey his respect to the workers and engineers. He said: You have persevered and done a lot of work, and you have been able to manufacture various precision machines like advanced European countries. On behalf of the Kingdom of Cambodia, I congratulate you on your greater achievements.

The distinguished guests then visited the Children's Palace.

In the evening, Shanghai Vice Mayor Xu Jianguo hosted a banquet to welcome Prince Sihanouk and all members of the Cambodian Royal National Delegation. After the banquet, a Yue Opera performance was held. (Attached picture)
Prime Minister Sihanouk shakes hands with Premier Zhou Enlai at Beijing Airport to say goodbye.

### 柬埔寨王国国家代表团由北京到达上海

1956-02-20
第1版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨王国国家代表团由北京到达上海
    新华社19日讯　以诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王为首的柬埔寨王国国家代表团，19日晨乘飞机离开北京，将在上海、广州等地参观后返国。中华人民共和国外交部礼宾司司长柯华同机陪送。
    北京西郊机场上，柬中两国国旗迎风招展。7时半，西哈努克亲王等由周恩来总理陪同乘汽车到达机场。在这里，贵宾们同前来送行的周恩来总理和陈毅、李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、彭真、陈叔通、叶剑英、章伯钧、张闻天等人一一亲热地握手辞行。
    乐队奏柬中两国国歌。
    西哈努克亲王在周恩来总理陪同下检阅排列在机场上的仪仗队。
    在上飞机前，西哈努克亲王等同前来送行的中华人民共和国国家机关各部门负责人，中国人民解放军高级将领，各民主党派、各人民团体负责人，北京市人民委员会负责人，首都各界人士，以及各国驻华使节和外交官员们握手道别。
    女学生们把鲜花献给了贵宾们。
    西哈努克亲王站在飞机舱门口，向送行的人们频频挥手。这时，机场上响起热烈的掌声。
    临行前，西哈努克亲王在机场上发表了书面谈话。
    今天到机场送行的各国驻华使节和外交官员，有波兰、越南民主共和国、罗马尼亚、蒙古、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚、匈牙利、芬兰、捷克斯洛伐克、巴基斯坦、南斯拉夫、印度、德意志民主共和国、挪威和阿富汗的大使，丹麦和瑞士的公使，英国的代办，瑞典、缅甸、印度尼西亚和苏联的临时代办。
    新华社上海19日电　柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王殿下和他所率领的柬埔寨王国国家代表团，今天上午乘专机到达上海。上海市各界人民5，000多人在机场上欢迎。
    上午11时38分，载送贵宾的专机降落在机场。上海市副市长许建国、盛丕华、曹荻秋、宋日昌，中国人民解放军驻上海部队负责人王必成中将、中国人民政治协商会议上海市委员会副主席胡厥文、黎照寰走到飞机前欢迎贵宾们。
    今天到机场欢迎的还有印度、挪威、捷克斯洛伐克、苏联、波兰驻上海总领事，英国代办处参赞及丹麦驻上海总领事馆人员。
    西哈努克亲王殿下在机场发表谈话：各位女士、各位先生：
    我荣幸地率领的柬埔寨王国国家代表团在首都北京受到盛大无比的款待之后，今天又来到著名的城市——上海作客，我们感到十分高兴和十分荣幸。
    你们的盛情欢迎，使我们深为感动。
    我们看到，在中国，我们到处受到伟大的中国人民兄弟般的接待。
    事实上，柬埔寨人民不仅是中国人民的邻居，柬埔寨人民和中国人民之间还有着几千年的血统关系。
    我们同中国人民的政府进行的热忱的接触，使得中柬两国间的关系更加紧密了。
    我们正在进入一个友好合作的新时代。
    我们之间的深厚情谊将有助于巩固亚洲和世界的和平。
    各位女士，各位先生，我再一次向你们表示热忱的感谢，并热烈地祝中国人民的繁荣。
    当贵宾们由许建国陪同步行穿过欢迎的群众时，人们挥舞着彩旗和鲜花，敲着锣鼓，向贵宾们欢呼致敬。从欢迎的队伍中不断跑出盛装的女孩子向西哈努克亲王殿下献花。
    据新华社上海19日讯　柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和他所率领的柬埔寨王国国家代表团19日下午在上海进行了参观访问。
    西哈努克亲王等参观了规模宏大的上海汽轮机厂和紧邻汽轮机厂的上海电机厂。
    贵宾们在这两个工厂里受到热情的接待。职工们在大门口夹道欢迎，在机器旁向贵宾们鼓掌致敬。西哈努克亲王亲切地和职工们握手。临行前，西哈努克亲王要求陪同参观的两厂厂长向工人和工程师们转致他的敬意。他说：你们坚持不懈地作了许多工作，已经能够和欧洲的先进国家一样制造出各种精密机器。我代表柬埔寨王国祝贺你们取得更大的成就。
    贵宾们接着还参观了少年宫。
    晚间，上海市副市长许建国设宴欢迎西哈努克亲王和柬埔寨王国国家代表团全体团员。宴会后并举行了越剧晚会。（附图片）
