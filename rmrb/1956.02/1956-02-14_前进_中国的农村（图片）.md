Forward -- China's Countryside
	Su Guang, Fang Cheng, Shen Tongheng, Miao Di, Ying Tao, Artist; Yuan Ying, Poem
What is depicted on the paintings is not a fantasy, but a typical little village 12 years from now; these are just some facts, with not much exaggeration added. In 12 years, when we open this picture again, everyone will say it is an outdated pattern.
Forward -- China's countryside!
Forward -- China's farmers!
Achieving the Forty Articles depends on nothing else but our own hands. What is depicted here is only the beginning; the wonderful life that cannot be painted is still ahead!

### 前进——中国的农村（图片）
苏光　方成　沈同衡　苗地　英韬　袁鹰
1956-02-14
第5版()
专栏：

    前进——中国的农村
    苏光　方成　沈同衡　苗地　英韬　画　袁鹰　诗描在画幅上的不是幻想，是12年后一小普通的村庄；这里只是一些事实，并没有加上多少夸张，12年后再打开这张画，人人都说它是过时的图样。前进——中国的农村！前进——中国的农民！实现四十条，不靠别的，靠我们自己的双手，这里画出的仅仅是个开始，画不出来的美好生活还在前头！
