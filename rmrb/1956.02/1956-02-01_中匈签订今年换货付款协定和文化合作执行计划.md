Title: China and Hungary Sign This Year's Goods Exchange and Payment Agreement and Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan
Author:
Date: 1956-02-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague Dispatch from Budapest: After negotiations in a sincere and friendly atmosphere, representatives of the People's Republic of China and the Hungarian People's Republic signed the China-Hungary 1956 Goods Exchange and Payment Agreement in Budapest on January 27.
    According to the agreement, China will supply Hungary with mineral ores, hardware, chemical and textile raw materials, tea, oilseeds, hides and agricultural and livestock products. Hungary will supply China with agricultural machinery, telecommunications equipment, precision instruments, power generation equipment, vehicles, pharmaceuticals, petroleum products, textiles and other goods.
    Kong Yuan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, signed the agreement on behalf of the Chinese side, and Derapa, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the Hungarian People's Republic, signed the agreement on behalf of the Hungarian side.
    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The 1956 Implementation Plan for the China-Hungary Cultural Cooperation Agreement was signed in Beijing on January 28. Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of Culture of the People's Republic of China, signed on behalf of the Chinese side, and Karai Gyula, Vice Minister of Culture of the Hungarian People's Republic, signed on behalf of the Hungarian side.
    The implementation plan stipulates measures for establishing direct contact and exchanging information between the scientific and educational institutions of the two countries, stipulates that the two countries will send scientists and educators to visit each other and increase the number of students studying abroad, stipulates that the two countries will send artists to visit each other and hold exhibitions, and other measures to expand cultural exchanges between the two countries.

### 中匈签订今年换货付款协定和文化合作执行计划

1956-02-01
第1版()
专栏：

    中匈签订今年换货付款协定和文化合作执行计划
    新华社布拉格电　布达佩斯消息：中华人民共和国和匈牙利人民共和国的代表在诚挚友好的气氛中进行了谈判以后，1月27日在布达佩斯签订了中匈1956年交换货物和付款协定。
    根据协定，中国将供应匈牙利矿砂、五金、化工和纺织原料，茶叶、油料、皮毛和农畜产品。匈牙利将供应中国农业机械、电讯设备、精密仪器、发电设备、车辆、药品、石油产品、纺织品和其他货物。
    代表中国方面在协定上签字的是中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长孔原，代表匈牙利方面在协定上签字的是匈牙利人民共和国对外贸易部副部长德罗帕。
    新华社讯　中匈文化合作协定1956年执行计划1月28日在北京签字。代表中国方面签字的是中华人民共和国文化部副部长丁西林，代表匈牙利方面签字的是匈牙利人民共和国文化部副部长卡拉伊·居拉。
    执行计划中规定了两国科学、教育机关建立直接联系和交换资料的措施，规定两国将互派科学家、教育家进行访问和增派留学生，规定了两国互派文艺工作者进行访问和互相举办展览会，以及其他扩大两国文化交流的措施。
