Title: The Soviet Union and Yugoslavia Sign Loan and Trade Credit Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-02-04
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 3rd (TASS dispatch) - On February 2nd, an agreement on loans and trade credits from the Soviet Union to Yugoslavia, totaling 84 million U.S. dollars, was signed in Moscow. This agreement was signed in accordance with the provisions of the protocol on economic negotiations signed in Moscow on September 1st of last year by delegations from the governments of the Soviet Union and Yugoslavia.
    According to the agreement, the Soviet Union will lend Yugoslavia the equivalent of 30 million U.S. dollars in freely convertible currency or gold. Yugoslavia can use this loan at will, in one lump sum or in installments, during the period from 1956 to 1958.
    The Soviet Union will grant Yugoslavia trade credits of 54 million U.S. dollars to pay for goods supplied by the Soviet Union to Yugoslavia during the period from 1956 to 1958. This credit will be repaid by Yugoslavia over 10 years after the loan is used up, with an annual interest rate of 2%.

### 苏联和南斯拉夫签订借款和贸易信贷的协定

1956-02-04
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联和南斯拉夫签订借款和贸易信贷的协定
    新华社据塔斯社莫斯科3日讯　2月2日在莫斯科签订了苏联给予南斯拉夫8，400万美元的借款和贸易信贷的协定。这个协定是根据苏联和南斯拉夫两国政府代表团去年9月1日在莫斯科签订的关于经济谈判的议定书的规定而签订的。
    根据这个协定，苏联将以自由兑换的货币或者黄金贷给南斯拉夫价值3，000万美元的借款。在1956到1958年期间，南斯拉夫可以随意一次或分期用完这笔贷款。
    苏联贷给南斯拉夫5，400万美元的贸易信贷，这笔信贷用来支付1956到1958年期间苏联供给南斯拉夫的商品的贷款。这笔信贷将由南斯拉夫在贷款用完后的10年中偿清，年利2％。
