Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De Leaves Poland
Author:
Date: 1956-02-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 3rd: After a 4-day visit to Poland at the invitation of the Polish government, Vice Chairman Zhu De of the People's Republic of China and his entourage left Poland on the evening of February 2nd.

At the station to see off Vice Chairman Zhu De were Chairman of the State Council of the Polish People's Republic, Zawadzki, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Cyrankiewicz, and representatives of the Polish State Council, the government, and social organizations. Zawadzki delivered a speech when bidding farewell to Vice Chairman Zhu De. He said: The visit of Vice Chairman Zhu De and his entourage to Poland will greatly contribute to the further strengthening of the friendly relations between Poland and the People's Republic of China and the comprehensive cooperation between the two countries. This continuous enhancement of friendship and cooperation contributes to the cause of peace and Poland's socialist construction.

In his reply, Vice Chairman Zhu De pointed out: During their visit to Poland, they felt the brotherly friendship of the Polish people towards the Chinese people. The development of this friendship between China and Poland will greatly accelerate the development of our country's socialist construction and help strengthen the power of the camp of peace, democracy, and socialism headed by the Soviet Union.

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 3rd: Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Zhu De, along with Liu Lantao, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, paid a farewell visit to Beirut, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, on the afternoon of the 2nd.

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 3rd: Vice Chairman Zhu De of the People's Republic of China visited the Zeran Automobile Factory in the suburbs of Warsaw on the 2nd. Each workshop of the factory was decorated with the Chinese national flag. Every time Zhu De entered a workshop, the workers applauded and cheered. Zhu De constantly shook hands warmly with the workers. The workers, who were enthusiastically discussing the draft of the factory's five-year plan, told Zhu De that they would complete the five-year plan in four years, and that by 1959 they would produce an average of 88 cars per day.

### 朱德副主席离开波兰

1956-02-04
第4版()
专栏：

    朱德副主席离开波兰
    新华社华沙3日电　中华人民共和国副主席朱德应邀在波兰访问了4天以后，已经在2月2日晚上和他的随行人员离开波兰。
    到车站欢送朱德副主席的有波兰人民共和国国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基，部长会议主席西伦凯维兹，以及波兰国务委员会、政府和社会团体的代表。萨瓦茨基在送别朱德副主席的时候致词。他说：朱德副主席和他的随行人员的访问波兰，将大大地有助于波兰和中华人民共和国之间的友好关系以及两国之间全面合作的更进一步的加强。这种友谊和合作的不断增进有助于和平事业和波兰的社会主义建设事业。
    朱德副主席致答辞的时候指出：他们在波兰访问期间，感觉到了波兰人民对中国人民的兄弟般的友谊，中、波两国之间的这种友谊的发展将大大加速我们国家的社会主义建设的发展，并且有助于加强以苏联为首的和平、民主和社会主义阵营的力量。
    据新华社华沙3日电　中华人民共和国副主席、中国共产党中央委员会书记处书记朱德，偕同中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员刘澜涛等，在2日下午拜会了波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记贝鲁特，向他辞行。
    据新华社华沙3日电　中华人民共和国副主席朱德2日参观了华沙郊区的泽兰汽车工厂。这个工厂的各个车间都挂有中国国旗。朱德每走进一个车间，工人们都鼓掌和欢呼。朱德不断同工人们亲切地握手。正在热烈讨论本工厂五年计划草案的工人们向朱德表示，他们将在4年内完成五年计划，他们到1959年平均每天出产的汽车将达到88辆。
