Title: "Sino-Soviet Friendship" Agricultural Cooperative Holds Naming Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1956-02-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 13th: A naming ceremony for the "Sino-Soviet Friendship" Agricultural Production Cooperative was held in Shijingshan District, Beijing, on the 13th, attended by 5,000 people.

    After the ceremony began, Guo Zhen, the director of the "Sino-Soviet Friendship" Agricultural Production Cooperative, announced according to the decision of the general meeting of all members that their cooperative would be formally named the "Sino-Soviet Friendship" Agricultural Production Cooperative from now on. He said that their cooperative had increased the yield of wheat, corn, and other four crops by 33% to two times by learning from the advanced Soviet close-planting experience. Now, they have formulated a seven-year long-term plan to comprehensively learn from advanced Soviet experiences and strive to complete the tasks proposed in the "National Agricultural Development Program (Draft) from 1956 to 1967" by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China ahead of schedule.

    Zhang Youyu, Deputy Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Deputy Mayor of Beijing, Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Li Haiqiu, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, spoke at the meeting to express their congratulations.

    Qi Gangning, Second Secretary of the Soviet Embassy in China, read out congratulatory messages signed by Buyanov, Chairman of the "Ilyich" Collective Farm in the Lenin District of Moscow Oblast, Socialist Labor Hero; Gneralov, Chairman of the "Stalin" Collective Farm in the Lukhovitsky District, Socialist Labor Hero, and Deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union; and 9 others.

### “中苏友好”农业合作社举行命名大会

1956-02-14
第1版()
专栏：

    “中苏友好”农业合作社举行命名大会
    据新华社13日讯　北京市石景山区13日举行了有5，000人参加的“中苏友好”农业生产合作社命名大会。
    大会开始以后，“中苏友好”农业生产合作社主任郭振根据全体社员大会的决定宣布：他们这个社从现在起正式命名为“中苏友好”农业生产合作社。他说，他们这个社由于学习苏联密植先进经验，使小麦、玉米等4种作物的产量，提高了33％到两倍。现在，他们已经订出了七年远景规划，要全面学习苏联先进经验，争取提前完成中共中央关于“1956年到1967年全国农业发展纲要（草案）”所提出的任务。
    中共北京市委员会副书记、北京市副市长张友渔，中苏友好协会总会秘书长钱俊瑞，和苏联驻华大使馆临时代办李海秋都在大会上讲话表示祝贺。
    苏联驻华大使馆二等秘书齐刚宁 在大会上宣读了莫斯科州列宁区“伊里奇”集体农庄主席、社会主义劳动英雄布扬诺夫，鲁贺维茨区“斯大林”集体农庄主席、社会主义劳动英雄、苏联最高苏维埃代表格涅拉洛夫等9人签名的贺词。
