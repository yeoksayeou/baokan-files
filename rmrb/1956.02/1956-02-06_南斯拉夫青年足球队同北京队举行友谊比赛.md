Title: Yugoslav Youth Football Team Holds Friendly Match with Beijing Team
Author:
Date: 1956-02-06
Page: 1

    Yugoslav Youth Football Team Holds Friendly Match with Beijing Team
    Xinhua News Agency, 5th—The Yugoslav Youth Football Team played its third friendly match since arriving in China against the Beijing team at the Xian Nong Tan Stadium in Beijing on the afternoon of the 5th. The Yugoslav team won with a score of three to one.
    Today, those present to watch the match included: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Deng Xiaoping, Chen Yi, and Li Fuchun, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu, as well as more than 20,000 people from all walks of life in the capital.
    Yugoslav Ambassador to China Popovic, along with diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China, also watched today's match.
    Today, the Yugoslav team's lineup was: Goalkeeper Vereš; Right Back Sokičić, Left Back Popović; Right Midfielder Krstić, Center Back Crnković, Left Midfielder Boškov; Right Winger Liposinović, Right Inside Forward Doproiak, Center Forward Antić, Left Inside Forward Veselinović, Left Winger Birinčević. The substitute players were Goalkeeper Beala; Defenders Šandek and Radović; Forwards Mujić, Kurdović, and Filhadović.

### 南斯拉夫青年足球队同北京队举行友谊比赛

1956-02-06
第1版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫青年足球队同北京队举行友谊比赛
    据新华社5日讯　南斯拉夫青年足球队5日下午在北京市先农坛体育场同北京队举行了来中国以后的第三场友谊比赛。南斯拉夫队以三比一获胜。
    今天，到场观看比赛的有：中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，副总理邓小平、陈毅、李富春，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、北京市市长彭真，最高人民法院院长董必武，以及首都各界人士共两万多人。
    南斯拉夫驻华大使波波维奇和各国驻华使节和外交官员也观看了今天的球赛。
    今天，南斯拉夫队出场的是：守门员维累什；右后卫索基契，左后卫波波维契；右前卫克尔斯蒂契，中卫次尔恩科维契，左前卫鲍什科夫；右边锋利包希诺维契，右内锋多泼拉克，中锋安蒂契，左内锋维塞利诺维契，左边锋比尔林切维契。替补队员是守门员倍阿拉；后卫山戴克、拉道维契；前锋牧伊契、库尔多维契、菲尔哈多维契。
