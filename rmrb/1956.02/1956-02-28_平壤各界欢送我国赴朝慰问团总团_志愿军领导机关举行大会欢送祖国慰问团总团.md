Title: Pyongyang Circles Give a Fond Farewell to Our Country's慰问团 General Delegation to Korea, Volunteer Army Leading Organs Hold Grand Meeting to Welcome祖国慰问团 General Delegation

Author:
Date: 1956-02-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 26th—People from all walks of life in Pyongyang held a grand meeting at the Moranbong Theater on the afternoon of the 26th to bid farewell to the personnel of the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea headed by Chief Delegate Wang Ziyi, who are about to return home after completing their慰问 work. Various sub-delegations of the慰问团 are still continuing their慰问 activities among the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army troops.

Choe Jong-hak, Director of the General Political Bureau of the Korean People's Army, delivered a farewell speech at the meeting. He requested that the慰问团 convey the Korean people's warm gratitude to the Chinese people upon their return home.

Wang Ziyi delivered a farewell speech at the meeting. He said that after returning to China, the慰问团 would convey to the Chinese people many vivid and touching facts they had heard and witnessed in Korea, as well as the many brilliant achievements in the restoration and construction of North Korea, so that the deep friendship between the Chinese and Korean peoples would be further consolidated and developed.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 26th—The leading organs of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army held a grand meeting on the afternoon of the 25th to bid farewell to the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea.

Wang Yunrui, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the慰问团 on behalf of all officers and soldiers of the Volunteer Army at the meeting. Wang Yunrui said that encouraged by the people of the motherland, all officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army are determined to make greater achievements in all aspects in the new year; and continue to strictly guard their positions, contribute all their strength to defend and support the socialist construction of the motherland, and strive for a peaceful resolution of the Korean issue.

Wang Ziyi, the head of the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, gave a farewell speech and said that after the慰问团 returns to the motherland, it will definitely tell the people of the motherland about the heroic struggle, hard work, and high patriotism and internationalism of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army in Korea. Let the workers, peasants, and intellectuals of the motherland take the Volunteer Army as an example, work harder, produce harder, and support the Volunteer Army with greater victories and achievements in socialist construction.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 27th—The personnel of the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea left Pyongyang by train at noon on the 27th to return home.

### 平壤各界欢送我国赴朝慰问团总团  志愿军领导机关举行大会欢送祖国慰问团总团

1956-02-28
第4版()
专栏：

    平壤各界欢送我国赴朝慰问团总团
    志愿军领导机关举行大会欢送祖国慰问团总团
    据新华社平壤26日电　平壤市各界人民26日下午在牡丹峰剧场举行大会，欢送结束了慰问工作即将回国的以王子宜总团长为首的中国人民赴朝慰问团总团人员。慰问团各分团仍继续在朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军部队中进行慰问活动。
    朝鲜人民军总政治局长崔宗学在大会上致欢送词。他要求慰问团回国后向中国人民转达朝鲜人民对他们的热烈的感谢。
    王子宜在会上致告别词。他说，慰问团回国后将把在朝鲜耳闻目见的许多生动感人的事实和朝鲜北部恢复建设中的许多辉煌成就传达给中国人民，使朝中人民的深厚友谊更加巩固和发展。
    据新华社平壤26日电　中国人民志愿军领导机关在25日下午举行大会欢送中国人民赴朝慰问团总团。
    中国人民志愿军副参谋长王蕴瑞代表志愿军全军官兵在大会上对慰问团表示衷心感谢。王蕴瑞说，在祖国人民鼓舞下，中国人民志愿军全体官兵决心在新的一年中，在各方面作出更大的成绩；并继续严守自己的阵地，为保卫和支援祖国的社会主义建设、为争取和平解决朝鲜问题而贡献自己的一切力量。
    中国人民赴朝慰问团总团长王子宜致告别词说，慰问团在回到祖国以后，一定把中国人民志愿军在朝鲜英勇奋斗、艰苦努力的事迹和高度的爱国主义和国际主义精神，告诉祖国人民。让祖国的工人、农民和知识分子以志愿军为榜样，更加努力工作，努力生产，用社会主义建设的更大的胜利和成就来支援志愿军。
    据新华社平壤27日电　中国人民赴朝慰问团总团人员27日中午乘火车离开平壤回国。
