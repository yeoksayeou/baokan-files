Title: The Institute of Metallurgy and Ceramics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Successfully Trial-Produces Industrial Glass Fiber

Author:
Date: 1956-02-11
Page: 3

    Institute of Metallurgy and Ceramics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences
    Successfully Trial-Produces Industrial Glass Fiber
    The glass fiber—continuous boron electrical insulating glass fiber—finer than nine-tenths of the diameter of a human hair has been successfully trial-produced by the Institute of Metallurgy and Ceramics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. This glass fiber can be woven into cloth tape that can withstand high temperatures, making it an excellent electrical insulating material. The cloth woven from this glass fiber can also be pressed with certain organic plastics to form a plate with the strength of steel but lighter than aluminum, making this plate a good material for the aviation and shipbuilding industries. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 科学院冶金陶瓷研究所  试制工业用的玻璃丝成功

1956-02-11
第3版()
专栏：

    科学院冶金陶瓷研究所
    试制工业用的玻璃丝成功
    比一根头发直径还细十分之九左右的玻璃丝——连续的硼质电气绝缘玻璃纤维，已经由中国科学院冶金陶瓷研究所试制成功。这种玻璃纤维可以织成能够耐较高温度的布带，这是一种很好的电气绝缘材料。这种玻璃纤维织成的布还可以和某些有机塑料压成一种强度和钢板一样大重量比铝还轻的板，这种板是航空和造船工业中的良好材料。　（新华社）
