Title: Soviet Deputy Military Attaché Holds Reception Celebrating 38th Anniversary of Soviet Army

Author:
Date: 1956-02-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd: The Soviet Embassy's Deputy Military Attaché in China, Colonel Fominko, held a reception on the evening of February 23rd to celebrate the 38th anniversary of the Soviet Army.

Invited to the reception were Marshal Peng Dehuai, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council of the People's Republic of China, Vice Premier of the State Council, and Minister of National Defense; Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi; Marshal Ye Jianying, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council; Senior General Huang Kecheng, Vice Minister of National Defense; Senior General Xiao Ke, Vice Minister of National Defense; Senior General Li Da, Vice Minister of National Defense; Senior General Chen Geng, Senior General Xu Guangda, Senior General Li Kenong, Senior General Zhang Aiping, Senior General Xiao Hua, Senior General Gan Siqi, Senior General Peng Shaohui, Admiral of the Navy Su Zhenhua, Air Force Senior General Liu Yalou, Engineer Corps Senior General Chen Shiju, Senior General Yang Chengwu, Senior General Hong Xuezhi, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General Wang Shangrong, Lieutenant General Wan Yi, Lieutenant General Liu ShaoWen, Lieutenant General Liu Zhijian, Lieutenant General Wang Zonghuai, Lieutenant General Yu Qiuli, Vice Admiral of the Navy Luo Shunchu, Air Force Lieutenant General Wang Bingzhang, Artillery Lieutenant General Qiu Chuangcheng, colonels of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei and other Vice Ministers of relevant ministries; Wu Yuzhang, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Xue Zizheng, Vice Mayor of Beijing; and people from all walks of life in the capital.

Diplomatic envoys of various countries to China, military attachés and deputy military attachés of embassies in China were invited to attend the reception this evening.

At the reception, Colonel Fominko and Marshal Peng Dehuai delivered speeches successively.

During the reception, guests frequently stood up to toast the unbreakable friendship between the people and armies of the Soviet Union and China, and the achievements of the people of the Soviet Union and China in defending peace and construction.

### 苏联驻华代理武官举行招待会  庆祝苏军建军38周年

1956-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联驻华代理武官举行招待会
    庆祝苏军建军38周年
    新华社23日讯　苏联驻华大使馆代理武官富敏科上校为庆祝苏军建军38周年，在23日晚举行招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的，有中华人民共和国国防委员会副主席、国务院副总理、国防部长彭德怀元帅，国务院副总理陈毅，国防委员会副主席叶剑英元帅，国防部副部长黄克诚大将、萧克上将、李达上将，中国人民解放军高级将领陈赓大将、许光达大将、李克农上将、张爱萍上将、萧华上将、甘泗淇上将、彭绍辉上将、苏振华海军上将、刘亚楼空军上将、陈士榘工兵上将、杨成武上将、洪学智上将、萧向荣中将、王尚荣中将、万毅中将、刘少文中将、刘志坚中将、王宗槐中将、余秋里中将、罗舜初海军中将、王秉璋空军中将、邱创成炮兵中将和校官们，外交部副部长姬鹏飞和其他有关各部副部长，中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章，北京市副市长薛子正和首都各界人士。
    各国驻华使节和驻华大使馆武官、副武官应邀出席了今晚的招待会。
    会上，富敏科上校和彭德怀元帅先后致词。
    席间，宾主频频起立，为苏中两国人民和军队间的牢不可破的友谊，为苏中两国人民在保卫和平和建设事业中的成就而干杯。
