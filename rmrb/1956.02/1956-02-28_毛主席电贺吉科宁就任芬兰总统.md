Title: Chairman Mao Congratulates Kekkonen on Assuming the Presidency of Finland
Author:
Date: 1956-02-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 27th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram congratulating Kekkonen on assuming the presidency of the Republic of Finland. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Excellency Kekkonen, President of the Republic of Finland:

    On the occasion of Your Excellency's assumption of the Presidency of the Republic of Finland, I extend my warm congratulations and wish for the further development of friendly relations between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Finland.

                                Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
    February 27, 1956

### 毛主席电贺吉科宁就任芬兰总统

1956-02-28
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席电贺吉科宁就任芬兰总统
    新华社27日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东打电报祝贺吉科宁就任芬兰共和国总统，电文如下：芬兰共和国总统吉科宁阁下：
    值阁下就任芬兰共和国总统之际，谨致热烈的祝贺，并祝中华人民共和国同芬兰共和国的友好关系进一步发展。
                                中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
    1956年2月27日
