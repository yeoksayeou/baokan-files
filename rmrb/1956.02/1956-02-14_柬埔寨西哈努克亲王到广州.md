Title: Prince Sihanouk of Cambodia Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1956-02-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, February 13th. Prince Norodom Sihanouk, head of the Cambodian Royal National Delegation, arrived in Guangzhou by special plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China.

Members of the Cambodian Royal National Delegation arriving on the same plane included: Ran Niete, Member of the Royal High Council; Chin Di, Member of the Royal High Council; Sang Nian, Vice-President of the Kingdom's Assembly; Eng Miass, Member of the National Assembly; Dr. Nil Smoouk, Member of the Kingdom's Assembly; Colonel Nu Hu, Chief of Staff of the Royal Air Force; Ron So Fang, Deputy Secretary of State for Farmer Welfare; Phong Salen, Private Secretary to the Prince; and other delegation members.

The special plane carrying Prince Sihanouk and others arrived at Guangzhou Airport at 2:30 this afternoon. At that time, more than 1,500 people from all walks of life in Guangzhou gathered at the airport, which was decorated with the flags of the Kingdom of Cambodia and the People's Republic of China, to welcome the distinguished guests in the rain. They waved flowers and handkerchiefs, cheering and applauding warmly.

As Prince Sihanouk stepped off the plane, Tao Zhu, Governor of Guangdong Province, Huang Yongsheng, Head of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Garrison in Guangzhou, Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou, Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, who had come from Beijing especially to greet the distinguished guests, as well as responsible persons from the Guangdong and Guangzhou municipal Party and government organs, democratic parties, and people's organizations, stepped forward to greet him. Prince Sihanouk shook hands with them one by one.

After the band played the national anthems of Cambodia and China, Prince Sihanouk, accompanied by Tao Zhu, Governor of Guangdong Province, reviewed the guard of honor. Prince Sihanouk and the delegation members also accepted flowers presented by the children of Guangzhou.

Subsequently, Prince Sihanouk delivered a speech at the airport.

As Prince Sihanouk and others, accompanied by Tao Zhu, Governor of Guangdong Province, walked out of the airport to the guesthouse, people from all walks of life in Guangzhou lined the streets, waving flowers and shouting slogans: "Welcome Prince Norodom Sihanouk!" "Welcome the Cambodian Royal National Delegation!" "Long live the friendship between the Chinese and Cambodian people!" "Long live world peace!"

Also welcoming Prince Sihanouk at the airport this afternoon were the Soviet and Polish consuls in Guangzhou.

Around 4 o'clock in the afternoon, the distinguished guests visited the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall and toured the city of Guangzhou.

In the evening, Prince Sihanouk and all members of the delegation attended a welcome banquet hosted by Tao Zhu, Governor of Guangdong Province.

### 柬埔寨西哈努克亲王到广州

1956-02-14
第1版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨西哈努克亲王到广州
    新华社广州13日讯　应中华人民共和国政府邀请前来中国访问的柬埔寨王国国家代表团团长诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王在今天下午乘专机到达广州。
    同机到达的柬埔寨王国国家代表团团员有：皇廷最高会议委员兰·涅特，皇廷最高会议委员钦·迪，王国会议副议长桑·年，国民会议议员恩格·米阿斯，王国会议议员尼尔·斯莫乌克博士，皇家空军参谋长努·呼上校，农民福利副国务秘书罗恩·索方，亲王私人秘书丰·萨伦以及代表团随员等。
    西哈努克亲王等乘坐的专机在下午两点三十分到达广州机场。这时候，广州市各界人民1，500多人聚集在悬挂着柬埔寨王国和中华人民共和国国旗的机场上，冒雨欢迎贵宾们。他们挥舞着鲜花和手帕，热烈地欢呼鼓掌。
    西哈努克亲王刚刚走下飞机，广东省省长陶铸，中国人民解放军广州驻军首长黄永胜，广州市市长朱光和由北京专程前来迎接贵宾们的外交部礼宾司司长柯华，以及广东省和广州市党政机关和各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人都走上前去迎接。西哈努克亲王和他们一一握手。
    在乐队奏柬中两国国歌以后，西哈努克亲王由广东省省长陶铸陪同检阅了仪仗队。西哈努克亲王和代表团团员并接受广州市少年儿童的献花。
    接着，西哈努克亲王在机场上发表了讲话。
    当西哈努克亲王等由广东省省长陶铸陪同步出机场前往迎宾馆休息时，列队在机场两旁夹道欢迎的广州市各界人士挥舞着鲜花，不停地高呼：“欢迎诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王！”“欢迎柬埔寨王国国家代表团！”“中柬两国人民的友谊万岁！”“世界和平万岁！”
    今天下午在机场欢迎西哈努克亲王的还有苏联和波兰驻广州的领事。
    下午4点多钟，贵宾们参观了中山堂并观看了广州市容。
    晚间，西哈努克亲王和代表团全体人员应邀出席了广东省省长陶铸举行的欢迎宴会。
