Title: Yugoslav Youth Football Team Holds Friendly Match with "August 1st" Football Team
Author:
Date: 1956-02-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 2nd. The Yugoslav Youth Football Team held its first friendly match in Beijing against the Chinese People's Liberation Army "August 1st" Football Team at the Beijing Xiannongtan Stadium on the afternoon of February 2nd.

    Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and Vice Premiers Deng Xiaoping and Chen Yi watched the match and, before the match began, met with the leader of the Yugoslav Youth Football Team, Bo. Pišič, Vice President of the Yugoslav Football Association, the football team's coach Bo. Marjanović, doctor A. Obradović, captain A. Baštrovič, V. Kravić, and all the players. After the match, Premier Zhou Enlai, along with Deng Xiaoping, Chen Yi, the Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popović, and others, went to the field to shake hands with the players of the Yugoslav Youth Football Team and the "August 1st" Football Team to express their congratulations.

    A welcome ceremony was held before the match.

    Cai Shufan, Deputy Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, delivered a welcoming speech. Bo. Pišič, leader of the Yugoslav Youth Football Team, delivered a reply.

    In today's friendly match, the Yugoslav Youth Football Team won with a score of six to one.

    Also present to watch the match today were Ye Jianying, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council, Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council, Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to China and more than 20,000 Beijing spectators. (Attached picture)
    Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council, watching the game with Yugoslav Ambassador to China Popović and his wife. Our reporter Cang Shi photographed.

### 南斯拉夫青年足球队同“八一”足球队举行友谊赛

1956-02-03
第1版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫青年足球队同“八一”足球队举行友谊赛
    据新华社2日讯  南斯拉夫青年足球队同中国人民解放军“八一”足球队2日下午在北京先农坛体育场举行了在北京的首次友谊比赛。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，副总理邓小平、陈毅到场观看了这次比赛，并且在比赛开始前接见了南斯拉夫青年足球队领队、南斯拉夫足球联合会副主席勃·彼希契，足球队教练勃·马尔亚诺维契，医生亚·奥勃拉道维契，队长亚·巴什特罗维契、伏·克拉维契和全体队员。比赛结束以后，周恩来总理和邓小平、陈毅，南斯拉夫驻华大使波波维奇等一同来到运动场上，同南斯拉夫青年足球队的队员们，“八一”足球队的队员们一一握手，表示祝贺。
    在比赛以前举行了欢迎仪式。
    中华人民共和国体育运动委员会副主任蔡树藩致欢迎词。南斯拉夫青年足球队领队勃·彼希契致答词。
    今天的友谊比赛，南斯拉夫青年足球队以六比一获胜。
    今天出席观看比赛的，还有国防委员会副主席叶剑英，国务院秘书长习仲勋，外交部副部长张闻天等，以及各国驻华使节和北京观众两万多人。（附图片）
