Title: Widespread Snowfall in North China

Author:

Date: 1956-02-27
Page: 2

Widespread Snowfall in North China

　　Beginning on the night of the 23rd, there was widespread snowfall in North China. The snowfall covered the provinces of Shanxi, Hebei, and Shandong, as well as the northern part of Henan Province and the central part of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. This is the largest snowfall in North China since the beginning of winter. The snowfall was heaviest near Beijing, reaching a depth of more than two inches. The areas of Baoding, Cangxian, and Yuxian have all exceeded the average precipitation for February in past years.

　　Rainfall in North China has been insufficient for the past two months, and this snowfall is very beneficial to crops. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 华北地区普遍降雪

1956-02-27
第2版()
专栏：

    华北地区普遍降雪
    从23日夜间开始，华北地区普遍降雪。这次降雪范围遍及山西、河北、山东等省和河南省北部、内蒙古自治区中部。这是入冬以来华北地区最大的一场雪。其中以北京附近雪量最大，深达两寸多。保定、沧县、蔚县一带也都超过历年2月的平均降水量。
    过去两个月华北地区雨水不足，这场雪对农作物很有利。（新华社）
