Title: Burmese Government Holds Border People's Conference in Lashio
Author:
Date: 1956-02-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, February 9th: The Burmese Union Government held a border people's conference in Lashio, within Burmese territory on the Sino-Burmese border, from February 7th to 8th.

The Burmese Union Government's Minister of the Interior, Bo Khin Maung Galei, Minister of Kachin Affairs and Chairman of Kachin State, Duwa Zau Lawn, Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung, and senior military and political personnel of the Burmese Union Government attended the meeting.

Chinese Ambassador to Burma, Yao Zhongming, Chinese Consul General in Lashio, Wang Ze, Secretary-General of the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee, Zhang Zizhai, and Chairman of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Region of Yunnan Province, Dao Jingban, were also invited to attend the meeting.

On the afternoon of February 8th, a mass meeting was held in Lashio with the participation of approximately two thousand residents of various ethnic groups from the Burmese border area. The meeting read out a congratulatory message from the Prime Minister of the Burmese Union, U Nu. The message said: This meeting has far-reaching benefits. China and Burma are friendly countries. This meeting will further promote the friendly relations between China and Burma.

When Bo Khin Maung Galei spoke at the meeting, he pointed out that the purpose of this meeting was to further consolidate the friendly relations established by the Prime Ministers of China and Burma. He said: Our two countries, China and Burma, are connected by borders, share the same water source, and many residents of the two countries have even formed kinship ties. The people of China and Burma should treat each other as brothers.

When Duwa Zau Lawn spoke at the meeting, he praised the Yunnan region of China for its good adherence to the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence announced by the Prime Ministers of China and Burma. He said: The people of our Kachin State resolutely implement these principles.

In his speech, Yao Zhongming congratulated the success of the meeting and the people of the Burmese border region. He mentioned that after the Prime Ministers of China and Burma issued a joint statement on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, the friendship between the two countries had been further developed and consolidated. He said: Among the people on the border between our two countries, there are not only the same ethnic groups, frequent economic and cultural ties, but also close kinship relations. Therefore, the people on the border between the two countries have always been closely related and lived in harmony.

In his speech, Zhang Zizhai said: The Yunnan region of China has in the past and continues to adhere to the established peaceful foreign policy of the Chinese government, and to the famous Five Principles to develop the good-neighborly friendship between the people of the two border regions and to strengthen cultural and trade exchanges. We will continue to work hard to continuously enhance the friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries in the future.

Their speeches were warmly welcomed by the masses.

That evening, residents of various ethnic groups in the Lashio area performed ethnic songs and dances in the square near the meeting venue to warmly celebrate the successful conclusion of the meeting.

### 缅甸政府在雷基召开边境人民会议

1956-02-10
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸政府在雷基召开边境人民会议
    新华社仰光9日电　缅甸联邦政府在2月7日到8日在中缅边境缅甸境内的雷基召开了一个边境人民会议。
    缅甸联邦政府内政部长波庆貌格礼，克钦事务部长兼克钦邦主席杜瓦藻朗，缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂和缅甸联邦政府的高级军政人员出席了这个会议。
    中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明，中国驻腊戍总领事王泽，中国云南省人民委员会秘书长张子斋，云南省德宏傣族景颇族自治区主席刀京版等也应邀出席了会议。
    2月8日下午，在雷基举行了有缅甸边境各族居民约两千人参加的群众大会。大会宣读了缅甸联邦总理吴努的贺词。贺词说：这次会议具有深远的益处。缅中两国是友好的国家。这一次会议将进一步促进缅中两国的友好关系。
    波庆貌格礼在会上讲话的时候指出：这次会议的目的是为了使缅中两国总理所奠定的友好关系更加巩固。他说：我们缅中两国边境相连、饮水同源，甚至有许多两国居民结成了亲戚。缅中两国人民应当像兄弟一样友好相处。
    杜瓦藻朗在会上讲话时称赞中国云南地方很好地遵守了缅中两国总理所宣布的和平共处的五项原则。他说：我们克钦邦的人民坚决执行这些原则。
    姚仲明在讲话时对会议的成功和缅甸边境地区人民表示祝贺。他谈到在中缅两国总理发表了和平共处五项原则的联合声明之后，两国之间的友谊获得了进一步的发展和巩固。他说：在我们两国边境的人民中，不但有相同的民族，频繁的经济和文化上的联系，而且还有密切的亲戚关系。所以两国边境的人民一向是休戚相关、和睦共处的。
    张子斋在讲话中说：中国云南地方过去和现在都遵照中国政府既定的和平外交政策、遵照著名的五项原则为发展两国边境地区人民的睦邻友好和加强文化贸易的往来而努力。我们今后还将继续努力以不断增进两国的友好合作关系。
    他们的讲话受到群众的热烈欢迎。
    这天傍晚，雷基一带的各族居民在举行会议的会址附近广场上表演了民族歌舞，热烈庆祝会议的成功闭幕。
