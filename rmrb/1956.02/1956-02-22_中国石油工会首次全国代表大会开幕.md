Title: Inauguration of the First National Congress of the Chinese Petroleum Trade Union
Author:
Date: 1956-02-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 21st — The first National Congress of the Chinese Petroleum Trade Union opened in Beijing today. The main tasks of this meeting are: to review and summarize the petroleum trade union work in the past few years, to determine the guidelines and tasks for the petroleum trade union work in the future, and to elect and form the National Committee of the Chinese Petroleum Trade Union.

    At today's meeting, Zhang Zhaomei, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Petroleum Trade Union, made a report entitled "Striving to Mobilize All Staff and Workers in the Petroleum Industry to Complete the First Five-Year Plan Ahead of Schedule." He pointed out that the petroleum industry's tasks for the first three years of the First Five-Year Plan have been overfulfilled. Now, the production enthusiasm of petroleum workers in various regions is very high. Many enterprises, workshops, teams, and drilling teams have proposed initiatives and guarantees to complete the First Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule. As long as the petroleum trade union organizations at all levels better unite and organize all staff and workers, learn and apply advanced experiences, and fully tap the internal potential of enterprises, the First Five-Year Plan can be completed ahead of schedule.

    At the meeting, the congratulatory message from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to the Congress was read.

    More than 200 representatives from 36 units in the national petroleum industry system, including geological exploration, oil extraction, oil refining, and capital construction, attended the conference.

    Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Dong Xin, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Zhou Wenlong, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Petroleum Industry, and representatives from the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, the National Committee of the Chinese Heavy Industry Trade Union, and other units attended today's meeting.

### 中国石油工会首次全国代表大会开幕

1956-02-22
第1版()
专栏：

    中国石油工会首次全国代表大会开幕
    新华社21日讯　中国石油工会第一次全国代表大会今天在北京开幕。这次会议的主要任务是：检查和总结过去几年来的石油工会工作，确定今后石油工会工作的方针和任务，选举并产生中国石油工会全国委员会。
    今天的会议上，中国石油工会筹备委员会主任张兆美作了“为动员石油工业中全体职工争取提早完成第一个五年计划而奋斗”的报告。他指出，第一个五年计划中前三个年度的石油工业任务已经超额完成，现在各地石油工人的生产热情很高，许多企业、车间、班组、钻井队等纷纷提出提早完成第一个五年计划的倡议和保证条件。只要各级石油工会组织更好地团结和组织全体职工，学习和运用先进经验，充分挖掘企业内部潜在力量，第一个五年计划是可以提早完成的。
    会上，宣读了中国共产党中央委员会向代表大会的祝词。
    参加这次大会的有全国石油工业系统的地质勘探、采油、炼油和基本建设等36个单位的200多个代表。
    中华全国总工会主席赖若愚、中华全国总工会书记处书记董昕、石油工业部副部长周文龙以及青年团中央、中国重工业工会全国委员会等单位的代表，出席了今天的会议。
