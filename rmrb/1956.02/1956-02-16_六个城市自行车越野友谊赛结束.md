Title: Six-City Bicycle Cross-Country Friendship Competition Concludes
Author:
Date: 1956-02-16
Page: 2

The six-city bicycle cross-country friendship competition, involving young male and female bicycle athletes from Beijing, Tianjin, Tangshan, Baoding, Zhangjiakou, and Shijiazhuang, was held on the morning of the 15th on the highway from Chaoyangmen outside Beijing to Tongxian, braving the cold wind.

That evening, the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League held an award ceremony and get-together for the male and female athletes who participated in the six-city bicycle cross-country friendship competition. Liu Xiyuan, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Youth League, presented awards to the male and female bicycle teams that won the top three places in the overall team score, the top five female athletes, and the top ten male athletes in the competition.

The top three teams in the men's team overall score were the bicycle teams from Beijing, Tianjin, and Tangshan. The top three teams in the women's team overall score were the bicycle teams from Beijing, Tangshan, and Tianjin. Among the top five female athletes, the first, second, third, and fifth places were all athletes from Beijing; the fourth place was an athlete from Shijiazhuang. All of the top ten male athletes were from Beijing.

(According to Xinhua News Agency report) (Attached picture)

### 六个城市自行车越野友谊赛结束

1956-02-16
第2版()
专栏：

    六个城市自行车越野友谊赛结束
    北京、天津、唐山、保定、张家口、石家庄六个城市的男、女青年自行车运动员15日上午冒着寒风在北京市朝阳门外通往通县的公路上，举行了自行车越野友谊比赛。
    当天晚上，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会为参加六个城市自行车越野友谊比赛的男女运动员举行了发奖和联欢晚会。青年团中央委员会书记处书记刘西元向在这次比赛中获得团体总分前3名的男女自行车队、获得优胜的前5名女运动员和前10名男运动员发了奖。
    获得男子组团体总分前3名的是北京、天津和唐山市的自行车队。获得女子组团体总分前3名的是北京、唐山和天津市的自行车队。在获得优胜的前5名女运动员中第一、二、三、五名都是北京市的运动员；第四名是石家庄市的运动员。获得男子组前10名的都是北京市的运动员。
                  （据新华社讯）（附图片）
