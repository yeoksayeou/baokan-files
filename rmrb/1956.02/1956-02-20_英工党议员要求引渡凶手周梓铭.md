Title: British Labour MP Demands Extradition of Murderer Zhou Zhiming
Author:
Date: 1956-02-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, 16th: British Labour MP, Konni Zilliacus, said in Parliament on the 15th that the British government should request the US government's assistance in extraditing Zhou Zhiming, the murderer who sabotaged the Indian "Kashmir Princess" plane and has fled to Taiwan.

Zilliacus said that the Chiang Kai-shek regime's survival in Taiwan is entirely dependent on American aid and American arms. Therefore, Foreign Secretary Lloyd should request the US government to order "its protectorate" to extradite "this wanted murderer".

### 英工党议员要求引渡凶手周梓铭

1956-02-20
第4版()
专栏：

    英工党议员要求引渡凶手周梓铭
    新华社16日讯　伦敦消息：英国工党议员康尼·齐里亚库斯15日在议会中说，英国政府应该要求美国政府协助引渡已经逃到台湾去的破坏印度“克什米尔公主号”飞机的凶手周梓铭。
    齐里亚库斯说，蒋介石政权之得以在台湾生存下去，全赖美国的援助和美国的军火，因此，外交大臣劳埃德应该要求美国政府令“它的被保护人”引渡“这名被索取中的凶手”。
