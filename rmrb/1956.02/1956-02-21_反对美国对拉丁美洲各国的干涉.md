Title: Opposing US Interference in Latin American Countries
Author: Pablo Neruda
Date: 1956-02-21
Page: 3

    Opposing US Interference in Latin American Countries
    Chilean Poet Pablo Neruda
    The countries of the Americas were born in the struggle against colonial powers, so our people welcome and support the independence movements of the Asian and African peoples. We believe that US support for colonialism is a betrayal of American principles. Furthermore, we oppose US aggression against Guatemala, the conquest of Puerto Rico, and political and economic interference in Latin American countries. We pay tribute to the People's China because it is a country that has set an example for Asia. We extend fraternal greetings.

### 反对美国对拉丁美洲各国的干涉
巴勃罗·聂鲁达
1956-02-21
第3版()
专栏：

    反对美国对拉丁美洲各国的干涉
    智利诗人　巴勃罗·聂鲁达
    美洲各国是在反对殖民列强的斗争中诞生的，因此我们各国人民欢迎并支持亚非人民的独立运动。我们认为美国对殖民主义的支持，是背叛美洲原则的行为。此外，我们反对美国侵略危地马拉，征服波多黎各，对拉丁美洲各国进行政治上与经济上的干涉。我们向人民中国致敬，因为它是一个替亚洲作出了榜样的国家。谨致兄弟般的敬礼。
