Title: Chinese Government Delegation to Leipzig Fair Departs for Germany
Author:
Date: 1956-02-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency Dispatch:
    The People's Republic of China Government Delegation to the Leipzig Fair, consisting of more than 20 people and led by Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 15th for the German Democratic Republic.

### 我国政府参观莱比锡博览会代表团赴德

1956-02-16
第4版()
专栏：

    我国政府参观莱比锡博览会代表团赴德
    新华社讯　中华人民共和国政府参观莱比锡博览会代表团一行20余人，由中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸率领，在15日早晨乘飞机离北京赴德意志民主共和国。
