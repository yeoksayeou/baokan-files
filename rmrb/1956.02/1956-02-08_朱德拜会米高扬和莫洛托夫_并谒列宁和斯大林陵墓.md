Title: Zhu De Visits Mikoyan and Molotov, and Pays Respects at the Lenin and Stalin Mausoleums
Author:
Date: 1956-02-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 7th: Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, paid a visit to Mikoyan, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, at 11:30 am on February 7th.

Accompanying him were Marshal Nie Rongzhen, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council of the People's Republic of China, Liu Lantao, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Liu Xiao, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union.

Present at the meeting was Fedin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union.

Zhu De and Mikoyan held a one-hour conversation.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 7th: Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, paid a visit to Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, at 3:00 pm on February 7th.

Accompanying him were Marshal Nie Rongzhen, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council of the People's Republic of China, Liu Lantao, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Liu Xiao, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union.

Present at the meeting was Fedin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union.

Zhu De and Molotov held a conversation lasting approximately one hour.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 7th: Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, went to Red Square at 11:00 am on February 7th to pay respects at the Lenin and Stalin Mausoleums, and laid two wreaths in front of the mausoleums.

Accompanying Vice Chairman Zhu De were Marshal Nie Rongzhen, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council of the People's Republic of China, Liu Lantao, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Liu Xiao, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, among others.

Accompanying them on the Soviet side were Gorkin, Deputy Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Vodelin, Commandant of the Kremlin Garrison, Gudev, Director of the Far East Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, and Fomin, Chief of Protocol of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, among others.

### 朱德拜会米高扬和莫洛托夫  并谒列宁和斯大林陵墓

1956-02-08
第4版()
专栏：

    朱德拜会米高扬和莫洛托夫
    并谒列宁和斯大林陵墓
    新华社莫斯科7日电　中华人民共和国副主席朱德2月7日上午11时30分拜会了苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬。
    随同前往的有中华人民共和国国防委员会副主席聂荣臻元帅、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员刘澜涛和中国驻苏联大使刘晓。
    拜会时在座的有苏联外交部副部长费德林。
    朱德和米高扬进行了一小时的谈话。
    新华社莫斯科7日电　中华人民共和国副主席朱德2月7日下午3时拜会了苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部长莫洛托夫。
    随同前往的有中华人民共和国国防委员会副主席聂荣臻元帅、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员刘澜涛和中国驻苏联大使刘晓。
    拜会时在座的有苏联外交部副部长费德林。
    朱德和莫洛托夫进行了约一小时的谈话。
    新华社莫斯科7日电　中华人民共和国副主席朱德2月7日上午11时前往红场拜谒了列宁和斯大林陵墓，并且在陵墓前献了两个花圈。
    随同朱德副主席谒陵的有中华人民共和国国防委员会副主席聂荣臻元帅、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员刘澜涛和中国驻苏联大使刘晓等人。
    苏联方面陪同前往的有苏联最高苏维埃主席团副秘书戈尔金、克里姆林宫卫戍司令沃杰林、苏联外交部远东司司长顾德夫、苏联最高苏维埃主席团礼宾长弗明等人。
