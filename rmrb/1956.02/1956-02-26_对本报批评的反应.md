Title: Reactions to the Newspaper's Criticism
Author:
Date: 1956-02-26
Page: 2

    Reactions to the Newspaper's Criticism
    On December 14th of last year, this newspaper, in an article entitled "Such Lavish Temporary Construction," criticized the 306 Exploration Team of the Geological Bureau of the Ministry of Heavy Industry for serious wasteful behavior in the construction of temporary projects. Now the 306 Exploration Team of the Geological Bureau of the Ministry of Heavy Industry has sent a letter of self-criticism.

### 对本报批评的反应

1956-02-26
第2版()
专栏：

    对本报批评的反应
    去年12月14日，本报在“这么阔气的临时工程”一文中，批评重工业部地质局306勘探队在建造临时工程中有严重的浪费行为。现在重工业部地质局306勘探队已来信检讨。
