Title: Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation Arrives in Cairo for Visit
Author:
Date: 1956-02-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 27th: The Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation, invited by the Egyptian government to visit Egypt, arrived in Cairo, the capital of Egypt, by plane on the afternoon of February 25th. The delegation was led by Bawerhan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Director of the China Islamic Association, and representative of the National People's Congress.

The delegation was welcomed at the airport by Egyptian government officials. Those present at the airport to welcome the delegation included: Najib Haissin, Deputy Undersecretary of the Egyptian Ministry of Education; Ali Sahar, representative of Ahmed Hassani Bakuri, Minister of Religious Affairs; El Sayad, representative of Anwar Sadat, Minister of State; as well as representatives from the Department of National Guidance and Sitki, the manager of the Egyptian National Theater, and others.

Chairman Bawerhan delivered a speech at the airport, expressing his gratitude for the welcome from the Egyptian government.

Haissin delivered a welcoming speech, saying that since the Bandung Conference, the Egyptian people have gained a further understanding of China through the visits of Minister of Religious Affairs Bakuri and other delegations. He expressed his belief that through this visit and performance of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation in Egypt, the people of Egypt and China will become even closer.

### 我国文化艺术代表团到达开罗访问

1956-02-28
第4版()
专栏：

    我国文化艺术代表团到达开罗访问
    新华社27日讯　开罗消息：应埃及政府邀请到埃及访问的中华人民共和国文化艺术代表团，在中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席、中国伊斯兰教协会主任、全国人民代表大会代表鲍尔汉团长率领下，2月25日下午乘飞机到达埃及首都开罗。
    代表团在机场受到埃及政府官员们的欢迎。到机场欢迎的有：埃及教育部的代表副国务秘书纳吉布·海辛、宗教事务部长艾哈迈德·哈萨尼·巴库里的代表阿里·萨哈尔、国务部长安瓦尔·萨达特的代表艾尔·萨雅德，以及国家指导部的代表和埃及国家剧院经理锡特基等。
    鲍尔汉团长在机场致词，对埃及政府的欢迎表示感谢。
    海辛致欢迎词说，在万隆会议以后，埃及人民通过宗教事务部长巴库里和其他代表团的访问，对于中国已经有了进一步的了解。他表示相信，通过这一次中国文化艺术代表团在埃及的访问演出，埃中两国人民将会更加接近。
