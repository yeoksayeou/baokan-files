Title: Japanese House of Representatives Passes Resolution Demanding Prohibition of Nuclear Weapons Testing
Author: 
Date: 1956-02-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo: The Japanese House of Representatives held a plenary session on February 9 and unanimously passed the "Resolution Demanding the Prohibition of Atomic and Hydrogen Bomb Testing" jointly proposed by the Socialist Party and the Liberal Democratic Party at the suggestion of the Socialist Party. The House of Councillors will also discuss this bill on February 10.

### 日本众议院通过决议  要求禁止试验核子武器

1956-02-10
第4版()
专栏：

    日本众议院通过决议
    要求禁止试验核子武器
    据新华社讯　东京消息：日本国会众议院2月9日举行全体会议，一致通过了在社会党建议下由社会党和自由民主党联合提出的“要求禁止试验原子弹和氢弹决议案”。参议院也将在2月10日讨论这个议案。
