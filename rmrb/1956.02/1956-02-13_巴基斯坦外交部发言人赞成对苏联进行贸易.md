Title: Pakistani Foreign Ministry Spokesman Favors Trade with the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-02-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 12th: A Pakistani Foreign Ministry spokesman said on the 11th that trade "could be beneficial" "in promoting friendly relations between the Soviet Union and Pakistan."

The spokesman made the remarks in response to a reporter's question about Pakistan's upcoming trade negotiations with the Soviet Union.

He also said: "The United States has no reason to be alarmed by our trade negotiations with Russia. Such negotiations are taking place all over the world, and all countries are conducting them."

### 巴基斯坦外交部发言人赞成对苏联进行贸易

1956-02-13
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦外交部发言人赞成对苏联进行贸易
    新华社12日讯　喀喇蚩消息：巴基斯坦外交部发言人在11日说：“在促进苏联和巴基斯坦的友好关系中”，贸易“可以成为有益的东西”。
    发言人是在答复新闻记者关于巴基斯坦行将和苏联举行贸易谈判的问题时说这番话的。
    他还说：“美国没有理由对我们同俄国进行贸易谈判感到惊慌。这种谈判全世界到处都在进行，所有各国都在进行。”
