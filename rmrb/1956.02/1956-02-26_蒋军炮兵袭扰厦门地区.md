Title: Chiang Kai-shek's Artillery Harasses the Xiamen Area
Author:
Date: 1956-02-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 25th — On the 24th, Chiang Kai-shek's artillery, entrenched on Jinmen Island, fired more than 190 shells at the Xiamen Island and Dadeng Island areas, destroying three civilian houses on Xiamen Island and injuring one resident.
    From the 19th to the 24th, Chiang Kai-shek's artillery bombarded the Xiamen, Dadeng, and Lianhe areas every day, and our artillery returned fire.

### 蒋军炮兵袭扰厦门地区

1956-02-26
第1版()
专栏：

    蒋军炮兵袭扰厦门地区
    新华社福建前线25日电　24日，盘踞金门岛上的蒋军炮兵向我厦门岛和大嶝岛地区发射炮弹190多发，打毁厦门岛上民房3间，打伤居民1人。
    从19日到24日，蒋军炮兵每天都向我厦门、大嶝、莲河地区轰击，我军炮兵给予还击。
