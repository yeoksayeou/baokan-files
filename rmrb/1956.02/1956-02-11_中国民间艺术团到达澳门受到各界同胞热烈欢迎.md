Title: Chinese Folk Art Troupe Arrives in Macau, Receiving Warm Welcome from Compatriots

Author: 
Date: 1956-02-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency report:

    The Chinese Folk Art Troupe, comprising over 190 members and led by Ouyang Shan, Chairman of the South China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, arrived in Macau by train from Guangzhou on the 9th, responding to an invitation from the Macau Chinese General Chamber of Commerce to perform in Macau.

    Among those welcoming them at the station were Ho Yin, Chairman of the Macau Chinese General Chamber of Commerce; Ma Man-kei, Vice Chairman; Liang Pei, Chairman of the Macau Federation of Trade Unions; Zhang Qinghui, Chairman of the Macau Democratic Women's Federation; Rong Zhansheng, representative of the Macau Student Union; Cui Deqi, Chairman of the Macau Tung Sin Tong Charitable Society; and representatives from various trade unions, public organizations, and compatriots from all walks of life in Macau, totaling over 1,000 people.

    On the evening of the 9th, the Macau compatriots' Welcome Committee for the Chinese Folk Art Troupe's Visit and Performance held a grand reception at the International Hotel, hosting a banquet for all members of the art troupe.

    The Chinese Folk Art Troupe will hold a preview reception on the 11th and officially begin performances on the 12th.

### 中国民间艺术团到达澳门受到各界同胞热烈欢迎

1956-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

    中国民间艺术团到达澳门受到各界同胞热烈欢迎
    据新华社讯　应澳门中华总商会邀请前往澳门演出的中国民间艺术团全体团员190多人，在团长华南文学艺术界联合会主席欧阳山率领下，9日下午由广州乘车到达澳门。
    到车站欢迎的有澳门中华总商会理事长何贤、副理事长马万祺、澳门工会联合总会理事长梁培、澳门民主妇女联合会主席张晴晖、澳门学生联合会代表容战生、澳门同善堂理事长崔德祺和澳门各业工会、公众团体代表以及各界同胞共1，000多人。
    9日晚上，澳门各界同胞欢迎中国民间艺术团访澳演出委员会在国际酒店举行盛大的酒会，欢宴艺术团全体团员。
    中国民间艺术团将在11日举行招待预演，12日正式演出。
