Title: Bulganin's Answers to Reporters Evoke Reaction in Pakistan; Foreign Minister Chaudhry Says Efforts Will Be Made to Maintain Friendly Relations with the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-02-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 8th: Pakistani Foreign Minister Chaudhry issued a statement on February 7th through the government's publicity broadcasting department, saying: "The Pakistani government will always strive to maintain friendly relations with the Soviet Union and improve these relations as much as possible."
    He made this statement when commenting on Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Bulganin's reply to questions from the editor of the "Pakistan United Press." He also said that Pakistan greatly needs economic cooperation from major powers and always keeps this need in mind when acting.
    Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 8th: TASS Karachi, 7th: Newspapers in the Pakistani capital published on February 7th news of Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Bulganin's reply to the "Pakistan United Press" regarding the issue of Pakistani-Soviet relations.
    The newspapers published the full text of the "Pakistan United Press" questions and Bulganin's answers. The headlines of the newspapers were: "Bulganin Talks About Pakistani-Soviet Relations," "Russia Suggests Concluding a Trade Agreement," and "Russia Suggests Improving Relations with Pakistan on the Basis of the Five Principles."

### 布尔加宁答记者问在巴基斯坦的反应  乔德里外长说要努力保持同苏联的友好关系

1956-02-09
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁答记者问在巴基斯坦的反应
    乔德里外长说要努力保持同苏联的友好关系
    新华社8日讯　喀喇蚩消息：巴基斯坦外交部长乔德里2月7日通过政府宣传广播部发表声明说：“巴基斯坦政府将永远努力保持同苏联的友好关系，并且尽可能地改善这种关系。”
    他是在评论苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁答复“巴基斯坦统一通讯社”编辑的问题的时候发表这个声明的。他还说，巴基斯坦非常需要大国的经济合作，而且在行动的时候始终把这种需要记在心头。
    新华社8日讯　塔斯社喀喇蚩7日讯：巴基斯坦首都各报在2月7日都发表了苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁答复“巴基斯坦统一通讯社”关于巴基斯坦和苏联关系问题的消息。
    各报登载了“巴基斯坦统一通讯社”的问题和布尔加宁的答复的全文。各报的标题是：“布尔加宁谈巴苏关系”，“俄国建议缔结贸易协定”，“俄国建议在五项原则的基础上改进同巴基斯坦的关系”。
