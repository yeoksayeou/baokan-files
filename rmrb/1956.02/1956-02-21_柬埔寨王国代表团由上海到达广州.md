Title: Cambodian Royal Delegation Arrives in Guangzhou from Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1956-02-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, February 20th: The Cambodian Royal National Delegation, headed by Prince Norodom Sihanouk, left Shanghai by special plane this morning for Guangzhou.

Before boarding the plane, Prince Sihanouk and others shook hands with Xu Jianguo, Sheng Pihua, Cao Diqiu, and Song Richang, Vice Mayors of Shanghai; Hu Juewan, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Lieutenant General Wang Bicheng, Commander of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; heads of Shanghai's various organizations and groups; people from all walks of life; and consuls general and diplomatic officials of various countries stationed in Shanghai to bid farewell.

Prince Sihanouk presented Vice Mayor Xu Jianguo with exquisite silver kettles and silver boxes produced in Cambodia. Sihanouk said, "Thank you very much for your warm and brotherly reception."

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, February 20th: The Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, Prince Norodom Sihanouk, and the Cambodian Royal National Delegation he leads arrived in Wuhan at 12:30 noon today and took a plane to Guangzhou at 1:30 pm. Zhang Tixue, Governor of Hubei Province, Song Kanfu, Mayor of Wuhan, and Lieutenant General Tan Furen, Head of the Wuhan Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, greeted and saw off the distinguished guests at the airport and had lunch with them.

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, February 20th: The Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, Prince Norodom Sihanouk, and the Cambodian Royal National Delegation he leads, arrived in Guangzhou by special plane at 4:40 pm today. Welcoming the distinguished guests at the airport were Tao Zhu, Governor of Guangdong Province, Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou, General Huang Yongsheng, Head of the Guangzhou Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and heads of party and government organs, democratic parties, and people's organizations in Guangdong Province and Guangzhou, as well as religious figures.

In the evening, Prince Sihanouk and all members of the delegation were invited to attend a banquet hosted by Tao Zhu, Governor of Guangdong Province, in their honor.

### 柬埔寨王国代表团由上海到达广州

1956-02-21
第1版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨王国代表团由上海到达广州
    据新华社上海20日电　以诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王为首的柬埔寨王国国家代表团今天上午乘专机离开上海前往广州。
    西哈努克亲王等人上飞机前，同前来送行的上海市副市长许建国、盛丕华、曹荻秋、宋日昌，中国人民政治协商会议上海市委员会副主席胡厥文，中国人民解放军上海警备区司令员王必成中将，以及上海各机关团体负责人、各界人士、各国驻上海总领事和外交官员们一一握手道别。
    西哈努克亲王把柬埔寨出产的精致的银壶和银盒赠送给副市长许建国。西哈努克说：“非常感谢你们热诚的兄弟般的接待。”
    新华社武汉20日电　柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和他所率领的柬埔寨王国国家代表团在今天中午12时30分到达武汉，1时30分乘飞机去广州。湖北省省长张体学、武汉市市长宋侃夫和中国人民解放军武汉驻军首长谭甫仁中将等，都到机场欢迎和欢送，并且同贵宾们共进午餐。
    新华社广州20日电　柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和他所率领的柬埔寨王国国家代表团，今天下午4时40分乘专机到达广州。在机场上欢迎贵宾们的，有广东省省长陶铸，广州市市长朱光，中国人民解放军广州驻军首长黄永胜上将，广东省和广州市党政机关、民主党派和人民团体的负责人以及宗教界人士。
    晚间，西哈努克亲王和代表团全体人员应邀出席了广东省省长陶铸为他们举行的宴会。
