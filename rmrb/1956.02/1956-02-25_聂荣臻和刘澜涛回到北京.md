Title: Nie Rongzhen and Liu Lantao Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-02-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 24th: Nie Rongzhen and Liu Lantao, members of the People's Republic of China delegation that attended the 80th birthday celebration of Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, and members of the Central Committee delegation of the Communist Party of China that attended the Second Congress of the Romanian Workers' Party, returned to Beijing by train on the 24th.

### 聂荣臻和刘澜涛回到北京

1956-02-25
第1版()
专栏：

    聂荣臻和刘澜涛回到北京
    据新华社24日讯　前往参加德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克80寿辰庆祝典礼的中华人民共和国代表团和参加罗马尼亚工人党第二次代表大会的中国共产党中央委员会代表团团员聂荣臻和刘澜涛，24日乘火车回到北京。
