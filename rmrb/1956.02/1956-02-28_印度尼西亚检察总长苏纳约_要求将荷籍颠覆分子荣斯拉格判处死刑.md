Title: Indonesian Attorney General Sunarjo Demands Death Penalty for Dutch Subversive Rong Schlag
Author:
Date: 1956-02-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 24th. Indonesian Attorney General Sunarjo demanded the death penalty for Rong Schlag, a Dutch subversive leader, during the 60th trial of the Rong Schlag case on February 23rd.

Sunarjo pointed out that the defendant Rong Schlag, who had served as a Dutch intelligence officer, had personally led an armed subversive organization against the Republic of Indonesia. The defendant had organized and instigated conspiratorial meetings and provided weapons, ammunition, food, and money to support rebel organizations such as the "Islamic State Movement" and the "Indonesian Islamic Army" against the government.

Sunarjo pointed out that witnesses testified that in these criminal activities, especially in the airdrops to supply armed bandits, planes from the U.S. Embassy in Indonesia also participated.

During this trial of Rong Schlag, the courtroom was packed with angry crowds. When the defendant appeared in court, they shouted, "Death penalty! Death penalty!"

The Indonesian "People's Daily" editorial on February 24 welcomed Sunarjo's demand for the death penalty for Rong Schlag, arguing that it reflected the wishes of the Indonesian people. The "People's Daily" stated that the Rong Schlag incident was not just an individual issue, but a colonialism issue.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 26th. The "East Star News" reported on the 25th that political figures in Jakarta, the capital of Indonesia, believe that Chief Prosecutor Sunarjo's demand to sentence Dutch subversive Rong Schlag to death is reasonable.

The report said that the Indonesian General Labor Union and the Oil Workers Union have expressed their support for Sunarjo's demand. The Indonesian General Labor Union pointed out that this demand is in line with the wishes of Indonesian workers.

### 印度尼西亚检察总长苏纳约  要求将荷籍颠覆分子荣斯拉格判处死刑

1956-02-28
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚检察总长苏纳约
    要求将荷籍颠覆分子荣斯拉格判处死刑
    新华社雅加达24日电　印度尼西亚检察总长苏纳约2月23日在对荷兰籍颠覆分子荣斯拉格案件进行的第60次审讯时要求将荷兰籍颠覆活动领袖荣斯拉格判处死刑。
    苏纳约指出，曾充当荷兰情报官的被告荣斯拉格曾经亲自领导反对印度尼西亚共和国的武装颠覆组织，被告曾组织策动阴谋活动的会议，并且输送武器、弹药、粮食和金钱支援“回教国运动”和“印度尼西亚回教军”等叛乱组织来反对政府。
    苏纳约指出，证人的证词表明，在这些犯罪活动中，特别是在接济武装匪徒的空投中，美国驻印度尼西亚大使馆的飞机也参加了。
    在这次审讯荣斯拉格的时候，法庭里挤满了愤怒的群众。当被告在法庭出现的时候，他们高喊着：“死刑！死刑！”
    印度尼西亚“人民日报”2月24日的社论欢迎苏纳约所提出的关于把荣斯拉格判处死刑的要求，这家报纸认为它反映了印度尼西亚人民的愿望。“人民日报”说，荣斯拉格事件不只是个人的问题，而且是殖民主义问题。
    新华社雅加达26日电　“东星报”25日报道，印度尼西亚首都雅加达的政界人士认为，首席检察官苏纳约关于判处荷兰颠覆分子荣斯拉格以死刑的要求是合理的。
    消息说，印度尼西亚总工会和石油工人联合会已经表示支持苏纳约的要求。印度尼西亚总工会指出，这个要求是符合印度尼西亚工人的愿望的。
