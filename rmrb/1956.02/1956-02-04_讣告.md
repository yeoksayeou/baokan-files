Obituary

Comrade Yang Weiyu, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Light Industry of the People's Republic of China, member of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and Standing Committee member of the Central Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association, passed away due to illness at 1:10 AM on February 3, 1956, in Beijing, at the age of 68. The body has been placed in a coffin on the same day and moved to Jiaxing Temple, Xihuangchenggen, Di'anmenwai, in this city. A public memorial service will be held from 9:00 AM to 11:00 AM on February 8th, after which the coffin will be moved and buried in the Babaoshan Revolutionary Cemetery. Friends of Comrade Yang Weiyu during his lifetime who wish to send wreaths or elegiac couplets are requested to send them directly to the office of this association at Jiaxing Temple. This is a formal announcement.

### 讣告

1956-02-04
第4版()
专栏：

    讣告
    中华人民共和国轻工业部副部长、中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会委员、中国民主建国会中央委员会常务委员杨卫玉同志，因病于1956年2月3日上午1时10分在北京逝世，享年68岁。已于当日成殓，移灵于本市地安门外西黄城根嘉兴寺。兹定于2月8日上午9时至11时举行公祭，公祭后起灵安葬于八宝山革命公墓。杨卫玉同志生前友好，如有赠送挽联花圈者，请迳送嘉兴寺本会办事处，特此讣告。
