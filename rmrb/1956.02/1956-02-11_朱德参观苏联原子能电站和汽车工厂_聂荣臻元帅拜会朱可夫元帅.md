Title: Zhu De Visits Soviet Atomic Energy Power Station and Automobile Factory; Marshal Nie Rongzhen Calls on Marshal Zhukov
Author:
Date: 1956-02-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 10th: Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and his entourage formally concluded their visits in Moscow on February 10th.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 10th: Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, accompanied by Nie Rongzhen, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council of the People's Republic of China, Liu Lantao, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Liu Xiao, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, and other accompanying personnel, visited the Atomic Energy Power Station of the Soviet Academy of Sciences on February 9th.

While visiting the atomic energy power station, Vice Chairman Zhu De and others listened to detailed explanations from the responsible staff of the power station and also watched the documentary film "The First in the World" about the power station.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 10th: Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, accompanied by his entourage, visited the Stalin Automobile Factory on the morning of February 10th.

After arriving at the factory, Vice Chairman Zhu De and others were accompanied by Strokin, Minister of the Soviet Automobile Industry, and Kvymov, Director of the factory, for the visit. Vice Chairman Zhu De visited the factory's tool workshop, casting workshop, and assembly workshop in detail, and watched the production processes of these workshops. While visiting the workshops, the workers applauded warmly to express their respect to Zhu De. Chinese interns who were interning in the workshops also came to pay their respects to Zhu De and talked about their internship experiences.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 10th: Marshal Nie Rongzhen, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council of the People's Republic of China, called on Marshal Zhukov, Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union, at 3 p.m. on February 10th and had a 35-minute conversation with him.

### 朱德参观苏联原子能电站和汽车工厂  聂荣臻元帅拜会朱可夫元帅

1956-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德参观苏联原子能电站和汽车工厂
    聂荣臻元帅拜会朱可夫元帅
    新华社莫斯科10日电　中华人民共和国副主席朱德和他的随行人员在2月10日正式结束了在莫斯科的参观和访问。
    据新华社莫斯科10日电　中华人民共和国朱德副主席，2月9日偕同中华人民共和国国防委员会副主席聂荣臻元帅、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员刘澜涛、中国驻苏联大使刘晓和其他随行人员参观了苏联科学院原子能电力站。
    朱德副主席等在原子能电力站参观时，听取了电力站负责工作人员的详细解释，还看了关于这个电力站的纪录影片“世界上第一个”。
    据新华社莫斯科10日电　中华人民共和国副主席朱德偕同随行人员在2月10日上午参观了斯大林汽车工厂。
    朱德副主席等到达工厂以后，由苏联汽车工业部部长斯特罗金和工厂厂长克维莫夫等陪同参观。朱德副主席详细地参观了工厂的工具车间、铸造车间、装配车间，看了这些车间的生产过程。在参观车间时，车间工人热烈鼓掌，向朱德表示敬意。在车间实习的中国实习生也纷纷前来向朱德致敬。他们谈了生产实习的情况。
    新华社莫斯科10日电　中华人民共和国国防委员会副主席聂荣臻元帅2月10日下午3时拜会了苏联国防部长朱可夫元帅，并且同他作了35分钟的谈话。
