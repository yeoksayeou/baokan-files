Title: Construction Workers of the Shizitan Hydropower Station Strive to Complete Construction Tasks Ahead of Schedule
Author: 
Date: 1956-02-15
Page: 2

Construction workers of the Shizitan Hydropower Engineering Bureau, after repeated discussions, decided to complete all civil engineering construction of the Shizitan Hydropower Station one year and one month ahead of schedule, before the end of November this year, and ensure the completion of the installation of two turbine generator units before the end of this year. It was also decided to complete the expansion project of the Shangdong Hydropower Station before National Day; and to start construction of the Huilongzhai Hydropower Station ahead of schedule in the third quarter of this year. This decision was unanimously adopted at the recent general meeting of employee representatives of the bureau.
	(Our correspondent stationed in Chengdu)

### 建设狮子滩水电站职工  争取提前完成建设任务

1956-02-15
第2版()
专栏：

    建设狮子滩水电站职工
    争取提前完成建设任务
    狮子滩水力发电工程局全体职工经过反复讨论，决定提早一年零一个月，在今年11月底以前完成狮子滩水电站的全部土木建筑工程，并保证在今年年底以前完成两台水轮机机组的安装工作。还决定在国庆节前完成上硐水电站扩建工程；在今年第三季度提早开工建设回龙寨水电站。这项决定是在最近举行的全局职工代表大会上一致通过的。
            （本报驻成都记者）
