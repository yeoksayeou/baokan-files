Title: Actively Carry Out the Struggle Against Colonialism
Author: 
Date: 1956-02-21
Page: 4

    Actively Carry Out the Struggle Against Colonialism
    In Indonesia The All-Indonesia People's Congress has sent a telegram to the World Federation of Democratic Youth, supporting the holding of "Day of Struggle Against the Colonial System" on February 21.
    The telegram pointed out that through the activities of the "Day of Struggle Against the Colonial System", unity will be strengthened, and the idea of ​​eliminating colonialism will be more deeply rooted in the hearts of the people.
    In Algeria On February 16, Algerian people's armed uprising forces equipped with submachine guns fought with French troops for 8 hours in the Nimancha Mountains, 96 miles south of Constantine. According to a United Press report, 28 French soldiers were killed or wounded.
    According to an announcement by the French authorities in Algeria on February 18, 6,000 French people began to withdraw from an area of ​​70 square miles, 75 miles west of Oran, along the Moroccan border. The people's armed forces in this area are particularly active.
    In Morocco Eight attacks on French troops occurred in Rabat and Casablanca, Morocco, on the evening of February 16 and the morning of February 17. In Berguent on the border between Morocco and Algeria, the Moroccan people's armed forces engaged in fierce fighting with French troops.
    In Casablanca, Rabat, Oujda, and other places, an underground organization called the "Black Crescent Society" distributed leaflets announcing the launch of a struggle for the freedom of Morocco.
    In Tunisia Recently, incidents against France's continued control of Tunisia have also occurred continuously. The French Resident General in Tunisia, Seydoux, admitted: Recently, "the situation in Tunisia has deteriorated," and he "feels very disturbed" by this situation.
                (Xinhua News Agency)

### 积极进行反对殖民主义的斗争

1956-02-21
第4版()
专栏：

    积极进行反对殖民主义的斗争
    在印度尼西亚　全印度尼西亚人民代表大会已经打电报给世界民主青年联盟，支持在2月21日举行“反对殖民制度斗争日”。
    电报指出，通过举行“反对殖民制度斗争日”的活动，将加强团结，并且使消灭殖民主义的思想更加深入人心。
    在阿尔及利亚　2月16日，装备着手提机关枪的阿尔及利亚人民武装起义部队同法国军队在君士坦丁以南96英里的尼曼查山进行了8小时战斗，据合众社消息，法军死伤了28人。
    据阿尔及利亚法国当局2月18日宣布，在阿兰以西75英里，沿摩洛哥边境的面积达70平方英里地区，6，000名法国人开始撤退。这个地区的人民武装部队特别活跃。
    在摩洛哥　摩洛哥的拉巴特和卡萨布兰卡在2月16日晚上和17日早晨接连发生8起袭击法军事件。在摩洛哥同阿尔及利亚边界的伯尔日，摩洛哥人民武装力量同法军发生了激战。
    在卡萨布兰卡、拉巴特和欧奇达等地，一个名叫“黑新月社”的地下组织散发了传单，宣布要展开争取摩洛哥自由的斗争。
    在突尼斯　突尼斯最近也不断发生反对法国继续控制突尼斯的事件。法国驻突尼斯总督赛杜承认：最近，“突尼斯的局势恶化了”，他对这种情况“感到非常不安”。
                （据新华社讯）
