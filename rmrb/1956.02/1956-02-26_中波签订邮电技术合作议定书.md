Title: China and Poland Sign Postal and Telecommunications Technology Cooperation Protocol
Author:
Date: 1956-02-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 25th -- The Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the People's Republic of China and the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the People's Republic of Poland, in accordance with the Sino-Polish Agreement on Technical and Technological Scientific Cooperation, signed a protocol in Beijing on the 25th regarding postal and telecommunications technology cooperation. Representing the Chinese side was Shen Guang, Assistant Minister of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the People's Republic of China. Representing the Polish side was Mieczysław Fryszczak, head of the Polish Postal and Telecommunications Delegation and Director of the Central Broadcasting Station Administration of the Polish Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications.

### 中波签订邮电技术合作议定书

1956-02-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中波签订邮电技术合作议定书
    新华社25日讯　中华人民共和国邮电部和波兰人民共和国邮电部，根据中波技术和技术科学合作协定，25日在北京签订了关于邮政和电信技术合作的议定书。代表中国方面签字的是中华人民共和国邮电部部长助理申光，代表波兰方面签字的是波兰人民共和国邮电代表团团长、波兰邮电部中央广播电台管理局局长密·弗利萨克。
