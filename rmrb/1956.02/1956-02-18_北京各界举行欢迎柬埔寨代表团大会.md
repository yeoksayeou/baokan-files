Title: Beijing Circles Hold Grand Meeting to Welcome Cambodian Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-02-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 17th. All circles in Beijing held a grand welcome meeting this afternoon to welcome the Cambodian Royal National Delegation headed by His Royal Highness Prince Norodom Sihanouk.

More than 8,000 people from all walks of life in Beijing, including well-known social figures, famous peace advocates, scientists, professors, engineers, cultural workers, male and female workers, students, monks, lay Buddhists, other religious figures, government employees, and citizens, dressed in festive attire, joyfully came to attend this grand welcome meeting.

The meeting was presided over by Peng Zhen, Mayor of Beijing.

The meeting was held in the competition hall of the Beijing Gymnasium. The venue was filled with an atmosphere of friendship and joy. At the entrance of the venue, a red velvet screen read: "Long Live Sino-Cambodian Friendship!" The national flags of China and Cambodia were hung in the center behind the rostrum; eight huge colored flags, each painted with a dove of peace and olive branches, were hung behind and opposite the rostrum. A huge banner on the wall opposite the rostrum read: "Long Live the Friendship between the Chinese and Cambodian Peoples!" "Long Live Peace in Asia and the World!" Many colored flags fluttered around the venue.

At 4:50 p.m., amidst a storm of applause and cheers, Prince Norodom Sihanouk, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai and Mayor Peng Zhen, ascended the rostrum. Prince Sihanouk frequently waved to the people who were standing and cheering.

Also on the rostrum were members of the Cambodian Royal National Delegation: Member of the Royal High Council, Lan. ? Te, Member of the Royal High Council, Chin. Di, Deputy Speaker of the Royal Council, Sang. Nian, Member of the National Assembly, Eng. Mi Ass, Member of the Royal Council, Dr. Nil. Smo Uk, Chief of Staff of the Royal Air Force, Colonel Nu. Hu, Deputy Secretary of State for Farmer Welfare, Ron. So Fang, and Private Secretary to the Prince, Feng. Sa Lun.

Also on the rostrum were Li Jishen and Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council, Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General, Marshal Ye Jianying and Fu Zuoyi, Vice-Chairmen of the National Defense Council, Zhang Wentian, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, General Liu Yalou, Commander of the Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Liu Ren, Chairman of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Zhang Youyu, Wang Kunlun, Xue Zizheng, Cheng Hongyi, and Jia Tingsan, Vice Mayors of Beijing, Xiao Ming, Liang Sicheng, Yu Xinqing, and Ling Qijun, Vice-Chairmen of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, as well as responsible persons of the Beijing organizations of various democratic parties, responsible persons of various people's organizations in Beijing, and responsible persons of the PLA units stationed in Beijing.

The band played a welcoming tune, and a group of children in brightly colored costumes presented flowers to the distinguished guests.

The welcome meeting began, and the band played the national anthems of Cambodia and China.

Peng Zhen delivered a welcome speech first. On behalf of all the citizens of Beijing, he expressed his heartfelt thanks and sincere respect to Prince Sihanouk and the Cambodian Royal National Delegation he led for their friendly visit, and asked them to convey the friendship of the people of Beijing and the Chinese people to the citizens of Phnom Penh and the entire Cambodian people.

To the accompaniment of music, Peng Zhen presented gifts and flags to Prince Sihanouk on behalf of the people of Beijing.

Prince Sihanouk then delivered a reply. He expressed his deep gratitude to the citizens of Beijing gathered at this grand and enthusiastic welcome meeting. He said: Being among you who treat us so kindly, and on your great land, we feel completely at home. During his speech, continuous applause resounded throughout the venue.

At the welcome meeting, 320 artists from six art units in Beijing jointly performed a wonderful "Festival Carnival Song and Dance" program for the distinguished guests. After the performance, the delegation presented flowers to the artists, and Prince Sihanouk shook hands with the artists cordially to thank them. At this time, Beijing artists flocked to Prince Sihanouk, and cheers rang out throughout the venue.

The meeting concluded amidst warm applause and music.

After the welcome meeting, the distinguished guests visited the training hall and swimming pool of the Beijing Gymnasium. (Attached pictures)

### 北京各界举行欢迎柬埔寨代表团大会

1956-02-18
第1版()
专栏：

    北京各界举行欢迎柬埔寨代表团大会
    新华社17日讯　北京市各界人民今天下午隆重举行欢迎大会，欢迎以诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王殿下为首的柬埔寨王国国家代表团。
    北京市的各界社会名流、著名的和平人士、科学家、教授、工程师、文化工作者、男女工人、学生、法师、居士和其他宗教界人士、机关工作人员和市民们共8，000多人，穿着节日的盛装，愉快地前来参加这个盛大的欢迎会。
    大会由北京市市长彭真主持。
    大会在北京体育馆的比赛馆内举行。会场上充满了一片友好和欢乐的空气。会场的入口处，在一面红丝绒的屏风上写着：“中柬两国友好万岁！”主席台后面的正中悬挂着中柬两国国旗；主席台后面和对面悬着8面巨大的彩色旗帜，上面分别画着和平鸽和橄榄叶。在主席台对面墙壁上的巨幅标语上写着：“中柬两国人民的友谊万岁！”“亚洲和世界和平万岁！”许多面的彩旗在会场四周飒飒飘扬。
    下午4时50分，在暴风雨般的掌声和一片欢呼声中，诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王由周恩来总理和彭真市长陪同登上主席台。西哈努克亲王频频向全场起立欢呼着的人们挥手致意。
    随同登上主席台的，还有柬埔寨王国国家代表团团员：皇廷最高会议委员兰·?特，皇廷最高会议委员钦·迪，王国会议副议长桑·年，国民会议议员恩格·米阿斯，王国会议议员尼尔·斯莫乌克博士，皇家空军参谋长努·呼上校，农民福利副国务秘书罗恩·索方，亲王私人秘书丰·萨伦。
    在主席台上的，还有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、陈叔通，国务院副总理陈毅、秘书长习仲勋，国防委员会副主席叶剑英元帅、傅作义，外交部副部长张闻天，中国人民解放军空军司令员刘亚楼上将，中国人民政治协商会议北京市委员会主席刘仁，北京市副市长张友渔、王昆仑、薛子正、程宏毅、贾庭三，中国人民政治协商会议北京市委员会副主席萧明、梁思成、余心清、凌其峻，以及各民主党派北京市组织负责人，北京市各人民团体负责人和中国人民解放军驻京部队负责人。
    乐队奏起欢迎曲，一群穿着鲜丽服装的儿童们向贵宾们献了鲜花。
    欢迎大会开始，乐队奏柬、中两国国歌。
    彭真首先致欢迎词。他代表北京市全体市民对西哈努克亲王和他所率领的柬埔寨王国国家代表团这次的友好访问表示衷心的感谢和真诚的敬意，并请他们把北京市人民和中国人民的友谊带给金边市的市民和柬埔寨全体人民。
    在乐曲声中，彭真代表北京市各界人民向西哈努克亲王赠礼和赠旗。
    西哈努克亲王接着致答词。他对聚集在这个盛大而热烈的欢迎会上的北京市民们表示深深的感谢。他说：处在对待我们这样亲切的你们当中，处在你们的伟大的国土上，我们感到完全像在家里一样。在他讲话的时候，经久不息的掌声不断地响彻会场。
    在欢迎大会上，北京六个艺术单位320多位文艺工作者联合为贵宾们表演了精采的“节日联欢大歌舞”节目。表演毕，代表团向艺术家们献花，西哈努克亲王同艺术家们亲切地握手致谢。这时北京艺术家们拥向西哈努克亲王，全场响起一片欢呼声。
    大会在热烈的掌声和乐曲声中结束。
    在欢迎大会以后，贵宾们参观了北京体育馆的练习馆和游泳馆。（附图片）
