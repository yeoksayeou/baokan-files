Title: Chairman Mao Receives Yugoslav Youth Football Team; Vice Premier Chen Yi Hosts Banquet to Bid Farewell

Author: 
Date: 1956-02-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China received all members of the Yugoslav Youth Football Team, headed by B. Bešić, at 11:15 PM on the 6th.

Present at the reception were Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Mayor of Beijing; Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council; Cai Shufan, Lu Han, Huang Qixiang, and Rong Gaotang, Vice Ministers of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission; Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Yu Xinqing, Director of the Bureau of Ceremonies of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; and Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Also present were the Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popović, and his wife, and Embassy Counselor Milicevic and his wife.

Xinhua News Agency, 6th: Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 6th to bid farewell to all members of the Yugoslav Youth Football Team.

The Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popović, and his wife were invited to attend the banquet.

Also attending the banquet were Cai Shufan, Lu Han, Huang Qixiang, and Rong Gaotang, Vice Ministers of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission, and Chai Zemin, Director of the Beijing Municipal Physical Culture and Sports Commission, among others.

Chen Yi delivered a speech at the banquet. He said that the Yugoslav Youth Football Team had made a cordial and friendly visit to China for 10 days, writing the first page in the history of sports exchanges between the Chinese and Yugoslav peoples. The superb skills displayed by the Yugoslav Youth Football Team in China aroused great interest among Chinese audiences. He thanked the Yugoslav Youth Football Team for its valuable activities in enhancing understanding and friendship between the Chinese and Yugoslav peoples and promoting the development and improvement of Chinese football. Chen Yi said that the Chinese people cherish the fraternal friendship that has long existed between the Chinese and Yugoslav peoples and are willing to make every effort to consolidate and deepen this friendship.

B. Bešić, the leader of the Yugoslav Youth Football Team, then spoke. He thanked the Chinese people for their warm and fraternal welcome. He said, "We will always be happy for every minute we spent in your lovely country." He said that a good start had been made in the contact between the sports circles of Yugoslavia and China. He believed that this contact would be further developed and strengthened. He also said that they were deeply moved by the achievements made by China in sports and socialist construction, because these achievements were achieved in such a short period of time as never before.

After the banquet, Cai Shufan, on behalf of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, presented gifts to all members of the Yugoslav Youth Football Team. B. Bešić also presented gifts to Chen Yi and others on behalf of the Yugoslav Youth Football Team. (Photo attached)

### 毛主席接见南青年足球队  陈毅副总理设宴欢送南青年足球队

1956-02-07
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见南青年足球队
    陈毅副总理设宴欢送南青年足球队
    新华社6日讯　中华人民共和国毛泽东主席在6日晚间11时15分接见了以勃·彼希契为首的南斯拉夫青年足球队全体人员。
    接见的时候在座的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、北京市市长彭真，国务院副总理陈毅，体育运动委员会副主任蔡树藩、卢汉、黄琪翔、荣高棠，外交部副部长张闻天，全国人民代表大会常务委员会典礼局局长余心清，外交部礼宾司司长柯华。
    接见的时候，南斯拉夫驻中国大使波波维奇和他的夫人、大使馆参赞米里切维奇和他的夫人也在座。
    新华社6日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅6日晚设宴欢送南斯拉夫青年足球队全体人员。
    南斯拉夫驻中国大使波波维奇和他的夫人应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的还有体育运动委员会副主任蔡树藩、卢汉、黄琪翔、荣高棠和北京市体育运动委员会主任柴泽民等人。
    陈毅在宴会上首先致词。他说，南斯拉夫青年足球队在中国作了10天亲切而友好的访问，在中、南两国人民体育来往的历史上写下了第一页。南斯拉夫青年足球队在中国表现的高超技术，使中国观众感到极大的兴趣。他感谢南斯拉夫青年足球队在增进中、南两国人民之间的了解和友谊，以及推动中国足球运动的发展和提高方面所作的有价值的活动。陈毅说，中国人民十分珍视中、南两国人民早已产生的兄弟友谊，并且愿意尽一切努力来巩固和加深这种友谊。
    南斯拉夫青年足球队领队勃·彼希契接着讲话。他感谢中国人民给予他们的热诚的兄弟般的欢迎。他说，“我们将为我们在你们可爱的国家所度过的每一分钟而永远感到快乐。”他说，在南、中两国体育运动界的接触方面，已经获得良好的开端。他相信这种接触将得到进一步的发展和加强。他还说，他们为中国在体育运动方面和社会主义建设方面所获得的成就深深感动，因为这种成就是在从来没有过的这样短的时间内所获得的。
    宴会以后，蔡树藩代表中华人民共和国体育运动委员会向南斯拉夫青年足球队全体人员赠送了礼品。勃·彼希契也代表南斯拉夫青年足球队向陈毅等人赠送了礼品。（附图片）
