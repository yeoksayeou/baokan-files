Title: Czechoslovak Government Calls UN Secretary-General to Protest US Releasing Provocative Balloons
Author:
Date: 1956-02-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, via Czechoslovak News Agency: Czechoslovak Foreign Minister David sent a telegram to United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld on February 7th, protesting the United States' release of balloons carrying provocative leaflets and military reconnaissance balloons into Czechoslovak airspace.
    The telegram stated that after lodging a protest with the United States government without result, the Czechoslovak government considered it necessary to draw Hammarskjöld's attention to the fact that these activities of American organizations and military institutions "are in complete violation of the fundamental requirements of international law and the principles of the United Nations."

### 捷政府致电联合国秘书长  抗议美国放送挑衅性气球

1956-02-10
第4版()
专栏：

    捷政府致电联合国秘书长
    抗议美国放送挑衅性气球
    新华社据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道捷克斯洛伐克共和国外交部长戴维在2月7日打电报给联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德，抗议美国把装有挑衅性传单的气球和军事侦察气球放送到捷克斯洛伐克领土上空。
    电报说，在向美国政府提出抗议没有结果以后，捷克斯洛伐克政府认为有必要提请哈马舍尔德注意美国的组织和军事机构的这些活动“是同国际法的根本要求和联合国的原则完全相违反的”。
