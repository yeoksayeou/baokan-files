Title: Poland Trains Cadres in the Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy
Author:
Date: 1956-02-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, February 21st – According to a report by the Polish Press Agency on February 20th: The Nuclear Research Institute of the Polish Academy of Sciences is establishing special training courses for engineers and scientific workers in the scientific and industrial sectors to provide them with training in nuclear physics and chemistry, especially in the application of radioactive isotopes. On February 20th, the fifth such training course for dozens of engineers from various industrial sectors commenced in Warsaw. Participants in this training course will study the principles of nuclear physics and chemistry, isotope physics, research methods for various radioactive effects, and methods for protecting health.
	In April, the Nuclear Research Institute of the Polish Academy of Sciences will establish a special training course in radioactive isotopes for chemical engineers. It will also establish two such training courses for biologists and agricultural scientific researchers, and another training course for industrial engineers.

### 波兰培养和平利用原子能方面的干部

1956-02-22
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰培养和平利用原子能方面的干部
    新华社华沙21日电　据波兰通讯社20日报道：波兰科学院核子研究所正在为科学和工业各部门的工程师和科学工作者设立特别训练班，以便在核子物理学和化学、特别是放射性同位素的应用方面给予他们以训练。2月20日，为工业各部门的数十名工程师举办的第五个这种训练班在华沙开课了。参加这个训练班的人将学习核子物理学和化学的原理、同位素物理学、各种放射作用的研究方法以及保护健康的方法。
    在4月份，波兰科学院核子研究所将为化学工程师设立一个放射性同位素方面的特别训练班。它还要为生物学家和农业科学研究工作者成立两个这样的训练班，再为工业工程师成立一个训练班。
