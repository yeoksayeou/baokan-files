Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station and Moscow Radio Station to Jointly Present Special Program on February 14

Author:
Date: 1956-02-13
Page: 3

    Central People's Broadcasting Station and Moscow Radio Station
    to Jointly Present Special Program on February 14
    In celebration of the 6th anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship and Alliance and Mutual Assistance and the opening of the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Central People's Broadcasting Station and Moscow Radio Station will jointly present a special program on February 14, broadcasting congratulatory speeches by Peng Zhen, Mayor of Beijing, Furtseva, First Secretary of the Moscow City Committee, factory recordings, reports, and music, etc.
    The Central People's Broadcasting Station will also continuously broadcast programs celebrating the 6th anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship and Alliance and Mutual Assistance and the opening of the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

### 中央人民广播电台和莫斯科广播电台  2月14日联合举办特别节目

1956-02-13
第3版()
专栏：

    中央人民广播电台和莫斯科广播电台
    2月14日联合举办特别节目
    为庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订6周年和苏联共产党第二十次代表大会开幕，中央人民广播电台和莫斯科广播电台在2月14日联合举办特别节目，播送北京市长彭真、莫斯科市委第一书记福尔采娃等的祝贺讲话，工厂录音报道和音乐等。
    中央人民广播电台还要连续播送庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订6周年和苏联共产党第二十次代表大会开幕的节目。
