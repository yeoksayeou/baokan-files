Title: The 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union Concludes

Author:

Date: 1956-02-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 26th (TASS): The 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union concluded on February 25th in the Moscow Kremlin.

    At the closing session, the delegates unanimously adopted the directives on the Sixth Five-Year Plan for the Development of the National Economy of the USSR for 1956-1960.

    Subsequently, Ye. Gromov, Chairman of the Ballot Counting Committee, announced to the Congress the results of the election of the central organs of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union: The new Central Committee consists of 133 members and 122 alternate members; the Central Auditing Commission consists of 63 members. All the delegates present warmly welcomed the election results of the Party Central organs, with prolonged applause.

    N.S. Khrushchev, on behalf of the Presidium, submitted the draft resolution on drafting the new program of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union for discussion at the Congress. The Congress unanimously adopted this resolution.

    Finally, the chairman of the meeting, Khrushchev, pointed out that the discussion of all the items on the agenda of the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union had concluded, and announced the closing of the Congress. All the delegates present stood up and enthusiastically sang the Party anthem, "The Internationale." Thunderous cheers echoed in the hall, and the whole audience shouted: "Long live the Communist Party of the Soviet Union!" "Long live the Leninist Central Committee!" "Long live Leninism!"

    (Special dispatch from our newspaper Moscow, February 26) On the 25th, when the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union victoriously concluded, many enterprises and institutions in Moscow held grand celebration rallies. Members and alternate members of the CPSU Central Committee, delegates to the Congress, and ministers all participated in these mass rallies. Representatives of fraternal parties from various countries also attended 34 mass rallies respectively.

    Zhu De and Tan Zhenlin of the delegation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China attended a rally held at the First Ball Bearing Plant with the participation of about 9,000 workers and staff. Comrade Zhu De delivered a speech at the meeting, congratulating the success of the 20th Congress of the CPSU, and was warmly welcomed. It was broadcast by Moscow Radio that evening.

    At a rally of more than 8,000 workers and staff held at the Stalin Automobile Plant, Togliatti, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party, spoke. At the workers' rally at the "Red Warrior" factory, Walter Ulbricht, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, spoke. The delegation of the Polish United Workers' Party attended the workers' rally at the "Red Proletarian" factory, and Cyrankiewicz spoke. The delegation of the Romanian Workers' Party attended the workers' rally at the "Hammer and Sickle" factory, and Chişinevschi spoke. The delegation of the Bulgarian Communist Party attended the workers' rally at the "Dynamo" factory, and Damyanov spoke. The delegation of the Albanian Labor Party attended the workers' rally at the "ATE-1" factory, and Hoxha spoke. The delegation of the Spanish Communist Party attended the workers' rally at the Three Mountains factory, and Ibárruri spoke. The delegation of the Indian Communist Party attended the workers' rally at the Ilyich factory, and Ghosh spoke.

    All mass rallies unanimously adopted resolutions, expressing the workers' warm support for the resolutions of the 20th Congress of the CPSU, and assured the CPSU Central Committee that they would use all their strength to realize the program of communist construction stipulated by the Party Congress.

### 苏联共产党第二十次代表大会闭幕

1956-02-27
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联共产党第二十次代表大会闭幕
    新华社26日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科25日讯：苏联共产党第二十次代表大会2月25日在莫斯科克里姆林宫闭幕。
    在闭幕会议上，代表们一致通过了关于1956—1960年苏联发展国民经济的第六个五年计划的指示。
    接着，票数统计委员会主席叶·格罗莫夫向代表大会宣布苏联共产党中央机构的选举结果：新的中央委员会由133个委员和122个候补委员组成；中央检查委员会由63个委员组成。到会的全体代表们热烈欢迎党中央机构的选举结果，鼓掌声经久不息。
    尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫代表主席团把关于草拟苏联共产党新党纲的决议草案提交大会讨论。大会一致通过了这个决议。
    最后，会议主席赫鲁晓夫指出，苏联共产党第二十次代表大会的各项议程的讨论已经结束，并且宣布代表大会闭幕。到会的代表们全体起立，热烈兴奋地高唱党歌“国际歌”。大厅里响起了雷动般的欢呼声，全场高呼：“苏联共产党万岁！”“列宁式的中央委员会万岁！”“列宁主义万岁！”
    本报莫斯科26日专电　25日，当苏联共产党第二十次代表大会胜利结束的时候，莫斯科许多企业和机关举行了盛大的庆祝大会。苏共中央委员们和候补委员们，代表大会的代表们，部长们都参加这些群众大会。各国兄弟党代表也分别出席34个群众大会。
    中共中央代表团朱德和谭震林出席了在第一滚珠轴承制造厂举行的约9，000职工参加的大会。朱德同志在大会上致词，祝贺苏共第二十次代表大会的成功，受到热烈的欢迎。当晚就由莫斯科无线电台转播了。
    在斯大林汽车制造厂举行的8，000多职工大会上，意大利共产党中央委员会总书记陶里亚蒂讲了话。在“红色壮士”工厂的职工大会上，德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记乌布利希讲了话。波兰统一工人党代表团参加了“红色无产者”工厂职工大会，西伦凯维兹讲了话。罗马尼亚工人党代表团参加了“镰刀与锤子”工厂职工大会，基辛涅夫斯基讲了话。保加利亚共产党代表团参加了“狄纳莫”工厂职工大会，达米扬诺夫讲了话。阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表团参加了“亚特一号”工厂职工大会，霍查讲了话。西班牙共产党代表团参加了三山工厂职工大会，伊巴露丽讲了话。印度共产党代表团参加了伊里奇工厂职工大会，高士讲了话。
    所有群众大会一致通过决议，表示劳动者热烈拥护苏共第二十次代表大会的决议，并且向苏共中央保证将用一切力量来实现党代表大会所规定的共产主义建设纲领。
