Title: Indonesian Communist Party Demands Abolition of the "Round Table Conference Agreement"
Author:
Date: 1956-02-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 21st - According to Antara News Agency, the Indonesian Communist Party parliamentary group submitted a draft resolution to the parliamentary advisory committee on February 20th, formally demanding the unilateral abolition of the "Round Table Conference Agreement" that Indonesia was forced to sign with the Netherlands in 1949.

The draft resolution demands that Indonesia no longer be bound by the "Round Table Conference Agreement" and that a work plan be drafted to resolve the problems arising from the abolition of the "Round Table Conference Agreement."

The draft resolution condemns the stubborn attitude of the Dutch government. The draft resolution points out that the "Round Table Conference Agreement" stipulates that the Netherlands controls important sectors of the Indonesian economy. This agreement should no longer continue to exist. Dutch colonial rule over West Irian violates the principles of self-determination, justice, and peace.

### 印度尼西亚共产党要求废止“圆桌会议协定”

1956-02-22
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚共产党要求废止“圆桌会议协定”
    新华社雅加达21日电　据安塔拉通讯社报道，印度尼西亚共产党国会党团在2月20日向国会谘询委员会提出决议草案，正式要求单方面废止印度尼西亚1949年被迫同荷兰签订的“圆桌会议协定”。
    这个决议草案要求宣布印度尼西亚不再受“圆桌会议协定”的约束，并且草拟解决由于废止“圆桌会议协定”而产生的问题的工作计划。
    决议草案谴责荷兰政府的顽固态度。决议草案指出，“圆桌会议协定”规定荷兰控制印度尼西亚经济的重要部门。这个协定不应再继续存在。荷兰对西伊里安的殖民统治是违反自决、正义和和平的原则的。
