Title: Czechoslovakia and Yugoslavia Expand Economic Ties
Author:
Date: 1956-02-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, February 12th: Government delegations of the Czechoslovak Republic and the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia held negotiations in Prague from January 30th to February 11th regarding the expansion of economic ties between the two countries. On February 11th, an agreement on resolving previous asset issues between the two countries was signed in Prague. The two countries also signed agreements on long-term supply of goods and on the Czechoslovak government providing the Yugoslav government with a loan for the purchase of basic equipment and machinery. According to a special agreement, Yugoslavia can also obtain loans to purchase industrial goods and daily necessities. At the same time, negotiations on signing a general trade agreement for 1956 have also begun.
In addition, the two countries also signed a temporary agreement on scientific and technological cooperation.

### 捷南两国扩大经济联系

1956-02-14
第4版()
专栏：

    捷南两国扩大经济联系
    新华社据塔斯社布拉格12日讯　捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团和南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国政府代表团从1月30日到2月11日在布拉格举行了关于扩大两国经济联系问题的谈判。2月11日在布拉格签订了关于解决以前两国之间产生的资产问题的协定。两国还签订了关于长期供应货物以及关于捷克斯洛伐克政府贷给南斯拉夫政府一笔供购买基本设备和机器之用的贷款的协定。根据一项特别协定，南斯拉夫还可获得贷款来购买工业品和日用品。同时，关于签订1956年一般贸易协定的谈判也已经开始进行。
    此外，两国还签订了临时科学技术合作协定。
