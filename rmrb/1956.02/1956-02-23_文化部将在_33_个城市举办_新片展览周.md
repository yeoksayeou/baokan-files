Title: Ministry of Culture to Hold "New Film Exhibition Week" in 33 Cities
Author:
Date: 1956-02-23
Page: 2

The Ministry of Culture has decided to hold a "New Film Exhibition Week" in 33 cities across the country in two batches, starting in mid-March.

During the exhibition week, the following feature films produced in 1955 will be screened: "Dong Cunrui," "Raging Sea Light Cavalry," "Song Jingshi," "South Island Storm," "Spring in the Water Village"; the stage documentary "Mei Lanfang's Stage Art"; as well as color animation and scenery films: "Why is the Crow Black," "Encounter in the Wild," "Mr. Dong Guo," "Little Bear's Journey," "Magic Brush," "Huangshan Scenery," "Guilin Landscape," etc.

The exhibition week will be held from March 16th to 22nd first in 16 cities including Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Shenyang, Chengdu, Xi'an, and Urumqi; and from March 28th to April 3rd in 17 cities including Qiqihar, Lushun-Dalian, Fushun, Hohhot, Guiyang, Lanzhou, and Xining.
(Xinhua News Agency)

### 文化部将在33个城市举办“新片展览周”

1956-02-23
第2版()
专栏：

    文化部将在33个城市举办“新片展览周”
    文化部决定从3月中旬开始在全国33个城市分两批举办“新片展览周”。
    展览周中将放映1955年出品的故事片：“董存瑞”、“怒海轻骑”、“宋景诗”、“南岛风云”、“水乡的春天”、；舞台纪录片：“梅兰芳的舞台艺术”；以及彩色美术片和风景片：“乌鸦为什么是黑的”、“野外的遭遇”、“东郭先生”、“小熊的旅行”、“神笔”、“黄山风景”、“桂林山水”等。
    展览周从3月16日到22日将先在北京、天津、上海、沈阳、成都、西安、乌鲁木齐等16个城市举行；从3月28日到4月3日将在齐齐哈尔、旅大、抚顺、呼和浩特、贵阳、兰州、西宁等17个城市举行。
                      （新华社）
