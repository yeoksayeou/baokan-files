Title: China Red Cross Donates Funds to Aid Cambodian Phnom Penh Fire Victims
Author: 
Date: 1956-02-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 19th — Hu Lansheng, Vice President of the Chinese Red Cross, and Lin Shixiao, Deputy Secretary-General, visited Mr. Lan Nget and Mr. Chim Ti, members of the National Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia and members of the High Royal Council, on the afternoon of the 18th. They expressed the Chinese people's sympathy and condolences to the people of Phnom Penh, the capital of the Kingdom of Cambodia, who suffered from the fire, and donated 80,000 yuan on behalf of the Chinese Red Cross to help the disaster-stricken people. Prince Norodom Sihanouk, head of the National Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia, immediately sent a letter to the Chinese Red Cross expressing his gratitude.
    The Red Tu Road Ahua Xi District in the suburbs of Phnom Penh, the capital of the Kingdom of Cambodia, suffered a major fire on December 28-29 last year, with 5,000 houses burned down and more than 10,000 residents affected.

### 中国红十字会捐款救济柬埔寨金边火灾灾民

1956-02-20
第1版()
专栏：

    中国红十字会捐款救济柬埔寨金边火灾灾民
    新华社19日讯　中国红十字会副会长胡兰生和副秘书长林士笑在18日下午拜访了柬埔寨王国国家代表团团员：皇廷最高会议委员兰·涅特和皇廷最高会议委员钦·迪，向他们表示了中国人民对柬埔寨王国首都金边市遭受火灾的人民的同情和慰问，并代表中国红十字会捐赠人民币八万元，作为救济受灾人民的费用。柬埔寨王国国家代表团团长西哈努克亲王当即致函中国红十字会，表示感谢。
    柬埔寨王国首都金边市郊区红涂路阿华细区曾在去年12月28—29日发生大火，有5，000间房屋被烧毁，一万多居民受灾。
