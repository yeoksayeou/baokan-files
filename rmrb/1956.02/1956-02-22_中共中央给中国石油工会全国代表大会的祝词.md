Title: CPC Central Committee's Congratulatory Message to the National Congress of the China Petroleum Trade Union
Author:
Date: 1956-02-22
Page: 1

To the Comrades of the First National Congress of the China Petroleum Trade Union, forwarded by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions:

    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China warmly congratulates the opening of your congress and wishes the meeting a success.

    Since the founding of the People's Republic of China, all workers, engineering and technical personnel, and enterprise administrative leaders in the petroleum industry have engaged in creative labor and have initially learned and mastered certain modern technologies. Now, China's petroleum industry is rapidly growing into an independent industrial sector.

    At present, the primary task before you is to vigorously explore natural petroleum resources, obtain oil fields with abundant reserves, and refine various types of oil needed by our country using the latest methods; at the same time, develop synthetic petroleum and accelerate and further change the backward state of China's petroleum industry, so that the development of the petroleum industry meets the needs of national construction and the people. The task undertaken by all employees in the petroleum industry is significant and glorious.

    It is hoped that through this congress, you will further strengthen the ties between the trade union and the masses, raise the political awareness of the workers, carry forward the working class's fine tradition of hard work, vigorously promote advanced experience, develop mass rationalization proposals, learn and master new technologies; carry out mass criticism and self-criticism, oppose right-leaning conservative thinking; organize advanced individuals to provide comradely assistance to those lagging behind, so that the ranks of socialist competition participants become more extensive, in order to comprehensively improve work, improve production, and make China's petroleum industry produce more, faster, better, and more economically, and complete or overfulfill the glorious task of the first five-year plan ahead of schedule.
                                      The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
                                February 21, 1956

### 中共中央给中国石油工会全国代表大会的祝词

1956-02-22
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央给中国石油工会全国代表大会的祝词中华全国总工会转中国石油工会第一次全国代表大会的全体同志们：
    中国共产党中央委员会热烈地祝贺你们的大会开幕，并预祝会议成功。
    中华人民共和国成立以来，石油工业的全体工人、工程技术人员和企业行政领导人员进行了创造性的劳动，已经初步学会和掌握了某些近代技术。现在，我国石油工业正在迅速地成长为一个独立的工业部门。
    目前，摆在你们面前的首要任务，是大力勘探天然石油资源，获得储量丰富的油田，并用最新方法炼出我国所需要的各种油类；同时发展人造石油，加速地、进一步地改变我国石油工业的落后状态，使石油工业的发展，适应国家建设和人民的需要。石油工业部门全体职工所担负的这个任务是重大的和光荣的。
    希望你们经过这次大会进一步地密切工会和群众的联系，提高职工群众的政治觉悟，发扬工人阶级艰苦奋斗的优良传统，努力推广先进经验，发展群众性的合理化建议，学习和掌握新技术；开展群众性的批评和自我批评，反对右倾保守思想；组织先进者对落后者进行同志般的帮助，使社会主义竞赛参加者的队伍更广泛，以求全面改进工作，改善生产，使我国石油工业生产得又多、又快、又好、又省，提早完成和超额完成你们所担负的第一个五年计划的光荣任务。
                                      中国共产党中央委员会
                                1956年2月21日
