Title: Lao Kingdom Army Invades Territory of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1956-02-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 1st: Vietnam News Agency reported on February 1st: On January 26th, a platoon of soldiers from the Lao Kingdom Army crossed the border between Vietnam and Laos and entered the territory of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. This force, coming from Nong Yi in the Xieng Khouang Province of northern Laos, robbed several Vietnamese villages along the border, shot and killed two Vietnamese border guards, seized a batch of military supplies and property, and kidnapped one person to Laos.

The aforementioned acts of aggression by the Lao Kingdom Army in the Vietnamese border region constitute a blatant provocation against the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. Moreover, this is part of a conspiracy by the U.S. imperialist elements and their lackeys in Laos to harass the Vietnam-Laos border, attempting to sow hatred between the peoples of the two countries.

### 老挝王国军队侵犯越南民主共和国领土

1956-02-03
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝王国军队侵犯越南民主共和国领土
    据新华社河内1日电　越南通讯社1日讯：老挝王国陆军部队一排多人在1月26日越过了越南和老挝的边界进入了越南民主共和国的领土。这一支从老挝北部的川圹省农易来的部队，在沿边界的地方抢劫了好几个越南村庄，开枪打死了两名越南边防人员，劫去了一批军火和财产，并且把一个人绑架到老挝去了。
    老挝王国部队在越南边界地区进行的上述侵犯行动构成了对越南民主共和国的露骨的挑衅行动。而且，这是美帝国主义分子和他们在老挝的走狗骚扰越南—老挝边境，企图在两国人民之间散播仇恨的阴谋的一部分。
