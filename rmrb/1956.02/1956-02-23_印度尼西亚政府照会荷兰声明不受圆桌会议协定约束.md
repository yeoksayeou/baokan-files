Title: Indonesian Government Notifies the Netherlands of its Declaration Not to be Bound by the Round Table Conference Agreements
Author:
Date: 1956-02-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 22nd - The Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs announced on February 21st that the Indonesian government had delivered a note to the Netherlands on February 21st, declaring that Indonesia was no longer bound by the financial and economic agreements and other agreements concluded at the Round Table Conference. It was also announced that the government had submitted a bill to the parliament on the same day to abolish the Indonesia-Netherlands Union.

### 印度尼西亚政府照会荷兰声明不受圆桌会议协定约束

1956-02-23
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚政府照会荷兰声明不受圆桌会议协定约束
    新华社雅加达22日电　印度尼西亚外交部2月21日宣布，印度尼西亚政府在2月21日交给荷兰一项照会，声明印度尼西亚不再受圆桌会议上缔结的财政经济协定和其他协定的约束。同时还宣布，政府已经在同一天向国会提出了关于废除印度尼西亚—荷兰联邦的法案。
