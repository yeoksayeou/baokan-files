Title: Hunan Province Produces a Large Quantity of High-Quality Timber

Author: 
Date: 1956-02-29
Page: 1

Hunan Province produces a large quantity of high-quality timber. This timber is transported through Dongting Lake to various regions, supporting the country's construction.
