Title: Soviet-Japanese Negotiations Continue
Author:
Date: 1956-02-12
Page: 4

    Soviet-Japanese Negotiations Continue
    Xinhua News Agency, London, February 11th: TASS, London, February 11th: A joint communiqué on the Soviet-Japanese negotiations was released in London, stating:
    At the February 10th meeting, Soviet representative Malik and Japanese representative Shunichi Matsumoto continued to discuss the draft of the Soviet-Japanese peace treaty, article by article.
    The next meeting will be held on February 14th.

### 苏日谈判继续举行

1956-02-12
第4版()
专栏：

    苏日谈判继续举行
    新华社11日讯　塔斯社伦敦11日讯：伦敦发表了苏日谈判联合公报，公报说：
    苏联代表马立克和日本代表松本俊一在2月10日会议上继续逐条讨论了苏日和约草案。
    下次会议将在2月14日举行。
