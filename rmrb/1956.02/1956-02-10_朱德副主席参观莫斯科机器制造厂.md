Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De Visits Moscow Machine Manufacturing Plant
Author:
Date: 1956-02-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 9th. Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, accompanied by Marshal Nie Rongzhen, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council of the People's Republic of China, Liu Lantao, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and other accompanying personnel, visited the Moscow Machine Manufacturing Plant on the afternoon of February 8th.

Vice Chairman Zhu De and others were accompanied to the factory by Gorkin, Deputy Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Fomin, Chief of Protocol, Gudev, Director of the Far East Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wen Ning, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union.

Vice Chairman Zhu De and others visited the various production workshops of the factory, observing the workers' operations and the production process of each workshop. During the visit, Fo. Balajin, First Deputy Minister of the Soviet Aviation Industry, Komarov, the factory director, and others who accompanied them provided introductions and explanations.

### 朱德副主席参观莫斯科机器制造厂

1956-02-10
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德副主席参观莫斯科机器制造厂
    新华社莫斯科9日电　中华人民共和国副主席朱德，2月8日下午偕同中华人民共和国国防委员会副主席聂荣臻元帅、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员刘澜涛及其他随行人员，参观了莫斯科机器制造厂。
    朱副主席等由苏联最高苏维埃主席团副秘书戈尔金、礼宾长弗明、苏联外交部远东司司长顾德夫和中国驻苏联大使馆参赞温宁等人陪同来到工厂。
    朱德副主席等参观了这个工厂的各个生产车间，观看了工人进行操作的情况和各个车间的生产过程。在参观的时候，陪同他们参观的苏联航空工业部第一副部长佛·拔拉金和工厂厂长科马罗夫等在旁边作了介绍和说明。
