Title: Premier Zhou Hosts Banquet for Thai People's Goodwill Mission to Promote Friendship with China
Author:
Date: 1956-02-12
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, February 11th—Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, hosted a banquet at noon on the 11th to entertain Nai Thep Chutinan, head of the Thai People's Goodwill Mission to Promote Friendship with China, Deputy Head Nai Chem Phuriphat, and ten members of the delegation.

    Present at the banquet were Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the NationalPeople's Congress Peng Zhen, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Jiakang, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Zhang Xiruo, Secretary-General Wu Maosun, and Vice President of the Buddhist Association of China Zhao Puchu.

    The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere, and the hosts and guests repeatedly raised their glasses to toast Sino-Thai friendship.

### 周总理宴请泰国人民促进友好访华团

1956-02-12
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理宴请泰国人民促进友好访华团
    新华社11日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在11日中午设宴招待泰国人民促进友好访华团团长乃贴·触的努七、副团长乃琛·蒲里博和10位团员。
    出席作陪的，有国务院副总理陈毅，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长彭真，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部部长助理陈家康，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、秘书长吴茂荪，中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初。
    宴会在友好的空气中进行，宾主在席间一再举杯祝中泰友好。
