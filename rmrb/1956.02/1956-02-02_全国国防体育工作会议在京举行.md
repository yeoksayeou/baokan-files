Title: National Defense Sports Work Conference Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-02-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 1st dispatch – The Central National Defense Sports Club held the 1956 National Defense Sports Work Conference in Beijing from January 27th to 31st.

    The conference formulated a preliminary plan for national defense sports work in 1956. The plan stipulates that in 1956, the country will carry out 14 national defense sports activities including shooting, motorcycles, radio, equestrianism, ship models, aviation, gliding, parachuting, and aircraft models; leadership organizations for national defense sports work will be established in cities above the prefectural level; a large number of amateur and full-time cadres will be trained; competitive activities will be vigorously carried out and international competitions will be actively participated in; and a total of 321 various national defense sports clubs, sports stations, training grounds and schools will be established.

    The conference also summarized the achievements of national defense sports work in 1955 and criticized the right-leaning conservative ideas existing in national defense sports work.

    Representatives from sports commissions of various provinces, cities, and autonomous regions, national defense sports cadres, representatives from the military sports departments of the Communist Youth League committees of various provinces, cities, and autonomous regions, and representatives from relevant central departments attended the meeting.

### 全国国防体育工作会议在京举行

1956-02-02
第1版()
专栏：

    全国国防体育工作会议在京举行
    新华社1日讯　中央国防体育俱乐部从1月27日到31日在北京召开1956年全国国防体育工作会议。
    会议制定了1956年国防体育工作的初步计划。计划中规定：1956年，全国将开展射击、摩托车、无线电、马术、舰船模型、飞行、滑翔、跳伞和航空模型等14个国防体育活动项目；在省辖市以上的城市将建立国防体育工作的领导机构；大量训练业余和专职干部；大力开展竞赛活动，并且积极参加国际竞赛；建立各种国防体育俱乐部、运动站、教练场和学校等共321个。
    会议还总结了1955年国防体育工作的成绩，批判了国防体育工作中存在的右倾保守思想。
    参加会议的有各省、市、自治区体育运动委员会的代表，国防体育干部，青年团各省、市、自治区委员会军事体育部的代表，以及中央各有关部门的代表。
