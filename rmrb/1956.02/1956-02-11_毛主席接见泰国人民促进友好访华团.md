Title: Chairman Mao Receives the Thai People's Delegation for Promoting Friendship with China

Author:
Date: 1956-02-11
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 10th — Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China received the Thai People's Delegation for Promoting Friendship with China, led by Nai Thep Chotinuchit, head of the delegation, and Nai Chem Bhulibot, deputy head of the delegation, along with 10 other members of the delegation, from 10:00 PM to 11:30 PM this afternoon.

    Also present at the reception were Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Peng Zhen and Chen Shutong, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Zhang Xiruo, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Jiakang, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Yu Xinqing, and Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Wu Maosun. (Attached photo)

### 毛主席接见泰国人民促进友好访华团

1956-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见泰国人民促进友好访华团
    新华社10日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东在10日下午10时到11时半，接见了泰国人民促进友好访华团团长乃贴·触的努七、副团长乃琛·蒲里博和10位团员。
    接见的时候在座的，有国务院总理周恩来、副总理陈毅，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长彭真、陈叔通，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，外交部部长助理陈家康，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长余心清，中国人民外交学会秘书长吴茂荪。（附图片）
    毛泽东主席和乃贴·触的努七团长握手。中为中国人民外交学会会长张奚若。
                                   吕厚民摄（新华社稿）
