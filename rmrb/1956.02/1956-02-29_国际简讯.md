Title: International Briefs
Author:
Date: 1956-02-29
Page: 4

    △ According to the Indian Press Trust: Indian troops entered Chhad Bet in the Kutch region at 8 pm on February 26. Pakistani troops who had arrived in Chhad Bet on February 17 have withdrawn.
    △ At the invitation of the Leningrad City Soviet, a delegation from the Manchester City Council, led by the Mayor of Manchester, arrived in Leningrad on February 25. The delegation will stay in Leningrad for 10 days.

### 国际简讯

1956-02-29
第4版()
专栏：

    国际简讯
    △据印度报业托辣斯消息：印度军队已经在2月26日下午8时进入喀齐邦的查德贝特。在2月17日开到查德贝特的巴基斯坦军队已经撤退。
    △应列宁格勒市苏维埃的邀请、由曼彻斯特市长率领的曼彻斯特市政委员会代表团在2月25日到达列宁格勒。代表团将在列宁格勒停留10天。
