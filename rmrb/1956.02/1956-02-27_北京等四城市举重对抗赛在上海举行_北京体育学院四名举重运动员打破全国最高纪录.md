Title: Beijing and Three Other Cities Hold Weightlifting Competition in Shanghai; Four Weightlifters from Beijing Institute of Physical Education Break National Records

Author:

Date: 1956-02-27
Page: 3

Beijing and three other cities, Tianjin, Shanghai, and Guangzhou held a weightlifting competition in Shanghai on the evening of February 25th. Weightlifters from the Beijing Institute of Physical Education participated in a demonstration, breaking national records in the flyweight and featherweight classes.

During the demonstration, Chen Yongbo, a flyweight weightlifter from Beijing Institute of Physical Education, achieved a total weightlifting score of 300 kg, exceeding his own national record by 5 kg, and equaling the total score of the third-place finisher in the same class at the 15th Olympic Games. His clean and jerk score was 95 kg, exceeding his own national record by 3 kg, and equaling the single event first place score of the same class at the 15th Olympic Games. Xue Deming, a flyweight weightlifter from Beijing Institute of Physical Education, achieved a clean and jerk score of 120.5 kg, breaking the national record of 120 kg. His total score was 295 kg, equaling the national record.

Chen Jingkai and Yin Guohua, featherweight athletes from Beijing Institute of Physical Education, achieved total scores of 305 kg and 297.5 kg respectively during the demonstration, both exceeding the national record of 295 kg held by Yin Guohua. Chen Jingkai’s clean and jerk score was 127.5 kg, exceeding the national record of 119.5 kg. Yin Guohua and Chen Jingkai, in their 4th snatch attempts (scores not counted in the total), lifted 93 kg and 92.5 kg respectively, both exceeding the national record of 92 kg.

In the flyweight weightlifting competition, Li Jiyuan, a 19-year-old youth from Guangzhou, won first place with a total score of 250 kg. Chen Naibai from Tianjin and Fei Fanglai from Shanghai won second and third place respectively. Their total scores were both 247.5 kg.

In the featherweight weightlifting competition, Cheng Xiaodong from Guangzhou won first place with a total score of 275 kg. Huang Huicheng and Gu Baoxiang from Shanghai won second and third place respectively, with total scores of 267.5 kg and 265 kg.

More than 40 athletes participated in this competition. The February 25th competition lasted from 7:00 PM to 11:50 PM. There were over 2,000 spectators. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 北京等四城市举重对抗赛在上海举行  北京体育学院四名举重运动员打破全国最高纪录

1956-02-27
第3版()
专栏：

    北京等四城市举重对抗赛在上海举行
    北京体育学院四名举重运动员打破全国最高纪录
    1956年北京、天津、上海、广州四城市举重对抗赛25日晚上在上海体育馆举行了第一场比赛。北京体育学院举重队的运动员参加了表演。他们打破了最轻量级和次轻量级全国最高纪录。
    在表演中，北京体育学院的最轻量级举重运动员陈永博的举重总成绩达到了300公斤，比他自己保持的全国最高纪录多了5公斤，而且同第十五届奥林匹克运动会同级第三名的总成绩相等。他双手推举的成绩是95公斤，比他自己保持的全国最高纪录多了3公斤，而且相等于第十五届奥林匹克运动会同级单项第一名的成绩。北京体育学院的最轻量级举重运动员薛德明双手挺举的成绩是120.5公斤，打破了120公斤的全国最高纪录。他的总成绩是295公斤，同全国最高纪录相等。
    北京体育学院的次轻量级运动员陈镜开和殷国华在表演中，总成绩分别达到了305公斤和297.5公斤，都超过了殷国华保持的295公斤的全国最高纪录。陈镜开双手挺举的成绩是127.5公斤，超过了119.5公斤的全国最高纪录。殷国华和陈镜开在第4次（成绩不计算在总成绩内）双手抓举的时候，分别举起了93公斤和92.5公斤，都超过了92公斤的全国最高纪录。
    在最轻量级举重对抗赛中，获得第一名的是广州市19岁的青年黎纪源。他的总成绩是250公斤。天津市的陈迺柏和上海市的费芳来分别获得了第二和第三名。他们的总成绩都是247.5公斤。
    在次轻量级举重对抗赛中，广州市的程晓东以275公斤的总成绩获得第一名。上海市的黄惠成和顾宝祥分别以267.5公斤和265公斤的总成绩获得第二和第三名。
    参加这次对抗赛的有40多名运动员。25日的比赛由晚上7时进行到11时50分。观众有2，000多人。（新华社）
