Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Hosts Banquet for the National Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1956-02-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 15th: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, hosted a grand banquet this evening with 600 people in attendance, to welcome His Royal Highness Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, and the entire members of the National Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia he led.

    At 8:20 PM, amidst welcoming music, His Royal Highness Prince Sihanouk and the entire members of the delegation he led, accompanied by Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, and others, entered the hall. A long and enthusiastic applause erupted in the hall at this moment.

    Premier Zhou Enlai and His Royal Highness Prince Sihanouk delivered speeches successively at the banquet. At the conclusion of their speeches, they proposed toasts for the health of His Majesty King Norodom Suramarit of the Kingdom of Cambodia and Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, and the band played the Cambodian and Chinese national anthems respectively.

    Throughout the speeches of Premier Zhou Enlai and His Royal Highness Prince Sihanouk, the entire venue resounded with stormy applause.

    During the banquet, guests and hosts frequently rose to toast the friendly cooperation between the people of China and Cambodia, the growing prosperity and strength of China and Cambodia, and lasting peace in the Far East and the world.

    Present at the banquet today were Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yi and Li Fuchun; Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Li Weihan, and Chen Shutong; Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen; Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng; Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council Marshal Ye Jianying, Cheng Qian, Fu Zuoyi, and Long Yun; and Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhang Bojun and Burhan.

    Also present at the banquet were Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Directors of various offices Luo Ruiqing, Jia Tuofu, and Wang Shoudao; Ministers of various ministries Xie Juezai, Shi Liang, Qian Ying, Zhang Naiqi, Zeng Shan, Ye Jizhuang, Wang Heshou, Zhang Linzhi, Chen Yu, Yang Yichen, Sha Qianli, Teng Daiyuan, Zhu Xuefan, Liao Luyan, Liang Xi, Ma Wenrui, Shen Yanbing, and Zhang Xiruo; Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, Zhang Hanfu, and Ji Pengfei; Members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Wang Kunlun, Wang Weizhou, Wu Yuzhang, Li Shucheng, Li Zhuchen, Zhou Jianren, Shao Lizi, Hu Yaobang, Gao Chongmin, Ma Mingfang, Zhang Bangying, Zhang Nanxian, Zhang Su, Xu Guangping, Xu Deheng, Chen Shaoxian, Cheng Zihua, Huang Shaohong, Liao Chengzhi, Liu Ningyi, Cai Tingkai, Lai Ruoyu, Luo Longji, Chen Qiyuan, and Deng Chumin; Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army General Chen Geng; Senior Generals Zhang Zongxun, Li Kenong, and Peng Shaohui; Deputy Directors of the General Political Department Senior Generals Xiao Hua and Gan Siqi.

    Also present at the banquet were the Vice President of the Supreme People's Court; Deputy Directors of various offices of the State Council; Vice Ministers of various ministries and Deputy Directors of various committees; Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; responsible persons of various departments, services, and arms of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; responsible persons of various democratic parties and people's organizations; Vice Mayors of Beijing; and representatives from the scientific, educational, literary, journalistic, and religious circles of Beijing, as well as social celebrities and representatives of various ethnic groups.

    Heads of diplomatic missions from various countries to China were also invited to the banquet. They were the ambassadors of Poland, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Romania, Mongolia, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Bulgaria, Albania, Hungary, Finland, Czechoslovakia, Pakistan, Yugoslavia, India, the German Democratic Republic, Norway, and Afghanistan; the ministers of Denmark and Switzerland; the charges d'affaires of the Netherlands and the United Kingdom; and the acting charges d'affaires of Sweden, Burma, Indonesia, and the Soviet Union.

### 周恩来总理设宴招待柬埔寨王国国家代表团

1956-02-16
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理设宴招待柬埔寨王国国家代表团
    新华社15日讯　柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王殿下和他所率领的柬埔寨王国国家代表团全体人员，今晚应邀出席了中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来为欢迎他们而隆重举行的，有600人参加的宴会。
    下午8时20分，在欢迎曲声中，西哈努克亲王殿下和他所率领的代表团全体人员由刘少奇委员长和周恩来总理等陪同，进入大厅。这时，大厅里响起了长久的热烈的掌声。
    周恩来总理和西哈努克亲王殿下在宴会上先后致词。在他们讲话结束的时候，他们分别为柬埔寨王国国王诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特陛下和中华人民共和国主席毛泽东的健康干杯，乐队分别奏柬埔寨国歌和中国国歌。
    周恩来总理和西哈努克亲王殿下在讲话的时候，全场不断报以暴风雨般的掌声。
    席间，宾主频频起立，互为中柬两国人民的友好合作，为中柬两国的日益繁荣富强，为远东和世界的持久和平而干杯。
    出席今天宴会的，有国务院副总理陈毅、李富春，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、黄炎培、李维汉、陈叔通，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、北京市市长彭真，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，国防委员会副主席叶剑英元帅、程潜、傅作义、龙云，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧、鲍尔汉。
