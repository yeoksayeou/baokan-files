Title: Japan Peace Preservation Committee Decides on This Year's Movement Policy
Author:
Date: 1956-02-22
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 20th: The Japan Peace Preservation Committee held a plenary meeting in Tokyo on February 18th and 19th. The meeting decided on the movement policy for 1956. This policy stipulates that the main tasks of the Japan Peace Preservation Committee at present are: to oppose the expansion of military bases; to oppose the malicious revision of the constitution and promote the movement to safeguard the peaceful constitution; to promote the restoration of diplomatic relations between Japan and China; to promote the early conclusion of an agreement in the Japan-Soviet negotiations; to strengthen the national movement against atomic and hydrogen bomb tests in the Pacific region and to promote the conclusion of a convention banning atomic weapons by all relevant countries; to demand the immediate return of Okinawa; and to demand the success of the Japan-Korea talks on the issue of overseas Chinese held in Pyongyang.
    The meeting adopted a statement calling on the Japanese people, together with all peace-loving people, to fight for the conclusion of an international agreement on disarmament and the prohibition of atomic weapons.
    The meeting also decided that the Japan Peace Preservation Committee should be reorganized in accordance with democratic principles, and that the charter should be revised and the executive organs re-elected at the next temporary plenary meeting.

### 日本拥护和平委员会决定今年运动方针

1956-02-22
第4版()
专栏：

    日本拥护和平委员会决定今年运动方针
    新华社20日讯　东京消息：日本拥护和平委员会2月18日和19日在东京举行全体会议。会议决定了1956年的运动方针。这个方针规定：日本拥护和平委员会当前的主要任务是：反对扩大军事基地；反对恶劣地修改宪法，推进维护和平宪法的运动；促进恢复日中邦交；促使日苏谈判早日达成协议；加强反对在太平洋区域试验原子弹和氢弹，促使各有关国家缔结禁止原子武器公约的国民运动；要求立即归还冲绳；要求在平壤举行的日本—朝鲜关于侨民问题的会谈成功。
    会议通过了一项声明，号召日本人民同所有爱好和平的人们一起，为要求签订裁军和禁止原子武器的国际协定而斗争。
    会议还决定，日本拥护和平委员会应该按照民主原则改组，并且在下次临时全体会议上修改章程和改选执行机关。
