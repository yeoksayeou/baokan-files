Title: Dong Shouyi at the International Olympic Committee Meeting Demands the Revocation of Recognition of the So-Called "National Sports Federation of China" in Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1956-02-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 1st — The International Olympic Committee held its 51st session in Cortina d'Ampezzo, Italy, from January 24th to 25th.

    Dong Shouyi, member of the International Olympic Committee and Vice-Chairman of the Chinese Olympic Committee, attended the meeting. He issued a statement at the meeting demanding that the International Olympic Committee revoke its recognition of the so-called "National Sports Federation of China" in Taiwan. In his statement, Dong Shouyi introduced the development of sports in New China and the reorganization of the original National Sports Federation of China into the All-China Sports Federation. He also emphasized that Taiwan is an inseparable part of China, and the sports organization in Taiwan Province should be an agency under the leadership of the All-China Sports Federation (Chinese Olympic Committee). According to the provisions of the Olympic Charter, a country can only have one National Olympic Committee. Therefore, the International Olympic Committee should revoke its recognition of the so-called "National Sports Federation of China" in Taiwan.

    However, the meeting, using the excuse that this issue was not included in the original agenda, did not make a decision on this matter.

### 董守义在国际奥林匹克委员会会议上  要求撤销对台湾的所谓中华全国体育协进会的承认

1956-02-02
第1版()
专栏：

    董守义在国际奥林匹克委员会会议上
    要求撤销对台湾的所谓中华全国体育协进会的承认
    新华社布拉格1日电　国际奥林匹克委员会1月24日到25日在意大利的科蒂纳丹佩佐举行了第51届会议。
    国际奥林匹克委员会委员、中国奥林匹克委员会副主席董守义出席了这次会议。他在会上发表声明，要求国际奥林匹克委员会撤销对台湾的所谓“中华全国体育协进会”的承认。董守义在声明中介绍了新中国体育运动的发展和原中华全国体育协进会改组为中华全国体育总会的情况，并且着重指出台湾是中国不可分割的领土，中国台湾省的体育组织应当是属于中华全国体育总会（中国奥林匹克委员会）领导的一个省的机构。根据奥林匹克运动会宪章的规定，一个国家只能有一个国家奥林匹克委员会，因此，国际奥林匹克委员会应当撤销对所谓台湾“中华全国体育协进会”的承认。
    但是，会议以这个问题没有列入原订议程为借口，没有对这个问题作出决定。
