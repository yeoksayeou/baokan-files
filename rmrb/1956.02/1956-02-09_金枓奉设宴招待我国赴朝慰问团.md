Title: Kim Tu-bong Hosts Banquet for Chinese People's Delegation to Korea
Author:
Date: 1956-02-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 8th. Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, held a banquet in Pyongyang tonight to entertain the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea. Wang Zi'yi, head of the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, and the deputy heads and other members of the delegation attended the banquet.

Those present from the Korean side included: Park Jung-ai, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party; Choi Chang-ik, Vice-Premier of the Cabinet; General Kim Kwang-hyup, Chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army; and responsible persons of the Korean Workers' Party and the Korean government, senior generals of the Korean People's Army, and figures from various democratic parties, people's organizations, and cultural and artistic circles.

Representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteers, Pan Zili, Chinese Ambassador to North Korea, and diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union and other countries to North Korea were also invited to the banquet.

### 金枓奉设宴招待我国赴朝慰问团

1956-02-09
第4版()
专栏：

    金枓奉设宴招待我国赴朝慰问团
    新华社平壤8日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉今天晚上在平壤举行宴会，招待中国人民赴朝慰问团。中国人民赴朝慰问团总团长王子宜和各副总团长及其他团员应邀出席了宴会。
    朝鲜方面出席作陪的有：朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、内阁副首相崔昌益、朝鲜人民军总参谋长金光侠大将及朝鲜劳动党和朝鲜政府负责人、朝鲜人民军高级将领、各民主党派、人民团体、文化艺术界人士。
    中国人民志愿军代表团、中国驻朝鲜大使潘自力和苏联及其他国家驻朝外交使节也应邀出席了宴会。
