Title: Renowned Linguist and Philologist Yang Shuda Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1956-02-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

    Renowned linguist and philologist Professor Yang Shuda passed away in Changsha on the 14th due to hypertension. He was 71 years old.

    Chairman Mao Zedong sent a special telegram of condolence. Zhou Enlai, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, sent a wreath. Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and others also sent telegrams of condolence.

    A memorial service was held in Changsha on the 16th, and on the same day, Yang Shuda's remains were buried in the scenic Yuelu Mountain in the suburbs. Nearly 400 people, including Party and government leaders of Hunan Province and people from all walks of life, attended the funeral.

    Yang Shuda was a specially invited person to the Second Plenary Session of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC, a member of the Department of Philosophy and Social Sciences of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, a representative of the Hunan Provincial People's Congress, a member of the Hunan Provincial People's Committee, Curator of the Hunan Provincial Museum of Literature and History, and a professor at Hunan Normal College. He was from Changsha, Hunan Province. In his early years, he studied ancient Chinese grammar, and later he studied philology. He authored more than 20 works on language and literature, including "Advanced Chinese Grammar," "Ci Quan," "Collected Essays on Epigraphy and Philology from the Jiju Residence," "A Description of Bronze Inscriptions from the Jiju Residence," and "A Study of Oracle Bone Inscriptions and Trivial Records from the Jiju Residence." He had previously taught at Peking University, Tsinghua University, Beijing Normal University, and other universities. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 著名语言文字学家杨树达逝世

1956-02-28
第3版()
专栏：

    著名语言文字学家杨树达逝世
    中国著名语言文字学家杨树达教授14日因高血压症在长沙逝世。享年71岁。
    毛泽东主席特致电悼念。政协全国委员会主席周恩来赠送了花圈。中国科学院院长郭沫若等人也致电吊唁。
    16日，长沙举行了追悼会，同日，杨树达遗体被安葬在市郊名胜区岳麓山。送葬的有湖南省党、政负责人和各界人士近400人。
    杨树达是政协二届全国委员会第二次全体会议特别邀请人士、中国科学院哲学社会科学部委员、湖南省人民代表大会代表、湖南省人民委员会委员、湖南省文史馆馆长和湖南师范学院教授。他是湖南省长沙人。早年研究古代汉语语法，后来研究文字学。著有“高等国文法”、“词全”、“积微居金石小学论丛”、“积微居金文述”、“积微居甲文学说卜辞琐记”等20多种语文著作。生前曾经在北京大学、清华大学、北京师范大学等校任教。　（新华社）
