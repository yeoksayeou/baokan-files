Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Issues Notice: Convening National Conference of Advanced Producers Representatives
Author:
Date: 1956-02-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 20th: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions issued a notice on February 20th regarding the convening of a National Conference of Advanced Producers Representatives. The abstract is as follows:

In order to mobilize all staff and workers to launch a socialist emulation drive to welcome the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China and to complete the First Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, according to the resolution of the Fourth Session of the Seventh Executive Committee, decided to convene a National Conference of Advanced Producers Representatives in Beijing on April 30, 1956. Trade union organizations at all levels should further mobilize all staff and workers to comprehensively launch a socialist emulation drive, to do more, faster, better and more economically in production and work, and to bring forth more advanced producers and send outstanding representatives to participate in the National Conference of Advanced Producers Representatives.

I. Qualifications of Representatives:

	1. Advanced producers who have completed the targets stipulated in the First Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule;
	2. Advanced producers who have reached excellent quality standards;
	3. Advanced producers who have achieved success in learning and promoting advanced experience or in mastering advanced technology and trial-producing new products;
	4. Advanced producers who have achieved excellent results in conservation;
	5. Outstanding rationalization proposalists and organizers of rationalization proposal work;
	6. Advanced producers who can regularly help workers who do not meet the quota to improve to an advanced production level;
	7. Advanced engineering and technical personnel and staff;
	8. Advanced workers who have made outstanding achievements in their work (such as excellent educators, scientific workers, commercial workers, etc.);
	9. Representatives of advanced groups, workshops, and enterprises.

II. Methods of Selection of Representatives:

	1. Advanced producer representatives should, in principle, be selected by industrial system as industrial representatives, and each region should select regional representatives (including representatives of enterprises that have not yet organized national industrial trade unions).
	2. Each enterprise unit shall convene a conference of advanced producers or a conference of representatives to evaluate and select representatives to attend the industrial or provincial and municipal advanced producers representative conferences.
	3. The national committees (preparatory committees, working committees) of each industrial trade union, in conjunction with the corresponding economic departments, shall convene advanced producer representative conferences (or model worker conferences) to elect industrial representatives to attend the National Conference of Advanced Producers Representatives to form an industrial delegation. Industrial trade unions that do not convene advanced producer representative conferences may use recommendation methods to produce representatives to attend the National Conference of Advanced Producers Representatives (but representatives recommended to attend the National Conference of Advanced Producers Representatives must be advanced producers selected by the grassroots). The provincial and municipal local trade unions, in conjunction with the provincial and municipal people's committees, shall convene advanced producer representative conferences (or model worker conferences) to elect regional representatives to attend the National Conference of Advanced Producers Representatives, forming provincial and municipal local delegations.

III. A total of 5,800 representatives will attend the conference, with the following allocation of places:

	1. 4,540 representatives selected by industry.
	2. 1,000 representatives selected by provinces and municipalities.
	3. 260 specially invited representatives.

### 中华全国总工会发出通知  召开全国先进生产者代表会议

1956-02-21
第1版()
专栏：

    中华全国总工会发出通知
    召开全国先进生产者代表会议
    据新华社20日讯　中华全国总工会20日发出关于召开全国先进生产者代表会议的通知，摘要如下：
    为了动员全体职工开展社会主义竞赛迎接中国共产党第八次全国代表大会，提前完成第一个五年计划，根据中华全国总工会第七届执行委员会第四次会议的决议决定在1956年4月30日在首都北京召开全国先进生产者代表会议，工会各级组织应进一步发动全体职工全面开展社会主义竞赛，把生产和工作做得更多、更快、更好、更省，涌现更多的先进生产者，并选派其中优秀的代表参加全国先进生产者代表会议。
    一、代表的条件：
    1．提前完成第一个五年计划规定指标的先进生产者；
    2．达到优等质量指标的先进生产者；
    3．在学习与推广先进经验或在掌握先进技术试制新产品方面有成就的先进生产者；
    4．在节约方面有优良成绩的先进生产者；
    5．优秀的合理化建议者和合理化建议工作的组织者；
    6．能够经常帮助达不到定额的工人提高到先进生产水平的先进生产者；
    7．先进的工程技术人员和职员；
    8．在工作中有优秀成绩的先进工作者（如优秀的教育工作者、科学工作者、商业工作者等等）；
    9．先进小组、车间、企业的代表。
    二、代表的产生办法：
    1．先进生产者代表原则上按产业系统选出产业代表，各地方选出地区性的代表（其中包括尚未组织全国产业工会的企业的代表）。
    2．各企业单位召开先进生产者大会或代表会议，评选出席产业或省、市地方先进生产者代表会议的代表。
    3．各产业工会全国委员会（筹委会、工作委员会）会同相应的经济部门召开先进生产者代表会议（或劳动模范大会），选出出席全国先进生产者代表会议的产业代表，组成产业代表团，不召开先进生产者代表会议的产业工会可用推荐办法产生出席全国先进生产者代表会议的代表（但被推荐出席全国先进生产者代表会议的代表，必须是经基层选出的先进生产者），各省、市地方工会会同省、市人民委员会召开先进生产者代表会议（或劳动模范大会）选举出席全国先进生产者代表会议的地区代表，组成省、市地方代表团。
    三、出席会议的代表共5，800名，名额分配如下：
    1、按产业选出的代表4，540名。
    2、按省、市地方选出的代表1，000名。
    3、特邀代表260名。
