Title: Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Continue
Author:
Date: 1956-02-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, February 19th: The Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks held their 37th meeting at the Palais des Nations on the morning of February 18th at 10:00 AM. The meeting adjourned at 1:45 PM. The next meeting will be held on the 24th.

### 中美两国大使级会谈继续举行

1956-02-20
第1版()
专栏：

    中美两国大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦19日电　中美两国大使级会谈2月18日上午10时在国联大厦举行第三十七次会议。会议在13时45分休会。下次会议将在24日举行。
