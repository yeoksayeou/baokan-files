Title: Our Delegation to North Korea Extends Greetings to Korean and Chinese Troops
Author:
Date: 1956-02-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 20th: Representatives from the general delegation and the first sub-delegation of the Chinese People's Delegation to North Korea, as well as actors from the Yue Opera Troupe and the Acrobatic Troupe, have been conducting extensive consolation activities and performances at the leadership organs of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the command organs of various armed services.

Representatives of the delegation reported to the officers and soldiers of the Volunteer Army leadership organs on the socialist construction situation in the motherland, and held symposiums with representatives of meritorious personnel from various armed services. Meng Tai, a representative of the delegation and a national model worker in industry, reported at one symposium how the workers of the repair shop he led were striving to complete the first Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule and above quota. The representatives of meritorious personnel repeatedly applauded to pay him their respects. Wang Anming, an officer of the artillery troops, pinned the military medal awarded to him by the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea on Meng Tai's chest on the spot.

Wang Ziyi, the head of the general delegation, led some representatives to extend greetings to the leading organs of the Volunteer Army's logistics units, braved wind and snow to sweep the tomb of martyr Luo Shengjiao, and extended greetings to the Korean people of Shuocang-ri on the "Luo Shengjiao River".

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 20th: Le Songsheng and Zhang Zizhen, deputy heads of the Chinese People's Delegation to North Korea, along with some representatives and members of the song and dance troupe, have been extending greetings to the Korean People's Army on the eastern front in recent days. Another group of literary and artistic workers from the delegation is also conducting consolation performances in the People's Army troops on the western front.

After holding a grand consolation meeting at the leading organ of a certain unit of the People's Army on the eastern front, the delegation representatives went in groups to various units of the People's Army to conduct consolation and discussions. Braving the severe cold of minus 20 degrees Celsius and wind and snow, when the representatives climbed the famous Eagle Peak, the officers and soldiers of the People's Army guarding there immediately rushed down from various positions to warmly welcome the Chinese friends from afar. Le Songsheng, the deputy head of the delegation, presented a banner to the People's Army troops guarding Eagle Peak and expressed the lofty respect of the Chinese people to them.

### 我国赴朝慰问团慰问朝中部队

1956-02-22
第4版()
专栏：

    我国赴朝慰问团慰问朝中部队
    新华社平壤20日电　中国人民赴朝慰问团总团和第一分团的代表及越剧团、杂技团的演员，在中国人民志愿军领导机关和各兵种指挥机关进行广泛的慰问活动和慰问演出。
    慰问团代表分别向志愿军领导机关的官兵作了祖国社会主义建设情况的报告，并且同各兵种部队的功臣代表举行了座谈会。慰问团代表、全国工业劳动模范孟泰在一次座谈会上报告了他领导的修理场的工人们，如何为提前并且超额完成第一个五年计划而努力，功臣代表们一再鼓掌向他致敬。炮兵部队军官王安明当场把朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府授予他的军功章别在孟泰的胸前。
    慰问团总团长王子宜率领部分代表还到志愿军后勤部队领导机关进行了慰问，并且冒着风雪祭扫了罗盛教烈士墓，慰问了“罗盛教河”畔朔仓里的朝鲜人民。
    新华社平壤20日电　中国人民赴朝慰问团副总团长乐松生、张梓桢和部分代表及歌舞团人员，连日在朝鲜东线慰问朝鲜人民军。慰问团的另一部分文艺工作人员，也在西线人民军部队中进行慰问演出活动。
    慰问团代表在东线人民军某部领导机关举行了盛大的慰问大会以后，又分组深入到人民军各部队中去进行慰问和座谈。代表们冒着零下20多度的严寒和风雪，攀登上著名阵地鹰峰的时候，守卫在那里的人民军官兵立刻从各个阵地上飞奔下来热情地迎接来自远方的中国友人。慰问团副总团长乐松生献给守卫鹰峰的人民军部队一面锦旗，并且代表中国人民向他们表示崇高的敬意。
