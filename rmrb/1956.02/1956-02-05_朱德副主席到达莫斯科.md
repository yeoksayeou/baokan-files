Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1956-02-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 4th - The delegation of the People's Republic of China, headed by Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Moscow by special train at 9:50 am on February 4th. Accompanying Vice Chairman Zhu De were Marshal Nie Rongzhen, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council of the People's Republic of China, and Liu Lantao, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China.

The sound of military music rang out at the Moscow Belarus Station, and the special train slowly entered the station. After Zhu De got off the train, he shook hands with the people who came forward to welcome him.

Those who came to the station to welcome the delegation today included: Mikoyan, Molotov, Soviet Minister of Defense Zhukov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Fedin, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Moscow City Soviet of Working People's Deputies Bobrovnikov, Director of the Far East Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs Gudev, Moscow City Defense Commander Kolesnikov, as well as other government personnel and Soviet and foreign journalists.

Many heads of diplomatic missions in the Soviet Union also came to the station to welcome the delegation. Liu Xiao, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, and embassy staff also came to the station to welcome them.

Today, the national flags of China and the Soviet Union were hung at the Belarus Station, and honor guards were lined up on the open-air platform. The band played the national anthems of China and the Soviet Union. Vice Chairman Zhu reviewed the honor guard.

Vice Chairman Zhu delivered a speech on the station platform. He said: The great Soviet Union, with the brilliant light of its socialist and communist construction, illuminates the path for working people all over the world to strive for peace and build a better life. The great peace policy pursued by the Soviet Union has made a great contribution to the cause of world peace. The Chinese people will never forget the deepest brotherly friendship given by the Soviet people. Since the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Chinese people have received sincere, comprehensive, and great assistance from the Soviet people and government in the arduous struggle to restore the economy and establish a socialist economic foundation. For this, the Chinese people express their heartfelt thanks. Please allow me to convey the Chinese people's warm respect and best wishes to the Soviet people.

Fomin, Chief of Protocol of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and Wen Ning, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, went to the Polish-Soviet border to welcome Vice Chairman Zhu.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 4th - At 3:00 pm on the 4th, Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, went to the Kremlin to call on Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. Accompanying him were Marshal Nie Rongzhen, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council of the People's Republic of China, Liu Lantao, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, and Liu Xiao, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union.

Also present at the meeting were Begov, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and Fomin, Chief of Protocol.

### 朱德副主席到达莫斯科

1956-02-05
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德副主席到达莫斯科
    新华社莫斯科4日电　2月4日上午9时50分，以中华人民共和国副主席朱德为首的中华人民共和国代表团乘专车到达莫斯科。和朱德副主席一道前来的有中华人民共和国国防委员会副主席聂荣臻元帅和中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员刘澜涛。
    莫斯科白俄罗斯车站响起军乐的声音，专车缓缓驶进车站。朱德下车后同走上前来欢迎的人们一一握手。
    今天前来车站欢迎的有：米高扬、莫洛托夫、苏联国防部长朱可夫、外交部副部长费德林、莫斯科市劳动人民代表苏维埃执行委员会主席波布罗夫尼科夫、苏联外交部远东司司长顾德夫、莫斯科市城防司令科列斯尼科夫以及其他政府人员和苏联和外国的新闻记者们。
    到车站欢迎的还有许多国家驻苏外交使节。中国驻苏大使刘晓和使馆人员也到车站欢迎。
    今天，白俄罗斯车站上悬挂着中苏两国国旗，露天站台排列着整齐的仪仗队。乐队奏中苏两国国歌。朱副主席检阅了仪仗队。
    朱副主席在车站月台上发表了讲话。他说：伟大的苏联，用它的社会主义、共产主义建设事业的灿烂的光辉照耀着全世界劳动群众争取和平和建立美好生活的道路。苏联所奉行的伟大的和平政策对世界和平事业作出了巨大贡献。中国人民永远不会忘记苏联人民所给予的最深厚的兄弟友谊。中国人民建国以来在恢复经济和建立社会主义经济基础的艰巨斗争中，获得了苏联人民和政府的真诚的、全面的伟大帮助。对于这些，中国人民表示衷心的感谢。请允许我转达中国人民对苏联人民的热烈的敬意和最好的祝愿。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团礼宾长弗明以及中国驻苏大使馆参赞温宁曾到波苏边境欢迎朱副主席。
    新华社莫斯科4日电　4日下午3时，中华人民共和国副主席朱德前往克里姆林宫拜会苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫。随同前往拜会的有：中华人民共和国国防委员会副主席聂荣臻元帅、中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员刘澜涛和中国驻苏联大使刘晓。
    拜会时在座的有：苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书别哥夫和礼宾长弗明。
