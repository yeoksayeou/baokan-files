Title: All-China Women's Federation and Other Organizations to Regularly Convene National Representatives' Meeting of Industrialists' and Businessmen's Families and Female Industrialists and Businesswomen

Author: 
Date: 1956-02-20
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency report:

    The All-China Women's Federation, the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, and the Central Standing Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association jointly issued a notice on the 18th regarding the convening of a National Representatives' Meeting of Industrialists' and Businessmen's Families and Female Industrialists and Businesswomen:

    The notice stated: In order to extensively and deeply mobilize the families of industrialists and businessmen and female industrialists and businesswomen nationwide to study diligently, further transform their thinking, and raise their socialist consciousness, so as to play a greater positive role in the great cause of socialist construction and socialist transformation, it has been decided to convene the National Representatives' Meeting of Industrialists' and Businessmen's Families and Female Industrialists and Businesswomen in Beijing on March 28, 1956. The meeting will be attended by 1,000 representatives, who will be recommended by provinces (autonomous regions) and cities according to the allocated quota. The families of large, medium, and small industrialists and businessmen should account for about 80% of all representatives, and the remaining 20% should include a certain number of female large, medium, and small industrialists and businessmen and capitalist agents.

    The notice stated: In order to do a good job in organizing the work during the meeting and assisting in the communication work after the meeting, in addition to the formal representatives, 180 cadres from the All-China Women's Federation, the Federation of Industry and Commerce, and the China Democratic National Construction Association system who are participating in the transformation of capitalist industry and commerce will attend the meeting as observers.

    The notice pointed out: In the process of preparing for this meeting, all localities should, under the leadership of the local Chinese Communist Party committees, mobilize the forces of relevant parties to vigorously strengthen the work of industrialists' and businessmen's families and female industrialists and businesswomen; at the same time, carry out extensive publicity activities in order to further mobilize and organize all industrialists' and businessmen's families and female industrialists and businesswomen to study diligently, work actively, and sincerely accept socialist transformation.

Xinhua News Agency report:
    The preparatory committee for the National Representatives' Meeting of Industrialists' and Businessmen's Families and Female Industrialists and Businesswomen was formally established on the 18th. This preparatory committee is composed of 30 people, with Zhang Yun, Vice Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, as the director of the preparatory committee, and Hu Zi'ang, Luo Shuzhang, and Pu Jiexiu as deputy directors.

### 全国妇联等定期召开全国工商业者家属和女工商业者代表会议

1956-02-20
第2版()
专栏：

    全国妇联等定期召开全国工商业者家属和女工商业者代表会议
    中华全国民主妇女联合会、中华全国工商业联合会、中国民主建国会中央常务委员会在18日联合发出关于召开全国工商业者家属和女工商业者代表会议的通知：
    通知中说：为了广泛深入地动员全国工商业者家属和女工商业者努力学习，进一步改造思想，提高社会主义觉悟，以便在伟大的社会主义建设和社会主义改造事业中发挥更大的积极作用，特定于1956年3月28日在北京召开全国工商业者家属和女工商业者代表会议。出席会议的代表共1，000人，由各省（自治区）、市按分配名额推选。代表中，大、中、小工商业者家属应占到全部代表的80％左右，其他20％左右应包括一定数量的女的大、中、小工商业者和资方代理人。
    通知中说：为了作好会议期间的组织工作和会后协助进行传达工作，除正式代表外，另由全国民主妇联、工商联、中国民主建国会系统共派参加资本主义工商业改造工作的干部180人列席此次会议。
    通知指出：各地在进行此项会议的准备过程中，应该在当地中国共产党党委领导下，动员有关方面的力量，大力加强工商业者家属和女工商业者的工作；同时广泛展开宣传活动，以便进一步动员组织全体工商业者家属和女工商业者努力学习、积极工作，诚心诚意地接受社会主义改造。
                  （据新华社讯）
    全国工商业者家属和女工商业者代表会议的筹备委员会已经在18日正式成立。这个筹备委员会由30人组成，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席章蕴为筹备委员会主任，胡子婴、罗叔章、浦洁修为副主任。
                  （据新华社讯）
