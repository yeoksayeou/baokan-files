    "What's being transported?" "Wood."
    "What's being transported away?" "Wood." Wei Qimei
    The Mudanjiang Forestry Area ships out large quantities of timber every year. However, the Mudanjiang Fourth Housing Construction Section (under the leadership of the Harbin Railway Bureau) insists on transporting the timber it needs from elsewhere, causing a great waste in transportation!
