Title: Youth Gatherings in Various Cities Commemorate "Day of Struggle Against Colonial System"
Author:
Date: 1956-02-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 21st: Over 800 young people in Beijing gathered on February 21st to commemorate the "Day of Struggle Against Colonial System." Wan Taihe, Chairman of the Beijing Student Federation, said at the meeting that today's "Day of Struggle Against Colonial System" is being commemorated amidst a new upsurge in the global anti-colonial movement. The Asian-African Conference marked the end of the era in which imperialism could arbitrarily enslave the people of other countries. After describing the efforts of young people of various oppressed nations in opposing the colonial system, striving for national independence, and defending world peace, he said that Chinese youth, with deep sympathy, have infinite concern for the situation and lives of the young people of oppressed nations. The conference passed an open letter to the youth of colonial and oppressed countries. The letter said: Your anti-colonial struggle is of great significance today. Your struggle has shaken the rule of the colonialists, powerfully stopped the evil conspiracies of the American aggressive bloc, and is leading to the disintegration of the entire imperialist colonial system. The youth of Beijing are always watching the further magnificent development of the anti-colonial struggles of the people and youth of various colonial and oppressed countries with incomparable concern, and are encouraged by your continuous victories in the struggle.

Youth in Shanghai, Tianjin, Shenyang, Guangzhou, Wuhan, Chongqing, Xi'an and other cities also held separate gatherings to commemorate the "Day of Struggle Against Colonial System." At the commemoration meetings in these cities, Chinese youth expressed heartfelt sympathy and full support for the just struggles of various colonial countries. The commemoration meetings in the aforementioned cities also separately adopted telegrams and letters to the youth of Japan, Latin America, the Middle East, India, and Africa.

### 各城市青年集会纪念“反对殖民制度斗争日”

1956-02-22
第1版()
专栏：

    各城市青年集会纪念“反对殖民制度斗争日”
    据新华社21日讯　北京市800多名青年21日集会纪念“反对殖民制度斗争日”。北京市学生联合会主席万泰和在会上说，今天“反对殖民制度斗争日”是在全世界反殖民主义运动新的高涨中来纪念的。亚非会议标志着帝国主义任意奴役别国人民的时代已经一去不复返了。他叙述了各被压迫民族青年在反对殖民制度、争取民族独立、保卫世界和平所作的努力以后说，中国青年怀着深厚的同情，对被压迫民族青年的处境及生活寄以无限关怀。大会通过了给殖民地和被压迫国家青年的一封公开信。信中说：你们反殖民主义的斗争在今天有着重大的意义。你们的斗争，动摇了殖民主义者的统治，有力地阻止了美国侵略集团的罪恶阴谋，并且正在导致整个帝国主义殖民体系的瓦解。北京青年时刻都在以无比关怀的心情，注视着各殖民地和被压迫国家人民和青年反殖民主义斗争的进一步壮阔发展，并且为你们在斗争中不断取得的胜利所鼓舞。
    上海、天津、沈阳、广州、武汉、重庆、西安等城市的青年，也分别集会，纪念“反对殖民制度斗争日”。在这些城市的纪念会上，我国青年表示衷心同情并且全力支持各殖民地国家的正义斗争。上述城市的纪念会，并分别通过致日本、拉丁美洲、中近东、印度、非洲青年的电文和信件。
