Title: Signing of the 1956 Executive Plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-02-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, February 15th - Bucharest, February 14th: The 1956 Executive Plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement was signed in Bucharest, the capital of Romania, on February 13th, after friendly and cooperative discussions between representatives of the People's Republic of China and the Romanian People's Republic.

    Ke Bonian, head of the Chinese delegation and Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Romania, signed on behalf of the Chinese side; Rosianu, head of the Romanian delegation and Chairman of the Romanian Association for Foreign Culture, signed on behalf of the Romanian side.

    After the signing ceremony, the Romanian delegation hosted a banquet for the Chinese delegation and guests who attended the signing ceremony. In the evening, the Chinese delegation also hosted a banquet for the Romanian delegation and guests who attended the signing ceremony.

    The banquets proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 中罗文化合作协定1956年执行计划签字

1956-02-17
第1版()
专栏：

    中罗文化合作协定1956年执行计划签字
    新华社布拉格15日讯　布加勒斯特14日消息：中罗文化合作协定1956年执行计划，经过中华人民共和国和罗马尼亚人民共和国双方代表在友好合作气氛中进行商谈后，2月13日在罗马尼亚首都布加勒斯特举行了签字仪式。
    代表中国方面签字的，是中国代表团团长、中华人民共和国驻罗马尼亚大使柯柏年；代表罗马尼亚方面签字的，是罗马尼亚代表团团长、罗马尼亚对外文化协会主席罗山亚努。
    签字仪式完毕后，罗马尼亚代表团宴请中国代表团和参加签字仪式的来宾。当晚，中国代表团也宴请了罗马尼亚代表团和参加签字仪式的来宾。
    宴会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
