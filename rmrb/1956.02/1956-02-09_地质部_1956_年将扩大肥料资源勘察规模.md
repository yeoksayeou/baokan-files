Title: Ministry of Geology to Expand Fertilizer Resource Exploration in 1956
Author:
Date: 1956-02-09
Page: 1

    The Ministry of Geology will expand the scale of fertilizer resource exploration in 1956 to meet the needs of the country's accelerated development of chemical fertilizer production. In 1956, geological workers will explore phosphate mines in Sichuan, Yunnan, Anhui, Guizhou, Shaanxi, Guangxi and other provinces, and the exploration projects will increase two and a half times compared with 1955; the amount of drilling will increase more than one and a half times compared with 1955. In 1956, pyrite will also be explored in 6 regions to provide raw material production areas for manufacturing sulfuric acid. Sulfuric acid is an indispensable raw material for the production of nitrogen fertilizer, phosphate fertilizer, and potash fertilizer. In addition, two mineral producing areas for manufacturing potash fertilizer will also be explored. In the past, China has not been able to produce potash fertilizer. After geological workers have ascertained the underground conditions in these known mineral producing areas, they will provide plant design data for the establishment of fertilizer plants.
    The Ministry of Geology will also organize a large number of geological workers this year to look for new producing areas of phosphorus, potassium, and pyrite in 40 regions across the country. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 地质部1956年将扩大肥料资源勘察规模

1956-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    地质部1956年将扩大肥料资源勘察规模
    地质部在1956年将扩大肥料资源的勘察规模，以满足国家加速发展化学肥料生产的需要。1956年，地质工作者将在四川、云南、安徽、贵州、陕西、广西等省份勘探磷矿，勘探的项目将比1955年增加二倍半；钻探工作量将比1955年增加一倍半以上。1956年还要在6个地区勘探黄铁矿，以提供制造硫酸的原料产地。硫酸是生产氮肥、磷肥和钾肥不可缺少的原料。此外，还要勘探两个制造钾肥的矿产地。过去，中国还一直不能生产钾肥。地质工作者在这些已经知道的矿产地探明地下情况以后，将为建立肥料厂提供建厂设计资料。
    地质部在今年还将组织大批地质工作者在全国40个地区寻找磷、钾和黄铁矿的新产地。（据新华社讯）
