Title: Members of the Cambodian Delegation Conduct Separate Courtesy Calls
Author:
Date: 1956-02-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 16th: Members of the Kingdom of Cambodia National Delegation: Sang Nhean, Vice-President of the Council of the Kingdom; Eng Meas, Member of the National Assembly; and Dr. Nguon Smouk, Member of the Council of the Kingdom, paid a courtesy call on Peng Zhen, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, on the afternoon of the 16th. Present at the meeting were Li Shucheng, Hu Yuzhi, Zhang Su, and Liao Chengzhi, members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Yu Xinqing, Deputy Secretary-General.

On the same afternoon, Colonel Nhiek Tioulong, member of the Kingdom of Cambodia National Delegation and Chief of Staff of the Royal Air Force, paid a courtesy call on Marshal Ye Jianying, Vice-Chairman of the National Defense Council, General Li Da, Deputy Minister of National Defense, and General Liu Yalou, Commander of the Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Ron Soeung Sophan, member of the Kingdom of Cambodia National Delegation and Deputy Secretary of State for Farmers' Welfare, also paid a courtesy call on Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture, and Fu Zuoyi, Minister of Water Resources, this afternoon. Liu Ruilong, Vice Minister of Agriculture, and Wei Zhenwu, Assistant Minister, were also present at the meeting.

### 柬埔寨代表团团员分别进行拜会活动

1956-02-17
第1版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨代表团团员分别进行拜会活动
    新华社16日讯　柬埔寨王国国家代表团团员：王国会议副议长桑·年，国民会议议员恩格·米阿斯，王国会议议员尼尔·斯莫乌克博士在16日下午拜会了中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长彭真。拜会时在座的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员李书城、胡愈之、张苏、廖承志和副秘书长余心清。
    同日下午，柬埔寨王国国家代表团团员、皇家空军参谋长努·呼上校拜会了国防委员会副主席叶剑英元帅、国防部副部长李达上将和中国人民解放军空军司令员刘亚楼空军上将。
    柬埔寨王国国家代表团团员、农民福利副国务秘书罗恩·索方也在今天下午拜会农业部部长廖鲁言和水利部部长傅作义。拜会的时候，农业部副部长刘瑞龙、部长助理魏震五也在座。
