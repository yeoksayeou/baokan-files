    Friendly Exchanges
    △The Thailand People's Promotion of Friendship to China Delegation, led by Nai Thep Chotnuchit, President of the delegation, left Guangzhou on the 19th to return home via Hong Kong, after visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.
    △The Chinese Women's Cultural Delegation, headed by Li Dequan, arrived in Rome on February 17th. The delegation is visiting Italy at the invitation of the Italian Women's League.

### 友好往来

1956-02-20
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    △应中国人民外交学会邀请来中国访问参观的泰国人民促进友好访华团由团长乃贴·触的努七率领，19日离开广州取道香港回国。
    △以李德全为首的中国妇女文化代表团一行已经在2月17日到达罗马。代表团是应意大利妇女联盟的邀请到意大利作友好访问的。
