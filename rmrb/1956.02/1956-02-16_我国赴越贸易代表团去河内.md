Title: China's Trade Delegation to Vietnam Leaves for Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1956-02-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency News:
    China's trade delegation to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by Xie Xuegong, the head of the delegation and Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, departed from Beijing for Hanoi on the 14th.

### 我国赴越贸易代表团去河内

1956-02-16
第4版()
专栏：

    我国赴越贸易代表团去河内
    新华社讯　我国赴越南民主共和国贸易代表团，在团长、对外贸易部副部长解学恭率领下，已经在14日由北京出发前往河内。
