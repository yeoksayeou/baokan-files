Title: Chinese and Italian Commercial Counselors in the UK Hold Another Meeting on Trade Negotiation Issues
Author:
Date: 1956-02-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, London, 24th: On February 21st, the Commercial Counselor of the Office of the Chargé d'Affaires of the People's Republic of China in the UK and the Commercial Counselor of the Italian Embassy in the UK held their third meeting to discuss how to conduct trade negotiations.

### 中意两国驻英商务参赞  再次会谈贸易谈判问题

1956-02-25
第1版()
专栏：

    中意两国驻英商务参赞
    再次会谈贸易谈判问题
    新华社24日讯　伦敦消息：2月21日中华人民共和国驻英代办处商务参赞同意大利驻英大使馆商务参赞就如何进行贸易谈判问题进行了第三次会谈。
