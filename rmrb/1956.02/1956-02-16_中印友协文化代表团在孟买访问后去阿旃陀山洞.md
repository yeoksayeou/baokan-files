Title: Sino-Indian Friendship Association Cultural Delegation Visits Ajanta Caves After Visiting Mumbai
Author:
Date: 1956-02-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Mumbai, 13th: The Mumbai branch of the India-China Friendship Association held a farewell reception on the evening of the 12th at the local Napean Sea Road Garden to bid farewell to the Sino-Indian Friendship Association Cultural Delegation, which is currently visiting Mumbai.

When the chairman of the Mumbai branch of the India-China Friendship Association, Karanjia, delivered the opening speech, he guaranteed that the Indian people support the Chinese people's struggle to liberate Taiwan and expressed that the Indian people are grateful to the Chinese people for supporting India's struggle to liberate Goa. He said that the 1 billion people of India and China are much stronger than atomic bombs. The Indian people are opposing the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization and the Baghdad Pact based on this confidence.

The members of the delegation left Mumbai on the morning of the 13th for the Ajanta Caves and Ellora Caves.

### 中印友协文化代表团在孟买访问后去阿旃陀山洞

1956-02-16
第4版()
专栏：

    中印友协文化代表团在孟买访问后去阿旃陀山洞
    据新华社孟买13日电　印中友好协会孟买分会12日晚在本地纳浦花园举行了欢送会，欢送正在孟买访问的中印友好协会文化代表团。
    印中友协孟买分会主席卡朗吉亚在致开会词的时候，保证印度人民支持中国人民解放台湾的斗争，并且表示印度人民感谢中国人民支持印度解放果阿的斗争。他说，印度和中国10亿人民比原子弹强大得多。印度人民就是根据这种信心来反对东南亚条约组织和巴格达条约的。
    代表团团员们13日上午离开孟买前往阿旃陀山洞和爱楼罗山洞。
