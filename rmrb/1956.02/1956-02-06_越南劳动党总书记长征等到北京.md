Title: Vietnam Workers' Party General Secretary Truong Chinh Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-02-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. Vietnam Workers' Party General Secretary Truong Chinh and Politburo member Le Duc Tho, representing the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, were invited to attend the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 5th while en route through China. Deng Xiaoping, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary-General of the CPC Central Committee, Wang Jiaxiang, member of the CPC Central Committee, Yang Shangkun, Deputy Secretary-General of the CPC Central Committee, and Li Chuli and Li Qixin, responsible staff members of the CPC Central Committee organs, were at the station to welcome them.

Hoang Van Hoan, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, and Pham Binh, Second Secretary, were also at the station to welcome them.

### 越南劳动党总书记长征等到北京

1956-02-06
第1版()
专栏：

    越南劳动党总书记长征等到北京
    新华社5日讯　越南劳动党总书记长征、政治局委员黎德寿代表越南劳动党中央，应邀前往参加苏联共产党第二十次代表大会，路经中国，在5日下午到达北京。到车站欢迎的有中共中央政治局委员、中共中央秘书长邓小平，中共中央委员王稼祥，中共中央副秘书长杨尚昆，中共中央机关负责工作人员李初梨、李启新等。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使黄文欢和二等秘书范平也到车站欢迎。
