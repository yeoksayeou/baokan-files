Title: CPSU Congress Passes Central Committee Work Report
Author:
Date: 1956-02-21
Page: 4

    CPSU Congress Passes Central Committee Work Report
    Moscow dispatch from our newspaper, February 20th – After extensive discussions of the CPSU Central Committee's report, the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union unanimously decided to conclude the discussion at its afternoon session on the 20th. 51 speakers took the floor during the five-day discussion. The congress unanimously passed the resolution on the CPSU Central Committee's work report proposed by Kozlov on behalf of the Moscow, Leningrad, Ukraine, Belarus, and other Party organizations. When the resolution was passed, all the delegates stood up and the hall resounded with cheers. Subsequently, the congress unanimously adopted the report of the CPSU Central Auditing Commission.
    Moscow dispatch from our newspaper, February 20th – The 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, after adjourning on the 19th, reconvened on the 20th to continue discussing the CPSU Central Committee's work report. At the morning session on the 20th, speeches were delivered by Mustafayev, representative of the Azerbaijan Party organization; Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union; Muratov, representative of the Tatar Party organization; Daniyalov, representative of the Dagestan Party organization; the writer Sholokhov; Zhezhalin, representative of the Stalingrad Party organization; and the scientist Kurchatov. Sholokhov's brilliant critical speech was warmly applauded by the delegates. He suggested that writers should go deep into factories and rural areas, live with workers and peasants, and he raised the issue of paying attention to the training of the younger generation of writers. In his speech, Kurchatov talked about the achievements of the Soviet Union in the peaceful use of atomic energy and the work that should be paid attention to in the future. At the morning session, greetings were delivered by Aidit, representative of the Indonesian Communist Party; Tsedenbal, representative of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party; and Aaltonen, representative of the Finnish Communist Party.
    Xinhua News Agency dispatch from Moscow via TASS – A reception was held at the Kremlin on February 19th to entertain representatives of communist parties and other fraternal parties from various countries who are attending the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

### 苏共代表大会通过中央工作报告

1956-02-21
第4版()
专栏：

    苏共代表大会通过中央工作报告
    本报莫斯科20日专电　苏联共产党第二十次代表大会广泛地讨论了苏共中央的报告后，在20日下午会议上一致决定结束讨论。在五天讨论中，发言者51人。大会一致通过科兹洛夫代表莫斯科、列宁格勒、乌克兰、白俄罗斯等等党组织所提出的关于苏共中央工作报告的决议。通过这个决议的时候，全体代表起立，欢呼声响彻大厅。接着，大会一致通过了苏共中央检察委员会的报告。
    本报莫斯科20日专电　苏联共产党第二十次代表大会于19日休会后在20日复会，继续讨论苏共中央工作报告。在20日上午会议上先后发言的有阿塞拜疆党组织代表穆斯塔费夫，苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫，鞑靼党组织代表穆拉托夫，达吉斯坦党组织代表达尼雅洛夫，作家萧洛霍夫，斯大林格勒党组织代表热加林，科学家库尔查托夫。萧洛霍夫的精采的批评性发言，受到代表们的热烈鼓掌，他建议作家要深入到工厂和农村中去，和工人农民一起生活，他提出注意培养年青一代作家问题。库尔察托夫发言中谈到苏联和平利用原子能的成就和今后应注意的工作。在上午会议上，印度尼西亚共产党代表艾地，蒙古人民革命党代表泽登巴尔和芬兰共产党代表阿尔托宁致了贺词。
    新华社据塔斯社莫斯科讯　2月19日在克里姆林宫举行了招待会，招待参加苏联共产党第二十次代表大会的各国共产党和其他兄弟党的代表。
