Title: Nehru Says Force Will Be Used to Prevent Portuguese Encroachment on Indian Territory
Author:
Date: 1956-02-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, February 18th. According to the Press Trust of India on the 17th, Indian Prime Minister Nehru said in the People's Assembly on February 17th, in response to inquiries from members of parliament regarding the encroachment on the Indian border by Portuguese armed personnel on February 5th and 8th, that any Portuguese personnel encroaching on Indian territory in the future would be arrested and detained. The Indian government will also use force where necessary to prevent them from entering Indian territory.

He said that after the incidents of Portuguese armed personnel encroaching on the Indian border, the Indian government, in addition to protesting to the Portuguese authorities, had taken steps to strengthen the border police.

### 尼赫鲁表示将用武力防止葡萄牙人侵犯印度领土

1956-02-20
第4版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁表示将用武力防止葡萄牙人侵犯印度领土
    新华社新德里18日电　据印度报业托辣斯17日讯：印度总理尼赫鲁2月17日在人民院答复议员就葡萄牙武装人员在2月5日和8日侵犯印度边境事件所提出的询问时说今后任何侵犯印度领土的葡萄牙人员将遭到逮捕和扣留，印度政府还将在必要的地方使用武力来防止他们进入印度领土。
    他说，在发生葡萄牙武装人员侵犯印度边境的事件以后，印度政府除了向葡萄牙当局提出抗议以外，还采取了步骤来加强边境警察。
