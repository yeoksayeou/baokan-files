Title: Key Project of the Northern Section of the Neijiang-Kunming Railway: Construction of the Fuxi River Iron Bridge Begins
Author:
Date: 1956-02-24
Page: 1

    Key Project of the Northern Section of the Neijiang-Kunming Railway
    Construction of the Fuxi River Iron Bridge Begins
    The key project of the Nei-Yi section of the Neijiang-Kunming Railway—the Fuxi River Iron Bridge—commenced construction on the afternoon of February 21st. This bridge is 183.68 meters long and 26 meters high. Semi-mechanized construction will be used during the construction process. (New Zigong Daily Editorial Department)

### 内昆铁路北段重点工程  釜溪河铁桥动工兴建

1956-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

    内昆铁路北段重点工程
    釜溪河铁桥动工兴建
    内昆铁路内宜段重点工程——釜溪河铁桥，已经在2月21日下午开工兴建。这座桥长183.68公尺，高26公尺。修建过程中将采用半机械化施工。　　　（新自贡报编辑部）
