Title: Reactions to Premier Zhou's Political Report
Author:
Date: 1956-02-06
Page: 4

    Strive for Lasting Peace, Strive for People's Democracy!
    The February 3rd issue, number 5, of the 1956 edition of the newspaper "Strive for Lasting Peace, Strive for People's Democracy!", the organ of the Information Bureau of the Communist and Workers' Parties published in Bucharest, contained the full text of the Draft National Agricultural Development Program of the People's Republic of China for 1956 to 1967 and the political report made by Premier Zhou Enlai at the meeting of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.
    
    Soviet Union
    The "Pravda," "Izvestia," "Trud," and "Red Star" newspapers published on January 31st the news of the opening of the Second Session of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference on January 30th, and also published the main contents of the political report made by Zhou Enlai at the meeting. The newspapers also reported on the meeting of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on the issue of intellectuals.
    
    Vietnam
    The "Nhan Dan" newspaper published a commentary on February 1st saying that Premier Zhou Enlai, in his political report at the Second Session of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference on January 30th, suggested reconvening the Geneva Conference on Indochina and believed that the three countries of the Vietnam International Commission—India, Poland, and Canada—should be invited to participate in order to discuss measures to ensure the continued implementation of the Geneva Accords in Vietnam.
    
    The commentary said that the Vietnamese people, who have consistently and unswervingly struggled for the respect of the Geneva Accords and the resolution of the political issues of Vietnam, warmly approve and support the proposal put forward by the Government of the People's Republic of China aimed at resolving the political issues of Vietnam stipulated in the Geneva Accords.
    
    Czechoslovakia
    "Rude Pravo," in its February 2nd commentary on Premier Zhou Enlai's political report at the meeting of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, said that all the peaceful proposals put forward by Zhou Enlai at the meeting regarding the most pressing Asian issues are in line with the interests of Asian countries.
    
    Referring to the proposal to convene a Far East conference to resolve the peaceful reunification of Korea, the newspaper pointed out that Asian countries are particularly qualified to resolve this issue. The newspaper emphasized that Asian countries consider the proposal to convene a Geneva Conference on Indochina as an important contribution to strengthening peace in Southeast Asia.
    
    After mentioning Zhou Enlai's appeal to the Kuomintang military and political personnel in Taiwan in his report, "Rude Pravo" pointed out that the People's Republic of China is using all possible means to peacefully liberate Taiwan. The newspaper also mentioned Zhou Enlai's statement that Taiwan will be liberated by war if necessary.

### 关于周总理的政治报告的反应

1956-02-06
第4版()
专栏：

    关于周总理的政治报告的反应
    争取持久和平，争取人民民主！
    2月3日在布加勒斯特出版的共产党和工人党情报局机关报“争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”1956年第五期刊载了中华人民共和国1956年到1967年全国农业发展纲要草案全文和周恩来总理在中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会会议上所作的政治报告。
    苏联
    “真理报”、“消息报”、“劳动报”、“红星报”1月31日登载了中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第二次全体会议1月30日开幕的消息，并且登载了周恩来在会上作的政治报告的主要内容。报纸还报道了中国共产党中央委员会召开的关于知识分子问题会议的消息。
    越南
    “人民报”2月1日发表评论说，周恩来总理1月30日在中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第二次全体会议上所作的政治报告中，建议重新召开有关印度支那问题的日内瓦会议，并且认为应该邀请越南国际委员会的三个国家——印度、波兰和加拿大参加，以便讨论保证在越南继续执行日内瓦协定的措施。
    评论说，一贯坚持不渝地为尊重日内瓦协定和解决越南政治问题而进行斗争的越南人民，热烈赞成和支持中华人民共和国政府所提出的这个旨在解决日内瓦协定所规定的越南政治问题的建议。
    捷克斯洛伐克
    “红色权利报”在2日评论周恩来总理在中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会会议上所作的政治报告时说，周恩来在会上提出的中国关于解决最迫切的亚洲问题的一切和平建议，是符合亚洲国家的利益的。
    这家报纸在谈到召开远东会议来解决朝鲜的和平统一问题的建议时指出，亚洲国家特别有资格来解决这个问题。这家报纸强调指出，亚洲国家认为关于召开有关印度支那问题的日内瓦会议的建议，是对加强东南亚和平的重要贡献。
    “红色权利报”在提到周恩来的报告中对台湾国民党军政人员发出的号召以后指出，中华人民共和国正在利用一切可能来和平解放台湾。报纸还提到周恩来关于在必要的时候将用战争方式解放台湾的声明。
