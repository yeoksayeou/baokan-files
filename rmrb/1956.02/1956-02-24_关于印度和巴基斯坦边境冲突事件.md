Title: On the Border Conflict Between India and Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1956-02-24
Page: 4

    Regarding the Border Conflict Between India and Pakistan

    Prime Minister Nehru's Statement
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, February 23rd – Indian Prime Minister Nehru stated in the People's House on February 22nd that on February 19th, Pakistani troops in Chhad Bet in the Kutch region of India's northwest frontier used automatic weapons to fire upon an Indian military patrol, wounding 3 Indian soldiers and killing 3 camels.

    Nehru said that this was a serious act of intrusion into Indian territory, and the government will take necessary actions regarding this matter.

    Nehru said that Chhad Bet is an uninhabited area that is flooded for 4 months of the year and used as pasture for the remainder of the time. He mentioned that last year, Pakistan attempted to claim that this was a disputed area belonging to Pakistan. Nehru said that it is undoubtedly a part of India, and India has always exercised ownership.

    Pakistani Government Communiqué
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, February 23rd – According to a Press Trust of India dispatch from Karachi: The Pakistani government issued a communiqué stating that a conflict occurred on the border between India and Pakistan. The conflict occurred between Indians and a police force from Sindh (a province of Pakistan). The communiqué also stated that Pakistani troops were not involved in any conflict with Indian troops.

### 关于印度和巴基斯坦边境冲突事件

1956-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

    关于印度和巴基斯坦边境冲突事件
    尼赫鲁总理谈话
    新华社新德里23日电　印度总理尼赫鲁2月22日在人民院指出，巴基斯坦军队2月19日在印度西北边境喀齐邦的查德贝特用自动武器向印度的军事巡逻队开火，有3名印度士兵负伤，3匹骆驼被打死。
    尼赫鲁说，这是严重地侵入印度领土的行动，政府将就这件事采取必要的行动。
    尼赫鲁说，查德贝特是没有人居住的地区，那里每年有4个月为水淹没，其余的时候被用来作为牧场。他提到，在去年，巴基斯坦曾企图表明这是有所争执的地区，是属巴基斯坦的。尼赫鲁说，无疑的它是印度的一部分，所有权一直由印度行使。
    巴基斯坦政府的公报
    新华社新德里23日电　据印度报业托辣斯喀喇蚩消息：巴基斯坦政府发表公报说，在印度和巴基斯坦边境发生了冲突。冲突是在印度人同信德（巴基斯坦的一省）警察队之间发生的。公报还说，巴基斯坦军队没有卷入同印度军队发生的任何冲突中。
