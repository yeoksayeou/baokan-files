Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1956-02-24
Page: 4
    △ After a 28-day visit to China, the Gymnastics Team of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 23rd to return home.
    The Russian Gymnastics Team held 10 gymnastics performances in Beijing, Tianjin, Xi'an, Nanjing, and Shanghai; they also held a friendly competition with Chinese gymnasts in Beijing. The audience totaled more than 50,000 people.
    △ The Chinese government delegation to the Leipzig International Fair arrived in Berlin by plane on the afternoon of February 20th. The Leipzig International Fair will open on the 26th.
    △ Professor Duan Xuefu, Director of the Department of Mathematics and Mechanics at Peking University, left Beijing by plane on the 21st for India to attend the South Asian Mathematics Education Conference. This conference is scheduled to be held in Mumbai from February 22nd to 28th.

### 友好往来

1956-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

    友好往来
    △俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国体操队在中国访问了28天以后，23日上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    俄罗斯体操队在北京、天津、西安、南京和上海共举行了10场体操表演；在北京还同中国的体操运动员举行了一次友谊比赛。观众共有5万多人。
    △中国政府参加来比锡国际博览会的代表团已在2月20日下午乘飞机到达柏林。来比锡国际博览会将于26日开幕。
    △北京大学数学力学系主任段学复教授21日由北京乘飞机去印度，参加南部亚洲数学教育会议。这个会议定于2月22日到28日在孟买举行。
