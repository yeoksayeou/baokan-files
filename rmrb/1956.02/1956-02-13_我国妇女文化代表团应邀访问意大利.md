Title: China's Women's Cultural Delegation Visits Italy at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1956-02-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 12th - At the invitation of the Italian Women's League, the All-China Women's Federation has sent a women's cultural delegation to visit Italy. The head of the delegation is Li Dequan. The delegation of four left Beijing for Rome on February 10.

### 我国妇女文化代表团应邀访问意大利

1956-02-13
第4版()
专栏：

    我国妇女文化代表团应邀访问意大利
    新华社12日讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会应意大利妇女联盟的邀请，派了一个妇女文化代表团访问意大利。代表团团长是李德全。代表团一行4人已在2月10日离北京前往罗马。
