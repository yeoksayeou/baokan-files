Title: Picture
Author: Meng Zhaorui
Date: 1956-02-13
Page: 4

    The Beijing Opera Troupe of the China National Academy of Chinese Theatre Arts, which accompanied the Chinese People's Delegation to North Korea, gave its first performance in Pyongyang on February 7 at the Moranbong Theater. Premier Kim Il-sung attended the performance. The picture shows members of the delegation presenting flowers to Premier Kim Il-sung. (Photo by Meng Zhaorui, Xinhua News Agency)
