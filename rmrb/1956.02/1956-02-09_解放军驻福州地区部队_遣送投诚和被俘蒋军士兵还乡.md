Title: PLA Troops Stationed in Fuzhou Area Repatriate Surrendered and Captured Chiang Kai-shek Soldiers to Their Hometowns
Author:
Date: 1956-02-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 7th: The leadership of the People's Liberation Army troops stationed in the Fuzhou area repatriated seven surrendered and captured Chiang Kai-shek soldiers to their hometowns on the 6th and 7th. Each of them received ample travel expenses and daily necessities. Among them were Zhao Zhimin, a soldier from the Second Battalion of the "Anti-Communist National Salvation Army" who defected from Jinmen Island by drifting across the sea, and Zhang Tao (also known as Li Gui), a captured soldier from the directly subordinate reconnaissance team of the Second General Team of the Chiang Kai-shek army's "Anti-Communist National Salvation Army," and others.

### 解放军驻福州地区部队  遣送投诚和被俘蒋军士兵还乡

1956-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    解放军驻福州地区部队
    遣送投诚和被俘蒋军士兵还乡
    据新华社福建前线7日电　中国人民解放军驻福州地区部队领导机关在6日和7日遣送7名投诚和被俘的蒋军士兵回乡。他们每人都领到充足的路费和日用品。其中有从金门岛漂海投诚归来的蒋军“反共救国军”第二总队二大队士兵赵治民和被俘的蒋军“反共救国军”第二总队直属侦察队士兵张涛（又名李贵）等。
