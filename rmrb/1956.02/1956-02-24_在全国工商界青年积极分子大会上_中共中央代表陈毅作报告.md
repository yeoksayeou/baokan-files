Title: At the National Congress of Young Activists from Industry and Commerce, Chen Yi, Representative of the CPC Central Committee, Delivers Report
Author:
Date: 1956-02-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd: Chen Yi, representative of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, delivered an important report today at the National Congress of Young Activists from Industry and Commerce.

    He began by extending warm congratulations to the congress. In his report, he praised the active role played by young activists from industry and commerce in the socialist transformation of capitalist industry and commerce. He also gave important instructions on how young activists from industry and commerce, and young people in industry and commerce nationwide, should continue to work hard, carry out transformation, and on issues such as the arrangement, future, and learning of young people in industry and commerce.

    His report was received with extremely warm welcome from the entire audience. At the end of the report, the entire audience stood up and shouted "Long live the Communist Party of China" and "Long live Chairman Mao."

### 在全国工商界青年积极分子大会上  中共中央代表陈毅作报告

1956-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

    在全国工商界青年积极分子大会上
    中共中央代表陈毅作报告
    新华社23日讯　中国共产党中央委员会代表陈毅今天在全国工商界青年积极分子大会上作了重要报告。
    他首先向大会致以热烈的祝贺。他在报告中，赞扬了工商界青年积极分子在资本主义工商业的社会主义改造中所表现的积极作用，并且对工商界青年积极分子和全国工商界青年在今后如何继续努力、进行改造以及工商界青年的安排、前途和学习等问题，作了重要指示。
    他的报告，受到全场极为热烈的欢迎。报告结束的时候，全场起立，高呼“中国共产党万岁”、“毛主席万岁”。
