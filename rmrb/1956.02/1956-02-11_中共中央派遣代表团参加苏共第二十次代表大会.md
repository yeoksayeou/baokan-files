Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Delegation to Attend the 20th Congress of the CPSU
Author:
Date: 1956-02-11
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, February 10th—The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, has sent a delegation headed by Zhu De, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, to the Soviet Union to attend the upcoming 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. The members of the delegation are: Deng Xiaoping, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and Tan Zhenlin, Wang Jiaxiang, and alternate member of the Central Committee Liu Xiao.
    Apart from Comrades Zhu De and Liu Xiao, who are already in Moscow, Comrades Deng Xiaoping, Tan Zhenlin, and Wang Jiaxiang left Beijing by plane for Moscow at 8:00 AM on February 9th.

### 中共中央派遣代表团参加苏共第二十次代表大会

1956-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央派遣代表团参加苏共第二十次代表大会
    新华社10日讯　中共中央委员会应苏共中央委员会的邀请，派遣以中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记朱德为首的代表团，赴苏联参加即将举行的苏联共产党第二十次代表大会。代表团的团员有：中共中央政治局委员邓小平，中共中央委员谭震林、王稼祥和候补中央委员刘晓。
    除朱德和刘晓两同志已经在莫斯科外，邓小平、谭震林、王稼祥同志一行也已经在2月9日上午8时，乘飞机离开北京前往莫斯科。
