Title: Ministry of Culture Invites Art界 Figures Attending the CPPCC National Committee Meeting to Symposium; All-China Women’s Federation and Beijing Women’s Federation Host Female Members of the CPPCC National Committee, etc.
Author:
Date: 1956-02-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 8th – The Ministry of Culture on February 8th invited figures from the art界 attending and observing the Second Session of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference to separate symposiums.

The symposiums were presided over by Qian Junrui and Xia Yan, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Culture, and the head of the Art Affairs Administration Bureau of the Ministry of Culture. At the meetings, actors, directors, musicians, and artists spoke freely about the question of better leveraging the power of the art界 and the question of strengthening learning; they also exchanged views with the responsible personnel of the Ministry of Culture on the overall plan for art undertakings.

This newspaper reports that the All-China Democratic Women’s Federation and the Beijing Democratic Women’s Federation hosted female members and observers attending the Second Session of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People’s Political Consultative Conference at the auditorium of the Beijing Peace Hotel on the morning of February 8th.

Zhang Yun, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women’s Federation, on behalf of the hosts, extended a welcome and congratulations to the guests. She said, "We hope that after returning to their respective posts, all sisters will actively lead the broad masses of women to march towards socialism."

At the reception, the guests performed many programs.

### 文化部邀出席政协全国委员会会议艺术界人士座谈  全国妇联和北京妇联招待政协全国委员会女委员等

1956-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    文化部邀出席政协全国委员会会议艺术界人士座谈
    全国妇联和北京妇联招待政协全国委员会女委员等
    据新华社8日讯　文化部在8日邀请出席和列席中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第二次全体会议的艺术界人士分别举行座谈会。
    座谈会由文化部副部长钱俊瑞、夏衍和文化部艺术事业管理局负责人主持。会上，演员、导演、音乐家和美术家们畅谈了关于更好地发挥艺术界力量的问题和加强学习的问题；他们还和文化部的负责人员对艺术事业的全面规划交换了意见。
    本报讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会和北京市民主妇女联合会，2月8日上午在北京和平宾馆礼堂招待出席中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第二次全体会议的女委员和女列席人员。
    中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席章蕴代表主人，对客人们表示欢迎和祝贺。她说：“我们希望各位姊妹们在回到各自的工作岗位以后，积极地带动广大妇女群众，向社会主义迈进。”
    招待会上，来宾们表演了许多节目。
