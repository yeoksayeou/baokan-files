Title: Naming Ceremony of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Agricultural Production Cooperative in the Suburbs of Beijing (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1956-02-14
Page: 3

Beijing Suburbs Sino-Soviet Friendship Agricultural Production Cooperative Naming Ceremony

    Left picture: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Agricultural Production Cooperative in Shijingshan District, Beijing suburbs, held its naming ceremony on February 13. Li Haiqiu (second from left), Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, Qian Junrui (first from left), Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Zhang Youyu (third from left), Deputy Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Deputy Mayor of Beijing, attended the ceremony. Xinhua News Agency reporter Yu Huiru

    Bottom picture: Venue of the naming ceremony of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Agricultural Production Cooperative in Shijingshan District, Beijing suburbs. Xinhua News Agency reporter Yu Huiru

### 北京郊区中苏友好农业生产合作社命名典礼大会（图片）

1956-02-14
第3版()
专栏：

    北京郊区中苏友好农业生产合作社命名典礼大会
    左图：北京市郊石景山区的中苏友好农业生产合作社在2月13日举行命名典礼大会。苏联驻华大使馆临时代办李海秋（左二）和中苏友好协会总会秘书长钱俊瑞（左一）、中共北京市委副书记、北京市副市长张友渔（左三）等参加了大会。	  新华社记者喻惠如摄
