Title: Chairman Mao Receives Norwegian Ambassador Kloug-Hansen
Author:
Date: 1956-02-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd - Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China received Kloug-Hansen, the first Ambassador to China from the Kingdom of Norway, on the afternoon of the 23rd. Present at the reception were Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Huang Hua, Director of the Department of Western European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department.

### 毛主席接见挪威大使克洛格—亨生

1956-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见挪威大使克洛格—亨生
    新华社23日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东于23日下午接见挪威王国首任驻华大使克洛格—亨生。接见的时候，在座的有中华人民共和国总理兼外交部长周恩来，外交部西欧非洲司司长黄华和礼宾司副司长王拓。
