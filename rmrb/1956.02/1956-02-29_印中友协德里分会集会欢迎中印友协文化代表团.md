Title: Delhi Branch of the India-China Friendship Association Holds Rally to Welcome the Sino-Indian Friendship Association Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-02-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, February 27th. The Delhi Branch of the India-China Friendship Association held a mass rally at Gandhi Square in Old Delhi on the evening of February 26th to welcome the Sino-Indian Friendship Association Cultural Delegation, headed by Wu Han, which recently returned after visiting more than 30 cities in India.

About 3,000 people attended the welcoming meeting, including Delhi State Development Minister Brahm Prakash, members of parliament, members of the Delhi State Legislative Assembly, social celebrities, and cultural workers.

President Radha Raman of the Delhi Branch of the India-China Friendship Association, President Kechil of the All-India Peace Council, and President Sendral of the India-China Friendship Association all spoke, welcoming the delegation and emphasizing the long-standing friendship between India and China.

Wu Han delivered a speech in reply, thanking them for the warm welcome to the delegation. He said that cultural exchanges between China and India have played an important role in promoting cultural ties between the two countries in the past.

At the meeting, artists from China and India performed interesting Indian and Chinese song and dance programs.

### 印中友协德里分会集会欢迎中印友协文化代表团

1956-02-29
第4版()
专栏：

    印中友协德里分会集会欢迎中印友协文化代表团
    新华社新德里27日电　印中友好协会德里分会2月26日晚间在旧德里的甘地广场举行群众欢迎会，欢迎最近在印度访问了30多个城市之后回来的以吴晗为首的中印友好协会文化代表团。
    出席欢迎会的有德里省开发部长布拉姆·普拉卡希、国会议员们、德里省立法会议议员们、社会名流和文化工作者等约3，000人。
    印中友好协会德里分会主席拉达·拉曼、全印和平理事会主席克其鲁和印中友好协会主席森德拉尔都讲话表示欢迎代表团，并且强调指出印度和中国的悠久的友谊。
    吴晗致答词，感谢对代表团的热烈欢迎。他说，中国和印度之间的文化交流在过去的历史中，曾经对促进两国之间的文化联系起了重要的作用。
    会上，印中两国的艺术家表演了有趣的印度和中国的歌舞节目。
