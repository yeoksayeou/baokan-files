Title: Voroshilov's 75th Birthday: Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, and Zhou Enlai Send Congratulatory Telegram
Author:
Date: 1956-02-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd: February 4th, 1956 is the 75th birthday of Kliment Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai jointly sent a telegram to Voroshilov to congratulate him. The congratulatory telegram is as follows:

Dear Comrade K.Y. Voroshilov:

On the occasion of your 75th birthday, we, on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China, extend our warm congratulations to you.

We sincerely wish you good health and longevity, and wish you even more brilliant achievements in leading the Soviet people in the great cause of communist construction and in defending world peace.

Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China
Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
Beijing, February 3, 1956

### 伏罗希洛夫75岁寿辰  毛泽东、刘少奇、周恩来致电祝贺

1956-02-04
第1版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫75岁寿辰
    毛泽东、刘少奇、周恩来致电祝贺
    新华社3日讯　1956年2月4日是苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫75岁寿辰。毛泽东主席、刘少奇委员长和周恩来总理联名打电报给伏罗希洛夫，向他祝贺。贺电如下：亲爱的克·叶·伏罗希洛夫同志：
    当你75岁寿辰时，我们谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民向你致热烈的祝贺。
    衷心地祝你健康长寿，并祝你在领导苏联人民进行伟大的共产主义建设和保卫世界和平的事业中，获得更光辉的成就。
    中华人民共和国主席毛泽东
    中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长　刘少奇
    中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                                                1956年2月3日于北京
