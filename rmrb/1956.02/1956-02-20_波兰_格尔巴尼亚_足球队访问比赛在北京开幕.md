Title: Polish "Gwardia" Football Team's Visit and Competition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-02-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 19th: The opening ceremony of the Polish Krakow "Gwardia" football team's visit and competition in China was held on the afternoon of February 19th at the Xian Nong Tan Stadium in Beijing.

    Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Ji Pengfei, Vice Chairmen of the Sports Commission Cai Tingkai, Cai Shufan, Lu Han, Huang Qixiang, Rong Gaotang, as well as the Polish Ambassador to China Kirilo and diplomatic envoys from various countries attended the opening ceremony and watched the match.

    Before the opening ceremony, Chen Yi met with the person in charge of the Polish Krakow "Gwardia" football team. After the match, Chen Yi, Guo Moruo, Polish Ambassador to China Kirilo, and others went to the field to shake hands with the football players from both Poland and China to express their congratulations.

    Vice Chairman of the Sports Commission Cai Tingkai delivered a welcome speech at the opening ceremony, saying that the visit of the Polish Krakow "Gwardia" football team to China, bringing with it the good experience of Polish football, will certainly promote the developing Chinese football movement.

    The leader of the Polish Krakow "Gwardia" football team, A. Gudowski, also spoke. On behalf of the football team and Polish athletes, he congratulated the Chinese people on achieving further successes in developing sports and socialist construction.

    After the opening ceremony, the Polish Krakow "Gwardia" football team played a friendly match with the Beijing Youth Football Team. The Beijing Youth Football Team won three to two.

    Xinhua News Agency, February 18th: Vice Chairman of the Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, Cai Shufan, received the leader of the Polish Krakow "Gwardia" football team, A. Gudowski, deputy leader J. Mokrzecki, director of the "Gwardia" club R. Łowcz, coach J. Kusinka, and doctor M. Bochnic on the afternoon of February 18th.

### 波兰“格尔巴尼亚”足球队访问比赛在北京开幕

1956-02-20
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰“格尔巴尼亚”足球队访问比赛在北京开幕
    据新华社19日讯　波兰克拉科夫市“格尔巴尼亚”足球队来中国访问比赛的开幕式19日下午在北京市先农坛体育场举行。
    国务院副总理陈毅，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若，外交部副部长章汉夫、姬鹏飞、体育运动委员会副主任蔡廷锴、蔡树藩、卢汉、黄琪翔、荣高棠，以及波兰驻华大使基里洛克和各国驻华外交官员出席了开幕式；并且观看了比赛。
    陈毅在开幕式以前，接见了波兰克拉科夫市“格尔巴尼亚”足球队的负责人。在比赛结束以后，陈毅、郭沫若和波兰驻华大使基里洛克等又来到球场上，同波、中两国足球队员握手，表示祝贺。
    体育运动委员会副主任蔡廷锴在开幕式上致欢迎词说，波兰克拉科夫市“格尔巴尼亚”足球队访问中国，带来波兰足球运动的良好经验，一定会推动正在发展的中国足球运动。
    波兰克拉科夫市“格尔巴尼亚”足球队领队亚·古多夫斯基也讲了话。他代表这个足球队和波兰的运动员们祝贺中国人民在发展体育运动和社会主义建设中获得更进一步的成就。
    开幕式以后，波兰克拉科夫市“格尔巴尼亚”足球队同北京青年足球队举行了友谊比赛。北京青年足球队以三比二获胜。
    据新华社18日讯　中华人民共和国体育运动委员会副主任蔡树藩18日下午接见了波兰克拉科夫市“格尔巴尼亚”足球队领队亚·古多夫斯基，副领队约·莫克尔净斯基，“格尔巴尼亚”俱乐部主任鲁·洛伏斯，教练约·库欣卡和医生米·鲍赫尼克。
