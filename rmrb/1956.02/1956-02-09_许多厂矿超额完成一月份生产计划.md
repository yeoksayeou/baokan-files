Title: Many Factories and Mines Exceeded January Production Plans
Author:
Date: 1956-02-09
Page: 1

A number of factories and mines in Chongqing, Lushun-Dalian, Jiangsu, and Shanxi, among other places, exceeded their state production plans for January.

Local industries in Chongqing exceeded the January gross output value plan by 6.3%. The plan completed this month was 8.22% higher than December of last year, which had the highest plan fulfillment. Many factories in Chongqing that are guaranteeing the early completion of the Five-Year Plan, such as the Qijiang Auto Parts Factory and the Chongqing Diesel Engine Factory, fully exceeded the state plan for January.

In Lushun-Dalian, 12 state-owned factories and 21 local state-owned factories completed their January output value plans. The Dalian Chemical Factory produced an extra 505 tons of ammonium sulfate for farmers in January. The quality of steel at the Dalian Steel Plant has already surpassed the targets originally set for 1957. The Dalian General Machinery Factory completed the orders for three types of equipment for the First Automobile Works ahead of schedule in January. The Dalian Shipyard also delivered a batch of sand dredgers for the Dahuofang Reservoir ahead of schedule.

A number of state-owned, local state-owned, and joint state-private factories and mines in Nanjing, Xuzhou, Wuxi, Suzhou, and Xin Hailian in Jiangsu Province exceeded their January production plans. In Nanjing, 37 state-owned, local state-owned, and joint state-private factories completed or exceeded their January production plans ahead of schedule. Many workers at the state-owned Nanjing Machine Tool Factory broke new production quotas in January. The local state-owned Jiangsu Machinery Factory completed its January gross output value plan 9 days ahead of schedule.

81 local state-owned factories and mines in Shanxi Province exceeded their gross output value plan for January this year. The gross output value of local state-owned industries in January increased by 18.78% compared with the same period last year. 22 major products, including electricity, steel ingots, sulfur, cotton yarn, cotton cloth, heavy leather, and coarse woolen fabrics, all exceeded their plans.

### 许多厂矿超额完成一月份生产计划

1956-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    许多厂矿超额完成一月份生产计划
    重庆、旅大、江苏、山西等地一批厂矿，超额完成了1月份的国家生产计划。
    重庆市地方工业各厂超额6.3％完成了1月份生产总值计划。这个月完成的计划，比去年完成计划最高的12月份还多8.22％。重庆许多保证提前完成五年计划的工厂，如綦江汽车配件厂和重庆柴油机厂等，都全面地超额完成了1月份的国家计划。
    旅大市有12个国营工厂和21个地方国营工厂完成了1月份产值计划。大连化学工厂1月份为农民多生产了505吨硫铵。大连钢厂钢的质量已经超过了原订1957年的指标。大连通用机器厂在1月份已经提前完成了第一汽车厂的3种设备的订货。大连造船公司也提前交出了大伙房水库的一批挖砂船订货。
    江苏省南京、徐州、无锡、苏州和新海连等市一批国营、地方国营和公私合营厂矿超额完成了1月份生产计划。南京市提前或超额完成1月份生产计划的有37个国营、地方国营和公私合营工厂。国营南京机床厂1月份很多工人都突破了新的生产定额。地方国营江苏机器厂提前9天完成1月份生产总值计划。
    山西省81个地方国营厂矿超额完成了今年1月份的生产总值计划。地方国营工业1月份的生产总值比去年同期增长了18.78％。电力、钢锭、硫磺、棉纱、棉布、重革、粗纺毛织品等22种主要产品都超额完成了计划。
