Title: The Closing of the First National Congress of Jiu San Society Members
Author:
Date: 1956-02-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 16th: The first National Congress of Jiu San Society Members closed on the 16th.

    During the meeting, the delegates listened to Xu Deheng's work report and Tu Changwang's report on the revision of the Society's charter. The delegates also studied and discussed the documents of the Second Session of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the report on intellectuals made by Zhou Enlai, Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, at the meeting on intellectuals convened by the CPC Central Committee. In the discussions, the delegates unanimously agreed that in the high tide of socialist construction and socialist transformation in our country, Jiu San Society organizations at all levels should more actively promote and help members and the intellectuals they connect with to give full play to their strength, improve their professional level, raise their socialist consciousness, and bravely march towards science, in order to complete the glorious tasks entrusted by the country.
    Today, the congress elected the new Central Committee of the Jiu San Society. The chairman of the Central Committee is Xu Deheng, the vice chairman is Liang Xi, and the secretary-general is Tu Changwang.
    The congress unanimously adopted the resolution on the work report, the Jiu San Society's charter, and the resolution on the report of the proposal examination committee.
    The congress also passed a message of respect to Chairman Mao Zedong.
    Liang Xi delivered the closing speech at the meeting. He said that the times are calling to us: intellectuals must contribute to socialism. With the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, as long as we are honest, diligent, and guard against exaggeration and arrogance, we will surely be able to shoulder the mission of this era and be a thorough socialist intellectual.

### 九三学社首届全国社员代表大会闭幕

1956-02-17
第1版()
专栏：

    九三学社首届全国社员代表大会闭幕
    据新华社16日讯　九三学社第一届全国社员代表大会16日闭幕。
    会议期间，代表们听了许德珩的社务报告和涂长望的关于社章修改的报告。代表们还学习和讨论了中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第二次全体会议的文件和中共中央书记周恩来在中共中央召开的关于知识分子问题的会议上作的关于知识分子问题的报告。代表们在讨论中，一致认为，在我国社会主义建设和社会主义改造的高潮中，九三学社各级组织应该更积极地推动并且帮助社员和所联系的知识分子充分发挥力量，提高业务水平，提高社会主义觉悟，勇敢地向科学进军，以完成国家给予的光荣任务。
    今天，大会选出了九三学社的新的中央委员会。中央委员会主席为许德珩，副主席为梁希，秘书长为涂长望。
    大会一致通过了关于社务报告的决议，九三学社社章和关于提案审查委员会的报告的决议。
    大会还通过了向毛泽东主席的致敬电。
    会议由梁希致闭幕词。他说，时代在向我们召唤：知识分子必须为社会主义贡献力量。我们有中国共产党和毛主席的领导，只要我们老老实实、勤勤恳恳、力戒浮夸和骄傲，我们一定能够担当起这个时代的使命，一定能够做一个不折不扣的社会主义的知识分子。
