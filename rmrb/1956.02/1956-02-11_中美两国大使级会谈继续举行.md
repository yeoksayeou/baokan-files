Title: Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Continue

Author: 
Date: 1956-02-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 9th - The Sino-U.S. ambassadorial talks held their 36th meeting on February 9th. The meeting adjourned at 14:55. The next meeting will be held on February 18th.

### 中美两国大使级会谈继续举行

1956-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

    中美两国大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦9日电　中美两国大使级会谈2月9日举行第36次会议。会议在14时55分休会，下次会议将在2月18日举行。
