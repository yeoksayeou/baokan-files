		Joyful Spring Festival
		On Spring Festival day, returned overseas Chinese youth and youth from the capital held a Spring Festival get-together at the Soviet Exhibition Center. At the gathering, the Central National Song and Dance Ensemble performed choral pieces. 本报 Wang Zhiyuan shot

		During each Spring Festival, the Liulichang area of Qianmen in Beijing is the liveliest place, where people can buy all kinds of folk-style children's toys. Xinhua News Agency reporter Yu Huiru shot

		On the evening of February 11th, the Beijing Workers' Club held a Spring Festival party for workers, peasants, the People's Liberation Army, and intellectuals. Workers dressed as the God of Longevity and Spring Maiden met with scientist Hua Luogeng and playwright Ouyang Yuqian, who were invited to participate in the party. 本报 reporter Gao Liang shot

		The amateur Beijing Opera troupe of the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant performed for farmers in Gucheng Township, Shijingshan District, Beijing. Before the performance, the actors met with the head and actors of the Gucheng Township rural drama troupe. 本报 reporter Cang Shi shot

		A scene from "Red Phoenix Happiness" performed by the amateur Beijing Opera troupe of the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant in Gucheng Township, Shijingshan District, Beijing. 本报 reporter Gao Liang shot

### 欢乐的春节（图片）

1956-02-14
第5版()
专栏：

    欢乐的春节
    春节这一天，归国华侨青年同首都青年在苏联展览馆举行春节联欢会。会上，中央民族歌舞团演出合唱节目。　				本报　王志渊摄
    每到春节，北京市前门区的厂甸是最热闹的地方，人们在这里可以买到各种富有民间特色的儿童玩具。				新华社记者        喻惠如摄
    2月11日晚，北京市工人俱乐部举办了工人、农民、解放军和知识分子的联欢会。工人化装的寿星和春姑娘等同应邀参加联欢会的科学家华罗庚、戏剧家欧阳予倩在联欢会上会面。  				本报记者　高　粮摄
    石景山钢铁厂职工业余京剧团到北京市石景山区古城乡为农民们演出。演出前，演员们同古城乡农村剧团的团长和演员们会面。    				本报记者　苍　石摄
    石景山钢铁厂职工业余京剧团在北京市石景山区古城乡演出“红鸾禧”的一个场面。				本报记者　高　粮摄
