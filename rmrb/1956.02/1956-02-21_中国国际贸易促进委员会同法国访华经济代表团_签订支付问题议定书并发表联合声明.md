Title: China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and French Economic Delegation to China Sign Payment Agreement and Issue Joint Statement
Author:
Date: 1956-02-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th dispatch. After friendly consultations, the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and the French Economic Delegation to China signed the Protocol on Payment Issues and the Protocol on Payment Methods in Beijing on the 19th, and issued a joint statement. Lu Xuzhang signed on behalf of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and Henri Rochereau, the head of the French Economic Delegation to China, signed on behalf of the French side.

The joint statement said: The China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and the French Economic Delegation to China conducted discussions on the development of trade between China and France, and both sides expressed their pleasure at reaching an agreement on payment issues. After friendly and full exchange of views, both sides believe that there is great potential for the development of trade between China and France, and are willing to work to expand Sino-French trade and promote the normalization of Sino-French trade relations. Both sides are satisfied that a considerable number of contracts can be concluded in such a short period of time. Both sides agreed that the terms of payment, delivery, and other conditions related to trade between China and France should be handled on the principle of equality and mutual benefit.

Both sides believe that under this spirit and the agreed measures, trade between China and France will be further developed, the ties between the peoples of China and France will be closer, and it will bring new contributions to the common ideals of world peace and prosperity.

### 中国国际贸易促进委员会同法国访华经济代表团  签订支付问题议定书并发表联合声明

1956-02-21
第1版()
专栏：

    中国国际贸易促进委员会同法国访华经济代表团
    签订支付问题议定书并发表联合声明
    新华社20日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会同法国访华经济代表团经过融洽的商谈后，19日在北京签订了关于支付问题的议定书和关于支付方式的议定书，并且发表了联合声明。代表中国国际贸易促进委员会签字的是卢绪章，代表法国访华经济代表团签字的是团长亨利·罗谢罗。
    联合声明说：中国国际贸易促进委员会和法国访华经济代表团，就发展中法两国间的贸易进行了讨论，并且对支付问题已达成协议，对此，双方都表示高兴。双方经过融洽的充分的交换意见，认为中法两国之间的贸易，有很大的发展可能，并且愿为扩大中法贸易和促进中法贸易关系的正常化而努力。双方对于此次在短期内即能缔订相当数量的合同感到满意。双方同意中法两国间的贸易中有关支付、交货等条件，应以平等互利的原则来处理。
    双方认为在这样的精神和已达成协议的措施下，将使中法两国间的贸易获得进一步的发展，并且将促使中法两国人民的联系愈加密切，也将对世界和平和繁荣的共同理想带来了新的贡献。
