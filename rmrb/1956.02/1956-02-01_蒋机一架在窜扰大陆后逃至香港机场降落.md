Title: A Chiang Kai-shek Plane Flees to Hong Kong Airport After Intrusion into the Mainland
Author:
Date: 1956-02-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 31st: On January 31st at 11:21 AM, a Chiang Kai-shek F-86 fighter plane intruded into the mainland airspace from an area south of Xiamen and north of Dongshan Island, harassing Yunxiao in Fujian Province and Raoping and Wuhua in Guangdong Province. The Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force immediately scrambled to intercept. Under pursuit by the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force, this Chiang Kai-shek plane fled southwest via Zijin and Huiyang, and at 12:20 PM, landed at Kai Tak Airport in Hong Kong.

Our Ministry of Foreign Affairs immediately notified the British Chargé d'affaires of this incident on the afternoon of the 31st and requested that he immediately inform the British authorities in Hong Kong to detain the Chiang Kai-shek plane and the personnel on board.

### 蒋机一架在窜扰大陆后逃至香港机场降落

1956-02-01
第1版()
专栏：

    蒋机一架在窜扰大陆后逃至香港机场降落
    新华社31日讯　1月31日上午11时21分，蒋军F——86型战斗机一架自厦门以南、东山岛以北处窜入大陆上空，骚扰福建省的云霄和广东省的饶平、五华等地区。中国人民解放军空军飞机当即起飞截击。这架蒋军飞机在中国人民解放军空军飞机追击下，经紫金、惠阳向西南方向逃窜，在12时20分，降落到香港启德机场。
    我外交部在31日下午立即将这一事件通知英国代办，并且要求他立即告诉香港英国当局将这架蒋机和机上人员予以扣留。
