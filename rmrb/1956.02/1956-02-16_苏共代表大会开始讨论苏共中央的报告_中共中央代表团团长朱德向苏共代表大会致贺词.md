Title: CPSU Congress Begins Discussion of the CPSU Central Committee Report; Zhu De, Head of the CPC Central Committee Delegation, Delivers Congratulatory Message to the CPSU Congress
Author:
Date: 1956-02-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 15th: The 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union continued on February 15th.

    At the morning session, Comrade P.G. Moskatov, Chairman of the Inspection Commission of the CPSU Central Committee, delivered the work report of the Inspection Commission of the CPSU Central Committee. Then, discussions began on the work report of the CPSU Central Committee and the work report of the Inspection Commission. Representatives from Moscow, Ukraine, Leningrad, Belarus, Latvia, Rostov, and Uzbekistan spoke successively. They unanimously supported the work report of the CPSU Central Committee, explained the achievements made by the people of their regions under the leadership of the Party Central Committee, and examined shortcomings in the spirit of criticism and self-criticism.

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 15th: At the afternoon session of the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union on February 15th, Zhu De, head of the CPC Central Committee delegation, delivered a speech to the Congress. When Comrade Zhu De stepped onto the podium, the entire audience stood up and erupted in thunderous applause. Zhu De, on behalf of the 9 million members of the Chinese Communist Party and the 600 million Chinese people, paid tribute to the CPSU Congress. At this moment, the whole place resounded with stormy applause. Finally, when Zhu De read the congratulatory message from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, signed by Comrade Mao Zedong, it again aroused prolonged applause from all the representatives. Every paragraph, even every sentence, of the congratulatory message from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China received warm applause, fully reflecting the profound fraternal friendship between the Communist Parties of China and the Soviet Union and the people of the two countries.

### 苏共代表大会开始讨论苏共中央的报告  中共中央代表团团长朱德向苏共代表大会致贺词

1956-02-16
第4版()
专栏：

    苏共代表大会开始讨论苏共中央的报告
    中共中央代表团团长朱德向苏共代表大会致贺词
    本报莫斯科15日专电　苏联共产党第二十次代表大会2月15日继续开会。
    在上午会议上，苏共中央检查委员会主席波·格·莫斯卡托夫同志作了苏共中央检查委员会的工作报告。接着开始进行关于苏共中央工作报告和检查委员会工作报告的讨论。莫斯科、乌克兰、列宁格勒、白俄罗斯、拉脱维亚、罗斯托夫、乌兹别克斯坦的代表先后发言。他们一致拥护苏共中央的工作报告，说明自己地区的人民在党中央领导下所取得的成就，并且以批评和自我批评的精神检讨了缺点。
    本报莫斯科15日专电　在苏联共产党第二十次代表大会15日下午会议上，中共中央代表团团长朱德向大会致词。当朱德同志走上讲台时，全场起立，发出雷动的掌声。朱德代表900万中国共产党党员和6亿中国人民向苏共代表大会致敬。这时，全场响起了暴风雨般的掌声。最后，当朱德宣读由毛泽东同志签字的中国共产党中央委员会的贺词的时候，又引起了全场的代表们的经久不息的掌声。中国共产党中央委员会的贺词，每一段甚至每一句，都获得了热烈的掌声，充分反映了中苏两国共产党和两国人民的兄弟般的深厚友谊。
