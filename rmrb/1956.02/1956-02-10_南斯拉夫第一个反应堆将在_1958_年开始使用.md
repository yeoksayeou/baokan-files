Title: Yugoslavia's First Reactor to Begin Operation in 1958
Author:
Date: 1956-02-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, 9th - According to the Yugoslav News Agency, Yugoslavia's first nuclear energy reactor is expected to begin operation around the end of 1958. This was stated by Savic, Vice-Chairman of the Yugoslav Federal Nuclear Energy Commission, to a reporter from Belgrade Radio on February 7th.

Savic said that the reactor, with a power of 6,500 to 10,000 kilowatts, will be built in cooperation between Yugoslavia and the Soviet Union, and will use Uranium-235 as fuel. This reactor will not only enable Yugoslavia to widely use isotopes in various fields, but will also promote Yugoslavia's scientific research and technological development in the field of nuclear energy.

### 南斯拉夫第一个反应堆将在1958年开始使用

1956-02-10
第4版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫第一个反应堆将在1958年开始使用
    新华社9日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社贝尔格莱德讯：南斯拉夫第一个核子能反应堆大约在1958年底就可以开始使用。这是南斯拉夫联邦核子能委员会副主席萨维奇2月7日对贝尔格莱德广播电台的记者说的。
    萨维奇说，这个反应堆的功率为6，500到1万瓩，将由南斯拉夫和苏联合作建造，它使用的燃料是铀235。这个反应堆不仅将使南斯拉夫能够在各方面广泛地使用同位素，而且还将促进南斯拉夫在核子能方面的科学研究工作和技术的发展。
