Title: Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks Continue

Author: 
Date: 1956-02-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, February 25th. The Sino-US ambassadorial talks held their 38th meeting at the Palais des Nations at 10:00 a.m. on February 24th. The meeting adjourned at 2:30 p.m. The next meeting will be held on March 1st.

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1956-02-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦25日电　中美两国大使级会谈2月24日上午10时在国联大厦举行第38次会议。会议在14时30分休会。下次会议将在3月1日举行。
