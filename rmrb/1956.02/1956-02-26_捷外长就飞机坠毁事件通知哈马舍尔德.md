Title: Czechoslovak Foreign Minister Notifies Hammarskjöld Regarding Plane Crash

Author:

Date: 1956-02-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, February 25th: The Czechoslovak News Agency reports that the Foreign Minister of the Czechoslovak Republic, Mr. David, telegraphed the Secretary-General of the United Nations, Mr. Hammarskjöld, on February 24th, stating that recently a Czechoslovakian aircraft collided with an American balloon illegally released from a West German base into Czechoslovakia, resulting in a tragic accident.

David said, "This plane crash, which resulted in the death of 22 people and serious injuries to 4 passengers, is an extremely serious demonstration of the fact that the broadcasting of these balloons poses a significant danger to air transportation and violates the 1944 Chicago International Civil Aviation Convention, which forms the basis of the International Civil Aviation Organization, a specialized agency of the United Nations."

David requested that the Secretary-General of the United Nations distribute the text of this telegram to all member states of the United Nations.

### 捷外长就飞机坠毁事件通知哈马舍尔德

1956-02-26
第4版()
专栏：

    捷外长就飞机坠毁事件通知哈马舍尔德
    新华社布拉格25日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道：捷克斯洛伐克共和国外交部长戴维在2月24日打电报通知联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德说：最近捷克斯洛伐克一架飞机同从西德基地非法放送到捷克斯洛伐克的美国气球相撞，发生了不幸的事故。
    戴维说，“这次飞机坠毁事件造成了22人死亡和4名乘客受重伤，它极其严重地证明了这一事实：放送上述气球对空中运输造成了巨大的危险，并且是违反构成联合国特别机构国际民用航空组织基础的1944年芝加哥国际民用航空公约的。”
    戴维要求联合国秘书长把这份电报的文本分发给联合国各会员国。
