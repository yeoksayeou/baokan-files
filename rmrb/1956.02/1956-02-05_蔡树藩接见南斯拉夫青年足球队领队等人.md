Title: Cai Shufan Receives Leader of Yugoslav Youth Football Team and Others
Author:
Date: 1956-02-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 4th -- Cai Shufan, Deputy Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, received Bor. Pesić, the leader of the Yugoslav Youth Football Team and Vice President of the Yugoslav Football Association, team captain Ja. Baštrović, and Vo. Kravić, on the afternoon of the 4th.

    During the meeting, both sides had a cordial two-and-a-half-hour conversation on issues such as participating in international sports activities and strengthening exchanges between football teams and other sports teams of the two countries. Cai Shufan also introduced the development of sports in China over the past few years to Bor. Pesić and others.

    According to this newspaper, the Yugoslav Youth Football Team will play a friendly match with the Beijing Football Team on the 5th. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the live match from 3 pm on the 5th.

### 蔡树藩接见南斯拉夫青年足球队领队等人

1956-02-05
第1版()
专栏：

    蔡树藩接见南斯拉夫青年足球队领队等人
    新华社4日讯　中华人民共和国体育运动委员会副主任蔡树藩4日下午接见了南斯拉夫青年足球队领队、南斯拉夫足球联合会副主席勃·彼希契，队长亚·巴什特罗维契和伏·克拉维契。
    接见的时候，双方就参加国际体育活动和加强两国足球队以及其他运动队之间的往来等问题，亲切地作了两个半小时的交谈。蔡树藩还向勃·彼希契等人介绍了几年来中国体育运动的发展情况。
    本报讯　南斯拉夫青年足球队5日将和北京足球队进行友谊比赛。中央人民广播电台从5日下午3时起广播比赛实况。
