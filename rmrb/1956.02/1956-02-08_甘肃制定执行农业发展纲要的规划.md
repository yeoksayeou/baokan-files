Title: Gansu Formulates a Plan for Implementing the Agricultural Development Program
Author:
Date: 1956-02-08
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency

    Gansu Formulates a Plan for Implementing the Agricultural Development Program
    The Gansu Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China recently held its Seventh Plenary (Enlarged) Session, attended by secretaries of local committees and some county committee secretaries. Based on the National Agricultural Development Program (Draft) for 1956-1967 proposed by the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, this meeting formulated specific plans for Gansu Province to implement the program.
    According to this plan, Gansu Province will complete the advanced form of agricultural collectivization by the end of 1957. The plan requires that the targets for Gansu Province's agricultural crops stipulated in the state's First Five-Year Plan be fulfilled one year ahead of schedule, this year. The total grain output should reach 10.35 billion jin, an increase of 2.5 billion jin compared to 1955, exceeding the state's 1957 target by 24%; the total cotton output should reach 252,000 dan of ginned cotton, an increase of 152,200 dan compared to 1955, exceeding the state's 1957 target by more than double.
    This plan calls for increasing the area of irrigated land in the province by 40 million mu within 12 years. Together with the existing 9.64 million mu, the average area of irrigated land per agricultural person will be 2.7 mu, fundamentally changing the arid conditions.
    This plan also requires planting and cultivating forests on more than 150 million mu within 12 years, basically afforesting all barren mountains and wastelands in the province. The plan requires that within 12 years, measures such as building terraced fields, constructing flood control dams, and planting grass and trees be taken in the Loess Plateau region to control soil erosion over an area of 240,000 square kilometers.
    This plan also puts forward specific requirements and measures for eliminating diseases, animal plagues, pests, harmful birds, and harmful animals, as well as developing science, culture, education, health, transportation, posts and telecommunications, and land reclamation and immigration.
    The meeting decided to distribute this plan to various localities for extensive discussion, and requires that specific plans be formulated at each level. Currently, it is first required to launch a vigorous agricultural production increase movement throughout the province.
                                        (Xinhua News Agency)

### 甘肃制定执行农业发展纲要的规划

1956-02-08
第2版()
专栏：

    甘肃制定执行农业发展纲要的规划
    中共甘肃省委员会最近举行了有各地委书记和部分县委书记参加的第七次全体会议（扩大）。这个会议根据中共中央政治局提出的1956年到1957年全国农业发展纲要（草案）制定了甘肃省贯彻执行的具体规划。
    根据这个规划，甘肃省要在1957年底完成高级形式的农业合作化。这个规划要求提前一年即在今年完成国家第一个五年计划规定甘肃省农作物的指标。其中粮食总产量要达到103.5亿斤，比1955年增产25亿斤，超过1957年国家指标24％；棉花总产量要达到252，000担皮棉，比1955年增产152，200担，超过1957年国家指标一倍以上。
    这个规划要求在12年内全省增加水浇地4，000万亩，连现有的964万亩，全省农业人口每人平均有水浇地二亩七分，即基本改变干旱面貌。
    这个规划还要求在12年内造林、育林15，000多万亩，基本上绿化全省光山荒山。规划要求黄土高原区在12年内采取修梯田、修拦洪坝和种草、植树等办法控制水土流失面积24万平方公里。
    这个规划还对消灭疾病、兽疫、害虫、害鸟、害兽和发展科学文化、教育卫生、交通邮电以及开荒移民等方面，提出了具体的要求和措施。
    会议决定将这个规划发给各地展开广泛的讨论，并且要求逐级定出具体规划；目前首先要求在全省范围内掀起一个热火朝天的农业增产运动。
                    （新华社）
