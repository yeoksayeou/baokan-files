Title: Democratic League Central Committee Elects Chairman, Vice-Chairmen and Standing Committee Members
Author:
Date: 1956-02-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The Second Central Committee of the China Democratic League held its first plenary session on the 21st. The meeting elected Shen Junru as Chairman of the Central Committee; Zhang Bojun, Luo Longji, Ma Xulun, Shi Liang (female), and Gao Chongmin as Vice-Chairmen; Qiu Zhe, Ye Duyi, Liu Qingyang (female), Wu Han, Li Wenyi (female), Li Xiangfu, Shen Zhiyuan, Xin Zhichao, Zhou Xinmin, Zhou Jingwen, Chen Wangdao, Hu Yuzhi, Xu Shouxuan, Ma Zhemin, Xu Jie, Xu Guangping (female), Guo Zechen, Guo Qiaoran, Peng Zemin, Zeng Zhaolun, Hua Luogeng, Fei Xiaotong, Min Ganghou, Huang Yanpei, Huang Yaomian, Yang Mingxuan, Chu Tunan, Pan Dakui, Pan Guangdan, Deng Chumin, Qian Duansheng, Sa Kongliao, and Han Zhao'e as members of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee.

### 民主同盟中央委员会选出主席、副主席和常委

1956-02-23
第1版()
专栏：

    民主同盟中央委员会选出主席、副主席和常委
    据新华社讯　中国民主同盟第二届中央委员会21日举行第一次全体会议。会议选出沈钧儒为中央委员会主席；章伯钧、罗隆基、马叙伦、史良（女）、高崇民为副主席；丘哲、叶笃义、刘清扬（女）、吴晗、李文宜（女）、李相符、沈志远、辛志超、周新民、周鲸文、陈望道、胡愈之、徐寿轩、马哲民、许杰、许广平（女）、郭则沉、郭翘然、彭泽民、曾昭抡、华罗庚、费孝通、闵刚侯、黄炎培、黄药眠、杨明轩、楚图南、潘大逵、潘光旦、邓初民、钱端升、萨空了、韩兆鹗为中央常务委员会委员。
