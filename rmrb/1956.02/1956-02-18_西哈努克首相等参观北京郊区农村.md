Title: Sihanouk and Prime Minister Visit Rural Areas in the Suburbs of Beijing

Author:

Date: 1956-02-18
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, February 17th. Cambodian Prime Minister Prince Norodom Sihanouk and members of the Cambodian Royal Delegation visited cooperative rural areas in the suburbs of Beijing and the state-owned Beijing South Suburb Farm on the morning of the 17th. When Prince Sihanouk visited the Chenguang Agricultural Production Cooperative in Luquan Township, Nanyuan District, and heard the introduction by Deputy Director Liu Baozhong, he said, "Your country has achieved great prosperity, and your system has brought good results. I wish your country eternal prosperity."

The distinguished guests first visited the Chenguang Agricultural Production Cooperative, the Luquan Township Primary School, the supply and marketing cooperative, and the maternal and child health station in Luquan Township, Nanyuan District. Liu Baozhong, the deputy director of this cooperative, told the distinguished guests that after liberation, the farmers of Luquan Township, through land reform and taking the road of mutual aid and cooperation, have seen their lives improve day by day. In the past few years, they have built more than 700 new houses. Now, more than 98% of the farmers in the township have joined the Chenguang Agricultural Production Cooperative.

At 11 a.m., Prince Sihanouk and his entourage arrived at the state-owned Beijing South Suburb Farm for a visit. The distinguished guests visited the farm's various agricultural machinery and livestock farms, and finally, they viewed the farm's land by car.

### 西哈努克首相等参观北京郊区农村

1956-02-18
第2版()
专栏：

    西哈努克首相等参观北京郊区农村
    据新华社17日讯　柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和柬埔寨王国国家代表团人员，17日上午访问了合作化的北京郊区农村和国营北京市南郊农场。西哈努克亲王在参观南苑区鹿圈乡的晨光农业生产合作社时，听了副主任刘宝忠的介绍以后说，你们的国家得到了极大繁荣，你们的制度带来了良好的结果，祝你们国家永远繁荣。
    贵宾们首先参观了南苑区鹿圈乡的晨光农业生产合作社，鹿圈乡的小学、供销合作社和妇幼保健站。这个合作社的副主任刘宝忠告诉贵宾们，鹿圈乡的农民在解放后经过土地改革和走互助合作的道路，生活一天比一天改善，几年来就一共盖了700多间新房。现在，全乡有98％以上的农户都已经参加了晨光农业生产合作社。
    上午11时，西哈努克亲王等一行来到国营北京市南郊农场参观。贵宾们参观了农场的各种农业机械和牧畜场，最后乘汽车观看了农场的土地。
