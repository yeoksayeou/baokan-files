Title: Liu Ningyi and Others Invited to Attend the Italian General Confederation of Labour Congress
Author:
Date: 1956-02-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Liu Ningyi, head of the delegation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and vice chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, was invited to attend the Fourth Congress of the Italian General Confederation of Labour, left Beijing by plane for Rome on the 16th. Delegation member Liu Changsheng is already abroad.
    After attending this meeting, the delegation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions will also participate in the 29th Executive Bureau meeting of the World Federation of Trade Unions held in Rome.

### 刘宁一等应邀出席意大利总工会代表大会

1956-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

    刘宁一等应邀出席意大利总工会代表大会
    新华社16日讯　应邀出席意大利总工会第四次代表大会的中华人民共和国工会代表团团长、中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一，16日乘飞机离北京前往罗马。代表团团员刘长胜已经在国外。
    中华人民共和国工会代表团出席这次会议以后，还要参加在罗马召开的世界工会联合会第二十九次执行局会议。
