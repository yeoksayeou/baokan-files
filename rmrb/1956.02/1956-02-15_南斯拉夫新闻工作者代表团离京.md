Title: Yugoslav Journalists Delegation Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-02-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 14th: The Yugoslav journalists delegation, led by Pirpić, departed Beijing in batches by plane on the 13th and 14th, after a nearly 50-day visit to China. Delegation leader Pirpić and two members left Beijing by plane via Guangzhou for a visit to Burma, while the remaining members departed Beijing by plane to return to their country.

Those who saw them off at the airport included: Deng Tuo, President of the All-China Journalists Association; Vice Presidents Wu Lengxi, Mei Yi, and Wang Yunsheng; Xu Huang, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; responsible individuals from Beijing newspapers and periodicals; representatives of the Chinese journalists delegation that had visited Yugoslavia; and officials from the Yugoslav Embassy in China.

### 南斯拉夫新闻工作者代表团离京

1956-02-15
第4版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫新闻工作者代表团离京
    新华社14日讯　以皮尔皮奇为首的南斯拉夫新闻工作者访华代表团在中国作了将近50天的参观访问以后，13日和14日分批乘飞机离开北京。代表团团长皮尔皮奇和团员两人从北京乘飞机经广州前往缅甸访问，其他团员从北京乘飞机启程回国。
    到机场欢送的有：中国新闻工作者联谊会会长邓拓，副会长吴冷西、梅益、王芸生，外交部新闻司副司长徐晃，北京各报刊负责人和曾经访问南斯拉夫的中国新闻工作者代表团人员，以及南斯拉夫驻华大使馆官员。
