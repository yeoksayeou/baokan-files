Title: President Pieck Approves the Sino-German Treaty of Friendship and Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1956-02-06
Page: 4
　
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, February 4th: According to a report by the German News Agency, the President of the German Democratic Republic, Pieck, has approved the Sino-German Treaty of Friendship and Cooperation.

### 皮克总统批准中德友好合作条约

1956-02-06
第4版()
专栏：

    皮克总统批准中德友好合作条约
    新华社柏林4日电　据德意志通讯社报道，德意志民主共和国总统皮克批准了中德友好合作条约。
