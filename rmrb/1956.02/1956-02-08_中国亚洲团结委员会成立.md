Title: China Asian Solidarity Committee Established
Author:
Date: 1956-02-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The China Asian Solidarity Committee was formally established in Beijing today. This organization, aimed at strengthening friendly cooperation and solidarity between China and Asian countries, was established in accordance with the resolution of the Asian Countries Conference held in New Delhi, India, from April 6th to 10th, 1955.

The inaugural meeting was held at 6:30 PM. Premier Zhou Enlai, along with Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Peng Zhen, Dong Biwu, Chen Yi, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, Zhang Bojun, Chen Jiageng, Bao Erhan, and others, attended the meeting.

More than 1,000 people from all walks of life from the capital and across the country participated in the inaugural meeting. They included: members of the Chinese Preparatory Committee for the Asian Countries Conference, representatives from various democratic parties and people's organizations, workers, farmers, scientists, engineers, professors, scholars, doctors, writers, playwrights, artists, musicians, poets, Catholics and Christians, journalists, industrialists and businessmen, representatives from various ethnic minorities, religious circles, and overseas Chinese, as well as people from all sectors of society from provinces, cities, and autonomous regions across the country.

The meeting was presided over by Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese Preparatory Committee for the Asian Countries Conference, who also delivered the opening remarks.

Liu Ningyi, a member of the Chinese Preparatory Committee for the Asian Countries Conference, gave a report on the establishment of the "China Asian Solidarity Committee." All participants at the inaugural meeting unanimously raised their hands to approve the proposal to establish the China Asian Solidarity Committee.

Finally, the meeting unanimously elected the Chairman, Vice-Chairmen, Secretary-General, Deputy Secretary-Generals, and members of the China Asian Solidarity Committee. Guo Moruo was elected as Chairman, Mao Dun, Li Dequan, Liao Chengzhi, Liu Ningyi, and Liu Geping were elected as Vice-Chairmen, Liu Ningyi (concurrently) was elected as Secretary-General, and Chen Hansheng and Zheng Senyu were elected as Deputy Secretary-Generals.

When Guo Moruo announced the formal establishment of the China Asian Solidarity Committee after the election, the audience erupted in thunderous applause. Guo Moruo said that defending world peace is the glorious duty of every person in the world. We must respond to Chairman Mao's call, unite with the people of all Asian countries, unite with the people of all countries in the world, and strive to defend Asian peace and world peace.

### 中国亚洲团结委员会成立

1956-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

    中国亚洲团结委员会成立
    新华社7日讯　中国亚洲团结委员会今天在北京正式成立。这个加强中国同亚洲国家间的友好合作和团结的组织，是根据1955年4月6日至10日在印度新德里举行的亚洲国家会议的决议而建立的。
    成立大会在下午6时30分举行。周恩来总理和沈钧儒、郭沫若、彭真、董必武、陈毅、李维汉、陈叔通、章伯钧、陈嘉庚、鲍尔汉等都出席了大会。
    参加成立大会的，有来自首都和全国各地的各界人士1，000多人，他们是：亚洲国家会议中国筹备委员会的委员，全国各民主党派、各人民团体的人士，工人，农民，科学家，工程师，教授，学者，医生，作家，戏剧家，美术家，音乐家，诗人，天主教徒和基督教徒，新闻工作者，工商业者，各兄弟民族、宗教界和华侨方面人士以及来自全国各省、市、自治区的社会各界人士等。
    大会由亚洲国家会议中国筹备委员会主席郭沫若主持并致开会词。
    亚洲国家会议中国筹备委员会委员刘宁一作了关于成立“中国亚洲团结委员会”的报告。参加成立大会的全体人员，一致举手通过成立中国亚洲团结委员会的建议。
    会议最后一致选出了中国亚洲团结委员会主席、副主席、秘书长、副秘书长和委员。主席是郭沫若，副主席是茅盾、李德全、廖承志、刘宁一、刘格平，秘书长是刘宁一（兼），副秘书长是陈翰笙、郑森禹。
    当郭沫若在选举以后宣布中国亚洲团结委员会正式成立的时候，全场响起经久不息的掌声。郭沫若说，保卫世界和平是全世界每一个人的光荣职责。我们要响应毛主席的号召，同亚洲各国人民团结一致，同全世界各国人民团结一致，为保卫亚洲和平和保卫世界和平而奋斗。
