Title: Sihanouk Says Cambodia Will Adhere to Neutrality
Author:
Date: 1956-02-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 7th: According to a Reuters dispatch from Phnom Penh, Prince Sihanouk of Cambodia said on February 6th: "We must adhere to neutrality and not participate in any bloc. The first thing we must do is redouble our efforts to irrigate our rice paddies. Let us rely on ourselves and not depend on foreign countries."

Sihanouk said this upon returning to Phnom Penh after visiting the Philippines. He said that in Manila he "was invited to join the Manila Treaty on the grounds that Cambodia is a small country." Sihanouk said that his reply to this invitation was: "No."

### 西哈努克说  柬埔寨要遵守中立

1956-02-08
第4版()
专栏：

    西哈努克说
    柬埔寨要遵守中立
    新华社河内7日电　据路透社金边消息，柬埔寨的西哈努克亲王2月6日说：“我们一定要遵守中立，不参加任何集团。我们要做的事情首先是加倍努力，来灌溉我们的稻田。让我们依靠我们自己，不要依赖外国。”
    西哈努克是在访问了菲律宾以后回到金边的时候这样说的。他说，他在马尼拉时“被邀请参加马尼拉条约，理由是：柬埔寨是一个小国。”西哈努克说，他对这种邀请的答复是：“不行”。
