Title: Voroshilov Cables Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, and Zhou Enlai to Thank Them for Their Congratulations on His 75th Birthday
Author:
Date: 1956-02-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 12th. Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, sent a cable to Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai, thanking them for their congratulations on his birthday. The cable is as follows:

To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

To Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China:

To Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:

Dear Comrades,

I extend my heartfelt gratitude to you for your warm congratulations and good wishes on my 75th birthday, and through you, to the government of the People's Republic of China and the great fraternal Chinese people.

					K. Voroshilov
					Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics
					February 11, 1956, Moscow Kremlin

### 伏罗希洛夫致电毛泽东刘少奇周恩来  感谢对他75岁寿辰的祝贺

1956-02-13
第1版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫致电毛泽东刘少奇周恩来
    感谢对他75岁寿辰的祝贺
    新华社12日讯　苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫打电报给毛泽东主席、刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理，感谢他们在他的生日的时候对他的祝贺。电文如下：北京中华人民共和国主席毛泽东同志：中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇同志：中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志：亲爱的同志们：
    当我75岁生日的时候，承致热烈的祝贺和良好的愿望，谨向你们，并且通过你们向中华人民共和国政府和伟大的兄弟般的中国人民表示衷心的感谢。	苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟	最高苏维埃主席团主席  克·伏罗希洛夫	1956年2月11日于莫斯科克里姆林宫
