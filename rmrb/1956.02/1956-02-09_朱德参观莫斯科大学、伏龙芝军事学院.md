Title: Zhu De Visits Moscow University and Frunze Military Academy
Author:
Date: 1956-02-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 8th: Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, accompanied by Nie Rongzhen, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council of the People's Republic of China, Liu Lantao, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Liu Xiao, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, and other accompanying personnel, visited the Soviet State "Lomonosov" Moscow University on Lenin Hills on February 7th.

　　At about 4 p.m., Vice Chairman Zhu De and others arrived at Moscow University by car.

　　Soviet Minister of Higher Education Yelyutin, Deputy Minister Prokofiev, Moscow University Rector Petrovsky, Vice Rector Vovchenko, faculty and staff of Moscow University, Soviet students, and hundreds of Chinese students stood in the entrance corridor to welcome them.

　　Vice Chairman Zhu De and others first went to the Rector's office. Rector Petrovsky introduced the situation of Moscow University. He said that it was an honor for the school to have Vice Chairman Zhu De visit. Moscow University regards Chinese culture, which has a long history, with respect. Moscow University and Peking University have established close ties. In this room are also displayed the large vase and embroidery and other gifts sent by Peking University on the 200th anniversary of Moscow University. Petrovsky praised the academic achievements of Chinese students.

　　Vice Chairman Zhu De expressed his gratitude to Moscow University for training personnel for China's socialist construction.

　　Subsequently, Rector Petrovsky presented the 200th anniversary commemorative medal of Moscow University, the history of the school, and a picture album of the school to the guests.

　　After leaving the Rector's office, Vice Chairman Zhu De visited the museum, laboratories, classrooms, and indoor swimming pool of the Department of Geology, and also visited the well-equipped student dormitories. Vice Chairman Zhu De also made a special visit to the rooms where Chinese students lived.

　　Afterwards, a grand welcoming meeting was held in the auditorium. More than 1,000 Soviet students and Chinese students attended the welcoming meeting. When Vice Chairman Zhu De entered the auditorium, the attendees applauded warmly and presented flowers to Vice Chairman Zhu.

　　At the meeting, student representative Dorofeeva and Chinese student Zhang Dake delivered welcome speeches.

　　Vice Chairman Zhu De made a short speech. He said that it was very happy to study in this school, and wished them to quickly master the new science and technology of the new era. He encouraged Chinese students to achieve the "three goods" proposed by Chairman Mao, to concentrate on their studies, and to engage in the construction of a socialist society in the future.

　　Vice Chairman Zhu De visited Moscow University for nearly two hours, and left an inscription in the commemorative book when he left the school.

　　On the same evening, Vice Chairman Zhu De and his entourage went to the Soviet Grand Theater to watch Tchaikovsky's opera "The Queen of Spades." Begov, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Fedelin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Gudev, Director of the Far East Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, and Fomin, Chief of Protocol of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, accompanied them to watch the opera.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 8th: Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, accompanied by Nie Rongzhen, Liu Lantao, and other accompanying personnel, visited the Frunze Military Academy on the morning of February 8th, and was warmly welcomed by the faculty and students of the academy.

　　In the Dean's office, General Kurochkin, the President of the Military Academy, introduced the history of the academy and the students' learning situation to Vice Chairman Zhu De.

　　Then, Vice Chairman Zhu De and others made a two-hour visit. Vice Chairman Zhu De and others visited classrooms, student study rooms, teaching assignments, and various teaching equipment, and carefully viewed the Soviet Patriotic War situation map in the classroom, and listened to explanations about the various battles of the Patriotic War.

　　Accompanying Vice Chairman Zhu De on the visit today were Marshal Sokolovsky, First Deputy Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union, Fomin, Chief of Protocol of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Han Zhenji, Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, and other personnel.

### 朱德参观莫斯科大学、伏龙芝军事学院

1956-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德参观莫斯科大学、伏龙芝军事学院
    新华社莫斯科8日电　中华人民共和国副主席朱德，2月7日偕同中华人民共和国国防委员会副主席聂荣臻元帅、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员刘澜涛、中国驻苏联大使刘晓及其他随行人员参观了列宁山的苏联国立“洛蒙诺索夫”莫斯科大学。
    下午4时许，朱德副主席等乘车到达莫斯科大学。
    苏联高等教育部长叶留金、副部长普罗科菲耶夫，莫斯科大学校长彼得罗夫斯基、副校长伏夫钦科、莫斯科大学教职员、苏联学生和中国留学生数百人站在大门入口走廊里欢迎。
    朱德副主席等首先到校长室。彼得罗夫斯基校长介绍了莫斯科大学的情况。他说：朱德副主席的来访是学校的荣幸。莫斯科大学以尊敬的心情对待有着悠久历史的中国文化。莫斯科大学和北京大学建立了密切的联系。在这个房间里还陈列着北京大学在莫斯科大学200周年时送来的大花瓶和刺绣等礼品。彼得罗夫斯基盛赞中国留学生的学习成绩。
    朱德副主席对莫斯科大学为中国培养建设社会主义的人材表示感谢。
    随后，彼得罗夫斯基校长把莫斯科大学200周年纪念章、学校历史、学校图片册等礼品赠送给来宾。
    从校长室出来以后，朱德副主席参观了地质系的博物馆、实验室、课堂、室内游泳池等地方，并且参观了设备齐全的学生宿舍。朱德副主席还特地到中国学生住的房间里参观。
    随后，在大礼堂内举行了盛大的欢迎会。参加欢迎会的有苏联学生和中国留学生1，000多人。当朱德副主席步入大礼堂时，到会者热烈鼓掌，并且向朱副主席献花。
    会上，学生代表多罗菲耶娃和中国留学生张大可致欢迎词。
    朱德副主席作了简短讲话。他说：在这个学校里学习是很幸福的，愿你们迅速地掌握着新时代的新科学和新技术。他勉励中国留学生作到毛主席提出的“三好”，专心学习，将来从事建设社会主义社会。
    朱德副主席在莫斯科大学共参观了近两小时，离开学校的时候，还在纪念册上题词留念。
    同日晚间，朱德副主席和随行人员到苏联大剧院观看了柴可夫斯基的歌剧“黑桃皇后”。陪同前往观剧的有：苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书别哥夫、苏联外交部副部长费德林、苏联外交部远东司司长顾德夫和苏联最高苏维埃主席团礼宾长弗明。
    新华社莫斯科8日电　中华人民共和国副主席朱德，2月8日上午偕同聂荣臻、刘澜涛和其他随行人员参观了伏龙芝军事学院，受到这个学院教官和学员的热烈欢迎。
    在院长室里，军事学院院长库罗奇金上将给朱德副主席介绍了学院的历史和学员们的学习情况。
    然后，朱德副主席等作了两小时的参观。朱德副主席等参观了教室、学员作业室、教学作业和各种教学设备，并且详细观看了教室中的苏联卫国战争形势图，听了有关卫国战争各次战役的解释。
    今天陪同朱德副主席前往参观的有：苏联国防部第一副部长索科洛夫斯基元帅、苏联最高苏维埃主席团礼宾长弗明、中国驻苏联大使馆武官韩振纪和其他人员。
