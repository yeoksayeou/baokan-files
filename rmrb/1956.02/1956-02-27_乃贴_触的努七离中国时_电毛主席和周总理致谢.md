Title: Nai Thi Thuch's Departure from China: Telegrams of Thanks to Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou
Author:
Date: 1956-02-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 26th — Nai Thi Thuch, the leader of the Thai People's Promotion of Friendship Visiting China Delegation, who recently visited China, sent identical telegrams to Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai on February 19th when he left Guangzhou. The telegram stated:

    "My delegation members and I departed Guangzhou on the 19th, traveling very comfortably. We deeply appreciate your hospitality. We hope our friendship continues to develop."

### 乃贴·触的努七离中国时  电毛主席和周总理致谢

1956-02-27
第1版()
专栏：

    乃贴·触的努七离中国时
    电毛主席和周总理致谢
    新华社26日讯　最近来中国访问的泰国人民促进友好访华团团长乃贴·触的努七在2月19日离开广州出境时，曾经把内容相同的电报分别拍发给毛泽东主席和周恩来总理。电文说：
    “我和我的团员于19日离广州，一路上非常安适，深深感谢你的款待。希望我们的友谊继续发展。”
