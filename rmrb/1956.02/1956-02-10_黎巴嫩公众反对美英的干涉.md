Title: Lebanese Public Opposes US-UK Interference
Author:
Date: 1956-02-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, 8th (TASS) – According to newspaper reports, the Executive Committee of the Conference of Representatives of Lebanese political parties and groups held a meeting to discuss the statements and joint declarations of Eisenhower and Eden. The meeting passed a resolution protesting the interference of the United States and the United Kingdom in the internal affairs of Arab countries.

### 黎巴嫩公众反对美英的干涉

1956-02-10
第4版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩公众反对美英的干涉
    新华社据塔斯社贝鲁特8日讯　据报纸消息说，黎巴嫩各政党和团体代表会议执行委员会举行会议，讨论了艾森豪威尔和艾登的声明和联合宣言。会议通过决议，抗议美英两国干涉阿拉伯国家的内政。
