Title: Lei Renmin Hosts Banquet to Bid Farewell to the French Economic Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1956-02-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 17th. Lei Renmin, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, hosted a banquet at noon on February 17 to bid farewell to the French Economic Delegation to China, headed by Henri Rochereau.
    Among those present were Li Zhuchen and Ji Chaoding, Vice Chairmen of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Lu Xuzhang, a member of the Council, Qiao Peixin, Acting Deputy General Manager of the Bank of China, and heads of various specialized import and export corporations in China.

### 雷任民设宴欢送法国访华经济代表团

1956-02-19
第1版()
专栏：

    雷任民设宴欢送法国访华经济代表团
    新华社17日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民17日中午设宴欢送以亨利·罗谢罗为首的法国访华经济代表团。
    出席作陪的有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席李烛尘、冀朝鼎，委员卢绪章，中国银行代副总经理乔培新和中国进出口各专业公司的负责人。
