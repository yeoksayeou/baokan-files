Title: Six-City Bicycle Cross-Country Race Held Today in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-02-15
Page: 3

The youth bicycle cross-country friendship race organized by the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League and involving six cities – Beijing, Tianjin, Tangshan, Shijiazhuang, Baoding, and Zhangjiakou – is scheduled to begin at 8:30 AM on the 15th outside Chaoyangmen in the capital. The race is divided into two groups: the men's group covering a distance of 60 li, and the women's group covering a distance of 36 li.

At 1:00 PM, after the race concludes, all athletes will form a large bicycle team and parade through the city. Led by 12 motorcycles, the procession will depart from Chaoyangmen, proceed through Dongsi, Dongdan, Tiananmen, Xidan, and Xisi, and exit through Xizhimen to the Central Youth League School.

A total of 210 athletes from the participating cities are competing. Among them, workers and female athletes each account for approximately one-third. The athletes also include national youth socialist construction activists, officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, as well as farmers, government employees, medical workers, and students. Athletes from Tianjin, Tangshan, Shijiazhuang, Baoding, and Zhangjiakou have already arrived in Beijing by bicycle on the 13th. Male and female bicycle athletes from Beijing went to Qinghe, Tongxian, and Changxindian respectively to welcome them.

### 六城市自行车越野比赛今日在京举行

1956-02-15
第3版()
专栏：

    六城市自行车越野比赛今日在京举行
    青年团中央组织的北京、天津、唐山、石家庄、保定、张家口六城市的青年自行车越野友谊比赛，定于15日上午8时半在首都朝阳门外举行。比赛共分两组。男子组的距离为60华里，女子组的距离为36华里。
    下午1时，比赛结束后，全体运动员将组成自行车大队在市内游行，由12辆摩托车领头，从朝阳门出发，经东四、东单、天安门、西单、西四，出西直门到中央团校。
    参加比赛的各城市运动员共210人。其中工人和女运动员都占三分之一左右。运动员中还有全国青年社会主义建设积极分子、中国人民解放军军官和士兵，以及农民、机关工作人员、医务工作者和学生等。天津、唐山、石家庄、保定、张家口五个城市的运动员已经在13日先后骑自行车到达北京。北京市的男、女自行车运动员曾分别到清河、通县和长辛店去迎接他们。
