Title: Prime Minister Sihanouk Leads Cambodian Delegation to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-02-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 14th – Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, leading the Cambodian National Delegation at the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Beijing by special plane on the afternoon of the 14th for a visit to China. At the airport, Prince Sihanouk told the more than 6,000 welcoming people that he firmly hoped for friendship and peaceful coexistence between Cambodia and China.

Welcoming him at the airport today were Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Chen Yun and Chen Yi, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Shen Junru, and Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Marshal Ye Jianying, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhang Bojun and Bao Erhan, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, as well as heads of government departments, people from all walks of life, Chinese and foreign journalists, cadres, workers, students, young people of various ethnic minorities, Buddhist monks, and followers of other religions.

Also present at the airport to welcome the delegation were the ambassadors of Poland, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Romania, Mongolia, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Bulgaria, Albania, Hungary, Finland, Czechoslovakia, Pakistan, Yugoslavia, India, the German Democratic Republic, Norway, and Afghanistan; the ministers of Denmark and Switzerland; the charges d'affaires of the Netherlands and the United Kingdom; and the acting charges d'affaires of Sweden, Burma, Indonesia, and the Soviet Union.

The Beijing West Suburb Airport was festively decorated today to welcome the arrival of the distinguished guests. The national flags of the Kingdom of Cambodia and the People's Republic of China were hung at the airport, with silk red flags planted on both sides. A large horizontal banner with gold characters on a red background hung over the archway at the entrance of the airport, reading: "Welcome the National Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia Headed by Prince Norodom Sihanouk." Welcoming crowds holding bouquets and colorful flags lined the airport perimeter.

At 3:05 PM, the plane carrying Prince Sihanouk and members of the delegation landed slowly amidst warm applause and cheers. Premier Zhou Enlai went to the plane to shake hands with Prince Sihanouk and the delegation members one by one. Subsequently, the distinguished guests were introduced to the main leaders of the Chinese government and various sectors.

The band played the national anthems of Cambodia and China.

Prince Sihanouk, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, reviewed the guard of honor lined up at the airport. Then, the distinguished guests received flowers from female students.

Prince Sihanouk and members of the delegation were then introduced to the heads of various departments of the national government of the People's Republic of China, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, people from all walks of life, and diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China, and shook hands with them one by one.

After reviewing the parade of the guard of honor, Prince Sihanouk delivered a speech.

Subsequently, Prince Sihanouk and others, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai and others, toured the airport grounds to meet the welcoming crowds. At this time, the welcomers waved bouquets, threw colorful paper flowers and streamers, and enthusiastically applauded and chanted slogans. Many gorgeously dressed girls ran out of the crowd and presented bouquets of flowers to Prince Sihanouk. The airport was filled with jubilation, lasting for more than ten minutes.

At 3:40 PM, Prince Sihanouk and others, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai and others, traveled by car to the guesthouse to rest.

As the cars carrying the distinguished guests left the airport, the band played marches, and the welcoming crowds at the airport and the thousands of people gathered in front of the airport gate and nearby residents enthusiastically cheered and applauded, paying tribute to the distinguished guests.

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, February 14th – The Cambodian National Delegation headed by Prince Norodom Sihanouk left Guangzhou for Beijing at 8:17 AM on the 14th.

The national flags of the Kingdom of Cambodia and the People's Republic of China fluttered at the airport. Representatives from all walks of life who came to see them off had already gathered here. Among those seeing them off were Guangdong Province Governor Tao Zhu, Vice Governors He Ximing and Chen Rutang, Guangzhou Mayor Zhu Guang, Vice Mayor Xue Yan, General Huang Yongsheng of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, leaders of various social organizations, and cultural workers.

Accompanying Prince Sihanouk on his flight from Guangzhou to Beijing were Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, and others.

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, February 14th – The Cambodian National Delegation headed by Norodom Sihanouk arrived in Wuhan from Guangzhou by special plane at 11:00 AM today. After resting and dining at the airport, the distinguished guests left Wuhan for Beijing by special plane at 12:00 PM.

Hubei Province Governor Zhang Tixue, Wuhan Mayor Song Kanfu, Commander Kong Qingde of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Wuhan, as well as heads of democratic parties, people's organizations, and representatives of religious circles from the province and city, went to the airport to welcome and see off the distinguished guests. ### 西哈努克首相率领柬埔寨代表团到京

1956-02-15
第1版()
专栏：

    西哈努克首相率领柬埔寨代表团到京
    新华社14日讯　应中华人民共和国政府邀请前来中国访问的柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和他所率领的柬埔寨王国国家代表团，在14日下午乘专机到达北京。西哈努克亲王在机场上向6，000多名欢迎的群众说，他坚定地希望柬中两国友好和和平共处。
    今天到机场欢迎的，有国务院总理周恩来，副总理陈云、陈毅，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、北京市市长彭真，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长陈叔通，国防委员会副主席叶剑英元帅，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧、鲍尔汉和外交部副部长张闻天，以及政府各部门负责人、社会各界人士、中外记者、机关干部、工人、学生、各少数民族青年、佛教僧侣和其他宗教信徒。
    到机场欢迎的还有波兰、越南民主共和国、罗马尼亚、蒙古、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚、匈牙利、芬兰、捷克斯洛伐克、巴基斯坦、南斯拉夫、印度、德意志民主共和国、挪威和阿富汗的大使，丹麦、瑞士的公使，荷兰、英国的代办，瑞典、缅甸、印度尼西亚、苏联的临时代办。
    北京西郊机场今天为欢迎贵宾们的到来披上了盛装。机场上悬挂着柬埔寨王国和中华人民共和国的国旗，在两旁插着绸制的红旗。机场入口处牌坊上悬挂着红底金字的横幅大标语，上面写着：“欢迎以诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王为首的柬埔寨王国国家代表团”。在机场四周排列着拿着花束和彩色旗帜的欢迎队伍。
    下午3时另5分，西哈努克亲王和代表团团员乘坐的飞机在热烈的掌声和欢呼声中徐徐着陆。周恩来总理到飞机跟前向西哈努克亲王以及代表团人员一一握手。接着，贵宾们被介绍同中国政府和各方面主要负责人见面。
    乐队奏柬中两国国歌。
    西哈努克亲王由周恩来总理陪同检阅了排列在机场上的仪仗队。然后，西哈努克亲王和代表团团员接受了女学生的献花。
    西哈努克亲王和代表团团员接着被介绍给前来欢迎的中华人民共和国国家机关各部门负责人、中国人民解放军高级将领、社会各界人士以及各国驻华使节和外交官员，并且同他们一一握手。
    在检阅仪仗队的分列式以后，西哈努克亲王发表了讲话。
    随后，西哈努克亲王等由周恩来总理等陪同绕场一周，同欢迎的群众见面。这时候，欢迎者挥舞着花束，抛掷着彩色纸花和纸条，并且热烈地鼓掌和欢呼口号。许多盛装的女孩子从人群中跑出来，把一束又一束的鲜花献给西哈努克亲王。机场上一片欢腾，持续达十多分钟。
    3时40分，西哈努克亲王等由周恩来总理等陪同乘车赴迎宾馆休息。
    当贵宾们乘坐的汽车离开机场的时候，乐队奏起了进行曲，机场上欢迎的人群和聚集在机场门前成千的群众以及附近的居民，都热烈欢呼鼓掌，向贵宾们致意。
    新华社广州14日电　以诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王为首的柬埔寨王国国家代表团于14日上午8时17分离广州前往北京。
    机场上飘扬着柬埔寨王国和中华人民共和国的国旗。前来欢送的各界人民代表早已聚集在这里。在送行的人群中，有广东省省长陶铸、副省长贺希明、陈汝棠，广州市市长朱光、副市长薛焰，中国人民解放军黄永胜上将，各社会团体的领导人员以及文化工作者。
    陪同西哈努克亲王飞离广州前往北京的有中华人民共和国外交部礼宾司司长柯华等。
    新华社武汉14日电　以诺罗敦·西哈努克为首的柬埔寨王国国家代表团，今天上午11时乘专机从广州到达武汉。贵宾们在机场休息用餐后，12时乘专机离开武汉前往北京。
    湖北省省长张体学，武汉市市长宋侃夫、中国人民解放军驻武汉部队首长孔庆德以及省市各民主党派、人民团体的负责人和宗教界代表等曾到机场欢迎和欢送贵宾。（附图片）
