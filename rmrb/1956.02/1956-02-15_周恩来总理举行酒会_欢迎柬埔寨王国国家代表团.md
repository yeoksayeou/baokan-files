Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Holds Reception to Welcome the National Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1956-02-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 14th—Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, held a reception this afternoon to welcome Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, and all members of the National Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia he leads.

This grand reception was held in the Ziguang Pavilion of Zhongnanhai. At 5:15 PM, Prince Sihanouk and all members of the delegation he led arrived at the Ziguang Pavilion. Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian welcomed the distinguished guests at the entrance of the hall. Then, in a warm and friendly atmosphere, the hosts and guests gathered together to talk.

At the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai introduced the distinguished guests to people from all walks of life in China who had gathered in the hall.

At today's reception, the distinguished guests met with Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yun and Peng Dehuai; Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Li Weihan, and Chen Shutong; Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen; Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council Marshal Ye Jianying, Cheng Qian, Fu Zuoyi, and Long Yun; and Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhang Bojun and Bao Erhan.

The distinguished guests also met with Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun; Ministers Luo Ruiqing, Shi Liang, Qian Ying, Zhang Naiqi, Ye Jizhuang, Huang Jing, Jia Tuofu, Teng Daiyuan, Zhu Xuefan, Liao Luyan, Liang Xi, Shen Yanbing, Yang Xiufeng, and Zhang Xiruo; Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Ji Pengfei; Members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Wang Kunlun, Wu Yuzhang, Li Zhuchen, Shao Lizi, Ma Yinchu, Zhang Su, Xu Deheng, Huang Shaohong, Huang Kecheng, Liao Chengzhi, Cai Tingkai, Lai Ruoyu, and Luo Longji; and Deputy Chiefs of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army General Chen Geng, Senior General Zhang Zongxun, and Senior General Li Kenong.

Also attending the reception were Vice Ministers of various ministries and Vice Chairmen of various committees of the People's Republic of China, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, leaders of various democratic parties and people's organizations, representatives from the scientific, educational, literary, journalistic, and religious circles in Beijing, social celebrities, and representatives of various ethnic groups.

More than 200 people attended today's reception.

Xinhua News Agency, February 14th—At 7:00 PM on the 14th, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai invited the head of the National Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia Prince Norodom Sihanouk, and delegation members: Member of the Royal Supreme Council Ran Nieth, Member of the Royal Supreme Council Khim Tit, and Vice-President of the National Assembly Sang Nhean to dinner.

### 周恩来总理举行酒会  欢迎柬埔寨王国国家代表团

1956-02-15
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理举行酒会
    欢迎柬埔寨王国国家代表团
    新华社14日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天下午举行酒会，欢迎柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和他所率领的柬埔寨王国国家代表团全体人员。
    这个盛大的酒会是在中南海紫光阁举行的。5时零15分，西哈努克亲王和他所率领的代表团全体人员来到紫光阁。国务院总理周恩来、副总理陈毅和外交部副部长张闻天在大厅入口处欢迎贵宾们。接着，宾主们在热烈友好的空气中，围聚一起交谈。
    在酒会上，周恩来总理介绍贵宾们同聚集在大厅里的中国各界人士一一见面。
    在今天的酒会上，贵宾们会见了国务院副总理陈云、彭德怀，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、黄炎培、李维汉、陈叔通，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、北京市市长彭真，国防委员会副主席叶剑英元帅、程潜、傅作义、龙云，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧、鲍尔汉。
    贵宾们还会见了国务院秘书长习仲勋，各部部长罗瑞卿、史　良、钱　瑛、章乃器、叶季壮、黄　敬、贾拓夫、滕代远、朱学范、廖鲁言、梁　希、沈雁冰、杨秀峰、张奚若，外交部副部长章汉夫、姬鹏飞，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员王昆仑、吴玉章、李烛尘、邵力子、马寅初、张　苏、许德珩、黄绍竑、黄克诚、廖承志、蔡廷锴、赖若愚、罗隆基，中国人民解放军副总参谋长陈赓大将、张宗逊上将、李克农上将。
    出席酒会的还有中华人民共和国各部副部长、各委员会副主任，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长，中国人民解放军高级将领，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员，各民主党派、各人民团体负责人，北京科学、教育、文艺、新闻、宗教界人士和社会名流，各民族代表等。
    今天出席酒会的共有200多人。
    新华社14日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在14日晚7时，邀请柬埔寨王国国家代表团团长、柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王，代表团团员：皇廷最高会议委员兰·涅特，皇廷最高会议委员钦·迪和王国会议副议长桑·年共进晚餐。
    陪同进餐的，有国务院副总理陈毅，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、北京市市长彭真和外交部副部长张闻天。（附图片）
