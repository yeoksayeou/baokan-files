Title: Kaesong Solemnly Pays Respects at the Cemetery of Revolutionary Martyrs of the Korean-Chinese Side
Author:
Date: 1956-02-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, February 9th. More than 10,000 people from all walks of life in Kaesong, as well as representatives from the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers stationed in the Kaesong area, braved light rain and snow to solemnly pay respects at the Korean People's Army Martyrs Cemetery and the Chinese People's Volunteers Martyrs Cemetery at the foot of Songaksan Mountain in Kaesong on the 8th.

Among those who participated in the tomb sweeping were a delegation of people from all walks of life in Korea headed by Li Zhouyuan, Minister of Finance of the Korean Cabinet, a delegation of the Korean People's Army headed by Major General Li Fangnan, a delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteers congratulating the Korean People's Army on Army Day headed by Major General Ren Rong, personnel from the Korean-Chinese side of the Korean Military Armistice Commission headed by Major General Zheng Guolu, and responsible persons from the Kaesong City Labor Party organizations and government agencies.

Representatives from the Chinese People's Delegation for Comforting the Korean People and members of the song and dance troupe, who are currently carrying out comfort activities in the Kaesong area, also went to pay respects, led by Deputy Head Luo Feng.

### 开城隆重祭扫朝中烈士陵园

1956-02-10
第4版()
专栏：

    开城隆重祭扫朝中烈士陵园
    新华社开城9日电　开城市各界人民和驻开城地区的朝鲜人民军、中国人民志愿军部队代表1万多人，8日冒着细雨小雪隆重祭扫开城松岳山下的朝鲜人民军烈士陵园和中国人民志愿军烈士陵园。
    参加扫墓的有以朝鲜内阁财政相李周渊为首的朝鲜各界人民代表团、李芳男少将为首的朝鲜人民军代表团、以任荣少将为首的中国人民志愿军祝贺朝鲜人民军建军节代表团、以郑国禄少将为首的朝鲜军事停战委员会朝中方面代表团的人员，开城市的劳动党组织和政府机关负责人。
    正在开城地区进行慰问工作的中国人民赴朝慰问团代表和歌舞团人员，在副总团长洛风率领下，也前往祭扫。
