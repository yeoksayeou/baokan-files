Title: 55 Delegations from Fraternal Communist and Workers' Parties Attend the CPSU Congress
Author: 
Date: 1956-02-17
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 15th - TASS reports: 55 delegations from fraternal Communist and Workers' Parties attended the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union as guests.
    The representatives of the Chinese Communist Party are: Zhu De, Deng Xiaoping, Tan Zhenlin, Wang Jiaxiang and Liu Xiao;
    The representatives of the Polish United Workers' Party are: Bierut, Cyrankiewicz, Zawadzki, Berman and Morawski;
    The representatives of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia are: Novotny, Zapotocky, Siroky, Fierlinger and Barak;
    The representatives of the French Communist Party are: Thorez, Duclos, Guyot and Pierre Doize;
    The representatives of the Italian Communist Party are: Togliatti, Scoccimarro, Montagnana, Bugliani, Cacciapuoti and Buffalini;
    The representatives of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany are: Ulbricht, Grotewohl, Dieckmann and Norden;
    The representatives of the Hungarian Working People's Party are: Rákosi, Kovács and Szalai;
    The representatives of the Romanian Workers' Party are: Gheorghiu-Dej, Chişinevschi, Constantinescu and Borile;
    The representatives of the Bulgarian Communist Party are: Chervenkov, Yugov, Damyánov, Ganev and Zvezhdov;
    The representatives of the Albanian Party of Labour are: Hoxha, Shehu, Nushi, Kondi and Paçrami;
    The representatives of the Korean Workers' Party are: Choi Yong-kun, Lee Hyo-soon, Heo Bin and Lee Sang-jo;
    The representatives of the Vietnam Workers' Party are: Trường Chinh and Lê Đức Thọ;
    The representative of the Indonesian Communist Party is: D.N. Aidit;
    The representatives of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party are: Tsedenbal, Damba and Tumur-Ochir;
    The representative of the Communist Party of India is: Ajoy Kumar Ghosh;
    The representatives of the Communist Party of Finland are: Suvanto and Malmberg;
    The representatives of the Communist Party of Spain are: Ibárruri and four other representatives;
    The representatives of the Communist Party of Great Britain are: Pollitt, Dutt and Mathews;
    The representatives of the Communist Party of Germany are: Reimann and two other representatives;
    The representatives of the Communist Party of Austria are: Koplenig, Fürnberg and Honner;
    The representatives of the Communist Party of Argentina are: Codovilla, Lora Roldán and Gioldi;
    The representatives of the Communist Party of Syria and Lebanon are: Bakdash, Chaoui, Faisal, Madoian and Sawaya;
    The representatives of the Communist Party of Sweden are: Hagberg, Lager and Hermansson;
    The representatives of the Mexican Communist Party are: Encina, Encarnación and Terrasas;
    The representatives of the Communist Party of Denmark are: Larsen and Nørlund;
    The representatives of the Communist Party of Norway are: Lovlien, Lie and Larsen;
    The representative of the Communist Party of Trieste is: Vidali;
    The representatives of the Communist Party of the Netherlands are: Groot and Hoekstra;
    The representatives of the Communist Party of Belgium are: Burnelle, Deconinck and Volders;
    The representatives of the Labour-Progressive Party of Canada are: Buck and Ryerson;
    The representatives of the Icelandic United Socialist Party are: Andrésson and Dobbiansson;
    The representatives of the Swiss Party of Labour are: Vincent and Muhlethaler;
    The representatives of the Communist Party of Luxembourg are: Urbany and Hoffmann;
    The representatives of the Communist Party of Israel are: Mikunis and Habibi;
    The representatives of the Communist Party of Uruguay are: Arismendi, Pastorino and Saporín.
    In addition, Communist and Workers' Parties from Brazil, Japan, Venezuela, Chile, Bolivia, Cuba, Costa Rica, Colombia, Australia, and other countries also sent representatives to the conference.

1956-02-17
第4版()
专栏：
