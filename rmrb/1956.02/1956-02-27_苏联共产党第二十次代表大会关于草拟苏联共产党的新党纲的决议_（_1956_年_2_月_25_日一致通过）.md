Title: Resolution of the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union on Drafting a New Program of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-02-27
Page: 1

    The 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union entrusts the Central Committee to draft a draft program of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union based on the basic principles of Marxist-Leninist theory creatively developed on the basis of the historical experience of our party, the experience of fraternal parties in socialist countries, the experience and achievements of the entire international communist movement and the workers' movement, and taking into account the long-term plans for communist construction and the economic and cultural development of the Soviet Union that are being drafted.
    The Congress instructs the Central Committee to publish the draft Party Program in a timely manner before the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union in order to conduct extensive discussions. (Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 25th, via TASS)

### 苏联共产党第二十次代表大会关于草拟苏联共产党的新党纲的决议  （1956年2月25日一致通过）

1956-02-27
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联共产党第二十次代表大会关于草拟苏联共产党的新党纲的决议
    （1956年2月25日一致通过）
    苏联共产党第二十次代表大会责成中央委员会根据在我党的历史经验、社会主义国家兄弟党的经验、整个国际共产主义运动和工人运动的经验和成就的基础上创造性地发展着的马克思列宁主义理论的基本原理，并且考虑到草拟中的关于共产主义建设、苏联经济和文化发展的远景计划，草拟苏联共产党的党纲草案。
    代表大会责成中央委员会在苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会之前及时地公布党纲草案，以便进行广泛的讨论。（新华社据塔斯社莫斯科25日电）
