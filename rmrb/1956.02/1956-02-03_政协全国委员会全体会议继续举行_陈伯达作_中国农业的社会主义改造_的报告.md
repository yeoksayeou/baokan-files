Title: CPPCC National Committee Holds Continued Plenary Session; Chen Boda Delivers Report on "The Socialist Transformation of Chinese Agriculture"
Author:
Date: 1956-02-03
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, February 2nd. The Second Plenary Session of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) continued in the afternoon of February 2nd.
    Chen Boda, Standing Committee Member of the National Committee of the CPPCC and Deputy Director of the Rural Work Department of the CCP Central Committee, delivered a report on "The Socialist Transformation of Chinese Agriculture" at the meeting.
    The following people spoke at today's meeting: Huang Yanpei, Zhang Bojun, Liang Sicheng, Zhou Fengjiu, Ou Xie·Tudengsangque, Huang Qixiang, Li Chunqing, Cheng Qian, Zhong Huilan, Chen Heqin, Chen Qiyou, Zhou Shilu, Tu Baoqi, Yuan Hechai, Wang Zhuquan, and Huba Meng.
    Today's meeting was held from 3:00 PM to 7:00 PM.
    In the morning, the members and attendees continued their group discussions.
    The meeting will continue on the 3rd.

### 政协全国委员会全体会议继续举行  陈伯达作“中国农业的社会主义改造”的报告

1956-02-03
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会全体会议继续举行
    陈伯达作“中国农业的社会主义改造”的报告
    新华社2日讯　中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第二次全体会议2日下午继续举行。
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员、中共中央农村工作部副部长陈伯达在会上作了“中国农业的社会主义改造”的报告。
    在今天会上发言的有：黄炎培、章伯钧、梁思成、周凤九、欧协·土登桑却、黄琪翔、李纯青、程潜、钟惠澜、陈鹤琴、陈其尤、周拾禄、屠宝琦、袁鹤侪、王竹泉、祜巴勐。
    今天会议从下午三时进行到七时。
    上午，出席会议的委员和列席人员继续进行了分组讨论。
    会议将在3日继续举行。
