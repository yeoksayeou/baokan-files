Title: Yellow River Survey and Design Institute Established
Author:
Date: 1956-02-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, 10th. The Yellow River Survey and Design Institute was declared established in Zhengzhou on the 7th.

This institute was established to strengthen the survey and design work in the fundamental solution to the Yellow River problem, and is under the leadership of the Yellow River Water Conservancy Commission. The institute will particularly concentrate technical forces to better complete the first phase of the project to fundamentally eliminate the Yellow River's water hazards and develop the Yellow River's water conservancy.

Currently, the institute is simultaneously improving its organizational structure and actively preparing technical and economic reports on the comprehensive utilization of the main tributaries of the Yellow River, the Wuding River, the Jing River, and the Sanchuan River, preparing preliminary designs for the Yi River and Luo River reservoirs, and conducting survey and design work for the first and second iron water hydropower stations of the Yellow River irrigation Ji-Wei main canal.

### 黄河勘测设计院成立

1956-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

    黄河勘测设计院成立
    新华社郑州10日电　黄河勘测设计院于7日在郑州宣布成立。
    这个院是为了加强黄河治本中的勘测设计工作成立的，归黄河水利委员会领导。这个院将特别集中技术力量，更好地完成根治黄河水害和开发黄河水利的第一期工程。
    现在这个院正一面健全机构，一面积极作对于黄河主要支流无定河、泾河、三川河的综合利用技术经济报告，作伊河和洛河水库的初步设计，以及作引黄灌溉济卫总干渠第一、二号铁水水力发电站的勘测设计等工作。
