Title: 1956 National Ice Sports Meet Concludes; 13 Male and Female Athletes Break 8 National Records
Author:
Date: 1956-02-02
Page: 3

The 1956 National Ice Sports Meet held in Changchun concluded on January 30th.

At this sports meet, 13 male and female athletes broke the 1955 national speed skating record 32 times in 8 events; among them, 9 male and female athletes broke the national all-around speed skating record.

Xu Mingshu, a female athlete from Harbin, broke her own national record of 2 minutes 2.2 seconds in the women's 1,000-meter speed skating with a time of 1 minute 55.4 seconds; broke the national record of 11 minutes 17.7 seconds in the women's 5,000-meter speed skating with a time of 10 minutes 57.7 seconds; and broke her own national all-around speed skating record of 251.903 points with a score of 246.320 points. Xu Mingshu also won first place in the women's 500-meter and 3,000-meter speed skating events with times of 57.3 seconds and 6 minutes 33.3 seconds respectively. In the performance before the closing ceremony, she broke the national record of 55.3 seconds in the women's 500-meter speed skating with a time of 55 seconds.

Jin Fushun, a female athlete from Harbin, and Sun Hongxia, a female athlete from the China Railway Sports Association, also broke the national record in the women's 1,000-meter speed skating. Li Fulian and Lu Chengyu, female athletes from Qiqihar, also broke the national record in the women's 5,000-meter speed skating.

In the men's 500-meter speed skating competition, Zhang Shaojun, Hu Shuanglai, Zheng Hongdao, and Ren Shijun, all athletes from Harbin, broke the national record of 47.6 seconds. Among them, Zhang Shaojun had the best result, with a time of 46.5 seconds.

In the men's 5,000-meter speed skating competition, Lin Zhenkun, an athlete from Harbin, broke his own national record of 9 minutes 12.4 seconds with a time of 9 minutes 3.2 seconds. Pu Dahua, an athlete from Jilin, also broke the national record with a time of 9 minutes 11 seconds.

In the men's 1,500-meter speed skating competition, Lin Zhenkun, Zheng Hongdao, Zhang Shaojun, and Ren Shijun from Harbin broke the national record of 2 minutes 37.4 seconds. Lin Zhenkun had the best result, with a time of 2 minutes 31.6 seconds.

In the men's 10,000-meter speed skating competition, Lin Zhenkun broke his own national record of 19 minutes 35.3 seconds with a time of 18 minutes 18.6 seconds; he also broke the national all-around speed skating record of 218.595 points with a score of 208.783 points. Pu Dahua from Jilin, and Ren Shijun, Zhang Shaojun, Zheng Hongdao, Zhang Pengrui, and Liu Yuanlong from Harbin, also broke the national record in the men's 10,000-meter speed skating. Zheng Hongdao, Ren Shijun, Pu Dahua, Zhang Shaojun, Hu Shuanglai, Liu Yuanlong, and Zhang Pengrui also broke the national all-around speed skating record.

The men's Group A figure skating champion was Tian Jichen from Harbin; the women's Group A figure skating champion was Wang Ying from Harbin.

In the ice hockey final, the Harbin ice hockey team won the championship; the Beijing ice hockey team won the second place.

In the youth speed skating competition, the Jilin youth speed skating team won the first place in the overall team score of the 6-city youth speed skating competition.
                  (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 1956年全国冰上运动会闭幕  13名男女运动员打破8项全国最高纪录

1956-02-02
第3版()
专栏：

    1956年全国冰上运动会闭幕
    13名男女运动员打破8项全国最高纪录
    在长春举行的1956年全国冰上运动会已经在1月30日闭幕。
    这次运动会有8项13名男、女运动员打破了32次速度滑冰1955年的全国最高纪录；其中有9名男、女运动员突破了速度滑冰全能的全国最高纪录。
    哈尔滨市的女运动员许明淑以1分55秒4的成绩，打破了她自己保持的女子1，000公尺速度滑冰2分2秒2的全国最高纪录；以10分57秒7的成绩，打破了女子5，000公尺速度滑冰11分17秒7的全国最高纪录；以246．320分的成绩，打破了她自己保持的女子速度滑冰全能251.903分的全国最高纪录。许明淑还分别以57秒3和6分33秒3的成绩，取得了女子500公尺和3，000公尺速度滑冰的第一名。她在闭幕式前的表演中又以55秒的成绩打破了女子500公尺速度滑冰55秒3的全国最高纪录。
    打破女子1，000公尺速度滑冰全国最高纪录的还有哈尔滨市的女运动员金福顺和中国火车头体育协会的女运动员孙洪霞。打破女子5，000公尺速度滑冰全国最高纪录的还有齐齐哈尔市的女运动员李福莲和卢成玉。
    在男子500公尺速度滑冰比赛中，哈尔滨市的运动员张绍君、胡双来、郑弘道、任世俊4个人都打破了47秒6的全国最高纪录，其中张绍君的成绩最好，是46秒5。
    在男子5，000公尺速度滑冰比赛中，哈尔滨市的运动员林振坤以9分3秒2的成绩，打破了他自己保持的9分12秒4的全国最高纪录。吉林市的运动员朴达华的成绩是9分11秒，也打破了全国最高纪录。
    在男子1，500公尺速度滑冰比赛中，打破2分37秒4全国最高纪录的有哈尔滨市的林振坤、郑弘道、张绍君和任世俊。林振坤的成绩最好，是2分31秒6。
    在男子10，000公尺速度滑冰比赛中，林振坤又以18分18秒6的成绩，打破了他自己保持的19分35秒3的全国最高纪录；他又以208.783分的成绩，打破了男子速度滑冰全能218.595分的全国最高纪录。打破男子10，000公尺速度滑冰全国最高纪录的还有吉林市的运动员朴达华和哈尔滨市的运动员任世俊、张绍君、郑弘道、张鹏瑞和柳元龙。打破男子速度滑冰全能全国最高纪录的还有郑弘道、任世俊、朴达华、张绍君、胡双来、柳元龙和张鹏瑞。
    男子甲组花样滑冰冠军是哈尔滨市运动员田继陈；获得女子甲组花样滑冰冠军的是哈尔滨市运动员王樱。
    在冰球决赛中，哈尔滨市冰球队获得冠军；北京市冰球队获得亚军。
    在少年速度滑冰比赛中，吉林市少年速度滑冰代表队获得了6个城市少年速度滑冰对抗赛的团体总分第一名。
                  （据新华社讯）
