Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Holds Reception to Celebrate the Sixth Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship

Author:
Date: 1956-02-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 14th: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held a reception in Beijing tonight to celebrate the sixth anniversary of signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance.

    Among those attending the reception were Wu Yuzhang, Guo Moruo, and Liu Ningyi, vice presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Council, and many council members in Beijing.

    Also present at the reception were Li Haiqiu, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, embassy staff, and Soviet experts in Beijing.

    More than 1,200 people attended the reception.

    Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Council of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, spoke at the meeting. He warmly congratulated the sixth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty and Mutual Assistance and the opening today of the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. On behalf of all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and on behalf of President Soong Ching Ling, he extended heartfelt congratulations and gratitude to the great Soviet people.

    He said: Over the past six years, the fraternal friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union has been greatly developed and consolidated. China and the Soviet Union have carried out close unity and cooperation in politics, economy, culture and all other aspects. The unbreakable Sino-Soviet alliance has always been an important factor in safeguarding peace in the Far East and the world. Our people will continue to strengthen unity with the Soviet people, maintain high vigilance against the imperialists' conspiracy to intensify international tensions, and unremittingly struggle for the security of the Far East and the world, and for the liberation of our territory, Taiwan.

    Qian Junrui also said: The economic and cultural cooperation between China and the Soviet Union is an important factor for the victorious advance of our socialist construction. Our people will work harder to learn from the Soviet people's experience in building socialism and communism, learn Soviet science and technology, and build our socialist motherland with greater enthusiasm, and strive to complete the first five-year plan ahead of schedule and above quota.

    Subsequently, the congratulatory telegrams exchanged between the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign countries to celebrate the sixth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance were read out at the meeting.

    The evening ended with music and dance performances.

### 中苏友协总会举行晚会庆祝中苏友好同盟六周年

1956-02-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友协总会举行晚会庆祝中苏友好同盟六周年
    据新华社14日讯　中苏友好协会总会今晚在京举行晚会，庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订六周年。
    参加晚会的有中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章、郭沫若、刘宁一，理事会秘书长钱俊瑞和在京理事多人。
    参加晚会的还有苏联驻中国大使馆临时代办李海秋和使馆人员以及在京的苏联专家。
    参加晚会的共有1，200多人。
    中苏友好协会总会理事会秘书长钱俊瑞在会上讲话。他热烈祝贺中苏友好同盟互助条约签订六周年和苏联共产党第二十次代表大会的在今天开幕。他以中苏友好协会全体会员的名义、并且代表宋庆龄会长向伟大的苏联人民致以衷心的祝贺和感谢。
    他说：六年来，中苏两国人民的兄弟般的友谊得到了极大的发展和巩固，中苏两国在政治、经济、文化各个方面进行了亲密无间的团结与合作。牢不可破的中苏同盟一直是保卫远东和世界和平的重要因素。我国人民将继续加强和苏联人民的团结，并且对帝国主义者加剧国际紧张局势的阴谋保持高度的警惕，为远东和世界的安全，为解放我国领土台湾而坚持不懈地斗争。
    钱俊瑞又说：中苏两国经济与文化的合作，对于我国社会主义建设事业的胜利前进是一个重要因素。我国人民将更加努力学习苏联人民建设社会主义和共产主义的经验，学习苏联的科学和技术，以更加高涨的热情来建设我们的社会主义的祖国，为提早和超额完成第一个五年计划而奋斗。
    接着，在会上宣读了中苏友好协会总会和苏联对外文化协会为了庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订六周年的相互的贺电。
    晚会最后演出了音乐舞蹈节目。
