Title: Chiang Kai-shek Troops Occupying Gaodeng Island Shell Fishing Boats
Author:
Date: 1956-02-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 10th -- At 10:28 on the 9th, Chiang Kai-shek's artillery, entrenched on Gaodeng Island, fired more than 70 shells at two fishing boats fishing in the sea 5,000 meters northeast of Gaodeng Island. These two fishing boats belonged to fishermen from Meihua Town, Changle County, Fujian Province. One of the fishing boats was hit by 6 Chiang Kai-shek shells, the boat was damaged and drifted to the sea near Gaodeng Island, and was towed away by a Chiang Kai-shek speedboat. The whereabouts of the 7 fishermen on board are unknown. The other fishing boat returned safely under the cover of our army's artillery fire.

From 20:18 to 20:30 on the 9th, 3 Chiang Kai-shek warships fired more than 70 shells at areas near Huangqi Town. At around 18:00 on the same day, Chiang Kai-shek's artillery, entrenched on Kinmen Island, also fired 54 shells near Huzishan Mountain on Xiamen Island.

### 盘踞高嶝岛的蒋军炮击渔船

1956-02-13
第1版()
专栏：

    盘踞高嶝岛的蒋军炮击渔船
    新华社福建前线10日电　9日10时28分，盘踞高嶝岛的蒋军炮兵向在高嶝岛东北5，000公尺海面捕鱼的两只渔船打了70多发炮弹。这两只渔船是福建省长乐县梅花镇渔民的。其中一只渔船被蒋军打中6发炮弹，船被打坏，漂到高嶝岛附近海面，被蒋军快艇拖走。船上的7个渔民下落不明。另一只渔船在我军炮火掩护下安全归来。
    9日20时18分到30分，蒋军兵舰3艘向黄岐镇附近地区发射炮弹70多发。同日18时左右，盘踞金门岛的蒋军炮兵还向厦门岛虎仔山附近发射炮弹54发。
