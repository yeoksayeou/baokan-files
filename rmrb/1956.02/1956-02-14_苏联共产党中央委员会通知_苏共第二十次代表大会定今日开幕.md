Title: Notice from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union: The 20th Congress of the CPSU is scheduled to open today

Author:
Date: 1956-02-14
Page: 4

    Notice from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union
    The 20th Congress of the CPSU is scheduled to open today
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 13th, TASS dispatch: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union informs the delegates attending the 20th Party Congress that the congress will open tomorrow (February 14th) at 10:00 AM in the Grand Kremlin Palace.

### 苏联共产党中央委员会通知  苏共第二十次代表大会定今日开幕

1956-02-14
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联共产党中央委员会通知
    苏共第二十次代表大会定今日开幕
    新华社13日讯　塔斯社莫斯科13日电　：苏联共产党中央委员会通知出席第二十次党代表大会的代表，本次代表大会将于明日（2月14日）上午10时在大克里姆林宫会议厅开幕。
