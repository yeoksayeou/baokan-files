Title: Cambodian Prime Minister's Secretariat Issues Communiqué on Sihanouk's Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1956-02-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 11th. According to a Phnom Penh radio broadcast on the morning of the 11th, the Secretariat of Cambodian Prime Minister Sihanouk issued a communiqué stating that Sihanouk had decided to visit China. This visit was at the invitation of Zhou Enlai, Premier of the People's Republic of China. The communiqué said that the visit represented Cambodia's policy of neutrality and its desire to establish relations with all countries, especially Asian countries. This visit is a continuation of Sihanouk's visits to various countries. The visit will also strengthen relations between Cambodia and the People's Republic of China and will enhance the existing friendship between the two countries.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 11th. According to a Reuters dispatch from Phnom Penh, Cambodian Prime Minister Prince Sihanouk announced on February 10th that he would lead a Cambodian delegation from Phnom Penh to Beijing at the invitation of Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai.

Prince Sihanouk said that the purpose of the visit was to strengthen the long-standing friendly ties between the two countries, help alleviate the atmosphere of international distrust, and strengthen peace.

### 柬埔寨首相秘书处就西哈努克访问中国发表公报

1956-02-12
第1版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨首相秘书处就西哈努克访问中国发表公报
    新华社河内11日电　据金边电台11日晨广播，柬埔寨首相西哈努克的秘书处发表公报说，西哈努克已经决定访问中国。这次访问是由中华人民共和国总理周恩来邀请的。公报说，这次的访问表示了柬埔寨的中立政策和想同各国尤其是亚洲国家建立关系的愿望。这次的访问是西哈努克在各国的访问的继续。这次访问也将密切柬埔寨和中华人民共和国两国之间的关系，并且将加强两国原有的友谊。
    新华社河内11日电　据路透社金边消息，柬埔寨首相西哈努克亲王2月10日宣布他应中国总理周恩来的邀请将率领一个柬埔寨代表团离金边去北京访问。
    西哈努克亲王说，这次访问的目的是加强两国之间悠久的友好联系，协助减轻国际不信任空气和加强和平。
