Title: Zvankov Discusses the Soviet Union's Construction of Atomic Icebreakers
Author:
Date: 1956-02-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 1st (TASS dispatch) - The Soviet Union's Sixth Five-Year Plan stipulates the establishment of atomic power equipment for transportation and the manufacture of icebreakers equipped with atomic engines. F. Zvankov, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union's General Institute of Transportation Problems and Corresponding Academician of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, told a TASS reporter that completing this task would be particularly beneficial to the development of science and the national economy. The large-scale research work being carried out by Soviet scientists, such as research in the Far North and in Antarctica, requires the use of the latest transportation tools with powerful horsepower.

Atomic ships are a new type of vessel. These ships use atomic power equipment. The Soviet Union now possesses all the necessary conditions for building such ships. Several types of atomic power equipment can be used as ship engines. These devices have a high effective utilization rate and are very economical. For example, one kilogram of uranium used in these devices emits the equivalent of 2,500 tons of anthracite in terms of heat. Ships equipped with ordinary engines must carry large amounts of fuel, while atomic ships require very little fuel. For example, a 15,000-horsepower ship uses an average of 85 tons of liquid fuel per day, while an atomic ship of the same horsepower requires only 55 cubic centimeters of uranium.

The achievements the Soviet Union has made in nuclear physics are now not only being used to expand power and technological capabilities. Soviet researchers have also mastered the method of tracer atoms, which can be used to explore mysteries that have been rarely touched upon until now. For example, the Soviet transportation science community is now using this method to determine the degree of machine wear, the thickness of scale buildup in locomotive and ship boilers, and to detect defects in axles and rails, etc.

### 兹房科夫谈苏联制造原子破冰船的问题

1956-02-03
第4版()
专栏：

    兹房科夫谈苏联制造原子破冰船的问题
    新华社据塔斯社莫斯科1日讯　苏联第六个五年计划规定要建立运输上用的原子动力设备和制造装有原子发动机的破冰船。苏联总体运输问题研究所副所长、苏联科学院通讯院士佛·兹房科夫向塔斯社记者说，完成这一任务对发展科学和发展国民经济特别有用处。苏联科学家所进行的大规模的研究工作，例如在极北地区和在南极进行的研究工作，需要使用有强大马力的最新式的运输工具。
    原子船是船只中的新的一类。在这种船上使用的是原子动力设备。苏联现在具备有建造这种船只的一切必要条件。有好几种原子动力设备可以用作船的发动机。这些设备的有效利用率很高，而且还很经济。例如，这些设备所用的一公斤铀，就发出热量的能力说，等于2，500吨无烟煤。装有普通发动机的船只必须装载大量的燃料，而原子船只需要极少量的燃料。例如，15，000马力的轮船平均每昼夜要用85吨液体燃料，而同样马力的原子船只要55公分铀。
    苏联在核子物理学上取得的成就，现在不仅被利用来扩大动力和技术的能力。苏联研究人员现在还掌握了示踪原子的方法，利用这种方法可以探索直到现在还很少触及的奥秘。例如，苏联的运输科学界现在利用这种方法来测定机器磨损的程度，以及机车和轮船的锅炉中积存的水垢的厚度，发现轴和钢轨中的缺陷等等。
