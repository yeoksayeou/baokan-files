Title: Yugoslav Youth Football Team Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1956-02-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 7th: The Yugoslav Youth Football Team, headed by B. Piščić, left Beijing by special plane on the morning of the 7th to return home after a 10-day visit to China. Cai Shufan and Lu Han, Deputy Directors of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, and others were at the airport to see them off. Personnel from the Yugoslav Embassy in China were also at the airport to see them off.

On the 6th, B. Piščić, the leader of the Yugoslav Youth Football Team, delivered a radio address to the Chinese people on the Central People's Broadcasting Station.

### 南斯拉夫青年足球队离京回国

1956-02-10
第1版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫青年足球队离京回国
    据新华社7日讯　以勃·彼希契为首的南斯拉夫青年足球队在中国访问了10天以后，7日上午乘专机离北京回国。到机场欢送的有中华人民共和国体育运动委员会副主任蔡树藩、卢汉等。南斯拉夫驻中国大使馆人员也到机场欢送。
    6日，南斯拉夫青年足球队领队勃·彼希契在中央人民广播电台对中国人民发表了广播演说。
