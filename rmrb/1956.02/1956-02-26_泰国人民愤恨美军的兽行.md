Title: Thai People Resent American Soldiers' Brutality
Author:
Date: 1956-02-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, February 25th dispatch from Bangkok: According to Thai newspapers, American soldiers participating in the recent Manila Pact Organization military exercises in Bangkok committed many crimes in Bangkok, including insulting Thai women, fighting, and other acts that disrupted Thai social order.

On February 16th, a Thai woman fainted after being ravaged by six or seven American soldiers and was sent to the hospital for treatment. The brutality of the American soldiers has aroused great indignation among the Thai public. Nai Thongyoo, a former Thai parliamentarian, told reporters on February 17th that this greatly hurt the self-esteem of the Thai people.

The "Samseri Newspaper" published an editorial on the 20th saying that this behavior of insulting Thai women like robbers made the Thai people feel very painful. This incident is something the Thai people must remember for a long time.

### 泰国人民愤恨美军的兽行

1956-02-26
第4版()
专栏：

    泰国人民愤恨美军的兽行
    据新华社25日讯　曼谷消息：据泰国报纸报道，参加最近在曼谷举行的马尼拉条约组织军事演习的美国军人，在曼谷曾经干了许多侮辱泰国妇女、打架和其他扰乱泰国社会秩序的罪行。
    2月16日，有一个泰国妇女在遭到六、七个美国士兵的蹂躏后昏倒，被送入医院救治。美国军人的暴行引起了泰国公众很大的愤慨。泰国前议员乃通裕2月17日就这件事向报界发表谈话说，这使泰国人民的自尊心大大受到伤害。
    “桑塞里报”20日发表社论说，这种像强盗一样侮辱泰国妇女的行为，使泰国人感到非常痛苦。这一事件，是一件泰国人民必须长期记住的事情。
