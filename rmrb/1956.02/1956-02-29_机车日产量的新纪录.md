Title: New Record for Daily Locomotive Output
Author:
Date: 1956-02-29
Page: 2

New Record for Daily Locomotive Output

    The Wang Enxiang train crew of the Changchun Locomotive Depot, Changchun Railway Sub-bureau, completed 1.57 million ton-kilometers of transport with a steam locomotive in the 24 hours from 6:00 AM on the 25th to 6:00 AM on the 26th, creating a new national record for daily output per locomotive, exceeding the state quota by 90%. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 机车日产量的新纪录

1956-02-29
第2版()
专栏：

    机车日产量的新纪录
    长春铁路分局长春机务段汪恩祥包车组从25日早晨6点钟到26日早晨6点钟的24小时内，用一台蒸汽机车完成了157万吨公里的运输任务，创造了每台机车日产量的全国新纪录，超出国家定额90％。（据新华社讯）
