Title: The 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union Opens
Author:
Date: 1956-02-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 14th, TASS dispatch: The 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union opened on the morning of February 14th in the Grand Kremlin Palace in Moscow. Delegates from all over the country attended the congress, along with many guests: workers from industrial enterprises, advanced agricultural workers, leading government and party personnel, and scientific and cultural workers.

The congress opened under the chairmanship of Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

In his opening remarks, Khrushchev said that during the period since the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, we lost outstanding leaders of the communist movement—Stalin, Gottwald, and Tokuda Kyuichi. At Khrushchev's suggestion, all those present stood and observed a moment of silence in their memory.

Khrushchev then announced that representatives of communist and workers' parties from many countries had come to participate in the congress. The delegates to the congress expressed a warm welcome to the foreign guests.

The congress elected the leading bodies of the congress—the Presidium, the Secretariat, the Editorial Committee, and the Credentials Committee. Subsequently, the following agenda was adopted: (1) Report of the Central Committee of the CPSU—Reporter: Khrushchev, Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPSU;
        (2) Report of the Inspection Committee of the Central Committee of the CPSU—Reporter: Moskatov, Chairman of the Inspection Committee;
        (3) The Directives of the 20th Congress of the CPSU on the Sixth Five-Year Plan (1956-1960)—Reporter: Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union;
        (4) Election of the Party Central Organs.

Then, Khrushchev gave a report on the first item on the agenda, and all those present applauded him warmly.

### 苏联共产党第二十次代表大会开幕

1956-02-15
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联共产党第二十次代表大会开幕
    新华社14日讯　塔斯社莫斯科14日电：苏联共产党第二十次代表大会2月14日上午在莫斯科大克里姆林宫开幕。出席代表大会的有全国各地的代表，此外，还有许多来宾：工业企业的工人、先进农业工作者、政府和党的领导工作人员、科学和文化工作者。
    代表大会在苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫主持下开幕。
    赫鲁晓夫在致开幕词的时候说，在苏联共产党第十九次代表大会闭幕以来的这一段时期内，我们丧失了共产主义运动的杰出领袖——斯大林、哥特瓦尔德和德田球一。在赫鲁晓夫的提议下，全体到会者起立，为他们志哀。
    赫鲁晓夫接着宣布，前来参加代表大会的有许多国家的共产党和工人党的代表。大会代表们向外国来宾表示热烈的欢迎。
    大会选举了代表大会的领导机构——主席团、秘书处、编纂委员会和资格审查委员会，随后，通过大会议程如下：（一）苏共中央委员会报告——报告人是苏共中央委员会书记赫鲁晓夫；
        （二）苏共中央检查委员会报告——报告人是检查委员会主席莫斯卡托夫；
        （三）苏共第二十次代表大会对第六个五年计划（1956—1960年）的指示——报告人是苏联部长会议
              主席布尔加宁；
        （四）党中央机构的选举。
    接着，赫鲁晓夫就第一项议程作了报告，全体到会的人都向他热烈鼓掌。
