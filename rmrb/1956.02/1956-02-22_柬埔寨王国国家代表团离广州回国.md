Title: National Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia Departs Guangzhou for Home

Author:

Date: 1956-02-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, February 21st. The national delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia, headed by Prince Norodom Sihanouk, left Guangzhou by plane this morning to return home.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Tao Zhu, Governor of Guangdong Province, Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou, General Huang Yongsheng, Commander of the Guangzhou Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, and more than 2,000 people from all walks of life in Guangzhou.

The Soviet and Polish consuls in Guangzhou also came to see the distinguished guests off.

The national flags of Cambodia and China were hung at the airport.

At 7:40 a.m., Prince Sihanouk arrived at the airport by car from the Yingbin Hotel. At this moment, applause and cheers resounded throughout the airport. Welcomers threw colored paper towards the distinguished guests. Many gorgeously dressed young women ran out of the welcoming crowd and presented bouquets of flowers to Prince Sihanouk.

Prince Sihanouk frequently waved to the welcoming crowd.

After the band played the national anthems of Cambodia and China, Prince Sihanouk, accompanied by Tao Zhu, Governor of Guangdong Province, reviewed the guard of honor.

Then, Prince Sihanouk boarded the plane and bid farewell to the cheering and applauding send-off party at the cabin door. The plane left Guangzhou at 8 a.m.

### 柬埔寨王国国家代表团离广州回国

1956-02-22
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨王国国家代表团离广州回国
    新华社广州21日电　以诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王为首的柬埔寨王国国家代表团今天上午乘飞机离开广州回国。
    到机场欢送的，有广东省省长陶铸、广州市市长朱光、中国人民解放军广州驻军首长黄永胜上将、中华人民共和国外交部礼宾司司长柯华，以及广州各界人民两千多人。
    苏联和波兰驻广州领事也前来为贵宾们送行。
    机场上悬挂着柬中两国国旗。
    7时40分，西哈努克亲王乘车从迎宾馆来到机场。这时候，机场上掌声和欢呼声响成一片。欢送者把彩色纸片向贵宾们撒去。许多穿着盛装的女青年从欢送队伍中跑出来把大束鲜花献给西哈努克亲王。
    西哈努克亲王频频向欢送的人群招手致意。
    在乐队奏柬中两国国歌以后，西哈努克亲王由广东省省长陶铸陪同，检阅了仪仗队。
    然后，西哈努克亲王登上飞机，在机舱口，向着欢呼鼓掌的送行者道别。飞机在8时离开广州。
