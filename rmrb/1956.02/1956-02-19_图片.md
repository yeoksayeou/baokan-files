Title: Increased Production at the Joint State-Private Chongqing Guanshengyuan Food Company Cannery
Author:
Date: 1956-02-19
Page: 1

The output of the Joint State-Private Chongqing Guanshengyuan Food Company Cannery has increased by more than 20% compared to before the joint venture. Its meat canned goods are sold in Tibet, Chongqing, and Xi'an, and a large quantity is also exported.
