Title: Public Rallies Held Across India Protesting US Connivance with Syngman Rhee's Sabotage of the Korean Armistice

Author:

Date: 1953-07-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 30th - People across India, responding to the call of the All-India Peace Council, held rallies on the third anniversary of the Korean War, protesting the US connivance with Syngman Rhee's sabotage of the Korean armistice and demanding peace in Korea.

    The Delhi and East Punjab Peace Committees separately organized public rallies on the 25th to commemorate "Korea Day." These rallies strongly condemned the US connivance with Syngman Rhee's actions to sabotage the Korean armistice, thereby obstructing all peace efforts. Participants in the rallies declared that such conspiracies must not be allowed to succeed.

    The Delhi State Peace Committee held a public rally on the 27th. The meeting passed a resolution demanding an immediate armistice in Korea. The meeting was chaired by Rana Jang Bahadur Singh, a member of the Delhi State Legislative Assembly. The resolution condemned the US government and the UN Command for condoning Syngman Rhee's conspiratorial threat to undermine peace in Korea, and demanded the immediate return of the "released" prisoners of war, adherence to the agreement between the two sides on the repatriation of prisoners of war, and the swift signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement.

    Meanwhile, public rallies were also held in Tahora Dun City in Uttar Pradesh and Amritsar City in East Punjab, under the auspices of the Peace Committees of Uttar Pradesh and East Punjab, respectively. The rally in Amritsar passed a resolution condemning Syngman Rhee's provocative actions to sabotage the Korean armistice with the connivance of the UN Command. The resolution demanded full implementation of the agreement on the repatriation of prisoners of war.

### 印度各地纷纷举行公众集会  抗议美国纵容李承晚破坏朝鲜停战

1953-07-01
第4版()
专栏：

    印度各地纷纷举行公众集会
    抗议美国纵容李承晚破坏朝鲜停战
    【新华社新德里三十日电】印度各地人民响应全印和平理事会的号召，在朝鲜战争三周年的时候举行集会，抗议美国纵容李承晚破坏朝鲜停战，要求实现朝鲜和平。
    德里和东旁遮普省的和平委员会在二十五日分别组织了纪念“朝鲜日”的公众集会，这些集会严厉斥责美国纵容李承晚破坏朝鲜停战从而阻挠一切和平努力的行为。参加集会的人宣布：决不许这种阴谋得到成功。
    德里邦和平委员会于二十七日举行了一次公众集会。会上通过了要求立即实现朝鲜停战的决议。集会由德里邦立法会议议员拉纳·詹格·巴哈杜尔·辛格担任主席。决议谴责美国政府和联合国军司令部纵容李承晚实现他的破坏朝鲜和平的威胁阴谋，并要求立即追回被“释放”的战俘，遵守双方关于遣返战俘问题的协议，迅速签订朝鲜停战协定。
    同时，北方省台霍拉登市以及东旁遮普省阿姆利则市也分别在北方省和东旁遮普省的和平委员会的主持下举行了公众集会。阿姆利则市的集会通过了一项决议，谴责李承晚在联合国军司令部纵容下采取的破坏朝鲜停战的挑衅行为。决议要求彻底履行关于遣返战俘的协议。
