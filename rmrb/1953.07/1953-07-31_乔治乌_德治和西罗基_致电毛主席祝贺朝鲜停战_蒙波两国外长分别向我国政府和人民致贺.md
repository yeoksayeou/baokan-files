Title: Gheorghiu-Dej and Široký Send Telegrams to Chairman Mao Congratulating Him on the Korean Armistice; Mongolian and Polish Foreign Ministers Send Congratulations to Our Government and People Separately
Author:
Date: 1953-07-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of China received a telegram from Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic. The text of the telegram is as follows:
Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:
	As the Korean Armistice Agreement is signed and the Korean War ends, the Romanian people extend warm congratulations to the great Chinese people.
	This event is a great victory for the heroic Korean people and the brave Chinese People's Volunteers in defending world peace.
	The Romanian people, infinitely loyal to the cause of peace, hope that the Chinese people will achieve further success in the cause of consolidating this great victory: this victory is a major contribution to the cause of consolidating world peace.
	Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej
	Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic
	Bucharest, July 30, 1953

Xinhua News Agency, 30th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of China received a telegram from Czechoslovak Premier Viliam Široký. The text of the telegram is as follows:
Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:
	All the people of Czechoslovakia have learned with satisfaction and pleasure of the signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement, which is a great victory in the struggle for peace in the Far East and the world. This historic event was made possible by the efforts of the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, supported by the people of all countries. On behalf of the government and all the people of Czechoslovakia, the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic hereby extends its sincere congratulations to the government of the People's Republic of China.
	Viliam Široký
	Premier of the Czechoslovak Republic
	Prague, July 29, 1953

Xinhua News Agency, 30th, Ulaanbaatar: Mongolian Foreign Minister Jalkhasuren Raidan on the 28th met with the Chargé d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Mongolia and congratulated the leaders of our Central People's Government on the Korean Armistice on behalf of the Mongolian people and government.
	Jalkhasuren Raidan said during the meeting with Chen Ying, Chargé d'affaires of our Embassy in Mongolia: Under the firm efforts of the Chinese and Korean people and the support of peace-loving people all over the world, the Korean Armistice Agreement was finally signed yesterday. The Mongolian people and government feel extremely happy and encouraged by this. To this end, Prime Minister Tsedenbal has extended his heartfelt congratulations to Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai and hopes to convey them on his behalf.

Xinhua News Agency, 30th, Warsaw: Polish Foreign Minister Skrzeszewski met with the Chargé d'affaires of our Embassy in Poland on the 27th and congratulated our Central People's Government and the people of the whole country on the Korean Armistice on behalf of the Polish government and people.
	Skrzeszewski said during the meeting with Yang Qiliang, Chargé d'affaires of our Embassy in Poland: "Please convey to the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people the infinite joy of the Polish government and people at the signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement today. Poland will make every effort to enable the Korean Armistice Agreement to be implemented as soon as possible and smoothly, in accordance with its government's repeated expression of unwavering commitment to peace, and thereby contribute to the consolidation of peace in the Far East and the world."

### 乔治乌—德治和西罗基  致电毛主席祝贺朝鲜停战  蒙波两国外长分别向我国政府和人民致贺

1953-07-31
第1版()
专栏：

    乔治乌—德治和西罗基
    致电毛主席祝贺朝鲜停战
    蒙波两国外长分别向我国政府和人民致贺
    【新华社三十日讯】我中央人民政府毛泽东主席收到罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席格·乔治乌—德治的来电。电文如下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东同志：
    当朝鲜停战协定签订和朝鲜战争终止之际，罗马尼亚人民谨向伟大的中国人民致以热烈的祝贺。
    这一事件是英雄的朝鲜人民和勇敢的中国人民志愿军保卫世界和平的巨大胜利。
    对和平事业无限忠诚的罗马尼亚人民希望中国人民在巩固这个巨大胜利的事业中获得进一步的成就：这个胜利对巩固世界和平的事业是一个重大的贡献。
    罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席
        格·乔治乌—德治
        一九五三年七月三十日于布加勒斯特
    【新华社三十日讯】我中央人民政府毛泽东主席收到捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理西罗基的来电。电文如下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东同志：
    捷克斯洛伐克全体人民以满意和愉快的心情获悉了朝鲜停战协定签订的消息，停战协定的签订是争取远东和世界和平的斗争中的巨大胜利。这一具有历史意义的事件之所以成为可能，是由于获得各国人民支持的苏联、中华人民共和国与朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的努力。捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府借此谨以政府和捷克斯洛伐克全体人民的名义，向中华人民共和国政府致以真诚的祝贺。
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理西罗基
    一九五三年七月二十九日于布拉格
    【新华社三十日讯】乌兰巴托讯：蒙古人民共和国外交部部长贾尔卡赛汗二十八日接见我国驻蒙古大使馆临时代办，代表蒙古人民和政府向我中央人民政府领袖祝贺朝鲜停战。
    贾尔卡赛汗在接见我驻蒙古大使馆临时代办陈应时说：在中朝人民的坚定努力和全世界爱好和平人民的拥护下，朝鲜停战协定终于在昨天签字。蒙古人民和政府对此感到极大的欢欣和鼓舞。为此，泽登巴尔总理特向毛泽东主席、周恩来总理致以衷心的祝贺，希代为转达。
    【新华社三十日讯】华沙讯：波兰人民共和国外交部部长斯克热歇夫斯基二十七日接见我驻波兰人民共和国大使馆临时代办，代表波兰政府和人民向我中央人民政府和全国人民祝贺朝鲜停战。
    斯克热歇夫斯基在接见我驻波大使馆临时代办杨琪良时说：“请将波兰政府和人民对今日所举行的朝鲜停战协定签字所表示的无限愉快的心情，转达给中华人民共和国中央人民政府和中国人民。波兰将本着它的政府多次表示过的对和平信守不渝的愿望，尽一切力量来使朝鲜停战协定能够尽快地、顺利地实现，并以此作为它对巩固远东和全世界和平的贡献。”
