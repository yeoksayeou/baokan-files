Title: Albanian Labor Party, Council of Ministers and Presidium of the National Assembly Hold Joint Meeting, Adopt Resolution on Reorganizing Government Agencies, Enver Hoxha Reelected Chairman of the Council of Ministers
Author:
Date: 1953-07-27
Page: 4

    [Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 26th dispatch] According to a TASS dispatch from Tirana on the 25th, the Albanian Telegraph Agency reported: The Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania, and the Presidium of the National Assembly held a joint meeting on July 23rd. At the meeting, a resolution on reorganizing government agencies was adopted. In order to streamline the central state administrative agencies, reduce their administrative expenses, and make it easier to conduct effective and direct leadership, the meeting decided to abolish the Presidium of the Council of Ministers, merge several ministries, and have ten ministries in charge of managing all state affairs. The meeting also decided to establish a State Planning Commission and a State Control Commission under the Council of Ministers; the State Control Commission is based on the original Ministry of Supervision. Enver Hoxha was appointed Chairman of the Council of Ministers; Mehmet Shehu was appointed Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Internal Affairs; Hysni Kapo was appointed Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Agriculture and Procurement; Behar Shtylla was appointed Minister of Foreign Affairs; Major General Beqir Balluku was appointed Minister of Defense; Spiro Koleka was appointed Minister of Industry and Construction; Gogo Nushi was appointed Minister of Trade and Transportation; Bedri Spahiu was appointed Minister of Education and Culture; Tuk Jakova was appointed Minister of Finance; Bilbil Klosi was appointed Minister of Justice; Midad Shuteriqi was appointed Minister of Health; Koço Theodhosi was appointed Chairman of the State Planning Commission; Shefqet Peci was appointed Chairman of the State Control Commission.

### 阿尔巴尼亚劳动党、部长会议和国民议会主席团开联席会议  通过关于改组政府机构的决议  恩维尔·霍查连任部长会议主席

1953-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党、部长会议和国民议会主席团开联席会议
    通过关于改组政府机构的决议
    恩维尔·霍查连任部长会议主席
    【新华社二十六日讯】据塔斯社地拉那二十五日讯，阿尔巴尼亚通讯社消息：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议及国民议会主席团，在七月二十三日举行了联席会议。会上通过了一项关于改组政府机构的决议。为了精简中央国家行政机构，以便减少其行政费用，使它更加便于进行有效的和直接的领导，会议决定撤销部长会议主席团，合并若干部，由十个部掌管管理国家的全部事务。会议并决定在部长会议下设立国家计划委员会和国家监察委员会；国家监察委员会以原有的监察部为基础。任命恩维尔·霍查为部长会议主席；穆罕默德·谢胡为部长会议副主席兼内务部长；希斯尼·卡博为部长会议副主席兼农业和采购部长；贝哈尔·什图拉为外交部长；贝基尔·巴卢库少将为国防部长；斯皮罗·科列加为工业和建筑部长；果戈·努什为贸易和交通部长；伯德里·斯巴修为教育文化部长；图克·雅科瓦为财政部长；比尔比尔·克洛西为司法部长；米达尔·什图拉为卫生部长；科乔·蒂奥多西为国家计划委员会主席；谢夫克特·佩奇为国家监察委员会主席。
