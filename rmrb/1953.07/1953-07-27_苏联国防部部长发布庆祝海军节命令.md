Title: Soviet Minister of Defense Issues Order Celebrating Navy Day
Author:
Date: 1953-07-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 26th, TASS dispatch: The Soviet Minister of Defense issued the following order celebrating Navy Day:

	Comrades, sailors, warrant officers, and non-commissioned officers!
	Comrades, officers and generals!

	Today, the Soviet people and their armed forces celebrate Soviet Navy Day.

	On this Navy Day, I extend my greetings and congratulations to you, and wish the Soviet Navy soldiers and shipbuilding industry workers new achievements in strengthening the Soviet state's naval power and improving their combat readiness.

	In commemoration of Soviet Navy Day, I order:

	Today, July 26, 1953, warships of all fleets and coastal artillery shall fire a twenty-one gun salute each.

	Long live the Soviet Navy!
	Long live the Soviet people and their heroic armed forces!
	Long live the Soviet government!
	Long live the Communist Party of the Soviet Union!

	Soviet Minister of Defense, Marshal of the Soviet Union
	N.A. Bulganin

### 苏联国防部部长发布庆祝海军节命令

1953-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联国防部部长发布庆祝海军节命令
    【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十六日电：苏联国防部部长发布庆祝海军节的命令如下：
    海军战士、尉官和士官同志们！
    校官和将军同志们！
    今天，苏联人民和他们的武装部队庆祝苏联海军节。
    在这海军节的时候，我谨向你们致意和祝贺，并祝苏联海军战士和造船工业工作人员在加强苏维埃国家的海军力量和提高他们的战斗准备方面，获得新的成就。
    为了纪念苏联海军节，我命令：
    今天，一九五三年七月二十六日，各舰队的军舰及海岸大炮各鸣放礼炮二十一响。
    苏联海军万岁！
    苏联人民和他们的英勇的武装部队万岁！
    苏联政府万岁！
    苏联共产党万岁！
    苏联国防部部长、苏联元帅
    尼·亚·布尔加宁
