Title: Choe Yong-gon Appointed Vice Premier of the North Korean Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1953-07-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 5th—The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued a decree on the 4th, appointing Choe Yong-gon, Vice Marshal and Minister of National Defense of the Cabinet, as Vice Premier of the Cabinet.

### 崔庸健任朝鲜内阁副首相

1953-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

    崔庸健任朝鲜内阁副首相
    【新华社平壤五日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会四日发布政令，任命内阁民族保卫相崔庸健次帅为内阁副首相。
