Title: Chinese Red Cross Society Hosts Banquet for "Chinese Martyrs' Remains Escort Group" and Others

Author:
Date: 1953-07-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 9th: The President of the Chinese Red Cross Society, Li Dequan, and Vice Presidents Peng Zemin and Hu Lansheng hosted a banquet on the evening of the 9th to welcome the visiting Japanese "Chinese Martyrs' Remains Escort Group" and the "Patriotic Overseas Chinese Remains Escort Group Returning Martyrs’ Souls from Japan." Among those attending the banquet were Liao Chengzhi, Wu Yunfu from the Chinese People's Relief Association, Zhao Puchu and Guo Peng from the Buddhist Association of China, Zhu Shilun from the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Jin Ziming from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Liao Mengxing from the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the writer Han Zi, and Zhuang Xiquan and Peng Guanghan from the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, among others. During the banquet, President Li Dequan delivered a speech, thanking the Japanese democratic organizations, peace-loving people, and patriotic overseas Chinese in Japan for their efforts, and expressed the hope that they would continue to assist in sending the remains of Chinese anti-Japanese martyrs back to China.

### 中国红十字会总会设宴招待“中国人殉难者遗骨护送团”等

1953-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中国红十字会总会设宴招待“中国人殉难者遗骨护送团”等
    【新华社九日讯】中国红十字会总会会长李德全、副会长彭泽民、胡兰生在九日晚设宴招待抵京的日本“中国人殉难者遗骨护送团”和“殉难英灵留日华侨护送团”。出席宴会作陪的有廖承志，中国人民救济总会伍云甫，中国佛教协会赵朴初、郭朋，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会朱世纶，中华全国总工会金子明，中华全国民主妇女联合会廖梦醒，作家菡子，中央人民政府华侨事务委员会庄希泉、彭光涵等多人。席间李德全会长致词，感谢日本民主团体及爱好和平人士和爱国旅日华侨的努力，并望他们继续协助将中国抗日烈士遗骨送回中国。
