Title: The Friendship and Unity of All Nationalities in the Soviet Union is Unshakable; Party Organizations in Various Republics and Regions Continue to Meet, Supporting the Decisions of the CPSU Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1953-07-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th: TASS reports: Communist Party Central Committees of the Soviet Union's constituent republics and local Party organizations continue to hold plenary sessions with Party activists participating, discussing the decisions of the CPSU Central Committee Plenary Session and the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet regarding Beria. Speakers at the meetings unanimously denounced the crimes of the traitor Beria and adopted resolutions endorsing the decisions of the CPSU Central Committee Plenary Session.

The plenary session of the Moldavian Republic Communist Party Central Committee, held jointly with Party activists, expressed great satisfaction with the timely and decisive measures taken by the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee to expose Beria's crimes. Speakers at the meeting pointed out that at numerous mass meetings held in Moldavian enterprises, collective farms, and state farms, working people unanimously denounced Beria, the enemy who attempted to undermine the Party and the Soviet people of the socialist motherland. The Moldavian people assured the CPSU Central Committee and the Soviet government that they are loyal to the cause of communist construction and determined to contribute all their strength to the victorious realization of the Fifth Five-Year Plan.

The Lithuanian Republic Communist Party Central Committee held a plenary session in a completely unanimous atmosphere. Speakers in the discussion pointed out that under the leadership of the Party, in the friendly family of all fraternal nationalities, Lithuania has transformed from an economically backward and imperialistically enslaved country into a prosperous industrial and collective farm country. Kaznauskas Kaige, Secretary of the Kaunas City Party Committee, said, "We will never surrender these historical victories to anyone. The working class of Lithuania and the entire working people of the republic will tirelessly strengthen and develop the friendship with the great Russian people and the people of all nationalities of the Soviet Union."

The Georgian Republic Communist Party Central Committee and the Tbilisi City Committee held a joint plenary session with Party activists participating. Gegeshidze, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Tbilisi City Soviet of Working People's Deputies, said at the meeting that the Georgian people will forever link their destiny with the great Russian people. The Georgian people are endlessly grateful for the enormous and selfless assistance given by the Russian people. No dark forces, no agents of foreign imperialism, can shake the unity and friendship of all nationalities of the Soviet Union, nor can they shake the Georgian people's love and trust for their elder brother—the great Russian people.

The Karelian-Finnish Republic Communist Party Central Committee and the Petrozavodsk City Committee also held a joint plenary session with Party activists participating. The meeting reflected the will of the entire Party organization of the republic, unanimously supporting the measures taken by the CPSU Central Committee to liquidate Beria's anti-Party and anti-state crimes.

### 苏联各族人民的友谊和团结是不可动摇的  各加盟共和国及各地党组织继续集会拥护苏共中央的决定

1953-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联各族人民的友谊和团结是不可动摇的
    各加盟共和国及各地党组织继续集会拥护苏共中央的决定
    【新华社十六日讯】  据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联各加盟共和国共产党中央委员会和各地党组织，继续举行有党的活动分子参加的全体会议，讨论苏共中央全体会议和最高苏维埃主席团关于贝利亚的决定。在会上发言的人，一致痛斥叛徒贝利亚的罪行，并通过决议拥护苏共中央全体会议的决定。
    摩尔达维亚共和国共产党中央委员会会同党活动分子举行的全体会议，对苏共中央委员会主席团为揭发贝利亚的罪行所采取的及时和坚决的措施，表示莫大的欣慰。在会上发言的人都指出，在摩尔达维亚的各企业、集体农庄和国营农场中举行的许多群众大会上，劳动人民一致痛斥企图破坏社会主义祖国的党和苏联人民的敌人贝利亚。摩尔达维亚人民向苏共中央委员会和苏联政府保证：他们忠诚于共产主义建设事业，决心为胜利地实现第五个五年计划而贡献出一切力量。
    立陶宛共和国共产党中央委员会在完全一致的气氛中举行了全体会议。讨论中发言的人指出，在党的领导下，在各兄弟民族友好的大家庭里，立陶宛已经由经济落后的和受帝国主义奴役的国家，变成
    一个繁荣的工业、集体农庄的国家。考那斯市党委会书记卡兹纳乌斯卡伊吉说，我们永远不会把这些历史性的胜利交给任何人。立陶宛的工人阶级和共和国的全体劳动人民，将不倦地加强和发展与伟大的俄罗斯人民以及苏联各族人民的友谊。
    格鲁吉亚共和国共产党中央委员会和第比利斯市委员会，举行了有党活动分子参加的联合全体会议。第比利斯市劳动人民代表苏维埃执行委员会主席格格什泽在会上说，格鲁吉亚人民永远把自己的命运与伟大的俄罗斯人民联系在一起。格鲁吉亚人民对俄罗斯人民给予的巨大和无私的帮助，是感谢不尽的。任何黑暗势力、任何外国帝国主义的代理人，都不能动摇苏联各族人民的团结和友谊，不能动摇格鲁吉亚人民对他们的老大哥——伟大的俄罗斯人民的热爱和信任。
    卡累利阿—芬兰共和国共产党中央委员会和彼得罗萨佛德斯克市委会，也举行了有党活动分子参加的联合全体会议。会议反映了共和国整个党组织的意志，一致拥护苏共中央委员会为清算贝利亚的反党、反国家的罪行所采取的措施。
