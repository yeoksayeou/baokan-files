Title: China Youth Delegation Leaves Beijing for Romania
Author:
Date: 1953-07-08
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, July 7th: The Chinese Youth Delegation, which will participate in the Third World Youth Congress and the Fourth World Youth and Students Festival for Peace and Friendship to be held in Bucharest, the capital of the Romanian People's Republic, left Beijing by train on the morning of the 7th, led by Deputy Heads of Delegation Liu Daosheng and Qu Tangliang (Head of Delegation Hu Yaobang will depart later due to other matters).
    The delegation consists of a total of 415 people, representing young people of various democratic classes from different regions, ethnic groups, occupations, and religious beliefs in China. Among them are young labor models from the industrial and agricultural production fronts, young combat heroes from the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, young people from ethnic minorities, young people from religious circles, youth workers, and a youth art troupe consisting of 240 people (including a choir, a classical song and dance drama troupe, an orchestra, and an acrobatic troupe), as well as a sports delegation that left Beijing on June 30th.
    On the 7th, those who went to the station to see them off included: Qian Sanqiang and Wu Han, Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth; Rong Gaotang, Luo Yi, Zhang Ze, and Hu Keshi, Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League; and Guo Zuowen, Ambassador of the Romanian People's Republic to China, and Counselor Xilaide, along with six others.
    According to Xinhua News Agency: Chinese youth have prepared many exquisite and beautiful gifts for young friends from all countries participating in the Third World Youth Congress and the Fourth World Youth and Students Festival for Peace and Friendship. These gifts have been taken abroad by the Chinese Youth Delegation.
    The Chinese youth have prepared 60 large boxes of gifts for young people from various countries. Among them are embroidered portraits of the great revolutionary leaders Lenin and Stalin, embroidered portraits of Chairman Mao Zedong, the great leader of the Chinese people, a woven portrait of Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej specially for Romanian youth, silk scarves woven with peace doves and patterns from Dunhuang murals, and many world-renowned Chinese specialties such as cloisonné, lacquerware, and carpets. Particularly touching are the many precious gifts carefully made by the youth of many troops, factories, mines, and schools. Young soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army also used captured American aggressor's shell casings to make vases with exquisite patterns and lamps made of parachutes as gifts for young people from various countries.
    These gifts express the enthusiasm of Chinese youth in building and working in their happy motherland, as well as their deep friendship with peace-loving young friends from all countries.

### 我国青年代表团离京前往罗马尼亚

1953-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我国青年代表团离京前往罗马尼亚
    【新华社七日讯】参加即将在罗马尼亚人民共和国京城布加勒斯特举行的第三届世界青年代表大会和第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的中国青年代表团，由副团长刘导生、区棠亮率领（团长胡耀邦因事稍迟启程），在七日上午乘车离京。
    代表团全体共四百一十五人，他们代表着我国不同地区、不同民族、不同职业和不同宗教信仰的各民主阶级的青年，其中有工业、农业生产战线上的青年劳动模范，中国人民解放军和中国人民志愿军的青年战斗英雄，少数民族青年，宗教界青年，青年工作者以及一个由二百四十人组成的青年艺术团（其中包括合唱队、古典歌舞剧队、乐队、杂技队）和已于六月三十日离京出国的体育代表队。
    七日，前往车站欢送的有：中华全国民主青年联合会副主席钱三强、吴晗，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记荣高棠、罗毅、章泽、胡克实及罗马尼亚人民共和国驻华大使郭佐文，参赞席拉德等六人。
    据新华社讯：中国青年为参加第三届世界青年代表大会和第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的各国青年朋友们，准备了许多精致美好的礼物。这些礼物已由中国青年代表团携带出国。
    中国青年准备送给各国青年的礼品共有六十大箱，其中有伟大革命领袖列宁、斯大林的绣像，有中国人民的伟大领袖毛泽东主席的绣像，有专为送给罗马尼亚青年的乔治乌—德治的织锦像，有织着和平鸽和敦煌壁画花纹的丝织头巾，还有很多驰名世界的中国特产，如景泰蓝、漆器、地毯等。特别感人的是许多部队、工厂、矿山、学校的青年们亲手精心制造的许多珍贵的礼品。中国人民志愿军青年战士们还用缴获的美国侵略者的炮弹壳制成了镂刻着精美花纹的花瓶和用降落伞制成的台灯，作为送给各国青年们的礼物。
    这些礼物表达了中国青年在自己幸福的祖国里建设和劳动的热情，以及他们对于各国爱好和平的青年朋友们深切的友谊。
