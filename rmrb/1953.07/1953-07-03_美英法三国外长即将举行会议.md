Title: Meeting of Foreign Ministers of the United States, Britain, and France to be Held Soon
Author:
Date: 1953-07-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, June 1st: The U.S. State Department announced on June 30th that the foreign ministers of the United States, Britain, and France are scheduled to meet in Washington on July 10th.

### 美英法三国外长即将举行会议

1953-07-03
第4版()
专栏：

    美英法三国外长即将举行会议
    【新华社一日讯】华盛顿消息：美国国务院在六月三十日宣布：美、英、法三国外交部长定于七月十日在华盛顿举行会议。
