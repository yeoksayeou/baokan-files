Title: Korean Cabinet Decides to Have Holiday on the 28th to Commemorate the Conclusion of the Armistice Agreement
Author:
Date: 1953-07-29
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 28th: The Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued an instruction stating: The Korean people waged a heroic three-year struggle against the armed aggression of the American imperialist aggressors and their lackey, the traitorous Syngman Rhee gang, to defend the freedom and independence of the fatherland. As a result of this struggle, the enemy and we concluded an armistice agreement, and we achieved a glorious victory.
    In order to commemorate this victory, the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea has decided to designate July 28th of this year as a day of rest, and the national flag will be hung throughout the country on this day.
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 28th: The Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued an announcement stating: In view of the realization of the armistice, from July 28, 1953, the nighttime blackout, which has been implemented since the beginning of the war to deal with enemy air raids, will be lifted; at the same time, the restrictions on nighttime curfew hours, which were implemented to maintain wartime social order and public safety, will be lifted.

### 朝鲜内阁决定廿八日放假纪念缔结停战协定  内务省公告解除灯火管制和夜间通行时间的限制

1953-07-29
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜内阁决定廿八日放假纪念缔结停战协定
    内务省公告解除灯火管制和夜间通行时间的限制
    【新华社平壤二十八日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁发布指示说：朝鲜人民为反对美帝国主义武装侵略者及其走狗李承晚卖国匪帮，为保卫祖国自由独立，进行了三年的英勇斗争。这种斗争的结果，使敌人和我们缔结了停战协定，我们取得了光辉的胜利。
    为了纪念这个胜利，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁决定将今年七月二十八日订为休息日，这一天，在全国各地悬挂国旗。
    【新华社平壤二十八日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内务省发布公告说：鉴于停战实现，从一九五三年七月二十八日起，解除战争开始以来为对付敌人的空袭而实施的夜间灯火管制；同时解除为维持战时社会秩序和人民安全而实施的夜间通行时间限制。
