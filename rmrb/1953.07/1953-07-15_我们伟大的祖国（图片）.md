    Our Great Motherland
    The worker masses of private factories have made great efforts to complete the state's processing and ordering tasks. This is the Shanghai Private Public Electric Motor Factory. They are launching a labor competition to strive to overfulfill the task of assembling 75-horsepower high-voltage motors.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    私营工厂的工人群众，为完成国家加工订货的任务，做了很大的努力。这是上海私营公用电机制造厂的工人。他们正展开劳动竞赛，争取超额完成装配七十五匹马力高压电动机的任务。
