Title: Calcutta India-China Friendship Association Holds New China Photo Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1953-07-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, July 2nd: The Calcutta India-China Friendship Association held a photo exhibition of the People's Republic of China on June 25th and 26th, introducing the achievements of New China.

    Those who attended the opening ceremony of the exhibition included Dr. Ahmed, Minister of Agriculture of West Bengal Province, Dr. Suniti Kumar Chatterji, Speaker of the Legislative Assembly of West Bengal Province, Liu Yufeng, Consul General of the People's Republic of China in Calcutta, as well as prominent cultural and educational workers and representatives of overseas Chinese in Calcutta.
    The exhibition displayed 150 photographs introducing the tremendous achievements New China has made in industry, agriculture and culture. The exhibition attracted many viewers and was warmly praised by the audience.

### 加尔各答印中友协  举办新中国图片展览会

1953-07-03
第4版()
专栏：

    加尔各答印中友协
    举办新中国图片展览会
    【新华社新德里二日电】加尔各答印中友好协会在六月二十五日和二十六日举办中华人民共和国图片展览会，介绍新中国成就。
    参加展览会开幕仪式的有西孟加拉省农业部长艾哈迈德博士、西孟加拉省立法院议长苏尼蒂·库马尔·查特吉博士、中华人民共和国驻加尔各答总领事柳雨峰以及加尔各答的著名文化教育工作者和华侨代表等。
    展览会陈列了一百五十幅图片，介绍新中国在工业、农业和文化方面所得到的巨大成就。展览会吸引了许多观众，受到观众的热烈赞扬。
