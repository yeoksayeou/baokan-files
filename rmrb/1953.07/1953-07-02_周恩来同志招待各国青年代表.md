Title: Comrade Zhou Enlai Hosts Representatives of Youth from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1953-07-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 1st: Zhou Enlai, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, hosted a banquet on the afternoon of July 1st to entertain the representatives of the Soviet Union's Lenin Communist Youth League, who were invited to China to attend the Second National Congress of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, as well as youth representatives from Korea, Vietnam, Poland, Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, Bulgaria, Mongolia, Japan, Indonesia, Italy, France, the United Kingdom, and Australia.

Accompanying Comrade Zhou Enlai at the reception were Xi Zhongxun, Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and more than 30 members of the Presidium of the Second National Congress of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, including Hu Yaobang, Li Chang, Rong Gaotang, Liu Daosheng, and Luo Yi.

At the meeting, Comrade Zhou Enlai delivered a welcoming speech, and Comrade V.E. Semichastny, head of the delegation of the Soviet Union's Lenin Communist Youth League, also delivered a speech of thanks on behalf of the foreign guests present. The hosts and guests frequently raised their glasses to the success of the Second National Congress of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, to the unity and friendship of peace-loving youth around the world, to the consolidation and development of the peace and democratic camp led by the Soviet Union, and to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong.

The reception was filled with an atmosphere of close friendship and affection.

### 周恩来同志招待各国青年代表

1953-07-02
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来同志招待各国青年代表
    【新华社一日讯】中国共产党中央委员会书记周恩来，在七月一日下午设宴招待应邀前来我国参加中国新民主主义青年团第二次全国代表大会的苏联列宁共产主义青年团的代表和朝鲜、越南、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、保加利亚、蒙古、日本、印尼、意大利、法国、英国、澳大利亚的青年代表们。
    出席招待会作陪的，有中国共产党中央委员会宣传部部长习仲勋和中国新民主主义青年团第二次全国代表大会主席团胡耀邦、李昌、荣高棠、刘导生、罗毅等三十余人。
    会上，周恩来同志致词欢迎，苏联列宁共产主义青年团代表团团长弗·埃·谢米察斯特尼也代表到会外宾致答谢词。宾主频频举杯为中国新民主主义青年团第二次全国代表大会的成功，为全世界爱好和平的青年的团结与友谊，为以苏联为首的和平、民主阵营的巩固与发展，为毛泽东主席的健康而干杯。
    招待会洋溢着亲密友爱的空气。
