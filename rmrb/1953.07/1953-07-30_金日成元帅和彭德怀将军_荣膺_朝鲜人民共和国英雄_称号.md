Title: Marshal Kim Il-sung and General Peng Dehuai Awarded the Title of "Hero of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea"
Author:
Date: 1953-07-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 29th: The Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued a decree on the 28th, awarding the title of "Hero of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea" to Marshal Kim Il-sung, Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army, and General Peng Dehuai, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army.

The decree awarding the title of "Hero of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea" to Marshal Kim Il-sung, Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army, states: The Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, in view of the fact that Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army, Marshal Kim Il-sung, in the just war of liberation of the Korean people against the armed aggression of the American imperialist aggressors and in defense of the freedom and independence of the motherland, organized and led the entire people to victory in the war, and with his outstanding command of the heroic Korean People's Army, ensured the glorious victory of our people, hereby awards him the title of "Hero of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea," and simultaneously awards him the First Class National Flag Order and the Gold Star Medal.

The decree awarding the title of "Hero of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea" to General Peng Dehuai, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, states: In the just war of liberation of the fatherland against the armed aggression of American imperialism, the great Chinese people, under the lofty banner of resisting America and aiding Korea, sent the heroic Volunteer Army to Korea to aid our people with their blood, repelled the enemy's offensive, and achieved a glorious victory. The Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, in view of the fact that General Peng Dehuai, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, with his outstanding command of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, established special meritorious service in guaranteeing the victory of our people in our fatherland's war of liberation, hereby awards him the title of "Hero of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea," and simultaneously awards him the First Class National Flag Order and the Gold Star Medal.

### 金日成元帅和彭德怀将军  荣膺“朝鲜人民共和国英雄”称号

1953-07-30
第1版()
专栏：

    金日成元帅和彭德怀将军
    荣膺“朝鲜人民共和国英雄”称号
    【新华社平壤二十九日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会于二十八日发布政令，授予朝鲜人民军最高司令官金日成元帅和中国人民志愿军司令员彭德怀将军以“朝鲜人民共和国英雄”的称号。
    授予朝鲜人民军最高司令官金日成元帅以“朝鲜人民共和国英雄”称号的政令说：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会，鉴于朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相、朝鲜人民军最高司令官金日成元帅，在朝鲜人民为反对美帝国主义武装侵略者和保卫祖国自由独立的正义解放战争中，组织和领导全体人民走向战争胜利，并以对英雄的朝鲜人民军的卓越指挥，保证了我们人民取得光辉的胜利，特授予“朝鲜人民共和国英雄”的称号，同时授予一级国旗勋章和金星勋章。
    授予中国人民志愿军司令员彭德怀将军以“朝鲜人民共和国英雄”称号的政令说：在反对美帝国主义武装侵略者的正义的祖国解放战争中，伟大的中国人民在抗美援朝的崇高旗帜下，派遣了英雄的志愿军到朝鲜来，以鲜血援助我们人民，击破了敌人的进攻，取得了光辉的胜利。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会鉴于中国人民志愿军司令员彭德怀将军在我们的祖国解放战争中，以对中国人民志愿军的卓越指挥，在保障我们人民的胜利中建立了特殊的功勋，特授予“朝鲜人民共和国英雄”称号，同时授予一级国旗勋章和金星勋章。
