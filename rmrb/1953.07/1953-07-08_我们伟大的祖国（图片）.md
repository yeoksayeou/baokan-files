    Our Great Motherland
    The largest water conservancy and irrigation project in Hubei Province, the Chang Canal, has been completed. This canal, located between Nanzhang and Yicheng, is 47 kilometers long and can irrigate 103,000 mu of land. Farmers from the surrounding villages have all come here to visit.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    湖北省最大的水利灌溉工程——长渠，已经完工。这条水渠在南漳和宜城之间，长四十七公里，能灌溉十万三千亩土地。四乡的农民们都赶到这里来参观。
