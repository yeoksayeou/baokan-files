Title: Molotov Sends a Telegram to Li Dongjian of the Korean Ministry of Foreign Affairs
Author: 
Date: 1953-07-29
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 28th. TASS, Moscow, 28th: V. Molotov, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, sent a telegram to Li Dongjian of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The text of the telegram is as follows: Comrade Li Dongjian, Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea: The signing of the armistice agreement and the end of the war imposed on the Korean people are a great victory for the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the selfless Chinese People's Volunteers, and also the most significant achievement of the camp of peace and democracy. The heroic Korean people, who have made enormous sacrifices to defend their independence and freedom, can now set about solving the task of restoring the unification of their motherland, restoring the damaged cities and villages, and improving their national economy. In all of this, the Korean people will certainly receive the active support and assistance of the Soviet Union and other democratic countries.
    V. Molotov, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Moscow, July 27, 1953

### 莫洛托夫致电朝鲜外务省李东建

1953-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫致电朝鲜外务省李东建
    【新华社二十八日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十八日电：苏联外交部部长维·莫洛托夫致电朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务省李东建。电文如下：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务省李东建同志：停战协定的签字和强加于朝鲜人民的战争的结束，是朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和忘我的中国人民志愿军的一大胜利，同时也是和平民主阵营的最重大的成就。为保卫自己的独立和自由而作了巨大牺牲的英勇的朝鲜人民，现在能够着手解决恢复自己祖国统一的任务，着手恢复受害的城市和乡村，着手提高本国国民经济了。在这一切方面朝鲜人民一定可以得到苏联和其他民主国家的积极支持和帮助。
    苏联外交部部长维·莫洛托夫一九五三年七月二十七日莫斯科
