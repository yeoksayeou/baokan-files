Title: Telegram of Tribute to the Korean People's Army
Author:
Date: 1953-07-30
Page: 4

    Telegram of Tribute to the Korean People's Army
    To Marshal Kim Il-sung, Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army:
    The signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement marks a great victory for the Korean people in their struggle for independence. The representatives of all sectors of the capital, gathered at the celebration rally for the signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement, extend our highest respect and warmest congratulations to you, all the officers and soldiers of the heroic Korean People's Army, and all the people of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.
    For three years, the Korean people and army have waged a heroic and extraordinary struggle, repelling the attacks of the aggressors. Your victory has opened the way for a peaceful resolution of the Korean issue and has given tremendous encouragement to peace-loving people all over the world.
    The Chinese people assure you that we will continue to strengthen the unbreakable friendship forged between the peoples of China and Korea in the struggle against aggression, and we wish the Korean people new victories in their future struggles.
    Celebration Rally of All Circles in the Capital for the Signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement
    July 29, 1953

### 向朝鲜人民军致敬电

1953-07-30
第4版()
专栏：

    向朝鲜人民军致敬电朝鲜人民军最高司令官金日成元帅：
    朝鲜停战协定签字，朝鲜人民在争取独立的斗争中获得了伟大胜利。首都各界庆祝朝鲜停战协定签字大会谨向您和英勇的朝鲜人民军全体将士、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国全体人民致崇高的敬礼和热烈的祝贺。
    三年来，朝鲜军民进行了英勇卓绝的斗争，打退了侵略者的进攻。你们的胜利开辟了和平解决朝鲜问题的道路，给了全世界爱好和平的人民以莫大的鼓舞。
    中国人民向您保证继续加强朝中两国人民在反侵略斗争中结成的牢不可破的友谊，并祝朝鲜人民在今后的斗争中获得新的胜利。
    首都各界庆祝朝鲜停战协定签字大会
    一九五三年七月二十九日
