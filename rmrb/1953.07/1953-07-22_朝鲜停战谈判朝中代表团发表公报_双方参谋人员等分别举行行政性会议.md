Title: Korean Armistice Negotiations: Korean-Chinese Delegation Issues Communiqué; Staff Officers of Both Sides Hold Administrative Meetings
Author:
Date: 1953-07-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, July 21st: The Korean-Chinese delegation to the Korean armistice negotiations issued a communiqué on July 21st: Staff officers, liaison officers, and interpreters of both sides held administrative meetings on July 21st.

### 朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团发表公报  双方参谋人员等分别举行行政性会议

1953-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团发表公报
    双方参谋人员等分别举行行政性会议
    【新华社开城二十一日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团七月二十一日发表公报：双方参谋人员、双方联络官及双方译员七月二十一日分别举行了行政性会议。
