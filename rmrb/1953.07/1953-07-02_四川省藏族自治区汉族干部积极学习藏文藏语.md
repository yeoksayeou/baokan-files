Title: Han Cadres in Sichuan Tibetan Autonomous Region Actively Study Tibetan Language

Author:

Date: 1953-07-02
Page: 3

Cultural Briefs:

    Han Cadres in Sichuan Tibetan Autonomous Region Actively Study Tibetan Language
    Han cadres in the Sichuan Tibetan Autonomous Region are actively studying the Tibetan language. Cadres from the autonomous region's level organs have formed seven study groups, totaling more than 920 people. Each class has hired Tibetan teachers, and classes are held for two hours each week. Han cadres participating in the study in other regions have also developed study plans and systems. Currently, the seven Tibetan language study classes at the autonomous region level have entered phonetic learning. According to the plan, from learning letters, phonetics, grammar, sentence construction to composition, the course will be completed in nine months. Han cadres' learning of the Tibetan language has received enthusiastic help from Tibetan people and cadres in the autonomous region.

### 四川省藏族自治区汉族干部积极学习藏文藏语

1953-07-02
第3版()
专栏：文化简讯

    四川省藏族自治区汉族干部积极学习藏文藏语
    四川省藏族自治区汉族干部积极学习藏文藏语。自治区一级机关的干部组成了七个学习班，共九百二十多人，每班都聘有藏族教员，每周上课两小时。其他各地参加学习的汉族干部，也都订出了学习计划和制度。现在，自治区一级机关的七个藏文藏语学习班，已进入拼音学习。按计划从学习字母、拼音、文法、造句到作文，将在九个月内学完。汉族干部学习藏文藏语，受到自治区藏族人士和干部的热情帮助。
