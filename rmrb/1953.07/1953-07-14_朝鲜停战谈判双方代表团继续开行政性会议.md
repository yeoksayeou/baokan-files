Title: Korean Armistice Negotiations: Both Sides' Delegations Continue Administrative Meetings
Author:
Date: 1953-07-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, July 13th. The Korean Armistice Negotiations, the Korean-Chinese delegation released a communique on July 13th: The two delegations held an administrative meeting on July 13th. The next meeting is scheduled to be held at 11:00 am on July 14th.

### 朝鲜停战谈判双方代表团继续开行政性会议

1953-07-14
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判双方代表团继续开行政性会议
    【新华社开城十三日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团七月十三日发表公报如下：双方代表团七月十三日举行行政性会议，下一次会议定于七月十四日上午十一时举行。
