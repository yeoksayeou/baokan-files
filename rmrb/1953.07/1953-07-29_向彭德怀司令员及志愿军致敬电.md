Title: Telegram of Respect to Commander Peng Dehuai and the Chinese People's Volunteers

Author: 
Date: 1953-07-29
Page: 1

Telegram of Respect to Commander Peng Dehuai and the Chinese People's Volunteers

To Commander Peng Dehuai of the Chinese People's Volunteers and all commanders, combatants, staff officers, political workers, logistics workers, medical and health workers, cultural and educational workers, and all wounded and sick comrades:

    On the occasion of the signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement, we, on behalf of the people of the whole country, extend our highest respects to you and congratulate you on this significant victory achieved together with the Korean People's Army.

    Dear comrades! For more than two years, you have carried out the will of the people of the motherland and fought bravely and fearlessly alongside the Korean People's Army, making a great contribution to the cause of opposing aggression and defending peace. Now that the Korean Armistice Agreement has been signed and the path to a peaceful resolution of the Korean issue has been opened, the sacred task entrusted to you by the people of the motherland has been gloriously and initially accomplished. The people of the whole country deeply thank you and extend our warm greetings to you.

    The people of the whole country resolutely support the Korean Armistice Agreement, which has been signed by both sides, and are determined to work for its complete realization. The people of the whole country will continue to support you and the Korean People's Army in the unremitting struggle to ensure the Korean armistice and strive for a peaceful resolution of the Korean issue.

    Eternal glory to the martyrs who sacrificed themselves in the War to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea!

    Long live the Chinese People's Volunteers!

    The Chinese People's Committee for Resisting America and Aiding Korea, July 28, 1953

### 向彭德怀司令员及志愿军致敬电

1953-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

    向彭德怀司令员及志愿军致敬电中国人民志愿军彭德怀司令员和全体指挥员、战斗员、参谋工作人员、政治工作人员、后勤工作人员、医疗卫生工作人员、文化教育工作人员以及全体伤病员同志们：
    值此朝鲜停战协定签订之际，我们谨代表全国人民，向你们致崇高的敬礼，并祝贺你们与朝鲜人民军一道所取得的这一重大的胜利。
    亲爱的同志们！两年多以来，你们秉承祖国人民的意志，和朝鲜人民军在一起进行了英勇无畏的斗争，对反对侵略保卫和平的事业作了伟大的贡献。现在朝鲜停战协定已经签订，和平解决朝鲜问题的道路已经打开，祖国人民交付给你们的神圣任务已经光荣地初步实现了。全国人民深切地感谢你们，并向你们致热烈的慰问。
    全国人民坚决地拥护已由双方签字的朝鲜停战协定，并决心为其彻底实现而努力。全国人民将继续支援你们和朝鲜人民军，为保障朝鲜停战和争取和平解决朝鲜问题而不懈地斗争。
    在抗美援朝战争中牺牲的烈士们永垂不朽！
    中国人民志愿军万岁！
    中国人民抗美援朝总会  一九五三年七月二十八日
