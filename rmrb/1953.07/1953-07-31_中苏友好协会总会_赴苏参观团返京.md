Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters Visiting Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-07-31
Page: 1

    Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters
    Visiting Delegation Returns to Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, July 29th: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters visiting delegation, led by Li Furen (团长), with Liao Jingtian and Li Zhanwu as deputy leaders (副团长), consisting of forty-two people, arrived in Changchun on July 17th. They stayed in Changchun for ten days, organizing materials and summarizing their experiences. They returned to Beijing on the 29th. Those who welcomed them at the station included Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters; Li Zhigan, Secretary-General of the North China Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Gao Zhendong, Deputy Secretary-General of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and over fifty others.
    Vassikov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, and Sokolov, representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, also welcomed them at the station.

### 中苏友好协会总会  赴苏参观团返京

1953-07-31
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友好协会总会
    赴苏参观团返京
    【新华社二十九日讯】中苏友好协会总会赴苏参观团团长李敷仁，副团长廖经天、李沾吾等一行四十二人已于七月十七日抵长春，在长春停留十天，整理资料和做总结，于二十九日回到北京，到车站欢迎的有中苏友好协会总会总干事钱俊瑞，华北中苏友好协会总干事李之乾，北京市中苏友好协会副总干事高振东等五十余人。
    苏联驻我国大使馆临时代办华司考，苏联对外文化协会代表索高洛夫等也到站欢迎。
