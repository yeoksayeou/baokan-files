Title: Mongolian People Prepare to Celebrate the 32nd Anniversary of the People's Revolution
Author:
Date: 1953-07-10
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator: TASS reports: July 11th marks the 32nd anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution. Mongolia is making nationwide preparations for the celebration. Various enterprises, state farms, and livestock cooperatives are calculating the results of their pledged labor. In Ulan Bator, the capital of the Mongolian People's Republic, construction workers have achieved great success in the competition to welcome this anniversary. The famous Soviet bricklayer Korolev helped them apply his high-speed bricklaying method. Using this method, Mongolian workers increased the efficiency of bricklaying from 1,000 bricks to 7,000 to 10,000 bricks per shift.
    To celebrate the glorious 32nd anniversary of the revolution, the shoe workers of the Choibalsan United Industrial Factory, the Sukhbaatar Power United Plant, and the workers of the Stalin Meat United Factory have already overfulfilled their semi-annual production targets and achieved new successes. Livestock cooperatives and state farms have made great progress in animal husbandry.

### 蒙古人民筹备庆祝人民革命三十二周年

1953-07-10
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民筹备庆祝人民革命三十二周年
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社乌兰巴托讯：七月十一日是蒙古人民革命三十二周年纪念日。蒙古全国正在筹备庆祝。各个企业、国营农场和畜牧合作社都在计算它们曾经提出保证的劳动成果。在蒙古人民共和国的首都乌兰巴托，建筑工人在迎接这一纪念日的竞赛里，获得了很大的成就。著名的苏联砌砖工人科罗列夫，帮助他们应用他的高速砌砖法。蒙古工人用了这种方法，使每班砌砖效率由一千块提高到七千至一万块。
    为了庆祝光荣的革命三十二周年纪念日，乔巴山联合工业工厂的制鞋工人，苏赫—巴托尔动力联合厂和斯大林肉类联合工厂工人们已经超额完成了半年生产目标，并且获得了新的成就。畜牧合作社和国营农场，在畜牧业方面有了很大的进步。
