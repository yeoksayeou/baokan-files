Title: Song Qingling, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's National Committee for the Defense of Children, Donates Children's Welfare Fund
Author:
Date: 1953-07-24
Page: 3

Song Qingling, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's National Committee for the Defense of Children, has always been concerned about children's welfare. She recently donated 52.8 million yuan in RMB from the royalties of her famous work "Struggle for New China" to the committee as a children's welfare fund, for the purpose of carrying out children's welfare work in the future.

### 中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会主席  宋庆龄捐赠儿童福利基金

1953-07-24
第3版()
专栏：

    中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会主席
    宋庆龄捐赠儿童福利基金
    中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会宋庆龄主席一贯关怀儿童福利，近将她的名著“为新中国奋斗”稿费人民币五千二百八十万元捐赠给该会作为儿童福利基金，为今后开展儿童福利事业之用。
