Title: Three Resolutions of the Second National Congress of the Communist Youth League

Author:

Date: 1953-07-03
Page: 3

    Three Resolutions of the Second National Congress of the Communist Youth League
    Resolution on the Work Report
    The Second National Congress of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League unanimously approved the work report "Unite the Youth of the Whole Country, and Advance Courageously in the Great Ranks of Building the Motherland" made by Comrade Hu Yaobang. The Congress calls on all members of the League to unite the broad masses of young people under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and strive to realize the tasks put forward in the report.
    Resolution on Amending the League Constitution
    The Second National Congress of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League unanimously approved Comrade Li Chang's "Report on Amending the League Constitution."
    The Congress approved the "Draft Amendment to the Constitution of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League" submitted by the First Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League and amended by the Congress. From the date of its adoption, this charter shall be the official constitution of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League.
    Resolution on Strengthening Sino-Soviet Friendship, Learning from the Soviet Union, and Joining the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association as a Group Member
    Strengthening the friendly cooperation between the people of China and the Soviet Union and learning from the great Soviet people are major political tasks for our people. The Congress unanimously resolved that the Chinese New Democratic Youth League join the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association as a group member, and called on all members of the League to actively promote the movement to learn from the Soviet Union among the broad masses of young people across the country, and to consolidate and develop the greatest friendship between the people and youth of China and the Soviet Union by all means.

### 青年团第二次全国代表大会的三项决议

1953-07-03
第3版()
专栏：

    青年团第二次全国代表大会的三项决议
    关于工作报告的决议
    中国新民主主义青年团第二次全国代表大会一致同意胡耀邦同志所作的工作报告“团结全国青年，在建设祖国的伟大行列中奋勇前进”。大会号召全体团员，在中国共产党的领导下，团结广大青年，为实现报告中所提出的各项任务而奋斗。
    关于修改团章的决议
    中国新民主主义青年团第二次全国代表大会一致同意李昌同志所作的“关于修改团章的报告”。
    大会批准中国新民主主义青年团第一届中央委员会提出的、经大会修正的“中国新民主主义青年团团章修改草案”。从通过之日起，这一章程即为中国新民主主义青年团的正式团章。
    关于加强中苏友好、学习苏联，并参加中苏友好协会为团体会员的决议
    加强中苏两国人民的友好合作和向伟大的苏联人民学习，是我们全国人民的重大政治任务。大会一致决议，中国新民主主义青年团加入中苏友好协会为团体会员，并号召全体团员，在全国广大青年中积极推行学习苏联的运动，用一切办法巩固和发展中苏两国人民和青年之间的最伟大的友谊。
