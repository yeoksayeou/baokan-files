Title: Meeting of the Standing Council of the Sino-Japanese Friendship Association Decides on Activities for "Sino-Japanese Friendship Month"
Author:
Date: 1953-07-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, July 20th: The Sino-Japanese Friendship Association held its second Standing Council meeting in Tokyo on July 6th after the Third National Congress. The meeting discussed and decided on several issues concerning the "Sino-Japanese Friendship Month" to be held in September and October.

The Standing Council decided that the purpose of holding the "Sino-Japanese Friendship Month" is to celebrate the fourth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, and to implement the resolution made at the Third National Congress of the Sino-Japanese Friendship Association, which was to "develop the Sino-Japanese Friendship Association into an organization of the entire people," through various activities. In terms of organizational work, a "Countermeasures Committee for Sino-Japanese Friendship Month" will be formed by representatives of the organization, finance, and culture committees of the Sino-Japanese Friendship Association Council, and an executive committee for the "Sino-Japanese Friendship Month" will be formed by groups such as peace, trade, labor unions, farmers, youth, women, students, overseas Chinese, and Korean residents.

The activities of the "Sino-Japanese Friendship Month" include: 1. Launching a campaign to send people's representatives to China to celebrate the National Day of the People's Republic of China; 2. Holding a Sino-Japanese Friendship Workers' Conference; 3. Holding celebrations for the fourth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China throughout the country; 4. Holding a Sino-Japanese Cultural Exchange Exhibition and a cultural festival; 5. Promoting the movement to invite the Chinese Red Cross to visit Japan.

### 日中友协常任理事会开会  决定有关“日中友好月”的活动内容

1953-07-21
第4版()
专栏：

    日中友协常任理事会开会
    决定有关“日中友好月”的活动内容
    【新华社二十日讯】东京消息：七月六日，日中友好协会在东京召开了第三届全国大会以后的第二次常任理事会。会上讨论并决定了一些有关将在九、十月间举行的“日中友好月”的问题。
    常任理事会决定举行“日中友好月”的目的，是庆祝中华人民共和国建国四周年，并通过各种活动来贯彻日中友协第三届全国大会上所作出的“把日中友好协会发展成为全国人民的组织”的决议。在组织工作方面：将由日中友协理事会的组织、财政、文化等委员会的代表组成“日中友好月对策委员会”，并由和平、贸易、工会、农民、青年、妇女、学生、华侨、朝鲜侨民等团体组成“日中友好月”的执行委员会。
    “日中友好月”的活动内容包括：一、展开派遣人民代表到中国去庆祝中华人民共和国国庆节的运动；二、举行日中友好工人会议；三、在全国各地举行庆祝中华人民共和国建国四周年大会；四、举行日中文化交流展览大会和文化节；五、促进邀请中国红十字会来日本访问的运动。
