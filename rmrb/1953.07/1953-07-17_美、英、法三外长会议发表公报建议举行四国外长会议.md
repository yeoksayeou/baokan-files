Title: U.S., British, and French Foreign Ministers' Meeting Issues Communiqué Suggesting a Four-Power Foreign Ministers' Conference
Author: 
Date: 1953-07-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 16th: TASS Washington, 16th: French Foreign Minister Bidault, British Acting Foreign Secretary Salisbury, and U.S. Secretary of State Dulles held a meeting in Washington from July 10th to 14th. A communiqué was issued after the meeting. The communiqué stated that the meeting discussed many issues of common concern to the three governments. The meeting specifically discussed European issues, including the European Defense Community, and the issues of Germany and Austria. The meeting also studied the Far East issue: the situation in Korea and the positions of the three countries towards China. The current situation in Indochina was also discussed.
    The participants in the meeting decided to suggest holding a meeting of the foreign ministers of France, Britain, the United States, and the Soviet Union to discuss the German issue and the conclusion of a peace treaty with Austria.

### 美、英、法三外长会议发表公报建议举行四国外长会议

1953-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

    美、英、法三外长会议发表公报建议举行四国外长会议
    【新华社十六日讯】  塔斯社华盛顿十六日讯：法国外交部长皮杜尔，英国代理外交大臣萨利斯柏利和美国国务卿杜勒斯于七月十日至十四日在华盛顿举行会议。会后发表了公报。公报中说，会议讨论了许多与三国政府共同有关的问题。会议特别讨论了欧洲问题，包括欧洲防务集团问题，德国和奥地利问题。会议同时研究了远东问题：朝鲜局势，三国对中国的立场。目前印度支那的形势也曾被讨论。
    会议参与者决定建议举行法国、英国、美国和苏联的外交部长会议来讨论德国问题和缔结对奥和约问题。
