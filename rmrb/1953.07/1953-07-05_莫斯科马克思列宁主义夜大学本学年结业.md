Title: Moscow Marxist-Leninist Evening University Graduates This Academic Year
Author:
Date: 1953-07-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 4th: The two-year Marxist-Leninist Evening University, organized by the Moscow City Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, has completed its academic year. A graduation and summary meeting was held on the 2nd. The evening university includes twenty-nine branches, with approximately 16,000 students. Since its establishment, 6,834 students have graduated from the school. Students who obtained "Excellent" and "Good" grades accounted for 90.8 percent of the total. Most of the graduates are cadres from Party, government, and economic departments, as well as scientific workers, teachers, doctors, technicians, and literary and art workers. After studying, the vast majority of students have gained systematic knowledge of the history of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the history of the Soviet Union, political economics, dialectical and historical materialism, Soviet foreign policy, and modern international relations.

### 莫斯科马克思列宁主义夜大学本学年结业

1953-07-05
第4版()
专栏：

    莫斯科马克思列宁主义夜大学本学年结业
    【新华社莫斯科四日电】苏联共产党莫斯科市委员会举办的两年制马克思列宁主义夜大学，已经结束了本学年的课业，在二日举行了学生毕业和总结学习的大会。夜大学包括二十九个分校，共有学员约一万六千人。该校成立以来，已有六千八百三十四个学员毕业。学员中获得“优”和“好”的学习成绩的，占百分之九十点八，毕业生中多半是党、政、经济部门的干部，科学工作者以及教师、医生、技术人员和文艺工作者。绝大多数学员经过学习以后，都获得了关于苏联共产党历史、苏联历史、政治经济学、辩证唯物主义和历史唯物主义、苏联对外政策和现代国际关系等的系统知识。
