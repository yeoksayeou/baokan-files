Title: Military Armistice Commission Established
Author:
Date: 1953-07-28
Page: 1

    Military Armistice Commission Established
    [Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, July 27th] The Korean Armistice Negotiation Delegation of the Korean and Chinese sides issued the following communique at 6:00 PM on July 27th:
    Following the signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement at 10:00 AM on July 27th, both negotiating parties, in accordance with the provisions of the Armistice Agreement, established the Military Armistice Commission and scheduled its first meeting for 11:00 AM on July 28th.

### 军事停战委员会成立

1953-07-28
第1版()
专栏：

    军事停战委员会成立
    【新华社开城二十七日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团七月二十七日下午六时发表公报如下：
    七月二十七日十时朝鲜停战协定签字后，谈判双方根据停战协定的规定成立军事停战委员会，并定于七月二十八日上午十一时举行军事停战委员会第一次会议。
