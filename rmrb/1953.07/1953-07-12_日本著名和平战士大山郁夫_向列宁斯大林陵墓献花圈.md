Title: Prominent Japanese Peace Advocate Ikuo Oyama Lays Wreath at Lenin-Stalin Mausoleum
Author:
Date: 1953-07-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th: TASS reports: Professor Ikuo Oyama, the distinguished Japanese public figure and Stalin International Peace Prize laureate currently visiting the Soviet Union, laid a wreath at the Lenin-Stalin Mausoleum on July 10th. The inscription on the wreath read:

	“To Lenin and Stalin,
	 Respectfully presented by the Japan Peace Committee”

The Japanese Women's Delegation, currently visiting Moscow, was present at the wreath-laying ceremony.

### 日本著名和平战士大山郁夫  向列宁斯大林陵墓献花圈

1953-07-12
第4版()
专栏：

    日本著名和平战士大山郁夫
    向列宁斯大林陵墓献花圈
    【新华社讯】塔斯社莫斯科十日电：现在正在访问苏联的杰出的日本公众领袖、“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金得奖人大山郁夫教授，七月十日在列宁斯大林陵墓前献花圈。花圈的题词如下。
    “献给列宁和斯大林
                日本拥护和平委员会敬献”
    献花圈时在场的有目前正在访问莫斯科的日本妇女代表团。
