	Cultural Briefs
	Central People's Broadcasting Station to broadcast "College Entrance Guidance Program for High School Graduates"
	To help 1953 high school graduates determine their college preferences and prepare for college entrance, the Central People's Broadcasting Station has decided to broadcast a "College Entrance Guidance Program for High School Graduates." The program will air from 8:00 PM to 9:00 PM on July 27th to 31st. The program schedule is as follows:
	July 27th: (1) Zeng Zhaolun, Deputy Chairman of the National College Entrance Examination Committee, will speak on: "Obey the Needs of the Country, Actively Prepare Conditions, and Welcome the College Entrance Examination." (2) Fan Zhongmin, a first-year student in the Department of Political Economics at Peking University, will speak on: "How I Determined My College Preferences and Started My New Studies."
	July 28th: Zhang Jian, Deputy Director of the Office of the National College Entrance Examination Committee, will speak on: "Going to College for the Needs of the Great Construction of the Motherland."
	July 29th: (1) Qian Weichang, Dean of Studies at Tsinghua University, will speak on: "Have You Thought About Why You Want to Apply to an Engineering College?" (2) Sun Xiaocun, President of Beijing Agricultural University, will speak on: "Talking About the Importance of Agricultural and Forestry Undertakings in National Construction."
	July 30th: (1) Ding Haochuan, Dean of Studies at Beijing Normal University, will speak on: "Applying to Teachers' College to Prepare to Undertake the Sacred Task of Cultivating the Next Generation of the Motherland." (2) Xu Zhiqi, a high school graduate from the Affiliated Girls' High School of Beijing Normal University, will speak on: "Learning to Be a People's Teacher." (3) Hou Zhenbiao, a high school graduate from Hebei Beijing High School, will speak on: "Why I Want to Study at Teachers' College."
	July 31st: Qian Sanqiang, Director of the Institute of Modern Physics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, will speak on: "The Importance of Basic Science in the Construction of the Motherland."
		The Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications Issues the "Defend World Peace" Commemorative Stamps for the Third Time
	In order to commemorate the World People's Peace Congress held in Vienna on December 12, 1952, and to commemorate the new achievements in the struggle of the people of the world for peace, the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government will issue the "Defend World Peace" commemorative stamps for the third time on July 25th of this year. The design of this stamp is the third flying dove of peace painted by the famous French painter Picasso for the World People's Peace Congress, which symbolizes that the struggle of the people of the world for peace has reached a higher stage. The stamps are available in three denominations: 800 yuan (purple), 400 yuan (brown), and 250 yuan (green), all of which are horizontal rectangles and finely printed by intaglio printing.

### 文化简讯

1953-07-24
第3版()
专栏：文化简讯

    文化简讯
    中央人民广播电台举办“高中毕业生升学指导节目”
    中央人民广播电台为帮助一九五三年高中毕业生确定升学志愿和做好升学准备，决定举办“高中毕业生升学指导节目”。节目时间在七月二十七日到三十一日的晚上二十点到二十一点。节目日程如下：
    七月二十七日：（一）全国高等学校招生委员会副主任委员曾昭抡讲：“服从国家需要，积极准备条件，迎接升学考试”。（二）北京大学政治经济系一年级学生范中民讲：“我怎样确定升学志愿投入新的学习”。
    七月二十八日：全国高等学校招生委员会办公室副主任张健讲：“为着祖国伟大建设事业的需要而升学”。
    七月二十九日：（一）清华大学教务长钱伟长讲：“你想想为什么你要考工学院？”（二）北京农业大学校长孙晓村讲：“谈谈农林事业在国家建设中的重要性”。
    七月三十日：（一）北京师范大学教务长丁浩川讲：“投考师范，准备担当起培养祖国下一代的神圣任务”。（二）北京师范大学附属女子中学高中毕业生徐稚岐讲：“学习做一个人民教师”。（三）河北北京中学高中毕业生侯震标讲：“我为什么要学师范”。
    七月三十一日：中国科学院近代物理研究所所长钱三强讲：“在祖国建设中的基础科学的重要性”。
        邮电部第三次发行“保卫世界和平”纪念邮票
    中央人民政府邮电部为了纪念一九五二年十二月十二日在维也纳召开的世界人民和平大会，纪念全世界人民争取和平斗争的新成就，将在今年七月二十五日第三次发行“保卫世界和平”纪念邮票。这种邮票的图案是法国名画家毕加索为世界人民和平大会所画的第三幅展翅飞翔的和平鸽，它象征着全世界人民争取和平的斗争达到了更高的阶段。邮票的面值分八百元（紫色）、四百元（棕色）、二百五十元（绿色）三种，都是横长方形，用凹版精印的。
