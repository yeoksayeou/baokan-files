Title: Guo Moruo and Others Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-07-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 7th:

    Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council and Chinese council member attending the World Peace Council meeting in Budapest, along with council members Mao Dun, Tsai Ting-kai, Li Dequan, Wu Yaozong, and specially invited representatives Ulan, Tserennam, and Rashidah, a group of eight, returned to Beijing by plane on the 7th.

    Chinese council member Liu Ningyi, who also attended the World Peace Council meeting, had already returned to Beijing on June 28th.

### 郭沫若等回到北京

1953-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若等回到北京
    【新华社七日讯】出席世界和平理事会布达佩斯会议的中国理事、世界和平理事会副主席郭沫若，理事、世界和平理事会常务委员会委员茅盾、理事蔡廷锴、李德全、吴耀宗，特邀代表乌兰、策仁娜姆、拉希达一行八人，七日乘飞机回到北京。
    出席世界和平理事会会议的中国理事刘宁一，已先在六月二十八日回到北京。
