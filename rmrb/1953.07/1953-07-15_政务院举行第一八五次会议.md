Title: The State Council Holds its 185th Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1953-07-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, July 14th: The 185th meeting of the Central People's Government State Council was held on the afternoon of the 9th. After listening to an explanation given by Liao Luyan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Central People's Government State Council, the meeting approved the directive on issuing agricultural loans. After listening to an explanation given by Liang Xi, Minister of the Central People's Government Ministry of Forestry, the meeting approved the directive on launching a mass movement to carry out afforestation, cultivate forests, and protect forests. In addition, the meeting also approved various appointment and removal cases and cases submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval of appointments.

### 政务院举行第一八五次会议

1953-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一八五次会议
    【新华社十四日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百八十五次政务会议在九日下午举行。会议在听取了中央人民政府政务院廖鲁言副秘书长所作的说明后，通过了关于发放农业贷款的指示。会议在听取了中央人民政府林业部梁希部长所作的说明后，通过了关于发动群众开展造林、育林、护林运动的指示。此外，会议并通过了各项任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案。
