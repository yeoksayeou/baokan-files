Title: All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Hosts Indian Art Delegation
Author:
Date: 1953-07-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 25th

    The All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles held a reception at the Beijing International Club on July 25th to host the Indian Art Delegation. The reception was presided over by Zhou Yang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles. Among those present were literary and art figures Zheng Zhenduo, Hong Shen, Zhou Libo, Cao Yu, Ai Qing, Lü Ji, Zhao Feng, Ma Ke, Jiang Feng, Zhang Geng, Li Bozhao, Shi Dongshan, Cai Chusheng, Xie Bingxin, Zha Fuxi, Tian Yu, Xin Fengxia, and Liu GuanYi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace. Zhou Yang delivered a speech at the beginning of the reception, extending a warm welcome to the delegation. He said that the people of China and India have a long-standing friendship and cultural relationship. The Chinese people have long been aware of the achievements of the Indian people in culture and art. He then pointed out that from the delegation's performances in China, one can see the excellent art of the peace-loving Indian people. The delegation's performances will further promote the friendship and cultural exchange between the people of China and India. Sachin Sen Gupta, head of the delegation, delivered a reply, hoping that the people of China and India will enhance the long and profound friendship between the two countries in their joint efforts to maintain peace. Afterwards, all members of the Indian Art Delegation and the Chinese writers and artists who attended the reception were divided into groups of literature, drama, music, and dance to discuss various issues related to the literature and art of the two countries. Through free and friendly conversations, the writers and artists of China and India expressed their earnest desire to learn from each other. A banquet was held after the meeting, and Chinese acrobatics were performed to add to the fun.

### 我全国文联招待印度艺术代表团

1953-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我全国文联招待印度艺术代表团
    【新华社二十五日讯】中华全国文学艺术界联合会二十五日在北京国际俱乐部举行招待会，招待印度艺术代表团。招待会由中华全国文学艺术界联合会副主席周扬主持。出席作陪的有文学艺术界人士郑振铎、洪深、周立波、曹禺、艾青、吕骥、赵沨、马可、江丰、张庚、李伯钊、史东山、蔡楚生、谢冰心、查阜西、田雨、新凤霞以及中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一等人。周扬在招待会开始时致词，对代表团表示热烈欢迎。他说：中印两国人民有着悠久的友谊和文化关系。中国人民很早以来就知道印度人民在文化艺术上的成就。他接着指出，从代表团在中国的演出，可以看到爱好和平的印度人民的优秀艺术。代表团的演出必将进一步促进中印两国人民的友谊和文化交流。代表团团长萨钦·森古普塔致答词，希望中印两国人民在维护和平的共同努力中，增进两国人民久远深厚的友谊。接着，印度艺术代表团全体团员和出席招待会的中国文学家、艺术家们，分成文学、戏剧、音乐和舞蹈等小组，交谈有关两国文学和艺术的各项问题。中印两国文学家和艺术家们通过自由和亲切的交谈，表达了互相观摩、互相学习的殷切愿望。会后举行宴会，并演出中国杂技节目助兴。
