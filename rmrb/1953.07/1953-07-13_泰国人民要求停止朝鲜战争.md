Title: Thai People Demand an End to the Korean War
Author:
Date: 1953-07-13
Page: 1

　　Thai People Demand an End to the Korean War
　　(Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, July 11th) TASS, Bangkok, July 10th: Thailand's "Daily Mail" writes that the newspaper has received many letters from Thai people demanding an end to the Korean War.

### 泰国人民要求停止朝鲜战争

1953-07-13
第1版()
专栏：

    泰国人民要求停止朝鲜战争
    【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社曼谷十日讯：泰国的“每日邮报”写道，报馆收到许多泰国人要求结束朝鲜战争的来信。
