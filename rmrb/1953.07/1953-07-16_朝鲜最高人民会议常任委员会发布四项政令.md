Title: Supreme People's Assembly Standing Committee of North Korea Issues Four Decrees
Author:
Date: 1953-07-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 15th: The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued four decrees on the 13th: Appointing Park Ui-wan, Minister of Railways of the Cabinet, as Vice Premier of the Cabinet; relieving Park Ui-wan of his post as Minister of Railways; appointing Kim Hoi-il, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Railways of the Cabinet, as Minister of Railways; and appointing Ju Hwang-seop, President of the State Arbitration Institute of the Cabinet, as Minister without Portfolio of the Cabinet.

### 朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会发布四项政令

1953-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会发布四项政令
    【新华社平壤十五日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会在十三日发布四项政令：任命内阁铁道相朴义琓为内阁副首相，解除朴义琓的铁道相职务，任命内阁铁道省副相金会一为铁道相，任命内阁国家仲裁院院长朱晃燮为内阁无任所相。
