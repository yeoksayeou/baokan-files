Title: Korean Armistice Talks: Staff Officers and Others from Both Sides Continue Administrative Meetings
Author:
Date: 1953-07-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, July 25th—The Korean Armistice Negotiation’s Korean-Chinese delegation released the following communique on July 25th: Staff officers, liaison officers and translators from both sides held separate administrative meetings on July 25th.

### 朝鲜谈判双方参谋人员等续开行政性会议

1953-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜谈判双方参谋人员等续开行政性会议
    【新华社开城二十五日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团七月二十五日发表公报如下：双方参谋人员、联络官与译员七月二十五日分别举行行政性会议。
