Title: CPSU Central Committee Plenary Session Decides to Expel Traitor Beria from the Party

Author:

Date: 1953-07-11
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th – TASS, Moscow, 10th: Today, the newspapers published the following news about the plenary session of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.
    A few days ago, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union held a plenary session.
    After listening to and discussing the report made by G.M. Malenkov on behalf of the Presidium of the Central Committee regarding L.P. Beria's anti-Party and anti-state crimes of undermining the Soviet state for the benefit of foreign capital and treacherously attempting to place the Soviet Ministry of Internal Affairs above the Soviet government and the Communist Party, the plenary session of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union specially decided to remove L.P. Beria from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and to expel him from the Communist Party of the Soviet Union as an enemy of the Communist Party and the Soviet people.

### 苏联共产党中央举行全体会议决定开除叛徒贝利亚出党

1953-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联共产党中央举行全体会议决定开除叛徒贝利亚出党
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十日电：今天各报刊载了关于苏联共产党中央委员会全体会议的下列消息。
    几天以前，苏联共产党中央委员会举行了全体会议。
    苏联共产党中央委员会全体会议，在听取并讨论了格·马·马林科夫代表中央委员会主席团所作的关于拉·巴·贝利亚为了外国资本的利益而破坏苏维埃国家、背信弃义地企图把苏联内务部放在苏联政府和共产党之上的反党和反国家罪行的报告之后，特决定把拉·巴·贝利亚从苏联共产党中央委员会清除出去，并把他作为共产党和苏维埃人民的敌人而开除出苏联共产党。
