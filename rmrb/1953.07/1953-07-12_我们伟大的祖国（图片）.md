Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-07-12
Page: 1
The Nanjing Film Machinery Factory, under the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, has successfully trial-produced 16mm projectors. It is now producing them in large quantities to supply the needs of film projection teams in factories, rural areas, troops, and schools throughout the country. The picture shows workers in the assembly group assembling projectors.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-07-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中央人民政府文化部电影局所属南京电影机械厂，试制十六公厘放映机成功。现已大量生产，供应各地工厂、农村、部队和学校放映队的需要。这是该厂装配组的工人正在装配放映机的情形。
