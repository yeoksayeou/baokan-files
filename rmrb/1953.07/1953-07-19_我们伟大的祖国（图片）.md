    Our Great Motherland
    Kelp is a nutritious algae that grows in areas north of the subarctic zone. After several years of experimental cultivation in our country, it has become a variety adapted to temperate zones. This is a picture of workers at the Shandong Aquatic Products Farm inspecting the growth of kelp that has been artificially transplanted.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    海带是生长在亚寒带以北地区的一种富有营养的藻类。经过我国几年来的试验养殖，它已经成为适应温带的品种了。这是山东水产养殖场的工人正在检查用人工移植的海带的生长情形。
