Title: The Soviet Union Will Send a Large Number of Artists and Athletes to the World Youth Festival
Author:
Date: 1953-07-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 17th. TASS reports: The Soviet delegation to the Fourth World Youth and Students Festival for Peace and Friendship will include more than 900 young artists and athletes who will participate in the entire program of the festival. The Soviet republics have widely held various competitions and gatherings to select representatives and prepare programs.

    At the festival, the Moscow Higher Education Students' Choir and the Russian folk song choir from Omsk, Siberia, will perform choral works, including classical songs, Russian folk songs, songs by Soviet composers, and songs by people of other countries. The Moscow State Conservatory Symphony Orchestra will perform new works by young Soviet composers and classical works from Russia and the West, and the Kazakh folk instrument orchestra will perform Kazakh folk songs and dances and works by Kazakh composers. Folk dance troupes from the Moldavian Republic and dance troupes from Georgia will perform dances of various ethnic groups of the Soviet Union.

    Soviet athletes are preparing to participate in various friendship competitions at the festival, including gymnastics, track and field, swimming, diving, water polo, rowing, boxing, wrestling, weightlifting, basketball, and volleyball.

    During the festival, the Soviet delegation will hold an art exhibition in Bucharest, exhibiting famous Soviet paintings and drawings specially created by young artists for the festival. There will also be an exhibition displaying photos and documents depicting the happy lives of Soviet youth. Soviet feature films, documentaries, and popular science films will also be shown during the festival.

### 苏联将有大批艺术家和运动员参加世界青年联欢节

1953-07-19
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联将有大批艺术家和运动员参加世界青年联欢节
    【新华社十八日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科十七日讯：苏联参加第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的代表团，将包括九百多个青年艺术家和运动员，他们将参加联欢节的全部节目。苏联各加盟共和国曾广泛举行了选拔代表和准备节目的各种比赛和集会。
    在联欢节上，莫斯科高等学校学生合唱队和西伯利亚鄂木斯克城的俄罗斯民歌合唱队将表演合唱，他们的节目中包括古典歌曲、俄罗斯民歌、苏联作曲家的歌曲和外国人民的歌曲等。莫斯科国立音乐院学生交响乐队将演奏苏联青年作曲家的新作品与俄罗斯和西方的古典作品，哈萨克民间乐器乐队将演出哈萨克民间歌舞和哈萨克作曲家的作品。摩尔达维亚共和国的民间舞蹈团及格鲁吉亚的舞蹈团，将表演苏联各族人民的舞蹈。
    苏联运动员们准备参加联欢节的各种友谊比赛。包括体操、田径赛、游泳、跳水、水球、划船、拳击、摔角、举重、篮球和排球等。
    在联欢节期间，苏联代表团将在布加勒斯特举行一个美术展览会，展览苏联名画以及青年艺术家们特别为联欢节而作的图画。还有一个展览会，它将陈列描述苏联青年幸福生活的照片和文件。苏联的故事片、记录片与科学普及片也将在联欢节期中放映。
