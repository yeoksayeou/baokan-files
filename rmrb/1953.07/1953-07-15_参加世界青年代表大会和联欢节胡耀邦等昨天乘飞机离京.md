Title: Hu Yaobang and Others Left Beijing by Plane Yesterday to Attend the World Youth Congress and Festival

Author:

Date: 1953-07-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th: Hu Yaobang, head of the Chinese youth delegation to the 3rd World Youth Congress and the 4th World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival, Zhou Weizhi, head of the Chinese Youth Art Troupe, and Wu Xueqian, secretary-general of the Chinese Youth Delegation, and four others, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 14th.

### 参加世界青年代表大会和联欢节胡耀邦等昨天乘飞机离京

1953-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    参加世界青年代表大会和联欢节胡耀邦等昨天乘飞机离京
    【新华社十四日讯】参加第三届世界青年代表大会和第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的中国青年代表团团长胡耀邦、中国青年艺术团团长周巍峙、中国青年代表团秘书长吴学谦等七人在十四日上午乘飞机离京。
