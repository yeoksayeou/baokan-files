Our Great Motherland
Farmers everywhere are paying attention to protecting cotton seedlings in order to strive for a bumper cotton harvest this year. This is a scene of male and female members of the Qiu Laoyun Agricultural Production Cooperative in Xingtang County, Hebei Province. They are spraying insecticide to prevent cotton aphids.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    各地农民正在注意保护棉苗，争取今年棉花丰收。这是河北省行唐县仇老云农业生产合作社的男女社员们。他们正在喷射杀虫剂防治棉蚜虫。
