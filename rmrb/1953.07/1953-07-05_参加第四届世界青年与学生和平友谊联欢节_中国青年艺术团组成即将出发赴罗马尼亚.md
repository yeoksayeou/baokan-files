Title: Chinese Youth Art Troupe to Participate in the 4th World Youth and Student Festival for Peace and Friendship, Set to Depart for Romania
Author:
Date: 1953-07-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 4th: After more than three months of preparation, the Chinese Youth Art Troupe, which will participate in the 4th World Youth and Student Festival for Peace and Friendship to be held in Bucharest, the capital of Romania, this August, has been officially formed. The art troupe will perform Chinese musical, dance, classical opera, and acrobatic programs rich in national characteristics for peace-loving youth around the world.

The art troupe is divided into five sections: choir, dance team, classical opera team, orchestra, and acrobatics team, totaling 240 people. It is one of the largest art troupes China has ever sent to the World Youth and Student Festival. Most of its members are selected from famous domestic art organizations, such as the Central Song and Dance Troupe, the Central Experimental Opera House, the Central Conservatory of Music and its branches, the Chinese Opera Research Institute, the China Acrobatic Troupe, the Northeast People's Art Theater, the Shanghai Orchestra, and the Shanghai Broadcasting Orchestra. A portion of the members are outstanding folk artists and amateur singers who participated in the First National Folk Music and Dance Performance Conference. Among the members of the art troupe, Zhou Weizhi, Li Ling, Dai Ailian, Wang Kun, Guo Lanying, Li Shaochun, Ye Shengzhang, Zhou Guangren, Yang Jiaren, Zhang Yan, Li Zhishu, Ge Shuwang, and Jin Yeqin are all well-known artists and actors in China. Huang Hong, who participated in the First National Folk Music and Dance Performance Conference, is also a popular singer.

In order to select the artistic programs for the 4th World Youth and Student Festival for Peace and Friendship, Beijing held the First National Folk Music and Dance Performance Conference in April. Afterwards, the Chinese Youth Art Troupe held more than ten music and dance performances to carefully study each program. The programs that have been selected now include: songs such as "The East is Red," "Ode to the Motherland," "Ode to Stalin," "Song of Defending Peace," "Heroes Conquer the Dadu River," "A Story on Flying Tiger Mountain," as well as folk songs and ballads from Xinjiang, Qinghai, Yunnan, Mongolia, and Xikang; dances such as the North China folk dance "Lion," "Running Donkey," the Northeast folk dance "Single Drum," the Anhui folk dance "Flower Drum Lantern," the Fujian folk dance "Tea Picking Dance," as well as "Tibetan Dance," "Red Silk Dance," and "Lotus Dance"; classical operas such as "Havoc in Heaven," "San Cha Kou," "Yandang Mountain," and "Autumn River"; acrobatics such as "Jars," "Vehicle Skills," "Leather Whip," and "Martial Arts"; folk music includes flute solos, suona solos, pipa solos, and Cantonese music.

The Chinese Youth Art Troupe is a component of the Chinese Youth Delegation participating in the 4th World Youth and Student Festival for Peace and Friendship, and it will depart for Romania along with the delegation. The main leaders of the art troupe are: Zhou Weizhi as the head of the troupe, Li Ling and Dai Ailian as the deputy heads, and Wang Songsheng as the secretary-general.

### 参加第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节  中国青年艺术团组成即将出发赴罗马尼亚

1953-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

    参加第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节
    中国青年艺术团组成即将出发赴罗马尼亚
    【新华社四日讯】经过三个多月的筹备，我国参加今年八月在罗马尼亚首都布加勒斯特举行的第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的中国青年艺术团已经正式组成。艺术团将在全世界爱好和平的青年面前，表演我国的富有民族色彩的音乐、舞蹈、古典歌舞剧、杂技等艺术节目。
    艺术团分合唱队、舞蹈队、古典歌舞剧队、乐队、杂技队等五个部分，共二百四十人，是我国历次参加世界青年与学生联欢节的艺术团中较大的一个团。它的成员，大部分是从国内著名的艺术组织，如中央歌舞团、中央实验歌剧院、中央音乐学院本院及分院、中国戏曲研究院、中国杂技团、东北人民艺术剧院、上海乐团、上海广播乐团等单位挑选而来；一部分是参加第一届全国民间音乐舞蹈会演大会的优秀民间艺人以及业余的歌唱家。艺术团的成员中，如周巍峙、李凌、戴爱莲、王昆、郭兰英、李少春、叶盛章、周广仁、杨嘉仁、章彦、李志曙、佫树旺、金业勤等，都是国内著名的艺术家和演员；曾经参加过第一届全国民间音乐舞蹈会演大会的黄虹，也是一个受欢迎的歌手。
    为了选拔参加第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的艺术节目，四月间，北京曾举行了第一届全国民间音乐舞蹈会演大会，接着中国青年艺术团又曾举行了十几次音乐和舞蹈表演会，对每一个节目进行了仔细的研究。现在已经挑选出的节目：歌唱部分有“东方红”、“歌唱祖国”、“斯大林颂”、“保卫和平之歌”、“英雄战胜大渡河”、“飞虎山上的一段故事”以及新疆、青海、云南、蒙古、西康等地区的民歌和民谣；舞蹈部分有华北民间舞蹈“狮子”、“跑驴”，东北民间舞蹈“单鼓”，安徽民间舞蹈“花鼓灯”，福建民间舞蹈“采茶舞”，以及“西藏舞”、“红绸舞”、“荷花舞”等；古典歌舞有“闹天宫”、“三岔口”、“雁荡山”、“秋江”等；杂技有“坛子”、“车技”、“皮条”、“武术”等；民间音乐有笛子独奏、唢呐独奏、琵琶独奏和广东音乐。
    中国青年艺术团是参加第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的中国青年代表团的一个组成部分，它即将随同代表团出发赴罗马尼亚。艺术团的主要负责人是：团长周巍峙，副团长李凌、戴爱莲，秘书长王松声。
