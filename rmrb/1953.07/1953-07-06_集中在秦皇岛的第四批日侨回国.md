Title: The Fourth Group of Japanese Nationals Concentrated in Qinhuangdao Repatriated

Author:

Date: 1953-07-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Qinhuangdao, 5th: The fourth group of 1,817 Japanese nationals concentrated in Qinhuangdao who wished to return to their country, set sail on the Japanese ship "Takasago Maru" on the afternoon of the 4th. The repatriated Japanese nationals received extensive care and enthusiastic assistance from the Hebei Branch of the Chinese Red Cross. They expressed their gratitude for this sincere care and assistance from the Chinese people. Before their departure, they presented two banners to the Chinese Red Cross and the Qinhuangdao Seamen's Union respectively. The banners read: "We thank the Chinese people for their great friendship. May the people of China and Japan unite more closely and strive for the realization of peace in Asia and the world."

### 集中在秦皇岛的第四批日侨回国

1953-07-06
第4版()
专栏：

    集中在秦皇岛的第四批日侨回国
    【新华社秦皇岛五日电】集中在秦皇岛的第四批愿意回国的日本侨民一千八百一十七人，已在四日下午乘日本轮船“高砂丸”启程回国。回国日侨受到了中国红十字会河北省分会的多方照顾和热情协助。他们对中国人民这种真诚的照顾和帮助表示感谢。临行前，他们全体将两面锦旗分别赠给中国红十字会和秦皇岛市海员工会。锦旗上写着：“我们感谢中国人民的伟大友谊，中日两国人民更亲密地团结起来，为实现亚洲和世界和平而奋斗。”
