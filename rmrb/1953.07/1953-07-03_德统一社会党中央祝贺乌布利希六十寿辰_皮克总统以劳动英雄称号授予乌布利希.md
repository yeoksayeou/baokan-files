Title: SED Central Committee Congratulates Ulbricht on his Sixtieth Birthday; President Pieck Awards Ulbricht the Title of Hero of Labor
Author:
Date: 1953-07-03
Page: 4

    SED Central Committee Congratulates Ulbricht on his Sixtieth Birthday
    President Pieck Awards Ulbricht the Title of Hero of Labor
    [Xinhua News Agency, 2nd dispatch] German News Agency, Berlin dispatch: The Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, on behalf of the party and the working people, wrote to Walter Ulbricht, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of theGerman Democratic Republic, warmly congratulating him on his sixtieth birthday, and praising his contributions to the party and the people.
    [Xinhua News Agency, 2nd dispatch] According to TASS, Berlin, 1st dispatch: Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, awarded Walter Ulbricht, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic, the honorary title of Hero of Labor, in recognition of his outstanding achievements in developing the Republic's peaceful economy, defending peace, and building a unified, democratic Germany. The German News Agency reported that the award ceremony was held on June 30, Ulbricht's sixtieth birthday.

### 德统一社会党中央祝贺乌布利希六十寿辰  皮克总统以劳动英雄称号授予乌布利希

1953-07-03
第4版()
专栏：

    德统一社会党中央祝贺乌布利希六十寿辰
    皮克总统以劳动英雄称号授予乌布利希
    【新华社二日讯】德意志通讯社柏林讯：德国统一社会党中央委员会代表党和劳动人民写信给德国统一社会党中央委员会总书记、德意志民主共和国部长会议副主席乌布利希，热诚祝贺他的六十寿辰，并称颂他对于党和人民的功绩。
    【新华社二日讯】据塔斯社柏林一日讯：德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克以劳动英雄的光荣称号授予德意志民主共和国部长会议副主席瓦尔特·乌布利希，奖励他在发展共和国的和平经济，保卫和平和建立统一、民主的德国等工作中的杰出功绩。德意志通讯社报道，授奖典礼已在六月三十日，乌布利希六十岁寿辰时举行。
