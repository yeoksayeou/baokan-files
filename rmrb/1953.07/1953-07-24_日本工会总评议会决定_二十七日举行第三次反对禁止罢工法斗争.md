Title: All-Japan Council of Trade Unions Decides to Hold Third Struggle Against Anti-Strike Law on the 27th
Author:
Date: 1953-07-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 23rd: To defend the basic rights of the working class, the All-Japan Council of Trade Unions (abbreviated as "Sohyo") decided at a meeting of its executive council on the 21st to hold a third nationwide struggle on the 27th to protest the anti-strike law. This fascist bill was passed by the House of Representatives of Japan on the 11th and is currently under consideration in the House of Councillors.

	Three million members of the thirty-seven trade union units affiliated with "Sohyo" will conduct the struggle on this day through methods such as timed strikes, mass protest rallies, and taking leave. Among them, the Japan Coal Miners' Union, with approximately 270,000 members, will hold a 24-hour strike; the Japan Electrical Industry Workers' Union, with approximately 110,000 members, will stop supplying electricity to major enterprises for one hour between 8:00 a.m. and 4:00 p.m.; and the Japan Private Railway Workers' Union Federation, with approximately 120,000 members, will simultaneously hold sit-in protests at train stations nationwide.

### 日本工会总评议会决定  二十七日举行第三次反对禁止罢工法斗争

1953-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

    日本工会总评议会决定
    二十七日举行第三次反对禁止罢工法斗争
    【新华社二十三日讯】东京消息：为了保卫工人阶级的基本权利，日本工会总评议会（简称“总评”）在二十一日举行的干事会会议上决定在二十七日举行第三次抗议禁止罢工法的全国规模的斗争。这一法西斯法案已于十一日在日本众议院通过，目前正在参议院审议中。
    “总评”属下的三十七个工会单位的三百万会员将在这天分别以定时罢工、群众抗议大会和请假等方式进行斗争。其中拥有会员约二十七万人的日本煤矿工会将举行二十四小时罢工；拥有会员约十一万人的日本电气产业工会将在上午八时至下午四时之间对大企业停止供电一小时；拥有会员约十二万人的日本私营铁道工会总联合会将在全国各火车站同时举行静坐抗议。
