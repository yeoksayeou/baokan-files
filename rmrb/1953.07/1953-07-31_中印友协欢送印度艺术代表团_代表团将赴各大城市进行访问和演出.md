Title: Sino-Indian Friendship Association Hosts Farewell for Indian Art Delegation; Delegation to Visit and Perform in Major Cities

Author:
Date: 1953-07-31
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, July 30th: The Indian Art Delegation, which arrived in Beijing on the 20th, will continue its visits and performances in major cities after holding several music and dance performances in Beijing. The Sino-Indian Friendship Association hosted a banquet on the evening of the 30th to bid farewell to Sachin Sengupta, head of the Indian Art Delegation, and all its members.
    The banquet was presided over by Chen Hansheng, Vice President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association. More than 100 people attended, including responsible persons from the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and well-known writers, musicians, dramatists, and actors from the capital.
    Also attending the banquet were Mr. Gao Baoding, Counselor of the Indian Embassy in China, and many international friends in Beijing.
    Chen Hansheng delivered a speech at the banquet, thanking the Indian Art Delegation for performing many excellent and nationally-flavored music and dance programs in Beijing. He said: The delegation's performances and their exchanges with Beijing's writers and artists on literary and artistic matters have made a great contribution to the friendship between the two peoples. He proposed a toast to the friendly cooperation between the peoples of China and India.
    Sachin Sengupta, head of the delegation, then spoke. He said: During our visit to China, we have received warm hospitality and meticulous care from all aspects of the Chinese people. This spirit will inspire the friendship between the peoples of China and India, so that it will never be frustrated. Your kindness to us is so great that it is very difficult for us to express our feelings in words. We promise to do our best to work with you in India to strive for peace and friendship among all countries in the world.
    Varathol Narayana Menon, a laureate poet from South India, in his seventies, also spoke enthusiastically at the banquet. He said: What makes us most happy is that we learned in Beijing the news of the signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement. Also at this time, Chairman Mao Zedong, the world's greatest peace lover, received us, so we are particularly excited. The old poet proposed a toast to the cause of world peace.
    After the banquet, all members of the Indian Art Delegation attended a music and dance soiree sponsored by the All-China Musicians Association, and watched music and dance performances by Beijing artists.
    According to Xinhua News Agency: The last music and dance performance of the Indian Art Delegation in Beijing, jointly sponsored by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, was held at the Beijing Youth Palace Theater on the evening of the 29th.
    Eight hundred people from the capital's literary and art circles attended the evening, including Lü Ji, Hong Shen, Yu Pingbo, Zhao Feng, He Qifang, Sun Shen, and Zhong Jingwen. Diplomatic envoys and personnel from various countries stationed in China also attended the performance.
    Indian artists performed twenty music and dance programs at the soiree. These programs were full of strong national characteristics and received warm applause from the audience.
    After the performance, the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles presented flowers and gifts to the Indian Art Delegation. Sachin Sengupta, head of the Indian Art Delegation, then gave a speech to express his gratitude. He believed that these gifts were a manifestation of the Chinese people's love for Indian art and their friendship with the Indian people. He also expressed that he would bring the friendship of the Chinese people back to the Indian people.
    Furthermore: On the evening of the 28th, all members of the Indian Art Delegation visited the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and presented gifts to the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace.
    When the Indian Art Delegation presented gifts, Vice Chairman Chen Shutong and Sachin Sengupta, head of the Indian Art Delegation, both spoke, expressing their warm wishes for the friendly cooperation and cultural exchanges between the peoples of China and India, and for jointly striving for peace in the Far East and the world.
    After the Indian Art Delegation presented gifts, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace also presented gifts to all members of the Indian Art Delegation.

### 中印友协欢送印度艺术代表团  代表团将赴各大城市进行访问和演出

1953-07-31
第1版()
专栏：

    中印友协欢送印度艺术代表团
    代表团将赴各大城市进行访问和演出
    【新华社三十日讯】二十日到达北京的印度艺术代表团，在北京举行了多次的音乐舞蹈表演之后，即将赴各大城市继续进行访问或演出。中印友好协会特在三十日晚举行宴会，欢送印度艺术代表团团长萨钦·森古普塔和全体团员。
    宴会由中印友好协会副会长陈翰笙主持。出席作陪的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会负责人，首都著名的作家、音乐家、戏剧家、演员等共一百多人。
    出席宴会的还有印度驻我国大使馆参赞高伯登和在京的国际友人多人。
    陈翰笙在宴会上致词，感谢印度艺术代表团在北京演出了许多卓越的富有民族色彩的音乐和舞蹈节目。他说：代表团的演出以及他们和北京的作家、艺术家们所作的有关交流两国文学艺术的交谈，对两国人民的友谊，是一种很大的贡献。他提议为中印两国人民的友好合作干杯。
    代表团团长萨钦·森古普塔接着讲话。他说：我们这次来到中国，受到中国人民各方面的热忱的招待和无微不至的照顾。这种精神将鼓舞着中印两国人民的友谊，使它永远不会受到挫折。你们对我们的好意如此之大，使我们非常不容易用言语来表达我们的感情。我们保证要竭尽我们的能力，使印度与你们在一起努力，争取世界各国间的和平与友好。
    南印度的桂冠诗人，七十多岁的瓦拉托尔·纳拉雅纳·梅农也在宴会上热情地讲话。他说：我们最愉快的是我们在北京知道了朝鲜停战协定签字的消息。也在这时候，世界上最伟大的和平爱好者毛泽东主席接见了我们，所以我们特别地兴奋。老诗人提议为全世界的和平事业干杯。
    宴会后，印度艺术代表团全体人员又出席了中华全国音乐工作者协会主办的音乐舞蹈晚会，观看了北京文艺工作者所表演的音乐舞蹈节目。
    据新华社讯：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和中华全国文学艺术界联合会联合主办的印度艺术代表团在北京的最后一次音乐舞蹈演出晚会，二十九日晚在北京青年宫剧场举行。
    出席晚会的有首都文学艺术界人士吕骥、洪深、俞平伯、赵沨、何其芳、孙慎、钟敬文等八百人。各国驻华使节和外交人员也出席了演出晚会。
    印度艺术家们在晚会上表演了二十个音乐舞蹈节目。这些节目都富有浓厚的民族色彩，获得了全场热烈的掌声。
    表演结束后，中华全国文学艺术界联合会向印度艺术代表团献花献礼。印度艺术代表团团长萨钦·森古普塔接着致辞表示感谢。他认为这些礼物是中国人民爱好印度艺术和对印度人民友谊的表示。他并表示要把中国人民的友谊带回去给印度人民。
    又讯：印度艺术代表团全体人员二十八日晚访问中国人民保卫世界和平委员会，并向中国人民保卫世界和平委员会献礼。
    在印度艺术代表团献礼时，陈叔通副主席和印度艺术代表团团长萨钦·森古普塔都讲了话，一致对中印两国人民的友好合作、文化交流以及共同争取远东和世界和平表示了热情的愿望。
    印度艺术代表团献礼后，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会也向印度艺术代表团全体人员赠送了礼品。
