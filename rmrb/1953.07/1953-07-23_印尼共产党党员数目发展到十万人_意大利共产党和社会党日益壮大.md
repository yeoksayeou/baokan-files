Title: Indonesian Communist Party Membership Reaches 100,000; Italian Communist Party and Socialist Party Growing Stronger

Author:
Date: 1953-07-23
Page: 4

    Indonesian Communist Party Membership Reaches 100,000
    Italian Communist Party and Socialist Party Growing Stronger
    [Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd] According to the newspaper "For Lasting Peace, For People's Democracy!": The membership of the Indonesian Communist Party has increased tenfold this year, reaching 100,000. In many places on the islands of Sumatra, Sulawesi, Kalimantan, and Bali, where there were no party branches before, party branches have now been established.
    [Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd] TASS Rome, July 20th: Democratic newspapers report that the Italian Communist Party and Socialist Party are growing stronger.
    According to "l'Unità," the number of Communist Party members in the Province of Campania has increased by 8,061 since 1952. After the elections, 2,979 people in Rome (including 1,200 women) joined the Communist Party.
    "Avanti!" reports: In the past few days, more than 1,000 working people have joined the Socialist Party.

### 印尼共产党党员数目发展到十万人  意大利共产党和社会党日益壮大

1953-07-23
第4版()
专栏：

    印尼共产党党员数目发展到十万人
    意大利共产党和社会党日益壮大
    【新华社二十二日讯】据“争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”报刊载：印度尼西亚共产党党员数目今年发展到十倍，达十万人。在苏门答腊、苏拉威西、加里曼丹和峇里等岛上许多以前没有党支部的地方，现在都已经设立了党支部。
    【新华社二十二日讯】塔斯社罗马二十日讯：民主报纸报道：意大利共产党和社会党日益壮大。
    据“团结报”报道，卡潘尼亚省共产党党员比一九五二年增加了八千零六十一人。选举后，罗马有二千九百七十九人（其中有一千二百名妇女）加入了共产党。
    “前进报”报道：在过去几天，有劳动人民一千多人加入了社会党。
