Title: "August 1st" Sports Meet Film to be Screened in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-07-31
Page: 3

Beijing to Screen "August 1st" Sports Meet Film

    To celebrate the 26th anniversary of the "August 1st" Army Day, ten cinemas in Beijing will specially screen the film "August 1st" Sports Meet starting from July 31st. This is China's first vibrant color documentary, jointly produced by the Beijing Film Studio and the Chinese People's Liberation Army Film Studio.
    This film fully records the wonderful events of the Chinese People's Liberation Army's "August 1st" 25th Anniversary Sports Meet, held in Beijing from August 1st to 11th, 1952. Among them are spectacular aerobatic flight performances, male and female paratrooper jumps, water sports such as sampan races, armed swimming competitions, motorcycle displays by armored units, equestrian stunts by cavalry soldiers, and friendly matches between the visiting basketball team of the People's Republic of Poland and the "August 1st" basketball team of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. In this film, we can also see our great leader Chairman Mao Zedong and Commander-in-Chief Zhu De reviewing the strong sports army of the whole army.
    This film also records the wonderful programs of the all-army art review performance held during the "August 1st" Sports Meet. The film also records the performance of the Czechoslovak – Vit Nejedlý – Army Art Troupe at the "August 1st" Sports Meet gala. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 北京上映“八一”运动大会影片

1953-07-31
第3版()
专栏：

    北京上映“八一”运动大会影片
    为了庆祝“八一”建军节二十六周年，北京的十家电影院从七月三十一日起特别选映“八一”运动大会一片。这是由北京电影制片厂和中国人民解放军电影制片厂联合摄制的中国第一部色彩鲜艳的五彩记录片。
    这部片子完整地记录了一九五二年八月一日到十一日在北京举行的中国人民解放军“八一”建军节二十五周年体育运动大会的各项精彩节目，其中有精彩的航空飞行特技表演、男女伞兵跳伞表演、水上体育的舢板竞赛、武装游泳比赛、装甲部队的摩托车表演和骑兵战士们的马术特技表演以及来我国访问的波兰人民共和国的篮球代表队和中国人民解放军“八一”篮球队的友谊比赛表演等。在这部影片中还可以看到我们伟大的领袖毛泽东主席和朱德总司令在检阅全军的雄健的体育大军。
    这部影片中也记录了“八一”体育运动大会期间所举行的全军文艺检阅会演中的精彩节目。影片还记录了应邀来我国的捷克斯洛伐克——维特·尼耶德利——军队文艺工作团在“八一”体育运动大会联欢会上的表演。　（新华社）
