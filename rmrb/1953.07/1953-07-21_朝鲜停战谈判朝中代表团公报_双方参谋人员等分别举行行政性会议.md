Title: Korean Armistice Talks: Korean-Chinese Delegation Communiqué, Staff Officers and Others from Both Sides Hold Administrative Meetings Separately
Author:
Date: 1953-07-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, July 20th—The Korean Armistice Talks Korean-Chinese Delegation issued the following communiqué on July 20th: Staff officers from both sides, liaison officers from both sides, and translators from both sides held administrative meetings separately on July 20th.

### 朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团公报  双方参谋人员等分别举行行政性会议

1953-07-21
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团公报
    双方参谋人员等分别举行行政性会议
    【新华社开城二十日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团七月二十日发表公报如下：双方各组参谋人员、双方联络官与双方译员，七月二十日分别举行行政性会议。
