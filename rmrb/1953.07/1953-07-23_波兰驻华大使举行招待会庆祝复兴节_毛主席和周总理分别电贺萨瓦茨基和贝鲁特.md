Title: Polish Ambassador to China Holds Reception to Celebrate National Day of Rebirth; Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Congratulatory Telegrams to Zawadzki and Bierut
Author:
Date: 1953-07-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd: July 22nd is the ninth anniversary of the National Day of Rebirth of the People's Republic of Poland. The Ambassador of the People's Republic of Poland to China, Kirilo Kobylansky, held a reception at the Beijing Hotel on the afternoon of the 22nd. Those invited to attend were: Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De and Li Jishen, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Huang Kecheng, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Wu Xiuquan, heads of various departments of the Central People's Government, heads of various people's organizations, cultural and artistic figures, labor models, combat heroes, and Vice Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, and over six hundred others. Also invited to attend were diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China. Lieutenant General Blecha of Czechoslovakia and Major General Ungrowski of Poland, members of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission for the Korean Armistice, invited by the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Central People's Government of our country to participate in the work of the Korean Armistice Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission, were also invited to attend.

Ambassador Kobylansky and Vice Chairman Zhu De delivered speeches successively, mutually congratulating the eternal friendship between the Chinese and Polish peoples, the greater achievements of the two peoples in national construction, and wishing the strength of the world's peace and democracy camp to grow stronger day by day.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere. Celebratory music rose from time to time, and the hosts and guests frequently toasted the friendship between the Polish and Chinese peoples, the health of the leaders of the two peoples, and the health of the great Soviet people and their leaders.

After the reception, a grand ball was held.

Furthermore, Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government sent a telegram to Chairman Zawadzki of the State Council of the People's Republic of Poland, and Premier Zhou Enlai of the Government Administration Council sent a telegram to Chairman Bierut of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Poland to express congratulations.

### 波兰驻华大使举行招待会庆祝复兴节  毛主席和周总理分别电贺萨瓦茨基和贝鲁特

1953-07-23
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰驻华大使举行招待会庆祝复兴节
    毛主席和周总理分别电贺萨瓦茨基和贝鲁特
    【新华社二十二日讯】七月二十二日是波兰人民共和国国家复兴节九周年纪念日。波兰人民共和国驻中国大使基里洛克特于二十二日下午在北京饭店举行招待会。应邀出席的有：中央人民政府副主席朱德和李济深，人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长黄克诚，外交部副部长伍修权及中央人民政府各部门首长，各人民团体负责人，文化艺术界人士，劳动模范，战斗英雄以及北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等六百多人。应邀到会的还有各国驻华使节和外交官员。应朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府和我国中央人民政府邀请、参加朝鲜停战中立国监察委员会工作的捷克斯洛伐克委员布莱希中将和波兰委员翁格洛夫斯基少将，也应邀到会。
    基里洛克大使和朱德副主席相继致词，互祝波中两国人民的永久友谊，两国人民在国家建设中获得更大的成就，并祝世界和平民主阵营的力量日益强大。
    招待会在热烈友好的气氛中进行，庆祝的乐声时起，宾主频频为波、中两国人民的友谊、为两国人民的领袖的健康而干杯，为伟大的苏联人民及其领袖的健康而干杯。
    会后，举行了盛大的舞会。
    又讯：中央人民政府毛泽东主席曾致电波兰人民共和国国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基，政务院周恩来总理致电波兰人民共和国部长会议主席贝鲁特表示祝贺。
