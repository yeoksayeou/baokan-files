    Our Great Motherland
    The output of the trifoliate orange flowers, jasmine flowers and white orchid flowers, special products of Suzhou, Jiangsu Province, used specifically for tea making, has greatly increased due to the development of foreign trade. This is a scene of flower farmers picking trifoliate orange flowers in Huqiu Town, Suzhou.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-07-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    江苏省苏州的特产——专为制茶用的玳玳花、茉莉花和白兰花，由于对外贸易的开展，年产量已大为增加。这是苏州虎丘镇的花农在采摘玳玳花的情形。
