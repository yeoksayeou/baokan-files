Title: Czechoslovak and Polish Members and First Group of Staff Depart for Korea to Participate in Work of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission for the Korean Armistice

Author:

Date: 1953-07-28
Page: 4

    Participating in the Work of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission for the Korean Armistice
    Czechoslovak and Polish Members and First Group of Staff Depart for Korea
    [Xinhua News Agency, 28th] At the invitation of the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Lieutenant General Blecha, the Czechoslovak member, and Major General Unglowski, the Polish member, who will participate in the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission for the Korean Armistice, along with the first group of staff members from their respective countries participating in the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission, have departed from Beijing for Korea.
    Those who went to the station to see them off included: Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Jiang Keding, Deputy Director of the Foreign Liaison Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, and others.
    Also at the station to see them off were Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Kousal, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, Xu Zhe, and Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the People's Republic of Poland in China, Gura.

### 参加朝鲜停战中立国监察委员会工作  捷、波委员和首批工作人员动身赴朝鲜

1953-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

    参加朝鲜停战中立国监察委员会工作
    捷、波委员和首批工作人员动身赴朝鲜
    【新华社二十八日讯】应朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府和中华人民共和国中央人民政府邀请，参加朝鲜停战中立国监察委员会工作的捷克斯洛伐克委员布莱希中将和波兰委员翁格洛夫斯基少将，偕同各该国参加中立国监察委员会的首批工作人员业已自北京动身前往朝鲜。
    赴车站送行的有：中央人民政府外交部伍修权副部长，人民革命军事委员会办公厅萧向荣主任，外交部交际处王倬如处长，人民革命军事委员会对外联络处蒋克定副处长等。
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻华大使康萨拉，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办徐哲和波兰人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办古拉也到车站送行。
