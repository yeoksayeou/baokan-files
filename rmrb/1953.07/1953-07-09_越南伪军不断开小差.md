Title: Desertion Among Vietnamese Puppet Troops Continues
Author:
Date: 1953-07-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 8th Dispatch: According to the Vietnam News Agency, incidents of war-weariness and desertion are constantly occurring among puppet troops in various parts of Vietnam. According to incomplete statistics, from January 15 to June 15 this year, in Vinh Phuc province northwest of Hanoi alone, 665 puppet soldiers deserted.

In Kien An city, near Haiphong, the largest port in North Vietnam, in the ten days following the People's Army's surprise attack on April 20, more than 200 puppet soldiers in Kien An province deserted or successfully applied for demobilization.

In May, more than 60 puppet soldiers in Thai Binh, Ha Nam and other provinces deserted, some of them even bringing their weapons to join the Vietnamese People's Army.

### 越南伪军不断开小差

1953-07-09
第4版()
专栏：

    越南伪军不断开小差
    【新华社八日讯】据越南通讯社讯：越南各地伪军厌战、开小差的事情不断发生。据不完全的统计，从今年一月十五日到六月十五日，仅在河内西北的永福省，就有伪军六百六十五人开了小差。
    在北越最大的港口海防附近的建安城于四月二十日遭到人民军突袭以后的十天中，建安省伪军开小差的或要求复员成功的就有二百人以上。
    五月里，太平、河南等省有六十多名伪军开小差，他们有些还带着武器投奔越南人民军。
