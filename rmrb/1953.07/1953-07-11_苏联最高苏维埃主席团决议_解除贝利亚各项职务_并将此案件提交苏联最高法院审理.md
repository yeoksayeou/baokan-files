Title: Resolution of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union: Dismissal of Beria from All Positions and Submission of the Case to the Supreme Court of the Soviet Union for Trial
Author:
Date: 1953-07-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th. TASS Moscow, 10th: Today, newspapers published the following announcement of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union:

In view of the recent discovery of anti-state crimes committed by L. P. Beria, who, for the benefit of foreign capital, undermined the Soviet state, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, having reviewed the report of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union on this matter, hereby resolves:

(1) To dismiss L. P. Beria from his posts as First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Minister of Internal Affairs of the Soviet Union.

(2) To submit the case of L. P. Beria's crimes to the Supreme Court of the Soviet Union for trial.

### 苏联最高苏维埃主席团决议  解除贝利亚各项职务  并将此案件提交苏联最高法院审理

1953-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃主席团决议
    解除贝利亚各项职务
    并将此案件提交苏联最高法院审理
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十日电：今天各报刊载了苏联最高苏维埃主席团的下列公告：
    鉴于在最近期间发现拉·巴·贝利亚有为了外国资本的利益而破坏苏维埃国家的反国家的罪行，苏联最高苏维埃主席团在审议了苏联部长会议关于这一问题的报告之后，特决议：
    （一）解除拉·巴·贝利亚所任苏联部长会议第一副主席职务和苏联内务部部长职务。
    （二）把关于拉·巴·贝利亚的罪行案件提交苏联最高法院审理。
