Title: Korean Armistice Talks: North Korean-Chinese Delegation Issues Communiqué; Both Sides' Staff Officers and Liaison Officers Hold Separate Administrative Meetings
Author:
Date: 1953-07-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, July 24th: The North Korean-Chinese delegation to the Korean armistice talks issued the following communiqué on July 24th: Staff officers from both sides and liaison officers from both sides held separate administrative meetings on July 24th.

### 朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团发表公报  双方参谋人员与联络官分别开行政性会议

1953-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团发表公报
    双方参谋人员与联络官分别开行政性会议
    【新华社开城二十四日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团七月二十四日发表公报如下：双方参谋人员与双方联络官七月二十四日分别举行行政性会议。
