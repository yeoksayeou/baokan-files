Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station to Broadcast News of Korean Armistice Agreement Signing Today
Author:
Date: 1953-07-27
Page: 1

    Central People's Broadcasting Station
    Today Broadcasting News of Korean Armistice Agreement Signing
    [This newspaper dispatch] The Central People's Broadcasting Station is scheduled to broadcast the news of the signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement and related documents today at 11:00 AM, 1:00 PM, 6:10 PM, and 8:00 PM. All regions are requested to pay attention to organizing listening sessions.

### 中央人民广播电台  今天播送朝鲜停战协定签字消息

1953-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民广播电台
    今天播送朝鲜停战协定签字消息
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台定于今天播送朝鲜停战协定签字消息和朝鲜停战协定等文件，播送时间为十一点、十三点、十八点十分和二十点，希各地注意组织收听。
