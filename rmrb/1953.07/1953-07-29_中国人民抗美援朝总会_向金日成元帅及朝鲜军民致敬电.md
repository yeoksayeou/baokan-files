Title: China People's Anti-American and Aid-Korea Headquarters Congratulatory Telegram to Marshal Kim Il-sung and the Korean People and Army
Author:
Date: 1953-07-29
Page: 1

To the esteemed Marshal Kim Il-sung, and to all commanders and fighters of the heroic Korean People's Army, and to all the Korean people:
    As the Korean Armistice Agreement is signed, the China People's Anti-American and Aid-Korea Headquarters, on behalf of the Chinese people, extends warm congratulations and lofty respects to you and to all the Korean People's Army and the Korean people.
    The signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement is a great victory for the heroic Korean People's Army and the Korean people in their struggle to defend Korean independence and strive for a peaceful resolution of the Korean issue, a great victory for the anti-aggression struggle of the Chinese and Korean people, and a great victory for the world's people's movement to defend peace.
    The Chinese people resolutely support the Korean Armistice Agreement and are determined, on the basis of the unbreakable fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Korean people, to work together with the Korean people to ensure the full realization of all the provisions of the Korean Armistice Agreement and to continue to work for the peaceful settlement of the Korean issue.
    Long live the heroic Korean People's Army and the Korean people!
    Long live the unbreakable unity of the Chinese and Korean people!
    Long live the Democratic People's Republic of Korea!
    China People's Anti-American and Aid-Korea Headquarters, July 28, 1953

### 中国人民抗美援朝总会  向金日成元帅及朝鲜军民致敬电

1953-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民抗美援朝总会
    向金日成元帅及朝鲜军民致敬电敬爱的金日成元帅并转英勇的朝鲜人民军全体指挥员、战斗员同志和全体朝鲜人民：
    当此朝鲜停战协定签订之际，中国人民抗美援朝总会代表中国人民谨向您以及全体朝鲜人民军与朝鲜人民，致热烈的庆贺和崇高的敬礼。
    朝鲜停战协定的签订，是英雄的朝鲜人民军和朝鲜人民保卫朝鲜独立和争取朝鲜问题和平解决的斗争的伟大胜利，是朝中两国人民反侵略斗争的伟大胜利，是世界人民保卫和平运动的伟大胜利。
    中国人民坚决拥护与支持朝鲜停战协定，决心在朝中两国人民牢不可破的兄弟友谊的基础上，和朝鲜人民一道，为保障朝鲜停战协定各项条款的完全实现，并为朝鲜问题的和平解决而继续努力。
    英雄的朝鲜人民军和朝鲜人民万岁！
    朝中两国人民牢不可破的团结万岁！
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国万岁！
    中国人民抗美援朝总会一九五三年七月二十八日
