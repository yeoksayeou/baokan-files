Title: Mei Lanfang Performs for Workers in Wuxi
Author:
Date: 1953-07-03
Page: 3

Cultural Briefs:

    Mei Lanfang Performs for Workers in Wuxi
    To execute his plan of performing for workers, the famous Peking Opera actor Mei Lanfang led his Peking Opera troupe to Wuxi from June 16th to 28th and performed famous plays such as "Farewell My Concubine," "Qi Shuang Hui," "Drunken Beauty," and "Phoenix Returns to its Nest," which were warmly welcomed by workers from various factories in Wuxi. Workers from factories such as Qingfeng and Shenxin organized collective ticket purchases for each performance. Many farmers from the suburbs of Wuxi also traveled long distances to watch.

### 梅兰芳在无锡为工人演出

1953-07-03
第3版()
专栏：文化简讯

    梅兰芳在无锡为工人演出
    著名京戏演员梅兰芳为执行他为工人演出的计划，在六月十六日至二十八日率领他的京剧团在无锡演出“霸王别姬”、“奇双会”、“醉酒”、“凤还巢”等名剧，受到无锡各工厂工人们热烈的欢迎。庆丰、申新等厂工人每场都组织集体购票。无锡市郊不少农民也远道赶来观看。
