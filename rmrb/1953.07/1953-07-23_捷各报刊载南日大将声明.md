Title: Czechoslovakian Newspapers Publish General Nam Il's Statement
Author:
Date: 1953-07-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 22nd: Czechoslovakian newspapers on the 21st prominently published the news of General Nam Il's statement at the Korean armistice talks on the 19th. "Red Right Newspaper," "Labor Newspaper," "People's Defense Newspaper," and "Youth Front Newspaper" published the full text of this statement.
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 22nd: Moscow newspapers on the 22nd all published excerpts from the "People's Daily" editorial in Beijing, "The United States Must Fulfill Its Guarantees."

### 捷各报刊载南日大将声明

1953-07-23
第1版()
专栏：

    捷各报刊载南日大将声明
    【新华社布拉格二十二日电】捷克斯洛伐克报纸二十一日都以显著地位刊载了南日大将十九日在朝鲜停战谈判会议上发表声明的消息。“红色权利报”、“劳动报”、“人民防线报”和“青年阵线报”刊载了这个声明的全文。
    【新华社莫斯科二十二日电】莫斯科各报二十二日都刊载了北京“人民日报”社论“美国必须履行它的保证”的摘要。
