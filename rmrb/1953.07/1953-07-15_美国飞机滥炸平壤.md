Title: American Planes Indiscriminately Bomb Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1953-07-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang: On the afternoon of July 12th, a large number of American planes bombed Pyongyang. The central and southern districts of Pyongyang suffered heavy bombing and strafing.

    American planes dropped many fragmentation and cluster bombs on the market in the central district. When people in the market ran to air raid shelters to hide, American planes strafed them with machine guns. American planes destroyed dozens of air raid shelters and houses, and killed many civilians.

### 美国飞机滥炸平壤

1953-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    美国飞机滥炸平壤
    【新华社十四日讯】据塔斯社平壤讯：七月十二日下午，大批美国飞机空袭平壤。平壤市中区和南区，受到了猛烈的轰炸和扫射。
    美国飞机对中区的市场投下了许多杀伤弹和开花弹。当市场上的人奔往防空洞躲避时，美国飞机就用机关枪扫射他们。美国飞机炸毁了几十个防空洞和住房，并炸死了许多老百姓。
