Moscow Radio Summer Mandarin Broadcast Schedule for 1953

Time: 01:00-01:30 (06:00-06:30). Wavelength: 25 (11000); 31 (9000); 49 (6000); 457.3 (656). 477.0 (629); 547.4 (548); 1666.0 (180).
Time: 10:30-11:00 (15:30-16:00); 11:30-12:00 (16:30-17:00). Wavelength: 19 (15000); 25 (11000); 31 (9000); 49 (6000); 457.3 (656); 477.0 (629); 547.4 (548); 1666.0 (180).
Time: 13:00-13:30 (18:00-18:30); 14:00-14:30 (19:00-19:30). Wavelength: 19 (15000); 25 (11000); 31 (9000); 49 (6000); 422.5 (710); 457.3 (656); 477.0 (629); 547.4 (548); 1666.0 (180).
Time: 17:00-18:00 (22:00-23:00). Wavelength: 19 (15000); 25 (11000); 31 (9000); 49 (6000); 422.5 (710); 457.3 (656); 477.0 (629); 547.4 (548); 1666.0 (180).
Time: 15:30-16:00 (20:30-21:00) - Cantonese Broadcast. Wavelength: 19 (15000); 25 (11000).

Explanation: Time refers to Moscow time, with Beijing time in parentheses. Wavelength is calculated in meters, with the frequency in parentheses. The shortwave and its frequency refer to a frequency band.

Moscow Radio Mandarin broadcasts (in Beijing dialect and Cantonese) daily: Soviet news, international news, information on all aspects of the Soviet Union and various People's Democratic countries, as well as commentary and talks on international political issues.

Moscow Radio broadcasts summaries of Soviet newspapers daily from 19:00 to 19:30 Beijing time.

From 22:00 to 23:00 on Sundays, we answer listeners' letters and questions. Soviet music, Chinese music, and Russian classical music are broadcast on Tuesdays. Broadcasts to the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association are on Saturdays.

Music is broadcast from 15:30 to 16:00 on Sundays.

Literary and artistic broadcasts are from 18:00 to 18:30 on Sundays.
