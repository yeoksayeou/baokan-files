Title: US-UK-France Summit Postponed
Author:
Date: 1953-07-03
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, London, 30th. The British government announced on June 27th that British Prime Minister Churchill, on the advice of his doctors, was taking complete rest. The US, UK and French leaders have therefore decided to postpone indefinitely the meeting scheduled to be held in Bermuda. The meeting was originally scheduled for mid-June and was subsequently postponed twice to June 29th and July 8th.
    Acting British Prime Minister Butler announced in the British Parliament on June 29th that, in view of the postponement of the Bermuda meeting, the United States, Britain and France were discussing holding a meeting of the three foreign ministers in the near future.

### 美英法三国首脑会议延期

1953-07-03
第4版()
专栏：

    美英法三国首脑会议延期
    【新华社三十日讯】伦敦消息：英国政府六月二十七日宣布：英国首相邱吉尔遵照医生劝嘱进行完全休养，美、英、法三国首脑决定在百慕大举行的会议已因此无限期地延期。这个会议最初决定在六月中举行，其后曾二度延期至六月二十九日及七月八日。
    在邱吉尔休养时期代理英国首相巴特勒六月二十九日在英国议会中宣称：鉴于百慕大会议延期，美、英、法三国正在商议由三国外交部长在最近举行会议。
