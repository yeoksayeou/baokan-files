    Our Great Motherland
    Lushan is one of China's summer resorts. Now, sanatoriums and rest homes for workers are constantly being established here. Many workers come here for rest and recuperation. This is a scene of recuperating workers touring "Little Tianchi" in Lushan.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-07-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    庐山是我国避暑胜地之一。现在这里正在不断地建立工人疗养院和休养所。许多工人都到这里来休养。这是休养的工人们在庐山“小天池”游览的情形。
