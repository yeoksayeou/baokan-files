Title: Brazilian People from All Walks of Life Demand the Conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact
Author:
Date: 1953-07-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: The Brazilian people are increasingly strongly demanding that international disputes be resolved peacefully through consultation and that a five-power peace pact be concluded.

    The Brazilian newspaper "Workers' Voice" said: "The proposition of resolving international problems through consultation is gaining more and more supporters in our country." The newspaper reported that the Brazilian president has received hundreds of letters and telegrams from all over the country, requesting the Brazilian representative to the United Nations to support the proposal to conclude a five-power peace pact.

    At mass rallies in Rio de Janeiro, Sao Paulo, and other Brazilian cities, the attendees all demanded consultations, an immediate end to the Korean War, and the conclusion of a peace pact.

### 巴西各阶层人民要求缔结五大国和平公约

1953-07-19
第4版()
专栏：

    巴西各阶层人民要求缔结五大国和平公约
    据新华社讯：巴西人民正日益强烈地要求用协商方式和平解决国际争端，缔结五大国和平公约。
    巴西“工人呼声报”说：“用协商方式解决国际问题的主张在我国正得到愈来愈多的支持者。”该报报道说，巴西总统已收到数百封来自全国各地的信件和电报，要求驻联合国的巴西代表支持缔结五大国和平公约的建议。
    在里约热内卢、圣保罗和巴西其它城市的群众集会上，到会群众都要求进行协商，要求立即停止朝鲜战争，要求缔结和平公约。
