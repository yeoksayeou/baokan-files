Title: Vietnamese and Japanese Women Representatives Invited to Visit China Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-07-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 10th: Eight Vietnamese representatives invited to visit China after attending the World Women's Congress arrived in Beijing by plane on the 10th.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Cao Mengjun, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Lu Cui, Director of the International Work Department, Qi Yun, Deputy Director, and Zhou Liang, First Secretary of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, and more than ten others.

The foreign guests received flowers from the welcomers at the airport.

The list of Vietnamese women representatives invited to visit China is as follows:
　　Hoang Thi Ai: Member of the Standing Committee of the Vietnam Women's Union Central Committee, Head of the Vietnamese Delegation to the World Women's Congress
　　Le Thai Bao: Member of the Executive Committee of the Vietnam Women's Union Central Committee, Head of the Women's Union of the Third War Zone in Vietnam, Deputy Head of the Vietnamese Delegation to the World Women's Congress
　　Phan Thi An: Secretary-General of the Vietnam National Committee for the Protection of Children
　　Vu Thi Hang: Rural woman, mother of "Vietnam's Zoya"
　　Vu Thi Cai: Member of the Anti-Japanese Administrative Committee of the Fourth War Zone in Vietnam
　　Quach Thi Tue: Agricultural labor model
　　Cai Thi Lien: Representative of ethnic minorities, model cadre
　　Do Thi Kim Quy: Labor model of the Vietnam National Defense Supply Factory

Xinhua News Agency, July 12th: Six Japanese representatives and one staff member, invited to visit China after attending the World Women's Congress, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 12th.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Cao Mengjun, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Lu Cui, Director of the International Work Department, Liao Mengxing, Deputy Director, Qi Yun, and Yu Feng, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Artists Association, and more than ten others.

The list of Japanese women representatives invited to visit China is as follows:
　　Akamatsu Toshiko: Deputy Head of the Japanese Delegation to the World Women's Congress, painter, author of "Atomic Bomb Explosion Picture", winner of the World Peace Council Peace Prize.
　　Hamada Itoe: Secretary-General of the Japan Women's Organizations Federation, Chairman of the Japan Working Women's Federation, Chairman of the Nakano Ward Peace Women's Association in Tokyo.
　　Takahashi Shizae: Deputy Minister of the Women's Department of the Chiba Prefectural Government Employees Union of the Japan Local Government Employees Union.
　　Ogasawara Sadako: Deputy Minister of the Peace Department of the Japan Christian Women's Temperance Society, member of the Japan Peace Liaison Committee.
　　Miyagi Fujiko: Textile worker.
　　Endo Chie: Minister of the Women's Department of the Fukushima Prefectural Government Employees Union.
　　Takara Makiko: Translator.

### 应邀来我国访问的越南日本妇女代表抵京

1953-07-13
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国访问的越南日本妇女代表抵京
    【新华社十日讯】应邀来我国访问的出席世界妇女大会的越南代表八人，在十日乘机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有：中华全国民主妇女联合会副秘书长曹孟君，国际工作部部长陆璀、副部长戚云，越南民主共和国驻华大使馆一等秘书周亮等十余人。
    外宾们在机场接受了欢迎者的献花。
    应邀来我国访问的越南妇女代表名单如下：黄氏爱    越南妇联中央常务委员会委员、出席世界妇女大会越南代表团团长黎泰宝    越南妇联中央执行委员会委员、越南第三战区妇联负责人、出席世界妇女代表大会越南代表团副团长潘氏安    越南全国保卫儿童委员会总书记武氏恒    农村妇女，“越南的卓娅”的母亲武氏彩    越南第四战区抗战行政委员会委员郭氏雀    农业劳动模范蔡氏莲    少数民族代表，模范干部杜氏金龟    越南国防军需厂劳动模范
    【新华社十二日讯】应邀来我国访问的日本出席世界妇女大会的代表六人和工作人员一人，在十二日下午到北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中华全国民主妇女联合会副秘书长曹孟君，国际工作部部长陆璀，副部长廖梦醒、戚云，中华全国美术工作者协会副秘书长郁风等十余人。
    应邀来我国访问的日本妇女代表名单如下：赤松俊子    出席世界妇女大会日本代表团副团长，画家，“原子弹爆炸图”的作者，世界和平理事会和平奖获得者。滨田糸卫    日本妇女团体联合会事务局长，日本劳动妇女联合会委员长，东京都中野区和平妇女会会长。高桥志佐江    日本地方公务员工会千叶县政府公务员工会妇女部副部长。小笠原贞子    日本基督教妇女矫风会和平部副部长，日本和平联络会会员。宫城富士子    纺织女工。远藤千枝    福岛县政府公务员工会妇女部部长。高良真木子    翻译。
