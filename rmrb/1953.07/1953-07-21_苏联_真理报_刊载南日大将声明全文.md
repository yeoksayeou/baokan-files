Title: The Soviet "Pravda" Publishes the Full Text of General Nam Il's Statement
Author:
Date: 1953-07-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 20th. "Pravda" published on the 20th the full text of General Nam Il's statement at the Korean armistice talks on the 19th, as reported by Xinhua News Agency.
