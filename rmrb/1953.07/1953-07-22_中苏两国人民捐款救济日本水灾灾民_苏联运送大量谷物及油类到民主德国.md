Title: Chinese and Soviet People Donate to Relieve Japanese Flood Victims; Soviet Union Transports Large Quantities of Grain and Oil to East Germany
Author:
Date: 1953-07-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 21st: The severe flooding that occurred in Kyushu, Japan, and other areas since late June has aroused deep concern among the Chinese people. Upon learning of the disaster in Japan, the Red Cross Society of China not only sent a telegram to Tatsuji Fuse, Chairman of the Japan National Relief Society, expressing the Chinese people's concern and sympathy for the Japanese people affected by the disaster, but also carried out work to raise disaster relief funds. On July 20, the Red Cross Society of China remitted 1,236,470,000 RMB (equivalent to £17,850) to the Japan National Relief Society for distribution to the Japanese people affected by the disaster.

Xinhua News Agency, 21st: TASS, Tokyo, 19th: According to Kyodo News, Soviet delegation counselor Pavlichev handed over 3.6 million yen to the Japanese Red Cross on July 18 as relief funds for the flood victims in northern Kyushu.

Xinhua News Agency, 21st: According to the German News Agency, Berlin, 20th: A large amount of food transported from the Soviet Union to the German Democratic Republic in the first half of July included 5,000 tons of butter, 4,000 tons of vegetable oil, more than 1,000 tons of animal fats, and nearly 70,000 tons of grain.

### 中苏两国人民捐款救济日本水灾灾民  苏联运送大量谷物及油类到民主德国

1953-07-22
第4版()
专栏：

    中苏两国人民捐款救济日本水灾灾民
    苏联运送大量谷物及油类到民主德国
    【新华社二十一日讯】自六月下旬起在日本九州等地发生的大水灾，引起了中国人民的深切关怀。我中国人民救济总会得悉日本这次灾情后，除曾致电日本国民救援会主席布施辰治表示中国人民对日本受灾人民的关怀和慰问外，并进行了筹集救灾款项的工作。七月二十日，中国人民救济总会已将筹得的救灾捐款人民币十二亿三千六百四十七万元（合一万七千八百五十英镑）汇往日本国民救援会转交日本受灾人民。
    【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社东京十九日讯：据共同社报道，苏联代表团参事巴夫里切夫在七月十八日将捐款三百六十万日元交给日本红十字会，作为对九州北部水灾灾民的救济金。
    【新华社二十一日讯】据德意志通讯社柏林二十日讯：七月上半月自苏联运到德意志民主共和国的大量食物中，包括五千吨牛油，四千吨植物油，一千吨以上的动物脂肪和将近七万吨的谷物。
