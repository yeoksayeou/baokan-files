Title: China's Industrial and Agricultural Exhibition to Open Soon in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1953-07-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th. The "People's Republic of China Industrial and Agricultural Exhibition" is scheduled to open on July 11th in the Central Recreation Park in Moscow. Currently, the construction and arrangement of the exhibition hall are proceeding intensively. This exhibition is very large, covering an area of 5,000 square meters, and is divided into six parts: the Sino-Soviet Friendship Section, the Resources, Minerals, Transportation, and Water Conservancy Section, the Mechanical and Electrical Chemical Industry Section, the Agriculture, Forestry, and Food Section, the Light Industry and Textile Section, and the Handicraft Section. There are a total of more than 4,000 exhibits. The Soviet people are eagerly awaiting the opening of the exhibition. Every day, people ask the Chinese staff arranging the exhibition about the opening date and the content of the exhibition.

### 我国工农业展览会即将在莫斯科开幕

1953-07-10
第4版()
专栏：

    我国工农业展览会即将在莫斯科开幕
    【新华社莫斯科八日电】“中华人民共和国工农业展览会”，定于七月十一日在莫斯科中央休息公园开幕。现在展览会会馆的搭盖和布置工作正在紧张进行。这次展览会的规模很大，共占地五千平方公尺，共分六大部分：中苏友好部分，资源矿产交通水利部分，机电化工部分，农林食品部分，轻工业纺织部分和手工艺品部分。总计共有四千多种展览品。苏联人民正在热情地等待着展览会的开幕。每天都有人向布置展览会的我国工作人员询问开幕日期和展览内容。
