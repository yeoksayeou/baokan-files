Title: East Berlin's Major Newspapers Publish General Nam Il's Statement
Author:
Date: 1953-07-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 21st. East Berlin's major newspapers prominently published the news on July 21st regarding General Nam Il's statement at the Korean armistice negotiations on the 19th. "Neues Deutschland" and "Tägliche Rundschau" published the full text of this statement.

### 东柏林各大报  刊载南日大将的声明

1953-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

    东柏林各大报
    刊载南日大将的声明
    【新华社柏林二十一日电】东柏林各大报在二十一日以显著地位刊载了南日大将十九日在朝鲜停战谈判会议上发表声明的消息。“新德意志报”和“每日评论”刊载了这个声明的全文。
