Title: Korean Armistice Negotiations: Both Sides' Delegations Continue to Hold Administrative Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-07-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, July 16th. The Korean Armistice Negotiation's Chinese and Korean delegation issued the following communique on July 16th: The two delegations held an administrative meeting on July 16th. Our side suggested an adjournment. The next meeting is scheduled for 2:00 PM on July 18th.

### 朝鲜停战谈判双方代表团续开行政性会议

1953-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判双方代表团续开行政性会议
    【新华社开城十六日电】    朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团七月十六日发表公报如下：双方代表团七月十六日举行行政性会议，我方建议休会，下一次会议定于七月十八日下午二时举行。
