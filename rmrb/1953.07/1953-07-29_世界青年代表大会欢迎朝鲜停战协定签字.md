Title: World Youth Congress Welcomes the Signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement
Author:
Date: 1953-07-29
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 28th: At the morning session of the World Youth Congress on the 27th, the head of the Chinese youth delegation, Hu Yaobang, announced in his speech that the Korean Armistice Agreement had been signed. This announcement immediately triggered enthusiastic cheers from the entire audience. Delegates stood up one after another, rushing towards the Korean and Chinese delegations, shaking hands, embracing, and kissing incessantly. Hundreds of joyful and emotionally moved delegates went up to the stage one after another, embracing Hu Yaobang. Hu Yaobang's chest was covered with various badges, and he was lifted onto the shoulders of American and British delegates. Delegates threw piles of flowers around Hu Yaobang. Soviet, French, Romanian, and Indian youths sang, cheered, and shouted "Peace" in various languages. They also lifted the Korean delegate onto their shoulders. This heated and emotional scene lasted for an hour.

### 世界青年代表大会欢迎朝鲜停战协定签字

1953-07-29
第4版()
专栏：

    世界青年代表大会欢迎朝鲜停战协定签字
    【新华社二十八日讯】布加勒斯特消息：在世界青年代表大会二十七日上午会议上，中国青年代表团团长胡耀邦在他的演说中宣布朝鲜停战协定已经签字。这个宣布顿时引起全场的热烈欢呼。代表们纷纷站起来，跑到朝鲜和中国代表团那里去，不停地握手、拥抱和接吻。成百个欢欣和情绪激动的代表一个接着一个地跑到讲台上去，与胡耀邦拥抱。胡耀邦的胸前被挂满了各种徽章，他被美国和英国代表抬到肩上。代表们把大堆的鲜花投到胡耀邦的周围。苏联、法国、罗马尼亚、印度青年们唱着，欢呼着，用各种不同的语言高喊着“和平”。他们并把朝鲜代表抬到他们肩上。这样热烈激动的场面历时达一小时之久。
