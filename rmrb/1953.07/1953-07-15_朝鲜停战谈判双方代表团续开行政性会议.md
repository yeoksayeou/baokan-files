Title: Korean Armistice Talks: Both Sides' Delegations Continue Administrative Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-07-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, July 14th. The Korean Armistice Negotiation Delegation of the Korean and Chinese side released a public announcement on July 14th as follows: The two sides' delegations held an administrative meeting on July 14th. The next meeting is scheduled to be held at 11:00 AM on July 15th.

### 朝鲜停战谈判双方代表团续开行政性会议

1953-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判双方代表团续开行政性会议
    【新华社开城十四日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团七月十四日发表公报如下：双方代表团七月十四日举行行政性会议，下一次会议定于七月十五日上午十一时举行。
