Title: Czech and Polish Members of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission Arrive in Kaesong
Author:
Date: 1953-07-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, July 30th—Lieutenant General Blech of Czechoslovakia, a member of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission for the Korean Armistice, and Major General Unglowski of Poland, a member of the Commission, along with the first group of 23 staff members from both countries participating in the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission, arrived in Kaesong at 11 p.m. on the 30th. Those who welcomed them included: Major General Li Sang-cho, the chief delegate on our side to the Military Armistice Commission, as well as delegates Major General Park Il-young, Major General Choi Yong-han, Major General Ding Guoyu, and Chai Chengwen; Yu Pei-mu, Chairman of the Kaesong City Committee of the Korean Workers' Party; and Ji Bong-gwan, Vice-Chairman of the Kaesong City People's Committee, among others.

### 中立国监察委员会的捷、波委员到达开城

1953-07-31
第1版()
专栏：

    中立国监察委员会的捷、波委员到达开城
    【新华社开城三十日电】参加朝鲜停战中立国监察委员会工作的捷克斯洛伐克委员布莱希中将，和波兰委员翁格洛夫斯基少将，连同两国参加中立国监察委员会的首批工作人员二十三人，在三十日晚十一时到达开城。前往欢迎的有：军事停战委员会我方首席委员李相朝将军，委员朴一英将军、崔龙汉将军、丁国钰将军、柴成文将军，和朝鲜劳动党开城市委员会委员长喻培穆，开城市人民委员会副委员长吉凤官等人。
