Title: China Peace Committee Holds Report Meeting, Guo Moruo Reports on Achievements of World Peace Council Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-07-18
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, July 17th: The China People's Committee for World Peace held a report meeting on the 16th, where Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China People's Committee for World Peace, Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council, and head of the Chinese delegation to the World Peace Council Budapest meeting, reported on the proceedings and achievements of the World Peace Council Budapest meeting.
    The report meeting was presided over by Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairman of the China People's Committee for World Peace. Attendees included Li Jishen, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), Jiang Guangnai, member of the Standing Committee, Shao Lizi, Zhang Xiruo, Liang Xi, Liu Ningyi, and Luo Longji, members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the CPPCC who were in Beijing and members of the Standing Committee of the China People's Committee for World Peace, heads of various departments of the China People's Committee for World Peace, World Peace Council members Cai Tingkai, Li Dequan, Lu Cui, Liu Guanyi, and special invited representatives to the Budapest meeting, Laxi Da, and Ulan.
    After listening to Guo Moruo's report, those who attended the report meeting believed that the appeal of the World Peace Council Budapest meeting reflected the sincere wishes of the people of the world and encouraged the fighting spirit of the people of the world in their struggle for peace. The participants unanimously expressed their warm support for the various resolutions of the Budapest meeting and will take practical actions to promote their realization.

### 中国人民保卫世界和平委员会举行报告会  郭沫若报告世界和平理事会会议的成就

1953-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会举行报告会
    郭沫若报告世界和平理事会会议的成就
    【新华社十七日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会于十六日举行报告会，由中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席、世界和平理事会副主席、中国出席世界和平理事会布达佩斯会议代表团团长郭沫若，报告世界和平理事会布达佩斯会议的经过和会议的成就。
    报告会由中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通主持。出席报告会的有中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会副主席李济深、常务委员会委员蒋光鼐，全国委员会在京委员、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员会委员邵力子、张奚若、梁希、刘宁一、罗隆基和中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的各部负责人、世界和平理事会理事蔡廷锴、李德全、陆璀、刘贯一和中国出席布达佩斯会议特邀代表拉希达、乌兰等人。
    参加报告会的人在听取了郭沫若的报告以后，认为世界和平理事会布达佩斯会议的号召，反映了全世界人民的真诚愿望，鼓舞了全世界人民争取和平的斗志。与会者对于布达佩斯会议的各项决议一致表示热烈拥护，并将以实际行动，促其实现。
