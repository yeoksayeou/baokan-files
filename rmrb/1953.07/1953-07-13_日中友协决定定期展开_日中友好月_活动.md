Title: Japan-China Friendship Association Decides to Regularly Launch "Japan-China Friendship Month" Activities
Author:
Date: 1953-07-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, July 11th: July 7th marked the 16th anniversary of the "Lugouqiao Incident," the day Japanese militarists launched their war of aggression against China. The Japan-China Friendship Association held a memorial meeting in Tokyo on the 7th, expressing the Japanese people's will to oppose the revival of Japanese militarism, which brought them endless disasters. To further promote the friendship between the people of Japan and China, the Japan-China Friendship Association has decided to designate September 18th to October 19th as "Japan-China Friendship Month." During this period, various activities will be launched to celebrate the founding of the People's Republic of China.

### 日中友协决定定期展开“日中友好月”活动

1953-07-13
第4版()
专栏：

    日中友协决定定期展开“日中友好月”活动
    【新华社十一日讯】东京消息：七月七日是日本军国主义者发动侵华战争的“芦沟桥事变”十六周年纪念日，日中友好协会七日在东京举行了纪念会，表示日本人民反对给他们带来了无穷灾难的日本军国主义的复活的意志。日中友好协会为了进一步促进日中两国人民的友谊，特决定从九月十八日起到十月十九日止为“日中友好月”。在这期间，将展开各种活动，庆祝中华人民共和国的成立。
