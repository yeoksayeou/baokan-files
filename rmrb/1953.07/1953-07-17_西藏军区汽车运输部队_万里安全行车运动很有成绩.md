Title: The Tibet Military Region's Motor Transport Unit Achieves Great Success in the 10,000-Li Safe Driving Campaign

Author:

Date: 1953-07-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Ya'an: Since launching the 10,000-Li Safe Driving Campaign on the Kangzang Highway in April last year, the Motor Transport Unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Tibet Military Region has achieved great success. According to incomplete statistics from five units, 62 vehicles have safely traveled over 20,000 kilometers, 29 vehicles have safely traveled over 30,000 kilometers, and 151 transportation model workers have emerged.

In order to overcome the mountain roads and climate of the Kangzang Plateau, the Motor Transport Unit launched the "Three Diligences" (diligent inspection, diligent repair, diligent use of the brain) and "Four Defenses" (anti-freeze, anti-skid, anti-collision, and fire prevention) campaign, which prolonged the life of the vehicles. From April to June of this year, the transportation unit completed 104% to 122% of its transportation tasks, ahead of schedule by five days to half a month.

### 西藏军区汽车运输部队  万里安全行车运动很有成绩

1953-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

    西藏军区汽车运输部队
    万里安全行车运动很有成绩
    据新华社雅安电：中国人民解放军西藏军区汽车运输部队，从去年四月在康藏公路上展开了万里安全行车运动以来，已有了很大成绩。根据五个单位的不完全统计，有六十二辆汽车安全行驶了两万公里以上，有二十九辆汽车安全行驶三万公里以上，并出现了一百五十一名运输模范。
    汽车运输部队为了战胜康藏高原的山路和气候，曾展开三勤（勤检查、勤修理、勤用脑筋）和四防（防冻、防滑、防碰、防火）运动，延长了车辆的寿命，今年四月至六月，运输部队提前五天到半个月完成了运输任务百分之一百零四到百分之一百二十二。
