Title: Korean Armistice Talks: Both Sides' Delegations Continue Administrative Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-07-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, July 11th: The Korean Armistice Negotiation's Korean-Chinese delegation released the following communique on July 11th: The two sides' delegations held an administrative meeting on July 11th. The next meeting is scheduled to be held at 11:00 AM on July 12th.

### 朝鲜停战谈判双方代表团继续开行政性会议

1953-07-12
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判双方代表团继续开行政性会议
    【新华社开城十一日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团七月十一日发表公报如下：双方代表团七月十一日举行行政性会议。下一次会议定于七月十二日上午十一时举行。
