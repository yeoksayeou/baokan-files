Title: New York "Daily Worker" Denounces War Cries of U.S. Warmongers, Calls on American People to Strive for a Swift Armistice in Korea
Author:
Date: 1953-07-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 15th:

    The New York "Daily Worker" published an editorial on the 10th, denouncing the war cries of U.S. warmongers and calling on the American people to strive for a swift armistice in Korea. The editorial stated, "Nolans and McCarthys continue to shout that America must continue to bleed in order to support the puppet dictator Syngman Rhee, while making the war profiteers happy." The editorial said, "This is no accident. Among the enemies who most hate the Korean armistice are McCarthy's backers, the Texas oil billionaires. They have made huge profits from the blood shed by Americans." The editorial said, "McCarthy and Nolans have exerted enormous pressure to force the White House to continue the war and carry out new provocations to obstruct the armistice."

    The editorial said that even before the White House replied to the Korean and Chinese side agreeing to resume armistice talks, "Eisenhower respectfully summoned Nolans and their ilk to the White House to seek their consent and opinions." The editorial said, "The people of the country do not need to summon them to seek their opinions and consent, but rather need to conduct a strict investigation into the conspiracy to wage all-out war in Asia. Every hour that the armistice is still delayed is costing the lives of American soldiers." The editorial said, "If Eisenhower's reply to the Korean and Chinese side has been approved by McCarthys and Nolans, then America's hope of peace is slim." The editorial said, "Let us not be deceived by the increasing war propaganda carried out by certain senators. The people should rise up and demand of the White House: not another soldier, not another dollar, to Syngman Rhee! Immediately subdue this provocateur! Immediately achieve an armistice! Immediately hold a great power peace conference!"

### 纽约“工人日报”斥美国好战分子的战争叫嚣  号召美国人民努力争取迅速实现朝鲜停战

1953-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

    纽约“工人日报”斥美国好战分子的战争叫嚣
    号召美国人民努力争取迅速实现朝鲜停战
    【新华社柏林十五日电】纽约“工人日报”十日发表社论，斥责美国的好战分子的战争叫嚣，号召美国人民努力争取迅速实现朝鲜停战。社论说：“诺兰与麦卡锡之流仍在大喊大叫说美国必须继续流血，以便支持傀儡独裁者李承晚，同时使得发战争财的人高兴。”社论说，“这不是偶然的事情，在最憎恨朝鲜停战的敌人中间有着麦卡锡的后台老板，得克萨斯州的石油亿万豪富。他们从美国人所流的血中间获得了巨额利润。”社论说，“麦卡锡与诺兰之流为了迫使白宫把战争继续下去，并进行新的挑衅来阻挠停战，施用了十分巨大的压力。”
    社论说，就在白宫答复朝中方面同意恢复停战谈判的信件以前，“艾森豪威尔恭敬地把诺兰这班人召进白宫，征求他们的同意和意见”。社论说，“全国人民并不需要召他们来征求他们的意见和同意，而是需要对在亚洲进行全面战争的阴谋进行严格的调查。在停战仍被拖延的时候，每一个钟头都在使美国军人丧失生命。”社论说，“假如艾森豪威尔给朝中方面的答复曾得到麦卡锡和诺兰之流的同意的话，那么美国得到和平的希望就微乎其微了。”社论说，“让我们不要被某些参议员所进行的愈来愈多的战争宣传迷惑了。人民应该群起向白宫要求：不要再给李承晚一个兵，一块美元，立刻制服这个挑衅者，立刻实现停战！立刻举行大国和平会议！”
