Title: Two Japanese Groups Escorting the Remains of Anti-Japanese Martyrs Visited Beijing and Returned to Japan
Author:
Date: 1953-07-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 11th:

The entire personnel of the Japanese "Group for Escorting the Remains of Chinese Martyrs" and the "Group for Escorting the Remains of Overseas Chinese Martyrs Left in Japan" left Beijing on the morning of the 11th to return to Japan after a two-day visit in Beijing. Liao Chengzhi, as well as Lin Shixiao and Ni Feijun from the Red Cross Society of China, Tang Mingzhao and Zhu Shilun from the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Zhuang Xiquan, Peng Guanghan, and Chen Manyun from the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, Qiu Ji from the Beijing Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese, Zhao Puchu and Guo Peng from the Buddhist Association of China, Jin Ziming from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Liao Mengxing from the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and writer Han Zi and others were at the station to see them off.

### 日本护送我抗日烈士遗骨的两团体在北京参观后返日

1953-07-12
第4版()
专栏：

    日本护送我抗日烈士遗骨的两团体在北京参观后返日
    【新华社十一日讯】日本“中国人殉难者遗骨护送团”和“殉难英灵留日华侨护送团”全体人员在京参观两天后，已在十一日晨离京返日。到站欢送的有廖承志以及中国红十字会总会林士笑、倪斐君，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会唐明照、朱世纶，中央人民政府华侨事务委员会庄希泉、彭光涵、陈曼云，北京归国华侨联谊会邱及，中国佛教协会赵朴初、郭朋，中华全国总工会金子明，中华全国民主妇女联合会廖梦醒及作家菡子等多人。
