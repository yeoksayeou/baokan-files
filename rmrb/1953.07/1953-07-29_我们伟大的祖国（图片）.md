    Our Great Motherland
    Beidaihe in summer is bustling again. Workers from all over the country have come here in batches for rest and recuperation. This is a corner of the Beidaihe seaside. Art troupe members are performing dances for model coal miners.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    夏天的北戴河又开始热闹起来了。各地工人已经一批一批地到这里来休养。这是北戴河海滨的一角。文工团员们正向煤矿劳动模范们表演舞蹈。
