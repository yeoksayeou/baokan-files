Author:
Date: 1953-07-03
Page: 3

Cultural Briefs:

    Xi'an Daily Begins Publication on July 1st
    The Xi'an Daily, under the leadership of the Xi'an Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, began publication on July 1st. The Xi'an Daily is published daily, with each issue consisting of one four-folio sheet with four pages. The newspaper will implement an editorial policy of localization and mass appeal, with workers as its primary readership.

### 西安日报在七月一日创刊

1953-07-03
第3版()
专栏：文化简讯

    西安日报在七月一日创刊
    中国共产党西安市委员会领导的西安日报，已在七月一日创刊。西安日报每日出版四开四版一张。该报将贯彻地方化和群众化的编辑方针，读者对象以工人为主。
