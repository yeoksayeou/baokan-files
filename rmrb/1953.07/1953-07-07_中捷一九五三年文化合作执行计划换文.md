Title: Exchange of Notes on the 1953 Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan
Author:
Date: 1953-07-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The 1953 Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan, formulated in accordance with the Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Agreement, was exchanged on June 27th, after consultation and agreement between the two parties, by Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China, and Mr. Kousal, Ambassador of the Czechoslovak Republic to China, representing their respective governments.

### 中捷一九五三年文化合作执行计划换文

1953-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

    中捷一九五三年文化合作执行计划换文
    【新华社六日讯】根据中捷文化合作协定制订的中捷一九五三年文化合作执行计划，经双方商谈协议后，已在六月二十七日，由我中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权和捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻我国大使康萨拉，分别代表中华人民共和国中央人民政府和捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府换文。
