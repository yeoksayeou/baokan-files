Title: Our Side Proposes Postponement of Delegation Conference to 19th for Resumption
Author:
Date: 1953-07-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, July 18th. The Korean Armistice Negotiation Korean-Chinese delegation issued the following communique on July 18th:

    On July 18th, our chief representative, through our chief liaison officer, notified the other side that, due to administrative reasons, our side suggests that the delegation conference be postponed to 2:00 PM on July 19th for resumption, to which the other side agreed.

### 我方建议双方代表团大会延至十九日复会

1953-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我方建议双方代表团大会延至十九日复会
    【新华社开城十八日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团七月十八日发表公报如下：
    七月十八日，我方首席代表经由我方首席联络官通知对方：由于行政理由，我方建议双方代表团大会延至七月十九日下午二时复会，对方同意。
