Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-07-28
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The slogan "Learn from the Soviet Union" is increasingly becoming a conscious action of the working masses in our country. Workers at the Jinan Second Machine Factory have successfully trial-produced a Soviet-style nine-meter gantry planer and are now continuing to manufacture a Soviet-style four-meter gantry planer. This is a picture of the workers assembling the four-meter gantry planer.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-07-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    向苏联学习的口号日益成为我国工人群众自觉的行动。济南第二机器厂职工试制苏联式九公尺的龙门刨床业已成功，现正继续制造苏联式四公尺的龙门刨床。这是工人们正在装配四公尺龙门刨床的情形。
