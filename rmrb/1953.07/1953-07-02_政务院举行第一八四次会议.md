Title: 政务院举行第一八四次会议 (Government Administration Council Holds 184th Meeting)
Author: 
Date: 1953-07-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 1st July — The 184th Government Administration Council meeting of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of June 25th. The meeting listened to and approved the report on the main points of work for 1953 made by Wang Heshou, Minister of the Central People's Government Ministry of Heavy Industry. The meeting also passed the General Rules for Setting Up People's Supervision Correspondents by Supervisory Organs of People's Governments at All Levels. In addition, the meeting passed various appointment and removal cases.

### 政务院举行第一八四次会议

1953-07-02
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一八四次会议
    【新华社一日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百八十四次政务会议在六月二十五日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府重工业部王鹤寿部长所作的该部一九五三年工作要点的报告。会议并通过了各级人民政府监察机关设置人民监察通讯员通则。此外，会议通过了各项任免案。
