Title: Chinese New Year Picture Exhibition Opens in Romanian Capital
Author: 
Date: 1953-07-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 14th: Bucharest dispatch: The "People's Republic of China New Year Picture" exhibition, sponsored by the Romanian Association for Foreign Culture, recently opened in Bucharest. The exhibition displays many Chinese New Year pictures reflecting various aspects of the lives of the people in new China.

    At the opening ceremony of the exhibition, Mi·Rosianu, Chairman of the Broadcasting Committee of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic, said in his speech: This exhibition provides another opportunity to promote mutual understanding between the Romanian and Chinese people and strengthen the friendship between the two peoples. Wang Youping, Chinese Ambassador to Romania, said in his speech that this exhibition is a concrete manifestation of the implementation of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement and is deeply convinced that this exhibition will help promote the exchange of cultural and artistic experiences between the two countries and enhance the friendship between the two peoples. After the opening ceremony, the attendees visited the exhibited Chinese New Year pictures with great interest.

### 在罗马尼亚首都我国年画展览会开幕

1953-07-15
第4版()
专栏：

    在罗马尼亚首都我国年画展览会开幕
    【新华社布拉格十四日电】布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚对外文化协会主办的“中华人民共和国年画”展览会，已于日前在布加勒斯特开幕。展览会展出了许多反映新中国人民各方面生活的中国年画。
    在展览会开幕仪式上，罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议广播委员会主席米·罗山亚努致词说：这个展览会又提供了一个机会，来促进罗中两国人民的互相了解和加强两国人民的友谊。我国驻罗马尼亚大使王幼平致词说，这个展览会是执行中罗文化合作协定的一个具体表现，深信这个展览会将有助于两国文化艺术经验的交流和两国人民友谊的增进。开幕仪式举行后，到会的人怀着很大的兴趣参观了展出的中国年画。
