    The North China Region Higher Education Enrollment Committee Established
    Under the leadership of the National Higher Education Enrollment Committee, the North China Region Higher Education Enrollment Committee was officially established in Beijing on July 13th. This committee is responsible for organizing and leading the enrollment work in the North China region.
    The North China Region Higher Education Enrollment Committee is composed of the academic affairs directors or deputy academic affairs directors of eighteen universities in the North China region, responsible comrades from nine units including the Education Bureau of the North China Administrative Committee, the Education Department of the Hebei Provincial People's Government, the Education Department of the Shanxi Provincial People's Government, the Education Bureau of the Tianjin Municipal People's Government, the Education Bureau of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, the Beijing Municipal Health Bureau, the North China Working Committee of the Communist Youth League, the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, and the Beijing Municipal Student Federation. The committee is headed by Zhou Peiyuan (Academic Affairs Director of Peking University), with Qian Weichang (Academic Affairs Director of Tsinghua University), Wu Daren (Academic Affairs Director of Nankai University), and Lin Chuanding (Deputy Academic Affairs Director of Beijing Normal University) serving as deputy directors. Currently, the committee's office has been established, with Deputy Director Lin Chuanding concurrently serving as the office director. The office is located at Beijing Normal University, Dingfu Street, Beijing.
    The office handles the physical examinations, registration, examinations, and organization of grading for applicants. The office's reception hours for visitors are scheduled for Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays from 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM.

    文化简讯
    华北区高等学校招生工作委员会已成立
    华北区高等学校招生工作委员会在全国高等学校招生委员会领导下，已于七月十三日在北京正式成立。该委员会负责组织和领导华北区的招生工作。
    华北区高等学校招生工作委员会，由华北区的高等学校中的十八个学校的教务长或副教务长、华北行政委员会教育局、河北省人民政府教育厅、山西省人民政府教育厅、天津市人民政府教育局、北京市人民政府教育局、北京市人民政府卫生局、青年团华北工委、青年团北京市委、北京市学联等九个单位的负责同志组成。该委员会由周培源（北京大学教务长）任主任委员，钱伟长（清华大学教务长）、吴大任（南开大学教务长）、林传鼎（北京师范大学副教务长）任副主任委员。目前，该委员会办公室已成立，由林传鼎副主任委员兼办公室主任。办公室设在北京定阜大街北京师范大学。
    办公室办理报考生的体格检查、报名、考试、组织阅卷等工作。办公室接待来访者的时间定于每星期一、三、五下午三时到六时。
