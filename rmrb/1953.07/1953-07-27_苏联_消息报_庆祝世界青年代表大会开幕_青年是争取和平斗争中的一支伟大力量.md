Title: Soviet "Izvestia" Celebrates the Opening of the World Youth Congress: Youth are a Great Force in the Struggle for Peace
Author:
Date: 1953-07-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 26th: Izvestia published an editorial on the 25th entitled "World Youth Congress," stating: The Third World Youth Congress opens today in Bucharest, the capital of Romania. Approximately 1,500 youth representatives from around 100 countries will gather together. The agenda includes issues such as the struggle for peace and national independence, the rights and cultural exchange of youth, and youth's solidarity in the struggle for a better future. The Congress will summarize the work of the World Federation of Democratic Youth since the Second World Youth Congress. The preparations for the Congress demonstrate that the majority of youth in various countries support the convening of the Congress.

    In capitalist countries, colonies, and dependent countries, the younger generation lives a difficult and rightless life. For example, in Italy, more than 600,000 young men and women are unemployed. In the United States, 74 percent of young people under the age of 21 are unemployed. In capitalist countries, young workers often receive much less pay than adult workers for doing the same work. The situation for youth in colonies and dependent countries is even worse. Warmongers treat youth as cannon fodder, as tools for implementing their misanthropic plans. In the wars being waged by imperialist aggressors in Korea, Vietnam, and Malaya, hundreds of thousands of young people have sacrificed their lives or been maimed. The preparations for war mean indescribable suffering for youth in capitalist countries. It deprives hundreds of thousands of young men and women of the opportunity to receive an education. Imperialists are doing their utmost to poison the minds of the younger generation with militarism and racial hatred, turning youth into docile executors of their sinister will.

    Youth from all over the world have risen to struggle against the aggressive actions of the enemies of peace and democracy, against new wars, and against policies that create hunger, poverty, and unemployment. The representatives participating in the Youth Congress will report on the progress of this struggle and the growth of the ranks of democratic youth.

    The editorial concludes that the World Youth Congress and the subsequent World Youth and Students Festival for Peace and Friendship will be a powerful demonstration of the world's younger generation's increasingly strong determination to safeguard peace and the freedom and national independence of their motherlands, and to oppose the aggressive actions of imperialist aggressors. Youth are a great force in the struggle for peace. Neither terror nor threats, neither lies nor slander can destroy this force. The voice of progressive youth from the platform of the World Youth Congress will be a voice for peace and international friendship.

### 苏联“消息报”庆祝世界青年代表大会开幕  青年是争取和平斗争中的一支伟大力量

1953-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联“消息报”庆祝世界青年代表大会开幕
    青年是争取和平斗争中的一支伟大力量
    【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：消息报二十五日以“世界青年代表大会”为题发表社论说：第三届世界青年代表大会今天在罗马尼亚首都布加勒斯特开幕。大约一百个国家的一千五百多个青年代表将聚会一堂，大会议程包括为和平和民族独立而斗争、青年的权利和文化交流、青年在争取美好未来的斗争中的团结等问题。代表大会将总结世界民主青年联盟在第二届世界青年代表大会以后的工作。大会的筹备情况说明，世界各国青年大多数都支持大会的召开。
    在资本主义国家、殖民地和附属国中，青年一代过着艰苦的、毫无权利的生活。例如在意大利，有六十多万男女青年没有职业。在美国，二十一岁以下的青年中有百分之七十四失业。在资本主义国家中，青年工人和成年工人虽做同样的工作，但所得报酬通常要少得多。殖民地和附属国青年的处境更为恶劣。战争贩子们把青年当作炮灰、当作实行他们的仇视人类计划的工具。在帝国主义侵略者正在进行的朝鲜、越南和马来亚战争中，已有数十万青年牺牲了，或者变成了残废。备战对资本主义国家青年说来意味着难以形容的痛苦。它剥夺了数十万青年男女受教育的机会。帝国主义者极力用军国主义和种族仇恨来毒化年青一代的思想，把青年变成他们的罪恶意志的驯服的执行者。
    世界各国的青年已经起来进行斗争，反对和平民主敌人的侵略行动，反对新战争，反对造成饥饿、贫穷和失业的政策。参加青年代表大会的代表们将在大会上报告这个斗争的进行情况和民主青年的队伍发展壮大的情况。
    社论最后说，世界青年代表大会和随后的世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节将是一个强大的示威，表明世界年青一代维护和平和祖国的自由与民族独立、反对帝国主义侵略者的侵略行为的日益加强的战斗决心。青年是争取和平斗争中的一支伟大力量。恐怖或威胁、谎言或诽谤都不能破坏这支力量。进步青年在世界青年代表大会讲台上发出的呼声，将成为和平与国际友谊的呼声。
