Title: Korean Armistice Negotiations: Korean-Chinese Delegation Issues Communiqué; Both Delegations to Resume Administrative Meeting Today
Author:
Date: 1953-07-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, July 9th – The Korean-Chinese delegation to the Korean armistice negotiations issued the following communiqué on July 9th: Marshal Kim Il-sung, Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army, and General Peng Dehuai, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, sent a letter to General Clark, Commander-in-Chief of the United Nations Command, on July 7th, agreeing to a regular meeting of the negotiating delegations of both sides to discuss the implementation of the armistice agreement and the various preparatory work before the signing of the armistice agreement.
    On July 9th, the chief negotiators of both sides, through their liaison officers, agreed that the plenary session of both delegations would resume the administrative meeting at 11:00 a.m. on July 10th.

### 朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团发表公报  双方代表团今天恢复行政性会议

1953-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团发表公报
    双方代表团今天恢复行政性会议
    【新华社开城九日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团七月九日发表公报如下：七月七日朝鲜人民军最高司令官金日成元帅与中国人民志愿军司令员彭德怀将军致函联合国军总司令克拉克将军，同意双方谈判代表团定期会晤，商谈有关停战协定的实施以及停战协定签字前各种准备工作的问题。
    七月九日双方首席谈判代表通过双方联络官，商定双方代表团大会于七月十日上午十一时恢复行政性会议。
