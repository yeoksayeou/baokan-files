Title: Mongolian-Soviet Cultural Association Hosts Soviet Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1953-07-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, July 8th: TASS Ulaanbaatar, July 7th: The Central Committee of the Mongolian-Soviet Cultural Association held a reception on the 6th to welcome the Soviet cultural delegation and Soviet actors who came to the Mongolian People's Republic to participate in the Mongolian-Soviet Friendship Month.

    Among those present at the reception were: the Soviet cultural delegation headed by Soviet People's Artist S.A. Gerasimov, many Soviet actors, Mongolian People's Republic Prime Minister Tsedenbal, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, ministers, Mongolian army generals, and scientific and artistic workers of the Mongolian People's Republic. Also present at the reception were Soviet Ambassador to the Mongolian People's Republic G.I. Ivannikov, diplomatic representatives of China, Korea, and Poland, and members of the Polish Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble who are visiting the Mongolian People's Republic.

    The reception was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 蒙苏文化协会招待苏联文化代表团

1953-07-09
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙苏文化协会招待苏联文化代表团
    【新华社八日讯】塔斯社乌兰巴托七日讯：蒙苏文化协会中央委员会在六日举行招待会，招待前来蒙古人民共和国参加蒙苏友好月的苏联文化代表团和苏联演员。
    出席招待会的有：以苏联人民艺术家谢·安·格拉西莫夫为首的苏联文化代表团、许多苏联演员；蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔、蒙古人民革命党中央委员会政治局委员、各部长、蒙古陆军将领以及蒙古人民共和国的科学和艺术工作者。出席招待会的还有苏联驻蒙古人民共和国大使格·伊·伊凡尼科夫，中国、朝鲜和波兰的外交代表以及正在访问蒙古人民共和国的波兰玛佐夫舍歌舞团团员。
    招待会在热烈友好的气氛中举行。
