Title: Yunnan Various Ethnic Groups Organize Comfort Groups to Console Border Troops and People of All Ethnic Groups
Author:
Date: 1953-07-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 14th. The Yunnan Province Various Ethnic Groups Border Comfort Group, since departing from Kunming in batches in early April, has so far visited more than ten counties in the border areas of Yunnan Province, including Baoshan, Tengchong, Yingjiang, Mianning, Cheli, Fohai, Nanjiao, Honghe, and Jinping. They have extended their regards to the Chinese People's Liberation Army border troops, ethnic work cadres, and people of all ethnic minorities.

During their visits, the Comfort Group conveyed the love and hopes of the people of the motherland to them, reported on the great achievements of the motherland's construction over the past three years, and explained that these achievements were inseparable from their arduous work in the border areas. Each report by the Comfort Group was warmly welcomed by the soldiers stationed on the border, ethnic work cadres, and people of all ethnic groups. After the Comfort Group conducted visits and communication in the Kawa Mountains on the southern border of Yunnan, the Chinese People's Liberation Army soldiers stationed there were greatly inspired. They said: Although we are guarding the Kawa Mountains far from the mainland of the motherland, Chairman Mao and the people of the motherland are concerned about us.

The Comfort Group brought a large number of comfort letters and gifts. Soldiers, cadres, and people of all ethnic groups thanked the people of the motherland for their meticulous care. When the border soldiers received comfort items inscribed with the words "Dedicated to the Defenders of the Motherland," they felt extremely honored. They all expressed their determination to maintain and cherish the honor given by the people of the motherland, guard the border of the motherland, and defend the construction of the motherland.

The Comfort Group's film team and acrobatics troupe cooperated with the Comfort Group to conduct screenings and performances in various border areas.

### 云南各族各界组织慰问团慰问边疆驻军和各族人民

1953-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

    云南各族各界组织慰问团慰问边疆驻军和各族人民
    【新华社昆明十四日电】云南省各族各界边疆慰问团，自四月初由昆明分批出发，到目前为止，已在祖国云南省边疆的保山、腾冲、盈江、缅宁、车里、佛海、南峤、红河、金屏等十多个县，向中国人民解放军边防部队、民族工作干部和各少数民族人民进行了慰问。
    慰问团在各地进行慰问时，传达了祖国人民对他们的热爱和希望，报告了祖国三年来建设的伟大成就，并说明这些成就和他们在边疆地区的艰苦工作是分不开的。慰问团的每一次报告，都赢得驻守边疆的战士、民族工作干部和各族人民的热烈欢迎。慰问团在滇南边疆佧瓦山进行慰问传达后，驻守在该地的中国人民解放军战士，十分感奋。他们说：我们虽然守卫在距祖国内地遥远的佧瓦山上，但是，毛主席和祖国人民都在关心我们。
    慰问团带去大批的慰问信、慰问品。战士们、干部们和各族人民都感谢祖国人民对他们无微不至的关怀。当边防战士们收到写有“献给祖国保卫者”字样的慰问品时，感到无上光荣。他们都表示要保持和爱护祖国人民给予的光荣，守卫祖国边疆，保卫祖国的建设。
    慰问团的电影队和杂技团，配合慰问团深入边疆各地放映和进行了慰问演出。
