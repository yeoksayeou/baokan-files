Title: The German Communist Party Issues a Statement on the Signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement: The Korean Armistice Inspires the Movement to Strive for a Peaceful Resolution of the German Question

Author: 
Date: 1953-07-31
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 29th (TASS dispatch from Berlin, July 29th): The German News Agency, Dusseldorf, July 28th dispatch, the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the German Communist Party issued a statement regarding the signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement. The statement said: The signing of the armistice agreement is a great victory for the Korean people, the Chinese People's Volunteers, and all peace-loving people around the world. The signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement has great significance for Germany because it has given new impetus to the movement to strive for a peaceful resolution of the German question. The statement emphasized: All Germans urgently demand that an all-German agreement be reached on the issue of all-German elections, the establishment of an all-German government, and the conclusion of a peace treaty with Germany. The statement said: The recent proposals of the government of the German Democratic Republic should no longer be ignored. Every German knows from the Korean experience that the method of consultation and agreement consistently advocated by the German Communist Party is correct.

### 德国共产党就朝鲜停战协定签订发表声明  朝鲜停战使争取和平解决德国问题运动得到鼓舞

1953-07-31
第4版()
专栏：

    德国共产党就朝鲜停战协定签订发表声明
    朝鲜停战使争取和平解决德国问题运动得到鼓舞
    【新华社二十九日讯】塔斯社柏林二十九日讯：德意志通讯社杜塞尔多夫二十八日消息，德国共产党中央委员会书记处发表了关于朝鲜停战协定签订的声明。声明说：停战协定的签订是朝鲜人民、中国人民志愿军和全世界爱好和平的人民所获得的巨大胜利。朝鲜停战协定的签订对于德国具有重大的意义，因为它使争取和平解决德国问题的运动得到了新的鼓舞。声明着重指出：全体德国人迫切要求在全德选举问题上取得全德协议，成立一个全德政府和缔结对德和约。声明说：德意志民主共和国政府最近的建议不应该再被忽视了。每个德国人从朝鲜的经验知道：德国共产党所一贯主张的协商和协议的办法是正确的。
