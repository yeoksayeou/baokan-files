Title: The Government of the German Democratic Republic Further Improves the Living Standards of the Working People
Author:
Date: 1953-07-26
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 24th: According to the "Neues Deutschland" newspaper, the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic adopted several measures on July 23rd to further improve the living standards of the working people of the Republic. Among these measures, one stipulates increasing the wages of certain workers in state-owned enterprises and increasing the wages of employees in state-owned retail and cooperative trade systems. Some resolutions stipulate amending the tax system and reducing income tax rates.

### 民主德国政府进一步改善劳动人民生活

1953-07-26
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国政府进一步改善劳动人民生活
    【新华社二十五日讯】塔斯社柏林二十四日讯：据“新德意志报”报道，德意志民主共和国部长会议在七月二十三日通过了更进一步改善共和国劳动人民生活的若干措施。这些措施中，有一项规定提高国营企业某些工人的工资，并且提高国营零售业和合作社贸易系统营业员的工资。有一些决议规定修改税收制度，削减所得税税率。
