Title: Resources from the Soviet Union, Poland, Albania, and Other Countries Continuously Shipped to Democratic Germany
Author:
Date: 1953-07-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 17th: According to the German News Agency, in the first half of July, Frankfurt (on the Oder) received a total of 3,000 railcars of valuable food from the Soviet Union for Democratic Germany, including large quantities of butter, fats, other oils, canned fish, and so on. In addition, 84 railcars of pork, 9 railcars of butter, and 39 railcars of canned fish were shipped from Poland. Six railcars of tobacco were shipped from Albania.

### 苏、波、阿等国物资源源运到民主德国

1953-07-18
第4版()
专栏：

    苏、波、阿等国物资源源运到民主德国
    【新华社十七日讯】据德意志通讯社讯：七月上半月，法兰克福（奥得河畔）共到了苏联运给民主德国的三千车皮宝贵的食物，包括大量的牛油、脂肪、其他油类、罐头鱼等等。此外，又从波兰运到八十四车皮的猪，九车皮牛油，三十九车皮的罐头鱼。从阿尔巴尼亚运到六车皮的烟草。
