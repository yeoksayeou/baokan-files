Title: Our Cultural Counselor in India Hosts Farewell Banquet for Indian Art Delegation Visiting China
Author: 
Date: 1953-07-16
Page: 1

    Our Cultural Counselor in India Hosts Farewell Banquet for Indian Art Delegation Visiting China
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 15th - On the eve of the Indian Art Delegation's departure for China, Kang Maozhao, Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in India, held a reception to bid farewell to the delegation members.
    Officials from the Indian Ministry of External Affairs and the Ministry of Education, Dr. Kitchlew, Chairman of the All-India Peace Council, Dr. Gyan Chand, Vice-Chairman of the All-India Peace Council, as well as well-known artists, cultural workers, and journalists from Delhi were also invited to attend the reception.
    At the end of the reception, delegation members Ustad Vilayat Hussain Khan, a sitar player, and Damayanti Joshi, a famous female dancer, performed. Their performances were warmly welcomed.

### 我驻印度大使馆文化参赞为印度访华艺术代表团饯行

1953-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我驻印度大使馆文化参赞为印度访华艺术代表团饯行
    【新华社新德里十五日电】中国驻印度大使馆文化参赞康矛召在印度访华艺术代表团启程前往中国前夕，举行招待会，为代表团人员饯行。
    应邀参加招待会的还有印度外交部和教育部的官员、全印和平理事会主席克其鲁博士、全印和平理事会副主席吉安·昌德博士以及德里的著名艺术家、文化工作者和新闻界人士等。
    在招待会结束时，代表团团员、西塔（印度乐器名）演奏家瓦拉雅特·侯赛尼·康恩和著名女舞蹈家达玛扬蒂·乔希进行了表演。他们的表演受到热烈的欢迎。更正
    九日本报刊登的格·马·马林科夫“关于党组织在工业和运输业方面的任务”一文，在第三版第二栏倒数第十九行中“只有八万二千人……”应改为“只有六万八千人……”。
