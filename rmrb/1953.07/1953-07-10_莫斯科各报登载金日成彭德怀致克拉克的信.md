Title: Moscow Newspapers Publish Kim Il-sung and Peng Dehuai's Letter to Clark
Author:
Date: 1953-07-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 9th. All Moscow newspapers on the 9th published the letter from Marshal Kim Il-sung and Commander Peng Dehuai of the Korean-Chinese Delegation at the Panmunjom Korean Armistice Negotiations to General Clark, Commander-in-Chief of the United Nations Forces, on July 7th.

### 莫斯科各报登载金日成彭德怀致克拉克的信

1953-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科各报登载金日成彭德怀致克拉克的信
    【新华社莫斯科九日电】莫斯科各报九日都登载了板门店朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团公布的金日成元帅和彭德怀司令员七月七日致联合国军总司令克拉克的信。
