Title: China's Delegation Leaves Beijing for Vienna to Attend World Congress of Teachers
Author:
Date: 1953-07-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 12th: The World Congress of Teachers will be held in Vienna, the capital of Austria, starting July 21st. The Chinese delegation to the conference left Beijing by plane on the 12th to attend the meeting.

The delegation list is as follows:

	Head of Delegation: Fang Ming, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Educational Workers' Union of China

	Members: Sheng Jin (female), Deputy Director of the Labor Protection Department of the National Committee of the Educational Workers' Union of China

	Zhang Chao, Deputy Director of the International Department of the National Committee of the Educational Workers' Union of China (also Secretary of the Delegation)

	Liu Honghe, Director of the Hebei Provincial Working Committee of the Educational Workers' Union of China

	Li Guangqi, Vice Chairman of the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the Educational Workers' Union of China

	Hu Zhitao (female), Deputy Director of Studies, Affiliated Women's High School of Normal University

	Zhang Junde, Model Teacher, Inventor of the Lunar Globe

The delegation is carrying a set of "Education and Teachers of the People's Republic of China" pictures, which will be exhibited during the conference.
