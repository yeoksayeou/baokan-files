Title: Celebrating the Opening of China's Industrial and Agricultural Exhibition in Moscow; Reception Held by the Presidium of the Soviet Chamber of Commerce
Author: 
Date: 1953-07-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 18th: To celebrate the opening of the "People's Republic of China Industrial and Agricultural Exhibition" in Moscow, the Presidium of the Soviet Chamber of Commerce held a reception on the evening of the 16th. Invited to attend were Ge Baoquan, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, embassy staff, Li Qiang, Commercial Counselor, staff of the Commercial Counselor's Office, and Chinese personnel responsible for arranging the exhibition. The Soviet side was represented by Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs and current Soviet Ambassador to China, Kuznetsov, Deputy Ministers of Domestic and Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union, Borisov and Kuruzkov, Chairman of the Soviet Chamber of Commerce, Nesterov, and others. Also invited to attend were diplomatic envoys to the Soviet Union from countries that have diplomatic relations with China.

### 庆贺我国工农业展览会在莫斯科开幕  苏联商会主席团举行招待会

1953-07-20
第4版()
专栏：

    庆贺我国工农业展览会在莫斯科开幕
    苏联商会主席团举行招待会
    【新华社莫斯科十八日电】为庆贺“中华人民共和国工农业展览会”在莫斯科的开幕，苏联商会主席团在十六日晚举行招待会。应邀出席的有我国驻苏联大使馆临时代办戈宝权和大使馆人员、商务参赞李强和商务参赞处人员，以及负责布置展览会的我国工作人员。苏联方面出席的有：苏联外交部副部长、现在国内的苏联驻中国大使库兹涅佐夫，苏联国内和对外贸易部副部长波里索夫和库鲁兹科夫，苏联商会主席涅斯捷罗夫等。应邀出席的还有与我国有邦交关系的各国驻苏联外交使节。
