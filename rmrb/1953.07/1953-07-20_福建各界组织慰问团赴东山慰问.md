Title: Fujian Circles Organize慰问团 to Visit Dongshan

Author:
Date: 1953-07-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, 19th—The Fujian Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Fujian Provincial People's Government, various democratic parties, and people's organizations have jointly formed a慰问团, led by Meng Dongbo, Secretary-General of the Fujian Provincial People's Government. On July 19th, they went to Dongshan to visit the Chinese People's Liberation Army coastal defense troops who defeated the remnant bandit troops of Chiang Kai-shek and the local residents who assisted the troops in combat. The 慰问团 brought a large number of gifts and letters of慰问. The Fujian Provincial Peking Opera Troupe, the Highway Art Troupe, the Southern Fujian Art Troupe, and seven film projection teams also accompanied the 慰问团.

The Political Department of the Fujian Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army also sent Jiang Junji, Director of the Cultural Department of the department, on the 18th, leading the Hua East Military Region People's Liberation Army Theater Drama Troupe, the Fujian Military Region Art Troupe Song and Dance Team, and a film team performing in Fujian, and brought a large number of gifts and letters of慰问 to visit the coastal defense troops in Dongshan.

### 福建各界组织慰问团赴东山慰问

1953-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

    福建各界组织慰问团赴东山慰问
    【新华社福州十九日电】中国共产党福建省委员会、福建省人民政府、各民主党派和各人民团体联合组成慰问团，由福建省人民政府秘书长孟东波率领，在七月十九日前往东山慰问击溃蒋介石残余匪军的中国人民解放军海防部队和协助部队作战的当地居民。慰问团带有大批慰问品和慰问信。随同慰问团前往的还有福建省京剧团、公路文工团、闽南文工团和七个电影放映队等。
    中国人民解放军福建军区政治部，也在十八日派出该部文化部部长蒋峻基，带领来福建演出的华东军区解放军剧院话剧队、福建军区文工团歌舞队和电影队并携带大批慰问品和慰问信，前往东山慰问海防部队。
