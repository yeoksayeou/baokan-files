Title: Korean Armistice Talks: Korean-Chinese Delegation Issues Communiqué; Staff Officers and Liaison Officers of Both Sides Hold Administrative Meetings Separately
Author:
Date: 1953-07-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, July 23rd: The Korean-Chinese delegation to the Korean armistice talks issued the following communiqué on July 23rd: Staff officers of both sides and liaison officers of both sides held administrative meetings separately on July 23rd.

### 朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团发表公报  双方参谋人员和联络官分别开行政性会议

1953-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团发表公报
    双方参谋人员和联络官分别开行政性会议
    【新华社开城二十三日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团七月二十三日发表公报如下：双方参谋人员与双方联络官七月二十三日分别举行行政性会议。
