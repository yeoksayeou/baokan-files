Title: Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Appoints Several New Ministers and Ambassadors
Author: 
Date: 1953-07-19
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 17th (TASS dispatch): The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has appointed Vyacheslav Malyshev as Minister of the Ministry of Medium Machine Building Industry of the USSR, and removed him from his post as Minister of the Ministry of Transport and Heavy Machine Building Industry of the USSR.
    Ivan Nosenko has been appointed Minister of the Ministry of Transport and Heavy Machine Building Industry of the USSR.
    The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has appointed Anatoly Lavrentiev as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the USSR to Iran, and removed him from his post as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the USSR to the Romanian People's Republic.
    The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has removed I. Sadchikov from his post as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the USSR to Iran, and will assign him to another position.

### 苏联最高苏维埃主席团任命几个新部长和驻外使节

1953-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃主席团任命几个新部长和驻外使节
    【新华社讯】塔斯社莫斯科十七日电：苏联最高苏维埃主席团任命维亚切斯拉夫·马利歇夫为苏联中型机器制造工业部部长，解除他的苏联运输和重型机器制造工业部部长的职务。
    伊凡·诺森科被任命为苏联运输和重型机器制造工业部部长。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团任命阿纳托利·拉夫连捷夫为苏联驻伊朗特命全权大使，解除他的苏联驻罗马尼亚人民共和国特命全权大使的职务。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团解除伊·萨德契科夫的苏联驻伊朗特命全权大使的职务，另有任用。
