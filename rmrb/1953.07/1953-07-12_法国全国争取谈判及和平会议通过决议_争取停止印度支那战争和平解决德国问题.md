Title: French National Conference for Negotiation and Peace Passes Resolution: Striving to Stop the Indochina War and Peacefully Resolve the German Question
Author:
Date: 1953-07-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Paris: TASS reports that the Standing Committee of the French National Peace Council issued a communique stating that 1,870 delegates from eighty provinces participated in the National Conference for Negotiation and Peace held in Paris on June 27th and 28th. The delegates included prominent peace fighters, representatives of many people's organizations, and representatives and members of many political parties and trade unions.

    According to the communique, the declaration unanimously adopted by the conference stipulated several goals, and the conference situation showed that it is possible to unite the French people in the struggle to achieve these goals.

    The National Peace Council decided to launch a movement to immediately stop the Indochina War through negotiation; to reject the Bonn and Paris treaties and strive for a solution to the German question; and to strive for the settlement of international disputes through negotiation.

### 法国全国争取谈判及和平会议通过决议  争取停止印度支那战争和平解决德国问题

1953-07-12
第4版()
专栏：

    法国全国争取谈判及和平会议通过决议
    争取停止印度支那战争和平解决德国问题
    【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社巴黎讯：法国全国和平理事会常务委员会发表公报说：来自八十省的一千八百七十位代表参加了六月二十七日和二十八日在巴黎举行的全国争取谈判及和平会议。代表中包括卓越的和平战士、许多人民团体的代表以及许多政治党派的代表与工会会员。
    据公报说：会议一致通过的宣言中规定了一些目标，会议情况并说明把法国人民团结在争取达到这些目标的斗争中是可能的。
    全国和平理事会决定发动一个运动，争取通过协商的办法立刻停止印度支那战争；拒绝波恩和巴黎条约和争取解决德国问题；争取通过协商的办法来解决国际纠纷。
