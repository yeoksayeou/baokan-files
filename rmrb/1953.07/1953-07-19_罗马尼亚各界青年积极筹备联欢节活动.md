Title: Romanian Youth From All Walks of Life Actively Prepare for Festival Activities
Author:
Date: 1953-07-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, July 17th: Romanian youth are enthusiastically preparing for the Fourth World Youth and Student Festival for Peace and Friendship, which will soon be held in their capital.

    Romanian young workers have launched a labor competition to welcome the festival, with more than 300,000 participants, of whom more than 25,000 have already overfulfilled their annual production plans.

    While completing their production tasks, young workers in Bucharest are also enthusiastically participating in the construction of buildings for the festival. Bucharest's largest new park is nearing completion. Many newly built or renovated stadiums, concert halls, open-air theaters, swimming pools, etc., will also be completed rapidly.

    Young artists are actively preparing programs for the festival. A competition to select the best artists to participate in the festival is underway throughout the country. 3,800 young people participated in solo and folk art competitions. 1,500 winners of district-level competitions are participating in state-level competitions. A choir composed of more than 2,000 Romanian youth will give concerts during the festival. Athletes are also stepping up their practice to participate in the festival's programs. Six hundred athletes will participate in the festival's friendly matches.

### 罗马尼亚各界青年积极筹备联欢节活动

1953-07-19
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚各界青年积极筹备联欢节活动
    【新华社十七日讯】据塔斯社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚青年正在进行如火如荼的筹备工作，迎接即将在他们首都举行的第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节。
    罗马尼亚青年工人为着迎接联欢节发起了劳动竞赛，参加者已有三十万人以上，其中有两万五千多人已超额完成了本年度的生产计划。
    布加勒斯特的青年工人在完成生产任务之余，还热烈参加了准备供联欢节使用的建筑物的建设工作。新建的布加勒斯特最大的公园即将完工。许多新建的或整修的运动场、音乐厅、露天剧院、游泳池等也将迅速完工。
    青年艺术家们积极为联欢节准备节目。挑选参加联欢节的最优秀艺术家的比赛正在全国各地进行。三千八百名青年参加了独唱和民间艺术的比赛。一千五百名区级比赛的优胜者正参加州一级的比赛。一个由两千多名罗马尼亚青年组成的合唱团，将在联欢节期间开演奏会。运动员们也正加紧练习参加联欢节的节目。六百名运动员将参加联欢节的友谊赛。
