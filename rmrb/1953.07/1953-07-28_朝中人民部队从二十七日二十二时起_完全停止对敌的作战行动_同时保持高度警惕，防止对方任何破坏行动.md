Title: Korean-Chinese People's Forces Completely Cease Combat Operations Against the Enemy from 22:00 on the 27th, While Maintaining High Vigilance to Prevent Any Destructive Actions by the Other Side
Author:
Date: 1953-07-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, July 27th. In accordance with the armistice order issued by Marshal Kim Il-sung, Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army, and General Peng Dehuai, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, all armed forces of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers have completely ceased combat operations against the enemy on the entire Korean front from 22:00 Korean time on July 27th.

	The infantry, artillery, and tank units of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers, who fought victoriously near the 38th parallel, simultaneously ceased firing, bombarding, and all combat operations against the enemy along the military demarcation line, which stretches for more than 220 kilometers across central Korea.

	The powerful coastal defense forces (including naval and coastal artillery units) of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers, guarding the eastern and western coasts and islands of all sizes in northern Korea, also simultaneously ceased shelling and other combat operations against enemy ships and islands controlled by the enemy.

	The air force units of the Korean People's Army, which strongly defended the skies of northern Korea, also simultaneously ceased combat operations.

	Currently, the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers are arranging the implementation of the various provisions of the armistice agreement on the already-ceasefired front, and all military forces, supplies, and equipment will be withdrawn from the demilitarized zone within seventy-two hours after the armistice agreement takes effect. At the same time, every commander and fighter keeps in mind the orders of Marshal Kim Il-sung and Commander Peng Dehuai, maintaining a high degree of vigilance, holding their positions, and preventing any aggression and destructive actions from the other side.

### 朝中人民部队从二十七日二十二时起  完全停止对敌的作战行动  同时保持高度警惕，防止对方任何破坏行动

1953-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

    朝中人民部队从二十七日二十二时起
    完全停止对敌的作战行动
    同时保持高度警惕，防止对方任何破坏行动
    【新华社朝鲜前线二十七日电】朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军全体武装部队遵照朝鲜人民军最高司令官金日成元帅和中国人民志愿军司令员彭德怀将军发布的停战命令，已于七月二十七日朝鲜时间二十二时起在朝鲜全线完全停止了对敌军的作战行动。
    曾胜利地战斗在三八线附近的朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军的步兵部队、炮兵部队和坦克部队，在横贯朝鲜中部约二百二十多公里长的军事分界线上，同时停止了对敌军的射击、轰击和一切作战行动。
    守卫在朝鲜北部东、西两侧海岸和大小岛屿上的朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军的强大的海防部队
    （包括海军和海岸炮兵部队），也同时停止了对敌军舰艇及为敌军所控制的岛屿进行炮击和其他作战行动。
    强有力地保卫朝鲜北部天空的朝鲜人民军的空军部队，也同时停止了作战行动。
    目前，朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军正在已经停火的战线上安排履行停战协定各项条款的工作，所有军事力量、供应和装备将在停战协定生效后的七十二小时内自非军事区撤离。同时，每个指挥员、战斗员都牢记着金日成元帅和彭德怀司令员的命令，保持高度的警惕性，坚守阵地，防止来自对方的任何侵袭和破坏行动。
