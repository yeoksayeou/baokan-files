Title: Our Country's Youth Art Troupe Holds Grand Performance in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1953-07-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 24th: The Youth Art Troupe of the Chinese Youth Delegation, which is participating in the World Youth and Students for Peace and Friendship Festival and passing through Moscow, held a grand performance on the evening of the 22nd at the open-air theater of the Gorky Central Cultural Recreation Park. This was the official performance of the art troupe after the previews at the Pushkin Theater and the Trade Union Building. More than 12,000 spectators enjoyed the songs and dances of the Chinese youth. A banner reading "Long Live the Permanent and Unbreakable Friendship between Chinese and Soviet Youth" was hung above the stage. After the performance began, the members of the art troupe sang "Moscow-Beijing," "The East is Red," "Song of Stalin," and the Qinghai folk song "Half a Moon Climbing Up" in the first half of the performance. They played "Hundred Birds Paying Homage to the Phoenix" on the Suona horn, sang a Kunming ditty (female voice) as a solo, and performed the "Lotus Lantern" dance from southern Shaanxi and the "Lion Dance" from Hubei. The programs performed in the second half included: "Single Drum" from the Northeast, "Tea Picking Dance" from Fujian, a male solo (in Russian) of the Russian folk song "Song of the Volga Boatmen," and Peking Opera "Yandang Mountain." The audience praised the Chinese youth art troupe for the Sino-Soviet friendship, and the magnificent, beautiful, and lively art from the folk. At the end of the performance, all the actors bid farewell to the audience amidst warm applause and flower offerings, and the entire stage and audience sang "The March of the World's Democratic Youth."

The art troupe left Moscow for Bucharest on the 24th.

### 我国青年艺术团  在莫斯科举行盛大公演

1953-07-25
第4版()
专栏：

    我国青年艺术团
    在莫斯科举行盛大公演
    【新华社莫斯科二十四日电】参加世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节路过莫斯科的中国青年代表团的青年艺术团，二十二日晚在高尔基中央文化休憩公园的露天剧院，举行了盛大的公演。这是艺术团在普希金剧院和工会大厦预演之后的正式演出。有一万二千多个观众欣赏了中国青年的歌舞。舞台顶上横挂着“中苏两国青年永久的牢不可破的友谊万岁”的标语。表演开始后，艺术团团员在上半场合唱了“莫斯科—北京”、“东方红”、“斯大林之歌”和青海民歌“半个月亮爬上来”，用唢呐演奏了“百鸟朝凤”，独唱了昆明小调（女音），并表演了陕南的“荷花灯”舞和湖北的“舞狮”。下半场表演的节目有：东北的“单鼓”、福建的“采茶舞”、男音独唱（用俄语）俄罗斯民歌“伏尔加船夫曲”和京剧“雁荡山”。观众们都盛赞青年艺术团在演出中所表现的中苏友谊、和来自民间的雄壮、美丽、活泼的艺术。表演结束时，全体演员在观众热烈的鼓掌和献花中和观众道别，台上台下合唱了“世界民主青年进行曲”。
    艺术团已于二十四日离莫斯科赴布加勒斯特。
