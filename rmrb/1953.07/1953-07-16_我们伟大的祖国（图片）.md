Our Great Motherland
　　The Dutchman's pipe cactus bloomed in Zhongshan Park in the capital on the evening of July 15th. The Dutchman's pipe cactus flowers are pure white, grow from the edges of the leaves, and are fragrant and beautiful. They bloom once a year, and only at night. From the initial bloom to the withering, it only takes three or four hours, so the saying goes, "a flash in the pan."

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    首都中山公园七月十五日晚上昙花盛开。昙花花朵纯白色，从叶边生出，清香美丽，一年开一次，都在夜里开，从初放到谢落，只有三、四小时，所以俗语说是“昙花一现”。
