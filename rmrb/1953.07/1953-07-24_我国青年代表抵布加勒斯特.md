Title: Chinese Youth Representatives Arrive in Bucharest
Author:
Date: 1953-07-24
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, July 23rd: A portion of the Chinese youth delegation attending the Third World Youth Congress and the Fourth World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival, led by deputy heads Liu Daosheng and Qu Tangliang, arrived in Bucharest on the 22nd. They were welcomed by the Second Secretary of the Romanian Labor Youth League, Boergone, and many others.

### 我国青年代表抵布加勒斯特

1953-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

    我国青年代表抵布加勒斯特
    【新华社布加勒斯特二十三日电】出席第三届世界青年代表大会及第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的中国青年代表团一部分代表，在副团长刘导生、区棠亮率领下，于二十二日抵达布加勒斯特。欢迎他们的有罗马尼亚劳动青年联盟第二书记柏尔郭涅等多人。
