Title: Korean Armistice Talks: Korean-Chinese Delegation Issues Communiqué, Staff Officers from Both Sides Hold Administrative Meetings in Groups
Author:
Date: 1953-07-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, July 22nd—The Korean-Chinese delegation to the Korean armistice talks issued a communiqué on July 22nd: Staff officers from both sides held administrative meetings in groups on July 22nd.
