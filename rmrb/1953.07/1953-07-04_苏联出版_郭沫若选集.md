Title: Soviet Union Publishes "Selected Works of Guo Moruo"
Author:
Date: 1953-07-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 1st. The Soviet State Publishing House for Literary Books recently published a Russian version of "Selected Works of Guo Moruo." The selection includes poems, essays, and dramas written by the author in various periods.

The famous Soviet writer Nikolai Tikhonov published an article in "Pravda" on June 29th, introducing and commenting on the selection. Tikhonov pointed out that "Selected Works of Guo Moruo" is a valuable book. Soviet readers are reading the works of this outstanding modern Chinese writer with great interest.

### 苏联出版“郭沫若选集”

1953-07-04
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联出版“郭沫若选集”
    【新华社莫斯科一日电】苏联国家文学书籍出版局不久以前出版了俄文版“郭沫若选集”。选集包括了作者在各个时期所写的诗歌、散文和戏剧等。
    著名的苏联作家尼·吉洪诺夫六月二十九日在“真理报”上发表文章加以评介。吉洪诺夫指出：“郭沫若选集”是一本珍贵的书。苏联读者正在怀着很大的兴趣来阅读这位杰出的现代中国作家的作品。
