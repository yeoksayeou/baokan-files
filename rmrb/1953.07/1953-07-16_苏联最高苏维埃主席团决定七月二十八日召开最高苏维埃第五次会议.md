Title: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Decides to Convene the Fifth Session of the Supreme Soviet on July 28

Author: 
Date: 1953-07-16
Page: 1

    Decision of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union
    To convene the Fifth Session of the Supreme Soviet on July 28
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 15th] TASS Moscow, July 14th: Order of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union regarding the convocation of a session of the Supreme Soviet of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics.
    The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union hereby decides: To convene the Fifth Session of the Supreme Soviet of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics in Moscow on July 28, 1953. Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, K. Voroshilov. Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, N. Begov

### 苏联最高苏维埃主席团决定七月二十八日召开最高苏维埃第五次会议

1953-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃主席团决定
    七月二十八日召开最高苏维埃第五次会议
    【新华社十五日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十四日电：苏联最高苏维埃主席团关于召开苏联最高苏维埃会议的命令。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团兹决定：一九五三年七月二十八日在莫斯科召开苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟最高苏维埃第五次会议。苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席克·伏罗希洛夫苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书纳·别哥夫
