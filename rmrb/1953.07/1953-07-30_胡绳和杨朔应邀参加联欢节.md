Title: Hu Sheng and Yang Shuo Invited to Attend the Festival
Author:
Date: 1953-07-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 28th: Hu Sheng, a Chinese historian, and Yang Shuo, a writer, special guests invited by the International Preparatory Committee of the 4th World Youth and Students for Peace and Friendship Festival to attend the festival, left Beijing by plane for Bucharest on the 28th.

### 胡绳和杨朔应邀参加联欢节

1953-07-30
第6版()
专栏：

    胡绳和杨朔应邀参加联欢节
    【新华社二十八日讯】应第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节国际筹备委员会邀请参加联欢节的特邀来宾、我国历史学家胡绳和作家杨朔，于二十八日乘飞机离北京前往布加勒斯特。
