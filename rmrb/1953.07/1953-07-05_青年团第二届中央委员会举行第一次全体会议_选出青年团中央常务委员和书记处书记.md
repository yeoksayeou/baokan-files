Title: The Second Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Holds its First Plenary Session: Elects Standing Committee Members and Secretaries of the Secretariat
Author: 
Date: 1953-07-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 4th: The Second Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League held its first plenary session on the afternoon of July 4th. The main tasks of the meeting were: to elect the Standing Committee Members of the Second Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League and the Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Youth League Central Committee, and to discuss how to implement Chairman Mao's instructions on the work of the Youth League and the resolutions of the Second National Congress of the Youth League into the actual work of the Youth League in the future. One hundred and ten Central Committee members and thirty-three alternate Central Committee members attended today's meeting. The results of the election were as follows:

    I. Standing Committee Members of the Central Committee:
    Hu Yaobang, Liao Chengzhi, Jiang Nanxiang, Li Chang, Rong Gaotang, Liu Daosheng, Luo Yi, Wang Zonghuai, Qu Tangliang (female), Zhang Ze, Hu Keshi, Xiang Nan, Li Chao, Wang Wei, Han Xiacun, Yang Haibo, Wang Yuhui, Liu Xiyuan, Chen Xuzong, Zhu Yujin, Jia Shouyi.

    II. Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee:
    Hu Yaobang, Liao Chengzhi, Liu Daosheng, Luo Yi, Wang Zonghuai, Rong Gaotang, Qu Tangliang (female), Zhang Ze, Hu Keshi.

The meeting will continue its discussions on July 5th and 6th, and Hu Yaobang will make a concluding summary.

### 青年团第二届中央委员会举行第一次全体会议  选出青年团中央常务委员和书记处书记

1953-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

    青年团第二届中央委员会举行第一次全体会议
    选出青年团中央常务委员和书记处书记
    【新华社四日讯】七月四日下午，中国新民主主义青年团第二届中央委员会举行第一次全体会议，会议的主要任务是：选举中国新民主主义青年团第二届中央委员会常务委员和青年团中央书记处书记，并讨论如何将毛主席对青年团工作的指示与青年团第二次全国代表大会的决议贯彻到今后青年团的实际工作中去。出席今日会议的有中央委员一百一十人，候补中央委员三十三人。会议选举结果为：
    一、中央委员会常务委员：
    胡耀邦、廖承志、蒋南翔、李昌、荣高棠、刘导生、罗毅、王宗槐、区棠亮（女）、章泽、胡克实、项南、李超、王伟、韩夏存、杨海波、王宇辉、刘西元、陈绪宗、朱语今、贾守义。
    二、中央书记处书记：
    胡耀邦、廖承志、刘导生、罗毅、王宗槐、荣高棠、区棠亮（女）、章泽、胡克实。七月五日、六日，会议将继续进行讨论，最后将由胡耀邦作总结。
