Title: Indian Art Delegation Arrives in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1953-07-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 20th: The Indian Art Delegation, consisting of twenty-nine members led by the head of the delegation, Sachin Sengupta, and organized by the All-India Peace Council at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, arrived in Beijing by train from Guangzhou on the evening of the 20th.

Those who welcomed them at the station included Liu Ningyi and Liu Guanyi, Secretary-Generals of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Wu Maosun, Deputy Secretary-General, and Luo Longji, Yu Zhenying, Tan Tiwu, Mo Yichang, Tang Mingzhao, Zhu Shilun, and other heads of departments, Chen Hansheng, Vice President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, and literary and art figures such as Zheng Zhenduo, Hong Shen, Cao Yu, Zhao Feng, Cai Chusheng, Ma Ke, Jin Ziguang, and many young literary and art workers from the capital.

Also welcoming them at the station were Kamegawa Togo, Hui Dun, and Wan Tuli, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee.

When the delegation arrived at the station, young literary and art workers from the capital presented flowers to all members of the delegation to pay their respects.

Many of the delegation members are famous Indian artists. They will perform various musical and dance programs rich in Indian national color in Beijing and major cities in our country. Their visit to our country will undoubtedly further enhance the friendship and cultural exchange between the Chinese and Indian people and their cooperation in striving for peace in the Far East and the world.

### 印度艺术代表团昨天到达北京

1953-07-21
第1版()
专栏：

    印度艺术代表团昨天到达北京
    【新华社二十日讯】应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的邀请，由全印和平理事会组织派遣来我国访问的印度艺术代表团一行二十九人，在团长萨钦·森古普塔率领下，二十日晚从广州乘火车抵达北京。
    到车站欢迎的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘宁一、刘贯一，副秘书长吴茂荪和各部负责人罗隆基、于振瀛、谭惕吾、莫艺昌、唐明照、朱世纶等，中印友好协会副会长陈翰笙，以及文学艺术界人士郑振铎、洪深、曹禺、赵沨、蔡楚生、马可、金紫光等和首都的青年文艺工作者多人。
    到车站欢迎的还有亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长龟田东伍、惠吞、万徒勒里。
    代表团到达车站时，首都的青年文艺工作者曾向全团人员献花致敬。
    代表团团员中，有很多是印度著名的艺术家。他们将在北京和我国各大城市表演富于印度民族色彩的各种音乐、舞蹈节目。他们在我国的访问活动，无疑将进一步增进中印两国人民的友谊与文化交流，和两国人民在争取远东和世界和平中的合作。
