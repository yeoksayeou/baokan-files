Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Today Recording of Commander Peng Dehuai's Speech
Author:
Date: 1953-07-29
Page: 2

    Central People's Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Today
    Recording of Commander Peng Dehuai's Speech
    The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast at 7:00 this morning a recording transmitted from the Korean front of General Peng Dehuai, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, giving a speech after the signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement, and will rebroadcast it at 11:30 and 21:00.

### 中央人民广播电台今日播送  彭德怀司令员的谈话录音

1953-07-29
第2版()
专栏：

    中央人民广播电台今日播送
    彭德怀司令员的谈话录音
    中央人民广播电台在今天早晨七时播送从朝鲜前线传送来的中国人民志愿军司令员彭德怀将军在朝鲜停战协定签字后发表的谈话录音，并将在十一时三十分和二十一时重播。
