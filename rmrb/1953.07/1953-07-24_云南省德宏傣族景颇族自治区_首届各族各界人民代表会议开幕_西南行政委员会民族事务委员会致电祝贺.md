Title: The First Session of the People's Representative Conference of All Ethnic Groups in Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Region, Yunnan Province, Opens; Southwest Administrative Committee's Ethnic Affairs Commission Sends Congratulatory Telegram
Author:
Date: 1953-07-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, July 23rd: The first session of the People's Representative Conference of All Ethnic Groups in Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Region, Yunnan Province, solemnly opened on July 18th in Mangshi, Luxi County. At this conference, the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Region People's Government will be officially established. The 385 attendees included representatives from the Dai, Jingpo, and other ethnic groups in the autonomous region, as well as representatives from the People's Liberation Army stationed troops and government agencies. Guo Yingqiu, representative of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Vice Chairman of the Yunnan Provincial People's Government, Wang Jiaguang, Chief of the Business Section of the Ethnic Affairs Commission of the Southwest Administrative Committee, Wang Lianfang, Deputy Secretary of the Border Region Committee of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Zheng Gang, Secretary of the Baoshan Local Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, were all present to provide guidance.

At the meeting, Dao Jingban (Dai nationality), the executive chairman of the conference, spoke. He said: The opening of the first session of the People's Representative Conference of All Ethnic Groups in Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Region is a great joyous event for the people of all ethnic groups in the border region and the realization of Chairman Mao's ethnic policy. The people of all ethnic groups in the border region are truly becoming masters of their own affairs. Guo Yingqiu, representative of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Vice Chairman of the Yunnan Provincial People's Government, spoke at the meeting. He pointed out: In the three years since the liberation of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Region, great achievements have been made in suppressing bandits and special agents, establishing democratic political power, and carrying out production, cultural and educational hygiene, and training ethnic minority cadres. As a result, social order in the border region has been unprecedentedly stable, the people of all ethnic groups and all circles have become increasingly united, and at the same time, they have begun to improve their lives. Guo Yingqiu concluded: "The Chinese Communist Party, Chairman Mao, and the Central People's Government are very concerned about the people of all ethnic groups in the autonomous region. The convocation of the autonomous region's representative conference marks the beginning of a bright and happy new era for the people of all ethnic groups. The Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Yunnan Provincial People's Government will follow Chairman Mao's instructions and the requirements of the people of all ethnic groups and provide more assistance to the autonomous region."

The conference received enthusiastic congratulations from people of all ethnic groups across the country. The conference received congratulatory telegrams from the Ethnic Affairs Commission of the Southwest Administrative Committee and the Yunnan Provincial People's Government. The conference also received many embroideries and exquisite congratulatory banners. Dai and Jingpo women from Yingjiang County jointly knitted a portrait of Chairman Mao as a congratulatory banner to send to the conference.

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, July 23rd: The Ethnic Affairs Commission of the Southwest Administrative Committee sent a telegram congratulating the opening of the first session of the People's Representative Conference of All Ethnic Groups in the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Region, Yunnan Province. The telegram stated: Due to the implementation of Chairman Mao's great ethnic policy in your region, ethnic unity has been enhanced and national defense has been consolidated. On this basis, the Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Region People's Government will be established. This is an unprecedentedly great victory won by the people of all ethnic groups in your region under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party, and it marks that the people of all ethnic groups in your region are heading towards a new, bright and beautiful future. A warm congratulatory telegram is sent to you. We hope that the people of all ethnic groups in the autonomous region will continue to strengthen ethnic unity under the leadership of the autonomous region's People's Government in the future and strive to further consolidate national defense and develop the autonomous region's economic and cultural construction undertakings.

### 云南省德宏傣族景颇族自治区  首届各族各界人民代表会议开幕  西南行政委员会民族事务委员会致电祝贺

1953-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

    云南省德宏傣族景颇族自治区
    首届各族各界人民代表会议开幕
    西南行政委员会民族事务委员会致电祝贺
    【新华社昆明二十三日电】云南省德宏傣族景颇族自治区首届各族各界人民代表会议于七月十八日在潞西县芒市隆重开幕。在这次会议上将正式成立德宏傣族景颇族自治区人民政府。出席会议的包括自治区的傣族、景颇族及其他各民族的各界代表及中国人民解放军驻军、人民政府机关代表等三百八十五人。中共云南省委员会代表、云南省人民政府副主席郭影秋、西南行政委员会民族事务委员会业务处处长王家广、中共云南省委员会边疆委员会副书记王连芳和中共保山地方委员会书记郑刚等都亲临指导。
    会上，大会执行主席刀京版（傣族）讲话。他说：德宏傣族景颇族自治区首届各族各界人民代表会议的开幕，是边疆各族人民的大喜事，是毛主席民族政策的实现。边疆各族人民真正当家作主人了。中共云南省委员会代表、云南省人民政府副主席郭影秋在会上讲话。他指出：德宏傣族景颇族自治区解放三年多来，在清剿土匪特务、民主建政和进行生产、文教卫生、培养民族干部工作等都有很大成就。因此，边疆社会秩序空前安定，各族各界人民日益团结，同时开始改善了生活。郭影秋最后说：“中国共产党、毛主席和中央人民政府对自治区的各族人民非常关怀。自治区代表会议的召开，标志着各族人民走向一个光明幸福的新时代。中共云南省委员会和云南省人民政府将遵循毛主席的指示及各族人民的要求，给自治区更多的帮助。”
    大会受到全国各地各族人民的热烈祝贺。大会收到西南行政委员会民族事务委员会和云南省人民政府的贺电。大会还收到许多刺绣和精致的贺幛，盈江县傣族和景颇族妇女共同编织了一幅毛主席像，作为贺幛送给大会。
    【新华社昆明二十三日电】西南行政委员会民族事务委员会，致电祝贺云南省德宏傣族景颇族自治区首届各族各界人民代表会议开幕。电文称：由于毛主席伟大的民族政策在你区的贯彻执行，增强了民族团结，巩固了祖国国防，将在此基础上成立傣族景颇族自治区人民政府，这是你区各族人民在毛主席、中国共产党的英明领导下所获得的空前伟大的胜利，并标志着你区各族人民走向新的光明美好的前途。特电致以热烈的祝贺。我们希望自治区各族人民，今后在自治区人民政府领导下，继续加强民族团结，为进一步巩固祖国国防、发展自治区的经济、文化建设事业而奋斗。
