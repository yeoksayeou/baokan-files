Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-07-11
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The smelting workers of the small open-hearth furnace of Chongqing State-owned No. 101 Steel Plant, having learned the Soviet "Rapid Steelmaking Method," created a new record of smelting a furnace of steel in three hours and twenty-three minutes during the May production competition. The workers are pouring steel ingots here.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    重庆国营一○一钢铁厂小型平炉的冶炼工人，学习了苏联的“快速炼钢法”，在五月份生产竞赛中创造了三小时二十三分冶炼一炉钢的新纪录。工人们正在这里浇注钢锭。
