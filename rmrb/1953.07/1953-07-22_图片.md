Title: "People's Republic of China Industrial and Agricultural Exhibition" Opens
Author:
Date: 1953-07-22
Page: 4

    The "People's Republic of China Industrial and Agricultural Exhibition" has opened in the Central Cultural Resting Park named after Gorky. The picture shows Ge Baoquan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Soviet Union, delivering a speech at the opening ceremony of the exhibition.
    More than 4,000 exhibits are displayed at the "People's Republic of China Industrial and Agricultural Exhibition." The picture shows the China-Soviet Friendship Department, where visitors are viewing the silk tapestry "Great Friendship."
