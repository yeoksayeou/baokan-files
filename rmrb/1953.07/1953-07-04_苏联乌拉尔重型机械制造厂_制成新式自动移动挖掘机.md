Title: New Automatic Mobile Excavator Made by the Soviet Ural Heavy Machinery Plant
Author:
Date: 1953-07-04
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 2nd. The Ural Heavy Machinery Plant in Sverdlovsk produced a new type of automatic mobile excavator on June 30th. The excavator has an arm length of 65 meters and a bucket capacity of 20 cubic meters. Like previous excavators, it takes 60 to 75 seconds for each digging, turning, unloading, and returning cycle, but its productivity is 30 percent higher than past excavators. This giant excavator can dig 15,000 cubic meters of soil per day and transport the soil to a place 130 meters away. The soil dug up by its bucket in one scoop can fill seven five-ton trucks. To transport this machine to the construction site, one hundred train cars are needed. This new type of automatic mobile excavator is a new creation of the Soviet machine manufacturing industry. In manufacturing this excavator, the factory's designers improved the structure of previous excavators but used most of the machines from previous excavators, thus shortening its manufacturing process. During the manufacturing process, all the factory workers participated in the labor.

### 苏联乌拉尔重型机械制造厂  制成新式自动移动挖掘机

1953-07-04
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联乌拉尔重型机械制造厂
    制成新式自动移动挖掘机
    【新华社莫斯科二日电】斯维德洛夫斯克的乌拉尔重型机械制造厂，在六月三十日制成了一部新式的自动移动挖掘机。这部挖掘机臂长六十五公尺，吊斗的容积达二十立方公尺。和以前的挖掘机一样，它每次挖土、转动、卸土、回转也需要六十到七十五秒钟，但生产率比过去的挖掘机提高百分之三十。这部巨型挖掘机每昼夜能挖一万五千立方公尺的泥土，并把泥土送到一百三十公尺以外的地方去。它的吊斗一次挖出的土，可以装满七部五吨重的大卡车。要把这部机器送到工地，就需要一百个火车车皮。这部新式的自动移动挖掘机是苏联机器制造业的新创造。在制造这部挖掘机时，工厂的设计师们改进了以前挖掘机的构造，但使用了以前挖掘机的大部分机械，所以缩短了它的制造过程。在制造时，全厂工作人员都参加了劳动。
