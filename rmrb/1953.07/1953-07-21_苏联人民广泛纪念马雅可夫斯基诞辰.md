Title: Soviet People Widely Commemorate Mayakovsky's Birthday
Author:
Date: 1953-07-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 20th: The Soviet people widely commemorate the 60th anniversary of the birth of Vladimir Mayakovsky (July 19), an outstanding Soviet poet. In Moscow, the State Mayakovsky Memorial Museum organized three mobile exhibitions as early as the beginning of July, introducing the poet's childhood, student days, and his literary career. The Soviet Association for the Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge, major libraries, museums, workers' clubs, etc., continuously held commemorative meetings, exhibitions, report meetings, recitations, theatrical performances, and film screenings to commemorate the event. The grand commemorative meeting held on the 18th in the Column Hall of the Moscow Trade Union Building vividly demonstrated the unity and fraternal friendship of the literary circles of all Soviet nationalities. Writers from Moscow, Leningrad, as well as the Ukrainian, Belarusian, Georgian, Azerbaijani, Turkmen, Lithuanian and other republics spoke at the meeting and recited Mayakovsky's poems in their native languages. They unanimously believed that the poet's works reflected the struggle of people of all countries for a better future, and emphasized the great significance of his works for the development of the national cultures of the Soviet republics. Chinese poet Xiao San, German writer Bredel, Italian writer Germanetto, and Bulgarian writer Karolev also spoke at the meeting. After the meeting, a grand performance was held, in which artists from Moscow theaters recited Mayakovsky's poems and staged his plays. On the 19th, grand literary evenings were held in major theaters and parks in Moscow. More than 10,000 people attended the evening party at the Green Theater in Gorky Central Park of Culture and Rest.

Commemorative activities were also held in other parts of the Soviet Union. In Leningrad, clubs and cultural palaces held seminars and reports on the poet, and libraries organized book and picture exhibitions. On the 19th, the Central Kirov Park of Culture and Rest also held a grand literary evening, "Mayakovsky - the most talented and outstanding poet of our time". In Tashkent, the capital of Uzbekistan, a literary and artistic evening was also held on the 19th, and Uzbek poets and artists recited Mayakovsky's poems in Uzbek. In Georgia, libraries in various cities held exhibitions introducing the poet. The republic published a volume of selected works by Mayakovsky. Mayakovsky Village, the poet's birthplace, was particularly lively these days. Tens of thousands of people visited the place where the poet was born and spent his childhood. Representatives from Moscow literary circles, working people from Leningrad, Kiev, Tbilisi and nearby villages participated in the commemorative meeting here.

In addition, to commemorate the 60th anniversary of Mayakovsky's birth, the Soviet Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications began issuing a color commemorative stamp on July 19.

### 苏联人民广泛纪念马雅可夫斯基诞辰

1953-07-21
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联人民广泛纪念马雅可夫斯基诞辰
    【新华社二十日讯】苏联人民广泛纪念苏联杰出诗人弗拉吉米尔·马雅可夫斯基诞生六十周年（七月十九日）。在莫斯科，国立马雅可夫斯基纪念博物馆，早在七月初就组织了三个流动展览会，介绍诗人的童年、学生时代和他的文学事业。苏联政治与科学知识普及协会、各大图书馆、博物馆、工人俱乐部等，连续举行了纪念会、展览会、报告会、朗诵会、戏剧演出和电影放映等纪念活动。十八日，在莫斯科工会大厦圆柱大厅中举行的盛大纪念会，生动地表现了苏维埃各民族文学界的团结一致和兄弟般的友谊。参加这次大会的莫斯科、列宁格勒以及乌克兰、白俄罗斯、格鲁吉亚、阿塞拜疆、土库曼、立陶宛等加盟共和国的文学家，纷纷在会上发言，并用本民族的语言朗诵了马雅可夫斯基的诗篇。他们一致认为这位诗人的作品反映了各国人民争取美好未来的斗争，并着重指出了他的著作对于发展苏联各加盟共和国的民族文化的重大意义。在会上发言的还有中国诗人萧三、德国作家布莱德尔、意大利作家杰曼尼托和保加利亚作家卡罗列夫等。会后举行了盛大的表演会，由莫斯科各剧院的艺术家们朗诵马雅可夫斯基的诗篇和上演他的戏剧本。十九日，莫斯科各大剧院和公园里都举行了盛大的文学晚会。参加高尔基中央文化休憩公园绿荫剧院的晚会的有一万多人。
    苏联其他各地也进行了纪念活动。在列宁格勒，俱乐部和文化宫都举行了关于诗人的座谈会和报告会，各图书馆都组织了书籍图片展览会。十九日，中央基洛夫文化休憩公园还举行了盛大的“马雅可夫斯基——我们时代最有天才的优秀诗人”文学晚会。在乌兹别克首都塔什干，十九日也举行了文学艺术晚会，乌兹别克诗人和艺术家以乌兹别克的语言朗诵了马雅可夫斯基的诗篇。在格鲁吉亚，各城市的图书馆都举办了介绍这位诗人的展览会。共和国出版了一卷马雅可夫斯基选集。诗人的诞生地马雅可夫斯基村，这几天特别热闹。成千上万的人访问了诗人诞生和度过童年的地方。莫斯科文学界代表、来自列宁格勒、基辅、第比利斯和附近村落的劳动人民都参加了这里的纪念会。
    又讯，为了纪念马雅可夫斯基诞生六十周年，苏联邮电部在七月十九日开始发行了一种彩色纪念邮票。
