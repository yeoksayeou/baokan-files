Title: Korean Armistice Talks: Both Sides' Delegations Continue Administrative Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-07-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, July 12th: The Korean Armistice Negotiation Delegation of the Korean and Chinese side issued the following communique on July 12th: Both delegations held an administrative meeting on July 12th. The next meeting is scheduled to be held at 11:00 am on July 13th.

### 朝鲜停战谈判双方代表团续开行政性会议

1953-07-13
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判双方代表团续开行政性会议
    【新华社开城十二日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团七月十二日发表公报如下：双方代表团七月十二日举行行政性会议，下一次会议定于七月十三日上午十一时举行。
