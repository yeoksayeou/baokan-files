Title: German Communist Party Central Secretariat Calls on All Patriotic Forces in West Germany to Support the East German Government's Proposal for Holding All-German Conferences
Author:
Date: 1953-07-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 23rd (TASS): The German News Agency relayed a statement from the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the German Communist Party. The statement endorsed the proposal of the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic to hold all-German conferences of representatives from East and West Germany, and called on all patriotic forces in West Germany to support the initiative of the government of the German Democratic Republic. The statement emphasized: "Supporting the proposal of the German Democratic Republic government means taking a practical path towards striving for the unification of Germany."

### 德共中央书记处号召西德一切爱国力量  支持民主德国政府关于举行全德会议的建议

1953-07-25
第4版()
专栏：

    德共中央书记处号召西德一切爱国力量
    支持民主德国政府关于举行全德会议的建议
    【新华社二十四日讯】据塔斯社柏林二十三日讯：德意志通讯社转播了德国共产党中央委员会书记处的声明。声明赞同德意志民主共和国部长会议关于举行东西德代表的全德会议的建议，并号召西德一切爱国力量支持德意志民主共和国政府的倡议。声明中着重指出：“支持德意志民主共和国政府的建议，就意味着沿着实际的道路来争取德国的统一。”
