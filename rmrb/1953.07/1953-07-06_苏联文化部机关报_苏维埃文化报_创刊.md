Title: Organ of the Soviet Ministry of Culture, "Soviet Culture Newspaper," Launched

Author:
Date: 1953-07-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 5th: TASS reports: The first issue of the "Soviet Culture Newspaper," the organ of the Soviet Ministry of Culture, was published on July 4th.

    The editorial is entitled "Striving for the Further Prosperity of Soviet Culture." The editorial points out that the publication of this new newspaper is of great significance in promoting the cause of Soviet cultural construction. The editorial board hopes that cultural workers will use this newspaper to boldly raise pressing issues, exchange opinions and experiences, and extensively develop criticism and self-criticism in order to further enhance socialist culture.

### 苏联文化部机关报  “苏维埃文化报”创刊

1953-07-06
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联文化部机关报
    “苏维埃文化报”创刊
    【新华社五日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联文化部机关报“苏维埃文化报”第一号已在七月四日出版。
    社论的题目是“争取苏维埃文化的进一步繁荣”社论指出，出版这个新报纸对于促进苏联的文化建设事业是具有重大意义的。编辑部希望文化工作者利用这个报纸大胆地提出迫切的问题、交换意见和经验并广泛地展开批评和自我批评以便进一步提高社会主义文化。
