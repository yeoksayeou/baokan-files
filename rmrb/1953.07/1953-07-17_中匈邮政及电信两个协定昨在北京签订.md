Title: Sino-Hungarian Postal and Telecommunications Agreements Signed in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1953-07-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th. To facilitate communication and cultural exchange between the peoples of China and the Hungarian People's Republic, representatives of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government of China and the Ministry of Transportation and Posts and Telecommunications of the Hungarian People's Republic held talks on postal and telecommunications matters between the two countries.

The talks were conducted in an extremely friendly and harmonious atmosphere, and both parties officially signed the Sino-Hungarian Postal and Telecommunications Agreements in Beijing on July 16th.

Signing the agreements on the Chinese side was Zhu Xuefan, Minister of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government, and on the Hungarian side was Sándor Csatorday, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Hungarian People's Republic to China.

Participating in the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were Wang Zigang, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government; Zhao Zhigang, Director of the General Office; Su Younong, Director of the Postal Administration and concurrently Director of the International Relations Department; Wen Ning, Deputy Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department; and on the Hungarian side, Csatorday, Secretary of the Embassy of the Hungarian People's Republic to China, and others.

Both Minister Zhu Xuefan and Ambassador Sándor Csatorday delivered speeches after the signing, unanimously expressing their congratulations on the signing of these two agreements and pointing out that these two agreements are of great significance in the economic and cultural cooperation between China and Hungary.

After the signing ceremony, Minister Zhu Xuefan hosted a banquet in honor of Ambassador Sándor Csatorday and the embassy staff.

### 中匈邮政及电信两个协定昨在北京签订

1953-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

    中匈邮政及电信两个协定昨在北京签订
    【新华社十六日讯】  为便利我国与匈牙利人民共和国两国人民间的通信联络和文化交流，我中央人民政府邮电部与匈牙利人民共和国交通及邮电部代表，曾就两国间邮电通信事项，举行会谈。
    会谈在极其友好与融洽的气氛中进行，双方于七月十六日在北京正式签订了中匈邮政及电信两个协定。
    在协定上签字的，中国方面为中央人民政府邮电部部长朱学范，匈牙利方面为匈牙利人民共和国驻中国特命全权大使夏法朗柯。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有中央人民政府邮电部副部长王子纲、办公厅主任赵志刚，邮政总局局长兼国际关系处处长苏幼农，外交部苏联东欧司副司长温宁，交际处副处长沈平等；匈牙利方面有匈牙利人民共和国驻中国大使馆秘书察托尔道伊等。
    朱学范部长和夏法朗柯大使都在签字后致词，对这两个协定的签订，一致表示庆贺，并指出这两个协定在中匈两国经济及文化合作上具有重要意义。
    在签字仪式完毕后，朱学范部长欢宴了夏法朗柯大使和大使馆人员。
