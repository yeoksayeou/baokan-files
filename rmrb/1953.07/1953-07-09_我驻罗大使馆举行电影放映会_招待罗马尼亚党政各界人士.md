Title: Chinese Embassy in Romania Holds Film Screening to Entertain People from Romanian Party, Government, and All Circles

Author: 
Date: 1953-07-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 7th: Bucharest News: The Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Romanian People's Republic held a film screening on the 3rd, showing the Chinese documentary "Romanian Troops Song and Dance Troupe in China." Invited to watch the film were: members of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, senior government officials, leaders of the cultural circles, heads of mass organizations, and Stakhanovite workers. Also invited to watch were the famous Brazilian peace activist E. Branco, and members of foreign women's delegations who were invited to visit Romania after attending the World Women's Congress.

This film describes the performances of the Romanian Troops Song and Dance Troupe in our country last autumn. The film was very well received by the foreign guests during the screening.

### 我驻罗大使馆举行电影放映会  招待罗马尼亚党政各界人士

1953-07-09
第4版()
专栏：

    我驻罗大使馆举行电影放映会
    招待罗马尼亚党政各界人士
    【新华社布拉格七日电】布加勒斯特讯：中华人民共和国驻罗马尼亚人民共和国大使馆，在三日举行电影放映会，放映中国记录片“罗马尼亚部队歌舞团在中国”。应邀到会观看的有：罗马尼亚工人党中央委员，政府高级人员，文化界领袖，群众团体的负责人和斯大哈诺夫式工作者。应邀观看的还有巴西著名的和平人士埃·布兰柯，和参加世界妇女大会后应邀到罗马尼亚访问的外国妇女代表团的团员们。
    这部影片叙述罗马尼亚部队歌舞团去年秋季在我国演出时的情况。影片放映时，受到外宾很大的欢迎。
