Title: Congratulating the Signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement, Malenkov and Molotov Send a Telegram to Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou
Author:
Date: 1953-07-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 29th—Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs received a telegram from G. Malenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, and V. Molotov, Minister of Foreign Affairs. The full text of the telegram is as follows: To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, and Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China: The signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement and the end of the Korean War are a great victory for the heroic Korean people and the brave Chinese People's Volunteers. The people of all nationalities of the Soviet Union warmly congratulate the great Chinese people on this great victory in the cause of defending the Far East and world peace. After the signing of the armistice agreement and the end of the war, the task facing the peace-loving people of all countries is to consolidate these achievements in the struggle for peace and the freedom and independence of all nations.
    Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR G. Malenkov
    Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR V. Molotov
    Moscow, July 28, 1953

### 庆贺朝鲜停战协定的签订  马林科夫莫洛托夫致电毛主席周总理

1953-07-30
第1版()
专栏：

    庆贺朝鲜停战协定的签订
    马林科夫莫洛托夫致电毛主席周总理
    【新华社二十九日讯】我中央人民政府毛泽东主席和政务院周恩来总理兼外交部部长，收到苏联部长会议主席格·马林科夫和外交部部长维·莫洛托夫的来电。电文全文如下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东同志：中华人民共和国政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来同志：朝鲜停战协定的签字和朝鲜战争的结束，是英雄的朝鲜人民和英勇的中国人民志愿军的伟大胜利。苏联各族人民热烈地祝贺伟大的中国人民在保卫远东及世界和平的事业中所取得的这一伟大胜利。在停战协定签字和战争结束之后，摆在爱好和平的各国人民面前的任务是：在争取和平及各民族自由和独立的斗争中去巩固这些成就。
    苏联部长会议主席  格·马林科夫
    苏联外交部部长  维·莫洛托夫
    一九五三年七月二十八日于莫斯科
