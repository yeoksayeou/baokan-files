Title: Philippine People's Liberation Army Launches Over 600 Attacks in Six Months
Author:
Date: 1953-07-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Manila, 4th: The Philippine People's Liberation Army is actively launching attacks, continuously striking the Quirino government's puppet army. Based solely on the figures admitted by the Quirino puppet government on June 30th, the Philippine People's Liberation Army launched 626 attacks in the first half of 1953. Eighty-seven puppet soldiers were killed, and one hundred and twenty-five puppet soldiers were wounded.

### 菲律宾解放军半年出击六百多次

1953-07-07
第4版()
专栏：

    菲律宾解放军半年出击六百多次
    【新华社四日讯】马尼拉消息：菲律宾人民解放军活跃地出击，不断给季里诺政府傀儡军以打击。仅根据季里诺傀儡政府在六月三十日所承认的数字，一九五三年上半年菲律宾人民解放军出击六百二十六次。打死傀儡军八十七人，打伤傀儡军一百二十五人。
