    Ambassador Ochirbat's Resume
    Bayanbator Ochirbat was born in 1919. He graduated from the Ulaanbaatar City Marxism-Leninism Night University. From 1933 to 1938, he worked as a typesetter at the printing bureau. From 1938 to 1950, he worked in the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic. In October 1950, he was transferred to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, where he served as a member of the committee of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic and as the director of the Protocol Department.

### 奥其尔巴特大使简历

1953-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

    奥其尔巴特大使简历
    巴扬巴托·奥其尔巴特生于一九一九年。曾毕业于乌兰巴托市马克思—列宁主义夜大学。一九三三年到一九三八年在印刷局当排字工人。一九三八年到一九五○年在蒙古人民共和国内务部工作。一九五○年十月调外交部工作，任蒙古人民共和国外交部委员会委员及交际司司长等职。
