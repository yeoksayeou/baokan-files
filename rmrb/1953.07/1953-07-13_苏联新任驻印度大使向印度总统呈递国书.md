Title: New Soviet Ambassador to India Presents Credentials to Indian President
Author:
Date: 1953-07-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Delhi: TASS reports that the new Soviet Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to India, Benediktov, presented his credentials to the President of the Republic of India, Prasad, on the 4th. Ambassador Benediktov was accompanied by officials from the Soviet Embassy in India.

Those participating in the ceremony included R.K. Nehru, Secretary of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs, Chopra, Director of Protocol, and other senior officials of the Ministry of External Affairs.

At the presentation of credentials, the Soviet Ambassador and the Indian President delivered speeches.

After the ceremony, the President and the Ambassador held talks.

### 苏联新任驻印度大使向印度总统呈递国书

1953-07-13
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联新任驻印度大使向印度总统呈递国书
    【新华社六日讯】塔斯社德里讯：苏联驻印度特命全权大使别涅吉克托夫在四日向印度共和国总统普拉沙德呈递国书。陪同别涅吉克托夫大使的有苏联驻印度大使馆的官员。
    参加这次仪式的有印度外交部秘书鲁·克·尼赫鲁、交际司司长乔普拉和外交部其他高级官员。
    在呈递国书时，苏联大使和印度总统曾先后致词。
    仪式结束以后，总统和大使进行了谈话。
