Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-07-31
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Our country's first automobile manufacturing plant has started construction. This is a modern factory built with the assistance of the Soviet Union. On July 15th, workers poured the first truckload of mud into the foundation of the factory building, beginning this great project.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-07-31
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国第一个汽车制造厂已经开工兴建。这是在苏联的援助下建立的一个现代化工厂。工人们在七月十五日把第一车泥浆灌在厂房基础上，开始了这一巨大的工程。
