Title: National Congress of the Youth League Approves Draft Amendments to the League Constitution
Author:
Date: 1953-07-01
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th dispatch: The Second National Congress of the China New Democratic Youth League continued this morning, the 30th.

    Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government, delivered a special report to the congress on cultural and educational work. In his report, he explained the current basic situation and future guiding principles and tasks of China's cultural and educational work, and put forward several opinions on the tasks of the China New Democratic Youth League in participating in the country's cultural and educational construction.

    Then, the congress voted on the "Report on the Revision of the League Constitution" made to the congress by Li Chang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the First Central Committee of the Youth League, and the "Draft Amendments to the Constitution of the China New Democratic Youth League" as revised by the congress. As a result, the congress unanimously approved Li Chang's report and approved the draft amendments to the League Constitution. The congress decided that, from today onwards, the revised League Constitution shall be the official constitution of the China New Democratic Youth League.

    On the 28th, representatives from various regions held group discussions on the "Report on the Revision of the League Constitution" and the "Draft Amendments to the Constitution of the China New Democratic Youth League"; on the 29th, the representatives visited the Summer Palace, a famous scenic spot in the capital.

    The meeting continued to be adjourned in the afternoon of the 30th.

### 青年团全国代表大会批准团章修改草案

1953-07-01
第1版()
专栏：

    青年团全国代表大会批准团章修改草案
    【新华社三十日讯】中国新民主主义青年团第二次全国代表大会三十日上午继续举行。
    中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会秘书长钱俊瑞向大会作了关于文化教育工作的专题报告。他在报告中说明了我国文化教育工作目前的基本情况和今后的方针任务，并就中国新民主主义青年团在参加国家文化教育建设工作方面的任务，提出了几点意见。
    接着，大会对青年团第一届中央委员会书记处书记李昌向大会所作的“关于修改团章的报告”，和经大会修正的“中国新民主主义青年团团章修改草案”进行表决，结果，大会一致同意李昌所作的报告，并批准了团章修改草案。大会决议，从今天起，修改后的团章，即为中国新民主主义青年团的正式团章。
    二十八日，各地代表曾分组讨论了“关于修改团章的报告”和“中国新民主主义青年团团章修改草案”；二十九日，代表们游览了首都名胜——颐和园。
    三十日下午，会议继续休会。
