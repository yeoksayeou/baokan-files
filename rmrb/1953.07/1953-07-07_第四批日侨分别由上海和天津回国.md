Title: The Fourth Group of Japanese Nationals Repatriated from Shanghai and Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1953-07-07
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 6th: The fourth group of Japanese nationals who wished to return to their country, totaling two thousand nine hundred and fifteen people and concentrated in Shanghai and Tianjin, have recently left China to return to Japan.
    The fourth group of Japanese nationals concentrated in Shanghai, numbering one thousand and six people, returned to their country on the Japanese ships "Hakuryu Maru" and "Hakusan Maru" on the 3rd. The fourth group of Japanese nationals concentrated in Tianjin, numbering one thousand nine hundred and nine people, left Tanggu on the Japanese ship "Koa Maru" on the 4th to return to their country. Before their departure, many staff members from the Shanghai and Tianjin branches of the Chinese Red Cross Society went to the docks to see them off.

### 第四批日侨分别由上海和天津回国

1953-07-07
第4版()
专栏：

    第四批日侨分别由上海和天津回国
    【新华社六日讯】分别集中在上海和天津的第四批愿意回国的日本侨民二千九百十五人，日前已离开中国返回日本。
    集中在上海的第四批回国日侨一千零六人，是在三日乘日本轮船“白龙丸”和“白山丸”回国的。集中在天津的第四批回国日侨一千九百零九人，四日乘日本轮船“兴安丸”离开塘沽返国。临行时，中国红十字会上海市分会和天津市分会工作人员多人分别到码头欢送。
