Title: French Invasion Commander Navarre Admits the Strength of the Vietnamese People's Army
Author:
Date: 1953-07-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, July 19th: "Liberation" published a statement by Navarre, Commander-in-Chief of the French forces in Indochina, to the press. Navarre said: "I must say that our army has been retreating for two years. The communist strongholds created by the Viet Minh's offensive are constantly expanding and expanding." He said: The Viet Minh's combat troops have never been so strong before, and they can launch offensives at will after the rainy season ends.
    "Liberation" also reported that on the night of June 16, soldiers from two Khmer indigenous infantry companies under the command of the French military headquarters defected to the Khmer Liberation Army with all their weapons.

### 侵越法军头子纳瓦尔承认越南人民军力量强大

1953-07-20
第4版()
专栏：

    侵越法军头子纳瓦尔承认越南人民军力量强大
    【新华社十九日讯】据塔斯社巴黎十七日讯：“解放报”登载了印度支那法军总司令纳瓦尔对报界发表的谈话。纳瓦尔说：“我必须说，两年来我们的军队一直在后撤。越盟军进攻所造成的共产党据点不断地扩大再扩大。”他说：越盟的作战部队以前从来没有这样强大，等雨季结束，他们能随心所欲地开始进攻。
    “解放报”又报道说：六月十六日夜间，在法军司令部统辖下的两个高棉土著步兵连士兵携带了他们所有的武装，投诚高棉解放军。
