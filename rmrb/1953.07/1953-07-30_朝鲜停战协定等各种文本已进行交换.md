Title: Exchange of Various Texts Including the Korean Armistice Agreement Completed
Author:
Date: 1953-07-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, July 29th: Following the formal signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement and its temporary supplementary agreement by the chief representatives of both sides' armistice negotiation delegations at 10:00 AM on July 27th, all versions of the texts have been signed by the commanders of both sides and exchanged at Panmunjom on the afternoon of July 29th.

According to the agreement between the two sides, each side will retain two sets of the armistice agreement texts prepared separately in Korean, Chinese, and English. In addition, two sets of texts prepared separately by both sides will be kept by the Military Armistice Commission.

### 朝鲜停战协定等各种文本已进行交换

1953-07-30
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战协定等各种文本已进行交换
    【新华社开城二十九日电】朝鲜停战协定及其临时补充协议的各种文本，继双方停战谈判代表团双方首席代表于七月二十七日上午十时正式签字之后，已全部由双方司令官予以签署，并在七月二十九日下午在板门店进行了交换。
    根据双方的协议，每方将保留双方分别准备的朝、中、英文三种停战协定文本两套，另有双方分
    别准备的文本两套，将交军事停战委员会保存。
