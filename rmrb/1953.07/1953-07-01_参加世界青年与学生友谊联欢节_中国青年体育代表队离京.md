Title: Chinese Youth Sports Delegation Leaves Beijing to Participate in the World Youth and Student Friendship Festival
Author:
Date: 1953-07-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. The Chinese Youth Delegation's sports team, participating in the Fourth World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival to be held in Bucharest, the capital of Romania, in August this year, left Beijing by train on the 30th for Moscow, en route to Romania.

The Chinese Youth Delegation's sports team includes male and female basketball, volleyball, track and field athletes, and swimmers, totaling eighty people. Among them are workers, farmers, soldiers, and students, all outstanding athletes selected from the 1953 National Basketball and Volleyball Tournament and the National Track and Field and Swimming Athlete Selection Trials.

### 参加世界青年与学生友谊联欢节  中国青年体育代表队离京

1953-07-01
第1版()
专栏：

    参加世界青年与学生友谊联欢节
    中国青年体育代表队离京
    【新华社三十日讯】参加今年八月在罗马尼亚首都布加勒斯特举行的第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的中国青年代表团体育代表队，在三十日乘火车离北京赴莫斯科转赴罗马尼亚。
    中国青年代表团体育代表队包括男、女篮球、排球、田径等项运动员和游泳选手共八十人，其中有工人、农民、战士和学生，他们都是在一九五三年全国篮、排球运动大会和全国田径、游泳运动员选拔测验中选拔出来的优秀运动员。
