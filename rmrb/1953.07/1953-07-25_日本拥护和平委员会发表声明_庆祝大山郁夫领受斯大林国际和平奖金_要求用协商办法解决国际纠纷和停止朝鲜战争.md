Title: Japan Peace Liaison Committee Issues Statement Celebrating Ikuo Oyama's Receipt of Stalin International Peace Prize, Demanding Resolution of International Disputes Through Negotiation and Cessation of the Korean War
Author:
Date: 1953-07-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 24th: The Japan Peace Liaison Committee issued a statement on July 10th, celebrating the Japanese peace fighter Ikuo Oyama's receipt of the "Strengthening International Peace" Stalin International Prize, and demanding the resolution of international disputes through negotiation and the immediate cessation of the Korean War. The statement first pointed out: The fact that Ikuo Oyama, Chairman of the Japan Peace Liaison Committee, won the "Strengthening International Peace" Stalin International Prize is not only an honor for Ikuo Oyama, who has fought for the defense of peace and against war for thirty years, but also a joyous event for all the Japanese people who are fighting for peace and independence. This will give infinite strength to the developing Japanese peace movement and the anti-military base struggle prevalent throughout the country. The statement proposed: We must unite with peace-loving people all over the world to resolve all international disputes through consultation. And demand the convening of a Five-Power Peace Conference of the Soviet Union, China, the United States, Britain, and France. We strongly demand an immediate end to the Korean War, and oppose Asians fighting Asians. The statement concludes: We once again guarantee that, for the sake of the stable life of the Japanese people, for the democracy, independence and peace of Japan, we will unite people from all walks of life and resolutely fight for the defense of peace and against war.

### 日本拥护和平委员会发表声明  庆祝大山郁夫领受斯大林国际和平奖金  要求用协商办法解决国际纠纷和停止朝鲜战争

1953-07-25
第4版()
专栏：

    日本拥护和平委员会发表声明
    庆祝大山郁夫领受斯大林国际和平奖金
    要求用协商办法解决国际纠纷和停止朝鲜战争
    【新华社二十四日讯】东京消息：日本拥护和平委员会在七月十日发表声明，庆祝日本和平战士大山郁夫领受“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金，并要求用协商办法解决国际纠纷和立即停止朝鲜战争。声明首先指出：日本拥护和平委员会主席大山郁夫荣获“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金一事，不仅是为保卫和平、反对战争而斗争了三十年的大山郁夫一个人的光荣，同时也是为争取和平、独立而战斗着的全日本人民的一件喜事。这会给予正在发展着的日本和平运动和普遍于全国的反对军事基地斗争以无限的力量。声明中提出：我们要和全世界爱好和平的人士团结一致，用协商的办法解决一切国际纠纷。并要求召开苏、中、美、英、法五大国和平会议。我们强烈地要求立即结束朝鲜战争，同时反对用亚洲人打亚洲人。声明最后写道：我们再一次保证，为了日本人民的生活安定，为了日本的民主、独立与和平，我们要团结各阶层人民，为拥护和平，反对战争而坚决地斗争下去。
