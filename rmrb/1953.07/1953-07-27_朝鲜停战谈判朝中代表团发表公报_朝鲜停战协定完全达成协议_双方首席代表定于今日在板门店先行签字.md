Title: Korean Armistice Talks: Sino-North Korean Delegation Issues Communiqué - Korean Armistice Agreement Fully Reached - Both Chief Delegates Scheduled to Sign First in Panmunjom Today
Author:
Date: 1953-07-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, July 26th: The Sino-North Korean delegation to the Korean Armistice Talks issued the following communiqué on July 26th: The Korean Armistice Agreement has been fully agreed upon by both negotiating parties. Both parties are scheduled to have our delegation's chief delegate, General Nam Il, and the opposing delegation's chief delegate, Lieutenant General Harrison, sign it first in Panmunjom, Korea, at 10:00 AM Korean time on July 27th. It will then be sent to Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army, Marshal Kim Il-sung, and Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, General Peng Dehuai, and Commander-in-Chief of the United Nations Command, General Clark, for their respective signatures. Both parties have agreed that the time of the signing by the chief delegates of both parties shall be the official signing time of the Armistice Agreement. All provisions regarding the ceasefire and all other provisions in the Armistice Agreement, its annexes, and supplementary agreements shall take effect twelve hours after the official signing time, i.e., at 10:00 PM Korean time on July 27th.

### 朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团发表公报  朝鲜停战协定完全达成协议  双方首席代表定于今日在板门店先行签字

1953-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团发表公报
    朝鲜停战协定完全达成协议
    双方首席代表定于今日在板门店先行签字
    【新华社开城二十六日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团七月二十六日发表公报如下：朝鲜停战协定已由谈判双方完全达成协议，双方定于七月二十七日朝鲜时间上午十时，在朝鲜板门店由我方代表团首席代表南日大将与对方代表团首席代表哈利逊中将先行签字，然后送朝鲜人民军最高司令官金日成元帅及中国人民志愿军司令员彭德怀将军与联合国军总司令克拉克上将分别签字。双方商定以双方首席代表签字的时间作为停战协定的正式签字时间，双方关于停火的规定及停战协定及其附件及补充协议中的一切其他规定，均一律于正式签字时间的十二小时后起亦即七月二十七日朝鲜时间下午十时起生效。
