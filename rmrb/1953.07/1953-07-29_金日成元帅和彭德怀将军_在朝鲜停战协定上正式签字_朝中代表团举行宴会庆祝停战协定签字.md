Title: Marshal Kim Il-sung and General Peng Dehuai Officially Sign the Korean Armistice Agreement; Korean-Chinese Delegations Hold Banquet to Celebrate the Signing of the Armistice Agreement
Author:
Date: 1953-07-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 28th: Marshal Kim Il-sung, Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army, formally signed the Korean Armistice Agreement and the Temporary Supplementary Agreement brought from Kaesong by General Nam Il, Chief Delegate of the Korean-Chinese Delegation to the Korean Armistice Negotiations, at 10:00 PM on July 27th in Pyongyang. Among those present at the signing ceremony were Korean party and government leaders and senior generals of the Korean People's Army.

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, July 28th: General Peng Dehuai, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, formally signed the Korean Armistice Agreement and the Temporary Supplementary Agreement at 9:30 AM on July 28th in Kaesong. Over a hundred senior officers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army were present at the signing. On the evening of July 27th, the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army frontline troops stationed in Kaesong held a grand welcoming ceremony to welcome General Peng Dehuai, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and Vice Marshal Choi Yong-kun, Deputy Commander of the Supreme Headquarters of the Korean People's Army, to Kaesong. General Peng Dehuai and Vice Marshal Choi Yong-kun addressed the welcomers at the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, July 28th: On July 27th, the Korean-Chinese Delegation to the Korean Armistice Negotiations held a banquet to celebrate the signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement. Those attending the banquet included: Vice Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and Minister of National Defence, Vice Marshal Choi Yong-kun, Deputy Commander of the Supreme Headquarters of the Korean People's Army, General Peng Dehuai, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, General Du Ping, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, representatives and heads of various departments of the Korean-Chinese Delegation to the Korean Armistice Negotiations, senior generals of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army on the Kaesong front, and more than ninety foreign journalists. At the banquet, everyone frequently toasted to the great victory of the Korean Armistice Negotiations, to the great victory of the close unity and joint combat of the Korean and Chinese people and the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, to the great victory of the worldwide people's struggle for peace and against war, and to the health of Marshal Kim Il-sung, Chairman Mao Zedong, and Chairman Malenkov.

### 金日成元帅和彭德怀将军  在朝鲜停战协定上正式签字  朝中代表团举行宴会庆祝停战协定签字

1953-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

    金日成元帅和彭德怀将军
    在朝鲜停战协定上正式签字
    朝中代表团举行宴会庆祝停战协定签字
    【新华社平壤二十八日电】朝鲜人民军最高司令官金日成元帅七月二十七日下午十时于平壤在朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团首席代表南日大将自开城带来的朝鲜停战协定和临时补充协议上正式签字。举行签字仪式时，在场的尚有朝鲜党政首长及朝鲜人民军高级将领等多人。
    【新华社开城二十八日电】中国人民志愿军司令员彭德怀将军，于二十八日上午九时三十分在开城在朝鲜停战协定和临时补充协议上正式签字。签字时在场的尚有中国人民志愿军高级军官百余人。二十七日晚间，中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军驻开城前线部队举行盛大欢迎会，欢迎中国人民志愿军司令员彭德怀将军和朝鲜人民军最高司令部副司令官崔庸健次帅到达开城。彭德怀将军和崔庸健次帅曾在会上向欢迎者讲话。
    【新华社开城二十八日电】七月二十七日，朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团举行宴会，庆祝朝鲜停战协定签字。参加宴会的有：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相兼民族保卫相、朝鲜人民军最高司令部副司令官崔庸健次帅，中国人民志愿军司令员彭德怀将军，中国人民志愿军政治部副主任杜平将军，朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团各代表和各部门首长，开城前线朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军的高级将领，和各国新闻记者等共九十多人。宴会上大家频频为朝鲜停战谈判的伟大胜利，为朝中人民和朝鲜人民军、中国人民志愿军的亲密团结和并肩作战的伟大胜利，为全世界人民争取和平、反对战争的伟大胜利，为金日成元帅、毛泽东主席、马林科夫主席的健康而干杯。
