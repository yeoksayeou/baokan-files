Title: North Korea Publishes Collection of Essays by Leaders of Communist and Workers' Parties of Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1953-07-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 8th. "Labor News" reports: The Korean Workers' Party Publishing House recently published the first collection of "Important Essays by Leaders of Fraternal Communist and Workers' Parties." The collection includes important essays published by leaders of fraternal parties in China, the German Democratic Republic, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, Albania, as well as France and Italy, in the newspaper "For Lasting Peace, For People's Democracy!"

"Labor News" pointed out: These essays provide concrete instructions on the line of struggle, strategy, and tactics for the Communist and Workers' parties of various countries standing at the forefront of the struggle for peace and democracy. The struggle experience of fraternal Communist and Workers' parties will greatly contribute to our victory.

Prior to this, the Korean Workers' Party Publishing House had already published the documents of the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) and Stalin's "Economic Problems of Socialism in the USSR" in December last year, and published special collections such as "In Memory of the Great Marshal Stalin" and "Lenin and Stalin on Socialist Economic Construction" in the first half of this year.

### 朝鲜出版各国共产党及工人党领袖论文集

1953-07-09
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜出版各国共产党及工人党领袖论文集
    【新华社平壤八日电】“劳动新闻”报道：朝鲜劳动党出版社最近出版了“兄弟共产党及工人党领袖重要论文集”第一集。论文集收集了中国、德意志民主共和国、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、罗马尼亚、阿尔巴尼亚以及法国、意大利等国各兄弟党领袖在“争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”报上发表过的重要论文。
    “劳动新闻”指出：这些论文给予站在争取和平与民主的斗争前列的各国共产党、工人党以斗争路线和战略、战术的具体指示。兄弟共产党及工人党的斗争经验，对于我们的胜利将有很大的贡献。
    在这以前，朝鲜劳动党出版社已在去年十二月出版苏联共产党（布）第十九次代表大会文件和斯大林著的“苏联社会主义经济问题”，在今年上半年又出版了“悼念伟大的斯大林大元帅”和“列宁、斯大林论社会主义经济建设”等专集。
