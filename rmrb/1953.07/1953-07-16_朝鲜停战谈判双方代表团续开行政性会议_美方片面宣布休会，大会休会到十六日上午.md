Title: Korean Armistice Negotiations: Both Sides' Delegations Resume Administrative Meeting; US Side Unilaterally Announces Recess, Plenary Session Recesses Until the Morning of the 16th
Author: 
Date: 1953-07-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 15th—The Korean Armistice Negotiation Korean-Chinese delegation issued the following communique on July 15th:
	The two sides' delegations held an administrative meeting on July 15th. The other side unilaterally announced a recess and walked out of the meeting. The plenary session recessed until 11:00 AM on July 16th.

### 朝鲜停战谈判双方代表团续开行政性会议  美方片面宣布休会，大会休会到十六日上午

1953-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判双方代表团续开行政性会议
    美方片面宣布休会，大会休会到十六日上午
    【新华社开城十五日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团七月十五日发表公报如下：
    双方代表团七月十五日举行行政性会议，对方片面宣布休会走出会场。大会休会到七月十六日上午十一时。
