    Our Great Motherland
    This year, our country's Scientific Education Film Studio will produce fifteen scientific education films to gradually popularize general scientific knowledge among the people. This is a scene of the factory's photographer taking pictures of a total lunar eclipse last night.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国科学教育电影制片厂今年将摄制十五部科学教育影片，使一般的科学知识逐渐普及到人民群众中去。这是该厂摄影师昨日夜晚拍摄月全食的一个场面。
