Title: Korean Armistice Talks: Administrative Meeting Held Between Both Delegations

Author:

Date: 1953-07-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, July 10th: The Korean Armistice Negotiation's Korean-Chinese delegation released the following communique on July 10th: Both delegations held an administrative meeting on July 10th. The next meeting is scheduled for 11:00 AM on July 11th.

### 朝鲜停战谈判双方代表团举行行政性会议

1953-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判双方代表团举行行政性会议
    【新华社开城十日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团七月十日发表公报如下：双方代表团七月十日举行行政性会议，下一次会议定于七月十一日上午十一时举行。
