Title: Indian Art Delegation Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1953-07-18
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, July 17th: The Indian Art Delegation, consisting of 29 members led by delegation head Sachin Sengupta, arrived in Guangzhou by special train at 5:07 PM on the 16th, having been invited to visit our country by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace. More than 30 people were at the station to welcome them, including Zhang Canqun, Vice Chairman of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Zhou Guojin, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangzhou Federation of Literary and Art Circles.

### 印度艺术代表团到广州

1953-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    印度艺术代表团到广州
    【新华社广州十七日电】应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的邀请前来我国访问的印度艺术代表团一行二十九人，由团长萨钦·森古普塔率领，在十六日下午五时零七分乘专车到达广州。到车站欢迎的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广州市分会副主席张?村和广州市文学艺术界联合会筹备会副主任周国瑾等三十多人。
