Title: Japanese Yoshida Government Persecutes Korean Residents

Author: 
Date: 1953-07-08
Page: 4

    Japanese Yoshida Government Persecutes Korean Residents
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 7th] TASS Pyongyang, June 6th: The Korean Central News Agency published a message pointing out that the Japanese Yoshida government is stepping up the persecution of Korean residents in Japan.
    The Korean Central News Agency reported news from Tokyo saying: Eighty policemen from the Tokyo Metropolitan Police Department broke into the residences of Korean residents in Koto Ward, arrested nine Koreans without any reason, and took them to the police station. On the same day, detectives from the police station arrested three more Koreans in Ota Ward. On July 2nd, police in Ibaraki Prefecture searched the residences of Korean residents in Konishi Village (transliteration), arrested ten Koreans, and confiscated their property.
    The Korean Central News Agency pointed out: These illegal actions by the Japanese authorities have aroused great indignation among Korean residents in Japan and the Japanese people.

### 日本吉田政府迫害朝鲜侨民

1953-07-08
第4版()
专栏：

    日本吉田政府迫害朝鲜侨民
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社平壤六日讯：朝鲜中央通讯社发表了一项消息，指出日本吉田政府正在加紧迫害在日本的朝鲜侨民。
    朝鲜中央通讯社报道来自东京的消息说：八十名东京警视厅的警察闯入江东区朝鲜侨民的住宅，毫无理由地捕去九个朝鲜人，并把他们带到了警察局。同一天，警察局的侦探又在大田区捕走了三个朝鲜人。在七月二日，茨城县的警察搜查了小西村（译音）的朝鲜侨民住宅，逮捕了十个朝鲜人，并没收了他们的财产。
    朝鲜中央通讯社指出：日本当局的这种非法行为，激起了在日本的朝鲜侨民和日本人民的极大愤慨。
