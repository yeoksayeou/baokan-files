Title: Enlarged Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party Held; Rákosi Elected as Secretary of the Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1953-07-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, June 30th: TASS reports that the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party held an enlarged plenary session in Budapest on June 27th and 28th. The meeting discussed the reports on the political and economic situation made by Mátyás Rákosi and Imre Nagy, and adopted relevant resolutions.

The Central Committee elected the Politburo. Elected as members of the Politburo were: Mátyás Rákosi, Imre Nagy, Ernő Gerő, András Hegedüs, István Hidas, István Kristóf, Rudolf Földvári, Lajos Ács, Mihály Farkas. István Bata and Béla Szalai were elected as alternate members of the Politburo.

Alternate Central Committee members Lajos Ács and István Bata were elected as official Central Committee members, and Béla Szalai and Béla Veg were elected into the Central Committee.

The Central Committee abolished the original Organization Bureau and Secretariat, and abolished the post of General Secretary.

Mátyás Rákosi, Lajos Ács, and Béla Vég were elected as Secretaries of the new Secretariat and Secretaries of the Central Committee.

### 匈牙利劳动人民党中央举行扩大全会  拉科西当选为中央委员会书记

1953-07-02
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利劳动人民党中央举行扩大全会
    拉科西当选为中央委员会书记
    【新华社一日讯】塔斯社布达佩斯六月三十日讯：匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会于六月二十七日和二十八日在布达佩斯举行扩大的全体会议。会议讨论了马加斯·拉科西和伊姆雷·纳吉所作的关于政治和经济形势的报告，并通过了有关的决议。
    中央委员会选出了政治局。当选为政治局委员的是：马·拉科西，伊·纳吉，埃·格罗，安·赫格居斯，伊·希达希，伊·克里斯托夫，鲁·福尔德瓦里，拉·阿契，米·若费尼奇。伊·巴塔和伯·萨拉伊当选为政治局候补委员。
    中央委员会候补委员拉·阿契和伊·巴塔被选为正式中央委员，伯·萨拉伊和伯·维格被选入中央委员会。
    中央委员会取消了原有的组织局和书记处，并取消总书记一职。
    马·拉科西、拉·阿契和伯·维格被选为新的书记处书记和中央委员会书记。
