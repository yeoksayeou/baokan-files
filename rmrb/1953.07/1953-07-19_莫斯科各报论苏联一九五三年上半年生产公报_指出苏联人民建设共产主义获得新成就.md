Title: Moscow Newspapers on the Soviet Union's Production Communiqué for the First Half of 1953: Pointing Out New Achievements of the Soviet People in Building Communism
Author:
Date: 1953-07-19
Page: 4

    Moscow Newspapers on the Soviet Union's Production Communiqué for the First Half of 1953
    Pointing Out New Achievements of the Soviet People in Building Communism
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 18th] Moscow newspapers published on the 17th the communiqué of the Central Statistical Bureau of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union on the results of the implementation of the plan for the development of the national economy of the Soviet Union in the first half of 1953. All newspapers pointed out that this is a new achievement of the Soviet people in the work of building communism. The "Pravda" editorial said: "This communiqué shows that our powerful motherland is confidently advancing along the road of building communism. Under the leadership of the Communist Party, the Soviet people, closely united around the Party's battle banner, are fighting selflessly to complete and overfulfill the Five-Year Plan and are achieving new successes in the development of the socialist economy and culture." The "Izvestia" editorial said: "The achievements of communist construction in our country are the result of the firm alliance between workers and peasants, the increasingly strengthened friendship among all nationalities of the Soviet Union, and the continuous consolidation of the spiritual and political unity of Soviet society. These achievements have been attained due to the implementation of the policies formulated by the Communist Party. The Soviet people are filled with unprecedented and boundless creativity, united around their beloved Party, and confidently advancing along the road of successfully building communism in our country."

### 莫斯科各报论苏联一九五三年上半年生产公报  指出苏联人民建设共产主义获得新成就

1953-07-19
第4版()
专栏：

    莫斯科各报论苏联一九五三年上半年生产公报
    指出苏联人民建设共产主义获得新成就
    【新华社莫斯科十八日电】莫斯科各报都在十七日发表了苏联部长会议中央统计局关于苏联一九五三年上半年发展国民经济计划执行结果的公报。各报都指出这是苏联人民在建设共产主义的工作中所获得的新成就。“真理报”的社论说：“这个公报显示，我们强大的祖国满怀信心地循着建设共产主义的道路前进。苏联人民在共产党的领导下，紧密地团结在党的战斗旗帜的周围，正在奋不顾身地为完成和超额完成五年计划而斗争，并在发展社会主义经济和文化方面获得新的成就。”“消息报”社论说：“我国共产主义建设的成就，是工农牢固地联盟的结果，是苏联各族人民的友好日益加强和苏维埃社会精神上和政治上的团结不断地巩固的结果。这些成就是由于贯彻实行共产党所制订的政策而达到的。苏联人民充满着空前的无穷创造力，团结在自己亲爱的党的周围，满怀信心地循着在我国胜利地建设共产主义的道路前进。”
