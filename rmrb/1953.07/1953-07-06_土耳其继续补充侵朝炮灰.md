Title: Turkey Continues to Replenish Cannon Fodder in Korean War

Author: 
Date: 1953-07-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Istanbul, July 5th: TASS reported from Istanbul on the 4th: According to newspaper reports, a new unit has been formed in Turkey, which will be sent to Korea to supplement the Turkish Brigade. The first batch of this unit was sent to Korea a few days ago. The second batch of two thousand Turkish officers and soldiers will go to Korea on July 2nd, and the third batch of one thousand six hundred will leave on July 5th. The Turkish army will be transported by American ships, departing from Izmir.
    This is the fourth replenishment of the Turkish Brigade during the Korean War.

### 土耳其继续补充侵朝炮灰

1953-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

    土耳其继续补充侵朝炮灰
    【新华社五日讯】塔斯社伊斯坦布尔四日讯：根据报纸的报道，在土耳其已经编成了一个新的部队，它将被送到朝鲜补充土耳其旅。这个部队的第一批已于数日前送往朝鲜。第二批二千名土耳其官兵将在七月二日去朝鲜，第三批一千六百人将于七月五日前去。土耳其军队将由美国船运送，从伊斯密尔出发。
    在朝鲜战争中，这是土耳其旅第四次的补充。
