Title: Commemorating the Fourth Anniversary of Dimitrov's Death, Chervenkov and Others Lay Wreaths at Dimitrov's Mausoleum, Personnel from the Soviet, Chinese, Polish, and Romanian Embassies in Bulgaria Lay Wreaths

Author:

Date: 1953-07-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 4th: According to TASS Sofia, July 3rd: To commemorate the fourth anniversary of the death of Georgi Dimitrov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria and General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, Vulko Chervenkov, led government ministers and members of the Party Central Committee in laying wreaths at the Georgi Dimitrov Mausoleum on the 3rd.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 4th: The Bulgarian Telegraph Agency reported that on the fourth anniversary of Dimitrov's death (July 2nd), personnel from the Soviet, Chinese, Polish, and Romanian embassies in Bulgaria laid wreaths at the Dimitrov Mausoleum.

### 纪念季米特洛夫逝世四周年  契尔文科夫等向季米特洛夫陵墓献花圈  苏、中、波、罗驻保使馆人员前往献花圈

1953-07-05
第4版()
专栏：

    纪念季米特洛夫逝世四周年
    契尔文科夫等向季米特洛夫陵墓献花圈
    苏、中、波、罗驻保使馆人员前往献花圈
    【新华社四日讯】据塔斯社索非亚三日讯：为纪念乔治·季米特洛夫逝世四周年，保加利亚人民共和国部长会议主席、保加利亚共产党中央委员会总书记伏·契尔文科夫，在三日率领政府部长和党中央委员们向乔治·季米特洛夫陵墓献花圈。
    【新华社布拉格四日电】保加利亚通讯社报道，在季米特洛夫逝世四周年纪念日（七月二日），苏联、中国、波兰和罗马尼亚驻保加利亚大使馆人员，前往季米特洛夫陵墓献花圈。
