Title: Mongolian People Celebrate the 32nd Anniversary of the Revolution
Author:
Date: 1953-07-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, July 14th: The celebration of the 32nd anniversary of the victory of the Mongolian Revolution has concluded in Ulaanbaatar after three consecutive days. Over these days, tens of thousands of people from all over Mongolia visited "Naadam" Square. An exhibition was held here, illustrating the achievements of the Mongolian People's Republic in animal husbandry, industry, transportation, education, and health. A special room in the exhibition was dedicated to introducing the achievements of the Soviet Union's communist construction.
    During the celebration, sports competitions were held, and Mongolian and Soviet actors and the Polish Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble performed.
    Finally, a closing ceremony was held in "Naadam" Square.

### 蒙古人民欢庆革命胜利三十二周年

1953-07-16
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民欢庆革命胜利三十二周年
    【新华社十五日讯】据塔斯社乌兰巴托十四日讯：蒙古人民庆祝革命胜利三十二周年连续进行了三天以后，已在乌兰巴托结束。这几天，来自蒙古各地的数万人民参观了“那多木”广场。这里举行了展览会，说明蒙古人民共和国在畜牧业、工业、运输、教育、卫生等方面获得的成就。展览会专设一室，介绍苏联共产主义建设的成绩。
    在庆祝期间，举行了运动竞赛会，蒙古、苏联的演员和波兰玛佐夫舍歌舞团都作了演出。
    最后，在“那多木”广场举行了庆祝的闭幕大会。
