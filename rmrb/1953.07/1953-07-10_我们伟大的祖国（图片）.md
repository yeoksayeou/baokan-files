Author:
Date: 1953-07-10

    Our Great Motherland
    On the banks of the beautiful Ili River in Xinjiang, a new girls' high school has been established. Studying here are young women from six ethnic groups from the Tianshan pastoral area and along the Ili River. This is a group of female students, just out of class.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在新疆美丽的伊犁河畔，新成立了一所女子高级中学。在这里读书的有来自天山牧区和伊犁河沿岸的六个民族的青年女子。这是成群结队的女学生，她们刚刚下课。
