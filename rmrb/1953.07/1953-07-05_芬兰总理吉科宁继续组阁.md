Title: Finnish Prime Minister Kekkonen Continues Cabinet Formation
Author: 
Date: 1953-07-05
Page: 4
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, July 4th: TASS, Helsinki, July 3rd: According to the Finnish News Agency, Finnish Prime Minister Kekkonen paid a visit to the Finnish President last night, reporting on discussions with various parties regarding resolving the government crisis. The President entrusted Kekkonen with the task of forming a new government. Kekkonen accepted the commission.
　　Note: Finnish Prime Minister Kekkonen submitted his government's resignation to the President of the Republic on June 29th.

### 芬兰总理吉科宁继续组阁

1953-07-05
第4版()
专栏：

    芬兰总理吉科宁继续组阁
    【新华社四日讯】塔斯社赫尔辛基三日讯：据芬兰电讯社讯：芬兰政府总理吉科宁于昨晚进谒芬兰总统，报告与各党派商谈消除政府危机的情况。总统委托吉科宁组织新政府。吉科宁接受了委托。
    按：芬兰总理吉科宁是在六月二十九日向共和国总统提出政府辞呈的。
