Title: Soviet Komsomol Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1953-07-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 14th: The Soviet Lenin Communist Youth League delegation, invited to attend the Second National Congress of the China New Democratic Youth League, departed Beijing by plane on the morning of the 14th to return to the Soviet Union after visiting Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other places. Luo Yi, Rong Gaotang, Zhang Ze, and Hu Keshi, Secretaries of the Central Secretariat of the China New Democratic Youth League, and personnel from the Soviet Embassy in China were at the airport to see them off.

### 苏联青年团代表团离京返国

1953-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联青年团代表团离京返国
    【新华社十四日讯】应邀参加中国新民主主义青年团第二次全国代表大会的苏联列宁共产主义青年团代表团，赴南京、上海、杭州等地参观后，在十四日上午乘机离京返苏。前往机场欢送的有中国新民主主义青年团中央书记处书记罗毅、荣高棠、章泽、胡克实及苏联驻华大使馆人员。
