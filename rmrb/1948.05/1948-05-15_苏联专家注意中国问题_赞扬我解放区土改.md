Title: Soviet Experts Pay Attention to the Chinese Question, Praising Land Reform in Our Liberated Areas
Author:
Date: 1948-05-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, May 13th: Moscow dispatch: A scientific conference on the problems of Eastern countries was recently held in Leningrad. Various special reports were presented on the deepening crisis of the colonial system in the Far East and Near East, and the rising of national consciousness among the people. The report by Professor Stein, Doctor of Economics and a well-known Sinologist, attracted the most attention. He emphatically pointed out that after World War II, China has become a country where the national liberation movement has made very significant progress, and emphasized the great significance of the land reform implemented in the Chinese liberated areas.

### 苏联专家注意中国问题  赞扬我解放区土改

1948-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联专家注意中国问题
    赞扬我解放区土改
    【新华社陕北十三日电】莫斯科讯：列宁格勒日前举行研究东方各国问题的科学会议，会上有关于远东与近东殖民地制度危机的加深与人民民族意识高涨的各种专题报告。经济学博士著名汉学家斯坦因教授所作的报告，最引人注意。他着重指出：第二次世界大战后，中国已成为一个民族解放运动进展十分显著的国家，并强调中国解放区所实行的土地改革的伟大意义。
