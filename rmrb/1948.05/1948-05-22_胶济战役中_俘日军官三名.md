Title: Three Japanese Officers Captured in the Jiaoji Campaign
Author:
Date: 1948-05-22
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, East China Front, 20th. Among the captives from the Kuomintang bandit army's 45th Reorganized Division captured in the central section of the Jiaoji Campaign, we have identified three more Japanese officers: Fukui Kai, alias Fu Jinghui, a captain instructor of the 212th Brigade, from Hiroshima, formerly belonging to the Japanese 12th Army; Matsumoto Taku, a captain military doctor of the division's field hospital, from Tokyo, formerly a lieutenant military doctor of the Japanese Jinan 159th Garrison Hospital; and Onda Noboru, alias Tian Shaoen, a captain communications officer of the division's communications battalion, from Gunma Prefecture, formerly a communications technician of the Japanese 12th Army.

### 胶济战役中　俘日军官三名

1948-05-22
第2版()
专栏：

　　胶济战役中
  　俘日军官三名
    【新华社华东前线二十日电】在胶济中段战役被俘的国民党匪军整四十五师俘虏中，我又查出日籍军官三名，计二一二旅上尉教官福井回，化名富景辉，广岛人，原属日寇第十二军。师野战医院上尉军医松本卓，东京人，原属日寇济南一五九兵站医院中尉军医。及师通讯营上尉通讯士恩田升，化名田绍恩，群马县人，原属日寇第十二军通讯技手。
