Correspondence

No. 27

To comrades of local county committees, offices, district committees, work groups, and correspondents:

How is the production in your area progressing? Has the sowing been completed? In places where sowing has been completed, how are you organizing sideline production during the gap between sowing and wheat harvesting? Please organize reports quickly!

The main problems in production: how to implement the voluntary and equivalent mutual aid policy to rectify mutual aid teams; how to compensate middle peasants who were wrongly struggled against, and how to settle landlords and rich peasants so that they can actively produce; how to solve the specific difficulties of poor and hired peasants in production, please report on these emphatically, and write down the solutions in detail and specifically.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　The People's Daily Correspondence Section

### 通讯往来　第二十七号
本报通联科
1948-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　通讯往来
    第二十七号各地县委、办公室、区分委、工作组、通讯员同志：
    你们那里的生产搞得怎样了？是否已完成下种？完成下种的地方，如何在下种与麦收的空隙组织副业生产？请赶快组织报导！
    生产中的主要问题：如何贯彻自愿等价的互助政策整顿互助组；如何补偿错斗中农，安置地主富农，使他们能够积极生产；如何解决贫雇农民生产中的具体困难，请着重报导，并将解决问题的办法详细具体的写出来。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　本报通联科
