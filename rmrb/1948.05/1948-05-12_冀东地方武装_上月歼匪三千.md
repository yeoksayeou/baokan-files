Title: Local Armed Forces in Eastern Hebei Annihilate 3,000 Bandits Last Month
Author:
Date: 1948-05-12
Page: 2
　　
    Local Armed Forces in Eastern Hebei
    Annihilate 3,000 Bandits Last Month

    (Xinhua News Agency, Eastern Hebei, 10th) The headquarters of the Eastern Hebei Military Region recently announced the results of the local armed forces' battles in April as follows: Eight enemy strongholds of Chiang Kai-shek's bandits, including Xiadian on the Tongxian-Tangshan Highway and Huzhuang in the east of Tongxian city, were captured or forced to retreat. A total of 2,906 enemies were annihilated, including 1,678 killed or wounded regular enemy soldiers and 1,228 captured alive. The following were seized: two Type 92 infantry guns, three 60mm mortars, two mortars, nine grenade launchers, 58 light and heavy machine guns, 24 submachine guns, 810 long and short guns, 104 shells, 86,659 rounds of ammunition, 1,202 hand grenades, and a portion of other materials.

### 冀东地方武装  上月歼匪三千

1948-05-12
第2版()
专栏：

    冀东地方武装
    上月歼匪三千
    【新华社冀东十日电】冀东军区司令部顷公布四月份地方武装战果如下：攻克与逼退通县唐山公路上之夏店及通县城东之胡庄等蒋匪据点八处，歼敌二千九百零六名，其中毙伤敌正规军一千六百七十八名、生俘一千二百二十八名，缴获九二步兵炮二门、六○炮三门、迫击炮二门、掷弹筒九个、轻重机枪五十八挺、冲锋枪二十四支、长短枪八百十支、炮弹一○四发、子弹八万六千六百五十九发、手榴弹一千二百零二枚，及其他物资一部。
