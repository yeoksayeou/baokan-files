Title: New York Citizens Demonstrate Against Screening of Anti-Soviet Film
Author:
Date: 1948-05-27
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 23rd: TASS, New York: The broad masses of progressive people in the United States have expressed strong anger and protest against the screening of the fabricated anti-Soviet film "The Iron Curtain" in American cinemas. Ten thousand New York citizens broke through various illegal suppressions by the police on the 11th and held a large-scale demonstration. Demonstrators distributed thousands of leaflets calling on the people to boycott the cinemas showing the film. At the same time, various progressive groups in New York set up picket lines around the "Roxy" cinema, where the film was being screened, and distributed tens of thousands of leaflets condemning the film every day. The film also encountered similar protests when it was shown in Cleveland, Chicago, and other places.

### 纽约市民示威游行抗议反苏影片上演

1948-05-27
第2版()
专栏：

　　纽约市民示威游行抗议反苏影片上演
    【新华社陕北二十三日电】塔斯社纽约讯：美国广大进步群众对美国影院上演反苏的捏造影片“铁幕”一事，表示强烈的愤怒与抗议。纽约市民万人于十一日冲破警察的种种非法镇压，举行了大规模的示威游行，示威者散发成千张传单，呼吁人民抑制上演该片的电影院，同时纽约各进步团体每日均在上演该片的“罗基西”影院周围布置纠察线，散发成万张遣责该片的传单，该片在克利夫兰、芝加哥等地演出时，亦遭遇同样的抗议。
