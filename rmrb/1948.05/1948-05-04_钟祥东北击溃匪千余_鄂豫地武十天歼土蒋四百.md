Title: Northeast of Zhongxiang Crushes Over a Thousand Bandits; Local Armed Forces in Hubei-Henan Eliminate 400 Local Chiang Troops in Ten Days
Author:
Date: 1948-05-04
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains Front, 2nd: On the 25th, the People's Liberation Army of the Jianghan Military Region, in Yangzi Town, northeast of Zhongxiang, crushed over a thousand enemies and local Chiang troops who were creating disturbances, including a battalion of the 246th Regiment of the 82nd Brigade. Over fifty were killed or wounded, including a company commander, and thirty-seven were captured alive. Three light machine guns, seven submachine guns, and twenty-five rifles were seized, along with some ammunition and military supplies.

Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains Front, 2nd: Local armed forces of the Hubei-Henan Military Region actively captured and eliminated roving local Chiang bandits. From the 10th to the 20th, they engaged in eleven battles, killing or wounding 113 bandits including bandit chief Wang Keyi (killed), capturing 346 bandits alive, seizing nine light and heavy machine guns, one 60mm mortar, and 170 rifles, capturing twenty pillboxes, destroying fifteen *li* of highway, and collecting six hundred *jin* of electric wire.

Xinhua News Agency, East China, 2nd: Delayed news: The Jingjiang People's Armed Forces, active along the Yangtze River, ambushed seven Chiang bandit gasoline boats carrying military rice on the Jiangbian River in the Baimu District on the 11th of last month, capturing the leading three boats and seizing over a thousand *shi* of rice.

### 钟祥东北击溃匪千余　鄂豫地武十天歼土蒋四百

1948-05-04
第2版()
专栏：

　　钟祥东北击溃匪千余
  　鄂豫地武十天歼土蒋四百
    【新华社中原前线二日电】江汉军区解放军二十五日在钟祥东北之洋梓镇，击溃出扰之敌八二旅二四六团一个营及土蒋共千余名，毙伤其连长以下五十余名，生俘三十七名，缴轻机枪三挺，冲锋枪七支，步枪二十五支，弹药及军用品一部。
    【新华社中原前线二日电】鄂豫军区地方武装积极捕歼流窜之土蒋匪，自十日至二十日中，共作战十一次，毙伤匪首王克夷（毙）以下一百一十三名，生俘匪三百四十六名，缴获轻重机枪九挺，六○炮一门，步枪一百七十支，攻克碉堡二十处，破坏公路十五里，收割电线六百斤。
    【新华社华东二日电】迟到消息：活跃长江沿岸的靖江人民武装，上月十一日在柏木区江边奇袭载运军米之蒋匪汽油船七艘，俘获其为首三艘，缴大米一千余石。
