Title: Indonesian People's Armed Forces Attack Dutch Imperialist Barracks
Author: 
Date: 1948-05-12
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, May 10th: Paris dispatch: One thousand Indonesian people's armed forces, wielding spears and other weapons, recently attacked Dutch military barracks in Nanjiliwen. It is reported that 200 Dutch invading troops were killed. According to a report from the Associated Press on the 7th, Dutch imperialist officials also admitted "heavy casualties."

### 印尼人民武装袭击荷帝兵营

1948-05-12
第2版()
专栏：

    印尼人民武装袭击荷帝兵营
    【新华社陕北十日电】巴黎讯：手持标枪等武器的一千名印尼人民武装，于日前曾袭击南井里汶之荷军兵营。据悉，毙荷侵略军二百人。据合众社七日电透露：荷帝官方亦供认“伤亡甚大”。
