Title: Burmese Communist Armed Forces Grow Stronger, Attack Enemy Near Rangoon
Author:
Date: 1948-05-18
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 12th: Rangoon dispatch: Burmese Communist armed forces attacked enemy strongholds eight miles from Rangoon at the end of last month, engaging the defending enemy in fierce fighting. A unit of the Burmese Communist armed forces also successfully ambushed the defeated enemy. It is reported that slogans opposing the reactionary government of Tekin Nu have been found posted on the streets of Rangoon by the Burmese Communists. At the same time, traffic between Rangoon and Mandalay has been paralyzed due to the destruction of bridges in many places.

### 缅共武装更加强大  进袭仰光附近敌人

1948-05-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　缅共武装更加强大
    进袭仰光附近敌人
    【新华社陕北十二日电】仰光讯：缅共武装于上月底曾袭击距仰光八里之敌据点，与守敌激战。缅共武装一部并胜利伏击败敌。据悉，在仰光大街上已发现缅共张贴反对德钦努反动政府的标语。同时，仰光——曼德勒间之交通因多处桥梁被焚毁而陷于瘫痪状态。
