　　Important Correction
    In the "Taihang Branch of Xinhua News Agency Editor's Note" at the end of the front-page headline news "Changzhi Municipal Party Committee Inspects the Roots of Left-Leaning Errors" on the second page of this newspaper on May 15th, the fourteen characters "(Mao Zedong: On the Current Situation and Our Tasks)" should be removed. This was due to an oversight by the editor, and we hereby make a correction and offer our apologies.
