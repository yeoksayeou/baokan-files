Title: Czechoslovak Communist Party Strong Development - Has 1.4 Million Members
Author:
Date: 1948-05-10
Page: 4
　　
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 8th: Prague dispatch: The Czechoslovak Communist Party continues to grow stronger. According to the Czechoslovak Communist Party's newspaper: In the first three weeks of April, 185,390 people joined the Czechoslovak Communist Party; and during the "Gottwald Week" from March 7th to 15th, 190,138 people joined the party; prior to this, as of the end of February, the Czechoslovak Communist Party already had 1,412,000 members.
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 8th: Prague dispatch: After Czechoslovakia and Bulgaria signed a mutual assistance treaty on April 23rd, they signed a temporary trade agreement and a social security cooperation agreement on the 25th. The Czechoslovak government also agreed to lend part of its assets in Bulgaria to the Bulgarian government for the purpose of developing the national economy.

### 捷共强大发展　已有党员百四十万

1948-05-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　捷共强大发展
  　已有党员百四十万
    【新华社陕北八日电】布拉格讯：捷共继续壮大。据捷共机关报载：四月份头三周中有十八万五千三百九十人参加捷共；而在三月七日至十五日的“哥特瓦尔德周”中，则有十九万零一百三十八人入党；前此，截至二月底，捷共已有党员一百四十一万二千人。
    【新华社陕北八日电】布拉格讯：捷保于四月廿三日签订互助条约后，廿五日又签订了临时贸易协定和社会治安合作协定。捷政府并同意将其在保国的一部份资产借予保政府，作为发展国家经济之用。
