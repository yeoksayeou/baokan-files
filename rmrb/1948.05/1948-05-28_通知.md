　　Recently, this station has received letters from some military families asking why their letters, which were sent a long time ago, have not been broadcast yet? Others ask if there are any replies. Apart from individual replies that have been sent, we would like to further explain here: The reason why letters sent a long time ago have not been broadcast is because of time constraints, apart from those not received by this station. This station can broadcast at most seven or eight letters a day (broadcasting letters can only take up a certain amount of time), while we receive more than a dozen or twenty to thirty letters every day, resulting in delays. If the front lines write back, they are sent directly through military mail, so please wait a little longer, military families.
