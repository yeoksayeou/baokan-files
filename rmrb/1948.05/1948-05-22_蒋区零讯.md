Title: Chiang Controlled Area News Briefs
Author:
Date: 1948-05-22
Page: 2

　　Chiang Controlled Area News Briefs
    ▲The Chiang bandit government formally removed Chen Cheng from his concurrent positions as Chief of Staff and Director of the "Northeast Headquarters" on the 12th. Gu Zhutong, Commander-in-Chief of the Army of the bandit Ministry of National Defense, was appointed as Chief of Staff, and Yu Hanmou, Director of the Quzhou Pacification, was appointed as Commander-in-Chief of the Army.
    ▲The Chiang bandit Ministry of National Defense announced the so-called "Military-Political Coordination Plan for Bandit Suppression Areas" on the 12th, stipulating the establishment of "Political Affairs Committees" under the Pacification General Headquarters in various bandit areas, with the bandit commanders of each area concurrently serving as chairmen, and the civil administration of each area also under the jurisdiction of the bandit military leaders.

### 蒋区零讯

1948-05-22
第2版()
专栏：

　　蒋区零讯
    ▲蒋匪政府已于十二日正式撤免匪参谋总长兼“东北行辕”主任陈诚本兼各职，改任匪国防部陆军总司令顾祝同为匪参谋总长，并改以匪衢州绥靖主任余汉谋为匪陆军总司令。
    ▲蒋匪国防部十二日公布所谓“剿匪区军政配合方案”，规定在匪各区绥靖总司令部下设立“政务委员会”，由匪各区总司令兼主委、各区民政亦归匪军首脑统辖。
