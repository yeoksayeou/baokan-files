Title: In the Northern Section of the Pinghan Railway, the KMT's New Second Army: A Company Gloriously Uprises; Army Commander Gao Zhuodong Wounded by Our Forces
Author:
Date: 1948-05-31
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji Front, 28th: Eighty-five officers and soldiers of the Special Service Company (minus one platoon) of the 31st Division of the Chiang bandit New Second Army, stationed in Dingxing, in the northern section of the Pinghan Railway, gloriously revolted and entered the liberated area on the 19th, led by Company Commander Zhang Guodong and Division Chief of Staff Ma Zi'an. They brought with them five light machine guns, twelve submachine guns, two grenade launchers, nine rifle grenade launchers, fifty-three rifles, three pistols, and over ten thousand rounds of ammunition. They were warmly welcomed by the military and civilians of the liberated areas along the way.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji, 28th: A unit of the North China People's Liberation Army ambushed Chiang bandit New Second Army Commander Gao Zhuodong and his guard battalion, who were en route from Baoding to Beiping, near Baitabu, ten miles north of Xushui city, in the northern section of the Pinghan Railway on the 20th. The bandit Gao was wounded on the spot and fled to Beiping in a panic. In this battle, over thirty bandits were killed, fifty-six bandits were captured, one bandit armored vehicle and five American trucks were destroyed, and ten light and heavy machine guns, three submachine guns, and forty-seven rifles and pistols were seized.

### 平汉北段蒋军新二军　一个连光荣起义　军长高卓东被我击伤

1948-05-31
第2版()
专栏：

　　平汉北段蒋军新二军
  　一个连光荣起义
  　军长高卓东被我击伤
    【新华社晋察冀前线二十八日电】驻平汉北段定兴之蒋匪新二军三十一师特务连（缺一个排）官兵八十五名，十九日在连长张国栋及师参谋长马子安率领下，光荣起义开入解放区，携来轻机枪五挺，冲锋枪十二支，掷弹筒两个，枪榴弹筒九个，步枪五十三支，手枪三支，子弹万余发，沿途备受解放区军民欢迎。
    【新华社晋察冀二十八日电】华北人民解放军一部，二十日在平汉北段徐水城北十里之白塔埠附近，伏击由保定出发前往北平之蒋匪新二军军长高卓东及其警卫部队一营。高匪当场被击负伤，狼狈向北平逃窜，是役共毙匪三十余名，俘匪五十六名，击毁匪装甲车一辆，美式卡车五辆，缴获轻重机枪十挺，冲锋枪三支，长短枪四十七支。
