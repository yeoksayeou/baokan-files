Title: Inner Mongolia Autonomous Government Anniversary: All Circles Send Telegrams to Chairman Mao to Pay Respects
Author:
Date: 1948-05-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 13th: On the "May 1st" anniversary of the establishment of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Government, all circles in Ulanhot City held a grand celebration meeting and sent telegrams to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, stating: "Under your leadership and assistance, the people of Inner Mongolia have liberated vast areas. The Inner Mongolian people have achieved the people's unification, solidarity, and national autonomy for the first time and established the autonomous government. This fully proves the correctness of the policy you put forward regarding the minority nationalities of China. All ethnic minorities in China will follow this path to achieve liberation. Today is the anniversary of the establishment of the autonomous government, coinciding with the recovery of the revolutionary sacred place of Yan'an, symbolizing that the national victory is not far off. We extend our warm congratulations and pay our sincere respects to you. We are determined to continue our efforts under your leadership, cooperate with the nationwide victorious counteroffensive, and strive to realize a new China united by all nationalities."

### 内蒙自治政府周年纪念  各界电毛主席致敬

1948-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    内蒙自治政府周年纪念
    各界电毛主席致敬
    【新华社东北十三日电】“五一”内蒙古自治政府成立周年纪念日，乌兰浩特市各界曾热烈举行庆祝大会，并电毛主席、朱总司令暨中共中央委员会致敬，电称：“内蒙古人民在你们的领导和帮助下，广大地区已获得解放。内蒙古民族第一次实现了人民的统一、团结与民族自治，建立了自治政府。这充分证明了你们提出来的中国少数民族政策的正确。中国各少数民族均将循着这条道路而获得解放。今天是自治政府成立周年纪念，适逢革命圣地延安已告光复，象征全国胜利为期不远，谨向你们致热烈的庆贺，并致亲切的敬意。我们决在你们领导下继续努力，配合全国的胜利大反攻，为实现各民族联合的新中国而奋斗”。
