Title: Italian People's Democratic Front Reaffirms Determination to Struggle
Author:
Date: 1948-05-10
Page: 4

　　Italian People's Democratic Front
  　Reaffirms Determination to Struggle
    【Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 5th】Rome dispatch: The Presidium and Executive Committee of the Italian People's Democratic Front, chaired by the socialist Enrico Meier, held a joint meeting on the 29th of last month. The meeting's communique reaffirmed the determination of the parties and democratic groups participating in the Front to continue the common struggle under the new circumstances, in order to defend democracy and various reforms that conform to constitutional principles. The communique also exposed to the country and the world the illegal nature of this election, saying that it was conducted under unprecedented material and spiritual pressure, as well as shameless foreign interference.

### 意国民主阵线　重申斗争决心

1948-05-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　意国民主阵线
  　重申斗争决心
    【新华社陕北五日电】罗马讯：意人民民主阵线主席团及执委会，在社会党人恩利考·麦尔主持下，于上月二十九日召开联席会议。会议公报重申参加该阵线的各政党与民主团体在新的情况下继续共同斗争的决心，以保卫民主与符合于宪法原则的各种改革。公报并向全国及全世界揭露这次选举的非法性质说：它是在史无前例的物质与精神压力以及无耻的外国干涉下举行的。
