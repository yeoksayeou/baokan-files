Title: Joint Statement by Hong Kong Democratic Parties in Response to Our Party's Political Propositions
Author:
Date: 1948-05-13
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 11th: Hong Kong Dispatch: Leaders of various democratic parties residing in Hong Kong issued a joint statement on the 5th, responding to the Chinese Communist Party's proposition of "swiftly convening a political consultative conference to discuss and realize the convocation of a people's representative assembly and the establishment of a democratic coalition government." The statement expressed complete agreement and support for the aforementioned proposition; at the same time, it condemned the Nanjing Chiang Kai-shek government for stealing national power and selling out Chinese sovereignty to American imperialism. Signatories to the statement included responsible persons from the Democratic League, the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang, the National Salvation Association, and the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party (i.e., the Third Party).

### 香港各民主党派联合声明  响应我党政治主张

1948-05-13
第2版()
专栏：

    香港各民主党派联合声明
    响应我党政治主张
    【新华社陕北十一日电】香港讯：寓港各民主党派领袖，于五日发表联合声明，响应中国共产党关于“迅速召开政治协商会议，讨论并实现召集人民代表大会，成立民主联合政府”之主张，声明表示完全同意及支持上述主张；同时谴责南京蒋介石政府盗窃国家政权及出卖中国主权给美帝国主义之行为。签署于该项声明者包括民主同盟、国民党革命委员会、救国会、农工民主党（即第三党）等各民主政团负责人。
