Title: Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Distributes 3.8 Billion Yuan in Interest-Free Agricultural Loans
Author:
Date: 1948-05-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northwest, 4th. The Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Government has recently distributed 3.8 billion yuan (local currency) in interest-free agricultural loans to help farmers in the border region, who suffered from the Chiang Kai-shek and Hu Zongnan bandit disaster, to restore and develop production. The loans have been distributed to various special districts based on local conditions, including 1.3 billion yuan for the Yan'an sub-district, of which 300 million yuan has been designated by the Border Region Government for use in Yan'an and newly recovered areas.

### 陕甘宁发放无利农贷三十八亿元

1948-05-07
第2版()
专栏：

　　陕甘宁发放无利农贷三十八亿元
    【新华社西北四日电】陕甘宁边区政府顷发放无利农贷本币三十八亿元，帮助受蒋胡匪灾的边区农民恢复与发展生产，并已依据各地情况分发各专区，其中延属分区共十三亿元，内三亿元经边府指定用于延安及各新收复区。
