Recruitment Notice for the Car Department Training Class of the Railway Bureau, Jin-Ji-Lu-Yu Border Region Government, Transportation Department

    I. Purpose: To cultivate railway management cadres and develop the railway transportation construction cause of the new democratic country.
    II. Quota: Thirty
    III. Qualifications: 1. Graduates of junior high school or those with equivalent education. 2. Aged between 18 and 25. 3. Healthy with no serious illness or bad habits. 4. Those who are interested in railway work and can endure hardship.
    IV. Registration Date: From June 15th to June 20th.
    V. Registration Location: Handan Railway Station.
    VI. Registration Procedures: Applicants must personally submit their graduation certificates or certificates from government agencies or schools to exchange for examination permits.
    VII. Examination Date and Subjects: 1. Examination Date: Examinations will begin on June 21st. 2. Examination Subjects: Oral test, physical examination, Chinese language, mathematics, general knowledge, and intelligence test.
    VIII. Examination Location: This Bureau in Handan.
    IX. Training Period: Temporarily set for one month, starting from July 1st.
    X. Remuneration: All expenses for meals, accommodation, lectures, etc., during the training period will be borne by this Bureau. After graduation, the Bureau will assign jobs, and the remuneration will be in accordance with the employee treatment methods of this railway.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Director Wang Xiaobin

### 晋冀鲁豫边区政府、交通厅馆涉铁路局车务训练班招生简章
王效斌
1948-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　晋冀鲁豫边区政府、交通厅馆涉铁路局车务训练班招生简章
    一、宗旨：为培养铁路管理干部发展新民主主义国家铁路交通建设事业。
    二、名额：三十名
    三、资格：１、初中毕业或有同等学历者。２、年在十八岁以上二十五岁以下者。３、身体健康没有恶疾或不良嗜好者。４、对铁路工作有志趣并能吃苦耐劳者。
    四、报名日期：自六月十五日起至六月二十日止。
    五、报名地点：邯郸车站。
    六、报名手续：本人须亲自交验毕业证书或机关学校证明文件换取考试证。
    七、考试日期及科目：１、考试日期：六月二十一日开始考试。２、考试科目：口试，体格检查，国文，数学，常识，智力测验。
    八、考试地点：邯郸本局。
    九、训练时间：自七月一日起暂定为一个月。
    十、待遇：训练期间一切膳宿讲义等费全由本局负责，毕业后由本局分配工作，其待遇按本路员工待遇办法。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　局长　王效斌
