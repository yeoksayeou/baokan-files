Title: Jinbei Our Army Liberates Ying County; Yu-Shaan-E Recovers Jingziguan, Shangnan, and Lushi
Author:
Date: 1948-05-29
Page: 1
　　Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji Front, 27th: A unit of the North China People's Liberation Army liberated Ying County, a key military location in northern Shanxi, on the 24th. The defending enemy, Yan's bandit Zhang Pu's Provisional First Regiment and others, numbering over four thousand, suffered more than half casualties under our fierce attack. Fearing complete annihilation, the enemy abandoned the city and fled towards Datong with the support of bandit troops from Datong. Currently, there are no enemy traces in the entire Ying County area.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains Front, 27th: A unit of the People's Liberation Army, combined with local armed forces from a certain sub-district of Yu-Shaan-E, simultaneously recovered Jingziguan, a vital transportation hub between the three provinces of Henan, Shaanxi, and Hubei, and Shangnan County, located northwest of Jingziguan in Shaanxi Province, on the 19th. The defending enemies in both locations fled upon hearing the news. Our troops pursued and annihilated the fleeing enemies, capturing over sixty bandits including company commanders and below, seizing fifty-five rifles, and over five thousand rounds of ammunition. On the morning of the 20th, local armed forces from a certain sub-district of Yu-Shaan-E recovered Lushi County, located north of Jingziguan. The defending enemy fled towards the north of the city, and our troops are currently in pursuit.

### 晋北我军解放应县　豫陕鄂收复荆紫关商南卢氏

1948-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　晋北我军解放应县
  　豫陕鄂收复荆紫关商南卢氏
    【新华社晋察冀前线二十七日电】华北人民解放军一部，二十四日解放晋北军事要地应县城。守敌阎匪暂一团张朴部等四千余人，经我勇猛攻击伤亡过半，该敌畏惧被我全歼，乃在大同匪军接应下，弃城向大同逃窜。现应县全境已无敌踪。
    【新华社中原前线二十七日电】解放军一部结合豫陕鄂某分区地方武装，于十九日同时收复豫陕鄂三省交通要冲荆紫关及其西北陕西境内的商南县城。两地守敌均闻风逃窜，我追歼逃敌，两地共俘匪连长以下六十余名，缴步枪五十五支，子弹五千余发。二十日晨豫陕鄂某分区地方武装收复荆紫关以北的卢氏县城，守敌向城北方向逃窜，我正追歼中。
