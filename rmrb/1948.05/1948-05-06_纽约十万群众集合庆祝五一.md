Title: Ten Thousand People Gather in New York to Celebrate May Day

Author:

Date: 1948-05-06
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 4th: New York - The American people celebrated May Day under the slogans of opposing anti-communism, anti-democracy, and defeating the conscription legislation. More than 100,000 people participated in the celebration rally held in New York, and the participants expressed their firm will to fight for the defense of peace and democracy. Mass rallies were also held simultaneously in major cities in Kansas, South Carolina, and other states. Black singer Paul Robeson, at a celebration meeting in Charleston, South Carolina, called for the election of Wallace. Third-party presidential candidate Wallace delivered a speech at a mass rally in Topeka, Kansas, denouncing the so-called "Sabotage Control Act" proposed by the House Un-American Activities Committee as "another measure to push the United States further towards fascism"; he stated: "The American reactionaries cannot scare me into not fighting for peace and defending American democracy with the threat of ten years in prison."

### 纽约十万群众集合庆祝五一

1948-05-06
第2版()
专栏：

　　纽约十万群众集合庆祝五一
    【新华社陕北四日电】纽约讯：美国人民在反对反共反民主与击败征兵立法的口号下庆祝五一节。纽约举行的庆祝集会有十万余人参加，与会者表示为保卫和平民主而斗争的坚强意志。堪萨斯州、南卡罗林纳及其他州的各大城市，亦同时举行群众大会。黑人歌王罗伯逊在南卡罗林纳州查勒士登城的庆祝会上，呼吁选举华莱士。第三党总统候选人华莱士在堪萨斯州托皮卡城的群众大会上发表演说，对非美活动委员会所提出的所谓“破坏行为控制法”，斥之为“欲使美国进一步走向法西斯的另一措施”；他方称：“美国反动派以十年监禁的威胁，是不能吓唬住我不去为和平与保卫美国的民主而奋斗。”
