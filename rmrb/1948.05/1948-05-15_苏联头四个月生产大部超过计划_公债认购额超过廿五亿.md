Title: Soviet Production Largely Exceeds Plans in First Four Months; Bond Subscription Exceeds 2.5 Billion
Author:
Date: 1948-05-15
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 13th: Moscow dispatch: Various industries in the Soviet Union have successively completed and exceeded their plans for the first four months of this year, "marking a further increase in production" (TASS). The power industry's production exceeded the target by 3.4%; western coal mines increased production by 25% compared to the same period last year, and eastern coal mines increased production by 10.3%; cement increased by 43%; the output of silk, cotton, linen, and wool textiles in the textile industry increased by 30% to 44%; the food industry exceeded the plan by 9%, with sugar increasing by 43% compared to the same period last year, bread increasing by 49%, vegetable oil increasing by 51%, and canned goods increasing by 69%. As of early May, 250 factories in Moscow city and oblast, over 100 factories in Leningrad, 50 factories in Rostov Oblast, all textile industries in Kazakhstan, and dozens of mines and building material factories have all completed their May plans.
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 13th: Moscow dispatch: The new national construction bond of 20 billion rubles issued by the Soviet Union on the 3rd, as of the 7th, has sold 22.592233 billion rubles, exceeding the original amount by over 2.5 billion. The Soviet Ministry of Finance issued an order on the 9th to stop subscriptions.

### 苏联头四个月生产大部超过计划  公债认购额超过廿五亿

1948-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联头四个月生产大部超过计划
    公债认购额超过廿五亿
    【新华社陕北十三日电】莫斯科讯：苏联各业已纷纷完成并超过本年头四个月的计划，“标帜着生产的进一步提高”（塔斯社）。电力工业生产超过目标百分之三点四；西部煤矿比去年同期增产百分之二十五，东部煤矿增产百分之十点三；洋灰增产百分之四十三；纺织业丝、棉、麻、毛织品的产量增加百分之三十至四十四；食品业超过计划百分之九，其中糖比去年同期增产百分之四十三，面包增产百分之四十九，植物油增产百分之五十一，罐头增产百分之六十九。五月初，莫斯科市与州的二百五十座工厂、列宁格勒的百余座工厂、罗夫州的五十座工厂、卡萨克斯坦的全部纺织业、几十座矿坑与建筑器材工厂，都已完成五月份计划。
    【新华社陕北十三日电】莫斯科讯：苏联三日发行的二百亿卢布复兴建设新公债，截至七日止，已卖出二百二十五亿九千二百二十三万三千卢布，超过原定数目达二十五亿余。苏联财政部已于九日下令停止认购。
