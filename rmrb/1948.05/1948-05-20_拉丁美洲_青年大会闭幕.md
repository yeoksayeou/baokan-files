Title: Latin American Youth Congress Concludes
Author:
Date: 1948-05-20
Page: 2

　　Latin America
  　Youth Congress Concludes
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 12th: News from various sources: The Latin American Democratic Youth Congress, which began on April 26th in Mexico, successfully concluded a few days ago. Hundreds of representatives from youth organizations in Mexico, Cuba, Puerto Rico, Guatemala, Costa Rica, and other countries attended the congress. The meeting discussed the current tasks of Latin American youth and their struggle for peace, democracy, independence, and progress. It also made important resolutions, vehemently condemning the expansionist policies of the United States, opposing the dictatorial regimes in Chile, Cuba, and other Latin American countries, and demanding independence for Puerto Rico. According to a TASS dispatch on the 7th, the convening of the congress is a major event in the Latin American youth movement, consolidating the unity of Latin American youth.

### 拉丁美洲　青年大会闭幕

1948-05-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　拉丁美洲
  　青年大会闭幕
    【新华社陕北十二日电】各方讯：四月二十六日开始在墨西哥举行的拉丁美洲民主青年大会，已于日前胜利闭幕。出席大会的有来自墨西哥、古巴、波多黎各、危地马拉、哥斯达黎加等国青年团体代表数百人。会议讨论了拉丁美洲青年的当前任务及为争取和平、民主、独立与进步而斗争等问题；并作出重要决议，猛烈谴责美国的扩张政策，反对智利、古巴等拉丁美洲国家中的独裁政权；并要求给予波多黎各以独立。据塔斯社七日电称：大会的召开是拉丁美洲青年运动中的一件重大事件，它巩固了拉丁美洲青年的团结。
