Title: General Rain in the Jin-Sui Border Region
Author:
Date: 1948-05-12
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Sui, 10th. On the 6th, another general rain fell in various parts of the border region. The emancipated peasants are now stepping up the planting of cotton and autumn crops such as millet. Since the beginning of spring, the weather has been favorable, and summer crops such as wheat and beans are growing vigorously, the best in ten years, foreshadowing a bumper harvest.

### 晋绥边区普降透雨

1948-05-12
第2版()
专栏：

    晋绥边区普降透雨
    【新华社晋绥十日电】六日边区各地再次普降透雨，翻身农民现正加紧种棉花及糜谷等秋季作物。入春以来，风调雨顺，小麦、豆类等夏季作物青苗茁壮，为十年来所未有，预兆丰收。
