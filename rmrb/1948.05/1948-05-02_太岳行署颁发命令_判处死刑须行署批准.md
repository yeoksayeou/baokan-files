Title: Taiyue Administrative Office Issues Order: Death Sentences Must Be Approved by the Administrative Office
Author:
Date: 1948-05-02
Page: 1

　　Taiyue Administrative Office Issues Order
  　Death Sentences Must Be Approved by the Administrative Office
    [Taiyue News] In order to prohibit the phenomenon of indiscriminate killings, the Taiyue Administrative Office issued an order to all commissioners and county heads on April 18, specifying the authority to impose death sentences. The order stated: It is now stipulated that within this region, except for the counties of Ping, Jie, and Ling in the First District and the counties in the Fifth District, where the county administrative conference can discuss and decide on death sentences and report them to the Administrative Office for record-keeping, all other special counties are not allowed to approve executions. If there are cases deserving the death penalty, the Administrative Office should be consulted for approval before execution. All death sentences must be carried out by shooting, and no other methods are permitted.

### 太岳行署颁发命令　判处死刑须行署批准

1948-05-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　太岳行署颁发命令
  　判处死刑须行署批准
    【太岳消息】太岳行署为了禁止乱杀人的现象，四月十八日给各专员县长下了一道命令，具体作出杀人权限的规定。上面说：现规定本区除一分区之平、介、灵及五分区各县，可给县行政会议研究决定判处死刑并呈报行署备案外，其他各专县概不得批准杀人。如有应判处死刑者，应请示行署批准后执行。一切死刑只许执行枪决，不许使用其他任何方法。
