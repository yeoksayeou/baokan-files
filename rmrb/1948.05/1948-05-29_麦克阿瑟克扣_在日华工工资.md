Title: MacArthur Deducts Wages of Chinese Laborers in Japan
Author:
Date: 1948-05-29
Page: 2

　　MacArthur Deducts
　　Wages of Chinese Laborers in Japan
　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 25th—A Nanjing dispatch from the Kuomintang Central News Agency on the 22nd revealed that the reactionary Japanese government, under the protection of MacArthur, has unreasonably deducted $500,000 in wages from the 3,000 Chinese laborers recently repatriated from Japan, and has also confiscated their meager savings. The Chiang government dares not express any opposition to this.

### 麦克阿瑟克扣  在日华工工资

1948-05-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　麦克阿瑟克扣
    在日华工工资
    【新华社陕北二十五日电】国民党中央社南京二十二日消息透露：麦克阿瑟庇护下的日本反动政府，竟无理克扣最近由日回国的三千华工工资五十万美元，并将他们身边仅有的积蓄横加没收，蒋政府对此不敢有所表示。
