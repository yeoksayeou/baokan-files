Title: Soviet New Ambassador Roshchin Arrives in China
Author:
Date: 1948-05-27
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 22nd. Shanghai dispatch: The newly appointed Soviet Ambassador to Nanjing, Roshchin, arrived in Shanghai by ship from Vladivostok on the 20th and took a train to Nanjing that evening to take up his new post.

### 苏新大使罗申来华

1948-05-27
第2版()
专栏：

　　苏新大使罗申来华
    【新华社陕北二十二日电】上海讯，苏联新任驻南京大使罗申于二十日由海参崴乘轮抵沪，当晚即乘车赴宁履新。
