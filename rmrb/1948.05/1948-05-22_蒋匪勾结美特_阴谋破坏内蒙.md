Title: Chiang Kai-shek Bandits Collude with U.S. Special Agents to Conspire to Sabotage Inner Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1948-05-22
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Jin Sui, 20th: Reliable sources confirm: In order to devastate the people's autonomous movement in Inner Mongolia, the Chiang Kai-shek bandit government has obtained the cooperation of U.S. intelligence agencies to establish a specialized organization in Baotou, Suiyuan, to engage in a sabotage plan against the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Government. The sabotage plan includes poisoning efforts, which involve supplying poisonous grain to the people of Inner Mongolia and spreading infectious diseases through Inner Mongolian livestock. U.S. spy Calvin, along with the German assassination and poisoning experts the Soderbaum brothers whom he employs, are currently in charge of this conspiracy.

### 蒋匪勾结美特　阴谋破坏内蒙

1948-05-22
第2版()
专栏：

　　蒋匪勾结美特
  　阴谋破坏内蒙
    【新华社晋绥二十日电】确息：为了摧残内蒙人民自治运动，蒋匪政府已得到美国间谍机关的合作，在绥远包头建立专门组织，以从事对于内蒙古人民自治政府的破坏计划。在破坏计划中，包括放毒的工作，即将有毒的粮食供给内蒙古人民，并以内蒙古的牲畜作媒介散布传染病。美国间谍加立文及其所任用的德国暗杀和放毒专家索得朋兄弟二人，现正主持该项阴谋工作。
