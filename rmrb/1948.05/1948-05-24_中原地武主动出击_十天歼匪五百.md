Title: Central Plains Armed Forces Take the Initiative: 500 Bandits Eliminated in Ten Days
Author:
Date: 1948-05-24
Page: 2

　　Central Plains Armed Forces Take the Initiative
  　Ten Days of Eliminating 500 Bandits
    [Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains Front, 22nd] (1) The local armed forces of the Hubei-Henan Military Region cleared out bandits, and from the 1st to the 8th, wiped out one squadron of the Luoshan Security Regiment and three township offices, killing and capturing over 120 bandits. (2) The local armed forces of the Henan-Anhui-Jiangsu Military Region actively launched attacks to protect the wheat harvest. From the 1st to the 10th, they fought seven battles, recovering Shangcai City and five enemy strongholds, killing and wounding over 390 bandits including Jia Mingfang, the squadron leader of the Kuomintang security forces. They captured Yu Luzhong, the Fuyang supervisor of the 14th Pacification Zone, and Wu Hongxiu, the squadron leader, along with over 172 others.
    In these two battles, a total of one 60mm mortar, eleven light machine guns, over 170 rifles and carbines, and over 15,000 rounds of ammunition were seized.

### 中原地武主动出击　十天歼匪五百

1948-05-24
第2版()
专栏：

　　中原地武主动出击
  　十天歼匪五百
    【新华社中原前线二十二日电】（一）鄂豫军区地方武装清剿土匪，一至八日歼罗山保安团一个中队及乡公所三个，毙俘匪一百二十余名。（二）豫皖苏军区地方武装积极出击，保卫麦收，一日至十日内共作战七次，收复上蔡城及敌据点五处，毙伤土蒋匪保安中队长贾名芳以下三百九十余名，生俘十四绥区阜阳监督官郁碌中，中队长吴洪秀以下一百七十二名。
    以上两役共缴获六○炮一门、轻机枪十一挺、步马枪一百七十余支、子弹一万五千余发。
