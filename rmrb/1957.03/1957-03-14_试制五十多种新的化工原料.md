Trial Production of More Than Fifty New Chemical Raw Materials
The Shanghai Chemical Industry Department is actively engaged in trial production and increased production of various chemical raw materials that previously needed to be imported. From the fourth quarter of last year to the end of February this year, the factories affiliated with the Shanghai Chemical Raw Materials Industrial Company have successively trial-produced more than fifty new chemical raw materials. In addition, the production of more than ten chemical raw materials that are currently in tight supply in the market is being increased.

### 试制五十多种新的化工原料

1957-03-14
第5版()
专栏：

    试制五十多种新的化工原料
    上海市化学工业部门积极试制和增产各种原来需要进口的化工原料。从去年第四季度起到今年2月底止，上海市化工原料工业公司所属各厂，已经陆续试制了五十多种新的化工原料。另有十多种当前市场供应紧张的化工原料，都在增加生产。
