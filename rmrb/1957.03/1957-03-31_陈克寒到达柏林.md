Title: Chen Kehan Arrives in Berlin
Author:
Date: 1957-03-31
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 30th. The Socialist Countries Publishing Conference is scheduled to open in Leipzig on April 7th. Chen Kehan, head of the Chinese delegation and Vice Minister of the Chinese Ministry of Culture, who is preparing to attend the conference, arrived in Berlin by plane on the afternoon of March 29th.

### 陈克寒到达柏林

1957-03-31
第5版()
专栏：

    陈克寒到达柏林
    新华社柏林30日电　社会主义国家出版业会议订于4月7日在来比锡开幕。准备参加会议的中国代表团团长、中国文化部副部长陈克寒已经在3月29日下午乘飞机到达柏林。
