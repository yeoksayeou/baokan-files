Title: People of Jakarta Demand Expulsion of United Press International Reporter
Author:
Date: 1957-03-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 3rd: The chairman of the Action Committee for Supporting Sukarno's Plan from all circles in Jakarta, Sujarto Rebowo, published a statement in the newspaper on the 2nd, demanding the expulsion of the United Press International reporter. He pointed out that the UPI reporter's February 28th report on the situation in Jakarta was "completely untrue, completely provocative, and harbored the purpose of subversive activities."

The aforementioned UPI dispatch described the situation in Jakarta as "full of danger" and "the situation is serious"; and portrayed the people's welcome of the President's plan as "activities of communist terrorists."

### 雅加达人民要求驱逐合众社记者

1957-03-04
第6版()
专栏：

    雅加达人民要求驱逐合众社记者
    据新华社雅加达3日电  雅加达各界人民支持苏加诺方案行动委员会主席苏哈托·雷博2日在报上发表谈话，要求把合众社记者驱逐出境。他指出，合众社记者2月28日关于雅加达情况的报道“完全不确实，完全是挑拨性的，并且包藏着颠复活动的目的”。
    上述合众社电讯曾经把雅加达的情况说成“充满了危险”，“情势严重”；并且把人民对总统方案的欢迎描写成“共产党恐怖分子的活动”。
