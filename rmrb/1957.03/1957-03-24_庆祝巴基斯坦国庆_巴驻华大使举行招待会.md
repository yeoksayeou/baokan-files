Title: Pakistan's Ambassador to China Holds Reception to Celebrate Pakistan's National Day
Author:
Date: 1957-03-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd. The Pakistani Ambassador to China, Ahmed, held a grand reception tonight to celebrate the National Day of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan.

Among those attending the reception were Vice Chairpersons Song Qingling, Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, and Peng Zhen; Vice Premier Deng Zihui; Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the CPPCC, Zhang Bojun and Bao Erhan; Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council, Cheng Qian, Zhang Zhizhong, and Fu Zuoyi; Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu; people from all walks of life; and diplomatic envoys from various countries to China, totaling more than 600 people.

At the beginning of the reception, Ambassador Ahmed invited his Chinese and foreign guests to toast the friendship between Pakistan and China, the happiness of the Chinese people, and the good health of Chairman Mao Zedong.

Following this, Vice Chairperson Song Qingling raised her glass to congratulate the prosperity and strength of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan and to wish President Mirza good health.

The reception lasted for two hours.

### 庆祝巴基斯坦国庆　巴驻华大使举行招待会

1957-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝巴基斯坦国庆
　　巴驻华大使举行招待会
    新华社23日讯　巴基斯坦驻华大使阿哈默德今晚举行盛大招待会，庆祝巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国国庆。
    出席招待会的，有副委员长宋庆龄、李济深、郭沫若、彭真，副总理邓子恢，政协全国委员会副主席章伯钧、包尔汉，国防委员会副主席程潜、张治中、傅作义，外交部副部长章汉夫和各方面人士，以及各国驻中国的使节等共六百多人。
    招待会开始的时候，阿哈默德大使请他的中外客人为巴中两国友谊，为中国人民的幸福，为毛泽东主席的身体健康干杯。
    接着，宋庆龄副委员长举杯祝贺巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国的繁荣富强，祝贺米尔扎总统的身体健康。
    招待会共举行了两个小时。
