Title: Japan to Hold "Month for Restoring Japan-China Diplomatic Relations" in May
Author:
Date: 1957-03-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, March 1st: The Standing Council of the Japan-China Friendship Association decided at a meeting held on February 28th to designate May of this year as "Month for Restoring Japan-China Diplomatic Relations." During this month, activities promoting cultural and economic exchange between Japan and China will be carried out throughout Japan, and signatures will be collected demanding the early restoration of Japan-China diplomatic relations.

The meeting also decided that, with the National Conference for the Restoration of Japan-China and Japan-Soviet Diplomatic Relations as the center, a nationwide organization should be formed comprising all groups and individuals who hope for the early restoration of diplomatic relations between Japan and China to promote the restoration of Japan-China diplomatic relations.

### 日本将在五月举行“恢复日中邦交月”

1957-03-02
第5版()
专栏：

    日本将在五月举行“恢复日中邦交月”
    新华社1日讯  东京消息：日中友好协会常任理事会在2月28日举行的会议决定，把今年5月定为“恢复日中邦交月”。在这一个月中，日本全国各地将进行促进日中文化、经济交流等运动，并且征集要求早日恢复日中邦交的签名。
    会议还决定，要以恢复日中、日苏邦交国民会议为中心，组成包括所有希望日中两国早日恢复邦交的团体和个人的促进恢复日中邦交的全国性组织。
