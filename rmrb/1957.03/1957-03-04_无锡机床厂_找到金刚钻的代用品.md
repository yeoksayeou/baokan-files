Title: Wuxi Machine Tool Plant Finds Substitute for Diamond Drills
Author:
Date: 1957-03-04
Page: 2

    Wuxi Machine Tool Plant
    Finds Substitute for Diamond Drills
    (Our dispatch) The Wuxi Machine Tool Plant has found a substitute for diamond drills, which can save more than 1,600 yuan annually and ensure the normal progress of production.
    After a certain period of grinding, the grinding wheel surfaces of the plant's two external grinders become uneven and need to be smoothed with diamond drills. However, diamond drills are expensive and difficult to buy. Comrade Ilyin, a Soviet expert working in the plant, suggested modifying broken grinding wheel pieces to replace diamond drills and personally guided the experiment, making this work a success.

### 无锡机床厂  找到金刚钻的代用品

1957-03-04
第2版()
专栏：

    无锡机床厂
    找到金刚钻的代用品
    本报讯  无锡机床厂找到了金刚钻的代用品，全年可以节约一千六百多元，而且保证了生产的正常进行。
    这个厂的两台外元磨在经过一定时期的磨削后，砂轮表面就高低不平，需要用金刚钻将它磨平。但是，金刚钻又贵又难买。在这个厂帮助工作的苏联专家伊林同志建议改制坏砂轮片来代替金刚钻，并亲自指导试验，使这一工作获得成功。
