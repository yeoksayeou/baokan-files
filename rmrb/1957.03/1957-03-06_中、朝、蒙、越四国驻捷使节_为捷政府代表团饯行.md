Title: Envoys to Czechoslovakia from China, North Korea, Mongolia, and Vietnam Host Farewell Banquet for Czechoslovak Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-03-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 5th – Cao Ying, the Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, Yang Yong-seon, the North Korean Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, Rabdan, the Mongolian Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, and Nguyen Tri Huu, the Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Embassy in Czechoslovakia, jointly held a reception at the Chinese Embassy on the evening of the 4th to bid farewell to the Czechoslovak government delegation headed by Prime Minister Široký, which is about to visit these four countries.

Prime Minister Široký and his wife, as well as other members of the delegation, attended the reception.

Also attending the reception were Czechoslovak Communist Party and government leaders Novotný, Fierlinger, Ďuriš, Kopecký, Barak, representatives from the National Front, mass organizations, and various social circles, as well as diplomatic envoys from many countries, totaling over four hundred people.

### 中、朝、蒙、越四国驻捷使节  为捷政府代表团饯行

1957-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

    中、朝、蒙、越四国驻捷使节
    为捷政府代表团饯行
    新华社布拉格5日电　中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使曹瑛、朝鲜驻捷大使杨永笋、蒙古驻捷大使拉布丹、越南驻捷大使馆临时代办阮知友，4日晚间在中国大使馆联合举行招待会，为即将到这四个国家访问的以西罗基总理为首的捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团饯行。
    西罗基总理和他的夫人以及代表团其他成员都出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的还有捷克斯洛伐克共产党和政府领导人诺沃提尼、费林格、杜兰斯基、柯别茨基、巴拉克，民族阵线、群众团体和社会各界的代表，许多国家的外交使节等共约四百余人。
