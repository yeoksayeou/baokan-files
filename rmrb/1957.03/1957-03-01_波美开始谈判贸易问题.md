Title: Polish-U.S. Trade Negotiations Begin
Author:
Date: 1957-03-01
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: A Polish trade delegation began negotiations with U.S. government representatives in Washington on the 26th to discuss expanding Polish-U.S. trade relations.
    When the head of the Polish delegation, Kotlitsky, arrived in the United States on the 21st, he told reporters: "We are very interested in purchasing cotton, grain, oilseeds, machinery, and especially mining machinery, on credit terms."

### 波美开始谈判贸易问题

1957-03-01
第5版()
专栏：

    波美开始谈判贸易问题
    据新华社讯  波兰贸易代表团26日在华盛顿同美国政府代表开始举行谈判，讨论扩大波美贸易关系的问题。
    波兰代表团团长科特利次基21日到达美国的时候曾经向记者说：“我们对于按照信贷条件购买棉花、谷物、油料种籽、机器、特别是采矿机器，很感兴趣”。
