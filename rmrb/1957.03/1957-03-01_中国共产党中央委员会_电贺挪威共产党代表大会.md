Title: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Sends a Congratulatory Message to the Congress of the Communist Party of Norway
Author:
Date: 1957-03-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 1st. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China today sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Norway, congratulating them on the convocation of the Ninth Congress of the Communist Party of Norway. The telegram reads as follows:

To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Norway, and to all the representatives of the Ninth Congress of the Communist Party of Norway, comrades:

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, on behalf of all its members and the entire Chinese people, extends warm and fraternal congratulations to the Ninth Congress of the Communist Party of Norway.

We congratulate your Ninth Congress for making new contributions to consolidating the unity of the Party, consolidating the unity of the Norwegian working class and all patriotic and democratic forces, and consolidating the internationalist unity of the working classes of all countries; we congratulate you on achieving new successes in the struggle to defend European and world peace, and in the struggle to safeguard the interests of the working people and strive for social progress.

                  The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
                    March 1, 1957

### 中国共产党中央委员会  电贺挪威共产党代表大会

1957-03-01
第1版()
专栏：

    中国共产党中央委员会
    电贺挪威共产党代表大会
    新华社1日讯  中国共产党中央委员会今天打电报给挪威共产党中央委员会，祝贺挪威共产党第九次代表大会的召开。电文如下：挪威共产党中央委员会转挪威共产党第九次代表大会全体代表同志们：
    中国共产党中央委员会谨代表全体党员和全中国人民向挪威共产党第九次代表大会致以热烈的兄弟般的祝贺。
    我们祝贺你们的第九次代表大会，对于巩固党的团结，巩固挪威工人阶级和一切爱国民主力量的团结，巩固各国工人阶级的国际主义的团结，作出新的贡献；祝贺你们在保卫欧洲和世界和平的斗争中，在维护劳动人民利益和争取社会进步的斗争中，获得新的成就。
                  中国共产党中央委员会
                    1957年3月1日
