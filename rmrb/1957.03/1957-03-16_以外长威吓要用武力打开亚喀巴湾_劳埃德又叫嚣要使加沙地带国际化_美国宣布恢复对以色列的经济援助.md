Title: Israeli Foreign Minister Threatens to Use Force to Open the Gulf of Aqaba, Lloyd Again Clamors for Internationalization of the Gaza Strip, United States Announces Resumption of Economic Aid to Israel
Author:
Date: 1957-03-16
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Jerusalem, Israeli Sector, March 15th: Israeli Foreign Minister Golda Meir threatened on the evening of the 14th that if Egypt re-closed the Gulf of Aqaba, then "Israel will use warships to guarantee the navigation of Israeli ships."

Meir said when speaking at a meeting of the Central Committee of the main ruling party, the Israeli Labor Party, that the Israeli Navy would try to maintain the opening of the Straits of Tiran at the mouth of the Gulf of Aqaba.

Xinhua News Agency, London, March 15th: British Foreign Secretary Lloyd reiterated in the House of Commons debate on the Middle East issue on the 14th that the British government believes that the Gaza Strip, whether from a military or civil affairs point of view, must be placed under some form of international administration. He said: "No country has legal sovereignty over Gaza," and this area should be the responsibility of the United Nations.

Lloyd also reiterated that the Gulf of Aqaba is an "international waterway" and should allow "free navigation" in this waterway.

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, March 15th: US State Department spokesman White announced on the 14th that the United States will resume economic aid to Israel. The United States is planning to discuss this issue with the competent Israeli authorities.

He said that aid to Israel would be a resumption of the plans that were suspended after Israel's attack on Egypt, but it does not rule out the possibility of new aid to Israel. White said that according to the interrupted plan, the United States had promised to provide Israel with 20 million US dollars in economic aid, of which 15 million US dollars would be used to purchase surplus agricultural products, and 5 million US dollars would be used to import "saleable goods."

When reporters asked whether the United States would resume aid to Egypt and Jordan, White declined to answer.

### 以外长威吓要用武力打开亚喀巴湾  劳埃德又叫嚣要使加沙地带国际化  美国宣布恢复对以色列的经济援助

1957-03-16
第7版()
专栏：

    以外长威吓要用武力打开亚喀巴湾
    劳埃德又叫嚣要使加沙地带国际化
    美国宣布恢复对以色列的经济援助
    据新华社15日讯  耶路撒冷以色列区消息：以色列外交部长梅厄夫人14日晚上威胁说，如果埃及重新封锁亚喀巴湾，那末“以色列将要使用军舰来保障以色列船只的通航”。
    梅厄夫人在主要执政党以色列工党中央委员会会议上发言的时候说，以色列海军将要设法维持亚喀巴湾口蒂朗海峡的开放。
    据新华社15日讯  伦敦消息：英国外交大臣劳埃德14日在下院辩论中东问题的时候再次声明，英国政府认为，加沙地带不论从军事观点或民政观点来说，必须置于某种形式的国际管理之下。他说：“没有一个国家对于加沙有着法律上的主权”，应该把这个地带作为联合国的责任。
    劳埃德并且重申亚喀巴湾是一条“国际水道”，应该允许在这条水道“自由通航”。
    新华社15日讯  华盛顿消息：美国国务院发言人怀特14日宣布，美国将恢复对以色列的经济援助。美国正在打算就这个问题同以色列的主管当局商谈。
    他说，对以色列的援助将是恢复在以色列进攻埃及以后停顿下来的计划，但是也不排斥可能对以色列进行新的援助。怀特说，根据已经中断的计划，美国曾经答应给予以色列以二千万美元的经济援助，其中一千五百万美元用来购买剩余农产品，五百万美元用来进口“可出售的商品”。
    当新闻记者问及美国是否会恢复对埃及和约旦的援助时，怀特拒绝作答。
