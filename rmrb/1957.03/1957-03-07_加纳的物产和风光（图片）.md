Ghana's Products and Scenery

    Top Left: Ghana is an agricultural country. The main crop is cocoa, which people call green gold, and its output accounts for one-half of the world's total output. This is a Ghanaian farmer admiring plump cocoa pods.
    Top Right: The scenery of the Volta River in Ghana. The Volta River region produces bauxite.
    Bottom: Ghana is rich in gold. The name "Gold Coast" comes from its gold production. For centuries, colonialists plundered large amounts of gold from here. At present, gold ranks first in Ghana's mineral production value. These are students from the Tarkwa School of Mines visiting a gold mine.
                    (Xinhua News Agency Draft)

### 加纳的物产和风光（图片）

1957-03-07
第6版()
专栏：

    加纳的物产和风光
    左上　加纳是一个农业国。主要农作物是可可，人们称之为绿色金子，产量占世界总产量的二分之一。这是加纳农民正在欣赏肥硕的可可。
    右上　加纳伏尔达河的风景，伏尔达河地区出产铁矾土。
    下　加纳盛产黄金，“黄金海岸”的得名就是因产金而来。几个世纪以来，殖民者从这里掠夺了大量黄金。目前加纳的矿产中，黄金占产值的第一位。这是塔尔克瓦金矿学院的学生在参观金矿。
                    （新华社稿）
