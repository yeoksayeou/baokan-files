Title: After Returning from India, the Panchen Erdini First Meets the Dalai Lama in Xigaze; Panchen Erdini Hosts a Banquet for the Dalai Lama
Author:
Date: 1957-03-11
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Xigaze, 9th – The Dalai Lama arrived in Xigaze from Gyangzê at noon on the 4th and met with the Panchen Erdini at Tashilhunpo Monastery in Xigaze on the 8th. This is the first meeting between the two Tibetan regional leaders since their return from India.
    At 11:30 AM on the 8th, amidst the welcoming procession of lamas from Tashilhunpo Monastery and the sound of Buddhist music, the Dalai Lama and his main accompanying officials arrived at the entrance of the "Pu Guan Palace" in Tashilhunpo Monastery, where the Panchen Erdini and the main officials of the Panchen Kanpo Lhentsok Committee greeted them. The Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdini exchanged hadas and performed the traditional forehead-touching ceremony. The lamas of Tashilhunpo Monastery also held a grand religious welcoming ceremony for the Dalai Lama.
    Xinhua News Agency, Xigaze, 10th – The Panchen Erdini hosted a banquet at Tashilhunpo Monastery on the evening of the 9th to entertain the Dalai Lama and his accompanying officials.
    During the banquet, the Panchen Erdini first expressed his gratitude to the Dalai Lama for personally attending the banquet. He raised his glass and said: Let us drink to the unity of the Tibetan and Han nationalities and the internal unity of the Tibetan people, to the future development of various undertakings in Tibet and the happiness of the Tibetan people, and to the health of Chairman Mao.
    The Dalai Lama then spoke, thanking the host for the hospitality. In his speech, he expressed his hope that everyone would work together to build a new Tibet. The Dalai Lama compared the path of development in Tibet to the life of the Tibetan sage Milarepa. He said: Although Milarepa's life was initially filled with many difficulties, he finally achieved complete success. Finally, the Dalai Lama raised his glass to wish everyone good health.

### 访印归来后在日喀则首次会面  班禅额尔德尼欢宴达赖喇嘛

1957-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

    访印归来后在日喀则首次会面
    班禅额尔德尼欢宴达赖喇嘛
    据新华社日喀则9日电　达赖喇嘛4日中午由江孜到达日喀则，8日在日喀则扎什伦布寺会见了班禅额尔德尼。这是两位西藏地方的领袖从印度回来后的第一次会面。
    8日上午十一时半，达赖喇嘛和他的主要随行官员在扎什伦布寺喇嘛们的夹道欢迎和佛乐声中，来到扎什伦布寺的“普观宫”门口，班禅额尔德尼和班禅堪布会议厅委员会的主要官员在这里迎接。达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼互赠哈达，并且按照传统的礼节行了碰头礼。扎什伦布寺的喇嘛们还为达赖喇嘛举行了隆重的宗教欢迎仪式。
    新华社日喀则10日电　班禅额尔德尼9日傍晚在扎什伦布寺设宴招待达赖喇嘛和随行官员。
    席间，班禅额尔德尼首先对达赖喇嘛亲自出席这次宴会表示感谢。他举杯说：为了藏、汉民族的团结和西藏民族内部的团结，为了西藏今后各项事业的发展和西藏人民的幸福，为了毛主席的健康而干杯。
    达赖喇嘛接着讲话感谢主人的盛情招待。他在讲话中希望大家共同努力建设新西藏。达赖喇嘛把西藏的发展道路比成西藏圣者米拉日巴的一生。他说：虽然米拉日巴的一生在开头是经过了许多困难，但最后终于得到了圆满的成果。最后达赖喇嘛举杯祝大家身体健康。
