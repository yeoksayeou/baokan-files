Title: Hungary Arrests Another Group of Armed Bandits
Author:
Date: 1957-03-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 9th -- Hungarian public security organs arrested a group of bandits in Szolnok County on the 5th, who were preparing for an armed rebellion on March 15th. These bandits were directly commanded by a counter-revolutionary organization lurking in Budapest. This counter-revolutionary group hid a large number of weapons in various places and also drew up a plan for an armed rebellion.

### 匈又逮捕一批武装匪徒

1957-03-11
第5版()
专栏：

    匈又逮捕一批武装匪徒
    据新华社布达佩斯9日电　匈牙利公安机关5日在索尔诺克州逮捕了一批准备在3月15日武装暴动的匪徒。这些匪徒是直接受潜伏在布达佩斯的反革命组织指挥的。这个反革命集团在各地隐藏了大批武器，并且还拟订了武装暴动的计划。
