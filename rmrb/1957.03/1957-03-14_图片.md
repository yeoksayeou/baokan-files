Author: Lu Yuxiang
Date: 1957-03-14
Page: 5

    The winter wheat of the Huan Cheng Eighth Agricultural Cooperative in Suining County, Jiangsu Province, has turned yellow due to the drought. In order to promote the turning of the wheat seedlings to green, the cooperative organized a youth shock brigade to apply fertilizer (human manure) to the wheat seedlings. Here are the young female team members applying fertilizer. (Draft from Suining News)
