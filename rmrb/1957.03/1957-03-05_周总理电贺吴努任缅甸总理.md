Title: Premier Zhou Cables Congratulations to U Nu on Assuming Office as Prime Minister of Burma
Author: 
Date: 1957-03-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram today to U Nu, congratulating him on his assumption of the office of Prime Minister of the Union of Burma. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To His Excellency U Nu, Prime Minister of the Union of Burma:

I am pleased to learn of Your Excellency's assumption of the office of Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, and I would like to extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and in my own name.

The friendly and cooperative relations between China and Burma have achieved good development in recent years on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. I firmly believe that this friendly and cooperative relationship will be further strengthened with Your Excellency's re-assumption of the post of Prime Minister, and I believe that Your Excellency will make even greater contributions to the cause of maintaining peace in Asia and the world with your outstanding talents.

　　Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai
　　March 4, 1957

### 周总理电贺吴努任缅甸总理

1957-03-05
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电贺吴努任缅甸总理
    新华社4日讯  中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天打电报给吴努，祝贺他荣任缅甸联邦政府总理。电文如下：缅甸联邦政府总理吴努阁下：
    欣悉阁下荣任缅甸联邦政府总理，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府并且以我个人名义向阁下致以热烈的祝贺。
    中缅两国的友好合作关系，近几年来在和平共处五项原则基础上已经获得了良好的发展。我深信这种友好合作的关系，将会随阁下再度荣任总理而得到进一步的加强，并且相信，阁下以卓越的才能对维护亚洲和世界和平的事业将会作出更大的贡献。
      中华人民共和国国务院总理  周恩来  1957年3月4日
