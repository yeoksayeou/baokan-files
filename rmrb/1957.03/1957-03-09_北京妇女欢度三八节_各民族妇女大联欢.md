Title: Beijing Women Celebrate International Women's Day; Grand Gathering of Women of All Nationalities

Author:
Date: 1957-03-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 8th: The All-China Democratic Women's Federation and the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation held a grand gathering tonight at the Beijing Hotel with more than 4,000 people to celebrate International Women's Day, "March 8th."

Among those attending the evening's event were Vice Premier of the State Council Bo Yibo, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Wang Jiaxiang and Zhang Hanfu, Second Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party Liu Ren, Vice Mayor of Beijing Zhang Youyu, Vice Chairpersons of the All-China Women's Federation Li Dequan, Xu Guangping, and Zhang Yun, and Chairperson of the Beijing Women's Federation Zhang Xiaomei.

Heads of diplomatic missions and their wives, diplomats and their wives, female diplomats, and foreign experts in Beijing and their wives also attended the evening's event.

Xinhua News Agency, March 8th: More than 1,700 women of over forty nationalities in the capital held a get-together in the Beijing Hotel hall on the evening of the 7th to celebrate International Women's Day, "March 8th."

The hall was filled with laughter. The women wore their own national costumes. Here were advanced producers, deputies to the National People's Congress and the Beijing Municipal People's Congress, members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, as well as agricultural cooperative directors, teachers, and students.

After a speech by Xu Guangping, Vice Chairperson of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the evening began. A Tatar woman, Meizhalade, with white egret feathers in her hair, strolled into the hall to the music, her soft arms undulating like gentle waves. Uyghur dancer Kambarhan, stepping to the melody of "I Love the Motherland," danced around the hall. Manchu artist Guan Xuezhen performed qinshu to the melodious sound of the zither.

As the singing and dancing were in full swing, Premier Zhou Enlai arrived at the hall. Cheers and applause immediately erupted in the hall. Premier Zhou congratulated the women of all nationalities on the festival with a smile on his face. He encouraged everyone to continue to move forward, to build a socialist society, and to build an even more moral society—a communist society.

### 北京妇女欢度三八节  各民族妇女大联欢

1957-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

    北京妇女欢度三八节
    各民族妇女大联欢
    据新华社8日讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会和北京市民主妇女联合会今晚在北京饭店举行四千多人的盛大联欢会，庆祝“三八”国际妇女节。
    参加今天晚会的有国务院副总理薄一波，外交部副部长王稼祥、章汉夫，中共北京市委第二书记刘仁，北京市副市长张友渔，全国妇联副主席李德全、许广平、章蕴，北京市妇联主席张晓梅等。
    各国驻华使节和夫人、外交官和夫人，女外交官，在京各国男女专家和专家的夫人也参加了晚会。
    新华社8日讯　在首都的四十多个民族的妇女一千七百多人，7日晚在北京饭店大厅里举行联欢会，庆祝“三八”国际妇女节。
    大厅里到处是欢笑声。妇女们穿着自己的民族服装。这里有先进生产者，全国和北京市的人民代表大会代表，政协全国委员会委员，还有农业社主任、教师和学生。
    中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平致词后，晚会开始。头插白翎毛的塔塔尔族女郎美扎拉德，在乐声中漫步舞入大厅，轻柔的手臂如软浪起伏。维吾尔族舞蹈家康巴尔汉踏着“我爱祖国”乐曲的旋律，环舞全场。满族女艺人关雪贞在琴声琤琮中说了琴书。
    歌舞正酣，周恩来总理来到了大厅。大厅里立刻掀起了欢呼声和掌声。周总理笑容满面地向各民族妇女们祝贺节日。他勉励大家继续前进，建成社会主义社会，建成一个更有道德的社会——共产主义社会。
