Title: French Communist Party Delegation Holds Talks with Yugoslav Communists
Author:
Date: 1957-03-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 17th: According to the Yugoslav News Agency, the Central Committee of the French Communist Party, at the invitation of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, has sent a delegation to Belgrade to hold talks with representatives of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia. The delegation is led by F. Piette, a member of the Political Bureau of the French Communist Party.

Piette told reporters from the Yugoslav News Agency that during the talks, representatives from both parties will exchange opinions on all issues related to the current international workers' movement. He said that the French Communists are pleased with the resumption of contact between the two parties.

### 法共派代表团同南共会谈

1957-03-19
第6版()
专栏：

    法共派代表团同南共会谈
    新华社18日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社巴黎17日讯：法国共产党中央委员会应南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员会的邀请，已经派出一个代表团到贝尔格莱德去同南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟的代表举行会谈。代表团由法国共产党政治局委员弗·皮佑率领。
    皮佑对南斯拉夫通讯社记者发表谈话说，在这次会谈中，两党代表将就一切同当前国际工人运动有关的问题普遍交换意见。他说，法国共产党人对两国共产党恢复接触感到满意。
