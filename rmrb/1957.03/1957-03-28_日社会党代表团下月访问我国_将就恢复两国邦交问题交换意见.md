Title: Japanese Socialist Party Delegation to Visit China Next Month to Exchange Views on Restoring Diplomatic Relations Between the Two Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-03-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, March 27th: The Japanese Socialist Party, at its Central Executive Committee meeting held today, officially decided to send a delegation led by Secretary-General Inejiro Asanuma to visit China.

The Socialist Party delegation is scheduled to depart around April 10th. During its visit to China, the delegation, in its capacity as the official representative of the Socialist Party, will exchange views with Chinese leaders on the issue of restoring diplomatic relations between Japan and China.

The Socialist Party delegation will consist of ten members:

Leader: Inejiro Asanuma

Members: Seiichi Katsumada (Chairman of the Special Committee for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations), Tadataka Sata (Director of the International Bureau), Masu Zomei (Director of the Planning Bureau), Tomomi Narita (Director of the Secretariat of the Policy Deliberation Council), Hideo Yamahana (Vice Chairman of the Special Committee for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations), Shichiro Hozumi (Minister of Foreign Affairs), Ryosaku Sasaki (Director of the Secretariat of the Special Committee for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations).

Attendants: Yuji Yoneyama (Member of the Secretariat of the Policy Deliberation Council), Takuya Sato (Member of the International Bureau).

### 日社会党代表团下月访问我国　将就恢复两国邦交问题交换意见

1957-03-28
第6版()
专栏：

　　日社会党代表团下月访问我国
　　将就恢复两国邦交问题交换意见
    新华社27日讯　东京消息：日本社会党在今天举行的中央执行委员会会议上，正式决定派遣由书记长浅沼稻次郎率领的代表团到中国访问。
    社会党的代表团预定在4月10日前后启程。在中国进行访问期间，代表团将以社会党的正式代表的资格同中国的领导人就恢复日、中两国外交关系问题交换意见。
    社会党的代表团将由十人组成：
    团长：浅沼稻次郎
    团员：胜间田清一（恢复日中邦交特别委员会委员长）、佐多忠隆（国际局长）、曾弥益（企划局长）、成田知已（政策审议会事务局长）、山花秀雄（恢复日中邦交特别委员会副委员长）穗积七郎（外交部长）、佐佐木良作（恢复日中邦交特别委员会事务局长）
    随员：米山雄治（政策审议会事务局员）、佐藤拓弥（国际局员）
