　　High Yield Record of Jute

　　The Shuisheshe Agricultural Cooperative in Dongguan County, which was awarded the Ministry of Agriculture’s bumper harvest award in 1955 and was named a first-class labor model unit in Guangdong Province, set another high yield record for jute last year. Jute production increased by 18% compared to the previous year. The high-yield land yielded more than 1,207 jin per mu. Currently, the cooperative's jute is on display at the National Agricultural Exhibition.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Zhang Luo (Manuscript from Southern Daily)
　　Left picture: Members of the Shuisheshe Agricultural Cooperative in Dongguan County pulling jute in the jute field.
　　Top picture: Processed refined jute.
　　Right picture: The cooperative has established a refined jute processing group, and members are soaking jute.

### 黄麻高产纪录（图片）
张洛摄
1957-03-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　黄麻高产纪录
    获得农业部1955年丰产奖励和被评为广东省一等劳动模范单位的东莞县水蛇涌农业社，去年又创黄麻高产纪录。黄麻产量比前年提高18％。丰产地每亩收成一千二百零七斤多。现在，这个社的黄麻正在全国农业展览会上展出。
                                张洛摄（南方日报社稿）
