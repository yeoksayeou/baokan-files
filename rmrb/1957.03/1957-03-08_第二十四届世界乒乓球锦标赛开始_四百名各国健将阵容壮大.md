Title: The 24th World Table Tennis Championships Begin; Four Hundred Athletes from Various Countries Form a Strong Lineup
Author:
Date: 1957-03-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, 7th. The 24th World Table Tennis Championships began on the 7th at the "Royal Arena" in Stockholm, the capital of Sweden. Thirty-six teams, comprising nearly four hundred famous table tennis players from various countries, are participating in the competition. This is the largest World Table Tennis Championship to date.

The competition will run from March 7th to March 15th. The first four days are for the men's and women's team events. There will be a rest day on the 11th, and the men's and women's singles, doubles, and mixed doubles matches will begin on the 12th.

Thirty-three teams are participating in the "Swaythling Cup" - Men's Team event, and they are divided into four groups to compete in a round-robin format within their respective groups.

Twenty-six teams are participating in the "Corbillon Cup" - Women's Team event, divided into three groups for competition.

The Chinese men's team was drawn into Group 1. Also in Group 1 are the seeded team Romania, as well as India, Portugal, Belgium, Switzerland, Scotland, and Luxembourg.

The Chinese women's team is in Group 3, along with England, the United States, Wales, the Netherlands, Belgium, Switzerland, Denmark, and Norway.

The German table tennis team participating in this competition is jointly composed of representatives from the German Democratic Republic and West Germany. The Soviet Union has sent observers to visit the competition for the first time.

### 第二十四届世界乒乓球锦标赛开始  四百名各国健将阵容壮大

1957-03-08
第6版()
专栏：

    第二十四届世界乒乓球锦标赛开始
    四百名各国健将阵容壮大
    据新华社7日讯　斯德哥尔摩消息：第二十四届世界乒乓球锦标赛7日在瑞典首都斯德哥尔摩的“皇家体育馆”开始。参加这次比赛的有三十六个队，包括将近四百名各国著名的乒乓球运动员。这是历届世界乒乓球赛以来规模最大的一次。
    比赛将从3月7日进行到3月15日。前四天是男子、女子团体赛。11日休息一天，12日起进行男、女单打、双打和男女混合双打的比赛。
    参加这次“斯韦特林杯”——男子团体赛的有三十三个队，他们分成四个组分别进行组内循环比赛。
    参加这次“考比伦杯”——女子团体赛的有二十六个队，分成三个组进行比赛。
    中国男子队抽签结果分在第一组。在第一组的还有种子队罗马尼亚队以及印度、葡萄牙、比利时、瑞士、苏格兰和卢森堡等七个队。
    中国女子队分在第三组，同组的是英格兰、美国、威尔士、荷兰、比利时、瑞士、丹麦和挪威等八个队。
    参加这次比赛的德国乒乓球队是由民主德国和西德的代表联合组成的。苏联第一次派遣了观察员前去参观比赛。
