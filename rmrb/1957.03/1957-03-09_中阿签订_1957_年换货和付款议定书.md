Title: China and Albania Sign 1957 Trade and Payment Agreement
Author:
Date: 1957-03-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 8th: The governments of the People's Republic of China and the People's Republic of Albania have conducted sincere and friendly consultations on trade and loan usage issues for 1957 and have reached an agreement. Today in Beijing, they signed the "Protocol on the Exchange of Goods and Payments between the Governments of China and Albania for 1957" and the "Protocol on the Use of Loans and Non-repayable Aid from the Government of the People's Republic of China by the Government of the People's Republic of Albania in 1957."

The representative of the Government of the People's Republic of China who signed the protocol was Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China; the representative of the Government of the People's Republic of Albania was Kiço Ngjela, Head of the Trade Delegation of the Government of the People's Republic of Albania and Minister of Trade.

### 中阿签订1957年换货和付款议定书

1957-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

    中阿签订1957年换货和付款议定书
    新华社8日讯　中华人民共和国政府和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府双方对1957年的贸易和贷款使用等问题进行了诚恳友好的洽商，现已达成了协议，今天在北京签订了“中阿两国政府关于1957年交换货物和付款的议定书”和“阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府1957年使用中华人民共和国政府的贷款和无偿援助款项的议定书”。
    在议定书上签字的中华人民共和国政府代表是中华人民共和国对外贸易部部长叶季壮；阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府代表是阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府贸易代表团团长、贸易部部长基·恩杰拉。
