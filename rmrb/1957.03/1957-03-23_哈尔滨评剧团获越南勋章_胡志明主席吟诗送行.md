Title: Harbin Pingju Opera Troupe Awarded Vietnamese Medal; President Ho Chi Minh Recites Poem for Farewell

Author:
Date: 1957-03-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 22nd — The Chinese Harbin Pingju Opera Troupe has been awarded the Labour Medal, First Class, of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

The Harbin Pingju Opera Troupe concluded its nearly two-month visit to Vietnam today and left Hanoi to return home.

At a farewell party held last night, President Ho Chi Minh presented the Labour Medal, First Class, and a banner embroidered with the words "Unity and Friendship" to the Harbin Pingju Opera Troupe.

At the time of parting, President Ho Chi Minh recited the lines from the famous Vietnamese epic poem "Kim Van Kieu," "This evening I see you off on your return, next year, this day, we shall meet again," as a parting message.

President Ho Chi Minh also instructed the Pingju Opera Troupe to convey his and the Vietnamese people's greetings to Chairman Mao Zedong and the Chinese people.

### 哈尔滨评剧团获越南勋章　胡志明主席吟诗送行

1957-03-23
第5版()
专栏：

　　哈尔滨评剧团获越南勋章
  　胡志明主席吟诗送行
    新华社河内22日电　中国哈尔滨评剧团获得了越南民主共和国一级劳动勋章。
    哈尔滨评剧团今天结束了在越南近两个月的访问，离开河内回国。
    在昨天晚上举行的送别晚会上，胡志明主席把一级劳动勋章和一面绣有“团结友谊”字样的锦旗赠给哈尔滨评剧团。
    胡志明主席在临别的时候，吟咏了越南著名的长诗“金云翘”中的诗句“今夕送君归去，明年今日后会有期”作为赠言。
    胡志明主席还嘱咐评剧团把他和越南人民的问候带给毛泽东主席和中国人民。
