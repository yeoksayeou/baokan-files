Title: Syria-Israel Border Conflict Occurs
Author:
Date: 1957-03-29
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, March 28th: A Syrian military spokesperson stated on March 27th that a conflict occurred on the Syrian-Israeli border last night, initiated by Israeli forces firing first.
　　The spokesperson said that fighting near the Syrian fortresses of Dardara and the Huleh Lake area continued from the night of March 26th into the morning of the following day.
　　The spokesperson stated that there were no casualties on the Syrian side.

### 叙以边境发生冲突

1957-03-29
第6版()
专栏：

　　叙以边境发生冲突
    新华社28日讯  大马士革消息：叙利亚军方发言人3月27日说，叙利亚以色列边境昨晚发生冲突，冲突是以色列军首先开火引起的。
    这位发言人说，达尔达拉和豪勒湖附近的两个叙利亚要塞战斗从3月26日晚继续到次日清晨。
    发言人说，叙利亚方面没有伤亡。
