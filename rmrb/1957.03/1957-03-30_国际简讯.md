　　International Briefs

　　Chinese Journalists Delegation Visits Soviet Union
    A Chinese journalists delegation headed by Xu Zhucheng, director of the Shanghai "Wenhui Bao" arrived in Moscow by plane on March 28. The delegation was invited to visit the Soviet Union by the Information Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Soviet Journalists Association.
    Bulgarian Trade Union Delegation Visits Hungary
    A Bulgarian trade union delegation arrived in Budapest on March 27 for a visit. The delegation brought gifts worth 800,000 leva (Bulgarian currency) to Hungarian workers, including fabrics, clothing, shoes, food, etc.
    Japanese Table Tennis Team Visits Hungary
    The Japanese men's and women's table tennis teams, invited to visit Hungary, played two friendly matches with Hungarian players on March 25 and 26. As a result of the matches, the Japanese women's team won 5-3, and the Japanese men's team won 5-2.
　　　　　　　　(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 国际简讯

1957-03-30
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

　　国际简讯
　  我新闻工作者代表团赴苏访问
    由上海“文汇报”社长徐铸成率领的中国新闻工作者访苏代表团3月28日乘飞机到达莫斯科。代表团是应苏联外交部新闻司和苏联新闻工作者协会的邀请到苏联访问的。
    保加利亚工会代表团访匈
    保加利亚工会代表团3月27日到达布达佩斯访问。代表团给匈牙利工人带来了价值八十万列弗（保币）的礼物，其中包括布匹、服装、鞋、食品等。
    日本乒乓球队在匈牙利访问
    应邀访问匈牙利的日本男女乒乓球队在3月25日和26日同匈牙利的选手进行了两场友谊比赛。比赛结果，日本女子队以五比三获胜，日本男子队以五比二获胜。
                  （据新华社讯）
