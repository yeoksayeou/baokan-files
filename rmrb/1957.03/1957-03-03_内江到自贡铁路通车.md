Title: Railway from Neijiang to Zigong Opens to Traffic
Author:
Date: 1957-03-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, 2nd. The Neijiang-Zigong section of the Neikun Railway began temporary operations on the 1st, handling passenger and freight transport. Currently, one mixed train and one freight train operate daily.

### 内江到自贡铁路通车

1957-03-03
第2版()
专栏：

    内江到自贡铁路通车
    新华社成都2日电  内昆铁路内江—自贡段，1日开办临时营业，办理客货运输。现在每日开行混合列车一对和货物列车一对。
