Title: Czechoslovak Government Delegation Returns to Beijing; Beijing to Hold Welcome Rally Today
Author:
Date: 1957-03-26
Page: 1

　　The Czechoslovak Government Delegation Returns to Beijing
　　Beijing to Hold Welcome Rally Today
　　Xinhua News Agency, March 25th. After visiting seven cities, including Xi'an, Chengdu, Nanning, Guangzhou, Hangzhou, Shanghai, and Nanjing, the Czechoslovak Government Delegation, led by Premier Viliam Široký, returned to Beijing this afternoon.

　　Vice Premier of the State Council Nie Rongzhen and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei were among those who welcomed the delegation at the airport. Personnel from the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, as well as the Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, Hoang Van Hoan, also went to the airport to welcome them.

　　Xinhua News Agency, March 25th. People from all walks of life in Beijing will hold a rally on the afternoon of the 26th to welcome the Government Delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic, headed by Premier Viliam Široký. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the rally live on the first program channel starting at 2:50 PM on the 26th.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, March 25th. Premier Viliam Široký and all members of the Government Delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic arrived in Nanjing by special plane at approximately 10:00 AM today. The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at the airport by more than 2,000 representatives from all walks of life in Jiangsu Province and Nanjing City.

　　After disembarking from the plane, Premier Široký and others went to the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum to pay their respects. They presented a wreath to the statue of Mr. Sun Yat-sen in the memorial hall, and then entered the tomb chamber, walked around the mausoleum, and paid their respects to Mr. Sun Yat-sen's reclining statue and coffin.

　　After paying their respects at the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum, Premier Široký and others laid a wreath at the Rain Flower Terrace Martyrs' Cemetery.

　　At noon today, Governor of Jiangsu Province Hui Yuyu and others accompanied Premier Široký for lunch.

### 捷政府代表团回到北京　北京今天将举行欢迎大会

1957-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　捷政府代表团回到北京
　　北京今天将举行欢迎大会
    新华社25日讯　威廉·西罗基总理和他所率领的捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团在访问了西安、成都、南宁、广州、杭州、上海、南京等七个城市以后，今天下午回到北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有国务院副总理聂荣臻和外交部副部长姬鹏飞等人。捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆人员，以及越南民主共和国驻华大使黄文欢也前往机场欢迎。
    新华社25日讯　北京市各界人民将在26日下午举行大会欢迎以威廉·西罗基总理为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团。中央人民广播电台将在26日下午十四时五十分开始在第一种节目时间里转播大会的实况。
    据新华社南京25日电　威廉·西罗基总理和他率领的捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团全体人员，今天上午十时许乘专机到达南京。贵宾们在机场上受到了江苏省和南京市各界代表两千多人的热烈欢迎。
    西罗基总理等下飞机以后，即到中山陵谒陵。他们在陵堂里向中山先生坐像献了花圈，然后进入墓室，绕陵寝一周，瞻仰了中山先生卧像和灵榇。
    西罗基总理等在中山陵谒陵以后，到雨花台烈士墓前献了花圈。
    今天中午，江苏省省长惠浴宇等陪同西罗基总理共进午餐。
