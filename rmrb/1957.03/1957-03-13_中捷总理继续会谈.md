Title: Chinese and Czechoslovak Premiers Continue Talks
Author:
Date: 1957-03-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 12th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China and Premier Viliam Široký of the Czechoslovak Republic held their second meeting on March 12th. Simultaneously, leaders of relevant departments of the Chinese government exchanged views with members of the government delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic on further strengthening cooperation between the two countries.

The talks will continue.

### 中捷总理继续会谈

1957-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中捷总理继续会谈
    新华社12日讯　中华人民共和国总理周恩来同捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理威廉·西罗基在3月12日举行了第二次会谈。同时，中国政府有关部门领导人同捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团团员就进一步加强两国合作问题交换了意见。
    会谈将继续进行。
