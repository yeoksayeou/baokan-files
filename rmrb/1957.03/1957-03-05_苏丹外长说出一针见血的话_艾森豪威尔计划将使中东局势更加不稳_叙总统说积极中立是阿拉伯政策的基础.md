Title: Sudanese Foreign Minister Speaks Incisively: Eisenhower Plan Will Further Destabilize the Middle East; Syrian President Says Positive Neutrality is the Foundation of Arab Policy
Author: 
Date: 1957-03-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, March 4th, Khartoum dispatch: Sudanese Foreign Minister Mahgoub told reporters on March 3rd that if Eisenhower's Middle East plan is accepted, it will destabilize the Middle East situation and further expand the Cold War.

He said upon returning here from the United Nations in New York that the purpose of the plan is not to help Middle Eastern countries defend against Western aggression.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 3rd: Syrian President Kuwatly told a "Pyramid Newspaper" reporter on March 2nd that the positive neutrality reiterated at the recent Arab four-nation summit in Cairo is "the soundest foundation of Arab policy, a policy that aims to spare Arab countries from the disasters of the current conflict between the two major camps."

Kuwatly added: "We are not communists, and we are not satellite states. We ourselves will fill the vacuum in our country, if there is such a vacuum. We will never allow anyone to set up bases on our territory or make it a sphere of influence."

Kuwatly said: "It would be a good thing if the major powers helped the Arab countries, not only for the principles of human justice and peace but also for the economic interests of all, and strengthened their ability to maintain this neutrality."

### 苏丹外长说出一针见血的话  艾森豪威尔计划将使中东局势更加不稳  叙总统说积极中立是阿拉伯政策的基础

1957-03-05
第6版()
专栏：

    苏丹外长说出一针见血的话
    艾森豪威尔计划将使中东局势更加不稳
    叙总统说积极中立是阿拉伯政策的基础
    新华社4日讯  喀土穆消息：苏丹外交部长马哈古卜3日对记者说，艾森豪威尔的中东计划如果被接受的话，它将会使中东的局势不稳定，使冷战更加扩大。
    他在从纽约联合国回到这里时说，这个计划的目的并不是为了帮助中东国家防御西方的侵略。
    据新华社开罗3日电  叙利亚总统库阿特利2日对“金字塔报”记者说，最近在开罗举行的阿拉伯四国首脑会议所重申的积极中立是“阿拉伯政策的最健全的基础，这个政策的目的是使阿拉伯国家免受目前两大阵营之间的冲突的灾祸”。
    库阿特利又说：“我们不是共产主义者，我们也不是卫星国。我们自己将填补我们国家的真空，如果有这种真空的话。我们决不允许任何人在我们的领土上设立基地，或者使它成为一种势力范围”。
    库阿特利说：“如果大国不仅为了人类的正义和和平的原则，而且为了全体的经济利益来帮助阿拉伯国家和加强它们维护这种中立的能力，那是一件好事”。
