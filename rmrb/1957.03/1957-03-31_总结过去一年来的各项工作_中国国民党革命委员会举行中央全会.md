Title: Summarizing the Work of the Past Year: The Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Holds Central Plenary Session
Author:
Date: 1957-03-31
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, March 30th—The Second Plenary Session of the Third Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang was held in Beijing from the 25th to the 30th. The meeting summarized the work of the past year since the Third Plenary Session.

　　A total of one hundred and forty central committee members and alternate central committee members attended the meeting. The meeting listened to Chairman Li Jishen's work report on the Standing Committee of the Third Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang. The members held full discussions in plenary sessions and group meetings on the domestic and international situation, the policy of "long-term coexistence and mutual supervision," the issue of striving for the peaceful liberation of Taiwan, the policy of "letting a hundred flowers bloom and a hundred schools of thought contend," and strengthening the political and ideological education of members of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, developing the organization, and other issues.

　　The meeting passed a resolution this afternoon. The resolution stated that the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang firmly supports China's peaceful foreign policy, strengthens the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, develops and enhances China's friendly cooperation with Asian and African countries, opposes the expansionist policy of U.S. imperialism, and remains vigilant at all times to thwart its aggressive conspiracies.

　　The resolution believes that China's socialist construction has made tremendous achievements, and the socialist revolution has achieved a decisive victory. The resolution stated that we firmly support all measures to expand socialist democracy and correctly handle contradictions among the people, implement the policy of "counteracting what is reactionary and correcting what is wrong," and continue to consolidate the people's democratic dictatorship.

　　The resolution pointed out that under the policy of "long-term coexistence and mutual supervision," it is necessary to correctly understand and implement this policy, faithfully accept the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, adhere to the political line of serving socialism, closely connect with the masses and reflect their opinions and demands, assist the government in correctly handling contradictions among the people, humbly accept criticism from the Communist Party, and actively offer suggestions and criticisms to the Communist Party. The resolution stated that we must also learn from the leading Chinese Communist Party, oppose subjectivism in thinking, bureaucracy in work, and sectarianism in organization, and encourage members to voluntarily participate in the Chinese Communist Party's rectification study.

　　The resolution believes that helping members carry out ideological transformation is a long-term political task of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, and we should focus on encouraging and helping members consciously and voluntarily study Marxist-Leninist theory. The resolution requires all members to distinguish between right and wrong, differentiate between ourselves and the enemy, and draw a clear ideological line between socialist democracy and bourgeois democracy.

　　Regarding the policy of "letting a hundred flowers bloom and a hundred schools of thought contend," the resolution stated that we should advocate thorough openness, free debate, adhere to the truth, patiently persuade, cultivate the habit of criticism and self-criticism, and achieve mutual assistance and common progress.

　　The resolution calls on all members and the masses they connect with to continue to work hard for the peaceful liberation of Taiwan, expose at any time the conspiracy of U.S. imperialism to occupy Taiwan for a long time, urge Kuomintang military and government personnel in Taiwan to awaken as soon as possible, appreciate Dr. Sun Yat-sen's spirit of patriotism and anti-imperialism, respond to the motherland's call of "one family of patriots" and "patriotism regardless of sequence," take the path of peaceful liberation of Taiwan, and complete the great cause of national reunification.

　　The resolution requires all members of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang and the masses they connect with to fully recognize the long-term and arduous nature of building socialism, actively participate in the movement to increase production and practice economy based on the principle of building the country through diligence and thrift, oppose extravagance and waste, carry forward the spirit of hard struggle, do a good job, and complete tasks.

　　The resolution also touched on the organizational work of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang and the work of contacting people in society.

### 总结过去一年来的各项工作　中国国民党革命委员会举行中央全会

1957-03-31
第4版()
专栏：

    总结过去一年来的各项工作
　　中国国民党革命委员会举行中央全会
    新华社30日讯  中国国民党革命委员会第三届中央委员会第二次全体会议，25日到30日在北京举行。会议总结了三全大会以后一年来的工作。
    参加会议的中央委员和候补中央委员共一百四十人。会议听取了李济深主席关于中国国民党革命委员会第三届中央委员会常务委员会工作报告。各位委员在大会和小组会上对国内外形势，“长期共存，互相监督”的方针，争取和平解放台湾问题，“百花齐放，百家争鸣”的方针和加强中国国民党革命委员会成员的政治思想教育、发展组织等问题进行了充分的讨论。
    会议在今天下午通过了一项决议。决议说，中国国民党革命委员会坚决拥护我国和平外交政策，加强以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结，发展和增进我国同亚非国家的友好合作，反对美国帝国主义的扩张政策，随时提高警惕，粉碎它的侵略阴谋。
    决议认为，我国的社会主义建设已经取得巨大的成就，社会主义革命已经取得决定性的胜利。决议说，我们坚决拥护扩大社会主义民主、正确处理人民内部矛盾的一切措施，贯彻“有反必肃、有错必纠”的方针，继续巩固人民民主专政。
    决议指出，在“长期共存，互相监督”的方针下，要正确认识和贯彻执行这一方针，忠诚接受中国共产党的领导，坚持为社会主义服务的政治路线，密切联系群众并反映他们的意见和要求，协助政府正确处理人民内部的矛盾，虚心接受共产党的批评、并向共产党积极提出建议和批评。决议说，我们还要向领导的中国共产党学习，反对思想上的主观主义、工作上的官僚主义、组织上的宗派主义，并且鼓励成员自愿参加中国共产党的整风学习。
    决议认为，帮助成员进行思想改造是中国国民党革命委员会长期的政治任务，应当着重鼓励和帮助成员自觉自愿学习马克思列宁主义理论。决议要求全体成员明辨是非，分清敌我，划清社会主义民主与资产阶级民主的思想界限。
    关于“百花齐放，百家争鸣”的方针，决议说，要提倡彻底敞开，自由辩论，坚持真理，耐心说服，养成批评与自我批评的习惯，达到互相帮助，共同进步。
    决议号召全体成员和所联系的群众，继续为争取和平解放台湾而努力，随时揭露美国帝国主义企图长期霸占台湾的阴谋，促使台湾国民党军政人员及早觉醒，体念孙中山先生爱国反帝的精神，响应祖国“爱国一家”、“爱国不分先后”的号召，走和平解放台湾的道路，完成祖国统一大业。
    决议要求中国国民党革命委员会全体成员和所联系的群众，充分认识建设社会主义的长期性和艰巨性，本着勤俭建国的方针，积极投入增产节约运动，反对铺张浪费，发扬艰苦奋斗的精神，做好工作，完成任务。
    决议中还谈到中国国民党革命委员会的组织工作和联系社会人士等工作。
