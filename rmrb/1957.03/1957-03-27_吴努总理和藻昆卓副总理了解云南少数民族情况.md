Title: Prime Minister U Nu and Deputy Prime Minister Sao Hkun Hkio Learn About the Situation of Ethnic Minorities in Yunnan

Author:

Date: 1957-03-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 26th: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu and Deputy Prime Minister Sao Hkun Hkio met with Wang Lianfang, Deputy Director of the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee's Ethnic Affairs Commission, this afternoon in a villa in Kunming. Prime Minister U Nu and Deputy Prime Minister Sao Hkun Hkio listened with great interest to Wang Lianfang's introduction to the situation of ethnic minorities in Yunnan Province. Wang Lianfang introduced to the distinguished guests the situation in Yunnan Province regarding the implementation of the policy of ethnic unity and equality, the completion of land reform in ethnic border areas through peaceful consultation, and the development of mutual aid and cooperation. Prime Minister U Nu praised Yunnan Province's achievements in consulting with public leaders and the masses of various ethnic groups on matters concerning ethnic border work.

### 吴努总理和藻昆卓副总理了解云南少数民族情况

1957-03-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　吴努总理和藻昆卓副总理了解云南少数民族情况
    据新华社昆明26日电　缅甸联邦吴努总理、藻昆卓副总理今天下午在昆明的一座别墅里会见云南省人民委员会民族事务委员会副主任王连芳。吴努总理和藻昆卓副总理以很大的兴趣听了王连芳所作的关于云南省少数民族情况的介绍。王连芳向贵宾介绍了云南省在执行民族团结、民族平等政策和边疆民族地区采用和平协商方法完成土地改革及开展互助合作问题的情况。吴努总理对于云南省在边疆民族工作中遇事和各民族公众领袖人物及群众商量，因而取得的成绩表示赞扬。
