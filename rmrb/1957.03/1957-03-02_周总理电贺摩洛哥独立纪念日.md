Title: Premier Zhou Sends Congratulatory Message on Morocco's Independence Day
Author:
Date: 1957-03-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 1st. Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, today sent a telegram to the Prime Minister of Morocco, Si Bekai, congratulating him on Morocco's Independence Day. The telegram reads as follows: To His Excellency Si Bekai, Prime Minister of Morocco:

    On the occasion of Morocco's Independence Day, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to the government and people of Morocco and to Your Excellency, on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China and in my own name. I hope that the existing friendly relations between our two countries will develop further.
    
                                        Zhou Enlai
                         Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                                            March 1, 1957, Beijing

### 周总理电贺摩洛哥独立纪念日

1957-03-02
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电贺摩洛哥独立纪念日
    新华社1日讯  中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天致电摩洛哥首相西·贝凯，祝贺摩洛哥独立纪念日。电文如下：摩洛哥首相西·贝凯阁下：
    值此摩洛哥独立纪念日之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和中国人民，并且以我个人名义向摩洛哥政府、摩洛哥人民和阁下表示热烈的祝贺。祝我们两国间已有的友好关系得到发展。
                中华人民共和国国务院总理  周恩来
                  1957年3月1日于北京
