Title: Hungary: Many Enterprises Launch Labor Emulation Drives to Welcome the 12th Anniversary of Liberation
Author:
Date: 1957-03-28
Page: 5

　　To Welcome the 12th Anniversary of Liberation

　　Hungary: Many Enterprises Launch Labor Emulation Drives
　　Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, March 27th: To welcome the 12th anniversary of Hungary's liberation on April 4th, some industrial enterprises in Hungary have launched the first socialist labor emulation drive since the October events.
    The competition first unfolded in the workshops of the largest industrial enterprise in Hungary, the Danube Pantaleon Steel United Enterprise. Metallurgical workers guarantee that in the near future, the production of products will reach the highest indicators before last year's events.
    Socialist competitions before the festival are also underway in the Chepel Machine Manufacturing United Enterprise and some large enterprises in the capital, Budapest.
    Hungarian miners, standing at the forefront of the production front, decided to continue to increase coal production to welcome the festival. News from Hungary's industrial sector said that the vast majority of coal industry trusts have recently completed their production plans for the first quarter of this year.

### 迎接解放十二周年　匈牙利许多企业展开劳动竞赛

1957-03-28
第5版()
专栏：

　　迎接解放十二周年
　　匈牙利许多企业展开劳动竞赛
    新华社布达佩斯27日电　为了迎接4月4日匈牙利解放十二周年，匈牙利一些工业企业展开了十月事件以来第一次社会主义劳动竞赛。
    竞赛首先在匈牙利最大的工业企业——多瑙潘泰莱钢铁联合企业的各个车间中展开。冶金工人保证在最近期间，在生产产品方面达到去年事件前的最高指标。
    节日前的社会主义竞赛，在切佩尔机器制造联合企业和首都布达佩斯的一些大型企业中也在进行着。
    站在生产战线最前列的匈牙利矿工们为了迎接节日，决定继续提高煤的产量。来自匈牙利工业部门的消息说，绝大多数的煤业托辣斯最近已经完成了今年第一季度的生产计划。
