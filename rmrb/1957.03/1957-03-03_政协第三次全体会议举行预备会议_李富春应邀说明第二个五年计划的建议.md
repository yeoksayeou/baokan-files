Title: CPPCC Third National Committee Holds Preparatory Meeting; Li Fuchun Invited to Explain the Proposal for the Second Five-Year Plan
Author:
Date: 1957-03-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 2nd. The Preparatory Meeting of the Third Session, Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference was held this afternoon. Vice Premier of the State Council and Director of the State Planning Commission, Li Fuchun, was invited to the meeting to explain the proposal for the Second Five-Year Plan for the Development of the National Economy.

### 政协第三次全体会议举行预备会议  李富春应邀说明第二个五年计划的建议

1957-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

    政协第三次全体会议举行预备会议
    李富春应邀说明第二个五年计划的建议
    新华社2日讯  中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第三次全体会议今天下午举行预备会议，国务院副总理、国家计划委员会主任李富春应邀在会上作了关于发展国民经济的第二个五年计划的建议的说明。
