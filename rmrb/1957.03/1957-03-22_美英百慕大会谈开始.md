Title: US-British Bermuda Talks Begin
Author:
Date: 1957-03-22
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Bermuda, March 21st: British Prime Minister Macmillan and US President Eisenhower arrived in Bermuda on the 20th and discussed the agenda for the formal talks that evening. They will begin formal talks today.

　　Lloyd accompanied Macmillan. Dulles arrived in Bermuda on the 20th from Washington.

### 美英百慕大会谈开始

1957-03-22
第7版()
专栏：

　  美英百慕大会谈开始
    据新华社21日讯　百慕大消息：英国首相麦克米伦和美国总统艾森豪威尔20日先后到达百慕大，并在当天晚上就正式会谈的议程进行了商谈。他们从今天开始正式会谈。
    劳埃德随同麦克米伦到达。杜勒斯20日从华盛顿赶到百慕大。
