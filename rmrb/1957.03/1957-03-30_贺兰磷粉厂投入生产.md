Title: Helan Phosphate Fertilizer Plant Goes Into Production
Author:
Date: 1957-03-30
Page: 2

　　Helan Phosphate Fertilizer Plant Goes Into Production
　　Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, 29th: The newly built local state-owned Helan Phosphate Fertilizer Plant, located at the eastern foot of the Helan Mountains in Gansu Province, officially went into production on the 28th.

    This factory can produce 10,000 tons of phosphate fertilizer annually, enough to supply 800,000 mu of paddy fields for use as fertilizer. Using this fertilizer can increase the unit area yield of crops by more than 10%.

    According to existing geological data and preliminary estimates by Soviet experts, the Suyukou phosphate mine area in the central part of the Helan Mountains, within a 28-square-kilometer area, contains approximately 3 million tons of phosphate ore.

### 贺兰磷粉厂投入生产

1957-03-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　贺兰磷粉厂投入生产
　　据新华社兰州29日电  在甘肃省贺兰山东麓新建成的地方国营贺兰磷粉厂，28日正式投入生产。
    这座工厂每年能生产磷粉一万吨，够供应八十万亩水田用作肥料。用这种肥料，可以提高农作物单位面积产量10％以上。
    根据现有地质资料和苏联专家的初步估计，在贺兰山中部地带的苏峪口磷矿区周围二十八平方公里内，蕴藏的磷矿约有三百多万吨。
