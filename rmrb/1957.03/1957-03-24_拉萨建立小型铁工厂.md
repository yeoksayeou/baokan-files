Title: Small Iron Factory Established in Lhasa
Author:
Date: 1957-03-24
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, March 21st. The small iron factory that the State Council decided to help the Tibet local government establish has begun construction in the northern suburbs of Lhasa.

　　Tibetan and Han workers are busy laying the foundation and transporting building materials here. The factory building and ancillary buildings, covering an area of more than 3,000 square meters, are expected to be completed in June, and a complete set of machinery and equipment is also on its way to Lhasa.

　　After the factory is completed, it will mainly manufacture agricultural, pastoral, and handicraft production tools and spare parts for simple machines.

### 拉萨建立小型铁工厂

1957-03-24
第2版()
专栏：

　  拉萨建立小型铁工厂
    新华社拉萨21日电　国务院决定帮助西藏地方建立的小型铁工厂，已在拉萨市北郊动工兴建。
    藏汉族工人正在这里忙着打地基，运送着建筑材料。三千多平方公尺的厂房和附属建筑物，预计6月间可完成，全套机器设备也正在运拉萨途中。
    这个厂建成后，主要制造农、牧、手工业生产工具和简单机器的配件。
