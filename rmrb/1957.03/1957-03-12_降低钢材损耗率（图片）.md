Author: Sun Baopu
Date: 1957-03-12

Reducing Steel Loss Rate
The workers of the Qingdao Jianhua Joint Public-Private Steel Rolling Mill, in order to achieve the plan of increasing steel production by 8,800 tons, have achieved great results in improving equipment, learning advanced experience, reducing steel loss rate, and improving labor organization. This is a steel rolling worker working hard.

Photographed by Sun Baopu (Manuscript from Dazhong Daily)

### 降低钢材损耗率（图片）
孙保朴
1957-03-12
第5版()
专栏：

    降低钢材损耗率
    公私合营青岛建华轧钢厂的职工们，为了实现增产八千八百吨钢材计划，在改进设备、学习先进经验、降低钢材损耗率、改进劳动组织等方面，都收到了巨大的成效。这是轧钢工人在紧张劳动。
    孙保朴摄（大众日报社稿）
