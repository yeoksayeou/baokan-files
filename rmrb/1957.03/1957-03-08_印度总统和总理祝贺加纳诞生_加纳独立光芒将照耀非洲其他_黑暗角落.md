Title: Indian President and Prime Minister Congratulate Ghana on its Birth; The Light of Ghana's Independence Will Shine on Other "Dark Corners" of Africa
Author:
Date: 1957-03-08
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 6th: Indian President Prasad and Prime Minister Nehru sent telegrams congratulating Ghana on its independence. Nehru said in his congratulatory telegram that Ghana's independence symbolized "freedom shaking the whole of Africa." It has "great significance not only for Africa but also for Asia, and to some extent, for the whole world."

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 7th: Indian Prime Minister Nehru said on the 6th at a meeting organized by the Indian African Students Association to celebrate Ghana's independence that the light of Ghana's independence would shine on other "dark corners" of the African continent.

### 印度总统和总理祝贺加纳诞生  加纳独立光芒将照耀非洲其他“黑暗角落”

1957-03-08
第6版()
专栏：

    印度总统和总理祝贺加纳诞生
    加纳独立光芒将照耀非洲其他“黑暗角落”
    据新华社新德里6日电　印度普拉沙德总统和尼赫鲁总理打电报祝贺加纳独立。尼赫鲁在贺电中说，加纳的独立象征着“震动整个非洲的自由”。它“不仅对非洲，而且对亚洲有重大意义，在某种程度上，对全世界也有重大意义”。
    据新华社新德里7日电　印度总理尼赫鲁6日在印度非洲学生协会为庆祝加纳独立所组织的一次会议上说，加纳独立的光芒将照耀非洲大陆的其他“黑暗角落”。
