Title: China's Glass Shipped to Ghana
Author:
Date: 1957-03-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency: Shanghai has shipped more than 300 cases of glass to Ghana (Gold Coast) this year. These goods are exported by the Shanghai Public-Private Joint Industrial Products Export Company.

Shanghai and Ghana have had trade relations since the beginning of 1956. In addition to glass, the goods shipped from here also include newsprint, paint brushes, etc.

### 我国玻璃运往加纳

1957-03-10
第5版()
专栏：

    我国玻璃运往加纳
    新华社讯　上海口岸今年已有三百多箱玻璃运往加纳（黄金海岸）。这些商品是上海公私合营工业品出口公司经营出口的。
    从1956年年初起，上海口岸和加纳就有了贸易联系，从这里运出的货物除了玻璃以外，还有新闻纸、漆刷等。
