Title: A Hundred-Jin Giant Pumpkin
Author: Jing Fugui
Date: 1957-03-11
Page: 3
Agricultural News

    A Hundred-Jin Giant Pumpkin
    Fu Guangming, a member of the Gaojia Village Agricultural Cooperative in Libao District, Jingchuan County, Gansu Province, planted two pumpkin vines last year. They yielded six large pumpkins, the largest of which weighed 104 jin, and the smallest weighed 40 jin. The six pumpkins together weighed 360 jin. (Jing Fugui)

### 百余斤的大南瓜
景富贵
1957-03-11
第3版()
专栏：农业珍闻

    百余斤的大南瓜
    甘肃泾川县荔堡区高家村农业社社员富光明，去年种了两棵南瓜，结了六个大南瓜，其中最大的一个南瓜有一百零四斤重，最小的一个也有四十斤重，六个南瓜总共重量有三百六十斤。　（景富贵）
