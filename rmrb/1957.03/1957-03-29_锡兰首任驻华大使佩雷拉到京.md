Title: Ceylon's First Ambassador to China, Perera, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-03-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 28th— Ceylon's first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Wilmot Abraham Perera, and his entourage arrived in Beijing by train tonight. Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs Qiao Guanhua and Director of the Protocol Department Wang Zhuoru were at the station to greet them.

Members of the diplomatic corps and diplomatic officials from various countries also went to welcome them.

### 锡兰首任驻华大使佩雷拉到京

1957-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　锡兰首任驻华大使佩雷拉到京
    新华社28日讯  锡兰驻中国首任特命全权大使威耳莫特·阿伯拉罕·佩雷拉及随行人员在今天晚上乘火车到达北京。前往车站迎接的有外交部部长助理乔冠华、礼宾司司长王倬如等。
    各国驻华使节和外交官员也前往迎接。
