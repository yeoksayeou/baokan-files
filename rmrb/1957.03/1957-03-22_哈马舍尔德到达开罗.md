Title: Hammarskjöld Arrives in Cairo
Author:
Date: 1957-03-22
Page: 7

　　Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 21st: United Nations Secretary-General Dag Hammarskjöld arrived in Cairo at 1:00 AM today. He declined to make any statement to reporters after disembarking from the plane. However, his spokesman, Mr. Schmidt, said that Hammarskjöld would hold a series of exploratory talks in Egypt regarding the Suez Canal and the activities of the United Nations Emergency Force.

### 哈马舍尔德到达开罗

1957-03-22
第7版()
专栏：

　  哈马舍尔德到达开罗
    新华社开罗21日电　联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德在今天上午一时到达开罗。他下飞机后拒绝对新闻记者发表任何谈话。但是他的发言人舒密特说，哈马舍尔德将在埃及就苏伊士运河和联合国紧急部队的活动进行一系列的探讨性会谈。
