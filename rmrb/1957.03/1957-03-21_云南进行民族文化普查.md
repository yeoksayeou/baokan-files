Title: Yunnan to Conduct Ethnic Cultural Census
Author:
Date: 1957-03-21
Page: 7

　　Yunnan to Conduct Ethnic Cultural Census
    (Xinhua News Agency dispatch) Yunnan Province will conduct a comprehensive survey of the cultural resources of all ethnic minorities within the province this year.
    This year's survey will focus on the Bai people in Dali and the Dai people in Xishuangbanna and Dehong regions. The Provincial Cultural Bureau will dispatch comprehensive work teams to these three regions to assist in collecting and organizing the folk literature, art, music, and historical documents of the Bai and Dai peoples. The collected cultural relics will be displayed locally in small exhibitions. Where conditions permit, the work teams will also assist ethnic minorities in establishing their own cultural work organizations and rural clubs.
    In addition to some Han cadres, ethnic intellectuals, professional artists, and folk artists will make up a large proportion of those participating in the survey work. In addition, a group of ethnic minority youths will be equipped and trained to become ethnic cultural workers.
    The survey work on the cultures of other ethnic minorities in the province will be the responsibility of the cultural sections of each special district and autonomous prefecture (district).

### 云南进行民族文化普查

1957-03-21
第7版()
专栏：

　  云南进行民族文化普查
    据新华社讯　云南省今年将对省内各少数民族的文化蕴藏作一次普遍调查。
    今年的调查工作将以大理的白族、西双版纳和德宏地区的傣族为重点。由省文化局派遣综合工作组去这三个地区，帮助收集整理白、傣二族的民间文学、美术、音乐和历史文献等。收集到的文物，将就地作小型展出。在有条件的地方，工作组还要协助少数民族成立自己的文化工作组织和农村俱乐部。
    参加调查工作的，除部分汉族干部外，民族知识分子、职业艺术家和民间艺人占有很大比例。此外还配备有一批少数民族青年，他们将被培养成为民族文化工作者。
    对全省其它少数民族文化的调查工作，将由各专区、自治州（区）的文化科负责。
