Title: Gomulka Receives Pollitt and Gollan
Author:
Date: 1957-03-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 23rd -- According to TASS, Warsaw, March 23rd: The First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Gomulka, received the Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Communist Party of Great Britain, Pollitt, and the General Secretary of the Communist Party of Great Britain, Gollan, who are visiting Warsaw. Members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party were present at the meeting.

### 哥穆尔卡接见波立特和高兰

1957-03-24
第5版()
专栏：

    哥穆尔卡接见波立特和高兰
    新华社23日讯  据塔斯社华沙23日讯：波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记哥穆尔卡接见了在华沙访问的英国共产党执行委员会主席波立特和英国共产党总书记高兰。接见时在座的有波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员们。
