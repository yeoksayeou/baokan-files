Title: Nigeria Demands Independence in Two Years; Tunisian Prime Minister Firmly Advocates for Algerian Independence
Author:
Date: 1957-03-29
Page: 6

　　Nigeria Demands Independence in Two Years
　　Tunisian Prime Minister Firmly Advocates for Algerian Independence
    Xinhua News Agency, March 28th: The Nigerian Federal House of Representatives passed a resolution unanimously on March 26th, demanding independence within the British Commonwealth in 1959. This resolution will serve as the negotiating guideline for the Nigerian representatives attending the Nigerian Constitutional Conference in London on May 23rd.
    Members of the three major Nigerian parties—the Northern People's Congress, the Action Group, and the National Council of Nigeria and the Cameroons—unanimously supported this resolution in their speeches.
    Xinhua News Agency, March 28th: Government delegations from Tunisia and Morocco are currently negotiating a treaty of friendship and mutual assistance.
    At a press conference on the 26th, Tunisian Prime Minister Bourguiba stated that "no difficulties have been encountered in concluding the Tunisian-Moroccan treaty" during the negotiation process.
    The "Al Alam," the organ of the Moroccan Istiqlal Party, published an interview with Bourguiba. When discussing Tunisia and Morocco's attitudes toward the Algerian issue, he said: "Only by granting Algeria independence can the independence of Tunisia and Morocco be consolidated and guaranteed." He emphasized, "Only when Algeria has gained independence and that independence is consolidated will it be possible to cooperate with France on the basis of freedom and common interests."

### 尼日利亚要求后年独立　突尼斯首相坚决主张阿尔及利亚独立

1957-03-29
第6版()
专栏：

　　尼日利亚要求后年独立
　　突尼斯首相坚决主张阿尔及利亚独立
    新华社28日讯  尼日利亚联邦众议院3月26日以一致票通过决议，要求在1959年获得在英联邦内的独立。这个决议将作为5月23日在伦敦参加尼日利亚制宪会议的尼日利亚代表的谈判方针。
    尼日利亚的三个主要政党——北方人民大会党、行动党和尼日利亚喀麦隆民族委员会的议员在发言时都一致支持这个决议。
    据新华社28日讯  突尼斯和摩洛哥两国政府代表团正在就缔结友好互助条约问题进行谈判。
    在26日的记者招待会上，突尼斯首相布尔吉巴表示，在谈判过程中，“在缔结突尼斯—摩洛哥条约方面，没有碰到什么困难”。
    摩洛哥独立党的机关报“旗帜报”刊载了一篇布尔吉巴同记者的谈话。在谈到突尼斯和摩洛哥对阿尔及利亚问题的态度时，他说：“只有给予阿尔及利亚独立，突尼斯和摩洛哥两国的独立才能稳固和得到保障。”他强调指出，“只有在阿尔及利亚获得了独立和这种独立巩固了的时候，才有可能在自由和共同利益的基础上同法国进行合作。”
