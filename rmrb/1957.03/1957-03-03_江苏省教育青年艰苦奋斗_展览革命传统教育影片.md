Title: Jiangsu Province Educates Youth on Hard Work; Exhibition of Revolutionary Tradition Education Films
Author:
Date: 1957-03-03
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 2nd. Jiangsu Province will hold "Revolutionary Tradition Education Film Exhibitions" in all cinemas throughout the province from March 1st to May, each lasting seven to ten days. This film exhibition is organized by the Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the Communist Youth League and the Jiangsu Provincial Bureau of Culture in conjunction with the propaganda campaign of "Inheriting Revolutionary Traditions and Promoting the Spirit of Hard Struggle." During the exhibition, the focus will be on introducing the films "Shangganling" and "Mother."

To better meet the needs of the broad masses of young people, fifteen films popular with the masses, such as "Crossing the River Reconnaissance," "Taking Huashan Mountain Intelligently," "Dong Cunrui," "Fighting North and South," "Sons and Daughters of China," "Shangrao Concentration Camp," and "For Peace," have also been selected for additional screenings in various locations.

### 江苏省教育青年艰苦奋斗  展览革命传统教育影片

1957-03-03
第7版()
专栏：

    江苏省教育青年艰苦奋斗
    展览革命传统教育影片
    新华社南京2日电  江苏省自3月1日到5月间在全省各电影院先后举行为期七天到十天的“革命传统教育影片展览”。这次的影片展览是青年团江苏省委员会和江苏省文化局配合“继承革命传统，发扬艰苦奋斗精神”的宣传活动而举办的。展览期间，将着重介绍“上甘岭”、“母亲”两部影片。
    为了更好地满足广大青年需要，还选择了群众所喜爱的“渡江侦察记”、“智取华山”、“董存瑞”、“南征北战”、“中华女儿”、“上饶集中营”、“为了和平”等十五部影片，供各地加场放映。
