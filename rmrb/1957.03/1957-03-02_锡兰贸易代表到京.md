Title: Ceylon Trade Representatives Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-03-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 1st – At the invitation of the China National Sundries Export Corporation, Cereals, Oils and Foodstuffs Export Corporation, Native Produce Export Corporation, and Foodstuffs Export Corporation, the five-member Ceylon trade delegation arrived in Beijing by plane yesterday afternoon to discuss trade matters with our country.

The five representatives include Mr. V. Subasinghe, Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Ceylon Wholesale Cooperative Organization, and Mr. KPG. Perera, Assistant Director of the Ceylon Government Commercial Bureau.

### 锡兰贸易代表到京

1957-03-02
第1版()
专栏：

    锡兰贸易代表到京
    新华社1日讯  应中国杂品、粮谷油脂、土产、食品等出口公司邀请来我国洽谈贸易的锡兰贸易代表五人，在昨天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    这五个代表中，有锡兰批发合作组织董事会主席V·苏巴辛赫先生和锡兰政府商业局局长助理KPG·彼立拉先生。
