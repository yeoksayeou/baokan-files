Title: Resolution of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Executive Committee on Convening the Eighth National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions (March 21, 1957)
Author:
Date: 1957-03-22
Page: 1

　　The All-China Federation of Trade Unions Executive Committee passed the following resolution on March 21, 1957, regarding the convening of the Eighth National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions:

　　It is decided that the Eighth National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions will be convened within the year 1957.

　　The main agenda of this congress is:
  (1) Work report of the Seventh Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions;
  (2) Report on amending the Constitution of the Trade Unions of the People's Republic of China;
  (3) Election of the Eighth Executive Committee and the Funds Review Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

### 中华全国总工会执行委员会全体会议关于召开中国工会第八次全国代表大会的决议（1957年3月21日）

1957-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　中华全国总工会执行委员会全体会议关于召开中国工会第八次全国代表大会的决议
　　　（1957年3月21日）
　　决定在1957年年内召开中国工会第八次全国代表大会。
　　这次大会的主要议程是：
  （一）中华全国总工会第七届执行委员会的工作报告；
  （二）关于修改中华人民共和国工会章程的报告；
  （三）选举中华全国总工会第八届执行委员会和经费审查委员会。
