Title: Vietnam and Yugoslavia Establish Formal Diplomatic Relations

Author:

Date: 1957-03-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 10th: The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam issued a communique today stating that the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the government of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, after negotiations, have decided to establish diplomatic relations and mutually dispatch representatives at the level of Ambassadors Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary.

### 越南同南斯拉夫正式建交

1957-03-11
第5版()
专栏：

    越南同南斯拉夫正式建交
    据新华社河内10日电　越南民主共和国外交部今天发表公报说，越南民主共和国政府同南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国政府在经过谈判以后，已经决定两国建立外交关系并且互派特命全权大使级的代表。
