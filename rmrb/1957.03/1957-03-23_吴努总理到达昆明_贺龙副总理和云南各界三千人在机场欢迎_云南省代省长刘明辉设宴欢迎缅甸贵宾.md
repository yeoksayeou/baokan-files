Title: Prime Minister U Nu Arrives in Kunming; Vice Premier He Long and 3,000 People from All Circles in Yunnan Welcome Him at the Airport; Acting Governor of Yunnan Province Liu Minghui Hosts a Banquet to Welcome the Distinguished Burmese Guests
Author:
Date: 1957-03-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, March 22nd: The Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, who is visiting Kunming City, Yunnan Province, arrived in Kunming by special plane at 2:40 this afternoon. Vice Premier He Long of the State Council of our country, who came to Kunming to welcome Prime Minister U Nu, greeted Prime Minister U Nu at the airport today.

Accompanying Prime Minister U Nu to Kunming were the Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Union of Burma, Sao Khun Hkio, Daw Khin Kyi (wife of Aung San), Deputy Minister of Shan State Affairs, U Khim Maung, and Prime Minister U Nu's two sons. Yao Zhongming, China's Ambassador to Burma, also accompanied them to Kunming.

As Prime Minister U Nu appeared at the cabin door with a smile, more than 3,000 people of various ethnic groups, dressed in brightly colored costumes and holding the national flags of China and Burma, danced and shouted slogans such as "Welcome Prime Minister U Nu" and "China and Burma are 'Pauk Phaw' (brothers)." After Prime Minister U Nu got off the plane, he shook hands with Vice Premier He Long of the State Council of our country, Acting Governor of Yunnan Province Liu Minghui, Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Kunming, General Xie Fuzhi, Mayor of Kunming Pan Shuoduan, and the heads of democratic parties and people's organizations of Yunnan Province and Kunming City.

After Prime Minister U Nu reviewed the guard of honor, Acting Governor Liu Minghui delivered a welcome speech. On behalf of the people of all ethnic groups in Yunnan Province, he expressed a warm welcome to Prime Minister U Nu and the distinguished Burmese guests for their visit to Kunming.

Then Prime Minister U Nu addressed the 3,000 people at the airport. He said: "Not long ago, I came to Kunming. At that time, I promised to come to Kunming again in March of this year to meet my relatives and friends. Now, my wish has been fulfilled." He continued, "Every time I come to Kunming, I find it different each time. Today, I feel that our friendship has been further strengthened."

Prime Minister U Nu said that many Chinese leaders, such as Premier Zhou Enlai, Marshal He Long, and Marshal Chen Yi, have visited Burma twice or three times. I have also visited China many times. The purpose of our mutual visits is to promote the kinship-like friendly relations between our two countries. Prime Minister U Nu added: "At present, there are still many countries in the world that hate each other, creating international tensions. If there is another world war, the entire world will be in danger. I believe that the best way to consolidate world peace and develop friendship between countries is through mutual visits."

Prime Minister U Nu also introduced his friend, Deputy Prime Minister Sao Khun Hkio, Daw Khin Kyi, and his two sons to the people who came to welcome him, according to Burmese customs. He also shouted slogans in Chinese such as "Long live world peace" and "Long live Sino-Burmese friendship."

Also at the airport to welcome Prime Minister U Nu were U Ba Maung, Ambassador of the Union of Burma to our country, U Maung Maung Toe, Consul General of the Union of Burma in Kunming, and officials from the Consulate of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming.

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, March 22nd: Acting Governor of Yunnan Province Liu Minghui held a banquet this evening to welcome the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, and Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Sao Khun Hkio, Daw Khin Kyi, and Deputy Minister of Shan State Affairs, U Khim Maung.

Vice Premier He Long attended the banquet. Also present were General Xie Fuzhi, Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Kunming, Mayor of Kunming Pan Shuoduan, Head of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Prefecture of Yunnan Province, Dao Jingban (Dai nationality), Deputy Head Lei Chunguo (Jingpo nationality), and Deputy Head of the Xishuangbanna Dai Autonomous Prefecture, Dao Youliang (Dai nationality).

U Ba Maung, Ambassador of Burma to our country, and Yao Zhongming, our country's Ambassador to Burma, were also invited to attend the banquet. Also present at the banquet were U Maung Maung Toe, Consul General of Burma in Kunming, and officials from the Consulate of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming.

The banquet was held amidst brisk and lively light music. Those attending the banquet raised their glasses with Prime Minister U Nu, offering their best wishes. Acting Governor Liu Minghui raised his glass and expressed a warm welcome to Prime Minister U Nu and the distinguished Burmese guests. Prime Minister U Nu raised his glass and said, "China's Yunnan Province and the Union of Burma are so close. I hope that in the future, when I come to Yunnan, you will treat me like family." During the banquet, he also invited Vice Premier He Long, Acting Governor Liu Minghui, General Xie Fuzhi, and all the friends attending the banquet to Burma to celebrate the Water Splashing Festival. He said: In Burma, this is a very grand and joyful festival. We will definitely splash you with water enthusiastically.

After the banquet, Prime Minister U Nu also attended a cultural performance held in his honor.

### 吴努总理到达昆明　贺龙副总理和云南各界三千人在机场欢迎　云南省代省长刘明辉设宴欢迎缅甸贵宾

1957-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　吴努总理到达昆明
　　贺龙副总理和云南各界三千人在机场欢迎
　　云南省代省长刘明辉设宴欢迎缅甸贵宾
    新华社昆明22日电　来我国云南省昆明市访问的缅甸联邦总理吴努，今天下午二时四十分乘专机到达昆明。为欢迎吴努总理而来到昆明的我国国务院贺龙副总理今天在机场上欢迎吴努总理。
    随同吴努总理来到昆明的，有缅甸联邦副总理兼外交部长藻昆卓，昂山夫人（都庆枝），掸邦事务部政务次长吴季密和吴努总理的两位公子。我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明也陪同来到昆明。
    当吴努总理微笑着出现在机舱门口时，穿着鲜艳服装、拿着中缅两国国旗的三千多名各族群众跳着、欢呼“欢迎吴努总理”、“中缅人民是‘胞波’”等口号。吴努总理走下飞机后，和我国国务院贺龙副总理，云南省代省长刘明辉，中国人民解放军驻昆明部队首长谢富治上将，昆明市市长潘朔端以及云南省、昆明市各民主党派、人民团体的负责人等握手见面。
    吴努总理检阅了仪仗队后，刘明辉代省长致欢迎词。他代表云南省各族人民对吴努总理和缅甸贵宾们这次来昆明访问表示热烈欢迎。
    接着吴努总理在机场上向三千多群众讲了话。他说：“不久前，我曾经来到过昆明。当时，我曾答应要在今年3月份再到昆明来会见我的亲戚朋友们，现在，我的这个愿望已经实现了。”他接着说：“每当我来到昆明的时候，都发现一次比一次不同，今天在这里，我感到我们之间的友谊又更加加强了。”
    吴努总理说，中国的许多领袖，如周恩来总理、贺龙元帅和陈毅元帅都曾经两次、三次地访问过缅甸。我也曾经多次地访问过中国。我们互相访问的目的，就是为了促进我们两国之间亲戚般的友好关系。吴努总理又说：“目前，世界上还有许多国家互相仇视，造成了国际上的紧张局势。如果世界上再有另一次大战，那么，整个世界都将处在危险之中。我认为，为了巩固世界和平，发展各国之间友谊的最好方法就是互相访问。”
    吴努总理还按照缅甸的风俗，将他的朋友藻昆卓副总理、昂山夫人以及他的两位公子介绍给前来欢迎他的人们。并用中国话高呼“世界和平万岁”、“中缅友谊万岁”等口号。
    在机场欢迎吴努总理的，还有缅甸联邦驻我国大使吴拉茂，缅甸联邦驻昆明总领事吴茂茂铎和越南民主共和国驻昆明领事馆的官员等。
    新华社昆明22日电　云南省代省长刘明辉今天晚间举行宴会，欢迎缅甸联邦吴努总理和副总理兼外交部长藻昆卓、昂山夫人、掸邦事务部政务次长吴季密。
    贺龙副总理出席了宴会。出席作陪的还有中国人民解放军驻昆明部队首长谢富治上将，昆明市市长潘朔端，云南省德宏傣族景颇族自治州州长刀京版（傣族）、副州长雷春国（景颇族），西双版纳傣族自治州副州长刀有良（傣族）等。
    缅甸驻我国大使吴拉茂，我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明也应邀出席了宴会。出席宴会的还有缅甸驻昆明总领事吴茂茂铎和越南民主共和国驻昆明领事馆的官员。
    宴会在节奏明快的轻音乐声中进行。出席宴会的人们纷纷以最美好的祝愿与吴努总理一同碰杯。刘明辉代省长举杯，对吴努总理和缅甸贵宾们表示热烈的欢迎。吴努总理举杯说：“中国的云南省和缅甸联邦之间是如此亲密，我希望今后我来到云南时，你们把我当家里人一样的来接待我。”他在席间还邀请贺龙副总理、刘明辉代省长、谢富治上将以及出席宴会的全体朋友到缅甸去欢度泼水节。他说：在缅甸，这是一个非常盛大而愉快的节日。我们一定热情地给你们泼水。
    宴会后，吴努总理还出席了为欢迎他举行的文娱晚会。
