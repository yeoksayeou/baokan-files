Title: Disarmament Subcommittee Discusses the Issue of Atomic and Nuclear Explosion Tests: US Representative Stubbornly Opposes Soviet Proposal
Author:
Date: 1957-03-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, March 29th: The United Nations Disarmament Commission Subcommittee continued its secret meeting on March 28th. According to reports from Western news agencies, the meeting began to discuss the issue of atomic and nuclear explosion tests that day.

It is said that Soviet representative Zorin requested a cessation of atomic and hydrogen weapon tests at the meeting and put forward proposals. US representative Stassen stated that atomic and nuclear explosion tests could not be stopped without conditions such as an inspection system. Stassen demanded that the Soviet Union provide materials on all its past nuclear explosions and that experts first study whether all tests could be detected.

The next meeting of the committee will be held on April 1st.

### 裁军小组委员会　讨论原子核爆炸试验问题　美国代表顽固反对苏联建议

1957-03-30
第6版()
专栏：

　　裁军小组委员会
　　讨论原子核爆炸试验问题
　　美国代表顽固反对苏联建议
    新华社29日讯  伦敦消息：联合国裁军委员会小组委员会3月28日继续举行秘密会议。西方通讯社透露出来的消息说，这天会议开始讨论原子核爆炸试验问题。
    据说，苏联代表佐林在会上要求停止进行原子武器和氢武器的试验，提出了建议。美国代表史塔生表示，在没有视察制度等项条件以前，不能停止原子核爆炸试验。史塔生要求苏联提供它过去进行的所有原子核爆炸的材料，并且由专家先来研究所有试验是否都可以侦察出来的问题。
    委员会下次会议将在4月1日举行。
