Title: Syria Closes Air France Office

Author: 
Date: 1957-03-19
Page: 6

    Syria Closes Air France Office

    Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 18th—Syria ordered the closure of the French airline office on the 17th, and also banned French airline planes from using Syrian airports and flying over Syrian airspace.
    An official statement said that the ban was implemented because the company violated the Arab countries' blockade against Israel.

### 叙封闭法航空公司办事处

1957-03-19
第6版()
专栏：

    叙封闭法航空公司办事处
    新华社18日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚17日命令封闭法国航空公司办事处，并且禁止法国航空公司飞机使用叙利亚飞机场和飞越叙利亚领空。
    一项官方声明说，实施这项禁令是由于这家公司违反了阿拉伯国家对以色列的封锁令。
