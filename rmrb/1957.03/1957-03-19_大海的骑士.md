Title: Knights of the Sea
Author: Lǐ Yīng
Date: 1957-03-19
Page: 8

    Knights of the Sea
    Li Ying
    The sky is a狰狞face,
    The crests of waves are sharp teeth;
    Several alarms have already passed,
    Where does the missing fishing boat drift?
    Tonight, how many hearts are strung on the rain,
    How many hearts are tossed in the sea!
    Suddenly, a leaf-like small boat emerges from a crack in the clouds,
    It seems to boldly take flight on the sea,
    But the wind grabs it, the waves tug at it,
    It rolls incessantly in the black waves.
    Mountain-high waves, do not strike it,
    It is searching for the capsized boat…
    Look at the two sailors standing at the bow,
    Scanning the raging sea with lights;
    What shakes the sea is not the raging wind,
    But the warriors' robust strength.
    The light is a majestic sword,
    They will seize the wind and rain, and put them in the cabin.
    On the sea at night, this is the only pulse—
    A propeller, a few beating sailors' hearts.
    Raging wind and rain, you should know,
    Who is the true master of the night, the knights of the sea.

### 大海的骑士
李瑛
1957-03-19
第8版()
专栏：

    大海的骑士
    李瑛
    天空是狰狞的脸，
    浪尖是锐利的牙齿；
    几次警报已经过去，
    失踪的渔船飘在哪里？
    今夜呵，有多少颗心穿在雨上，
    多少颗心翻在海里！
    突然云缝中钻出一只树叶一样的小艇，
    它像要在海面大胆地飞起，
    可是风抓住它、浪扯住它，
    它在黑色的波涛中，不住打滚。
    山一样高的波涛呵，不要冲击它吧，
    它正在寻找倾复的船只……
    你看船头上站着的两个水兵，
    正用灯光横扫着疯狂的海水；
    那摇动大海的不是狂风呵，
    而是战士们雄健的膂力。
    那灯光是一把威严的宝剑，
    他们要把风雨捉住，放进舱里。
    夜的海上，这是唯一的脉搏了——
    一只螺旋桨、几颗跳动的水兵的心。
    肆虐的风雨呀，你应该知道了，
    谁才是夜的真正的主人，大海的骑士。
