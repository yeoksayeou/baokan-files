Author: Shi Qimin
Date: 1957-03-27

　　Collecting Seedlings
    The 300,000 seedlings cultivated last year at the Communist Youth League Farm at the foot of Qingyuan Mountain, North Gate, Quanzhou City, Fujian Province, have grown up. These two female farm members are collecting seedlings in preparation for supplying afforestation efforts in Quanzhou City.

### 收树苗（图片）

1957-03-27
第3版()
专栏：

　　收树苗
    福建泉州市北门清源山麓共青团农庄，去年培植的三十万株树苗已经长大了。这两位女庄员正在收树苗，准备供应泉州市造林用。
