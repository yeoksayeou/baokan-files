Title: Mayor Peng Zhen Hosts Banquet for Czechoslovak Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-03-28
Page: 1

　  Mayor Peng Zhen Hosts Banquet for Czechoslovak Government Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, March 27th: Mayor of Beijing, Peng Zhen, hosted a banquet at Quanjude this evening for the Czechoslovak government delegation headed by Prime Minister Viliam Široký.
    Premier Zhou Enlai attended the banquet this evening.
    Among those present were Vice Premier of the State Council, Bo Yibo; Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Li Jishen; Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council, Cheng Qian; Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ji Pengfei; Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, Cao Ying; Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Zhang Bojun; Chairman of the Beijing Committee of the CPPCC and Second Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPC, Liu Ren; and Vice Mayors of Beijing, Zhang Youyu and Yue Songsheng.

### 彭真市长宴请捷政府代表团

1957-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

　  彭真市长宴请捷政府代表团
    新华社27日讯　北京市市长彭真，今晚在全聚德宴请以威廉·西罗基总理为首的捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团。
    周恩来总理出席了今晚的宴会。
    出席作陪的有国务院副总理薄一波，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深，国防委员会副主席程潜，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使曹瑛，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧，政协北京市委员会主席、中共北京市委第二书记刘仁，北京市副市长张友渔、乐松生等人。
