Title: Sino-Mongolian Cultural Cooperation Plan for This Year Signed
Author:
Date: 1957-03-10
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 9th. The Sino-Mongolian Cultural Cooperation Plan for 1957 was signed in Ulaanbaatar on the 8th. The cultural delegations of the two countries issued a press communique as follows:

    Recently, the cultural delegations of the People's Republic of China and the Mongolian People's Republic held talks in Ulaanbaatar on the 1957 Cultural Cooperation Plan between the two countries, and signed it on March 8.
    According to the plan, in order to exchange experiences with each other, the two countries will send cultural, scientific, writer, and artistic delegations, and further develop cooperative relations in science, national education, health, film, and radio.
    The two sides also initially exchanged views on the long-term plans for cultural cooperation between the two countries. In very friendly and cordial talks, both sides were pleased to note that cultural relations between the people of China and Mongolia are developing day by day on the basis of mutual respect, equality, and mutual benefit.
    Both sides unanimously believe that the further expansion of cultural relations between the two countries will help consolidate the fraternal friendship between the people of China and Mongolia and safeguard the cause of world peace.

### 中蒙今年文化合作计划签字

1957-03-10
第6版()
专栏：

    中蒙今年文化合作计划签字
    新华社乌兰巴托9日电　中蒙两国1957年文化合作计划8日在乌兰巴托签字，两国文化代表团发表新闻公报如下：
    最近，中华人民共和国和蒙古人民共和国文化代表团就两国1957年文化合作计划在乌兰巴托市进行了会谈，并且在3月8日签字。
    根据计划，两国间为了相互交流经验，将互派文化、科学、作家和艺术的代表团，并且进一步发展科学、国民教育、卫生、电影和广播方面的合作关系。
    双方就两国文化合作的远景规划也初步交换了意见。在非常友好和亲切的会谈中，双方满意地认为：在互相尊重平等互利的原则基础上，中蒙两国人民之间的文化关系正在日益发展。
    双方一致认为：两国文化关系的进一步扩大将有助于巩固中蒙两国人民的兄弟友谊和维护世界和平的事业。
