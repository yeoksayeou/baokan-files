Title: Over 100,000 Italian Workers Strike Demanding Improved Economic Conditions
Author:
Date: 1957-03-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rome, February 2nd: According to TASS, Italian postal and telecommunications employees began a general strike on the night of February 28th, demanding improved economic conditions. This strike was initiated by the three largest trade union organizations: the Italian General Confederation of Labor, the "Italian Confederation of Workers' Trade Unions," and the "Italian Labor Union." Approximately 105,000 workers nationwide participated in the strike. International Notes

### 要求改善经济状况  意大利十万余工人罢工

1957-03-03
第6版()
专栏：

    要求改善经济状况
    意大利十万余工人罢工
    新华社2日讯  据塔斯社罗马讯：意大利邮电员工在2月28日夜间开始总罢工，要求改善经济状况。这次罢工是由意大利总工会、“意大利劳动人民工会联合会”和“意大利劳工联盟”这三个最大的工会组织发动的。全国参加罢工的劳动人民在十万五千人左右。国际札记
