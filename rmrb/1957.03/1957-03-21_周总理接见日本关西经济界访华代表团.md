Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Delegation from the Kansai Economic Community of Japan

Author:

Date: 1957-03-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 20th. Premier Zhou Enlai received this afternoon the delegation from the Kansai Economic Community of Japan, headed by Koichi Yoshino, and the deputy heads Yosamatsu Matsubara, Shinji Mori, Secretary-General Tsutaru Kawakatsu, and all members of the delegation.

Present at the reception were Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Lei Renmin, Li Zhucheng, Ji Chaoding, Vice-Chairmen of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; and Liao Chengzhi.

### 周总理接见日本关西经济界访华代表团

1957-03-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　周总理接见日本关西经济界访华代表团
    新华社20日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了日本关西经济界访华友好代表团团长吉野孝一，副团长松原与三松、森新治，秘书长川胜传及全体团员。
    接见时在座的有中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸，副主席雷任民、李烛尘、冀朝鼎，及廖承志等人。
