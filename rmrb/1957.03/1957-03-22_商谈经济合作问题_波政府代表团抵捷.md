Title: Polish Government Delegation Arrives in Czechoslovakia to Discuss Economic Cooperation Issues
Author:
Date: 1957-03-22
Page: 7

　  Discussing Economic Cooperation Issues
　  Polish Government Delegation Arrives in Czechoslovakia
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 21st - A Polish government delegation headed by Polish Deputy Prime Minister Jaroszewicz arrived in Prague on the 20th to hold talks with relevant Czechoslovakian parties on economic cooperation issues.
    After visiting Asian countries, Polish Prime Minister Cyrankiewicz will also visit Czechoslovakia and will sign an agreement.

### 商谈经济合作问题  波政府代表团抵捷

1957-03-22
第7版()
专栏：

　  商谈经济合作问题
　  波政府代表团抵捷
    据新华社华沙21日讯　以波兰部长会议副主席雅罗谢维奇为首的波兰政府代表团20日到达布拉格，将就经济合作问题同捷克斯洛伐克有关方面举行会谈。
    波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹在访问亚洲各国以后也将访问捷克斯洛伐克，并且将签订协定。
