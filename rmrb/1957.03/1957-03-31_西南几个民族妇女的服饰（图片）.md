　　Clothing of Women from Several Ethnic Minorities in Southwestern China

　　In the rich and beautiful southwestern part of our motherland, live many ethnic groups with long-standing cultural and artistic traditions. The clothing of each ethnic group has its own unique style. Women use ordinary fabrics to sew various styles of clothing, and adorn them with delicate embroidery and decorations, making them beautiful and elegant.

### 西南几个民族妇女的服饰（图片）
袁渊
1957-03-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　西南几个民族妇女的服饰
    在富饶而美丽的祖国西南部，居住着许多民族，他们有悠久的文化艺术传统。各民族的服饰具有不同的风格。妇女们用普通的衣料缝制各种式样的服装，并配上精细的刺绣和装饰品，显得美观、雅致。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　  本报记者  袁渊摄
