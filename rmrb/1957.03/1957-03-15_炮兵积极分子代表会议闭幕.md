Title: Artillery Activists' Representative Conference Concludes
Author: 
Date: 1957-03-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 14th: The Chinese People's Liberation Army Artillery Activists' Representative Conference concluded this afternoon.

At the conference, the artillery leadership organs rewarded 108 advanced units and 655 activist representatives. At the same time, they rewarded the winners of the artillery officer shooting competition organized by the "People's Artillery" magazine, as well as officers and soldiers who had made significant achievements in creation and invention.

Marshal Ye Jianying attended the closing ceremony of the conference. He encouraged the activist representatives to strive for greater achievements in the training of 1957.

During the conference, eight representatives of activists and advanced units made reports on their typical deeds. General Gan Siqi, Deputy Director of the General Political Department, gave instructions to the representatives.

### 炮兵积极分子代表会议闭幕

1957-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

    炮兵积极分子代表会议闭幕
    新华社14日讯  中国人民解放军炮兵积极分子代表会议于今天下午闭幕。
    会上，炮兵领导机关奖励了一百零八个先进单位和六百五十五名积极分子代表，同时奖励了参加“人民炮兵”杂志社组织的炮兵军官射击竞赛的优胜者，以及在创造发明方面有重要成就的军官和战士。
    叶剑英元帅出席了会议闭幕式，他勉励积极分子代表们，争取在1957年的训练中，做出更大的成绩。
    会议期间，有八名积极分子和先进单位的代表作了典型事迹报告，总政治部副主任甘泗淇上将对代表们作了指示。
