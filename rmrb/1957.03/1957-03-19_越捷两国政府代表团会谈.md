Title: Vietnam-Czechoslovakia Government Delegations Hold Talks
Author: 
Date: 1957-03-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 18th—The Vietnamese government announced today that the government delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic, headed by Prime Minister Široký, and the government delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, headed by Prime Minister Pham Van Dong, held talks at the Vietnamese Prime Minister's Office this morning. The two sides discussed the current international situation, strengthening the unity between socialist countries, and the friendly relations between the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Czechoslovak Republic.
    The talks were conducted in a sincere and friendly atmosphere, and a complete consensus of opinion was reached on the issues discussed. The two delegations will continue to hold talks.

### 越捷两国政府代表团会谈

1957-03-19
第6版()
专栏：

    越捷两国政府代表团会谈
    新华社河内18日电　越南政府今天发表公报说，以西罗基总理为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团和以范文同总理为首的越南民主共和国政府代表团今天上午在越南总理府举行会谈，双方就目前国际局势、加强社会主义阵营各国间的团结和越南民主共和国同捷克斯洛伐克共和国的友谊关系等问题进行了讨论。
    会谈在诚恳和友好的气氛中进行，并且对所讨论的问题取得了完全一致的意见。双方代表团还要继续进行会谈。
