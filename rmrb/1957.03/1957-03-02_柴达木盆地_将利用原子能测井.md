Title: Qaidam Basin to Utilize Nuclear Logging
Author:
Date: 1957-03-02
Page: 2

    Qaidam Basin
    Will Utilize Nuclear Logging

    (Our report) The Lenghu oil exploration area in the Qaidam Basin will utilize a globally scientifically advanced achievement—nuclear logging equipment—for logging work.

    According to logging engineers, under the special conditions of the Qaidam Basin's strata and groundwater having high salinity, using nuclear logging may obtain more accurate data and more correctly determine the location of oil and gas layers, thereby ensuring the quality of oil testing work.

    This advanced set of nuclear logging instruments was recently imported from the Soviet Union.

### 柴达木盆地  将利用原子能测井

1957-03-02
第2版()
专栏：

    柴达木盆地
    将利用原子能测井
    本报讯  柴达木盆地冷湖石油探区将利用世界科学的先进成就——原子能测井仪进行测井工作。
    据测井工程人员估计，在柴达木盆地地层和地下水含盐多的特殊条件下，利用原子能测井可能获得较正确的资料，比较正确地确定油、气层的位置，从而保证试油工作的质量。
    这套先进的原子能测井仪器，是最近由苏联进口的。
