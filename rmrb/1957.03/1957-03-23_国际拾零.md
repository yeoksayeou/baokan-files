　　International Gleanings
    Pibul Songgram Reappointed as Prime Minister of Thailand
    Thai officials announced on the evening of the 21st that King Bhumibol Adulyadej had reappointed Pibul Songgram as Prime Minister and ordered him to form a new government. According to Thai newspapers, the work of forming a new government is encountering difficulties. Disagreements within the ruling party are also developing.
    Nobusuke Kishi Elected President of the Liberal Democratic Party
    The Liberal Democratic Party, Japan's ruling party, held a convention on the 21st and elected current cabinet Prime Minister Nobusuke Kishi as party president.
    According to Kyodo News, after Nobusuke Kishi assumes the post of party president, infighting within the Liberal Democratic Party is expected to intensify.
    Heusinger Leads West German Army, Navy, and Air Force
    The West German government appointed Lieutenant General Heusinger as Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff of the West German Armed Forces on the 20th. The Chiefs of Staff of the Army, Navy, and Air Force will be led by Heusinger. During World War II, Heusinger served as the Operations Chief of the General Staff of the Nazi German Army and was arrested and dismissed from his post in 1944 in connection with the assassination attempt on Hitler.

### 国际拾零

1957-03-23
第6版()
专栏：国际拾零

　　国际拾零
    銮披汶连任泰国总理
    泰国官方21日晚间宣布，国王普密蓬·阿杜德任命銮披汶再度出任总理，并且命令他组织新政府。据泰国报纸报道，组织新政府的工作正遭遇困难。执政党内部的分歧意见也在发展。
    岸信介当选自由民主党总裁
    日本执政党自由民主党21日举行代表大会，选出现内阁首相岸信介为党总裁。
    据共同社报道，在岸信介就任党总裁以后，预料自由民主党内的明争暗斗将更加剧烈。
    豪辛格领导西德陆海空军
    西德政府20日任命豪辛格中将为西德武装部队参谋长联席会议主席。陆海空军参谋长将由豪辛格领导。豪辛格在第二次世界大战期间曾经担任纳粹德国陆军总部作战部长，在1944年由于谋刺希特勒案件的牵连而被捕去职。
