Title: Czechoslovak National Assembly Opens; Passes Bills on Expanding the Powers and Tasks of Local People's Committees
Author:
Date: 1957-03-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 8th: The spring session of the Czechoslovak National Assembly opened in Prague on the 6th. The meeting passed two amended bills aimed at enabling citizens to participate more in social activities and greatly expanding the powers and tasks of local people's committees.

The meeting was presided over by President Zápotocký. After discussion, the meeting unanimously passed two amended bills regarding the People's Committee Election Law and the People's Committee Law. The amended bills stipulate that the number of members of people's committees in villages with a population of less than 2,000 should be increased, so that the number of existing local people's committee members will increase by 15,000 to 16,000. The amended bills also stipulate that people who are not members of the various people's committees can also be elected as members of the standing committees of the people's committees. According to the new amended bills, the People's Committees are fully responsible for organizing the economic and cultural construction of their regions. According to the national budget adopted by the government, the income of the People's Committees will increase by 62% this year, and expenditures will increase by 72%.

### 捷克斯洛伐克国民议会开幕  通过扩大地方人民委员会的职权和任务等法案

1957-03-09
第6版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克国民议会开幕
    通过扩大地方人民委员会的职权和任务等法案
    新华社布拉格8日电　捷克斯洛伐克国民议会春季会议6日在布拉格开幕。会议通过了两个目的在于使公民更多地参加社会活动以及大大扩大地方人民委员会的职权和任务的两个修正法案。
    会议由萨波托斯基总统主持。会议经过讨论，一致通过了关于人民委员会选举法和人民委员会法的两个修正法案。修正法案规定要增加人口不满两千的乡村的人民委员会委员的名额，这样，现有的地方人民委员会委员人数将增加一万五千到一万六千人。修正法案还规定，今后不担任各个人民委员会委员的人也可以当选为人民委员会常设委员会的委员。根据新的修正法案，人民委员会对于组织自己地区的经济和文化建设的工作负有全部的责任。根据政府所通过的国家预算，人民委员会收入今年将增加62％，支出将增加72％。
