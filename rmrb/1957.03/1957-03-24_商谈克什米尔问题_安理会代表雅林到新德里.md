Title: Discussing the Kashmir Issue: Security Council Representative Jarring Arrives in New Delhi
Author:
Date: 1957-03-24
Page: 6

    Discussing the Kashmir Issue
    Security Council Representative Jarring Arrives in New Delhi
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 23rd: The President of the Security Council for February, Jarring, arrived here on the evening of the 22nd from Karachi to continue his mission of exploring possibilities for resolving the Kashmir issue.

### 商谈克什米尔问题  安理会代表雅林到新德里

1957-03-24
第6版()
专栏：

    商谈克什米尔问题
    安理会代表雅林到新德里
    据新华社新德里23日电　安全理事会2月份主席雅林在22日晚上从卡拉奇到达这里，以继续他的探讨解决克什米尔问题的可能性的使命。
