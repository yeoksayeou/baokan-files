Title: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Congratulatory Telegrams on Pakistan's National Day
Author:
Date: 1957-03-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 22nd -- Chairman Mao Zedong sent a telegram to Pakistani President Mirza, congratulating him on the National Day of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan. The full text is as follows:
    To His Excellency Iskander Mirza, President of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan:
    On the occasion of the National Day of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, I extend my sincere congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of Pakistan on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish the Islamic Republic of Pakistan increasing prosperity and the Pakistani people increasing happiness, and I wish Your Excellency good health.
            Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, March 21, 1957, Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, March 22nd -- Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to Pakistani Prime Minister Suhrawardy, congratulating him on the National Day of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan. The full text is as follows:
    To His Excellency H. S. Suhrawardy, Prime Minister of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan:
    On the occasion of the National Day of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, I extend my warm congratulations to the Government of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan and to Your Excellency on behalf of the Chinese Government and in my own name.
    In the past year, with the increasingly frequent contacts between China and Pakistan, especially the mutual visits of leaders of the two countries, the friendship between the two countries has been further enhanced. I sincerely hope that the friendly relations between China and Pakistan will continue to develop, thereby making greater contributions to the cause of promoting friendship and unity among Asian countries and world peace.
      Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, March 21, 1957, Beijing

### 毛主席和周总理　电贺巴基斯坦国庆

1957-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席和周总理
　　电贺巴基斯坦国庆
    新华社22日讯　毛泽东主席致电巴基斯坦总统米尔扎，祝贺巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国国庆，全文如下：巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国总统伊斯坎德尔·米尔扎先生阁下：
    值此巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国国庆之际，我谨代表中国人民，并且以我个人的名义向阁下和巴基斯坦人民致以诚挚的祝贺。祝巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国日益繁荣，巴基斯坦人民日益幸福，并祝阁下身体健康。
            中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东　1957年3月21日于北京
    新华社22日讯　周恩来总理致电巴基斯坦总理苏拉瓦底，祝贺巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国国庆，全文如下：巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国总理侯·沙·苏拉瓦底先生阁下：
    值此巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国国庆之际，我谨代表中国政府，并且以我个人的名义向巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国政府和阁下致以热烈的祝贺。
    在过去一年中，随着中巴两国间日益频繁的接触，特别是两国领导人员的相互访问，两国间的友谊已经有了进一步的增进。我衷心地希望：中巴两国间的友好关系将继续不断地发展，从而在促进亚洲各国友好团结和世界和平的事业中作出更大的贡献。
      中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来　1957年3月21日于北京
