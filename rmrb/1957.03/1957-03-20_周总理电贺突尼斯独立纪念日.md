Title: Premier Zhou Cables Congratulations on Tunisian Independence Day
Author:
Date: 1957-03-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 19th. Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to Tunisian Prime Minister Habib Bourguiba on March 18th, congratulating him on Tunisian Independence Day. The text of the telegram is as follows:

His Excellency Habib Bourguiba, Prime Minister of Tunisia:

On the occasion of Tunisian Independence Day, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to the Tunisian government, the Tunisian people, and Your Excellency on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people, and in my own name. I wish the Tunisian people new achievements in the cause of building an independent and prosperous country, and wish the friendly relations between our two countries grow stronger day by day.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Beijing, March 18, 1957

### 周总理电贺突尼斯独立纪念日

1957-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电贺突尼斯独立纪念日
    新华社19日讯　周恩来总理18日致电突尼斯首相哈比布·布尔吉巴，祝贺突尼斯独立纪念日。电文如下：突尼斯首相哈比布·布尔吉巴阁下：
    值此突尼斯独立纪念日之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和中国人民，并且以我个人名义向突尼斯政府、突尼斯人民和阁下表示热诚的祝贺。祝突尼斯人民在建设独立繁荣的国家的事业中获得新的成就，祝我们两国之间的友好关系日益发展。
      中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
        1957年3月18日于北京
