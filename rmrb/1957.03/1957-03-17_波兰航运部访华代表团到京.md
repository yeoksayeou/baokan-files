Title: Polish Ministry of Shipping Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-03-17
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 16th. The Polish Ministry of Shipping delegation, headed by Polish Minister of Shipping, Darski, arrived in Beijing by plane today. Minister of Communications Zhang Bojun and others welcomed them at the airport.
    Charge d'Affaires ad interim Dubnitzky of the Polish Embassy also came to the airport to greet them.
    The Polish Ministry of Shipping delegation is visiting China at the invitation of Zhang Bojun, Minister of Communications of the People's Republic of China. Some members of the delegation have already arrived in Beijing.

### 波兰航运部访华代表团到京

1957-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰航运部访华代表团到京
    新华社16日讯　以波兰航运部部长达尔斯基为首的访华代表团，今天乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有交通部部长章伯钧等人。
    波兰大使馆临时代办邓布尼茨基也到机场迎接。
    波兰航运部访华代表团是应中华人民共和国交通部部长章伯钧邀请来我国访问的。代表团部分团员已先行到达北京。
