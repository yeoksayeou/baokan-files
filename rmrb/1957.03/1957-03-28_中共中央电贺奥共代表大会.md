CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Message to Austrian Communist Party Congress

(Our dispatch) The 17th National Congress of the Communist Party of Austria is scheduled to be held from March 28th to 31st. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a congratulatory message to the congress. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Austria, and to all comrades of the 17th National Congress of the Communist Party of Austria:

As the Communist Party of Austria convenes its 17th National Congress, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, on behalf of all its members and the people of the whole country, extends to you warm fraternal congratulations.

The Communist Party of China and the Chinese people are following with great concern and sympathy the staunch struggle waged by your party and your people for peace, democracy, and socialism. The Communist Party of China sincerely wishes that your congress will achieve new successes in consolidating the unity of the party, consolidating the unity of the working class and all patriotic and democratic forces, consolidating the internationalist unity of the world working class, in leading the broad masses of working people to defend the social achievements already won, safeguard national independence, adhere to national neutrality, guarantee European collective security, and strive for world peace.

Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
March 25, 1957

### 中共中央电贺奥共代表大会

1957-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

　  中共中央电贺奥共代表大会
    本报讯　奥地利共产党第十七次全国代表大会订于3月28日到31日举行。中国共产党中央委员会致电大会表示祝贺。电文如下：奥地利共产党中央委员会转奥地利共产党第十七次全国代表大会全体同志们：
    正当奥地利共产党召开第十七次全国代表大会的时候，中国共产党中央委员会谨代表全体党员和全国人民向你们致以热烈的兄弟般的祝贺。
    中国共产党和中国人民以极大的关怀和同情，注视着你们的党和你们的人民为争取和平、民主和社会主义而进行的坚强的斗争。中国共产党谨祝你们的代表大会在巩固党的团结、巩固工人阶级和一切爱国民主力量的团结、巩固世界工人阶级国际主义的团结的事业上，在领导广大劳动人民保卫既得的社会成果、维护民族的独立、坚持国家的中立、保障欧洲集体安全和争取世界和平的斗争中，获得新的成就。
                       中国共产党中央委员会　1957年3月25日
