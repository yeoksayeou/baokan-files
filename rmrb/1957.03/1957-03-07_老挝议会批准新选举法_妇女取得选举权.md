Title: Lao Parliament Approves New Election Law, Women Obtain Right to Vote
Author: 
Date: 1957-03-07
Page: 6

    Lao Parliament Approves New Election Law
    Women Obtain Right to Vote
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 6th - According to TASS, Vientiane, 5th, the Lao Kingdom's National Assembly special session unanimously approved the draft of the new election law. According to this election law, women have also obtained the right to vote.

### 老挝议会批准新选举法  妇女取得选举权

1957-03-07
第6版()
专栏：

    老挝议会批准新选举法
    妇女取得选举权
    据新华社6日讯　据塔斯社河内5日讯，万象消息：老挝王国国民议会特别会议一致批准了新选举法草案。根据这个选举法，妇女也取得了选举权。
