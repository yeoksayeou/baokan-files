Title: Chu Tunan Hosts Banquet for Indian Dancers
Author:
Date: 1957-03-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 27th — Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the Indian dancers Lakshman sisters and other Indian artists who came to China for performance tours. Among those present at the banquet tonight were Chen Hansheng, Vice President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, dancer Dai Ailian, and Wu Xue, President of the China Youth Art Theater.
The wife of Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, and personnel from the Indian Embassy in China also attended the banquet.

### 楚图南欢宴印度舞蹈家

1957-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　楚图南欢宴印度舞蹈家
    新华社27日讯  中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南今晚设宴欢迎印度舞蹈家拉克希曼姐妹和一同前来中国访问演出的其他几位印度艺术家。出席今晚宴会的有中印友好协会副会长陈翰笙，舞蹈家戴爱莲，中国青年艺术剧院院长吴雪等。
    印度驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁的夫人和印度驻华大使馆人员也出席了宴会。
