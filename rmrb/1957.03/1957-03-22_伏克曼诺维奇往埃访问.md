Title: Vukmanović to Visit Egypt
Author:
Date: 1957-03-22
Page: 7

　　Vukmanović to Visit Egypt
    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Vukmanović, Vice-President of the Federal Executive Council of Yugoslavia, left Belgrade on the 20th to visit Egypt.

### 伏克曼诺维奇往埃访问

1957-03-22
第7版()
专栏：

　  伏克曼诺维奇往埃访问
    据新华社讯　南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席伏克曼诺维奇20日离开贝尔格莱德前往埃及访问。
