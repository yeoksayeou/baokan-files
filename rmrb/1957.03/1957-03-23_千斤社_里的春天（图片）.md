Title: Spring in the "Thousand Catty Cooperative" (Photos)
Author: Jin Jiahua Qian Yihua
Date: 1957-03-23
Page: 3

　　Spring in the "Thousand Catty Cooperative"
　　The 1,664 mu of paddy fields in the Shima Agricultural Cooperative of Caili Township, Yibin City, Sichuan Province, produced an average of more than 1,019 catties of grain per mu last year, earning the title of "Thousand Catty Cooperative." This cooperative has set a production increase target for this year: a 20% increase on last year's production.
　　To strive for an even greater harvest this year, cooperative members are actively participating in various spring plowing and preparation activities.
　　Top Left: Using waterwheels to draw winter water from low-lying areas into ponds to ensure irrigation water for more than 95% of the double-cropping rice fields.
　　Top: Reinforcing field ridges to prevent winter water loss.
　　Left: Transporting large quantities of fertilizer back from Yibin City.
　　　　　　　　　　Photo by Jin Jiahua and Qian Yihua (Sichuan Peasant Newspaper)

### “千斤社”里的春天（图片）
金嘉华　钱一华
1957-03-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　“千斤社”里的春天
    四川省宜宾市菜利乡石马农业社的一千六百六十四亩多稻田，去年平均每亩产粮食一千零一十九斤多，获得了“千斤社”的称号。这个社提出了今年的增产任务是：在去年的基础上增产20％。
    为了争取今年更大的丰收，社员们积极地参加了各项春耕备耕工作。左上　把低处的冬水车到塘里，来保证95％以上的双季稻的灌溉用水。上　加固田坎，防止冬水流失。左　从宜宾市区运回大批肥料。　　　　　　　　　　金嘉华　钱一华摄（四川农民报社稿）
