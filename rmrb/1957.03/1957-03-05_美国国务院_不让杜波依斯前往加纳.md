Title: U.S. State Department Prevents Du Bois from Traveling to Ghana
Author:
Date: 1957-03-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, March 2nd (TASS): According to the "Worker Daily" report, the U.S. State Department refused to issue passports to the famous American progressive Black scholar William Du Bois and his wife, the female writer Shirley Graham Du Bois, to attend Ghana's independence ceremony.

### 美国国务院  不让杜波依斯前往加纳

1957-03-05
第6版()
专栏：

    美国国务院
    不让杜波依斯前往加纳
    据新华社2日讯  塔斯社纽约2日讯：据“工人日报”报道，美国国务院拒绝发给美国著名进步黑人学者威廉·杜波依斯和他的妻子、女作家歇利·格雷姆·杜波依斯前往参加加纳独立典礼的出国护照。
