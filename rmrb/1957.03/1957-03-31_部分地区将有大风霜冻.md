Title: Strong Winds and Frost Expected in Some Areas
Author:
Date: 1957-03-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 30th. The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a strong wind and cooling forecast at 7:00 AM today:

It is expected that in the next 24 hours, the Bohai Sea and the northern part of the Yellow Sea will experience northerly winds of force 6 or 7, and the southern part of the Yellow Sea and the East China Sea will also change from southerly winds of about force 6 to northerly winds of force 6 or 7, with maximum wind force reaching force 8 on the sea surface; the lower reaches of the Yellow River, the Huai River basin, and the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River will experience northerly winds of force 5 or 6. In areas north of the main stream of the Huai River, there will be frost or frost damage in the mornings of the day after tomorrow when the wind stops and the sky clears.

### 部分地区将有大风霜冻

1957-03-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　部分地区将有大风霜冻
    新华社30日讯  中央气象科学研究所今天上午七点发布大风降温预告：
    预计未来二十四小时内，渤海和黄海北部将有六、七级偏北风，黄海南部和东海也将由六级左右的偏南风转为六、七级偏北风，海面上最大风力将达八级；黄河下游、淮河流域和长江中下游有五、六级偏北风。淮河主干以北地区，明后天早晨风停天晴时，将有霜或霜冻出现。
