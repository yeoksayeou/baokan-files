Title: A Chili Pepper Weighs Fourteen Ounces
Author: Jia Zicai
Date: 1957-03-11
Page: 3

    A Chili Pepper Weighs Fourteen Ounces
    Zuo Renjiang, the chief accountant of the Qianjin (Forward) Cooperative in Liaozhai Township, Suixian County, Hubei Province, planted three chili pepper trees that yielded a total of eighteen *jin* of chili peppers. The largest weighed fourteen ounces each, and the average weighed half a *jin*. He plans to keep these chili seeds for planting and continue cultivation. (Jia Zicai)

### 一个辣椒十四两重
加子才
1957-03-11
第3版()
专栏：农业珍闻

    一个辣椒十四两重
    湖北随县廖寨乡前进社总会计左仁江栽的三棵辣椒树，共结了十八斤辣子，大的每个有十四两重，一般的也有半斤重。他准备将这些辣椒籽留作种籽，继续培植。（加子才）
