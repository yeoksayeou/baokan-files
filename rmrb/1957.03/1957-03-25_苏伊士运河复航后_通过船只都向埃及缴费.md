Title: After the Suez Canal Reopened, Passing Ships Pay Fees to Egypt
Author:
Date: 1957-03-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 24th: Younis, head of the Egyptian Suez Canal Authority, said on the 23rd that since the canal was reopened to navigation, all ships passing through the canal have paid tolls to Egypt.

### 苏伊士运河复航后　通过船只都向埃及缴费

1957-03-25
第6版()
专栏：

　  苏伊士运河复航后
　　通过船只都向埃及缴费
    新华社24日讯　开罗消息：埃及苏伊士运河管理局负责人尤尼斯23日说，自从运河恢复通航以来，所有通过运河的船只都向埃及付了通行费。
