Title: Chinese Youth Delegation Visits Japan
Author:
Date: 1957-03-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: A ten-member Chinese youth delegation, led by head of delegation Liu Xiyuan, arrived in Tokyo by plane on the afternoon of the 23rd.

Nishiyama Naohide, Vice Chairman of the Japan Youth Organizations Council, representatives of many Japanese youth, women's organizations, and labor unions, representatives of Korean youth in Japan, and about 150 overseas Chinese in Japan welcomed the first Chinese youth delegation to visit Japan since the founding of New China at the airport.

All members of the Chinese Youth Delegation attended a welcome banquet held by the Japan Youth Organizations Council on the evening of March 24th.

On the morning of the 25th, the Chinese Youth Delegation left Tokyo to visit Itako Town in Ibaraki Prefecture. When the Chinese Youth Delegation arrived in Itako by boat, 1,500 people welcomed them at the pier. These Japanese youth, women, and students waved Chinese and Japanese flags and sang the song "Tokyo-Beijing."

### 我青年代表团在日本访问

1957-03-27
第5版()
专栏：

　　我青年代表团在日本访问
    据新华社讯　中国青年访日代表团一行十人，在团长刘西元率领下，23日下午乘飞机到达东京。
    日本青年团协议会副会长西山尚秀和日本许多青年、妇女团体和工会的代表、旅日朝鲜青年的代表以及旅日华侨约一百五十人到机场欢迎新中国成立后第一个访问日本的中国青年代表团。
    中国青年代表团全体人员3月24日晚上应邀出席了日本青年团协议会举行的欢迎宴会。
    中国青年代表团25日上午离开东京到茨城县的潮来镇访问。中国青年代表团坐船到达潮来的时候，有一千五百人在码头欢迎。这些日本青年、妇女和学生们摇着中日两国国旗，唱着“东京—北京”歌。
