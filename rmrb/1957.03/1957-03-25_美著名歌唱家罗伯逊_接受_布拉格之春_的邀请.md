Title: Prominent American Singer Robeson Accepts Invitation to "Prague Spring"

Author:

Date: 1957-03-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 24th: The famous African-American singer Paul Robeson has accepted the invitation to participate in the 1957 (12th) "Prague Spring" International Music Festival.

In a telegram to the International Music Festival Committee, Robeson expressed his belief that the festival will greatly contribute to the understanding of the importance of cultural exchange between the people of all countries, and will be a strong support for the cause of peace and friendship among the people of all countries participating in the festival.

### 美著名歌唱家罗伯逊　接受“布拉格之春”的邀请

1957-03-25
第5版()
专栏：

　  美著名歌唱家罗伯逊
　　接受“布拉格之春”的邀请
    新华社布拉格24日电　美国著名黑人歌唱家保罗·罗伯逊已经接受了参加1957年（第十二届）“布拉格之春”国际音乐节的邀请。
    罗伯逊在拍给国际音乐节委员会的电报中表示相信，音乐节将大大有助于对各国人民之间文化交流的重要性的了解，而且对所有参加音乐节的国家的人民间和平和友谊的事业将是有力的支持。
