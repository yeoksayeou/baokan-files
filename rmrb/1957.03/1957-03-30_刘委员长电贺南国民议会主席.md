Title: Chairman Liu Sends Congratulatory Telegram to the President of the Yugoslav National Assembly
Author:
Date: 1957-03-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 29th: Chairman Liu Shaoqi's telegram to Peter Stambolic, President of the National Assembly of Yugoslavia, reads in full:
Comrade Peter Stambolic, President of the Federal National Assembly of Yugoslavia:

　　I am delighted to learn of your election as President of the Federal National Assembly of Yugoslavia, and I extend my warm congratulations to you on behalf of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China and in my own name.

　　I wish you new achievements in the cause of socialist construction in Yugoslavia.

　　Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi
　　March 27, 1957

### 刘委员长电贺南国民议会主席

1957-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　刘委员长电贺南国民议会主席
    新华社29日讯  刘少奇委员长致南斯拉夫国民议会主席彼得·斯坦鲍利奇的贺电，全文如下：南斯拉夫联邦国民议会主席彼得·斯坦鲍利奇同志：
    欣悉您当选为南斯拉夫联邦国民议会主席，我谨代表中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会并以我个人的名义向您致热烈的祝贺。
    祝您在南斯拉夫社会主义建设事业中获得新的成就。
      中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长  刘少奇
                                           1957年3月27日
