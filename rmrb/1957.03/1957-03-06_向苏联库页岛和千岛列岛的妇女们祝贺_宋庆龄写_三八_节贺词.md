Title: Soong Ching-ling Writes Greetings for "March 8th" International Women's Day, Extending Congratulations to Women of Soviet Sakhalin and the Kuril Islands
Author:
Date: 1957-03-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 5th – Soong Ching-ling wrote a congratulatory message for the women of Soviet Sakhalin and the Kuril Islands on the occasion of "March 8th" International Women's Day. This message was written at the request of Comrade Girzhiko, Chairman of the Trade Union Committee of the Sakhalin State Organs. The full text of the congratulatory message is as follows:

    Dear Sisters of Sakhalin and the Kuril Islands:

    On the occasion of "March 8th" International Women's Day, first of all, please allow me to represent all the sisters of China and, in my personal name, congratulate you on the brilliant achievements you have made in the past year in the struggle for the liberation of women and the defense of world peace!

    We see that the imperialists are trying every possible means to launch wars of aggression, but the united strength of women all over the world is one of the main factors in defeating all the schemes and tricks of the aggressors.

    Dear sisters! Let us contribute all our strength to the struggle for the complete liberation of women and the protection of children's rights, and for the defense of world peace!

    I wish you a more fulfilling and happier life!

        Soong Ching-ling
        Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China
        President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
        Honorary President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation

### 向苏联库页岛和千岛列岛的妇女们祝贺  宋庆龄写“三八”节贺词

1957-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

    向苏联库页岛和千岛列岛的妇女们祝贺
    宋庆龄写“三八”节贺词
    新华社5日讯　宋庆龄为苏联库页岛和千岛列岛的妇女们写了一篇祝贺“三八”国际妇女节的贺词，这篇贺词是应库页岛国家机关工会委员会主席吉日科的请求而作的。贺词全文如下：亲爱的库页岛和千岛列岛的姊妹们：
    值此“三八”国际妇女节之际，首先，请允许我代表全中国的姊妹们；并以我个人的名义，祝贺你们在过去一年中，为争取妇女的解放；和保卫世界和平的斗争中所获得的光辉成就！
    我们看到，帝国主义者正在千方百计地想要发动侵略战争，但是，全世界妇女的团结一致的力量，是击败一切侵略者的阴谋诡计的主要因素之一。
    亲爱的姊妹们！让我们为争取妇女的彻底解放和保卫儿童的权利；为保卫世界和平，贡献出自己的一切力量吧！
    祝你们的生活过得更美满，更幸福！	中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长	中苏友好协会总会会长	中华全国民主妇女联合会名誉主席  宋庆龄
