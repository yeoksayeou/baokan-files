Title: List of Appointments by the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress

Author:
Date: 1957-03-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 27th – The following is the list of appointments approved by the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress:

List of Procurators of the Supreme People's Procuratorate (seven people, listed in order of the number of strokes in their surnames):

　　Yu Chenggjiu, Wang Bin, Li Yuan, Gao Huamin, Gao Zhengquan, Lou Tingbo, Qian Feng
