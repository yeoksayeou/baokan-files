Title: Marshal Nie Rongzhen Departs Ghana for Return to China
Author:
Date: 1957-03-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Accra, 7th: Marshal Nie Rongzhen, the special envoy of the People's Republic of China to the independence celebration of Ghana, left Accra today for his return to China.

### 聂荣臻元帅离加纳回国

1957-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

    聂荣臻元帅离加纳回国
    新华社阿克拉7日电　中华人民共和国参加加纳庆祝独立典礼特使聂荣臻元帅今天离开阿克拉回国。
