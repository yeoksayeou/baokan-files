Title: Chinese People's Committee for World Peace Cables Support to the People of Ceylon, Protesting British Nuclear Weapons Tests on Christmas Island
Author:
Date: 1957-03-02
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 1st. The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace today sent a telegram to the Ceylon Peace Council, forwarding it to the Ceylon People's Assembly protesting the British government's nuclear weapons tests on Christmas Island, supporting the struggle of the Ceylon people.
    The telegram stated that the Chinese people consistently advocate the prohibition of the use and testing of nuclear weapons, and harbor deep sympathy for the Japanese people who have suffered profound disasters from American atomic bomb and hydrogen bomb tests. The Chinese people firmly support the Soviet government's proposal to prohibit atomic weapons and hydrogen weapons and, first of all, to prohibit the testing of these weapons, and are willing to work together with the people of Ceylon, Asia, and the world to strive for an international agreement on the prohibition of atomic weapons and hydrogen weapons and to defend world peace.

### 我保卫世界和平委员会致电支持锡兰人民  抗议英国在圣诞岛试验核子武器

1957-03-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我保卫世界和平委员会致电支持锡兰人民
    抗议英国在圣诞岛试验核子武器
    新华社1日讯  中国人民保卫世界和平委员会，今天打电报给锡兰和平理事会转锡兰人民抗议英国政府在圣诞岛试验核子武器大会，支持锡兰人民的斗争。
    电报说，中国人民一贯主张禁止核子武器的使用和试验，并对曾经遭受过美国原子弹轰炸和氢弹试验深重灾害的日本人民抱深厚的同情。中国人民坚决拥护苏联政府关于禁止原子武器氢武器并首先禁止这些武器的试验的建议，并愿与锡兰人民及亚洲和全世界人民一起，为争取就禁止原子武器氢武器问题达成国际协议，保卫世界和平而共同努力。
