Title: Cyrankiewicz to Visit North Korea in April
Author:
Date: 1957-03-13
Page: 6

    Cyrankiewicz to Visit North Korea in April
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 12th - At the invitation of the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, a government delegation of the Polish People's Republic, headed by Chairman of the Council of Ministers Cyrankiewicz, will visit Korea in April this year.

### 西伦凯维兹四月访问朝鲜

1957-03-13
第6版()
专栏：

    西伦凯维兹四月访问朝鲜
    新华社平壤12日电　应朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府的邀请，以部长会议主席西伦凯维兹为首的波兰人民共和国政府代表团将在今年4月访问朝鲜。
