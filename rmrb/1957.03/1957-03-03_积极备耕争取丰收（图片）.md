Title: Actively Prepare for Farming and Strive for a Bumper Harvest (Picture)
Author: Xiao Feng
Date: 1957-03-03

    Actively Prepare for Farming and Strive for a Bumper Harvest
    The members of the Qunfeng Agricultural Cooperative in Yuyao County, Zhejiang Province, are stepping up their preparations for farming to strive for an even greater harvest this year. Left picture: Members are picking out miscellaneous grains and barnyard grass seeds from the high-quality rice seeds. Right picture: Repairing farm tools.
