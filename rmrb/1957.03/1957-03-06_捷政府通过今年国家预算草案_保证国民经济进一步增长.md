Title: Czechoslovak Government Approves Draft National Budget for This Year, Guaranteeing Further Growth of the National Economy
Author:
Date: 1957-03-06
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 5th: The Czechoslovak News Agency reports that the Czechoslovak government recently held a meeting and approved the draft national budget for 1957, and discussed issues related to decentralizing central power and streamlining institutions.

According to the draft budget, the total national budget revenue for 1957 will increase by 8.8% compared to last year, and expenditures will increase by 8.9% compared to last year. Among them, expenditures for economic, cultural, and social welfare aspects will increase compared to last year, while expenditures for national defense and administration will decrease compared to last year.

The new budget will guarantee the implementation of the 1957 national economic plan. According to the 1957 national plan, industrial production this year will increase by 7.8% compared to 1956. Among them, the production of means of production will increase by 8.1%, and the production of consumer goods will increase by 7.3%.

The industrial growth rate in the Slovak region will still be faster than in the Czech region, increasing by 9% this year.

The total investment in basic construction throughout the country will increase by 13%. Among them, housing construction investment will increase by approximately 21%.

The total value of foreign trade in 1957 will increase by 11%, national income will increase by 7%, and personal consumption will increase by 8.6%. The labor force participating in the national economy will increase by 2.6%.

The meeting decided that the number of staff in government ministries and central agencies will be reduced by 13,000 this year.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague: The Czechoslovak News Agency reports that Czechoslovak Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Kozor said at the Commercial Workers' Union Congress held in Prague that the total value of Czechoslovak foreign trade in 1956 reached 19 billion crowns (Czech currency), an increase of 16% compared to 1955 and 80% compared to 1948.

Last year, the export and import volumes of foreign trade increased by 19.2% and 12.6% respectively. The trade volume with capitalist countries increased by 33.2% compared to the previous year; this is the result of developing trade relations with underdeveloped countries.

Kozor pointed out that trade with socialist countries remains the foundation of Czechoslovakia's foreign trade, and last year the trade volume with socialist countries accounted for 64.3% of the total foreign trade volume.

Kozor also said that last year, the export volume of machinery industry products accounted for 40% of the total export volume, while before the war, the export volume in this area only accounted for 6.3%. In terms of imports, raw materials accounted for more than 50% of the total import volume, ranking first, followed by agricultural products and grain.

It is expected that the foreign trade volume will increase by 11% this year. The trade volume with socialist countries will increase by 8%, and the trade volume with capitalist countries will increase by 16%.

### 捷政府通过今年国家预算草案  保证国民经济进一步增长

1957-03-06
第5版()
专栏：

    捷政府通过今年国家预算草案
    保证国民经济进一步增长
    新华社布拉格5日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道：捷克斯洛伐克政府最近举行会议，通过了1957年国家预算草案，并且讨论了有关分散中央权力和精简机构的问题。
    根据预算草案，1957年国家预算收入总额将比去年增加8.8％，支出比去年将增加8.9％，其中用于经济、文化和社会福利方面的开支将比去年增加，用于国防和行政方面的开支将比去年减少。
    新预算将保证1957年国民经济计划的执行。按照1957年国家计划规定，今年的工业生产将比1956年增加7.8％。其中生产资料生产将增加8.1％，消费品生产将增加7.3％。
    斯洛伐克地区的工业增长速度仍然将比捷克地区为快，今年将增加9％。
    整个国家基本建设投资总额将增加13％。其中房屋建筑投资大约将增加21％。
    1957年对外贸易总额将增加11％，国民收入将增加7％，个人消费将提高8.6％。参加国民经济工作的劳动力将增加2.6％。
    会议决定，今年政府各部和中央机关的职员人数将精减一万三千人。
    据新华社布拉格电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道，捷克斯洛伐克对外贸易部副部长科佐尔在布拉格举行的商业工会代表大会上说，1956年捷克斯洛伐克对外贸易总值达到了一百九十亿克朗（捷币），这比1955年增加了16％，比1948年增加了80％。
    去年，对外贸易的输出额和输入额分别增加了19.2％和12.6％。同资本主义国家的贸易额比前一年增加了33.2％；这是开展同不发达国家的贸易关系的结果。
    科佐尔指出，同社会主义国家的贸易仍然是捷克斯洛伐克对外贸易的基础，去年同社会主义国家的贸易额占对外贸易总额的64.3％。
    科佐尔又说，去年，机械工业产品的输出量占总输出量的40％，而战前这方面的输出量只占6.3％。在输入方面，原料占总输入量的50％以上，居第一位，农产品和粮食占第二位。
    预计，今年对外贸易量将增加11％。同社会主义国家的贸易量增加8％，同资本主义国家的贸易量增加16％。
