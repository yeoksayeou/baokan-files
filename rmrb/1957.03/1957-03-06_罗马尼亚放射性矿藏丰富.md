Title: Romania Rich in Radioactive Mineral Deposits
Author:
Date: 1957-03-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, March 5th: Romanian geologists, after several years of exploration, have discovered that Romania is one of the richest countries in Europe in terms of radioactive mineral deposits. Recently, minerals containing uranium, titanium-thorium, molybdenum, and phosphorus have been discovered in some areas.

In addition, in the iron ore field, geologists have also discovered important ores containing manganese and iron, and have found new iron ore and manganese ore fields. In this country, which ranks second in Europe in terms of oil production, coal seams with reserves of hundreds of millions of tons and new salt deposits with reserves of billions of tons have recently been discovered.

### 罗马尼亚放射性矿藏丰富

1957-03-06
第5版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚放射性矿藏丰富
    据新华社布加勒斯特5日电  罗马尼亚的地质学家经过几年的勘察工作，发现罗马尼亚在放射性矿物的蕴藏方面，是欧洲最富饶的国家之一。最近，在一些地区已经发现了含有铀、钛钍、钼、磷的矿物。
    此外，在铁矿苗方面，地质学家们还发现了含有锰和铁的重要矿石，并且找到了新的铁矿苗和锰矿苗。在这个石油产量占欧洲第二位的国家中，最近又发现了蕴藏量达几亿吨的煤层和几十亿吨的新的盐产地。
