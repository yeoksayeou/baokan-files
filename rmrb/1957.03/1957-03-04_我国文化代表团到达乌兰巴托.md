Title: China Cultural Delegation Arrives in Ulaanbaatar
Author:
Date: 1957-03-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 2nd—The Chinese cultural delegation led by Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Minister of Culture, arrived in Ulaanbaatar by train today. The delegation will discuss the 1957 Sino-Mongolian cultural cooperation plan with the Mongolian side.

### 我国文化代表团到达乌兰巴托

1957-03-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我国文化代表团到达乌兰巴托
    新华社乌兰巴托2日电  由文化部副部长张致祥带领的中国文化代表团今天坐火车到达乌兰巴托。代表团将和蒙古方面商谈1957年中蒙文化合作计划。
