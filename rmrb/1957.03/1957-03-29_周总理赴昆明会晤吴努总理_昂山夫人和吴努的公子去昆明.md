Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Goes to Kunming to Meet Premier U Nu; Daw Aung San and U Nu's Son Go to Kunming
Author:
Date: 1957-03-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 28th: Premier Zhou Enlai took a plane to Kunming via Chengdu this morning to meet with the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu.

Xinhua News Agency, March 28th: Daw Aung San (Daw Khin Kyi) and Prime Minister U Nu's son left Beijing by plane for Kunming this morning. Cao Mengjun, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and the wife of Yao Zhongming, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, accompanied them.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Li Dequan, Shi Liang, and Xu Guangping, Vice-Presidents of the National Women's Federation, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and his wife, Qi Yanming, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council, Zheng Zhenduo, President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, Cheng Hongyi, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and the heads of various women's organizations in Beijing.

The wife of the Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung, and the Chargé d'Affaires of the Burmese Embassy in China, U Ba Maung, and his wife also saw them off at the airport.

Last night, the Chargé d'Affaires of the Burmese Embassy in China, U Ba Maung, and his wife held a banquet for Daw Aung San and Prime Minister U Nu's son's visit to Beijing.

### 周总理赴昆明会晤吴努总理　昂山夫人和吴努的公子去昆明

1957-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　周总理赴昆明会晤吴努总理
　　昂山夫人和吴努的公子去昆明
    新华社28日讯  周恩来总理今天上午乘飞机取道成都前往昆明，同缅甸联邦吴努总理会见。
    据新华社28日讯  昂山夫人（都庆枝）和吴努总理的公子今天上午乘飞机离开北京去昆明。中华全国民主妇女联合会书记处书记曹孟君和中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明的夫人陪同前往。
    到机场送行的有全国妇联副主席李德全、史良、许广平、外交部副部长章汉夫和夫人、国务院副秘书长齐燕铭、中缅友好协会会长郑振铎、北京市副市长程宏毅以及在京的各妇女团体的负责人。
    缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂的夫人、缅甸驻华大使馆临时代办吴巴茂和夫人也到机场送行。
    昨晚，缅甸驻华大使馆临时代办吴巴茂和夫人曾为昂山夫人和吴努总理的公子来北京访问举行宴会。
