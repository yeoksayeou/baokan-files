Title: Czechoslovak United Agricultural Cooperative Congress Concludes; Decides to Strive for Greater Achievements in Cooperativization

Author:

Date: 1957-03-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 25th. The Third Congress of Czechoslovak United Agricultural Cooperatives concluded in Prague on March 24th. Participants at the congress discussed the report by Czechoslovak Minister of Agriculture and Forestry, Bakula, and adopted a resolution.

The resolution stated: "The congress affirms that uniting small and medium farmers into unified agricultural cooperatives, thereby transitioning our rural areas to socialism, is correct. United agricultural cooperatives have made tremendous contributions to the development of agricultural production in recent years. Therefore, the congress points out that we must strive for greater achievements in agricultural cooperativization. This is the fundamental condition for continuously improving agricultural production and raising the material living standards of farmers."

The resolution emphasized that by the end of the Second Five-Year Plan, i.e., in 1960, the socialist sector in agriculture should occupy a decisive advantage. By that time, agricultural production should increase by 30% compared to 1955.

### 捷统一农业合作社代表大会闭幕　决在合作化方面争取更大成绩

1957-03-26
第5版()
专栏：

　　捷统一农业合作社代表大会闭幕
　　决在合作化方面争取更大成绩
    新华社布拉格25日电　捷克斯洛伐克统一农业合作社第三次代表大会3月24日在布拉格闭幕。大会参加者讨论了捷克斯洛伐克农林部长巴库拉的报告，并且通过了一项决议。
    决议说：“大会确认，把小农和中农联合在统一农业合作社内，从而使我国农村过渡到社会主义是正确的。统一农业合作社最近几年对发展农业生产有着巨大的贡献。因此，大会指出，在农业合作化方面必须争取更大的成绩。这是不断提高农业生产、提高农民物质生活的基本条件”。
    决议强调指出，到第二个五年计划结束的时候，即在1960年，农业中的社会主义部分应当占决定性的优势。到那个时候，农业生产应当比1955年增长30％。
