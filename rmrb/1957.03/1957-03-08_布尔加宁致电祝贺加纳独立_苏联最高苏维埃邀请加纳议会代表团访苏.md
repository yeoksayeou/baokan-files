Title: Bulganin Sends Congratulations on Ghana's Independence; Soviet Supreme Soviet Invites Ghanaian Parliamentary Delegation to Visit the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1957-03-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 7th, via TASS: Soviet Premier Bulganin sent a telegram to Ghanaian Prime Minister Kwame Nkrumah, congratulating the government and the entire people of Ghana on their country's independence. Bulganin said: "I sincerely wish you and the people of Ghana success and prosperity on the road to independence and progress."

Xinhua News Agency, Accra, March 6th, via TASS: The Soviet Union has formally invited an eight- to ten-member Ghanaian parliamentary delegation to visit the Soviet Union.

On the 4th, Benediktov, head of the Soviet delegation attending the celebration of Ghana's independence, presented congratulatory letters from Lobanov, Chairman of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet, and Lacis, Chairman of the Soviet of Nationalities, to Emmanuel Quist, Speaker of the Ghanaian Legislative Assembly. The letters contained the invitation. The congratulatory letters also stated that the Soviet Supreme Soviet hopes to establish direct contact with the Ghanaian Legislative Assembly.

### 布尔加宁致电祝贺加纳独立  苏联最高苏维埃邀请加纳议会代表团访苏

1957-03-08
第6版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁致电祝贺加纳独立
    苏联最高苏维埃邀请加纳议会代表团访苏
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁拍电报给加纳总理恩克鲁玛，向加纳政府以及全体人民祝贺他们的国家获得独立。布尔加宁说：“我衷心地祝您和加纳人民在独立和进步的道路上获得成就和繁荣。”
    新华社6日讯　据塔斯社阿克拉讯：苏联正式邀请一个由八人到十人组成的加纳议会代表团访问苏联。
    4日，苏联出席加纳独立庆祝典礼的代表团团长别涅吉克托夫把苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席洛巴诺夫和民族院主席拉齐斯的贺信交给了加纳立法议会议长艾曼伊尔·克威斯特，信中提出了这项邀请。贺信还说，苏联最高苏维埃希望同加纳的立法议会建立直接联系。
