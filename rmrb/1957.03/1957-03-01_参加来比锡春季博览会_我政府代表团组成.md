Title: Composition of Our Government Delegation to Attend the Leipzig Spring Fair
Author:
Date: 1957-03-01
Page: 5

    Our Government Delegation to Attend the Leipzig Spring Fair Composed
    Xinhua News Agency, 27th. Our government delegation to attend the opening ceremony of the 1957 Leipzig Spring Fair to be held on March 2nd has been formed. The head of the delegation is Zeng Yongquan, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the German Democratic Republic. The members are Zhao Weichang, Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China to the German Democratic Republic, and Xie Qin, head of the Exhibition Group of the People's Republic of China to the Leipzig Spring Fair.
    In addition to attending the opening ceremony of the fair, the delegation will also visit various parts of the German Democratic Republic.

### 参加来比锡春季博览会  我政府代表团组成

1957-03-01
第5版()
专栏：

    参加来比锡春季博览会
    我政府代表团组成
    新华社27日讯  我国参加将在3月2日举行的1957年来比锡春季博览会开幕仪式的政府代表团业已组成。代表团团长为中华人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国特命全权大使曾涌泉，团员为中华人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国大使馆商务参赞赵维昌和中华人民共和国参加来比锡春季博览会展览团团长谢钦。
    代表团除参加博览会开幕仪式外，还将在德意志民主共和国各地参观。
