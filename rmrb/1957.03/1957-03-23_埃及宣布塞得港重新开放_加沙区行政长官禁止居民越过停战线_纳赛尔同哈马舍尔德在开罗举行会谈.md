Title: Egypt Announces Reopening of Port Said; Gaza District Administrator Forbids Residents from Crossing Armistice Line; Nasser and Hammarskjöld Hold Talks in Cairo
Author:
Date: 1957-03-23
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, March 22nd Dispatch: Cairo News: Egyptian authorities officially announced on the evening of the 21st that Port Said would be reopened to all ships from the 21st.

The Egyptian Ports and Lighthouses Administration officially notified all shipping companies that all ships would be permitted to enter and exit Port Said harbor from sunrise to 5:00 PM starting from the 21st.

Xinhua News Agency, March 22nd Dispatch: Gaza City News: The Egyptian Administrative Governor of the Gaza Strip, Latif, issued an order on the 20th prohibiting any residents of the Gaza Strip from going to areas within 300 yards of the armistice line, and forbidding them from crossing the armistice line for any reason.

Another report said that a portion of the barracks of the United Nations Emergency Force stationed in the Gaza Strip had been removed on the 20th, and the United Nations Emergency Force stationed on the armistice line between the Gaza Strip and Israel had been increased.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 22nd: Egyptian President Nasser and United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld held talks in Cairo on the afternoon of the 21st.

United Nations Under-Secretary-General Bunche, Egyptian Foreign Minister Fawzi, and Director of the Political Bureau of the Egyptian Presidential Palace, Sabri, attended the meeting held at Nasser's residence.

Hammarskjöld's press officer, Smith, told reporters that Hammarskjöld would discuss many issues with Nasser, including the Suez Canal issue and the activities of the United Nations Emergency Force.

### 埃及宣布塞得港重新开放　加沙区行政长官禁止居民越过停战线　纳赛尔同哈马舍尔德在开罗举行会谈

1957-03-23
第6版()
专栏：

　　埃及宣布塞得港重新开放
　　加沙区行政长官禁止居民越过停战线
  　纳赛尔同哈马舍尔德在开罗举行会谈
    据新华社22日讯　开罗消息：埃及当局21日晚间正式宣布，塞得港从21日起向一切船只重新开放。
    埃及港口和灯塔管理局正式通知各航运公司说，从21日起许可一切船只从日出到下午五时驶入和驶出塞得港港口。
    据新华社22日讯　加沙城消息：埃及驻加沙地带行政长官拉蒂夫20日发布命令，禁止加沙地带的任何居民到离停战线三百码以内的地区去，并且无论有什么理由都不准越过停战线。
    另有消息说，驻在加沙地带内的联合国紧急部队的营房在20日已经撤除一部分，驻在加沙地带与以色列之间停战线上的联合国紧急部队已有增加。
    据新华社开罗22日电　埃及总统纳赛尔和联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德21日下午在开罗举行了会谈。
    联合国副秘书长本奇、埃及外交部长法齐和埃及总统府政治局长萨布里出席了这次在纳赛尔的官邸中举行的会议。
    哈马舍尔德的新闻发布官史密斯对新闻记者说，哈马舍尔德将和纳赛尔讨论许多问题，包括苏伊士运河问题和联合国紧急部队的活动问题在内。
