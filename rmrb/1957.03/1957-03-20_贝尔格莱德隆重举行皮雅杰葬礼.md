Title: Belgrade Holds Solemn Funeral for Pijade
Author:
Date: 1957-03-20
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, March 19th: A solemn ceremony was held in Belgrade on the afternoon of the 18th to bury the remains of the late President of the Federal People's Assembly of Yugoslavia, Pijade.

For two days before the funeral, from early morning until late at night, tens of thousands of people entered the parliament building where the coffin was placed to pay their final respects to Pijade. On the 17th, representatives from the Chinese and other countries' embassies in Yugoslavia also paid their respects before Pijade's remains.

At 2:00 PM on the 18th, Yugoslav President Tito and members of the Central Executive Committee of the League of Communists carried Pijade's coffin onto the hearse. The funeral procession moved towards the Cemetery of National Heroes. When Pijade's remains were buried at the cemetery, President Tito delivered a eulogy at the graveside.

### 贝尔格莱德隆重举行皮雅杰葬礼

1957-03-20
第7版()
专栏：

    贝尔格莱德隆重举行皮雅杰葬礼
    据新华社贝尔格莱德19日电18日下午，贝尔格莱德人以隆重的仪式安葬南斯拉夫联邦国民议会故主席皮雅杰的遗体。
    在葬礼举行以前的两天，每天从清晨到深夜都有成千上万的人走进停放灵柩的议会大厦，向皮雅杰作最后告别。17日，中国和其他一些国家驻南斯拉夫大使馆的代表也到皮雅杰的灵前致哀。
    18日下午二时，南斯拉夫总统铁托和共产主义者联盟中央执行委员会的委员们把皮雅杰的灵柩抬上灵车。送葬的行列向民族英雄墓地进发。在墓地安葬皮雅杰的遗体的时候，铁托总统在墓前致了悼词。
