Title: Inner Mongolia Establishes Illiteracy Elimination Association
Author:
Date: 1957-03-28
Page: 7

　　Xinhua News Agency Report: Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Illiteracy Elimination Association was established on the 24th in Hohhot.
    There are more than three million illiterate young and middle-aged people of all nationalities in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, and they are eagerly demanding to learn culture. At present, there are 4,299 grassroots illiteracy elimination organizations throughout the region, with nearly 300,000 members. Many intellectuals, people from all walks of life, students, workers, and stationed troops enthusiastically participated in the work of the Illiteracy Elimination Association.

### 内蒙古成立扫盲协会

1957-03-28
第7版()
专栏：

　　内蒙古成立扫盲协会
    新华社讯　内蒙古自治区扫除文盲协会24日在呼和浩特成立。
    内蒙古自治区有各民族青壮年文盲三百多万人，他们迫切要求学习文化。现在全自治区扫盲基层组织已有四千二百九十九个，会员发展到将近三十万人。许多知识分子、社会人士、学生、职工和驻军热情地参加了扫盲协会的工作。
