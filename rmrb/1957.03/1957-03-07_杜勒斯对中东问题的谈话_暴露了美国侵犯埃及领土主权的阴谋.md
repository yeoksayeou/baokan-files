Title: Dulles' Talk on the Middle East Question Exposes US Conspiracy to Infringe on Egyptian Territorial Sovereignty
Author:
Date: 1957-03-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 6th: US Secretary of State Dulles said at a press conference on the 5th that the obligations undertaken by the United States regarding the Israeli withdrawal have been stated in public documents, and there are no other private guarantees. In answering a series of questions raised by reporters, Dulles explained that the United States believes the Strait of Tiran is an international waterway, and "the waterway must be open unless the International Court of Justice makes a contrary decision." At the same time, he admitted that "in a sense, the Strait of Tiran is territorial waters."

He said that after the Israeli withdrawal, the Suez Canal should be opened immediately and rapidly. He believes that an agreement should be reached on how to pay tolls and implement the principles of canal operation stipulated by the Security Council last October.

Dulles also emphasized that there should be no discrimination against British, French, and Israeli ships in the opening of the canal. He said that the issues to be resolved include: the resumption of navigation on the canal, the conditions for the resumption of navigation on the canal, the future status of the canal, and the treatment of American business people in the region in the future.

Referring to the upcoming Southeast Asia Treaty Organization meeting, Dulles said that the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization countries are "concerned" about the situation in Indonesia. Although Indonesia is not part of the treaty area, it is geographically part of Southeast Asia.

Dulles said that the US government is considering inviting Japanese Prime Minister Nobusuke Kishi to visit the United States, but no formal invitation has been extended yet.

Regarding the US government's ban on journalists visiting China, Dulles said that the US government's attitude has not changed, but "we are continuing to study and explore this issue to see if we can find a way to better meet the requirements of news coverage without appearing to generally lower the barriers or allow what the Chinese Communists call 'cultural exchanges'."

### 杜勒斯对中东问题的谈话  暴露了美国侵犯埃及领土主权的阴谋

1957-03-07
第6版()
专栏：

    杜勒斯对中东问题的谈话
    暴露了美国侵犯埃及领土主权的阴谋
    据新华社6日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国务卿杜勒斯5日在记者招待会上说，美国在以色列撤军上承担的义务已经在公开文件中表明，没有另外的私下保证。杜勒斯在回答记者提出的一连串问题时解释说，美国认为蒂朗海峡是一条国际水道，“航道必须开放，除非国际法院作出相反的决定”。同时，他承认，“在某种意义上说来蒂朗海峡是领海”。
    他说，在以色列撤军后，苏伊士运河应当马上迅速开放。他认为对于如何支付通行税和实现安全理事会去年10月所规定的运河经营的原则，应当达成一个协议。
    杜勒斯还强调，运河的开放对于英、法和以色列的船只不应该有所歧视。他说，有待解决的问题还有：运河恢复通航，运河恢复通航的条件，运河未来的地位和美国企业界人士将来在那个地区受到的待遇等。
    杜勒斯在谈到即将举行的东南亚条约组织会议时说，东南亚条约组织国家对于印度尼西亚的局势感到“关切”。印度尼西亚虽然不是这个条约地区的一部分，可是它在地理上是属于东南亚地区的。
    杜勒斯说，美国政府正在考虑邀请日本新首相岸信介访问美国，但是还没有正式邀请。
    谈到美国政府禁止记者访问中国的问题，杜勒斯说，美国政府这个态度还没有改变，但是“我们正在继续研究和探讨这个问题，看看是不是可以找出什么办法来更好地满足新闻报道的要求，而不致看起来好像是把壁垒普遍放低了，或者是准许进行中国共产党人所谓的‘文化交流’了。”
