Title: Soong Ching-ling Hosts Informal Dinner for Aung San's Wife
Author:
Date: 1957-03-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 24th. Soong Ching-ling, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Honorary President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, hosted an informal dinner at her home today for Aung San's wife (Daw Khin Kyi) and Prime Minister U Nu's son, who are visiting Beijing.

　　Vice-Chairman Soong Ching-ling and Aung San's wife had met several times in Rangoon. They chatted intimately like old friends. Vice-Chairman Soong Ching-ling told Aung San's wife that this visit was too short, and she hoped that next time Aung San's wife had the opportunity to visit China, she could stay for a few more days and visit child welfare work in various places.

　　Vice-Chairman Soong Ching-ling and Aung San's wife exchanged gifts. Vice-Chairman Soong also gave a gift to U Nu's son and asked him to pass a gift to Prime Minister U Nu's wife.

　　Cao Mengjun, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and the wife of Yao Zhongming, the Chinese Ambassador to Myanmar, were present at the dinner.

### 宋庆龄举行便宴招待昂山夫人

1957-03-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　宋庆龄举行便宴招待昂山夫人
    新华社24日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、中华全国民主妇女联合会名誉主席宋庆龄，今天午间在她家里举行便宴，招待前来北京访问的昂山夫人（都庆枝）和吴努总理的公子。
    宋庆龄副委员长和昂山夫人曾经在仰光多次见面。她们像老朋友一样亲密地交谈着。宋庆龄副委员长对昂山夫人说，这次访问太短促了，她希望下次昂山夫人有机会来中国访问时能够多逗留几天，多参观一下各地的儿童福利工作。
    宋庆龄副委员长和昂山夫人彼此赠送了礼物。宋副委员长还赠送礼物给吴努的公子，并且请他把一份礼物转送给吴努总理的夫人。
    今天出席作陪的有全国民主妇联书记处书记曹孟君和中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明的夫人。
