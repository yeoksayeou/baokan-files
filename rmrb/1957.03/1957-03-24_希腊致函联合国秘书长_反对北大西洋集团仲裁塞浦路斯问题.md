Title: Greece Sends Letter to UN Secretary-General Opposing North Atlantic Group Arbitration of Cyprus Issue
Author:
Date: 1957-03-24
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, New York, March 23rd: Greek Representative to the United Nations, Palamas, stated in a letter to Hammarskjöld on the 22nd that the Greek government cannot accept the British government's proposal to have the North Atlantic Treaty Organization arbitrate the Cyprus issue.

### 希腊致函联合国秘书长  反对北大西洋集团仲裁塞浦路斯问题

1957-03-24
第6版()
专栏：

    希腊致函联合国秘书长
    反对北大西洋集团仲裁塞浦路斯问题
    据新华社23日讯　纽约消息：希腊驻联合国代表帕拉马斯22日在给哈马舍尔德的信里声明，希腊政府不能接受英国政府提出的由北大西洋公约组织仲裁塞浦路斯问题的建议。
