Title: Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party Launches "Party Life" Magazine
Author:
Date: 1957-03-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency Report: The Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party launched the "Party Life" magazine on the eve of the 36th anniversary of the party's founding (March 1st). This magazine mainly publishes articles on party life and political theory. The inaugural issue contains articles such as "The Social Nature of the Individual Economy" and "Mongolian Handicraft Production Cooperatives and Their Prospects."

### 蒙古人民革命党  创办“党的生活”杂志

1957-03-02
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民革命党
    创办“党的生活”杂志
    据新华社讯  蒙古人民革命党中央委员会在建党三十六周年（3月1日）前夕创办了“党的生活”杂志。这个杂志主要刊载关于党的生活和政治理论等方面的文章。创刊号登载了“个体经济的社会性质”、“蒙古手工业生产合作社及其远景”等文章。
