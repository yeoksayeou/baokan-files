Title: Our People Support the Egyptian People's Just Struggle - Nasser Sends Letter of Thanks
Author:
Date: 1957-03-03
Page: 5

Our People Support the Egyptian People's Just Struggle
    Nasser Sends Letter of Thanks

    This newspaper reports: Egyptian President Gamal Abdel Nasser wrote a letter on January 31st of this year to the staff of Hongmen School in Xinxiang County, Henan Province, China, thanking them for their support of the Egyptian people. During the Egyptian people's war against the British and French aggression, our people wrote many letters to the Egyptian people expressing their support for their just struggle. Many people have now received replies signed by the Egyptian President. President Nasser said in his reply: "I am very grateful for the noble feelings expressed in your letter supporting Egypt's opposition to the tripartite aggression. I would like to sincerely tell you that Egypt will never forget the efforts and spirit of those who defended Egypt or participated in the cause of upholding justice in any way. We sincerely appreciate this noble attitude of freedom-loving people, and appreciate those who fight for the cause of justice and righteousness."

### 我国人民支持埃及人民的正义斗争  纳赛尔复信表示感谢

1957-03-03
第5版()
专栏：

    我国人民支持埃及人民的正义斗争
    纳赛尔复信表示感谢
    本报讯  埃及总统加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔在今年1月31日写信给我国河南省新乡县洪门学校职工，感谢他们对埃及人民的支持。在埃及人民反抗英、法侵略战争期间，我国人民曾写了许多信给埃及人民，表示支援他们的正义斗争。现在已有不少人收到了埃及总统签署的回信。纳赛尔总统的复信中说：“十分感激你们在来信中所表达的支持埃及反对三国侵略的高贵的感情。我愿恳切地向你们说，对于那些保卫了埃及或者在任何意义上参加了维护正义事业的人们的努力和精神，埃及将永志不忘。我们真诚地感激爱好自由的人们的这种高尚的态度，感激那些为公理和正义事业而战斗的人们。”
