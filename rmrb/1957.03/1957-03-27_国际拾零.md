　　International Miscellany
　　South Korean Youth Refuse to Enlist
　　According to figures released by Syngman Rhee's Ministry of National Defense, from the start of "reporting" draft dodgers in November of last year to March 11th of this year, the number of South Korean youth evading military service has increased to 51,584. In addition, according to data from the South Korean Army authorities, from November of last year to February 19th of this year, a total of more than 39,000 young people have been arrested for refusing to enlist.
　　US Navy Holds Large-Scale Exercise in the Philippines
　　The US Seventh Fleet and Marine Corps began the largest landing exercise conducted by the US military in the Pacific since World War II in Lingayen Gulf, northeast of Manila, on the morning of the 25th. A total of 60,000 US military personnel participated in the exercise. During the exercise, a jet fighter crashed, and the pilot was killed.
　　There are 100,000 to 110,000 US Troops Stationed in Japan
　　Japanese Defense Agency Vice Minister Keiji Masuhara told the House of Representatives Cabinet Committee on the 26th that there are 100,000 to 110,000 US troops stationed in Japan. Among them, the army has 30,000 to 40,000, the navy nearly 20,000, and the air force 50,000.

### 国际拾零

1957-03-27
第6版()
专栏：国际拾零

　  国际拾零
    南朝鲜青年拒绝当兵
    据李承晚国防部发表的数字，从去年11月开始“检举”逃避兵役者起到今年3月11日为止，南朝鲜逃避兵役的青年已经增加到五万一千五百八十四名。另据南朝鲜陆军当局的资料，从去年11月到今年2月19日由于拒绝当兵而遭逮捕的青年共三万九千多人。
    美海军在菲举行大演习
    美国第七舰队和海军陆战队从25日早上开始在马尼拉东北的仁牙兰湾举行第二次世界大战以后美军在太平洋举行的规模最大的登陆演习。参加演习的美军人员共有六万人。在演习中，有一架喷气式战斗机失事，驾驶员死亡。
    驻日美军有十万到十一万
    日本防卫厅次长增原惠吉26日告诉众议院内阁委员会说，驻日美军的兵力有十万人到十一万人。其中陆军三万人到四万人，海军将近两万人，空军五万人。
