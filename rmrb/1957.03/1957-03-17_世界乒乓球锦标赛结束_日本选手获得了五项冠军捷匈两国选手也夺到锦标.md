Title: World Table Tennis Championships Conclude; Japanese Players Win Five Championships; Players from Czechoslovakia and Hungary Also Win Titles
Author:
Date: 1957-03-17
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 16th dispatch: Stockholm news: The 24th World Table Tennis Championships have concluded after nine days of intense competition.

Players from Japan, Czechoslovakia, and Hungary shared the seven championship titles. The Japanese players performed the best, winning an unprecedented five titles, including the men's team, women's team, men's singles, women's singles, and mixed doubles.

People believe that the reasons for the Japanese team's great success are: table tennis is very popular in Japan, new forces are constantly emerging, the Japanese players' forehand high-speed smashing style has advantages, and the players receive rigorous training and have good physical strength.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 16th dispatch: Stockholm news: Japanese table tennis players won three more championships in the 24th World Table Tennis Championships on March 15th: men's singles, women's singles, and mixed doubles.

Competing for the men's singles world championship were the defending champion Ogimura (Japan) and the previous champion Tanaka. As a result, Tanaka defeated Ogimura three to zero.

The women's singles champion was Eguchi, who has won multiple Japanese championships.

The mixed doubles champions were Ogimura and Eguchi.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 16th dispatch: Stockholm news: Engineer Andreadis (Czechoslovakia) and worker Stipek (Czechoslovakia) defeated Japanese players Ogimura and Tanaka by a score of three to one on March 15th to win the men's doubles championship at the 24th World Table Tennis Championships. Ogimura was one of the defending men's doubles champions.

This is the fifth time Andreadis has won the world championship title, and it is the third time Stipek has won the world championship title.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 16th dispatch: Stockholm news: Hungarian players Mossoczy and Simon won the women's doubles championship at this year's World Table Tennis Championships on March 15th.

Mossoczy and Simon engaged in a fierce battle with English players Haydon and Diani Rowe in the final, winning three to two.

This was the first time Mossoczy and Simon participated in the World Table Tennis Championships.

### 世界乒乓球锦标赛结束  日本选手获得了五项冠军捷匈两国选手也夺到锦标

1957-03-17
第7版()
专栏：体育新闻

    世界乒乓球锦标赛结束
    日本选手获得了五项冠军捷匈两国选手也夺到锦标
    据新华社柏林16日电　斯德哥尔摩消息：第二十四届世界乒乓球锦标赛经过九天紧张的比赛已经结束。
    日本、捷克斯洛伐克和匈牙利的选手分得了这次比赛中的七项冠军。日本选手的成绩最好，他们空前的一共得了男子团体、女子团体、男子单打、女子单打、男女混合双打等五项冠军。
    人们认为日本队能够取得这样大胜利的原因是因为：乒乓球运动在日本非常普遍，新生力量不断地涌现，日本选手那种正手高速度的猛抽猛杀的打法有优越的地方，选手们得到严格的训练和具有良好的体力也是重要的原因。
    据新华社柏林16日电　斯德哥尔摩消息：日本乒乓球选手3月15日在第二十四届世界乒乓球锦标赛的比赛中又取得了男子单打、女子单打和男女混合双打三个冠军。
    争夺男子单打世界冠军的是上届冠军荻村（日）和前届冠军田中。结果田中以三比○战胜荻村。
    获得女子单打冠军的是多次获得日本冠军的江口。
    获得男女混合双打冠军的是荻村和江口。
    据新华社柏林16日电　斯德哥尔摩消息：工程师安德里亚迪斯（捷）和工人斯提佩克（捷）3月15日以三比一的比数击败日本选手荻村和田中而获得第二十四届世界乒乓球锦标赛男子双打的冠军。荻村是上届男子双打冠军之一。
    安德里亚迪斯这次是第五次获得世界冠军的称号，斯提佩克也已经是第三次获得世界冠军的称号。
    据新华社柏林16日电　斯德哥尔摩消息：匈牙利选手莫沙奇和西蒙在3月15日赢得了这一届世界乒乓球锦标赛的女子双打冠军。
    莫沙奇和西蒙在决赛中同英格兰选手海顿和戴安尼·罗进行了激烈的争夺，结果三比二取得胜利。
    莫沙奇和西蒙还是第一次参加世界乒乓球锦标赛。
