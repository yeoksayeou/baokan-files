Title: Czechoslovak Government Delegation Visits and Tours Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1957-03-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, March 22nd. Prime Minister Viliam Široký and all members of the Czechoslovak government delegation he led, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai and others, visited the Du Jinsheng Silk Weaving Factory this morning.
　　Prime Minister Široký and Premier Zhou Enlai visited various workshops in the silk weaving factory and were warmly welcomed by the workers. Factory Director Qiu Nan'an presented Prime Minister Široký with a silk woven portrait of President Zápotocký on behalf of all the factory workers, and presented Mrs. Široký with brocade fabric for clothing.
　　Subsequently, the distinguished guests traveled by car to visit the Qiantang River Bridge. Prime Minister Široký and others enjoyed the scenery of the Qiantang River at the center of the bridge.
　　Prime Minister Široký and others also climbed Moon Wheel Mountain and visited the Liuhe Pagoda standing on this mountain, followed by a visit to the Hupao Spring.
　　At noon today, Premier Zhou Enlai invited Prime Minister Široký and his wife, as well as the members of the delegation, to lunch at the Lou Wai Lou Restaurant on the shore of West Lake.
　　In the evening, Prime Minister Široký and all members of the Czechoslovak government delegation watched the Zhejiang Yue Opera Troupe perform "The Butterfly Lovers," and Premier Zhou Enlai also attended the performance.

### 捷政府代表团在杭州参观游览

1957-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　捷政府代表团在杭州参观游览
    据新华社杭州22日电　威廉·西罗基总理和他率领的捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团全体团员，今天上午由周恩来总理等陪同，参观了都锦生丝织厂。
    西罗基总理和周恩来总理在这个丝织厂里参观了各个车间，受到了工人们的热烈欢迎。厂长裘南安代表全厂工人向西罗基总理赠送了萨波托斯基总统的丝织像，向西罗基总理夫人赠送了织锦缎的衣料。
    随后，贵宾们乘汽车前往钱江大桥参观。西罗基总理等在桥中心欣赏了钱塘江的景色。
    西罗基总理等还登上月轮山，参观了矗立在这座山上的六和塔，接着又参观了虎跑泉。
    今天中午，周恩来总理邀请西罗基总理和夫人以及代表团的团员们，在西湖滨的楼外楼共进午餐。
    晚间，西罗基总理和捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团全体团员观看了浙江越剧团演出的“梁山伯与祝英台”，周恩来总理也出席了晚会。
