Author: Liu Entai
Date: 1957-03-26
Page: 2

　　New-Style Mechanical Farm Tools
    The state-owned Jilin Farm Implement Factory plans to trial-produce seven new types of agricultural machinery in 1957, modeled after Soviet designs. Among them, five large agricultural machines, including stationary grain dryers, feed steamers, and land rollers, have been successfully trial-produced. These products will be supplied to state-owned farms, livestock farms, and agricultural production cooperatives in various regions.
    This is a stationary grain dryer. This machine can dry more than 2,000 jin of grain per hour.

### 新式机械农具（图片）
刘恩泰
1957-03-26
第2版()
专栏：

　　新式机械农具
    国营吉林农具厂，计划在1957年仿照苏联式样试制七种新式农业机械。其中固定式谷物干燥机、饲料蒸煮器和镇压器等五种大型农业机械已试制成功。这些产品将供给各地国营农场、牧畜场和农业生产合作社使用。
    这是固定式谷物干燥机，这种机器每小时能烘干谷物两千多斤。
                                     新华社记者　刘恩泰摄
