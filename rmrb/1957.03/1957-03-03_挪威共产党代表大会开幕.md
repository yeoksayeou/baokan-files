Title: Norwegian Communist Party Congress Opens
Author:
Date: 1957-03-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Oslo, March 2nd: According to TASS, the Ninth Congress of the Norwegian Communist Party opened in Oslo on March 1st. The Party Chairman, Emil Lövlien, delivered a report entitled "The Norwegian Communist Party's Struggle for Peace, Democracy, and National Independence."

Two hundred and forty delegates and guests attended the congress. The guests included representatives from fraternal Communist and Workers' Parties of Sweden, Denmark, Finland, France, Czechoslovakia, Poland, and other countries.

A congratulatory telegram from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union was read at the congress. The congress also received congratulatory telegrams from the Communist Parties of China, Britain, and the Netherlands.

Representatives of the fraternal parties from various countries addressed the congress with congratulatory messages.

The congress is continuing.

### 挪威共产党代表大会开幕

1957-03-03
第5版()
专栏：

    挪威共产党代表大会开幕
    新华社2日讯  据塔斯社奥斯陆讯：挪威共产党第九次代表大会3月1日在奥斯陆开幕。党的主席埃米尔·济夫林作了“挪威共产党为争取和平、民主和民族独立而斗争”的报告。
    出席这次代表大会的有二百四十名代表和来宾。来宾中包括瑞典、丹麦、芬兰、法国、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰等国兄弟共产党和工人党的代表。
    大会上宣读了苏联共产党中央委员会的贺电。大会还收到了中国、英国和荷兰共产党的贺电。
    到会的各国兄弟党的代表都向大会致了祝词。
    大会在继续进行。
