Title: Peddling the "Eisenhower Doctrine": U.S. Presidential Special Mission to the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1957-03-15
Page: 7

    Peddling the "Eisenhower Doctrine"
    U.S. Presidential Special Mission to the Middle East
    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, March 13th: A U.S. presidential special mission led by Representative Richards, chairman of the House Foreign Affairs Committee, set off for the Middle East on the 12th to peddle the "Eisenhower Doctrine." Before his departure, Richards issued a statement saying that he would try to eliminate "misunderstandings" about the plan.
    The news said that Richards may visit eighteen countries, with Lebanon being the first.

### 兜售“艾森豪威尔主义”  美国总统特别使团去中东

1957-03-15
第7版()
专栏：

    兜售“艾森豪威尔主义”
    美国总统特别使团去中东
    新华社13日讯  华盛顿消息：以众议院外交委员会主席理查兹为首的美国总统特别使团12日启程前往中东兜售“艾森豪威尔主义”。理查兹在动身前发表声明说，他要设法消除对这个计划的“误解”。
    消息说，理查兹可能将访问十八个国家，第一个国家是黎巴嫩。
