Title: Chairman Mao and Others Receive Guizhou and Inner Mongolia Visiting Groups
Author:
Date: 1957-03-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. Chairman Mao Zedong, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Deng Xiaoping, Li Jishen, Huang Yanpei, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, Zhang Bojun and others received all members of the Guizhou Province Ethnic Groups Visiting Group and the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Visiting Group this evening.

### 毛主席等接见贵州内蒙古两个参观团

1957-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席等接见贵州内蒙古两个参观团
    新华社2日讯  毛泽东主席、周恩来总理和邓小平、李济深、黄炎培、李维汉、陈叔通、章伯钧等，在今天晚间接见了贵州省各民族参观团和内蒙古自治区参观团的全体人员。
