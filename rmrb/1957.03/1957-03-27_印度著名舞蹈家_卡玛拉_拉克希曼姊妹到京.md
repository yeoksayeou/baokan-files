Title: Prominent Indian Dancers, Kamala Laxman and Sisters, Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-03-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 26th: Kamala Laxman, an outstanding Indian classical dancer, and her twelve-year-old sister, Radha Laxman, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. They are visiting China and will perform at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.
    While in Beijing, these two Indian artists will not only perform publicly but also help the China Youth Art Theatre rehearse the dance scenes in the Indian poetic drama "Shakuntala."
    Kamala Laxman is one of the most famous dancers in India today. She started learning to dance at the age of five. Her specialty, Bharatanatyam, is one of the four major schools of Indian classical dance. She has performed in countries such as England, France, Italy, Switzerland, and Egypt.
    The Laxman sisters' mother, a musician, also came to China with them. Also accompanying them are a dance partner and two accompanying musicians.
    Among those who went to the airport to welcome them were Wu Huazhi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, dancer Dai Ailian, and Wu Xue, Director of the China Youth Art Theatre. The wife of Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, and personnel from the Indian Embassy also welcomed them at the airport. (See photo attached)

### 印度著名舞蹈家　卡玛拉·拉克希曼姊妹到京

1957-03-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度著名舞蹈家
　　卡玛拉·拉克希曼姊妹到京
    新华社26日讯　优秀的印度古典舞蹈家卡玛拉·拉克希曼和她十二岁的妹妹腊达·拉克希曼今天下午乘飞机到达北京。她们是应中国人民对外文化协会邀请，到我国来作访问演出的。
    在北京期间，这两位印度艺术家除了公开演出外，还要帮助中国青年艺术剧院排演印度诗剧“沙恭达罗”里面的舞蹈场面。
    卡玛拉·拉克希曼是当今印度最有名的舞蹈家之一。她从五岁起就开始学舞，她所擅长的婆罗多舞是印度古典舞蹈四大派别之一。她曾经在英、法、意、瑞士、埃及等国演出过。
    拉克希曼姊妹的母亲是一位音乐家，这次也一同前来我国。同行的还有一位伴舞者和两位伴奏音乐家。
    前往机场欢迎的有对外文化协会副秘书长吴化之，舞蹈家戴爱莲和中国青年艺术剧院院长吴雪等人。印度驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁的夫人和印度大使馆人员也到机场欢迎。（附图片）
