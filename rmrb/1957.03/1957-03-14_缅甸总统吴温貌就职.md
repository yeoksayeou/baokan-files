Title: Burmese President U Win Maung Inaugurated
Author:
Date: 1957-03-14
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, March 13th: The third President of the Union of Burma, U Win Maung, was sworn into office at ten o'clock this morning at a joint session of the Chamber of Nationalities and the Chamber of Deputies, before the Chief Justice of the Union Supreme Court, U Thein Maung, and the judges.

Those attending the presidential inauguration ceremony included Prime Minister U Nu, Deputy Prime Ministers, cabinet ministers, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Burma.

Prime Minister U Nu held a banquet last night for the outgoing President of the Union, Dr. Ba U, whose presidential term expired yesterday.

On March 12th, the meeting of the Burmese Chamber of Deputies discussed the no-confidence motion against the government proposed by opposition leader U Ba Swe. The motion was rejected in the vote. (Attached picture)

### 缅甸总统吴温貌就职

1957-03-14
第6版()
专栏：

    缅甸总统吴温貌就职
    据新华社仰光13日电  缅甸联邦第三任总统吴温貌今天上午十点钟在民族院和代表院联席会议上以及联邦最高法院首席法官吴登貌和法官们的面前宣誓就职。
    参加总统就职典礼的有总理吴努、副总理、内阁部长以及各国驻缅甸的外交使节。
    吴努总理在昨天晚上为卸任的联邦总统巴宇博士举行了宴会，巴宇博士的总统任期在昨天期满。
    3月12日，缅甸代表院举行的会议讨论了反对派领袖吴埃貌提出的对政府的不信任案。这个提案在表决时被否决。（附图片）
