Title: Over Ten Thousand Officers and Soldiers of the Vietnamese People's Army Join the Party

Author:

Date: 1957-03-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, Electric Dispatch: According to the Vietnam News Agency, the "People's Army Daily," the newspaper of the Vietnamese People's Army, reported that in the past two years, 15,284 officers and soldiers of the People's Army have been approved to join the Vietnam Workers' Party. Most of them have undergone arduous tests in war and work.

### 越南人民军万余官兵入党

1957-03-05
第5版()
专栏：

    越南人民军万余官兵入党
    据新华社河内电  据越南通讯社讯：越南人民军机关报“人民军队报”报道，在过去两年，有一万五千二百八十四名人民军军官和士兵被批准参加越南劳动党。他们大部分都在战争和工作中经过艰苦的考验。
