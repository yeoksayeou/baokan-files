Title: Sino-Pakistani Friendship Association Holds Film Reception to Celebrate Pakistan National Day
Author:
Date: 1957-03-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 22nd: The Sino-Pakistani Friendship Association held a film reception this evening at the Beijing Peace Hotel to celebrate the Republic Day of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan (March 23rd).

The Pakistani films "Prime Minister Suhrawardy's Visit to China" and "Pakistani Folk Song and Dance" were warmly welcomed at the reception. The Chinese feature film "Battle in the Desert" was also shown at the reception.

Liu Geping, President of the Sino-Pakistani Friendship Association, Qian Duansheng, Vice President, Zhang Jie, Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association, and more than 300 people from all walks of life in the capital attended the film reception.

Ahmed, Ambassador of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan to China, his wife, and embassy staff were invited to attend the reception.

### 中巴友协举行电影晚会庆祝巴基斯坦国庆

1957-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　中巴友协举行电影晚会庆祝巴基斯坦国庆
    新华社22日讯　中巴友好协会今天晚上在北京和平宾馆举行电影招待晚会，庆祝巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国共和日（3月23日）。
    巴基斯坦影片“苏拉瓦底总理访问中国”、“巴基斯坦民间歌舞”在晚会上受到热烈欢迎，晚会上还放映了中国故事片“沙漠里的战斗”。
    中巴友好协会会长刘格平、副会长钱端升，中国伊斯兰教协会副主任张杰，以及首都各界人士三百多人出席了电影晚会。
    巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国驻华大使阿哈默德和夫人以及大使馆人员应邀出席了晚会。
