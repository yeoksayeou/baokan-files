Title: To Prevent British Hydrogen Bomb Tests: Kochi Prefecture, Japan, to Send Protest Ship to Christmas Island

Author:

Date: 1957-03-26
Page: 5

　　To Prevent British Hydrogen Bomb Tests
　　Kochi Prefecture, Japan, to Send Protest Ship to Christmas Island

Xinhua News Agency, March 25th, Tokyo dispatch: A mass rally attended by 1,500 people was held in Kochi Prefecture, Japan, on March 24th, at which it was decided to send a protest ship to Christmas Island to prevent British nuclear weapons testing. The rally also passed a resolution calling on the United States, Britain, and the Soviet Union to cease the testing and manufacturing of nuclear weapons. This rally was initiated by the Kochi Prefecture government. Kochi Prefecture is one of Japan's three major tuna ports. Local fishermen believe that the British hydrogen bomb tests scheduled to be held on Christmas Island will cause them enormous losses.

The Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs also made the same decision on March 8th.

### 阻止英国试验氢弹　日本高知县将派抗议船到圣诞岛去

1957-03-26
第5版()
专栏：

　　阻止英国试验氢弹
　　日本高知县将派抗议船到圣诞岛去
    据新华社25日讯　东京消息：日本高知县在3月24日举行了有一千五百人参加的群众大会，会上决定派遣抗议船到圣诞岛去阻止英国试验核武器。大会还通过了要求美、英、苏三国停止试验和制造核武器的决议。这次大会是高知县政府发起举行的。高知县是日本三大鲔鱼港之一。当地渔民们认为，英国预定在圣诞岛举行的氢弹试验将会使他们遭受巨大的损失。
    日本禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会在3月8日也曾经作出同样的决定。
