Title: Difficult and Easy (An Indian Story)
Author: Guangguang
Date: 1957-03-17
Page: 8

There was a man who asked a wise man:

	"What is easy for us and what is difficult?"

The wise man replied:

	"It is easy to come up with ideas, but it is difficult to implement them." (Translated from the Soviet Union's "Satire and Humor" in 1956)
	
	Guangguang, translator

### 难与易（印度故事）
光光
1957-03-17
第8版()
专栏：

    难与易（印度故事）
    有一个人问贤者：
    “对我们来说，什么是容易的和什么是难的？”
    贤者回答：
    “出主意很容易，而实现它很难。”			  （译自苏联1956年“讽刺与幽默”）
    光光译
