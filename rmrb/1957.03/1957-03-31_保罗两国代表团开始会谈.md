Title: Bulgarian and Romanian Delegations Begin Talks
Author:
Date: 1957-03-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, March 30th: The government and party delegations of Bulgaria and Romania began talks on the afternoon of March 29th.

The Bulgarian side participating in the talks included Zhivkov, Yugov, Traikov, Damyanov, and others.

The Romanian side participating in the talks included Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Stoica, Borila, and others.

At noon that day, Yugov hosted a banquet in honor of the delegation.

### 保罗两国代表团开始会谈

1957-03-31
第5版()
专栏：

    保罗两国代表团开始会谈
    据新华社索非亚30日电　保加利亚和罗马尼亚两国政府和党的代表团在3月29日下午开始会谈。
    保加利亚方面参加会谈的有日夫科夫、于哥夫、特莱科夫、达米扬诺夫等。
    罗马尼亚方面参加会谈的有乔治乌—德治、斯托伊卡、波里勒等。
    当日午间，于哥夫设宴招待了代表团。
