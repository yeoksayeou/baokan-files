Title: The Golfing Master and the Caddie Who Borrows Clubs (Picture)
Author: Hua Junwu
Date: 1957-03-27
Page: 6

　　The Golfing Master and the Caddie Who Borrows Clubs
  　Hua Junwu
    At London Airport, when reporters asked British Prime Minister Macmillan, who was going to the Bermuda Conference, whether he planned to play golf with the US President in Bermuda, Macmillan replied, "I didn't bring my clubs, but I dare say I can borrow a few." He also said, "I am not a very good golfer, and I know the President plays very well."

### 打球的老爷和借到球棒的跟班（图片）
华君武
1957-03-27
第6版()
专栏：

　　打球的老爷和借到球棒的跟班
  　华君武
    在伦敦机场上，记者问到百慕大开会的英国首相麦克米伦是否打算在百慕大和美国总统打高尔夫球，麦克米伦回答说：“我没有带我的球棒，但是我敢说，我可以借到几支”。他又说：“我并不是一个很好的高尔夫球手，而我知道总统是打得很好的。”
