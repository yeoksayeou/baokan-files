Title: Strengthening the Fraternal Ties Between the Chinese and Polish Peoples: Polish Government Delegation to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1957-03-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. At the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, a government delegation of the People's Republic of Poland, headed by Chairman of the Council of Ministers Jozef Cyrankiewicz, will visit China in April this year. Premier Cyrankiewicz's visit to China is a return visit to Premier Zhou Enlai's visit to Poland in July 1954 and January this year.

The Polish government delegation's visit to China will further strengthen the fraternal ties between the Polish and Chinese peoples.

### 加强中波人民兄弟般的联系  波兰政府代表团将访华 

1957-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

    加强中波人民兄弟般的联系
    波兰政府代表团将访华
    新华社8日讯　应中华人民共和国政府邀请以部长会议主席约瑟夫·西伦凯维兹为首的波兰人民共和国政府代表团，将于今年4月间访问中国。西伦凯维兹总理访问中国是对周恩来总理在1954年7月和今年1月访问波兰的回访。
    波兰政府代表团前来中国访问，将进一步加强波兰人民同中国人民之间的兄弟般的联系。
