Title: First Batch of Light Tonnage Ships Pass Through Suez Canal
Author:
Date: 1957-03-12
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 10th: Following the reopening of the Suez Canal to light tonnage vessels, the first batch of ships passed through the canal on March 9th.

    On this day, the 250-ton Dutch ship "Kleven Egge" sailed from the port of Suez at the southern end of the canal to Port Said at the northern end. At the same time, the 147-ton yacht "Kuwait Marawah" and the 238-ton Greek tugboat "Michalis" sailed from the northern end of the canal to the southern end of the port of Suez.

### 第一批轻吨位船只通过苏伊士运河

1957-03-12
第6版()
专栏：

    第一批轻吨位船只通过苏伊士运河
    新华社开罗10日电　在苏伊士运河向轻吨位船只重新开放以后，第一批船只已经在3月9日通过运河。
    这一天，二百五十吨的荷兰船“克雷文·埃格号”从运河南端的苏伊士港驶到北端的塞得港。同时，一百四十七吨的游艇“科威特·马腊耳维号”和二百三十八吨的希腊拖船“米哈利斯号”从运河北端驶到南端的苏伊士港。
