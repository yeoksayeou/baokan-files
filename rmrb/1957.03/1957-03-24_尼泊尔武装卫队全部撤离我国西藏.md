Title: Nepalese Armed Guards Completely Withdraw from Our Country's Tibet

Author:

Date: 1957-03-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, March 23rd: The Nepalese armed guard detachments stationed in Lhasa, Shigatse, Gyantse, Nyalam, and Kyirong in the Tibet region of China, along with all their weapons and ammunition, had withdrawn from the aforementioned locations by March 18th. The withdrawal of these armed guards was carried out in accordance with the exchange of notes on September 20, 1956, between Pan Zili, the Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, and Chuda Prasad Sharma, the Foreign Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal. Article 2 of the exchange of notes stipulated that these armed guards would be completely withdrawn within six months from the date of the exchange of notes.

The Nepalese armed guard detachments stationed in Lhasa, Shigatse, and Gyantse in the Tibet region of China left Chinese territory via Yatung, and the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region of China dispatched special vehicles to escort them; the Nepalese armed guard detachments stationed in Nyalam and Kyirong left Chinese territory from their respective locations, and they also received assistance and facilitation from the Chinese side.

### 尼泊尔武装卫队全部撤离我国西藏

1957-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　尼泊尔武装卫队全部撤离我国西藏
    新华社拉萨23日电　尼泊尔驻在中国西藏地方拉萨、日喀则、江孜、聂拉木、吉隆等处的武装卫队连同全部武器、弹药，已经在3月18日从上述地点撤退。撤退这些武装卫队是按照中国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力和尼泊尔王国外交大臣丘·普·夏尔玛在1956年9月20日的换文执行的，换文第二条规定这些武装卫队自照会互换的日期起六个月内完全撤退。
    驻在中国西藏地方拉萨、日喀则、江孜的尼泊尔武装卫队取道亚东离开中国国境，中国西藏自治区筹备委员会曾派专车护送；驻在聂拉木、吉隆的尼泊尔武装卫队由当地离开中国国境，他们也得到中国方面给予的协助和便利。
