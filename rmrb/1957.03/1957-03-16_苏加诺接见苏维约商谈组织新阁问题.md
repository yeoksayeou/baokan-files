Title: Sukarno Meets with Suwirjo to Discuss Formation of New Cabinet

Author:

Date: 1957-03-16
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 15th: According to the Indonesian News Agency, President Sukarno summoned Suwirjo, Chairman of the Indonesian National Party, to the Independence Palace this afternoon to discuss the formation of a new cabinet. It is also reported that the President is also preparing to summon Idham Chalid, Chairman of the Nahdlatul Ulama (Islamic Scholars Association), to discuss this matter.

The Indonesian National News Agency said today that the new cabinet to be formed in the short term will be centered on the Indonesian National Party and the Nahdlatul Ulama, because these two parties have reached a good understanding on "implementing the President's plan within the effective scope."

### 苏加诺接见苏维约商谈组织新阁问题

1957-03-16
第7版()
专栏：

    苏加诺接见苏维约商谈组织新阁问题
    据新华社雅加达15日电  据印度尼西亚新闻社报道，苏加诺总统今天下午在独立宫召见了印度尼西亚国民党总主席苏维约商谈组织新阁的问题。另外有消息说，总统还准备召见伊斯兰教师联合会主席伊达姆·查利德商谈这个问题。
    印度尼西亚民族通讯社今天说，将在短期内成立的新阁将以国民党和伊斯兰教师联合会为核心，因为这两个党在“有效范围内实施总统方案”上取得了良好的谅解。
