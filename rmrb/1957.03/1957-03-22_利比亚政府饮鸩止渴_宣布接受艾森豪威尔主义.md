Title: Libyan Government Drinks Poison to Quench Thirst, Announces Acceptance of the Eisenhower Doctrine
Author:
Date: 1957-03-22
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Tripoli, March 21st: Following talks between U.S. President Eisenhower's special envoy Richards and the Libyan government, a joint communique was issued on the 20th stating that they "agreed to cooperate in order to smoothly implement" the "Eisenhower Doctrine."

The communique also stated that the United States would provide Libya with "additional economic assistance."

Libya is a North African Arab country with a population of over one million. In 1953, Britain obtained the privilege of stationing troops in Libya. In 1954, the United States also obtained military bases in the country.

### 利比亚政府饮鸩止渴　宣布接受艾森豪威尔主义

1957-03-22
第7版()
专栏：

　  利比亚政府饮鸩止渴
　　宣布接受艾森豪威尔主义
    据新华社21日讯　的黎波里消息：美国总统特使理查兹同利比亚政府举行会谈后在20日发表联合公报说，它们“同意进行合作以便顺利地实行”“艾森豪威尔主义”。
    公报还说：美国将向利比亚提供“额外的经济援助”。
    利比亚是北非的一个人口一百多万人的阿拉伯国家。英国在1953年取得了在利比亚驻军的特权。美国在1954年也在这个国家取得了军事基地。
