Title: Premier Zhou Arrives in Kunming for Talks with Prime Minister U Nu
Author:
Date: 1957-03-30
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, March 29th: Premier Zhou Enlai arrived in Kunming by special plane at 12:30 noon today.

　　The Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, and Deputy Prime Minister Sao Khun Kyi, who are currently visiting Kunming, welcomed Premier Zhou Enlai at the airport.

　　Also present at the airport to welcome him were Vice Premier He Long, Xie Fuzhi and Yu Yichuan, secretaries of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Liu Minghui, acting governor of Yunnan Province, and responsible persons from various democratic parties and people's organizations in Yunnan Province and Kunming City.

　　The Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, Yao Zhongming, and officials from the consulates of the Union of Burma and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming also welcomed him at the airport.

　　After Premier Zhou Enlai disembarked from the plane, he cordially greeted Prime Minister U Nu and Deputy Prime Minister Sao Khun Kyi. Prime Minister U Nu replied in Chinese, "Very good."

　　Premier Zhou Enlai then accompanied Prime Minister U Nu by car to his residence to rest.

　　Premier Zhou Enlai stopped in Chengdu for half a day yesterday on his way.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, March 29th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council held talks here this afternoon with Prime Minister U Nu of the Union of Burma.

　　Also present at the talks were the Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Union of Burma, Sao Khun Kyi, and the Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung.

　　On the Chinese side, those participating in the talks included Vice Premier He Long, Xie Fuzhi, First Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Liu Minghui, Acting Governor of Yunnan Province, and Yao Zhongming, our country's Ambassador to Burma.

### 周总理到昆明同吴努总理会谈

1957-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　周总理到昆明同吴努总理会谈
    新华社昆明29日电  周恩来总理今天中午十二时半乘专机到达昆明。
    正在昆明访问的缅甸联邦吴努总理、藻昆卓副总理到机场欢迎周恩来总理。
    到机场欢迎的，还有贺龙副总理，中共云南省委员会书记谢富治、于一川，云南省代省长刘明辉和云南省、昆明市各民主党派和人民团体的负责人。
    缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂，中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明，和缅甸联邦、越南民主共和国驻昆明领事馆的官员也到机场欢迎。
    周恩来总理走下飞机后，向吴努总理、藻昆卓副总理亲切问候。吴努总理用中国话回答说：“很好”。
    接着周恩来总理陪送吴努总理乘车到住地休息。
    周恩来总理昨天路过成都曾停留半天。
    新华社昆明29日电  国务院总理周恩来今天下午同缅甸联邦总理吴努在此间晤谈。
    在谈话时，缅甸联邦副总理兼外交部长藻昆卓和缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂也在场。
    中国方面参加谈话的有副总理贺龙，中共云南省委第一书记谢富治，云南省代省长刘明辉和我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明。
