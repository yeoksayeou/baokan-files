Title: China and Czechoslovakia Sign This Year's Goods Exchange and Payment Agreement
Author: 
Date: 1957-03-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 7th. China and Czechoslovakia signed the 1957 Goods Exchange and Payment Agreement in Prague on March 6th.

According to the new agreement, the trade volume between China and Czechoslovakia in 1957 will increase compared to 1956. China will supply Czechoslovakia with mineral products, hides, bast fibers, silk, tea, and other industrial raw materials and daily necessities. Czechoslovakia will supply China with steam station equipment, various steels and steel plates, cutting machine tools, welding machines, agricultural machinery, steel rails, machine oil, chemical products, and so on.

Signing on behalf of the Chinese side was Kong Yuan, Head of the Government Trade Delegation of the People's Republic of China and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade. Signing on behalf of the Czechoslovak side was Otto Kožol, Head of the Government Trade Delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic and First Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade.

### 中捷签订今年换货和付款协定

1957-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

    中捷签订今年换货和付款协定
    据新华社布拉格7日电　中捷1957年换货和付款协定3月6日在布拉格签订。
    根据新的协定，1957年，中捷两国的贸易额将比1956年有所增长。中国将供应捷克斯洛伐克矿产品、皮张、麻类、丝绸、茶叶及其他工业原料和生活必需品等。捷克斯洛伐克将供应中国蒸气站设备、各种钢材和钢板、切削机床、焊接机器、农业机械、钢轨、机器用油、化学产品等。
    代表中国方面签字的是中华人民共和国政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长孔原。代表捷克斯洛伐克方面签字的是捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部第一副部长奥托·科佐尔。
