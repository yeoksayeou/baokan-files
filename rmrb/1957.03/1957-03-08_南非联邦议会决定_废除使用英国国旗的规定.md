Title: South African Federal Parliament Decides to Abolish the Use of the British Flag
Author:
Date: 1957-03-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, March 7th, Cape Town dispatch: The South African Federal House of Assembly passed a bill to amend the national flag during its second reading on the 5th. This bill requires the cancellation of the British flag's status as the South African Federal flag, and the adoption of South Africa's own flag as the sole national flag. Previously, Interior Minister Dönges stated last week that the meaning of the flag amendment also includes that the South African Federation should only have one national anthem. This indicates that the South African Federation also intends to abolish the British national anthem "God Save the Queen" as the national anthem.

### 南非联邦议会决定  废除使用英国国旗的规定

1957-03-08
第6版()
专栏：

    南非联邦议会决定
    废除使用英国国旗的规定
    新华社7日讯　开普敦消息：南非联邦众议院在5日二读通过了一项关于国旗的修正法案，这项法案要求取消英国国旗作为南非联邦国旗的地位，而以南非自己的国旗作为唯一的国旗。在此以前，内政部长唐吉斯上星期曾经说过，国旗修正案的含义还包括南非联邦只应当有一个国歌。这表示南非联邦也要废除以英国国歌“上帝保祐女王”作为国歌的规定。
