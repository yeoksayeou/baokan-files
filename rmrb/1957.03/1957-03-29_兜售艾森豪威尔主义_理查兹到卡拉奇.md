Title: Richards Peddles the Eisenhower Doctrine in Karachi
Author:
Date: 1957-03-29
Page: 6

　　Peddling the Eisenhower Doctrine
　　Richards Arrives in Karachi
Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, March 28th. U.S. President's Special Envoy James Richards arrived in Karachi on the afternoon of March 27th from Tehran, continuing his visit to promote the "Eisenhower Doctrine."
    He has already visited Lebanon, Libya, Turkey, and Iran. After Pakistan, he will go to Afghanistan.
    In a prepared statement, Richards said that the "Eisenhower Doctrine" is "purely voluntary" and would only be implemented when directly requested by a country in the Middle East.

### 兜售艾森豪威尔主义　理查兹到卡拉奇

1957-03-29
第6版()
专栏：

　  兜售艾森豪威尔主义
　　理查兹到卡拉奇
    据新华社卡拉奇28日电  美国总统特使詹姆斯·理查兹3月27日下午从德黑兰到达卡拉奇，继续进行推销“艾森豪威尔主义”的访问。
    他已经访问了黎巴嫩、利比亚、土耳其和伊朗。在到巴基斯坦之后，还要到阿富汗去。
    理查兹在一篇事先准备好的声明中说，“艾森豪威尔主义”“纯粹是自愿性质的”，只有在中东某个国家直接要求时才加以实施。
