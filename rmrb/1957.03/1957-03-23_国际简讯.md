　　International Briefs

　　Chinese Trade Union Delegation Visits Arab Countries
    The first Chinese trade union delegation to visit Arab countries arrived in Cairo on the 21st. The delegation, led by Dong Xin, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, is visiting at the invitation of the Arab Trade Union Federation.
    Approximately one hundred trade union representatives warmly welcomed the Chinese trade union representatives at the Cairo airport.
　　International Mathematicians' Congress Opens in Berlin
    The International Mathematicians' Congress, organized by the German Academy of Sciences, opened in Berlin on the 21st. One hundred scientists from Democratic Germany, West Germany, the Soviet Union, France, and other countries are participating in the congress.
    The congress will last for three days.
                    (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 国际简讯

1957-03-23
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

　　国际简讯
　  我工会代表团访问阿拉伯国家
    到阿拉伯国家访问的第一个中国工会代表团在21日到达开罗。由中华全国总工会书记处书记董昕率领的代表团是应阿拉伯国家工会联合会的邀请前来访问的。
    约有一百名工会代表在开罗机场热烈欢迎中国工会代表。
　　国际数学家代表大会在柏林开幕
    由德国科学院组织的国际数学家代表大会21日在柏林开幕。参加大会的有民主德国、西德、苏联、法国等国的一百个科学家。
    这次代表大会将举行三天。
                    （据新华社）
