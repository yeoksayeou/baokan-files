Title: Delegation from Gifu Prefecture, Japan, Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1957-03-02
Page: 1

    Delegation from Gifu Prefecture, Japan, Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, 28th — The eighteen-member delegation from various circles in Gifu Prefecture, Japan, who are visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, arrived in Beijing by train today.

### 日本岐阜县代表团到京

1957-03-02
第1版()
专栏：

    日本岐阜县代表团到京
    新华社28日讯  应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的日本岐阜县各界访华代表团一行十八人，已经在今天乘火车到达北京。
