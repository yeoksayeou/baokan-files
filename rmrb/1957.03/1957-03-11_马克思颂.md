Author: Huang Yanpei
Date: 1957-03-11

Ode to Marx
Huang Yanpei
Revised March 1957

Marx was born in Germany on May 5, 1818, and died in England on March 14, 1883.

In 1936, I read a three-volume biography of Marx, and later I thoroughly studied the Communist Manifesto co-authored by Marx and Engels. Four years ago, I bravely read Marx's "Das Kapital," but only finished the first volume. It aroused my inner admiration. Under the correct leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, New China is advancing along the road of socialism in accordance with the laws of social development and the specific conditions of China, using peaceful transformation methods! Forward! At this time, I have revised this poem again and made it public for advice.

Someone asked if Marxism can be criticized? Chairman Mao Zedong replied: Marxism is not afraid of criticism. If it is overthrown by criticism, it means it is not the truth.

(I) London City,
　　West of the city center, school district,
　　Young people come and go, medieval buildings,
　　How solemn!
　　It's a library, it's the British Museum Library,
　　What's it called? Britain. (Full name is "British Museum Library".)
　　Entered the door,
　　Got a book retrieval slip, a man, dark face, beautiful beard,
　　Burying his head in searching,
　　In copying,
　　Constantly researching in the piles of books,
　　Like this, day after day,
　　Year after year,
　　Ten years, more than ten years, the ground he treads is sunken,
　　The chair he sits on is worn through, it's in the nineteenth century—
　　Between the fifties and sixties.

(II) How prosperous the factory,
　　Turbid air,
　　Rumbling sound,
　　Going to work,
　　Getting off work,
　　A large group leaves,
　　And another large group enters.
　　What?
　　There are also ten-year-old child laborers,
　　There are also six-year-olds, even four-year-olds, they are all told to stand on the work chair
　　And not allowed to leave for less than ten hours. These young children,
　　How can they endure it?
　　Too inhumane!
　　Too inhumane!
　　They even feed them opium every day,
　　Feeding them like little monkeys,
　　Simply not letting them grow up. Absurd!
　　Endless absurdity,
　　People are angry, stamping their feet,
　　The bosses are reveling, applauding, a man, dark face, beautiful beard,
　　Sees it clearly,
　　And writes it all in his book.

(III) After all, it is a fleeting ephemera,
　　Flourishing for a while,
　　Withering for a while, always unable to escape cyclical changes.
　　What is the reason? They all rely on exploiting the surplus labor of workers
　　To make a fortune,
　　Only knowing myself,
　　Not allowing you,
　　Even less allowing him. After a while,
　　They feel a crisis!
　　A big crisis!
　　The problem is the inability to make the market larger and bigger. Fly! Fly!
　　Despite flying colonialist fighter planes, run! Run!
　　Despite running imperialist tanks,
　　However, the contradiction between market and productive forces, the former increasing in arithmetic progression,
　　Cannot match the latter increasing in geometric progression.
　　Of course, the main thing is that the exploited laboring masses
　　Are rapidly impoverished.
　　Ding!
　　The death knell is ringing,
　　Ding! Ding!
　　Whose house is it? The capitalist.

(IV) State ownership of the means of production,
　　Eliminate private property,
　　The first voice shouted like this,
　　Please read the "Communist Manifesto." "Let the ruling classes tremble at a communist revolution!
　　The proletarians have nothing to lose but their chains." "From each according to his ability,
　　To each according to his need,"
　　Isn't it ideal enough! This is the manifestation of the superiority of communist society.
　　First step:
　　"From each according to his ability,
　　To each according to his work." This is the characteristic of life in a socialist society.
　　Discovered successively by geniuses. National collectivized agriculture has been realized,
　　Capitalist economic components have been eliminated, this is the world's first socialist country, the Soviet Union.

(V) World socialism is developing,
　　The upsurge is emerging,
　　Waves are splashing, from Eastern Europe to Asia,
　　From Moscow to Beijing, from the nineteen-teens,
　　To the forties and fifties, evolving from development,
　　New Democracy has emerged.
　　From this comes the national united front,
　　The people's democratic united front, who is following the law of social development,
　　Correctly matching its specific conditions?
　　Let people strictly test. Grasping the principle,
　　Adapting to the particularity,
　　Is this Marxism?
　　Who says it isn't? "Different countries,
　　Different methods,"
　　Clearly written in the Communist
　　Manifesto long ago.
　　The truth is before our eyes,
　　Who discovered it first? Let thousands upon thousands of people,
　　Thousands upon thousands of families, believe in him,
　　Commemorate him,
　　Salute him,
　　In everyone's heart,
　　On every family's wall, a man, dark face, beautiful beard.
　　This year marks the 74th anniversary of his departure from the world.

### 马克思颂
黄炎培
1957-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    马克思颂
    黄炎培
    1957年3月修改本
    马克思1818年5月5日出生于德国，1883年3月14日病殁于英国。
    我在1936年得读马克思传三大册，以后熟读了马克思和恩格斯合著的共产党宣言。四年以前，奋勇地读马克思“资本论”，但仅得读完第一卷。激起我内心的敬仰。曾经写一首诗来纪念马克思。新中国在中国共产党正确领导下，依着社会规律性的发展，按着中国特定的条件，采用和平改造方法，向着社会主义大道而前进！前进！在此时间，我把这首诗重加修改，公开求教。
    有人问马克思主义是不是可以批评？毛泽东主席答：马克思主义是不怕批评的。如被批评倒了，就说明不是真理了。
    （一）伦敦市，
    市中心的西偏，学校区，
    来来往往的是青年，中古式的大厦，
    多么庄严！
    是图书馆，是博物院图书馆，
    题着什么名？不列颠。（全名是“不列颠博物院图书馆”。）
    进了门，
    取得了领书签，一人，黑脸、美髯，
    埋着头在翻检，
    在抄写，
    不断地在书堆里钻研，
    这样，一天，又一天，
    一年，又一年，
    十年，还不止十年，踏的地为他而凹，
    坐的椅为他而穿，是在十九世纪——
    五十、六十年代之间。
   （二）好繁荣的工厂，
    浑浊的空气，
    轰隆轰隆的声响，
    上工，
    放工，
    一大群放，
    又一大群上。
    怎么？
    十岁童工也有，
    六岁的也有，连四岁的，都叫他们站在做工椅
    上，不满十小时不放。这般小的孩子们，
    那里撑得住呢？
    太不人道了！
    太不人道了！
    天天还把鸦片来喂，
    喂成小猴子一样，
    简直不让他们成长。荒唐！
    说不完的荒唐，
    人们在愤怒，在顿足，
    老板们在狂欢，在鼓掌，一人，黑脸、美髯，
    看得明明白白了、
    一齐写在他的书上。
   （三）到底是一现的昙花，
    一会儿繁荣，
    一会儿雕谢，总跳不出周期性的变化。
    为的是什么？它们都靠着剥削工人们的剩余劳动
    来发家，
    只知有我，
    不许有你，
    更那许有他。一会儿，
    它们感到危机！
    大危机！
    问题在无法使市场更多、更大。飞！飞！
    尽管飞着殖民主义者的战斗机，奔！奔！
    尽管奔着帝国主义者的坦克车，
    无奈市场和生产力间的矛盾，前者数学级数的增加，
    无法配合后者几何级数的增加。
    当然，主要还是被剥削的劳动大
    众在高速度贫困化。
    当！
    丧钟在响，
    当！当！
    是那家？资本家。
    （四）生产资料国有，
    消灭财产私有，
    第一声这样高喊，
    请读“共产党宣言”。“让那些统治阶级在共产主义革命
    面前发抖吧！
    无产者只失却了颈上的锁链。”“各尽所能，
    各取所需，”
    还不够理想么！这是共产主义社会优越性的表现。
    第一步：
     “各尽所能，
    按劳取酬。”这是社会主义社会的生活特点。
    先后给天才者发见。全国集体化农业实现了，
    资本主义经济成分被消灭了，这是世界第一个社会主义国家苏联。
    （五）世界性的社会主义在发展，
    高潮的涌现，
    波沫的四溅，从东欧到亚洲，
    从莫斯科到北京，从二十世纪一十年代，
    到四十、五十年代，从发展中演变，
    新民主主义出现了。
    由此而来的，民族统一战线，
    人民民主统一战线，是谁跟着社会规律性的发展，
    正确地配合着它特定的条件？
    都让人们严格地考验。掌握着原则性，
    适应着特殊性，
    这是不是马克思主义呢？
    谁说不是？“不同的国家，
    不同的办法”，
    早明明白白地写上了共产党
      宣言。
    真理都在眼前，
    是谁发见最先？让千千万万人，
    千千万万家，信仰他，
    纪念他，
    敬礼他，
    人人心里头，
    家家壁上头，一人，黑脸、美髯。
    到今年，他离开了世界74周年。
