Title: Zhengzhou Abrasive Wheel Factory (Picture)
Author: Sha Sha
Date: 1957-03-24
Page: 2

　　Zhengzhou Abrasive Wheel Factory
    The Zhengzhou Abrasive Wheel Factory, China's first new comprehensive grinding tool manufacturing plant, is under intense construction. This factory is being built with the help of the German Democratic Republic and will produce products such as grinding wheels and emery.

### 郑州砂轮厂（图片）
沙莎
1957-03-24
第2版()
专栏：

　　郑州砂轮厂
    我国第一座新型的综合性的研磨工具制造厂——郑州砂轮厂正在紧张施工。这个厂是民主德国帮助我国建设的，建成后将生产砂轮、金钢砂等产品。
　　　　　　　    沙莎摄（郑州日报社稿）
