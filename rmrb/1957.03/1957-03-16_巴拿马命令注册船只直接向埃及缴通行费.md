Title: Panama Orders Ships Registered There to Pay Suez Canal Tolls Directly to Egypt
Author:
Date: 1957-03-16
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, March 15th: Panama newspaper reports: Panama's Foreign Minister Aquilino Boyd has ordered that all ships registered in Panama must pay Suez Canal tolls directly to Egypt when passing through the Suez Canal, and not to the "Suez Canal Users Association."

### 巴拿马命令注册船只直接向埃及缴通行费

1957-03-16
第7版()
专栏：

    巴拿马命令注册船只直接向埃及缴通行费
    据新华社15日讯  巴拿马报纸消息：巴拿马外交部长阿基利诺·波伊德已经命令凡在巴拿马注册的船只在经过苏伊士运河的时候一律直接向埃及缴纳通行费而不要缴给“运河使用国协会”。
