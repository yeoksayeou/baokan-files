Title: Our Men's Table Tennis Team Wins All Six Matches, Defeating Romania Five to Two
Author:
Date: 1957-03-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, March 10th: Stockholm dispatch: The Chinese men's and women's table tennis teams both achieved major victories in the World Table Tennis Championships on March 9th. Following the Chinese women's team's victory over the England team in the morning, the Chinese men's team defeated the world-renowned Romanian team five to two in the evening, maintaining an excellent record of six wins in six matches. If the Chinese men's team wins against the Belgian team, they can secure the group championship and participate in the quarter-finals.

The Romanian team was the third-place finisher in the previous tournament and has famous players such as Gantner (the sixth-seeded player in this singles competition), Retter, and Harasztozy. They had not lost before their match against the Chinese team.

The results of each match were: Jiang Yongning vs. Gantner - 21-18, 24-26, 16-21. Wang Chuanyao vs. Harasztozy - 21-19, 11-21, 23-21. Fu Qifang vs. Retter - 21-12, 21-18. Wang Chuanyao vs. Gantner - 21-13, 21-14. Jiang Yongning vs. Retter - 14-19, 7-8 (score when the allotted time expired). Fu Qifang vs. Harasztozy - 14-21, 21-17, 21-17. Wang Chuanyao vs. Retter - 21-12, 21-15.

### 我国男子乒乓球队六战六胜  以五比二击败罗马尼亚队

1957-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    我国男子乒乓球队六战六胜
    以五比二击败罗马尼亚队
    新华社柏林10日电　斯德哥尔摩消息：中国男女乒乓球队3月9日在世界乒乓球锦标赛中都取得了重大的胜利。继中国女子选手在上午击败了英格兰队以后，中国男子队又在晚上以五比二击败了世界著名的罗马尼亚队，而保持着六战六胜的优异成绩。中国男子队只要再战胜比利时队，就可以取得小组冠军而参加复赛。
    罗马尼亚队是上届的第三名，拥有著名的选手甘特纳（这届单打的第六号种子选手）、雷特和哈拉斯托西。他们在同中国队比赛前一直没有输过。
    各盘比赛的结果是：姜永宁对甘特纳——二十一比十八，二十四比二十六，十六比二十一。王传耀对哈拉斯托西——二十一比十九，十一比二十一，二十三比二十一。傅其芳对雷特——二十一比十二，二十一比十八。王传耀对甘特纳——二十一比十三，二十一比十四。姜永宁对雷特——十四比十九，七比八（规定时间已到时的比数）。傅其芳对哈拉斯托西——十四比二十一，二十一比十七，二十一比十七。王传耀对雷特——二十一比十二，二十一比十五。
