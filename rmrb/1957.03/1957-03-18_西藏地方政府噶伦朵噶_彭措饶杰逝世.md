Title: The death of Dorje Phagpa Tulkue, Kalon of the Local Government of Tibet

Author:

Date: 1957-03-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Xigaze, March 15th: Dorje Phagpa Tulkue, a member of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, Kalon of the Local Government of Tibet, and Lieutenant General and Second Deputy Commander of the Tibet Military Region, passed away here on the afternoon of the 13th due to illness at the age of sixty-one.

    That evening, Major General Jin Shaoshan, Deputy Political Commissar of the Tibet Military Region, and Hui Yiran, Director of the Rural Work Department of the CPC Tibet Working Committee, went to offer condolences on behalf of the Tibet Military Region and the CPC Tibet Working Committee, respectively.

    Dorje Phagpa Tulkue contracted arteriosclerosis while traveling with the Dalai Lama from India back to China via Xigaze. He unfortunately passed away despite medical treatment.

### 西藏地方政府噶伦朵噶·彭措饶杰逝世

1957-03-18
第6版()
专栏：

    西藏地方政府噶伦朵噶·彭措饶杰逝世
    新华社日喀则15日电　西藏自治区筹备委员会委员、西藏地方政府噶伦、西藏军区第二副司令员朵噶·彭措饶杰中将于13日下午9时在这里患病逝世，享年六十一岁。
    当晚，西藏军区副政治委员金绍山少将和中共西藏工委农村工作部部长惠毅然分别代表西藏军区和中共西藏工委前往吊唁致哀。
    朵噶·彭措饶杰这次随达赖喇嘛从印度回国途经日喀则患血管硬化症，经治疗无效，不幸逝世。
