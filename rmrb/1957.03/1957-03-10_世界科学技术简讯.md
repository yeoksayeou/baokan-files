World Science and Technology Briefs

    Solar Reflector
    Recently, a scientist in Alma-Ata, Soviet Union, created a solar reflector that can be used to treat many kinds of skin diseases, including skin tuberculosis. If used to irradiate duck eggs, the hatched ducklings grow much faster than ordinary ducklings.
    This solar reflector is constructed from specially made flat mirrors arranged in a concave frame. When sunlight passes through the mirrors, ultraviolet rays are absorbed and lose their destructive power, while infrared rays are concentrated. This intense infrared radiation can stimulate the vitality of human or other animal organic tissues and eliminate harmful bacteria.
    Most Efficient Jet Engine
    A British aircraft company recently announced the creation of the world's most efficient jet engine. It has a thrust of 9,000 kilograms and can enable an aircraft to fly at an altitude of 17,000 meters at a speed of 1,600 to 3,200 kilometers per hour. A British expert said that the limit that jet engines could normally reach now seems to have been achieved.
    Russian-English Automatic Translator
    Recently, a peculiar Russian-English automatic translator appeared in Michigan, USA, arousing great interest. It possesses 64,000 corresponding Russian and English vocabulary words and can automatically translate Russian works on theoretical and applied physics into English. (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 世界科学技术简讯

1957-03-10
第6版()
专栏：

    世界科学技术简讯
    太阳反射镜
    苏联阿拉木图一位科学家不久前创制了一种太阳反射镜，它可以用来治疗许多种皮肤病，其中包括皮肤结核。如果用它来照射鸭蛋，孵出的雏鸭要比一般雏鸭长得快得多。
    这种太阳反射镜是由一些特制的平面镜装置在一个凹形的框子里构成的。太阳光通过镜子时，紫外线被吸收而丧失破坏力，同时，红外线便被集中起来。这种强烈的红外线能够激起人类或其他动物有机体组织的生命力，消灭有害的细菌。
    功效最大的喷气发动机
    英国一家飞机公司最近宣布制成了一种世界上功效最大的喷气发动机。它有九千公斤的推力，能够使飞机在一万七千公尺高空飞行的时候，每小时的速度达一千六百公里到三千二百公里。英国一位专家说，通常喷气发动机可能达到的限度现在似乎已经达到了。
        俄英自动翻译机
    美国密执安州不久前出现了一架奇特的俄英自动翻译机，引起了人们巨大的兴趣。它拥有俄、英文对等的字汇各六万四千个，能够自动地把有关理论物理和实用物理方面的俄文著作译成英文。（新华社讯）
