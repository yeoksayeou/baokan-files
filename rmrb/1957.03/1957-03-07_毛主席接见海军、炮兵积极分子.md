Title: Chairman Mao Receives Naval and Artillery Activists
Author:
Date: 1957-03-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th - Chairman Mao Zedong and Marshal He Long, this afternoon, received all the representatives of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Naval Activist Representative Conference and the Chinese People's Liberation Army Artillery Activist Representative Conference.

### 毛主席接见海军、炮兵积极分子

1957-03-07
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见海军、炮兵积极分子
    新华社6日讯　毛泽东主席和贺龙元帅，在今天下午先后接见了中国人民解放军海军积极分子代表会议和中国人民解放军炮兵积极分子代表会议的全体代表。
