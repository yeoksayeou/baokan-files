Title: Picture
Author: Wang Shaoqing
Date: 1957-03-11
Page: 4

    These are two students who have just left school, one named Han Fuchen and the other named Li Guiqin. They are now responsible for the electrical and mechanical maintenance and guiding workers in repair work at the Longfeng Mine in Fushun. Since starting work, they have ensured timely electricity and ventilation for the coal miners, and no accidents have occurred.

            Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Shaoqing
