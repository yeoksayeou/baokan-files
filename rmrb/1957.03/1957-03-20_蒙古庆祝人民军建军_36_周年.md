Title: Mongolia Celebrates the 36th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Army
Author:
Date: 1957-03-20
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, March 19th: The Political Department of the Mongolian People's Army held a meeting on the evening of the 18th to commemorate the 36th anniversary of the founding of the People's Army.

    At the meeting, Mongolian Minister of Military and Public Security, Lieutenant General Dorj, called on the People's Army to be vigilant against imperialist conspiracies, improve combat skills, and defend peace.

    Uudval, Chairman of the Central Council of Mongolian Trade Unions, Dangaasuren, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Youth League, and Dangaa, Second Secretary of the Ulan Bator City Committee of the People's Revolutionary Party, congratulated the People's Army on behalf of workers, youth, and the people of the capital, respectively.

    After the meeting, the People's Army Song and Dance Troupe performed song and dance programs.

### 蒙古庆祝人民军建军36周年

1957-03-20
第7版()
专栏：

    蒙古庆祝人民军建军36周年
    新华社乌兰巴托19日电　蒙古人民军政治部18日晚上举行人民军建军三十六周年纪念会。
    蒙古军事和公安部长道尔吉中将在会上要求人民军警惕帝国主义的阴谋，提高战斗技术，保卫和平。
    蒙古工会中央理事会主席乌德瓦尔、革命青年团中央委员会书记丹嘎苏伦和人民革命党乌兰巴托市委员会第二书记丹嘎分别代表工人、青年和首都人民向人民军祝贺。
    会后，人民军歌舞团表演了歌舞节目。
