Title: The United States is deliberately stirring up trouble in Indonesia; Dulles said we must support the separatists
Author:
Date: 1957-03-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 24th: "East Star Daily" reported on the 23rd, according to delayed news, US Secretary of State Dulles recently stated at the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization Council meeting that in order to bring Indonesia into the sphere of influence of the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization, it is necessary to weaken the position of the Indonesian central government. Therefore, it is very necessary to support the separatist movements in Indonesia and the "ideal" of certain elements to establish an "Indonesian Federal State."

Dulles also expressed concern at the meeting that the situation in Indonesia would make it less likely for the Masyumi Party to come to power.

### 美国存心在印度尼西亚捣乱　杜勒斯说必须支持分裂分子

1957-03-25
第6版()
专栏：

　　美国存心在印度尼西亚捣乱
　　杜勒斯说必须支持分裂分子
    新华社雅加达24日电　“东星报”23日报道，据迟到消息，美国国务卿杜勒斯最近在东南亚条约组织理事会会议上表示，为了把印度尼西亚拉入东南亚条约组织的势力范围之内，必须把印度尼西亚中央政府的地位削弱，因此非常必要支持印度尼西亚的分裂分子的运动和某些分子的成立“印度尼西亚联邦国”的“理想”。
    杜勒斯在会上并且表示担忧印度尼西亚的局势会使马斯友美党执政的可能性变得更小。
