Title: Siroky Visits Lushun-Dalian
Author:
Date: 1957-03-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency

　　Premier Siroky Visits Lushun-Dalian

According to Xinhua News Agency, the Czechoslovak government delegation headed by Premier Viliam Široký arrived in Lushun-Dalian, at the southernmost tip of the Liaodong Peninsula, by plane at 12:10 on the 28th from Beijing. The distinguished guests were welcomed at the airport by more than 3,000 representatives from all walks of life in Lushun-Dalian. That afternoon, accompanied by Song Li, the Mayor of Lushun-Dalian, and Hu Zhonghai, the Secretary of the Lushun-Dalian Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the distinguished guests visited the Dalian Shipbuilding Company.

On the 29th, Premier Viliam Široký and the Czechoslovak government delegation he led, accompanied by the leaders of the Lushunkou Base of the Chinese People's Navy, visited the Lushunkou Naval Base. The buildings and ships of all sizes in the base were fully decorated according to the highest naval etiquette to salute Premier Široký. Premier Široký reviewed the naval honor guard and the naval officers and soldiers lined up on the docks and ship decks. Then, Premier Široký and the members of the delegation visited several different types of ships. At noon, Premier Široký and others took a warship out of the harbor for a cruise.

Premier Široký left the following inscription on the warship: The Czechoslovak government delegation wishes the Navy of the People's Republic of China great achievements.

### 西罗基总理访问旅大

1957-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　西罗基总理访问旅大
    据新华社讯  以威廉·西罗基总理为首的捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团28日十二时十分由北京乘飞机到达辽东半岛最南端的旅大市。贵宾们在机场受到旅大市各界代表三千多人的欢迎。当天下午，贵宾们由旅大市市长宋黎、中共旅大市委书记胡忠海陪同参观了大连造船公司。
    29日，威廉·西罗基总理和他所率领的捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团由中国人民海军旅顺口基地首长陪同，参观了海军旅顺口基地。基地上的建筑物和大小舰艇上，都按照海军最高礼节挂满旗向西罗基总理致敬。西罗基总理检阅了海军仪仗队和排列在码头、舰艇甲板上的海军官兵。然后，西罗基总理和代表团团员们参观了几艘不同类型的舰艇。中午，西罗基总理等乘军舰出港游览。
    西罗基总理在军舰上留下了这样的题词：捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团祝中华人民共和国的海军取得很多的成就。
