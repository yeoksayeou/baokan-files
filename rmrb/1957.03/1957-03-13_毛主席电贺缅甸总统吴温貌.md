Title: Chairman Mao Cables Congratulations to Burmese President U Win Maung
Author:
Date: 1957-03-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 12th. Chairman Mao Zedong sent the following congratulatory telegram to the President of the Union of Burma, U Win Maung:

To His Excellency U Win Maung, President of the Union of Burma:

I am delighted to learn of Your Excellency's appointment as President of the Union of Burma and extend my warm congratulations. I wish the Union of Burma prosperity and strength, and I wish the friendly and cooperative relations between China and Burma even greater development.

Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
March 12, 1957

### 毛主席电贺缅甸总统吴温貌

1957-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席电贺缅甸总统吴温貌
    新华社12日讯　毛泽东主席致缅甸联邦总统吴温貌的贺电如下：缅甸联邦总统吴温貌阁下：
    欣悉阁下荣任缅甸联邦总统，谨致热烈的祝贺。祝缅甸联邦繁荣富强，祝中缅两国间的友好合作关系获得更进一步的发展。
      中华人民共和国主席毛泽东　1957年3月12日
