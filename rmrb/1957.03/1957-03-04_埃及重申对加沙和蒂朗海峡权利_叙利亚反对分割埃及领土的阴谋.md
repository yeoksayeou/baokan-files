Title: Egypt Reiterates Rights to Gaza and Straits of Tiran; Syria Opposes Conspiracy to Divide Egyptian Territory
Author:
Date: 1957-03-04
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, March 3rd: New York dispatch: Egyptian delegation spokesman to the United Nations General Assembly, Hassani, reiterated on the 2nd Egypt's sovereignty over the Gaza Strip and the Straits of Tiran (Note: at the entrance to the Gulf of Aqaba).
    Hassani told reporters that regarding the Gaza Strip issue, "Our position is that the mission of the United Nations Emergency Force stationed there is solely to supervise the withdrawal of Israeli troops and the takeover by the Egyptian government in accordance with the armistice agreement." Regarding the Straits of Tiran, "These are Egyptian territorial waters. Our claim to them is based on international law and all guidelines and regulations regarding such matters."
    Xinhua News Agency, March 3rd: Damascus dispatch: Syrian Prime Minister al-Assali said on the 2nd that Arab countries are insisting on the implementation of the resolution passed at the recent Arab four-nation summit, demanding Israel's immediate and unconditional withdrawal of troops.
    Al-Assali said at a press conference that Israel must not be allowed to gain any benefit from its aggression against Egypt.
    Syrian Foreign Minister Bitar said that any proposal to separate Gaza and Egyptian territory is a serious threat to peace in the Middle East.
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 3rd: Egypt's "Al-Ahram" said on the 2nd that Egypt will not be bound by any promises the United States makes to Israel.
   "Al-Ahram" said, "Israel can ask the United States for any guarantees it wants, and the United States can give Israel any promises it is willing to give, but no demand by Israel and no promise by the United States can prescribe any rights for anyone against us."
    The newspaper pointed out that according to the United Nations resolution, Israel's withdrawal must be unconditional. It also said that regarding Israel's attitude and any country that supports it, "Egypt will know what to do."

### 埃及重申对加沙和蒂朗海峡权利  叙利亚反对分割埃及领土的阴谋

1957-03-04
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及重申对加沙和蒂朗海峡权利
    叙利亚反对分割埃及领土的阴谋
    新华社3日讯  纽约消息：埃及出席联合国大会代表团发言人哈萨尼2日重申埃及对加沙地带和蒂朗海峡（附注：在亚喀巴湾的入口）的主权。
    哈萨尼对新闻记者说，关于加沙地带问题，“我们的立场是，联合国紧急部队驻在那里的任务仅仅是监督以色列军队撤退和埃及政府根据停战协定进行接管。”关于蒂朗海峡问题，“这是埃及的领海。我们对它的要求是以国际法和关于这类问题的一切准则和规定为根据的。”
    据新华社3日讯  大马士革消息：叙利亚总理阿萨利2日说，阿拉伯各国正坚持要求实现最近阿拉伯四国首脑会议上通过的要求以色列立即无条件撤军的决议。
    阿萨利在一次记者招待会上说，决不能允许以色列从它对埃及的侵略行动中获得任何好处。
    叙利亚外交部长比塔尔说，任何企图把加沙和埃及领土分割开来的建议都是对中东和平的严重威胁。
    据新华社开罗3日电  埃及“金字塔报”2日说，埃及将不受美国给以色列的任何诺言的约束。
   “金字塔报”说，“以色列可以向美国要求它所想要的任何保证，而美国可以给以色列它愿意给的任何保证的诺言，但是以色列的任何要求和美国的任何诺言都不能为任何人规定任何权利来反对我们。”
    这家报纸指出，根据联合国决议，以色列撤退必须是无条件的。它并且说，对于以色列的态度和任何支持它的国家，“埃及将会知道怎么办。”
