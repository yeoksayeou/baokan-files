Title: Qaidam Exploration Team Discovers Large Lead-Zinc Mine
Author: Li Zhi'an
Date: 1957-03-31
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Da Qaidam, March 30th (Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Zhi'an): A geological exploration team conducting exploration in a certain area of Qaidam recently formally submitted a geological report to the Ministry of Geology stating that this area contains rich lead-zinc deposits. The geological engineers of this exploration team said that this is one of the largest lead-zinc mines discovered in China so far.
    This exploration team has been working hard here for more than two years.
    This lead-zinc mine was reported to the government by local people in 1952. Based on this clue, field exploration personnel conducted exploration here.

### 柴达木勘探队发现大铅锌矿
李治安
1957-03-31
第2版()
专栏：

　　柴达木勘探队发现大铅锌矿
    据新华社大柴旦30日电  新华社记者李治安报道：在柴达木某地进行勘探的地质勘探队，最近正式向地质部提出了这个地区的含有丰富铅锌矿的地质报告书。这个勘探队的地质工程师说，这是目前我国发现的最大铅锌矿之一。
    这个勘探队已经在这里进行了两年多的艰苦劳动。
    这个铅锌矿是当地群众在1952年向政府报告的。根据这个线索，野外勘探人员才在这里进行了勘探。
