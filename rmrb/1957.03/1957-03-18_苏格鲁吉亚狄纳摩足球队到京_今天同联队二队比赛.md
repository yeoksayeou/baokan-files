Title: FC Dinamo Tbilisi Arrives in Beijing; To Play Friendly Match Today Against Joint Team B

Author:
Date: 1957-03-18
Page: 6

    FC Dinamo Tbilisi Arrives in Beijing
    To Play Friendly Match Today Against Joint Team B
    (Our Newspaper) News: The Soviet Georgian Republic's FC Dinamo Tbilisi team, a group of sixteen, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 16th. On the afternoon of the 18th, they will play a friendly match against a Joint Team B composed of the Beijing Youth Team and the "August 1st" team at the Xian Nong Tan Stadium. They will return home after the match.
    FC Dinamo Tbilisi was passing through Beijing on their way back home after competing in Burma. In Rangoon, they played two matches against the Burmese First National Mixed Team, winning with scores of nine to one and five to four respectively. Among the sixteen players, there are three Honored Athletes. The team leader, head coach, and coach are also Honored Athletes. FC Dinamo Tbilisi ranked eighth in last year's All-Soviet Football Championship.

### 苏格鲁吉亚狄纳摩足球队到京  今天同联队二队比赛

1957-03-18
第6版()
专栏：

    苏格鲁吉亚狄纳摩足球队到京
    今天同联队二队比赛
    本报讯　苏联的格鲁吉亚共和国第比里斯“狄纳摩”足球队一行十六人，在16日下午到达北京。他们将在18日下午在先农坛体育场同北京青年队和“八一”队混合组成的联队二队进行一场友谊比赛。比赛完毕后即返国。
    第比里斯“狄纳摩”队是在缅甸比赛后返国途中路过北京的。他们在仰光曾两次同缅甸第一国家混合队比赛，分别以九比一和五比四获胜。十六名队员中，有三个功勋运动员，领队、主任教练和教练也都是功勋运动员。第比里斯“狄纳摩”队是去年全苏足球冠军赛的第八名。
