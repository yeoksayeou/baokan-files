Title: Xinjiang Cold Storage Plant Enters Production
Author:
Date: 1957-03-20
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency report:

    A newly built cold storage plant in Urumqi has officially gone into production. The plant's freezing workshop can freeze fresh meat into frozen meat with a temperature of minus 20 degrees Celsius in one day and night. Six cold storage rooms with different temperatures can store a total of more than 580 tons of frozen meat, frozen fish, fresh eggs, and fruits, respectively.

### 新疆一冷冻厂投入生产

1957-03-20
第2版()
专栏：

    新疆一冷冻厂投入生产
    据新华社讯　乌鲁木齐市新建的一座冷冻厂已经正式投入生产。这个厂的冷冻车间，可以把鲜肉在一昼夜间冻成温度为摄氏零下二十度的冻肉。六所不同温度的冷藏库，可以分别贮藏冻肉、冻鱼、新鲜鸡蛋和水果等共五百八十多吨。
