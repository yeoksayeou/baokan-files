Title: Japanese Textile Workers' Union Inspection Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-03-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 16th: Chen Shaomin, Chairman of the National Committee of the Textile Workers' Union of China, received all members of the Japanese National Federation of Textile Industry Workers' Unions China Inspection Team this morning.

　　This inspection team arrived in Beijing yesterday at the invitation of the National Committee of the Textile Workers' Union of China. On their way to Beijing, the inspection team visited textile factories in Wuhan, Zhengzhou, and other places.

### 日纤维业工会考察团到京

1957-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

    日纤维业工会考察团到京
    新华社16日讯　中国纺织工会全国委员会主席陈少敏在今天上午接见了日本全国纤维产业工会同盟中国考察团全体团员。
    这个考察团是应中国纺织工会全国委员会的邀请在昨天到达北京的。考察团在来北京途中曾在武汉、郑州等地参观了纺织工厂。
