Title: World Federation of Trade Unions Sends Congratulations on Ghana's Independence
Author:
Date: 1957-03-08
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 7th. The World Federation of Trade Unions sent telegrams on the 5th to Ghanaian Prime Minister Kwame Nkrumah and the Gold Coast Trades Union Congress, congratulating the people of Ghana on their declaration of independence.
    In its telegram to the Gold Coast Trades Union Congress, the World Federation of Trade Unions expressed its belief that the workers and trade unions of Ghana will unite more broadly to complete the new tasks of building the country and consolidating national sovereignty, in order to improve the living standards of workers and expand their rights.

### 世界工联致电祝贺加纳独立

1957-03-08
第6版()
专栏：

    世界工联致电祝贺加纳独立
    据新华社布拉格7日电　世界工会联合会5日分别打电报给加纳总理恩克鲁玛和黄金海岸工会大会，祝贺加纳人民宣布独立。
    世界工会联合会在致黄金海岸工会大会的电报中表示相信加纳的工人和工会将更广泛地团结起来，完成建设国家和巩固国家主权的新任务，以便提高工人的生活水平和扩大他们的权利。
