Title: Chinese Academy of Sciences Zoological Research Laboratory Announces Animal Science Research Plan, Will Conduct Large-Scale Animal Science Research Work in China
Author:
Date: 1957-03-06
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency:

    Chinese Academy of Sciences Zoological Research Laboratory
    Announces Animal Science Research Plan
    Will Conduct Large-Scale Animal Science Research Work in China
    Xinhua News Agency: China's zoological research work will enter a new stage of large-scale development.
    Starting this year, field stations will be established in Inner Mongolia, Urumqi, Hainan Island, and other places with abundant and diverse animal life. An animal farm or zoo specifically for animal science researchers will be built in Beijing. This will enable animal scientists to gradually conduct surveys of animal distribution in various major regions, clarifying the species and habits of animals living on the Chinese mainland, in the oceans, and in inland rivers. This will provide systematic data for the planned development of these resources.
    The key points of this research plan were announced at the inaugural meeting of the Academic Committee of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Zoological Research Laboratory, which opened on the 4th. The plan is scheduled to be completed in six years. According to this plan, emphasis will be placed on investigating birds and mammals in the Northwest, Southwest, Inner Mongolia, and tropical regions; aquatic animals in coastal areas and some rivers; and beneficial and harmful animals related to agriculture, forestry, animal husbandry, and hygiene. At the same time, research in animal morphology, ecology, and other aspects will be strengthened.
    The plan stipulates the establishment of a Central Zoological Museum in the capital to house specimens collected by animal scientists from various regions. It will also popularize zoological knowledge among the masses, enabling them to understand the country's rich animal resources. The Chinese Academy of Sciences Zoological Research Laboratory has already collected more than 87,000 specimens.
    The Chinese Academy of Sciences Zoological Research Laboratory was established and developed in 1953 on the basis of the Animal Specimen Committee. The existing staff has increased from 18 to more than 200 people. Over the past four years, it has conducted surveys and research on animal resources in Northeast China, South China, the Yellow Sea, and the Bohai Sea; conducted surveys of birds and animals in conjunction with agricultural production; and compiled illustrated descriptions of fur-bearing animals. Many special studies have also been carried out on the history of Chinese zoology.
    The Academic Committee of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Zoological Research Laboratory, established today, is composed of sixteen academically accomplished experts from relevant universities, agriculture, aquatic product departments, and the Chinese Academy of Sciences system throughout the country.

### 中国科学院动物研究室  宣布动物科学研究计划  将大规模开展我国动物科学研究工作

1957-03-06
第7版()
专栏：

    中国科学院动物研究室
    宣布动物科学研究计划
    将大规模开展我国动物科学研究工作
    新华社讯　我国动物学研究工作将进入大规模开展的新阶段。
    今年开始，就要在动物比较丰富而种类又很多的内蒙古、乌鲁木齐、海南岛等地建立工作站，在北京要建设一个专供动物科学工作者用的动物饲养场或动物园，以便动物科学工作者逐步地在各个大区进行动物区系的调查，弄清生长在我国大陆、海洋、内河里的动物种类、生活习性，为有计划的开发这些资源提供系统的资料。
    这个研究计划要点是在4日开幕的中国科学院动物研究室学术委员会成立会上宣布的，这个计划预定六年完成。按照这个计划，要着重调查西北、西南、内蒙古和热带地区的鸟类和兽类，沿海及部分河流的水生动物，有关农林畜牧卫生的益害动物，同时也要加强动物形态和生态等各方面的研究。
    计划中规定在首都筹建中央动物博物馆，收藏动物科学工作者在各地采集来的标本，同时向群众普及动物学的知识，使群众了解祖国丰富的动物资源。现在中国科学院动物研究室采集到的标本已有八万七千多号。
    中国科学院动物研究室是1953年在动物标本委员会的基础上建立并发展起来的，现有的工作人员已由过去十八人增加到了二百多人。四年来已经在东北、华南和黄海、渤海等地区进行了动物资源的调查研究；结合农业生产对鸟兽进行了调查；编著了毛皮兽图说。关于我国动物学史等方面也作了很多专题研究。
    今天成立的中国科学院动物研究室学术委员会，是由全国各有关的高等学校、农业、水产部门和中国科学院系统的十六位学术上有成绩的专家组成的。
