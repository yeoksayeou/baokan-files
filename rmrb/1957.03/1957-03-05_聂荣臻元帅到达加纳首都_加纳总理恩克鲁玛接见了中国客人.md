Title: Marshal Nie Rongzhen Arrives in the Capital of Ghana; Ghanaian Prime Minister Nkrumah Receives Chinese Guests
Author:
Date: 1957-03-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Accra, 4th: Marshal Nie Rongzhen, the special envoy of the People’s Republic of China to participate in the independence celebration ceremony of Ghana (Gold Coast), and his entourage arrived here on the 3rd. They were welcomed at the airport by Minister of Trade and Labor, Kozo Botsio, Minister Without Portfolio, Iddrissu, and other government leaders.

    When Marshal Nie Rongzhen delivered a speech at the airport, he expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the Ghanaian government for the invitation and the welcome extended to him. He said: "The Chinese people have always had deep sympathy for the struggle of your people for national independence. At the time of your historic independence celebration ceremony, during these days of jubilation for the Ghanaian people, I bring you the warm congratulations and highest respect of the people of the People's Republic of China."
   “The delegations of our two governments had friendly contacts during the Bandung Conference. I am convinced that, with your country's independence, the friendly relations between our two countries will be further developed."
   “Long live the friendship between China and Ghana! Long live the independence of Ghana!”
    That evening, Ghanaian Prime Minister Nkrumah received Marshal Nie Rongzhen at the State House and held very friendly talks. Nie Rongzhen was accompanied to the State House by Ke Hua, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
    Also present at the meeting were Ghanaian Minister of Trade and Labor, Botsio, and Minister of Finance, Gbedemah.

### 聂荣臻元帅到达加纳首都  加纳总理恩克鲁玛接见了中国客人

1957-03-05
第1版()
专栏：

    聂荣臻元帅到达加纳首都
    加纳总理恩克鲁玛接见了中国客人
    新华社阿克拉4日电  中华人民共和国参加加纳（黄金海岸）庆祝独立典礼特使聂荣臻元帅和随员三日到达这里。他们在机场上受到了贸易和劳工部长科佐·博齐约、不管部长伊杜塞以及其他政府领导人的欢迎。
    聂荣臻元帅在机场致词的时候，表示衷心感谢加纳政府的邀请和对他的欢迎。他说：“中国人民一向对贵国人民争取民族独立的斗争怀着深厚的同情。在贵国的具有历史意义的庆祝独立典礼的时候，在加纳人民兴高采烈的这些日子里，我给你们带来了中华人民共和国人民的热烈祝贺和最高的敬意。
   “我们两国政府的代表团在万隆会议期间进行了友好的接触。我深信：由于贵国取得独立，我们两国之间的友好关系将进一步发展。
   “中国和加纳的友谊万岁！加纳的独立万岁！”
    当天晚上，加纳总理恩克鲁玛在国家大厦接见了聂荣臻元帅，并且进行了非常友好的谈话。聂荣臻是在中国外交部西亚非洲司司长柯华陪同下前往国家大厦的。
    会谈时在座的还有加纳贸易和劳工部长博齐约和财政部长格贝德马赫。
