Title: Executive Committee Meeting of the International Organization of Journalists to be Held in Beijing, President of the International Organization of Journalists and Representatives from Various Countries Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-03-31
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, March 30th. The President of the International Organization of Journalists (IOJ), Jean-Maurice Hermann (France), Vice President Mrs. K.M. Ridberg (Finland), and Secretary-General Jaroslav Knobloch (Czechoslovakia) arrived in Beijing by plane at 3:30 this afternoon. They are here to attend the Executive Committee meeting of the International Organization of Journalists in Beijing.

Also arriving in Beijing on the same flight were representatives from various countries attending the Executive Committee meeting of the International Organization of Journalists: Georg Klaus, Chairman of the Journalists' Union of the German Democratic Republic, First Secretary Grau; Jan Halpern, Acting Editor-in-Chief of the Polish "Warsaw Life Newspaper"; Teofil Glowacki, Editor-in-Chief of the "Voice of Labor Newspaper"; Victor Baladeanu, Editorial Board Member of the Romanian "Spark Newspaper"; Buzil Sion, Editorial Board Member of the "Romanian Free Newspaper"; and Mexican journalist Gregoria Rosas Herrera.

Liubin Vilev, Vice Chairman of the Bulgarian Journalists Association and Deputy Editor-in-Chief of "Worker's Cause Newspaper," and Ochirlin Penchag, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the Mongolian "Truth Newspaper," and Naidanchin Zeinzaf, Secretary-General of "Crocodile" magazine, also arrived in Beijing by plane today.

Ahmed Kassem Gouda, board member of the Middle East News Agency and Editor-in-Chief of the Egyptian "Republic Newspaper," who was invited to attend the meeting as a guest, also arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them today included Deng Tuo, Vice President of the International Organization of Journalists and Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association; Pavel I. Pronin, Deputy Secretary-General of the International Organization of Journalists; Hyun Pil-hoon, the Korean representative attending the meeting; Nguyen Thanh Le, the Vietnamese representative; and Wu Lengxi, Jin Zhonghua, Wang Yi, and Li Bingquan, the Chinese representatives.

Pavel I. Pronin (Soviet Union), Deputy Secretary-General of the International Organization of Journalists, Hyun Pil-hoon, Editor-in-Chief of the Korean "Labor News," Li Chang-soo, Member of the Korean Journalists Union Committee, Nguyen Thanh Le, Secretary-General of the Vietnamese Journalists Association and Editorial Board Member of the "People's Newspaper," Le Hao, correspondent of the Vietnamese News Agency stationed in Beijing, and Herfian, correspondent of the Romanian News Agency stationed in Beijing, had already arrived in Beijing earlier.

Representatives from Hungary, Czechoslovakia, and the Soviet Union attending this meeting, as well as guests from Indonesia and Pakistan invited to attend this meeting, will also arrive soon.

### 出席国际新闻工作者协会执委会北京会议  国际新闻工作者协会主席和各国代表到京

1957-03-31
第5版()
专栏：

    出席国际新闻工作者协会执委会北京会议
    国际新闻工作者协会主席和各国代表到京
    新华社30日讯　国际新闻工作者协会主席让·莫里斯·埃尔曼（法国），副主席克·麦·里德堡夫人（芬兰），秘书长雅罗斯拉夫·克诺布洛赫（捷克斯洛伐克）今天下午三时三十分乘飞机到达北京。他们是前来出席国际新闻工作者协会执行委员会北京会议的。
    同机到达北京的，还有出席国际新闻工作者协会执行委员会北京会议的各国代表：德意志民主共和国新闻工作者联合会主席格奥尔格·克劳斯、第一书记格劳耳，波兰“华沙生活报”代理总编辑杨·哈尔佩恩、“劳动之声报”总编辑特奥菲尔·格洛瓦茨基，罗马尼亚“火花报”编委维克多·巴拉德亚努、“罗马尼亚自由报”编委布齐尔·西昂，墨西哥记者格雷戈丽亚·罗萨斯·埃雷拉。
    保加利亚新闻工作者协会副主席、“工人事业报”副总编辑柳宾·维列夫，蒙古“真理报”副主编奥奇连·彭查格、“鳄鱼”杂志秘书长纳伊丹钦·泽因扎夫，也在今天先后乘飞机到达北京。
    应邀参加会议的来宾、中东通讯社董事、埃及“共和国报”总编辑艾哈迈德·卡塞姆·古达，也在今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    今天先后到机场欢迎的，有国际新闻工作者协会副主席、中华全国新闻工作者协会主席邓拓，国际新闻工作者协会副秘书长帕维尔·伊·普罗宁，出席会议的朝鲜代表玄弼勋，越南代表阮成黎，中国代表吴冷西、金仲华、王揖、李炳泉等。
    出席会议的国际新闻工作者协会副秘书长帕维尔·伊·普罗宁（苏联），朝鲜“劳动新闻”总编辑玄弼勋、朝鲜记者同盟委员会委员李昌洙，越南新闻工作者协会总书记、“人民报”编委阮成黎，越南通讯社驻北京记者黎豪，罗马尼亚通讯社驻北京记者赫尔淮安，都已先期到达北京。
    出席这次会议的匈牙利、捷克斯洛伐克、苏联的代表以及应邀参加这次会议的印度尼西亚和巴基斯坦的来宾也将在最近到达。
