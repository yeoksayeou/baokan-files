Title: World Ice Hockey Championship Continues

Author: 
Date: 1957-03-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 2nd. The World Ice Hockey Championship continued with four games yesterday and the day before.

In the four games between the Soviet team and the Polish team, the Czechoslovak team and the Finnish team, the Swedish team and the Austrian team, and the Japanese team and the East German team, the Soviet team defeated the Polish team with a score of ten to one, the Czechoslovak team defeated the Finnish team with a score of three to zero, and the Swedish team defeated the Austrian team with a score of ten to zero.

Currently, the Soviet team and the Swedish team are leading with eight points each. Next, the Czechoslovak team has six points, and the Finnish team has four points. It is expected that there will be fierce matches between the Soviet team and the Czechoslovak team today, and between the Soviet team and the Swedish team on March 5th.

### 世界冰球赛继续举行

1957-03-03
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    世界冰球赛继续举行
    据新华社莫斯科2日电  世界冰球赛昨天和前天继续进行了四场比赛。
    苏联队对波兰队、捷克斯洛伐克队对芬兰队、瑞典队对奥地利队和日本队对民主德国队这四场比赛中，苏联队以十比一胜波兰队，捷克斯洛伐克队以三比零胜芬兰队，瑞典队以十比零胜奥地利队。
    现在，苏联队和瑞典队领先，各得八分。其次，捷克斯洛伐克队得六分，芬兰队得四分。预料今天苏联队和捷克斯洛伐克队以及3月5日苏联队和瑞典队将有激烈比赛。
