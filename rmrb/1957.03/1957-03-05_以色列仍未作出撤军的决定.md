Title: Israel Still Has Not Made a Decision to Withdraw Troops
Author:
Date: 1957-03-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jerusalem, Israeli Area, March 4th: After holding a four-hour meeting on the morning of March 3rd, the Israeli cabinet still has not taken action to withdraw its troops from the Gulf of Aqaba and the Gaza Strip. The Israeli Foreign Ministry spokesman definitively announced on March 2nd that the Israeli cabinet would make a final decision on the withdrawal issue at this meeting. An official communique also stated that the cabinet would hold another meeting on the 4th in order to issue a final statement to the Israeli parliament on the afternoon of the 4th.

Ben-Gurion conferred with the US Ambassador to Israel, Lawson, for thirty-five minutes on the afternoon of March 3rd. The French Ambassador to Israel also went to Ben-Gurion's home to hold discussions with him.

### 以色列仍未作出撤军的决定

1957-03-05
第6版()
专栏：

    以色列仍未作出撤军的决定
    据新华社4日讯  耶路撒冷以色列区消息：以色列内阁在3日上午举行一次四小时的会议后，仍然还没有采取把它的军队撤出亚喀巴湾和加沙地带的行动。以色列外交部发言人2日曾经肯定地宣布，以色列内阁要在这次会议上就撤军问题作出最后决定。官方公报还说，内阁要在4日再举行一次会议，以便在4日下午向以色列议会发表最后的声明。
    本—古里安在3日下午同美国驻以色列大使劳逊商谈了三十五分钟。法国驻以色列大使也曾到本—古里安的家里同他举行了商谈。
