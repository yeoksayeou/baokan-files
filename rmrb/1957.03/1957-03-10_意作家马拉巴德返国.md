Title: Italian Writer Malaparte Returns Home

Author:

Date: 1957-03-10
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The famous Italian writer, Malaparte, who came to China to attend the Commemoration of the 20th Anniversary of Lu Xun's Death and the 90th Anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's Birth, had a relapse of an old illness during his visit to China. After consultations and short-term treatment by Soviet, Italian, and Chinese doctors, he left Beijing today accompanied by his family to return to his country. Those who saw him off at the airport included Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Xia Yan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture, Li Kehong, President of the China Union Medical College Hospital, and Xing Ye, Director of the Foreign Literature Committee of the Chinese Writers Association, and many others.

### 意作家马拉巴德返国

1957-03-10
第6版()
专栏：

    意作家马拉巴德返国
    新华社8日讯　来我国参加鲁迅逝世二十周年纪念会及孙中山九十周年诞辰纪念会的意大利著名作家马拉巴德，在我国访问期间，旧病复发，经苏联、意大利及我国医生的会诊和短期治疗后，已于今日由他的家属陪同离北京返国。到机场送行的有中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南、文化部副部长夏衍、中国协和医院院长李克鸿及中国作家协会外国文学委员会主任邢野等多人。
