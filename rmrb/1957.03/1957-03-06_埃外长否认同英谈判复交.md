Title: Egyptian Foreign Minister Denies Negotiations with Britain to Restore Relations
Author:
Date: 1957-03-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, March 5th. According to a report in Cairo's "Al-Ahram", Egyptian Foreign Minister Fawzi, when interviewed by the newspaper's reporter in New York on the 4th, flatly denied newspaper reports that he was secretly holding talks with Britain at the United Nations on the issue of restoring diplomatic relations.

Fawzi said that these reports were groundless and that he had no such intention.

The "New York Times" dispatch from Cairo on March 2 stated that Egyptian President Nasser was "eager to restore diplomatic relations with Britain". It said, "In recent weeks, Egyptian Foreign Minister Fawzi has been in quiet contact with Britain at the United Nations regarding the restoration of relations."

### 埃外长否认同英谈判复交

1957-03-06
第6版()
专栏：

    埃外长否认同英谈判复交
    新华社5日讯　据开罗“金字塔报”报道，埃及外交部长法齐4日在纽约接见这家报纸的记者时，断然否认报纸上关于他在联合国中暗地里同英国就恢复外交关系问题进行会谈的消息。
    法齐说，这些消息是毫无根据的，而且他没有这个打算。
      “纽约时报”3月2日发自开罗的一条消息曾说，埃及总统纳赛尔“急于与英国恢复外交关系”。它说，“在最近几星期内，埃及外交部长法齐在联合国一直在与英国就恢复关系进行悄悄的接触。”
