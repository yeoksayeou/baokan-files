Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Congratulations on Ghana's Independence
Author: 
Date: 1957-03-09
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram on the 7th to the Ghana Trades Union Congress and the Ghana Free Trade Union Congress, congratulating them on Ghana's independence.
    The telegram stated: On behalf of all Chinese workers, we extend our heartfelt congratulations to our Ghanaian worker brothers on your country's independence. We wish you new achievements in safeguarding national independence, building your motherland, and improving the people's livelihood. May the friendship between our two countries' trade unions be strengthened day by day!

### 我国总工会电贺加纳独立

1957-03-09
第6版()
专栏：

    我国总工会电贺加纳独立
    新华社8日讯　中华全国总工会7日打电报给加纳工会大会和加纳自由工会大会，祝贺加纳独立。
    电报说，谨代表中国的全体工人向加纳工人兄弟们衷心地祝贺贵国的独立。祝你们在维护民族独立、建设祖国和提高人民生活的事业中获得新的成就。愿我们两国工会的友谊日益加强！
