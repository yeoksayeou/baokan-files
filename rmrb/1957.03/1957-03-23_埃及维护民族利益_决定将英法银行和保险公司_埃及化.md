Title: Egypt Upholds National Interests, Decides to "Egyptianize" British and French Banks and Insurance Companies
Author:
Date: 1957-03-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 21st: Egyptian Finance Minister al-Kaissouni announced on the 20th that the Egyptian government has decided to "Egyptianize" nine British and French banks, seventeen British and French insurance companies, and all shares of these two countries in Egyptian companies.

Egyptian President Nasser issued a decree on January 15th stipulating that all foreign banks, insurance companies, and trade organizations in Egypt must be owned by Egyptian citizens.

### 埃及维护民族利益　决定将英法银行和保险公司“埃及化”

1957-03-23
第6版()
专栏：

　　埃及维护民族利益
　　决定将英法银行和保险公司“埃及化”
    新华社21日讯　开罗消息：埃及财政部长凯舒尼20日宣布，埃及政府决定将九个英国和法国的银行、十七个英法保险公司以及在埃及公司里的这两个国家的所有的股份实行“埃及化”。
    埃及总统纳赛尔曾经在1月15日颁布一项法令，规定所有在埃及的外国银行、保险公司和贸易机构必须改归埃及公民所有。
