Title: Bulgarian National Assembly Approves This Year's Budget
Author:
Date: 1957-03-16
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, March 15th. The seventh session of the Bulgarian National Assembly closed on the 14th.

    The meeting unanimously approved the government's proposed 1957 national budget. The budget stipulates an income of over 18.5 billion leva and an expenditure of over 18.424 billion leva.

    In the budget, investments for developing the national economy account for more than 56%, an increase of 682 million leva compared to last year. Investment in agriculture (including state loans to agricultural production cooperatives) totals 2.869 billion leva. According to the budget, the lives of the Bulgarian people will be further improved. For example, the budget stipulates that this year's family child allowance will be 423 million leva, an increase of 300 million leva compared to the past; it also stipulates the use of 300 million leva to increase the wages of low-paid workers, 150 million leva to reduce food prices and improve food quality in canteens for workers and students, and 250 million leva to increase the purchase prices of certain agricultural products.

### 保国民议会通过今年预算

1957-03-16
第7版()
专栏：

    保国民议会通过今年预算
    新华社索非亚15日电  保加利亚国民议会第七次会议14日闭幕。
    会议一致通过了政府提出的1957年国家预算。预算规定收入为一百八十五亿多列弗，支出为一百八十四亿二千四百多万列弗。
    在预算中，用于发展国民经济的投资占56％以上，比去年增加六亿八千二百万列弗。对农业的投资（包括给农业生产合作社的国家贷款在内）共计二十八亿六千九百万列弗。根据预算，保加利亚人民的生活将得到进一步的改善。例如，预算规定，今年家庭子女补助金为四亿二千三百万列弗，比过去增加三亿列弗；此外还规定用三亿列弗来提高低薪职工的工资，用一亿五千万列弗来降低职工、学生等食堂的食品价格和提高食品质量，用两亿五千万列弗来提高某些农产品的收购价格等。
