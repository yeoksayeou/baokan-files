Title: Britain Replies to Japan, Refuses to Cancel Hydrogen Bomb Test
Author:
Date: 1957-03-25
Page: 6
　
　Xinhua News Agency, London, March 24th: Assistant Under-Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs Morland received Japanese Ambassador to Britain, Nishiharu Hiko on the 23rd and handed him the British government's reply to the Japanese government's notes of February 14th and March 6th. This reply "formally rejected" Japan's request to cancel the hydrogen bomb test at Christmas Island.

### 英国复照日本  拒绝取消氢弹试验

1957-03-25
第6版()
专栏：

　  英国复照日本
　  拒绝取消氢弹试验
    据新华社24日讯　伦敦消息：英国外交部助理次官摩兰德在23日接见了日本驻英大使西春彦，把英国政府对日本政府2月14日和3月6日的照会的复照交给他。这个复照“正式拒绝了”日本关于取消在圣诞岛的氢弹试验的要求。
