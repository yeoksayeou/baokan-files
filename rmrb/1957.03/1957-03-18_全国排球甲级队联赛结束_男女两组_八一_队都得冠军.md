Title: National Volleyball League Concludes; Both Men's and Women's "August 1st" Teams Win Championships
Author:
Date: 1957-03-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, March 17th. The 1957 National Volleyball League, held in Guangzhou, concluded today. The final standings of the twelve Class A men's volleyball teams and seven Class A women's volleyball teams after round-robin play are as follows:

    Men's Group:

    Chinese People's Liberation Army "August 1st" Team, Beijing Team, Guangzhou Team, Wuhan Institute of Physical Education Team, Shanghai Institute of Physical Education Team, Chengdu Institute of Physical Education Team, Chinese People's Liberation Army Public Security Army Team, Chinese People's Liberation Army Guangzhou Garrison Team, China Coal Mine Sports Association Team, Shenyang Institute of Physical Education Team, Chinese People's Liberation Army Nanjing Garrison Team, and Chongqing Team.

    Women's Group:

    Chinese People's Liberation Army "August 1st" Team, Shenyang Institute of Physical Education Team, Beijing Team, Shanghai Institute of Physical Education Team, Chengdu Institute of Physical Education Team, Chongqing Team, and Wuhan Institute of Physical Education Team.

### 全国排球甲级队联赛结束  男女两组“八一”队都得冠军

1957-03-18
第6版()
专栏：体育新闻

    全国排球甲级队联赛结束
    男女两组“八一”队都得冠军
    新华社广州17日电　在广州举行的1957年全国排球甲级队联赛今天全部结束。十二个甲级男子排球队和七个甲级女子排球队经过循环赛后的名次如下：
    男子组：
    中国人民解放军“八一”队、北京市队、广州市队、武汉体育学院队、上海体育学院队、成都体育学院队、中国人民解放军公安军队、中国人民解放军驻广州部队队、中国煤矿体育协会队、沈阳体育学院队、中国人民解放军驻南京部队队和重庆市队。
    女子组：
    中国人民解放军“八一”队、沈阳体育学院队、北京市队、上海体育学院队、成都体育学院队、重庆市队和武汉体育学院队。
