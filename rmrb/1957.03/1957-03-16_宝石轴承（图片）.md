Title: Jewel Bearing (Picture)
Author: Hu Wei
Date: 1957-03-16
Page: 6

	Jewel Bearing
	The instrument heart, the jewel bearing, which is only one-quarter the size of a grain of rice, has been successfully trial-produced at the Harbin Electric Meter Instrument Factory.
	The processing of this bearing is very complex. A piece of natural agate gemstone is placed on a Soviet-made table-top machine and needs to go through twenty-eight processes to be made.
	The left picture shows the first batch of trial-produced agate jewel bearings.
	The picture below shows worker Zhang Jinxue using a Soviet-style three-axis grooving machine to process jewel bearings. Xinhua News Agency reporter Hu Wei photographed.

### 宝石轴承（图片）
胡伟
1957-03-16
第6版()
专栏：

    宝石轴承
    只有一粒米的四分之一那样大的仪表心脏——宝石轴承，已经在哈尔滨电表仪器厂试制成功。
    这种轴承的加工过程很复杂。一块天然玛瑙宝石，放在苏联特制的台式机器上，需要经过二十八道工序才能制成。
    左图是第一批试制出来的玛瑙宝石轴承。
    下图是工人张金学用苏式三轴挖槽机加工宝石轴承。 		 新华社记者  胡伟摄
