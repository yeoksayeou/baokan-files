Title: Egypt Prepares to Hold General Elections
Author:
Date: 1957-03-13
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 12th - The Egyptian Ministry of Interior announced on the 11th the law regarding members of the Egyptian National Assembly. This is the first formal announcement by the Egyptian government regarding holding general elections after the revolution.
    The new law stipulates that the National Assembly will have 350 members, representing the same number of constituencies, and the members will be elected by secret ballot. Former members of the royal family are not eligible to be candidates.
    Egyptian newspapers report that preparations are underway for the first general election in Egypt since the revolution. The election for the National Assembly was originally scheduled for last year but was postponed due to the invasion by Britain, France, and Israel.

### 埃及准备举行大选

1957-03-13
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及准备举行大选
    新华社开罗12日电　埃及内政部11日公布了埃及国民议会议员的法律。这是埃及政府到目前为止关于革命后举行大选的第一个正式公告。
    新法律规定，国民议会将有三百五十名议员，代表同样数目的选区，议员以秘密投票选举出来。前王室成员不得为候选人。
    埃及报纸报道，埃及革命后第一次大选的准备工作正在进行中。国民议会的选举原定在去年举行。但是由于英、法、以三国的侵略而推迟了。
