Title: Xia Yan Receives the Famous French Film Actor Philippe
Author: 
Date: 1957-03-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 19th. Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan received the famous French film and theater actor Gérard Philipe and his wife this afternoon. Present at the meeting were Wang Lanxi, Assistant Minister of Culture, Situ Huimin, Deputy Director of the Film Bureau, and Zhu Boshen, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 夏衍接见法国著名电影演员菲利浦

1957-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

    夏衍接见法国著名电影演员菲利浦
    新华社19日讯　文化部副部长夏衍今天下午接见了法国著名电影和戏剧演员钱拉·菲利浦和夫人。接见时在座的有文化部部长助理王阑西，电影局副局长司徒慧敏和对外文化协会副秘书长朱伯深。
