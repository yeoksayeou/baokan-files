Title: Syria, Lebanon, Iraq Agree to Send Delegation to Inspect Consequences of British Aggression Against Yemen
Author:
Date: 1957-03-18
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 17th: Arab League Secretary-General Hassouna announced on the 16th that Syria, Lebanon, and Iraq have agreed to send an Arab state delegation to visit Yemen and inspect the consequences of British aggression against Yemen's southern border.

Prior to this, Yemen had formally requested the Arab League to send a delegation to Yemen.

Hassouna said that he and Sudanese Foreign Minister Muhammad Ahmad Mahgoub had informed United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld of the developments in the Yemen border situation.

Hassouna also said that Britain has expressed its willingness to negotiate with Yemen.

### 叙黎伊三国同意派代表团视察英国侵略也门的后果

1957-03-18
第7版()
专栏：

    叙黎伊三国同意派代表团视察英国侵略也门的后果
    新华社开罗17日电　阿拉伯联盟秘书长哈苏纳16日宣布，叙利亚、黎巴嫩和伊拉克已经同意派遣阿拉伯国家代表团到也门去访问，并且视察英国对也门南部边境进行侵略的后果。
    在这以前，也门曾经正式要求阿拉伯联盟派遣一个代表团去也门。
    哈苏纳说，他和苏丹外交部长穆罕默德·艾哈迈德·马哈古卜已经把也门边境局势的发展情况告诉联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德。
    哈苏纳还说，英国已经表示愿意同也门进行谈判。
