Title: Korean Gymnastics Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-03-19
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 18th: The Democratic People's Republic of Korea's men's and women's gymnastics team, invited to visit China, arrived in Beijing today. The Korean gymnastics team consists of nine male athletes and eight female athletes, led by Zhao Chunhuan.

### 朝鲜体操队到京

1957-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜体操队到京
    据新华社18日讯　应邀来中国访问的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国男女体操队今天到达北京。朝鲜体操队中，有九名男运动员和八名女运动员，领队是赵春焕。
