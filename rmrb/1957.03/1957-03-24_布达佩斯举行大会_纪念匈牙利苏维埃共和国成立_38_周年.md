Title: Budapest Holds Meeting to Commemorate the 38th Anniversary of the Hungarian Soviet Republic
Author:
Date: 1957-03-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, March 23rd. The Budapest Municipal Provisional Executive Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, the newly established "Táncsics Club" (replacing the former "Petőfi Club"), and the Party History Institute jointly held a meeting on the 21st to commemorate the 38th anniversary of the founding of the Hungarian Soviet Republic.

Gyula Hevesi, Chairman of the "Táncsics Club" and Academician of the Academy of Sciences, delivered a report at the meeting. In his report, he recounted the experiences and lessons of the Hungarian proletarian revolution of 1919. He pointed out that the glorious traditions of the 1919 revolution were inherited and carried forward again after the liberation in 1945; and after the recent twists and turns, the Socialist Workers' Party has once again embarked on the revolutionary workers' movement and the true path of serving the people of the 1919 proletarian revolution.

Those who participated in this commemorative meeting included: Members of the Provisional Central Executive Committee of the Socialist Workers' Party, Marosán, Rónai, Somogyi, members of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, members of the Budapest Municipal Party Committee, leaders of the "Táncsics Club", representatives of the People's Army, and representatives of women, youth, and workers.

### 布达佩斯举行大会  纪念匈牙利苏维埃共和国成立38周年

1957-03-24
第5版()
专栏：

    布达佩斯举行大会
    纪念匈牙利苏维埃共和国成立38周年
    新华社布达佩斯23日电匈牙利社会主义工人党布达佩斯市临时执行委员会、新建立的“坦契奇俱乐部”（代替过去的“裴多菲俱乐部”）和党史学院21日联合举行了纪念匈牙利苏维埃共和国成立三十八周年大会。
  “坦契奇俱乐部”主席、科学院社会科学院士海维西·久洛在会上作了报告。他在报告中叙述了1919年匈牙利无产阶级革命的经验教训。他指出，1919年革命的光荣传统在1945年解放后又重新得到了继承和发扬；而在经过最近的波折以后，社会主义工人党又重新走上了革命工人运动和1919年无产阶级革命的真正为人民服务的道路。
  参加这个纪念大会的有：社会主义工人党临时中央执行委员会委员马罗山、罗奈伊、绍莫吉、匈牙利人民共和国主席团委员，布达佩斯市党委会的委员，“坦契奇俱乐部”的领导人，人民军代表，妇女、青年和工人代表等。
