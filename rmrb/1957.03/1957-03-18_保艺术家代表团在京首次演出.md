Title: Bulgarian Artists' Delegation Holds First Performance in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-03-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 16th: The Bulgarian Artists' Delegation held its first official performance in Beijing tonight at the People's Theater.

	The ballet "The Dying Swan," performed by Bulgarian Sofia National Opera Ballet dancer Sulekova, was received with the warmest welcome. Thousands of audience members also enjoyed the performances of Bulgarian bass singer Kozhuharov and soprano singer Anastasova with great interest tonight.

	During tonight's nearly two-and-a-half-hour performance, the seven Bulgarian artists performed a total of eleven programs, which also included violin solos, piano solos, and dances.

	After the performance, Vice Minister of Culture Qian Junrui went to the stage to shake hands with the Bulgarian artists and congratulate them on their successful performance.

	The Bulgarian Artists' Delegation will continue to perform in Beijing on the 17th.

### 保艺术家代表团在京首次演出

1957-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

    保艺术家代表团在京首次演出
    据新华社16日讯　保加利亚艺术家代表团今晚在人民剧场举行来北京后的第一次正式演出。
    保加利亚索非亚国家歌剧院芭蕾舞演员苏列柯娃表演的芭蕾舞“天鹅之死”，受到了最热烈的欢迎。上千名观众今晚还怀着极大的兴趣欣赏了保加利亚男低音歌唱家科茹哈罗夫和女高音歌唱家阿纳斯塔索娃的演唱。
    在今晚将近两个半小时的演出中，保加利亚的七位艺术家共演出了十一个节目，其中还包括小提琴独奏、钢琴独奏和舞蹈。
    在演出结束后，文化部副部长钱俊瑞到舞台上同保加利亚艺术家们握手，祝贺他们的演出成功。
    保加利亚艺术家代表团17日将在北京继续演出。
