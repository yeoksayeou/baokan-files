Title: Jordan Supports Egypt's Recovery of Gaza Strip; National Holiday for Three Days to Celebrate Abolition of Anglo-Jordanian Treaty
Author:
Date: 1957-03-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, March 13th: Jordanian Minister of State for Foreign Affairs Abdullah Rimawi said on the 12th that Jordan supports any action by Egypt to restore its authority in the Gaza Strip.

Rimawi said that the Jordanian government believes that the Gaza Strip is "a part of the land forcibly taken by the Israelis, and this land must be returned to the Egyptian government."

Xinhua News Agency, March 13th: The Jordanian government issued an official statement on the 12th stating that the Jordanian government has ordered a three-day national holiday from March 14th to 16th to celebrate the abolition of the Anglo-Jordanian Treaty. During the holiday, public places across the country will be decorated, and celebratory activities will be held to celebrate this major event.

### 约旦支持埃及收复加沙地区  全国放假三天庆祝废除英约条约

1957-03-14
第6版()
专栏：

    约旦支持埃及收复加沙地区
    全国放假三天庆祝废除英约条约
    据新华社13日讯  约旦外交国务大臣阿卜杜拉·里马维12日说，约旦支持埃及恢复它在加沙地区的职权的任何行动。
    里马维说，约旦政府认为，加沙地区是被以色列人“强夺去的土地的一部分，这些土地必须交还埃及政府”。
    新华社13日讯  约旦官方12日发表公报说，约旦政府已经下令从3月14日到16日全国放假三天，庆祝废除英约条约。在假日的几天中，全国的公共场所都要装饰起来，举行庆祝活动，庆贺这一重大事件。
