Title: CPPCC National Committee Meeting Holds Group Discussions
Author:
Date: 1957-03-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The Third Plenary Session of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) today held group discussions on the report made by Zhou Enlai, Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC and Premier of the State Council, at yesterday's meeting on his visit to eleven Asian and European countries.

　　Today's discussions were conducted in nineteen groups divided by region. Some groups, while discussing Zhou Enlai's report, also discussed the work report of the Standing Committee of the CPPCC National Committee made by Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the CPPCC National Committee, on behalf of the Standing Committee of the CPPCC National Committee.

　　The Proposals Examination Committee of the Third Plenary Session of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC held a plenary meeting this afternoon, discussing the review opinions made by the three review groups on the 104 proposals made by the members. As of last night, the members had submitted 170 proposals.

### 政协全国委员会会议进行分组讨论

1957-03-07
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会会议进行分组讨论
    新华社6日讯　中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第三次全体会议，今天分组讨论中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会主席、国务院总理周恩来在昨天会议上所作的关于访问亚洲和欧洲十一国的报告。
    今天的讨论是按地区分成十九个组进行的。有的组在讨论周恩来的报告同时，还讨论了政协全国委员会副主席陈叔通代表政协全国委员会常务委员会所作的关于常务委员会工作报告。
    政协第二届全国委员会第三次全体会议提案审查委员会今天下午举行了全体会议，就三个审查小组对委员们的一百零四件提案所作出的审查意见进行了讨论。截至昨天晚上止，委员们提出的提案已有一百七十件。
