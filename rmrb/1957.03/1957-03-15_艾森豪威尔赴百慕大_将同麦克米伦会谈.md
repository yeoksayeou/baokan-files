Title: Eisenhower to Visit Bermuda for Talks with Macmillan
Author:
Date: 1957-03-15
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, March 14th: U.S. President Eisenhower said at a press conference on the 13th that he would set sail for Bermuda on the evening of the 14th to hold talks with British Prime Minister Macmillan there next week.

He said that he would discuss issues related to the North Atlantic Treaty Organization, the Middle East, and other important regions of the world with Macmillan.

### 艾森豪威尔赴百慕大  将同麦克米伦会谈

1957-03-15
第7版()
专栏：

    艾森豪威尔赴百慕大
    将同麦克米伦会谈
    据新华社14日讯  华盛顿消息：美国总统艾森豪威尔13日在记者招待会上说，他将在14日晚上乘船赴百慕大，以便下星期同英国首相麦克米伦在那里举行会谈。
    他说，他将要同麦克米伦谈到北大西洋公约问题，中东问题和世界上其他一些重要地区的问题。
