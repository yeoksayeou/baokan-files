Title: Dulles Revives Plan for International Control of the Suez Canal and Threatens to Use Atomic Weapons in Southeast Asia
Author:
Date: 1957-03-16
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, March 14th, Canberra dispatch: U.S. Secretary of State Dulles said at a press conference on the 13th that U.S. warships and troops are already equipped with atomic weapons and will use these weapons if they are called upon to take military action in the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization area.

Dulles declined to comment on the recent situation in the Gaza Strip. He said, "I'll leave that question to Washington."

Speaking on the Suez Canal issue, he reiterated the U.S. position expressed at the London conference. He said the Eighteen-Nation proposal "remains the best way to solve this problem." This proposal is based on the Dulles Plan, which advocated international control of the Suez Canal and was rejected by Egypt.

Dulles said that the United States has ties with the Baghdad Pact in many respects and hopes to have more ties with it "without becoming a formal member."

### 杜勒斯重提国际管制苏伊士运河计划  并叫嚷要在东南亚使用原子武器

1957-03-16
第7版()
专栏：

    杜勒斯重提国际管制苏伊士运河计划
    并叫嚷要在东南亚使用原子武器
    据新华社14日讯  堪培拉消息：美国国务卿杜勒斯13日在记者招待会说，美国的军舰和部队已经装备原子武器，一旦它们被要求在东南亚条约组织地区采取军事行动，它们将使用这些武器。
    杜勒斯拒绝对加沙地带最近的局势发表意见。他说，“我要把这个问题留给华盛顿。”
    他在谈到苏伊士运河问题的时候重申美国在伦敦会议上所表示的意见。他说，十八国方案“仍然是解决这个问题的最好办法”。这个方案就是以主张国际管制苏伊士运河的杜勒斯计划为基础的，它曾经被埃及拒绝。
    杜勒斯说，美国在许多方面同巴格达条约有着联系，并且希望会同它有更多的联系，“而不成为它的一个正式成员”。
