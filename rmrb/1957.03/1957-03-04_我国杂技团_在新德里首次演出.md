Title: China's Acrobatic Troupe Gives First Performance in New Delhi
Author:
Date: 1957-03-04
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 3rd: The Chinese Acrobatic Troupe gave its first performance in the Indian capital on the evening of the 2nd to an audience of approximately 10,000 people.

Prime Minister Nehru, along with Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili and diplomatic envoys from various countries to India, also watched the acrobatic performance. A total of eighteen acts were performed in the first performance. Each act was met with enthusiastic applause.

### 我国杂技团  在新德里首次演出

1957-03-04
第5版()
专栏：

    我国杂技团
    在新德里首次演出
    据新华社新德里3日电  中国杂技团2日晚上在印度首都对约一万人举行了首次演出。
    尼赫鲁总理以及中国驻印度大使潘自力和各国驻印度外交使节也观看了杂技表演。首次演出一共表演了十八个节目。每一个节目都博得了热烈的掌声。
