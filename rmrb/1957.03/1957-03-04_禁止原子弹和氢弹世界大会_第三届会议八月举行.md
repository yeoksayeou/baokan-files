Title: World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs to Hold Third Meeting in August
Author:
Date: 1957-03-04
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, March 3rd: The first meeting of the first National Board of Directors of the Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, held in Tokyo on the 2nd and 3rd, decided to hold the third World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs around August 6th this year in Tokyo, and appealed to all countries around the world for assistance.
The Board of Directors elected Yasui Iku as Chairman of the Council, and also elected Hirano Yoshitaro and thirty-seven others as Standing Directors. With the establishment of the National Board of Directors, the Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs has transformed from a temporary organization into a permanent organization.
According to the Council, as of March 3rd, the organizations that have joined the Council include forty-six national organizations such as labor, cultural, women's, student, peace, and religious organizations, and forty-six local councils against atomic and hydrogen bombs.

### 禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会  第三届会议八月举行

1957-03-04
第5版()
专栏：

    禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会
    第三届会议八月举行
    新华社3日讯  东京消息：日本禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会2日和3日两天在东京举行的第一届第一次全国理事会会议决定，今年8月6日左右在东京举行第三届禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会，并且吁请世界各国给予帮助。
    理事会选出安井郁为协议会理事长，并且选出平野义太郎等三十七人为常任理事。由于全国理事会的成立，日本禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会从此由临时性组织变成了永久性组织。
    据协议会宣布，到3月3日为止，加入协议会的团体有劳工、文化、妇女、学生、和平、宗教等四十六个全国性组织和四十六个地方禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会。
