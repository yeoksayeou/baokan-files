Title: President of the Yugoslav Federal Assembly Pijade Dies in Paris, Chairman Liu Shaoqi Sends Condolences

Author:
Date: 1957-03-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, 16th—President of the Yugoslav Federal Assembly and member of the Executive Committee of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, Moša Pijade, passed away in Paris on the evening of March 15th due to a severe heart condition. Pijade was on his way back to the country on the afternoon of the 15th by plane after leading a Yugoslav parliamentary delegation on a visit to Britain when he stopped in Paris. He only fell ill fifteen minutes before his death and could not be saved in time.
That evening, the Yugoslav Federal Executive Council issued an announcement signed by President Tito, deciding that the entire country would mourn Pijade from March 15th to 19th.
Xinhua News Agency, 16th—Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram to the Yugoslav Federal Assembly to express condolences on the passing of Comrade Pijade. The telegram reads as follows:
To the Yugoslav Federal Assembly:
Deeply saddened to learn of the passing of Comrade Moša Pijade, President of the Yugoslav Federal Assembly, in Paris on March 15th. Please accept my condolences.
Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China
March 16, 1957

### 南议会主席皮雅杰在巴黎逝世  刘少奇委员长致电吊唁

1957-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

    南议会主席皮雅杰在巴黎逝世
    刘少奇委员长致电吊唁
    新华社贝尔格莱德16日电　南斯拉夫联邦国民议会主席、南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员会执行委员会委员皮雅杰3月15日晚间因患严重的心脏病在巴黎逝世。皮雅杰是在率领南斯拉夫议会代表团访问了英国以后在15日下午乘飞机回国途中在巴黎停留的。从发病到逝世只有十五分钟，因此不及救治。
    当天晚间，南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会发布了由铁托总统签署的一项公告，决定从3月15日到19日全国为皮雅杰致哀。
    新华社16日讯　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会刘少奇委员长致电南斯拉夫联邦国民议会吊唁皮雅杰同志逝世。电文如下：南斯拉夫联邦国民议会：
    惊悉南斯拉夫联邦国民议会主席莫沙·皮雅杰同志3月15日在巴黎逝世，不胜哀悼。谨电吊唁。
    中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇
        1957年3月16日
