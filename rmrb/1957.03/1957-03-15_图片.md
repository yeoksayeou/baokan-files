    CPPCC members Zhao Yu (first from the left) and Xie Nanguang (first from the right) are chatting. They are old friends from more than twenty years ago. From 1932 to 1935, they worked together in the Shanghai Overseas Chinese United Association, raising donations from overseas Chinese to actively support the Northeast Anti-Japanese Volunteer Army in combat. This time they met again at the CPPCC meeting. Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Qingbiao
