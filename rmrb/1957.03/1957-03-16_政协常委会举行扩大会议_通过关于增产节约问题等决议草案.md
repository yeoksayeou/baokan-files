Title: CPPCC Standing Committee Holds Enlarged Meeting, Passes Draft Resolutions on Increasing Production and Practicing Economy
Author:
Date: 1957-03-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 15th—The Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held an enlarged meeting this evening.

    The meeting approved the supplementary list (draft) of the Standing Committee members of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC.

    After listening to Zhang Bojun, convener of the resolution drafting committee of the Third Plenary Session of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC, explain the draft resolutions, the meeting passed the resolution (draft) on the work report of the Standing Committee and the resolution (draft) on increasing production and practicing economy.

    After Zhang Naiqi, convener of the proposal examination committee of the Third Plenary Session of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC, made an explanation, the meeting passed the examination opinions (draft) on the proposals.

    The draft list, resolutions, and examination opinions mentioned above will be submitted to the plenary session of the National Committee for deliberation.

### 政协常委会举行扩大会议  通过关于增产节约问题等决议草案

1957-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

    政协常委会举行扩大会议
    通过关于增产节约问题等决议草案
    新华社15日讯  中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会今晚举行扩大会议。
    会议通过了政协第二届全国委员会常务委员的增选名单（草案）。
    会议在听取了政协第二届全国委员会第三次全体会议决议起草委员会召集人章伯钧的关于决议草案的说明后，通过了关于常务委员会工作报告的决议（草案）和关于增产节约问题的决议（草案）。
    在政协第二届全国委员会第三次全体会议提案审查委员会召集人章乃器作了说明后，会议通过了关于提案的审查意见（草案）。
    上述名单、决议和审查意见的草案都将提交全国委员会全体会议审议。
