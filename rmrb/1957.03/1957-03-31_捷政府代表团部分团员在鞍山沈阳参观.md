Title: Part of the Czechoslovak Government Delegation Visits Anshan and Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1957-03-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Anshan, March 30th: This morning at 10:00 AM, part of the Czechoslovak Republic Government Delegation, accompanied by Wang Heshou, Director of the State Construction Commission and Minister of the Metallurgical Industry, arrived in Anshan by special plane. Those who welcomed them at the airport included Wang Yixin, Deputy Mayor of Anshan, Yang Shijie, First Secretary of the Anshan Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Yuan Zhen, Manager of Anshan Iron and Steel Company, and heads of various people's organizations.
	
	Part of the delegation visited the Large Rolling Mill, the Seamless Steel Tube Plant, and the No. 2 Thin Plate Plant, observing the rolling of channel steel by the rolling workers.

	During the visit to the Seamless Steel Tube Plant, members of the delegation also met Czechoslovak experts Obladal, Niečas, and Roshi, who were assisting in the construction of Anshan Iron and Steel.

	After visiting the Seamless Steel Tube Plant, Mr. Černík, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and a member of the Czechoslovak Republic Government Delegation, addressed more than 3,000 steelworkers gathered in front of the plant.

	Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, March 30th: Part of the Czechoslovak Republic Government Delegation, accompanied by Wang Heshou, Director of the State Construction Commission of China, arrived in Shenyang by plane from Anshan at 4:45 PM today.

	Du Zheheng, Governor of Liaoning Province, Li Tao, Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Liu Baotian, Mayor of Shenyang, Jiao Ruoyu, First Secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Lieutenant General Du Ping, Head of the Shenyang Garrison, were among those who welcomed them at the airport.

	Pak Sung-chul, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, who came to Shenyang to welcome the Czechoslovak Republic Government Delegation, was also present at the airport.

	Consuls General and consulate staff from the Soviet Union and North Korea in Shenyang were also present at the airport to welcome the delegation.

	After a short rest at the Liaoning Hotel, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Governor Du Zheheng and Mayor Liu Baotian, visited the Northeast Industrial Exhibition Hall.

### 捷政府代表团部分团员在鞍山沈阳参观

1957-03-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　捷政府代表团部分团员在鞍山沈阳参观
    据新华社鞍山30日电  今天上午十时，捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团部分团员在国家建设委员会主任、冶金工业部部长王鹤寿等陪同下，乘专机到达鞍山。到机场欢迎的有鞍山市副市长王一新，中共鞍山市委员会第一书记杨世杰，鞍山钢铁公司经理袁振以及各人民团体的负责人。
    代表团部分团员们参观了大型轧钢厂、无缝钢管厂和第二薄板厂，观看了轧钢工人轧制槽钢的情景。
    在无缝钢管厂参观时，代表团团员还会见了在鞍钢帮助建设的捷克斯洛伐克专家奥布列达尔、涅洽斯和罗希。
    在参观无缝钢管厂以后，捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团团员、捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会书记契尔尼克向聚集在无缝钢管厂门前的三千多个钢铁工人讲了话。
    新华社沈阳30日电  捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团部分团员，在我国国家建设委员会主任王鹤寿陪同下，今天下午四点四十五分乘飞机由鞍山到达沈阳。
    辽宁省省长杜者蘅、中共辽宁省委员会书记李涛、沈阳市市长刘宝田、中共沈阳市委员会第一书记焦若愚、沈阳驻军首长杜平中将等人到机场欢迎。
    来到沈阳迎接捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务省副相朴成哲也到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的还有苏联、朝鲜驻沈阳总领事和领事馆人员。
    贵宾们乘车到辽宁宾馆稍事休息后，即在省长杜者蘅、市长刘宝田陪同下参观了东北工业陈列馆。
