Title: Tibetan New Year's Day - March 2nd: Tibetans in Beijing Extend New Year Greetings to Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou
Author:
Date: 1957-03-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 2nd: On the evening of the Tibetan New Year's Day of the Fire Rooster Year—March 2nd, Tibetans in Beijing went to the Qin Zheng Dian Hall in Zhongnanhai to extend New Year greetings to Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai.

At 8 p.m., Tibetans dressed in festive attire arrived at the Qin Zheng Dian Hall. Among them were officials from the Tibetan visiting delegation, the head of the Tibetan Youth Visiting Delegation, Tibetan representatives and youth representatives attending the Second Member Representative Assembly of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, the director of the Dalai Lama's Office in Beijing, the director of the Panchen Lama's Office in Beijing, and the head of the Tibetan Song and Dance Troupe.

As they arrived, Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai greeted them with smiles, standing in the center of the hall. Also present were Vice Premier Deng Xiaoping, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Huang Yanpei, Li Weihan, and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhang Bojun, President of the Buddhist Association of China Xirao Jiacuo, Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, and Vice Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission Wang Feng.

The Tibetans presented Hada scarves to Chairman Mao, Premier Zhou, and the leaders present, one by one. After accepting the Hada scarves, Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou returned the gesture by presenting Hada scarves to them and wishing them a Happy New Year.

### 藏历新年元旦——3月2日  在京的西藏人士向毛主席周总理拜年

1957-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

    藏历新年元旦——3月2日
    在京的西藏人士向毛主席周总理拜年
    新华社2日讯  藏历火鸡年元旦——3月2日晚上，在北京的西藏人士前往中南海勤政殿向毛泽东主席和周恩来总理拜年。
    晚上八时，身着节日盛装的藏族人士来到了勤政殿。他们当中有西藏参观团的官员，西藏青年参观团的负责人，出席全国工商联第二届会员代表大会的西藏代表和西藏的青年代表，达赖驻京办事处和班禅驻京办事处的处长，以及西藏歌舞团的负责人。
    当他们来到的时候，毛泽东主席和周恩来总理微笑着站在大厅中央迎接他们。在这里，还有副总理邓小平，副委员长李济深、黄炎培、李维汉、陈叔通，中国人民政协全国委员会副主席章伯钧，中国佛教协会会长喜饶嘉错，国务院秘书长习仲勋，民族事务委员会副主任汪锋。
    西藏人士向毛主席、周总理和在场的领导人员一一献了哈达。毛主席和周总理在接受哈达以后向他们一一回敬了哈达，并且祝贺他们新年好。
