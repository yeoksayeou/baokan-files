Title: Wishing Agricultural Labor Models a Smooth Journey Home

Author: 
Date: 1957-03-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd — The representatives attending the National Agricultural Labor Model Representative Conference will begin to leave Beijing this evening in succession and return to their production posts.

Before and after the end of the conference, the representatives had extensive contact with people from all walks of life in Beijing. National renowned agricultural labor models Guo Yu'en, Lü Hongbin, Han En, Li Shunda, and Xue Zhanchun, who created the national high cotton yield record, as well as water conservancy model worker Xie Hongyou and others, were invited to the General Office of the State Council, the Ministry of Construction Engineering, Peking University, the Physical Culture and Sports Commission, the Beijing Agricultural Mechanization Institute, and the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and more than 50 other government organs, organizations, and schools, to make reports on issues such as agricultural cooperative production and construction.

In addition, the representatives were also invited to participate in symposiums held by the Rural Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, the Ministry of Internal Affairs, the Ministry of Education, and some news and publishing organizations.

### 祝农业劳动模范回乡一路顺风

1957-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

    祝农业劳动模范回乡一路顺风
    据新华社2日讯  出席全国农业劳动模范代表会议的代表，从今晚起将陆续离京，返回生产岗位。
    在会议结束前后，代表们和北京各界进行了广泛接触。全国著名农业劳动模范郭玉恩、吕鸿宾、韩恩、李顺达和创造全国棉花高产纪录的薛占春以及治淮模范谢洪友等人，应邀分别到国务院秘书厅、建筑工程部、北京大学、体育运动委员会、北京农业机械化学院和中国人民解放军京津卫戍司令部等五十多个机关团体和学校，作了关于农业社生产建设等问题的报告。
    此外，代表们还应邀分别参加了中共中央农村工作部、内务部、教育部和一些新闻出版机关所举行的座谈会。
