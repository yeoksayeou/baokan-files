    International Briefs
    
    Soviet and Yugoslav Trade Expanded This Year
    The trade protocol signed between the Soviet Union and Yugoslavia in Moscow on the 26th stipulates that trade between the two countries will be further expanded this year on the basis of 1956.
    
    Yugoslav Basketball Team Departs for China
    The Yugoslav basketball team, invited by the Chinese Sports Commission to conduct friendly matches in China, took a plane from Belgrade to China at 10:00 a.m. on February 28.
    
    The basketball team consists of thirty-two people, with thirteen male and thirteen female players each. The basketball team will conduct one month of matches and visits in China.
    
    Budapest Repairs 10,000 Homes
    To date, Budapest has repaired 10,000 homes that were damaged in the October incident last year. Repair work is currently underway on another 3,000 homes.
    
    In addition to repairing damaged homes, the government has also decided to build a new batch of homes in the city and suburbs. Three hundred new homes have recently been built on Gellebēs Street alone. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 国际简讯

1957-03-02
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    苏南两国今年扩大相互贸易
    苏联同南斯拉夫26日在莫斯科签订的1957年贸易议定书规定，今年将在1956年的基础上进一步扩大两国之间的贸易。
    南篮球队启程来我国
    应中国体育运动委员会邀请到中国进行友谊比赛的南斯拉夫篮球队，已经在2月28日上午十时从贝尔格莱德乘飞机来中国。
    这个篮球队是由三十二人组成的，男女队员各十三名。篮球队将在中国进行一个月的比赛和访问。
    布达佩斯修复一万套住宅
    到目前为止，布达佩斯已经修复了一万套在去年十月事件中遭到破坏的住宅。现在正在进行其他三千套住宅的修复工作。
    除了修复被破坏的住宅外，政府还决定在市内和郊区兴建一批新住宅。仅在盖莱贝什街，最近已经建好了三百套新住宅。（据新华社讯）
