Title: Anglo-Singapore Negotiations Temporarily Suspended
Author: 
Date: 1957-03-19
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, London, March 18th: Negotiations between Singapore and Britain regarding the issue of Singapore achieving internal self-government within the Commonwealth have been temporarily suspended due to a request for postponement from the British side.
    It is reported that at the meeting on the 14th, there were indications that Britain insisted on retaining a voice in domestic affairs that had an impact on Singapore's foreign affairs and defense, and no progress was made on this point in the negotiations.

### 英新谈判暂时中断

1957-03-19
第6版()
专栏：

    英新谈判暂时中断
    据新华社18日讯　伦敦消息：新加坡和英国之间关于新加坡在英联邦内实行内部自治问题的谈判，由于英国方面要求延期而暂时中断。
    据报道，14日的会议上有迹象表明，英国坚持要在与新加坡的外交和国防有影响的内政问题上保留发言权，对于这一点，谈判没有取得进展。
