Title: Mining Slag Steel (Picture)
Author: Yang Limen
Date: 1957-03-07
Page: 2

    Mining Slag Steel
    A large electric shovel is mining slag steel at the slag dump on the northwest side of the Anshan Iron and Steel Works. This waste slag pile, which is about two million cubic meters, can yield about 150 kilograms of slag steel suitable for steelmaking per cubic meter.　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Yang Limen photo

### 开采渣钢（图片）
杨礼门
1957-03-07
第2版()
专栏：

    开采渣钢
    大电铲正在鞍钢厂区西北侧的矿渣堆积场里开采渣钢。这块约有二百多万立方公尺的废钢渣堆，每一立方公尺就可开采一百五十公斤左右可供炼钢的渣钢。　　新华社记者　杨礼门摄
