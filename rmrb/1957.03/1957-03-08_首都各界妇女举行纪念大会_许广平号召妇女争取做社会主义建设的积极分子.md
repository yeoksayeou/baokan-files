Title: Women from All Circles in the Capital Hold Commemorative Meeting; Xu Guangping Calls on Women to Strive to be Active Participants in Socialist Construction
Author:
Date: 1957-03-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 7th: Women from all circles in the capital, numbering over 1,200, held a commemorative meeting at the Capital Theater today for International Women's Day.

Cai Chang, President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and his wife, were among those who attended the meeting. The wives of foreign ambassadors and diplomats stationed in China, female diplomats, as well as foreign female experts and the wives of experts in Beijing also attended the meeting.

Zhang Xiaomei, Chairman of the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, delivered the opening speech, saying: "Every time we celebrate another 'March 8th' International Women's Day, we see the new achievements our motherland has obtained and the new achievements the women's movement has achieved. These achievements are worthy of our joy and celebration." Zhang Xiaomei then reported on the achievements of Beijing women in various fields last year.

Xu Guangping, Vice-President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, then spoke. She described the unprecedented achievements made by women across the country in various fields during the upsurge of the socialist revolution last year, and called on women nationwide to respond to the call of the Party and the government to increase production and practice economy, build the country diligently and thriftily, manage the household diligently and thriftily, and strive to be active participants in socialist construction. She also expressed her views on contraception and late marriage.

Amid applause, the wife of the Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mrs. Gregor, was invited to speak. On behalf of the women from the diplomatic corps in Beijing who were invited to the meeting, she congratulated the Chinese women on the festival. She said: "I wish you even greater achievements in your work now and in the future, I wish you many achievements in educating your lovely children, and I wish your great motherland even more beauty, prosperity, and strength!" After her speech, the wives of foreign ambassadors to China presented flower baskets to the meeting.

Also speaking at the meeting were Zhou Meihua, a worker from the Beijing No. 1 Machine Tool Plant, Hou Xiuying, Deputy Director of the Dongfanghong Agricultural Cooperative in Fengtai District, Beijing, Ye Gongshao, Professor of Beijing Medical College, and Pei Yurong, a family member of a worker.

A group of children walked onto the stage with flowers. A girl read a dedication to her mother in front of the microphone. After she finished reading, the mothers and aunties sitting on the stage excitedly picked up the children, and the audience applauded thunderously.

After the meeting, the Central Song and Dance Troupe performed song and dance programs.

### 首都各界妇女举行纪念大会  许广平号召妇女争取做社会主义建设的积极分子

1957-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

    首都各界妇女举行纪念大会
    许广平号召妇女争取做社会主义建设的积极分子
    据新华社7日讯　首都各界妇女一千二百多人今天在首都剧场举行纪念“三八”国际妇女节大会。
    中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅、北京市副市长张友渔和夫人等出席了大会。各国驻华使节和外交官的夫人，女外交官，以及在北京的外国女专家和专家的夫人也出席了大会。
    北京市民主妇女联合会主席张晓梅致开会词说：每当我们又度过一次“三八”节的时候，我们都看到祖国所获得的新的成就，看到妇女运动所获得的新的成就。这些成就是值得我们欢欣鼓舞和庆祝的。张晓梅接着报告了去年北京市妇女各方面的成就。
    中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平接着讲话。她叙述了去年社会主义革命高潮中全国妇女在各方面取得的前所未有的成就，号召全国妇女响应党和政府增产节约的号召，勤俭建国，勤俭治家，争取做社会主义建设的积极分子。她还就避孕和晚婚的问题发表了意见。
    在掌声中，捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使格里哥尔的夫人应邀讲话。她代表北京的外交使团中被邀请到会的妇女们向中国妇女们祝贺节日。她说：我祝你们现在和将来在工作中取得更大的成就，祝你们在教育可爱的儿童方面取得许多成就，祝你们伟大的祖国更加美好更加富强！讲话后，外国驻华使节夫人向大会献花篮。
    在会上讲话的还有北京第一机床厂工人周美华，北京丰台区东方红农业社副社长侯秀英，北京医学院教授叶恭绍，职工家属裴玉蓉。
    一群孩子拿着鲜花走上主席台。一个女孩子在扩音器前朗读了给妈妈的献词。当她读完后，坐在主席台上的妈妈和阿姨们激动地将孩子们抱了起来，这时全场响着雷动的掌声。
    大会结束后，中央歌舞团表演了歌舞节目。
