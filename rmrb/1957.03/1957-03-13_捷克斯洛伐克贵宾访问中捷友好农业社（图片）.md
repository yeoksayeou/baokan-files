Title: Czechoslovak Guests Visit the Sino-Czechoslovak Friendship Agricultural Cooperative (Photos)
Author: Gao Liang
Date: 1957-03-13
Page: 2

    Czechoslovak Guests Visit the Sino-Czechoslovak Friendship Agricultural Cooperative
    Right picture: Guo Rong (first person on the right), director of the Sino-Czechoslovak Friendship Agricultural Production Cooperative, introduced the cooperative's development and production status at a symposium, and solicited criticisms and guidance from the guests.
    Bottom left picture: Guests from the Czechoslovak government delegation led by Prime Minister Široký were warmly welcomed by cooperative members when they arrived at the Sino-Czechoslovak Friendship Agricultural Production Cooperative in Beijing.
    Bottom right picture: Mrs. Široký is particularly concerned about the cooperative's women's work. This is a picture of her visiting Liang Huaizhen (fourth person from the right), deputy director of the cooperative, and Peng Lianyu (first person on the right), director of the Women's Federation of Zhangguozhuang Township. How intimately they are talking! (Photo by our reporter Gao Liang)

### 捷克斯洛伐克贵宾访问中捷友好农业社（图片）
高粮
1957-03-13
第2版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克贵宾访问中捷友好农业社
    右图：中捷友好农业生产合作社主任郭荣（右第一人）在座谈会上介绍了合作社的发展情况和生产状况后，向贵宾们征求批评和指导意见。
    下左图：西罗基总理率领的捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团的贵宾们到达北京市中捷友好农业生产合作社时，受到社员们的夹道欢迎。
    下右图：西罗基夫人对农业社的妇女工作特别关心，这是她正访问该社副主任梁怀贞（右起第四人）和张郭庄乡妇联主任彭连玉（右第一人）的情形。她们谈得多亲切啊！    （本报记者　高粮摄）
