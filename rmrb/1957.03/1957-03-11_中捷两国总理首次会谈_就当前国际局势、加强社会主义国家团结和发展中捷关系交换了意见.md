Title: First Meeting Between Chinese and Czechoslovak Premiers: Exchanged Views on Current International Situation, Strengthening Unity of Socialist Countries, and Developing Sino-Czechoslovak Relations
Author:
Date: 1957-03-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th - The Premier of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, and the Premier of the Czechoslovak Republic, Viliam Široký, held their first meeting in Beijing on March 10, 1957.

The representatives of the People's Republic of China participating in the talks included: Vice Premiers of the State Council He Long and Bo Yibo, Chairman of the State Construction Commission Wang Heshou, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Minister of Health Li Dequan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, and Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Cao Ying. Those present at the meeting were Liu Jingyu, Acting Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern European Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations.

The members of the government delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic participating in the talks included all delegation members: Chairman of the State Planning Commission O. Šimůnek, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia O. Černík, Minister of Foreign Affairs V. David, Minister of Finance J. Ďuriš, Chairman of the State Construction Commission E. Slechta, Minister of Health J. Plojhar, Minister of Local Economy J. Kyselý, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade O. Kožol, and Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Gregor. Those present at the meeting were Deputy Director of the Premier's Office F. Stránský, Director of the Foreign Affairs Department of the Premier's Office F. Vavřička, Director of the Press Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs M. Haruška, and Deputy Dean of the Czech Institute of Technology, Professor F. Hrabitz.

Both sides exchanged views on the current international situation, strengthening the unity of socialist countries, and further developing the mutual relations between China and Czechoslovakia.

The talks took place in a cordial and friendly atmosphere.

The talks will continue. (Attached Picture)

Above: Premier Zhou Enlai and Premier Široký at their first meeting on March 10.
  　Xinhua News Agency Reporter Liu Qingrui.

### 中捷两国总理首次会谈  就当前国际局势、加强社会主义国家团结和发展中捷关系交换了意见

1957-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

    中捷两国总理首次会谈
    就当前国际局势、加强社会主义国家团结和发展中捷关系交换了意见
    新华社10日讯　中华人民共和国周恩来总理同捷克斯洛伐克共和国威廉·西罗基总理，于1957年3月10日在北京举行了第一次会谈。
    中华人民共和国政府方面参加会谈的有：国务院副总理贺龙、薄一波，国家建设委员会主任王鹤寿，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，文化部部长沈雁冰，卫生部部长李德全，外交部副部长姬鹏飞和中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使曹瑛。列席会谈的有外交部苏联东欧司代司长刘靖宇、对外文化联络局代局长陈忠经。
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团参加会谈的有全体团员：国家计划委员会主席奥·西穆涅克，捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会书记奥·契尔尼克，外交部部长瓦·戴维，财政部部长尤·杜里什，国家建设委员会主席埃·斯列赫塔，卫生部部长约·普洛伊哈，地方经济部长约·基塞利，对外贸易部副部长奥·科佐尔，捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使格里哥尔。列席会谈的有总理办公厅副主任弗·斯特兰斯基，总理办公厅外交司司长弗·瓦弗日奇卡，外交部新闻司司长米·加鲁什卡，捷克工学院副院长弗·格拉比茨教授。
    双方就当前国际局势、加强社会主义国家的团结和进一步发展中捷两国相互关系交换了意见。
    会谈是在亲切和友好的空气中进行的。
    会谈将继续进行。（附图片）
