Title: Egypt, Syria, Jordan Sign Cultural Union Agreement
Author:
Date: 1957-03-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Egypt, Syria, and Jordan signed a Cultural Union Agreement in Damascus, the capital of Syria, on March 25th. The cultural agreement includes plans to unify the school curricula and educational systems of the three countries.

### 埃叙约签文化联合协定

1957-03-27
第6版()
专栏：

　　埃叙约签文化联合协定
    据新华社讯　埃及、叙利亚、约旦三国文化联合协定3月25日在叙利亚首都大马士革签字。文化协定包括统一三国的学校课程和教育制度的计划。
