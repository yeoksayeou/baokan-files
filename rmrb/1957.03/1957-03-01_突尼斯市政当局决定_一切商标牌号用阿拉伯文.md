Title: Tunisian Municipal Authorities Decide All Trademarks to Use Arabic

Author:
Date: 1957-03-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, 27th. Local newspapers recently published a notice from the Tunisian municipal authorities stating that, starting March 20th, all commercial, industrial, and other trademarks within Tunis must be in Arabic.

The announcement also stated: Trademarks using both Arabic and other languages are also permitted.

### 突尼斯市政当局决定  一切商标牌号用阿拉伯文

1957-03-01
第6版()
专栏：

    突尼斯市政当局决定
    一切商标牌号用阿拉伯文
    新华社27日讯  突尼斯消息：当地各报纸最近刊登的突尼斯市政当局的公告说，从3月20日起，突尼斯市内的一切商业、工业和其他商标牌号都必须用阿拉伯文。
    公告还说：阿拉伯文和其他文字并用的商标牌号也可以使用。
