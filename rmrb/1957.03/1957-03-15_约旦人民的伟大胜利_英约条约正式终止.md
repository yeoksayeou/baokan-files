Title: Great Victory for the Jordanian People: Anglo-Jordanian Treaty Officially Terminated
Author:
Date: 1957-03-15
Page: 7

    Xinhua News Agency, Amman, March 14th: Jordan formally terminated the "Anglo-Jordanian Treaty of Friendship and Alliance" concluded with Britain in 1948 on the 13th. According to the agreement, British troops stationed in Jordan should withdraw within six months after the official termination of the treaty.
    Jordanian Prime Minister Nabulsi and British Ambassador to Jordan, Johnston, signed the documents terminating the treaty and exchanged them. Nabulsi said during the exchange of documents: "This is the happiest moment of my life. Jordan has suffered greatly from restrictions on its independence and freedom, and now it can freely participate in the liberated Arab ranks that are fighting for the glory and unity of the Arab nation."

### 约旦人民的伟大胜利  英约条约正式终止

1957-03-15
第7版()
专栏：

    约旦人民的伟大胜利
    英约条约正式终止
    据新华社14日讯  安曼消息：约旦在13日正式终止了1948年同英国缔结的“英—约友好同盟条约”。根据协议，英国驻约旦军队应在条约正式终止后的6个月内撤出。
    约旦首相纳布西和英国驻约旦大使约翰斯敦在终止条约的文件上签字后，双方互换了文件。纳布西在交换文件的时候说：“这是我有生以来最快活的时刻。约旦曾经由于对它的独立和自由的限制而受到巨大的痛苦，现在它可以自由地参加正在为阿拉伯民族的光荣和团结而奋斗的解放了的阿拉伯行列了。”
