Title: Prime Minister Široký Arrives in Nanning
Author:
Date: 1957-03-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, 16th – Prime Minister Viliam Široký and the Czechoslovak government delegation he led arrived in Nanning at 12:40 noon today by plane from Chengdu. Wei Guoqing, Governor of Guangxi Province, Xiao Yizhou, Secretary of the Guangxi Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Qin Yingji, Deputy Governor and Governor of the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Prefecture, and more than two thousand cadres and people of all nationalities welcomed the distinguished guests at the airport. Also present to welcome the distinguished guests were Wu Tingying, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, who was sent by the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to meet Prime Minister Široký and others for their visit, Dinh Van Đe, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Czechoslovakia, and Nguyen Van Thuy, representative of the Consulate of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Nanning.

At 3 p.m., Prime Minister Široký and members of the delegation, accompanied by Wei Guoqing and others, visited the People's Committee of the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Prefecture. Qin Yingji, the governor of the autonomous prefecture, introduced the political, economic, and cultural situation of the autonomous prefecture before and after its establishment to the distinguished guests.

After listening to the briefing, Prime Minister Široký delivered a speech in front of the People's Committee office building of the autonomous prefecture to the cadres and people of all nationalities gathered there. He said: We have just heard about the results of the implementation of the ethnic policies of the Chinese Communist Party and the Chinese government. From Beijing to this part of southern China, everything we have seen has deeply convinced us that the wise policies of the Chinese Communist Party have achieved great success.

Prime Minister Široký also said that the victory of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese revolution shattered the chains of oppression of all ethnic groups and opened up broad avenues for the free development of all minority nationalities. All ethnic groups in China are making great efforts to develop socialist industry and agriculture. As a result of the implementation of correct ethnic policies, all ethnic groups in China have been firmly united. The brotherly unity of all ethnic groups is a great driving force for achieving socialist victory.

In the evening, Governor Wei Guoqing and others gave Prime Minister Široký and others a detailed introduction to the socialist construction of Guangxi Province.

### 西罗基总理到南宁

1957-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

    西罗基总理到南宁
    新华社南宁16日电　威廉·西罗基总理和他率领的捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团今天中午十二时四十分乘飞机从成都到达南宁。广西省省长韦国清、中共广西省委员会书记萧一舟、副省长兼桂西僮族自治州州长覃应机等和两千多名各民族干部和群众在机场上欢迎贵宾。在这里欢迎贵宾的还有越南民主共和国政府派来迎接西罗基总理等前往访问的外交部礼宾司司长武庭莹，越南民主共和国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使馆临时代办丁文德，以及越南民主共和国驻南宁领事馆代表阮文崔。
    下午三时，西罗基总理和代表团团员们由韦国清等陪同访问了桂西僮族自治州人民委员会。自治州州长覃应机向贵宾们介绍了自治州成立前后的政治、经济、文化状况。
    西罗基总理在听完情况介绍以后，在自治州人民委员会办公大楼前向聚集在那里的各民族干部和群众发表了讲话。他说：我们刚才听到了关于中国共产党和中国政府民族政策执行的结果。我们从北京来到中华人民共和国南部的这个地方，所看到的一切使我们深深信服中国共产党英明的政策获得了巨大的成就。
    西罗基总理又说，中华人民共和国和中国人民革命的胜利，粉碎了各民族受压迫的锁链，给各少数民族开辟了自由发展的广阔道路。中国各个民族正在以巨大的努力发展社会主义工业和农业。由于执行正确的民族政策的结果，中国各民族已巩固地团结起来了。各民族兄弟般的团结是取得社会主义胜利的巨大推动力量。
    晚间，韦国清省长等向西罗基总理等详尽的介绍了广西省社会主义建设的情况。
