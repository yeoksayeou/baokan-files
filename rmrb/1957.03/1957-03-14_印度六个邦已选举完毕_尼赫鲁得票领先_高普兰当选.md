Title: Elections Completed in Six Indian States; Nehru Leads in Votes; Gopalan Elected
Author:
Date: 1957-03-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 12th: India's general election has been completed in six states (Andhra, Assam, Kerala, Bombay, Madras, and Madhya Pradesh) and one federal territory (Manipur). Among the sixty-eight seats in the People's Chamber that have already been announced, the Congress Party won fifty-two, the People's Socialist Party won six, the Communist Party of India won two, and independent candidates won three. Among those elected is Gopalan, the leader of the Communist Party of India's parliamentary group in the outgoing parliament. According to newspaper reports, Prime Minister Nehru is already far ahead in the vote count. Among the one thousand three hundred and sixty-five state legislative assembly seats that have already been announced, the Congress Party won nine hundred and twenty-seven, the People's Socialist Party won ninety-five, the Communist Party of India won sixty-five, and the Indian People's Alliance won twenty-seven.

### 印度六个邦已选举完毕  尼赫鲁得票领先  高普兰当选

1957-03-14
第6版()
专栏：

    印度六个邦已选举完毕
    尼赫鲁得票领先  高普兰当选
    据新华社新德里12日电  印度的普选已经在六个邦（安得拉、阿萨姆、喀拉拉、孟买、马德拉斯和中央邦）和一个联邦领地（曼尼普尔）进行完毕。在已经宣布的人民院的六十八个席位中，国大党得五十二席，人民社会党得六席，印度共产党得二席，无党派人士得三席。当选的人有印度共产党本届国会党团领袖高普兰。据报纸消息说，尼赫鲁总理在计票中已经遥遥领先。在已经宣布的一千三百六十五个邦立法会议席位中，国大党得九百二十七席，人民社会党得九十五席，印度共产党得六十五席，印度人民同盟得二十七席。
