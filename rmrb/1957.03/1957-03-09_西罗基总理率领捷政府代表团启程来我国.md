Title: Siroky Leads Czechoslovak Government Delegation to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1957-03-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 8th. At the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, the Government Delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic, led by Premier Viliam Široký, departed Prague by plane this afternoon via Moscow for a friendly visit to Beijing. Mrs. Široký also accompanied the delegation. Cao Ying, the Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, accompanied the delegation to Beijing.

Present at the airport to see off the delegation were leaders of the Czechoslovak government and the Czechoslovak Communist Party, as well as representatives from all walks of life. Yang Yong-seop, the Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to Czechoslovakia, Sang-Rabdan, the Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to Czechoslovakia, Nguyen Tri Ngo, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to Czechoslovakia, staff members of the Chinese Embassy in Czechoslovakia, and diplomatic envoys from many other countries also went to the airport to see them off. Before departing, Premier Široký reviewed the guard of honor at the airport.

### 西罗基总理率领捷政府代表团启程来我国

1957-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

    西罗基总理率领捷政府代表团启程来我国
    新华社布拉格8日电　应中华人民共和国政府的邀请前往中国作友好访问的捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团在威廉·西罗基总理的率领下，今天下午乘飞机离布拉格经莫斯科前往北京。西罗基夫人也和代表团同行。中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使曹瑛陪同代表团前往北京。
    到机场欢送代表团的有捷克斯洛伐克政府和捷克斯洛伐克共产党的领导人和社会各界的代表。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻捷大使杨永笋，蒙古人民共和国驻捷大使桑·拉布丹，越南民主共和国驻捷大使馆临时代办阮智吾以及中国驻捷大使馆的工作人员和其他许多国家的外交使节也到机场送行。西罗基总理临行前在飞机场检阅了仪仗队。
