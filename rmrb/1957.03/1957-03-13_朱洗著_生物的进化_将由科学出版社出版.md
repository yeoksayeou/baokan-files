Title: Zhu Xi's "The Evolution of Organisms" to be Published by Science Press
Author:
Date: 1957-03-13
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Biologist Zhu Xi has completed a 600,000-word work, "The Evolution of Organisms," which will be published by Science Press.

How did organisms evolve from simple to complex, and from lower to higher?

In his new book, Zhu Xi provides a relatively comprehensive and complete introduction to this most fundamental question in biology. He describes in detail the origins of evolutionary thought since ancient times, and lists various discovered evidences and facts about evolution. In the part exploring the mechanisms of evolution and the causes of variations, he introduces the evolutionary theories of different schools of thought from around the world, analyzes and compares them, and puts forward his own views.

### 朱洗著“生物的进化”将由科学出版社出版

1957-03-13
第7版()
专栏：

    朱洗著“生物的进化”将由科学出版社出版
    据新华社讯　生物学家朱洗完成了一个六十万字的著作“生物的进化”，这本书将由科学出版社出版。
    生物是怎样由简单进化到复杂、由低级到高级的？
    朱洗在自己的新著作中，对这个生物学上最根本的问题作了比较全面、完整的介绍。他详细叙述了自古以来进化思想的渊源，列举出关于进化的各种已发现的证据和事实。在探讨进化的基理、产生变异的原因的部分，他介绍了世界各国不同学派的进化学说，并加以分析比较，提出了自己的看法。
