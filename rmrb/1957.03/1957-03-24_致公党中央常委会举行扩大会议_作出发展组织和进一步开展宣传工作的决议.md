Title: China Zhi Gong Party Central Standing Committee Holds Enlarged Meeting, Resolves to Develop Organization and Further Carry Out Propaganda Work
Author:
Date: 1957-03-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd. The China Zhi Gong Party held its Fourth (Enlarged) Meeting of the Central Standing Committee in Beijing from the 21st to the 23rd. In addition to the members of the Central Standing Committee, the meeting was attended by Central Committee members in Beijing, heads of various Central departments, and Zhi Gong Party members who recently came to Beijing to attend the Third Session of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC. The meeting was presided over by Chairman Chen Qiyou.

At this meeting, emphasis was placed on studying Chairman Mao's report "On the Correct Handling of Contradictions Among the People" delivered at the Supreme State Conference, and at the same time, the resolutions of the Third Session of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC were studied and discussed. Based on the instructions in Chairman Mao's report and the spirit of the CPPCC resolutions, the work of the China Zhi Gong Party over the past six months was discussed and reviewed. The meeting proceeded in a warm atmosphere, with enthusiastic speeches. In their speeches, everyone unanimously expressed their support for Chairman Mao's instructions and the various resolutions of the National Committee of the CPPCC, and expressed their willingness to further study and conscientiously implement them in their work.

The meeting also made resolutions on developing the organization and further carrying out propaganda work. The resolution on developing the organization further clarified the targets and locations for developing the organization, as well as the indicators for increasing institutions in various regions, emphasizing that development should be combined with consolidation. The resolution on further carrying out propaganda work clarified the China Zhi Gong Party's guidelines for external propaganda and internal education.

The meeting also resolved to endorse the resolutions and instructions of the Third Session of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC, and that organizations at all levels and all members should conscientiously study the various documents of this CPPCC meeting and implement them.

### 致公党中央常委会举行扩大会议　作出发展组织和进一步开展宣传工作的决议

1957-03-24
第4版()
专栏：

    致公党中央常委会举行扩大会议
　　作出发展组织和进一步开展宣传工作的决议
    新华社23日讯　中国致公党在21日到23日在北京举行了中央常务委员会第四次（扩大）会议。参加这次会议的除了中央常务委员外，还有在京中央委员、中央各部门负责人和最近来京出席政协第二届全国委员会第三次会议的致公党成员。会议由陈其尤主席主持。
    在这次会议上，着重学习了毛主席在最高国务会议上所作的“关于正确地处理人民内部矛盾的问题”的报告，同时学习和讨论了政协第二届全国委员会第三次会议的各项决议。并根据毛主席报告中的指示和政协决议的精神，讨论和检查了致公党半年多来的工作。会议始终在热烈的气氛中进行，发言踊跃。大家在发言中对毛主席的指示和全国政协的各项决议一致表示拥护，并愿更进一步学习，在工作中切实贯彻执行。
    会议并作出了关于发展组织和进一步开展宣传工作的决议。在发展组织的决议中，更具体地明确了发展组织的对象、地方和各地发展组织增建机构的指标，强调发展应与巩固相结合。在进一步开展宣传工作的决议中，明确了致公党进行对外宣传和对内教育的方针。
    会议还作出决议：拥护政协第二届全国委员会第三次会议的决议和指示，各级组织以及全体成员应认真学习这次政协会议的各项文件，并加以贯彻。
