Title: Czechoslovak Government Delegation Visits the Palace Museum Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1957-03-28
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, March 27th: Members of the Czechoslovak Republic Government Delegation visited the Palace Museum this morning.

Accompanying them were Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Minister of Culture, and Wu Zhongchao, Director of the Palace Museum.

The “sundial” in front of the Hall of Supreme Harmony attracted the distinguished guests. Zheng Zhenduo explained that it was used for timekeeping by the ancient Chinese people, and he also explained the method of timekeeping to the guests. The ancient Chinese cultural relics displayed in the Hall of Supreme Harmony also aroused the interest of the distinguished guests. The wife of Cao Ying, the Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, introduced many historical anecdotes about the Palace Museum to Mrs. Široký.

Falling behind the visiting group was the Minister of Finance of the Czechoslovak Republic, J. Ďuriš, who was busy photographing the buildings in the Palace Museum.

Before saying goodbye to the Palace Museum, O. Černík, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, wrote an inscription for the Palace Museum, saying that they were deeply impressed by the ancient culture of the Chinese people over the past thousands of years and the spirit of protecting these treasures.

This afternoon, the preparatory committee of the Chinese Catholic Patriotic Association held a tea party to entertain the Minister of Health of the Czechoslovak Republic, Plojhar.

### 捷政府代表团昨日游览故宫

1957-03-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　捷政府代表团昨日游览故宫
    新华社27日讯　捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团团员今天上午参观了故宫。
    陪同参观的有文化部副部长郑振铎和故宫博物院院长吴仲超。
    太和殿前的“日晷”，吸引了贵宾们。郑振铎为他们解释说，这是古时中国人民计时用的，他并且为客人们讲解了计时的方法。太和殿内陈列的中国古代文物也引起了贵宾们的兴趣。中国驻捷大使曹瑛的夫人，向西罗基夫人介绍了许多有关故宫的典故。
    老是落在参观队伍后面的是捷克斯洛伐克共和国财政部长尤·杜里什，他忙着把故宫里的建筑物摄入镜头。
    在告别故宫前，捷政府代表团团员、捷共中央委员会书记奥·契尔尼克为故宫博物院题字说，他们对中国人民千百年来的古老文化和保护这些宝藏的精神，留下很深的印象。
    今天下午，中国天主教友爱国会筹备委员会举行茶会，招待捷克斯洛伐克共和国卫生部长普洛伊哈。
