Title: Six Swiss and Italian Cultural Figures Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-03-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 29th: Professor An Babel of the University of Geneva, Switzerland, Professor Hen Ziegler, and the Italian cultural delegation consisting of A. Giugnanino, M. Impereal, L. Lanziotti, and M. Benedetti, arrived in Beijing yesterday at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. Zhu Boshen, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Professor Feng Youlan of Peking University, along with many others, welcomed them at the airport.

### 瑞意文化人士六人到京

1957-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

　  瑞意文化人士六人到京
    新华社29日讯  应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的瑞士日内瓦大学安·巴贝尔教授和亨·齐格莱教授二人及意大利文化代表团阿·吉乌干尼诺、马·英彼雷阿利、里·兰齐奥蒂和马·贝内狄克特等四人已于昨日抵京。到机场欢迎的有中国人民对外文化协会副秘书长朱伯深、北京大学教授冯友兰等多人。
