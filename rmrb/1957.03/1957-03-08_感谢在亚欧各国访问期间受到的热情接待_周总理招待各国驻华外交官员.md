Title: Premier Zhou Hosts Diplomatic Officials from Various Countries Stationed in China, Expressing Gratitude for the Warm Reception Received During Visits to Asian and European Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-03-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th March: Premier Zhou Enlai held a reception at the International Club this evening to entertain the diplomatic envoys and economic representatives stationed in China from the Asian and European countries he recently visited.

The diplomatic envoys and heads of economic delegations from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Cambodia, India, Burma, Pakistan, the Soviet Union, Poland, Hungary, and Afghanistan stationed in China, as well as the Ceylon trade representative currently in Beijing, were invited to attend the reception. The Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China was also invited. Representing the Chinese side at the reception were Vice Premier He Long, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Wang Jiaxiang, Zhang Hanfu, and Ji Pengfei, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Vice Minister Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan, General Xiao Hua and Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, personnel who accompanied Premier Zhou Enlai on his visits abroad, and responsible persons from the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, the Sino-Pakistan Friendship Association, and the Sino-Nepalese Friendship Association.

Premier Zhou Enlai had cordial conversations with the guests at the reception and expressed his gratitude for the warm and friendly reception he received in those countries.

### 感谢在亚欧各国访问期间受到的热情接待  周总理招待各国驻华外交官员

1957-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

    感谢在亚欧各国访问期间受到的热情接待
    周总理招待各国驻华外交官员
    新华社7日讯　周恩来总理今晚在国际俱乐部举行酒会，招待最近访问过的亚洲和欧洲各国驻中国的外交使节和经济代表。
    越南民主共和国、柬埔寨、印度、缅甸、巴基斯坦、苏联、波兰、匈牙利、阿富汗等国驻中国的外交使节和经济代表团团长，现在北京的锡兰贸易代表等应邀出席了酒会。德意志民主共和国驻华大使也应邀出席了酒会。中国方面参加招待会的，有贺龙副总理，外交部副部长王稼祥、章汉夫、姬鹏飞，对外贸易部部长叶季壮、副部长雷任民，文化部副部长夏衍，中国人民解放军萧华上将、萧向荣中将，随同周恩来总理出国访问的人员，以及中苏友好协会、中缅友好协会、中巴友好协会和中尼友好协会的负责人。
    周恩来总理在酒会上同来宾们进行了亲切的谈话，并且感谢在这些国家受到的热情友好的招待。
