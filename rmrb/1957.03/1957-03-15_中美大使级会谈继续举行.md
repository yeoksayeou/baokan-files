Title: Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Continue
Author:
Date: 1957-03-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, March 14th: The Sino-U.S. ambassadorial talks held their sixty-sixth meeting today. The meeting adjourned after two hours. The next meeting will be held on April 11th.

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1957-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦14日电  中美两国大使级会谈今天举行第六十六次会议。会议举行了两个小时后休会。下次会议将在4月11日举行。
