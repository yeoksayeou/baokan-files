Title: Voroshilov and Bulganin Receive Hungarian Government and Party Delegations Respectively
Author:
Date: 1957-03-22
Page: 7

　　Voroshilov and Bulganin
    Respectively Receive Hungarian Government and Party Delegations

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 21st: According to TASS, Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, and Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, received the Hungarian People's Republic government and the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party delegation headed by János Kádár in the Kremlin on the 20th.

    On the same day, Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko and Defense Minister Marshal Zhukov respectively received delegation members, Hungarian Foreign Minister Imre Horváth and Defense Minister Lieutenant General Géza Révész.

    On this day, Bulganin also held an early banquet for the delegation in the Grand Kremlin Palace. Soviet party and government leaders such as Voroshilov, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Molotov, Pervukhin, Saburov, Suslov, Khrushchev, and Zhukov attended. The Hungarian government and party delegation visited the Lenin and Stalin Mausoleum in Red Square that afternoon and laid wreaths. In addition, delegation members, accompanied by Soviet state leaders, watched the ballet "Romeo and Juliet" at the Bolshoi Theatre. (Attached Picture)

### 伏罗希洛夫和布尔加宁  分别接见匈政府和党代表团

1957-03-22
第7版()
专栏：

　　伏罗希洛夫和布尔加宁
    分别接见匈政府和党代表团
    新华社21日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫、苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁，20日在克里姆林宫分别接见了以卡达尔·亚诺什为首的匈牙利人民共和国政府和匈牙利社会主义工人党的代表团。
    同日，苏联外交部长葛罗米柯和国防部长朱可夫元帅分别接见了代表团团员、匈牙利外交部长霍瓦特·伊姆雷和国防部长雷维斯·格佐中将。
    这一天，布尔加宁还在大克里姆林宫为代表团举行了早宴。伏罗希洛夫、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、莫洛托夫、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、苏斯洛夫、赫鲁晓夫、朱可夫等苏联党和政府领导人出席作陪。匈牙利政府和党的代表团在这一天下午前往红场拜谒了列宁和斯大林墓，并且敬献了花圈。此外，代表团人员在苏联国家领导人的陪同下还到大剧院观看了巴蕾舞剧“罗米欧与朱丽叶”。（附图片）
