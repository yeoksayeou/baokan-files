Title: Ambassador Geng Biao Hosts Banquet for Pakistani President
Author:
Date: 1957-03-29
Page: 1
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, March 28th. Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan, Geng Biao, held a banquet on the evening of March 27th to entertain President Mirza.

　　Mrs. Mirza and Interior Minister Ghulam Ali Talpur were also invited to attend the banquet.

　　At the banquet, Ambassador Geng Biao said that during Premier Zhou Enlai's visit to Pakistan, he was warmly received by President Mirza. He once again expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the President and hoped to further develop the friendship and cooperation between China and Pakistan.

　　President Mirza said in his reply that Pakistan was very pleased to receive Premier Zhou Enlai and hoped that he would visit again. He said, "Premier Zhou Enlai is one of the few people I admire." He hoped that cooperation between the two countries would become closer and closer. He proposed a toast to the prosperity, greatness, and glory of China, and to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai.

### 耿飚大使宴请巴基斯坦总统

1957-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　耿飚大使宴请巴基斯坦总统
    新华社卡拉奇28日电  中国驻巴基斯坦大使耿飚在3月27日晚上举行宴会招待米尔扎总统。
    米尔扎夫人和内政部长吴拉姆·阿里·塔尔普也应邀出席了宴会。
    耿飚大使在宴会上讲话说，中国总理周恩来在访问巴基斯坦期间，受到了米尔扎总统的热诚款待，他再一次地表示他对总统的衷心感谢，并且希望进一步发展中国和巴基斯坦之间的友谊和合作。
    米尔扎总统致答辞说，巴基斯坦非常高兴接待周恩来总理，并且希望他再来访问。他说：“周恩来总理是我所敬佩的几个人中的一个。”他希望两国之间的合作将会愈来愈密切。他提议为中国的繁荣、伟大和光荣干杯，为毛泽东主席和周恩来总理的健康干杯。
