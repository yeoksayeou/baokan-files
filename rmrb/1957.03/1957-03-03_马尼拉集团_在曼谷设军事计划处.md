Title: Manila Group Establishes Military Planning Office in Bangkok
Author:
Date: 1957-03-03
Page: 6

    Manila Group
    Establishes Military Planning Office in Bangkok
    Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, 2nd. The Manila Treaty Organization yesterday established its military planning office in Bangkok. This planning office, headed by Philippine General Santos, will be responsible for adjusting the military plans of the Manila Treaty Organization and organizing military exercises.

### 马尼拉集团  在曼谷设军事计划处

1957-03-03
第6版()
专栏：

    马尼拉集团
    在曼谷设军事计划处
    据新华社2日讯  曼谷消息：马尼拉条约组织昨天在曼谷设立了它的军事计划处。这个以菲律宾将军桑托斯为首的计划处的任务，将是调整马尼拉条约组织的军事计划和组织军事演习。
