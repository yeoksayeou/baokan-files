Title: Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-03-25
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, March 24th: Nie Rongzhen, Special Envoy of the People's Republic of China and Vice Premier of the State Council, Marshal Nie Rongzhen, and his entourage, who were invited to attend the independence celebrations of Ghana, returned to Beijing by plane this morning. Those who greeted him at the airport included Zeng Yifan, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council, Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and He Gongkai, Deputy Director of the Department of West Asian and African Affairs.

### 聂荣臻副总理回到北京

1957-03-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　聂荣臻副总理回到北京
    新华社24日讯　应邀参加加纳庆祝独立典礼的中华人民共和国特使、国务院副总理聂荣臻元帅和随员，今天早晨乘飞机回到北京。到机场迎接的，有国务院副秘书长曾一凡、外交部副部长章汉夫、国防部办公厅主任萧向荣、外交部礼宾司司长王倬如、西亚非洲司副司长何功楷。
