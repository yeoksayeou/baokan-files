Title: Chinese Mechanical Workers Support British Workers' Strike
Author:
Date: 1957-03-28
Page: 6

　　Chinese Mechanical Workers
　　Support British Workers' Strike
Xinhua News Agency, March 26th. Kang Yonghe, Chairman of the China National Committee of the Machinery and Metallurgical Workers' Union of China, today sent a telegram to Mr. Brassedon, Chairman of the Confederation of Shipbuilding and Engineering Unions, and Mr. Martin, General Secretary, supporting the strike movement of British mechanical and shipbuilding workers. The telegram said: "Chinese mechanical workers extend close solidarity and firm support to the British mechanical and shipbuilding workers who are waging a great struggle to raise wages and defend their living standards, and wish you victory in your struggle."

### 我国机械工人　支持英国工人的罢工

1957-03-28
第6版()
专栏：

　　我国机械工人
　　支持英国工人的罢工
    新华社26日讯　中国第一机械工会主席康永和今天打电报给英国造船工人和机械工业工人工会联合会主席布拉塞顿和总书记马丁，支持英国机械工人和造船工人的罢工运动。电报说：“中国机械工人谨向正在为争取提高工资和维护自己的生活水平而进行巨大斗争的英国机械和造船工人表示紧密的团结和坚决的支持，愿你们的斗争获得胜利。”
