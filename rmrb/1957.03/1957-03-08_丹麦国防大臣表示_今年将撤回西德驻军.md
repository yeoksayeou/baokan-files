Title: Danish Defense Minister Says Troops Will Be Withdrawn from West Germany This Year
Author:
Date: 1957-03-08
Page: 6

    Danish Defense Minister Says
    Troops Will Be Withdrawn from West Germany This Year
    Xinhua News Agency, Copenhagen, 7th - Danish Defense Minister Poul Hansen said in parliament on the 5th that the Danish government hopes to withdraw Danish troops stationed in West Germany before mid-November this year. The law regarding the stationing of Danish troops in West Germany should expire in May this year, and Hansen requested that the validity of this law be extended by 6 months.
    The total number of Danish troops currently stationed in West Germany is approximately 1,500.

### 丹麦国防大臣表示  今年将撤回西德驻军

1957-03-08
第6版()
专栏：

    丹麦国防大臣表示
    今年将撤回西德驻军
    新华社7日讯　哥本哈根消息：丹麦国防大臣保罗·汉森5日在议会中说，丹麦政府希望在今年11月中旬以前撤回丹麦驻扎在西德的军队。关于丹麦在西德驻扎军队的法律应该在今年5月里满期，汉森要求把这条法律的有效期限延长6个月。
    丹麦目前驻在西德的军队总人数约为一千五百人。
