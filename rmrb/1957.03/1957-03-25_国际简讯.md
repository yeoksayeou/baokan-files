International Briefs

    East Germany Uses Airplanes for Fertilization
    The aerial chemical flight group of the East German Airlines began fertilizing farmland in the Strausberg region of the Frankfurt/Oder district on the 21st. Each aircraft can fertilize ten hectares of farmland per hour.

    Czechoslovakia and Bulgaria Sign Trade and Payment Agreement
    Bulgaria and Czechoslovakia signed a trade and payment agreement for 1957 in Sofia on the 23rd.

    Hungarian Government Builds Flood Control Facilities
    Despite financial difficulties, the Hungarian Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government has decided to allocate 30 million forints this year for the construction of flood control facilities along the Danube and Tisza rivers.

    French CGT Delegation Leaves Yugoslavia for Home
    After a week-long visit to Yugoslavia, the delegation of the French General Confederation of Labor (CGT) flew back to France from Zagreb on the 23rd. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 国际简讯

1957-03-25
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

　　国际简讯
    民主德国用飞机施肥
    民主德国航空公司的航空化学飞行组21日开始在奥得河畔法兰克福专区的斯特劳斯堡地区对耕地施肥。每一架飞机每小时可以对十公顷耕地施肥。
    捷保签订换货支付协定
    保加利亚同捷克斯洛伐克23日在索非亚签订了1957年换货和支付协定。
    匈政府修建防洪设备
    匈牙利工农革命政府今年在财政困难的情况下，仍决定拨款三千万福林作修建多瑙河和蒂萨河沿岸的防洪设备之用。
    法总工会代表团离南返国
    法国总工会代表团在南斯拉夫作了一周访问之后，23日由萨格勒布乘飞机回国。　（据新华社讯）
