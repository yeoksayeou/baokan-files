Title: Nixon Receives Cold Reception in Sudan; U.S. to Build Base in Ethiopia
Author:
Date: 1957-03-15
Page: 7

    Nixon Receives Cold Reception in Sudan
    U.S. to Build Base in Ethiopia
    Xinhua News Agency, March 14th: U.S. Vice President Richard Nixon received a cold reception from the local people when he arrived in Khartoum, the capital of Sudan, on March 13th. Before Nixon arrived in Khartoum, leaflets were distributed telling him to "go home."
    Nixon subsequently held talks with Sudanese Prime Minister Khalil. Nixon said after the talks that Khalil emphasized that Sudan needed economic assistance rather than military aid, but that it should not be attached to any conditions.
    Xinhua News Agency, March 13th: Ethiopian Emperor Selassie, after meeting with U.S. Vice President Nixon on March 12th, told American journalists that Ethiopia had agreed to allow the United States to build an air force communications center in his country.

### 尼克松在苏丹受到冷遇  美国将在埃塞俄比亚建基地

1957-03-15
第7版()
专栏：

    尼克松在苏丹受到冷遇
    美国将在埃塞俄比亚建基地
    据新华社14日讯  美国副总统尼克松3月13日到达苏丹首都喀土穆的时候受到了当地人民冷淡的接待。在尼克松到达喀土穆之前，有人散发传单，要他“回家去”。
    尼克松随后同苏丹总理哈利勒举行了会谈。尼克松在会谈后说，哈利勒强调指出，苏丹需要经济援助而不是军事援助，但是不得附加任何条件。
    据新华社13日讯  埃塞俄比亚皇帝塞拉西在3月12日同美国副总统尼克松会谈后向美国新闻记者表明，埃塞俄比亚已经同意美国在他的国家建立一个空军通讯中心。
