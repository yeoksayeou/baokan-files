Title: Indonesian Peace Committee Protests Manila Group's Interference in Internal Affairs
Author:
Date: 1957-03-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 11th: The Indonesian National Peace Committee issued a statement today protesting the Manila Treaty Organization Council meeting's plan to discuss the Indonesian issue. The statement said that the Indonesian people do not want any foreign interference in Indonesia's internal affairs.

The statement said that the Indonesian people hope to further strengthen the relationship between Asian and African countries, especially Southeast Asian countries, which has been increasingly consolidated since the Bandung Asian-African Conference.

### 印度尼西亚和平委员会抗议马尼拉集团干涉内政

1957-03-12
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚和平委员会抗议马尼拉集团干涉内政
    新华社雅加达11日电　印度尼西亚全国和平委员会今天发表声明，抗议马尼拉条约组织理事会会议计划讨论印度尼西亚问题。声明说，印度尼西亚人民不希望任何外国干涉印度尼西亚的内政。
    声明说，印度尼西亚人民希望进一步加强在万隆亚非会议以后日益巩固的亚非国家，特别是东南亚国家之间的关系。
