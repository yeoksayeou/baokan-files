Title: Saving Steel by Utilizing Waste Materials (Pictures)
Author: Yang Putao, Pu Bingyan, Zhang Ruihua
Date: 1957-03-09
Page: 5

Saving Steel by Utilizing Waste Materials

    Top Image: The state-owned Shanghai No. 18 Cotton Textile Factory processed nearly 900,000 cotton blankets last year using waste cotton from the textile factory and scraps of fabric left over from people's tailoring, supplying both domestic and international markets.
              Xinhua News Agency reporter Yang Putao Photo

    Top Left Image: The joint state-private Changzhou Dingtai Yuan Smelting Factory cooperated with the machine factories under the Changzhou Heavy Industry Bureau to successfully trial-smelt a type of recycled iron using scrap iron – iron filings – from the machine factories. This recycled iron can still be supplied to the machine industry as raw material.
        Pu Bingyan Photo (Draft from Changzhou Workers' Newspaper)

    Left Image: Weng Bing (standing in the back), an engineer at the Taiyuan Heavy Machinery Factory, helped technician Yu Shouxi design the wheels for the 50-ton bridge crane main carriage, changing the wheel diameter from 900 mm to 600 mm. Each crane saves 1,880 kilograms of steel. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Ruihua Photo

### 节约钢材利用废料（图片）
杨溥涛  浦炳炎  张瑞华
1957-03-09
第5版()
专栏：

    节约钢材利用废料
    上图：国营上海第十八棉纺织厂去年用纺织厂里不要的废花和人们裁衣剩下的碎布加工织成了近九十万条棉毯，供应国内外市场。
              新华社记者　杨溥涛摄
    左上图：公私合营常州鼎泰元冶炼厂和常州市重工业局所属的机器厂合作，利用机器厂中的废料——铁屑，试炼成一种再生铁成功。这种再生铁仍可供给机器工业作原料。
        浦炳炎摄（常州工人报社稿）
    左图：太原重型机器厂的工程师翁秉（后立者）帮助技术员余寿熙设计五十吨桥式起重机大车车轮，把车轮直径由九百公厘改为六百公厘。每台起重机节省钢材一千八百八十公斤。　新华社记者　张瑞华摄
