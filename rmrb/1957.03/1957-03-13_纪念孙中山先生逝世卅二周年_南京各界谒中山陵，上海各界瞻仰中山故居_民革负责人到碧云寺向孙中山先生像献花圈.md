Title: Commemorating the 32nd Anniversary of the Death of Mr. Sun Yat-sen
Author:
Date: 1957-03-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, March 12th: People from all walks of life in Jiangsu Province and Nanjing City paid their respects at the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum today to commemorate the 32nd anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's death.

　　At around 9 a.m., people from all walks of life held a memorial service in Mr. Sun Yat-sen's memorial hall. Guan Wenwei, Vice Governor of Jiangsu Province, Peng Chong, Mayor of Nanjing City, Ren Chonggao, member of the Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), and Xu Meifeng, Vice Chairman of the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the CPPCC, presented wreaths to Mr. Sun Yat-sen's seated statue on behalf of people from all walks of life. Afterwards, people from all walks of life entered the tomb chamber and paid their respects to Mr. Sun Yat-sen's reclining statue.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, March 12th: Today is the 32nd anniversary of the death of Mr. Sun Yat-sen, and people from all walks of life in Shanghai went to Mr. Sun Yat-sen's former residence to pay their respects and present wreaths.

　　Xinhua News Agency, March 12th: Today is the 32nd anniversary of the death of Mr. Sun Yat-sen. Cai Tingkai and Zhang Zhizhong, Vice Chairmen of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, along with more than 40 standing committee members and heads of various departments, went to the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall in Biyun Temple at 10 a.m. to present wreaths to Mr. Sun Yat-sen's statue and observed a moment of silence.

### 纪念孙中山先生逝世卅二周年  南京各界谒中山陵，上海各界瞻仰中山故居  民革负责人到碧云寺向孙中山先生像献花圈

1957-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

    纪念孙中山先生逝世卅二周年
    南京各界谒中山陵，上海各界瞻仰中山故居
    民革负责人到碧云寺向孙中山先生像献花圈
    据新华社南京12日电　江苏省和南京市各界人士今天谒中山陵，纪念孙中山先生逝世三十二周年。
    九时许，各界人士在中山先生灵堂里举行了谒陵仪式。江苏省副省长管文蔚、南京市市长彭冲、政协江苏省委员会委员任崇高、政协南京市委员会副主席徐美峰，代表各界人士向中山先生坐像敬献花圈。随后，各界人士进入墓室，瞻仰了中山先生卧像。
    据新华社上海12日电　今天是孙中山先生逝世三十二周年纪念日，上海市各界人士前往中山故居瞻仰并敬献花圈。
    新华社12日讯　今天是孙中山先生逝世三十二周年。中国国民党革命委员会副主席蔡廷锴、张治中以及常务委员和各部负责人四十多人，在上午十时前往碧云寺孙中山纪念堂向孙中山先生像敬献花圈，并且默哀致敬。
