Title: For a Great Harvest (Picture)
Author: Wang Shilong
Date: 1957-03-02
Page: 3

    For a Great Harvest
    In order to strive for a comprehensive bumper harvest this year, the "August 1st" Agricultural Cooperative in the suburbs of Xinxiang City, Henan Province, has proposed a plan to apply two and a half large carts of manure per mu of land. This is a scene of more than 20 rubber-wheeled horse carts from the cooperative delivering manure. (Photo by Wang Shilong, Xinxiang Daily)

### 为了大丰收（图片）
王世龙
1957-03-02
第3版()
专栏：

    为了大丰收
    河南省新乡市郊区“八一”农业社为争取今年全面大丰收，提出了每亩地上两大车半粪肥的计划。这是社里的二十多辆胶轮马车送粪的情形。  王世龙摄（新乡日报社稿）
