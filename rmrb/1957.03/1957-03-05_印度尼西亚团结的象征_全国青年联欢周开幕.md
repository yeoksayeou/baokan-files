Title: Indonesian Unity Symbolized: National Youth Week Opens
Author:
Date: 1957-03-05
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 4th: The Indonesian National Youth Week opened in Surabaya on the morning of the 3rd. President Sukarno and more than 3,300 young people from all over Indonesia attended the opening ceremony of this Unity Week. This is the first time in Indonesian history that so many young people with different political views, religious beliefs, and belonging to different ethnic groups and professions have gathered together.
　　President Sukarno delivered a speech at the opening ceremony of the Youth Week.
　　He said that the youth all have the ideal of realizing national unity and independence. Our ideals and unity will not disappear like the morning dew. But ideals and unity can only be achieved through struggle.

### 印度尼西亚团结的象征  全国青年联欢周开幕

1957-03-05
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚团结的象征
    全国青年联欢周开幕
    据新华社雅加达4日电  印度尼西亚全国青年联欢周3日上午在泗水开幕。苏加诺总统同来自印度尼西亚全国各地的青年三千三百多人参加这个象征团结的联欢周的开幕仪式。这许多有着不同的政治见解、宗教信仰以及属于不同的民族和职业的青年集合在一起，在印度尼西亚历史上是第一次。
    苏加诺总统在联欢周的开幕仪式上发表了讲话。
    他说，青年们都有实现国家的统一和独立的理想。我们的理想和团结不会像朝露一样消失。但是理想和团结要经过斗争才能实现。
