Title: Egyptian Administrator Arrives in Gaza
Author:
Date: 1957-03-16
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 15th: The Middle East News Agency reported that the Egyptian government-appointed administrator of the Gaza Strip, Latif, arrived in Gaza City on March 14th.

Latif and a group of Egyptian military and administrative officials he led were warmly welcomed by Gaza residents.

### 埃及行政长官到达加沙

1957-03-16
第7版()
专栏：

    埃及行政长官到达加沙
    新华社开罗15日电  据中东通讯社报道，埃及政府任命的加沙地带行政长官拉蒂夫在3月14日到达加沙城。
    拉蒂夫和他率领的一批埃及军事、行政官员受到加沙居民的热烈欢迎。
