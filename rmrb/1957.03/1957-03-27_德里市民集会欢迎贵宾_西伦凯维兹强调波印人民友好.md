Title: Delhi Citizens Rally to Welcome Distinguished Guest; Cyrankiewicz Emphasizes Polish-Indian Friendship
Author:
Date: 1957-03-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 26th:
    Delhi Citizens Rally to Welcome Distinguished Guest
    Cyrankiewicz Emphasizes Polish-Indian Friendship
    Polish Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Józef Cyrankiewicz, stated at a Delhi citizen welcome rally on the evening of March 25th: Poland and India are linked by a common desire for peace and prosperity, and they share a common language of peace, freedom, and progress.
    Cyrankiewicz delivered a speech to thousands of Delhi citizens gathered in the square at the ancient Red Fort. He said that the Polish people, who suffered too many losses in the last world war, know the value of peace. They are now building a new socialist life and pursuing a peaceful foreign policy. The Indian people hold great sympathy for Poland.
    Cyrankiewicz said that the Polish people deeply sympathize with India, knowing the long history of India's struggle for independence and its tremendous efforts in construction. Poland is willing to expand cooperation with India for the benefit of our two peoples and for the sake of peace.
    Cyrankiewicz and Indian Prime Minister Nehru were cheered by the crowd when they appeared at the welcome rally. Huge crowds lined the streets to welcome the Polish distinguished guest.
    On the morning of the same day, Cyrankiewicz also separately visited Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan and Prime Minister Nehru. He and his entourage also visited the Indian National Physical Laboratory and the Indian Agricultural Research Institute.
    In the afternoon, they also attended a reception held by the Indo-Polish Cultural Association. Many Indian scholars, artists, and well-known social figures expressed a warm welcome to their distinguished guests at the meeting.

### 德里市民集会欢迎贵宾　西伦凯维兹强调波印人民友好

1957-03-27
第5版()
专栏：

　　德里市民集会欢迎贵宾
　　西伦凯维兹强调波印人民友好
    新华社新德里26日电　波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹3月25日晚间在德里市民欢迎会上说：波兰和印度由对于争取和平和繁荣的共同愿望联结在一起，它们有着和平、自由和进步的共同语言。
    西伦凯维兹在古老的红堡中向聚集在广场上的成千德里市民发表演说。他说：在上一次世界大战中遭受到太多损失的波兰人民知道和平的宝贵。他们现在正在建设社会主义新生活和奉行和平的对外政策。印度人民对于波兰抱着很大的同情。
    西伦凯维兹说，波兰人民是深切同情印度的，他们知道印度争取独立斗争的长期历史和它在建设中所作的巨大努力。波兰愿意为了我们两国人民的利益和和平的利益扩大同印度的合作。
    西伦凯维兹同印度总理尼赫鲁出现在欢迎会上的时候受到到会群众的欢呼。巨大的人群排列在街道的两旁欢迎波兰贵宾。　　西伦凯维兹在这一天的上午还曾分别拜访了印度副总统拉达克里希南和总理尼赫鲁。他和他的随行人员还参观了印度国立试验所和印度农业研究所。
    这天下午，他们还参加了印波文化协会举行的招待会。会上许多印度学者、艺术家和知名社会人士对他们的贵宾表示了热烈的欢迎。
