Title: Indian Bharatanatyam Dancers, the Kamala Sisters, Perform in Beijing for the First Time (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1957-03-31
Page: 7

    Indian Bharatanatyam Dancers
    Kamala Sisters Perform in Beijing for the First Time
    The first performance in China by the famous Indian Bharatanatyam dancers Kamala Lakshman and her sister was held on March 29th at the CPPCC Auditorium in Beijing.
    Right picture: Kamala and Radha sisters performing "Song of Ramayana".
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Ji Lianbo photo
    Bottom right picture: Kamala's performance of the snake dance is most popular with Beijing audiences.
    Bottom picture: After the performance, the Indian dancers took a group photo with the Chinese dancers.
        Our reporter Gao Liang photo

### 印度婆罗多舞舞蹈家  卡玛拉姐妹在京首次演出（图片）

1957-03-31
第7版()
专栏：

    印度婆罗多舞舞蹈家
    卡玛拉姐妹在京首次演出
    印度婆罗多舞著名舞蹈家卡玛拉·拉克希曼姐妹在中国的首次演出于3月29日在北京政协礼堂举行。
    右图：卡玛拉和拉达姐妹俩演出的罗摩衍之歌。
    新华社记者　冀连波摄
    右下图：卡玛拉表演的蛇舞最受北京观众欢迎。
    下图：演出结束后，印度舞蹈家同中国舞蹈家们合影。
        本报记者　高粮摄
