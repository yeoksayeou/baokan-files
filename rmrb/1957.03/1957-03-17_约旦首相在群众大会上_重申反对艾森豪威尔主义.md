Title: Jordanian Prime Minister Reiterates Opposition to the Eisenhower Doctrine at Mass Rally
Author:
Date: 1957-03-17
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, March 16th, Amman Dispatch: Jordanian Prime Minister Nabulsi reiterated at a mass rally organized by the government on the 15th that his government opposes the "Eisenhower Doctrine."

Nabulsi said: "Since the announcement of Eisenhower's Middle East policy, my government has declared its opposition to this doctrine." He said, "The government's policy will never be bound or constrained by imperialism."

Nabulsi added: "We do not recognize any vacuum; Arab nationalism occupies this vacuum." He said, "We do not recognize any country's right to directly or indirectly interfere in our affairs."

Nabulsi accused the West of plotting "conspiracies" in the Gaza and Sharm el-Sheikh areas, attempting to internationalize these regions.

Xinhua News Agency, March 16th, Damascus Dispatch from TASS: According to Amman reports, the National Guidance Committee of Nablus has requested the national committees of other cities and large towns in Jordan to call on the Jordanian people to hold a general strike during the visit of President Eisenhower's special envoy Richards to Jordan to protest against the "Eisenhower Doctrine."

The members of the national committees of each city include representatives of almost all political parties and organizations. The committees actively participate in the political, economic, and cultural life of the cities.

Xinhua News Agency, March 16th, Tripoli Dispatch: Libyan Prime Minister Mustafa Ben Halim said at a banquet he hosted for US Vice President Nixon on March 15th that his government "in principle welcomes the Eisenhower Doctrine."

According to Halim, the purpose of the Eisenhower Doctrine is to "help" Libya maintain its independence.

### 约旦首相在群众大会上  重申反对艾森豪威尔主义

1957-03-17
第7版()
专栏：

    约旦首相在群众大会上
    重申反对艾森豪威尔主义
    据新华社16日讯　安曼消息：约旦首相纳布西15日在政府所组织的群众大会上重申，他的政府反对“艾森豪威尔主义”。
    纳布西说：“自从艾森豪威尔的中东政策宣布以来，我的政府就宣布反对这个主义。”他说：“政府的政策决不受帝国主义的束缚或约束。”
    纳布西又说：“我们不承认有什么真空，阿拉伯民族主义占有这种真空。”他说：“我们不承认任何国家有任何权利对我们的事务进行直接或者间接的干涉。”
    纳布西指责西方正在对加沙和沙姆沙伊赫地区策划“阴谋”，企图使这些地区国际化。
    据新华社16日讯　塔斯社大马士革15日讯：据安曼消息，纳布鲁斯市的民族指导委员会要求约旦其他城市和大村镇的民族委员会号召约旦人民在艾森豪威尔总统的特使理查兹访问约旦期间举行总罢工，对“艾森豪威尔主义”表示抗议。
    各市民族委员会的成员几乎包括所有政党和组织的代表。委员会积极参加各市的政治、经济和文化生活。
    据新华社16日讯　的黎波里消息：利比亚首相穆斯塔法·本·哈利姆3月15日在他为美国副总统尼克松举行的宴会上说，他的政府“在原则上欢迎艾森豪威尔主义”。
    据哈利姆说，艾森豪威尔主义的目的是“帮助”利比亚维持独立。
