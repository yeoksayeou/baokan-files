Title: Aung San's Wife Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-03-24
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd: Daw Khin Kyi, wife of the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, and U Nu's son, who accompanied U Nu on his visit to Kunming, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The wife of Yao Zhongming, the Chinese Ambassador to the Union of Burma, accompanied them.
　　Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Song Qingling, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Li Dequan, Shi Liang, and Xu Guangping, Vice Presidents of the All-China Women's Federation, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Qi Yanming, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council, and responsible persons of various women's organizations in Beijing.
　　The wife of the Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung, and the wife of the Pakistani Ambassador to China, Ahmed, also went to the airport to welcome them.
　　This evening, the All-China Women's Federation held a banquet to welcome Daw Khin Kyi and U Nu's son. (Attached picture)
　　Daw Khin Kyi shakes hands with Song Qingling at Beijing Airport. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zheng Xiaozhen

### 昂山夫人到达北京

1957-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　昂山夫人到达北京
    新华社23日讯　随同缅甸联邦总理吴努到昆明访问的昂山夫人（都庆枝）和吴努的公子，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。中国驻缅甸联邦大使姚仲明的夫人陪同前来。
    前往机场欢迎的有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全、史良、许广平，外交部副部长章汉夫，国务院副秘书长齐燕铭以及在京的各妇女团体等负责人。
    缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂的夫人和巴基斯坦驻华大使阿哈默德的夫人也到机场欢迎。
    今天晚间，中华全国民主妇女联合会举行宴会，为昂山夫人和吴努的公子洗尘。（附图片）
　　昂山夫人在北京机场和宋庆龄握手。　新华社记者　郑小箴摄
