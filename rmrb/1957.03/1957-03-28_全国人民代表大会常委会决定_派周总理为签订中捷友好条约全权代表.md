Title: NPC Standing Committee Decides to Appoint Premier Zhou as Plenipotentiary Representative for Signing the Sino-Czech Friendship Treaty
Author:
Date: 1957-03-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 27th — The 53rd meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress was held on the morning of the 26th.

　　The meeting heard Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai's report on the negotiations between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government Delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic, and decided to appoint Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai as the plenipotentiary representative for signing the Treaty of Friendship and Cooperation between the People's Republic of China and the Czechoslovak Republic.

　　The meeting also adopted decisions on appointments and removals, appointments, and approvals of appointments and removals.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会决定  派周总理为签订中捷友好条约全权代表

1957-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民代表大会常委会决定
　  派周总理为签订中捷友好条约全权代表
    新华社27日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会第五十三次会议在26日上午举行。
    会议听取了国务院总理周恩来关于中华人民共和国政府和捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团进行谈判的问题的报告，并且决定派国务院总理周恩来为签订中华人民共和国和捷克斯洛伐克共和国友好合作条约的全权代表。
    会议还通过了决定任免、任命和批准任免的名单。
