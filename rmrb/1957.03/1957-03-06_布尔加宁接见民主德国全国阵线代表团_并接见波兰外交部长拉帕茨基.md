Title: Bulganin Receives Delegation from National Front of Democratic Germany and Receives Polish Foreign Minister Rapacki
Author:
Date: 1957-03-06
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 5th. TASS reported: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, received the delegation from the National Front of Democratic Germany, which is currently visiting the Soviet Union, on the 4th.

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 5th. TASS reported: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, received Polish Foreign Minister Rapacki on the 4th.

### 布尔加宁接见民主德国全国阵线代表团  并接见波兰外交部长拉帕茨基

1957-03-06
第5版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁接见民主德国全国阵线代表团
    并接见波兰外交部长拉帕茨基
    新华社5日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁4日接见了正在苏联进行访问的民主德国全国阵线代表团。
    新华社5日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁4日接见了波兰外交部长拉帕茨基。
