Title: Japanese Film Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-03-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Japanese film delegation of eleven people, led by head of delegation Ushihara Kyohiko, arrived in Beijing tonight at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the China Film Workers Association.

### 日本电影界访华代表团到达北京

1957-03-05
第1版()
专栏：

    日本电影界访华代表团到达北京
    新华社4日讯  应中国人民对外文化协会和中国电影工作者联谊会邀请来华访问的日本电影界访华代表团一行十一人，在团长牛原虚彦率领下已于今晚到达北京。
