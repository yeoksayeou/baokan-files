Title: Cyrankiewicz to Visit Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1957-03-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 8th. Polish News Agency reported on the 7th that Polish Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Cyrankiewicz, will visit Vietnam in early April at the invitation of the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

### 西伦凯维兹将访问越南

1957-03-09
第6版()
专栏：

    西伦凯维兹将访问越南
    新华社华沙8日电　据波兰通讯社7日讯：波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹应越南民主共和国政府的邀请，将在4月初访问越南。
