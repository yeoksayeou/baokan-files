Title: Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital Renamed Beijing China-Soviet Friendship Hospital
Author:
Date: 1957-03-22
Page: 1

　　Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital
　　Renamed Beijing China-Soviet Friendship Hospital
　　Xinhua News Agency, 21st: Today, Zhu Zhongli, Vice President of the Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital, told Xinhua reporters: In order to forever commemorate the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people and the achievements of the Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital, the Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital has decided to change its name to Beijing China-Soviet Friendship Hospital.
    Zhu Zhongli said that the hospital will certainly inherit the excellent medical style of Soviet experts and advanced Soviet medical experience, and better serve the health of the people. In the future, the hospital will change its past system of only accepting patients with difficult and complicated diseases, open outpatient clinics, and charge fees for inpatients who were previously free. She also said that the hospital will also accept the teaching tasks of our country's health cadres' advanced studies.

### 北京苏联红十字医院　改名为北京中苏友谊医院

1957-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京苏联红十字医院
　　改名为北京中苏友谊医院
    据新华社21日讯　今天，北京苏联红十字医院副院长朱仲丽对新华社记者说：为了永远纪念中苏两国人民的友谊和北京苏联红十字医院的功绩，北京苏联红十字医院决定改名为北京中苏友谊医院。
    朱仲丽说，今后医院一定继承苏联专家的优秀医疗作风和苏联先进医学经验，更好地为人民的健康服务。今后医院将改变过去只接受疑难病症患者的制度，开放门诊，过去住院病人免费，今后将收费。她还说，今后医院还将接受我国卫生干部进修医院的教学任务。
