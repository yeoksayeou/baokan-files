Title: Sports Competitions in China to Gradually Increase in April
Author:
Date: 1957-03-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 27th. As the weather warms up, sports competitions in China will gradually increase in April. This month, larger-scale sports events will include soccer, basketball, swimming, water polo, cycling, and cross-country running.

In terms of international sports activities, the Soviet Latvian men's and women's volleyball teams and the German Democratic Republic weightlifting team will visit China this month and hold friendly matches with Chinese athletes. The Cambodian national soccer team and the Ceylon tennis team will also visit China for competitions for the first time.

In ball sports, the second stage of the first round of the 1957 National Soccer League A-League will be held this month in Beijing, Tianjin, Shenyang, Dalian, Changchun. The first stage of the 1957 National Soccer League B-League will be held in Beijing, Tianjin, Shenyang, Dalian, Fushun, Changchun, and Harbin. The 1957 National Basketball League A and B League regional competitions will also begin.

The 1957 Eight Cities Swimming Championships and the 1957 Four Unit Water Polo Tournament will both begin in early April in Beijing. The 1957 National Cycling Championships will also be held in Beijing in late April. The 1957 Fifteen Cities Boxing Championships will begin in early April in Shanghai.

The 1957 National 15-kilometer Cross-Country Race will be held separately in cities with suitable conditions in April. Cities with particularly good conditions will also pilot cross-country races with distances greater than 15 kilometers for the first time.

### 我国体育比赛　四月份将逐渐增多

1957-03-28
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

　　我国体育比赛
　　四月份将逐渐增多
    新华社27日讯  气候转暖，四月份我国体育比赛活动将逐渐增多。在这个月中，规模较大的体育比赛项目将有足球、篮球、游泳、水球、自行车和越野跑等。
    在国际体育活动方面，苏联拉脱维亚男女排球队和德意志民主共和国举重队将在这个月来中国访问，并同中国的运动员举行友谊比赛。柬埔寨国家足球队和锡兰网球队，也将第一次来中国访问比赛。
    在球类方面，这个月将在北京、天津、沈阳、旅大、长春举行1957年全国足球甲级队联赛第一循环的第二阶段比赛，在北京、天津、沈阳、旅大、抚顺、长春和哈尔滨，将举行1957年全国足球乙级队联赛的第一阶段比赛。1957年全国篮球甲、乙级队分区比赛也将开始举行。
    1957年八城市游泳锦标赛和1957年四单位水球对抗赛，都将从四月上旬开始在北京举行。四月下旬还将在北京举行1957年全国自行车锦标赛。1957年十五城市拳击锦标赛，将从4月上旬开始在上海举行。
    1957年全国十五公里越野跑比赛，四月份将在有条件的城市中分别举行。条件特别好的城市，还将第一次试办十五公里以上距离的越野跑比赛。
