Title: Pakistani People Celebrate National Day; President Mirza Defends U.S.-Engineered Military Blocs
Author:
Date: 1957-03-25
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, March 24th: Pakistan celebrated the first anniversary of the adoption of its Republic Constitution on March 23rd. In the morning, an armed forces review was held in the city center. President Mirza reviewed the troops and delivered a speech to the assembled forces.

Prime Minister Suhrawardy's National Day message called on the people to continue dedicating themselves to those enduring virtues that guarantee greater glory for their beloved homeland.

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, March 24th: Pakistani President Mirza delivered a radio address on Pakistan's National Day. He defended military treaties, saying that the Baghdad Pact and the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization (SEATO) were "defensive treaties not directed against any country in the world."

He said that neutralism is "meaningless" and runs counter to my understanding of Islamic doctrine.

He said that our "great ally" the United States is helping us in every possible way, both economically and militarily.

Referring to the Kashmir issue, Mirza reiterated the proposal to hold a plebiscite under the auspices of the United Nations to determine the status of Kashmir.

### 巴基斯坦人民欢度国庆　米尔扎总统为美国拼凑的军事集团辩护

1957-03-25
第6版()
专栏：

　　巴基斯坦人民欢度国庆
　　米尔扎总统为美国拼凑的军事集团辩护
    据新华社卡拉奇24日电　巴基斯坦在3月23日庆祝通过共和国宪法一周年纪念。上午，在市中心举行了武装部队检阅式。米尔扎总统检阅了部队，并且向密集的军队发表了演说。
    苏拉瓦底总理的共和国国庆节日文告，号召人民继续把他们自己献身给那些保证他们可爱的祖国得到更大的荣誉的永久的品德。
    据新华社卡拉奇24日电　巴基斯坦总统米尔扎在巴基斯坦国庆日发表广播演说。他为军事条约辩护说，巴格达条约和东南亚条约是“不针对世界上任何国家的防御性条约”。
    他说，中立主义是“没有意思的”，它同我对伊斯兰教义的理解背道而驰。
    他说，我们“伟大的盟国”美国用一切可能的办法在经济和军事两方面帮助我们。
    米尔扎在谈到克什米尔问题时重申在联合国的主持下举行公民投票来决定克什米尔的地位的主张。
