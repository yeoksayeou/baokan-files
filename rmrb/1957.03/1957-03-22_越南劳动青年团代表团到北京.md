Title: Delegation of the Vietnam Labor Youth League Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-03-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 21st: A delegation of the Vietnam Labor Youth League, headed by Nguyen Lam, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Labor Youth League, arrived in Beijing by train tonight.

Hu Yaobang and Hu Keshi, Secretaries of the Central Secretariat of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and heads of departments of the Central Committee of the League and the Youth Department of the General Political Department of the Military Commission, went to the station to welcome them.

### 越南劳动青年团代表团到北京

1957-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　越南劳动青年团代表团到北京
    新华社21日讯　以越南劳动青年团中央委员会第一书记阮蓝为首的越南劳动青年团代表团一行十人，今天晚上乘火车到达北京。
    中国新民主主义青年团中央书记处书记胡耀邦、胡克实，团中央各部和军委总政治部青年部负责人等前往车站欢迎。
