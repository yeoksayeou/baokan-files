Title: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Telegrams to Congratulate the King of Cambodia on His Birthday and National Day
Author:
Date: 1957-03-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 5th. Chairman Mao Zedong sent a telegram to congratulate King Norodom Suramarit of the Kingdom of Cambodia on his birthday and the National Day of the Kingdom of Cambodia. The telegram reads as follows: His Majesty King Norodom Suramarit:

    On the occasion of Your Majesty's birthday and the National Day of the Kingdom of Cambodia, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to Your Majesty and the people of your country on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish the friendship between China and Cambodia will be further consolidated and developed on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, I wish the Kingdom of Cambodia prosperity and its people thriving, and I wish Your Majesty good health.
                    Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, March 5, 1957

Xinhua News Agency, March 5th. Premier Zhou Enlai today sent telegrams separately to the national leaders of the Kingdom of Cambodia to congratulate King Norodom Suramarit on his birthday and the National Day of the Kingdom of Cambodia.

    In his telegram to King and Queen Norodom Suramarit, Premier Zhou Enlai said: "On the occasion of Your Majesty's birthday and the National Day of the Kingdom of Cambodia, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to Your Majesty. I would like to take this opportunity to express my sincere gratitude once again to Your Majesty the King and Queen for the kind care and hospitality shown to us during our visit to your country. I wish the Kingdom of Cambodia prosperity, and Your Majesty the King and Queen health and longevity."

    In his telegram to Prince Sihanouk, Premier Zhou Enlai extended warm congratulations to His Royal Highness the Prince and the Cambodian people on behalf of the Chinese people and in his own name. The telegram stated:
      "During our visit to your country, we received the kind care and warm reception from His Royal Highness the Prince, and the scenes of our visit to your country remain in our fond memories.

    The Chinese people are pleased to see that the traditional friendly relations between our two countries have been further developed through the mutual visits of the leaders of our two countries. I wish the friendship between China and Cambodia will grow day by day on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, and I wish His Royal Highness and the Cambodian people further achievements in the cause of national construction and safeguarding peace in Asia and the world."

    In his telegram to Prime Minister San Yun, Premier Zhou Enlai extended sincere congratulations to the government of the Kingdom of Cambodia and Prime Minister San Yun on behalf of the Chinese government and in his own name. In his telegram, Premier Zhou Enlai also wished the friendly relations established between China and Cambodia on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence would be increasingly consolidated, and that economic cooperation and cultural exchanges between the two countries would be increasingly developed.

### 毛主席和周总理  电贺柬埔寨国王寿辰和国庆

1957-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席和周总理
    电贺柬埔寨国王寿辰和国庆
    新华社5日讯　毛泽东主席致电祝贺柬埔寨王国国王诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特寿辰并柬埔寨王国国庆。电文如下：国王诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特陛下：
    值此陛下寿辰和柬埔寨王国国庆之际，我谨代表中国人民，并且以我个人名义，向陛下和贵国人民表示热诚的祝贺。祝中柬两国之间的友谊在和平共处五项原则的基础上更加巩固和发展，祝柬埔寨王国国家繁荣、人民兴旺，并祝陛下身体健康。
                    中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东　1957年3月5日
    新华社5日讯　周恩来总理今天分别致电柬埔寨王国国家领导人，祝贺诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特国王寿辰以及柬埔寨王国国庆。
    周恩来总理在给诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特国王和王后的贺电中说：“值此国王陛下寿辰和柬埔寨王国国庆之际，我谨向陛下表示热烈的祝贺。我愿意借着这个机会再一次向国王和王后陛下对我们在访问贵国期间所给予的亲切关怀和优遇表示诚挚的谢意。祝柬埔寨王国繁荣，祝国王和王后陛下健康长寿。”
    周恩来总理在给西哈努克亲王的贺电中，代表中国人民并且以他个人的名义向亲王殿下和柬埔寨人民表示热烈的祝贺。电文中说：
      “我们在贵国访问的时候，受到亲王殿下亲切的关怀和热情的接待，在贵国访问的情景一直留在我们美好的记忆中。
    中国人民满意地看到：通过我们两国领导人员的相互访问，我们两国间传统的友好关系已经有了新的发展。祝中柬两国的友谊在和平共处五项原则的基础上日益增进，祝殿下和柬埔寨人民在国家建设和维护亚洲和世界和平的事业中获得进一步的成就。”
    周恩来总理在给桑云首相的电文中代表中国政府，并且以他个人的名义向柬埔寨王国政府和桑云首相致热诚的祝贺。周恩来总理在电文中还祝中柬两国在和平共处五项原则的基础上建立起来的友好关系日益巩固，祝两国间的经济合作和文化交流日益发展。
