    Top Left: This is the Zemianske Kostoľany thermal power plant under construction, currently one of the largest and most modern power plants in Slovakia. Once fully completed, it will supply abundant electricity to nearby cities and factories.

    Top Right: In Slovakia, hundreds of new factories are being built to transform this agricultural region into a modern industrial area. The picture shows a panoramic view of the magnesium plant in Lubeník, Košice region, Slovakia.

    Left: On the banks of the Vltava River near Prague, construction has begun on Czechoslovakia's first atomic energy building – the Institute of Nuclear Physics. This is a corner of the institute's construction site. According to a decision made by the Czechoslovak government in early January this year, the institute's experimental reactor will begin operating within this year.
　  ČTK (New China News Agency dispatch)

### 图片

1957-03-28
第5版()
专栏：

    上左：这是正在建设中的泽免斯克·科斯托郎尼火力发电厂，是斯洛伐克目前最大的和最现代化的发电厂之一。全部建成后，将供应附近城市和工厂以丰富的电力。
    上右：在斯洛伐克，正在兴建成百座新的工厂，以便把这个农业区改变为现代化的工业区。图为斯洛伐克科息斯区鲁本尼地方的制镁厂全景。
    左图：在布拉格附近的伏尔塔瓦河畔，开始了捷克斯洛伐克第一个原子能建筑——核子物理研究所的建筑工程。这是该所建筑工地的一角。根据捷克斯洛伐克政府今年1月初的决定，这个研究所的实验用反应堆将在今年以内开始使用。
　  捷克斯洛伐克通讯社稿（新华社发）
