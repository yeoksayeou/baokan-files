Title: French Film Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-03-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, March 30th – The French film delegation invited to visit China by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the China Film Workers Association arrived in Beijing today. The delegation is led by Jacques Flaud, leader of the French Film Center, and includes members Raoul Ploquin, President of the French Film Export Association, and Robert Clavel, General Representative.

Also arriving on the same flight was French director Roger Pigaut.

Tonight, Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Wang Lanxi, Director of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture, held a banquet to welcome Flaud and others and to bid farewell to Mr. and Mrs. Philippe.

### 法国电影界代表团到达北京

1957-03-31
第5版()
专栏：

    法国电影界代表团到达北京
    据新华社30日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会和中国电影工作者联谊会邀请来我国访问的法国电影界代表团团长、法国电影中心领导人雅克·弗洛，团员、法国电影对外传播协会会长腊耳·普洛坎，总代表罗伯·克拉维内等三人今日抵北京。
    同飞机来的还有法国导演罗歇·比果。
    今晚，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南和文化部电影事业管理局局长王阑西特举行宴会，欢迎弗洛等并欢送菲利浦夫妇。
