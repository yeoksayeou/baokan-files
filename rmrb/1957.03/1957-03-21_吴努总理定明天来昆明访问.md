Title: Prime Minister U Nu Scheduled to Visit Kunming Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1957-03-21
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 20th — The Burmese government announced on the evening of the 19th that Burmese Prime Minister U Nu will visit Kunming, the capital of Yunnan Province, China, on March 22nd.
    Accompanying the Burmese Prime Minister on this visit are: Deputy Prime Minister Sao Khun Hkio, Daw Khin Kyi (wife of Aung San), and U Kyi Myint, Parliamentary Secretary of the Shan State Affairs Department.
    Chinese Ambassador to Burma, Yao Zhongming, will accompany the Burmese Prime Minister and his party to Kunming.

### 吴努总理定明天来昆明访问

1957-03-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　吴努总理定明天来昆明访问
    新华社仰光20日电　缅甸政府19日晚宣布，缅甸总理吴努将在3月22日前往中国云南省省会昆明访问。
    随同缅甸总理进行这次访问的有：藻昆卓副总理，昂山夫人（都庆枝），和掸邦事务部政务次长吴季密。
    中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明将陪同缅甸总理等一行到昆明。
