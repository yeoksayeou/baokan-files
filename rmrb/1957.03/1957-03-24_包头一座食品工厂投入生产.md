Title: A Food Factory in Baotou Goes into Production
Author:
Date: 1957-03-24
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency Report: A food factory in Baotou City, capable of producing more than one million kilograms of bread, cakes, biscuits, and candies annually, has recently been completed and put into production.

　　The majority of the workers in this factory are workers from the former Shanghai Duoduo Candy Factory, Shidai Biscuit Factory, and other food factories. They came last year to support the construction of Baotou.

### 包头一座食品工厂投入生产

1957-03-24
第2版()
专栏：

　  包头一座食品工厂投入生产
    据新华社讯　一座每年可以生产一百多万公斤面包、蛋糕、饼干、糖果的食品工厂，最近在包头市建成投入生产。
    这座工厂大多数工人是过去上海多多糖果厂、时代饼干厂等食品工厂的工人，他们是去年前来支援包头建设的。
