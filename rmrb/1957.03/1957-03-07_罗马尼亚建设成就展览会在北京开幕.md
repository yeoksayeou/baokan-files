Title: Romanian Construction Achievements Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-03-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 6th. The "Exhibition of the People's Republic of Romania Advancing on the Road of Socialist Construction" opened today at the Cultural Palace of the Working People in the capital.

　　This exhibition, which will last for a month, introduces Romania's achievements during the first Five-Year Plan (1951-1955).

　　Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan, along with ambassadors and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China, visited the exhibition accompanied by the Romanian Ambassador to China, Chioroiu.

### 罗马尼亚建设成就展览会在北京开幕

1957-03-07
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚建设成就展览会在北京开幕
    新华社6日讯　“在社会主义建设道路上前进的罗马尼亚人民共和国展览会”今天在首都劳动人民文化宫开幕。
    这个将要展出一个月的展览会，介绍了罗马尼亚在第一个五年计划（1951——1955年）中的成就。
    文化部副部长夏衍、各国驻中国的使节和外交官员在罗马尼亚驻中国大使乔洛尤陪同下参观了展览会。
