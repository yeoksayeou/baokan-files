Title: Egyptian-Syrian-Jordanian Experts Adopt Plan to Unify Curriculum and Education System of the Three Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-03-02
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 1st - Experts from Egypt, Syria, and Jordan adopted a plan on February 27 at their final meeting regarding the unification of the three countries' curriculum and education system.
    This meeting of experts began on February 16 in Cairo. The draft of the three-nation cultural agreement passed at the meeting also stipulated the promotion of cultural cooperation (including the exchange of technical personnel and experts) and the establishment of a permanent joint committee to supervise the implementation of the agreement's provisions.
    The education ministers of these three Arab nations will soon hold a meeting to sign this agreement.

### 埃叙约专家通过计划  统一三国课程和教育制度

1957-03-02
第6版()
专栏：

    埃叙约专家通过计划
    统一三国课程和教育制度
    新华社开罗1日电  埃及、叙利亚和约旦三国的专家在2月27日举行的最后一次会议上通过了关于统一三国的课程和教育制度的计划。
    这个专家会议是2月16日开始在开罗举行的。会上通过的三国文化协定草案还规定了促进文化合作 （包括交换技术人员和专家）以及设立一个常设联合委员会来监督执行这个协定的条款。
    这三个阿拉伯国家的教育部长不久将举行一次会议，以便签订这项协定。
