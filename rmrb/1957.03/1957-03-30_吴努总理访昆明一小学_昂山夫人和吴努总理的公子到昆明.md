Title: Prime Minister U Nu Visits a Primary School in Kunming; Madame Aung San and Prime Minister U Nu's Son Arrive in Kunming
Author:
Date: 1957-03-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, March 29th: Burmese Union Prime Minister U Nu paid a special visit yesterday to the primary school for children of employees of the Tenth Metallurgical Construction Company of the Ministry of Metallurgical Industry in Kunming.

　　All the students of the school and the children of the kindergarten welcomed Prime Minister U Nu by singing "Welcome Uncle U Nu" and held a variety show to welcome the distinguished guests.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, March 29th: Madame Aung San (Daw Khin Kyi) and Prime Minister U Nu's son arrived in Kunming by plane today from Chengdu.

　　Yesterday, Madame Aung San and Prime Minister U Nu's son visited Du Fu Thatched Cottage and Qingyang Palace (Flower Fair) on the outskirts of Chengdu. Last night at 7 p.m., Sichuan Provincial Governor Li Da-zhang held a banquet to welcome Madame Aung San. Premier Zhou Enlai, who arrived in Chengdu yesterday, also attended the banquet.

### 吴努总理访昆明一小学　昂山夫人和吴努总理的公子到昆明

1957-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　吴努总理访昆明一小学
　　昂山夫人和吴努总理的公子到昆明
    据新华社昆明29日电  缅甸联邦吴努总理昨天特地到冶金工业部在昆明的第十冶金建筑总公司职工子弟小学进行了访问。
    这个学校的全体学生和幼儿园的儿童们唱着“欢迎吴努伯伯”的歌子欢迎吴努总理，并举行了游艺会欢迎贵宾。
    据新华社昆明29日电  昂山夫人（都庆枝）和吴努总理的公子今天从成都乘飞机到达昆明。
    昂山夫人和吴努总理的公子昨天路过成都时曾参观了成都市郊的名胜杜甫草堂和青羊宫（花会）。昨晚七时，四川省省长李大章为欢迎昂山夫人举行了宴会。昨天到达成都的周恩来总理也出席了这次宴会。
