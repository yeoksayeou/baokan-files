Title: People of All Circles in Beijing Over-subscribe to Public Bonds
Author:
Date: 1957-03-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 25th: As of today, people from all circles in Beijing have subscribed to public bonds totaling 19,248,000 yuan, exceeding the promotion plan by 9.17%. More than 90 central government organizations have completed their subscriptions within two weeks, with subscriptions exceeding the plan by 41%. More than 100,000 members of handicraft production cooperatives over-fulfilled their subscription plan in just over ten days. The subscription activities of workers, staff, and teachers have not yet fully concluded, but the subscription figure has already exceeded the planned target by 12.64%.

Currently, suburban farmers and some factories and construction units are continuing to subscribe.

### 北京市各界人民　认购公债超过计划

1957-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京市各界人民
　　认购公债超过计划
    新华社25日讯　到今天为止，北京市各界人民认购公债已达一千九百二十四万八千多元，超过推销计划9.17％。中央机关有九十多个单位在两周时间内全部结束了认购，认购数超过计划41％。十多万手工业生产合作社社员在十多天时间内，就超额完成了认购计划。职工及公教人员的认购活动还没有全部结束，但认购数字已超过预定计划12.64％。
    目前，郊区农民和部分工厂、建筑等单位正在继续认购。
