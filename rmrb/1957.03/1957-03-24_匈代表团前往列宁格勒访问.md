Title: Hungarian Delegation Visits Leningrad
Author:
Date: 1957-03-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 23rd, TASS dispatch: The Premier of the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government and Chairman of the Provisional Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, János Kádár, the President of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, István Dobi, and other members of the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government and the Socialist Workers' Party delegation, left Moscow on the evening of the 22nd to visit Leningrad. Accompanying the delegation were Mikhail Suslov, member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPSU, and others.

During the day on the 22nd, the Hungarian government and party delegation visited the Lenin Central Luzhniki Stadium in Moscow and watched a gymnastics performance by athletes from the Moscow City Sports Games. (Attached picture)

March 21st: The Hungarian government delegation visits a Moscow aircraft factory. This is a rally held by the workers in the assembly shop to welcome the guests. TASS telephoto (Xinhua News Agency)

### 匈代表团前往列宁格勒访问

1957-03-24
第5版()
专栏：

    匈代表团前往列宁格勒访问
    新华社23日讯据塔斯社莫斯科讯：匈牙利工农革命政府总理和社会主义工人党临时中央委员会主席卡达尔、匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比以及匈牙利工农革命政府和社会主义工人党的代表团的其他团员，22日晚间离莫斯科前往列宁格勒访问。陪同代表团前往的有苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员、苏共中央书记苏斯洛夫等人。
    22日白天，匈牙利政府和党的代表团在莫斯科参观了列宁中央卢日尼基运动场，并且观看了莫斯科市运动大会的运动员们的体操表演。（附图片）
    3月21日匈牙利政府代表团参观莫斯科飞机工场，这是工人们在装配车间举行集会欢迎客人们。塔斯社传真照片（新华社发）
