Title: British Prime Minister Also Sends Congratulatory Letter
Author:
Date: 1957-03-08
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, London, 7th: British Prime Minister Macmillan sent a congratulatory letter to Ghanaian Prime Minister Nkrumah on Ghana's Independence Day.

　　On the 6th, Macmillan also specially extended congratulations to the people of Ghana on a British Broadcasting Corporation radio program.

### 英首相也致函祝贺

1957-03-08
第6版()
专栏：

    英首相也致函祝贺
    据新华社7日讯　伦敦消息：英国首相麦克米伦在加纳独立日给加纳总理恩克鲁玛写了一封贺信。
    6日，麦克米伦还特地在英国广播公司的无线电节目里向加纳人民表示祝贺。
