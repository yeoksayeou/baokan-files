Title: Soviet-Polish Agreement on Repatriation of Polish Nationals Signed
Author:
Date: 1957-03-28
Page: 5

　　Soviet-Polish Agreement on Repatriation of Polish Nationals Signed
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 26th: According to TASS, after negotiations, the Soviet and Polish governments signed an agreement in Moscow on March 25 regarding the dates and procedures for the future repatriation of Polish nationals from the Soviet Union.

    The agreement stipulates that Polish citizens who held Polish nationality before September 17, 1939, have the right to return to their country. The repatriation of Polish citizens who wish to return will continue until December 31, 1958.

### 苏波签订遣返波侨协定

1957-03-28
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏波签订遣返波侨协定
    新华社26日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联政府和波兰政府经过谈判以后，3月25日在莫斯科签订了关于今后从苏联遣送波兰人回国的日期和手续的协定。
    这个协定规定，1939年9月17日以前曾有波兰国籍的波兰人有权回国，愿意回国的波兰人的遣返工作将进行到1958年12月31日为止。
