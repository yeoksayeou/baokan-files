Title: Poland and India Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1957-03-29
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 28th. Polish Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Cyrankiewicz, and Indian Prime Minister Nehru signed a cultural cooperation agreement in New Delhi on March 27th.

　　The agreement stipulates that Poland and India will exchange visits by scientific, cultural, and artistic representatives, exchange students, and arrange cooperation between journalists' groups, news agencies, and newspapers. The two countries will organize scientific and cultural exhibitions, theatrical performances, concerts, radio broadcasts, and film screenings, as well as take other measures to develop cultural cooperation.

　　Both parties will discuss the issue of establishing cultural institutes in their respective countries.

　　The agreement also stipulates that one country will train experts for the other in scientific and technical schools. In order to supervise the implementation of the agreement, committees with subcommittees will be established in Warsaw and New Delhi, respectively.

　　The agreement is valid for five years.

### 波印签订文化合作协定

1957-03-29
第5版()
专栏：

　　波印签订文化合作协定
    新华社新德里28日电  波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹和印度总理尼赫鲁3月27日在新德里签订了一项文化合作协定。
    协定规定，波兰和印度之间将互派科学、文化和艺术代表进行访问，互派留学生，安排新闻工作者团体、通讯社和报刊之间的合作。两国将组织科学和文化展览会、戏剧表演、演奏会、无线电广播和放映电影，以及采取其他发展文化合作的措施。
    双方将讨论关于在它们的国内成立文化研究所的问题。
    协定还规定一国为另一国在科学和技术学校里培养专家。为了监督协定的履行，将分别在华沙和新德里设立附有小组委员会的委员会。
    协定的有效期为五年。
