Title: Cyrankiewicz to Visit Burma
Author:
Date: 1957-03-03
Page: 5
　
    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 2nd. The Burmese Foreign Ministry announced on the evening of the 1st that Polish Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Cyrankiewicz, will visit Burma from March 18th to 21st at the invitation of the Burmese government.
    Burmese Prime Minister U Nu visited Poland in 1955. Cyrankiewicz's visit to Burma is a return visit.

### 西伦凯维兹将访缅

1957-03-03
第5版()
专栏：

    西伦凯维兹将访缅
    新华社仰光2日电  缅甸外交部1日晚宣布：波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹应缅甸政府的邀请，将在3月18日到21日访问缅甸。
    缅甸总理吴努曾经在1955年访问过波兰，西伦凯维兹这次访问缅甸是答谢访问。
