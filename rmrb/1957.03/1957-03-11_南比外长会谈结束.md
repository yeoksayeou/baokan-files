Title: South-Belgian Foreign Ministers' Talks Conclude
Author: 
Date: 1957-03-11
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Brussels, March 10th: According to the Yugoslav News Agency, the talks between the Yugoslav State Secretary for Foreign Affairs, Popović, who is on an official visit to Brussels, and the Belgian Foreign Minister, Spaak, have concluded. A statement issued after the talks stated that the two sides "fruitfully exchanged views on a number of current international affairs issues" during the meeting.
    The statement also said: "The Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia and the Kingdom of Belgium expressed satisfaction with the development of relations between the two countries and hope to further improve and consolidate these relations."

### 南比外长会谈结束

1957-03-11
第5版()
专栏：

    南比外长会谈结束
    据新华社10日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社布鲁塞尔9日消息，到布鲁塞尔作正式访问的南斯拉夫外交国务秘书长波波维奇同比利时外交大臣斯巴克举行的会谈已经结束。会谈结束后发表的声明说，双方在会见期间，“就当前国际事务方面的若干问题富有成效地交换了意见”。
    声明还说：“南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国和比利时王国对两国之间关系的发展表示满意，希望愈益改善和巩固这种关系。”
