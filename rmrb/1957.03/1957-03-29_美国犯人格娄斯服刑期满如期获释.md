Title: American Prisoner Gross Released on Schedule After Serving Sentence
Author:
Date: 1957-03-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, March 28th. American prisoner Gross was released on schedule by our competent authorities on March 28th, after serving his full sentence.

Gross was arrested in Qingdao on March 29, 1951, for espionage activities.

### 美国犯人格娄斯服刑期满如期获释

1957-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

    美国犯人格娄斯服刑期满如期获释
    新华社上海28日电  美国犯人格娄斯在3月28日服刑期满，我主管机关已经如期将他释放。
    格娄斯是1951年3月29日在青岛因间谍活动被捕的。
