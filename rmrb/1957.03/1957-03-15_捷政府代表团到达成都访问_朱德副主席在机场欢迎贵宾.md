Title: Czechoslovak Government Delegation Arrives in Chengdu for Visit; Vice Chairman Zhu De Welcomes Distinguished Guests at Airport
Author:
Date: 1957-03-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, March 14th: The Czechoslovak Government Delegation led by Prime Minister Viliam Široký arrived in Chengdu by plane at 11:30 this morning from Xi'an. The distinguished guests were welcomed at the airport by Vice Chairman Zhu De and his wife, Governor of Sichuan Province Li Dazhang, First Secretary of the Sichuan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party Li Jingquan, and others in Chengdu.

　　Chengdu is currently full of spring, with lush greenery everywhere. People wearing colorful spring clothes carried red plum blossoms and other flowers to welcome the distinguished guests.

　　After Prime Minister Široký and others had lunch with Vice Chairman Zhu De and others, they were accompanied by Li Dazhang, Li Jingquan, and others in the afternoon to visit scenic spots and historical sites such as Du Fu Thatched Cottage, Wuhou Temple, and Wangjiang Tower.

　　In the evening, Sichuan Provincial Governor Li Dazhang held a banquet to welcome Prime Minister Široký and the Czechoslovak Government Delegation. Vice Chairman Zhu De and his wife also attended the banquet.

### 捷政府代表团到达成都访问  朱德副主席在机场欢迎贵宾

1957-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

    捷政府代表团到达成都访问
    朱德副主席在机场欢迎贵宾
    据新华社成都14日电  威廉·西罗基总理和他所率领的捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团今天上午十一时三十分从西安乘飞机到达成都。贵宾们在机场上受到了在成都的朱德副主席和夫人，四川省省长李大章、中共四川省委员会第一书记李井泉等人的欢迎。
    目前成都春意泱然，到处是一片葱绿，人们穿着五颜六色的春装，携着红梅花等鲜花来欢迎贵宾。
    西罗基总理等同朱德副主席等共进午餐后，下午由李大章、李井泉等陪同游览了杜甫草堂、武侯祠和望江楼等名胜古迹。
    晚间，四川省省长李大章举行宴会，欢迎西罗基总理和捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团。朱德副主席和夫人也出席了宴会。
