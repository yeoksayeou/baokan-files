Title: Celebrating the Danish King's Birthday: Danish Ambassador to China Holds Reception
Author:
Date: 1957-03-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 11th: The Danish Ambassador to China, Mr. গ্রেggeসেন, held a reception tonight to celebrate the birthday of His Majesty King Frederick IX of Denmark.

    Premier Zhou Enlai attended the reception. He represented the Chinese government and people in congratulating King Frederick IX of Denmark on his health and longevity. He successively proposed toasts for the prosperity of the Kingdom of Denmark and the happiness of the Danish people, for the friendship between China and Denmark, for the health of King Frederick IX of Denmark, and for the health of Danish Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Hansen.

    Ambassador গ্রেggeসেন also successively proposed toasts for the prosperity of China and the happiness of its people, for the health of Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, and for the health of Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of the State Council.

    Among those present at the reception tonight were Shen Junru, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; He Long, Vice Premier of the State Council; Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General; Zhang Bojun, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; and other responsible persons of relevant ministries, as well as people who had visited Denmark and people from all walks of life in Beijing.

    Heads of diplomatic missions and economic delegations of various countries in China were also invited to attend.

### 庆祝丹麦国王诞辰  丹麦驻华大使举行招待会

1957-03-12
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝丹麦国王诞辰
    丹麦驻华大使举行招待会
    新华社11日讯　丹麦驻中国大使格瑞杰生今晚举行招待会，庆祝丹麦国王斐德烈九世陛下诞辰。
    周恩来总理出席了招待会。他代表中国政府和人民祝贺丹麦国王斐德烈九世健康长寿。他先后提议为丹麦王国的繁荣和丹麦人民的幸福，为中丹两国的友好，为丹麦国王斐德烈九世和为丹麦王国首相兼外交大臣汉森的健康干杯。
    格瑞杰生大使也先后提议为中国的繁荣和人民的幸福，为中华人民共和国主席毛泽东和为国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来的健康干杯。
    出席今晚招待会的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长沈钧儒，国务院副总理贺龙，秘书长习仲勋，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧，外交部副部长章汉夫和其他有关各部负责人，以及曾经访问过丹麦的人士和北京各界人士。
    各国驻华使节和经济代表团负责人也应邀出席。
