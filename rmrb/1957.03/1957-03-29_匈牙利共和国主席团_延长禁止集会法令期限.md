Title: Hungarian Presidential Council Extends Decree Banning Gatherings

Author:
Date: 1957-03-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, March 28th: According to "People's Freedom," the Presidential Council of the Hungarian People's Republic has decided to extend the validity period of the special decree issued last year, which prohibits marches and gatherings without government permission, to the end of June 1957.

The Hungarian government has also instructed all schools to have a one-day holiday on April 4th to commemorate the 12th anniversary of the liberation of Hungary. After class on April 3rd, commemorative activities can be held by class.

### 匈牙利共和国主席团  延长禁止集会法令期限

1957-03-29
第5版()
专栏：

　  匈牙利共和国主席团
    延长禁止集会法令期限
    新华社布达佩斯28日电  据“人民自由报”报道，匈牙利人民共和国主席团决定把去年颁布的关于非经政府许可禁止游行和集会的特别法令的有效期间，延长到1957年6月底。
    匈牙利政府还指示各学校，在4月4日匈牙利解放十二周年纪念日放假一天。在4月3日课后，可以班级为单位举行纪念活动。
