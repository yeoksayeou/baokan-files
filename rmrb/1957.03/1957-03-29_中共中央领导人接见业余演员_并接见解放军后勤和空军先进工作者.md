Title: Central Committee Leaders of the Chinese Communist Party Receive Amateur Performers, Logistics Personnel of the People's Liberation Army, and Air Force Advanced Workers

Author:
Date: 1957-03-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 27th:  中共中央领导人周恩来、朱德、董必武、彭真、康生、薄一波、聂荣臻，在今天下午接见了参加第二届全国民间音乐舞蹈会演的包括二十八个民族的一千多位业余演员。
The CCP Central Committee leaders Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Dong Biwu, Peng Zhen, Kang Sheng, Bo Yibo, and Nie Rongzhen, received over a thousand amateur performers of twenty-eight nationalities who participated in the Second National Folk Music and Dance Festival this afternoon.

　　在接见的时候，藏族演员代表向周恩来、朱德等同志献了哈达，周恩来和朱德等同志回敬了哈达。
During the reception, representatives of the Tibetan performers presented Hada scarves to Comrades Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, etc., and Comrades Zhou Enlai and Zhu De returned the Hada scarves.

　　新华社27日讯  中共中央领导人周恩来、朱德、董必武、彭真、康生、薄一波、聂荣臻、粟裕，在今天下午接见了出席中国人民解放军后勤先进工作者代表会议的全体代表。
Xinhua News Agency, March 27th: The CCP Central Committee leaders Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Dong Biwu, Peng Zhen, Kang Sheng, Bo Yibo, Nie Rongzhen, and Su Yu, received all the representatives attending the Representative Conference of Advanced Workers in the Logistics of the Chinese People's Liberation Army this afternoon.

　　新华社27日讯  中共中央领导人朱德、董必武、彭真、康生、薄一波、聂荣臻、粟裕，在今天下午接见了出席中国人民解放军空军直属机关先进工作者大会的全体代表。
Xinhua News Agency, March 27th: The CCP Central Committee leaders Zhu De, Dong Biwu, Peng Zhen, Kang Sheng, Bo Yibo, Nie Rongzhen, and Su Yu, received all the representatives attending the Congress of Advanced Workers of the Directly Subordinate Organs of the Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army this afternoon.
