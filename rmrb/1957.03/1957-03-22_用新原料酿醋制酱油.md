Title: Brewing Vinegar and Making Soy Sauce with New Raw Materials
Author:
Date: 1957-03-22
Page: 6
 　　
　　Recently, potato and sesame cake vinegar and soy sauce have appeared in the market of Jining City, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, and the taste is even better than those made from millet and soybeans. The Jining City Brewing Industry Production Cooperative, which first adopted this new substitute, has saved 210,000 catties of millet and soybeans.

### 用新原料酿醋制酱油

1957-03-22
第6版()
专栏：
 　 用新原料酿醋制酱油
    内蒙古自治区集宁市的市场上最近出现了一种用土豆、麻饼做的醋和酱油，味道比用小米和黄豆做成的还好。首先采用这种新的代用品的集宁市酿造业生产合作社，已经节约了二十一万斤小米、黄豆。
