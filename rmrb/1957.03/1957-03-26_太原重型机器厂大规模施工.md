Title: Large-Scale Construction at Taiyuan Heavy Machinery Plant
Author: Ma Ming
Date: 1957-03-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency reporter Ma Ming reports: Workers constructing the Taiyuan Heavy Machinery Plant are seizing the favorable season and working hard. At the construction sites of the steel casting workshop and four other major production workshops, one can see complex steel frames, factory building columns, and various large components ready for installation everywhere.

Since construction began in 1950, this year marks a year of large-scale progress in the plant's building construction. This year, the civil engineering of the steel casting workshop, hydraulic press workshop, third metalworking workshop, and second heat treatment workshop will be basically completed, and some machinery and equipment will be installed.

The large-scale gas station, with fifteen gas generators produced by the plant itself, is already installed. Two open-hearth furnaces produced by the plant are also being installed in the steel casting workshop recently. (Attached picture)

### 太原重型机器厂大规模施工
马明
1957-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　太原重型机器厂大规模施工
    据新华社记者马明报道：建设太原重型机器厂的工人们正在抓紧施工的有利季节紧张劳动。在铸钢车间等四个主要生产车间的施工现场，到处可以看到竖立起来的结构复杂的钢房架、厂房柱子和准备待装的各种大型构件。
    这个工厂从1950年开工以来，今年是厂房建筑工程大规模进行的一年。今年内将有铸钢车间、水压机车间、第三金工车间和第二热处理车间基本完成土建工程，并且安装部分机器设备。
    规模宏大的煤气站有十五台本厂生产的煤气炉，已经安装就绪。本厂生产的两台平炉，最近也正在铸钢车间里安装。（附图片）
