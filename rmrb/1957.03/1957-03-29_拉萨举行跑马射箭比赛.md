Title: Lhasa Holds Horse Racing and Archery Competition
Author:
Date: 1957-03-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 28th -- Yesterday, the annual horse racing and archery competition was held on the vast meadow behind the Potala Palace in Lhasa. More than three hundred farmers dressed as ancient knights participated in the competition. These knights, wearing armor and riding fast horses, with gunpowder guns and bows and arrows on their backs, one after another, shot at two targets only about thirty meters apart on the side of the road with gunpowder guns and bows and arrows while galloping on horseback. Some old knights, using skillful and rapid movements, hit both targets in a row, winning applause and cheers from the audience.
　　
About three or four thousand spectators attended yesterday's competition. Most of the officials of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region and the Tibetan local government watched the competition here. Leading cadres and officers at all levels of the Tibet Working Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army were also invited to visit the site. The meadow was filled with colorful tents, and many citizens brought food and played here with their families.

### 拉萨举行跑马射箭比赛

1957-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　拉萨举行跑马射箭比赛
    新华社拉萨28日电  在拉萨布达拉宫背后的广阔草滩上，昨天举行了一年一度的跑马射箭比赛。有三百多个农民化装成的古装骑士参加竞赛。这些穿戴着盔甲的骑士，骑着快马，背着火药枪和弓箭，一个接一个地，在疾驰着的马上用火药枪和弓箭，向路旁相距仅约三十公尺的两个靶的射击。有些老骑士运用熟练、迅速的动作，连中两靶，赢得了观众的喝采和掌声。
    在昨天的比赛中，约有三四千观众。西藏自治区筹备委员会和西藏地方政府的官员，多数在这里观看比赛。中共西藏工委、中国人民解放军西藏军区的各级负责干部和军官，也应邀到场参观。草滩上到处是彩色的帐篷，许多市民携带着食品，全家在这里游玩。
