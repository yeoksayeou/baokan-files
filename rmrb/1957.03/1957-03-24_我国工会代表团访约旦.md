Title: Chinese Trade Union Delegation Visits Jordan
Author:
Date: 1957-03-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 23rd: The Chinese trade union delegation left here by plane today for Amman.

The Chinese trade union delegation, which is visiting Arab countries at the invitation of the International Confederation of Arab Trade Unions, will stay in Jordan for two days.

### 我国工会代表团访约旦

1957-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

　  我国工会代表团访约旦
    新华社开罗23日电　中国工会代表团今日乘飞机离此前往安曼。
    应阿拉伯工会国际联合会邀请正在访问阿拉伯国家的中国工会代表团将在约旦作两天的逗留。
