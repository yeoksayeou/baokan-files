Title: Soviet-Polish Treaty Signed on Border Section Adjacent to the Baltic Sea
Author:
Date: 1957-03-07
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 6th. TASS reports: After several days of talks in Moscow, the Soviet and Polish delegations signed a treaty on March 5th regarding the demarcation of the Soviet-Polish border line in the section adjacent to the Baltic Sea. The treaty stipulates that the existing border between the Soviet Union and Poland in the section adjacent to the Baltic Sea, as defined by the 1945 Berlin Conference, will be demarcated on the ground in 1957. The treaty will come into effect from the date of exchange of instruments of ratification. Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko signed the treaty on behalf of the Soviet side; Polish Foreign Minister Rapacki signed on behalf of the Polish side.

### 苏波签订波罗的海邻近地段国界条约

1957-03-07
第5版()
专栏：

    苏波签订波罗的海邻近地段国界条约
    新华社6日讯　据塔斯社报道：苏联代表团和波兰代表团在莫斯科经过几天会谈后，5日签订了关于苏波两国波罗的海邻近地段国境线定界条约。条约规定，1945年柏林会议规定的、苏联和波兰在波罗的海邻近地段现有的国界将于1957年就地划定。条约从交换批准证书之日起开始生效。代表苏联方面在条约上签字的是苏联外交部长葛罗米柯；代表波兰方面签字的是波兰外交部长拉帕茨基。
