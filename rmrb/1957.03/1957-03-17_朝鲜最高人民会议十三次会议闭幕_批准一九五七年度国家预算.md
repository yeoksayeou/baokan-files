Title: The Thirteenth Session of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea Closes: Approves the 1957 National Budget
Author: 
Date: 1957-03-17
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 16th - The Thirteenth Session of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea closed today.

    At today's meeting, all the deputies unanimously approved the 1957 national budget and the 1955 national budget settlement proposed by the North Korean Cabinet. At yesterday's and today's meetings, fifteen deputies expressed their opinions. They unanimously supported the 1957 national budget. The deputies said that in this budget, expenditures on the people's economy and social culture accounted for the vast majority of total expenditures, and that this budget was a budget for peace. The implementation of this budget will further develop the Republic's industry and agriculture, and further improve the people's lives.

### 朝鲜最高人民会议十三次会议闭幕  批准一九五七年度国家预算

1957-03-17
第7版()
专栏：

    朝鲜最高人民会议十三次会议闭幕
    批准一九五七年度国家预算
    新华社平壤16日电　朝鲜最高人民会议第十三次会议今天闭幕。
    在今天的会议上，全体议员一致通过了朝鲜内阁提出的1957年度国家预算和1955年度国家预算的结算。在昨天和今天的会议上，有十五位议员发表了意见。他们一致支持1957年度的国家预算，议员们说，在这个预算中，人民经济和社会文化方面的支出占了支出总额的绝大部分，这个预算是和平的预算；执行这个预算的结果将使共和国的工业和农业进一步发展，人民生活进一步提高。
