Title: "Lokomotiv" to Play Team China One Today; Central Broadcasting Station to Broadcast the Game

Author:

Date: 1957-03-22
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 21st. The Soviet "Lokomotiv" Voluntary Sports Association football team will play a friendly match against the Chinese National Team One football team on the afternoon of the 22nd. The Central People's Broadcasting Station has decided to broadcast the entire match live, starting at 2:30 p.m. that day, using the frequency of its Channel One program.

### “火车头”今战联一队　中央广播电台将转播战况

1957-03-22
第7版()
专栏：

　　“火车头”今战联一队　中央广播电台将转播战况
    新华社21日讯　苏联“火车头”志愿体育协会足球队22日下午将同我国联队一队足球队进行友谊比赛。中央人民广播电台决定从当天下午两点三十分开始，用第一种节目的频率，转播这场比赛的全部实况。
