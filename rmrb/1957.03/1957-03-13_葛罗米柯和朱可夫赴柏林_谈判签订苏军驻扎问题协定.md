Title: Gromyko and Zhukov Go to Berlin to Negotiate and Sign Agreement on the Stationing of Soviet Troops

Author:

Date: 1957-03-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 12th: TASS reports that Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko and Defense Minister Zhukov flew to Berlin on March 12th to negotiate with the government of the German Democratic Republic on signing an agreement regarding the stationing of Soviet troops in the German Democratic Republic.

### 葛罗米柯和朱可夫赴柏林  谈判签订苏军驻扎问题协定

1957-03-13
第6版()
专栏：

    葛罗米柯和朱可夫赴柏林
    谈判签订苏军驻扎问题协定
    新华社莫斯科12日电　据塔斯社报道，苏联外交部长葛罗米柯和国防部长朱可夫3月12日乘飞机赴柏林，将同德意志民主共和国政府就签定一项关于苏军驻扎民主德国问题的协定进行谈判。
