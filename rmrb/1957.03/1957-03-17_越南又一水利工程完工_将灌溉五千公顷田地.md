Title: Another Water Conservancy Project Completed in Vietnam, Irrigating 5,000 Hectares of Land
Author:
Date: 1957-03-17
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 16th. The Lam Thao and Ha Mao water conservancy irrigation projects in Phu Tho Province, Vietnam, have been fully completed.

This project is one of the many important water conservancy projects in northern Vietnam. Construction began in July last year and includes a 30-kilometer-long canal and eleven 15-kilometer-long irrigation canals. An electric pumping station built near the Red River will inject river water into this irrigation system.

The Lam Thao water conservancy irrigation system will irrigate 5,000 hectares of land. It will enable this area to plant two rice crops, which is estimated to increase grain production by 5,000 tons per year.

### 越南又一水利工程完工  将灌溉五千公顷田地

1957-03-17
第7版()
专栏：

    越南又一水利工程完工
    将灌溉五千公顷田地
    据新华社河内16日电　越南富寿省临洮、河貌水利灌溉工程已经全部完工。
    这个工程是越南北方许多重要水利工程中的一个，去年7月开始修建，包括一条长三十公里的运河和长十五公里的十一条灌溉渠。靠近红河修建的一个电力抽水站将河水注入这个灌溉系统。
    临洮水利灌溉系统将灌溉五千公顷的田地。它使这一地区可以种上两季水稻，估计每年可以增产五千吨谷子。
