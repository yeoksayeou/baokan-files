Author: Gao Guoquan
Date: 1957-03-09
Page: 5

Annual Iron Production Can Be Increased by Ten Thousand Tons

    Benxi Iron and Steel Plant No. 1 is implementing various production increase measures, and it is estimated that it can produce an additional 10,000 tons of iron annually, and can save a large amount of raw coal and coke.
    Due to the implementation of strengthening and improving the density of briquettes and the furnace charging method in the briquetting workshop, more than 2,000 tons of briquettes were produced from January to late February. This is a large amount of briquettes being transported to the blast furnace.

                              Xinhua News Agency reporter Gao Guoquan

### 全年可增产万吨铁（图片）
高国权
1957-03-09
第5版()
专栏：

    全年可增产万吨铁
    本溪炼铁一厂正在推行各项增产措施，估计全年可多产万吨铁，并可节约大量原煤焦炭。
    团矿工段由于实行了加强改进团矿密度和装炉办法。从1月到2月下旬，共增产二千多吨团矿。这是大量团矿运往高炉。
                              新华社记者　高国权摄
