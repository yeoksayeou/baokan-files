Title: Sukarno Arrives in Medan
Author:
Date: 1957-03-17
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 16th: President Sukarno arrived in Medan, the capital of North Sumatra province, by plane at noon today. He will attend the opening ceremony of the Second Asian Christian Leaders Conference held near Lake Toba in Medan. Army Chief of Staff Nasution and all officers attending the All-Indonesia Military Region Commanders Conference went to the airport to see him off. Lieutenant Colonel Kinting, acting commander of the First Military Region of North Sumatra, flew to Medan with the President.

Before leaving Jakarta, the President convened a joint meeting of the military region commanders and the secretaries-general of the central government ministries at the State Palace. According to Antara News Agency, the President spoke at the meeting about the implementation of the military martial law order. He asked the military and political sides to cooperate well with each other according to the existing top-down system.

### 苏加诺到达棉兰

1957-03-17
第7版()
专栏：

    苏加诺到达棉兰
    据新华社雅加达16日电　苏加诺总统今天中午坐飞机到达北苏门答腊省省会棉兰。他将出席在棉兰附近多巴湖边召开的第二届亚洲基督教领袖会议的开幕式。陆军参谋长纳苏蒂安和所有出席全印度尼西亚军区司令会议的军官都到机场送行。北苏门答腊第一军区代理司令京廷中校一起坐飞机去棉兰。
    总统在离开雅加达以前在国家宫召集了各军区司令和中央政府各部秘书长的联席会议。据安塔拉通讯社报道，总统在会上讲到执行军事戒严令的问题。他要求军政双方按现有自上而下的系统很好地互相配合。
