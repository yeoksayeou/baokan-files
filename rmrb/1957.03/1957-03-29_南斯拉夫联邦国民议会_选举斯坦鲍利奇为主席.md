Title: Yugoslav Federal National Assembly Elects Stambolić as Chairman
Author:
Date: 1957-03-29
Page: 5

　　Yugoslav Federal National Assembly
　　Elects Stambolić as Chairman
    Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, 27th — The Yugoslav Federal National Assembly unanimously elected Petar Stambolić as Chairman of the Federal National Assembly at a joint session of the two houses on the morning of the 26th.
    Stambolić is a member of the Executive Committee of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia and Chairman of the Serbian National Assembly.

### 南斯拉夫联邦国民议会　选举斯坦鲍利奇为主席

1957-03-29
第5版()
专栏：

　　南斯拉夫联邦国民议会
　　选举斯坦鲍利奇为主席
    新华社贝尔格莱德27日电  南斯拉夫联邦国民议会在26日上午两院联席会议上一致选举彼得·斯坦鲍利奇为联邦国民议会主席。
    斯坦鲍利奇是南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员会执行委员会委员、塞尔维亚国民议会主席。
