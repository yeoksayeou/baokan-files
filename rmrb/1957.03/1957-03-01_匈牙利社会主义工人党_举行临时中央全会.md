Title: Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party Holds Interim Central Plenary Session
Author:
Date: 1957-03-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 28th - "People's Liberty Daily" in Hungary published today the news of the interim Central Committee plenary session of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party held on the 26th.

The news said that the interim Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, based on the reports by comrades Kádár and Kish, discussed the following issues: 1. Current issues and tasks; 2. Organizational work and personnel matters. During the discussion, seventeen comrades spoke in addition to the speakers.

The interim Central Committee, according to the agenda, adopted the resolution on strengthening the Party's leadership and several other resolutions. At this meeting, fourteen interim Central Committee members and two Executive Committee members were also elected. A five-person secretariat with certain powers was established under the Executive Committee, and a five-person Central Supervisory Committee was established.

The Secretariat consists of the following five people: János Kádár, Fock Jenő, Kállai Gyula, Kiss Károly, Marosán György.

### 匈牙利社会主义工人党  举行临时中央全会

1957-03-01
第5版()
专栏：

    匈牙利社会主义工人党
    举行临时中央全会
    新华社布达佩斯28日电  匈牙利“人民自由报”今天发表了匈牙利社会主义工人党26日举行的临时中央委员会全体会议的消息。
    消息说，匈牙利社会主义工人党临时中央委员会根据卡达尔和基什同志的报告讨论了下列问题：一、目前的问题和任务；二、组织工作和人选问题。在讨论中，除报告人外，有十七位同志发了言。
    临时中央委员会按照议事日程，通过了关于加强党的领导的决议和其他几项决议。在这次会议上，还补选了十四位临时中央委员会委员和两位执行委员会委员，在执行委员会下设立了具有一定权限的五人书记处，设立了由五人组成的中央监察委员会。
    书记处由以下五人组成：亚诺什·卡达尔，福斯克·扬洛，卡拉伊·居拉，基什·卡罗伊，马罗山·捷尔吉。
