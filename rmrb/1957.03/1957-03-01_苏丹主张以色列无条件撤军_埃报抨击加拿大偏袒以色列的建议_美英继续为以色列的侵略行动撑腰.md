Title: Sudan Insists on Unconditional Israeli Withdrawal; Egyptian Press Criticizes Canada's Pro-Israeli Proposal; US and Britain Continue to Support Israeli Aggression

Author: 
Date: 1957-03-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 28th - London dispatch: Sudanese Foreign Minister Mahgoub said at London Airport on the 27th that Israel's withdrawal from Egyptian territory must be unconditional.

He said: "Any attempt to impose conditions on the withdrawal will not improve the situation, but will only make it worse."

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 28th: "Al-Gomhouria" said in a 27th editorial that Canada's proposal to the UN General Assembly on the 26th "is almost a replica of the demands that Israel is now insisting on as a prerequisite for withdrawal."

"Al Shaab" said that the purpose of Canada's proposal is to make it possible for Israel to be rewarded for its aggression before withdrawing from Gaza and the Gulf of Aqaba. Another purpose is to resolve the Palestine issue in favor of Israel.

"Al Ahram" said that Israel's repeated demands for conditions for withdrawal and "guarantees" before withdrawal are encouraged by the United States' willingness to consider such conditions and "guarantees." The United States' intention to avoid imposing sanctions on Israel has also encouraged Israel in the same way.

Xinhua News Agency, 28th - New York dispatch: The UN General Assembly meeting scheduled for the 27th to discuss the issue of Israeli withdrawal was postponed. A US spokesman to the United Nations said on the 27th that the United States would submit a proposal to the United Nations advocating that Israeli troops withdraw from the areas they occupy, and that UN forces occupy these areas. At the same time, the proposal would also mention the issue of "freedom of navigation" in the Gulf of Aqaba. The US spokesman made it clear that the US proposal was intended to replace proposals to sanction Israel.

Xinhua News Agency, 28th - London dispatch: British Foreign Secretary Lloyd reiterated in the House of Commons on the 27th that the British government's position is that Israeli withdrawal must be conditional on "resolving the Gaza Strip and the Gulf of Aqaba issues."

Lloyd said that the United Nations should pass a resolution that not only stipulates the withdrawal of Israeli troops, but also stipulates freedom of navigation in the Gulf of Aqaba and the administration of the Gaza area.

### 苏丹主张以色列无条件撤军  埃报抨击加拿大偏袒以色列的建议  美英继续为以色列的侵略行动撑腰

1957-03-01
第6版()
专栏：

    苏丹主张以色列无条件撤军
    埃报抨击加拿大偏袒以色列的建议  美英继续为以色列的侵略行动撑腰
    据新华社28日讯  伦敦消息：苏丹外交部长马哈古卜27日在伦敦飞机场上说，以色列从埃及领土上撤军必须是无条件的。
    他说：“任何强加撤军条件的企图都不会使局势有所改善，而只会使局势变得更加严重。”
    据新华社开罗28日电 “共和国报”27日社论说，加拿大26日向联合国大会提出的建议“几乎正是以色列现在正坚持要作为撤军先决条件的那些要求的复制品”。
   “人民报”说，加拿大的建议的目的是使以色列有可能在撤出加沙和亚喀巴湾以前由于它的侵略行动而得到酬劳。另一个目的是使巴勒斯坦问题在有利于以色列的情况下得到解决。
   “金字塔报”说，以色列一再提出撤军的条件和在撤军以前的“保证”是受到美国愿意考虑这种条件和“保证”的鼓励的。美国企图避免对以色列实行制裁的打算也同样地鼓励了以色列。
    据新华社28日讯  纽约消息：原订在27日举行的讨论以色列撤军问题的联合国大会全体会议延期举行。美国驻联合国发言人27日说，美国将在联合国提出一个提案，这个提案主张以色列军队撤出它占领的地区，而由联合国部队占领这些地区。同时，提案中还要提到亚喀巴湾“自由航行”的问题。美国发言人表明，美国这个提案是要用来代替制裁以色列的提案的。
    据新华社28日讯  伦敦消息：英国外交大臣劳埃德27日在下院重申英国政府的态度是，以色列撤军必须以“解决加沙地带和亚喀巴湾问题”为条件。
    劳埃德说，联合国应该通过一项决议，这项决议不仅规定以色列部队的撤退，也规定在亚喀巴湾的通航自由和对加沙地区的管理。
