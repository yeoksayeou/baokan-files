Title: Cadres Transferred to Work Deeply in Rural Areas in Wenchuan and Other Counties of Aba Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture
Author:
Date: 1957-03-26
Page: 4
　
　　Aba Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture, Wenchuan and Other Counties
　  Cadres Transferred to Work Deeply in Rural Areas

    Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, March 21st:  Recently, in the Aba Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture of Sichuan Province, in Maerkang, Wenchuan, Li, and Mao Counties, more than 310 cadres have been transferred to work at the grassroots level. Some county-level and section-level cadres will respectively serve as secretaries of the CCP district committees, secretaries of township branches, district heads, and township heads, residing in rural areas for extended periods to lead farmers in production.

    In addition, the CCP Wenchuan County Committee has also decided that county committee members will spend at least three to four months each year delving into townships and agricultural cooperatives to guide production and work. Leading cadres from various government departments will also spend four to six months each year immersed in rural areas and grassroots units, understanding the situation and assisting in resolving specific problems.

### 阿坝藏族自治州汶川等县  抽调干部深入农村工作

1957-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　阿坝藏族自治州汶川等县
　  抽调干部深入农村工作
    新华社成都21日电　四川省阿坝藏族自治州马尔康、汶川、理县和茂县，最近抽调三百一十多名干部，深入基层工作。有些县级和科级干部将分别担任中共区委书记、乡支部书记、区长和乡长等职务，长期住在农村，领导农民生产。
    此外，中共汶川县委会还决定，县委委员每年至少用三、四个月时间，深入乡和农业社，指导生产和工作，政府各部门负责干部，每年也将用四个月到半年的时间深入农村和基层单位，了解情况，帮助下面解决具体问题。
