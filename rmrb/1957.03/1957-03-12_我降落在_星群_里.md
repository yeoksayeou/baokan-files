Title: I Landed in a "Galaxy"
Author: Gobi Zhou
Date: 1957-03-12
Page: 8

I landed in a "Galaxy"
Gobi Zhou
　　I was flying in the high sky,
　　The setting sun ignited the boundless white clouds,
　　How many golden stars emerged in the sky,
　　How many bright lights glittered on the ground,
　　I landed in a "Galaxy,"
　　Landed in the city of Tashkent.
　　The shady road of towering white poplars,
　　The colorful buildings in the depths of the shade,
　　Like those huge gems,
　　Like those towering flower trees,
　　I flew from the ice and snow,
　　Here the cotton still welcomes the wind and dew.
　　The sunlight shines on the lingering green leaves,
　　In winter, the spring thunder resounded in my heart,
　　I saw the high mountains of cotton,
　　I saw the flowing water of cotton yarn,
　　I saw the long rainbow of printed cloth,
　　I saw the magnificent power of the people of Tashkent:
　　Before, this was arid land,
　　From your hands it received sweet rain;
　　Before, this was barren land,
　　From your hands white gold was born;
　　Your hands create endless happiness,
　　Your hands hold the fate of the future.
　　1956, 12, 23, on the Black Coast

### 我降落在“星群”里
戈壁舟
1957-03-12
第8版()
专栏：

    我降落在“星群”里
    戈壁舟
    我在高空飞行，
    夕阳点燃了无边的白云，
    天上冒出了多少金星，
    地上闪出了多少明灯，
    我降落在“星群”里，
    降落在塔什干城。
    冲天白杨的林荫路，
    五色楼房在林荫深处，
    像那巨大的宝石，
    像那耸天的花树，
    我从冰雪中飞来，
    这里的棉花还迎着风露。
    阳光照着迟迟未落的绿叶，
    冬天里我心中响起了春雷，
    我看见了棉花的高山，
    我看见了棉纱的流水，
    我看见了花布的长虹，
    我看见了塔什干人民力量的雄伟：
    从前这里是干涸的土地，
    从你们手中得到甘霖；
    从前这里是贫瘠的土地，
    从你们手中生出白金；
    你们的手创造出无穷的幸福，
    你们的手掌握着未来的命运。
      1956年、12、23，在黑海岸
