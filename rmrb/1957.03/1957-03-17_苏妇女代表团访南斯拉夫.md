Title: Soviet Women's Delegation Visits Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1957-03-17
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, March 16th: A Soviet women's delegation arrived in Belgrade on the morning of the 15th. The delegation is led by Popova, Chairman of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee and Secretary of the Central Council of Soviet Trade Unions. The delegation will be visiting Yugoslavia for a period of two weeks.

### 苏妇女代表团访南斯拉夫

1957-03-17
第7版()
专栏：

    苏妇女代表团访南斯拉夫
    新华社贝尔格莱德16日电　苏联妇女代表团15日上午到达贝尔格莱德。代表团由苏联妇女反法西斯委员会主席、苏联工会中央理事会书记波波娃率领。代表团将在南斯拉夫作为时两星期的访问。
