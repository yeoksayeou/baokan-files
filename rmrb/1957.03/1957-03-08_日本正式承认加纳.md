Title: Japan Formally Recognizes Ghana
Author:
Date: 1957-03-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, March 6th: The Japanese government announced that Japan today formally recognized the new independent African nation of Ghana.

The documents of Japan's recognition of Ghana were delivered to the Ghanaian government today by Kojima Tetsuzo (Liberal Democratic Party member of the House of Representatives), the Japanese special envoy currently in Accra, the capital of Ghana, attending the celebration of Ghana's independence.

### 日本正式承认加纳

1957-03-08
第6版()
专栏：

    日本正式承认加纳
    新华社6日讯　东京消息：日本政府宣布，日本今天正式承认非洲新的独立国加纳。
    日本承认加纳的文件已经在今天由目前正在加纳首都阿克拉参加庆祝加纳独立典礼的日本特使小岛彻三（自由民主党众议员）送交加纳政府。
