Xinhua News Agency: Urumqi expands Weihu Liang Power Plant

According to Xinhua News Agency, the Weihu Liang Power Plant in Urumqi began expansion construction in August 1956 to address the electricity shortage caused by industrial and urban development. After the expansion, the total power generation will be 25,000 kilowatts. (Picture attached)

### 乌鲁木齐扩建苇湖梁发电厂

1957-03-24
第2版()
专栏：

　　乌鲁木齐扩建苇湖梁发电厂
    据新华社讯　乌鲁木齐市苇湖梁发电厂为了改变工业和城市发展造成的电量供不应求的情况，从1956年8月开始扩建工程。扩建后总发电量为二万五千瓩。（附图片）
