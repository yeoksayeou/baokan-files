Title: CPPCC National Committee Plenary Session Closes: Approves Political Resolution, Resolution on the Work Report of the Standing Committee, Resolution on Increasing Production and Practicing Economy, Resolution on the Examination of Proposals, and List of Newly Elected Members of the Standing Committee
Author: 
Date: 1957-03-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 20th: The Third Plenary Session of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference closed this afternoon.

　　The afternoon session began at 4:15 PM.

　　The meeting first adopted the political resolution of the Third Plenary Session of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC. The political resolution pointed out that the meeting agreed with Chairman Mao Zedong's report on the correct handling of contradictions among the people, which was made at the enlarged Supreme State Conference, and agreed with Premier Zhou Enlai's report on his visit to eleven countries in Asia and Europe. The meeting believes that this visit by the Chinese government delegation has strengthened the unity of the socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union and promoted the friendship between our country and the nationalist countries of Asia, which is of great significance for maintaining world peace and the cause of human progress. The resolution called on the people of all nationalities across the country, all democratic parties, all people's organizations, overseas Chinese, and all patriotic people to unite more closely, continue to consolidate and expand the people's democratic united front and the international peace united front, and strive to build the motherland, liberate Taiwan, and defend world peace.

　　The meeting then adopted the resolution on the work report of the Standing Committee. The resolution pointed out that the main task of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference at present is to further strengthen the great unity of the people of the whole country, expand democratic life, and mobilize all positive factors, in accordance with the principle of "long-term coexistence and mutual supervision," and strive to build a great socialist industrial country.

　　The meeting adopted the resolution on increasing production and practicing economy. The resolution pointed out that increasing production and practicing economy should be regarded as a regular political task; increasing production and practicing economy should implement the policy of "overall planning and appropriate arrangements." The resolution called on the people of the whole country to unite closely, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and the People's Government, to vigorously carry out the movement to increase production and practice economy, oppose waste, and strive to successfully complete the 1957 national economic construction plan and prepare a good foundation for the Second Five-Year Plan.

　　The meeting heard the report of the Proposals Examination Committee on the examination of proposals and adopted the resolution on the examination of proposals.

　　The meeting also adopted the list of newly elected members of the Standing Committee.

　　At 5:10 PM, the meeting closed amidst warm applause.

### 政协全国委员会全体会议闭幕　通过政治决议、关于常委会工作报告的决议、关于增产节约问题的决议、关于提案审查的决议和增选常务委员名单

1957-03-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　政协全国委员会全体会议闭幕
    通过政治决议、关于常委会工作报告的决议、关于增产节约问题的决议、关于提案审查的决议和增选常务委员名单
    新华社20日讯　中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第三次全体会议今天下午闭幕。
    下午会议在四时十五分开始。
    会议首先通过了政协第二届全国委员会第三次全体会议的政治决议。政治决议指出，会议同意毛泽东主席在扩大的最高国务会议上所作的关于正确处理人民内部矛盾问题的报告，同意周恩来总理关于访问亚洲和欧洲十一国的报告。会议认为我国政府代表团的这次访问，加强了以苏联为首的社会主义国家的团结，增进了我国和亚洲各民族主义国家的友谊，这对于维护世界和平和人类进步事业具有重大的意义。决议号召全国各民族人民、各民主党派、各人民团体、国外华侨和一切爱国人士，更加紧密地团结起来，继续巩固和扩大人民民主统一战线和国际和平统一战线，为建设祖国、解放台湾、保卫世界和平而努力奋斗。
    会议接着通过了关于常务委员会工作报告的决议。决议指出，中国人民政治协商会议当前的主要任务，是根据“长期共存、互相监督”的方针，进一步加强全国人民大团结，扩大民主生活，调动一切积极因素，为建成一个伟大的社会主义工业国而奋斗。
    会议通过了关于增产节约问题的决议。决议指出，应该把增产节约当作一个经常性的政治任务；增产节约要贯彻执行“统筹兼顾、适当安排”的方针。决议号召全国人民紧密地团结起来，在中国共产党和人民政府的领导下，大力进行增产节约、反对浪费的运动，为胜利地完成1957年的国民经济建设计划、为第二个五年计划准备良好的基础而奋斗。
    会议听取了提案审查委员会的提案审查报告，并通过了关于提案审查的决议。
    会议还通过了增选常务委员名单。
    五时十分，会议在热烈掌声中闭幕。
