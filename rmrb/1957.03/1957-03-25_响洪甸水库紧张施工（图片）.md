Tense Construction at the Xianghongdian Reservoir

　　The Xianghongdian Reservoir is being built on the Xiyuan River, the western source of the Pi River, within the borders of Jinzhai County, Anhui Province. The reservoir's dam site is located in a narrow and steep river valley between the two banks. The dam type is a concrete gravity arch dam. The use of this type of dam is a first in China's water conservancy construction.
　　Currently, all employees of the reservoir are working with great socialist labor enthusiasm, striving to impound floodwaters before this year's flood season.

### 响洪甸水库紧张施工（图片）

1957-03-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　响洪甸水库紧张施工
    响洪甸水库建筑在安徽省金寨县境内的淠河西源上。水库的坝址位于两岸狭窄而陡峻的河谷内，坝型采用混凝土重力拱坝。采用这种坝型在我国水利建设中还是第一次。
      目前水库全体职工正以极大的社会主义劳动热情工作着，争取在今年汛期前拦蓄洪水。
