Title: Nepalese Prime Minister States Willingness to Accept Soviet Economic Aid
Author:
Date: 1957-03-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, March 11th: According to a Kathmandu dispatch from the Indian Press Trust: Nepalese Prime Minister Acharya said at a reception on the 9th welcoming the American Ambassador to Nepal, Mr. Bunker, that Nepal's relations with China, the Soviet Union, and other countries are becoming increasingly friendly, and no one should be unhappy about this.

When reporters asked if the Nepalese government had received any formal proposals for Soviet economic aid, the Prime Minister said, "No." He then added, "If they suggest it, what harm is there? We have already accepted China's unconditional economic aid."

### 尼泊尔首相表示  愿接受苏联经济援助

1957-03-12
第6版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔首相表示
    愿接受苏联经济援助
    新华社11日讯　据印度报业托辣斯加德满都消息：尼泊尔首相阿查里雅9日在欢迎美国驻尼泊尔大使班克的一个招待会上说，尼泊尔同中国、苏联和其它国家的关系愈来愈友好，任何人都不应该对此心怀不满。
    在记者问到尼泊尔政府是否接到苏联经济援助的任何正式建议时，首相说：“没有”。他接着又补充说，“如果他们建议的话，那又有什么害处呢？我们已经接受了中国的无条件的经济援助”。
