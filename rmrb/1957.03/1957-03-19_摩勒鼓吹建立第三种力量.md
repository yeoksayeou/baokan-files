Title: Mollet Advocates Establishing a Third Force
Author:
Date: 1957-03-19
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 18th dispatch from Chateauroux: French Premier Mollet stated at a banquet in Chateauroux on the 17th that in order for Europe to maintain equilibrium between the Soviet Union and the United States, it is necessary to establish a "third international force."
    When discussing the relationships between Western European countries, Mollet emphasized Anglo-French "understanding" and the "harmonious relationship" between France and West Germany. However, he also called on people not to be "overly suspicious and mistrustful" when dealing with suggestions for building a "new Europe."

### 摩勒鼓吹建立第三种力量

1957-03-19
第6版()
专栏：

    摩勒鼓吹建立第三种力量
    据新华社18日讯　沙托卢消息：法国总理摩勒17日在沙托卢的一次宴会上发表谈话说，为了使欧洲能够在苏联和美国之间保持均势，必须建立“第三种国际力量”。
    在谈到西欧国家之间的关系的时候，摩勒强调了英法“谅解”和法国同西德的“和谐关系”。但是，他同时又呼吁人们在对待建立“新欧洲”的建议方面不要“过分怀疑和互不信任”。
