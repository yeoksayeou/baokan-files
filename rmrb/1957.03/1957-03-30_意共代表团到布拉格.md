Title: Italian Communist Party Delegation Arrives in Prague
Author:
Date: 1957-03-30
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 29th - An Italian Communist Party delegation arrived in Prague this morning. The head of the delegation is Pajetta, a member of the leading body of the Italian Communist Party. The members include Colombi, a member of the leading body of the Italian Communist Party, and Xie Jia, a member of the Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party.
　　The delegation will hold talks with the Czechoslovak Communist Party, the main issue for discussion is the exchange of experiences.

### 意共代表团到布拉格

1957-03-30
第5版()
专栏：

　　意共代表团到布拉格
    据新华社布拉格29日电  意大利共产党代表团今天早晨到达布拉格。代表团团长是意共领导机构委员巴叶塔，团员有意共领导机构委员科隆比和意共中央委员谢嘉等。
    代表团将和捷克斯洛伐克共产党举行会谈，讨论的主要问题是交流经验。
