Title: Chairman Liu Sends Congratulations to Romanian National Assembly Chairman
Author:
Date: 1957-03-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 29th. Chairman Liu Shaoqi's telegram of congratulations to Constantin Pârvulescu, Chairman of the National Assembly of Romania, is as follows:

Comrade Constantin Pârvulescu, Chairman of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic:

I am delighted to learn of your continued election as Chairman of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic. On behalf of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China and in my own name, I extend to you my warm congratulations.

I wish you new achievements in the cause of socialist construction in Romania and in strengthening the unity of the socialist camp.

　　Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　March 27, 1957

### 刘委员长电贺罗国民议会主席

1957-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　刘委员长电贺罗国民议会主席
    新华社29日讯  刘少奇委员长致罗马尼亚国民议会主席帕伏列斯库的贺电，全文如下：罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席康斯坦丁·帕伏列斯库同志：
    欣悉您继续当选罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席，我谨代表中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会并以我个人的名义向您致热烈的祝贺。
    祝您在罗马尼亚社会主义建设事业和加强社会主义阵营的团结中获得新的成就。
      中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长  刘少奇
                                             1957年3月27日
