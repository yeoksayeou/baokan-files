Title: Prime Minister U Nu Tours Scenic Spots in Kunming
Author:
Date: 1957-03-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, March 23rd. The Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, and the Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Sao Khun Hkio, who are visiting Kunming, today toured the famous scenic spots of Kunming, the Western Hills and Dianchi Lake.

　　At around 10 o'clock in the morning, Prime Minister U Nu, accompanied by Vice Premier He Long, the Burmese Ambassador to China U Hla Maung, and the Chinese Ambassador to Burma Yao Zhongming, drove to Xiyuan for sightseeing. This secluded villa is located on the shore of Dianchi Lake at the foot of the Western Hills. The distinguished guests strolled along the pool in the warm sunshine. The vast Dianchi Lake shimmered, and light fishing boats passed in front of the distinguished guests from time to time. Among the colorful flowers in the poolside garden, Prime Minister U Nu and Vice Premier He Long had a long and friendly conversation and took a group photo with the ambassadors.
　　At the same time, the Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Union of Burma, Sao Khun Hkio, accompanied by Acting Governor Liu Minghui, toured the Western Hills. They overlooked the entire view of Dianchi Lake from the Dragon Gate on the Western Hills.
　　Prime Minister U Nu will continue to visit Kunming in the next few days.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, March 25th. The Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, and the Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Sao Khun Hkio, accompanied by Vice Premier of the State Council He Long, Acting Governor of Yunnan Province Liu Minghui, and the ambassadors of the two countries, continued their sightseeing in Kunming.
　　Yesterday, the distinguished guests visited the hot spring sanatorium more than 40 kilometers away from Kunming City, and worshiped Buddha at Caoxi Temple near the hot spring. The distinguished guests also viewed the Udumbara tree planted in the Ming Dynasty in this temple. In the evening, Vice Premier He Long hosted a banquet at the hot springs to entertain Prime Minister U Nu and Deputy Prime Minister Sao Khun Hkio, etc.
　　Today, the distinguished guests visited two factories in the suburbs of Kunming.

### 吴努总理游览昆明风景区

1957-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　吴努总理游览昆明风景区
    新华社昆明23日电　来昆明访问的缅甸联邦总理吴努和副总理兼外交部长藻昆卓，今天分别游览了昆明著名风景区西山和滇池。
    上午十时许，吴努总理在贺龙副总理、缅甸驻我国大使吴拉茂、我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明等陪同下，驱车前往西园游览。这座幽静的别墅座落在西山脚下的滇池岸边。贵宾们浴着和暖的阳光在池畔散步，辽阔的滇池波光闪闪，不时有轻盈的渔船从贵宾的面前驶过。在池滨花园的万紫千红丛中，吴努总理和贺龙副总理作了长时间的友好交谈，并一同和大使们合影留念。
    同时，缅甸联邦副总理兼外交部长藻昆卓在刘明辉代省长的陪同下游览了西山。他们在西山的龙门上俯视了滇池全景。
    吴努总理今后几天将继续在昆明参观访问。
    新华社昆明25日电　缅甸联邦总理吴努和副总理兼外交部长藻昆卓，在国务院副总理贺龙、云南省代省长刘明辉和两国大使的陪同下，继续在昆明参观游览。
    昨天，贵宾们曾到距昆明市区四十多公里的疗养地温泉游览，并在温泉附近的曹溪寺拜了佛。贵宾们还在这个寺院里观看了在明朝时栽种的优昙婆罗树。晚间，贺龙副总理在温泉设便宴招待吴努总理和藻昆卓副总理等。
    今天，贵宾们参观了昆明郊区的两个工厂。
