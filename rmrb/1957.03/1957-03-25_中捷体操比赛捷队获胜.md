Title: China-Czechoslovakia Gymnastics Competition: Czechoslovakian Team Wins
Author:
Date: 1957-03-25
Page: 4

　　Sports News

Xinhua News Agency, March 24th: The Czechoslovakian men's and women's gymnastics teams held a friendly competition with the Chinese men's and women's gymnastics teams at the Beijing Gymnasium this afternoon and evening. In this competition, which lasted more than six hours, the Czechoslovakian men's and women's gymnastics teams won with a total score of 462.8. The total score of the Chinese men's and women's gymnastics teams was 457.

　　The first place in the men's and women's individual all-around total scores was also won by Czechoslovakian athletes Škvora (56.45 points) and Bosáková (37.7 points) respectively. Chinese athlete Wang Huize won second place in the men's individual all-around total score (56.05 points). Chinese athletes Guo Keyu and Lan Yalan won third place (36.85 points) and fourth place (36.65 points) in the women's individual all-around total score. The second place in the women's individual all-around was won by Czechoslovakian athlete Marejková, with a total score of 37.

　　Tang Guozhong, a 21-year-old Chinese male gymnast, showed a very high level of performance in the pommel horse competition today. He defeated all his opponents with a score of 9.55 points (out of a possible 10 points) to win first place – including Czechoslovakian athlete Bím, who won fourth place in pommel horse at the 16th Olympic Games, and Xu Renjie, China's pommel horse champion last year.

　　Bosáková, a Czechoslovakian meritorious athlete who won second place in balance beam at the 16th Olympic Games, won first place in the balance beam (9.6 points) today, but her extremely bold free exercise performance was even more warmly welcomed by the audience. Her free exercise received a higher score - 9.7. Together with the uneven bars event, Bosáková won a total of four first places today. She scored 9.6 points on the uneven bars.

### 中捷体操比赛捷队获胜

1957-03-25
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

　　中捷体操比赛捷队获胜
    据新华社24日讯  捷克斯洛伐克男女体操队今天下午和晚上在北京体育馆同中国男女体操队举行了友谊比赛。在这次历时六个多钟头的比赛中，捷克斯洛伐克男女体操队以四六二点八的总分获得胜利。中国男女体操队的总分是四五七。
    男女个人全能总分的第一名，也分别被捷克斯洛伐克运动员什克沃尔（五十六点四五分）和博萨科娃（三十七点七分）获得。中国运动员王辉泽获得了男子个人全能总分的第二名（五十六点零五分）。中国运动员郭可愚和蓝亚兰获得了女子个人全能总分的第三名（三十六点八五分）和第四名（三十六点六五分）。女子个人全能的第二名被捷克斯洛伐克运动员玛雷依科娃获得，总分是三十七。
    中国二十一岁的男子体操选手唐国忠今天在鞍马比赛中表现了非常高的水平，他以九点五五分（满分是十分）的成绩战胜了所有的对手——其中包括曾经在第十六届奥林匹克运动会上获得鞍马第四名的捷克斯洛伐克运动员比姆和中国去年的鞍马冠军徐仁杰，而获得第一名。
    曾经在第十六届奥林匹克运动会上获得平衡木第二名的捷克斯洛伐克功勋运动员博萨科娃，今天虽然得到了平衡木（九点六分）比赛的第一名，但是她那极其豪放的自由体操表演，受到观众更热烈的欢迎。她的自由体操获得了更高的分数——九点七。连同高低杠这个项目，博萨科娃今天共获得了四个第一名。她高低杠得了九点六分。
