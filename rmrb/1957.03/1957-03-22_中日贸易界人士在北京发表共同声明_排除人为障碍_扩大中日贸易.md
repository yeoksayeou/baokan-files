Title: Sino-Japanese Trade Figures in Beijing Issue Joint Statement: Eliminate Artificial Obstacles, Expand Sino-Japanese Trade
Author:
Date: 1957-03-22
Page: 1
　　
　　Sino-Japanese Trade Figures in Beijing Issue Joint Statement
　　Eliminate Artificial Obstacles, Expand Sino-Japanese Trade
    Xinhua News Agency, March 21st. The China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and the Japanese Kansai Economic Circles Visiting Friendship Delegation today signed a joint statement in Beijing on promoting Sino-Japanese trade issues. Signing on behalf of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade were Chairman Nan Hanchen, Vice-Chairmen Lei Renmin, Li Zhuchen, and Vice-Chairman and Secretary-General Ji Chaoding. Signing on behalf of the Japanese Kansai Economic Circles Visiting Friendship Delegation were Head of the Delegation Koichi Yoshino, Deputy Heads Yosamatsu Matsubara, Shinji Mori, and Secretary-General Den Kawakatsu.
    Liao Chengzhi and others also attended the signing ceremony.
    The Japanese Kansai Economic Circles Visiting Friendship Delegation left Beijing this afternoon for Shanghai. They will visit Shanghai for a few days before returning home via Guangzhou.
    Xinhua News Agency, March 21st. The Joint Statement of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and the Japanese Kansai Economic Circles Visiting Friendship Delegation on Promoting Sino-Japanese Trade Issues. The full text is as follows:
    The Japanese Kansai Economic Circles Visiting Friendship Delegation made a friendly visit to China at the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade. During this period, both sides discussed the issue of expanding Sino-Japanese trade and unanimously agreed that from the perspective of the historical and geographical conditions of China and Japan and the needs of economic development, further expanding the trade relations between the two countries on the basis of equality and mutual benefit not only conforms to the deep aspirations of the people of the two countries, but also has broad prospects. Both sides unanimously agreed that this is of great significance for strengthening the friendship between the people of China and Japan and promoting the normalization of Sino-Japanese relations. However, there are still artificial obstacles on the Japanese side, which seriously affect the due development of trade relations between the two countries. Both sides are willing to work to eliminate these artificial obstacles and promote the normalization of Sino-Japanese trade relations.
    In the talks, both sides unanimously agreed:
    (1) Based on the principle of equivalent barter, mutually and reliably provide each other with the important materials needed by both sides for a long time, such as China supplying Japan with coal, iron ore, salt, etc., and Japan supplying China with heavy machinery, ships, telecommunications equipment, ferrous metals, non-ferrous metals, etc., and are willing to work hard to promote its early realization.
    (2) According to needs and possibilities, both sides are willing to work hard to promote the growth of trade between the two countries and expand the exchange of commodity varieties.
    (3) With the development of Sino-Japanese economic and trade relations, they are willing to increasingly strengthen technical exchanges between the two countries.

### 中日贸易界人士在北京发表共同声明　排除人为障碍　扩大中日贸易

1957-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　中日贸易界人士在北京发表共同声明
　　排除人为障碍　扩大中日贸易
    新华社21日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会同日本关西经济界访华友好代表团今天在北京签定了一项关于促进中日贸易问题的共同声明。代表中国国际贸易促进委员会签字的是主席南汉宸，副主席雷任民、李烛尘和副主席兼秘书长冀朝鼎。代表日本关西经济界访华友好代表团签字的是团长吉野孝一，副团长松原与三松、森新治和秘书长川胜传。
    参加签字仪式的还有廖承志等人。
    日本关西经济界访华友好代表团已在今天下午离开北京去上海，他们将在上海参观几天后取道广州回国。
    新华社21日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会和日本关西经济界访华友好代表团就促进中日贸易问题的共同声明。全文如下：
    日本关西经济界访华友好代表团应中国国际贸易促进委员会的邀请来中国进行了友好访问。在此期间，双方就扩大中日贸易问题进行了商讨，一致认为从中日两国历史、地理的条件和经济发展的需要来看，在平等互利的基础上，进一步扩大两国贸易关系，不仅符合两国人民的深切愿望，而且具有广阔的前途，并且一致认为这对加强中日两国人民的友谊和促进中日关系正常化有着重要的意义。但是在日本方面还存在着人为障碍，严重地影响了两国贸易关系应有的发展，双方愿为排除这些人为障碍和促进中日贸易关系正常化而努力。
    在会谈中，双方一致同意：
    （1）根据对等易货的原则，相互间有保证地长期供应双方所需要的重要物资，如中国供应日本煤、铁砂、盐等，日本供应中国重型机器、船舶、电讯器材、黑色金属、有色金属等，并且愿意努力促其尽快实现。
    （2）根据需要与可能，双方愿意努力促进两国贸易的增长和扩大商品品种的交换。
    （3）随着中日经济贸易关系的发展，愿意日益加强两国间的技术交流。
