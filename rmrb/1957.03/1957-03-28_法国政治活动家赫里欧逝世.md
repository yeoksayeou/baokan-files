Title: French Political Figure Herriot Dies
Author:
Date: 1957-03-28
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 27th: French political veteran, honorary lifetime Speaker of the National Assembly, and lifetime chairman of the Radical Socialist Party, Édouard Herriot, passed away in Lyon on March 26th after a long illness at the age of eighty-four.
　　Herriot was awarded the International Peace Prize in 1954 and served as honorary chairman of the World Peace Congress in 1955 and honorary chairman of the Franco-Soviet Friendship Association. Before the Second World War, Herriot served as Prime Minister of the French cabinet three times. After Hitler occupied France, Herriot was arrested and imprisoned in 1942 for participating in the resistance movement, and later transferred to a German prison. He was released by Soviet troops in 1945. (Attached photo)

### 法国政治活动家赫里欧逝世

1957-03-28
第6版()
专栏：

　　法国政治活动家赫里欧逝世
    新华社27日讯  巴黎消息：法国政界元老、国民议会终身名誉议长、激进社会党终身主席爱德华·赫里欧在长期患病之后，于3月26日在里昂逝世，享年八十四岁。
    赫里欧曾经获得1954年国际和平奖金，并且曾经担任过1955年世界和平大会名誉主席和法苏友好协会名誉主席。在第二次世界大战前，赫里欧曾经三度出任法国内阁总理。在希特勒占领法国后，赫里欧因为参加抵抗运动而在1942年被捕下狱，后来又被移到德国监狱。1945年被苏联军队释放。（附图片）
