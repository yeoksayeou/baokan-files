Title: Hungarian Communist Youth League Holds Inaugural Congress; Chinese Communist Youth League Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Telegram

Author:

Date: 1957-03-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, March 22nd: The Hungarian Communist Youth League held its inaugural congress in Budapest on the 38th anniversary of the founding of the Hungarian Soviet Republic (March 21st). Komócsin Zoltán, chairman of the preparatory committee, delivered a report at the congress. He announced the formal establishment of the Hungarian Communist Youth League, the direct successor to the Communist Youth League of Labor established in 1919.

He said that the central task of the Communist Youth League is to conduct Marxist-Leninist education for Hungarian youth. Domestically, the League should establish close friendly cooperation with other democratic youth mass organizations. Internationally, it should participate in the activities of the World Federation of Democratic Youth and cooperate with the youth of socialist countries, especially the youth of the Soviet Union.

Fok Jenő, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, congratulated the participants of the congress on behalf of the Party Central Committee.

Congratulatory telegrams from youth organizations in the Soviet Union, China, and other countries, as well as from the World Federation of Democratic Youth, were read at the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency: The Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League sent a telegram on the 20th to the inaugural congress of the Hungarian Communist Youth League, congratulating them on the opening of the congress.

### 匈牙利共青团举行成立大会　我青年团中央致电祝贺

1957-03-23
第5版()
专栏：

　　匈牙利共青团举行成立大会
　　我青年团中央致电祝贺
    据新华社布达佩斯22日电　在匈牙利苏维埃共和国成立三十八周年纪念日（3月21日），匈牙利共产主义青年团在布达佩斯举行了成立大会。筹备委员会主席科莫钦·佐尔坦在大会上作了报告。他宣布，匈牙利共产主义青年团正式建立，它是1919年建立的共产主义劳动青年联盟的直接继承者。
    他说，共青团的中心工作是对匈牙利青年进行马克思列宁主义教育。共青团在国内要同其它民主青年群众组织建立紧密的友好合作，在国外，要参加世界民主青年联盟的活动，并且同社会主义国家的青年，首先是同苏联青年合作。
    匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会书记福克·耶诺代表党中央委员会向参加大会的人祝贺。
    会上宣读了苏联、中国和其他国家青年组织的贺电以及世界民主青年联盟的贺电。
    据新华社讯　中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会20日致电匈牙利共产主义青年团成立大会，对大会的召开表示祝贺。
