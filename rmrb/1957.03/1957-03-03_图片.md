    Agronomists from the Institute of Botany's Genetics Research Office of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the North China Institute of Agricultural Sciences have achieved results in their research on the vernalization and photoperiod stages of autumn-sown wheat in China. This research enables people to reduce blindness when selecting parents for breeding new varieties, and at the same time, to have a further understanding of the commonalities and characteristics of varieties in various regions, providing a theoretical basis for regionalization research of varieties. The left picture shows agronomist Huang Jifang (right) and researcher Liu Zongqi (left) observing wheat experiments in the greenhouse. The right picture shows agronomist Huang Jifang (right) dissecting the fine structure of the wheat growth cone with a stereoscopic microscope. Xinhua News Agency reporter Fu Jun

    中国科学院植物研究所遗传研究室和华北农业科学研究所的农学家们，对中国秋播小麦春化阶段和光照阶段的特性研究已经有了结果。这一研究使人们在培养新品种选择亲本时可减少盲目性，同时对各个地区品种的共同性和品种的特性有进一步了解，为品种区域化研究提供了理论根据。左图为农学家黄季芳（右）和研究人员刘宗岐（左）在观察温室内的小麦试验。右图为农学家黄季芳（右）在用立体显微镜解剖小麦生长锥的细微构造。  新华社记者  傅军摄
