Title: Voroshilov to Visit Indonesia in May
Author:
Date: 1957-03-20
Page: 7

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 19th: The Indonesian Foreign Ministry announced today that Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, will pay a state visit to Indonesia in early May at the invitation of President Sukarno.
    Voroshilov and his party are scheduled to stay in Indonesia for seventeen days, visiting Bandung, Yogyakarta, Solo, Surabaya, Medan, and Bali.

### 伏罗希洛夫五月访印度尼西亚

1957-03-20
第7版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫五月访印度尼西亚
    新华社雅加达19日电　印度尼西亚外交部今天宣布，苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫应苏加诺总统的邀请，将在5月初到印度尼西亚进行国事访问。
    伏罗希洛夫一行准备在印度尼西亚停留十七天，到万隆、日惹、梭罗、泗水、棉兰和巴厘等地进行访问。
