Title: Spring plowing is busy on both banks of the Honghe River (Picture)
Author: Zhao Suhua
Date: 1957-03-09
Page: 1

    Spring plowing is busy on both banks of the Honghe River
    Young women of the Longhe Township (Dai Autonomous Township) Advanced Agricultural Production Cooperative, Moshaba, Fourth District, Xinping County, Yunnan Province, pull up rice seedlings every afternoon during the spring plowing season to prepare for transplanting the next day.
                                Photographed by Zhao Suhua (Yunnan Daily Press release)
