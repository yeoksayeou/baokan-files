Title: Neolithic Artifacts Unearthed in Hubei Add Splendor to Ancient Art History
Author:
Date: 1957-03-18
Page: 8
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 17th. In recent years, Neolithic late-stage cultural relics have been discovered in Tianmen and Jingshan counties, Hubei Province, many of which are rare in other ancient cultural sites throughout the country. Excavated in Qujialing, Jingshan County, are finely textured "eggshell painted pottery" and glossy "black pottery." Excavated in Shijiahe, Tianmen County, are very small clay (red pottery) birds and animals, including chickens, ducks, dogs, etc. In the Shijiahe site, painted pottery spinning wheels, discovered for the first time in the country, were also unearthed. These valuable materials not only fill the archaeological gap in the Hubei region but also add a splendid page to the history of ancient Chinese art.

　　In addition, a celadon artifact—an upturned lotus zun (pot)—excavated from the tomb of Emperor Wu of the Southern Qi Dynasty, Xiao Ze, Yongming three years (485 AD) was discovered in Fujiawan, Wuchang, Wuhan. This complete and exquisite six-eared celadon pot provides accurate and reliable information for the study of the development of Chinese porcelain.

### 湖北出土的新石器时代文物  为远古美术史增添光

1957-03-18
第8版()
专栏：

    湖北出土的新石器时代文物
    为远古美术史增添光彩
    新华社武汉17日电　最近几年在湖北天门、京山县等地曾发现出新石器时代晚期的文物，其中有许多是全国其他古文化遗址中所少见的。在京山县屈家岭出土的有质地细薄的“蛋壳彩陶”和色彩光泽的“黑陶”。在天门县石家河出土的有很小的泥塑（红陶）禽兽，里边有鸡、鸭、狗等。在石家河遗址中还发掘出在全国尚属第一次发现的绘彩的陶纺轮。这些宝贵的资料，不仅填补了湖北地区考古学上的空白，同时也为我国远古美术史上增添了光彩的一页。
    此外，在武汉市武昌傅家湾发现了南朝齐武帝萧赜永明三年（公元四八五年）墓中出土的青瓷器——仰复莲花尊（壶），这个青瓷六耳壶完整而又精美，对研究我国瓷器发展提供了准确而可靠的资料。
