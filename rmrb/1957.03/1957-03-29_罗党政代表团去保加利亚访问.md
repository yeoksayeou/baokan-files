Title: Romanian Party and Government Delegation Visits Bulgaria
Author:
Date: 1957-03-29
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, March 28th: Newspapers here report that the Romanian Workers' Party and government delegation left by train this morning to visit Bulgaria. The head of the delegation is Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party and member of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly. Members include Stoica, member of the Political Bureau of the Workers' Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Borila, member of the Political Bureau of the Workers' Party and Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and Ploeţiţa, member of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party and Minister of Foreign Affairs.
　　Bulgarian Ambassador to Romania, Pavlov, accompanied the delegation to Sofia.

### 罗党政代表团去保加利亚访问

1957-03-29
第5版()
专栏：

　　罗党政代表团去保加利亚访问
    新华社布加勒斯特28日电  此间报纸报道，罗马尼亚工人党和政府代表团今天早晨乘火车前往保加利亚访问。代表团团长是罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会第一书记、国民议会主席团委员乔治乌—德治，团员有工人党政治局委员、部长会议主席斯托伊卡，工人党政治局委员、部长会议副主席波里勒，工人党中央委员、外交部长普利奥蒂亚萨等。
    保加利亚驻罗马尼亚大使巴夫洛夫陪同代表团前往索非亚。
