Title: West Germany Orders Large Quantities of Armaments
Author: 
Date: 1957-03-04
Page: 6

    West Germany Orders Large Quantities of Armaments
    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd dispatch: West German newspapers reported that the West German Ministry of Defense has for the first time ordered 2,800 armored vehicles from Britain, worth 400 million marks. A large French armaments factory, "Schneider-Creusot Armaments Factory," also received an order worth 400 million marks from the Bonn Ministry of Defense.

### 西德订购大批军火

1957-03-04
第6版()
专栏：

    西德订购大批军火
    据新华社2日讯  西德报刊报道，西德国防部第一次向英国订购了二千八百辆装甲车，价值四亿马克。法国一个大军火工厂“施奈德—克勒佐军火厂”也接到了波恩国防部四亿马克的订货。
