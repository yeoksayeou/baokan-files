Title: After Ending Their Visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Czechoslovak Government Delegation Arrives in Guangzhou from Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1957-03-21
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, March 20th. After concluding their visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Prime Minister Viliam Široký and the entire delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic government he led arrived in Guangzhou by special plane this afternoon from Hanoi to continue their tour of China.

　　More than 3,000 representatives from all walks of life in Guangzhou welcomed the distinguished guests at the airport. Among those in the welcoming ranks were Feng Baiju, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, leading figures from the Party, government, and military organs of Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City; Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council, who arrived in Guangzhou the day before yesterday to accompany the distinguished guests on their continued tour of China, Cao Ying, Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, Zhang Shijie, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; as well as the Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, A. Gregor, and personnel from the Soviet and Polish consulates in Guangzhou.

　　After resting briefly at their residence, Prime Minister Široký and the delegation, accompanied by Feng Baiju, Zhu Guang, the Mayor of Guangzhou, and others, visited the Peasant Movement Training Institute. Here, the distinguished guests listened to Zhu Guang's introduction to the circumstances of the Peasant Movement Training Institute and toured the classrooms, student dormitories, and Comrade Mao Zedong's former residence.

### 结束在越南民主共和国的访问后　捷政府代表团由河内到广州

1957-03-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　结束在越南民主共和国的访问后
　　捷政府代表团由河内到广州
    据新华社广州20日电　威廉·西罗基总理和他所率领的捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团全体人员，结束了在越南民主共和国的访问以后，今天下午自河内乘专机飞抵广州，继续进行在我国的参观访问。
    广州市各界代表三千多人在机场上欢迎贵宾。在欢迎的行列里，有广东省副省长冯白驹，广东省和广州市党政军机关的领导人；有前天到达广州准备陪同贵宾继续在我国参观访问的国务院秘书长习仲勋，中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使曹瑛，外交部礼宾司副司长张世杰；还有捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使安·格里哥尔以及苏联和波兰驻广州领事馆人员。
    西罗基总理和代表团人员赴寓所稍事休息以后，即由冯白驹和广州市市长朱光等陪同参观了农民运动讲习所。贵宾们在这里听朱光介绍了农民运动讲习所的情况，参观了讲习所的课堂、学生宿舍和毛泽东同志当年的住室等处。
