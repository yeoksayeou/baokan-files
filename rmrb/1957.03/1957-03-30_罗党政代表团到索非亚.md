Title: Romanian Party and Government Delegation Arrives in Sofia
Author:
Date: 1957-03-30
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, March 29th: The Romanian Workers' Party and government delegation headed by Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej arrived in Sofia on the afternoon of March 28th.

　　Welcoming the delegation at the station were Bulgarian Communist Party and government leaders Zhivkov, Yugov, and others, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Sofia.

### 罗党政代表团到索非亚

1957-03-30
第5版()
专栏：

　　罗党政代表团到索非亚
    新华社索非亚29日电  以乔治乌—德治为首的罗马尼亚工人党和政府代表团3月28日下午到达索非亚。
    到车站欢迎代表团的有保加利亚共产党和政府领导人日夫科夫、于哥夫等以及驻索非亚的各国使节。
