Title: President Ho Chi Minh Receives Chinese Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-03-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 28th – President Ho Chi Minh received the Chinese Cultural Delegation headed by Xia Yan and other members this morning, and talked with them for forty minutes. Present at the reception were Vietnamese Vice Minister of Culture Cu Huy Can and Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam Luo Guibo, among others.

### 胡志明主席接见我文化代表团

1957-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　胡志明主席接见我文化代表团
    新华社河内28日电  胡志明主席在今天上午接见了中国文化代表团团长夏衍和其他团员，交谈了四十分钟。接见时在座的，有越南文化部副部长瞿辉近和中国驻越南大使罗贵波等人。
