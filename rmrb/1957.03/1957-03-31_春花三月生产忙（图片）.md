Title: Busy Production in the Flourishing Spring of March (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1957-03-31
Page: 3

　　Busy Production in the Flourishing Spring of March
    1. Spring arrives in Xinjiang. In Bayandai Township, Yining County, Yili River, members of the Red Flag Agricultural Cooperative are driving carts, crossing the snowy fields, and delivering manure to the fields.
                                        Photo by Liu Cheng
    2. Members of the Red Flag Agricultural Cooperative in Nanhai County, Guangdong Province, are engaged in spring plowing.
                                        Photo by Zhao Muzhi (Southern Daily News Contribution)
    3. Members of the Meizhuang Agricultural Cooperative in Qiongshan County, Hainan Island, are collecting seedlings in the tender green rice paddies. Once the final harrowing is complete, they can begin transplanting the rice seedlings here.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　            Photo by Yang Guangzhou
    4. Members of the Jinxing Agricultural Cooperative in Chaoyang Township, Chaoyang District, Harbin City, are actively inspecting new farm tools, waiting for the snow to melt so they can begin spring plowing.
                                        Photo by Yu Min (Heilongjiang Daily News Contribution)
