Title: Nie Rongzhen Arrives in London En Route Home

Author:

Date: 1957-03-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, 9th: Marshal Nie Rongzhen, the Chinese special envoy who attended the independence ceremony of Ghana, arrived in London on the 8th en route home.

　　In a letter thanking Ghanaian Prime Minister Nkrumah, Nie Rongzhen said: "I am convinced that our participation in the Ghanaian independence ceremony will not only be an unforgettable event in our lives, but will also symbolize the beginning of the future development of the fraternal friendship between our two peoples."

### 聂荣臻回国途中到达伦敦

1957-03-10
第6版()
专栏：

    聂荣臻回国途中到达伦敦
    新华社伦敦9日电　参加加纳独立典礼的中国特使聂荣臻元帅在回国途中8日到达伦敦。
    聂荣臻在向加纳总理恩克鲁玛致谢的信件中说：“我确信，我们参加加纳独立典礼不仅将成为我们终生难忘的事件，而且也象征着我们两国人民的兄弟友谊在今后的发展的开端。
