Title: Cyrankiewicz to Visit Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1957-03-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 1st – According to the Polish Press Agency on March 1st: At the invitation of King Suramarit and Prince Sihanouk of Cambodia, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic, Józef Cyrankiewicz, will pay an official visit to Cambodia.

This visit will be in return for Prince Sihanouk's visit to Poland last year.

### 西伦凯维兹将访问柬埔寨

1957-03-02
第5版()
专栏：

    西伦凯维兹将访问柬埔寨
    新华社华沙1日电  据波兰通讯社1日讯：波兰人民共和国部长会议主席西伦凯维兹应柬埔寨国王苏拉玛里特和西哈努克亲王的邀请，将前往柬埔寨作正式访问。
    这次访问将是对西哈努克亲王去年访问波兰的答聘。
