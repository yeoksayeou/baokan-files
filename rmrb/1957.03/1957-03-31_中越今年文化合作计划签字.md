Title: China and Vietnam Sign This Year's Cultural Cooperation Plan
Author:
Date: 1957-03-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 30th. The 1957 cultural cooperation plan between China and Vietnam was signed in Hanoi today.

According to the plan, the Vietnamese side will send delegations of culture, education, health, news, publishing, film (already sent), and song and dance troupes to China for visits; the Chinese side will send a Pingju opera troupe (already sent), journalists, music and dance workers, and other experts to Vietnam for visits.

Both sides will also increase the number of students studying abroad, promote cooperation in news and broadcasting, and strengthen ties in translation, publishing, and information exchange.

The Chinese side will also hold the "Chinese Arts and Crafts Exhibition" and the "Chinese Print Exhibition" in Vietnam.

Xia Yan, head of the Chinese cultural delegation, and Qu Huy Can, head of the Vietnamese cultural delegation, signed the cooperation plan on behalf of their respective governments.

### 中越今年文化合作计划签字

1957-03-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　中越今年文化合作计划签字
    新华社河内30日电  中越两国1957年文化合作计划今天在河内签字。
    根据计划，越南方面将分别派文化、教育、卫生、新闻、出版、电影（已派）等代表团和歌舞团到中国访问；中国方面派评剧团（已派）、记者、音乐舞蹈工作者和其他专家到越南访问。
    双方并且将互相加派留学生，促进在新闻、广播方面的合作，加强在翻译、出版和资料交换方面的联系。
    中国方面还要在越南举行“中国工艺美术品展览”和“中国版画展览”。
    中国文化代表团团长夏衍和越南文化代表团团长瞿辉近代表两国政府在合作计划上签字。
