Title: This Year Will Commemorate Seven World Cultural Figures; Memorial Service for Russian Composer Glinka to Be Held Tonight
Author:
Date: 1957-03-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 26th. The Chinese Committee for the Commemoration of World Cultural Figures for 1957 has been formed. Guo Moruo is the Chairman, and Chu Tunan is the Vice-Chairman.

The World Peace Council has decided that the world cultural figures to be commemorated in 1957 are Italian playwright Carlo Goldoni, Czechoslovak philosopher, theologian, educator and writer Jan Amos Comenius, American poet Henry Longfellow, French mathematician Auguste Comte, Swedish naturalist Carl Linnaeus, British poet and painter William Blake, and Russian composer Glinka.

The committee decided to commemorate them by holding academic gatherings such as report meetings, symposiums, exhibitions, and concerts. The committee is composed of ten units, including the China Peace Committee, the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the China National Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

The memorial service for the Russian composer Glinka will be held at the Tianqiao Theater on the evening of March 27th.

### 今年将纪念七位世界文化名人　俄罗斯作曲家格林卡纪念会今晚举行

1957-03-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　今年将纪念七位世界文化名人
　　俄罗斯作曲家格林卡纪念会今晚举行
    新华社26日讯　中国1957年纪念世界文化名人委员会已经组成。郭沫若任主席，楚图南任副主席。
    世界和平理事会决定1957年纪念的世界文化名人是意大利剧作家卡尔罗·哥尔多尼、捷克斯洛伐克哲学家、神学家、教育家和作家杨莫斯·考门斯基、美国诗人亨利·朗费罗、法国数学家奥古斯特·孔德、瑞典自然学家卡尔·林内、英国诗人和画家威廉·布莱克和俄罗斯作曲家格林卡。
    委员会决定采取学术性集会，如报告会、座谈会、展览会、音乐会等形式来进行纪念。委员会是由中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国人民对外文化协会、中国文学艺术界联合会、中国科学院、中苏友好协会等十个单位组成的。
    关于俄罗斯作曲家格林卡的纪念会，将在3月27日晚上在天桥剧场举行。
