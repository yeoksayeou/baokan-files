Title: Czechoslovakian Men's and Women's Gymnastics Teams Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-03-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, March 9th. The Czechoslovakian men's and women's gymnastics team, a group of twenty-five people invited to visit China, arrived in Beijing tonight.

The Czechoslovakian men's and women's gymnastics team consists of eight male athletes and nine female athletes. According to their leader, Lyudmila Peshkova, president of the Czechoslovakian Gymnastics Association, two male athletes and three female athletes participated in the 16th Olympic Games last year. Female athlete Yeva Bosakova won second place in the balance beam competition at that event. The Czechoslovakian men's and women's gymnastics teams won fourth and fifth place respectively in the team competition at that event.

The Czechoslovakian men's and women's gymnastics team will leave Beijing on March 12th to perform in Chongqing first. Afterwards, they will also visit Wuhan, Beijing, and Tianjin. They are scheduled to hold friendly matches with Chinese male and female gymnasts in Beijing in late March.

### 捷男女体操队抵京

1957-03-10
第6版()
专栏：体育新闻

    捷男女体操队抵京
    新华社9日讯　应邀来中国访问的捷克斯洛伐克男、女体操队一行二十五人，今晚到达北京。
    捷克斯洛伐克男、女体操队中，有八名男运动员和九名女运动员。据他们的领队——捷克斯洛伐克体操协会主席佩什科娃·刘德米拉说，其中有两名男运动员和三名女运动员去年曾经参加过第十六届奥林匹克运动会。女运动员耶娃·博萨科娃在那次运动会上获得平衡木比赛的第二名。捷克斯洛伐克男、女体操队在那次运动会上分别获得团体第四和第五名。
    捷克斯洛伐克男、女体操队将在3月12日离开北京先去重庆表演。以后，他们还要访问武汉、北京和天津。他们预定在3月下旬在北京同中国男、女体操运动员举行友谊比赛。
