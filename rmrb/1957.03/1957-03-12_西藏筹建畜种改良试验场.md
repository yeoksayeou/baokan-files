Title: Tibet Prepares to Build Livestock Breeding Experimental Farm
Author:
Date: 1957-03-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa - Tibet's first livestock breeding experimental farm has begun construction in the Angdo Gaja tribe, north of the Heihe River.

The experimental farm is constructing farm buildings and stables while simultaneously selecting and breeding local species and cultivating pasture. In addition, 200 mu of tea fields are being reclaimed.

Currently, some staff members have arrived at the Angdo Gaja tribal pasture, preparing to carry out livestock improvement work.

### 西藏筹建畜种改良试验场

1957-03-12
第5版()
专栏：

    西藏筹建畜种改良试验场
    据新华社拉萨讯　西藏第一个畜种改良试验场已经在黑河北面的安多噶加部落开始筹建。
    试验场一面修建场房和厩舍，一面进行土种选育、培植牧草。此外还开垦二百亩茶场。
    现在，已有部分工作人员到达安多噶加部落牧场上，准备进行种畜的改良工作。
