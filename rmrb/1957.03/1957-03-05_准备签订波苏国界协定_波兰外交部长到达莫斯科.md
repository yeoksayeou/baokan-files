Title: Preparing to Sign Polish-Soviet Border Agreement: Polish Foreign Minister Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1957-03-05
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 4th. TASS reports: Polish People's Republic Foreign Minister Rapacki and his entourage arrived in Moscow on the 4th, preparing to sign an agreement with the Soviet government regarding the existing Polish-Soviet border in the Baltic region and to conduct related negotiations.

    Rapacki made a brief statement at the Belorussian Railway Station. He said: "The Polish people clearly know that the relationship between Poland and the Soviet Union, based on the principles of proletarian internationalism, is in line with the vital interests of the Polish people and is conducive to the common cause of our two peoples, and the cause of European security and peace." On the same day, Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko met with Rapacki.

### 准备签订波苏国界协定  波兰外交部长到达莫斯科

1957-03-05
第5版()
专栏：

    准备签订波苏国界协定
    波兰外交部长到达莫斯科
    新华社4日讯  据塔斯社莫斯科讯：波兰人民共和国外交部长拉帕茨基和随行人员4日到达莫斯科，准备同苏联政府签订关于波苏两国波罗的海地区现有国界的协定和进行有关谈判。
    拉帕茨基在白俄罗斯车站上发表了简短的声明。他说：“波兰人民清楚地知道，以无产阶级国际主义原则为基础的波苏两国的关系符合波兰人民的切身利益，有利于我们两国人民的共同事业、欧洲安全与和平事业”。当天，苏联外交部长葛罗米柯接见了拉帕茨基。
