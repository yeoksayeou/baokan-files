Title: Japanese Kansai Economic Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-03-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 5th. A Japanese Kansai Economic Delegation arrived in Beijing by plane today. This fourteen-member delegation is comprised of leading industrialists from the Kansai region, centered around Osaka, in industries such as shipbuilding, machinery, chemical industry, rayon, and dye manufacturing.

### 日本关西经济界访华友好代表团到北京

1957-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

    日本关西经济界访华友好代表团到北京
    新华社5日讯　日本关西经济界访华友好代表团今天乘飞机到达北京。这个十四个人的代表团是由日本以大阪为中心的关西地区的造船、机械、化学工业、人造丝、染料等制造工业方面的主要工业家组成的。
