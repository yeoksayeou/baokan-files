Title: CPPCC Plenary Session Continues General Debate
Author:
Date: 1957-03-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 13th: The Third Plenary Session of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference continued its general debate this afternoon.

    Huang Yanpei, Vice Chairman of the CPPCC National Committee, was the first to speak today, focusing on offering suggestions on the formulation and implementation of national plans. He expressed hope that planning agencies would strengthen ties with implementing departments and regions, that scientific research institutions would strengthen ties with planning agencies and implementing departments, and that implementing departments would enhance the seriousness of plan implementation, avoiding mid-course changes to plans as much as possible.
    Dai Ji, Vice Chairman of the CPPCC Anhui Provincial Committee, spoke about developing pig production. He suggested gathering experts and experienced veteran farmers to research and develop a new nationwide plan that is suitable for the situation after agricultural cooperation, making the new pig farming industry more economical and larger in scale than individual agricultural pig farming.
    Renowned veteran traditional Chinese medicine doctor Ran Xuefeng hoped that Western medicine practitioners would focus on the fundamental principles of traditional Chinese medicine when studying it, integrating the essence of thousands of years of TCM experimentation into Western medical theories to complement each other.
    Yue Chongdai, Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Taoist Association, briefly introduced the origin and evolution of Taoism. He expressed that in the future, he would unite Taoists across the country to love the motherland, actively participate in socialist construction and the cause of defending world peace, and assist the government in implementing the policy of freedom of religious belief.
    Cui Dongbo, Vice Principal of Hangzhou No. 1 Middle School, who attended the meeting as an observer, advocated that the allocation of middle school teachers in the future should implement the spirit of "overall consideration and appropriate arrangement."
    Wei Lihuang advised his old friends in Taiwan not to hesitate or linger any longer, and even less to act as pawns of American imperialism. He urged them to respond to Premier Zhou's call, return as soon as possible, and work together to build our new China.
    Yin Zanhsun, Deputy Director of the Department of Biology and Earth Sciences of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, believes that there are significant shortcomings in the allocation of geological work in China in the three areas of production, scientific research, and education. He suggested transferring the majority of geologists who graduated from older universities from production posts in batches to support geological research institutions and universities lacking teaching staff.
    Xue Dubi, Standing Committee member of the CPPCC Shanghai Municipal Committee, spoke about his understanding of the principle of "long-term coexistence and mutual supervision."
    Cheng Qian spoke about his impressions of participating in the National People's Congress delegation's visits to the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Romania, and Bulgaria. He said that his deepest impression was the profound comradely friendship of the people of the Soviet Union and other Eastern European fraternal countries towards the Chinese people.
    Ning Wu, Vice Governor of Liaoning Province, used the example of Liaoning Province's increased agricultural production and farmers' increased income to illustrate that agricultural cooperatives are successful.
    Hu Shuhua, professor at the Beijing Institute of Iron and Steel Technology, made some suggestions on increasing production and practicing economy in heavy industry.
    Li Junlong, Standing Committee member of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, focused on his impressions of inspecting Hainan Island. He suggested organizing an exploration team to clarify the island's resource situation and to formulate a long-term development plan for Hainan.
    Wang Ligeng, Shi Xiaoshan, Li Zongen, Chen Jingyun, Chen Cuizhen, Yan Renying, Meng Jimao, Lin Sixin, Jin Baoshan, Yao Kefang, Shi Jinmo, Zhao Shuping, Xu Songming, Qin Bowen, Zhang Xiaoqian, Huang Dingchen, Yang Chongrui, Zhong Huilan, and Yan Fuqing, nineteen people from the medical and health fields, jointly spoke today. They focused on the benefits of late marriage and family planning.
    Those who gave written speeches today were Ma Xulun, Guo Dihuo, Jiang Hengyuan, Guo Qiaoran, Hu Yisheng, Shen Zhaonian, Meng Mudi, and Wang Mingxuan.
    Ma Xulun talked about how democratic parties can further and effectively implement the policy of "long-term coexistence and mutual supervision."
    Guo Dihuo talked about his experiences and opinions from participating in the National People's Congress delegation's visits to the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Romania, and Bulgaria.
    Jiang Hengyuan expressed his views on issues such as student grading in schools at all levels, the implementation of the "two-part system" in middle and primary schools, and strengthening education for children and young people in society.
    Guo Qiaoran and Hu Yisheng suggested in a joint speech that branches of various specialized societies be established in various regions to lead various scientific research efforts.
    Shen Zhaonian introduced the situation of elderly people at the Hubei Provincial Museum of Literature and History giving play to their expertise and actively participating in various tasks such as compiling historical data on natural disasters in Hubei Province and concise records of counties and cities.
    Meng Mudi suggested cultivating various cash crops with higher economic value to meet domestic and export needs.
    Wang Mingxuan, who attended the meeting as an observer, said that our people across the country should work harder to increase production, practice economy, build the country diligently and frugally, and contribute their strength.
    A total of thirty-nine people spoke today.
    This morning, the meeting held group discussions; in the evening, the members visited the National Agricultural Exhibition currently on display in Beijing.
    The meeting will continue tomorrow.

### 政协全体会议继续大会讨论

1957-03-14
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全体会议继续大会讨论
    据新华社13日讯  中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第三次全体会议今天下午继续大会讨论。
    政协全国委员会副主席黄炎培今天第一个发言，他着重就国家计划的制定和执行提出意见。他希望计划机构首先和执行部门、执行地区加强联系，希望科学研究机构和计划机构、各执行部门加强联系，同时希望执行部门提高执行计划的严肃性，尽量避免中途变更计划的情况。
    政协安徽省委员会副主席戴戟谈到了发展养猪生产的问题。他认为不妨集合专家和有经验的老农，研讨一个具有全国性的符合于农业合作化以后的情况的新规划，使新的养猪业比个体农业养猪更为经济，规模更为巨大。
    著名老中医冉雪峰希望西医学习中医时要在学术根本上着眼，将中医数千年实验精蕴融纳入西医学理中，相得益彰。
    中国道教协会筹备委员会主任委员岳崇岱简略地介绍了道教的起源和沿革。他表示，今后要团结全国的道教徒，爱护祖国，积极参加社会主义建设和保卫世界和平事业，协助政府贯彻宗教信仰自由政策。
    列席会议的杭州一中副校长崔东伯主张今后中学教师的调配，应该贯彻“统筹兼顾，适当安排”的精神。
    卫立煌劝告在台湾的老朋友，不要再彷徨徘徊，更不要作美帝国主义的爪牙，赶快响应周总理的号召，早早回来，一起同心协力建设我们的新中国。
    科学院生物学地学部副主任尹赞勋认为，我国地质工作在生产、科学研究、教育三方面的力量配置上，存在着较大的缺点。他建议要分批地把大多数旧大学出身的地质学家由生产岗位上调出来，支援地质科学研究机构和缺乏师资的高等学校。
    政协上海市委员会常务委员薛笃弼谈到了他对“长期共存，互相监督”方针的体会。
    程潜谈到了他参加全国人民代表大会代表团访问苏、捷、罗、保等国的印象。他说感受最深的是，苏联及东欧兄弟国家人民对中国人民的深厚的同志友谊。
    辽宁省副省长宁武以辽宁省的农业增产和农民增加收入的情况，说明农业合作社是办得成功的。
    北京钢铁工业学院教授胡庶华，就重工业的增产节约方面提出了一些建议。
    民革中央常务委员李俊龙着重谈到了视察海南岛的观感。他建议组织勘察队，弄清楚岛上的资源情况，并且拟定海南建设远景规划。
    王历畊、石筱山、李宗恩、陈景云、陈翠贞、严仁英、孟继懋、林斯馨、金宝善、姚克方、施今墨、赵树屏、徐诵明、秦伯未、张孝骞、黄鼎臣、杨崇瑞、钟惠澜、颜福庆等十九位医药卫生界人士，今天联合发言。他们着重谈到了迟婚和计划生育的好处。
    今天书面发言的有马叙伦、郭棣活、江恒源、郭翘然、胡一声、沈肇年、孟目的、王明选。
    马叙伦谈到了民主党派如何进一步切实贯彻“长期共存，互相监督”方针的问题。
    郭棣活谈了他参加全国人民代表大会访问团访问苏、捷、罗、保等国的体会和意见。
    江恒源就各级学校学生考试记分，中、小学试行“二部制”，社会方面对儿童青年加强教育等问题，发表了意见。
    郭翘然和胡一声在联合发言中建议：在各地区成立各专门学会的分会，以领导各种科学研究工作。
    沈肇年介绍了湖北省文史馆的老人发挥专长，积极参加编纂湖北省自然灾害历史资料和县市简志等各项工作的情况。
    孟目的建议培植经济价值较高的各种农作物，以应国内和出口的需要。
    列席会议的王明选说，我们全国人民要更加努力增产节约，勤俭建国，贡献自己的力量。
    今天发言的共有三十九人。
    今天上午，会议进行了分组讨论；晚上，委员们参观了正在北京展出的全国农业展览会。
    明天，会议将继续举行。
