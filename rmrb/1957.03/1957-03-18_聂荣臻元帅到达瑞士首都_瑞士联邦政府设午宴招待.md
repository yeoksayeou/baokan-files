Title: Marshal Nie Rongzhen Arrives in Swiss Capital, Swiss Federal Government Hosts Luncheon

Author:

Date: 1957-03-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 16th: Nie Rongzhen, Vice Premier of the State Council and special envoy of China participating in the Ghana Independence Celebration, arrived in Bern, the capital of Switzerland, on the 14th. On the 15th, he met with the Swiss Federal Minister of Politics, Mr. Petitpierre.

    The Swiss Federal Government hosted a luncheon on the 15th in honor of Nie Rongzhen. Those attending the banquet from the Swiss side included Petitpierre, Minister of Military Affairs Chaudet, and many other military and political leaders.

    Nie Rongzhen is scheduled to leave Switzerland for Czechoslovakia on the 18th.

### 聂荣臻元帅到达瑞士首都  瑞士联邦政府设午宴招待

1957-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

    聂荣臻元帅到达瑞士首都
    瑞士联邦政府设午宴招待
    新华社16日讯　日内瓦消息：中国参加加纳独立庆典特使、国务院副总理聂荣臻元帅14日到达瑞士首都伯尔尼。他在15日拜会了瑞士联邦政治部长彼蒂彼爱。
    瑞士联邦政府在15日设午宴招待聂荣臻。瑞士方面出席宴会的有彼蒂彼爱、军事部长萧德以及其他军政首长多人。
    聂荣臻订于18日离瑞士前往捷克斯洛伐克。
