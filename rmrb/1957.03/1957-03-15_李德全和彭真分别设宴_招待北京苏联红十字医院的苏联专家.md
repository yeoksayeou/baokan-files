Title: Li Dequan and Peng Zhen Hosted Banquets Separately to Entertain Soviet Experts from the Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital
Author:
Date: 1957-03-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 14th — Li Dequan, Minister of Public Health and President of the Red Cross Society of China, hosted a banquet tonight to entertain the Soviet experts from the Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital who are about to return home. A.S. Abramov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, was also invited to attend.

On the evening of the 13th, Peng Zhen, Mayor of Beijing, also hosted a banquet to entertain the Soviet experts from the Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital.

### 李德全和彭真分别设宴  招待北京苏联红十字医院的苏联专家

1957-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

    李德全和彭真分别设宴
    招待北京苏联红十字医院的苏联专家
    新华社14日讯  卫生部部长、中国红十字会总会会长李德全今晚设宴招待即将回国的北京苏联红十字医院的苏联专家们。苏联驻中国大使馆参赞阿布拉西莫夫也应邀出席。
    13日晚，北京市市长彭真也设宴招待了北京苏联红十字医院的苏联专家们。
