Title: Automobile Production Task to be Completed Ahead of Schedule
Author:
Date: 1957-03-14
Page: 5

    Automobile Production Task to be Completed Ahead of Schedule
    Since the First Automobile Manufacturing Plant started production last year, the number of automobiles produced has reached more than half of the 4,000-automobile production target set by the First Five-Year Plan.
    The First Automobile Manufacturing Plant has preliminarily decided to produce 7,000 automobiles this year, and it is expected to complete the production task stipulated in the First Five-Year Plan half a year ahead of schedule, around June.
    This year, the factory will also manufacture parts needed to assemble 240 trolley buses for the capital and engine and chassis assemblies needed to modify 390 buses for the four cities of Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, and Harbin.

### 汽车生产任务将提前完成

1957-03-14
第5版()
专栏：

    汽车生产任务将提前完成
    第一汽车制造厂从去年开工生产以来到现在，所生产的汽车已经达到第一个五年计划规定生产四千辆汽车任务的一半以上。
    第一汽车制造厂今年初步决定生产七千辆汽车，大约在6月份可以提前半年完成第一个五年计划规定的生产任务。
    在今年，这个工厂还将制造供首都装配二百四十辆份无轨电车所需要的零件，和供北京、上海、天津、哈尔滨四个城市改制三百九十辆份公共汽车所需要的发动机及底盘总成。
