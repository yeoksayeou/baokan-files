Title: Turkey follows the US step by step, accepting the US plan to enslave the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1957-03-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Ankara, March 23rd: Turkey, a member of the Baghdad Pact, officially expressed its support for the "Eisenhower Doctrine" on the 22nd. This is the result of two days of talks between US presidential special envoy Richards and the Turkish government.

The communique stated that both parties agreed on some ways to provide economic and military assistance to Turkey.

### 土耳其对美国亦步亦趋  接受美国奴役中东计划

1957-03-25
第6版()
专栏：

　  土耳其对美国亦步亦趋
　  接受美国奴役中东计划
    据新华社23日讯　安卡拉消息：巴格达条约的成员国土耳其22日正式对“艾森豪威尔主义”表示支持。这是美国总统特使理查兹同土耳其政府进行了两天会谈的结果。
    公报说，双方商定了一些方式，给予土耳其的经济和军事援助。
