Title: Czechoslovak Government Delegation Visits Dujiangyan
Author:
Date: 1957-03-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, March 15th. Premier Viliam Široký and the entire Czechoslovak government delegation, accompanied by Sichuan Provincial Governor Li Dazhang, today visited the famous Dujiangyan irrigation project. The distinguished guests also visited the No. 1 Advanced Agricultural Cooperative in Xipu Township, Pixian County. In the afternoon, Vice Chairman Zhu De met with Premier Viliam Široký and held cordial talks.

### 捷政府代表团参观都江堰

1957-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

    捷政府代表团参观都江堰
    据新华社成都15日电  威廉·西罗基总理和捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团全体团员，今天由四川省省长李大章陪同参观了著名水利工程——都江堰。贵宾们还访问了郫县犀浦乡第一高级农业社。下午，朱德副主席会见了威廉·西罗基总理，进行了亲切的谈话。
