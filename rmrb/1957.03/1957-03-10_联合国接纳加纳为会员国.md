Title: The United Nations Accepts Ghana as a Member State
Author:
Date: 1957-03-10
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, New York, March 9th: The United Nations General Assembly unanimously adopted a resolution on the 8th, based on the recommendation of the Security Council, to admit Ghana as a member of the United Nations. With this, the number of United Nations member states has increased to eighty-one.

### 联合国接纳加纳为会员国

1957-03-10
第6版()
专栏：

    联合国接纳加纳为会员国
    据新华社9日讯　纽约消息：联合国大会根据安全理事会推荐在8日一致通过接纳加纳为联合国会员国。这样，联合国会员国就增加到了八十一国。
