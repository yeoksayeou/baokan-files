Title: National Gymnastics Competition Held in Beijing
Author: Shi Yuzhong
Date: 1957-03-23
Page: 4

　　National Gymnastics Competition Held in Beijing

　　This newspaper reports that the first national gymnastics competition of the year – the Seventeen Cities Gymnastics Individual Event Championship – began on the 21st at the Beijing Gymnasium.

　　The two hundred and forty male and female athletes participating in this competition, from seventeen cities, are divided into Group A and Group B according to their technical level for the competition. The competition stipulates that only optional routines will be performed, and the difficulty of the movements for Group A and Group B athletes must reach the standards of Master of Sport and First-Class Athlete, respectively.

　　The men's Group B events held the day before yesterday have concluded. The top three finishers are:

　　Floor Exercise: Yu Liefeng from Shanghai (9.25 points), Zheng Hongtao from Nanjing (9.25 points), Zhang Baogen from Beijing (9.05 points), Lü Zongguang from Shenyang (9.05 points).

　　Horizontal Bar: Zhou Tianzhou from Shanghai (9.15 points), Bo Yunxiao from Beijing (9 points), Zhang Baogen from Beijing (8.9 points).

　　Parallel Bars: Yu Liefeng from Shanghai (9.25 points), Gao Minquan from Nanjing (9.25 points), Lin Kai from Tianjin (9.25 points).

　　Pommel Horse: Liu Rongdi from Tianjin (9.15 points), Yu Liefeng from Shanghai (9.2 points), Lin Kai from Tianjin (9 points).

　　Rings: Yu Liefeng from Shanghai (9.15 points), Wang Zhenqing from Xi'an (9 points), Wang Mengkui from Beijing (9 points).

　　Vault (Long Horse): Yue Zhenxiang from Shanghai (9.6 points), Zheng Linxiang from Guangzhou (9.4 points), Zhang Baogen from Beijing (9.3 points).

　　The competition will last for three days and is scheduled to close on March 23rd.

　　The seventeen cities participating in the competition are: Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Chongqing, Wuhan, Shenyang, Xi'an, Nanjing, Changchun, Harbin, Hohhot, Hangzhou, Fuzhou, Baoding, Jinan, and Taiyuan.

　　This newspaper reports that in the women's Group B four-event competition held on the 22nd at the Seventeen Cities Gymnastics Individual Event Championship, Beijing athletes achieved the most outstanding results. They won the top four places in the balance beam, the top five places in the floor exercise, two first places in the uneven bars, and two second places in the vault.

　　The nineteen-year-old Beijing athlete, Huang Xinhe's floor exercise was particularly appealing to the audience. She was agile and supple, and all her movements contained rich emotion and graceful ballet poses. As she finished her routine, the entire audience applauded enthusiastically, indicating even before the judges that they wanted to give her a high score. She ultimately won the floor exercise championship with 9.6 points. Second and third places went to Wang Ping and Huang Meilin, both from Beijing. In the uneven bars and balance beam competitions, Huang Xinhe also surpassed her opponents with her light, rhythmic, and extremely skilled consecutive movements, winning two first places. Wang Ping from Beijing and Yin Yinfang from Nanjing tied with Huang Xinhe in score and jointly won first place in the uneven bars. Second place in the balance beam went to Chen Dexing from Beijing, and third place went to Ma Qiaoyun from Beijing.

　　The vault champion was Lu Huifang from Hohhot, and second place went to Huang Meilin and Chi Xinghua, both from Beijing. (Shi Yuzhong)

### 全国性体操比赛在北京举行
施毓忠
1957-03-23
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

　　全国性体操比赛在北京举行
    本报讯　今年第一次全国性体操比赛——十七城市体操单项锦标赛21日在北京体育馆开始举行。
    参加这次比赛的十七个城市的二百四十名男女运动员，他们都按照技术水平的不同，分成甲、乙两组进行比赛。大会规定这次只进行自选动作比赛，并且甲、乙组运动员的动作难度必须分别达到运动健将级和一级运动员的标准。
    前天举行的男子乙组各项比赛已经结束，获得前三名的是：
    自由体操：上海市于烈烽（9．25分）、南京市郑宏涛（9.25分）、北京市张宝根（9.05分）、沈阳市吕宗广（9.05分）。
    单杠：上海市周天骤（9.15分）、北京市薄云霄（9分）、北京市张宝根（8.9分）。
    双杠：上海市于烈烽（9.25分）、南京市高民权（9.25分）、天津市林楷（9.25分）。
    鞍马：天津市刘荣弟（9.15分）、上海市于烈烽（9.2分）、天津市林楷（9分）。
    吊环：上海市于烈烽（9.15分）、西安市王祯庆（9分）、北京市王孟奎（9分）。
    支撑跳跃（纵跳马）：上海市乐振祥（9.6分）、广州市郑粦祥（9.4分）、北京市张宝根（9.3分）。
    这次比赛要进行三天，预定于3月23日闭幕。
    参加比赛的十七个城市是：北京、天津、上海、广州、重庆、武汉、沈阳、西安、南京、长春、哈尔滨、呼和浩特、杭州、福州、保定、济南和太原。
    本报讯　在十七个城市体操单项锦标赛22日举行的女子乙组四项比赛中，北京市运动员的成绩最为突出。她们获得了平衡木前四名、自由体操前五名、高低杠两个第一名和横跳马两个第二名。
    十九岁的北京市运动员黄新河的自由体操特别吸引观众，她身体灵活，体态柔软，所有的动作都含有丰富的情感和优美的芭蕾舞姿，在她做完动作时，全体观众都在裁判员之先用热烈的掌声来表示要给予高额分数，她终于以九点六分获得了自由体操冠军，第二名和第三名是北京市王萍和黄美林。在高低杠和平衡木的比赛中，黄新河又以轻快而有节奏的接连极为圆熟的动作胜过了对手取得了两个第一名。与黄新河分数相等同时获得高低杠第一名的还有北京市王萍和南京市殷银芳。平衡木第二名为北京市陈德星所得，第三名是北京市马巧云。
    横跳马冠军为呼和浩特市陆慧舫占有，第二名是北京市黄美林和迟兴华。　（施毓忠）
