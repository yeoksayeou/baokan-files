Title: Ministry of Culture Holds Song and Dance Beijing Opera Gala to Welcome Czechoslovak Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-03-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 12th. Premier Viliam Široký and members of the Czechoslovak Republic Government Delegation attended a song and dance Beijing Opera gala tonight. This gala was held by the Ministry of Culture to welcome the Czechoslovak Republic Government Delegation. Accompanying the distinguished guests to watch the song and dance Beijing Opera performance were Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers He Long, Li Xiannian, Bo Yibo, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu, Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Ji Pengfei, Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Cao Ying, as well as heads of various departments of the government, democratic parties, and people's organizations.

Diplomatic envoys of various countries to China and Czechoslovak experts and students in Beijing also attended the gala.

### 文化部举行歌舞京剧晚会欢迎捷政府代表团

1957-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

    文化部举行歌舞京剧晚会欢迎捷政府代表团
    新华社12日讯　威廉·西罗基总理和捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团团员，今天晚上出席了歌舞京剧晚会。这个晚会是文化部为了欢迎捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团而举行的。陪同贵宾们观看歌舞京剧演出的，有国务院总理周恩来、副总理贺龙、李先念、薄一波，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长彭真、陈叔通，最高人民法院院长董必武，国务院秘书长习仲勋，文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长章汉夫、姬鹏飞，中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使曹瑛，以及政府各部门、各民主党派和各人民团体的负责人。
    各国驻我国的外交使节以及在京的捷克斯洛伐克的专家、留学生也出席了晚会。
