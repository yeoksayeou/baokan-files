Title: Women from All Circles in Hong Kong and Macau Gather to Commemorate "March 8th" International Women's Day
Author:
Date: 1957-03-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, 10th - Women from all walks of life in Hong Kong and Macau have gathered around March 8th to commemorate "March 8th" International Women's Day.

    At the tea party held by the Hong Kong and Kowloon Federation of Trade Unions on March 5th to celebrate "March 8th" for female worker representatives from various trade unions, the female workers spoke with excitement about the continuous emergence of female people's representatives, female factory directors, female cooperative chairpersons, and female scientists in the motherland, as well as the situation of women working alongside men in various construction positions in the motherland to devote themselves to socialist construction and living a stable and happy life. At the meeting, the female workers also expressed great concern for the tragic lives of compatriots and women in Taiwan. They hope that the Kuomintang people in Taiwan will respond to the motherland's call for the peaceful liberation of Taiwan and make contributions to the cause of the motherland's reunification.
    The Hong Kong and Kowloon Women's Welfare Association also jointly held a celebration rally on the 8th.
    Women from all circles in Macau held a celebration rally with more than 1,500 participants on "March 8th." Zhang Qinghui, the chairman of the conference, spoke at the meeting, hoping that women from all walks of life in Macau will continue to expand unity and develop women's and children's welfare undertakings in the future. She also stated that Macau women are very sympathetic to the current situation of women in Taiwan. She hopes that women will put forth their strength to promote the early peaceful liberation of Taiwan.
    Shops, theaters, bookstores, and buses in Macau also specially offered discounted or free services to women on March 8th.

### 香港澳门各界妇女纷纷集会纪念“三八”节

1957-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    香港澳门各界妇女纷纷集会纪念“三八”节
    新华社香港10日电　香港和澳门的各界妇女在3月8日前后纷纷集会，纪念“三八”国际妇女节。
    在港九工会联合会3月5日举行的各工会女工代表庆祝“三八”节茶叙会上，女工们怀着兴奋的心情谈到了祖国不断涌现出女人民代表、女厂长、女社长和女科学家的情况，以及妇女在祖国各个建设岗位上和男子一起致力于社会主义建设事业，过着安定而愉快的生活的情形。会上女工们还对台湾同胞和台湾妇女所过的悲惨生活表示很大的关怀。她们希望在台湾的国民党人，响应祖国和平解放台湾的号召，为祖国的统一事业立下功劳。
    港九各区妇女福利会在8日也联合举行了联欢大会。
    澳门各界妇女在“三八”节那天举行了一个有一千五百多人参加的庆祝大会。大会主席张晴晖在会上发言，希望澳门各阶层妇女今后继续扩大团结和发展妇女儿童福利事业。她还表示，澳门妇女对台湾妇女今天的处境非常同情。她希望妇女们拿出力量来，促使早日和平解放台湾。
    澳门的商店、戏院、书店和公共汽车，还特别在3月8日那天廉价或免费招待妇女。
