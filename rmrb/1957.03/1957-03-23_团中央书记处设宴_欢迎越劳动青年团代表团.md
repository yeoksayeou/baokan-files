Title: Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Holds Banquet to Welcome Delegation of the Vietnam Labor Youth Union

Author:

Date: 1957-03-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 22nd: The Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League held a banquet this evening to welcome all members of the Vietnam Labor Youth Union delegation who are here to investigate the work of the Youth League. Present at the banquet were Hu Yaobang and Hu Keshi, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Youth League, responsible persons from various departments of the Central Committee of the Youth League, and Wang Zhaohua, First Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Youth League.

### 团中央书记处设宴　欢迎越劳动青年团代表团

1957-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

    团中央书记处设宴
　　欢迎越劳动青年团代表团
    新华社22日讯　中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处今晚设宴欢迎前来我国考察青年团工作的越南劳动青年团代表团全体成员。出席作陪的有青年团中央书记处书记胡耀邦、胡克实，青年团中央各部负责人和青年团北京市委员会第一书记王照华。
