Title: Egypt to Build Large Reservoir
Author: 
Date: 1957-03-07
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 5th: The Egyptian government will build a large reservoir in the Natron Valley, between Cairo and Alexandria, to contain the floodwaters of the Nile River.
    The governor of Liberation Province announced on the 3rd that using the water stored in this reservoir for irrigation will turn 1.2 million feddans (one feddan equals 6.3 Chinese mu) of land into fertile fields, increasing Egypt's national income by 50 million Egyptian pounds annually. The cost of the reservoir project is estimated at approximately 18 million Egyptian pounds.
    The project is scheduled to begin on July 1st of this year and is expected to be completed within four to five years.

### 埃及将建一大水库

1957-03-07
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及将建一大水库
    新华社开罗5日电　埃及政府将在开罗和亚历山大港之间的纳特龙山谷修建一座巨大的水库，以拦蓄尼罗河的洪水。
    解放省行政长官3日宣布，利用这座水库贮蓄的水进行灌溉，将能使一百二十万费丹（一费丹合六点三华亩）的土地变为良田，每年将给埃及的国民收入增加五千万埃镑。水库工程的费用估计约需一千八百万埃镑。
    这项工程计划将于今年7月1日开始执行，预定在四、五年内完成。
