Title: Mongolia Raises Purchase Prices for Livestock Products
Author:
Date: 1957-03-02
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 1st. The Mongolian Council of Ministers and the Central Committee of the People's Revolutionary Party have decided to raise the purchase prices of livestock products starting from March 1st. According to this decision, the average purchase price of sheepskin will be increased by 75.5%, and the purchase prices of horsehide, cowhide, etc., will also be increased.
    This is the third measure taken by the Mongolian government and party this year to improve the material life of the people. Prior to this, the prices of medicines and more than 400 kinds of daily necessities had been reduced. The Mongolian "Unen" published an editorial on February 28, saying that this measure is the result of increasing labor productivity and reducing administrative expenses in the second five-year plan (1953-1957) for developing the national economy.

### 蒙古提高畜产品收购价格

1957-03-02
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古提高畜产品收购价格
    据新华社乌兰巴托1日电  蒙古部长会议和人民革命党中央委员会决定从3月1日起提高畜产品的收购价格。根据这项决定，绵羊皮的收购价格平均将提高75.5％，马皮、牛皮等的收购价格也都提高了。
    这是蒙古政府和党为提高人民的物质生活在今年采取的第三项措施，在这以前曾经降低过药品和四百多种日用品的价格。蒙古“真理报”2月28日发表社论说，这项措施是由于在发展国民经济的第二个五年计划（1953—1957年）中提高劳动生产率和减缩行政费用的结果。
