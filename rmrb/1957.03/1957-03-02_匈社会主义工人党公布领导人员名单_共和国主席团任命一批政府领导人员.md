Title: Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party Announces Leadership List, Presidential Council Appoints Government Leaders

Author:

Date: 1957-03-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, March 1st: The plenary session of the Provisional Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, held on February 26th, after re-electing members of the leadership, has increased the number of members of the Provisional Central Committee to thirty-seven, and the number of members of the Executive Committee to ten.

The members of the Executive Committee are: Apro Antal, Biszku Bela, Feher Lajos, Kadar Janos, Kallai Gyula, Kish Karoly, Marosan Gyorgy, Munnich Ferenc, Ronai Sandor, Szamosi Miklos.

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, March 1st: An official announcement was published here on February 28th stating that the Presidential Council of the Hungarian People's Republic appointed Munnich Ferenc as First Deputy Prime Minister of the Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government, Biszku Bela as Minister of Interior, Frigyes Doleschall as Minister of Health, Kallai Gyula as Minister of Culture, and Geza Revesz as Minister of Defense.

The Presidential Council of the Hungarian People's Republic relieved Ronai Sandor, Chairman of the National Assembly, of his position as a member of the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government.

The new government personnel were sworn into office on February 28th.

### 匈社会主义工人党公布领导人员名单  共和国主席团任命一批政府领导人员

1957-03-02
第5版()
专栏：

    匈社会主义工人党公布领导人员名单
    共和国主席团任命一批政府领导人员
    新华社布达佩斯1日电  匈牙利社会主义工人党临时中央委员会2月26日举行的全体会议，在补选了领导机构的成员以后，临时中央委员会委员已经增加到三十七人，执行委员会委员增加到十人。
    执行委员会的成员是：奥普罗·安托尔，比斯库·贝洛，费赫尔·拉约什，卡达尔·亚诺什，卡拉伊·久洛，基什·卡罗伊，马罗山·捷尔吉，明尼赫·费伦茨，罗奈伊·山多尔，绍莫吉·米克洛什。
    新华社布达佩斯1日电  2月28日这里发表正式公告说，匈牙利人民共和国主席团任命明尼赫·费伦茨为工农革命政府第一副总理，比斯库·贝洛为内务部长，弗里吉斯·多利夏尔为卫生部长，卡拉伊·久洛为文化部长，格佐·雷维斯为国防部长。
    匈牙利人民共和国主席团免除国民议会主席罗奈伊·山多尔的匈牙利工农革命政府委员的职务。
    政府的新人员已经在2月28日宣誓就职。
