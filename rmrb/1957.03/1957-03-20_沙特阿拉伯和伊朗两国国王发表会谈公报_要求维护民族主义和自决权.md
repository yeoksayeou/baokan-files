Title: Saudi Arabia and Iran Jointly Issue Communiqué Calling for Safeguarding Nationalism and the Right to Self-Determination
Author: 
Date: 1957-03-20
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, March 19th, Riyadh Dispatch: Following talks held in Riyadh, the capital of Saudi Arabia, between Saudi Arabian King Saud and Iranian King Pahlavi, a joint communiqué was issued on the 18th. The communiqué called on all countries to respect and safeguard nationalism and the right to self-determination against imperialist policies.

The communiqué stated that after discussing international issues, particularly those related to the Middle East, the two Kings unanimously agreed on the following:

    1. A peaceful resolution to the Middle East issue depends on resolving the Palestinian issue and restoring the rights of the Palestinian Arabs;
    2. All international issues should be resolved peacefully in accordance with the United Nations Charter;
    3. The unity of Muslim countries should be strengthened;
    4. The two Kings are satisfied with the achievements of the United Nations' efforts during the recent Middle East crisis;
    5. The two Kings agreed that nationalism and the right to self-determination against imperialist policies should be respected and safeguarded by all countries.

The Iranian King arrived in Riyadh on the 17th. Prior to arriving in Riyadh, he had visited the Islamic holy cities of Mecca and Medina for three days. The Iranian King left Saudi Arabia on the 18th and returned to Tehran.

### 沙特阿拉伯和伊朗两国国王发表会谈公报  要求维护民族主义和自决权

1957-03-20
第7版()
专栏：

    沙特阿拉伯和伊朗两国国王发表会谈公报
    要求维护民族主义和自决权
    新华社19日讯　利雅得消息：沙特阿拉伯国王沙特和伊朗国王巴列维在沙特阿拉伯首都利雅得举行会谈后，在18日发表了联合公报。公报中要求各国尊重和维护反对帝国主义政策的民族主义和自决权。
    公报说，两位国王就国际问题特别是中东问题会谈以后，一致认为：
    一、中东问题的和平解决取决于巴勒斯坦问题的解决和恢复巴勒斯坦阿拉伯人的权利；
    二、一切国际问题应该依照联合国宪章和平解决；
    三、应该加强穆斯林国家的团结；
    四、两位国王对联合国在最近的中东危机期间所作的努力的成绩感到满意；
    五、两位国王同意：反对帝国主义政策的民族主义和自决权应当得到各国的尊重和维护。
    伊朗国王是在17日到达利雅得的。他在到利雅得之前曾经在伊斯兰教的圣地麦加和麦地那访问了三天。伊朗国王在18日离开沙特阿拉伯回到了德黑兰。
