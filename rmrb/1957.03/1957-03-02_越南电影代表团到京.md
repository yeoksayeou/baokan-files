Title: Vietnamese Film Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-03-02
Page: 1

    Vietnamese Film Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, 28th – A five-member delegation of film workers from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, headed by Le Nguyen, arrived in Beijing by train tonight.
