Title: Over 20,000 Jin of Manure Dug from a Pigeon Hole
Author:
Date: 1957-03-27
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Xining, 19th – Ma Erbu Du (Hui nationality), a 17-year-old Youth League member of the No. 281 Agricultural Cooperative in Yaocao Township, Datong County, Qinghai Province, recently dug out more than 20,000 jin of pigeon manure from a pigeon hole in the mountains.

### 一个鸽子洞掏出两万多斤粪

1957-03-27
第3版()
专栏：

　  一个鸽子洞掏出两万多斤粪
    据新华社西宁19日电　青海省大通县药草乡二八一农业社的一位十七岁的青年团员马二卜都（回族），最近在山上的一个鸽子洞里掏出了二万多斤鸽子粪。
