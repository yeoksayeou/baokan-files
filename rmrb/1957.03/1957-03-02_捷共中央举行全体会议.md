Title: CPCz Central Committee of Czechoslovakia Holds Plenary Session
Author:
Date: 1957-03-02
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 1st: According to the Czechoslovak News Agency, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia held a plenary session in Prague on February 27th and 28th.
    The First Secretary of the CPCz Central Committee, Novotný, delivered a summary report at the meeting on the talks held by the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia with the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Communist and Workers' Parties of other countries in recent months.
    The plenary session discussed issues related to improving the effectiveness of the national economy. Ďuranský delivered a report on this issue. After the discussion, the meeting adopted corresponding resolutions.

### 捷共中央举行全体会议

1957-03-02
第5版()
专栏：

    捷共中央举行全体会议
    新华社布拉格1日电  据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道：捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会2月27日和28日在布拉格举行了全体会议。
    捷共中央第一书记诺沃提尼在会上作了捷克斯洛伐克共产党最近几个月来同苏联共产党和其他国家共产党、工人党举行会谈情况的总结报告。
    全体会议讨论了同提高国民经济效果有关的问题。杜兰斯基就这个问题作了报告。在讨论结束以后，会议通过了相应的决议。
