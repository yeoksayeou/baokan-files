Title: "Acta Mechanica Sinica" Launched

Author: 
Date: 1957-03-27
Page: 7

    "Acta Mechanica Sinica" Launched
    (Xinhua News Agency) The inaugural issue of "Acta Mechanica Sinica," edited by mechanician Qian Xuesen, was published on the 22nd. The inaugural issue features papers such as "Vorticity Structure of Homogeneous Isotropic Turbulence in the Late Stage of Decay" by Zhou Peiyuan and Cai Shutang, and "Aerodynamic Problems of Long Turbine Blades in Turbomachinery" by Wu Zhonghua.
      In addition to publishing research papers and research notes, "Acta Mechanica Sinica" will also publish articles on paper discussions and book reviews in the future.
    Thirty-one people, including mechanicians Zhou Peiyuan and Cai Fangyin, serve as members of the editorial board.

### “力学学报”创刊

1957-03-27
第7版()
专栏：

    “力学学报”创刊
    据新华社讯　力学家钱学森主编的“力学学报”创刊号已在22日出版。在创刊号中，刊有周培源、蔡树棠的“均匀各向同性湍流在后期衰变时的涡性结构”。吴仲华的“透平机械长叶片气体动力学问题”等论文。
      “力学学报”除刊登研究论文和研究简报外，今后还将刊登论文讨论和书评等方面的文章。
    力学家周培源、蔡方荫等三十一人担任编辑委员会的委员。
