Title: Further Strengthening the Work of Local CPPCCs: CPPCC National Committee Holds Symposium for Discussion
Author:
Date: 1957-03-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd—The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held a symposium in Beijing to discuss issues related to further strengthening the work of local political consultation.

    The symposium, which began on the 21st, concluded today. At the meeting, the heads of CPPCC local committees from various regions, based on the specific circumstances of their localities, offered opinions and suggestions on various issues related to further developing the work of local CPPCCs. The meeting held that local CPPCC organizations at all levels should further expand democratic life, fully promote the spirit of consultation, actively participate in consultations on various important local measures and relevant policies and guidelines, actively play a "mutual supervision" role, more broadly connect with people from all walks of life, further organize political and theoretical study for people from all walks of life, and enrich and improve the daily work of the various working groups of local CPPCC organizations.

    The symposium was presided over by Zhang Bojun, Vice Chairman of the National Committee and Director of the Local Work Committee of the National Committee. Zhang Bojun and Xing Xiping, Secretary-General of the National Committee, both spoke at the meeting.

    Attending the meeting were chairmen, vice-chairmen, secretaries-general, or standing committee members of CPPCC local committees from twenty-two provinces, two autonomous regions, three municipalities directly under the central government, and one hundred and forty-six cities, as well as deputy directors, members of the Local Work Committee of the CPPCC National Committee, and heads of other departments of the National Committee, totaling more than 250 people.

### 进一步加强地方政协的工作　政协全国委员会召开座谈会进行了讨论

1957-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　进一步加强地方政协的工作
　　政协全国委员会召开座谈会进行了讨论
    新华社23日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会在北京举行座谈会，讨论了关于进一步加强地方政治协商工作等问题。
    座谈会是在21日开始的，于今天结束。在会上，来自各地的政协地方委员会负责人根据各地的具体情况，对有关进一步开展地方政协工作的各项问题，提出了意见和建议。会议认为，政协各地方组织应进一步扩大民主生活，充分发扬协商精神，积极参与当地各种重要措施和有关政策、方针问题的协商，积极发挥“互相监督”作用，更加广泛地联系社会各界人士，进一步组织各界人士的政治理论学习，充实和改进各地政协组织的各个工作组的日常工作。
    座谈会由全国委员会副主席、全国委员会地方工作委员会主任委员章伯钧主持。章伯钧和全国委员会秘书长邢西萍都在会上讲了话。
    出席这次会议的，有全国二十二个省、两个自治区、三个直辖市和一百四十六个市的政协地方委员会主席、副主席、秘书长或常务委员，政协全国委员会地方工作委员会副主任委员、委员以及全国委员会其他部门负责人，共二百五十多人。
