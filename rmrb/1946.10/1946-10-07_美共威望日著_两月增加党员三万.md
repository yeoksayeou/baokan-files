Title: The Rising Prestige of the American Communist Party: Membership Increases by 30,000 in Two Months
Author:
Date: 1946-10-07
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Yan'an, 4th: New York dispatch: The prestige of the American Communist Party among the people is growing daily. It has been announced that there are already 100,000 party members, whereas in July of this year, the American Communist Party had 70,000 members, an increase of 30,000 members in two months. The American Communist Party not only has a broad influence in the labor movement, but also in certain newspapers, magazines, publishing, broadcasting, and film industries.

### 美共威望日著  两月增加党员三万

1946-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　美共威望日著
    两月增加党员三万
    【新华社延安四日电】纽约讯：美国共产党在人民中的威信日益增长。据宣布已有党员十万人，而在今年七月美共党员计有七万人，两月之中增加党员三万人。美共不仅在工人运动中，且在若干报纸、杂志、出版、广播与电影各业中，均有广泛影响。
