Title: Celebrating the Establishment of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Revolutionary Committee
Author:
Date: 1968-08-28
Edition: 2

On the joyous occasion of the establishment of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Revolutionary Committee, the revolutionary people of all ethnic groups in Guangxi are overjoyed and singing loudly. They sincerely wish our great leader Chairman Mao a long, long life! Long, long life! Long, long life!

	New China News Agency photographer (telephoto)
