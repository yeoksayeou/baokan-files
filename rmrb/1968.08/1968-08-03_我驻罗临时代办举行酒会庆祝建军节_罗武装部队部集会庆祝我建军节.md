Title: Chinese Chargé d'Affaires Hosts Reception to Celebrate Army Day
Author:
Date: 1968-08-03
Edition: 5

	Chinese Chargé d'Affaires Hosts Reception to Celebrate Army Day
	Romanian Ministry of Defense Holds Meeting to Celebrate Chinese Army Day

	BEIJING, Aug. 1 (Xinhua) — Chinese Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Ma Xusheng hosted a reception on July 31 to celebrate the 41st anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

	Attendees included Romanian Deputy Foreign Minister Marin, Romanian Deputy Minister of Defense Burca, Romanian military officers, and representatives from various sectors.

	Diplomatic envoys and military attachés from several countries in Romania were also invited to the reception.

	BEIJING, Aug. 1 (Xinhua) — The Romanian Ministry of Defense held a meeting on July 29 at the "Soldier's Home" in Bucharest to celebrate the 41st anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

	Romanian First Deputy Chief of the General Staff Orban and 300 other officers attended the meeting.

	Chinese Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Ma Xusheng and staff from the Chinese Embassy in Romania were invited to the meeting.

	Orban, the First Deputy Chief of the General Staff, and Ma Xusheng, the Chargé d'Affaires ad interim, both spoke at the meeting.

### 我驻罗临时代办举行酒会庆祝建军节  罗武装部队部集会庆祝我建军节

1968-08-03
第5版()
专栏：

　　我驻罗临时代办举行酒会庆祝建军节
　　罗武装部队部集会庆祝我建军节
    新华社布加勒斯特一日电　中国驻罗马尼亚大使馆临时代办马叙生七月三十一日晚举行酒会，热烈庆祝中国人民解放军建军四十一周年。
    出席酒会的有：罗马尼亚外交部副部长马林，罗马尼亚武装部队部副部长布尔卡，罗马尼亚武装部队的军官以及各界代表。
    一些国家驻罗马尼亚的外交使节和武官也应邀出席了酒会。
    新华社布加勒斯特一日电　罗马尼亚武装部队部七月二十九日晚在布加勒斯特“军人之家”举行集会，庆祝中国人民解放军建军四十一周年。
    罗马尼亚武装部队第一副总参谋长奥尔班等三百名军官出席了集会。
    中国驻罗马尼亚大使馆临时代办马叙生以及使馆的工作人员应邀出席了集会。
    奥尔班第一副总参谋长和马叙生代办先后在集会上讲了话。
