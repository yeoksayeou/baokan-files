Title: Young Intellectuals Settle in Heilongjiang
Author:
Date: 1968-08-21
Edition: 4

In Huade County, Jilin Province, at the Fangshen Gangzi Production Brigade of the Nanweizi Commune, intellectual youth Guo Guizhen (front right) is engaging in a discussion with poor and lower-middle peasants in the fields, sharing experiences on criticizing and repudiating revisionism.
