Title: The Working Class Must Lead Everything
Author:
Date: 1968-08-27
Edition: 2

The single-volume edition of "The Working Class Must Lead Everything" has been published.

	New China News Agency, August 26th

	The important article "The Working Class Must Lead Everything" by Comrade Yao Wenyuan, published in the second issue of 1968 of "Red Flag" magazine, conveys our great leader Chairman Mao's extremely important latest instructions. This article has been published as a single-volume edition by the People's Publishing House and will begin to be distributed in the Beijing New China Bookstore on August 27th. It will also be distributed in New China Bookstores across the country in the near future.

### 《工人阶级必须领导一切》单行本出版

1968-08-27
第2版()
专栏：

    《工人阶级必须领导一切》单行本出版
    新华社二十六日讯　《红旗》杂志一九六八年第二期发表的姚文元同志的重要文章《工人阶级必须领导一切》，传达了我们伟大领袖毛主席的极为重要的最新指示。这篇文章，已由人民出版社出版单行本，八月二十七日开始在北京新华书店发行，并将在全国各地新华书店陆续发行。
