Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Cambodian Friends
Author:
Date: 1968-08-22
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi met with the Cambodian friends today evening. The Cambodian delegation, led by President of the National Assembly of the Kingdom of Cambodia, Mr. Khieu Kimsun, held friendly and cordial talks with Chen Yi. Present at the meeting were Chinese officials Han Nianlong and Ding Xilin. The Chargé d'Affaires of the Cambodian Embassy in China, Mr. Touk Doung, was also present.

After the meeting, Vice Premier Chen Yi hosted a banquet in honor of the Cambodian friends.

The Cambodian delegation arrived in Beijing on August 20th on their way back home after visiting the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

### 陈毅副总理接见柬埔寨朋友

1968-08-22
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见柬埔寨朋友
    新华社二十一日讯　国务院副总理陈毅，今天晚上接见了以柬埔寨王国议会主席郭基贤为首的柬埔寨王国议会代表团，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时，我国有关方面负责人韩念龙、丁西林在座。柬埔寨驻中国大使馆临时代办杜克武提也在座。
    接见以后，陈毅副总理设宴招待柬埔寨朋友。
    柬埔寨王国议会代表团是访问朝鲜民主主义人民共和国以后，在回国途中于二十日到达北京的。
