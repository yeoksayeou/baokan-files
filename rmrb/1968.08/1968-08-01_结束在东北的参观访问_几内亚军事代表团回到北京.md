Title: Guinean Military Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Northeast
Author:
Date: 1968-08-01
Edition: 6

	The Guinean military delegation, led by Colonel Kamman Diaby, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the National People's Army of the Republic of Guinea, concluded their visit to Northeast China today and returned to Beijing by plane.

	The Guinean military delegation left Beijing on the 26th to visit Northeast China. During their stay, they visited factories, military farms, and suburban people's communes in Shenyang, Harbin, and Changchun. They also watched military performances by the militia and revolutionary cultural programs. The Guinean guests were warmly welcomed by the Chinese People's Liberation Army soldiers and revolutionary masses wherever they went.

	The Chargé d'Affaires of the Embassy of the Republic of Guinea in China, Kamara, accompanied the Guinean military delegation on their visit to Northeast China.

### 结束在东北的参观访问　几内亚军事代表团回到北京

1968-08-01
第6版()
专栏：

　　结束在东北的参观访问
　　几内亚军事代表团回到北京
    新华社三十一日讯　由几内亚共和国国家人民军副总参谋长卡曼·迪亚比上校率领的几内亚军事代表团，今天结束了在我国东北的参观访问，乘飞机回到北京。
    几内亚军事代表团是在二十六日离开北京去东北参观访问的。代表团在东北期间，先后在沈阳、哈尔滨、长春等地参观访问了工厂、军垦农场和郊区人民公社，并观看了民兵的军事表演和革命文艺节目。几内亚客人所到之处，受到了中国人民解放军指战员和革命群众的热烈欢迎。
    几内亚共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办卡马拉陪同几内亚军事代表团到东北参观访问。
