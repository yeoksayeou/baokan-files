Title: Each Mango Weighs a Thousand Jin
Author: Lǐ Zhēng Shī Xìng
Date: 1968-08-08
Edition: 6

	Hot tears well up,
	Cheers echo like thunder.
	In our hands, we hold the precious gift sent by Chairman Mao,
	Each mango weighs a thousand jin!

	Each mango weighs a thousand jin!
	This is the greatest care from our great leader!
	Chairman Mao is always in our hearts,
	Our spirits surge like a mighty tide.
	The revolutionary army is unstoppable,
	We follow closely behind the supreme commander!

	Each mango weighs a thousand jin!
	This is the greatest trust from our great leader!
	The revolutionary vanguard bears the heavy burden,
	Fighting spirit soaring, spirits high.
	We will not disappoint Chairman Mao's earnest expectations,
	Marching forward, never stopping!

	Each mango weighs a thousand jin!
	This is the greatest support from our great leader!
	Firmly grasp the most powerful ideological weapon,
	Smash the plots of the class enemy.
	Unite in will, unite in action,
	Resolutely implement Chairman Mao's latest directives!

	Each mango weighs a thousand jin!
	This is the greatest encouragement from our great leader!
	Face all hardships and dangers,
	Follow Chairman Mao's great strategic deployment.
	Red flags fly high, dancing in the sky,
	Forever loyal to the proletarian headquarters led by Chairman Mao!
