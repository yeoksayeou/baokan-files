Title: Comrade Kang Sheng and Comrade Yao Wenyuan Meet with the Italian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist) Delegation and Have a Very Cordial Conversation with Comrades Osvaldo Becchi and Dino Dini
Author:
Date: 1968-08-14
Edition: 1

	Kang Sheng, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and advisor to the Central Cultural Revolution Group, and Yao Wenyuan, a member of the Central Cultural Revolution Group, met and hosted a banquet for the delegation from the Italian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist) on August 10. The delegation was composed of Osvaldo Becchi, a member of the Political Bureau and the Secretariat of the Central Committee, and Dino Dini, a member of the Political Bureau. They had a very cordial conversation.

### 康生和姚文元同志　会见意共（马克思列宁主义）代表团　同奥斯瓦尔多·贝契和迪诺·迪尼同志进行了极其亲切的交谈

1968-08-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　康生和姚文元同志
　　会见意共（马克思列宁主义）代表团
　　同奥斯瓦尔多·贝契和迪诺·迪尼同志进行了极其亲切的交谈
    新华社十三日讯　中国共产党中央委员会政治局常委、中央文化革命小组顾问康生同志和中央文化革命小组成员姚文元同志，十日会见并宴请了由意大利共产党（马克思列宁主义）中央委员会政治局委员、书记处书记奥斯瓦尔多·贝契和政治局委员迪诺·迪尼同志组成的访华代表团，同他们进行了极其亲切的交谈。
