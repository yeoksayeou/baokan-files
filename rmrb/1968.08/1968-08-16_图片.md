Title: Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin Receive Representatives of the Capital's Working Class and PLA Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1968-08-16
Edition: 5

Chairman Mao, the sun in our hearts, and his close comrade-in-arms, Vice Chairman Lin, received representatives of the capital's working class and soldiers and officers of the People's Liberation Army. This is the greatest care, the greatest trust, the greatest education, and the greatest support for the working class and the military and civilian population of the entire nation.
