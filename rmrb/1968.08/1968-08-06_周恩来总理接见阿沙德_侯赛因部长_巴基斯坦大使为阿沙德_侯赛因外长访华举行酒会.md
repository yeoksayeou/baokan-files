Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Minister Asad Husain, Pakistan Ambassador Holds Reception for Asad Husain's Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1968-08-06
Edition: 1

Premier Zhou Enlai today received Pakistan's Foreign Minister Asad Husain and his wife, as well as other accompanying personnel. Premier Zhou Enlai had a friendly and cordial conversation with Minister Asad Husain.

Present at the reception were Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qian Yechen, and others.

Pakistan's Ambassador to China, Sultan Muhammad Khan, was also present at the reception.

New China News Agency, August 5th: In honor of Pakistan's Foreign Minister Asad Husain and his wife's friendly visit to China, Pakistan's Ambassador to China, Sultan Muhammad Khan, held a reception tonight.

Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, and relevant officials from the government's departments, the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee, and other relevant parties, including Qian Yechen, Xie Huai-de, Jia Shi, Zhou Rongguo, representatives of the Five Good Soldiers of the Beijing-based troops, and responsible members of various revolutionary mass organizations in Beijing, were invited to attend the reception.

The reception was held in an atmosphere filled with the friendship between the peoples of China and Pakistan.

Ambassadors from various countries to China also attended the reception.

### 周恩来总理接见阿沙德·侯赛因部长　巴基斯坦大使为阿沙德·侯赛因外长访华举行酒会

1968-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　周恩来总理接见阿沙德·侯赛因部长
　　巴基斯坦大使为阿沙德·侯赛因外长访华举行酒会
    新华社五日讯　国务院总理周恩来，今天接见了巴基斯坦外交部长阿沙德·侯赛因和夫人以及其他随行人员。周恩来总理同阿沙德·侯赛因部长进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅，外交部副部长韩念龙等。
    接见时，巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆·汗也在座。
    新华社五日讯　为巴基斯坦外交部长阿沙德·侯赛因和夫人来中国进行友好访问，巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆·汗今天晚上举行酒会。
    国务院总理周恩来，副总理兼外交部长陈毅，政府有关部门、北京市革命委员会等有关方面的负责人韩念龙、谢怀德、贾石、周荣国，驻北京部队五好战士代表，北京市各革命群众组织的负责成员，应邀出席了酒会。
    酒会在充满中国和巴基斯坦两国人民友好的气氛中进行。
    各国驻中国使节也出席了酒会。
