Title: Welcoming the Pakistani Foreign Minister Visiting Our Country
Author:
Date: 1968-08-06
Edition: 5

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs held an evening of artistic performances today to welcome the Pakistani Foreign Minister, Asad Hussain, who is visiting our country for a friendly visit.

Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi and other relevant officials accompanied Asad Hussain, the Foreign Minister, and his wife, along with other accompanying personnel, to watch the Revolutionary Modern Ballet "The Red Detachment of Women" performed by the Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers Ballet Troupe.

The Pakistani Ambassador to China, Sultan M. Khan, and diplomatic personnel from the embassy, as well as other Pakistani friends in Beijing, attended the evening performance.

After the performance, Vice Premier Chen Yi accompanied Foreign Minister Hussain and others onto the stage to warmly shake hands with the performers and congratulate them on their successful performance.

On August 5, Premier Zhou Enlai received Pakistani Foreign Minister Asad Hussain and his wife, along with other accompanying personnel.

### 欢迎来我国访问的巴基斯坦外交部长　我外交部举行文艺晚会　国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅等出席晚会

1968-08-06
第5版()
专栏：

　　欢迎来我国访问的巴基斯坦外交部长
　　我外交部举行文艺晚会
　　国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅等出席晚会
    新华社五日讯　外交部今天举行文艺晚会，欢迎前来我国进行友好访问的巴基斯坦外交部长阿沙德·侯赛因。
    国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅和其他有关方面负责人，陪同阿沙德·侯赛因部长和夫人以及其他随行人员，观看了工农兵芭蕾舞剧团演出的革命现代芭蕾舞剧《红色娘子军》。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆·汗和大使馆外交官员，以及在北京的其他巴基斯坦朋友，出席了晚会。
    演出结束后，陈毅副总理陪同侯赛因部长等走上舞台，和演员们热烈握手，祝贺他们演出成功。（附图片）
    国务院总理周恩来八月五日接见了巴基斯坦外交部长阿沙德·侯赛因和夫人，以及其他随行人员。
