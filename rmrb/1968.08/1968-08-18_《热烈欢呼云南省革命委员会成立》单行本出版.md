Title: Warmly Congratulate the Establishment of the Yunnan Provincial Revolutionary Committee
Author:
Date: 1968-08-18
Edition: 2

The single-volume edition of "Warmly Congratulate the Establishment of the Yunnan Provincial Revolutionary Committee" has been published. The editorial from the People's Daily and the Liberation Army Daily, which conveys the great leader Chairman Mao's extremely important latest instructions, has been published as a single-volume edition by the People's Publishing House. It will begin distribution in Beijing on August 18 and will be distributed across the country in due course.

### 《热烈欢呼云南省革命委员会成立》单行本出版

1968-08-18
第2版()
专栏：

    《热烈欢呼云南省革命委员会成立》单行本出版
    新华社十六日讯　传达了伟大领袖毛主席极为重要的最新指示的《人民日报》、《解放军报》社论《热烈欢呼云南省革命委员会成立》，已由人民出版社出版单行本，八月十八日开始在北京发行，并将在全国各地陆续发行。
