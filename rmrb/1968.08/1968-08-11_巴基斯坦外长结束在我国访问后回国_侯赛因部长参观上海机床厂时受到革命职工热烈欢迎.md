Title: Pakistani Foreign Minister Ends Visit to China and Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1968-08-11
Edition: 5

Pakistani Foreign Minister Ashad Hussain and his wife, along with other accompanying personnel, concluded their friendly visit to China and departed from Shanghai by airplane this afternoon, heading back to their country via Guangzhou.

Leaders of the Shanghai Revolutionary Committee, including Wang Shaoyong, Ji Yingbai, and Feng Guozhu, as well as hundreds of representatives and revolutionary masses from Shanghai's revolutionary mass organizations, gathered at the airport to bid them farewell. Han Nianlong, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, who accompanied Hussain to Shanghai, also went to the airport to see them off.

Hussain arrived in Shanghai from Hangzhou on the 9th. That evening, the Shanghai Revolutionary Committee held a banquet to warmly welcome the Pakistani friends. Wang Shaoyong and Hussain both spoke at the banquet, expressing their wishes for the further consolidation and development of the friendship between the peoples of China and Pakistan. Wang Shaoyong said that Hussain and his wife's visit to China this time has made new contributions to Sino-Pakistani friendship and the friendly cooperative relations between the two countries. We, the people of Shanghai, along with the people of the entire country, admire the efforts made by the Pakistani government to safeguard national sovereignty and oppose foreign aggression, and the principled stance of the Pakistani government and people in maintaining the development of friendly relations with China.

In his speech, Hussain spoke about the friendship between the peoples of Pakistan and China, recalling with joy the reception by the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao. He said that the friendship between Pakistan and China is built on the basis of mutual respect and mutual benefit, and it has withstood the test of time. We will never forget the firm support given to us by the Chinese government and the great Chinese people in our time of difficulty. He wished Chairman Mao Zedong good health and eternal life!

When Hussain left Shanghai, the welcoming crowd, holding high the portrait of our great leader Chairman Mao and the portrait of Pakistani President Ayub Khan, gathered at the airport under the fluttering flags of China and Pakistan. They beat drums and shouted slogans, warmly bidding farewell to the guests. Hussain repeatedly waved to the crowd.

Pakistan's Ambassador to China, Sultan M. Khan, and Pakistani trainees in Shanghai also went to the airport to see them off.

During his return journey through Guangzhou, Hussain and his delegation were greeted by leaders of the Guangdong Provincial and Guangzhou Municipal Revolutionary Committees, including Wang Shudao, Huang Yuying, Jiao Linyi, and Sun Yiwu, who came to the airport to welcome and see them off.

New China News Agency, Shanghai, August 10th: Pakistani Foreign Minister Ashad Hussain today, during his visit to the Shanghai Machine Tool Factory, said that the achievements you have made in training technical personnel from among the workers have been affirmed by Chairman Mao Zedong. I wish you even greater success in the future.

When Hussain and his wife, along with the accompanying personnel, arrived at the Shanghai Machine Tool Factory, they were warmly welcomed by the revolutionary workers.

After visiting the Shanghai Machine Tool Factory, Hussain enthusiastically praised the revolutionary workers of the factory for following the words of the great leader Chairman Mao, achieving great success through self-reliance. He also praised the high-precision machine tools designed and manufactured by technicians of worker origin, which have surpassed the level of industrially advanced countries in the world.

Yesterday, Hussain and other Pakistani friends also visited the industrial exhibition and the docks in Shanghai.

### 巴基斯坦外长结束在我国访问后回国　侯赛因部长参观上海机床厂时受到革命职工热烈欢迎

1968-08-11
第5版()
专栏：

　　巴基斯坦外长结束在我国访问后回国
　　侯赛因部长参观上海机床厂时受到革命职工热烈欢迎
    新华社十日讯　巴基斯坦外交部长阿沙德·侯赛因和夫人以及其他随行人员，结束了在我国的友好访问，今天下午乘飞机离开上海经广州回国。
    上海市革命委员会领导成员王少庸、姬应白、冯国柱，以及上海革命群众组织的负责成员和革命群众数百人，到机场热烈欢送。陪同侯赛因部长到上海访问的外交部副部长韩念龙也到机场欢送。
    侯赛因部长是在九日从杭州到达上海的。当天晚上，上海市革命委员会举行宴会，热烈欢迎巴基斯坦朋友。王少庸和侯赛因部长在宴会上先后讲话，祝愿中巴两国人民的友谊进一步巩固和发展。王少庸说，侯赛因部长和夫人这次来我国进行友好访问，对中巴友谊和两国友好合作关系作出了新的贡献。我们上海人民和全国人民一样，对巴基斯坦政府为维护国家主权、反对外来侵略所作的努力，对巴基斯坦政府和人民坚持发展同中国的友好关系的原则立场，表示钦佩。
    侯赛因部长在讲话中谈到巴中两国人民的友谊时，高兴地回顾了中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席对他们的接见。他说，巴中两国的友谊是在互相尊重、平等互利的基础上建立起来的，是经得起时间考验的。我们永远不会忘记中国政府和伟大的中国人民在我们困难时给予我们的坚定的支持。他祝毛泽东主席健康、万寿无疆！
    侯赛因部长今天离开上海的时候，欢送群众高举着我们伟大领袖毛主席的画像，举着巴基斯坦总统阿尤布·汗的画像，聚集在飘扬着中巴两国国旗的机场上，敲起锣鼓，高呼口号，热烈欢送客人。侯赛因部长不断向欢送的人们挥手致意。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆·汗和巴基斯坦在上海的实习生也到机场欢送。
    侯赛因部长等回国途中经过广州时，广东省、广州市革命委员会负责人王首道、黄育英、焦林义、孙亦武等，前往机场迎送。
    新华社上海十日电　巴基斯坦外交部长阿沙德·侯赛因今天在参观上海机床厂时说，你们在从工人中培养技术人员方面做出的成绩，受到了毛泽东主席的肯定，祝愿你们今后取得更大的成就。
    侯赛因部长和夫人以及随行人员来到上海机床厂的时候，受到了革命职工的热烈欢迎。
    侯赛因部长在参观了上海机床厂以后，热情称赞上海机床厂革命职工听伟大领袖毛主席的话，坚持自力更生取得的巨大成就。他还赞扬工人出身的技术人员设计制造出来的高级精密机床，超过了世界上工业发达国家的水平。
    侯赛因部长等巴基斯坦朋友，昨天还在上海参观了工业展览会和码头。
