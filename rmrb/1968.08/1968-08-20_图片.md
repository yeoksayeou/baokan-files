Title: The Revolutionary Committee of Lingbao County Studies Chairman Mao's Latest Instructions on the Three-In-One Combination and the Teaching of "Streamlining Administration"
Author:
Date: 1968-08-20
Edition: 6

The Revolutionary Committee of Lingbao County is earnestly studying Chairman Mao's latest instructions on the three-in-one combination of cadres from the Party, the army, and the masses, as well as his teachings on "streamlining administration." They are determined to build the Revolutionary Committee into a revolutionary and closely-linked-with-the-masses battle command center.
