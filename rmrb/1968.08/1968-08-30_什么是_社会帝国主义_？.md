Title: What is "Social Imperialism"?
Author:
Date: 1968-08-30
Edition: 4

On August 23, this newspaper's editorial noted: "The Soviet revisionist renegade clique has long since degenerated into social imperialism."

Social imperialism is imperialism that flies the banner of "socialism." The great Lenin, when denouncing the Second International's revisionists for supporting the bourgeoisie's imperialist and colonialist policies, pointed out: "These renegades are a bunch of social imperialists, 'socialism in words, imperialism in deeds,' that is, opportunism has turned into imperialism." (From "The Tasks of the Third International," Lenin's Collected Works, Volume 29, page 458)

Since seizing control of the party and the state, the Soviet revisionist renegade clique has not only fully restored capitalism at home but also aggressively pursued imperialist policies abroad, eagerly collaborating with U.S. imperialism in a counterrevolutionary global conspiracy to redraw the map of the world. It treats some countries as its colonies, carrying out ruthless plunder and enslavement; it also infiltrates and controls some countries through economic "aid" and military "aid." The Soviet revisionist renegade clique hangs out a "socialist" signboard but engages in imperialist activities. The armed occupation of Czechoslovakia this time is a typical and prominent exposure of its social imperialist ugly face.

Our great leader Chairman Mao pointed out 28 years ago: "The proletariat of each capitalist country is gradually freeing itself from the influence of social imperialist Social Democracy and declaring its support for the liberation movements of the colonies and semi-colonies." ("On New Democracy," Selected Works of Mao Zedong, page 661) Social Democracy's social imperialism has long been spat upon by the proletariat and the revolutionary people. The social imperialism of the Soviet revisionist renegade clique will also inevitably meet with complete failure.

### 什么是“社会帝国主义”？

1968-08-30
第4版()
专栏：名词解释

    什么是“社会帝国主义”？
    八月二十三日本报评论员文章《苏联现代修正主义的总破产》指出：“苏修叛徒集团早已堕落成为社会帝国主义。”
    社会帝国主义，就是打着“社会主义”旗号的帝国主义。伟大的列宁在痛斥第二国际的修正主义者支持资产阶级的帝国主义政策和殖民主义政策的时候，曾经指出：这些叛徒是一伙社会帝国主义者，是“口头上的社会主义实际上的帝国主义，即机会主义变成了帝国主义”。（《论第三国际的任务》，《列宁全集》第二十九卷第四五八页）
    苏修叛徒集团自从篡夺了党和国家的领导权以后，不仅在国内全面复辟资本主义，同时对外狂热地推行帝国主义政策，加紧同美帝国主义进行反革命全球勾结，妄图重新瓜分世界。它把一些国家当作自己的殖民地，进行残酷的掠夺和奴役；并且通过经济“援助”、军事“援助”，对一些国家进行渗透和控制。苏修叛徒集团挂的是“社会主义”的招牌，干的是帝国主义的勾当。这次武装侵占捷克斯洛伐克，就是它的社会帝国主义丑恶面目最典型、最突出的大暴露。
    我们伟大的领袖毛主席在二十八年前就曾经指出：“各个资本主义国家的无产阶级一天一天从社会帝国主义的社会民主党的影响下面解放出来并宣布他们赞助殖民地半殖民地解放运动”。（《新民主主义论》，《毛泽东选集》第六六一页）社会民主党的社会帝国主义，早已为无产阶级和广大革命人民所唾弃。苏修叛徒集团的社会帝国主义，也必将遭到彻底破产。
