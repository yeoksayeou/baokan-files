Title: Good News Flies to the Warship
Author: Ding Fuhé
Date: 1968-08-10
Edition: 6

The golden sunrise reflects off the waves,
Good news flies to the warship,
Sailors jump for joy,
Strumming guitars and beating drums, they sing songs of praise:
Dear Chairman Mao, you are the red sun in our hearts,
You always care for workers, peasants, and soldiers day and night;
You give us bountiful mangoes,
Each one embodies so much heartfelt expectation!
The gift is precious and inspiring,
It adds to our boundless wisdom and strength.
Dear Chairman Mao, you support us,
We fear no hardships or storms and rough seas!
We will thoroughly smash the "multiple centers" theory,
And unite more firmly around Chairman Mao;
We will never stop fighting until we defeat all wolves,
And dare to make the red flag fly over the globe!

### 喜讯飞到军舰上
丁福合
1968-08-10
第6版()
专栏：

　　喜讯飞到军舰上
　　海军某部　丁福合
　　金灿灿的旭日映海浪，
　　喜讯展翅飞到军舰上，
　　水兵们乐得蹦三尺，
　　拉琴击鼓把赞歌唱：
　　敬爱的毛主席啊，我们心中的红太阳，
　　您日夜把工农兵挂心上；
　　赠送给咱丰硕的大芒果，
　　一个个凝聚了多少殷切的期望！
　　礼物珍贵啊鼓舞大，
　　给我们增添无穷智慧和力量。
　　敬爱的毛主席啊，您给我们撑腰，
　　我们不怕千难万险，狂风恶浪！
　　彻底砸烂“多中心论”，
　　在毛主席周围团结得更坚强；
　　杀不尽豺狼决不收兵，
　　敢教寰球红旗扬！
