Title: The Third Volume of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" Published in Japanese and Russian
Author:
Date: 1968-08-02
Edition: 1

The third volume of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" in Japanese and Russian has recently been published by the Beijing Foreign Languages Publishing House. With this, the Japanese version of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" has been published from the first to the third volume, and the Russian version from the first to the fourth volume has been completed.

The pocket-sized edition of the English version of "Mao Zedong Military Writings" has also been published by the Beijing Foreign Languages Publishing House.

Additionally, the Beijing Foreign Languages Publishing House has published "Chairman Mao on People's War" in fifteen languages, including English, French, Spanish, Russian, Japanese, German, Arabic, Vietnamese, Esperanto, Thai, Burmese, Indonesian, Hindi, Portuguese, and Italian.

The Thai and Burmese versions of "Chairman Mao's Six Military Works" have also been published.

### 《毛泽东选集》第三卷日、俄文版出版

1968-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　《毛泽东选集》第三卷日、俄文版出版
    新华社三十一日讯  《毛泽东选集》第三卷的日文版和俄文版，最近已由北京外文出版社出版。至此，《毛泽东选集》日文版已出版了第一卷至第三卷，俄文版第一卷至第四卷已经出齐。
    《毛泽东军事文选》英文版五十开本的袖珍本，也已由北京外文出版社出版。
    已经由北京外文出版社出版的，还有《毛主席论人民战争》的英、法、西、俄、日、德、阿拉伯、越南、世界语、泰、缅甸、印尼、印地、葡萄牙、意大利等十五种文字的版本。
    《毛主席的六篇军事著作》的泰、缅甸文版也已经出版。
