Title: Celebrating the 41st Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1968-08-01
Edition: 3

For the celebration of the 41st anniversary of the founding of the People's Liberation Army, the Ministry of National Defense held an evening of artistic performances to entertain the military attachés and their wives from various countries stationed in China.

The evening featured a performance of the revolutionary modern Beijing opera "Sha Jianhu" by the Beijing Opera Troupe, which was enthusiastically received.

The Guinean military delegation, currently on a friendly visit to China, was also invited to attend the evening's events.

Vice Minister of National Defense Su Yu, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Liberation Army Wang Xinting and Wen Yuceng, Political Commissar of the General Logistics Department Zhang Chuming, and other leaders from various military branches attended the evening and watched the performance. Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Xu Yixin also attended the evening.

The evening concluded with the song "The Voyage of the Great Sea Depends on the Helmsman."

### 庆祝中国人民解放军建军四十一周年　国防部举行文艺晚会招待各国驻华使节武官　北京京剧团演出的《沙家浜》受到热烈欢迎

1968-08-01
第3版()
专栏：

　　庆祝中国人民解放军建军四十一周年
　　国防部举行文艺晚会招待各国驻华使节武官
　　北京京剧团演出的《沙家浜》受到热烈欢迎
    新华社三十一日讯　为庆祝中国人民解放军建军四十一周年，国防部今晚举行文艺晚会，招待各国驻中国的使节、武官和他们的夫人。
    晚会上，由北京京剧团演出了革命现代京剧《沙家浜》。演出受到热烈欢迎。
    正在我国进行友好访问的几内亚军事代表团应邀出席了晚会。
    国防部副部长粟裕，中国人民解放军副总参谋长王新亭、温玉成，总后勤部政治委员张池明，以及各军种、兵种负责同志出席晚会陪同观看。外交部副部长徐以新也出席了晚会。
    晚会在《大海航行靠舵手》的歌声中结束。
