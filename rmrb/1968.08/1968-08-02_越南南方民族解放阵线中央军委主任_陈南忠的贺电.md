Title: Greetings from Chen Nam Trung, Chief of the Central Military Commission of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1968-08-02
Edition: 4

	The Chief of the Central Military Commission of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, Chen Nam Trung, sent a telegram to Minister of National Defense Lin Biao to congratulate the Chinese People's Liberation Army on its 41st anniversary.

	The telegram said, on the occasion of the 41st anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, I, on behalf of all the cadres and soldiers of the people's liberation armed forces in South Vietnam, and in my personal capacity, extend my warmest congratulations to you and all the officers and soldiers of the heroic Chinese People's Liberation Army.

	The telegram said, over the past forty years, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party headed by the respected leader Mao Zedong, the Chinese People's Liberation Army has achieved brilliant military feats in the history of the Chinese people's revolutionary struggle, making a decisive contribution to the victory of the Chinese revolution.

	The telegram said, now, the Chinese People's Liberation Army has become a powerful force armed with Mao Zedong Thought, equipped with modern technology, and is defending the fruits of socialist construction in the great People's Republic of China with the seven hundred million Chinese people. It has frustrated all the conspiracies and war provocations of U.S. imperialism and its lackeys, making significant contributions to the cause of safeguarding peace in Asia and the world.

	The telegram said, the people of Vietnam and the people's liberation armed forces are delighted to see the continuous strengthening of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and wish you and the fraternal Chinese people greater achievements in socialist construction, national defense consolidation, and the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

	The telegram said, the people's liberation armed forces in South Vietnam have displayed revolutionary heroism, learned from the thorough revolutionary and tenacious fighting spirit of the Chinese people and the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and are determined to press on with greater vigor and accuracy to strike at the U.S. aggressors and their lackeys, and carry the anti-U.S. war of national salvation through to the end.

	The telegram said, on this occasion, we express our heartfelt thanks to the Chinese Communist Party, the Chinese government, the Chinese people, and the fraternal Chinese People's Liberation Army for their firm support and tremendous, effective assistance in the struggle of the people of South Vietnam to defeat the U.S. aggressors and their lackeys.

	May the indestructible friendship and combat unity between the people of South Vietnam and the people's liberation armed forces and the Chinese people and the People's Liberation Army last forever.

### 越南南方民族解放阵线中央军委主任  陈南忠的贺电

1968-08-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南南方民族解放阵线中央军委主任
    陈南忠的贺电
    新华社一日讯　国防部长林彪同志收到了越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会军事委员会主任陈南忠发来的祝贺中国人民解放军建军四十一周年的电报。
    电报说，值此中国人民解放军建军四十一周年之际，我代表越南南方各人民解放武装力量全体干部、战士，以及我个人的名义，向您和英雄的中国人民解放军全体指战员致以最亲切的祝贺。
    电报说，四十多年来，在以尊敬的领袖毛泽东为首的中国共产党的领导下，中国人民解放军在中国人民革命斗争的历史上立下了光辉的战功，为中国的革命胜利作出决定性的贡献。
    电报说，现在，中国人民解放军已经成为一支用毛泽东思想武装起来的，有现代技术装备的，强大的军队，它正与七亿中国人民一起保卫伟大的中华人民共和国的社会主义建设成果，粉碎美帝国主义及其走狗的一切阴谋和战争挑衅，为保卫亚洲和世界和平事业作出了重大贡献。
    电报说，对于中国人民解放军的不断强大，越南人民和各人民解放武装力量感到高兴，并祝你们和兄弟的中国人民，在社会主义建设、巩固国防和无产阶级文化大革命中，取得更大的成就。
    电报说，越南南方各人民解放武装力量发挥了革命英雄主义，学习了中国人民和中国人民解放军彻底革命，顽强战斗的精神，决心乘胜前进，更猛、更准地打击美国侵略者及其走狗，把抗美救国战争进行到底。
    电报说，趁此机会，我们对中国共产党，中国政府，中国人民和兄弟的中国人民解放军在为越南南方人民战胜美国侵略者及其走狗的斗争中，所给予的坚决支持和巨大的、有效的援助，表示衷心的感谢。
    祝越南南方人民和各人民解放武装力量与中国人民和人民解放军之间的牢不可破的友谊和战斗团结永世长存。
