Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Another Batch of U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-08-09
Edition: 5

The military and civilians of North Vietnam have shot down another batch of U.S. aircraft.

	According to Xinhua News Agency in Hanoi on August 7: It was announced here that on the morning of August 5, the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province and Vinh Linh District in North Vietnam shot down one invading U.S. aggressor aircraft each.

	According to Xinhua News Agency in Hanoi on August 6: It was announced here that from July 31 to August 3, the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province in North Vietnam shot down a total of five invading U.S. aircraft in four days.

	The military and civilians of Quang Binh Province also shot down two U.S. aggressor aircraft on July 27 and August 1.

	According to Xinhua News Agency in Hanoi: It was announced here that the military and civilians of Vinh Linh, Quang Binh, and Nghe An in North Vietnam shot down a total of four invading U.S. aircraft on July 26 and 27.

	Previously, the military and civilians of Quang Binh and Quang Tri Provinces shot down three U.S. aircraft on July 24 and 25.

	According to Xinhua News Agency in Hanoi: It was announced here that on July 29, the military and civilians of Nghe An Province in North Vietnam shot down two U.S. aircraft in a counterattack against U.S. air aggressors.

	According to Xinhua News Agency in Hanoi: It was announced here that the military and civilians of the Vinh Linh region in North Vietnam shot down one invading U.S. aggressor aircraft each on July 24 and 25.

	The military and civilians of Quang Binh Province also shot down one U.S. aggressor aircraft on July 25.

	According to Xinhua News Agency in Hanoi: It was announced here that on July 29, the military and civilians of Quang Binh and Nghe An Provinces in North Vietnam shot down a total of three invading U.S. aircraft in a counterattack against U.S. air aggressors. On the same day, the artillery units of Quang Binh Province also hit and set fire to an invading U.S. warship.

### 越南北方军民又击落一批美机

1968-08-09
第5版()
专栏：

　　越南北方军民又击落一批美机
    新华社河内七日电  据这里公布：越南北方广平省和永灵区军民五日晨分别击落入侵的美国强盗飞机各一架。
    新华社河内六日电据这里公布：越南北方广平省军民从七月三十一日，到八月三日四天内共击落入侵的美机五架。
    河静省军民在七月二十七日和八月一日也击落美国强盗飞机两架。
    新华社河内电　据这里公布：越南北方永灵、广平和义安等地军民，七月二十六日和二十七日先后共击落入侵的美机四架。
    在这以前，河静和广平两省军民，七月二十四日和二十五日也曾击落美机三架。
    新华社河内电　据这里公布：越南北方义安省军民，七月二十九日在反击美国空中强盗的战斗中，一举击落美机两架。
    新华社河内电　据这里公布：越南北方永灵地区军民于七月二十四日和二十五日各击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架。
  广平省军民在二十五日也击落一架美国强盗飞机。
    据新华社河内电　据这里公布：越南北方广平和义安两省军民，七月二十九日在反击美国空中强盗的战斗中，共击落入侵的美机三架。同一天，广平省炮兵部队还把一艘入侵的美国军舰击中起火。
