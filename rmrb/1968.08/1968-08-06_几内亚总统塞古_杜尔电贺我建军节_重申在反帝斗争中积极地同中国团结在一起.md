Title: Guinea President Sekou Toure Cables Greetings on China's Army Day
Author:
Date: 1968-08-06
Edition: 5

The Chinese government has received a cable from Ahmed Sekou Toure, President of the Republic of Guinea, congratulating the Chinese People's Liberation Army on its 41st anniversary.

In his cable, President Sekou Toure said: On the occasion of the 41st anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, he has the honor and pleasure, on behalf of the Guinean people, the Guinean Democratic Party, and the Guinean government, and in his personal capacity, to extend warm congratulations.

The cable said: "We reiterate to the friendly Chinese people: In your struggle against imperialism and for the great happiness and prosperity of China, we stand actively in solidarity with you."

### 几内亚总统塞古·杜尔电贺我建军节　重申在反帝斗争中积极地同中国团结在一起

1968-08-06
第5版()
专栏：

　　几内亚总统塞古·杜尔电贺我建军节
　　重申在反帝斗争中积极地同中国团结在一起
    新华社五日讯　我国政府收到了几内亚共和国总统艾哈迈德·塞古·杜尔发来的祝贺中国人民解放军建军四十一周年的电报。
    塞古·杜尔总统在电报中说，在中国人民解放军建军四十一周年的时候，他荣幸地并高兴地代表几内亚人民、几内亚民主党和几内亚政府，并以他个人的名义，表示热烈的祝贺。电报说：“我们向友好的中国人民重申：在你们反对帝国主义和争取伟大的中国的幸福、繁荣的崇高斗争中，我们积极地和你们团结在一起。”
