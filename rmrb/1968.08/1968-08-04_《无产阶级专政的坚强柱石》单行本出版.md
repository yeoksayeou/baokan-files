Title: The Steadfast Pillar of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat
Author:
Date: 1968-08-04
Edition: 2

    The single-volume edition of "The Steadfast Pillar of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat" has been published.

    Xinhua News Agency, August 3rd: The important editorial "The Steadfast Pillar of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat," published by the People's Daily, Red Flag Magazine, and the Liberation Army Daily to celebrate the 41st anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, has been published in a single-volume edition by the People's Publishing House. The book features two photographs of the great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin Biao, along with an important inscription by Vice Chairman Lin Biao: "The Chinese People's Liberation Army is a force armed with Mao Zedong Thought, a force that serves the people wholeheartedly, and therefore an invincible force." This book will be available for purchase at the Beijing Xinhua Bookstore starting August 5th and will be gradually available at Xinhua Bookstores nationwide.

### 《无产阶级专政的坚强柱石》单行本出版

1968-08-04
第2版()
专栏：

　　《无产阶级专政的坚强柱石》单行本出版
    新华社三日讯　《人民日报》、《红旗》杂志、《解放军报》为庆祝中国人民解放军建军四十一周年发表的重要社论《无产阶级专政的坚强柱石》，已由人民出版社出版单行本。书的前面刊有伟大统帅毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席的两幅照片以及林彪副主席的重要题词：“中国人民解放军是毛泽东思想武装起来的队伍，是全心全意为人民服务的队伍，因而是战无不胜的队伍。”这本书将从八月五日起在北京新华书店发行，并将陆续在全国各地新华书店发行。
