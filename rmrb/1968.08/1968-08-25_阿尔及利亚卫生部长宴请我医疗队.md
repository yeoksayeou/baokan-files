Title: Algerian Minister of Health Hosts Chinese Medical Team
Author:
Date: 1968-08-25
Edition: 6

Algerian Minister of Health Tegini Hadam hosted a reception for the newly arrived Chinese medical team on the evening of the 23rd in this city and held a banquet in their honor.

The Chinese chargé d'affaires in Algeria, Chen Jinlong, was also invited to attend the banquet. The banquet was held in a friendly atmosphere.

The Chinese medical team, consisting of more than 50 people, arrived in Algiers, the capital of Algeria, on the 21st. Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Hassan Abdelgani, Director of the Technical Cooperation Department of the Algerian Ministry of Public Health, and Brahim Mokrane, Deputy Director of the Public Health Department.

This Chinese medical team will go to western Algeria to serve the Algerian people.

### 阿尔及利亚卫生部长宴请我医疗队

1968-08-25
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚卫生部长宴请我医疗队
    新华社阿尔及尔二十三日电　阿尔及利亚公共卫生部长特吉尼·哈达姆二十三日晚上在这里接见了新到达的中国医疗队全体成员，并设宴招待了他们。
    中国驻阿尔及利亚大使馆临时代办陈晋龙也应邀出席了宴会。宴会是在友好的气氛中进行的。
    这个中国医疗队一行五十多人是在二十一日到达阿尔及利亚首都阿尔及尔的。到机场迎接的有阿尔及利亚公共卫生部技术合作司长哈萨尔·阿卜杜勒加尼和公共卫生司副司长布拉西·莫克兰等人。
    这个中国医疗队将赴阿尔及利亚西部为阿尔及利亚人民服务。
