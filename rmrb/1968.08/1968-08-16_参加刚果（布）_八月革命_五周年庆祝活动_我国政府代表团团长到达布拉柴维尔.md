Title: Delegation Head Arrives in Brazzaville to Participate in the 5th Anniversary Celebration of the August Revolution in Congo (Brazzaville)
Author:
Date: 1968-08-16
Edition: 6

The head of the delegation of the People's Republic of China to participate in the 5th anniversary celebration of the August Revolution in Congo (Brazzaville), Huang Hua, the Chinese Ambassador to the Arab Republic of Egypt, arrived in Brazzaville, the capital of Congo (Brazzaville), by plane on the morning of the 13th.

He was welcomed at the airport by the chairman of the reception committee of the National Committee for the Celebration, Bouru, the responsible person of the Congo (Brazzaville) Friendship Association for the People of All Countries, and diplomatic envoys from many countries to Congo (Brazzaville).

The chargé d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Congo (Brazzaville), Li Chen Guang, and representatives of Chinese technical personnel in Congo (Brazzaville) also went to the airport to welcome him.

### 参加刚果（布）“八月革命”五周年庆祝活动  我国政府代表团团长到达布拉柴维尔

1968-08-16
第6版()
专栏：

    参加刚果（布）“八月革命”五周年庆祝活动
    我国政府代表团团长到达布拉柴维尔
    新华社布拉柴维尔十三日电　参加刚果（布）“八月革命”五周年庆祝活动的中华人民共和国政府代表团团长、中国驻阿联大使黄华，十三日上午乘飞机到达刚果（布）首都布拉柴维尔。
    他在机场受到庆典全国委员会的接待委员会主席布尔乌德、刚果（布）争取各国人民友好协会负责人以及许多国家驻刚果（布）的外交使节们的欢迎。
    中国驻刚果（布）大使馆临时代办李琛光以及在刚果（布）的中国技术人员的代表也到机场迎接。
