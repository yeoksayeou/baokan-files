Title: On the Glorious Day of Celebrating the Second Anniversary of the Publication of the Communiqué of the Eleventh Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Party, the Great Leader Chairman Mao and His Close Comrade-in-Arms Vice Chairman Lin Receive Comrades from the Six Regions' Army, Navy, and Air Force Cadres' Study Classes

Author:
Date: 1968-08-12
Edition: 1

In the glorious days when hundreds of millions of military and civilian personnel are celebrating the second anniversary of the publication of the communiqué of the Eleventh Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Party, the great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin received comrades from the army, navy, and air force cadres' study classes in the six regions of Fuzhou, Wuhan, Chengdu, Kunming, Xinjiang, and Tibet.

Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, Jiang Qing, Zhang Chunqiao, Yao Wenyuan, Xie Fuzhi, Huang Yongsheng, Wu Faxian, Ye Qun, Wang Dongxing, Wen Yucheng, and others participated in the reception.

Chairman Mao was in high spirits and radiant, and together with his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin, he was very pleased to meet with the comrades from the military units in Fuzhou, Wuhan, Chengdu, Kunming, Xinjiang, and Tibet, as well as other revolutionary soldiers. Chairman Mao frequently waved to the revolutionary soldiers and applauded warmly for a long time.

The entire scene was filled with excitement. The revolutionary soldiers waved their red copies of "Quotations from Chairman Mao," shouting excitedly: "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Long, long live Chairman Mao!" "Wishing Chairman Mao eternal life! Eternal life!" They expressed their determination to follow Chairman Mao's great strategic deployment and resolutely carry out all battle orders from the proletarian headquarters, and to carry the Cultural Revolution through to the end.

Xinhua News Agency, August 11th: Our most beloved great leader and great commander Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin, along with Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, Jiang Qing, Zhang Chunqiao, Yao Wenyuan, Xie Fuzhi, Huang Yongsheng, Wu Faxian, Ye Qun, Wang Dongxing, and Wen Yucheng, received comrades from the Mao Zedong Thought Cadres' Study Classes of the Chinese People's Liberation Army in the regions of Fuzhou, Wuhan, Chengdu, Kunming, Xinjiang, and Tibet, representatives from the Second Session of the Model Company Representatives Conference of the Navy, and representatives from the Second Session of the Active Learners and Appliers of Mao Zedong Thought in the Beijing Region Artillery. They also received revolutionary soldiers from some regions who are currently in Beijing attending Mao Zedong Thought Study Classes.

In recent days, hundreds of millions of military and civilian personnel across the country have enthusiastically celebrated the second anniversary of the publication of Chairman Mao's "Bombard the Headquarters" big-character poster, the decision on the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution personally drafted by Chairman Mao, and the communiqué of the Eleventh Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Party. They have enthusiastically celebrated the great victories achieved in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution over the past two years and expressed their determination to closely unite under the leadership of the proletarian headquarters headed by Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin, to unify their will, steps, and actions, to thoroughly criticize the reactionary bourgeois "theory of multiple centers," and to achieve a comprehensive victory in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. At this moment, Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin's reception of the revolutionary soldiers is the greatest care, encouragement, and support for the military and civilian personnel across the country.

More than twenty thousand revolutionary soldiers, filled with joy, gathered in the Great Hall of the People. They repeatedly sang "Chairman Mao, we are forever loyal to you" and read aloud the affectionate instructions of the great leader Chairman Mao: "You should pay attention to state affairs and carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution through to the end!" The reception venue was filled with a revolutionary atmosphere of boundless love, faith, admiration, and loyalty to the great commander Chairman Mao. The front of the second and third floors were adorned with portraits of Chairman Mao, embodying the deep class feelings of the revolutionary soldiers' eternal loyalty to Chairman Mao, eternal loyalty to Mao Zedong Thought, and eternal loyalty to Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line.

The most glorious moment arrived. Chairman Mao, in high spirits and radiant, together with his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin, was very pleased to come to the reception venue and meet with the revolutionary soldiers. Chairman Mao frequently waved to the revolutionary soldiers in the entire venue and applauded warmly for a long time.

At this moment, the reception venue was filled with excitement. The revolutionary soldiers waved their red copies of "Quotations from Chairman Mao," shouting excitedly: "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Long, long live Chairman Mao!" "Wishing Chairman Mao eternal life! Eternal life!"

Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin shook hands with each of the responsible comrades participating in the reception and had cordial conversations with them.

Also participating in the reception were:

Members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee: Dong Biwu, Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, and Ye Jianying;

Responsible comrades from the military regions of Fuzhou, Wuhan, Chengdu, Kunming, Xinjiang, and Tibet: Han Xianchu, Pi Dingjun, Zeng Siyu, Liu Feng, Liang Xingchu, Zhang Guohua, Tan Furen, Chen Kang, Zhou Xing, Long Shujin, Wang Enmao, Saifuding, Guo Peng, Zeng Yongya, Ren Rong, and Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme;

Responsible comrades from the General Departments of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the National Defense Science and Technology Commission, and related departments: Gu Yu, Li Zuopeng, Qiu Huizuo, Liu Xianquan, Wang Shusheng, Li Tianyou, Wang Xinting, Zhang Chuming, Zhang Lingbin, Zhang Xianyue, Zhang Tianyun, Xiao Li, Nie Jifeng, Luo Xunchu, Liu Huaqing, Cai Shungli, and Chen Huatang;

Responsible comrades from various military branches and arms: Xiao Jinguang, Wang Hongkun, Zhang Xiuquan, Zhao Qimin, Zhou Xihan, Wang Bingzhu, Wang Huijiu, Cao Lihuai, Qian Renong, Chang Qinkun, Tan Jiaxu, Chen Renqi, Wu Lie, Huang Zhiyong, Zhang Yizhang, Luo Huasheng, Jiang Wen, Huang Wenming, and Li Zhen;

Responsible comrades from the Beijing region troops, Beijing Garrison Command, Shanxi Revolutionary Committee, and the Revolutionary Committee Preparation Group of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region: Zheng Weishan, Li Xuefeng, Liu Geping, Chen Xianrui, Huang Zuozhen, Yang Junsheng, Wu Zhong, Zhang Riquing, Yuan Zhen, Wei Guoqing, Ou Zhifu, Wu Jinnan, Wei Youzhi, Jiao Hongguang, and Yu Qili. Also present were Yu Qili and others.

The reception ended, but the revolutionary soldiers continued to shout for a long time: "Long live Chairman Mao! Long live! Long, long live! Wishing Chairman Mao eternal life! Eternal life!" "Long live the invincible Mao Zedong Thought!" They sang: "We have seen the great leader Chairman Mao. Chairman Mao! Chairman Mao! Revolutionary soldiers are forever loyal to you, following your proletarian headquarters, unifying our will, steps, and actions, launching a fierce attack on the class enemies, and achieving a comprehensive victory in the Cultural Revolution."

The revolutionary soldiers said that following the proletarian headquarters headed by Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin is victory. They expressed their determination to hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, to study and apply Chairman Mao's works, especially to study and apply Chairman Mao's series of extremely important latest instructions on the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, to follow Chairman Mao's great strategic deployment, to resolutely carry out all battle orders from the proletarian headquarters, and to carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution through to the end!

The golden sun rises from the east, and its brilliant rays illuminate the earth; Chairman Mao receives the revolutionary soldiers, and Chairman Mao is forever with us. Great leader Chairman Mao, we are determined to be infinitely loyal to you, to your brilliant thoughts, and to your proletarian revolutionary line. Under your leadership, we will unite and achieve a comprehensive victory in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!

Millions of hearts are beating fiercely, and millions of smiling faces are facing the red sun. Revolutionary soldiers hold up the red book, sincerely wishing the great leader Chairman Mao eternal life! Eternal life! Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter.

### 在亿万军民欢庆党的八届十一中全会公报发表两周年的光辉日子里　伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林副主席　接见六地区陆海空部队干部学习班的同志

1968-08-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　在亿万军民欢庆党的八届十一中全会公报发表两周年的光辉日子里
　　伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林副主席
　　接见六地区陆海空部队干部学习班的同志
    周恩来、陈伯达、康生、李富春、江青、张春桥、姚文元、谢富治、黄永胜、吴法宪、叶群、汪东兴、温玉成等同志参加接见
    毛主席精神焕发，神采奕奕，和他的亲密战友林副主席十分高兴地同福州武汉成都昆明新疆西藏地区部队同志和其他革命战士亲切见面。毛主席向革命战士频频招手，长时间热烈鼓掌
    全场一片欢腾。革命战士挥动着红彤彤的《毛主席语录》，无比激动地高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“毛主席万万岁！”“敬祝毛主席万寿无疆！万寿无疆！”他们表示，一定紧跟毛主席的伟大战略部署，坚决执行无产阶级司令部的一切战斗号令，把文化大革命进行到底
    新华社十一日讯　我们最敬爱的伟大领袖、伟大统帅毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席，以及周恩来、陈伯达、康生、李富春、江青、张春桥、姚文元、谢富治、黄永胜、吴法宪、叶群、汪东兴、温玉成等同志，今天接见了中国人民解放军福州、武汉、成都、昆明、新疆、西藏地区陆、海、空军部队毛泽东思想干部学习班的同志，出席海军第二次四好连队代表大会和北京地区炮兵第二次活学活用毛泽东思想积极分子代表大会的代表，以及正在北京参加毛泽东思想学习班的一些地区的革命战士。
    连日来，全国亿万军民热烈欢呼毛主席的《炮打司令部》大字报、毛主席亲自主持制定的《中国共产党中央委员会关于无产阶级文化大革命的决定》和《中国共产党第八届中央委员会第十一次全体会议公报》发表两周年，热烈欢呼两年来无产阶级文化大革命所取得的伟大胜利，表示决心紧密地团结在以毛主席为首、林副主席为副的无产阶级司令部的领导下，统一意志，统一步伐，统一行动，彻底批判反动的资产阶级的“多中心论”，夺取无产阶级文化大革命的全面胜利。在这样的时刻，毛主席和林副主席接见革命战士，这是对全国军民的最大关怀、最大鼓舞、最大支持。
    被接见的两万多革命战士，幸福地聚集在人民大会堂。他们一遍遍高唱《毛主席啊，我们永远忠于您》，齐声朗读伟大领袖毛主席的亲切教导：“你们要关心国家大事，要把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底！”接见会场里，充满了对伟大统帅毛主席无限热爱、无限信仰、无限崇拜、无限忠诚的革命气氛，二楼和三楼的前沿挂满了毛主席像，凝结着革命战士永远忠于毛主席，永远忠于毛泽东思想，永远忠于毛主席的无产阶级革命路线的深厚阶级感情。
    最光荣的时刻来到了。毛主席精神焕发，神采奕奕，和他的亲密战友林彪副主席十分高兴地来到接见会场，同革命战士亲切见面。毛主席向全场革命战士频频招手，长时间地热烈鼓掌。
    这时，接见会场一片欢腾。革命战士挥动着红彤彤的《毛主席语录》，无比激动地高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“毛主席万万岁！”“敬祝毛主席万寿无疆！万寿无疆！”
    毛主席和林副主席同参加接见的负责同志一一握手，并且同他们亲切交谈。
