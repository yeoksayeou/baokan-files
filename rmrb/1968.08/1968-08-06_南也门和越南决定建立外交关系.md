Title: South Yemen and Vietnam Decide to Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1968-08-06
Edition: 6

	South Yemen and Vietnam Decide to Establish Diplomatic Relations

	Xinhua News Agency, Aden: The Aden Radio broadcast on July 30th: The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of South Yemen announced in a statement on July 29th that in order to strengthen the friendly relations between the People's Republic of South Yemen and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the two governments have decided to establish diplomatic relations. This agreement was signed by the two countries' ambassadors to the United Arab Republic in Cairo on July 29th.

### 南也门和越南决定建立外交关系

1968-08-06
第6版()
专栏：

　　南也门和越南决定建立外交关系
    新华社亚丁电　亚丁电台七月三十日广播：南也门人民共和国外交部七月二十九日在一项声明中宣布，为了增强南也门人民共和国和越南民主共和国之间的友好关系，两国政府决定建立外交关系。这项协定是两国驻阿联大使七月二十九日在开罗签定的。
