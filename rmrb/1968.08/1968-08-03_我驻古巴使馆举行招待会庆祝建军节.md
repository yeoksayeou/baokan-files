Title: The Chinese Embassy in Cuba Holds a Reception to Celebrate the Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1968-08-03
Edition: 5

The Chinese Embassy in Cuba held a reception on the evening of July 31 to enthusiastically celebrate the 41st anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

The Chargé d'Affaires of the People's Republic of China in Cuba, Li Shanyi, hosted the reception. Attendees included government officials from Cuba, the Chairman of the Cuba-China Friendship Association, Baldomero Alvarez, representatives from various sectors of Cuban society, friends from other Latin American countries, and overseas Chinese in Cuba, totaling over 200 people.

Diplomatic envoys from several countries to Cuba also attended the reception.

During the reception, many foreign friends had cordial conversations with the staff of the Chinese Embassy. They warmly congratulated the Chinese People's Liberation Army on its 41st anniversary, highly praised the invincible Mao Zedong Thought, and enthusiastically celebrated the great victories achieved by the Chinese Cultural Revolution.

### 我驻古巴使馆举行招待会庆祝建军节

1968-08-03
第5版()
专栏：

　　我驻古巴使馆举行招待会庆祝建军节
    新华社哈瓦那一日电  中华人民共和国驻古巴大使馆临时代办李善一七月三十一日晚间举行招待会，热烈庆祝中国人民解放军建军四十一周年。
    出席招待会的有古巴的政府官员，古中友好协会主席巴尔多梅罗·阿尔瓦雷斯，古巴各界人士和拉丁美洲其他国家的朋友以及旅古华侨共二百多人。
    一些国家驻古巴的外交使节也出席了招待会。
    在招待会上，许多外国朋友同中国使馆工作人员进行了亲切的交谈。他们热烈祝贺中国人民解放军建军四十一周年，高度赞扬战无不胜的毛泽东思想，热烈祝贺中国无产阶级文化大革命所取得的伟大胜利。
