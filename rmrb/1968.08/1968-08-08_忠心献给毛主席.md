Title: Loyalty Offered to Chairman Mao
Author: Li Zuomin
Date: 1968-08-08
Edition: 6

	Loyalty Offered to Chairman Mao
	Navy Unit Li Zuomin

	The surging east wind and the red flag,
	The vast red waves shine under the red sun,
	Chairman Mao presents a large mango,
	The thunderous news spreads across the motherland.

	Chairman Mao presents a large mango,
	This is the greatest support and encouragement for us,
	Chairman Mao's heart is forever connected with the revolutionary masses,
	Chairman Mao is always fighting alongside us.

	Remembering the past, the long journey of ten thousand miles,
	Chairman Mao led the workers and peasants in fierce battles;
	Looking at today, the red waves roll at Tiananmen Square,
	Welcoming the complete victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

	Millions of red hearts are beating fiercely,
	The blood in our bodies surges endlessly;
	We will definitely advance bravely along Your revolutionary line,
	And carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution through to the end!

### 忠心献给毛主席
李作民
1968-08-08
第6版()
专栏：

　　忠心献给毛主席
　　海军某部　李作民
　　浩荡的东风火红的旗，
　　万顷红涛耀红日，
　　毛主席赠送大芒果，
　　春雷般的喜讯传遍祖国大地。
　　毛主席赠送大芒果，
　　是对我们的最大支持、最大激励，
　　毛主席永远和革命群众心连心，
　　毛主席时刻和我们战斗在一起。
　　忆往昔，迢迢征途万里远啊，
　　毛主席率领工农鏖战急；
　　看今朝，天安门前红浪卷，
　　迎接无产阶级文化大革命的全面胜利。
　　千万颗红心啊在激烈地跳动，
　　周身的热血啊翻滚不息；
　　我们一定沿着您的革命路线奋勇前进，
　　把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底！
