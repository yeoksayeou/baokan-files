Author:
Date: 1968-08-20
Edition: 5

Cambodia Holds Ceremony for the Handing Over of Anti-Cholera Vaccines Donated by China

New China News Agency, Phnom Penh, August 18 - The Royal Government of Cambodia held a ceremony on August 14 to hand over two million doses of anti-cholera vaccines donated by the People's Republic of China. The ceremony was presided over by His Excellency Huong Houng Sath, Deputy Prime Minister of the Royal Government of Cambodia.

The Charge d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy, Zheng Sixiong, and Madame Dip Man, Minister of Public Health, Labor, and Social Affairs of Cambodia, attended the ceremony.

Huong Houng Sath, on behalf of the Royal Government of Cambodia, accepted this precious gift and delivered a speech. He expressed his "deepest gratitude to the Chinese brothers and their great leaders." In his speech, he also highly praised the continuous and effective assistance provided by China to Cambodia in various ways.

Huong Houng Sath warmly praised the friendship and cooperation between the peoples of China and Cambodia and wished the Chinese government and people victory under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao.

Charge d'Affaires Zheng Sixiong also spoke at the ceremony. Zheng Sixiong strongly condemned the heinous crimes of American imperialism's continuous aggression against Cambodia in his speech. He stated that the Chinese people, following the great teachings of Chairman Mao, will resolutely support the Cambodian government and people in their just struggle against American imperialist aggression.

### 柬政府举行我赠柬抗霍乱疫苗的交接仪式

1968-08-20
第5版()
专栏：

    柬政府举行我赠柬抗霍乱疫苗的交接仪式
    新华社金边十八日电　柬埔寨王国政府八月十四日举行了中华人民共和国赠给柬埔寨王国两百万支抗霍乱疫苗的交接仪式。柬埔寨王国政府第二副首相黄鸿萨主持了这个仪式。
    中国大使馆临时代办郑斯雄和柬埔寨公共卫生、劳工和社会活动大臣狄普曼夫人出席了交接仪式。
    黄鸿萨在仪式上代表柬埔寨王国政府接受了这批珍贵的礼物，并且发表了讲话。他“向中国兄弟及其伟大的领导人表示最衷心的感谢。”他在讲话中还高度评价了中国不断以各种方式给予柬埔寨的有效的援助。
    黄鸿萨热烈赞扬中柬两国人民之间的友谊和合作，并且祝愿中国政府和人民在毛主席的英明领导下取得胜利。
    郑斯雄临时代办也在仪式上讲了话。郑斯雄在讲话中强烈谴责美帝国主义不断侵略柬埔寨的滔天罪行。他表示，中国人民遵循毛主席的伟大教导，必将更坚决地支持柬埔寨政府和人民抗击美帝国主义侵略的正义斗争。
