Title: Iraq Announces Formation of New Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1968-08-03
Edition: 6

	Iraq announced the formation of a new cabinet. According to a message from Baghdad, the Iraqi Revolutionary Command Council announced the establishment of a new cabinet at dawn on August 1. President Bakr will concurrently serve as the prime minister of the new cabinet.

	The new cabinet consists of twenty-six members, eleven of whom were members of the first cabinet formed after the July 17 Iraqi coup. Key members of the new cabinet include Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Defense Hardan Abdul Ghafar al-Tikriti, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of the Interior Salah Mahdi Amash, Minister of Foreign Affairs Abdul Karim Abdul Sattar al-Shehli, and Minister of Finance Amin Abdul Karim. It is reported that the Revolutionary Command Council dissolved the previous cabinet led by Abdul Razzaq al-Naif on July 30. Naif was relieved of his duties as prime minister, and Ibrahim Abdul Rahman Daoud was removed from his position as minister of defense.

### 伊拉克宣布成立新内阁

1968-08-03
第6版()
专栏：

    伊拉克宣布成立新内阁
    新华社二日讯　巴格达消息：伊拉克革命指挥委员会八月一日凌晨宣布成立新内阁。由贝克尔总统兼任新内阁总理。
    新内阁由二十六人组成，其中十一人是七月十七日伊拉克政变后第一次内阁的成员。新内阁成员中包括副总理兼国防部长哈尔丹·阿卜杜勒·加法尔·提克里蒂、副总理兼内政部长萨拉赫·马赫迪·阿马什、外交部长阿卜杜勒·卡里姆·阿卜杜勒·萨塔尔·谢赫利和财政部长阿明·阿卜杜勒·卡里姆等。据报道，伊拉克革命指挥委员会七月三十日解散了以阿卜杜勒·拉扎克·纳伊夫为首的前内阁。解除了纳伊夫的总理职务和易卜拉欣·阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼·达乌德国防部长的职务。
