Title: Representatives of the Beijing Artillery Force's Active Learners and Users of Mao Zedong Thought Share Joyous News
Author:
Date: 1968-08-13
Edition: 3

Representatives of the Beijing Artillery Force's conference of active learners and users of Mao Zedong Thought, with hearts filled with boundless love and loyalty to Chairman Mao, swiftly conveyed the tremendous news of seeing the great leader Chairman Mao to their comrades in arms.
