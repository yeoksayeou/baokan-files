Title: Reception Held by Chinese Chargé d'Affaires in Pakistan for Returning Pakistani Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1968-08-22
Edition: 5

Chinese Chargé d'Affaires in Pakistan Holds Reception for Returning Pakistani Foreign Minister

NEW DELHI, Aug. 19 (Xinhua) -- Chinese Chargé d'Affaires in Pakistan, Xu Ying, held a reception on the evening of the 19th in this city for Pakistani Foreign Minister Asad Hussain, who had just returned from a visit to the People's Republic of China.

In his speech at the reception, Hussain expressed his deep admiration for the great achievements made by the Chinese people under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong in building their own country.

When talking about the friendly relations between Pakistan and China, Hussain said, "The friendly relations between Pakistan and China are based on the solid foundation of mutual respect for sovereignty and territorial integrity, non-interference in internal affairs, and are not based on expediency. This friendship will certainly be further developed."

Chargé d'Affaires Xu Ying, in his speech, reiterated that China supports the Pakistani people in their struggle to maintain national independence and resist foreign aggression, and supports the struggle of the Kashmiri people for the right to self-determination.

The reception was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere. After the reception, a documentary film about the Pakistani Foreign Minister's visit to China was shown.

Attending the reception were some Pakistani government ministers and other high-ranking military and political officials.

### 为巴基斯坦外长访华回国  我驻巴临时代办举行招待会

1968-08-22
第5版()
专栏：

    为巴基斯坦外长访华回国
    我驻巴临时代办举行招待会
    新华社拉瓦尔品第十九日电　中国驻巴基斯坦大使馆临时代办徐英，十九日晚在这里为最近访问中华人民共和国归来的巴基斯坦外交部长阿沙德·侯赛因举行招待会。
    侯赛因在招待会上的讲话中表示，中国人民在伟大领袖毛泽东主席的领导下，在建设自己的国家方面取得的巨大进展，使他深受感动。
    在谈到巴中两国的友好关系时，侯赛因说，巴基斯坦和中国的友好关系是建立在相互尊重主权、领土完整、互不干涉内政的原则的牢固基础上的，而不是以权宜之计为基础的。这种友谊必将进一步发展。
    徐英代办在讲话中重申，中国支持巴基斯坦人民维护民族独立、抵抗外来侵略的斗争，支持克什米尔人民争取自决权利的斗争。
    招待会是在热烈亲切的气氛中进行的。招待会后还放映了巴基斯坦外交部长访问中国的纪录片。
    出席招待会的有巴基斯坦的一些政府部长以及其他高级军政官员。
