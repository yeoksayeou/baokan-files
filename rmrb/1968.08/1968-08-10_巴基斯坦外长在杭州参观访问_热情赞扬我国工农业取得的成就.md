Title: Pakistani Foreign Minister Visits Hangzhou and Praises China's Industrial and Agricultural Achievements
Author:
Date: 1968-08-10
Edition: 5

Pakistani Foreign Minister Visits Hangzhou and Praises China's Industrial and Agricultural Achievements

New China News Agency, Hangzhou, August 9th - Pakistani Foreign Minister Asad Husain visited the Zhejiang Flax Textile Factory on August 8th. After hearing about the workers' achievements in studying and applying Chairman Mao's works and promoting both revolution and production, he said, "Chairman Mao is your great leader and also a great friend of our country and people. I wish you good health and longevity, Chairman Mao! I wish you new heights in production under Chairman Mao's wise leadership."

Foreign Minister Asad Husain and his wife, along with other accompanying personnel, arrived in Hangzhou on August 7th, escorted by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong. They were warmly welcomed by relevant officials and hundreds of revolutionary masses of Hangzhou at the airport.

After visiting Hangzhou for two days, the Pakistani friends departed for Shanghai by special plane this morning to continue their visit. Pakistani Ambassador to China Sultan M. Khan also accompanied Foreign Minister Husain to Shanghai.

On the evening of their arrival in Hangzhou, Chairman of the Zhejiang Revolutionary Committee Nan Ping hosted a banquet in their honor. Nan Ping and Husain both spoke at the banquet. They wished for the friendship between the peoples of China and Pakistan to be further strengthened and developed.

Husain said, "We are honored to have been received by Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai in Beijing. Chairman Mao's remarks were very cordial and significant."

Husain said, "Your support for our struggle to maintain national independence and sovereignty, and your support for the people of Jammu and Kashmir in their struggle for self-determination, is not only admired by the Pakistani people but by people all over the world. He also warmly praised the achievements of our country's industrial and agricultural production. He concluded by toasting to the longevity of Chairman Mao Zedong!"

During their stay in Hangzhou, Foreign Minister Husain and his wife, along with the accompanying personnel, also visited the Zhejiang Flax Textile Factory and the Dongfanghong Silk Weaving Factory, where they were warmly welcomed by the revolutionary workers of both factories.

When Foreign Minister Husain left Hangzhou today, responsible officials from the Zhejiang Revolutionary Committee, Chen Liyun, Lai Keke, Wang Zida, military representatives from the three services, and representatives from provincial and municipal revolutionary mass organizations, were at the airport to see them off.

### 巴基斯坦外长在杭州参观访问　热情赞扬我国工农业取得的成就

1968-08-10
第5版()
专栏：

　　巴基斯坦外长在杭州参观访问
　　热情赞扬我国工农业取得的成就
    新华社杭州九日电　巴基斯坦外交部长阿沙德·侯赛因八日在杭州参观浙江麻纺织厂时，听了关于工人们活学活用毛主席著作，在抓革命、促生产方面所取得的成就后说，毛主席是你们的伟大领袖，也是我国和我国人民的伟大朋友，我愿和你们一起祝毛主席身体健康、万寿无疆！祝你们在毛主席的英明领导下，在生产方面跨上新的高度。
    阿沙德·侯赛因外长和夫人以及其他随行人员，由我国外交部副部长韩念龙陪同，在七日到达杭州。他们在机场受到有关方面负责人和杭州革命群众数百人的热烈欢迎。
    巴基斯坦朋友在杭州访问了两天以后，今天上午乘专机离开杭州前往上海参观访问。巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆·汗也陪同侯赛因外长前往上海。
    侯赛因外长等一行到达杭州的当天晚上，浙江省革命委员会主任南萍曾设宴欢迎客人。南萍和侯赛因在宴会上先后讲话。他们祝愿中巴两国人民的友谊日益巩固和发展。
    侯赛因说，我们在北京受到毛泽东主席和周恩来总理的接见，感到很荣幸。毛主席的谈话很亲切，具有重要的意义。
    侯赛因说，你们支持我国为维护民族独立和主权而进行的斗争，支持查谟和克什米尔人民为争取民族自决权而进行的斗争，不仅巴基斯坦人民而且全世界人民都感到钦佩。他还热情赞扬我国工农业生产所取得的成就。他最后举杯祝毛泽东主席万寿无疆！
    在杭州期间，侯赛因外长和夫人以及随行人员，还参观了浙江麻纺织厂和杭州东方红丝织厂，受到这两个厂革命职工的热烈欢迎。
    侯赛因外长今天离开杭州的时候，浙江省革命委员会负责人陈励耘、赖可可、王子达，驻军三军代表和省、市革命群众组织代表，到机场热烈欢送。
