Title: Military and Civilians of Quảng Bình Province Shoot Down Five American Planes in One Day
Author:
Date: 1968-08-15
Edition: 6

The military and civilians of Quảng Bình Province in Vietnam shot down five invading American aggressor planes on August 9. This is the highest number of enemy planes shot down in a single day in Quảng Bình Province since the beginning of August.

On August 3 and 11, the military and civilians of Vĩnh Linh District and Quảng Trị Province successively set two American warships on fire.

### 越南广平省军民一天击落美机五架

1968-08-15
第6版()
专栏：

　　越南广平省军民一天击落美机五架
    新华社河内十三日电　据这里今天公布：越南北方广平省军民九日共击落五架入侵的美国强盗飞机（前报道为一架），这是八月份开始以来广平省军民击落敌机最多的一天。
    在三日和十一日，永灵区和河静省军民曾先后把两艘美国军舰打得燃烧起来。
