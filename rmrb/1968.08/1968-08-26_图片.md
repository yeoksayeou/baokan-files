Title: Albanian Workers Make New Contributions on the Production Front
Author:
Date: 1968-08-26
Edition: 6

Workers in Albania are making new achievements on the production front. Workers at the "Sickle and Hammer" knitting factory in Korçë are working diligently. They are determined to produce a large quantity of high-quality knitted goods for the country and actively support socialist construction.
