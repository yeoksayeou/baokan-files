Title: Each Mango Shines with Golden Light
Author: Zhang Caiqin, Ma Changlin
Date: 1968-08-08
Edition: 6

Each mango shines with golden light,
Beijing knitwear factory workers Zhang Caiqin and Ma Changlin
Red light fills the sky with countless rays,
Great news spreads across the seas,
Great Leader presents mangoes,
People cheer, shaking the mountains and rivers.

Each mango shines with golden light,
Lamps illuminate the heart.
Precious gifts arrive at the factory,
Revolutionary workers beam with joy.

Old worker holds the mango,
A warm current flows through the heart:
"Chairman Mao, Chairman Mao,
Your kindness is endless.
We are infinitely loyal to you,
Old in age, red in heart, with unyielding spirit!"

Female worker on the blocking machine gazes with love,
As if Chairman Mao stands by her side:
"Chairman Mao, Chairman Mao,
You sent our dear soldiers,
Bringing your brilliant thoughts,
The red sun illuminates the knitwear factory.
Your precious gifts,
Inspire us to 'grasp revolution, promote production.'
We will weave our sincere wishes for you,
Into the beautiful fabric."

Young men run and call out,
Their passionate blood turns into oaths:
"Chairman Mao, Chairman Mao,
We are infinitely loyal to your revolutionary line,
Your orders, we will carry out,
In storms and waves, we will be tempered!"

Each mango shines with golden light,
Lamps illuminate the heart.
Chairman Mao waves his hand, we march forward,
Swearing to make the world red and radiant!

### 颗颗芒果金光闪
张才钦　马长林
1968-08-08
第6版()
专栏：

　　颗颗芒果金光闪
　　北京针织总厂工人　张才钦　马长林
　　霞光万道红满天，
　　特大喜讯四海传，
　　伟大领袖赠芒果，
　　万众欢呼震河山。
　　颗颗芒果金光闪，
　　盏盏明灯照心坎。
　　珍贵礼物送到厂，
　　革命工人尽开颜。
　　老工人手中捧芒果，
　　一股热流暖心间：
　　  “毛主席啊，毛主席，
　　您的恩情说不完。
　　我们无限忠于您，
　　人老心红斗志坚！”
　　挡车女工越看越爱看，
　　就好象毛主席站在身边：
　　“毛主席啊，毛主席，
　　是您派来亲人解放军，
　　带来了您的光辉思想，
　　红太阳把针织总厂照遍。
　　您的珍贵礼物，
　　激励着我们‘抓革命，促生产’。
　　我们要在锦绣的布匹上，
　　织进对您的衷心祝愿。”
　　小伙子们奔走呼唤，
　　满腔的热血化作誓言：
　　“毛主席啊，毛主席，
　　我们无限忠于您的革命路线，
　　您有令我们就行，
　　大风大浪里受锻炼！”
　　颗颗芒果金光闪，
　　盏盏明灯照心坎。
　　毛主席挥手我前进，
　　誓叫全球红烂漫！
