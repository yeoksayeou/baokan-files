Title: Workers at Dalian Port
Author: Lü Dà Xuān
Date: 1968-08-06
Edition: 4

    Under the brilliant guidance of Chairman Mao's latest instructions, revolutionary workers at Dalian Port are vigorously promoting the revolution and vigorously advancing production. The production situation is getting better and better. This is a scene of workers busily unloading cargo.
