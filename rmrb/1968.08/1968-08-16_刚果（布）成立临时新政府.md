Title: The Republic of the Congo (Brazzaville) Establishes a New Provisional Government
Author:
Date: 1968-08-16
Edition: 6

The President of the Republic of the Congo (Brazzaville), Massamba-Debat, announced in a radio broadcast on August 5 that a new provisional government has been formed in the Republic of the Congo (Brazzaville). The new provisional government consists of twelve members. The former government of the Republic of the Congo (Brazzaville) submitted its resignation to the Head of State on August 4.

According to a broadcast by the "Voice of the Congo (Brazzaville) Revolution" radio station on August 7, the Republic of the Congo (Brazzaville) has established a National Revolutionary Committee consisting of thirty-two members. The National Revolutionary Committee will be the highest organ of state.

### 刚果（布）成立临时新政府

1968-08-16
第6版()
专栏：

    刚果（布）成立临时新政府
    新华社布拉柴维尔十三日电　刚果（布）总统马桑巴—代巴八月五日在一次广播讲话中，宣布组成了刚果（布）共和国临时新政府，临时新政府由十二人组成。原来的刚果（布）政府已于四日向国家元首提出了辞职。
    据“刚果（布）革命之声”电台七日广播，刚果（布）成立了由三十二人组成的全国革命委员会。全国革命委员会将是国家的最高机构。
