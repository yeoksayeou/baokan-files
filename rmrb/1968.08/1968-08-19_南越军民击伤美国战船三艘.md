Title: South Vietnamese Military and Civilians Damage Three U.S. Warships
Author:
Date: 1968-08-19
Edition: 5

South Vietnamese Military and Civilians Damage Three U.S. Warships

According to a report by Xinhua News Agency in Hanoi on the 17th, the Vietnam South Liberation News Agency reported that the people's liberation armed forces in the Binh Duong Province of South Vietnam recently intercepted and attacked a U.S. military convoy on the Long Tau River near Saigon, damaging three warships.

The report stated that on the night of August 8th at 11 PM, the people's liberation armed forces intercepted a U.S. military convoy on the Long Tau River, 22 kilometers southeast of Saigon, setting one of the warships on fire. On the 10th, the artillery units of the people's liberation armed forces hit a 10,000-ton U.S. military transport ship on the Long Tau River, which immediately caught fire. On the same morning, the people's liberation armed forces also damaged another 8,000-ton U.S. military transport ship on the same river.

### 南越军民击伤美国战船三艘

1968-08-19
第5版()
专栏：

    南越军民击伤美国战船三艘
    据新华社河内十七日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方边和省人民解放武装力量最近在西贡附近的龙都河上连续截击美国侵略军的船队，共击伤战船三艘。
    报道说，八月八日夜间十一时，人民解放武装力量在西贡市东南二十二公里的龙都河上截击一支美军船队，把其中一艘战船打得燃烧起来。十日，人民解放武装力量的炮兵部队又击中龙都河上的一艘载重量为一万吨的美国军用运输船，这艘敌船立即燃起熊熊大火。同日上午，人民解放武装力量还在这条河上击伤了另一艘载重量为八千吨的美国军用运输船。
