Title: Congratulations on the 41st Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1968-08-04
Edition: 5

Congratulations on the 41st Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army

	Romanian Armed Forces Minister
	Yan Jionicha's Congratulatory Telegram

	Xinhua News Agency, August 3rd: Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense Lin Biao received a congratulatory telegram from Romanian Socialist Republic Minister of Armed Forces Yan Jionicha, congratulating the Chinese People's Liberation Army on its 41st anniversary.

	The telegram said, on the occasion of the 41st anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, I, on behalf of all the military personnel of the Romanian Socialist Republic Armed Forces, and in my personal capacity, extend my sincere congratulations to you and all Chinese military personnel. The Romanian Socialist Republic Armed Forces and all its military personnel hold deep respect and friendship for the Chinese people and their military, and are pleased with the achievements of the Chinese People's Liberation Army since its establishment. On this festive occasion, I wish all the military personnel of the Chinese People's Liberation Army new and significant achievements in strengthening the national defense forces of the People's Republic of China.

	North Korean Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense
	Kim Chang-hong's Congratulatory Telegram

	Xinhua News Agency, August 3rd: Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense Lin Biao received a congratulatory telegram from North Korean Democratic People's Republic Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense Kim Chang-hong, congratulating the Chinese People's Liberation Army on its 41st anniversary.

	The telegram said, on the occasion of the 41st anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, I, on behalf of the officers and soldiers of the Korean People's Army, extend warm congratulations to the brotherly Chinese People's Liberation Army. The Chinese People's Liberation Army has established glorious merits in the long and arduous struggle against the aggression of U.S. and Japanese imperialism and domestic reactionaries, and has achieved great successes in the cause of strengthening national defense.

	I wish the officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army greater achievements in the cause of strengthening national defense.

	Pakistani Army Chief of Staff
	Yahya Khan's Congratulatory Telegram

	Xinhua News Agency, August 3rd: General Staff Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Huang Yongsheng received a congratulatory telegram from Pakistani Army Chief of Staff Yahya Khan, congratulating the Chinese People's Liberation Army on its 41st anniversary.

	The telegram said, on the occasion of your country's Armed Forces Day, I, on behalf of the Pakistani Army and myself, extend my heartfelt congratulations to you and the Chinese Armed Forces. May the relations between the two countries' armed forces become closer and the friendship stronger.

### 祝贺中国人民解放军建军四十一周年

1968-08-04
第5版()
专栏：

　　祝贺中国人民解放军建军四十一周年
    罗马尼亚武装部队部部长
    扬·约尼查的贺电
    新华社三日讯　国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪同志收到了罗马尼亚社会主义共和国武装部队部部长扬·约尼查上将发来的祝贺中国人民解放军建军四十一周年的电报。
    电报说，值此中国人民解放军建军四十一周年之际，我代表罗马尼亚社会主义共和国武装部队全体军人，并以我个人的名义向您和全体中国军人表示衷心的祝贺。罗马尼亚社会主义共和国武装部队全体军人对中国人民和他的军队怀着深切的敬意和友谊，对中国人民解放军从成立到现在所取得的成就感到高兴，当此节日之际，祝愿中国人民解放军的全体军人在巩固中华人民共和国国防力量中取得新的显著的成就。
    朝鲜内阁副首相兼民族保卫相
    金昌凤的贺电
    新华社三日讯　国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪同志收到了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相兼民族保卫相金昌凤大将发来的祝贺中国人民解放军建军四十一周年的电报。
    电报说，值此中国人民解放军建军四十一周年之际，我以朝鲜人民军官兵的名义，向兄弟的中国人民解放军全体指战员致以热烈的祝贺。中国人民解放军，在反对美日帝国主义的侵略和国内反动派的长期艰苦的斗争中，建立了光辉的功勋，并在加强国防力量的事业中，取得了很大的成绩。
    祝中国人民解放军官兵，在加强国防力量的事业中，取得更大的成绩。
    巴基斯坦陆军总司令
    叶海亚·汗的贺电
    新华社三日讯　中国人民解放军总参谋长黄永胜同志收到了巴基斯坦陆军总司令叶海亚·汗将军发来的祝贺中国人民解放军建军四十一周年的电报。
    电报说，值此贵国武装部队日我谨代表巴基斯坦陆军和我个人向您和中国武装部队致以最衷心的祝贺。祝两国武装部队之间关系更密切、友谊更加强。
