Title: The Single Edition of "Unite Under the Leadership of the Proletarian Headquarters with Chairman Mao at the Helm" is Published
Author:
Date: 1968-08-08
Edition: 1

To commemorate the second anniversary of the publication of Chairman Mao's "Bombard the Headquarters (My Big-Character Poster)," the important editorial "Unite Under the Leadership of the Proletarian Headquarters with Chairman Mao at the Helm" published in the People's Daily has been published as a single edition by the People's Publishing House. It will begin to be distributed in the New China Bookstore in Beijing on August 8th and will subsequently be distributed in New China Bookstores across the country.

### 《在以毛主席为首的无产阶级司令部的领导下团结起来》单行本出版

1968-08-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　《在以毛主席为首的无产阶级司令部的领导下团结起来》单行本出版
    新华社七日讯　为纪念毛主席《炮打司令部（我的一张大字报）》发表两周年，《人民日报》发表的重要社论《在以毛主席为首的无产阶级司令部的领导下团结起来》，已由人民出版社出版单行本，八月八日开始在北京新华书店发行，并将在全国各地新华书店陆续发行。
