Title: The Revolutionary Oil Painting "Chairman Mao Goes to Anyuan" Shines with the Brilliant Light of Mao Zedong Thought
Author:
Date: 1968-08-27
Edition: 5

The revolutionary oil painting "Chairman Mao Goes to Anyuan," which shines with the brilliant light of Mao Zedong Thought, has been warmly welcomed and praised by Greek sailors. They eagerly view the great image of Chairman Mao when he went to Anyuan, filled with deep respect.
