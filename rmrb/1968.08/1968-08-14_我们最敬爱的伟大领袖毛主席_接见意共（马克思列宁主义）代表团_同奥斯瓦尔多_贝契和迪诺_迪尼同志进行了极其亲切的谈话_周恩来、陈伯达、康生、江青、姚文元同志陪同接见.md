Title: Our Most Beloved Great Leader Chairman Mao Receives the Italian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist) Delegation and Has an Extremely Cordial Conversation with Comrades Osvaldo Becchi and Dino Di Ni
Author:
Date: 1968-08-14
Edition: 1

Our most beloved great leader, Chairman Mao Zedong, received the Italian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist) delegation, which was invited to visit our country. The delegation consists of Osvaldo Becchi, a member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist), and Dino Di Ni, a member of the Political Bureau.

Chairman Mao had an extremely cordial conversation with them.

Comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Jiang Qing, and Yao Wenyuan accompanied the reception.

Our most beloved great leader, Chairman Mao Zedong, on August 13, received the Italian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist) delegation, which was invited to visit our country. The delegation consists of Osvaldo Becchi, a member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist), and Dino Di Ni, a member of the Political Bureau, and had an extremely cordial conversation with them.

### 我们最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席　接见意共（马克思列宁主义）代表团　同奥斯瓦尔多·贝契和迪诺·迪尼同志进行了极其亲切的谈话　周恩来、陈伯达、康生、江青、姚文元同志陪同接见

1968-08-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　我们最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席
　　接见意共（马克思列宁主义）代表团
　　同奥斯瓦尔多·贝契和迪诺·迪尼同志进行了极其亲切的谈话
　　周恩来、陈伯达、康生、江青、姚文元同志陪同接见
    新华社十三日讯　我们最敬爱的伟大领袖、中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志，今天接见了应邀正在我国进行访问的意大利共产党（马克思列宁主义）代表团。这个代表团，由意大利共产党（马克思列宁主义）中央委员会政治局委员、书记处书记奥斯瓦尔多·贝契和政治局委员迪诺·迪尼同志组成。
    毛主席同他们进行了极其亲切的谈话。
    陪同接见的，有周恩来、陈伯达、康生、江青、姚文元同志。
    我们最敬爱的伟大领袖、中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志，八月十三日接见了应邀正在我国进行访问，由意大利共产党（马克思列宁主义）中央委员会政治局委员、书记处书记奥斯瓦尔多·贝契和政治局委员迪诺·迪尼同志组成的意大利共产党（马克思列宁主义）代表团，同他们进行了极其亲切的谈话。
