Title: Celebrating Romania's National Day
Author:
Date: 1968-08-26
Edition: 6

The Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries and the China-Romania Friendship Association held a reception in Beijing yesterday to celebrate Romania's National Day.

Vice Premier Chen Yi and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo attended the reception.

Romanian Ambassador to China Dumba and diplomatic staff from the embassy were invited to attend the reception.

At the reception, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries Ding Xilin and Ambassador Dumba raised their glasses in a toast, wishing for the continuous development of the friendship between the peoples of China and Romania.

Attendees also included relevant officials from China, including Qiao Guanhua, Li Qiang, Pan Zhenwu, and Zhou Rongguo, representatives from the Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Team of the capital's workers, representatives of the Five Good Soldiers from the Beijing Garrison, leaders of revolutionary mass organizations in Beijing, and leaders of the China-Romania Friendship People's Commune Revolutionary Committee.

After the reception, a Romanian film was screened.

Today, the China-Romania Friendship Commune held a film evening to celebrate Romania's National Day. Ambassador Dumba and diplomatic staff from the embassy attended the film evening.

### 庆祝罗马尼亚国庆  对外友协和中罗友协举行招待会

1968-08-26
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝罗马尼亚国庆
    对外友协和中罗友协举行招待会
    新华社二十五日讯对外友协和中罗友协昨天在北京举行招待会，庆祝罗马尼亚国庆。
    国务院副总理陈毅，人大常委会副委员长郭沫若，出席了招待会。
    罗马尼亚驻中国大使杜马和大使馆外交官员应邀出席了招待会。
    招待会上，对外友协副会长丁西林和杜马大使先后举杯，共祝中罗两国人民的友谊不断发展。
    出席招待会的，还有我国有关方面负责人乔冠华、李强、潘振武、周荣国，首都工人毛泽东思想宣传队代表，驻京部队五好战士代表，北京市革命群众组织负责成员，和中罗友好人民公社革命委员会负责人。
    会后，放映了罗马尼亚电影。
    今天，中罗友好公社为庆祝罗马尼亚国庆，举行电影晚会。杜马大使和大使馆外交官员出席了电影晚会。
