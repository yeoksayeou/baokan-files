Title: Military and Civilians in West Ninh Province Annihilate Over 3,500 Enemy Troops in Six Days; People's Armed Forces in Quang Ngai Province Attack Bases and Outposts, Dealing Heavy Blows to U.S. Puppet Forces
Author:
Date: 1968-08-31
Edition: 6

	The General Command of the Liberation Army in the West Ninh battlefield announced in a special bulletin on August 25 that the military and civilians of West Ninh Province, from the night of August 17 to August 23, launched continuous attacks on the enemy, annihilating over 3,500 enemy troops, including 2,700 U.S. aggressor troops. In the battle, the military and civilians of West Ninh Province also destroyed 455 enemy vehicles and 37 heavy artillery pieces, and shot down 17 enemy aircraft.

	The bulletin pointed out that the 18 battalions of the U.S. 25th Infantry Division's 1st Reinforced Brigade in West Ninh Province all suffered heavy losses, with 14 battalions being annihilated or severely damaged. At the same time, under the attacks of the military and civilians of West Ninh Province, nearly 400 puppet soldiers and officials defected to the people's side.

	According to a broadcast by the Liberation Radio of South Vietnam, the people's liberation armed forces in Quang Ngai Province, from the night of August 21 to August 23, launched consecutive attacks on many enemy military outposts in the suburbs of Quang Ngai City, Phu Yen County, and Son Ha County. They annihilated an entire U.S. Army battalion, severely damaged a puppet regiment and a battalion, and killed or wounded hundreds of enemy troops.

	The report said that on August 21, the people's liberation armed forces in Quang Ngai Province launched an attack on the enemy outpost in the Go Hoi area, 8 kilometers west of Quang Ngai City, and consecutively defeated the enemy's two reinforcements, annihilating six puppet battalions. They also killed or wounded hundreds of enemy troops, destroyed eight armored vehicles, shot down or damaged two aircraft, and seized many weapons and military supplies.

	In Phu Yen County, the people's liberation armed forces launched four consecutive attacks on the U.S. 198th Infantry Brigade and the 11th Infantry Brigade stationed at the Phu Lien Base and Tho San Base on August 23. They annihilated a U.S. aggressor battalion, killing or wounding over 450 U.S. troops, and destroyed five artillery pieces and six vehicles.

	In Son Ha County, the people's liberation armed forces, from the night of August 22 to the night of August 23, consecutively surrounded and attacked the enemy, annihilating over 170 enemy troops, and destroying many enemy warehouses and fortifications.

	According to another report by the Liberation Communication Agency of South Vietnam, from August 2 to August 12, the guerrilla forces and local troops in various parts of Quang Ngai Province consecutively attacked the enemy, annihilating nearly 530 enemy troops, destroying four bulldozers, demolishing 20 enemy barracks, leveling more than ten "strategic hamlets" and residential concentration areas, and damaging 15 kilometers of roads.

### 南越西宁省军民六天歼敌三千五  广义省人民武装攻基地打据点沉重打击美伪军

1968-08-31
第6版()
专栏：

    南越西宁省军民六天歼敌三千五
    广义省人民武装攻基地打据点沉重打击美伪军
    新华社河内二十九日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方西宁战场解放军指挥部二十五日在一项特别公报中宣布，西宁省军民在八月十七日深夜到二十三日期间连续进攻敌人，共消灭敌人三千五百多名，其中包括美国侵略军二千七百名。西宁省军民在战斗中还击毁敌人军车四百五十五辆和重型大炮三十七门，击落敌机十七架。
    公报指出，美国第二十五步兵师在西宁省的第一加强旅的十八个连，都全部挨了揍，其中有十四个连被歼灭或受到重创。与此同时，在西宁省军民的进攻下，还有近四百名伪军和伪政权人员投向人民方面。
    新华社河内二十九日电　据越南南方解放电台广播：越南南方广义省人民解放武装力量，在八月二十一日夜间到二十三日，接连进攻广义市郊、平山县和山河县的许多敌军据点，全歼美军一个营，重创伪军一个团和一个营，另外还打死打伤敌人数百名。
    消息说，广义省人民解放武装力量在二十一日向广义市西面八公里的鹅黄地区的敌军据点发动进攻，并且接连打败了敌人的两次增援，歼灭伪军六个连，另外还打死打伤敌人数百名，击毁装甲车八辆，击落击伤飞机两架，缴获许多武器和军用物资。
    在平山县，人民解放武装力量在二十三日接连向驻扎在平莲基地和土山基地的美国第一九八步兵旅和第十一步兵旅发动四次进攻，歼灭美国侵略军一个营，打死打伤美军四百五十多名，击毁大炮五门和军车六辆。
    在山河县，人民解放武装力量从二十二日深夜到二十三日深夜连续包围和进攻敌军，消灭敌人一百七十多名，摧毁了敌人许多仓库和工事。
    另据越南南方解放通讯社报道，广义省各地游击队和地方部队，从八月二日到十二日的十天内接连袭击敌人，消灭敌人近五百三十名，击毁推土机四辆，摧毁敌军营房二十幢，夷平“战略村”和居民集中区十多个，破坏了十五公里长的公路。
