Title: Revolutionary Workers and Technicians Develop First Domestic Salt Harvesting Machine
Author:
Date: 1968-08-25
Edition: 2

Revolutionary workers of the Qingdao Salt Administration and scientific and technical personnel of the Salt Administration of the First Light Industry Ministry, armed with Mao Zedong Thought, have successfully developed China's first 20-horsepower self-propelled short-term crystallization light salt harvesting machine. The efficiency of this new machine is more than ten times that of manual salt harvesting. The photo was taken by a Xinhua News Agency reporter.
