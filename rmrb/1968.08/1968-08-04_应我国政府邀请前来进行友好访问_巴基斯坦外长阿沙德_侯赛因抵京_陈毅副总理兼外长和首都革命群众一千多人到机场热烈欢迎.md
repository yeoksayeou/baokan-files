Title: Pakistani Foreign Minister Ashad Hussain Arrives in Beijing for a Friendly Visit
Author:
Date: 1968-08-04
Edition: 1

At the invitation of the Chinese government, Pakistani Foreign Minister Ashad Hussain arrived in Beijing today for a friendly visit.

	New China News Agency, August 3rd: Pakistani Foreign Minister Ashad Hussain, at the invitation of the Chinese government, arrived in Beijing by airplane today.

	Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, along with over a thousand revolutionary masses from the capital, went to the airport to give him a warm welcome.

	Today, the flags of China and Pakistan were flying high in the sky above the capital airport. Revolutionary masses from the capital held up portraits of our great leader Chairman Mao and Pakistani President Ayub Khan, gathering at the airport, beating drums and gongs, welcoming the friends from the neighboring friendly country of Pakistan.

	Minister Ashad Hussain and his wife, along with other accompanying personnel, stepped off the plane to a warm round of applause. Subsequently, Vice Premier Chen Yi accompanied Minister Ashad Hussain and others around the field to meet with the welcoming crowd. At this time, the revolutionary masses waved red copies of the "Quotations from Chairman Mao," shouting slogans: "Long live the friendship between the peoples of China and Pakistan!" "Firmly support the Pakistani people in their struggle to maintain national independence and oppose foreign aggression!" "Firmly support the Kashmiri people in their struggle for national self-determination!" "Firmly support the Vietnamese people's war of resistance against American aggression for national salvation!" "Firmly support the anti-imperialist, anti-colonialist struggles of the peoples of Asia, Africa, and Latin America for national independence and the maintenance of national independence!" "Firmly support the revolutionary struggles of the peoples of Europe and North America!" "Down with U.S. imperialism!" "Down with modern revisionism headed by the Soviet revisionist clique!" "Long live the invincible Marxism-Leninism-Mao Zedong Thought!" "Long live the complete victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!" "Long live the Pakistani people!" "Salute to President Ayub Khan!" "Long live Chairman Mao!"

	Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included relevant leaders from the government departments, the People's Liberation Army, the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee, and other relevant units, such as Wen Yucheng, Han Nianlong, and Zhou Rongguo. Representatives of the Five Good Soldiers of the Beijing Garrison and leaders of various revolutionary mass organizations in Beijing were also present.

	The responsible person from the Protocol Department of the Chinese Foreign Ministry, who went to Shanghai to welcome the delegation, and Pakistani Ambassador to China Sultan M. Khan, accompanied Minister Ashad Hussain to Beijing.

	Diplomatic officials from the Pakistani Embassy in China and envoys from various countries to China also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

	When Minister Ashad Hussain and his delegation passed through Shanghai today, they were warmly welcomed by leaders of the Shanghai Municipal Revolutionary Committee, including Ji Yingwu and Feng Guozhu.

### 应我国政府邀请前来进行友好访问　巴基斯坦外长阿沙德·侯赛因抵京　陈毅副总理兼外长和首都革命群众一千多人到机场热烈欢迎

1968-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　应我国政府邀请前来进行友好访问
　　巴基斯坦外长阿沙德·侯赛因抵京
　　陈毅副总理兼外长和首都革命群众一千多人到机场热烈欢迎
    新华社三日讯　巴基斯坦外交部长阿沙德·侯赛因，应我国政府邀请前来进行友好访问，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅和首都革命群众一千多人，前往机场热烈欢迎。
    今天，首都机场上空飘扬着中国和巴基斯坦两国国旗。首都革命群众高举着我们伟大领袖毛主席的画像和巴基斯坦总统阿尤布·汗的画像，聚集在机场上，敲锣打鼓，欢迎来自友好邻邦的巴基斯坦朋友。
    阿沙德·侯赛因部长和夫人以及其他随行人员，在热烈的掌声中走下飞机。随后，陈毅副总理陪同阿沙德·侯赛因部长等绕场一周，同欢迎群众见面，这时，革命群众挥动红光闪闪的《毛主席语录》，高呼口号：“中国和巴基斯坦两国人民的友谊万岁！”“坚决支持巴基斯坦人民维护民族独立、反对外来侵略的斗争！”“坚决支持克什米尔人民争取民族自决权利的斗争！”“坚决支持越南人民的抗美救国战争！”“坚决支持亚、非、拉各国人民的反帝反殖、争取和维护民族独立的斗争！”“坚决支持欧洲、北美各国人民的革命斗争！”“打倒以美国为首的帝国主义！”“打倒以苏修领导集团为中心的现代修正主义！”“战无不胜的马克思主义、列宁主义、毛泽东思想万岁！”“无产阶级文化大革命的全面胜利万岁！”“巴基斯坦人民万岁！”“向阿尤布·汗总统致敬！”“毛主席万岁！”
    前往机场欢迎的，还有政府有关部门、中国人民解放军、北京市革命委员会等有关方面的负责人温玉成、韩念龙、周荣国，驻北京部队五好战士代表，北京市各革命群众组织的负责成员。
    专程前往上海迎接的我国外交部礼宾司负责人和巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆·汗，陪同阿沙德·侯赛因部长到达北京。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使馆外交官员，各国驻中国使节，也到机场迎接。
    阿沙德·侯赛因部长等今天路过上海的时候，受到上海市革命委员会领导成员姬应伍、冯国柱等的热烈欢迎。
