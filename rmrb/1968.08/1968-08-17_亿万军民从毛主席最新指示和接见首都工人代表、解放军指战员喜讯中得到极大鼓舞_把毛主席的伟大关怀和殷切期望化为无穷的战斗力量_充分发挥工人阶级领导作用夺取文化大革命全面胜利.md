Title: The Billions of Military and Civilians are Greatly Inspired by Chairman Mao's Latest Instructions and the News of His Reception of Capital Workers and PLA Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1968-08-17
Edition: 2

The billions of military and civilians are greatly inspired by Chairman Mao's latest instructions and the news of his reception of capital workers and PLA soldiers. They will turn Chairman Mao's great care and earnest expectations into boundless fighting strength. They will fully play the leading role of the working class to achieve a comprehensive victory in the Cultural Revolution.

According to a Xinhua News Agency report dated the 16th, at the time when the country is celebrating Chairman Mao's extremely important latest instruction, "Our country has 700 million people. The working class is the leading class. The leading role of the working class in the Cultural Revolution and all work must be fully exerted. The working class must also continuously raise its political awareness in the struggle," our most beloved great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin Biao warmly received the representatives of the capital's working class and PLA soldiers. This great news has greatly inspired the working class and billions of military and civilians across the country. With boundless loyalty to Chairman Mao, they have decided to turn Chairman Mao's great care and earnest expectations into boundless fighting strength. They will more closely unite around the great commander Chairman Mao and the proletarian headquarters led by Chairman Mao with Vice Chairman Lin as his deputy. They will fully exert the leading role of the working class in all aspects, thoroughly smash the reactionary bourgeoisie's "multi-center theory," and use the invincible Mao Zedong Thought to unify understanding, unify steps, and unify actions. They will achieve a comprehensive victory in the proletarian Cultural Revolution.

The great news spread, from the capital to the borders, from cities to the countryside, across our vast motherland, everywhere is in jubilation. Revolutionary masses repeatedly and excitedly shouted, "Long live the great leader Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years! Ten thousand times ten thousand years!" In major cities and industrial areas, firecrackers and drumbeats never stopped, parade queues never ended, and banners expressing joy and resolve to Chairman Mao covered the streets. Everyone was immersed in the joy of the working class's festival. Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, Shenyang, Guangzhou, Changchun, Nanjing, Jinan, Zhengzhou, Changsha, Guiyang, Lanzhou, and many other cities held celebrations and oath-taking meetings with thousands, tens of thousands, to hundreds of thousands of people. People enthusiastically shouted: Chairman Mao's reception and the issuance of his latest instructions are the greatest care, greatest trust, greatest education, and greatest inspiration for the working class and revolutionary people across the country! Factories, enterprises, institutions, schools, and military barracks held study sessions, discussion meetings, and sharing meetings. They organized Mao Zedong Thought study classes to further study and understand Chairman Mao's latest instructions. Some factories and enterprises immediately organized Mao Zedong Thought propaganda teams to carry out extensive and in-depth propaganda activities, making Chairman Mao's latest instructions known to every household and deeply rooted in people's hearts.

Comrades from the capital and other places who participated in the workers and peasants Mao Zedong Thought propaganda teams and workers Mao Zedong Thought propaganda teams drew boundless spiritual strength from Chairman Mao's great care. Comrades from the capital's workers and peasants Mao Zedong Thought propaganda team, after seeing the great leader Chairman Mao yesterday, held a grand celebration all night, expressing their loyalty and resolve to Chairman Mao. They said with great excitement, Chairman Mao recently gave us precious gifts, followed by the latest instructions to fully exert the leading role of the working class, and yesterday he personally received us. Truly, the party's favor is greater than the heavens, and parents' love is not as great as Chairman Mao's love. We solemnly vow: We will forever be loyal to Chairman Mao, forever loyal to Mao Zedong Thought, forever loyal to Chairman Mao's revolutionary line, and defend with our lives the proletarian headquarters led by Chairman Mao with Vice Chairman Lin as his deputy. We will be friends with those who support the proletarian headquarters and fight to the death with those who oppose it. We will definitely follow the great commander Chairman Mao and be at the forefront in the battle to achieve a comprehensive victory in the proletarian Cultural Revolution and create new achievements. The revolutionary old workers of the Tianjin San Tiao Shi Mao Zedong Thought propaganda team, inspired by the news of Chairman Mao receiving the representatives of the capital's working class, today rushed to universities to propagate and implement Chairman Mao's series of latest instructions. They said, Chairman Mao cares for and supports us the most. We are the closest to Chairman Mao. We will do as Chairman Mao says. We will definitely, together with revolutionary teachers, students, and staff, thoroughly criticize the reactionary revisionist educational line of China's Khrushchev and carry the proletarian educational revolution to the end.

The Shanghai workers' class united fighting organization—the Shanghai Workers Revolutionary Rebel Headquarters—on behalf of Shanghai's one million workers, firmly declared: Chairman Mao's latest instructions are the great guideline for us to strengthen our ranks. We must continuously arm ourselves with Mao Zedong Thought, overcome various non-proletarian ideologies, achieve ideological revolutionization, and truly build our working class into a force armed with Mao Zedong Thought, a force that serves the people wholeheartedly, and an invincible force. We must thoroughly criticize the reactionary bourgeoisie's "multi-center theory," timely expose and thoroughly smash the plots of a handful of counter-revolutionaries who attempt to sabotage and split the proletarian headquarters, further strengthen our revolutionary nature, scientific nature, and organizational discipline. Under the command of the proletarian headquarters led by Chairman Mao, we will contribute all our strength to achieve a comprehensive victory in the proletarian Cultural Revolution.

The revolutionary workers of Anshan Steel Company, Baotou Steel Company, Wuhan Steel Company, Nanchang Steel Plant, Guiyang Steel Plant, and other steel enterprises said, Chairman Mao is our great savior, the reddest red sun in our hearts. We must closely unite around the leadership center of the proletarian headquarters led by Chairman Mao with Vice Chairman Lin as his deputy, forever obey this center, step by step follow this center, and defend this center with our lives. The revolutionary workers of the First Automobile Works in Changchun, the East Is Red Tractor Factory in Luoyang, Beijing Micro Motor Factory, Jinan Machine Tool Factory, and other machinery factories expressed that Chairman Mao's latest instructions have pointed out the direction for our working class. We must fully implement Chairman Mao's latest instructions in all work and play the role of the main force. The railway workers of Zhengzhou Railway Bureau, Hohhot Railway Bureau, Nanchang Railway Division, and the "Mao Zedong" locomotive crew, and the miners of Fushun, Benxi, and Kaiping coal mines, after hearing the news, were all excited and inspired. They all expressed that they must carry forward the thorough revolutionary spirit of the proletariat, strengthen the revolutionary nature and organizational discipline of the proletariat, consolidate the revolutionary great alliance and revolutionary tripartite combination, and respond to Chairman Mao's care with outstanding achievements in both revolution and production. The revolutionary sailors of the advanced collective "East Is Red No. 35" on the Yangtze River excitedly said, we the working class will not disappoint Chairman Mao's expectations. We must always study and apply Mao Zedong Thought, follow Chairman Mao's great strategic deployment, and achieve a comprehensive victory in the proletarian Cultural Revolution.

The great leader Chairman Mao taught: "The final distinction between revolutionary, non-revolutionary, or counter-revolutionary intellectuals is whether they are willing and able to unite with the workers, peasants, and soldiers." Revolutionary intellectuals and revolutionary Red Guards across the country, inspired by the great news of Chairman Mao receiving the representatives of the capital's working class, reviewed Chairman Mao's teachings, seriously studied Chairman Mao's latest instructions on fully exerting the leading role of the working class, and all expressed their willingness to consciously accept the leadership of the working class, use the worldview of the proletariat to transform themselves, and resolutely follow the path of uniting with workers, peasants, and soldiers as indicated by Chairman Mao.

Revolutionary Red Guards and revolutionary teachers and students from Beijing Tsinghua University, Shanghai Fudan University, Nanjing University, Suzhou Medical College, Hefei University of Technology, Anhui Institute of Technology, Anhui Medical College, Zhejiang University, Harbin Institute of Technology, Qinghai Nationalities Institute, and other schools, after studying Chairman Mao's latest instructions, received profound education. Many revolutionary teachers and students emphasized that the working class is the main force of the proletarian Cultural Revolution and also the main force of the proletarian educational revolution. The educational revolution in schools must be led by the working class to be carried out to the end. The Red Guards of Shijiazhuang City's Revolutionary Committee reviewed and summarized the experience of organizing nearly 40,000 revolutionary teachers and students to go deep into factories, mines, rural areas, and military units to learn from workers, poor and lower-middle peasants, and the PLA. The revolutionary young soldiers of the Red Guards unanimously believed that only by listening to Chairman Mao's words, honestly accepting the leadership of the working class, and following the path of uniting with workers, peasants, and soldiers, can intellectuals achieve ideological revolutionization and follow the bright path.

The graduates of Qinghai Province Xining City Commercial School, at a study meeting, solemnly vowed to Chairman Mao: We will definitely follow Chairman Mao's words; we will definitely follow the path of uniting with workers and peasants. We will definitely implement Chairman Mao's latest instructions and strive to be as selfless as the working class, wholeheartedly serving the people. The Red Guards of Shanghai's higher education institutions' Revolutionary Committee and Shanghai's middle schools' Revolutionary Committee said, we Red Guards will definitely follow Chairman Mao's teachings, learn from the working class, and be the little students of the working class. We must firmly criticize the reactionary "multi-center theory," thoroughly overcome the bourgeois factionalism and anarchist trends in our ranks, oppose all two-faced behavior, further consolidate the revolutionary great alliance and revolutionary tripartite combination in thought, strengthen revolutionary organizational discipline, closely unite around the proletarian headquarters led by Chairman Mao with Vice Chairman Lin as his deputy, and achieve a comprehensive victory in the proletarian Cultural Revolution.

Members of revolutionary committees and PLA soldiers fighting on the "three supports, two militaries" frontline, inspired by the great news of Chairman Mao receiving the representatives of the capital's working class, are further seriously studying Chairman Mao's latest instructions and quickly taking measures to firmly implement them. Many members of revolutionary committees and "three supports, two militaries" personnel, reviewing more than two years of proletarian Cultural Revolution struggle practice, deeply felt that the working class has the deepest feelings for the great leader Chairman Mao and most firmly implements Chairman Mao's latest instructions. Believing in the working class is the direction, relying on the working class is powerful, and fully exerting the leading role of the working class can make the proletarian dictatorship more consolidated. They expressed that they must further arm the working class with the great Mao Zedong Thought, do a good job for the working class, firmly support the revolutionary actions and pioneering spirit of the working class, fully exert their leading role in the Cultural Revolution and all work, and carry the unprecedented proletarian Cultural Revolution to the end!
