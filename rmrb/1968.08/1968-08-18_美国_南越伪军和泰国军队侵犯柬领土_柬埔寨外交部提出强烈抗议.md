Title: U.S.-South Vietnamese Puppet Army and Thai Military Invade Cambodian Territory
Author:
Date: 1968-08-18
Edition: 6

	U.S.-South Vietnamese Puppet Army and Thai Military Invade Cambodian Territory
	Cambodian Foreign Ministry Issues Strong Protest

	New China News Agency, Phnom Penh, August 14 — According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on August 13, the Cambodian Foreign Ministry on August 10 sent separate notes to the U.S. government and the Saigon puppet regime, strongly protesting the 15 instances of invasion and aggression by the U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet army into Cambodian territory in June 1968, which resulted in casualties and property damage among the peaceful residents along the Cambodian border.

	The notes stated that the Royal Government demands that the U.S. government and the Saigon puppet regime cease these criminal acts and compensate the families of the victims for their losses.

	New China News Agency, Phnom Penh, August 15 — According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on August 14, the Cambodian Foreign Ministry on August 10 sent a note to the Bangkok puppet regime, strongly protesting the multiple incursions by the Thai military into Cambodian territory in Oddar Meanchey and Battambang provinces on July 12, 13, and 16, during which Cambodian residents were abducted.

	The note demands that the Thai puppet regime cease these bandit-like actions and immediately release the abducted individuals.

### 美国—南越伪军和泰国军队侵犯柬领土  柬埔寨外交部提出强烈抗议

1968-08-18
第6版()
专栏：

    美国—南越伪军和泰国军队侵犯柬领土
    柬埔寨外交部提出强烈抗议
    新华社金边十四日电　据柬埔寨新闻社十三日报道，柬埔寨外交部八月十日分别照会美国政府和西贡傀儡集团，强烈抗议美国—南越伪军在一九六八年六月十五次侵犯和侵略柬埔寨，使柬埔寨边境的和平居民遭受伤亡和财产遭到损失。
  照会说，王国政府要求美国政府和西贡傀儡集团停止这种罪恶行径并对受难者的家属赔偿损失。
    新华社金边十五日电　据柬埔寨新闻社十四日报道，柬埔寨外交部八月十日照会曼谷傀儡当局，强烈抗议泰国军队七月十二日、十三日和十六日多次侵入柬埔寨奥多棉藏省和马德望省境内，劫走柬埔寨居民。
    照会要求泰国傀儡当局停止这种强盗行径并立即释放被劫走的人员。
