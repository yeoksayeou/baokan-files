Title: Sudan Government Trains Youth to Join Palestinian Armed Struggle
Author:
Date: 1968-08-01
Edition: 5

The Sudanese government is training young volunteers to join the armed struggle of the Palestinian people against aggression. Sudanese Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Ali Abdel Rahman recently praised the Palestinian resistance movement as a courageous and just cause during a meeting with a representative of a Palestinian nationalist organization. He stated that the Sudanese government fully supports the armed struggle of the Palestinian people.

The vice premier also mentioned that the Council of Ministers has decided to establish a committee to train and prepare Sudanese youth who volunteer to join the armed struggle in Palestine.

### 苏丹政府培训青年参加巴勒斯坦人民武装斗争　赞扬巴勒斯坦抵抗运动是英勇正义的事业

1968-08-01
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏丹政府培训青年参加巴勒斯坦人民武装斗争
　　赞扬巴勒斯坦抵抗运动是英勇正义的事业
    新华社喀土穆二十九日电　据苏丹《群众日报》最近报道，苏丹政府决定培养训练志愿参加巴勒斯坦人民的反侵略武装斗争的苏丹青年。苏丹副总理兼外交部长阿里·阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼最近在接见巴勒斯坦民族主义组织一位代表时，赞扬巴勒斯坦抵抗运动是英勇正义的事业。他表示，苏丹政府完全支持巴勒斯坦人民的武装斗争。
    这位副总理还说，苏丹部长会议已决定成立一个委员会，对志愿到巴勒斯坦参加武装斗争的苏丹青年进行培养训练。
