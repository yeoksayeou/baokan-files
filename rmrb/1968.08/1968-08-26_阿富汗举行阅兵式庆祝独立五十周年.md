Title: Afghanistan Holds Military Parade to Celebrate 50th Anniversary of Independence
Author:
Date: 1968-08-26
Edition: 6

Afghanistan held a military parade in its capital, Kabul, on the 23rd to celebrate the 50th anniversary of its independence.

King Mohammad Zahir Shah spoke before the parade, reviewing and praising the history of the Afghan people's bloody struggle against British colonialists.

The Afghan people defeated the British colonial army on May 27, 1919, and declared independence on August 23.

### 阿富汗举行阅兵式庆祝独立五十周年

1968-08-26
第6版()
专栏：

    阿富汗举行阅兵式庆祝独立五十周年
    新华社喀布尔二十三日电　阿富汗首都喀布尔二十三日举行阅兵仪式，庆祝阿富汗独立五十周年。
    国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿在阅兵前讲话，回顾并且赞扬了阿富汗人民与英国殖民主义者浴血奋战的历史。
    阿富汗人民一九一九年五月二十七日打败了英国殖民军后，于八月二十三日宣布独立。
