Title: Illinois Electrical Installers Persist in Strike; Telephone Lines in Chicago Paralyzed, Dealing New Blow to Monopoly Groups
Author:
Date: 1968-08-14
Edition: 6

		Electrical installers in the U.S. state of Illinois have persisted in their strike, paralyzing hundreds of telephone lines in Chicago and dealing a new blow to American monopoly capital groups.

		According to a report from Washington on August 13, electrical installers at the Bell Telephone Company of Illinois, numbering over 10,000, have been on strike since May 8 and have persisted for over three months. In recent days, the striking workers' fighting spirit has grown increasingly strong, and they have repeatedly cut the cables of this telephone company. A spokesperson for the company admitted that since the strike began, there have been 230 instances of cable cutting.

### 美国伊利诺斯州电气安装工人坚持罢工　芝加哥市内电话线路陷于停顿给垄断集团新的打击

1968-08-14
第6版()
专栏：

　　美国伊利诺斯州电气安装工人坚持罢工
　　芝加哥市内电话线路陷于停顿给垄断集团新的打击
    新华社十三日讯　华盛顿消息：美国伊利诺斯州罢工的电话工人在八月十一日截断了两条电缆，使芝加哥市内的几百条电话线路陷于停顿，给了美国垄断资本集团一个新的打击。
    伊利诺斯州贝尔电话公司一万多名电气安装工人的大罢工，从五月八日开始，已坚持三个多月。最近几天，罢工工人的战斗精神越来越强烈，他们不断截断这家电话公司的电缆。据这家电话公司的发言人承认，从罢工爆发以来，已发生二百三十起截断电缆的事件。
