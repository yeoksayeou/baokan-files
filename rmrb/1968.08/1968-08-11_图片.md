Title: Revolutionary Workers of Kailuan Coal Mine Criticize the Counter-Revolutionary Fallacies
Author:
Date: 1968-08-11
Edition: 3

Revolutionary workers of the Kailuan Coal Mine are using various forms to extensively carry out the revolutionary great criticism. This is a scene from the Mao Zedong Thought study class at Tangjiazhuang Mine, where an old worker uses his personal experiences to vehemently denounce the counter-revolutionary fallacy of "exploitation is meritorious" advocated by the Chinese Khrushchev.
