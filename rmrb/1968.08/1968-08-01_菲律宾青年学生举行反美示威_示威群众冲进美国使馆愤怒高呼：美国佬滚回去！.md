Title: Filipino Youth Students Hold Anti-American Protest
Author:
Date: 1968-08-01
Edition: 6

	Filipino youth students held an anti-American protest.

	Protesters stormed the U.S. embassy, angrily shouting: "Yankees, go home!"

	New China News Agency, August 31st: Manila reports: Filipino youth students in the capital Manila held a protest march on the 30th.

	According to reports, over 300 youth students held a protest march in the evening of the 30th, protesting against the U.S. invasion of Vietnam. The protesters held anti-American slogans, strongly condemning the U.S. imperialists' heinous crimes against the Vietnamese people. The protesters also condemned the Filipino authorities for following the U.S., making the Philippines "Americanized."

	On the same day, about 200 youth students, holding anti-American slogans, protested against the U.S. Marines killing a Filipino youth. The protesters stormed the U.S. embassy, angrily shouting: "Yankees, go home!"

### 菲律宾青年学生举行反美示威　示威群众冲进美国使馆愤怒高呼：美国佬滚回去！

1968-08-01
第6版()
专栏：

　　菲律宾青年学生举行反美示威
　　示威群众冲进美国使馆愤怒高呼：美国佬滚回去！
    新华社三十一日讯  马尼拉消息：菲律宾首都马尼拉的青年学生，三十日举行反美示威游行。
    据报道，三百多名青年学生三十日晚上举行示威游行，抗议美国侵越战争。示威群众高举反美标语牌，强烈谴责美帝国主义对越南人民犯下的滔天罪行。示威群众还谴责菲律宾当局追随美国，使菲律宾“美国化”。
    同一天，约二百名青年学生，举着反美标语牌，抗议美国海军陆战队打死一名菲律宾青年。示威者冲进了美国大使馆，愤怒高呼：“美国佬滚回去！”
