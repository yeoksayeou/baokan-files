Title: Lao Patriotic Forces Bravely Fight to Expand Victories
Author:
Date: 1968-08-31
Edition: 6

Lao patriotic armed forces bravely fight to expand victories
Patriotic military and civilians in Xam Neua and other provinces shoot down another batch of American aggressor planes

KANKO, August 26 (Xinhua) - According to a report from the Pathet Lao News Agency, local forces and guerrillas in Laos continue to strike the enemy in various places, expanding their victories.

On August 17, local forces and guerrillas in Xam Neua Province attacked a nest of bandits, who were raised by the Americans, in Phou Khen. After a short battle, they captured this bandit nest.

In this battle, the patriotic armed forces killed and wounded 61 bandits, seizing a batch of weapons and military supplies, including a mortar and a recoilless rifle. On the same day, the patriotic armed forces also fired mortar shells at the bandit positions in the Mang Song and Nakhang areas, causing enemy casualties.

The report said that from early July to August 9, local forces and guerrillas in Xam Neua Province annihilated 52 bandits and special forces raised by the Americans, seizing 32 weapons.

Patriotic armed forces in Xieng Khouang Province killed 39 enemy soldiers and seized 11 weapons in the two weeks leading up to August 13.

According to a broadcast by the Pathet Lao radio station, local forces and guerrillas in Udomxay Province struck bandit harassment and counterattacked enemy "sweeps" eight times in July, killing a total of 152 enemy soldiers, including 120 killed. Among the killed enemy were a battalion commander and two company commanders. Additionally, local forces and guerrillas seized a batch of weapons and military supplies.

Local forces and guerrillas in Hongsa County, Savannakhet Province, actively counterattacked enemy attacks on liberated areas, killing 31 enemy soldiers from July 4 to July 14, including a company commander and two platoon leaders.

KANKO, August 30 (Xinhua) - According to a broadcast by the Pathet Lao radio station, on August 15, the guerrillas and people of Mang Hongsa, Savannakhet Province, shot down an American aggressor plane with rifles.

KANKO, August 28 (Xinhua) - According to a broadcast by the Pathet Lao radio station, on August 24 at 15:20, multiple batches of American imperialist jet planes carried out savage bombing and strafing in densely populated areas of Xam Neua Province. The well-prepared patriotic military and civilians of Xam Neua Province struck back at the invading enemy, shooting down two American aggressor planes.

KANKO, August 27 (Xinhua) - According to a broadcast by the Pathet Lao radio station, on August 24, patriotic military and civilians in Xam Neua Province hit two enemy planes, causing them to catch fire during the battle against American air aggressors.

KANKO (Xinhua) - According to a broadcast by the Pathet Lao radio station, on July 17, guerrillas in eastern Laos shot down an invading American aggressor plane with rifles. Previously, on July 6, guerrillas in this province also shot down a reconnaissance plane that had infiltrated the liberated area with rifles.

KANKO (Xinhua) - According to a broadcast by the Pathet Lao radio station, under the heavy blows of the Lao patriotic military and civilians, rightist military forces in Laos are increasingly surrendering to the people.

The broadcast said that facing the brilliant victories of the Lao patriotic military and civilians and the heavy defeats of American imperialism and its lackeys, more and more rightist military officers and soldiers in Laos have realized that to escape the fate of being cannon fodder for American imperialism, there is only one way—surrender to the people. In Savannakhet Province alone, 207 rightist military personnel surrendered to the people in July.

From July 12 to 23, 60 rightist military personnel and bandits in Xam Neua Province surrendered to the people.

Six soldiers from the 16th Regular Battalion of the rightist military stationed in Luang Prabang Province also surrendered to the people on July 26.

### 老挝爱国武装英勇战斗扩大战果  桑怒等省爱国军民又击落一批美国强盗飞机

1968-08-31
第6版()
专栏：

    老挝爱国武装英勇战斗扩大战果
    桑怒等省爱国军民又击落一批美国强盗飞机
    新华社康开二十六日电　据巴特寮通讯社报道，老挝地方部队和游击队继续在各地连续打击敌人，扩大战果。
    桑怒省地方部队和游击队八月十七日袭击了美国豢养的匪徒在这个省富欣沙的巢穴，经过短时间的战斗，就攻克了这个匪巢。
    在这场战斗中，爱国武装力量打死打伤匪徒六十一名，缴获一批武器和军用物资，其中包括一门迫击炮和一门无后座力炮。同一天，爱国武装力量还向芒松和纳康地区的匪徒阵地发射迫击炮弹，使敌军遭到损失。
    报道说，丰沙里省的地方部队和游击队，从七月初到八月九日歼灭美国豢养的匪徒和别动队五十二名，缴获武器三十二件。
    川圹省爱国武装力量在到八月十三日为止的两周内，打死敌人三十九名，缴获武器十一件。
    另据巴特寮电台广播，乌多姆塞省地方部队和游击队七月份打击匪徒骚扰以及反击敌人“扫荡”八次，共歼敌一百五十二名，其中打死一百二十名。在打死的敌人中包括营长一名，连长两名。另外，地方部队和游击队还缴获一批武器和军用物资。
    沙耶武里省洪沙县地方部队和游击队积极反击敌人对解放区的进攻，从七月四日到七月十四日打死敌人三十一名，其中包括连长一名，排长两名。
    新华社康开三十日电据巴特寮电台广播，老挝沙耶武里省芒洪沙游击队和人民八月十五日用步枪击落美国强盗飞机一架。
    新华社康开二十八日电　据巴特寮电台广播，八月二十四日十五时二十分，美帝国主义的多批喷气式飞机对桑怒省人口稠密区进行野蛮轰炸和扫射，严阵以待的桑怒省爱国军民迎头痛击来犯的敌人，击落两架美国强盗飞机。
    新华社康开二十七日电　据巴特寮电台广播，桑怒省爱国军民八月二十四日在反击美国空中强盗的战斗中，使两架敌机中弹起火。
    新华社康开电　据巴特寮电台广播，七月十七日老挝东方省游击队用步枪击落入侵解放区的美国强盗飞机一架。在这以前，这个省的游击队，在七月六日还用步枪击落了窜入解放区的美国侦察机一架。
    新华社康开电　据巴特寮电台广播，在老挝爱国军民的沉重打击下，老挝右派军队纷纷向人民投诚。
    广播说，面对老挝爱国军民取得的辉煌胜利和美帝国主义及其走狗的惨重失败，越来越多的老挝右派军队官兵认识到，要摆脱为美帝国主义充当炮灰的命运，只有一条路——投向人民。仅沙湾拿吉省，七月份就有老挝右派军队二百零七名向人民投诚。
    桑怒省从七月十二日到二十三日，右派军队和土匪共有六十人向人民投诚。
    驻在琅勃拉邦省的老挝右派军队第十六正规营的士兵六人，也在七月二十六日向人民投诚。
