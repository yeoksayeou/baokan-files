Title: The U.S. Imperialists Send Another 4,000 Troops to South Vietnam
Author: 
Date: 1968-08-03
Edition: 6

The U.S. imperialists, while shamelessly peddling the "peace talks" deception, have simultaneously dispatched a large number of troops to South Vietnam. According to an announcement made by the U.S. military command in South Vietnam on August 1, another 4,000 U.S. aggressor troops have recently arrived in South Vietnam. Thus, the total number of U.S. troops in South Vietnam has now reached 540,000.

### 美帝又增派四千侵越炮灰到南越

1968-08-03
第6版()
专栏：

    美帝又增派四千侵越炮灰到南越
    新华社二日讯　西贡消息：美帝国主义在大肆玩弄“和谈”骗局的同时，又大量增派侵略军到南越。据侵越美军司令部一日宣布，又有四千名美国侵略军在最近到达南越。这样，到目前为止，侵越美军总人数已达五十四万。
