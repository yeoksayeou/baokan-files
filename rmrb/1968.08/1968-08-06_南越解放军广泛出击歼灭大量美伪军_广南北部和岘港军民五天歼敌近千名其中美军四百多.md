Title: The Liberation Army of South Vietnam Launches Widespread Attacks, Annihilating a Large Number of American and Puppet Forces
Author:
Date: 1968-08-06
Edition: 6

The Liberation Army of South Vietnam has launched widespread attacks, annihilating a large number of American and puppet forces. In the northern part of Quang Nam and the city of Da Nang, the military and civilians have annihilated nearly a thousand enemy soldiers in five days, including over four hundred American soldiers.

According to a report from the Liberation Communications Agency of South Vietnam, the people's liberation armed forces in Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Gia Lai, and My Tho provinces have recently launched continuous attacks on the enemy, annihilating a large number of American and puppet forces.

From July 22 to July 26, the military and civilians in the northern part of Quang Nam and Da Nang launched fierce attacks on many enemy strongholds. Preliminary reports indicate that nearly a thousand enemy soldiers were annihilated, including over four hundred American aggressors. Thirty-two aircraft were shot down or destroyed, twenty-five military vehicles were destroyed, twenty-two warehouses were burned, and seven bridges were blown up. Additionally, more than two hundred households were liberated.

On July 24, the people's liberation armed forces in the southern part of Quang Nam launched a fierce attack on the command post of the U.S. 198th Infantry Brigade, the Chu Lai airfield, and the parking lot near the airfield. Many American aggressors were annihilated, causing the Chu Lai airfield's runway and barracks to catch fire. Fifteen military vehicles were destroyed, fifty barracks and two bunkers were demolished, and five helicopters sent for rescue were shot down.

On July 22 and 23, the people's liberation armed forces in Quang Ngai province attacked the city of Quang Ngai and fifteen other targets, annihilating nearly two hundred and fifty enemy soldiers and seizing a large amount of military supplies.

On July 21, the people's liberation armed forces in Gia Lai province ambushed an enemy convoy on Highway 19, destroying one armored vehicle and twelve trucks loaded with military supplies, annihilating many American aggressors.

From July 25 to July 27, the people's liberation armed forces in My Tho province continuously bombarded the American aggressors stationed at the Pleiku base for three days, killing and wounding two hundred and twenty enemy soldiers, and destroying many barracks and combat equipment. On the night of July 27, the people's liberation armed forces also attacked the puppet military training ground in Chau Thanh county, killing over two hundred enemy soldiers in a few minutes of battle.

On the night of July 7, the people's liberation armed forces in Soc Trang province completely annihilated a puppet "pacification" unit and severely damaged an enemy security company, killing and wounding over one hundred enemy soldiers. On the night of July 24, the people's liberation armed forces attacked a company of enemy soldiers heading to the Ba Cam stronghold in Chau Thanh county, completely annihilating the enemy company after a short battle.

From July 21 to July 24, the people's liberation armed forces in Go Cong county, Gia Dinh province, continuously repelled multiple counterattacks by American aggressors, annihilating over one hundred enemy soldiers.

From July 14 to July 24, the people's liberation armed forces in Sa Dec province thwarted multiple "sweeping" operations launched by the enemy in the suburbs of Sa Dec city, annihilating and capturing 268 enemy soldiers, destroying or damaging five armored vehicles, destroying one cannon, burning one ammunition depot, and seizing a large amount of military supplies.

### 南越解放军广泛出击歼灭大量美伪军　广南北部和岘港军民五天歼敌近千名其中美军四百多

1968-08-06
第6版()
专栏：

　　南越解放军广泛出击歼灭大量美伪军
　　广南北部和岘港军民五天歼敌近千名其中美军四百多
    新华社河内五日电  据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方广南、广义、嘉莱、美萩等省人民解放武装力量，最近连续袭击敌人，歼灭大量美伪军。
    广南省北部和岘港军民，在七月二十二日到二十六日期间，猛烈进攻敌人许多据点。据初步消息，共消灭敌人近一千名，其中包括美国侵略军四百多名，击落击毁飞机三十二架，击毁军车二十五辆，烧毁仓库二十二座，炸毁桥梁七座。此外，还解放了二百多户居民。
    广南省南部人民解放武装力量在七月二十四日猛烈进攻美国第一九八步兵旅指挥所、朱莱机场和机场附近的停车场，消灭许多美国侵略军，使朱莱机场的停机坪和宿舍起火燃烧，击毁军车十五辆，摧毁营房五十座和碉堡两座，击落前往救援的直升飞机五架。
    广义省人民解放武装力量在七月二十二日和二十三日袭击了广义市和其他十五个目标，消灭敌人近二百五十名，缴获许多军用物资。
    嘉莱省人民解放武装力量七月二十一日在第十九号公路上伏击敌人一支车队，击毁装甲车一辆和满载军用物资的卡车十二辆，消灭许多美国强盗。
    美萩省人民解放武装力量七月二十五日、二十六日和二十七日接连三天炮轰驻扎在平德基地的美国侵略军，打死打伤敌人二百二十名，摧毁许多营房和作战工具。人民解放武装力量七月二十七日夜间还袭击了周城县的伪军训练场，经过几分钟的战斗，就打死敌人二百多名。
    朔庄省人民解放武装力量在七月七日夜间全歼伪军一支“绥靖”队，重创敌人一个保安连，共打死打伤敌人一百多名。七月二十四日夜间人民解放武装力量又袭击前往周城县巴营据点的一连敌军，经过短时间的战斗，就全歼了这连敌军。
    嘉定省古芝县人民解放武装力量，在七月二十一日到二十四日期间连续击退了美国强盗进行的多次反扑，消灭敌人一百多名。
    沙沥省人民解放武装力量，从七月十四日到二十四日期间，粉碎了敌人在沙沥市郊区发动的多次“扫荡”，共消灭和俘虏敌人二百六十八名，击毁击伤装甲车五辆，击毁大炮一门，烧毁弹药库一座，缴获大批军用物资。
