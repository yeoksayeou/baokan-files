Title: U.S. Military Aircraft Invades Our Airspace
Author:
Date: 1968-08-07
Edition: 6

U.S. military aircraft invaded our airspace

We issue the 462nd serious warning

New China News Agency, August 6th

At 1:44 PM to 1:46 PM on August 5th, one U.S. military aircraft invaded the airspace above the sea east of Yancity, Jiangsu Province. On the same day, from 11:56 AM to 12:11 PM, another U.S. military aircraft invaded the airspace above Dong Island and Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands, Guangdong Province. Regarding the aforementioned military provocations by U.S. military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on instructions, issues the 462nd serious warning.

### 美国军用飞机侵入我国领空　我提出第四百六十二次严重警告

1968-08-07
第6版()
专栏：

　　美国军用飞机侵入我国领空
　　我提出第四百六十二次严重警告
    新华社六日讯　八月五日十三时四十四分至十三时四十六分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我江苏省盐城以东地区领海上空。同日十一时五十六分至十二时十一分，还有美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的东岛、永兴岛地区领空。对于美国军用飞机的上述军事挑衅活动，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百六十二次严重警告。
