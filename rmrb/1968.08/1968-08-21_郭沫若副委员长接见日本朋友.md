Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Meets with Japanese Friends
Author:
Date: 1968-08-21
Edition: 6

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo met with the executive director of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, Kawahara Shichiro, on August 19 and had a cordial and friendly conversation with him.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本朋友

1968-08-21
第6版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见日本朋友
    新华社二十日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若，十九日接见了日中文化交流协会常务理事河原崎长十郎，同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
