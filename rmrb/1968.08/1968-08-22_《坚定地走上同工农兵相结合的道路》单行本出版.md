Title: Steadfastly Take the Path of Integrating with Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1968-08-22
Edition: 2

To commemorate the second anniversary of the great leader Chairman Mao's first inspection of the Red Guards, the important editorial "Steadfastly Take the Path of Integrating with Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers," published in the People's Daily, has been published as a standalone book by the People's Publishing House. It will begin distribution at the Beijing New China Bookstore on August 22 and will subsequently be available at New China Bookstores across the country.

### 《坚定地走上同工农兵相结合的道路》单行本出版

1968-08-22
第2版()
专栏：

    《坚定地走上同工农兵相结合的道路》单行本出版
    新华社二十一日讯　为纪念伟大统帅毛主席首次检阅红卫兵两周年，《人民日报》发表的重要社论《坚定地走上同工农兵相结合的道路》，已由人民出版社出版单行本，八月二十二日开始在北京新华书店发行，并将在全国各地新华书店陆续发行。
