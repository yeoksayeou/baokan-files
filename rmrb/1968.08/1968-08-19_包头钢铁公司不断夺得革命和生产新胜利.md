Title: Baotou Iron and Steel Corporation Continues to Achieve New Victories in Revolution and Production
Author:
Date: 1968-08-19
Edition: 2

Baotou Iron and Steel Corporation, under the guidance of the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, with the help of the People's Liberation Army's leftist cadres, has taken class struggle as the key link, firmly and unswervingly carried out Chairman Mao's great policy of "grasp revolution, promote production." By concentrating on revolution and vigorously promoting production, it has achieved double victories in revolution and production. Since May this year, it has overfulfilled the production plan every month, with significant increases in the output of major products such as steel, iron, coke, and ore, and continuous improvement in product quality.

Since the establishment of the Baotou Iron and Steel Corporation Revolutionary Committee, it has followed Chairman Mao's great teaching of "never forget class struggle," using Chairman Mao's latest series of instructions as weapons. It has organized various types of Mao Zedong Thought study classes, combining the revolutionary criticism and the work of cleaning up class ranks, mobilizing the masses to launch a fierce attack on a handful of class enemies. Through the struggle, the broad revolutionary masses have greatly heightened their class consciousness, and the enthusiasm for revolution and production has surged to an unprecedented height.

At present, Baotou Iron and Steel Corporation is presenting a scene of great revolutionary enthusiasm everywhere. The broad revolutionary masses are full of spirit and high morale. They are determined to further implement Chairman Mao's latest series of instructions and achieve new merits in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

### 包头钢铁公司不断夺得革命和生产新胜利

1968-08-19
第2版()
专栏：

    包头钢铁公司不断夺得革命和生产新胜利
    包头钢铁公司革命委员会高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，在人民解放军支左人员的帮助下，以阶级斗争为纲，坚定不移地贯彻执行毛主席“抓革命，促生产”的伟大方针，狠抓革命，猛促生产，夺得了革命和生产的双胜利。从今年五月份以来，月月超额完成生产计划，钢、铁、焦炭、矿石等主要产品的产量都有较大幅度增长，产品质量不断提高。
    包钢革委会成立以后，遵照毛主席“千万不要忘记阶级斗争”的伟大教导，以毛主席一系列最新指示为武器，大办各种类型的毛泽东思想学习班，把革命的大批判和清理阶级队伍的工作结合起来，放手发动群众，向一小撮阶级敌人发起猛烈进攻。广大革命职工在斗争中大大提高了阶级觉悟，革命和生产的积极性空前高涨。
    目前，包钢到处呈现一片大好的革命景象。广大革命职工意气风发，斗志昂扬，他们决心进一步落实毛主席的一系列最新指示，在无产阶级文化大革命中立新功。
