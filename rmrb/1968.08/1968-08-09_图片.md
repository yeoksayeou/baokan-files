Title: Shanghai Textile Mill No. 17 Workers Study Important Editorial
Author:
Date: 1968-08-09
Edition: 4

Workers at Shanghai Textile Mill No. 17 have diligently studied the important editorial published by the People's Daily to commemorate the second anniversary of Mao Zedong's "Bombard the Headquarters" big-character poster. They discussed their experiences from the past two years of struggle in connection with the editorial, deeply feeling that every victory in revolution and production at their mill has been achieved by following Chairman Mao's great strategic deployments. They expressed their determination to strengthen revolutionary discipline and organization, to closely unite around the proletarian headquarters headed by Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin, and to achieve a comprehensive victory in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter, telephoto)
