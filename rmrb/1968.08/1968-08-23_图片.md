Title: Shanghai Cotton Mill No. 5 Workers Intensify Study of Mao Zedong Thought and Boost Production
Author:
Date: 1968-08-23
Edition: 4

Shanghai Cotton Mill No. 5's revolutionary workers are vigorously conducting Mao Zedong Thought study classes and tapping into production potential. Since the beginning of this year, production has seen continuous victories. This is a scene from a study class composed of workers, cadres, and technical personnel, where they are discussing and solving new problems that arise in class struggle and production struggle.
