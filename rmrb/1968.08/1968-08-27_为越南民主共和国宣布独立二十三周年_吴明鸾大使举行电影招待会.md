Title: Ambassador Vu Minh Loan Holds Film Screening for the 23rd Anniversary of the Declaration of Independence of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1968-08-27
Edition: 5

To celebrate the 23rd anniversary of the declaration of independence of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Ambassador Vu Minh Loan of Vietnam to China held a film screening in Beijing today. Relevant officials from our country, including Luo Guibo, Li Qiang, Xie Huai De, Pan Zhenwu, and Ding Xilin, representatives from the Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Team of the capital's workers, representatives of the Five Good Soldiers stationed in Beijing, and representatives from various revolutionary mass organizations in Beijing, were invited to attend the screening.

The permanent representative of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam to China, Nguyen Van Quang, also attended the film screening. During the event, a film depicting the heroic struggle of the Vietnamese military and people against American aggressors was shown.

### 为越南民主共和国宣布独立二十三周年  吴明鸾大使举行电影招待会

1968-08-27
第5版()
专栏：

    为越南民主共和国宣布独立二十三周年
    吴明鸾大使举行电影招待会
    新华社二十六日讯　为庆祝越南民主共和国宣布独立二十三周年，越南驻中国大使吴明鸾今天在北京举行电影招待会。我国有关方面负责人罗贵波、李强、谢怀德、潘振武、丁西林，首都工人毛泽东思想宣传队代表，驻北京部队五好战士代表，北京市各革命群众组织代表，应邀出席了招待会。
    越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长阮文广也出席了电影招待会。会上，放映了反映越南军民英勇打击美国侵略强盗的影片。
