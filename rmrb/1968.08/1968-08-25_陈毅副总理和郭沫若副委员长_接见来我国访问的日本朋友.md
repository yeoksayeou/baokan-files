Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi and Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Receive Japanese Friends Visiting China
Author:
Date: 1968-08-25
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Chen Yi and Vice Chairman Guo Moruo of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress received the full delegation of the Fourth Japanese Student Friendship Visit Group and the Fourth Japanese Teachers and Staff Friendship Visit Group, who came to China for a visit. They had a cordial and friendly conversation.

Present at the reception were responsible persons from relevant departments such as the Revolutionary Committee of Higher Education Institutions in Beijing, the China-Japan Friendship Association, and the China International Travel Service.

### 陈毅副总理和郭沫若副委员长  接见来我国访问的日本朋友

1968-08-25
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理和郭沫若副委员长
    接见来我国访问的日本朋友
    新华社二十三日讯　国务院副总理陈毅和人大常委会副委员长郭沫若，今天接见来我国参观的日本第四次学生友好参观团和日本第四次教职员友好参观团的全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时，首都大专院校红代会、中日友好协会、中国国际旅行社等有关方面负责人在座。
