Title: Lao Patriotic Soldiers and People Fight Bravely, Inflicting Heavy Losses on the Enemy
Author:
Date: 1968-08-11
Edition: 5

Patriotic soldiers and people in various provinces of Laos have been fighting bravely, inflicting heavy losses on the enemy. The patriotic armed forces in Savannakhet Province have annihilated and dismantled over 1,000 enemy forces in the first seven months of this year.

According to a report by the Pathet Lao News Agency, the patriotic armed forces in Savannakhet Province of Laos have engaged in more than 100 battles in the first seven months of this year, annihilating and dismantling 1,662 enemy forces. Among the annihilated enemy forces, 564 were killed, 418 were wounded, and 239 were captured. Additionally, under the influence of the patriotic soldiers and people's political propaganda work, 441 enemy forces have turned their guns towards the people.

In these seven months, the patriotic armed forces in Savannakhet Province have also shot down 26 enemy aircraft, destroyed 24 bridges on enemy supply lines, and seized a large amount of weapons.

According to a report by the Pathet Lao News Agency, the patriotic soldiers and people in Udomxay Province have been continuously fighting, launching attacks on the right-wing military forces stationed in the Xieng Ngeun area of this province. From July 4 to 20, they annihilated 90 enemy forces, including 80 killed, including one battalion commander.

According to a broadcast by the Pathet Lao Radio Station, at the end of July, the guerrillas in Xieng Khouang Province attacked the right-wing military forces in the Meng Sui area, eliminating 23 enemy forces and seizing a batch of weapons.

The guerrillas in Attapeu Province killed 11 enemy forces on June 8 and seized a batch of weapons.

According to a report by the Pathet Lao News Agency, the patriotic armed forces in Xieng Khouang Province bravely repelled the enemy's ground and air attacks on the liberated areas of this province in July, annihilating 127 enemy forces and shooting down four U.S. aircraft.

The report said that in a single battle in Muan Song County, the patriotic armed forces annihilated 114 enemy forces.

According to another report, the local forces and guerrillas in Luang Prabang Province launched a sudden attack on the enemy's positions in Nam Nua on July 27, killing 22 enemy forces on the spot, destroying one mortar and one recoilless rifle, and seizing a large amount of weapons and equipment.

According to a broadcast by the Pathet Lao Radio Station, on June 27, U.S. bandits and their lackeys dispatched a batch of aircraft to savagely bomb the liberated areas of Sayaboury Province. The local patriotic soldiers and people immediately retaliated, and in the battle, the guerrillas shot down two U.S. aircraft with rifles.

### 老挝各省爱国军民英勇战斗痛歼敌人　沙湾拿吉省爱国武装今年头七个月歼敌一千多

1968-08-11
第5版()
专栏：

　　老挝各省爱国军民英勇战斗痛歼敌人
　　沙湾拿吉省爱国武装今年头七个月歼敌一千多
    新华社康开九日电　据巴特寮通讯社报道，老挝沙湾拿吉省爱国武装力量在今年的头七个月里共进行战斗一百多次，歼灭和瓦解敌人一千六百六十二名。
    在被歼灭的敌军中，包括打死五百六十四名，打伤四百一十八名和俘虏二百三十九名。另外，在爱国军民的政治宣传工作影响下，四百四十一名敌军调转枪口，投向人民方面。
    在这七个月里，沙湾拿吉省爱国武装力量还击落敌机二十六架，摧毁敌人交通线上的桥梁二十四座，并缴获大批武器。
    新华社康开六日电　据巴特寮通讯社报道，老挝乌多姆塞省爱国军民连续战斗，不断对盘踞在这个省寮沙诺地区的右派军队发动进攻，从七月四日到二十日，共歼敌九十名，其中打死八十名，包括营长一名。
    另据巴特寮电台广播，七月底，川圹省游击队在孟绥地区袭击右派军队，消灭敌人二十三名，缴获一批武器。
    阿速坡省游击队，在六月八日打死敌人十一名，缴获一批武器。
    新华社康开七日电　据巴特寮通讯社报道，老挝桑怒省爱国武装力量，七月份英勇粉碎了敌人对这个省解放区发动的地面和空中进攻，歼灭敌人一百二十七名，击落美国飞机四架。
    报道说，单单在芒松县的一次战斗中，爱国武装力量就歼灭敌军一百一十四名。
    另据报道，琅勃拉邦省的地方部队和游击队，七月二十七日对南牙的敌军阵地发动突然袭击，当场打死敌人二十二名，击毁迫击炮和无后座力炮各一门，缴获大量武器装备。
    新华社康开七日电　据巴特寮电台广播，六月二十七日，美国强盗及其走狗出动一批飞机野蛮轰炸沙耶武里省解放区，当地爱国军民立即给予迎头痛击，在战斗中，游击队用步枪击落美机两架。（附图片）
    老挝人民解放军以凌厉的攻势歼灭敌人的有生力量，攻占了敌军团指挥所。　新华社稿
