Title: Congo (Brazzaville) Holds Its Second Cultural Week
Author:
Date: 1968-08-25
Edition: 6

Congo (Brazzaville) held the closing ceremony of its second cultural week on August 18 at the Revolutionary Sports Stadium in Brazzaville.

During this cultural week, more than 3,000 athletes and cultural workers from various regions of Congo (Brazzaville) participated in sports competitions, performed folk dramas and dances, and held art exhibitions.

The performances by Congolese cultural workers, such as "The People of Freedom" and "The Voice of the Peasants," reflected the struggle of the Congolese people against colonial rule and expressed their determination to defend national independence and oppose imperialism.

### 刚果（布）举行第二届文化周

1968-08-25
第6版()
专栏：

    刚果（布）举行第二届文化周
    据新华社布拉柴维尔电　刚果（布）第二届文化周十八日在布拉柴维尔革命体育场举行盛大的闭幕仪式。
    在这届文化周期间，来自刚果（布）各个地区的三千多名运动员和文艺工作者举行了体育比赛，演出了民间戏剧、民间舞蹈，举办了艺术展览。
    刚果（布）文艺工作者演出的《自由的人民》、《农民的呼声》等戏剧，反映了刚果（布）人民反对殖民统治的斗争，表达了他们保卫民族独立、反对帝国主义的决心。
