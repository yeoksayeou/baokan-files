Title: In Praise of the Capital's Worker-Peasant Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Teams
Author: Běihǎi Jiànduì Hónghǎi Chéng
Date: 1968-08-10
Edition: 6

In praise of the Capital's Worker-Peasant Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Teams

The great leader presents mangos,
Good news spreads, shaking the sky,
The Worker-Peasant Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Teams,
A red flag dances in the eastern wind!

Heroic flowers bloom red,
Every heart turns towards Mao Zedong,
The great commander gives the order,
Implement the latest directives and take the lead!

You hold high a single banner,
Mao Zedong Thought shines red across the sky;
Marching forward on the revolutionary path,
You do not rely on old achievements but create new ones.

You carry a priceless treasure,
Bravely attacking the revisionist educational line,
Fighting side by side with the Red Guards,
Scaling new heights in the educational revolution!

You are the main force of the revolution,
The five continents are in your hearts;
Shouldering a thousand-pound burden,
"Sweep away all harmful elements"!

Rain and dew nourish the seedlings,
Mao Zedong Thought cultivates heroes,
The Worker-Peasant Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Teams,
Millions of red flags dance in the eastern wind!

### 赞首都工农毛泽东思想宣传队
红海城
1968-08-10
第6版()
专栏：

　　赞首都工农毛泽东思想宣传队
　　北海舰队　红海城
　　伟大领袖赠芒果，
　　喜讯飞传震长空，
　　工农毛泽东思想宣传队，
　　一面红旗舞东风！
　　英雄花开朵朵红，
　　颗颗红心向着毛泽东，
　　伟大统帅一声令下，
　　落实最新指示打先锋！
　　你们高举一杆旗，
　　毛泽东思想映天红；
　　革命路上阔步走，
　　不吃老本立新功。
　　你们怀揣无价宝，
　　向修正主义教育路线勇猛进攻，
　　同红卫兵小将并肩战斗，
　　教育革命攀高峰！
　　你们是革命的主力军，
　　五洲四海装胸中；
　　千斤重担肩上挑，
　　“要扫除一切害人虫”！
　　雨露滋润禾苗壮，
　　毛泽东思想育英雄，
　　工农毛泽东思想宣传队，
　　千万面红旗舞东风！
