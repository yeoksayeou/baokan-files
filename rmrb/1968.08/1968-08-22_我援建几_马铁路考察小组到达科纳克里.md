Title: Chinese Survey Team for the Guinea-Mali Railway Arrives in Conakry
Author:
Date: 1968-08-22
Edition: 5

	According to the agreement signed in Beijing in May this year between the Chinese government and the governments of Guinea and Mali on the construction of the Guinea-Mali Railway, the Chinese survey team for the construction of the Guinea-Mali Railway arrived in Conakry, the capital of Guinea, on the evening of the 19th.

	The Chinese survey team was welcomed at the airport by Pierre Dian, Director of the National Railway Bureau of Guinea, and Baldé Mouctar, Director of the Office of the Minister of Transport of Guinea.

	On the 20th, the Minister of Transport of Guinea, Morry Kéita, received the entire Chinese survey team and had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.

### 我援建几—马铁路考察小组到达科纳克里

1968-08-22
第5版()
专栏：

    我援建几—马铁路考察小组到达科纳克里
    新华社科纳克里二十日电　根据中国政府和几内亚、马里政府今年五月在北京签订的关于修建几内亚—马里铁路的协定，中国帮助修建几内亚—马里铁路的考察小组，十九日晚到达几内亚首都科纳克里。
    中国考察小组在机场受到几内亚全国铁路局局长皮埃尔·迪亚纳和几内亚运输国务秘书办公厅主任巴尔德·穆克塔尔的欢迎。
    二十日，几内亚运输国务秘书莫里·凯塔接见了中国考察小组全体成员，并同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
