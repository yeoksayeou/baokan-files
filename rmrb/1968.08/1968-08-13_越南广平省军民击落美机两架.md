Title: Military and Civilians in Quang Binh Province Shoot Down Two U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-08-13
Edition: 6

Military and civilians in Quang Binh Province shoot down two U.S. aircraft.

	New China News Agency, Hanoi, August 11 — According to reports from here: On August 6, militia in Xuan Hoa County, Quang Binh Province, shot down one U.S. aggressor jet.

	On August 8, military and civilians in Ly Thuong Kiet County, the same province, shot down another U.S. aircraft.

### 越南广平省军民击落美机两架

1968-08-13
第6版()
专栏：

　　越南广平省军民击落美机两架
    新华社河内十一日电　据这里公布：越南北方广平省宣化县民兵，在六日击落美国强盗喷气式飞机一架。
    这个省丽水县军民八日又击落了美国飞机一架。
