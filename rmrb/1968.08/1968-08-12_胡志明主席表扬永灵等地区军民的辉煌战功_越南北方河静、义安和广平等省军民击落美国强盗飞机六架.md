Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Praises the Glorious Victories of the Military and People of Vinh Linh and Other Regions
Author:
Date: 1968-08-12
Edition: 6

Chairman Ho Chi Minh praises the glorious victories of the military and people of Vinh Linh and other regions.
Military and people of the provinces of Ha Tinh, Nghe An, and Quang Binh in North Vietnam shoot down six American aggressor planes.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 11th - According to reports from newspapers here today: Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam wrote a letter on August 10th to the military and people of Vinh Linh, representing the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Workers' Party and the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, to commend them for their glorious victories of shooting down 200 American aggressor planes and sinking or damaging 33 American aggressor warships.

	In his letter, Chairman Ho praised the military and people of Vinh Linh for "fighting well, producing well, and doing well in air defense and serving the frontline." Chairman Ho said in his letter: "The military and people of Vinh Linh are fighting better and stronger."

	Chairman Ho called upon the military and people of Vinh Linh in his letter to "carry forward the victories, resolutely defeat the American aggressors' war of destruction together with the people of the North, and make contributions together with the heroic people of the South to achieve a complete victory in the struggle against American aggression for national salvation."

	At the end of his letter, Chairman Ho presented a poem to the military and people of Vinh Linh:

		Strike the American aggressors to smithereens,
		Five continents praise the heroes of Vinh Linh.

	It is also reported that Chairman Ho Chi Minh recently signed an order to award the military and people of Vinh Linh, who achieved the glorious victories of shooting down 200 American aggressor planes and sinking or damaging 33 American aggressor warships, a Second Class Independence Medal for the second time.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 9th - According to reports from newspapers here on the 9th: Chairman Ho Chi Minh recently wrote a letter to the military and people of Quang Binh Province, commending them for their glorious victories of shooting down 500 American aggressor planes. The letter said that by August 3rd this year, the military and people of Quang Binh Province had shot down 500 American aggressor planes and sunk or damaged 42 American warships of various types. Since the end of March this year, the American aggressors have launched even more savage attacks on Quang Binh Province. However, since early April this year, the military and people of Quang Binh Province have shot down another 100 American planes.

	In his letter, Chairman Ho Chi Minh called upon the people, troops, and cadres of Quang Binh Province not to be complacent due to their victories, but to remain vigilant at all times, and to vigorously carry out competitions in fighting well, producing well, and doing an excellent job in transportation and air defense, in order to achieve more and greater victories and advance resolutely together with the people of the whole country to thoroughly defeat the American aggressors.

	Chairman Ho Chi Minh recently also signed an order to award Quang Binh Province a Second Class Independence Medal for the second time.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi - According to reports from newspapers here on the 5th: Chairman Ho Chi Minh wrote a letter to the military and people of Ha Tinh Province, commending them for their victories of shooting down 200 American planes.

	In his letter, Chairman Ho Chi Minh called upon the people, troops, and cadres of Ha Tinh Province to carry forward their victories, unite closely, fight better, produce better, and do a better job in transportation and air defense, in order to be worthy of the heroic tradition of the Ha Tinh-Soviet.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 9th - According to reports published here, the military and people of Ha Tinh Province in North Vietnam shot down one invading American aggressor plane on August 6th. The military and people of Nghe An Province also shot down one invading American aggressor plane on August 7th.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 10th - According to reports published here, the military and people of Ha Tinh Province in North Vietnam shot down one invading American aggressor plane each on August 6th, 7th, and 8th. The military and people of Quang Binh Province also shot down one enemy plane on August 9th.

	Additionally, the military and people of Nghe An and Ha Tinh Provinces hit one enemy ship each on August 8th.

### 胡志明主席表扬永灵等地区军民的辉煌战功　越南北方河静、义安和广平等省军民击落美国强盗飞机六架

1968-08-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　胡志明主席表扬永灵等地区军民的辉煌战功
　　越南北方河静、义安和广平等省军民击落美国强盗飞机六架
    新华社河内十一日电　据这里报纸今天报道：越南民主共和国主席胡志明十日写信给永灵地区军民，代表越南劳动党中央和越南民主共和国政府表扬他们取得击落二百架美国强盗飞机和击沉击伤三十三艘美国强盗军舰的辉煌胜利。
    胡主席在信中赞扬永灵地区军民“杀敌好，生产好，人民防空工作做得好，为前线服务好”。胡主席在信中说：“永灵地区军民越战越胜，越战越强。”
    胡主席在信中号召永灵地区军民“发扬胜利，坚决同北方军民一起彻底打败美国强盗的破坏性战争，同英雄的南方同胞一起为夺取抗美救国事业的彻底胜利作出贡献”。
    胡志明主席在信末，赠给永灵地区军民一首诗：
    打得美贼粉身碎骨，
    五洲称赞永灵英雄。
    另据报道：胡志明主席最近签署一项命令，决定再次授予取得击落美国强盗飞机二百架和击沉击伤美国强盗军舰三十三艘的辉煌战功的永灵地区军民一枚二级独立勋章。
    新华社河内九日电　据这里报纸九日报道：胡志明主席最近写信给广平省军民，表彰他们取得击落美国强盗飞机五百架的辉煌战功。信中说，截止今年八月三日，广平省军民击落了美国强盗飞机五百架、击沉击伤各种类型美国军舰四十二艘。从今年三月底以来，美国强盗对广平省进行了比过去更为野蛮的袭击，然而从今年四月初以来，广平省军民又击落了一百架美国飞机。
    胡志明主席在信中号召广平省人民、部队和干部，不可因胜利而主观轻敌，必须时刻警惕，大力开展战斗好、生产好、十分良好地从事交通运输和人民防空工作的竞赛，以便夺取更多更大的胜利，同全国人民一起坚决前进，彻底战胜美国侵略强盗。
    胡志明主席最近还签署了一项命令，决定再次授予广平省一枚二级独立勋章。
    新华社河内电　据这里报纸五日报道：胡志明主席写信给河静省军民，表扬他们击落二百架美国飞机的战功。
    胡志明主席在信中号召河静省人民、部队和干部，必须发扬胜利，紧密团结，战斗得更好，生产得更好，把交通运输和人民防空工作搞得更好，以无愧于英雄的义静苏维埃传统。
    新华社河内九日电　据这里公布，越南北方河静省军民六日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架。义安省军民七日也击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架。
    新华社河内十日电　据这里公布，越南北方河静省军民在八月六日、七日和八日三天各击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架，广平省军民在八月九日也击落敌机一架。
    另外，义安和河静两省军民八月八日分别击中敌船敌舰各一艘。
