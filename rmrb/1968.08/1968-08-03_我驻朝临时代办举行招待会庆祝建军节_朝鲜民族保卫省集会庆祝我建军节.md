Title: Chinese Chargé d'Affaires in North Korea Holds Reception to Celebrate Army Day; North Korea's Ministry of People's Armed Forces Holds Meeting to Celebrate Chinese Army Day
Author:
Date: 1968-08-03
Edition: 5

	Chinese Chargé d'Affaires in North Korea Holds Reception to Celebrate Army Day

	North Korea's Ministry of People's Armed Forces Holds Meeting to Celebrate Chinese Army Day

	Peking, August 2 (New China News Agency) — Pyongyang: The Chinese chargé d'affaires in North Korea, Wang Peng, held a reception on August 1 to celebrate the 41st anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

	Present at the reception were: North Korea's Minister of Social Security, Seok San; Deputy Foreign Minister Kim Jae-bong; Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army, Lieutenant General Choe Jae-chun; Deputy Chairman of the North Korean Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Kim Kwang-hyeop; and others.

	Diplomatic envoys and military attachés from some countries stationed in North Korea were also invited to the reception. Some members of the Chinese People's Volunteers delegation to the Military Armistice Commission, representatives of Chinese engineering and technical personnel assisting North Korea, and representatives of the Chinese community also attended the reception.

	Peking, August 2 (New China News Agency) — Pyongyang: The Ministry of People's Armed Forces of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea held a meeting on the evening of July 30 to celebrate the 41st anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Lieutenant General Choe Jae-chun, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army, and military officers and soldiers attended the meeting.

	The Chinese chargé d'affaires in North Korea, Wang Peng, and other embassy staff were also invited to the meeting.

	At the meeting, Lieutenant General Park Do-sam, Director of the Ministry of People's Armed Forces, and Wang Peng spoke.

### 我驻朝临时代办举行招待会庆祝建军节　朝鲜民族保卫省集会庆祝我建军节

1968-08-03
第5版()
专栏：

　　我驻朝临时代办举行招待会庆祝建军节
　　朝鲜民族保卫省集会庆祝我建军节
    新华社二日讯　平壤消息：中国驻朝鲜大使馆临时代办王彭，一日举行招待会，庆祝中国人民解放军建军四十一周年。
    出席招待会的有：朝鲜社会安全相石山、外务省副相金载凤、朝鲜人民军副总参谋长崔在春少将、朝鲜对外文化联络委员会副委员长金宽燮等。
    一些国家驻朝鲜的外交使节和武官应邀出席了招待会。朝鲜军事停战委员会中国人民志愿军代表团部分人员、中国援朝工程技术人员代表和华侨代表也出席了招待会。
    新华社二日讯　平壤消息：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国民族保卫省七月三十日晚举行集会，庆祝中国人民解放军建军四十一周年。朝鲜人民军副总参谋长崔在春少将和军官及战士代表参加了集会。
    中国驻朝鲜大使馆临时代办王彭和大使馆其他人员也应邀出席了集会。
    朝鲜民族保卫省局长朴道三少将和王彭在会上讲了话。
