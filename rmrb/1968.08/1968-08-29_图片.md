Title: Over 200,000 Military and Civilians in Nanjing Hold a Rally and Parade
Author:
Date: 1968-08-29
Edition: 3

On August 26, over 200,000 military personnel and civilians in Nanjing held a rally and parade. They enthusiastically celebrated the issuance of Chairman Mao's extremely important latest directive and the publication of Comrade Yao Wenyuan's important article "The Working Class Must Lead Everything."
