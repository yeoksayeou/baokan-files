Title: Delegation of the Communist Party of Italy (Marxist-Leninist) Leaves Beijing to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1968-08-17
Edition: 2

A delegation from the Communist Party of Italy (Marxist-Leninist), consisting of Central Committee Political Bureau member and Secretariat member Osvaldo Becchi and Political Bureau member Dino Dini, concluded their visit to China and departed Beijing for their home country on the afternoon of the 15th.

Member of the Central Cultural Revolution Group Yao Wenyuan comrade went to the airport to see them off.

### 意共（马克思列宁主义）代表团离京回国  中央文革小组成员姚文元同志到机场欢送

1968-08-17
第2版()
专栏：

    意共（马克思列宁主义）代表团离京回国
    中央文革小组成员姚文元同志到机场欢送
    新华社十六日讯　由意大利共产党（马克思列宁主义）中央委员会政治局委员、书记处书记奥斯瓦尔多·贝契和政治局委员迪诺·迪尼同志组成的访华代表团，结束了在中国的访问，于十五日下午离京回国。
    中央文化革命小组成员姚文元同志到机场欢送。
