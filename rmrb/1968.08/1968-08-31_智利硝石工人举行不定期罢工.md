Title: Chilean Nitrate Workers Stage Indefinite Strike
Author:
Date: 1968-08-31
Edition: 5

Chilean nitrate workers stage indefinite strike

	BEIJING, Aug. 30 (Xinhua) -- A total of 6,200 nitrate workers in Chile announced an indefinite strike on August 29 to protest against the brutal exploitation by U.S. capitalists and to demand wage increases.

	Chile is a country rich in nitrate resources. Most of Chile's nitrate production is controlled by the U.S.-owned Anglo-Lautaro Nitrate Company. Recently, this American company, through the establishment of a "joint venture" nitrate company with the Chilean government, has monopolized the entire nitrate production in Chile.

### 智利硝石工人举行不定期罢工

1968-08-31
第5版()
专栏：

    智利硝石工人举行不定期罢工
    新华社三十日讯　圣地亚哥消息：智利六千二百名硝石工人二十九日宣布举行不定期罢工，抗议美国资本家对他们进行残酷的剥削，要求增加工资。
    智利是一个盛产硝石的国家。智利的硝石生产大部分由美资安格洛—劳塔罗硝石公司控制。不久前，这家美国公司通过同智利政府建立“联营”硝石公司的办法，霸占了智利的全部硝石生产。
