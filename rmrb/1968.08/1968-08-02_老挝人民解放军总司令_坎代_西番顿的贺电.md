Title: Congratulatory Telegram from Kaysone Phomvihane, Commander-in-Chief of the Lao People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1968-08-02
Edition: 4

New China News Agency, August 1st: Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense Lin Biao received a congratulatory telegram from Kaysone Phomvihane, Commander-in-Chief of the Lao People's Liberation Army, on the occasion of the 41st anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

The telegram said, "On the occasion of the 41st anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, I, on behalf of the officers and soldiers of the Lao People's Liberation Army, extend our heartfelt greetings to you and the heroic Chinese People's Liberation Army."

The telegram said, "Under the wise and correct leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong and the Chinese Communist Party, the Chinese People's Liberation Army has accomplished the great historical task of liberating the vast territory in the past 41 years. At present, it has become the pillar of the victory of China's socialist revolution and socialist construction."

The great revolutionary spirit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army is an example for the Lao People's Liberation Army. It has inspired the fighting will of the Lao People's Liberation Army.

The telegram said, "The Chinese People's Liberation Army, armed with Mao Zedong Thought, is not only a powerful force to defend great socialist China but also a strong force to oppose the aggressive schemes of U.S. imperialism and to defend world peace."

The telegram said, "On the occasion of the 41st anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, I wish the great Chinese People's Liberation Army greater strength and more brilliant achievements in national defense construction and socialist construction."

### 老挝人民解放军总司令  坎代·西番顿的贺电

1968-08-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　老挝人民解放军总司令
    坎代·西番顿的贺电
    新华社一日讯　国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪同志收到了老挝人民解放军总司令坎代·西番顿发来的祝贺中国人民解放军建军四十一周年的电报。
    电报说，正当中国人民解放军建军四十一周年的时候，我谨代表老挝人民解放军指战员，向您和英雄的中国人民解放军表示衷心的敬意。
    电报说，中国人民解放军在以毛泽东主席为首的中国共产党的英明、正确的领导下，在过去的四十一年里完成了解放辽阔国土的伟大历史任务。目前，又成了保障中国社会主义革命和社会主义建设胜利的柱石。
    中国人民解放军的伟大革命精神，是老挝人民解放军的榜样。它鼓舞了老挝人民解放军的战斗意志。
    电报说，用毛泽东思想武装起来的中国人民解放军，不仅是一支捍卫伟大的社会主义中国的强大力量，也是一支反对美帝国主义侵略阴谋和捍卫世界和平的坚强力量。
    电报说，在庆祝中国人民解放军建军四十一周年的时候，我祝愿伟大的中国人民解放军日益强大，在国防建设和社会主义建设事业中取得更加辉煌的成就。
