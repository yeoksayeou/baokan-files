Title: China and Congo (Brazzaville) Sign Memorandum of Talks on China's Assistance in Establishing State Farms in Congo (Brazzaville)
Author:
Date: 1968-08-16
Edition: 6

China and Congo (Brazzaville) signed a memorandum of talks on China's assistance in establishing state farms in the Kongue region (17 kilometers from the capital Brazzaville) on August 12 in Brazzaville. The Charge d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Congo (Brazzaville), Li Chen-guang, and the representative of the Congolese Minister of Planning, Foreign Minister Monjo, signed the memorandum on behalf of their respective countries.

The Congolese Minister of Agriculture, Water Resources, and Forests, Gombo Ogustin, also attended the signing ceremony.

Monjo, in his speech, emphasized the fraternal friendship and effective cooperation between the peoples of the two countries. He praised China's aid as being highly effective and a great encouragement to the Congolese people. He expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the Chinese people, the Chinese government, and the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao.

Li Chen-guang, in his remarks, wished the Congolese people greater achievements in their struggle against imperialism and colonialism and in building an independent national economy.

### 中国和刚果（布）签订中国帮助刚果（布）建立国营农场的会谈纪要

1968-08-16
第6版()
专栏：

    中国和刚果（布）
    签订中国帮助刚果（布）建立国营农场的会谈纪要
    新华社布拉柴维尔十三日电　中国和刚果（布）十二日在这里签订了在孔贝地区（距首都布拉柴维尔十七公里）中国帮助刚果（布）建立国营农场的会谈纪要。中国驻刚果（布）大使馆临时代办李琛光和刚果（布）负责计划的国务部长的代表、外交部长蒙乔分别在会谈纪要上签了字。
    刚果（布）农业、水利和森林部长贡博·奥古斯坦也出席了签字仪式。
    蒙乔在致词中强调刚、中两国人民之间的兄弟般的友谊和有成效的合作。他赞扬中国的援助一直是卓有成效的，是对刚果人民的巨大鼓舞。他表示衷心感谢中国人民、中国政府和中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席。
    李琛光代办在讲话中祝刚果（布）人民在反对帝国主义和殖民主义、建设独立的国民经济的斗争中取得更加伟大的成就。
