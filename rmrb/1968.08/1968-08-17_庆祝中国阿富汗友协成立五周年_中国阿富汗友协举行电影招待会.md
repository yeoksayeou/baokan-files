Title: Celebrating the Fifth Anniversary of the China-Afghanistan Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1968-08-17
Edition: 5

The China-Afghanistan Friendship Association held a film screening event this evening to enthusiastically celebrate the fifth anniversary of the China-Afghanistan Friendship Association. The event was held to wish for the continuous development of the friendship between the peoples of China and Afghanistan.

The screening featured a documentary film of our great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin Biao receiving revolutionary soldiers, as well as a documentary film reflecting the support for the army and love for the people.

Afghanistan's Ambassador to China, Suhail, and diplomatic officials from the embassy, as well as Afghan friends in Beijing, were invited to attend the film screening.

Relevant officials from our country, including Ding Xilin and Han Nianlong, as well as representatives from various revolutionary mass organizations in Beijing, also attended the film screening.

### 庆祝中国阿富汗友协成立五周年  中国阿富汗友协举行电影招待会

1968-08-17
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝中国阿富汗友协成立五周年
    中国阿富汗友协举行电影招待会
    新华社十四日讯  中国阿富汗友好协会今晚举行电影招待会，热烈庆祝中国阿富汗友好协会成立五周年，祝中国和阿富汗两国人民的友谊不断发展。招待会上放映了我们伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席接见革命战士的纪录影片，以及反映拥军爱民的纪录影片。
    阿富汗驻中国大使苏海尔和使馆外交官员，以及在北京的阿富汗朋友，应邀出席了电影招待会。
    我国有关方面的负责人丁西林、韩念龙，以及北京市各革命群众组织的代表，也出席了电影招待会。
