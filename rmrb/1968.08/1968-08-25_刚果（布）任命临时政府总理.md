Title: Congo (Brazzaville) Appoints Interim Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1968-08-25
Edition: 6

According to a report by Xinhua News Agency from Brazzaville, the "Voice of the Congo (Brazzaville) Revolution" radio station broadcasted on the 22nd that Congo (Brazzaville) President Massamba-Debat, based on the recommendation of the Congo (Brazzaville) National Revolutionary Committee, issued an order appointing Captain Alfred Raoul as the interim Prime Minister of the Republic of Congo (Brazzaville).

This interim government was formed on August 5th.

### 刚果（布）任命临时政府总理

1968-08-25
第6版()
专栏：

    刚果（布）任命临时政府总理
    据新华社布拉柴维尔电　据“刚果（布）革命之声”电台二十二日广播，刚果（布）总统马桑巴—代巴根据刚果（布）全国革命委员会的建议，颁布一项法令，任命阿尔弗雷德·拉乌尔上尉为刚果（布）共和国临时政府总理。
    这个临时政府是在八月五日组成的。
