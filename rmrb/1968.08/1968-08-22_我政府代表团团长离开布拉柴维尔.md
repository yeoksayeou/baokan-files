Title: Chinese Government Delegation Head Leaves Brazzaville
Author:
Date: 1968-08-22
Edition: 5

The head of the Chinese Government delegation, Huang Hua (Chinese Ambassador to the United Arab Republic), left Brazzaville on the evening of the 20th after participating in the celebrations for the fifth anniversary of the "August Revolution" in Congo (Brazzaville).

He was seen off at the airport by the Deputy Director of the Asian Affairs Department of the Congolese (Brazzaville) Foreign Ministry, Bernard Lobaki, the head of the Protocol Department, and diplomatic envoys from many countries to Congo (Brazzaville).

The Charge d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Congo (Brazzaville), Li Chen Guang, and representatives of Chinese technical personnel in Congo (Brazzaville) also went to the airport to see him off. During his visit to Brazzaville, Huang Hua was received by Pascal Lissouba, the Congolese (Brazzaville) Minister of State for Planning, and Nicholas Monjo, the Foreign Minister, who had cordial and friendly conversations with him.

### 我政府代表团团长离开布拉柴维尔

1968-08-22
第5版()
专栏：

    我政府代表团团长离开布拉柴维尔
    新华社布拉柴维尔二十一日电　中华人民共和国政府代表团团长黄华（中国驻阿联大使），在参加了刚果（布）“八月革命”五周年庆祝活动后，二十日晚上离开布拉柴维尔。
    他在机场受到刚果（布）外交部亚洲司副司长伯纳·洛巴基、礼宾司负责人以及许多国家驻刚果（布）的外交使节的欢送。
    中国驻刚果（布）大使馆临时代办李琛光以及在刚果（布）的中国技术人员的代表也到机场送行。黄华在布拉柴维尔访问期间，刚果（布）负责计划的国务部长帕斯卡尔·利苏巴和外交部长尼古拉·蒙乔分别接见了他，并同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
