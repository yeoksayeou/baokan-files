Title: Comrade N. Samaraweera Leaves Beijing to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1967-06-10
Edition: 2

The Central Committee member and Political Bureau member of the Ceylon Communist Party, Comrade N. Samaraweera, along with his wife, left Beijing this morning to return home after visiting China.

Comrade Kang Sheng, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Comrade Liu Ningyi, a member of the Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Communist Party of China, Comrade Zhao Yimin, an alternate member of the Central Committee, and Comrade Cao Yiyou went to the airport to see them off.

In the afternoon of June 7, Comrade Kang Sheng had a cordial talk with Comrade Samaraweera and hosted a dinner for Comrade Samaraweera and his wife and daughter. Those who participated in the talk and the dinner included Comrade Liu Ningyi, Comrade Zhao Yimin, and Comrade Cao Yiyou. During his stay in China, Comrade Samaraweera visited factories and schools in Beijing and Shanghai, and had an in-depth understanding of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

### 桑穆加塔桑同志离京回国  康生刘宁一等同志到机场欢送

1967-06-10
第2版()
专栏：

    桑穆加塔桑同志离京回国
    康生刘宁一等同志到机场欢送
    新华社九日讯　锡兰共产党中央政治局委员、书记处书记纳·桑穆加塔桑同志偕夫人，在我国访问以后，今晨离开北京回国。
    中共中央政治局常委康生同志，中共中央委员、中共中央书记处书记刘宁一同志，中共中央候补委员赵毅敏同志以及曹轶欧同志前往机场欢送。
    七日下午，康生同志和桑穆加塔桑同志进行亲切的谈话，并且宴请了桑穆加塔桑同志和他的夫人、女儿。参加谈话和宴会的有，刘宁一同志、赵毅敏同志和曹轶欧同志等。桑穆加塔桑同志在中国期间，曾访问北京和上海的工厂和学校，深入地了解了无产阶级文化大革命的情况。
