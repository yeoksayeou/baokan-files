Title: Chairman Shehu Hosts Banquet for Ambassador Liu Xiao
Author:
Date: 1967-06-26
Edition: 5

	Albania's Prime Minister Shehu hosted a banquet for China's new ambassador to Albania, Liu Xiao, at the "Partisan Palace" in Tirana on the evening of June 24.

	Albania's beloved leader, Comrade Enver Hoxha, and other party and state leaders, including Qemal Stafa, Beqir Balluku, Hysni Kapo, Spiro Koleka, Kadri Hazbiu, Ramiz Alia, Manush Myftiu, Petro Dume, Koço Theodhosi, and Pilo Peristeri, attended the banquet.

	Comrade Yao Wenyuan, the leader of the Chinese Red Guards delegation visiting Albania on a friendly visit, and team member Tan Houlan, as well as Chinese diplomatic personnel from the Chinese Embassy in Albania, were also invited to attend the banquet. Chairman Shehu and Ambassador Liu Xiao both spoke at the banquet.

	Comrade Enver Hoxha and Chairman Shehu also had a friendly and cordial conversation with Comrade Yao Wenyuan, the leader of the Chinese Red Guards delegation. The banquet was held in a very friendly and cordial atmosphere.

### 谢胡主席设宴招待刘晓大使  霍查同志等领导人出席并同姚文元同志亲切谈话

1967-06-26
第5版()
专栏：

    谢胡主席设宴招待刘晓大使
    霍查同志等领导人出席并同姚文元同志亲切谈话
    新华社地拉那二十五日电　阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席谢胡，二十四日晚上在地拉那“游击队宫”设宴招待中国驻阿尔巴尼亚新任大使刘晓。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民的敬爱领袖霍查，以及党和国家的其他领导人查尔查尼、巴卢库、努什、托斯卡、卡博、阿里雅、马尔科、科列加、凯莱齐、哈兹比乌、蒂奥多西、皮里斯特里等出席了宴会。
    正在阿尔巴尼亚进行友好访问的中国红卫兵代表团团长姚文元和团员谭厚兰以及中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆外交人员也应邀出席了宴会。在宴会上，谢胡主席和刘晓大使先后讲了话。
    霍查同志和谢胡等同志还同中国红卫兵代表团团长姚文元同志进行了亲切友好的交谈。宴会是在十分亲切友好的气氛中进行的。
