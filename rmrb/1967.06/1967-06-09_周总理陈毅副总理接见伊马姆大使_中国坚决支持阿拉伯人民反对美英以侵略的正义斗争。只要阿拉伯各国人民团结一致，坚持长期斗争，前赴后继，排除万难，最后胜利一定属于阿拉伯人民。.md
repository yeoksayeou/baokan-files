Author:

Edition: 1

Premier Zhou and Vice Premier Chen Yi Meet with Ambassador Imam

China resolutely supports the just struggle of the Arab people against the aggression of the United States, Britain, and Israel. As long as the peoples of the Arab countries unite, persist in a protracted struggle, and bravely overcome all difficulties, final victory will surely belong to the Arab people.

	BEIJING, June 8 (Xinhua) — Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi met with the Ambassador of the United Arab Republic to China, Imam, this afternoon.

	During their friendly conversation with Ambassador Imam, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi expressed their admiration for the fighting spirit of the people of the UAR and the Arab countries. They reiterated that in the struggle of the people of the UAR and all Arab peoples to counter the aggression of American and British imperialism and their tool, Israel, the Chinese government and people stand firmly on the side of the UAR and all Arab peoples, resolutely supporting their just struggle against the aggression of the United States, Britain, and Israel. Premier Zhou Enlai expressed his belief that as long as the peoples of the Arab countries unite, persist in a protracted struggle, and bravely overcome all difficulties, final victory will surely belong to the Arab people.

	Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi requested Ambassador Imam to convey their regards to President Nasser.

	During the conversation, Ambassador Imam introduced the situation of the struggle of the people of the UAR and the Arab peoples to counter the aggression of the United States, Britain, and Israel.

1967-06-09
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理陈毅副总理接见伊马姆大使
    中国坚决支持阿拉伯人民反对美英以侵略的正义斗争。只要阿拉伯各国人民团结一致，坚持长期斗争，前赴后继，排除万难，最后胜利一定属于阿拉伯人民。
    新华社八日讯　国务院总理周恩来和国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅，今天下午接见了阿拉伯联合共和国驻中国大使伊马姆。
    周恩来总理和陈毅副总理兼外交部长在同伊马姆大使进行的友好谈话中，对阿联人民和阿拉伯各国人民的斗争勇气表示钦佩，并且重申在阿联人民和全体阿拉伯人民反击美英帝国主义及其工具以色列的侵略的斗争中，中国政府和中国人民坚决站在阿联和全体阿拉伯人民一边，坚决支持他们反对美、英、以侵略的正义斗争。周恩来总理表示相信，只要阿拉伯各国人民团结一致，坚持长期斗争，前赴后继，排除万难，最后胜利一定是属于阿拉伯人民的。
    周恩来总理和陈毅副总理请伊马姆大使转达对纳赛尔总统的敬意。
    伊马姆大使在谈话中，介绍了阿联人民和阿拉伯人民反击美、英、以侵略的斗争形势。
