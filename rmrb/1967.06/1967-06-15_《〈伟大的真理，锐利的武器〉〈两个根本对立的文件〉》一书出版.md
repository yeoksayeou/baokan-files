Title: The Publication of "Great Truth, Sharp Weapon" and "Two Fundamentally Opposing Documents"
Author:
Date: 1967-06-15
Edition: 1

The book "Great Truth, Sharp Weapon" and "Two Fundamentally Opposing Documents" has been published by the People's Publishing House. Starting from June 13th, it has been distributed in Beijing and will soon be available nationwide.

The content of this book includes: the editorials "Great Truth, Sharp Weapon" and "Long Live the Revolutionary Critical Spirit" published by "Red Flag" magazine and the People's Daily to celebrate the first public release of Chairman Mao's five combative documents on literary and artistic issues; the editorials "Two Fundamentally Opposing Documents," "An Important Document of the Proletarian Cultural Revolution," and "Take Up the Pen and Hold the Gun Tightly to Fight for the Defense of the Proletarian Dictatorship" published by "Red Flag" magazine, the People's Daily, and the PLA Daily to discuss the significance of the "Summary of the Symposium on Army Literary and Art Work Convened by Comrade Lin Biao and Comrade Jiang Qing."

### 《〈伟大的真理，锐利的武器〉〈两个根本对立的文件〉》一书出版

1967-06-15
第1版()
专栏：

    《〈伟大的真理，锐利的武器〉〈两个根本对立的文件〉》一书出版
    新华社十四日讯  《〈伟大的真理，锐利的武器〉〈两个根本对立的文件〉》一书已由人民出版社出版，从六月十三日起在北京发行，并将在全国各地发行。
    本书内容包括：《红旗》杂志、《人民日报》为欢呼毛主席关于文学艺术问题的五个战斗性文件第一次公开发表而分别发表的社论《伟大的真理，锐利的武器》、《革命的批判精神万岁》；《红旗》杂志、《人民日报》、《解放军报》为论述《林彪同志委托江青同志召开的部队文艺工作座谈会纪要》的重要意义而分别发表的社论《两个根本对立的文件》、《无产阶级文化革命的重要文件》、《拿起笔杆握紧枪，为保卫无产阶级政权而斗争》。
