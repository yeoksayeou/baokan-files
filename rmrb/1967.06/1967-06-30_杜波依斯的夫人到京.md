Title: Mrs. Du Bois Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-06-30
Edition: 6

Famous black leader W.E.B. Du Bois's wife Shirley Graham Du Bois arrived in Beijing this evening by plane from Shanghai. She is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries.

### 杜波依斯的夫人到京

1967-06-30
第6版()
专栏：

    杜波依斯的夫人到京
    新华社二十九日讯　著名黑人领袖杜波依斯的夫人秀莱·格拉汗·杜波依斯今晚由上海乘飞机到达北京。她是应中国人民对外文化友好协会的邀请来我国进行友好访问的。
