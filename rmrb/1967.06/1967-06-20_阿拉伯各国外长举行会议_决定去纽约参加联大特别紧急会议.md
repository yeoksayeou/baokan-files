Title: Arab Foreign Ministers Hold Meeting Decide to Go to New York to Attend UN Special Emergency Session
Author:
Date: 1967-06-20
Edition: 5

Arab foreign ministers held a meeting in Kuwait from the evening of June 17 to the morning of June 18. The meeting's communiqué stated: "Arab foreign ministers met in Kuwait to discuss the current situation in the Middle East caused by Israel's aggression against Arab countries."

The meeting announced: "Arab oil-producing countries will resolutely implement their resolution to stop supplying oil to countries supporting Israel—America and Britain."

The Arab foreign ministers unanimously decided to go to New York to continue discussions on the Middle East situation. They will participate in the "United Nations Special Emergency Session" in New York.

### 阿拉伯各国外长举行会议  决定去纽约参加联大特别紧急会议

1967-06-20
第5版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯各国外长举行会议
    决定去纽约参加联大特别紧急会议
    新华社开罗十九日电　据开罗报纸十九日报道，阿拉伯国家外交部长十七日晚上到十八日早晨在科威特举行会议。会议公报说：“阿拉伯外长在科威特举行会议，讨论了由于以色列侵略阿拉伯国家而造成的目前中东局势。”
    会议宣布：“阿拉伯产油国家坚决地执行它们停止供应石油给支持以色列的国家——美国和英国——的决议。”
    阿拉伯国家外长一致决定去纽约继续就中东局势进行讨论，他们将在纽约参加“联合国特别紧急会议”。
