Title: Brave Guerrilla Warfare Against Israeli Invaders: Palestinian and Egyptian Soldiers Continue to Fight
Author:
Date: 1967-06-15
Edition: 5

Palestinian guerrilla forces and Egyptian soldiers in areas occupied by Israel are continuing their fight, resisting the Israeli invaders who are puppets of American and British imperialism.

According to a report from Gaza on the 13th, the Palestinian people, fueled by both new and old grievances, are bravely engaging in guerrilla warfare against the Israeli invaders backed by the United States and Britain. Agence France-Presse reported that Palestinians, filled with hatred, are "engaging in a new battle" with Israeli occupation forces in the northeast region of Gaza, which is under Israeli control. In Gaza City, despite strict curfews, "Israeli invaders risk their lives if they step out at night."

The report states that in the Gaza Strip, soldiers of the Palestinian Liberation Army are continuing their struggle, "firing at Israeli patrols from their hiding spots." Snipers active in the small forests outside Gaza City lay new mines every night to strike at the Israeli invaders. The Israeli military admitted that on the 12th, a jeep triggered a mine in the outskirts of Gaza. Two were killed and three injured.

Egyptian soldiers surrounded in the scorching desert of the Sinai Peninsula, despite facing shortages of water and food, continue to fight. On the 12th, they killed two Israeli invaders. The Israeli military commander in Gaza, Moshe Dayan, helplessly admitted, "Currently in Sinai, a unprecedented tragedy is unfolding... If you venture a little too far, you will be killed."

### 英勇展开游击战抗击以色列侵略者  巴勒斯坦和阿联战士坚持战斗

1967-06-15
第5版()
专栏：

    英勇展开游击战抗击以色列侵略者
    巴勒斯坦和阿联战士坚持战斗
    新华社十四日讯　巴勒斯坦游击队和阿联士兵在被以色列侵占的地区仍在坚持战斗，抗击美英帝国主义的走狗——以色列侵略者。
    据十三日来自加沙的消息报道，巴勒斯坦人民怀着新仇旧恨，英勇地展开游击战，打击美英支持下的以色列侵略者。法新社在一则消息中说，满怀仇恨的巴勒斯坦人正在同以色列侵略军在被以色列侵略军占领的加沙东北地区“展开一场新的战斗”。在加沙城，尽管实行严格的宵禁，但是以色列侵略者“夜里走出房门，就有生命危险”。
    消息说，在加沙地带，巴勒斯坦解放军士兵在坚持斗争，“他们从掩体内向以色列的巡逻队开火”。活跃在加沙城郊外的小树林里的狙击手，每天晚上埋下新的地雷，打击以色列侵略者。以色列军方承认，十二日，一辆吉普车在加沙郊外触了地雷。两人死亡，三人受伤。
    被包围在酷热的西奈地区沙漠地带的阿联士兵，在缺水缺粮的情况下，仍在继续坚持战斗。十二日，他们打死了两名以色列侵略者。以色列驻加沙的军事头目摩西·戈兰无可奈何地承认，“目前在西奈，发生了空前的悲剧……你要是稍微走远一点，就会被杀死”。
