Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Receives Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-06-09
Edition: 6

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo today received the full delegation of the Japan International Trade Promotion Local Parliamentarians' League visiting China, headed by Tanaka Akira. He had a friendly conversation with them.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人

1967-06-09
第6版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人
    新华社七日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若今天接见了以田中晃为首的日本国际贸易促进地方议员联盟访华代表团全体成员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
