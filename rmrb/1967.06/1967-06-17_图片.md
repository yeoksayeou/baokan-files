Title: Citizens of Damascus Demonstrate in Front of US and British Embassies
Author:
Date: 1967-06-17
Edition: 6

On June 6, citizens of Damascus, the capital of Syria, held a demonstration in front of the American and British embassies. The photo shows angry demonstrators tearing down the copper nameplate of the American embassy and holding it up, shouting, “Down with American imperialism!” “Down with Johnson!”
