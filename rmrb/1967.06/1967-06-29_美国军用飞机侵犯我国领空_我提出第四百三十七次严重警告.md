Title: U.S. Military Aircraft Violates China's Airspace
Author:
Date: 1967-06-29
Edition: 4

U.S. military aircraft violated China's airspace

China issues 437th serious warning

New China News Agency, June 28th: At 11:19 AM to 11:35 AM on June 28th, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace above the sea near the northeastern region of Wenchang County, Hainan Island, Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 11:29 AM to 11:59 AM, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace above Yongxing Island and Dong Island in the Xisha Islands, Guangdong Province. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs spokesperson, on behalf of the Ministry, issues the 437th serious warning regarding this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft.

### 美国军用飞机侵犯我国领空  我提出第四百三十七次严重警告

1967-06-29
第4版()
专栏：

    美国军用飞机侵犯我国领空
    我提出第四百三十七次严重警告
    新华社二十八日讯  六月二十八日十一时十九分至十一时三十五分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省海南岛文昌县东北地区领海上空。同日十一时二十九分至十一时五十九分美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛、东岛地区领空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百三十七次严重警告。
