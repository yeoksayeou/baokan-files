Title: Ambassador Huang Hua Calls on President Nasser and Presents Premier Zhou's Telegram of Support
Author:
Date: 1967-06-08
Edition: 5

Chinese Ambassador Huang Hua called on President Nasser of the United Arab Republic on the morning of June 7 and presented a telegram from Premier Zhou En-lai, dated June 6, expressing the Chinese government and people's resolute support for the struggle of the Arab people against imperialism.

President Nasser and Ambassador Huang had a friendly talk. During the conversation, they discussed the current situation of the Arab people's struggle against the aggression of the United States and Israel.

### 黄华大使拜会纳赛尔总统  向他递交周总理的支持电报

1967-06-08
第5版()
专栏：

    黄华大使拜会纳赛尔总统  
    向他递交周总理的支持电报
    新华社开罗七日电　中国驻阿联大使黄华七日上午拜会阿联总统纳赛尔，递交了周恩来总理六日给纳赛尔总统的表示中国政府和人民坚决支持阿拉伯人民反帝斗争的电报。
    纳赛尔总统和黄华大使进行了友好的谈话。在谈话中，他们讨论了阿拉伯人民反对美国—以色列侵略的斗争的当前情况。
