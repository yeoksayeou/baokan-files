Title: Heartily Congratulate the Successful Explosion of China's First Hydrogen Bomb
Author:
Date: 1967-06-22
Edition: 3

	Heartily Congratulate the Successful Explosion of China's First Hydrogen Bomb

	Telegram from President Sekou Toure of the Republic of Guinea to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China:

	We are pleased to learn of your hydrogen bomb test. We extend our congratulations on this successful test. This success is a reliable guarantee for the construction of prosperous and independent nations, where the working people will live in happiness and peace. We express our respect for the will of the people of China to fight against imperialism. We reaffirm the support of the people, government, and party of Guinea for you, and we are convinced that the cooperative relations between our two countries will be further strengthened.

	With the highest respect,

	President of the Republic of Guinea, Ahmed Sekou Toure
	June 20, 1967, Conakry

	Telegram from Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Head of State of Cambodia, to Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:

	We are very pleased to learn of the great success of China's first hydrogen bomb explosion. On behalf of the people and government of Cambodia, and in my personal capacity, I extend our deepest admiration and warmest congratulations to the scientists, engineers, and all other personnel who contributed to this historic achievement.

	This achievement undoubtedly places the People's Republic of China among the most scientifically advanced nations in the world. We, like our Chinese brothers, are proud of this. Friendly China, relying on its own efforts, has mastered atomic energy in just a few years; this greatly inspires all developing countries in Asia, Africa, and Latin America.

	Finally, we once again express our respect for China's reaffirmation of its peaceful wish not to be the first to use weapons of mass destruction. Thus, China's hydrogen bomb strengthens the confidence of all anti-imperialist nations fighting against the aggressive and nuclear blackmail of American war hawks.

	We wish the Chinese people success under the guidance of Chairman Mao Zedong's thoughts. With the highest respect.

	Head of State of Cambodia, Norodom Sihanouk
	June 20, 1967, Phnom Penh

	Telegram from the Political Bureau of the French Communist Movement (Marxist-Leninist):

	The Political Bureau of the French Communist Movement (Marxist-Leninist) sent a telegram on June 18 to the Communist Party of China, the Government of China, and the people of China, heartily congratulating the successful explosion of China's first hydrogen bomb.

	The telegram said: June 17, 1967, will be a memorable day in the history of the peoples of various countries breaking free from imperialist oppression. On this day, under the guidance of the great thought of Mao Zedong, the People's Republic of China successfully exploded a hydrogen bomb of tremendous power.

	The United States and the Soviet Union conducted hundreds of atmospheric nuclear tests to achieve this result, while China, in just over two years and six tests, has achieved it. This vividly demonstrates that the Chinese people, rich in revolutionary spirit, relying on their own efforts, are advancing with giant strides and mastering all fields of science and technology.

	This is a great victory for the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and Mao Zedong Thought.

	The telegram pointed out that the successful explosion of China's first hydrogen bomb is a vivid proof that the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution has promoted production.

	The telegram said, "The U.S. imperialists and Soviet revisionists are trembling in the face of the extraordinary achievements of the Chinese people. This achievement thoroughly shatters their nuclear blackmail against the revolutionary movements of the world.

	"Just as Johnson and Kosygin are meeting in Washington to forge a genuine counter-revolutionary holy alliance, the successful explosion of China's hydrogen bomb shatters their evil plots against the people fighting for their own liberation."

	The telegram said, "The French Communist Movement (Marxist-Leninist) extends warm congratulations to the scientific researchers, technicians, workers, soldiers, and all the people of China for their great contributions to the liberation of the oppressed peoples and the maintenance of world peace.

	"The Political Bureau, on behalf of the French Communist Movement (Marxist-Leninist), expresses deep admiration and infinite gratitude to the Communist Party of China and Chairman Mao Zedong of the Communist Party of China."

	Telegram from the First Secretary of the Austrian Marxist-Leninist Party to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China:

	Inspired by the new great victory of Mao Zedong Thought, the Austrian Marxist-Leninist Party extends fraternal combat greetings.

	First Secretary of the Austrian Marxist-Leninist Party, Franz Strobl
	June 19, 1967, Vienna

	(Xinhua News Agency)

### 热烈祝贺我国第一颗氢弹爆炸成功

1967-06-22
第3版()
专栏：

    热烈祝贺我国第一颗氢弹爆炸成功
    几内亚共和国总统杜尔贺电北京中国共产党中央委员会毛泽东主席阁下：
    得悉你们氢弹试验的消息，我们怀着愉快的心情向这次试验致意。我们热烈祝贺你们这次试验的成功。这一成功是建设繁荣、独立国家的可靠保证，在这些国家里劳动人民将生活在幸福与和平之中。我们谨向人民中国为消灭帝国主义而斗争的意志表示敬意。我们重申几内亚人民、几内亚政府和几内亚党对你们的声援，并深信在我们两国之间业已存在的合作关系将进一步加强。
    顺致最崇高的敬意
                        几内亚共和国总统艾哈迈德·塞古·杜尔
                            一九六七年六月二十日于科纳克里
    柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王贺电北京中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来先生阁下：
    我们非常高兴地获悉中国第一颗氢弹爆炸的巨大成功。我谨代表柬埔寨人民、王国政府，并以我个人的名义向对这一历史性成就作出贡献的科学家、工程师及所有其他人员表示我们深深的钦佩，和致以最热烈的祝贺。
    这一成就确定无疑地使中华人民共和国进入世界上科学最发达的大国之列。我们同中国兄弟一样，为此感到自豪。友好的中国自力更生，只用了几年的时间，就掌握了原子能；这使所有发展中的亚洲、非洲和拉丁美洲各国都得到巨大的鼓舞。
    最后，我们对于中国重申它保证决不首先使用大规模毁灭性武器的和平愿望，再次表示敬意。这样，中国的氢弹就加强了一切反对美国好战分子进行侵略和原子讹诈的反帝国家的信心。
    祝中国人民在毛泽东主席的思想指引下取得成就。顺致最崇高的敬意。
                              柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克
                                  一九六七年六月二十日于金边
    法国共产主义运动（马列）政治局贺电
    法国共产主义运动（马克思列宁主义）政治局十八日致电中国共产党、中国政府和中国人民，热烈祝贺我国第一颗氢弹爆炸成功。
    贺电说，一九六七年六月十七日，在各国人民摆脱帝国主义奴役的历史上将是值得纪念的一天。
      “正是这一天，在伟大的毛泽东思想指导下的中华人民共和国，成功地爆炸了一颗威力非常强大的氢弹。
    “美国和苏联在大气层中进行了几百次原子试验才达到这样的结果，而中国仅仅在两年多一点的时间内经过六次试验就达到了。
    “这就突出地证明了，富有杰出革命精神的中国人民，自力更生，以巨人的步伐向前迈进，掌握了科学技术的一切领域。
    “这是无产阶级文化大革命和毛泽东思想的伟大胜利。”
    贺电指出，中国第一颗氢弹爆炸成功是无产阶级文化大革命促进了生产的鲜明证据。
    贺电说，“美帝和苏修在中国人民的非凡成就面前吓得发抖，中国人民的这一成就，彻底打破了他们用来反对全世界革命运动的核讹诈。
    “正当约翰逊和柯西金在华盛顿会晤，以缔结名副其实的反革命神圣同盟的时候，中国热核炸弹的爆炸成功，粉碎了他们反对为自己的解放而斗争的人民的邪恶阴谋。”
    贺电说，“法国共产主义运动（马克思列宁主义）向中国科学研究人员、技术人员、工人、战士和全体中国人民表示热烈祝贺，祝贺他们为被压迫人民的解放、为维护世界和平所作出的巨大贡献。
    “政治局谨代表法国共产主义运动（马克思列宁主义），向中国共产党，向中国共产党主席毛泽东同志，表示深深的敬佩和无限的感激。”奥地利马列主义党第一书记贺电中国共产党中央委员会：
    为毛泽东思想的新的伟大胜利所鼓舞，奥地利马克思列宁主义党致以兄弟般的战斗的敬礼。
            奥地利马克思列宁主义党第一书记弗朗茨·施特罗伯尔
                            一九六七年六月十九日于维也纳
                                          （新华社）
