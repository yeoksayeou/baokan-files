Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1967-06-08
Edition: 1

	All reactionaries are paper tigers. From a long-term point of view, the reactionaries appear terrifying, but in reality, they have no great power. The truly powerful force does not belong to the reactionaries, but to the people.

		From "Talk with American Journalist Anna Louise Strong"

### 毛主席语录

1967-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    一切反动派都是纸老虎。看起来，反动派的样子是可怕的，但是实际上并没有什么了不起的力量。从长远的观点看问题，真正强大的力量不是属于反动派，而是属于人民。
                  《和美国记者安娜·路易斯·斯特朗的谈话》
