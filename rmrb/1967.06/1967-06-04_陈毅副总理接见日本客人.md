Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-06-04
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi met with the full delegation of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association Tokai Bureau Friendship Trade Company visiting China, led by Matsuoka Takeichiro, this morning. He had a friendly conversation with them.

### 陈毅副总理接见日本客人

1967-06-04
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见日本客人
    新华社二日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见由松冈武一郎率领的日本国际贸易促进协会东海总局友好商社访华代表团全体成员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
