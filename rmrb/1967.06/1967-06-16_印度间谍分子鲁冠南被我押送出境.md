Title: Indian Spy Lu Guannan Expelled from China
Author:
Date: 1967-06-16
Edition: 2

Indian spy Lu Guannan, who was sentenced to expulsion from China, was escorted out of the border at Shenzhen this morning by our public security personnel and Red Guards.

Revolutionary masses in Shenzhen held a demonstration when Lu Guannan was being escorted out of the border. They shouted slogans such as "Down with American imperialism!" "Down with Soviet revisionism!" "Down with the Indian reactionaries!" "Spy Lu Guannan, get out of China!" Lu Guannan left the Shenzhen border in disgrace amidst the angry shouts of the demonstrators.

### 印度间谍分子鲁冠南被我押送出境

1967-06-16
第2版()
专栏：

    印度间谍分子鲁冠南被我押送出境
　　新华社广州十五日电  被我判处驱逐出境的印度驻华大使馆间谍分子鲁冠南，今天清晨在我公安人员和红卫兵的押送下，被驱逐出深圳边境。
    深圳的革命群众在间谍分子鲁冠南被我押送出境的时候愤怒地举行了示威。他们高呼“打倒美帝国主义！”“打倒苏联修正主义！”“打倒印度反动派！”“间谍分子鲁冠南滚出中国去！”鲁冠南在示威群众的愤怒口号声中灰溜溜地离开深圳边境。
