Date: 1967-06-03
Edition: 6

Yemen President, Egyptian and Kuwaiti National Assemblies Praise China's Support for Arab People

Chinese People Are Reliable Friends of the Arab People

	SANAA, May 28 (Xinhua) -- Yemen President Abdul Rahman al-Iryani on May 28 praised the Chinese people's support for the Arab people's struggle against American imperialist aggression.

	President al-Iryani said, "The great Chinese people stand on the side of the Arab people. They resolutely support the Arab people's struggle against Israeli and American imperialist aggression."

	He said, "The Chinese people will forever be friends of the Arab people. With reliable friends and strong forces, we will certainly defeat American imperialism and Israel. The final victory belongs to the Arab people."

	BEIJING, June 2 (Xinhua) -- The National People's Congress received a telegram on May 31 from Anwar Sadat, Speaker of the Egyptian National Assembly, expressing gratitude to the Chinese government and people for their support for Egypt and the Arab people's anti-imperialist struggle. The telegram said, "We sincerely appreciate and value the understanding shown by your friendly people and government of our current position in the face of the growing aggression crisis in the Arab region and the Middle East," "We thank you for supporting our country's legitimate right to exercise sovereignty over its territory and territorial waters, and the right to retaliate against Israeli aggression and provocation."

	The telegram also said, "The support of the Chinese government and people, as well as the people of the world, for the Arab people, makes us firmly believe that our cause and stance are just, and that our struggle against aggression is not isolated."

	BEIJING, June 2 (Xinhua) -- The National People's Congress received a telegram on June 1 from the Kuwaiti National Assembly expressing gratitude to the Chinese people for their support for the Arab people's just struggle against imperialism and colonialism.

	The telegram said, "We sincerely thank you for your friendly support for the Arab people in their struggle to restore their rights, which have been taken away by the Zionist group of states."

	The telegram expressed, "We highly appreciate your noble stance, which the Arab people will never forget."

### 也门总统、阿联和科威特国民议会赞扬我对阿拉伯人民的支持  中国人民是阿拉伯人民的可靠朋友

1967-06-03
第6版()
专栏：

    也门总统、阿联和科威特国民议会赞扬我对阿拉伯人民的支持
    中国人民是阿拉伯人民的可靠朋友
    新华社萨那电　也门总统萨拉勒五月二十八日赞扬中国人民支持阿拉伯人民反对美帝国主义侵略的斗争。
    萨拉勒总统说：“伟大的中国人民站在我们阿拉伯人民一边，他们坚决支持阿拉伯人民反对以色列和美帝国主义侵略的斗争。”
    他说：“中国人民永远是阿拉伯人民的朋友。有了可靠的朋友和强大的力量，我们一定能击败美帝国主义和以色列。最后胜利属于阿拉伯人民。”
    新华社二日讯　全国人民代表大会五月三十一日收到阿拉伯联合共和国国民议会议长安瓦尔·萨达特打来的电报，感谢中国政府和中国人民对阿联和阿拉伯各国人民反帝斗争的支持。电报说：“最诚挚地感谢和欣赏你们友好的人民和政府对我们目前在阿拉伯地区和中东日益增长的侵略危机中所采取的立场的充分理解”，“感谢你们支持我国在自己的领土和领水上行使主权的合法权利，以及对以色列的侵略和挑衅进行反击的权利。”
    电报还说，中国政府和人民以及世界各国人民对阿拉伯人民的支持，“使我们坚信，我们的事业和立场是正义的，我们反对侵略的斗争不是孤立的。”
    新华社二日讯　全国人民代表大会一日收到科威特国民议会打来的电报，感谢中国人民支持阿拉伯人民反帝反殖的正义斗争。
    电报说：“我们衷心地感谢你们对阿拉伯人民的友好支持，支持他们为恢复被剥夺的权利而反对犹太复国主义集团国家的具有历史意义的立场。”
    电报表示：“我们高度地评价你们的崇高立场，对此，阿拉伯人民将永世不忘。”
