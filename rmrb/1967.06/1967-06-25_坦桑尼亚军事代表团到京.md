Title: Tanzanian Military Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-06-25
Edition: 5

Tanzanian Military Delegation Arrives in Beijing

According to Xinhua News Agency, the Tanzanian military delegation, led by the head of the delegation and the Deputy Director of the Logistics and Engineering Department of the Tanzanian People's Defense Forces, Colonel Kashmiri, arrived in Beijing by plane this evening. The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese Ministry of National Defense.

Vice Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Peng Shaohui, and Deputy Director of the General Logistics Department, Tang Tianji, as well as leaders from various military branches and relevant departments, were at the airport to give a warm welcome.

Diplomats from the Tanzanian Embassy in China and Tanzanian friends in Beijing also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 坦桑尼亚军事代表团到京

1967-06-25
第5版()
专栏：

    坦桑尼亚军事代表团到京
    据新华社二十四日讯　坦桑尼亚联合共和国军事代表团，在团长、坦桑尼亚人民国防军总司令部后勤工程部部长卡什米里中校率领下，今晚乘飞机到达北京。代表团是应我国国防部邀请，前来进行友好访问的。
    中国人民解放军副总参谋长彭绍辉、总后勤部副部长唐天际，以及各军种、兵种和有关方面负责人，到机场热烈欢迎。
    坦桑尼亚驻中国大使馆外交官员和在北京的坦桑尼亚朋友，也到机场迎接。
