    Quotations from Chairman Mao
    We have previously formulated a democratic method for resolving contradictions among the people into a specific formula, which is called "unity-criticism-unity." To put it in more detail, it means starting from the desire for unity, going through criticism or struggle to resolve contradictions, and thus achieving new unity on a new basis. From "On the Correct Handling of Contradictions Among the People"

### 毛主席语录

1967-06-21
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    我们曾经把解决人民内部矛盾的这种民主的方法，具体化为一个公式，叫做“团结——批评——团结”。讲详细一点，就是从团结的愿望出发，经过批评或者斗争使矛盾得到解决，从而在新的基础上达到新的团结。《关于正确处理人民内部矛盾的问题》
