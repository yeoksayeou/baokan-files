Title: Vice Premier Xie Fuzhi Meets with Guests from the Congo (Brazzaville)
Author:
Date: 1967-06-23
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Xie Fuzhi today in the afternoon met with the Congolese (Brazzaville) administrative inspection delegation led by the Director of the Office of the Ministry of the Interior, Muberi Gregoire. They had a friendly and cordial conversation.

The Congolese (Brazzaville) Ambassador to China, Bazanga, was also present.

### 谢富治副总理接见刚果（布）客人

1967-06-23
第5版()
专栏：

    谢富治副总理接见刚果（布）客人
    新华社二十二日讯　谢富治副总理今天下午接见了由刚果（布）内政部办公厅主任穆贝里·格雷戈瓦率领的刚果（布）行政考察团，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    刚果（布）驻中国大使巴赞加也在座。
