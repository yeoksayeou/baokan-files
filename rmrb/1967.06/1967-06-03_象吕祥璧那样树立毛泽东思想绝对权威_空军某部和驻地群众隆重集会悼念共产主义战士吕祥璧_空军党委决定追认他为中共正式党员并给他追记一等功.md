Title: Learn from Lyu Xiangbi to Establish the Absolute Authority of Mao Zedong Thought
Author:
Date: 1967-06-03
Edition: 3

People's Liberation Army Air Force troops and local revolutionary masses recently held a grand meeting in a certain place in Jiangsu Province to mourn Lyu Xiangbi, who sacrificed his life to save Red Guards. The meeting announced that, in order to commemorate Lyu Xiangbi, the Air Force Party Committee decided to posthumously recognize Lyu Xiangbi as a formal member of the Communist Party of China and awarded him a first-class merit citation.

At the meeting, the responsible person from the unit where the martyr served read the decision of the Air Force Party Committee. The decision stated that Lyu Xiangbi actively responded to the great call of Vice Chairman Lin, studied and applied Chairman Mao's works, broke down private interests and established public interests, and transformed his thoughts, establishing the absolute authority of Mao Zedong Thought in his mind. He was nurtured and grew up under the guidance of Mao Zedong Thought and emerged as another great communist warrior in our army during the great proletarian cultural revolution.

The decision announced that the Air Force Party Committee posthumously recognizes Lyu Xiangbi as a formal member of the Communist Party of China and awards him a first-class merit citation. The decision called on all officers and soldiers of the Air Force to learn from Lyu Xiangbi's noble spirit of self-sacrifice, inherit and carry forward the glorious traditions of our army, actively respond to Chairman Mao's great call to support the army and love the people, and firmly implement Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line. It urged them to thoroughly criticize the handful of power-holders within the Party who are taking the capitalist road and to make new contributions and achievements for the great proletarian cultural revolution.

A representative of the Air Force leadership read the eulogy from the Air Force Party Committee.

A responsible person from the Beijing Military Region Air Force leadership represented the leadership and expressed deep sorrow for the martyr, extending warm condolences to the martyr's family.

The martyr's father, Lyu Anwei, also spoke at the meeting. Speakers at the meeting also included representatives of the local proletarian revolutionary factions, representatives from the Army and Navy, and Lyu Xiangbi's former comrades-in-arms. They all expressed their determination to love the masses like Lyu Xiangbi, using their lives and blood to defend Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line.

The meeting concluded with the resounding song "Sailing the Seas Depends on the Helmsman" and the slogan "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years!"

### 象吕祥璧那样树立毛泽东思想绝对权威  空军某部和驻地群众隆重集会悼念共产主义战士吕祥璧  空军党委决定追认他为中共正式党员并给他追记一等功

1967-06-03
第3版()
专栏：

    象吕祥璧那样树立毛泽东思想绝对权威
    空军某部和驻地群众隆重集会悼念共产主义战士吕祥璧
    空军党委决定追认他为中共正式党员并给他追记一等功
    据新华社南京二日电  人民解放军空军某部指战员和当地革命群众，最近在江苏省某地隆重集会，悼念为抢救红卫兵小将而牺牲的吕祥璧同志。会上宣布，为了表彰和纪念吕祥璧同志，根据他生前的申请，空军党委决定追认吕祥璧同志为中国共产党正式党员，并给他追记一等功。
    会上，烈士生前所在部队负责人宣读了空军党委的决定。决定说，吕祥璧同志积极响应林副主席的伟大号召，活学活用毛主席著作，破私立公，改造思想，在头脑里树立了毛泽东思想的绝对权威。他是在毛泽东思想哺育下成长起来的，是在无产阶级文化大革命的烈火中涌现出来的我军又一个伟大的共产主义战士。
    决定宣布空军党委追认吕祥璧同志为中共正式党员，并给他追记一等功。决定号召空军部队全体指战员学习吕祥璧同志舍己救人的高贵品德，继承和发扬我军的光荣传统，积极响应毛主席提出的拥军爱民的伟大号召，坚定不移地执行毛主席的无产阶级革命路线，彻底批判党内最大的一小撮走资本主义道路的当权派，为无产阶级文化大革命建新功、立新劳。
    空军领导机关代表读了空军党委的悼词。
    北京部队空军领导机关负责人代表领导机关，向烈士表示沉痛的哀悼，对烈士家属表示亲切的慰问。
    烈士的父亲吕安渭也在大会上讲了话。在会上讲话的还有当地无产阶级革命派的代表，陆、海军部队代表和吕祥璧生前的战友，他们表示，一定象吕祥璧同志那样热爱人民群众，用生命和鲜血来保卫毛主席的无产阶级革命路线。
    大会在《大海航行靠舵手》的嘹亮歌声和“毛主席万岁！万万岁”的口号声中结束。
