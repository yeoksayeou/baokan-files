Money – The Purpose of Going to University

Why go to university?

A student from Leningrad University answered: “After graduating from high school, I have done all kinds of work. In the end, I still decided to come to university because I hope to live a better life.”

A laboratory assistant, a night school student at Leningrad University, said: “Why do I still come to night school after a day’s hard work? You know, without a university diploma, one cannot get anywhere. No one will treat you as a human being, and you will forever only earn these few tens of rubles.”

### 金钱——读大学的目的

1967-06-02
第5版()
专栏：复辟资本主义

    金钱——读大学的目的
    为什么要上大学呢？
    列宁格勒大学一个学生回答：“中学毕业后，我什么工作都干过，最后还是决定要来考大学，因为我希望生活得更好一些。”
    一位实验员、列宁格勒大学的夜校学生说：“为什么一天劳累之余我还来上夜大学呢？要知道，在我们这里，没有大学毕业证书就寸步难行，谁也不把你当人看，就永远只能挣这几十个卢布。”
