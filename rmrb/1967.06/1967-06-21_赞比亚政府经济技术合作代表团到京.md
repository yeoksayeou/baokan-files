Title: Zambian Government Economic and Technical Cooperation Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-06-21
Edition: 5

A delegation from the Zambian government, led by Arthur N. Wina, the Minister of Finance, arrived in Beijing this evening by airplane.

The delegation was welcomed at the airport by Fang Yi, Director of the State Council's Foreign Economic Relations Commission, Deputy Director Yang Lin, and Vice Minister of Communications Ge Chen.

### 赞比亚政府经济技术合作代表团到京

1967-06-21
第5版()
专栏：

    赞比亚政府经济技术合作代表团到京
    新华社二十日讯　由赞比亚财政部长阿瑟·尼·勒·维纳率领的赞比亚共和国政府经济技术合作代表团，今晚乘飞机到达北京。
    对外经委主任方毅、副主任杨琳，交通部副部长葛琛等到机场迎接赞比亚朋友。
