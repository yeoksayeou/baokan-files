Title: Swedish Ambassador Hosts Flag Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1967-06-07
Edition: 4

The Swedish Ambassador to China, Petter Pettersson, held a reception today to celebrate Sweden's National Flag Day. Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibao and relevant officials were invited to attend the reception. Diplomats from various countries stationed in China also attended the reception.

### 瑞典驻华大使举行国旗日招待会

1967-06-07
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典驻华大使举行国旗日招待会
    新华社六日讯　瑞典驻中国大使佩特里今天举行招待会，庆祝瑞典国旗日。外交部副部长罗贵波和有关方面负责人应邀出席了招待会。各国驻中国使节也出席了招待会。
