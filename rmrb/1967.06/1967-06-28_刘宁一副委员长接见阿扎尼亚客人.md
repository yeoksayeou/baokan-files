Title: Vice Chairman Liu Ningyi Meets with Guests from Azania
Author:
Date: 1967-06-28
Edition: 5

Vice Chairman Liu Ningyi of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress received Allan Leballo, Acting Chairman of the Pan-Africanist Congress of Azania, and members of the delegation from the Pan-Africanist Congress of Azania visiting China, led by him, this morning. He had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

### 刘宁一副委员长接见阿扎尼亚客人

1967-06-28
第5版()
专栏：

    刘宁一副委员长接见阿扎尼亚客人
    新华社二十七日讯　人大常委会副委员长刘宁一今天上午接见阿扎尼亚泛非主义者大会代理主席勒巴洛，和由他率领的阿扎尼亚泛非主义者大会访华代表团的成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
