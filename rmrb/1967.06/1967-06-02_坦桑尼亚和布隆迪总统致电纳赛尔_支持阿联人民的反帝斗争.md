Title: Presidents of Tanzania and Burundi Send Telegram to Nasser Supporting the Egyptian People's Anti-Imperialist Struggle
Author:
Date: 1967-06-02
Edition: 6

Tanzania President Nyerere and Burundi President Micombero recently sent telegrams to Egyptian President Nasser, supporting the Egyptian people's struggle against imperialism.

In a telegram dated May 31, President Nyerere stated that the government and people of Tanzania sympathize with and support the Egyptian people's struggle to defend the freedom and sovereignty of the Arab nation.

The telegram expressed that Tanzania supports "your struggle against imperialism and the defense of your rights."

President Micombero of Burundi stated in his telegram that Burundi unreservedly supports the Egyptian people's struggle against imperialist aggressors.

The telegram said: "In the struggle against the enemy, Egypt and other Arab countries are fighting against the imperialist powers hiding behind this enemy. The people of Burundi will always sympathize with and fully, unconditionally support the peoples of all countries who are fighting for freedom, sovereignty, and dignity." The telegram concluded by saying that the people of Burundi are confident that the final victory will belong to the Arab people.

### 坦桑尼亚和布隆迪总统致电纳赛尔  支持阿联人民的反帝斗争

1967-06-02
第6版()
专栏：

    坦桑尼亚和布隆迪总统致电纳赛尔 
    支持阿联人民的反帝斗争
    新华社一日讯　坦桑尼亚总统尼雷尔和布隆迪总统米孔贝罗最近打电报给阿联总统纳赛尔，支持阿联人民反对帝国主义的斗争。
    尼雷尔总统在五月三十一日的电报中说，坦桑尼亚政府和人民同情和支持阿联人民保卫阿拉伯民族的自由和主权的斗争。
    电报表示，坦桑尼亚支持“你们反对帝国主义、保卫自己的权利”。
    布隆迪总统米孔贝罗在电报中说，布隆迪无保留地支持阿联人民同帝国主义侵略者进行的斗争。
    电报说：在同敌人进行的斗争中，阿联和其他阿拉伯国家正在对隐藏在这个敌人身后的帝国主义大国进行斗争。布隆迪人民永远同情和完全地、无条件地支持为争取自由、主权和尊严的各国人民。电报最后说，布隆迪人民坚信，最后胜利一定属于阿拉伯人民。
