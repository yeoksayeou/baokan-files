Title: Vice Chairman Lin Attends Joint Performance by the Three Services Stationed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-06-10
Edition: 1

Our great leader Chairman Mao's close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin Biao, along with Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, Nie Rongzhen, Xie Fuzhi, Jiang Qing, Yang Chengwu, Su Yu, Wang Li, Guan Feng, Chen Boda, Yao Wenyuan, Wang Dongxing, Wang Xinting, Qiu Huizao, Ye Qun, and other comrades, watched a joint performance by the revolutionary cultural troops of the PLA's land, sea, and air forces stationed in Beijing this evening at the Great Hall of the People.

The revolutionary cultural troops of the PLA's land, sea, and air forces stationed in Beijing performed a variety of rich and colorful programs tonight, including the large-scale song and dance "Celebrating the 25th Anniversary of the Publication of Chairman Mao's Brilliant Work 'Talks at the Yan'an Forum on Literature and Art'," "Long Live Chairman Mao's Revolutionary Line on Literature and Art," "Revolutionary Historical Songs Performance Singing," the dance "Chairman Mao Comes to Our Ship," "Fiercely Attacking the Top Capitalist-Roader in the Party," and many other solo performances by male and female singers. These programs shine with the brilliant thoughts of Mao Zedong and are the rich fruits of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

Comrades who accompanied the viewing tonight included the responsible comrades of the PLA's land, sea, and air forces stationed in Beijing: Xiao Jinguang, Wu Faxian, Yu Lijin, Li Zuopeng, Zheng Weishan, Li Tianhuan, Wang Hongkun, Chen Xianrui, and Zhang Xiuquan.

The revolutionary rebel mass organizations of the cultural and sports units of the PLA's land, sea, and air forces stationed in Beijing that participated in tonight's performance included: the Red Rebel Fleet of the Navy Political Department's Troupe, the Revolutionary Committee of the Air Force Political Department's Troupe, the Preparatory Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Beijing Military Region's Friendship Troupe, the Revolutionary United Headquarters of the Beijing Military Region's Air Force Troupe, the Preparatory Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Second Artillery's Star Fire Troupe, the Preparatory Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Beijing Military Region's Band, the Red Flag Revolutionary Rebel Team of the "August 1st" Sports Team, the Revolutionary Rebel Team of the Air Force Sports Team, and the Preparatory Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Beijing Military Region's Sports Team.

After the performance, Vice Chairman Lin Biao and Comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, Jiang Qing, and others went on stage, shook hands warmly with the revolutionary cultural troops, congratulated them on their successful performance, and posed for a group photo with the revolutionary cultural troops. The revolutionary cultural troops were extremely excited and enthusiastic, holding up red "Quotations from Chairman Mao" and shouting for a long time: "Long live Chairman Mao! Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years!"

### 林副主席观看驻京三军联合演出  周恩来陈伯达康生李富春李先念聂荣臻谢富治江青杨成武等同志同看演出

1967-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

    林副主席观看驻京三军联合演出
    周恩来陈伯达康生李富春李先念聂荣臻谢富治江青杨成武等同志同看演出
    新华社九日讯　我们伟大领袖毛主席的亲密战友林彪副主席以及周恩来、陈伯达、康生、李富春、李先念、聂荣臻、谢富治、江青、杨成武、粟裕、王力、关锋、戚本禹、姚文元、汪东兴、王新亭、邱会作、叶群等同志，今天晚上在人民大会堂观看了人民解放军驻京陆海空三军革命文艺战士的联合演出。
    驻京陆海空三军革命文艺战士今晚演出的节目有大型歌舞《欢庆毛主席的光辉著作〈在延安文艺座谈会上的讲话〉发表二十五周年》、《毛主席的革命文艺路线胜利万岁》、《革命历史歌曲表演唱》，舞蹈《毛主席来到我们军舰上》、《向党内头号走资本主义道路当权派猛烈开火》，以及男女声独唱等许多丰富多采的节目。这些节目闪耀着伟大的毛泽东思想的光辉，是无产阶级文化大革命的丰硕成果。
    今晚陪同观看演出的，有驻京陆海空三军负责人萧劲光、吴法宪、余立金、李作鹏、郑维山、李天焕、王宏坤、陈先瑞、张秀川等同志。
    参加今晚演出的驻京陆海空三军文体单位的革命造反派群众战斗组织有：海政文工团、军乐队红色造反舰队，空政文工团革命委员会，北京部队战友文工团革命委员会临时筹委会，北京部队空军文工团革命造反联合总部，第二炮兵星火文工团革命委员会临时筹委会，北京部队军乐队革命委员会临时筹委会，“八一”体工队红旗革命造反队，空军体工队革命造反队，北京部队体工队革命委员会临时筹委会。
    演出结束以后，林彪副主席和周恩来、陈伯达、康生、李富春、江青等同志走上舞台，和革命文艺战士亲切握手，祝贺他们成功的演出，并和革命文艺战士们一起合影。革命文艺战士个个无比兴奋和激动，高举红彤彤的《毛主席语录》，长时间地纵情欢呼：“毛主席万岁！毛主席万岁！万万岁！”
