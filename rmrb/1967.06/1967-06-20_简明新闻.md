    Brief News

    South Yemen People's Armed Forces Blow Up British Oil Pipeline

    On the night of June 16, the South Yemen People's Armed Forces blew up the oil pipeline connecting the British refinery to the port of Aden.

    This successful attack on British colonialists' oil facilities came after the U.S. and British imperialists supported Israel's armed aggression against Arab countries.

    The 30-kilometer-long pipeline was blown up 15 kilometers west of Aden, with oil spilling across a large section of the road beside the pipeline. It will take three to four days to repair.

    Egypt Bans U.S. and British Civilian Aircraft from Landing or Overflying

    According to a report by Egypt's _Al-Ahram_ newspaper on June 17, Cairo International Airport, which was closed after the U.S., British, and Israeli aggression, will reopen starting June 19. The Egyptian government has decided not to allow U.S. and British imperialist civilian aircraft to land at Egyptian airports or fly over Egyptian airspace. Egypt's United Arab Airlines has also decided to cancel its flights to London.

    South Korean People Protest Election Fraud

    For five consecutive days, students and residents in various parts of South Korea have held rallies and protests, strongly condemning the fraudulent "parliamentary" elections staged by the Park Chung-hee clique on June 8.

    The number of participants in this protest movement is increasing, and within a few days, the flames of protest have spread across major cities in South Korea, causing the Park Chung-hee clique to panic. Despite the Park Chung-hee clique's use of large-scale repression and deception to extinguish the South Korean people's resistance, the people's struggle is just beginning to flourish.

    Japanese Scientific Instruments Exhibition Successfully Concludes

    The Japanese Scientific Instruments Exhibition in Tianjin concluded successfully on June 17. Despite the sabotage by U.S. imperialism and the Japanese reactionary government of Sato, as well as the obstruction by Japanese Communist Party revisionists, the exhibition, which lasted for 14 days, attracted more than 100,000 visitors. The exhibition further promoted the fighting friendship between the peoples of China and Japan.

    简明新闻
    南也门人民武装炸断英输油管
    南也门人民武装突击队十六日夜间炸断了英国炼油厂通到亚丁港口的输油管。
    这是美、英帝国主义支持以色列向阿拉伯国家发动武装侵略后，南也门人民武装对英国殖民主义者的石油设施进行的一次成功的袭击。
    这条长三十公里的输油管在亚丁以西的十五公里处被炸断，石油流满了油管旁边的一大段公路，需要三、四天才能修理好。
    阿联不许美英民航机降落过境
    据阿联《金字塔报》十七日报道，在美英以发动武装侵略后封闭的阿联开罗国际机场，将从十九日起重新开放。阿联政府决定不许美英帝国主义的民航机在阿联机场降落，也不许它们飞越阿联领空。阿联的阿拉伯联合航空公司也决定取消飞往伦敦的班机。
    南朝鲜人民示威抗议选举骗局
    南朝鲜各地的学生和居民连续五天举行集会和示威游行，强烈抗议朴正熙集团策划的六月八日伪“国会”选举骗局。
    参加这个抗议运动的人数越来越多，几天之内，抗议的怒火就燃遍了南朝鲜各大城市，使朴正熙集团惊慌万状，坐卧不安。尽管朴正熙集团采取大规模镇压和欺骗的两面手法，企图扑灭南朝鲜人民的反抗怒火，但是南朝鲜人民的斗争正方兴未艾。
    日本科学仪器展览会胜利闭幕
    在天津举行的日本科学仪器展览会十七日胜利闭幕。冲破美帝国主义和日本佐藤反动政府的破坏，以及日共修正主义分子的阻挠而举行的这次展览会，在展出的十四天中接待了十万多名观众。展览会进一步促进了中日两国人民的战斗友谊。　　　　（据新华社讯）
