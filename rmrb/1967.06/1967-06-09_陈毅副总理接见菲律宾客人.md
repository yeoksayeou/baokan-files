Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Philippine Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-06-09
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Chen Yi met with Philippine Congressman Vicente Saquing, Vice Chairman of the Philippine Movement for Nationalism Antonio Araneta Jr., General Secretary Jose Sison and his wife, and Philippine friend Clemente Araneta this morning. Vice Premier Chen Yi had a friendly conversation with them.

Vice Premier Chen Yi met with Teodoro Locsin, publisher and editor-in-chief of the Philippine weekly "Free Press," and deputy editor-in-chief Nick Joaquin this morning and had a friendly conversation with them.

### 陈毅副总理接见菲律宾客人

1967-06-09
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见菲律宾客人
    新华社七日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见菲律宾众议员文森索·萨贡，菲律宾促进民族主义运动副主席小安顿纽·阿兰尼塔、总书记何塞·西松和夫人，以及菲律宾朋友克莱敏特·安顿纽。陈毅副总理同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    新华社七日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见菲律宾《自由新闻》周刊社长兼总编辑迪奥多罗·洛幸，副总编辑尼克·华金等，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
