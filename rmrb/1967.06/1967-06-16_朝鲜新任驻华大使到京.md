Title: New North Korean Ambassador Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-06-16
Edition: 6

The new Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the People's Republic of China, Hyun Jun-gyeok, arrived in Beijing by airplane this noon.

### 朝鲜新任驻华大使到京

1967-06-16
第6版()
专栏：

    朝鲜新任驻华大使到京
    新华社十五日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国新任驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使玄峻极，今天中午乘飞机到达北京。
