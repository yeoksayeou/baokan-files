Title: Chairman of the Malian Journalists' Association Gologo Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-06-09
Edition: 6

Mamadou Gologo, a representative of Mali to the fifth plenary meeting of the secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists' Association, member of the Political Bureau of the Malian Union Party, Minister of Information and Tourism of Mali, and Chairman of the Malian Journalists' Association, arrived in Beijing by plane this evening.

### 马里记协主席戈洛戈抵京

1967-06-09
第6版()
专栏：

    马里记协主席戈洛戈抵京
    据新华社八日讯　出席亚非新闻工作者协会第五次书记处全会的马里代表、马里苏丹联盟党政治局委员、马里新闻、游览部部长、马里记协主席马马杜·戈洛戈，今天晚上乘飞机到达北京。
