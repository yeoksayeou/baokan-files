Title: Premier Zhou and President Kaunda Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1967-06-23
Edition: 1

Premier Zhou Enlai and President Kenneth Kaunda of Zambia held talks in a friendly atmosphere this morning.

The Chinese side participating in the talks included: Vice Premier Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, Acting Minister of Foreign Trade Lin Haiyun, Deputy Foreign Minister Xu Yixin, Deputy Director of the State Foreign Economic Commission Xie Huaiyi, Deputy Director of the State Council for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan, and Chinese Ambassador to Zambia Qin Lizhen.

The Zambian side participating in the talks included: Minister of Finance Wina, Minister of Agriculture Mudenda, Minister of Cooperation, Youth, and Social Development Banda, Minister of State for Barotseland Province Monga, Assistant Minister of State for Western Province Chalikulima, and Permanent Secretary of the Foreign Ministry Jona.

### 周总理和卡翁达总统举行会谈

1967-06-23
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理和卡翁达总统举行会谈
    新华社二十二日讯  国务院总理周恩来同赞比亚共和国总统卡翁达今天上午在友好的气氛中进行了会谈。
    中国方面参加会谈的有：国务院副总理陈毅、李先念，外贸部代理部长林海云，外交部副部长徐以新，对外经委副主任谢怀德，对外文委副主任楚图南，中国驻赞比亚大使秦力真等。
  赞比亚方面参加会谈的有：财政部长维纳，农业部长穆登达，合作、青年和社会发展部长班达，驻巴罗兹省国务部长蒙加，驻西方省助理国务部长查利库利马，外交部常任秘书乔纳等。
