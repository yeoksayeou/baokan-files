Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and President Kaunda Continue Talks, China and Zambia Sign Economic and Technical Agreement
Author:
Date: 1967-06-24
Edition: 1

Premier Zhou Enlai continued talks with Zambian President Kenneth David Kaunda this afternoon.

The talks were conducted in a friendly atmosphere.

New China News Agency, June 23rd. The economic and technical cooperation agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Republic of Zambia was signed this evening in Beijing.

Premier Zhou Enlai and Zambian President Kenneth David Kaunda attended the signing ceremony.

Minister of the State Planning Commission Fang Yi and Zambian Minister of Finance Arthur N. L. Wina signed the agreement on behalf of their respective governments.

Chinese officials attending the signing ceremony included Vice Premier Li Xiannian, Deputy Foreign Minister Xu Yixin, Deputy Minister of the State Planning Commission Yang Lin, and Deputy Minister of Communications Ge Chen.

Zambian officials attending the signing ceremony included Minister of Agriculture Mudenda, Minister of Co-operation, Youth, and Social Development Hayton Banda, Provincial Minister of Barotseland Monga, Assistant Provincial Minister of Western Province Chalikulima, Permanent Secretary of the Office of the President and Vice Secretary of the Cabinet Siyomunji, Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Jona, Chief Private Secretary to the President Ngoma, Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Works Mapoma, Under Secretary of the Ministry of Co-operation, Youth, and Social Development Chisanga, and Director of the News Agency Kamana.

### 周恩来总理卡翁达总统继续会谈中赞两国政府签订经济技术协定

1967-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理卡翁达总统继续会谈中赞两国政府签订经济技术协定
    新华社二十三日讯　国务院总理周恩来今天下午继续同赞比亚共和国总统卡翁达进行会谈。
    会谈在友好的气氛中进行。
    新华社二十三日讯　中华人民共和国政府和赞比亚共和国政府经济技术合作协定，今晚在北京签字。
    国务院总理周恩来和赞比亚共和国总统肯尼思·戴维·卡翁达参加了签字仪式。
    对外经委主任方毅和赞比亚财政部长阿瑟·尼·勒·维纳分别代表两国政府在协定上签字。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的，还有国务院副总理李先念，外交部副部长徐以新，对外经委副主任杨琳，交通部副部长葛琛等。
    赞比亚方面参加签字仪式的，有农业部长穆登达，合作、青年和社会发展部长海顿·班达，驻巴罗兹省国务部长蒙加，驻西方省助理国务部长查利库利马，人事办公室常任秘书兼内阁副秘书西约蒙吉，外交部常任秘书乔纳，总统首席私人秘书恩戈马，工程部常任秘书马波玛，合作、青年和社会发展部下秘书奇桑加，新闻局局长卡马纳等。
