Title: Warm Congratulations on the Successful Detonation of China's First Hydrogen Bomb
Author:
Date: 1967-06-23
Edition: 5

	Warm Congratulations on the Successful Detonation of China's First Hydrogen Bomb

	Telegram of Congratulations from the Political Bureau of the Belgian Communist Party

	On June 19, Jacques Grippa, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Belgian Communist Party, sent a telegram to the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on behalf of the Political Bureau of the Belgian Communist Party, extending warm congratulations on the successful detonation of China's first hydrogen bomb. The telegram said: "Our party extends fraternal and warm congratulations to the Communist Party of China, to the workers, engineers, technicians, and scientists who have undertaken this task, to the People's Liberation Army, to the working class of China, and to the revolutionary people of China on the great victory they have achieved in the line of scientific experiments, as well as in the line of class struggle and production.

	"This is a great victory for the dictatorship of the proletariat, a great victory for the Chinese proletarian revolution, a great victory for the creative execution of the revolutionary line of the Communist Party of China, a great victory for Marxism-Leninism, and a great victory for Mao Zedong Thought.

	"This is a great victory won by the revolutionary people of China through self-reliance, overcoming numerous difficulties in the face of aggression and provocation by imperialism and the treacherous betrayal by Khrushchev and his accomplices and successors."

	The telegram pointed out: "This is a great victory that strikes at U.S. imperialism and its lackeys and running dogs, and at the revisionist partners centered around the clique that has usurped the power of the Soviet Union, striking at the nuclear blackmail and nuclear threat of the U.S. and the Soviet Union."

	The telegram said: "Our party highly praises the solemn assurance once again put forward by the Chinese government: that China will never be the first to use nuclear weapons. The Chinese people and the Chinese government will, as always, continue to strive together with all the peace-loving peoples and countries of the world to work for the complete prohibition and thorough destruction of nuclear weapons."

	Statement by the Executive Committee of the Revolutionary Communist Party of Chile

	The statement issued by the Executive Committee of the Revolutionary Communist Party of Chile pointed out that the successful detonation of China's first hydrogen bomb is a brilliant victory of great Mao Zedong Thought.

	The statement emphasized that the successful detonation of China's first hydrogen bomb is "a heavy blow to U.S. imperialism and its partner, Soviet revisionism," and is "a powerful support to the Vietnamese people and the Arab people who are fighting against U.S. imperialism."

	The statement further pointed out, "China's hydrogen bomb is a weapon in the hands of the people of the world."

	Telegram of Congratulations from the Central Committee of the "June 14" Revolutionary Movement of the Dominican Republic to the Communist Party of China:

	To the glorious people of China and the great leader of the Chinese Party, Comrade Mao Zedong, we extend warm congratulations on your nuclear test, which has once again achieved inspiring results. This is a heavy blow to imperialism, reactionaries of all countries, and modern revisionism, and a great encouragement to the revolutionary struggles of the peoples of Asia, Africa, and Latin America.

	Central Committee of the "June 14" Revolutionary Movement of the Dominican Republic
	June 18, 1967, Paris

	Telegram of Congratulations from the National Committee of the Progressive Labor Party of the United States to Chairman Mao

	Congratulations on the historic hydrogen bomb test. This is a heavy blow to imperialism and revisionism. This is a great victory for the Cultural Revolution and for the revolutionary people of the world. It once again proves the invincible power of Marxism-Leninism. The main base of the world revolution, China, is further strengthened under the guidance of Mao Zedong Thought, inspiring the revolutionaries in the United States.

	National Committee of the Progressive Labor Party of the United States
	June 19, 1967, New York

	(Xinhua News Agency)

### 热烈祝贺我国第一颗氢弹爆炸成功

1967-06-23
第5版()
专栏：

    热烈祝贺我国第一颗氢弹爆炸成功
    比利时共产党政治局的贺电
    比利时共产党中央委员会书记雅克·格里巴代表比利时共产党政治局十九日致电中国共产党中央委员会政治局，热烈祝贺我国第一颗氢弹爆炸成功。电报说：“我们党为中华人民共和国第一颗氢弹爆炸的成功向中国共产党，向从事于这一任务的工人、工程技术人员和科学工作者，向人民解放军，向中国的工人阶级以及中国的革命人民致以兄弟般的热烈祝贺，祝贺他们在科学实验战线也是在阶级斗争和生产战线上取得的这一新的伟大胜利。
    “这是无产阶级专政的伟大胜利，是中国无产阶级革命的伟大胜利，是创造性地执行中国共产党的革命路线的伟大胜利，是马克思列宁主义的伟大胜利，是毛泽东思想的伟大胜利。
    “这是中国革命人民依靠自力更生，面临帝国主义的侵略和挑衅以及赫鲁晓夫及其同伙和继承者的无耻叛变克服了重重困难所取得的伟大胜利。”
    电报指出：“这是一个伟大的胜利，它打击了美帝国主义及其帮凶、走狗和以篡夺了苏联政权的集团为中心的修正主义伙伴，打击了美苏的核讹诈和核威胁。”
    电报说：“我们党高度赞扬中国政府再次郑重提出的如下保证：在任何时候、任何情况下，中国都不会首先使用核武器。中国人民和中国政府，将一如既往地继续同全世界一切爱好和平的人民和国家一道，共同努力，为全面禁止和彻底销毁核武器而奋斗。”
    智利革命共产党执行委员会的声明
    智利革命共产党执行委员会发表的声明指出，中国第一颗氢弹爆炸成功是伟大的毛泽东思想的光辉胜利。
    声明强调指出，中国第一颗氢弹爆炸成功是“对美帝国主义和它的同伙苏联修正主义的一个沉重打击”，也是“对正在与美帝国主义进行斗争的越南人民和阿拉伯人民的强有力的支援”。
    声明又指出，“中国的氢弹就是全世界人民手中的武器。”
