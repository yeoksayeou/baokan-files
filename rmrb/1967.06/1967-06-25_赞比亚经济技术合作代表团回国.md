Title: Zambian Economic and Technical Cooperation Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1967-06-25
Edition: 5

The Zambian economic and technical cooperation delegation, led by Zambia's Minister of Finance Arthur N. Wina, left Beijing by plane today to return home.

The delegation was seen off at the airport by the Minister of Foreign Economic Relations, Fang Yi, Deputy Minister Yang Lin, and Vice Minister of Communications, Ge Chen.

### 赞比亚经济技术合作代表团回国

1967-06-25
第5版()
专栏：

    赞比亚经济技术合作代表团回国
    新华社二十四日讯　由赞比亚财政部部长阿瑟·尼·勒·维纳率领的赞比亚共和国政府经济技术合作代表团，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    对外经委主任方毅、副主任杨琳，交通部副部长葛琛等前往机场欢送。
