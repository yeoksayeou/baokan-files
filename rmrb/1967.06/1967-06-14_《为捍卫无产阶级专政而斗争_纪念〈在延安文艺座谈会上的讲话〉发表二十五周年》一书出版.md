Title: In Defense of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat: Commemorating the 25th Anniversary of the Publication of "Talks at the Yan'an Forum on Literature and Art"
Author:
Date: 1967-06-14
Edition: 1

The book "In Defense of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat: Commemorating the 25th Anniversary of the Publication of 'Talks at the Yan'an Forum on Literature and Art'" has been published.

New China News Agency, June 13th: Comrade Chen Boda and Comrade Wang Li's speeches at the capital's commemoration meeting for the 25th anniversary of the publication of Chairman Mao's brilliant work "Talks at the Yan'an Forum on Literature and Art," as well as the meeting's "Letter of Salutation to Chairman Mao," Comrade Yao Wenyuan's speech at the Shanghai commemoration meeting, and the editorials published by the journals "Red Flag," "People's Daily," "Liberation Army Daily," and Shanghai's "Wenhui Daily" to commemorate the 25th anniversary of the publication of the "Talks," have been compiled by the People's Publishing House into the book "In Defense of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat: Commemorating the 25th Anniversary of the Publication of 'Talks at the Yan'an Forum on Literature and Art'."

This book was released in Beijing on June 11th and will soon be distributed nationwide.

### 《为捍卫无产阶级专政而斗争——纪念〈在延安文艺座谈会上的讲话〉发表二十五周年》一书出版

1967-06-14
第1版()
专栏：

    《为捍卫无产阶级专政而斗争——纪念〈在延安文艺座谈会上的讲话〉发表二十五周年》一书出版
    新华社十三日讯　陈伯达同志、戚本禹同志在首都纪念毛主席的光辉著作《在延安文艺座谈会上的讲话》发表二十五周年大会上的讲话和大会《给毛主席的致敬信》，姚文元同志在上海纪念大会上的讲话，以及《红旗》杂志、《人民日报》、《解放军报》和上海《文汇报》分别发表的纪念《讲话》发表二十五周年的社论，已由人民出版社汇集成《为捍卫无产阶级专政而斗争——纪念〈在延安文艺座谈会上的讲话〉发表二十五周年》一书出版。
    这本书已于六月十一日在北京发行，全国各地也将陆续发行。
