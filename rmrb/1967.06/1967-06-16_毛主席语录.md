Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1967-06-16
Edition: 1

	These comrades have an incorrect way of looking at problems. They do not look at the essential and mainstream aspects of problems, but instead emphasize those non-essential and non-mainstream aspects. It should be pointed out: one cannot ignore non-essential and non-mainstream aspects of problems; one must solve them one by one. However, one should not regard these as the essence and mainstream, to the point of confusing one's own direction.

	*From "On the Question of Agricultural Cooperation"*

### 毛主席语录

1967-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    这些同志看问题的方法不对。他们不去看问题的本质方面，主流方面，而是强调那些非本质方面、非主流方面的东西。应当指出：不能忽略非本质方面和非主流方面的问题，必须逐一地将它们解决。但是，不应当将这些看成为本质和主流，以致迷惑了自己的方向。
                                      《关于农业合作化问题》
