Title: To Achieve Bumper Harvests and Fill the Granaries
Author: Tang Maolin
Date: 1967-06-16
Edition: 4

    To ensure bumper harvests and fill the granaries, the students of the second production brigade of Huai Shu Dai in Deng County, Henan Province, have organized themselves. Carrying the red "Quotations from Chairman Mao," and singing songs of Mao's quotations, they enthusiastically help the collective by picking up stray grains of wheat.
