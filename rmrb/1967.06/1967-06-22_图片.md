Title: Zambian President Kaunda Arrives in Beijing
Author: Lü Xiangyou
Date: 1967-06-22
Edition: 4

Zambian President Kenneth Kaunda arrived in Beijing by special plane. Accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, President Kaunda greeted the welcoming crowds of the capital.  Photographed by People's Daily reporter Lü Xiangyou.
