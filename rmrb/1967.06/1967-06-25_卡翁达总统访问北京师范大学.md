Title: President Kaunda Visits Beijing Normal University
Author:
Date: 1967-06-25
Edition: 2

President Kaunda of Zambia and other Zambian friends visited Beijing Normal University today and were warmly welcomed by the Red Guards and all revolutionary teachers and students.

President Kaunda and other Zambian friends held a friendly discussion with the Red Guards. The leader of the Revolutionary Committee of Beijing Normal University introduced the situation of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution at the school to the Zambian guests. He said, "We are Chairman Mao's little red soldiers. We will resolutely hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought and carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution through to the end, fighting to thoroughly eliminate imperialism, revisionism, and old and new colonialism."

President Kaunda said at the meeting, "The forces of imperialism and old and new colonialism must be thoroughly eliminated from the earth. The Chinese people have been resolutely fighting against imperialism and old and new colonialism, and the Zambian people are doing the same. At present, the Republic of Zambia is surrounded by imperialism and old and new colonialism, but we Zambian people are not afraid at all."

President Kaunda and other Zambian friends also visited some of the teaching instruments at Beijing Normal University.

### 卡翁达总统访问北京师范大学

1967-06-25
第2版()
专栏：

    卡翁达总统访问北京师范大学
    新华社二十四日　讯赞比亚共和国总统卡翁达和其他赞比亚朋友，今天访问了北京师范大学，受到红卫兵小将和全体革命师生的热烈欢迎。
    卡翁达总统和其他赞比亚朋友，同红卫兵小将进行了友好的座谈。北京师范大学革命委员会负责人，向赞比亚客人介绍了学校的无产阶级文化大革命情况。他说，我们是毛主席的红小兵，我们坚决高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底，为彻底消灭帝国主义、修正主义和新老殖民主义而斗争。
    卡翁达总统在座谈会上说，帝国主义和新老殖民主义的力量必须在地球上彻底消灭。你们中国人民对帝国主义和新老殖民主义作了坚决的斗争，赞比亚人民也正这样做。目前赞比亚共和国是受着帝国主义和新老殖民主义包围的，但是，我们赞比亚人民一点也不害怕。
    卡翁达总统和其他赞比亚朋友在北京师范大学还参观了部分教学仪器。
