Title: Delegation of the Dhofar Liberation Front Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-06-24
Edition: 5

The delegation of the Dhofar Liberation Front, led by the Front's leader Muhammad Ahmad Qasani, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 22nd. The delegation members include Salim Ali Musallam, a member of the Political Committee of the Dhofar Liberation Front, and Ahmad Suhail Fareh, a member of the Military Committee. They are visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

### 佐法尔解放阵线代表团到京

1967-06-24
第5版()
专栏：

    佐法尔解放阵线代表团到京
    新华社二十三日讯　佐法尔解放阵线代表团，在解放阵线领导成员穆罕默德·艾赫迈德·加萨尼率领下，于二十二日乘飞机抵达北京。代表团团员有佐法尔解放阵线的政治委员会成员萨里姆·阿里·穆萨拉姆和军事委员会成员艾赫迈德·苏海尔·法雷赫。他们是应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的。
