Title: Foreign Friends Celebrate China's Hydrogen Bomb Test
Author:
Date: 1967-06-19
Edition: 7

On the 17th, at a banquet celebrating the 20th anniversary of the publication of Xinhua News Agency dispatches in London, foreign friends enthusiastically applauded and shouted "Long live Chairman Mao!" upon hearing the news of China's successful detonation of its first hydrogen bomb.
