Title: The Secretariat Meeting of the Asian-African Journalists Association Discusses the General Report
Author:
Date: 1967-06-17
Edition: 3

The fifth plenary meeting of the secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists Association discussed the general report delivered by General Secretary Charoen yesterday.

Representatives held a full and heated discussion at today's meeting. In their speeches, they unanimously expressed their full agreement with the general report, pointing out that the report, using Mao Zedong Thought, analyzed the current international situation and reflected the revolutionary line of the Asian-African Journalists Association in opposing imperialism, opposing modern revisionism, opposing the reactionaries of various countries, and supporting the revolutionary struggles of the peoples of various countries.

Representatives in their speeches enthusiastically endorsed and supported the proposal in the general report that learning, applying, and defending Mao Zedong Thought is the absolute task of revolutionary journalists. They pledged to spread Mao Zedong Thought to the revolutionary peoples of Asia and Africa and to promote the revolutionary struggles of the peoples of Asia and Africa.

### 亚非记协书记处会议讨论总报告

1967-06-17
第3版()
专栏：

    亚非记协书记处会议讨论总报告
    新华社十六日讯　亚非新闻工作者协会第五次书记处全体会议今天讨论了总书记查禾多昨天作的总报告。
    代表们在今天的会议上进行了充分热烈的讨论。他们在发言中一致表示完全赞同总报告，指出，总报告运用毛泽东思想分析了当前国际形势，体现了亚非新闻工作者协会反对帝国主义、反对现代修正主义、反对各国反动派，支持各国人民革命斗争的革命路线。
    代表们在发言中热烈拥护和支持总报告中提出的学习、运用和捍卫毛泽东思想是革命新闻工作者的绝对任务。他们表示一定要把毛泽东思想传播给亚非革命人民，推动亚非人民的革命斗争。
