Title: U.S. Ships and Planes Intrude into Our Territorial Waters and Airspace
Author:
Date: 1967-06-15
Edition: 2

On June 13 at 20:40 until June 14 at 3:36, a U.S. warship intruded into our territorial waters east of Baijian Island in Fujian Province. On June 13 from 4:09 to 13:26 and from 16:06 to June 14 at 3:57, a U.S. warship intruded into our territorial waters south of Dongshan Island in Fujian Province and south of Shantou and Daxing Mountain in Guangdong Province. On June 14 from 12:19 to 12:30, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into our airspace over Yongxing Island and Dongdao Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. Regarding the military provocations by the U.S. warships and military aircraft, the spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the Ministry, issued the 436th serious warning.

### 美舰美机侵入我领海领空  我提出第四百三十六次严重警告

1967-06-15
第2版()
专栏：

    美舰美机侵入我领海领空
    我提出第四百三十六次严重警告
    新华社十四日讯　六月十三日二十时四十分至十四日三时三十六分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省白犬列岛以东地区领海。六月十三日四时九分至十三时二十六分和十六时六分至十四日三时五十七分，美国军舰一艘，两次侵入我福建省东山岛以南和广东省汕尾、大星山以南地区领海。十四日十二时十九分至十二时三十分，美国军用飞机一架，又侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛、东岛地区领空。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百三十六次严重警告。
