Title: Such a "Highest Ideal"
Author:
Date: 1967-06-02
Edition: 5

Moscow University has a Soviet student who has two photos cut from an American magazine pasted on his bedhead: one is of an American streamlined car, and the other is of a woman sitting on a beach. This student shamelessly openly tells others: the things in the two photos are his "highest ideal" in life.

### 如此“最高理想”

1967-06-02
第5版()
专栏：

    如此“最高理想”
    莫斯科大学有个苏联学生在床头贴着两张从美国画报上剪下来的照片：一张是一辆美国流线型汽车，另一张是一个坐在海滩上的女人。这个学生毫不知耻地公开对人说：两张照片上的东西，就是他一生中的“最高理想”。
