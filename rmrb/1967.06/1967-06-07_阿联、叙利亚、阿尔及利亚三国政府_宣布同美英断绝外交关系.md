Title: Egypt, Syria, Algeria Announce Severance of Diplomatic Relations with the US and UK
Author:
Date: 1967-06-07
Edition: 5

Egypt, Syria, and Algeria have announced the severance of diplomatic relations with the United States and the United Kingdom.

	CAIRO, June 6 (Xinhua) - According to a report from the Middle East News Agency, the Egyptian Ministry of Foreign Affairs issued a statement on June 6 announcing that the Egyptian government has severed diplomatic relations with the United States and the United Kingdom.

	The statement said: Egypt believes that the United States and the United Kingdom have actually participated in the aggression against the entire Arab nation, violating the territorial integrity of Arab countries.

	According to another report from the Middle East News Agency, Damascus Radio broadcasted on June 6 that the Syrian government has decided to sever diplomatic relations with the United States and the United Kingdom. The radio said that the United States and the United Kingdom have conspired with Israel to participate in the aggression against Arab countries.

	ALGIERS, June 6 (Xinhua) - The Algerian Ministry of Foreign Affairs issued a statement late on June 6 announcing that the Algerian government has severed diplomatic relations with the United States.

	The statement said: It has been proven that the United States and the United Kingdom have clearly carried out aggression against various Arab countries. Therefore, the Algerian government has decided, together with some Arab countries, to sever diplomatic relations with the United States.

	The Algerian government has already severed diplomatic relations with the United Kingdom since 1965 due to its opposition to the British support for the unilateral declaration of "independence" by the white colonial regime in Southern Rhodesia.

	(Accompanying photo)

	On June 5, the Algerian people held an anti-American demonstration. The photo shows angry demonstrators throwing stones at the American cultural center. (Photo by Xinhua reporter, telephoto)

### 阿联、叙利亚、阿尔及利亚三国政府  宣布同美英断绝外交关系

1967-06-07
第5版()
专栏：

    阿联、叙利亚、阿尔及利亚三国政府
    宣布同美英断绝外交关系
    新华社开罗六日电  据中东通讯社报道，阿联外交部六日发表声明，宣布阿联政府同美国和英国断绝外交关系。
    声明说：阿联认为，美国和英国实际上参与了对整个阿拉伯国家的侵略行动，侵犯了阿拉伯国家的领土完整。
    另据中东通讯社报道，大马士革电台六日广播，叙利亚政府决定同美国和英国政府断绝外交关系。电台说，美国和英国同以色列合谋，参与了对阿拉伯国家的侵略。
    新华社阿尔及尔六日电　阿尔及利亚外交部六日晚发表公报，宣布阿尔及利亚政府同美国政府断绝外交关系。
    公报说，事实证明，美国和英国对阿拉伯各国进行了明显的侵略。为此，阿尔及利亚政府决定和一些阿拉伯国家的政府一起同美国断绝外交关系。
    阿尔及利亚政府由于反对英国支持南罗得西亚白人殖民政权片面宣布“独立”，已经在一九六五年同英国政府断绝了外交关系。
