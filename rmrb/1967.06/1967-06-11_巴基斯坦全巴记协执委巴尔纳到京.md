Title: Pakistani Journalist and Executive Committee Member of the All-Pakistan Newspaper Employees Confederation, Barna, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-06-11
Edition: 5

Barna, a representative from Pakistan attending the upcoming fifth session of the secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists Association, and an executive committee member of the All-Pakistan Newspaper Employees Confederation and correspondent for the "Pakistan Times" in Dhaka, the capital of East Pakistan, arrived in Beijing by airplane this evening.

### 巴基斯坦全巴记协执委巴尔纳到京

1967-06-11
第5版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦全巴记协执委巴尔纳到京
    据新华社十日讯　出席即将召开的亚非新闻工作者协会第五次书记处全会的巴基斯坦代表、巴基斯坦全巴记协执委、《巴基斯坦时报》驻东巴首府达卡记者明哈杰·巴尔纳，今天晚上乘飞机到达北京。
