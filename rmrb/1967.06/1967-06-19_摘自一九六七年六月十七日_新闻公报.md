Title: China Successfully Tests Hydrogen Bomb
Author:
Date: 1967-06-19
Edition: 7

China has developed the atomic bomb, the guided missile, and now the hydrogen bomb. This greatly boosts the morale of the revolutionary people of the world and strikes a heavy blow to the arrogance of imperialism, modern revisionism, and the reactionaries of all countries.
