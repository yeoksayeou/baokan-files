Title: Heartiest Congratulations on the Success of China's First Hydrogen Bomb Test
Author:
Date: 1967-06-19
Edition: 5

Leaders of Vietnam Send Telegram to Chairman Mao
Heartiest Congratulations on the Success of China's First Hydrogen Bomb Test
The brilliant achievement of the Chinese people has made a tremendous contribution to the struggle of the world's people against U.S.-led imperialism and is a powerful inspiration for the Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. aggression for national salvation.

New China News Agency, June 18th: Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Chairman Long Zheng of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Premier Pham Van Dong of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong and the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and the Government of the People's Republic of China, expressing their warmest congratulations on the successful test of China's first hydrogen bomb. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

To Chairman Mao Zedong in Beijing,
The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China,
The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China,
The Government of the People's Republic of China,

On the occasion of the successful test of China's first hydrogen bomb, we, on behalf of the Vietnamese people, the Vietnamese Workers' Party, the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and in our personal capacities, extend our warmest congratulations to you.

This great achievement marks a new stage in China's mastery of nuclear weapons.

China already has the atomic bomb, and now it has the hydrogen bomb, which not only makes the national defense forces of the People's Republic of China even stronger but also makes a tremendous contribution to the struggle of the world's people against U.S.-led imperialism and for the defense of peace.

The Vietnamese people are delighted by this brilliant achievement of the Chinese people and regard it as a powerful inspiration for their own struggle against U.S. aggression for national salvation and for the struggle of the world's working class and peoples for peace, national independence, democracy, and socialism.

Please convey our warm congratulations to the officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, to the Chinese workers, and to the scientific and technological workers.

We wish you good health.

Ho Chi Minh
Chairman of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam

Long Zheng
Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam

Pham Van Dong
Premier of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam

Hanoi, June 18, 1967

### 最热烈祝贺我国第一颗氢弹爆炸成功

1967-06-19
第5版()
专栏：

    越南领导人致电毛泽东主席
    最热烈祝贺我国第一颗氢弹爆炸成功
    中国人民的这一辉煌成就，为世界人民反对以美国为首的帝国主义的斗争作出了巨大贡献，是对越南人民抗美救国斗争的强大鼓舞力量。
    新华社十八日讯　越南民主共和国主席胡志明、越南民主共和国国会常务委员会主席长征、越南民主共和国政府总理范文同，今天打电报给毛泽东主席和中国共产党中央委员会、人大常委会和中国政府，热烈祝贺中国第一颗氢弹爆炸成功。贺电全文如下：北京毛泽东主席同志，中国共产党中央委员会，中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会
  常务委员会，中华人民共和国政府
    值此中国第一颗氢弹爆炸成功之际，我们代表越南人民、越南劳动党、越南民主共和国国会、越南民主共和国政府，并以个人的名义，谨向你们表示最热烈的祝贺。
    这个伟大成就标志着中国在掌握核武器方面进入了一个新的发展阶段。
    中国已经有了原子弹，现在又有了氢弹，不仅使中华人民共和国的国防力量更加强大，而且为世界人民反对以美国为首的帝国主义和保卫和平的斗争作出了巨大贡献。
    越南人民为兄弟的中国人民的这个辉煌成就而感到欢欣鼓舞，并且把它看作是对自己的抗美救国斗争以及对世界工人阶级和世界人民争取和平、民族独立、民主和社会主义的斗争的强大鼓舞力量。
    请你们向中国人民解放军指战员、中国工人兄弟姐妹和科学技术工作者转达我们亲切的祝贺。
    祝你们身体健康。			越南民主共和国主席  胡志明
                        越南民主共和国
                        国会常务委员会主席  长征
                        越南民主共和国政府总理  范文同				一九六七年六月十八日于河内
