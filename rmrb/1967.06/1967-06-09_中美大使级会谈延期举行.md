Title: Sino-American Ambassadorial-Level Talks Postponed
Author:
Date: 1967-06-09
Edition: 6

The 133rd session of the Sino-American ambassadorial-level talks, originally scheduled for June 7, has been postponed to June 14, 1967, due to administrative reasons. Both sides agreed to the postponement.

### 中美大使级会谈延期举行

1967-06-09
第6版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈延期举行
    新华社华沙六日电　原定在六月七日举行的中美大使级会谈第一百三十三次会议，由于行政方面的原因，双方同意推迟到一九六七年六月十四日举行。
