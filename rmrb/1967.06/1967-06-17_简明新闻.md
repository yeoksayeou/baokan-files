	Brief News

	The Republic of the Congo (Brazzaville) Nationalizes Two French Companies

	The government of the Republic of the Congo (Brazzaville) announced on the afternoon of the 15th that it would nationalize two French companies based in the Congolese capital, Brazzaville.

	This measure was decided during a full session of the National Assembly held that afternoon. The session passed laws proposed by the government to establish a state-owned power company and a state-owned water supply company.

	West Pakistan Government Decides to Waive Tuition Fees for Arab Students

	Mohammad Musa, the governor of West Pakistan, notified all universities and educational institutions in West Pakistan on the 11th to waive all tuition and boarding fees for Arab students until further notice.

	This measure was taken to support the Arab people in their anti-aggression struggle.

	Mohammad Musa ordered all educational authorities in the province to provide every possible assistance to Arab students studying in West Pakistan and to care for their living conditions.

	U.S. Declares "Oil Emergency"

	Due to the punitive measures taken by Arab countries, such as cutting off oil supplies, against U.S. aggressors, the U.S. Department of the Interior announced on the 10th with unease that the United States is in a state of "oil emergency."

	Assistant Secretary of the Interior, Moore, admitted that "if the current suspension of supplies continues for a few more weeks," there will be "very serious transportation and supply problems."

    简明新闻
    刚果（布）把两家法国公司收归国有
    刚果（布）政府十五日下午宣布把设在刚果（布）首都布拉柴维尔的两家法国公司收归国有。
    这一措施是在当天下午召开的国民议会全体会议上作出的。这次会议通过了政府提出的关于建立一家国营电力公司和一家国营自来水公司的法律。
    西巴政府决定免收阿拉伯学生学费
    西巴基斯坦省督穆罕默德·穆萨十一日通知西巴基斯坦各大学和教育机关，免收阿拉伯学生的一切学费和膳宿费，直到有进一步的决定时为止。
    采取这个措施，是为了对反侵略斗争中的阿拉伯各国人民表示支援。
    穆罕默德·穆萨命令全省各教育当局对在西巴基斯坦学习的阿拉伯学生提供一切可能的帮助，并且关心他们的生活。
    美帝宣布出现“石油紧急状态”
    由于阿拉伯国家对美国侵略者进行惩罚而采取的切断石油供应等措施，美国内政部十日不安地宣布，美国已经出现“石油紧急状态”。
    美国内政部助理部长穆尔承认，“如果目前停止供应的情况再继续几个星期”，就要发生“非常严重的运输和供应问题”。
                        （据新华社讯）
