Title: Publication of the Book "Minutes of the Military Art Work Symposium Convened by Comrade Jiang Qing on Behalf of Comrade Lin Biao"
Author:
Date: 1967-06-07
Edition: 4

The book "Minutes of the Military Art Work Symposium Convened by Comrade Jiang Qing on Behalf of Comrade Lin Biao" has been published.

	New China News Agency, June 5th: After the publication of "Comrade Lin Biao's Letter to the Standing Committee of the Central Military Commission" and "Minutes of the Military Art Work Symposium Convened by Comrade Jiang Qing on Behalf of Comrade Lin Biao," they have been warmly welcomed by the broad masses of workers, peasants, soldiers, revolutionary cadres, and revolutionary literary and art workers. To meet the needs of the broad revolutionary masses for studying these two important documents that shine with the brilliance of Mao Zedong Thought, the People's Publishing House has published a book titled "Minutes of the Military Art Work Symposium Convened by Comrade Jiang Qing on Behalf of Comrade Lin Biao."

	This book will be available for purchase starting June 6th at the New China Bookstore in Beijing and will be distributed nationwide in the near future.

### 《林彪同志委托江青同志召开的部队文艺工作座谈会纪要》一书出版

1967-06-07
第4版()
专栏：

    《林彪同志委托江青同志召开的部队文艺工作座谈会纪要》一书出版
    新华社五日讯　《林彪同志给中央军委常委的信》和《林彪同志委托江青同志召开的部队文艺工作座谈会纪要》发表后，受到广大工农兵、革命干部和革命文艺工作者的热烈欢呼。为满足广大革命群众对这两个闪耀着毛泽东思想光辉的重要文件的学习需要，已由人民出版社以《林彪同志委托江青同志召开的部队文艺工作座谈会纪要》为书名出版。
    这本书，从六日起在北京新华书店发行，并将在全国陆续发行。
