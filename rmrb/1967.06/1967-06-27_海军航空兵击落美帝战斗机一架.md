Title: Naval Aviation Forces Shoot Down One U.S. Imperialist Fighter Jet
Author:
Date: 1967-06-27
Edition: 1

Another Major Victory in Defending the Motherland and the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution

Naval Aviation Forces Shoot Down One U.S. Imperialist Fighter Jet

The Central Military Commission issued an order of commendation stating that this victory is a great encouragement to the patriotic struggle of our compatriots in Hong Kong and Kowloon against British imperialism and violence, and a powerful support for the Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. imperialism for national liberation.

Xinhua News Agency, June 26th - This afternoon, a U.S. imperialist F-4C type fighter jet intruded into the airspace over the southeastern region of Hainan Island and carried out a war provocation. It was immediately shot down by our heroic People's Liberation Army Naval Aviation Forces.

This victory in battle is another major victory achieved by the Naval Aviation Forces in defending the national defense of the motherland and the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, following the People's Liberation Army Air Force's consecutive shooting down of six U.S. aggressor aircraft in the Guangxi region from April 24th to June 12th.

Xinhua News Agency, June 26th - The Central Military Commission today issued an order of commendation, commending the certain unit of the People's Liberation Army Naval Aviation Forces that shot down a U.S. imperialist F-4C type fighter jet over the southeastern region of Hainan Island this afternoon.

The order of commendation states: This battle was fought accurately, fiercely, and well. This victory is a victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, a victory of the proletarian revolutionary line represented by Chairman Mao, and a victory of the great and invincible Mao Zedong Thought.

The order of commendation points out: At the moment when the people of the whole country and the people of the world are enthusiastically celebrating the successful explosion of our country's first hydrogen bomb, and when our Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution has achieved decisive victories, your victory report has arrived, greatly boosting the morale of the revolutionary people and greatly dampening the enemy's arrogance. This victory is a great encouragement to the patriotic struggle of our compatriots in Hong Kong and Kowloon against British imperialism and violence, and a powerful support for the Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. imperialism for national liberation. This victory in battle is the result of your high举毛泽东思想伟大红旗, emphasizing proletarian politics, and earnestly studying and applying Chairman Mao's works; it is the result of your resolutely carrying out the instructions of our great leader Chairman Mao on grasping the revolution, promoting preparedness, promoting work, and promoting production. For this, a special commendation is hereby issued, and heartfelt congratulations are extended to the combatants.

The order of commendation states: Chairman Mao teaches us: "The imperialists and domestic reactionaries will never willingly accept their defeat. They will make their last struggles." It is hoped that you will summarize your experiences earnestly, remain humble and vigilant, and strive for further victories, enhance your vigilance, strengthen your preparedness, and "spare no effort to annihilate the invading enemy." Strive for greater victories in defending the national defense and the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

### 海军航空兵击落美帝战斗机一架

1967-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

    保卫祖国和保卫无产阶级文化大革命的又一重大胜利
    海军航空兵击落美帝战斗机一架
    中央军委颁发嘉奖令指出，这一胜利，是对港九爱国同胞反英抗暴斗争的极大鼓舞，是对越南人民抗美救国斗争的有力支持
    新华社二十六日讯　今天下午，美帝国主义F—4C型战斗机一架，侵入我国海南岛东南地区上空，进行战争挑衅，当即被我英雄的人民解放军海军航空兵某部一举击落。
    这次战斗胜利，是继人民解放军空军部队在四月二十四日至六月十二日连续在广西地区上空击落六架美国强盗飞机之后，海军航空兵在保卫祖国国防和保卫无产阶级文化大革命中所取得的又一新的重大胜利。
    新华社二十六日讯　中共中央军委今天颁发嘉奖令，表扬二十六日下午在海南岛东南地区上空一举击落美帝国主义F—4C型战斗机的中国人民解放军海军航空兵某部。
    嘉奖令说，这次战斗，打得准，打得狠，打得好。这一胜利，是无产阶级文化大革命的胜利，是以毛主席为代表的无产阶级革命路线的胜利，是伟大的战无不胜的毛泽东思想的胜利。
    嘉奖令指出，正当全国人民和全世界人民热烈欢呼我国第一颗氢弹爆炸成功的时候，正在我国无产阶级文化大革命取得决定性胜利的时候，你们的捷报传来，大长革命人民的志气，大灭敌人的威风。这一胜利，是对我港九爱国同胞反英抗暴斗争的极大鼓舞，是对越南人民抗美救国斗争的有力支持。这次战斗的胜利，是你们高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，大力突出无产阶级政治，认真地活学活用毛主席著作的结果；是你们坚决执行我们伟大领袖毛主席关于抓革命，促战备、促工作、促生产指示的结果。为此，特予通令嘉奖，并向参战指战员表示最热烈的祝贺。
    嘉奖令说，毛主席教导我们：“帝国主义者和国内反动派决不甘心于他们的失败，他们还要作最后的挣扎。”希望你们认真总结经验，戒骄戒躁，再接再厉，提高警惕，加强战备，“全力以赴，务歼入侵之敌。”为保卫国防和保卫无产阶级文化大革命争取更大的胜利。
