Cambodia Protests Thai Infringement of Cambodian Territory and Waters

Cambodia's Ministry of Foreign Affairs has recently sent four notes to the Thanom-Kittikachorn regime in Thailand, strongly protesting the repeated infringements by Thai troops on Cambodian territory.

The notes state that Thai ships have repeatedly violated Cambodian territorial waters in March. Thai troops have also laid mines in Cambodian territory.

Malaysian Crowds Protest Against Persecution of Political Prisoners

More than a thousand people in Penang, a city in northern Malaysia, held a demonstration on May 28 to protest against the persecution of political prisoners by the "Malaysia" regime of Rahman, a puppet of U.S. and British imperialism. They also firmly supported the hunger strike of political prisoners in the Batu Gajah concentration camp.

The demonstrators, filled with indignation, waved red flags and banners, shouting slogans along the way: "Down with the Rahman regime!" "Against dictatorship!" They also sang revolutionary songs such as "Unity is Strength" and "Strive for Victory."

Mahjoub Forms New Sudanese Government

The new government of Sudan was announced on May 29. Muhammad Ahmad Mahjoub is the Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs.

The previous government, led by Sadiq al-Mahdi as Prime Minister, resigned after the Constituent Assembly voted no confidence on May 15.

U.S. Imperialism Intensifies Suppression of Latin American Armed Struggles

The U.S. imperialists recently held consecutive meetings of the chiefs of the armies and air forces of American countries, plotting to intensify the suppression of guerrilla activities in Latin America.

These two meetings began in mid-May and were secretly held in the capitals of Colombia and Venezuela, where guerrilla activities are relatively active. It is revealed that the army chiefs' meeting discussed the issue of some countries jointly sending troops to suppress armed struggles in a certain country.

    柬抗议泰国侵犯柬领土、领海
    柬埔寨外交部最近四次照会泰国他侬—巴博集团，强烈抗议泰国军队多次侵犯柬埔寨的罪行。
    照会说，泰国船只三月份多次侵犯柬埔寨领海。泰国军队还在柬埔寨领土埋设地雷。
    马来亚群众示威反对迫害政治犯
    马来亚北部槟榔屿市的一千多群众，二十八日举行示威游行，抗议美、英帝国主义的傀儡“马来西亚”拉赫曼集团虐待政治犯，并坚决支持在华都牙也集中营里的政治犯们所进行的绝食斗争。
    情绪激昂的示威群众，高举红旗和横幅标语，沿途愤怒高呼：“打倒拉赫曼集团！”“反对独裁！”并高唱《团结就是力量》、《争取胜利》等革命歌曲。
    马赫古卜组成苏丹新政府
    苏丹新政府已于二十九日宣布组成。穆罕默德·艾哈迈德·马赫古卜任总理兼外交部长。
    以萨迪克·马赫迪为总理的前政府，是在制宪议会于五月十五日投不信任票后辞职的。
    美帝加紧镇压拉美人民武装斗争
    美帝国主义最近连续召开美洲国家陆军首脑会议和空军首脑会议，阴谋加紧镇压拉丁美洲人民的游击活动。
    这两个会议从五月中旬开始，分别在游击队比较活跃的哥伦比亚和委内瑞拉的首都秘密举行。据透露，在陆军首脑会议上，曾讨论纠合一些国家联合出兵镇压某一个国家中的武装斗争的问题。
                                             （据新华社讯）
