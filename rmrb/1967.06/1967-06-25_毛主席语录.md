	Chairman Mao's Quotations
	Imperialism fears most the awakening of the peoples of Asia, Africa, and Latin America, and the awakening of the peoples of the world. We must unite to drive American imperialism out of Asia, Africa, and Latin America and send it back to its own home.

		From the talk with Japanese, Cuban, Brazilian, and Argentine friends in Wuhan

### 毛主席语录

1967-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    帝国主义最怕的是亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲人民觉悟，怕世界各国人民的觉悟。我们要团结起来把美帝国主义从亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲赶回它的老家去。
         《在武汉接见日本、古巴、巴西和阿根廷的朋友时的谈话》
