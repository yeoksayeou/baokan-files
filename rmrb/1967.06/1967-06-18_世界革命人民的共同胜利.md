Title: The Common Victory of the Revolutionary People of the World
Author: Lǐ Dōngmín
Date: 1967-06-18
Edition: 3

At a time when our country's unprecedented Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution has achieved decisive victories, and when revolutionary waves are surging across the globe, another piece of news that shook all of China and the world has come: our country's first hydrogen bomb has successfully exploded!

This is another great victory for Mao Zedong Thought, another brilliant achievement of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. Every victory of Mao Zedong Thought is a common victory for the revolutionary people of the world, and it greatly inspires the revolutionary masses and the revolutionary people of the world.

The successful explosion of our country's first hydrogen bomb has greatly boosted the morale of the revolutionary masses and the Red Guards, and has greatly dampened the spirits of the handful of power-holders within the Party who are taking the capitalist road and the revisionists. It has greatly boosted the morale of the revolutionary people of all countries and has greatly dampened the spirits of imperialism, modern revisionism, and the reactionaries of all countries.

The successful explosion of our country's first hydrogen bomb is a great support and encouragement to the Vietnamese people who are on the frontline of the struggle against American imperialism. It is a great support and encouragement to the Arab people who are bravely fighting against the aggression of American and British imperialism and their faithful running dogs, Israel.

The successful explosion of our country's first hydrogen bomb is a great support and encouragement to our patriotic compatriots in Hong Kong who are fighting against the brutal oppression of British imperialism.

The successful explosion of our country's first hydrogen bomb has dealt a powerful blow to the imperialists, Soviet revisionists, and reactionaries of all countries who are at their wits' end. The dream of nuclear monopoly by the imperialists, revisionists, and reactionaries has once again been shamefully shattered, and the nuclear blackmail and deceitful talk of the paper tigers have proven useless. The revolutionary people of the world are jubilant and cheering.

Our day of joy is the day of misfortune for imperialism, modern revisionism, and the reactionaries of all countries. We, the revolutionary masses and the Red Guards, solemnly declare to the world: the Chinese people armed with Mao Zedong Thought are invincible and will definitely triumph over all enemies. We sternly warn the handful of scoundrels who dare to provoke us: you cannot escape the punishment of the revolutionary people. This is an inevitable law of history. We have the invincible Mao Zedong Thought. Not only can we manufacture hydrogen bombs, but we can also create all kinds of miracles.

We, the Red Guards, are the revolutionary youth of the Mao Zedong era. We will, like the PLA officers and soldiers, workers, engineering and technical personnel, and scientific workers who conducted the hydrogen bomb explosion test, study and apply Chairman Mao's works actively, break down selfishness and establish public spirit, and promote Mao Zedong Thought. We will overthrow, smash, and expose the handful of power-holders within the Party who are taking the capitalist road, and carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution through to the end!

### 世界革命人民的共同胜利
李冬民
1967-06-18
第3版()
专栏：

    世界革命人民的共同胜利
    北京市革命委员会常委
    首都中等学校红代会负责人  李冬民
    在我国史无前例的无产阶级文化大革命取得决定性胜利的时候，在全世界革命浪潮汹涌澎湃的时候，又一个震撼全中国，震撼全世界的喜讯传来：我国第一颗氢弹爆炸成功了！
    这是毛泽东思想的又一伟大胜利，无产阶级文化大革命的又一辉煌成果。毛泽东思想的每一个胜利，都是全世界革命人民的共同胜利，都极大地鼓舞着无产阶级革命派和全世界革命人民。
    我国第一颗氢弹爆炸成功，大长了无产阶级革命派和红卫兵的志气，大灭了党内最大的一小撮走资本主义道路当权派和修正主义分子的威风；大长了全世界各国革命人民的志气，大灭了帝国主义、现代修正主义和各国反动派的威风。
    我国第一颗氢弹爆炸成功，对在反美斗争最前线的越南人民是一个极大的支持，对在英勇抗击美英帝国主义及其忠实走狗以色列侵略的阿拉伯人民是一个极大的支持、极大的鼓舞。
    我国第一颗氢弹爆炸成功，对在香港反对英帝国主义残酷压迫的爱国同胞，是一个极大的支持，极大的鼓舞。
    我国第一颗氢弹爆炸成功，给了穷途末路的帝国主义、苏联修正主义和各国反动派以有力的打击。帝、修、反核垄断的迷梦再一次可耻地破灭了，纸老虎的核讹诈和骗人的鬼话不中用了。全世界革命人民为之振奋和欢呼。
    我们无产阶级革命派高兴之日，就是帝国主义、现代修正主义、各国反动派晦气之时。我们无产阶级革命派和红卫兵，向世界庄严宣告：用毛泽东思想武装起来的中国人民，绝不可侮，一定要压倒一切敌人。我们严厉警告一小撮敢于向我们挑衅的坏蛋们，你们逃脱不了革命人民的惩罚。这是历史的必然。我们有战无不胜的毛泽东思想，我们不但能够制造氢弹，而且可以创造一切人间奇迹。
    我们红卫兵是毛泽东时代的革命青年，我们要象从事氢弹爆炸试验的解放军指战员、工人、工程技术人员和科学工作者那样，活学活用毛主席著作，破私立公，大树毛泽东思想，把党内最大的一小撮走资本主义道路的当权派斗倒、斗垮、斗臭，把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底！
