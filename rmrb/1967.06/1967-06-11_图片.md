Title: Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, and Jiang Qing Watch a Performance
Author: Lü Xiangyou
Date: 1967-06-11
Edition: 3

	Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Jiang Qing, and other comrades watched the revolutionary modern Peking opera "Harbor" performed by the Shanghai Peking Opera Theater at the Great Hall of the People on June 10th. After the performance, they went on stage to take a group photo with the actors.

		Photo by our reporter Lü Xiangyou
