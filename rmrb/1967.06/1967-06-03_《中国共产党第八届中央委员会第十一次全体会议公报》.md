Title: Report of the Eleventh Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1967-06-03
Edition: 1

To arm the masses of workers, peasants, soldiers, revolutionary intellectuals, and the broad cadre with Mao Zedong Thought, to further promote the revolutionization of people's thinking, is the most reliable and fundamental guarantee to prevent revisionism, prevent the restoration of capitalism, and to ensure the victory of our socialist and communist cause.
