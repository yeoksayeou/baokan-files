Title: Chinese Red Guards Delegation Departs for Tirana
Author:
Date: 1967-06-22
Edition: 1

At the invitation of the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Labor Youth League, the Chinese Red Guards delegation, led by Comrade Yao Wenyuan, departed Beijing by plane on June 20th and headed for Tirana.

The members of the Chinese Red Guards delegation are: Tan Houlan, Liu Xichang, Song Yimin, Chen Ganfeng, Cheng Jinhua, and Gao Jinghui.

Those who came to the airport to see them off included Comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Xie Fuzhi, Liu Ningyi, Jiang Qing, Xiao Hua, Yang Chengwu, Zhang Chunqiao, Wang Li, Guan Feng, Chen Boda, Zhao Yimin, and others.

Also present at the airport were representatives from the Beijing Revolutionary Committee, the Beijing Workers' Congress, the Red Guards of higher education institutions, and the Red Guards of middle schools, including Nie Yuanzi, Ding Guanyi, Wang Laomin, Lu Wenge, Dai Baoyuan, Li Longhe, Lin Xiaomang, Wang Qun, Li Dongmin, Chen Yongkang, and over 2,000 Red Guards from the capital.

The Albanian Ambassador to China, Baba Ni, was also at the airport to see them off.

The members of the Chinese Red Guards delegation wore Mao badges on their chests and held copies of the "Little Red Book," the selected works of Chairman Mao. They were full of spirit and vigor. When they arrived at the airport with Zhou Enlai and other comrades, the Red Guards raised high the portraits of Chairman Mao, waved red flags, beat drums, and shouted in unison for a long time: "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Long live Comrade Enver Hoxha!" They asked the delegation to convey the fighting friendship of the Chinese Red Guards and revolutionary youth to their Albanian comrades.

### 我国红卫兵代表团离京前往地拉那

1967-06-22
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟第五次代表大会
    我国红卫兵代表团离京前往地拉那
    周恩来陈伯达康生谢富治刘宁一江青萧华杨成武等同志到机场送行
    新华社二十一日讯  应邀参加阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟第五次代表大会的中国红卫兵代表团，在团长姚文元同志率领下，二十日乘飞机离开北京，前往地拉那。
    中国红卫兵代表团团员有：谭厚兰、刘锡昌、宋义民、陈敢峰、程金香、高静慧。
    到机场送行的，有周恩来、陈伯达、康生、谢富治、刘宁一、江青、萧华、杨成武、张春桥、王力、关锋、戚本禹、赵毅敏等负责同志。
    到机场送行的，还有北京市革命委员会、北京市工代会、大专院校红代会、中学红代会的负责成员和代表聂元梓、丁国钰、王老民、鲁文阁、戴宝元、李龙河、林晓芒、王群、李冬民、陈永康，以及首都红卫兵二千多人。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使馆参赞巴巴尼也到机场送行。
    中国红卫兵代表团的成员们，胸前佩带着金光闪闪的毛主席像章，手捧红彤彤的革命宝书《毛主席语录》，精神抖擞，生气勃勃。当他们同周恩来等同志来到机场时，红卫兵小将们高高举起毛主席像，挥动一面面红旗，敲锣打鼓，长时间地有节奏地高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“恩维尔·霍查同志万岁！”请代表团把中国红卫兵和革命青年的战斗友谊，带给兄弟的阿尔巴尼亚战友们。
