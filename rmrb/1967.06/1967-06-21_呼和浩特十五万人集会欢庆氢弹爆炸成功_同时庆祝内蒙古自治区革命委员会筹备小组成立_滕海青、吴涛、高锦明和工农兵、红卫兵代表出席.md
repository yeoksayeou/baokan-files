Title: 150,000 Gather in Hohhot to Celebrate Hydrogen Bomb Success and Formation of Revolutionary Committee Preparatory Group

Author:

Date: 1967-06-21

Edition: 3

Hohhot: 150,000 Gather to Celebrate Hydrogen Bomb Success and Formation of Revolutionary Committee Preparatory Group

Hohhot, June 20 — A grand rally of 150,000 revolutionary masses, revolutionary cadres, and local military personnel was held in Hohhot on the evening of June 18 to celebrate the successful detonation of China's first hydrogen bomb and the formal establishment of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Revolutionary Committee Preparatory Group.

The meeting was presided over by Wu Tao, Deputy Director of the Inner Mongolia Revolutionary Committee Preparatory Group and Political Commissar of the Inner Mongolia Military Region. Teng Haiqing, Director of the Inner Mongolia Revolutionary Committee Preparatory Group and Acting Commander of the Inner Mongolia Military Region, addressed the meeting. He said that the successful detonation of China's first hydrogen bomb is a brilliant achievement of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and a great victory for Mao Zedong Thought. He said that the Inner Mongolia Revolutionary Committee Preparatory Group will definitely follow the teachings of Chairman Mao, firmly rely on the revolutionary cadres, firmly rely on the broad revolutionary masses, and firmly rely on the majority of the cadres to thoroughly overthrow the handful of power-holders taking the capitalist road within the Party, and rapidly promote the great alliance and the "three-in-one combination" of the revolution.

Representatives of revolutionary workers, revolutionary peasants, the People's Liberation Army, and the Three-in-One Red Guards of Hohhot all spoke at the meeting, repeatedly expressing their enthusiastic praise for the successful detonation of China's first hydrogen bomb. They all expressed their determination to push the mass movement of studying and applying Chairman Mao's works to a new stage in the revolutionary mass criticism movement, and to achieve the great alliance and the "three-in-one combination" of the revolution.

High Jinxing, a member of the Inner Mongolia Revolutionary Committee Preparatory Group, also spoke at the meeting.

The meeting finally adopted a telegram of congratulations to our most, most respected great leader Chairman Mao.

### 呼和浩特十五万人集会欢庆氢弹爆炸成功  同时庆祝内蒙古自治区革命委员会筹备小组成立  滕海青、吴涛、高锦明和工农兵、红卫兵代表出席

1967-06-21
第3版()
专栏：

    呼和浩特十五万人集会欢庆氢弹爆炸成功
    同时庆祝内蒙古自治区革命委员会筹备小组成立
    滕海青、吴涛、高锦明和工农兵、红卫兵代表出席
    据新华社呼和浩特二十日电　呼和浩特市无产阶级革命派、革命群众和当地驻军十五万人，十八日晚上举行盛大集会，热烈庆祝我国第一颗氢弹爆炸成功，并庆祝内蒙古自治区革命委员会筹备小组的正式成立。
    内蒙古革命委员会筹备小组副组长、内蒙古军区政治委员吴涛主持了这次大会。内蒙古革命委员会筹备小组组长、内蒙古军区代司令员滕海青在大会上讲了话。他说，我国第一颗氢弹爆炸成功，这是我国无产阶级文化大革命的光辉成果，这是毛泽东思想的伟大胜利。他说，内蒙古革命委员会筹备小组，一定遵循毛主席的教导，坚决依靠无产阶级革命派，坚决依靠广大革命群众，坚决依靠干部的大多数，彻底斗倒党内一小撮走资本主义道路当权派，迅速促进革命的大联合和革命的“三结合”。
    革命工人代表、革命农民代表、解放军代表、呼和浩特市三司红卫兵代表等在大会发言中，都一再热烈欢呼我国第一颗氢弹爆炸成功，都表示一定要在革命的大批判运动中，把活学活用毛主席著作的群众运动推向新阶段，实现革命的大联合和“三结合”。
    内蒙古革命委员会筹备小组成员高锦明也在大会上发言。
    大会最后通过了给我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席的致敬电。
