Title: Chinese Ambassador to the UAR Huang Hua Calls on President Nasser
Author:
Date: 1967-06-10
Edition: 1

Chinese Ambassador to the UAR Huang Hua Calls on President Nasser

	Conveys China's Firm Support for the UAR and the Arab People's Struggle Against Aggression

	Strongly condemns US, British imperialism and their tool Israel's aggression against Arab countries; expresses firm belief that as long as the Arab people unite, persist in protracted struggle, and overcome all difficulties, despite any setbacks and twists and turns, final victory will surely belong to the Arab people

	BEIJING, June 9 (Xinhua) — Chinese Ambassador to the UAR Huang Hua called on President Nasser of the UAR this morning at 10:00 AM. Ambassador Huang conveyed Premier Zhou Enlai's message of firm support from the Chinese government and people for the UAR people and the Arab people's struggle against aggression.

	Ambassador Huang strongly condemned the aggression of US and British imperialism and their tool Israel against Arab countries. He expressed that the 700 million Chinese people, armed with the great thought of Mao Zedong, firmly stand on the side of the UAR and the Arab people. The Chinese government and people firmly believe that as long as the Arab people unite, persist in protracted struggle, and overcome all difficulties, despite any setbacks and twists and turns, final victory will surely belong to the Arab people.

### 我国驻阿联大使黄华拜会纳赛尔总统  转达中国对阿联和阿拉伯各国人民的坚决支持

1967-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我国驻阿联大使黄华拜会纳赛尔总统
    转达中国对阿联和阿拉伯各国人民的坚决支持
    强烈谴责美英以对阿拉伯国家的侵略；表示坚信，只要阿拉伯各国人民团结一致，坚持长期斗争，前赴后继，排除万难，不管遇到什么挫折和曲折，最后胜利一定属于阿拉伯人民
    新华社开罗九日电　中国驻阿联大使黄华今日上午十时拜会了阿联总统纳赛尔。黄华大使代表周恩来总理转达了中国政府和人民对阿联人民和阿拉伯各国人民反侵略斗争的坚决支持。
    黄华大使强烈谴责美英帝国主义及其工具以色列对阿拉伯国家的侵略，并表示，用伟大的毛泽东思想武装起来的七亿中国人民坚定不移地站在阿联和阿拉伯各国人民一边。中国政府和中国人民坚信，只要阿拉伯各国人民团结一致，坚持长期斗争，前赴后继，排除万难，不管遇到什么挫折和曲折，最后胜利一定属于阿拉伯人民。
