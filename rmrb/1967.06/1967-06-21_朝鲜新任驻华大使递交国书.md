Title: New North Korean Ambassador Presents Credentials
Author:
Date: 1967-06-21
Edition: 5

The new Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, Hyun Jun-gyeok, along with the entire diplomatic staff of the embassy, presented his credentials to Vice Chairman Song Qingling this afternoon.

Vice Chairman Song Qingling and Ambassador Hyun Jun-gyeok had a friendly conversation.

Present at the presentation of credentials were Deputy Foreign Minister Han Nianlong and others.

### 朝鲜新任驻华大使递交国书

1967-06-21
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜新任驻华大使递交国书
    新华社二十日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国新任驻中国特命全权大使玄峻极，偕同大使馆全体外交人员，今天下午向宋庆龄副主席递交国书。
    宋庆龄副主席和玄峻极大使进行了友好的谈话。
    递交国书时在场的，有外交部副部长韩念龙等。
