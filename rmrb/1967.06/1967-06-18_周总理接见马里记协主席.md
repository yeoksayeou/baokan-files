Title: Premier Zhou Meets Chairman of the Malian Journalists' Association
Author:
Date: 1967-06-18
Edition: 4

Premier Zhou received the Chairman of the Malian Journalists' Association this evening.

A Xinhua News Agency report dated the 17th states: Premier Zhou Enlai this evening received Mamadou Gologo, a member of the Political Bureau of the Sudanese Union Party of Mali, Minister of Information and Tourism of Mali, and Chairman of the Malian Journalists' Association. He had a warm and friendly conversation with him. Mamadou Gologo is currently attending the fifth plenary session of the Secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists' Association held in Beijing.

Present at the reception were Mu Xin and others.

### 周总理接见马里记协主席

1967-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理接见马里记协主席
    新华社十七日讯　周恩来总理今晚接见了马里苏丹联盟党政治局委员、马里新闻、游览部部长、马里记协主席马马杜·戈洛戈，同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。马马杜·戈洛戈正在参加在北京举行的亚非新闻工作者协会书记处第五次全会。
    接见时在座的有穆欣等。
