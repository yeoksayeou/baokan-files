Title: Iraqi President and Acting Foreign Minister Thank China for Supporting the Arab People
Author:
Date: 1967-06-06
Edition: 6

Iraqi Deputy Foreign Minister Nouri Jamill met with the Chinese Chargé d'Affaires in Iraq, Zhang Weiping, on June 4th in Baghdad. He conveyed the gratitude of Iraqi President Abdul Rahman Arif to the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao, the Chinese government, and the Chinese people for their support of the Arab people's struggle.

Jamill said that President Arif expressed his gratitude to the Chinese government and people for their support of the Arab people in their conflict with Israel and its imperialist backers. The President praised China's stance in supporting the Arab people's struggle for freedom and the elimination of imperialist influence.

Nouri Jamill also wished the Chinese people prosperity, strength, and happiness.

Xinhua News Agency, June 5th: Iraqi Acting Foreign Minister Abdul Gani Rawi sent a letter to Chinese Foreign Minister Chen Yi on June 1st, expressing gratitude for China's support of the Arab people's just cause at a time when Arab countries are facing threats of aggression from the United States and Israel.

Rawi mentioned in his letter, "I am particularly referring to your country's recent support for the Arabs' indisputable rights over their territorial waters. This honorable stance is just one in a series of honorable positions that your country has taken in the cause of defending the rights of all peoples, in the struggle against all forms of colonialism, and in exposing the imperialist conspiracies against Arab countries."

Rawi praised the Chinese people for their consistent and resolute stand with the Arab people, describing them as loyal allies in the Arab people's just struggle against imperialism.

### 伊拉克总统和代外长感谢我国对阿拉伯人民的支持  中国人民是阿拉伯人民的忠实战友

1967-06-06
第6版()
专栏：

    伊拉克总统和代外长感谢我国对阿拉伯人民的支持
    中国人民是阿拉伯人民的忠实战友
    新华社巴格达四日电　伊拉克外交部次长努里·贾米勒四日在这里会见了中国驻伊拉克大使馆临时代办张维平，转达了伊拉克总统阿里夫对中国人民伟大领袖毛主席、中国政府和人民支持阿拉伯人民的斗争的谢意。
    贾米勒说，阿里夫总统感谢中国政府和人民在阿拉伯人民同以色列及其后台帝国主义的争端中对阿拉伯人民的支持。总统赞扬这种支持阿拉伯人民争取自由和消除帝国主义影响的斗争的崇高立场。
    努里·贾米勒还祝愿中国人民强盛、繁荣和幸福。
    新华社五日讯　伊拉克代理外交部长阿布杜勒·加尼·拉维一日写信给中国外交部长陈毅，感谢中国政府在阿拉伯国家遭受美国和以色列侵略威胁的时刻，所采取的支持阿拉伯人民正义事业的崇高立场。
    拉维在信中说：“我所指的特别是贵国最近的支持阿拉伯人对其领海的无可争议的权利的立场。这一光荣立场仅仅是贵国在维护各国人民权利的事业中，在反对一切形式的殖民主义的斗争中和在揭露帝国主义反对阿拉伯国家的阴谋方面所持的一系列光荣态度之一。”
    拉维在信中赞扬中国人民一贯坚决地站在阿拉伯人民一边，是阿拉伯人民反对帝国主义的正义斗争的忠实战友。
