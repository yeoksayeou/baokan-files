Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-06-25
Edition: 5

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo today met with the leader of the Japanese Scientific Instruments Exhibition Team, Hao Yuan Ding Si, and other responsible members of the exhibition team. He had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人

1967-06-25
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人
    新华社二十四日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若今天接见了日本科学仪器展览团团长萩原定司以及展览团的其他负责人，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
