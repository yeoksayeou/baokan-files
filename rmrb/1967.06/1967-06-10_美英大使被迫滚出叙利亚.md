Title: U.S. and British Ambassadors Forced Out of Syria
Author:
Date: 1967-06-10
Edition: 5

	U.S. and British Ambassadors Forced Out of Syria

	According to Xinhua News Agency in Damascus, on the 8th, the U.S. and British imperialist ambassadors and embassy staff in Syria, under the supervision of Syrian armed police, slunk away from Syrian territory. The flags of U.S. and British imperialism were also lowered from Syrian soil.

	The U.S. and British imperialists instigated and supported Israel's sudden aggression against Arab countries on the 5th. The next day, Syria immediately announced the severance of diplomatic relations with the United States and Britain.

### 美英大使被迫滚出叙利亚

1967-06-10
第5版()
专栏：

    美英大使被迫滚出叙利亚
    据新华社大马士革八日电　美、英帝国主义驻叙利亚的大使和大使馆人员，八日在叙利亚武装警察的监视下，灰溜溜地离开了叙利亚国境，美、英帝国主义的国旗也从叙利亚的领土上降了下来。
    美、英帝国主义策动和支持以色列在五日悍然向阿拉伯国家发动突然侵略，第二天叙利亚立即宣布同美国和英国断绝外交关系。
