Title: Vietnamese Embassy and the Delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam Hold a Reception and Film Show
Author:
Date: 1967-06-23
Edition: 5

Vietnamese Embassy and the Delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam Hold a Reception and Film Show

New China News Agency, June 22nd: The Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China and the Permanent Delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam in China held a reception and film show today. The event celebrated the great victories of the military and people of South Vietnam in the winter and spring of 1966-1967, the downing of 2,000 American aggressor aircraft by the military and people of North Vietnam, and the successful detonation of China's first hydrogen bomb.

Attendees included relevant officials from China and representatives of the proletarian revolutionary faction.

During the reception, a documentary film reflecting the Vietnamese people's heroic struggle against American aggression for national salvation was screened.

### 越南使馆和南越民族解放阵线代表团  举行酒会和电影招待会

1967-06-23
第5版()
专栏：

    越南使馆和南越民族解放阵线代表团
    举行酒会和电影招待会
    新华社二十二日讯  越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆和越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团，今天举行酒会和电影招待会，庆祝越南南方军民一九六六年到一九六七年冬春季大捷和越南北方军民击落美国强盗飞机二千架的胜利，同时庆祝中国第一颗氢弹爆炸成功。
    出席招待会的，有我有关方面负责人和无产阶级革命派代表。
    招待会上，放映了反映越南人民抗美救国英勇斗争的纪录影片。
