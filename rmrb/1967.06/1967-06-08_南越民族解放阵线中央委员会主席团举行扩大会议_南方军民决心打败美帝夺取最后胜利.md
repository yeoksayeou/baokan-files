Title: Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam Holds Enlarged Meeting
Author:
Date: 1967-06-08
Edition: 4

The Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam held an enlarged meeting in mid-May. The meeting reviewed the victories achieved by the military and people of South Vietnam from the winter of 1966 to the spring of 1967 (from October 1966 to April 1967) in various fields. The meeting reaffirmed the determination of the military and people of South Vietnam to carry the struggle against American imperialism to the end and achieve final victory.

The Chairman of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, Nguyen Huu Tho, presided over the meeting. The meeting heard reports on the military achievements of the Liberation Army in countering the enemy's "pacification" plan, the political struggle of the South Vietnamese people, the work of disintegrating the puppet army, the consolidation and expansion of the liberated areas, and the movement of the urban people against the U.S.-puppet clique.

The meeting's communiqué, after quoting the brilliant achievements of the military and people of South Vietnam in this dry season, emphasized that in this dry season, the U.S. aggressors did not achieve any of their goals in launching a second "offensive."

The communiqué pointed out the great significance of the winter-spring victories and emphasized the rapid growth of the main forces and local forces of the Liberation Army, especially the rapid growth of the guerrilla forces.

The communiqué also stressed the enthusiastic support given to the people of South Vietnam by the people of North Vietnam and the people of the world.

The communiqué stated, "We are determined to carry the struggle against American imperialism to the end and achieve final victory. Our people have the determination and strength to accomplish this task. We believe that we have already won and will win even greater victories."

### 南越民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团举行扩大会议  南方军民决心打败美帝夺取最后胜利

1967-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

    南越民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团举行扩大会议
    南方军民决心打败美帝夺取最后胜利
    据新华社河内五日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团五月中旬举行扩大会议，回顾了越南南方军民在一九六六年冬季到一九六七年春季（从一九六六年十月到一九六七年四月）取得的各方面的胜利，并且重申，越南南方军民决心把抗美救国斗争进行到最后胜利，打败美帝国主义。
    越南南方民族解放阵线主席团主席阮友寿主持了这次会议。会议听取了关于解放军反击敌人的“绥靖”计划所取得的军事成就，南越人民的政治斗争和瓦解伪军工作，巩固和扩大解放区以及城市人民反对美伪集团的运动的报告。
    会议公报在引述了越南南方军民在这个旱季中所取得的辉煌战果之后强调指出，在这个旱季里，美国侵略者没有达到他们发动第二次“反攻”的任何目标。
    公报指出了冬—春季胜利的巨大意义，并且强调指出解放军主力部队和地方部队的迅速成长，特别是游击队的迅速成长。
    公报还着重指出了越南北方人民和世界人民给予越南南方人民的热情支援。
    公报指出，我们决心把抗美救国斗争进行到最后胜利，打败美帝国主义。我国人民有决心和力量来完成这项任务，我们相信，我们已取得了胜利，并将取得更大的胜利。
