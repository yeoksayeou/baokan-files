Title: Leaders of the Japanese Scientific Instruments Exhibition Delegation Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-06-07
Edition: 4

The chairman of the Japan External Trade Organization, Shibahara Sadao, and the deputy leader of the Japanese Scientific Instruments Exhibition Delegation, Shimura Yoshihiro, arrived in Beijing by plane this evening.

### 日本科学仪器展览团领导人到京

1967-06-07
第4版()
专栏：

    日本科学仪器展览团领导人到京
    新华社六日讯　日本国际贸易促进协会常务理事、日本科学仪器展览团团长萩原定司和副团长志村义博，今晚乘飞机到达北京。
