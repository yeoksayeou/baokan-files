	Brief News

	Japan-China Friendship Association (Orthodox) Kyoto Prefecture Headquarters Established
	Japanese friends in Kyoto held a grand meeting on the 18th, amidst the cheers for the successful explosion of China's first hydrogen bomb, and announced the formal establishment of the Japan-China Friendship Association (Orthodox) Kyoto Prefecture Headquarters. The preparatory committee head, Kondo Ryozou, emphasized at the founding meeting that the guiding ideology of the struggle of the Japan-China Friendship Association (Orthodox) Kyoto Headquarters is the thought of Chairman Mao Zedong—the great teacher, great leader, great helmsman, and great commander of the Chinese and world peoples.

	Zimbabwe African National Liberation Army Sabotages Railway
	A special unit of the Zimbabwe African National Liberation Army blew up a section of the railway 8 miles from Que Que on June 15, causing a freight train loaded with tobacco and other goods bound for South Africa to derail. Another unit of the Zimbabwe African National Liberation Army burned the maize stored in three large farms owned by colonizers near Karoi.

	Mizo Tribal Armed Forces Severely Punish Reactionary Officials
	According to a revelation by the Indian government on the 18th, Mizo tribal armed forces secretly entered the city of Aizawl in Assam on the night of the 16th, capturing five officials of the reactionary government and seven members of the county council, two of whom were government officials who were beaten to death. The "security forces" of India hurriedly dispatched troops to "encircle and suppress" the Mizo tribal armed forces. The Mizo tribal armed forces bravely counterattacked and, with swift action, took the captured reactionary officials into the dark forest, leaving the "security forces" at a loss.

	UAR Bans Screening of American and British Films
	UAR Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Culture Sawwat Okasha ordered on the 17th that American and British films be banned from screening in the UAR. The Franklin Publishing House in Cairo, USA, is also supervised by Egyptian writing and publishing organizations.

	All Members of a Branch of the Pakistan-Soviet Friendship Association Resign
	Due to the Soviet revisionists' capitulation to U.S. imperialism and betrayal of the Arab people, all members of the executive body and all members of the Pakistan-Soviet Friendship Association in Tobadak Singh District, West Pakistan, announced their withdrawal from the association. The chairman, secretary-general, vice-chairman, joint secretary, and treasurer of the Pakistan-Soviet Friendship Association in Tobadak Singh District issued a joint statement: "Due to the role played by the Soviet Union in the Middle East, which is against the interests of the Arab people and a betrayal of the Arab people, anyone who is upright will not continue to maintain relations with the policies of the Soviet Union and maintain friendship with the Soviet Union." (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 简明新闻

专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    日中友协（正统）京都府总部成立
    京都的日本朋友，十八日举行盛大集会，在庆祝中国第一颗氢弹爆炸成功的欢呼声中，宣布正式成立日中友好协会（正统）京都府总部。筹备会负责人近藤良男在成立大会上强调指出，日中友好协会（正统）京都总部斗争的指导思想，是中国和世界人民的伟大的导师、伟大的领袖、伟大的统帅、伟大的舵手毛主席的思想——毛泽东思想。
    津巴布韦民族解放军破坏铁路
    津巴布韦非洲民族解放军的一支特别部队六月十五日在距离奎奎八英里的地方炸毁了一段铁路，使一列开往南非的装载烟草和其他物资的货车出轨。津巴布韦非洲民族解放军的另一支部队焚烧了殖民者在卡罗伊附近的三个大农场里储存的玉米。
    印米佐部族武装严惩反动官吏
    据印度政府十八日透露，米佐部族武装力量十六日夜间悄悄地进入阿萨姆邦的艾贾尔城，抓到反动政府的五个官员和县议会的七个议员，其中两个政府官员被打死。印度的“治安部队”慌忙四处出动，开枪射击，企图“围剿”米佐部族武装。米佐部族武装力量英勇反击，并以神速行动带着抓到的反动官员进入黑暗的密林之中，使“治安部队”束手无策。
    阿联禁止上映美英两国的电影
    阿联副总理兼文化部长萨瓦特·奥卡沙十七日下令，禁止美国和英国电影在阿联上映。
    美国在开罗的富兰克林出版社也由埃及写作和出版团体进行监督。
    巴苏友协一分会全体会员退会
    由于苏修屈从美帝、叛卖阿拉伯人民，西巴基斯坦莱雅普尔县托巴德辛格大区的巴基斯坦—苏联友好协会执行机构全体成员和全体会员宣布退出这个协会。托巴德辛格大区的巴基斯坦—苏联友好协会的主席、秘书长、副主席、联合秘书和司库发表联合声明说：“由于苏联在中东扮演的角色，违背阿拉伯人民的利益，是对阿拉伯人民的背叛，因此，凡是正直的人都不会继续同苏联的政策保持关系，同苏联保持友谊。”（据新华社讯）
