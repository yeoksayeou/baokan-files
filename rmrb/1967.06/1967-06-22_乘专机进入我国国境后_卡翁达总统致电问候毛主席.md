Title: Upon Entering Chinese Territory by Special Plane, President Kaunda Cables Greetings to Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1967-06-22
Edition: 1

Upon entering our country's territory by special plane, President Kaunda of Zambia sent a cable to greet our most, most respected great leader Chairman Mao. The full text of the cable is as follows:

Beijing, Chairman Mao Zedong:

When I flew over your great country to make my first visit to the People's Republic of China, I extend my heartfelt, fraternal greetings to you and all the Chinese people, and I sincerely wish the People's Republic of China success in its future development. I am very much looking forward to this visit, which marks an important milestone in the development of relations between our two countries. I look forward to meeting you and your comrades.

Kaunda

June 21, 1967

### 乘专机进入我国国境后  卡翁达总统致电问候毛主席

1967-06-22
第1版()
专栏：

    乘专机进入我国国境后
    卡翁达总统致电问候毛主席
    新华社二十一日讯　我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席，今天收到赞比亚共和国总统肯尼思·戴维·卡翁达在乘专机进入我国国境以后发来的电报。电报全文如下：北京，毛泽东主席：
    当我飞抵你们伟大的国家上空，来对中华人民共和国进行首次访问的时候，我向您和全体中国人民致以衷心的、兄弟般的问候，并谨祝中华人民共和国在今后的发展中获得成功。我十分盼望进行这次访问，它在我们两国关系的发展中标志着一个很重要的里程碑。我期待着会见您和您的同志们。
                              卡翁达
                    一九六七年六月二十一日
