Title: Learn from the People's Liberation Army
Author: Běijīng Yóupiào Chǎng Gémìng Zàofǎn Zǒngbù
Date: 1967-06-18
Edition: 3

Chairman Mao teaches us: "People's social existence determines their thoughts. Once the correct ideas representing the advanced class are grasped by the masses, they become a material force for transforming society and the world." The successful detonation of China's first hydrogen bomb is the result of the People's Liberation Army, workers, and national defense engineering and technical personnel studying and applying Chairman Mao's works. They have turned the high revolutionary enthusiasm released in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution into a tremendous material force, achieving the success of the hydrogen bomb explosion in a short period, and bringing China's national defense science into a new stage. All these vividly prove that Mao Zedong Thought has tremendous power and is the highest authority guiding the entire nation, the Party, and the army.

We are determined to respond to Chairman Mao's great call: Learn from the People's Liberation Army. We are determined to launch a new wave of mass study and application of Chairman Mao's works. At present, we must concentrate our efforts on studying Chairman Mao's great classic work "On the Correct Handling of Contradictions Among the People," to smash selfishness and establish public spirit, to promote the thought revolution of the worker ranks, to grasp the revolution, to promote production, to further improve revolutionary unity and the revolutionary three-in-one combination, to unite the majority of the masses, to form a mighty army of the cultural revolution, to raise the level of the great criticism, to overthrow and expose the Party's leading capitalist-roader, to thoroughly eliminate their poison, to make great Mao Zedong Thought prevail throughout China and the world!

### 向人民解放军学习
北京邮票厂革命造反总部
1967-06-18
第3版()
专栏：

    向人民解放军学习
    北京邮票厂革命造反总部
    毛主席教导我们：“人们的社会存在，决定人们的思想。而代表先进阶级的正确思想，一旦被群众掌握，就会变成改造社会、改造世界的物质力量。”我国第一颗氢弹爆炸成功，是解放军、工人和国防工程技术人员活学活用毛主席著作的结果。他们把在无产阶级文化大革命中迸发出来的高度革命热情，变成了巨大的物质力量，在很短的时间内取得氢弹爆炸的成功，使我国的国防科学进入了一个崭新的阶段。这些都雄辩地证明了毛泽东思想具有无比强大的威力，是统帅全国、全党、全军的最高权威。
    我们决心响应毛主席的伟大号召：向解放军学习。我们决心掀起一个群众性的活学活用毛主席著作的新高潮。在当前，就是要集中精力学好毛主席的《关于正确处理人民内部矛盾的问题》这篇伟大的经典著作，大破私字，大立公字，促进职工队伍的思想革命化，狠抓革命，猛促生产，进一步搞好革命的大联合和革命的三结合，团结群众的大多数，组成浩浩荡荡的文化革命大军，把大批判提到更新的水平，把党内最大的走资本主义道路的当权派斗倒斗臭，彻底肃清他们的流毒，让伟大的毛泽东思想红遍全中国，红遍全世界！
