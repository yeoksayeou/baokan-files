Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-06-27
Edition: 6

Premier Zhou Enlai received Japanese trade union activist and Vice Chairman of the Japan Metal and Machinery Workers' Union, Takano Minoru, this evening and had a cordial and friendly conversation with him.

Present at the reception were Tang Zhang and Wang Xiaoyun.

Japanese friend Nishisonoji Koichi, who is in Beijing, was also present.

Premier Zhou Enlai received the leader of the Japanese Scientific Instruments Exhibition Team, Hagiwara Sadashi, and other leaders of the exhibition team this evening and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Zhang Ben and Liu Xifeng, among others.

Japanese friend Nishisonoji Koichi, who is in Beijing, was also present.

### 周恩来总理接见日本客人

1967-06-27
第6版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见日本客人
    新华社二十六日讯　周恩来总理今晚接见日本工会活动家、日本五金机械工会副委员长高野实，同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有唐章、王晓云。
    在北京的日本朋友西园寺公一也在座。
    新华社二十六日讯　周恩来总理今晚接见日本科学仪器展览团团长萩原定司以及展览团的其他负责人，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有张本、刘希文等。
    在北京的日本朋友西园寺公一也在座。
