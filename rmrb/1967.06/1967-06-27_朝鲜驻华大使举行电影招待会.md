Title: North Korean Ambassador Holds Film Screening
Author:
Date: 1967-06-27
Edition: 6

	North Korean Ambassador Holds Film Screening

	Xinhua News Agency, June 26th: To condemn American imperialism's invasion of Korea seventeen years ago, the Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, Hyun Jun-geuk, held a film screening today.

	Relevant officials from China were invited to attend the screening.

### 朝鲜驻华大使举行电影招待会

1967-06-27
第6版()
专栏：

    朝鲜驻华大使举行电影招待会
    新华社二十六日讯　为谴责美帝国主义在十七年前发动侵朝战争，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使玄峻极今天举行了电影招待会。
    我有关方面负责人应邀出席了招待会。
