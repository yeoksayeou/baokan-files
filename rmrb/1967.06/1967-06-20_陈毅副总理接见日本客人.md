Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-06-20
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi received the full delegation of the Japan-China Friendship Association (Orthodox) Youth Delegation led by Eno Motofu on the 16th and had a cordial conversation with them.

### 陈毅副总理接见日本客人

1967-06-20
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见日本客人
    新华社讯　陈毅副总理十六日接见了由榎本庸夫率领的日本日中友协（正统）青年代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切的谈话。
