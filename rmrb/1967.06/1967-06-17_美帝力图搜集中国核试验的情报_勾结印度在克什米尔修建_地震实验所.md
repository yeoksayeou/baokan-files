Title: The U.S. Empire Seeks to Gather Intelligence on China's Nuclear Tests
Author:
Date: 1967-06-17
Edition: 6

The U.S. imperialists, who are so frightened by China's nuclear tests that they can't sleep, are using every possible means to gather intelligence on China's nuclear tests. Recently, they have colluded with the Indian reactionaries to build a so-called "largest earthquake observatory in Asia" in the Indian-occupied area of Kashmir to detect China's nuclear tests. According to Indian officials, this "observatory" will use the latest U.S. equipment, capable of recording distant and low-intensity earthquakes. It is currently under construction near Hari Parbat Fort in Srinagar, the capital of the Indian-occupied Kashmir region. This place is very close to China's western regions.

### 美帝力图搜集中国核试验的情报  勾结印度在克什米尔修建“地震实验所”

1967-06-17
第6版()
专栏：

    美帝力图搜集中国核试验的情报
    勾结印度在克什米尔修建“地震实验所”
    据新华社十六日讯　新德里消息：对中国的核试验害怕得睡不着觉的美帝国主义者，用各种各样办法来搞中国核试验的情报，最近又伙同印度反动派在克什米尔印占区修建了一个所谓“亚洲最大的地震实验所”，来探测中国的核试验。据印度官方人士透露，这个“实验所”将使用美国最新式的装置，能够记录远距离的、低强度的地震。它正在印占克什米尔地区首府斯利那加附近的哈里帕巴特堡修建中。这个地方离中国的西部地区很近。
