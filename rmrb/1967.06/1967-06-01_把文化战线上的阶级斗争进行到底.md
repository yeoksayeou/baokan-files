Title: Carry the Class Struggle on the Cultural Front to the End
Author: Fǎncí
Date: 1967-06-01
Edition: 5

The Chinese Cultural Revolution of the proletariat has greatly strengthened the dictatorship of the proletariat and, following the example of the Paris Commune, has promoted extensive mass democracy. It is not enough to have the socialization of industry and agriculture. Without a thorough socialist cultural revolution to eradicate the roots of bourgeois ideology, the dictatorship of the proletariat cannot be consolidated. It is foolish to believe that after the proletariat seizes power, antagonistic contradictions no longer exist. The example of the counter-revolutionary rebellion in Hungary (such as the Petőfi Club) proves this to us. Therefore, class struggle in various forms, especially on the cultural front, must be carried out resolutely. Good always develops in the struggle against evil. Struggle is life. If we separate ourselves from class struggle and continuous revolution, we cannot continue to develop and will ultimately retreat to capitalism. To win in the class struggle, we must rely on the great thought of Mao Zedong. The revolutionary left can use this great thought to thoroughly smash all traitors and bad elements.

West Germany, Franz

### 把文化战线上的阶级斗争进行到底
弗朗茨
1967-06-01
第5版()
专栏：国外来信

    把文化战线上的阶级斗争进行到底
    中国无产阶级文化大革命，大大加强了无产阶级专政，并按照巴黎公社的榜样促进广泛的群众民主。单单有工农业社会主义化还不够。不进行一次深刻的社会主义文化革命以便连根铲除资产阶级的思想，无产阶级专政就不能巩固。如果相信无产阶级夺取政权以后就不再存在对抗性矛盾，那将是愚蠢的。匈牙利的反革命叛乱的例子
  （例如裴多菲俱乐部）向我们证明了这一点。因此，多方面的阶级斗争，尤其是文化战线上的阶级斗争必须坚决进行，好的总是在同恶的斗争中发展起来的。斗争就是生活。如果我们脱离了阶级斗争和不断革命，我们就不能继续发展下去，最后必然倒退到资本主义去。为了能在阶级斗争中取胜，我们必须依靠伟大的毛泽东思想。革命的左派能够利用这种伟大的思想彻底粉碎一切叛徒和坏人。
                                      西德　弗朗茨
