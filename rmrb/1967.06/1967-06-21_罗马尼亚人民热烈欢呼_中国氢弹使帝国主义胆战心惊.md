Title: Romanian People Warmly Celebrate China's Hydrogen Bomb, Making Imperialists Tremble

Author:

Date: 1967-06-21

Edition: 4

Romanian people were overjoyed upon hearing the news of China's successful detonation of its first hydrogen bomb. Many people expressed their warm congratulations to Chinese comrades in Bucharest.

A young woman said, "I greatly admire the great achievement of the Chinese people. This is the result of studying Chairman Mao Zedong's works."

A worker joyfully said, "The successful detonation of China's first hydrogen bomb has made imperialists tremble and restless day and night."

A university student said, "The speed at which China is developing nuclear weapons is astonishing. This hydrogen bomb explosion shows that China's scientific development has reached an extremely high level."

An official from a central government agency firmly shook hands with a Chinese comrade and said, "I extend my warmest and most sincere congratulations to the Chinese people. This hydrogen bomb explosion is a great achievement of the Chinese people. It is very necessary for you to conduct hydrogen bomb explosions to defend peace."

### 罗马尼亚人民热烈欢呼  中国氢弹使帝国主义胆战心惊

1967-06-21
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚人民热烈欢呼
    中国氢弹使帝国主义胆战心惊
    新华社布加勒斯特电　罗马尼亚人民听到中国第一颗氢弹爆炸成功的消息后，欢欣鼓舞。许多人纷纷向在这里的中国同志表示热烈祝贺。
    一位女青年说，“我非常敬佩中国人民取得的这一伟大成就。这是中国人民学习毛泽东主席著作的结果。”
    一位工人兴高采烈地说，“中国第一颗氢弹爆炸成功，使帝国主义者胆战心惊，日夜不安。”
    一位大学生说，“中国发展核武器的速度是惊人的。这次氢弹爆炸，说明中国科学的发展已经达到极高的水平。”
    一位中央机关的工作人员紧紧地握住中国同志的手说，“向中国人民表示最热烈、最诚挚的祝贺。这次氢弹爆炸是中国人民的伟大成就。你们为了保卫和平，进行氢弹爆炸是十分必要的。”
