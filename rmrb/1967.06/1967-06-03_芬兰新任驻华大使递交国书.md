Title: Finland's New Ambassador to China Presents Credentials
Author:
Date: 1967-06-03
Edition: 6

	Finland's new ambassador to the People's Republic of China, Vanri Heilaenius, presented his credentials to Vice Chairman Song Qingling this afternoon, accompanied by diplomatic staff from the embassy.

	After presenting his credentials, Vice Chairman Song Qingling had a friendly conversation with Ambassador Heilaenius. Present were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo and others.

### 芬兰新任驻华大使递交国书

1967-06-03
第6版()
专栏：

    芬兰新任驻华大使递交国书
    新华社二日讯　芬兰共和国新任驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使凡利·海莱尼乌斯，今天下午偕同大使馆外交人员，向中华人民共和国副主席宋庆龄递交国书。
    递交国书后，宋庆龄副主席同海莱尼乌斯大使进行了友好的谈话。在场的有外交部副部长罗贵波等。
