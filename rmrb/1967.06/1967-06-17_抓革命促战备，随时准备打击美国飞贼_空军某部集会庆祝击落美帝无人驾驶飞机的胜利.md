Title: Seize the Revolution, Promote War Preparedness, and Stand Ready to Strike Down American Bandits
Author:
Date: 1967-06-17
Edition: 3

An air force unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army held a grand victory celebration meeting today in a certain place in Guangxi to celebrate the significant victory of shooting down an American imperialist military unmanned reconnaissance aircraft by our air force unit on June 12. The meeting commended the units and personnel who performed meritoriously in the battle. The participating officers and soldiers enthusiastically cheered: This is a new victory of the invincible Mao Zedong Thought. They excitedly said: A thousand victories, ten thousand victories, all victories are due to the red sun in our hearts, our most beloved and respected great leader Chairman Mao.

Participants in the victory celebration meeting included Wang Huijiu, a responsible person of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force, Long Daquan, a responsible person of the Guangzhou Military Region Air Force, and representatives from local revolutionary mass organizations and militia, totaling more than 700 people.

At the meeting, Wang Huijiu read the commendation order from the Central Military Commission of the Communist Party of China. Representatives from various sides read congratulatory telegrams and letters. Wang Huijiu, Long Daquan, and other comrades presented Mao Zedong's works, Mao Zedong badges, and meritorious awards to the units and personnel who performed meritoriously in the battle.

Wang Huijiu spoke at the meeting, enthusiastically congratulating the participating units and soldiers on their new victory of upholding the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought to defend the country's airspace and the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. Wang Huijiu encouraged the participating units and soldiers to resolutely implement Chairman Mao's great directive of seizing the revolution and promoting war preparedness, summarize battle experiences, closely monitor the war provocations of American imperialism and its running dog, the Chiang Kai-shek bandits, strengthen war preparedness, and achieve new merits in defending the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line.

A representative from the local revolutionary mass organizations and a representative from the meritorious battle unit, Liu Guangcai, also spoke at the meeting. Liu Guangcai said, We must always remember Chairman Mao's teachings, seize the revolution, promote war preparedness, and be ready at any time to give the invading enemy a fatal blow!

### 抓革命促战备，随时准备打击美国飞贼  空军某部集会庆祝击落美帝无人驾驶飞机的胜利

1967-06-17
第3版()
专栏：

    抓革命促战备，随时准备打击美国飞贼
    空军某部集会庆祝击落美帝无人驾驶飞机的胜利
    据新华社南宁十六日电  中国人民解放军空军某部，今天下午在广西某地隆重举行祝捷庆功大会，庆祝六月十二日我空军部队击落美帝国主义军用无人驾驶侦察机的重大胜利，表扬作战有功单位和人员。参加大会的指战员，热烈欢呼：这是战无不胜的毛泽东思想的新胜利。他们激动地说：千胜利，万胜利，胜利归功于我们心中最红最红的红太阳、我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席。
    参加祝捷庆功大会的有中国人民解放军空军负责人王辉球，广州部队空军负责人龙道权以及当地革命群众组织代表、民兵代表等七百多人。
    会上，王辉球同志宣读了中共中央军委的嘉奖令，各方面代表宣读了贺电、贺信。王辉球、龙道权等同志将毛主席著作、毛主席像章和立功奖状授给作战有功单位和人员。
    王辉球同志在大会上讲了话，热烈祝贺参战部队指战员高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，为保卫祖国领空和保卫无产阶级文化大革命所取得的新胜利。王辉球勉励参战部队指战员坚决贯彻毛主席抓革命，促战备的伟大指示，总结战斗经验，密切注视美帝国主义及其走狗蒋介石匪帮的战争挑衅活动，加强战备，为保卫无产阶级文化大革命，保卫毛主席的无产阶级革命路线立新功。
    当地革命群众组织代表和作战有功部队代表刘光才也在会上讲了话。刘光才说，我们一定牢记毛主席的教导，抓革命，促战备，随时准备给予来犯的敌人以致命的打击！
