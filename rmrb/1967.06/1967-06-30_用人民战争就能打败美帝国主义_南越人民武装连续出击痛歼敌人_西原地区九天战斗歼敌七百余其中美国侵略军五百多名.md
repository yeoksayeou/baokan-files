Title: People's War Can Defeat American Imperialism
Author:
Date: 1967-06-30
Edition: 6

	The armed forces of the people of South Vietnam have launched continuous attacks, inflicting heavy losses on the enemy. In the Western Highlands region, over a period of nine days, the military and civilian population annihilated more than 700 enemy troops, including over 500 American aggressors, destroyed 20 cannons, two helicopters, 42 armored vehicles, and other military vehicles.

	According to the Liberation Radio of South Vietnam, broadcast by the New China News Agency in Hanoi on the 28th, the liberation armed forces in the Western Highlands region of South Vietnam launched continuous attacks on many enemy strongholds from the 10th to the 18th. According to incomplete statistics, in these nine days, the military and civilian population in the Western Highlands region annihilated more than 700 enemy troops, including over 500 American aggressors, destroyed 20 cannons, two helicopters, 42 armored vehicles, and other military vehicles.

	On the night of the 9th and the early morning of the 10th, the liberation armed forces in Gia Lai Province attacked six strongholds of the U.S. 4th Infantry Division and the "pacification" cadre training center in Pleiku, annihilating 415 American aggressors and 70 "pacification" cadres, destroying 38 military vehicles, one ammunition depot, and hundreds of barracks. On the same night, the liberation armed forces also attacked another enemy base, annihilating 46 enemy troops and destroying two military vehicles.

	On the night of the 14th, the liberation armed forces launched a fierce attack on the base of the 1st Battalion of the 10th Regiment of the U.S. 4th Infantry Division. The shells accurately hit the center of the enemy base, killing and wounding 110 American aggressors, destroying two helicopters, two M-113 armored vehicles, and 14 tents. On the same night, the liberation armed forces in Kontum Province attacked the enemy base in Le Khe, annihilating 102 enemy troops.

	The liberation armed forces in Kontum Province, riding on the momentum of victory, launched a fierce artillery bombardment on the base of the 42nd Regiment of the puppet army in New Pleiku on the night of the 16th and the early morning of the 17th. The enemy's airfield suffered severe damage, a large number of tents were burned, and many ammunition depots were hit and caught fire, with explosions echoing throughout the region until 9 a.m. the next day. The liberation armed forces destroyed 18 155mm and 105mm cannons. On the night of the 17th, the liberation armed forces bombarded the enemy base in New Pleiku again, inflicting heavy losses on the enemy.

	On the night of the 18th, the liberation armed forces in Gia Lai Province bombarded the enemy base in the northern part of Dak To, killing and wounding many enemy troops and destroying two 105mm cannons.

### 用人民战争就能打败美帝国主义  南越人民武装连续出击痛歼敌人  西原地区九天战斗歼敌七百余其中美国侵略军五百多名

1967-06-30
第6版()
专栏：

    用人民战争就能打败美帝国主义
    南越人民武装连续出击痛歼敌人
    西原地区九天战斗歼敌七百余其中美国侵略军五百多名
    新华社河内二十八日电　据越南南方解放电台广播：越南南方西原地区解放武装力量，在十日到十八日的九天里，向美伪军的许多据点连续发动袭击。据不完全统计，在这九天里，西原地区军民歼敌七百多名，其中包括美国侵略军五百多名，击毁大炮二十门、直升飞机两架、装甲车和其他军车四十二辆。
    九日夜里和十日凌晨，嘉莱省解放武装力量袭击波来古美国第四步兵师和伪“绥靖干部”训练中心的六个据点，消灭美国侵略军四百一十五名和“绥靖干部”七十名，击毁军车三十八辆、弹药库一座和营房成百间。当天夜里解放武装力量还袭击了敌军的另一驻地，消灭伪军四十六名，击毁军车两辆。
    十四日夜间，解放武装力量又猛烈袭击美国第四步兵师第十团第一营驻地。炮弹准确地击中敌军驻地中心区，共打死打伤美国侵略军一百一十名，击毁直升飞机两架、M—113型装甲车两辆和帐篷十四座。当天夜里，昆嵩省解放武装力量袭击黎格蓝敌军驻地，消灭敌人一百零二名。
    昆嵩省解放武装力量乘胜前进，在十六日夜里和十七日凌晨猛烈炮轰新景伪军第四十二团驻地，敌军机场遭到严重破坏，大批帐篷被烧毁，许多弹药库中弹起火，爆炸声响彻整个地区，直到第二天上午九时止。解放武装力量击毁一五五和一零五毫米大炮十八门。十七日夜里，解放武装力量再次炮轰新景敌军驻地，使敌人受到严重损失。
    十八日夜里，嘉莱省解放武装力量炮轰德基北部敌军驻地，打死打伤许多敌人，击毁一零五毫米大炮两门。
