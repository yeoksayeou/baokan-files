Exhibition of Soviet Propaganda Posters and Caricatures

    For the Happiness of the People! by Ivanov, 1950
    The Soviet Propaganda Poster and Caricature Exhibition, sponsored by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the All-China Association of Fine Arts Workers, and the Central Academy of Fine Arts, opened on April 3rd in the auditorium of the Central Academy of Fine Arts in Beijing.
    The audience at the exhibition particularly favored propaganda posters with the theme of praising leaders.
    Peace Will Surely Triumph! by Koletsky, 1950
    Text on the picture: Kuibyshev Hydroelectric Station, Stalingrad Hydroelectric Station, Turkmen Main Canal, Kakhovka Hydroelectric Station.
    Long Live the Friendship Between the Peoples of China and the Soviet Union! by Ivanov, 1949
    Answer the Instigators of New Wars by Completing 200 Percent of the Plan. by Koletsky, 1950
    The Marshall Plan in Action. by Kukryniksy, 1949
    Take Good Care of Young Livestock. by Yelanova, 1950
    We Once Defended Leningrad, Now We Will Restore It. by Serov, 1950
    A Decisive Battle to Defend the Motherland! by Rukov, 1943
    Chiang Kai-shek at His Last Post. by Yefimov, 1950
    Landscape Painting of Anglo-American Cooperation. by Ganf, 1949
    Collective Farmers, Build Ponds! by Moxin, 1949
