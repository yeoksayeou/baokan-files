Title: Ho Chi Minh Issues Letter to the People, Calling for a Patriotic Emulation Campaign to Begin on May 1st
Author:
Date: 1951-04-27
Page: 4

　　Ho Chi Minh Issues Letter to the People
　  Calling for a Patriotic Emulation Campaign to Begin on May 1st
    [Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, 26th] Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, 25th: Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, recently issued a letter to the people of Vietnam on the occasion of the upcoming May Day.

    The letter to the people points out: This year's May Day is a day for the Vietnamese people to celebrate the Vietnamese Labor Party. The Vietnamese people will unite more closely to carry out the war of resistance and have more confidence in victory.

    The letter to the people calls on all Vietnamese people to launch a new patriotic emulation movement starting from May 1st. The aim is to annihilate more enemies, increase industrial and agricultural production, and mobilize all the people to participate more actively in the war of resistance, in order to expand the military and political victories gained at the beginning of this year.

    The letter to the people also calls on the Vietnamese people to dedicate themselves to the cause of safeguarding world peace.

### 胡志明发表告人民书  号召自五一起展开爱国竞赛

1951-04-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　胡志明发表告人民书
　  号召自五一起展开爱国竞赛
    【新华社二十六日讯】越南通讯社北越二十五日讯：越南民主共和国主席胡志明，顷就即将到来的“五一”节发表告越南全国人民书。
    告人民书指出：今年“五一”节是越南人民祝贺越南劳动党的日子。越南人民今后将更紧密地团结起来进行抗战，并对胜利更有信心。
    告人民书号召越南全体人民从“五一”开始，展开新的爱国竞赛运动，目的是歼灭更多的敌人，提高工业和农业的生产，发动全体人民更积极地参加抗战工作，以扩大今年年初所获得的军事上和政治上的胜利。
    告人民书并号召越南人民致力于维护世界和平的事业。
