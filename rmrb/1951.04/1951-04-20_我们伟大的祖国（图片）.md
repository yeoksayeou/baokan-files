　　Our Great Motherland (Picture)
　　Window decorations are a popular folk art in our country. They are patterns cut or carved from various colored papers by working women or craftsmen, depicting various things in people's lives. This is a stall selling window decorations in Zhuolu Chengguan, Chahar, where young women are selecting and buying their favorite patterns.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　我们伟大的祖国（图片）
    窗花是我国民间普遍流行的艺术品。它是劳动妇女或手艺人，用各色纸张，剪成或刀刻的图案画，表现着人民生活中的各种事物。这是察哈尔涿鹿城关卖窗花的小摊，青年妇女们正在这里选买她们所喜爱的花样。
