Title: Peak Coal Mine Labor Model Congress Sends a Telegram to Chairman Mao, Guaranteeing the Creation of a "Safe Production Season"
Author:
Date: 1951-04-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 8th:

　　The Labor Model Congress of the state-owned Fengfeng Coal Mine sent a telegram to Chairman Mao on March 28th, guaranteeing the creation of a "Safe Production Season" in the second quarter of this year. The telegram first reported to Chairman Mao that the mine had completed and exceeded its 1950 production tasks, and recently created a new national record of 254 tons of coal mined using pneumatic picks. The telegram then stated that in the future, they would further unite and motivate all employees, continue to carry out the Resist America, Aid Korea movement, welcome the "May Day" Labor Day, and guarantee the achievement of full safe production for the entire second quarter. The labor models present at the meeting stated that they would create more new records in the patriotic labor emulation, especially to create collective labor models, and use practical actions to oppose the United States' arming of Japan and defend world peace.

### 峰峰煤矿劳动模范大会致电毛主席　保证创造“安全生产季”

1951-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　峰峰煤矿劳动模范大会致电毛主席
  　保证创造“安全生产季”
    【新华社八日讯】国营峰峰煤矿劳动模范大会于三月二十八日致电毛主席，保证要在今年第二季创造“安全生产季”。该电首先向毛主席报告了该矿完成并超过一九五○年的生产任务，并于最近创造风镐采煤二百五十四吨的全国新纪录的情况。该电继即提出，今后要进一步团结和带动全体职工，继续展开抗美援朝运动，迎接“五一”劳动节，保证要在今年第二季达到全季安全生产的目的。到会的劳动模范表示：要在爱国主义劳动竞赛中创造出更多的新纪录，特别要创造集体的劳动模范，用实际行动反对美国武装日本，保卫世界和平。
