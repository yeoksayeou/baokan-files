Title: Upholding the Conclusion of a Peace Treaty, Opposing the United States Arming Japan: Jiusan Society Members in Beijing Gather and Sign

Author:
Date: 1951-04-24
Page: 3

　　Upholding the Conclusion of a Peace Treaty, Opposing the United States Arming Japan
　  Jiusan Society Members in Beijing Gather and Sign
　　Jiusan Society members in Beijing held a meeting on April 22nd to sign in support of concluding a peace treaty and opposing the arming of Japan, and held a forum to welcome "May Fourth." Society members Xu Deheng, Meng Xianzhang, Huang Guozhang, Xue Yu, Yan Jici, Yang Zhongjian, Li Jinxi, Yu Pingbo, and others attended. Xu Deheng, chairman of the society, reported on the significance of the meeting, calling on all members to respond to the resolution of the World Peace Council with resolute action, broadly unite people from the cultural, educational, and scientific fields, to support the conclusion of a peace treaty among the five major powers, oppose the U.S. imperialists' separate peace treaty with Japan and the arming of Japan, advocate the signing of a comprehensive peace treaty with Japan, and strive to promote the glorious tradition of science and democracy of the "May Fourth" Movement. Then, a signing ceremony was held to support the conclusion of a peace treaty and on the Japan issue. All members present unanimously signed and mobilized relatives, friends, and colleagues of each member to sign. In their speeches, everyone unanimously believed that to commemorate "May Fourth" today, it is necessary to carry forward the revolutionary spirit of "May Fourth" against imperialism and feudalism, promote the glorious tradition of democracy and science of "May Fourth," broadly unite people from the national cultural, educational, and scientific fields around the Chinese Communist Party, and under the leadership of Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought, strive to complete the arduous tasks of anti-imperialism, anti-feudalism, and anti-bureaucratic capitalism, and build a new democratic China.

### 拥护缔结和平公约，反对美国武装日本  九三学社在京社员集会签名

1951-04-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　拥护缔结和平公约，反对美国武装日本
　  九三学社在京社员集会签名
    九三学社在北京社员于四月二十二日举行集会，签名拥护缔结和平公约及反对武装日本，并举行座谈迎接“五四”。到社员许德珩、孟宪章、黄国璋、薛愚、严济慈、杨钟健、黎锦熙、俞平伯等。该社主席许德珩报告开会意义，号召全体社员用坚决的行动来响应世界和平理事会的决议，广泛地团结文教科学界的人士，为拥护缔结五大国和平公约，反对美帝国主义单独对日媾和及武装日本，主张签定全面的对日和约，并为发扬“五四”科学与民主的光荣传统而奋斗。继举行拥护缔结和平公约及关于日本问题的签名。全体到会社员一致签名，并发动每一社员的亲戚、朋友和同事们签名。在发言中，大家一致认为今天纪念“五四”必须发挥“五四”反帝反封建的革命精神，发扬“五四”民主与科学的光荣传统，广泛地团结全国文教科学界的人士在中国共产党的周围，在马克思列宁主义和毛泽东思想领导之下，为完成反帝、反封建、反官僚资本的艰巨任务，建设新民主主义的新中国而奋斗。
