Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-04-07
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Now is the most suitable season for tree planting. The people in the suburbs of Beijing are planting trees on a large scale. This is a group of Communist Youth League members from Xiaohuangzhuang planting elm trees on the hillside. This village planted a total of 2,800 trees this year.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    现在是植树的最适宜的季节。北京市郊区人民正大量进行植树。这是小黄庄的几个青年团员在山坡上种植榆树。该村今年共植树二千八百棵。
