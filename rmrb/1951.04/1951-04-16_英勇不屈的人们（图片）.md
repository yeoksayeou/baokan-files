Title: Indomitable People (Picture)
Author: Yan Han
Date: 1951-04-16
Page: 3

    Indomitable People
    Painting by Yan Han
    In 1942, the Japanese invaders carried out a large-scale "mopping-up" operation against the Anti-Japanese Democratic Base Area of ​​Central Hebei, and deliberately breached dikes to flood our Anti-Japanese Democratic Base Area. Under the leadership of the Communist Party and the democratic government, the people of Central Hebei waged a heroic struggle against the enemy. This oil painting depicts the scene of cadres and militiamen in the Baiyangdian area of ​​Central Hebei rescuing the affected people.

### 英勇不屈的人们（图片）
彦涵
1951-04-16
第3版()
专栏：

    英勇不屈的人们
    彦涵作
    一九四二年日寇对冀中抗日民主根据地进行大“扫荡”，并决堤放水，企图淹没我抗日民主根据地。冀中人民在共产党与民主政府领导下，向敌人进行英勇的斗争。这幅油画是描绘冀中白洋淀地区的干部及民兵援救受难人民的情形。
