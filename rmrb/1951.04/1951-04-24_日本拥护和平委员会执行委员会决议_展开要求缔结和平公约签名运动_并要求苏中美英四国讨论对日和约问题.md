Page: 4
　
　　Resolution of the Executive Committee of the Japan Peace Supporters' Committee
　  Launch a Signature Campaign to Demand the Conclusion of a Peace Pact
　  And Demand that the Soviet Union, China, the United States, and the United Kingdom Discuss the Japanese Peace Treaty Issue
    [Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd dispatch] TASS, April 22nd dispatch: United Press reported that the meeting of the Executive Committee of the Japan Peace Supporters' Committee has resolved to implement a number of measures to defend peace. The resolution specifically stipulates the launch of a broad movement to collect signatures for the World Peace Council's declaration demanding the conclusion of a peace pact by the five major powers. The launch of this movement must be closely integrated with the following movements: strive for the conclusion of a comprehensive peace treaty and oppose the arming of Japan; more actively organize local peace committees and hold more peace defense conferences; prepare for a representative conference of cultural circles in order to establish contact with cultural circles in other countries through the World Peace Council; demand that the governments of the Soviet Union, China, the United States, and the United Kingdom convene a four-nation conference on the Japanese peace treaty issue.

### 日本拥护和平委员会执行委员会决议  展开要求缔结和平公约签名运动  并要求苏中美英四国讨论对日和约问题

1951-04-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　日本拥护和平委员会执行委员会决议
　  展开要求缔结和平公约签名运动
　  并要求苏中美英四国讨论对日和约问题
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社二十二日讯：联合通讯社消息，日本拥护和平委员会执行委员会会议已决议实行若干保卫和平的措施。该决议特别规定要展开一个广泛的运动，为世界和平理事会要求缔结五大国和平公约的宣言征集签名。这一运动的展开必须与下列运动密切结合：争取缔结全面和约、反对武装日本；更加积极地组织地方和平委员会，举行更多的保卫和平会议；筹备文化界代表会议，以便经由世界和平理事会与其他各国文化界建立联系；要求苏、中、美、英四国政府就对日和约问题召开四国会议。
