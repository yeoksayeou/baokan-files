Title: All-China Students' Federation, Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and All-China Youth Federation Host Banquet for IUS Executive Committee

Author:

Date: 1951-04-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 25th: The All-China Students' Federation, the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and the All-China Youth Federation jointly hosted a banquet this evening in Beijing for IUS President Gromann, members of the Executive Committee, student representatives from various countries attending this session of the Executive Committee, and staff. Xie Bangding, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, Jiang Nanxiang, Deputy Secretary, and Qian Junrui and Sha Qianli, Vice Chairmen of the All-China Youth Federation, attended the reception. Cai Chang, Zhu Xuefan, Sakov, and Zhao Zhongchi were also present. During the banquet, hosts and guests repeatedly toasted to the great unity of students, youth, and people all over the world, to the great victory of the Chinese and Korean people, and to the cause of world peace.

### 全国学联、团中央及全国青联欢宴国际学联执委

1951-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国学联、团中央及全国青联欢宴国际学联执委
    【新华社二十五日讯】中华全国学生联合会、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会和中华全国民主青年联合总会今晚联合欢宴来北京出席国际学生联合会执行委员会会议的国际学生联合会主席格罗曼、执行委员会委员及参加本届执委会的各国学生代表和工作人员。中华全国学生联合会主席谢邦定、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬、副书记蒋南翔和中华全国民主青年联合总会副主席钱俊瑞、沙千里出席招待，蔡畅、朱学范、沙可夫和赵仲池等作陪。宴席上宾主一再为全世界学生、青年和人民的大团结，为中朝人民的伟大胜利和全世界的和平事业干杯。
