Title: Lao Resistance Government Rally Commemorates "Lao People's Hatred Day" Defense Minister Calls on Armed Forces to Strive to Kill the Enemy
Author:
Date: 1951-04-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 20th. According to Vietnam News Agency on the 17th: The Lao Resistance Government held a memorial meeting on March 21st, "Lao People's Hatred Day," to commemorate the massacre of thousands of Lao and Vietnamese residents by French colonialists in Thakhek (a city on the Mekong River in central Laos) on March 21, 1946. This anniversary was decided at a meeting of the Lao National Assembly last August.
    Those attending the memorial meeting included Lao Prime Minister Souphanouvong, government ministers, representatives of the Lao National United Front, and representatives of the Vietnamese and Cambodian national united fronts. Souphanouvong called on the Lao people and army to unite and fight against aggression, and to cooperate with the Vietnamese and Cambodian people to jointly defeat French colonialists and American interventionists. Xin Kaibo, vice chairman of the Lao National United Front, called on all Lao people to unite more closely within the Lao National United Front to jointly support the government.
    Lao Defense Minister Kaysone also issued an order calling on the Lao armed forces to launch a competition to kill more enemy troops to avenge the Lao people.

### 老挝抗战政府集会  纪念“老挝人民仇恨日”  国防部长号召武装部队努力杀敌

1951-04-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　老挝抗战政府集会
　  纪念“老挝人民仇恨日”
　  国防部长号召武装部队努力杀敌
    【新华社二十日讯】据越南通讯社十七日讯：老挝抗战政府于三月二十一日“老挝人民仇恨日”举行纪念会，纪念一九四六年三月二十一日法国殖民者在塔克（老挝中部湄公河边的城市）屠杀数千老挝和越南的居民。这一个纪念日是老挝国民议会于去年八月举行的会议上决定的。
    参加纪念会的有老挝总理苏发奴冯、政府各部部长、老挝民族统一阵线代表，以及越南和柬埔寨的民族统一阵线代表。苏发奴冯号召老挝人民和军队团结起来，进行反对侵略的斗争，并与越南、柬埔寨人民合作，共同击败法国殖民主义者和美国干涉者。老挝民族统一阵线副主席辛开坡号召老挝全体人民更密切地团结在老挝民族统一阵线内，共同支持政府。
    老挝国防部长凯森并发布命令，号召老挝的武装部队展开竞赛，杀死更多的敌军，为老挝人民复仇。
