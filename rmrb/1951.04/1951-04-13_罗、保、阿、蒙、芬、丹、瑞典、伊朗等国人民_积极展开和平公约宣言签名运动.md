Title: People of Romania, Bulgaria, Albania, Mongolia, Finland, Denmark, Sweden, Iran, and Other Countries Actively Launch Signature Campaign for Peace Pact Declaration
Author:
Date: 1951-04-13
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 12th dispatch: People of Romania, Bulgaria, Albania, Mongolia, Finland, Denmark, Sweden, and Iran are actively preparing for and launching signature campaigns for the Peace Pact Declaration.
    Romania began collecting signatures on the 10th, and more than 200,000 workers signed on the first day. Workers at the Resita Metallurgical United Plant overfulfilled their production plan by 2,721 tons of pig iron on this day to celebrate the start of the signature campaign. The Romanian General Confederation of Labor, as well as youth, women's, cultural, and religious groups, have issued statements calling on the people to unanimously support the signature campaign.
    Bulgaria is scheduled to begin collecting signatures on the 16th. Peace conferences have been held in cities, villages, factories, agricultural cooperatives, and schools across the country, and special committees have been elected to be responsible for collecting signatures and establishing new peace committees. Many famous Bulgarian poets and writers have published articles in newspapers, widely publicizing the World Peace Council's declaration and urging the people to unanimously sign the declaration.
    Albania is scheduled to launch the signature campaign on the 12th. Leaders of the Albanian Muslim people and leaders of religious groups have issued statements calling on believers to unanimously support the signature campaign.
    The Mongolian National Committee for the Defense of Peace has decided to collect signatures from April 20th to May 20th.
    Finnish peace fighters are scheduled to launch the signature campaign on the 22nd, with a target of collecting one million signatures. Publicity work for the signature campaign is being vigorously carried out, and people's organizations such as youth and women will hold competitions.
    The Danish Peace Committee held a meeting on the 9th to celebrate the start of the signature campaign for the Peace Pact Declaration. Members of the Danish Communist Youth League collected 2,928 signatures in the capital, Copenhagen, on the first day of the signature campaign.
    The signature campaign in Sweden is progressing victoriously. In one residential area in the western district of Stockholm alone, 166 out of 186 households signed the declaration.
    In Iran, as of the 9th, 151,000 people had signed the Peace Pact Declaration. A peace conference of 15,000 people was held in Babol to discuss the World Peace Council's declaration.

### 罗、保、阿、蒙、芬、丹、瑞典、伊朗等国人民  积极展开和平公约宣言签名运动

1951-04-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　罗、保、阿、蒙、芬、丹、瑞典、伊朗等国人民
    积极展开和平公约宣言签名运动
    【新华社十二日讯】罗、保、阿尔巴尼亚、蒙古、芬兰、丹麦、瑞典和伊朗等国人民积极筹备并展开和平公约宣言的签名运动。
    罗马尼亚于十日开始征集签名，第一天已有二十万以上的工人签了名。雷希卡冶金联合厂的工人在这一天超计划生产了二千七百二十一吨铣铁，以庆祝签名运动的开始。罗马尼亚总工会和青年、妇女、文化及宗教团体均已纷纷发表声明，号召人民一致支持签名运动。
    保加利亚定于十六日开始征集签名。全国各城市、乡村、工厂、农业合作社和学校都举行了和平大会，选出了特别委员会负责人征集签名和成立新的和平委员会。保加利亚许多著名的诗人和作家在报纸上发表文章，广泛宣传世界和平理事会的宣言，并敦促人民一致在宣言上签名。
    阿尔巴尼亚已定于十二日展开签名运动。阿尔巴尼亚回教人民的领袖和宗教团体的领袖均发表声明，号召教徒一致支持签名运动。
    蒙古拥护和平全国委员会已决定在四月二十日至五月二十日征集签名。
    芬兰的和平战士定于二十二日展开签名运动，征集签名的目标为一百万人。签名运动的宣传工作正在大力进行着，青年妇女等人民团体将举行竞赛。
    丹麦和平委员会于九日举行集会，庆祝和平公约宣言签名运动的开始。丹麦共产主义青年团团员在签名运动的第一天，就在首都哥本哈根征集了二千九百二十八个签名。
    瑞典的签名运动正胜利进行着，仅在首都斯德哥尔摩西区的一个住宅区，一百八十六户居民中就有一百六十六户在宣言上签了名。
    伊朗截至九日为止，已有十五万一千人在和平公约宣言上签了名。为了讨论世界和平理事会的宣言，巴布尔曾举行了一万五千人的和平大会。
