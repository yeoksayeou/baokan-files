Title: Conference on Handling Christian Groups Receiving American Subsidies Concludes; Adoption of Handling Measures and Joint Declaration; Representatives Unanimously Agree to Organize the "Chinese Christian Resist-America Aid-Korea Three-Self Patriotic Movement Committee"
Author:
Date: 1951-04-22
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, April 21st—The conference on handling Christian groups receiving American subsidies, convened by the Religious Affairs Department of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council, concluded on the 21st after lasting six days.

　　This conference marks a closer unity of Christian figures around the People's Government under the banner of patriotism, a resolute severing of all ties with American imperialism, and a thorough eradication of the influence of American imperialist cultural aggression. The conference discussed and adopted the "Measures for Handling Christian Groups Receiving American Subsidies," which will be submitted to the Government Administration Council for approval and implementation. The conference also adopted a joint declaration by representatives of various Christian churches and groups across China.

　　In order to enable Christians throughout the country to participate extensively in the Resist-America Aid-Korea Movement and promote the Three-Self Patriotic Reform Movement, the representatives of various Christian churches and groups unanimously agreed to organize the "Chinese Christian Resist-America Aid-Korea Three-Self Patriotic Movement Committee." Wu Yaozong, Chen Jianzhen, Chen Chonggui, Tu Yuqing, Deng Yuzhi, Liu Liangmo, Cui Xianxiang, Shen Derong, Xie Yongqin, Luo Guanzong, Shi Ruzhang, Li Chuwen, Zheng Jianye, Zhao Fusan, Wang Zizhong, Han Wenzao, Zhao Zichen, Xiao Guogui, Yu Yun, Shao Jingsan, Jing Dianying, Xiong Zhenpei, Wei Zhuomin, Wu Yifang, and Li Shoupao were selected to form a preparatory committee. Wu Yaozong was elected as chairman, and Chen Jianzhen, Chen Chonggui, Tu Yuqing, and Deng Yuzhi as vice-chairmen. The conference authorized the committee to announce its official establishment upon completion of the preparatory work.

　　All the representatives present presented a banner to Chairman Mao with infinite respect, which was accepted by Guo Moruo, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council, on behalf of Chairman Mao. The conference also passed a telegram of respect to Chairman Mao and a telegram of respect to the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean People's Army.

　　The conference concluded after a closing speech by Wu Yaozong.

### 处理接受美国津贴的基督教团体会议闭幕  通过处理办法及联合宣言  代表们一致同意组织“中国基督教抗美援朝三自革新运动委员会”

1951-04-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　处理接受美国津贴的基督教团体会议闭幕
　  通过处理办法及联合宣言
　  代表们一致同意组织“中国基督教抗美援朝三自革新运动委员会”
    【新华社二十一日讯】中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会宗教事务处召集的处理接受美国津贴的基督教团体会议，历时六天，已于二十一日闭幕。
    这次会议标志着基督教人士在爱国主义的旗帜下，更密切地团结在人民政府的周围，坚决斩断与美帝国主义的一切关系，彻底肃清美帝国主义文化侵略的影响。会议讨论并通过了“对于接受美国津贴的基督教团体处理办法”，该办法将呈请政务院批准后实施。会议又通过了全中国基督教各教会各团体代表联合宣言。
    为着使全国各地基督教徒普遍参加抗美援朝运动和促进自立革新运动，到会的基督教各教会各团体代表一致同意组织“中国基督教抗美援朝三自革新运动委员会”，推出吴耀宗、陈见真、陈崇桂、涂羽卿、邓裕志、刘良模、崔宪详、沈德溶、谢永钦、罗冠宗、施如璋、李储文、郑建业、赵复三、王梓仲、韩文藻、赵紫宸、萧国贵、俞筠、邵镜三、敬奠瀛、熊真沛、韦卓民、吴贻芳、李寿葆等二十五人先成立筹备委员会，并推吴耀宗为主席，陈见真、陈崇桂、涂羽卿、邓裕志为副主席。大会授权该委员会在筹备工作完成后即宣告正式成立。
    到会全体代表怀着无限的敬意向毛主席献呈锦旗，由政务院副总理郭沫若代表接受。大会并通过了向毛主席致敬电及向中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军致敬电。
    会议由吴耀宗致闭幕词后结束。
