Title: International Student Union Executive Committee Arrives in Beijing Yesterday; Our Student Union Chairman Delivers Speech at Airport Expressing Warm Welcome; Gromann Praises Our Country's Students' Struggle to Defend Peace
Author:
Date: 1951-04-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th: The International Student Union (ISU) President Gromann, members of the Executive Committee, student representatives from various countries attending the ISU Executive Committee meeting, and staff members, a total of thirty-six people, accompanied by Ke Zaishuo, representative of the All-China Students' Federation to the ISU, arrived in Beijing by plane at 5:30 PM today.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included: Yang Cheng, Vice President of the International Student Union; Xie Bangding, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation; Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League; Jiang Nanxiang, Deputy Secretary; representatives from the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government, the China People's Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea General Association, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; representatives from the Beijing Students' Federation, the Beijing Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, and the Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Democratic Youth Federation; and over 500 students from universities and middle schools in Beijing. Also present to welcome them were Park Chol-bok and other Korean student representatives and Nguyen Cang and other Vietnamese representatives who had arrived in Beijing earlier to attend the ISU Executive Committee meeting.

After the representatives from various countries disembarked, a welcoming ceremony was held at the airport. Xie Bangding, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, delivered a welcome speech, expressing a warm welcome. He said: We Chinese students have always resolutely supported the International Student Union, the great unity of democratic students around the world, and the cause of world peace.

We believe that this meeting of the ISU Executive Committee will further strengthen the ties between our Chinese students and the students of Eastern countries and the ISU, and will more powerfully advance the struggle of Eastern countries' students and democratic students around the world against aggression and in defense of peace.

International Student Union President Gromann said in his reply: Students around the world have been watching the heroic struggle of Chinese students and the Chinese people against the Kuomintang rule and American intervention with high praise and deep concern and sympathy. We are very pleased to know that Chinese students are actively fighting with us to defend peace. We also know that the solid friendship between the people, youth, and students of China and the Soviet Union is an important guarantee of world peace. He concluded by wishing the Chinese people more success under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong. The welcoming ceremony concluded with the band playing the World Democratic Youth Anthem.

### 国际学联执委昨天到达北京  我学联主席在机场致词表示热烈欢迎  格罗曼盛赞我国学生保卫和平的斗争

1951-04-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　国际学联执委昨天到达北京
    我学联主席在机场致词表示热烈欢迎
    格罗曼盛赞我国学生保卫和平的斗争
    【新华社二十四日讯】来北京出席国际学生联合会执行委员会会议的国际学生联合会主席格罗曼、执行委员会委员、列席会议的各国学生代表及工作人员等一行三十六人，由中华全国学生联合会驻国际学联代表柯在铄陪同于今日下午五时半飞抵北京。
    赴机场欢迎者有：国际学生联合会副主席杨诚，中华全国学生联合会主席谢邦定，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬，副书记蒋南翔，中央人民政府教育部、中国人民抗美援朝总会、中华全国总工会、中华全国民主妇女联合会和中苏友好协会总会的代表，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，北京市学生联合会、中国新民主主义青年团北京市委员会和北京市民主青年联合会筹备委员会的代表，以及北京市大、中学生等五百余人。前往欢迎者并有日前抵京参加国际学联执委会的朝鲜学生代表朴铁福等及越南代表阮苍等。
    各国代表下机后，欢迎会即在机场举行。中华全国学生联合会主席谢邦定致欢迎词，表示热烈欢迎。他说：我们中国学生从来就坚决拥护国际学联，拥护世界民主学生的大团结，拥护世界和平事业。
    我们相信：国际学联执委会这次会议，一定会使我们中国学生和东方国家学生与国际学联的联系更加密切，使东方国家学生和世界民主学生反对侵略、保卫和平的斗争，更加有力地向前发展。
    国际学生联合会主席格罗曼致答词称：全世界的学生一直怀着高度的赞扬和深切的关怀与同情，注视着中国学生和中国人民一道进行反对国民党统治及美国干涉的英勇斗争。我们很高兴地知道，中国学生与我们一同为保卫和平而积极斗争。我们也知道，中苏两国人民、青年及学生之间的巩固友谊，是世界和平的重要保证。他最后祝中国人民在毛泽东主席的领导下得到更多的成功。欢迎会最后在乐队高奏世界民主青年进行曲声中结束。
