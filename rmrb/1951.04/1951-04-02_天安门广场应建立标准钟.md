Title: A Standard Clock Should Be Built in Tiananmen Square
Author: 
Date: 1951-04-02
Page: 2

Column: Reader's Letter

    A Standard Clock Should Be Built in Tiananmen Square
    To the Editor:
        I suggest that a standard clock be built in Tiananmen Square, with the clock tower serving as the time standard for Beijing. Tiananmen is a place for mass gatherings in the capital, one of the national emblem designs of the People's Republic of China, a place loved by 475 million people, and a place longed for by peace-loving people all over the world. It is necessary to build a clock tower here. I hope the relevant authorities will consider it.
                                            A Reader from Beijing

### 天安门广场应建立标准钟

1951-04-02
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    天安门广场应建立标准钟编辑同志：
    我建议在天安门广场建立一座标准钟，标准钟的钟楼，作为北京市的对时标准。天安门是首都群众性集会的场合，是中华人民共和国的国徽图案之一，是四亿七千五百万人民所热爱的地方，也是全世界爱好和平的人民所向往的地方。在这里修盖一座钟楼是必要的。希望有关机关考虑。
                                            北京市一读者
