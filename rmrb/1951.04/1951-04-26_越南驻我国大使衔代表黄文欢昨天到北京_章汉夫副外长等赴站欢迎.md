Title: Vietnamese Ambassador-Designate to Our Country, Hoang Van Hoan, Arrived in Beijing Yesterday; Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu and Others Welcomed Him at the Station
Author: 
Date: 1951-04-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 25th: Hoang Van Hoan, Ambassador-Designate of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the People's Republic of China, arrived in Beijing by train at 6:40 AM today. Those who went to the station to welcome him included Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Chen Jiakang, Acting Director of the Asian Department; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department; Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; Tang Yanjie, Chief of Staff of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command; Soviet Ambassador to China, Roshchin; Korean Ambassador to China, Li Zhouyuan; Romanian Ambassador to China, Rudenko; Polish Ambassador to China, Burgin; Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic to China, Koenig; Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Petkov; Counselor of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, Hasek; Counselor of the Mongolian Embassy in China, Labdan; Counselor of the Hungarian Embassy in China, Fekete; Third Secretary of the Indian Embassy in China, Kumar; Third Secretary of the Burmese Embassy in China, Charlie Piroo; and Attaché of the Indonesian Embassy in China, Yusuf, and others.

### 越南驻我国大使衔代表黄文欢昨天到北京  章汉夫副外长等赴站欢迎

1951-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　越南驻我国大使衔代表黄文欢昨天到北京
　  章汉夫副外长等赴站欢迎
    【新华社二十五日讯】越南民主共和国驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使衔代表黄文欢（Hoang Van Hoan）于今日上午六时四十分乘车抵达北京。赴站欢迎者有我中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫、亚洲司代司长陈家康、交际处处长王倬如、北京市人民政府副市长吴晗、京津卫戍司令部参谋长唐延杰、苏联驻华大使罗申、朝鲜驻华大使李周渊、罗马尼亚驻华大使鲁登科、波兰驻华大使布尔金、德意志民主共和国驻华外交使团团长柯尼希、保加利亚驻华大使彼得科夫、捷克驻华大使馆参赞哈谢克、蒙古驻华大使馆参赞拉布丹、匈牙利驻华大使馆参赞费凯尔特、印度驻华大使馆三等秘书古玛尔、缅甸驻华大使馆三等秘书查理辟鲁、及印度尼西亚驻华大使馆随员尤索夫等。
