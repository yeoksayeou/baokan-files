Title: Beijing Science Community Responds to Call of Resist America, Aid Korea General Association; Signature and Voting Rally to be Held Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1951-04-21
Page: 1

　　Beijing Science Community Responds to Call of Resist America, Aid Korea General Association; Signature and Voting Rally to be Held Tomorrow
    [Our Newspaper] In response to the call of the Resist America, Aid Korea General Association, the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies, the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization, and the Joint Office of the Chinese Social Science Research Associations will hold a "Beijing Science Community Rally to Support the Conclusion of a Peace Pact and Oppose U.S. Imperialism Arming Japan Signature and Voting Rally" at 2:30 pm on the 22nd of this month. The rally will mobilize natural science workers and social science workers in Beijing to participate in signing their names to support the conclusion of a peace pact by the five major powers, voting against U.S. imperialism's separate peace treaty with Japan, opposing U.S. imperialism's arming of Japan, and passing a declaration to Resist America and Aid Korea. The rally is jointly organized by the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies, the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization, and the Joint Office of the National Social Science Research Associations. The main agenda of the meeting includes a report by Vice Premier Guo Moruo, a signature by all to support the conclusion of a peace pact by the five major powers, voting against U.S. imperialism's separate peace treaty with Japan, opposing U.S. imperialism's arming of Japan, and the adoption of a declaration of resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea.

### 北京科学界响应抗美援朝总会号召  明日举行签名投票大会

1951-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京科学界响应抗美援朝总会号召
    明日举行签名投票大会
    【本报讯】中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会、全国科学技术普及协会，及中国社会科学各研究会联合办事处为响应抗美援朝总会的号召，将于本月二十二日下午二时半举行“北京科学界拥护缔结和平公约，反对美帝国主义武装日本签名投票大会”，发动北京市自然科学工作者与社会科学工作者参加签名投票。大会主要的议程有郭沫若副总理报告，全体签名拥护五大国缔结和平公约，投票反对美帝国主义单独对日媾和，反对美帝国主义武装日本及通过抗美援朝的宣言。此次大会，由中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会、全国科学技术普及协会及全国社会科学研究会联合办事处共同筹备。
