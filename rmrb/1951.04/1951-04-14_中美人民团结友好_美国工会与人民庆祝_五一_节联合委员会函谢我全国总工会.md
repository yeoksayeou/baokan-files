Title: Sino-American People's Solidarity and Friendship: U.S. Labor Unions and People's Joint Committee for Celebrating May Day Expresses Thanks to All-China Federation of Trade Unions

Author:

Date: 1951-04-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 12th: The New York Labor Unions and People's Joint Committee for Celebrating May Day in the United States sent a letter to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions on March 27th, expressing their gratitude for the All-China Federation of Trade Unions' congratulatory telegram to the committee's May Day preparatory meeting on March 23rd. The reply is as follows:

Dear Comrades,

On behalf of the five hundred representatives of labor unions, mass organizations, fraternal societies, and cultural groups who attended the May Day preparatory meeting on March 25th, please accept our thanks!

Your inspiring congratulatory telegram to the meeting demonstrates the solidarity and friendship between the American and Chinese peoples. The peace-loving forces in the United States are fighting against a third world war, demanding an end to the Korean War, and striving for peace and genuine democracy. We hope that the May Day demonstration in New York will be a further contribution. The representatives at the preparatory meeting enthusiastically called on the people of New York City to participate in the May Day demonstration, calling on them to advance together with the workers of America and the workers of the world who are at the forefront of the struggle for peace.

We have just received news from Washington that the Supreme Court has refused to hear the appeal of Willie McGee, an innocent black man sentenced to death in the electric chair. This action by the Supreme Court undoubtedly allows the lynchers in the South to murder innocent black people with impunity in a legal manner. Progressive forces are doing everything possible to mobilize the American people to stop this legal lynching. All support from overseas will have a great effect, and we still have time to save the life of this innocent man.

With comradely greetings!

          Executive Secretary
              Louis Vance
        March 27, 1951

### 中美人民团结友好  美国工会与人民庆祝“五一”节联合委员会函谢我全国总工会

1951-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　中美人民团结友好
    美国工会与人民庆祝“五一”节联合委员会函谢我全国总工会
    【新华社十二日讯】美国纽约工会与人民庆祝“五一”节联合委员会接到中华全国总工会三月二十三日致该委员会“五一”节筹备大会的贺电后，于三月二十七日函复中华全国总工会表示谢意。复信如下：亲爱的同志们：
    谨代表三月二十五日出席五一节筹备大会的各工会、群众组织、兄弟会和文化团体的五百名代表们向你们致谢意！
    你们致大会的令人鼓舞的贺电表现了美国人民与中国人民之间的团结友好。美国爱好和平的力量正在为反对第三次世界大战，要求结束朝鲜战争，并为争取和平与真正的民主而斗争着。我们希望五一节在纽约举行的大示威将是更进一步的贡献。出席筹备大会的代表们热烈地号召纽约城的人民参加五一节的示威游行，号召他们和美国的工人以及在争取和平的斗争中站在最前线的全世界工人一齐前进。
    我们刚接到华盛顿传来的消息，说最高法院拒绝审理被判决在电椅上处死的无辜黑人——威利·麦基的上诉案。最高法院这种行为无疑就是准许南部的私刑者以合法的方式肆无忌惮地谋杀无辜的黑人。进步力量正在竭尽全力动员着美国人民起来制止这种合法的私刑。从海外寄来的一切支援都会产生极大的作用，现在我们仍旧来得及拯救这个无辜者的性命。谨致同志的敬礼！
          执行秘书
              路易·万斯道
        一九五一年三月二十七日
