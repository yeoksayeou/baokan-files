Title: Establishment of the North China Working Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League
Author:
Date: 1951-04-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, dated 19th: A joint conference of the secretaries of the Communist Youth League committees of the five provinces and two municipalities (Beijing, Tianjin) in North China was held in Beijing from April 1st to 8th. The North China Working Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League was officially established at the meeting. The Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League appointed Xu Shiping, Li Chao, Jiang Yi, Xiao Ping, and Yang Bozheng, among others, fifteen people as members, with Xu Shiping and Li Chao as Secretary and Deputy Secretary respectively. The meeting decided on the guiding principles and tasks for the work of the North China Youth League in 1951: to further popularize and deepen patriotic education on resisting America and aiding Korea among the vast number of League members and youth; to establish and improve the League's educational system in a planned way; to strengthen the training and education of League cadres; to improve the League's work style and leadership style; to assist the Party in establishing a propaganda network in a planned way; to closely connect with the broad masses of young people in all aspects, and to strive to consolidate the League's organization, enhance the League's combat effectiveness, and assist the Party and the People's Government in realizing the six major tasks of North China this year.

### 中国新民主主义青年团  华北工作委员会成立

1951-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国新民主主义青年团
    华北工作委员会成立
    【新华社十九日讯】中国新民主主义青年团华北五省、二市（北京、天津）的团委书记联席会议，于四月一日至八日在北京举行。会上正式成立了中国新民主主义青年团华北工作委员会。中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会派许世平、李超、蒋毅、肖平、杨伯箴等十五人为委员，并以许世平、李超为正副书记。会议决定了一九五一年华北青年团的工作方针与任务：要求进一步普及和深入对广大团员和青年的抗美援朝爱国主义教育；有计划地建立与健全团的教育制度；加强对团的干部的训练和教育；改进团的工作作风与领导作风；有计划地协助党建立宣传网；从各方面密切与广大青年群众的联系，为巩固团的组织，提高团的战斗力，协助党和人民政府实现今年华北六大任务而奋斗。
