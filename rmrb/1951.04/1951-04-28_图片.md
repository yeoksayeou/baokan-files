Author:
Date: 1951-04-28

    (Above) On the afternoon of April 7th, 35,000 people from all walks of life in Wuchang City gathered to denounce the bloody crimes of special agent and local bully Zou Haishan, habitual bandit Yuan Haishan, and Han Qinbang.
    (Below) At the denunciation rally held by all sectors of society in Wuchang City, the Wuhan Military Control Commission publicly announced the death sentence for counter-revolutionaries such as Zou Haishan, to the thunderous cheers of the masses.
