Title: Promoting Sports Activities for Troops and the People Nationwide: People's Liberation Army Holds Ball Games; Competition Tournament to Open Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1951-04-18
Page: 1

　　Promoting Sports Activities for Troops and the People Nationwide
    People's Liberation Army Holds Ball Games
    Competition Tournament to Open Tomorrow
    [This newspaper's dispatch] To welcome the "May Fourth" National Basketball and Volleyball Competition Tournament, and to further promote and advance sports movements within the troops and among the people nationwide, the Chinese People's Liberation Army has decided to hold an army-wide basketball and volleyball competition tournament at the Xian Nong Tan Public Stadium in Beijing from the 19th to the 23rd of this month. Thirteen units are participating in this competition tournament, including various first-level military regions, the Navy and Air Force, various special forces, and directly subordinate organs of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission. During the tournament, the Third Field Army football team will hold a friendly match with the Beijing sports community, and the North China Military Region baseball team will also hold a demonstration. All members of the teams have now arrived in Beijing.

### 提倡部队和全国人民体育活动  人民解放军举行球赛  竞赛大会定于明日揭幕

1951-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　提倡部队和全国人民体育活动
    人民解放军举行球赛
    竞赛大会定于明日揭幕
    【本报讯】中国人民解放军为迎接“五四”全国篮排球竞赛大会，进一步提倡与推动部队和全国人民的体育运动，决定于本月十九日至二十三日在北京先农坛公共体育场举行全军篮排球竞赛大会。参加这次竞赛大会的，有各一级军区、海空军、各特种兵部队暨人民革命军事委员会直属机关等十三个单位。在大会期间，第三野战军足球队将与北京市体育界进行友谊赛，华北军区棒球队亦将举行表演。现在各单位的队员已全部抵达北京。
