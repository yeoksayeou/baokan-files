Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-04-15
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Farmers in Aksu, southern Xinjiang, were overjoyed to see the portrait of their leader sent by the Central Northwest Visiting Group, and introduced it to each other, saying, "Look, this is Chairman Mao, who is leading us to turn over and live a good life."

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-04-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新疆南部阿克苏地方的农民，看到中央西北访问团送给他们的领袖像后，非常欢喜，互相介绍说：“看，这就是领导咱们翻身过好日子的毛主席。”
