Title: People of Czechoslovakia, Poland, Romania, Bulgaria, Finland, Denmark, Iran, and Israel Actively Launch Signature Campaign for Declaration on Peace Pact

Author: 
Date: 1951-04-06
Page: 4

　　People of Czechoslovakia, Poland, Romania, Bulgaria, Finland, Denmark, Iran, and Israel
　  Actively Launch Signature Campaign for Declaration on Peace Pact
    [Xinhua News Agency, 5th] The people of Czechoslovakia, Poland, Romania, Bulgaria, Finland, Denmark, and Iran are actively carrying out peace movements and collecting signatures for the Declaration on the Peace Pact. Czechoslovak peace fighters began holding a "Demonstration Month for Defense of Peace" on the 1st. The working people of Prague, Bohemia, Ostrava, and Malada Boleslav held rallies or demonstrations, resolutely demanding that the five major powers conclude a peace pact. The Polish National Committee for the Defense of Peace recently decided to hold a "National Peace Vote" in mid-May. Peace fighters in the Port of Zdzieszowice have held a grand demonstration. Demonstrators unanimously expressed their determination to complete Poland's six-year economic construction plan ahead of schedule, in response to the declaration and resolutions of the World Peace Council. Romanian peace fighters are scheduled to launch a signature campaign for the Declaration on the Peace Pact on the 10th. Local peace committees have organized tens of thousands of propagandists to promote the Declaration on the Peace Pact to the people. Activists of local peace committees in Bulgaria are widely promoting the declaration of the World Peace Council to the masses. In Blagoevgrad alone, ninety such propaganda meetings have been held. The Bulgarian Writers' Association has established many propaganda teams and dispatched them to factories, agricultural cooperatives, and construction sites to carry out activities.
    The Standing Committee for the Defense of Peace in Finland has decided to launch a signature campaign for the Declaration on the Peace Pact recently. All members of the committee have unanimously signed the declaration. The Danish Peace Committee is currently distributing tens of thousands of signature booklets printed with the Declaration of the World Peace Council, and has issued a letter to the people of the country, calling on the people to actively participate in the signature campaign. In Iran, as of the 1st, more than 100,000 people have signed the Declaration on the Peace Pact.
    [Report from this newspaper] According to a Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Telegraph Agency of the Soviet Union, Tel Aviv dispatch: The Political Bureau of the Israeli Communist Party, at its meeting on March 25, passed a resolution protesting the treasonous policies of the Ben-Gurion government and supporting the decisions made by the First Session of the World Peace Council. The resolution denounced the Ben-Gurion government's treasonous policy of requesting "US aid" and the aggressive nature of US loans, and called for the launch of a broad signature campaign against the remilitarization of West Germany and demanding that the five major powers conclude a peace pact.

### 捷、波、罗、保、芬、丹、伊朗和以色列等国人民  拥护和平公约宣言积极展开签名运动

1951-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　捷、波、罗、保、芬、丹、伊朗和以色列等国人民
　  拥护和平公约宣言积极展开签名运动
    【新华社五日讯】捷、波、罗、保、芬兰、丹麦和伊朗各国的人民积极开展和平运动，并征集和平公约宣言的签名。捷克斯洛伐克的和平战士于一日开始举行“保卫和平示威月”，布拉格、波希米亚、俄斯特拉发和马拉达·布列斯拉夫等地的劳动人民，纷纷集会或举行示威游行，坚决要求五大国缔结和平公约。波兰全国保卫和平委员会最近决定在五月中旬举行“全国和平投票”，兹杰辛港的和平战士已举行了盛大的示威游行。示威者一致表示要提前完成波兰六年经济建设计划，以响应世界和平理事会的宣言和决议。罗马尼亚的和平战士已定于十日展开和平公约宣言的签名运动。各地方和平委员会已组织了成千上万的宣传员，向人民宣传和平公约宣言。保加利亚各地方和平委员会的积极分子，正广泛向群众宣传世界和平理事会的宣言。仅在布拉高耶夫格勒斯基一地，就举行了九十次这样的宣传大会。保加利亚作家协会已成立了许多宣传工作队，分赴各地的工厂、农业合作社和建设场所进行活动。
    芬兰保卫和平常设委员会已决定在最近展开和平公约宣言的签名运动，该会全体委员已一致在宣言上签了名。丹麦和平委员会现正散发数万本印有世界和平理事会宣言的签名册，并发表了告全国人民书，号召全国人民积极参加签名运动。在伊朗，截至一日止已有十万余人在和平公约宣言上签了名。
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：电通社特拉维夫讯：以色列共产党政治局会议，于三月二十五日通过决议，抗议本·古里安政府的卖国政策，并支持世界和平理事会第一届会议所作的决定。决议痛斥本·古里安政府请求“美援”的卖国政策及美国贷款的侵略性质，并号召发动一个反对使西德重新军国主义化和要求五大国缔结和平公约的广泛的签名运动。
