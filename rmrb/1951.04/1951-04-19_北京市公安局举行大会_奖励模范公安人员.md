Title: Beijing Public Security Bureau Holds Conference to Commend Model Police Officers
Author:
Date: 1951-04-19
Page: 1

　　Beijing Public Security Bureau Holds Conference
    to Commend Model Police Officers
    [Our Newspaper's Report] The Beijing Municipal People's Government Public Security Bureau solemnly held the "1950 Performance Appraisal and Award Summary Conference" in the Zhongshan Park Concert Hall on April 18th.
    Vice Director Feng Jiping, chairman of the conference, pointed out in his opening speech: "In the past year, our work has made great improvements and achievements. This is inseparable from the leading and guaranteeing role of our 1,965 model working comrades. Today's meeting is to commend and reward our model workers, in order to further consolidate and promote the existing achievements."
    Luo Ruiqing, also the Director, pointed out in his report summarizing the performance appraisal and awards: "Beijing's public security work has been successful because we have heavily cracked down on counter-revolutionaries, defended the capital, consolidated the revolutionary order, and protected the lives and property of two million people. These achievements are the result of Chairman Mao and the correct leadership of the higher authorities, the support of the broad masses, the efforts of all cadres and people's police, and the leading role of many meritorious models." He called on all comrades to learn from the meritorious models. Then, Director Luo clearly pointed out: "The central task of public security work at present is to suppress counter-revolutionaries. All work should be done to ensure the success of the work of suppressing counter-revolutionaries, so as to eliminate all hidden enemies and thoroughly eliminate counter-revolutionary elements." Finally, Director Luo emphasized: "The people's police must further strengthen ties with the people; only in this way can we consolidate the existing achievements."
    Vice Mayor Zhang Youyu and Xi Congzhen, Deputy Director of the Office of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, encouraged the meritorious models in their speeches and called for continued efforts.
    Amidst military music and warm applause, Director Luo personally presented awards to the meritorious models.
    After the award ceremony, Li Shubin and Liu Baotian, representatives of the meritorious models, delivered their acceptance speeches. They first thanked Chairman Mao, the Communist Party of China, the People's Government, and the leaders of higher authorities; then they made a guarantee to the conference: "We will not be arrogant or complacent, but will humbly learn and do our work even better."

### 北京市公安局举行大会  奖励模范公安人员

1951-04-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京市公安局举行大会
    奖励模范公安人员
    【本报讯】北京市人民政府公安局于四月十八日在中山公园音乐堂隆重地举行了“一九五○年考绩评奖总结大会”。
    大会主席冯基平副局长在致开会词中指出：去年一年中，我们的工作有了很大的改进和成绩，这是和我们一千九百六十五个模范工作同志的带头作用和保证作用分不开的。今天这个会就是要表扬和奖励我们的模范工作者，以求进一步地巩固和发扬已有的成绩。”
    罗瑞卿兼局长报告考绩评奖总结时指出：北京的公安工作是有成绩的，因为我们重重地打击了反革命，捍卫了首都，巩固了革命秩序，保护了二百万人民的生命财产的安全。这些成绩，是毛主席和上级的正确领导、广大群众的支持、全体干部、人民警察的努力，和许多功臣模范的骨干带头作用的结果。他号召全体同志向功臣模范学习。接着，罗兼局长明确地指出：目前公安工作的中心任务，就是镇压反革命。一切工作都应该是为了作好镇压反革命工作，以求铲除一切暗藏的敌人，彻底肃清反革命分子。最后，罗兼局长又着重指出：人民警察必须进一步地加强和人民群众的联系；只有这样，才能巩固已有的成绩。
    张友渔副市长和中国共产党北京市委员会办公室副主任席从真，在讲话中都勉励了功臣模范，并号召继续努力。
    在军乐和热烈的掌声中，罗兼局长亲自给功臣模范发奖。
    发奖仪式毕，功臣模范代表李树彬和刘宝田致答词。他们首先感谢毛主席、中国共产党、人民政府和上级同志的领导；接着向大会提出保证：不骄傲，不自满，虚心学习，把工作做得更好。
