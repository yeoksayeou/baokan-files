Order of the Ministry of Finance of the Central People's Government
    Now, in accordance with the decision of the Government Administration Council, the Measures for the Collection of Unified Sales Tax on Cotton Yarn and the Measures for Supplementary Tax on Cotton Yarn and Cotton Cloth Inventory are hereby formulated and promulgated. It is hoped that all levels of tax authorities and all public and private factories and merchants in various regions will abide by and implement them.
    This order

                                        Minister Bo Yibo
                                        Vice Minister Rong Zihe
                                                Wang Shao'ao
                                            April 1, 1951

### 中央人民政府财政部令

1951-04-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　中央人民政府财政部令
    兹依照政务院决定，制定棉纱统销税征收办法及棉纱棉布存货补税办法，一并公布之。希各级税务机关及各地公私厂商全体遵照执行。
    此令
                                        部长  薄一波
                                        副部长  戎子和
                                                王绍鏊
                                            一九五一年四月一日
