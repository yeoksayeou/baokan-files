Title: World Federation of Trade Unions Delegation Led by Liu Ningyi Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1951-04-26
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, April 25th—The World Federation of Trade Unions (WFTU) delegation headed by Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, including representatives from the WFTU Secretariat and the Chairman of the Pakistan Trade Union, a total of eight people, returned to Beijing on the 23rd after attending the Seventh Session of the United Nations Economic Commission for Asia and the Far East and participating in the Second All-Burma Labor Congress. Liu Ningyi and others were warmly welcomed by workers in Burma, Pakistan, and India.

### 世界工联代表团刘宁一等返抵北京

1951-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界工联代表团刘宁一等返抵北京
    【新华社二十五日讯】以中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一为首的包括世界工联书记处代表和巴基斯坦工会主席的世界工联代表团一行八人，列席了联合国亚洲暨远东经济委员会第七届会议和参加全缅甸第二届劳工代表大会后，已于二十三日返抵北京。刘宁一等曾受到缅甸、巴基斯坦和印度等国工人的热烈欢迎。
