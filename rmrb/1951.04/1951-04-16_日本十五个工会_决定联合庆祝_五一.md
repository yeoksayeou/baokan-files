Title: Fifteen Japanese Trade Unions Decide to Jointly Celebrate "May Day"
Author:
Date: 1951-04-16
Page: 4

　　Japanese Fifteen Trade Unions
　  Decide to Jointly Celebrate "May Day"
    [Xinhua News Agency, 14th] TASS reported on the 11th: The Japan United Press reported: A meeting of representatives from fifteen trade unions, including the Japanese Metallurgical Workers' Union and the National Railway Workers' Union, has been held in Osaka. The meeting of representatives passed a resolution to jointly celebrate the May Day International Labor Day under the following slogans: "Oppose arming Japan, demand the conclusion of a comprehensive peace treaty, oppose the cession of military bases!" "Demand that the five major powers conclude a peace pact and defend world peace!" "Oppose military budgets!" "Demand the improvement of the people's material and cultural living conditions!" "Let us unite in the democratic national front for peace and independence!"

### 日本十五个工会  决定联合庆祝“五一”

1951-04-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　日本十五个工会
　  决定联合庆祝“五一”
    【新华社十四日讯】塔斯社十一日电，日本联合通讯社报道：日本冶金工会和国营铁路工会等十五个工会的代表会议已在大阪举行。代表会议通过决议，要在下列口号下联合庆祝五一国际劳动节：“反对武装日本，要求缔结全面和约，反对割让军事基地！”“要求五大国缔结和平公约，保卫世界和平！”“反对军事预算！”“要求改善人民物质与文化生活条件！”“让我们为了和平与独立而在民主民族战线中团结起来！”
