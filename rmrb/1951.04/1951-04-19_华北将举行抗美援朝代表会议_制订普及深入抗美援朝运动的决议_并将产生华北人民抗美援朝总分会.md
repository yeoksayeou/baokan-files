Title: North China to Hold Representatives' Conference on Resisting America and Aiding Korea; Resolution to be Formulated to Popularize and Deepen the Movement to Resist America and Aid Korea; North China People's General Sub-branch for Resisting America and Aiding Korea Will Be Established

Author:

Date: 1951-04-19
Page: 1

　　[This Newspaper Despatch] The North China People's Representatives' Conference on Resisting America and Aiding Korea will be held on April 22nd. Preparations for the conference are underway.

　　The China Peace Committee, has appointed Nie Zhen, Zhang Pan Shi, Ping Jie San, Wu Han, Zhu Ji Sheng, Dong Lu An, Zhang Zhi Xiang, Li Ji Huan, Bai Jian and other nine people to form a preparatory committee to prepare for the North China People's Representatives' Conference on Resisting America and Aiding Korea. The preparatory committee held its first meeting on the 17th. At the meeting, Comrade Nie Zhen reported on the preparation plan. After discussion, the meeting content, procedures, the number of representatives from each region, and the method of representative selection were decided. Representatives should be broadly inclusive of advanced patriotic people from all strata of society. This upcoming representatives' conference will, in addition to formulating a resolution to popularize and deepen the Resist America and Aid Korea movement in the North China region, also elect the North China People's General Sub-branch for Resisting America and Aiding Korea to uniformly lead the region's patriotic Resist America and Aid Korea movement in the future. Its birth will not only immediately and powerfully promote a new patriotic upsurge for the "May 1st" anti-American demonstration by the people of the entire region, but will also lead the region's patriotic Resist America and Aid Korea movement to a new, in-depth stage.

### 华北将举行抗美援朝代表会议  制订普及深入抗美援朝运动的决议  并将产生华北人民抗美援朝总分会

1951-04-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北将举行抗美援朝代表会议
    制订普及深入抗美援朝运动的决议
    并将产生华北人民抗美援朝总分会
    【本报讯】华北人民抗美援朝代表会议将于四月二十二日举行，大会筹备工作正进行中。
    中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会，委定聂真、张磐石、平杰三、吴晗、朱继圣、董鲁安、张致祥、李济寰、白坚等九人组成筹备委员会，筹备召开华北人民抗美援朝代表会议。该筹备委员会于十七日举行首次会议。会议上由聂真同志报告了筹备计划，经过讨论议定了会议内容、会议程序及各地区出席代表人数、代表产生方法等，代表务求广泛能包括各阶层先进爱国人士。行将到来的这一代表会议，除制订普及深入华北区抗美援朝运动的决议外，并将用选举方法产生华北人民抗美援朝总分会，以统一领导今后全区抗美援朝爱国运动。它的诞生，不仅会立即有力地促进全区人民“五一”反美大示威的爱国新高潮，并将使全区抗美援朝爱国运动走向新的深入的阶段。
