Title: Beijing Industrial and Commercial Circles Establish Anti-Revolutionary Purge Pact
Author:
Date: 1951-04-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 12th—

　　Three hundred and sixty-four representatives from Beijing's industrial and commercial circles, at the Popularize and Deepen Resist-America, Aid-Korea Conference held on the 6th of this month, established an Anti-Revolutionary Purge Pact. The content of the pact includes widely publicizing the government's policy of combining suppression and leniency towards counter-revolutionaries; assisting the government in purging counter-revolutionaries, breaking through personal feelings to expose all counter-revolutionaries who are enemies of the people; raising vigilance, strengthening organization, and preventing special agents from infiltrating, etc. The conference also issued a declaration stating: We, the industrialists and businesspeople of the capital, in order to consolidate our people's democratic regime, to ensure the safety of our lives and property and the development of our businesses, have decided to assist the government in thoroughly purging all remaining counter-revolutionary forces.

### 北京市工商界订立肃清反革命公约

1951-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京市工商界订立肃清反革命公约
    【新华社十二日讯】北京市工商业代表三百六十四人于本月六日举行的普及深入抗美援朝大会上，订立了肃清反革命公约。公约内容有广泛宣传政府对于反革命分子镇压与宽大相结合的政策；协助政府肃清反革命，破除情面检举一切与人民为敌的反革命分子；提高警惕，严密组织，防范特务混入等项。大会并发表宣言称：我们首都工商业者，为了巩固我们人民民主政权，为了保障我们生命财产的安全和业务的发展，决定协助政府彻底肃清一切反革命残余势力。
