Title: My Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Zhang Wentian, Visits Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Zorin
Author:
Date: 1951-04-19
Page: 4
　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 18th: According to TASS, Zhang Wentian, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, visited Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Zorin on the 16th to discuss matters concerning the presentation of his credentials to the Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.

### 我驻苏大使张闻天拜会苏副外长佐林

1951-04-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　我驻苏大使张闻天
　  拜会苏副外长佐林
    【新华社十八日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：中华人民共和国新任驻苏联特命全权大使张闻天，十六日拜会苏联外交部副部长佐林，商谈大使向苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席呈递国书事宜。
