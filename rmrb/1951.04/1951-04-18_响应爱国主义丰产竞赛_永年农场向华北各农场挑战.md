Title: Responding to the Patriotic Emulation Campaign for Increased Production: Yongnian Farm Challenges Farms in North China
Author:
Date: 1951-04-18
Page: 6

To the editors: please forward to all state-owned farm cultivation team tractor drivers in North China:

　　In response to the call from the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government to launch a patriotic emulation campaign for increased production, we propose the following conditions to challenge you:

　　1. Resolutely fulfill the 1951 production tasks, with unit yield exceeding that of ordinary farmers cultivating land of the same soil quality by 20%.

　　2. Ensure fixed personnel and quotas, maximize the effectiveness of machinery, and increase labor productivity by 20% compared to 1950. Save 15% on fuel consumption, and improve the quality of cultivation.

　　3. Drive carefully, maintain diligently, ensure longer life of machine parts, and guarantee no loss or damage to tools and parts.

　　To achieve the above goals, we guarantee to:

　　1. Unleash high levels of creativity, develop rationalization proposals, and exchange experiences in a timely manner.

　　2. Participate in various labor activities, being a mechanic when on the machine and a farm worker when off the machine.

　　3. Strengthen fire prevention, anti-espionage, anti-sabotage, and anti-theft work to ensure the safety of people's property.

　　4. Ensure daily adherence to two hours of political, professional, and cultural learning to improve political understanding and technical skills.

　　5. Strictly abide by various rules and regulations, and achieve unity and mutual assistance, without causing disputes.

　　6. Pay attention to public health, appropriately carry out recreational activities to ensure physical health, and strive to avoid sick leave.

　　7. Take advantage of various opportunities to promote the resistance against America and aid to Korea to nearby farmers, and to promote the improvement of agricultural technology.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　All tractor drivers of the State-owned Yongnian Farm Cultivation Team

### 响应爱国主义丰产竞赛  永年农场向华北各农场挑战

1951-04-18
第6版()
专栏：

　　响应爱国主义丰产竞赛  永年农场向华北各农场挑战编辑同志请转华北区各国营农场耕作队拖拉机驾驶员同志们：
    为了响应中央人民政府农业部开展爱国主义丰产竞赛运动的号召，我们提出下列条件，向你们挑战：
    一、坚决完成一九五一年生产任务，单位产量要比普通农民所耕种的同等土质的土地提高百分之二十。
    二、确实做到定员定额，发挥机械效能，劳动生产率要比一九五○年提高百分之二十。燃料消耗节省百分之十五，并提高耕作质量。
    三、细心驾驶，认真保养，保证增长机件寿命，保证不丢损工具和机件。
    为了达到以上目标我们保证做到：
    一、发挥高度创造性，开展合理化建议，及时交流经验。
    二、参加各种劳动，做到上机为机工，下机为农工。
    三、加强防火、防特务、防奸、防盗工作，以保证人民财产的安全。
    四、保证每天坚持二小时政治、业务及文化学习，提高政治认识和技术水平。
    五、严格遵守各种制度，并做到团结互助，不闹意见。
    六、注意公共卫生，适当开展文娱活动，以保证身体健康，争取不请病假。
    七、利用各种机会，向附近农民作抗美援朝宣传及改进农业技术的宣传。
            国营永年农场耕作队全体拖拉机驾驶员
