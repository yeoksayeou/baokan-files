Title: Government Administration Council Holds 82nd Meeting Approving Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications' Report on Last Year's Work Summary and This Year's Plan and Guidelines
Author:
Date: 1951-04-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 27th—The 82nd Government Administration Council meeting of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 27th. The meeting heard and approved the report made by Zhu Xuefan, Minister of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government, on the department's 1950 work summary and the 1951 postal and telecommunications plan and guidelines, and approved various appointment and removal proposals.

### 政务院举行第八十二次会议  批准邮电部关于去年工作总结与今年计划方针的报告

1951-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院举行第八十二次会议
　  批准邮电部关于去年工作总结与今年计划方针的报告
    【新华社二十七日讯】中央人民政府政务院第八十二次政务会议于二十七日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府邮电部朱学范部长所作的该部一九五零年工作总结与一九五一年邮电计划方针的报告，并通过了各项任免案。
