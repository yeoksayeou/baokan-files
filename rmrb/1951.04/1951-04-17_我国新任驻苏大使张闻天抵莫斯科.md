Title: Our Newly Appointed Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Zhang Wentian, Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1951-04-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 16th. Zhang Wentian, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People’s Republic of China to the Soviet Union, accompanied by his staff, arrived in Moscow by plane at 4 p.m. on the 15th. Those who greeted him at the airport included Zeng Yongquan, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of our embassy in the Soviet Union, and all members of the embassy; Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Zorin; Chairman of the Moscow City Soviet Yasnov; Commander of the Moscow Garrison, Lieutenant General Sinilov; Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Kurazhenkov; and Director of the First Far Eastern Department, Tong Jin. Also present to welcome him were: Bulgarian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Blagoeva; Romanian Ambassador Bujik; Mongolian Ambassador Idanjab; Hungarian Ambassador Zoklaton; Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic, Abel; Burmese Ambassador U Ang; Albanian Minister Natanaili; Finnish Minister Sanstrom; Korean Chargé d'affaires Lee Dong-geon; Czechoslovak Chargé d'affaires Etsni; Polish Chargé d'affaires Pogoniles; Israeli Chargé d'affaires Agayman; and Indian Republic Counselor Gandivia, among others.

### 我国新任驻苏大使张闻天抵莫斯科

1951-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　我国新任驻苏大使张闻天抵莫斯科
    【新华社莫斯科十六日电】中华人民共和国新任驻苏联特命全权大使张闻天偕随员一行，已于十五日下午四时飞抵莫斯科。到机场欢迎者有我驻苏大使馆临时代办曾涌泉及使馆全体人员、苏联外交部副部长佐林、莫斯科市苏维埃主席雅斯诺夫、莫斯科卫戍司令西尼洛夫中将、苏联外交部交际司长库拉任柯夫、第一远东司长童金。欢迎者尚有：保加利亚驻苏大使布拉果耶娃、罗马尼亚大使布吉克、蒙古大使伊丹甲布、匈牙利大使佐克拉顿、德意志民主共和国外交使团团长阿贝尔、缅甸大使宇昂、阿尔巴尼亚公使纳塔奈利、芬兰公使桑斯特罗姆、朝鲜代办李东健、捷克斯洛伐克代办埃茨尼、波兰代办波高尼勒斯、以色列代办阿盖曼和印度共和国参事甘迪维亚等多人。
