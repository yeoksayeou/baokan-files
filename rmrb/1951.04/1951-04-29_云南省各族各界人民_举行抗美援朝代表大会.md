Title: Yunnan Province People of All Nationalities and Circles Hold Anti-American and Aid-Korea Representative Conference
Author:
Date: 1951-04-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 25th. The people of all nationalities and circles in Yunnan Province held an Anti-American and Aid-Korea Representative Conference in Kunming City on the 18th. More than 1,400 representatives from Yiliang, Qujing, Wuding and other three special districts of the province, as well as all nationalities and circles in Kunming City, and returned overseas Chinese attended the meeting. In addition to summarizing the achievements already made in the Anti-American and Aid-Korea Movement in the province, the meeting also criticized the incorrect thinking in some areas of regarding the Anti-American and Aid-Korea Movement as a temporary task. The meeting clearly pointed out that the Anti-American and Aid-Korea Movement is a long-term political task, and it is necessary to establish institutions, strengthen organizational leadership and ideological leadership, so that everyone receives patriotic education. At the meeting, all the representatives signed in support of the World Peace Council's declaration on concluding a peace pact and unanimously voted against the United States arming Japan.

### 云南省各族各界人民  举行抗美援朝代表大会

1951-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　云南省各族各界人民
　  举行抗美援朝代表大会
    【新华社昆明二十五日电】云南省各民族各界人民于十八日在昆明市举行抗美援朝代表大会，出席的有该省宜良、曲靖、武定等三个专区及昆明市各民族各界和归国华侨等代表共一千四百多人。会议除总结了该省抗美援朝运动已经获得的成绩外，并批判了某些地区把抗美援朝运动看做临时任务的不正确的思想。会议明确指出：抗美援朝运动是一个长期的政治任务，必须建立机构，加强组织领导和思想领导，使每一个人都受到爱国主义教育。会上，全体代表签名拥护世界和平理事会关于缔结和平公约的宣言，并一致投票反对美国武装日本。
