　　Order of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government
　  Zheng Cai Zi No. 69
    The "Provisional Regulations on Punishing Crimes of Endangering National Currency" are hereby formulated and promulgated for implementation.
    This order is hereby issued.
          Premier Zhou Enlai
                April 19, 1951
