Title: The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference's Appeal for Celebrating the May 1st Labor Day in 1951
Author:
Date: 1951-04-17
Page: 1

　　The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
    An Appeal for Celebrating the May 1st Labor Day in 1951
    I. Long live the May 1st Labor Day, a festival of solidarity and struggle for working people around the world!
    II. Celebrate the great victories of the Chinese working people in the past year!
    III. Celebrate the great victory of the Chinese people's anti-imperialist struggle!
    IV. Salute the Chinese People's Liberation Army!
    V. Celebrate the great victory of the Chinese People's Volunteers on the Korean front!
    VI. Salute the Chinese People's Volunteers!
    VII. Salute the stretcher bearers, transport teams, medical teams, railway transportation workers, and rear service personnel who support the People's Volunteers!
    VIII. Celebrate the great victory of the Korean people's anti-aggression war!
    IX. Salute the Korean People's Army!
    X. Long live the solidarity between the Chinese and Korean peoples!
    XI. Salute the comrades who were wounded fighting against U.S. aggression and aiding Korea!
    XII. Eternal glory to the martyrs who sacrificed themselves fighting against U.S. aggression and aiding Korea!
    XIII. Celebrate the great victory of the Vietnamese people's anti-aggression war!
    XIV. Salute the people of Asian countries who oppose imperialist aggression!
    XV. Oppose U.S. imperialism's aggression against Taiwan!
    XVI. Oppose U.S. imperialism's aggression against Korea!
    XVII. Oppose U.S. imperialism's oppression of the Japanese people!
    XVIII. Oppose U.S. imperialism's rearming of Japan and plotting a separate peace treaty with Japan!
    XIX. Oppose U.S. imperialism's division of Germany and arming of West Germany!
    XX. Asian people unite against U.S. imperialism!
    XXI. People of the world unite against U.S. imperialism!
    XXII. Defend world peace!
    XXIII. Support the resolutions of the World Peace Congress!
    XXIV. Support the resolutions of the World Peace Council!
    XXV. Demand the conclusion of a peace pact among China, the Soviet Union, the United States, Britain, and France!
    XXVI. Long live the world peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union!
    XXVII. Long live the solidarity between the two great nations of China and the Soviet Union!
    XXVIII. Everyone participate in resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea!
    XXIX. Everyone work to consolidate national defense!
    XXX. Convene people's representative conferences at all levels, strengthen people's governments at all levels, and consolidate the people's democratic regime!
    XXXI. Resolutely suppress counter-revolutionaries and eliminate enemy spies!
    XXXII. Salute the People's Public Security Forces, the People's Police, and the People's Public Security Workers!
    XXXIII. Salute the combat heroes, labor models, and advanced workers!
    XXXIV. Workers, strive to produce, develop patriotic labor competitions, unite with engineering and technical personnel, improve production technology, guarantee the completion of production plans, and strive to gradually improve the lives of workers!
    XXXV. Workers, work hard to study politics, culture, and technology, and become advanced skilled workers!
    XXXVI. Resolutely implement the Trade Union Law!
    XXXVII. Support the government's implementation of labor insurance!
    XXXVIII. Factory leaders, rely on workers to manage factories well and realize democratic management and unified leadership within factories!
    XXXIX. Enterprise leaders, learn economic accounting systems and oppose waste!
    XL. Support land reform, long live the emancipation of the peasants across the country!
    XLI. Peasants, work hard at spring plowing, participate in water conservancy projects, avoid disasters, and strive for a bumper harvest!
    XLII. Peasants in areas where land reform has been implemented should organize labor mutual aid, improve agricultural technology, develop sideline production, work hard to study politics and culture, participate in political activities, and strengthen national power!
    XLIII. Cooperative workers, work hard to serve members and develop urban-rural mutual assistance!
    XLIV. Young students, pay attention to physical exercise, study diligently, and prepare to serve the motherland!
    XLV. Women across the country, abandon all shackles of feudal ideology, work hard to study politics and culture, and actively participate in economic, political, and cultural activities!
    XLVI. Cultural and educational workers, strive to popularize and improve patriotic culture and education, and educate more talent for the country!
    XLVII. Scientific and technical workers, work hard to master progressive theories and technologies, closely integrate with practice, and serve national construction!
    XLVIII. Industrial and commercial entrepreneurs, work hard to improve management, implement labor-capital consultation, develop economic undertakings, support government laws and decrees, actively pay taxes, and actively participate in national construction!
    XLIX. Believers of all religions, promote patriotism and participate in the anti-imperialist and anti-feudal front!
    L. All democratic parties, democratic personages from all walks of life, and people's organizations consolidate and develop their organizations, and strive to consolidate and expand the people's democratic united front, thoroughly realize the Common Program, and accomplish the various work tasks of the country!
    LI. All ethnic groups in the country unite to oppose imperialist aggression and build an independent, unified, and great motherland!
    LII. Implement ethnic regional autonomy, strive to develop the economy and culture in minority areas, and gradually improve the lives of the people!
    LIII. Overseas Chinese unite, cherish the motherland, and strive to defend their legitimate rights and interests!
    LIV. Long live the great unity of the Chinese people!
    LV. Long live the great People's Republic of China!
    LVI. Long live the Communist Party of China!
    LVII. Long live Chairman Mao, the great leader of the Chinese people!
                April 16, 1951
