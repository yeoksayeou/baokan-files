Title: Capital "May Day" Preparatory Committee Established, Decides to Hold Major Demonstration on May Day
Author:
Date: 1951-04-21
Page: 1

　　Capital Celebrates May Day Preparatory Committee Established
    Decides to Hold Major Demonstration on May Day
    [Beijing News Agency] The capital's various circles have formally established the preparatory committee for celebrating "May Day" Labor Day on the 11th of this month in order to prepare for the celebration of "May Day". Those responsible for central, North China, and municipal-level organizations attended the establishment meeting. The meeting decided to hold a city-wide workers' and people from all walks of life demonstration parade on May 1st; and decided to move the weekly holiday of factories on April 29th to May 2nd for rest. On the evening of May 1st and May 2nd, various recreational programs will be held, and all parks in the city will provide free park access for workers on May 1st and 2nd.
    The preparatory committee for celebrating "May Day" Labor Day is now working at the Xingjian Association in Zhongshan Park. The telephone numbers of the secretariat of the association are: Fifth Bureau 3196, Fifth Bureau 5158.

### 首都庆祝五一筹委会成立  决定五一举行大示威

1951-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　首都庆祝五一筹委会成立
    决定五一举行大示威
    【京市新闻处讯】首都各界为筹备庆祝“五一”节，已经在本月十一日正式成立了庆祝“五一”劳动节筹备委员会。成立会到有中央、华北及市级各机关负责人，会上决定在五月一日举行全市工人及各界人民的示威大游行；并决定将各工厂在四月二十九日的星期例假移到五月二日休息。在五月一日的晚上和五月二日将举办各种游艺节目，全市各公园亦在五月一、二日免费招待工人游园。
    庆祝“五一”劳动节筹备委员会现已在中山公园行健会开始办公，该会秘书处的电话是：五局三一九六，五局五一五八。
