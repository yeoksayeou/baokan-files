Title: Over 200 American Planes Invade Our Fujian Coastal Areas, Strafing in the Western Suburbs of Fuzhou and Killing Our Peaceful Residents. Our People Are Paying Serious Attention to the Crimes of American Aggressors Expanding Their Aggression
Author:
Date: 1951-04-15
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 14th: More than 200 American planes conducted reconnaissance activities in the coastal areas of Fuzhou (Minhou), Putian, Hui'an, Quanzhou (Jinjiang), Xiamen and other areas of Fujian Province, our territory, from 11:00 AM to 12:30 PM on the 11th of this month. Among them, 48 American planes invaded the airspace over villages south of Quanzhou; 36 invaded the airspace over villages southeast of Hui'an; 2 invaded the airspace over the Xiamen urban area; and 5 successively invaded the airspace over the Fuzhou urban area. The American planes that invaded Fuzhou also strafed with machine guns in the western suburbs of the city, killing peaceful residents. This crime of expanding aggressive activities by the American aggressors has aroused the serious attention of the Chinese people.

### 美机二百余架侵扰我福建沿海  在福州西郊进行扫射杀害我和平居民  我国人民严重注意美国侵略者扩大侵略的罪行

1951-04-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　美机二百余架侵扰我福建沿海
    在福州西郊进行扫射杀害我和平居民
    我国人民严重注意美国侵略者扩大侵略的罪行
    【新华社十四日讯】美国飞机二百多架于本月十一日上午十一时至十二时半在我国领土福建省福州（闽侯）、莆田、惠安、泉州（晋江）、厦门等沿海地区进行侦察活动。其中有美机四十八架侵入泉州以南乡村上空；三十六架侵入惠安东南的乡村上空；二架侵入厦门市区上空；五架先后侵入福州市区上空。侵入福州之美机，并曾于该市西郊用机枪进行扫射，杀害和平居民。美国侵略者这种扩大侵略活动的罪行，已引起中国人民的严重注意。
