Title: Agenda of the Seventh Congress of the Italian Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1951-04-04
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Rome, 3rd. TASS reports: The Seventh National Congress of the Italian Communist Party began in Rome on the 3rd. The session is scheduled for six days, ending on the 8th. According to the Secretariat of the Italian Communist Party, the agenda of the congress is as follows: 1. The Italian people's struggle for peace, labor, and freedom (report by Palmiro Togliatti, General Secretary of the Italian Communist Party). 2. The unity of the working class in the current situation (report by Luigi Longo, Deputy General Secretary of the Italian Communist Party). 3. Elections.

### 意共第七次代表大会议程

1951-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　意共第七次代表大会议程
    【新华社三日讯】据塔斯社罗马讯：意大利共产党第七次全国代表大会已于三日起在罗马举行。会期定为六天，至八日结束。据意共书记处宣布，大会议程如下：一、意大利人民为争取和平、为劳动及自由的斗争（由意共总书记托里亚蒂作报告）。二、工人阶级在目前情况中的团结（由意共副总书记隆哥作报告）。三、选举。
