Title: Dennis Cables Soong Ching Ling, Thanking Her for Her Congratulations on His Release

Author:

Date: 1951-04-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd:

Dennis cables Soong Ching Ling
Thanks her for her congratulations on his release

The General Secretary of the American Communist Party, Dennis, cabled back to Soong Ching Ling, Chairman of the China Welfare Institute, on the 9th, in response to her congratulatory telegram of March 30th. The cable stated:

I was overjoyed to receive your telegram congratulating me on my release from prison. The brilliant contributions of the Chinese people and their vanguard, the Communist Party, in the struggle to defend world peace illuminated my dark days of imprisonment.

Please believe that I, and our entire Party, will do everything in our power to unite the American people so that they can join the struggle of all humanity around the world to avoid the disaster of a third world war.

### 但尼斯电复宋庆龄  感谢对于他出狱的祝贺

1951-04-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　但尼斯电复宋庆龄
　  感谢对于他出狱的祝贺
    【新华社二十三日讯】美共总书记但尼斯于九日电复中国人民救济总会宋庆龄主席三月三十日的贺电。电称：
    我十分欣悦地接到你祝贺我出狱的电报。中国人民和他们的先锋队——共产党在保卫世界和平的斗争中的辉煌贡献，曾照耀着我被监禁的黑暗日子。
    请相信：我和我们整个的党，将竭尽全力团结美国人民，以使他们参加到全世界人类为避免第三次世界大战的灾难的斗争中去。
