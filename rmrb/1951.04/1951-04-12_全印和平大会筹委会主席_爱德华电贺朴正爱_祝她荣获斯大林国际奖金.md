Title: Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the All-India Peace Congress, Edward, Congratulates Park Chung-ae on Winning the Stalin International Peace Prize

Author:

Date: 1951-04-12
Page: 4

　　Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the All-India Peace Congress,
    Edward, Congratulates Park Chung-ae
    On Winning the Stalin International Peace Prize
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, April 11th] Edward, Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the All-India Peace Congress and member of the World Peace Council, who is currently visiting Shanghai, sent a telegram to Park Chung-ae, Chairman of the Korean Democratic Women's League, congratulating her on winning the Stalin International Peace Prize "For Strengthening International Peace." The telegram reads as follows:
    "I extend my congratulations to you on the Stalin International Prize Committee's recognition of your contribution to the cause of world peace and security. Millions of people in India are closely watching the heroic struggle of your brave country to maintain freedom and independence and to oppose American imperialist aggression. I wish you success in defeating the American imperialists."

### 全印和平大会筹委会主席  爱德华电贺朴正爱  祝她荣获斯大林国际奖金

1951-04-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　全印和平大会筹委会主席
    爱德华电贺朴正爱
    祝她荣获斯大林国际奖金
    【新华社上海十一日电】现在访问上海的全印和平大会筹备委员会主席、世界和平理事会理事爱德华，致电祝贺朝鲜民主妇女同盟委员长朴正爱荣获“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金，电文如下：
    “你对世界和平与安全事业的贡献获得了斯大林国际奖金委员会的表扬，谨向你致贺。印度千百万人民密切注视着你的勇敢的国家为保持自由与独立、反对美帝国主义侵略而进行的英勇斗争。谨祝你们成功并击败美帝国主义者。”
