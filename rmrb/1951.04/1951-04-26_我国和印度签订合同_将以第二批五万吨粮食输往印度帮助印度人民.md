Title: China and India Sign Contract to Ship Second Batch of 50,000 Tons of Grain to India to Help the Indian People
Author: 
Date: 1951-04-26
Page: 1

　　China and India Sign Contract
　  To Ship Second Batch of 50,000 Tons of Grain to India to Help the Indian People
    Xinhua News Agency, April 25th: In order to help the Indian people solve their food difficulties and enhance the friendship between the Chinese and Indian people, the Central People's Government of China and the Government of the Republic of India signed a contract in Beijing on April 25th for China to ship a second batch of 50,000 tons of grain to India. Lu Xuzhang, manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, signed on behalf of the Central People's Government of China, and Mr. Gaul, Counselor of the Indian Embassy in China, signed on behalf of the Government of the Republic of India. Lin Haiyun, Director of the Foreign Trade Department of the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government, Wang Chaocheng, Section Chief of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Kumar, Secretary of the Indian Embassy in China, attended the signing ceremony. The shipment of this batch of grain will begin within the month.

### 我国和印度签订合同  将以第二批五万吨粮食输往印度帮助印度人民

1951-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国和印度签订合同
　  将以第二批五万吨粮食输往印度帮助印度人民
    【新华社二十五日讯】为了帮助印度人民解决粮食困难，增进中印两国人民的友谊，我中央人民政府与印度共和国政府于四月二十五日在北京签订了中国以第二批五万吨粮食输往印度的合同。代表我中央人民政府签字者为中国进出口公司经理卢绪章，代表印度共和国政府签字者为印度驻华大使馆高尔参赞。参加签字仪式者有中央人民政府贸易部国外贸易司司长林海云、外交部亚洲司科长王朝成，印度驻华大使馆秘书古玛尔等。该批粮食将于月内开始起运。
