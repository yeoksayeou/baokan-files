Title: Hungarian Ambassador Száffránkó Holds National Day Reception
Author: 
Date: 1951-04-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th—Hungarian Ambassador to China, Mr. Száffránkó, held a reception in Beijing at 7 p.m. on the 4th to celebrate the National Day of the Hungarian People's Republic.

Those invited to attend included: Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China; Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the State Council of the Central People's Government; Huang Yanpei, Vice Premier; Shen Junru, President of the Supreme People's Court; heads of various departments of the Central People's Government; and representatives of various people's organizations.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries to China who were invited to attend included: Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, Czechoslovak Ambassador Weiskopf, Korean Ambassador Lee Joo-yeon, Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Indian Ambassador Pannikar, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic König, Mongolian Ambassador Jalkhajasürén, Burmese Ambassador U Myint Thein, Swedish Ambassador Amashton, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Danish Minister Munk, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy Dzieferasiński, Counselor of the Swiss Legation Steiner, Second Secretary of the Indonesian Embassy Tan Tao Mo, and embassy staff from various countries. The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

The Hungarian Embassy screened a Hungarian documentary film the day before to entertain people from all walks of life in the capital.

### 匈牙利大使夏法朗柯举行国庆招待会

1951-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　匈牙利大使夏法朗柯
    举行国庆招待会
    【新华社四日讯】匈牙利人民共和国驻我国大使夏法朗柯，为庆祝匈牙利人民共和国国庆日，特于四日下午七时在北京举行招待会。
    应邀出席的有：中华人民共和国中央人民政府李济深副主席，中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来、副总理黄炎培，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，中央人民政府各部门首长和各人民团体代表等多人。
    各国驻华外交使节应邀出席的有：苏联大使罗申、捷克斯洛伐克大使魏斯柯普夫、朝鲜大使李周渊、罗马尼亚大使鲁登科、印度大使潘尼迦、德意志民主共和国外交使团团长柯尼希、蒙古大使贾尔卡赛汗、缅甸大使吴敏登、瑞典大使阿马斯顿、保加利亚大使彼得科夫、丹麦公使穆克、波兰大使馆临时代办戴斐拉辛斯基、瑞士公使馆参赞施禔纳、印尼大使馆二等秘书谭陶墨及各国使馆人员。招待会在热烈友好气氛中进行。
    在此前一日，匈牙利大使馆曾放映匈牙利纪录影片招待首都各界。
