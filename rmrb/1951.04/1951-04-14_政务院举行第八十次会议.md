Title: Government Administration Council Holds Eightieth Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-04-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 13th.
　　The eightieth meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 13th. The meeting heard and approved the report by Li Fanwu, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Forestry and Land Reclamation of the Central People's Government, on the department's 1950 Forestry Work Summary and the 1951 Forestry Work Plan, as well as the summary of the National Forestry Conference in February 1951. The meeting also approved the report of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council on the situation of state-owned enterprises in Hong Kong and the handling of personnel returning to the country. In addition, the meeting approved several appointment and removal cases and submitted appointment and removal cases to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第八十次会议

1951-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院举行第八十次会议
    【新华社十三日讯】中央人民政府政务院第八十次政务会议于十三日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府林垦部副部长李范五所作的该部一九五零年林业工作总结和一九五一年的林业工作计划，以及一九五一年二月全国林业会议总结的报告。会议并批准了政务院财政经济委员会关于在香港国营企业情况及对于回国人员处理的报告。此外，会议通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
