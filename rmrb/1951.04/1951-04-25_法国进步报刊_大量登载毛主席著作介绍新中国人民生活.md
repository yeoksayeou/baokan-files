Title: French Progressive Newspapers Extensively Publish Chairman Mao's Works, Introducing the People's Life in New China
Author:
Date: 1951-04-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 19th: News from Paris: French progressive newspapers have recently used extensive space to publish the works of the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao Zedong, and to introduce the people's life in New China.

The weekly "Line of Fifty," which mainly introduces the lives and construction of people's democratic countries, has been continuously publishing Chairman Mao's "On New Democracy" since its previous issue. In this issue, it also published Comrade Liu Shaoqi's report on the revision of the Party Constitution made at the Seventh National Congress of the Communist Party of China, regarding the part on Mao Zedong Thought, and published photographs of Chairman Mao on the cover and back cover from the May Fourth Movement to all the main stages of the Chinese people's revolution.

The latest issue of the L'Humanité weekly used more than a quarter of its space to introduce New China. Under the title "475 Million Liberated People," the newspaper published the most outstanding works of modern Chinese art, including sculptures, woodcuts, paintings, cartoons, and New Year pictures. It also published an article by the famous French writer Godard, discussing the US imperialist's plan of aggression against China, the great significance of the victory of the Chinese People's Revolution, and the inevitable failure of reactionary forces.

### 法国进步报刊  大量登载毛主席著作介绍新中国人民生活

1951-04-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　法国进步报刊
    大量登载毛主席著作介绍新中国人民生活
    【新华社布拉格十九日电】巴黎消息：法国进步报刊最近用大量篇幅刊载中国人民伟大领袖毛泽东主席的作品并介绍新中国人民的生活。
    以介绍人民民主国家的生活和建设为主的“五○线”周刊，从前一期起就连续刊登毛主席的“新民主主义论”。这一期，它又刊出了刘少奇同志在中国共产党第七次全国代表大会上所作修改党章报告中关于论毛泽东思想的部分，并且在封面和封底上刊登了毛主席从五四运动时期起在中国人民革命所有主要阶段中的照片。
    最近一期的人道报星期刊，用了四分之一以上的篇幅来介绍新中国。该报在“四亿七千五百万被解放了的人民”的标题下刊登了现代中国艺术中最杰出的作品，其中有雕刻、木刻、绘画、漫画和年画。它还刊登了法国著名作家古达德的一篇文章，谈论美帝国主义对中国的侵略计划、中国人民革命胜利的伟大意义和反动势力不可避免的失败。
