Title: Picture

Author: 
Date: 1951-04-06
Page: 4

The reactionary Yoshida government of Japan is following the war policy of American imperialism, and the disaster of a new war is once again threatening the Japanese people. Japanese veterans, who suffered from the disasters of World War II, are actively participating in the movement to defend world peace. The picture shows Japanese veterans collecting signatures for peace on the streets of Tokyo.
