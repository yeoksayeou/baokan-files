Title: Representatives of Various Countries Discuss Bellinger's Report to Prepare for the World Youth and Student Peace Festival
Author:
Date: 1951-04-30
Page: 4
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: The Executive Committee of the International Student Union, after hearing the report by International Student Union Secretary-General Bellinger on "Preparations for the Third World Youth and Student Peace Festival" at its afternoon meeting on the 28th, heard speeches from representatives of twenty-one countries, including the Soviet Union, on the preparations for the festival at its meeting on the 29th. It also elected Bellinger, Soviet representative Fudoven, and German representative Teba, among others, to form a committee of seven to draft a resolution.

　　In his report, Bellinger pointed out: War instigators have always used various methods to provoke disputes among people of various countries as an important step in war preparations. Therefore, peace-loving people and youth from all countries should do everything possible to promote understanding and cooperation with each other in order to defeat the conspiracy of war instigators. The World Youth and Student Peace Festival is held for this purpose. The world's youth and students should not only show their firm will to defend peace at the festival, but also show the great strength to defend peace.

　　German student representative Teba, reporting on the preparations of German youth for the festival, said: Two million young people and students from the German Democratic Republic and West Germany will participate in the festival. The vast number of German youth and students are accelerating the construction of sports fields, theaters, and dormitories with their labor to welcome youth representatives from various countries. At the meeting, Teba presented the first commemorative medal of the festival to Grohmann.

　　Xie Bangding, Chairman of the All-China Student Federation, reported on the preparations of Chinese youth and students for the festival. He said: Chinese youth and students agree with the International Student Union's proposal to establish a solidarity fund to help youth from colonies and dependent countries to participate widely in the festival, and will make every effort to help youth and students from Southeast Asian countries to participate in the festival.

　　At the meeting on the 29th, Grohmann announced the congratulatory telegrams received by the Executive Committee from students from all parts of China. Model workers from Beijing's young workers came to the meeting to present gifts to the Executive Committee, and women representatives came to the meeting to present flowers, which were warmly welcomed.

### 各国代表讨论贝林格报告  筹备世界青年学生和平联欢节

1951-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　各国代表讨论贝林格报告
　  筹备世界青年学生和平联欢节
    【新华社二十九日讯】国际学生联合会执行委员会在二十八日下午会议上听取了国际学联总书记贝林格关于“第三届世界青年与学生和平联欢节的筹备工作”的报告后，在二十九日会议上听取了苏联等二十一个国家代表关于联欢节筹备工作的发言，并推选了贝林格、苏联代表佛道文和德国代表特巴等七人组成起草决议的委员会。
    贝林格在报告中指出：战争挑拨者一向利用各种方法在世界各国人民间进行挑拨，并以此作为战争准备的重要步骤之一。因此，世界各国爱好和平的人民和青年应尽一切力量来促进彼此之间的了解与合作，以击破战争挑拨者的阴谋。世界青年学生和平联欢节就是为了这一目的而举行的。世界青年和学生不仅要在联欢节中显示保卫和平的坚决意志，而且要显示出保卫和平的巨大力量。
    德国学生代表特巴报告德国青年筹备联欢节的情况说：二百万来自德意志民主共和国和西德的青年和学生将参加联欢节。广大的德国青年和学生正用自己的劳动，加速修建运动场、剧院和宿舍，来欢迎各国青年代表。特巴在会上以第一枚联欢节纪念章赠给格罗曼。
    中华全国学生联合会主席谢邦定报告了我国青年和学生筹备联欢节的情况。他说：中国青年和学生赞成国际学联设立团结基金以帮助殖民地、附属国的青年广泛参加联欢节的意见，并将尽最大努力来帮助东南亚各国青年和学生参加联欢节。
    在二十九日会议上，格罗曼宣布了执委会收到的中国各地学生的贺电。北京市青年工人的劳动模范到会向执委会献礼，妇女代表到会献花，受到热烈欢迎。
