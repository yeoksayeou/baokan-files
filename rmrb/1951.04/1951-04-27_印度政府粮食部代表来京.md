Title: Indian Government Food Department Representative Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1951-04-27
Page: 1

　　Indian Government Food Department Representative Arrives in Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, 26th] Mr. Deng Lai, Joint Secretary of the Food Department of the Government of the Republic of India, arrived in Beijing by car at 8:40 this morning on official business. Those who greeted him at the station included Lin Haiyun, Director of the Foreign Trade Department of the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government, Han Xu, Section Chief of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Mr. Gore, Counselor of the Indian Embassy in China.

### 印度政府粮食部代表来京

1951-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　印度政府粮食部代表来京
    【新华社二十六日讯】印度共和国政府粮食部联合秘书邓来先生因公来华，已于今晨八时四十分乘车抵北京，赴站欢迎者有我中央人民政府贸易部国外贸易司司长林海云，外交部交际处科长韩叙及印度驻华大使馆参赞高尔等。
