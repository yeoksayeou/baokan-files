Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-04-14
Page: 1

Increasing cotton production is a task in our country's agricultural production increase movement. This is Guo Yousheng, a model agricultural laborer from Zhongran Village, Qingyuan County, Hebei Province. In order to realize the cotton production increase plan, he mobilized his entire family to select cotton seeds before planting. He said: "Only by selecting seeds can we increase production and achieve a bumper harvest."

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    增产棉花是我国农业增产运动中的一项任务。这是河北省清苑县中冉村的农业劳动模范郭有生。他为了实现棉花增产计划，发动全家在种棉以前进行棉花选种。他说：“只有选种才会增产丰收。”
