Title: All-China Railway Workers' Union Cables Congratulations on the First Anniversary of the Founding of the China Changchun Railway Company
Author:
Date: 1951-04-25
Page: 2

The All-China Railway Workers' Union
　Cables Congratulations on the First Anniversary of the Founding of the China Changchun Railway Company

　　This month's 25th is the anniversary of the founding of the China Changchun Railway Company. The All-China Railway Workers' Union specially sent a telegram to the company to congratulate it. The congratulatory telegram is as follows:
To the Board of Directors of the China Changchun Railway Company
Chairman Yu Guang-sheng, Vice Chairman M. S. Yelogov, and Changchun Railway Administration Director En. A. Grunichev, and Deputy Director Liu Juying:

　　We are delighted to celebrate the first anniversary of the founding of the China Changchun Railway Company. We extend our warmest congratulations to you and all Chinese and Soviet personnel!

　　Over the past year, in the friendly and cooperative relationship between China and the Soviet Union, on the one hand, Soviet experts have provided their advanced experience and given us sincere assistance; on the other hand, Chinese cadres and employees have learned the advanced experience of Soviet experts with a sincere and humble attitude, making the Changchun Railway the first in the Chinese People's Railways to implement the economic accounting system, establishing a model of high efficiency and low cost. This is a remarkable achievement of your and all Chinese and Soviet personnel's efforts to complete the glorious tasks given by the governments of China and the Soviet Union. We are glad that the Chinese People's Railway has a model for advancement, and we are even more grateful to Marshal Stalin and the Soviet government for this great friendship.

                All-China Railway Workers' Union
              April 22, 1951 (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中国铁路工会全国委员会  电贺中国长春铁路公司成立一周年

1951-04-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　中国铁路工会全国委员会
　  电贺中国长春铁路公司成立一周年
    本月二十五日为中国长春铁路公司成立一周年纪念日，中国铁路工会全国委员会特致电该公司祝贺。贺电如下：中国长春铁路公司理事会
  余光生主席  姆·斯·叶洛果夫副主席并长春铁路管理局  恩·阿·格鲁尼切夫局长  刘居英副局长：
    欣逢中国长春铁路公司成立一周年纪念，谨向你们与全体中苏人员致最热烈的祝贺！
    一年以来，你们在中苏友好合作的关系中，一方面苏联专家提供其先进经验，给予我们以真诚的援助；一方面中国干部和员工以诚恳虚心的态度，学习了苏联专家的先进经验，已使长春铁路在中国人民铁路中第一个实行了经济核算制，树立了效率高、成本低的模范。这是你们和全体中苏人员努力完成中苏两国政府所给予的光荣任务的显著成就。我们庆幸中国人民铁路事业有了前进的榜样，我们更感谢斯大林大元帅和苏联政府这种伟大友谊的帮助。
                中国铁路工会全国委员会
              一九五一年四月二十二日（新华社）
