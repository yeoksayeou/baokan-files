Title: U.S. Planes Again Invade My Antung City Yesterday, Dropping Over a Hundred Bombs, Slaughtering Our Peaceful Residents
Author:
Date: 1951-04-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Antung, 12th. Thirty-one B-29 U.S. aircraft, under the cover of more than forty fighter planes, invaded the airspace of my Antung city at 9:15 a.m. today, indiscriminately bombing the urban area, dropping over a hundred and ten bombs. Twelve of these U.S. aircraft later flew to the Jiuliancheng district in the suburbs of Antung city for bombing and strafing. This is a new bloody atrocity committed by the American aggressors after bombing Antung, Linjiang, Ji'an, and Kuandian on March 30, March 31, and April 7.

### 美机昨天又侵袭我安东市  投弹百十余枚屠杀我和平居民

1951-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　美机昨天又侵袭我安东市
    投弹百十余枚屠杀我和平居民
    【新华社安东十二日电】美国飞机Ｂ二九式机三十一架，在驱逐机四十余架掩护下，于今日上午九时十五分侵入我安东市上空，向市区滥肆轰炸，投弹达一百十余枚。该批美机中之十二架，后又飞至安东市郊九连城区进行轰炸扫射。这是美国侵略者继三月三十日、三十一日和四月七日轰炸安东、临江、辑安、宽甸等地后的新的血腥暴行。
