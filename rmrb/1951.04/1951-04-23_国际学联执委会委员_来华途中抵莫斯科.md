Title: International Student Union Executive Committee Members Arrive in Moscow En Route to China
Author:
Date: 1951-04-23
Page: 4
　　
　　International Student Union Executive Committee Members
　　Arrive in Moscow En Route to China
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 21st] According to TASS Moscow: The International Student Union (ISU) Executive Committee members and their party, who were invited by the All-China Students' Federation and were traveling from Prague to Beijing to attend the ISU Executive Committee meeting, arrived in Moscow on the 19th. Moscow students held a grand welcoming ceremony. Kozymazov, Chairman of the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee, addressed the meeting, saying that the arrival of the ISU Executive Committee in the Soviet Union will strengthen the friendship and cooperation between Soviet students and young people from all countries. ISU President Grossman, on behalf of the world's students, conveyed his greetings to the Soviet students.

### 国际学联执委会委员  来华途中抵莫斯科

1951-04-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　国际学联执委会委员
　  来华途中抵莫斯科
    【新华社二十一日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：应中华全国学生联合会的邀请，自布拉格前往北京出席国际学生联合会执行委员会会议的国际学联执委会委员等一行，已于十九日抵达莫斯科。莫斯科学生举行了盛大的欢迎会，苏联青年反法西斯委员会主席科齐马索夫在会上致词称：国际学联执委会抵达苏联，将加强苏联学生与各国青年的友谊和合作。国际学联主席格罗曼代表世界学生向苏联学生致意。
