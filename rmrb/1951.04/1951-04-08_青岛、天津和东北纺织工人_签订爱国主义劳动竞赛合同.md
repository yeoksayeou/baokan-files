Title: Qingdao, Tianjin, and Northeast Textile Workers Sign Patriotic Labor Emulation Contract
Author:
Date: 1951-04-08
Page: 2
 
　　Qingdao, Tianjin, and Northeast Textile Workers
　  Sign Patriotic Labor Emulation Contract
    Chen Weiji, Deputy Minister of the Textile Industry Department, calls on textile workers in all regions to actively respond and lay the foundation for implementing economic accounting.
    After the Qingdao Municipal Committee of the Chinese Textile Workers' Union, representing all textile workers in Qingdao, issued a patriotic emulation challenge to textile workers nationwide on January 31, it received responses from the Northeast Regional Committee and the Tianjin Municipal Committee of the Textile Workers' Union, representing textile workers in those two regions. Now, the patriotic labor emulation contract for over 90,000 textile workers in these three regions was signed on April 2 in Beijing, under the auspices of the National Committee of the Textile Workers' Union.
    When signing this emulation contract, the National Committee of the Textile Workers' Union put forward opinions on the evaluation of this competition to the National Labor Emulation Appraisal Committee of the Textile Workers' Union and the textile workers in the three regions: requiring textile workers participating in this competition to give play to their initiative and creativity, to improve technology and work methods, to assist the administration or the capitalists to establish and improve various production management systems, to achieve high output, good quality, low cost, and fewer accidents, and to raise the production level of the textile industry a step further. It is also stipulated that under normal production conditions, rush production or extended working hours are not allowed.
    Chen Weiji, Deputy Minister of the Central Textile Industry Department, also came to participate in the signing ceremony. In his speech, he said: This competition will lay the foundation for implementing economic accounting, and he called on textile workers in other regions to actively respond, so that this competition will develop into a nationwide movement.
    Deputy Minister Chen Weiji's call was warmly supported by the factory directors and chairmen of the trade union committees of the textile factories from various places who participated in the signing ceremony.          (Xinhua News Agency)

### 青岛、天津和东北纺织工人  签订爱国主义劳动竞赛合同

1951-04-08
第2版()
专栏：

　　青岛、天津和东北纺织工人
　  签订爱国主义劳动竞赛合同
    纺织工业部陈维稷副部长号召各地纺织工人都积极应战，为贯彻经济核算打下基础。
    中国纺织工会青岛市委员会，代表青岛全市纺织工人，一月三十一日向全国纺织工人提出爱国主义竞赛挑战后，得到纺织工会东北区委员会和天津市委员会代表两地纺织工人的应战。现在这三个地区九万多纺织工人的爱国主义劳动竞赛合同，已经在纺织工会全国委员会的主持下，四月二日在北京签订了。
    在签订这次竞赛合同时，纺织工会全国委员会向纺织工会全国劳动竞赛评判委员会和三个地区的纺织工人，提出对这次竞赛的评判意见：要求参加这次竞赛的纺织工人发挥积极性和创造性，从改进技术和工作法，协助行政或资方建立和健全各种生产管理制度，作到产量高、质量好、成本低、事故少，把纺织工业的生产水平提高一步。并且规定在正常的生产情况下，不许突击生产，或延长工时。
    中央纺织工业部陈维稷副部长也来参加签订合同的仪式，他在致词中说：这次竞赛将要为贯彻经济核算打下基础，他号召其他地区的纺织工人，也要积极应战，使这个竞赛发展成为一个全国性的运动。
    陈维稷副部长的这个号召受到了参加这个签订合同仪式的各地纺织厂厂长和各纺织厂工会委员会主席的热烈拥护。          （新华社）
