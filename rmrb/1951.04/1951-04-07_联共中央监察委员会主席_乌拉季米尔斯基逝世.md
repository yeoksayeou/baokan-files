Title: Chairman of the Central Auditing Commission of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks), Vladimirsky, Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1951-04-07
Page: 1

　　Chairman of the Central Auditing Commission of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks)
　  Vladimirsky Passes Away
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 5th] TASS Moscow, 3rd: The Chairman of the Central Auditing Commission of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks), Vladimirsky, passed away on the 2nd. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union issued an obituary, stating: "The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union announce with deep sorrow that one of the oldest activists of the Bolshevik Party and the Soviet state, a selfless fighter for the cause of the workers, Chairman of the Central Auditing Commission of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks), and Deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Comrade Mikhail Fyodorovich Vladimirsky, passed away on April 2nd at 9 a.m. after a long and serious illness. The death of Vladimirsky, who dedicated his life to the cause of communism and devoted all his efforts to the happiness of the people, is a great loss to the Communist Party and the Soviet people."
    Another report from Moscow Radio on the 3rd: The Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union has decided to establish a funeral committee for Vladimirsky, composed of the following members: Suslov (Chairman), Shkiryatov, Pospelov, Gorkin, Ignatiev, Rumyantsev, and Krumin.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 5th] TASS Moscow: The funeral of Vladimirsky, the old leader of the Communist Party (Bolsheviks) and the Soviet state, Chairman of the Central Auditing Commission of the All-Union Communist Party, and Deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, was held in Moscow on the 4th. Hundreds of thousands of Moscow residents paid their respects to Vladimirsky's remains. A memorial meeting was held in Red Square, where speeches were given by Suslov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party, Pospelov, Member of the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party, and Rumyantsev, Secretary of the Moscow City Committee of the All-Union Communist Party. Members of the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party and Soviet government officials attended the funeral. Vladimirsky's ashes were buried in the Kremlin wall.

### 联共中央监察委员会主席  乌拉季米尔斯基逝世

1951-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　联共中央监察委员会主席
　  乌拉季米尔斯基逝世
    【新华社五日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科三日讯：苏联共产党（布）中央监察委员会主席乌拉季米尔斯基于二日逝世。苏联共产党中央委员会及苏联部长会议发表讣告如下称：“苏联共产党（布）中央委员会及苏联部长会议怀着深切的悲痛通告：布尔什维克党及苏维埃国家最老的活动家之一、为劳动者的事业而忘我奋斗的战士、联共（布）中央监察委员会主席、苏联最高苏维埃代表米海尔·费多罗维奇·乌拉季米尔斯基同志，在长久重病之后，已于四月二日上午九时逝世。毕生献身于共产主义事业并竭尽全力为人民幸福而奋斗的乌拉季米尔斯基的逝世，是共产党及苏维埃人民的重大损失。”
    另据莫斯科电台三日广播：苏联部长会议已决定成立乌拉季米尔斯基治丧委员会，组成人员如下：苏斯洛夫（主席）、施基利亚托夫、波斯伯洛夫、高尔金、伊格纳捷夫、鲁米扬切夫及克鲁宾。
    【新华社五日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党（布）和苏维埃国家的老领袖、联共中央监察委员会主席、苏联最高苏维埃代表乌拉季米尔斯基的葬仪于四日在莫斯科举行。数十万莫斯科人民瞻仰了乌拉季米尔斯基的遗容。追悼大会在红场举行，在会上讲话的有联共中央委员会书记苏斯洛夫、联共中央委员会委员波斯伯洛夫及联共莫斯科市委会书记鲁米扬切夫。参加葬仪的有联共中央委员会委员及苏联政府人员。乌拉季米尔斯基的骨灰葬于克里姆林宫墙内。
