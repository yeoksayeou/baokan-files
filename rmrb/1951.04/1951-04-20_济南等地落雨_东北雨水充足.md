Title: Rain in Jinan and Other Places; Ample Rainfall in the Northeast
Author:
Date: 1951-04-20
Page: 1

　　Jinan and Other Places Receive Rain
  　Northeast Has Ample Rainfall
    [By Our Correspondent] Following rain in Xi'an and Zhengzhou, the Jinan area of Shandong also experienced a light rain on the afternoon of the 18th, but the rainfall was not significant. The vicinity of Kaifeng, Henan, also had light rain on the afternoon of the 18th. The area near Shijiazhuang, Hebei, received light rain on the morning of the 18th. In other news: This spring, the entire Northeast region has had ample rainfall, and the area near Shanghai in East China has also enjoyed favorable weather.

### 济南等地落雨　东北雨水充足

1951-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　济南等地落雨
  　东北雨水充足
    【本报讯】继西安、郑州落雨后，山东济南一带又于十八日下午微雨，但雨量不大。河南开封附近亦于十八日下午落微雨。河北石家庄附近于十八日上午降小雨。另息：今春东北全区雨水充足，华东上海附近亦风调雨顺。
