Title: Indian People of All Strata Support Signature Campaign
Author:
Date: 1951-04-16
Page: 4
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 14th: The Indian people are enthusiastically supporting the conclusion of a peace pact. The signature campaign for the Peace Pact Declaration is being actively prepared and carried out in the Indian provinces of West Bengal, Mumbai, Bihar, Madras, East Punjab, Assam, and cities such as Calcutta, Mumbai, and Delhi.
　　The Preparatory Committee for the Second All-India Peace Congress launched a signature campaign for the five major powers to conclude a peace pact on India's "National Peace Day" (March 25th). The All-India Trade Union Congress issued a statement in Mumbai calling on Indian workers to sign the Peace Pact Declaration. The All-India Students' Federation called on Indian youth and students to actively participate in the signature collection campaign; and called on them to support the initiative of twenty-eight students to guarantee the collection of 100,000 signatures for the peace pact in order to welcome the upcoming World Festival of Democratic Youth and Students. The Bihar Committee of the Communist Party of India, the Delhi Democratic Youth League and the Democratic Youth Federation, and the leader of the All-India Forward Bloc, Yaji, have all issued calls for a signature campaign in support of the Peace Pact Declaration.
　　In West Bengal, the signature campaign is gaining widespread support from people of all strata. In Midnapore, ten thousand peace supporters held a congress. The majority of those who attended the congress signed the Peace Pact Declaration. The laundry workers of Calcutta and the employees of "Albert David Pharmaceutical Company" signed the Peace Pact Declaration on the 5th and 6th respectively. Farmers in Birbhum district signed the declaration at rallies. Twenty peace committees have been established in Assam and have collected 7,000 signatures for the peace declaration. Residents of Mumbai held a rally on the 5th, passing a resolution in support of the Peace Pact Declaration; and demanding an end to the imperialist massacre in Korea.
　　The Preparatory Committee for the West Bengal Peace Congress has called on peace fighters in the province to collect 200,000 signatures for the Peace Pact Declaration before the Peace Congress is held in the province in the first half of May. Preparations are underway for a multi-party peace conference in Madras; and the signature campaign is also being launched at the same time.

### 印度各阶层人民拥护签名运动

1951-04-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度各阶层人民拥护签名运动
    【本报讯】据新华社新德里十四日电：印度人民热烈拥护缔结和平公约。和平公约宣言的签名运动正在印度西孟加拉、孟买、比哈尔、马德拉斯、东旁遮普、阿萨姆等省以及加尔各答、孟买、德里等城市积极筹备和开展中。
    第二届全印和大筹备委员会在印度“全国和平日”（三月二十五日）发起了要求五大国缔结和平公约的签名运动。全印总工会曾在孟买发表声明，号召印度工人在和平公约宣言上签名。全印学生联合会号召印度青年学生积极参加征集签名运动；并号召他们支持二十八个学生的倡议，保证为和平公约征集十万人签名，以迎接即将到来的世界民主青年和学生联欢节。印度共产党比哈尔委员会、德里民主青年联盟和民主青年联合会以及全印前进集团领袖耶齐，都发出了拥护和平公约宣言的签名运动的号召。
    在西孟加拉，签名运动正获得各阶层人民的普遍拥护。在米德纳浦，一万和平拥护者举行了大会。参加大会的大多数人都在和平公约宣言上签了名。加尔各答洗衣业职工和“阿尔培·大卫制药公司”职工，曾先后于五日和六日在和平公约宣言上签了名。比尔坡姆区农民在举行集会时都在宣言上签了名。阿萨姆成立了二十个和平委员会，并已为和平宣言征集了七千人的签名。孟买居民五日举行集会，通过拥护和平公约宣言的决议；并要求停止帝国主义在朝鲜的屠杀。
    西孟加拉和平大会筹备委员会号召该省的和平斗士，在五月上半月该省举行和平大会之前为和平公约宣言征集二十万人的签名。马德拉斯召开各党派和平会议的筹备工作正在完成中；签名运动也在同时展开。
