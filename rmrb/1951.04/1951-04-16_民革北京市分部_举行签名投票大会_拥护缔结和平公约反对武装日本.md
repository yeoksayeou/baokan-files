Title: Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Beijing Municipal Committee Holds Signature and Voting Meeting to Support the Conclusion of a Peace Pact and Oppose the Arming of Japan
Author:
Date: 1951-04-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th: The Beijing Municipal Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang held a signature and voting meeting on the 14th to support the conclusion of a peace pact and resolutely oppose the arming of Japan. A total of 1,102 members of the Revolutionary Committee in Beijing and working cadres from the central and municipal-level organs of the party attended. Ning Wu, Chairman of the meeting and convener of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Revolutionary Committee, spoke, followed by Cai Tingkai, member of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee, who reported on his impressions of attending the World Peace Council. Then Chen Jiakang was invited to report, and he gave a detailed explanation of the issue of concluding a peace pact and resolutely opposing the arming of Japan. At the meeting, Ning Wu read out the declaration of the World Peace Council on the conclusion of a peace pact, which was signed by all. Then the chairman put forward a vote on the Japanese issue, which was also unanimously approved by all present. Ning Wu reported on the spot that the number of people who signed the declaration and voted on the Japanese issue was 1,102 each, and the audience responded with warm applause. Finally, the participants unanimously shouted slogans such as "Support the conclusion of a peace pact" and "Resolutely oppose the arming of Japan," and the mood was extremely enthusiastic.

### 民革北京市分部  举行签名投票大会  拥护缔结和平公约反对武装日本

1951-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　民革北京市分部
    举行签名投票大会
    拥护缔结和平公约反对武装日本
    【新华社十五日讯】中国国民党革命委员会北京市分部，于十四日举行拥护缔结和平公约坚决反对武装日本签名投票大会。出席民革在北京党员及该党中央与市级各机关工作干部共一千一百零二人。大会主席、民革北京市分部召集人宁武讲话后，由民革中央委员蔡廷锴报告出席世界和平理事会的感想。继请陈家康报告，他对于缔结和平公约和坚决反对武装日本的问题，作了详尽的阐明。大会由宁武宣读世界和平理事会关于缔结和平公约的宣言，经全体签名；再由主席提出关于日本问题的投票，亦经全体一致投票表示赞成。宁武当场报告在宣言上签名及对日本问题投票的人数，各计一千一百零二人，全场报以热烈的掌声。最后，与会者一致高呼“拥护缔结和平公约”、“坚决反对武装日本”等口号，情绪极为热烈。
