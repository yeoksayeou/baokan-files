Author:
Date: 1951-04-17
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Today is the first anniversary of the liberation of Hainan Island. Hainan Island is located south of the Leizhou Peninsula, with blue seas and green forests, which is very beautiful.
    Hainan Island is located in the subtropics and produces coconuts, betel nuts, rubber, and so on. The island's iron ore is world-famous. This is the coastal scenery of Hainan Island.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    今天是海南岛解放一周年纪念。海南岛位在雷州半岛以南，碧海绿林，十分美丽。
    海南岛地处亚热带，产椰子、槟榔、橡胶等等。该岛的铁矿，是世界著名的。这是海南岛的滨海风光。
