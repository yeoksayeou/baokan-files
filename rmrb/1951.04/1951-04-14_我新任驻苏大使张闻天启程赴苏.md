Title: My Newly Appointed Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Zhang Wentian, Departs for the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1951-04-14
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 13th: My newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Soviet Union, Zhang Wentian, along with his entourage, departed Beijing by plane on the morning of the 13th to take up his post in Moscow. Those who went to the airport to see him off included: Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Zhu Zaoguan, Deputy Director of the General Office; Yu Xinqing, Director of the Central People's Government Bureau of Protocol; Zhang Hanfu and Wu Xiuquan, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs; Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Li Guozhang, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Public Security; Hu Renkui, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Trade; Sha Kefu, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Culture; Yang Shangkun, Director of the General Office of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League; Jiang Nanxiang, Deputy Secretary; Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the China People's Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea General Association; Shao Lizi, Standing Committee Member; Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Qiao Guanhua, Vice President; Zhao Zhongchi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and other individuals, totaling over a hundred people. Soviet Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Mr. and Mrs. Roshchin, along with embassy staff, also went to see him off. (Attached picture)
　　Ambassador Zhang Wentian raised his hand to thank those who came to see him off after boarding the plane. News Photography Bureau, Qi Guanshan Photo

### 我新任驻苏大使张闻天启程赴苏

1951-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　我新任驻苏大使张闻天启程赴苏
    【新华社十三日讯】中华人民共和国新任驻苏联特命全权大使张闻天偕随员一行，于十三日上午乘飞机离北京赴莫斯科莅任。前往机场欢送者有：人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻，办公厅副主任朱早观，中央人民政府典礼局局长余心清，外交部副部长章汉夫、伍修权，办公厅主任王炳南，公安部办公厅主任李国章，贸易部办公厅主任胡仁奎，文化部办公厅主任沙可夫，中国共产党中央办公厅主任杨尚昆，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬，副书记蒋南翔，中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席陈叔通，常务委员邵力子，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，副会长乔冠华，中苏友好协会总会副总干事赵仲池，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等百余人。苏联驻我国特命全权大使罗申夫妇及大使馆人员亦前往欢送。（附图片）
