Title: Maintain World Peace and Strive for the Conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact! People of All Countries Actively Prepare to Celebrate May Day
Author:
Date: 1951-04-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd dispatch: The working people of Britain, the United States, Denmark, Norway, Uruguay, and other countries are actively preparing to celebrate International Labor Day on May 1st. Workers in London will hold a May Day celebration rally and a grand demonstration to defend peace on May 1st. Their slogans for celebrating May Day are: "The British people defend world peace," "Eisenhower, go back to America," "Cut capitalist profits," "Reduce prices," and "Advance towards socialism," etc.

Vance, secretary of the Joint Committee for May Day Celebrations by American Trade Unions and People, issued a statement saying that many local branches of American trade unions have expressed support for the Joint Committee's call to hold May Day demonstrations.

The Executive Committee of the Central Committee of the Danish Communist Party has issued a May Day message, calling on Danish Communists, Social Democrats, and non-partisan workers to unite and fight to maintain peace and improve living conditions, and unanimously demand that the five major powers conclude a peace pact. The message also put forward the following slogans for celebrating May Day: "Oppose the militarization of Denmark, oppose the extension of military service, oppose raising prices, oppose the re-militarization of West Germany, Americans get out of Denmark, Denmark withdraws from the North Atlantic Treaty, Long live peace, Long live socialism!"

Many trade union organizations in Norway have decided to closely integrate the struggle to defend peace and improve workers' living conditions with the activities of celebrating May Day. Twelve trade unions in Bergen recently jointly published an open letter, calling on the working people of Bergen to hold May Day demonstrations under the following slogans: "Oppose the expansion of armaments and war preparations! Defend peace and improve living conditions! Strive for Norway's withdrawal from the North Atlantic Treaty!"

The printing, transportation, and textile workers in Montevideo, the capital of Uruguay, the flour workers in San Jose, the construction industry trade union and dockworkers' trade union in Paysandú, and the workers in Salto have recently held special meetings, deciding on specific methods for celebrating May Day. Uruguayan farmers are more active in the preparations for celebrating May Day than in the past. The slogan for the Uruguayan working people to celebrate May Day is: "Proletarians unite, strive for peace, strive for national liberation, strive for national progress, strive for the conclusion of a five-power peace pact."

### 维护世界和平争取缔结五大国和平公约！  各国人民积极筹备庆祝五一节

1951-04-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　维护世界和平争取缔结五大国和平公约！
　  各国人民积极筹备庆祝五一节
    【新华社二十三日讯】英、美、丹麦、挪威、乌拉圭等国劳动人民正积极筹备庆祝五一国际劳动节。伦敦的工人将于五月一日举行劳动节庆祝大会和保卫和平的盛大示威游行。他们庆祝五一节的口号是：“英国人民保卫世界和平”、“艾森豪威尔滚回美国去”、“削减资本家利润”、“降低物价”和“向社会主义前进”等。
    美国工会与人民庆祝五一节联合委员会秘书万斯道发表声明称：美国许多工会的地方分会已表示支持联合委员会关于举行五一节示威游行的号召。
    丹麦共产党中央委员会执行委员会已发出五一节文告，号召丹麦共产党、社会民主党及无党派的工人们团结起来，为维护和平及改善生活条件而斗争，并一致要求五大国缔结和平公约。文告并提出了庆祝五一节的口号如下：“反对丹麦军国主义化，反对延长兵役期限，反对抬高物价，反对西德重新军国主义化，美国人滚出丹麦去，丹麦脱离北大西洋公约，和平万岁、社会主义万岁！”
    挪威的许多工会组织已决定把保卫和平及改善工人生活条件的斗争，与庆祝五一节的活动密切结合起来。卑尔根十二个工会最近联合发表公开信，号召卑尔根劳动人民在下列口号下举行五一节示威游行：“反对扩张军备与战争准备！保卫和平与改善生活条件！争取挪威退出北大西洋公约！”
    乌拉圭首都蒙得维的亚的印刷、运输和纺织工人，圣约瑟的面粉工人，派桑杜的建筑业工会和码头工人工会以及萨尔托的工人们，最近纷纷举行特别会议，决定了庆祝五一节的具体办法。乌拉圭的农民在庆祝五一节的筹备工作中比过去更加活跃。乌拉圭劳动人民庆祝五一节的口号是：“无产阶级团结起来，争取和平，争取民族解放，争取国家进步，争取缔结五大国和平公约。”
