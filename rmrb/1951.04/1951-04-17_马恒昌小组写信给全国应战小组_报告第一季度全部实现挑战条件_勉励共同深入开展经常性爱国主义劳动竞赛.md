Author:

Date: 1951-04-17
Page: 2

The Ma Hengchang Team Writes to National Challenge Teams
Reports Complete Fulfillment of First Quarter Challenge Conditions
Encouraging Jointly Deepening Regular Patriotic Labor Emulation

　　The Ma Hengchang Team wrote to challenge teams across the country, reporting on the team's production situation in the first quarter and encouraging everyone to jointly strive to continue deepening the regular patriotic labor emulation. The original letter is as follows:

Dear Comrades of Challenge Teams across the Country:

　　Since we launched the challenge, we have received your enthusiastic response, which has greatly encouraged the production enthusiasm of all workers in our team. In the first quarter, we have achieved all challenge conditions. Nine apprentices are now able to operate lathes, and five apprentices can work independently; the attendance rate has reached 100%; the quality has reached 99.75%; the whole quarter completed the processing task of 652 types of products totaling 3,143 pieces; technicians have initially learned the "preliminary operation method of high-speed cutting." This is due to the correct leadership of the factory administration, the CCP Committee, and the Trade Union, as well as your enthusiastic encouragement. Although we have achieved initial results, we will never be arrogant and are determined to learn from your new experiences to enrich and improve our team. We have received letters from brotherly teams, but due to time constraints and writing ability, we cannot reply to each one, so we apologize to you here. We believe that with your help and our own efforts, we will continuously correct existing shortcomings and continuously improve and advance. May we unite and help each other and make progress together!

　　Comrades: We must consolidate our existing achievements, continue to deepen the regular patriotic labor emulation to build our country's strong national defense and economic strength, defend the security of the motherland, and safeguard peace in Asia and the world.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Northeast Fifth Machine Factory Ma Hengchang Team
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Xinhua News Agency)

### 马恒昌小组写信给全国应战小组  报告第一季度全部实现挑战条件  勉励共同深入开展经常性爱国主义劳动竞赛

1951-04-17
第2版()
专栏：

　　马恒昌小组写信给全国应战小组
    报告第一季度全部实现挑战条件
    勉励共同深入开展经常性爱国主义劳动竞赛
    马恒昌小组写信给全国各地应战小组，报告该组第一季度的生产情况，并勉励大家共同为继续深入开展经常性的爱国主义劳动竞赛而奋斗。原信如下：全国各地亲爱的应战小组同志们：
    自我们提出挑战后，得到了你们的热烈响应，大大鼓舞了我们全组工友的生产热情。在第一季度里，我们已实现了各项挑战条件。九名徒工已能上床子，五名徒工能独立工作；出勤率达到百分之百；质量达到百分之九十九点七五；全季完成了六百五十二种计三千一百四十三件产品的加工任务；技工初步学会了“高速度切削初步操作方法”。这是本厂行政、中共党委和工会的正确领导，以及你们的热情鼓舞所获得的。现在我们虽已获得初步成绩，但是我们决不骄傲，并决心学习你们的新经验来充实和提高我们小组。我们曾接到各兄弟小组的来信，但因为时间限制和写作能力的关系，不能一一复信，特在这里向你们致歉。我们相信：由于你们的帮助和我们自己的努力，将使我们不断地改正现存缺点，不断地提高与前进。愿我们互相团结帮助，共同进步！
    同志们：我们要巩固已有的成绩，继续深入开展经常性的爱国主义劳动竞赛，来建设我们祖国强大的国防力量与经济力量，保卫祖国的安全，维护亚洲与世界的和平。
            东北第五机器厂马恒昌小组启
                （新华社）
