Title: All-China Women's Federation Sends Congratulatory Messages to the International Democratic Women's Federation and Women's Organizations in the Soviet Union and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1951-04-30
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: The All-China Democratic Women's Federation has sent a telegram to the International Democratic Women's Federation, congratulating them on the "May Day" Labor Day. The telegram stated: We sincerely wish that the ranks united under the International Democratic Women's Federation will grow stronger in the ranks of world peace defenders. Chinese women will continue to struggle under the leadership of the International Democratic Women's Federation to defend world peace, maintain Asian security, and fight for the survival and happiness of women and children.
　　
    Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: The All-China Democratic Women's Federation has sent a telegram to the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, congratulating them on the "May Day" Labor Day. The telegram stated: "Let us join hands more closely to resolutely crush the aggression of US imperialism and any war instigation. In the future, our Chinese sisters will learn from your glorious example of hard work and building a happy new society with even greater enthusiasm." At the same time, the Federation also sent telegrams to the Korean Democratic Women's League, the German Democratic Women's Federation, the Central Council of Working Women of the Mongolian People's Republic, the Hungarian Democratic Women's Alliance, the Albanian Women's Union, the Vietnamese Democratic Women's Federation, the Czechoslovak Women's Federation, the Polish Women's League, the Romanian Democratic Women's Federation, the Bulgarian Democratic Women's Federation, and the Chinese People's Volunteers, expressing warm congratulations and wishing them greater victories in the common struggle to defend world peace.

### 全国妇联给国际妇联和苏联等国妇女团体的贺电

1951-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　全国妇联给国际妇联和苏联等国妇女团体的贺电
    【新华社二十九日讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会顷致电国际民主妇女联合会，祝贺“五一”劳动节。贺电称：谨祝在全世界和平保卫者的队伍中，团结在国际民主妇联下的队伍日益壮大。中国妇女必将继续在国际民主妇联领导下，为保卫世界和平，维护亚洲安全，争取妇女与儿童的生存和幸福而斗争。
    【新华社二十九日讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会顷致电苏联妇女反法西斯委员会，祝贺“五一”劳动节。贺电称：“让我们更紧密地携起手来，坚决粉碎美帝国主义的侵略和任何的战争挑拨。今后中国的姊妹们将以更高涨的热忱，学习你们艰苦奋斗和建设幸福新社会的光辉榜样。”与此同时，该会并分别致电朝鲜民主妇女总同盟、德国民主妇女联合会、蒙古人民共和国劳动妇女中央理事会、匈牙利民主妇女联盟、阿尔巴尼亚妇女联合会、越南民主妇女联合会、捷克妇女联合会、波兰妇女联盟、罗马尼亚民主妇女联合会、保加利亚民主妇女联合会及中国人民志愿军等，表示热烈的祝贺，并预祝在保卫世界和平的共同斗争中，获得更大的胜利。
