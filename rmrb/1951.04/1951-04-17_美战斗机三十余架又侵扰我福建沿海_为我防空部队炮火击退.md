Title: More Than Thirty U.S. Fighter Planes Intrude Upon the Coastal Areas of Fujian Again; Repelled by Our Air Defense Forces' Artillery Fire
Author:
Date: 1951-04-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 16th: Following the intrusion of a large number of American planes into the coastal areas of Fujian Province on the 11th, on the morning of the 13th, at approximately 9:00 AM, more than thirty American jet fighters intruded into the airspace over Fuzhou, Changle, Chongwu (southeast of Hui'an), Xiamen, and other areas of Fujian Province for reconnaissance, and were repelled by the artillery fire of our air defense forces.

### 美战斗机三十余架又侵扰我福建沿海  为我防空部队炮火击退

1951-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　美战斗机三十余架又侵扰我福建沿海
　  为我防空部队炮火击退
    【新华社十六日讯】继十一日大批美机侵扰我国福建省沿海地区后，十三日上午九时许，又有美国喷气式战斗机三十余架侵入我福建省的福州、长乐、崇武（在惠安东南）、厦门等地上空盘旋侦察，当为我防空部队的炮火击退。
