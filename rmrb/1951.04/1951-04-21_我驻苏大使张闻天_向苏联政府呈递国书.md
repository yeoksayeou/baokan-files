Title: Our Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Zhang Wentian, Presents Credentials to the Soviet Government
Author:
Date: 1951-04-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 19th. Zhang Wentian, the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of our country to the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, accompanied by Zeng Yongquan, Minister-Counselor of the Embassy, Counselors Ge Baoquan and Zhang Guan, Commercial Counselors Jiang Zemin and Wang Mingzhen, Military Attaché Ji He, and eleven other embassy staff members, went to the Kremlin on the 18th to present his credentials to Nikolai Shvernik, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. The following individuals participated in the ceremony: Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Zorin, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Gorkin, Director of the First Far Eastern Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs Tunkin, Deputy Director Kurdyukov, and Director of the Protocol Department Kurazhenkov. Ambassador Zhang Wentian delivered a eulogy before presenting his credentials. Shvernik delivered a reply after the presentation of credentials. Ambassador Zhang Wentian then introduced the embassy staff to Shvernik. After the ceremony, everyone posed for a photograph, and Ambassador Zhang Wentian also spoke with Shvernik.

### 我驻苏大使张闻天  向苏联政府呈递国书

1951-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　我驻苏大使张闻天
    向苏联政府呈递国书
    【新华社莫斯科十九日电】我国驻苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟特命全权大使张闻天，于十八日偕大使馆公使衔参赞曾涌泉，参赞戈宝权、张观，商务参赞江泽民、汪名震，武官吉合及使馆人员等十一人，赴克里姆林宫向苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席什维尔尼克呈递国书。参加典礼者有：苏联外交部副部长佐林，苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书高尔金，苏联外交部第一远东司司长童金，副司长库尔久科夫，交际司司长库拉任柯夫。张闻天大使在呈递国书前致颂词。呈递国书后什维尔尼克致答词。张闻天大使继介绍大使馆人员与什维尔尼克相见。典礼完成后全体摄影，张闻天大使并与什维尔尼克谈话。
