    Our Great Motherland
    From the production competition of Red May last year to today's patriotic production competition to resist US aggression and aid Korea, workers across the country have overfulfilled their production tasks with unparalleled enthusiasm. This is a picture of the workers at the Liulihe Cement Plant in the production competition.

    我们伟大的祖国
    全国工人从去年红五月的生产竞赛，一直到今天的抗美援朝爱国主义生产竞赛期间，以无比的热情超额完成了生产任务。这是在生产竞赛中的琉璃河水泥厂的工人。
