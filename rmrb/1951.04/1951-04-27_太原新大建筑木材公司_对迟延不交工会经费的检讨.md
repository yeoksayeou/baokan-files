Author:

Page: 2

A Response to Readers' Criticisms and Suggestions in the People's Daily

Taiyuan Xin Da Building Timber Company
Self-Criticism for Delay in Paying Trade Union Funds

Editor:
We sincerely accept the criticism of our refusal to pay trade union funds in your newspaper's March 21st "Readers' Letters" page. Our self-criticism is as follows:

In early October 1950, Comrade Shi Hailin of the Taiyuan Municipal Federation of Trade Unions came to our company to urge the payment of trade union funds. At that time, the company's supervisory committee member had gone to Hankou to purchase timber, and the manager was sick in the hospital. In addition, last year our company was under the leadership of the agency's production committee and had not yet joined the trade union. Therefore, the accounting department told the comrades of the trade union: that they did not understand how the trade union funds were spent, and would pay if it was appropriate after discussing it with the leaders. As a result, the trade union funds were not paid on time, and the trade union funds for August and September were only paid in late October last year. We are a public enterprise and should be a model for abiding by and implementing government laws and regulations. However, due to our insufficient study and understanding of policies, the carelessness of many cadres in the company, and the serious bureaucracy in leadership and failure to conduct timely inspections, the above errors were caused. We must take this as a warning and strengthen the policy and ideological education of all employees in the future, and resolutely abide by government laws and regulations.

### 太原新大建筑木材公司  对迟延不交工会经费的检讨

1951-04-27
第2版()
专栏：对人民日报读者批评建议的反应

　　太原新大建筑木材公司
    对迟延不交工会经费的检讨编辑同志：
    贵报三月二十一日读者来信专页上对我们拒交工会经费的批评，我们诚恳接受。兹作检讨如下：
    一九五○年十月上旬，太原市总工会石海林同志到本公司催缴工会经费。当时公司的监委赴汉口购木料，经理病在医院，加以去年本公司属机关生产委员会领导，尚未参加工会，因此会计科就对工会的同志说：不了解工会经费怎样开支，待和领导同志研究后该交就交。这样工会经费就未能按期交纳，去年十月下旬才交八、九月份的工会经费。我们是公营企业，应该是遵守和执行政府法令的模范，但由于我们对政策的学习了解不够，公司不少干部工作马虎，领导上存在着严重的官僚主义，未及时检查，因而造成上述错误。我们当引以为戒，今后定要加强全体职工的政策思想教育，并坚决遵守政府法令。
          山西太原市新大建筑木材公司
