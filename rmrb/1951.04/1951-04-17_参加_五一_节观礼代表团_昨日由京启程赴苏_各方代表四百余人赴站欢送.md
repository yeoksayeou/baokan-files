Title: "May Day" Observation Delegation Departs from Beijing for Soviet Union Yesterday; Representatives from All Sides See Them Off at the Station

Author:

Date: 1951-04-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association delegation to the Soviet Union to participate in the "May Day" celebrations, led by Head of Delegation Lin Boqu and Deputy Head of Delegation Shen Junru, departed from Beijing at 10:00 PM on the 16th. The delegation consists of 28 people, including 24 representatives of workers, farmers, the military, women, the scientific community, the cultural community, and staff members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, as well as 4 staff members.

More than 400 people came to the station to see them off, including Li Jishen and Huang Yanpei, Vice Presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, as well as directors and staff of the Association, representatives from various units of the Central People's Government and various people's organizations, and people from cultural and educational circles. Soviet Ambassador to China, Mr. Roshchin, and members of the Soviet Embassy also came to the station to see them off.

Huang Yanpei, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, delivered a brief farewell speech at the station, explaining the important significance of the delegation's visit to the Soviet Union to participate in the "May Day" celebrations, and wishing all the representatives a victorious return.

Head of Delegation Lin Boqu gave a reply. He said: "The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association delegation is very honored to have the opportunity to go to our great ally, the Soviet Union, to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations. In addition to attending the celebrations, we will also try to contact the Soviet people as widely as possible. On the one hand, we will learn from the Soviet people's rich experience in struggle and construction, and on the other hand, we will also tell our friends about the Chinese people's work in resisting U.S. aggression and defending world peace, so as to strengthen the friendship and unity between the Chinese and Soviet people, and use this invincible force to oppose U.S. aggression and defend world peace."

Before boarding the train, staff members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association presented flowers to each representative.

After participating in the "May Day" celebrations in the Soviet Union, some of the representatives will also go to Berlin to attend the founding meeting of the German-Chinese Friendship Association.

### 参加“五一”节观礼代表团  昨日由京启程赴苏  各方代表四百余人赴站欢送

1951-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　参加“五一”节观礼代表团
　  昨日由京启程赴苏
　  各方代表四百余人赴站欢送
    【新华社十六日讯】中苏友好协会赴苏参加“五一”节观礼代表团由林伯渠团长、沈钧儒副团长率领，于十六日晚十时离京启程。该代表团共计二十八人，包括工人、农民、部队、妇女、科学界、文化界及中苏友好协会工作者代表二十四人，工作人员四人。
    到车站欢送者，有中苏友好协会总会副会长李济深、黄炎培及该会理事、干事，中央人民政府各单位和各人民团体的代表，文化教育界人士，共四百余人。苏联驻华大使馆罗申大使及该使馆人员亦到车站欢送。
    中苏友好协会总会副会长黄炎培在车站致简短欢送词，说明代表团赴苏参加“五一”观礼的重要意义，并祝全体代表胜利归来。
    代表团团长林伯渠致答词。他说：“中苏友好协会代表团能有机会到我们伟大的盟邦苏联去参加“五一”国际劳动节的观礼，我们感到非常的荣幸。我们此去除了观礼，还要尽可能广泛地和苏联人民接触，一方面学习苏联人民丰富的斗争经验和建设经验，另一方面我们也将把中国人民抗美援朝、保卫世界和平的工作告诉我们的朋友，以加强中苏两国人民的友好团结，并用这一无敌的力量，来反对美国侵略，保卫世界和平。”
    代表登车前，由中苏友好协会总会及北京市中苏友好协会工作人员向各代表献花。
    该代表团在苏联参加“五一”节观礼后，其中一部分代表并将赴柏林参加德中友好协会成立大会。
