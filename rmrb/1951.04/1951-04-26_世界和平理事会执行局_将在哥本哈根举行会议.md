Title: World Peace Council Executive Bureau to Hold Meeting in Copenhagen
Author:
Date: 1951-04-26
Page: 4

　　World Peace Council Executive Bureau
　  Will Hold Meeting in Copenhagen
    [Xinhua News Agency, Prague, April 24th] According to a TASS report from Prague on the 24th, the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council will hold a meeting from May 5th to 9th. After exchanging views with the Danish Peace Committee, the Secretariat of the World Peace Council plans to hold the meeting in Copenhagen, the capital of Denmark.

### 世界和平理事会执行局  将在哥本哈根举行会议

1951-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　世界和平理事会执行局
　  将在哥本哈根举行会议
    【新华社二十五日讯】据塔斯社布拉格二十四日讯：世界和平理事会执行局将于五月五日至九日举行会议。世界和平理事会秘书处与丹麦和平委员会交换意见后，准备以丹麦首都哥本哈根为会议地址。
