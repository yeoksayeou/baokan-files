Title: Today is the National Day of Hungary　Chairman Mao and Foreign Minister Zhou Send Congratulatory Telegrams
Author:
Date: 1951-04-04
Page: 1
　　
　　Today is the National Day of Hungary
    Chairman Mao and Foreign Minister Zhou Send Congratulatory Telegrams
    [Xinhua News Agency, 3rd] April 4th is the National Day of the Hungarian People's Republic. Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai sent telegrams to Chairman Sandor Ronai of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic and Foreign Minister Gyula Kallai on April 3rd, respectively, to express their congratulations.

### 今天是匈牙利国庆纪念日  毛主席及周外长致电祝贺

1951-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　今天是匈牙利国庆纪念日        
    毛主席及周外长致电祝贺
    【新华社三日讯】四月四日为匈牙利人民共和国国庆纪念日。我中央人民政府毛泽东主席及外交部周恩来部长曾于四月三日分别致电匈牙利人民共和国主席团桑德·罗奈伊主席及外交部古拉·卡莱部长，表示祝贺。
