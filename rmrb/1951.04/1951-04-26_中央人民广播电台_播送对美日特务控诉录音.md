Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Recordings of Accusations Against American and Japanese Spies
Author:
Date: 1951-04-26
Page: 3

The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast accusations from people across the country against the crimes of American and Japanese imperialism and their running dogs, the Chiang Kai-shek bandit agents, during the "Special Program for Defending World Peace and Opposing American Armament of Japan" from April 26th to 29th.

April 26th: From 18:00 to 18:30, a recording of the accusation by Chen Jianzhen, Chairman of the Anglican Bishop's Court of the China Anglican Church in Wuhu, at the "Conference on Handling Christian Organizations Accepting American Subsidies," against Zhu Youyu, former Anglican Bishop and General Secretary of the Anglican Central Office. From 18:30 to 19:30, a recording of the accusation by Cui Xianxiang, General Secretary of the National General Assembly of the Chinese Christian Church in Shanghai, at the same conference, against Bi Fanyu, former Rural Church Work Secretary of the National General Assembly of the Christian Church and an American spy. From 19:30 to 20:00, a recording of the accusation by Chen Chonggui, President of Chongqing Theological Seminary, at the same conference, against American imperialism for distorting Christian doctrine and carrying out cultural aggression against China.

April 27th: From 18:00 to 18:40, recordings of accusations by Xu Yingchao, a professor at Beijing Normal University, and Liu Pengzhi, a student at Beijing Hebei High School, at the "Enlarged Joint Conference of People's Representatives from All Circles in Beijing," against the atrocities of Kuomintang agents. From 18:40 to 19:20, a recording of a speech by Han Wenqing, a housewife from the Second District of Beijing, at the "Beijing Women's Broadcast Accusation Meeting Against American Armament of Japan." From 19:20 to 20:00, a recording of the accusation by Wang Shuyun, a housewife from the Sixth District of Beijing, against traitorous agents.

April 28th: Recordings of accusations from people of all circles in Tianjin, Shanghai, Chongqing and other places against the crimes of American and Japanese imperialism and their running dogs, the Chiang Kai-shek bandit agents.

April 29th: Recordings of broadcast accusation meetings against American and Japanese imperialism by the people on the Yalu River border and workers in major factories and mines in Northeast China, and recordings of the anti-revolutionary accusation meeting by representatives from all circles in Wuhan.

### 中央人民广播电台  播送对美日特务控诉录音

1951-04-26
第3版()
专栏：

　　中央人民广播电台
    播送对美日特务控诉录音
    中央人民广播电台二十六日至二十九日“保卫世界和平、反对美国武装日本特别节目”播送全国人民对美、日帝国主义及其走狗蒋匪特务罪行的控诉。
    二十六日：十八点到十八点三十分，芜湖中华圣公会主教院主席主教陈见真在“处理接受美国津贴的基督教团体会议”上，对前圣公会主教、圣公会中央办事处总干事朱友渔的控诉录音。十八点三十分到十九点三十分，上海中华基督教会全国总会总干事崔宪详在同一会议上对前基督教会全国总会乡村教会事工干事、美国特务毕范宇的控诉录音。十九点三十分到二十点，重庆神学院院长陈崇桂在同一会议上，对美帝国主义歪曲基督教教义，对中国进行文化侵略的控诉录音。
    二十七日：十八点到十八点四十分，北京师范大学教授徐英超和北京河北高师学生刘鹏志在“北京市市区各界人民代表扩大联席会议”上，对国民党特务暴行的控诉录音。十八点四十分到十九点二十分，北京市二区家庭妇女韩文清在“北京妇女反对美国武装日本广播控诉大会”上发言的录音。十九点二十分到二十点，北京市六区家庭妇女王淑芸对汉奸特务的控诉录音。
    二十八日：天津、上海、重庆等地各界人民对美、日帝国主义及其走狗蒋匪特务罪行的控诉录音。
    二十九日：鸭绿江边人民及东北各重要工厂、矿山工人对美、日帝国主义广播控诉大会和武汉各界人民代表控诉反革命大会的录音。
