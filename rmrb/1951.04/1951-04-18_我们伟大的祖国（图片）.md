Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-04-18
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    In order to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea and greet the May 1st Labor Day in 1951, the working class of New China is intensifying the patriotic production competition. This is the State-owned Shanghai No. 3 Cotton Textile Factory, which is working hard to produce high-quality 20-count yarn. The female workers in the packaging room are packing briskly.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为了抗美援朝和迎接一九五一年的五一劳动节，新中国的工人阶级正加紧爱国主义的生产竞赛。这是国营上海第三棉纺织厂，正努力生产质量优良的二十支纱。打包间的女工们敏捷地在进行打包。
