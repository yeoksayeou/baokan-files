Title: Chinese Delegation Leaves Czechoslovakia for Home After the World Federation of Scientific Workers Congress Concludes
Author:
Date: 1951-04-26
Page: 4
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 25th - The Chinese delegation to the Second Congress of the World Federation of Scientific Workers, led by Liang Xi and Mao Yisheng, left Prague on the 24th via Moscow to return home. After the congress, the delegation visited many cultural and scientific institutions in Czechoslovakia. Before leaving Czechoslovakia, the delegation issued a statement expressing gratitude to the Czech government for its warm hospitality, stating: "We have witnessed the victory of socialism and the people's democratic system. We believe that the cause of peace will surely triumph."

### 世界科协代表大会闭幕后  我国代表团离捷返国

1951-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　世界科协代表大会闭幕后
　  我国代表团离捷返国
    【新华社布拉格二十五日电】由梁希、茅以升率领的中国出席世界科学工作者协会第二届代表大会的代表团，于二十四日离布拉格取道莫斯科回国。代表团在大会闭幕后，曾参观了捷克斯洛伐克的许多文化与科学机关，代表团在离捷前发表声明，对捷克政府的热诚款待表示感谢，声明称：“我们已经目睹社会主义和人民民主制度的胜利。我们相信和平事业必定获胜。”
