Date: 1951-04-04

    On April 4, 1945, the great Soviet army liberated Hungary.
    The picture shows the Liberty Statue on Gellért Hill in Hungary, symbolizing the liberation of the Hungarian people and expressing eternal gratitude to the Soviet army.
