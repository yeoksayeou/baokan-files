Title: At a Conference on Handling Christian Organizations Receiving American Subsidies, Vice Premier Guo Called for Active Participation in the Patriotic Movement; Representatives Accused Imperialist Elements and Reactionary Elements Within the Church of Crimes
Author:
Date: 1951-04-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 19th -- The conference on handling Christian organizations receiving American subsidies, convened by the Religious Affairs Department of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the State Council of the Central People's Government, was held for the fourth day today. The conference listened to a report by Vice Premier Guo Moruo of the State Council. Subsequently, the representatives spoke one after another, vehemently denouncing the crimes of imperialist elements and reactionary elements within the Christian Church.

　　After analyzing the current domestic and international situation, Guo Moruo said: Patriotic religious figures should be with the people of the whole country and actively participate in the great Resist America, Aid Korea patriotic movement, participate in the signing of the Peace Covenant Declaration and voting on the Japanese issue, and participate in the May Day demonstration; so as to further raise their thinking in this patriotic movement and completely sever all ties with American imperialism.

　　The conference began the denunciations in a passionate atmosphere. Those who successively took the stage to denounce the criminal activities of imperialist elements and reactionary elements within the church included Cui Xianxiang, General Secretary of the National General Assembly of the Church of Christ in China; Shao Jingsan, General Secretary of the Nanjing Church of Christ in China; Shen Derong, editor of Tian Feng Weekly; Shi Zhongyi, executive of the Jiangsu-Zhejiang Christian Rural Service Federation; Shi Ruzhang, director of the Student Department of the National Association of the Young Women's Christian Association of China; Jiang Changchuan, Bishop of the North China Diocese of the Methodist Church of China; Li Muqun, pastor of the Chongqing Lutheran Church; Jiang Wenhan, Deputy General Secretary of the National Association of the Young Men's Christian Association of China; Hu Yiyun, General Secretary of the Guangdong Christian Association; and Hu Zuyin, Honorary General Secretary of the Guang Xue Hui. The conference will continue the denunciations tomorrow.

### 在处理接受美国津贴的基督教团体会议上  郭副总理号召积极参加爱国运动  各代表控诉教会内帝国主义分子和反动分子的罪恶

1951-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　在处理接受美国津贴的基督教团体会议上
    郭副总理号召积极参加爱国运动
    各代表控诉教会内帝国主义分子和反动分子的罪恶
    【新华社十九日讯】中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会宗教事务处召集的处理接受美国津贴的基督教团体会议，今日为第四天。大会听了政务院副总理郭沫若的报告。接着，各代表纷纷发言，激昂地控诉基督教会内部的帝国主义分子和反动分子的罪恶。
    郭副总理在分析了目前的国内外形势后说：爱国的宗教界人士，应该和全国人民在一起，积极参加伟大的抗美援朝爱国运动，参加和平公约宣言的签名和对日本问题的投票，参加五一节的大示威；以便在这一爱国运动中把思想提高一步，彻底和美帝国主义斩断一切联系。
    大会在激昂的气氛中开始了控诉。相继登台控诉教会内帝国主义分子和反动分子的罪恶活动的，有中华基督教会全国总会总干事崔宪详、南京中华基督教会总干事邵镜三，天风周刊主编沈德溶，江浙基督教乡村服务联合会干事施中一，中华基督教女青年会全国协会学生部主任施如璋，中华基督教卫理公会华北教区会督江长川，重庆路德会牧师李牧群，中华基督教青年会全国协会副总干事江文汉，广东基督教协进会总干事胡翼云及广学会义务总干事胡祖荫等。大会将在明日继续进行控诉。
