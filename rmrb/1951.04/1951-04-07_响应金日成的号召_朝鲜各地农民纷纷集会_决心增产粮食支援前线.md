Title: Responding to Kim Il-sung's Call, Farmers in Various Parts of Korea Gathered to Resolve to Increase Grain Production to Support the Front Lines
Author: 
Date: 1951-04-07
Page: 4

　　Responding to Kim Il-sung's Call
　  Farmers in Various Parts of Korea Gathered
　  Resolved to Increase Grain Production to Support the Front Lines
    [Xinhua News Agency, 6th] According to TASS, Pyongyang, 5th: Farmers in villages throughout South Pyongan Province, Korea, held mass meetings to discuss the "Letter to the People" from the Central Committee of the Fatherland Front and the instructions given by General Kim Il-sung in his talks with farmers in South Pyongan Province. In his talks with the farmers, General Kim Il-sung emphasized that the struggle to increase grain production is a struggle to win victory on the front lines and to win freedom for the fatherland. The farmers declared their determination to overcome all difficulties, victoriously complete the spring plowing, and fulfill their responsibilities in the Fatherland Liberation War. At all the meetings, the farmers passed resolutions expressing their determination to do everything possible to increase grain production to support the front lines. They also sent telegrams to salute the heroic soldiers of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers.

### 响应金日成的号召  朝鲜各地农民纷纷集会  决心增产粮食支援前线

1951-04-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　响应金日成的号召
　  朝鲜各地农民纷纷集会
　  决心增产粮食支援前线
    【新华社六日讯】据塔斯社平壤五日讯：朝鲜平安南道各村庄的农民纷纷举行群众大会，讨论祖国战线中央委员会的告人民书和金日成将军与平安南道农民谈话时所作的指示。金日成将军在与农民的谈话中强调，增加粮食生产的斗争，就是争取前线胜利和争取祖国自由的斗争。农民们声明他们决心克服一切困难，胜利地完成春耕，负起他们在祖国解放战争中的责任。在所有的集会上，农民们都通过决议，表示他们决心尽一切努力，增加粮食生产支援前线。他们并发出电报向英勇的朝鲜人民军将士和中国人民志愿军致敬。
