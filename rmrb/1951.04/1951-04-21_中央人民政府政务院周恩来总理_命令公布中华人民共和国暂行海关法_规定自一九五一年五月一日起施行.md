Title: Premier Zhou Enlai of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government Orders the Promulgation of the Interim Customs Law of the People's Republic of China, Stipulating Implementation from May 1, 1951
Author:
Date: 1951-04-21
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 20th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the Government Administration Council issued an order on April 18th to promulgate the Interim Customs Law of the People's Republic of China, which was passed by the 77th Government Administration Council meeting. The order stipulates that the Interim Customs Law will be implemented from May 1, 1951. From the date of implementation, all rules, regulations, and laws related to customs business that conflict with the Interim Customs Law of the People's Republic of China shall be abolished.

### 中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理  命令公布中华人民共和国暂行海关法  规定自一九五一年五月一日起施行

1951-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理
    命令公布中华人民共和国暂行海关法
    规定自一九五一年五月一日起施行
    【新华社二十日讯】周恩来总理于四月十八日命令公布政务院第七十七次政务会议通过的中华人民共和国暂行海关法。命令规定该暂行海关法自一九五一年五月一日起施行。自施行之日起，一切有关海关业务的章则法令，凡与中华人民共和国暂行海关法有抵触者，应一律废止。
