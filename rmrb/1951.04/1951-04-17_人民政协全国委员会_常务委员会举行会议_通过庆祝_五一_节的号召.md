Title: National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Standing Committee Holds Meeting, Adopts Appeal to Celebrate "May Day"

Author: 
Date: 1951-04-17
Page: 1

　　People's Political Consultative Conference National Committee
　　Standing Committee Holds Meeting
　　Adopts Appeal to Celebrate "May Day"
    [Xinhua News Agency, 16th] The Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference held a meeting on the afternoon of the 16th. Attending and present at this meeting were members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee in Beijing, members of the Central People's Government Committee, government councilors of the Government Administration Council, and heads of propaganda departments of various democratic parties, totaling 34 people. The meeting adopted an appeal to celebrate the May 1st Labor Day of 1951 and discussed preparations for a conference of secretaries-general of local consultative committees to be convened by the National Committee.

### 人民政协全国委员会  常务委员会举行会议  通过庆祝“五一”节的号召

1951-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　人民政协全国委员会
　  常务委员会举行会议
　  通过庆祝“五一”节的号召
    【新华社十六日讯】中国人民政协全国委员会常务委员会于十六日下午举行会议。出席和列席这次会议的有全国委员会在北京常务委员，中央人民政府委员会委员，政务院政务委员以及各民主党派宣传部长等共三十四人。会议通过了为庆祝一九五一年五一劳动节的号召，并商谈了全国委员会召集的各地协商委员会秘书长会议的准备工作。
