Author:
Date: 1951-04-29

　　Order of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government
　  Order No. 68 (Political Affairs and Finance)
    The "Provisional Regulations on Mining of the People's Republic of China" have been adopted by the 64th meeting of this Council and are hereby promulgated for implementation.
    This order is hereby issued.
          Premier Zhou Enlai
            April 18, 1951
