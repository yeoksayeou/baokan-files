Title: All-India Peace Congress Preparatory Committee Chairman Edward Arrives in Shanghai from Beijing
Author:
Date: 1951-04-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 7th. Edward, Chairman of the All-India Peace Congress Preparatory Committee and member of the World Peace Council, arrived in Shanghai by train from Beijing at 8:00 AM on the 7th. Zhou Erfu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, Jin Zhonghua, Director of the Propaganda Department, Lu Yi, Head of the Secretariat, and Yeye Sen, Indian Consul General in Shanghai, welcomed him at the station. At 6:00 PM this evening, Soong Ching Ling, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government, hosted a banquet for Mr. Edward, accompanied by Xia Yan, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, and Jin Zhonghua. Mr. Edward will stay in Shanghai for several days, visiting various new constructions in Shanghai.

### 全印和平大会筹委会主席爱德华由北京到上海

1951-04-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　全印和平大会筹委会主席爱德华由北京到上海
    【新华社上海七日电】全印和平大会筹备委员会主席、世界和平理事会理事，爱德华于七日晨八时由北京乘车抵上海。中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会上海分会副总干事周而复、宣传部长金仲华、秘书处长陆诒及印度驻上海总领事雁谒森等，均往车站欢迎。今晚六时，中央人民政府宋庆龄副主席欢宴爱氏，中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会上海分会副主席夏衍及金仲华等作陪。爱氏在上海将逗留数日，参观上海市各项新建设。
