Title: The China Resist America Aid Korea General Association and the Central People's Broadcasting Station Organize Special Broadcast Programs for the Volunteer Army
Author:
Date: 1951-04-30
Page: 3

　　The China Resist America Aid Korea General Association and the Central People's Broadcasting Station
　　Organize Special Broadcast Programs for the Volunteer Army
    The China Resist America Aid Korea General Association and the Central People's Broadcasting Station have agreed to organize special broadcast programs for the Chinese People's Volunteer Army during the May Day holiday (from May 1st to 5th). Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Resist America Aid Korea General Association, Vice Chairman Chen Shutong, Li Lisan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, and Liu Yingyuan, a nationally renowned labor model, will give radio speeches to the Volunteer Army. The broadcast time is from 12:15 PM to 12:45 PM. The wavelength is the same as the troop broadcast program. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 抗美援朝总会和中央人民广播电台  为志愿军组织特别广播节目

1951-04-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　抗美援朝总会和中央人民广播电台
　  为志愿军组织特别广播节目
    中国人民抗美援朝总会和中央人民广播电台商妥，在五月节（从五月一日至五日）为中国人民抗美援朝志愿军组织特别广播节目。由中国人民抗美援朝总会主席郭沫若、副主席陈叔通、中华全国总工会副主席李立三、全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬、全国闻名的劳动模范刘英源对志愿军作广播演讲。广播时间为中午十二时十五分至十二时四十五分。波长同部队广播节目。（新华社）
