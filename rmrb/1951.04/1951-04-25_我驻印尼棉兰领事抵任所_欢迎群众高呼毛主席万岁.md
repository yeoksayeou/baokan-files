Title: My Consul to Medan, Indonesia Arrives at Post; Welcoming Crowds Shout "Long Live Chairman Mao!"
Author:
Date: 1951-04-25
Page: 4

　　My Consul to Medan, Indonesia Arrives at Post
    Welcoming Crowds Shout "Long Live Chairman Mao!"
    [Xinhua News Agency, April 24th] Medan News: Our country's consul to Medan, Indonesia, Shen Yiping, arrived in Medan by plane on the 19th of this month. Consul Shen Yiping received a warm welcome upon arrival at Medan Airport. The welcoming procession stretched for two and a half kilometers, including over a thousand students on bicycles. As Consul Shen Yiping drove past, the welcoming crowds lined the streets, shouting, "Long Live Chairman Mao Zedong!" "Long Live the Friendship between the Indonesian and Chinese People!"

### 我驻印尼棉兰领事抵任所  欢迎群众高呼毛主席万岁

1951-04-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　我驻印尼棉兰领事抵任所
    欢迎群众高呼毛主席万岁
    【新华社二十四日讯】棉兰讯：我国驻印尼棉兰的领事沈一平于本月十九日飞抵棉兰。沈一平领事抵达棉兰机场时受到热烈的欢迎。欢迎的行列长达二公里半，其中有千余位骑脚踏车的学生。当沈一平领事乘车驶过时，欢迎群众夹道欢呼：“毛泽东主席万岁！”“印、中人民友谊万岁！”。
