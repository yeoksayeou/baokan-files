Title: Condolences on the Death of Vladimirsky: Telegram from the CPC Central Committee to the CPSU Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1951-04-07
Page: 1

　　Condolences on the Death of Vladimirsky
　　To the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks) from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China:
　　The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China expresses its condolences on the death of Comrade Mikhail Fedorovich Vladimirsky, Chairman of the Central Auditing Commission of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks), who devoted his life to the cause of communism.
　　　　　　The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
　　　　　　April 6, 1951

### 吊唁乌拉季米尔斯基的逝世  中共中央电联共中央

1951-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　吊唁乌拉季米尔斯基的逝世
　  中共中央电联共中央苏联共产党（布）中央委员会：
    中国共产党中央委员会对于毕生献身于共产主义事业的苏联共产党（布）中央监察委员会主席米海尔·费多罗维奇·乌拉季米尔斯基同志的逝世，谨致吊唁。
              中国共产党中央委员会
        一九五一年四月六日
