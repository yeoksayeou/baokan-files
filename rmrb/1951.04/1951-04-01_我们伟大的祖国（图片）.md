    Our great motherland
    A patriotic production emulation campaign is also developing in the countryside. The Liu Chunshan mutual aid team in Zhongmachi Village, Qingyuan County, Hebei Province, bought a new-style waterwheel in order to realize this year's agricultural production increase plan. They are carefully inspecting it in preparation for spring plowing.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    爱国主义生产竞赛在农村里也发展起来了。河北清苑县中马池村的刘春山互助组，为实现今年的农业增产计划，买了新式水车。他们正在加以细心检查，准备春耕。
