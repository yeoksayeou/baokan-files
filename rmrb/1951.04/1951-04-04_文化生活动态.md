　　Cultural Life Updates
　　　　Exhibition of Soviet Propaganda Posters and Satirical Drawings Opens
    The "Exhibition of Soviet Propaganda Posters and Satirical Drawings," jointly sponsored by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association National Headquarters, the All-China Fine Arts Workers Association, and the Central Academy of Fine Arts, opened yesterday. Among those attending the opening ceremony were Li Jishen and Huang Yanpei, vice presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association National Headquarters; Ma Xulun, Cai Tingkai, and Shao Lizi, directors; Qian Junrui, secretary-general; Xu Beihong, president of the Central Academy of Fine Arts; and Chinese writers and artists Shakoff, Ai Qing, Wang Chaowen, Gu Yuan, Yuan Shuipai, and Fang Cheng. Soviet Ambassador to China, Roshchin, Soviet representative of the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association to China, Safonov, and personnel from the Soviet Embassy were also invited to attend.
    The propaganda posters in this exhibition are divided into ten categories: Lenin and Stalin—the creators of the world's first socialist country; the struggle of the domestic war and the suppression of counterrevolutionaries; the peaceful labor of the Soviet people; the Great Patriotic War; the restoration and development of the national economy after the war; the victory of Soviet democracy; happy children and youth; defending the construction of peace; we support peace; defending the cause of peace; and marching towards the road of communism under the banner of Lenin and under the leadership of Stalin. The satirical drawings include: portraits of capitalists; the American way of life; exposing the conspiracies of warmongers; destroyers of the United Nations; such Anglo-American relations; Wall Street on the eve of a major storm; an album of war criminals; and more than one hundred pieces featuring the disciples and grandchildren of Hitler.
　　　　Rapid Development of Secondary Education in Inner Mongolia
    Secondary education in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region is developing rapidly. The four eastern leagues of the region now have ten secondary schools, and the number of students has increased from 1,939 to 3,759, an increase of almost one-fold; the enrollment rate accounts for over 80 percent of primary school graduates. It is estimated that by the end of this year, there will be 15,000 graduates from complete primary schools, and if calculated according to this year's enrollment rate of 80 percent, there will be about 10,000 students entering middle schools next year.

### 文化生活动态

1951-04-04
第3版()
专栏：文化生活动态

　　文化生活动态
            苏联宣传画和讽刺画展览会揭幕
    中苏友好协会总会、中华全国美术工作者协会、中央美术学院联合主办的“苏联宣传画和讽刺画展览会”，已于昨日揭幕。参加揭幕典礼的有中苏友好协会总会副会长李济深、黄炎培，理事马叙伦、蔡廷锴、邵力子，总干事钱俊瑞，中央美术学院院长徐悲鸿，中国作家、艺术工作者沙可夫、艾青、王朝闻、古元、袁水拍、方成等。苏联驻我国大使罗申、苏联对外文化协会驻我国代表萨福隆及苏联大使馆人员亦应邀参加。
    这次展览的宣传画分十大类，即：列宁、斯大林——全世界第一个社会主义国家的创造者，国内战争和镇压反革命的斗争，苏联人民的和平劳动，伟大的卫国战争，战后国民经济的恢复与发展，苏联民主的胜利，幸福的儿童和青年，保卫和平的建设，我们拥护和平，保卫和平事业及在列宁旗帜下，在斯大林领导下向共产主义道路迈进。讽刺画有：资本家造像、美国的生活方式、揭破战争贩子的阴谋、联合国的破坏者、如此美英关系、华尔街在大雷雨的前夕、战犯画册、希特勒的徒子徒孙等一百多幅。
            内蒙古中等教育迅速发展
    内蒙古自治区中等教育迅速发展。该区东部四盟，已有中等学校十所，学生人数由一千九百三十九名增至三千七百五十九名，增加几近一倍；升学率占小学毕业生百分之八十强。据估计，今年年底将有一万五千名完全小学毕业生，若按今年百分之八十的就学率计算，来年升入中学的学生约有万余人。
