Title: Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Sends Congratulatory Message to the 7th Congress of the Italian Communist Party
Author: 
Date: 1951-04-03
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd: The Seventh Congress of the Italian Communist Party is scheduled to be held on April 3rd. The Central Committee of the CPC has sent a telegram of congratulations. The telegram is as follows:
To all the delegates of the Seventh Congress of the Italian Communist Party, comrades:
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm congratulations to you and wishes your congress success. We wish the Italian Communist Party greater victory in leading the Italian people in the struggle against wars of aggression, for lasting peace, and for the democracy of the Italian people.
                                  The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China

### 中国共产党中央委员会  电贺意共第七次代表大会

1951-04-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国共产党中央委员会       
    电贺意共第七次代表大会
    【新华社二日讯】意大利共产党第七次代表大会定于四月三日举行。中共中央特致电祝贺，电文如下：意大利共产党第七次代表大会全体代表同志们：
    中国共产党中央委员会谨向你们致热烈的庆贺，祝你们的大会成功，祝意大利共产党在领导意大利人民为反对侵略战争，争取持久和平及为意大利人民民主的斗争中获得更大的胜利。
                                  中国共产党中央委员会
