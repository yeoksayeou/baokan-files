Author:
Date: 1951-04-08

    Our Great Motherland
    Resolutely opposing American imperialism's rearmament of Japan has become one of the important components of the Chinese people's movement to defend world peace, oppose American aggression, and popularize and deepen the patriotic movement to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea. This is a majestic militia contingent during the demonstration rally of the people of Fencheng, Shanxi, on March 22 against American imperialism's rearmament of Japan.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-04-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    坚决反对美帝国主义重新武装日本，已经成为中国人民保卫世界和、反对美国侵略、普及与深入抗美援朝爱国运动的重要内容之一。这是三月二十二日山西汾城人民反对美帝国主义重新武装日本浒行示威大会中雄壮的民兵队伍。
