Title: European People Support Peace Pact Declaration; People of Germany, Hungary, Bulgaria and Other Countries Fully Launch Signature Campaign; Mass Rallies in Various Parts of France Demand Cessation of Arming West Germany
Author:
Date: 1951-04-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd Dispatch: The people of Germany, Hungary, Bulgaria, France, Austria, and Norway are enthusiastically supporting the World Peace Council's declaration on the Five Great Powers concluding a peace pact. The Berlin Peace Committee of Greater Berlin, Germany, held a plenary session on March 31, resolving to urge peace fighters in Berlin's enterprises, schools, and residential areas to hold mass meetings to discuss the World Peace Council's declaration and the resolutions of the European Workers' Congress. Young people in Magdeburg are attaching copies of the Peace Pact Declaration and the pamphlet of Stalin's interview with a Pravda reporter to balloons and kites and sending them to West Germany to help the West German people understand these two historic documents. Hungarian scientific workers held a meeting on March 29, calling on scientists across the country to unanimously demand that the Five Great Powers conclude a peace pact and actively participate in the peace movement. Various people's organizations in Bulgaria have responded to the declaration on concluding a peace pact. The Central Committee of the Dimitrov People's Youth League issued a letter to young peace fighters on the 1st, calling on young people across the country to unanimously sign the Peace Pact Declaration. The Holy Synod of the Bulgarian Orthodox Church also held a special meeting to discuss the Peace Pact Declaration and issued an open letter to clergy across the country, calling on clergy and believers to unanimously sign the Peace Pact Declaration and actively participate in the activities of local peace committees.

Mass rallies demanding peace are being held in various towns and cities in France. In Elbeuf, the local branches of the Society of Friends of Peace, the Peace Committee, the Communist Party, the Socialist Party, and the League for Human Rights recently jointly held a rally demanding the convocation of a Five Great Powers conference to conclude a peace pact and demanding the cessation of arming West Germany. Austrian youth are fully engaged in collecting signatures for the Peace Pact Declaration, setting an initial target of 100,000 people. The Hadeland Trade Union Federation in Norway and the workers of the Liljeberg factory in Oslo, the capital, enthusiastically support the conclusion of a peace pact and urge the convening of a national Norwegian peace conference to further promote the movement to defend peace.

Xinhua News Agency, 1st Dispatch: TASS, Rome: The Italian National Peace Council held a meeting here on March 30. The meeting resolved to launch a signature campaign for the Peace Pact Declaration. Nenni, Vice-Chairman of the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council and Chairman of the Italian National Peace Council, spoke at the meeting, suggesting the adoption of a resolution calling for a nationwide signature campaign to support the World Peace Council's Berlin meeting’s declaration demanding the conclusion of a Five Great Powers peace pact. Representatives of the peace councils of various provinces and representatives of various people's organizations actively participating in the peace struggle unanimously adopted it. The resolution stated: The Italian National Peace Council calls on local peace councils, all democratic individuals and groups cooperating with the National Council, and all those who have signed the ban on atomic weapons to begin the broadest national discussion on the issue of concluding a Five Great Powers peace pact. It is imperative that everyone in every town, village, factory, institution, university, and family know about the declaration demanding the conclusion of a Five Great Powers peace pact. Everyone who knows that peace cannot be waited for but must be fought for must quickly join this sacred struggle to make the people speak and silence the cannons. Signing the declaration demanding the conclusion of a peace pact means that every upright Italian citizen undertakes the obligation to fight to save his country, his family, and his hometown.

### 欧洲人民支持和平公约宣言  德、匈、保等国人民全力展开征集签名  法国各地群众集会要求停止武装西德

1951-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　欧洲人民支持和平公约宣言       
    德、匈、保等国人民全力展开征集签名       
    法国各地群众集会要求停止武装西德
    【新华社二日讯】德、匈、保、法、奥、挪威六国人民热烈支持世界和平理事会关于五大国缔结和平公约的宣言。德国大柏林市和平委员会于三月三十一日举行全体会议，决议敦促柏林各企业、学校和住宅区的和平战士举行群众大会，讨论世界和平理事会的宣言及欧洲工人代表大会的决议。马格德堡的青年正把和平公约宣言和斯大林对真理报记者谈话的小册子系在汽球和风筝上送往西德，帮助西德人民认识这两项历史性的文件。匈牙利科学工作者于三月二十九日举行会议，号召全国科学家一致要求五大国缔结和平公约并积极参加和平运动。保加利亚各人民团体纷纷响应缔结和平公约的宣言，季米特洛夫人民青年联盟中央委员会于一日发表了告青年和平战士书，号召全国青年一致在和平公约宣言上签名。保加利亚正教会圣议会也举行了讨论和平公约宣言的特别会议，发表了致全国教士的公开信，号召教士与教徒一致在和平公约宣言上签名，并积极参加各地方和平委员会的活动。
    法国各城镇正纷纷举行要求和平的群众大会。在埃尔柏夫，和平之友社、和平委员会、共产党与社会党的地方支部与人权联盟，最近联合举行大会，要求召开五大国会议缔结和平公约，并要求停止武装西德。奥地利青年正全力为和平公约宣言征集签名，他们确定初期目标为十万人。挪威哈达兰恩工会联合会和首都奥斯陆利列堡工厂的工人热烈拥护缔结和平公约，并敦促召开挪威全国和平大会，以进一步展开保卫和平的运动。
    【新华社一日讯】塔斯社罗马讯：三月三十日意大利全国和平理事会在此间举行会议。会议已决议开展和平公约宣言的签名运动。世界和平理事会执行局副主席、意大利全国和平理事会主席南尼在会上发言，建议通过一项决议，号召展开全国性的签名运动，以支持世界和平理事会柏林会议要求缔结五大国和平公约的宣言。到会的各省和平理事会代表及积极参加和平斗争的各人民团体的代表们一致予以通过。决议称：意大利全国和平理事会号召各地和平理事会、所有与全国理事会合作的民主人士、民主团体以及所有曾签名禁止原子武器的人，开始对缔结五大国和平公约的问题进行最广泛的全国性的讨论，必须使每一个城镇、乡村、工厂、机关、大学和家庭里的人都知道要求缔结五大国和平公约的宣言。每一个知道不能等待和平而必须争取和平的人都必须迅速地参加这个叫人民讲话叫大炮沉默的神圣斗争。在要求缔结和平公约的宣言上签名、便意味着每一个正直的意大利公民承担了为挽救他的祖国、他的家庭和他的家乡而斗争的义务。
