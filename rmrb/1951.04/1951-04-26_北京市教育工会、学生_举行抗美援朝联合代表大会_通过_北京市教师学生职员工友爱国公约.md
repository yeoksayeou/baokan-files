Title: Beijing Educational Union and Students Hold Joint Representative Conference for Resisting U.S. Aggression and Aiding Korea; Adopt "Beijing Teachers, Students, Staff, and Workers Patriotic Convention"
Author:
Date: 1951-04-26
Page: 3

　　The Beijing Educational Union and the Beijing Students' Joint Representative Conference for Resisting U.S. Aggression and Aiding Korea was held on the 21st and 22nd. The conference summarized the anti-U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea movement in various schools in the city over the past six months, successfully resolved the issue of deepening and popularizing the anti-U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea movement in the future, and adopted the "Beijing Teachers, Students, Staff, and Workers Patriotic Convention." After speeches by Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, the conference listened to Qian Duansheng, Chairman of the Beijing Committee of the Educational Union, on the "Work Report on Deepening and Popularizing the Anti-U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea Movement in Beijing Schools," and a report by Qian Junrui, Deputy Minister of Education. In addition, eleven people made typical speeches, including Wu Jingchao, a professor at Tsinghua University, reporting on his personal gains from visiting the land reform; Zhang Jingyue, a professor at Peking University, reporting on his personal gains from participating in rural propaganda; People's University and four other schools, as well as elementary school teachers from the city, introducing their experiences in the anti-U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea movement; and students and workers denouncing the atrocities of U.S. and Japanese imperialism. The representatives at the conference unanimously expressed their firm opposition to the U.S. arming Japan and their support for the resolution of the World Peace Council. The conference finally passed a telegram of "Protest Against the U.S. Unilaterally Formulating the Draft of the Peace Treaty with Japan," as well as congratulatory telegrams to Chairman Mao, the Chinese People's Volunteers, and General Kim Il-sung.

### 北京市教育工会、学生  举行抗美援朝联合代表大会  通过“北京市教师学生职员工友爱国公约”

1951-04-26
第3版()
专栏：

　　北京市教育工会、学生
    举行抗美援朝联合代表大会
    通过“北京市教师学生职员工友爱国公约”
    北京市教育工会、北京市学生抗美援朝联合代表大会于二十一日、二十二日举行。会上总结了半年来全市各校抗美援朝运动，圆满地解决了今后深入和普及抗美援朝运动的问题，并通过“北京市教师、学生、职员、工友爱国公约”。大会由中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬、北京市人民政府张友渔副市长讲话后，听取了教育工会北京市委员会主席钱端升关于“北京市各学校深入和普及抗美援朝运动的工作报告”，和教育部钱俊瑞副部长的报告。另有十一人作典型发言，其中有清华大学教授吴景超报告个人参观土地改革的收获，北京大学教授张景钺报告个人参加农村宣传的收获，人民大学等四校及全市小学教师介绍抗美援朝运动的经验，以及学生、工友对美、日帝国主义暴行的控诉等。大会代表一致表示：坚决反对美国武装日本，拥护世界和平理事会的决议。大会最后通过“抗议美国片面制订对日和约草案”的通电，及向毛主席、中国人民志愿军、金日成将军等的致敬电文。
