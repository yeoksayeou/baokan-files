Title: Pakistani Representative Ali Arrives in Beijing to Negotiate Establishment of Diplomatic Relations with Us
Author:
Date: 1951-04-25
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, April 24th: The Pakistani government has appointed Mr. Ahmed Ali as its representative to negotiate with the Central People's Government of our country on the establishment of diplomatic relations. Mr. Ahmed Ali arrived in Beijing at 5:18 PM today. Han Xu, Section Chief of the Protocol Department of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, went to the station to greet him.

### 巴基斯坦代表阿里抵京  与我谈判建立外交关系

1951-04-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　巴基斯坦代表阿里抵京
    与我谈判建立外交关系
    【新华社二十四日讯】为与我国中央人民政府就建立外交关系问题进行谈判，巴基斯坦政府已指派阿默德·阿里先生为谈判代表。阿默德·阿里先生业于今日下午五时十八分抵京，我外交部派交际处科长韩叙赴站迎接。
