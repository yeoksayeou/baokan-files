Title: Workers of the Wafangdian Textile Factory Report Production Achievements to Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1951-04-04
Page: 6

Workers of the Wafangdian Textile Factory
Reporting Production Achievements to Chairman Mao

Respected Chairman Mao:

　　Our factory victoriously completed the first quarter's yarn and cloth production tasks ahead of schedule on March 17th. We are very happy to have increased the strength to resist America and aid Korea with our practical actions of completing production tasks ahead of schedule.

　　Now, we are further developing the patriotic production competition to welcome the great festival of the world's working class - "May Day". Regarding the second quarter's production tasks, we strive to exceed the national plan by 10 percent and reduce costs by 7 percent. We are fully confident of achieving this.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　All workers of the Liaodong Wafangdian Textile Factory

### 瓦房店纺织厂职工  向毛主席报告生产成绩

1951-04-04
第6版()
专栏：

　　瓦房店纺织厂职工
    向毛主席报告生产成绩敬爱的毛主席：
    我厂于三月十七日胜利地提前完成今年第一季的纱、布生产任务，特向你报捷。我们能用提前完成生产任务的实际行动增加了抗美援朝的力量，心里非常高兴。
    现在，我们正进一步开展爱国主义生产竞赛，来迎接全世界工人阶级伟大的节日——“五一”劳动节。关于第二季的生产任务，我们争取超过国家计划百分之十，降低成本百分之七，我们完全有信心作到。
                                    辽东瓦房店纺织厂全体职工
