Title: In early April, in areas north and south of the 38th parallel, the Chinese and Korean People's forces annihilated more than 6,000 enemies. The Volunteer Army leadership called on the entire army to fight against enemy aircraft.
Author:
Date: 1951-04-15
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, April 14th: The Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army annihilated more than 6,000 enemy troops in early April. From April 1st to 10th, the Korean and Chinese People's forces continued to fight the enemy bravely andResourcefully in areas south and north of the 38th parallel, killing, wounding, and capturing more than 6,000 enemy troops, of which 4,771 were American invading troops. At the same time, the Korean and Chinese People's forces shot down a total of 11 enemy planes and damaged 3 enemy planes over the front and rear, destroyed 14 enemy tanks and 8 vehicles, and seized some weapons and ammunition.
    Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, April 14th: Among the 14 enemy planes shot down and damaged by the Korean and Chinese People's forces in early April, one was shot down by a driver of the Volunteer Army Logistics Department with a rifle. Automobile driver Zhao Baoyin and his assistant Zeng Xinren protected the car they were driving and all the materials with their heroic actions of actively shooting at enemy planes; therefore, they were each honorably awarded a special-class merit once and each received a bonus of 100,000 RMB. The Volunteer Army leadership has issued a circular to commend units and individuals who actively fought against enemy aircraft, and called on the entire army to universally promote this spirit of fighting against the air, and strive to organize various firearms to bravely shoot at enemy aircraft and create more brilliant results.

### 四月上旬在北纬三十八度线南北地区  中朝人民部队歼敌六千余人  志愿军领导机关号召全军对敌机作战

1951-04-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　四月上旬在北纬三十八度线南北地区
    中朝人民部队歼敌六千余人
    志愿军领导机关号召全军对敌机作战
    【新华社朝鲜前线十四日电】朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军于本月上旬又歼灭敌军六千余人。本月一日至十日，朝、中人民部队在北纬三十八度线以南和以北地区继续英勇机智地与敌军作战，毙伤俘敌军六千余人，其中美国侵略军占四千七百七十一人。与此同时，朝、中人民部队在前线和后方上空共击落敌机十一架，击伤敌机三架，击毁敌军坦克十四辆，汽车八辆，并缴获武器、弹药一部。
    【新华社朝鲜前线十四日电】朝、中人民部队于本月上旬击落、击伤的十四架敌机中，其中有一架是志愿军后勤部的汽车司机用步枪击落的。汽车司机赵宝印和助手曾新仁，用自己积极射击敌机的英勇行为，保护了他们所驾驶的汽车和全部物资；因而，各光荣地记上特等功一次，各得奖金人民币十万元。志愿军领导机关特发出通报，表扬对敌机积极作战的单位和个人，并号召全军普遍发挥这种对空作战的精神，力求组织各种火器对敌机进行英勇射击，创造更辉煌的战果。
