Title: A Certain Volunteer Army Anti-Aircraft Artillery Unit Shot Down and Damaged Five Enemy Aircraft; This Unit Had Previously Shot Down and Damaged Forty-Seven Enemy Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1951-04-05
Page: 1

　　Volunteer Army anti-aircraft artillery unit
    Shot down and damaged five enemy aircraft
    This unit had previously shot down and damaged forty-seven enemy aircraft
    [Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, 4th] A certain anti-aircraft artillery unit of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army shot down two enemy B-26 bombers and one F-80 fighter on March 23rd and 24th, and also damaged two enemy aircraft. In twenty-six air combat operations from February 11th to March 12th, the unit shot down eleven enemy aircraft and damaged thirty-six enemy aircraft, achieving brilliant results and receiving commendations from leading organs.

### 志愿军某高射炮部队  击落击伤敌机五架  该部前曾击落击伤敌机四十七架

1951-04-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　志愿军某高射炮部队
    击落击伤敌机五架       
    该部前曾击落击伤敌机四十七架
    【新华社朝鲜前线四日电】中国人民志愿军某高射炮部队在三月二十三、二十四两日击落敌Ｂ二六型轰炸机两架，Ｆ八○型战斗机一架，此外并击伤敌机二架。该部在二月十一日至三月十二日二十六次对空作战中，击落敌机十一架，击伤敌机三十六架，战果辉煌，已受到领导机关的通报表扬。
