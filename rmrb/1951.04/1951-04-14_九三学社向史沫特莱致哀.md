Title: The Jiu San Society Expresses Condolences to Agnes Smedley

Author:
Date: 1951-04-14
Page: 3

To the Preparatory Committee for the Memorial Service for Agnes Smedley, C/O All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles:

    The American revolutionary writer, Miss Agnes Smedley, made unparalleled contributions to the cause of the liberation of the Chinese people during her lifetime. As Miss Smedley's ashes are transported to the capital of the new People's China—Beijing, we express our deepest condolences to this great writer and close friend of the Chinese people.

        The Jiu San Society

### 九三学社向史沫特莱致哀

1951-04-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　九三学社向史沫特莱致哀
    中华全国文学艺术界联合会转史沫特莱女士追悼大会筹备会：
    美国革命作家史沫特莱女士生前对于中国人民解放事业尽了无可比拟的贡献。当兹史沫特莱女士的骨灰运来人民新中国的首都——北京之际，我们对这位伟大的作家、中国人民的亲密友人敬致深切的哀悼。
        九三学社
