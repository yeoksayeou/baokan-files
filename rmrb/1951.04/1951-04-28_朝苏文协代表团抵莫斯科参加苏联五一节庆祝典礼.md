Title: Korean-Soviet Cultural Association Delegation Arrives in Moscow to Participate in Soviet May Day Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1951-04-28
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 27th: TASS Moscow reports: A delegation from the Korean-Soviet Cultural Association arrived in Moscow on April 26th. The delegation is led by Kim Hee-il, Vice Minister of Transportation, and includes representatives from workers, intellectuals, and the Korean People's Army. The guests will examine the life and work of the Soviet people and will participate in the May Day celebration.

### 朝苏文协代表团抵莫斯科参加苏联五一节庆祝典礼

1951-04-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝苏文协代表团抵莫斯科参加苏联五一节庆祝典礼
    【新华社二十七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：朝苏文化协会代表团已于四月二十六日抵达莫斯科。该代表团由交通副相金熙一率领，包括职工、知识分子和朝鲜人民军的代表们。嘉宾们将考察苏联人民的生活与工作，并将参加五一节庆祝盛典。
