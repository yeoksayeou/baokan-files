Title: Third Batch of Railway Workers from Various Places Volunteer to Join the Korean War Depart

Author:

Date: 1951-04-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th: Railway Bureau employees' volunteer transport teams from Shanghai, Tianjin, Jinan, Zhengzhou, Taiyuan, and Hengyang have departed in batches to volunteer in the Korean War. This is the third group of railway employees in our country to volunteer to assist Korea. When they departed, they were warmly seen off by the local people and railway workers.

### 各地第三批铁路员工  志愿援朝队出发

1951-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　各地第三批铁路员工
　  志愿援朝队出发
    【新华社四日讯】上海、天津、济南、郑州、太原、衡阳各地铁路管理局职工志愿援朝运输队，已分批先后出发。这是我国铁路员工第三批志愿援朝的人员。他们出发时，受到当地人民和铁路职工们的热烈欢送。
