Title: Vietnam General Confederation of Labor Issues Instructions for Celebrating May Day

Author:
Date: 1951-04-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, April 25th: The Standing Committee of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor has notified its branches throughout the country to prepare for the celebration of International Labor Day, May 1st. In the notice issued to local branches, the Standing Committee stated: The Vietnamese working people and all people will join the people and working people of other countries around the world on May 1st to demand that the five major powers, the Soviet Union, China, the United States, Britain, and France, conclude a peace pact; and express their determination to actively support the struggle of the Korean people and the Chinese People's Volunteers.

The Vietnam General Confederation of Labor also stipulated that, starting from May 1st, all branches should respond to President Ho Chi Minh's call to conduct new patriotic emulation drives in various departments, with the aim of eliminating more enemy troops and increasing urban and rural production. Trade unions should widely publicize the declaration of the World Peace Council and collect signatures for the declaration on the conclusion of a peace pact.

### 越南总工会发出庆祝五一节指示

1951-04-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南总工会发出庆祝五一节指示
    【新华社二十五日讯】据越南通讯社北越二十三日讯：越南总工会常设委员会通知全国各地的分会，筹备庆祝“五一”国际劳动节。常委会在发给各地分会的通知中说：越南劳动人民和全体人民将在“五一”节与世界其他各国人民和劳动人民一道，要求苏、中、美、英、法五大国缔结和平公约；并表示决心积极支持朝鲜人民和中国人民志愿军的斗争。
    越南总工会并规定各分会自五月一日起响应胡志明主席的号召，在各部门进行新的爱国主义竞赛，目的在消灭更多的敌军和提高城乡生产。工会要广泛宣传世界和平理事会的宣言，征集缔结和平公约宣言的签名。
