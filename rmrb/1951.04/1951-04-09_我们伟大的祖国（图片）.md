Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-04-09
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    After liberation, the fishermen on the shores of Dongting Lake organized production and sales cooperatives and freely fished in the vast lake. The miserable days of "fishermen being poor for generations" are gone forever. Now is the fishing season, and these two young fishermen, having caught a boatload of fish, are on their way to deliver it to the cooperative's sales department.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    解放后洞庭湖滨的渔民们，组织了产销合作社，在水阔天空的湖泊里自由地捕鱼。过去的“捕鱼人儿世世穷”的苦难日子，已一去不复返了。现在正是捕鱼的季节，这两个青年渔民，捕了一船的鱼，正要赶送到合作社的门市上去。
