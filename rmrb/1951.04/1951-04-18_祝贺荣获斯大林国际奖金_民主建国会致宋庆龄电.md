Title: Congratulations on Winning the Stalin International Peace Prize: Telegram from the China Democratic National Construction Association to Soong Ching Ling
Author:
Date: 1951-04-18
Page: 1

　　Congratulations on Winning the Stalin International Peace Prize
    Telegram from the China Democratic National Construction Association to Soong Ching Ling
    Vice Chairman Soong: We are delighted to learn that you have been awarded the first "International Stalin Prize for Strengthening Peace Among Peoples." All comrades of our China Democratic National Construction Association extend our congratulations on your honor! We are willing to fight together with you under the victorious banner of Chairman Mao to implement the resolutions of the World Peace Council and eliminate the aggression of American imperialism to the very end!
                  China Democratic National Construction Association
                April 17th

### 祝贺荣获斯大林国际奖金  民主建国会致宋庆龄电

1951-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　祝贺荣获斯大林国际奖金
    民主建国会致宋庆龄电
    宋副主席：敬悉您荣膺了首届“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金。我们民主建国会全体同志对于您的荣誉，敬致祝贺！我们并愿在毛主席胜利的旗帜之下，和您共同为贯彻世界和平理事会的决议，消灭美帝国主义侵略而奋斗到底！
                  民主建国会
                四月十七日
