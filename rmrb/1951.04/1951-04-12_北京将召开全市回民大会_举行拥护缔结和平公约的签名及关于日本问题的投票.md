Title: Beijing to Convene Citywide Hui People's Congress to Hold Signatures in Support of Concluding a Peace Pact and Voting on the Japan Issue
Author: Feng Wancai
Date: 1951-04-12
Page: 1

　　Beijing to Convene Citywide Hui People's Congress
  　To Hold Signatures in Support of Concluding a Peace Pact and Voting on the Japan Issue
    [Beijing Municipal News Agency Dispatch] Representatives from all circles of the Hui people in Beijing, imams from various mosques, principals of Hui schools, and Hui cadres from government offices met on the 9th and resolved to convene a citywide Hui People's Congress at the Hui People's College at 2:00 PM on April 14th. The meeting will discuss the declaration and resolutions of the World Peace Council; hold signatures in support of the five major powers concluding a peace pact; hold a vote against U.S. imperialism rearming Japan; protest the French imperialists' armed suppression of the Moroccan people; support the liberation movement of the Moroccan people; and establish the Beijing Municipal Hui People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace and Opposition to American Aggression. (Feng Wancai)

### 北京将召开全市回民大会　举行拥护缔结和平公约的签名及关于日本问题的投票
冯万才
1951-04-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京将召开全市回民大会
  　举行拥护缔结和平公约的签名及关于日本问题的投票
    【京市新闻处讯】北京市各界回民代表、各清真寺阿訇、回民学校校长、机关回民干部等于九日开会，决议四月十四日下午二时在回民学院召开全市回民大会，会上将讨论世界和平理事会的宣言和决议；举行拥护五大国缔结和平公约的签名；举行反对美帝国主义重新武装日本的投票，抗议法帝国主义武装镇压摩洛哥人民的暴行，声援摩洛哥人民的解放运动；并成立北京市回民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会。（冯万才）
