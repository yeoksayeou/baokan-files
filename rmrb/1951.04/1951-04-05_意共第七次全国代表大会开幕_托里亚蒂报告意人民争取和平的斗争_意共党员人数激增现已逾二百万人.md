Title: Italian Communist Party Holds Seventh National Congress; Togliatti Reports on Italian People's Struggle for Peace; Italian Communist Party Membership Soars, Now Exceeding Two Million

Author:

Date: 1951-04-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th: According to TASS, Rome, April 3rd, the Seventh National Congress of the Italian Communist Party opened on the morning of the 3rd at the Adriano Theater in Rome. Brother parties from France, Hungary, Trieste, Austria, and Switzerland sent representatives to attend the congress. When the leaders of the Italian Communist Party, headed by Togliatti, appeared on the stage, the entire venue erupted in enthusiastic cheers.

谢嘉 (Xiè Jiā), Deputy General Secretary of the Italian Communist Party, delivered the opening address. He briefly retraced the historical path taken by the Italian Communist Party over the past thirty years since its founding and proposed cheering for Stalin, the leader of the working people of all countries. Immediately, shouts of "Long live Stalin! Long live the Bolshevik Party! Long live the Soviet Union!" resounded throughout the hall.

纳托利 (Nàtuōlì), Secretary of the Rome Provincial Committee of the Italian Communist Party, paid tribute to the congress on behalf of the 100,000 party members in the capital and the Rome province, as well as all the democratic people of the capital. 摩兰地 (Mólándì), Deputy General Secretary of the Italian Socialist Party, congratulated the Italian Communist Party on behalf of the 700,000 members of the Italian Socialist Party. He pointed out that the Italian Communist Party and the Italian Socialist Party are closely united by the Joint Action Pact, which is a bulwark for peace and the progress of Italy.

托里亚蒂 (Tuōlìyàdì), General Secretary of the Italian Communist Party, delivered a report on "The Italian People's Struggle for Peace, for Labor, and for Freedom."

Xinhua News Agency, April 3rd: TASS, Rome, April 2nd: L'Unità recently published figures illustrating the increase in the number of members of the Italian Communist Party from 1945 to 1951, stating: When the Italian Communist Party held its Fifth Party Congress in December 1945, it had 1,718,836 members. Currently, the number of party members, plus members of the Italian Communist Youth Federation, has reached 2,576,487, of which 463,894 are members of the Communist Youth League (the Communist Youth League was re-established in 1949). During the same period, the number of grassroots party organizations has increased from 29,230 to 52,482.

### 意共第七次全国代表大会开幕  托里亚蒂报告意人民争取和平的斗争  意共党员人数激增现已逾二百万人

1951-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　意共第七次全国代表大会开幕       
    托里亚蒂报告意人民争取和平的斗争       
    意共党员人数激增现已逾二百万人
    【新华社四日讯】据塔斯社罗马三日讯，意大利共产党第七次全国代表大会已于三日晨在罗马阿达里亚诺戏院开幕。法国、匈牙利、的里雅斯特、奥地利和瑞士各国兄弟党，都派有代表参加大会。当以托里亚蒂为首的意共领袖们在主席台上出现时，全场响起了热烈的欢呼。
    意共副总书记谢嘉致开幕辞。他简单地追述了意共成立三十年来所走过的历史道路，并建议为各国劳动人民的领袖斯大林欢呼。顿时，“斯大林万岁！布尔什维克党万岁！苏联万岁！”的欢呼声响彻会场。
    意共罗马省委书记纳托利代表首都和罗马省十万党员及首都全体民主人民，向大会致敬。意大利社会党副总书记摩兰地代表意大利社会党七十万党员向意大利共产党致贺。他指出：意大利共产党和社会党是由两党一致行动公约紧紧结合起来的，这个公约是争取和平及意大利进步的堡垒。
    意共总书记托里亚蒂在会上作了关于“意大利人民为争取和平、为劳动及自由的斗争”的报告。
    【新华社三日讯】塔斯社罗马二日讯：团结报顷发表数字，说明自一九四五至一九五一年期间意大利共产党党员增加的情形说：意共在一九四五年十二月，举行第五次党代表大会时，意共共有党员一百七十一万八千八百三十六人，目前，党员加上意大利共产主义青年团团员已有二百五十七万六千四百八十七人，其中有四十六万三千八百九十四人是共青团团员（共青团是在一九四九年恢复的）。在同一时期内，党的基层组织已由二万九千二百三十个增加到五万二千四百八十二个。
