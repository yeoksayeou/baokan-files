Title: Burmese Newspaper "Progress" Publishes Article Opposing US Arming of Japan
Author:
Date: 1951-04-10
Page: 4

　　Burmese Newspaper "Progress" Publishes Article
　  Opposing US Arming of Japan
    [Xinhua News Agency, 8th] Yangon dispatch: The "Progress" newspaper, published in Yangon, the capital of Burma, published an article on March 27th, the sixth anniversary of Burma's Anti-Japanese War, opposing American imperialism's arming of Japan. The newspaper wrote: The brutal persecution we suffered from Japan six years ago is unforgettable. We do not want to see fascist Japan revived again. MacArthur's rearmament of Japan will turn Japan into a fascist devil again. The newspaper emphasized: We cannot forget the Anti-Japanese War Day, and we cannot but express the same concern about the arming of Japan. To commemorate the Anti-Japanese War Day, we must oppose the arming of Japan.

### 缅甸《进步报》撰文  反对美国武装日本

1951-04-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　缅甸《进步报》撰文
　  反对美国武装日本
    【新华社八日讯】仰光消息：在缅甸首都仰光出版的《进步报》，在三月二十七日缅甸抗日六周年纪念日著文反对美帝国主义武装日本。该报写道：六年前，我们所受到的日本的残暴迫害是令人难忘的。我们不愿看到再使法西斯日本复苏。麦克阿瑟重新武装日本的做法，将使日本再成为法西斯魔鬼。该报强调说：我们不能忘记抗日节，我们对武装日本不能不同样表示关切。我们纪念抗日节，就要反对武装日本。
