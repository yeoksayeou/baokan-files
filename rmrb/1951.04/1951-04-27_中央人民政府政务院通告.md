Title: Circular of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government
Author:
Date: 1951-04-27
Page: 1

　　Circular of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government

　　It is hereby stipulated that the holiday arrangements for all organs, organizations, schools, and factories in Beijing for the "May 1st" Labor Day this year shall be as follows:

　　1. Sunday, April 29th, will be a normal working day without a holiday.
　　2. May 1st will be a one-day holiday.
　　3. May 2nd will be a one-day compensatory holiday.

　　It is hoped that you will comply with and implement this.

### 中央人民政府政务院通告

1951-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央人民政府政务院通告
    兹规定北京市各机关、团体、学校、工厂在本年“五一”劳动节前后放假办法如下：
    一、四月二十九日（星期日）一律照常工作，不放假；
    二、五月一日放假一天；
    三、五月二日补假一天。
    望即遵照实行。
