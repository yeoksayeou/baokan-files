Title: People's Democratic Countries' Massive Aid to Korea Arrives; Korean Government Distributing to War Zone Victims; International Women's Federation Calls on Women of All Countries to Support Korean People
Author:
Date: 1951-04-04
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 3rd. Materials of aid and consolation from the people of various people's democratic countries to the Korean People's Army and the Korean people are continuously arriving here.

　　It is learned here that, in addition to the large amount of materials aided by the Chinese people, the materials received from other people's democratic countries recently include: more than 7,000 yards of woolen fabrics, 253 boxes of medicines, and 177 boxes of underwear and other clothing donated by the people of Czechoslovakia; 27 boxes of medicines and 6 boxes of medical equipment donated by the people of Poland; 475 boxes of medicines and clothing donated by the people of the German Democratic Republic; and woolen fabrics, cotton fabrics, blankets, comfort bags, and films donated by the people of Hungary.

　　The Mongolian people once donated 14 train cars of comfort items, including woolen and fur clothing, as well as cotton clothing, fur boots, leather shoes, and other fur products that are specialties of Mongolia.

　　The materials donated by the people of various people's democratic countries to the Korean people are being distributed to the disaster victims in the war zones by the people's political organs in various parts of Korea. As of March 22, the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea had distributed 1.38 million yards of white cloth to disaster victims in various places. The disaster victims who received the aid materials expressed their infinite gratitude to the people of all fraternal countries. Kim Sun-hwan, a disaster victim in the northern district of Pyongyang, said when he received the aid materials: "When we receive the aid materials from the people of various people's democratic countries, we deeply realize that hundreds of millions of people around the world are standing on our side and sincerely helping us. The aid from the various people's democratic countries has further increased our confidence in defeating the American aggressors and strengthened our strength to overcome difficulties."

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 3rd. According to the suggestion made by the Chinese representative at the Berlin meeting of the International Democratic Women's Federation Council, the Secretariat of the International Democratic Women's Federation has decided to launch a movement to support the Korean people and to collect all possible items to help Korean women and children. The Secretariat sent letters to the women's organizations of various countries on March 20, praising the heroic Korean people who are defending the cause of peace and the happy future of all mankind. The letter stated: Our solemn task is to support the struggle of the Korean people and alleviate the suffering of Korean sisters in the war. We must donate clothes and children's clothing, medicines, and as much cash as possible. Women's groups in various countries should immediately launch a fundraising campaign and a campaign to collect and sew clothes, and mobilize workers, doctors, and even artists to participate in the support work; radio broadcasts, newspapers, and all other methods should be used to explain the current situation in Korea and the power that the assistance of women around the world will generate. The letter concluded by saying: Through this support movement, let the mothers and children of Korea feel our enthusiasm and friendship, and feel the power of the people of the world. People all over the world are helping them save their motherland and defend the survival and peace of the world.

### 人民民主国家人民大量援朝物资抵朝  朝鲜政府正陆续分给战区灾民  国际妇联号召各国妇女支援朝鲜人民

1951-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　人民民主国家人民大量援朝物资抵朝       
    朝鲜政府正陆续分给战区灾民      
    国际妇联号召各国妇女支援朝鲜人民
    【新华社平壤三日电】各人民民主国家人民援助和慰劳朝鲜人民军和朝鲜人民的物资，正源源运抵此间。
    据此间获悉，除中国人民援助的大量物资外，最近收到的其他人民民主国家的物资有：捷克斯洛伐克人民赠送的毛织物七千多码，医药品二百五十三箱，内衣及其他衣类一百七十七箱；波兰人民赠送的医药品二十七箱，医疗器具六箱；德意志民主共和国人民赠送的医药品及衣服共四百七十五箱；匈牙利人民赠送的有毛织物、棉织物、毛毯、慰问袋及影片等。
    蒙古人民曾赠送慰问品十四车厢，其中有蒙古特产的毛皮被服，及棉衣、毛皮靴、皮鞋和其他毛皮制品等。
    各人民民主国家人民赠给朝鲜人民的物资，正由朝鲜各地人民政权机关陆续分发给战区灾民。到三月二十二日止，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府已将白布一百三十八万码分发给各地灾民。得到援助物资的灾民，对各兄弟国家的人民表示无限的感激。平壤市北区灾民金顺僩在领到援助物资时说：“我们在领到各人民民主国家人民的援助物资时，深刻地认识到全世界千百万人民是站在我们这一边；并在诚挚地帮助着我们。各人民民主国家的援助，使我们更加提高了战胜美国侵略者的信心，并加强了我们克服困难的力量。”
    【新华社布拉格三日电】国际民主妇女联合会书记处根据国际民主妇女联合会理事会柏林会议中国代表提出的建议，决定发动一个支援朝鲜人民的运动，募集一切可能帮助朝鲜妇女儿童的物品。书记处已于三月二十日分函通知所属各国妇女组织，赞扬正在保卫和平事业和全人类幸福前途的英勇的朝鲜人民。该函称：我们的庄严的任务是支援朝鲜人民的斗争，减轻朝鲜姊妹们在战争中遭受的苦难。我们必须捐输衣服及儿童衣物、药品以及尽可能多的现款。各国妇女团体应立即开展募款运动和征募与缝制衣服的运动，并动员工人、医生以至艺术家来参加支援工作；要利用无线电广播、报纸及其他一切方法来说明朝鲜的现况，并说明全世界妇女的援助将产生力量。该函在结语中称：通过这一支援运动，让朝鲜的母亲和儿童们感到我们的热情和友谊，感到世界人民的力量。全世界的人民都在帮助他们挽救他们的祖国，保卫世界的生存和和平。
