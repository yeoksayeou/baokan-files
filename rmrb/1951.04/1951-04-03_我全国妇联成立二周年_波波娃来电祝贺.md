Title: All-China Women's Federation Celebrates Second Anniversary; Popova Sends Congratulatory Telegram
Author:
Date: 1951-04-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: April 3rd marks the second anniversary of the founding of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation. Popova, Chairman of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, sent a congratulatory telegram. The text of the telegram is as follows:

Dear Madam Cai Chang:
On the occasion of the second anniversary of the founding of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, I extend warm congratulations and respect to you and the heroic Chinese sisters.

The All-China Democratic Women's Federation represents the true interests of Chinese women. It calls upon and mobilizes millions of women to develop the national economy and culture of the new China. Under the banner of the Women's International Democratic Federation, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation is actively fighting to consolidate and expand the unity of international women, oppose war, defend peace, and strive for a bright future for children. We wish you, dear friends, new successes in your future work.

                      Nina Popova, Chairman of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee

### 我全国妇联成立二周年  波波娃来电祝贺

1951-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　我全国妇联成立二周年        
    波波娃来电祝贺
    【新华社讯】四月三日为中华全国民主妇女联合会成立二周年纪念日。苏联妇女反法西斯委员会主席波波娃特来电祝贺。电文如下：亲爱的蔡畅夫人：
    兹值中华全国民主妇女联合会成立二周年纪念日，谨向您和英勇的中国姊妹们致热烈的祝贺和敬礼。
    中华全国民主妇女联合会代表着中国妇女的真正利益，它号召和动员千百万妇女群众去发展新中国的国民经济与文化。在国际民主妇联的旗帜下，中华全国民主妇女联合会为巩固与扩大国际妇女的团结，反对战争，保卫和平，以及争取儿童的美满的未来而积极斗争着。祝你们，亲爱的朋友们，在您们将来的工作中获得新的成功。
                      苏联妇女反法西斯委员会主席尼娜·波波娃
