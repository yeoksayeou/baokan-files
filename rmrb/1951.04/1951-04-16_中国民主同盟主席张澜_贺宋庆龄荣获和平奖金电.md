Title: Zhang Lan, Chairman of the China Democratic League, Congratulatory Telegram to Soong Ching Ling on Winning the Stalin Peace Prize

Author: 
Date: 1951-04-16
Page: 1

　　China Democratic League Chairman Zhang Lan
    Congratulatory Telegram to Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling on Winning the Peace Prize

    Vice Chairman Soong:
    I am delighted to learn that you have been awarded the inaugural "Strengthening International Peace" Stalin International Peace Prize. I extend my heartfelt congratulations and respect.

    This noble and great honor is a shining symbol of your consistent efforts, along with the people of the entire nation under the leadership of Chairman Mao, to safeguard world peace. Inspired by this great honor, all members of our Democratic League are willing to further strive to resolutely implement the World Peace Council's ten resolutions and completely crush American imperialist aggression!

          Zhang Lan
                April 14, 1951

### 中国民主同盟主席张澜  贺宋庆龄荣获和平奖金电

1951-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国民主同盟主席张澜
    贺宋庆龄荣获和平奖金电宋副主席：
    欣悉您荣膺首届“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金，谨致衷心的祝贺与敬意。
    这种崇高而伟大的荣誉，是您一贯地和全国人民在毛主席领导下、努力保卫世界和平的光辉标志。在这伟大荣誉的鼓舞下，我们民主同盟全体同志愿更进一步地为坚决执行世界和平理事会十项决议、彻底粉碎美国帝国主义侵略而斗争！
          张澜
                一九五一年四月十四日
