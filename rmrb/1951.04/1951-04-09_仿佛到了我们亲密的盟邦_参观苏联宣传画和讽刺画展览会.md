Title: As if Arriving in Our Close Ally -- Visiting the Soviet Propaganda Painting and Caricature Exhibition
Author: Yuan Shuipai
Date: 1951-04-09
Page: 3

　　As if Arriving in Our Close Ally

　  ——Visiting the Soviet Propaganda Painting and Caricature Exhibition
　　Here is not a quiet art gallery
　　Here is a bustling square, factory, and coal mine.
　　Strong men and women run out from the pictures, cheering and singing loudly,
　　You seem to have traveled to our close ally!

　　The voice of a powerful country resounds in your ears:
　　The steel furnace sputters sparks, the tractor rolls past,
　　Young people make vows, airplanes soar in the sky,
　　Women look at fat babies and say: Brave man, grow up quickly!

　　Peaceful construction! Respond to the war criminals' madness with overproduction.
　　Hitler's bloody aggression will never be forgotten:
　　Look, here are bloody bayonets pointing at mothers and children;
　　There, Ukrainian female guerrillas are tied to barbed wire.

　　Fight to Berlin! Glory to the people, glory to the armed forces!
　　Fireworks bloom like flowers in the sky, victory medals hang on chests,
　　Standing on Lenin's tomb is the great organizer of victory.
　　Follow him forever, follow the Bolshevik Party forever!

　　In that dark corner of the room, there are also photos of war criminals hanging,
　　Blood-stained fingerprints are pressed on their criminal records.
　　Churchill, Acheson, MacArthur...skinned, revealing their true forms.
　　The painter's pen pricks them, making them scream like rats...

　　Here is not a quiet art gallery,
　　Here is like a classroom telling a splendid history.
　　Learn from these firm, brave, wise, and self-respecting people,
　　Fight! Achieve victory! Just like them!
　　(Note) Most of the paintings mentioned in the poem are included in the photos published on this page today.

### 仿佛到了我们亲密的盟邦  ——参观苏联宣传画和讽刺画展览会
袁水拍
1951-04-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　仿佛到了我们亲密的盟邦
　  ——参观苏联宣传画和讽刺画展览会
     这里不是一个静悄悄的画廊
      这里是热闹的广场、工厂和煤矿。
      健壮的男女从图画里跑出来，欢呼，高唱，
      你仿佛旅行到了我们亲密的盟邦！
　
      一个强大国家的声音在你耳边喧响：
      炼钢炉喷溅火星，拖拉机滚过身旁，
      青年发出誓言，飞机在天空翱翔，
      妇女望着胖娃娃说：壮士，你快快成长！
　
      和平建设！用超额生产来答复战犯的疯狂。
      希特勒的血腥侵略，谁也不会遗忘：
      你看，这儿滴血的刺刀指着母亲和孩子；
      那儿乌克兰的女游击队员被绑在铁丝网上。
　
      打到柏林去！光荣归于人民，归于武装力量！
      烟火像花朵般开满天空，胜利的勋章挂在胸膛，
      列宁墓上站着的是伟大的胜利的组织者。
      永远跟着他，永远跟着布尔什维克党！
　
      在那阴暗的屋角里，还挂着战犯的照相，
      血染的指印捺在他们犯罪的纪录上。
      邱吉尔、艾奇逊、麦克阿瑟…剥了皮，现了形。
      画家的笔尖刺得他们像老鼠一样尖声叫嚷…
　
      这里不是一个静悄悄的画廊，
      这里像是一所讲述着绚烂的历史的课堂。
      学习这些坚定、勇敢、智慧、自尊的人们，
      战斗！取得胜利！就跟他们一样！
     （注）诗中所谈到的几幅画，大都包括在今天本版刊出的照片中。
