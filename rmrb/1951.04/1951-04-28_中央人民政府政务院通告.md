Title: Circular of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government
Author: 
Date: 1951-04-28
Page: 1

The following provisions are hereby made regarding the holiday arrangements for all agencies, organizations, schools, and factories in Beijing around the "May 1st" Labor Day this year:

　　I. Sunday, April 29th, will be a normal working day and there will be no holiday;
　　II. May 1st will be a one-day holiday;
　　III. May 2nd will be a one-day compensatory holiday.

　　It is hoped that you will comply with and implement this.

### 中央人民政府政务院通告

1951-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央人民政府政务院通告
    兹规定北京市各机关、团体、学校、工厂在本年“五一”劳动节前后放假办法如下：
    一、四月二十九日（星期日）一律照常工作，不放假；
    二、五月一日放假一天；
    三、五月二日补假一天。
    望即遵照实行。
