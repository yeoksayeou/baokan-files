Title: Government Administration Council Holds 79th Meeting Approves Comprehensive Report on Industrial Conference Passes Decision on State-Owned Industrial Production and Construction
Author:
Date: 1951-04-07
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 6th: The 79th Government Administration Council meeting of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 6th. The meeting listened to and approved the comprehensive report on the first Industrial Conference made by Song Shaowen, Director of the Central Financial and Economic Planning Bureau of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council, and adopted the decision of the Government Administration Council on state-owned industrial production and construction in 1951. The meeting also listened to and approved the report made by Minister of Light Industry Huang Yanpei of the Central People's Government on the ministry's work summary for 1950 and the guidelines and tasks for 1951. In addition, the meeting approved several appointment and removal cases and submitted several appointment and removal cases for approval by the Central People's Government Committee.

### 政务院举行第七十九次会议  批准关于工业会议的综合报告  通过国营工业生产建设的决定

1951-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院举行第七十九次会议
　  批准关于工业会议的综合报告
　  通过国营工业生产建设的决定
    【新华社六日讯】中央人民政府政务院第七十九次政务会议于六日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央财政经济计划局宋劭文局长所作的政务院财政经济委员会关于第一届工业会议的综合报告，通过了政务院关于一九五一年国营工业生产建设的决定。会议并听取和批准了中央人民政府轻工业部黄炎培部长所作的该部一九五零年工作总结与一九五一年的方针和任务的报告。此外，会议通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
