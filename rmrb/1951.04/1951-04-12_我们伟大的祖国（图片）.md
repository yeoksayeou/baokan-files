Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-04-12
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Our country is a big family of united and loving ethnic groups. This is a female teacher at a Uyghur primary school in Xinjiang, teaching students to read People's Pictorial. They are looking at the great image of the people's leader and the scenes of the construction of the new China with infinite innocence and joy.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-04-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我们的国家是一个各民族团结友爱的大家庭。这是新疆维吾尔族乡村小学的女教师，正在教学生们看人民画报。她们用充满着无限天真和喜悦的心情，望着人民领袖的伟大形象和新中国建设的图景。
