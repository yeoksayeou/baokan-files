Title: Pictures

Author: 
Date: 1951-04-24
Page: 4

Under the brutal oppression of the US-Japan reactionaries, the workers and peasants of Japan are increasingly strengthening their struggle for democracy and the right to live. The upper picture shows workers at the "Dai Nippon Printing Factory" fighting against the police who came to suppress them for opposing the factory's dismissal of progressive employees. The lower picture shows Japanese farmers demonstrating and marching to demand tax cuts. (Photos provided by the News Photography Bureau)
