Title: List of Committee Members and Alternate Members of the Tianjin Catholic Patriotic Reform Movement Promotion Committee
Author:
Date: 1951-04-09
Page: 1

　　Tianjin Catholic Patriotic Reform Movement Promotion Committee
　  List of Committee Members and Alternate Members
    [This newspaper's dispatch] The list of twenty-three committee members and three alternate committee members of the Tianjin Catholic Patriotic Reform Movement Promotion Committee is as follows:
    Committee Members: Wu Kezhai, Zhang Yushi, Zhao Zhenya, Hui Ruyu, Nie Guoping, Li Dongxu, Fan Enkun, Li Yaozong, Wu Jiansheng, Nie Guofan, Bai Zhanyou, Li Heding, Li Liangchen, Zhang Fukuan, Sun Zhefu, Dong Mingchun, Sui Shouyi, Jia Bin, Zhao Xifan, Sun Jiayu, Li Dongshan, Wu Dongchen, Liu Bozi.
    Alternate Committee Members: Jiang Zhongyu, Zhou Zongqi, Su Baozhao.
