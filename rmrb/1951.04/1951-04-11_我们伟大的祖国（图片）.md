    Our Great Motherland
    The water conservancy construction of the New China is developing rapidly. This is a scene of the ongoing Huaiyin Sluice repair project on the canal. The construction team is hoisting steel plates weighing thousands of pounds to drive sturdy steel sheet piles into the lock.

    我们伟大的祖国
