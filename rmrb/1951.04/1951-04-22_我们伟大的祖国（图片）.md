Our Great Motherland
　　Today is the third anniversary of the People's Liberation Army's recovery of Yan'an. Yan'an was the sacred place of the Chinese people's revolution during the Anti-Japanese War. This is the Pagoda Hill outside the South Gate of Yan'an.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

专栏：

　　我们伟大的祖国
    今天是人民解放军收复延安的三周年纪念日。延安是抗日战争期间中国人民革命的圣地。这是延安南门外的宝塔山。
