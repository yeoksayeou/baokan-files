Title: China Youth Daily, Organ of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, to Launch Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1951-04-26
Page: 1

　　China Youth Daily, Organ of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, to Launch Tomorrow

　　[This Dispatch] To strengthen guidance of the work of the Communist Youth League, and to conduct education on Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought for youth both within and outside the League, the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League will publish "China Youth Daily" on April 27th. The target audience of the newspaper includes young workers, farmers, students, cadres, staff, and teachers. The content will include reports on major domestic and international events, reports on youth activities (including reports on culture, education, and sports), analysis of youth thought, experiences in youth work, scientific knowledge, literary works, stories of revolutionary leaders and people's heroes, reference materials for propagandists and study tutors, book and film reviews, article writing techniques, readers' letters, illustrations, photographs, etc. The language will strive to be popular and accessible, so that young people with a junior high school education level can understand it.

　　"China Youth Daily" will be published three times a week, on Tuesdays and Fridays, and will be distributed by the post office.

### 青年团中央机关报中国青年报明天创刊

1951-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　青年团中央机关报中国青年报明天创刊
    【本报讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会，为了加强指导青年团的工作，和对青年团内外的青年进行马克思列宁主义、毛泽东思想的教育，将于四月二十七日出版《中国青年报》。该报对象为青年工人、农民、学生、干部、职员、教员等。内容有国内外大事、青年活动报道（包括文教体育报道）、青年思想分析、青年工作经验、科学知识、文艺作品、革命领袖和人民英雄的故事、宣传员和学习辅导员讲话参考资料、书影剧评介、文章作法、读者来信、图画、照片等，文字力求通俗化，凡具有高小文化程度的青年就能够看懂。
    《中国青年报》为三日刊，定每星期二、五出版，由邮局发行。
