Date: 1951-04-14

Under the policy of the United States to rearm Japan, the lives of the Japanese people are becoming increasingly impoverished. The number of unemployed and homeless people is constantly increasing. The upper picture shows a group of unemployed people waiting for job referrals in front of the Ueno Public Employment Security Office in Tokyo. The lower picture shows a group of homeless people temporarily living in makeshift shelters between large, open-air iron cylinders in a factory somewhere in Japan.
    (Photos provided by the News Photography Bureau)
