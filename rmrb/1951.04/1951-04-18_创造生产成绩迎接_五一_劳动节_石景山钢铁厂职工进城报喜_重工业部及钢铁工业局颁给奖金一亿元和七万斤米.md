Title: Creating Production Achievements to Welcome the "May Day" Labor Day: Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant Workers Enter the City to Announce Good News; Ministry of Heavy Industry and Iron and Steel Industry Bureau Awarded a Bonus of 100 Million Yuan and 70,000 Jin of Rice
Author:
Date: 1951-04-18
Page: 1

　　Creating Production Achievements to Welcome the "May Day" Labor Day
　  Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant Workers Enter the City to Announce Good News
　  Ministry of Heavy Industry and Iron and Steel Industry Bureau Awarded a Bonus of 100 Million Yuan and 70,000 Jin of Rice
    [Xinhua News Agency, 17th] In order to welcome this year's "May Day" Labor Day, the workers of Beijing Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant have launched a patriotic production competition to resist US aggression and aid Korea since the beginning of spring, and have achieved eight major results. These achievements are: (1) A certain blast furnace previously required six months for major repairs, but this time it only took 53 days to complete the entire overhaul; (2) In spring production, the output of another newly repaired blast furnace reached 238% of the highest record during the Japanese puppet period, and the quality also exceeded the standards stipulated by the Iron and Steel Industry Bureau; (3) Coke production has repeatedly set nine new records, reaching 200% of the output during the Japanese puppet period, and the cost has been reduced by 18.5% compared to the regulations; (4) The turnover rate of in-plant train transportation increased from 1.27 to 1.63; (5) The finished product rate of cast pipes was stipulated to be 91%, but the average finished product rate in spring reached 93.5%, and even reached 97.65% in March; (6) The power department basically achieved safe operation, and the coal consumption in March was further reduced to 0.098 kg per kilowatt-hour compared to February; (7) The cost calculation of the accounting department can be completed before the 15th of the following month, setting a new national record; (8) Cold waste materials worth 20 billion yuan were cleared out.
    On the 17th, the factory workers formed a reporting team of 160 people to report the good news of production to the Central People's Government, the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Ministry of Heavy Industry of the Central People's Government, the Iron and Steel Industry Bureau, the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, and the Municipal Youth Committee and other organs and organizations. This reporting team was warmly welcomed. The Ministry of Heavy Industry awarded a bonus of 100 million yuan for this purpose, and the Iron and Steel Industry Bureau of the Ministry also awarded a bonus of 70,000 jin of rice to reward the factory workers. In the good news letter from the factory workers to Chairman Mao, they wrote: "Respected Chairman Mao: In order to welcome our working class's great festival, 'May Day,' all the workers of our Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant have launched a patriotic production competition to resist US aggression and aid Korea, and have created unprecedented new records in all aspects. We are now reporting the good news to you with very excited and happy hearts."

### 创造生产成绩迎接“五一”劳动节  石景山钢铁厂职工进城报喜  重工业部及钢铁工业局颁给奖金一亿元和七万斤米

1951-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　创造生产成绩迎接“五一”劳动节
　  石景山钢铁厂职工进城报喜
　  重工业部及钢铁工业局颁给奖金一亿元和七万斤米
    【新华社十七日讯】北京石景山钢铁厂职工为了迎接今年的“五一”劳动节，自入春以来即展开抗美援朝爱国主义生产竞赛，获得八项主要成绩，这些成绩是：（一）某高炉过去大修需时六月，这次只用了五十三天功夫，即全部完成了大修工程；（二）在春季生产中，另一新修复的高炉的产量，达到日伪时期最高纪录的百分之二百三十八，而且质量也超过了钢铁工业局所规定的标准；（三）焦炭产量连创九次新纪录，达到日伪时期产量百分之二百，成本较规定减低百分之一八·五；（四）厂内火车运输周转率由一·二七提高到一·六三；（五）铸管的成品率，规定是百分之九一，而春季平均成品率竟达百分之九三·五，三月份更达百分之九七·六五；（六）动力部门基本上作到安全运转，三月份煤耗较二月份又降低至每度○·○九八公斤；（七）会计部门的成本计算，在次月十五日前即可完成，创全国新纪录；（八）清理出价值二百亿元的冷废物品。
    该厂职工特于十七日组成一个一百六十人的报喜队，前往中央人民政府、政务院财政经济委员会、全国总工会、中央人民政府重工业部、钢铁工业局及中共北京市委会、市总工会、市青委会等机关团体报告生产喜讯。这一报喜队曾受到热烈的欢迎，重工业部为此颁发了奖金一亿元，该部钢铁工业局亦颁发了奖金七万斤米，以奖励该厂职工。该厂职工给毛主席的报喜信上写道：“敬爱的毛主席：为了迎接咱们工人阶级伟大的节日“五一”，我们石景山钢铁厂全体职工，展开了抗美援朝爱国主义生产竞赛运动，在各方面都创造了空前的新纪录，现在以非常兴奋快乐的心情向您报喜。”
