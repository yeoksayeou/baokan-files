Title: Foreign Minister Zhou Receives Hoang Van Hoan
Author:
Date: 1951-04-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 26th—Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, received Hoang Van Hoan, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary Representative of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs at 3:30 p.m. today to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials. Those present included Chen Jiakang, Acting Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 周外长接见黄文欢

1951-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　周外长接见黄文欢
    【新华社二十六日讯】中央人民政府外交部周恩来部长于今日下午三时半在外交部接见越南民主共和国驻华首任特命全权大使衔代表黄文欢，商谈呈递国书事宜。在座者尚有外交部亚洲司代司长陈家康、交际处处长王倬如等。
