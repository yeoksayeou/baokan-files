Title: Cai Chang Sends a Telegram to the Hungarian Democratic Women's League to Congratulate Hungary's National Day
Author:
Date: 1951-04-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd:

　　Cai Chang Sends a Telegram to the Hungarian Democratic Women's League to Congratulate Hungary's National Day

    On April 4th, the National Day of the Hungarian People's Republic, Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, sent a telegram to the Hungarian Democratic Women's League to congratulate them. The telegram reads as follows: To Mrs. Magda Joboru, General Secretary of the Hungarian Democratic Women's League:

    On the occasion of the National Day of the Hungarian People's Republic, I extend my warm congratulations to the Hungarian sisters on behalf of all the women of China; and I wish the Hungarian people and women even greater success in defending world peace and completing the Five-Year Economic Plan.

                                      Cai Chang, March 30th

### 蔡畅致电匈民主妇联  祝贺匈牙利国庆日

1951-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　蔡畅致电匈民主妇联        
    祝贺匈牙利国庆日
    【新华社二日讯】四月四日是匈牙利人民共和国国庆日，中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅致电匈牙利民主妇女联盟祝贺。电文如下：匈牙利民主妇女联盟总书记玛格达·尤波鲁夫人：
    兹值匈牙利人民共和国国庆日，谨代表全中国妇女向匈牙利姊妹们致热烈的庆贺；并预祝匈牙利人民和妇女在保卫世界和平，完成五年经济计划的工作中，获得更大的成功。
                                      蔡畅  三月三十日
