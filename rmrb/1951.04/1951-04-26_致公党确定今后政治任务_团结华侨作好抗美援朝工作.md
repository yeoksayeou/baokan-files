Title: Zhi Gong Party Determines Future Political Tasks: Unite Overseas Chinese to Do a Good Job in Resisting America and Aiding Korea

Author:
Date: 1951-04-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, April 21st: The Central Committee of the China Zhi Gong Party held its Fourth Session of the Second Plenary Enlarged Meeting in Guangzhou in early April. A total of twenty-eight people attended the meeting, including members of the Central Committee of the Party, alternate members of the Central Committee, and representatives from home and abroad.

　　The meeting listened to and discussed the report of the person in charge of the Party Central Committee on the current political tasks and the Party affairs work in the past year. The meeting determined that the Party's future political tasks are: to unite overseas Chinese and fight for the three central tasks of resisting America and aiding Korea, land reform, and suppressing counter-revolutionaries. The meeting determined that the Party's future organizational policy is: to focus on consolidating existing organizations domestically, and to expand organizations abroad on the basis of consolidation. At the same time, the meeting decided that all party members should unite and educate the vast number of overseas Chinese and their relatives in the three major movements of resisting America and aiding Korea, land reform, and suppressing counter-revolutionaries, and consolidate the people's democratic united front.

　　At the meeting, Rao Zhangfeng, Acting Minister of the United Front Work Department of the South China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, was invited to give a report on the current situation. Situ Meitang, a leader of overseas Chinese in the Americas, reported on his visit to the land reform in central Guangdong and the protection of the interests of overseas Chinese in the land reform.

### 致公党确定今后政治任务  团结华侨作好抗美援朝工作

1951-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　致公党确定今后政治任务
　  团结华侨作好抗美援朝工作
    【新华社广州二十一日电】中国致公党中央委员会于四月上旬在广州举行第四届第二次全会扩大会议。出席会议的有该党中央委员、候补中央委员及海内外各地代表共二十八人。
    会议听取和讨论了该党中央负责人关于当前政治任务及一年来党务工作的报告。会议确定该党今后的政治任务是：团结华侨，为抗美援朝、土地改革、镇压反革命三大中心工作而斗争。会议确定该党今后的组织方针是：国内方面侧重巩固现有组织，国外方面，则在现有基础上从巩固中去扩大组织。同时，会议决定全党党员应在抗美援朝、土地改革、镇压反革命三大运动中团结与教育广大华侨与侨眷，巩固人民民主统一战线。
    会上，中共中央华南分局统一战线工作部代理部长饶彰风曾应邀作了关于目前形势的报告。美洲华侨领袖司徒美堂作了参观粤中土地改革的情况及土地改革中华侨利益受到保护的报告。
