Title: General Notice from the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association: Launching Sino-Soviet Friendship Correspondence; Calling for Emulation of Tianjin's Methods to Mobilize the Masses to Write Letters to Soviet Friends on "May 1st" and "May 4th"

Author: 
Date: 1951-04-30
Page: 1

　　General Notice from the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
　  Launching Sino-Soviet Friendship Correspondence
　  Calling for Emulation of Tianjin's Methods to Mobilize the Masses to Write Letters to Soviet Friends on "May 1st" and "May 4th"
    Xinhua News Agency, 29th: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association General Headquarters has issued a notice to its branches throughout the country to launch a Sino-Soviet friendship correspondence campaign during the "May 1st" Labor Day and "May 4th" Youth Day. The notice states: The Tianjin Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, in conjunction with the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, the Municipal Communist Youth League Working Committee, the Municipal Democratic Youth Federation, and the Municipal Student Federation, has initiated a "Sino-Soviet Friendship Correspondence Campaign" to write letters to Soviet workers and young friends on "May 1st" Labor Day and "May 4th" Youth Day. This movement is of great significance in enhancing the friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union. It is hoped that the Friendship Associations at all levels will follow Tianjin's methods and the General Headquarters' previous regulations to vigorously mobilize the local masses to carry out this campaign. Letters from various regions can first be submitted to the local Friendship Associations, and then forwarded to the General Headquarters for distribution to Soviet friends.

### 中苏友好协会总会通报  开展中苏友好通信  号召仿效天津办法发动群众于“五一”“五四”写信给苏联朋友

1951-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏友好协会总会通报
　  开展中苏友好通信
　  号召仿效天津办法发动群众于“五一”“五四”写信给苏联朋友
    【新华社二十九日讯】中苏友好协会总会为在“五一”劳动节与“五四”青年节开展中苏友好通信运动，特向全国各地分会发出通报。该通报称：天津市中苏友好协会与市总工会、市青年团工作委员会、市民主青年联合会、市学生联合会等联合发起“中苏友好通信运动”，在“五一”劳动节与“五四”青年节给苏联工人、青年朋友写信。这一运动对增进望各级中苏两国人民间友谊有极大的意义。友协仿照天津的办法和总会以前的规定大力发动当地群众开展此项运动。各地信件可先交各地友协，然后汇交总会发给苏联友人。
