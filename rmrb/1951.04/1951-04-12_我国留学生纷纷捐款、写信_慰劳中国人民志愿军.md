Title: Chinese Students Abroad Donate Funds and Write Letters to Console the Chinese People's Volunteers

Author:
Date: 1951-04-12
Page: 4

　　Chinese Students Abroad Donate Funds and Write Letters to Console the Chinese People's Volunteers

Xinhua News Agency, 11th: Chinese students studying abroad and staff members of embassies abroad continue to donate funds to console the Chinese People's Volunteers. The China People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea recently received three batches of consolation money donated by Chinese students, including donations from Chinese students in Romania totaling 371,311 yuan in Renminbi, donations from Chinese students in India totaling 23,937,000 yuan in Renminbi, and donations from Chinese students in Switzerland totaling 42,901,300 yuan in Renminbi.

The association also recently received a letter of condolence written by all the female staff members of our embassy in Hungary to the Chinese People's Volunteers on "March 8" Women's Day, with a donation of consolation money totaling 4,764,549 yuan in Renminbi. The letter stated that they are determined to take practical actions to oppose American aggression and guarantee to regularly introduce the great achievements of the Volunteer Army in Korea to the Hungarian people.

### 我国留学生纷纷捐款、写信  慰劳中国人民志愿军

1951-04-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　我国留学生纷纷捐款、写信
    慰劳中国人民志愿军
    【新华社十一日讯】我海外留学生和驻外大使馆工作人员继续捐款慰劳中国人民志愿军。中国人民抗美援朝总会最近收到了三批中国留学生捐献的慰劳金，计有罗马尼亚中国留学生的捐款合人民币三七一、三一一元，印度中国留学生的捐款合人民币二三、九三七、○○○元，瑞士中国留学生的捐款合人民币四二、九○一、三○○元。
    该会最近又收到了我驻匈牙利大使馆全体女工作人员在“三八”妇女节写给中国人民志愿军的慰问信，附捐献的慰劳金共计人民币四、七六四、五四九元。信中表示决以实际行动反对美国侵略，并保证将志愿军在朝鲜的伟大战绩经常介绍给匈牙利人民。
