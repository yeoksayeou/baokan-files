Title: Volunteer Army Air Defense Force's Three-Day Achievement: 23 Enemy Planes Shot Down or Damaged; A Certain Unit Shot Down or Damaged Five Enemy Planes Each in Five Days
Author: 
Date: 1951-04-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, April 27th: In the three days from the 21st to the 23rd of this month, the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's air defense forces shot down 17 enemy planes and damaged 6 enemy planes over the front and rear areas. A certain air defense unit, in its air defense operations at the front, achieved brilliant results by shooting down and damaging five enemy planes each in five days, thanks to its spirit of calm and boldness in combat and its ability to meticulously organize a fire net and suddenly open fire on diving enemy planes. In addition to awarding the unit a commendation, the unit's superior leading organ issued a circular to commend them.

### 志愿军防空部队三天战果  击落击伤敌机廿三架  某部五天击落击伤敌机各五架

1951-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　志愿军防空部队三天战果
　  击落击伤敌机廿三架
　  某部五天击落击伤敌机各五架
    【新华社朝鲜前线二十七日电】中国人民志愿军防空部队在本月二十一日至二十三日的三天内，又在前线和后方上空击落敌机十七架，击伤敌机六架。某防空部队在前线对空作战中由于发扬沉着大胆的战斗精神，并能周密组织火网，对俯冲的敌机突然一齐开火，因而获得五天内击落击伤敌机各五架的辉煌战果。该部上级领导机关除给该部记功外，并予以通报表扬。
