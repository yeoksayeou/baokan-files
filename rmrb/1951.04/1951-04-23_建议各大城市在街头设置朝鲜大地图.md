Title: Suggest Setting Up Large Maps of Korea on Streets in Major Cities
Author: Yao Tianbao Wang Zhongzhu Zhou Shaoli Peng Lin Xiong Dehui
Date: 1951-04-23
Page: 2

    Suggestion to set up large maps of Korea on the streets of major cities
    To the Editors:
    We saw in a Soviet pictorial a large map of Korea in a prosperous street, clearly marked with arrows indicating the current war situation, and with text explaining the purpose of the American imperialist aggression against Korea. We think this propaganda method is very good. We hope that propaganda organs throughout the country can set up large Korean maps in busy traffic areas, marking the development of the war situation, to help people understand the Korean war situation.
      Hunan Provincial No. 1 Middle School Yao Tianbao, Wang Zhongzhu, Zhou Shaoli, Peng Lin, Xiong Dehui

### 建议各大城市在街头设置朝鲜大地图
姚天保　王中柱　周绍力　彭霖　熊德辉
1951-04-23
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    建议各大城市在街头设置朝鲜大地图编辑同志：
    我们在一本苏联画报上，看到在一个繁荣的街道上，有一个很大的朝鲜地图，上面清楚地画着箭头说明目前战局形势，并用文字说明美帝国主义侵略朝鲜的目的。我们认为这样的宣传方法很好。希望全国各地宣传机关都能在交通要道设置大型的朝鲜地图，标明战争形势的发展，以帮助人民了解朝鲜战局。
      湖南省立第一中学  姚天保、王中柱、周绍力、彭霖、熊德辉
