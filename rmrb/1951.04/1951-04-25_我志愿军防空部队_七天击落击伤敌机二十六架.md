Title: Our Volunteer Army Anti-Aircraft Units Shot Down and Damaged 26 Enemy Planes in Seven Days
Author:
Date: 1951-04-25
Page: 1

　　Our Volunteer Army Anti-Aircraft Units
　  Shot Down and Damaged 26 Enemy Planes in Seven Days
    [Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, 24th] From the 16th to the 22nd of this month, the anti-aircraft units of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army shot down 16 enemy planes and damaged 10 enemy planes over the front lines and rear areas of Korea.

### 我志愿军防空部队  七天击落击伤敌机二十六架

1951-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　我志愿军防空部队
　  七天击落击伤敌机二十六架
    【新华社朝鲜前线二十四日电】中国人民志愿军防空部队从本月十六日至二十二日又在朝鲜前线和后方的上空击落敌机十六架，击伤敌机十架。
