Title: Beijing Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association Calls for Donations of Books and Newspapers for the Volunteer Army
Author:
Date: 1951-04-06
Page: 3

The Beijing branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression issued a notice on the 2nd to collect books, magazines, and pictorials for the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. The original text is as follows:

Since joining forces with the Korean People's Army, our People's Volunteer Army has repeatedly defeated the American aggressors and achieved glorious and decisive victories, which are of great significance for defending peace in the Far East and the world.

In order to enable our People's Volunteer Army to understand the achievements of the people of the motherland in all aspects of the Resist America, Aid Korea patriotic movement, and to meet their urgent need for cultural nourishment, this association has launched a campaign to collect books, magazines, and pictorials for the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. We call on all factories, schools, parties, organizations, and groups in the city to actively respond to this work and enthusiastically donate, in order to give our People's Volunteer Army greater encouragement culturally and spiritually.

Specific measures:

　　(1) People from all walks of life should be widely mobilized to enthusiastically donate all kinds of popular books, magazines, and pictorials suitable for soldiers to read (the content should preferably praise the heroic deeds of the people and the positive producers related to the Resist America, Aid Korea patriotic production competition).

　　(2) People from all walks of life can hand in donated books and newspapers to the Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression in their respective units, or to the branches of Beijing Xinhua Bookstore entrusted by this association, for collection and transfer to the People's Volunteer Army.

　　(3) This work will start from April 1st and end on April 30th.

### 北京市抗美援朝分会  号召为志愿军捐书报

1951-04-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　北京市抗美援朝分会
　  号召为志愿军捐书报
    北京市中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会，二日发出为我人民志愿军募集图书、杂志、画报的通知，原文如下：
    我人民志愿军自与朝鲜人民军并肩作战以来，已数次打败美国侵略军，取得了光辉的决定性的胜利，对保卫远东与世界的和平，有极重大的意义。
    为使我人民志愿军了解祖国人民在抗美援朝爱国运动中各方面的成就，并满足他们对文化食粮的迫切需要，本会特发起为中国人民志愿军募集图书、杂志、画报。并号召全市所有工厂、学校、党派、机关、团体积极响应此项工作，踊跃捐赠，以期在文化上与精神上给予我人民志愿军更大的鼓励。
    具体办法：
    （一）由各界人民普遍发动踊跃捐赠各种适合于战士阅读的通俗书刊、画报、连环画等，（其内容以歌颂人民英雄事迹及有关抗美援朝爱国生产竞赛积极生产者为最好）。
    （二）各界人民捐赠书报可交到各单位的保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会，或本会委托的北京新华书店各分店，以便代收汇集转送人民志愿军。
    （三）此项工作自四月一日起至四月三十日截止。
