International Developments

	Vietnam Youth Cadre Conference Calls for the Collection of Signatures for Declarations Supporting the Conclusion of a Peace Pact
	All participants in the Vietnam Youth Salvation Association cadre conference unanimously signed the World Peace Council's declaration on the conclusion of a peace pact among the five major powers at the March 20th meeting. At that meeting, Nguyen Xuan Tram, Deputy Secretary of the Vietnam Peace Committee, reported on the situation of the World Peace Council meeting in Berlin and the growing strength of world peace forces. He called on Vietnamese youth to launch a nationwide movement to collect signatures for the World Peace Council's declaration.
	Korean Cabinet Decides to Reward Agricultural Labor Heroes
	The Korean Cabinet issued instructions on the 13th, stipulating that "Victory Flag" and certificates of merit be awarded to cities, counties, and villages with excellent results in the agricultural production increase emulation campaign, in order to encourage farmers in various places to increase grain production, support the front lines, and supply the rear.

### 国际动态

1951-04-23
第4版()
专栏：国际动态

　　国际动态
  越南青年干部会议号召征集拥护缔结和平公约宣言签名
    参加越南青年救国会干部会议的全体人员在三月二十日会议中，一致在世界和平理事会关于缔结五大国和平公约的宣言上签了名。在那次会议中，越南和平委员会副书记阮春针报告了世界和平理事会在柏林开会的情形及目前世界和平力量日益壮大的情形。他号召越南青年发动一个全国规模的运动，为世界和平理事会的宣言征集签名。
  朝内阁决定奖励农业劳动英雄
    朝鲜内阁于十三日发布指示，规定以“胜利旗”和奖状授予在农业增产竞赛运动中成绩优良的市、县、村，以鼓励各地农民增产粮食，支援前线和供应后方。
