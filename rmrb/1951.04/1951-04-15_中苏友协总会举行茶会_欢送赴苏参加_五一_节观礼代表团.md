Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters Holds Tea Party to See Off Delegation to Soviet Union to Attend "May Day" Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1951-04-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 14th: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters, at the invitation of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, is sending a delegation to the Soviet Union to participate in the "May Day" celebrations. The Association held a tea party at 2:00 PM on the 14th to see off the delegation. Those present included Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Zhang Lan, and Huang Yanpei, vice presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters, as well as directors and staff members of the Association in Beijing, Soviet Ambassador to China N.V. Roshchin and personnel from the Soviet Embassy, Soviet representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries S.A. Safonov, the head of the delegation to the Soviet Union to attend the "May Day" celebrations Lin Boqu, the deputy head Shen Junru and all the representatives, as well as representatives from the China People's Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea Headquarters, the International Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Liaison, the Academy of Sciences, the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and various news organizations, totaling more than 90 people.

　　Guo Moruo, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters, delivered a farewell speech at the meeting, saying: The May Day celebration in Moscow is a concentrated expression of the great victory of the working people of the world. The trip of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association's delegation to the Soviet Union to participate in the May Day celebration is of great significance. The Soviet Union has a wealth of valuable experience worth learning from. He hoped that the delegation would have good gains to bring back. He wished the delegation success in completing its mission and a victorious return.

　　Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the China People's Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea Headquarters, He Xiangning and Li Lisan, directors of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters, also spoke. Ambassador Roshchin was also invited to speak, and he hoped that the representatives would introduce China's economic and cultural construction and the living conditions of workers and farmers to the Soviet people, and tell the Soviet people about the great victories and heroic achievements of the Chinese people in resisting American aggressors in Korea.

　　Lin Boqu and Shen Junru, the head and deputy head of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association's delegation to the Soviet Union to participate in the "May Day" celebrations, delivered speeches in reply, both expressing that the delegation will ensure that the victory of the Chinese people is told to the Soviet people, learn from the Soviet people, and successfully complete this glorious mission.

　　The meeting proceeded in an excited and joyful atmosphere throughout. The meeting adjourned at 4:00 PM.

### 中苏友协总会举行茶会  欢送赴苏参加“五一”节观礼代表团

1951-04-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏友协总会举行茶会
    欢送赴苏参加“五一”节观礼代表团
    【新华社十四日讯】中苏友好协会总会应苏联对外文化协会的邀请，派遣代表团前往苏联参加“五一”节观礼。该会于十四日下午二时举行茶会，欢送即将赴苏观礼的代表团。到会的有中苏友好协会总会副会长李济深、郭沫若、张澜、黄炎培及该会在北京理事、干事，苏联驻华大使馆罗申大使及该大使馆人员，苏联对外文化协会驻华代表萨福隆等，赴苏参加“五一”节观礼团团长林伯渠、副团长沈钧儒和全体代表，以及中国人民抗美援朝总会，中央人民政府外交部国际司、对外文化联络局、科学院、中国人民外交学会、各新闻机关等代表，共计九十余人。
    中苏友好协会总会副会长郭沫若在会上致欢送词时说：莫斯科的“五一”劳动节纪念，是全世界劳动人民伟大胜利的集中表现。中苏友好协会赴苏参加“五一”节观礼代表团此行意义非常重大。苏联有很丰富的宝贵经验值得我们学习，希望代表团有很好的收获带回来。他预祝代表团完成任务、胜利归来。
    继续讲话的还有中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席陈叔通、中苏友好协会总会理事何香凝、李立三。罗申大使也应邀讲话，他希望代表们把中国的经济、文化建设和工人、农民的生活情况介绍给苏联人民，把中国人民在朝鲜抗击美国侵略者所得到伟大胜利和英雄的事业告诉苏联人民。
    中苏友好协会赴苏参加“五一”节观礼代表团正副团长林伯渠、沈钧儒相继致答词，他们都表示：代表团将保证把中国人民的胜利告诉苏联人民，并向苏联人民学习，胜利完成此行光荣任务。
    会议始终在兴奋愉快的气氛中进行。四时散会。
