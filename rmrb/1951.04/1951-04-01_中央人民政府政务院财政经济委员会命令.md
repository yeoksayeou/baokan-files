Title: Order of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government
Author:
Date: 1951-04-01
Page: 2

　　Order of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government
    The "Implementation Measures for the Provisional Regulations on Private Enterprises" are hereby promulgated, and it is hoped that they will be followed and implemented accordingly.
                                            Director: Chen Yun
                                  March 30, 1951
