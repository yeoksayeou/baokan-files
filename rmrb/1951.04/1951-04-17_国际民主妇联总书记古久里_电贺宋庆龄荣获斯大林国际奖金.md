Title: Secretary-General of the Women's International Democratic Federation, Eugénie Cotton, Cables Congratulations to Soong Ching Ling on Winning the Stalin International Peace Prize

Author:

Date: 1951-04-17
Page: 4

　　Secretary-General of the Women's International Democratic Federation, Eugénie Cotton
    Cables Congratulations to Soong Ching Ling on Winning the Stalin International Peace Prize

    [Xinhua News Agency, 16th] Madam Eugénie Cotton, Secretary-General of the Women's International Democratic Federation, sent a cable from Berlin on the 10th to Vice-Chairman Soong Ching Ling congratulating her on winning the "Strengthening International Peace" Stalin International Peace Prize. The cable reads as follows:

    We are delighted to learn that Your Excellency has been awarded the Stalin International Peace Prize for your valuable contributions to the cause of strengthening world peace. The Women's International Democratic Federation extends its warmest congratulations.

        Eugénie Cotton

### 国际民主妇联总书记古久里  电贺宋庆龄荣获斯大林国际奖金

1951-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　国际民主妇联总书记古久里
    电贺宋庆龄荣获斯大林国际奖金
    【新华社十六日讯】国际民主妇女联合会总书记瓦扬——古久里夫人，十日自柏林电贺宋庆龄副主席荣获“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金。电文如下：
    欣闻阁下由于对加强世界和平事业的宝贵贡献，获得斯大林国际奖金，国际民主妇女联合会谨致以热烈的祝贺。
        瓦扬——古久里
