Title: Party Organizations in Beijing, Tianjin, and Other Places Hold Discussions on the Issue of Suppressing Counter-Revolutionaries
Author:
Date: 1951-04-11
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Local organizations of various democratic parties in Beijing, Tianjin, Chongqing, and other places have successively held symposiums to discuss the issue of suppressing counter-revolutionaries. The Beijing branch of the China Democratic League invited people from the capital's judicial circles on the 4th to hold a discussion on the Regulations on the Punishment of Counter-Revolutionaries. At a symposium held on the 26th of last month, the preparatory committee of the Tianjin branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, the participants unanimously agreed that vigilance should be heightened at all times to strictly prevent counter-revolutionary elements from carrying out activities, and that the policy of suppressing counter-revolutionaries should be widely publicized to the citizens. The Tianjin branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association invited people from the city's business and industrial circles on the 23rd of last month to hold a symposium to discuss how the business and industrial circles could assist the government in suppressing counter-revolutionaries. The Chongqing Temporary Working Committee of the association held ten symposiums continuously from the 19th to the 23rd of last month, with more than 500 people from various industries such as cotton spinning and cotton yarn participating. The Southern General Branch of the China Democratic League invited professors from universities in Guangzhou to a forum on the 25th of last month, and the participants spoke enthusiastically. Li Zucai, a professor at Zhongshan University, described the crimes of counter-revolutionary elements he witnessed while working in Guangxi, thereby illustrating the necessity of suppressing counter-revolutionary activities.

### 北京天津等地各党派地方组织　座谈镇压反革命问题

1951-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京天津等地各党派地方组织
　　座谈镇压反革命问题
    【新华社九日讯】北京、天津、重庆等地各民主党派地方组织相继举行座谈会，讨论镇压反革命问题。中国民主同盟北京市支部于四日邀请首都司法界人士举行座谈，讨论惩治反革命条例。中国国民党革命委员会天津市分部筹备委员会在上月二十六日召开的座谈会上，与会者一致认为：应该随时提高警惕，严防反革命分子活动，并应广泛向市民宣传镇压反革命的政策。民主建国会天津分会于上月二十三日邀集该市工商界人士举行座谈，讨论了工商界如何协助政府镇压反革命的问题。该会重庆市临时工作委员会于上月十九日至二十三日连续举行了十一次座谈会，参加座谈的包括棉纺、花纱等各行业人士五百余人。中国民主同盟南方总支部于上月二十五日邀集广州市各大学教授座谈，与会者都热烈发言。中山大学教授李祖材叙述了他在广西工作时亲眼看到的反革命分子的罪行，以此说明镇压反革命活动的必要。
