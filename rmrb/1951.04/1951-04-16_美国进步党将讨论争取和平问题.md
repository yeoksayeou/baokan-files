Title: American Progressive Party to Discuss the Issue of Striving for Peace
Author:
Date: 1951-04-16
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, New York, 15th. TASS dispatch: Vinson, Chairman of the National Committee of the American Progressive Party, and Baldwin, Secretary, announced: "The National Committee of the Progressive Party will hold a special meeting in Chicago on April 14th and 15th to specifically discuss the issue of mobilizing to strive for peace." They announced: "At this critical autumn in our history, the National Committee of the Progressive Party is holding a meeting to plan a strategy for strengthening the peace forces of our people, to answer those who want to drive the country towards war and suppress the voices of the American people, and to cooperate with all groups pursuing peace, civil rights, and prosperity, in order to lay the foundation for participating in the 1952 election."

### 美国进步党将讨论争取和平问题

1951-04-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国进步党将讨论争取和平问题
    【新华社十五日讯】塔斯社纽约十四日讯：美国进步党全国委员会主席宾逊与书记鲍尔温宣布：“进步党全国委员会定于四月十四、十五两日在芝加哥举行特别会议，专门讨论动员起来争取和平的问题。”他们宣布：“在我们历史上这一危急之秋，进步党全国委员会举行会议，是为了策划加强我国人民和平力量的方针，以回答那些要把国家驱向战争和压制美国人民的声音的人们，并与一切追求和平、民权与繁荣的团体合作，奠定参加一九五二年选举的基础。”
