Title: List of Leaders of the Second Council and Working Bodies of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs
Author:
Date: 1951-04-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th dispatch: The list of leaders of the Second Council and working bodies of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs is as follows: Honorary President: Zhou Enlai. President: Zhang Xiruo. Vice Presidents: Zhou Gengsheng, Chen Hansheng, Hu Yuzhi, Qian Duansheng, Qiao Guanhua. Secretary-General: Wu Maosun. Council Members: Wang Bingnan, Wang Tieya, Wu Xiuquan, Shen Duanxian, He Sijing, Shao Lizi, Jin Zhonghua, Ke Bonian, Xu Yongying, Huan Xiang, Chen Jiakang, Weng Dujian, Zhang Hanfu, Huang Qixiang, Mei Ru'ao, Fei Xiaotong, Fei Qing, Zhang Mingyang, Zhang Jiongbo, Lu Cui, Yang Gang, Liao Chengzhi, Liu Guanyi, Liu Ningyi, Fan Hong, Lai Yali, Qian Junrui, Ji Chaoding, Luo Longji, Gong Peng.

　　Research Committee Director: Luo Longji. Deputy Directors: Ke Bonian, Ji Chaoding, Wang Tieya.

　　Compilation Committee Director: Qiao Guanhua. Deputy Directors: Huan Xiang, Chen Tiqiang, Feng Binfu.

　　Library Director: Hu Yuzhi. Deputy Directors: Zhang Jiongbo, Yang Gang, Gong Peng.

### 中国人民外交学会二届理事会及工作机构负责人名单

1951-04-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民外交学会二届理事会及工作机构负责人名单
    【新华社九日讯】中国人民外交学会第二届理事会及工作机构负责人名单如下：名誉会长：周恩来。会长张奚若。副会长：周鲠生、陈翰笙、胡愈之、钱端升、乔冠华。秘书长吴茂荪。理事：王炳南、王铁崖、伍修权、沈端先、何思敬、邵力子、金仲华、柯柏年、徐永煐、宦乡、陈家康、翁独健、章汉夫、黄琪翔、梅汝璈、费孝通、费青、张明养、张絅伯、陆璀、杨刚、廖承志、刘贯一、刘宁一、樊弘、赖亚力、钱俊瑞、冀朝鼎、罗隆基、龚澎。
    研究委员会主任：罗隆基。副主任：柯柏年、冀朝鼎、王铁崖。
    编译委员会主任：乔冠华。副主任：宦乡、陈体强、冯宾符。
    图书资料室主任：胡愈之。副主任：张絅伯、杨刚、龚澎。
