Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-04-04
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Women in New China are experts on the production front. Di Fenglan, a female labor model from Shijiazhuang's Shizhuang Village in Hebei Province, is leading her sister, Di Fengzhen, driving a cart to deliver fertilizer to the fields. She said, "More fertilizer in the spring plowing means more grain in the autumn harvest, ensuring this year's output exceeds 1950."

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新中国的妇女是生产战线上的能手。河北石家庄市郊使庄村的妇女劳动模范狄凤兰，带着她的妹妹狄凤珍，正赶着大车，向地里运送肥料。她说：“春耕多上肥，秋天多打粮，保证今年产量超过一九五○年。”
