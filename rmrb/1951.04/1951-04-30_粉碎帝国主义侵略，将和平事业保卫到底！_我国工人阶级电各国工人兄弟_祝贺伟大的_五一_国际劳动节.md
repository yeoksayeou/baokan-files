Title: Smash Imperialist Aggression and Defend the Cause of Peace to the End! Chinese Working Class Cables to Worker Brothers in All Countries to Congratulate the Great "May Day" International Labor Day
Author:
Date: 1951-04-30
Page: 4

The All-China Federation of Trade Unions Cables to the World Federation of Trade Unions Comrades Vittorio and Saillant:

	On the occasion of the May Day International Labor Day, I, on behalf of 5,490,000 organized workers in China, extend warm brotherly greetings to you and to the workers of the world, and wish the World Federation of Trade Unions, leading the proletariat of the world, even more brilliant achievements in the struggle against a new war and in defense of peace. On this great festival, the Chinese working class and all working people will hold large-scale demonstrations nationwide, demonstrating our determination to smash the imperialist aggressors and defend the cause of peace to the end.
		All-China Federation of Trade Unions
			April 29

The All-China Federation of Trade Unions Cables to the Chairman of the Central Council of Trade Unions of the Soviet Union, Comrade Kuznetsov:

	On the occasion of the "May Day" International Labor Day, I, on behalf of the Chinese working class, extend my highest respects to you and the All-Soviet Union Central Council of Trade Unions, and wish the Soviet working class even greater victories in the cause of building communism!
		All-China Federation of Trade Unions
			April 29, 1951

The All-China Federation of Trade Unions Cables to the Working People of All Countries

	[Our Newspaper's Dispatch] The All-China Federation of Trade Unions, on behalf of the Chinese working class, sent telegrams to the trade unions and working people of Eastern European new democratic countries and countries in Europe and the Americas, congratulating them on "May Day" International Labor Day. In the congratulatory telegrams to the trade unions and working people of new democratic countries such as Poland, Czechoslovakia, Romania, Bulgaria, Albania, Hungary, and the German Democratic Republic, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions expressed its highest respect and admiration for their great achievements in the construction of socialism and their great contributions to the cause of world peace, and expressed its conviction that they would achieve even greater victories in the brilliant cause of building socialism and in the struggle for lasting world peace in 1951.

	In the congratulatory telegrams to the working people of the United States, Britain, France, Italy, and Latin America, who are still under imperialist control, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions congratulated them on the remarkable achievements they have made in the struggle for world peace, against imperialism, and for improving the living and working conditions of workers; and wished them even greater success in the struggle for world peace and against imperialist aggression on the day of the great unity of the world's working class - "May Day".

	All the congratulatory telegrams expressed the firm conviction that, under the leadership of the socialist Soviet Union, the bulwark of world peace and democracy, and united with all peace-loving people in the world, the world's working class will surely be able to smash imperialist aggression and strive for lasting world peace.

The Chairman of the Chinese Railway Trade Union Cables to the General Secretary of the International Transport Workers' Federation, Comrade Molaru:

	On the occasion of the May Day International Labor Day, may transport workers all over the world unite more closely around the International Trade Union and strive for lasting peace and the vital interests of the working class.
			Li Jiebo, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union

The Chairman of the Chinese Textile Trade Union Cables to the Chairman and General Secretary of the Textile and Garment Workers' International, Comrades Nos and Boursky:

	On the occasion of "May Day," I, on behalf of the Chinese textile workers, extend warm congratulations to the textile workers all over the world. On this day, the Chinese textile workers will join the people of the whole country in holding grand demonstrations to show our determination to oppose imperialist aggression and defend world peace.
			Chen Shaomin, Chairman of the Chinese Textile Trade Union, April 27

The Director of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives Cables to the Central Union of Cooperatives of the Soviet Union:

	On this glorious international labor festival, the cooperative workers of the People's Republic of China extend warm respect to the Soviet working people and cooperative workers! The peace-loving working people and cooperative members all over the world are closely united, and under the leadership of the great Soviet Union and the teacher of mankind, Marshal Stalin, they will surely be able to smash the war conspiracies of imperialism, defend world peace, and build a bright and happy future!
		Bo Yibo, Director of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives, Beijing

### 粉碎帝国主义侵略，将和平事业保卫到底！  我国工人阶级电各国工人兄弟  祝贺伟大的“五一”国际劳动节

1951-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　粉碎帝国主义侵略，将和平事业保卫到底！
　  我国工人阶级电各国工人兄弟  祝贺伟大的“五一”国际劳动节
  全国总工会致世界工联电世界工会联合会维多里奥及赛扬同志：
    欣逢五一国际劳动节，谨代表中国五百四十九万有组织的工人向您们、并向全世界的劳动者致热烈的兄弟的敬礼，并祝世界工联领导着全世界的无产阶级在反对新战争、保卫和平的斗争中获得更辉煌的成就。中国的工人阶级及全体劳动人民在这个伟大的节日，将举行全国规模的大示威，显示我们粉碎帝国主义侵略者将和平事业保卫到底的决心。
          中华全国总工会
              四月二十九日
  全国总工会致全苏工会中央理事会主席电全苏工会中央理事会库兹涅佐夫主席：
    值此“五一”国际劳动节日，谨代表中国工人阶级向你和全苏工会致崇高的敬礼，并祝苏联工人阶级在建设共产主义的事业中获得更大的胜利！
          中华全国总工会
              一九五一年四月二十九日
  全国总工会致各国劳动人民电
    【本报讯】中华全国总工会代表中国工人阶级分别拍电报给东欧新民主主义国家和欧洲、美洲各国家工会和劳动人民，祝贺“五一”国际劳动节。在给波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚、匈牙利、德意志民主共和国等新民主主义国家的工会和劳动人民的贺电中，中华全国总工会对他们在走向社会主义的建设中的伟大成就和对世界和平事业的伟大贡献，表示了崇高的敬意和钦佩，并确信他们一九五一年在建设社会主义的辉煌事业上和争取世界持久和平的斗争中，将获得更大的胜利。
    在给尚在帝国主义控制下的美国、英国、法国、意大利和拉丁美洲的劳动人民的贺电中，中华全国总工会对他们在争取世界和平、反对帝国主义、改善工人生活条件和工作条件的斗争中获得的显著成绩，表示祝贺；并预祝他们在全世界工人阶级大团结的日子——“五一节”，在争取世界和平、反对帝国主义侵略的斗争中获得更大的成功。
    所有的贺电都表示出坚强的信念，即：全世界工人阶级在世界和平民主堡垒——社会主义苏联的领导下，和全世界一切爱好和平的人民团结在一起，一定能粉碎帝国主义的侵略，争取到世界的持久和平。
  中国铁路工会主席致陆空运输工人工会国际总书记电陆空运输工人工会国际莫拉鲁总书记：
    欣逢五一国际劳动节，愿全世界陆空运输工人更加紧密地团结在工会国际的周围，为争取持久和平及工人阶级的切身利益而奋斗。
            中国铁路工会全国委员会主席  李颉伯
  中国纺织工会主席致纺织服装工会国际主席电纺织服装工会国际主席诺斯、总书记布尔斯基同志：
    兹值“五一”劳动节，谨代表中国纺织工人向全世界纺织工人热烈祝贺。中国纺织工人将在这一天与全国人民一道举行盛大的示威游行，以显示我们反对帝国主义侵略、保卫世界和平的决心。
            中国纺织工会主席陈少敏  四月二十七日
  中国合作总社主任致苏联合作社中央联盟电苏联合作社中央联盟：
    际此光荣的国际劳动节日，中华人民共和国合作社工作者向苏联劳动人民及合作社工作者致热烈的敬意！全世界爱好和平的劳动人民与合作社员紧密地团结在一起，在伟大的苏联及人类的导师斯大林大元帅领导下，必能粉碎帝国主义的战争阴谋，保卫世界和平，建设光明美满的未来！
      中华全国合作社联合总社主任薄一波  北京
