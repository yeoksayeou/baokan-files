Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-04-24
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Two years ago today, the People's Liberation Army liberated Taiyuan, the capital of Shanxi Province. This is a scene from the recent demonstration in Taiyuan by the people against the United States rearming Japan. The demonstrators unanimously shouted "Oppose the U.S. separate peace treaty with Japan!" "Oppose the U.S. rearming Japan!" "Defend world peace!"

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-04-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    两年前的今天，人民解放军解放了山西省会太原。这是太原市人民最近举行反对美国重新武装日本的示威游行的情形。参加游行的人们一致高呼“反对美国单独对日媾和！”“反对美国重新武装日本！”“保卫世界和平！”
