Title: Panchen Erdene Arrives in Beijing Yesterday; Vice-Chairman Zhu, Vice-Chairman Li, Premier Zhou, and Others Greet Him at the Station; Premier Zhou Hosts a Banquet to Welcome Panchen and His Entourage

Author:
Date: 1951-04-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th: Panchen Erdene and the responsible officials and entourage of the Panchen Kampus Assembly Hall, a total of forty-five people, arrived in the capital at 2:00 PM today by special train from Xi'an. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Vice-Chairman of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Li Jishen, Premier of the Government Administration Council Zhou Enlai, Vice-Premiers Chen Yun, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Chen Shutong, Vice-President of the Supreme People's Court Zhang Zhizrang, Deputy Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Li Liuru, Lan Gongwu, Acting Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Nie Rongzhen, Secretary-General of the Government Administration Council and Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission Li Weihan, Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, as well as heads of the political and legal affairs committee, the financial and economic affairs committee, the cultural and educational affairs committee, the people's supervision committee of the Government Administration Council, various ministries, commissions, academies, administrations, and banks of the Central People's Government, all committee members of the Nationalities Affairs Commission in Beijing, and responsible persons of various people's organizations, totaling more than ninety people. In addition, there were more than five hundred people, including cadres of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, students from the Nationalities Institute, the Mongolian and Tibetan Institute, and the middle school attached to the Normal University, monks and lamas from Beijing temples, and representatives of Tibetans in Beijing. The welcoming crowd held huge banners written in both Tibetan and Chinese: "Welcome Panchen Erdene" and "Unite All Nationalities of the People's Republic of China". The welcoming ceremony was extremely solemn and grand.

The special train carrying Panchen and others was pulled by a locomotive decorated with red and yellow silk, and slowly entered the platform of Beijing Railway Station amidst military music. After Panchen got off the train, he first presented Hada (the most honorable gift of the Tibetan people) to the Vice-Chairman of the Central People's Government, the Premier and Vice-Premiers of the Government Administration Council, the Acting Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, and the Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government. He then accepted flowers presented by children and Hada presented by representatives of Beijing temples and Tibetans in Beijing, and immediately walked out of the station side by side with Vice-Chairman Zhu, Vice-Chairman Li, Premier Zhou and others of the Central People's Government.

After Panchen exited the station, he rode in a specially prepared yellow car of the Central People's Government to his residence accompanied by Li Weihan, Secretary-General of the Government Administration Council. Minister of Public Security of the Central People's Government Luo Ruiqing, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission Liu Geping, and Deputy Director of the General Office of the Central People's Government Yu Xinqing accompanied them.

In the evening, Premier Zhou of the Government Administration Council hosted a banquet to welcome Panchen and his entourage. During the banquet, Panchen Erdene delivered a speech. The banquet was filled with a cordial and joyful atmosphere from beginning to end.

### 班禅额尔德尼昨日抵京  朱副主席李副主席周总理等赴站欢迎  周总理设宴为班禅及其随行官员洗尘

1951-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　班禅额尔德尼昨日抵京
　  朱副主席李副主席周总理等赴站欢迎
　  周总理设宴为班禅及其随行官员洗尘
    【新华社二十七日讯】班禅额尔德尼先生暨班禅堪布会议厅各负责官员及随从一行四十五人，已于今日下午二时由西安乘专车抵达首都。前往车站欢迎者有中央人民政府副主席朱德、李济深，政务院总理周恩来，副总理陈云、郭沫若、黄炎培，中国人民政协全国委员会副主席陈叔通，最高人民法院副院长张志让，最高人民检察署副检察长李六如、蓝公武，人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻，政务院秘书长兼民族事务委员会主任委员李维汉，北京市人民政府市长彭真，以及政务院政治法律委员会、财政经济委员会、文化教育委员会、人民监察委员会和中央人民政府各部、会、院、署、行首长，民族事务委员会全体在京委员，各人民团体的负责人等共九十余人。此外，尚有民族事务委员会机关干部、民族学院、蒙藏学院、师范大学附属中学学生，北京市寺院僧众喇嘛及在京藏族人士代表等五百余人。欢迎队伍高举着用藏、汉两种文字书写的巨幅标语：“欢迎班禅额尔德尼先生”、“中华人民共和国各民族团结起来”。欢迎仪式极为庄严隆重。
    班禅等所乘的专车系由用红黄两色彩绸装饰的机车牵引，在军乐声中徐徐开进北京车站月台。班禅下车后，先向中央人民政府副主席、政务院总理、副总理，人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长及中央人民政府民族事务委员会主任委员献赠哈达（藏族极尊贵的礼物），并接受少年儿童的献花和北京市寺院代表和在京藏族人士献赠哈达，旋即与中央人民政府朱副主席、李副主席、政务院周总理等并肩走出车站。
    班禅出站后，由政务院李维汉秘书长同乘中央人民政府特备黄色汽车至驻地。伴送者有中央人民政府公安部罗瑞卿部长，民族事务委员会刘格平副主任委员，中央人民政府办公厅余心清副主任等。
    当晚，政务院周总理设宴为班禅及其随行官员洗尘。席间，班禅额尔德尼先生曾发表讲话。宴会自始至终充满了亲切愉快的气氛。（附图片）
