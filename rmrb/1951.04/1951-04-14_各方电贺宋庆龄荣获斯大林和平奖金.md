Title: Congratulations from Various Parties on Soong Ching Ling Receiving the Stalin Peace Prize

Author:
Date: 1951-04-14
Page: 1

The following telegrams were sent congratulating Soong Ching Ling, Chairman of the China Welfare Institute, on receiving the "Strengthening International Peace" Stalin International Prize.

Li Jishen, Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, telegram to Vice-Chairman Soong:
　　
　　I am delighted to hear that you have been awarded the esteemed "Strengthening International Peace" Stalin International Prize, and the entire nation shares this honor. As a follower of Mr. Sun Yat-sen, I am immensely proud that you have faithfully implemented Mr. Sun's principle of "peaceful struggle" for decades and have earned the respect of peace-loving people worldwide. I extend my heartfelt congratulations and highest respect on behalf of all members of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Li Jishen

China Peace Committee,telegram to Chairman Soong Ching Ling of the China Welfare Institute:

　　Congratulations on receiving the first "Strengthening International Peace" Stalin International Prize, which represents the glory of our people's heroic struggle against American aggression and in defense of world peace.
　　　　China Peace Committee
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　April 11th

Sino-Soviet Friendship Association telegram to Vice President Soong Ching Ling:

　　We are immensely excited and honored that you have been awarded the "Strengthening International Peace" Stalin International Prize. More than five million members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association nationwide are determined to redouble our efforts under your guidance to resist American aggression and aid Korea, strengthen Sino-Soviet friendship, and defend peace in the Far East and the world.
　　　　Sino-Soviet Friendship Association April 12th

China Welfare Institute telegram to Chairman Soong:

　　We are delighted to learn that you have been awarded the "Strengthening International Peace" Stalin International Prize, which is not only your glory and the glory of all staff members, but also the glory of the peace-loving Chinese people. We hereby send our congratulations and wish you good health!
　　　　China Welfare Institute April 12th

Red Cross Society of China telegram to China Welfare Institute, forwarding to Chairman Soong Ching Ling:

　　We feel boundless honor and inspiration that you have been awarded the "Strengthening International Peace" Stalin Prize.
　　For decades, you have strived to benefit the Chinese people and eliminate the root causes of disaster for the Chinese people through various forms of struggle. These struggles and achievements simultaneously strike and satirize the inhumane measures of all imperialist countries.
　　As a people's relief and welfare organization, the Red Cross Society of China will, under your guidance, better promote revolutionary humanitarianism, improve people's health and welfare work and international relief work, consolidate China's construction, combat American imperialist aggression, and wage a more tenacious struggle for world peace.
　　　　Red Cross Society of China

### 各方电贺宋庆龄荣获斯大林和平奖金

1951-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　各方电贺宋庆龄荣获斯大林和平奖金
    【本报讯】中国人民救济总会主席宋庆龄荣获“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金后，各方纷纷致电祝贺。兹志各电如下：
  民革主席李济深电宋副主席：
    欣闻您荣获崇高的“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金，全国人民同感光荣。孙中山先生的信徒对您数十年来忠心贯彻孙先生的“和平奋斗”的主张而得到全世界爱好和平人民的尊重，更感到无上的荣幸。我代表民革全党同志向您致衷心的祝贺和崇高的敬礼。
                  李济深
  中国人民保卫世界和平  反对美国侵略委员会电中国人民救济总会主席宋庆龄先生：
    祝贺你获得首届“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金，这表现着我国人民反对美国侵略保卫世界和平的英勇斗争的光荣。
          中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会
                四月十一日
  中苏友好协会总会电宋庆龄副会长：
    对您荣获“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金，我们感到无限兴奋和光荣。全国五百余万中苏友好协会会员，决心在您的指导下，加倍努力，为抗美援朝，加强中苏友好，保卫远东和世界和平而斗争。
        中苏友好协会总会  四月十二日
  中国人民救济总会电宋主席：
    欣悉您荣获“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金，这不仅是您的光荣，全体工作人员的光荣，而且是爱好和平的中国人民的光荣。谨电奉贺，并祝健康！
          中国人民救济总会  四月十二日
  中国红十字会总会电中国人民救济总会转宋庆龄主席：
    我们对您获得了“加强国际和平”斯大林奖金感到无比的荣誉与鼓舞。
    数十年来您在各种不同的斗争方式下为中国人民谋取福利，为中国人民消灭灾难根源而努力，这些斗争过程和成果同时是对帝国主义国家一切非人道措施的打击与讽刺。
    作为一个人民救济福利团体之一的中国红十字会，将在您的指导下，更好地发扬革命人道主义，做好人民卫生福利工作及国际救助工作，来巩固中国的建设，打击美帝国主义侵略，为世界和平作更顽强的斗争。
        中国红十字会总会
