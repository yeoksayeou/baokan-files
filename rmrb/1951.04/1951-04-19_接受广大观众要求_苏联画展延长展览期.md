Title: Accepting Broad Audience Requests: Soviet Art Exhibition Extended

Author:

Date: 1951-04-19
Page: 4

　　Accepting Broad Audience Requests
　  Soviet Art Exhibition Extended
    [Report from this newspaper] The Soviet propaganda poster and satirical painting exhibition, jointly organized by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the National Fine Arts Workers Association, and the Central Academy of Fine Arts, was originally scheduled to end on the 18th of this month. Now, due to the requests of the broad audience, it has been decided to extend the exhibition until the 22nd of this month.

### 接受广大观众要求  苏联画展延长展览期

1951-04-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　接受广大观众要求
　  苏联画展延长展览期
    【本报讯】中苏友好协会总会、全国美术工作者协会及中央美术学院举办的苏联宣传画和讽刺画展览会，原定本月十八日结束，现因广大观众的要求，决定延期至本月二十二日截止。
