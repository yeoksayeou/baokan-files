Title: China's Railway Medical Personnel's Third Medical Team Departs for Korea; Henan Province Volunteer Surgical Team Also Departs
Author:
Date: 1951-04-26
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, April 21st—The third group of Chinese Railway Volunteers to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea departed by train for Korea on April 21st. This team includes volunteer doctors, nurses, and health workers from the Shanghai, Tianjin, Hengyang, Jinan, Taiyuan, and Zhengzhou Railway Bureaus, as well as the Beijing Railway Hospital, to assist Korea. In addition, there are transportation and dispatch personnel. The Ministry of Railways held a grand farewell party the day before their departure.
    Xinhua News Agency, Kaifeng, April 24th—The first team of the Henan Province medical workers' volunteer surgical team to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea departed for Korea on the 20th to work. Before the team's departure, more than 4,000 medical workers and representatives from all walks of life in Henan Province and Kaifeng City held a grand farewell party. The pharmaceutical and industrial and commercial sectors of Kaifeng City also donated more than 100 kinds of medicines and medical equipment, as well as 4.8 million yuan in cash for the purchase of medicines. These donated items have been taken to the front lines by the team.

### 我国铁路医务人员第三批医疗队出发赴朝鲜  河南省志愿手术队也已出发

1951-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国铁路医务人员第三批医疗队出发赴朝鲜
　  河南省志愿手术队也已出发
    【新华社二十一日讯】第三批中国铁路抗美援朝志愿卫生医疗队，已于四月二十一日乘车赴朝。该队包括上海、天津、衡阳、济南、太原、郑州六个铁路管理局及北京铁路医院志愿援朝的医师、护士及卫生工作干部。此外尚有运输调度工作人员。出发的前一日，铁道部机关曾举行盛大的欢送会。
    【新华社开封二十四日电】河南省医务工作者组织的抗美援朝志愿手术队第一队于二十日出发赴朝鲜工作。该队出发前，河南省及开封市的医务工作者与各界代表四千余人举行了盛大的欢送会。开封市医药界及工商界并捐献药品与医疗器材等一百余种，并有购买药品的现款四百八十余万元。该项捐献物品已由该队带往前方。
