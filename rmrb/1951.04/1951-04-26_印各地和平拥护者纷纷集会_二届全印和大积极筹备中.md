Title: Peace Supporters in Various Parts of India Hold Meetings; Preparations for the Second All-India Peace Congress are in Full Swing
Author:
Date: 1951-04-26
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, 24th: According to the "Crossroads" weekly, India is actively preparing to hold the Second All-India Peace Congress. In West Bengal, Bombay Province, Tamil Nadu, Orissa, East Punjab, and other cities, peace supporters have held meetings, unanimously supporting the resolutions of the World Peace Council and congratulating the winners of the Stalin International Peace Prize.

### 印各地和平拥护者纷纷集会  二届全印和大积极筹备中

1951-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　印各地和平拥护者纷纷集会
　  二届全印和大积极筹备中
    【新华社二十四日讯】塔斯社德里讯：据“十字路”周报讯：印度全国正积极筹备举行第二届全印和平大会。在西孟加拉、孟买省、泰密兰、奥里萨、东旁遮普以及其他各地的城市，和平拥护者纷纷举行大会，一致拥护世界和平理事会的决议，并向斯大林国际和平奖金得奖人致贺。
