Title: Prague International Trade Fair to be Held in Czech Capital in Late May
Author:
Date: 1951-04-25
Page: 4
　
　　Prague International Trade Fair
　　To be Held in Czech Capital in Late May
    [News from This Newspaper] The 52nd Prague International Trade Fair will be held in Prague, the capital of Czechoslovakia, from May 20th to June 5th. In addition to Czechoslovakia, the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and various People's Democratic countries, Pakistan, Egypt, the United Kingdom, Denmark, France, the Netherlands, Austria, Italy, Switzerland and other countries will also participate in this fair.
    During the fair, the "Prague Spring Music Festival" will also be held, and famous musicians from all over the world will participate in the performance.

### 布拉格国际商业博览会  五月下旬在捷京举行

1951-04-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　布拉格国际商业博览会
　  五月下旬在捷京举行
    【本报讯】第五十二届布拉格国际商业博览会，将于五月二十日至六月五日在捷克斯洛伐克首都布拉格举行。参加此次博览会的国家除捷克斯洛伐克、苏联、中华人民共和国、各人民民主国家外，尚有巴基斯坦、埃及、英国、丹麦、法国、荷兰、奥地利、意大利、瑞士等国家。
    在博览会期间，并将举行“布拉格春季音乐会”，世界各国的著名音乐家均将参加演奏。
