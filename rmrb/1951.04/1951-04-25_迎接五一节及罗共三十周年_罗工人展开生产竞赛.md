Title: Romanian Workers Launch Production Competition to Greet May Day and the 30th Anniversary of the Romanian Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1951-04-25
Page: 4
　　
    To greet May Day and the 30th Anniversary of the Romanian Communist Party
    Romanian Workers Launch Production Competition
    [News from this newspaper] According to Xinhua News Agency: The Romanian working people are enthusiastically launching a patriotic production competition to greet the May Day Labor Day and the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Romanian Communist Party on May 8. The production competition has already achieved great results. The "Soviet-Romanian Hardware Factory" overfulfilled its plan by 309 tons of milled iron in the first six days of the competition and by 870 tons of steel in the first seven days. Steelworkers at the "Stiegel Noshiu" factory have reduced smelting time by an average of one hour and increased steel production by 61 kilograms per square meter of furnace surface per hour. Kopaiding, a famous coal mining shock worker in Longnia, has begun production according to the July plan. Cement workers exceeded the first quarter production plan by 6.54 percent on March 27. As of April 13, the "Lanne Textile Factory" in Timisoara had overfulfilled its production plan by more than 10 million lei, and the "Teba" textile factory in Arad had overfulfilled its production plan by 4.2 million lei.

### 迎接五一节及罗共三十周年  罗工人展开生产竞赛

1951-04-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　迎接五一节及罗共三十周年
    罗工人展开生产竞赛
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：罗马尼亚劳动人民正热烈展开爱国主义生产竞赛，迎接五一劳动节和五月八日罗马尼亚共产党成立三十周年。生产竞赛已经获得了巨大的成绩。“苏罗五金工厂”在竞赛的头六天内超计划生产了三百零九吨铣铁，在头七天内超计划生产了八百七十吨钢。“斯提格尔·诺修”工厂的炼钢工人已平均缩短了冶炼时间一小时，并使每一平方公尺炉面每小时多炼钢六十一公斤。隆尼亚的著名采煤突击工人柯派丁的工作班已开始按照七月份的计划进行生产。水泥业工人已在三月二十七日超过了第一季生产计划的百分之六点五四。到四月十三日为止，的密索拉的“兰内纺织工厂”已超计划生产价值一千多万列伊的产品，阿拉德的“帖巴”纺织厂超计划生产的产品价值达四百二十万列伊。
