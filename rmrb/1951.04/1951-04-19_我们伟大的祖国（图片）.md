Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-04-19
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The production competition to welcome the May 1st Labor Day has spread to every factory, every place, and every person in various regions. Lu Qiquan, an old worker at the state-owned Shanghai No. 2 Textile Machinery Factory, is determined to fulfill the challenge conditions in the competition and bought a pair of reading glasses. He is concentrating on repairing machine parts.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-04-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    迎接五一劳动节的生产竞赛，普及到各地工厂的每一处每一人了。国营上海第二纺织机器制造厂的老工友陆启泉，决心完成竞赛中的应战条件，买了一副老花眼镜。正在聚精会神地修造机器零件。
