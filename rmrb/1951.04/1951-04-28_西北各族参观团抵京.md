Title: Northwest Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1951-04-28
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, April 27th: The Northwest Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation, led by Head Zhabuding, Deputy Heads Ni Huade and Danji, totaling twenty-five people, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of April 27th. Peng Sike, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, and others went to the station to welcome them.
　　The delegation includes members of six ethnic groups: Mongolian, Hui, Tibetan, Uyghur, Kazakh, and Tatar. They will visit scenic spots and historical sites, factories, and schools in the capital, and present a flag to the leaders of the Central People's Government as a tribute.

### 西北各族参观团抵京

1951-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　西北各族参观团抵京
    【新华社二十七日讯】西北各民族来京参观团团长扎布丁、副团长倪华德、丹吉等一行二十五人于本月二十七日下午抵达北京。中央人民政府民族事务委员会办公厅副主任朋斯克等前往车站欢迎。
    该团成员包括蒙、回、藏、维吾尔、哈萨克、塔塔尔等六个民族，将在首都参观名胜古迹、工厂、学校，并向中央人民政府首长献旗致敬。
