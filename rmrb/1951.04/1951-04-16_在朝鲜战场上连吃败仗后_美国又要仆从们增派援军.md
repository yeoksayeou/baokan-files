Title: After Repeated Defeats on the Korean Battlefield, the United States Demands its Vassals Send More Reinforcements
Author:
Date: 1951-04-16
Page: 4

　　After Repeated Defeats on the Korean Battlefield,
　  the United States Demands its Vassals Send More Reinforcements
    [Xinhua News Agency, 13th dispatch] TASS, New York, 12th dispatch: According to a report by Rosenthal, a New York Times reporter, from Success Lake, the United States insists that certain United Nations member states send more troops to Korea. The United States is demanding that countries with troops in Korea regularly send reinforcements to Korea in order to maintain the highest possible number of troops there.

### 在朝鲜战场上连吃败仗后  美国又要仆从们增派援军

1951-04-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　在朝鲜战场上连吃败仗后
　  美国又要仆从们增派援军
    【新华社十三日讯】塔斯社纽约十二日电：据纽约时报记者罗森塔尔自成功湖报道，美国坚持要某些联合国会员国再派军队前往朝鲜。美国要求有军队在朝鲜的国家经常派援军前往朝鲜，使那里的军队人数保持最高额。
