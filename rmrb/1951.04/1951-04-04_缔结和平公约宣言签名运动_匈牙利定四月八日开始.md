Title: Declaration Signing Campaign for the Conclusion of a Peace Pact - Hungary Set to Begin on April 8th
Author:
Date: 1951-04-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, March 3rd: Hungarian peace activists are scheduled to launch a nationwide signature campaign for the declaration of the conclusion of a peace pact by the five major powers on April 8th. The Presidium of the Hungarian Trade Union Council held a meeting on March 31st and issued a letter to all working people, calling on them to actively participate in the signature campaign, widely publicize the resolutions of the World Peace Council, and educate the people of the country with the spirit of bravely defending peace. The meeting also called on workers, staff, and intellectuals to expand labor competitions to strengthen the peace and democracy camp.

### 缔结和平公约宣言签名运动  匈牙利定四月八日开始

1951-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　缔结和平公约宣言签名运动       
    匈牙利定四月八日开始
    【新华社三日讯】据塔斯社布达佩斯讯：匈牙利和平战士定于四月八日在全国展开五大国缔结和平公约宣言的签名运动。匈牙利工会理事会主席团已于三月三十一日举行会议，并发表了告全体劳动人民书，号召积极参加签名运动，广泛宣传世界和平理事会决议，并以英勇保卫和平的精神教育全国人民。会议并号召工人、职员及知识分子扩大劳动竞赛，以加强和平与民主阵营。
