Title: Five Latin American Countries to Hold Meeting to Protest the Convening of the Washington Conference; Uruguay Decides to Collect Signatures for Declaration on Peace Pact
Author:
Date: 1951-04-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Montevideo, April 8th: TASS reports: The Uruguay National Peace Committee held an enlarged meeting here, attended by representatives of twenty-five progressive groups. All representatives unanimously approved the resolutions of the World Peace Council meeting and decided to quickly begin collecting signatures for the declaration calling for the conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact. Participants broadly put forward a series of measures to ensure the successful implementation of this signature campaign. These measures are: expanding the National Peace Committee; establishing new local peace committees; organizing a broad propaganda campaign to explain the significance of these resolutions to the people. The meeting also passed a resolution to support, by all possible means, the meeting of peace advocates from Argentina, Brazil, Chile, Paraguay, and Uruguay, scheduled to be held in Montevideo on April 13th, to protest the resolutions passed by the Conference of Foreign Ministers of the Americas. The National Peace Committee decided to hold the first Uruguay National Peace Congress in July.

### 拉丁美洲五国将举行会议  抗议华盛顿会议的召开  乌拉圭决定征集和平公约宣言签名

1951-04-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　拉丁美洲五国将举行会议
　  抗议华盛顿会议的召开
　  乌拉圭决定征集和平公约宣言签名
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社蒙得维的亚八日讯：乌拉圭全国和平委员会在此间举行扩大会议，出席者有二十五个进步团体的代表。全体代表一致通过世界和平理事会会议的决议，并且决定迅速开始为要求缔结五大国和平公约的宣言征集签名。与会者概括地提出了一系列的措施，以保证这个签名运动的胜利进行。这些措施是：扩大全国和平委员会；建立新的地方和平委员会；组织广大的宣传运动，以便向人民解说这些决议的意义。会议并通过决议，要用一切可能的办法支持阿根廷、巴西、智利、巴拉圭和乌拉圭的和平拥护者定于四月十三日在蒙得维的亚召开的抗议美洲各国外长会议所通过的决议的会议。全国和平委员会决定在七月举行乌拉圭第一次全国和平代表大会。
