Title: Anti-American and Aid-Korea Patriotic Movement Popularized in Remote Areas of Xinjiang
Author:
Date: 1951-04-07
Page: 1

　　Anti-American and Aid-Korea Patriotic Movement
　  Popularized in Remote Areas of Xinjiang
    【Report from this newspaper】According to Xinhua News Agency: The Anti-American and Aid-Korea patriotic movement of people of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang has spread to various remote areas. In the past month, people in the Altai Mountains, Tacheng area, and the major southern Xinjiang towns of Kashgar and Hotan have successively held rallies and demonstrations, and have formulated patriotic pacts. Women and even Young Pioneers in more than ten counties and cities including Dihua, Kashgar, Hami, Jinghua, and Turpan have held rallies or parades against the rearmament of Japan and to defend world peace. Three thousand Ahongs, village elders, and religious leaders from forty-five Islamic mosques in Dihua also held a patriotic parade against the American rearmament of Japan.

### 抗美援朝爱国运动普及新疆边远地区

1951-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　抗美援朝爱国运动
　  普及新疆边远地区
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：新疆各民族人民的抗美援朝爱国运动，普及到各边远地区。最近一个月以来，阿尔泰山区、塔城区及南疆重镇喀什、和阗等地人民，都先后集会和示威游行，并且订立了爱国公约。迪化、喀什、哈密、景化、吐鲁番等十多县市的妇女以至于少年儿童队都举行了反对武装日本、保卫世界和平的集会或游行。迪化市四十五个伊斯兰教寺坊的阿訇、乡老、教长等共三千人，也举行了反对美国武装日本的爱国游行。
