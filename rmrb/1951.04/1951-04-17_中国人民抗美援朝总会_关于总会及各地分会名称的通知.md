Author:
Date: 1951-04-17
Page: 1

China People's Anti-American and Aid-Korea General Association
Notice Regarding the Names of the General Association and Local Branches

In order to unify the names of the General Association and local branches, the following provisions are hereby made:

  I. The official name of this association remains "China People's Committee for Defending World Peace and Opposing American Aggression," abbreviated as "China People's Anti-American and Aid-Korea General Association." The general rule is: use the official name for international activities; use the abbreviation for domestic activities.

 II. The local organizations of this association in major administrative regions are called General Branches, and all local organizations at all levels above the county and city level below the major administrative regions are uniformly called Branches.

III. The official names of branches at all levels shall still be uniformly called "China People's Committee for Defending World Peace and Opposing American Aggression, General Branch of [Location]" or "Branch," and need not be changed.

IV. The abbreviations of branches at all levels shall uniformly be "[Location] Anti-American and Aid-Korea General Branch" or "Branch." (For example: "Northeast Anti-American and Aid-Korea General Branch," "Hebei Province Anti-American and Aid-Korea Branch," "Beijing Anti-American and Aid-Korea Branch," etc.)

### 中国人民抗美援朝总会  关于总会及各地分会名称的通知

1951-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民抗美援朝总会
　  关于总会及各地分会名称的通知
    为统一总会及各地分会名称，特作如下规定：
    一、本会正式名称，仍为“中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会”，简称：“中国人民抗美援朝总会”。一般规定：对有关国际活动用正式名称；对国内活动用简称。
    二、本会各大行政区之地方组织称为总分会，大行政区以下县市以上各级地方组织一律称为分会。
    三、各级分会正式名称，仍统称为“中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会某地总分会”或“分会”，不必更改。
    四、各级分会简称，一律为“某地抗美援朝总分会”或“分会”。（例如：“东北抗美援朝总分会”，“河北省抗美援朝分会”，“北京市抗美援朝分会”等等。）
