Title: Overseas Chinese Groups and Schools in Burma Jointly Write Letters to Console the Chinese People's Volunteers

Author:

Date: 1951-04-03
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 2nd: The Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, Shwebo Preparatory Committee, the Shwebo Democratic League Branch in Burma, the Shwebo Overseas Chinese community organizations City Stalls Association, Youth Diligence Society, Yude Alumni Association, Yude Student Self-Government Association, and the faculty and staff of Yude School, jointly wrote letters on March 20 to express their condolences to the Chinese People's Volunteers. The letter stated: Your glorious victory makes us feel the greatness of the motherland's new strength. We, who are far away from the motherland, warmly support and support the great movement of the motherland to resist US aggression and aid Korea to protect our homes and defend the country, and we vow to be your backing.

### 缅甸华侨团体及学校联合写信慰问志愿军

1951-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　缅甸华侨团体及学校联合写信慰问志愿军
    【新华社二日讯】仰光消息：中国国民党革命委员会竖榜埠筹委会、缅甸竖榜埠民主同盟支部及该埠华侨社团市摊公会、青年勤奋社、育德校友会和育德学生自治会以及育德学校的教职员，于三月二十日联合写信慰问中国人民志愿军。信中说：你们的光荣胜利使我们感到祖国新生力量的伟大。远离祖国的我们，热烈拥护与支持祖国抗美援朝保家卫国的伟大运动，并誓为你们的后盾。
