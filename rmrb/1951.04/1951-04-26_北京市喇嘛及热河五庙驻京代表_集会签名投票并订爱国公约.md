Title: Lamas in Beijing and Representatives of the Five Temples in Rehe Hold a Meeting to Sign Votes and Formulate a Patriotic Pact
Author:
Date: 1951-04-26
Page: 1

　　Beijing Lamas and Representatives of the Five Temples in Rehe Stationed in Beijing
　　Hold a Meeting to Sign Votes and Formulate a Patriotic Pact
    [Our Newspaper] More than 40 lamas from 33 lama temples in Beijing and representative lamas stationed in Beijing from the Five Temples in Rehe recently held a meeting to resist the United States and aid Korea. They unanimously supported the declaration and resolution of the World Peace Council, signed their names in support of the conclusion of a peace pact among the five major powers, and voted against the United States arming Japan. At the same time, they passed five patriotic covenants.
    The covenants expressed their determination to support all policies and abide by all laws and decrees of the People's Government; support the declaration and resolution of the World Peace Council; support the conclusion of a peace pact among the five major powers; oppose the United States arming Japan; oppose the United States unilaterally concluding a peace treaty with Japan; oppose the United States arming West Germany; defend world lasting peace; work hard in production; work hard in learning; work hard in promoting resistance to the United States and aid to Korea; support the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers; support the government's policy of suppressing counter-revolutionaries; resolutely not allow special agents to hide in the temples; and assist the government in eliminating special agents and bandits; be determined to unite with the people of all ethnic groups throughout the country, regardless of religious beliefs, help each other, love each other, and oppose acts of splitting unity.
        (Mǎndūhū)

### 北京市喇嘛及热河五庙驻京代表  集会签名投票并订爱国公约

1951-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京市喇嘛及热河五庙驻京代表
　  集会签名投票并订爱国公约
    【本报讯】北京市三十三座喇嘛寺庙的喇嘛及热河五庙驻北京代表喇嘛四十余人，最近举行抗美援朝会议，一致拥护世界和平理事会的宣言和决议，并签名拥护缔结五大国和平公约，投票反对美国武装日本。同时通过了五条爱国公约。
    公约中表示决拥护人民政府一切政策，遵守人民政府一切法令；拥护世界和平理事会宣言和决议，拥护缔结五大国和平公约，反对美国武装日本，反对美国单独对日媾和，反对美国武装西德，保卫世界持久和平；努力生产，努力学习，努力宣传抗美援朝，支援朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军；拥护政府镇压反革命的政策，决不容特务分子在庙内隐匿，并协助政府肃清特务土匪；决与全国各族人民团结一致，不分宗教信仰畛域，互助友爱，反对分裂团结的行为。
        （满都呼）
