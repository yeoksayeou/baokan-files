Title: People's Liberation Army Basketball and Volleyball Competition Held in Beijing; Commander-in-Chief Zhu and Acting Chief of Staff Nie Attend Opening Ceremony

Author:
Date: 1951-04-21
Page: 3

　　People's Liberation Army Basketball and Volleyball Competition Held in Beijing
    Commander-in-Chief Zhu and Acting Chief of Staff Nie Attend Opening Ceremony
    To welcome the "May Fourth" National Basketball and Volleyball Competition and to further promote sports activities in the armed forces, the General Political Department of the Central People's Government's Revolutionary Military Commission held the Chinese People's Liberation Army 1951 Basketball and Volleyball Competition in Beijing starting on the 19th, scheduled to conclude on the 23rd. Thirteen units participated in this competition, including major military regions, field armies, the Navy, the Air Force, various special forces, public security forces, and directly subordinate organs of the Central People's Government's Revolutionary Military Commission, with a total of twelve men's basketball teams, seven men's volleyball teams, six women's basketball teams, and four women's volleyball teams.
    The opening ceremony was held at 10:00 a.m. on the 19th. Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Acting Chief of Staff Nie Rongzhen of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Deputy Director Xiao Hua of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, and leaders of various special forces attended the ceremony. Acting Chief of Staff Nie Rongzhen addressed all the basketball and volleyball team members participating in the competition. He instructed: "To strengthen national defense construction and military training, master various modern combat techniques, closely coordinate infantry with various technical arms, master the art of joint operations of various arms, and give full play to the organization, mobility, continuity, and arduousness in combat, the People's Liberation Army needs a healthy body for the vast number of commanders and fighters. Therefore, the armed forces must launch sports activities." He continued, "Our sports activities should make more use of the excellent condition of the armed forces' strict organization to carry out collective sports activities, making them mass-based. Our sports activities should be suitable for the needs of combat and national defense construction. We should learn more and create boldly any sports activities that meet these needs." He concluded, "Our sports activities should promote the spirit of unity and progress, advocate revolutionary competition, and oppose championship-ism."
    The competition officially began after the opening ceremony concluded at 11:00 a.m. (Xinhua News Agency) (Attached photos)

### 人民解放军篮排球竞赛大会在北京举行  朱总司令聂代总参谋长等参加大会揭幕式

1951-04-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　人民解放军篮排球竞赛大会在北京举行
    朱总司令聂代总参谋长等参加大会揭幕式
    中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部为迎接“五四”全国篮、排球竞赛大会，与进一步开展部队体育运动，于十九日起在北京举行中国人民解放军一九五一年篮、排球竞赛大会，预定二十三日结束。参加这次竞赛大会的有各大军区和各野战军、海军、空军、各特种部队、公安部队、中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会直属机关等十三个单位，计有男子篮球队十二个队，男子排球队七个队，女子篮球队六个队，女子排球队四个队。
    大会于十九日上午十时举行揭幕式。中国人民解放军朱德总司令、人民革命军事委员会聂荣臻代总参谋长、人民革命军事委员会总政治部萧华副主任和各特种部队首长均亲临参加。聂荣臻代总参谋长并向全体参加竞赛的篮、排球队队员们讲话。他指示：“人民解放军为了加强国防建设和军事训练，掌握各种现代作战技术，使步兵与各技术兵种紧密协同，掌握各兵种联合作战的艺术与高度发挥作战中的组织性、机动性、连续性和艰苦性，需要广大指挥员与战斗员有健康的身体，因此，部队必须展开体育运动。”他继称：“我们的体育运动，要多多利用部队组织严密这个优良条件，进行集体的体育活动，使之具有群众性。我们的体育活动，应适合于战斗和国防建设的需要。凡是适合这种需要的体育活动，要多多学习，大胆创造。”他最后指出：“我们的体育运动，应发扬团结进步的精神，提倡革命竞赛，反对锦标主义。”
    揭幕式于十一时结束后，即正式开始举行竞赛。（新华社）（附图片）
