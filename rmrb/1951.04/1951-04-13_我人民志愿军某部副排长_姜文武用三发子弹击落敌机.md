Title: Jiang Wenwu, Deputy Platoon Leader of a Certain Unit of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, Shoots Down an Enemy Plane with Three Bullets
Author:
Date: 1951-04-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, 12th: Jiang Wenwu, deputy platoon leader of a certain unit of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, bravely fought back against two enemy naval fighter planes that were diving and strafing at a certain location on the front line on March 15th. He shot down one of them with only three bullets. Jiang Wenwu has been awarded a special-class merit for this.

### 我人民志愿军某部副排长  姜文武用三发子弹击落敌机

1951-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　我人民志愿军某部副排长
    姜文武用三发子弹击落敌机
    【新华社朝鲜前线十二日电】中国人民志愿军某部副排长姜文武，于三月十五日在前线某地以轻机枪奋勇迎击俯冲扫射的两架敌海军战斗机，只用三发子弹就将其中的一架击落。姜文武已因此荣获记特等功一次。
