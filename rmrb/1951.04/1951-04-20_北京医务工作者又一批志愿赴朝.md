Title: Another Group of Beijing Medical Workers Volunteers to Go to Korea
Author: Jia Wu
Date: 1951-04-20
Page: 1

　　Another Group of Beijing Medical Workers Volunteers to Go to Korea
    [This newspaper dispatch] Recently, another anti-American and aid-Korea volunteer medical team composed of medical workers in Beijing set off to work. The team includes a total of 36 doctors and nurses from various public and private hospitals, led by Dr. Ge Bingren, director of surgery at the Municipal Third Hospital, and Dr. Meng Guangdong from the Central People's Hospital as the deputy leader. This is the fifth group of medical workers dispatched from Beijing after the anti-American and aid-Korea surgical team and the medical and disease prevention service team. (Jia Wu)

### 北京医务工作者又一批志愿赴朝
甲午
1951-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京医务工作者又一批志愿赴朝
    【本报讯】北京市医务工作者最近又组成了一个抗美援朝志愿医疗队出发工作。该队包括各公私立医院医师护士共卅六人，由市立第三医院外科主任葛秉仁医师任队长，中央人民医院医师孟广栋任副队长。这是北京市继抗美援朝手术队及医防服务队后出发的第五批医务工作者。（甲午）
