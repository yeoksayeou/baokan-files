Title: Government Administration Council Holds its Eighty-First Meeting Approving Reports from the Ministry of Culture and the Ministry of Health Regarding Last Year's Work Summary and This Year's Work Plan Highlights
Author:
Date: 1951-04-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th — The Central People's Government Administration Council held its eighty-first plenary meeting this afternoon. The meeting heard and approved the report by Zhou Yang, Vice Minister of the Central People's Government Ministry of Culture, on the national cultural and artistic work of 1950 and the main points of the ministry's work plan for 1951. It also passed regulations on adjusting the cultural administrative institutions of provincial and municipal people's governments and instructions on the reform of opera. The meeting also heard and approved the report by Li Dequan, Minister of the Central People's Government Ministry of Health, on the ministry's work summary for 1950 and the main points of the work plan for 1951. In addition, the meeting passed a number of appointment and removal cases and submitted appointment and removal cases to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第八十一次会议  批准文化部和卫生部关于去年工作总结和今年工作计划要点的报告

1951-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院举行第八十一次会议
    批准文化部和卫生部关于去年工作总结和今年工作计划要点的报告
    【新华社二十日讯】中央人民政府政务院第八十一次政务会议于二十日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府文化部周扬副部长所作的一九五零年全国文化艺术工作与一九五一年该部工作计划要点的报告，通过了关于调整省、市人民政府文化行政机构的规定和关于戏曲改革工作的指示。会议并听取和批准了中央人民政府卫生部李德全部长所作的该部一九五零年工作总结和一九五一年工作计划要点的报告。此外会议通过任免案以及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
