Title: Place Names That Insult Ethnic Minorities Should Be Changed
Author: Liu Quan
Date: 1951-04-25
Page: 2

Reader's Letter

Place Names That Insult Ethnic Minorities Should Be Changed

To the Editor:

　　There is a Pingyi County in Qujing Prefecture, Yunnan Province. This place name is the product of the Kuomintang reactionaries' Han chauvinism. Now, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, all ethnic groups in the country are closely united around Chairman Mao. Such place names with reactionary meaning and insulting ethnic minorities should not continue to exist. I hope the People's Government will quickly change it.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Henan Luohe Post Office Liu Quan

　　Editor's Note: Comrade Liu Quan's opinion is correct. All place names set by past reactionary rulers that insult ethnic minorities should be changed.

### 侮辱少数民族的地名应改掉
刘泉
1951-04-25
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

  侮辱少数民族的地名应改掉编辑同志：
    云南省曲靖专区有一个平彝县。这个地名，是国民党反动派大汉族主义者的产物。现在在中国共产党的领导下，全国各兄弟民族亲密地团结在毛主席的周围。这种含有反动意义、侮辱少数民族的地名是不应该继续存在的，希望人民政府迅速把它改掉。
          河南漯河邮局  刘泉
    编者按：刘泉同志的意见是正确的。所有过去反动统治者定下的侮辱少数民族的地名都应该予以更改。
