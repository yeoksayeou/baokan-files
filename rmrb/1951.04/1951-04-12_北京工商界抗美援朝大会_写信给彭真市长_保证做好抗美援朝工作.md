Title: Beijing Business Community Holds Resist-America Aid-Korea Conference; Writes Letter to Mayor Peng Zhen, Guaranteeing to Do a Good Job in the Resist-America Aid-Korea Work
Author:
Date: 1951-04-12
Page: 1

　　[News from the Beijing Municipal News Agency] The Beijing business community held a general and in-depth Resist-America Aid-Korea movement conference, which passed a letter to Mayor Peng Zhen. The original letter is as follows: Respected Mayor Peng:
    In order to respond to the instructions of the China People's Resist-America Aid-Korea General Association and your opening speech at the first session of the Third Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference regarding the popularization and deepening of the Resist-America Aid-Korea movement, we specially held a conference today and unanimously passed our specific work plan for the popularization and deepening of the Resist-America Aid-Korea movement in the Beijing business community, which will be implemented immediately. At the same time, in order to consolidate our people's democratic regime and the safety of our lives and property and the development of our business, the representatives of various industries present at the meeting unanimously agreed that we must contribute all our strength to actively support the front line in order to win the final victory in Resist-America Aid-Korea. We must also assist the government in suppressing and eliminating all counter-revolutionary elements who disrupt the rear, in order to achieve the goal of resisting foreign aggression and pacifying domestic affairs. Therefore, we unanimously agreed to sign a convention on eliminating counter-revolutionaries and abide by it together. Now we guarantee to you: We will definitely do a good job in popularizing and deepening the Resist-America Aid-Korea movement and assisting the government in eliminating counter-revolutionary work, and we will resolutely fight to complete the above tasks!
    With best regards,
　　      The Beijing Business Community's General and In-depth Resist-America Aid-Korea Movement Conference

### 北京工商界抗美援朝大会　写信给彭真市长　保证做好抗美援朝工作

1951-04-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京工商界抗美援朝大会
  　写信给彭真市长
  　保证做好抗美援朝工作
    【京市新闻处讯】北京市工商界举行普及深入抗美援朝运动大会，通过给彭真市长的信，原信如下：敬爱的彭市长：
    我们为了响应中国人民抗美援朝总会和您在北京市第三届第一次各界人民代表会议开幕词中关于普及深入抗美援朝运动的指示，特于今日举行大会，一致通过了我们北京市工商界普及深入抗美援朝运动具体工作计划，并即将付诸实施；同时为了巩固我们人民民主政权和我们生命财产的安全与业务的发展，到会各业代表，一致认为：必须贡献一切力量，积极支援前线，以争取抗美援朝的最后胜利；对于扰乱后方的一切反革命分子，也必须协助政府予以镇压与肃清，方能达到攘外安内之目的。因此，一致同意签订肃清反革命公约，共同遵守。现在我们向您保证：一定做好普及和深入抗美援朝运动和协助政府肃清反革命工作，并为完成上项任务而坚决奋斗！
    此致敬礼
　　      北京市工商界普及深入抗美援朝运动大会
