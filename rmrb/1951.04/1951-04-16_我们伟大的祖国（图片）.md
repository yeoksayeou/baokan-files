    Our Great Motherland
    The Beijing Postal Administration has trained a group of young female postal workers. Dressed in green uniforms and riding green bicycles, they deliver letters just like their male counterparts.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-04-16

专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    北京邮政管理局培养了一批女青年邮递员。她们穿上绿色的制服，骑着绿色的自行车，和男邮递员一样地送信。
