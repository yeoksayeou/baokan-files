Title: Indonesian Overseas Chinese Write Letters and Donate Money to Comfort the Chinese and Korean People's Warriors
Author:
Date: 1951-04-02
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, 1st dispatch: Indonesian overseas Chinese have recently been writing letters and donating money to comfort the Chinese and Korean soldiers on the Korean front. The Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression received on February 24th a letter of sympathy from a group of Indonesian overseas Chinese democratic businessmen, extending their condolences to the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean People's Army, along with RMB 50 million. The letter expressed their respect for the heroic and skillful fighting of the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean People's Army, and guaranteed their eternal support.
　　
The Indonesian Sumatran Overseas Chinese Women's Association and eleven overseas Chinese women's associations in Medan and other places affiliated with the association also jointly wrote a letter to comfort the Chinese and Korean soldiers. In their letter of sympathy, they wrote: Most of the patriotic overseas Chinese abroad have also launched a patriotic movement of "Resisting America and Aiding Korea, Protecting Our Homes and Defending Our Country." We overseas Chinese women in Indonesia and peace-loving women all over the world pledge to be your backing.
　　
The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression recently also received HKD 3,000 from three overseas Chinese in Surabaya, Indonesia, as a contribution to comfort the Chinese and Korean soldiers, equivalent to RMB 11.64 million.

### 印尼华侨纷纷写信捐款  慰问中朝人民战士

1951-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　印尼华侨纷纷写信捐款        
    慰问中朝人民战士
    【新华社一日讯】印尼华侨最近纷纷写信和捐款慰劳朝鲜前线的中朝战士。中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会广州分会于二月二十四日接到了署名“一群印尼华侨民主商人”的慰劳中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军的慰问信一封，附人民币五千万元。信中对中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军的英勇善战表示敬意，并保证永远支持他们。
    苏门答腊苏东华侨妇女总会及该会所属棉兰等地十一个华侨妇女会也联合写信慰问中朝战士。她们在慰问信中写道：在海外的大多数的爱国侨胞，也展开了“抗美援朝保家卫国”的爱国运动。我们在印尼的华侨妇女及全世界爱好和平的妇女誓为你们的后盾。
    中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会最近还收到印尼泗水市三位侨胞慰劳中朝战士的慰劳金港币三千元，合人民币一千一百六十四万元。
