    Our Great Motherland
    The National Beijing Library is one of the largest libraries in our country. It was established in 1928 and currently has a collection of more than 2 million volumes, of which nearly 150,000 are rare books. Since liberation, 450,000 new books have been added, and the number of readers has greatly increased. In 1950, a total of 408,000 people visited the library to read books.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国立北京图书馆是我国最大的图书馆之一。它建立于一九二八年，现有藏书共二百余万册，其中有将近十五万册善本书。解放后啬了四十五万册新书，阅览了数也大为增加。一九五○年来馆阅书的共计四十万八千余人。
