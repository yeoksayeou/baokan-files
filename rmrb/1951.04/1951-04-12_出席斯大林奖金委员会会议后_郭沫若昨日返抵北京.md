Title: Guo Moruo Returns to Beijing Yesterday After Attending the Stalin Prize Committee Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-04-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 11th: Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and Vice Chairman of the Stalin International Peace Prize Committee, returned to Beijing on the afternoon of the 11th after attending the meeting of the Stalin International Peace Prize Committee in the Soviet Union. Those who went to the airport to welcome him included Chen Shutong, Lu Dingyi, Wu Xiuquan, Li Siguang, Tao Menghe, Zhu Kezhen, Wu Youxun, Yang Hansheng, Yu Xinqing, Liu Guanyi, Jin Cheng and many others.

### 出席斯大林奖金委员会会议后　郭沫若昨日返抵北京

1951-04-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　出席斯大林奖金委员会会议后
  　郭沫若昨日返抵北京
    【新华社十一日讯】中国科学院院长、“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金委员会副主席郭沫若，在赴苏联出席“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金委员会会议后，于十一日下午返抵北京。赴机场欢迎者有：陈叔通、陆定一、伍修权、李四光、陶孟和、竺可桢、吴有训、阳翰笙、余心清、刘贯一及金城等多人。
