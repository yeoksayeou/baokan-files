Title: Congratulations on Winning the Stalin International Prize for "Strengthening International Peace" Premier Zhou Sends a Telegram to Vice-Chairman Soong Ching Ling, Shanghai Branch of the Resist-America Aid-Korea Association Sends Representatives to Congratulate and Pay Respects, Women's Organizations from the Soviet Union, Poland and China Also Send Telegrams of Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1951-04-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 11th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government sent a telegram to Vice-Chairman Soong Ching Ling, congratulating her on winning the Stalin International Prize for "Strengthening International Peace". The telegram reads as follows: Vice-Chairman Soong:
　　I would like to express my warmest congratulations to you on winning the lofty Stalin International Prize for "Strengthening International Peace".
　　　  Zhou Enlai, April 11th

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, April 11th: The Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, on the occasion of Soong Ching Ling, Chairman of the China Welfare Institute, winning the Stalin International Prize for "Strengthening International Peace," specially dispatched Sheng Pihua, Vice-Chairman of the Branch; Jin Zhonghua, Head of the Propaganda Department; Wang Yunsheng, Director-General; Zhou Erfu, Deputy Director-General; and Standing Committee members Xie Renbing, Hu Xiufeng, Liu Simu, and others, seven in total, to congratulate Soong Ching Ling and present flowers to pay respects at 4:00 PM on the 10th. When Wang Yunsheng delivered the congratulatory speech on behalf of the Branch, he pointed out: This honor is a grand gift that Mr. Soong has received for his decades of struggle for the liberation of the Chinese people and the cause of world peace. At the same time, the Chinese people also feel honored. The people of Shanghai and all of China will be greatly encouraged by this and will redouble their efforts to complete the cause of defending world peace and opposing American aggression. In addition to expressing her gratitude, Soong Ching Ling also read a written statement, encouraging the people of the whole country to step up the struggle for world peace. On the same day, all newspapers in Shanghai published commentaries to congratulate the winners of the Stalin International Prize for "Strengthening International Peace".

Xinhua News Agency, April 11th: The Vice-Chairman of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, Bafinova, and the Chairman of the Polish Women's League, Mrs. Msilova, sent telegrams to congratulate Soong Ching Ling, Vice-Chairman of the Central People's Government of China, on winning the Stalin International Prize for "Strengthening International Peace". Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, also sent a telegram to Vice-Chairman Soong to congratulate her. She also sent telegrams to congratulate Mrs. Gordon, Chairman of the Women's International Democratic Federation, and Mrs. Park Jung-ae, Chairman of the Korean Democratic Women's League, who also won this honor.

### 祝贺荣获“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金　周总理致电宋庆龄副主席　上海抗美援朝分会派代表道贺并致敬　苏联波兰及我国妇女团体亦致电祝贺

1951-04-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　祝贺荣获“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金
  　周总理致电宋庆龄副主席
  　上海抗美援朝分会派代表道贺并致敬
  　苏联波兰及我国妇女团体亦致电祝贺
    【新华社十一日讯】中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理致电宋庆龄副主席，祝贺荣获“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金。电文如下：宋副主席：
    对您荣获崇高的“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金，谨电致最热诚的祝贺。
　　　  周恩来  四月十一日
    【新华社上海十一日电】中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会上海分会，以中国人民救济总会主席宋庆龄荣获“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金，特推派该会副主席盛丕华、宣传部长金仲华、总干事王芸生，副总干事周而复，常委谢仁冰、胡绣枫、刘思慕等七人，于十日下午四时向宋庆龄道贺并献花致敬。王芸生代表该会致贺词时指出：这种荣誉是宋先生数十年来为中国人民解放及世界和平事业奋斗而得到的隆重礼物。同时中国人民也感到光荣。上海和全中国人民将因此得到更大的鼓舞，而加倍努力来完成保卫世界和平与反对美国侵略的事业。宋庆龄除表示答谢外，并宣读书面谈话，勉励全国人民加紧展开争取世界和平的斗争。同日，上海各报均发表评论，对“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金的获得者表示庆贺。
    【新华社十一日讯】苏联妇女反法西斯委员会副主席巴菲诺娃及波兰妇女联盟主席姆西洛娃夫人电贺我国中央人民政府宋庆龄副主席荣获“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金。我中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅亦致电宋副主席祝贺。并电贺同时获得此项荣誉的国际民主妇女联合会主席戈登夫人及朝鲜民主妇女同盟主席朴正爱夫人。
