Title: Liu and Deng Commend Meritorious Troops, Our Army Recaptures Mengcheng in Northern Anhui
Author:
Date: 1947-11-05
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency,鄂豫皖 Front, 3rd: Following the great victory at Jieling Gaoshanpu, in which two and a half brigades of Chiang Kai-shek's bandits were annihilated, Generals Liu and Deng issued a special order to commend all participating troops, and awarded the Wujiang troops (including the Independent Brigade) a major merit, and the Huaihe troops a merit. At the same time, they called on all commanders and fighters to launch a campaign to earn merits, continue their efforts, and strive for greater victories.
    Xinhua News Agency, Su Lu Yu Wan Front, 3rd: The People's Liberation Army recaptured Mengcheng County on October 30, annihilating and capturing a part of the Chiang Kai-shek bandit security team in the county.

### 刘邓嘉奖有功部队  皖北我军再克蒙城

1947-11-05
第1版()
专栏：

    刘邓嘉奖有功部队
    皖北我军再克蒙城
    【新华社鄂豫皖前线三日电】此次界岭高山铺大捷，歼灭蒋匪两个半旅。刘邓两将军特通令嘉奖所有参战部队，并给乌江部队（包括独立旅）记大功一次，淮河部队记功一次。同时号召全体指战员开展立功运动，再接再厉，争取更大胜利。
    【新华社苏鲁豫皖前线三日电】解放军于十月三十日再克蒙城县城，歼俘该县蒋匪保安队一部。
