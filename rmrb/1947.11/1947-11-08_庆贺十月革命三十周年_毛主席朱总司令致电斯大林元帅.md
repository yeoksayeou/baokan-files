Title: Celebrating the 30th Anniversary of the October Revolution, Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu Send a Telegram to Marshal Stalin
Author:
Date: 1947-11-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 6th: Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Comrade Zhu De, Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, sent a telegram to Marshal Stalin on the 6th to celebrate the 30th anniversary of the October Revolution. The original telegram is as follows:

Marshal Stalin:

	On behalf of the Communist Party of China and the Chinese people who are fighting for independence and democracy, we warmly celebrate the 30th anniversary of the Great October Revolution, celebrate the ever-growing strength of the Soviet Union, the pioneer of the worldwide anti-imperialist struggle, and celebrate the growing great fighting friendship between the two great nations of China and the Soviet Union.

		Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Mao Zedong
		Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Zhu De

### 庆贺十月革命三十周年  毛主席朱总司令致电斯大林元帅

1947-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    庆贺十月革命三十周年
    毛主席朱总司令致电斯大林元帅
    【新华社陕北六日电】中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志及中国人民解放军总司令朱德同志，于六日致电斯大林大元帅，庆贺十月革命三十周年。原电如次：斯大林大元帅：
    我们代表正在为独立民主而战斗的中国共产党和中国人民，热烈庆祝伟大十月革命的三十周年，庆祝全世界反帝国主义斗争的先驱——苏联日益强盛，庆祝中、苏两大民族的伟大战斗友谊日益增进。
          中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东
          中国人民解放军总司令朱德
