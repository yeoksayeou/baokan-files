Title: Ancient Books, Paintings, and Antiques Should Be Sent to the Border Region Government for Preservation
Author:
Date: 1947-11-21
Page: 2

To the People's Daily:

You are the mouthpiece of the people, speaking on behalf of the people. We, the people of Chengnan Town, Meng County, under the leadership of the Communist Party and Chairman Mao, have avenged a thousand years of hatred and overthrown centuries of oppression. Only today have the people begun to dare to hold their heads high. In this struggle, we obtained a complete set of the "Zizhi Tongjian" in the large-format Mao Bian paper edition, totaling one hundred volumes and two hundred and ninety-four chapters. In addition, there is a copy of the "Zizhi Tongjian Shiwu Bianwu," in four volumes and twelve chapters. Everyone unanimously agrees to send it to the newspaper office that speaks for the people as a historical reference.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　With Respect!

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Presented by all of Chengnan Town, Meng County, Henan

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　November 1st

(Editor's Note: We deeply appreciate the kindness of the elders, brothers, and sisters of Chengnan Town. However, ancient books of such value are unlikely to serve their due purpose if kept at a newspaper office. We suggest sending them to the local county government for transfer to the border region government for proper preservation. At the same time, we hope that all localities will pay attention to the preservation of ancient books, paintings, and all other antiques and instruments of research value, and promptly send them to the local county government for transfer to the border region government for preservation.)

### 古书、古画、古董应送边府保存

1947-11-21
第2版()
专栏：

　　古书、古画、古董应送边府保存人民日报社：
    你是人民的喉舌，代表人民说话的，我们孟县南镇人民在共产党毛主席领导下，把千年仇恨报了，百世压迫推翻了，人民今天才算开始敢抬头了。我们在这次斗争中得到了一部毛边纸大版“资治通鉴”共一百本，二百九十四卷。另外还有“资治通鉴释文辩误”四本十二卷一部，大家一致意见，送给代表人民说话的报社，以作历史参考。　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　此致敬礼！
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　河南孟县城南镇全体敬赠
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　十一月一日    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
    （编者按：我们深切感谢城南镇农民父老兄弟姊妹们的厚意。不过象这样有价值的古书，放在报馆难以起它应有作用，我们的意见，把它送给当地县政府转送边区政府，好好保存起来。同时希望各地如有古书、古画以及其他一切有研究价值的古董、仪器，都要注意保管，并迅速送交当地县政府转送边区政府保存。）
