Title: 150,000 Farmers from South of the Yellow River Participate in the Sabotage of the Longhai Railway; Capture of Bandit Brigade Commander Ding Baoren in the Battle of Dangshan
Author:
Date: 1947-11-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Front Lines of Su, Lu, Yu, and Wan, 14th: 150,000 liberated peasants from the south bank of the Yellow River in Ji, Lu, Yu, participated in the sabotage of the Longhai Railway. There were 8,000 stretchers, 20,000 porters, 50,000 road demolition team members, and tens of thousands of militia guides and self-defense team members. While heroically supporting the People's Liberation Army, the revenge team members and liberated peasants, under the cover of the People's Liberation Army, wielded hammers and steel picks, actively destroying the road, shouting "Smash Chiang Kai-shek's dog legs, pull out Chiang Kai-shek's tendons." Their morale was high. As of the 12th, the military and civilians in the liberated areas had completely overturned more than 300 li of tracks and bridges west of Xuzhou.
    Xinhua News Agency, Front Lines of Su, Lu, Yu, and Wan, 14th: The People's Liberation Army captured Dangshan on the evening of the 10th. They broke into the city in just a quarter of an hour and resolved the battle in three hours. The entire 7th Brigade headquarters of Chiang's Bandit Temporary 24th Division and its subordinate 1st and 2nd Regiments (missing one battalion), as well as the Dangshan County Security Team and Military Police Team, were completely wiped out. Ding Baoren, Brigade Commander of the Temporary 24th Division's 7th Brigade, Kong Zhensheng, Commander of the 1st Regiment, Su Hanjie, Commander of the 2nd Regiment, and the Secretary-General and County Magistrate of the Chiang Party in Dangshan County, and more than 3,000 other officers and soldiers were captured alive. More than 200 bandits were killed or wounded. Nineteen field guns, mountain guns, and mortars, 140 light and heavy machine guns, 1,643 rifles and carbines, one ammunition depot, hundreds of thousands of catties of military grain, two locomotives, and dozens of carriages were seized.

### 黄河南十五万农民参加陇海路破击战　砀山之役擒匪旅长丁保仁

1947-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　黄河南十五万农民参加陇海路破击战
  　砀山之役擒匪旅长丁保仁
    【新华社苏鲁豫皖前线十四日电】冀鲁豫黄河南岸十五万翻身农民，参加陇海路破击战，计有八千付担架，两万挑工，五万破路队员及数万民兵向导。自卫队员，复仇队员，翻身农民们一面英勇支援解放军，一面在解放军掩护下，挥动铁锤、钢镢，积极破路，并叫着“砸断蒋介石狗腿，抽掉蒋介石脚筋”，情绪昂扬，迄十二日解放区军民已将徐州以西三百余里路轨桥梁全部翻身。
    【新华社苏鲁豫皖前线十四日电】解放军十日晚攻克砀山，仅一刻钟即突入城内，历三小时解决战斗，全歼蒋匪暂二十四师第七旅旅部与所属第一第二（缺一个营）两个团及砀山县保安队、宪兵队等部，生俘暂二十四师第七旅旅长丁保仁，第一团团长孔祯生、第二团团长苏汉杰，及蒋党砀山县书记长、县长等以下官兵三千余名，毙伤匪二百余名。缴获野炮、山炮、迫击炮十九门，轻重机枪一百四十挺，步马枪一千六百四十三支，弹药库一座，军粮数十万斤，火车头两个，车厢数十节。
