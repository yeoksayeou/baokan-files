    An Essential Reference Book for Comrades Participating in Land Reform
    Marx, Engels, Lenin, Stalin and Mao on the Peasant Land Question
    Compiled by the Land Question Research Association
    “Theory is the guide to action!”
    This book is intended as a general theoretical reference for comrades participating in land reform and studying land reform issues.
    The editing method of this book is to list the issues raised in the peasant struggles of various historical periods in the order of social development, and how Marx, Engels, Lenin, Stalin, and Mao resolved these issues at different stages of the revolution.
    The book is divided into four sections: 1. Peasants under the feudal system. 2. The bourgeoisie and the peasants. 3. The proletariat and the peasants. 4. The land question of Chinese peasants. Attached are the full text of Chairman Mao's "Report on an Investigation of the Peasant Movement in Hunan" and "Preface to Rural Surveys".

### 参加土改同志的一本必备参考书  马恩列斯毛论农民土地问题  土地问题研究会编

1947-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    参加土改同志的一本必备参考书
    马恩列斯毛论农民土地问题
    土地问题研究会编
    “理论是行动的指南”！
    这本书，就是给参加土改和研究土改问题的同志们，作一般理论的参考用的。
    本书编辑方法，是按照社会发展的顺序，列举出各个历史时期农民斗争中所提出的问题，以及马、恩、列、斯、毛，在不同的革命阶段上，怎样来解决了这些问题的。
    全书分四节：一、封建制度下的农民。二、资产阶级与农民。三、无产阶级与农民。四、中国农民土地问题。并附录有毛主席的“湖南农民运动考察报告”、“农村调查序言”全文。
