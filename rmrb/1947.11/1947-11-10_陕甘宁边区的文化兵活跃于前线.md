Title: Cultural Soldiers of the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Active on the Front Lines
Author:
Date: 1947-11-10
Page: 4

Cultural News

    Cultural Soldiers of the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Active on the Front Lines
    The Northwest Literary and Art Work Troupe, led by the Border Region Literary Association, has traveled with the army to various locations and has given more than thirty performances. "Red Cloth Strip," which reflects the relationship between the army and the people, and "Entering the City" (a Western-style drama), which reflects the struggle against the enemy, are deeply praised by the masses. The troupe also conducts propaganda in the army and rural areas through picture-story books, wall paintings, current affairs illustrations, storytelling, and singing. The total audience has reached more than 41,000 people. The Yanchang County small propaganda team has traveled to more than 50 villages with new foreign films, attracting an audience of more than 6,000 people.

### 陕甘宁边区的文化兵活跃于前线

1947-11-10
第4版()
专栏：文化消息

    陕甘宁边区的文化兵活跃于前线
    边区文协领导的西北文艺工作团随军辗转各地，已公演三十余次，反映军民关系的“红布条”与反映对敌斗争的“进城”（西剧），深受群众赞扬。该团并以连环画、墙画、时事图解、说书、歌咏等在部队及农村中进行宣传，总计观众达四万一千余人，延长县小型宣传队则携带新洋片在五十余个村庄巡回出观众达六千余人。
