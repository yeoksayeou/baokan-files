Title: Soviet Youth Write to Stalin Promising to Fulfill Great Construction Plans and Firmly Master Marxism-Leninism-Stalinism
Author:
Date: 1947-11-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 7th: Moscow News: To celebrate the October Revolution, 26,474,000 members of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League and young men and women in the Soviet Union jointly wrote a letter to Stalin. The original letter stated: "Dear Leader and Teacher: Today, we solemnly swear before our country and people to always be loyal children of our great motherland and to dedicate our entire lives to adding glory to the motherland and strengthening its power. We assure you that in order to realize the great construction plans you have formulated, we will work tirelessly and join the ranks of advanced workers in industry, transportation, and agriculture. We assure you that we will resolutely master the theories of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin. We swear to be forever vigilant and not allow the enemy to use tricks or take advantage of gaps to attack us. In the future, our younger generation will surely be cultivated into loyal, hardworking children whose lives are full of joy, who are not afraid of dangers, and who are prepared to overcome all difficulties."

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 7th: Moscow News: On the occasion of the great 30th anniversary of the October Revolution, democratic youth organizations from various countries around the world have sent congratulatory telegrams to the Soviet Anti-Fascist Youth Committee. The French Democratic Youth Committee stated in its congratulatory telegram: "French youth admire the great victories achieved by Soviet youth and guarantee that French youth will resolutely fight for the peace and independence of the country." The Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Youth League stated: "Romanian youth and their motherland have been liberated from fascist oppression thanks to the assistance of the heroic Soviet army and youth." The Albanian People's Youth Association expressed heartfelt congratulations in its congratulatory telegram and pointed out: "The great Soviet Union and Stalin are the saviors of our country's freedom, independence, democracy, and prosperity." The Free Austrian Youth League also sent congratulations to Soviet youth.

### 苏联青年致书斯大林  保证实现伟大建设计划  坚定掌握马恩列斯学说

1947-11-10
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联青年致书斯大林
    保证实现伟大建设计划
    坚定掌握马恩列斯学说
    法罗等国青年团体电贺苏十月革命节
    【新华社陕北七日电】莫斯科讯：为庆祝十月革命节，苏联二千六百四十七万四千共产主义青年团团员暨男女青年，联名致书斯大林。原书内称：亲爱的领袖和导师：今天，我们在我们的国家和人民面前郑重宣誓，永作我伟大祖国忠诚的儿女，并贡献整个生命为祖国增光，并加强其力量。我们向您保证：为了实现您所制定的伟大建设计划，我们将不息地工作，并参加工业、运输业及农业的先进工人的行列。我们向您保证：我们要坚决掌握马、恩、列、斯的学说。我们宣誓永远警惕，不许敌人使用诡计，利用间隙袭击我们。将来我们年青的一代必将培育成为忠诚、刻苦、生活充满愉快的儿女，不畏险阻，并准备克服一切困难。
    【新华社陕北七日电】莫斯科讯：际此伟大的十月革命三十周年纪念，世界各国民主青年团体纷向苏联青年反法西斯委员会致送贺电。法国民主青年委员会在贺电内称：法国青年钦慕苏联青年所获得的伟大胜利，并保证法国青年将为国家的和平与独立而坚决斗争。罗马尼亚工人青年联盟中央委员会称：罗国青年及其祖国，由于苏联英勇的陆军及青年的援助，由法西斯压迫下获得了解放。阿尔巴尼亚人民青年协会在贺电中表示衷心的祝贺后，指出：“伟大的苏联和斯大林，是我国自由、独立、民主与繁荣的救星。”自由奥地利青年团亦向苏联青年致贺忱。
