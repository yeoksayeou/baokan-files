Title: Chiang Kai-shek Bandit's Beining Railway Traffic Further Disrupted; Our Army Captures Tangjia Station; Taihe and Guangshan in Northern Anhui and Eastern Henan Liberated
Author:
Date: 1947-11-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northeast Front, 6th: Simultaneously with the annihilation of more than 3,000 Chiang Kai-shek bandits at Jiuguantaimen, another unit of the Democratic Allied Army launched a major sabotage campaign on the 140-mile stretch between Xinmin and Dahu Mountain on the Beining Railway, the artery of Chiang Kai-shek bandit's civil war, on the 1st and 2nd, making the Chiang Kai-shek bandit's Beining Railway traffic even more fragmented. Within two days, they captured Tangjia Station north of Dahu Mountain, completely annihilated a company and another platoon of the enemy's New Sixth Army, as well as a traffic police detachment and an armored train, totaling more than 340 people. They captured 303 enemy officers and soldiers, including the enemy's Major Armored Train Captain, seized two artillery pieces, twelve light and heavy machine guns, more than 50,000 rounds of ammunition, destroyed an armored truck train, completely destroyed 82 li of railway, and destroyed 22 iron bridges at Liuhegou, Luoyanghe and other places, captured 13 bridgehead bunkers, and destroyed more than 5,200 rails and more than 46,000 sleepers.

Xinhua News Agency, Su-Lu-Yu-Wan Front, 6th: The People's Liberation Army captured Taihe County, northern Anhui, for the third time on the morning of the 2nd, and the defending enemy fled south to Fuyang. Furthermore, the People's Liberation Army annihilated a squadron of Chiang Kai-shek bandit security forces near Shenqiu on the 1st, capturing more than 30 enemies.

Xinhua News Agency, Hubei-Henan-Anhui Front, 6th: The People's Liberation Army recovered Guangshan County for the third time on the 2nd. The results of the battle are pending investigation.

### 蒋匪北宁路交通更形破碎  我军攻占唐家车站  皖北豫东克太和光山

1947-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    蒋匪北宁路交通更形破碎
    我军攻占唐家车站
    皖北豫东克太和光山
    【新华社东北前线六日电】与九关台门歼灭蒋匪三千余人同时，民主联军另部，一、二两日在蒋匪内战动脉北宁路新民与大虎山一百四十余里间，展开大破击战，使蒋匪北宁路交通更形支离破碎。二日内计攻克大虎山以北之唐家车站，全歼守敌新六军一个连另一个排，及一个交警分队，一个战车列队共三百四十余人。内俘敌少校战车列队长以下官兵三百零三名，缴获炮两门，轻重机枪十二挺，子弹五万余发，炸毁装甲大车一列，彻底摧毁铁路八十二里；并毁柳河沟、洛阳河等处铁桥二十二处，攻占桥头堡垒十三座，毁铁轨五千二百余条，枕木四万六千余根。
    【新华社苏鲁豫皖前线六日电】解放军于二日晨第三次攻克皖北之太和县城，守敌南窜阜阳。又解放军一日于沈邱附近歼灭蒋匪保安队一个中队，俘敌三十余。
    【新华社鄂豫皖前线六日电】解放军二日第三次收复光山县城，战果待查。
