Title: Chiang Kai-shek Gang Conceals Miserable Defeat, Restores News Censorship
Author:
Date: 1947-11-30
Page: 1

　　Chiang Kai-shek Gang Conceals Miserable Defeat
　　Restores News Censorship
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, November 27th: According to reports from various sources, the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang, at the end of its rope, is intensifying its strangulation of culture in its controlled areas and establishing large concentration camps. TASS Shanghai dispatch of the 24th, quoting the "Millard's Review," reports: Recently, terror has once again spread through Kunming, and the Chiang Kai-shek bandits have arrested seventy teachers and students from various schools in the area; even Chiang Kai-shek government employees have been arrested. The Shanghai Xinzhi Bookstore has been closed down by the Chiang Kai-shek bandits, and "Shi Yu Wen" magazine has been forced to cease publication. According to a TASS Shanghai dispatch of the 12th, quoting the "Southeast Daily" reports: The Chiang Kai-shek bandits have established five large concentration camps in various locations to detain patriotic and democratic figures from the Chiang-controlled areas. In addition, according to a United Press dispatch from Shanghai on the 24th: In order to conceal its disastrous military defeats and its fascist atrocities, the Chiang Kai-shek bandits in Hankou have formally restored news censorship, and newspaper proofs must be submitted for inspection before publication.

### 蒋匪掩饰惨败　恢复新闻检查

1947-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　蒋匪掩饰惨败
  　恢复新闻检查
    【新华社陕北二十七日电】据各方消息报导：穷途末路的蒋家匪帮，正在其统治区域内加紧绞杀文化，并建立大集中营。据塔斯社沪二十四日电引密勒士评论报消息：最近恐怖再度弥漫昆明，蒋匪又在该地各校捕去师生七十人，蒋匪公务员亦有被捕者。上海新知书店已被蒋匪封闭，时与文杂志被迫停刊。据塔斯社上海十二日电引东南日报消息：蒋匪又在各地建立大集中营五所，以拘禁蒋区爱国民主人士。另据合众社二十四日沪讯：为了掩饰军事惨败及其法西斯暴行，汉口蒋匪已正式恢复新闻检查，各报校样在出版前均须送检。
