Date: 1947-11-23
Page: 1

　　Taihang Postal Administration Notice
  　Xing You Ji Zi No. 5
    Having received the Border Region Government Postal Administration Bureau's Postal Planning Letter No. 7, announcing an imminent increase in postage rates, postage stamps valued at five yuan and below will no longer be needed. Therefore, a deadline has been set for their use. Based on the situation in this area, it has been decided that various postage stamps valued at one yuan and below will be discontinued and invalidated immediately. Postage stamps valued at two yuan and five yuan will be discontinued and invalidated from January 1, 1948. It is hoped that all sectors of society, government agencies, and organizations will use these postage stamps as soon as possible to avoid them becoming invalid and non-refundable after the deadline. This notice is hereby issued.
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Director Zhang Yuru
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　November 13th

### 太行邮政管理局通告　行邮计字第五号

1947-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　太行邮政管理局通告
  　行邮计字第五号顷奉
    边府邮政管理总局邮计字第七号通知增加邮资在即，五元及五元以下之邮票将不需用，故限期贴用，兹根据本区情形决定一元以下之各种邮票自即日起停止使用作废，二元及五元之邮票自三十七年一月一日起停止贴用作废。希我各界人士、机关、团体尽先使用此项邮票，以免逾期作废不能调换。特此通告
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　局长张予如
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　十一月十三日
