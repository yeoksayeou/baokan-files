Title: Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Yan'an Sub-region Guerrilla Force Grows Twentyfold in Six Months, Eliminating Over Four Thousand Chiang Kai-shek Bandits
Author:
Date: 1947-11-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northwest, 9th: The guerrilla forces in the Yan'an sub-region of the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region have grown twentyfold in six months of fighting against Chiang Kai-shek's army. The Yan'an guerrilla force has developed the fastest and captured the most, killing and wounding over 1,850 enemies in the past six months. According to incomplete statistics, the guerrilla forces in various parts of the Yan'an sub-region have killed, wounded, and captured a total of 4,239 enemy soldiers in the past six months (mid-March to the end of October). Our guerrilla forces suffered only 147 casualties, a ratio of 1 to 29 compared to the enemy's losses. We captured 19 machine guns, 2 sixty-millimeter mortars, over 70,000 rounds of ammunition, one radio station, 593 livestock, over 1,000 bags of flour, and shot down one enemy plane, destroying 13 enemy vehicles.

### 陕北延属分区游击队　半年壮大二十倍　歼灭蒋匪四千余

1947-11-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　陕北延属分区游击队
  　半年壮大二十倍
  　歼灭蒋匪四千余
    【新华社西北九日电】陕甘宁边区延属分区各地游击队半年与蒋军作战中壮大了二十倍。延安游击队发展最快缴获最多，半年来毙伤敌即达一千八百五十余名。延属各地游击队半年来（三月中旬至十月底），据不完全统计，共毙伤俘敌四千二百三十九名。我游击队仅伤亡一百四十七名，我敌损失为一与二十九之比。缴获机枪十九挺，六零炮二门，子弹七万余发，电台一部，牲口五百九十三头，洋面千余袋，并击落敌机一架，毁敌汽车十三辆。
