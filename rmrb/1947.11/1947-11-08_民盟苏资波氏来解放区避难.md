Title: Mr. Su Zibo of the Democratic League of China Seeks Refuge in the Liberated Area
Author:
Date: 1947-11-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jinji-Luyu, 6th: Mr. Su Zibo of the Democratic League of China in Xi'an, disguised himself to escape persecution by the Chiang Kai-shek bandits, and arrived safely in the Taiyue Liberated Area on the 30th of last month. According to him, when the Chiang Kai-shek bandits shot and killed Du Binche and Wang Juren (formerly mistranslated as Wang Chubo), the persons in charge of the Northwest General Branch of the Democratic League of China, his name was also on the list of those to be shot. Mr. Su expressed extreme indignation at the Chiang Kai-shek bandits' terrorist methods of eliminating the Democratic League, saying: "Such brutality by the Chiang Kai-shek bandits only shows their death throes and dying flashes." Mr. Su is from Hancheng, Shaanxi. During the Xi'an Incident, he served as the director of the political department of General Yang Hucheng's troops.

### 民盟苏资波氏来解放区避难

1947-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    民盟苏资波氏来解放区避难
    【新华社晋冀鲁豫六日电】西安民盟苏资波先生化装逃避蒋匪迫害，于上月卅日安抵太岳解放区。据谈蒋匪枪杀民盟西北总支部负责人杜斌丞、王菊人（前误译为王楚伯）两氏时，他亦在被枪杀名字之内。苏氏对蒋匪消灭民盟的恐怖手段极表愤慨，他说：“蒋匪此种横暴只表现其临死挣扎，回光返照而已。”苏氏为陕西韩城籍，西安事变时任杨虎城将军所部政治处长。
