Title: Local Liaoji Armed Forces Capture Kangping City, Annihilating Over 400 Bandits
Author:
Date: 1947-11-27
Page: 1

　　Liaoji Local Armed Forces
    Capture Kangping City, Annihilating Over 400 Bandits
    【Xinhua News Agency Northeast, 25th】 Delayed News: Between the 10th and 17th of this month, our Liaoji local armed forces fought three battles at Kangping, defeating part of Chiang bandit's New Sixth Army and hundreds of puppet bandits who invaded the city three times, securing the city. The bandits fled towards Faku. In this battle, we killed and wounded over 100 officers and soldiers, including Zhang Mingduo, the regimental commander of the 505th Regiment of the 169th Division of the New Sixth Army, and battalion commanders, and captured over 300 enemies. We seized four artillery pieces, 22 light and heavy machine guns, over 80,000 rounds of ammunition, one vehicle, and a portion of other military supplies.

### 辽吉地方武装  攻克康平城歼匪四百余

1947-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　辽吉地方武装
    攻克康平城歼匪四百余
    【新华社东北二十五日电】迟到消息：辽吉我地方武装于本月十日至十七日间三战康平，三次击败侵入该城之蒋匪新六军一部及伪匪数百人，确保该城，匪向法库逃去。是役我毙伤匪新六军一六九师五○五团团长张鸣铎及营长以下官兵一百余人，俘敌三百余人。缴获炮四门，轻重机枪二十二挺，子弹八万余发，汽车一辆及其他军用品一部。
