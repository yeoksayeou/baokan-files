Title: Commemorating the Eleventh Anniversary of the Defense of Madrid: Spanish Communist Party Calls on the People to Unite; Guerrilla Forces Control the Gragales Area
Author:
Date: 1947-11-15
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 13th: Madrid dispatch: Radio Free Spain Pyrenees broadcast on the 7th that the Spanish Communist Party issued a communique to commemorate the eleventh anniversary of the defense of Madrid, calling on the Spanish people to unite and fight against Franco's rule and establish a democratic republic. The communique also called for the expansion of the Spanish government-in-exile to include representatives of the Spanish working class, so that the government would have the nature of a united anti-Franco government. The communique stated: "We call on all patriotic forces to cooperate and strive to create a strong resistance movement throughout the country."
   Another dispatch: Spanish guerrillas celebrated the eleventh anniversary of the defense of Madrid with victorious combat operations. The guerrillas achieved victories in Cordoba (south), Toledo (southwest of Madrid), and the Levant region. The Levant guerrillas have been particularly outstanding in the anti-Franco struggle, and they have already controlled the vast Gragales area. In the past ten months, the guerrillas have fought a total of 693 battles.

### 纪念保卫马德里十一周年　西共号召人民团结  游击队控制格拉格爱区

1947-11-15
第3版()
专栏：

　　纪念保卫马德里十一周年
  　西共号召人民团结
    游击队控制格拉格爱区
    【新华社陕北十三日电】马德里讯：自由西班牙比利耐卡玛电台七日广播，西共为纪念保卫马德里十一周年发表公报，号召西班牙人民团结起来，为反佛朗哥统治并建立民主共和国而斗争。公报并要求扩大西流亡政府，使西班牙工人阶级的代表参加，俾政府具有反佛朗哥联合政府的性质。公报称：“我们要求一切爱国力量合作，争取在全国创造强大的抵抗运动”。
    另讯：西班牙游击队以胜利的战斗行动来庆祝保卫马德里十一周年纪念日。游击队在科尔多巴（南部）多列多（马德里西南）与勒凡特等地得到了胜利，在反佛朗哥斗争中，表现特别卓越的是勒凡特游击队，他们已控制了广大格拉格爱区域。过去十个月中，游击队共作战六百九十三次。
