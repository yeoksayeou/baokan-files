Title: Our Army on the Yu-Shaan East Line Advances Northwest of Nanyang, Liberating Zhenping and Angao Towns, Annihilating a Kuomintang Bandit Security Regiment
Author:
Date: 1947-11-22
Page: 1

　　[Xinhua News Agency, Yu-Shaan Front, 20th] A unit of our army on the eastern front liberated Zhenping County, a strategic military town northwest of Nanyang, on the morning of the 17th, annihilating the enemy's Second Security Regiment. The results of the battle are still being verified. This is the 23rd county town liberated by our army since advancing into Yu-Shaan.
　　[Xinhua News Agency, Yu-Shaan Front, 20th] A unit of our guerrilla corps in the Nanyang area liberated Angao Town, a stronghold of Kuomintang bandits located 50 li northwest of Nanyang City on the east bank of the Liao River, on the 15th, capturing more than 150 enemy soldiers, including the deputy commander of the First Security Regiment.
　　[Xinhua News Agency, Yu-Shaan Front, 20th] Light auspicious snow fell in the new areas of western Henan this morning and stopped in the evening. Farmers say that this is timely and beneficial for the autumn wheat, and they are extremely happy.

### 豫陕东线我军挺进南阳西北　解放镇平及安皋镇  歼蒋匪一个保安团

1947-11-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　豫陕东线我军挺进南阳西北
  　解放镇平及安皋镇
    歼蒋匪一个保安团
    【新华社豫陕前线二十日电】东线我军一部于十七日晨解放军事重镇南阳西北之镇平县城，歼敌保安第二团，战果正清查中。此为我进军豫陕后解放之第二十三座县城。
    【新华社豫陕前线二十日电】南阳地区之我游击兵团一部，十五日解放南阳城西北五十里潦河东岸蒋匪据点安皋镇，俘敌保安第一团副团长以下一百五十余名。
    【新华社豫陕前线二十日电】豫西新区各地今晨初降瑞雪，入晚始停。农家谈称：此对秋麦适时有益；均极喜悦。
