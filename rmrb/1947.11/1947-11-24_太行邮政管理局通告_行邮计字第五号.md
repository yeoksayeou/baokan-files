Title: Taihang Postal Administration Notice, Xing You Ji No. 5

Author:

Date: 1947-11-24
Page: 1

Taihang Postal Administration Notice
Xing You Ji No. 5
It has been reported that the Postal Administration Bureau of the Border Region Government's You Ji No. 7 notice is about to increase postal rates, and stamps of five yuan and below will no longer be needed. Therefore, a deadline has been set for their use. Based on the situation in this area, it has been decided that all kinds of stamps of one yuan and below will be discontinued and invalidated immediately, and stamps of two yuan and five yuan will be discontinued and invalidated from January 1, 1937. It is hoped that all sectors of society, institutions, and organizations will use these stamps as soon as possible to avoid expiration and inability to exchange them.
    This is hereby announced.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Director Zhang Yuru
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　November 13th

### 太行邮政管理局通告　行邮计字第五号

1947-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　太行邮政管理局通告
  　行邮计字第五号顷奉
    边府邮政管理总局邮计字第七号通知增加邮资在即，五元及五元以下之邮票将不需用，故限期贴用，兹根据本区情形决定一元以下之各种邮票自即日起停止使用作废，二元及五元之邮票自三十七年一月一日起停止贴用作废。希我各界人士、机关、团体尽先使用此项邮票，以免逾期作废不能调换。
    特此通告
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　局长张予如
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　十一月十三日
