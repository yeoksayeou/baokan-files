Title: Changzhi Fourth District Villages Initiate Labor Exchange and Mutual Aid for Land Tillage

Author:

Date: 1947-11-05
Page: 2

(From our Changzhi correspondent) In order to complete autumn plowing and autumn reclamation in the whole district earlier and more comprehensively, the Fourth District is launching a labor exchange and assistance movement of village assisting village and group assisting group. On the 26th, Beida Zhang set off to assist Nanhe, Qiaotou assisted Nan Zhang, Xi Zhang Guanjia assisted Pingjia Zhuang and Xi Gou, Huo Village assisted Xingma, and mutual assistance between groups has also been fully launched.

### 长治四区各村变工互援垡地

1947-11-05
第2版()
专栏：

    长治四区各村变工互援垡地
    【本报长治讯】四区为更早的全区全面完成秋耕秋屯，正开展村援助村组援助组的变工援助运动，二十六日北大掌出发援助南河，桥头援助南掌，西掌关家援助平家庄、西沟，霍村援助行马，组与组的互相援助也已全面展开。
