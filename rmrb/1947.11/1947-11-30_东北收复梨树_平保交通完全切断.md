Title: Northeast Recovers Lishu, Pingbao Transportation Completely Cut Off
Author:
Date: 1947-11-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Northeast Front, 28th: A cavalry unit of the Democratic Allied Forces recaptured Lishu County, more than 40 miles north of Siping, on the 24th. The defending bandit Chiang's security team of more than 100 men fled in panic. The city was recovered by us on October 1st and occupied by Chiang's bandits on November 2nd.
Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji, 28th: Delayed news: Our Pingxi and Jixi local troops continuously damaged the railway between Ping and Bao again from the 6th to the 12th of this month, and successively captured Wolonggang, Liangzhuang west of Changxindian, Hengqiao south of Changping, Baibao southeast of Laishui city, Gaolou and other five strongholds, killing and wounding more than 130 bandits, destroying more than 30 miles of the Pinghan Road, destroying six bridges, and 19 bunkers. Chiang's bandit traffic between Ping and Bao has been completely cut off.
Xinhua News Agency, Shandong, 28th: Three hundred members of Chiang Kai-shek's Yantai Security Brigade raided Qujia Village on the 15th and were severely attacked by our guerrillas, resulting in 30 enemy deaths and injuries. At dawn on the same day, our local armed forces ambushed more than 100 men of the traitor Sun Zhenxian's unit near Sunjiatan in the southeast of Yantai, killing and wounding 60 enemies. On the morning of the 6th, a company of the Transport Battalion of Chiang Kai-shek's 8th Reorganized Division and a part of the "Returning Home Corps" were ambushed by our guerrillas in Caojia Village in the suburbs of Yantai, capturing nine enemies alive, killing and wounding four, and seizing some ammunition.
Xinhua News Agency, East China, 28th: After our Lunan troops captured towns such as Yixian, Zaozhuang, and Pixian in mid-month, Chiang Kai-shek hurriedly transferred about five battalions of Feng Zhian's 33rd Army from the Yunhe River to reinforce Xuzhou. Our army resisted them separately, killing, wounding, and capturing 249 enemies. On the 19th, 20th, 21st and 23rd, they successively withdrew from Yiqiu and Zao three places. The vast rural areas around them are still under our control.

### 东北收复梨树　平保交通完全切断

1947-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　东北收复梨树
  　平保交通完全切断
    【新华社东北前线二十八日电】民主联军铁骑一部，二十四日再度克复四平以北四十余里之梨树县城，守敌蒋匪保安队百余人仓惶逃窜。该城系十月一日为我收复，十一月二日为蒋匪侵占。
    【新华社晋察冀二十八日电】迟到消息：我平西、冀西地方部队于本月六日至十二日，再次连续破击平保间铁路，并先后攻克长辛店西之卧龙岗、亮庄及昌平南之横桥、涞水城东南之白堡、高楼等五据点，毙伤匪一百三十余人，破除平汉路三十余里，毁桥梁六座，碉堡十九座，蒋匪平保交通已完全割断。
    【新华社山东二十八日电】蒋匪烟（台）市保安队三百人于十五日窜扰曲家村，遭我游击队痛击，毙伤三十余。同日拂晓我地武在烟台东南孙家滩附近伏击汉奸孙振先部百余名，毙伤敌六十余名。六日上午蒋匪整八师运输营一个连及“还乡团”一部，于烟市郊之曹家村遭我游击队伏击，生俘敌九名，毙伤四名，缴弹药一部。
    【新华社华东二十八日电】鲁南我军于本月中旬攻克峄县、枣庄、邳县等城镇后，蒋匪慌忙自运河南调来防御徐州之冯治安部三十三军约五个营兵力增援，我军分别予以阻击，毙伤俘敌二百四十九名后，于十九、二十、二十一、三日先后撤离峄邱、枣三地，周围广大农村现仍为我控制中。
