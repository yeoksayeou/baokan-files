Title: Chiang Kai-shek Plane Captured North of Yuncheng
Author:
Date: 1947-11-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jinji-Luyu, 17th. At 4:00 PM on the 6th, a Chiang Kai-shek plane, which was conducting reconnaissance over the Yellow River, caught fire and crashed north of Yuncheng. Second Lieutenant pilot Hou Xinyou was captured by our forces. Five machine guns and a radio transmitter on the plane were intact. Hou stated that he was from Gaoyang, Hebei, and had been sent by Chiang Kai-shek to study in the United States and India. He returned to the country last December. This time, he took off from Jinan.

### 郓城以北地区俘获蒋机一架

1947-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　郓城以北地区俘获蒋机一架
    【新华社晋冀鲁豫十七日电】六日下午四日，沿黄河上空侦察之蒋机一架，在郓城以北地区失火下坠，少尉驾驶员侯新有为我俘获。机上之机枪五挺，电台一部均完好。侯称：彼为河北高阳人，曾被蒋派往美国与印度学习，去年十二月始归国。彼此次系由济南起飞。
