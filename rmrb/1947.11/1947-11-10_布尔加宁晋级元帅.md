Title: Bulganin Promoted to Marshal

Author: 
Date: 1947-11-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 7th. Moscow dispatch: In recognition of General Bulganin, Minister of the Armed Forces of the Soviet Union, for his great contributions during the Patriotic War and in the post-war construction of the army, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union issued an order on the 3rd, promoting him to Marshal of the Soviet Union. Bulganin is a highly respected politician and military strategist in the Soviet Union. He was born in 1895 in Nizhny Novgorod, now Gorky City. He joined the Bolshevik Party in 1917 and commanded operations on the Dagestan front during the Civil War. In 1922, he served at Moscow's largest electrical equipment factory and was later promoted to factory director. In 1931, he was elected Chairman of the Moscow Soviet. In 1937, he became Chairman of the Council of People's Commissars of the Russian Federation. In 1938, he became Deputy Chairman of the Council of People's Commissars of the Soviet Union and Director of the State Bank of the Soviet Union. After the outbreak of the Soviet-German War in 1941, Bulganin served on the Western Front Military Council, organizing the defense of Moscow and thwarting the German offensive. In December 1942, he was promoted to Lieutenant General, commanding counter-offensives on various battlefields. In November 1944, he was again promoted to Colonel General. After 1944, he served as Deputy Minister of the Armed Forces of the Soviet Union and a representative to the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. This March, he succeeded Stalin as Minister of the Armed Forces and has been awarded the Order of Lenin, the Order of Suvorov, the Order of Kutuzov, and the Order of the Red Star.

### 布尔加宁晋级元帅

1947-11-10
第3版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁晋级元帅
    【新华社陕北七日电】莫斯科讯：苏联武装部队部长布尔加宁上将因在爱国战争时及战后建军中之伟大功勋，苏联最高苏维埃主席团，特于三日下令晋级为苏联元帅。按布氏在苏联为众望所归之政治家及军事家，一八九五年诞生于内日尼格勒，即现在之高尔基城，一九一七年加入布尔塞维克党，内战时期即在达吉斯坦前线指挥作战。一九二二年服务于莫斯科苏联最大之电工器材厂，继被升为厂长。一九三一年被选为莫斯科苏维埃主席，一九三七年任俄罗斯联邦人民委会主席，一九三八年任苏联人民委员会副主席及苏联国家银行行长，一九四一年苏德战争爆发后，布氏任西线军委，组织莫斯科保卫战，粉碎了德军进攻。一九四二年十二月擢升为中将，在各战场指挥反攻。一九四四年十一月又擢升为上将，一九四四年后任苏联武装部队副部长，苏联最高苏维埃代表。今年三月继斯大林为武装部长，历获列宁勋章，苏沃罗夫勋章，启图索夫勋章，红星勋章等。
