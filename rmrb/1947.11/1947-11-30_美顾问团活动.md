Title: Activities of the US Advisory Group
Author:
Date: 1947-11-30
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 28th: According to Reuters and the Central News Agency, the head of the US military advisory group, Rooks, after inspecting Hankou, arrived in Chengdu on the 24th, accompanied by Tang Enbo, the deputy commander of the Chiang Kai-shek bandit army, to inspect the Chiang Kai-shek Central Military Academy and the Air Force Technical School, and flew back to Nanjing on the 26th. It is reported that Rooks' trip to Hankou and Chengdu is related to the US imperialism's strengthening of its invasion bases in Central and Southwest China. According to Hong Kong's "Hua Shang Bao" (Chinese Merchant News), Chongqing has also been selected by the US advisory group as a training location for the Chiang army. Reuters also revealed that Rooks may personally participate in the discussion of the Chiang bandit's Central China military plan, and said that the duties of the US military advisory group will be expanded in accordance with the Greek US advisory group model. This news shows that the US military advisory group has directly participated in the supreme command of Chiang bandit's civil war.

### 美顾问团活动

1947-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　美顾问团活动
    【新华社陕北二十八日电】据路透社及中央社消息：美军顾问团团长鲁克斯于视察汉口后，于二十四日由蒋匪陆军副司令汤恩伯伴同转抵成都，视察蒋匪中央军校与空军技术学校，二十六日飞返南京。据悉，鲁克斯汉口与成都之行，与美帝国主义加强其华中与西南侵略基地有关。据香港华商报消息，重庆亦被美顾问团选择为练训蒋军地点。路透社消息且透露，鲁克斯可能亲自参与蒋匪华中军事计划的商讨，并谓美军顾问团的职务，将仿照希腊美顾问团予以扩大。此讯显示美军顾问团已直接参加蒋匪内战最高指挥。
