Title: The Eyuwan Capital During the Anti-Japanese War, Xuanhuadian, is Liberated Again; Lishan City is also Captured by Our Forces
Author:
Date: 1947-11-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Eyuwan Front, 1st dispatch: Belated news: Part of the People's Liberation Army, combined with local armed forces, liberated Lishan County and Xuanhuadian on the 15th of last month, annihilating more than 100 Chiang bandit security troops. Xuanhuadian was the capital of our Eyuwan Border Region during the Anti-Japanese War. In July of last year, the Chiang bandits violated the agreement and besieged it, and we were forced to withdraw from the area.

### 抗日时期鄂豫皖首府  宣化店重告解放  礼山城亦被我攻克

1947-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

    抗日时期鄂豫皖首府
    宣化店重告解放
    礼山城亦被我攻克
    【新华社鄂豫皖前线一日电】迟到消息：解放军一部分结合地方武装于上月十五日解放礼山县城和宣化店，歼蒋匪保安队百余名。按宣化店在抗日战争时为我鄂豫皖边区首府所在地，去年七月蒋贼违约围攻，我被迫撤退该地。
