Title: Political Department of the Hebei-Shandong-Henan River Defense Headquarters Prepares to Convene Waterborne Model Hero Conference
Author:
Date: 1947-11-24
Page: 2

　　Political Department of the Hebei-Shandong-Henan River Defense Headquarters
　　Prepares to Convene Waterborne Model Hero Conference
　　[Hebei-Shandong-Henan Yellow River News, 22nd] The Political Department of the River Defense Headquarters is preparing for a Model Hero Conference. Since June 30th of this year, when the Liu-Deng army crossed the river to the south, our 3,000 sailors have used their blood and sweat to guarantee north-south transportation. In the process of completing these great tasks, a large number of waterborne heroes and model workers have emerged, and many model boats have been created. In order to commend and celebrate their achievements, the Political Department of the River Defense Headquarters has decided to convene a Model Hero Conference on the 23rd of this month to reward the meritorious people who have performed meritorious service for the people. Model heroes who will attend the military region's Model Hero Conference will also be selected. Each unit has selected its own model heroes, totaling more than one hundred. The conference preparatory committee is actively preparing everything, and a grand occasion is expected at that time.

### 冀鲁豫河防司令部政治处  筹开水上英模大会

1947-11-24
第2版()
专栏：

　　冀鲁豫河防司令部政治处
    筹开水上英模大会
    【本报冀鲁豫黄河二十二日电】河防司令部政治处正筹备英模大会。从今年六月三十日刘邓大军南渡起，到现在止，我三千水手用血汗保证了南北交通，在完成这些伟大任务当中，涌现了大批的水上英雄、工作模范，创造了不少的模范船。为了表模庆功，河防司令部政治处特决定于本月二十三日，召开英模大会，奖励为人民立功的功臣，并将选出出席军区英模大会的英模，现各单位已选出自己的英模共计百余名。大会筹委会正积极准备一切，届时当有一番盛况。
