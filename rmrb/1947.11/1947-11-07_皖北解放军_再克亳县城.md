Title: Anhui Northern Liberation Army Recaptures Bo County Town
Author:
Date: 1947-11-07
Page: 1

    Anhui Northern Liberation Army
    Recaptures Bo County Town
    Xinhua News Agency, Su Lu Yu Wan, 5th: A unit of the People's Liberation Army recaptured Bo County town for the second time on October 31st. The defending enemy fled north towards Shangqiu.
    Xinhua News Agency, East China, 5th: Belated news: The Huaihai People's Liberation Army in northern Anhui and southern Anhui, coordinating with each other, advanced into the Xu (yi) Feng (yang) Jia (shan) area on October 4th to wipe out small groups of enemies, capturing 46 Chiang Kai-shek soldiers alive, killing 4, and seizing one light machine gun and twenty rifles. There are currently no enemy traces in the area.

### 皖北解放军  再克亳县城

1947-11-07
第1版()
专栏：

    皖北解放军
    再克亳县城
    【新华社苏鲁豫皖五日电】解放军一部于十月三十一日二次攻克亳县城，守敌北窜商邱。
    【新华社华东五日电】迟到消息：淮北淮南蒋后人民解放军互相配合，于十月四日挺进盱（眙）凤（阳）嘉（山）地区肃清小股敌人，计生俘蒋军四十六名，击毙四名，缴获轻机枪一挺，步枪二十支，现该地区已无敌踪。
