Title: Taiyue Nineteenth Military Sub-district Local Armed Forces Annihilate Over a Thousand Bandits in One and a Half Months, Exceeding Four-Month Annihilation Target

Author:

Date: 1947-11-05
Page: 1

    Taiyue Nineteenth Military Sub-district Local Armed Forces
    Annihilate Over a Thousand Bandits in One and a Half Months
    Exceeding Four-Month Annihilation Target
    [This newspaper's Taiyue dispatch of the 3rd] The Taiyue Nineteenth Military Sub-district's local armed forces annihilated over a thousand enemies in forty-eight days, exceeding their four-month annihilation target by a quarter. In order to actively participate in the great counter-offensive, the Nineteenth Military Sub-district's local armed forces drafted a plan in September to annihilate 810 enemies from September to December. Due to the unit's active and continuous search for and annihilation of the enemy's weaknesses, after the Xiangling battle, a total of 1,057 enemies were killed, wounded, or captured in forty-eight days. The unit seized four cannons of various types, 34 light and heavy machine guns, 425 rifles and pistols, six grenade launchers, two Sten submachine guns, seven boxes of mortar shells, and over 20,000 rounds of various bullets. The unit also recovered eight towns, including Xiangling City, and Jiade, Eshe (20 li southeast of Linfen), Fushou, Nanxindian, Jindian Town (15 li north of Xiangling), Liucun, and Xiaoyu (15 li west of the Fen River, Linfen).

### 太岳十九分区地方武装  一个半月歼匪千余  超过四个月歼敌任务

1947-11-05
第1版()
专栏：

    太岳十九分区地方武装
    一个半月歼匪千余
    超过四个月歼敌任务
    【本报太岳三日电】太岳十九分区地方武装，四十八天歼敌一千余人；并超过四个月歼敌任务的四分之一。十九分区地方武装为积极投入大反攻，曾在九月份拟定了九月至十二月四个月的歼敌八百一十人的计划，由于该部队积极的不间断的找寻敌人弱点歼灭敌人，至此次襄陵战斗后，前后四十八天中，共毙伤俘敌一千零五十七人，缴各种炮四门，轻重机枪三十四挺，长短枪四百二十五支，掷弹筒六个，斯登手提枪二支，迫击炮弹七箱，各种子弹二万余发。收复襄陵城及贾得、鹅舍（临汾东南二十里）福寿、南辛店、金店镇（襄陵北十五里）、刘村、小榆（临汾河西十五里）等城镇八座。
