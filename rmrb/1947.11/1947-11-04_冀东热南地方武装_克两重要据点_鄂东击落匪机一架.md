Title: Local Armed Forces in Eastern Hebei and Southern Rehe Capture Two Important Strongholds; Eastern Hubei Shoots Down a Bandit Plane
Author:
Date: 1947-11-04
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Eastern Hebei, 2nd. Belated news: Local armed forces in Eastern Hebei captured two major towns south of the Beining Railway, Daxinzhuang (southeast of Tangshan) and Yingshouyingzi outside Chengde, on the 20th and 21st of last month. They annihilated a part of Chiang Kai-shek's bandit gang in Daxinzhuang. The enemy in Yingshouyingzi fled back to Chengde on the evening of the 21st. Note: Yingshouyingzi is a coal mining area. A power plant was built during the Japanese occupation. It was recovered by us after August 15th, and occupied by the Chiang bandit Security Third Regiment on September 18th.
    Xinhua News Agency, Eastern Hubei-Henan-Anhui Front, 2nd. On the 29th of last month, in the battle to annihilate the enemy's 40th Division and 82nd Brigade northeast of Qichun, we shot down a Chiang bandit "B51" fighter plane. The pilot, Chen Haiquan, was captured. Also, according to the final count of the battle results, we captured 478 light machine guns, instead of the previously reported 298.

### 冀东热南地方武装  克两重要据点  鄂东击落匪机一架

1947-11-04
第1版()
专栏：

    冀东热南地方武装
    克两重要据点
    鄂东击落匪机一架
    【新华社冀东二日电】迟到消息：冀东地方武装于上月二十日及二十一日连克北宁路以南大新庄（在唐山东南），及承德外围之鹰手营子两大重镇。歼大新庄之蒋匪伙会一部。魔手营子之敌则于二十一日晚逃回承德。按：鹰手营子为煤矿区，日寇侵占时期建有发电厂，“八一五”后即为我收复，九月十八日为蒋匪保安第三团所侵占。
    【新华社鄂豫皖前线二日电】上月二十九日，蕲春东北歼灭敌四十师及八十二旅战斗中，我击落蒋匪“Ｂ５１”驱逐机一架。驾驶员陈海泉被俘。又息：该次战果中，据最后清查，我缴获轻机枪四百七十八挺，前报为二百九十八挺。
