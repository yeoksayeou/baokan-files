Title: League of Communists' Slogans Commemorating the October Revolution
Author:
Date: 1947-11-06
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, November 4th: TASS, Moscow, November 1st: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union has issued the following slogans commemorating the 30th anniversary of the Great Socialist October Revolution:

　　(1) Hail the 30th anniversary of the Great Socialist October Revolution!

　　(2) Pay tribute to the people who fought for the victory of democracy and socialism!

　　(3) Long live the cooperation of all countries in the world striving for lasting peace!

　　(4) Working people of all countries, expose the instigators of new wars and defend the cause of peace!

　　(5) Fraternal greetings to the Slavic nations liberated from the shackles of the German aggressors, long live the indestructible friendship of the Slavic nations!

　　(6) The Soviet regime of the working people of the Soviet Union has united all ethnic groups in our country into a unified body of fraternal love, strengthening the indestructible friendship of all nationalities of the Soviet Union!

　　(7) Long live the Soviet Union, a reliable fortress of peace and security, a reliable fortress of national freedom and independence!

　　(8) Long live the Soviet Army! It is the meritorious liberator who defends our country's freedom and independence!

　　(9) Soldiers, sergeants, officers, and generals of the Soviet Army and Navy: Tirelessly master your military and political knowledge, grasp the experience of the Great Patriotic War, master your combat skills, and strengthen the power of the armed forces of the Soviet state!

　　(10) Long live the border guards who defend our borders with a hundredfold vigilance!

　　(11) Pay tribute to the heroes of the Soviet Union! Pay tribute to the heroes of socialist labor! They are the best sons and daughters of our great country!

　　(12) Workers, peasants, and Soviet intellectuals, make new achievements and struggle to complete and exceed the post-war Five-Year Plan!

　　(13) Pay tribute to the men and women workers, engineers, and technicians in various industries who have completed the second year of the Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule!

　　The following slogans are addressed to the workers, engineers, and technicians in basic industries, public utilities, transportation, and trade sectors, and to the collective farmers and agricultural experts, urging them to increase production and further strengthen the Soviet national economy.

　　To scientists, writers, and artists, the Central Committee of the League of Communists calls on them to enrich science and technology with new research, inventions, and discoveries, to boldly suggest and resolutely apply scientific achievements to production, and to create artistic works worthy of the great Soviet era and characterized by a high degree of ideological content.

　　The following slogans also call on:

　　Men and women teachers and public education workers, to improve the level of education, cultivate the younger generation's spirit of selflessness and love for the motherland, and cultivate active and firm fighters for the victory of communism!

　　The whole nation should take care of the honored veterans of the Patriotic War and the family members of the Soviet soldiers who sacrificed themselves for the freedom and independence of the motherland!

　　Soviet trade unions should launch socialist competitions to complete and exceed the post-war Five-Year Plan and accumulate the experience of those pioneers who have opened up new paths in production! Always care about the material and cultural improvement of the working people!

　　Long live the Soviet women! They are the great force of Soviet society and active participants in our country's political, economic, and cultural life.

　　Soviet young men and women, master the most advanced technology, science, and culture. Firmly and bravely prepare to overcome difficulties. Work selflessly for the benefit of our country!

　　Students of Soviet schools, tirelessly master knowledge and prepare to be strong fighters for the cause of Lenin and Stalin!

　　Communist Party members and Communist Youth League members, for the sake of new and powerful progress in our motherland's economy and culture, and for the sake of further strengthening the power of the Soviet state, stand at the forefront of the struggle for this cause!

　　Long live the powerful Soviet Union - the consolidated fortress of friendship, happiness, and glory of all nationalities in our country!

　　Long live the great Bolshevik Party, the party of Lenin and Stalin! The Party is the vanguard of the Soviet people, strengthened in battle, the inspirer and organizer of our victory!

　　Advance under the banner of Lenin, under the leadership of Stalin! Advance towards the victory of Communism!

### 联共纪念十月革命口号

1947-11-06
第1版()
专栏：

    联共纪念十月革命口号
    【新华社陕北四日电】塔斯社莫斯科一日电：苏联共产党中央委员会发布纪念社会主义十月革命三十周年口号如下：
    （一）迎接伟大社会主义十月革命三十周年！
    （二）向为民主与社会主义的胜利而战斗的人民致敬！
    （三）为巩固的和平而奋斗的世界各国合作万岁！
    （四）一切国家的劳动人民，揭露新战争的煽动者，保卫和平事业！
    （五）自从德国侵略者桎梏中解放出来的斯拉夫民族致兄弟的敬礼，斯拉夫民族不可摧毁的友谊万岁！
    （六）苏联的劳动人民苏维埃政权，已经把我国国内一切民族团结成兄弟友爱的统一体，加强苏联各民族不可摧毁的友谊！
    （七）和平与安全的可靠堡垒，民族自由和独立的可靠堡垒——苏联万岁！
    （八）苏军万岁！它是保卫我国自由独立的功勋卓著的解放者！
    （九）陆海军战士们、士官、校尉、苏联陆海军将领们：不倦地精通你们的军事和政治知识，掌握伟大爱国战争的经验，精通你的作战技能，加强苏维埃国家武装部队的威力！
    （十）以百倍警惕保卫我国边疆的边防军万岁！
    （十一）向苏联英雄们致敬！向社会主义劳动英雄们致敬！他们是我们伟大国家的最优秀儿女！
    （十二）工人们、农民们、苏维埃知识分子们进一步取得新的成绩，为争取完成并超过战后五年计划而斗争！
    （十三）向各业提前完成五年计划第二年计划的男女工人们，工程师和技师们致敬！
    以下的口号是向基本工业、公用事业，运输、贸易部门的工人、工程师与技师的号召，向集体农民及农业专家的号召，敦促他们增加生产进一步加强苏联国民经济。
    对科学家、文学和艺术工作者们，联共中央号召他们，以新的研究、发明和发现来丰富科学和技术，大胆地建议，坚决地把科学的成就应用到生产中去，创造不愧于伟大苏维埃时代的、具有高度思想内容之特点的艺术作品。
    以下的口号又号召：
    男女教师们、公共教育工作人员们，增进教育水平，培养年青一代大公无私与热爱祖国的精神，培植积极而坚定的、争取共产主义胜利的战士！
    全国照顾爱国战争的荣誉军人，和为祖国自由独立而牺牲的苏维埃战士遗属！
    苏维埃职工会，为完成并超过战后五年计划而展开社会主义竞赛，积累那些在生产中开辟新路的先驱者的经验！时刻关心劳动人民物质与文化的提高！
    苏维埃妇女万岁！他们是苏维埃社会的伟大力量，是我国政治、经济和文化生活的积极参加者。
    苏维埃男女青年，精通最前进的技术、科学和文化。坚定而勇敢地准备克服困难。奋不顾身地为我们国家的利益而工作！
    苏维埃学校的学生们，不倦地精通知识，准备作一个列宁、斯大林事业的坚强战士！
    共产党员和共产主义青年团团员们，为求得我祖国经济与文化之新的、强有力的前进，为争取苏维埃国家威力进一步的加强，站到为这事业斗争的最前列去！
    强大的苏联——我国各族人民的友谊、幸福和光荣的巩固堡垒——万岁！
    伟大的布尔什维克党列宁和斯大林的党万岁！党是在战斗中增强了的苏维埃人民的前卫、我们胜利的鼓舞者和组织者！
    在列宁的旗帜下，在斯大林的领导下前进！向着共产主义的胜利前进！
