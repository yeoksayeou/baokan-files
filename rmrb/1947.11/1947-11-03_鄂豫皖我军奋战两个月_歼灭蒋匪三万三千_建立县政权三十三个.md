Title: Our Army Fought Valiantly in the Hubei-Henan-Anhui Border Region for Two Months, Annihilating 33,000 Chiang Kai-shek Bandits, Establishing 33 County-Level Governments
Author:
Date: 1947-11-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hubei-Henan-Anhui Front, 1st: The Hubei-Henan-Anhui Front Headquarters has just issued its ninth communiqué, announcing the following results of our army's operations from August 29th, when it fully entered the Dabie Mountains, to October 29th, a period of two months: (1) Annihilation of Chiang Kai-shek's Regular Army and Local Security Forces in terms of strength and unit designation: (A) Annihilation of Chiang Kai-shek's Regular Army includes the entire 82nd Brigade of the 52nd Division, two battalions of the 99th Regiment of the 33rd Brigade, a part of the 88th Division Headquarters, the entire 62nd Brigade, the 40th Division Headquarters, the entire 106th Brigade, the entire 115th Regiment of the 39th Brigade, the entire 29th Regiment of the New 10th Brigade of the 58th Division, the entire 564th Regiment of the 188th Brigade of the 46th Division, a part of the New 17th Brigade Headquarters of the 56th Division, the entire three battalions of the First Regiment and the first and second battalions of the Second Regiment, most of the Sixth Regiment of the Second Brigade of the 203rd Division, and parts of the 85th Division, 56th Division, 58th Division, 7th Division, 48th Division, and 46th Division, totaling approximately 4,000 men. In total, 30,382 Chiang Kai-shek regular army troops were annihilated, including 10,680 enemy killed or wounded, and 19,702 enemy troops captured, including Tang Jia'yi, Deputy Brigade Commander of the 62nd Brigade. (B) Annihilation of Chiang Kai-shek's Local Security Forces includes security forces from various counties such as Jingfu and Lihuang, as well as parts of the Eastern Hubei Security Regiment and the Second General Team of the Hubei Security Forces, totaling 2,730 local reactionary troops annihilated, including 880 killed or wounded, including the puppet county magistrate of Jingfu, Li Jiangang, and 1,850 officers and soldiers captured, including Zhou Xiaoxuan, Deputy Commander of the Eastern Hubei Security Forces, Xiong Zhenxuan, Commander of the Second Column of the Hubei Security Forces, and Huang Guoru, puppet county magistrate of Jingfu. (C) In total, 33,112 Chiang Kai-shek regular army and local troops were annihilated, including 11,560 killed or wounded, and 21,552 officers and soldiers captured. (D) Major seizures, according to incomplete statistics, include: 4 American-style mountain guns, 75 mortars, 71 small cannons, 96 grenade launchers, 152 heavy machine guns, 635 light machine guns, 75 dual-purpose light and heavy machine guns, 45 submachine guns, 9,948 rifles and carbines, 263 pistols, 1.1 million rounds of various bullets, 2,800 rounds of various artillery shells, 32 radio stations, 11 trucks, 280 mules and horses, 79 telephones, one power plant, two repair shops, and many other spoils of war. (E) Three Chiang Kai-shek planes shot down. (2) A total of 23 county towns were captured, including Guangshan, Huangchuan, Gushi, Jingfu, Shangcheng, Macheng, Lihuang, Lu'an, Huoshan, Huang'an, Luotian, Yingshan, Shucheng, Xishui, Tongcheng, Lujiang, Guangji, Susong, Huangmei, Qianshan, Yuexi, Taihu, and Wangjiang. Among them, those recovered twice include: Guangshan, Huangchuan, Gushi, Lihuang, Lu'an, Huoshan, Shucheng, Tongcheng, Lujiang, Xishui, Huangmei, Guangji, Susong, Qianshan, and Taihu, totaling 15 towns; those recovered three times include: Jingfu and Yingshan, totaling 2 towns; and those recovered four times include: Luotian, totaling 1 town. Over 100 important towns and strongholds were captured, including Wuxue, Songbu, Qiting, San Dian Street, Xinzhou, Hekou Town, Tuanfeng, Xiaba River, Konglong Town, Lijiaji, and Huayang Town, liberating a population of over 5 million. Of the above 23 county towns, the following 10 county towns are still under our army's control today: Jingfu, Lihuang, Yuexi, Qianshan, Taihu, Wangjiang, Susong, Huangmei, Guangji, and Xishui. (3) Thirty-three patriotic and democratic county governments were established, including: 16 counties in western Anhui: Dushan (northwest of Huoshan), Lu'an, Huoshan, Huoqiu, Lihuang, Shucheng, Shuliu (between Shucheng and Lu'an), Yuexi, Tongcheng, Huxi (west of Chaohu Lake), Tonglu (between Tongcheng and Lujiang), Qianshan, Qianhuai (between Qianshan and Huaining), Taihu, Susong, and Yingshan; 7 counties in southeastern Henan: Shangcheng, Gushi, Guangshang (between Guangshan and Shangcheng), Huangchuan, Guangshan, Luoshan, and Jingfu; 10 counties in eastern Hubei: Lishan, Huang'an, Mapo (between Macheng and Huangpi), Macheng, Xishui, Luotian, Huanggang, Guangji, Huangmei, and Chun. Although some of the above county towns are still occupied by Chiang Kai-shek bandits, they are only trapped inside the city, and the vast countryside is under our army's control.

### 鄂豫皖我军奋战两个月  歼灭蒋匪三万三千  建立县政权三十三个

1947-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

    鄂豫皖我军奋战两个月
    歼灭蒋匪三万三千
    建立县政权三十三个
    【新华社鄂豫皖前线一日电】鄂豫皖前线司令部顷发表第九号公报，公布我军自八月二十九日全部进入大别山起，至十月二十九日止，两个月战绩如下：（一）歼灭蒋匪正规军及地方保安团队兵力和番号（甲）歼灭蒋匪正规军计有五二师八二旅全部，三三旅九九团两个营，八八师师直一部，六二旅全部，四十师师部，一○六旅全部，三九旅之一一五团全部，五八师新十旅二十九团全部，四六师一八八旅五六四团全部，五六师新十七旅旅直一部，一团三营及二团一、二两个营全部，二○三师二旅六团两个营大部，另毙伤俘八五师、五六师、五八师、七师、四八师、四六师各一部，约四千余人，共计歼灭蒋匪正规军三万零三百八十二名，内毙伤敌一万零六百八十名。俘虏敌六二旅副旅长汤家揖以下一万九千七百零二名。（乙）歼灭蒋匪地方保安团队有：经扶、立煌等各县保安团队，及鄂东保安团、湖北保安第二总队等各一部，共计歼杂顽二千七百卅名，内毙伤经扶伪县长李建岗以下八百八十名，俘虏鄂东保安副指挥官周晓轩、湖北保安第二纵队司令熊振轩、经扶伪县长黄国儒等以下官兵一千八百五十名。（丙）以上总共歼灭蒋匪正规军与地方军三万三千一百十二名，内毙伤一万一千五百六十名，俘虏官兵两万一千五百五十二名。（丁）主要缴获据不完全统计有：美式山炮四门，迫击炮七十五门，小炮七十一门，掷弹筒九十六个，重机枪一百五十二挺，轻机枪六百卅五挺，轻重两用机关枪七十五挺，手提机枪四十五挺，步马枪九千九百四十八支，短枪二百六十三支，各种子弹一百一十万发，各种炮弹二千八百发，电台卅二部，汽车十一辆，骡马二百八十匹，电话机七十九架，发电厂一座，修械所两座，其他胜利品甚多。（戊）击落蒋机三架。（二）共计攻克县城二十三座，计有光山、潢川、固始、经扶、商城、麻城、立煌、六安、霍山、黄安、罗田、英山、舒城、浠水、桐城、庐江、广济、宿松、黄梅、潜山、岳西、太湖、望江等，其中收复两次者计有：光山、潢川、固始、立煌、六安、霍山、舒城、桐城、庐江、浠水、黄梅、广济、宿松潜山、太湖等十五座，收复三次者计有：经扶，英山两座，收复四次者有罗田一座，攻克武穴，宋埠、岐亭、三店街、新洲、河口镇、团风、下巴河、孔垅镇、李家集、华阳镇等重镇据点一百余处，解放人口五百余万。以上二十三个县城今日仍为我军控制者有：经扶、立煌、岳西、潜山、太湖、望江、宿松、黄梅、广济、浠水等十座县城。（三）建立了三十三个爱国民主县政府，计：皖西有独山（在霍山西北）、六安、霍山、霍邱、立煌、舒城、舒六（在舒城、六安间）、岳西、桐城、湖西（在巢湖西）、桐庐（桐城和庐江间）、潜山、潜怀（潜山怀宁间）、太湖、宿松、英山等十六个县。豫东南有商城、固始、光商（光山商城间）、横川、光山、罗山、经扶等七个县。鄂东有礼山、黄安、麻陂、（麻城黄陂间）麻城、浠水、罗田、黄冈、广济、黄梅、春等十个县。以上某些县城目前虽还有蒋匪盘据，但仅困守城内，广大乡村均在我军控制之下。
