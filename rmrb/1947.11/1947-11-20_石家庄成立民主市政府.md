Title: Shijiazhuang Establishes Democratic Municipal Government
Author:
Date: 1947-11-20
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji, 18th: Shijiazhuang News: The democratic municipal government and garrison headquarters of this city have been formally established. Mayor Ke Guangshi has arrived and taken office. The municipal government is working with the garrison troops to investigate hidden spies and traitors, and to sort out existing factories and enterprises, as well as to appease the citizens. The market is gradually becoming active, and two theaters have also resumed performances.

### 石家庄成立民主市政府

1947-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　石家庄成立民主市政府
    【新华社晋察冀十八日电】石家庄讯：本市民主市政府及卫戍司令部已正式成立。市长柯广施已到任视事，市府正联合卫戍部队清查隐匿之奸特及清理原有之工厂企业，并进行安抚市民。现市面已渐见活跃，两所戏院亦已恢复演出。
