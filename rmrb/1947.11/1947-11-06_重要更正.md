    Important Corrections
    According to a telegram from the Xinhua News Agency: In the report by Zhdanov at the Nine-Party Conference, as published in this newspaper on the second, the five characters "其军队数量" (qí jūn duì shù liàng - the quantity of its army) in the fourth paragraph of the third section are a mistake for the character "或" (huò - or); in the nineteenth paragraph of the third section, "美国西方集团的好汉们" (Měiguó xīfāng jítuán de hǎohànmen - the heroes of the American Western bloc) is a mistake for "美国式的西方集团" (Měiguó shì de xīfāng jítuán - the American-style Western bloc). The thirty-two characters in the twenty-second paragraph of the third section, namely "有些国家……事实的过程" (yǒuxiē guójiā...shìshí de guòchéng - some countries...the process of facts), is a mistake for "反帝国主义阵营所给与‘马歇尔计划’的评价可完全证诸事实" (fǎn dìguó zhǔyì zhènyíng suǒ gěi yǔ 'Mǎxiē'ěr jìhuà' de píngjià kě wánquán zhèngzhū shìshí - the evaluation of the "Marshall Plan" given by the anti-imperialist camp can be fully evidenced by facts).
    Furthermore, in the article "Zhdanov's Criticism of Aristotle's Philosophy," published on the fifth, the "八月" (bā yuè - August) in the first sentence is a mistake for "六月" (liù yuè - June); the "八月廿四日" (bā yuè niàn sì rì - August 24th) under the ninetieth character is a mistake for "六月廿日" (liù yuè niàn rì - June 20th). These corrections are hereby made together.

### 重要更正

1947-11-06
第1版()
专栏：

    重要更正
    据新华总社来电：二日本报所载日丹诺夫于九国党代会议之报告第三部分第四段内“其军队数量”五字乃“或”字之误；第三部分第十九段内“美国西方集团的好汉们”乃为“美国式的西方集团”之误。第三部分第二十二段，即“有些国家……事实的过程”共三十二字，乃“反帝国主义阵营所给与‘马歇尔计划’的评价可完全证诸事实”之误。
    又五日所载日丹诺夫评亚氏哲学一文中，第一句中之“八月”乃“六月”之误；第九十字下“八月廿四日”乃“六月廿日”之误。特此一并更正。
