Title: Our Army Repeatedly Sweeps Away Chiang Kai-shek Bandits in Henan, Anhui, and Jiangsu; Recaptures Shangcai, Huaiyang, Woyang, and Xincai; Chiang Kai-shek's Bandit Security Regiments in Henan Province Are Almost Entirely Annihilated
Author: 
Date: 1947-11-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Su Lu Yu Wan Front, 1st: The People's Liberation Army continues to repeatedly sweep away Chiang Kai-shek's bandits on the vast plains of Henan, Anhui, and Jiangsu. On the 27th of last month, it captured Shangcai County, 60 li east of the central section of the Pinghan Railway. On the same day, it re-entered the cities of Huaiyang in eastern Henan and Woyang in northern Anhui. On the 28th, it re-entered Xincai County. The enemy troops guarding each city dared not confront us and fled in a scramble. The local armies of the Henan, Anhui, and Jiangsu liberated areas cooperated in the fighting and on the 27th, recaptured the important town of Suixi Kou, 60 kilometers southwest of Xuzhou, killing 7 enemies, capturing 10, and seizing a portion of supplies. Immediately afterwards, they swept through the Baisahnji area, capturing alive over 200 Chiang Kai-shek bandits and weapons.

Xinhua News Agency, Su Lu Yu Wan Front, 1st: Chiang Kai-shek's bandits' only six provincial security regiments in Henan Province have been almost entirely annihilated by the People's Liberation Army. The Sixth Regiment was annihilated in Suixian in February, two battalions of the Fifth Regiment were annihilated in Qixian in September, and the Third and Fourth Regiments were annihilated in Zhoujiakou on October 23. In the Chenliu battle, the First and Second Regiments were also completely wiped out. Only one battalion of the remaining Fifth Regiment is incomplete. Kaifeng, guarded by Liu Maoen, Chairman of Henan Province for Chiang Kai-shek's bandits, is also under the threat of the People's Liberation Army's powerful offensive and is in imminent danger.

Xinhua News Agency, Su Lu Yu Wan Front, 1st: The results of the battle to liberate Chenliu County, 45 li southeast of Kaifeng, on the 26th of last month, have been verified as follows: The entire Chiang Kai-shek Henan Provincial Security First and Second Regiments and the Chenliu County Security Team were annihilated, including 959 Chiang Kai-shek bandits captured alive below the rank of deputy regimental commander, over 200 enemies killed or wounded, and the seizure of 2 mortars, 7 heavy machine guns, 19 light machine guns, 349 rifles, over 100,000 rounds of ammunition, over 80 mortar shells, and many other military supplies.

### 豫皖苏我军反复扫荡蒋匪  克上蔡淮阳涡阳新蔡  河南省蒋匪保安团被歼殆尽

1947-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

    豫皖苏我军反复扫荡蒋匪
    克上蔡淮阳涡阳新蔡
    河南省蒋匪保安团被歼殆尽
    【新华社苏鲁豫皖前线一日电】解放军往来豫皖苏大平原上，继续反复扫荡蒋匪。于上月二十七日攻克平汉路中段东侧六十里之上蔡县城。同日，再度进驻豫东之淮阳、皖北之涡阳两城。二十八日，再度进驻新蔡县城。各城守敌不敢当锋，争先逃遁。豫皖苏解放区地方军配合作战，于二十七日再度攻克徐州西南六十公里之重镇濉溪口，毙敌七名，俘十名，缴获一部。继即扫荡百善集地区，生俘蒋匪人枪二百余。
    【新华社苏鲁豫皖前线一日电】蒋匪河南省仅有之六个省保安团，已被解放军歼灭殆尽。计第六团于二月间在睢县被歼，第五团两个营于九月间在杞县被歼，第三、第四两团于十月二十三日在周家口被歼。陈留一役，第一、第二两团又全部覆灭。仅胜下之第五团一个营，亦残缺不全。蒋匪河南省主席刘茂恩负责看守的开封，亦处于解放军强大攻势威胁下，岌岌可危。
    【新华社苏鲁豫皖前线一日电】解放军上月二十六日解放开封东南四十五里陈留县城之役，战果查明如下：全歼蒋匪河南省保安第一、二两团及陈留县保安队，计生擒蒋匪保安团副团长以下九百五十九名，毙伤敌二百余名，缴获迫击炮两门、重机枪七挺、轻机枪十九挺、步枪三百四十九支、子弹十万余发、迫击炮弹八十余发，其他军用品甚多。
