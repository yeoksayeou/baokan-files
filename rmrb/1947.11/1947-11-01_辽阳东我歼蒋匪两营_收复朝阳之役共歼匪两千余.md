Title: East of Liaoyang, We Annihilated Two Battalions of Chiang Kai-shek's Bandits; In the Battle to Recover Chaoyang, We Annihilated More Than 2,000 Bandits
Author:
Date: 1947-11-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northeast Front, 30th: A certain unit of the Democratic Allied Army annihilated nearly two battalions of Chiang Kai-shek's 52nd Army in Liaoyang. At 8:00 AM on the 25th, the 1st Battalion of the 4th Regiment of the 2nd Division and the 3rd Battalion of the 75th Regiment of the 25th Division of Chiang Kai-shek's 52nd Army, stationed in Xiaotunzi east of Liaoyang, fled towards Da'anping. Our troops launched a counterattack eight hours after the enemy occupied the area. After fierce fighting until 9:00 AM on the 26th, the majority of the enemy was annihilated by our forces, and two battalions of enemy reinforcements, supported by five armored vehicles from Xiaotunzi, were repelled. In this battle, we killed or wounded over 300 enemy officers and soldiers, including the deputy regimental commander and four company commanders of the 4th Regiment, and captured 278 enemy officers and soldiers. We seized eight artillery pieces, 41 light and heavy machine guns, and three armored vehicles.

Xinhua News Agency, Northeast Front, 30th: In the battle to recover Chaoyang on the 23rd by the Jicha Rehe Liaoning People's Liberation Army, it has been confirmed that the enemy's 3rd Regiment of the Provisional 51st Division, as well as units of the 3rd Cavalry Army and the Security Police Squadron, totaling more than 2,000 men, were annihilated (the previous telegram reported over 1,700 men).

Xinhua News Agency, Northeast Front, 30th: Delayed news: In the battle to recover Xinqiu southeast of Fuxin on the 17th, Tang Baohuang, the major general division commander of Chiang Kai-shek's Provisional 51st Division, who died a pointless death, had his remains moved by us to the rear of our positions under artillery fire, purchased a coffin for him, and after the battle, we wrote a letter and sent two of Tang's soldiers, Wang Yuzhuo and others, providing them with 50,000 yuan in travel expenses to escort the body to the enemy's Shenyang headquarters. In this battle, after investigation, a total of 14 enemy officers at the rank of colonel or above were killed or wounded, including Wang Dao, commander of the 2nd Regiment, Gong Guocui, lieutenant colonel deputy commander, and Hu Chen, major battalion commander.

### 辽阳东我歼蒋匪两营  收复朝阳之役共歼匪两千余

1947-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    辽阳东我歼蒋匪两营
    收复朝阳之役共歼匪两千余
    【新华社东北前线三十日电】民主联军某部在辽阳歼蒋匪五十二军近两个营。二十五日八时驻辽阳东小屯子蒋匪五十二军第二师第四团第一营，及二十五师七十五团第三营向大安平窜犯，我军于敌进占后八小时施行反击，激战至二十六日晨九时，该敌大部被我歼灭，并击退自小屯子以装甲车五辆掩护增援之敌两个营。是役我毙伤敌第四团副团长及连长四名以下官兵三百余，俘敌官兵二百七十八名。缴获炮八门，轻重机枪四十一挺，装甲车三辆。
    【新华社东北前线三十日电】冀察热辽人民解放军二十三日收复朝阳之役，查明歼敌暂五十一师第三团，及骑三军、保警中队各一部共计二千余人（前电为一千七百余人。）
    【新华社东北前线三十日电】迟到消息：十七日我军收复阜新东南新邱之役中，无谓牺牲之蒋军暂五十一师少将师长唐保黄，其遗尸在炮火下为我移往阵地后方，购棺装殓，战斗结束后，我修函派遣唐之士兵王玉琢等二人，发给路费五万元，护送至敌沈阳行辕。是役毙伤敌经查明者，校级以上军官共十四名，其中有第二团团长王道、中校副团长龚国粹、少校营长胡忱等。
