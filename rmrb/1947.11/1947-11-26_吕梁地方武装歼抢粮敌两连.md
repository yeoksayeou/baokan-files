Title: Lüliang Local Armed Forces Annihilate Two Companies of Enemy Looting Grain
Author:
Date: 1947-11-26
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Jin Sui, 21st: Delayed news: A certain unit of the Lüliang local armed forces ambushed a battalion of the Yan bandit's 70th Division, which had come out of the city to loot grain, at Shangwen Village, 20 miles southwest of Fenyang, at dawn on the 5th of this month. The battle lasted only 40 minutes, annihilating two companies of the enemy and part of a machine gun company. Over 60 enemies, including the company commander, were killed or wounded, and over 70 were captured alive, including the company political instructor, Li Qingshan. A portion of their weapons was seized.

### 吕梁地方武装歼抢粮敌两连

1947-11-26
第2版()
专栏：

　　吕梁地方武装歼抢粮敌两连
    【新华社晋绥二十一日电】迟到消息：吕梁地方武装某部，于本月五日拂晓在汾阳西南二十里之尚文村，伏击出城抢粮之阎匪七十师一个营，战斗仅四十分钟，歼敌两连及机枪连一部，毙伤敌连长以下六十人，生俘连政指李青山以下七十余人，缴获武器一部。
