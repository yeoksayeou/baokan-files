Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Meets with Prime Minister Ne Win
Author:
Date: 1960-01-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 24th: Premier Zhou Enlai met with the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, General Ne Win, and his entourage this evening: U Khin Tun Aung, U Ba Swe, Aung Gyi, Ba Hlaing, U Win Pe, U Khin Nyo, U Hla Khin Maung, Lun, U Ba Thein, U Soe Saing, and U Tun Tin, among others.

Present at the meeting was the Burmese Ambassador to China, Kyaw Winn.

Also present were Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Geng Biao, Lieutenant General of the Air Force Wang Bingzhang, our Ambassador to Burma Li Yimang, Director of the Department of Treaty and Law of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Yao Zhongming, Director of the First Asian Department Zhang Wenjin, and Director of the Protocol Department Yu Peiwen, etc.

### 周恩来总理会见奈温总理

1960-01-25
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理会见奈温总理
    新华社24日讯　周恩来总理今天晚间会见了缅甸联邦总理奈温将军和他的随行人员：吴千吞昂、吴巴盛、昂季、巴林顿、吴温佩、吴钦纽、吴拉钦貌、伦、吴巴漆、吴苏幸和吴吞丁等。
    会见时缅甸驻中国大使叫温在座。
    在座的，有国务院副总理陈毅，外交部副部长章汉夫、耿飚，空军中将王秉璋，我国驻缅甸大使李一氓，外交部条约法律司司长姚仲明、第一亚洲司司长章文晋、礼宾司司长俞沛文等。
