Title: Our Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to Guinea Presents Letter of Introduction to Guinean President
Author:
Date: 1960-01-02
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, December 31st. Zhao Yuan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Republic of Guinea, presented his letter of introduction to Guinean President and Minister of Foreign Affairs Sékou Touré on December 31st.
    At the presentation ceremony, Zhao Yuan conveyed the best wishes of the Chinese government and people to the Guinean government and people. He expressed his hope that the friendly relations between the two countries would be further developed.
    President Sékou Touré welcomed the Chinese Chargé d'Affaires ad interim. He said that he believed that the friendly relations between Guinea and China would gradually develop.
    Secretary-General of the Guinean Presidential Office, Jean Faragué, and Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs, Cissoko Fodé, also attended the presentation ceremony.

### 我驻几内亚临时代办  向几内亚总统呈递介绍信

1960-01-02
第3版()
专栏：

    我驻几内亚临时代办
    向几内亚总统呈递介绍信
    新华社科纳克里12月31日电  中华人民共和国驻几内亚共和国大使馆临时代办赵源12月31日向几内亚总统兼外交部长塞古·杜尔呈递了代办介绍信。
    在呈递仪式上，赵源向几内亚政府和人民转达了中国政府和人民的最好祝愿。他希望，两国之间的友好关系将得到进一步的发展。
    塞古·杜尔总统对中国临时代办表示欢迎。他说，他相信几内亚和中国之间的友好关系将逐渐发展。
    几内亚总统办公厅秘书长让·法拉格和负责外交事务的国务秘书西斯·福德也出席了呈递仪式。
