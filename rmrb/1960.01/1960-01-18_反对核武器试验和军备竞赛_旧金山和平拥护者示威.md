Title: Protesting Nuclear Weapons Tests and the Arms Race: San Francisco Peace Advocates Demonstrate
Author: 
Date: 1960-01-18
Page: 6

    Opposing Nuclear Weapons Tests and the Arms Race
    San Francisco Peace Advocates Demonstrate
    Xinhua News Agency, San Francisco, January 17th: More than five hundred peace advocates held a demonstration in San Francisco on the 16th to oppose nuclear weapons tests and the arms race.
    At the head of the procession were baby carriages with children in them, adorned with peace slogans. Demonstrators carried signs for peace and against nuclear weapons testing, singing hymns as they marched through the business district. (Attached photo)
    Pictured is the scene of the San Francisco people's demonstration. Faxed photo (Xinhua News Agency)

### 反对核武器试验和军备竞赛  旧金山和平拥护者示威

1960-01-18
第6版()
专栏：

    反对核武器试验和军备竞赛
    旧金山和平拥护者示威
    据新华社17日讯　旧金山消息：五百多名和平拥护者16日在旧金山举行示威游行，反对核武器试验和军备竞赛。
    走在游行队伍前头的是贴着和平标语的坐有小孩的小推车。示威者高举着和平和反对核武器试验的标语，唱着赞美歌通过商业区。（附图片）
