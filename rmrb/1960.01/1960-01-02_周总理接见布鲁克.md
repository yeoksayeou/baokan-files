Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Brooke
Author:
Date: 1960-01-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 1st: Premier Zhou Enlai met with the former Rajah of Sarawak, Anthony Brooke, this afternoon. The host and guest had a friendly conversation. Present at the meeting were Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General, and Ni Yongye, Specialist of the First Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Mr. Brooke arrived in Beijing yesterday for a visit. At noon today, Hu Yuzhi hosted a banquet to welcome him.

### 周总理接见布鲁克

1960-01-02
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见布鲁克
    新华社1日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了前沙捞越亲王安东尼·布鲁克。宾主进行了友好的交谈。接见时在座的有中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之、副秘书长吴晓达，外交部第一亚洲司专员倪永业。
    布鲁克先生是昨天到达北京访问的，今天中午，胡愈之曾设宴欢迎。
