Title: Beijing Youth of All Circles Gather to Welcome Youth Returning from Indonesia
Author:
Date: 1960-01-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 14th. Over 300 young people from all walks of life in Beijing gathered at the Peace Hotel tonight to warmly welcome over 100 overseas Chinese youth who recently returned to the capital from Indonesia.

At the meeting, Wang Zhaohua, First Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, extended a warm welcome and cordial greetings to the overseas Chinese youth returning from Indonesia on behalf of the city's youth and members of the Communist Youth League. He said that our great socialist motherland cares for its children in every possible way, whether they are at home or abroad. Wang Zhaohua said that after returning to the country, you should listen to the words of the Party and Chairman Mao, study hard, train well, raise your awareness, and become red and expert youth of the Mao Zedong era, contributing your youth to the great motherland.

Chen Manyun, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Municipal Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese; Han Deguang, an advanced producer at the Beijing Electron Tube Factory and a returned overseas Chinese youth from Indonesia; and Lin Lifen, a graduate student from Beijing Agricultural University and a returned overseas Chinese youth from Indonesia, spoke at the meeting, detailing the great achievements of the motherland over the past ten years and their respective situations after returning to the country.

Guo Yue, a representative of returned overseas Chinese students, said at the meeting: We have felt warmth everywhere since we stepped on the land of our beloved motherland. We must actively study and work hard under the leadership of the Party and Chairman Mao, under the banner of the General Line, the Great Leap Forward, and the People's Communes, and contribute our strength to building socialism.

At the meeting, everyone joined in a get-together, watched wonderful performances by the Central Song and Dance Troupe and the China Acrobatic Troupe, and the film "Celebrating the Tenth Anniversary."

Today's gathering was jointly held by the Beijing Municipal Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese, the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, the Beijing Municipal Federation of Students, and the Beijing Municipal Youth Federation. Among those who attended the welcoming meeting were Zhang Chao, Vice Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation; Wang Yuting, Secretary-General of the All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese; Yan Boming, Deputy Director of the Beijing Municipal Education Bureau; Sun Fuling, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Municipal Youth Federation; and Wu Yixiu, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Municipal Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese.

### 北京各界青年集会  欢迎从印度尼西亚回国青年

1960-01-15
第4版()
专栏：

    北京各界青年集会
    欢迎从印度尼西亚回国青年
    新华社14日讯　北京市各界青年三百多人今晚在和平宾馆集会，热烈欢迎一百多名最近由印度尼西亚回到首都的华侨青年。
    会上，共青团北京市委第一书记王照华代表全市青年和共青团员，向由印度尼西亚回来的华侨青年表示热烈的欢迎和亲切的问候。他说，我们伟大的社会主义祖国，对自己的儿女的关怀是无微不至的，不论他们是在国内，还是在国外。王照华说，你们回国以后，要听党和毛主席的话，努力学习，好好锻炼，提高觉悟，做一个毛泽东时代的又红又专的青年，为伟大的祖国贡献出自己的青春。
    北京市归国华侨联合会副主席陈曼云，北京电子管厂先进生产者、印度尼西亚归侨青年韩德光，北京农业大学毕业学生、印度尼西亚归侨青年林黎奋先后在会上讲了话，他们详细地介绍了祖国十年来的伟大成就和他们各自回国以后的情况。
    归国华侨学生代表郭说悦在会上讲话说：自从我们踏上亲爱的祖国的土地以后，处处感到温暖。我们一定要在党和毛主席的领导下，在总路线、大跃进和人民公社的旗帜下，积极学习，努力工作，为建设社会主义贡献出自己的力量。
    会上，大家一起联欢，观看了中央歌舞团、中国杂技团表演的精彩节目和电影“欢庆十周年”。
    今天的集会是由北京市归国华侨联合会、共青团北京市委员会、北京市学生联合会、北京市青年联合会联合举行的。参加欢迎会的，还有中华全国青年联合会副主席张超、中华全国归国华侨联合会秘书长王雨亭、北京市教育局副局长阎伯铭、北京市青联副主席孙孚凌、北京市归国华侨联合会副主席吴益修等。
