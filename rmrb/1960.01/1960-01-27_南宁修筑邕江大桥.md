Title: Nanning Constructs Yongjiang Bridge
Author:
Date: 1960-01-27
Page: 2

Column: Construction Flowers Welcome Spring

    Nanning Constructs Yongjiang Bridge
    The Yongjiang Bridge, linking the Jiangnan and Jiangbei new industrial zones of Nanning, the capital of Guangxi, has recently commenced construction.
    This bridge is 366 meters long and is a modern bridge for both highway and urban use. After the bridge is completed, it will greatly accelerate the construction speed of basic construction projects distributed on both banks and the development of industrial production.

### 南宁修筑邕江大桥

1960-01-27
第2版()
专栏：建设鲜花迎春开

    南宁修筑邕江大桥
    联接广西首府南宁市江南、江北两个新工业区的邕江大桥已在最近开工。
    这座桥全长三百六十六米，是座现代化的公路和城市混合使用桥。大桥建成后，将大大加快分布在两岸的基本建设项目的施工速度和工业生产的发展。
