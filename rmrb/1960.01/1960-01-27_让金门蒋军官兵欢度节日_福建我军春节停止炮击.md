Title: Fujian PLA to Cease Bombardment During Spring Festival, Allowing Kinmen Chiang Troops to Celebrate

Author:
Date: 1960-01-27
Page: 4

    To Allow Kinmen Chiang Troops to Celebrate the Holiday
    Fujian PLA to Cease Bombardment During Spring Festival
    Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, January 26th: A spokesman for the Fujian Front Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army announced: January 28th is the traditional Spring Festival holiday for the Chinese people. In order to allow compatriots and Chiang troops on the islands of Greater Kinmen, Lesser Kinmen, Greater Tan, and Lesser Tan to quietly celebrate this holiday, the artillery units of the People's Liberation Army on the Fujian Front are ordered to cease bombardment on the single days of January 27th and 29th, 1960, as a gesture of concern.

### 让金门蒋军官兵欢度节日  福建我军春节停止炮击

1960-01-27
第4版()
专栏：

    让金门蒋军官兵欢度节日
    福建我军春节停止炮击
    新华社福建前线26日电　中国人民解放军福建前线司令部发言人宣称：1月28日是我国人民传统的春节佳日。为了使大金门、小金门、大担、二担等岛屿上的同胞和蒋军官兵，能够安静地欢度这个节日，福建前线人民解放军炮兵部队，奉命在1960年1月27、29两个单日停止炮击，以示关怀。
