Title: U.S. Warship Intrudes into Fujian Waters; I Issue 82nd Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-01-17
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 16th -- On January 16th, from 5:00 AM to 10:45 AM, a U.S. warship intruded into the sea area of Haitan and Dongyin regions of Fujian Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. warship, a spokesman for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 82nd serious warning.

### 美国军舰一艘侵入福建海域  我提出第82次严重警告

1960-01-17
第2版()
专栏：

    美国军舰一艘侵入福建海域
    我提出第82次严重警告
    新华社16日讯　1月16日五时至十时四十五分，美国军舰一艘侵入我福建省海坛、东引地区海域。对于美国军舰这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第八十二次严重警告。
