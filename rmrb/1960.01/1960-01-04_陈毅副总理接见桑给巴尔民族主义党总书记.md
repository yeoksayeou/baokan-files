Title: Chen Yi Receives the Secretary-General of the Zanzibar Nationalist Party
Author:
Date: 1960-01-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 3rd: Vice Premier Chen Yi received Abdul Rahman Mohamed, Secretary-General of the Zanzibar Nationalist Party, and his wife, who are currently visiting China, this afternoon. The guests and hosts held friendly talks.

Present at the meeting were Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, and Wang Mingyuan, Acting Secretary-General.

This evening, Burhan, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, hosted a banquet in honor of Abdul Rahman Mohamed and his wife. (Attached picture)

### 陈毅副总理接见桑给巴尔民族主义党总书记

1960-01-04
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见桑给巴尔民族主义党总书记
    新华社3日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见了正在我国访问的桑给巴尔民族主义党总书记阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼·穆罕默德和他的夫人。宾主进行了友好的交谈。
    接见时在座的有中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志、代秘书长王明远。
    今天晚间，中国亚非团结委员会副主席包尔汉设宴欢迎阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼·穆罕默德和他的夫人。（附图片）
