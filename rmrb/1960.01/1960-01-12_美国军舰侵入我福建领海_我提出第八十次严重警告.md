Title: U.S. Warship Intrudes into Fujian Territorial Waters; I Issue the Eightieth Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-01-12
Page: 4

    U.S. Warship Intrudes into Fujian Territorial Waters
    I Issue the Eightieth Serious Warning
  Xinhua News Agency, 11th. At 12:50 to 17:30 on January 11, a U.S. warship intruded into the sea area of ​​Baiquan and Haitan areas of Fujian Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. warship, a spokesman for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was ordered to issue the eightieth serious warning.

### 美国军舰侵入我福建领海  我提出第八十次严重警告

1960-01-12
第4版()
专栏：

    美国军舰侵入我福建领海
    我提出第八十次严重警告
  新华社11日讯　1月11日十二时五十分至十七时三十分，美国军舰一艘侵入我福建省白犬、海坛地区海域。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第八十次严重警告。
