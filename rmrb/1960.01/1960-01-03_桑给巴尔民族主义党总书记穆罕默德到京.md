Title: Zanzibar Nationalist Party Secretary-General Muhammad Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-01-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, January 1st: Abdul Rahman Muhammad, Secretary-General of the Zanzibar Nationalist Party, and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to our country at the invitation of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity.
    Wang Mingyuan, Acting Secretary-General of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, Liao Mengxing, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Women's Federation, and Qian Dawei, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Youth Federation, went to the airport to welcome them.

### 桑给巴尔民族主义党总书记穆罕默德到京

1960-01-03
第5版()
专栏：

    桑给巴尔民族主义党总书记穆罕默德到京
    新华社1日讯　应中国亚非团结委员会邀请，前来我国访问的桑给巴尔民族主义党总书记阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼·穆罕默德偕夫人，于今日乘飞机抵达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的，有中国亚非团结委员会代秘书长王明远、全国妇联国际联络部副部长廖梦醒、全国青联国际联络部副部长钱大卫等人。
