Title: Our Cultural Friendship Delegation Visits Burma
Author:
Date: 1960-01-23
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, 22nd. At the invitation of the Government of the Union of Burma, the Chinese Cultural Friendship Delegation, leading the Chinese National Song and Dance Troupe, left Beijing by plane this morning for Rangoon for a friendly visit. The head of the cultural delegation is Chu Tunan, and the deputy heads are Zhao Puchu and Lin Lin. The delegation will visit Burma for a month.

### 我文化友好代表团去缅甸访问

1960-01-23
第3版()
专栏：

    我文化友好代表团去缅甸访问
    新华社22日讯　应缅甸联邦政府邀请前往缅甸作友好访问的中国文化友好代表团，今天早晨率领中国民族歌舞团，乘飞机离开北京前往仰光。文化代表团团长是楚图南，副团长赵朴初、林林。代表团将在缅甸访问一个月。
