  　Rare News from Qinghai Animal Husbandry
    Ewe Gives Birth to Quadruplets
    Rouba (Tibetan), the deputy leader of the first production team of the Suode Brigade of the Jianghe People's Commune in Tianjun County, Qinghai Province, had a ewe in his flock that gave birth to four lambs at the end of last year.

### 青海牧业珍闻

1960-01-20

专栏：

  　青海牧业珍闻
    绵羊一胎四羔
    青海省天峻县江河人民公社索德大队第一生产队副队长柔巴（藏族）放牧的羊群中，有一只母绵羊在去年年底一胎产了四只羊羔。
