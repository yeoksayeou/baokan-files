Title: Plotting to Ship Atomic Weapons to Thailand: US Army Secretary Active in Thailand
Author:
Date: 1960-01-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, January 5th: According to Thai radio broadcasts, U.S. Army Secretary Bruck arrived in Bangkok by plane on the 4th to conduct activities.

According to the American "Newsweek" magazine published at the end of December last year, the United States is plotting to ship atomic weapons to Thailand. The news said that officials from the U.S. Department of the Army are currently drafting a plan to deploy an airborne infantry team equipped with atomic weapons in Thailand.

### 策划把原子武器运泰  美陆军部长到泰国活动

1960-01-07
第6版()
专栏：

    策划把原子武器运泰
    美陆军部长到泰国活动
    据新华社5日讯　曼谷消息：据泰国电台广播，美国陆军部长布鲁克在4日乘飞机到曼谷进行活动。
    据去年12月底出版的美国“新闻周刊”透露，美国正在策划把原子武器运入泰国。消息说，美国陆军部的官员目前正在拟订一项关于把配备有原子武器的空军步兵队派驻泰国的计划。
