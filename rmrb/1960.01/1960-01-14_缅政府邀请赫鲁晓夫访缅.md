Title: Burmese Government Invites Khrushchev to Visit Burma
Author: 
Date: 1960-01-14
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 12th - TASS, Moscow: The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, received the Burmese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, U Hla Maung, on the 12th and held talks with him.
    The Ambassador conveyed an invitation from the Burmese government to Khrushchev, inviting him to visit Burma when he visits Indonesia. Khrushchev expressed his heartfelt gratitude for this gracious invitation and expressed his hope to be able to visit.

### 缅政府邀请赫鲁晓夫访缅

1960-01-14
第6版()
专栏：

    缅政府邀请赫鲁晓夫访缅
    新华社莫斯科12日电　塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议赫鲁晓夫12日接见了缅甸驻苏联大使吴进，并和他谈了话。
    大使向赫鲁晓夫转达了缅甸政府的一项邀请，请他在访问印度尼西亚的时候也访问缅甸。赫鲁晓夫衷心感谢这一盛情的邀请，并表示希望能够去访问。
