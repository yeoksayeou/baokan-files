Title: Hunan Discovers Large Salt Mine
Author:
Date: 1960-01-16
Page: 2

　　Hunan Discovers Large Salt Mine
　　(Our report) A survey team from the Salt Industry Bureau of the Ministry of Light Industry recently discovered a large ancient salt mine in the Chashan'ao area of ​​Hengyang Prefecture, Hunan Province.
　　According to existing data, the thickness of this deposit is more than 200 meters, of which other rocks account for about 50% to 60%. Preliminary estimates suggest that the cumulative thickness of the salt layer is over 100 meters. This discovery provides a new raw material base for the salt-making industry in the interior of our country and also solves the salt problem in Hunan, a "salt-deficient province."

### 湖南发现大盐矿

1960-01-16
第2版()
专栏：

    湖南发现大盐矿
    本报讯　轻工业部制盐工业局勘察队，最近在湖南省衡阳专区茶山坳一带，发现了巨大的古盐矿。
    根据现有资料看来，这一矿床的厚度在二百米以上，其中夹有其他岩石约50％至60％。据初步估计，盐矿层累计厚度约在一百米以上。这一发现，为我国内地制盐工业提供了新的原料基地，也解决了湖南“无盐省”的用盐问题。
