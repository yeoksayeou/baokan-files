Title: Qinghai Lake Brand Projector
Author:
Date: 1960-01-08
Page: 2

Qinghai Lake Brand Projector

    Following the successful trial production of a 16mm film projector in June of last year, the Qinghai Provincial Film Machinery Factory has recently produced the "Qinghai Lake" brand 303 film projector.
    The Qinghai Provincial Film Machinery Factory will produce this film projector in batches this year to meet the needs of the rapid development of the film industry in Qinghai Province.
    (All news in this column is according to Xinhua News Agency dispatches.)

### 青海湖牌放映机

1960-01-08
第2版()
专栏：

    青海湖牌放映机
    青海省电影机械厂继去年六月试制成功十六毫米的电影放映机以后，最近又制成“青海湖”牌三○三电影放映机。
    青海省电影机械厂今年将成批生产这种电影放映机，以适应青海省电影事业迅速发展的需要。
    （本栏新闻全系据新华社讯）
