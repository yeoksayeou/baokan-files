Title: German Government Delegation Departs to Visit China, Mongolia, North Korea, and Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1960-01-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, January 5th: A government delegation from the German Democratic Republic, headed by Deputy Prime Minister and Minister for Foreign and Intra-German Trade, Heinrich Rau, left here this morning for a visit to Asia. The delegation will visit China, Mongolia, the Democratic People's Republic of North Korea, and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
    The delegation includes Werner, an alternate member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, and Wenzel, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, among others.
    The Chinese, North Korean, and Vietnamese ambassadors and the Mongolian chargé d'affaires to the German Democratic Republic jointly held a farewell reception for the delegation last night.

### 前来访问中蒙朝越四国  德政府代表团启程

1960-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

    前来访问中蒙朝越四国
    德政府代表团启程
    新华社柏林5日电　以德意志民主共和国副总理、对外和东西德贸易部长海因里希·劳为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团，今天上午离开这里前往亚洲访问。代表团将访问中国、蒙古、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和越南民主共和国。
    代表团的成员有德国统一社会党中央委员会政治局候补委员维尔纳，外交部第一副部长文策尔等人。
  驻德意志民主共和国的中国、朝鲜和越南的大使以及蒙古代办昨晚曾为代表团联合举行了欢送会。
