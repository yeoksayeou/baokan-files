Title: Let's Go, Let's Announce the Good News!
Author: Luo Jinxin
Date: 1960-01-09
Page: 2

Letter to the Editor:

    Let's Go, Let's Announce the Good News! "Old Zhang, Old Li, let's go, everyone go announce the good news!" "No, team leader, how many times do we have to run every day?" "I say, why not just install the loudspeaker at the Party Committee!" "Right! Save running by pulling a wire, and let radio waves help our voices... ."
    Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant Luo Jinxin
