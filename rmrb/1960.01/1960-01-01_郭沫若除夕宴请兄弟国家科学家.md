Title: Guo Moruo Hosts New Year's Eve Banquet for Scientists from Fraternal Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-01-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 31st. Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, held a banquet this evening to entertain scientists from the Soviet Union and other fraternal countries who are currently visiting and lecturing in our country, to celebrate the New Year's Eve and welcome 1960 together.

　　In his speech, Guo Moruo warmly congratulated the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union for the comprehensive development of its construction endeavors in 1959, and congratulated the further strengthening of cooperation in scientific work among socialist countries.

　　Among those attending the banquet were Li Siguang, Zhang Jingfu, Tao Menghe, Zhu Kezhen, Wu Youxun, vice presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, as well as the heads of various departments and related research institutes of the Academy of Sciences.

### 郭沫若除夕宴请兄弟国家科学家

1960-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若除夕宴请兄弟国家科学家
    新华社31日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若今天晚间举行宴会，招待正在我国访问、讲学的苏联和其他兄弟国家的科学家，共同欢度除夕和迎接1960年。
    郭沫若在致词中热烈祝贺以苏联为首的社会主义阵营各国的建设事业在1959年获得了全面发展，并且祝贺社会主义各国在科学工作方面的协作进一步加强。
    出席宴会作陪的，有中国科学院副院长李四光、张劲夫、陶孟和、竺可桢、吴有训，以及科学院所属各学部和各有关研究所的负责人等。
