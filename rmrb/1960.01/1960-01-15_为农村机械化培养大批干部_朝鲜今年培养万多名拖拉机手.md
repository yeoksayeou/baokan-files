Title: Training a Large Number of Cadres for Rural Mechanization: North Korea to Train Over 10,000 Tractor Drivers This Year
Author:
Date: 1960-01-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 13th - According to the Korean Central News Agency, North Korea will train over 10,000 tractor drivers this year in various parts of the north.

Since last autumn, approximately 5,400 rural youths have received training as tractor drivers, mechanics, and other technical personnel.

In the northern part of the republic, there are now more than eighty agricultural machinery stations, employing over 9,000 workers.

Responding to the call of the Korean Workers' Party, the agricultural machinery stations, as the center of this country's technological revolution, are providing guidance and assistance to the work of agricultural cooperatives. At the tractor driver training centers, courses are offered on the mechanical performance of tractors and agricultural machinery.

Within a year, 80% to 85% of agricultural cultivation in northern Korea will be mechanized.

### 为农村机械化培养大批干部  朝鲜今年培养万多名拖拉机手

1960-01-15
第6版()
专栏：

    为农村机械化培养大批干部
    朝鲜今年培养万多名拖拉机手
    新华社平壤13日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社消息：朝鲜北部各地今年将训练一万多名拖拉机驾驶员。
    从去年秋天以来，大约五千四百名农村青年接受了担任拖拉机驾驶员、机械员和其他技术人员的训练。
    在共和国北部，现在有八十多个农业机器站，这些农业机器站有九千多名工人。
    为了响应朝鲜劳动党的号召，作为这个国家技术革命的中心的农业机器站正在对农业合作社的工作给予指导和帮助。在拖拉机驾驶员训练中心，开设着关于拖拉机和农业机器机械性能的课程。
    在一年内，朝鲜北部的农业耕作将有80％到85％用机器来进行。
