Title: Serve Production Better
Author: Xin Dingguo
Date: 1960-01-09
Page: 8

    Serve Production Better

    I am a purchasing agent, a vanguard on the commercial front. The goods I am responsible for purchasing are instruments and accessories for machines, boilers, ships, and water supply equipment, indispensable commodities for the construction of our motherland. Therefore, in the new year, I am determined to exert even greater efforts, adhere to putting politics in command, strengthen the production perspective, closely collaborate with industry and commerce, familiarize myself with industrial production conditions, and support industry's "good start, complete success, and sustained success."
    Purchasing Agent, Shanghai Hardware and Machinery Purchasing and Supply Station, Xin Dingguo

### 更好地为生产服务
忻定国
1960-01-09
第8版()
专栏：新春述志

    更好地为生产服务
    我是一个采购员，是商业战线上的尖兵。我负责采购的商品，都是机器、锅炉、轮船和自来水设备上的仪表配件，是祖国建设事业中不可缺少的商品。因此，在新的一年，我决心鼓足更大的干劲，坚持政治挂帅，加强生产观点，密切工商协作，熟悉工业生产情况，支持工业生产“开门红、满堂红、红到底”。
    上海五金机械采购供应站采购员　忻定国
