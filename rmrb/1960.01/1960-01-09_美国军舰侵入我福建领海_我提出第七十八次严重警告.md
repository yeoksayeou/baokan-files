Title: U.S. Warship Intrudes into China's Fujian Territorial Waters; I Issue the 78th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-01-09
Page: 4

    U.S. Warship Intrudes into China's Fujian Territorial Waters
    I Issue the 78th Serious Warning
    Xinhua News Agency, 8th January: On January 8th, from 02:32 to 08:07, a U.S. warship intruded into the sea area of ​​Baiquan and Haitan areas of Fujian Province, China. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. warship, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was ordered to issue the 78th serious warning.

### 美国军舰侵入我福建领海  我提出第七十八次严重警告

1960-01-09
第4版()
专栏：

    美国军舰侵入我福建领海
    我提出第七十八次严重警告
    新华社8日讯　1月8日二时三十二分至八时零七分，美国军舰一艘侵入我国福建省白犬、海坛地区海域。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第七十八次严重警告。
