Title: Mongolian and German Democratic Republic Government Delegations Hold Talks; Mongolian Party and Government Leaders Host Banquet for German Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-01-09
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 7th - Mongolian and German Democratic Republic government delegations held talks in Ulaanbaatar today.
    The Mongolian side participating in the talks included Molomjamts, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Tsend, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, Luvsan, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Shagdarjav, Minister of Foreign Affairs, Delegjav, Minister of Foreign Trade, and others.
    The German Democratic Republic side participating in the talks included Heinrich Rau, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign and Intra-German Trade, Werner, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Wenzel, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and others.
    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 8th - The government delegations of the Mongolian People's Republic and the German Democratic Republic signed a protocol here today, in which the German Democratic Republic will assist Mongolia in building a meat processing complex in Ulaanbaatar.
    The factory will be built with material and technical assistance from the German Democratic Republic between 1961 and 1963.
    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 8th - The German Democratic Republic government delegation, headed by Deputy Prime Minister Heinrich Rau, received a warm welcome from the Mongolian Party, government, and people during their visit to Mongolia. On the evening of the 7th, the Mongolian Council of Ministers held a grand banquet to welcome the German Democratic Republic government delegation. Mongolian Party and government leaders Tsedenbal, Sambuu, Tsend, Molomjamts, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Mongolia attended the banquet. Tsedenbal and Heinrich Rau spoke at the banquet.
    On the 8th, the delegation visited the Mongolian State Printing Plant. The printing plant's employees held a grand welcome rally. On the evening of the 8th, the German Democratic Republic Ambassador to Mongolia, Dick, hosted a reception for the delegation's visit to Mongolia.
    After concluding their visit to Mongolia, the delegation will visit China on the 9th.

### 蒙德两国政府代表团举行会谈  蒙古党政领导人欢宴德国贵宾

1960-01-09
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙德两国政府代表团举行会谈
    蒙古党政领导人欢宴德国贵宾
    新华社乌兰巴托7日电　蒙古和德意志民主共和国两国政府代表团今天在乌兰巴托举行会谈。
    蒙古方面参加会谈的有部长会议第一副主席莫洛姆扎木茨、蒙古人民革命党中央政治局委员曾德、部长会议副主席鲁布桑、外交部长沙格达尔苏伦、对外贸易部长德勒格尔扎布等人。
    德意志民主共和国方面参加会谈的有副总理、对外和东西德贸易部长海因里希·劳，德国统一社会党中央政治局候补委员维尔纳、外交部第一副部长文策尔等人。
    新华社乌兰巴托8日电　蒙古人民共和国政府代表团和德意志民主共和国政府代表团今天下午在这里签订了德意志民主共和国帮助蒙古在乌兰巴托建设一座肉类联合工厂的议定书。
    这座工厂将由德意志民主共和国给予物质技术帮助，在1961—1963年内建成。
    据新华社乌兰巴托8日电　以副总理海因里希·劳为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团在蒙古访问期间，受到了蒙古党、政府和人民的热烈欢迎。7日晚，蒙古部长会议举行了盛大宴会，欢迎德意志民主共和国政府代表团。蒙古党政领导人泽登巴尔、桑布、曾德、莫洛姆扎木茨，以及各国驻蒙古的外交使节出席了宴会。泽登巴尔和海因里希·劳在宴会上讲了话。
    8日，代表团参观了蒙古国家印刷厂。印刷厂的职工举行了盛大欢迎集会。8日晚，德意志民主共和国驻蒙古大使迪克为代表团访问蒙古举行了招待会。
    代表团结束在蒙古的访问后将于9日到中国访问。
