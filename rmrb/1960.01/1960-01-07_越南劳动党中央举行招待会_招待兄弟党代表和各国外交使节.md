Title: Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party Holds Reception for Representatives of Fraternal Parties and Foreign Diplomatic Envoys
Author:
Date: 1960-01-07
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 6th. According to Vietnam News Agency: The Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party held a reception on the 5th to celebrate the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Vietnamese Labor Party. Representatives of communist and workers' parties from various countries and diplomatic envoys of various countries stationed in Hanoi were invited.
    Also present at the reception were the heads and deputy heads of the expert groups from various countries who are currently assisting Vietnam in its construction.
    The hosts included Pham Van Dong, Nguyen Duy Trinh, and Pham Hung, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party.
    Pham Van Dong delivered a speech on behalf of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party to the guests.
    He expressed his respect for the rapid progress made by the socialist camp in building socialism and communism.

### 越南劳动党中央举行招待会  招待兄弟党代表和各国外交使节

1960-01-07
第5版()
专栏：

    越南劳动党中央举行招待会
    招待兄弟党代表和各国外交使节
    据新华社河内6日电　据越南通讯社讯：越南劳动党中央委员会为庆祝越南劳动党建党三十周年，5日举行招待会，招待各国共产党、工人党的代表和各国驻河内的外交使节。
    出席招待会的还有目前正在帮助越南进行建设的各国专家团的团长和副团长。
    主人中有越南劳动党中央委员会政治局委员范文同、阮维桢和范雄。
    范文同代表越南劳动党中央委员会向来宾致辞。
    他对社会主义阵营在建设社会主义和共产主义的道路上突飞猛进表示敬意。
