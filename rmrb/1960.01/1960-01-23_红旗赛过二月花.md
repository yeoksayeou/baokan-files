Title: Red Flag Surpasses February Flowers
Author: Zhou Yelu
Date: 1960-01-23
Page: 8

    Red Flag Surpasses February Flowers
    Before the spring thunder, the spring is already strong,
    The sound of the Great Leap Forward echoes across the mountains and plains,
    The red flag surpasses February flowers,
    Vowing to fight "winter leisure" and reverse the universe.
    The sky is three feet higher, the earth changes its appearance,
    Mountains tamed, water subdued, listening to commands,
    Barren mountains and wild slopes transformed into orchards;
    Spring flowers must exceed "thousand, five, ten thousand"!
          Zhejiang Shaoxing Jidong Commune Zhou Yelu

### 红旗赛过二月花
周也鲁
1960-01-23
第8版()
专栏：

    红旗赛过二月花
    春雷未响春意浓，
    漫山遍野跃进声，
    红旗赛过二月花，
    誓战“冬闲”扭乾坤。
    天高三尺地改貌，
    山驯水服听使唤，
    荒山野坡变果园；
    春花要超“千、五、万”！
          浙江绍兴稽东公社　周也鲁
