Title: The Soviet Union Helps Afghanistan Build a Highway
Author:
Date: 1960-01-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, 11th. TASS reported: Afghanistan and the Soviet Union, based on existing agreements between the two countries, signed a contract in Kabul on the 11th regarding Soviet technical assistance to Afghanistan in the construction of the Kushk-Gawhat-Kandahar highway. This highway is seven hundred and fifty kilometers long.

### 苏联帮助阿富汗修筑公路

1960-01-15
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联帮助阿富汗修筑公路
    新华社讯　塔斯社喀布尔11日讯：阿富汗和苏联根据两国现有的协定，11日在喀布尔签订了一项关于苏联在技术上帮助阿富汗修筑库什克—盖瓦特—堪达加尔公路的合同。这条公路长达七百五十公里。
