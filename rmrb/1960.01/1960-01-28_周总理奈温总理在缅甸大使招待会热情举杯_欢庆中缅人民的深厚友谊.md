Title: Premier Zhou and Prime Minister Ne Win Enthusiastically Toast at Burmese Ambassador's Reception, Celebrating the Deep Friendship Between the Chinese and Burmese People

Author: 
Date: 1960-01-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 27th: Our reporter reports: On the evening of the Chinese people's traditional festival – Lunar New Year's Eve, the Burmese Ambassador to China, U Kyaw Winn, held a grand reception for Prime Minister Ne Win's visit to China. At the reception, Prime Minister Ne Win and Premier Zhou Enlai enthusiastically wished that the friendship between China and Burma would be increasingly consolidated and continuously developed.

The reception was held in the spacious banquet hall of the Beijing Hotel. The hall was decorated with lanterns and colorful hangings, and more than 500 Chinese and foreign guests joyfully celebrated the deep friendship between the great Chinese and Burmese people.

Premier Zhou Enlai raised his glass at the reception and said in his toast that he was very happy to be able to spend Lunar New Year's Eve with the respected Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, General Ne Win, and other distinguished Burmese guests, just as the Chinese people were joyfully celebrating the Spring Festival. Premier Zhou Enlai proposed a toast: to the increasingly consolidated and developing "Pauk-Phaw"-like kinship friendship between the great Chinese and Burmese people, to the unity of Asian and African countries, to the friendship of people all over the world, to the good health of Prime Minister Ne Win, to the good health of other distinguished Burmese guests, to the good health of the host of today's reception, Ambassador U Kyaw Winn and his wife, to the good health of all friends at the Burmese Embassy, and to everyone's Happy Spring Festival! Warm applause resounded through the hall.

Prime Minister Ne Win then stood up to deliver a speech. He said that when they arrived in China, the Chinese people were celebrating their traditional festival, which was an auspicious time. They felt very honored to visit China on such a day. Prime Minister Ne Win asked everyone to join him in a toast to the Chinese people's physical and mental well-being and good health during the Spring Festival, to the good health of Premier Zhou Enlai, Marshal Chen Yi, and other Chinese leaders, to the further consolidation and development of the friendship between China and Burma on the existing foundation, and to world peace. At this moment, thunderous applause echoed through the banquet hall for a long time.

Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi improvised a poem to praise the friendship between China and Burma. The verses are:

　　"The beautiful Spring Festival arrives, welcoming distinguished guests.
　　Hosts and guests are delighted, sharing the wine in our palms."

He then proposed a toast to the friendship between the Chinese and Burmese people, to world peace, and to the good health of Prime Minister Ne Win, Foreign Minister U Chit Thoung, and other distinguished Burmese guests. At this moment, the banquet hall was filled with laughter and applause.

The Burmese Foreign Minister U Chit Thoung also delivered an enthusiastic speech, saying that the Chinese New Year is an auspicious day. He felt very happy to be a guest in China on this auspicious day. He proposed a toast to the health, happiness, and smooth work of Premier Zhou Enlai, Foreign Minister Chen Yi and his wife, the Vice Ministers of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs and their wives, and to the friendship between Burma and China.

Ambassador U Kyaw Winn delivered a speech at the beginning of the reception, proposing a toast to the strengthening of friendship between Burma and China, and to world peace.

Prime Minister Ne Win's entourage: Attorney General of the Union of Burma, U Ba Swe, Deputy Chief of Staff, Brigadier Aung Gyi, Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, U Ba Lwin, Prime Minister's Secretary, U Win Pe, Joint Secretary of the Border Areas Administration, U Khin Nyo, Director of the Survey Department, U Hla Kin Maung, Chief of Military Intelligence, Lieutenant Colonel Lun, Deputy Director of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, U Tun Tin, etc., all attended the reception.

Also present at tonight's reception were Vice Premier of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Huang Yanpei and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Bao Erhan, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Fu Zuoyi, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Geng Biao, Chinese Ambassador to Burma Li Yimang, President of the China-Burma Friendship Association Zhang Youyu, and people from all walks of life in China. ### 周总理奈温总理在缅甸大使招待会热情举杯  欢庆中缅人民的深厚友谊

1960-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理奈温总理在缅甸大使招待会热情举杯
    欢庆中缅人民的深厚友谊
    新华社27日讯　本社记者报道：在中国人民传统的节日——春节除夕的晚上，缅甸驻中国大使叫温为奈温总理访问中国举行了盛大招待会。在会上，奈温总理和周恩来总理热情地预祝中缅两国的友谊日益巩固，不断发展。
    招待会在北京饭店宽敞的宴会厅举行，大厅里张灯结彩，五百多名中外人士喜气洋洋地欢庆伟大的中缅两国人民的深厚友谊。
    周恩来总理在招待会上举杯致词说，正当中国人民兴高彩烈欢度春节的时候，我们今天能够同我们尊敬的缅甸联邦总理奈温将军阁下和缅甸的其他贵宾们一起度过除夕，感到非常高兴。周恩来总理提议，为伟大的中缅两国人民“胞波”般的亲戚友谊日益巩固和发展，为亚非各国的团结，为世界各国人民的友好，为世界和平，为奈温总理阁下身体健康，为缅甸的其他贵宾身体健康，为今天招待会的主人叫温大使和他的夫人身体健康，为缅甸大使馆的所有朋友身体健康，为大家春节好，干杯。大厅里响起了热烈的掌声。
    奈温总理紧接着起立致词。他说，我们来到中国的时候，中国人民正在欢度自己的传统节日，这是一个良好的时辰，在这样的日子里在中国访问，我们感到非常荣幸。奈温总理请大家同他一起，为中国人民春节身心愉快健康，为周恩来总理、陈毅元帅和中国其他领导人身体健康，为中缅两国友谊在已有的基础上进一步巩固和发展，为世界和平，干杯。这时候，春雷般的掌声又长久地震荡着宴会大厅。
    陈毅副总理兼外交部长即席赋诗歌颂中缅友谊。诗句是：
    “阳春佳节至，欢迎贵宾来。
    主客皆大喜，共尽掌中杯。”
    他接着提议，为中缅两国人民的友谊，为世界和平，为奈温总理、吴千吞昂外交部长和缅甸其他贵宾们的身体健康，干杯。这时候，宴会厅里一片欢笑和掌声。
    缅甸联邦外交部长吴千吞昂也热情地致词，他说，中国人民的新春佳节是吉利的日子。在这个吉利的日子里在中国作客，感到非常高兴。他提议为周恩来总理、陈毅外交部长和他的夫人、中国外交部副部长和他们的夫人的健康、幸福和工作顺利，为缅中两国的友谊，干杯。
    叫温大使在招待会开始的时候致词，他提议为缅甸和中国友谊的加强，为世界和平，干杯。
    奈温总理的随行人员：缅甸联邦检察总长吴巴盛，副总参谋长昂季准将，外交部常任秘书巴林顿，总理秘书吴温佩，边境地区管理局联合秘书吴钦纽，测绘局长吴拉钦貌，军事情报处长伦中校，外交部副司长吴吞丁等，都出席了招待会。
    出席今晚招待会的，还有国务院副总理习仲勋，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长黄炎培、陈叔通，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，国防委员会副主席傅作义，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，外交部副部长章汉夫、耿飚，我国驻缅甸大使李一氓，中缅友好协会会长张友渔等和中国各方面的人士。（附图片）
