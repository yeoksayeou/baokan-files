International Briefs

    Pakistani Authorities Arrest a Group of People
    Karachi Dispatch: According to newspaper reports, on December 29, 1959, three individuals, including two former leaders of the Pakistan National Awami Party, were arrested by authorities in Sargodha, West Pakistan, under the "Martial Law Ordinance." They were accused of "inciting the people against the military." Three journalists were also arrested in connection with this case.
    Argentine Workers Held a Strike on New Year's Eve
    Buenos Aires Dispatch: Forty-five thousand transport workers, forty-five thousand postal workers, and two hundred and fifty thousand commercial employees in the Argentine capital held strikes on December 30th and 31st, 1959, demanding improved living conditions.

### 国际简讯

     国际简讯
     巴当局逮捕一批人
    卡拉奇讯　据报纸报道，1959年12月29日，包括两位前巴基斯坦民族人民党领袖在内的三位人士在西巴基斯坦的萨戈达被当局援引“戒严法条例”逮捕。他们被控“煽动人民反对军队”。因这一案件而被捕的还有三位新闻工作者。
  阿根廷工人在除夕举行罢工
    布宜诺斯艾利斯讯　阿根廷首都的四万五千名运输业工人和四万五千名邮政工人、二十五万商业雇员于1959年12月30日、31日举行了要求改善生活条件的罢工。
