Title: Indian President Prasad Hosts a Banquet for Voroshilov
Author:
Date: 1960-01-23
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, January 21st. Indian President Prasad hosted a banquet on the 20th to entertain Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.

In his speech at the banquet, Prasad pointed out that the friendship between the people of the Soviet Union and India is constantly developing and strengthening. He expressed his gratitude for the economic and technical assistance provided by the Soviet Union to India.

In his reply, Voroshilov said that the visit of the Soviet delegation to India at the invitation of the Indian government proves that both countries have the desire to further develop friendly relations and cooperation. The ties between the two countries in the fields of economy, science, technology, and culture are strengthening.

On the 21st, Voroshilov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union Kozlov, and representative of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Furtseva, accompanied by President Prasad and Nehru, visited the World Agricultural Exposition. After the visit, they arrived at a mass rally held at the open-air theater of the exhibition hall.

In his speech at the meeting, Voroshilov said that the Soviet Union is very happy to contribute its experience and achievements to India to develop India's industry and agriculture. He also praised the new achievements made by the Indian people and the government in the development of the entire national economy.

In his speech, Prasad praised that Soviet agriculture has reached a very high level of development.

On the 20th, Nehru held talks with Voroshilov, Kozlov, Furtseva and First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union Kuznetsov.

### 印度总统普拉沙德  设宴招待伏罗希洛夫

1960-01-23
第6版()
专栏：

    印度总统普拉沙德
    设宴招待伏罗希洛夫
    据新华社德里21日电　印度总统普拉沙德20日设宴招待苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫。
    普拉沙德在宴会上致词时指出，苏印两国人民的友谊在不断发展和加强。他对苏联给印度的经济和技术援助表示感谢。
    伏罗希洛夫在答词中说，苏联代表团应印度政府邀请访问印度，证明两国都有进一步发展友好关系和合作的愿望。两国在经济、科学、技术、文化方面的联系正在加强。
    伏罗希洛夫、苏联部长会议第一副主席科兹洛夫、苏联最高苏维埃代表福尔采娃21日在普拉沙德总统和尼赫鲁陪同下参观了世界农业博览会。参观后他们来到了在展览馆的露天剧场上举行的群众大会。
    伏罗希洛夫在大会上致词说，苏联很高兴把它的经验和成就贡献给印度，以发展印度的工农业。他还赞扬印度人民和政府在整个民族经济发展方面获得的新成就。
    普拉沙德在讲话中赞扬苏联农业达到了很高很高的发展水平。
    20日尼赫鲁同伏罗希洛夫、科兹洛夫、福尔采娃和苏联外交部第一副部长库兹涅佐夫进行了会谈。
