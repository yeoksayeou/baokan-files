Title: Hydropower Station Built in Eastern Tibet
Author:
Date: 1960-01-05
Page: 2
新年新建设

    Hydropower Station Built in Eastern Tibet
    Changdu, the central city of the eastern Tibetan region, recently started construction on a hydropower station with a generating capacity of 1,000 kilowatts. This hydropower station is being built on the Omo Chu River near Changdu and is the second larger power station currently under construction in the Tibetan region.

### 西藏东部建水电站

1960-01-05
第2版()
专栏：新年新建设

    西藏东部建水电站
    西藏东部地区中心城市昌都，最近动工兴建一座发电能力一千瓩的水电站。这座水电站修建在昌都附近的鄂穆楚河上，是西藏地区目前建设的第二个较大的电站。
