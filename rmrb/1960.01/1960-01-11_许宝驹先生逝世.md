Title: Mr. Xu Baoju Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1960-01-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 10th: Mr. Xu Baoju, Deputy to the National People's Congress, Member of the Bills Committee of the National People's Congress, and Standing Committee Member of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, passed away in Beijing at 4:18 PM on January 10, 1960, due to illness. He was 61 years old.

The Funeral Committee for Mr. Xu Baoju was established on the 10th. A public memorial service will be held at Jiaxing Temple at 10:00 AM on January 13th.

    Obituary
Mr. Xu Baoju, Deputy to the National People's Congress, Member of the Bills Committee of the National People's Congress, and Standing Committee Member of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, passed away in Beijing at 4:18 PM on January 10, 1960, due to illness. A public memorial service will be held at Jiaxing Temple at 10:00 AM on January 13th. This is a formal announcement.

### 许宝驹先生逝世

1960-01-11
第4版()
专栏：

    许宝驹先生逝世
    新华社10日讯　全国人民代表大会代表、全国人民代表大会法案委员会委员、中国国民党革命委员会中央常务委员许宝驹先生，因病于1960年1月10日下午四时十八分在北京逝世。享年六十一岁。
    许宝驹先生治丧委员会已于10日成立。定于1月13日上午十时在嘉兴寺举行公祭。
     讣告
    全国人民代表大会代表，全国人民代表大会法案委员会委员、中国国民党革命委员会中央常务委员许宝驹先生，因病于1960年1月10日下午四时十八分在北京逝世。定于1月13日上午十时在嘉兴寺公祭。特此讣告。　　　　　　　　　　　许宝驹先生治丧委员会
