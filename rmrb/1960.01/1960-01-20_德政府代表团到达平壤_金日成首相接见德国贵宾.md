Title: GDR Government Delegation Arrives in Pyongyang; Premier Kim Il-sung Receives German Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-01-20
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 19th. The government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of External and Intra-German Trade, Heinrich Rau, arrived in Pyongyang by plane at noon today for a visit to Korea at the invitation of the Korean government.

    Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included: Kim Il, First Vice Premier of the Cabinet of Korea; Vice Premier Li Joo-yeon; Li Hyo-soon and Rim Hae, members of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea; and Foreign Minister Park Sung-chul. Diplomatic envoys of various countries stationed in Korea also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

    Kim Il spoke at the airport, extending a warm welcome to the delegation. He said that the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the German Democratic Republic are both members of the socialist family headed by the Soviet Union and are fighting against imperialist aggression. The German Democratic Republic has provided great assistance to Korea's post-war economic construction. He said that the visit of the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic would contribute to the further development of the friendship between the peoples of Korea and Germany.

    Heinrich Rau said in his speech that the government and people of the German Democratic Republic support the proposals of the Supreme People's Assembly and the government of Korea regarding the peaceful reunification of Korea, and advocate the withdrawal of the United States from South Korea. He also expressed his gratitude for the Korean government's support for the German Democratic Republic government's proposal for the peaceful reunification of Germany.

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 19th. Premier Kim Il-sung of the Cabinet of Korea and Chairman Choi Yong-kun of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea received the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Deputy Prime Minister Heinrich Rau, which is visiting Korea, this afternoon.

    Present at the reception were: Kim Il, Li Hyo-soon, Rim Hae, Li Joo-yeon, Park Sung-chul and other leaders of the Korean Workers' Party and government.

    This afternoon, the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic laid wreaths at the Memorial Tower of the Martyrs of the Korean People's Army, the Liberation Tower commemorating the liberation of Korea by the Soviet Army, and the Korean-Chinese Friendship Tower commemorating the achievements of the Chinese People's Volunteers.

### 德政府代表团到达平壤  金日成首相接见德国贵宾

1960-01-20
第6版()
专栏：

    德政府代表团到达平壤
    金日成首相接见德国贵宾
    新华社平壤19日电　德意志民主共和国副总理兼对外与东西德贸易部部长海因里希·劳率领的德意志民主共和国政府代表团，应邀到朝鲜访问，在今天中午乘飞机到达平壤。
    到机场欢迎的有：朝鲜内阁第一副首相金一，副首相李周渊，朝鲜劳动党中央常务委员李孝淳和林海，外务相朴成哲等。各国驻朝鲜外交使节也到机场欢迎代表团。
    金一在机场上讲话，向代表团表示热烈欢迎。他说，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和德意志民主共和国都是以苏联为首的社会主义大家庭的成员，都为抗击帝国主义的侵略进行斗争。德意志民主共和国对朝鲜战后经济建设给予莫大的援助。他说，这次德意志民主共和国政府代表团的访问，将为进一步发展朝德两国人民的友谊作出贡献。
    海因里希·劳在讲话时表示：德意志民主共和国政府和人民支持朝鲜最高人民会议和政府的关于和平统一朝鲜的建议，并且主张美国撤出南朝鲜。他还对朝鲜政府支持德意志民主共和国政府的和平统一德国的建议表示感谢。
    新华社平壤19日电　朝鲜内阁首相金日成和朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健今天下午接见了来朝鲜访问的以海因里希·劳副总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团。
    接见时在座的有：金一、李孝淳、林海、李周渊、朴成哲等朝鲜劳动党和政府的领导人。
    今天下午，德意志民主共和国政府代表团向朝鲜人民军烈士纪念塔、纪念苏军解放朝鲜的解放塔、纪念中国人民志愿军的功勋的朝中友谊塔献了花圈。
