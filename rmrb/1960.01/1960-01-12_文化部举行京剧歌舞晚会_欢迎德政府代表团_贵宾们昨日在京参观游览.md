Title: Ministry of Culture Holds Peking Opera and Dance Soiree to Welcome German Government Delegation; Distinguished Guests Visited and toured Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1960-01-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 11th:  Deputy Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Heinrich Rau, his wife, and all the distinguished guests of the German Democratic Republic government delegation, enjoyed a Peking Opera and dance performance by artists from the capital at the Tianqiao Theater this evening.

Accompanying the distinguished guests were Vice Premier of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, and others.

Tonight's soiree was held by the Ministry of Culture to warmly welcome the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic.  At the soiree, performers from the China Peking Opera Theater and the Central Song and Dance Troupe performed Peking Opera and dance programs such as "Peacock Dance," "Broken Bridge," "Monkey King," and "Rainbow Bridge Presents Pearls."

After the performance, Deputy Prime Minister Heinrich Rau and delegation members Paul Verner, Otto Winzer, and Paul Wandel, accompanied by Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun, went on stage to shake hands warmly with the performers and present them with flower baskets.

Xinhua News Agency, January 11th: Deputy Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Heinrich Rau, his wife, and the distinguished guests of the German Democratic Republic government delegation, today visited the National Industrial and Transportation Exhibition, and toured the Palace Museum and the Summer Palace. Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun accompanied the distinguished guests on a tour of the Summer Palace and had lunch with them at the Tingli Hall.

Today, members of the German Democratic Republic government delegation: Paul Verner, alternate member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, and Otto Winzer, member of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, gave reports on German issues at the invitation of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Institute of International Relations, respectively. Their reports were warmly welcomed.

### 文化部举行京剧歌舞晚会  欢迎德政府代表团  贵宾们昨日在京参观游览

1960-01-12
第1版()
专栏：

    文化部举行京剧歌舞晚会
    欢迎德政府代表团
    贵宾们昨日在京参观游览
    新华社11日讯　德意志民主共和国副总理海因里希·劳和他的夫人以及德意志民主共和国政府代表团全体贵宾，今天晚间在天桥剧场欣赏了首都艺术工作者演出的京剧和歌舞节目。
    陪同贵宾们观看演出的，有国务院副总理习仲勋，文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长曾涌泉等人。
    今天的晚会是文化部为热烈欢迎德意志民主共和国政府代表团而举行的。晚会上，中国京剧院和中央歌舞团的演员们演出了“孔雀舞”、“断桥”、“美猴王”、“虹桥赠珠”等京剧、歌舞节目。
    演出结束后，海因里希·劳副总理和代表团团员保罗·维尔纳、奥托·文策尔、保罗·汪戴尔由习仲勋副总理陪同登上舞台，同演员们亲切握手，并向演员们赠送了花篮。
    新华社11日讯　德意志民主共和国副总理海因里希·劳和他的夫人以及德意志民主共和国政府代表团的贵宾们，今天参观了全国工业交通展览会，游览了故宫博物院和颐和园。习仲勋副总理陪同贵宾们游览颐和园，并且在听鹂馆同贵宾们共进午餐。
    今天，德意志民主共和国政府代表团团员：德国统一社会党中央政治局候补委员保罗·维尔纳和德国统一社会党中央委员、外交部第一副部长奥托·文策尔分别应中共北京市委和国际关系研究所的邀请，作了有关德国问题的报告。他们的报告受到热烈欢迎。
