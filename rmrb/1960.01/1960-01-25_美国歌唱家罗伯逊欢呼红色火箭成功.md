Title: American Singer Robeson Cheers the Success of the Red Rocket
Author:
Date: 1960-01-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 24th: TASS, Moscow, January 24th: The famous African-American singer Paul Robeson told a reporter from the Soviet "Literature and Life" newspaper: The successful launch of the new and powerful ballistic rocket by the Soviet Union convincingly demonstrates once again that socialist society possesses unlimited possibilities to accomplish extraordinary miracles.
    This fighter for peace emphasized that it is particularly gratifying that all the achievements of the Soviet state are for peaceful purposes, for the benefit of mankind, and are contributions to the world's scientific and cultural treasure.

### 美国歌唱家罗伯逊欢呼红色火箭成功

1960-01-25
第6版()
专栏：

    美国歌唱家罗伯逊欢呼红色火箭成功
    新华社莫斯科24日电　据塔斯社莫斯科24日讯：美国著名黑人歌唱家罗伯逊对苏联“文学与生活报”记者说：苏联发射新的威力强大的弹道火箭成功，再一次令人信服地说明，社会主义社会拥有无限的可能性来完成不平凡的奇迹。
    这位和平战士强调指出，特别令人高兴的是，苏维埃国家的一切成就都是为了和平目的，为了造福人类，都是对世界科学文化宝库的贡献。
