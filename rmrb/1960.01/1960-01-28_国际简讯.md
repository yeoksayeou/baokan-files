International Briefs

    Britain Establishes New Middle East Command
    London dispatch: The British Ministry of Defense announced on the 26th that Britain has decided to establish a new Middle East Command, headquartered in Episcopi, Cyprus. This new command will uniformly govern all British army and air force units in the Mediterranean region, including Cyprus, Libya, and the island of Malta, and will continue to intensify its control and suppression of the Middle East, especially the Arabian Peninsula, in order to safeguard the enormous oil interests of British monopoly capital in this region. It is revealed that this British action is also taken in response to the military expansion of the United States in the Middle East.

    U.S. Subpoenas Youth Attending Festival
    New York dispatch: The U.S. House Un-American Activities Committee has begun "investigating" the activities of American youth who participated in the 7th World Youth and Student Festival for Peace and Friendship last summer. The committee will hold a meeting in Washington on February 2nd and subpoena representatives of five New York youths.
    The U.S. Youth Festival Preparatory Committee published a statement in the newspaper saying that the purpose of this "investigation" is to oppose cultural exchange and maintain the tense state of the "Cold War."

    World Federation of Democratic Youth Protests Anti-Semitic Provocations
    Budapest dispatch: The Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth issued a statement strongly protesting and demanding an end to the fascist and anti-Semitic provocations that have erupted in West Germany and other countries in Europe and the Americas.

    Unemployment Rises in Britain
    London dispatch: According to the British Ministry of Labor, the number of unemployed people in Britain on January 11 was 460,000 (the number of unemployed people in Northern Ireland was not included), an increase of 40,000 compared to about a month earlier, on December 7 last year.

### 国际简讯

1960-01-28
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    英国设新的中东司令部
    伦敦讯　英国国防部26日宣布，英国决定建立一个新的中东司令部，总部设在塞浦路斯的埃毕斯科比。这个新的司令部将统一管辖地中海地区，其中包括塞浦路斯、利比亚和马耳他岛的全部英国陆军和空军，继续加紧对中东特别是阿拉伯半岛地区的控制和镇压，以维护英国垄断资本在这一地区巨大的石油利益。据透露，英国这一行动也是针对着美国在中东地区的军事扩张而采取的。
    美国传讯参加联欢节青年
    纽约讯　美国众议院非美活动调查委员会开始“调查”去年夏天参加第七届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的美国青年的活动。委员会2月2日将在华盛顿举行会议，并且传讯五名纽约青年的代表。
    美国青年联欢节筹备委员会在报上发表声明说，这一“调查”的目的是反对文化交流和保持“冷战”的紧张状态。
    世界青联抗议反犹挑衅
    布达佩斯讯　世界民主青年联盟书记处就在西德以及欧洲和美洲的其他国家爆发的法西斯和反犹挑衅发表了声明，坚决抗议并要求结束这种挑衅。
    英国失业人数上升
    伦敦讯　据英国劳工部宣布，1月11日英国的失业人数是四十六万人（北爱尔兰的失业人数没有包括在内），比大约一个月以前，即去年12月7日的失业人数增加了四万人。
