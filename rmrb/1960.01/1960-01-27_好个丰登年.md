Title: What a Bumper Year
Author: Zhang Dengfeng
Date: 1960-01-27
Page: 8

    What a Bumper Year
    Zhang Dengfeng, Railway Worker, Suzhou, Jiangsu
    The mountains are smiling,
    The seas are smiling,
    The motherland is jubilant.
    The sky is singing,
    The earth is singing,
    Many happy events for the New Year.
    The city is dancing,
    The countryside is dancing,
    Beating the drums of victory with joy.
    Steel like the sea,
    Grain topping the sky,
    What a bumper year!
