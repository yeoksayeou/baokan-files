Title: Fifty-Eight Prominent British Figures Protest U.S. Decision to Resume Nuclear Testing
Author:
Date: 1960-01-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, January 7th: Fifty-eight prominent British figures have jointly issued a statement calling on the United States to reconsider its decision to discontinue the suspension of nuclear weapons testing.

The statement was released by the British Campaign for Nuclear Disarmament. It stated that the U.S. decision setbacks the progress made in reaching an agreement in 1959 and threatens the hopes for moving towards disarmament in 1960. The statement calls on people to write to the U.S. Embassy in London to protest the U.S. government's decision.

Signatories to the statement include eight Fellows of the Royal Society, two Archbishops, as well as scientists, actors, writers, and musicians. Among them are Professor Joseph Rotblat, a nuclear physicist who participated in the Los Alamos atomic bomb project, Professor Earl Russell, Baroness Wootton of Abinger, and Lord Boyd Orr.

### 英国五十八名著名人士  抗议美国不再停止核试验

1960-01-11
第6版()
专栏：

    英国五十八名著名人士
    抗议美国不再停止核试验
    新华社伦敦7日电　五十八名英国著名人士联名发表一项声明，呼吁美国重新考虑关于不再继续禁止核武器试验的决定。
    这个声明是英国争取裁减核军备运动发表的。声明说，美国的决定使得1959年在达成协议方面取得的进展遭到了挫折，使1960年向裁军方向前进的希望受到了威胁。声明号召人们写信给美国驻伦敦大使馆对美国政府的决定表示抗议。
    在声明上签名的人包括八名皇家学会会员，两名大主教，以及科学家、演员、作家和音乐家。其中有参加洛萨拉摩斯原子弹工程的核物理学家约瑟夫·罗特布拉特教授，厄尔·鲁塞尔教授，阿宾格的·伍顿男爵夫人，波伊德·奥尔勋爵。
