Title: State Council Holds 93rd Plenary Meeting; Approves Provisional Regulations on Health Protection for Radioactive Work
Author:
Date: 1960-01-08
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th -- The State Council of the People's Republic of China held its ninety-third plenary meeting on the morning of the 7th.

    The meeting approved the Provisional Regulations on Health Protection for Radioactive Work and approved the State Council's proposal to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress regarding the establishment of the General Bureau of Foreign Economic Liaison.

    Finally, the meeting approved matters concerning changes in county, city, and administrative divisions, as well as a list of appointments and removals.

### 国务院举行第九十三次全体会议  通过关于放射性工作卫生防护暂行规定

1960-01-08
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行第九十三次全体会议
    通过关于放射性工作卫生防护暂行规定
    新华社7日讯　中华人民共和国国务院在7日上午举行了第九十三次全体会议。
    会议通过了关于放射性工作卫生防护暂行规定，通过了国务院关于设立对外经济联络总局问题向全国人民代表大会常务委员会提出的议案。
    会议最后通过了县、市、行政区划变更事项和任免名单。
