Title: Two Writers from the German Democratic Republic Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-01-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency: Two writers from the German Democratic Republic, Dr. Manfred Haekel and Erich Köhler, who visited our country according to the 1959 executive plan of the China-German Democratic Republic Cultural Cooperation Agreement, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 8th to return home.

### 两位德意志民主共和国作家回国

1960-01-11
第4版()
专栏：

    两位德意志民主共和国作家回国
    新华社讯　根据中国德意志民主共和国文化合作协定1959年执行计划来我国访问的德意志民主共和国作家曼弗里特·海克尔博士和艾里希·柯勒，8日早晨乘飞机离开北京返国。
