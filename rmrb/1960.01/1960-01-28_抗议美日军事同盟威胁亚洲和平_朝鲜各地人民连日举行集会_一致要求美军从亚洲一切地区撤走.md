Title: Protesting the US-Japan Military Alliance Threatening Asian Peace, People in Various Parts of Korea Hold Rallies for Days, Unanimously Demanding US Troops Withdraw from All Areas of Asia
Author:
Date: 1960-01-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, January 26th: According to reports in Pyongyang newspapers, people in various parts of Korea have been holding rallies for days, condemning the US-Japan reactionaries for concluding the US-Japan "Treaty of Mutual Cooperation and Security."

The employees of the Heechon Machine Tool Factory held a rally on the 24th. Factory Director Li Yuan Guan pointed out in his report at the meeting that the conclusion of the US-Japan military alliance is not only a serious challenge to peace in Asia and the world, but also a direct threat to the Korean people. He firmly demanded the immediate abolition of this treaty, and expressed active solidarity and support for the Japanese people's struggle against the conclusion of this treaty.

The Korea-China Friendship Agricultural Cooperative in Sunan County, South Pyongan Province, also held a rally recently. Deputy Director Jin Li Bi and many cooperative members spoke at the meeting, unanimously demanding that US troops withdraw from South Korea, Japan, and all areas of Asia, so that the region becomes a peaceful area. They emphasized that the conclusion of the US-Japan military alliance treaty is a last-ditch struggle of the US-Japan reactionary group.

The faculty and students of Kim Il Sung University also held a rally on the 24th. Han Soo Dong, the first vice president of the university, and many students emphasized in their speeches at the meeting that the US-Japan military alliance treaty is a breeding ground for new war dangers in the Far East and Asia.

They especially emphasized that the Korean people will crush the enemy's provocations and strive for the peaceful reunification of the motherland.

### 抗议美日军事同盟威胁亚洲和平  朝鲜各地人民连日举行集会  一致要求美军从亚洲一切地区撤走

1960-01-28
第5版()
专栏：

    抗议美日军事同盟威胁亚洲和平
    朝鲜各地人民连日举行集会  一致要求美军从亚洲一切地区撤走
    新华社平壤26日电　据平壤报纸报道，朝鲜各地人民连日举行集会，谴责日美反动派缔结日美“共同合作和安全条约”。
    熙川机床厂职工24日举行集会。厂长李元官在会上作报告时指出，日美军事同盟的缔结，不仅是对亚洲和世界和平的严重挑战，而且也是对朝鲜人民的直接威胁。他坚决要求立即废除这一条约，并对日本人民为反对这一条约的缔结而进行的斗争表示积极的声援和支持。
    平安南道顺安郡朝中友谊农业社也在最近举行集会。副社长金礼弼和许多社员在会上讲话时，一致要求美军从南朝鲜、日本和一切亚洲地区撤走，使这一地区成为和平地区。他们强调，日美军事同盟条约的缔结是美日反动集团的垂死挣扎。
    金日成综合大学的师生们也在24日举行集会。这个大学的第一副校长韩秀东和许多学生在会上讲话时强调，日美军事同盟条约是制造远东和亚洲新战争危险的温床。
    他们特别强调，朝鲜人民将粉碎敌人的挑衅，争取祖国的和平统一。
