Title: State Council Holds Ninety-Fourth Plenary Meeting; Approves China-GDR Treaty of Commerce and Navigation
Author:
Date: 1960-01-21
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 20th — The State Council held its ninety-fourth plenary meeting on the afternoon of the 20th, approving the Treaty of Commerce and Navigation between the People's Republic of China and the German Democratic Republic, and decided to submit the treaty to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for review and ratification.
    The meeting decided to establish Heze City in Shandong Province and abolish Heze County, with the administrative area of the original Heze County becoming the administrative area of Heze City.
    The meeting also approved appointments and removals.

### 国务院举行第九十四次全体会议  通过中德通商航海条约

1960-01-21
第4版()
专栏：

    国务院举行第九十四次全体会议
    通过中德通商航海条约
    新华社20日讯　国务院在20日下午举行第九十四次全体会议，通过了中华人民共和国和德意志民主共和国通商航海条约，并且决定把这个条约提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议批准。
    会议决定，设立山东省菏泽市，撤销菏泽县，以原菏泽县的行政区域为菏泽市的行政区域。
    会议还通过了任免事项。
