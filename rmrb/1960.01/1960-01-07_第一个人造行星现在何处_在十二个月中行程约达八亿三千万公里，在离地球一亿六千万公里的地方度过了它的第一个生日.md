Title: Where is the First Artificial Planet Now?
Author:
Date: 1960-01-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, January 5th: According to a report by the Polish Press Agency on January 4th, on the occasion of the first anniversary (January 2nd) of the launch of mankind's first artificial planet by the Soviet Union, the Science and Technology Department of the Polish Workers' Press Agency called the Moscow Planetarium to inquire about the current whereabouts of the first artificial planet.

The director of the Moscow Planetarium, Mr. Bazekin, said that the artificial planet spent its first birthday approximately 160 million kilometers away from Earth. In twelve months, it traveled approximately 830 million kilometers, which is equivalent to more than 220 times the distance between the Moon and the Earth.

The comrades from the Workers' Press Agency asked: Has this artificial planet completed its journey around the Sun?

Bazekin replied that it had not. The orbits of the Earth and the artificial planet around the Sun are similar, but the diameter of the artificial planet's orbit is longer (about 343 million kilometers), so in twelve months, the Earth can orbit the Sun once, while the artificial planet needs fifteen months.

Question: So, the artificial planet will complete its journey around the sun around April. Will we be able to see it from Earth then?

Bazekin replied, no. Only in four years will the artificial planet be closest to Earth. But even then, it will be millions of kilometers away, and its size is only that of a small car, so we will not see it.

Another question: Will we never be able to see it with the naked eye?

Bazekin finally replied, I think so. Unless in the future, mankind learns to build a spacecraft that can catch up with the speed of this artificial planet and ride this spacecraft to catch up with it.

### 第一个人造行星现在何处  在十二个月中行程约达八亿三千万公里，在离地球一亿六千万公里的地方度过了它的第一个生日

1960-01-07
第5版()
专栏：

    第一个人造行星现在何处
    在十二个月中行程约达八亿三千万公里，在离地球一亿六千万公里的地方度过了它的第一个生日
    新华社5日讯　据波兰通讯社4日报道：在苏联发射的人类第一个人造行星上天一周年（1月2日）之际，波兰工人通讯社科学技术部打电话给莫斯科天文馆，询问了关于第一个人造行星目前的行踪。
    莫斯科天文馆馆长巴泽金说，人造行星在离地球约一亿六千万公里的地方度过了它的第一个生日。在十二个月中它所经过的行程约达八亿三千万公里，这段路程相当于月球和地球之间距离的二百二十倍还要多一些。
    工人通讯社的同志们问道：这个人造行星是否走完了它绕太阳旋转一周的路程？
    巴泽金回答说，没有。地球和人造行星绕太阳旋转的轨道相差不多，但人造行星轨道的直径要长些（三亿四千三百万公里左右），所以，在十二个月内，地球能绕太阳转一周，而人造行星需要十五个月。
    问：那么约在4月间人造行星完成绕太阳一周的路程，那时我们可以从地球上看到吗？
    巴泽金回答，不，只有在四年以后，人造行星才会距离地球最近。但是，那时，相距也有几百万公里，而它的体积只有小汽车那样大，因此，我们也不会看到它。
    又问：我们用肉眼是永远也看不到它了？
    巴泽金最后回答说，我认为是这样。除非将来人类学会制造一种速度可赶上这个人造行星的宇宙飞船，而且乘坐这种宇宙飞船去追上它。
