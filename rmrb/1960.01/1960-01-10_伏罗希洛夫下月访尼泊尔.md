Title: Voroshilov to Visit Nepal Next Month
Author:
Date: 1960-01-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 9th: TASS reported on the 9th that, at the invitation of King Mahendra of Nepal and the Nepalese government, Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Kozlov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and Furtseva, a representative of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, will visit the Kingdom of Nepal in early February this year. This is a return visit to the visit of the King and Queen of Nepal and other Nepalese activists to the Soviet Union in the summer of 1958.

### 伏罗希洛夫下月访尼泊尔

1960-01-10
第3版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫下月访尼泊尔
    新华社莫斯科9日电　塔斯社9日报道：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫、苏联部长会议第一副主席科兹洛夫和苏联最高苏维埃代表福尔采娃，应尼泊尔国王马亨德拉和尼泊尔政府的邀请，将于今年2月初访问尼泊尔王国。这是对尼泊尔国王和王后以及尼泊尔其他活动家在1958年夏季访问苏联的回访。
