Title: International News Agencies Directors' Conference Concludes; Cuban President and Prime Minister Receive Representatives from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-01-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency Reports: The International News Agencies Directors' Conference, convened by the leadership of Latin American News Agency, was held in Havana from January 12th to 20th.

During the conference, Cuban President Dorticos, Prime Minister Castro, Minister of Revolutionary Armed Forces Raúl Castro, one of the leaders of the armed forces and President of the National Bank Guevara, Director of the National Institute of Agrarian Reform Jiménez, Minister of Education Art, Minister of Agriculture Miret, and Minister of Transportation Ortuski separately received representatives from various countries' news agencies who attended the conference.

The representatives visited agricultural cooperatives, tobacco factories, settlements, and other places of interest.

### 国际通讯社社长会议闭幕  古巴总统和总理等分别接见了各国代表

1960-01-22
第6版()
专栏：

    国际通讯社社长会议闭幕
    古巴总统和总理等分别接见了各国代表
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：拉丁美洲通讯社领导机构召集的国际通讯社社长会议12日到20日在哈瓦那举行。
    会议进行期间，古巴总统多尔蒂科斯、总理卡斯特罗、革命武装部部长劳尔·卡斯特罗、武装部队领导人之一兼国家银行行长格瓦拉、土地改革全国委员会主任希门尼斯、教育部长阿特、农业部长米雷特和交通部长奥耳图斯基先后接见了参加会议的各国通讯社代表。
    代表们参观了农业合作社、烟草厂、居民点和其他一些有兴趣的地方。
