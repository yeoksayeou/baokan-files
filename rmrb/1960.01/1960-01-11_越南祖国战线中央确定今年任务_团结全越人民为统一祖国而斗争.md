Title: Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front Defines This Year's Tasks: Unite All Vietnamese People to Struggle for a Unified Motherland
Author:
Date: 1960-01-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 10th -- The 11th enlarged meeting of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front concluded on the 9th.

The meeting adopted a resolution on this year's tasks, stating that in 1960, the Fatherland Front will vigorously promote the struggle for a unified motherland; broadly unite the people of the whole country to strive for the victorious completion of the Three-Year Plan and for the construction of a strong socialist North; strengthen international solidarity and contribute to the struggle to defend world peace.  Sun Duc Thang, Chairman of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Fatherland Front, pointed out in his closing remarks that in 1959, through the practice and study of socialist transformation, the Fatherland Front was further consolidated and expanded.

### 越南祖国战线中央确定今年任务  团结全越人民为统一祖国而斗争

1960-01-11
第5版()
专栏：

    越南祖国战线中央确定今年任务
    团结全越人民为统一祖国而斗争
    据新华社河内10日电　越南祖国战线中央委员会第十一次扩大会议已经在9日闭幕。
    会议通过关于今年任务的决议说，1960年祖国战线将大力推进统一祖国的斗争；广泛团结全国人民，为胜利完成三年计划、为建设强大的社会主义北方而奋斗；增强国际团结，为保卫世界和平的斗争作出贡献。祖国战线中央委员会主席团主席孙德胜致闭幕词时指出，1959年，通过社会主义改造的实践和学习，祖国战线进一步巩固和扩大了。
