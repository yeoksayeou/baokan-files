Title: All-China Women's Federation Hosts Banquet for Japanese Women's Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-01-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 14th — The All-China Women's Federation hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the Japanese Women's Delegation headed by Takegami Masako.

    Li Dequan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, spoke at the banquet, extending a warm welcome to the Japanese Women's Delegation. She said that the women of China and Japan have been friendly for many years, and we greatly admire the courageous spirit shown by the Japanese people and Japanese women in their just struggle to win Japan's independence and defend world peace. She said that the Japanese people's struggle against US imperialism will surely be victorious, and victory belongs to the Japanese people.

    The head of the delegation, Takegami Masako, said in her speech that she hoped that the women of Japan and China would join hands to work for the realization of world peace.

    Also present at tonight's banquet were members of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation, including Shen Zijiu and Cao Mengjun.

### 全国妇联欢宴日本妇女代表团

1960-01-15
第4版()
专栏：

    全国妇联欢宴日本妇女代表团
    新华社14日讯　全国妇联今晚设宴欢迎以竹上正子为首的日本妇女代表团。
    全国妇联副主席李德全在宴会上讲话，向日本妇女代表团表示热烈的欢迎。她说，中日两国妇女多年来一直很友好，我们非常敬佩日本人民和日本妇女在争取日本的独立和保卫世界和平的正义斗争中所表现的勇敢精神。她说，日本人民反对美帝国主义的斗争一定会获得胜利，胜利是属于日本人民的。
    竹上正子团长在讲话中表示，希望日中两国妇女共同携起手来，为实现世界和平而努力。
    出席今晚宴会的，还有全国妇联主席团委员沈兹九、曹孟君等人。
