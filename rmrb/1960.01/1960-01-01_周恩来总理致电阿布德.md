Title: Premier Zhou Enlai's Telegram to Abboud
Author:
Date: 1960-01-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 31st. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram today to Sudanese Prime Minister Ibrahim Abboud, congratulating him on the Independence Day of the Republic of Sudan. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

To His Excellency General Ibrahim Abboud, Prime Minister of the Republic of Sudan:

On the occasion of the Independence Day of the Republic of Sudan, I would like to express my warm congratulations to the Government of the Republic of Sudan and to Your Excellency on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and in my own name.

We are pleased to see that with the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Sudan, the friendly relations between our two countries have been further developed. May the friendship between China and Sudan be continuously consolidated and strengthened. May the Republic of Sudan prosper.

Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
December 31, 1959, Beijing

### 周恩来总理致电阿布德

1960-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理致电阿布德
    新华社31日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天打电报给苏丹共和国总理易卜拉欣·阿布德，祝贺苏丹共和国独立纪念日。电报全文如下：苏丹共和国总理易卜拉欣·阿布德将军阁下：
    欣逢苏丹共和国独立纪念日，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府，并且以我个人的名义向苏丹共和国政府和阁下表示热烈的祝贺。
    我们高兴地看到，随着中国和苏丹两国外交关系的建立，我们两国间的友好关系有了进一步的发展。祝中国和苏丹两国间的友谊不断巩固和加强。祝苏丹共和国繁荣昌盛。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
    1959年12月31日于北京
