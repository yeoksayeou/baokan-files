Title: Two Soviet Philosophers Come to China to Lecture
Author:
Date: 1960-01-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: According to the Sino-Soviet Academies of Sciences scientific cooperation agreement, two Soviet philosophers have come to China to lecture at the invitation of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. They are: Corresponding Member of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Iovchuk, and Corresponding Member of the Soviet Academy of Pedagogical Sciences, Professor Kedrov. They have already given several academic reports in Beijing and will visit and lecture in Tianjin, Shanghai and other places.

### 两位苏联哲学家来中国讲学

1960-01-11
第4版()
专栏：

    两位苏联哲学家来中国讲学
    新华社讯　根据中苏两国科学院科学合作协定，两位苏联哲学家已应中国科学院的邀请来中国讲学。他们是：苏联科学院通讯院士约夫楚克和苏联教育科学院通讯院士凯德罗夫教授。他们已经在北京作了若干学术报告，并且将赴天津、上海等地访问讲学。
