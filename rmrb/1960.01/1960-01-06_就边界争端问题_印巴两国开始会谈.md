Title: On the Border Dispute, India and Pakistan Begin Talks
Author:
Date: 1960-01-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, January 4th: Negotiations between India and Pakistan regarding the border dispute between West Pakistan and India began this afternoon in Lahore, the capital of West Pakistan.

Participating in the negotiations were the Pakistani government delegation led by Interior and Police Intelligence Minister Sheikh and the Indian government delegation led by Indian Minister of Steel, Mines, and Fuel, S. S. Singh.

### 就边界争端问题  印巴两国开始会谈

1960-01-06
第6版()
专栏：

    就边界争端问题
    印巴两国开始会谈
    新华社卡拉奇4日电　印度和巴基斯坦关于西巴基斯坦同印度边界争端的谈判，今天下午在西巴基斯坦首府拉合尔开始举行。
    参加谈判的是以内政和警察情报部长谢赫为首的巴基斯坦政府代表团和以印度钢铁、矿业和燃料部长萨·斯·辛格为首的印度政府代表团。
