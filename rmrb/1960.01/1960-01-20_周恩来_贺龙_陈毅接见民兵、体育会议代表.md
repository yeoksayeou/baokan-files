Title: Zhou Enlai, He Long, and Chen Yi Receive Representatives of Militia and Sports Conferences
Author:
Date: 1960-01-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th — Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, and He Long and Chen Yi, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee, received all the representatives attending the National Militia Work Conference and the National Sports Work Conference this afternoon.

### 周恩来　贺龙　陈毅接见民兵、体育会议代表

1960-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来　贺龙　陈毅接见民兵、体育会议代表
    新华社19日讯　中共中央副主席周恩来，中央政治局委员贺龙、陈毅，今天下午接见了出席全国民兵工作会议和全国体育工作会议的全体代表。
