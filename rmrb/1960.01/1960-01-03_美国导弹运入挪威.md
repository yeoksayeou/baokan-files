Title: US Missiles Transported to Norway
Author:
Date: 1960-01-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Oslo, January 1st: A batch of American "Honest John" missiles were transported yesterday to the Salangsverket missile base in northern Norway. After the missiles are transported to Norway, the next step for the North Atlantic Treaty Organization Command is to transport atomic warheads to Norway.

### 美国导弹运入挪威

1960-01-03
第5版()
专栏：

    美国导弹运入挪威
    据新华社1日讯　奥斯陆消息：一批美国的“诚实的约翰”式导弹昨天运到挪威北部的萨兰斯维尔克特导弹基地。导弹运入挪威后，北大西洋集团司令部下一步就打算把原子弹头运入挪威。
