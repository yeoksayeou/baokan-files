Title: Investigating the Possibility of American Investment: Three American Delegations Active in Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1960-01-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, January 18th: A trade delegation of 39 members dispatched by the Greater Detroit Board of Commerce of the United States arrived in Karachi on the 17th. They will stay in Pakistan for three days.

It is reported that the purpose of these Americans' visit to Pakistan is to familiarize Detroit entrepreneurs with Pakistan's economic situation and to investigate the possibility of investing in Pakistan.

Including this delegation, there are now three American delegations engaged in activities in Pakistan with the same purpose. The other two delegations are a trade delegation led by Mr. Bucher, an official of the U.S. Department of Commerce, and a delegation led by Mr. Brand, General Manager of the U.S. Development Loan Fund.

### 调查美国投资的可能性  美国三个代表团在巴基斯坦活动

1960-01-20
第5版()
专栏：

    调查美国投资的可能性
    美国三个代表团在巴基斯坦活动
    新华社卡拉奇18日电　美国大底特律商务委员会派遣的一个由三十九人组成的贸易代表团在17日到达卡拉奇。他们将在巴基斯坦停留三天。
    据报道，这些美国人到巴基斯坦的目的是使底特律企业家熟悉巴基斯坦的经济情况和调查在巴基斯坦投资的可能性。
    连同这个代表团，现在共有三个美国代表团怀着同样目的在巴基斯坦进行活动。其他两个代表团，一个是由美国商务部官员布查率领的贸易代表团，一个代表团是由美国开发贷款基金会总经理布兰德率领的代表团。
