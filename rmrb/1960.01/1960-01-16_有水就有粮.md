Title: Where There is Water, There is Grain

Author:

Date: 1960-01-16
Page: 3

Where there is water, there is grain,
With sufficient water, the granary is full,
The people's commune is good,
With surplus grain every year.

### 有水就有粮

第3版()

    有水就有粮
