Title: German Guests Leave Zhengzhou to Visit Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1960-01-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, January 13th: Deputy Prime Minister Heinrich Rau and the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic he led, after concluding their visit in Zhengzhou, took a plane to Wuhan at 2:30 this afternoon.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Zhao Wenfu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Henan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and Deputy Governor of Henan Province; Li Li, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Henan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China; and Li Qingwei, Deputy Governor of Henan Province. The head of the German Democratic Republic experts working at the Zhengzhou Grinding Wheel Factory also went to the airport to see them off.

When the distinguished guests visited the Zhengzhou Grinding Wheel Factory yesterday, the factory's employees held a grand meeting to welcome them. Wang Zhiguang, Secretary of the Zhengzhou Grinding Wheel Factory Committee of the Communist Party of China, expressed his heartfelt gratitude on behalf of all the factory's employees to the government and people of the German Democratic Republic for their strong support to the factory. Deputy Prime Minister Heinrich Rau said in his speech: "In your beautiful factory, the friendly cooperation between the people of Germany and China is embodied." Deputy Prime Minister Heinrich Rau presented a beautiful tape recorder to the factory.

This morning, the distinguished guests visited the Dongjiao People's Commune in Zhengzhou. After the visit, the distinguished guests presented the commune with a phonograph, records, and albums. The commune cadres and members presented the distinguished guests with peanuts and candied dates they produced.

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, January 13th: Deputy Prime Minister Heinrich Rau and all the distinguished guests of the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic he led, accompanied by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan and Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Ma Zhenwu, arrived in Wuhan by special plane from Zhengzhou this afternoon for a visit.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the distinguished guests included Zhang Tixue, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and Governor of Hubei Province; Liu Huinong, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Mayor of Wuhan; and Major General Wu Shi'an, head of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Wuhan. After arriving in Wuhan, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Zeng Yongquan, Zhang Tixue, and others, visited the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge and toured the city of Wuhan. In the evening, the leaders of the Hubei Province and Wuhan City Party and government held a dinner with all the distinguished guests of the delegation.

### 德贵宾离郑州到武汉访问

1960-01-14
第4版()
专栏：

    德贵宾离郑州到武汉访问
    据新华社郑州13日电　海因里希·劳副总理和他率领的德意志民主共和国政府代表团，结束了在郑州的参观访问以后，在今天下午二时三十分乘飞机前往武汉访问。
    到机场欢送的，有中共河南省委书记处书记、河南省副省长赵文甫，中共河南省委书记处书记李立，河南省副省长李庆伟等。在郑州砂轮厂帮助工作的德意志民主共和国专家负责人也到机场送行。
    贵宾们昨天到郑州砂轮厂参观时，这个厂的职工曾隆重集会欢迎。中共郑州砂轮厂委员会书记王之光代表全厂职工，对德意志民主共和国政府和人民对这个厂的大力援助表示衷心的感谢。海因里希·劳副总理在讲话中说：在你们这个美丽的工厂里，体现了德中两国人民的友谊合作。海因里希·劳副总理，把一架精美的录音机赠送给这个厂。
    今天上午，贵宾们到郑州市东郊人民公社参观。参观以后，贵宾赠送这个公社一部留声机和唱片及画册，公社干部和社员们把自己生产的花生和焦枣赠送给贵宾。
    新华社武汉13日电　海因里希·劳副总理和他率领的德意志民主共和国政府代表团全体贵宾，在外交部副部长曾涌泉和外交部礼宾司副司长马振武陪同下，今天下午由郑州乘专机到达武汉访问。
    到机场欢迎贵宾的，有中共湖北省委书记处书记、湖北省省长张体学，中共武汉市委书记处书记、武汉市市长刘惠农，中国人民解放军驻武汉部队首长吴世安少将等。贵宾们到武汉后，由曾涌泉、张体学等人陪同，参观了武汉长江大桥，游览了武汉市容。晚间，湖北省和武汉市党政负责人同代表团全体贵宾共进晚餐。
