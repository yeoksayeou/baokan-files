    International Briefs
    Indian Vice President Travels to London
    New Delhi Dispatch: Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan left here on the evening of the 8th for a visit to London.
    According to the Press Trust of India, Radhakrishnan said that after visiting London, he would travel from there to visit the Scandinavian countries.
    Laotian "Provisional Government" Sworn In
    Hanoi Dispatch: According to a broadcast from Vientiane Radio on the 8th, the Laotian "Provisional Government" headed by Kou Abhay met with the King on January 7th and was sworn in at Wat Xieng Thong in Vientiane on January 8th.

### 国际简讯

1960-01-10
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    印度副总统前往伦敦
    新德里讯　印度副总统拉达克里希南8日晚离开这里前往伦敦访问。
    据印度报业托辣斯报道，拉达克里希南说，他在访问伦敦以后，将从那里前往斯堪的纳维亚国家访问。
    老挝“临时政府”宣誓就职
    河内讯　据万象电台8日广播，以古·阿派为首的老挝“临时政府”在1月7日晋见国王之后，1月8日已在万象薛隆吉寺宣誓就职。
