Title: Chinese Painting and Print Exhibition Opens in Cairo
Author:
Date: 1960-01-30
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, January 27th. A Chinese Painting and Print Exhibition opened in Cairo today.
    The opening ceremony was presided over by Okasha, Minister of Culture and National Guidance of the Egyptian Region of the United Arab Republic. Attendees at the opening ceremony included Hashim, Minister of Education of the Egyptian Region, Hassouna, Secretary-General of the Arab League, as well as Egyptian and foreign artists.
    The exhibition featured works by contemporary Chinese artists. These artworks aroused great interest among the audience. The unique styles of the late Chinese painters Qi Baishi and Xu Beihong received high praise.

### 中国绘画与版画展览会在开罗开幕

1960-01-30
第3版()
专栏：

    中国绘画与版画展览会在开罗开幕
    新华社开罗27日电　中国绘画与版画展览会今天在开罗开幕。
    开幕式由阿联埃及地区文化和国家指导部长奥卡夏主持。参加开幕式的有埃及地区教育部长哈希姆、阿拉伯联盟秘书长哈苏纳、以及埃及和外国的艺术家等。
    展览会展出了中国当代艺术家的作品。这些艺术品引起观众的极大兴趣。已故中国画家齐白石和徐悲鸿的独特风格得到很高的评价。
