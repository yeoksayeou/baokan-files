Author: 
Date: 1960-01-22
Page: 1

    Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China

    The 12th meeting of the Standing Committee of the Second National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China adopted the decision on the appointment and removal of assistant judges of the Supreme People's Court and local people's courts at all levels on January 21, 1960, and it is hereby promulgated.
                            Liu Shaoqi
                            Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                                    January 21, 1960

### 中华人民共和国主席令

1960-01-22
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国主席令
    中华人民共和国第二届全国人民代表大会常务委员会于1960年1月21日第十二次会议通过了关于最高人民法院和地方各级人民法院助理审判员任免问题的决定，现予公布。
                            中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                                    一九六○年一月二十一日
