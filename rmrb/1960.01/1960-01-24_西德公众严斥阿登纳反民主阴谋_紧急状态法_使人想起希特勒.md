Title: West German Public Severely Condemns Adenauer's Anti-Democratic Conspiracy; "Emergency Law" Reminiscent of Hitler
Author:
Date: 1960-01-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Bonn, January 22nd: The broad public in West Germany is severely condemning the West German government's drafted anti-democratic "Emergency Law."

The two opposition parties in the Bundestag—the West German Social Democratic Party and the Free Democratic Party—have both announced that they will oppose the bill. The West German Ironworkers' Union Council, the largest trade union in West Germany, believes that the government's proposed "Emergency Law" attempts to use the state's coercive power to abolish important democratic rights, and this bill will enable the government to restrict and even prohibit the activities of various trade unions.

The West German press is also fiercely criticizing the "Emergency Law." The *Neue Rhein Zeitung* wrote that the "Emergency Law" "is reminiscent of the terrible moments under Hitler's rule." The *Westfälische Rundschau* opposes the bill's provision for the use of the Federal Defense Forces in resolving domestic political conflicts.

The "Emergency Law"'s clause regarding the implementation of martial law in West Germany with only a simple majority vote in the Bundestag has aroused public condemnation. The *Stuttgarter Zeitung* believes that the requirement that a simple majority in the Bundestag has the power to declare an indefinite state of emergency is unacceptable.

### 西德公众严斥阿登纳反民主阴谋  “紧急状态法”使人想起希特勒

1960-01-24
第5版()
专栏：

    西德公众严斥阿登纳反民主阴谋
    “紧急状态法”使人想起希特勒
    据新华社22日讯　据塔斯社波恩讯：西德广大公众严厉谴责西德政府草拟的反民主的“紧急状态法”。
    联邦议院中的两个反对党——西德社会民主党和自由民主党都宣布它们将反对这个法案。西德最大的工会——五金工人工会理事会认为，政府提出的“紧急状态法”企图用国家的强制力量来取消重要的民主权利，这个法案将使政府能够限制，甚至禁止各工会的活动。
    西德报界也猛烈抨击“紧急状态法”。“新来因报”写道，“紧急状态法”“使人想起希特勒统治下可怖的时刻。”“威斯特伐利亚评论报”反对法案所规定的在解决国内政治冲突时使用联邦国防军的做法。
    “紧急状态法”中关于只要联邦议院简单多数通过就可以在西德实行戒严状态的条例，引起了公众的谴责。“斯图加特日报”认为，联邦议院简单多数就有权宣布无限期实行紧急状态的要求是不能接受的。
