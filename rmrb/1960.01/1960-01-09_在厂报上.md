    *In the Factory Newspaper*
    Ode to Joy
    Oh, 1959, your tasks were completed well and early, the big red "Joy" character sends good news to the people; the old men stroke their beards, the children set off firecrackers, the leaping red flag gives a thumbs-up to praise you! The coal sea churns with joyous waves, snowflakes flutter happily in the sky, singing praises to 1959's meritorious service, celebrating "1960" to continue leaping forward, the red flag is unfurled again.
    Datong Coal Mine, Zhen Rong

### 在厂报上

第2版()
专栏：来信

    ＊在厂报上＊
    欢乐颂
    1959年啊，你的任务完成的又好又早，大红的“喜”字向人们传来了捷报；老人们捋着胡子，孩子们放着鞭炮，跃进的红旗呀，也挠起大拇指把你夸耀！煤海翻滚着欢乐的波涛，雪花在天空中愉快地飞飘，歌赞1959年的汗马功劳，欢庆“1960”继续跃进红旗又展开了。
    大同煤矿　祯荣
