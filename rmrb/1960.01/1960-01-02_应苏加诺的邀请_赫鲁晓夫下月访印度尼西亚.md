Title: At Sukarno's Invitation, Khrushchev to Visit Indonesia Next Month
Author:
Date: 1960-01-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 1st: Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Khrushchev will pay a friendly visit to Indonesia in February 1960.

As previously reported, Indonesian President and Prime Minister Dr. Sukarno invited Khrushchev to pay a friendly visit to Indonesia. Khrushchev told Sukarno last November that he accepted the invitation with sincere gratitude, believing that direct contact between national leaders and political figures of various countries is an important means of bringing the people of all countries closer together and helps to develop and strengthen mutual understanding.

Recently, Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Khrushchev and Indonesian President and Prime Minister Sukarno exchanged views on the upcoming visit, and an agreement was reached: Khrushchev will visit Indonesia in February 1960.

Both sides expressed their firm belief that Khrushchev's visit to Indonesia will be a new and important stage in the development of the existing friendship and cooperation between the Soviet Union and Indonesia, and will help to further ease international tensions and strengthen peace in Asia and the world.

### 应苏加诺的邀请  赫鲁晓夫下月访印度尼西亚

1960-01-02
第3版()
专栏：

    应苏加诺的邀请
    赫鲁晓夫下月访印度尼西亚
    新华社1日讯　塔斯社莫斯科1日讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫将在1960年2月间到印度尼西亚作友好访问。
    前已报道，印度尼西亚总统兼总理苏加诺博士曾经邀请赫鲁晓夫到印度尼西亚作友好访问。赫鲁晓夫在去年11月间曾对苏加诺说，他以真诚感谢的心情接受这个邀请，并认为，各国的国家领导人和政治活动家之间的直接接触是各国人民之间接近的重要手段，而且有助于发展和加强相互谅解。
    最近，苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫和印度尼西亚共和国总统兼总理苏加诺之间就即将进行访问的问题交换了意见，结果达成了协议：赫鲁晓夫将在1960年2月间访问印度尼西亚。
    双方表示深信：赫鲁晓夫访问印度尼西亚将是发展苏联和印度尼西亚之间现存的友谊和合作方面的新的重要阶段，并将有助于进一步缓和国际紧张局势和加强亚洲和世界和平。
