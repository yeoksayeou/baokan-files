Title: Nixon Clamors for U.S. Not to Disarm
Author:
Date: 1960-01-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, January 17th: U.S. Vice President Nixon, when speaking in Florida on the 15th about the Soviet Union's decision to reduce its armed forces by 1.2 million, stated that the United States is not prepared to reduce its armed forces. He emphasized, "Under no circumstances should the United States and its allies reduce their strength."

    At the same time, the U.S. Vice President, who has begun his presidential campaign activities, also tried his best to boast about the United States' military strength.

### 尼克松叫嚷美国不裁军

1960-01-18
第5版()
专栏：

    尼克松叫嚷美国不裁军
    据新华社17日讯　华盛顿消息：美国副总统尼克松15日在佛罗里达谈到苏联决定裁减武装部队一百二十万的时候表示，美国不准备裁减武装部队。他强调说：“在任何情况之下，美国及其盟国都不应当削减它们的力量。”
    与此同时，这位开始了他的总统竞选活动的美国副总统，还竭力吹嘘美国的军事力量。
