International Briefs

    Vietnam Establishes Population Census Committee
    Hanoi Dispatch: According to the Vietnam News Agency on the 19th, the Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam recently issued an order on the establishment of a Central Steering Committee for Population Census and population census committees at all levels. The Central Steering Committee, headed by Nguyen Khang, Minister of the Prime Minister's Office, is tasked with assisting the Council of Ministers in guiding the population census work throughout the North.
    Soviet Minister of Power Station Construction Leaves Cairo for Home
    Cairo Dispatch: Soviet Minister of Power Station Construction, Novikov, left Cairo on the morning of the 20th to return home.

    国际简讯
    越南成立人口调查委员会
    河内讯　据越南通讯社19日消息：越南民主共和国总理最近颁布了关于成立人口调查中央指导委员会和各级人口调查委员会的命令。以总理府部长阮康为首的中央指导委员会的任务是：协助政府部长会议指导全北方的人口调查工作。
    苏联电站建造部长离开罗回国
    开罗讯　苏联电站建造部长诺维科夫20日上午离开开罗回国。
