Title: Chairman Liu and Premier Zhou Send Congratulatory Messages on Burmese Independence Day
Author: 
Date: 1960-01-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 3rd -- Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, sent a telegram today to the President of the Union of Burma, U Win Maung, to congratulate him on the twelfth anniversary of the independence of the Union of Burma. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Excellency U Win Maung, President of the Union of Burma:

On the occasion of the twelfth anniversary of the independence of the Union of Burma, I extend warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of Burma on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. May the friendship between China and Burma grow stronger day by day. May Burma enjoy prosperity and its people happiness. I wish Your Excellency good health.

Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
January 3, 1960, in Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, January 3rd -- Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, sent a telegram today to the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, Ne Win, to congratulate him on the twelfth anniversary of the independence of the Union of Burma. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Excellency General Ne Win, Prime Minister of the Union of Burma:

On the occasion of your country's National Day, I extend heartfelt congratulations to Your Excellency and the government of your country on behalf of the government of the People's Republic of China and in my own name.

The people of China and Burma have enjoyed close and friendly relations since ancient times. Since the people of both countries shook off the shackles of foreign aggressors and achieved national independence, the traditional friendship between our two countries has been further developed under the guidance of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, and economic and cultural ties between the two countries have also been greatly enhanced. The Chinese government and people sincerely hope that, through the joint efforts of the governments and people of both countries, the friendly relations between China and Burma will be continuously strengthened.

I wish the government and people of the Union of Burma new achievements in building their country and safeguarding peace in Asia and the world.

Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
January 3, 1960, in Beijing

### 刘主席周总理电贺缅甸国庆

1960-01-04
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席周总理电贺缅甸国庆
    新华社3日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇今天打电报给缅甸联邦总统吴温貌，祝贺缅甸联邦独立十二周年。电文如下：缅甸联邦总统吴温貌阁下：
    欣逢缅甸联邦独立十二周年，我谨代表中国人民，并且以我个人的名义向阁下和缅甸人民表示热烈的祝贺。祝中缅两国的友谊与日俱增。祝缅甸国家繁荣，人民幸福。祝阁下身体健康。
     中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
     一九六○年一月三日于北京
    新华社3日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天打电报给缅甸联邦总理奈温，祝贺缅甸联邦独立十二周年，电文如下：缅甸联邦总理奈温将军阁下：
    在贵国国庆的时候，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府，并且以我个人的名义向阁下和贵国政府表示衷心的祝贺。
    中缅两国人民自古以来就有着亲密友好的关系。自从两国人民摆脱外国侵略者的枷锁而取得国家的独立以来，我们两国间的传统友谊在和平共处五项原则的指导下，得到了新的发展，两国间的经济和文化联系也有了很大的增进。中国政府和人民衷心地希望，通过两国政府和人民的共同努力，中缅两国的友好关系将获得不断的加强。
    祝缅甸联邦政府和缅甸人民在建设自己国家和维护亚洲和世界和平的事业中取得新的成就。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
    一九六○年一月三日于北京
