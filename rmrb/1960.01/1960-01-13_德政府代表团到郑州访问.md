Title: German Government Delegation Visits Zhengzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-01-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 12th—The government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, led by Deputy Prime Minister Heinrich Rau, arrived in Zhengzhou by special plane this morning from Beijing for a visit, accompanied by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan and Deputy Director of the Protocol Department Ma Zhenwu. Those who welcomed them at the airport included Zhao Wenfu, Secretary of the Henan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Vice Governor of Henan Province; Li Li, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Henan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Li Qingwei, Vice Governor of Henan Province; Wang Lizhi, First Secretary of the Zhengzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Yan Xinwen, Secretary of the Secretariat; Zhang Beichen and Li Dexin, Vice Mayors of Zhengzhou, and others.
    After arriving in Zhengzhou, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Zeng Yongquan, Zhao Wenfu, and others, visited the Zhengzhou No. 2 Steel Plant, the Grinding Wheel Factory, and the No. 3 State-owned Cotton Mill, and toured the city of Zhengzhou, receiving a warm welcome.
    Tonight, the Henan Provincial and Zhengzhou Municipal Party and government organs hosted an informal banquet to entertain all the distinguished guests of the delegation.
    When the distinguished guests left Beijing, those who saw them off at the airport included Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Lin Haiyun and Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission Liu Mingfu, and others.

### 德政府代表团到郑州访问

1960-01-13
第1版()
专栏：

    德政府代表团到郑州访问
    据新华社12日讯　由海因里希·劳副总理率领的德意志民主共和国政府代表团，在外交部副部长曾涌泉、礼宾司副司长马振武陪同下，今天上午由北京乘专机到达郑州访问。到机场欢迎的，有中共河南省委书记、河南省副省长赵文甫，中共河南省委书记处书记李立，河南省副省长李庆伟，中共郑州市委第一书记王黎之、书记处书记延新文，郑州市副市长张北辰、李德新等。
    贵宾们到郑州后，由曾涌泉、赵文甫等人陪同，参观了郑州第二钢铁厂、砂轮厂和国棉三厂，并游览了郑州市容，受到了热烈欢迎。
    今晚，河南省和郑州市党政机关设便宴招待代表团全体贵宾。
    贵宾们离开北京时，到机场送行的有对外贸易部副部长林海云和国家计划委员会副主任刘明夫等。
