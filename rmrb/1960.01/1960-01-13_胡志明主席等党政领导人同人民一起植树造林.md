Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh and Other Party and Government Leaders Join the People in Planting Trees
Author:
Date: 1960-01-13
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 12th. Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam joined the people of Hanoi in planting trees at the Seven Mau Park in Hanoi on the afternoon of the 11th. Chairman Ho planted a banyan tree in this park, which is under construction.

　　Chairman Ho cordially talked with the surrounding masses during the break. He inquired about the progress of the labor emulation campaign and praised the labor enthusiasm of the masses. The people participating in the labor assured Chairman Ho that they would work hard.

　　Other leaders of the Vietnamese Labor Party and the government, Pham Van Dong, Truong Chinh, Vo Nguyen Giap, etc., also participated in tree planting in the "Youth Forest" of Dien Bridge in the west of Hanoi and the Seven Mau Park on January 10, respectively.

### 胡志明主席等党政领导人同人民一起植树造林

1960-01-13
第6版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席等党政领导人
    同人民一起植树造林
    新华社河内12日电  越南民主共和国主席胡志明11日下午同河内人民一起在河内七亩公园植树。胡主席在这个正在建设的公园里种了一棵榕树。
    胡主席在休息的时候同周围的群众亲切地谈话。他询问了劳动竞赛的情况，赞扬了群众的劳动热情。参加劳动的人们向胡主席保证要好好地劳动。
    越南劳动党和政府的其他领导人范文同、长征、武元甲等也在1月10日分别在河内西面的演桥的“青年林”和七亩公园里参加了植树。
