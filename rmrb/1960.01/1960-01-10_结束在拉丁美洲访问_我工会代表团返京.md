Title: My Trade Union Delegation Returns to Beijing After Concluding Visit to Latin America
Author:
Date: 1960-01-10
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The Chinese Trade Union Delegation, invited to attend the Second Congress of the United Workers' Federation of Chile, returned to Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 8th after visiting Chile, Uruguay and Venezuela.

### 结束在拉丁美洲访问  我工会代表团返京

1960-01-10
第3版()
专栏：

    结束在拉丁美洲访问
    我工会代表团返京
    新华社9日讯　应邀参加智利工人统一工会第二次代表大会的中国工会代表团，在访问智利、乌拉圭、委内瑞拉后，于8日下午乘飞机回到北京。
