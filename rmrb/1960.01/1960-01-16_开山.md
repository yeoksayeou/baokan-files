Title: Mountain Opening
Author:
Date: 1960-01-16
Page: 3

    Mountain Opening
    Cannon smoke fills the air, obscuring the sky,
    Ten thousand hammers fall, and smoke rises from the earth,
    Where are the heroes who split the mountain?
    The red flag flies high on the mountain peak.
