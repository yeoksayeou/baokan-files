Title: Disregarding the Strong Opposition of the Japanese People, Japan and the United States Reach Agreement on Revising the "Security Treaty"
Author:
Date: 1960-01-07
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, January 6th: Japanese Foreign Minister Aiichiro Fujiyama and US Ambassador to Japan Douglas MacArthur II held their twenty-second meeting today, reaching a complete agreement on the last remaining issues regarding the revision of the US-Japan "Security Treaty." Consequently, the formal negotiations between Japan and the United States to revise this treaty and establish an aggressive military alliance, which began on October 1, 1958, concluded today after one year and three months. Currently, the United States and the Nobusuke Kishi government are preparing to sign this new US-Japan military alliance treaty in Washington on the 19th.

### 不顾日本人民强烈反对  日美达成修改“安全条约”的协议

1960-01-07
第6版()
专栏：

    不顾日本人民强烈反对
    日美达成修改“安全条约”的协议
    据新华社6日讯　东京消息：日本外务相藤山爱一郎和美国驻日本大使麦克阿瑟第二今天举行第二十二次会谈，就修改日美“安全条约”的最后尚未解决的问题达成了完全的协议。因此，日美两国关于修改这个条约来建立侵略性军事同盟的正式谈判从1958年10月1日开始后经过一年零三个月的时间在今天已经结束。目前，美国和岸信介政府正在准备于19日在华盛顿签订这个新的日美军事同盟条约。
