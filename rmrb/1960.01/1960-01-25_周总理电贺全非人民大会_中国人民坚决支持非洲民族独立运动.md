Title: Premier Zhou Cables Congratulations to the All-African People's Conference: Chinese People Firmly Support the African National Independence Movement
Author:
Date: 1960-01-25
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, January 24th. Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, today sent a cable congratulating the convocation of the Second All-African People's Conference. The text of the cable is as follows:

    Tunis, Tunis City, All-African People's Conference:

    On the occasion of the convocation of the Second All-African People's Conference, I, on behalf of the Chinese government and people, and in my own name, extend warm congratulations to the conference.

    In recent years, the great African people, in their heroic struggle to win and safeguard national independence, have dealt a heavy blow to the forces of imperialism and colonialism, thereby making outstanding contributions to world peace, Afro-Asian solidarity, and the cause of human progress. The Chinese people resolutely stand on the side of the African people and vigorously support the African people's struggle for national independence until complete victory is achieved. I wish the conference success and that it will make new contributions to the further development of the African national independence movement.

        Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                      January 24, 1960, in Beijing

### 周总理电贺全非人民大会  中国人民坚决支持非洲民族独立运动

1960-01-25
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电贺全非人民大会
    中国人民坚决支持非洲民族独立运动
    新华社24日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天打电报祝贺第二届全非人民大会的召开。电文如下：突尼斯，突尼斯市，全非人民大会：
    在此第二届全非人民大会召开的时候，我谨代表中国政府和人民并以我个人名义向大会表示热烈的祝贺。
    近年来，伟大的非洲人民在争取和维护民族独立的英勇斗争中，给了帝国主义和殖民主义势力以沉重的打击，从而为世界和平、亚非团结和人类进步事业，作出了卓越的贡献。中国人民坚决地站在非洲人民一边，大力支持非洲人民的争取民族独立的斗争，直到取得完全的胜利。祝大会成功并为非洲民族独立运动的进一步发展作出新的贡献。
        中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                      1960年1月24日于北京
