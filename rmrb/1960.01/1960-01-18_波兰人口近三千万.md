Title: Polish Population Nears 30 Million
Author:
Date: 1960-01-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, January 17th. According to statistics from the Polish Central Statistical Office, the population of Poland at the end of 1959 was 29.5 million, an increase of approximately 500,000 compared to 1958.

Of Poland's total population, 15.3 million are women.

### 波兰人口近三千万

1960-01-18
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰人口近三千万
    新华社华沙17日电　据波兰中央统计局的统计，到1959年年底，波兰的人口为二千九百五十万人，大约比1958年增加了五十万人。
    在波兰的总人口中，妇女有一千五百三十万人。
