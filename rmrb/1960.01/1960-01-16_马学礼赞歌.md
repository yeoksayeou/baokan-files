Title: Ode to Ma Xue Li
Author:
Date: 1960-01-16
Page: 2

    Ode to Ma Xue Li
    Believe in the masses, believe in the Party
    Dare to innovate, have courage
    Step forward when difficulties arise
    Relinquish credit to others
    Learn from the advanced
    Help the backward

### 马学礼赞歌

1960-01-16
第2版()
专栏：

    马学礼赞歌
    相信群众相信党
    大胆革新有胆量
    见困难就上
    见荣誉就让
    见先进就学
    见后进就帮
