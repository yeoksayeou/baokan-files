Title: Voroshilov Departs for India at the Invitation of the Indian President and Government
Author:
Date: 1960-01-20
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 19th: TASS Moscow, 19th: The President of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Voroshilov, the First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Kozlov, and the Deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Furtseva, departed this morning by "Tu-O4" airplane for Delhi on a friendly visit at the invitation of the President of India, Prasad, and the Indian government.
    Those who went to Vnukovo Airport to see them off were: Aristov, Ignatov, Kuusinen, Mikoyan, Mukhitdinov, Suslov, Shvernik, Pospelov, Kosygin, Polyansky, ministers of various ministries, Marshals of the Soviet Union, foreign envoys, and many representatives of the working people of Moscow.

### 应印度总统和政府邀请  伏罗希洛夫启程赴印

1960-01-20
第6版()
专栏：

    应印度总统和政府邀请
    伏罗希洛夫启程赴印
    新华社19日讯　塔斯社莫斯科19日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫、苏联部长会议第一副主席科兹洛夫和苏联最高苏维埃代表福尔采娃，今天上午乘“图——○四”飞机前往德里作亲善访问。他们是应印度总统普拉沙德和印度政府的邀请前往印度访问的。
    前往伏努科夫机场欢送的有：阿里斯托夫、伊格纳托夫、库西宁、米高扬、穆希金诺夫、苏斯洛夫、什维尔尼克、波斯别洛夫、柯西金、波利扬斯基，各部部长、苏联元帅、外国使节和莫斯科劳动人民的许多代表。
