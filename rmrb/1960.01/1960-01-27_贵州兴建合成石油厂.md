Title: Guizhou to Build Synthetic Oil Plant
Author:
Date: 1960-01-27
Page: 2
Construction Flowers Welcome Spring Bloom

    Guizhou to Build Synthetic Oil Plant

    Guizhou Province's first synthetic oil plant recently started construction in Pingba County.

    The Guizhou Synthetic Oil Plant is one of the key construction projects of Guizhou Province this year. Once completed, it will be able to produce 50,000 tons of oil annually. The plant will use anthracite coal as its raw material.

### 贵州兴建合成石油厂

1960-01-27
第2版()
专栏：建设鲜花迎春开

    贵州兴建合成石油厂
    贵州省第一座合成石油厂最近在平坝县动工兴建。
    贵州合成石油厂是贵州省今年重点建设项目之一，建成以后，每年可以生产石油五万吨。这个厂将以无烟煤作原料。
