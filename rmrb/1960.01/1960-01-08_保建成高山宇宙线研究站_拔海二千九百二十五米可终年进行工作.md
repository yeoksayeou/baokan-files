Title: Ba Jiancheng Highland Cosmic Ray Research Station: 2,925 Meters Above Sea Level, Capable of Year-Round Operation
Author:
Date: 1960-01-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 7th. Bulgarian newspapers report that Bulgaria's first highland cosmic ray research station was recently established on Stalin Peak, at an altitude of 2,925 meters.

Currently, with the exception of the "Jungfrau" station in the Swiss Alps, the Bulgarian research station is the highest cosmic ray research station in Europe. However, the "Jungfrau" station can only operate in the summer because the Alps are inaccessible in winter. The research station on Stalin Peak, however, can operate year-round. The Bulgarian cosmic ray research station was built according to a design by Bulgarian engineer Petkov. It is a huge building occupying 187 square meters. The first floor is made of stone and concrete. The second floor houses offices and instrument rooms. To allow cosmic rays to freely penetrate the instruments, the second floor is made entirely of wood. The research station's equipment is electrified and maintains contact with nearby settlements via an electric elevator. Therefore, all supply problems to the research station can be solved even during the worst storms.

Currently, instrument installation is underway at the research station, with most of the instruments manufactured in Hungary. In addition to Bulgarian scientists, Hungarian scientists, led by the world-renowned physicist János Lajos, will also be working on Stalin Peak.

### 保建成高山宇宙线研究站  拔海二千九百二十五米可终年进行工作

1960-01-08
第5版()
专栏：

    保建成高山宇宙线研究站
    拔海二千九百二十五米可终年进行工作
    新华社索非亚7日电　据保加利亚报纸报道，不久前，在拔海二千九百二十五米的斯大林峰上，诞生了保加利亚第一座高山宇宙线研究站。
    目前，除瑞士阿尔卑斯山上的“少女”站外，保加利亚的研究站是欧洲最高的宇宙线研究站。但是，“少女”站只能在夏季工作，因为冬季阿尔卑斯山是攀登不上的。而这座斯大林峰上的研究站可终年工作。保加利亚的宇宙线研究站是根据保加利亚工程师彼得科夫设计建造的。这是一个占地一百八十七平方米的巨大建筑物。第一层是用石块和混凝土造成的。第二层是办公室和仪器室。为了使宇宙线能自由地透入仪器，第二层完全是用木制的。研究站的设备是电气化的，通过电力升降机与附近的居民点保持联系。因此在最大暴风雨时也能解决研究站的一切供应问题。
    目前研究站正在进行仪器安装工作，其中大部分的仪器都是匈牙利制造的。除保加利亚科学家之外，在斯大林峰上工作的还将有以世界闻名的物理学家亚诺希·拉约什为首的匈牙利科学家。
