Grafting Crops Without Manual Labor

The Grafting Machinery Scientific Research Group of Qionghai County, Hainan District, Guangdong Province, has recently successfully trial-produced two universal herbaceous crop grafting cutting machines.

This universal herbaceous crop grafting cutting machine has a simple structure and is easy to carry; it has high cutting efficiency and good grafting quality. This universal herbaceous crop grafting cutting machine can be applied to the grafting of melons and fruits such as: pumpkin, wax gourd, watermelon, bottle gourd, cantaloupe, bitter gourd, eggplant, tomato, potato, sweet potato, and yam.

### 嫁接作物不用手工
钟声
1960-01-07
第2版()
专栏：

    嫁接作物不用手工
    广东省海南区琼海县嫁接机械科学研究小组，最近试制成功两台万能草本作物嫁接切机。
    这种万能草本作物嫁接切机构造简单，携带便利；切削效率高；嫁接质量好。这种万能草本作物嫁接切机可以应用于：南瓜、东瓜、西瓜、蒲瓜、甜瓜、苦瓜、茄子、番茄、马铃薯、甘薯、红薯等瓜果嫁接。
