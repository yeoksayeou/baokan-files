International Briefs

    West German-Italian Talks Conclude
    Rome Dispatch: The talks between West German Chancellor Adenauer and Italian President Gronchi and Prime Minister Segni ended in Rome on the 24th. A joint communique issued after the talks stated that the two governments believe that "future East-West negotiations should be based on complete agreement among the Western allies." The communique said that both sides believe that "the Berlin issue should be resolved on this basis." However, the communique did not explicitly state whether the Italian government supports Adenauer on the Berlin issue.
    Lee Kuan Yew and Djuanda Issue Joint Communique
    Jakarta Dispatch: Singapore Prime Minister Lee Kuan Yew concluded his visit to Indonesia. He and Indonesian First Minister Djuanda issued a joint statement on their talks, saying that the Singapore government has taken and will continue to take the necessary steps to ensure that Indonesia's security is not undermined by activities based in Singapore. The Singapore government fully supports Indonesia's struggle to recover West Irian.

    国际简讯
    西德意大利会谈结束
    罗马讯　西德总理阿登纳同意大利总统格隆基和总理塞尼间的会谈24日在罗马结束。会谈结束后发表的联合公报说，两国政府认为，“今后的东西方谈判应该以西方盟国之间的完全一致为基础”，公报说，双方认为，“柏林问题应该在这个基础上解决”。但是，公报没有明确说明意大利政府在柏林问题上是否支持阿登纳。
    李光耀朱安达发表会谈公报
    雅加达讯　新加坡总理李光耀结束了对印度尼西亚的访问。他同印度尼西亚第一部长朱安达发表了关于双方会谈的联合声明说，新加坡政府已经采取并将继续采取必要的步骤，以保证印度尼西亚的安全不遭受以新加坡为基地的活动所破坏。新加坡政府完全支持印度尼西亚为收回西伊里安而作的斗争。
