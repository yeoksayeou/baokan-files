Title: Molomjamts and Luvsan Receive Chinese Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-01-13
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, January 12th: Molomjamts, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, and Deputy Chairman Luvsan received the Chinese government delegation headed by Xie Fusheng, the Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, on the 11th.
    The delegation will soon hand over a wool textile factory built with Chinese assistance to Mongolia. The wool textile factory is one of the projects built with Chinese assistance to Mongolia under the 1956 agreement on Chinese economic and technical assistance to Mongolia.

### 莫洛姆扎木茨和鲁布桑接见我国政府代表团

1960-01-13
第6版()
专栏：

    莫洛姆扎木茨和鲁布桑接见我国政府代表团
    新华社乌兰巴托12日电  蒙古人民共和国部长会议第一副主席莫洛姆扎木茨和副主席鲁布桑11日接见了以中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生为首的中国政府代表团。
    这个代表团最近将把中国帮助蒙古建设的一座毛纺织厂移交给蒙古。这座毛纺织厂是根据1956年签订的关于中国给予蒙古经济和技术援助的协定，由中国帮助蒙古建设的项目之一。
