Title: The Soviet Union Abolishes the All-Union Ministry of Internal Affairs; Powers Transferred to the Republics

Author:
Date: 1960-01-15
Page: 6

    The Soviet Union Abolishes the All-Union Ministry of Internal Affairs
    Powers Transferred to the Republics
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 14th: TASS, Moscow, January 14th: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, at the request of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, has abolished the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Soviet Union and transferred its powers to the Ministries of Internal Affairs of the various republics.

### 苏联撤销全苏内务部  职权移交加盟共和国

1960-01-15
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联撤销全苏内务部
    职权移交加盟共和国
    新华社莫斯科14日电　塔斯社莫斯科14日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团根据苏联部长会议的请求，撤销了苏联内务部，将其职权移交给各加盟共和国内务部。
