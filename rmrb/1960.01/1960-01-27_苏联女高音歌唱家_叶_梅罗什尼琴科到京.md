Title: Soviet Soprano Ye. Meloshnichenko Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-01-27
Page: 5

    Soviet Soprano
    Ye. Meloshnichenko Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, 25th — Soviet soprano Ye. Meloshnichenko and pianist Yerovayeva arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. Ye. Meloshnichenko won second place in the 1958 French Toulouse International Singing Competition. This time she is visiting China in accordance with the 1960 Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement.
    Ye. Meloshnichenko will hold her first performance in China on the 30th at the auditorium of the National Culture Palace.

### 苏联女高音歌唱家  叶·梅罗什尼琴科到京

1960-01-27
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联女高音歌唱家
    叶·梅罗什尼琴科到京
    新华社25日讯　苏联女高音歌唱家叶·梅罗什尼琴科和钢琴家耶尔沃娃，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。叶·梅罗什尼琴科曾经荣获1958年法国土鲁斯国际歌唱比赛第二名。这次她是根据1960年中苏文化合作协定来中国访问的。
    叶·梅罗什尼琴科30日将在民族文化宫礼堂举行访华首次演出。
