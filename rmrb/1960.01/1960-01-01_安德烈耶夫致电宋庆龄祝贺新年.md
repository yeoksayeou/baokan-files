Title: Andreyev Sends New Year Greetings to Soong Ching Ling
Author:
Date: 1960-01-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 31st. Andreyev, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, sent a telegram to Soong Ching Ling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, on December 30th to congratulate her on the New Year of 1960.

The telegram said that the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association cordially extends to all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and to you personally, Comrade Soong Ching Ling, congratulations on the New Year of 1960.

Dear friends, please accept our best wishes to you, and through you to all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and wish you further achievements in the great cause of building socialism and strengthening world peace.

### 安德烈耶夫致电宋庆龄祝贺新年

1960-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

    安德烈耶夫致电宋庆龄祝贺新年
    新华社31日讯　苏中友好协会理事会主席安德烈耶夫12月30日打电报给中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄，祝贺1960年新年。
    电文说，苏中友好协会理事会衷心地向中苏友好协会全体会员和您个人，宋庆龄同志，祝贺1960年新年。
    亲爱的朋友们，谨向你们，并通过你们向中苏友好协会全体会员表示最良好的祝愿，祝你们在建设社会主义和加强世界和平的伟大事业中获得进一步的成就。
