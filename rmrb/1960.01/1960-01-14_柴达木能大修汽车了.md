Title: Qaidam Can Overhaul Cars Now
Author:
Date: 1960-01-14
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency

    Qaidam Can Overhaul Cars Now
    (Xinhua News Agency) A car repair factory that can overhaul eight hundred to one thousand vehicles annually has recently been completed and officially put into production in Golmud City, Qaidam Basin.
    After this factory is officially put into production, cars driving in the Qaidam Basin and on the Qinghai-Tibet Highway can be overhauled nearby, without having to go to Xining and Lanzhou for major repairs.

### 柴达木能大修汽车了

1960-01-14
第2版()
专栏：

    柴达木能大修汽车了
    据新华社讯　一座每年可以大修八百到一千辆车辆的汽车修理厂，最近在柴达木盆地葛尔穆市建成并正式投入生产。
    这个工厂正式投入生产后，行驶在柴达木盆地和青藏公路上的汽车，便可以就近大修，不必到西宁和兰州去大修了。
