Title: Lao Army Intensifies Military Control After Seizing Power; Announces New Government to be Formed Soon
Author:
Date: 1960-01-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 4th: According to Vientiane Radio, the spokesman of the Lao Royal Armed Forces General Headquarters, Sisouk Na Champassak, announced at a press conference this morning that the Kingdom of Laos will rapidly form a new government in two or three days. As previously reported, the extreme pro-American "Committee for the Defense of National Interests" of Laos and the heads of the Royal Armed Forces have seized power by force.

According to the announcement, the General Headquarters of the Royal Armed Forces wrote to the French Ambassador to Laos on January 2, notifying him that the army had taken control of national leadership. The letter requested the French Ambassador to convey this matter to the French government and the diplomatic corps in Vientiane.

In another communique, Phatamavong announced that the army would severely punish "anyone who takes actions that undermine national peace and order and create difficulties for national security." Sources say that the General Headquarters of the Royal Armed Forces has implemented military censorship of news reports.

### 老挝军队攫取政权后加紧军事控制  宣布不久就要成立新政府

1960-01-05
第5版()
专栏：

    老挝军队攫取政权后加紧军事控制
    宣布不久就要成立新政府
    据新华社河内4日电　据万象电台广播，老挝王国军队总司令部发言人薛苏·纳占巴塞今天上午在记者招待会上宣布，老挝王国将在两三天以后迅速成立新政府。前已报道，极端亲美的老挝“保卫国家利益委员会”和王国军队的首脑们已经用武力攫取了政权。
    据宣布，王国军队总司令部已经在1月2日写信通知法国驻老挝大使，军队已经掌握国家领导权。信里要求法国大使把这件事转告法国政府和万象的外交团。
    巴坦马冯在另一项公报中宣布，军队将严厉惩罚“采取破坏国家安宁、秩序，为国家的安全制造困难的行动的任何人。”有消息说，王国军队总司令部已经对新闻报道实施军事检查。
