Title: Hungarian Presidential Council Appoints Government Personnel: Kállai Gyula Appointed First Deputy Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1960-01-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, according to "People's Freedom News" reports, the Presidential Council of the Hungarian People's Republic held a meeting on the 15th. Based on the recommendations of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, the Workers' and Peasants’ Revolutionary government, and the Presidium of the National Council of the Patriotic People's Front, the Presidential Council relieved Kállai Gyula and Marosán György of their positions as Ministers of State, and Dőgei Imre of his position as Minister of Agriculture. The Presidential Council appointed Kállai Gyula as First Deputy Prime Minister.

Losonczi Pál, member of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and president of the Red Star Agricultural Production Cooperative, winner of the Kossuth Prize, was appointed Minister of Agriculture. Faluvégi Lajos, member of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and Chairman of the National Cooperative Council, was appointed Minister of Finance.

With the consent of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, the Workers' and Peasants’ revolutionary government, and the Presidium of the National Council of the Patriotic People's Front, the Presidential council of the Hungarian People's Republic recommended that the next National Assembly elect Marosán as a member of the Presidential Council of the People's Republic.

The Presidential Council relieved Nográdi Sándor of his position as Ambassador to the People's Republic of China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; and appointed Dőgei Imre as Ambassador to the People's Republic of China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

### 匈主席团任命政府人员  卡拉伊·久洛任第一副总理

1960-01-19
第6版()
专栏：

    匈主席团任命政府人员
    卡拉伊·久洛任第一副总理
    新华社布达佩斯电　据“人民自由报”报道，匈牙利人民共和国主席团15日举行会议。根据社会主义工人党中央委员会、工农革命政府和爱国人民阵线全国委员会主席团的建议，主席团免去卡拉伊·久洛和马罗山·捷尔吉国务部长的职务，以及德盖伊·伊姆雷农业部长的职务。主席团任命卡拉伊·久洛为第一副总理。
    匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员、红星农业生产合作社荣获科苏特奖金的社长洛绍契·帕耳被任命为农业部长，社会主义工人党中央委员、全国合作社联合会主席?尔什·雷热被任命为财政部长。
    匈牙利人民共和国主席团在社会主义工人党、工农革命政府和爱国人民阵线全国委员会主席团的同意下，建议下届国民议会选马罗山为人民共和国主席团委员。
    主席团免去诺格拉第·山多尔驻中华人民共和国和越南民主共和国大使的职务；并任命德盖伊·伊姆雷为驻中华人民共和国和越南民主共和国的大使。
