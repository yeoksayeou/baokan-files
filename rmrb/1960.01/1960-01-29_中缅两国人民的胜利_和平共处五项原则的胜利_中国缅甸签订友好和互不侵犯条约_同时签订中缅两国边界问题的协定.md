Title: The Victory of the Chinese and Burmese People, The Victory of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence: China and Burma Sign Treaty of Friendship and Non-Aggression; Agreement on the Boundary Question Between China and Burma Signed Simultaneously
Author:
Date: 1960-01-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 28th -- The "Agreement Between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Union of Burma on the Question of the Boundary Between the Two Countries" and the "Treaty of Friendship and Non-Aggression Between the People's Republic of China and the Union of Burma" were signed simultaneously in Beijing at 6:50 p.m. today.

Representing the Chinese side in signing the agreement and treaty was Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China; representing the Burmese side in signing the agreement and treaty was Ne Win, Prime Minister of the Union of Burma.

Chairman Liu Shaoqi attended the signing ceremony.

Also participating in the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were Chen Yi and Tan Zhenlin, Vice Premiers of the State Council; Huang Yanpei, Chen Shutong, and Panchen Erdeni Chökyi Gyaltsen, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Zhang Hanfu and Geng Biao, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs; General Zhang Aiping, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Li Yimang, Chinese Ambassador to Burma; Zhang Wenjin, Director of the First Asian Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Yao Zhongming, Director of the Department of Treaty and Law; Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department; Pu Shouchang, Secretary of the Premier's Office; and Colonel Jiang Keding, Military Attaché of our Embassy in Burma.

Participating in the signing ceremony on the Burmese side were U Chan Tun Aung, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Union of Burma; U Ba Swe, Attorney General of the Union of Burma; Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Deputy Chief of Staff; Barrington, Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; U Win Pe, Secretary to the Prime Minister; U Khin Nyo, Joint Secretary of the Border Areas Administration; U Hla Kin Maung, Director of Survey; Lieutenant Colonel Lun, Director of Military Intelligence; U Ba Chii, Director of the Asian-African Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; U Soe Htin, Director of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and U Tun Tin, Deputy Director of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, etc. The Burmese Ambassador to China, Kyao Winn, and Military Attachés Lieutenant Colonel Liu Tang and Lieutenant Colonel Kyin Shamen, also attended the signing ceremony.

After the signing, the guests and hosts warmly shook hands and offered each other champagne in celebration. (Attached picture)

    The picture shows Premier Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister Ne Win signing the agreement and treaty on behalf of their respective sides.
                  　Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Qingbiao

### 中缅两国人民的胜利　和平共处五项原则的胜利  中国缅甸签订友好和互不侵犯条约  同时签订中缅两国边界问题的协定

1960-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中缅两国人民的胜利　和平共处五项原则的胜利
    中国缅甸签订友好和互不侵犯条约  同时签订中缅两国边界问题的协定
    新华社28日讯　“中华人民共和国政府和缅甸联邦政府关于两国边界问题的协定”和“中华人民共和国和缅甸联邦之间的友好和互不侵犯条约”，今天下午六时五十分同时在北京签字。
    代表中国方面在协定和条约上签字的，是中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来；代表缅甸方面在协定和条约上签字的，是缅甸联邦总理奈温。
    刘少奇主席参加了签字仪式。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的，还有国务院副总理陈毅、谭震林，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长黄炎培、陈叔通、班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞，外交部副部长章汉夫、耿飚，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张爱萍上将，中国驻缅甸大使李一氓，外交部第一亚洲司司长章文晋、条约法律司司长姚仲明、礼宾司司长俞沛文，总理办公室秘书浦寿昌，我国驻缅甸大使馆武官蒋克定上校。
    缅甸方面参加签字仪式的，有缅甸联邦外交部长吴千吞昂，缅甸联邦检察总长吴巴盛，副总参谋长昂季准将，外交部常任秘书巴林顿，总理秘书吴温佩，边境地区管理局联合秘书吴钦纽，测绘局长吴拉钦貌，军事情报处长伦中校，外交部亚非司长吴巴漆，外交部司长吴苏幸，外交部副司长吴吞丁等。缅甸驻中国大使叫温和大使馆武官柳当上校、敬沙门上校，也参加了签字仪式。
    签字以后，宾主热情握手，并互进香槟酒祝贺。（附图片）
