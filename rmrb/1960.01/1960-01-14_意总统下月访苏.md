Title: Italian President to Visit Soviet Union Next Month
Author: 
Date: 1960-01-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 12th: TASS Moscow announced that the President of the Italian Republic, Giovanni Gronchi, will arrive in Moscow on February 6th and will visit the Soviet Union until February 11th.
    This decision was made after President Gronchi was unable to visit the Soviet Union on the originally scheduled date of January 8th due to illness, and after an exchange of views between the Soviet and Italian sides.

### 意总统下月访苏

1960-01-14
第6版()
专栏：

    意总统下月访苏
    新华社13日讯　塔斯社莫斯科12日讯：莫斯科正式宣布，意大利共和国总统格隆基将于2月6日到达莫斯科，并将在苏联访问到2月11日。
    这一决定是格隆基总统因病不能在原定1月8日到苏联访问之后，根据苏意双方交换意见的结果作出的。
