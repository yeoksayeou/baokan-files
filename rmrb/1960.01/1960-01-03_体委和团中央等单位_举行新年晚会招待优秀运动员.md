Title: The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission and the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League and other Units Hold New Year's Party to Entertain Outstanding Athletes
Author:
Date: 1960-01-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd -- The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, the Beijing Physical Culture and Sports Commission, and the Beijing Committee of the Communist Youth League held a New Year's party at the Beijing Hotel tonight to entertain outstanding athletes.

Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers He Long, Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, Bo Yibo, Luo Ruiqing, and Xi Zhongxun, as well as responsible persons from the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and other units, attended the New Year's party. A total of two thousand people attended the party.

### 体委和团中央等单位  举行新年晚会招待优秀运动员

1960-01-03
第4版()
专栏：

    体委和团中央等单位
    举行新年晚会招待优秀运动员
    新华社2日讯　国家体委、共青团中央委员会、北京市体委、共青团北京市委员会今晚在北京饭店举行新年联欢晚会，招待优秀运动员。
    周恩来总理、贺龙、陈毅、李先念、薄一波、罗瑞卿、习仲勋副总理，以及国家体委、团中央等单位的负责人，出席了联欢晚会。出席晚会的共有二千人。
