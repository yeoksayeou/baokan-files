    Universal Celebration
    Guo Moruo

    Under the brilliant illumination of the General Line, 1959 saw another Great Leap Forward on the foundation of the Great Leap Forward of 1958. The four major indicators of steel, coal, grain, and cotton, as well as other important products, exceeded the targets of the Second Five-Year Plan in two years. Therefore, I composed four "Sixteen-Character Poems" to celebrate.

    One
    Steel,
    Charging ahead on the battlefield,
    Surging fiercely,
    Heat waves roll the red outline!

    Two
    Coal,
    Gushing black gold emerges from the earth's core,
    True strength bold,
    Fierce flames spew bright brilliance!

    Three
    Grain,
    The power of the People's Communes is displayed,
    Celebrating the bumper harvest,
    Overthrowing the great famine!

    Four
    Cotton,
    The Five-Year Plan completed in two years,
    Six hundred million people,
    The world knows no cold!
                        January 24, 1960

### 普天同庆
郭沫若
1960-01-28
第8版()
专栏：

    普天同庆
    郭沫若
    在总路线的光辉照耀之下，1959年在1958年的大跃进的基础之上又继续大跃进。钢煤粮棉四大指标和其他重要生产品都以两年工夫超额完成了第二个五年计划。爰成“十六字令”四首，以志庆祝。
    一
    钢，
    一马当先上战场，
    奔腾烈，
    热浪卷红纲！
    二
    煤，
    滚滚乌金出地维，
    真力猛，
    烈焰吐明辉！
    三
    粮，
    人民公社威力扬，
    丰收庆，
    打倒大灾荒！
    四
    棉，
    五年计划两年完，
    人六亿，
    天下不知寒！
                        1960年1月24日
