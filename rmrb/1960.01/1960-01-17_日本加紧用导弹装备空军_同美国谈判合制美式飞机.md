Title: Japan Intensifies Efforts to Equip Air Force with Missiles; Negotiating with the United States to Jointly Manufacture American Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1960-01-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo: Japan and the United States began negotiations in Tokyo on the 12th regarding the joint venture manufacture of "Lockheed F104J" jet fighters. This is another step by the Nobusuke Kishi government to accelerate the equipping of missiles by relying on US funds and technology.

The Nobusuke Kishi government plans to have Japanese arms companies produce 200 "Lockheed F104J" jet fighters to arm the Japanese Air Force during the period from 1960 to 1965. This type of aircraft is capable of carrying "Sidewinder" air-to-air missiles.

### 日本加紧用导弹装备空军  同美国谈判合制美式飞机

1960-01-17
第4版()
专栏：

    日本加紧用导弹装备空军
    同美国谈判合制美式飞机
    据新华社讯　东京消息：日本和美国12日在东京开始谈判两国合资制造“洛克希德F104J”式喷气战斗机问题。这是岸信介政府依靠美国资金和技术加紧装备导弹的又一个步骤。
    岸信介政府准备在1960年到1965年期间由日本军火公司生产二百架“洛克希德F104J”式喷气战斗机来武装日本空军。这种飞机能够装载“响尾蛇”空对空导弹。
