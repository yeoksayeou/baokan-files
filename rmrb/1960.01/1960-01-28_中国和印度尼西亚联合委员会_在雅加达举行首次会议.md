Title: China-Indonesia Joint Committee Holds First Meeting in Jakarta
Author:
Date: 1960-01-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, January 27th: The China-Indonesia Joint Committee, responsible for implementing the treaty between China and Indonesia on the issue of dual nationality, held its first meeting this morning in Jakarta, presided over by the Indonesian Chief Representative, Susanto Tirtoprodjo.

Susanto Tirtoprodjo announced after the meeting, based on mutual agreement, that the meeting studied the working procedures and methods of the Joint Committee and decided that the chairmanship would be held alternately by the chief representatives of China and Indonesia in the future.

The Joint Committee will meet once a week, and more meetings will be held when necessary. The next meeting will be held on February 3rd.

Today's meeting was conducted in a friendly atmosphere.

### 中国和印度尼西亚联合委员会  在雅加达举行首次会议

1960-01-28
第5版()
专栏：

    中国和印度尼西亚联合委员会
    在雅加达举行首次会议
    新华社雅加达27日电　负责实施中国和印度尼西亚关于双重国籍问题的条约的中国和印度尼西亚联合委员会今天上午在印度尼西亚首席代表苏山多·蒂托普罗佐主持下举行第一次会议。
    苏山多·蒂托普罗佐根据双方协议在会后宣布，会议研究了联合委员会的工作程序和工作方法问题，并决定以后将由中国和印度尼西亚首席代表轮流担任主席。
    联合委员会的会议将每星期举行一次，在必要时将举行更多的会议。下次会议将于2月3日举行。
    今天的会议是在友好的气氛中进行的。
