Title: Sino-German Talks Held
Author:
Date: 1960-01-11
Page: 1

    Sino-German Talks Held
    Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Li Xiannian, and Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign and Intra-German Trade of the German Democratic Republic, Heinrich Rau, held talks this afternoon.
    The following members of the Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic participated in the talks on the German Democratic Republic side: Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Paul Verner; First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Otto Winzer; and Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Wang De.
    The following participated in the talks on the Chinese side: Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zeng Yongquan; Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission, Liu Mingfu; Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Lin Haiyun; and Deputy Director of the Scientific and Technological Commission, Wu Heng.
    The talks were conducted in a cordial and friendly atmosphere.
    This morning, all the distinguished guests of the delegation visited the magnificent buildings such as the Great Hall of the People, the History Museum, the National Culture Palace, and the Beijing Railway Station.

### 中德双方举行会谈

1960-01-11
第1版()
专栏：

    中德双方举行会谈
    新华社10日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理李先念和德意志民主共和国副总理兼对外与东西德贸易部长海因里希·劳，在今天下午举行了会谈。
    德意志民主共和国方面参加会谈的有德意志民主共和国政府代表团全体团员：德国统一社会党中央政治局候补委员保罗·维尔纳，外交部第一副部长奥托·文策尔，德意志民主共和国驻中国大使汪戴尔。
    中国方面参加会谈的有：外交部副部长曾涌泉，国家计划委员会副主任刘明夫，对外贸易部副部长林海云，科学技术委员会副主任武衡。
    会谈是在亲切、友好的气氛中进行的。
    今天上午，代表团全体贵宾参观了人民大会堂、历史博物馆、民族文化宫和北京火车站等宏伟的建筑。
