Title: Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks Continue

Author: 
Date: 1960-01-20
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, January 19th: The ninety-fifth meeting of the Sino-US ambassadorial talks was held in Warsaw at 2:00 PM today. The meeting lasted for two hours.
    After the meeting, both sides announced that the next meeting will be held at 2:00 PM on February 23rd.

### 中美大使会谈继续举行

1960-01-20
第6版()
专栏：

    中美大使会谈继续举行
    新华社华沙19日电　中美两国大使级会谈第九十五次会议，今天下午二时在华沙举行。会议历时二小时。
    会后双方宣布，下次会议将在2月23日下午二时举行。
