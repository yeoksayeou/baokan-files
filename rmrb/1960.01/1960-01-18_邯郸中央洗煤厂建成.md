Title: Handan Central Coal Washery Completed
Author:
Date: 1960-01-18
Page: 2

The Handan Central Coal Washery, a modern large-scale coal washery, has recently been officially put into production.

The Handan Central Coal Washery is the first large-scale modern coal washing plant designed and constructed by our country itself. The entire production process is highly mechanized and automated.

### 邯郸中央洗煤厂建成

1960-01-18
第2版()
专栏：

    邯郸中央洗煤厂建成
    一座现代化大型洗煤厂——邯郸中央洗煤厂已于最近正式投入生产。
    邯郸中央洗煤厂是我国自己设计和施工的第一座大型现代化洗选厂。整个生产过程是高度机械化、自动化的。
