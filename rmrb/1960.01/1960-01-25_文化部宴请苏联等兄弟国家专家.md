Title: Ministry of Culture Hosts Banquet for Experts from Soviet and Other Fraternal Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-01-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 24th: The Ministry of Culture held a get-together this evening at the Peace Hotel, hosting a banquet for all the Soviet, Czechoslovak, and German Democratic Republic experts in Beijing working at the Ministry of Culture, celebrating the upcoming Spring Festival.

Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture and Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, wished everyone a happy Spring Festival at the meeting, and congratulated everyone on the upcoming tenth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance.

Soviet Embassy in China Counselor Sudarikov and Soviet expert Kravchenko, who were invited to attend the banquet, spoke at the meeting.

After the banquet, Chinese artists performed wonderful songs, dances, music, folk art and other programs.

### 文化部宴请苏联等兄弟国家专家

1960-01-25
第4版()
专栏：

    文化部宴请苏联等兄弟国家专家
    新华社24日讯　文化部今晚在和平宾馆举行联欢会，宴请文化部所有在北京的苏联、捷克斯洛伐克和德意志民主共和国的专家，欢庆即将来临的春节。
    文化部部长、中苏友好协会总会副会长沈雁冰在会上祝大家愉快地度过春节；并对即将来临的中苏友好同盟互助条约签定十周年表示庆贺。
    应邀出席宴会的苏联驻华大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫和苏联专家克拉夫钦科在会上讲了话。
    宴会后，中国艺术家们表演了歌舞、音乐、曲艺等精采节目。
