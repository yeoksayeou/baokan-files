Title: Baghdad Holds Grand Parade to Celebrate Iraqi Army Day
Author:
Date: 1960-01-08
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, January 6th: A grand parade with hundreds of thousands of participants was held in Baghdad, the capital of Iraq, on the 6th to celebrate Army Day.

    The parade was organized by representatives of patriotic forces. This was the first parade since last July in which all social and professional groups participated. Peace warriors led the march, followed by the Lawyers' Union. Various farmers' associations responded to the call of the chairman of the General Federation of Farmers' Associations and participated in the parade.

    The marchers shouted slogans celebrating Army Day, promoting unity between the army and the people, opposing the Iranian government's troop buildup on the Iraqi border, supporting the restoration of the rights of the Arabs in Palestine who have been deprived, and paying tribute to the Algerian and Omani people in combat. Prime Minister Qasim, along with cabinet ministers, walked onto the balcony in front of the Ministry of Defense and greeted the crowd.

### 庆祝伊拉克陆军节  巴格达举行盛大游行

1960-01-08
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝伊拉克陆军节
    巴格达举行盛大游行
    据新华社巴格达6日电　伊拉克首都巴格达在6日举行了有几十万人参加的盛大游行庆祝陆军节。
    这次游行是由爱国力量的代表组织的。这是从去年7月以来第一次所有的社会和专业团体都参加的游行。和平战士的队伍走在行列的最前面。随后是律师联合会的队伍。各农民协会响应农民协会总联合会主席的号召参加了游行。
    游行的人高呼庆祝陆军节、军民团结、反对伊朗政府在伊拉克边境集结军队、支持恢复巴勒斯坦的阿拉伯人被剥夺的权利、向在战斗中的阿尔及利亚人民和阿曼人民致敬的口号。卡塞姆总理同内阁部长们一起走上国防部前面的阳台，向群众致意。
