Title: Algerian National Liberation Army Launches Heroic Attacks
Author:
Date: 1960-01-27
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, January 23rd: The Supreme Command of the Algerian National Liberation Army today issued a military communique here stating that, between January 14th and 21st, the Algerian National Liberation Army killed 145 French soldiers and wounded 31. Another 133 French soldiers and six Foreign Legion soldiers surrendered to the National Liberation Army with their weapons. At the same time, the Algerian National Liberation Army destroyed 11 French tanks and 16 armored vehicles, and burned down seven French military camps, four houses, and three oil depots.

The communique said that during this period, the Algerian National Liberation Army engaged in fierce battles with the French army in many places. Algerian commandoes attacked three enemy centers and completely destroyed them.

The communique said that eight Algerian nationalists held out against a battalion of enemy troops for six hours. After killing 35 enemy soldiers, they heroically sacrificed themselves.

The communique also said that the Algerian National Liberation Army opened a large gap in the electric grid set up by the French army on the western border of Algeria, and also broke through the electric grid of the French "Maurice Line" on the eastern border.

### 阿民族解放军英勇出击

1960-01-27
第6版()
专栏：

    阿民族解放军英勇出击
    新华社开罗23日电　阿尔及利亚民族解放军最高司令部今天在这里发表的一项军事公报说，在1月14日到21日期间，阿尔及利亚民族解放军共打死法军一百四十五名，打伤三十一名。另有一百三十三名法军和六名外籍军团的士兵携带武器向民族解放军投诚。同时，阿尔及利亚民族解放军还摧毁法军十一辆坦克和十六辆装甲车，焚毁法军七所军营、四所房子和三所油库。
    公报说，在这期间，阿尔及利亚民族解放军在许多地方同法军进行了猛烈的战斗。阿尔及利亚突击队袭击了敌军的三个中心，并把它们彻底摧毁。
    公报说，八个阿尔及利亚民族主义者同一营敌军相持了六小时。他们在击毙了三十五名敌军以后，壮烈牺牲。
    公报还说，阿尔及利亚民族解放军在阿尔及利亚西部边境把法军设立的电网打开了大缺口，并且还突破了东部边境的法军“莫里斯防线”的电网。
