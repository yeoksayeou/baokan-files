Title: Voroshilov Arrives in India for Visit, People of New Delhi Line the Streets to Welcome Soviet Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-01-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 20th: At the invitation of Indian President Prasad, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Voroshilov, arrived in New Delhi today for a visit. First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Kozlov, and representative of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Furtseva, also arrived on the same plane at the invitation of the Indian government. Voroshilov and his party were warmly welcomed at the airport by Indian President Prasad, Prime Minister Nehru, government ministers, senior officials, prominent figures from all walks of life, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to India.

Tens of thousands of people in the Indian capital warmly welcomed the Soviet leaders today with countless garlands, enthusiastic slogans, and sustained applause. They gathered early this morning from all directions near the airport and along the streets, waiting to welcome the distinguished guests.

In his welcoming speech at the airport, President Prasad said that Voroshilov's visit fulfilled a long-held wish of India. "I am confident that your visit will further strengthen the ties between our two countries."

Amidst enthusiastic cheers, Voroshilov delivered a reply. He said that he hoped his visit "will help promote mutual understanding and friendship between our two countries." He said, "The Soviet Union firmly believes that all contentious issues in international relations can and must be resolved only through peaceful means."

Along the nine-mile road from the airport to the city center, the Soviet guests were greeted by dense crowds of citizens lining the streets. They shouted slogans such as "Long live the friendship between India and the Soviet Union!"

### 伏罗希洛夫到达印度访问  新德里人民夹道欢迎苏联贵宾

1960-01-21
第5版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫到达印度访问
    新德里人民夹道欢迎苏联贵宾
    据新华社新德里20日电　苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫应印度总统普拉沙德的邀请，今天到达新德里访问。苏联部长会议第一副主席科兹洛夫，苏联最高苏维埃代表福尔采娃等应印度政府的邀请也同机到达。伏罗希洛夫等一行在机场受到印度总统普拉沙德、总理尼赫鲁、政府部长们、高级官员，各界著名人士以及各国驻印度外交使节的热烈欢迎。
    成千上万的印度首都人民今天以无数的花环、热诚的口号和经久不息的掌声热烈欢迎苏联领导人。他们今天一清早就从四面八方聚集到机场附近和街道两旁等待着欢迎贵宾。
    普拉沙德总统在机场上致欢迎词时说，伏罗希洛夫的访问使印度的一个宿愿实现了。“我确信，您的这次访问将使我们两国关系更加密切”。
    在热烈的欢呼声中伏罗希洛夫致答词。他说，希望他这次访问“将有助于促进我们两国之间的相互了解和友谊”。他说，“苏联坚决认为，一切引起争论的国际关系问题，能够而且必须只通过和平手段予以解决。”
    在从机场通往市区的九英里的路上，苏联客人们受到密密层层的市民的夹道欢迎。他们高呼“印度和苏联的友谊万岁”等口号。
