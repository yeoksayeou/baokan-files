Title: Deputy Prime Minister Heinrich Rau Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-01-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, January 24th: After concluding their visit to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign and Intra-German Trade of the German Democratic Republic, Heinrich Rau, and the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic he led, arrived in Guangzhou this afternoon from Pyongyang by special plane en route to a visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

    The national flags of the German Democratic Republic and China were flying at the airport today. The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at the airport by Zhao Ziyang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Zhu Guang, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province and Mayor of Guangzhou; Wei Jinfei, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province; Major General Lai Chunfeng, head of the Guangzhou Garrison, and responsible persons from provincial and municipal departments. Children from Guangzhou presented flowers to the distinguished guests.

    Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of our country, who had come to Guangzhou by plane from Beijing especially to greet the delegation, was also present at the airport to welcome them today.

    Paul Wandel, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, also arrived in Guangzhou from Beijing by plane to welcome the delegation.

    Consular personnel from various countries stationed in Guangzhou and experts from the German Democratic Republic working in Guangzhou also came to the airport to greet them.

    In the afternoon, accompanied by Zhu Guang, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province and Mayor of Guangzhou, the distinguished guests visited the Yonghan North Road commercial district, the Xidi Department Store, and toured the city. In the evening, Tao Zhu, member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and First Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, met with Deputy Prime Minister Heinrich Rau at the Guangdong Guest House and hosted a banquet to welcome all the distinguished guests of the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic.

### 海因里希·劳副总理到广州

1960-01-25
第4版()
专栏：

    海因里希·劳副总理到广州
    新华社广州24日电　德意志民主共和国副总理兼对外与东西德贸易部部长海因里希·劳和他所率领的德意志民主共和国政府代表团，结束了在朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的访问后，在前往越南民主共和国访问途中今天下午自平壤乘专机到达广州。
    机场上今天悬挂着德意志民主共和国和中国两国的国旗。贵宾们在机场受到了中共广东省委书记处书记赵紫阳，广东省副省长、广州市市长朱光，广东省副省长魏今非，驻广州部队负责人赖春风少将以及省、市各部门负责人的热烈欢迎。广州市的少年儿童向贵宾献了鲜花。
    今天，前往机场欢迎的还有特地从北京乘飞机来广州迎接代表团的我国外交部礼宾司副司长马振武。
    德意志民主共和国驻华大使保罗·汪戴尔也从北京乘飞机到广州迎接代表团。
    各国驻广州的领事人员和德意志民主共和国在广州工作的专家也到机场迎接。
    下午，贵宾们在广东省副省长、广州市市长朱光陪同下，参观了永汉北路商业区，西堤百货商店，并参观了市容。当晚，中共中央委员、中共广东省委第一书记陶铸在广东省迎宾馆会见了海因里希·劳副总理，并设宴欢迎德意志民主共和国政府代表团的全体贵宾。
