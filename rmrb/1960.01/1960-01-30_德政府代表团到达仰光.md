Title: German Government Delegation Arrives in Rangoon
Author:
Date: 1960-01-30
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 29th. A government delegation from the German Democratic Republic, led by Deputy Prime Minister and Minister for Foreign and Intra-German Trade, Heinrich Rau, arrived in Rangoon this afternoon at the invitation of the Burmese government.

Senior Burmese government officials greeted the delegation at the airport. Diplomatic envoys from some countries to Burma were also present at the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 德政府代表团到达仰光

1960-01-30
第3版()
专栏：

    德政府代表团到达仰光
    新华社仰光29日电　由副总理兼对外与东西德贸易部长海因里希·劳率领的德意志民主共和国政府代表团应缅甸政府的邀请，在今天下午到达仰光。
    缅甸政府的高级官员到机场欢迎代表团。到机场欢迎代表团的，还有一些国家驻缅甸的外交使节。
