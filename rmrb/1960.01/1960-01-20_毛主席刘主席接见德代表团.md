Title: Chairman Mao and Chairman Liu Receive the German Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-01-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, January 19th: During the visit of the Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic to Shanghai, Chairman Mao Zedong and Chairman Liu Shaoqi received the head of the delegation, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign and Intra-German Trade of the German Democratic Republic, member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, and member of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, Heinrich Rau, and all members of the delegation: Alternate member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, First Secretary of the Greater Berlin District Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, member of the National Front Committee of Democratic Germany, and member of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, Paul Verner, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, member of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, and member of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, Otto Winzer, and Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Paul Wandel.

Chairman Mao Zedong and Chairman Liu Shaoqi had a cordial conversation with the distinguished guests.

Also present at the meeting were Ke Qingshi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, First Secretary of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee, and Mayor of Shanghai, and Zeng Yongquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs. (Attached photo)

Chairman Mao Zedong and Chairman Liu Shaoqi posing for a group photo when receiving the Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic. The third person from the left is Deputy Prime Minister Heinrich Rau. Xinhua News Agency reporters Hou Bo and Meng Qingbiao

### 毛主席刘主席接见德代表团

1960-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席刘主席接见德代表团
    新华社上海19日电　在德意志民主共和国政府代表团访问上海期间，毛泽东主席和刘少奇主席接见了代表团团长、德意志民主共和国副总理兼对外与东西德贸易部部长、德国统一社会党中央政治局委员、德意志民主共和国人民议院议员海因里希·劳和代表团全体团员：德国统一社会党中央政治局候补委员、德国统一社会党大柏林专区委员会第一书记、民主德国全国阵线委员会委员、德意志民主共和国人民议院议员保罗·维尔纳，德意志民主共和国外交部第一副部长、德国统一社会党中央委员、德意志民主共和国人民议院议员奥托·文策尔，德意志民主共和国驻中国大使保罗·汪戴尔。
    毛泽东主席、刘少奇主席同贵宾们作了亲切的交谈。
  接见时在座的，有中共中央政治局委员、中共上海市委第一书记、上海市市长柯庆施，外交部副部长曾涌泉。（附图片）
