Title: GDR Government Delegation Visits Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1960-01-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, January 15th: The Vice-Premier of the German Democratic Republic, Heinrich Rau, and the delegation he led, arrived in Shanghai by special plane from Wuhan this morning, accompanied by the Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs of our country, Zeng Yongquan, and others.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Cao Diqiu, Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Mayor of Shanghai; Liu Shuzhou, Alternate Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Mayor of Shanghai; Niu Shucai and Jin Zhonghua, Vice Mayors of Shanghai; Lieutenant General Wang Bicheng, Commander of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; responsible persons from various departments of the Shanghai Municipal Party and government organs and people's organizations; and council members of the Sino-German Friendship Association in Shanghai. Also present at the airport to welcome them were Ackermann, Acting Consul General of the German Democratic Republic in Shanghai, consuls from various countries stationed in Shanghai, and experts from the German Democratic Republic in Shanghai.

In the afternoon, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Zeng Yongquan, Cao Diqiu, and others, visited the site of the First National Congress of the Communist Party of China—the Shanghai Revolutionary History Memorial Hall, and the Children's Palace, and toured the urban area of Shanghai by car. When the distinguished guests arrived at the Children's Palace, hundreds of Young Pioneers greeted them enthusiastically by blowing bugles and beating drums. The children also performed songs, dances, and musical programs for the distinguished guests, and presented them with their own embroidery, sculptures, and other works, as well as the Young Pioneers' flag, as souvenirs. Otto Winzer, member of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, spoke to the children enthusiastically, wishing them to become outstanding builders of the great communist cause. The distinguished guests also presented gifts to the children.

In the evening, Ke Qingshi, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, First Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Mayor of Shanghai, hosted a banquet to welcome the distinguished guests.

### 德政府代表团在上海参观访问

1960-01-16
第4版()
专栏：

    德政府代表团在上海参观访问
    新华社上海15日电　海因里希·劳副总理和他率领的德意志民主共和国代表团，今天上午在我国外交部副部长曾涌泉等陪同下，由武汉乘专机到达上海。
    到机场欢迎的，有中共上海市委书记处书记、上海市副市长曹荻秋，中共上海市委书记处候补书记、上海市副市长刘述周，上海市副市长牛树才、金仲华，中国人民解放军上海警备区司令员王必成中将，上海市党政机关各部门和各人民团体的负责人，以及中德友好协会在上海理事等。到机场欢迎的，还有德意志民主共和国驻上海代理总领事阿克曼，各国驻上海领事和在上海的德意志民主共和国专家。
    下午，贵宾们在曾涌泉、曹荻秋等人陪同下，参观了中国共产党第一次全国代表大会会址——上海革命历史纪念馆和少年宫，并驱车游览了上海市区。当贵宾们到少年宫的时候，几百个少先队员吹着小喇叭、敲着小鼓，热情地欢迎贵宾，孩子们还为贵宾表演了歌唱、舞蹈和音乐节目，并把自己制作的刺绣、雕塑等作品和少先队队旗等送给贵宾留念。德国统一社会党中央委员、德意志民主共和国外交部第一副部长奥托·文策尔热情地对孩子们讲了话，他预祝孩子们成为伟大的共产主义事业的优秀建设者。贵宾们还给孩子们赠送了礼品。
    晚间，中共中央政治局委员、中共上海市委第一书记、上海市市长柯庆施设宴欢迎贵宾。
