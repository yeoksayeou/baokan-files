Title: Our Ambassador to Pakistan Arrives

Author: 
Date: 1960-01-31
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, January 29th: Ding Guoyu, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Pakistan, arrived in Karachi on the evening of January 29th.

Those who welcomed him at the airport included: the Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Pakistani Ministry of Foreign Affairs, diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union and Czechoslovakia to Pakistan, trade representatives from Poland and Hungary to Pakistan, all diplomatic personnel from the Chinese Embassy, and representatives of overseas Chinese in Pakistan.

### 我驻巴大使到任

1960-01-31
第3版()
专栏：

    我驻巴大使到任
    新华社卡拉奇29日电　中华人民共和国新任驻巴基斯坦特命全权大使丁国钰29日晚到达卡拉奇。
    到机场上欢迎的有：巴基斯坦外交部礼宾司副司长、苏联及捷克斯洛伐克驻巴基斯坦的外交使节、波兰及匈牙利驻巴基斯坦的贸易代表，以及中国大使馆全体外交人员和在巴基斯坦的华侨代表。
