Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Secretary-General of the Britain-China Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1960-01-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 24th: Vice Premier Chen Yi received Mr. and Mrs. Derribon, the Secretary-General of the Britain-China Friendship Association, who are currently visiting our country, this morning. The hosts and guests had a friendly conversation.

Present at the reception were Zhang Zhixiang, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Zhu Boshen, Deputy Secretary-General.

### 陈毅副总理接见英中友协秘书长

1960-01-25
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见英中友协秘书长
    新华社24日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了正在我国访问的英中友协秘书长德里本夫妇。宾主进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中国人民对外文化协会副会长张致祥，副秘书长朱伯深。
