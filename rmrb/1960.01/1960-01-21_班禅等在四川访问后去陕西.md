Title: Panchen and Others Visit Sichuan, Then Go to Shaanxi
Author:
Date: 1960-01-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, January 20th: Panchen Erdeni Chökyi Gyaltsen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Acting Director of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, and Pabala Gelag Namgyal, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, along with their entourage, after visiting Chengdu and Chongqing, left Chongqing by special train for Xi'an today, accompanied by Wang Feng, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee and Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, and Zhou Renshan, Deputy Secretary of the CPC Tibet Working Committee.

Panchen Erdeni and Pabala and their party arrived in Chengdu on the 14th. On the evening of the 17th, the Sichuan Provincial People's Committee and the Chengdu Municipal People's Committee held a farewell banquet for them. They left for Chongqing by special train that evening, and Li Dazhang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Sichuan Provincial Committee of the CPC and Governor of Sichuan Province, and others went to the station to see them off. Panchen Erdeni and Pabala and their party visited Chongqing for three days.

### 班禅等在四川访问后去陕西

1960-01-21
第4版()
专栏：

    班禅等在四川访问后去陕西
    据新华社重庆20日电　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞，和政协全国委员会副主席、西藏自治区筹备委员会副主任委员帕巴拉·卓列朗杰以及他们的随行人员，在成都和重庆访问以后，由中共中央统战部副部长、民族事务委员会副主任汪锋和中共西藏工委副书记周仁山陪同，今天乘专车离开重庆去西安。
    班禅额尔德尼和帕巴拉等一行是在14日到达成都的。17日晚上，四川省人民委员会和成都市人民委员会为他们举行了欢送宴会。他们在当晚乘专车去重庆，中共四川省委书记处书记、四川省省长李大章等曾前往车站欢送。班禅额尔德尼和帕巴拉等在重庆访问了三天。
