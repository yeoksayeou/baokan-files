Title: Soviet Academic Circles Actively Prepare for International Congress of Orientalists
Author: Hu Xi
Date: 1960-01-15
Page: 7
Academic and Cultural Trends

    Soviet Academic Circles Actively Prepare for International Congress of Orientalists
    According to the decision of the 24th International Congress of Orientalists in 1957, the 25th Congress will be held in the Soviet Union. This is the first International Congress of Orientalists held in a socialist country.
    The Soviet academic community attaches great importance to the preparations for this congress. The Preparatory Committee, organized by the Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, is chaired by Corresponding Academician Gafurov of the Soviet Academy of Sciences. Three thematic committees have also been established under the Preparatory Committee: the Committee on Ancient Oriental History, the Committee on the Publication of Manuscript Documents of Oriental Peoples, and the Committee on Oriental Linguistics.
    In addition to discussing traditional scientific issues in ancient Oriental history, archaeology, medieval studies, and Oriental classical philology, this congress will also study all the scientific issues that arise in the current historical processes of Oriental countries and regions. The Preparatory Committee has determined that a special group on Chinese studies will be established at this congress in order to broadly discuss the many academic issues raised by the Chinese people in the development of history, language, culture, and philosophical and socio-political thought, as well as to study the historical experience of the Chinese people in achieving national and social liberation and carrying out socialist construction under the guidance of the Chinese Communist Party.
    The congress will be held in Moscow in August this year. The congress will be divided into twenty groups according to the various departments of Oriental studies. Before the opening of the congress, a large number of works on the economic, political, and cultural development of modern Oriental countries will be published.　　　　　(Hu Xi)

### 苏联学术界积极筹备  国际东方学家代表大会
虎溪
1960-01-15
第7版()
专栏：学术文化动态

    苏联学术界积极筹备
    国际东方学家代表大会
    根据1957年第二十四届国际东方学家代表大会的决定，第二十五届大会将在苏联举行。这是在社会主义国家举行的第一次国际东方学家会议。
    苏联学术界对于这次大会的准备工作极为重视。苏联科学院主席团组织的筹备委员会，系由苏联科学院通讯院士加富罗夫担任主席。筹备委员会下还成立了三个专题委员会：古代东方史问题委员会，东方各族人民文献手稿出版问题委员会，东方语言学问题委员会。
    这次大会除了将讨论古代东方史、考古学、中世纪学和东方古典语文学方面的传统科学问题以外，还将研究当前东方国家和地区的历史进程中出现的一切科学问题。筹备委员会已经确定在这次大会上设立一个专门的中国学小组，以便广泛地讨论中国人民在历史、语文、文化以及哲学思想和社会政治思想发展中提出的许多学术问题，以及研究中国人民在中国共产党指引下取得民族解放和社会解放、进行社会主义建设的历史经验。
    这次大会将在今年8月间在莫斯科举行。大会将按照东方学各个部门分为二十个小组进行工作。在大会开幕前，将出版大量关于现代东方各国的经济、政治和文化发展的著作。　　　　　（虎溪）
