Title: Madagascar Government Decides to Replace a French Bureau Chief

Author:

Date: 1960-01-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tananarive, January 4th: The government of Madagascar, an island nation off the east coast of Africa, recently appointed Madagascan Victor Leimanitra as acting director of the National Security Bureau. He replaces the French Pierre Commeny as director. Leimanitra was previously his assistant. Madagascar is a member state of the "French Community." When Madagascar's President Tsiranana visited Paris last month, he told French President de Gaulle that January 1960 would be a good time to discuss transferring responsibilities from the "Community" to the Republic of Madagascar.

### 马达加斯加政府决定  接替一名法国人的局长职位

1960-01-06
第6版()
专栏：

    马达加斯加政府决定
    接替一名法国人的局长职位
    据新华社4日讯　塔那那利佛消息：非洲东海岸岛国马达加斯加政府最近任命马达加斯加人维克多·勒马尼特腊担任国家保安局的代理局长。他接替法国人皮埃尔·康梅尼担任的局长职务。勒马尼特腊过去是他的助理。马达加斯加是“法兰西共同体”成员国之一。马达加斯加总统齐腊纳纳上个月在巴黎访问时说，他曾向法国总统戴高乐建议：1960年1月将是讨论把责任从“共同体”移交给马达加斯加共和国的良好时机。
