Title: Iraqi News Agency Authorized Statement: Advocating Peaceful Resolution of Disputes with Iran, Denying the Lie Spread by Associated Press about Iraqi Troop Mobilization
Author:
Date: 1960-01-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, January 2nd: The Iraqi News Agency was authorized today to deny the Associated Press report that Iraq had amassed many troops on the Iraqi-Iranian border.

This is the second time Iraq has denied amassing troops in this border area, the first denial being on December 24th of last year.

The Iraqi News Agency was also authorized to declare that Iraq is willing to resolve the dispute with Iran through peaceful means.

### 伊拉克通讯社受权声明  主张和平解决同伊朗的争端  否认美联社散布的伊拉克集结军队的谎言

1960-01-04
第6版()
专栏：

    伊拉克通讯社受权声明
    主张和平解决同伊朗的争端
    否认美联社散布的伊拉克集结军队的谎言
    新华社巴格达2日电　伊拉克通讯社今天受权否认，美联社所谓伊拉克集结许多军队在伊拉克和伊朗边境的消息。
    这是伊拉克第二次否认曾在这一带边境集结军队，第一次否认是在去年12月24日。
    伊拉克通讯社还受权声明，伊拉克愿意通过和平途径解决和伊朗之间的争端。
