Title: Polish-Soviet Union Sign This Year's Cultural Cooperation Plan
Author:
Date: 1960-01-15
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, January 11th. Poland and the Soviet Union signed the 1960 execution plan for the cultural cooperation agreement in Warsaw on the 11th.

The plan stipulates the continued development of cooperation in science, higher education, culture, art, radio, and television. The two countries will extensively exchange scientific workers and students.

In 1960, some Soviet art groups, such as the Bolshoi Theatre Ballet Company, the Vakhtangov Theatre of Moscow, and the Soviet Circus, will perform in Poland. The Słowacki National Theatre of Krakow and the "Mazowsze" Song and Dance Ensemble of Poland will visit the Soviet Union. In addition, both sides will hold exhibitions for each other.

### 波苏签订今年文化合作计划

1960-01-15
第6版()
专栏：

    波苏签订今年文化合作计划
    新华社华沙电　波兰和苏联11日在华沙签订了文化合作协定1960年执行计划。
    计划规定在科学、高等教育、文化、艺术、广播和电视等方面继续发展合作。两国将广泛地互派科学工作者和留学生。
    1960年，苏联一些艺术团体，如苏联大剧院芭蕾舞剧团、莫斯科的瓦赫坦戈夫话剧院、苏联马戏团等将到波兰演出。波兰埃德格夫的斯洛瓦茨基国家剧院，“玛佐夫舍”歌舞团均将访问苏联。此外，双方还将互相举办展览会。
