Title: Bulgarian Steelworker Milev Expresses Immense Confidence: 1960 Production Plan Will Definitely Be Overfulfilled
Author: Qu Jitao
Date: 1960-01-04
Page: 5

    Sofia, January 1st (People's Daily reporter Qu Jitao) - On the eve of the New Year, I visited Bulgaria's steel base, the Lenin Steel Plant. In the economic leap forward of 1959, all the plant's employees demonstrated unprecedented labor enthusiasm and initiative. They completed the year's production plan eight days ahead of schedule, on December 23rd. Now, all the plant's employees are welcoming the even more glorious and arduous tasks of 1960 with full confidence.

    Svilin Milev, an outstanding rolling mill worker at the Lenin Steel Plant, recipient of the Order of Labor, foreman of a work team and Party branch secretary in the rolling mill workshop, introduced the labor enthusiasm of his partners to the reporter. He said, "Like all the plant's employees, the hundred or so rolling mill workers in our work team actively responded to the Party's call for a leap forward, putting forward more than a hundred rationalization proposals throughout the year. Due to the implementation of these proposals, the production of the entire rolling mill workshop has increased significantly, averaging 118% of the plan each month."

    Milev then described to the reporter the workers' immense confidence in completing the 1960 production plan. He said, "The main issue we are all considering now is not just completing the 1960 plan, but how to overfulfill it, to produce more steel and meet the needs of the country…."

    Finally, Milev expressed great admiration for the labor achievements created by Chinese steelworkers in 1959. He repeatedly asked the reporter to convey this lofty New Year's wish from Bulgarian steelworkers to their Chinese steelworker brothers: Congratulations to the Chinese steelworker brothers for catching up with and surpassing Britain in steel production at an early date….

### 保加利亚炼钢工人米列夫表示无比信心  1960年的生产计划一定能够超额完成
曲季涛
1960-01-04
第5版()
专栏：

    保加利亚炼钢工人米列夫表示无比信心
    1960年的生产计划一定能够超额完成
    本报索非亚1日电　记者曲季涛报道：新年前夕，记者访问了保加利亚的钢铁基地——列宁炼钢厂，这个厂的全体职工们，在1959年经济的跃进中表现了空前的劳动热情和积极性。他们提前八天，于12月23日完成了全年的生产计划。现在，全厂职工正满怀信心地迎接1960年的更光荣更艰巨的任务。
    列宁炼钢厂一个优秀轧钢工人、人民劳动勋章获得者斯威林·米列夫——轧钢车间的一个工作班的工长和党支部书记，向记者介绍了他的伙伴们的劳动热情。他说：“同全厂职工一样，我们这个工作班的一百来个轧钢工人，积极响应党的跃进号召，一年内先后曾提出了一百多条合理化建议。由于这些建议的实施，全轧钢车间的生产增长了很多，平均每月都完成计划的118％。”
    接着米列夫向记者叙述了工人们对完成1960年的生产计划的无比信心。他说：“我们大家现在所考虑的主要问题，已不是完成而是如何超额完成1960年计划，生产更多的钢，满足国家的需要……。”
    米列夫最后表示非常钦佩中国炼钢工人们1959年创造的劳动功勋。他一再请记者转达保加利亚炼钢工人对中国炼钢工人的这一崇高的新年祝愿：祝贺中国炼钢工人兄弟们在钢的产量方面，早日赶上和超过英国……。
