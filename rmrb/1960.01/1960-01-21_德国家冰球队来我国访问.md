Title: German Ice Hockey Team Visits China
Author:
Date: 1960-01-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 20th: The German Democratic Republic's national ice hockey team, a group of twenty-three people led by team leader Hans Simon, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

The German Democratic Republic's national ice hockey team is the first sports team from a fraternal country to visit China this year, comprising seventeen athletes. The team members participated in the World Ice Hockey Championships last year and have rich experience. During this visit to China, they will hold friendly matches with Chinese ice hockey teams in Harbin, Jilin, and other places. The visiting team will take a train to Harbin tonight and is scheduled to hold its first friendly match in China with the Harbin ice hockey team on the 23rd.

### 德国家冰球队来我国访问

1960-01-21
第4版()
专栏：

    德国家冰球队来我国访问
    新华社20日讯　德意志民主共和国国家冰球队一行二十三人，今天在领队汉斯·西蒙率领下乘飞机到达北京。
    德意志民主共和国国家冰球队是今年第一个应邀来我国访问的兄弟国家的体育运动队，共有十七名运动员。队员们去年曾经参加过世界冰球锦标赛，有着丰富的经验。这次来我国访问，将在哈尔滨、吉林等地同我国的冰球队举行友谊比赛。客队在今晚乘火车前往哈尔滨，预定23日同哈尔滨冰球队举行访华第一场友谊比赛。
