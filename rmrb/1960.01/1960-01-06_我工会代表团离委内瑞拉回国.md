Title: Our Trade Union Delegation Leaves Venezuela for Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-01-06
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Caracas, 2nd. The Chinese trade union delegation, which had been visiting Venezuela at the invitation of the Venezuelan government, left here today for home. Before departure, the Chinese trade union delegation issued a letter of congratulations to the Venezuelan people and friends, congratulating the increasing consolidation of friendship between the two peoples, and expressing gratitude to the Confederation of Venezuelan Workers for inviting the delegation to visit Venezuela.

### 我工会代表团离委内瑞拉回国

1960-01-06
第5版()
专栏：

    我工会代表团离委内瑞拉回国
    据新华社加拉加斯2日电　应邀访问委内瑞拉的中国工会代表团今天离开这儿回国。中国工会代表团在动身前发表了一封给委内瑞拉人民和朋友们的祝贺信，祝贺两国人民的友谊日益巩固，并且感谢委内瑞拉工人联合会邀请代表团到委内瑞拉访问。
