Title: All-China Youth Federation Representative Issues Statement Condemning West Germany's Persecution of Young Peace Fighters
Author:
Date: 1960-01-13
Page: 5

    All-China Youth Federation Representative Issues Statement
    Condemning West Germany's Persecution of Young Peace Fighters
    Xinhua News Agency, January 9th. A representative of the All-China Youth Federation issued a statement regarding the West German government's illegal trial of West German youth Gert Hillmann and two others.
    In the statement, the representative of the All-China Youth Federation pointed out: Recently, the Special Political Court in Dortmund, West Germany, under the charge of participating in the festival and "threatening the state," illegally tried Gert Hillmann, Klaus Steffens, and Walter Fischer, former heads of the West German festival preparatory committee.
    Chinese youth firmly support the struggle of West German youth against the United States rearming West Germany, against the revival of militarism, and for the peaceful reunification of Germany.
    Chinese youth express extreme anger at this barbaric behavior of the West German authorities and firmly demand an immediate end to their persecution.

### 我青联负责人发表谈话  谴责西德迫害青年和平战士

1960-01-13
第5版()
专栏：

    我青联负责人发表谈话
    谴责西德迫害青年和平战士
    据新华社9日讯  中华全国青年联合会负责人就西德政府非法审讯西德青年格特·希耳曼等三人发表谈话。
    中华全国青年联合会负责人在发表谈话时指出：最近西德多特蒙德特别政治法庭以参加联欢节“威胁国家”为罪名，非法审讯了西德联欢节筹备委员会前负责人格特·希耳曼、克劳斯·斯特芬斯和瓦尔特·费歇等三人。
    中国青年坚决支持西德青年反对美国重新武装西德、反对复活军国主义、争取德国和平统一的斗争。
    中国青年对西德当局的这种横蛮行为表示极大的愤怒，并坚决要求立即停止对他们的迫害。
