Title: Vice Premier Rau Sends Telegram of Thanks to Premier Zhou
Author:
Date: 1960-01-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 19th January: Premier Zhou Enlai received a telegram from Heinrich Rau, Vice Premier of the German Democratic Republic, en route from China to Korea, expressing gratitude for the warm hospitality extended to the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic during its visit to China. The text of the telegram is as follows: To Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:

On behalf of the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, we would like to express our sincere and heartfelt thanks to you once again for your warm hospitality. Our visit to your country was an unforgettable event. The enthusiasm and diligence with which the Chinese people, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, are carrying out socialist construction at an unprecedented speed have left a deep impression on us and will serve as an example in our struggle for socialist construction and the peaceful resolution of the German question.

Heinrich Rau, Vice Premier of the German Democratic Republic, January 19th

### 劳副总理电谢周总理

1960-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    劳副总理电谢周总理
    新华社19日讯　周恩来总理收到德意志民主共和国副总理海因里希·劳在离华赴朝途中发来的电报，对德意志民主共和国政府代表团在访问中国期间所受到的殷勤招待表示感谢。电文如下：中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志：
    我们谨以德意志民主共和国政府代表团的名义，对你们的殷勤招待再一次向你表示诚挚的、衷心的谢意。在你们国家的访问对我们是一件难忘的事情。中国人民在中国共产党的领导下，以空前的速度进行社会主义建设的那种热情和勤劳，给我们留下了深刻的印象，并将成为我们在社会主义建设和和平解决德国问题的斗争中的榜样。
     德意志民主共和国副总理　海因里希·劳　一月十九日
