Title: The Song of the Heavenly Horse
Author: Zou Difan
Date: 1960-01-27
Page: 8
The Song of the Heavenly Horse
　　Zou Difan

　　A group of heavenly horses gallop in the sky,
　　Flying over mountains and passes, thousands upon thousands heavy.
　　The galloping hooves stir wind and cloud,
　　A proud roar colors the heavens and earth red.
　　The heavenly horse is the General Line,
　　Soaring into the sky in the Great Leap Forward,
　　Super-quota targets reveal the golden list,
　　How can the fireworks of victory not fill my heart?

　　Steel exceeds the quota, throwing a red line,
　　Taking the lead, striding over 1962.
　　Grain, like a fast horse, whips past disasters,
　　Coal and cotton rush forward and seize the lead again...

　　Treasures abound everywhere,
　　Piles of gold and jade are reported at all times,
　　Awakening poor mountains to extract gold mines,
　　Fighting droughts and transforming barrenness into bountiful years...

　　The leader's thought shines like the sun and moon,
　　The Party's leadership turns the universe around,
　　Sharing weal and woe, our will becomes a fortress,
　　Diligent hands and minds will surely conquer heaven.

　　1959,
　　1959,
　　An industrial system lays down a steel array:
　　Large chimneys blow towards fields of rice and wheat,
　　Small machine tools ring out in thatched sheds,
　　Men, women, young and old all pitch in,
　　Large, medium, and small factories all emit smoke.

　　1959,
　　1959,
　　The people's communes weave brocade at the grassroots level:
　　Agriculture, forestry, animal husbandry, sideline occupations, and fishery are all developed,
　　Workers, peasants, merchants, students, and soldiers are all mobilized,
　　Labor enthusiasm bears abundant fruit,
　　Technical innovations bloom brightly.

　　1960, advance!
　　1960, forward!
　　Factory smoke columns form a chorus lineup,
　　Irrigation systems compose a melody praising great harmony,
　　Steel is the key link, the strongest sound,
　　One pig per mu, abundant grain...

　　A group of heavenly horses gallop in the sky,
　　Flying over mountains and passes, thousands upon thousands heavy.
　　The galloping hooves stir wind and cloud,
　　A proud roar colors the heavens and earth red.

　　The New Year brings a new Great Leap Forward,
　　Soaring into the sky in the Great Leap Forward.
　　Golden lists are revealed day after day,
　　Songs of triumph are sung everywhere.
　　The symphony of industry and agriculture has a national flavor,
　　The conductor is our leader Mao Zedong.
　　Once the order is given, the mountains and rivers change,
　　A group of heavenly horses gallop in the sky...

### 天马歌
邹荻帆
1960-01-27
第8版()
专栏：

    天马歌
    邹荻帆
    一群天马行太空，
    飞越关山千万重。
    滚滚风云马蹄急，
    昂首一声天地红。
    天马就是总路线，
    一飞冲天跃进中，
    超额指标揭金榜，
    胜利的焰火怎不喷满我心胸。
    钢铁超额完成指标抛红线，
    一马当先跨过1962年。
    粮食快马挥鞭胜灾害，
    煤棉猛冲猛打又占先……
    聚宝藏珠处处是，
    堆金积玉时时传，
    摇醒穷山取金矿，
    大战荒旱变丰年……
    领袖思想昭日月，
    党的领导乾坤旋，
    肝胆相照志成城，
    手脑并勤定胜天。
    1959年，
    1959年，
    工业体系布下钢铁阵：
    大烟囱吹向稻麦田，
    小机床响在茅棚间，
    男女老少都动手，
    大中小型齐冒烟。
    1959年，
    1959年，
    人民公社基层织锦绣：
    农林牧副渔一齐搞，
    工农商学兵总动员，
    劳动热情结硕果，
    技术革新花朵鲜。
    1960年，前进，
    1960年，向前！
    工厂烟柱合唱列阵容，
    灌溉系统乐谱颂大同，
    以钢为纲最强音，
    一亩一猪粮食丰……
    一群天马行太空，
    飞越关山千万重。
    滚滚风云马蹄急，
    昂首一声天地红。
    新年又有新跃进，
    一飞冲天跃进中。
    日日月月揭金榜，
    时时谱唱满堂红。
    工农业交响乐民族风，
    指挥就是领袖毛泽东。
    号令一出山河改，
    一群天马行太空……
