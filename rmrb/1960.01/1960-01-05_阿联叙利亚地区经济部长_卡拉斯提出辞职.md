Title: 阿联叙利亚地区经济部长 卡拉斯提出辞职 (United Arab Republic Syrian Region Minister of Economy, Kalas, Resigns)
Author:
Date: 1960-01-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, January 4th: According to reports in newspapers here today, Khalil Kalas, Minister of Economy for the Syrian Region of the United Arab Republic, submitted his resignation on the 3rd. President Nasser has accepted his resignation.

Prior to this, former UAR Vice President and Minister of Justice Hurani, Minister of (Central) Culture and National Guidance Bitar, Syrian Region Minister of Land Reform Hamdun, and Minister of Social Affairs and Labor Kannut had already resigned last week. They, along with Kalas, were all former members of the Syrian Arab Ba'ath Socialist Party.

### 阿联叙利亚地区经济部长  卡拉斯提出辞职

1960-01-05
第5版()
专栏：

    阿联叙利亚地区经济部长
    卡拉斯提出辞职
    新华社开罗4日电　据这里报纸今天报道，阿拉伯联合共和国叙利亚地区经济部长哈利勒·卡拉斯在3日提出辞职。纳赛尔总统已经接受了他的辞职。
    在这以前，前阿联副总统兼司法部长胡拉尼、（中央）文化和国家指导部长比塔尔、叙利亚地区土地改革部长哈姆杜恩以及社会事务和劳工部长坎努特已在上星期辞职。他们和卡拉斯都是前叙利亚阿拉伯复兴社会党党员。
