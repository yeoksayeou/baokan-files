Title: Gannan People's Broadcasting Station Completed
Author: 
Date: 1960-01-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency report: The Gannan Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture People's Broadcasting Station has recently been officially completed and has begun broadcasting.

The Tibetan people on the Gannan grassland can now hear news and excellent ethnic art programs broadcast in Tibetan from this radio station.

### 甘南人民广播电台建成

1960-01-18
第4版()
专栏：

    甘南人民广播电台建成
    据新华社讯　甘南藏族自治州人民广播电台最近正式建成，并开始播音。
    甘南草原上的藏族人民已经从收音机里，听到这个电台用藏语播送的新闻和各民族优秀文艺节目。
