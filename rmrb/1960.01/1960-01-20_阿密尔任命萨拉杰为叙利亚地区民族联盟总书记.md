Title: Amir Appoints Sarraj as Secretary-General of the National Union for the Syrian Region
Author:
Date: 1960-01-20
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, January 14th - According to a report by Damascus Radio this afternoon, Amir, the Vice President of the United Arab Republic responsible for Syrian regional affairs, issued Decision No. 1 of 1960, appointing Sarraj as the Secretary-General of the National Union for the Syrian Region.
    The radio station stated that this decision takes effect from the date of its promulgation.
    Sarraj is currently the Minister of Interior for the Syrian Region.

### 阿密尔任命萨拉杰为叙利亚地区民族联盟总书记

1960-01-20
第5版()
专栏：

    阿密尔任命萨拉杰为叙利亚地区民族联盟总书记
    新华社大马士革14日电　据大马士革电台今天下午报道，负责叙利亚地区事务的阿联副总统阿密尔发布1960年第一号决定，任命萨拉杰为叙利亚地区民族联盟总书记。
    电台说，这项决定从发布之日起生效。
    萨拉杰迄今是叙利亚地区的内政部长。
