Title: Brazenly Violating the Korean Armistice Agreement: The United States to Strengthen US Troops in South Korea
Author:
Date: 1960-01-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 20th: Washington dispatch: U.S. Army Secretary Brucker openly stated in Washington on the 19th that the United States, disregarding the Korean Armistice Agreement, intends to strengthen its troops stationed in South Korea.

Brucker, having just returned to the United States after a three-week tour of the Far East, said that while he was in South Korea, the commander of the U.S. invasion forces in South Korea, Magruder, had made "many requests" regarding equipment. Brucker said that Army officials would study Magruder's requests very carefully.

On January 15, Magruder, while meeting with a United Press International reporter in Seoul, South Korea, stated that he had requested that missiles, communications equipment, and radar be transported to South Korea.

### 悍然违反朝鲜停战协定  美国要加强南朝鲜美军

1960-01-22
第6版()
专栏：

    悍然违反朝鲜停战协定
    美国要加强南朝鲜美军
    新华社20日讯　华盛顿消息：美国陆军部长布鲁克19日在华盛顿公然表示，美国要不顾朝鲜停战协定，加强它驻在南朝鲜的军队。
    布鲁克刚在远东进行了三星期的活动后回到美国，他说，他在南朝鲜时，美国驻南朝鲜侵略军总司令马格鲁德曾经在装备方面提出“许多要求”。布鲁克说，陆军官员们将非常仔细地研究马格鲁德的要求。
    马格鲁德1月15日在南朝鲜的汉城接见合众国际社记者时表示，他要求把导弹、通讯设备和雷达运入南朝鲜。
