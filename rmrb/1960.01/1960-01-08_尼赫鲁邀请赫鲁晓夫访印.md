Title: Nehru Invites Khrushchev to Visit India
Author:
Date: 1960-01-08
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th January. TASS, Moscow, 6th January: The Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Khrushchev, received the Indian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Menon, on the 6th and talked with him.

    The Ambassador handed Khrushchev a letter from Indian Prime Minister Nehru. The letter invited Khrushchev to visit India when he visits Indonesia.

    Khrushchev warmly thanked him for the kind invitation and said that he hoped to be able to accept the invitation.

### 尼赫鲁邀请赫鲁晓夫访印

1960-01-08
第5版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁邀请赫鲁晓夫访印
    新华社7日讯　塔斯社莫斯科6日讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫6日接见了印度驻苏联大使梅农、并和他谈了话。
    大使向赫鲁晓夫递交了印度总理尼赫鲁的一封信。这封信邀请赫鲁晓夫去印度尼西亚访问的时候也去印度访问。
    赫鲁晓夫热烈感谢这一盛情邀请，并且表示他希望能够应邀。
