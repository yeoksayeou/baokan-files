Title: Inner Mongolia Builds First Tractor Repair and Assembly Plant
Author:
Date: 1960-01-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot. Inner Mongolia's first tractor repair and assembly plant recently began construction in the suburbs of Hohhot.

When completed, this tractor repair and assembly plant will be able to repair and assemble 400 tractors annually and produce 500 tons of various tractor parts. The large and small parts used for repair and assembly can basically be self-sufficient.

### 内蒙古兴建第一座拖拉机修配厂

1960-01-03
第3版()
专栏：

    内蒙古兴建第一座拖拉机修配厂
    据新华社呼和浩特讯　内蒙古第一座拖拉机修配厂最近在呼和浩特市郊区动工兴建。
    这座拖拉机修配厂建成后，每年能修配拖拉机四百台，生产拖拉机各种配件五百吨；修配所用的大小零件基本上可以自给自足。
