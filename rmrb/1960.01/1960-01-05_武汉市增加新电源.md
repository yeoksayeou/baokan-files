Title: Wuhan Increases New Power Source
Author:
Date: 1960-01-05
Page: 2

New Year New Construction

    Wuhan Increases New Power Source
    The fifth turbine generator set of the Wuhan Qingshan Thermal Power Plant has been installed and began generating electricity on New Year's Eve.
    The fifth high-temperature and high-pressure generator set is the first generator set in the second phase of the Qingshan Thermal Power Plant project and is highly automated. Its completion and power generation will greatly contribute to ensuring the continued leap forward in industrial production in the Wuhan and Huangshi areas this year.

### 武汉市增加新电源

1960-01-05
第2版()
专栏：新年新建设

    武汉市增加新电源
    武汉青山热电厂第五号汽轮发电机组安装完毕，除夕开始发电。
    高温高压的第五号发电机组，是青山热电厂第二期工程中的第一个发电机组，自动化程度很高。它的建成发电，对保证武汉和黄石地区今年工业生产持续跃进有很大作用。
