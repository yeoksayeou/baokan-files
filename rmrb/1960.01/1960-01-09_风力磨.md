Title: Windmill
Author: Chen Xiaojin Chen Tao
Date: 1960-01-09
Page: 8

    Windmill
    Chen Xiaojin　Painting
    Chen Tao　Poem
    Gusts of wind blow,
    The mill sings happily,
    Turning and turning,
    Grinding flour for thousands of families.
    The heavy work of the past,
    Has now become easy and relaxed,
    No need to move your hands too much,
    Just sit and watch.
    Good technical innovation,
    Utilizing nature,
    A red heart and skillful hands,
    Abilities surpassing Lu Ban.
    (Attached picture)

    风力磨
    陈小津　画
    陈涛　诗风儿阵阵吹，磨儿唱得欢，转呀转呀转，磨出千家面。从前繁重活，而今变轻闲，不消多动手，坐在一旁看。技术革新好，利用大自然，心红手就巧，本事赛鲁班。
