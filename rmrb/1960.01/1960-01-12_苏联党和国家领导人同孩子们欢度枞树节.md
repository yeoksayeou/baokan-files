Title: Soviet Party and State Leaders Celebrate the Fir Tree Festival with Children
Author:
Date: 1960-01-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 10th: According to TASS, Soviet Party and state leaders Khrushchev and Voroshilov visited the traditional New Year's Fir Tree Festival at the Kremlin on January 9th as guests of the children.

Since the New Year, more than four thousand children have participated in the Fir Tree Festival held in the Great Kremlin Palace every day.

The children happily welcomed their guests. Khrushchev and Voroshilov watched the short comedy "A Journey to the Land of Myths Fulfilled" with the children. The characters included: Knowledgeable Fairy, Labor Magician, Corn Queen, Harvest King, Atomic Magician, Chemical Sorceress, Santa Claus, and Snow White.

After the performance, two girls and a boy presented flowers to the guests on behalf of all the children participating in the Fir Tree Festival. Khrushchev and Voroshilov also took photos with the children.

### 苏联党和国家领导人同孩子们欢度枞树节

1960-01-12
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联党和国家领导人同孩子们欢度枞树节
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党和国家领导人赫鲁晓夫和伏罗希洛夫9日到克里姆林宫传统的新年枞树节去做儿童们的客人。
    新年以来，每天都有四千多名儿童来参加在大克里姆林宫举行的枞树节。
    儿童们欢天喜地地迎接了自己的客人。赫鲁晓夫和伏罗希洛夫同孩子们一起观看了短喜剧“应验的神话之国旅行记”。剧中人有：知识小仙、劳动大魔师、玉蜀黍皇后、丰收国王、原子魔术师、化学女魔术师、圣诞老人和白雪公主。
    表演完毕后，两个女孩和一个男孩代表所有参加枞树节的小朋友向客人们献花。赫鲁晓夫和伏罗希洛夫还同孩子们一起照了相。
