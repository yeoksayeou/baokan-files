Title: General Zhang Aiping Visits Brigadier General Aung Gyi
Author:
Date: 1960-01-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 28th. General Zhang Aiping, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, visited Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces of the Union of Burma, and Lieutenant Colonel Lun, Director of Military Intelligence, at the hotel on the morning of the Spring Festival.

    During the visit, Colonel Liu Dang, Military Attaché of the Embassy of Burma in China, and Colonel Jing Shamen were present.

    Accompanying General Zhang Aiping on the visit were Major General Wang Xinggang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense, Colonel Jiang Keding, Military Attaché of our Embassy in Burma, and Colonel Ye Xiuzhi, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Department of the Ministry of National Defense.

### 张爱萍上将拜访昂季准将

1960-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    张爱萍上将拜访昂季准将
    新华社28日讯　中国人民解放军副总参谋长张爱萍上将在春节的上午前往宾馆，拜访了缅甸联邦国防军副总参谋长昂季准将和军事情报处处长伦中校。
    拜访时，缅甸驻中国大使馆武官柳当上校和敬沙门上校都在座。
    随同张爱萍上将前往拜访的，有国防部办公厅副主任王兴纲少将、我国驻缅甸大使馆武官蒋克定上校和国防部外事处副处长叶修直上校。
