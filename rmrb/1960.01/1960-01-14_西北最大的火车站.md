Title: The Largest Railway Station in Northwest China

Author:

Date: 1960-01-14
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency report: The construction of the New Lanzhou Station, the largest railway station in Northwest China, has begun. According to the design, the station building will be 165 meters long and 75 meters wide, with a construction area of 20,000 square meters. Equipped with modern facilities, it will be one of the larger stations in the country.

### 西北最大的火车站

1960-01-14
第2版()
专栏：

    西北最大的火车站
    据新华社讯　西北地区最大的铁路车站——新兰州站动工兴建。按照设计，这个车站的站舍长一百六十五米，宽七十五米，建筑面积有二万平方米，设备现代化，它将是全国几个较大的车站之一。
