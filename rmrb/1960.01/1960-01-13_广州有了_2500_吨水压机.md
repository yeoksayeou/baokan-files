Title: Guangzhou Has a 2500-Ton Hydraulic Press
Author:
Date: 1960-01-13
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency Report: The first 2,500-ton hydraulic press in South China has recently been officially put into production at the Guangzhou Heavy Machinery Plant.

After this hydraulic press is put into production, it can usually forge steel ingots weighing 25 to 30 tons, and at most can forge steel ingots weighing 65 tons. Forgings required for 10,000-ton ocean-going ships, crankshafts for 2,000-horsepower diesel engines, forgings for 50,000-kilowatt steam turbines and sugar industry machinery, as well as forgings for steel rolling mills below 850 mm, can all be processed here. This is of great significance for changing the weak forging capacity in South China and accelerating the development of metallurgy, machinery, chemical industry, power, shipbuilding, sugar production and other industries in South China.

### 广州有了2500吨水压机

1960-01-13
第2版()
专栏：

    广州有了2500吨水压机
    据新华社讯  华南第一台二千五百吨水压机，最近已在广州重型机器厂正式投入生产。
    这台水压机投入生产后，通常可以锻造重二十五到三十吨的钢锭，最大能锻造六十五吨的钢锭。一万吨远洋轮船所需的锻件、二千马力柴油机的曲轴、五万瓩汽轮机和糖业机械的锻件，以及八百五十公厘以下轧钢机的锻件等，都可以在这里加工。这对改变华南地区锻压力量薄弱的面貌和加速发展华南地区的冶金、机械、化工、动力、造船、制糖等工业，具有重要意义。
