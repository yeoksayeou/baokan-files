Title: Vietnam Builds Phosphate Plant in Six Months, Mainly Equipped with Domestic Machines, with an Annual Output of 3,000 Tons
Author:
Date: 1960-01-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 10th -- According to the Vietnam News Agency today: A phosphate plant with an annual output of 3,000 tons was completed on January 6th, the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Vietnam Labor Party, in central northern Vietnam.

	The factory is mainly equipped with domestically manufactured machines, and it was completed in six months. The construction of this factory is to meet the needs of developing local industry in northern Vietnam.

	On the same day, a meteorological station was also completed in Nghe An Province. It is one of the four large meteorological stations in the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. This meteorological station is equipped with the latest instruments provided by the Soviet Union, China, and the German Democratic Republic as aid.

### 越南六个月建成一座磷酸盐工厂  主要以国产机器装备  年产量达三千吨

1960-01-13
第6版()
专栏：

    越南六个月建成一座磷酸盐工厂
    主要以国产机器装备  年产量达三千吨
    新华社河内10日电  据越南通讯社今天报道：一个年产量达三千吨的磷酸盐工厂于1月6日越南劳动党成立三十周年的时候，在越南北方中部建成。
    这个工厂是主要以国内制造的机器装备起来的，它在六个月内就建成了。建设这个工厂是为了满足越南北方发展地方工业的需要。
    同一天，义安省还建成了一个气象站。它是越南民主共和国四个大气象站之一。这个气象站是以苏联、中国和德意志民主共和国作为援助提供的最新式的仪器装备起来的。
