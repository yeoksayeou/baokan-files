Title: Always Remember in Our Hearts
Author: Xu Qintang
Date: 1960-01-21
Page: 8

    Always Remember in Our Hearts
    Mother praises the commune as better than heaven,
    I will always remember it in my heart,
    Changing poverty's thousand-斤 burden,
    The commune helps us carry eight hundred 斤,
    We can give our lives, but not the commune,
    Grip our guns tightly, defend the roots of happiness.
    Grandma spoke golden words:
    All thanks to Chairman Mao's good leadership.
    Heaven is big, earth is big, but neither is very big,
    Neither is as great as our Chairman Mao.
    Raise the revolutionary red flag high and hold it firmly,
    Embark on a long journey, facing the east wind.
    We give our red hearts to Chairman Mao,
    And raise our guns to swear:
    Forever listen to the Party,
    Forever listen to Chairman Mao,
    Forever follow the people,
    Forever be docile tools.
          Stationed in Nanjing Troops　Xu Qintang

### 时刻记在心
许钦堂
1960-01-21
第8版()
专栏：

    时刻记在心
    娘夸公社比天好，
    儿当时刻记在心，
    改变贫困千斤担，
    公社帮咱挑八百斤，
    能舍命不能舍公社，
    握紧枪杆保卫幸福根。
    大娘金口玉言说的好：
    全凭咱毛主席好领导。
    天大、地大、都不大，
    都没有咱毛主席伟大。
    革命红旗举得高握得牢，
    跨上长途迎着东风飘。
    我们红心交给毛主席，
    高举起枪来宣誓：
    永远听党的话，
    永远听毛主席的话，
    永远跟人民走，
    永远做驯服的工具。
          驻南京部队　许钦堂
