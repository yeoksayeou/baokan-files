Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Delegation from the German Government
Author:
Date: 1960-01-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: Premier Zhou Enlai received Heinrich Rau, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign and Intra-German Trade of the German Democratic Republic, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, and member of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, along with all members of the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic this afternoon: Paul Verner, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, First Secretary of the Greater Berlin District Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Member of the National Front Committee of Democratic Germany, and Member of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic; Otto Winzer, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Member of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, and Member of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic; and Paul Wandel, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China. During the meeting, Premier Zhou Enlai had a cordial and friendly conversation with the distinguished guests.

Present at the meeting were Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yi and Li Xiannian, Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, Vice Ministers of Foreign Trade Lei Renmin and Lin Haiyun, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission Liu Mingfu, and Deputy Director of the Science and Technology Commission Wu Heng.

The distinguished guests of the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic returned to Beijing by special plane this afternoon from Shanghai.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 18th: After concluding their visit to Shanghai, Deputy Prime Minister Heinrich Rau and the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic he led, left Shanghai by plane this morning to return to Beijing, accompanied by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan and others.

They were seen off at the airport by Cao Diqiu, Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Mayor of Shanghai; Liu Shuzhou, Alternate Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Mayor of Shanghai; Vice Mayors of Shanghai Niu Shucai and Jin Zhonghua; Lieutenant General Wang Bicheng, Commander of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; as well as heads of various commissions and bureaus of the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee, the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and various people's organizations, and directors of the Sino-German Friendship Association in Shanghai.

Also at the airport to see them off were Ackermann, Acting Consul General of the German Democratic Republic in Shanghai, consuls of various countries in Shanghai, and experts from the German Democratic Republic assisting in construction in Shanghai. (Attached picture)

### 周恩来总理接见德政府代表团

1960-01-19
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见德政府代表团
    新华社18日讯　周恩来总理在今天下午接见了德意志民主共和国副总理兼对外与东西德贸易部部长、德国统一社会党中央政治局委员、德意志民主共和国人民议院议员海因里希·劳和德意志民主共和国政府代表团全体团员：德国统一社会党中央政治局候补委员、德国统一社会党大柏林专区委员会第一书记、民主德国全国阵线委员会委员、德意志民主共和国人民议院议员保罗·维尔纳，外交部第一副部长、德国统一社会党中央委员、德意志民主共和国人民议院议员奥托·文策尔，德意志民主共和国驻中国大使保罗·汪戴尔。在接见的时候，周恩来总理同贵宾们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有国务院副总理陈毅、李先念，中共中央委员伍修权，外交部副部长曾涌泉，对外贸易部副部长雷任民、林海云，国家计划委员会副主任刘明夫，科学技术委员会副主任武衡。
    德意志民主共和国政府代表团的贵宾们是今天下午由上海乘专机返回北京的。
    新华社上海18日电　海因里希·劳副总理和他率领的德意志民主共和国政府代表团，结束了在上海的访问以后，今天上午在外交部副部长曾涌泉等陪同下，乘飞机离开上海返回北京。
    到机场欢送的，有中共上海市委书记处书记、上海市副市长曹荻秋，中共上海市委书记处候补书记、上海市副市长刘述周，上海市副市长牛树才、金仲华，中国人民解放军上海警备区司令员王必成中将，以及上海市人民委员会各委局、政协上海市委员会和各人民团体的负责人，中德友好协会在上海的理事等。
    到机场欢送的还有德意志民主共和国驻上海代理总领事阿克曼、各国驻上海领事和在上海帮助建设的德意志民主共和国专家。（附图片）
