Title: Discussion by the Economic Geography Teaching and Research Section of Renmin University of China: What are the Objects and Tasks of Economic Geography?
Author:
Date: 1960-01-12
Page: 7

The Economic Geography Teaching and Research Section of Renmin University of China recently held an academic discussion on the subject and tasks of economic geography.

Some comrades believe that economic geography is the science of studying the laws of production distribution. Production distribution is a form of material movement that reflects production. Under certain conditions, this form repeats, forming a law. The most basic content of this law is that production should be close to the source of raw materials and the consumption areas. Other comrades believe that the study of economic geography must not be separated from specific regions, but should take regions under certain social conditions as the object. Therefore, it is not only the study of production distribution, but also the science of studying regional economic complexes. Still other comrades believe that this science studies the characteristics of production development in various countries and regions, the differences and regularities of geographical distribution of production, the types of regional production complexes, and the conditions under which these characteristics, differences, regularities, and types are formed.

Some comrades believe that the research object of economic geography is the characteristics of economic development and the spatial combination of economies in various countries and regions. Because it specifically studies the economic phenomena of various countries and regions, it also describes and explains the economic features and economic spatial combinations of various countries and regions from a geographical perspective. Although this feature includes production distribution, production distribution is not the object of this science.

Some comrades proposed that Marxist economic geography is a science that studies the layout of social productive forces, the conditions and characteristics of production development in modern countries and regions. It differs from related social and economic sciences and natural sciences in that it conducts comprehensive research. Another view is that in socialist countries, economic geography comprehensively, integrally, and also roughly studies the production allocation of countries and regions, studies how various different socio-historical conditions, economic and natural conditions individually and comprehensively affect production allocation, and studies the general laws of production allocation under different social systems and the specific regularities of production allocation in different economic types of regions.

### 中国人民大学经济地理教研室讨论  经济地理学的对象和任务是什么

1960-01-12
第7版()
专栏：学术文化动态

    中国人民大学经济地理教研室讨论
    经济地理学的对象和任务是什么
    中国人民大学经济地理教研室，不久前就经济地理学的对象和任务问题举行了学术讨论会。
    有的同志认为，经济地理学是研究生产分布规律的科学。生产分布是反映生产这一物质运动的一种形式，在一定的条件下，这种形式重复出现，形成一种规律。这规律的最基本的内容就是生产要接近原料地和消费地。有的同志则认为，经济地理学的研究绝不能离开具体的地区，而是以一定社会条件下的区域为对象的。因此，它不仅是研究生产分布的，而且是研究地区经济综合体的科学。也有同志认为，这门科学是研究各国各地区生产发展的特点，生产地理分布的差异和规律性，生产地域综合的类型，以及这些特点、差异、规律性、类型所借以形成的诸条件的科学。
    有些同志认为，经济地理学的研究对象是各国各地区经济发展的特点和经济的空间结合状况。由于它是具体地研究各国各地区的经济现象，所以它也即是从地理角度来描述和解释各国各地区的经济面貌和经济空间的结合的特征。这特征虽包括生产分布在内，但生产分布并不是这门科学的对象。
    有的同志提出，马克思主义经济地理学是以研究现代各国各地区社会生产力布局、生产发展的条件和特点为对象的科学，它和有关的社会经济科学、自然科学不同之点在于它进行全面的研究。也有一种意见认为，在社会主义国家中，经济地理学是全面地、综合性地、同时也是轮廓地研究国家与地区的生产配置，研究各种不同社会历史条件、经济与自然条件如何单独地、综合地影响着生产配置，研究在不同社会制度下生产配置的一般规律以及不同经济类型地区的生产配置的具体规律性。
