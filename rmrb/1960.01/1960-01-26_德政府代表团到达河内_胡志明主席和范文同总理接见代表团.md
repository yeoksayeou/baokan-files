Title: German Government Delegation Arrives in Hanoi; Chairman Ho Chi Minh and Premier Pham Van Dong Receive the Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-01-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 25th: At the invitation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, led by Deputy Prime Minister and Minister for Foreign and Intra-German Trade Heinrich Rau, arrived in Hanoi by plane this morning for a friendly visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Welcoming the distinguished guests at Gia Lam Airport were Deputy Prime Ministers of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Pham Hung, Truong Chinh, and Phan Ke Toai, ministers and deputy ministers of various government departments, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Vietnam.

The distinguished guests were warmly applauded and cheered by the welcoming crowds at the airport.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 25th: Chairman Ho Chi Minh and Premier Pham Van Dong of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam received the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, led by Deputy Prime Minister Heinrich Rau, this afternoon.

Deputy Prime Minister Pham Hung of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam held a grand banquet tonight to warmly welcome the German government delegation.

In his speech, he warmly praised the German people for their resolute and heroic struggle for peace, democracy, and unity. He said that in this struggle, the Vietnamese people stand shoulder to shoulder with the German people.

Deputy Prime Minister Heinrich Rau then spoke. He highly praised the achievements made by the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in implementing its Three-Year Plan. Deputy Prime Minister Heinrich Rau strongly condemned U.S. imperialism for supporting the Ngo Dinh Diem clique, violating the Geneva Accords, establishing military bases in South Vietnam, and undermining the peaceful reunification of Vietnam. He expressed that the German people deeply sympathize with the struggle of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and are willing to support the Vietnamese people's struggle with all possible strength.

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, January 25th: Deputy Prime Minister Heinrich Rau and the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic he led left Guangzhou by special plane this morning for a visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

### 德政府代表团到达河内  胡志明主席和范文同总理接见代表团

1960-01-26
第6版()
专栏：

    德政府代表团到达河内
    胡志明主席和范文同总理接见代表团
    据新华社河内25日电　应越南民主共和国的邀请，由德意志民主共和国副总理兼对外与东西德贸易部长海因里希·劳率领的德意志民主共和国政府代表团今天上午坐飞机到达河内。代表团将在越南民主共和国进行友好访问。
    到嘉林机场欢迎贵宾的有越南民主共和国副总理范雄、长征、潘继遂，政府各部正、副部长和各国驻越南的外交使节。
    贵宾们在机场上受到前来欢迎的群众的热烈鼓掌和欢呼。
    据新华社河内25日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明和总理范文同今天下午分别接见了德意志民主共和国副总理海因里希·劳率领的德意志民主共和国政府代表团。
    越南民主共和国副总理范雄今天晚上隆重举行宴会，热烈欢迎德国政府代表团。
    他在讲话中热烈地赞扬了德国人民为和平、民主和统一而进行的坚决和英勇的斗争。他说，在这个斗争中，越南人民同德国人民并肩站在一起。
    海因里希·劳副总理接着讲话。他盛赞了越南民主共和国为实现三年计划所取得的成就。海因里希·劳副总理强烈谴责美帝国主义扶植吴庭艳集团，违反日内瓦协议，在南越建立军事基地，破坏越南的和平统一。他表示，德国人民深深地同情越南民主共和国的斗争，并愿尽可能的力量支持越南人民的斗争。
    新华社广州25日电　海因里希·劳副总理和他率领的德意志民主共和国政府代表团，今天上午乘专机离开广州前往越南民主共和国访问。
