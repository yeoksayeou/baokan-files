Title: State Council Holds Ninety-Fifth Plenary Meeting, Approves Signing of Sino-Burmese Boundary Agreement and Treaty of Friendship
Author: 
Date: 1960-01-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 28th. The State Council held its ninety-fifth plenary meeting today to discuss the issue of signing the "Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Union of Burma on the Question of the Boundary between China and Burma" and the "Treaty of Friendship and Mutual Non-Aggression between the People's Republic of China and the Union of Burma." It agreed to sign the aforementioned agreement and treaty, and proposed to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress that Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council be appointed as the plenipotentiary representative for the signing of the agreement and treaty.

### 国务院举行第九十五次全体会议  同意签订中缅边界协定和友好条约

1960-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行第九十五次全体会议
    同意签订中缅边界协定和友好条约
    新华社28日讯　国务院今天举行第九十五次全体会议，讨论了同缅甸联邦签订“中华人民共和国政府和缅甸联邦政府关于两国边界问题的协定”和“中华人民共和国和缅甸联邦之间的友好和互不侵犯条约”的问题，同意签订上述协定和条约，并且提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会决定派国务院总理周恩来为签订上述协定和条约的全权代表。
