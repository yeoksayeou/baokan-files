Title: Tsedenbal Receives Chinese Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-01-14
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, January 13th: Tsedenbal, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, received this afternoon the entire Chinese government delegation headed by Xie Fusheng, Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, which is handing over the wool textile factory. Present at the reception were Molomjamts, First Deputy Chairman of the Mongolian Council of Ministers, Deputy Chairman Lubusan, Lubusang Qiaomubuer, head of the Mongolian government delegation for receiving the wool textile factory and Minister of Industry, and others.

### 泽登巴尔接见我国政府代表团

1960-01-14
第6版()
专栏：

    泽登巴尔
    接见我国政府代表团
    新华社乌兰巴托13日电　蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记、部长会议主席泽登巴尔今天下午接见了以中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生为首的中国政府移交毛纺织厂的代表团全体人员。接见时在座的有，蒙古部长会议第一副主席莫洛姆扎木茨，副主席鲁布桑，蒙古政府接收毛纺织厂代表团团长、工业部长鲁布桑乔姆博尔等人。
