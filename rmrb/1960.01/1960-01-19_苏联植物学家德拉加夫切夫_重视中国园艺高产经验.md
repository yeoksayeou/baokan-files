Title: Soviet Botanist Dragavtsev Values China's High-Yielding Horticultural Experience
Author:
Date: 1960-01-19
Page: 6

Soviet botanist Professor Dragavtsev, a famous botanist from Kazakhstan, said that the study of China's high-yield horticultural experience has aroused great interest in the Soviet Union. The quantitative and qualitative changes that have taken place in China's fruit tree cultivation industry in the past decade are unprecedented in China's plant cultivation industry. Thanks to the various superb agricultural techniques formulated according to natural conditions, China became one of the countries with the highest horticultural yields in 1958.

He also said that it has been proven that mandarin oranges, loquat trees, and some other Chinese fruit trees have played an important role in the formation of the Soviet subtropical fruit tree industry. He believes that certain types of Chinese pear trees, apricot trees, peach trees, chestnut trees, and many other fruit trees will be of great significance to further enriching and improving the varieties of Soviet orchards. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 苏联植物学家德拉加夫切夫  重视中国园艺高产经验

1960-01-19
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联植物学家德拉加夫切夫
    重视中国园艺高产经验
    苏联哈萨克著名植物学家德拉加夫切夫教授说，研究中国园艺方面获得高产的经验引起了苏联极大的兴趣。最近十年内，中国果树栽培业中所发生的量和质的变化在中国植物栽培业中是史无前例的。由于根据自然条件而制定的各种高超的农业技术，中国在1958年成了园艺方面收获量最高的国家之一。
    他还说，已经证实，柑树、枇杷树和其他一些中国果树在形成苏联亚热带果木业中已经起了重要的作用。他认为，某些种类的中国梨树、杏树、桃树、栗树和其他许多果树对进一步丰富和改善苏联果树园的品种会有很大的意义。　（据新华社讯）
