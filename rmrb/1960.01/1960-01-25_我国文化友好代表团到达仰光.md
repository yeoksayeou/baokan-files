Title: Chinese Cultural Friendship Delegation Arrives in Rangoon
Author:
Date: 1960-01-25
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 24th: The Chinese Cultural Friendship Delegation, headed by Chu Tunan, arrived here by plane this morning. At the invitation of the government of the Union of Burma, the delegation is leading the Chinese National Song and Dance Troupe on a month-long visit to Burma.

The delegation and the song and dance troupe were warmly welcomed at the airport by Burmese Minister of Culture U Chit Thoung and senior officials from the Ministry of Culture. Burmese artists presented flowers to the delegation. Lei Yang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, as well as representatives from Vietnam and North Korea to Burma, also welcomed them at the airport.

The Chinese National Song and Dance Troupe will begin performing in Rangoon on January 25th.

### 我国文化友好代表团到达仰光

1960-01-25
第6版()
专栏：

    我国文化友好代表团到达仰光
    新华社仰光24日电　以楚图南为首的中国文化友好代表团今天上午乘飞机到达这里。代表团应缅甸联邦政府的邀请，率领中国民族歌舞团，将在缅甸作为时一个月的访问。
    代表团和歌舞团在机场受到缅甸文化部长吴漆东和文化部高级官员们的热烈欢迎。缅甸艺术家们向代表团献花。中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办雷扬以及越南和朝鲜驻缅甸的代表也到机场欢迎。
    中国民族歌舞团将从1月25日开始在仰光演出。
