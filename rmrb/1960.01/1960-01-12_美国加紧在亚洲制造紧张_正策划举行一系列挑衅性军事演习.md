Title: U.S. Intensifies Tension Creation in Asia; Planning a Series of Provocative Military Exercises
Author:
Date: 1960-01-12
Page: 6
    The United States is intensifying the creation of tension in Asia and is planning to hold a series of provocative military exercises.
    (Reported by this newspaper) Under the planning of the United States, the Southeast Asia aggressive bloc will hold another series of provocative military exercises soon.
  According to a Bangkok dispatch, the Thai naval authorities announced on the 8th that the naval mine warfare exercise jointly launched by the United States and Thailand will be held from January 18th to 23rd in the Sattahip base area of the Thai Navy. This exercise is one of a series of exercises conducted by the United States in the Southeast Asian region to promote its aggressive plans. Five U.S. minesweepers and seven Thai warships will participate in the exercise. U.S. warships will visit Bangkok before the exercise.
    Furthermore, according to the headquarters of the "Southeast Asia Treaty Organization" military bloc, five Southeast Asia Treaty Organization countries, including the United States, Britain, France, Thailand, and Australia, will participate in amphibious reconnaissance exercises from January 28th to February 7th in the Rayong area of the Gulf of Siam, Thailand.
    All member states of this aggressive military bloc will send observers to visit this exercise, which aims to intensify the tense situation in Southeast Asia. According to a report from Bangkok, Southeast Asia Treaty Organization Secretary-General, Pote Sarasin, declared in Bangkok on the 5th that the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization Council meeting would be held in Washington on May 23rd this year. Sarasin specifically pointed out that the date of the meeting is after the summit meeting.

### 美国加紧在亚洲制造紧张  正策划举行一系列挑衅性军事演习

1960-01-12
第6版()
专栏：

    美国加紧在亚洲制造紧张
    正策划举行一系列挑衅性军事演习
    本报讯　在美国的策划下，东南亚侵略集团最近又将举行一连串挑衅性的军事演习。
  据曼谷消息，泰国海军当局8日宣布，美国和泰国联合发起的海军水雷战演习，将从1月18日到23日在泰国海军的梭桃邑基地地区举行。这次演习是美国在东南亚地区推行侵略计划举行的一系列演习中的一次。美国的五艘扫雷舰和泰国的七艘军舰将参加这次演习。美国军舰将在演习之前访问曼谷。
    又据“东南亚条约组织”军事集团总部宣布，五个东南亚条约组织国家，包括美国、英国、法国、泰国和澳大利亚，将参加从1月28日到2月7日在泰国暹罗湾的罗勇地区举行的水陆两栖侦察演习。
    这个侵略性军事集团的所有成员国，都将派遣观察员参观这次旨在于加剧东南亚地区紧张局势的演习。来自曼谷的消息说，东南亚条约组织秘书长沙拉信5日在曼谷宣称，东南亚条约组织理事会会议，将在今年5月23日在华盛顿举行。沙拉信特别指出，举行会议的日期，是在最高级会议以后。
