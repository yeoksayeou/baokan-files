Title: NPC Standing Committee, State Council, and CPPCC National Committee Hold New Year's Eve Celebration to Celebrate Victory; Party and State Leaders Attend Reception and Evening Party; Chairman Liu Shaoqi Speaks at Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-01-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st January. The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the State Council, and the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference jointly held a reception and New Year's Eve party in the Great Hall of the People on the evening of New Year's Day 1960 to celebrate the great achievements made by the people of the whole country in 1959 and to wish for even greater victories in 1960.

Party and state leaders Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Deng Xiaoping, and others attended the reception and party. Chairman Liu Shaoqi spoke at the reception, and his speech was warmly welcomed.

Also present at the reception and party were members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee Peng Zhen, Luo Ronghuan, Chen Yi, and He Long; alternate members of the Political Bureau Lu Dingyi, Kang Sheng, and Bo Yibo; alternate secretaries of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee Yang Shangkun and Hu Qiaomu; Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Chen Shutong, and Lin Feng; Vice Premiers of the State Council Nie Rongzhen, Luo Ruiqing, and Xi Zhongxun; Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC Bao Erhan; Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong and Fu Zuoyi; as well as responsible persons from various departments of the state organs, responsible persons from various departments of the CPC Central Committee, responsible persons from various democratic parties and people's organizations, generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, deputies to the National People's Congress and members of the CPPCC in Beijing, responsible persons from various departments in Beijing Municipality, and people from all walks of life in the capital.

After the reception, a grand evening party began. Party and state leaders and people from all walks of life enjoyed Peking opera, Kunqu opera, Sichuan opera, and films; some watched table tennis exhibition matches and Chinese chess exhibition matches; and some danced ballroom dances. The entire Great Hall of the People was filled with the joy of victory. (Attached picture)

Right picture: Chairman Liu Shaoqi raises his glass to celebrate the New Year after speaking at the reception.
Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Qingbiao

### 人大常委会、国务院和政协全国委员会  元旦联欢祝胜利  党和国家领导人出席酒会和晚会，刘少奇主席在会上讲了话

1960-01-02
第1版()
专栏：

    人大常委会、国务院和政协全国委员会
    元旦联欢祝胜利
    党和国家领导人出席酒会和晚会，刘少奇主席在会上讲了话
    新华社1日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会、国务院和政协全国委员会，1960年元旦晚上在人民大会堂联合举行酒会和联欢晚会，欢庆全国人民在1959年取得的伟大成就，预祝1960年获得更伟大的胜利。
    党和国家的领导人刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、邓小平等，出席了酒会和晚会。刘少奇主席在酒会上讲了话，他的讲话受到了热烈的欢迎。
    出席酒会和晚会的，还有中共中央政治局委员彭真、罗荣桓、陈毅、贺龙，政治局候补委员陆定一、康生、薄一波，中央书记处候补书记杨尚昆、胡乔木，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若、黄炎培、陈叔通、林枫，国务院副总理聂荣臻、罗瑞卿、习仲勋，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，国防委员会副主席张治中、傅作义，以及国家机关各部门的负责人，中共中央各部门的负责人，各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人，中国人民解放军的将军们，在京的全国人民代表大会代表和政协委员，北京市各部门的负责人和首都各界人士。
    酒会结束以后，盛大的晚会开始。党和国家领导人及社会各界人士，有的欣赏京剧、昆曲、川剧、电影，有的观看乒乓球表演赛和象棋表演赛，有的跳交谊舞。整个人民大会堂里，充满了胜利的欢乐。（附图片）
    右图：刘少奇主席在酒会上讲话后，举杯祝贺新年。
