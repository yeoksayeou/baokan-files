International Briefs

Security Council Supports Cameroon's Admission to the United Nations
    New York dispatch The United Nations Security Council has unanimously adopted a decision supporting the Republic of Cameroon's application to join the United Nations and recommending that the next session of the United Nations General Assembly accept the application.

British 500,000 Railway Workers to Hold General Strike
    London dispatch The British National Union of Railwaymen decided on the evening of the 29th to hold a general strike on February 15. The union demanded an immediate increase in the wages of 500,000 railway workers, which was rejected by the British official Transport Commission. The union's leadership issued a statement saying that there was "no other choice" but to stop work.

### 国际简讯

1960-01-31
第3版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    安理会支持喀麦隆加入联合国
    纽约讯　联合国安全理事会已经一致通过决定，支持喀麦隆共和国关于加入联合国的申请，并且建议联合国大会下一届会议接受申请。
    英五十万铁路工人将总罢工
    伦敦讯　英国全国铁路工会29日晚上决定在2月15日举行总罢工。这个工会要求立即增加五十万铁路工人的工资，遭到了英国官方的运输委员会的拒绝。工会的领导机构发表声明说，除了停止工作以外，“别无其他抉择”。
