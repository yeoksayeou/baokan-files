Title: President Guo Moruo Hosts Scientists from Brotherly Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-01-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 28th: Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, hosted a Spring Festival banquet tonight to entertain scientists from the Soviet Union, the German Democratic Republic, Czechoslovakia, and Hungary who are helping the institutes of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the China University of Science and Technology.

Invited to the banquet were more than 40 scientists from various brotherly countries in Beijing, including Soviet philosopher and Corresponding Academician Yovchuk, educator and Corresponding Academician Kedrov, German Democratic Republic electronics expert Dr. Kopf, Czechoslovak philosopher Dr. Kala, and Hungarian physicist Szalai Fedacs.

Attending as hosts were Li Siguang, Zhang Jingfu, Tao Menghe, Zhu Kezhen, and Wu Youxun, Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Secretary-General Pei Lisheng, and more than 100 people, including heads of research institutions in Beijing and prominent scientists.

In his speech at the banquet, Guo Moruo expressed his gratitude to the scientists of the brotherly countries for their outstanding contributions to China's scientific cause, and raised his glass to congratulate the overall upsurge of the socialist and communist cause of the brotherly countries headed by the Soviet Union, the overall development of the scientific cause, and the comprehensive and thorough victory of Marxism-Leninism.

Corresponding Academician Yovchuk then delivered a speech, extending his best wishes to the Chinese comrades for a happy traditional Spring Festival, and congratulating them on the greater achievements made in China's scientific undertakings and the work of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

### 郭沫若院长招待兄弟国家科学家

1960-01-29
第2版()
专栏：

    郭沫若院长招待兄弟国家科学家
    新华社28日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若今晚举行春节宴会，招待帮助中国科学院各研究所和中国科学技术大学工作的苏联、德意志民主共和国、捷克斯洛伐克和匈牙利的科学家。
    应邀出席宴会的有苏联哲学家约夫楚克通讯院士、教育学家凯得洛夫通讯院士、德意志民主共和国电子学家柯甫博士、捷克斯洛伐克哲学家卡拉博士、匈牙利物理学家萨尔卡依·费达茨等在北京的各兄弟国家科学家共四十多人。
    出席作陪的有中国科学院副院长李四光、张劲夫、陶孟和、竺可桢、吴有训，秘书长裴丽生等以及北京各研究机构的负责人、著名科学家共百余人。
    郭沫若在宴会上致词感谢各兄弟国家科学家对中国科学事业作出的卓越贡献，并举杯祝贺以苏联为首的各兄弟国家的社会主义和共产主义事业的全面高涨、科学事业的全面发展以及马克思列宁主义的全面的彻底的胜利。
    约夫楚克通讯院士接着致词，他向中国同志祝贺传统的春节愉快；祝贺中国科学事业和中国科学院的工作取得更大的成就。
