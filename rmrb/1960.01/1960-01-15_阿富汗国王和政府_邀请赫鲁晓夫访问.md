Title: Afghan King and Government Invite Khrushchev to Visit
Author:
Date: 1960-01-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 14th: TASS, Moscow, 13th: King Mohammed Zahir Shah of Afghanistan and the Afghan government have invited Khrushchev to visit Afghanistan in February on his return from Indonesia.
    Khrushchev gratefully accepted the invitation.

### 阿富汗国王和政府  邀请赫鲁晓夫访问

1960-01-15
第6版()
专栏：

    阿富汗国王和政府
    邀请赫鲁晓夫访问
    新华社莫斯科14日电　塔斯社莫斯科13日讯：阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿和阿富汗政府邀请赫鲁晓夫在2月份由印度尼西亚回国时访问阿富汗。
    赫鲁晓夫以感谢的心情接受了这一邀请。
