Title: US Intensifies Tension in the Far East; Southeast Asia Aggression Bloc to Hold Amphibious Exercise
Author:
Date: 1960-01-03
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, January 1st: Official sources here announced tonight that the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization's amphibious reconnaissance exercise, initiated by the United States and Thailand, will be held from January 28th to February 7th in the Rayong area of the Gulf of Siam. Participating in this exercise, aimed at intensifying the tense situation in the Southeast Asian region by this aggressive military bloc, will be the "frogman" underwater demolition teams, reconnaissance teams, mine disposal teams, as well as a submarine and other naval vessels from the United States, Britain, France, Thailand, and Australia. All member states of this military bloc will send observers to visit this exercise.

### 美国加剧远东紧张局势  东南亚侵略集团将举行两栖演习

1960-01-03
第5版()
专栏：

    美国加剧远东紧张局势
    东南亚侵略集团将举行两栖演习
    据新华社卡拉奇1日电　此间官方今夜宣布，美国和泰国发起的东南亚条约组织的两栖侦察演习将从1月28日到2月7日在暹罗湾的罗勇区域举行。参加这个侵略性军事集团的这次目的在于加剧东南亚地区紧张局势的演习的，将有美国、英国、法国、泰国和澳大利亚的“蛙人”水底破坏队、侦察队、水雷处理队以及一艘潜水艇和其他海军舰只。这个军事集团的所有成员国将派观察员参观这次演习。
