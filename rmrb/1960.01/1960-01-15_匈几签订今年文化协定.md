Title: Hungary and Guinea Sign This Year's Cultural Agreement
Author:
Date: 1960-01-15
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Konakry, January 13th: An agreement on cultural cooperation for 1960 was signed between the Hungarian People's Republic and the Republic of Guinea in Konakry on the 12th. The agreement stipulates that the two countries will cooperate in the fields of art, science, and culture.

    The agreement was signed by Hungarian People's Republic Minister of Foreign Affairs, Sik Andrei, and the Republic of Guinea's Minister of National Education, Barry Diawandou.

### 匈几签订今年文化协定

1960-01-15
第6版()
专栏：

    匈几签订今年文化协定
    新华社13日讯　塔斯社科纳克里讯：匈牙利人民共和国和几内亚共和国之间12日在科纳克里签订了一项文化协定。协定规定两国将在艺术、科学和文化方面进行合作。
    这项协定是由匈牙利人民共和国外交部部长西克·安德烈和几内亚共和国国民教育部部长巴里·迪亚万杜签署的。
