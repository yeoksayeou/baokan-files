Title: Japanese Women's Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-01-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 11th -- The Japanese Women's Delegation, consisting of four members led by Ms. Takegami Masako, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included Li Dequan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, and Shen Zijiu, member of the Presidium.

### 日妇女代表团到京

1960-01-12
第4版()
专栏：

    日妇女代表团到京
    新华社11日讯　应中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会邀请来我国访问的日本妇女代表团一行四人，在团长竹上正子率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有全国妇联副主席李德全、主席团委员沈兹九等人。
