Title: Instrument of Ratification of the Treaty Between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia on the Question of Dual Nationality

Author:

Date: 1960-01-26
Page: 1

The signing ceremony for the exchange of instruments of ratification was held in Beijing. The treaty and the exchange of letters between the two Prime Ministers came into effect simultaneously. The Joint Committee for the implementation of the treaty on dual nationality between the two countries was established in Jakarta yesterday.

Xinhua News Agency, January 25th - The press communique regarding the exchange of instruments of ratification in Beijing concerning The Treaty between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia on the Question of Dual Nationality is as follows:

Press Communique (January 25, 1960)

The Treaty between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia on the Question of Dual Nationality, signed in Bandung on April 22, 1955, has been ratified by both parties in accordance with their respective constitutional procedures. The instruments of ratification of the treaty were exchanged in Beijing on January 20, 1960. Representing their respective governments in signing the certificates of exchange of instruments of ratification of the treaty were, on the Chinese side, Foreign Minister Chen Yi, and on the Indonesian side, Indonesian Ambassador to China Sukardjo. Both sides' representatives delivered speeches at the ceremony of exchanging the instruments of ratification.

Premier of the State Council of China Zhou Enlai attended the signing ceremony for the exchange of instruments of ratification.

Also attending the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were: Liao Chengzhi, Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Wenjin, Director of the First Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Yao Zhongming, Director of the Treaty and Law Department, Qin Lizhen, Director of the Consular Department, and Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department. On the Indonesian side were: Counselor of the Embassy Suleiman, First Secretary Yowano, Third Secretary Dawato, Third Secretary Soeratmo, and Attache Kasari Hamid.

According to the provisions of the treaty, the treaty on dual nationality came into effect on the date of the exchange of instruments of ratification.

According to the provisions of the exchange of letters between the two Prime Ministers on June 3, 1955, regarding the implementation measures for the treaty on the question of dual nationality, the exchange of letters between the two Prime Ministers and the treaty on dual nationality came into effect simultaneously.

The exchange of letters between the two Prime Ministers stipulates the establishment of a Joint Committee in Jakarta, composed of representatives from both the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia. The Chinese government has appointed Ambassador Huang Zhen as the chief representative of the Chinese side on the Joint Committee. The Indonesian government has appointed Susanto Tirtoprodjo, Director of the Legal Department of the Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, as the chief representative of the Indonesian side on the Joint Committee. The Joint Committee was established in Jakarta on January 25, 1960, and Indonesian Foreign Minister Dr. Subandrio attended the establishment ceremony.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, January 25th - The Joint Committee for the implementation of the treaty between China and Indonesia on the Question of Dual Nationality was established at noon today in Jakarta. Indonesian Foreign Minister Subandrio attended the establishment ceremony of the committee. Attending the ceremony on the Chinese side were Huang Zhen, the chief representative of China attending the Joint Implementation Committee and Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia, Liu Yufeng, representative and Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Indonesia, as well as Zhao Zhengyi, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy, and Li Jusheng, Chinese Consul General in Jakarta; Attending the ceremony on the Indonesian side were Tirtoprodjo, the Indonesian chief representative and Director of the Legal Department of the Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and all the representatives, Suwito, Secretary-General of the Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Hasona, Acting Director of the Information Department of the Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Subandrio, Huang Zhen, and Tirtoprodjo all delivered speeches at the ceremony.

The representatives of both sides took a photograph when the ceremony was over. The date of the next meeting of the Joint Committee will be determined at another time.

### 中华人民共和国和印度尼西亚共和国互换双重国籍问题条约批准书

1960-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国和印度尼西亚共和国
    互换双重国籍问题条约批准书
    互换批准书的签字仪式在北京举行，条约和两国总理的换文同时生效为实施两国关于双重国籍问题条约的联合委员会昨日已在雅加达成立
    新华社25日讯　关于中华人民共和国和印度尼西亚共和国就双重国籍问题的条约在北京互换批准书的新闻公报，全文如下：
    新闻公报（1960年1月25日）
    中华人民共和国和印度尼西亚共和国1955年4月22日在万隆签订的关于双重国籍问题的条约，已由双方按照各自的宪法程序加以批准。1960年1月20日双方在北京互换了条约的批准书。代表本国政府在互换条约批准书证书上签字的，中国方面为外交部长陈毅，印度尼西亚方面为印度尼西亚驻华大使苏加佐。在互换批准书的仪式上，双方代表发表了讲话。
    中国国务院总理周恩来参加了互换批准书签字的仪式。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面还有：华侨事务委员会主任廖承志、外交部副部长章汉夫、外交部第一亚洲司司长章文晋、条约法律司司长姚仲明、领事司司长秦力真和礼宾司副司长马振武。印度尼西亚方面有：驻华大使馆参赞苏雷曼、一等秘书约瓦诺、三等秘书达瓦托、三等秘书苏拉脱莫和随员卡沙里·哈密。
    根据条约的规定，关于双重国籍问题的条约在互换批准书之日起生效。
    根据两国总理1955年6月3日关于双重国籍问题的条约实施办法的换文的规定，两国总理的换文和双重国籍条约同时生效。
    两国总理换文规定，在雅加达成立一个由中华人民共和国和印度尼西亚共和国双方代表组成的联合委员会。中国政府已经任命黄镇大使为联合委员会中中国方面的首席代表。印度尼西亚政府已任命印度尼西亚外交部法律司司长苏山多·蒂托普罗佐为联合委员会中印度尼西亚方面的首席代表。联合委员会已于1960年1月25日在雅加达成立，印度尼西亚外交部长苏班德里约博士参加了成立仪式。
    新华社雅加达25日电　为实施中国和印度尼西亚关于双重国籍问题的条约的联合委员会今天中午在雅加达成立。印度尼西亚外交部长苏班德里约参加了委员会的成立仪式。中国方面参加仪式的，有中国出席联合实施委员会的首席代表、中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇和代表、中国驻印度尼西亚大使馆参赞柳雨峰，以及中国大使馆参赞赵政一和中国驻雅加达总领事李菊生；印度尼西亚方面参加仪式的，有印度尼西亚首席代表、印度尼西亚外交部法律司司长蒂托普罗佐和所有的代表，印度尼西亚外交部秘书长苏维托以及印度尼西亚外交部新闻司代理司长哈索纳。苏班德里约、黄镇和蒂托普罗佐都在仪式上讲了话。
    仪式完毕时，双方代表摄了影。联合委员会下次会议的日期将在另一个时候确定。
