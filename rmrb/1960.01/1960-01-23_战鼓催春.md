Title: War Drums Urge Spring

Author: 
Date: 1960-01-23
Page: 8

    War Drums Urge Spring
    Heroes stride into the year '60,
    Opening the door brings joy, a sea of red,
    Steel production reports fly like snowflakes,
    The vast countryside bustles in "winter leisure."
    The war drums thunder through the sky,
    The red sun shines brightly, the spring scenery is good,
    Ten thousand horses gallop without stopping,
    A bright future lies on the sunny path.

### 战鼓催春

1960-01-23
第8版()
专栏：

    战鼓催春
    英雄跨进六○年，
    开门见喜红一片，
    钢铁捷报雪片飞，
    广大农村忙“冬闲”。
    战鼓咚咚震太空，
    红日当空春光好，
    万马奔腾不停蹄，
    锦绣前程阳关道。
