Song of the Construction Workers
Plumber Xue Dianju

　　Leap forward people welcome the leap forward year,
　　Construction workers swear an oath:
　　Determined to make the motherland change,
　　To become like a big garden.
　　I am a gardener in the garden,
　　I must be a "top scholar of flowers,"
　　This year I'll become a six-sided hand,
　　Reform tools in fifty ways.
　　No longer use shovels and picks to dig soil,
　　No longer use shoulders to carry soil,
　　Implement mechanization of construction,
　　Work efficiency doubles.

### 建筑工人之歌
薛殿举
1960-01-18
第2版()
专栏：

    建筑工人之歌
    水暖工　薛殿举
    跃进人迎跃进年，
    建筑工人发誓言：
    决心要叫祖国变，
    变得像个大花园。
    我是花园一花匠，
    一定当个“花状元”，
    今年变成六面手，
    改革工具五十件。
    挖土再不用锹镐，
    运土再不用肩担，
    施工实现机械化，
    工作效率成倍翻。
