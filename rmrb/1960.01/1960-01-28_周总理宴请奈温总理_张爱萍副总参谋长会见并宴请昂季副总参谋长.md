Title: Premier Zhou Hosts Prime Minister Ne Win; Deputy Chief of General Staff Zhang Aiping Meets and Hosts Deputy Chief of General Staff Aung Gyi
Author:
Date: 1960-01-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 27th: Premier Zhou Enlai hosted a luncheon today in honor of Prime Minister Ne Win and all the distinguished Burmese guests accompanying him on his visit to China.

Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi and responsible persons from relevant departments were present. Burmese Ambassador to China, Kyaw Winn, was also invited to attend.

Xinhua News Agency, January 27th: General Zhang Aiping, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, met this morning with Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Burmese Armed Forces, and Lieutenant Colonel Lun, Director of Military Intelligence, who are accompanying Prime Minister Ne Win on his visit to our country. Present at the meeting were Major General Wang Xinggang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense, Colonel Jiang Keding, Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, and Colonel Ye Xiuzhi, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of National Defense. Colonel Lwin Maung and Colonel Kyaing Shain, Military Attachés of the Burmese Embassy in China, were also present at the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, January 27th: General Zhang Aiping, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, held a banquet this evening in honor of Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Burmese Armed Forces, and Lieutenant Colonel Lun, Director of Military Intelligence, who are accompanying General Ne Win, Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, on his visit to our country.

Colonel Lwin Maung and Colonel Kyaing Shain, Military Attachés of the Burmese Embassy in China, were also invited to the banquet. Present were Air Force Lieutenant General Wang Bingzhang, Lieutenant General Gan Weihan, Lieutenant General Liu ShaoWen, Major General Wang Xinggang, and other senior generals, as well as Colonel Jiang Keding, Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Burma.

### 周总理宴请奈温总理  张爱萍副总参谋长会见并宴请昂季副总参谋长

1960-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理宴请奈温总理
    张爱萍副总参谋长会见并宴请昂季副总参谋长
    新华社27日讯　周恩来总理今天中午设便宴招待奈温总理和随他前来中国访问的缅甸全体贵宾。
    出席作陪的，有陈毅副总理兼外交部长和有关部门的负责人。缅甸驻中国大使叫温也应邀出席。
    新华社27日讯　中国人民解放军副总参谋长张爱萍上将今天上午会见了随同奈温总理来我国访问的缅甸国防军副总参谋长昂季准将、军事情报处长伦中校。会见时在座的有：国防部办公厅副主任王兴纲少将、中国驻缅甸大使馆武官蒋克定上校、国防部外事处副处长叶修直上校。会见时，缅甸驻中国大使馆武官柳当上校和敬沙门上校也在座。
    新华社27日讯　中国人民解放军副总参谋长张爱萍上将今天晚上举行宴会，宴请随同缅甸联邦总理奈温将军前来我国访问的缅甸联邦国防军副总参谋长昂季准将和军事情报处长伦中校。
    缅甸驻中国大使馆武官柳当上校、敬沙门上校也应邀出席了宴会。出席作陪的有：王秉璋空军中将、甘渭汉中将、刘少文中将、王兴纲少将等高级将领以及中国驻缅甸大使馆武官蒋克定上校。
