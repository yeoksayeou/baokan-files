Title: Strengthening Friendship and Cooperation Between the Two Peoples: German Government Delegation Arrives in Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1960-01-07
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, January 6th: A government delegation from the German Democratic Republic, headed by Deputy Prime Minister and Minister for Foreign and Intra-German Trade, Heinrich Rau, arrived in Ulaanbaatar by plane this afternoon. The delegation will be here for a three-day friendly visit.
    Welcoming the delegation at the airport were Mongolian Party and government leaders Tsedenbal, Molomjamts, Foreign Minister Shagdaršüren, and diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Mongolia.
    First Deputy Chairman of the Mongolian Council of Ministers, Molomjamts, delivered a welcoming speech at the airport. He said that the friendly cooperation based on the principles of Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism closely links the peoples of Mongolia and Germany. This visit by the delegation will surely further promote friendly cooperation between the two peoples.
    Heinrich Rau then spoke. He thanked the Mongolian government for the invitation and extended brotherly warm congratulations to the Mongolian people. He said that the people of the German Democratic Republic are very pleased to see the resolution of the Fourth Plenary Session of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, which will further accelerate the pace of Mongolia's development. That afternoon, the delegation paid a visit to Tsedenbal, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers.

### 加强两国人民的友好和合作  德政府代表团抵蒙

1960-01-07
第5版()
专栏：

    加强两国人民的友好和合作
    德政府代表团抵蒙
    新华社乌兰巴托6日电　以德意志民主共和国副总理、对外和东西德贸易部长海因里希·劳为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团，今天下午乘飞机抵达乌兰巴托。代表团将在这里进行三天的友好访问。
    到飞机场欢迎的有蒙古党政领导人曾德、莫洛姆扎木茨、外交部长沙格达尔苏伦以及各国驻蒙古的外交使节。
    蒙古部长会议第一副主席莫洛姆扎木茨在机场上致欢迎词。他说，以马克思列宁主义和无产阶级国际主义原则为基础的友好合作，把蒙德两国人民紧密地联结在一起。这次代表团的访问必将进一步促进两国人民的友好合作。
    海因里希·劳接着讲话。他感谢蒙古政府的邀请，并向蒙古人民致以兄弟般的热烈祝贺。他说，德意志民主共和国人民非常高兴地看到蒙古人民革命党四中全会的决议，这个决议将进一步加快蒙古的发展速度。当天下午，代表团拜会了蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记、部长会议主席泽登巴尔。
