Title: Sino-Indian Friendship Association Holds Reception to Celebrate Indian Republic Day
Author: 
Date: 1960-01-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, January 25th: The President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, Ding Xilin, held a reception this evening to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Republic of India on January 26th.

Attending today's reception were: members of the Council of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association in Beijing and well-known figures from all walks of life, including Xia Yan, Lao She, Ji Chaoding, Xie Bingxin, Chen Shuliang, and others.

The Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indian Embassy in China, C. V. K. Pillai, and embassy staff, as well as Indian experts, professors, and students in Beijing, were also invited to attend the reception.

### 中印友协举行酒会庆祝印度国庆

1960-01-26
第6版()
专栏：

    中印友协举行酒会庆祝印度国庆
    新华社25日讯　中国印度友好协会会长丁西林今天晚间举行酒会，庆祝1月26日印度共和国成立十周年。
    出席今天酒会的有：中印友协在京理事和各界知名人士夏衍、老舍、冀朝鼎、谢冰心、陈叔亮等。
    印度驻华大使馆临时代办康南皮利和大使馆人员，以及印度在京专家、教授和留学生也应邀出席了酒会。
