Title: Secretary-General of the Britain-China Friendship Association Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-01-31
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 29th — Mr. and Mrs. Deriabin, the Secretary-General of the Britain-China Friendship Association, who were invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China, left Beijing by plane this morning to return to their country.

Mr. and Mrs. Deriabin arrived in Beijing on December 14th last year. They visited the Sanmenxia, Chengdu, Chongqing, Kunming, Guangzhou, and Wuhan.

### 英中友协秘书长回国

1960-01-31
第3版()
专栏：

    英中友协秘书长回国
    据新华社29日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的英中友协秘书长德里本夫妇，今天早晨乘飞机离开北京回国。
    德里本夫妇是在去年12月14日到达北京的。他们在中国访问了三门峡、成都、重庆、昆明、广州、武汉等城市。
