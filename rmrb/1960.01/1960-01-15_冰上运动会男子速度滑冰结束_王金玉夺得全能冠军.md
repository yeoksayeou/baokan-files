Title: Ice Sports Meet Men's Speed Skating Concludes; Wang Jinyu Wins All-Around Championship
Author:
Date: 1960-01-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jilin, January 13th: The 1960 National Ice Sports Meet men's speed skating competition concluded entirely on the afternoon of the 13th. Wang Jinyu, an eighteen-year-old athlete from Heilongjiang, won the all-around championship (score of 196.583 points). The following are the rankings: Luo Zhihuan (200.005 points), Li Taiquan (200.826 points), and Yang Jucheng (201.570 points), all from Heilongjiang; and Li Fengzhe (205.190 points) and Wen Yongwu (206.436 points) from Jilin.

In the 1,500-meter and 10,000-meter speed skating competitions held on the morning and afternoon of the 13th, Wang Jinyu won both events, with times of 2 minutes 20.8 seconds and 17 minutes 49.2 seconds, respectively. The following are the rankings for the 1,500-meter race: Yang Jucheng, Li Taiquan, Luo Zhihuan, Zheng Hongdao, and Li Fengzhe. The following are the rankings for the 10,000-meter race: Luo Zhihuan, Li Taiquan, Li Tianhao, Shi Zhixin, and Wen Yongwu.

### 冰上运动会男子速度滑冰结束  王金玉夺得全能冠军

1960-01-15
第4版()
专栏：

    冰上运动会男子速度滑冰结束
    王金玉夺得全能冠军
    据新华社吉林13日电　1960年全国冰上运动会男子速度滑冰比赛13日下午全部结束。十八岁的黑龙江选手王金玉获得了全能冠军（成绩一九六点五八三分）。以下的名次是：黑龙江罗志焕（二○○点○○五分）、李太权（二○○点八二六分）、杨菊成（二○一点五七○分）和吉林的李凤哲（二○五点一九○分）、文永伍（二○六点四三六分）。
    13日上午和下午举行的一千五百米和一万米速度滑冰比赛中，王金玉夺得两项冠军，成绩分别是二分二十秒八和十七分四十九秒二。一千五百米比赛以下名次是：杨菊成、李太权、罗志焕、郑弘道和李凤哲。一万米比赛以下名次是：罗志焕、李太权、李天镐、石质信和文永伍。
