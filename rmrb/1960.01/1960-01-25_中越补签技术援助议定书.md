Title: China and Vietnam Supplement and Sign Technical Assistance Protocol
Author:
Date: 1960-01-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 24th. Due to the continuous development of scientific and technological cooperation between China and Vietnam, the amount of technical cooperation between the two countries in 1959 significantly increased compared to previous years. Based on the situation of cooperation between the two sides, a supplementary protocol on the provision of technical assistance by the Government of the People's Republic of China to the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in 1959 was signed. This protocol was signed in Beijing on December 31, 1959.

According to this protocol, China will send technical personnel to Vietnam to work in the fields of water conservancy, construction, meteorology, light industry, and railways. Vietnam will send personnel to China for observation and internships in the fields of light industry, transportation, aquatic products, medicine and health, banking operations, and statistics. In addition, China will donate technical data on locomotives, railway bridges, highway construction, meteorology, and leather manufacturing, as well as experimental instruments and articles for astronomy and agriculture, to Vietnam.

Wu Heng, Deputy Director of the Science and Technology Commission of the People's Republic of China, and Tran Tu Binh, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, signed the protocol.

The Chinese side participating in the signing ceremony included Luo Shuzhang, Vice Minister of Light Industry, Ma Hui zhi, Vice Minister of Communications, and Chen Shuliang, Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

The Vietnamese side participating in the signing ceremony included Tran Trong Quat, Counselor of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, and Deng Qingyun, Commercial Counselor.

### 中越补签技术援助议定书

1960-01-25
第4版()
专栏：

    中越补签技术援助议定书
    新华社24日讯　由于中越两国在科学技术合作方面的不断发展，1959年双方进行技术合作数量都比前几年有大量增加。根据双方进行合作的情况，补签了1959年中华人民共和国政府对越南民主共和国政府提供技术援助的议定书。这个议定书，已于1959年12月31日在北京签订。
    按照这个议定书，中国在水利、建筑、气象、轻工、铁路等方面派技术人员去越南工作。越南在轻工、交通、水产、医药卫生、银行业务、统计等方面派人来中国考察和实习。此外，中国赠给越南机车车辆、铁路桥梁、公路建筑、气象、皮革制造等方面的技术资料及天文、农业等方面的试验仪器和物品。
    在议定书上签字的是中华人民共和国科学技术委员会副主任武衡、越南民主共和国驻中国大使陈子平。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的，有轻工业部副部长罗叔章、交通部副部长马辉之、外交部第二亚洲司司长陈叔亮。
    越南方面参加签字仪式的，有越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆参赞陈仲括、商务参赞邓青云等。
