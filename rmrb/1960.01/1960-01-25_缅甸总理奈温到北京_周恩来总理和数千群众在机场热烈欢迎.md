Title: Burmese Prime Minister Ne Win Arrives in Beijing; Premier Zhou Enlai and Thousands of People Give a Warm Welcome at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1960-01-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 24th -- The Prime Minister of our friendly neighbor, the Union of Burma, General Ne Win, and his entourage, arrived in Beijing by special plane on the evening of the 24th for a friendly visit to our country.

At the Capital Airport, adorned with the national flags of China and Burma, the distinguished Burmese guests were warmly welcomed by Premier Zhou Enlai and other national leaders, as well as thousands of people. As Prime Minister Ne Win, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, met the welcoming crowds standing in the cold winter wind, people, filled with passion for Sino-Burmese friendship, enthusiastically shouted: "Welcome, Prime Minister Ne Win!" "Long live Sino-Burmese friendship!" "Long live the solidarity of Asian and African countries!" "Long live world peace!" to pay respect to Prime Minister Ne Win and other distinguished guests who brought the friendship of the Burmese people.

Accompanying Prime Minister Ne Win on the visit were: Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Union of Burma, U Chan Tun Aung; Attorney General of the Union of Burma, U Ba Swe; Deputy Chief of Staff, Brigadier Aung Gyi; Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, James Barrington; Prime Minister's Secretary, U Win Pe; Joint Secretary of the Border Areas Administration, U Khin Nyo; Director of the Survey Department, U Hla Kin Maung; Head of Military Intelligence, Lieutenant Colonel Lun; Director of the Asian-African Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, U Ba Chit; Director of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, U Soe Hlaing; Deputy Director of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, U Tun Tin, and others.

At 6:40 PM, night had already fallen. When Prime Minister Ne Win stepped out of the cabin under the glare of the airport spotlights, Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Chen Yi and Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Burhan Shahidi, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong and Fu Zuoyi, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and our country's ambassador to Burma Li Yimang, all went to the side of the plane to greet him. After the military band played the national anthems of Burma and China, Prime Minister Ne Win, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, reviewed the guard of honor. A group of Young Pioneers presented flowers to Prime Minister Ne Win and all the distinguished Burmese guests.

Also present at the airport to welcome the guests were the responsible persons of various departments of state organs, democratic parties, and people's organizations, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, responsible persons of Beijing, and people from all walks of life in the capital.

Diplomatic officials of the Burmese Embassy in China, as well as envoys and diplomatic officials of various countries in China, also went to the airport to welcome them.

Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of our country, and Burmese Ambassador to China, Kyaw Winn, accompanied Prime Minister Ne Win to Beijing today.

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, January 24th -- The Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, Ne Win, and his entourage, who are visiting our country at the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai, arrived in Guangzhou from Rangoon via Hong Kong by plane this morning. Burmese Ambassador to China, Kyaw Winn, also arrived in Guangzhou.

Guangzhou Airport was freshly decorated today. The national flags of China and Burma and various colored flags fluttered in the wind. At 10:20 AM, the special plane carrying the distinguished guests slowly landed, and enthusiastic applause immediately resounded among the welcoming crowd at the airport. Prime Minister Ne Win stepped out of the cabin and was immediately warmly welcomed by Zhu Guang, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province and Mayor of Guangzhou, Li Jiaren, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, and Lai Chunfeng, the responsible person of the troops stationed in Guangzhou. Then, the military band played the national anthems of Burma and China, and Prime Minister Ne Win, accompanied by Vice Governor Zhu Guang, reviewed the guard of honor. He also waved to the 1,500 people who were warmly welcoming them. Children from Guangzhou presented flowers to the distinguished guests.

At 10:40 AM, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of our country, who had rushed from Beijing to greet the distinguished guests, flew to Beijing.

Czechoslovak Consul in Guangzhou, Kopecky, Vietnamese Acting Consul General in Guangzhou, Le Chung Thuy, and others also went to the airport to see off the distinguished guests.

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, January 24th -- Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, Ne Win, made a short stop in Wuhan today. Zhang Tixue, Governor of Hubei Province, Liu Huinong, Mayor of Wuhan, and Zhang Guangcai, the responsible person of the Wuhan Garrison, went to the airport to welcome the distinguished guests.

After Prime Minister Ne Win had lunch in Wuhan, he took a special plane to Beijing at around 2:00 PM, accompanied by Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of our country.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 23rd -- The Prime Minister of Burma, General Ne Win, at the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, left here at 11:00 PM on the 23rd by special plane for a friendly visit to China. Accompanying General Ne Win on his visit to China were Minister of Foreign Affairs U Chan Tun Aung, Attorney General U Ba Swe, Deputy Chief of Staff Brigadier Aung Gyi, Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs James Barrington and other senior military and civilian officials.

Those who saw him off at Rangoon Airport were Chief Justice of the Supreme Court U Min Thein, former Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities Sao Hkun Hkio, members of the Cabinet, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Air Force Clift, Chairman of the National Security Council Colonel Maung Maung, Mayor of Rangoon Colonel Tun Sein and foreign diplomatic envoys in Rangoon.

### 缅甸总理奈温到北京  周恩来总理和数千群众在机场热烈欢迎

1960-01-25
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸总理奈温到北京
    周恩来总理和数千群众在机场热烈欢迎
    新华社24日讯　我国友好近邻——缅甸联邦总理奈温将军和他的随行人员，应邀来我国进行友好访问，于24日傍晚乘专机到达北京。
    缅甸贵宾们在飘扬着中国和缅甸两国国旗的首都机场上，受到周恩来总理等国家领导人和数千群众的热烈欢迎。当奈温总理由周恩来总理陪同和伫立在严冬寒风中的欢迎群众见面的时候，人们满怀中缅友好的激情，热烈高呼：“欢迎奈温总理！”“中缅友好万岁！”“亚非国家团结万岁！”“世界和平万岁！”向带来缅甸人民友谊的奈温总理和其他贵宾们致敬。
    随同奈温总理前来访问的，有缅甸联邦外交部长吴千吞昂，缅甸联邦检察总长吴巴盛，副总参谋长昂季准将，外交部常任秘书巴林顿，总理秘书吴温佩，边境地区管理局联合秘书吴钦纽，测绘局长吴拉钦貌，军事情报处长伦中校，外交部亚非司长吴巴漆，外交部司长吴苏幸，外交部副司长吴吞丁等。
    六时四十分，夜幕已经降临。当奈温总理在机场聚光灯照射下步出机舱的时候，国务院总理周恩来，副总理陈毅、习仲勋，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若、陈叔通，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，国防委员会副主席张治中、傅作义，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，外交部副部长章汉夫，我国驻缅甸大使李一氓等，都走到机旁迎接。军乐队奏缅甸和中国国歌后，奈温总理由周恩来总理陪同检阅了仪仗队。一群男女少年先锋队员向奈温总理和全体缅甸贵宾献了鲜花。
    今天前往机场欢迎的，还有国家机关各部门、各民主党派、人民团体的负责人，中国人民解放军高级将领，北京市负责人和首都各方面的人士。
    缅甸驻中国大使馆外交官员，以及各国驻中国的使节和外交官员，也都到机场欢迎。
    我国外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文和缅甸驻中国大使叫温今天陪同奈温总理到达北京。
    新华社广州24日电　应周恩来总理邀请来我国进行友好访问的缅甸联邦总理奈温和他的随行人员，今天上午从仰光乘飞机经香港到达广州。缅甸驻华大使叫温也一同到达广州。
    广州机场上今天装饰一新。中、缅两国的国旗和各色彩旗迎风飘扬。十时二十分，贵宾们乘坐的专机徐徐着陆，在机场上欢迎的群众中立刻响起了热烈的掌声。奈温总理步出机舱后，立即受到广东省副省长、广州市市长朱光，广东省副省长李嘉人，驻广州部队负责人赖春风等人的热烈欢迎。接着，军乐队奏起了缅、中两国的国歌，奈温总理在朱光副省长陪同下检阅了仪仗队，他还不时向热烈欢迎他们的一千五百名群众挥手致意。广州市的少年儿童向贵宾们献了鲜花。
    十时四十分，贵宾们由专从北京赶来迎接贵宾的我国外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文陪同，乘飞机前往北京。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻广州领事考贝克、越南驻广州代总领事黎终始等也前往机场欢送贵宾。
    新华社武汉24日电　缅甸联邦奈温总理今天路过武汉时曾作短时停留。到机场欢迎贵宾的有湖北省省长张体学、武汉市市长刘惠农、武汉驻军负责人张广才。
    奈温总理在武汉用过午餐后，下午二时许，在我国外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文陪同下，乘专机前往北京。
    新华社仰光23日电　缅甸总理奈温将军应中华人民共和国政府的邀请于23日晚十一点从这里乘专机到中国作友好访问。陪同奈温将军一起来华访问的有外交部长吴千吞昂，检察总长吴巴盛，副总参谋长昂季准将，外交部常任秘书詹姆斯·巴林顿和其他高级军官和文职官员。
    到仰光机场送行的有最高法院首席法官吴敏登，前民族院议长萧恢塔，内阁各位部长，空军副参谋长克利夫特，国家安全委员会主席貌貌上校，仰光市长顿盛上校和在仰光的各国外交使节。（附图片）
