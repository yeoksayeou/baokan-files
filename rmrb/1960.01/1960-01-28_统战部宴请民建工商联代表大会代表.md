Title: United Front Work Department Hosts Banquet for Representatives of the China Democratic National Construction Association and the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Congress

Author:

Date: 1960-01-28
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, January 27th: The United Front Work Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China held a banquet in the banquet hall of the Great Hall of the People this evening to entertain all the representatives attending the China Democratic National Construction Association and the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Congress, as well as the heads of various democratic parties and non-party democratic人士 (ren shi - people or personages).

### 统战部宴请民建工商联代表大会代表

1960-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

    统战部宴请民建工商联代表大会代表
    新华社27日讯　中共中央统一战线工作部今晚在人民大会堂宴会厅举行宴会，招待出席中国民主建国会和中华全国工商业联合会代表大会的全体代表、各民主党派负责人和无党派民主人士。
