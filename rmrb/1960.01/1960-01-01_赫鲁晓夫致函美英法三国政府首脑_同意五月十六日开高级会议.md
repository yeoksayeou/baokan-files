Title: Khrushchev Sends Letter to Heads of Government of the United States, Britain, and France Agreeing to Hold Summit Meeting on May 16th
Author:
Date: 1960-01-01
Page: 5
    
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 31st - TASS, Moscow, December 30th: Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Khrushchev, has written to the heads of government of the United States, Britain, and France, expressing his agreement to convene a four-power summit meeting in Paris on May 16, 1960.

    Khrushchev's letters to Eisenhower, Macmillan, and De Gaulle were delivered by Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko to the ambassadors of the United States, Britain, and France to the Soviet Union on December 30th.

    The letter states that it can now be noted with satisfaction that, as a result of consultations between the governments of the four major powers, complete agreement has been reached on the date and location of the summit meeting.

### 赫鲁晓夫致函美英法三国政府首脑  同意五月十六日开高级会议

1960-01-01
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫致函美英法三国政府首脑
    同意五月十六日开高级会议
    新华社莫斯科31日电　据塔斯社莫斯科30日讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫写信给美、英、法三国政府首脑，表示同意于1960年5月16日在巴黎召开四国最高级会议。
    赫鲁晓夫写给艾森豪威尔、麦克米伦和戴高乐的信，已由苏联外交部长葛罗米柯在12月30日交给了美、英、法三国驻苏联的大使。
    信中说，现在可以满意地指出，由于四大国政府协商的结果，对最高级会议的日期和地点，已经得出了完全一致的意见。
