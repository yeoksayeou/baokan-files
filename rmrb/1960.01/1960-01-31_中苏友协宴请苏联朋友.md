Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Hosts Banquet for Soviet Friends
Author:
Date: 1960-01-31
Page: 3
　　Xinhua News Agency, 30th. To celebrate the 1960 Spring Festival, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held a banquet this afternoon to entertain Soviet Ambassador to China, Chervonenko, and other responsible personnel and their wives from the embassy; and to entertain Soviet experts working for the Russian-language Friendly Weekly and Soviet journalists stationed in Beijing.

　　The banquet was presided over by Li Dequan, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters.

　　Among those present were Shao Lizi, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters, Qian Junrui, Secretary-General, council members Shi Liang and Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, and Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhang Zhixiang. Liao Mosha, Vice President of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and more than sixty others were also present.

### 中苏友协宴请苏联朋友

1960-01-31
第3版()
专栏：

    中苏友协宴请苏联朋友
    新华社30日讯　中苏友好协会总会和北京市中苏友好协会，为了欢度1960年春节，今天下午举行宴会，招待苏联驻华大使契尔沃年科及大使馆的其他负责人员和夫人；招待在俄文友好周刊工作的苏联专家和苏联驻京记者。
    宴会由中苏友好协会总会副会长李德全主持。
    出席作陪的，有中苏友好协会总会副会长邵力子，秘书长钱俊瑞，理事史良、丁西林，外交部副部长曾涌泉，对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥。北京市中苏友好协会副会长廖沫沙等六十多人。
