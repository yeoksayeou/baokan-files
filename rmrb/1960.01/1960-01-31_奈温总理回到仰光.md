Title: Ne Win Returns to Rangoon
Author:
Date: 1960-01-31
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 30th. Burmese Federal Prime Minister General Ne Win returned to Rangoon by special plane at 1:30 PM today, after a six-day visit to China at the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai.

　　At Rangoon Airport, Prime Minister Ne Win was warmly welcomed by more than a thousand people. The Burmese flag was hung on the airport building. Burmese senior government officials, senior military officers, representatives from all walks of life, and foreign envoys stationed in Rangoon, who came to greet him at the airport, were all enthusiastically discussing the development of friendly relations between China and Burma and the significance of the agreement on the border issue and the treaty of friendship and mutual non-aggression signed by the two neighboring countries.

　　Charge d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, Lei Yang, the head of the Chinese Cultural Friendship Delegation currently visiting Burma, Chu Tunan, and the members of the delegation, as well as all members of the Chinese National Song and Dance Troupe, went to the airport to welcome him.

　　After General Ne Win and his entourage got off the plane, they warmly shook hands with the welcomers. General Ne Win happily accepted flowers presented by a member of the Chinese National Song and Dance Troupe and Burmese overseas Chinese.

### 奈温总理回到仰光

1960-01-31
第3版()
专栏：

    奈温总理回到仰光
    新华社仰光30日电　缅甸联邦总理奈温将军应周恩来总理的邀请在中国访问六天以后，今天下午一时半乘专机回到仰光。
    在仰光机场上，奈温总理受到一千多人的热烈欢迎。机场大厦上挂着缅甸国旗。到机场迎接的缅甸高级政府官员、高级军官、各界代表、外国驻仰光的使节，都在热烈地谈论着中缅两国友好关系的发展和这两个邻国签订的关于边界问题的协定以及友好和互不侵犯条约的意义。
    中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办雷扬、正在缅甸访问的中国文化友好代表团团长楚图南和团员们以及中国民族歌舞团全体团员都到机场欢迎。
    奈温将军和他的随行人员走下飞机后，同欢迎者热烈握手。奈温将军愉快地接受了中国民族歌舞团的一位团员和缅甸华侨的献花。
