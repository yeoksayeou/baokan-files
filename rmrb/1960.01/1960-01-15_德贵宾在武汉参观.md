Title: German Dignitaries Visit Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1960-01-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, January 14th: Deputy Prime Minister Heinrich Rau and the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic he leads, accompanied by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, Secretary of the Hubei Provincial Party Committee and Vice Governor of Hubei Province Zhao Xinchu, Secretary of the Wuhan Municipal Party Committee and Mayor of Wuhan Liu Huinong, today visited the Wuhan Iron and Steel Company, the Wuhan Heavy Machinery Plant, and the Wuhan Boiler Plant. The dignitaries were warmly welcomed by the workers everywhere they went.
    In the morning, when the dignitaries visited the Wuhan Heavy Machinery Plant and the Boiler Plant, they inquired in detail about the workers' production and living conditions. Before leaving the Wuhan Boiler Plant, Deputy Prime Minister Heinrich Rau addressed the workers. He said: In a short period of time, we have seen you making great leaps forward everywhere, and such leaps forward have great significance in the world. The German people will always stand with you and work together to build socialism. His speech was met with enthusiastic applause from the workers.
    In the afternoon, the dignitaries visited the Wuhan Iron and Steel Company's No. 1 High-Yield Furnace and Nos. 2 and 3 Open Hearth Furnaces, and attended a welcome meeting held by more than 500 workers of Wuhan Iron and Steel. Paul Verner, candidate of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and First Secretary of the Greater Berlin Committee, spoke at the meeting.

### 德贵宾在武汉参观

1960-01-15
第4版()
专栏：

    德贵宾在武汉参观
    新华社武汉14日电　海因里希·劳副总理和他率领的德意志民主共和国政府代表团，今天在外交部副部长曾涌泉，中共湖北省委书记处书记、湖北省副省长赵辛初，中共武汉市委书记处书记、武汉市市长刘惠农等人陪同下，参观了武汉钢铁公司、武汉重型机床厂和武汉锅炉厂。贵宾们每到一地，都受到了职工们的热烈欢迎。
    上午，贵宾们到武汉重型机床厂和锅炉厂参观时，详尽地询问了工人的生产和生活情况。在离开武汉锅炉厂前，海因里希·劳副总理向工人们讲了话。他说：在短短的时间内，我们看到了你们到处在大跃进，这样的跃进在世界上有重大的意义。德国人民永远和你们站在一起，共同为建设社会主义而努力。他的讲话不断受到工人们热烈鼓掌。
    下午，贵宾们到武钢参观了武钢一号高产和二、三号平炉，出席了武钢五百多工人举行的欢迎会。德国统一社会党中央政治局候补委员、大柏林市委第一书记保罗·维尔纳在会上讲了话。
