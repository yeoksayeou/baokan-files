Title: From a People's Daily Editorial, August 7, 1959
Author:
Date: 1960-01-25
Page: 4

All leadership methods that have proven effective in the Great Leap Forward since 1958, such as putting politics in command; airing views, contending, and debating—what to do, what to air views on, what to debate; cadres cultivating experimental plots; cadres at all levels going deep into the countryside, deep into the fields, participating in production, and leading production; as well as on-the-spot meetings, appraisals, and competitions, etc., should all be continued, combined with the current situation, and further developed.

### 摘自本报1959年8月7日社论

1960-01-25
第4版()
专栏：

    凡是在1958年以来的大跃进中已经证明是行之有效的领导方法，例如政治挂帅；鸣放辩论，做什么、鸣放什么、辩论什么；干部种试验田；各级干部深入农村，深入田间，参加生产，领导生产；以及现场会议，评比竞赛等等，都应该结合当前情况，继续实行，并且加以发扬。
                      ——摘自本报1959年8月7日社论
