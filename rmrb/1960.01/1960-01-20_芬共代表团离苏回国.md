Title: Finnish Communist Party Delegation Leaves Soviet Union for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-01-20
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 18th - According to TASS, the Finnish Communist Party delegation, headed by Finnish Communist Party Central Committee Chairman Aimo Aaltonen, left Moscow for home on the 16th. The delegation visited the Soviet Union from December 22, 1959 to January 16, 1960.

### 芬共代表团离苏回国

1960-01-20
第6版()
专栏：

    芬共代表团离苏回国
    新华社18日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：以芬兰共产党中央委员会主席艾摩·阿尔托宁为首的芬兰共产党代表团，16日离莫斯科回国。代表团从1959年12月22日至1960年1月16日在苏联进行了访问。
