Title: President Liu to Visit German Democratic Republic at the Invitation of President Pieck
Author:
Date: 1960-01-21
Page: 1

    Accepting President Pieck's Invitation
    President Liu to Visit German Democratic Republic

    Xinhua News Agency, January 20th: Comrade Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, has invited Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, to pay a friendly visit to the German Democratic Republic at an appropriate time. Chairman Liu Shaoqi has accepted this invitation.

### 接受皮克总统的邀请  刘主席将访问德意志民主共和国

1960-01-21
第1版()
专栏：

    接受皮克总统的邀请
    刘主席将访问德意志民主共和国
    新华社20日讯　德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克同志邀请中华人民共和国主席刘少奇同志在适当时机到德意志民主共和国进行友好访问。刘少奇主席已经接受了这项邀请。
