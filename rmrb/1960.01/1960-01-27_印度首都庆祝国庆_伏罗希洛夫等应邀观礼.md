Title: Indian Capital Celebrates National Day; Voroshilov and Others Invited to Attend
Author:
Date: 1960-01-27
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 26th: New Delhi, the capital of India, held a military parade and mass procession today to celebrate National Day.

Indian President Prasad reviewed the parade. More than 3,000 officers and soldiers from the Army, Navy, and Air Force were inspected. Many citizens and students participated in the parade.

Foreign guests invited to attend the ceremony included Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Kozlov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Furtseva, representative of the Supreme Soviet, Nepalese Prime Minister Koirala, and the Polish delegation of legal workers.

President Prasad held a National Day reception at the Presidential Palace on the 25th. Soviet guests such as Voroshilov were invited to attend the reception.

### 印度首都庆祝国庆  伏罗希洛夫等应邀观礼

1960-01-27
第6版()
专栏：

    印度首都庆祝国庆
    伏罗希洛夫等应邀观礼
    新华社新德里26日电　印度首都新德里今天举行了庆祝国庆节的军事检阅和群众游行。
    印度总统普拉沙德检阅了游行队伍。受检阅的有陆、海、空军的三千多名官兵。参加游行的有许多市民和学生。
    应邀观礼的外宾有苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫、部长会议第一副主席科兹洛夫、最高苏维埃代表福尔采娃、尼泊尔首相柯伊拉腊和波兰法律工作者代表团等。
    普拉沙德总统25日在总统府举行国庆节招待会。伏罗希洛夫等苏联贵宾应邀参加了招待会。
