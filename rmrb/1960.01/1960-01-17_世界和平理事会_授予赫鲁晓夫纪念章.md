Title: World Peace Council Awards Medal to Khrushchev
Author:
Date: 1960-01-17
Page: 3

    World Peace Council
    Awards Medal to Khrushchev
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 16th – According to TASS, Soviet Premier Khrushchev accepted a medal from the World Peace Council on the 15th in the Kremlin. The medal was presented to the Soviet head of government by a member of the World Peace Council Presidium, the Ukrainian writer K.T. Korniichuk.
    The medal was created to commemorate the tenth anniversary of the peace movement and will be awarded to the most outstanding fighters for peace and friendship among nations in all countries.
    When presenting the medal, Korniichuk, on behalf of all peace advocates, expressed gratitude to Khrushchev for his great contribution to the cause of consolidating world peace.
    After receiving the medal, Khrushchev expressed his hope that all peace advocates would achieve new and great successes in their tireless activities to strengthen peace.

### 世界和平理事会  授予赫鲁晓夫纪念章

1960-01-17
第3版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会
    授予赫鲁晓夫纪念章
    新华社16日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫15日在克里姆林宫接受了世界和平理事会纪念章。纪念章由世界和平理事会主席团委员、乌克兰作家考?楚克授给苏联政府首脑。
    纪念章是为纪念和平运动十周年而设置的，它将授给各国争取和平和各国间的友谊的最杰出的战士。
    考?楚克在授纪念章时，代表所有和平拥护者对赫鲁晓夫为巩固全世界和平事业所做出的巨大贡献表示感谢。
    赫鲁晓夫在接受纪念章之后，表示希望所有的和平拥护者在他们为加强和平的孜孜不倦的活动中，取得新的、巨大的成就。
