Title: Omani Nationalists Actively Struggle, British Colonialists are Alarmed and Uneasy
Author:
Date: 1960-01-06
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 31st: According to a statement issued by the Office of the Imam of Oman in Cairo, the increasingly frequent activities of Omani nationalists have made the British colonialists in Muscat uneasy and alarmed.

The statement said that the British military commander in Muscat, Matrah and the Batina region, Colonel Reed, and his chief of staff, had held meetings to study the situation caused by the activities of Omani nationalists.

### 阿曼民族主义者积极斗争  英殖民者惊慌不安

1960-01-06
第6版()
专栏：

    阿曼民族主义者积极斗争
    英殖民者惊慌不安
    据新华社开罗电　据阿曼教长驻开罗办事处12月31日发表的声明说，阿曼民族主义者日益频繁的活动，已经使得马斯喀特的英国殖民者感到不安和惊慌。
    声明说，马斯喀特、马特拉和巴提纳地区的英军司令李德上校和他的参谋长曾经开会研究由于阿曼民族主义者的活动所造成的局势。
