Title: Czechoslovak Communist Party Central Committee and GDR Leaders Point Out: Soviet Union's Renewed Disarmament Demonstrates National Strength
Author:
Date: 1960-01-17
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, January 16th: According to a report by the Czechoslovak News Agency: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia held a meeting on the 13th and 14th. The communique issued after the meeting said that the CPC Central Committee welcomed the new action of the Soviet Union to reduce its armed forces by 1.2 million.

The Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party believes that the Soviet Union's decision to disarm is an action to defend lasting peace and demonstrates the great strength of the Soviet state.

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, January 16th: According to local newspapers: Zhivkov, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, said that the decision of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union to reduce the Soviet armed forces once again is of historical significance, and it is a huge practical step in realizing the idea of complete and thorough disarmament in the world.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, January 15th: Walter Ulbricht, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, praised the Soviet Union's large-scale reduction of armed forces while speaking to cooperative farmers in Jesewitz on the evening of the 14th. He said that this is a major contribution to promoting mutual understanding among the major powers.

He pointed out that it is now up to Western countries to show how much armed forces they intend to reduce.

Otto Grotewohl, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, wrote in the "Berliner Morgenpost" that by announcing a large reduction in the number of troops, Khrushchev deprived the enemies of reconciliation and disarmament of their last weapons.

### 捷共中央和保德领导人指出  苏联再次裁军表明国力强大

1960-01-17
第3版()
专栏：

    捷共中央和保德领导人指出
    苏联再次裁军表明国力强大
    新华社布拉格16日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道：捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会在13日和14日举行会议。会后发表的公报说，捷共中央欢迎苏联裁减武装部队一百二十万人这一新的行动。
    捷共中央委员会认为，苏联裁军的决定是保卫持久和平的行动，并且表现了伟大的苏维埃国家的强大力量。
    新华社索非亚16日电　据此间报纸报道：保加利亚共产党中央委员会第一书记日夫科夫说，苏联最高苏维埃关于再一次裁减苏联武装部队的决定具有历史的意义，它是在全世界实现全面和彻底裁军思想的一个巨大的实际步骤。
    据新华社柏林15日电　德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记乌布利希14日晚间在耶塞维茨对合作社农民讲话时，赞扬苏联大量裁减武装部队的行动。他说，这是对促进各大国互相谅解的重大贡献。
    他指出，现在要由西方国家表明他们打算裁减多少武装部队了。
    德意志民主共和国人民议院主席团主席狄克曼在“晨报”上写道，由于宣布大量裁减军队的人数，赫鲁晓夫使和解和裁军的敌人失去了他们手中的最后武器。
