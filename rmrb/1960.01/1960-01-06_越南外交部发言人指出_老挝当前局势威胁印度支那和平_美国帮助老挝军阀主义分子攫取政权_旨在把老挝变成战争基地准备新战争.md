Title: Vietnamese Foreign Ministry Spokesman Points Out That the Current Situation in Laos Threatens Peace in Indochina; The United States Helps Lao Warlord Clique Seize Power; Aims to Turn Laos into a War Base to Prepare for a New War
Author:
Date: 1960-01-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 5th. The spokesman of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam today answered questions raised by the Vietnam News Agency reporter on the current situation in Laos.

The Foreign Ministry spokesman pointed out that the militants of the Lao "Committee for the Defense of National Interests," with the help of US imperialism, have used the army to force the Phoui Sananikone government to resign and force the Lao King Savang Vatthana to hand over power to the army, conspiring to establish a dictatorial warlord government in order to continue to expand the civil war, join the Southeast Asia aggressive bloc, and implement the aggressive policies of US imperialism.

The spokesman said that the Lao warlord clique's creation of the current tense situation in Laos is part of the US imperialist plot to turn South Vietnam and Laos into American war bases, threaten Cambodia's peaceful neutrality, directly threaten the security of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and prepare for a new war in Indochina.

The Foreign Ministry spokesman pointed out that the situation in Laos has clearly deteriorated, and this situation violates the Lao people's desire for peace and national harmony and is detrimental to peace in Indochina. He said that the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, as a neighbor of the Kingdom of Laos and as a party to the Geneva Accords on Laos, has always hoped to establish good neighborly relations with Laos and is particularly concerned about the increasingly serious situation in Laos. The spokesman then condemned the army of the Kingdom of Laos, under the command of the "Committee for the Defense of National Interests" group, for invading the territory of Banh and Cu Du districts of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on December 6, 27, and 29, 1959. He pointed out that this action by the pro-American elements in Laos will have serious consequences.

The Foreign Ministry spokesman finally pointed out: "The Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam firmly believes that in the context of the international situation trending towards détente, the vicious conspiracies of US imperialism and the 'Committee for the Defense of National Interests' group in Laos will certainly be opposed by the Lao people, the people of Asian and African countries, and the people of the world, and will ultimately suffer a miserable defeat."

### 越南外交部发言人指出  老挝当前局势威胁印度支那和平  美国帮助老挝军阀主义分子攫取政权  旨在把老挝变成战争基地准备新战争

1960-01-06
第6版()
专栏：

    越南外交部发言人指出
    老挝当前局势威胁印度支那和平
    美国帮助老挝军阀主义分子攫取政权
    旨在把老挝变成战争基地准备新战争
    新华社河内5日电　越南民主共和国外交部发言人今天就老挝当前局势答复了越南通讯社记者提出的问题。
    外交部发言人指出，老挝“保卫国家利益委员会”的好战分子在美帝国主义帮助下，已经利用军队强迫培·萨纳尼空政府辞职和强迫老挝国王萨旺·瓦达纳把政权交给军队，阴谋成立独裁的军阀政府，以便继续扩大内战，加入东南亚侵略集团，实行美帝国主义的侵略政策。
    发言人说，老挝军阀主义分子制造老挝当前的紧张局势是美帝国主义企图把南越和老挝变成美国的战争基地、威胁柬埔寨的和平中立、直接威胁越南民主共和国的安全、在印度支那准备新战争的阴谋的一部分。
    外交部发言人指出，老挝局势显然已经恶化了，这种局势违背了老挝人民希望和平和民族和睦的意愿，不利于印度支那的和平。他说，越南民主共和国作为老挝王国的邻邦，作为签订关于老挝的日内瓦协议的一方，一向希望同老挝建立睦邻关系，特别关心老挝日益严重的局势。发言人接着谴责老挝王国军队在“保卫国家利益委员会”集团的指挥下，在1959年12月6日、27日和29日侵入了越南民主共和国边发和寇都地区的领土。他指出，老挝亲美分子的这种行动将带来严重的后果。
    外交部发言人最后指出：“越南民主共和国政府坚信，在国际局势趋向缓和的情况下，美帝国主义和‘保卫国家利益委员会’集团在老挝的恶毒阴谋，一定会遭到老挝人民、亚非各国人民和全世界人民的反对，并且最后一定会遭到惨败。”
