Title: Eisenhower to Visit South America
Author:
Date: 1960-01-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 7th — White House Press Secretary Hagerty announced on the 6th that U.S. President Eisenhower will visit Brazil, Argentina, Chile and Uruguay from February 23rd to March 3rd, and will make a brief stop in Puerto Rico. He will be accompanied by Secretary of State Herter and some members of the National Advisory Committee on Inter-American Affairs.

The statement said that Eisenhower's visit to South America is intended to "publicly demonstrate his deep concern for all countries in the (South American) New World" and to "encourage the further development of the Inter-American system."

Eisenhower's trip to South America was decided at a time when anti-American sentiment in Latin American countries is rising and the U.S.'s dominant position is increasingly shaky.

### 艾森豪威尔将访南美

1960-01-08
第6版()
专栏：

    艾森豪威尔将访南美
    新华社7日讯　华盛顿消息：白宫新闻秘书哈格蒂6日发表声明说，美国总统艾森豪威尔将在2月23日到3月3日访问巴西、阿根廷、智利和乌拉圭等四个南美国家，并将在波多黎各作短暂的逗留。同行的有国务卿赫脱和泛美事务全国顾问委员会的一些成员。
    声明说，艾森豪威尔到南美的访问，目的是要“公开地显示他对（南美）新世界的所有国家的深切关心”，并且“鼓励进一步发展泛美制度”。
    艾森豪威尔的南美之行是在拉丁美洲国家的反美情绪高涨、美国的统治地位日益动摇的情况下决定的。
