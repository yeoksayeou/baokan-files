Title: Barkin's Funeral Held in Moscow
Author: 
Date: 1960-01-11
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 9th: The funeral of Academician Barkin, Vice President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences and a renowned metallurgist, was held in Moscow today.

    A memorial service, presided over by A. Zasyadko, Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, was held next to the grave in the Novodevichy Cemetery.

    V. Yelyutin, Minister of Higher and Secondary Specialized Education of the USSR, delivered a eulogy on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the USSR. Academician A.N. Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, delivered a eulogy on behalf of scientific workers. Many wreaths were placed in front of Academician Barkin's grave, including wreaths from the embassies and scientific workers of China and the German Democratic Republic, as well as the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences.

### 巴尔金葬礼在莫斯科举行

1960-01-11
第5版()
专栏：

    巴尔金葬礼在莫斯科举行
    新华社莫斯科9日电　苏联科学院副院长、著名的冶金学家巴尔金院士的葬礼今天在莫斯科举行。
    在新圣母公墓墓旁举行了追悼会，由苏联部长会议副主席亚·扎夏迪科主持。
    苏联高等和中等专业教育部部长叶留金代表苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议致悼词；苏联科学院院长?斯米扬诺夫代表科学工作者致悼词。在巴尔金院士的墓前放着许多花圈，其中有中国和德意志民主共和国的大使馆和科学工作者以及捷克斯洛伐克科学院献的花圈。
