Title: Cheering for the Red Communiqué
Author: Yan Ming
Date: 1960-01-28
Page: 8

    Cheering for the Red Communiqué
    Yan Ming
    Ode to the Red Communiqué
    One word,
    A mountain,
    Standing tall and majestic,
    Shining with red light!
    One word,
    A clap of thunder,
    Rolling loudly!
    The earth returns to spring!
    One step, a five-year plan,
    One step, another five-year plan,
    Ah, the five-year journey,
    A flash of lightning!
    Red Communiqué,
    Its brilliance reaches ten thousand feet high,
    The motherland leaps forward!
    Six hundred million people, ah, show their heroism!
    Motherland, ah, Motherland,
    Standing at the source of the Yellow River,
    Ascending to the peak of the Kunlun Mountains,
    Motherland, ah, Motherland,
    For your new starting point,
    Write ten thousand scrolls of poetry!
    Pluck the stars from the sky,
    Hold up ten thousand strands of morning glow,
    Motherland, ah, Motherland,
    For your new stage,
    Weave a magnificent embroidered scroll.
            January 23, 1960, night.

### 为红色公报欢呼
晏明
1960-01-28
第8版()
专栏：

    为红色公报欢呼
    晏明
    红色公报颂
    一个字，
    一座山，
    巍巍屹立，
    红光闪闪！
    一个字，
    一声雷，
    隆隆滚过！
    大地春回！
    一步，一个五年，
    一步，又一个五年，
    五年途程呵，
    一道闪电！
    红色公报，
    光芒万丈高，
    祖国飞跃前进！
    六亿人呵显英豪！
    祖国呵，祖国
    站在黄河源头，
    登上昆仑山巅，
    祖国呵，祖国，
    为你新的起点，
    写下万卷诗篇！
    摘下满天繁星，
    捧起万缕朝霞，
    祖国呵，祖国，
    为你新的阶段，
    织成锦绣画卷。
            1960年1月23日夜。
