Title: Voroshilov Hosts Banquet for Indian President
Author:
Date: 1960-01-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 23rd: Soviet Presidium of the Supreme Soviet Voroshilov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Kozlov, and Supreme Soviet representative Furtseva hosted a banquet on the evening of the 22nd to entertain Indian President Prasad.  Indian President Prasad, Prime Minister Nehru, and ministers of the Indian government attended the banquet.

Voroshilov delivered a speech at the banquet, thanking the Indian people for their hospitality towards the Soviet delegation, and said that the Soviet people also have deep feelings for the Indian people.

President Prasad gave a reply speech, praising the growing cooperation between the two countries in developing India's industry and agriculture.

On the morning of the 22nd, Voroshilov and Kozlov visited the village of Ajraon in Punjab, where they were warmly welcomed by more than 20,000 Indian people. At a rally attended by more than 20,000 Indian farmers welcoming the Soviet leaders, Voroshilov gave a speech at the request of the farmers. He expressed his hope that India would achieve further development in agricultural production.

### 伏罗希洛夫欢宴印总统

1960-01-25
第6版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫欢宴印总统
    据新华社新德里23日电　苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫、部长会议第一副主席科兹洛夫和最高苏维埃代表福尔采娃22日晚间设宴招待印度总统普拉沙德。出席宴会的有印度总统普拉沙德、总理尼赫鲁和印度政府的部长们。
    伏罗希洛夫在宴会上致词，感谢印度人民对苏联代表团的盛情，并且说苏联人民对印度人民也怀着深厚的感情。
    普拉沙德总统致答词时赞扬两国在印度发展工农业方面日益增长的合作。
    22日上午，伏罗希洛夫、科兹洛夫到旁遮普邦阿杰隆德村参观，受到那里的两万多印度人民的热烈欢迎。在欢迎苏联领导人的有二万多印度农民参加的大会上，伏罗希洛夫应农民们的请求发表了讲话。他表示希望印度将在农业生产方面取得进一步的发展。
