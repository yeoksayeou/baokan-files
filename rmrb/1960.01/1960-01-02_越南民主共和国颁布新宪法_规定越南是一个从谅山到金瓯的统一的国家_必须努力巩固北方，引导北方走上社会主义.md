Title: Democratic Republic of Vietnam Promulgates New Constitution: Stipulates that Vietnam is a unified country from Lang Son to Ca Mau, and must strive to consolidate the North and guide it towards socialism
Author:
Date: 1960-01-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 1st. President Ho Chi Minh issued an order today promulgating the new Constitution of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, which was unanimously adopted by the National Assembly of Vietnam on December 31, 1959.

All newspapers in Hanoi today published the full text of this new Constitution. In addition to the "Preamble," it is divided into ten chapters: "The Democratic Republic of Vietnam"; "Economic and Social System"; "Fundamental Rights and Obligations of Citizens"; "National Assembly"; "President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam"; "Council of Ministers of the Government"; "People's Councils and Administrative Committees at All Levels of Localities"; "People's Courts and People's Procuratorates"; "National Flag, National Emblem, Capital"; "Amendment of the Constitution."

The "Preamble" of the new Constitution states that "Vietnam is a unified country from Lang Son to Ca Mau." The Democratic Republic of Vietnam is "a people's democratic state led by the working class and based on the worker-peasant alliance." The Vietnamese people "must strive to consolidate the North, guide the North towards socialism, and continue to struggle for the peaceful reunification of the country and the completion of the national, people's democratic revolution."

The "Preamble" outlines the course of the Vietnamese people's victory in the struggle against the French colonialists and Japanese fascists, and then points out that "the great victory of the Vietnamese people is also the victory of the liberation movement of oppressed nations, the forces of world peace, and the socialist camp."

The "Preamble" concludes: "Under the wise leadership of the Vietnam Workers' Party, the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and President Ho Chi Minh, all the people of our country, broadly united in the National United Front, will surely win brilliant victories in the socialist construction of the North and in the cause of realizing a unified fatherland. Our people will surely build a peaceful, unified, independent, democratic, and prosperous Vietnam, and make due contributions to the cause of defending Southeast Asia and world peace."

After the victory of the August Revolution in Vietnam in 1945, the National Assembly of Vietnam adopted the first Constitution at a meeting held on January 6, 1946. As the revolution entered a new stage, the National Assembly of Vietnam established a Constitution Amendment Committee headed by President Ho Chi Minh to draft a new Constitution. Before being submitted to the eleventh session of the National Assembly for discussion and adoption, the draft of the new Constitution underwent enthusiastic nationwide discussion, supplementation, and revision.

### 越南民主共和国颁布新宪法  规定越南是一个从谅山到金瓯的统一的国家  必须努力巩固北方，引导北方走上社会主义

1960-01-02
第3版()
专栏：

    越南民主共和国颁布新宪法
    规定越南是一个从谅山到金瓯的统一的国家
    必须努力巩固北方，引导北方走上社会主义
    新华社河内1日电　胡志明主席今天发布命令，颁布了越南民主共和国的新宪法，新宪法是越南国会在1959年12月31日一致通过的。
    河内各报今天都刊载了这部新宪法的全文。它除了“序言”部分以外，共分“越南民主共和国”；“经济制度和社会制度”；“公民的基本权利和义务”；“国会”；“越南民主共和国主席”；“政府部长会议”；“地方各级人民议会和地方各级行政委员会”；“人民法院和人民检察院”；“国旗、国徽、首都”；“宪法的修改”等十章。
    新宪法的“序言”说，“越南是一个从谅山到金瓯的统一的国家”。越南民主共和国是“以工人阶级为领导，以工农联盟为基础的人民民主国家”。越南人民“必须努力巩固北方、引导北方走上社会主义，继续为和平统一祖国、完成全国的民族、人民民主革命任务而奋斗”。
    宪法“序言”概述了越南人民在反抗法国殖民者和日本法西斯的斗争中，取得胜利的经过之后指出，“越南人民的伟大胜利，也就是被压迫民族解放运动、世界和平力量和社会主义阵营的胜利。”
    “序言”最后说：“在越南劳动党、越南民主共和国政府和胡志明主席的英明领导下，广泛地团结在民族统一战线中的我国全体人民，必将在北方的社会主义建设和实现统一祖国的事业中赢得辉煌的胜利。我国人民必将胜利地建成一个和平、统一、独立、民主和富强的越南，为保卫东南亚和世界和平事业作出应有的贡献。”
    越南1945年8月革命胜利后，越南国会曾在1946年1月6日举行的会议上通过了第一部宪法。由于革命进入新的阶段，越南国会成立了以胡志明主席为首的修改宪法委员会，从事制订新宪法草案的工作。新宪法草案在提交国会第十一次会议讨论通过之前，经过了全民的热烈讨论和补充修改。
