Title: Celebrating Great Victory, Praising the Communiqué
Author: Zhang Zhimin
Date: 1960-01-24
Page: 8

　　Celebrating great victory, praising the communiqué,
　　Mountains cheer, the earth smiles.
　　The communiqué hangs touching heaven and earth,
　　Every word is taller than Mount Tai.
　　How should these joyful days be celebrated?
　　A mass chorus of ten thousand people is too small a scale.
　　We must pluck the moon to use as a gong,
　　And raise Kunlun to beat as a drum.
　　Tear down rainbows to build triumphal arches,
　　Summon spring thunder to serve as salutes.
　　1959 has passed,
　　Clothed in ten thousand rays of golden light.
　　Steel flowers fly, illuminating the sky red,
　　Grain stores soar, reaching the ninth heaven.
　　Coal fields are boundless, oil like the sea,
　　Cotton piles stand tall, higher than the clouds.
　　Six hundred million people, with lofty ambitions, move the universe,
　　Six hundred million people's voices shake the earth.
　　The Great Leap Forward’s pace is like a swift horse,
　　The General Line’s radiance is like a lighthouse.
　　The People's Commune is an evergreen flower,
　　The Party's red flag is a guidepost.
　　Chairman Mao's thought is a priceless treasure,
　　Chairman Mao's giant hand is a bridge to the heavens.
　　With soaring spirits, we move mountains and drain seas,
　　Creating a paradise on earth with our own hands.
　　Production targets shoot for the Big Dipper,
　　Reaching into the heavenly palace to pluck peaches of immortality.
　　Leap forward, leap forward, leap forward again,
　　Each wave higher than the last.
　　The embroidered future is infinitely beautiful,
　　The rising sun shines brightly on the sunny path…

### 祝大捷　颂公报
张志民
1960-01-24
第8版()
专栏：

    祝大捷　颂公报
    张志民
    祝大捷呀唱公报，
    高山欢呼大地笑。
    公报挂起接天地，
    字字都比泰山高。
    大喜日子怎庆祝？
    万人合唱规模小。
    要摘月亮当锣打，
    举起昆仑当鼓敲。
    扯下彩虹搭牌楼，
    唤来春雷作礼炮。
    1959年过去了，
    披着金光万万道。
    钢花飞起照天红，
    粮囤高耸冲九宵。
    煤田无边油似海，
    棉堆高矗比云高。
    六亿人壮志乾坤动，
    六亿人声浪大地摇。
    大跃进步伐骏马飞，
    总路线光芒灯塔照。
    人民公社是长春花，
    党的红旗是指路标。
    毛主席的思想是无价宝，
    毛主席的巨手是通天桥。
    意气风发移山海，
    人间天堂双手造。
    生产指标射北斗，
    伸手天宫摘蟠桃。
    跃进，跃进，再跃进，
    一浪更比一浪高。
    锦绣前程无限美，
    旭日春光阳关道……
