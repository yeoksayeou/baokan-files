Title: Delegation of the German Government Continues Visit in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1960-01-17
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, January 16th: Vice Premier Heinrich Rau and the delegation of the German Democratic Republic government he leads, accompanied by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan and Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Vice Mayor of Shanghai Cao Diqiu, today visited the Shanghai Steam Turbine Factory.

    When the distinguished guests arrived at the factory, groups of workers stood on the sidewalks and warmly applauded to welcome them. The distinguished guests carefully inspected the manufacturing of the blades for the 25,000-kilowatt steam turbine and the main shaft for the 12,000-kilowatt steam turbine. During the visit, Vice Premier Heinrich Rau frequently shook hands with the young workers working beside the machines and asked about their production and living conditions with concern.

    As the distinguished guests were leaving the factory, the factory负责人 presented a package of blade models to the distinguished guests as a souvenir.

    Today, the distinguished guests also visited the Shanghai Electric Machinery Factory and the newly built workers' housing.

### 德政府代表团继续在上海参观访问

1960-01-17
第2版()
专栏：

    德政府代表团继续在上海参观访问
    新华社上海16日电　海因里希·劳副总理和他率领的德意志民主共和国政府代表团，今天在我国外交部副部长曾涌泉和中共上海市委书记处书记、上海市副市长曹荻秋等人陪同下，参观了上海汽轮机厂。
    当贵宾们来到这个厂的时候，成群的工人站在走道上热烈鼓掌欢迎。贵宾们仔细地参观了二万五千瓩汽轮机的叶片和一万二千瓩汽轮机主轴的制造情况。海因里希·劳副总理在参观时，不时地和在机器旁边工作的青年工人握手，并关切地询问他们的生产和生活情况。
    在贵宾们离开这个厂的时候，工厂的负责人把一包叶片模型送给贵宾留念。
    今天，贵宾们还参观了上海电机厂和新建的工人住宅。
