Title: Ministry of Foreign Trade Hosts Trade Delegations from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-01-29
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, January 28th. Lei Renmin, Acting Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, held a banquet this afternoon at the New Qiao Hotel Auditorium to celebrate the 1960 Spring Festival and to entertain trade delegations from various countries who are currently in Beijing for negotiations. Lin Haiyun and Lu Xuzhang, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, attended the banquet.

### 对外贸易部招待各国贸易代表团

1960-01-29
第2版()
专栏：

    对外贸易部招待各国贸易代表团
    新华社28日讯　对外贸易部代理部长雷任民，为欢庆1960年春节，今天下午在新侨饭店礼堂举行宴会，招待目前在京谈判的各国贸易代表团。对外贸易部副部长林海云、卢绪章等出席了宴会。
