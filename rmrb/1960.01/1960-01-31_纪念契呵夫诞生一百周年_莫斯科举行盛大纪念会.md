Title: Grand Commemoration Held in Moscow to Mark the Centenary of Chekhov's Birth
Author:
Date: 1960-01-31
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 29th: To commemorate the centenary of the birth of the famous Russian writer Chekhov, the Soviet Grand Theater held a grand commemoration and performance on the evening of the 29th.

Soviet party and government leaders Aristov, Brezhnev, Ignatov, Mikoyan, Khrushchev, and others attended the commemoration.

The First Secretary of the Board of the Soviet Writers' Association, Fedin, delivered the opening speech, saying that today, all over the world, people are commemorating this artist who opened a new page in the history of realist short stories and the history of new drama. This Russian writer had already gained the title of a great writer during his lifetime.

Following this, the Soviet writer Katayev gave a report on Chekhov's works.

The famous American peace warrior and singer Paul Robeson also spoke at the meeting and enthusiastically sang Russian folk songs.

After the commemorative performance began, Soviet actors recited excerpts from Chekhov's works and performed the writer's famous drama "The Three Sisters." The Grand Theater Orchestra also performed a symphony created based on Chekhov's works. On the 29th, other major Soviet cities also held commemorations and performances.

### 纪念契呵夫诞生一百周年  莫斯科举行盛大纪念会

1960-01-31
第3版()
专栏：

    纪念契呵夫诞生一百周年
    莫斯科举行盛大纪念会
    新华社莫斯科29日电　为纪念俄国著名作家契呵夫诞生一百周年，苏联大剧院29日晚举行盛大纪念会和演出。
    苏联党和政府领导人阿里斯托夫、勃列日涅夫、伊格纳托夫、米高扬、赫鲁晓夫等出席了纪念会。
    苏联作家协会理事会第一书记费定致开幕词说，今天，世界各地都在纪念这位在现实主义短篇小说史和新戏剧史中开辟了新的一页的艺术家。这位俄国作家在生前就已经获得了伟大作家的称号。
    接着，苏联作家卡达耶夫作了关于契呵夫的创作的报告。
    美国著名的和平战士和歌手罗伯逊也在会上发言，并且热情地演唱了俄罗斯民歌。
    纪念演出开始后，苏联演员们朗诵了契呵夫作品的片段，表演了这位作家的著名戏剧“三姊妹”。大剧院乐队还演奏了根据契呵夫作品创作的交响乐。29日，苏联其他大城市也举行了纪念会和演出活动。
