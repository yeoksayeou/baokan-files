Title: China and Myanmar Premiers Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1960-01-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 25th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China and Prime Minister Ne Win of the Union of Burma held talks on the morning of January 25th.

Xinhua News Agency, January 25th: The Ministry of Culture and the China-Myanmar Friendship Association held an evening party tonight at the National Cultural Palace to warmly welcome General Ne Win, Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, and his entourage, who are currently visiting our country.

Premier Ne Win, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi, watched the Chinese dance drama "The Mermaid." The Burmese Ambassador to China, Kyao Winn, was invited to attend the evening party.

Also accompanying the distinguished guests to watch the dance drama were Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Minister Xia Yan, President of the China-Myanmar Friendship Association Zhang Youyu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and our country's Ambassador to Myanmar Li Yimang, among others.

After the evening party, Premier Ne Win, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi, stepped onto the stage, shook hands with the Chinese artists who performed "The Mermaid," and presented flower baskets. (Attached picture)

### 中缅两国总理举行会谈

1960-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中缅两国总理举行会谈
    新华社25日讯　中华人民共和国总理周恩来和缅甸联邦总理奈温，25日上午举行了会谈。
    新华社25日讯　文化部和中缅友好协会今晚在民族文化宫举行晚会，热烈欢迎正在我国访问的缅甸联邦总理奈温将军和他的随行人员。
    奈温总理在陈毅副总理的陪同下，观看了中国舞剧“鱼美人”。缅甸驻华大使叫温应邀出席了晚会。
    陪同贵宾观看舞剧的还有文化部部长沈雁冰、副部长夏衍，中缅友好协会会长张友渔，外交部副部长章汉夫和我国驻缅甸大使李一氓等。
    晚会结束后，奈温总理由陈毅副总理陪同步上舞台，同演出“鱼美人”的中国艺术家们握手，并赠送了花篮。（附图片）
