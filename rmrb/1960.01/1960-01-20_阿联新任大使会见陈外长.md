Title: UAR's New Ambassador Meets with Foreign Minister Chen Yi
Author:
Date: 1960-01-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 19th—The newly appointed Ambassador of the United Arab Republic to China, Salah El Din Tarazi, met with Foreign Minister Chen Yi this morning to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials. Present at the meeting were Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and He Ying, Director of the Department of West Asian and African Affairs.

### 阿联新任大使会见陈外长

1960-01-20
第4版()
专栏：

    阿联新任大使会见陈外长
    新华社19日讯　阿拉伯联合共和国新任驻华大使萨拉赫丁·塔拉齐今天上午会见我外交部长陈毅，商谈递交国书事宜。会见时在座的有外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文，西亚非洲司司长何英。
