Title: Khrushchev to Visit India
Author:
Date: 1960-01-29
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 27th (TASS): It has been officially announced here that Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Khrushchev has accepted the invitation to visit India on his way to Indonesia. He is scheduled to arrive in Delhi on February 11.

He will stay in Delhi for two days and will also visit the Suratgarh State Farm and the Bhilai Steel Plant, before traveling to Burma via Calcutta on February 16.

### 赫鲁晓夫将访印度

1960-01-29
第3版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫将访印度
    新华社28日讯　塔斯社莫斯科27日讯：此间正式宣布：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫已经接受了在去印度尼西亚访问的途中访问印度的邀请，他预定在2月11日到达德里。
    他将在德里逗留两天，还将参观苏拉特加赫国营农场和比莱炼钢厂，然后在2月16日经过加尔各答前往缅甸。
