Title: US Warship Intrudes into Fujian Territorial Waters; We Issue 79th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-01-10
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, January 9th: A U.S. warship intruded into the waters of the Baiquan and Haitan areas of Fujian Province, China, from 8:20 to 12:58 on January 9th. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. warship, a spokesperson for the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 79th serious warning.

### 美国军舰侵入我福建领海  我提出第79次严重警告

1960-01-10
第2版()
专栏：

    美国军舰侵入我福建领海
    我提出第79次严重警告
    新华社9日讯　1月9日八时二十分至十二时五十八分，美国军舰一艘侵入我国福建省白犬、海坛地区海域。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第七十九次严重警告。
