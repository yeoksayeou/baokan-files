Title: The Times Have Changed
Author: Li Ying
Date: 1960-01-19
Page: 8

    The Times Have Changed
    Li Ying
What shall we give to the Sixties?
  What shall we give to the Sixties?
  Records of Mars exploration,
  Spaceships,
  A green lawn and a man-made sea?
  However, at the very beginning of its arrival,
  It was stained with a touch of winter color:
  By the gunpowder smoke of American gunpowder,
  By the sinful deals of Nobusuke Kishi.
  Oppose! Oppose the Japan-U.S. military alliance!
  This bayonet wants to cleave peace open,
  I want to cry out in the name of my children’s father:
  Stop, don’t bring sorrow to the world!
  We once used our own hands to defeat Japanese imperialism; the days of rampant bandits are gone, today is no longer the era of the past! Don't rekindle the wildfire of aggression, burning our green forest belt; the flames will swallow you yourselves, leaving only a handful of dust. At that time, Sixties, the gifts we give you
  Will add an extra edition of the death of imperialism; when more flowers bloom furiously in the spring, I know you will be even more lovely!
  A cry comes from the East...A cry comes from the East, like a typhoon sweeping the Japanese coast, no, this is the voice of the Japanese people: give us independence, democracy, security! No more cold tears, no more nights with curfews, no more scorched earth, no more white lightning flashes. This year is a fifteen-year-old child, who does not yet understand the disaster of war; a sixty-five-year-old grandfather looks at him, blinking silently...A cry comes from the East, this cry shakes the mountains for thousands of miles, the ocean surges, the sky changes, everyone is a volcano about to erupt!

### 时代已经变了
李瑛
1960-01-19
第8版()
专栏：

    时代已经变了
    李瑛我们把什么送给六十年代
  我们把什么送给六十年代？
  把火星探险记录，
  把宇宙飞船，
  把一片绿茵和人造海？
  然而在它到来的开始，
  就被染上一点冬天的颜色：
  被美国火药的硝烟，
  被岸信介的罪恶买卖。
  反对！反对日美军事同盟！
  这一把刺刀想把和平劈开，
  我要以孩子父亲的名义疾呼：
  住手，不许给世界带来悲哀！
  我们曾用自己的双手，打败日本帝国主义者；强盗横行的日子消逝了，今天再不是从前的年代！不许再烧起侵略的野火，烧毁我们的绿色的林带；烈焰将吞尽你们自己，剩下的只是一把尘埃。那时，六十年代，我们送给你的
  礼品，要加一张帝国主义死亡的号
  外；当春天更多的花朵怒放的时刻，我知道你就更加可爱！
  从东方传来一片呼喊……从东方传来一片呼喊，像台风卷遍日本海岸，不，这是日本人民的声音：给我们独立、民主、安全！再不要冰冷的眼泪，再不要灯火管制的夜晚，再不要一片焦黑的大地，再不要一道白色的闪电。今年是十五岁的孩子，还不懂得战争的灾难；六十五岁的老爷爷望着他，沉默的眨一眨眼……从东方传来一片呼喊，这呼喊摇动着万里群山，海洋激荡，天空变化，每个人都是一座爆发的火山！
