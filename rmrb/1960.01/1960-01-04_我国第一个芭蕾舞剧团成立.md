Title: China's First Ballet Troupe Established
Author:
Date: 1960-01-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: China's first ballet troupe—the Experimental Ballet Troupe of the Beijing Dance School—held its inaugural ceremony in Beijing on December 30, 1959. Responsible persons from central and Beijing departments concerned, as well as fraternal art troupes, attended the ceremony to offer congratulations. This ballet troupe is mainly composed of graduates of the Ballet Department of the Beijing Dance School, with some young teachers and students also participating.

Xia Yan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, Chen Kehan, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Wang Kunlun, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, spoke at the meeting, encouraging the first batch of ballet dancers in China to rely closely on the leadership of the Party and strive to become an artistic force that is both red and expert.

At the meeting, Soviet expert Gusev delivered a warm congratulatory speech, hoping that the young Chinese ballet dancers would grow rapidly.

### 我国第一个芭蕾舞剧团成立

1960-01-04
第4版()
专栏：

    我国第一个芭蕾舞剧团成立
    据新华社讯　我国第一个芭蕾舞剧团——北京舞蹈学校实验芭蕾舞剧团，1959年12月30日在北京举行成立典礼，中央和北京有关部门、各兄弟艺术团体负责人都到会庆贺。这个芭蕾舞剧团是以北京舞蹈学校芭蕾舞剧科应届毕业生为主，还有一部分青年教师和学生参加。
    中华人民共和国文化部副部长夏衍、中共北京市委书记处书记陈克寒、北京市副市长王昆仑先后在会上讲话，勉励我国第一批芭蕾舞演员，紧紧地依靠党的领导，锻炼成为一支又红又专的艺术队伍。
    会上苏联专家古雪夫热情洋溢地致贺词，希望中国年轻的芭蕾舞演员迅速地成长起来。
