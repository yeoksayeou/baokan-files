Title: Basutoland's First Election: Pro-Independence Candidate Wins
Author:
Date: 1960-01-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Johannesburg, 23rd: Basutoland, a British "protectorate" in South Africa, held its first election on the 20th.

According to preliminary election results, the Basutoland African National Congress candidate and the party's Secretary-General, Ntsu Mokhehle, won in the Maseru district, the capital. The Basutoland African National Congress, one of Basutoland's two major political parties, is fighting for complete independence as soon as possible.

### 巴苏陀兰首次选举  主张独立的候选人获胜

1960-01-27
第6版()
专栏：

    巴苏陀兰首次选举
    主张独立的候选人获胜
    据新华社23日讯  约翰内斯堡消息：在南非的英国“保护国”巴苏陀兰于20日举行了第一次选举。
    根据选举的初步结果，巴苏陀兰非洲人国民大会候选人、这个党的总书记卡克特拉在首都马塞卢区获胜。作为巴苏陀兰两个主要政党之一的巴苏陀兰非洲人国民大会正在为早日实现完全独立而斗争。
