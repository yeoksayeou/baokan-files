Title: Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee Holds Meeting to Discuss Industrial Issues; Extensive Discussions Held Throughout the Country Before the Meeting, with Many Specific Suggestions Put Forward
Author:
Date: 1960-01-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 26th - According to the Bulgarian News Agency, the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party held a plenary session on the 26th to discuss industrial issues. The meeting was opened by Zhivkov, First Secretary of the BCP Central Committee. Zolov, Secretary of the BCP Central Committee, delivered a report on "Further Development of Heavy Industry, Expanding the Raw Material Base of Heavy Industry, and Specialization and Collaboration of Industry."

	Stoyanov, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the BCP Central Committee, then delivered a report on "Accelerating Technological Progress in Industry."

	The meeting then began discussing their reports.

	The meeting continues.

	Prior to the plenary session of the BCP Central Committee, more than 12,400 meetings were held throughout Bulgaria under the leadership of Party committees at all levels to extensively discuss issues in industrial development. More than 500,000 people participated in these meetings, putting forward more than 42,000 specific and feasible suggestions. It is estimated that the adoption of these suggestions will yield economic benefits of more than 2.5 billion leva (Bulgarian currency).

### 保共中央开会讨论工业问题  会前全国各地曾进行广泛讨论提出很多具体建议

1960-01-28
第6版()
专栏：

     保共中央开会讨论工业问题
    会前全国各地曾进行广泛讨论提出很多具体建议
    新华社索非亚26日电　据保加利亚通讯社报道，保加利亚共产党中央委员会26日举行全体会议，讨论工业问题。会议由保共中央第一书记日夫科夫主持开幕。保共中央书记处书记佐洛夫在会上作了“关于进一步发展重工业、扩大重工业的原料基地以及工业的专业化和协作”问题的报告。
    保共中央政治局候补委员斯托扬诺夫接着在会上作了关于“加速工业中的技术进步”的报告。
    会议接着开始讨论他们的报告。
    会议继续进行。
    在保共中央召开这次全体会议以前，保加利亚全国各地在各级党委领导下举行了一万二千四百多次会议，广泛地讨论工业发展中的问题。五十万多人参加了这些会议，共提出了四万二千多条具体可行的建议，预计，采用这些建议后将可以获得二十五亿列弗（保币）以上的经济利益。
