Title: Hungarian Mathematician Paul Erdős Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-01-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. At the invitation of Hua Luogeng, Director of the Institute of Mathematics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, the famous Hungarian mathematician and corresponding academician of the Hungarian Academy of Sciences, Professor Paul Erdős, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 16th for a visit to China.

### 匈牙利著名数学家恩德斯·巴尔到京

1960-01-19
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利著名数学家恩德斯·巴尔到京
    新华社16日讯　应中国科学院数学研究所所长华罗庚邀请来我国访问的匈牙利著名数学家、匈牙利科学院通讯院士恩德斯·巴尔教授，16日乘飞机到达北京。
