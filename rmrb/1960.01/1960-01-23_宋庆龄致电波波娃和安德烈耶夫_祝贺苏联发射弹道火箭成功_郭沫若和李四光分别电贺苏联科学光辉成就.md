Title: Soong Ching Ling Sends Telegram to Popova and Andreyev Congratulating the Soviet Union on the Successful Launch of a Ballistic Missile; Guo Moruo and Li Siguang Send Telegrams Separately Congratulating the Soviet Union on its Brilliant Scientific Achievements
Author:
Date: 1960-01-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, January 22nd: Soong Ching Ling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, sent a telegram to Popova, Chairman of the Presidium of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Andreyev, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, warmly congratulating the Soviet Union on the successful launch of a multi-stage ballistic missile into the Pacific Ocean. The telegram reads as follows:
    Dear Comrade Popova! Dear Comrade Andreyev!
    We are delighted to learn of the Soviet Union's successful launch of a powerful multi-stage ballistic missile into the Pacific Ocean. On behalf of all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, I extend to you, and through you, to the great Soviet people, the outstanding Soviet scientists, engineers, technicians, and workers, our heartfelt and warm congratulations!
    The success of the Soviet Union's new rocket test marks a new and significant step forward in the noble cause of humanity's conquest of outer space. It demonstrates to the people of the world the bright and splendid prospects for humanity's realization of interstellar travel. It brings boundless encouragement to all progressive mankind.
    The new rocket successfully launched by the Soviet Union serves the cause of peace. The high accuracy and immense power of the new Soviet rocket demonstrate that it is a rocket of peace and also the most powerful weapon for defending the cause of peace.
    I wish the great Soviet people new and greater achievements in the glorious cause of conquering outer space!
    Long live the great and unbreakable Sino-Soviet friendship!
                                  Soong Ching Ling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
                                                      January 22, 1960

Xinhua News Agency, January 22nd: Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, sent a telegram to Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, warmly congratulating the Soviet Union on the successful launch of a multi-stage ballistic missile into the Pacific. The telegram reads as follows:
Soviet Academy of Sciences, Moscow
Respected President Nesmeyanov:
    I am delighted to learn that the Soviet Union successfully launched a multi-stage ballistic missile into the Pacific region on the evening of January 20, 1960. Please allow me, on behalf of the Chinese scientists and myself, to extend to you, and through you, to the Soviet scientists, our warm congratulations and high respect!
    The Soviet Union's launch of a multi-stage ballistic missile into the Pacific region, according to the determination of the measurement data, proves the high accuracy of the rocket control system. The successful launch of the powerful Soviet multi-stage ballistic missile guarantees that Soviet science will move further along the path of conquering outer space and studying the planets of the solar system. This is good news for the socialist camp and all peace-loving people around the world, and it also gives encouragement and strength to the people fighting for the cause of peace.
    We wish the Soviet scientific research cause further development and greater and more creative achievements!
                                          Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences
                                                        January 21, 1960

Xinhua News Agency, January 22nd: Li Siguang, Chairman of the China Association for Science and Technology, today sent a telegram to Sirazhiddinov, Chairman of the All-Union Council of Scientific and Technical Societies, Mitin, Chairman of the All-Union Society for Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge, and Golyukov, Chairman of the Trade Union of Workers in Higher Education and Scientific Institutions of the Soviet Union, congratulating the Soviet Union on the successful launch of the first powerful experimental rocket.
    The telegram said: The successful launch of this Soviet experimental rocket marks that the high development and leap forward progress of Soviet science and technology has entered a new stage of exploring the universe. It has made good preparations for launching heavy Earth satellites and realizing space flights to the planets of the solar system, and provided more favorable conditions for mankind to conquer outer space. This brilliant achievement of Soviet science and technology once again demonstrates the unparalleled superiority of the socialist system, and further proves that the East wind prevails over the West wind.
    The telegram concluded: "On behalf of all members of the China Association for Science and Technology, I extend to you, and through you, to the Soviet scientists, our warm congratulations, and wish you greater achievements in your scientific endeavors."

### 宋庆龄致电波波娃和安德烈耶夫  祝贺苏联发射弹道火箭成功  郭沫若和李四光分别电贺苏联科学光辉成就

1960-01-23
第3版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄致电波波娃和安德烈耶夫
    祝贺苏联发射弹道火箭成功
    郭沫若和李四光分别电贺苏联科学光辉成就
    新华社22日讯　中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄致电苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会理事会主席团主席波波娃和苏中友好协会理事会主席安德烈耶夫，热烈祝贺苏联向太平洋地区发射多级弹道火箭成功。电文如下：亲爱的波波娃同志！亲爱的安德烈耶夫同志！
    欣闻苏联向太平洋地区成功地发射出威力强大的多级弹道火箭，谨代表中苏友好协会全体会员，向你们，并通过你们，向伟大的苏联人民、卓越的苏联科学家、工程技术人员和工人表示衷心的热烈的祝贺！
    苏联新火箭试验的成功，标志着苏联在人类征服宇宙空间的崇高事业中，又向前迈出了新的巨大的一步。它向全世界人民展示了人类实现星际旅行的光辉烂灿的前景。它给全体进步人类带来了无限的鼓舞力量。
    苏联成功发射的新火箭是为和平事业服务的。苏联新火箭的高度准确性和强大的威力，表明了它是和平的火箭，也是保卫和平事业的最强有力的武器。
    谨祝伟大的苏联人民在征服宇宙空间的光辉事业中获得新的更大的成就！
    伟大的牢不可破的中苏友谊万岁！
                          中苏友好协会总会会长　宋庆龄
                                            1960年1月22日
    新华社22日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若致电苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫，热烈祝贺苏联向太平洋发射多级弹道火箭成功。电文如下：苏联莫斯科苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫敬爱的涅斯米扬诺夫院长：
    欣闻苏联于1960年1月20日晚间成功地在苏联往太平洋区发射了一个多级弹道火箭。请允许我代表中国科学工作者和我个人，向您并通过您向苏联科学工作者，致以热烈的祝贺和崇高的敬意！
    苏联这次往太平洋区发射了一个多级弹道火箭，根据进行测量数据的确定，证明了火箭控制系统的高度准确性。强大的苏联多级弹道火箭的顺利发射，保证苏联科学沿着征服宇宙空间和研究太阳系行星的道路进一步前进。这是社会主义阵营和全世界爱好和平的人民的福音，同时也给予为和平事业而斗争的人民以鼓舞和力量。
    我们预祝苏联科学研究事业更进一步的发展，获得更伟大和创造性的成就！
                                  中国科学院院长　郭沫若
                                              1960年1月21日
    新华社22日讯　中华全国科学技术协会主席李四光今天致电全苏科学技术协会主席西鲁雅诺夫、全苏政治和科学知识普及协会主席米丁和苏联高等教育和科学工作者工会主席果里夫哥夫，祝贺苏联发射第一个强大试验火箭成功。
    电文说：苏联这次试验性火箭发射成功，标志着苏联科学技术的高度发展和飞跃进步已进入了探索宇宙的一个新阶段。它为发射重型地球卫星和实现向太阳系行星的宇宙飞行，作了良好的准备；为人类征服宇宙空间提供了更有利的条件。苏联科学技术的这一辉煌成就，再一次说明了社会主义制度的无比优越性，进一步证明了东风压倒西风。
    电文最后说：“我谨代表中国科学技术协会全体会员向您，并通过您向苏联的科学家致以热烈的祝贺，祝贺你们在科学事业中获得更伟大的成就。”
