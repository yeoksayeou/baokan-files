Title: CPCz Central Committee Discusses Novotný Report; Decides to Convene National Congress of the Party in July; Will Discuss the Third Five-Year Plan and New Constitution
Author:
Date: 1960-01-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, January 15th: The Czechoslovak News Agency reported that the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia held a meeting here on the 13th and 14th to discuss the report made by First Secretary of the Party Central Committee Novotný on the redivision of national administrative regions and other issues, and adopted corresponding resolutions.

The meeting unanimously adopted a resolution to convene the National Congress of the Party on July 5th of this year, at which the Third Five-Year Plan (1961-1965) for the development of the national economy and Czechoslovakia's new constitution will be discussed.

The meeting also adopted a resolution on redividing national administrative regions and further strengthening socialist democracy in the institutions and work of the People's Committees. The resolution instructed the government to formulate proposals based on the following principles and submit them to the National Assembly for discussion: abolish the existing nineteen regions and establish ten new regions (seven in the Czech region and three in the Slovak region). The division of these regions should be consistent with the existing economic zones to the greatest extent possible, and the capital Prague will constitute a separate independent regional unit; maintain the township, county, and regional People's Committees, with the county People's Committee being the main link in the People's Committee system; continue to have the central ministries lead industrial enterprises of national importance, while the People's Committees lead enterprises of local importance; and the national economic plan is a law that all agencies and organizations must unconditionally abide by.

The resolution stated that the basis of the entire activity of the People's Committees is democratic centralism. This Leninist principle harmoniously combines the leadership of the central government on matters of principle with the broad powers and responsibilities of lower levels in practical work leadership, and enables all the people to participate most widely in management and to exert the greatest enthusiasm.

The Central Committee also adopted a resolution on reorganizing the existing regional and county party organizations according to the new administrative divisions.

### 捷共中央讨论诺沃提尼报告  决定七月召开党的全国代表会议  将讨论第三个五年计划和新宪法

1960-01-18
第5版()
专栏：

    捷共中央讨论诺沃提尼报告
    决定七月召开党的全国代表会议
    将讨论第三个五年计划和新宪法
    新华社布拉格15日电　捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道：捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会13日和14日在这里举行会议，讨论了党中央第一书记诺沃提尼所作的关于重新划分国家行政区划等问题的报告，并通过了相应的决议。
    会议一致通过了关于在今年7月5日召开党的全国代表会议的决议，代表会议将讨论发展国民经济的第三个五年计划（1961—1965年）和捷克斯洛伐克的新宪法。
    会议还通过了一项关于重新划分国家行政区划，在人民委员会的机构及其工作中进一步加强社会主义民主的决议。决议责成政府拟定以下列原则为基础的各项建议，并把这些建议提交国民议会讨论：撤销现有的十九个州，成立十个新的州（七个在捷克地区，三个在斯洛伐克地区），这些州的划分应当在最大程度上同现有的经济区相一致，而首都布拉格将单独构成一个独立的区域单位；保持乡、县和州人民委员会，其中县人民委员会是人民委员会制度中的主要环节，继续由中央各部领导具有全国重要性的工业企业，而人民委员会领导具有地方重要性的企业；国民经济计划是一切机关和组织必须无条件遵守的法律。
    决议说，人民委员会的整个活动的基础是民主集中制。这个列宁主义的原则把中央在原则问题上的领导同下级在实际工作领导方面的广泛权限和职责和谐地结合起来，并且可以使全体人民最广泛地参加管理工作和发挥最大的积极性。
    中央委员会还通过了关于按新的行政区划改组现有的州、县党组织的决议。
