Treasure Within a Treasure
　　Coal is a treasure within a treasure,
　　Indispensable for production and life;
　　If used to refine petroleum, it transforms into four kinds of treasure. (Note)
　　Note: Using coal to extract petroleum not only yields oil products, but also semi-coke, coal gas, chemical raw materials and other products.

### 宝中宝

1960-01-14
第2版()
专栏：

    宝中宝
    煤是宝中宝，
    生产、生活少不了；
    如若用它炼石油，摇身化为四宗宝。（注）
    注：用煤提炼石油，除可得到油品外，还可以得到半焦、煤气、化工原料等产品。
