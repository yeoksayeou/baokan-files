Title: Sihanouk Fetes Chinese Experts
Author:
Date: 1960-01-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 5th -- Cambodian Prime Minister Prince Sihanouk held a banquet at the Royal Palace on the evening of the 4th, to entertain the six Chinese experts who helped build the Cambodian Royal Radio Station. This radio station was a gift from Premier Zhou Enlai to Prince Sihanouk. The first phase of the radio station project has recently been completed.

Also attending the banquet was Cambodian Secretary of State for Information, Long Boret. Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia, Wang Youping, and Commercial Counselor, Ye Jinghao, were also invited to attend the banquet.

After the banquet, Prince Sihanouk personally awarded the Chinese expert, Xiong Jingzhao, the Gold Medal of the Officer of Common Friendship, and awarded the other five experts the Silver Medal of the Royal Knight of Common Friendship. He also asked the Chinese experts to convey his deep gratitude to Premier Zhou Enlai. He thanked Premier Zhou for giving him a gift that symbolized the friendship between the people of the two countries.

Xiong Jingzhao delivered a speech on behalf of the Chinese experts, thanking Prince Sihanouk for the honor he had bestowed upon them.

### 西哈努克宴请我国专家

1960-01-07
第6版()
专栏：

    西哈努克宴请我国专家
    新华社金边5日电　柬埔寨首相西哈努克亲王4日晚间在王宫举行宴会，招待帮助建设柬埔寨皇家电台的六位中国专家。这个电台是周恩来总理赠送给西哈努克亲王的礼物。电台的第一期工程已在最近完成。
    出席宴会的还有柬埔寨新闻国务秘书龙波雷。中国驻柬埔寨大使王幼平和商务参赞叶景灏也应邀出席了宴会。
    宴会后，西哈努克亲王亲自授予中国专家熊景钊共同友谊军官金质奖章，授予其他五位专家共同友谊皇家骑士银质奖章。他还请中国专家们转达他对周恩来总理的深切谢意。他感谢周总理赠送给他的象征着两国人民的友谊的礼物。
    熊景钊代表中国专家致辞，感谢西哈努克亲王给予他们的荣誉。
