Title: Soviet Political Delegation Visits the United States
Author:
Date: 1960-01-31
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, New York, January 30th: According to TASS, the Soviet political delegation headed by D.S. Polyansky, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Russian Federation, arrived in New York yesterday for a visit to the United States.

### 苏政治家代表团访美

1960-01-31
第3版()
专栏：

    苏政治家代表团访美
    新华社30日讯　据塔斯社纽约讯：由俄罗斯联邦部长会议主席德·斯·波利扬斯基率领的苏联政治家代表团昨天到达纽约，他们将在美国访问。
