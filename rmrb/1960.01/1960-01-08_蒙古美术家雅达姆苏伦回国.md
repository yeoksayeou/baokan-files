Title: Mongolian Artist Yadamusurun Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-01-08
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, January 6th. Yadamusurun, Honored Artist of the Mongolian People's Republic and winner of the Choibalsan Prize, who came to China under the 1959 executive plan of the Sino-Mongolian Cultural Cooperation Agreement to assist in the preparation of the Mongolian People's Republic Fine Arts Exhibition, returned home by train on the afternoon of the 6th.

### 蒙古美术家雅达姆苏伦回国

1960-01-08
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古美术家雅达姆苏伦回国
    新华社6日讯　根据中蒙文化合作协定1959年执行计划来我国协助筹办蒙古人民共和国造型艺术展览会的蒙古人民共和国功勋美术家、乔巴山奖金获得者雅达姆苏伦，在6日下午乘火车回国。
