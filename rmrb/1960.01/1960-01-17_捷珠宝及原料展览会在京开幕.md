Title: Czechoslovak Jewelry and Raw Material Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-01-17
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 16th – The Czechoslovak Jewelry and Raw Material Exhibition opened today at the sample exhibition hall of the Commercial Office of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China. The exhibition displays many exquisite artificial jewels and raw materials manufactured in Jablonec nad Nisou. Jablonec nad Nisou's artificial jewelry is world-renowned and has been successfully exhibited at more than fifty international fairs and exhibitions.

### 捷珠宝及原料展览会在京开幕

1960-01-17
第2版()
专栏：

    捷珠宝及原料展览会在京开幕
    新华社16日讯　捷克斯洛伐克珠宝及原料展览会，今天在捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使馆商务处样品展览所开幕。展览会展出了亚伯浪耐克斯城市制造的许多精致的人造珠宝和原料。亚伯浪耐克斯的人造珠宝驰名世界，在历次五十多个国际博览会和展览会上的展出都获得了成功。
