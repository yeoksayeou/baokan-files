Title: China's First Floating Dock Built in Chongqing Before the New Year

Author:
Date: 1960-01-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing: China's first self-designed and manufactured floating dock has been completed in Chongqing. A launching ceremony was held on December 27th of last year, and it was officially put into production.

The completion of this dock is of great significance for the repair of large ships on the upper reaches of the Yangtze River. Now that this dock has been completed, it can undertake the repair tasks of various large ships operating on the upper reaches of the Yangtze River.

### 我国第一艘浮沉船  坞年前在重庆建成

1960-01-03
第3版()
专栏：

    我国第一艘浮沉船坞年前在重庆建成
    据新华社重庆讯　我国自己设计制造的一艘浮沉船坞，在重庆建成。去年十二月二十七日举行了沉浮典礼，正式投入生产。
    这艘船坞的建成，对长江上游大型船舶的修理有着重大的意义。现在，这艘船坞建成了，它就可以担负长江上游行驶的各种大型船舶的修理任务。
