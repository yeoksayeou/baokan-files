Title: Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council, NPC Deputy, Standing Committee Member of the CPPCC, Standing Committee Member of the Kuomintang Revolutionary Committee, Wei Lihuang, Dies of Illness
Author:
Date: 1960-01-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 17th: Wei Lihuang, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council, Deputy to the National People's Congress, Standing Committee Member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and Standing Committee Member of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, passed away in Beijing at 0:40 on January 17th due to a long-term coronary arteriosclerotic heart disease complicated by pneumonia, despite medical treatment.

Wei Lihuang was from Hefei City, Anhui Province, and was 64 years old this year.

Xinhua News Agency, January 17th: Mr. Wei Lihuang's remains were placed in a coffin at 4:00 PM on the 17th.

Present to personally oversee the placement in the coffin were Li Weihan and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Xi Zhongxun, Vice Premier of the State Council; Zhang Zhizhong, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council and Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Kuomintang Revolutionary Committee; Bao Erhan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC; some members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; some members of the National Defense Council; some Standing Committee members of the National Committee of the CPPCC; some Standing Committee members and Central Committee members of the Central Committee of the Kuomintang Revolutionary Committee; as well as friends of Mr. Wei Lihuang during his lifetime.

Mr. Wei Lihuang's wife and son were present at the bedside during the placement in the coffin.

Obituary

Mr. Wei Lihuang, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council, Deputy to the National People's Congress, Standing Committee Member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and Standing Committee Member of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, passed away in Beijing at 0:40 AM on January 17, 1960, due to illness. This announcement is hereby made.

### 国防委员会副主席、人大代表、政协常委、民革常委  卫立煌病逝

1960-01-18
第1版()
专栏：

    国防委员会副主席、人大代表、政协常委、民革常委
    卫立煌病逝
    新华社17日讯　国防委员会副主席、全国人民代表大会代表、政协全国委员会常务委员、民革中央常务委员卫立煌，因长期患冠状动脉硬化心脏病，最近又患肺炎，经医治无效，于1月17日零时四十分在北京逝世。
    卫立煌是安徽省合肥市人，今年六十四岁。
    新华社17日讯　卫立煌先生的遗体在17日下午4时入殓。
    亲视入殓的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李维汉、陈叔通，国务院副总理习仲勋，国防委员会副主席、民革中央副主席张治中，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，全国人民代表大会常务委员会部分委员，国防委员会部分委员，政协全国委员会部分常务委员，民革中央部分常务委员和中央委员，以及卫立煌先生的生前友好等。
    入殓时，卫立煌先生的夫人、儿子在旁守灵。
    讣　告
    国防委员会副主席、全国人民代表大会代表、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员、中国国民党革命委员会中央常务委员卫立煌先生于1960年1月17日晨零时40分因病在北京逝世，特此讣告。
