Title: Opening Doors to Create Miracles
Author: Yan Shuben
Date: 1960-01-23
Page: 8

    Opening doors to create miracles,
    Mounting the steed,
    Whipping up the Great Leap Forward;
    Opening doors to create miracles,
    Heroes battle the year 1960.
    Machines roar to the heavens,
    Lathes spin as fast as lightning;
    Iron filings fly like flower petals,
    Production targets soar like rockets.
    The first round is already a victory,
    Tomorrow will bring even more frequent good news.
    Thousands of brave generals display their talents,
    Let the good news of the whole year pile up into high mountains.
            Jilin, Yan Shuben
