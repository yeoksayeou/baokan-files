Title: China and Cuba Sign First Trade Agreement: Purchase of 50,000 Tons of Cuban Raw Sugar
Author:
Date: 1960-01-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th — After friendly discussions on the basis of equality and mutual benefit, the China National Foodstuffs Export Corporation and Cuban trade organizations signed a contract on December 31st of last year for China to purchase 50,000 tons of Cuban raw sugar. The offshore price of the sugar is 2.93 cents per pound, with delivery in January and February of this year.

    This is the first trade agreement signed between China and Cuba.

### 我国同古巴签订第一个贸易合同  购买古巴的原糖五万吨

1960-01-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我国同古巴签订第一个贸易合同
    购买古巴的原糖五万吨
    新华社11日讯　中国食品出口公司与古巴贸易机构经过友好的商谈，在平等互利的基础上于去年12月31日签订了中国购买古巴五万吨原糖的合同。糖的离岸价格为每磅二点九三美分，于今年1、2月交货。
    这是我国第一次同古巴签订的贸易合同。
