Title: New UAR Ambassador to China Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-01-18
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, January 17th -- The new Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the United Arab Republic to China, Mr. Salah El Dine Tarazi, arrived in Beijing by train tonight.
    Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was present at the station to welcome him.

### 阿联新任驻华大使到京

1960-01-18
第4版()
专栏：

    阿联新任驻华大使到京
    新华社17日讯　阿拉伯联合共和国新任驻华特命全权大使萨拉赫丁·塔拉齐先生，在今天晚上乘火车到达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文。
