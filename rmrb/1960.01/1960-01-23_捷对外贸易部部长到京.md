Title: Czechoslovak Foreign Trade Minister Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-01-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, January 22nd. Czechoslovak Republic's Minister of Foreign Trade, Mr. Krajcir, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 21st at the invitation of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of China.

Those who went to the airport to welcome him included Lei Renmin, Acting Minister of Foreign Trade, Lin Haiyun, Vice Minister, Jia Shi, Director of the Second Bureau, Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Soviet and European Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Yu Mei, Director of the Haihe General Bureau of the Ministry of Communications, and Gao Shirong, Commercial Counselor of our Embassy in Czechoslovakia.

Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Bushniak, Counselor Mr. Stehlik, Mr. Netolicky, Deputy Head of the Czechoslovak Government Trade Delegation, and Mr. Shevchik, Deputy Head and Commercial Counselor stationed in China, who came to China to discuss trade issues between China and Czechoslovakia this year, also went to the airport to greet him.

On the morning of the 22nd, Acting Minister Lei Renmin held talks with Minister Krajcir. A welcoming banquet was held that evening. Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Bushniak, also attended the banquet.

### 捷对外贸易部部长到京

1960-01-23
第3版()
专栏：

    捷对外贸易部部长到京
    新华社22日讯　捷克斯洛伐克共和国对外贸易部部长克拉伊契尔应对外贸易部邀请来我国访问，已于21日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有我对外贸易部代理部长雷任民、副部长林海云，第二局局长贾石，外交部苏欧司副司长陈伯清，交通部海河总局局长于眉和我驻捷商务参赞高仕融等人。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使布希尼亚克、参赞石杰基那，前来我国商谈今年中捷两国贸易问题的捷克斯洛伐克政府贸易代表团副团长涅托利茨基和副团长、驻华商务参赞舍伏契克等人也去机场迎接。
    22日上午，雷任民代理部长同克拉伊契尔部长进行了会谈。当晚举行了欢迎宴会。捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使布希尼亚克也参加了宴会。
