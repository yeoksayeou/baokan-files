Title: China and East Germany Sign Treaty of Commerce and Navigation; Simultaneously Sign Goods Exchange Agreement for 1960-1962
Author:
Date: 1960-01-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 18th: The Treaty of Commerce and Navigation between the People's Republic of China and the German Democratic Repubic, and the Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Goverment of the German Democratic Republic on the exchange of goods from 1960 to 1962, were signed simultaneously in Beijing this afternoon.

    Premier Zhou Enlai attended the signing ceremony.

    At 5:30 PM, Vice Premier Li Xiannian and Vice Premier Heinrich Rau, representing China and East Germany respectively, signed the Treaty of Commerce and Navigation between the People's Republic of China and the German Democratic Republic, and the Agreement between the Government of the People' Republic of China and the Government of the German Democratic Republic on the exchange of goods from 1960 to 1962. After the signing, the hosts and guests warmly shook hands and toasted with champagne.

    Chinese participants in the signing ceremony also included Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yi and Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo and Huang Yanpei, member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, Vice Ministers of Foreign Trade Lei Renmin and Lin Haiyun, Vice Chairman of the State Planning Commission Liu Mingfu, Vice Chairman of the Science and Technology Commission Wu Heng, and others.

    German Democratic Republic participants in the signing ceremony also included alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany Paul Verner, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs and member of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany Otto Winzer, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China Wang Daiel, and others. ### 中德签订通商航海条约  同时签订一九六○年到一九六二年换货协定

1960-01-19
第1版()
专栏：

    中德签订通商航海条约
    同时签订一九六○年到一九六二年换货协定
    新华社18日讯　中华人民共和国和德意志民主共和国通商航海条约、中华人民共和国政府和德意志民主共和国政府关于1960年到1962年交换货物协定，今天下午在北京同时签字。
    周恩来总理参加了签字仪式。
    五时三十分，李先念副总理和海因里希·劳副总理，分别代表中德两国在中华人民共和国和德意志民主共和国通商航海条约、中华人民共和国政府和德意志民主共和国政府关于1960年到1962年交换货物协定上签了字。签字以后，宾主热烈握手，并进香槟酒祝贺。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的，还有国务院副总理陈毅、习仲勋，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若、黄炎培，中共中央委员伍修权，外交部副部长曾涌泉，对外贸易部副部长雷任民、林海云，国家计划委员会副主任刘明夫，科学技术委员会副主任武衡等。
    德意志民主共和国方面参加签字仪式的，还有德国统一社会党中央政治局候补委员保罗·维尔纳，外交部第一副部长、德国统一社会党中央委员奥托·文策尔，德意志民主共和国驻中国大使汪戴尔等。（附图片）
    李先念副总理（右）和海因里希·劳副总理分别代表中德两国在中华人民共和国和德意志民主共和国通商航海条约、中华人民共和国政府和德意志民主共和国政府关于1960年到1962年交换货物协定上签字。
              新华社记者　刘东鳌摄
