Title: Minhang
Author: Zheng Chengyi
Date: 1960-01-28
Page: 8

Minhang

Zheng Chengyi, worker at Shanghai Dafeng No. 2 Factory

First Street

Thinking of going to Minhang,
Unconsciously arriving in paradise.
The loudspeaker sings amidst the colorful clouds,
The park is built in the center of the street;
The main street is full of tall buildings,
The factory chimneys stand in rows;
"Welcome new masters!"
The new village is filled with the fragrance of paint.

Walking on the street,
Passion burns like fire –
A graveyard erected into First Street,
Spring rain and bamboo shoots grow one after another;
A blank sheet of paper is the richest,
The Party paints a beautiful landscape on it.

To the Vertical Lathe Operator

Looking up at the vertical lathe,
Magnificent like a triumphal arch,
A huge commander's flag,
Glitters on the top of the lathe.

You press the button,
The whole earth is rotating.
Ah! We are repairing the Earth,
Humming songs, sweating profusely…

(Note) Minhang is one of Shanghai's newly built satellite cities.

### 闵行
郑成义
1960-01-28
第8版()
专栏：

    闵行
    上海达丰二厂工人　郑成义
    一号街
    心想去闵行
    不觉到天堂
    喇叭唱在彩霞间，
    公园造在街中央；
    大街高楼幢连幢，
    工厂烟囱行对行；
    “欢迎新主人！”
    新村飘漆香。
    走在街道上，
    激情火样旺——
    坟地矗起一号街，
    春雨春笋接着长；
    一张白纸最富有，
    党给画上好风光。
    给立车手
    仰看立式车床，
    凯旋门般雄壮，
    一面巨幅帅旗，
    在车顶上闪光。
    你把电钮一按，
    整个地球都在旋转。
    呵！我们是在修理地球呵，
    哼着歌，冒着热汗……
        （注）闵行是上海新建的卫星城之一。
