Title: Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam Holds Reception Celebrating the 10th Anniversary of Sino-Vietnamese Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1960-01-19
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 18th. Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam He Wei held a reception at the Chinese Embassy tonight to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Vietnam.

Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Pham Van Dong attended the reception. Also attending the reception were government ministers and the President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, Phi ớc, as well as foreign diplomatic envoys to Vietnam.

At the reception, Ambassador He Wei proposed a toast to the greater victories achieved in the socialist construction of North Vietnam, the struggle for national reunification, and on the diplomatic front, to the close unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and to the unbreakable friendship between China and Vietnam.

Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Pham Van Dong said in his speech at the reception: We must strengthen the friendship between Vietnam and China and other socialist countries. He proposed a toast to peace and friendship among socialist countries and all countries in the world.

The reception took place in a friendly and warm atmosphere. After the meeting, the Chinese film "Five Golden Flowers" was shown.

### 庆祝中越建交十周年  我驻越大使举行酒会

1960-01-19
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝中越建交十周年
    我驻越大使举行酒会
    新华社河内18日电　中国驻越南大使何伟今天晚上在中国大使馆举行酒会，庆祝中越两国建交十周年。
    范文同总理兼外交部长出席了酒会。出席酒会的还有政府部长们和越中友协会长斐?以及各国驻越南外交使节。
    何伟大使在酒会上提议为越南北方的社会主义建设事业、国家统一斗争和外交战线上取得更伟大的胜利，为以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的紧密团结，为中越两国牢不可破的友谊而干杯。
    范文同总理兼外交部长在酒会上致词说，我们一定要加强越中两国和其它社会主义国家的友谊。他提议为社会主义各国和世界各国的和平、友谊而干杯。
    酒会在充满友好、热烈的气氛中进行。会后放映了中国电影“五朵金花”。
