Title: Widespread Auspicious Snowfall in Hubei

Author: 
Date: 1960-01-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, January 1st. On New Year's Eve—from December 29th to the morning of December 31st—auspicious snow fell throughout Hubei Province. The general snowfall was about two to three millimeters. The area with the heaviest snowfall received six millimeters.

This was the most widespread and largest snowfall in Hubei Province since the beginning of winter. This snowfall is very beneficial for killing overwintering pests, loosening the soil, and promoting wheat growth.

### 湖北普降瑞雪

1960-01-02
第2版()
专栏：

    湖北普降瑞雪
    新华社武汉1日电　新年前夕——12月29日到31日晨，湖北省各地普降瑞雪。一般降雪二到三毫米左右。降雪最大的地区下了六毫米。
    这次是湖北省入冬以来最普遍最大的一场雪。这场雪对灭杀越冬虫害，疏松土壤，促进小麦生长有很大好处。
