Title: Premier Zhou Cables Cameroonian Prime Minister to Congratulate Cameroon's Independence
Author:
Date: 1960-01-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 1st. Former French Cameroons declared independence today. Premier Zhou Enlai sent a cable to the Prime Minister of the Republic of Cameroon to congratulate the Cameroonian people. The full text of the congratulatory message is as follows:

To His Excellency the Prime Minister of the Republic of Cameroon, Yaoundé, Cameroon:

On the occasion of Cameroon's declaration of independence, I am honored to extend warm congratulations to the Cameroonian people on behalf of the Government and people of the People's Republic of China. I wish the Cameroonian people new achievements in the cause of safeguarding national independence.

Zhou Enlai
Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
January 1, 1960, Beijing

### 周总理电喀麦隆总理  祝贺喀麦隆独立

1960-01-02
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电喀麦隆总理
    祝贺喀麦隆独立
    新华社1日讯　前法属喀麦隆已于今天宣布独立。周恩来总理特致电喀麦隆共和国总理，向喀麦隆人民表示祝贺，贺电全文如下：喀麦隆雅温得喀麦隆共和国总理阁下：
    值此喀麦隆宣布独立之际，我荣幸地代表中华人民共和国政府和人民向喀麦隆人民致以热烈的祝贺。祝喀麦隆人民在维护民族独立的事业中取得新的成就。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
    1960年1月1日于北京
