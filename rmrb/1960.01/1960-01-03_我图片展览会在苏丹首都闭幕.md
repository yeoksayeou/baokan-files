Title: My Photo Exhibition Closes in Sudanese Capital
Author:
Date: 1960-01-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Khartoum, January 1st: The People's Republic of China Photo Exhibition held in Khartoum, the capital of Sudan, closed on the evening of December 31st.

During the twelve-day exhibition, a total of 14,000 people visited, with two days specifically reserved for Sudanese women.

Many visitors wrote down their impressions in the guestbook. They all believed that the exhibition helped them better understand the new life of the Chinese people and unanimously praised the great achievements made by the Chinese people in the past decade. Many also expressed the hope that more such exhibitions would be held to further understand China. All major newspapers in Khartoum published comments on the exhibition. "Al-Rai al-Aam" praised the exhibition for successfully enabling the Sudanese people to understand the beautiful life of the Chinese people.

### 我图片展览会在苏丹首都闭幕

1960-01-03
第5版()
专栏：

    我图片展览会在苏丹首都闭幕
    据新华社1日讯　喀土穆消息：在苏丹首都喀土穆举行的中华人民共和国图片展览会在12月31日晚结束。
    在为时十二天的展出期间，共有一万四千人参观了展览会，其中两天专门供苏丹妇女参观。
    许多参观者在留言簿上写下了他们的观感。他们都认为，这次展览会有助于他们更好地了解中国人民的新生活，并一致赞扬中国人民在过去十年中所取得的伟大成就。有许多人还表示希望举行更多这样的展览会，以便进一步了解中国。喀土穆的所有重要报纸都就这次展览会发表评论。“舆论报”称赞说，展览会成功地使苏丹人民了解中国人民的美好生活。
