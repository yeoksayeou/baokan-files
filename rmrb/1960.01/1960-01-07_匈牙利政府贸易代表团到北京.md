Title: Hungarian Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-01-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. A Hungarian government trade delegation of eight people, led by Mr. Karádi Gyula, First Deputy Minister of the Hungarian Ministry of Foreign Trade, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. Those who welcomed them at the airport included Lin Haiyun, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, and Jia Shi, Director of the Second Bureau of our Ministry of Foreign Trade. Mr. Szigeti, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Hungarian Embassy in China, and Mr. Véghási, Commercial Counselor, also greeted them at the airport.

### 匈牙利政府贸易代表团到北京

1960-01-07
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利政府贸易代表团到北京
    新华社6日讯　匈牙利政府贸易代表团一行八人，在团长、匈牙利对外贸易部第一副部长卡拉迪·久洛率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有我对外贸易部副部长林海云、第二局局长贾石等。匈牙利驻华大使馆临时代办西格蒂和商务参赞韦哈西也到机场迎接。
