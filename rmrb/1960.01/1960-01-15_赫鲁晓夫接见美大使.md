Title: Khrushchev Receives U.S. Ambassador
Author:
Date: 1960-01-15
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 13th: TASS Moscow, January 13th -- Soviet Premier Khrushchev, at the request of U.S. Ambassador to the Soviet Union Thompson, received him on the 13th and held talks with him.

Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Kuznetsov was present.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见美大使

1960-01-15
第6版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见美大使
    新华社14日讯　塔斯社莫斯科13日讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫应美国驻苏联大使汤普森的请求，在13日接见了他，并且同他谈了话。
    在座的有苏联外交部副部长库兹涅佐夫。
