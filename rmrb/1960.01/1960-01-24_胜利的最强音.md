Title: The Strongest Voice of Victory
Author: Li Xue'ao
Date: 1960-01-24
Page: 8

    The Strongest Voice of Victory
    Beijing State-owned 541 Factory, Li Xue'ao
    What is called "the most impoverished"?
    What is called "the most bleak"?
    Western gentlemen,
    Please take a close look—
    These shining figures,
    These powerful answers!
    Please take a close look:
    Our steel,
    Our coal,
    Our lathes,
    Our electricity,
    Our railways,
    Our ships,...
    At what speed,
    Are racing forward—
    A five-year journey,
    Completed in two years!
    Please take a close look:
    Our grain,
    Our cotton,
    Our oil crops,
    Our tobacco,...
    Under the red sun of the People's Communes,
    How to drive away the great natural disasters
    How to seize great harvests—
    Every village has overflowing granaries!
    Every township has full granaries!
    Please take a close look:
    Our fields,
    Our workshops,
    Our schools,
    Our shops,
    Our military camps,
    Our government offices,
    Who doesn't cheer for our victory?
    Who isn't singing the general line—
    Our lighthouse in navigation!
    Our poem in the heart!
    Western gentlemen,
    Please take a close look,
    Please calculate carefully,
    What is called "the most impoverished"?
    What is called "the most bleak"?
    Our communiqué,
    Is the best answer—
    Each string of shining characters,
    Is the strongest voice of victory!
    Each string of shining characters,
    Is a shell to strike back at you!

    胜利的最强音
    北京国营五四一厂　李学鳌

    什么叫“最暗淡”！？

    我们的钢铁，
    我们的煤炭，
    我们的车床，
    我们的电，

    正以怎样快的速度，
    飞驰向前——
    五年征途，
    两年走完！
    请你们仔细看看：
    我们的粮，
    我们的棉，
    我们的油料，
    我们的烟叶，……
    在人民公社的红日下，
    怎样赶走大天灾
    怎样夺来大丰年——
    村村小囤流！
    乡乡大囤满！
    请你们仔细看看：
    我们的田野，
    我们的车间，
    我们的学校，
