Title: Congratulations on the Independence Day of the Republic of Sudan, Chairman Liu Shaoqi Sends a Telegram to Abboud
Author:
Date: 1960-01-01
Page: 4

    Congratulations on the Independence Day of the Republic of Sudan
    Chairman Liu Shaoqi Sends a Telegram to Abboud
    Xinhua News Agency, December 31st – Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, today sent a telegram to General Ibrahim Abboud, Chairman of the Supreme Council of the Armed Forces of the Republic of Sudan, to congratulate him on the Independence Day of the Republic of Sudan. The full text of the telegram is as follows:
    To His Excellency General Ibrahim Abboud, Chairman of the Supreme Council of the Armed Forces of the Republic of Sudan:
    On the occasion of the Independence Day of the Republic of Sudan, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of Sudan on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish the Sudanese nation prosperity and its people happiness. May the friendly relations between China and Sudan develop day by day.
    Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
    December 31, 1959, Beijing

### 祝贺苏丹共和国独立纪念日  刘少奇主席致电阿布德

1960-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

    祝贺苏丹共和国独立纪念日
    刘少奇主席致电阿布德
    新华社31日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇今天打电报给苏丹共和国武装部队最高委员会主席易卜拉欣·阿布德，祝贺苏丹共和国独立纪念日。电报全文如下：苏丹共和国武装部队最高委员会主席易卜拉欣·阿布德将军阁下：
    在苏丹共和国独立纪念日的时候，我谨代表中国人民，并且以我个人的名义向阁下和苏丹人民致以热诚的祝贺。祝苏丹国家繁荣、人民幸福。祝中国和苏丹之间的友好关系日益发展。
    中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
    1959年12月31日于北京
