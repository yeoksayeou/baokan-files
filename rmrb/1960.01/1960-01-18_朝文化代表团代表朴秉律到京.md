Title: Representative of Korean Cultural Delegation, Park Byung-ryul, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-01-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 17th: Park Byung-ryul, the representative of the Korean Cultural Delegation and Director of the Second Department of the Korean Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to discuss the 1960 implementation plan of the Sino-Korean Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

Greeting him at the station were Luo Jun, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and others.

### 朝文化代表团代表朴秉律到京

1960-01-18
第4版()
专栏：

    朝文化代表团代表朴秉律到京
    新华社17日讯　应我对外文化联络委员会邀请前来我国商订中朝文化合作协定1960年执行计划的朝鲜文化代表团代表、朝鲜对外文化联络委员会第二部部长朴秉律，今天下午乘火车到达北京。
    到车站欢迎的有我对外文化联络委员会副主任罗俊等。
