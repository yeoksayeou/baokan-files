Title: Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun Receives French Journalist Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-01-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 8th. Vice Premier of the State Council Xi Zhongxun received the members of the French local newspaper journalist delegation this morning: Marcel Rivière, Editor-in-Chief of the Lyon "Le Progrès," Marcel Gallasso, Editor-in-Chief of the "La Marseillaise," and André Fatral, Editor-in-Chief of the "Le Havre Libre."

Present at the reception were Wang Yi, Secretary-General of the All-China Journalists Association, and Xu Huang, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 习仲勋副总理接见法国记者代表团

1960-01-09
第4版()
专栏：

    习仲勋副总理接见法国记者代表团
    新华社8日讯　国务院副总理习仲勋今天上午接见了法国地方报纸记者代表团成员：里昂“进步报”总编辑马赛尔·里维埃，“马赛曲报”总编辑马赛尔·加拉索，“哈佛尔自由报”总编辑安德列·法特拉。
    接见时在座的，有中华全国新闻工作者协会秘书长王揖，外交部新闻司副司长徐晃。
