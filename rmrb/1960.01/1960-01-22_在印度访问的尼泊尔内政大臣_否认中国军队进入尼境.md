Title: Nepalese Home Minister Visiting India Denies Chinese Troops Entered Nepalese Territory
Author:
Date: 1960-01-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 21st: According to a report from Bareilly by the Indian Press Trust on the 20th, Nepalese Home Minister Uprety, currently visiting India with Nepalese Prime Minister Koirala, said that Chinese troops have not advanced to the Nepalese border, and they have not crossed into Nepal at any known location.

Uprety denied the fabrication by Bharat Shamsher, leader of the Nepalese Gorkha Party, regarding Chinese "aggression" against Nepal, saying that Bharat Shamsher "has his own interpretation of the situation."

### 在印度访问的尼泊尔内政大臣  否认中国军队进入尼境

1960-01-22
第6版()
专栏：

    在印度访问的尼泊尔内政大臣
    否认中国军队进入尼境
    新华社新德里21日电　据印度报业托辣斯20日自巴累利报道，目前同尼泊尔首相柯伊拉腊一起在印度访问的尼泊尔内政大臣乌帕德亚亚说，中国军队没有开到尼泊尔边境，他们也没有在任何已知的地点跨进尼泊尔。
    乌帕德亚亚否认尼泊尔廓尔喀党领袖巴拉特·沙姆谢尔所谓的中国人对尼泊尔进行了“侵略”的捏造，他说，巴拉特·沙姆谢尔“有他自己的对局势的解释”。
