Title: Laying Rails
Author: 
Date: 1960-01-08
Page: 2

Laying rails
Thousands of rails
laid into two lines
leading to mines and factories
leading to cities and villages
leading to forests and plains
leading to the distant frontier
Wherever the motherland needs us
there our flag will fly
