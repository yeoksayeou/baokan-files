Title: Seize the Pre-Spring, Welcome Spring Plowing (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1960-01-24
Page: 3

Spring plowing is approaching. People's communes in various regions are cleverly arranging their labor force, simultaneously focusing on basic farmland construction and field management of overwintering crops, while also actively preparing for spring sowing, striving to deliver early good news for spring production and lay a solid foundation for a bumper harvest this year.

    Sichuan Xindu County's Gaodi People's Commune meticulously manages rapeseed, striving for a good harvest of minor spring crops. The picture shows commune members transporting and applying fertilizer.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Sun Zhongjing

    Members of the Hongxing People's Commune on the outskirts of Beijing are hurrying to repair five-furrow plows in preparation for spring plowing.
                    Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian

    Members of the Tianhu Brigade of the Qixing People's Commune in Lianyuan County, Hunan, are applying pig manure to low-yield fields to increase soil fertility, striving to transform low-yield fields into high-yield ones.
                Tang Youting (Xinhua News Agency draft)

    Members of the Beiquan Brigade of the Yanghe People's Commune in Yongning County, Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region, are carefully selecting fine seeds for spring sowing.
            Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Wenyu

    Lin Qiang, a model animal breeder of the Zhaoling People's Commune in Yancheng County, Henan, feeds his oxen until they are strong and sturdy, ready for the upcoming spring plowing.
                Xinhua News Agency reporter Tang Maolin
