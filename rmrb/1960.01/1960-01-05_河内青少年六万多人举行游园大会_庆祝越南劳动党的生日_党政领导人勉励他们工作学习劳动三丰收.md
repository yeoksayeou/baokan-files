Title: Over 60,000 Hanoi Youths Hold Garden Party to Celebrate the Birthday of the Vietnamese Labor Party; Party and Government Leaders Encourage Them to Achieve Triple Success in Work, Study, and Labor
Author:
Date: 1960-01-05
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 3rd -- Over 60,000 youths and children from all walks of life in Hanoi, the capital of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, held a grand garden party in Bach Thao Park today, warmly celebrating the upcoming 30th anniversary of the founding of the Vietnamese Labor Party.
　　Prime Minister Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister Truong Chinh, Politburo members of the Labor Party Central Committee Le Duan and Nguyen Chi Thanh, and Secretary of the Hanoi Municipal Committee of the Labor Party, Tran Danh Tuyen, attended today's meeting.
　　Nguyen Chi Thanh, on behalf of the Labor Party Central Committee, spoke to the youths and children, praising the tremendous achievements they had made in the past year, and encouraging them to achieve even greater victories in work, study, and labor in the new year.
　　Pham Van Dong, Truong Chinh, Le Duan, Nguyen Chi Thanh and others toured the park, viewing various performances by the youths and children. Wherever they went, cheers resounded.
　　Today, Hanoi was bathed in brilliant sunshine, warm as spring, and the beautiful Bach Thao Park was lush with green grass and fluttering flags. Enthusiastic youths and children danced lion dances, tossed iron rings, walked tightropes, and engaged in various entertainment activities rich in national characteristics on the lawn.
　　Experts from fraternal countries who are helping Vietnam with construction were also invited to participate in the conference today.

### 河内青少年六万多人举行游园大会  庆祝越南劳动党的生日  党政领导人勉励他们工作学习劳动三丰收

1960-01-05
第6版()
专栏：

    河内青少年六万多人举行游园大会
    庆祝越南劳动党的生日
    党政领导人勉励他们工作学习劳动三丰收
    新华社河内3日电　越南民主共和国首都河内各界青年、少年儿童六万多人今天在百草公园举行盛况空前的游园大会，热烈庆祝即将到来的越南劳动党建党三十周年。
    范文同总理、长征副总理、劳动党中央政治局委员黎笋、阮志清，劳动党河内市委书记陈名宣等参加了今天的大会。
    阮志清代表劳动党中央向青年和少年儿童们讲话时，表扬了他们在过去一年里取得的巨大成绩，并且勉励他们在新的一年中在工作、学习、劳动各方面取得更大的胜利。
    范文同、长征、黎笋、阮志清等绕园一周观看了青年和少年儿童们的各种表演。他们走到那里，那里就响起欢呼声。
    今天，河内阳光灿烂，温暖如春，美丽的百草公园绿草如茵，旗帜招展。热情洋溢的青年和少年儿童们在草坪上尽情地舞着狮子、套铁圈、走悬木，进行着各种富有民族特色的娱乐活动。
    帮助越南建设的各兄弟国家专家今天也应邀参加了大会。
