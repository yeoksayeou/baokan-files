Title: The Hungarian Party and Government Present China with an X-ray Machine Vehicle
Author:
Date: 1960-01-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The X-ray machine vehicle, presented to the Chinese government by the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and the Hungarian Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government during the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of our country, has recently been handed over to relevant units by the Ministry of Health for use.

This X-ray machine vehicle is equipped with an X-ray machine, an indirect camera set capable of continuously photographing one hundred and eighty images per hour, a film developing room, a water storage tank, fuel heating, a generator, and other equipment, and can be used in rural areas or places lacking electricity.

### 匈牙利党和政府赠送我国一辆X光机汽车

1960-01-11
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利党和政府赠送我国一辆X光机汽车
    新华社9日讯　匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会和匈牙利工农革命政府在我国庆祝建国十周年时赠送给我国政府的X光机汽车一辆，最近由卫生部交有关单位使用。
    这一辆X光机汽车中装有X光机一台，间接摄影机一套，每小时能连续摄影一百八十张，并有洗片室、贮水罐、燃油暖气、发电机等设备，可以在乡村或缺乏电源的地方使用。
