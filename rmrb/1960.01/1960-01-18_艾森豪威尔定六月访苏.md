Title: Eisenhower Sets June Visit to Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1960-01-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 17th: TASS Moscow, January 17th: As previously announced, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, invited U.S. President Eisenhower to pay an official visit to the Soviet Union at his convenience. President Eisenhower accepted Khrushchev's invitation with satisfaction.

As a result of the subsequent direct exchange of letters between Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, and President Eisenhower, it was unanimously agreed that the U.S. President will visit the Soviet Union from June 10th to 19th, 1960.

### 艾森豪威尔定六月访苏

1960-01-18
第5版()
专栏：

    艾森豪威尔定六月访苏
    新华社17日讯　塔斯社莫斯科17日讯：前已公布，苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫曾经邀请美国总统艾森豪威尔在对他方便的时候到苏联作正式访问。艾森豪威尔总统满意地接受了赫鲁晓夫的邀请。
    由于苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫和艾森豪威尔总统随后直接交换信件的结果，一致同意，美国总统将在1960年6月10日到19日访问苏联。
