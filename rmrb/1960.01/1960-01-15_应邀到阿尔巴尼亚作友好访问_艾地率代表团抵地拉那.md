Title: Invited to Albania for a Friendly Visit, Aidit Leads Delegation to Tirana
Author:
Date: 1960-01-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, January 12th. A delegation of the Communist Party of Indonesia, led by its chairman, Aidit, arrived in Tirana today. The delegation is visiting Albania at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party.
　　
The delegation was welcomed at the airport by First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Hoxha, and other leaders.

### 应邀到阿尔巴尼亚作友好访问  艾地率代表团抵地拉那

1960-01-15
第6版()
专栏：

    应邀到阿尔巴尼亚作友好访问
    艾地率代表团抵地拉那
    新华社地拉那12日电　由印度尼西亚共产党主席艾地率领的印度尼西亚共产党代表团今天到达地拉那。代表团是应阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会的邀请到阿尔巴尼亚进行友好访问的。
    代表团在机场受到阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查和其他领导人的欢迎。
