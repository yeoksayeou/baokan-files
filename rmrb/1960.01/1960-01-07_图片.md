    The scientific research institute of the Sino-Soviet Friendship People's Commune in Jiangbei District, Chongqing, closely integrates with production. Based on summarizing the high-yield experience of the masses, it has conducted research on more than 100 projects in the past year or so. The picture shows scientific research personnel observing and recording the growth of Shandong parsley.
　  Xinhua News Agency reporter Jin Xuqi
