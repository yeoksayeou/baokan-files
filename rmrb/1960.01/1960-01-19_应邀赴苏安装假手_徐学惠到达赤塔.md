Title: Xu Xuehui Arrives in Chita to Receive Prosthetic Arm Installation in Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1960-01-19
Page: 6

    Xu Xuehui Arrives in Chita to Receive Prosthetic Arm Installation in Soviet Union
    Xinhua News Agency, Chita, 16th: According to TASS, Xu Xuehui, who bravely fought against the Kuomintang armed bandits on the Yunnan border to protect national property, arrived in Chita on the 15th at the invitation of Lomanov, the director of the Chita Orthopedic Equipment Factory in the Soviet Union. Upon arrival in Chita, Xu Xuehui was welcomed by Lomanov and his family. Xu Xuehui will have a prosthetic arm installed in Chita and will stay for nearly two weeks.

### 应邀赴苏安装假手  徐学惠到达赤塔

1960-01-19
第6版()
专栏：

    应邀赴苏安装假手
    徐学惠到达赤塔
    据新华社16日讯　据塔斯社赤塔讯：在云南边境英勇地同国民党武装残匪搏斗、保卫国家财产的徐学惠，应苏联赤塔整形器材厂厂长洛玛诺夫的邀请，已于15日抵达赤塔。到达赤塔时，徐学惠受到了洛玛诺夫和他的家里人的欢迎。徐学惠将在赤塔安装假手，逗留将近两个星期。
