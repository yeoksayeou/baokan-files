Title: Japanese Women's Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-01-28
Page: 4
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, January 27th. The Japanese Women's Delegation, consisting of four members led by Ms. Takegami Masako, concluded their friendly visit to China at the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation. They arrived in Guangzhou from Shanghai and Beijing and returned home via Shenzhen this morning.

　　During their stay in Guangzhou, the delegates participated in rallies held by people from all walks of life in Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City against the Japan-U.S. military alliance. Accompanied by responsible persons from the Guangzhou Women's Federation, they visited the Guangzhou Uprising Martyrs' Cemetery, the site of the Peasant Movement Training Institute founded by Comrade Mao Zedong, and Huanghuagang, among other places. They also visited the Huangpu People's Commune in the suburbs of Guangzhou. During their visits, the delegates expressed their admiration for the great enthusiasm of women in agricultural production and hoped that Chinese women would create even greater achievements in the continued Great Leap Forward.

### 日本妇女代表团回国

1960-01-28
第4版()
专栏：

    日本妇女代表团回国
    新华社广州27日电　应全国妇女联合会邀请前来我国进行友好访问的日本妇女代表团一行四人，在竹上正子团长的率领下，结束了在我国各地的访问，先后从上海、北京到达广州，并于今天上午经深圳回国。
    在广州期间，代表们曾参加了广东省、广州市各界人民反对日美军事同盟的集会。并在广州市妇联负责人的陪同下参观了广州公社起义烈士陵园、毛泽东同志主办的农民运动讲习所旧址和黄花岗等地，并访问了广州市郊区黄埔人民公社。在参观访问中，代表们对广大妇女在农业生产中的冲天干劲表示敬佩，并希望中国妇女在继续大跃进中创造更大的成就。
