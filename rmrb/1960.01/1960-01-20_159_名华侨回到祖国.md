Title: 159 Overseas Chinese Return to the Motherland
Author:
Date: 1960-01-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, January 19th: One hundred and fifty-nine overseas Chinese returned to Shenzhen on the 18th, amidst the warm reception of people from all walks of life in the motherland. Situ Zan, chairman of the Jakarta Chinese Overseas Chinese Association, who was forced to leave Indonesia, also returned with them.

　　Among this group of returned overseas Chinese, apart from thirty-eight from Singapore and Malaya, the rest were from Indonesia. Many of them had worked diligently in Indonesia for their entire lives, and now they are returning with only simple luggage.

### 159名华侨回到祖国

1960-01-20
第4版()
专栏：

    159名华侨回到祖国
    据新华社广州19日电　一百五十九名华侨，18日在祖国各界人民的热情接待下，回到了深圳。被迫离开印度尼西亚的雅加达中华侨团总会主席司徒赞也一道回来。
    在这批归侨中，除三十八名来自新加坡、马来亚外，其他都是从印度尼西亚回来的。其中有不少人在印度尼西亚辛勤劳动了一生，现在只带着简单的行囊回来。
