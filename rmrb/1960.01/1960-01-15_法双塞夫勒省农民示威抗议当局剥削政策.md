Title: French Deux-Sèvres Farmers Demonstrate Against Exploitative Policies of Authorities
Author:
Date: 1960-01-15
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 12th—Yesterday, 30,000 farmers in Niort, the capital of the French department of Deux-Sèvres, marched and demonstrated, demanding the restoration of the original price index for agricultural products, reduction of taxes, and an end to excessive intermediary exploitation.
    The demonstrating farmers eventually went to the provincial government and handed over a resolution containing the above demands to the government authorities.

### 法双塞夫勒省农民示威抗议当局剥削政策

1960-01-15
第5版()
专栏：

    法双塞夫勒省农民
    示威抗议当局剥削政策
    新华社巴黎12日电　法国双塞夫勒省首府尼奥尔昨天有三万农民游行示威，要求恢复农产品原来的价格指数，减少捐税和过度的中间剥削。
    示威的农民最后前往省政府，把一项包含上述要求的决议交给了政府当局。
