Title: U.S. Warship Intrudes into Fujian Territorial Waters; We Issue 83rd Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-01-18
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, January 17th: One U.S. warship intruded into the sea area of Haitan and Dongyin areas of Fujian Province from 07:40 to 15:10 on January 17th. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. warship, a spokesman of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 83rd serious warning.

### 美国军舰侵入我福建领海  我提出第83次严重警告

1960-01-18
第4版()
专栏：

    美国军舰侵入我福建领海
    我提出第83次严重警告
    新华社17日讯　1月17日七时四十分至十五时十分，美国军舰一艘侵入我福建省海坛、东引地区海域。对于美国军舰这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第八十三次严重警告。
