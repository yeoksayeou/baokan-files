Title: South Vietnamese Puppet Ngo Dinh Diem Arrives in Taiwan, Hustling to Assemble Military Bloc for the United States
Author: 
Date: 1960-01-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Taipei, 15th - Ngo Dinh Diem, the puppet "president" of South Vietnam fostered by the United States, arrived in Taipei today aboard a U.S. Air Force aircraft.

According to Taipei newspapers and Western news agencies today, under U.S. direction, Ngo Dinh Diem will be in Taiwan for four days, during which he will discuss the formation of a military alliance between the Chiang Kai-shek, Ngo Dinh Diem, and Syngman Rhee reactionary groups with the Chiang Kai-shek clique.

It is noted that Ngo Dinh Diem's "visit" to Taipei is taking place precisely at a time when the United States is reviving Japanese militarism by signing a new U.S.-Japan "Security Treaty" with the Nobusuke Kishi government and is stepping up its efforts to assemble a Northeast Asian aggressive bloc to provoke the Soviet Union and China.

### 南越傀儡吴庭艳到台湾  为美国拼凑军事集团奔波

1960-01-19
第5版()
专栏：

    南越傀儡吴庭艳到台湾
    为美国拼凑军事集团奔波
    新华社15日讯台北消息：美国扶植的南越傀儡“总统”吴庭艳今天坐一架美国空军飞机到达台北。
    据台北报纸和西方通讯社今天透露，在美国导演下，吴庭艳在台湾停留的四天中，将同蒋介石集团商谈缔结蒋介石、吴庭艳和李承晚三个反动集团的军事同盟问题。
    人们注意到，吴庭艳到台北“访问”，正是在这种时候进行的，即美国正通过同岸信介政府签订新的日美“安全条约”而使日本军国主义复活，并加紧拼凑向苏联和中国挑衅的东北亚侵略集团。
