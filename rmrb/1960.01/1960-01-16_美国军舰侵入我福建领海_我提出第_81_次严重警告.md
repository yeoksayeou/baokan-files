Title: U.S. Warship Intrudes into Fujian Territorial Waters; Our Country Issues the 81st Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-01-16
Page: 4

    U.S. Warship Intrudes into Fujian Territorial Waters
    Our Country Issues the 81st Serious Warning
    Xinhua News Agency, January 15th: From 3:00 AM to 12:20 PM on January 15th, a U.S. warship intruded into the waters of the Haitan and Dongyin areas of Fujian Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. warship, a spokesman for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the eighty-first serious warning.

### 美国军舰侵入我福建领海  我提出第81次严重警告

1960-01-16
第4版()
专栏：

    美国军舰侵入我福建领海
    我提出第81次严重警告
    新华社15日讯　1月15日三时至十二时二十分，美国军舰一艘侵入我福建省海坛、东引地区海域。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第八十一次严重警告。
