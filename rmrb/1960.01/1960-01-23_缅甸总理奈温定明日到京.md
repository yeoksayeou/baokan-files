Title: Burmese Prime Minister Ne Win to Arrive in Beijing Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1960-01-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, January 22nd: At the invitation of Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, General Ne Win, Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, will arrive in Beijing on January 24th for a friendly visit.

### 缅甸总理奈温定明日到京

1960-01-23
第3版()
专栏：

    缅甸总理奈温定明日到京
    新华社22日讯　缅甸联邦总理奈温将军应中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来的邀请，将于1月24日到北京进行友好访问。
