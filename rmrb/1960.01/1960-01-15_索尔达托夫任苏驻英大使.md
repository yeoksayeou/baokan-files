Title: Soldatov Appointed Soviet Ambassador to Britain
Author: 
Date: 1960-01-15
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 14th: TASS Moscow, January 13th: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union has appointed Alexander Soldatov as the Soviet Ambassador to Great Britain. Prior to accepting his new appointment, Soldatov served as the Director of the American Countries Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
    In connection with the assignment of Ya. Malik to another post, he was relieved of his duties as Soviet Ambassador to Great Britain.

### 索尔达托夫任苏驻英大使

1960-01-15
第6版()
专栏：

    索尔达托夫任苏驻英大使
    新华社莫斯科14日电　塔斯社莫斯科13日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团任命亚历山大·索尔达托夫为苏联驻英国大使。索尔达托夫在接受新的任命以前，曾任苏联外交部美洲司司长。
    由于雅·马立克另有任用，解除了他的苏联驻英国大使的职务。
