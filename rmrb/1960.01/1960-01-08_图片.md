Title: Picture
Author: Zhang Ruihua
Date: 1960-01-08
Page: 2

    The iron produced by small blast furnaces in Meng County is rolled into rails to build a local railway. This is a local train transporting coke for the Meng County Iron Plant. This small train can transport more than 100 tons of coke a day, equivalent to the transportation volume of more than 1,000 iron-wheeled vehicles. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Ruihua
