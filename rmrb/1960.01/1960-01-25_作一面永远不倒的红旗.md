Title: Forge a Red Flag That Never Falls
Author: Yinchuan Woolen Mill All Employees
Date: 1960-01-25
Page: 2

    Forge a Red Flag That Never Falls
    Yinchuan Woolen Mill All Employees
    Upon hearing the news that the main targets of the Second Five-Year Plan had been completed three years ahead of schedule, we all jumped up with joy. We excitedly shouted: Long live the General Line! Long live the People's Commune! Long live the Great Leap Forward!
    During the Second Five-Year Plan period, our factory also developed at a high speed. Although the factory was small, the machines were old, the people were young, and the technology was poor, under the leadership of the Party, everyone displayed soaring enthusiasm and finally created miracles with the old machines. Now, we can produce more than 30 kinds of products and more than 100 kinds of patterns. Among them, four major products, such as jacquard blankets, were rated as excellent products at the national professional conference. Jacquard carpets and Tao carpets have been selling well abroad. The production situation in January this year is also very good.
    We are determined to, under the slogan of "increasing production, increasing tasks without increasing personnel," increase the output value of 1960 by more than 30% on the basis of last year, the product quality should exceed the national advanced indicators, the cost should be reduced by about 80% on the basis of last year, and 15 kinds of new products should be trial-produced, and 38 kinds of patterns should be added. We must become a red flag of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region that never falls.

### 作一面永远不倒的红旗
银川毛纺厂全体职工
1960-01-25
第2版()
专栏：来信

    作一面永远不倒的红旗
    银川毛纺厂全体职工
    听到第二个五年计划主要指标提前三年完成的消息，我们都高兴得跳了起来。大家激动地高呼：总路线万岁！人民公社万岁！大跃进万岁！
    在第二个五年计划期间，我们厂也在高速度发展。虽然厂小、机器老、人年轻、技术差，可是在党的领导下，大家发挥了冲天的干劲，终于在老机器上创造出了奇迹。现在，我们已能生产三十多种产品，一百多种花色。其中提花毛毯等四种主要产品，在全国专业会议上被评为优秀产品。提花毯、道毯已畅销国外。今年，1月份的生产情况也很好。
    我们决心在“增产、增事不增人”的口号下，使1960年的产值在去年的基础上再增长30％以上，产品质量要超过全国先进指标，成本要在去年的基础上降低80％左右，试制新产品十五种，增加花色三十八种。我们一定要成为宁夏回族自治区的一面永远不倒的红旗。
