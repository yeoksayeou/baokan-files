Title: Premier Zhou Hosted a Banquet in Honor of the Panchen Lama and Pagbalha; Before the Banquet, He Met with the Panchen Lama and Had Cordial Talks with Him
Author:
Date: 1960-01-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, January 25th. Premier Zhou Enlai hosted a banquet at the National Culture Palace this evening in honor of Panchen Erdeni Qoigyijabu, Acting Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, and Pagbalha Geleg Namgyal, Vice Chairman of the Preparatory Committee.

    Before the banquet, Premier Zhou Enlai met with Panchen Erdeni and had cordial talks with him.

    Also invited to the banquet were Lhamin Yeshe Tsultrim, Ngawang Jinba, and Tanggyal Yarphel, Standing Committee Members of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region; Ngabo Ngawang Jigme and Dorje Pamo, Vice Chairmen of the Tibet Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; and Ngagqu Losang Chomphel, Vice President of the Tibet Branch of the Buddhist Association of China, among others.

    Xi Zhongxun, Vice Premier of the State Council; Li Weihan and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Zhang Zhizhong, Standing Committee Member; Burhan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Ping Jiesan, Deputy Director of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee; Wang Feng, Liu Chun, Sa Kongliao, Yang Jingren, Xie Hechou, and Yu Xinqing, Deputy Directors of the State Nationalities Affairs Commission; Zhou Renshan, Deputy Secretary of the CPC Tibet Working Committee; Xiraojiatso, President of the Buddhist Association of China; responsible persons of relevant central departments; and responsible persons of various people's organizations all attended the banquet.

    Xinhua News Agency, January 25th. Panchen Erdeni Qoigyijabu, Acting Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, and his entourage, accompanied by Wang Feng, Deputy Director of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee and Deputy Director of the State Nationalities Affairs Commission, and Zhou Renshan, Deputy Secretary of the CPC Tibet Working Committee, returned to Beijing today by special train after visiting various places.

    Burhan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, along with Pagbalha Geleg Namgyal, Vice Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, who had returned to Beijing the day before, were at the station to greet them.

    Also present at the station to welcome them were Liu Chun, Sa Kongliao, Yang Jingren, Xie Hechou, and Yu Xinqing, Deputy Directors of the State Nationalities Affairs Commission, among others.

### 周总理设宴招待班禅和帕巴拉  宴会前接见班禅，同他进行了亲切谈话

1960-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理设宴招待班禅和帕巴拉
    宴会前接见班禅，同他进行了亲切谈话
    新华社25日讯　周恩来总理今天晚间在民族文化宫设宴，招待西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞和副主任委员帕巴拉·卓列朗杰。
    在宴会以前，周恩来总理接见了班禅额尔德尼，和他进行了亲切的谈话。
    应邀出席宴会的，还有西藏自治区筹备委员会常务委员拉敏·益喜楚臣、纳旺金巴、唐曲雅培，政协西藏委员会副主席尧西·贡保才旦、多吉帕姆，中国佛教协会西藏分会副会长恩久·洛桑群培等。
    国务院副总理习仲勋，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李维汉、陈叔通，常务委员张治中，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，中共中央统战部副部长平杰三，民族事务委员会副主任汪锋、刘春、萨空了、杨静仁、谢鹤筹、余心清，中共西藏工委副书记周仁山，中国佛教协会会长喜饶嘉错，中央有关部门负责人和各人民团体负责人，都出席了宴会。
    新华社25日讯　西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞等一行，在中共中央统战部副部长、民族事务委员会副主任汪锋和中共西藏工委副书记周仁山陪同下，到各地参观访问以后，今天乘专车回到北京。
    政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏和前天先回到北京的西藏自治区筹备委员会副主任委员帕巴拉·卓列朗杰到车站迎接。
    前往车站迎接的，还有民族事务委员会副主任刘春、萨空了、杨静仁、谢鹤筹、余心清等。
