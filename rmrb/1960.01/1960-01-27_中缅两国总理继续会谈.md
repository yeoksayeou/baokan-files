Title: Chinese and Burmese Premiers Continue Talks
Author:
Date: 1960-01-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 26th: The Premier of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, and the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, General Ne Win, continued their talks on the morning of the 26th.

    The Chinese side participating in the talks included Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Director of the Department of Treaty and Law of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Yao Zhongming, Director of the First Asian Department Zhang Wenjin, Director of the Protocol Department Yu Peiwen, and Secretary of the Premier's Office Pu Shouchang.

    The Burmese side participating in the talks included Burmese Minister of Foreign Affairs U Chan Tun Aung, Burmese Attorney General U Ba Swe, Deputy Chief of Staff Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs U Ba Lwin, Prime Minister's Secretary U Win Pe, Joint Secretary of the Border Areas Administration U Khin Nyu, Director of the Survey Department U Hla Kin Maung, Head of Military Intelligence Lieutenant Colonel Lun, Director of the Asian and African Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs U Ba Chit, Director of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs U Su Htin, and Deputy Director of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs U Tun Tin.

    Li Yimang, China's ambassador to Burma, and Kyao Winn, Burma's ambassador to China, also participated in the talks.

    The talks proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

    Xinhua News Agency, January 26th: The Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, General Ne Win, and his entourage visited the Great Hall of the People and the National Culture Palace this noon. Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Kyao Winn, the Burmese Ambassador to China, accompanied Prime Minister Ne Win on the visit.

    When Prime Minister Ne Win arrived at the National Culture Palace, he was warmly welcomed by Yang Jingren, Vice Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, and other responsible persons. The distinguished guests viewed the exhibition of the achievements of China's nationalities work over the past decade, and also went up to the eleventh floor of the main building of the National Culture Palace, leaning on the railing to overlook the panorama of Beijing.

### 中缅两国总理继续会谈

1960-01-27
第1版()
专栏：

    中缅两国总理继续会谈
    新华社26日讯　中华人民共和国总理周恩来和缅甸联邦总理奈温将军，在26日上午继续举行会谈。
    中国方面参加会谈的，有国务院副总理陈毅，外交部副部长章汉夫，外交部条约法律司司长姚仲明、第一亚洲司司长章文晋、礼宾司司长俞沛文，总理办公室秘书浦寿昌。
    缅甸方面参加会谈的，有缅甸联邦外交部长吴千吞昂，缅甸联邦检察总长吴巴盛，副总参谋长昂季准将，外交部常任秘书巴林顿，总理秘书吴温佩，边境地区管理局联合秘书吴钦纽，测绘局长吴拉钦貌，军事情报处长伦中校，外交部亚非司长吴巴漆，外交部司长吴苏幸，外交部副司长吴吞丁。
    我国驻缅甸大使李一氓和缅甸驻中国大使叫温，也参加了会谈。
    会谈在友好的气氛中进行。
    新华社26日讯　缅甸联邦总理奈温将军和他的随行人员，今天中午参观了人民大会堂和民族文化宫。外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文和缅甸驻中国大使叫温陪同奈温总理参观。
    奈温总理到达民族文化宫的时候，受到民族事务委员会副主任杨静仁等负责人的热烈欢迎。贵宾们在这里观看了我国十年来民族工作成就的展览，还登上民族文化宫主楼的十一层楼上，依栏眺望北京全景。
