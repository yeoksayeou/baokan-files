Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Receives Burmese Prime Minister General Ne Win
Author:
Date: 1960-01-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 28th: Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, received the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, General Ne Win, this afternoon.

Also received were the following members accompanying Prime Minister Ne Win on his visit to our country: Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Union of Burma, U Chan Thun Aung; Attorney General of the Union of Burma, U Ba Swe; Deputy Chief of Staff, Brigadier Aung Gyi; Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, U Ba Lwin; Prime Minister's Secretary, U Win Pe; Joint Secretary of the Border Areas Administration Bureau, U Khin Nyo; Director of the Survey Bureau, U Hla Kin Maung; Head of Military Intelligence, Lieutenant Colonel Lun; Director of the Asian-African Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, U Ba Chi; Director of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, U Su Xin; and Deputy Director of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, U Tun Tin.

Present at the reception were Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Chen Yi and Tan Zhenlin, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Huang Yanpei, Chen Shutong, and Bainqen Erdini Qoigyi Gyaincain, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Geng Biao, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army General Zhang Aiping, Chinese Ambassador to Burma Li Yimang, Director of the First Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wenjin, Director of the Treaty and Law Department Yao Zhongming, Director of the Protocol Department Yu Peiwen, Secretary of the Premier's Office Pu Shouchang, and Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Burma Colonel Jiang Keding. Also present at the reception were Burmese Ambassador to China Kyao Winn and Military Attachés of the Embassy, Lieutenant Colonel Liu Dang and Lieutenant Colonel Jing Shamen. Chairman Liu Shaoqi held friendly talks with the distinguished guests during the reception. (Picture attached)

　　Chairman Liu Shaoqi received the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, General Ne Win, and his entourage on January 28th. The seventh person from the left in the front row is Prime Minister Ne Win. Xinhua News Agency reporters Meng Qingbiao and Zheng Xiaozhen.

### 刘少奇主席接见缅甸总理奈温将军

1960-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席接见缅甸总理奈温将军
    新华社28日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇，今天下午接见了缅甸联邦总理奈温将军阁下。
    同时还接见了随同奈温总理来我国访问的缅甸联邦外交部长吴千吞昂，缅甸联邦检察总长吴巴盛，副总参谋长昂季准将，外交部常任秘书巴林顿，总理秘书吴温佩，边境地区管理局联合秘书吴钦纽，测绘局长吴拉钦貌，军事情报处长伦中校，外交部亚非司长吴巴漆，外交部司长吴苏幸，外交部副司长吴吞丁等。
    接见时在座的，有国务院总理周恩来，副总理陈毅、谭震林，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长黄炎培、陈叔通、班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞，外交部副部长章汉夫、耿飚，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张爱萍上将，中国驻缅甸大使李一氓，外交部第一亚洲司司长章文晋，条约法律司司长姚仲明，礼宾司司长俞沛文，总理办公室秘书浦寿昌，我国驻缅甸大使馆武官蒋克定上校。接见时，缅甸驻中国大使叫温和大使馆武官柳当上校、敬沙门上校也在座。在接见的时候，刘少奇主席同贵宾们进行了友好的交谈。（附图片）
