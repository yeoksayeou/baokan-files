International Briefs

　　Cambodian Civil Servants and Soldiers Participate in Physical Labor
　　Phnom Penh Dispatch: The construction of six dams, four canals, and three highways, in which Cambodian civil servants and soldiers are participating according to this year's physical labor plan, began on the 2nd. These projects are being carried out in eleven provinces, Phnom Penh, and Sihanoukville.

　　Preparatory Work for the First Phase of the Aswan Dam in the UAR Nears Completion
　　Aswan Dispatch: Preparatory work for the official commencement of the first phase of construction of the Aswan High Dam is nearing completion, with the commencement date set for January 9. The construction preparation work is being carried out jointly by UAR experts and a Soviet expert group led by Professor Komzin.

### 国际简讯

1960-01-05
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    柬埔寨公务人员和军人参加体力劳动
    金边讯　柬埔寨公务人员和军人根据今年体力劳动计划而参加修建的六个水坝、四条水渠和三条公路的工程在2日开始。这些工程在十一个省、金边和西哈努克城进行。
    阿联阿斯旺水坝第一期工程准备工作接近完成
    阿斯旺讯　阿斯旺高水坝第一期建设工程正式动工的准备工作已近结束，动工日期定为1月9日。施工准备工作是由阿联专家和以科姆津教授为首的苏联专家组共同进行的。
