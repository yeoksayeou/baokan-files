Title: Secretary of the Romanian Writers Association Secretariat, Mihai Gafița, Returns Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-01-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th - Mihai Gafița, Secretary of the Romanian Writers Association Secretariat, concluded his visit to our country and left Beijing by plane on the 16th to return home.

Gafița arrived in our country on December 14th of last year for a visit, according to the 1959 implementation plan of the Sino-Romanian cultural cooperation agreement, and visited cities such as Beijing, Hefei, Shanghai, Guangzhou, and Wuhan.

### 罗作家协会书记处书记米哈依·加菲查回国

1960-01-19
第4版()
专栏：

    罗作家协会书记处书记米哈依·加菲查回国
    新华社16日讯　罗马尼亚作家协会书记处书记米哈依·加菲查结束了在我国的访问，16日乘飞机离开北京回国。
    加菲查是根据中罗文化合作协定1959年执行计划，在去年12月14日到我国访问的，曾经访问过北京、合肥、上海、广州、武汉等城市。
