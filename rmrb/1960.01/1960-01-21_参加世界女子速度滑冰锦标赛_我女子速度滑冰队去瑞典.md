Title: Our Women's Speed Skating Team to Sweden to Participate in the World Women's Speed Skating Championships
Author:
Date: 1960-01-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 19th: The Chinese Women's Speed Skating Team, a group of ten, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 19th for Sweden to participate in the 1960 World Women's Speed Skating Championships to be held in Östersund from January 30th to 31st.
The five female athletes of the skating team, led by Zhao Xiang, Vice Chairman of the Harbin Branch of the All-China Sports Federation, are: Sun Hongxia, Jin Meiyu, and Liu Fengrong, who recently won the top three places in the women's speed skating all-around at the 1960 National Ice Sports Meet in Jilin, and Yang Yunxiang and Cui Shunzi, who ranked fifth and sixth in the speed skating all-around. The coach of the skating team is Li Changxie.

### 参加世界女子速度滑冰锦标赛  我女子速度滑冰队去瑞典

1960-01-21
第4版()
专栏：

    参加世界女子速度滑冰锦标赛
    我女子速度滑冰队去瑞典
    据新华社19日讯　中国女子速度滑冰队一行十人，19日上午乘飞机离北京前往瑞典，参加1月30日到31日将在厄斯特松德城举行的1960年世界女子速度滑冰锦标赛。
    这个由中华全国体育总会哈尔滨分会副主席赵向率领的滑冰队的五名女选手是：最近在吉林获得1960年全国冰上运动会女子速度滑冰全能前三名的孙洪霞、金美玉和刘凤荣，速滑全能第五、六名的杨云香和崔顺子。滑冰队的教练是李昌燮。
