Title: We Have the Courage to Shoulder the Burden of the Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1966-08-10
Edition: 1

Most beloved Party Central Committee and most beloved Chairman Mao:

The Party Central Committee's decision on the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution shines with the brilliance of Mao Zedong Thought in every word and touches our hearts in every sentence, giving us boundless strength. We, the entire body of revolutionary workers, firmly support it.

We must hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, seriously study, and faithfully carry out the Party Central Committee's decision. With Chairman Mao's wise leadership and the Party backing us, we have the courage to shoulder the burden of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. We dare to fight, dare to venture, and dare to make revolution. Everyone will take up the pen of the proletariat, fight against all those in power within the Party who are taking the capitalist road and all representatives of the bourgeoisie, and sweep away all monsters and demons. We will resolutely carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution through to the end. We will not lay down our arms until we achieve complete victory.

Long live the victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!
Long live the invincible Mao Zedong Thought!
Long live the Communist Party of China!
Long live Chairman Mao!

New Cixu Uygur Autonomous Region Construction Bank All Revolutionary Workers

### 我们有勇气担当起文化大革命的重担

1966-08-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们有勇气担当起文化大革命的重担最敬爱的党中央最敬爱的毛主席：
    党中央关于无产阶级文化大革命的决定，字字都闪烁着毛泽东思想的光辉，句句都谈到我们的心坎里，给我们增添了无限的力量，我们全体革命职工表示坚决的拥护。
    我们一定要高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，认真学习和忠实执行党中央的决定。有毛主席的英明领导，有党给我们撑腰，我们有勇气担当起文化大革命的重担，“敢”字当头，敢斗、敢闯、敢革命。人人拿起无产阶级的笔杆子，把一切党内走资本主义道路的当权派和资产阶级代表人物斗倒，批透，横扫一切牛鬼蛇神，坚决把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底。不获全胜，决不收兵。
    无产阶级文化大革命胜利万岁！
    战无不胜的毛泽东思想万岁！
    中国共产党万岁！
    毛主席万岁！　　　　　　新疆维吾尔自治区建设银行全体革命职工
