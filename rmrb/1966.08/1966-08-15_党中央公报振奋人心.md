Title: The Central Committee's Communiqué Inspires the Heart
Author: Yú Xiǎo
Date: 1966-08-15
Edition: 4

The Central Committee's communiqué inspires the heart,
From the engineering corps of the People's Liberation Army, Yú Xiǎo

The sky's waves broadcast the communiqué,
The Central Committee issues a battle call;
Mountains laugh, waters laugh, people laugh,
The cheers echo like the tide!

Each word in the communiqué shines with gold,
The red flag of Mao Zedong's Thought flies high,
Every sentence resonates in our hearts,
The party's directives are firmly remembered.

The communiqué is like a marching order,
Seven hundred million people's spirits soar,
Daring to do, daring to venture, daring to revolutionize,
In great winds and waves, we show our heroism!

The communiqué is like a battle drum,
Revolutionary thunder shakes the four directions,
Ghosts and snakes are swept away,
We create a new world!

The communiqué is like a thousand-catty club,
Striking the death knell for imperialists, revisionists, and reactionaries,
Whoever dares to touch our iron-clad mountains,
We will completely and thoroughly annihilate them!

The Central Committee's communiqué stirs the heart,
Excited words pour out:
Following Chairman Mao,
We will remain revolutionary for life, unshakable!

### 党中央公报振奋人心
喻晓
1966-08-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　党中央公报振奋人心
　　解放军工程兵某部　喻晓
　　长空电波播公报，
　　党中央发出战斗号召；
　　山笑水笑人欢笑，
　　欢呼的声浪似海潮！
　　公报字字金光闪，
　　毛泽东思想红旗飘，
　　句句说到咱心坎里，
　　党的指示记得牢。
　　公报好似进军号，
　　七亿人民斗志高，
　　敢干敢闯敢革命，
　　大风大浪逞英豪！
　　公报如同战鼓响，
　　革命的风雷震四方，
　　牛鬼蛇神齐清扫，
　　新世界我们来创造！
　　公报就像千钧棒，
　　把帝、修、反的丧钟敲，
　　谁敢碰我们铁打的江山，
　　全部、干净把它消灭掉！
　　党中央公报震人心，
　　激动的话儿涌出口：
　　跟着领袖毛主席，
　　终生革命不动摇！
