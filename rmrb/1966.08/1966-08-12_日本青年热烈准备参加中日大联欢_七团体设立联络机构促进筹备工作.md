Title: Japanese Youth Eagerly Prepare for the Sino-Japanese Grand Festival
Author:
Date: 1966-08-12
Edition: 6

Japanese youth are enthusiastically preparing to participate in the second Sino-Japanese Youth Friendship Grand Festival, which is scheduled to take place in Beijing in early September.

On August 8, six Japanese youth organizations—including the Japan Socialist Youth League, the Japan Youth Federation, the Young Lawyers Association, the Japan-China Friendship Association, the All-Japan Federation of Agricultural Cooperatives, and the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan's Youth Policy Department—convened a meeting to discuss preparations for the festival. The meeting was attended by representatives from various youth organizations invited by three Chinese organizations.

During the meeting, the representatives exchanged information about their respective preparations for the second Sino-Japanese Youth Festival. Deepda Shuozhe, the chairman of the Japan Socialist Youth League, proposed the establishment of a liaison committee for the second Sino-Japanese Youth Friendship Grand Festival to expedite the preparations. The meeting also decided to form a "Liaison Conference for the Second Sino-Japanese Youth Friendship Grand Festival" consisting of the aforementioned six organizations plus the Neutral Trade Union Federation's Youth Policy Department, totaling seven organizations. This conference aims to promote the activities of the Sino-Japanese Youth Friendship Grand Festival.

### 日本青年热烈准备参加中日大联欢　七团体设立联络机构促进筹备工作

1966-08-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　日本青年热烈准备参加中日大联欢
　　七团体设立联络机构促进筹备工作
    新华社东京九日电  日本七个青年团体积极筹备参加将在九月上旬在北京举行的第二届中日青年友好大联欢。
    八日，日本社会主义青年同盟、日本青年团协议会、青年法律家协会、日本中国友好协会、全日本农民协会联合会、工会总评议会青年对策部等六团体召集应中国三团体邀请的各青年团体负责人举行座谈。
    在座谈会上，各团体交流了各自准备参加第二届中日青年大联欢的情况。会上，日本社会主义青年同盟委员长深田肇建议，迅速组成参加第二届日中青年友好大联欢联络委员会，以便大力促进参加大联欢的筹备工作。会上，还通过了由上述团体加上中立工会联合会青年对策部共七个团体组成“参加第二届日中青年友好大联欢联络会议”，推动中日青年友好大联欢活动。
