Title: The Party Central Committee's Decision Most Accurately Expresses Our Aspirations
Author:
Date: 1966-08-11
Edition: 2

Most respected Party Central Committee,
Most respected Chairman Mao:

The Party Central Committee's wise decision on the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution most accurately expresses the aspirations of the people of the entire nation and is a great joyous event for the people of the entire nation. On the morning of the 9th, all revolutionary workers of our factory held a rally. Everyone was spirited and enthusiastically cheered, unanimously expressing their most enthusiastic support and most resolute implementation of this decision.

Currently, the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and the socialist education movement being carried out in our factory are sweeping away all ghosts and monsters with the force of a landslide and the power of thunder. We are determined to raise the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought even higher, to further criticize with practical actions the old ideas, old culture, old customs, and old habits, to overthrow the capitalist-roaders in power within the Party, to criticize the representatives of the bourgeoisie, and to thoroughly carry out the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and the socialist education movement. We will grasp the revolution to promote production, ensuring victory in both revolution and production!

Long live the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!
Long live the great, glorious, and correct Communist Party of China!
Long live the great revolutionary leader Chairman Mao!

Lanzhou Synthetic Rubber Factory All Revolutionary Workers

### 党中央的决定最真切地表达了我们的心愿

1966-08-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　党中央的决定最真切地表达了我们的心愿最敬爱的党中央最敬爱的毛主席：
    党中央关于无产阶级文化大革命的英明决定，最真切地表达了全国人民的心愿，是全国人民的一件大喜事。我厂全体革命职工九日晨举行集会，大家精神振奋，热烈欢呼，一致表示最热烈地拥护和最坚决地贯彻执行这个决定。
    目前，我厂开展的无产阶级文化大革命和社会主义教育运动，正以排山倒海之势、雷霆万钧之力，横扫一切牛鬼蛇神。我们决心更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，进一步以实际行动，彻底批判旧思想、旧文化、旧风俗、旧习惯，斗垮党内走资本主义道路的当权派，批倒资产阶级代表人物，把无产阶级文化大革命和社会主义教育运动搞深搞透搞彻底。抓革命促生产，保证革命和生产建设双胜利！
    伟大的无产阶级文化大革命万岁！
    伟大、光荣、正确的中国共产党万岁！
    伟大的革命领袖毛主席万岁！
              兰州合成橡胶厂全体革命职工
