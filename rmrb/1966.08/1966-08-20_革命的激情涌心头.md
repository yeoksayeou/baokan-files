Title: Revolutionary Passion Surges in Our Hearts
Author: Wáng Shù
Date: 1966-08-20
Edition: 3

Revolutionary passion surges in our hearts.
The city of flowers, the sea of red flags,
Revolutionary teachers and students come from all directions.
The sun shines on Tiananmen Square under a clear blue sky,
Celebrating the new victories of Mao Zedong Thought.
What words can express our feelings?
Our beloved Chairman Mao is with us!
Looking at Chairman Mao, we do not want to leave,
Revolutionary passion surges in our hearts.
"Long live! Long live forever!" we shout,
Firmly following the path guided by Chairman Mao!
Chairman Mao's heart is connected with the people's,
Looking at the world, we do revolution.
Supporting the Party's decision of the Sixteen Points,
We welcome the new high tide of the Cultural Revolution.
The full meeting's communiqué is like timely rain,
Making us sing and dance with joy.
The revolutionary ship sails ten thousand miles,
The great helmsman is Chairman Mao.
Generation after generation listens to Chairman Mao's words,
Fearless of knife mountains and fire seas.
In great winds and waves, we hold high the red flag,
Firmly carrying out the Cultural Revolution to the end!
	China University of Science and Technology Wáng Shù

### 革命的激情涌心头
王术
1966-08-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　革命的激情涌心头
　　鲜花的城，红旗的海，
　　革命师生从八方来。
　　日照天安门晴空万里，
　　庆祝毛泽东思想的新胜利。
　　千言万语说哪一句？
　　敬爱的毛主席和我们在一起！
　　望着毛主席不想走，
　　革命的激情涌心头。
　　“万岁！万万岁！”高声呼，
　　坚决走毛主席指引的路！
　　毛主席和人民心连心，
　　放眼世界干革命。
　　拥护党决定的十六条，
　　迎接文化大革命新高潮。
　　全会公报是及时雨，
　　喜得我们载歌又载舞。
　　革命的大船行万里，
　　伟大的舵手是毛主席。
　　世世代代听毛主席的话，
　　刀山火海全不怕。
　　大风大浪里举红旗，
　　坚决把文化大革命进行到底！
　　    中国科学技术大学　王术
