Title: Overthrow, Smash, and Discredit All Gangsters
Author:
Date: 1966-08-10
Edition: 2

Upon hearing the radio broadcast of the Party Central Committee's decision on the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, our blood boiled with excitement, and the entire Tsinghua Garden was in an uproar. Our hearts flew to the side of Chairman Mao! The Party Central Committee has shown us the path forward for the Cultural Revolution. Before our eyes, there is a vast and bright future.

On our journey forward, we fear neither heaven nor earth. We are not afraid of difficulties and twists and turns, nor are we afraid of the sabotage by class enemies or the storms and waves of class struggle. We dare to rebel against all exploiting classes, to rebel against imperialism, revisionism, all reactionaries, and their running dogs, and to rebel against old ideas, old culture, old customs, and old habits.

Dear Party Central Committee, dear Chairman Mao, we pledge to you that we will resolutely implement the class line, build a genuine revolutionary leftist force, unite with the broad masses of revolutionary teachers, students, and staff, correctly handle contradictions between the enemy and us and those among the people, isolate a handful of those in power within the Party who are taking the capitalist road, and fight them down, smash them, and discredit them. We will not lay down our arms until we have completed the glorious task of "one fight, two critiques, three changes" that you have entrusted to us!

Forever follow Chairman Mao in the storms and waves of class struggle!

		Revolutionary students of the Department of Power Machinery and Agricultural Engineering, Tsinghua University

### 斗倒斗垮斗臭一切黑帮

1966-08-10
第2版()
专栏：

    斗倒斗垮斗臭一切黑帮
    当我们听到电台广播党中央关于无产阶级文化大革命的决定以后，我们周身的热血沸腾起来了，整个清华园沸腾起来了，我们的心飞到了毛主席的身边！党中央给我们指出了文化大革命前进的道路，在我们的眼前，是一片大好形势，一片光明。
    在我们前进的道路上，我们天不怕，地不怕，不怕困难曲折，不怕阶级敌人捣乱，不怕阶级斗争的大风大浪。我们敢于造一切剥削阶级的反，造帝国主义、修正主义、一切反动派和他们的走狗的反，造旧思想、旧文化、旧风俗、旧习惯的反。
    亲爱的党中央，亲爱的毛主席，我们向您保证，我们要坚决贯彻阶级路线，建立真正的革命左派的队伍，团结广大革命师生员工，正确处理敌我矛盾和人民内部矛盾，孤立一小撮党内走资本主义道路的当权派，把他们斗倒、斗垮、斗臭，不完成您交给我们的“一斗、二批、三改”的光荣任务，我们誓不收兵！
    永远紧跟毛主席在阶级斗争的大风大浪中前进！
    清华大学动力农业机械系燃○　革命同学
