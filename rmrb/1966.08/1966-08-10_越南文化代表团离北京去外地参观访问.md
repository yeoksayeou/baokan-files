Title: Vietnamese Cultural Delegation Leaves Beijing for Tours in Other Regions
Author:
Date: 1966-08-10
Edition: 6

A Vietnamese cultural delegation led by Phan Ngoc Quynh, head of the Vietnamese Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, left Beijing today for tours in other regions of China. The delegation was accompanied by Ding Xilin, vice-head of the Chinese Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Zhang Boju, head of the Chinese Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhou Yiping, Sun Xiaocun, and other relevant officials. Le Chong Chi, charge d'affaires of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, and Nguyen Minh Hoang, deputy head of the Vietnamese Liberation Front's permanent delegation in China, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 越南文化代表团离北京去外地参观访问

1966-08-10
第6版()
专栏：

    越南文化代表团离北京去外地参观访问
    新华社九日讯　由越南对外文委主任范玉纯率领的越南文化代表团，在对外文委副主任丁西林陪同下，今天离开北京去外地参观访问。
    到机场送行的，有对外文委主任张奚若，以及周一萍、孙晓村等有关方面负责人。越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办黎终始，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团副团长阮明芳，也前往机场送行。
