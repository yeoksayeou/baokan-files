Title: Chinese Government Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting the Congo (Brazzaville)
Author:
Date: 1966-08-28
Edition: 5

A delegation from the Chinese government, led by Minister of the Interior Zeng Shan, returned to Beijing by plane today after participating in the celebrations for the third anniversary of the August Revolution in the Congo (Brazzaville).

During their stay in the Congo (Brazzaville), the President of the National Assembly of the Congo (Brazzaville), Mouyabi, received the delegation led by Zeng Shan on the 19th.

On the 17th, Zeng Shan paid separate visits to the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ganao, the Minister of the Interior, Umbesa, and the Political Bureau member of the National Revolutionary Movement and the Second Vice-President of the National Assembly, Bukamba Joullian.

### 我政府代表团访刚果（布）后回京

1966-08-28
第5版()
专栏：

    我政府代表团访刚果（布）后回京
    据新华社二十六日讯　由内务部长曾山率领的中国政府代表团，在参加了刚果（布）八月革命三周年的庆祝活动以后，今天乘飞机回到北京。
    在刚果（布）期间，刚果（布）国民议会议长穆亚比十九日接见了中国政府代表团团长曾山。
    十七日，曾山团长分别拜会了刚果（布）外交部长加纳奥，内政部长翁贝萨和全国革命运动政治局委员、国民议会第二副议长布坎布·儒利昂。
