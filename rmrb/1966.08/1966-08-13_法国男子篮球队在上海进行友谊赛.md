Title: French Men's Basketball Team Plays Friendly Matches in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1966-08-13
Edition: 4

Shanghai men's basketball team defeated the French men's basketball team 53-50 in a friendly match on the evening of August 9. The exciting match won enthusiastic applause from more than 5,000 spectators.

The French men's basketball team arrived in Shanghai from Beijing on August 8.

On the evening of August 11, the French men's basketball team played their second friendly match in Shanghai against the Jiangsu men's basketball team at the Jiangwan Gymnasium. The visiting team won with a score of 59-50.

The visiting team left Shanghai for Guangzhou on August 12.

### 法国男子篮球队在上海进行友谊赛

1966-08-13
第4版()
专栏：

    法国男子篮球队在上海进行友谊赛
    据新华社上海电　上海男子篮球队九日晚上迎战法国男子篮球队时，以五十三比五十赢得了胜利。这场精彩的友谊比赛，博得全场五千多观众的热烈掌声。
    法国男子篮球队是八日由北京到达上海的。
    法国男子篮球队十一日晚上在江湾体育馆同江苏省男子篮球队进行访问上海的第二场友谊比赛。结果客队以五十九比五十获胜。
    客队十二日离开上海去广州。
