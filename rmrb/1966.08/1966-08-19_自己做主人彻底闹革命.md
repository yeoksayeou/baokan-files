Title: Be the Master and Carry Out a Thorough Revolution
Author:
Date: 1966-08-19
Edition: 7

We are filled with immense excitement as we stand with Chairman Mao to participate in the mass rally celebrating the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. Our hearts are pounding, and our eyes are shining with tears of excitement. We cheer, we sing, cheering for the great leader Chairman Mao, singing of Mao Zedong Thought shining with boundless brilliance, illuminating the rivers and lakes, pointing the way forward. Chairman Mao! You are our red sun. We love you, admire you, and revere you endlessly. We will forever follow you, read your books, listen to your words, and act according to your instructions, being your good students.

Most beloved leader Chairman Mao, it is you who ignited the blazing flames of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution at Beijing University, and under your personal command, broke through all the obstacles in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution at Beijing University. We must firmly remember your teachings, care about state affairs, be the master, and carry out a thorough revolution, pushing the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution to the end! We resolutely temper ourselves in the great revolutionary storms, learning to swim while swimming.

We know that revolution is not an easy task. In the past, the revolutionary forerunners shed their blood and lives to establish the proletarian state; now, the enemy without guns wants to seize our power through the corruption of old ideologies, coups, and other treacherous methods, aiming to restore capitalism. We will never allow it! We must achieve a complete victory in this Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

Most beloved Chairman Mao, we are the soldiers of the "Anti-Japanese War University" combat team of the Mathematics and Mechanics Department of Beijing University, all sons of the people, raised by the blood and sweat of the working people. We will never forget this. We pledge to you: We can withstand countless tests of struggle and all possible unexpected setbacks. We will carry on and pass down the glorious revolutionary tradition of the Anti-Japanese War University! We dare to smash all old frameworks, break through all old forces, and rebel against all old systems. Under the sunshine of Mao Zedong Thought, we will bravely fight in the torrents of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution! Fight!

Long live the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!

Long live the dictatorship of the proletariat!

Long live the Communist Party of China!

Long live the invincible Mao Zedong Thought!

Long live our great leader Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years! Ten thousand, ten thousand years!

	Beijing University Mathematics and Mechanics Department "Anti-Japanese War University" Combat Team
	All Soldiers

### 自己做主人彻底闹革命

1966-08-19
第7版()
专栏：

　　自己做主人彻底闹革命
    我们怀着万分激动的心情，同毛主席站在一起参加庆祝无产阶级文化大革命群众大会，我们的心在剧烈地跳动，我们的眼睛闪烁着激动的泪花。我们欢呼，我们歌唱，欢呼伟大的领袖毛主席，歌唱毛泽东思想放射着万丈光芒，照亮了五湖四海，指明了航行的方向。毛主席啊！您是我们的红太阳，我们对您无限热爱，无限崇拜，无限敬仰。我们永远跟着您，读您的书，听您的话，照您的指示办事，做您的好学生。
    最最敬爱的领袖毛主席，是您点燃了北大无产阶级文化大革命的熊熊烈火，是在您的亲自指挥下冲破了北大无产阶级文化大革命的重重障碍。我们一定牢牢记住您的教导，关心国家大事，自己做主人，彻底闹革命，把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底！我们坚决在革命的大风浪里锻炼成长，在游泳中学会游泳。
    我们知道，革命是一件不容易的事。过去，革命老前辈，抛头颅，洒鲜血，打下了无产阶级江山；如今，不拿枪的敌人，妄想通过旧的意识形态的腐蚀、搞政变等阴险毒辣的手段，夺取我们的政权，达到他们复辟资本主义的目的。我们坚决不答应！我们一定要夺取这场无产阶级文化大革命的彻底胜利。
    最最敬爱的毛主席，我们是北京大学数学力学系“抗大”战斗队战士，都是人民的儿子，都是劳动人民的血汗养大的，我们决不忘记这一点。我们向您保证：我们经得起千百万次斗争的考验，经得起一切可能的意外的挫折。我们要把抗大的光荣革命传统接过来，传下去！我们敢于打碎一切旧框框，冲破一切旧势力，造一切旧制度的反，在毛泽东思想的阳光照耀下，勇敢地在无产阶级文化大革命的激流中战斗！战斗！
    无产阶级文化大革命万岁！
    无产阶级专政万岁！
    中国共产党万岁！
    战无不胜的毛泽东思想万岁！
    我们伟大领袖毛主席万岁！万万岁！
        北京大学数学力学系“抗大”战斗队
          全体战士
