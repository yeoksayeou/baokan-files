Title: Zambian Trade Delegation Visits Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1966-08-31
Edition: 5

A Zambian government trade delegation led by Vice Minister of Industry and Commerce Chisata departed Beijing by airplane today for a visit to Shanghai.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Jia Shi and others.

### 赞比亚贸易代表团去上海访问

1966-08-31
第5版()
专栏：

    赞比亚贸易代表团去上海访问
    新华社三十日讯　以赞比亚工商部副部长齐萨特为首的赞比亚政府贸易代表团，今天乘飞机离开北京去上海访问。
    到机场欢送的，有对外贸易部副部长贾石等。
