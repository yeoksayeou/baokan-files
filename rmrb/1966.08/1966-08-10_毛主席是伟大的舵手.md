Title: Chairman Mao is the Great Helmsman
Author: Lí Ānguì  Wáng Shēngchén  Zhōu Zhèngqīng  Chén Wénpǔ
Date: 1966-08-10
Edition: 2

We are filled with boundless excitement as we listen to the decision of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. This is the greatest, most correct, and most brilliant decision, a programmatic document for the unprecedented Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, a new development of Marxism-Leninism, and a new victory of Mao Zedong Thought!

We enthusiastically hail this decision! We resolutely support this decision! We loyally carry out this decision!

This decision once again powerfully proves that our Party is truly a great, glorious, and correct Party. Mao Zedong Thought is the beacon of the Chinese revolution and the world revolution. Chairman Mao is the great helmsman of the revolution, guiding the revolutionary ship from victory to victory, toward the brilliant future of communist society!

We must strive to study this decision, loyally carry out this decision, enthusiastically propagate this decision, and bravely defend this decision! We must hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, hold high the great red banner of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, and carry forward the fearless spirit of daring to think, speak, act, venture, and make revolution. In the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, we must charge forward and fight to the end!

Long live the great Communist Party of China!

Long live the invincible Mao Zedong Thought!

Long live Chairman Mao!

	Department of Mathematics and Mechanics, Peking University
	Zhōu Zhèngqīng  Chén Wénpǔ
		Lí Ānguì  Wáng Shēngchén

### 毛主席是伟大的舵手
李安桂　王陞臣  周正清　陈文浦
1966-08-10
第2版()
专栏：

    毛主席是伟大的舵手
    我们怀着万分激动的心情，听了中共中央关于无产阶级文化大革命的决定。这是一个最伟大、最正确、最英明的决定，是史无前例的无产阶级文化大革命的纲领性的文件，是马克思列宁主义的新发展，是毛泽东思想的新胜利！
    我们最最热烈欢呼这个决定！最最坚决拥护这个决定！最最忠诚执行这个决定！
    这个决定再一次雄辩地证明了，我们的党不愧为伟大、光荣、正确的党，毛泽东思想是中国革命和世界革命的灯塔。毛主席是革命的伟大舵手，把革命的航船从胜利引向胜利，走向光辉灿烂的未来——共产主义社会！
    我们要努力学习这个决定，忠诚执行这个决定，热情宣传这个决定，勇敢捍卫这个决定！高高举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，高高举起无产阶级文化大革命伟大红旗，发扬敢想、敢说、敢做、敢闯、敢革命的大无畏精神，在无产阶级文化大革命中冲锋陷阵，将无产阶级文化大革命进行到底！
    伟大的中国共产党万岁！
    伟大的战无不胜的毛泽东思想万岁！
    毛主席万岁！
    北京大学数力系　周正清　陈文浦
                  李安桂　王陞臣
