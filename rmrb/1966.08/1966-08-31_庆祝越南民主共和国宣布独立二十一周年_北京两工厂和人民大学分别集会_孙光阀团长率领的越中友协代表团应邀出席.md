Title: Celebrating the 21st Anniversary of the Declaration of Independence of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-08-31
Edition: 6

Two factories and Peking University hold meetings to celebrate the 21st anniversary of the declaration of independence of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The delegation led by Sun Guanghuai, chairman of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association, is invited to attend.

According to a Xinhua News Agency report on the 29th, the revolutionary workers of the state-owned Beijing Electronic Tube Factory and the state-owned Beijing Second Radio Materials Factory held a meeting today to enthusiastically celebrate the 21st anniversary of the declaration of independence of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

The delegation led by Sun Guanghuai, chairman of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association, and Nguyen Quang Huy, counselor of the Vietnamese embassy in China, were invited to attend the celebration and were warmly welcomed by the workers.

A responsible person from the Beijing Electronic Tube Factory spoke at the meeting, enthusiastically praising the revolutionary spirit of the heroic Vietnamese people in their resolute struggle against American aggression and for national salvation. He also expressed his determination to be the backbone of the Vietnamese people and resolutely support the Vietnamese people's struggle against American aggression and for national salvation.

Sun Guanghuai, chairman, was invited to speak. He said that in the struggle against American aggression and for national salvation, we have always regarded China as a vast and reliable rear area.

Sun Guanghuai, chairman, said that at present, under the leadership of the Communist Party of China, the Chinese people are holding high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought and are successfully carrying out the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. The Vietnamese people are delighted and inspired by the brilliant achievements of their Chinese brothers in all fields.

After the meeting, the factory workers performed a grand chorus of "The East Is Red" and "We Are the Backbone of the Vietnamese People," and performed songs and dances such as "The Works of Chairman Mao Shine with Golden Light."

According to a Xinhua News Agency report on the 30th, tonight, the revolutionary teachers, students, and staff of Peking University held a meeting with the delegation led by Sun Guanghuai to enthusiastically celebrate the 21st anniversary of the declaration of independence of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The meeting was filled with a revolutionary and combative atmosphere. Before the meeting, the revolutionary teachers, students, and staff of the school collectively read the quotations of Chairman Mao, and sang revolutionary songs such as "The Sea Voyage Needs a Helm" with Vietnamese students.

Sun Guanghuai, chairman, was invited to speak at the meeting. He expressed his heartfelt thanks to the Chinese people for their great support to the Vietnamese people. He said that we are firmly convinced that, due to many favorable conditions at home and abroad, our struggle to build socialism and oppose imperialism will definitely be successful.

A responsible person from the Cultural Revolution Preparatory Committee of Peking University spoke at the meeting, expressing the most resolute support for the Vietnamese people's struggle against American aggression and for national salvation.

Sun Guanghuai presented a small model of an American plane made from the wreckage of a downed American plane and other gifts to the school.

### 庆祝越南民主共和国宣布独立二十一周年  北京两工厂和人民大学分别集会  孙光阀团长率领的越中友协代表团应邀出席

1966-08-31
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝越南民主共和国宣布独立二十一周年
    北京两工厂和人民大学分别集会
    孙光阀团长率领的越中友协代表团应邀出席
    据新华社二十九日讯  国营北京电子管厂和国营北京第二无线电器材厂革命职工代表今天举行集会，热烈庆祝越南民主共和国宣布独立二十一周年。
    孙光阀团长率领的越中友协代表团和越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆参赞阮光辉等，应邀参加了庆祝会，受到职工的热烈欢迎。
    北京电子管厂的一位负责人在会上讲话，热烈赞扬英雄的越南人民坚决进行抗美救国斗争的革命精神，并且表示誓做越南人民的后盾，坚决支持越南人民的抗美救国斗争。
    孙光阀团长应邀讲话。他说，在抗美救国斗争中，我们一直把中国看成是一个广阔和可靠的后方。
    孙光阀团长说，目前，中国人民在中国共产党领导下，高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，正在胜利地进行着无产阶级文化大革命。越南人民为兄弟的中国人民在各方面取得的辉煌成就感到欢欣鼓舞。
    会后，工厂工人表演了大合唱《东方红》、《誓做越南人民坚强后盾》，表演唱《毛主席著作闪金光》等歌舞节目。
    据新华社三十日讯中国人民大学革命师生员工今晚和以孙光阀为首的越中友协代表团一起举行集会，热烈庆祝越南民主共和国宣布独立二十一周年。会场上充满革命战斗的气氛。会前，学校的革命师生员工集体朗读毛主席语录，还和越南留学生一起高唱《大海航行靠舵手》等革命歌曲。
    孙光阀团长应邀在会上讲话，他表示衷心感谢中国人民对越南人民的巨大支援。他说，我们坚信，由于国内国际有着许多有利条件，我们建设社会主义和反对帝国主义的斗争一定能够成功。
    中国人民大学文化革命筹备委员会的一位负责人在会上讲话的时候，对越南人民的抗美救国斗争表示最坚决的支持。
    孙光阀把用打下的美国飞机残骸制成的飞机小模型等礼物送给了学校。
