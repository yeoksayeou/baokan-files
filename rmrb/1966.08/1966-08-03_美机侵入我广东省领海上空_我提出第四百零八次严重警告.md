Title: U.S. Plane Intrudes into Our Guangdong Province's Territorial Airspace
Author:
Date: 1966-08-03
Edition: 4

On August 2, at 10:58 AM to 11:20 AM, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into our Guangdong Province's territorial airspace south of Yalou County, Hainan Island. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the ministry, issued the 408th serious warning.

### 美机侵入我广东省领海上空  我提出第四百零八次严重警告

1966-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

    美机侵入我广东省领海上空
    我提出第四百零八次严重警告
    新华社二日讯　八月二日十时五十八分至十一时二十分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省海南岛崖县以南地区领海上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百零八次严重警告。
