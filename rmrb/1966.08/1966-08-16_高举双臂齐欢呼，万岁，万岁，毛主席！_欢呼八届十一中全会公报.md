Title: Raise Both Arms and Cheer, Long Live Chairman Mao! — Celebrating the Communiqué of the Eleventh Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee
Author: Chen Shixin
Date: 1966-08-16
Edition: 3

Raise both arms and cheer,
Long live, long live, Chairman Mao!
— Celebrating the Communiqué of the Eleventh Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee

By Chen Shixin, PLA Navy

The blue sky is flying the red flag,
Revolutionaries closely follow Chairman Mao.
Raise both arms and cheer,
Billions of voices shake the heavens and earth:
Long live the great Communist Party of China!
Long live the beloved Chairman Mao!

The blue sky is flying the red flag,
We closely follow Chairman Mao.
Crossing rapids, breaking waves,
One victory after another!

Following Chairman Mao,
The red flag of Jinggangshan rises at Tiananmen;
Following Chairman Mao,
The Three Rivers and Five Ridges change the world;
Following Chairman Mao,
The red flag of revolution is passed down;
Following Chairman Mao,
The proletarian's mountains are ironclad!

The blue sky is flying the red flag,
We closely follow Chairman Mao.
Through wind and rain,
Not a step away;
Until the seas dry up and the stones crumble,
Firm and unyielding.

Following the direction pointed out by Chairman Mao,
Using Mao Zedong Thought as our weapon,
Dare to revolutionize, dare to win,
Bravely charge against the demons and monsters!

The blue sky is flying the red flag,
The red lighthouse stands in the East.
Mao Zedong Thought shines with golden light,
Revolutionary storms rise across the continents!
Sweep away all demons and monsters,
Wherever we go, there are no enemies!

The red flag flies over the five continents and four seas,
The revolutionary people of the world closely follow Chairman Mao.
Raise both arms and cheer,
Billions of voices shake the heavens and earth:
Long live! Long live! The great Communist Party of China!
Long live! Long live! The beloved Chairman Mao!

### 高举双臂齐欢呼，万岁，万岁，毛主席！——欢呼八届十一中全会公报
陈诗信
1966-08-16
第3版()
专栏：

　　高举双臂齐欢呼，万岁，万岁，毛主席！
　　——欢呼八届十一中全会公报
　　解放军海军某部　陈诗信
　　万里蓝天飘红旗，
　　革命人紧紧跟着毛主席。
　　高举双臂齐欢呼，
　　亿万心声震天地：
　　万岁，伟大的中国共产党！
　　万岁，敬爱的毛主席！
　　万里蓝天飘红旗，
　　我们紧紧跟着毛主席。
　　闯险滩，破恶浪，
　　一个胜利接着一个胜利！
　　跟着毛主席，
　  井冈山的红旗在天安门
    升起；
　　跟着毛主席，
    三河五岭换天地；
　　跟着毛主席，
　  革命红旗代代举；
　　跟着毛主席，
　  无产阶级的江山是铁打的！
　　万里蓝天飘红旗，
　　我们紧紧跟着毛主席。
　　风吹雨打，
　　一步不离；
　　海枯石烂，
　　坚定不移。
　　照毛主席指出的航向，
　　用毛泽东思想作武器，
　　敢于革命，敢于胜利，
　　勇向牛鬼蛇神猛冲击！
　　万里蓝天飘红旗，
　　红色灯塔东方立。
　　毛泽东思想金光闪，
　　革命风暴五洲起！
　　横扫一切牛鬼蛇神，
　　所向之处全无敌！
　　五洲四海飘红旗，
　　世界革命人民紧紧
　　跟着毛主席。
　　高举双臂齐欢呼，
　　亿万心声震天地：
　　万岁！万岁！伟大的中国共产党！
　　万岁！万岁！敬爱的毛主席！
