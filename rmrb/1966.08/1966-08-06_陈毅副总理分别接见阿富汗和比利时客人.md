Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Afghan and Belgian Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-08-06
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Afghan delegation of news workers led by Deputy Director of the Afghan News and Culture Department, Qazi Khairuddin, and the delegation members on the morning of August 5. He had a friendly talk with them.

Present at the reception were Vice Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association Chang Chih-ching and Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Chen Wei-fan.

Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Belgian delegation of the Women's Alliance led by Marcel Akampo and the delegation members on the afternoon of August 5. He had a friendly talk with them.

Present at the reception were Vice Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation Kang K'o-ch'ing and Secretary of the Secretariat Li Pao-kuang.

### 陈毅副总理分别接见阿富汗和比利时客人

1966-08-06
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理分别接见阿富汗和比利时客人
    新华社五日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见阿富汗新闻文化部文学顾问齐亚·考里察达和由他率领的阿富汗新闻代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席常芝青、外交部新闻司副司长陈维帆等。
    新华社五日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见了由玛赛尔·阿坎波率领的比利时妇女联盟代表团全体成员，同她们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有全国妇联副主席康克清，书记处书记李宝光等。
