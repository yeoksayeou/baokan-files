Title: Invited to Participate in the Third Anniversary Celebration of the August Revolution in Congo (Brazzaville)
Author:
Date: 1966-08-15
Edition: 6

The Chinese government delegation, led by Interior Minister Zeng Shan, arrived here by plane in the afternoon of the 13th to participate in the third anniversary celebration of the August Revolution in Congo (Brazzaville) at the invitation of the Congolese (Brazzaville) government.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included Lenda Obé, a member of the Political Bureau of the National Revolutionary Movement of Congo (Brazzaville), Jean Bakkorolo, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Diyabua, a member of the Congolese (Brazzaville) August Revolution Third Anniversary Celebration Reception Committee.

Also present at the airport to welcome the delegation were Zhou Qiuye, the Chinese Ambassador to Congo (Brazzaville), and a member of the Chinese government delegation.

### 应邀参加刚果（布）八月革命三周年庆典　我政府代表团到刚果（布）

1966-08-15
第6版()
专栏：

　　应邀参加刚果（布）八月革命三周年庆典
　　我政府代表团到刚果（布）
    新华社布拉柴维尔十三日电　由内务部部长曾山率领的中国政府代表团应刚果（布）政府的邀请，十三日下午乘飞机到达这里，参加刚果（布）八月革命三周年庆典。
    到机场欢迎代表团的有刚果（布）全国革命运动政治局委员伦达·奥贝，外交部礼宾司司长巴克科罗·让，刚果（布）八月革命三周年庆祝活动接待委员会委员迪亚布阿。
    到机场欢迎代表团的还有中国驻刚果（布）大使、中国政府代表团团员周秋野。
