Title: New Arrivals of Chairman Mao's Selected Works
Author:
Date: 1966-08-13
Edition: 3

A truck from the New China Bookstore carrying "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" arrived at Peking University and was greeted with the most enthusiastic welcome by revolutionary teachers and students. They shouted, "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Long live Mao Zedong Thought!" "Carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution through to the end!"

		Photographed by our reporter

Revolutionary teachers, students, and staff of Tsinghua University held a grand meeting on August 12 to accept Chairman Mao's works. The picture shows the meeting venue.

		Photographed by our reporter
