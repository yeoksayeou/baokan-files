Title: Follow Chairman Mao, Be a Revolutionary for Life
Author: Miaodi
Date: 1966-08-12
Edition: 3

Follow Chairman Mao, be a revolutionary for life. This is the firm resolution of the revolutionary people of the world. The great leader Chairman Mao has pointed out: "The world is yours, as well as ours, but in the last analysis, it is yours. You young people, full of vigor and vitality, are in the bloom of life, like the sun at eight or nine in the morning. Our hope is placed on you." The revolutionary people of the world, especially the young people, must firmly grasp the great banner of Mao Zedong Thought, follow Chairman Mao's teachings, and march forward bravely on the revolutionary road.
