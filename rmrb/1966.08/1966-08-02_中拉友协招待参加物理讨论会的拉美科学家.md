Title: China-Latin America Friendship Association Hosts Latin American Scientists Attending Physics Discussion Meeting
Author:
Date: 1966-08-02
Edition: 6

The China-Latin America Friendship Association held a reception tonight to welcome Latin American scientists attending the Beijing Physics Discussion Meeting.

Chairman of the China-Latin America Friendship Association, Chu Tu-nan, and others attended the reception and gathered with scientists from Argentina, Chile, Colombia, Ecuador, Mexico, Uruguay, and other countries. They exchanged friendly greetings.

Chu Tu-nan spoke at the reception, praising the Latin American scientists for their contributions to enhancing friendship between the Chinese people and the peoples of Latin American countries, and for promoting unity and cooperation among scientists. He expressed his belief that this friendship and unity will continue to grow.

### 中拉友协招待参加物理讨论会的拉美科学家

1966-08-02
第6版()
专栏：

    中拉友协招待参加物理讨论会的拉美科学家
    新华社一日讯　中国拉丁美洲友好协会今晚举行酒会，招待参加北京物理讨论会的拉丁美洲各国科学家们。
    中拉友协会长楚图南等出席酒会，同应邀出席的阿根廷、智利、哥伦比亚、厄瓜多尔、墨西哥、乌拉圭等国的科学家们，欢聚一堂，友好相叙。
    楚图南在酒会上讲话，赞扬拉丁美洲科学家们为增进中国同拉丁美洲国家人民之间的友谊和科学家之间的团结合作作出了贡献，并表示相信，这种友谊和团结将不断发展。
