Title: The 1966 Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational is About to Commence
Author:
Date: 1966-08-17
Edition: 4

The organizing committee for the 1966 Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational has been established.

Chairman of the Chinese Table Tennis Association and Chairman of the Asian Region Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational Liaison Committee Chen Xian serves as the committee chairman, with Zhao Xiwu and Du Haoran as vice-chairmen.

The 1966 Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational, which has garnered widespread attention from the sports community of various countries, will be held in Beijing from August 27 to September 4.

So far, fourteen countries and regions have registered to participate in this invitational tournament. These countries and regions are: Cambodia, Ceylon, Iraq, Japan, Jordan, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Lebanon, Nepal, Pakistan, Palestine, Syria, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Yemen, and China. Among them, Jordan, Iraq, Lebanon, Palestine, and Yemen are participating in the Beijing Invitational for the first time.

Since the Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational began in 1964, the number of participating countries has increased from eight in the first year, with the scale of the event growing larger each year. This fully reflects the strong desire of the peoples of various countries to engage in mutual exchanges, foster friendship, strengthen unity, and independently develop sports.

Upon announcing this news, a responsible person from the Chinese Table Tennis Association expressed a warm welcome to the friends from various countries who will be participating in the invitational. They stated that the Chinese table tennis team, nurtured under the guidance of Mao Zedong Thought, will, through joint training and competitive activities in this invitational, foster friendship with athletes from various countries, promote mutual progress, and jointly improve, contributing to the cause of unity and anti-imperialism for the peoples of the world.

### 今年北京国际乒乓球邀请赛即将举行　已有十四个国家和地区应邀参加

1966-08-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　今年北京国际乒乓球邀请赛即将举行
　　已有十四个国家和地区应邀参加
    据新华社十六日讯  一九六六年北京国际乒乓球邀请赛组织委员会已经成立。
    中国乒乓球协会主席、亚洲地区北京国际乒乓球邀请赛联络委员会主席陈先担任组织委员会主席，副主席是赵希武、都浩然。
    新华社十六日讯　受到各国体育界广泛重视的一九六六年北京国际乒乓球邀请赛，八月二十七日到九月四日将在北京举行。
    到目前为止，已有十四个国家和地区报名参加本届邀请赛。这些国家和地区是：柬埔寨、锡兰、伊拉克、日本、约旦、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、黎巴嫩、尼泊尔、巴基斯坦、巴勒斯坦、叙利亚、越南民主共和国、也门和中国。其中约旦、伊拉克、黎巴嫩、巴勒斯坦、也门，是第一次参加北京邀请赛的。
    北京国际乒乓球邀请赛从一九六四年开始举办以来，参加的国家由第一年的八个，逐年增加，规模越来越大，充分反映了各国人民互相交往、增进友谊、加强团结和独立自主地发展体育运动的强烈愿望。
    中国乒乓球协会负责人在宣布这一消息时，对参加邀请赛的各国朋友表示热烈欢迎。他表示，在毛泽东思想哺育下成长的中国乒乓球队，将在这次邀请赛的共同练习和比赛活动中，和各国选手增进友好，互相促进，共同提高，为世界人民的团结反帝事业做出贡献。
