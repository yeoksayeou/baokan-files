Title: Long Live! Long Live! Great Chairman Mao!
Author: Han Xiao
Date: 1966-08-13
Edition: 2

	Long Live! Long Live! Great Chairman Mao!
	Guangzhou Military Region Naval Cultural Work Team	Han Xiao

	Most Beloved Party Central Committee,
	Most Beloved Chairman Mao!

	Your resounding voice is the battle call,
	Your warm instructions are all truth.
	Stirring the rivers and mountains,
	Inspiring the 700 million heroic sons and daughters.

	Long Live! Long Live! Great Chairman Mao!

	We, the revolutionary soldiers of the proletariat,
	Will definitely follow your wise directives,
	Care about state affairs,
	And resolutely carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution to the end!

	Most Beloved Party Central Committee,
	Most Beloved Chairman Mao!

	Your wise decisions are the guide to action,
	Your latest directives are sharp weapons.
	Concentrating the wisdom of the masses,
	Developing Marxism-Leninism.

	Long Live! Long Live! Great Chairman Mao!

	We, the new generation of communists,
	Will definitely follow your wise directives,
	Care about state affairs,
	And resolutely carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution to the end!
