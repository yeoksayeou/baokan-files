Title: Publication of the Bulletin of the Eleventh Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1966-08-16
Edition: 2

The bulletin of the eleventh plenary session of the eighth central committee of the Communist Party of China has been published as a separate volume by the People's Publishing House. Starting from August 16, it will be available for purchase at the New China Bookstore.
