	Quotations from Chairman Mao

	As long as all our brave and skilled fighters of the Eighth Route Army and the New Fourth Army not only know how to fight, know how to do mass work, and also know how to produce, we will fear no difficulties and will be, as Mencius said, "invincible under heaven." From "Organize!"

### 毛主席语录

1966-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    只要我们全体英勇善战的八路军新四军，人人个个不但会打仗，会作群众工作，又会生产，我们就不怕任何困难，就会是孟夫子说过的：“无敌于天下。”《组织起来》
