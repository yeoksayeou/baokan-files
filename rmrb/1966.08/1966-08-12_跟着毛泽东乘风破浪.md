Title: Following Chairman Mao to Break the Waves
Author: Aikewala Luobai'er
Date: 1966-08-12
Edition: 6

	Following Chairman Mao to Break the Waves
		Secretary of the Cameroon Labour Union Aikewala Luobai'er

	Chairman Mao is seventy years old,
	Swimming the Yangtze River once again,
	The good news has spread,
	People and revolutionaries of all countries are overjoyed.

	Your health is so robust,
	Like a green pine tree,
	This is the boundless happiness of the people of all countries.

	The sea surges,
	Waves roll,
	The sky is filled with golden clouds,
	The wind is strong, the sails are full,
	The ship of hope breaks through the waves,
	On the ship stands a giant,
	Mao Zedong,
	The successor of Marx, Lenin, and Stalin,
	The helmsman of the new era,
	Guiding the revolution of the people of all countries.

	You swim the Yangtze River again,
	Your people follow you, breaking through the waves,
	What joyous teachings!
	As your students,
	We will forever be loyal to your teachings,
	Like a sunflower turning towards the sun,
	Following your thoughts,
	Your thoughts are the sun.

	Long live Chairman Mao forever!

### 跟着毛泽东乘风破浪
艾克瓦拉·罗贝尔
1966-08-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　跟着毛泽东乘风破浪
　　喀麦隆劳工总联合会负责人　艾克瓦拉·罗贝尔
　　毛泽东七十高龄，
　　再次畅游长江，
　　喜讯传来，
　　各国人民和革命者心花怒放。
　　您的身体多么健康，
　　象青翠的劲松一样，
　　这是我们各国人民的无限幸福。
　　大海翻腾，
　　白浪滔滔，
　　满天金色的彩霞，
　　风急帆满，
　　希望的船破浪前进，
　　船上站立着一位巨人，
　　毛泽东，
　　马克思、列宁、斯大林的继承人，
　　新时代的舵手，
　　在引导着各国人民的革命。
　　您再次畅游长江，
　　您的人民跟着您乘风破浪，
　　可喜的教导！
　　作为您的学生，
　　我们永远忠于您的教导，
　　象葵花向阳那样，
　　追随您的思想，
　　您的思想就是太阳。
　　祝毛泽东万寿无疆！
      （喀麦隆劳工总联合会负责人艾克瓦拉·罗贝尔在听到毛主席畅游长江的消息后，在科纳克里发表了这首热情歌颂伟大的毛主席的诗。）
