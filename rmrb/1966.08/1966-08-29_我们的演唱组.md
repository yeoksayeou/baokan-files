Title: Our Singing Troupe
Author: Qian Hongchun
Date: 1966-08-29
Edition: 6

Our singing troupe,
Equipment is simple,
Two erhus and a flute,
Plus bamboo clappers and drums;
Don't underestimate our meager possessions,
Our performances are both wonderful and varied.

Our singing troupe,
Can sing and dance,
Sing a piece while marching,
Tell a quick story during training breaks;
Perform wherever we go,
With boundless energy.

Our singing troupe,
Are both cultured and martial,
Marksmanship, grenade throwing, and bayonet combat,
Mastered with skill;
On stage, they are excellent performers,
Off stage, they are fierce tigers.

Our singing troupe,
Everyone has great aspirations,
Perform for the revolution,
Guard the cultural front firmly,
Never forget class struggle,
Always remember Chairman Mao's words!

### 我们的演唱组
钱红春
1966-08-29
第6版()
专栏：连队生活短歌

    我们的演唱组
    解放军某部战士　钱红春
    我们的演唱组，
    装备很朴素，
    两把胡琴一支笛，
    外加竹板和锣鼓；
    别瞧家当少，
    节目精采又丰富。
    我们的演唱组，
    能歌也能舞，
    行军路上唱一段，
    训练间隙说快书；
    走到哪里演到哪，
    劲头实在足。
    我们的演唱组，
    能文更能武，
    射击、投弹和刺杀，
    练出一手硬功夫；
    台上都是好演员，
    台下都是小老虎。
    我们的演唱组，
    人人都把雄心树，
    为革命，演节目，
    文化阵地守牢固，
    永远不忘阶级斗争，
    毛主席的话紧记住！
