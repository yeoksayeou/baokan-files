Title: Opening the Little Red Book
Author: Qiáng Tèsōng
Date: 1966-08-18
Edition: 6

	Opening the Little Red Book,
	The heart is bright and clear,
	Following Chairman Mao's words,
	Actions have a direction.

	Opening the Little Red Book,
	The will to fight is stronger,
	Remembering the bitterness of class struggle,
	Grasp the gun in hand.

	Opening the Little Red Book,
	Look at the world,
	Following Chairman Mao,
	The red heart forever turns to the Party!

### 翻开语录本
邝特松
1966-08-18
第6版()
专栏：

　　翻开语录本
　　邝特松
　　翻开语录本，

　　翻开语录本，
