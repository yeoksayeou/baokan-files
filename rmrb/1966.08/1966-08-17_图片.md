Title: Workers of Shanghai High Bridge Chemical Factory Sing Revolutionary Songs and Celebrate the Party's Eighth Plenum of the Eleventh Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1966-08-17
Edition: 2

    Workers of the Shanghai High Bridge Chemical Factory sing revolutionary songs they composed themselves, such as "The Light of Chairman Mao's Works Shines Brightly," enthusiastically celebrating the communiqué of the Party's Eighth Plenum of the Eleventh Central Committee. They are determined to take Mao Zedong Thought as the guiding principle for all their work, striving for victories in both revolution and production.  Photograph by Xinhua News Agency reporter (telephoto).
