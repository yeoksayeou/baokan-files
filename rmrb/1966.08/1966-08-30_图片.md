Title: Agreement on Economic and Technical Aid from China to Vietnam Signed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-08-30
Edition: 6

On August 29, the agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on China's economic and technical aid to Vietnam was signed in Beijing. Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi attended the signing ceremony. The image shows Vice Premier Li Xiannian and Vice Premier Le Quy representing their respective governments signing the agreement. Xinhua News Agency photographer.
