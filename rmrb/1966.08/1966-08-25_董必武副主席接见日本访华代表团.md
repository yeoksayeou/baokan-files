Title: Vice Chairman Dong Biwu Meets with Japanese Delegation Visiting China
Author:
Date: 1966-08-25
Edition: 4

Vice Chairman Dong Biwu met with the Japanese delegation visiting China this afternoon. The delegation was led by Team Leader Makiuchi Takehito, and included members Seimei Goro and Hiratada Akira.

Present at the meeting were relevant officials Zhang Zhiran and Wang Jiren.

### 董必武副主席接见日本访华代表团

1966-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

    董必武副主席接见日本访华代表团
    新华社二十四日讯　董必武副主席今天下午接见日本访华代表团团长牧野内武人，团员清宫五郎、平田亮。
    接见时在座的，有张志让、王吉仁等有关方面负责人。
