Title: Liao Chengzhi Hosts Dinner for Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-08-10
Edition: 6

Liao Chengzhi, President of the China-Japan Friendship Association, hosted a dinner tonight to welcome a delegation of Japanese Social Party parliamentarians led by Tate Kenjiro.

Attending the dinner to accompany the guests were Zhao Anbo, Xiao Jinguang, Wang Xiaoyun, and others.

Japanese peace activist Nishisonji Koji, who is currently in Beijing, was also invited to the dinner.

### 廖承志宴请日本客人

1966-08-10
第6版()
专栏：

    廖承志宴请日本客人
    新华社九日讯　中日友协会长廖承志今晚设宴招待以楯兼次郎为首的日本社会党国会议员访华团。
    出席宴会作陪的，有赵安博、萧向前、王晓云等。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也应邀出席了宴会。
