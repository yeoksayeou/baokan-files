Title: Study Chairman Mao's Works for a Lifetime and Follow Chairman Mao in Revolution for a Lifetime
Author:
Date: 1966-08-26
Edition: 3

The broad revolutionary masses of Yinchuan, Hohhot, and Chongqing joyfully welcome the distribution of Chairman Mao's works.

Chairman Mao's works in hand, the revolutionary truth in heart, stand firm and clear in direction, march forward with heads held high following the Party.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Yinchuan, August 25th: The broad revolutionary masses of the Hui and Han nationalities in Yinchuan, the capital of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region, are overjoyed to hear the news of the mass printing and distribution of various editions of the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong."

Upon hearing the news of the mass distribution of the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong," the revolutionary masses enthusiastically beat drums and gongs, carrying large portraits of Chairman Mao and models of the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" to the bookstore entrances, celebrating this joyous event. They proudly hold up red "double happiness" characters and red-wrapped "Selected Works of Mao Zedong," excitedly shouting, "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Long live the invincible Mao Zedong Thought!" Some people's eyes well up with tears as they gaze at Chairman Mao's portrait on the book's frontispiece. Many revolutionary youngsters from primary and secondary schools, after purchasing the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong," expressed their determination to better study and apply Chairman Mao's works, further carrying forward the daring spirit of rebellion of the proletarian revolution, using Mao Zedong Thought, the most powerful spiritual atomic bomb, to sweep away all ghosts and monsters, destroy all old ideas, old culture, old customs, and old habits, and create a brand-new world of the proletariat.

The relevant departments of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region and the Xinhua Bookstore also sent a decorated car with over 2,200 copies of the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" and the "Quotations from Chairman Mao" to Ningxia University. The entire school of revolutionary teachers and students enthusiastically gathered around the decorated car, shouting and jumping with joy. The slogans of "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years! Ten thousand times ten thousand years!" accompanied by drumbeats and songs like "Sailing the Seas Depends on the Helmsman" echoed through the sky. Revolutionary students from the Class of 1969 in the Chinese Department excitedly composed and sang poems: "Chairman Mao's works in hand, the revolutionary truth in heart. Stand firm and clear in direction, march forward with heads held high following the Party. Chairman Mao's works never leave the side, the key to happiness and the guiding light. Hold high the red flag and march forward, forever be the red successors."

According to Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, August 24th: Over 7,100 revolutionary teachers, students, and staff from various colleges and universities in Hohhot, the capital of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, purchased the long-awaited revolutionary treasure, the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong," on the 22nd.

On that morning, the mobile supply decorated car of the Xinhua Bookstore drove into Inner Mongolia University, Inner Mongolia Normal University, Inner Mongolia Agricultural and Animal Husbandry College, and five other colleges and universities. Revolutionary teachers, students, and staff of various nationalities held up portraits of Chairman Mao, waving colorful flags and bouquets, singing and dancing. With high revolutionary enthusiasm, they sang songs like "Sailing the Seas Depends on the Helmsman" and "I Love to Read Chairman Mao's Book" for a long time, shouting slogans like "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand times ten thousand years!" Many minority revolutionary students also wore their national festival costumes, holding up the newly purchased "Selected Works of Mao Zedong," dancing revolutionary dances and singing revolutionary songs around Chairman Mao's portrait.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, August 24th: Chongqing began mass distribution of the "Quotations from Chairman Mao" on the 22nd, with over 280,000 revolutionary masses purchasing the newly published "Quotations from Chairman Mao" on that day.

The news of the Xinhua Bookstore beginning mass distribution of the "Quotations from Chairman Mao" spread, and people were overjoyed, spreading the word to each other. From the evening of the 21st, at around 7 or 8 o'clock, long queues formed in front of the 18 bookstores across the city, with people eager to buy Chairman Mao's works.

A middle school student, after purchasing the "Quotations from Chairman Mao," wrote excitedly in a red notebook: "Chairman Mao! You are the red sun in the hearts of revolutionary people around the world, you are the lighthouse of revolutionary people around the world. The Chinese people love you, the people of the world love you, because your thought is the most revolutionary and greatest thought in the world." While the Xinhua Bookstore was selling the "Quotations from Chairman Mao" in 18 bookstores, it also sent decorated cars to deliver the "Quotations from Chairman Mao" to factories and rural people's communes in the suburbs.

When Xinhua Bookstore staff delivered the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" to Beijing Institute of Mining and Technology, the students enthusiastically cheered, expressing their boundless love for Chairman Mao.

### 读一辈子毛主席著作  跟毛主席干一辈子革命  银川、呼和浩特、重庆广大革命群众欢呼得到毛主席著作

1966-08-26
第3版()
专栏：

    读一辈子毛主席著作  跟毛主席干一辈子革命
    银川、呼和浩特、重庆广大革命群众欢呼得到毛主席著作
    主席著作捧在手　革命真理记心头立场坚定方向明　昂首阔步跟党走
    据新华社银川二十五日电　宁夏回族自治区首府银川市广大回汉族革命群众怀着无比喜悦的心情，踊跃竞购新印刷的各种版本的《毛泽东选集》。
    革命群众听到大量发行《毛泽东选集》的喜讯后，纷纷敲锣打鼓，抬着毛主席的巨幅画像和《毛泽东选集》的模型到书店门口，欢庆这一大喜事。他们手捧着红“囍”字和红纸包着的《毛泽东选集》，激动地高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“战无不胜的毛泽东思想万岁！”有的人目光停留在书中扉页的毛主席像上，眼里滚动着激动的泪花……。许多中小学的革命小将买到《毛泽东选集》后，表示要更好地活学活用毛主席著作，进一步发扬敢于造反的无产阶级革命精神，用毛泽东思想这个最强大的精神原子弹，横扫一切牛鬼蛇神，摧毁一切旧思想、旧文化、旧风俗、旧习惯，打出一个崭新的无产阶级新世界。
    宁夏回族自治区有关部门和新华书店还派彩车把二千二百多册《毛泽东选集》和《毛主席语录》送到宁夏大学。全校革命师生围着彩车欢呼跳跃，“毛主席万岁！万岁！万万岁！”的口号声，伴随着锣鼓声和《大海航行靠舵手》等歌声响彻天空。中文系六九级革命学生兴奋地赋诗高歌：主席著作捧在手，革命真理记心头。立场坚定方向明，昂首阔步跟党走。主席著作不离身，开心钥匙指路灯。高举红旗向前进，永做红色接班人。
    据新华社呼和浩特二十四日电　内蒙古自治区首府呼和浩特市各高等院校的七千一百多名革命师生员工，二十二日买到了日夜渴望的革命宝书《毛泽东选集》。
    当天上午，新华书店的流动供应彩车驶进内蒙古大学、内蒙古师范学院、内蒙古农牧学院等六所高等院校时，各族革命师生员工举着毛主席画像，手持彩旗花束，载歌载舞。他们以高昂的革命豪情，高唱《大海航行靠舵手》《毛主席的书我最爱读》等歌曲。长时间地高呼“毛主席万岁！万万岁！”等口号。许多少数民族的革命学生，还穿上自己民族的节日服装，高举着刚刚买到的《毛泽东选集》，围绕着毛主席画像，跳起革命舞，唱起革命歌。
    据新华社重庆二十四日电　重庆市二十二日开始大量发行《毛主席语录》，当天就有二十八万多革命群众买到了新出版的《毛主席语录》。
    二十二日，新华书店开始大量发行《毛主席语录》的喜讯传来，人们欢欣若狂、奔走相告。从头天晚上七八点钟起，在全市十八个售书店门前，渴望买到毛主席著作的人们就排成了长长的行列。
    一个中学生买到《毛主席语录》后，在鲜红色的留言簿上激动地写道：“毛主席呀！您是世界革命人民心中的红太阳，您是世界革命人民的灯塔。中国人民热爱您，世界人民热爱您，因为您的思想是全世界最革命、最伟大的思想。”重庆新华书店在十八个售书店发售《毛主席语录》的同时，还开出了彩车把《毛主席语录》送到工厂和郊区农村人民公社。（附图片）
    当新华书店职工把《毛泽东选集》送到北京矿业学院的时候，学生们热烈欢呼，表达了他们对毛主席无限热爱的心情。
                                  新华社记者摄
