Title: Huis and Hans Overcome Drought and Pest Disasters, Ningxia's Precious Medicinal Material Goji Berries Harvested
Author:
Date: 1966-08-18
Edition: 3

Hui and Han community members have overcome severe drought and pest disasters, achieving a bumper harvest of goji berries. Currently, male and female community members are filled with the joy of a bountiful harvest, picking new fruits while enthusiastically selling dried fruits. According to reports from various places, this year's goji berry production in the entire region is expected to significantly increase compared to last year. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 回汉社员战胜旱灾虫灾  宁夏名贵药材枸杞丰收

1966-08-18
第3版()
专栏：

    回汉社员战胜旱灾虫灾
    宁夏名贵药材枸杞丰收
    我国枸杞主要产地宁夏，回、汉族社员高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，战胜了严重旱、虫灾害，夺得了枸杞的大丰收。目前，男女社员满怀丰收的喜悦，一边采摘新果，一边踊跃交售干果。据各地反映，今年全区枸杞的产量，将比去年有较大增长。　（据新华社）
