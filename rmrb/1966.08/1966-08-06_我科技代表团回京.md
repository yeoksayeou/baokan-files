Title: Chinese Science and Technology Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-08-06
Edition: 5

A delegation from the Ministry of Petroleum Industry led by Vice Minister Zhou Wenlong, which participated in the 10th session of the China-Germany Technical and Scientific Cooperation Standing Committee and the 11th session of the China-Romania Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee, returned to Beijing by plane today.

### 我科技代表团回京

1966-08-06
第5版()
专栏：

    我科技代表团回京
    据新华社五日讯　由石油工业部副部长周文龙率领的中国科学技术代表团，参加中德技术和技术科学合作常任委员会第十届会议和中罗科学技术合作委员会第十一届会议后，今天乘飞机回到北京。
