Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Five U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-08-31
Edition: 6

	The military and civilians of North Vietnam's Quang Ninh Province on August 29 successfully repelled another air raid by U.S. aggressors, shooting down two invading enemy aircraft.

	The military and civilians of North Vietnam's Thanh Hoa Province on August 28 shot down one invading U.S. jet aircraft.

	It was announced here on the evening of August 30 that the military and civilians of North Vietnam's Quang Ninh Province on August 30 shot down one invading U.S. aggressor aircraft.

	According to a supplementary report, the military and civilians of Nghe An Province also shot down one U.S. aggressor aircraft on August 17.

### 越南北方军民击落美机五架

1966-08-31
第6版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民击落美机五架
    新华社河内二十九日电　据这里公布：越南北方广宁省军民二十九日又粉碎了美国空中强盗的进攻，一举击落入侵敌机两架。
    新华社河内二十九日电　据这里公布：越南北方清化省军民，二十八日击落入侵的美国喷气式飞机一架。
    新华社河内三十日电　据这里三十日晚上公布：越南北方广宁省军民，三十日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架。
    另据补充报道，义路省军民在八月十七日也击落美国强盗飞机一架。
