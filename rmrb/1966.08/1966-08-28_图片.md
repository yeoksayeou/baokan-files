Title: Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and Vice Premier Chen Yi Attend the Opening Ceremony of the 1966 Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational
Author:
Date: 1966-08-28
Edition: 5

	Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and Vice Premier Chen Yi attended the opening ceremony of the 1966 Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational. They also met with the heads of the table tennis delegations from twelve countries and regions, and took a group photo with them.
