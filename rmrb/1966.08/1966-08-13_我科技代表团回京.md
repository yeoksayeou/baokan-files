Title: Chinese Science and Technology Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-08-13
Edition: 4

The Chinese science and technology delegation, led by team leader and chairman of the Chinese side of the Sino-Hungarian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee, Deputy Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building, Shen Hong, has returned to Beijing today after participating in the 10th session of the Sino-Hungarian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee and signing the session's minutes. The delegation departed from Budapest.

### 我科技代表团回京

1966-08-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　我科技代表团回京
    据新华社十日讯　由团长、中匈科学技术合作委员会中国组主席、第一机械工业部副部长沈鸿率领的中国科学技术代表团，在参加中匈科学技术合作委员会第十届会议并签订了会议议定书后，今天从布达佩斯回到北京。
