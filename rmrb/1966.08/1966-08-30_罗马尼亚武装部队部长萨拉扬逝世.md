Title: Romanian Minister of National Defense Leonte Saray Dies
Author:
Date: 1966-08-30
Edition: 6

Romanian Minister of National Defense Leonte Saray Dies

New China News Agency, Bucharest, August 28 — According to a broadcast by Bucharest Radio, the Central Committee of the Romanian Communist Party, the State Council of Romania, and the Council of Ministers jointly issued an obituary announcing that Leonte Saray, member of the Executive Committee of the Central Committee of the Romanian Communist Party and Minister of National Defense, passed away this afternoon due to illness.

The obituary states that the funeral will be held on August 31. The funeral committee will be headed by Nicolae Ceausescu, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Romanian Communist Party and President of the State Council.

### 罗马尼亚武装部队部长萨拉扬逝世

1966-08-30
第6版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚武装部队部长萨拉扬逝世
    新华社布加勒斯特二十八日电　据布加勒斯特电台广播，罗马尼亚共产党中央委员会，罗马尼亚国务委员会和部长会议联合发表讣告，宣布罗马尼亚共产党中央委员会执行委员会委员，罗马尼亚武装部队部长列·萨拉扬大将因重病不幸于二十八日下午逝世。
