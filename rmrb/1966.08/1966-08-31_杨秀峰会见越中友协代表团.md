Title: Yang Xiufeng Meets with the Vietnam-China Friendship Association Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-08-31
Edition: 6

Yang Xiufeng, president of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association, met with the delegation from the Vietnam-China Friendship Association led by Sun Guanguo this evening. They had dinner together and engaged in a warm and friendly conversation.

### 杨秀峰会见越中友协代表团

1966-08-31
第6版()
专栏：

    杨秀峰会见越中友协代表团
    新华社三十日讯　中越友协会长杨秀峰今晚会见了以孙光阀为首的越中友协代表团，并共进晚餐，宾主进行了亲切友好的交谈。
