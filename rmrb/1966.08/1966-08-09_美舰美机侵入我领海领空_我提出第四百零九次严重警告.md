Title: U.S. Ships and Planes Intrude into Our Territorial Waters and Airspace
Author:
Date: 1966-08-09
Edition: 6

U.S. ships and planes intrude into our territorial waters and airspace
We issue the 409th serious warning

New China News Agency, August 7th: At 5:49 AM to 7:18 AM, 11:13 AM to 1:30 PM, and 3:15 PM to 4:29 PM on August 7th, a U.S. military vessel intruded into our territorial waters off the eastern regions of Chongwu and Dongshan in Fujian Province three times. On the same day, from 10:48 AM to 11:00 AM, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace above the waters off the northeastern region of Wenchang County, Hainan Island, Guangdong Province. From 1:38 PM to 2:11 PM on the same day, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island and Dong Island in the Xisha Islands, Guangdong Province. Regarding the military provocation by the U.S. military vessels and aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the ministry, issues the 409th serious warning.

### 美舰美机侵入我领海领空  我提出第四百零九次严重警告

1966-08-09
第6版()
专栏：

    美舰美机侵入我领海领空
    我提出第四百零九次严重警告
    新华社七日讯　八月七日五时四十九分至七时十八分、十一时十三分至十三时三十分和十五时十五分至十六时二十九分，美国军舰一艘，三次侵入我福建省崇武、东山以东地区领海。同日十时四十八分至十一时，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省海南岛文昌县东北地区领海上空。同日十三时三十八分至十四时十一分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛永兴岛、东岛地区领空。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百零九次严重警告。
