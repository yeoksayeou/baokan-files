Title: Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational Competition Schedule Confirmed
Author:
Date: 1966-08-24
Edition: 4

The competition schedule for the seven events of the 1966 Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational has been determined through a draw today.

Participants in this invitational competition include 98 male and female athletes from 12 countries and regions: Cambodia, Ceylon, China, Japan, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Lebanon, Nepal, Pakistan, Palestine, Syria, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Yemen.

Attending the draw ceremony were the team leaders of the participating countries and regions. The Japanese team, which had not yet arrived in Beijing, was represented by Ohmura Kazuo, a representative of the Japanese peace activist Nishisonji Koichi, who is in Beijing. The draw was conducted based on the principle of friendly consultation and unanimous agreement among the leaders of the various teams.

The men's and women's team events will have 12 and 9 teams respectively. The first stage of both events will consist of three groups playing round-robin matches to determine the group standings. In the second stage, teams with the same standing from each group will play round-robin matches to determine the final standings. The draw results are as follows:

Men's First Group: China, Pakistan, Ceylon, Lebanon;
Second Group: North Korea, Vietnam, Syria, Yemen;
Third Group: Japan, Cambodia, Nepal, Palestine.

Women's First Group: China, Ceylon, Syria;
Second Group: Japan, Cambodia, Palestine;
Third Group: Vietnam, North Korea, Nepal.

The men's and women's singles events will have 65 and 33 participants respectively. They will play seven rounds (men) and six rounds (women) of elimination matches to determine the champions. The draw results for the men's singles include: Li Furong (China), Shalouf (Yemen), Red (Ceylon), Kim Chang-ho (North Korea), Nagatani Nobuhiko (Japan), A.P. Lohani (Nepal), Iyasa (Palestine), Zhou Lanxian (China) in the upper half; Pak Sin-il (North Korea), Kawar (Lebanon), Ikbal (Pakistan), Kagi Motoharu (Japan), Chiang Chhuon (Cambodia), Karsen (Syria), Nguyen Tam Phan (Vietnam), Zhuang Zedong (China) in the lower half.

The draw results for the women's singles include: Lin Huiching (China), Pha (Cambodia), Ratnayake (Ceylon), Nguyen Thi Mai (Vietnam), Li Heman (China), Kim Bangja (North Korea), Al-Mabruk (Palestine), Yamazaki Kyoko (Japan) in the upper half; Zheng Minzhi (China), Ho Xuan Lan (Vietnam), Wu Chingja (North Korea), Morisawa Sachiko (Japan), Li Li (China), Rana (Nepal), Al-Rasem (Syria), Fukatsu Naoko (Japan) in the lower half.

The participants in the three doubles events include: 32 pairs for men's doubles, which will play five rounds of elimination matches; 16 pairs for women's doubles, which will play four rounds of elimination matches; and 32 pairs for mixed doubles, which will play five rounds of elimination matches.

### 北京国际乒乓球邀请赛确定竞赛程序  十二个国家和地区九十八名选手参加各项比赛

1966-08-24
第4版()
专栏：

    北京国际乒乓球邀请赛确定竞赛程序
    十二个国家和地区九十八名选手参加各项比赛
    新华社二十二日讯  一九六六年北京国际乒乓球邀请赛七个项目的竞赛程序，今天已经抽签确定。
    参加这次邀请赛的，有柬埔寨、锡兰、中国、日本、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、黎巴嫩、尼泊尔、巴基斯坦、巴勒斯坦、叙利亚、越南民主共和国和也门等十二个国家和地区的九十八名男女选手。
    出席抽签仪式的有各参加国和地区代表团、队的领队。尚未到达北京的日本队，由在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一的代表奥村一男代表出席。这次抽签是根据各个代表团、队领队会议友好协商、一致确定的原则进行的。
    参加男、女团体赛的分别有十二个队和九个队。男、女第一阶段均分为三个组进行循环赛，确定小组名次。第二阶段各组同名次的队，再进行循环赛，确定最后名次。抽签结果如下：
    男子第一组：中国、巴基斯坦、锡兰、黎巴嫩；
    第二组：朝鲜、越南、叙利亚、也门；
    第三组：日本、柬埔寨、尼泊尔、巴勒斯坦。
    女子第一组：中国、锡兰、叙利亚；
    第二组：日本、柬埔寨、巴勒斯坦；
    第三组：越南、朝鲜、尼泊尔。
    参加男子和女子单打比赛的分别有六十五名和三十三名选手。他们将分别进行七轮（男）和六轮（女）淘汰赛，最后产生冠军。抽签结果，男子选手分布在上半区的包括：中国的李富荣、也门的夏勒夫、锡兰的雷德、朝鲜的金昌虎、日本的长谷川信彦、尼泊尔的亚·普·洛哈尼、巴勒斯坦的伊耶萨、中国的周兰荪；分布在下半区的包括：朝鲜的朴信一、黎巴嫩的卡瓦尔、巴基斯坦的伊克巴尔、日本的键本肇、柬埔寨的江楚南、叙利亚的卡森、越南的阮玉潘、中国的庄则栋。
    女子选手分布在上半区的包括：中国的林慧卿、柬埔寨的帕花、锡兰的拉特娜雅克、越南的阮氏梅、中国的李赫男、朝鲜的金邦子、巴勒斯坦的阿勒玛巴克、日本的山中教子；下半区的包括：中国的郑敏之、越南的何雪兰、朝鲜的吴贞子、日本的森泽幸子、中国的李莉、尼泊尔的拉纳、叙利亚的阿拉塞姆、日本的深津尚子。
    参加三个双打项目比赛的选手有：男子双打三十二对，将进行五轮淘汰赛；女子双打十六对，将进行四轮淘汰赛；男女混合双打三十二对，将进行五轮淘汰赛。
