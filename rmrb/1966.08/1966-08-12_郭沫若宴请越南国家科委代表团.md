Title: Guo Moruo Hosts Banquet for Vietnamese National Science and Technology Commission Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-08-12
Edition: 6

	Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, hosted a banquet this evening to warmly welcome the delegation from the National Science and Technology Commission of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by Vice Chairman Le Kiet.

	In their speeches at the banquet, Guo Moruo and Le Kiet enthusiastically praised the fighting friendship between the peoples of China and Vietnam. Those attending the banquet included Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Zhu Kezhen and Wu Youxun, among others.

	Vietnamese Ambassador to China, Counselor Lyh Chungshi, was also invited to attend.

### 郭沫若宴请越南国家科委代表团

1966-08-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　郭沫若宴请越南国家科委代表团
    新华社十一日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若今晚举行宴会，热烈欢迎由越南民主共和国国家科学技术委员会副主任黎克率领的越南国家科技委员会代表团。
    郭沫若和黎克在宴会上的讲话中，热烈颂扬了中越两国人民的战斗友谊。出席宴会的，有中国科学院副院长竺可桢、吴有训等。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆参赞黎终始等也应邀出席。
