Title: The Motivation for Our Advancement
Author: Duān Yǎrú
Date: 1966-08-11
Edition: 4

The decision by the Party Central Committee on the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution expresses what is in our hearts. It supports us, the broad masses of workers, peasants, and soldiers, and is a powerful weapon for sweeping away all monsters and demons in the Cultural Revolution. The Party Central Committee's decision is our lighthouse, our compass, our steering wheel, and the motivation for our advancement. The Party Central Committee's decision embodies Mao Zedong Thought. I will resolutely follow the instructions of the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao, put courage first, carry on the revolution unceasingly, and see the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution through to the end!

Worker, Beijing First Cotton Textile Mill, Duān Yǎrú

### 鼓舞我们前进的动力
段雅茹
1966-08-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　鼓舞我们前进的动力
    党中央关于无产阶级文化大革命的决定，说出了我们的心里话，为我们广大工农兵撑腰，是文化大革命中横扫一切牛鬼蛇神的有力武器。党中央的决定是灯塔，是指南针，是方向盘，是鼓舞我们前进的动力。党中央的决定是毛泽东思想的体现，我要坚决照党中央和毛主席的指示办事，在无产阶级文化大革命运动中，敢字当头，不断革命，将无产阶级文化大革命进行到底！
              北京第一棉纺织厂工人　段雅茹
