Title: The Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Hosts Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-08-06
Edition: 5

The Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Vice Chairman Lian Guan hosted banquets on August 4th and 5th to respectively entertain the Japanese Socialist Party parliamentary delegation led by Tate Kenjiro and the third group of Japanese Socialist Party activists led by Yamamoto Yutaro.

### 我外交学会宴请日本客人

1966-08-06
第5版()
专栏：

    我外交学会宴请日本客人
    据新华社五日讯　中国人民外交学会副会长连贯在八月四日和五日晚设宴，分别招待以楯兼次郎为首的日本社会党国会议员访华团和以山本勇太郎为首的第三批日本社会党活动家访华代表团。
