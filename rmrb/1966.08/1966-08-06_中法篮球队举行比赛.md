Title: Chinese and French Basketball Teams Hold a Match
Author:
Date: 1966-08-06
Edition: 5

The Chinese men's basketball team won against the French men's basketball team with a score of 66 to 53 tonight.

The match took place at the Beijing Workers' Gymnasium. The 16,000 spectators in the packed stands of the capital continuously applauded the excellent performances of both teams.

At the end of the first half, the home team led by 13 points with a score of 33 to 20. After the first half, the home team's players, following the teachings of Chairman Mao, remained humble in victory and undaunted in defeat, starting from zero to strive for the final victory. In the second half, although the home team's players once faced a fierce counterattack from the opposing team, narrowing the score to just a one-point difference, they remained composed and ultimately secured the victory.

### 中法篮球队举行比赛

1966-08-06
第5版()
专栏：

    中法篮球队举行比赛
    据新华社五日讯　中国男子篮球队今晚以六十六比五十三赢了法国男子篮球队。
    这场比赛在北京工人体育馆进行。坐满体育馆看台的首都一万六千人接连不断为双方运动员的精彩表演报以响亮的掌声。
    在上半时结束时，主队以三十三比二十的十三分优势居于领先地位。上半时比赛结束后，主队队员遵循毛主席的教导，胜不骄、败不馁，从零开始去争取最后胜利。在下半时，主队队员虽然曾一度由于对方的猛烈反攻，使比分缩小到仅以一分之差，仍然沉着应战，终于最后取得胜利。
