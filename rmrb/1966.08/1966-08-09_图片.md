Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Delegations and Supports Struggles
Author:
Date: 1966-08-09
Edition: 6

On August 8, Premier Zhou Enlai received the entire delegation of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic Cultural Delegation led by Fan Yupu. The photo shows a group photo taken during the reception.

New China News Agency photographer

On the same day, American black leader Robert F. Williams, after speaking at the capital's rally of people from all walks of life opposing American imperialism and supporting the struggle of American blacks against racial discrimination, shook hands warmly with Premier Zhou Enlai.

New China News Agency photographer
