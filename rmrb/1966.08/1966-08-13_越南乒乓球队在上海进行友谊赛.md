Title: Vietnamese Ping-Pong Team Plays Friendly Match in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1966-08-13
Edition: 4

The Vietnamese ping-pong team from the heroic land of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, which is on the frontline of the struggle against American imperialism, played a friendly match against the Shanghai Youth Ping-Pong Team at the Shanghai Stadium on the evening of the 10th. The match was warmly welcomed by over 3,000 spectators.

Throughout the three-and-a-half-hour match, the atmosphere was filled with revolutionary and combative friendship. The tenacious fighting spirit and excellent skills of the athletes from both sides continually earned them thunderous applause from the audience. The men's six matches and the women's four matches resulted in the home team winning four to two and four to zero, respectively.

Before the match, Shanghai Vice-Mayor Jin Zhonghua met with the visiting team's leader, Nguyen Duc Tho, and shook hands with and posed for a group photo with the athletes from both sides on the court.

The Vietnamese Democratic Republic Ping-Pong Team arrived in Shanghai from Guangzhou on the 7th of this month.

### 越南乒乓球队在上海进行友谊赛

1966-08-13
第4版()
专栏：

    越南乒乓球队在上海进行友谊赛
    据新华社上海十一日电　来自反美斗争最前线英雄国土的越南民主共和国乒乓球队，十日晚上在上海市体育馆同上海市青年乒乓球队进行了一场友谊比赛，受到全场三千多人的热烈欢迎。
    在这场历时三个多钟头的比赛中，场上始终洋溢着革命的、战斗的友谊。双方运动员的顽强奋战精神和良好球艺，不断赢得全场响亮的掌声。男子的六盘和女子的四盘比赛结果，主队选手分别以四比二和四比○获胜。
    比赛前，上海市副市长金仲华会见了客队领队阮得寿，并到球场上同双方运动员一一握手和合影。
    越南民主共和国乒乓球队是本月七日从广州到达上海的。
