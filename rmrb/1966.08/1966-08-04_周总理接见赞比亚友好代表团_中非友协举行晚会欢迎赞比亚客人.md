Title: Premier Zhou Receives Zambian Friendly Delegation; China-Africa Friendship Association Holds Evening Event to Welcome Zambian Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-08-04
Edition: 5

Premier Zhou today afternoon received Zambian Minister of Commerce and Industry Chimbah and the entire delegation of the Zambian Friendly Delegation led by him, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Lin Haicun, Nan Hanchen, Lei Renmin, and Hou Yefeng.

After the reception, Premier Zhou hosted a banquet to entertain the Zambian guests.

The China-Africa Friendship Association held an evening event today to welcome the Zambian Friendly Delegation led by Minister of Commerce and Industry Chimbah.

At the event, the Zambian guests, accompanied by Vice Chairman of the China-Africa Friendship Association Nan Hanchen, Acting Minister of Foreign Trade Lin Haicun, and others, watched the ballet "The Red Detachment of Women" performed by the Central Opera and Ballet Theater.

### 周总理接见赞比亚友好代表团  中非友协举行晚会欢迎赞比亚客人

1966-08-04
第5版()
专栏：

    周总理接见赞比亚友好代表团
    中非友协举行晚会欢迎赞比亚客人
    新华社三日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见赞比亚工商部长钦巴和由他率领的赞比亚友好代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有林海云、南汉宸、雷任民、侯野烽。
    接见以后，周总理设便宴招待赞比亚客人。
    新华社三日讯　中国非洲人民友好协会今天举行晚会，欢迎以工商部长钦巴为首的赞比亚友好代表团。
    在晚会上，赞比亚客人由中非友协副会长南汉宸、对外贸易部代理部长林海云等陪同，观看了中央歌剧舞剧院芭蕾舞剧团演出的芭蕾舞剧《红色娘子军》。
