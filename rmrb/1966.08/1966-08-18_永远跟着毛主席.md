Title: Forever Following Chairman Mao
Author: Zhu Weirong
Date: 1966-08-18
Edition: 6

Through countless storms of struggle,
Our hearts are close to Chairman Mao!
Chairman Mao is the beacon of the world's revolutionary people,
Mao Zedong Thought stands tall and firm!
We hold Chairman Mao's works in our hands,
And fear no storms or waves.
Our revolutionary spirit soars higher than the sky,
We fear no mountains of knives or seas of fire!
With great Mao Zedong Thought as our weapon,
We can fill the seas!
We can move the mountains!
We fear no evil spirits stirring up trouble,
The "thousand-jin hammer" is in our hands!
Mao Zedong Thought is our magic weapon for victory,
Chairman Mao leads us from victory to victory!
We will forever follow Chairman Mao,
And dedicate our lives to revolution until the end.
We will forever follow Chairman Mao,
And create a new, fiery century!

### 永远跟着毛主席
朱维荣
1966-08-18
第6版()
专栏：

　　永远跟着毛主席
　　朱维荣
　　穿过多少斗争的风云，
　　我们的心呵紧贴毛主席！
　　毛主席是世界革命人民的灯塔，
　　毛泽东思想顶天立地！
　　咱把毛主席著作拿手里，
　　惊涛骇浪路不迷。
　　革命的志气比天高，
　　刀山火海何所惧！
　　伟大的毛泽东思想作武器，
　　大海咱能填！
　　高山咱能移！
　　不怕牛鬼蛇神兴妖风，
　　“千钧棒”握在咱手里！
　　毛泽东思想是我们胜利的法宝，
　　毛主席领导我们从胜利走向胜利！
　　我们永远跟着毛主席，
　　一辈子干革命干到底。
　　我们永远跟着毛主席，
　　开创火红的新世纪！
