Title: Workers of Zhejiang Haining County Cement Products Factory Combine Industry and Agriculture
Author:
Date: 1966-08-06
Edition: 1

Workers of the Zhejiang Haining County Cement Products Factory, who combine industry and agriculture, are pouring and tamping cement culverts for irrigation. They are supporting agriculture. Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter.
