Date: 1966-08-30

	Notice

	According to the instructions of the Central Committee of the Party, the People's Daily will start publishing four editions daily from September 1, 1966. After the change, the People's Daily will continue to hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, face the broad masses of workers, peasants, and soldiers, vigorously propagate Mao Zedong Thought, propagate the broad masses of workers, peasants, and soldiers in studying and applying Chairman Mao's works, propagate the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, propagate the Party's policies and guidelines, reflect the great achievements of the socialist revolution and socialist construction, and propagate the excellent domestic and international situation. Page layout: The first page will feature editorials, important news, and dispatches; the second page will cover domestic political, economic, cultural, and educational news, commentaries, and dispatches; the third page will include international news, dispatches, briefs, commentaries, and current affairs speeches; the fourth page will feature various special and supplementary editions.

	After the People's Daily starts publishing four editions, the monthly subscription price will be 1.20 yuan. Readers are welcome to subscribe at local post offices.

	People's Daily, August 30, 1966

### 启事

1966-08-30
第6版()
专栏：

    启事
    根据党中央的指示，《人民日报》从一九六六年九月一日起，改为每天出四版。改版以后，《人民日报》将继续高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，面向广大工农兵群众，大力宣传毛泽东思想，宣传广大工农兵群众活学活用毛主席著作，宣传无产阶级文化大革命，宣传党的方针政策，反映社会主义革命和社会主义建设的伟大成就，宣传国内外大好形势。版面安排：第一版，社论、重要新闻和通讯；第二版，国内政治、经济、文教等方面的消息、评论、通讯；第三版，国际新闻、通讯、简讯、评论和时事讲话；第四版，各种专刊和副刊。
    《人民日报》改出四版后，每月定价为一元二角。欢迎读者到各地邮局订购。
                人民日报社一九六六年八月三十日
