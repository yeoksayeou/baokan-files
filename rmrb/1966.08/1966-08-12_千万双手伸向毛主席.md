Title: Ten Million Hands Reach Out to Chairman Mao
Author: Beijing Quyi Troupe
Date: 1966-08-12
Edition: 3

	Red flags are raised high, our hearts are stirred,
	Our steps are firm, our spirits are soaring,
	We rush to Zhongnanhai to report our joy,
	To present our resolve to Chairman Mao and the Party Central Committee.

	We firmly support the decision of the Cultural Revolution,
	We resolutely carry out the Sixteen Points.
	The Sixteen Points illuminate our path forward,
	The Sixteen Points give us confidence, resolve, and strength.

	We want to send our confidence, resolve, and wishes
	To Chairman Mao as soon as possible.

	Dear Chairman Mao, we will stand firm,
	In the class struggle, we will be brave and strong.

	As we approach the gates of Zhongnanhai,
	Suddenly, a roar of joy fills the air:
	"Chairman Mao has come, Chairman Mao has come!"
	The excited crowd surges like a vast ocean.

	Look, indeed, happiness descends from the heavens,
	Before our eyes appears the red sun.

	Our most beloved leader, Chairman Mao, comes to the reception stand,
	Chairman Mao stands on the platform.

	Chairman Mao stands among the people,
	The people gather around Chairman Mao.

	Our great leader is in excellent health,
	Spirited, with a radiant face.

	People's hearts are filled with happiness,
	Chairman Mao's health is the guarantee of the people's happiness.

	Chairman Mao warmly waves and greets the crowd,
	His affectionate gaze stirs our hearts.

	Chairman Mao says, "Comrades, hello! Comrades, hello!"
	He repeatedly walks to both sides of the platform.

	Chairman Mao says, "You must care about national affairs, you must carry the Cultural Revolution to the end!"
	We unanimously say: We will, we will!

	Ten million people look up,
	Ten million people bask in the sunshine of happiness,
	Ten million hands reach out to Chairman Mao,
	Ten million red hearts turn to the sun.

	"Long live, long live, ten thousand years!" 
	Our great leader lives forever!

	Chairman Mao, you understand the people's wishes the most,
	Your reception gives us boundless strength,
	You give us great encouragement,
	You always guide us in the right direction.

	We will definitely heed your words and care about national affairs,
	We will definitely arm ourselves with your thoughts.

	We will definitely carry the Cultural Revolution to the end,
	We will definitely overthrow all the black gangs,
	Overthrow those in power who are taking the capitalist road within the Party,
	Sweep away all the ghosts and monsters,
	Transform our worldview, eliminate old thoughts,
	Establish new proletarian customs.

	On this great day of August 10,
	We will forever remember it in our hearts.

	We will forever remember your kind instructions,
	We will forever remember your encouraging gaze.

	In revolutionary struggles, we dare to be pioneers,
	We dare to brave the flames, we dare to climb the mountains,
	We must become workers, peasants, intellectuals, and soldiers,
	In the great school of revolution, we will grow and be tempered.

	Forever holding high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought,
	Forward, forward, following you to the forefront of the revolution!

### 千万双手伸向毛主席
北京曲艺团
1966-08-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　千万双手伸向毛主席
　　北京曲艺团
　　红旗高举，心情激荡，
　　步伐矫健，斗志昂扬，
　　我们奔向中南海去报喜，
　　要把决心汇报给毛主席、党中央。
　　我们坚决拥护无产阶级文化大革命的决定，
　　我们坚决执行十六条总纲。
　　总纲照亮了我们前进的方向，
　　总纲给我们带来信心、决心和力量。
　　我们要把自己的信心、决心和愿望，
　　早早地送到毛主席身旁。
　　亲爱的毛主席，我们一定站稳立场，
　　在阶级斗争中勇敢坚强。
　　当我们走近中南海门前，
　　猛然间一片欢声震天响：
　　“毛主席来了，毛主席来了！”
　　激动的人群象奔腾的海洋。
　　看，果然是无比幸福从天降，
　　眼前出现了红太阳。
　　我们最最最敬爱的领袖毛主席来到群众接待站，
　　毛主席屹立在讲台上。
　　毛主席站在群众之中，
　　群众簇拥在主席身旁。
　　我们的伟大领袖身体十分健康，
　　精神焕发，满面红光。
　　人们心头涌起幸福的感受，
　　毛主席的健康是革命人民幸福的保障。
　　毛主席满腔热情向群众招手致意，
　　亲切的目光照得人们心情激荡。
　　毛主席说：“同志们好，同志们好！”
　　不断走向讲台两旁。
　　毛主席说：“你们要关心国家大事，要把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底！”
　　我们异口同声说：一定这样，一定这样！
　　千万人抬头仰望，
　　千万人沐浴着幸福的阳光，
　　千万双手伸向毛主席，
　　千万颗红心向太阳。
　　“万岁，万岁，万万岁！”
　　我们伟大的领袖万寿无疆！
　　毛主席，您最理解群众的愿望，
　　您的接待给了我们无穷力量，
　　您给了我们巨大的鼓舞，
　　您时刻给我们指引方向。
　　我们一定听您的话关心国家大事，
　　我们一定用您的思想把自己武装。
　　我们一定把文化大革命进行到底，
　　我们一定斗垮一切黑帮，
　　打倒党内走资本主义道路当权派，
　　把一切牛鬼蛇神横扫光，
　　改造世界观，清除旧思想，
　　树立无产阶级新风尚。
　　八月十日这个伟大日子，
　　我们永远永远记在心上。
　　我们永远记住您亲切的教导，
　　我们永远记着您鼓励的目光。
　　在革命斗争中敢当闯将，
　　火海敢闯，刀山敢上，
　　要做到亦工亦农亦文亦武，
　　在革命的大学校中锻炼成长。
　　永远高举着毛泽东思想伟大红旗，
　　向前，向前，跟着您奔向革命最前方！
