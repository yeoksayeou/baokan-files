Title: China and Vietnam Sign Scientific Cooperation Execution Plan
Author:
Date: 1966-08-24
Edition: 4

China and Vietnam Sign Scientific Cooperation Execution Plan

New China News Agency: The 1966-1967 execution plan for scientific cooperation between the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the State Committee for Science and Technology and the Academy of Social Sciences of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam was signed in Beijing on August 21.

Representatives of both sides who signed the execution plan were: Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Guo Moruo, and Deputy Director of the Vietnamese State Committee for Science and Technology, Li Ke. Relevant officials from China and the Vietnamese Ambassador to China, Li Zhongshi, attended the signing ceremony.

After the signing, Guo Moruo hosted a banquet to see off the Vietnamese guests.

### 中越签订科学合作执行计划

1966-08-24
第4版()
专栏：

    中越签订科学合作执行计划
    新华社讯　中国科学院和越南民主共和国国家科学技术委员会、社会科学院科学合作一九六六年到一九六七年执行计划，二十一日在北京签订。
    代表双方在执行计划上签字的是：中国科学院副院长竺可桢，越南国家科学技术委员会副主任黎克。我国有关方面负责人和越南驻中国大使馆参赞黎终始出席了签字仪式。
    签字后，竺可桢设宴欢送越南客人。
