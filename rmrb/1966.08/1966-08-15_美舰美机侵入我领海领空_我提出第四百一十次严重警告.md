Title: U.S. Ships and Aircraft Intrude into Our Territorial Waters and Airspace

Author:

Date: 1966-08-15

Edition: 6

U.S. ships and aircraft intrude into our territorial waters and airspace

We issue the 410th serious warning

New China News Agency, August 14th:

On August 14th, from 1:05 AM to 4:03 AM and from 12:26 PM to 7:40 PM, one U.S. military vessel intruded into our territorial waters off Pingtan and Huangqi in Fujian Province on two separate occasions. On the same day, from 9:19 AM to 9:27 AM, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace above the waters off Yulin, west of Hainan Island in Guangdong Province. In response to this military provocation by U.S. military vessels and aircraft, the spokesperson for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the Ministry, issues the 410th serious warning.

### 美舰美机侵入我领海领空　我提出第四百一十次严重警告

1966-08-15
第6版()
专栏：

    美舰美机侵入我领海领空
　　我提出第四百一十次严重警告
    新华社十四日讯　八月十四日一时零五分至四时零三分、十二时二十六分至十九时四十分，美国军舰一艘，先后两次侵入我福建省平潭、黄岐以东地区领海。同日九时十九分至九时二十七分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省海南岛榆林西南地区领海上空。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百一十次严重警告。
