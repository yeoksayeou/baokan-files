Title: Chairman Mao's "On Practice" Published in Sinhala in Ceylon
Author:
Date: 1966-08-12
Edition: 6

Chairman Mao Zedong's work "On Practice" has been published in Sinhala by the Colombo People's Publishing House and is now on sale here.

The book was translated by Ananda Kumara, Secretary-General of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association.

### 毛主席《实践论》僧伽罗文版在锡兰出版

1966-08-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　毛主席《实践论》僧伽罗文版在锡兰出版
    新华社科伦坡十一日电　毛泽东主席的著作《实践论》的僧伽罗文版，已经由科伦坡人民出版社出版，现在已在这里发行。
    这本书是由锡中友好协会秘书长阿南达·库马拉翻译的。
