Title: Premier Zhou Receives Vietnamese Government Economic Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-08-30
Edition: 1

Premier Zhou received the Vietnamese Government Economic Delegation led by Vice Premier Le Thanh Nghi of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam this afternoon and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Vice Premiers Chen Yi and Li Xiannian of the State Council, as well as relevant officials Fang Yi, Li Tianyou, Han Nianlong, Li Qiang, and Yang Lin.

The head of the permanent delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam in China, Chen Wencheng, was also present.

### 周总理接见越南政府经济代表团

1966-08-30
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见越南政府经济代表团
    新华社二十九日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见由越南民主共和国政府副总理黎清毅率领的越南政府经济代表团全体成员，和他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有国务院副总理陈毅、李先念，以及有关方面负责人方毅、李天佑、韩念龙、李强、杨琳等。
    越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长陈文成也在座。
