Title: The First Asian New Force Games to be Held in Cambodia in November
Author:
Date: 1966-08-17
Edition: 4

Many sports organizations from Asian countries have enthusiastically expressed their desire to participate in the First Asian New Force Games, which will be held in Cambodia on November 25 this year. By August 12, fourteen countries and regions have confirmed their participation in the games.

These countries and regions include: Palestine, Saudi Arabia, Ceylon, Iraq, Lebanon, Syria, Japan, Laos, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the People's Republic of China, Pakistan, Indonesia, and Yemen.

Prince Yessalor, the representative of the Royal Government of Cambodia to the Sports Bureau and Vice Chairman of the National Organizing Committee for the First Asian New Force Games, told our reporter: "Despite the despicable plots and pressures exerted by the International Olympic Committee and some international sports federations to incite certain countries not to participate in the Phnom Penh Games, I believe that the First Asian New Force Games will achieve great success."

### 第一届亚洲新运会十一月在柬举行　已有十四个国家和地区的体育组织表示参加

1966-08-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　第一届亚洲新运会十一月在柬举行
　　已有十四个国家和地区的体育组织表示参加
    新华社金边十三日电　许多亚洲国家的体育组织热烈表示要参加将于今年十一月二十五日在柬埔寨举行的第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会。到八月十二日为止，肯定表示要参加这次运动会的已经有十四个国家和地区的体育组织。
    这些国家和地区是：巴勒斯坦、沙特阿拉伯、锡兰、伊拉克、黎巴嫩、叙利亚、日本、老挝、越南民主共和国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、中华人民共和国、巴基斯坦、印度尼西亚和也门。
    柬埔寨王国政府驻体育局代表兼第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会全国组织委员会副主席埃萨罗亲王向本社记者说：“尽管国际奥林匹克委员会和某些国际体育联合会策划卑鄙阴谋和施加压力来煽动一些国家不参加金边的运动会，但是我认为，第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会必将取得巨大胜利。”
