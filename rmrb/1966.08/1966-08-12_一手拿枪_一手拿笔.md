Title: One Hand Holding a Gun, One Hand Holding a Pen
Author: Dù Chūnkē
Date: 1966-08-12
Edition: 3

	The Party Central Committee's Sixteen Points decision,
	Like thunder roaring across the sky in spring;
	Every word is truth,
	Every sentence resonates in our hearts.

	We are the sentinels at Tiananmen Gate,
	Guarding day and night by Chairman Mao's side.
	Chairman Mao's affectionate instructions,
	Are always in our hearts.

	Firmly carry out the Party Central Committee's decision,
	Swear to be the vanguard of the Cultural Revolution.
	Grip the two guns in our hands,
	Sweep away all the ghosts and monsters!

	Standing at Tiananmen, looking out in all directions,
	Doing revolution, braving storms,
	Forever following Chairman Mao,
	Forever following the Communist Party.

### 一手拿枪　一手拿笔
杜春科
1966-08-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　一手拿枪　一手拿笔
　　解放军某部守卫天安门的战士  杜春科
　　党中央十六条决定，
　　好象春雷震天响；
　　字字是真理，
　　句句说在我们心坎上。
　　我们是天安门前的哨兵，
　　日夜守卫在毛主席身旁。
　　毛主席的亲切教导，
　　时时刻刻记在心上。
　　坚决执行党中央的决定，
　　誓做文化大革命的闯将。
　　紧握手中的两枝枪，
　　把一切牛鬼蛇神全扫光！
　　站在天安门，放眼看四方，
　　干革命，闯风浪，
　　永远跟着毛主席，
　　永远跟着共产党。
