Title: Representatives from Various Countries Arrive in Beijing After the Twelfth World Conference to Ban the Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs
Author:
Date: 1966-08-07
Edition: 2

Representatives from Australia, New Zealand, Ceylon, Basutoland, Belgium, and Sudan, who attended the Twelfth World Conference to Ban the Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and then came to Beijing, posed for a group photo with Guo Moruo, Liao Chengzhi, Liu Ningyi, and others who went to the airport to welcome them. Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter.
