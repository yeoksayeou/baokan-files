Title: We Carry the Banner of Revolution
Author: Fu Jincheng
Date: 1966-08-17
Edition: 2

	Comrades gather around the radio,
	Tuning in to Beijing's broadcast.
	Thousands of mountains and rivers cannot block,
	The Party's voice echoes in our ears.
	The Central Committee's communiqué is like a beacon,
	Illuminating our hearts and minds.
	What a timely revolution,
	Each drop falls into our hearts.
	The Party understands the people's hearts,
	The communiqué guides us.
	Soldiers listen to the Party's words,
	We are the vanguards of the three great revolutions.
	Chairman Mao, the Central Committee,
	Your teachings we will never forget.
	We forge ahead on the revolutionary path,
	We carry the banner of revolution.
	Mao Zedong Thought is our guide,
	Riding horses and whipping forward, nothing can stop us.
	The revolutionary tide surges skyward,
	Sweeping away all monsters and demons.

### 革命大旗我们扛
傅金城
1966-08-17
第2版()
专栏：

　　革命大旗我们扛
　　解放军某部战士　傅金城
　　战友围着收音机，
　　朝着北京调波长。
　　千山万水隔不断，
　　党的声音耳边响。
　　党中央公报象明灯，
　　照得咱心明眼又亮。
　　好一场革命的及时雨，
　　点点洒在心坎上。
　　党最知咱群众的心，
　　公报给咱指方向。
　　战士最听党的话，
　　三大革命当闯将。
　　毛主席啊党中央，
　　您的教导永不忘。
　　革命道路我们闯，
　　革命大旗我们扛。
　　毛泽东思想作指南，
　　跃马扬鞭无阻挡。
　　革命浪潮冲天起，
　　牛鬼蛇神一扫光。
