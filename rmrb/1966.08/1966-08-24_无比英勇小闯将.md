Title: The Extremely Brave Little Vanguard
Author: Bái Shuǐ
Date: 1966-08-24
Edition: 3

Red armbands, red thoughts,
Red Guards open up new battlefields.
Guns aimed at the old world,
The march forward sounds loud and clear!

The eyes of the red youths are the brightest,
Armed with Mao Zedong Thought,
They dare to brave knife mountains and fire seas,
Marching straight ahead without hindrance.

New names, new signs,
The capital is taking on a new look.
The new wave of the Cultural Revolution
Sweeps through every street in Beijing.

Old customs and bad habits are eliminated,
The roots of revisionism are completely uprooted!
Monsters and demons are terrified,
Revolutionary people clap and laugh.

Songs ring out, spirits soar,
Extremely brave little vanguard.

You are the future of the motherland,
You are the hope of humanity,
Your rebellious spirit is the most noble.
Workers, peasants, and soldiers resolutely stand with you,
Sweep away all the garbage of the old society,
Build a brand new world!

### 无比英勇小闯将
白水
1966-08-24
第3版()
专栏：

   无比英勇小闯将
   解放军某部　白水
   红袖章，红思想，
   红卫兵开辟新战场。
   枪口对准旧世界，
   进军号吹得嗒嗒响！
   红色小将眼最亮，
   毛泽东思想来武装，
   刀山火海都敢闯，
   一往直前无阻挡。
   新名称，新字号，
   首都换上新容貌。
   文化革命新浪潮，
   席卷北京各街道。
   陈规陋习都铲除，
   修正主义根子全挖掉！
   牛鬼蛇神吓破胆，
   革命人民拍手笑。
   歌声响，斗志昂，
   无比英勇小闯将。
   你们是祖国的未来，
   你们是人类的希望，
   你们的造反精神最高尚。
   工农兵坚决和你们并肩战斗，
   扫除一切旧社会的垃圾，
   把崭新的世界建创！
