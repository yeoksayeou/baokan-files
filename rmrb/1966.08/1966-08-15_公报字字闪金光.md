Title: Every Word in the Communiqué Shines with Golden Light
Author: 北京曲艺团文化革命委员会 (Beijing Quyi Troupe Cultural Revolution Committee)
Date: 1966-08-15
Edition: 4

	Every word in the communiqué shines with golden light
	(Duet)
	Beijing Quyi Troupe Cultural Revolution Committee

	甲: A thunderous spring thunder echoes through the sky,
	乙: A thousand beams of golden light illuminate the four directions,
	甲: The Eighth Plenum of the Eighth Central Committee of the Party issues a communiqué,
	乙: Every word and sentence shines with golden light,
	甲: The communiqué embodies Mao Zedong Thought,
	合: It is the general guideline for the actions of 700 million people.
	甲: From the grasslands of Inner Mongolia to Hainan Island,
	乙: From the Himalayas to the Heilongjiang River,
	甲: Revolutionary masses unanimously support,
	乙: People of all ethnic groups sing praises together.
	甲: The communiqué speaks directly to our hearts,
	乙: The communiqué is our shared ideal and aspiration,
	甲: The communiqué has boosted our revolutionary confidence,
	乙: The communiqué has added boundless strength to us.
	甲: In the past four years, Chairman Mao's ten major decisions,
	合: Have helped us overcome difficulties, dangers, and stormy waves.
	甲: The whole country has seen a comprehensive leap forward,
	乙: Everywhere is a hot and revolutionary scene.
	甲: The communiqué issues a great call,
	乙: The Party Central Committee points out the direction forward.
	甲: Mao Zedong Thought is the spiritual food of revolutionary people,
	乙: It is the rain and sunshine that nurtures our growth!
	甲: It is a mirror, a microscope, and a telescope,
	乙: It exposes revisionism with nowhere to hide!
	甲: Mao Zedong Thought is a spiritual atomic bomb,
	乙: It possesses unparalleled powerful strength!
	甲: Study and apply Chairman Mao's works,
	乙: The PLA is our shining example,
	甲: Focus on the "use" of Chairman Mao's works,
	乙: Everyone arms themselves with Mao Zedong Thought.
	甲: Turn every industry into a great school of Mao Zedong Thought,
	乙: Let new people of communism grow rapidly.
	甲: We will forever follow Chairman Mao,
	乙: We will forever follow the Communist Party.
	甲: The great Party, the glorious Party,
	乙: The wise Party, the correct Party.
	甲: Chairman Mao personally founded it,
	乙: Mao Zedong Thought nurtured it.
	甲: It has gone through the longest and most arduous years,
	乙: It has weathered the most intense and complex storms,
	甲: It has led the people to topple the three big mountains,
	乙: The great nation stands tall in the East.
	甲: The great people stand tall and strong,
	乙: The great army is invincible.
	甲: The great nation, the great people,
	乙: The great army, the great Party,
	合: The great leader Chairman Mao,
	甲: Comes to steer and navigate,
	乙: Self-reliance,
	甲: Strive for strength,
	合: We will definitely fulfill the mission history has bestowed upon us,
	甲: We will definitely not disappoint the expectations of the world's people.
	乙: Overcome obstacles, difficulties, shortcomings, and mistakes,
	合: 700 million people unite to form an iron wall.
	甲: Walk the path no one has walked before,
	乙: Dare to forge ahead;
	甲: Climb the peaks no one has climbed before,
	乙: Be brave and tenacious.
	甲: Carry out the Cultural Revolution to the end,
	乙: Sweep away the rightist elements completely,
	甲: Carry out the socialist revolution to the end,
	乙: The iron fortress will forever shine brightly.
	甲: We will liberate Taiwan,
	乙: We will strengthen national defense,
	甲: If the U.S. imperialists dare to invade,
	合: We will definitely and thoroughly wipe them out!
	甲: Mao Zedong Thought is our guiding light,
	乙: The communiqué is our action guideline,
	甲: Long live Mao Zedong, long live the Communist Party,
	乙: Long live the invincible Mao Zedong Thought.
	合: Long live, long live, ten thousand years!
	Mao Zedong Thought is the eternal red sun that never sets.

### 公报字字闪金光
北京曲艺团文化革命委员会
1966-08-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　公报字字闪金光
　　（对口词）
　　北京曲艺团文化革命委员会
　　甲：一声春雷震天响，
　　乙：万道金光照四方，
　　甲：党的八届十一中全会发公报，
　　乙：字字句句闪金光，
　　甲：公报体现了毛泽东思想，
　　合：是七亿人民行动的总纲。
　　甲：从内蒙古草原到海南岛，
　　乙：从喜马拉雅山到黑龙江，
　　甲：革命群众一致拥护，
　　乙：各族人民同声颂扬。
　　甲：公报句句说在我们心坎上，
　　乙：公报是我们共同的理想和愿望，
　　甲：公报增加了我们革命的信心，
　　乙：公报给我们增添了无穷力量。
　　甲：四年来毛主席英明决策十大项，
　　合：使我们战胜了艰难险阻和惊涛骇浪。
　　甲：全国出现了全面跃进的局面，
　　乙：到处是热气腾腾的革命景象。
　　甲：公报发出了伟大号召，
　　乙：党中央指明了前进的方向。
　　甲：毛泽东思想是革命人民的精神食粮，
　　乙：是抚育我们成长的雨露阳光！
　　甲：是照妖镜，显微镜，望远镜，
　　乙：照得修正主义无处躲藏！
　　甲：毛泽东思想是精神原子弹，
　　乙：具有无比强大的力量！
　　甲：活学活用毛主席著作，
　　乙：解放军是我们的光辉榜样，
　　甲：在“用”字上狠下功夫，
　　乙：人人用毛泽东思想来武装。
　　甲：把各行各业办成毛泽东思想的大学校，
　　乙：让共产主义新人迅速成长。
　　甲：我们永远跟着毛主席，
　　乙：永远跟着共产党。
　　甲：伟大的党，光荣的党，
　　乙：英明的党，正确的党。
　　甲：毛泽东同志亲手缔造，
　　乙：毛泽东思想培育成长。
　　甲：经历了最长期、最艰苦的岁月，
　　乙：闯过了最激烈、最复杂的狂风巨浪，
　　甲：领导着人民推倒了三座大山，
　　乙：伟大的国家屹立在世界的东方。
　　甲：伟大的人民顶天立地，
　　乙：伟大的军队无比坚强。
　　甲：伟大的国家，伟大的人民，
　　乙：伟大的军队，伟大的党，
　　合：伟大的领袖毛主席，
　　甲：来掌舵，来领航，
　　乙：自力更生，
　　甲：奋发图强，
　　合：一定完成历史赋予我们的使命，
　　甲：一定不辜负世界人民的期望。
　　乙：克服阻力、困难、缺点和错误，
　　合：七亿人民团结成铁壁铜墙。
　　甲：走前人未走过的道路，
　　乙：敢冲敢闯；
　　甲：攀登前人未攀登过的高峰，
　　乙：英勇顽强。
　　甲：把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底，
　　乙：把资产阶级右派分子彻底扫光，
　　甲：把社会主义革命进行到底，
　　乙：铁打的江山永远灿烂辉煌。
　　甲：我们要解放台湾，
　　乙：我们要加强国防，
　　甲：美帝国主义胆敢来侵犯，
　　合：一定把它坚决、彻底、干净、全部消灭光！
　　甲：毛泽东思想是我们指路明灯，
　　乙：全会公报是我们的行动总纲，
　　甲：万岁毛泽东，万岁共产党，
　　乙：万岁战无不胜的毛泽东思想。
　　合：万岁万岁万万岁！
