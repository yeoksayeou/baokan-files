Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi Meet with Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-08-10
Edition: 3

Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi met with the Pakistani Parliamentary delegation visiting China.

New China News Agency, August 9th: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi met with the Speaker of the Pakistani National Assembly, Abdul Jabbar Khan, and his wife, as well as members of the Pakistani Parliamentary delegation led by him, this morning. They had a cordial and friendly conversation.

Present at the meeting were Vice Chairman Guo Moruo and his wife Yu Liqun, Vice Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Lian Guan, and China's Ambassador to Pakistan Zhang Wenjin.

Pakistan's Ambassador to China, Sultan Muhammad Khan, was also present.

On August 9th, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi met with the Speaker of the Pakistani National Assembly, Abdul Jabbar Khan, and his wife, as well as members of the Pakistani Parliamentary delegation led by him. The photo shows a group photo taken during the meeting. Abdul Jabbar Khan, the Speaker, is the sixth person from the right in the front row.

### 周恩来总理　陈毅副总理会见巴基斯坦议会访华代表团

1966-08-10
第3版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理　陈毅副总理会见巴基斯坦议会访华代表团
    新华社九日讯　国务院总理周恩来和副总理陈毅，今天上午会见巴基斯坦国民议会议长阿卜杜勒·贾巴尔·汗和夫人，以及由他率领的巴基斯坦议会访华代表团的成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    会见时在座的，有郭沫若副委员长和夫人于立群，人大常委会副秘书长连贯，我国驻巴基斯坦大使章文晋。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆罕默德·汗也在座。（附图片）
    八月九日，周恩来总理、陈毅副总理会见巴基斯坦国民议会议长阿卜杜勒·贾巴尔·汗和夫人，以及由他率领的巴基斯坦议会访华代表团。图为会见时合影。前右六是阿卜杜勒·贾巴尔·汗议长。
