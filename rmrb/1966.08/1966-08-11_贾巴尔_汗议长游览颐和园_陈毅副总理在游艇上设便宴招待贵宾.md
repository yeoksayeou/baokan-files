Title: Speaker Jabbar Khan Tours the Summer Palace
Author:
Date: 1966-08-11
Edition: 6

Speaker of the National Assembly of Pakistan, Abdul Jabbar Khan, and his wife, along with the parliamentary delegation led by him, visited the Summer Palace today, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife Zhang Qie.

Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife hosted a banquet on a boat for the Pakistani guests. Chen Yi raised his glass and proposed a toast to the everlasting friendship between the peoples of China and Pakistan. Speaker Abdul Jabbar Khan also raised his glass and exclaimed, "Pakistan and China are brothers, long live Pak-China friendship!"

Artists from China performed songs praising the great leader Chairman Mao, such as "The Boat Sails on Because of the Helmsman," and Pakistani folk songs for the guests. The female delegate of the delegation, Tao Li Azad, sang "Unity is Strength" in Chinese. The singing and applause were continuous. When the banquet ended, Chen Yi and the Pakistani friends sang "Unity is Strength" together, and the boat was filled with joy.

Pakistan's Ambassador to China, Sultan Mohammad Khan, was also invited to tour the garden.

Vice Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Lian Guan, and his wife, as well as China's Ambassador to Pakistan, Zhang Wenjin, also accompanied the guests on their tour.

This morning, the Pakistani guests visited the Beijing Institute of Physical Education and were warmly welcomed by the faculty and students. Yesterday afternoon, the Pakistani guests, accompanied by Vice Chairman Guo Moruo and his wife Yu Liqun, toured the Forbidden City.

### 贾巴尔·汗议长游览颐和园　陈毅副总理在游艇上设便宴招待贵宾

1966-08-11
第6版()
专栏：

　　贾巴尔·汗议长游览颐和园
　　陈毅副总理在游艇上设便宴招待贵宾
    据新华社十日讯　巴基斯坦国民议会议长阿卜杜勒·贾巴尔·汗和夫人以及由他率领的巴基斯坦议会访华代表团，今天在陈毅副总理和夫人张茜的陪同下，游览了颐和园。
    陈毅副总理和夫人在游艇上设便宴招待巴基斯坦贵宾。陈毅副总理举杯，提议为中巴两国人民之间的永恒友谊干杯。阿卜杜勒·贾巴尔·汗议长也举杯欢呼：“巴中是兄弟，巴中友好万岁！”
    参加宴会的我国文艺工作者为贵宾们演唱了歌颂伟大领袖毛主席的歌曲《大海航行靠舵手》等，以及巴基斯坦的民间歌曲。代表团女团员陶丽·阿扎德用中国话演唱了《团结就是力量》。歌声和掌声此起彼伏。宴会结束时，陈毅副总理和巴基斯坦朋友高唱《团结就是力量》，游艇上一片欢腾。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆罕默德·汗也应邀游园。
    人大常委会副秘书长连贯和夫人，以及我国驻巴基斯坦大使章文晋等，也陪同贵宾游园。
    今天上午，巴基斯坦贵宾参观北京体育学院时，受到了师生们的热烈欢迎。昨天下午，巴基斯坦贵宾由郭沫若副委员长和夫人于立群陪同游览了故宫。
