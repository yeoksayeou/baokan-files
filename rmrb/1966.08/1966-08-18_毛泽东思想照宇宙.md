Title: Mao Zedong Thought Illuminates the Universe
Author: Zhèng Yànpíng
Date: 1966-08-18
Edition: 2

The sun rises in the east with colorful clouds,
The Central Committee issues a communiqué,
Thunder roars across ten thousand miles,
The sea rejoices and the mountains laugh.

Magpies circle the red flag,
Drums of joy accompany the beating heart,
Songs of joy sing the red sun to happiness,
Reports of joy weave into red clouds floating.

Holding the communiqué, looking towards Beijing,
Waves of emotion surge and rise high.
Chairman Mao's voice echoes by my ear,
Every word and sentence soars into the clouds.

The communiqué beats the drum of progress,
The communiqué sounds the charge,
The communiqué points out the path to the future,
The communiqué builds a bridge to heaven.

The red communiqué illuminates the red heart,
A generation of heroes with great aspirations,
Four volumes of magnificent writings shine with gold,
Standing tall and facing the wind and storms.

With one hand holding a gun and the other a pen,
Standing at the forefront of the struggle.
Whoever dares to raise their claws,
Will be thoroughly annihilated without mercy!

Looking ahead, the future is bright and clear,
The east wind blows fiercely,
Mao Zedong Thought illuminates the universe,
The four seas surge with a red tide.

### 毛泽东思想照宇宙
郑彦平
1966-08-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　毛泽东思想照宇宙
　　解放军北京某部　郑彦平
　　旭日东升彩霞飘，
　　中央全会发公报，
　　春雷轰鸣传万里，
　　大海欢呼高山笑。
　　喜鹊围着红旗飞，
　　喜鼓伴随红心跳，
　　喜歌唱得红日乐，
　　喜报织成红云飘。
　　手捧公报望北京，
　　心潮澎湃逐浪高。
　　毛主席声音耳边响，
　　字字句句冲云霄。
　　公报擂动跃进鼓，
　　公报吹响进军号，
　　公报指出阳关路，
　　公报搭起通天桥。
　　红色公报照红心，
　　一代英雄壮志豪，
　　雄文四卷金光闪，
　　顶天立地迎风暴。
　　一手拿枪一手笔，
　　站在斗争最前哨。
　　谁若胆敢伸魔爪，
　　彻底消灭决不饶！
　　展望前程明又亮，
　　东风阵阵起狂飚，
　　毛泽东思想照宇宙，
　　四海翻腾涌红潮。
