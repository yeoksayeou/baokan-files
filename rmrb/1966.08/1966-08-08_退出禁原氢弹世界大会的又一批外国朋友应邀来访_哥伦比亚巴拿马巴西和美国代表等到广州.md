Title: Another Batch of Foreign Friends Who Withdrew from the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs Arrive in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1966-08-08
Edition: 4

Today in the afternoon, another batch of foreign friends who withdrew from the 12th World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs arrived in Guangzhou. They were invited by the Chinese People's Association for Peace and Friendship with Foreign Countries to visit China. More than 200 people from various circles in Guangzhou and responsible persons from various people's organizations in Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City went to the station to give them a warm welcome.

The foreign friends who arrived in Guangzhou today are: Ricardo Samper from Colombia, Menendez from Panama, A. M. Galliano from Brazil, and representatives from the United States. Secretary-General Senanayake and his wife arrive in Beijing

New China News Agency, August 7th: The Secretary-General of the Permanent Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Writers' Conference, Senanayake, and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane from Shanghai tonight. Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Chu Tu-nan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, Wang Minzhao, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association, Wang Yinpu, member of the Sino-Sri Lanka Friendship Association, and writers Huang Gang and Yang Mo, among others.

When Senanayake and his wife left Shanghai today, Vice Mayor Jin Zhonghua and others went to the airport to give them a warm send-off.

### 退出禁原氢弹世界大会的又一批外国朋友应邀来访  哥伦比亚巴拿马巴西和美国代表等到广州

1966-08-08
第4版()
专栏：

    退出禁原氢弹世界大会的又一批外国朋友应邀来访
    哥伦比亚巴拿马巴西和美国代表等到广州
    新华社广州七日电　今天下午，又有一批退出第十二届禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会的外国朋友到广州。他们是应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来我国访问的。广州市各界二百多人和广东省、广州市各人民团体负责人到车站热烈欢迎。
    今天到达广州的外国朋友是：哥伦比亚代表里卡多·桑佩尔、巴拿马代表梅嫩德斯、巴西代表阿·米·加利亚努以及美国代表等。森纳那亚克秘书长和夫人到京
    新华社七日讯　亚非作家会议常设局秘书长森纳那亚克和夫人，今天晚上乘飞机从上海到达北京。到机场迎接的有中国人民对外文化友好协会会长楚图南，中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席王敏昭，中锡友协理事王荫圃，以及作家黄钢、杨沫等。
    森纳那亚克等今天离开上海的时候，上海市副市长金仲华等到机场热烈欢送。
