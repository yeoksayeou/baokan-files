Title: Excerpts from the Report of the Eleventh Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Party
Author:
Date: 1966-08-16
Edition: 3

We must have the great aspirations and courage of the proletariat, daring to tread paths where no one has gone before, daring to scale peaks that no one has reached. We must build socialist China, which accounts for a quarter of the world's population, into an iron fortress of the proletariat that will never change color.

### 摘自党的八届十一中全会公报

1966-08-16
第3版()
专栏：

　　我们一定要有无产阶级的雄心壮志，敢于走前人没有走过的道路，敢于攀登前人没有攀登过的高峰。我们一定要把占世界人口四分之一的社会主义中国建设好，使它成为无产阶级的铁打的江山，永不变色。
             　　——摘自党的八届十一中全会公报
