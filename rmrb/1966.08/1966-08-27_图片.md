Title: Shanghai Red Guards Promote Revolutionary Change
Author:
Date: 1966-08-27
Edition: 3

On August 23rd, Red Guards from various schools in Shanghai took to the streets. They posted revolutionary leaflets and big-character posters, promoting the destruction of the old and the establishment of the new. They also advocated for and assisted shop workers in changing shop names that carried feudal, capitalist, and revisionist elements.
