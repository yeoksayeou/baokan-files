Title: The Revolutionary Masses of the Capital Send a Victory Report to the Party Central Reception Station
Author:
Date: 1966-08-12
Edition: 2

The revolutionary masses of the capital send a victory report to the Party Central Reception Station

Chairman Mao, who believes in the masses the most, cares for the masses the most, supports the masses the most, and is the most revered by the Party Central and the most revered Chairman Mao:

We, the most revered leader and great teacher, Chairman Mao, met with the revolutionary masses of the capital and personally issued a great battle call to the people of the nation: "You must care about state affairs and carry out the Cultural Revolution of the proletariat to the end."

How moved our hearts are! Our great leader, Chairman Mao, is the greatest leader and helmsman of the Chinese revolution and the world revolution. Chairman Mao believes in the masses the most, cares for the masses the most, and supports the masses the most. We feel incredibly fortunate and infinitely proud to have such a wise and great leader!

The great leader, Chairman Mao, personally leads us in this unprecedented Cultural Revolution of the proletariat. Chairman Mao's instructions speak to our hearts and give us boundless strength.

Respected Chairman Mao, we pledge to you: We will not fear any difficulties or dangers and will steadfastly follow you through the storms and waves. We will closely follow you and resolutely carry out the Cultural Revolution of the proletariat to the end.

Long live the Cultural Revolution of the proletariat!

Long live the invincible Mao Zedong Thought!

Long live, long live, and ten thousand times long live the great leader, Chairman Mao!

	China Academy of Sciences
	Institute of Automation
	All Revolutionary Comrades

The greatest trust, the greatest care, the greatest encouragement

Most revered Party Central,

Most revered Chairman Mao:

When we heard the news that our most, most great leader, Chairman Mao, met with the revolutionary masses of the capital, our hearts boiled over and could not calm down for a long time. Millions of red hearts simultaneously issued the strongest call: Long live Chairman Mao! Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand times long live Chairman Mao!

We feel that this is the greatest trust, the greatest care, and the greatest encouragement from the Party Central and Chairman Mao for the revolutionary masses. It greatly boosts the morale of the revolutionary masses and greatly demoralizes the enemy. We will resolutely follow Chairman Mao's words, care about state affairs, and carry out the Cultural Revolution of the proletariat to the end!

Respected Chairman Mao, you are the red sun in our hearts. You always stand at the forefront of the revolution, guiding the revolutionary masses in the direction of progress. Now you have personally formulated the "Sixteen Points." This is the guiding document for the Cultural Revolution of the proletariat, the beacon of the Cultural Revolution. We will definitely study this decision diligently, execute it resolutely, propagate it faithfully, and defend it bravely. We vow to carry out the Cultural Revolution of the proletariat to the end!

Long live the most, most revered leader, Chairman Mao!

	Beijing Institute of Foreign Languages
	Year 3, Class 3
	Revolutionary Teachers and Students

We will study the decision, execute the decision, and defend the decision

Most revered Party Central,

Most revered Chairman Mao:

When we heard from the radio and saw in the newspapers the decision of the Party Central regarding the Cultural Revolution of the proletariat, the entire factory was in jubilation.

In this unprecedented, great Cultural Revolution of the proletariat, our factory, like the rest of the nation, has seen an unprecedented good situation. The broad masses of workers have responded resolutely to the great call of the Party Central and Chairman Mao, hoisting high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, and opening fire on all anti-party, anti-socialist, and anti-Mao Zedong Thought ghosts and snakes, and have already achieved initial victories.

Just as the entire workforce is full of fighting spirit and advancing with momentum, the Party Central has timely issued the wise decision regarding the Cultural Revolution of the proletariat, further pointing out the direction for our advance. We will definitely study it carefully, execute it, and defend it.

Respected Party Central, respected Chairman Mao, we pledge to you that in this magnificent Cultural Revolution of the proletariat, we will definitely remember your teachings, fight resolutely, fear no difficulties, and root out all ghosts and snakes, and resolutely carry out the Cultural Revolution of the proletariat to the end.

Long live the Communist Party of China!

Long live the great leader, Chairman Mao!

	All Revolutionary Workers of Beijing Special Steel Factory

Please rest assured a hundred times, a thousand times, ten thousand times

Most revered Party Central,

Most revered Chairman Mao:

After hearing the decision of the Party Central regarding the Cultural Revolution of the proletariat, all revolutionary masses of the Central Song and Dance Ensemble were extremely moved, and tears of joy welled up in our eyes. Comrades ran to tell each other, and cheers lasted for a long time.

Great Party Central, respected Chairman Mao! The fire of the Cultural Revolution has been ignited in the Central Song and Dance Ensemble. The revolutionary masses are spirited and full of fighting spirit. With Chairman Mao's instructions, we fear nothing, dare to climb mountains, and dare to leap into fires, engaging in a life-and-death struggle with the anti-party, anti-socialist, anti-Mao Zedong Thought clique, black lines, and all ghosts and snakes. The Party Central and Chairman Mao have issued the decision regarding the Cultural Revolution of the proletariat, like a lighthouse illuminating the revolutionary course of the proletariat, guiding our direction of advance.

Chairman Mao said: Policy and strategy are the lifeblood of the Party.

Most, most revered Party Central, most, most revered Chairman Mao: Please rest assured, a hundred times assured, a thousand times assured, ten thousand times assured, we will definitely act according to this decision and resolutely carry out the Cultural Revolution of the proletariat to the end. Victory is ours. The Cultural Revolution of the proletariat will definitely achieve the greatest victory.

Long live the Cultural Revolution of the proletariat!

Long live the great Communist Party of China!

Long live our great leader, Chairman Mao!

	All Revolutionary Masses of the Central Song and Dance Ensemble
	Central Song and Dance Ensemble Cultural Revolution Committee
