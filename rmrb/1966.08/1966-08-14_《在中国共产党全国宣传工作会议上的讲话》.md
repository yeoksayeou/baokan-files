Title: Speech at the National Propaganda Work Conference of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1966-08-14
Edition: 5

	Revisionism is a bourgeois ideology. Revisionists obliterate the distinctions between socialism and capitalism, and between the dictatorship of the proletariat and the dictatorship of the bourgeoisie. What they advocate is, in essence, not the socialist line but the capitalist line.

### 《在中国共产党全国宣传工作会议上的讲话》

1966-08-14
第5版()
专栏：

　　《在中国共产党全国宣传工作会议上的讲话》
　　修正主义是一种资产阶级思想。修正主义者抹杀社会主义和资本主义的区别，抹杀无产阶级专政和资产阶级专政的区别。他们所主张的，在实际上并不是社会主义路线，而是资本主义路线。
