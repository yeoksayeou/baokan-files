Title: On the Correct Handling of Contradictions Among the People
Author:
Date: 1966-08-16
Edition: 6

The proletariat aims to transform the world according to its worldview, and the bourgeoisie also aims to transform the world according to its worldview. In this regard, the question of who will ultimately prevail between socialism and capitalism has not yet been truly resolved.

### 《关于正确处理人民内部矛盾的问题》

1966-08-16
第6版()
专栏：

　　《关于正确处理人民内部矛盾的问题》
　　无产阶级要按照自己的世界观改造世界，资产阶级也要按照自己的世界观改造世界。在这一方面，社会主义和资本主义之间谁胜谁负的问题还没有真正解决。
