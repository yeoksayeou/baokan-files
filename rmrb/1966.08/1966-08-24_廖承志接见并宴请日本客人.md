Title: Liao Chengzhi Meets and Hosts a Banquet for Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-08-24
Edition: 4

Liao Chengzhi, president of the China-Japan Friendship Association, met and hosted a banquet for a delegation of Japanese educators led by Oohiro Naofusa, consisting of 56 members, this afternoon. Present at the banquet were Zhao Anbo, Wang Xiaoyun, Zhang Yu, and others.

### 廖承志接见并宴请日本客人

1966-08-24
第4版()
专栏：

    廖承志接见并宴请日本客人
    新华社二十三日讯　中日友好协会会长廖承志今天下午接见并设宴招待以大广直为首的日本教职员友好参观团一行五十六人。在座的有赵安博、王晓云、张雨等。
