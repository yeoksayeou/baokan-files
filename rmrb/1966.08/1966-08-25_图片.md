Title: The Storm of Anti-American Struggle in Latin America
Author:
Date: 1966-08-25
Edition: 6

Today, in the "backyard" of the United States—Latin America, the struggle against American imperialism is surging, wave after wave. The bloody suppression by American imperialism not only fails to quell the anti-American storm but instead ignites an even stronger resistance from the people of Latin America. The image depicts a scene from the anti-American demonstration by the people of the Dominican Republic.
