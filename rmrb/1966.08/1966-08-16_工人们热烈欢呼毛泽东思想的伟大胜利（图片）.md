Title: Workers Joyfully Celebrate the Great Victory of Mao Zedong Thought
Author: Zhao Zhifang
Date: 1966-08-16
Edition: 3

Workers joyfully celebrate the great victory of Mao Zedong Thought

The workers are enthusiastically cheering the great victory of Mao Zedong Thought. This painting vividly captures the spirit of the times, reflecting the workers' boundless enthusiasm and determination to follow the guidance of Mao Zedong Thought. The scene is filled with energy and passion, showcasing the workers' unity and resolve in building a strong socialist nation under the leadership of Chairman Mao. The artwork beautifully portrays the workers' devotion and commitment to the revolutionary cause, highlighting the profound impact of Mao Zedong Thought on the Chinese people.
