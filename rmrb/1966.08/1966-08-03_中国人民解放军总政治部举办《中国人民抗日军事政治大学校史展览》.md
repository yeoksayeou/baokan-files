Title: The General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Holds the Exhibition on the History of the Chinese People's Anti-Japanese Military and Political University
Author:
Date: 1966-08-03
Edition: 5

The General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army is holding an exhibition titled "The History of the Chinese People's Anti-Japanese Military and Political University."

The exhibition will open on August 1 and is tentatively scheduled to run for three months.

Visitors must obtain tickets in advance and visit in groups.

Visiting hours are from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM daily (last entry at 4:00 PM). The exhibition is closed on Mondays and on Fridays for study sessions.

The exhibition is located on the fourth floor, central hall, of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Museum.

Contact number: 86.8271
