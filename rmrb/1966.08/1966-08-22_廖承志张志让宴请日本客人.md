Title: Liao Chengzhi and Zhang Zhiran Host a Banquet for Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-08-22
Edition: 5

According to a report by Xinhua News Agency on the 21st, the president of the China-Japan Friendship Association, Liao Chengzhi, and the vice-president of the Chinese Political and Legal Society, Zhang Zhiran, held a banquet today to welcome the Japanese delegation visiting China, led by Makino Uchitaro.

### 廖承志张志让宴请日本客人

1966-08-22
第5版()
专栏：

    廖承志张志让宴请日本客人
    据新华社二十一日讯　中日友好协会会长廖承志、中国政治法律学会副会长张志让，今天举行宴会，欢迎以牧野内武人为首的日本访华代表团。
