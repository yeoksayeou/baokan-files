Title: Zambian Friendship Delegation Leaves Beijing to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1966-08-07
Edition: 6

The Zambian friendship delegation led by Zambian Minister of Commerce and Industry Chiba concluded its friendly visit to China and departed from Beijing by plane this afternoon to return home.

A thousand people from various circles in the capital gathered at the airport, shouting slogans such as "Long live the friendship between the peoples of China and Zambia" and "Long live the unity of the peoples of Asia and Africa," to warmly see off the Zambian guests.

At the airport, Minister Chiba bid farewell to Lin Haiyun, Nan Hanchen, Lei Renmin, Hou Yefeng and other friends who came to see them off. Minister Chiba accepted the flowers presented and before boarding the plane, he shouted "Goodbye" to the crowd.

### 赞比亚友好代表团离京回国

1966-08-07
第6版()
专栏：

    赞比亚友好代表团离京回国
    新华社六日讯　由赞比亚工商部长钦巴率领的赞比亚友好代表团，结束了对中国的友好访问，今天下午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    首都各界一千人齐集机场，高呼“中赞两国人民友谊万岁”、“亚非人民团结万岁”等口号，热烈欢送赞比亚贵宾。
    钦巴部长在机场上，向欢送他们的林海云、南汉宸、雷任民、侯野烽等亲切告别。钦巴部长接受了献花，在登上飞机之前，向全场群众高呼“再见”。
