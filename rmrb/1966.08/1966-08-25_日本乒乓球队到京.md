Title: The Japanese Table Tennis Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-08-25
Edition: 4

The Japanese table tennis team participating in the 1966 Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational, consisting of 19 members, led by the acting chairman of the Japanese Table Tennis Association, Koji Goto, arrived in Beijing by plane this evening.

The organizing committee chairman of this invitational, Chairman Chen Xian of the Chinese Table Tennis Association, and Chinese table tennis athletes went to the airport to welcome them.

The Japanese table tennis team has a total of 14 male and female athletes, including the men's national champion Nobuhiko Hasegawa and outstanding athlete Manji Fukushima; women's world champion Naoko Fukatsu, national champion Sachiko Morisawa, and outstanding athlete Kyoko Yamazaki.

### 日本乒乓球队到京

1966-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

    日本乒乓球队到京
    新华社二十四日讯　参加一九六六年北京国际乒乓球邀请赛的日本乒乓球代表队一行十九人，由日本乒乓球协会代理会长后藤钾二率领，今晚乘飞机到达北京。
    这次邀请赛组织委员会主席、中国乒乓球协会主席陈先以及我国的乒乓球运动员前往机场迎接。
    日本乒乓球队共有男、女十四名选手，其中包括男子全国冠军长谷川信彦和优秀选手福岛万治；女子世界冠军深津尚子、全国冠军森泽幸子和优秀选手山中教子等。
