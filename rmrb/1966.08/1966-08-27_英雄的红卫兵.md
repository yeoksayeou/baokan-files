Title: Heroic Red Guards
Author: Píngjūn
Date: 1966-08-27
Edition: 3

Heroic Red Guards,
You are Chairman Mao's good students.
You love listening to Chairman Mao's words the most,
You are most willing to act according to Chairman Mao's instructions.
Your eyes are the brightest,
Your hearts are the reddest.

Heroic Red Guards,
You are the descendants of revolution.
Your age is not great,
But you are the masters of today's revolution.
You are the revolutionaries,
You are the gravediggers of the ghosts and monsters.

Heroic Red Guards,
You dare to break old habits and create new ones.
No more Afu heads, Hong Kong-style clothes, or rocket shoes,
From head to toe, a new style must be established.
Places like Wangfujing, Princess Tomb... all go to hell,
The socialist road will not allow decayed names to hang around anymore!

Heroic Red Guards,
You are truly the most lovable people.
We admire your fearless style,
We cheer for your rebellious spirit,
We learn from your daring to think, speak, and act,
We are glad that there are successors to the revolutionary cause.

### 英雄的红卫兵
平均
1966-08-27
第3版()
专栏：

  英雄的红卫兵
  解放军海军某部　平均
  英雄的红卫兵，
  你们是毛主席的好学生。
  你们最爱听毛主席的话，
  你们最愿照毛主席的指示行动。
  你们的眼睛最亮最亮，
  你们的心儿最红最红。
  英雄的红卫兵，
  你们是革命的子孙。
  你们的年纪不大，
  却是今天革命的主人。
  你们是革命的造反者，
  你们是牛鬼蛇神的掘墓人。
  英雄的红卫兵，
  你们最敢于破旧创新。
  阿飞头、港式服、火箭鞋不准再现，
  从头到脚要树立一派新风。
  什么王府井、公主坟……统统见鬼去吧，
  社会主义大道绝不许再挂腐朽的名！
  英雄的红卫兵，
  你们真正是最可爱的人。
  我们敬佩你们的无畏风格，
  我们欢呼你们的造反精神，
  我们学习你们敢想敢说敢闯，
  我们高兴革命大业后继有人。
