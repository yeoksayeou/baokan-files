Title: Zambian Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-08-29
Edition: 4

A trade delegation from the Zambian government led by the Deputy Minister of Industry and Commerce, Cisaat, arrived in Beijing today with a total of six members.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Jia Shi and others.

### 赞比亚政府贸易代表团到京

1966-08-29
第4版()
专栏：

    赞比亚政府贸易代表团到京
    新华社二十八日讯　以赞比亚工商部副部长齐萨特为首的赞比亚政府贸易代表团一行六人，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场迎接的有对外贸易部副部长贾石等。
