Title: Chinese and Cambodian Men's and Women's Volleyball Teams Hold Friendly Match in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-08-22
Edition: 5

The Cambodian national men's and women's volleyball teams held a friendly match with the Beijing men's volleyball team and the Chinese women's volleyball team at the Beijing Gymnasium this evening. The Beijing men's team and the Chinese women's team each won with a score of 3-0.

Vice Premier He Long and the Charge d'Affaires of the Cambodian Embassy in China, Shen Lisu, attended the match this evening. During the match, Vice Premier He Long met with the leader of the Cambodian national men's and women's volleyball teams, Song Yuan, and shook hands with the athletes of both teams. He also posed for a group photo with the Cambodian Charge d'Affaires, Shen Lisu.

### 中柬男女排球队在京进行友谊赛  贺副总理观看并接见客队领队

1966-08-22
第5版()
专栏：

    中柬男女排球队在京进行友谊赛
    贺副总理观看并接见客队领队
    据新华社二十一日讯  柬埔寨国家男、女排球队今晚在北京体育馆同北京男子排球队、中国女子排球队进行了友谊比赛。北京男子队和中国女子队分别以三比○获胜。
    贺龙副总理，柬埔寨驻中国大使馆临时代办沈力速观看了今晚的比赛。比赛中间，贺龙副总理接见柬埔寨国家男、女排球队领队松原，并且同柬埔寨临时代办沈力速走下主席台同双方运动员一一握手并在一起照了像。
