Title: President Masamba-Debat's Reply to Chairman Ho Chi Minh and Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho
Author:
Date: 1966-08-29
Edition: 5

The Congolese (Brazzaville) people firmly stand with the Vietnamese people

President Shukairy of the Palestine Liberation Organization expresses that the Palestinian people will fight alongside the Vietnamese people

	Xinhua News Agency, Brazzaville. Congolese (Brazzaville) President Masamba-Debat recently reaffirmed that the Congolese (Brazzaville) people firmly stand with the heroic Vietnamese people and wish the Vietnamese people's valiant struggle against imperialist aggression a victory.

	According to a broadcast by the national radio station here, President Masamba-Debat recently replied to a telegram from Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, expressing gratitude for his warm congratulations on the third anniversary of the August Revolution in Congo (Brazzaville).

	In the telegram, President Masamba-Debat said, "The Congolese people have always given a correct evaluation of the Vietnamese people's heroic struggle. I ask you to believe that the Congolese party, government, and people stand with you. We will persistently fulfill our mission, which is to provide all material and moral support to the liberation movements in Latin America and Asia and Africa."

	"I take this opportunity to wish you and all the Vietnamese people victory in expelling the imperialists and their accomplices from your land once and for all."

	In another telegram to Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, President Masamba-Debat said, "In the Vietnamese party and people's heroic struggle against imperialist aggression, the Congolese people stand with you. The victory you will achieve is a great contribution to the common struggle against imperialism and colonialism and for the freedom and independence of all peoples."

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Cairo. Chairman Shukairy of the Executive Committee of the Palestine Liberation Organization recently issued a statement, pledging firm support for the Vietnamese people's just struggle against American imperialism for the liberation and territorial integrity of Vietnam, and praising the Chinese government and people's full support for the Vietnamese people.

	The statement said, "The Palestinian people fully believe in the fighting unity between the Vietnamese people and the Palestinian people. They will fight shoulder to shoulder with the Vietnamese people because the struggle for freedom is indivisible." The statement pointed out, "In the face of the Vietnamese revolutionary giants, the conspiracies and crimes of American imperialism will be smashed to pieces."

	The statement praised the Chinese government's firm support for the Vietnamese people's struggle for freedom and its provision of all assistance to the Vietnamese people.

	The statement said, "The Chinese people have put their slogans into action. The Chinese government emphasized that it will fulfill its duties with those who fight for freedom. 'We pay tribute to the great Chinese people and the Chinese revolutionary government.'"

### 马桑巴—代巴总统复电胡志明主席和阮友寿主席  刚果（布）人民坚决站在越南人民一边  舒凯里主席表示巴勒斯坦人民要同越南人民并肩战斗

1966-08-29
第5版()
专栏：

    马桑巴—代巴总统复电胡志明主席和阮友寿主席
    刚果（布）人民坚决站在越南人民一边
    舒凯里主席表示巴勒斯坦人民要同越南人民并肩战斗
    新华社布拉柴维尔电  刚果（布）总统马桑巴—代巴最近重申刚果（布）人民坚决站在英雄的越南人民一边，并祝越南人民抗击帝国主义侵略的英勇斗争取得胜利。
    据这里国家电台广播，马桑巴—代巴总统最近复电越南南方民族解放阵线主席阮友寿，感谢他在刚果（布）八月革命三周年时所表达的热烈祝贺。
    马桑巴—代巴总统在电报中说，刚果人民“对越南人民的英勇斗争一贯给予正确的评价”，“我请你相信，刚果党、政府和人民站在你们一边。我们将坚持不懈地履行我们的使命，这就是向拉丁美洲和亚洲、非洲的解放运动提供一切物质和道义上的援助。
      “我借此机会，祝愿你和全体越南人民取得把帝国主义者及其帮凶从你们的国土上一劳永逸地驱逐出去的胜利。”
    马桑巴—代巴总统在另一份致越南民主共和国主席胡志明的复电中说：“在越南党和人民进行的抗击帝国主义侵略的英勇斗争中，刚果人民站在你们一边。你们将取得的胜利，是对反对帝国主义和殖民主义、争取各国人民自由和独立的共同斗争的伟大贡献。
    据新华社开罗电　巴勒斯坦解放组织执行委员会主席舒凯里最近发表声明，保证坚决支持越南人民反对美帝国主义、争取越南的解放和领土完整的正义斗争，并且赞扬中国政府和人民对越南人民的全力支持。
    声明说，巴勒斯坦人民完全相信战斗的越南人民和巴勒斯坦人民之间的战斗团结，他们将同越南人民并肩战斗，因为争取自由的斗争是不可分割的。声明指出，“在越南革命巨人的面前，美帝国主义的阴谋和罪恶将会被砸得粉碎。”
    声明赞扬中国政府坚决支持越南人民争取自由的斗争和它向越南人民提供的一切援助。
    声明说，中国人民把自己的口号付诸行动，中国政府强调指出它将同那些为自由而斗争的人们一道履行自己的义务。“我们向伟大的中国人民和中国革命政府致敬。”
