Title: Ten Songs of the "Sixteen Points" – Celebrating the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China's Decision on the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution
Author: Zuǒ Zhèng  Zhào Línglóng  Máo Pèiqí  Wèi Kǎi  Zhào Xiānxūn
Date: 1966-08-12
Edition: 3

Ten Songs of the "Sixteen Points"
– Celebrating the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China's Decision on the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution
Students of Renmin University of China: Zuǒ Zhèng  Zhào Línglóng  Máo Pèiqí  Wèi Kǎi  Zhào Xiānxūn

	First Song of the "Sixteen Points":
	The "Sixteen Points" are well-drafted,
	The brilliance of Mao Zedong Thought,
	A red sun shines in our hearts.

	Second Song of the "Sixteen Points":
	A new revolutionary proclamation,
	Struggle, criticize, and reform,
	Shouldering the heavy burden of revolution.

	Third Song of the "Sixteen Points":
	Four weapons firmly in hand:
	Speak out, air views fully, hold great debates,
	And revolutionary big-character posters.

	Fourth Song of the "Sixteen Points":
	Mobilizing the masses is most important,
	With "dare" as the banner, fight to the end,
	Sweep away all monsters and demons.

	Fifth Song of the "Sixteen Points":
	Class line strictly adhered to,
	Unite with the majority of the masses,
	Party policies must be remembered.

	Sixth Song of the "Sixteen Points":
	Distinguish between the two types of contradictions,
	Revolutionary comrades hold hands,
	Together against the enemy, united in heart.

	Seventh Song of the "Sixteen Points":
	Cheering for the masses' new creations:
	Cultural revolutionary committees,
	Blooming red flowers forever.

	Eighth Song of the "Sixteen Points":
	Reform old education,
	Shorten school terms and simplify courses,
	Smash the old ways of the bourgeoisie.

	Ninth Song of the "Sixteen Points":
	Achieve ideological revolution,
	Promote production through revolution,
	Raise a high tide of more, faster, better, and cheaper.

	Tenth Song of the "Sixteen Points":
	The revolutionary victory song shakes the heavens,
	Forever follow Chairman Mao,
	Hold high the red flag of revolution!

### 十唱“十六条”——欢呼中共中央关于无产阶级文化大革命的决定
左正　赵凌龙　毛佩琦  魏凯　赵先勋
1966-08-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　十唱“十六条”
　　——欢呼中共中央关于无产阶级文化大革命的决定
　　中国人民大学学生  左正　赵凌龙　毛佩琦  魏凯　赵先勋
　　一唱“十六条”，
    “十六条”订得好，
　　毛泽东思想放光芒，
　　一轮红日心头照。
　　二唱“十六条”，
　　革命的新文告，
　　一斗二批又三改，
　　革命重担肩上挑。
　　三唱“十六条”，
　　四大武器掌握牢：
　　大鸣大放大辩论，
　　还有革命的大字报。
　　四唱“十六条”，
　　发动群众最重要，
　　“敢”字当头去战斗，
　　牛鬼蛇神全扫掉。
　　五唱“十六条”，
　　阶级路线贯彻好，
　　团结群众大多数，
　　党的政策要记牢。
　　六唱“十六条”，
　　两类矛盾区分好，
　　革命同志手挽手，
　　共同对敌心一条。
　　七唱“十六条”，
　　欢呼群众的新创造：
　　文化革命委员会，
　　朵朵红花永不凋。
　　八唱“十六条”，
　　改革旧文教，
　　学制要短课要简，
　　砸烂资产阶级的老一套。
　　九唱“十六条”，
　　思想革命化要做到，
　　抓革命来促生产，
　　多快好省掀高潮。
　　十唱“十六条”，
　　革命凯歌震云霄，
　　永远跟着毛主席，
　　革命红旗举得高！
