Title: Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down 13 U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-08-24
Edition: 4

Vietnamese military and civilians shoot down 13 U.S. aircraft

	New Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 23rd: According to a report from here on the evening of the 23rd, the military and civilians of Quang Ninh Province in North Vietnam, in cooperation with the People's Navy, launched a fierce counterattack against invading U.S. aggressor aircraft on the 23rd, shooting down three aircraft in one go.

	According to another report, the military and civilians of Bac Ninh Province shot down one U.S. aggressor aircraft on the evening of the 21st.

	New Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 23rd: According to a report from here, the military and civilians of Hai Duong Province and Quang Ninh Province in North Vietnam shot down two invading U.S. aggressor aircraft on the morning of the 22nd. According to a supplementary message, the military and civilians of Phu Tho Province shot down one U.S. aggressor aircraft on the 7th.

	New Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 21st: According to a report from here, the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province in North Vietnam shot down one invading U.S. aggressor aircraft on the morning of the 20th.

	According to a supplementary message, the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province shot down one U.S. aircraft each on the 11th and 13th. The military and civilians of Quang Tri Province shot down three U.S. aircraft over three days on the 15th, 16th, and 18th.

### 越南军民击落美机十三架

1966-08-24
第4版()
专栏：

    越南军民击落美机十三架
    新华社河内二十三日电　据这里二十三日晚上公布：越南北方广宁省军民，在人民海军的配合下，二十三日猛烈反击入侵的美国强盗飞机，一举击落三架。
    另据报道，河北省军民二十一日晚上曾击落美国强盗飞机一架。
    新华社河内二十三日电　据这里公布：越南北方海阳省和广宁省军民，二十二日上午击落入侵的美国强盗飞机两架。另据补充消息，宣光省军民七日击落美国强盗飞机一架。
    新华社河内二十一日电　据这里公布：越南北方广平省军民二十日上午击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架。
    另据补充消息，广平省军民十一日和十三日各击落美国飞机一架，河静省军民十五日、十六日和十八日三天击落美国飞机三架。
