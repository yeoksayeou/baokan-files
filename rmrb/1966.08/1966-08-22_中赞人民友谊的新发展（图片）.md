Title: New Developments in Sino-Zambian Friendship
Author:
Date: 1966-08-22
Edition: 5

On August 19th, at the invitation of our country, Vice President Ruben Chitandiika Kamanga of the Republic of Zambia and the Zambian government delegation he led arrived in Beijing for a friendly visit. The photo shows Vice President Kamanga with Vice Chairman Dong Biwu and Premier Zhou Enlai at the airport.

Tiananmen Square was filled with joy, and the capital's cultural workers sang and danced enthusiastically to welcome Vice President Kamanga and other Zambian guests.

The people of the capital warmly welcomed Vice President Kamanga and other Zambian guests. On August 19th, Vice Chairman Dong Biwu and Premier Zhou Enlai held a banquet to warmly welcome Vice President Kamanga and the entire delegation of the Zambian government led by him. The photo shows Vice President Kamanga entering the banquet hall accompanied by Vice Chairman Dong Biwu, Premier Zhou Enlai, and others.

### 中赞人民友谊的新发展（图片）

1966-08-22
第5版()
专栏：

   中赞人民友谊的新发展
   八月十九日，应邀来我国进行友好访问的赞比亚共和国副总统鲁本·奇坦迪卡·卡曼加和由他率领的赞比亚共和国政府代表团到达北京。图为卡曼加副总统和董必武副主席、周恩来总理在机场上。
                              （本组照片均为新华社记者摄）
   天安门广场一片欢腾，首都文艺工作者载歌载舞，热烈欢迎卡曼加副总统和其他赞比亚贵宾。
   首都群众热烈欢迎卡曼加副总统等赞比亚贵宾。八月十九日，董必武副主席、周恩来总理举行宴会热烈欢迎卡曼加副总统和由他率领的赞比亚共和国政府代表团全体贵宾。图为卡曼加副总统由董必武副主席、周恩来总理等陪同步入宴会厅。
