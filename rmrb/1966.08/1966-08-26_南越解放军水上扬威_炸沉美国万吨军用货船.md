Title: The Liberation Army of South Vietnam Shows Its Might on the Water, Sinks a U.S. Ten-Thousand-Ton Military Cargo Ship
Author:
Date: 1966-08-26
Edition: 5

According to a report from Xinhua News Agency on the 24th, Saigon news: On the morning of the 23rd, the Liberation Army of South Vietnam blew up and sank a U.S. ten-thousand-ton military cargo ship.

According to reports from Western news agencies, the cargo ship was named "Barton L. Wadsworth." It was carrying U.S. military supplies heading to Saigon. It was struck and sunk by a mine from the Liberation Army in the Saigon River, about 30 kilometers east of Saigon. According to reports, at least six U.S. sailors were killed and four went missing.

### 南越解放军水上扬威  炸沉美国万吨军用货船

1966-08-26
第5版()
专栏：

    南越解放军水上扬威
    炸沉美国万吨军用货船
    据新华社二十四日讯　西贡消息：越南南方解放军二十三日上午炸沉一艘万吨美国军用货船。
    据西方通讯社报道，这艘货船名为“巴顿·拉奇·维多利号”，当时载着美国军用物资开往西贡。它在西贡以东三十余公里的西贡河上被解放军水雷击沉。据报道，美国水手至少有六名死亡，四名失踪。
