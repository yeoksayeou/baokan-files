Title: The Daur People Will Forever Follow Chairman Mao
Author: He Jianfu
Date: 1966-08-26
Edition: 4

Chairman Mao,
You are the red sun in our hearts,
You are the great leader of the world's people.
Your glory
Is brighter than ten thousand suns,
Shouting ten thousand long lives, ten thousand times ten thousand long lives,
Cannot express the Daur people's sincere feelings.
Your kindness is higher than the mountains,
Your radiance shines in all directions,
The Daur people will forever follow you,
Thoroughly make revolution and march forward.
With Chairman Mao, the Daur people
Clear the way and fight with high spirits,
Develop water conservancy and ensure a good harvest,
The red flags on the Red Flag Canal will forever wave.
Study Chairman Mao's works every day,
Never forget class struggle,
Production struggle reaches a high tide,
Scientific experiments are pioneers.
With Chairman Mao, the Daur people
Devote themselves to the three great revolutions,
With hearts for the country and eyes on the world,
Transform the land and build a new socialist homeland.
Swear to realize the Four Modernizations in the homeland,
Ensure harvests despite droughts and floods, produce more grain,
Tractors plow the fields, irrigate the land,
Forest belts form a network, lights shine brightly.
The beautiful scenery cannot be sung enough,
The sincere feelings cannot be expressed,
Beloved leader Chairman Mao,
The Daur people will forever march forward with you.

### 达斡尔人民永远跟着毛主席
何鉴福
1966-08-26
第4版()
专栏：“哈尔滨之夏”节目选

   达斡尔人民永远跟着毛主席
   达斡尔族　何鉴福
   毛主席哟，
   您是我们心中的红太阳，
   您是世界人民伟大的旗手哟。
   您的光辉，
   比千万个太阳还明亮，
   喊千声万岁万万岁，
   难表达达族人民的衷肠。
   您的恩情比山高哟，
   您的光芒照四方，
   达族人民永远跟着您，
   彻底闹革命奔向前方。
   达族人民有了毛主席，
   披荆斩棘斗志昂扬，
   兴修水利保丰收，
   红旗渠上红旗永远飘扬。
   毛主席著作天天学哟，
   阶级斗争永不忘哟，
   生产斗争掀高潮哟，
   科学实验当闯将哟。
   达族人民有了毛主席，
   三大革命献力量，
   心怀祖国、放眼世界，
   改天换地建设社会主义新家乡。
   誓叫家乡实现四化哟，
   旱涝保收多打粮哟，
   拖拉机耕地水浇田哟，
   林带成网电灯亮哟。
   美景唱不尽哟，
   衷情表不完，
   敬爱的领袖毛主席，
   达族人民跟您永向前。
