Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Burmese News Delegation, Team Leader Ding Tun Hosts Farewell Banquet
Author:
Date: 1966-08-11
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Chen Yi today received Burmese Propaganda Department Secretary Colonel Ding Tun and the entire Burmese news delegation led by him, engaging in a warm and friendly conversation.

Present at the reception were Vice Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association Chang Zhiqing, Deputy Director of the Foreign Ministry's News Department Chen Weifan, and others.

Colonel Ding Tun, secretary of the Burmese Propaganda Department and leader of the Burmese news delegation, hosted a banquet tonight to bid farewell to people from various circles in the capital.

The delegation returned to Beijing yesterday after concluding their visits and tours in Shanghai, Nanjing, Hangzhou, and other places. They will soon visit Northeast China and other regions.

Colonel Ding Tun and Vice Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association Chang Zhiqing both spoke at the banquet, emphasizing that China and Burma are close friendly neighboring countries, and the "Pauk-Phaw" friendship between the peoples and journalists of the two countries will continue to strengthen and develop.

Attending the banquet at the invitation were responsible persons and journalists from various relevant sectors in the capital, including Shi Huaipei, Hu Chi, Qian Diqian, Chen Weifan, and Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Burma Zhao Jiping.

The Secretary-General of the Asian-African Journalists Association, Chaharda, was also invited to attend.

Burmese Ambassador to China Thar Ma Duwa Sinwa Nao and his wife attended the banquet.

### 陈毅副总理接见缅甸新闻代表团　丁吞团长举行告别宴会

1966-08-11
第6版()
专栏：

　　陈毅副总理接见缅甸新闻代表团
　　丁吞团长举行告别宴会
    新华社十日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见缅甸宣传部秘书丁吞中校和由他率领的缅甸新闻代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席常芝青，外交部新闻司副司长陈维帆等。
    新华社十日讯　缅甸新闻代表团团长、缅甸宣传部秘书丁吞中校今晚举行宴会，向首都各界人士告别。
    代表团是结束在上海、南京、杭州等地的参观访问后在昨天回到北京的，并将于日内前往东北等地访问。
    丁吞中校和中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席常芝青先后在宴会上讲话，他们一致指出，中缅两国是亲密友好的邻国，两国人民和新闻工作者之间的“胞波”友谊将不断巩固和发展。
    应邀出席宴会的，有首都各有关方面负责人和新闻界人士史怀璧、胡痴、钱抵千、陈维帆，以及中国驻缅甸大使馆文化参赞赵冀平等。
    亚非新闻工作者协会总书记查禾多也应邀出席。
    缅甸驻中国大使沙马杜瓦·信瓦瑙和夫人出席了宴会。
