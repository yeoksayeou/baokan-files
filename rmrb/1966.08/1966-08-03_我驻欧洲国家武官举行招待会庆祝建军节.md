Title: Chinese Military Attachés in European Countries Hold Receptions to Celebrate the Anniversary of the Founding of the PLA
Author:
Date: 1966-08-03
Edition: 5

The military attachés of the People's Republic of China to Switzerland, France, Sweden, and Denmark held receptions on July 29 to celebrate the 39th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

The military attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Switzerland, Ding Shan, held a reception on July 29. Swiss government officials and high-ranking military officers, including Rear Admiral A. Schenk, the Chief of the General Staff, attended. A total of 230 people attended the reception.

The acting chargé d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Switzerland, Wang Jin, also participated in the reception.

The acting military attaché of the Chinese Embassy in France, Fang Wen, held a reception on July 29. French Navy Vice Chief of Staff, Rear Admiral Dab, French armed forces officers, and government officials attended. Chinese Ambassador Huang Zhen also attended the reception.

The military attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Sweden, Li Zi Mu, held a reception on July 29. Swedish high-ranking military officers, including the acting head of the Naval Staff, Larsen, as well as Swedish government officials and people from various circles, attended. The acting chargé d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Sweden, Li Dehua, also attended the reception.

Approximately 120 people from various circles in Denmark attended the reception held by the military attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Denmark, Liu Guangji. Attendees included Danish Air Force Commander, Major General Pagh, Navy Commander, Rear Admiral Tostrup, and other high-ranking officials.

Diplomatic envoys and military attachés from other countries stationed in Switzerland, France, Sweden, and Denmark also attended the receptions held by the Chinese military attachés.

The receptions were filled with a friendly atmosphere.

### 我驻欧洲国家武官举行招待会庆祝建军节

1966-08-03
第5版()
专栏：

    我驻欧洲国家武官举行招待会庆祝建军节
    新华社二日讯　中华人民共和国驻瑞士大使馆武官在七月二十九日，我国驻法国、瑞典和丹麦大使馆的武官分别在一日晚举行招待会，庆祝中国人民解放军建军三十九周年。
    瑞士政府官员和后方参谋长安·沈克上校等高级军官出席了我国驻瑞士大使馆武官丁山举行的招待会。出席招待会的共有二百三十多人。
    我国驻瑞士大使馆临时代办王进也参加了招待会。
    出席我国驻法国大使馆代理武官方文举行的招待会的，有法国海军副参谋长达布少将，法国武装部队军官和政府官员等人。出席招待会的还有中国大使黄镇。
    出席我国驻瑞典大使馆武官李子木举行的招待会的有瑞典军令部代理部长拉逊等高级军官，以及瑞典政府官员和各界人士。
    出席招待会的还有中国大使馆临时代办李德华。
    丹麦各界约一百二十人出席了我国驻丹麦大使馆武官刘广继举行的招待会。其中有丹麦空军司令佩格中将，海军司令托斯特鲁普海军中将和其他高级官员。
    其它国家驻瑞士、法国、瑞典和丹麦的外交使节和武官也出席了我国武官举行的招待会。
    招待会都充满友好的气氛。
