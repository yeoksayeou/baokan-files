Title: Black River Province Institute of Technology Revolutionary Teachers, Students, and Staff Brave the Rain to Report Joy and Congratulate
Author:
Date: 1966-08-11
Edition: 4

Revolutionary teachers, students, and staff of the Heilongjiang Institute of Technology braved the rain to go to the Heilongjiang Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China to report their joy and congratulations. They expressed their most resolute and enthusiastic support for the Party Central Committee's wise decision on the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

	New China News Agency Photographer (Telephoto)
