Title: The Entire Nation Should Become a University for Studying Mao Zedong Thought
Author:
Date: 1966-08-03
Edition: 2

Comrade Mao Zedong has called on the people of the entire nation to transform our country's factories, rural people's communes, schools, commerce, service industries, and party and government agencies into revolutionary universities, just like the People's Liberation Army.
