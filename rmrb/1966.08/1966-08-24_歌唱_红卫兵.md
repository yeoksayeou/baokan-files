Title: Singing the Praises of the Red Guards
Author: Chen Tao
Date: 1966-08-24
Edition: 3

	I sing a song,
	Singing of the Red Guards,
	Singing of the revolutionary youngsters,
	Singing of the red successors.
	Red Guards, Red Guards,
	Vibrant and full of life, making revolution,
	With great aspirations and fearing nothing,
	Daring to venture and lead the way.
	Old ideas, old culture,
	Old customs, old habits,
	Corrupting people's souls,
	All are harmful dust!
	How can we tolerate this?
	No! No! No!
	In our hands, we hold iron brooms,
	Sweeping it all away cleanly!
	Destroy the "Four Olds," establish the "Four News,"
	Carry out the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution,
	With high morale and firm belief,
	We will not stand down until we win!
	Everywhere are revolutionary slogans,
	Everywhere are heroic songs,
	Everywhere are revolutionary actions,
	Everywhere is a scene of fervent activity!
	Excellent! Excellent!
	The precious spirit of rebellion;
	Excellent! Excellent!
	The respectable and lovable Red Guards.
	I sing a song,
	Singing of the Red Guards,
	Holding high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought,
	March on! March on! March on!

### 歌唱“红卫兵”
陈涛
1966-08-24
第3版()
专栏：

   歌唱“红卫兵”
   陈涛
   我唱一支歌，
   歌唱红卫兵，
   歌唱革命的小将，
   歌唱红色的接班人。
   红卫兵呀红卫兵，
   生龙活虎闹革命，
   胸怀大志无所惧，
   敢闯敢干作先行。
   旧思想，旧文化，
   旧风俗，旧习惯，
   腐蚀人们的灵魂，
   都是害人的灰尘！
   这现象怎能容忍？
   不行！不行！不行！
   手中高举铁扫帚，
   把它一股脑儿扫除干净！
   破“四旧”，立“四新”，
   进行无产阶级文化大革命，
   斗志昂，信心高，
   不获胜利不收兵！
   到处是革命的标语，
   到处是豪迈的歌声，
   到处是革命的行动，
   到处是一片热气腾腾！
   好得很！好得很！
   可贵的造反精神；
   好得很！好得很！
   可敬可爱的红卫兵。
   我唱一支歌，
   歌唱红卫兵，
   高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，
   前进！前进！前进！
