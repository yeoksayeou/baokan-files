Title: Chinese and Cambodian Basketball Teams Hold Friendly Match in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-08-28
Edition: 5

The Cambodian national men's and women's basketball teams held a friendly match with the Chinese men's and women's basketball teams at the Beijing Gymnasium this evening. The match was warmly welcomed by the capital's audience.

The atmosphere of the match was friendly from beginning to end. The results were that the Chinese men's and women's teams won with scores of 110-80 and 121-38, respectively.

The Cambodian national men's and women's basketball teams will depart Beijing for Shenyang tomorrow.

### 中柬篮球队在京进行友谊赛

1966-08-28
第5版()
专栏：

    中柬篮球队在京进行友谊赛
    新华社二十七日讯  柬埔寨国家男女篮球队今晚在北京体育馆同中国男女篮球队进行友谊比赛时，受到了首都观众的热烈欢迎。
    比赛自始至终充满了友好的气氛。比赛结果，中国男女队分别以一百一十比八十和一百二十一比三十八取得了胜利。
    柬埔寨国家男女篮球队将于二十八日离开北京去沈阳访问。
