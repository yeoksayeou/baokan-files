Edition: 1

Vice President Kamanga Leads Delegation to Depart from Beijing
Dong Vice Chairman and Zhou Premier and Thousands of Capital Residents See Them Off at the Airport

BEIJING, Aug. 22 (Xinhua) — Vice President Ruben Chitandika Kamanga of Zambia and the Zambian government delegation he led concluded their friendly visit to China and departed by plane this morning.

Kamanga Vice President bid a cordial farewell to Dong Biwu Vice Chairman and Zhou Enlai Premier, who came to the airport to see them off. Kamanga Vice President asked Dong Vice Chairman and Zhou Premier to convey his warm greetings to Chairman Mao once again. He also said, “Our visit this time has been very successful!”

Thousands of people from various circles in the capital came to the airport to see off the Zambian guests. They warmly thanked Kamanga Vice President for the friendly sentiments he expressed after arriving in Beijing: the peoples of Zambia and China are now good friends and are prepared to remain good friends forever. The crowd also shouted slogans such as “Long live China-Zambia friendship” and asked the guests to take the friendship of the Chinese people to the brotherly people of Zambia.

A grand send-off ceremony was held at the airport, where the national flags of both countries were flying. The band played the national anthems of Zambia and China. Accompanied by Dong Biwu Vice Chairman and Zhou Enlai Premier, Kamanga Vice President inspected the honor guard. The guests circled the field to bid farewell to the crowd. People beat drums and gongs, waved colored flags, ribbons, and bouquets, and shouted, “Long live the unity of the Asian and African peoples,” “Down with imperialism and old and new colonialism.” Soldiers of the People’s Liberation Army clapped in rows, and armed male and female militiamen saluted the guests with their guns.

Before boarding the plane, Kamanga Vice President and Minister Kapwepwe received flowers from the Young Pioneers and shook hands and embraced with Dong Biwu Vice Chairman, Zhou Enlai Premier, and others. Kamanga Vice President ascended the stairs and waved both hands at the plane door to bid farewell to all the people who came to see them off.

Those who came to the airport to see them off today also included Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife Zhang Qie, Vice Premier Li Xiannian, Vice Chairman Yang Mingxuan of the Standing Committee of the National People’s Congress, Vice Chairman Gao Chongmin of the National Committee of the Chinese People’s Political Consultative Conference, Vice Chairman Fu Zuoyi of the National Defense Council, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qiao Guangping, and other responsible officials from various departments of the government, the People’s Liberation Army, and other relevant sectors. Envoys from various countries to China also came to the airport to see them off.

### 卡曼加副总统率代表团离京  董副主席周总理和首都各界数千人到机场欢送

1966-08-23
第1版()
专栏：

    卡曼加副总统率代表团离京
    董副主席周总理和首都各界数千人到机场欢送
    新华社二十二日讯  赞比亚共和国副总统鲁本·奇坦迪卡·卡曼加和由他率领的赞比亚共和国政府代表团，结束了在中国的友好访问，今天上午乘飞机离开我国。
    卡曼加副总统同前往机场欢送的董必武副主席、周恩来总理亲切告别。卡曼加副总统请董副主席和周总理转达他对毛主席再一次的良好问候。他还说：“我们这次访问非常成功！”
    首都各界数千人到机场欢送赞比亚贵宾。他们热烈感谢卡曼加副总统在来到北京后所表达的友好情谊：赞中两国人民现在是好朋友，并且准备将来永远做好朋友。人们还高呼“中国赞比亚友好万岁”等口号，请贵宾们把中国人民的友情带给兄弟的赞比亚人民。
    在飘扬着中赞两国国旗的机场上，举行了隆重的欢送仪式。乐队奏赞比亚和中国国歌。卡曼加副总统由董必武副主席和周恩来总理陪同，检阅了仪仗队。贵宾们绕场一周，同欢送的群众告别。人们擂鼓敲锣，挥动彩旗、彩带和花束，高呼：“亚非人民团结万岁”、“反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义”。人民解放军指战员列队鼓掌，全副武装的男女民兵向贵宾们行持枪礼。
    卡曼加副总统和卡普韦普韦部长在登上飞机之前，接受了少先队员的献花，并和董必武副主席、周恩来总理等握手、拥抱。卡曼加副总统走上扶梯，在机舱门前挥动双手，向全体欢送群众致意。
    今天到机场欢送的，还有陈毅副总理和夫人张茜，李先念副总理，杨明轩副委员长，政协全国委员会副主席高崇民，国防委员会副主席傅作义，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，政府其他各部门、中国人民解放军和各有关方面的负责人。各国驻中国的使节也到机场欢送。
