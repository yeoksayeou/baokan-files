Title: Polish Ambassador Leaves Post and Returns to Country
Author:
Date: 1966-08-17
Edition: 4

Polish Ambassador Leaves Post and Returns to Country

	New China News Agency, August 15th – The Ambassador of the Polish People's Republic to China, Knothe, has been recalled and will leave his post. He departed today in the morning by airplane from Beijing and returned to his country.

### 波兰大使离任回国

1966-08-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　波兰大使离任回国
    新华社十五日讯　波兰人民共和国驻中国大使克诺泰奉调离任，已在今天上午乘飞机离京回国。
