Announcement

	According to the instructions of the Party Central Committee, starting from September 1, 1966, the People's Daily will change to a four-page edition daily. After the change, the People's Daily will continue to hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, face the broad masses of workers, peasants, and soldiers, vigorously promote Mao Zedong Thought, promote the broad masses of workers, peasants, and soldiers in studying and applying Chairman Mao's works, promote the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, promote the Party's policies, reflect the great achievements of the socialist revolution and socialist construction, and promote the excellent domestic and international situation. Page arrangement: The first page will feature editorials, important news, and dispatches; the second page will cover domestic political, economic, cultural, and educational news, commentaries, and dispatches; the third page will include international news, dispatches, briefs, commentaries, and current affairs speeches; the fourth page will feature various special and supplementary editions.

	After the People's Daily changes to a four-page edition, the monthly subscription price will be 1.20 yuan. We welcome readers to subscribe at post offices nationwide.

	People's Daily, August 30, 1966

### 启事

1966-08-31
第6版()
专栏：

    启事
    根据党中央的指示，《人民日报》从一九六六年九月一日起，改为每天出四版。改版以后，《人民日报》将继续高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，面向广大工农兵群众，大力宣传毛泽东思想，宣传广大工农兵群众活学活用毛主席著作，宣传无产阶级文化大革命，宣传党的方针政策，反映社会主义革命和社会主义建设的伟大成就，宣传国内外大好形势。版面安排：第一版，社论、重要新闻和通讯；第二版，国内政治、经济、文教等方面的消息、评论、通讯；第三版，国际新闻、通讯、简讯、评论和时事讲话；第四版，各种专刊和副刊。
    《人民日报》改出四版后，每月定价为一元二角。欢迎读者到各地邮局订购。
                   人民日报社一九六六年八月三十日
