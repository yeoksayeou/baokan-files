Title: The Brightest and Reddest Red Sun
Author: Yuan Houchun, Gu Zhengfu
Date: 1966-08-19
Edition: 3

The brightest and reddest red sun,
Chairman Mao stands on Tiananmen!
Chairman Mao wears a majestic military uniform!
A red star shines brightly above his head,
This is the brightest and reddest red sun!

Looking at our beloved Chairman Mao,
A soldier examines himself—
Green military uniform,
Just like Chairman Mao’s!
Chairman Mao!
The gun in our hands is given by you,
You have armed us.
This uniform,
Contains so much of your trust,
So much of your hope!

Touching the red cap insignia and badge,
Straightening the rifle on the chest.
Our supreme commander, Chairman Mao,
Give the order!
You guide us on the path to victory,
We dare to face mountains of knives and seas of fire.
Pick up the pen, grasp the gun,
Level the mountains,
Part the waves,
Until all demons and monsters are swept away,
We will never take off our uniforms!
Defend the iron fortress for ten thousand generations,
Let the great red flag of Mao Zedong Thought fly forever in the world!

### 最亮最红的红太阳
袁厚春　顾正福
1966-08-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　最亮最红的红太阳
　　毛主席站在天安门上！
　　毛主席穿着威武的军装！
　　头顶上红星放光芒，
　　这是最亮最红的红太阳！
　　望着敬爱的毛主席，
　　战士又把自己周身打量——
　　绿色的军装啊，
　　和毛主席的一样！
　　毛主席啊！
　　我们手中的枪是您发给，
　　是您把我们来武装。
　　这一套军装啊，
　　包含着您给的多少信任，
    多少希望！
　　摸一摸鲜红的帽徽领章，
　　整一整胸前的五尺钢枪。
　　我们最高统帅毛主席，
　　您老人家下命令吧！
　　胜利的道路您指引，
　　刀山火海咱敢闯。
　　挥起笔，握紧枪，
　　踏平万重山，
　　辟开千层浪，
　　牛鬼蛇神不扫尽，
　　我们决不脱军装！
　　保卫铁打江山万万代，
　　让毛泽东思想伟大红旗在全世界永远飘扬！
        解放军某部政治部
              袁厚春　顾正福
