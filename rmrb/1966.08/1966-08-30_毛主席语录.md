	Chairman Mao's Quotations
		Youth is the most active and vigorous part of the whole social force. They are the most eager to learn and have the least conservative ideas, especially in the socialist era.

		From the commentary on the article "The Youth Shock Team of the Ninth Agricultural Production Cooperative in Newping, Zhongshan County"

### 毛主席语录

1966-08-30
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    青年是整个社会力量中的一部分最积极最有生气的力量。他们最肯学习，最少保守思想，在社会主义时代尤其是这样。
   《中山县新平乡第九农业生产合作社的青年突击队》一文按语
