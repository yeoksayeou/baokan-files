Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Cables Greetings on Swiss National Day
Author:
Date: 1966-08-01
Edition: 6

	New China News Agency, July 31st

	Chairman of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi today sent a telegram to the President of the Swiss Confederation, Hans Schaffner, to congratulate Switzerland on its national day (August 1st). The text of the telegram is as follows:

	To His Excellency President Hans Schaffner of the Swiss Confederation in Berne,

	On the occasion of the Swiss Confederation's national day, I extend my congratulations to Your Excellency and the Swiss people. I wish the Swiss Confederation prosperity and its people happiness.

	Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi

	July 31, 1966

### 刘少奇主席电贺瑞士国庆

1966-08-01
第6版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席电贺瑞士国庆
    新华社三十一日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇今天打电报给瑞士联邦主席汉斯·沙夫纳，祝贺瑞士国庆（八月一日）。电文如下：伯尔尼瑞士联邦主席汉斯·沙夫纳阁下：
    在瑞士联邦国庆的时候，我向阁下和瑞士人民表示祝贺。祝瑞士联邦繁荣、人民幸福。
      中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
            一九六六年七月三十一日
