Title: Evidence of India Following the U.S. in Creating "Two Chinas" and Colluding with the KMT to Oppose China
Author:
Date: 1966-08-03
Edition: 4

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China sent a note to the Indian Embassy in China on July 28, refuting the Indian government's various arguments regarding its conspiracy to follow U.S. imperialism in creating "two Chinas" and colluding with the KMT to oppose China. The ministry also lodged a strong protest against the Indian government. The two photographs published here are ironclad evidence of the Indian government's collusion with the KMT. The left photograph shows Indian President Radhakrishnan (right) meeting with the KMT's pseudo-Minister of Foreign Affairs Shen Xian. The right photograph shows Kamaraj (left), chairman of the Indian National Congress, meeting with the KMT's pseudo-Minister of Foreign Affairs Shen Xian.

New China News Agency

### 印度追随美帝制造“两个中国”和勾结蒋帮反华铁证（图片）

1966-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

    印度追随美帝制造“两个中国”和勾结蒋帮反华铁证
    中华人民共和国外交部七月二十八日照会印度驻华大使馆，驳斥印度政府对它追随美帝国主义制造“两个中国”和勾结蒋帮反华的阴谋活动所进行的种种狡辩，并且再次向印度政府提出强烈抗议。这里发表的两张照片是印度政府勾结蒋帮的铁证。左图：印度总统拉达克里希南（右）接见蒋帮伪外交部次长沈锜。右图：印度执政党国大党主席卡马拉季（左）接见蒋帮伪外交部次长沈锜。
