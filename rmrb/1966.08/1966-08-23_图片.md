Title: 500,000 Revolutionary Masses in Guangzhou Celebrate the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1966-08-23
Edition: 3

On August 19th, 500,000 revolutionary masses in Guangzhou held a grand rally to celebrate the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. The revolutionary masses attending the rally unanimously expressed their determination to study, understand, and apply the Sixteen Points in the struggle, and to carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution through to the end.
