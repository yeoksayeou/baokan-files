Title: Chairman Mao Receives Tanzanian Friendly Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-08-26
Edition: 1

Chairman Mao today afternoon received the full delegation of the Tanzanian Friendly Delegation led by Colonel Yusufu Shimidi.

Chairman Mao had a friendly and cordial conversation with the Tanzanian guests.

Those present at the reception included Premier Zhou Enlai, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Yang Chengwu, and Deputy Chief of Staff Peng Shaohui.

Also present at the reception was the Ambassador of the United Republic of Tanzania to China, Waziri Juma. (Photo attached)

On August 25, Chairman Mao received the full delegation of the Tanzanian Friendly Delegation led by Colonel Yusufu Shimidi. The photo shows a group photo taken during the reception.

### 毛泽东主席接见坦桑尼亚友好代表团

1966-08-26
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席接见坦桑尼亚友好代表团
    新华社二十五日讯　毛泽东主席今天下午接见了由尤素夫·希米德上校率领的坦桑尼亚友好代表团全体成员。
    毛主席同坦桑尼亚外宾进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    陪同接见的，有周恩来总理，中国人民解放军代总参谋长杨成武，副总参谋长彭绍辉。
    接见时，坦桑尼亚联合共和国驻华大使瓦齐里·朱马也在座。（附图片）
    八月二十五日，毛泽东主席接见由尤素夫·希米德上校率领的坦桑尼亚友好代表团全体成员。图为接见时合影。
