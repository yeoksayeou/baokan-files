Title: Coastal Defense Forces and Civilians Shoot Down Another U.S. Plane
Author:
Date: 1966-08-04
Edition: 5

	Coastal defense forces and civilians have shot down another U.S. plane. According to a report from here on the evening of the 3rd, U.S. aggressor aircraft again attacked a residential area in Haiphong, the second-largest city in North Vietnam, in the afternoon of the 3rd. The military and civilians of Haiphong fought bravely and shot down another U.S. plane. According to a supplementary report, the military and civilians of Quang Ninh Province also shot down an invading U.S. jet on the 1st.

### 海防军民又击落美机一架

1966-08-04
第5版()
专栏：

    海防军民又击落美机一架
    新华社河内三日电　据这里三日晚上公布：美国强盗飞机三日下午再次袭击越南北方第二大城市海防的一个居民区。海防市军民英勇战斗，又击落美国飞机一架。另据补充报道，广宁省军民一日也曾击落入侵的美国喷气式飞机一架。
