Title: South Vietnamese Liberation Army Annihilates U.S. Puppet Forces
Author:
Date: 1966-08-29
Edition: 5

The South Vietnamese Liberation Army has inflicted heavy casualties on the U.S. puppet forces. In Ba Dinh Province, they launched a fierce attack on the Australian mercenaries, annihilating over five hundred enemy troops.

	New China News Agency, Hanoi, August 28 — According to a report from the Liberation Communications Agency of South Vietnam: On August 17 and 18, the Liberation Army of Ba Dinh Province in South Vietnam launched consecutive attacks on a battalion and an armored vehicle detachment of the Australian mercenaries, annihilating over five hundred enemy troops, including two entire companies.

	On August 17, the Liberation Army of Ba Dinh Province launched an attack on the Australian mercenaries stationed at the Tho Mountain base, annihilating over one hundred enemy troops.

	In the afternoon of August 18, when the Australian mercenaries fell into the ambush set by the Liberation Army in Long Nhon Commune, the Liberation Army soldiers bravely assaulted the enemy, quickly annihilating a portion of the mercenaries. They then used concentrated firepower to fiercely mow down the remaining enemy troops.

	According to preliminary reports, the Liberation Army annihilated over four hundred Australian mercenaries in the battle on August 18, including two entire companies and severely damaged one company, destroying three M-113 type armored vehicles.

	Recently, the Command of the Liberation Armed Forces in South Vietnam decided to award the Third-Class Liberation Military Merit Medal to the Liberation Army units that achieved brilliant results in this battle.

	According to another report: From August 11 to 18, the guerrilla forces in Lai Thieu, Bien Hoa, and Chau Thanh counties of Thu Duc Province launched consecutive attacks on enemy strongholds, annihilating over one hundred twenty enemy troops, including eighty-five American aggressors.

### 南越解放军痛歼美国帮凶军  在巴地省猛攻澳军歼敌五百多名

1966-08-29
第5版()
专栏：

    南越解放军痛歼美国帮凶军
    在巴地省猛攻澳军歼敌五百多名
    新华社河内二十八日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方巴地省解放军八月十七日和十八日连续痛击澳大利亚帮凶军的一个营和一个装甲车支团，消灭敌人五百多名，其中全歼敌军两个整连。
    十七日，巴地省解放军向驻扎在土山基地的澳大利亚帮凶军发起进攻，消灭敌人一百多名。
    十八日下午，当澳大利亚帮凶军陷入解放军在隆新乡的伏击阵地时，解放军战士们勇猛袭击敌人，迅速消灭了一部分帮凶军。接着又用密集的火力向残存的敌人猛烈扫射。
    据初步消息，解放军在十八日的战斗中共消灭澳大利亚帮凶军四百多名，其中全歼敌军两个整连，重创一个连，击毁M—113型装甲车三辆。
    最近，越南南方解放武装力量指挥部决定把三级解放军功勋章授予在这次战斗中取得辉煌战绩的解放军部队。
    另据报道：土龙木省莱眺、边葛和周城三县的游击队，从八月十一日到十八日，接连进攻敌人的据点，共消灭敌人一百二十多名，其中包括美国侵略军八十五名。
