Title: Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation Visits China
Author:
Date: 1966-08-08
Edition: 4

On August 7, the Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation, at the invitation of Chairman Zhu De, arrived in Beijing by airplane. The photo shows the delegation posing together at the airport. The thirteenth person from the right in the front row is the delegation leader, Speaker Abdul Jabbar Khan.  Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter.
