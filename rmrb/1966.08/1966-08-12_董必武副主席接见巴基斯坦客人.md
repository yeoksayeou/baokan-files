Title: Vice Chairman Dong Biwu Meets with Pakistani Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-08-12
Edition: 6

Vice Chairman Dong Biwu met with Pakistani guests this afternoon. Xinhua News Agency, August 11th: Vice Chairman Dong Biwu met with Pakistani National Assembly Speaker Abdul Jabbar Khan and his wife, as well as the entire delegation of the Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation to China led by him, and had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.

Present at the meeting were Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo and his wife Yu Liqun, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Liu Ningyi, Vice Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Lian Guan and his wife Lin Lang, and Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan Zhang Wengjin.

Pakistani Ambassador to China Sultan Muhammad Khan was also present.

On August 11th, Vice Chairman Dong Biwu met with Pakistani National Assembly Speaker Abdul Jabbar Khan (front left sixth) and his wife, as well as the entire delegation of the Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation to China led by him. The photo shows a group photo taken during the meeting.

### 董必武副主席接见巴基斯坦客人

1966-08-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　董必武副主席接见巴基斯坦客人
    新华社十一日讯　董必武副主席今天下午接见巴基斯坦国民议会议长阿卜杜勒·贾巴尔·汗和夫人，以及由他率领的巴基斯坦议会访华代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有人大常委会副委员长郭沫若和夫人于立群，人大常委会副委员长刘宁一，人大常委会副秘书长连贯和夫人林琅，中国驻巴基斯坦大使章文晋等。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆罕默德·汗也在座。（附图片）
    八月十一日，董必武副主席接见巴基斯坦国民议会议长阿卜杜勒·贾巴尔·汗（前左六）和夫人，以及由他率领的巴基斯坦议会访华代表团全体成员。图为接见时合影。
