Title: Asian and Latin American Figures Express Unlimited Love and Admiration for Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1966-08-12
Edition: 6

	Asian and Latin American figures have expressed their unlimited love and admiration for Chairman Mao. They use Mao Zedong Thought as a guide for their revolutionary struggles. They regard the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China as a great innovation.

	BEIJING, Aug. 11 (Xinhua) -- Figures from some Asian and Latin American countries have told Xinhua correspondents that they have unlimited love and admiration for Chairman Mao Zedong. They enthusiastically praised Chairman Mao as the greatest contemporary Marxist-Leninist and Mao Zedong Thought as the beacon for the revolutionary struggles of the oppressed peoples.

	Two Japanese figures from Yamaguchi Prefecture told Chinese correspondents: "Chairman Mao is the beacon of world revolution. Mao Zedong Thought is the pinnacle of contemporary Marxism-Leninism. It is applicable not only to the Chinese revolution but also to the world revolution." They enthusiastically said: "When we talk about Chairman Mao, we say he is the leader of the world revolution; when we talk about China, we say it is the Yan'an of the world revolution, the red base of the world revolution." The two Japanese figures praised the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China as an innovation. They believe that this great revolution is something that the world has never seen before. The victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution of the Chinese people is also a victory for the Japanese people. We attach great importance to this victory.

	U Aung Tha, an executive committee member of the Myanmar People's Peace Committee, said, "The people of Myanmar love, respect, and admire Chairman Mao Zedong. Chairman Mao is not only the great leader of the Chinese people but also the great leader of the revolutionary people of the world."

	Pakistani writer Amanullah said: "Mao Zedong is the greatest contemporary Marxist-Leninist. His works summarize the revolutionary experiences of the Chinese people and the revolutionary peoples of the world, greatly enriching and developing Marxism-Leninism."

	A worker from the Farmers' Committee of Khachar County in West Pakistan said enthusiastically, "Mao Zedong is the greatest leader of the revolutionary peoples of the world." He said: "All Marxist-Leninists in the world know that Chairman Mao Zedong not only has a deep understanding of Marx's scientific socialism but also, like the great Lenin, develops Marxism-Leninism according to the requirements of his own country and the times."

	Pakistani famous writer and executive secretary of the Pakistan Writers' Association, Shakir Siddiqui, who attended the recent emergency meeting of Asian and African writers in Beijing, said in Karachi: "Chairman Mao is a world figure. Under his leadership, China has achieved unprecedented glorious achievements in such a short time."

	He said, Chairman Mao's works are not only a great treasure for the Chinese people but also a great treasure for all oppressed peoples in the world. Mao Zedong Thought has become the beacon for all oppressed peoples of Asia and Africa to find the correct direction and to inspire them to unite and fight.

	Franco, a representative from Panama who attended the 12th World Conference for the Prohibition of Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, highly evaluated Mao Zedong Thought, saying: "The Chinese people have united under Mao Zedong Thought, and Mao Zedong Thought has combined with the creative activities of the people." He said: "Our task is to learn Mao Zedong Thought and use it as our guide for the revolution."

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, Aug. 9 -- Amarte, who recently returned to Nepal after recuperating at the Asia-Africa Students' Sanatorium in Beijing, told Xinhua correspondents: "Today, the Chinese people, under the leadership of Chairman Mao, are advancing at a tremendous speed towards the development of their own country." In China, workers, peasants, students, intellectuals, and office workers, from the young to the old, under the guidance of Chairman Mao's thoughts, are exerting their wisdom in their respective fields of work, making the impossible possible.

	Amarte said: Today, under the leadership of Chairman Mao, China is vigorously carrying out the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. This revolution has won the heartfelt praise of the awakened revolutionary peoples of the world, because the awakened revolutionary peoples of the world all love and praise China as the rain in the dry season and the sun in the winter.

### 亚洲拉美人士表示无限热爱和敬仰毛主席　用毛泽东思想作为革命的指南　中国无产阶级文化大革命是伟大的创举

1966-08-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　亚洲拉美人士表示无限热爱和敬仰毛主席
　　用毛泽东思想作为革命的指南
　　中国无产阶级文化大革命是伟大的创举
    新华社十一日讯　亚洲和拉丁美洲一些国家的人士向新华社记者发表谈话，表示无限热爱、无限敬仰毛泽东主席。他们满怀激情地赞扬毛主席是当代最伟大的马克思列宁主义者，毛泽东思想是被压迫人民进行革命斗争的指路明灯。
    日本山口县的两位日本人士对中国记者说：毛泽东主席是世界革命的灯塔，毛泽东思想是当代马克思列宁主义的顶峰。它既适用于中国革命，也适用于世界革命。他们热情地说：当我们说到毛主席的时候，我们说他是世界革命的领袖；当我们说到中国的时候，是说它是世界革命的延安，是世界革命的红色根据地。这两位日本人士赞扬中国的无产阶级文化大革命是创举。他们认为，这个大革命是全世界从来没有搞过的。中国人民无产阶级文化大革命的胜利，同时也是日本人民的胜利，我们特别重视这个胜利。
    缅甸保卫世界和平委员会执行委员吴昂扎说，“缅甸人民热爱、尊重和敬仰毛泽东主席。毛主席不仅是中国人民的伟大领袖，也是全世界革命人民的伟大领袖。”
    巴基斯坦作家阿马努拉说：“毛泽东是当代最伟大的马克思列宁主义者。他的著作总结了中国人民和世界革命人民革命斗争的经验，大大地丰富和发展了马克思列宁主义。”
    西巴基斯坦哈查拉县农民委员会的一位工作人员热情洋溢地说，毛泽东是全世界革命人民最伟大的领袖。他说：“全世界的马克思列宁主义者都知道，毛泽东主席不仅对马克思的科学社会主义有深刻的研究，而且象伟大的列宁一样，根据本国社会和时代的要求，发展了马克思列宁主义。”
    曾经出席最近在北京召开的亚非作家紧急会议的巴基斯坦著名作家、巴基斯坦作家协会执行秘书肖卡特·西迪基在卡拉奇发表谈话说：“毛泽东主席是世界伟人。在他的领导下，中国在这样短短的时间里取得了辉煌的成就，这是史无前例的。”
    他说，毛主席的著作不仅是中国人民的巨大财富，也是世界上一切被压迫人民的巨大财富。毛泽东思想成了所有被压迫的亚非人民找到正确方向和鼓舞他们团结一致进行战斗的指路明灯。
    出席第十二届禁止原氢弹世界大会的巴拿马代表弗朗哥高度评价毛泽东思想说：“中国人民在毛泽东思想下团结起来了，毛泽东思想同人民群众的创造性活动结合起来了。”他说：“我们的任务是学习毛泽东思想，用毛泽东思想作为我们革命的指南”。
    据新华社加德满都九日电　在北京亚非学生疗养院疗养后最近返回尼泊尔的阿玛泰向新华社记者说：“今天中国人民在毛主席领导下正以巨大速度朝着发展自己国家的方向迈进。”在中国，工人、农民、学生、知识分子和机关工作人员，从年幼到年老的一切人，在毛主席思想指引下，在他们各自的工作范围内发挥自己的智慧，使一些被认为不可能的事情成为可能。
    阿玛泰说：今天中国在毛主席领导下，正在轰轰烈烈地进行着文化大革命。这场革命得到世界上觉醒的革命人民衷心的赞扬，因为全世界觉醒的革命人民都把中国当作旱季的雨和冬天的太阳那样来热爱和赞颂的。
