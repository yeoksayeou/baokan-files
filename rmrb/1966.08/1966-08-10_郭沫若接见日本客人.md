Title: Guo Moruo Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-08-10
Edition: 6

The honorary president of the China-Japan Friendship Association, Guo Moruo, held a reception this afternoon for the entire delegation of the Japanese Academic Observation Team led by Hanzui Ben. He had a friendly conversation with them. Present at the reception were Wang Xiaoyun, Zhang Yu, and others.

### 郭沫若接见日本客人

1966-08-10
第6版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见日本客人
    新华社九日讯　中日友好协会名誉会长郭沫若，今天下午接见了以幡井勉为首的日本学术参观团全体人员，同他们进行了友好谈话。接见时在座的有王晓云、张雨等。
