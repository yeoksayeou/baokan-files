Title: On New Democracy
Author: Mao Zedong
Date: 1966-08-18
Edition: 4

Lu Xun is the commander of China's cultural revolution. He is not only a great literary figure but also a great thinker and a great revolutionary. Lu Xun's spirit is the hardest, he has not an ounce of servility or flattery, which is the most precious characteristic of the people in colonial and semi-colonial countries. Lu Xun is the one who, on the cultural front, represents the majority of the nation, and who charges forward most correctly, most bravely, most resolutely, most loyally, and most enthusiastically against the enemy. The direction Lu Xun points to is the direction of the new culture of the Chinese nation.

### 《新民主主义论》

1966-08-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　《新民主主义论》
　　鲁迅是中国文化革命的主将，他不但是伟大的文学家，而且是伟大的思想家和伟大的革命家。鲁迅的骨头是最硬的，他没有丝毫的奴颜和媚骨，这是殖民地半殖民地人民最可宝贵的性格。鲁迅是在文化战线上，代表全民族的大多数，向着敌人冲锋陷阵的最正确、最勇敢、最坚决、最忠实、最热忱的空前的民族英雄。鲁迅的方向，就是中华民族新文化的方向。
