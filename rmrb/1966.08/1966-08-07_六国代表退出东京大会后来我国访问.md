Title: Six Nations' Representatives Withdraw from Tokyo Conference and Visit China
Author:
Date: 1966-08-07
Edition: 2

Uphold the Correct Line and Maintain the Glorious Tradition of the Anti-Atomic and Hydrogen Bomb Movement

Six nations' representatives, who withdrew from the 12th World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs in Tokyo to uphold the glorious tradition of the anti-atomic and hydrogen bomb movement, arrived in China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Peace and Disarmament. They arrived in Beijing by plane from Guangzhou this evening and were warmly welcomed by people from various circles in the capital.

Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Association for Peace and Disarmament, Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity and Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Association for Peace and Disarmament, Liu Ningyi, Head of the Chinese delegation to the 12th World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Association for Peace and Disarmament, and others, went to the airport to welcome the foreign friends.

When the guests stepped off the plane, the people from various circles in the capital who were waiting at the airport beat drums and gongs, waved bouquets, and paid high tribute to the foreign friends who had insisted on the correct line at the Tokyo conference and fought resolutely to uphold the glorious tradition of the anti-atomic and hydrogen bomb movement. The Young Pioneers presented flowers to the foreign friends.

The foreign friends who arrived in Beijing this evening are: Betty Little, Henry Bly, and William Morrow from the Australian delegation; Nancy Whalan Goddard from the New Zealand delegation; Lakshman Mututantri and Mrs. B.B. Mututantri from the Ceylon delegation; a representative from the Basutoland delegation; Dupire from the Belgian delegation; and Osman Hassan Ahmed from the Sudanese delegation.

Also present at the airport to welcome the guests were responsible persons from various people's organizations and members of the Chinese delegation to the 12th World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, including Nan Hancheng, Xu Guangping, Zhang Xiangshan, Tang Mingzhao, Wu Xueqian, Gu Dachun, Wu Yunfu, Yong Longgui, Liu Qingyang, Zhao Anbo, Qian Dawei, and Xu Kui.

Japanese peace activist Nishi Sonji and Sudanese peace activist Kail, who are in Beijing, also went to the airport to welcome the guests.

### 六国代表退出东京大会后来我国访问

1966-08-07
第2版()
专栏：

    坚持正确路线，维护禁止原子弹氢弹运动的光荣传统
    六国代表退出东京大会后来我国访问
    郭沫若廖承志刘宁一和首都各界群众到机场热烈欢迎
    新华社六日讯　为维护禁止原子弹氢弹运动的光荣传统而退出第十二届禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会的澳大利亚、新西兰、锡兰、巴苏陀兰、比利时和苏丹代表团的代表们，应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的邀请到中国访问，今天晚间由广州乘飞机到达北京，受到首都各界群众的热烈欢迎。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，中国亚非团结委员会主席、中国和委会副主席廖承志，准备出席第十二届禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会的中国代表团团长、中华全国总工会主席、中国和委会副主席刘宁一等，到机场热烈欢迎各国朋友。
    当客人们走下飞机时，在机场上迎候的首都各界群众敲锣打鼓，挥舞花束，向这次在东京大会上坚持正确路线，为维护禁止原子弹氢弹运动的光荣传统而进行坚决斗争的各国朋友致以崇高的敬意。少先队员们向各国朋友献花。
    今晚到达北京的各国朋友是：澳大利亚代表团贝蒂·利特尔，亨里·布利，威廉·莫罗；新西兰代表团南希·惠兰·戈达德；锡兰代表团拉克森·穆图坦特里，布·布·穆图坦特里夫人；巴苏陀兰代表团代表；比利时代表团杜皮尔；苏丹代表团奥斯曼·哈桑·艾哈迈德。
    到机场欢迎的，还有各人民团体负责人和准备出席第十二届禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会的中国代表团成员，他们当中有南汉宸、许广平、张香山、唐明照、吴学谦、顾大椿、伍云甫、勇龙桂、刘清扬、赵安博、钱大卫、徐葵等。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一，苏丹和平人士凯尔，也到机场欢迎。
