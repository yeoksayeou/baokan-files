Title: The Song "Never Forget"
Author:
Date: 1966-08-26
Edition: 4

The song "Never Forget"
Jixian County Cultural Center

	The song echoes far and wide, the east wind sweeps across the land.
	We sing of four "Never Forget," each word and sentence is truth,
	The banner is clear, guiding the direction.
	Never forget class struggle,
	Eyes clear, distinguishing right from wrong,
	Stand firm in eliminating the bourgeoisie and establishing the proletariat,
	Dare to brave the storms and waves.
	Never forget the dictatorship of the proletariat,
	Hold the power tightly, grasp the gun firmly,
	Fear neither open nor covert attacks,
	The people's country will last forever.
	Never forget to emphasize politics,
	Keep the four firsts in mind,
	Dare to attack the "I," the self,
	Forever revolutionary, heart with the Party.
	Never forget to hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought,
	Five continents and four seas in our hearts,
	Swear to carry the revolution to the end,
	A new world we shall create.
	Following Chairman Mao,
	Following the Communist Party,
	Forever toward the direction of victory.
	Following Chairman Mao,
	Following the Communist Party, forever on the road of revolution.

### “念念不忘”歌
集贤县文化馆
1966-08-26
第4版()
专栏：“哈尔滨之夏”节目选

  “念念不忘”歌
   集贤县文化馆
   歌声万里，东风浩荡。
   我们唱四个“念念不忘”，
   字字句句是真理，
   旗帜鲜明指方向。
   念念不忘阶级斗争，
   是非分明眼睛亮，
   兴无灭资立场稳，
   大风大浪也敢闯。
   念念不忘无产阶级专政，
   掌好印把子握紧枪，
   明枪暗箭都不怕，
   人民江山万年长。
   念念不忘突出政治，
   四个第一记心上，
   敢向“我”字猛开火，
   永远革命心向党。
   念念不忘高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，
   五洲四海心里装，
   誓把革命干到底，
   新的世界我们创。
   跟着毛主席，
   跟着共产党，
   永远朝着胜利的方向。
   跟着毛主席，
   跟着共产党永远走在革命的大路上。
