Title: The Third Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Party is Broadcast
Author:
Date: 1966-08-15
Edition: 3

After the broadcast of the communiqué of the Third Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Party, the revolutionary masses of the capital were overjoyed and organized joyous teams to report the good news to the reception station for the masses at the Party Central Committee. The picture shows the joyous masses shouting slogans.
