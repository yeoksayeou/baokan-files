Title: China-Africa Friendship Association Holds Tea Party to Celebrate the Third Anniversary of the Congolese Revolution
Author:
Date: 1966-08-14
Edition: 4

The China-Africa Friendship Association held a tea party on the afternoon of August 13 to warmly celebrate the third anniversary of the Congolese revolution.

The acting charge d'affaires of the Congolese embassy in China, Jean Daniel Bakula, and his wife were invited to attend.

Nan Hanjin, vice-chairman of the China-Africa Friendship Association, and Bakula both spoke at the tea party, expressing their shared wish for the friendship between the peoples of China and Congo to continue to develop and strengthen.

Those present at the tea party included Ai Wen, Shen Zizhu, and others.

Other diplomatic officials from the Congolese embassy in China and Congolese students studying in China were also invited to attend the tea party.

After the tea party, the large-scale color documentary "Song of Military Reclamation" was screened.

### 中非友协举行茶会庆祝刚果（布）革命三周年

1966-08-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　中非友协举行茶会庆祝刚果（布）革命三周年
    新华社十三日讯　中国非洲人民友好协会副会长南汉宸今天下午举行茶会，热烈庆祝刚果（布）革命三周年。
    刚果（布）驻中国大使馆临时代办让·丹尼尔·巴库拉和夫人应邀出席。
    南汉宸和巴库拉在茶会上讲话的时候，共同祝愿中国和刚果（布）两国人民的友谊日益得到发展和巩固。
    出席茶会的有艾汶，沈兹九等。
    刚果（布）驻中国大使馆的其他外交官员和在中国的留学生也应邀出席了茶会。
    茶会后放映了我国大型彩色纪录片《军垦战歌》。
