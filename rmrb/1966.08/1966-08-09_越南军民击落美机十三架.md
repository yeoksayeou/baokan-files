Title: Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down 13 U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-08-09
Edition: 6

Vietnamese military and civilians shoot down 13 U.S. aircraft

	According to Xinhua News Agency in Hanoi on the 8th, it was announced here on the evening of the 8th: The military and civilians of Hai Phong City and the provinces of An Zang, Lang Son, and Quang Ninh in North Vietnam foiled multiple attacks by U.S. aggressor aircraft on the 8th, shooting down six U.S. aircraft and capturing a U.S. pilot.

	Another report: The military and civilians of Hai Phong City and the provinces of Bac Ninh, Quang Ninh, Bac Thai, and Thanh Hoa fiercely repelled the invading U.S. aggressor aircraft on the 7th, shooting down seven U.S. aircraft and capturing a group of U.S. pilots.

### 越南军民击落美机十三架

1966-08-09
第6版()
专栏：

    越南军民击落美机十三架
    据新华社河内八日电　据这里八日晚上公布：越南北方海防市以及安沛、谅山和广宁等省的军民，八日又粉碎了美国强盗飞机的多次进攻，共击落美国飞机六架，并且活捉了美国飞行员。
    另讯：海防市和河北、广宁、北太、清化等省军民，七日猛烈迎击入侵的美国强盗飞机，共击落美机七架，活捉一批美国飞行员。
