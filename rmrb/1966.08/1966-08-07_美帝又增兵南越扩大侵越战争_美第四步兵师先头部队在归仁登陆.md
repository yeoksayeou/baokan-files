Title: The U.S. Again Sends Troops to South Vietnam, Expanding the War of Aggression
Author:
Date: 1966-08-07
Edition: 5

The U.S. Fourth Infantry Division's advance unit of 3,000 troops landed in the South Vietnamese port of Qui Nhon on August 6, further escalating the war of aggression in Vietnam.

According to Western news agencies, these 3,000 U.S. troops belong to the Second Brigade of the Fourth Infantry Division. Their arrival increases the total number of U.S. invading troops in South Vietnam to approximately 285,000.

The Fourth Infantry Division is one of the four divisions in the U.S. "strategic reserve." The deployment of the "strategic reserve" to the Vietnam battlefield to be sent to their deaths further illustrates the predicament the U.S. faces in the Vietnam War.

### 美帝又增兵南越扩大侵越战争  美第四步兵师先头部队在归仁登陆

1966-08-07
第5版()
专栏：

    美帝又增兵南越扩大侵越战争
    美第四步兵师先头部队在归仁登陆
    新华社六日讯　西贡消息：美国第四步兵师的一支先头部队三千人，六日开始在南越中部归仁海港登陆，以进一步扩大侵越战争。
    据西方通讯社报道，这三千名美军属于第四步兵师的第二旅，他们的到达使在南越的美国侵略军总数增加到大约二十八万五千人。
    第四步兵师是属于美国“战略后备部队”的四个师之一。抽调“战略后备部队”到南越战场送死，进一步说明了美国在侵越战争中的困境。
