Title: Afghan Journalists' Delegation Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-08-09
Edition: 6

A delegation of Afghan journalists, led by Qaziya Korichada, a literary consultant from the Afghan Ministry of News and Culture, departed from Beijing this morning. The delegation will continue their visit to southern parts of China before returning to their home country.

### 阿富汗新闻工作者代表团离京

1966-08-09
第6版()
专栏：

    阿富汗新闻工作者代表团离京
    据新华社七日讯　由阿富汗新闻文化部文学顾问齐亚·考里察达率领的阿富汗新闻工作者代表团于今晨离京去我国南方各地继续访问后回国。
