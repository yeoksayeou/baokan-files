Title: Celebrating the 21st Anniversary of Indonesia's Independence
Author:
Date: 1966-08-18
Edition: 5

	Indonesian Charge d'Affaires to China, Baren Sudadishastra, held a reception tonight to celebrate the 21st anniversary of the proclamation of the Republic of Indonesia's independence.

	Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs Qian Qichen, Deputy Director of the State Council's Foreign Affairs Office Chu Tunan, and other relevant officials attended the reception at the invitation of the Indonesian Embassy in China.

### 庆祝印度尼西亚宣布独立二十一周年　印尼驻华使馆临时代办举行招待会

1966-08-18
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝印度尼西亚宣布独立二十一周年
　　印尼驻华使馆临时代办举行招待会
    新华社十七日讯　印度尼西亚驻中国大使馆临时代办巴伦·苏达迪沙斯特拉今晚举行招待会，庆祝印度尼西亚共和国宣布独立二十一周年。
    外交部副部长韩念龙，对外文委副主任楚图南等有关方面负责人应邀出席了招待会。
