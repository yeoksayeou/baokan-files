Title: Cambodian Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Kan Tor Nor Sends a Telegram to Vice Premier Chen Yi
Author:
Date: 1966-08-27
Edition: 5

Cambodian Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Kan Tor Nor sends a telegram to Vice Premier Chen Yi, expressing gratitude for China's consistent support for the Cambodian people's struggle.

	New China News Agency, August 26th: Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi received a telegram from Cambodian Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Kan Tor Nor on August 20th. The text of the telegram is as follows:

	To His Excellency Chen Yi, Vice Premier and Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

	I am honored to inform you that the Royal Government has learned with great interest about the statement issued by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China on August 7, 1966. In the statement, the Chinese government and people resolutely condemn the aggressive crimes committed by American imperialism and its lackeys in the village of Trolot in the province of Pursat.

	Please allow me, on behalf of the entire Cambodian people and the Royal Government, to express our sincere and heartfelt gratitude to the People's Republic of China for its consistent support for the Cambodian people's just struggle, under the wise leadership of His Majesty Norodom Sihanouk, to safeguard the independence, neutrality, and territorial integrity of the Kingdom.

	The support from the friendly People's Republic of China is an invaluable encouragement to the Cambodian people when they face the evil plots of American imperialism and its accomplices.

	Please accept my highest regards.

	Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia
	Norodom Kan Tor Nor

	Phnom Penh, August 20, 1966

### 柬埔寨首相兼外交大臣康托尔致电陈毅副总理  感谢中国人民一贯支持柬埔寨人民斗争

1966-08-27
第5版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨首相兼外交大臣康托尔致电陈毅副总理
    感谢中国人民一贯支持柬埔寨人民斗争
    新华社二十六日讯  国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅收到了柬埔寨首相兼外交大臣康托尔二十日发来的一份电报。电文如下：北京中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅先生阁下阁下：
    我荣幸地告诉你，王国政府以极大的兴趣获悉中华人民共和国外交部一九六六年八月七日发表的声明。在声明中，中国政府和中国人民坚决谴责美帝国主义及其仆从对磅湛省特洛塔村的侵略罪行。
    请允许我代表全体柬埔寨人民和王国政府向中华人民共和国表示热烈的和深切的谢意，感谢中华人民共和国一贯支持柬埔寨人民在尊敬的国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王的英明领导下，为维护王国的独立、中立和领土完整而进行的正义斗争。
    在柬埔寨人民面临美帝国主义及其帮凶的罪恶阴谋时，友好的中华人民共和国的支持是对柬埔寨人民的不可估量的鼓舞。
    顺致最崇高的敬意。
  柬埔寨王国政府首相
  兼外交大臣
      诺罗敦·康托尔
     一九六六年八月二十日于金边
