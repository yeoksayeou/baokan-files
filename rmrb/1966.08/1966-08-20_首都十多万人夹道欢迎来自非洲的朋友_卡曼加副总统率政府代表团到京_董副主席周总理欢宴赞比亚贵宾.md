Title: Hundreds of Thousands of Beijing Residents Line the Streets to Welcome Friends from Africa
Author:
Date: 1966-08-20
Edition: 1

Vice President Ruben K. Kamanga of Zambia and the Zambian government delegation he leads arrived in Beijing today by special plane for a friendly visit to our country at the invitation of the Chinese government. They were greeted by more than ten thousand people from various sectors of the capital in a grand and enthusiastic manner.

Vice Chairman Dong Biwu, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife Zhang Qie, Vice Premier Li Xiannian, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Yang Mingxuan, and others welcomed the Zambian guests at the airport.

Accompanying Vice President Kamanga on his visit to China were Zambia's Minister of Foreign Affairs Simon Mwansa Kapwepwe, Mrs. Kapwepwe, Central Province Minister Shamabanse, Parliamentary Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Sikasula, Permanent Secretary of the National Development and Planning Office Julu, Director of the Treaty and Economic Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Mukka, and Presidential Office official Miti.

Today, the capital's airport and city streets were filled with a festive atmosphere of friendship. From Dongchang'an Street to the Western Suburbs Guest House, the welcoming crowds stretched for more than ten miles. Giant portraits of Vice President Kamanga were erected at major intersections along the route to the guesthouse. The streets were adorned with the national flags of China and Zambia and colorful banners. Large red banners hung on tall buildings on both sides of the street, bearing slogans such as "Long Live China-Zambia Friendship!" and "Oppose Imperialism and Old and New Colonialism!"

Vice President Kamanga and Minister Kapwepwe, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi, rode in open-top cars through the city, receiving the enthusiastic welcome of workers, students, government officials, People's Liberation Army soldiers, and militia from the capital. As the guests' convoy passed, the streets were filled with singing, slogans, and the sound of drums. Flowers, ribbons, and flags danced in the air.

When the guests arrived at Tiananmen Square, the square was immediately filled with singing, dancing, and the sound of drums. The welcoming crowd repeatedly shouted "Welcome! Welcome!" More than 2,500 amateur artists from the capital performed dances such as "The Thoughts of Mao Zedong Shine Bright," "Sunflowers Turn to the Sun," "Learn from Dazhai Across the Country," "Female Militia Dance," and "National Unity" to the tunes of "The Ship Sails with the Helmsman" and others. Children released countless colorful balloons into the sky. Vice President Kamanga and Minister Kapwepwe repeatedly waved and greeted the welcoming crowd.

The guests' special plane landed at 10:30 a.m. After Vice President Kamanga and the other guests disembarked, Vice Chairman Dong Biwu and Premier Zhou Enlai walked forward to warmly shake hands with Vice President Kamanga and the other guests.

A grand welcoming ceremony was held at the airport. The band played the national anthems of both countries. Vice President Kamanga, accompanied by Vice Chairman Dong Biwu and Premier Zhou Enlai, inspected the army honor guard. Young Pioneers presented flowers to all the guests.

The guests circled the field and met with the welcoming crowd. The airport was immediately filled with cheers. People shouted slogans such as "Welcome Zambian Guests," "Long Live the Unity of the People of Asia and Africa," and so on.

Those who also went to the airport to welcome the guests today included Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Gao Chongmin, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Fu Zuoyi, Vice Chief of the General Staff of the People's Liberation Army Li Tianyou, and leaders from various government departments, people's organizations, and Beijing Municipality.

Envoys from various countries to China also went to the airport to welcome the guests.

Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, who went to Guangzhou to welcome the guests, accompanied Vice President Kamanga from Guangzhou to Beijing.

According to a Xinhua News Agency report from Guangzhou on the 19th, the Zambian government delegation left Guangzhou by special plane this morning to go to Beijing. Guangdong Province Acting Governor Lin Liming and others went to the airport to see them off.

According to a Xinhua News Agency report on the 19th, Vice Chairman Dong Biwu and Premier Zhou Enlai held a banquet this evening to warmly welcome Vice President Ruben K. Kamanga of Zambia and the Zambian government delegation he leads.

Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice President Kamanga both spoke at the banquet. Premier Zhou Enlai emphasized that in the past two years, the friendly cooperative relations between China and Zambia have developed to a new level. The Chinese people will always stand firmly with the people of Zambia, Zimbabwe, and other African countries to carry on the struggle against imperialism and old and new colonialism to the end.

Vice President Kamanga said in his speech that the people of Zambia and China are now good friends and are determined to be good friends forever. Zambia is determined to strengthen the bonds of friendship between the two countries.

Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife Zhang Qie, Vice Premier Li Xiannian, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Yang Mingxuan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Gao Chongmin and Xu Deheng, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Fu Zuoyi and Cai Tingkai, Vice Chief of the General Staff of the People's Liberation Army Li Tianyou, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei and Han Nianlong, and leaders from various government departments, various democratic parties, people's organizations, and Beijing Municipality attended the banquet.

Envoys from various countries to China were also invited to attend the banquet. (Photo attached)

Vice President Kamanga and other Zambian guests, accompanied by Vice Chairman Dong Biwu and Premier Zhou Enlai, stepped out of the airport and received a warm welcome from the crowd. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter)

### 首都十多万人夹道欢迎来自非洲的朋友　卡曼加副总统率政府代表团到京　董副主席周总理欢宴赞比亚贵宾

1966-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　首都十多万人夹道欢迎来自非洲的朋友
　　卡曼加副总统率政府代表团到京
　　董副主席周总理欢宴赞比亚贵宾
    新华社十九日讯　赞比亚共和国副总统鲁本·奇坦迪卡·卡曼加和由他率领的赞比亚共和国政府代表团，应邀来我国进行友好访问，今天上午乘专机到达北京，受到首都各界十多万人隆重热烈的夹道欢迎。
    董必武副主席，周恩来总理，陈毅副总理和夫人张茜，李先念副总理，杨明轩副委员长等，到机场欢迎赞比亚贵宾。
    陪同卡曼加副总统前来我国访问的，有赞比亚共和国外交部长西蒙·姆旺萨·卡普韦普韦，卡普韦普韦夫人，中央省驻节部长沙马班塞，外交部议会秘书西卡苏拉，国家发展和计划办公室常任秘书朱卢，外交部条约与经济事务司司长穆卡，总统办公室官员米提。
    今天，首都机场和市内呈现一片友好欢乐的景象。从东长安街到西郊迎宾馆的街道上，欢迎贵宾的群众行列有十多里长。在通往宾馆的重要路口竖立着卡曼加副总统的巨幅画像。街道上空飘扬着中赞两国国旗和五彩缤纷的旗帜，两旁高大建筑物上挂着许多红色长幅，上面写着：“中国赞比亚友好万岁！”“反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义！”
    卡曼加副总统和卡普韦普韦部长，分别由周恩来总理和陈毅副总理陪同，乘敞篷汽车经过市区，接受了首都工人、学生、机关干部、人民解放军指战员、民兵的夹道欢迎。贵宾们的车队经过时，街道上歌声、口号声和鼓乐声响成一片，花束、彩带和彩旗交相舞动。
    贵宾们到达天安门广场时，广场上顿时歌舞欢腾，锣鼓喧天。欢迎的群众不断高呼“欢迎！”“欢迎！”首都两千五百人的业余文艺工作者，在《大海航行靠舵手》等乐曲声中，为贵宾们表演了《毛泽东思想闪金光》、《葵花向太阳》、《全国学大寨》、《女民兵舞》、《民族大团结》等舞蹈节目。少年儿童向空中放出了数不清的彩色气球。卡曼加副总统和卡普韦普韦部长不断向欢迎群众招手致意。
    贵宾们乘的专机是在十时半降落的。卡曼加副总统等贵宾走下飞机后，董必武副主席和周恩来总理等迎上前去，和卡曼加副总统等贵宾热烈握手。
    机场上举行了隆重的欢迎仪式。乐队奏赞中两国国歌。卡曼加副总统在董必武副主席和周恩来总理陪同下，检阅了陆军仪仗队。少先队员向全体贵宾献花。
    贵宾们绕场一周，和欢迎群众见面。机场上顿时欢声雷动。人们高呼：“欢迎赞比亚贵宾”、“亚非人民团结万岁”等口号。
    今天到机场欢迎的，还有政协全国委员会副主席高崇民，国防委员会副主席傅作义，中国人民解放军副总参谋长李天佑，政府各部门和各人民团体以及北京市的负责人。
    各国驻中国的使节也到机场迎接。
    专程前往广州迎接的我国外交部副部长姬鹏飞，陪同卡曼加副总统由广州到达北京。
    据新华社广州十九日电　赞比亚共和国政府代表团，今天早上乘专机离开广州前往北京。广东省代省长林李明等到机场欢送。
    据新华社十九日讯　董必武副主席和周恩来总理今晚举行宴会，热烈欢迎赞比亚共和国副总统鲁本·奇坦迪卡·卡曼加和由他率领的赞比亚共和国政府代表团。
    周恩来总理和鲁本·奇坦迪卡·卡曼加副总统先后在宴会上讲话。周恩来总理强调说，这两年来，中赞两国的友好合作关系有了新的发展。中国人民始终不渝地同赞比亚人民、津巴布韦人民和非洲各国人民站在一起，把反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义的斗争进行到底。
    卡曼加副总统在讲话中说，赞中两国人民现在是好朋友，并且准备将来永远做好朋友，赞比亚决心使两国的友好纽带不断加强。
    国务院副总理陈毅和夫人张茜，国务院副总理李先念，人大常委会副委员长杨明轩，政协全国委员会副主席高崇民、许德珩，国防委员会副主席傅作义、蔡廷锴，中国人民解放军副总参谋长李天佑，外交部副部长姬鹏飞、韩念龙和政府各部门、各民主党派、各人民团体以及北京市的负责人出席了宴会。
    各国驻中国使节也应邀出席了宴会。（附图片）
