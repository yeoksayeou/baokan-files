Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Four More U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-08-11
Edition: 6

North Vietnamese military and civilians have shot down four more U.S. aircraft.

	New China News Agency, Hanoi, August 10 — According to reports from here, the military and civilians of An Phu Province in North Vietnam shot down one invading U.S. aggressor aircraft on August 9. Additionally, it was reported that the military and civilians of Nghe An Province shot down two invading U.S. aggressor aircraft on August 7, and the military and civilians of Ha Tinh Province, in addition to the previously announced downed aircraft on August 4, also shot down another U.S. aircraft.

### 越南北方军民又击落美机四架

1966-08-11
第6版()
专栏：

　　越南北方军民又击落美机四架
    新华社河内十日电　据这里公布，越南北方安沛省军民九日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架。另据补充报道，义安省军民七日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机两架，广平省军民四日除前已公布的击落敌机一架外，还击落另一架美国飞机。
