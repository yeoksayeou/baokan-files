Title: Bulletin Arrives at the Workshop, Everyone Laughs Together
Author: Zhao Zhifang
Date: 1966-08-15
Edition: 4

The bulletin arrives at the workshop, and everyone laughs together. This scene vividly depicts the joyous atmosphere in the workshop after the publication of the "May 16th Notification." The workers, with bright smiles on their faces, are reading the bulletin attentively. Their expressions are full of excitement and determination, reflecting the great enthusiasm and confidence they have in carrying out the Cultural Revolution. This sketch captures a moment of unity and joy, showcasing the workers' strong support for the revolutionary cause.
