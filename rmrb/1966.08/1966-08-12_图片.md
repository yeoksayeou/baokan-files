Title: Beijing's People Joyfully Celebrate the Party Central Committee's Decision on the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1966-08-12
Edition: 3

The broad masses of the people in Beijing celebrated throughout the night, enthusiastically hailing the Party Central Committee's decision on the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. Young Pioneers took to the streets in the dead of night, banging drums and gongs, to report their joy to the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao.
