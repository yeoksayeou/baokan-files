Title: China and Zambia Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1966-08-23
Edition: 6

The governments of the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Zambia signed a cultural cooperation agreement in Beijing today.

Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice President Ruben Chitandika Kamanga of Zambia attended the signing ceremony.

Signing the agreement on behalf of both sides were: Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi of the People's Republic of China, and Minister of Foreign Affairs Simon Mwansa Kapwepwe of the Republic of Zambia.

Also present at the signing ceremony were Zhang Zhirou, Director of the State Council's Foreign Cultural Relations Commission, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qiao Guanhua of China. Members of the Zambian government delegation also attended the ceremony.

### 中赞两国政府签署文化合作协定  周恩来总理和卡曼加副总统参加了签字仪式

1966-08-23
第6版()
专栏：

    中赞两国政府签署文化合作协定
    周恩来总理和卡曼加副总统参加了签字仪式
    新华社二十二日讯　中华人民共和国政府和赞比亚共和国政府，今天在北京签署了一项文化合作协定。
    国务院总理周恩来和赞比亚共和国副总统鲁本·奇坦迪卡·卡曼加，参加了协定的签字仪式。
    代表双方在协定上签字的是：国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅，赞比亚共和国外交部长西蒙·姆旺萨·卡普韦普韦。
    参加签字仪式的，还有我国对外文委主任张奚若，外交部副部长姬鹏飞等。赞比亚共和国政府代表团的成员也参加了签字仪式。
