Title: PLA Troops in Beijing Hail Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1966-08-19
Edition: 6

	Soldiers and officers of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Beijing express their boundless excitement and pay tribute to the great leader, great commander, and great helmsman Chairman Mao. They pledge: "We will forever follow the Party and Chairman Mao in making revolution, and we will forever carry out revolution in accordance with Mao Zedong Thought."
