Title: Japanese Social Party Parliamentary Delegation Holds Farewell Banquet
Author:
Date: 1966-08-13
Edition: 4

A delegation of Japanese Social Party parliamentarians, led by Tate Kenjiro, held a farewell banquet this evening.

Attendees included relevant officials such as Liao Chengzhi, Zhao Anbo, Xiao Xiangqian, and Wang Xiaoyun.

Japanese peace advocate Sei Onji Koji, who is currently in Beijing, also attended the banquet.

### 日本社会党国会议员访华团举行告别宴会

1966-08-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　日本社会党国会议员访华团举行告别宴会
    新华社十二日讯　以楯兼次郎为首的日本社会党国会议员访华团，今晚举行告别宴会。
    出席宴会的，有廖承志、赵安博、萧向前、王晓云等有关方面负责人。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也出席了宴会。
