Title: Promote Friendship, Mutual Progress, Joint Improvement, Unite Against Imperialism: The Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational Opens Today
Author:
Date: 1966-08-27
Edition: 4

	Promote Friendship, Mutual Progress, Joint Improvement, Unite Against Imperialism
	The Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational Opens Today

	The Chinese Table Tennis Association Holds a Reception to Warmly Welcome Friends from Various Countries

	Xinhua News Agency, August 26th – The 1966 Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational will open tonight at the Beijing Workers' Gymnasium.

	The male and female athletes from twelve countries and regions participating in this invitational tournament engaged in a traditional activity before the competition – joint practice – and exchanged experiences with each other.

	The invitational tournament will feature seven events. The specific schedule is as follows: August 27th to 30th – men's and women's team competitions; September 1st to 4th – individual events.

	Xinhua News Agency, August 26th – The Chinese Table Tennis Association held a reception tonight to warmly welcome friends from various countries who have come to participate in the 1966 Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational.

	At the reception, filled with a spirit of combative unity and friendship, Chairman Chen Xian of the Chinese Table Tennis Association spoke. He said, "The scale of the Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational is expanding year by year. More and more friends are uniting under the banner of 'Promote Friendship, Mutual Progress, Joint Improvement, Unite Against Imperialism,' making greater contributions to promoting and developing the world's table tennis movement. Chen Xian pointed out in his speech that we believe that through friends' visits to China, it will greatly benefit the people of various countries who stand at the forefront of the anti-imperialist struggle to understand and recognize New China, thereby further strengthening our friendship and advancing the cause of our united struggle against imperialism."

	At the reception, friends from various countries frequently raised their glasses in toasts, expressing their sincere wishes for the success of this Beijing International Table Tennis Invitational.

	Salam Shulafa, the leader of the Palestinian table tennis delegation participating in the invitational for the first time, spoke amidst enthusiastic applause. He said, "This table tennis invitational will further strengthen our friendship and unity. Salam Shulafa said, "The Chinese people are at the forefront of the struggle. Currently, under the guidance of Mao Zedong Thought, the Chinese people are carrying out a Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution to resist the influence of bourgeois ideology. We fully support China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. He also said, "We express our respect for the Vietnamese people's struggle against American aggression and for national salvation, and we congratulate the heroic Vietnamese people on their ultimate victory in the anti-American struggle." Salam Shulafa concluded by shouting, "Long live the friendship of the peoples of the world! Long live the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution of China! Long live the struggle of the Chinese people and their great leader Chairman Mao Zedong!"

	Attending the reception were Vice Ministers of the State Sports Commission Rong Gaotang and Huang Zhong, as well as responsible officials from relevant departments such as Zhao Xiwu and Du Haoran.

### 增进友好  互相促进  共同提高  团结反帝  北京国际乒乓球邀请赛今日开幕  我国乒乓球协会举行招待会热烈欢迎各国朋友

1966-08-27
第4版()
专栏：

    增进友好  互相促进  共同提高  团结反帝
    北京国际乒乓球邀请赛今日开幕
    我国乒乓球协会举行招待会热烈欢迎各国朋友
    新华社二十六日讯　一九六六年北京国际乒乓球邀请赛将于二十七日晚在北京工人体育馆开幕。
    参加这次邀请赛的十二个国家和地区的男、女选手，赛前进行了富有特色的传统活动——共同练习，并且在一起交流了经验。
    邀请赛共进行七个项目的比赛。具体日程是：二十七日——三十日，男、女团体赛；九月一日——四日，单项比赛。
    新华社二十六日讯　中国乒乓球协会今晚举行招待会，热烈欢迎前来参加一九六六年北京国际乒乓球邀请赛的各国朋友。
    在充满着战斗的团结和友谊气氛的招待会上，中国乒乓球协会主席陈先讲了话。他说，北京国际乒乓球邀请赛的规模一年比一年扩大，越来越多的朋友团结在“增进友好、互相促进、共同提高、团结反帝”的旗帜下，对促进与发展世界乒乓球运动作出越来越大的贡献。陈先在讲话中最后指出，我们相信，通过朋友们到中国的访问，将大大有利于站在反帝斗争前列的各国人民对新中国的了解与认识，从而更好地加强我们之间的友好，推进我们团结反帝的事业更加向前发展。
    招待会上，各国朋友相互频频举杯祝酒，热情祝愿这次北京国际乒乓球邀请赛圆满成功。
    第一次参加邀请赛的巴勒斯坦乒乓球代表团团长萨拉姆·舒拉法，在热烈的掌声中讲了话。他说，这次乒乓球邀请赛，将进一步加强我们之间的友谊和团结。萨拉姆·舒拉法说，站在斗争最前列的是中国人民。现在中国人民正在毛泽东思想指导下进行着一场文化大革命，以抵制资产阶级思想影响。我们完全支持中国的这场文化大革命。他还说，我们对越南人民抗美救国斗争表示敬意，并祝贺英雄的越南人民在反美斗争中取得最后胜利。萨拉姆·舒拉法最后高呼：世界和平人民的友谊万岁！中国无产阶级文化大革命万岁！中国人民的斗争和他们的伟大领袖毛泽东主席万岁！
    出席招待会的，有国家体委副主任荣高棠、黄中，以及有关方面负责人赵希武、都浩然等。
