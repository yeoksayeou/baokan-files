Title: Cambodian Sports Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-08-12
Edition: 6

Cambodian Sports Delegation Arrives in Beijing

According to a report from Xinhua News Agency on the 11th, a three-person Cambodian sports delegation arrived in Beijing by plane on the 10th.
