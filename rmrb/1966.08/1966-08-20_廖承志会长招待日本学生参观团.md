Title: Chairman Liao Chengzhi Welcomes Japanese Student Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-08-20
Edition: 5

Chairman Liao Chengzhi of the China-Japan Friendship Association hosted a reception tonight, warmly welcoming the second batch of Japanese student delegation led by Nariyuki Narita.

Liao Chengzhi and Nariyuki Narita both spoke at the reception. They pointed out that the friendship and unity between the peoples and youth of China and Japan will overcome all obstacles and grow stronger.

The reception was filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere.

The second batch of Japanese student delegation, consisting of 99 people, arrived in Beijing by train from Shanghai this morning.

They were greeted enthusiastically by relevant officials and hundreds of students from Beijing's universities at the station.

### 廖承志会长招待日本学生参观团

1966-08-20
第5版()
专栏：

　　廖承志会长招待日本学生参观团
    据新华社十九日讯  中日友好协会会长廖承志今晚举行酒会，热烈欢迎由成田光生率领的第二批日本学生参观团。
    廖承志和成田光生先后在酒会上讲话。他们指出，中日两国人民和青年之间的友好团结，将冲破重重障碍日益增强。
    酒会上洋溢着热情友好的气氛。
    第二批日本学生参观团一行九十九人，是在今天上午乘火车从上海到达北京的。
    他们在车站上受到了有关方面负责人和首都高等学校学生数百人的热烈欢迎。
