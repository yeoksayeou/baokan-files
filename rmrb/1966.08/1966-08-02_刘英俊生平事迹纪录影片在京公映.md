Title: Documentary on Liu Yingjun's Life and Deeds Premieres in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-08-02
Edition: 4

A newsreel introducing the life and deeds of the great communist warrior Liu Yingjun, produced by the August First Film Studio, premiered in the capital today.

This film, through Liu Yingjun's diary manuscripts and various items he left behind, vividly reflects Liu Yingjun's noble qualities and heroic deeds in taking Chairman Mao's books as the guiding direction for all his actions, striving to learn Mao Zedong Thought, faithfully implementing Mao Zedong Thought, enthusiastically propagating Mao Zedong Thought, and bravely defending Mao Zedong Thought. The film also captures the activities of hundreds of millions of people across the nation who are fervently learning from Liu Yingjun.

### 刘英俊生平事迹纪录影片在京公映

1966-08-02
第4版()
专栏：

    刘英俊生平事迹纪录影片在京公映
    据新华社一日讯　介绍伟大的共产主义战士刘英俊生平事迹的新闻纪录片，由八一电影制片厂摄制完成，今天在首都上演。
    这部影片，通过刘英俊的日记手稿和他留下的各种遗物，生动地反映了刘英俊同志把毛主席的书当作一切行动的指导方向，努力学习毛泽东思想，忠实贯彻毛泽东思想，热情宣传毛泽东思想，勇敢捍卫毛泽东思想的高贵品质和英雄行为。影片还拍摄了全国亿万人民满怀激情学习刘英俊的各种活动。
