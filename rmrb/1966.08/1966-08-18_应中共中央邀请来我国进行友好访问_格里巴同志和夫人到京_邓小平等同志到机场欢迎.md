Title: At the Invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Comrade Grjeba and His Wife Arrive in Beijing for a Friendly Visit
Author:
Date: 1966-08-18
Edition: 1

At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Comrade Jacques Grjeba of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Belgium and his wife, Madeleine Grjeba, arrived in Beijing by plane from Guangzhou this evening. Comrade Jacques Grjeba and his wife are visiting China for a friendly visit at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

Comrade Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Comrade Liu Ningyi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Comrade Zhao Yimin, Alternate Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, went to the airport to welcome them.

Pioneers from the capital presented flowers to Comrade Jacques Grjeba and his wife.

When Comrade Jacques Grjeba and his wife passed through Guangzhou today, they were welcomed by Comrade Qu Mengjue, Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, and others.

### 应中共中央邀请来我国进行友好访问　格里巴同志和夫人到京　邓小平等同志到机场欢迎

1966-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　应中共中央邀请来我国进行友好访问
　　格里巴同志和夫人到京
　　邓小平等同志到机场欢迎
    新华社十七日讯　比利时共产党中央委员会书记雅克·格里巴同志和夫人玛德琳·格里巴，今天晚上乘飞机从广州到达北京。雅克·格里巴同志和夫人是应中国共产党中央委员会的邀请来我国进行友好访问的。
    中共中央总书记邓小平同志、中共中央书记处书记刘宁一同志和中共中央候补委员赵毅敏同志到机场欢迎。
    首都少先队队员向雅克·格里巴同志和夫人献了花。
    雅克·格里巴同志和夫人今天路过广州时，受到中共广东省委书记区梦觉等同志的欢迎。
