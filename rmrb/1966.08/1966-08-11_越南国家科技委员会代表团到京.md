Title: Delegation of the State Science and Technology Commission of Vietnam Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-08-11
Edition: 6

A delegation from the State Science and Technology Commission of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, invited by the Chinese Academy of Sciences to discuss and sign the 1966-1967 Implementation Plan for scientific cooperation between the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the State Science and Technology Commission and the Social Sciences Academy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, arrived in Beijing today. The delegation, led by its head and Vice Chairman of the State Science and Technology Commission, Le Khac, arrived by airplane.

### 越南国家科技委员会代表团到京

1966-08-11
第6版()
专栏：

　　越南国家科技委员会代表团到京
    据新华社十日讯　应中国科学院邀请来我国商谈签订中国科学院和越南民主共和国国家科学技术委员会、社会科学院科学合作一九六六——一九六七年执行计划的越南民主共和国国家科学技术委员会代表团，在团长、越南国家科学技术委员会副主任黎克率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
