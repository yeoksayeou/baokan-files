Title: Chairman Mao Receives Vice President Kamanga
Author:
Date: 1966-08-22
Edition: 1

Chairman Mao today morning received Vice President Ruben Chitandika Kamanga of the Republic of Zambia and members of the Zambian government delegation led by him.

Chairman Mao had a cordial and friendly conversation with the Zambian guests.

The members of the Zambian government delegation who were received are: Minister of Foreign Affairs Simon Mwansa Kapwepwe, Mrs. Kapwepwe, Minister of the Central Province Shamabanse, Parliamentary Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Sikazwe, Permanent Secretary of the Office of National Development and Planning Julu, Director of the Treaty and Economic Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Muka, and official of the President's Office Miti.

Those who accompanied the reception were Vice Chairman Dong Biwu, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Vice Premier Chen Yi, among others.

August 21, Chairman Mao received Vice President Ruben Chitandika Kamanga of the Republic of Zambia and members of the Zambian government delegation led by him. Left photo: Chairman Mao and Vice President Ruben Chitandika Kamanga (front left four) and other Zambian guests pose for a group photo.

### 毛泽东主席接见卡曼加副总统  同赞比亚共和国政府代表团的贵宾们进行了亲切友好的谈话

1966-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席接见卡曼加副总统
    同赞比亚共和国政府代表团的贵宾们进行了亲切友好的谈话
    新华社二十一日讯　毛泽东主席今天上午接见赞比亚共和国副总统鲁本·奇坦迪卡·卡曼加，和由他率领的赞比亚共和国政府代表团的成员。
    毛主席同赞比亚贵宾进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    被接见的赞比亚共和国政府代表团的成员是：外交部长西蒙·姆旺萨·卡普韦普韦，卡普韦普韦夫人，中央省驻节部长沙马班塞，外交部议会秘书西卡苏拉，国家发展和计划办公室常任秘书朱卢，外交部条约与经济事务司司长穆卡，总统办公室官员米提。
    陪同接见的，有董必武副主席，周恩来总理，陈毅副总理等。（附图片）
