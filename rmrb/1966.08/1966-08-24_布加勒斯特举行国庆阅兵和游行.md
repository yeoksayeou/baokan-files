Title: Bucharest Holds Military Parade and March for National Day
Author:
Date: 1966-08-24
Edition: 4

Bucharest held a grand military parade and mass march on the morning of August 23 to celebrate the National Day of the Socialist Republic of Romania.

Romanian party and government leaders such as Nicolae Ceaușescu, Chivu Stoica, and Maurer attended the review ceremony held at "Aviators" Square.

Today, other major cities in Romania also held mass marches.

Earlier, the Bucharest Municipal Committee of the Romanian Communist Party and the Bucharest Municipal People's Congress held a celebration for the Romanian National Day at the Palace of the Republic on the evening of August 22. Nicolae Ceaușescu and other Romanian party and government leaders attended the celebration.

### 布加勒斯特举行国庆阅兵和游行

1966-08-24
第4版()
专栏：

    布加勒斯特举行国庆阅兵和游行
    据新华社布加勒斯特二十三日电  罗马尼亚首都布加勒斯特二十三日上午举行了盛大的阅兵和群众游行，热烈庆祝罗马尼亚社会主义共和国国庆节。
    罗马尼亚党政领导人齐奥塞斯库、基伏·斯托伊卡和毛雷尔等出席了在“飞行员”广场举行的检阅仪式。
    今天，罗马尼亚其他主要城市也举行了群众游行。
    在这以前，罗马尼亚共产党布加勒斯特市委员会和布加勒斯特市人民会议，二十二日晚在共和国宫大厅举行了罗马尼亚国庆节的庆祝会。齐奥塞斯库等罗马尼亚党政领导人出席了庆祝会。
