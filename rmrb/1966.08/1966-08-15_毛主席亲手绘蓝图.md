Title: Chairman Mao Draws the Blueprint with His Own Hand
Author: Liu Da Rong Guo Shao Gui
Date: 1966-08-15
Edition: 4

	Thunder roars and shakes the sky,
	The communiqué of the plenary session shines brightly.
	The drums and gongs are beaten to celebrate,
	Ten thousand people sing songs of praise.
	Looking back at the road we've traveled,
	Victory blooms in red flowers;
	Looking ahead at the road to come,
	Ten thousand flags wave towards the sun.
	Chairman Mao draws the blueprint with his own hand,
	Seven hundred million people's spirits soar,
	Wielding the pen to sweep away demons,
	Raising the gun to fight the wolves.
	We break the old world,
	We create the new world;
	Ten thousand rivers and mountains form a rainbow,
	The Five-Year Plan shines brightly.
	Forever following Chairman Mao,
	Forever following the Communist Party,
	A red heart that never changes color,
	An iron fortress for ten thousand years!

### 毛主席亲手绘蓝图
刘大荣　郭绍贵
1966-08-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　毛主席亲手绘蓝图
　　解放军铁道兵某部  刘大荣　郭绍贵
　　春雷一声震天响，
　　全会公报放光芒。
　　敲锣打鼓来庆祝，　　
　　万众齐把赞歌唱。
　　走过的路回头望，
　　胜利红花朵朵香；
　　未来的路呀抬头看，
　　万里彩旗拥太阳。
　　毛主席亲笔绘蓝图，
　　七亿人民斗志昂，
　　挥动笔杆扫群魔，
　　举起枪杆打豺狼。
　　旧世界，我们破，
　　新世界，我们创；
　　万里河山搭彩虹，
　　五年计划放光芒。
　　永远跟着毛主席，
　　永远跟着共产党，
　　一颗红心不变色，
　　铁打江山万年长！
