Title: Vietnamese Acrobatic Troupe Arrives in Beijing for Continued Visit and Performance
Author:
Date: 1966-08-25
Edition: 4

The Vietnamese Democratic Republic Acrobatic Troupe, consisting of 55 members, led by Director Su Hai, arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon after visiting the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Mongolian People's Republic. They will continue their visit and performances in our country.

### 越南杂技团到京继续在我国访问演出

1966-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

    越南杂技团到京继续在我国访问演出
    据新华社二十四日讯  越南民主共和国杂技团一行五十五人，在团长苏海的率领下，访问了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和蒙古人民共和国后，今天下午乘火车到达北京，继续在我国访问演出。
