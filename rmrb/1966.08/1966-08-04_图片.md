Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Zambian Friendly Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-08-04

On August 3, Premier Zhou Enlai received a friendly delegation from Zambia led by Minister of Commerce and Industry Chibamba (fifth from the left). The photo shows the group posing together during the reception.  New China News Agency photographer.
