Title: Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation Leaves Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1966-08-24
Edition: 4

Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation Leaves Guangzhou

New China News Agency, Guangzhou -- Pakistani National Assembly Speaker Abdul Jabbar Khan and his wife, along with the Pakistani parliamentary delegation led by him, concluded their friendly visit to China. On the morning of the 21st, they departed from Guangzhou by train.

At the station, the national flags of China and Pakistan fluttered in the wind. The Pakistani guests, accompanied by Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo and his wife, and Deputy Governor of Guangdong Province Lin Liming and his wife, arrived at the Guangzhou Railway Station. Young Pioneers presented fresh flowers to the guests. Hundreds of people who came to see them off waved garlands and ribbons, repeatedly shouting slogans such as "Welcome to the Pakistani guests," "Long live the friendship between the peoples of China and Pakistan," and so on.

After boarding the train, the Pakistani guests repeatedly shouted in Chinese, "Long live Chairman Mao, ten thousand years!" "Long live the friendship between China and Pakistan!"

Those who came to see them off at the station also included Deputy Governor of Guangdong Province, Mayor of Guangzhou City Zeng Sheng and his wife, Deputy Mayor of Guangzhou City Luo Peiyuan, and other relevant officials.

Vice Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Lian Guan and his wife accompanied the guests to Shenzhen.

### 巴基斯坦议会访华代表团离开广州

1966-08-24
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦议会访华代表团离开广州
    新华社广州电　巴基斯坦国民议会议长阿卜杜勒·贾巴尔·汗和夫人，以及由他率领的巴基斯坦议会访华代表团，结束了在我国的友好访问，二十一日上午乘火车离开广州。
    车站上，中国和巴基斯坦国旗迎风招展。巴基斯坦贵宾由人大常委会副委员长郭沫若和夫人、广东省代省长林李明和夫人陪同，来到广州车站，少先队员向贵宾们献鲜花。数百名欢送的群众，热情地挥舞花环和彩带，不断高呼：“欢送巴基斯坦贵宾”、“中国和巴基斯坦两国人民友谊万岁”等口号。
    巴基斯坦贵宾登上列车后，不断用中国话高呼：“毛主席万岁，万万岁！”、“中巴两国的友谊万岁！”。
    到车站欢送的，还有广东省副省长、广州市市长曾生和夫人，广州市副市长罗培元，以及各有关方面负责人等。
    人大常委会副秘书长连贯和夫人陪送贵宾前往深圳。
