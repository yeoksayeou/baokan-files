Title: The Popular Edition of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" to be Sold at New Prices
Author:
Date: 1966-08-15
Edition: 4

	The popular edition of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" will be sold at new prices starting today. To meet the urgent need of the broad masses of the people to study the works of Chairman Mao, publishing and printing departments across the country are taking revolutionary measures to accelerate the printing of Chairman Mao's works. The popular edition of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong," Volumes 1 to 4, will be sold at the price of 2 yuan each.

### 《毛泽东选集》普及本按新定价发售

1966-08-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　《毛泽东选集》普及本按新定价发售
    新华社十四日讯　为了满足广大人民群众学习毛主席著作的迫切需要，各地出版、印刷部门正采取革命措施，千方百计地加速印制毛主席著作。《毛泽东选集》普及本，从现在起按新定价发售，一至四卷售价二元。
