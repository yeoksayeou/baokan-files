Title: Upholding Revolutionary Spirit to Break Conventions: Anhui Successfully Airdrops Fish Fry
Author:
Date: 1966-08-18
Edition: 3

Staff of the Anhui Provincial Civil Aviation Bureau have actively studied and applied Chairman Mao's works, upholding a revolutionary spirit to break conventions, and successfully airdropped fish fry using low-flying aircraft. They completed 21 flights, airdropping 63 million fish fry, with a survival rate of over 95%. This innovation significantly saved manpower and resources, shortened transportation time, and increased the survival rate of the fish fry.

### 发扬革命精神打破常规  安徽低空投撒鱼苗成功

1966-08-18
第3版()
专栏：

    发扬革命精神打破常规
    安徽低空投撒鱼苗成功
    安徽省民航局职工活学活用毛主席著作，以革命精神打破常规，用飞机低空直接空投鱼苗成功。他们飞行了二十一架次，空投鱼苗六千三百万尾，成活率都在百分之九十五以上。这一创举大大节约了人力物力，缩短了运输时间，提高了鱼苗成活率。
