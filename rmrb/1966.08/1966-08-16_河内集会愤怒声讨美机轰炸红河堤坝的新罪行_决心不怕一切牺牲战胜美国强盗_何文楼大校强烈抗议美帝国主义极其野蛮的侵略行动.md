Title: Hanoi Assembly Angrily Denounces U.S. Air Raids on Red River Dikes
Author:
Date: 1966-08-16
Edition: 4

Hanoi Assembly Angrily Denounces U.S. Air Raids on Red River Dikes
Determined to Overcome All Sacrifices to Defeat American Aggressors
Major General Ho Van Lau Strongly Protests U.S. Imperialism's Extremely Barbaric Aggressive Actions

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 15th—Hanoi residents from all walks of life held a rally on the evening of the 15th to angrily denounce the new bloody crime of U.S. aggressor planes bombing the Red River dikes and residential areas in the suburbs of Hanoi on the 13th. The attendees repeatedly raised their fists and shouted slogans such as "Down with U.S. aggressors!" "Fight to the end, thoroughly defeat the U.S. aggressors!" and so on.

	Nguyen Van Huong, Chairman of the Hanoi City Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front, said in his speech that the U.S. aggressor planes brazenly bombed the Red River dikes and residential areas on the 13th, once again committing a new crime of blood against Hanoi City and the Vietnamese people. The people of our capital are determined to join the people of the entire country, to strive for production and fight bravely, to avenge our fallen compatriots, and to demand blood debt from the U.S. aggressors.

	After Nguyen Van Huong's speech, family members of the victims and representatives from various sectors also took turns angrily denouncing the new bloody crime of the U.S. aggressors at the meeting. They unanimously expressed their determination to turn hatred into strength, to thoroughly implement Chairman Hu's great battle call, to fear no hardships and sacrifices, and to resolutely carry the anti-American, national salvation struggle to the end, thoroughly defeating the U.S. aggressors.

	The meeting unanimously passed a resolution stating that the crimes of the U.S. aggressors once again exposed their face as the most brutal war criminals in human history.

	The resolution said that the people of Hanoi are determined to make sacrifices, fear no hardships, and will not retreat in the face of any difficulties. They are determined to join the military and people of the entire country to continue giving the U.S. aggressors even more severe punishments until they are thoroughly and completely driven out of Vietnam.

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 13th—Major General Ho Van Lau, Head of the Liaison Mission of the General Staff of the Vietnam People's Army, sent an urgent telegram to Rahman, Chairman of the International Control Commission for Vietnam, on the 13th, strongly protesting the war crime of U.S. imperialism sending planes to bomb the Red River dikes and residential areas in the suburbs of Hanoi, the capital of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, at noon on the 13th.

	The telegram pointed out that this is an extremely barbaric act of aggression by U.S. imperialism against the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and a gross provocation against the global public opinion that loves peace and upholds justice.

### 河内集会愤怒声讨美机轰炸红河堤坝的新罪行　决心不怕一切牺牲战胜美国强盗　何文楼大校强烈抗议美帝国主义极其野蛮的侵略行动

1966-08-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　河内集会愤怒声讨美机轰炸红河堤坝的新罪行
　　决心不怕一切牺牲战胜美国强盗
　　何文楼大校强烈抗议美帝国主义极其野蛮的侵略行动
    据新华社河内十五日电　河内市各界人民十五日晚上举行集会，愤怒声讨美国强盗飞机十三日野蛮轰炸河内市郊的红河堤坝和居民区的新的血腥罪行。到会群众不断振臂高呼：“打倒美国侵略强盗！”“战斗到底，彻底战胜美国侵略强盗！”等口号。
    越南祖国战线河内市委员会主席阮文友在讲话中说，美国强盗飞机十三日悍然轰炸红河堤坝和居民区，再一次对河内市和越南人民欠下新的血债，我们首都人民决心同全国人民一道，努力生产，英勇战斗，决心为死难同胞报仇，向美国侵略强盗讨还血债。
    阮文友讲话后，受难者家属和各界人民代表也纷纷在会上愤怒控诉美国强盗这一新的血腥罪行。他们一致表示，决心化仇恨为力量，彻底实现胡主席的伟大战斗号召，不怕一切艰难困苦和牺牲，坚决把抗美救国斗争进行到底，彻底打败美国侵略强盗。
    集会最后一致通过决议指出，美国侵略强盗的罪行，再一次揭穿了它是人类历史上最残暴的战争罪犯的嘴脸。
    决议说，河内市人民决心不惜牺牲，不怕艰苦，在任何困难面前决不后退，决心同全国军民一起，继续给美国侵略者更加严厉的惩罚，直到把它们彻底、干净地赶出越南。
    据新华社河内十三日电　越南人民军总司令部联络代表团团长何文楼大校，十三日打紧急电报给越南国际委员会主席拉赫曼，强烈抗议美帝国主义十三日中午派飞机轰炸越南民主共和国首都河内市郊的红河堤坝和居民区的战争罪行。
    电报指出，这是美帝国主义对越南民主共和国进行的极其野蛮的侵略行动，是对世界爱好和平和主持公理的舆论的粗暴挑衅。
