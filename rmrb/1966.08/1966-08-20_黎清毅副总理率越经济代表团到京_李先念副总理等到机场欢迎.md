Title: Vice Premier Le Thanh Kiet Leads Vietnamese Economic Delegation to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-08-20
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Le Thanh Kiet Leads Vietnamese Economic Delegation to Beijing
Li Xiannian Vice Premier and Others Welcome at the Airport

New China News Agency, August 19th: Heroes from the frontlines of the anti-American struggle, the people of Vietnam, sent their envoy, Vice Premier Le Thanh Kiet of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and the Vietnamese government economic delegation he leads, who were invited to visit China for a friendly visit, arrived in Beijing by special plane on the 19th. Vietnamese Ambassador to China and Deputy Head of the Delegation Tran Xuan Binh arrived on the same plane.

When Vice Premier Le Thanh Kiet arrived in Beijing, he was warmly welcomed by Vice Premier Li Xiannian of the State Council, Minister of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Fang Yi, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Qiao Guanhua, and other responsible officials from relevant departments. Young Pioneers presented flowers to all the guests.

Also present at the airport to welcome the delegation were Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of Vietnam and Deputy Head of the Delegation Li Ban, who had arrived in Beijing earlier.

The head of the permanent representative office of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam in China, Tran Van Thanh, also came to the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 黎清毅副总理率越经济代表团到京  李先念副总理等到机场欢迎

1966-08-20
第5版()
专栏：

　　黎清毅副总理率越经济代表团到京
    李先念副总理等到机场欢迎
    新华社十九日讯　来自反美斗争最前线的英雄越南人民的使者、越南民主共和国政府副总理黎清毅，和由他率领的越南政府经济代表团应邀来我国进行友好访问，乘专机于十九日到达北京。越南驻中国大使、代表团副团长陈子平同机到达。
    黎清毅副总理到达北京时，受到了国务院副总理李先念，对外经济联络委员会主任方毅，对外贸易部副部长李强，外交部副部长乔冠华以及各有关方面负责人的热烈欢迎。少先队员向全体贵宾献了花。
    到机场欢迎的还有先期到达北京的越南对外贸易部副部长、代表团副团长李班。
    越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长陈文成也到机场欢迎。
