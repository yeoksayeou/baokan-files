Title: The Party Central Committee Plenum Communiqué is a Milestone of the Revolution
Author:
Date: 1966-08-14
Edition: 2

Most beloved Party Central Committee! Most beloved Chairman Mao!

The communiqué of the Eighth Plenum of the Eighth Central Committee is written in gold; every word is truth. This is the voice of our Party Central Committee, this is the voice of our most beloved leader, Chairman Mao. The communiqué is a milestone of the revolution, recording the great victories achieved on all fronts in our country. This is a new victory for Mao Zedong Thought. The communiqué is a war drum of the revolution, inspiring us to raise the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought even higher and to win new victories. The communiqué is a proclamation, declaring to the world: The broad masses of workers, peasants, and soldiers in China have grasped Mao Zedong Thought and created a new epoch for humanity. This is the guarantee of the complete victory of the Chinese revolution and the fundamental guarantee of the victory of the world people's revolution.

We must read Chairman Mao's books, listen to Chairman Mao's words, and act according to Chairman Mao's instructions. We must be good soldiers of Chairman Mao and resolutely carry the Cultural Revolution through to the end! We are always ready to annihilate the aggression of imperialism! We must definitely liberate Taiwan!

Long live the great Communist Party of China! Ten thousand years! Ten thousand times ten thousand years!

Long live the wise leader Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years! Ten thousand times ten thousand years!

Long live the invincible Mao Zedong Thought! Ten thousand years! Ten thousand times ten thousand years!

		All officers and soldiers of a certain naval unit

### 党中央全会公报是革命的里程碑

1966-08-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　党中央全会公报是革命的里程碑最敬爱的党中央最敬爱的毛主席：
    党的八届十一中全会公报的每一个字，都是用金子铸成的；公报的每一句话都是真理。这是我们党中央的声音，这是我们最最敬爱的领袖毛主席的声音。公报是革命的里程碑，它记载了我国各个战线上取得的伟大胜利，这是毛泽东思想的新胜利。公报是革命的战鼓，它鼓舞我们更高地举起毛泽东思想的伟大红旗，夺取新的胜利。公报是宣言书，它向全世界宣布：中国广大工农兵群众掌握毛泽东思想，创造了人类的新纪元，这是中国革命彻底胜利的保证，也是世界人民革命胜利的根本保证。
    我们一定要读毛主席的书，听毛主席的话，照毛主席的指示办事，做毛主席的好战士，坚决将文化大革命进行到底！我们时刻准备着消灭帝国主义的侵略！我们一定要解放台湾！
    伟大的中国共产党万岁！万万岁！
    英明的领袖毛主席万岁！万万岁！
    战无不胜的毛泽东思想万岁！万万岁！
