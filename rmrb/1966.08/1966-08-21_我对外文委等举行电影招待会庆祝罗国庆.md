Title: China's Foreign Affairs Committee and Others Hold a Film Reception to Celebrate Romania's National Day
Author:
Date: 1966-08-21
Edition: 3

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Foreign Affairs Committee, the Ministry of Culture, and the China-Romania Friendship Association jointly held a film reception to celebrate the national day of the Socialist Republic of Romania.

Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs Qiao Guanhua, Vice-Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee Chu Tu-Nan, Vice-Minister of Culture Zhao Xinchu, and Vice-Chairman of the China-Romania Friendship Association Zhou Wenchong attended the reception.

Romanian Ambassador to China Aurel Dumitru and his wife, other officials from the Romanian embassy, and cultural officials from various embassies in China also attended the reception.

### 我对外文委等举行电影招待会庆祝罗国庆

1966-08-21
第3版()
专栏：

    我对外文委等举行电影招待会庆祝罗国庆
    新华社二十日讯　对外文委、文化部和中罗友好协会，今天联合举行电影招待会，庆祝罗马尼亚社会主义共和国国庆。
    外交部副部长乔冠华、对外文委副主任楚图南、文化部副部长赵辛初、中罗友协副会长周文龙等出席了招待会。
    罗马尼亚驻中国大使奥列尔·杜马和夫人、使馆的其他官员，以及各国驻华使馆文化官员也出席了招待会。
