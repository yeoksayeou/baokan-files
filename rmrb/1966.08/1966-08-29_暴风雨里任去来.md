Title: Storms and Tempests, Come What May
Author: Xiang Wu
Date: 1966-08-29
Edition: 6

The battle cry,
Echoes across the four corners of our motherland;
The revolutionary tide,
Rolls over the rivers and mountains of our motherland.
Hooray! The army that assaults the old world,
Is invincible! Hooray!
The momentum of the Cultural Revolution,
Is overwhelming!

We, the Red Guards,
The new generation of our red motherland,
Firmly stand at the forefront of the struggle,
Charging ahead to blaze the trail.

We will rebel against all monsters and demons,
Rebel, rebel, rebel!
We will declare war on old ideas, old culture, old customs, and old habits,
Declare war, declare war, declare war!

Our hearts are boiling with a strong love for the people.
Our hearts are burning with a deep hatred for the enemy.
We are not afraid of the reactionaries calling us "arrogant" and "extreme."
We are not afraid of the bourgeois "gentlemen" calling us "reckless" and "chaotic."

We will march forward with our heads held high,
Storms and tempests, come what may.

Remember the days of old,
Our revolutionary forebears,
For the ideal of communism,
How many laid down their lives,
How many spilled their blood!

We will never forget,
The soldiers on the Long March, braving wind and snow;
We will never forget,
The martyrs at Jianchang Cave, whose blood stained the cliffs...

In memory of the martyrs,
We vow: We will definitely defend the fruits of the revolution,
And never let the enemy return!

Chairman Mao, we promise you,
We, the next generation of revolutionaries,
Will forever read your books,
Listen to your words,
Sail against the wind and waves, forever forward,
Our revolutionary spirit will never change.

For the cause of the Party,
We dare to climb the knife mountain,
We dare to descend into the sea of fire.

Comrades,
Quickly beat the drums of revolution even louder,
Beat them, comrades,
Quickly kindle the flames of victory even brighter,
Kindle them,
To thoroughly burn away all the thoughts of the exploiting classes,
To thoroughly sweep away all the dust of the old world.

To burn the black gangs into a state of panic;
To sweep the enemies off their feet completely.

Let the great red flag of Mao Zedong Thought,
Fly high across the world,
Let the torch of revolution,
Pass on to future generations, forever and ever!

### 暴风雨里任去来
向武
1966-08-29
第6版()
专栏：连队生活短歌

    暴风雨里任去来
    向武
    战斗的号角，
    响遍了祖国的四面八方；
    革命的巨浪，
    卷遍了祖国的锦绣山河。
    好呵！冲击旧世界的大军，
    所向无敌！好呵！
    文化大革命的气势，
    排山倒海！
    我们红卫兵，
    祖国红色的新一代，
    坚决站在斗争的最前列，
    冲锋陷阵把路开。
    我们要向一切牛鬼蛇神，
    造反，造反，造反！
    我们要向旧思想、旧文化、旧风俗、旧习惯，
    宣战，宣战，宣战！
    我们的胸中沸腾着对人民强烈的爱。
    我们的胸中燃烧着对敌人刻骨的恨。
    我们不怕保皇派说我们“狂妄”“过火”。
    我们不怕资产阶级“老爷”说我们“胡闹”“乱来”。
    我们要昂首阔步向前进，
    暴风雨里任去来。
    想当年，
    我们的革命前辈，
    为了共产主义理想，
    多少人，曾把头颅抛，
    多少人，热血洒尘埃！
    我们忘不了呵，
    长征路上战士迎风卧冰雪；
    我们忘不了呵，
    渣滓洞外烈士鲜血染悬岩……
    缅怀先烈，
    我们宣誓：一定要保卫革命成果，
    决不让敌人卷土重来！
    毛主席，我们向您保证，
    我们革命的后一代，
    永远读您的书，
    听您的话，
    乘风破浪永向前，
    革命本色永不改。
    为了党的事业，
    我们敢上刀山，
    我们敢下火海。
    同志们呵，
    快把革命的战鼓更重地擂起来，
    擂起来，同志们呵，
    快把胜利的烈火更旺地烧起来，
    烧起来，
    要彻底烧毁一切剥削阶级的思想，
    要彻底扫除一切旧世界的尘埃。
    要烧得一切黑帮丧魂失魄；
    要扫得一切敌人彻底垮台。
    让毛泽东思想伟大红旗，
    在全世界高高飘扬，
    让革命的火炬，
    传到万代千秋、千秋万代！
