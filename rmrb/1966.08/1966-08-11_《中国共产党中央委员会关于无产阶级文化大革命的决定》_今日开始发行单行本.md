Title: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China's Decision on the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution Begins Publication as a Standalone Edition
Author:
Date: 1966-08-11
Edition: 3

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China's Decision on the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution has been published as a standalone edition by the People's Publishing House and will be available for purchase at New China Bookstores starting from the 11th.
