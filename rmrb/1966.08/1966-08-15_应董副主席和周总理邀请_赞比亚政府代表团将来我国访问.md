Title: Zambian Government Delegation to Visit China at the Invitation of Vice Chairman Dong and Premier Zhou
Author:
Date: 1966-08-15
Edition: 6

At the invitation of Vice Chairman Dong Biwu and Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China, Vice President Ruben Chitandika Kamanga of the Republic of Zambia will lead a Zambian government delegation to visit China for a friendly visit in the near future.

### 应董副主席和周总理邀请  赞比亚政府代表团将来我国访问

1966-08-15
第6版()
专栏：

　　应董副主席和周总理邀请
    赞比亚政府代表团将来我国访问
    新华社十四日讯　应中华人民共和国副主席董必武和中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来的邀请，赞比亚共和国副总统鲁本·奇坦迪卡·卡曼加将于最近率领赞比亚共和国政府代表团，前来我国进行友好访问。
