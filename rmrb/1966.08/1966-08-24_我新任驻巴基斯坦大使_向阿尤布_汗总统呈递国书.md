Title: China's New Ambassador to Pakistan Presents Credentials to President Ayub Khan
Author:
Date: 1966-08-24
Edition: 4

Newly appointed Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan, Zhang Wenjin, presented his letter of credence to President Ayub Khan of Pakistan on the morning of the 23rd at the Presidential Palace in Rawalpindi.

President Ayub Khan and Ambassador Zhang Wenjin had a friendly conversation.

### 我新任驻巴基斯坦大使  向阿尤布·汗总统呈递国书

1966-08-24
第4版()
专栏：

    我新任驻巴基斯坦大使
    向阿尤布·汗总统呈递国书
    新华社拉瓦尔品第二十三日电　新任中国驻巴基斯坦特命全权大使章文晋，二十三日上午在这里的总统府向巴基斯坦总统阿尤布·汗呈递国书。
    阿尤布·汗总统和章文晋大使进行了友好的谈话。
