Title: Chinese and Malian Football Teams Hold Friendly Match; Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Malian Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-08-30
Edition: 6

The Malian national football team played their first friendly match in China against the Chinese national football team at the Beijing Workers' Stadium this evening. The result was a 4-0 victory for the Chinese team.

Vice Premier Chen Yi, and Charge d'Affaires of the Malian Embassy in China, Kaloum Senanta, watched the exciting performances of both teams with over 30,000 spectators from the capital.

Before the match, Vice Premier Chen Yi met with the Malian team's leader, the Malian Republic's sports commissioner, Bengoro Koulibaly. Chen Yi then descended from the VIP platform with Kaloum Senanta to shake hands with each of the athletes from both teams and posed for a group photo.

The Malian national football team will play their second friendly match in China against the Chinese youth football team in Beijing on the 30th.

### 中国马里足球队举行友谊赛  陈毅副总理接见马里客人

1966-08-30
第6版()
专栏：

    中国马里足球队举行友谊赛
    陈毅副总理接见马里客人
    新华社二十八日讯　马里国家足球队今晚在北京工人体育场同中国足球队进行访华首场友谊比赛。比赛结果，中国队以四比零获胜。
    陈毅副总理，马里驻中国大使馆临时代办卡尔苏姆·塞南塔同首都三万多人观看了双方的精彩表演。
    比赛前，陈毅副总理接见客队领队、马里共和国体育专员本戈罗·库利巴利，并且同卡尔苏姆·塞南塔走下主席台，同双方运动员一一握手，并且在一起照了像。
    马里国家足球队将于三十日同中国青年足球队在京进行访华第二场友谊赛。
