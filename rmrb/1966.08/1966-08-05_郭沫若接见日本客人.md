Title: Guo Moruo Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-08-05
Edition: 5

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo today received the delegation of the Japanese Shingon Buddhism Toyama Sect, headed by team leader Konozuka Juncho, and had a friendly conversation with them. Present at the reception were relevant officials Gao Shan, Awang Qizhu, Zhou Shugai, and others.

The Japanese Shingon Buddhism Toyama Sect delegation, at the invitation of the Chinese Buddhist Association, arrived in Beijing on July 28.

### 郭沫若接见日本客人

1966-08-05
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见日本客人
    新华社四日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若今天接见了以团长小野塚润澄率领的日本真言宗丰山派访华友好代表团，同他们进行了友好的谈话。接见时在座的有高山、阿旺嘉措、周叔迦等有关方面负责人。
    日本真言宗丰山派访华友好代表团是应中国佛教协会邀请在七月二十八日到北京的。
