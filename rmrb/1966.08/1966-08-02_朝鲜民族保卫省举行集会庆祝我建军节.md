Title: The Korean People's Armed Forces Ministry Holds a Rally to Celebrate the Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1966-08-02
Edition: 5

The Korean People's Armed Forces Ministry held a rally on the evening of the 30th to celebrate the 39th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Korean People's Army leaders and officers attended the rally.

The Chinese military attaché to Korea, Chen Jianwu, and other embassy officials were invited to attend the rally.

Korean People's Army Major General Choi Jong-gun and the Chinese military attaché to Korea, Chen Jianwu, both spoke at the rally.

After the rally, a Chinese film was screened.

### 朝鲜民族保卫省举行集会庆祝我建军节

1966-08-02
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜民族保卫省举行集会庆祝我建军节
    新华社平壤三十一日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国民族保卫省三十日晚上举行集会，庆祝中国人民解放军建军三十九周年。
    朝鲜人民军将领和军官参加了集会。
    中国驻朝鲜大使馆武官陈健吾和大使馆官员应邀出席了集会。
    朝鲜人民军少将崔钟健和中国驻朝鲜大使馆武官陈健吾在会上讲了话。
    会后，放映了中国电影。
