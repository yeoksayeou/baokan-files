Title: Vice Chairman Dong and Premier Zhou Meet Zambian Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-08-20
Edition: 5

Vice Chairman Dong and Premier Zhou met with Zambian guests
Premier Zhou holds talks with Vice President Kamanga

	BEIJING, Aug. 19 (Xinhua) — Vice Chairman Dong Biwu and Premier Zhou Enlai met with Zambian Republic Vice President Ruben Chitandika Kamanga and the entire delegation of the Zambian Republic government led by him this afternoon.

	Subsequently, Premier Zhou Enlai held talks with Vice President Ruben Chitandika Kamanga in a friendly and cordial atmosphere.

	Those present from the Chinese side for the meeting and talks included Vice Premiers Chen Yi and Li Xiannian, Deputy Foreign Minister Qiao Qingying, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Lu Xiuzhang, Deputy Director of the Foreign Economic Commission Li Yingji, Director of the Protocol Department of the Foreign Ministry Lai Yali, and Deputy Director of the African Affairs Department Hou Yefeng.

	Those present from the Zambian side for the meeting and talks included Foreign Minister Simon Mwansa Kapwepwe, Central Province Minister Shamabanse, Parliamentary Secretary of the Foreign Ministry Sikazwe, Permanent Secretary of the National Development and Planning Office Julu, and Director of the Treaty and Economic Affairs Department of the Foreign Ministry Mukaka.

### 董副主席周总理会见赞比亚贵宾  周总理同卡曼加副总统举行会谈

1966-08-20
第5版()
专栏：

　　董副主席周总理会见赞比亚贵宾
    周总理同卡曼加副总统举行会谈
    新华社十九日讯　董必武副主席和周恩来总理，今天下午会见赞比亚共和国副总统鲁本·奇坦迪卡·卡曼加和由他率领的赞比亚共和国政府代表团全体贵宾。
    随后，周恩来总理同鲁本·奇坦迪卡·卡曼加副总统，在亲切友好的气氛中举行了会谈。
    中国方面参加会见和会谈的，有国务院副总理陈毅、李先念，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，对外贸易部副部长卢绪章，对外经委副主任李应吉，外交部礼宾司司长赖亚力，非洲司副司长侯野烽。
    赞比亚方面参加会见和会谈的，有外交部长西蒙·姆旺萨·卡普韦普韦，中央省驻节部长沙马班塞，外交部议会秘书西卡苏拉，国家发展和计划办公室常任秘书朱卢，外交部条约与经济事务司司长穆卡等。（附图片）
　　八月十九日，董必武副主席、周恩来总理会见赞比亚共和国副总统鲁本·奇坦迪卡·卡曼加和由他率领的赞比亚共和国政府代表团全体贵宾。图为：董必武副主席、周恩来总理和鲁本·奇坦迪卡·卡曼加副总统（前左五）等赞比亚贵宾合影。　新华社记者摄
