Title: After Withdrawing from the 12th World Conference to Ban the Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs
Author:
Date: 1966-08-06
Edition: 5

After withdrawing from the 12th World Conference to Ban the Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs in Japan, five representatives from the delegations of Australia, New Zealand, and Ceylon, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Peace and Friendship, arrived in Guangzhou by train this afternoon.

They were warmly welcomed by leaders of various people's organizations in Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City, as well as over 200 local residents.

The friends who arrived in Guangzhou today are: Henry Bly from the Australian delegation, William Morrow, Nancy Whalan Goddard from the New Zealand delegation, Lakshman Mututantri from the Ceylon delegation, and Mrs. B.B. Mututantri.

### 退出第十二届禁止原氢弹世界大会后  澳大利亚新西兰锡兰朋友到广州

1966-08-06
第5版()
专栏：

    退出第十二届禁止原氢弹世界大会后
    澳大利亚新西兰锡兰朋友到广州
    据新华社广州五日电　退出日本第十二届禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会的澳大利亚、新西兰和锡兰代表团的五位代表，应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请前来我国进行友好访问，今天下午乘火车到达广州。
    他们受到广东省、广州市各人民团体负责人和二百多名群众的热烈欢迎。
    今天到达广州的这些朋友是：澳大利亚代表团的亨里·布利、威廉·莫罗，新西兰代表团的南希·惠兰·戈达德，锡兰代表团的拉克森·穆图坦特里、布·布·穆图坦特里夫人。
