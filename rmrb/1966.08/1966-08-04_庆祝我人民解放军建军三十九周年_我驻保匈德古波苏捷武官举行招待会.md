Title: Celebrating the 39th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1966-08-04
Edition: 5

Our military attachés in Bulgaria, Hungary, the German Democratic Republic, Cuba, Poland, the Soviet Union, and Czechoslovakia held receptions on August 1 and 2 to celebrate the 39th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Attending the reception held by our military attaché to Bulgaria, Duan Yifeng, on August 1 were: Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Bulgarian People's Army, Major General Gaytman, and diplomatic envoys and military attachés from various countries in Bulgaria.

Ambassador Xie Bangzhi of China to Bulgaria also attended the reception.

Attending the reception held by our military attaché to Hungary, Tie Lei, on August 1 were: Deputy Minister of Defense of Hungary, Major General Kasa, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Szilagyi, Commander of the Air Defense Forces, Major General Szilagyi, and Deputy Chief of the General Staff, Major General Kardos, among others.

Diplomatic envoys and military attachés from various countries in Hungary also attended the reception.

Ambassador Han Kehua of China to Hungary also attended the reception.

Attending the film reception held by our military attaché to the German Democratic Republic, Xi Yi, on August 1 were: Deputy Minister of Defense of the German Democratic Republic, Lieutenant General Arenshtan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade and Trade with West Germany, Wagner, and Lieutenant General Kunas of the German Democratic Republic People's Army, among others.

Diplomatic envoys and military attachés from various countries also attended the reception.

Ambassador Zhang Haifeng of China to the German Democratic Republic also attended the reception.

Attending the reception held by our deputy military attaché to Cuba, Wang Shaowu, on August 2 were: Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Cuba, Antonio Carrillo, Director of the Foreign Affairs Department of the Revolutionary Armed Forces, Pedro Luis Rodriguez, and Chairman of the Cuba-China Friendship Association, Baldomero Alvarez, among others.

Diplomatic envoys and military attachés from some countries in Cuba also attended the reception.

Ambassador Wang Youping of China to Cuba also attended the reception.

Attending the reception held by our military attaché to Poland, Wei Jingyuan, on August 2 were: Chief of the General Logistics Department of the Polish People's Army, Lieutenant General Zeminski, Deputy Director of the General Political Department, Major General Ksizarczyk, and Major General Szydlowski, among others.

Diplomatic envoys and military attachés from various countries also attended the reception.

Ambassador Wang Guoquan of China to Poland also attended the reception.

Attending the reception held by our military attaché to the Soviet Union, Zhong Yifu, on August 1 were: Deputy Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union, General Penikovsky, General Dagayev of the Defense Ministry, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Soviet Army, Lieutenant General Yegorov, and Vice Chairman of the Soviet-China Friendship Association, Rogov, among others.

Military attachés from various countries in the Soviet Union also attended the reception.

Chargé d'affaires Zhang Deguan of the Chinese Embassy to the Soviet Union also attended the reception.

Attending the reception held by our military attaché to Czechoslovakia, Lin Qian, on August 1 were: First Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Czechoslovak People's Army, Lieutenant General Kolovratnik, Deputy Director of the General Political Department, Major General Hejna, Commander of the Prague Garrison, Major General Hrad, and some members of the diplomatic corps in Prague, among others.

Ambassador Zhong Xidong of China to Czechoslovakia also attended the reception.

### 庆祝我人民解放军建军三十九周年  我驻保匈德古波苏捷武官举行招待会

1966-08-04
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝我人民解放军建军三十九周年
    我驻保匈德古波苏捷武官举行招待会
    新华社三日讯　我国驻保加利亚、匈牙利、德意志民主共和国、古巴、波兰、苏联、捷克斯洛伐克大使馆武官分别在八月一日和二日举行招待会，庆祝中国人民解放军建军三十九周年。
    出席我国驻保加利亚大使馆武官段一峰一日举行的招待会的有：保加利亚人民军总政治部副主任盖特曼少将以及各国驻保加利亚外交使节和武官等。
    我国驻保加利亚大使谢邦治也出席了招待会。
    出席我国驻匈牙利大使馆武官铁雷一日举行的招待会的有：匈牙利国防部副部长卡萨什少将，外交部副部长西拉吉，空军防空军司令西拉基少将和副总参谋长卡尔多什少将等人。
    出席招待会的还有各国驻匈牙利外交使节和武官。
    中国驻匈牙利大使韩克华也出席了招待会。
    出席一日我国驻德意志民主共和国大使馆武官席一举行的电影招待会的有：德意志民主共和国国防部副部长阿伦斯坦中将，对外和东西德贸易部副部长瓦格纳，德意志民主共和国人民军库纳斯中将等。
    出席招待会的还有各国外交使节和武官。
    中国驻德意志民主共和国大使张海峰也出席了电影招待会。
    出席我国驻古巴大使馆副武官王少吾二日举行的招待会的有：古巴外交部副部长安东尼奥·卡里略、革命武装部外事处处长佩德罗·路易斯·罗德里格斯和古巴—中国友好协会主席巴尔多梅罗·阿尔瓦雷斯等。
    出席招待会的还有一些国家驻古巴的外交使节和武官。
    我国驻古巴大使王幼平也出席了招待会。
    出席我国驻波兰大使馆武官魏精元二日举行的招待会的有：波兰人民军总后勤部主任泽明斯基中将，总政治部副主任克西沙尔契克中将，希德沃斯基少将等。
    出席招待会的还有各国外交使节和武官。
    中国驻波兰大使王国权也出席了招待会。
    出席我国驻苏联大使馆武官钟一夫一日举行的招待会的有：苏联国防部副部长佩尼科夫斯基大将，国防部达加耶夫上将，苏军总政治部副主任叶戈罗夫上将和苏中友协副主席罗果夫等。
    出席招待会的还有各国驻苏联大使馆的武官。
    中国驻苏联大使馆临时代办张德群也出席了招待会。
    出席我国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使馆武官林千一日举行的招待会的有：捷克斯洛伐克人民军第一副总参谋长科洛夫腊特尼克中将、总政治部副主任赫依纳少将、布拉格卫戍司令赫拉德中将和布拉格外交使团的一些成员等。
    中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使仲曦东也出席了招待会。
