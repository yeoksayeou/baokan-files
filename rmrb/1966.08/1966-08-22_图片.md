Title: Chairman Mao Comes Among Us
Author:
Date: 1966-08-22
Edition: 4

Chairman Mao has come among us, and the great leader's heart is closely connected with ours. We are determined to act according to your instructions. In the mass struggle of the Cultural Revolution, we will face the storms and gain experience.
