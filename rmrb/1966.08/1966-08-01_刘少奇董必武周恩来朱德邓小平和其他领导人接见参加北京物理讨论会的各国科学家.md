Title: Liu Shaoqi, Dong Biwu, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Deng Xiaoping and Other Leaders Receive Scientists Attending the Beijing Physics Discussion Conference
Author:
Date: 1966-08-01
Edition: 2

	Liu Shaoqi, Dong Biwu, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Deng Xiaoping and other leaders received scientists attending the Beijing Science Discussion Conference 1966 Summer Physics Discussion Conference.

	New China News Agency, August 31st: Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Dong Biwu, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Premier of the State Council, Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chen Yi, Li Fuchun, and He Long, Members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Premiers of the State Council, Chen Boda, Candidate Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Director of the Central Cultural Revolution Group, Kang Sheng, Candidate Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee, and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Bo Yibo, Candidate Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Premier of the State Council, Tao Zhu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee, Minister of Propaganda, and Vice Premier of the State Council, Li Xuefeng, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Nie Rongzhen, Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Vice Premier of the State Council, and Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council, Ye Jianying, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee and Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council, Xie Fuzhi, Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Premier of the State Council, Lin Feng, and Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Guo Moruo, Yang Mingxuan, and Peng Chuan, today in the afternoon received the scientific delegation and scientists from 33 countries and one regional academic organization attending the Beijing Science Discussion Conference 1966 Summer Physics Discussion Conference, as well as other foreign friends.

	Liu Shaoqi, Dong Biwu, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Deng Xiaoping and other party and state leaders received the scientific delegation and scientists from 33 countries and one regional academic organization attending the Beijing Science Discussion Conference 1966 Summer Physics Discussion Conference, as well as other foreign friends.

### 刘少奇董必武周恩来朱德邓小平和其他领导人接见参加北京物理讨论会的各国科学家

1966-08-01
第2版()
专栏：

    刘少奇董必武周恩来朱德邓小平和其他领导人接见参加北京物理讨论会的各国科学家
    新华社三十一日讯　中共中央副主席、中华人民共和国主席刘少奇，中共中央政治局委员、中华人民共和国副主席董必武，中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来，中共中央副主席、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德，中共中央总书记邓小平，中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅、李富春、贺龙，中共中央政治局候补委员、中央文化革命小组组长陈伯达，中共中央政治局候补委员、中共中央书记处书记、人大常委会副委员长康生，中共中央政治局候补委员、国务院副总理薄一波，中共中央书记处书记、中央宣传部部长、国务院副总理陶铸，中共中央书记处书记、人大常委会副委员长李雪峰，中共中央委员、国务院副总理、国防委员会副主席聂荣臻，中共中央书记处书记、国防委员会副主席叶剑英，中共中央委员、国务院副总理谢富治，中共中央委员、人大常委会副委员长林枫、刘宁一，人大常委会副委员长郭沫若、杨明轩和程潜，今天下午接见了参加北京科学讨论会一九六六年暑期物理讨论会的三十三个国家及一个地区性学术组织的科学家代表团和科学家，以及其他外国朋友。（附图片）
