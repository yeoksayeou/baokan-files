Title: Agreement on China's Economic and Technical Assistance to Vietnam Signed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-08-30
Edition: 1

The agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on China's economic and technical assistance to Vietnam was signed in Beijing today.

Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi attended the signing ceremony. Vice Premier Li Xiannian and Vice Premier Le Quyen, head of the Vietnamese Government Economic Delegation, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective governments.

Those present at the signing ceremony from the Chinese side included Fang Yi, Li Tianyou, Han Nianlong, Li Qiang, and Yang Lin, among others.

From the Vietnamese side, those present included Li Ban, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade and Deputy Head of the Vietnamese Government Economic Delegation, Chen Ziping, Vietnamese Ambassador to China and Deputy Head of the Vietnamese Government Economic Delegation, and other members of the delegation.

Chen Wencheng, Head of the Permanent Representative Office of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam in China, also attended the signing ceremony.

Li Xiannian hosted a banquet tonight to see off the Vietnamese Government Economic Delegation led by Vice Premier Le Quyen.

Vice Premier Chen Yi attended the banquet.

At the banquet, Vice Premier Li Xiannian and Vice Premier Le Quyen warmly congratulated the successful conclusion of the negotiations between the two countries. They unanimously pointed out that the agreement on China's economic and technical assistance to Vietnam signed by the two countries is another vivid manifestation of the fighting friendship between the two peoples.

Vice Premier Le Quyen said, "We are pleased to see that we have a great and reliable China as our rear. The unity and fighting friendship between Vietnam and China show that our two countries are in a front-rear and interdependent relationship. He expressed that the Vietnamese people are determined to fight to the end against U.S. imperialism until final victory.

Vice Premier Chen Yi and Sun Guanghuo, Head of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association delegation, spontaneously composed a poem, enthusiastically praising the fighting friendship between the two peoples. Vice Premier Chen Yi's poem said: "Vietnam is the front, China is the rear; the front covers the rear, the rear supports the front; defeating the U.S. imperialists is our common glory, glory, glory, glory, wishing Chairman Hu and Chairman Mao eternal life!"

The speeches and poems delivered at the meeting were met with enthusiastic applause.

Those invited to the banquet included Chen Wencheng, Head of the Permanent Representative Office of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam in China, and the China-Vietnam Friendship Association delegation led by Sun Guanghuo. Relevant leaders from our country, including Fang Yi, Han Nianlong, Li Qiang, Ding Xilin, and others, attended the banquet.

### 中国给予越南经济技术援助的协定在京签字  周总理陈副总理出席签字仪式，李先念副总理饯别越经济代表团

1966-08-30
第1版()
专栏：

    中国给予越南经济技术援助的协定在京签字
    周总理陈副总理出席签字仪式，李先念副总理饯别越经济代表团
    新华社二十九日讯　中华人民共和国政府和越南民主共和国政府关于中国给予越南经济技术援助的协定，今天在北京签字。
    周恩来总理和陈毅副总理出席了签字仪式。李先念副总理和越南民主共和国政府副总理、越南政府经济代表团团长黎清毅，分别代表两国政府在协定上签字。
    参加签字仪式的中国方面还有方毅、李天佑、韩念龙、李强、杨琳等。
    越南方面参加签字仪式的，有越南对外贸易部副部长、越南政府经济代表团副团长李班，越南驻中国大使、越南政府经济代表团副团长陈子平，以及代表团的其他成员。
    越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长陈文成也参加了签字仪式。
    新华社二十九日讯　李先念副总理今晚举行宴会，欢送黎清毅副总理率领的越南政府经济代表团。
    陈毅副总理出席了宴会。
    李先念副总理和黎清毅副总理在宴会上热烈祝贺两国的谈判圆满结束。他们一致指出，两国签订的关于中国给予越南经济技术援助的协定，是两国人民战斗友谊的又一次生动的体现。
    黎清毅副总理说，我们高兴地看到，我们有着兄弟的伟大的中国作为我们可靠的后方。越中之间的团结和战斗友谊，说明我们两国是前后方和唇齿相依的关系。他表示，越南人民决心同美帝国主义战斗到底，直到最后胜利。
    陈毅副总理和应邀参加宴会的越中友协代表团团长孙光阀即兴赋诗，热烈称颂中越两国人民的战斗友谊。陈毅副总理的诗说：“越南是前方，中国是后方；前方掩护后方，后方支援前方；打败美帝是我们的共同荣光，荣光，荣光，荣光，祝胡主席毛主席万寿无疆！”
    在会上的讲话和朗诵的诗，都激起了一阵阵热烈的掌声。
    应邀出席宴会的，有越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长陈文成和由孙光阀率领的越中友协代表团。我国各有关方面负责人方毅、韩念龙、李强、丁西林等出席了宴会。
