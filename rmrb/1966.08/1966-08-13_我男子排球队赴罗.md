Title: China's Men's Volleyball Team to Visit Romania
Author:
Date: 1966-08-13
Edition: 4

A delegation of 17, led by Zhang Zhikai, chairman of the Chinese Volleyball Association, departed from Beijing by plane this morning to visit Romania.

### 我男子排球队赴罗

1966-08-13
第4版()
专栏：

    我男子排球队赴罗
    新华社十日讯　由中国排球协会主席张之槐率领的中国男子排球队一行十七人，今天上午乘飞机离京去罗马尼亚访问。
