Title: Myanmar News Delegation Leaves Beijing for a Visit to Northeast China
Author:
Date: 1966-08-13
Edition: 4

	Myanmar News Delegation Leaves Beijing for a Visit to Northeast China

	New China News Agency, August 12th: A delegation from the Myanmar Ministry of Information, led by Secretary Ding Tun, left Beijing today for a visit to Northeast China.

	On the evening of the 11th, Vice Chairman Chang Zhiqing of the All-China Journalists Association hosted a farewell banquet for the guests. Myanmar Ambassador to China, Thaw Win, and his wife were invited to attend the banquet. Vice Chairman Hu Chi of the All-China Journalists Association and other journalists from the capital attended to accompany the guests.

	Deputy Director Shi Huibai of the State Council's Foreign Affairs Office, Deputy Director Chen Weifan of the Foreign Ministry's Information Department, and Cultural Counselor Zhao Jiaping from the Chinese Embassy in Myanmar also attended the banquet.

### 缅甸新闻代表团离京去东北访问

1966-08-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　缅甸新闻代表团离京去东北访问
    新华社十二日讯　缅甸宣传部秘书丁吞中校率领的缅甸新闻代表团，今天离开北京去东北访问。
    十一日晚，中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席常芝青设宴欢送客人。应邀出席宴会的有缅甸驻中国大使信瓦瑙和夫人等。中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席胡痴等首都新闻界人士出席作陪。
    对外文委副主任史怀璧、外交部新闻司副司长陈维帆和中国驻缅甸大使馆文化参赞赵冀平也出席了宴会。
