Title: The DPRK People's Army "August 28" Gymnastics Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-08-26
Edition: 6

The DPRK People's Army "August 28" Gymnastics Delegation, consisting of eleven members led by team leader Huang Zhen, arrived in Beijing by plane on the morning of the 25th at the invitation of the Chinese Ministry of National Defense for a visit to China.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the guests included Zhang Shaoting, Deputy Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, a representative from the State Sports Commission, and PLA gymnasts. The PLA athletes presented flowers to the guests. Colonel Choi Jeong-yong, military attaché of the DPRK Embassy in China, and others also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 朝鲜人民军“二八”体操代表团到京

1966-08-26
第6版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民军“二八”体操代表团到京
    本报讯　应我国防部邀请来我国进行访问的朝鲜人民军“二八”体操代表团一行十一人，在团长黄轸率领下，于二十五日上午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎客人的有中国人民解放军总政治部文化部副部长张少庭，国家体委代表，以及解放军体操运动员等。解放军运动员向客人献了花。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使馆武官崔济荣上校等也前往机场迎接。
