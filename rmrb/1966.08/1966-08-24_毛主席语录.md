Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1966-08-24
Edition: 1

The world is yours, it is also ours, but in the final analysis, it is yours. You young people are in the prime of life, like the sun at eight or nine in the morning. The future is in your hands.

		_From the Conversation with Our Students Studying in the Soviet Union_
