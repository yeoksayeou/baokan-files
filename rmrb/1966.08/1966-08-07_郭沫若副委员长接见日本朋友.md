Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Meets with Japanese Friends
Author:
Date: 1966-08-07
Edition: 6

Vice Chairman Guo Moruo of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress met with a delegation of Japanese Social Party lawmakers led by Shien Kenjiro and a delegation of Japanese Social Party activists led by Yamamoto Yutaro in the afternoon of August 6. Guo Moruo had a cordial and friendly conversation with them. Present at the meeting were Zhao Anbo, Wang Yinfu, Xiao Xiangqian, Wang Xiaoyun, and other guests. Japanese peace activist Nishi Sonji, who is in Beijing, was also present. (Photo attached)

On August 6, Vice Chairman Guo Moruo met with the entire delegation of Japanese Social Party lawmakers led by Shien Kenjiro (front row, sixth from the left) and the third delegation of Japanese Social Party activists led by Yamamoto Yutaro (front row, sixth from the right).

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本朋友

1966-08-07
第6版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见日本朋友
    新华社六日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若今天下午接见由楯兼次郎率领的日本社会党国会议员访华团和由山本勇太郎率领的第三批日本社会党活动家访华代表团，郭沫若和他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的有赵安博、王荫圃、萧向前、王晓云等。在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也在座。（附图片）
    八月六日，郭沫若副委员长接见由楯兼次郎（前左六）率领的日本社会党国会议员访华团和由山本勇太郎（前右六）率领的第三批日本社会党活动家访华代表团全体成员。
      新华社记者摄
