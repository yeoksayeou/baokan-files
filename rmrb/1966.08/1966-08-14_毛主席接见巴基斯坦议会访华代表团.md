Title: Chairman Mao Receives Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation Visiting China
Author:
Date: 1966-08-14
Edition: 2

Chairman Mao receives the Pakistani Parliamentary delegation visiting China

	New China News Agency, August 13th. Chairman Mao Zedong tonight received Pakistani National Assembly Speaker Abdul Jabbar Khan and his wife, as well as the entire delegation of the Pakistani Parliamentary delegation visiting China led by him, and had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.
	Present at the reception were Vice Chairman Kang Sheng and his wife, Vice Chairman Guo Moruo and his wife, Vice Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Lian Guan, and Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan Zhang Wenjin.
	Pakistani Ambassador to China Sultan Muhammad Khan was also present.
	Chairman Mao poses for a group photo with the Pakistani Parliamentary delegation visiting China, Speaker Abdul Jabbar Khan and his wife, and all members of the delegation. (Photo by New China News Agency reporter)

### 毛主席接见巴基斯坦议会访华代表团

1966-08-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　毛主席接见巴基斯坦议会访华代表团
    新华社十三日讯  毛泽东主席今晚接见巴基斯坦国民议会议长阿卜杜勒·贾巴尔·汗和夫人，以及由他率领的巴基斯坦议会访华代表团的全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在场的，有康生副委员长和夫人，郭沫若副委员长和夫人，人大常委会副秘书长连贯，中国驻巴基斯坦大使章文晋。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆罕默德·汗也在场。（附图片）
    毛主席接见巴基斯坦议会访华代表团，同阿卜杜勒·贾巴尔·汗议长和夫人，以及代表团的全体成员合影。　新华社记者摄
