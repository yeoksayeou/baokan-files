Title: South Vietnamese Liberation Army Night Raid on Norman Airbase Destroys 70 U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-08-07
Edition: 5

	South Vietnamese Liberation Army Night Raid on Norman Airbase Destroys 70 U.S. Aircraft
	Annihilates 150 U.S. Soldiers and Destroys a Missile Launch Site

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 5th – According to preliminary reports from the Liberation Communications Agency of South Vietnam, on the night of July 23rd, the liberation armed forces of Quang Nam Province in South Vietnam launched an attack on the U.S. airbase at Norman, southeast of Da Nang. The attack destroyed 70 U.S. aircraft and killed or wounded 150 U.S. pilots and technical personnel.

	The liberation armed forces also blew up 12 enemy barracks and burned down a missile launch site.

	This is the third time the liberation armed forces of South Vietnam have attacked this airbase. The first attack occurred on the night of October 27th last year, destroying 106 enemy aircraft and annihilating 283 U.S. personnel. The second attack took place on January 24th of this year, destroying or damaging 34 enemy aircraft and annihilating 130 U.S. personnel.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 6th – The Vietnam People's Army Newspaper published a commentary on August 6th, enthusiastically hailing the brilliant victory of the liberation armed forces of Quang Nam Province in South Vietnam in their night attack on the Norman U.S. airbase on July 23rd. This attack destroyed 70 U.S. aircraft and eliminated 150 U.S. soldiers.

	The commentary pointed out that this victory is a stern warning and powerful punishment to U.S. imperialism, and is a resounding response of the South Vietnamese people to the call of beloved Chairman Ho Chi Minh. It is also a perfect coordination with the victories of the northern military and civilians in consecutively shooting down many U.S. aggressor aircraft.

	The commentary called on the military and civilians of South and North Vietnam to respond to Chairman Ho's battle call, fight side by side, and unite in giving the U.S. aggressors and their lackeys even more powerful punishment.

	The Vietnam Fatherland Front Committee of Hai Phong City sent a telegram on the evening of August 5th to the military and civilians of Da Nang City, Quang Nam Province, South Vietnam, enthusiastically hailing the brilliant victory of the liberation armed forces in their attack on the Norman U.S. airbase.

### 南越解放军夜袭诺曼机场毁美机七十架  歼灭美军一百五十人烧毁一个导弹发射场

1966-08-07
第5版()
专栏：

    南越解放军夜袭诺曼机场毁美机七十架
    歼灭美军一百五十人烧毁一个导弹发射场
    新华社河内五日电  据越南南方解放通讯社初步消息，越南南方广南省解放武装力量七月二十三日夜袭击了岘港市东南的诺曼美军机场，击毁美机七十架，打死打伤美军驾驶员和技术人员一百五十人。
    解放武装力量还炸毁了敌人十二处营房，烧毁了一个导弹发射场。
    这是越南南方解放武装力量第三次袭击这个机场。第一次袭击是在去年十月二十七日晚上，击毁敌机一百零六架，歼灭美国佬二百八十三人。第二次袭击发生在今年一月二十四日，击毁击伤敌机三十四架，歼灭美国佬一百三十人。
    新华社河内六日电越南《人民军队报》六日发表评论，热烈欢呼越南南方广南省解放武装力量七月二十三日夜间袭击诺曼美军机场的辉煌胜利。这次袭击共击毁美机七十架，消灭美军一百五十名。
    评论指出，这个胜利是对美帝国主义的严厉警告和有力惩罚，也是越南南方人民对敬爱的胡志明主席的号召的响亮回答，是同北方军民接连击落许多美国强盗飞机的胜利的绝妙的配合。
    评论号召越南南方和北方军民响应胡主席的战斗号召，并肩战斗，同心协力，坚决给予美国强盗及其走狗更有力的惩罚。
    越南祖国战线海防市委员会五日晚上也打电报给越南南方广南省岘港市军民，热烈欢呼解放武装力量袭击诺曼美军机场的辉煌胜利。
