Title: Representatives Who Withdrew from the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs Visit Other Regions
Author:
Date: 1966-08-18
Edition: 5

Representatives who withdrew from the 12th World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs to uphold the honorable traditions of the movement and who were invited to China by the Chinese Committee for the Prevention of Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs have concluded their visit to Beijing. They left Beijing on the 15th and 16th to visit Northeast, East China, and South China.

Australian representative Betty Little and American representative Terma Toshiko Stayer had already left Beijing and returned to their countries earlier.

### 退出禁止原氢弹世界大会的代表去外地访问

1966-08-18
第5版()
专栏：

　　退出禁止原氢弹世界大会的代表去外地访问
    新华社十七日讯　为维护禁止原子弹氢弹世界运动光荣传统而退出第十二届禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会并应中国和委会邀请来我国访问的澳大利亚、巴苏陀兰、贝专纳、比利时、锡兰、智利、新西兰、西南非洲、斯威士兰、美国等部分外国朋友，结束了在北京的参观访问，已在十五、十六日离开北京分别前往东北、华东、华南等地参观访问。
    澳大利亚朋友贝蒂·利特尔和美国朋友特尔马·托希科·斯太曾已先行离京回国。
