Title: Delegation of Japanese Socialist Party Activists Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-08-04
Edition: 5

The third delegation of Japanese Socialist Party activists visiting China arrived in Beijing this evening, led by team leader Yamamoto Yuutarou. This delegation is composed of Socialist Party activists from the Japanese prefectures of Fukuoka, Okayama, and Niigata. Secretary-General Wang Yincai of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, along with relevant officials Xiao Xiangqian, Wang Xiaoyun, and leaders from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and other units, went to the airport to welcome them.

### 日本社会党活动家访华代表团到京

1966-08-04
第5版()
专栏：

    日本社会党活动家访华代表团到京
    新华社三日讯　第三批日本社会党活动家访华代表团，在团长山本勇太郎率领下今晚到达北京。这个代表团是由日本福冈、冈山、新潟三个县的社会党活动家代表组成的。外交学会秘书长王荫圃和各有关方面负责人萧向前、王晓云以及全国总工会等单位的负责人，前往机场欢迎。
