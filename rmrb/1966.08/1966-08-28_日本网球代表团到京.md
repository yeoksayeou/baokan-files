Title: Japanese Table Tennis Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-08-28
Edition: 5

The Japanese table tennis team, invited to visit China for friendly exchanges, arrived in Beijing this evening. The team, consisting of eight members, is led by Kakuji Tanikawa, the executive director of the Japanese Table Tennis Association. They arrived by plane.

### 日本网球代表团到京

1966-08-28
第5版()
专栏：

    日本网球代表团到京
    据新华社二十六日讯　应邀来我国进行友好访问的日本网球代表团一行八人，由日本网球协会常务理事长谷川宽治率领，今晚乘飞机到达北京。
