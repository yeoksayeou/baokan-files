Title: Chinese and Cambodian Volleyball Teams Hold Friendly Match in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-08-25
Edition: 4

The Cambodian national men's and women's volleyball teams held their final friendly matches in Beijing on the evening of August 22. The men's team from Cambodia defeated the men's team from Hubei with a score of 3-2, while the women's team from Cambodia lost to the Chinese women's youth volleyball team with a score of 0-3.

### 中柬排球队在京进行友谊赛

1966-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

    中柬排球队在京进行友谊赛
    据新华社讯　柬埔寨国家男、女排球队二十二日晚在北京体育馆同湖北男子排球队和中国青年女子排球队进行在京最后一次友谊比赛。客队男子队以三比二获胜，女子队以零比三失利。
