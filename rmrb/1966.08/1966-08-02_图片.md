Title: Celebrating the 39th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1966-08-02
Edition: 5

On August 1, the General Staff Department and the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army held a banquet to celebrate the 39th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The photo shows Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Yang Chengwu, delivering a speech at the banquet. Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter.
