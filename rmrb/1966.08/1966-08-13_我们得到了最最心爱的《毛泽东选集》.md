Title: We Have Received the Most Beloved "Selected Works of Mao Zedong"
Author: Jí Wànshòu
Date: 1966-08-13
Edition: 3

Today, we received the most beloved "Selected Works of Mao Zedong"! Our joy is indescribable! Our hearts burst with a thousand words, which became one sentence: "Long live Chairman Mao! Long live! Long live forever!"

Today, we excitedly and tearfully received the shining truth, the invincible ideological weapon, our compass for progress, and our sustenance for life! We cannot express our excitement, for this is the greatest joy of our lives, the happiest moment! From morning to night, everyone in our school is excited, cheering, and celebrating the receipt of this invincible ideological weapon!

We will use the brilliance of Mao Zedong Thought to expose the true forms of all anti-revolutionary elements, ghosts, and monsters, and thoroughly defeat, overthrow, and expose them! We will complete the tasks of "struggle, criticize, and reform" and vow to carry the Cultural Revolution of the proletariat to the end!

Let all ghosts and monsters tremble! We, the revolutionary people, will forever march forward with the shining "Selected Works of Mao Zedong"! The brilliance of Mao Zedong Thought will illuminate the entire world!

Beijing Institute of Technology, Class 62621
Jí Wànshòu, August 12, night

### 我们得到了最最心爱的《毛泽东选集》
吉万寿
1966-08-13
第3版()
专栏：

　　我们得到了最最心爱的《毛泽东选集》
    今天，我们领到了最最心爱的《毛泽东选集》！我们大家的高兴劲就甭提有多高了！我们心里的千言万语，迸发成了一句：“毛主席万岁！万岁！万万岁！”
    今天，我们激动地含着热泪接过的是金光闪闪的真理，是无往而不胜的思想武器，是我们前进的方向盘，是我们生存的食粮！我们再也无法表达我们激动的心情，因为这是我们一生最大的喜事，是我们最幸福的时刻！从早到晚，我们全院师生都激动着，欢呼着，庆贺我们领到了战无不胜的思想武器！
    我们要用毛泽东思想的光辉，把一切黑帮分子、牛鬼蛇神的原形统统照出来，把他们彻底斗倒、斗垮、斗臭！彻底完成“一斗，二批，三改”的任务，誓将无产阶级文化大革命进行到底！
    让一切牛鬼蛇神发抖吧！我们革命人民将永远高举金光闪闪的《毛泽东选集》前进！毛泽东思想的光辉要普照世界大地！
      北京工业学院六二六二一班
            吉万寿　八月十二日夜
