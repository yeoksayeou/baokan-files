Title: The Armed Struggle of the Angolan People Has Been Going On for Over Five Years
Author:
Date: 1966-08-26
Edition: 6

The armed struggle of the Angolan people has been going on for over five years. They have been continuously launching attacks, liberating vast areas of land, and dealing heavy blows to the Portuguese colonialists supported by American imperialism. The image shows Angolan patriotic warriors practicing their combat skills.
