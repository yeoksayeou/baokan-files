Title: Mao Zedong Thought Red Flags Fly Everywhere
Author: Han Xiao
Date: 1966-08-16
Edition: 3

The drums of progress are beating,
The bugles of advance are sounding,
We cheer,
The Party Central Committee has issued
The communiqué of the Eleventh Plenum of the Eighth Central Committee,
We sing,
The red flags of Mao Zedong Thought are flying everywhere!

We stand on the red stage
In front of Tiananmen,
While putting on makeup,
We listen to the broadcast,
Our hearts,
Like the surging waves,
Full of passion,
Blood boiling.

We stand on the red stage
In front of Tiananmen,
Chests puffed out,
Singing freely,
Our voices,
Like the spring wind howling,
So happy,
So proud!

We cheer:
All of China
Is steaming with energy,
The iron mountains and rivers
Are flying red flags.
The heroic people
Resolutely want to
Uproot the ghosts and monsters!

We sing,
The five continents
Are in turmoil,
The fighting people
Are raising their guns.
Resolutely want to
Knock down the American bandits!

We stand by the side of
Our most beloved
Great Leader
Chairman Mao
And solemnly swear:
We will forever
Follow the direction you point,
Along the course you open,
In the stormy seas
Advance with songs,
Fire at the class enemies!

The drums of progress are beating,
The bugles of advance are sounding,
We cheer,
The Party Central Committee has issued
The communiqué of the Eleventh Plenum of the Eighth Central Committee,
We sing,
The red flags of Mao Zedong Thought are flying everywhere!

### 毛泽东思想红旗漫天飘
韩笑
1966-08-16
第3版()
专栏：

　　毛泽东思想红旗漫天飘
　　广州部队海上文化工作队　韩笑
　　擂起跃进鼓，
　　吹响进军号，
　　我们欢呼
　　党中央公布了
　　八届十一中全会公报，
　　我们歌唱
　　毛泽东思想红旗漫天飘！
　　我们在天安门前
　　红色的舞台上，
　　一边化妆，
　　一边收听，
　　我们的心啊，
　　像起伏的波涛，
　　豪情滚滚，
　　热血滔滔。
　　我们在天安门前
　　红色的舞台上，
　　昂首挺胸，
　　纵情歌唱，
　　我们的歌声啊，
　　像春风在呼啸，
　　多么幸福，
　　多么自豪！
　　我们欢呼：
　　全中国
　　热气腾腾，
　　铁打的江山
　　红旗飘飘。
　　英雄的人民
　　坚决要把
    牛鬼蛇神
    连根拔掉！
　　我们歌唱
　　五大洲
　　云水翻腾，
　　战斗的人民
　　高举枪刀。
　　坚决要把
    美国强盗
    彻底打倒！
　　我们在最敬爱的
　　伟大领袖
　　毛主席的身边
　　庄严宣誓：
　　我们一定永远
　　朝着您指引的方向，
　　沿着您开辟的航道，
　　在大风大浪中
　　高歌猛进，
　　向阶级敌人
　　猛烈开炮！
　　擂起跃进鼓，
　　吹响进军号，
　　我们欢呼
　　党中央公布了
　　八届十一中全会公报，
　　我们歌唱
　　毛泽东思想红旗漫天飘！
