Title: A Japanese Guest Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-08-12
Edition: 6

A Japanese guest arrived in Beijing. According to a report from Xinhua News Agency on the 10th, Professor Tamura Saburo from the Faculty of Agriculture at Tokyo University in Japan arrived in Beijing by plane on the 9th, invited by the Chinese Agricultural Society to conduct an academic visit to China.

### 日本一位客人到京

1966-08-12
第6版()
专栏：

    日本一位客人到京
    据新华社十日讯　应中国农学会邀请前来中国进行学术访问的日本东京大学农学部教授田村三郎九日乘飞机到达北京。
