Title: Chairman Mao Receives Foreign Friends Attending the Beijing Physics Discussion Meeting
Author:
Date: 1966-08-01
Edition: 1

Chairman Mao receives foreign friends attending the Beijing Physics Discussion Meeting

Foreign scientists applauded enthusiastically for a long time and shouted, "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years!" Chairman Mao warmly shook hands with the foreign scientists

Xinhua News Agency, July 31st: Chairman Mao Zedong received the delegation of scientists from 33 countries and one regional academic organization attending the 1966 Summer Physics Discussion Meeting of the Beijing Science Discussion Meeting, as well as other foreign friends this evening.

When Chairman Mao met with the foreign scientists, the foreign scientists applauded enthusiastically for a long time and excitedly shouted, "Long live Chairman Mao! Long live Chairman Mao!"

In the midst of enthusiastic applause, Chairman Mao warmly shook hands with the foreign scientists and posed for a group photo with them.

After Chairman Mao posed for a group photo with the scientists and left the hall, the foreign scientists rushed towards Chairman Mao, continuously shouting, "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years!"

The party and state leaders who participated in the reception included: Zhou Enlai, Chen Yi, Li Fuchun, He Long, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Bo Yibo, Tao Zhu, Nie Rongzhen, Ye Jianying, Xie Fuzhi, Lin Feng, Liu Ningyi, Guo Moruo, Yang Mingxuan, and Peng Zhen, among others. (With photos)

### 毛主席接见参加北京物理讨论会的各国朋友

1966-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见参加北京物理讨论会的各国朋友
    各国科学家长时间地热烈鼓掌，高呼“毛主席万岁！万万岁！”毛主席同各国科学家亲切握手
    新华社三十一日讯　毛泽东主席今晚接见了参加北京科学讨论会一九六六年暑期物理讨论会的三十三个国家及一个地区性学术组织的科学家代表团和科学家，以及其他外国朋友。
    毛主席同各国科学家见面的时候，各国科学家长时间地热烈鼓掌，激动地高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“毛主席万岁！”
    毛主席在热烈的掌声中，同各国科学家亲切握手，并且同他们合影。
    毛主席同科学家们合影后离开大厅的时候，各国科学家拥向毛主席，不断高呼：“毛主席万岁！万万岁！”
    参加接见的党和国家领导人有：周恩来、陈毅、李富春、贺龙、陈伯达、康生、薄一波、陶铸、聂荣臻、叶剑英、谢富治、林枫、刘宁一、郭沫若、杨明轩和程潜等。（附图片）
