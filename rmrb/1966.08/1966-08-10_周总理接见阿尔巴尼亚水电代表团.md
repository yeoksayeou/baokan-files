Title: Premier Zhou Receives Albanian Water Power Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-08-10
Edition: 3

Premier Zhou received the Albanian Minister of Construction, Deputy Minister Shali Angoni, and the entire delegation of the Albanian Water Power delegation led by him this morning and had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.

	Those present at the reception included Xie Huai-de and Cheng Ming-sheng.

	The Albanian Ambassador to China, Nataneli, was also present. (Photo attached)

	On August 9, Premier Zhou received the Albanian Minister of Construction, Deputy Minister Shali Angoni, and the Albanian Water Power delegation led by him. The photo shows a group photo taken during the reception. Shali Angoni, the delegation leader, is the third person from the left in the front row. 	New China News Agency reporter.

### 周总理接见阿尔巴尼亚水电代表团

1966-08-10
第3版()
专栏：

    周总理接见阿尔巴尼亚水电代表团
    新华社九日讯　周恩来总理今天上午接见阿尔巴尼亚建工部副部长沙立·安果尼和由他率领的阿尔巴尼亚水电代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有谢怀德、程明升。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使纳塔奈利也在座。（附图片）
    八月九日，周恩来总理接见阿尔巴尼亚建工部副部长沙立·安果尼，和由他率领的阿尔巴尼亚水电代表团。图为接见时合影。前左三是沙立·安果尼团长。　　　　　　新华社记者摄
