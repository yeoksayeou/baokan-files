Title: Hold High the Great Red Banner of Mao Zedong Thought and Carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution Through to the End!
Author:
Date: 1966-08-19
Edition: 5

Hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought and carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution through to the end!

The great leader, great supreme commander, and great helmsman Chairman Mao met with the revolutionary masses attending the celebration of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

The million-strong masses attending the meeting expressed their determination to carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution through to the end under the leadership of the great leader Chairman Mao.

Chairman Mao had a cordial talk with the "Red Guards" of the capital.

Chairman Mao was with the revolutionary students and teachers from the capital and from other places who had come to Beijing.

	New China News Agency photographer

When our most beloved leader Chairman Mao appeared on the Tiananmen Gate, the soldiers and officers of the Beijing garrison of the People's Liberation Army shouted to the great commander Chairman Mao: We will forever follow Chairman Mao in making revolution.

	(All photos on this page, except those taken by the New China News Agency photographer, were taken by our newspaper's photographer)

The revolutionary students of the capital waved bouquets with hearts full of joy, cheering and paying tribute to the most beloved great leader Chairman Mao. New China News Agency photographer

### 高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底！（图片）

1966-08-19
第5版()
专栏：

　　高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底！
　　伟大的领袖、伟大的统帅、伟大的舵手毛主席同参加庆祝无产阶级文化大革命大会的革命群众见面。
　　参加大会的百万群众表示决心在伟大领袖毛主席领导下，把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底。
　　毛主席同首都“红卫兵”亲切交谈。
　　毛主席同首都以及外地来京的革命师生代表在一起。
                          新华社记者摄
　　当我们最敬爱的领袖毛主席出现在天安门城楼上的时候，中国人民解放军驻京部队指战员，向伟大的统帅毛主席高呼：我们永远跟着毛主席闹革命。
　　（本版图片除新华社记者所摄者外，均系本报记者摄）
　　首都革命学生以无比喜悦的心情，挥舞着花束，向最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席欢呼致敬。　新华社记者摄
