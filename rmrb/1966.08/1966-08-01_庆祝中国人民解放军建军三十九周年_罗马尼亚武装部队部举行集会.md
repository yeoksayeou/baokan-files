Title: Celebrating the 39th Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1966-08-01
Edition: 6

Romanian Ministry of National Defense Holds Meeting

	BUCHAREST, July 29 (Xinhua) -- The Romanian Ministry of National Defense held a meeting on the evening of July 28 to celebrate the 39th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

	More than 400 officers, including General Oprea, the First Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Romanian Armed Forces, attended the meeting.

	General Oprea and Wang Xiangsheng, the military attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Romania, both spoke at the meeting.

	After the meeting, the attendees visited an exhibition titled "Scenes from the Training and Life of the Chinese People's Liberation Army" and watched the Chinese film "Long Live the Victory of the People's War."

### 庆祝中国人民解放军建军三十九周年  罗马尼亚武装部队部举行集会

1966-08-01
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝中国人民解放军建军三十九周年
    罗马尼亚武装部队部举行集会
    新华社布加勒斯特二十九日电　罗马尼亚武装部队部二十八日晚举行集会，庆祝中国人民解放军建军三十九周年。
    罗马尼亚武装部队第一副总参谋长奥尔班中将等四百多名军官出席了集会。
    奥尔班和中国驻罗马尼亚大使馆武官王祥生在会上讲了话。
    会后，与会者参观了“中国人民解放军训练和生活片断”图片展览，并观看了中国电影《人民战争胜利万岁》。
