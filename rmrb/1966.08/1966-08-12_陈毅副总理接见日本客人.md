Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-08-12
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Japanese guests today morning. The New China News Agency reported on the 11th that Vice Premier Chen Yi met with the delegation of Japanese Social Party parliamentarians visiting China, led by Tate Kenjiro, and the third group of Japanese Social Party activists visiting China, led by Yamamoto Yutaro. Vice Premier Chen Yi had a friendly conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Zhao Anbo, Wang Yinpeng, Xiao Jinguang, Wang Xiaoyun, and others.

Japanese peace activist Nishisonji Koiichi, who is in Beijing, was also present.

### 陈毅副总理接见日本客人

1966-08-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　陈毅副总理接见日本客人
    新华社十一日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见由楯兼次郎率领的日本社会党国会议员访华团和由山本勇太郎率领的第三批日本社会党活动家访华代表团。陈毅副总理同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有赵安博、王荫圃、萧向前、王晓云等。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也在座。
