Title: Chairman of China Council for the Promotion of International Trade Hosts Dinner for Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-08-31
Edition: 5

China Council for the Promotion of International Trade Chairman Nan Hanchen held a dinner this evening to welcome the delegation of the Japan Association for the Promotion of International Trade, led by its Economic Friendship Delegation Chairman Kunabumi Katsurang, Deputy Chairman Sugimura Shuzō, and the Japan Association for the Promotion of International Trade Director Tanaka Shūjirō, along with other members of the delegation.

### 我国际贸易促进会主席宴请日本客人

1966-08-31
第5版()
专栏：

    我国际贸易促进会主席宴请日本客人
    据新华社三十日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸今晚举行宴会，欢迎日本国际贸易促进协会经济友好代表团团长国分胜范、副团长杉木重藏，日本国际贸易促进协会常务理事田中修二郎和代表团的其他成员。
