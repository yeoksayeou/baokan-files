Title: Burmese Medical Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-08-17
Edition: 4

According to Xinhua News Agency, August 15th: In accordance with the China-Burma cultural exchange plan, a Burmese medical delegation led by Dr. U Kyaw Sein, consisting of five members, arrived in Beijing by plane this evening.

### 缅甸医学代表团到京

1966-08-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　缅甸医学代表团到京
    据新华社十五日讯  根据中国缅甸文化交流计划，由吴觉盛大夫率领的缅甸医学代表团一行五人，今晚乘飞机到达北京。
