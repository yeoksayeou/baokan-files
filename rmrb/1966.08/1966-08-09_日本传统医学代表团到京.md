Title: Japanese Traditional Medicine Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-08-09
Edition: 6

According to the "Agreement on Friendly Exchanges Between the Peoples of China and Japan for the Year 1966" signed by the China-Japan Friendship Association and the Japan-China Friendship Association, a delegation of ten Japanese traditional medicine practitioners, led by Chairman Kashiwagi Masakazu, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

### 日本传统医学代表团到京

1966-08-09
第6版()
专栏：

    日本传统医学代表团到京
    新华社七日讯　根据中日友协和日中友协签订的“关于一九六六年度中日两国人民间友好往来项目议定书”，日本传统医学代表团一行十人，在团长柏木正一率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
