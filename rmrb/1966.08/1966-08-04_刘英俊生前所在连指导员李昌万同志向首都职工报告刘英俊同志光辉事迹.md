Title: Comrade Li Changwan, Political Instructor of the Unit Where Liu Yingjun Served, Reports on the Glorious Deeds of Comrade Liu Yingjun
Author:
Date: 1966-08-04
Edition: 2

Comrade Li Changwan, the political instructor of the unit where Liu Yingjun served, reported on the glorious deeds of Comrade Liu Yingjun to over 14,000 workers from more than 3,000 industrial, transportation, financial, and commercial enterprises in the capital on August 3. The report, titled "Learning from the Great Communist Warrior Comrade Liu Yingjun," was warmly received by the workers. The report meeting was jointly organized by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the Beijing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions.

After delivering his report, Comrade Ma Chungu, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, called on the workers to learn from Liu Yingjun, the people's good son and Chairman Mao's good soldier.

### 刘英俊生前所在连指导员李昌万同志向首都职工报告刘英俊同志光辉事迹

1966-08-04
第2版()
专栏：

    刘英俊生前所在连指导员李昌万同志向首都职工报告刘英俊同志光辉事迹
    据新华社三日讯　刘英俊同志生前所在连队指导员李昌万同志，八月三日向首都三千多个工交财贸企业的一万四千多名职工，作了《向伟大的共产主义战士刘英俊同志学习》的报告，受到广大职工的热烈欢迎。报告会是由中华全国总工会和北京市总工会联合举办的。
    李昌万同志作完报告以后，中华全国总工会副主席马纯古号召广大职工向人民的好儿子、毛主席的好战士刘英俊同志学习。
