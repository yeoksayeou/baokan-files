Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Two U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-08-12
Edition: 6

North Vietnamese military and civilians shoot down two U.S. aircraft.

New China News Agency, Hanoi, August 11 - It was announced here tonight that the military and civilians of Hai Phong City's Chi Pho Island and Quang Binh Province shot down two invading U.S. aggressor aircraft on August 11.

### 越南北方军民击落美机两架

1966-08-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　越南北方军民击落美机两架
    新华社河内十一日电　据这里十一日晚上公布：越南北方海防市吉婆岛军民和广平省军民，十一日共击落入侵的美国强盗飞机两架。
