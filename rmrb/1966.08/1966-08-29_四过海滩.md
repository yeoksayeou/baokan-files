Title: Four Crossings of the Beach
Author: Lǐ Jìngxīng
Date: 1966-08-29
Edition: 6

    Four Crossings of the Beach
    Guangzhou Military Region, a Five-Good Soldier, Li Jingxing

    Early spring, crossing the beach,
    Waves high, tides swift, winds fierce.
    Rich soil and fertile land hidden beneath the sea,
    Seagulls circle but dare not land.

    Mid-summer, crossing the beach,
    Newly built sea dikes wide and broad.
    By the quotation board, waves of rice roll,
    Muddy flats transformed into granaries.

    Late autumn, crossing the beach,
    Soldiers sharpen their sickles in the fields.
    Sharpening swords to fight the enemy,
    Sharpening sickles for a bountiful harvest.

    End of the year, crossing the beach,
    Tractors lined up to welcome me.
    The people's army, a great school,
    All sing the "Four Good" song together!

### 四过海滩
李竟兴
1966-08-29
第6版()
专栏：连队生活短歌

    四过海滩
    广州部队某部五好战士　李竟兴
    早春打从海滩过，
    浪高潮急旋风恶。
    肥泥沃土海底藏，
    鱼鹰盘旋不敢落。
    仲夏打从海滩过，
    新修海堤宽又阔。
    语录牌前稻浪滚，
    烂泥滩变米粮窝。
    深秋打从海滩过，
    战士田头把刀磨。
    磨快战刀为杀敌，
    磨快镰刀好收割。
    年终打从海滩过，
    拖拉机排队欢迎我。
    人民军队大学校，
    万众齐唱“四好”歌！
