Date: 1966-08-14
Edition: 2

Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation Visits a Typical Agricultural Exhibition in the Style of Dazhai

New China News Agency, August 13th: Speaker of the Pakistani National Assembly, Abdul Jabbar Khan, and his wife, along with the Pakistani parliamentary delegation led by him, visited the typical agricultural exhibition in the style of Dazhai this morning, accompanied by Vice Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Feng Xuan. The guests praised the glorious achievements made by the Chinese people in the agricultural front by taking the road of self-reliance.

The guests visited the Shahe People's Commune in the suburbs of Beijing and toured the Great Wall, accompanied respectively by Vice Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Luo Shuzhang, and his wife, and Lian Guan and his wife, yesterday and the day before yesterday.

### 巴基斯坦议会访华代表团参观大寨式农业典型展览

1966-08-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　巴基斯坦议会访华代表团参观大寨式农业典型展览
    新华社十三日讯　巴基斯坦国民议会议长阿卜杜勒·贾巴尔·汗和夫人，以及由他率领的巴基斯坦议会访华代表团，今天上午在人大常委会副秘书长冯铉的陪同下，参观了大寨式农业典型展览。贵宾们对中国人民在农业战线上走自力更生道路所取得的辉煌成就表示赞扬。
    贵宾们昨天和前天，分别由人大常委会副秘书长罗叔章、连贯和夫人陪同，访问了北京郊区沙河人民公社和游览了长城。
