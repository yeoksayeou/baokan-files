Author:
Date: 1966-08-17
Edition: 4

Prince Sihanouk Cables Premier Zhou
Thanking for the Warm Reception of the Cambodian Delegation

New China News Agency, August 15th: Premier Zhou Enlai received a cable on the 11th from the Head of State of Cambodia, Prince Norodom Sihanouk, expressing gratitude for the warm and fraternal reception given to the Cambodian delegation attending the 1966 Summer Physics Discussion Meeting in Beijing. The full text of the cable is as follows:

	Beijing
	Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
	Zhou Enlai:

	The warm and fraternal reception given to our delegation attending the Beijing Physics Discussion Meeting has deeply moved me. All our compatriots deeply feel the great respect shown to our representatives. Therefore, please allow me to express my most sincere thanks to you, to the Chinese leadership, and to the outstanding scientists of China. At the same time, please allow me to extend our warm congratulations to you on the tremendous achievements of this scientific conference. These achievements demonstrate to the world that China occupies a leading position among the highly developed countries. In continuing to rely on our own efforts to build our country, we have drawn great strength and increased our confidence from here. The progress made by China and the anti-imperialist countries of Asia and Latin America has convinced us that all the schemes of U.S. imperialism to obstruct our scientific, economic, and social development are doomed to failure. I once again assure you of our unwavering friendship and solidarity.

	Head of State of Cambodia, Norodom Sihanouk
	Phnom Penh, August 11, 1966

### 西哈努克亲王致电周总理　感谢对柬埔寨代表团的热烈接待

1966-08-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　西哈努克亲王致电周总理
　　感谢对柬埔寨代表团的热烈接待
    新华社十五日讯　周恩来总理收到了柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王十一日打给他的电报，感谢对参加北京科学讨论会一九六六年暑期物理讨论会的柬埔寨代表团的热烈的兄弟般的接待。电报全文如下：
　　北京中华人民共和国国务院总理
    周恩来阁下：
    参加北京物理讨论会的我国代表团所受到的热烈的、兄弟般的接待，使我深受感动。我国全体同胞都深深感到自己的代表所受到的莫大的尊敬。为此，请允许我向你、向中国领导人和中国杰出的科学家们表示最诚挚的感谢。同时请允许我向你表示我们热烈的祝贺，祝贺这次科学会议所取得的极为巨大的成就。这一成就向世界表明，在高度发展的那些国家中间，中国已经占有头等重要的地位。在继续依靠自己的努力进行国家建设中，我们从这里汲取了巨大的力量和增强了信心。中国和亚洲以及拉丁美洲反帝国家所取得的进步使我们深信，美帝国主义企图阻止我们的科学、经济和社会的发展的一切阴谋，是注定要失败的。我谨向你再次保证我们坚定不移的友谊和我们的团结一致。
        柬埔寨国家元首　诺罗敦·西哈努克
                  一九六六年八月十一日于金边
