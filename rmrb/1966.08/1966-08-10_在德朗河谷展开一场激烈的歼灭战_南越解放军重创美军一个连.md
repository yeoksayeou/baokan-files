Title: Fierce Annihilation Battle Unfolds in the Drang Valley
Author:
Date: 1966-08-10
Edition: 6

The Liberation Army of South Vietnam launched a fierce annihilation battle on August 8 in the Drang Valley near Pleiku in the Central Highlands of South Vietnam, inflicting heavy casualties on a company of the U.S. 1st Cavalry Division.

According to Western news agencies, this was the "most intense battle" in several days of large-scale "sweeping operations" by the U.S. 1st Cavalry Division in the area. The Liberation Army surrounded the U.S. company from three sides and delivered a "severe blow," causing "heavy losses" to the enemy.

According to a report from the New China News Agency in Hanoi on August 7, local forces and guerrillas in Me Linh District, Gia Dinh Province, South Vietnam, on July 23, once again thwarted a "sweeping operation" by the U.S.-puppet forces in the outskirts south of Saigon, killing 220 enemy troops, including 18 U.S. invaders.

According to another report, local forces and guerrillas in Cam Son, Hoi Duc, and Le Hong Phong counties in Binh Thuan Province, from July 16 to 29, launched successive attacks and ambushes on the enemy, killing a total of 223 enemy troops.

### 在德朗河谷展开一场激烈的歼灭战  南越解放军重创美军一个连

1966-08-10
第6版()
专栏：

    在德朗河谷展开一场激烈的歼灭战
    南越解放军重创美军一个连
    新华社九日讯　西贡消息：越南南方解放武装力量八日在南越中部高原波来梅附近的德朗河谷重创了美国侵略军第一骑兵师的一个连。
    据西方通讯社报道，这是美国第一骑兵师在这个地区发动大规模的“扫荡”的若干天以来“最激烈的一次交战”。解放武装力量从三面包围了美国第一骑兵师的这个连，并给以“严重打击”，使敌人“遭到了严重伤亡”。
    据新华社河内七日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方嘉定省芽?县的地方部队和游击队，七月二十三日在西贡市南部外围地区又一次粉碎了美伪军的“扫荡”，消灭敌军二百二十名，其中包括美国侵略军十八名。
    另据报道，平顺省咸顺、淮德、黎鸿峰三县地方部队和游击队，从七月十六日到二十九日，接连袭击和伏击敌人，共消灭敌军二百二十三名。
