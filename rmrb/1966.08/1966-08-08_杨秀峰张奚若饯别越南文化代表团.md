Title: Yang Xiufeng and Zhang Zhiwo Send Off the Vietnamese Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-08-08
Edition: 4

According to Xinhua News Agency, on August 7th, Yang Xiufeng, the president of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association, and Zhang Zhiwo, the director of the State Council's Foreign Cultural Relations Commission, held a reception to warmly send off the Vietnamese cultural delegation led by Phan Ngoc Binh, the director of the Vietnam Democratic Republic's Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee.

Yang Xiufeng, in his speech at the reception, enthusiastically praised the fighting friendship between the peoples of China and Vietnam.

Phan Ngoc Binh, the delegation leader, said in his speech, "In China, whether in government agencies, factories, or people's communes, we see everywhere the enthusiastic support of the Chinese people for the Vietnamese people who are fighting against American aggression and for national salvation. The Chinese people are filled with proletarian internationalist spirit for our struggle against American aggression and for national salvation. We firmly believe that the brotherly friendship between the peoples of Vietnam and China is unshakable, and we will forever be comrades-in-arms."

Zhang Zhiwo also toasted at the reception.

Attending the reception were various responsible officials including Ding Xilin, Li Qi, Cao Keqiang, and Sun Xiaocun. The charge d'affaires of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic's embassy in China, Ly Chung Chi, and the head of the Vietnam National Liberation Front's permanent delegation to China, Chen Wencheng, were also invited to the reception.

### 杨秀峰张奚若饯别越南文化代表团

1966-08-08
第4版()
专栏：

    杨秀峰张奚若饯别越南文化代表团
    据新华社七日讯　中越友协会长杨秀峰和对外文委主任张奚若今晚举行招待会，热烈欢送由越南民主共和国对外文化联络委员会主任范玉纯率领的越南文化代表团。
    杨秀峰在招待会上讲话的时候热烈颂扬中越两国人民的战斗友谊。
    范玉纯团长在讲话中说，我们在中国，无论是在机关、工厂或人民公社，到处看到中国人民对正在进行抗美救国斗争的越南人民的热情支持。中国人民对我们的抗美救国斗争事业充满了无产阶级国际主义精神。我们坚信，越中两国人民的兄弟友谊是不可动摇的，我们永远是战友。
    张奚若也在招待会上祝酒。
    出席招待会的，有各有关方面负责人丁西林、李琦、曹克强、孙晓村等。越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办黎终始，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长陈文成应邀出席了招待会。
