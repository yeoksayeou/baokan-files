Title: Premier Zhou Receives Vietnamese Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-08-09
Edition: 2

Premier Zhou today afternoon received the Vietnamese Democratic Republic Cultural Delegation led by Phan Ngoc Thuan, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Zhang Zaiyu, Director of the State Council's Foreign Affairs Committee, and Deputy Director Ding Xilin, among others.

Lai Chung Chi, Chargé d'Affaires of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic Embassy in China, was also present.

### 周总理接见越南文化代表团

1966-08-09
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理接见越南文化代表团
    新华社八日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见由范玉纯率领的越南民主共和国文化代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有对外文委主任张奚若、副主任丁西林等。
    越南民主共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办黎终始也在座。
