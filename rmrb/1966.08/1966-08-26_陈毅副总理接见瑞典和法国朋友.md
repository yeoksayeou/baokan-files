Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Swedish and French Friends
Author:
Date: 1966-08-26
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Chen Yi this morning received members of the Swedish "Torch Society" delegation led by Hans Isaacson, and members of the French-China Friendship Association University Student Delegation led by Pierre Bignier. He had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

The Swedish and French friends arrived in China in early August to conduct visits and tours.

### 陈毅副总理接见瑞典和法国朋友

1966-08-26
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见瑞典和法国朋友
    新华社二十五日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见以汉斯·伊萨克逊为首的瑞典“火炬社”代表团的成员，以比埃尔·比涅奥为首的法中友协大学生分会访华团的成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    瑞典和法国朋友是在八月初来到我国进行参观访问的。
