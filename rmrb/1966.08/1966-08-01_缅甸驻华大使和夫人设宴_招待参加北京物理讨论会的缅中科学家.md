Title: The Burmese Ambassador and His Wife Host a Banquet for Burmese and Chinese Scientists Attending the Beijing Physics Symposium
Author:
Date: 1966-08-01
Edition: 4

The Burmese Ambassador to China, Thakin Than Tun, and his wife hosted a banquet last night to welcome Burmese and Chinese scientists attending the Beijing Physics Symposium.

Attending the banquet were the leader of the Burmese scientific delegation, U Chit Maung, delegation member U Tin Maung, and the leader of the Chinese scientific delegation, Zhou Peiyuan, and deputy leader Li Yi, among others.

The banquet was held in a friendly atmosphere. Ambassador Than Tun, delegation leader U Chit Maung, and delegation leader Zhou Peiyuan all spoke at the banquet, expressing their hopes for the continued strengthening of friendly relations between the peoples of Burma and China, and for the continued development of friendly cooperation between scientists of the two countries.

### 缅甸驻华大使和夫人设宴  招待参加北京物理讨论会的缅中科学家

1966-08-01
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸驻华大使和夫人设宴
    招待参加北京物理讨论会的缅中科学家
    新华社三十一日讯　缅甸驻中国大使沙马杜瓦·信瓦瑙和夫人，昨晚设宴招待参加北京物理讨论会的缅甸和中国科学家。
    出席宴会的，有缅甸科学家代表团团长吴叫敏、团员吴达莱和中国科学家代表团团长周培源、副团长力一等。
    宴会在友好的气氛中进行。信瓦瑙大使、吴叫敏团长和周培源团长，先后在宴会上讲话，共祝中缅两国人民之间友好关系日益增强，两国科学家的友好合作关系不断发展。
