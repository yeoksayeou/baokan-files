Title: Pakistani Parliamentary Delegation Arrives in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1966-08-07
Edition: 2

At the invitation of Chairman Ju De, a Pakistani parliamentary delegation arrived in China for a friendly visit. The delegation, led by Abdul Jabbar Khan, Speaker of the National Assembly of Pakistan, and his wife, arrived in Shanghai by plane this afternoon.

Today, the flags of China and Pakistan fluttered in the wind at Hongqiao Airport, and colorful banners were everywhere. As the plane carrying the distinguished guests slowly landed, Shanghai Vice Mayor Li Ganceng and his wife Lyu Jiying, among others, went forward to greet the guests warmly and shake hands. Young Pioneers presented flowers to all the guests.

Also present at the airport to welcome the guests were Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Liu Jingji, Vice Secretary-General of the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee Zhang Yaohui, and other relevant officials.

People's Congress Standing Committee Vice Secretary-General Lian Guan and his wife Lin Lang, who came specially from Beijing to welcome the guests, were also at the airport.

### 巴基斯坦议会访华代表团到达上海

1966-08-07
第2版()
专栏：

    应朱德委员长的邀请来我国进行友好访问
    巴基斯坦议会访华代表团到达上海
    人大常委会副秘书长连贯和上海市副市长李干成等到机场欢迎
    新华社上海六日电　应人大常委会委员长朱德的邀请前来我国进行友好访问的巴基斯坦国民议会议长阿卜杜勒·贾巴尔·汗和夫人，以及由他率领的巴基斯坦议会访华代表团，今天下午乘飞机到达上海。
    今天虹桥机场上，中国、巴基斯坦两国国旗迎风飘荡，彩旗林立，当贵宾们乘坐的飞机徐徐着陆时，上海市副市长李干成和夫人吕继英等迎上前去，同贵宾们热烈握手致意。少先队员向全体贵宾献花。
    到机场欢迎贵宾的，还有政协上海市委员会副主席刘靖基，市人委副秘书长张耀辉，以及各有关方面负责人。
    专程由北京来上海迎接贵宾的人大常委会副秘书长连贯和夫人林琅也到机场欢迎。
