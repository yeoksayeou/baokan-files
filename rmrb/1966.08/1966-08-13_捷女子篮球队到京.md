Title: Czechoslovakian Women's Basketball Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-08-13
Edition: 4

At the invitation of the Chinese Basketball Association, the Czechoslovakian national women's basketball team, consisting of 16 members, arrived in Beijing this afternoon. The team is led by Josef Polak, vice-chairman of the Czechoslovakian Basketball Association.

### 捷女子篮球队到京

1966-08-13
第4版()
专栏：

    捷女子篮球队到京
    据新华社十二日讯  应邀来我国进行访问的捷克斯洛伐克国家女子篮球队一行十六人，由捷克斯洛伐克篮球协会副主席波拉克·约瑟夫率领，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
