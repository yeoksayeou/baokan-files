Title: Hill Chairman Says: The Global Revolutionary Situation is Excellent; The U.S. Imperialists and Soviet Revisionists Are Doomed
Author:
Date: 1966-08-28
Edition: 5

Melbourne, August 27th - The Chairman of the Australian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninists), Hill, published an article in the party's organ, the *Vanguard*, on the 18th. He pointed out that the people armed with the immortal thoughts of Marx, Engels, Lenin, Stalin, and Mao Zedong are invincible. "U.S. imperialism and its close ally, Soviet revisionism, are similarly doomed to failure."

The article points out that the current era is one of the decisive struggle between socialism and capitalism. "The entire world is presenting a revolutionary situation." The people are rising up to fight. "Their primary enemy is U.S. imperialism."

The article points out: In the overall situation, the focus of the struggle is Vietnam. The Vietnamese people, who are closely supported by the Chinese people and all revolutionary people around the world, are not only fighting for themselves but also for all revolutionary forces.

The article strongly condemns the U.S. imperialists' actions of expanding the aggressive war in Vietnam and points out: "This is the frenzied action caused by the crisis in the United States. This is indeed a sign of weakness." The article says, "It can be absolutely certain that U.S. imperialism will perish." Because it "is fighting an unjust war."

The article points out: "In the current situation, the Soviet revisionists are playing the most vicious role. They are genuine traitors."

The article points out that a new anti-U.S. storm is rising. No matter how frantic U.S. imperialism is, no matter how cunning Soviet modern revisionism is, they cannot save themselves from the inevitable fate of failure.

### 希尔主席发表文章说：全世界革命形势大好  美帝苏修注定要完蛋

1966-08-28
第5版()
专栏：

    希尔主席发表文章说：
    全世界革命形势大好  美帝苏修注定要完蛋
    新华社二十七日讯　墨尔本消息：澳大利亚共产党（马克思列宁主义者）主席希尔十八日在党的机关报《先锋报》上发表文章指出：用马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林和毛泽东的不朽的思想武装起来的人民是战无不胜的。“美帝国主义及其亲密的伙伴苏联修正主义同样地注定要完蛋。”
    文章指出，现在正处在社会主义同资本主义的决战时代。“全世界都呈现了革命的形势”。人民在起来斗争。“他们的首要敌人是美帝国主义。”
    文章指出：在整个局势中，斗争的焦点是越南。得到中国人民和全世界一切革命人民密切支持的越南人民，不仅是在为自己而战，而且是在为一切革命力量而战。
    文章强烈谴责美帝国主义在越南扩大侵略战争的行动，并且指出：“这是美国的危机所引起的疯狂行动。这的的确确是一种软弱的表现。”文章说，“可以绝对肯定，美帝国主义必将灭亡。”因为它“打的是一场非正义的战争”。
    文章指出：“在当前的形势下，苏联修正主义者起了最穷凶极恶的作用。他们是道道地地的叛徒。”
    文章指出，新的反美风暴正在兴起。不管美帝国主义如何疯狂，不管苏联现代修正主义如何狡诈，都挽救不了他们必然走向失败的命运。
