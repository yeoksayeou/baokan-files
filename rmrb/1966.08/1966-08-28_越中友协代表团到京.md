Title: Delegation of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-08-28
Edition: 5

At the invitation of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association, a delegation of five from the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, led by Vice Chairman of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association and Secretary of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Nguyen Quang Thieu, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon to participate in the activities celebrating the 21st anniversary of the declaration of independence of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and to conduct a friendly visit to China.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Zhang Zhirou, Director of the State Council's Foreign Cultural Relations Commission, Chu Tu-nan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, Ding Xilin, Vice President and member of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association, and other relevant personnel.

Lai Chung-shi, Counselor of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, and other embassy officials, as well as Nguyen Minh Phong, Deputy Head of the Permanent Representative Office of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam in China, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 越中友协代表团到京

1966-08-28
第5版()
专栏：

    越中友协代表团到京
    新华社二十七日讯　应中越友好协会邀请，前来我国参加庆祝越南民主共和国宣布独立二十一周年活动并进行友好访问的越中友协代表团一行五人，由越中友好协会副会长、越南民主共和国国会常务委员会秘书长孙光阀率领，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有对外文委主任张奚若，中国人民对外文化友好协会会长楚图南，副会长、中越友协理事丁西林及有关方面人士。
    越南民主共和国驻华大使馆参赞黎终始及使馆官员，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团副团长阮明芳等也到机场迎接。
