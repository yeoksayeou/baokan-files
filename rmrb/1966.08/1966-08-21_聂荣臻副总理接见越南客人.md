Title: Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen Receives Vietnamese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-08-21
Edition: 3

Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen today received the delegation of the State Science and Technology Commission of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by Vice Chairman Le Kich. The delegation members had a friendly and cordial conversation with Nie.

Present at the reception were relevant officials such as Zhu Kezhen, Wu Youxian, Pei Lisheng, and Qin Lisheng.

Vietnamese Ambassador to China, Counselor Le Zhongshi, was also present.

### 聂荣臻副总理接见越南客人

1966-08-21
第3版()
专栏：

    聂荣臻副总理接见越南客人
    新华社二十日讯　国务院副总理聂荣臻今天接见了由越南国家科学技术委员会副主任黎克率领的越南民主共和国国家科学技术委员会代表团全体成员，并和他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有竺可桢、吴有训、裴丽生、秦力生等有关方面负责人。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆参赞黎终始等也在座。
