Title: Chairman Liao Chengzhi Hosts Dinner for Japanese Scientists
Author:
Date: 1966-08-09
Edition: 6

Chairman Liao Chengzhi of the China-Japan Friendship Association hosted a dinner tonight for Japanese scientists participating in the Beijing Physics Discussion Meeting.

Liao Chengzhi and the leader of the Japanese scientists' delegation, Yoshida Shigeo, delivered warm and friendly speeches at the banquet.

Liao Chengzhi praised the Japanese scientists for their contributions to strengthening the friendship and solidarity between the peoples and scientists of China and Japan. He expressed the hope that such solidarity and friendly exchanges would continue to strengthen. Yoshida, the delegation leader, stated that upon returning to Japan, the Japanese scientists would definitely share their experiences and observations in China with their colleagues and work to promote friendship between the physicists and peoples of Japan and China.

Members of the Chinese scientists' delegation participating in the Beijing Science Discussion Meeting, as well as relevant officials such as Zhou Peiyuan, Zhao Jichang, Li Yi, Cai Ziquan, and Wang Xiaoyun, also attended the banquet and engaged in friendly conversations with the Japanese scientists.

### 廖承志会长宴请日本科学家

1966-08-09
第6版()
专栏：

    廖承志会长宴请日本科学家
    新华社七日讯　中国日本友好协会会长廖承志，今晚设宴招待参加北京物理讨论会的日本科学家们。
    廖承志和日本科学家代表团团长野上茂吉郎在宴会上发表了热情友好的讲话。
    廖承志赞扬日本科学家们为加强中日两国人民的友谊和科学家的团结合作作出了贡献，并希望这种团结和友好往来不断加强。野上团长表示说，日本科学家们回国以后，一定把在中国的所见所闻告诉他们的同行，为促进日中两国物理学家和人民的友谊而努力。
    参加北京科学讨论会的中国科学家代表团成员，以及有关方面负责人周培源、赵继昌、力一、蔡祖泉、王晓云等也出席宴会，同日本科学家们欢叙交谈。
