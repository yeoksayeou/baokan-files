Title: Pakistani Public Opinion Says Sino-Pakistani Friendship Cannot Be Strangled
Author:
Date: 1966-08-23
Edition: 5

Pakistani political observers and newspapers here strongly oppose the attempt by Chester Bowles, the U.S. Ambassador to India, to include Pakistan in an anti-China alliance in Asia, aiming to strangle Sino-Pakistani friendship.

On August 17, Bowles openly advocated in a press conference in New Delhi that Pakistan should join Japan, Indonesia, and India to oppose China.

According to the Pakistan News Agency, political observers here say this is an old trick played by imperialism. They believe that the U.S. conspiracy is an attempt to make Asians fight Asians. They are convinced that Pakistan will never become a member of such an alliance.

Pakistani political observers emphasize that Sino-Pakistani friendship is based on positive factors, not on expedient considerations. They say that the Pakistani people firmly believe that China is a peace-loving country and a tried-and-true friend of Pakistan.

The various newspapers here published on the 20th in prominent positions the talks of political observers reported by the Pakistan News Agency. The Pakistan Daily News on the 20th published an editorial saying that Bowles' hope that Pakistan will join an anti-China alliance is just a "pipe dream."

The newspaper said that Pakistan has repeatedly declared that under any circumstances, Pakistan will continue to maintain friendly relations with China.

### 巴基斯坦舆论说巴中友谊扼杀不了  鲍尔斯企图拉人反华是白日做梦

1966-08-23
第5版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦舆论说巴中友谊扼杀不了
    鲍尔斯企图拉人反华是白日做梦
    新华社拉瓦尔品第二十日电　巴基斯坦政治观察家和这里的报纸强烈反对美国驻印度大使切斯特·鲍尔斯妄图将巴基斯坦包括在亚洲的一个反华同盟内，以扼杀中巴友谊的阴谋。
    鲍尔斯八月十七日在新德里举行的一次记者招待会上讲话，明目张胆地鼓吹巴基斯坦应同日本、印度尼西亚、印度联合反对中国。
    据巴基斯坦报联社报道，这里的政治观察家们说，这是帝国主义一再玩弄的老一套把戏。他们认为，美国的这一阴谋是企图驱使亚洲人打亚洲人。他们相信，巴基斯坦决不会成为这样的一个同盟的成员。
    巴基斯坦的政治观察家们强调指出，中巴友谊是以积极的因素为基础的，不是权宜之计。他们说，巴基斯坦人民坚信，中国是一个爱好和平的国家，是巴基斯坦的经过考验的朋友。
    这里的各报二十日在显著地位刊登了巴基斯坦报联社报道的政治观察家们的谈话。巴基斯坦《建设报》二十日发表社论说，鲍尔斯希望巴基斯坦会参加反华同盟，这不过是“白日做梦”。
    这家报纸说，巴基斯坦已经一再宣告，在任何情况下，巴基斯坦将继续保持同中国的友好关系。
