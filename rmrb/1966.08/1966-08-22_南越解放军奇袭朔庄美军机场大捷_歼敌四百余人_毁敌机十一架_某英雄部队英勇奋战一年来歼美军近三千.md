Title: South Vietnamese Liberation Army Achieves Great Victory in Raid on Soctrang U.S. Airfield
Author:
Date: 1966-08-22
Edition: 5

The South Vietnamese Liberation Army launched a fierce attack on the Soctrang U.S. helicopter airfield on the evening of July 26, simultaneously shelling the enemy paratrooper training base in My Chau County. A total of 426 enemy personnel were annihilated, 11 enemy aircraft were destroyed, and one ammunition depot was blown up.

The liberation forces' raid on the Soctrang airfield was the tenth such attack since September 9, 1963. In the previous nine attacks, the liberation forces had destroyed and damaged 215 enemy aircraft and annihilated 522 enemy personnel. Including the results of this attack, the liberation forces have destroyed or damaged 226 enemy aircraft and annihilated 672 enemy personnel, including 183 U.S. aggressors.

The heroic unit of the South Vietnamese Liberation Army, which achieved the great victory at Van Xa, has been fighting bravely for a year, continuously creating new battle achievements. According to reports, by August 18 of this year, this heroic unit had engaged in 28 battles, completely annihilating two entire U.S. battalions and five companies, three entire enemy battalions and five companies, and 12 platoons, totaling 5,068 enemy personnel, including 2,839 U.S. aggressors.

Currently, all the officers and soldiers of this heroic unit are carrying out a heated campaign to kill the enemy and achieve merit under the great battle call of Chairman Hu, vowing to carry the great struggle of resisting the U.S. and saving the nation to the end, and to thoroughly and completely annihilate the U.S. aggressor forces.

According to a report from the New China News Agency on August 18, Saigon: On the evening of the 17th, the South Vietnamese Liberation Army launched successive attacks on U.S. military facilities near Saigon, striking fear into the enemy.

According to reports from Western news agencies, on the evening of the 17th, the South Vietnamese Liberation Army launched a fierce artillery barrage on the U.S. military intelligence headquarters and vehicle dispatch center near Tan Son Nhut Airport, just over a kilometer away from Saigon. The U.S. military spokesman in Saigon admitted that the liberation army's attack was "powerful." The Saigon puppet police also exclaimed that this was "the bravest attack."

### 南越解放军奇袭朔庄美军机场大捷  歼敌四百余人　毁敌机十一架  某英雄部队英勇奋战一年来歼美军近三千

1966-08-22
第5版()
专栏：

    南越解放军奇袭朔庄美军机场大捷
    歼敌四百余人　毁敌机十一架
    某英雄部队英勇奋战一年来歼美军近三千
    新华社河内二十日电  据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方朔庄省解放武装力量七月二十六日晚上，猛烈袭击朔庄美军直升飞机场，同时炮击美川县伪军伞兵训练基地，共歼灭敌人四百二十六名，击毁敌机十一架，炸毁军火库一座。
    解放武装力量对朔庄机场的这次奇袭，是从一九六三年九月九日以来对这个机场进行的第十次袭击。在前九次袭击中，朔庄省解放武装力量共击毁和击伤敌机二百十五架，歼灭敌人五百二十二名。连同这次袭击的战果计算在内，解放武装力量共击毁击伤敌机二百二十六架，歼灭敌人六百七十二名，其中包括美国侵略军一百八十三名。
    新华社河内二十一日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，取得文相大捷的南越解放军某英雄部队，一年来英勇奋战不断创立新战功。据报道，到今年八月十八日止，这支英雄部队先后进行了二十八次战斗，全歼美军两个整营、五个连，伪军三个整营、五个连和十二个排，共消灭敌人五千零六十八名，其中包括美国侵略者二千八百三十九名。
    目前这支英雄部队的全体指战员在胡主席的伟大战斗号召下，正在开展热火朝天的杀敌立功竞赛，誓把抗美救国的伟大斗争进行到底，彻底干净地消灭美国侵略军。
    据新华社十八日讯  西贡消息：越南南方解放军十七日晚上接连袭击西贡附近的美国军事设施，打得敌人惊慌不安。
    据西方通讯社报道，越南南方解放军十七日晚上以猛烈的炮火轰击了离西贡新山一机场只有一公里多的美国军事情报处和汽车集中调度场。西贡的美军发言人承认，解放军的这次袭击是“厉害的”。西贡的伪警察也惊呼，这次袭击是“最勇敢的袭击”。
