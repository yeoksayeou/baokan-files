Title: Chairman JU De and His Wife Meet the Congolese (Brazzaville) National Assembly President and His Wife
Author:
Date: 1966-08-29
Edition: 4

On August 28, Chairman Ju De and his wife Kang Keqing met with the President of the National Assembly of the Congo (Brazzaville), Muya Bi Andele Jie, and his wife, as well as the parliamentary delegation led by Muya Bi Andele Jie. The photo shows them taking a group picture during the meeting.  Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter.
