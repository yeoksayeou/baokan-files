Title: Syrian Ambassador Calls on Vice Premier Chen Yi
Author:
Date: 1966-08-13
Edition: 4

	Syrian Ambassador Calls on Vice Premier Chen Yi

	BEIJING, Aug. 12 (Xinhua) -- The new Syrian Ambassador to China, Bashir Sadik, called on Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi this morning to discuss the presentation of his credentials.

	Chen Yi had a friendly talk with him.

### 叙利亚驻华大使拜会陈毅副总理

1966-08-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　叙利亚驻华大使拜会陈毅副总理
    新华社十二日讯　阿拉伯叙利亚共和国新任驻中国大使巴希尔·萨迪克，今天上午拜会陈毅副总理兼外交部长，商谈递交国书事宜。
    陈毅副总理同他进行了友好的谈话。
