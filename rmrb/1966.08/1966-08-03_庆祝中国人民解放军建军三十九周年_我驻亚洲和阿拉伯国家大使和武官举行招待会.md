Title: Celebrating the 39th Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1966-08-03
Edition: 5

Chinese Ambassadors and Military Attaches in Asian and Arab Countries Hold Receptions

	NEW DELHI, Aug. 1 (Xinhua) -- Chinese Military Attache Chiang Keding held a reception at the Chinese Embassy in Pakistan on the evening of August 1 to celebrate the 39th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

	Pakistani National Assembly Speaker Abdul Jabbar Khan, Pakistani Army Vice Chief of Staff General Yahya Khan, other high-ranking military officers and government officials, as well as prominent figures from various sectors attended the reception. Diplomats and military attaches from various countries accredited to Pakistan also attended.

	Chinese Charge d'Affaires Xu Ying also attended the reception.

	The reception was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere. Guests and hosts discussed Sino-Pakistani friendship and wished for further consolidation and development of friendly cooperation between the two countries.

	According to Xinhua News Agency, August 2, Chinese Ambassadors and Military Attaches in Afghanistan, Burma, Nepal, and India held receptions on August 1 to celebrate the 39th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

	Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan Chen Feng held a reception in Kabul to celebrate the 39th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

	Guests included Afghan First Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Nur Ahmad Etemadi, Defense Minister Khan Mohammad, Chief of Staff Gulam Faruq, and 180 other diplomats and military attaches from various countries.

	In Burma, Chinese Military Attache Xu Chongjiu held a reception. Guests included Revolutionary Council Member Colonel Dan Sheng, former Burmese Ambassador to China General Zao Wen, and Burmese Foreign Ministry Permanent Secretary U Tun Shin, among others.

	Chinese Ambassador to Burma Geng Biao, as well as diplomats and military attaches from various countries accredited to Burma, also attended the reception.

	In Nepal, Chinese Military Attache Guo Dongjun held a reception. Guests included Nepalese Defense Minister Giri Prasad Budhathoki, Nepalese Army Chief of Staff Surendra Bahadur Shah, and over 100 other guests.

	Chinese Ambassador to Nepal Yang Gongsu and diplomats from various countries accredited to Nepal also attended the reception.

	In India, Chinese Military Attache Mei Yongxi and his wife held a reception.

	According to Xinhua News Agency, August 2, Chinese Embassies in Arab countries held receptions on July 31 and August 1 to celebrate the 39th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

	Guests at the reception held by Chinese Military Attache Yan Ling at the Chinese Embassy in Syria on the evening of July 31 included Syrian Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Mahous, Chief of Staff General Suweidani, and Defense Ministry Secretary General General Hazzouri, among 160 others.

	Guests at the reception held by Chinese Ambassador Huang Hua on August 1 included Egyptian Undersecretary of State for Foreign Affairs Abu Chadi, Brigadier General Said Nasser Dib, and Secretary-General of the Egyptian-Chinese Friendship Association Hassan Ashmawi, among approximately 200 others.

	Guests at the reception held by Chinese Military Attache Hou Jianye at the Chinese Embassy in Iraq on August 1 included Iraqi Minister of Information Affairs Safwani, Acting Chief of Staff Brigadier General Jadir Yasin Ani, and 390 others.

	Guests at the reception held by Chinese Acting Military Attache Yang Jinkuo at the Chinese Embassy in Algeria on August 1 included Algerian Defense Ministry Political Committee Secretary General Shab, Inspector General Zerjini, and other high-ranking government officials.

### 庆祝中国人民解放军建军三十九周年  我驻亚洲和阿拉伯国家大使和武官举行招待会

1966-08-03
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝中国人民解放军建军三十九周年
    我驻亚洲和阿拉伯国家大使和武官举行招待会
    新华社拉瓦尔品第一日电　中国驻巴基斯坦大使馆武官蒋克定一日晚在这里举行招待会，庆祝中国人民解放军建军三十九周年。
    巴基斯坦国民议会议长阿卜杜勒·贾巴尔·汗，巴基斯坦陆军副总司令叶海亚汗陆军中将，其他高级军官和政府官员以及各界知名人士等出席了招待会。出席招待会的还有各国驻巴基斯坦外交使节和武官。
    中国大使馆临时代办徐英也出席了招待会。
    招待会是在热烈友好的气氛中进行的。
    宾主在一起交谈中巴友谊，并且祝贺两国友好合作关系进一步巩固和发展。
    据新华社二日讯　中国驻阿富汗大使和驻缅甸、尼泊尔、印度等国的武官一日分别举行招待会，庆祝中国人民解放军建军三十九周年。
    中国驻阿富汗大使陈枫在喀布尔举行了庆祝中国建军节三十九周年的招待会。
    出席招待会的有阿富汗第一副首相兼外交大臣努尔·艾哈迈德·埃特马迪，国防大臣汗·穆罕默德大将，总参谋长古拉姆·法鲁克上将以及外交使节和各国驻这里的武官等一百八十位客人。
    在缅甸，中国驻缅甸大使馆武官徐冲九举行了招待会。
    出席的缅甸各界人士有：革命委员会委员丹盛上校，前缅甸驻中国大使叫温准将和缅甸外交部常任秘书吴吞辛等。
    中国驻缅甸大使耿飚，各国驻缅甸的使节和武官也出席了招待会。
    在尼泊尔，中国驻尼泊尔大使馆武官郭东俊举行了招待会。尼泊尔国防大臣吉里·普拉萨德·布达托基，尼泊尔皇家陆军总司令苏伦德拉·巴哈杜尔·沙阿上将等一百多位客人出席了招待会。
    中国驻尼泊尔大使杨公素以及驻尼泊尔的各国外交官员也出席了招待会。
    在印度，中国驻印度大使馆武官梅永熙和夫人举行了招待会。
    据新华社二日讯　中国驻阿拉伯国家大使馆先后在七月三十一日和八月一日举行招待会，庆祝中国人民解放军建军三十九周年。
    出席中国驻叙利亚大使馆武官严泠七月三十一日晚的招待会的有：叙利亚副总理兼外交部长马胡斯，武装部队参谋长斯韦达尼少将，国防部秘书长赫佐勒少将等一百六十人。
    出席中国驻阿联大使黄华一日晚上的招待会的有：阿联外交部国务秘书阿布·查迪，赛义德·纳赛尔·迪卜少将，阿联—中国友好协会秘书长哈桑·阿什马维等约二百人。
    出席中国驻伊拉克大使馆武官侯建业一日晚上的招待会的有：伊拉克新闻事务国务部长萨夫瓦尼，代理参谋长加迪尔·亚辛·阿尼少将等三百九十人。
    出席中国驻阿尔及利亚大使馆代理武官杨锦科一日晚上的招待会的有：阿尔及利亚国防部政治委员会的秘书长沙布少校，总监察泽尔吉尼少校等政府高级官员。
